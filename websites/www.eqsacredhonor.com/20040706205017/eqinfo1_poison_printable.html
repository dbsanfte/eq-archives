POISON RECIPES
*The strength of the poison effects is directly related to your level. A level 50 Rogue can make a more potent Spider poison than a level 25 Rogue.  Example:  A DD (direct damage) of 5-141 (35) does only 5 points of damage at low levels, which will progressively increase based on your level.  At level 35 and above, it can do 141 points of damage.
