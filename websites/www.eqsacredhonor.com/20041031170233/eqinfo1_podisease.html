Plane of Disease

Originally, before Planes of Power was 'solved', Plane of Disease was one of the few zones you could enter (through the 'faucet' [D] in Plane of Tranquility) without a 'flag'.

I made this (as well as Plane of Tranquility Map) by running around zone with 'log' on and logging my /LOCs, then plotted the points using graph paper (was fun using graph paper again!) and scanned the paper and touched up and added text and (straight?) lines to produce what I hope was a valuable asset to someone.. ever wonder my spider caves are 'unfinished'? ;P 

This map is NOT complete... Please check back for changes and modifications! If you have any comments or suggestions..
