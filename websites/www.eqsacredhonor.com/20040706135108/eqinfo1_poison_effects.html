Poison Making Guide

This guide explains which TYPES of poisons do WHAT to an enemy...

POISON EFFECTS

ANIMAL - This poison series is only useful for animals. In that case it does a decent damage, but it is only used on rare occasions.  Use it for:

  • Poisoning training

  • Taking out animals quite fast

BERSERKER MADNESS - This is a trade-off poison that is considered among many rogues as "useless".  The poisoned victim suffers a large damage over time, but experiences also a greater rush of adrenaline that increases strength and armor rating.  Use it for:

  • Attacking casters & killing weaker caster pets
  • Training poison making
  • Kiting
  • Increasing STR/AC 
    • Tier I - 6 (Lvl 1) to 47 (Lvl 42)/0 (Lvl 1) to 6 (Lvl 42).  
    • Tier II - 6 (Lvl 1) to 55 (Lvl 50)/0 (Lvl 1) to 8 (Lvl 50).  
    • Tier III - 26 (Lvl 1) to 80 (Lvl 55)/0 (Lvl 1) to 17 (Lvl 55).  
    • Tier IV - 6 (Lvl 1) to 47 (Lvl 60)/0 (Lvl 1) to 6 (Lvl 60).
  • DD/DoT 
    • Tier I - 11 (Lvl1) to 52 (Lvl 42)/30 (Lvl1) to 235 (Lvl 42).  
    • Tier II - 11 (Lvl1) to 60 (Lvl 50)/30 (Lvl1) to 275 (Lvl 50).  
    • Tier III - 11 (Lvl1) to 65 (Lvl 55)/155 (Lvl1) to 425 (Lvl 55).  
    • Tier IV - 11 (Lvl1) to 70 (Lvl 60)/305 (Lvl1) to 600 (Lvl 60).
  • The effect lasts for 30 seconds.
BLIND POISON - Good long duration poison. Makes this poison valuable for beasts as a "poor mans blind" as well as for PvP.  Dont use that in dungeons or your prey will bring his friends. Use it for:
  • Blinding your PvP opponent
  • Make beast run off.
  • Decreasing ATK by 5
  • Length of time depends on Tier Level:
    • Tier I - lasts for 42 seconds.  
    • Tier II - lasts for 72 seconds.  
    • Tier III - lasts for 102 seconds.
BRITTLE HASTE - Another trade-off series of poisons. The weakening effect is greatly reduced by a haste effect where the beast damages slower but more often than before.  Not all that useful.  Use it for:
  • Train poison making.
  • Debuffing already weak beasts for training defense and when increased speed is gulped by the inability to make damage.
  • Decreased AC/STR 
    • Tier I - 2 (Lvl 1) to 14 (Lvl 40)/11 (Lvl 1) to 55 (Lvl 40).  
    • Tier II - 2 (Lvl 1) to 16 (Lvl 48)/11 (Lvl 1) to 58 (Lvl 48).  
    • Tier III - 2 (Lvl 1) to 18 (Lvl 59)/11 (Lvl 1) to 69 (Lvl 59).  
    • Tier IV - 2 (Lvl 1) to 20 (Lvl 60)/11 (Lvl 1) to 70 (Lvl 60)
  • Increased Attack speed 
    • Tier I - 10%.  
    • Tier II - 15%.  
    • Tier III - 20%.  
    • Tier IV - 25%
  • Length of time depends on Tier Level:
    • Tier I - lasts for 60 seconds.  
    • Tier II - lasts for 72 seconds.  
    • Tier III - lasts for 84 seconds.  
    • Tier IV - lasts for 96 seconds.
CONTACT - A nice and direct DD series. General useful.  Use it:
  • quick opening move
  • for PvP opening damage
  • for group support
  • DD 
    • Tier I - 5 (Lvl 1) to 141 (Lvl 35).  
    • Tier II - 5 (Lvl 1) to 177 (Lvl 44).  
    • Tier III - 13 (Lvl 1) to 221 (Lvl 55).  
    • Tier IV - 13 (Lvl 1) to 445 (Lvl 55).
DIZZY - Dizzy makes the opponents more vulnerable to your attacks. It also reduces the armor class of the opponent a bit. All in all not very useful. Reducing agility is closely pointless, it just makes the prey run off earlier and it is slightly better to hit and when a prey has 1000AC a reduction by 21 is pointless too.  Use it for:
  • Training purpose
  • Decreasing AGI/AC 
    • Tier I - 5 (Lvl 1) to 25 (Lvl 40)/3 (Lvl 1) to 15 (Lvl 40).  
    • Tier II -  5 (Lvl 1) to 29 (Lvl 45)/3 (Lvl 1) to 17 (Lvl 48).  
    • Tier III - 5 (Lvl 1) to 32 (Lvl 54)/3 (Lvl 1) to 19 (Lvl 54).  
    • Tier IV - 5 (Lvl 1) to 35 (Lvl 60)/3 (Lvl 1) to 21 (Lvl 60).
  • The effect lasts for 3 minutes.
FEEBLE MIND - This is a very plain poisons. It simply drains a lot of mana from your opponent. That effect is useless against NPC's with their closely eternal mana, but it is a real nasty surprise for every caster in PvP.  Use it for:
  • Draining Mana from casters in PvP only.
  • Reduces Mana 
    • Tier I - 7 (Lvl 1) to 217 (Lvl 36).  
    • Tier II - 7 (Lvl 1) to 265 (Lvl 44).  
    • Tier III - 7 (Lvl 1) to 301 (Lvl 50).  
    • Tier IV - 7 (Lvl 1) to 355 (Lvl 59).
FLESH ROT - That is a real nice combination poison. It makes the flesh rot on the living opponent and causes great pain and suffering. Especially useful for tough melee opponents, reducing their DEX and making them suffer some damage over time as well.  Also valuable is the fact, that the DoT is fixed and does not change with the level of the maker - that makes it as a hard hitting but DEADLY poison for young rogues in longer fights.  Use it for:
  • Pvp combat vs. melee classes
  • Support poison for tough melee opponents & expect a longer fight
  • DD/DoT/Decrease DEX.  
    • Tier I - 5/152/6 (Lvl 1) to 42 (Lvl 38).  
    • Tier II - 5/184/6 (Lvl 1) to 51 (Lvl 46).  
    • Tier III - 5/232/6 (Lvl 1) to 63 (Lvl 58).
  • Length of time depends on Tier Level:
    • Tier I - lasts 228 seconds.  
    • Tier II - lasts 276 seconds.  
    • Tier III - lasts 348 seconds.
INJECTED - Makes small DD and large, fast DoT.  General useful.  Use it for:
  • Opening poison
  • Soloing & Group support
  • PvP combat
  • DD/DoT 
    • Tier I - 2 (Lvl 1) to 38 (Lvl 37)/10 (Lvl 1) to 190 (Lvl 37).  
    • Tier II - 2 (Lvl 1) to 46 (Lvl 45)/10 (Lvl 1) to 230 (Lvl 45).  
    • Tier III - 2 (Lvl 1) to 49 (Lvl 48)/10 (Lvl 1) to 245 (Lvl 48).  
    • Tier IV - 2 (Lvl 1) to 59 (Lvl 58)/10 (Lvl 1) to 295 (Lvl 58).  
    • Tier V - 3 (Lvl 1) to 121 (Lvl 60)/35 (Lvl 1) to 625 (Lvl 60).
  • The effect lasts 30 seconds
LIQUID SILVER - Direct damage poison for undead beasts.  ONLY affects undead.  Use it for:
  • soloing undead NPC's
  • DD
    • Tier I - 16 (Lvl 1) to 250 (Lvl 40).  
    • Tier II - 16 (Lvl 1) to 298 (Lvl 48).  
    • Tier III - 16 (Lvl 1) to 370 (Lvl 60).
LOWER RESIST - Typical group support poison. Your casters will like it. It simply lowers all resists except poison. This is especially useful for huge, tough, top-end-of-area beasts. Send it into the veins of Lady Vox and Nagafen.  Use it for:
  • In group to prepare a beast for caster attacks
  • Decreases ALL resists except Poison from 
    • Tier I - 3 (Lvl 1) to 10 (Lvl 14).  
    • Tier II - 3 (Lvl 1) to 15 (Lvl 24).  
    • Tier III - 3 (Lvl 1) to 20 (Lvl 34).  
    • Tier IV - 3 (Lvl 1) to 30 (Lvl 54).
  • The effect lasts for 3 minutes.
MUSCLE LOCK - Rogues have a snare! Soloing with this is quite fun. Take out your strongest arrows or throwing daggers and kite the beasts.  Use it for:
  • Snaring any opponent.   
    • Tier I - Reduces movement from 31% (Lvl 1) to 40% (Lvl 10).  
    • Tier II - Reduces movement from 31% (Lvl 1) to 50% (Lvl 20).  
    • Tier III - Reduces movement from 31% (Lvl 1) to 55% (Lvl 25).  
    • Tier IV - Reduces movement from 31% (Lvl 1) to 60% (Lvl 30).
  • In combo with the blind series: stab, stab, stab
  • Nice in Pvp, especially the combo with blind
  • Lasts 1 tick (6 seconds) per level.
PARALYZING ROOT - Root and reduces the attack speed of the opponent. Use with blind. The beast is rooted first and then gets a hit with the blinding poison. Now the prey is unable to attack anymore and will take one stab after the other. The ultimate soloing poison.  Use it for:
  • PvP - use the combo!
  • Group support for healthier tanks
  • Soloing
  • Reduces the attack speed by 50%
  • Reduces Movement by 95%
  • Length of time depends on Tier Level:
    • Tier I - lasts for 42 seconds.  
    • Tier II - lasts for 72 seconds.  
    • Tier III - lasts for 102 seconds.
SUMMONED - This is the ultimate poison when you battle some casters with a pet. It does a decent direct damage on the pet, often killing it in one blow.  Use it for:
  • Soloing a caster with a pet
  • PvP vs. casters with pet
SYSTEM SHOCK - This poison is quite useful when you are facing a casting opponent. It pushes the target back, inflicts some direct damage and does a VERY short stun. It usually interrupts all casting and gives you the opportunity to land another safe blow. It should be considered as a Contact-series poison with disturbing side effects. Use Contact for DD only, and this one against casters. Watch the casters hands and jump in when they start to glow.  Use it for:
  • Push an opponent over the cliff
  • General useful for the direct damage
  • Pushes target back 1 foot
  • Stuns for 0.0 seconds (spell interrupt)
WEAKEN - A very useful poison when in melee with hard hitting opponents.  Use it for:
  • Reducing the strength of your target
  • Soloing strong mobs
  • Lasts for 1 tick (6 seconds) per level.
