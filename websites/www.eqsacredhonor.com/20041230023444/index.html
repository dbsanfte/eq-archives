WELCOME HOME WoV!

The Members and Officers of Wrath of Vaer and Members and Officers of Sacred Honor are now ONE! We have MERGED and ALL of US are now better for this change. There will be some adjustment and some changes to be sure but because OUR guild's operation is virtually identical and other than a few new faces and more input WE expect a smooth transition! Please read the forums and I shall email the passwords for the members forums to all those who I have addresses for (generally from filling out the application). All WoV members will be FULL Members/Officers/Magistrates (as their status in WoV was) and will NOT have to go though any probation period. The application is designed for new applicants so just fill it in best you can! Please look at our rules, links, eqinfo and the rest of the site as WE believe MUCH will be of interest. If you have ANY questions please post them in the appropriate forum or direct them via email to me or an Officer or Council person. We will be changing the website so expect a NEW LOOK! The website will be administered by OUR guild leaders. Because only one can be named Guild Leader and since OUR guild is controlled by Council, Officers and Members it was decided to just leave Kaah as guild leader. The council will be virtually evenly split and as FIRST priority will be discussing any differences which will affect OUR guild! I am not certain of any greeting which WoV had but we've adopted a greeting of "HONOR FIRST!" which MOST reply with "UNITY FOREVER!". A hullo will suffice of course! LOL!
