The Sacrifice of Nine
You now have 9 painstaking, time consuming, finely crafted gifts:  Demi-sec Champagne, Mistletoe Cutting Sickle, Golden Idol of Tunare, Blessed Fishing Pole, Star Ruby Encrusted Stein, Ceremonial Solstice Robe, Lotus Pie, Misty Thicket Picnic, Lucky Cabbage.  Place all of them into any normal, non-cultural forge.  Now SACRIFICE!  Click combine (no worries, there is no fail rate)... And TA DA!  You receive back:

Xanthe's Earring of Nature:  LORE, NO DROP, AC 5, STR 8, STA 8, WIS 10, HP 55, mana 55, sv fire 7, sv cold 7, sv magic 7, recommended level 51, Effect:  Circle of the Combines, tiny, wt 0.1,DRU/ALL AND Protection of the Cabbage scroll - level 59 spell, no drop, 500 mana.

Now, are you NOT a druid?  Or simply insane?  Turn in Xanthe's Earring of Nature to Rebecca Cabbageleaf in Fungus Grove. She will give you the following in back:

Earring of the Solstice:  LORE, NO DROP, AC 5, STA 5, WIS 8, INT 8, HP 55, mana 55, sv fire 5, sv cold 5, sv magic 5, recommended level 51, Effect:  Flowing Thought 1, tiny, wt 0.1, ALL/ALL
