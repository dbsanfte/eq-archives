Planar Progression Guide

The Walk-Thru

Mithaniel Marr
  • Plane of Justice - Tier 1 Zone:  Entry to Plane of Valor and the Plane of Storm
    Find Mavuin in the basement. Hail him and say 'what information?' Do any one of the trials, the Trial of Execution seems to be the easiest. Once you slay the boss, loot your Mark of Trial  Hail the Tribunal who administered the trial and say, 'I have come to plead the case of Mavuin'. Go back to Mavuin in the basement jail and hail him - this results in a flag for Plane of Valor and Plane of Storms. Completion of all the trials allows access to the 7th Hammer - this is not needed to progress to elemental planes.
  • Plane of Valor - Tier 2 Zone:  Entry to the Halls of Honor
    Complete the quest to gain the key to Aerin`Dar's Keep. Slay Aerin`Dar and hail the Planar Projection, then fight you way down to the Halls of Honor zone in to complete the flag. You must zone into Halls of Honor from Plane of Valor once to complete your flag.
  • Halls of Honor - Tier 3 Zone:  Entry to the Temple of Marr (Halls of Honor B)
    Complete the 3 trials found within the various three temples to gain entry to the Temple of Marr.  The trials are:  Crazed Norrathians - protecting a girl, Village People - protecting a village and Rydda`Dar - slaying a dragon.
  • Temple of Marr (Halls of Honor B) - Tier 3 Zone:  Part-Entry to the Tower of Solusek Ro
    Slay Mithaniel Marr and hail the Planar Projection.
Agnarr
  • Plane of Justice - Tier 1 Zone:  Entry to Plane of Valor and the Plane of Storm
    See above if not completed yet.
  • Plane of Storms - Tier 2 Zone:  Entry to Torden, the Bastion of Thunder
    Askr needs your help. He seeks a giant's beard, bone, sash and head. Hail him and do what he tells you to do. You will also need 2 Esoteric Medallion's from 2 different sections in Plane of Storms as well.  Then zone into Torden, the Bastion of Thunder via the tornado in the cave inhabited with frogs. You must zone into Bastion of Thunder from the tornado at least once to receive your key.
  • Torden, the Bastion of Thunder - Tier 3 Zone:  Tower Access
    Obtain a Ring of Torden off one of the mini-bosses in the lesser towers.  Combine a sandstorm, lightning, tornado and blizzard gems obtained from mobs in the lesser tower in the ring to obtain an Enchanted Ring of Torden.
  • Torden, Bastion of Thunder - Tier 3 Zone:  Part-Entry to the Tower of Solusek Ro
    Once you have access to the Upper Towers, obtain an Unadorned Symbol of Torden (10-slot container dropped by 2nd floor bosses).  Obtain a sandstorm, lightning, tornado and blizzard spheres from lesser towers and combine them in the Unadorned Symbol of Torden to obtain a Symbol of Torden.  Go slay Agnarr the Storm Lord to free Karana.  Go up the stairs to the door next to Agnarr's room and hail Karana. Say 'I will follow the path of the Fallen.' to receive your flag.
Terris Thule
  • Plane of Nightmare - Tier 1 Zone:  Entry to the Lair of Terris Thule (Plane of Nightmare B) and Entry to Torment, Plane of Pain
    First you must hail Elder Poxbourne. He can be found in the 'sickbay' (the area of PoTranquility near beach with sick elders in it) Then hail Adroha Jezith and say 'tortured by nightmares' - this gives you a pre-flag. Head over to the Plane of Nightmares. Cross the chasm and find the hedge - it is across the river along the left zone wall. Hail Thelin Poxbourne and get ported up into the maze follow him around protecting him from danger. (Note:  He will only port up to 3 groups.) Once you slay the boss, hail the projection to complete the flag for Lair of Terris Thule (Plane of Nightmare B). Go back to sickbay and hail Elder Poxbourne and Adroha again. 
  • Lair of Terris Thule (Plane of Nightmare B) - Tier 1 Zone:  Entry to Torment, Plane of Pain
    Zone in to Plane of Nightmare B through the gate near the hedge and slay Terris Thule and hail the projection to complete the flag.  You are now flagged for Plane of Torment.

Bertoxxulous and Saryrn

  • Plane of Disease - Tier 1 Zone:  Entry to the Crypt of Decay (Ruins of Lxanvom)
    Get the pre-flag from Alder Fuirstel in PoTranquility standing next to the zone in. Say 'what ward' to receive your pre-flag. Enter Plane of Disease and slay Grummus and hail the Planar Projection. Then drop down the hole into the Crypt of Decay to finish the flag. You must drop down into Crypt of Decay from Plane of Disease at least once to complete the flag.  Return to sickbay and hail Elder Fuirstel at sickbay.
  • Crypt of Decay (Ruins of Lxanvom)- Tier 2 Zone:  Entry to the Depths of the Crypt of Decay
    Fight to Carprin then slay Carprin to trigger a ring like event. Keep moving forward and slaying the various mini-bosses in your way. Then slay the High Priest himself and hail the rat to complete the flag. Only 32 keys can be achieved per Carprin - High Priest Ultor Szanvon cycle.
  • Depths of the Crypt of Decay (Ruins of Lxanvom)- Tier 2 Zone:  Entry to Torment, Plane of Pain; Part-Entry to the Tower of Solusek Ro
    Slay the only mob down here to start the Bertoxxulous event. Slay Bertoxxulous and hail the projection to receive the flag.  Then zone out, go hail Alder and Elder again to complete flag.
  • Torment, Plane of Pain - Tier 2 Zone:  Part-Entry to the Tower of Solusek Ro
         Go to sickbay in PoTranquility. Hail Fahlia Shadyglade. Say 'what condition', 'what plane of torment' and 'I will go' and you will receive a flag. Go to Plane of Torment and gain entrance to Saryrn's tower.  To gain access to Saryrn start off by attacking Baraguj Szuul. When you do this, you will be teleported into his stomach. Mobs will come in waves until you reach the Unimaginable Horror. Slay the horror and you will be teleported back outside where you can attack and slay Baraguj. Slaying him gets you a container called the 'Mouths of Baraguj Szuul.'  Into the container, you need to put the following, which all drop in this Plane: Orb of Anguish, Orb of Agony, Orb of Pain and Orb of Suffering.  Combining them gets you the key to Saryrn's chamber.  Slay Mareeq to spawn Tylis. Go through his dream event to slay the Keeper of Sorrows - get a flag - Tylis ports you back down to the room before Saryrn. Slay Saryrn, hail Planar Projection. Go back to sickbay in Tranquility and hail both Tylis and Fahlia. 
Rallos Zek (Completing the Zek, Saryn & MM Chain PARTIALLY EP flags.)
  • Plane of Innovation Junkyard - Tier 1 Zone:  Entry to the Plane of Innovation Factory
    Nitram Anizok, pos1500, pos970 (take the right path from the zone in, he may not always be up, clear out mobs around the hut to spawn him)  Trigger the quest with the gnome by giving him Bundle of Super Conductive Wires, Copper Node and Intact Power Cell and follow him around protecting him. Eventually, he'll lead you to the dragon Xanamech Nezmirthafen. Slay him to receive the key to the Factory. You do not need the key to progress, but you need at least 1 person with it to open the factory door.
  • Plane of Innovation Factory - Tier 1 Zone:  Entry to Drunder, the Fortress of Zek (Plane of Tactics)
    Giwin Mirakon is located in the first room at the entrance of the Factory in Plane of Innovation. To enter the Factory, someone must be flagged from slaying Xanamech Nezmirthafen.  Hail the gnome at the start. Say 'I will test the machine' - this will get you a pre-flag. Then slay the Manaetic Behemoth deeper inside the factory and hail Giwin again. You are now flagged for Plane of Tactics.
  • Drunder, Fortress of Zek (Plane of Tactics) - Tier 3 Zone:  Part-Entry to the Tower of Solusek Ro
    Slay the Zek brothers, Tallon and Vallon. Hail the projection of receive the flag.
  • Drunder, Fortress of Zek (Plane of Tactics) - Tier 3 Zone:  Entry to Eryslai, the Kingdom of Wind; the Reef of Coirnav; Vegarlson, the Earthen Badland; Part-Entry to Solusek Ro's Chamber
    Slay Rallos Zek the Warlord. Hail the projection to receive your flag. You are now partially Elemental Planes Flagged.

At this point you should have slain Mithaniel Marr, Saryrn, Tallon, and Vallon Zek. Now find the seer in PoKnowledge - she's in the building right at zone in from Tranquility. Sit down and say 'unlock my memories' She won't necessarily have anything to say - this is for fixing flags if you do them out of order. Now go to Maelin. He is a gnome in the PoKnowledge library. To get to him go to the bottom floor of the library and find the elevator (it has a button in the wall) Ride the elevator up to the top and he's behind the table in front of a bookshelf. Hail him and say 'I have new information' and 'I have new lore'. You may need to do this a few times. Eventually he will tell you that you need to go find Rallos Zek. If you don't get this go back to the seer, sit, then say 'guided meditation' you should see this:

'The History translated for you reveals the fate of Zebuxoruk. Trapped in the Plane of Time you must gain the power of the elements to gain entrance.

The Cipher of the Divine Language appears on your arms for a brief moment then fades.

Mavuin is grateful to you for taking his case before the Tribunal. The information provided to you, that Mithaniel Marr and Karana carry information you should seek, could be quite useful.

Tylis has been removed from his agony.

Saved from a world of eternal nightmares, Thelin is forever in your debt.

Saved from certain doom, Milyk and Adler are forever in your debt.

Now that Grummus has been destroyed, the entrance to the Crypt of Bertoxxulous should open before your might.

The portal into the Plane of Fire has been altered. Miak needs you to find the correct way to channel the portal.

The words of Maelin echo in your mind, 'The Zeks and Solusek are planning an invasion in Norrath, find out more from Rallos if you can.'

If you don't see all the text above, go back through the guide and see what you're missing. Otherwise, you are ready for Solusek Ro's Tower.

Tower of Solusek Ro - Completion of Full Elemental Flagging

  • Tower of Solusek Ro - Tier 3 Zone:  Part-Entry to Solusek Ro's Chamber
    Slay the 5 mini-bosses: Jiva, Arylixir, Xuzl, Rizlona and the Protector of Dresolik to receive party entry into Solusek Ro's chamber. These 5 flags along with the Rallos Zek flag allow you to enter Solusek Ro's room, you can now port up into his chamber.
  • Tower of Solusek Ro, Solusek Ro's Chamber - Tier 3 Zone:  Entry to Doomfire, the Burning Lands (Plane of Fire)
    Slay Solusek Ro. Hail the projection and drop down the well into Doomfire. You must drop down into the well to finish off your flag.

Once you have slain Rallos along with the other listed flag mobs (Agnarr, etc) Go to Maelin, say 'I have new information' and you should see this:

'There is no way to escape from the prison that is The Plane of Time.  I am sorry but your quest for information ends here.  Time is something that none of us can escape.  That is however... back when the explorations of the Planes were more common, I would travel searching for knowledge and lore to bring back to Tanaan.  I stumbled into Plane of Innovation.  It was a great marvel to see indeed.  I found the creator of all things mechanical.  Meldrath the Marvelous was a kind and just gnome.  We spent many weeks together discussing all of his devices.  This included a machine that would allow you to open a tear into a period of time and enter into that time.  The machine was more of a flight of whimsy though as the power necessary to power such a machine was enormous.  He jokingly equated needing the very essence of the elements to power it.'

Maelin takes a deep breath and continues, 'I can see now that he was not joking at all,  Let us suppose that you travelers could venture into the Elemental Planes and retrieve this essence; and form it into one powerful conglomeration.  You could open the tear into the period of time before Zebuxoruk was imprisoned.  There is no way you can free him from his stasis now, but if you were to halt the Pantheon at the time of imprisonment.  Hah!  It could work I do believe.  Forgive me, but my old gnomish heart is alive with the excitement of possibilities.  Gather up your strength friends, travel into the deep elements.  You will need all of your wits about you.  Find the very essence of the elementals, and fuse them into one.  How to combine them I do not know, and can only wish you luck on finding that information.  If you can accomplish this please come get me.  I would like to record the events as they take place!'

You have received a character flag!

  • Eryslai, the Kingdom of Wind (Plane of Air) - Tier 4 Zone:  Home of Xegony
     Slay the avatars to gain access to Xegony. Slay Xegony to obtain one of the pieces needed for entrance to the Plane of Time, Amorphous Cloud of Air

  • Vegarlson, the Earthen Badland (Plane of Earth) - Tier 4 Zone:  Home of the Rathe
     Complete all the rings to spawn the Mystical Arbitor and slay him in order to gain access to Plane of Earth B. Once there, slay the Rathe counsel and the Avatar of Earth.  Loot Mound of Living Stone from Rathe to obtain one of the pieces needed for entrance to the Plane of Time.

  • Reef of Coirnav (Plane of Water) - Tier 4 Zone:  Home of Coirnav
    Slay Coirnav to obtain one of the pieces needed for entrance to the Plane of Time, Sphere of Coalesced Water

  • Doomfire, The Burning Lands (Plane of Fire) - Tier 4 Zone:  Home of Fennin Ro
    Slay Fennin Ro to obtain one of the pieces needed for entrance to the Plane of Time, Globe of Dancing Flame

The Plane of Time A and The Plane of Time B

  • The Plane of Time A - Tier 5 Zone:  Quintessence of Elements
     There is a book found on a slave in Plane of Tactics which contains this vital information:

'I, Frana Shimi, have speculated for the Lords of War an answer for a new type of container. The problem that you had presented to me was the ability to seal off the very power of the elements. No material has the ability to hold all of the powers of the elements at one time.
I believe though if you were to create an unfired medium container and then fire it with two high quality firing sheets covered in Multielemental glaze it would create an invulnerable container. To make the multielement glaze, you will need to mix together the four base element glazes: windy glaze, aqua glaze, earthen glaze, and magma glaze in a glaze mortar.
I dare not imagine why you would need to combine the quintessense of the elements, or what you would do with it. Please consider my time here spent well, and set my soul at rest. I have toiled here for long.'

  • Quintessence of Elements - Combine in an odylic vial 1 each of:  Globe of Dancing Flame, Sphere of Coalesced Water, Mound of Living Stone and Amorphous Cloud.

  • Odylic Vial (Pottery: Yield 1, trivial unknown) - Combine the following in a tanaan kiln:  2 high quality firing sheet, 1 multielement glaze and unfired medium container.

  • Multielement Glaze (Pottery: Yield 1, trivial no fail) - Combine in a glaze mortar 1 each of:  windy, earthen, aqua and magma glaze.

  • Windy Glaze (Pottery: Yield 1, trivial no fail) - Combine in a glaze mortar 1 each of: glaze lacquer, alabaster beak and ivory stormrider canine.

  • Earthen Glaze (Pottery: Yield 1, trivial no fail) - Combine in a glaze mortar 1 each of glaze:  glaze lacquer, liquefied earth and shattered crystal.

  • Aqua Glaze (Pottery: Yield 1, trivial no fail) - Combine in a glaze mortar 1 each of glaze:  glaze lacquer, emerald colored mephit scales and oil of frog.

  • Magma Glaze (Pottery: Yield 1, trivial no fail) - Combine in a glaze mortar 1 each of glaze:  glaze lacquer, solidified magma and fiery granite.

  • Unfired Medium Container (Pottery: Yield 1, trivial 36) - Combine in a pottery wheel, or tanaan pottery wheel 1 each of:  water flask, block of clay and medium jar sketch.

  • The alabaster beak and ivory stormrider canine drop in Plane of Air.

  • The liquefied earth and shattered crystal drop in Plane of Earth.

  • The emerald colored mephit scales and oil of frog drop in Plane of Water.

  • The solidified magma and fiery granite drop in Plane of Fire.

Once the Quintessence of Elements has been made go hail Maelin in the library of PoKnowledge.

Maelin tells you, 'The Quintessence!  Oh my this is amazing!  I have come into contact with Chronographer Muon in the realm of innovation.  Go to him, show him you have the power to activate machine.  I shall meet you there, this I must see!'

Go to Plane of Innovation, you must have the Factory key or have someone with you that does.  Upon entering the factory immediately turn left inside the first room, open the door there and you will find Chronographer Muon, a robot, in the back left corner. Hail him. Chronographer Muon tells you, 'Acquisition of power completed.  Would you like to be transported to the time-projection chamber?'  You say, 'Yes'.  Chronographer Muon tells you, 'Compliance.'  You are transported to an unknown area of the zone.  Go to the end of the walkway and find Maelin along with a time machine.  Hail him.

Loreseeker Maelin fidgets about with excitement 'So here you are, this is quite impressive.  I cannot wait to see the results of this impressive machine!  I have coordinated with the clockworks here that have not gone mad.  We have set the machine to tear a point of time open that should be equal to that based on the cipher and history that we have [researched].'

You say, 'What have you researched.'

Loreseeker Maelin says, 'Based on the findings from the information that you brought back to me I have determined the exact time to open.  I believe the machine will work.  Please step up and activate the machine!  Once you have formed a bond with the Plane of Time you will be able to access the Plane again through the Plane of Tranquility.  They have built a portal there, but no one was able to become attuned to that plane, until you that is.  Good Luck!'

Click the time machine in the middle and you will be transported to the Plane of Time.

The Seer Text Walk-Thru

Go find Seer Mal Nae`Shi at negative 40, negative 200 in PoKnowledge.

You say, 'Hail, Seer Mal Nae`Shi'
Seer Mal Nae`Shi snaps from her meditation, 'Greetings traveler, I am Mal Nae`Shi. I have come here to heighten my awareness through meditation, much can be learned through a brief exploration of one's self... Assuming there is something there to learn. While reaching inner peace is a journey each spirit must undertake on its own, I can begin your voyage with [guided meditation] which will aid your memory, once you are seated. I can also [unlock] some [memories] which may previously been inaccessible.'
You say, 'unlock memories'
You manage to recover some images from your childhood, but no recent events spark a memory.
You say, 'guided meditation'
 If her replies are the same after multiple 'unlock memories' and does not have the childhood quote, that is a clue to some flag/flags you are missing. Find the quote below for help on what to do.

Now say, 'guided meditation'.  Here is what they mean!
Blue text means you have finished a flag and are ready for more.
Purple bolded text means something is not finished for the final flag.
Black text is explanation.

  1. Tier 1 - Justice

The Charm and AA Points

To obtain your the Intricate Wooden Figurine Charm and start accumulating stats on it:

You say, 'Hail, Gram Dunnar'
The old dwarf looks up at you slowly, his wrinkled hands continuing to carve a small wooden object. He grins as he eyes your dust-covered outfit and well-used equipment. 'Ahh, a young adventurer,' he says. 'Warms my heart to see the spirit I once had still going strong. Got too old, you see.' He strokes his beard. 'Tried taking up a [craft], but it just isn't the same. Sure would be nice to hear some [stories] about those new places I'll never get to see.'
You say, 'what craft'
Gram Dunnar stops carving and holds up the object between his short fingers.  It is a figurine of a swordsman with many intricate details.  'No one really wants to buy them, these days.  If there's no magic in it...' he shrugs.  'Still, something to take up some time.'  He rummages through some finished pieces on the floor around him, picks up one, and tosses it to you.  'Here,' he says.  'Maybe it'll bring you some luck.'
You say, 'I will tell stories'
Gram listens quietly and nods occasionally as you tell him of your adventures. 'Good stories,' he says. 'Heard 'em before, but still bring back memories.' or he will give you different text that gives you an AA point if you have made planar progression.
