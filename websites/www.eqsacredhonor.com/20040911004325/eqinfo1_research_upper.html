For: Upper Level Spells

LoY Research Spells
Legacy of Ykesha has made available many spells previously only obtained from high level encounters through the use of the Research skill available to Intelligence based caster classes.  

The first thing any caster will require in order to make the new spells is their advanced research book for combining the research ingredients.  These containers are NO DROP, and given by Researcher Alvarik at pos 1370, pos 80 in the Gulf of Gunthak.

Hail him and say: "I am a Wizard", "I am a Necromancer", "I am a Enchanter" or "I am a Magician".  Whichever is the one appropriate for your class.

The new research books replace your old research books, but only if your research is 101 or higher.  The parchment and quill which are required for all the new research spells listed below are also available from starting city guild vendors.

Intelligence casters can also make spells for the Priest classes.  Any Intelligence caster can make any Priest class spell using their Advanced research tome and the appropriate components.

The scrolls which are created through the use of the research skill are tradeable and can be used by any applicable class.  This means for example that a Yaulp IV scroll scribed by a Necromancer can be used by either a Paladin or a Cleric.  Likewise a Paralyzing Earth scroll created by an Enchanter can be also used by a Cleric, Shaman, Necromancer or Wizard.

You also can get an automated quill which is created by gnomes with the tinkering skill.  This item has a 5% skill increase modifier which will help you to succeed in making the new spells.
