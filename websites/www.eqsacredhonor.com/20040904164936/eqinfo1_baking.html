Baking Made Easy!

From 0 to 250 Skill!

By Adrastea Trailtracker

You want to become a Grandmaster Baker?  This Guide will take you from 0 to 250.  The recipes below are what appear to be the easiest and fastest to build skill as quickly as possible for good, evil or neutral races.  No trips to Jaggedpine Forest, no endless foraging for the foragers.  And only minimal slaughtering for meat and eggs.  You will need a forager only for a few recipes.  Some of the recipes require other trades.  However, with the Bazaar in full operation, these items are easily accessible!  I did add those recipes needed by trade skill under the baking recipes if you are so inclined to make them yourself.  Also try and raise all your stats as high as possible, depending on the type class you are.  Pure casters & Shadow Knights - raise that Intelligence and Dexterity.  Priest classes, Paladins, Rangers and Beastlords - raise that Wisdom and Dexterity.  Melees - whichever is higher Wisdom or Intelligence, get it raised!  Again, raise that Dexterity as well.  It is a huge debate over the stats to raise, through different discussion boards and talking to people, not to mention my own trial and error.  This is the way to go.  It has worked well for me.  Also, visit your friendly neighborhood Gnome and get a Geerlok All Purpose Cooking Utensil, this item equips into Primary and adds a skill modifier of +5.  Now get out there and start Baking!
