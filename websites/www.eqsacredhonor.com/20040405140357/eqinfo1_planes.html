PLANES INFORMATION

The "Planes" are outside of the physical boundaries of Norrath and require 'porting' in/out. These are the zones that Gods, Godesses, Demi-God/desses and Heros reside.

Currently being 'revamped' by SoE, many changes are expected. Plane of Hate has already been 'revamped' which has made OUR map of Hate inaccurate.

OUR Planal Guides are meant to familiarize one with the planes and how to survive them. The source of information has been time-tested and confirmed via many sources (including direct, first-hand knowledge).
