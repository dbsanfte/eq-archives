FEW LITTLE FUN THINGS TO DO

Toxdil - When you give him 20gp pieces (NOT 2pp) and 2 snake venom sacs, he will give you a bottle of snake poison and a bit experience.

Gindlin Toxfodder - For 1 Crow's Special Brew, available from Crow at Crow's bar in Qeynos, 2 Spider Venom Sacs and 20gp you will get a flask of Spider Venom.

Conium Darkblade - Give him 3 Wasp Venom Sacs and 30gp and receive a flask of Wasp Poison.

Crookstinger Poison - Drops from the Crookstinger Wasp in Lesser Faydark.

Vial of Rabid Froth - Drops from the Undead gnoll Gnashmaw in South Karana.

Groflah Steadirt - Give Tumpy Tonics to him in the Freeport East Bar. This gives excellent Freeport Faction.

APPLYING POISON

Just click any piercing weapon onto the vial.  You will automatically sit and apply the poison.  After you apply poison to your blade, it will last until you zone or attempt to poison something.  Poison can be resisted by the target, and if it is, you have to reapply and try again.

VIALS

Vials are for storing the produced poison in a flask. You can buy them or produce them with pottery. It is strongly recommended you learn pottery because the produced vials are much cheaper than the bought vials. There are three kinds of vials.

Poison Vial
For easier poisons. This is the simplest and cheapest vial of all.  You use these for Trivial 0 to 32.

Lined Poison Vial
A little harder poisons use these vials.  You use these for Trivial 32 to 168.

Sealed Poison Vial
For the strongest poisons.  They are quite expensive, but the choice of an experienced rogue.  You use these for Trivial 168 to >250. 

SUSPENSIONS

The suspension fluids are liquids that are used to dissolve and bind the ingredients. Different suspensions are needed for different poisons. All of the suspensions have to be purchased from a merchant.

Suspension
The simple suspension for everyday. You use these for Trivial 0 to 95. 

Constrict Suspension
A better version of the ordinary suspension.  You use these for Trivial 32 to 204. 

Larent Suspension
Used for the tough poison, this suspension has excellent quality.  You use these for Trivial 183 to >250. 

Etheral Suspension
Only the strongest poisons use etheral. This kind of suspension is the magic kind for ingredients that refuse to bind with any other kind of suspension.  Therefore it is expensive and hard to get.  Not every merchant is able to sell you this precious fluid.  You use these for Trivial 205 to >250. 

TIERS

Tier I - Starter poisons for the novice poison maker.  Trivial 0 - 96

Tier II - Great intermediate poisons.  Trivial 97 - 183

Tier III - So you want to be a Master Poison Maker?  That is these.  Trivial 183 - 207

Tier IV - These are for expert poison makers.  Trivial 206 - 250+

Tier V - Sorry, no Grandmaster Trophy.  But GRATS if you are successfully making these!  Trivial 250+
