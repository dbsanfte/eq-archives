Plane of Tranquility

Plane of Tranquility is a neutral zone with Venders, Elders and quest NPC as well as being the gateway zone to the other planes of power

I made this (as well as Plane of Disease Map) by running around zone with log on and logging my /LOCs, then plotted the points using graph paper (was fun using graph paper again!) and scanned the paper and touched up and added text and (straight?) lines to produce what I hope was a valuable asset to someone.
