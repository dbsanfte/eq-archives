87 Reavers! (all in a row!)

Yesterday afternoon/evening (Dec. 8, 2002) we gathered some friends and allies together to do a couple of reavers in CoM. We took less than 3 full groups in and quickly dispatched the first wave. We decided to do what we refer to as the 'whole circuit' (go all the way up and do the 1 reaver at the 'end-of-the-line' in the floating buildings up the ramps) which we ended up hurrying for because Darkblood needed Lord Rak (who happened to spawn up the lift on our first cycle) so if we didn't hurry they would have to kill the reavers we were after to dispatch their epic mob safely. We levi'd down to find they were clearing to get to their mob and were already in the main room of the temple (behind the locked door that is between the gazebos).
This group of Darkblood saw we were ready to fish the reavers and kindly waited for us to do so... What happened next took everyone by surprise. On the last reaver pulled from up the lift (you know how when you kill a reaver it can spawn another reaver?) we got another reaver! And another! And another! and they just KEPT spawning! And spawning! The Darkblood group (I believe around 10 in number?) who was there (for Dryzun's epic piece), showed their HONOR by jumping to the task of handling additional mobs and additional healing while we just KEPT KILLING one reaver after another!
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We killed 87 reavers off of ONE pull!
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

After a dozen or so reavers fell Ecclesiastique took the initiative to /petition this event. We did not wish to take ANY chances of being acused of any exploiting and we were CYAing all who were there. A guide showed up and watched another dozen or so go down and then a FULL GM came and watched us kill the rest. Fatigue and cramps and an overwhelming sense of accomplishment displaced our adrenaline. I went ahead and pulled the reaver from the front room to SHOW the GM that the zone was NOT bugged. Only 3 total from THAT reaver!
I asked the FULL GM...
Quote:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You told Olovf, 'ok, thank you do you know if there is a record soemwhere of the most?'
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

and his reply..
Quote:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Olovf tells you, 'No..hehe, I'm pretty sure that was it though'
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Of the 87 killed we got only TWO Jade Reavers But those 2 JRs will ALWAYS be 2 of MY personal favorites! They were lottoed and went to be given to the appropriate druid/ranger for that part of their epic quest! WOOT!
Kudos to EVERYONE who helped (those there throughout the entire raid, the couple who arrived to help after we began, Darkblood (for thier ALL their help) and those supported us in channels and in spirit! We COULD not have done it otherwise!
