New Member Registration

Thank you for choosing to join Sacred Honor guild! The questions we ask allow us to get to know you a little better. Some questions are NOT used for OR against you but are general information questions just to let us know about the person behind the character. We currently have no age requirements or minimum level requirements. Please answer ALL questions truthfully. After all, it's the HONORABLE thing to do. :-)

1) What is your E-Mail Address?  

 

2) What is your character's name (first AND last)?

 

3) Why do you want to join Sacred Honor? (please list as many details as possible!)

  

4) How did you hear about Sacred Honor?

 

5) What is your TRUE age?  

 

6) What is your character's Race, Class, and Level?

 

7) What is your character's Date of birth (use /played command in game to find out)?

[Month Day Year - ie. December 31, 2000]

 

8) Have you ever been kicked from a guild before? If so, why?

 

9) Do you have any characters in other guilds? If so, please list character name/guild

 

10) Please list any guilds you have been accepted to in the past

 

11) Do you agree to a probation period? (Which allows you and us to decide if we are right for each other)  

 

12) What days and times do you USUALLY play?

 

13) What time zone are you in?

 

14) What is your gender? (player not character)

 

Again, thank you for taking the TIME to answer honestly and completely! It WILL work in your favor if you took the time! Your completed application will be considered and voted upon! Any player-character found submitting false information is subject to disciplinary action up to and including being asked to leave the guild.
