Welcome To Malevolent Intent's Recruitment Center.


Malevolent Intent is an American Based Guild with a playing schedule that may not best suit most European players. We will never restrict any applicant because of his/her physical locality, but we do wish to create an environment beneficial for all members.

Prior to posting here please make sure you have read and understand our guild rules. The guild charter can be found at The Covenant.

Take the time to really tell us about yourself.  The more information you provide, the better we'll be able to determine if you'll be a good fit for the guild.

Requirements for consideration:
Must be of level 55 or higher.
You must have common knowledge of the game and the zones which make up our playing world.
Most important -- Be a team player.

Should you meet these basic requirements your application will be submitted to the officers of Malevolent Intent.  You will then be contacted by our recruitment officer and asked to participate in guild events. After a few events the members and officers will take a vote on your application. If all goes well you will get an invite into our family... Goodluck.

If you have further questions please contact Ailaire in game.
