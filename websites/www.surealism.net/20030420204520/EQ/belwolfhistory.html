Belwolf the Warrior

I left halas because clanless people are considered non-enities there and I was tired of all the snow.
The rest is my business.
I am a berserker.
I am most at home in combat but am a peaceful and gentle man outside of a fight
but don't make any sudden moves right after the fight.
I love good food and pretty women. *smirks*
