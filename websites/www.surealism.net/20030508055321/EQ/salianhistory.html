Salian Cruento
 
 Salian peered  through the dense fog inhalling the cold morning air deep into  his lungs. It had been years since he had been back to his home town, of course there would be no welcome home dinners and no smileing faces to greet him. If he was lucky he might find a warm bed and a strong ale in a inn somewhere. Closeing his eyes breifly he remembers the night he left, the winds had told his mother of a comming change. Her Druidadic ways had kept her in touch with the Mother of All and she could often sense things long before anyone else. My father was a paladin and a true master of the blade, from my early days as a child I can remember him teaching me how to use the noble sword, how to wield the savage axe and even how to shred an enemies armor usening magical claws. I soon learned how to make the sword an extention of my body, I learned from my mother the ways of a druid how to use what the mother has given us to survive. The sun had just set and my father and I had just returned from the market, when we arrived at the house my mother was frantic and with fever. My father tried to help her rest and was finally able to get her to sleep, although I do not think she rested as I still remember hearing her screams late into the night.
 
 Hours had passed and still she did not stop screaming, the pain she was in must have been extream for my mother  was a strong woman who was very close to Tunare. I even remember curseing Tunare for allowing my mother to suffer. I had finally started to drift off to sleep  when smoke filled my lungs and I awoke  coughing and choking. I ran to my mothers room but she was gone, I heard my father yelling my name from outside the house and then I heard other voices off in the distance. Quickly I got out of the house that was now becomming engulfed in  flames, stepping outside I saw them the entire town must have been there screaming and shouting some in a tounge I had never heard. My father stood off to side while the mob had my mother tied to tree by the neck ready to hang her, I screamed but was silenced with a blow to my head. Everything went blurry and I could hardly hear but my blood  rage was past the point of reason now I ran forward towards my mother and picked up a sharp rock from  off the groud, as I reached the rope I began to cut  and but found a strong arm lift me from the ground and toss me aside yelling.
 "Wait your turn BOY we shall hang you soon enough half-breed!"
 I could do nother but watch as the rope around my mothers neck became tight and her breathing was slowed. She raised her hand and spoke, her voice was so quiet I could not hear what she said but as I felt the spirit of the wolf enter me I knew she ment for me to run . I ran. I ran until my legs would no longer move, the mob had given chase for some time but they where unable to catch me, I had escaped but my mother had not. Several days later I returned to my old house to bury my parents, but I could only find my mother corpse and so I buried her and made a vow to Tunare that I would not rest until the man who was responsible for my mothers death was brought to justice. Since that day I have hunted my father, as I will until I see my father tortued and killed as my mother was.
