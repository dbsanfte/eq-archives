Karvon the Necromancer
 
I come from the island of Erudin. Once a noble boy studying the arts of magi,
until a war broke out between the good magi erudites and the oukasted or should I say heretics.
Casualites were everywhere.....last thing I remember was a big exsplosion covered the lands......
luckly a man named Miragul saved me by casting a protective spell. Soon later he told me that good magic is for the weak
and I should study the art of the bad. I believe it soon enough after what happen many moments ago.
So I started studying the art of Necromancy... and so my story begins in Norrath as a young man.
