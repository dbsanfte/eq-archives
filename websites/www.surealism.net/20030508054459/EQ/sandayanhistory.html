Sandayan the Shadow Knight
 
Raised in Nektulos Forest I always believed I was meant for some greater purpose in life.
It wasn't until I had to make a trip to the city that I realized my calling soon to come.
On my way to the city I saw the most vile of all creatures, a creature whose stench poisoned the air in which we breathed,
these so called halflings violated our forest driving away all its virture.  As I looked upon this creature my blood began to boil,
it was as if an unknown force merged with my soul all that I knew at the moment was to destroy.
Surprisingly I killed the halfling with very little effort, as it's blood stained corpse laid below my feet I was entranced by it.
The sight of its blood covered on my dagger, what thrill it would be to kill another I thought to myself.
Still there was something missing the death of this being was to quik, if only to torture it a little longer,
to raise its spirit from its corpse and to have it become my slave to obey my every whim.
At last I found my destiny, I shall train at the lodge of the dead and become a knight of death.
