Bagum the Shadow Knight
 
 
 
Me was born in da swamp. Momma bashin da froggies to get meat for da family nd out mees pop.
Momma tell Bagum dat when he born he pick up stick nd bash wif momma. Momma likes Bagum best.
Me come from big family of Bashers nd day train Bagum make him good basher to.
When Bagum still little trolly me be in da swamp bashin da baby frogies when me find old trolly.
He tell Bagum dat he 4 year old. Now since Bagum never hear of dat number bfore me no dat dis trolly be old.
He tell Bagum dat he Shamin nd dat he gonna die. Me tell him ok and go back to bashin.
But bfore Bagum can leab he blow shiny stuff ober bagum. Bagum feel funny. Bagum feel like he can make majik now but not no how.
Mees goes to Dady nd he tell Bagum dat Bashers dont like dat majik stuff.
"Bagum no make majik Bagum BASH" he tell Bagum. Me not happy but mees say dat I bash like he tell me.
Momma hears Bagum and Dady words nd at me bedtime Momma tell Bagum story.
Momma say
~Long time ago when Bagums Dady was da little trolly like Bagum he was born to da family of de Shadow nights.
Bagums Dady try to do good for his Mommy and Dady but Bagums Dady no can make da majik.
Bagums Dady kiked out of de family neber to return. Bagums Dady leabe Da Swamp for long long long time so dat
no one remember Bagums Dady. He return a week later and no one remember dat he be Shadow night.
Bagums Dady say he Basher nd da Bashers welcome him. He be Basher eber since.~ Momma tell Bagum to go away
for da long long long time nd when Bagum come back he go to da Shadow nights.
Bagum become what Bagums Dady susposed to become. Bagum make Momma prod.
So mees leabe for da long long long time nd da week later me go to da Shadow nights nd say dat me one ob dem.
From den on me become Shadow night.
