Tanthalais the Ranger
 
Somewhere way back my mother was raped by a human who was on a raid
in the elven city but that was generations ago. I have been told the story many times
which makes me carefull and withdrawn in the company of humans unless they are my
close friends. The earliest memory I have on my adventures is the promise of one of my
human friends telling to come to Qeynos to adventure with me and being left to fend for myself.
