Fuzy the Warrior
 
 
Long long tym go, it leest seems dat way, me come krash'n inta dis wurld on mees heed
wull me mudder b mukin dwarf stuw (fudder says dat it was gud dat nit dat hims remmmemmber)
unyway whul mudder was cukin da stuw fudder was duin hims yeerlee grumin.
So whun me come krash'n inta dis world on me head mudder looked down und screem'd "wut's dat?"
fudder said " it Fuzy Toejam" , cuz heed jus got a reel prize of one frum behind hims toes und didnt realize wut mudder
was reely tulk'n about. by da way, fudder saved hims toejam und it on da self if'n uny ob yu's ebber wunt ta seas it.
soes a fuw muns goes by und me gets bigg enuff ta wulks, me goes wulk'n pasts me fudder und him goes "wut's dat?"
and mudder goes "it fuzy toejam deer" und dem bowff confoosed.
