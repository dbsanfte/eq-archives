Zaboxx the Shadow Knight
 
I was only a young one when the Knight of Innoruuk decided to bring me in their order,
seeing as I liked so much to give pain to all the lesser races. They formed me so I could learn
the ways of combat, with any kind of weapon, and to use necromantic magic,
all that knowledge to be able to inflict more pain in the glory of Innoruuk.
I was still young when I started to eradicate the halflings who infest our forest.
Bringing an arsenal of weapon on me and my fidel skeleton pet,
I roam the world in search of new victims to help me gain approval in the eyes of Innoruuk.
I now join this family in hope to gain more power and learn from others
who may have the knowledge to teach me new ways to give pain!
