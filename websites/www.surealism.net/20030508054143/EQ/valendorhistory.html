Valendor the Ranger
 
I was born in the lofty city of Kelethin. I lost my family as a very young elf.
The evil Orcs of Crushbone wiped out an entire caravan, in which we were traveling.
I was the only survivor, by way of hiding in a hollow log, which I landed next to,
as I was thrown off my father's back during the raid.
I have since dedicated my life to ridding my fair lands of the menace that
is Crushbone and it's filthy Orc inhabitants.  However, growing up with no family
has lent to my commitment to helping others in need, in the hopes that they
will learn from my example and help others when they are capable.
