Kilelen Mcbaine the Warrior
 
Growing as an orphen in Halas, I learned to trust in myself.. Although, living my
life alone has tought me humility. I take pity on those who struggle yet they
ask for no help. As a young warrior I found myself in battle after battle, very little
time to rest. Knowing only how to live by my sword I became a champion Gnoll slayer
and ventured to Blackburrow in an attempt to weaken their forces, this in turn has
kindled a frindship with the citizens of Qeynos. As my skills grew with time, I was
charged with the task of slaying the dishonorable Paglan. For this I recieved the
famous sword known as the Langseax of the Wolves. Three days passed since I
came to bare the sword I had sought after, the spirit of a warrior came to me in
the wastelands of Everfrost and spoke, "Kilelen, I've watched you grow into an
honorable warrior, You help those in need and destroy those who live by the
ways of the wicked. For this I bestow upon you a multitude of gifts. These
should help you as you journy through this harsh world in search of yourself
. Someday you will know the role you play in the world around you." With that,
he vanished. I am now a vetran warrior searching for the meaning of
my existance, but never forgeting where I came from.
