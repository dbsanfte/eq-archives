Jeranil the Shaman
 
 
 
This story begins many years ago when i was a small boy, my father had built us a hut outside of Halas.
It was late in the evening and i had just returned from a long day learning how to  wield the spear.
So i slept,  my father and mother had left to gather some food for the morrow when a goblin quietly
found his way into our home. He attacked quickly useing a sword slashed me across the face
leaving the scar you see today. It approached and raised its sword for a final blow that would end my life.
It never came, before the goblin could bring the sword down my father had called forth the
winds of the north and froze the goblin. My mother took the creature outside and finished it off.
The next day a messenger arrived at our house the said the orcs where on the war path and would be
in our area in a few hours, my father packed my things and sent me back to Halas with the messenger where i was
taken into the Shaman guild to keep safe, I was placed in a room with many other children from all over Ever Frost.
I spent days there, and soon parents started retrieving the other children. The days  turned into months
and soon i was the only child left, finally after 5 months of waiting a messenger arrived and told me that my
parents have been assigned to an out post deep within Perma Frost and that i can not venture with them so i must
wait here until thier job there is done. So I waited, another month went by and I became bored and left the room they
had me in, I found rooms that contained old books and began to read them, they taught of  the history of the Barbarian race,
of the seperation of the humans from us and then the erudites  from the humans. I studied all I could and soon found books
containing ancient texts of magic that where kept only for the select few who the Tribunal deemed worthy to learn.
My trips out became long and would some times last from first to light to just before the sun set, i learned all i could and
soon I learned all the books had to offer. Unsatisfied i went to the eldest Shaman and told him of my secret.  The elder
listened to him realizing that I had potential.  So he took me under his wing and tought me the ways of the Shaman..
then telling me to go out and find the answers to the questions in my heart...
