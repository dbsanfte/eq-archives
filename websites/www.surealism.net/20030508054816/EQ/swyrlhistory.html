I come from the North, the cold,  cold north. The lands near
my city are besieged by Ice Giants so I have heard. So, I
shall slay them all. However, it shall take time and skill before I am
mighty enough to do so. Until then? Well, I hope to magically enhance
many people (as my profession is blessed to be able to do) and boldly
hold my own in every battle. If you see me, give me a friendly wave or a
"hail" and I'll see how my magic (or skills) can aid you. That is the
true way of the Shaman, helpful. However, the true way of the barbarian
is to forge on alone against many odds. I shall try hard to avoid that
image. See you all in Norath.
