Rasir the Monk
 
 
 
As early as Rasir can remember, he lived on the streets of Freeport.
 Wandering from spot to spot with his family, his gang.  He was never at peace with crime,
and at age 18 he joined the Ashen Order.  During his training he hit a "wall".
He could not get stronger and this held him back.  As his friends advance well beyond
his level he took to alcohol.  It was then that he learned that with a mixture of meditation
and intoxication, he could perpetuate a semi-dream state which allowed him to take
advantage of his agility.  He formed "Dream Style", and has been excelling alone, ever since.
