Being raised in Ak'anon she grew to love the caves... then to hate them.  It wasnt as much claustrophobia
kicking in, it was more of the strict rule her parents placed her under of never leaving the cave.
It felt like a jail cell to her.  One night, under stealth and a couple of well sought after camo spells, she tip toed
out of the controlling home she grew up in, and with her step dwarven brother, Aammer, they made
their way to see the rest of the world.  Once they hit the ripe age of ten, the decided to
seperate and find profit and journeys on their own.  Her brother hating water, Tilifa loves the liquid...
she spends her free time swimming and fishing, and usually conforting Aianya when they travel on the boat together ~smiles.
 Now she travels and visits her brother often, but her mom and dad less.  She resides for the moment in Kunark
hunting the powerful goblins and tigers in the Lake of Ill Omen.  Being around those Iksar that hang there so much,
she has picked up on a few choice words she uses whenever she gets pissed.  You'll see her around usually running with her trusty
earth pet behind her.  When and if you do though see her running around, ask her about the unfortunate and strange
deaths that plague this poor gnome, and she will weave you a story that will drop your jaws and spill your beer.
