Gladriel the Druid
 
Gladriels mother was a Druid before her and her Father was a Ranger . She was apprenticed
to Surefall Glade at 12 years of age returning home infrequently from that time on.
She is a friend to all she meets and NEVER asks or takes payment for her skills .
Although some find difficulties in racial relations She counts Iskars,
an Ogre as her friends and denies no one if humanly possible even to
going well out of her way to help others. She met joclar on her travels
into the wastes of Everfrost. they have handfasted and have
been blessed by Karana with 4 daughters and a son.
 We look forward to meeting our extended family soon. May peace be at your
backs your roads fair and health walk hand in hand with you all until
we meet  fare you well
