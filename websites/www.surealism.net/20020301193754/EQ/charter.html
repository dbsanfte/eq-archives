Charter
 
What we request and expect no less:
We wish to have members whom have honor as part of their lifestyle, not just their vocabulary.
We believe that everyone has the same rights as another, whether they started today or last year.
We feel that your actions represent the guild as a whole, not just you.  We are seeking to
promote the guild as a strength, a collective union of spirits, working towards the common,
greater cause, promoting a better playing experience for all involved.  We expect all members
to assist each other as needed, for we ourselves would do so, to assist others, for we have honor
which demands as much.  While we do not tolerate, begging (unless its' their skill ~smiles) we
will help show those the way that might become lost on the long journey of travel through this
land called home.
 
Meetings:
 
We wish to a have "formal face to face meeting" at least once a month,
this way we can keep in touch with our "family" and to allow ample opportunity
for all to participate, we will also have an informal meeting once a month.  It will be required
for all involved in the guild to attend at least one of these meetings per month.
The place of meetings will be discussed amongst the group as a whole and find a
suitable place and time where all  may converge together at the same time.
It is imperative to check the message board for this is our lifeline.. our way of
communication, in order for everyone to "hear" what is going on.
 
Raids:
 
We plan on hold raids, which we will pre schedule and let members know of via the msg.
board,  These raids will be exciting and a challenge.  Learning each other's strengths and weaknesses.
While these raids are schedule mainly for guild members, we may allow others to participate on
an individual basis.  Remember we wish to promote, game play and excitement to others so they
to may experience the joys that Norrath has to offer.  However, if you are in a raid, we expect
you to be considerate of your comrades and let them know ahead of time if you need to leave.
Often, we notice that many other guilds are a come as you want and leave other's hanging.  We
wish to be united, serving one another as a family.  Guild raids will be pre approved by the class
elders and guild leaders prior to conducting.  We wish to give other guilds the courtesy of
announcing our raid locations so as to avoid conflict and confusion of pre camp raid areas.
 
Looting:
 
When we are in a group, we are expected to share as a whole.  Split loot is on and any items that
are obtain are given on an as need basis, or rolled for them, whichever is determined by the
leader of the group.  We expect all members to be honorable in this.  We are mature adults and
if their is bickering amongst for whatever may be consider as Phat Loot, then these items will
be held by the guild leader till a drawing can be held.  Everyone will get their chance for
that great loot.  We are expected to help one another out, not take advantage of them.
 
On Tradeskills and Commerce:
 
We feel that those that pursue the skills of trade are a great asset to any guild.  We expect
you to treat your guild members fairly when conducting a sale/buy.  We feel that if you are
selling to a guild member an item you have made then it should be only at cost. If you wish
to sell this item to a non guild member then we don't care what you charge them, just be
honorable in your dealings.
 
Kill Stealing:
 
We absolutely do NOT TOLERATE KS'g in any fashion or form.  We understand that it is a common
pastime for some.  We however, will not stoop to that level.  If we catch this, then you will
face the consequences.
 
Guild Type:
 
Role-playing is highly encourage.  Many members are role-players, whether tabletop, LARP,
internet wherever their origins came from.  Role-playing enhances the world of Norrath, adding
an extra charm to it.  Everyone has it in them.  We will not tolerate those that wish not to
role-play, whom attempts to stifle or ridicule those that do.  This does not mean that we will not allow
 non-role-playing members, for we will. because we know everyone has something to offer.
Variety is the key..imagination is the sweetness.
 
Family Member's Responsibility:
 
When first coming into the family, new members need to check the message board
to find the new members section, which tells you what is requested by the guild leader
in order to set up your member page.  This is a one time deal,  which only needs to be
updated at your discretion.  A valid email is a must..one can be provide if needed.
Another requirement, not hard but a  necessity is to view the message board..regularly.
We use this message board as means of communications for when we are unable to
reach you in the world of Norrath.. there is no exception to this...not to sound unreasonable
but,  you will  come to realize this is our lifeline.
 
Guild Leaders and Class Elders:
 
There are two Guild Leaders.  We consult with one another and have
final say when the needs arise.
 
There are Class Elders voted for and placed by members of that class. Every 3  months, there will
be another election if the members of the class feel it is necessary.  These Class Elders, handle
many of the day to day needs of their class members.  These Elders work hard to help make
life smoother and more enjoyable.   We work together, as a family to help make the guild
a better place..by organization, communication, and dedication.
 
Finally to sum it up....
 
We are a guild that wants to promote role-playing in a positive setting,
while adventuring the world of Norrath, uncovering it's many mysteries.
Allowing both old and new to come aboard, our common dedication of wanting
to assist our fellow inhabitants of Norrath a mutual trait which binds us together.
We believe everyone has honor and respect, even the dark races are known to possess
these strengths.  Both role-players and non-traditional players are encourage to participate.
Where Dark meets Light...But Bound By Neither..
