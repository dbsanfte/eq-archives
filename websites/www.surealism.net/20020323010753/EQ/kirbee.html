                                Kirbee Healmore                     High Elf                       Cleric
 

 
   Age: Unknown
 
 
 
 
 
Born into nobility,  Kirbee is not the snobby High Elf one would expect.
Haunted by a memory from her childhood, she is driven to excel in the art
of healing, so that never again shall she helplessly stand by as a loved
one bleeds to death.
Not all work and no play, Kirbee enjoys to party with her friends. She has
been seen dancing on table tops in the bars in Highpass having had a few
too many short beers. There is a depth to
Kirbee that casual observation will not discern. One must spend time
adventuring with the lady to learn her secrets.
