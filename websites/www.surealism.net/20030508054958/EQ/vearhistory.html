Vear the Monk
 
Raised in the Field of Bone, the young Iksar, Vear, felt very isolated.
Other hatchlings, were much larger than he, and their primative unbridled
agression confused him. For he was the type that enjoyed helping others,
and enjoyed life through the happiness of those around him.
One day he left his nest in Kunark and begun the life of a Monk,
following the example set before him by the Master Iksar Monk, Serynn.
The life of a Monk provided him what he needed.
Now, Vear Swiftfists travels the lands of Norath not for personal glory,
but searching for knowledge and aiding others.
