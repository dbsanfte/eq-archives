Tinithiel the Enchanter
 
: From  a Page discovered in the aqueducts below Qeynos
 
Introduction
 
 
      Little is known of the origins of the one who would call himself
Tinithiel. Whether that was his true name or not is still debated by
scholars. What is known is that he was a master of illusionary forms and the
manipulation of metals and mental behaviours. Even his original race is not
documented but folktales suggest he may have been birthed in Greater Faydark.
Other tales connect him with the District of Qeynos. His rather random nature
and acute shifts in personality have led some to believe he was an exile out
of Neriak but there is little to substantiate this and is widely disregarded.
Most of the lore contained within this volume has been gathered from records
once held private by the defunct order "Shadows of the Ebon Moon" and from
sources gathered at the Library of Erudin. Excerpts in chapter three are
taken from writings of Melonel Dimfiddle lll and various collections of
bard-song.
