Serthi Silverblade
 
At an early age, I was thrown into Everfrost to help my
elders to cope with the ice goblins problem and after a couple
of years, I was already battling bear cubs to get their fur for
protection. At around my 8th season, I was taken as an
apprentice under Master Owwiefixer to learn the fine art of
blacksmithing. At my 13th season, I finally mastered the basics of
blacksmithing and took on the learning of making the banded
armor, that I started to make for my fellow adventurer around
my 15th season.
Then I started to travel the surface of Norrath, selling my wares
around the world. During my trips, I visited every city on the
face of Norrath, exception made of those of the evil race. I also
visited the once fabulous city of Firiona Vie, on the continent
of Kunark, that the elves are trying to bring back to former glory.
It was during my wanderings that I decided to learn some new
skills that were going to help me: fletching to better pull the
beasts to me, tailoring to better prepare myself in equipment and
ingredient for smithing, and finally jewelcraft because the shiny
stones were so beautiful.
I then heard about a new continent, all recovered by ice,
Velious. Feeling nostalgic about my homeland, I decided to go
there to see new wonders. And there I met Ice giants and
dwarves inhabiting the frozen land, battling everyday against
fiercer beast that I had ever seen in my young life; the biggest
bears I have ever seen, wholly rhinos as big as young mammoth
and the baddest creatures I seen there, the Dragons.
After that, seeing that Velious didn't looked like home and still
being homesick, I decided to come back to Halas. It's on my
way there that I met Aianya, a wood elf who was seeking other
adventurers to form a guild, so we could be more efficient when
adventuring, to share our knowledge and help each other when
in need. Her words rung true to my ears and decided to join her
in forming the guild, the Shadows of the Ebon Moon.
Now in my 20th season, I know I still have much to learn. I'm
still looking to master the making of the Fine Plate armor, and
looking to perfect my fletching skill to be able to make the great
bow Trueshot. I'm not sure where I will be wandering next, nor
how to improve my skills, but I know that my fellow guildsman
will help me along the way...
