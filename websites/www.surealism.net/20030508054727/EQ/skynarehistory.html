Skynare the Ranger
 
I am Skynare son of Cyan Bloodbane the Lord of Silvermere!
 Though I have chosen a path laiden in the heart of the woods,
I still carry the ability to bring terror into the hearts of my enemies.
Though I am young, I am eager to learn and try to be a true follower of Tunare!
I have attempted on many occasions to adventure with various folks I have run accross
in my travels but alwasy find the call of the wild solitude too inviting.
When I travel with others I am very dependent, fore I never leave a fellow traveller behind
and would gladly give my life in battle if ever it was needed.
Though the majority of me time is spent alone learning to perfect my abilities.
