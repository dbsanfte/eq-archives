Think before clicking here, it spoils the FUN of doing it YOURSELF!!!!!
I know everyone is going to mob this site the second they notice it, but think before you click. This page will take the fun out of EQ, and I personally don't think you are doing yourself a favor looking on this site as soon as you buy the game. Stories to wet your tounge, those are cool, but in annother section. This spot is not for the new person who has tried little or nothing, but for the confused, hard-tried-and-failed player at the end of their rope! If you fall into that group, then click the above skull.

Of course, if you are well off and just want tips, take it from me: you write your own tips, and others make your to-the-detail maps and instructions. If all you want is annother persons labor so you can move through the game fast, don't bother. 99% of the fun of RPG's like this is doing it yourself, and clicking the skull before you try is exactly what I said: taking the easy way out. You get what you pay for or put in, and stealing unneeded help doesn't take much. In return, you will get a kick out of doing it, until you are dependant on another player's work. And then when something needs to be done, you once again do no work and say "oh yay, I did something that I couldn't do if I played fair." The truth is, it can be done fair, and it's harder, but the difficulty is the most enjoyable part.

But, when difficulty crosses the line of impossibility, this skull is for you.



One more tidbit. Neither CM nor I want to deal with people who just pile on here and whine about there not being the part they need here. CM and I will put up data as we learn it AT OUR OWN PACE IN A PATH WE SEE FIT!!! If niether of us and no one we know is a wizard, I doubt we will have directions to the wizard's guild in Freeport. So don't E-mail us with a complaint on what isn't listed that you need, instead find a site from someone else that has it listed.
