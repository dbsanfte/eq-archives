Greetings Officers and Applicants. This board is setup for the posting of the formal application to the guild at large. The formal application essentually announces to our members that you are requesting membership in our order and that you have discussed this at length with your assigned guild representation. The person(s) assigned to your application will tell you what content you should have in your application.


If you wish to join our order and have not yet been assigned someone to oversee your application and present it to council please see one of the Knights and High Knights listed in the Members list.
