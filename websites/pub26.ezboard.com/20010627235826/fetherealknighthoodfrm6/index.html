Raid and Team Planning

This board is for planning teams for larger scale events. Please try to post at least a week in advance of the date you propose. For events that require multiple teams such as a planes raid or kunark dungeon raid please post two weeks in advance. I would also like to see all multi-team raids proposed in General News first so that we know we have enough guild support to do the raid.
