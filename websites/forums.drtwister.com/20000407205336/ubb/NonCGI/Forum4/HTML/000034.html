A level 13 what? Different classes do well in the Dires at different times.

An Archer can actually do ok in the Dires around level 13...assuming he has a few friends. I took my archer, Stick, the house near Disaster Maze when he was 13 and did great.

A mage will not do well in the Dires until early 20s. Most of the creatures there have high magic resists.

Melee fighters should steer clear until mid to late 20s. Even the Drudge Lurkers are dangerous (level 40) to melee fighters and they are runts compared to most of the critters there.

You can always try out the Dires *somewhat* safely by taking the Mayoi portal to the South Dires. This takes you into a medium difficulty area ...for the Dires...and there is a portal back to Mayoi just a short sprint north.
