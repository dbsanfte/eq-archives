Well, not ghetto, but yet another way to make your own macros.

Most of the gravis controllers come with a keyboard>button macro feature so you can setup custom buttons for any game that uses a keyboard.

I have a gravis Xterminator, all i do is make it run something something over and over. Ill explain how im using it.

What i do is go into the macro making program that comes with the controller. Now, you have to write down or remember what you have on hotkeys in EQ. What you need to do is assign a button on the controller do have it do something on the hotkey list in eq over and over. If you noticed in EQ pressing Shift+1/2/4, etc, it switches the hotkey screens (you have 6). Using just 1,2,3, etc executes the hotkey'd *whatever*. Now what i did was just make a bunch of hotkeyd stuff and had the program do Shift+"X", "X"...shift+x being the screen number, x being the number of the hotkeyd skill/spell. This is hard to explain but really easy. Just have it repeat itself over and over, sucks tho u can have a limited ammount of macro.

I know going out and buying a 40 dollar controller is more then EQ Macros, but its a bit safer and if you have a gravis game pad, might as well do it.
