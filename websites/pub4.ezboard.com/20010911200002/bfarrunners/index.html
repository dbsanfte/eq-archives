The Far Runners Guild of the Rathe no longer exists. For those wanting to read the old forums, they are available at the following:

May 1999 - July 1999
July 1999-January 2000
January 2000 - July 2000
July 2000 - September 2000(Eternal Twilight)
September 2000(Legacy of Myth)

On September 28, 2000 the last of Legacy of Myth voted to merge with Jaded Band. The server split moved half of Jaded Band to the new server and the other half joined Eternal Archons Other guild members still playing can be found in Valiant Knights, Frayed Knot.
Both Far Runners and Legacy of Myth are completely gone now.

Much to my dismay, The Rathe Concillium and Eternal Twilight are slowly gaining old Far Runners. That is their choice, but history is bound to repeat when no one learns from it. Fortunately, not all of the old members are in ET, many have decided to go to EA.

Finally, Many of the original Far Runners have retired from Everquest to play other games and have set up a message board at Clan of Lost Souls

Note from Elaundar: I had made the decision to join Frayed Knot, as a member not as a leader. Despite my close friendships with Alika and Anora, I have since left them. As much as I wanted to just play the game, there were too many hard feelings toward me in the Freaks alliance due mostly from my actions last July, but also in large part due to the lies of Damine.

My upcomming plans are not set. As much as I love playing Everquest, I am afraid my time here is growing to an end. If you want to get in contact with me, drop me a line at elaundar@yahoo.com or give me a tell in game.

P.S. I seriously doubt this board is checked anymore, but either way I have created a new forum - Memories. Its an open forum, feel free to post what you remember about the Far Runners.
