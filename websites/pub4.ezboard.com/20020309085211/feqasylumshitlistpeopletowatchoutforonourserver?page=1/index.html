Welcome to the Lanys.com Shitlist!

#1 - All posts must be made with the subject heading bearing the name of the specific person being shitlisted. Posts must be done by players actually playing on Lanys T`Vyl, by a registered EZboard account.

#2 - No posts may be made anonymously. Your characters name in game must be made known if you are to put another character's name on the shitlist.

#3 - MOST posts are given a trial lifespan of 7 days. During this time, players may add other similar things happening with the shitlisted player, defend them, etc. However, the lifespan of a post is not guaranteed. Posts such as "JoeDr00d didn't port me to EC" will be deleted on sight, as will any posts that are deemed by the moderators to be unworthy of even a trial.

#4 - During this 7 day period, if the story is still being uncovered or added to, the post will be allowed to stay until a relative period of stasis. At the end of this period, it will be determined by the moderators whether or not this person is going to be deleted or remain on list.

#5 - Shitlisted Players that are deemed worthy of their placement here will be kept on the shitlist permanently, accessible from the main page of lanys.com as well as the forum itself. To be deemed worthy means that there has been enough testimony and evidence brought forth to make a substantial case. In the world of EQ, 100% proof is nearly impossible to come by, but common sense and good judgement are more than enough.

#6 - Shitlist posts that are deemed insufficient based on their merits will be locked and taken down, with an explanation given by the moderator. In some cases the explanation may be left open so players can respond, but in many, the case is closed when the moderator renders it so.

We'd like to stress the severity of being posted here. A post here means that the said player should be avoided at all costs, as they have proven themselves to be dishonorable. Please make every attempt to resolve the issue with the player's guild, the player's friends, the player, etc., before posting here. Please treat this as a last resort.

Also please realize that situations or cases arise where the moderators have to make a judgement call. In these cases, the decisions of the moderator are final, but must be made unanimously by the moderators.

Thanks.
