What it takes to be a BAD Clan member

Bad   Principles and Objectives
Bad members can only belong to one clan - BAD
We will only allow members who will wear the clan label with pride and who demonstate that they have the same objectives and ideals that the Clan has
In this respect Bad members are encouraged to wear their Bad title at all times
We only want the very best players possible and therefore they will likely be invited to become members as opposed to unknown players applying and passing the selection criteria
Besides playing skills we of course also only want members who fit in with the rest of the clan and who support and  promote the existing comradeship and fun environment that we have established and enjoy so much
We only want members that play regularly and tell the other members when they will be absent. There is no penalty for absence - there is though for unannounced absence over a lengthy period
Our current membership is comprised of mature people, mostly over the age of twenty and  it is therfore likely that new members will be from the same age group.  Although age is not the criteria - maturity is.
We do not want immature childish people of the type so frequently seen on the zone.  In fact a key criteria is that our members do not engage in forum flooding, flaming or other unprofessional posting. That is beneath a BAD
We play AOE and ROR and hopefully AOK when it is released
Our Objective is to be one of the smaller, tighter, exclusive, and hopefully therefore superior clans
While there is no maximum membership defined, it is intended that the Clan will remain small due to the high qualifications for membership and the desire of the existing members to keep our group tight and compact
Our Motto is "all for one and one for all". The worst offence that can be committed is to not support another member against non-members or to bad-mouth members or the Clan outside
We will settle all differences of opinion between members or any requirements for Clan discipline in the private area of our website and we will at all times present to the rest of the DM world an appearance of complete harmony and accord within the Clan
We will all be equals with a full voice in any matter and all clan business will be decided by all members voting. There will be no titles or bureaucracy in the clan with the one exception that: as the permanent titular leader of the clan Bad_Smell will have an extra vote only in the event of deciding voting deadlocks. Other than that exception, no member will have any benefits or advantages over any other member

Attaining membership

If these ideals and conditions seem draconian, dictatorial or are unacceptible to you then you obviously don't share the stated objectives of the BAD Clan and you are therefore not suitable to be a member
If you feel that our ideals and objectives fit yours and that you have the requisite skills, then apply to any Bad member with your membership request or post on our public forum

A majority vote is required, after full vetting for agreement to and passing these criteria,  to become a probationary member
You must be prepared for this first part of the process to take as long as four to six weeks to enable current members to play with you and assess your capabilities and personality.  Please do not expect this to be rushed or omitted and be prepared to have your application declined
Rejection of your application should not be considered in any way a personal insult but just a lack of fit from the existing members perception
Once you have attained the probationary vote there is then a 30 day period of probation followed by a 100% unanimous vote to become a full permanent member. The probationary period will allow the applicant the opportunity to demonstrate his ability to conform to the BAD ideals
Only after attaining this final vote  will you be entitled to don, and proudly wear your new BAD_title and at which time you are a full voting member of the clan with equal status in the running of the clan and in the selection of new members
GOOD LUCK!
