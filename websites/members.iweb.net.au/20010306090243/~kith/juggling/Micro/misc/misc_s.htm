Miscellaneous Manipulation

Introduction

This page describes random small object manipulation tricks. There are currently tricks with cards, rubber bands and a few other tricks.

Card Manipulation

By card manipulation I mean the manipulation of just one card like object (playing card, credit card, buisness card etc). If you are interested in more conventional card manpulation (ie manipulation of a standard deck of cards) there are many good books on the subject.

Many of the pencil and coin tricks can be performed with a card. Some of these include Behind The Hand Twirl, Finger 1 Spin, Two Finger Twirl and the Two Finger Switch among others.

Card Spin
Just spin the card in the same manner as the Coin Spin. The card can be spun while holding the edges of the card or the corners.


Extra Spin
If the card is long enough you can do an extra spin with finger 3.

Note: Throughout this move the card is spun about its short axis.

Start Start with the card held by one of the long edges, near a corner.
Description
  1. Spin the card 180 degrees as normal. The card is now held with the thumb on one side and finger 1 and 2 on the other side.
  2. Finger 3 now grabs the card on the same side as the thumb, and finger 1 and the thumb release the card.
  3. Continue the spin with fingers 2 and 3, until finger 1 can grab the card on the same side as finger 3.
  4. Finger 3 releases the card and you can return to the starting position from here. You will now be holding the other end of the card.


Card Throwing
There are many ways of throwing cards. They can be thrown long distances, with great force, or so that they return to your hand like a boomerang. I won't describe any of these methods beacuse I don't do them much, and they are described better in many good books on the subject. These books are typically found in magic shops, but you might also find one in a juggling shop.



Horizontal Spin, Continuous
This trick involves keeping the card spinning horizontally with the thumb and first three fingers of one hand.
Start Hold a card between the thumb and fingers 1,2 and 3 of one hand. The thumb should be placed at the center of one side of the card, while the fingers are placed on the other side of the card.
Description Now spin the card clockwise (Assuming the thumb is on top) by alternately pulling backwards on the card with finger 1 and pushing forwards with finger 3. Finger 2 remains mostly stationery during this trick.

The thumb moves right and left in time with the above two moves.



Horizontal Spin, Quick
This trick involves making a card spin horizontally a few times with only a small motion of the hand.
Start Start as for the previous method.
Description With a quick motion simultaneously push forwards with finger 3 and pull backwards with finger2 to generate spin on the card. The grip on the top of the card should be reduced so that the card can spin freely. The goal is to set the card spinning with a minimum amount of movement with the hand, then keep the hand still until the spin stops. I can get two rotations without any noticable movement of the hand. It helps if the card you are spinning has a slippery surface.


Long Axis Spin
Start Hold the card with the thumb on the middle of one side of the card, and all four fingers on the other side of the card.
Description
  1. Fingers 2 and 3 reach around to grab the card on the same side as the thumb.
  2. Release the card with the thumb and place it on the other side of the card. The card is now held with fingers 2 and 3 on one side and fingers 1, 4 and the thumb on the other.
  3. Release the grip with fingers 1 and 4 and place them on the other side of the card. You should now be back at the starting position.
Comments I find that I hold the card the the first knuckles of fingers 1 and 4.



Propeller Spin
This is a fun trick I used to do as a kid.
Start Take a small square piece of paper and fold a small section of each edge inwards. Place the middle of the paper against the index finger so that the folded edges are facing outwards as shown in the following ascii diagram:

  -|
   |======  <-Index finger
  -|
  ^
  Folded edges
Description Now run forwards, pushing against the paper with the index finger. If done right the paper will spin on the end of the finger like a propellor.


Short Axis Spin
Start Hold the card edgewise with the inside edges of fingers 1 and 4 at the midpoint of the longest edges.
Description Now rotate the card through 360 degrees by alternating pushing with the thumb and pulling with fingers 2 and 3.


Rubber Band Manipulation

There are a few manipulation tricks that I know that can be done with rubber bands.

Finger Walk
Start Stretch a rubber band between finger 4 and the thumb.
Description Now insert finger 3 into the loop and remove finger 4. Continue with finger 2 and finger 1. Now reverse the moves to complete the walk.


Flicking
Not really a manipulation trick but some cool ways to flick a rubber band. Use these powers in self defence only!
Description

Gun Flick

Hold out the a fist. Extend finger 1 and the thumb to make a "gun". Place one end of a rubber band over the tip of finger 1. Stretch it behind the thumb and down into the palm where it is held by finger 2. Point this "gun" at something and release the band with finger 2. The band should fly of the hand towards the target.

Two Finger Flick

Stretch a rubber band between the thumb and finger 2. Point finger 2 at something and release the grip with the thumb so that the band flies towards the target.

Now perform this flick so that the band flies straight up. As it falls down catch it on an extended finger.



Rotating Walk
This is a more interesting version of the Finger Walk.
Start Extend both index fingers, point them towards each other and place the right index finger over the left one. Stretch a rubber band between these fingers.
Description
  1. Rotate the fingers around each other through 360 degrees.
  2. Replace the left finger 1 with finger 2. Repeat Step 1.
  3. Replace finger 2 with finger 3. Repeat Step 1.
  4. Replace finger 3 with finger 4. Repeat Step 1.
  5. One more rotation and you can begin reversing the steps to get back to the start.
Comments This feels pretty cool when done really fast.



Rotating Walk - Two Hands
The "walk" can be performed with either hand and an interesting variation is to perform the walk on both hands at once by starting as above and performing the following steps:

Start Start as for the Rotating Walk.
Description
  1. Perform 180 degrees rotation while replacing left finger 1 with left finger 2.
  2. Perform 180 degrees rotation while replacing right finger 1 with right finger 2.
  3. Continue steps 1 and 2 replacing alternating left and right fingers on each 180 degree rotation until you get back to the start.


Thumb Spin
Start Hold the hand out with the thumb pointing up and a rubber band stretched between the thumb and finger 2. Make sure the band is on the very tip of finger 2 and below the knuckle of the thumb.
Description Now push on both strands of the band with finger 1 to form a triangle. Now release the band with finger 2 and if done correctly the rubber band should be spinning rapidly around the thumb.


Twists

These tricks involve twisting a rubber band with the fingers in various ways.

Index Twist
This is a simple move in which a rubber band is twisted with the index finger, then the move is immediately reversed.
Start Extend a rubber band between the thumb and fingers 2 and 3 so that a triangle is formed.
Description
  1. Insert finger 1 into the loop from the far side and remove the thumb. The band should now be stretched between fingers 1, 2 and three, but there is a twist between finger 1 and the other two fingers. Try to keep the same triangle shape.
  2. Insert the thumb into the loop of the band near fingers 2 and 3 and remove finger 1 to return to the starting position.


Thumb Twist
This is a simple move in which the band is twisted with the fingers and thumb.
Start Extend a rubber band between the thumb and finger 3.
Description
  1. Insert finger 2 into the band from the far side and remove the thumb. The band should now be extended between finger 2 and finger 3 and have one twist in it.
  2. Insert the thumb into the top loop of the band (near finger 2) and remove finger 2.
  3. Insert finger 1 into the top loop of the band from the far side and remove the thumb. The band is now extended over finger 3 and finger 1 and has two twists.
  4. Insert the thumb into the bottom loop (near finger 3) and remove finger 1 to remove the twists and return to the start.


Misc

Random stuff that does not fit anywhere else.

Key Ring Flip
This is a pretty simple move that requires a set of keys on a key ring.
Start Extend finger 1 and place the key ring on it, dangling the keys below.
Description Now flip the keys over finger 1 to land caught on top of the other three fingers. Release the keys with these fingers to return ot the start position. Repeat until you get to the lock you need the keys for ;-)

The reverse flip can also be done if the first was too easy!



Slinky
A slinky is one of those spring things that can walk down stairs on its momentum. There are two tricks that can be done with one (apart from the stair thing).

Hand Walk:
Hold out the left, palm up, with the fingers pointing right. Place the slinky on the left hand and use the right hand to grab the top of the slinky and pull it down onto the right hand (which is placed slightly lower than the left. The slinky should unfold out onto the right hand and continue turning over where it can be caught on the left hand and so on.

Wave:
Stretch the slinky out on a flat surface held between two hands. With the right hand quickly push to the left so that a wave is formed, which will slide through the slinky towards the left hand. As it reaches the left hand perform a similiar move to reverse the waves direction.



Ring Spin
This trick involves spinning a ring around a finger.
Start For this trick you need a ring that has a knob of some kind (ie a Huge Diamond!). I'll describe the trick assuming the ring is on finger 2 of the right hand and the knob is facing up.
Description
  1. Place the thumb on the underside of the ring and pull it to the left so that the know move towards finger 3.
  2. Bring finger 3 up on top of the knob and pull the knob underneath the hand.
  3. Use the thumb to rotate the knob around to the left side of finger 2 and back to the starting position.
