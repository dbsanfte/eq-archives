This is the level 50 quest for the level 49 spell Call of Flame. It is 206 direct damage flame based spell for 125 mana with a 2.0 second casting time. I've heard the recovery time being 10 seconds and 18 seconds. Hopefully I can test it myself soon and get verified stats. I found these steps on The Ranger's Glade website. I'll be verifying these steps soon I hope.

1) Find lily and Devin Ashwood in Kelethin

2) Hail lily

3) Say “I am a ranger” to lily

4) Say “I want to learn from him, how do I prove myself as a forest?” to Devin

5) Say “I will take letter ” to lily

6) Give letter to Ganelorn in east Karana

7) Get snake egg from Ganelorn take to Ranger Master in Kithcor

8) Get icon from Ranger Master and return it to Ganelorn in east Karana

9) Now Ganelorn requires 3 things for his student and deliver “a note of Credit” to a merchant.

A) Burn sash = Dropped by imp protectors in Sol b

B) Ademantine band = adamantite band is the rare drop from the hand in lower guk

C) Turn in “note of credit” to Aanina Rockfinder in KaladimB

D) Electrum bladed Wakizashi

    i. Butler fangs = dropped by butler in Mistmoore

    ii. Maid fangs = dropped by maid in Mistmoore

    iii. Turn in fangs to catliff in Mistmoore for Electrum bladed wakizasni.

10) Turn in all 4 items to Ganelorn in east Karana

11) This will spawn a high level poacher near by named Vance Bearstalker

12) Kill Vance and take his head

13) Give head to Ganlorn for spell.
