Thank you for considering membership in DOA. Please provide us with a little information about your character. We will review your application at our next meeting and email you a prompt response. Please note, you really should have been sent here by a current DOA member. Their recomendation at the meeting is usually needed for an application to be fully considered.

Currently new prospects go through several steps to become members of DOA

1. The prospect is sent to our web page to fill out an application

2. The application is discussed at our next meeting

3. The prospect is invited to a meeting to be introduced to current members

4. The prospect is temporarily tagged as a guild member to make it easier for the members at large to get aquainted

5. A final vote is taken on the new member. Any No vote is an automatic turn down on membership. This may seem a bit harsh, but we're a family and we trust each other. We fully expect any DOA member to be DOA forever. A No vote is not slight toward the prospect at all. We're an odd group and a No vote could be a compliment <g>.
