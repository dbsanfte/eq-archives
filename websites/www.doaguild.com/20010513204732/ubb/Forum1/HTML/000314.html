Time: 3pm EST
Mob: Eejag, lvl 55, max hits for 157, lives in lava that does 25 dmg/tic
Min Groups needed: 2, I want 3
Neccessary Classes: Melee (pure,hybrid need lots), Clerics (1 per group), Necro (DMF), Druid (regen)
Loot: Charred Scale for MONK epic (somebody loot if I'm dead and can't be rezzed)
Strat: I will get Eejag to spawn by issueing a challenge. All melee get hp buffed, DMF, and regen and move to me above the middle flume of the lava lake. Eejag is located halfway down the lake. Clerics med above lava and people need to surface for a heal. Cleric can also charge the lava, find the group, and cast group heals to prevent us from dieing to lava.

Place: Second Island Plane of Air
Time: Port up right after Lavastorm
Mob: Gwan, lv 57, has 14k hp, hits for 165, proc Fist of Air (knockback), enrages, 100% MR (melee range lure possible)
Min Groups needed: 2, I want 3(have 4 port stones)
Necessary Classes: Melee (pure/hybrid/pet need lots), Clerics (1 per group), Wizards (hopefully 1 per group, traveller)
Rules: NO AoE, NO Dueling, use /OOC in zone. Failure to follow rules can agro quest masters, dojourn, blademasters, etc.

Strat: We port up, no need to be buffed. We kill the fairies of island one and loot the swords. We kill the boss mob of island one and hand in swords to the Lunatic for keys to Dojorn isle. We port to Dojorn isle and enter windmill. I'll activate mobs when all have mana/buffed, and pull into the windmill. Dojorn and Bladestorms will not assis. We should never be fighting more than one mob unless somebody casts AoE.

FIGHT #3
Place: Mines of Nurga within Frontier Mountains
Time: After Plane of Air, we need to be ported to Dreadlands to run over to Frontier Mountains to find the Mines of Nurga
Mob: Trunt, lvl 59, double hits for 176, 19k hp, 100% MR
Min Groups needed: 2, I want 3
Neccessary Classes: Melee (of all kind and lots of them), Clerics (one per group), Enchanter (needed to charm ogre for activation, can be bypassed by monk sneak)
Loot: Trunt's Head needed for MONK epic (somebody with sneak loot if I'm dead and can't be rezzed)
Strat: Go to the mines, find the dumb ogre, wake the dumb ogre, kill the dumb ogre.

Place: Lake of Ill Omen at approx neg 500, neg 1000 on the beach overlooking the lake and ruins.
Time: After the Trunt fight I will need to hand in the head at the Overthere. This should only cause minimal dely. Good time for casters to bind near Ill Omen, and for everybody to call in reinforcements from friends . If fights 1-3 have taken too long, maybe this fight will be saved for another day, but I'd like to push to finish it.
