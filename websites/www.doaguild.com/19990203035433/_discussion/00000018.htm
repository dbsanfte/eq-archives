In honor of your website I have composed the DOA Creed:

If those committed to the quest fail, they will be forgiven. When lost, they will find another way. The moral imperative of the quest is the endeavor, whether successful or not, provided the effort is honorable and failure memorable. For those not dedicated to the Kingdom, forever mourn the loss of their souls and the suffering of banal existence.
