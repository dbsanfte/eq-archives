Perhaps I am speaking out of place, since I am relatively new to DOA, but I think we need to concern ourselves with our guild growing too quickly. I feel it is important to have strong bonds with your brothers, willing to risk life and gear to help in a crisis. You MUST learn to trust your guild mates, especially your healers. And these two things are so much easier obtained if you a have a strong, small, tight knit group. At Thursday meeting Kvasir said. "Damn, I can't keep up with everyone anymore!" I understand the reason for class leaders, and it was a great idea, but I feel it doesnt make it acceptable to get too big. If we have a 15-18 person guild with REGULAR active members we have plenty to have a good mix of classes. We have plenty to do some of the more difficult lairs. With each additional member you add, i sorta feel you take away the importance of every current standing member. And if you feel less important, you aren't going to put 100% into the guild, like MOY or SRD, who was recruiting anyone and everyone of the street. This is nothing personal against the newer members of DOA. Just some opinions from a pally :) Flagg___DOA
