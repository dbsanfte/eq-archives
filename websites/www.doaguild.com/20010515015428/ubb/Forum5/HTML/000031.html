Since Verrant seems to break the sound every other patch, go to start->settings->control panel->system->Hardware profile (I think, it'll show u the list of goodies in your computer). Then select your sound card, right click and pick properties, and tell it to disable in this profile. Now see if freezes/lockups have stoped. If so you know it's sound related and the best bet is new sound drivers and direct X 7.0a. I know of several folks theat installed Direct X 8.0 and had problems so severe, we had to reformat to get rid of it (no handy uninstall for direct x 8).

Another tip on Direct x, always find the Direct X home page on Microsofts site and download the full version. The updates are often missing important files that can cause you alot of grief. The full version will always have everything.
