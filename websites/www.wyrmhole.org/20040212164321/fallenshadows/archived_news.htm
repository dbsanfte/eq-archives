Moving on up!

[7/17/03]

Fallen Shadows has been VERY busy over the last month.  Lots of new mobs, crazy new loot.  It's been a blast.

It started with a trek out to Western Waste.  We were going to scout out some mobs when we noticed Sontalak was up.  With some of us being KoS to dragons now, we decided we had to clear him for safety reasons.  A quick call to arms and with in moments we had thirty people in the zone.   We prepared, then charged.  It was a tense moment at first, as we lost a couple people quickly at the beginning of the fight, but slowly the tide turned and we emerged victorious!  One tier four mob down !

Next, we set our sights on our first God, Cazic Thule.  Noticing he was up, but no one was in fear, we took the opportunity to get some revenge and have some fun.   After a quick clearing we set up camp outside his temple.  Then prepped and brought him in for some old school fun.  It was great fun, only losing a couple people.  In the end , the battle gecko of Petoonia got the final shot and the giant god came tumbling down.  That's two tier four mobs!

Our next big target was The Itraer Vius.  Last time we visited him, he gave us some hard times and we had to leave, well this time we kicked his ass royally.   A super fast run to his lair and an incredibly fun fight, we slowly worked his health down to nothing.  Score another tier four mob!

That's three tier four mobs. Not bad in a month!  But that's not all we have been doing.  We have killed Vindi a couple times.  More of the plane of innovation dragon Xanomech.  Killed Sontalak again, done Doomshade/Rumblecrush.   

We also had a number of new recruits (more then 10)  in the last month that have been a great addition to the guild.  People have been leveling like mad and gaining a ton of AA points.

Coming up, some more tier 4 mobs!  King tormax, Greig Venificus, maybe even into Ssra temple for some mobs.  Also we are just waiting for our turn to get into North Temple Of Veeshan which I have no doubt we can do easily!

Congrats Shadows, you have made the leadership proud, it was only this year Jan 31, 2003 that we got on our own first rotation with Severilous. In seven short months we have gone from having 25 people on raids to having 50  From racing for Gorenair and Talendor, to racing for Sontalak and The Itraer Vius.   In the upcoming month, we will be trying to enter the North Temple of Veeshan.   All while maintaining a great atmosphere of fun, willingness to help friends, and an amazing online family.  Thanks


-Micherick
