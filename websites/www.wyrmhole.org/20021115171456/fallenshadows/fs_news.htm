City of Mist

[11/12/02]

On a spur of the moment call to arms from Elenrak, a number of Shadows appeared in the God forsaken Swamp of No Hope to clean up a mess by the name of Kaggry Krup. This slimy beast carries a portion of the Shaman Cudgel quest and was washed down, then washed up by our Elite team.

After that we decided to go to City of Mist to attempt yet again for the ULTRA RARE (for Hutterr anyway) drop of a Jade Reaver. After respawns and deaths, we killed approximately 22 Black reavers and the 21st finally dropped the Jade Reaver.

After the reavers were taken care of, we decided to make a run at Neh' Ashir. He/She (there is still controversy) is a chanter with the unique ability to CH, but after a long fight went down in like fasion to Krup and the reavers.

Grats to all who attended, and thank you as well. It was great fun and puts a couple of us that much closer to epic status.
