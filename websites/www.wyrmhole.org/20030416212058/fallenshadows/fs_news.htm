Umbral Instanity !

[1/06/03]

What started out as another attempt at taking out Gorenaire last night ended up being one of the most fantastic nights we've had as Fallen Shadows.

We started mobilizing for Gorenaire when we saw 40+ members of another guild already in place to take her out. A quick check in umbral showed that Rumblecrush was roaming around, so we headed up there to see what we could do.
