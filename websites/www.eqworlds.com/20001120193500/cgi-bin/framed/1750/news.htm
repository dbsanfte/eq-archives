What the hell was that we thought. Out they come followed closely by the Goblin King himself! It turned out a Shadow Knight was invis at the Kings door and KILLed the Goblin king while we cleared the guards. ack! Not cool! 25 September 2000

As mentioned in an earlier rumor, there will indeed be two new servers in the near future.

Tholuxe Paells will be a server for new characters only (no moves) and will be brought up following the patch this Wednesday, September 27th.

Drinal will be a split server, initially accepting characters from Mithaniel Marr and Tunare.

One final note (from the latest patch): There is a potential problem with the /movelog command when issued by hundreds of people at one time. The Everquest Team highly recommends that you wait at least 15 minutes after registration opens prior to using the command. If you have any concerns that your command may not have gone through, you can zone and repeat the command if you wish.
