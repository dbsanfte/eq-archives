The Pros And Cons Of a Race
It can be sometimes hard to pick what race you are going to be when starting a new character. Heres a chart to help you out.

Race
Pros
Cons
Barbarian Very High Stats, Easy travel to newbie zone in Halas, Easy Travel to dungeon Not the greatest Starting City, Items more expensive
Dark Elf Good magic capabilities, Good stat advantage, Ultravision Very hard to leave continent, Items more expensive, Evil race, many cities will kill you on sight
Dwarf Good starting city, Easy travel to near by cities, Items less expensive, Allowed in all cities, Infravision Short, Newbie zones can be harsh, Hated by most evil races
Erudite Very good magic capabilities, Fast level up, VERY high intelligence Extremly low strength, Low stats, Cannot carry/hold to many veavy items
Gnome High intelligence, Allowed in all cities Low strength, Short
Half Elf Many starting cities, Easy level ups, Infravision Items more expensive in most cities
Halfling High Agility and Dex, Allowed in all cities, Items less expensive Not many class's to pick from, Short
High elf High Intelligence and wisdom, Good magic capabilities, Infravision, Infravision Low strength
Human Very large class selection, Very balanced class Low stats in some cases, Newbie zones overcrowded, Most other class's have better stats
Ogre VERY high stats, Makes a great shaman Easy to get lost, Low int, Evil race, Can't go to many cities, Killed on sight by guards in most cities
Troll Double life regeneration (when sitting life goes up 4 at a time not 2), High stats, Makes great shaman Easy to get lost, Low int, Evil race, Can't go to many cities, Killed on sight by guards in most cities
Wood Elf High Agility and Dex Low stats, Not much different from half elf
