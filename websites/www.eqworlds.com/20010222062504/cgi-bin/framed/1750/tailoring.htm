shields 5 Easy Steps To Tailoring

#1: Gain levels and use your attribute skills mostly on tailoring
#2: Buy a Sewing Kit It Will be around 7-9 gold
#3: Buy Patterns at leather store
#4: Kill animals, get pelts use chart below for what type of pelt required
#5: Right click on Sewing Kit.
Place Pelt and Pattern in.
Now click "Combine" Once
