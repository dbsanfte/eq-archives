Dwarves are strong short beings much like humans but more soupierer in strength and dexterity. Due to the underground environment they live in they have the ability of infravision allowing them to see in dark conditions. They always know were there going, they begin in extreme high sense heading allowing them to find home whenever they are lost. Plus dwarves are known for their high running speeds.
