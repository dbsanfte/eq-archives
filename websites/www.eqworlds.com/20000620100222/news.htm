How do Everquest OGGs Stack Up To Other Games?

The online gaming world is vast and teeming with life.  In this world
you have solo players and you have guilds of players.  But there is a
3rd category of group that is very very rare yet very visible and
active.  That group is know as the OGG's (Online Gaming Groups).
Everquest has some like The Syndicate.  Other types of games do as
well.  OGG's are a special kind of entity going beyond what a normal
guild offers.  How do Everquest's OGG's stack up against those from
other game worlds?  What makes an OGG different from a regular guild?
What does an OGG offer to the gaming world its in? Well Gamespot has
released the following article discussing what is an OGG, sharing the
most prevalent ones and  then discussing how they stack up.  Check it
out and see for yourself: 

http://www.gamesdomain.com/gdr.cgi?zones/adventure/apr.2.00.html
