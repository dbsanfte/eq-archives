Introduction



1.1 Objective

This FAQ was created with the intention to answer frequently Asked Questions regarding Ultima: Ascension, which is currently in development.

Hopefully, this document will also serve to dispel the many rumors that have been circulating in the Ultima and CRPG communities.

This FAQ was suggested by Brian Martin, former Associate Producer of Ascension, who has provided much of the information you will find here.

1.2 Disclaimer

Please keep in mind that Ascension is still in development and, as such, nothing about it is set in stone. The game design could possibly change by the time it is released.

Unless stated, all information presented in this FAQ has been confirmed by Origin or from an Origin-sanctioned source.
