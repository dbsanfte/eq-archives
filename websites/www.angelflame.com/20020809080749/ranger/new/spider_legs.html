Spider Legs (Ring of the Chameleon Quest)

Starting NPC: Sentry Kale
Starting Zone: Skyshrine
Minimum level: 45

Sentry Kale is looking for someone to perform a task for him. Ask him what task and he will explain that the spiders have been eating the food supplies.

You say, 'Hail, Sentry Kale'
Sentry Kale says 'Why come you to the home of the kin? Seek a home amongst the kin perhaps? Perhaps if you wish to prove yourself I may have a task for you.
You say, 'i wish to prove myself'
Sentry Kale says 'Very well, it is a minor task, but I am sure you are quite capable. Lately the food supply in the guards' galley has been dwindling. I fear that there are spiders amongst us who are pilfering our food when we do not see. I would ask of you to please seek out and eliminate these vermin for us. Bring their legs back to me and I shall reward you for your effort.

Go out and kill the four crystal spiders that spawn in the zone and bring back their legs as proof. 2 are near the bank, One spider is in the fountain. (You need to pull the spider to the corner of the pool or else the guardians in there will heal him again and again), and 1 is in a bedroom. They are on 2 hour spawn timers.

Most of the time you get some faction and coin, but sometimes he will give you a Chameleon ring.

**There is apparently 3 versions of Sentry Kale. I have yet to do this or any of these quests, so I am unsure which one you will want. I understand the different versions of Kale give out 3 separate quests so be sure the Kale you are giving your hard earned items to, is the one you want.
