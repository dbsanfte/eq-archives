Misty Thicket/Rivervale - How to get there quickly & Safely from Kelethin

 

To get to Misty Thicket, You will need to run to the Port in Butcher Block and board the Maidens Voyage Boat. Best bet is to try to get a SoW before starting off. To get to the port, head South West in Greater Faydark, the entrance to Butcher Block is almost on the South West corner of the Greater Fay Zone. Once you zone into BB, Follow the path. You will pass a hut with an anvil outside, the path will fork here, take a right at that fork. You will know you are heading the right way if you pass the city of Kaladim. Keep following the path to the Docks. Once on the Boat, you will need to take the second stop. You'll know its your stop because you will have just Zoned into Freeport.

Once in Freeport, Try to get a SoW again at the West Freeport gate, as you will need to run through 3 zones to get to Rivervale. You will also want to get bound here just incase you should die. That way if you do die, you only start back in Freeport, and not in Kelethin and have to do the boat ride over. Once you get a Sow and Bind, Head West. You will zone into East Commonlands. Keep running, if you get attacked, don't stop or turn around, keep running. you will then Zone into West Commonlands. Again keep running, don't stop and again if you get attacked, keep running.

The next zone is a very nasty one, Kithacor Forest. The zone line for Kithacor is at the North West corner of the West Commons zone. Once you get to it, stop. DO NOT zone in yet. Instead do a /time and see what time it is in-game. Never never enter Kithacor after 8:00 PM Game time. The things that spawn in there at night are levels 40+ and will kill you in one hit. If its between the hours of 8:00 PM and 7:00 AM Game Time, have a seat, relax and wait for the morning. All the nasty critters despawn about 7:00 AM Game Time. Best time to head into Kithacor is after 8:00 AM. Once it is a safe time to enter Kithacor, zone in and start running. You will want to stay on the zone wall on your right. Don't go to far up the zone wall or you will miss the entrance to Rivervale. Its not a long run, about 4 - 5 minutes tops, and if you still have SoW on, it will be an even shorter run. Again if anything attacks ya, don't stop keep running and look for the entrance.

Once you are safely in Rivervale, try to get bound there. (incase ya die) most players that can bind will be more then happy to bind a "Young" player. The bank is right behind the PoD. And the zone to Misty Thicket is basically right in front of the bank through that tunnel. There are guards in the area to help keep you safe. from levels 1 - 5 stay on the front side of the wall closest to the zone to Rivervale. Once you hit 5ish, head through the wall to the other side of Misty thicket that has higher level critters there.

There are also guards at the arch in the wall to help protect you as you level up. Keep in mind though, that these and all guards are kill stealers! So try to fight critters a bit away from the guards. You can easily get to 12th level in this zone. plus there are vendors that you can sell to all over the place, a bank near by, and guards to help keep you safe. Bears and wolves also wont attack you on site unless you are all ready fighting one. (I loved that about my Ranger).
