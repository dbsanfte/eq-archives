  June 19th, 2003

First off, a few notes... as most of you have figured out, It has been over 8 months for me since I have logged into EQ. Last week, I started the patcher, and downloaded all the new files & updates, then logged in. I of course, was welcomed by my family of Eternal Advocates, and it made me realize just how much I missed them all, and EQ in general.

So now, I have started up again. I have been trying to catch up on my flagging & levels, as well as get some of my gear upgraded. I logged off 8 months ago in some pretty top end and nice gear, and now I am so far behind! Wow how times change.

The Ranger's Resource ~ While this site was a passion of mine, and making it bigger and better was always my ultimate goal, I now just lack the time to dedicate to it any more. When I first created the site, I had a lot of spare time at my job to work on it. Yes I was lucky back then, I had a job that paid pretty decent money and that allowed me to play on the internet all day, and work on my html skills. Well that has all changed, and this site has been put on hold due to it.

The Ranger's Resource is not going anywhere, all be it, it may not get updated any more passed this one exception, but I will leave it up, as is, with all the work I have put into it over the two and a half years for all to use and enjoy.

I have updated the email forms, and am receiving email once again from them, so if you have any comments, etc. please feel free to send them.  =)

My love of my Family in EA is what has brought me back, and all the good times we share together. I wanted to say Thank You, Eternal Advocates, You guys are what make EQ so enjoyable!

Well that's really about it... well except for this shameless plug:

My siggy image, courtesy of EQ Pixel Army! Great little site that allows you to create you own custom siggy images of your characters! The people that created this site put a lot of hard work into it, and I think they did an absolute wonderful job! Way to go guys!

Perhaps one day I will find the time to devote to this site, and the Ranger Class again. But for now, you can always find me in-game on the Vazaelle server.

/Hugs to all!
