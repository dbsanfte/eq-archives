Angel's Journal through her Epic Quest

 

 Greetings and welcome! I recently started Rainangel's Epic Quest, and thought I would journal my progress through the quest in hopes it may one day help other Rangers with their Epic...

I started my Epic Quest on August 1st 2001

Below you will find my progress, some Screenshots and other information as I work my Epic Quest. Please keep in mind as I continue on my Epic, this page is going to get really long and may take some time to load... Please Email me if you have any suggestions for me, comments or anything! I'd love to hear from you!

~Rainangel

I started researching the Ranger Epic a long time ago, reading what I could on message forums everywhere and anywhere I could find, on other Ranger Guides, Everlore, Castersrealm... you name it I have been there. I have for a very long time looked so very forward to wielding my two epic weapons. Soon I will recognize that dream (or so I hope)...

Updated 11/13/2001 ~ I continued working my Epic Quest last night, with the help of my Guild Epic Heroes. Unfortunately, it did not go exactly as planned... please look below for all the details...

August 1st 2001

I started my Epic with the foraging part... I figured this would be the easiest to do and I can do this alone. During the week its hard for me to get with Xthisu or others and get good Experience as I get home late from work, and they don't stay on long after I log in. So I thought this would be perfect for me to get the things I can do on my own done and outta the way.

I first went to Misty Thicket to forage the Sweetened Mudroot. Needless to say I was not successful the first night. *sigh* I did have fun though, helping buff/SoW and heal the new players there trying to gain levels. I logged for the night without the Sweetened Mudroot... 

The next night I logged in, and within an hour had my Sweetened Mudroot! So I of course announced it to the zone, as they had all come to know me there by that time...(Total foraging time: 4-5 RL hours)

My next trip was to my home town of Kelethin/Greater Faydark to forage Ripened Heartfruit. Again this took me two nights to forage up. (I have a forage skill of over 130) but I did get the item. (Total foraging time: 3-4 RL hours)

August 9th 2001

After a weekend of gaining Experience and having fun with Xthisu & Epic Heroes, It was time to get back to foraging and my Epic... This time I was headed to Innothule Swamp for the Speckled Molded Mushroom. I meet another Wood-Elf Ranger there, also foraging for his. He was nice enough to get me drunk (Ok really drunk!) and I think we entertained each other during the night! Thank you Gilantz Gothammer for the fun time that night!

This one has taken the longest to forage. I spent over three nights here finally getting the item on my third night... (Total foraging time: 8-9 RL hours)

August 14th 2001

Next I headed to Everfrost for the Chilled Tundra Root. I got this one on the second night. (Total foraging time: 3-4 RL hours). 

Next I went to Friona Vie to forage up the Rose of Firiona (The last item I needed to be foraged). After I reached FV, I foraged up the rose in about 20 minutes!!

It was still early in the evening, so I decided to go ahead and start Part 4 of the Epic, The Rune Crested Bowl. I took the Rose of Firiona to Merdan Fleetfoot in Surefall Glade. I was quite careful about entering the zone, as I had never been here before and wasn't sure how the residents would look upon me. I was amiable to most of the residents, and to a few Apprehensive so I was set to wonder about the town.

I tracked for Merdan Fleetfoot and found him on the side of a lake staring across it at another NPC...

I hailed him, and then handed my Rose of Firiona to him to give to Niera. He returned a Wooden Painting to me as he didn't think Niera would like it anyway...

I then had to head to Frontier Mountains to seek out the Human Skeleton to hand the Wooden Painting to. Once I go there, he wasn't hard to find as he con'd blue to me and was on tracking. The hard part was actually handing him the Wooden painting... He wouldn't stop moving! And I was in an area with allot of blue con's so I was a bit nervous. I kept opening a trade window with the Painting in it, and he would get to far away and the trade would be canceled! I finally was able to give him the painting, and he in return gave me the Silver Chained Locket.

I then took the locket back to Surefall Glade to find Niera and return it to her...

She in turn gave me the Platinum Specked Powder as a reward for finding her Mother's Locket...

Well, That is as far as I have gotten so far. I plan to work more on it next week, and possibly start on Part 1 in Burning Woods. I also want to start at the beginning of Part 3 with Alrik Farsight in Timorous Deep...

August 20th 2001

Last night, I continued my Quest for my Epics... I ran into a lot of trouble and frustration, and you can read my Rant here:

Angel's First Rant about EverQuest

Anyway... I decided to go ahead and start Part 1 of the Epic Quest and Venture to Burning Woods in Kunark. I was quite a bit nervous to enter Burning Woods, and once I was in the zone, I looked to see what the level of players were there... Hmm I'm the only one that is below 50?

I levitated, invised & SoW'ed myself, and headed North to go find Telin Darkforest. I found him on Tracking as I got close to the Skyfire Mountains zone line. The gorilla's in this zone CAN see through Invis as they are considered "Undead" (Go-figure).

I initiated the Quest with Telin, he gave me the worn note, and then paid 50PP for a port outta BW as fast as I could!   :)

Next stop was to Greater Faydark and to visit Faelin Bloodbriar. I was a bit worried she wouldn't be up, and I would have to "camp" her and kill Orc Pawns... But luck was with me, and as I came to the area she spawns at, i found her on Tracking! She was running (Fast!) and I could hardly keep up with her. She wouldn't respond to Hails, and was moving so fast I didn't get a screenshot of her.   :(

I managed to give her the Worn Note from Telin, and she in return gave me her ring.

Next I was headed to West Commonlands to visit Giz X'Tin in Kithacor Forest. Before I did this though, as when I give Giz X'Tin Faelin's Ring, a Teir`Dal Reaver will spawn and can kill me easily... I went to Freeport and got a Bind there.

I also made sure it was early in the morning EQ time, so I don't run into any of those night nasties that wonder the woods of Kithacor. I found Giz X'Tin quite easily, and didn't think about shouting out what I was about to do and that a nasty level 50 Teir`Dal Reaver would be spawning and roaming the woods.   :(

I gave the ring to Giz X'Tin and sure enough, I have a Teir`Dal Reaver pounding on me. He hit me for over 200 points of damage right as he spawned, I turned and RAN RAN RAN to the zone line.   :)

Giz X'Tin had given me a Dark Metal Coin to return to Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods *sigh*. I decided instead, I would get to Fironia Vie, get bound there, and then log off for the night...

Well a few interesting things happened to me last night, and I never made it to Firiona Vie... All that is listed in my Rant...

Angel's First Rant about EverQuest

Tonight I plan to get to Firiona Vie, get Bound and hand the Dark Metal Coin to Telin Darkforest... Wish me luck!!

August 21st 2001

Last night, I was able to make it to Firiona Vie *finally* and paid for a Bind by the bank. It was time to head back to Burning Woods, to return the Dark Metal Coin to Telin Darkforest.

While I was running through Frontier Mountains, a few Giants decided I looked tasty, and thought they would "Hit on me" so I snared them and kept moving. I zoned into BWs, and started the long run to the Skyfire Mountains Zone. This time seemed a bit easier and I made it there pretty quickly.

Found Telin on Tracking and headed to go find him. He was a lot closer this time, and there we no other creatures in the area. I dropped Invis, and hailed him. I gave him the Dark Metal Coin and he quickly returned the Worn Dark Metal Coin to me. Re-Invised myself and started auctioning for a port outta there...

No luck... No one wanted to port me so I had to run the way back to FM. *sigh* I made it without any problems and decided I would go to the Overthere instead of FV. Right as I zoned into the Overthere...

You Have Been Disconnected...

*sigh* So I logged off for the night instead of fighting it...

August 22nd 2001

Well last night was the first night I didn't get a single Disconnect!! So I was able to get a lot done!!

I started by studying the Timorous Deep map form EQ Atlas. I noticed that the Bloated Belly from the Ogre Isle that has the Great Oowomp comes somewhat close to the isle with the Chessboard so I thought I would take the Bloated Belly and jump off as I got close. I was able to do this with out any problems and I found Alrik Farsight quite easily. I targeted him, ran a bit away, dropped Camo and con'd him. Hmmm he was indifferent to me. So I hailed him, and sure enough he would NOT talk to me *sigh*

This time instead of waiting for the boat, I noticed on the map that Ogre Isle is just straight North of here, and instead, I re-SoW'ed, Levitated and Camo'ed myself and started running North. Once I started getting close, I tracked for the Great Oowomp and got to the isle pretty fast and much easier then the boat. The Mudskimmer was there waiting for me too, to take me to Oasis.

Sense Alrik Farsight wouldn't speak to me, I had to do the mini-quest in Felwithe. I made it to Felwithe with the help of a Lords of War Guild member's Port, and found Niola Impholder in the Magicians Guild. I gave her 4 Bat Wings at a time until she was Amiable to me. (took 9 stacks of Bat Wings to go from Indifferent to Amiable) I then headed back to Oasis and the Ogre Isle...

This time instead of waiting for the Bloated Belly, I SoW'ed, Levitated and Camo'ed myself again, and headed South in Timorous Deep. (Why take the boat when you can walk on water?) The map below (courtesy of EQ Atlas) shows the route I took to get to Alrik Farsight.

Alrik Farsight now Con'd Amiable to me and I was able to start this part of my Epic Quest with him. After speaking with Alrik, he gave me the Crushed Pot.

Next I had to go back to Felwithe (lot of running around on the Epics!) and find Farios Elianos. I did find him, and I handed over the Crushed Pot to him...

He in return gave me an errand to run... I had to take his Grocery List to Merchant Nora in Felwithe.

I found Merchant Nora, and Handed over the Grocery List to her...

She then gave me the Bag of Provisions to take back to Farios Elianos... (Lots of running around... Told ya)

Gave Farios Elianos his bag of Provisions he needed so badly, and he finally gave me the receipt I was after!!

Next I had to head back to the Timorous Deep, and to Alrik Farsight. Again I took the long run across the water and found Alrik quite easily. I handed him the receipt, and he in turn gave me the Ancient Pattern...

Finally, That part of my Epic Quest completed. I have done just about all I can do on my own, and will need to wait quite a few levels before I start any other parts of it. So it may be some time before I update this page again...

Until I complete more of the quest, good luck to everyone and safe travels!!

November 13th 2001

Last night, Epic Heroes and I set up camp in East Karana to start the second part of my Epic Quest. We had 4 players there that needed to get this part done (2 Rangers & 2 Druids), and wanted to do all 4. I was third in line. We finished the first two without any problems.

When it came my turn, the NPC Druids decided not to work with me. I handed in the Worn Dark Metal Coin to Althele, and in turn handed in the next items to the NPC's in turn. I was then told to follow them to the gathering place and well... nothing happened. After about 2 minutes of waiting, the Druids started despawning leaving only Althele and me with out my original Coin I had turned in.

I immediately /petitioned and was greeted by a Guide within a few minutes. Of course there was nothing the Guide could do, and GM Bretten was off that day. This is a triggered spawn, and the Guide does think the GM Bretten will reimburse me my Original Worn Dark Metal Coin but the Guide of course, can make no promises.

So tonight when I log in, I will re-petition and see what happens. Worse case is, I will have to re-do the first step of my Epic. Not to hard but a lot of running around, and for someone that cannot port, not real easy either. The hardest part will be getting Felin Bloodbriar to spawn again. I was very lucky the first time as she just happened to be up. *sigh*

I will keep everyone posted of the outcome of all this. After last night though, I was completely disappointed and upset. I wasn't mad, just frustrated.

~Rainangel of Vazaelle
