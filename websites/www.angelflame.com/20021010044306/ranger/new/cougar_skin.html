Cougar Skin Mask Quest (Velious Quest)

Starts in: Iceclad Ocean Who: Ergrez Shortpaw
Minimum Level: 30 Reward: Cougarskin Mask

Quest Items: 

  • High Quality Cougar Skin
  • 2 Mammoth Meats

Additional Zones:

  • Eastern Wastes

Related Creatures:

  • Snow Cougars
  • Woolly Mammoths

Visit Ergrez Shortpaw (not Ergriz, his helper without a last name) and hail him. He is located in the gnoll camp SE of the Tower of Frozen Shadow. The gnolls in this camp are indifferent and do not seem to be KOS; I walked right into camp w/out sow on and was not attacked. He will tell you something about how he's the tailor for his clan, etc., and will say how he can make armor if you tell him what you have:

"Hail Ergrez Shortpaw". He will not respond so you ask, "what armor?"

Ergrez Shortpaw says "Many armors. All of animal hides from the islands. If you wanting armor then I can make for you. All I ask is you do me favor."

You say "What favor?"

Ergrez Shortpaw says "Snowfangs in need of much help lately. Once we mighty but now weak. You help us get stronger. If you want armor of wolves or cougar than I need, you bring twice as many materials. For each thing you wanting I need thing. Just tell me what furs you have and I tell you what I need."

Ask "What skin?"

Ergrez Shortpaw says "Yes. I take skin of animal that hunters catch and make into things. Very important, very big job for clan."

Then you have to tell him what skin you have, in this case, "I have high quality cougar skin."

Ergrez Shortpaw growls loudly. "I not like making things of cat fur. Smells bad, hurts nose. You want me to work with that you bring food, lots of food. You bring me mammoth meats."

At this point, give him the skin, and the 2 mammoth meats (unstacked of course).

It will say something about Ergrez working a while with the furs. It will NOT tell you that you gained experience, faction, or anything else. The mask will appear in your inventory.
