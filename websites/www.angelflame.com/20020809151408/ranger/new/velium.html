Velium Retrieval Quest (Bracer of Hammerfal)

Starting NPC: Sentry Kale
Starting Zone: Skyshrine
Minimum level: 45

 

Sentry Kale wants you to pick up some Velium in the Dwarven mines.  Ask Sentry Kale "What Tasks?" and this will get the Quest going and Kale will give you a note. Return to Thurgadin. When you jump over snowdrift into the mines, go straight and make first right. You will find a little pit with the Dwarf in it. Hail and do exchange visible, very important. Don't forget 50pp.

He will then give you a Velium shipment to return to Kale. Give it to Kale and he will occasionally reward you with a Bracer of Hammerfal. Before you give the shipment back to Kale, check to see if he is still giving out this quest. It may be that you need to trigger him again to get the right reward. There is apparently 3 versions of Sentry Kale. I have yet to do this quest, so I am unsure which one you will want. I understand the different versions of Kale give out 3 separate quests so be sure the Kale you are giving your hard earned items to, is the one you want. The Bracer of Hammerfal's Effect is Summon Shattering Hammer:
