Meet Crimsonrain Dark-Elf Shadow Knight of Vazaelle

 

Crimsonrain... Born just less then two weeks ago on Vazaelle (Mid-July 2001), has quickly become a well loved character for me. 

She is a Dark-Elf Shadow Knight born in Niriak. Her first adventure was the journey to West Freeport. having NO experience under her belt it was a long and dangerous run to Freeport. I wanted to take her there because I knew the area well, and I also knew the guards would keep me protected. (The look at me dubiously, but they protect me all the same).

Levels 1 - 4

She spent the first 4 Levels of her life here in West Freeport killing the odd Bat and Rats, Snakes, Fire Beetles and anything else that would cross her path. She is an unliked Race/Class, so She did not loot to much. She did have the benefit of having a much higher Ranger Relative to "Twink" her out a bit (hey I've been through this New Character part all ready and really do NOT want to do it again!!) Fine Plate Armor, 2 Handed Practice Sword, and some jewelry to up her stats a bit...

Levels 4 - 9

She spent the next 5 levels in West Commonlands with the help of a fellow Shadow Knight (Rawkhex) at the only Orc Camp. It was hard here but with only one or two deaths, she made it to level 9 and gained her first set of spells.

Levels 9 - 15

For the next few (6) levels, She stayed in West Commonlands Soloing Young Kodiaks, Willow Wisps, Pumas, Giant Spiders and anything else that Con'd blue to her. The Guards at the Toll Booth look upon her Dubiously, but they still guard her. And well whata ya know? Kizdean Gix and Dragoon Zytl like her!!! No unexpected attacks from these two!! None of the Vendors will sell/buy from here but the experience is great! At level 15, I have gained more spells including a low level Darkness and Fear!! Fear kiting here I come!!

Level 15 - Present

I am now currently in Oasis of Marr. I have gained another level, 16, and am continuing to gain experience for Crimson.
