The Grammar Manual ~ Ice Forged Shackles Quest (Velious)

Starting NPC: Ami
Starting Zone: Iceclad Ocean
Minimum level: 35


Ice Forged Shackles
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM NO DROP
AC 10
SLOT: Wrist
STR +5 DEX +5 AGI +5 HP +30
EFFECT: Velium Shards
WEIGHT: 0.5
Class: WAR CLR PAL RNG SHD BRD ROG
Race: HUM ERU ELF DEF HEF DWF HFL GNM

Speak to Ami the buson, by the igloos. To get her to talk, say Ye scurvy dog, then "what is a buson". She will mention a grammar manual. Ask her about the grammar manual and she will tell you it is missing Chapter P. She says to ask her friend about it. Find the lost gnome and tell him that Ami wants chapter P and he will tell you he threw it into the ocean. (You can actually skip this part).

Now go find the wandering gnoll fisherman. He needs help to release him from a curse. He needs some gifts to help him make a strong fishing pole. You must get raptor guts (tiger raptors-wakening) and a strong stick (Holgresh's in wakening) for him.

Give the gnoll those two things and an Enraged Walrus spawns. Kill the walrus - it's level 40 or lower. Once it is dead, an irritated gnoll spawns. Hail him and he asks if you still want the magic item. If you say "I want the magic item" he gives you a "chapter p". Give the chapter P to Ami at the gnome camp and she gives you a pirate book. Give that to Nilam the chef and he will reward you with the Shackles.

The following was posted on Allakhazam's site by an Anonymous. I thought he/she did I good job describing how she did this Quest, so I decided to add it... The only Edits I did were to spelling, and removed some un-needed comments...

1. Ran thru Kael, (I'm Kos as I'm on Veeshan faction) got me a bind on Wakening Lands side.

2. Ran to SkyShrine zone. On the left of the SS zone (facing out of the zone) there is a static spot where 3 to 5 Tigeraptors / Tigersourous pop. I just harmony/single pull them to zone and fear kite them. 1 hour got the guts.

3. I found a 46 Necro that was needing help killing Holgresh for a spell drop. We went to the Holgresh tunnel/cave (number 9 on map from EqAtlas) (Wakening Land Map) We didn't pull from inside the cave, there are 12 of those suckers in there, ALL within agro range of each other... BUT there are wanderers just outside the cave, so we set up camp on a wall just below the cave...(south of the number 9) and I single pulled a bunch to our camp. Was quite easy, a lot of the Holgresh were green to us, so we just fear kited them to death.

Well, this camp took 1 hour before I got my strong pole. The Necro was then joined on a off the spur moment Plane of Growth raid that 3 other guilds were conducting. So I thanked him and left.

4. I ran to Ami and did the stuff listed up top.

5. Went to find the wandering fisherman... he's NOT named wandering fisherman, he actually WANDERS on the island next to the gnoll camp. You'll see him, he's walking around holding a fishing pole. Hailing will not get him to stop, you have to say "curse". He then stops and spills his story. After reading these posts. I waited till the fisherman was near the village, Hailed him, then gave him the Raptor Guts and Strong Branch. An Enraged Walrus popped right in front of him.... see I'm level 44 and this is my main and I don't have a lot of cash for the god stuff, so I can't tank him. So I pulled the walrus to the gnoll camp and the whole camp jumped him...heheh, I just sat by till they killed him. The fisherman I gave the items to changed into the Irritated gnoll. I hailed him and said what is listed above. I got my Chapter P.

6. Ran back to Ami, gave her Chapter P. Got the Grammar Manual.

7. Ran to the chef (he's in igloo next to Ami) gave him the book. Got the Shackles!
