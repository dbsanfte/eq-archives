Trueshot Longbow Quest

Starts in: Kelethin Who: Maesyn Trueshot
Minimum Level: 12 Reward: Treant Bowstave

Quest Items: 

  • Dwarven Wire
  • Tumpy Tonics
  • Spirderling Silk
  • Micro Servos

Additional Zones:

  • Freeport West
  • Kaladim North
  • Steamfont Mountains
  • Ocean of Tears

Related Creatures:

  • A Spiderling
  • Jyle Windshot
  • Rogue Clockwork
  • Trentor Everhot
  • Tumpy Irontoe

Related Quests:

  • Tumpy Tonics

I have done this quest numerous times with Rainangel to try and make some money. This is not a hard quest, but it is a long and tedious one. Collecting the Micro Servos is one of the most annoying parts of this quest. And knowing that a completed Trueshot Long Bow only sells about 200 PP... makes you wonder if this quest is worth the time and effort. I personally think all Rangers should complete this quest at least once even if they fail to create the bow. Its a good learning experience and helps low level Rangers get out and see the world they live in. (Plus its a great break from "Hack N Slashing")

To get started on the quest, Find Maesyn Trueshot in the Ranger guild in Kelethin and say, "I will gather materials". he will then give you a small 4 slot pouch in which to place 1. A Dwarven Wire 2. A Micro Servo 3. 1 Spiderling Silk 4. 1 Treant Wood. OK now to go out and start collecting the items you need...

I started my Quest in Freeport as I read up on the quest before actually starting it. So I will write this as starting in Freeport. I was level 20 the first time I did this Quest.

In Freeport, look (heck you are a Ranger, Track for him!) for Jyle Windshot. He is upstairs in an inn (Cant remember the name off hand) not to far behind the West Gate. There is another Jyle Windshot near the Freeport Docks. This is NOT the Jyle you want. the Jyle you want will ask you for Lanterns in return for Treant Wood. Give him a lantern (I bought mine from a vendor just outside the bank of Freeport) and he will give you either Treant Wood or a Wooden Heart. (The Wooden Heart is for another Quest - Raincaller. Place the Wooden Heart in your bank for future use) If you get the Wooden Heart, give him another lantern till he does give you the Treant wood. Place the Treant Wood in you bank for later. Part 1 of Quest complete. Before leaving Freeport, visit the East Commonlands and kill Spiderlings for Spiderling Silk. You will only need one, but its a good bet to try to get a few. Place these in your bank with the Treant Wood. Now on to part 2 of the quest... Head over to the Freeport Docks to catch the Boat to Butcher Block. Before boarding the boat, see if you can get a SoW from anyone as it will greatly help you with the next step. You will also need to bring about 4 Flasks of Water. Place these in a slot in your pack that you will NOT drink them. You will need these for the Tumpy tonics

On the way to Butcher Block, the Boat will stop by Sister Isle. Inside the first Hut on the left almost directly behind the door, is a Ranger NPC that sells Kiola Nuts. You will need at least 4 of these nuts to get the Dwarven Wire, so be sure to bring some money with you (about 5 PP is a safe bet) and be sure to have room in your inventory as the nuts DO NOT stack. Again to be safe, buy about 8 of the nuts, any you don't use you can keep in your bank till a future use, or you can sell/eat them yourself. You will need 2 nuts per Dwarven Wire, and you will need 2 Wires per Trueshot Bow attempt. When the Boat stops at the dock on Sister isle, run quickly, buy the nuts and get back to the Boat! I have done this about 6 times and have never missed the Boat. (This is why SoW comes in handy here) If you do miss the Boat. Sit down on the dock and wait for the next one (about 30 minute wait) Don't indulge and go explore the Isle, as there are a few NPC's that do NOT like Rangers and will kill you on site. You safest place to wait is at the end of the dock. After getting back on the Boat, finish off the ride to Butcher Block. Part 2 completed...

OK you are now standing on the Dock in Butcher Block Mountains with at least 4 Kiola nuts and 4 Flasks of Water in your pack right? Next stop is Kaladim. (Follow the path from the docks, it will bring you to Kaladim's front gate) You will be dealing with 2 NPC's here in Kaladim, Tumpy Irontoe and Trentor Everhot. Trentor is the one you get the Dwarven Wire from, but he will only give you 1 wire for 2 Tumpy Tonics. So you need to get the Tumpy Tonics first. Once you zone into Kaladim, go straight then take a left at the tunnels. Start to track for Tumpy Irontoe. Once you find him, you don't need to say anything to him, just give him 1 Kiola Nut and 1 Flask of Water. He will then give you 1 Tumpy Tonic. Do this again to get the second, third and forth tonic. Make sure you place them in a slot in your inventory where you WILL NOT drink them. Next is Trentor Everhot. Head back down the tunnel from which you came, back to the front of the city. Once there head down the opposite tunnel. (You will zone here). Once you zone start tracking for Trentor. You should find him at the bottom level of his building, Everhot Forge. If he isn't there, he sometimes likes to wonder off, wait for him, he usually returns quickly. Again you do not have to say anything to get this part of the quest going. You need to give him 2 Tumpy Tonics for 1 Dwarven Wire. The Tumpy Tonics stack, be sure NOT to stack them in the trade window with Trentor, or you may lose them and get no wire. Do this twice to get a total of 2 Dwarven Wires. After getting the wire, run to the bank and place the wire with your other components. Head out of the city and back on the path towards Greater Faydark. Part 3 Completed.

Part 4 - Micro Servos (Steamfont Mountains)... This is the most tedious part of the Quest... Hunting Runaway & Rogue Clockworks for Micro Servos. Unfortunately, Micro Servos are NO DROP, so you have to kill the Clockworks and loot the Servos yourself. they are a pretty rare drop as well, maybe getting 1 Micro Servo per 6-8 Clockworks. OK you should be heading towards Butcher Block Mountains at this point. Zone into BB and follow the path. You will pass the Wizard Spire, and come to a fork in the road. On your right will be the Zone Line for Lesser Faydark. You need to travel through Lesser Faydark to get to Steamfont Mountains, where the Clockworks can be found. Once you zone into Lesser Faydark, take a left and stick right next to the wall. Don't leave the wall and keep running till you come to a passage on your left to Steamfont Mountains Zone. Its a very short run, (about 1 minute or so) and best bet, if you get attacked by anything, don't stop. Keep heading to that zone line instead of turning back. Once you zone into Steamfont, follow the path and keep tracking up for Rogue and Runaway Clockworks. Kill any and all you see. Again you will need 2 Micro Servos and this part of the quest will probably take about an hour to do. After you get the 2 Micro Servos, head back to Greater Faydark the way you came. (again going through Lesser Faydark). Once in Greater Faydark, head to Kelethin. Part 4 of the Quest is complete...

Once you get to Kelethin, head to the bank and get all the items you have collected so far. You should have: 1 Spiderling Silk, 1 Treant Wood, 2 Dwarven Wires, 2 Micro Servos, & the collection bag Maesyn Trueshot gave you. Place inside the bag 1 Spiderling Silk, 1 Treant Wood, 1 Dwarven Wires, 1 Micro Servo, and hit "Combine". This will create a new closed bag you will give back to Maesyn Trueshot, and he will then give you a Treant Bowstave. here is the kicker, after all that work and time, you still do not get your reward. You now need the Fletching Skill to create the bow. *sigh*

I created my first Trueshot Longbow at skill level 160. I worked very hard to collect all the items and get to that point that I wasn't going to fail at getting the bow due to low skill. I worked my fletching up for a week before attempting to assemble the bow. I was lucky and made the bow that I still have and use today. I have heard of people crafting the bow at skill levels of below 70... but it is up to you when you try. 

When you do decide to try, you will need: a Fletching Kit, The Treant Bowstave, 1 Micro Servo, 1 Dwarven Wire & a Planning Tool. Place all these items in your Fletching Kit, sit down, empty your hands and then hit "Combine". If you are lucky like I was, you will have a new Trueshot Bow. if not, well you can try again...

Tips and Tricks for the Trueshot Bow Quest:

  • When you first start out on the Quest, get enough of all the materials to have about 10 chances to make the bow. The Treant Bowstave is Lore, as is the Trueshot Bow, but this way if you fail, you have the items you need to try again.
  • Both Runaway & Rogue Clockworks drop the Micro Servos. No other creature in Steamfont carries them, and you will lose Faction killing other Clockwork type creatures.
  • Planning tools can be bought on Platform 28 in Kelethin at the "Trueshot Bows" Merchant for about 11 PP each.
  • If you fail at making the bow, the Planning tool will be returned to you on your curser.
  • Trueshot Bows sell anywhere from 150pp - 200pp.
