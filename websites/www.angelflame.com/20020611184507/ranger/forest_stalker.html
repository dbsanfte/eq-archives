Forest Stalker's Armor Quest Information and Guide

A visit to Gragek in Kael Drekkal will start you on the Quest for Forest Stalker's Armor. Visit the Temple of Veeshan with a VERY large party Raid and/or Guild Raid for drops of the "Ancient Tarnished Chain"

Return the Ancient Tarnished Chain Pieces with the 3 Gems listed below to Gragek to be rewarded with the Forest Stalker's Armor.
