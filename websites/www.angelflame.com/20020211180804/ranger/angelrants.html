Angel Rants about EverQuest *sigh*

 

August 20th 2001

I am a horrid typist, and I spent a bit of time typing this, so I figured I'd add it to my Ranger Guide for anyone interested in reading it...

I have never been one to Rant, or to go to the lengths of posting a Rant... But after last night, I just gotta say something!!! This is going to be long, and its not a complaint about another player, or another Guild, just about the Dynamics of EverQuest.

What was with last night anyway? I'm on a cable modem at home, not the best but not bad either. I almost never lose connection due to zoning let alone lose connection from lag. But last night, I was losing connection time and again both from zoning and from just sitting there! And every time I would log back in, I would download another patch...

OK I lost connection zoning into LOIO so I could make my way to Burning Woods for my Epic Quest. (I'm doing the parts of my Epic that I can do now to get them out of the way) OK I log back in and well I'm not at the zone line where I lost connection, but instead I'm in the middle of the zone with Invis off???? OK not a big deal, so I head to BW and get done what I need to do.

I then get a port to Butcher Block and well, lose connection again while being ported... (Download another patch) Log back in and I'm well... I have NO IDEA where I am, I think I fell through the world cause all I can see is the sky and what I think is the sun...

 

Get a message that "Player is beneath the World at xxx,xxx,xxx, Summoning to a Safe Location" and poof, I'm in front of Kaladim. OK not a big deal either, I head to Gfay to turn in my Worn Note to Faelin Bloodbriar and well what do ya know, she is up!! (Running really fast mind you and not answering to Hails but she is up!!) I manage to turn in my note to her and get her ring.



Next stop is West Commons so I get a port there, and run to Kithacor Woods, find this Giz X'Tin and give him the ring and get outta there with out getting killed by that nasty level 50 Reaver. OK so far so good...

Next stop is BB again so I can catch the boat back to FV... OK Pay 30PP for a port to BB, get there no problems and run to the docks to catch the shuttles. OK as I reach the dock I see the shuttles are just returning so I know I have a bit of a wait. No big deal , do some foraging, Talk with Hatchetface's Alt (sorry cant remember his name now) and relax a bit. about after 20 minutes of waiting, and the shuttles are about to leave to meet with the Maidens Voyage...

You have been disconnected...

*Sigh* OK if I log back on quickly, cast Levitate on myself, I can still make it to the shuttles before they zone!! Wrong! I log in to find myself at the Druid Ring in BB again!!! What is going on here? From what I could tell, the last save of my character was loaded because my spells were changed as so was my inventory (I had changed them while waiting for the shuttles just 10 minutes or so earlier).

OK so in hopes to STILL catch the shuttles before they leave, I run run run to the docks... As I approach the docks, I see the Shuttles are returning... *sigh* So here I go again, another long wait to catch the Maidens Voyage...

So I relax, Forage and work a few other skills and wait... about 20 minutes later, the shuttles start to head toward the zone line, and well I am still on them!! Great, maybe I can make it to FV and get bound before I have to log off for the night. (Yea Right) I zone on the shuttles into Timorous Deep, and start heading to the Maidens Voyage.

As I come around the big boat and am getting close to the platform, I see the other shuttle there... and it isn't moving away like it normally does... well my shuttle goes overtop of the other one, and PUSHES me OFF into the water!! Geeze what now???? So here I am swimming, in the TD, and we all know you cant get on a boat, especially a big one from the water - I do try without success and then watch the Maidens Voyage take off at Warp 9.9 toward FV leaving me behind...



OK /Petition I have been waiting for the boat to get to FV for over an hour now, after disconnects that cause me to miss the boat, then finally getting to the boat, I get pushed off by the other shuttle to now be swimming in the TD. Please help...

The Guide responds pretty quickly (Cant remember his name now, I'm really bad at names) saying he will be right with me... OK so I start swimming, checking Tracking to make sure I'm in no real danger and wait... I come across an Isle... hmmmm. Make it to the isle and send a tell to the Guide asking if he placed me here... (I have never been summoned before by a Guide/GM so I really have no idea what happens...) The Guide tells me he did not summon me there, and if I am on dry land now, there isn't any thing he can do for me... Hmmm let me get this straight, I fall into the water of the TD due to a bug with the boat system, manage to find land on my own, which I have absolutely NO WAY OF GETTING OFF OF ON MY OWN (except to swim) and YOU tell me there isn't anything you can do for me??????

You have been disconnected...

OK Frustration is setting in at this point... I re-log in (against me better judgment) and do a /who all GM. I see that the Guide I was talking to is no longer on line... So I send a tell to the only other guide online... /tell soandso_guide I'm sorry to interrupt, But I was speaking with another guide when I got disconnected and I see he is no longer online, do I need to re-petition? That Guide tells me no, that the guide I was speaking to got disconnected too and that he will contact me once he gets back logged in.

I see another isle across the way and start to head over there... then
You have been summoned...

I look around, I'm on Ogre Isle!! The Guide then sends me a tell that I can hop the Barrel Barge to Oasis... That's great, I don't mean to complain here but I was in BUTCHER BLOCK heading to FV, Not Oasis!!! I cannot get to FV from Oasis, nor Ogre Isle... (ok I can but its a much longer run and much more dangerous)

The Guide never responds... So I go to Oasis, and log out for the night with a head ache and Total Frustration with the Game Dynamics...

*sigh* is all I can say
