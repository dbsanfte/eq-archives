Epic Heroes and the Wurmslayer

 

Last night, Whytstar, Evasto, Saneldar, Amanar & I got together for a bit of fun, and to get all 5 of us the Famed "Wurmslayer" weapon. I took a few screenshots, and had great fun with this group of friends!

I wanted to share that fun I had, and include it here on my Ranger Guide for all to see...

I also want to take this time to tell everyone, Whytstar, Evasto, Saneldar & Amanar Thank You so much!! I have been wanting a Wurmy for the last few weeks, and because of you all, I shall soon own one!! THANK YOU!!

 I only have a few screenshots, not as many as I thought I had... *sigh*

I was unable to complete the Quest, as I had to get to bed due to working the next day... I do have all three scales and am ready to turn them into Squire Fuzzmin in Firiona Vie. I hope to do that this week sometime perhaps with a bit more help from Epic Heroes...

 

Again I had great fun, and I hope everyone else did too, and I look forward to more events with everyone!!

~Rainangel
