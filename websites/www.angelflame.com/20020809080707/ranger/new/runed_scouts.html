Runed Scout's Armour (Thurgadin Quest Armor)

You will need to have Amiable Faction with Thurgadin to even start this Quest. Argash will not speak to you until he views you Amiably. Killing the Giants that roam the areas of Velious will increase your Faction in Thurgadin (while decreasing others).

To get the Runed Scout's Armor, Visit Argash in the City of Thurgadin. You will then need to kill Giants in Kael Drekkal area. These Giants will drop on random and from what I understand, very rarely Corroded Chain Armor. You will also need to collect 3 Various Gems per piece of Runed Scout's Armor from Random Velious MoB's. (I have yet to find any in the last 2 weeks I have been in Velious)

Return the correct items to Argash, and he will reward your efforts with the piece of Runed Scout's Armor. Sounds pretty easy huh? I would suggest being at least level 45 (and bring some friends of about the same level) before attempting this quest.
