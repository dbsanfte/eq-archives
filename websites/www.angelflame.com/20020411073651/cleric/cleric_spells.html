Cleric Spells & Skill Information

Clerics have a long list of Spells available to them. From Direct Damage, to Resurrections, The best Healing Spells and more. As I have been leveling my Cleric, Eliania up, I was having a hard time deciding which spells to have mem'd in different situations. To help me out, I created a Spread Sheet of all the Clerics spells listed by Type (i.e.. DD, AE, Healings, Buffs etc.), The Mana required and Effects of the spell. You may find this spread sheet useful, so I have included it here. (Printable Microsoft Excel) Please click HERE to view/print the list.
