The Pirate's Lost Map Quest (Eye Patch of Plunder)

Starts in: Iceclad Ocean Who: The Lost Pirate
Minimum Level: 50 Reward: Eye Patch of Plunder

Quest Items: 

  • 4 Pieces of "The lost map"

Additional Zones:

  • Great Divide
  • Iceclad Ocean

Related Creatures:

  • A Lost Pirate
  • Ami the Buson
  • a Dire Wolf Stalker
  • Stormfeather
  • Lodizal
  • Tizmaks
  • Captain Nalot

Speak to the lost pirate. He will ask if you are lost. Say you are lost and he will reply and then say something about a lost map he made of Velious. Speak to Ami the buson, by the igloos. To get her to talk, say Ye scurvy dog, then "what is a buson". She will mention a grammar manual. Ask her about the grammar manual and she will tell you it is missing Chapter P (this is actually another quest).

Now go back to Lost Pirate and tell him that Ami wants Chapter P. He will then tell you he remembers and wants to know if you are wanting to go find all the pieces. Tell him, using his words as usual in quests, that you want to go find the pieces and he will give you a lore no drop book 4 slot container. This is bugged, and you have to either zone or log out to use it. 

Update: To get him to give you this book (took me a bit to figure this out) ~ make sure you don't have anything targeted (hit the escape key) and then say "I will gather the four parts". This part seems to be really bugged, and he doesn't respond to normal quest dialog.

The pieces of the map are Lore & No Drop.

1. The first piece of the map can be looted off of a Dire Wolf Stalker which spawns after you kill the dire wolves by the Tower of Frozen Shadow in the Iceclad Ocean zone.

2. The second piece is looted from Stormfeather in the Iceclad Ocean

3. The third is a mini quest of its own. Kill Tizmaks in Great Divide until you have 4 of the NO DROP chunks of meat. Hand 4 meats to Bloodmaw in the same zone. He will despawn after you give him the meat and then say "I want the map please" to the gnome captive that stands behind Bloodmaw. He will give you Map piece 3.

4. Part four can be looted off Lodizal in Iceclad Ocean.

Put all four pieces in the book and combine it. Then give it back to the Lost Pirate. He will give you back a Completed Iceclad Map. Take this to Captain Nalot and he will reward you with the Eyepatch of Plunder.
