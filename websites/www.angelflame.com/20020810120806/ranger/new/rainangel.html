Rainangel & Crescention in RL

Last updated: June 10th 2002

Where to start... well with a new layout for the site, I will start with how the Ranger's resource came about. I started playing EQ back in January 2001, and Vazaelle hadn't been up very long as a new blue, non-transfer server. Vazaelle was the server I started on, and is still were I reside. Back when I first created Rainangel, I was a true "newbie", and had absolutely NO idea what I was doing or getting into. At the time, I was still playing Ultima Online, and really had no interest in EQ. Crescention talked me into getting my own account, and starting up. So I did... and Hated it. I manage to ding to level 3 and then quit out of the game, heading back to my Tamer on Atlantic (which I still have) in Ultima Online. Crescention continued playing EQ, and even logged in Rainangel and managed to get her to level 10.

I came back to EQ, about a month later, back to Rainangel. Watching Crescention play really peeked my interest once again, and well, I couldn't let him have all the fun now could I? Rain was still in the Faydark area, level 10 with a few pieces of leather & silk, a vendor bought bow, and I cant even remember what sword she was wielding (I know it wasn't a magic one at that). A RL friend of ours, in-game name of Nuriel (DE Shadow Knight) was also playing EQ, about level 13ish. When I saw him in West Commons having fun, killing wisps and such, I decided that was were I wanted to go! (I know I am getting side tracked here, bare with me) My first boat ride was long and uneventful. I reached my new lands, managed to get bound from Crescentlion (yes Crescentlion was Cres's first Chanty, worshiping Tunare up to about level 17 till Cres found out, worshiping a deity as a Chanter isn't a good Idea).

Nuriel even gave me a Combine Long Sword (Wow!! My first magic weapon!) and I was off killing Wisps, bears and anything that con'd blue to me. Spent a lot of time running from Kizdean Gix and Dragoon Zytl (usually not surviving). But anyway...

It was during this time I took up looking for more information about Rangers on the internet. There was a lot of information everywhere, spread out amongst the various websites. That was good, but hard sometime to keep track of. I spent the weekends at Crescention's house, and while I was there, I didn't have access to all the websites I found as easily as I did while at my home. And that is how Angel's was started...

Web design is my passion, always has been from my early days first learning HTML till today (gah I hate Framed Websites). So I decided to start putting all the information I was gathering into one easy place I could find it. It started as random pages filled with info, Fletching tables, Skills and spells, basically anything I thought I would need.

I am a notorious insomniac. During times of my insomnia, I tend to brainstorm ideas, thoughts, layouts, how to make things better and easier. That was the time Angel's Ranger Guide & Info was truly born. I revamped the site from a collection of notes, word documents and ideas into a functional Class related website.
