April 4th, 2002 ~ Bow of Doom!! Eternal Advocates took on Doomshade this past Weekend and we kicked his arse!! Among his drops was this nice little bow…

And the graphic for the bow is outstanding! (Click on a Thumbnail to see the full Screenie!) Huge Grats to Adonna on her new Trueshot Discipline toy! /Hugs Adonna!

  

I was looking through some of my old screenshots I have posted on the Screenshots section… wow its been a long ride! Some of the oldest screenshots I have posted there are from April 2001, a year ago! (I started playing EQ in December 2000) From me in my mid-30’s taking on Cougars in Iceclad and my beloved Ivy-Etched armor, moving to the Great Divide and the Tizmaks there… The time I was in my first Guild, Lords of War (no longer exists from my knowledge) to meeting Eriul in Solb and later joining Epic Heroes up to now with Eternal Advocates… Seeing Rain change over the months in the weapons she wields to the armor she wears so proudly… It’s a great way to stroll down memory lane with your old screenshots. Think about when you first started playing your Ranger, how it was back then when you got an upgrade to that weapon or that armor. What about the time you got your first “Magical” weapon and whacked on every Wisp you came across? Remember how good you felt? That’s what I remember most about leveling up… Rain was my first character in EQ, She has never been Twinked or PL’d. I learned the game through her eyes and the world around her. I didn’t know about spoiler sites when I created her. She was a true Newbie… and she is the most fun I have ever had in EQ, and is my favorite character to play.

Speaking of Rainangel and changing times, Rain dinged 58 last night! Getting so close to the time I can start working my Alternate Skills. I decided when AA came out, (I was 52 at the time) that I would not divert any exp in AA until I dinged 60. I wanted to work on the levels more then the AA, and the new spells and better skills I get with the increase in levels. I still will continue to hold out on AA until I do ding the big six-zero.

I have some new screenies to add… Mostly of some of the encounters I have been a part of with Eternal Advocates. Saturday – Wow what can I say about this last Saturday? I got the opportunity to pull the Named Place Holders in Western Wastes for EA. I had a great time, died a lot and learned quite a bit. The screenies are of one of my massive over pulls, and even when invis’d and heading back to camp with a pull, other mobs do assist and aggro!! Even when they con indifferent to you!

 

That’s about it for the update, I have some changes possibly going on in Real Life and things might change a bit regarding Angel’s… Ill keep everyone updated as I know more of course!
