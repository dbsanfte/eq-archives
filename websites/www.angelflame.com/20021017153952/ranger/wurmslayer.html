The Dragon Scale (Wurmslayer) Quest - Submitted by Leafbringer Nathanial of the Lanys T'Vyl Server

   
 

This is a relatively easy quest that can be useful for two reasons: If you are a Warrior, Shadow Knight, Paladin or Ranger, the Wurmslayer is a nice, heavy-hitting weapon. If you are none of the above, you can still quest this item and sell it easily for at least a couple thousand plat (as of August 2001).

A Huge THANK YOU to Leafbringer Nathanial of the Lanys T'Vyl Server for submitting this Quest!

PART I - FIRONIA VIE START (this part is not necessary to complete the quest, it is simply the beginning of the story)

1) Track for Sir Hobble in Fironia Vie. He was green to a level 47 Ranger. He appears about every half an hour or so and begins walking, then running, through the lands surrounding the town. Note that he comes under attack rather frequently by the beasties in the area, and usually he is killed - so you'll need to find him before this happens and protect him when you do find him. Hobble is used to spawn Squire Fuzzmin. All you need to do is hail Sir Hobble. He will say something about a talking bear and beckon Squire Fuzzmin to check it out. It is around this time (give or take a few minutes) that the Squire will pop somewhere in the zone. He too cons green, and he too is on the move and generally under attack.

When hailed, and following the prompts, the following conversation ensued:

You say, "What quest is Sir Hobble on?"

Squire Fuzzmin says, "Sir Hobble is searching for [three special dragons]. For all I know they are not even dragons. He can't see or hear very well. He still thinks I am the only troll squire around." (note that Fuzzmin isn't a troll)

You say, "What three special dragons?"

Squire Fuzzmin says, "Apparently he is after three dragons which are supposedly named Azdalin, Gylton and Xyfl. He will not rest until he holds their scales. I wish you could [find the dragons].

You say, "I will find the dragons."

Squire Fuzzmin says, "If you bring me the three scales, I will be able to place them upon the next three victims of Sir Hobble. Then we can depart from this accursed isle. He would never know the difference. Do this and I will give you a warrior weapon out of the old knights armory."

PART II: THE SCALES

The three scales are as follows:

1. Drops off Azdalin, a named Wurm in the Burning Woods. Not too hard, level 49-50 ish? A 53 mage, his pet and I (47 ranger) took it down. Not a common pop but hunt the other Wurm Entalon (a bit harder to kill than Azdalin or Gylton) to supposedly help get Azdalin to spawn.

2. Drops off Gylton, a named Wurm in the Burning Woods. Pretty common pop. Not hard to take down. 49-50ish range.

3. The third scale is found by talking to Lord Lyxfl of Burwood (apparently the alternate form of the Wurm Xyfl) in the Temple of Solusek Ro. He wants a piece of tektite and a peridot. Tektite is a gargoyle located in the Overthere. Hand Tektite two carnelians (or kill him) and you will get a piece of tektite. Bring the tektite and a peridot back to the Temple and hand them to Lord Lyxfl, and receive the third scale. You can also kill Lord Lyxfl for the scale, but you should certainly bring a good group. It is also said that Xyfl sometimes appears in Wurm form in the Burning Wood and can be killed there for the scale. This ranger has spent hundreds of hours in Burning Wood and hasn't seen this particular spawn. Ever.

PART III - RETURNING THE SCALES

Back to Fironia Vie with your three scales and now you *must* track down Sir Hobble/Squire Fuzzmin. It's Fuzzmin who wants the scales. Give them to him and you will own the Wurmslayer!

Wurmy is not NO DROP, nor is it LORE, so you can do this over and over and make some nice cash.

Leafbringer's Note:

The most frustrating part of this quest to me was rounding Squire Fuzzmin up when I finally had all the scales. You may luck out and he may be up, but most likely you'll need to hang out near FV (I've found along the river leading out of FV toward the Lake of Ill Omen Zone is a good spot) and track for quite a while before Hobble shows up. Remember, be sowed, be camo'ed (so you don't attract eighty thousand drachnids on the run toward Hobble), and HAIL Hobble. Once you've hailed him, track again and search for Fuzzbin. Sometimes he's stationery in FV (nice), but most often he too is scramblin around the zone, fending off drachnids. Get there fast and give him the scales.
