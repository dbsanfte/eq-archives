Creating a Magelo Profile:

I wrote this up for my Guild, and thought it would be great to add to my site for all to use. It isn't a complete explanation of how to use Magelo, but it will get you started. Click HERE to be taken to the Magelo site to get started.

First off, you do not have to host any images to create a Magelo profile. Nor do you need to know any HTML or JAVA scripting to set one up. Its just a matter of getting in there, playing with the links and figuring it out as you go. It is really a great tool, and I enjoy keeping mine updated. I will try to explain it here best I can, because I am no expert really...

First of course, create a login (Username and Pass), then login to the site with the account you just created. Down on the left, is a link named "Characters", click on it...

OK you will then be able to add your first character, and Create a profile. Just click: "Add a Character" (at the top of the screen). Fill in all the blanks, getting the information you need from your nekked character in-game. If you are unsure what to do here, read the instructions under each field. Like to find out what your attack will be, wield a weapon that doesn't give any stat or attack bonuses (well a weapon with just damage and delay are best). And your AC is that when you are nekked  (Woot!).

Once you have all that info added, and the Character created on Magelo, then the fun begins. I hope you wrote down or can remember everything your character has equipped, and or in inventory... ok on with the good stuff:

Now that your basic profile is made, on the left hand side again, click the "Characters" link again. You should see your character on that screen now, something similar to mine:

The stats you see listed here, are your BASE Stats. Meaning what you have when you are completely nekked. What the links at the bottom do:

Update this character~ Allows you to update the basic profile information of the character. ie: the information you just entered, Level, name. background and image urls, Stats, etc. You can also add a "Bio" here (Take a look at mine if you are unsure)
Manage his/her profile~ This allows you to Equip your character, with drop down menu's etc. Again here is the part you will want to know the names of all the equipment your character is wearing...
Manage his/her keyring~ Allows you to add keys, ie: Trakanon Idol, Sleepers key, etc.
Manage his/her Inventory~ This is the part, that allows you to add bags and items to your general inventory... Can get a bit tricky here but isn't really that hard once you get the hang of it.
Manage his/her abilities~ Here is where you can add in your AA points you have earned... kind of self explanatory...
Manage his/her skills & Languages~ Again kind of self explanatory, just add the skills and languages you have...

Manage his/her Profile ~ Ok now click on "Manage his/her Profile"... You will see a screen with three box, err tables in it. On the top left is the character's Vital stats, on the top right is the characters Equipment (blank right now), and on the bottom is the Stored profil(s). Lets start with the Equipment. To add equipment to your profile, you simply click the [ edit ] link next to the slot you want to add an item to. You will then be able to choose the item, from a drop down menu similar to this:

Do this for all the items you want equipped on your character. (That's why I said I hope you had written down everything!) After you have added all your equipment to your profile, you need to make sure to save it, or all that work you just did will be lost. To save the inventory, at the bottom of the equipment list, is the place to save:

You can name it what ever you want, I choose my character name. Just be sure to click "Save"!! Once you save your inventory profile, click once again on the "Characters" link in the left hand menu.

Manage his/her keyring ~ Now you can update your characters Keyring. Click on the Manage his/her keyring Link. You will see a screen similar to this one:

On the left is keys you can add. On the right are the keys you have in your keyring. To add a key to your keyring, Highlight the key you want to add, and then press the top "arrow" button to add it. If you add a key by mistake, highlight that key, and press the bottom "arrow" to remove it. Be sure to hit the "Update" button to save before you leave this screen.

Manage his/her Inventory ~ Ok here is the part where you can add items to your general inventory. Click the link "Manage his/her inventory", and you will come to a screen like this:

To add an item (not a container, we will get to that part), type in the name of the item in the "add new item" field and hit proceed.

You will find a drop down list with the names of all the items that match your item name you typed in. Choose the correct one, enter the quantity, and then hit proceed. Do this for all the items you want added to your general inventory.

Ok now to add Containers and fill those containers with the items you just added. Click on the link "Bind Items to your profile inventory" (if you cannot find this link, cause you are off on some other page no one knows where, just go back to the left hand menu, click Characters, then manage his/her inventory.)

To add a bag, click on the slot you want to place the bag, like General 8, then in the "Bind an item" menu on the right, click on the radio button next to "Bag", then hit proceed. The next screen you will see the bag was added. Simply click on the bag to "open' it.

Now you can place the items you added to your General Inventory in the bag you added. To place the items in the bag, open the bag, click on an inventory slot in the bag, select the item you want to add from the drop down list, the be sure to hit the "Proceed" button:

Do this for all the items you want to add. You can also add as many bags as you want, up to a limit of 7. Once you get the hand of it, it goes easily.

Manage his/her abilities & Manage his/her skills & Languages ~ Click on these links, and add the skills you need. Pretty easy here so I wont go into detail about it. Just be sure you save before leaving any screens.

To get the URL link to your profile~ Click on the left hand menu link Characters, then on Manage his/her profile. In the bottom table, is the link to your profile. Just click on it, and it will open in a new window.

From there you can add it to your EZBoard profile etc.

That's basically it. As you play around with Magelo more you figure things out as you go. Hope this info helps!
