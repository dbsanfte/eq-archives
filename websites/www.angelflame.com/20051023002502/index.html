:: August 2005 ::  Dunbee's Epic Fight (Submitted by Goldeneyes)~

Here's the picture of Dunbee, Rezzoire ( a cleric we picked up ), and myself in front of the corpse of Dunbee's final epic fight. As you may or may not know, once you start the timer on the final fight of the Monk epic hit, if you don't get it done in time, the ability for the monk to own his 1.0 epic is gone forever, so I feel this is a Kodak Moment ^-^. Another round of 'Gratz!' to Dunbee for getting his 1.0 Epic as of August 13, 2005!!
