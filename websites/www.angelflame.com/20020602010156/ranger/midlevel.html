Shazda Kaekwon says 'There are many threats currently facing this village. The kobolds of Clan Kolbok are becoming bolder in pushing the boundaries of the territory in which they usually hunt. A few of the kobolds now hunt recklessly, killing for pleasure instead of sustenance. [Rognarog] the Infuriated is the most murderous of such kobolds. Then there are the [heretics] that have been invading both Clan Kolbok and Kejek territories alike, practicing their dark sorceries as a show of devotion to their faceless god.

You say, 'What about the Heretics?'

Should you face Rognarog and release him from this life I will reward you for his severed head.

The captain wants you to hunt Goblin Battlemasters. They sometimes drop Battlemaster Bracelets. Turn in 4 of the Bracelets to the Captain, and you will receive a random piece of Faydark Ringmain Armor, which has the same AC as Ringmail/Chainmail, plus stat modifiers and has a green color to it, very similar in color to Ivy Etched.

The Goblin Battlemasters in Lake of Ill Omen do drop the Bracelets that can be turned into the Captain for the Armor.
