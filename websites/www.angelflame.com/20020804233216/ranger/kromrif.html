Kromrif Military Leggings Information & Guide

 

Starts in: Great Divide Who:  Voresh the Hunter
Minimum Level: 45+ Reward: Kromrif Military Leggings

Quest Items: 

  • Ancient Coldain Scepter
  • Blood Wolf Harness
  • Gem of Persuasion
  • Harness of Control

Additional Zones:

  • Crystal Caverns
  • Thurgadin
  • Great Divide

Related Creatures:

  • Drakkel Blood Wolf
  • Shardwurm Broodmother
  • Voresh the Hunter
  • Erdarf Restil
  • Fergul Frostsky
  • Queen Drachnia

This quest starts with Voresh the Hunter. He is located in the Great Divide. You go through the Shardwurm Tunnels to the other side then go right and you will see his tent. (You ARE a Ranger - Track for him!!)

You will need to turn in a Blood Wolf Harness & a Gem of Persuasion to Voresh the Hunter in order to get the Harness of Control. To get the Drakkel Blood Wolf to spawn for the Blood Wolf Harness, camp near the Wizard Spires in GD, and kill all the Drakkel Dire Wolves you can. Keep an eye on tracking, as the Blood Wolf can and will spawn at anytime. (The Dire Wolves are place holders for the Blood Wolf). I at level 43 Fear kited the Blood Wolf without to many problems.

 To get the Gem of Persuasion, you need to go to Crystal Caverns, and camp the Queen Drachnia for the Scepter she drops. I am also told instead of using the scepter you can use a bottle of Karsinic Acid from random Orcs in Crystal Caverns. (I had used the Scepter.) After you get the Scepter/Acid - head to Thurgadin and go past the bank down the ramp and look on your left (Again you are a Ranger - Track!!) look for Erdarf Restil (he will be directly behind the door) in the pub. Give him the Scepter/Acid and he will give you the Gem of Persuasion and despawn.

Now it is time to get the Harness of Control. I would suggest Camo'ing yourself and taking the long route to Voresh the Hunter's tent. Make sure there aren't any Shardwurms near you, un-Invis and hand Voresh both the Gem and the Blood Wolf Harness. He will in return give you the Harness of Control. Quickly re-Invis yourself and head back to a safe area like Thurgadin.

This next part of the Quest took the longest for me. Waiting for the Broodmother to spawn. I got to this part of the Quest early in the Morning on a Sunday, and the Broodmother didn't spawn till after 6:00 PM that night. This also (to me anyway) the most exciting part of the Quest. You have to run into the Shardwurm tunnels, find the Broodmother and give her the Harness of Control, and then get out again. If you do a track before giving the Broodmother the harness, you will see Frugal is up and cons blue to levels 43-44ish. After you give the Broodmomma the Harness, Frugal despawns and respawn with the leggings and conned yellow to me (level 43-44ish). At this point, the Broodmother will then head to the small giant Fort right up above the Wizard Spires and start to kill the other named Frost Giant. Frugal sees her killing his buddy, decides he is in for it next and takes off toward the Eastern Wastes zone line. If Frugal makes it to the zone line, he will despawn and you will have to start the Quest completely over. This is where having your friends/guild mates comes in handy. Have someone (if not everyone) Snare/Root/Nuke/Shoot Frugal to get aggro and keep him from reaching the zone line. Then you can proceed to kill him and get the leggings. On my experience (twice now, once fro a guild member and once for myself) the Broodmother had become frugal's "Pet" instead of despawning when she killed the other named Giant. And she will attack anyone that gets close to Frugal. Best way to kill Frugal is to Snare him, the have Wizzy's, Druids and the such Nuke him til dead. The Broodmomma killed me when my Guild and I were working to get me the leggings. (I got to close to her *sigh*)

After Frugal is killed, the Broodmother will immediately despawn. Keep in mind the Leggings are NO DROP, so have the person the Quest was completed for loot Frugal. Be sure to do it quick so no one outside your group loots them.

The Quest wasn't that hard to do, and I thank my Guild Members and Lords of War for helping me obtain mine! (I love them!!)

Good luck to everyone attempting this Quest - the reward is most definitely worth the effort!!

Tips and Tricks for the Kromrif Military Leggings Quest:

  • When you are ready to give the Harness of Control to the Broodmother, best bet is to get a few friends and or Guild mates to wait near the zone line to EW on the hill. You don't want Fergul Frostsky to get to the EW zone and despawn.
  • To give the Harness of Control to the Broodmother, stand directly behind her (kinda on her tail) come un-Invis, drop the harness on her and then re-Invis yourself immediately and get outta there.
  • Remember, this Quest is a bit buggy (and from what I have heard the GM's deny that completely) and allot of times, the Broodmother becomes Fergal's pet and will attack you. She will only attack you when you are close to her or Fergal, so have some good Nuker's with you to help take Frugal down more safely.
  • I have heard and read allot of people being kill stealed after they turn in the Harness of Control. as Fergul re-spawns with the leggings on him, and others at this point steal the kill. one way to help prevent this is with friends/guild members camping near the Giant Fort while you turn in the Harness to the Broodmomma.
