Cleric Epic Quest

Below you will find a "Walk-Through" of the Cleric Epic Quest for the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh. Most of the information for the Cleric Epic Quest I found on other Websites like Storm over Norrath, EQ Casters Realm, EQ Cleric and by doing searches on the web. The credit DOES NOT go to me. I just organized it, re-worded parts of it, and spell checked it.

Part 1 - Obtain the Orb of Frozen Water

 

Zone: Lake Rathetear, Antonica
NPC/Creatures:
  • Lord Bergurgle
  • Shmendrik Lavawalker
  • Natasha Whitewater
  • Spirit of Flame

 

NPC/Creature: Lord Bergurgle

Level: 39-40

Location: Lake Rathetear, loc (2800, 150) in underwater cave. Cave entrance is, loc (2831, -912) .

Spawn Time: Rare spawn with a placeholder on a  28 minute  timer. Surrounding Dark Water goblins are on 6 minute timer. "Deep", a level 60 mob (beta neutral faction), can spawn in the tower nearby.

Hit Points: Approx. 1494

Melee Damage: Max. 97

Spells Cast: Complete Heal , does dispel

Loot: Lord Bergurgle's Crown

Notes: The spawn site is very wet. Take someone along who can cast enduring breath, and plan on bringing fish scales. Do not cast an AoE or it will aggro Natasha. Do not kill her, unless you want to camp Lord Bergurgle again. 

 

Give "Lord Bergurgle's Crown" to Shmendrik Lavawalker in Lake Rathetear, loc (3600, 0) and you will receive "Oil of Fennin Ro." Natasha Whitewater will attack him immediately after spawning. When he dies, kill the resulting "Spirit of Flame" and loot the "Damaged Goblin Crown."

 

NPC/Creature: a Spirit of Flame

Level: 40

Location: Lake Rathetear

Spawn Time: Spawns immediately in place when Shmendrik Lavawalker dies

Hit Points: Approx. 3300

Melee Damage: Max. 150

Spells Cast: None

Loot: Damaged Goblin Crown

Notes: Natasha Whitewater will attack the "a spirit of flame", which may in some cases cause its corpse to disappear. It may be possible to keep her out of the fight using root, mesmerize, atone, or something similar, but the safest way to do the quest is to kill Shmendrik Lavawalker and the "a spirit of flame" once to get the Damaged Goblin Crown, then wait for a second Shmendrik spawn to turn in Bergurgle's crown and complete this part of the quest.

Give the "Damaged Goblin Crown" to Natasha Whitewater, and you will receive the "Ornate Sea Shell. (She despawns after receiving "Damaged Goblin Crown.")

 

Zone: Timorous Deep, Kunark
NPC/Creatures: Omat Vastsea

Find Omat Vastsea in Timorous Deep, loc (-11567, -2227). To reach him, take the cave entrance at loc (-11530, -3728), then the entrance at (-11336, -1307). Give him the "Ornate Sea Shell " and you will receive the "Coral Statue of Tarew."

 

Zone: Temple of Solusek Ro, Antonica
NPC/Creatures:
  • a seeker
  • Plasmatic Priest

Find "a seeker" in Temple of Solusek Ro, loc (326,38). Give the "Coral Statue of Tarew" to "a seeker" and kill the "Plasmatic Priest" that spawns and loot the "Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe."

 

NPC/Creature: Plasmatic Priest

Level: 55

Location: Temple of Solusek Ro, loc (326,38).

Spawn Time: Spawns at 9am, despawns at 9pm (game time).

Hit Points: Approx. 3300

Melee Damage: Max. 150

Spells Cast: Complete Heal, Tremor, Annul Magic, Reckoning

Loot: Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe

Notes: AE Tremor spell can aggro surrounding temple mobs on him. If they get the kill, you lose the corpse and have to start over. Pulling him to the zone after turning in the statue is one way to avoid this result. He summons, so be sure not to damage him until you reach the zone. If you jump down from the upper level on the way to the zone, he has been observed to teleport directly to your group. Another potential way to manage the situation is to use Atone or another memory wipe spell to clear aggro on the mobs. Appears to be impervious to stun.

 

Zone: Solusek A, Antonica
NPC/Creatures:
  • Lord Gimblox

Find "Lord Gimblox" in Solusek A, loc (-796, -366).

 

NPC/Creature: Lord Gimblox

Level: 30

Location: Solusek A, loc (-796, -366) .

Spawn Time: Rare spawn, 19 minute timer, placeholder is a "Large Fire Goblin" or "fire goblin shaman". Clearing surrounding king/torturer spawn areas MAY increase spawn rate by some reports, though it is more likely to be strictly random.

Hit Points: Approx. 1800

Melee Damage: Max. 76

Spells Cast: None

Loot: Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring

Notes: He does not spawn in the king room. He spawns near the king room in the throne room. That is the room near the king room. This is the room that starts higher up near the king room. The room has two entrances, one is the door from the small room that has entrance to the secret hall and to the king room, the other entrance is into a small room with 2 Solusek spawns. When facing the room just entering from the entrance near the king room, there are three spawns, on on the left, one on the right, and one in front of you. The PH is the one on the left.

 

Zone: Timorous Deep, Kunark
NPC/Creatures: Omat Vastsea

Find Omat Vastsea again in Timorous Deep, loc (-11567, -2227). Give him the "Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe" and you will receive the "Orb of Frozen Water." Natasha Whitewater will spawn. **Note: Complete step 1 of "Orb of Clean Water" before zoning...

Part 2 - Obtain the Orb of Clean Water

Immediately after completing the previous step, turn in "Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring" to Natasha Whitewater in Lake Rathetear and receive the "Ornate Sea Shell".

 

Zone: Burning Woods, Kunark
NPC/Creatures:
  • Naxot Deepwater
  • Ixiblat Fer

Find Naxot Deepwater in Burning Woods, loc (3400, -2150). Give him the "Ornate Sea Shell" and you will receive the "Message to Natasha." Next, find Ixiblat Fer in Burning Woods, loc (-2000, 1500). Kill him and loot the "Scepter of Ixiblat Fer."

 

NPC/Creature: Naxot Deepwater

Level: 65

Location: Burning Woods, loc (-2000, 1500) .

Spawn Time: Triggered spawn by a cleric turning in an item to Naxot Deepwater.

Hit Points: Approx. 32000

Melee Damage: Max. 575

Spells Cast: AE Fire  300 pt dd, same as Noble Djorn/Overseer of Air, resistible

Loot: Scepter of Ixiblat Fer

Notes: This is a high level (52+), multi-group (24-30 person) encounter. Most people pull him to the Skyfire zone before fighting, and report that he follows pullers reliably without Aggroing additional mobs. His magic resistance is reportedly average to below average for his level, and he is NOT immune to fire-based spells. Mage fire pets will be immune to his AE fire proc, however. He will attempt to flee when low on health, and he apparently has a high regen rate which will take effect near the end of the fight if he is not finished quickly. Slowing/Debuffing him is highly recommended. Charmed Wurms do good damage as well. He is extremely MR. but not impossible to land spells. The key is to get a slow on him as he hits for 585max. Take a warrior with defensive, and a minimum of 4 clerics. Have the warrior tip defensive off and solo him for a good 20-30 seconds while the clerics chain cast CH (at 3 sec intervals) all this time have the chanter chain casting slow till it lands. Once the war has built aggro up, get all the pets in and send in the other tanks, don't even bother trying to land anything else bar wizzy lures.

 

Zone: Chardok, Kunark
NPC/Creatures:
  • Overking Bathezid Di'Zok

 

NPC/Creature: Overking Bathezid Di'Zok

Level: 63+

Location: Chardok, Kunark

Spawn Time: 2 Hours, NO known place holder

Hit Points: Approx. 32000

Melee Damage: Uber (approx. max 385)

Spells Cast: Fiery Death (AE, approx 500 hp Dmg, cancels 1 buff), Complete Heal, Gravity Flux, Gate

Loot: Singed Scroll which reads, "Greetings Overking Bathezid. The time has come for you to return the boon owed to me from so long ago. I have need of your most powerful summoners to bring Ixiblat Fer into the mortal realm and rain fire upon my enemies. Your old friend, Zordak Ragefire", other drops include Argent Defender (33/49 2h slash), Incarnadine Breastplate, Vibrating Gauntlets/Hammer of Infuse

Notes: This is a high level (55+), multi-group (24-30+ person) encounter. The best location for the fight is a matter of opinion. If you fight in his room, you must be careful not to aggro the queen, plus Gravity Flux may aggro mobs in above rooms (Herbalist room) if they are not kept clear. If you fight in the hallway, he may gate (cannot be interrupted) and begin summoning people to their deaths. Another successful tactic involved clearing up to the Herbalist room and fighting the Overking there. He has approx. 6 guards (avg. level may be ~54) with him. Draining his mana will prevent use of Complete Heal and Gate late in the fight.

 

Zone: Timorous Deep, Kunark
NPC/Creatures:
  • Omat Vastsea

Find Omat Vastsea in Timorous Deep, loc (-11567, -2227). To reach him, take the cave entrance at loc (-11530, -3728), then the entrance at (-11336, -1307). Give him the Scepter of Ixiblat Fer and Singed Scroll and receive the "Orb of Clear Water." **Note: Complete step 1 of "Orb of Clean Vapor" before zoning...

Part 3 - Obtain the Orb of Clean Vapor

Immediately after completing the previous step, give the "Message to Natasha" to Natasha Whitewater in Lake Rathetear to receive "Shimmering Pearl".

 

Zone: Timorous Deep, Kunark
NPC/Creatures:
  • Omat Vastsea

 

NPC/Creature: Zordak Ragefire / Zordakalicus Ragefire

Level: (initial spawn), 55 (after receiving "Shimmering Pearl"), 54 (dragon form)  

Location: Solusek B in back of Nagafen's lair.

Spawn Time: Uncertain. This was posted as an approximation for his spawn formula which may or may not be accurate: "At zone boot-up, do the following- spawn Nagafen, spawn Magus Rokyl, do not spawn Ragefire, set Ragefire-respawn to false. When Magus Rokyl dies, do the following- set Magus Rokyl respawn timer at four game-days, if Nagafen is not up and Ragefire-respawn = false then spawn Ragefire and set Ragefire-respawn to true. When Ragefire dies, do the following- set timer at 16 game-days, if Nagafen is not up at respawn time, then spawn Ragefire, otherwise do not respawn Ragefire and set Ragefire-respawn to false." Alternately, it is speculated "that his spawn is 0-65 hours (72 is the max I bet as one of the developers posted they can delay pops for up to 3 days) after naggy dies. Then after you kill him it is 23-24 hours."

Loot: Initially, he is a vendor that sells Imbue Fire Opal (Solusek Ro deity-specific spell) and Oil of Fennin Ro (~1700pp). In dragon form, always drops "Heart of Zordak Ragefire", plus one or more pieces of "Blazing" armor and Nagafen loot table items, including red dragon scales. Known "Blazing" items include Blazing Greaves of Fennin Ro, Blazing Vambraces, and a breastplate (AC 20, +5 WIS, +5 INT, +8 CHA, +20 SV FIRE, plate classes/all races but Iksar, effect: Sunbeam).

Notes: After being given the pearl, Zordak Ragefire will attack you. When he is killed, his true (dragon) form, Zordakalicus Ragefire, will spawn on the spot where he dies. Zordakalicus Ragefire is a clone of Lord Nagafen, but will not banish level 53+ characters. Normal Nagafen tactics apply, except that he runs at the end of the fight, as in the above picture, and may hit somewhat harder than Nagafen.

 Find Omat Vastsea in Timorous Deep, loc (-11567, -2227). To reach him, take the cave entrance at loc (-11530, -3728), then the entrance at (-11336, -1307). Give him the "Heart of Zordak Ragefire" and you will receive the "Orb of Vapor" and Jhassad Oceanson will spawn. **Note: Complete step 1 of "Orb of the Triumvirate" before zoning...

Part 4 - Obtain the Orb of the Triumvirate

Immediately after completing the previous step, turn in the "Orb of Frozen Water," "Orb of Clear Water," and "Orb of Vapor" to Jhassad Oceanson, receiving the "Orb of the Triumvirate" and resulting in the "Avatar of Water" to spawn.

Part 5 - Obtain the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh

Immediately after completing the previous step, turn in the "Orb of the Triumvirate" to the Avatar of Water, receiving "Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh".
