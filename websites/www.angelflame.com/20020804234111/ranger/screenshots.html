Update 8/13/2001: New screenshots for anyone interested!! I finally remembered to bring a Zip Disk with me to Schmoo's house so I could get some of the screenshots off my hard drive!! Well I did, and here they are...

 

  

I found a few Screenshots on my PC at home, nothing grand as most of my nice shoots are on my PC at Xthisu's house, and I haven't been able to get those yet. But here is what I have...

 

 

This past Weekend we had quite a bit of fun. Xthisu & I grouped up with Lord Zarf & Nuriel to take on some of those nasty Shard Wurms in the Great Divide. On Saturday, we headed over to Eastern Wastes and tackled the infamous Ry'gorr Clan.

  

Sunday brought a day of fun for myself... Xthisu (being the Lizard she is)  decided to work on her faction with Freeport, hoping to be able to bank there. So she took up camp at the Orc Camp in West Commons, while I sat at the Toll Booth, Giving out free SoW's to anyone that would poke me. I was also camping the Hill Giants, Griffins, & Dragoon Zytl. (yes I killed Gix about every time he spawned!) I was 39 when we arrived that morning, and thanks to good 'ol Dragoon, Dinged into 40 mid-afternoon. I got some nice screenshots, and the one of Dragoon is perfect... (he feared me right after I took that picture)

 

 

After a nasty fight with a Hill Giant, we were taken surprise by a second Hill Giant, and unfortunately, the Hill Giant killed Xthisu. The next bad thing was, X was bound all the way in Thurgadin!! *sigh* - but then, luck would come our way in the form of a level 59 Cleric... She was kind enough to Exp Rez Xthisu!! She didn't even ask for any compensation! She then buffed us with some crazy spells that put my Hit Points to almost 2000!

 

After Xthisu obtained about 6 backpacks full of Deathfist Belts, We headed towards Freeport and paid a high level Druid to cast Wolf Form on X... He then was able to turn the Belts in and gain the Faction he needed to enter Freeport West... *sigh* still need to do more work to get into Freeport East and to the bank...

 

 

Later that night, Xthisu & I headed back to Eastern Wastes to take on a few more of the Ry'gorr Clan... Lord Buil was on his way to meet us there and offer his support. We got an opportunity to slay a Manticore. Let me tell you, this thing Con'd blue to me at level 40, but it was one tuff beast! If it wasn't for Buil's Healing and X's Spells - I'm sure I would have died...

Camping the Tizmaks in the Great Divide... These wonderful little beast have leveled Xthisu & I from 35ish to 39 now... Great Exp and the loot isn't that bad either!! Nuriel is a big help as well...
