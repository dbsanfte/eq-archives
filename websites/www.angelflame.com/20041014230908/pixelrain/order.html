How to Order Custom Character Art from Pixelrain:

Ordering Custom Character Art from Pixelrain is easy! Just choose the best payment/delivery method that is best for you from below!  

I have been asked if I will accept in game Currency (Everquest Platinum) as payment, and Yes, You can pay me with in-game Platinum. However, I will ONLY accept Platinum on the Stromm Server at this time. Rule of thumb on Currency Exchange: $10.00 USD = 10,000 Platinum Pieces on the Stromm Server. If you have any Questions regarding paying with in-game Platinum, please do not hesitate to email me by clicking HERE.

Option 1:  PayPal (with e-mail delivery):

  • Click on the "" Button on the Character Art Page you are interested in buying.
  • I will email you the design set as soon as payment has been confirmed. Most orders filled within a week (depending on backlog).
  • If you have already added items to your cart, and are finished shopping, please click the "View cart" button below, to complete your order, and send the payment.

  • Download the  Order Form, print and complete all required fields.
