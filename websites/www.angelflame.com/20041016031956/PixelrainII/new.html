New Art from Pixelrain!

Here you will find all the latest creations from Pixelrain.

Tamber Trell :: Ayonae Ro Server ~ Tamber was a great model to do. I did something a bit different with her, and added some "Charm Bird" Wings. It turned out better then I had hoped for.

I did two versions for her, one with the Wings, and one with out.


Ripcord :: Outrider of Xev ~ Ripcord was a fun collection to do. I took quite a few screenshots of his character to get an idea of how I wanted his set to look. I finally decided on an "Action" pose. The background is the Plane of Growth.

 

 


Nikori Crimsonblade :: Warrior ~ This is another Siggy & Magelo I did for Nikori. The background is from the Omens of War Bata.

The Magelo on the right is also of Nikori, where I used a Screenshot from Doom3 as the background.


Sivaron of the Stromm Server ~ Sivaron wanted his Siggy/Magelo of his character in an animation. Of all the screenshots I took of him, this was the one that looked the best.

 

 

Kelrahn, Druid of Stromm ~ I reused some of my older images in Kelrahn's Art. I added a few spell effects, and highlighting effects. I used Jasc Paintshop Pro and other various programs.
