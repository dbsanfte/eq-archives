Magelo Backgrounds are the images you see behind a Character's Magelo Profile (Magelo Website). Click HERE to see an example, Crimsonrain Noirsoul, Shadowknight on Vazaelle. Magelo backgrounds allow you to illustrate your character with more then just a Profile.

Please click on an image to see the full size. Hold your mouse curser over an image to see a short description about the work.
