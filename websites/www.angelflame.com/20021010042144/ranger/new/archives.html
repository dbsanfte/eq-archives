Welcome to the News Archive Section

  September 3rd, 2002

My Time Off... It has been a few months now, and I have been on leave from Rainangel, Eternal Advocates, EQ and the Ranger's Resource. I did post a retirement post on the glade, but I never did update this site with the news. A lot of people asked me why I left EQ, and to be honest, it was just Burn Out. I was tired of the Exp Grinding, AA Points and the kill, kill, heal, kill, kill, heal. I love my Guild Eternal Advocates, and the people in it, EA is what had kept me going that long...

During my time off from EQ, I played a bit of Ultima Online again, reactivating my old account on the Atlantic Shard. I did finally reach GM Taming, and was even able to get a 110 Power Scroll and raise my Taming to 106.3+. One goal I do want to complete in UO is to Tame a Fire Steed, and I plan to do that soon. But again with UO, which caused me to leave the first time for EQ, is lack of content and other things to do. So UO has become dull once again.  =(

Time for a return... I miss EA, I miss my Ranger, Rainangel, and I miss the fun with raiding, and the friends I have grown to love in EQ. This passed week, I have been logging in more and more, and started a new Twink just to have some fun again. Who knows what Rainangel's future holds, I know I don't know. I do know, I want to have fun again, and I plan to spend more time in EQ and possibly reach my goals I set for Rain so long ago... The future is really wide open and I look onto it, with open eyes and a curious mind.

Yes, I will once again be updating and maintaining the Ranger's Resource as much as I can. This site is a passion, it always has been, and I see the ranger's Resource and Rainangel as one in the same.  =)
