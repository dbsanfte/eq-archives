About Me~ It is no secret that I really cannot free hand draw. It is just not a talent or skill I have taken on so well. But I do enjoy, and am getting good at manipulating/creating images on my computer. I first discovered how much I enjoy creating/building in a game back some years ago. The game was Command & Conquer: Red Alert. I took it from there to other games and to the web with the numerous websites I have designed.

After years of playing around in programs like Jasc Paint Shop and others, and creating lots of different images, I decided to create a website to display them in. After all, I do love to create websites!

So here is that collection, some new and bright, and some old where I was learning bigger and better things.

This is My Current Passion. Where I spend most of my time when I am not in Everquest kicking critters arses along side my second family, Eternal Advocates~
