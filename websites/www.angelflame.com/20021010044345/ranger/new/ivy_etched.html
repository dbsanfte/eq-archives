Ivy Etched Armor Quest

Below you will find detailed information regarding all the parts to the Ranger Ivy Etched Armor Quest.

Ivy Etched Bracers
Starts in: Kithacor Forest
Who: Gandari


You will need:

  • The bottom of the Rune of Ivy (from Orc named Redwind in Everfrost)

  • A watery ring (from a tidal lord in Dagnor's Cauldron)

  • 1 Fire Emerald

Redwind in Everfrost

Redwind is considered a Shadow Knight. Because of this, he has harm touch. For those of you that don’t know, that means 99% of the time, he will open up with a direct magic based attack (resistible, but rarely). He also likes to use the darkness series of SK spells (slows (like snare) the target, and also DoT’s them) and will fear from time to time. Redwind always drops the Rune of Ivy needed for the bracer. Using harmony on the other Orcs is a very effective move, only Redwind will come if used correctly. He can be soloed in the mid 30s without much problems (this was before rangers got SoW at 30 mind you, probably even easier now), or a group of mid to high 20s could kill him.

 

Aqua Goblin Tidal Lord in Dagnor's Cauldron

If you are able to kill Redwind, the Tidal Lord should be no problem. Be careful kiting this guy as the land here is rough and a sharp fall can kill you. There is a goblin camp outside the “hallway” to Unrest. If things go bad, stay near there, so you have a zone to run to for safety. Also, you can almost always find a nice soul to help you that has just been trained out of Unrest.

 

Ivy Etched Helm
Starts in: Kithacor Forest
Who: Gandari


You will need:

  • Top of the Rune of Ivy (from a Deathly Usher in Mistmoore)

  • Cyclops Charm (from Tarskuk in Rathe Mountains)

  • 1 Sapphire

Tarskuk in Rathe Mountains

Tarskuk is a named Cyclops. As you might have learned, all the “bigger” races run as
fast as Sow. Tarskuk is suppose to run faster then Sow. From what I understand, he dropped the necklace 3/4 times. Tarskuk was quite easy to get to spawn.
Kill all of the lizards, Skellie's, and Cyclops around the gypsy camp near the Lake Rathe zone line.

A Deathly Usher in Mistmoore

The Usher was found most commonly in the Jail area. There are a lot of spawns in this area sometimes, and it can get crazy. If you have trouble getting a group, there are 2 spawns in room number 14 that you can camouflage into, if you can manage to harmony pull or root park one, you should be able to hold the room (watch for trains) until you get the Usher. Also, MM is one of the most often trained zones. If you stay camo’ed (undead can see though camouflage, beware this) at the zone line, trash is always trained to the zone, if you watch, Ushers will almost always come with the deep trains. If someone takes out the train, the rune could drop off them, and if you ask nicely, they might let you loot (is NO DROP by the way).
