Quests for the Ranger (levels below 25)

 

I have received quite a few requests to add lower level Quests for Rangers. This is not a complete listing by any means... Most of these Quests have been taken from Allakhazam's site.

If you have discovered a Quest, and would like it added to Angel's Ranger Guide, Please Submit the quest by clicking HERE...

The Trueshot Bow Quest is also a great quest to do once you get about 15th Level...

Tattered Armor

*This Quest was submitted by Garryck of the Vazaelle Server*

Starting NPC: Ran Sunfire
Starting Zone: Greater Faydark
Minimum level: 5

Speak with the Ran Sunfire in the Kelethin Ranger Guild, and she will give you a bag. Combine 6 Pixie Dusts in the bag and return it to her. Return for experience, faction with TunaresScouts and money and an item, usually a piece of patchwork armor, but sometimes leather or rawhide.

A Tarnished Battle Axe

*This Quest was submitted by Garryck of the Vazaelle Server*

Starting NPC: Ranger Guild in Kelethin
Starting Zone: Greater Faydark
Minimum level: 5

Take 2 Orc Hatchets to the Male Trainer in the Kelethin Ranger Guild, and he returns 1-2 "A Tarnished Battle Axe" (6/40, 1HS) to you along with some exp. If you do not need the returned axes the merchants buy them for 1.8gp or so.

Bearskin Gloves

Starting NPC: Ged Twigborn
Starting Zone: Kithacor Forest
Minimum level: 7

Bearskin Gloves
AC 5
WT: 2.1
Class: WAR CLR PAL RNG SHD DRU MNK BRD ROG SHM
Race: ALL

There is an NPC named Ged Twigborn who hangs around another NPC and they will wander the forest at times. I usually see them in one of two places: either along the same wall as the zone to the Commonlands, or by turning on the path off the main road from the sign pointing towards Freeport.

Ged will make you some gloves for a price. The Bearskin Gloves cost 25 gold and a high quality bear skin.

Blackburrow Brewers

Starting NPC: Larsk Juton
Starting Zone: Surefall Glade
Minimum level: 12
Cloak of the Jaggedpine
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM NO DROP
AC 6
DEX +3
Class: RNG DRU
Race: HUM ELF HEF HFL

After you turn in Gnasher's head to Larsk Juton, he will sometimes say that he requires your services to [assist in the extermination of the gnoll brewers]. If you say the magic words, he elaborates. He tells you to go to Blackburrow to kill the gnoll brewers, and bring back any Blackburrow Casks. "Upon your third cask I shall reward you the [Cloak of Jaggedpine].

The casks are found on the Gnoll Master Brewers and "A gnoll brewer" which are a rare spawn at the end of the bottom bridge by the barrels in Blackburrow. The reward is the Cloak of Jaggedpine . He hints that one should hold on to it, because "you may never know when we may alter the enchantments placed upon the cloak."

Fish Dinner

Starting NPC: Roary Fishcatcher
Starting Zone: Kerra Island
Minimum level: 13
Kerran Fishing Spear
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM
DAMAGE 5 DELAY 30
DEX +2 AGI +2
SKILL: PIERCING
EFFECT: Enduring Breath
CHARGES: 3
Class: Ranger Shaman Shadow knight Bard Rogue Warrior
Race: ALL RACES

Roary Fishcatcher, in the hut immediately to your right upon entering Kerra Isle, is an excellent chef and loves to prepare different types of fish. He wishes you to venture to Nektulos Forest to hunt the Darkwater Piranha and return it to him for a reward. The fish swim in the Nektulos Forest River (they are a very rare spawn) and sometimes drop a Raw Darkwater Piranha Fish when killed. Bring back this delicacy and your reward is a Kerran Fishing Spear. Roary also tells you that he can recharge the enduring breath when it runs out.

Jaggedpine Croak Staff

Starting NPC: Corum Finisc
Starting Zone: Surefall Glade
Minimum level: 12
Jaggedpine Croak Staff
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM
DAMAGE 5 DELAY 31
SKILL: 1HBLUNT
STR +1 HP +5 SV FIRE +5
WT: 8.5
Class: RNG DRU
Race: HUM ELF HEF HFL

This quest starts in Surefall Glades. Talk with Corum Finisc, just outside the Jaggedpine of Surefall Glades. After some keywords, he will mention [crook]. This is a Jaggedpine crook staff that is broken in pieces in Qeynos Hills. Only Gnoll Hunters have the parts. The gnoll hunters are very rare and getting the staff pieces is difficult. However, once you have both, go back to Corum Finisc and he will assemble them for you.

Lionskin Gloves

Starting NPC: Ged Twigborn
Starting Zone: Surefall Glade
Minimum level: 14

Lionskin Gloves
AC 5
SV COLD +2
WT: 1.1
Class: WAR CLR PAL RNG SHD DRU MNK BRD ROG SHM
Race: ALL

There is an NPC named Ged Twigborn who hangs around another NPC and they will wander the forest at times. I usually see them in one of two places: either along the same wall as the zone to the Commonlands, or by turning on the path off the main road from the sign pointing towards Freeport.

Ged will make you some gloves for a price. The Lionskin gloves cost 96 gold and a high quality lion skin.

In order to get the Lionskin gloves, you need a high faction with Ged, which you can get by killing Shralok Orcs in the Forest. High Quality Lion Skins are dropped by Highland Lions in East Karana. I have been told that a tiger spawns some times in the south west corner of Kithacor Forest near the Orc spawn and gives a high quality skin as well.

Screaming Mace

Starting NPC: A Dwarven Smith
Starting Zone: Crushbone
Minimum level: 18
Screaming Mace
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM
DAMAGE 8 DELAY 35
SKILL: 1HBLUNT
EFFECT: Screaming Mace (Yaulp)
WT: 8.0
Class: Ranger Shaman Druid Cleric Paladin Shadow knight Bard Monk Rogue Warrior
Race: All

You need to get a Dwarven Ringmail Tunic (found on Crush) and a Bracer of Battle (found on the Orc Warlord) to complete this quest.

You are also supposed to have a Shiny Brass Shield (found on the Orc Trainer), but you should be able to do it without one. Then, go to the Slaver hut with the Dwarven Smith and the Elvin Priest.

First talk to the Dwarf smith, who talks about armor and asks for the Shiny Shield. If you have the shield you can give it to him and he will ask for the tunic. Give it to him, and you will receive a dwarven mace. Alternatively, if you don't have the shield or don't want to give it up (Why would you), you need to say to the smith "the king is dead" and then ask him "what about ringmail".

He will then ask for the tunic and give you the mace. Once you have the dwarven mace, talk to the Elvin Priest and say "the King is dead". He will then ask for the Bracer. If you give it to him, he gives you a Prayer Cloth of Tunare.

Then give both the mace and the prayer cloth to the Priest, and he gives you back a Screaming Mace, an 8/35 magical

Tiny Skeletons

Starting NPC: Narron Jenork
Starting Zone: Ak'Anon
Minimum level: 15

Watchman Boots
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM
AC 5
STA +5
EFFECT: Spirit of the Wolf
CHARGES: 1 
WT: 6.5
Class: WAR CLR PAL RNG SHD DRU MNK BRD ROG SHM
Race: ALL

This quest originates in Ak'Anon with Narron Jenork at the Gemchoppers Guile, but I don't think you really have to start it there. You need good faction with the Gemchoppers to complete the quest. There is a gnome guard named Watchman Dexlin that sometimes spawns at the stone bridge in North Karana.

Bilbis Briar is his placeholder, and he is a fairly rare spawn. Hail him and he says he's in need of a brave warrior. Say I am a brave warrior and he will tell you that he is looking for the remains of tiny skeletons. If you have amiable or better faction for the Gemchoppers he gives you a box with 4 slots and tells you to collect the remains (and you will take a hit with your gemchopper faction for some reason). After he gives you the box tiny skeletons will spawn between the stone bridge and the gypsy camp somewhere. They're around level 15. Kill them and they drop a crumpled skeleton remains.

He tells you to return with them before his sundial reaches 8, and I have been told that if you don't do this fast enough he will not give you anything for the box, but I am not sure how the timing is supposed to work. The tiny skeletons are hard to find, they're as small as brownies so a ranger with tracking would be a big help on this quest. When you get four and skeletons, combine them in the box and give it back to him. He will then give you back a box and tell you to return it to Narron Jenork in Ak'Anon. When you do, he gives you a nice pair of Watchman Boots.
