Meet Angel (Myself!!)

 

 So you have come here to learn about me eh? Well this might be long winded so grab a cup of coffee, sit back and relax and read on!

 

Strange how things tend to come full circle when you least expect them to. I received an email from someone anonymous with only a few hints at who it is... "A Shinning Knight", IDEM - yes, ALWAYS.

~Angel

Angel actually came to be back in Ultima Online as AngelFish on Atlantic. She was born to become a Grandmaster Fisher but became a lifetime for myself. After working her fishing so much, she had gotten the perfect Stats for an Archer/Warrior and thus my passion with Ranger type characters. I Gm'd Archery, Tactics, Swordsmanship, Wrestling, and finally Fishing with her before she retired late 1999ish.

After selling my account, I moved on to SphereServer (TUS at the time) and with the help of my now boyfriend Schmoo, created my own UO world named AngelFire. AngelFire was a great Player Run Shard and I learned allot about UO, scripting, and why things do and don't work within the world of UO. After about a year of uptime for AngelFire, Schmoo & I felt it was time to move on... (not to mention the cheating and lying some of the players of AngelFire were doing to destroy the shard *sigh*) *Fate & Paisley, We love and miss you!* We took AngelFire down for good in December of 2000.

Now with my new Desktop PC (The machine we ran AngelFire on), I returned to OSI with a new account. (What was I thinking???) I came back in complete shock at the new "Facets" of UO. What the heck is Trammel and Fellucia??? What??? 100k for a black dye tub? are you crazy??? Well after my initial shock, I adjusted.  I then ran into the people that bought my original AngelFish account, and they happily returned the account to me. (Really nice people!) The account was stripped of items and homes I had owned before, but I got my beloved AngelFish back! I then rebuilt the account, and created a new character - Akara (GM Tamer to be). I never did manage to GM her in Taming, got really close though (98.3) and had some good fun taming Nightmares and Dragons. But with good 'ol Origin doing wacky things and changing everything... then adding on Third dawn that was sooooo buggy it crashed every 5 minutes no matter what ya did... I felt it was time to leave OSI UO for good this time.

After the dismantling of AngelFire Player Run Shard, Schmoo had taken up playing EverQuest. I had at numerous times turned my nose up at it and continued playing UO but now, I was very interested in the game. I bought the game and installed it... Started a character... Rainangel (AngelFish & AngelFire names were both all ready taken *boo-hoo*) - Wood-Elf Ranger on Vazaelle (the server Schmoo played on). I was off killing bats and anything else near Kelethin and absolutely HATED the game! I didn't play it again for about 3 weeks.

Well I am now level 35 with Rainangel, and just got her a pair of Stormfeather Talons *Finally!* I also have an alt character, Skyeangel, Half-Elf Paladin on Vazaelle. In the banner picture above, those are my two beloved characters.

Well that's my story for the most part. I hope you enjoyed it!   :)

here is something funny to take a look at: My old Guild Web Page from Angels of Blood in Ultima Online Atlantic... I have never had the heart to take it down, as I have some great memories from friends and that game... its also one of the very first Web Page I ever created... when I look at it now... well OUF!!
