  September 25th, 2002

A few things... Mostly personal goals that I reached this last week. First off, the big one... I have been working for this for quite some time now:

Endless Quiver at long last. I am currently using Champion Mithril Arrows, 11 Damage & 150 Range. I have been doing a lot of 500+ damage non-Trueshot Crits since getting Endless Quiver. So for anyone that asks if Endless Quiver is worth the points, I would tell them, YES.  =)

Next to reach goal: Archery Master....

Another one of my goals I wanted to see one day, happened last night on a trip to NToV. We zone into ToV, make groups and all... then we were going to Call of the hero to NToV instead of killing the entrance mobs... Somewhere along the line, we decided to plow through it all, and hope for some Armor drops for those still in need of upgrades.

I never thought I would see it in my EQ lifetime, Just assumed it would never happen, and was content with all the wonderful upgrades Eternal Advocates has bestowed upon me all ready... but... Last night, a Drake of some sort (was to excited to notice what one dropped it) decided to drop a Ancient Tarnished Chain Tunic! I want to say Thank you to EA, this is something I have wanted for so long! /Hugs EA, you guys really are the best!

So now, I wear this little item:

Also last night, a Silk Robe also dropped, and was awarded to Alora in Eternal Advocates! I wanted to say again, a Huge Grats to you hon, the new robe looks really nice!  =)

/Huggles!
