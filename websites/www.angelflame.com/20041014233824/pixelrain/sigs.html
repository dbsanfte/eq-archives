Custom Signature Images by Pixelrain
$12.99 USD (Includes all custom Art Work & Effects) Signatures are images people like to use on message boards. These are the images that appear under the poster/users post text in a message forum.

Making Message Board Signature Images is probably one of my Favorite parts. There are just so many ways to express a character in them.

Nikori Crimsonblade :: Warrior ~ This is another Siggy I did for Nikori. The background is from the Omens of War Bata.
Tamber Trell :: Ayonae Ro Server ~ Tamber was a great model to do. I did something a bit different with her, and added some "Charm Bird" Wings. It turned out better then I had hoped for.
Ripcord :: Outrider of Xev ~ Ripcord was a fun collection to do. I took quite a few screenshots of his character to get an idea of how I wanted his set to look. I finally decided on an "Action" pose.
Nikori Crimsonblade :: Warrior ~ This Siggy is my latest creation. I used a Screenshot from Doom 3 as the background for both the Magelo Profile & the Siggy.

Sivaron of the Stromm Server ~ Sivaron wanted his Siggy/Magelo of his character in an animation. Of all the screenshots I took of him, this was the one that looked the best.
Kelrahn :: Druid of Stromm ~ I reused some of my older images in Kelrahn's Art. I added a few spell effects, and highlighting effects. I used Jasc Paintshop Pro and other various programs.
