Rain Caller Bow Quest

 



Talk to Kinool Goldsinger in the Enchanter Guild and ask him "Where can I get an Enchanted Trueshot Bow?"

Go through the dialog with him and you find that you need several items.

1) Faerie Dust

Although this is in the order you actually get the details, you may want to do all of the Antonica elements first and then do the Faydwer parts to save a couple of boat rides.

You need to talk to princess Jolena of the fairies about this. Asking her about it reveals that one of her wings was stolen by a bald person who smelled of rust and refuse. She asks you to bring her wing back and she'll give you the Fairy Dust.

In Ak'Anon by the Necro Guild there is a Gnome named Sanfyrd Featherhead. If you ask him where Princess Joleena's wing is, he will tell you that you can't have it because he read that it will make his hair grow back. He also mentions that he has no [friends].

Patronize him by saying "I am your friend" at which point he exclaims that you are "just saying that to get my feather, if you are really my friend you would help me get some hair tonic" (or something to that effect).

Converse with him further and he will give you a 10 slot crate to fill with hair tonics. You then have to jump the pond to west Freeport and visit a gnome merchant in a tent outside the west gate by the name of Ping Fuzzlecutter.

Ping sells the tonics you need (called Formula 5000), so just buy ten and combine them. Take the new box (no longer a container) back to Sanfyrd and he will ask one more favor before forking over the wing. He will give you his tattered toupee and ask that you get it fixed by the same man who sold him the tonic (this is the meat and potatoes of the quest).

So once again jump over the pond to West Freeport to talk to good old ping. NOTE: The wording here is important. You need to ask ping "can you repair a toupee?" I tried the word fix and it didn't work. Ping will go into the history of his wig, "The Mane Attraction" and tell you that he can still fix it because it is under warranty.

But in order to fix it you need to fetch him two clumps of hair from a hulking gorilla (the magillas in LTR of Cazic-Thule) and a lock of the golden tresses from a "certain mermaid".

This certain mermaid is a golden haired mermaid (that's the exact name of the NPC) that resides in Kedge. You will absolutely need at least two well balanced 40+ groups to safely do this (kedge is no joke).

Once you retrieve the mermaid hair, you go back to West Freeport and give Ping the 2 clumps of Gorilla hair, the golden locke, and the wig and you will get the mane attraction (ac 4 +10 cha).

Go back to Ak'Anon and give the wig to Sanfyrd to get the glimmering fairy wing.

Take the wing to Princess Joleen and she rewards you with the dust, called a bag of fairy gold dust. I am told she will give you the dust even if she cons apprehensive. Also, you can sneak up on her.

2) A Treant Heart

Wooden Hearts are dropped by Treants, but a Ranger shouldn't attack a Treant. Instead, you can get this by giving lanterns to Jyle Wyndshot in the hogcallers inn in Freeport (which you did for the Trueshot bow quest) and about every fourth lantern or so he gives you a heart instead of just Treant wood.

3) Your Trueshot Longbow

4) 3000gp.
