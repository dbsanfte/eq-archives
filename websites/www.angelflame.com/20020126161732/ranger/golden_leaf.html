Golden Leaf Armor Quest Information and Guide

A visit to Nalelin in Skyshrine will start you on the Quest for Golden Leaf Armor. Visit the Halls of Testing in the Temple of Veeshan with a VERY large party Raid and/or Guild Raid for drops of the "Unadorned Chain"

Return the Unadorned Chain Pieces with the 3 Gems listed below to Nalelin to be rewarded with the Golden Leaf Armor.

November 13th 2001 ~ I would like to thank Fleire Thorns, 60th Warder on Luclin for the great screenshots of his character wearing the Golden Leaf (Skyshrine) Quest Armor, Helm of the Tracker and the Bracer of Benevolence! Please click the thumbnails bellow to see a larger image...
