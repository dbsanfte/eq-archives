June 4th, 2002 ~ Few words: Change's are a coming!

Check back soon... Some good things incoming on the horizon for Angel's

April 30th, 2002 ~ OMG Look!

On Sunday April 28th, at around 10:30PM EST I finally gained my final level of 60! 92 Days Played time. Wow is all I could say. Wow is still all I can say. Its been a really long road. From the days back in the beginning in WC, hunting Wisp's with my first magical weapon (Combine LS), to the days of OT and moving on to Velious (Tizmaks for so many levels), to moving on to bigger and more dangerous things till today!

Rainangel has come such a long way. Along the way I made a lot of friends, had some really great times and took out some nasty mobs. 

One person I really want to thank (that doesn't live with me /hugs Crescention) is Eriul Stormrunner. You Eriul, have played such a huge role in Rain's life you wouldn't believe it. You gave me goals to work for, cheered me up at times I needed it, stood beside me as I advanced behind you, and well, to me have become a great friend. I could never thank you enough for all you have taught me about being a good Ranger.

And to my sweetie, Crescention ~ You, the one that got me out of UO and into EQ. You, the one that taught me how to play the game even when I hated EQ and was to stubborn to listen to you. You, that forged my first not so complete set of banded armor for Rain. You, who stood by me, waking up early to log in with me, PL'ing Eliania behind me, and being the sweet and caring person that you are... I love you babe.

And of course to Eternal Advocates ~ You guys and gals are some of the best people I have ever known in EQ. The way we all work together to accomplish a common goal. I love you guys, and I am finally home inside EA. /Hugs EA!

Well that's it, level 60. Wow. Really Wow. Now its time to move into more leveling by way of Alternate Advancement. I never put any exp into AA, didn't plan to until I hit 60. well now I can. So that is what I have planned for Rain in the future. Going for Archery Master and then eventually, Endless Quiver. As I said, still have a long road ahead of me...

This passed Saturday, EA took a trip up to ToV in hopes of some good armor drops. Well more then armor dropped, as EA dropped both Telkorenar & Gozzrem!

After taking these two nasty dragons down, we moved to the West Wing. Quite a bit of armor dropped, including almost a complete set of Plate! I also was awarded Chain Leggings as well! I was quite excited! It was an awesome trip to ToV and I look forward to many more!

/Hugs!

April 24th, 2002 ~ More stuff inbound for Angel's... I have uploaded the beginnings of the FAQ page for you all to take a look at for starters.

I also added another link to the left... The Ranger's Glade - A long over due link to be added. Great site for Ranger information, and one of if not the best Ranger Message forum on the net. Go drop by and say Hi if you haven't been there yet.

I also have a screenie of a friend of mine, Vashen Stormblade. He was kind enough to allow me to take a screenie of him in most of his Thorny Vine armor (Plane of Hate/Fear armor). I think he is wearing a Barbed Ringmail BP, but you can get an idea of what the Thorny Vine armor looks like on a  Half Elf Ranger: (Click on image to see full size view) /waves at Vashen!

Well that's it for now, I'm still working on other parts of the site to be updated, some screenies and such. Keep sending me those questions now that I got my email working correctly!!

~Rainangel
