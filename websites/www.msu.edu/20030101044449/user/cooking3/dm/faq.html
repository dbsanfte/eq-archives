What does Appotus Dominus mean?

There is actually a story about how we got our name that has a modicum of entertainment value.

Originally, we wanted to be called "Cobra Kai" after the sinister Dojo in the loved Karate Kid movies. However, we submitted this name and it was denied because it was supposedly "media". Despite our moans of protest the powers that be crushed our name and left us to curl in the fetal position. Many tears were shed, and bitter words were spoken about Verant.

Next, in out attempts to get a cool name past the 'thought police' or 'Gestapo' at Verant we came up with "Drunken Masters". The rejoicing began. This was our name, this was us, this was our true calling. As we talked more we discussed how we would like to kill large mobs in-game drunk. The excitement rippled through our small group as we patted ourselves on the back. Surely, this name would make it through!

Then, like an anvil from the sky, the slam down fell upon us. It nearly crushed us. I personally wanted weep for days in my room with a hearty supply of Bon Bons and Wendy's hamburgers. For what its worth they told us we should not have any reference to being drunk in the game.

This really crushed our spirits. However, Whiteout took it upon himself to do a little scholarly research. He translated Drunken Masters into Latin, and lo and behold it sounded cool. Next Verant asked us what this meant. We kindly told them it meant "clumsy masters". Whether out of stone cold laziness, the lack of desire to deal with our angry emails, or because they simply approved of this we were approved. Hence Appotus Dominus was born. It means "Drunken Masters" in Latin.


What is the basis for the guild?

The guild is based around four friends who like to play EQ together. Those founding fathers are Dapper, Duridain, Whiteout, Yamile, and Ciliem.. What makes the guild special is that on weekends we all go over to Dapper's house (much to the chagrin of his wife) and play EQ for something like 20 hours. We operate in a highly efficient way and we are good at what we do.

We have the guild tag for guildchat. If that's all we ever have it for, we frankly do not care. We are not desperate for your ass, or to change how we operate. If you suggest a lot of changes you will not be revered, bottom line. We are dogmatic and we all get angry quickly. The way to our hearts is through stone cold ass kissing. Don't want to? Good, we didn't need you anyway.


What are the goals for the guild?

We don't know, okay? Basically, we have these raging fights every weekend. Occasionally, I have wanted to blacken the eyes of many of my dear guildmates. We have problems getting along most times, so that makes it very hard for us to decide where we want it to go. There are only a few key things that we want to do right now:

(A) We are going to 60, asap.
(B) We want phat lewts.
(C) We will try almost anything.

So, whether we grow or not is up in the air. If you are cool, and we like playing with you then you will probably get an invite. If you have an interest in being a part of a small elite team, then we are interested. We want quality, not quantity.
