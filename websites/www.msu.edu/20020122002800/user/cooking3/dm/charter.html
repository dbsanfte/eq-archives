Guild Charter

Appotus Dominus was founded by 5 real life friends who have many extra accounts. At its inception, there were 6 separate members. These members were Ciliem, Dapper, Duridain, Whiteout, Yamile, and Dabamf. The foundation of the guild is the RL core of Yamile, Ciliem, Dapper, Whiteout and Duridain. Although, since Dabamf was there for pre-guild, we have a special love for him.

Let me give a rather lengthy dissertation that should clear up some questions you may have.

General Information

This is information that I consider not 100% necessary to know, but it explains things that happen sometimes. On the weekends, Dapper, Ciliem, Yamile, and Whiteout all gather at Dapper's home to play EQ. We start early and end late. This means a number of things to other guild members:

Most times, we want to group together. We began playing EQ so that we could all hang out together and group and do phat things. Obviously, we need the help of everyone else to help us get to where we all want to be. However, we also want to have fun. The most fun for us is honing our skills and playing together every week. While admittedly sometimes this arrangement leaves the odd members screwed, this is not by design. We sincerely are hurt when our members get left out in the cold on groups. We want to work up to where we can have two workable groups. However, for now, we take the people who are (a) on and who are (b) going to coincide most closely with what times we want to play.

Sometimes, when you group with us on these days we may not be chatty, or even unresponsive. This is kind of tough. We chat a lot amongst ourselves and with battle spam and everything going on we sometimes miss things that are said to us directly or indirectly. This is simply a consequence of the circumstances. If this happens, PIPE UP AND SAY IT AGAIN. We are not trying to ignore you, and don't want you to feel like you're being ignored. We are not ripping on you, or laughing at you either. If we thought you were stupid, a poor player, or generally lame you would not be in the guild. So, don't take it personally, and be persistent please.

Essentially, the guild is ruled by committee. This means that we have loud arguments on a frequent basis. Dapper, Ciliem, Yamile, and Whiteout will decide the course of what will happen. While this may seem like an "us" and "you" scenario the way it is written it is really no different than most successful guilds. In order for everyone to get where they want to be, we need a power structure. As such, we do want everyone to get phat lewts. The guild members are not simply pawns in our own schemes, but important in game friends. We have interest in getting loot for everyone. I sincerely hope that nobody feels at this point like they are getting shanked on loot.

Long Term Goals

In the long term we want to kill big daddies and take their loot. While member wise and level wise we are a little short, we can fix this. We are not looking to recruit people who we find to be lame.

  2. Undesirable Personality
  3. Greedy Loot Whore
