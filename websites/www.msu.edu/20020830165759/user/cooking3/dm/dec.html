Want in?

For whatever reason, we have been getting requests to join Appotus lately. We have created a procedure that will determine who is in and who is left to join Agony. This procedure works as follows:

  1. Let us know you're interested
  2. We will group with you as much as we can for a period of two weeks
  3. After the two weeks there will be a voting from all guild people.
  4. Options are No, Nuetral, Yes.
  5. If you are voted neutral there is another two week period
  6. If you get another neutral it will be translated to a 'no'.

Some people who we have already been recruited may simply be admitted if we have previously determined we like them. However, expect to go through this procedure to be admitted. We want to be a top quality guild, so we want top quality people. Contact dapper@appotusdominus.com for more information.

- Ciliem
