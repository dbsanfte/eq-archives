November 23, 2001

Chardok: Who Knew?

This weekend we decided after we had camped the Seb Crypt and some ridiculous monk drop in Karnor's that we wanted to do something a little bit out of the ordinary: have a dungeon crawl. We decided that we were going to attempt to penetrate as deep into Chardok as our mediocre levels would carry us. This week the, uhh, winning team was:

Simna 51 Warrior
Ciliem 52 Enchanter
Dapper 53 Druid
Keepson 51 Monk
Whiteout 52 Shaman
Yamile 49 Cleric

Discovering (the hard way most times) that Chardok repops at an insane rate we were able to run in quite deep. There were many strange (mostly caster, yay!) drops that were upgrades for many of our ranks as well as some cool weapons. Killing the mobs proved decent experience, and it was exciting to slip into the perilous depths of Chardok. Well, it was exciting and fun until we all got run down.

You see, we found this rather big daddy, and decided to camp him for a shoulder piece that we picked up. We sat in this breast shaped enclave and awaited our lewts. Well, as it turns out, Chardok pops extremely fast and there were wanderers. So, while I was messing with my insipid strength staff, many mobs came onto the scene at a quick rate following Keepson. Dapper and I were able to escape but Yamile was left behind to: MELEE THE MOBS!

Yam put up a grand fight and scored the "money shot" on the named mob. She quickly looted the item and proceeded to get ripped to shreds. A lengthy necro telling session ensued and finally we got our corpses. In addition to this, "Gotzcoffens" was born: a level 1 DE warrior who gotz our coffins. We got some loot, got run down, and generally had fun. While we would have loved to get more loot and get run down one less time, it was still quite a fun time.

- Ciliem

November 17, 2001

Hail, a Cute Djinn

Checkout the Rest of the Dijinn Screenshots.

It's true. This weekend we did an all out rape job on the Tower of Frozen Shadow. With unforeseen circumstances breaking up our Disco camp and leaving us with a lack of tanks, we decided to go to ToFS and simply rape it. Because some of us had never been there before, and it was a cool zone, we ported to Iceclad and entered the monstrosity.

We entered the early floors and killed many, many mobs. We grabbed the mobs, applied cleaner, and wiped the floor with floors 1-5. All 6 of us took off in different directions and proceeded to kill as many mobs as possible. Even I (Ciliem) was able to get involved in the slaughter, with limited success.

The first notable event of the evening, was that we got a Student's Homework. While not blockbuster fantabulous, this piece is pretty nice and allowed us to throw more plat into the guild coffers. I wish we could tell you what it dropped from, but I don't remember.

Next, Whiteout was unfortunate enough to get run down by some angry cooks. The healers searched for the unfortunate shaman to no avail. Needless to say, the laughing was racous and Whiteout became enraged at the pleasure we all took in his demise.

As we advanced up the tower, we reached a key holder holding a bowl. After kicking this key holder and taking her candy we found this bowl to be one of sorcerous nature. I (Ciliem) took the bowl immediately and put it in my hands. For the rest of the evening, I also hit mobs with the bowl doing 1 damage on more than 1 occasion. I also raised my 1hb skill to 1.

Finally, we made it to a strange room wherein an efreeti spawned. Throwing caution to the wind we charmed this efreeti, shrunk it, and took pictures of us with it (see above). The efreeti is simply adorable, however his drops are absolute shit.

After a short attempt to find Tserrina, we finally ported out. However, some decent experience did come at the top of the tower where the torches and the bats spawn. While the tower doesn't have top quality loot or experience really it was fun to go there for a change of pace.

The winning team for this event was:

Ciliem 52 Enchanter
Dapper 52 Druid
Dabamf 52 Monk
Keepson 52 Monk
Yamile 47 Cleric
Whiteout 52 Shaman

- Ciliem

November 2, 2001

We Watched the Roots Mini-series!

Checkout the Rest of the Naggy Screenshots.

Well, this isn't actually true, but we certainly could have in the amount of time it took these bumblers to put together a Nagafen raid. I (Ciliem) got a tell on Friday night as we were about to head into Sebilis asking if I wanted to attend a Nagafen raid, which I accepted. I brought my friends along and we proceeded to..wait. Next, we waited. After this, we waited...Finally, we penetrated into Sol B. The fire giants were cleared and we made it to the prep room. Next, the buffing fiasco began. A third rate buff circle began and groups spent well over an hour wandering around in a confused fashion. Next we logged out, put a haphazard strategy together and logged back in.

After quite a lengthy prepping session in which many peoples' buffs began to fade, we charged Nagafen. We won by sheer numbers. Nagafen was destroyed and we rejoiced (more because we wanted to go to bed than anything else).

After loads of drama with a ninja looted Flame Cloak we finally were snug in our beds after losing many rolls. We posed for a picture under Nagafen's head and bounced (see Left). This raid was poorly run because of a lack of people being quiet and a lack of the leader taking charge. Should Appotus Dominus ever organize anything like this people will be forced to STFU and the leader will be in constant communication with the masses.

- Ciliem
