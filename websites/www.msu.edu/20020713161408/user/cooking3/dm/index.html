June 28, 2002

V. Diddy

After posting somewhat regularly and pleasing my adoring fans, I sort of fell off the past couple of weeks. The reason for this istwo-fold. One, how many times do people really want to read about Trakanon, Wuoshi, and Dracoliche? Perhaps I have breathed life into these mundane kills one time too many, gotten excited about one too many cobalt bp, or had one too many disappointments with the Dracoliche's drops. Nonethless, I became complacent and failed to document some kills. For this, I am sorry. Doubltess many of you have been checking the site religiously to see which fantabulous drops we got off Wuoshi. The second reason (lest you forget two-fold), is because I am a lazy son of a bitch. I'll leave it up to you to decide which of these compelling arguements has more bearing on why the site has gone to the dogs (once again).

Anyway, the point of this sexy update was not for me to wax philosophical about the lack of recent updates! It was to announce that we ventured into Akheva ruins to kill some buoulder looking fellow: The Va'Dyn. You know the mob has to be a badass when he has "The" in front of it. Anyway, he has about 365904957398560743680 HP, and as you can see we got an add or three. Luckily the smaller rocky fellows only have around 9034759359205 HP, so they fell comparatively quickly.

You know the going is getting rough when you have a paladin offtanking. Well, if you could see a god damn thing in my picture you would see a paladin offtanking, and thus understand how dire it was. Trust me, there is one in there offtanking. Please do not run down my picture because I screwed up the entire dimensions of the front page to bring it to you.

Drops: Shaderock Amulet (Gratz Kinolen), Gold Inlaid Ulak (Gratz Vault), Blessing of Aegolism (Gratz Erline), Ball of Luclin Juice (Gratz Ciliem). *Due to the fact that I accientally looted things too quickly I was not able to bring you the alluring picture that normally accompanies this section. For that, I am truly sorry.

In Unrelated News...

All in the Family is about the funniest damn show I've ever seen in my life. I'd write more, but frankly if I wanted to write more I woulda thought of something better than that.

- Ciliem

June 7, 2002

Trakanon, the Saga Continues

In Trakanon part I tonight, it seemed we were cursed to never see the scaly fellow. Then, once it transpired, we wished we hadn't seen him at all. In textbook glitchy fashion Trakanon ran away at light speed, AOE'd most of the tanks to death, and gated. What new and exciting buffs is Tolajump casting on him now? Anyway, we bitched and moved on. After this small and embarassing wipe to Trak we cut him up with 0 deaths -- go figure.

Does Trakanon ever get a chance to close his mouth? Is it even possible? It makes one feel a little sorry for him. Of course, not so sorry that you don't want to coat your hands in his blood every three days.

Drops: Cobalt BP (Scubasteve), Cobalt BP (Simna), Traks Teeth (Various homos)

Zelnithak

For the first time we spied a named mob up in Umbral Plains. Unfortunately, it was this goofy ass motherfucker. I'm not ashamed to say that we zerged this mob with ~23 people and it was enjoyable. A mob that looks like this does not deserve the respect of anyone. To see this raggedy fool stagger and warp through the choppy UP landscape is truly a sight to behold. A for effort on trying to make a unique looking mob, E for the preposterous gayness that resulted from those well meaning creative juices.

Drops: Bracer of Fortitude (Carraaki), Tome of Self Reflection (Alueriniel)

- Ciliem
