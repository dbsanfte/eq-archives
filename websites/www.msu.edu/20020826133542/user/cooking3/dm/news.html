Gonna take it...

Right in...to.. the Danger Zone!

As the snappy title would indicate tonight, indeed, we took it right into the danger zone. My cryptic quoting of old movie soundtracks will be clear by the time you finish with this update. That, my friends, I promise you. Anyway, being angry at Eashen of the Sky for denying us loot over three percent life we demanded a rematch. Round 2: AD. (1-1)

The Loots: Onyx Chain Sleeves (Voidd), Claw of Lightning (Scubasteve)

Not quite so Far Into the DANGER ZONE!

Before peanut butter, there was jelly, before chocolate there was vanilla, and before Eashen was Ikitiar. We pounced on this fellow once again on the way to Eashen. He did teach some great lessons on agro control however, and he was only charging exps to teach the lesson. Many brave souls were ripped a new one senselessly. /sigh a win regardless. (2-0)

The Loots: Ancient Wyvern Hide Tunic (Dabamf), Cloak of Venom (Mindark), Ancient Wyvern Hide Sleeves (Vasilik)

Because I enjoy the song about the danger zone, and because Eashen of the Sky kind of flies and the like I have dedicated these kills to the movie "Top Gun". In related news, my "boss" at the library despises Tom Cruise on a very personal level. Anyway, I am making it clear that this is not a Tom Cruise dedication, but one to the film "Top Gun". Which, incidentally, may have been written by an alumni of Michigan State (Yam: It was). Although, that may be a different film. Whatever, we killed mobs and got loot.
