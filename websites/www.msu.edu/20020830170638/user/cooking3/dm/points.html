Current Point Totals

Here is a list of the current points totals for people that have come on an AD raid. Point totals are carried over indefinitely. If you are not listed on the most recent post update your total has not changed, so to find your current total look for the last date that your participated. To find out more about our point system please read out loot policies which explain how points are awarded and how deductions might be incurred. Please contact Ciliem if there are discrepancies or if you have questions about your total. For guild member that would like to see what has been claimed or what is the in guild vault please visit our Loot Forum for more information.
