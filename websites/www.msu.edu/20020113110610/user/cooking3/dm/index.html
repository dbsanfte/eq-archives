Lodizal (1)

In the hopes of beginning what should become a regular Appotus Dominus tradition we destroyed Lodizal today. While trying our hand at a few of the shrooms deep in Sebilis Fen's gimp ranger tracker spies the turtle roaming about in Iceclad. With a quick port out, we were on the scene.

These peeps destroyed Lodizal:

Dapper 56 Druid

Fenlaorn 58 Paladin

Whiteout 56 Shaman

Ciliem 55 Enchanter

Duridain 51 Warrior

Yamile 53 Cleric

Special Guest:

Zarciss (sp?) 54 Monk

 

See Big Lodizal Fall as Duridain, Fenlaorn and Zarciss take care of business.

We set up quickly, got an LFG monk who happened to be in the zone and proceeded to punk the turtle straight out. Nobody was run down and Yamile still had 40 percent of mana left when we finished. Who says pallies can't tank?

Hate: Punked

With the help of a few friends of the guild we were able to penetrate into the Plane of Hate and completely clear the first floor. The attendees were:

Guests:

Checkout the Rest of the PoH Screenshots.

There really were few deaths to speak of save one unsavory incident with a "very unpleasant" hand. We wiped the entire floor clean of loot bearing mobs and collected 2 Cleric BPs, 2 Warrior BP's. 2 epic pieces, and countless other pieces of armor to improve our guild. The raid overall was fairly uneventful and ran smoothly.

Perhaps the biggest accomplishment of the day was beating Maestro like a drum. We pulled him to the safe spot and waxed him with only a few deaths. (Tip: he really hates the Rune line of spells). Our mass tanking monking pallying power was too much for him and he was broken off with no melee casualties. In addition to Maestro, we also beat down the monk mini boss who was kicking it on the first floor.

Aside from this eventful moment there really isn't too much to say. We broke in, broke off mobs, and broke out. However, it did take us a fairly lengthy 10 hours because of levels and numbers. Next time, (soon) we are shaving off HOURS because our monks will be familiar, our enchanters will know what mobs mex easily, and our tanks will have levels and phat gear. Second floor: soon.

- Ciliem

December 29, 2001

Welcome New Members!

At roughly the same time we gained two new members into the Appotus Dominus crew. The first recruit we officially guilded was 54 Warrior Mycro. Mycro had been along with us on a couple raids and many long boring exp groups (and even the Xalgoz camp). We found his tanking to be admirable and more importantly his COMMITMENT and his no nonsense playstyle to be a fit with us. Mycro busted Hate's azz with us on Sunday and this was a great start to his AD career.

54 Cleric Battas was a long time friend who initially joined us in the Seb Disco a month or two ago. After grouping with him on and off over the past month both parties eventually came to the conclusion that we were like-minded in terms of loots and in game goals Battas has never failed us in battle and has many of the same traits that led us to recruit Mycro.

Welcome to Appotus Dominus!

Please remember to:
- visit forums regularly
- go to magelo.com, create your profile and send Yamile the link to it
- get to level 60. :)

- Ciliem
