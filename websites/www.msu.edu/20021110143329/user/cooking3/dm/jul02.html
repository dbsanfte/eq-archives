July 31, 2002

Grand Slam!

In life, there are really a few different types of grand slams. Understanding this is really a prerequisite for getting the most out of this snappy quip.

The first type: The bases are loaded, you're down by three runs, there are two outs, the count is 3-2, you have fouled off 10 consecutive pitches to stay alive, it's game 7 of the World Series, you sit on fastball, and boooooooom! You're a hero!

The second type: The Denny's Grand Slam breakfast.

This fine accomplishment certainly fits more into the second type of "Grand Slam" than the first. It was a pretty good deal, it filled the void, but to keep it real you know there was probably a better breakfast available somewhere.

So, without further ado, I give you Wuoshi, Severilous, Talendor, and Va'Dyn, and a dracoliche! Fuck, I wrote that whole story thinking there were only four kills. My grand slam metaphor is shot right in the ass. Bah fuck it, it's still a grand slam.

I'm doing this from memory and without my normal selection of prime screenshots so I'm going to haphazardly list all the loot. Bear with me loyal AD readers.

Wuoshi: Tanglewood Shield (Scubasteve), Some scales, a stupid ass scimtar.

Severilous: Green Scale, Torpor, Malo, probably some other preposterously shitty drops.

Talendor: Red Scale (Nife), Red Dragon Shield (Voidd), a bard horn or some shit.

Va'Dyn: Shaderock Robe (Nadilye), Shaderock Girdle (I forgot), Luclin Ball, a shitass spell

Dracobishe: Crimson Robe of amazing shittiness x2

So, once again, like the Denny's grand slam, we got an amazing deal but most of it is in the vault.

I dedicate these 5 sexy kills to:

Who else, but the Denny's Grand Slam breakfast! Let me also assure you folks that there is not a Denny's Grand Slam Breakfast in all the world that even slightly resembles that picture.

- Ciliem

...who in no way intends to berate the Denny's Grand Slam Breakfast only wishes to point out the obvious frugality in its construction.
