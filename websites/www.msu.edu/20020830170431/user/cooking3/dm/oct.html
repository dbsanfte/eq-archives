October 20, 2001

Efreeti Lord: Give us Boots plz thx.

Checkout the Rest of the Efreeti Screenshots

This weekend we decided that we wanted some boots. Therefore, we decided to go camp the Efreeti. The winning team was:

Ciliem: 47 Enchanter
Dapper: 48 Druid
Yamile: 43 Cleric
Duridain: 46 Warrior
Dabamf: 46 Monk
Whiteout: 44 Shaman

We managed to penetrate our way up to the efreeti, and kill him many many times. However, we did not get all of the pairs of boots we had envisioned for 2 reasons:

(1) Efreeti spawn was not happening for us.
(2) Dapper had to go upstairs and get chips, causing us to get run down.

So, Ciliem (me) and Whiteout came away with some sexy boots. Yamile and Dapper came away with tears in their eyes. That's the price you pay for chips.

- Ciliem
