April 29, 2002

Epic/ToV/Lodizal

This weekend we put in some good time in ToV, gathering more fabulous loots. (gratz Dapper BP) We've got most people down to where they are looking for pants and breastplates, and we're hoping to complete these few (but most important pieces) within the next few weeks. ToV this weekend coulda been better, but conversely coulda been worse also.

Next order of business, we killed General V'Ghera for Kinolen's epic in a rather bloody affair. Congratulations on your epic Kino, the first real Appotus Dominus rogue! We were insanely lucky and he did drop the quill giving us a rogue that can now really damage mobs. This affair coulda went better, but then, coulda been worse as well.

Finally we were lucky enough to get and kite Lodizal till we could get enough people on the scene. In a case of mistaken tortoises Forsaken Unity actually got the kill, but they were kind enough to give us the loot when they realized we engaged first. We give FU mad props for giving us the loots and being cool about the whole issue. Once again, Lodizal coulda gone better, but then could also have been worse.

In a mixed bag weekend we managed to not do our best work and still come out with a lot of really nice loot and an epic. Next weekend, we're hoping things will go a little closer to plan. (and if they don't you can bet Dapper will take it out of someone's ass - hey I sieved MOST of the glimmer drakes, what's the problem?)

- Ciliem
