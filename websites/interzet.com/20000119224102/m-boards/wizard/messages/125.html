I saw that they needed my help with the second group and ran inot the fray. While crushing on one with my Great Worn Staff, I cast fingers of fire.heres the result:
Orc Centurion says: Hit for 22 points damage
Orc Centurion says: Hit for 22 points damage
Orc Centurion says: Hit for 22 points damage
You hit Orc Centurion for 14pts damage.
One meelle round and 80pts of damage dispensed.
and only one of the Orcs turned on me. So I got to cast it again with the same results. Dont count out AE and PB spells, they can be handy. (on the down side, a similar expierince ended with Guard hits you for 1000 points damage, you are slain by the Guard. I never saw him.)
