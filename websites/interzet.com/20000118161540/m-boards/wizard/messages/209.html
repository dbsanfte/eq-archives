You're looking for Gnoll High Shaman and
Gnoll Necromancers. They can be found at BOTH
Lake Rathe and Paw. Lake Rathe is the easiest,
IMO, because the mob is right inside the zone.
It's the first set of critters you see (in the
daytime). They also drop Gold Stud Earrings
(+3 Int, +2 AC), Skull Of Jhen'Tra (+20 Mana),
and Runed Totem Staves. Just get a group
together once you hit 13 or 14 and go whack
yourself some gnolls. Then keep doing it until
you get the items you want.

If you dont know how to get to either place, get
some maps.
