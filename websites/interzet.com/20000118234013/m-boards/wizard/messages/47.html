I think you should start anew. Just 5 levels is easy to make up. It should be around 10-15 hours of playing. (At the teen levels, it takes probably 20 hours to playing to level.)

Mana is more important to wiz than any other spell class. Mage/Necro have their pets, cleric have armor and some melee abilities.

All our power (attack or defend) comes from mana.

Usually if i run out of mana, i am dead. I need mana to fight and i need mana to escape (shadow step and gate). In a group, if i am out of mana, i am useless.

Depending on your race, the number of points that you should have put into INT is different.

Take erudite as an example, he can put 25 pts into INT. That is a 5 pts per level difference in mana.

At level 12, it is a 60 pts difference which allows you to cast one more shock of lightning (78 dmg at 60 mana) which can mean life or death.

At level 20, it is a 100 pts difference. I usually keep about 100 mana for reserve. It basically allow me to shadow step 3 times and gate .. my escape option.
