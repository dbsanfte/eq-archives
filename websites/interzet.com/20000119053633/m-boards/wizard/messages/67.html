Sorry but the concensus seems to be that jewelry making isn't all that great. Most of the magical jewelry that can be made are pretty minor effects. Especially compared to the effects of jewelry that is found instead of player made.

Although some people have reported that Jaded jewelry not only increases the amount of mana a caster has but also the regeneration rate I serious doubt that it will affect that much. A jaded ring (+5 mana) only adds that much mana to your total. Not per level which would be an awesome item to have.

Even if it added +5 to the amount of mana regenerated per tick (while meditating) it still wouldn't be worth it. At 20th level an erudite wizard has 600 mana. That's 120 mana per bubble and each bubble gets regenerated over the course of about 40 seconds. The amount of mana regenerated is based on the skill roll made vs meditation (which is why sometimes mana does regen and others it regens a huge jump in one go) and then the +5 would be added if at all.

Doubtful at best, worthless at worst. Do you really want to pour 400pp into this skill??
