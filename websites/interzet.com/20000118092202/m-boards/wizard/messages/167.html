You put one point into research at level 16. You
buy a lexicon in your guild, this is the book that
is a container. You buy the other 4 books in
your guild, they range in price from a few gold
to quite a few plat. The first book is written
in Old Euridian, the second in Elder Elvish, the
third in Dragon, the fourth in Ancient Dragon.
These 4 books have all of the rune combos you need
to combine in your lexicon. You get the runes
from creataurs that are either intelligent, or
take magic weapons to hit, and the runes spawn on
about 1 in 50 kills. You are better off
advertising to buy them in all the zones you pass
through. Put the proper runes the book lists in
your lexicon and hit combine. If your prayers to
Innorruuk have been heard, you will create a spell
. Otherwise, you will fail, similar to smithing,
and the runes will be destroyed. Good luck, there
are many bugs, see my above posts for some of
them.
