: O'Kiels Radiation - Damage shield, cast on yourself or others. When engaged in melee it causes slight damage to the oppenent. Good to cast on the tank when real low on mana. Duration is very short.

: Eye of Zoom - Becareful with this spell. It works well but if a monster spots it, it will come after the caster. It's real hard to use as a spying device but for summoning creatures to fight it works excellent.
