When I'm the leader I make it clear that any named loot or special loot, as you found, is GROUP loot. In other words, it's up to the group to decide what do to with it. Also, if someone in the group wants it then they have to compensate the rest of the group, after all it was a GROUP effort! I try to get the group to agree to sell it to someone in our group at a reduced rate, this one is really hard to do heh.

IF no one can afford it, then auction it and split the auction money.

IF no one listens I boot them.

IF you join a group and this isn't made clear, leave. Not worth the head ache.
