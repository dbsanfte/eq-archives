in life. I'm nearly 12th level now and I'm finding that as long as I'm

protecting my butt, as in early levels. I'm gonna hang in there, just longing
for the day that I can get the spell that dishes out 1100 damage per shot.
I do wish that mana would regenerate quicker, however.
