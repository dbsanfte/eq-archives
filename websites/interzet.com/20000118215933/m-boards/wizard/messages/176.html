You get it from the Oracle. The Oracle is about level 40, a little under, but his guardian is VERY nasty. You can either kill the Oracle and get his robe, or give him the tome of Ages as a quest, and then he will give you the Robe. The oracle is found on one of the islands the ship goes by when you travel from Freeport? I forget exactly, check on the vault, there have been lots of postings and maps on how to get to him.
