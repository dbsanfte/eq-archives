: Can anybody tell me where to buy spells like 'root' et al outside of the Neriak Wizard's Guild. Thanks

You can buy crossover spells (spells that are used by more than one class) at another classes guild house. Try the Cleric guildhouse for Root and Gate.

Before you start relying on Root overly much though I would also get Fade. This spell costs only 10 mana and uses the same skill as Root. By maxxing out your Alteration skill your Root is more likely to not fizzle, be resisted and will last longer.
