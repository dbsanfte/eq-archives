Go north up into everfrost and hunt the tundra. Look around for warriors or shaman of similar levels group up and start hunting mammoth calfs, orcish shaman, mountaineers and troopers. Beware the Ice skeletons, they require magic to effect them and even a blue one will chew up an unprepared party. Spawn is good and not alot of people go up there. Try it out should be good for a couple of levels if not more.
