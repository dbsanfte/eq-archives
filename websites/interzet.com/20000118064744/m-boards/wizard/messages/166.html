: What's a good way for a DE wiz to earn pp? Specifically, what's the best hunt for money?

Probably your best bet is to team up with a cleric and go willow wisp hunting in the west commons. If you can get your hands on a Runed Totem staff you would be even better off. A typical wisp that glows at night will have a Lightstone you can sell for around a plat. Ocasionally you will get a Greater Lightstone that sells for around 4plat or more. At 12 th level you can solo these for alot of money and Exp.
Good Hunting!
