Everquest Online is a fan site with plenty of information about the online MMORPG from Sony: Everquest.

Everquest is the largest online roleplaying game to date with over 400,000 subscribers world-wide, 6 expansions, over 40 servers, and fan fairs with the most active and close-knit community around.

The multitude of activities you can do in Everquest are:
  • Choose from 15 different and unique races
  • Become one of 16 different classes, each with their own skills and abilities
  • Fight in large scale battles
  • Defeat enormous monsters
  • Buy and Sell to become a wealthy merchant
  • Join parties and groups to go hunting and questing with
  • Collect thousands of items
  • Learn thousands of spells to cast

How long is your typical gaming session when playing EQ? 5-6 hours

What does a typical EQ day consist of for you? Raiding, Experiencing, Tradeskilling, Playing alt, or just messing around finding bugs here and there

What was the race and class of your very first character? Wood Elf Ranger

What got you into gaming and what sort of games do you like the most? Started out playing a little D&D with some relatives but then when that died out I went to EQ game

What other hobbies or activities do you take part in, other then playing EverQuest? Softball, volunteering at my old High School, and an adult leader for a Boy Scout Troop

What is your highest level in EQ? 70 least till expansion comes out

Which classes in EQ have you NOT played? Bard, Necromancer, Wizard, Beastlord, Paladin (rest I played a little or have alt)

What is your favorite expansion? Kunark

What is your favorite npc? Don't really have a favorite one
