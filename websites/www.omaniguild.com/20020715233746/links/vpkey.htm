Printable Checklist


1. TRAKANON'S TOOTH --- Dropped by Trakanon who usually averages about 3 drops per death

2. MEDALLION OF KYLONG --- Give all 3 portions to Professor Akabao in the windmill in the Lake of Ill Omen, loc approx. neg 2600, neg 2900.

(Top portion) In Chardok there is a mob called Niblek, give him a black sapphire and a ruby and he gives you the top portion

(Middle portion) Dropped from Verix Kylox in Karnor's Castle. He's located in the basement area. Follow the moat to the right hand side and go into the underwater tunnel. He spawns around that area. This is a good xp camp.

(Bottom portion) Found in the Library of Kaesora. It's just lying on the floor, in the corner, of the 2nd floor inside. Getting there can be tricky as you need both Invis (at first for spiders) and Invis Undead (for the latter part with nearly all undead save one rare chomper). You drop down to the shaft midpoint at the entry. Go left and then right through the door. Pass through an invis wall and you're good to go from there.

3. MEDALLION OF JARSATH --- Give all 3 portions to Xiblin Fizzlefutt in the Timorous Deep (His loc is -5800,2950)

(Top portion) It's just lying around in the Swamp of No Hope at loc 52, 2935, -6 (note: near the water) This is the easiest part to get so grab it some time on your way to OS.

(Middle portion) Dropped by an Iksar monk in Firiona Vie named "an ancient Jarsath" (wanders near the Drolvarg camp - check tracking around the loc 270,-1900) (note: whenever you're in the zone, ask a cleric/SK/necro to sense undead, the only other undead mob in the zone is Velkis' pet, so if she's not up and the PC senses undead, you have an AJ up) This mob seems to be an 8 hour spawn, is up after a server reset, and has no known PH.

(Bottom portion) Dropped by the Bloodgill Marauder who guards the entrance to Veskar at the bottom of the Lake of Ill Omen (His loc is -380,-50,-190) This mob also seems to be an 8 hour spawn, is up after a reset, at has no known PH.

4. MEDALLION OF OBULUS --- Give all 3 portions to Slixin Klex in the Burning Woods located at -681, -1120, -560

(Top portion) Quest mob Sselot Dnaas who spawns in an island in Warsilks Woods at loc 3900, 300 (Give him a burnished wooden staff from Chardok, have an enchanter charm and do the trade)

(Middle portion) Dropped by a rotting skeleton at loc 2100, -5100 in the Dreadlands

(Bottom portion) Dropped by the Pained Soul in Trakanon just outside the Old Sebilis Portal The Pained Soul (and its placeholders) spawn at Loc: -1857, -4373. This mob is a serious bottleneck and is usually too dangerous to camp for non solo capable classes.

To get there:

Facing out of the Seb tunnel, take a right and go through door. Take a left and move up a bit. Then turn to your right and the pained soul/spec keeper/spec champion should be in front of you.

Take all 3 "completed" medallions and the tooth and return them to Emperor Ganak in Trakanon's Teeth to get the Key to Veeshan's Peak.
 

Extended Version - The Key of Veeshan

The main quest is given by Emperor Ganak (-2740, +2845) in Trakanon's Teeth (this is the same guy that gives the Sebilis key quest), but it involves several subquests. These are the quests for the medallions of Jarsath, Obulus, and Kylong. Each of the 3 medallions consists of 3 pieces each (9 medallion pieces + the tooth = 10 piece total quest for the key). Once you have all 3 medallions you simply hand them in along with one of Trakanon's fangs to get your Key of Veeshan (Note: You can not use the tooth dropped off the named trakaraptor in TT, it must be from the actual poison dragon Trakanon in Sebilis).

Hint: If you get the medallion pieces mixed up in your inventory, just get someone to cast identify on you while they're on your cursor and it will give you the name of the segment your holding.

Medallion of Jarsath

This quest is given by Xiblin Fizzlebik (-5777, +2943) in Timorous Deep, like each of the medallions, there are 3 pieces you
must return to him for the Medallion of Jasarth. By the way, this is the "easy" medallion. :)

The first portion is located in Firiona Vie and is dropped off the ancient Jarsath. The ancient is a level 40 Iksar skeleton
monk (4000 HP) that spawns very close to the mouth of the drolvarg temple at +647, -2097 and roams down the hill to the
nearby drolvarg camp then across the road a bit and back. I suggest killing all non-drolvarg NPCs in this area (which there
are very few) to get him to spawn, and from what I could deduct the placeholder is a leech that wanders in this area (Note:
this does not mean he necessarily does have a PH, it may just be a long spawn but this is what it appeared to be like). Also
he is the only undead mob in the whole zone (at the time this was written, assuming they don't add any), so sense the dead
works great if you have it. Approximate time it took me: 2 hours.

The next piece is on the blood gill marauder at the bottom of Lake of Ill Omen at -376, -52. He is a static non-roaming lake
goblin with 2 lesser guards, level 35-39, and the marauder himself is level 40 (3000 HP). He hits around the 90s, guards hit
in the 70s. His spawn point is a few feet inside the "entrance" to the underwater temple here (so bring your fishbone, local
enduring breath caster, or potions), even though it ends in a dead end (Note: this is the future site of the zone-in for the
pending underwater dungeon). The picture shows his exact spot, unagro'd and undisturbed (click to enlarge). Approximate time
it took me: 5 hours, but I left and came back once or twice and likely missed his spawn, I have returned twice since and he
was already up both times.

The last piece is incredibly easy, it's just a bag sitting on the ground in the Swamp of No Hope at +52, +2935, there are no
dangerous NPCs in the area. The exact respawn time of the bag is unknown to me, but reasonable. Approximate time: However
long it takes you to run to the Swamp.

Medallion of Obulus

Slixin Klex (-681, -1127) in Burning Woods holds the quest for this one.

This is one of the more agonizing pieces, requiring you to kill the pained soul in Trakanon's Teeth. The pained soul has a
single spawn, static, Iksar spectre placeholder at -1835, -4368, which is pretty close to the ramp that leads down to the
Sebilis bubble. He is about level 44 and hits for over 100 and his placeholder spawns every 8.5 minutes exactly. Approximate
time: It took me 6 hours, and from what I have heard it typically ranges from 4-10 hours for him to show up.

The next portion is from Dreadlands, dropped off a rotting skeleton. The rotting skeleton has a single, static, non-roaming
placeholder that spawns at +2246, -5113 which is up on the ledge above one of the spider intensive areas here. As you are
exiting the Frontier Mountains zone, hug the wall left and stay up farily high on it to find him. The spot has a quick (7
minute exactly) respawn, but he is somewhat uncommon, and is level 43 (5000 HP). Approximate time it took me: 2 hours.

The final medallion piece for this quest has its own subquest from Ssolet Dnaas (+4010, +396) in Warslilks Woods. Contrary to
the guide on AllaKhazam's site (which is what I tried to do the quest with, but lacks a ton of critical information), you
can't kill him for the piece or hand him the burnished wooden staff from Chardok, handing him the staff is your only option.
The staff is a random drop on any mob in Chardok (45-60 zone), and drops quite frequently (6 dropping in one night of hunting
is not uncommon). The only problem is he is on some type of Iksar faction (not Legion of Cabilis because I am warmly to them)
which most non-Iksars are KOS to. Unless you somehow have good enough faction with him to receive the quest, you have to
bring along an enchanter or bard to charm him, he is level 50 and very buggy (ie he won't even leave his spawn point if
agro'd). Once he is charmed simply hand him the staff to get your medallion piece. Approximate time: Minimal hours of Chardok
hunting + however long it takes you to find an enchanter and take them to Warslilks Woods.

Medallion of Kylong

This is the last and in my opinion hardest medallion. The quest is offered by Professor Akabo (-2540, -2994) in Lake of Ill
Omen. NOTE: The professor cons dubious to most people, but will still give you the medallion as long as he offers you the quest.

There is a subquest in Chardok given by the NPC ratman Niblek for the first piece. He requires you to bring him a Black
Sapphire and a Ruby (I buy rubies in Highpass) for his medallion segment. I provided a map to the right (click to enlarge
and note: the gold section is where you would be entering the tunnel from), but it isn't very good. However, the directions
to his area are simple, and he doesn't roam. As you run up the ramp to the exit a bit and turn around, look to the left of
the castle and you'll see a ledge that leads to a tunnel. You need levitate to get up here, and either a group to clear the
way or invis (Note: invis works great here on all the mobs in this tunnel except chodikai "dog" models which there can
be none or several in the path, up to you if you want to risk it, I did). Follow the tunnel down until the first fork which
is Y-shaped and take a right, at the second fork which is T-shaped take a left, and a right again at the 3rd and last fork
which is also T-shaped and just hug the wall right. Once you start seeing ratmen slaves (around the 3rd fork) you are pretty
much safe from harmful NPCs except the slaves themselves which are like level negative 10. (aka if you actually die to these
slaves solo or not you have no business in Veeshan's Peak :P) Niblek is indifferent to everyone as far as I know, simply hand
him the 2 gems for your piece and you are set. Approximate time it took me: 15-20 minutes, I got seen by a dog once and had
to gate, rest, and try again (but thats not counting getting gems, I already had a black sapphire when I decided to do the
quest).

There is a piece sitting on the ground on the 2nd floor of the library here, click the map on the right to enlarge. Kaesora
is a low (30ish) level zone and you will need both regular invis and invis vs. undead to get down here if you really don't
want to be seen, but the majority of the MOBs in the way are undead (the spiders are live). The "respawn" on this piece is 2 hours,
10 minutes. Approximate time: 5 minutes if the medallion segment is up to 2 hours 10 minutes if you just missed it.
