Our Goal is to establish a stable "high level" oriented guild, which promotes mature, roleplaying environment. We hope to venture into some of the most difficult areas in Norrath and establish our name as a skilled guild for others to look up to with a sense of trust and respect.

  In ancient Mediterranean era there existed a group called the Omani.  They were a secret society that carried out the dirty work of the governments.  They were a small, discreet group of very experienced and dangerous professionals.

  Omani sometimes has accomplished what no others have done before.
