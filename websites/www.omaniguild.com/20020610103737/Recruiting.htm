Recruitment:

  • If you are not Reliable, Capable, Dependable,  Patient and Communicative you need not apply.

  • If you are not looking to join a guild with friends, and a family atmosphere you need not apply.

  • If you are looking to join a guild, to reap for yourself, do not apply.

 

Requirements:

  • You must be 56th level or higher.

  • You must not be one of the current restricted classes.

  • You must read the rules (there will be a quiz) on Recruiting, Guild Decorum, Guildchat, Guild Goals, Loot, and Epic Loot.

  • You must have 60 days played or more on a main chosen character to be guilded.

  • You must have keys to Charasis, Sebilis, and working towards a Veeshans Peak key.

  • You must have an enduring breath item.

  • You must have maps of the zones, and thru zones, we frequent.

  • You must NOT share your account with anyone, or its information. (No multi-player characters)

  • You must have a Sponsor from within Omani to be elligable to recruit.

  • Lastly, your sponsor must contact either Azursa, Chadric, or Bumana to set an appointment for an interview.

 

Any questions to this point, may be directed at either Azursa, Chadric, or Bumana in game,

or on the public message board.

 

 

Recruting Process : what to expect

 

You will be expected to meet as many members of the guild as you can. This effort is yours alone, and part of what we look for when signing a new recruit. There is no shame in sending tells, simply to introduce yourself or ask for a group. It is however your Sponsors responsiblity to see that you are aware of the days/weeks plans and have made an attendance list. Everyone within Omani is very used to these processes, and all are usually very helpful to prospective recruits.

 

You will be in the recruiting process a total of two weeks time, and polled on the Monday following your 14th day. The poll will stay active for guild member voting for 7 days. One No vote, and you are rejected. More than 45% abstainations of your overall votes, will put you back into the recruiting process. A total of 30 yes votes will get you a guild invite.

 

So, our expectations of you are high. We like to see an effort of both time, and capability in playing your character.

 

We understand everyone, including ourselves, learns new things with time. No one within the guild is against teaching anyone who puts for the effort to learn.

 

We wish you luck in your endeavors.
