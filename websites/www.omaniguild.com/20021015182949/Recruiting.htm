Recruitment:

  • If you are not Reliable, Capable, Dependable,  Patient and Communicative you need not apply.

  • If you are not looking to join a guild with friends, and a family atmosphere you need not apply.

  • If you are looking to join a guild, to reap for yourself, do not apply.

 

Requirements:

  • You must be 60th level.

  • You must not be one of the current restricted classes.

  • You must read the rules (there will be a quiz) on Recruiting, Guild Decorum, Guildchat, Guild Goals, Loot, and Epic Loot.

  • You must have 60 days played or more on a main chosen character to be guilded.

  • You must have keys to Charasis, Sebilis, and working towards a Veeshan's Peak key.

  • You must have an enduring breath item (not a summoned item).

  • You must have at least one (1) instant cast buff item.

  • You must be able to raise your MR to 150 and any other resist above 100, simultaneously, unbuffed.

  • You must have maps of the zones, and thru zones, we frequent.

  • You must NOT share your account with anyone, or its information. (No multi-player characters)

  • You must have a Sponsor from within Omani to be eligible to recruit.

 

Recruiting Process : what to expect

 

You will be expected to meet as many members of the guild as you can. This effort is yours alone, and part of what we look for when signing a new recruit. There is no shame in sending tells, simply to introduce yourself or ask for a group. It is however your Sponsors responsibility to see that you are aware of the days/weeks plans and have made an attendance list. Everyone within Omani is very used to these processes, and all are usually very helpful to prospective recruits.

 

You will be in the recruiting process for a period of two to five weeks. Polls are posted on Mondays and will stay active for guild member voting for 7 days. A single No vote, and you are rejected. You must receive either 30 "yes" votes or 3/4 of the total votes, whichever is greater, to get a guild invite.

 

So, our expectations of you are high. We like to see an effort of both time, and capability in playing your character.

 

We understand everyone, including ourselves, learns new things with time. No one within the guild is against teaching anyone who puts for the effort to learn.

 

We wish you luck in your endeavors.

 

- Azursa Pevarra
