Fri June 28, 2002

Summertime is not Omani's main playing time, as most of us are not in the school age range.  This is the time when the working class go on vacation and away from the computer, therefore, things are always a little bit slower this time of the year for us.

With that out of the way, let me give you a brief summary of what happened this past couple of weeks or so.

- Dinged 60:  Congratulation Amanchara and Andezia !

- Epics: Congratz to Talloss, and Aerron !

- New Members: Welcome Artamas (60 Ranger) to the guild!!

- We killed our share of mobs, including, but not limited to Derakor the Vindicator, Rumblecrush, Doomshade, Trakanon, Gozzrem, lots and lots of named drakes from ToV, and a little fun trip to NToV to get used to the zone.  Again we killed several other mobs, that I am not at liberty to discuss.

We also did a couple of Fear raids to try to get some Epic pieces for some of our members (key word "try", as the stuff didn't drop).
