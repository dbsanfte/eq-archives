A Short History

Around the start of the summer 2000, the founders of Omani were all in separate guilds.  Bloodshard, Eludyan, Nabber were in Brothers of Fire, Azursa and Quellick were in Amber Order, Fhyre was in Harbingers of Innoruuk and Hideo was in Forsaken Conclave.  That's just a few names that come from the original group of people.

They grouped quite frequently and did things their own guilds could not do themselves.  At the time Hideo was not very happy in his current guild.  On July 27th 2000 he sent Azursa this email...  

"Dunno about you, but just a shot in the dark here.  Seems that its
always you, quellick, nabber, bloodshard, Eludyan and myself grouped.
We sometimes have some others involved but the core group appears to be
the previous mentioned people.  Ever thought of making our own little
guild heh?  Just and idea but I know how it is to start new guilds, can
be rough having helped start one."
Azursa then asked the others what they thought about that idea.  Most were reluctant they didn't want to leave their current guilds.  Time went on and Hideo and Azursa pushed the idea more frequently.  Hideo's main idea was to create a small guild of friends.  They weren't going to strive to compete with others, strive to be the first to kill this and that, simply to get along with people and have fun.

Eventually they all decided that another direction was best choice.  They were going to create a guild and determine their own direction instead of ride the coat tails of another.  The aforementioned people including a few others gathered and began the arduous task of deciding a name.

Unfortunately all the names we chose were rejected.  Some of which included, Illuminati, The Illuminati, The Foundation, Foundation, The Circle...  Finally after about a week of name rejection Azursa brought up Omani.  The name was excepted and Omani was born.

What is Omani ?

In ancient Mediterranean era there existed a group called the Omani.  They were a secret society that carried out the dirty work of the governments.  They were a small, discreet group of very experienced and dangerous professionals.

To this day, Omani is a well known and respected guild.  We treat others with the respect they deserve.  Welcome into our ranks many friends and skilled players.  Omani is relatively small guild compared to some but we can do things most others cannot.  We try to accomplish goals sometimes beyond our limits.  Omani doesn't always fail either, sometimes we have accomplished what no others have done before.
