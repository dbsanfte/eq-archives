Goal

To establish a stable "high level" oriented guild, which promotes mature, roleplaying environment.  We hope to venture into some of the most difficult areas in Norrath and establish our name as a skilled, guild for others to look up to with a sense of trust and respect.

Rules for Entrance

The minimum level required to join is now 56.  The level requirement may change in time as Omani grows larger.

  • You will have to group with guild members for at least 2 weeks (and no more than 4 weeks) on a regular basis.
  • Must be liked by all in the guild & have no questions of character or judgment that could hurt the image of the guild in the eyes of others.  Of course you are free to prove otherwise.
  • Must not be a multi-person played character.
  • You must have the Sebilis and HS keys (faction concern is the only acceptable reason for not having an HS key) and an Enduring Breath item.
  • You must have an "informal" interview with Azursa, Bumana or Chadric first (in game), to be considered as an applicant.

Sponsorship:

  • All new recruits must have a sponsor. Please be sure you know and are willing to vouch for a person you are sponsoring. We are trusting your judgment on this person and that you know them well. Don't sponsor a random person just because they ask.
  • To be able to sponsor someone, you have to have been a member of Omani for at least one month (30 days).  A person can only sponsor one recruit at a time.
  • The recruit must be willing to print out viable maps of the zones we frequent, unless they are familiar with all known zones.
     

Voting / Polls:

  • The first date as a recruit will be determined by when their sponsor posts that they are sponsoring them on our recruiting board after the recruit has had their interview wih Azursa, Chadric or Bumana. After a minimum of 2 weeks time a poll will be posted to vote on the recruit.
  • The recruit needs at least 30 "YES" votes to join and no (zero, none, zip, zilch) "NO" votes.
  • A recruit will have 7 days to attain these "yes" votes from 30 members of the guild after he/she is polled. When and if a person receives 30 votes, they will be invited asap.
  • If a prospective recruit, obtains more than 45% of the total votes in Abstentions, this person will be kept as a recruit pending a poll 1-2 weeks later. What this means is A) the Sponsor did not take his/her job to heart, and did not allow the recruit enough grouping time with the voting guild as a whole or B) The recruit did not make enough of an effort with his/her recruitment, and needs to be held back.
  • If after the second recruit period this person has still not made the required effort to be grouped/group with, he/she will be effectively back-burned until such a time as they can dedicate themselves to a guild such as ours.
  • IMPORTANT: After 7 days, if the person does not receive 30 "yes" votes, he/she will be turned down.
  • A legitimate NO, will also turn the recruit down.
  • Voting guidelines:
    Yes votes: you want them to join the guild. you feel they will be a good addition to the guild and are Omani material.
    No votes: you have reason to not want this person to join the guild, be it a history with this person or you know that his/her conduct is unfitting for our family.
    Abstains: you are indifferent toward this persons joining. you have never met or grouped with this person.

     

Guildchat Guidelines

Avoid excessive use of foul language

  • Avoid out of character topics (movies, shows, music) & dewdspeak
  • Avoid spamming guildchat examples of this are:
  1. Excessively asking for ports, rezzes, or prices. You may ask once, then again in a minute or two, maybe even again 15 minutes later, but don't keep asking every minute for a port.
  2. Repeating song lyrics over guildchat. Maybe a line or two, but don't start singing a whole song on guildchat.
  3. Using line wrap to make it look like someone else is saying something
  4. Asking for hand outs. Although, I don't think this will be a problem.
  5. Doing auctions for a 3rd party over guildchat. (ex. Bobb comes up to you and asks you "Can you see if anyone in your guild wants to buy a Ykesha?").  If you are doing the auctioning yourself please follow the spamming guidelines above.
  6. Basically if someone in guildchat asks you to stop doing something, please stop it! If you see a problem with it take to a guild officer. Mostly your find this is a case when 2 people find something humorous or interesting & one or more people don't. Tells make all happy.

Rules for being removed from the guild

When your conduct promotes an image unbecoming of the guild. Such as,

  • Kill-stealing excessively, "ninja-looting", creating a hostile environment for another guild member, purposely lying.
  • When such a situation occurs you may be removed immediately from the guild. You are henceforth on Probationary Status. If you feel you have been wronged you may take this up with another guild officer or the guild leader. Only the guild leader may re-establish someone who has been removed and placed on Probationary Status. You may have your guild status returned if it was wrongly removed. Almost no one is permanently removed. You may be asked to perform an act of penance to have your guild status returned and/or be asked to not be in the guild for a period of time (a week, a month, perhaps even years). If you do not perform this act then you may remain in Probationary Status and out of the guild. If you perform the penance decided upon by the officers & leader, you may have your status reestablished.
  • If deemed a player may be placed into Exiled Status. This player will never be allowed into the guild again. This would be an extreme case.

Twink & Secondary characters

The bringing in of secondary characters , referred to as twinks for the rest of this document is allowed. This is to assist in the continual access to guildchat as well as help in developing fellowship amongst the guild.

Bringing your twinks into the guild is not mandatory but is allowed. If the twink is below the level requirements of the guild (level 56 at this time) they must remain roleplay or anon.

Twinks are not allowed to request for equipment, ports, or rezzes over guild chat. They may ask specific members via tell to assist but this should be avoided. This is to avoid begging. Once they reach the level requirements of the guild this stipulation is lifted.

Twinks should readily identify who's twink they are if asked. If one is uncomfortable with this then do not bring the twink into the guild.

Any member of the guild who leaves or is removed from the guild must immediately remove all twinks as well. No exceptions!

Twinks in the guild must adhere to all rules & policies of the guild. Even if they are anonymous.

Loot

We use a combination of merit and random loot.  Mobs are separated on different tiers.

  • TIER 3 - All loot on this mob will be merited -- any new encounter requiring a 36+ raid and mobs that we kill less often.
  • TIER 2 - Only one or two specific items will be merited, other loot will be need before greed, random among who needs -- these are mobs that have at least a few (2-3) highly desired items.
  • TIER 1 - Need Before Greed / random among who needs -- mobs that are on a level to kill that it takes little preparation and most/all of the loot is not in high demand.

Spells:
Spells will be totally separate from all Tiers. Certain specific spells will also be merited based on seniority/raid participation/level.

Merit:
What is merit based on? A combination of seniority/raid participation/level. Raid leaders have the right to change the loot rules PRIOR to asking people to join the raid as long as they inform people of them.

Friends:
If a friend comes long, they will be eligible to roll on all items, except loot/spells that are merited. The guildie inviting someone is responsible for informing their friend of our loot rules.
