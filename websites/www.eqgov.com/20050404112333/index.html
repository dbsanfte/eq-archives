SOE and Blizzard Merger!!!
Posted By: Theramor - 04-01-2005 01:51 PM - Replies (0)
  Today, 04-01-2005, Blizzard Entertainment (BE) and Sony Online Entertainment (SOE) announce they will be merging in to one Entertainment conglomerate called “Blizzard and Sony” or simply (B.S.)

John Smedley, President SOE states: “We realize that by sharing our Accounting, Billing and Creative departments, we can see an immediate cost savings, and because of razor thin margins in such a competitive online market, the only way to really save money and make a profit is by merging so we can save on printing costs. I mean really, we both have “Entertainment” in our names and it seems so wasteful having two Huge Companies both wasting valuable resources just to display their name. Imagine how much ink and paper costs we can save by sharing those costs alone!”

“Another reason”, states Mike Morhaime, the President of Blizzard Entertainment, is we're losing customers to EQ2 because of our constant downtime and long wait ques to log in. If we can lose the customers to a game we own, it's win win for our pockets!” “So true “ says John Smedley, President SOE “If you think about it, our lack of PVP, guild halls and buggy patron system has been driving our customers to play WOW so why not capture their revenue by having WoW and EQ2 be part of one mega company? I mean my Mercedes isn't going to pay for itself!”

That brought chuckles from the champagne drinking, caviar eating, crowd of investors that were lining up to get their share of the money making machine.

Moorgard from SOE forums had this to say: “If we give our gamers enough BS, they'll come back for more.”

Both companies feel that by giving their customers B.S. when they ask for issues to be fixed, better content, or less downtime, their customers will either leave to play the other game, or be quiet and keep paying their monthly fees.

This is an obvious Win/Win for the B.S.ers.

The new slogan used by the merged company will be:



“With BS every gamer is happy!”

GoV-SoV Merger and Guild Writ Night
Posted By: Theramor - 01-21-2005 11:41 AM - Replies (0)
  Greetings all!

Our first bit of news concerns the merging of our Qeynos and Freeport based EQ2 Guilds.

Moorguard posted on the main EQ2 Boards that it is possible for Freeport Members to do Freeport Writs and a Qeynos Based Guild to gain status (and vice versa). Also, the members of the guild in Freeport will gain status points that they can use on Freeport Merchants.

In light of this we are officially uniting the guilds in EQ2.

Please see any of the Qeynos Guild Officers to be invited into Guardians of Veeshan. There will be still an officer in the Slayers of Veeshan to keep the name alive. Unfortunately you will lose any status points you have once you deguild from the Freeport Guild.

In light of that, our first Guild Event will be a Guild Writ Night.

Next Friday, January 28th at 6:00pm Pacific we'll work on Guild Writs and gain status for members and the newly united guild.

Check the boards for a post on the details shortly.


T
 

GoV EQ2 News
Posted By: Theramor - 01-03-2005 05:18 PM - Replies (0)
  Welcome back to Norrath after the holidays!

There are several threads on the EQ2 Boards that I'd like to bring to everyones attention.

First off, there is a Poll going on about nights for the Guild to work together on Guild Writs, Armour Quests, and Access Quests. Please choose a night or nights you could be available to help out your fellow guildmembers. There is also a discussion thread with the poll.

Another important topic going on is Tradeskill Books for Guild Members. If you have any spare Advanced Tradeskill Books, post on the thread what you have so they can be gotten into the hands of our Tradeskillers. The basic books available from the NPC Merchants only scratch the surface of the items that can be made. For example, you need an Advanced book to refine rare materials and then another Advanced Book to make Adept III Skills and Spells.

Nytak has started a thread for Tradeskill Work Orders. A place to post if you need any specialized items made, or to offer your work to other guild members. There are alot of interdepencies between the different tradeskill classes. This will help the tradeskillers get the items they need.

Auditu has started a thread on Adept Books. Go here if you have any extra Adept (or Master) book drops or completed Adept III Skills and Spells for the guild.

And last, but by no means least, Shatiean has posted a thread about his Teamspeak Server. I've used this before and its a great way to communicate between group / raid members much faster than typing. If you have a headset/mic combo and a good connection, consider giving this a try.

Snake appetizer to turkey dinner
Posted By: Aranash - 11-25-2004 08:40 AM - Replies (1)
  Well, me and Ima set out to work on Emp Ssra keys. There we were minding our own business when I decided to log in my ranger gimp to see what was up in Ssra. Well, low and behold mr Rhag Zhezum was up! By this time we already had a group there killing at Comm 1.

I sent a tell to the guild that we would be going after Rhag 1 and 2 and to come to Ssra if you could. A few more people came over and we were up to a group and a half.

We quickly moved over to Rhag 1, buffed, and the promptly killed him like he was a moss snake. He dropped nothing of much interest except for providing us with Remove Greater Curse and a Glowing Orb os Luclinite. Oh yeah, gratz my gimp ranger more Rhag 1 gear. I really should get him level 51 so he could use it =p

We then took a break for everyone to grab a bite to eat since we started really early.

After the break we headed up to Rhag 2. We were now up to 2 groups which is perfect for killing the over grown snake! We got there and buffed.... Then he died just as easily as Rhag 1! Gratz us another glowing orb of luclinite and a couple upgrades!

Just as we were finishing some people in AoF were finishing up a kill of RD with a pick up raid. I had already let them know what we were up to so they could help out if possible after the RD raid.

As we fought back down from Rhag 2 to the entrance they made there way over. We had some others log on and by the time everything was all said and done we had 21 people for Arch Lich. Would it be enough?.... I think it would be!

We quickly buffed not wanting the mobs on AL's floor to wipe and then we headed to him, killing everything in our path. We got to AL's floor and we started taking the mobs out with ease. All the tanks were offtanking, mobs were dying, and clerics were living.... It was a sight to behold, I think of beauty.

Once they were all dead we disarmed the traps and headed to AL's room were we made short work of his guards. We then went over strat and got setup for the kill. Aggro was built by the tanks and we were ready.....

In we went..... At first rampage was a little messed up but we eventually got that under control passing it between 3 tanks. For the most part AL..... Went much like Rhag 1 and 2..... He died easily as we steadily beat him down. We killed him flawlessly with no deaths and even had a rampage healer go LD midway through! It was truely a thing beauty, it was like poetry in motion, it almost brought a tear to my eye seeing everyone working together like that.

Thanks everyone there! It was a truely great raid to lead!
