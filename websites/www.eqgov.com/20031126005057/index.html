Finally
Posted by: [Aranash] on 11-15-2003 @ 01:24 PM

Well, with the last patch Sony had supposedly fixed Mujaki. So, we decided we would try it out and see if they really fixed it. Well, for once Sony did something right! They fixed th event!!! We all took our places and the south gravestone got a suprise as there were 3 Steeds waiting for them. Even though we lost Nathi we managed to get those 3 under control and killed before the event mobs start spawning.

For the event mobs our plan was to have 1 group at each of the 3 gravestones and 1 group in the middle to help out the 3 groups where needed. Once targetable we would gather and kill Mujaki. Our plan worked perfectly. No servants got through to the stage to assist Mujaki during our fight with him. The fight with Mujaki though messy went well with I think no deaths. Only thing I realized was.... He has a TON of hps.

In the end he was dead. It was a very night raid everyone. Awesome job all!
