Sleeper's Tomb
Posted By: Aranash - 03-21-2004 08:45 AM - Replies (2)
  Well, it was time for our weekly juant into Sleeper's Tomb. We started a bit late this week so everyone could go to the Arena and duel. We finally got started I believe around midnight. We had approx 18 which is a good number. We got ready for the Master of the Guard event started it. Though, this time things did not quite go as planned. We ended with far more mobs at one time then we could handle as we were still clearing the area when the event was started. About half of us zoned and we waited for the event to reset before going in and trying again.

Once it was reset we got and rezzed up and finished clearing the mobs. Then we got ready to start the event. Now, I have no idea why. But, this time we were getting 4 on aggro instead of 2 like the previous couple times we had done the event. Starting off with 4 was hurting us and we eventually had to zone out again. We let the event reset and tried to figure what we were doing wrong. Dae read of a different strat on the net while a friend of mine was telling me the samething. This made all the difference in the world. We got back in there rezzed up and got ready to do this again. This time everything went smooth except for Nev dying from trying to keep me alive.... I need AE tuant damn it! =p Once all the waves were dead we grabbed Master of the Guard and fell asleep! Once he was we moved into position for Progenitor.

Progenitor went easy as pie with about 15 people. Nothing really to say here except we owned him good.

With the new strat on Master of the Guard and already knowing Progenitor is easy. I strongly feel we can now do both these encounters with 12 or fewer people. We just have to make sure we have some clerics =)

A long night.....
Posted By: Aranash - 03-20-2004 08:32 AM - Replies (0)
  The night started early as me and Luha were out checking targets. Unfortunately KoE had just takening out Klandicar and were more then likely on to Sontalak. That meant no keys for us tonight. So we changed our attention to Luclin/PoP. Now, again if we had had more clerics we would of had an amazing night.

We found out that Rumblecrush was up and we quickly moved there as he does not require many to kill. We did great getting to him and killing him. We moved and dropped him in under 45 minutes. Was a nice short and sweet kill!

We then looked at what else was up. We knew Paffa, Carprin, Bertoxx trigger, and a few other flagging events were up. But, like I said earlier we had not clerics. So we moved to BoT and proceeded to do all 4 Lords.

For the most part we destroyed the Lords. Except for Kuanbyr and his tower. We really need to work on listening and paying attention. Way to many people died in this tower for no reason.

Other then that everything went smoothly for the most part. For the night we killed all 4 Lords and Rumblecrush. That's 5 targets in one night. Very nice work everyone.
