Agnarr The Storm Lord
Posted By: pippie - Today 02:05 AM - Replies (0)
  Well tonight was a new trial for the Guardians of Veeshan. The past couple of weeks we have been talking with a guild on Veeshan Named Crimson Tiers and decided tonight would be a great night to see how well we would raid together. So after a brief problem in Plane of Tactics trying to see if we could run at Tallon Zek we found out that IP and LoV had already engaged on Tallon. So we decided hell lets Try our first Raid together on Agnarr.

So we ran over to BoT and we got set up in the Safe room made groups and starting going over how things would work for the raid. After about 30 mins of setting up strat buffs and getting our egos in order we set off for the Courtyard.

Well someone forgot to invis or something and hell broke loose we actually wiped about 90 percent of the raid to adds and for some funny reason Auliffe decided to come down from the tower and smack everyone along with the rest of his cronies. So we let the mobs reset and rezzed back up in the graveyard and decided this time we will fight up to the courtyard. We got to the top and killed off the Storm watchers and go ready to start the event.

The First floor was interesting this was our first time doing anything together so once we figure out how to clear the 3 portals kill the 5 adds and beat the crap outta the first name we had it down.

Second Floor was pretty much the same kinda of Fight one thing was the mob that we had to kill had to be killed from a distance cuz he ae would eat us alive. So we ported up I lead the raid around the room killing off portals and adds while Gulween applicant of CT killed the Named. After the named went down we meded up a little bit and got ready to take out Agnarr.

Third Floor was kinda weird we had trouble porting up to Agnarrs level and Gulween died right away. I got up with the rest of the clerics and starting tanking Agnarr while Kyroskrane of CT lead the raid around killing the portals and the adds in the room.

ONce the Portals went down we all ganged up on Agnarr till he dropped to 75 percent life then one his friends comes outta the last portal raid went off the kill him while Imawarrior grabbed aggro from agnarr Cuz i died hehe. Imawarrior had agnarr the rest of the raid and did one outstanding job tanking this guy. People were getting rezzed back in and rebuffed for the next two name encounters it went pretty smooth from there. After the last named died Agnarr was at 22 percent health whole raid moved to him and he was down in about another minute.
