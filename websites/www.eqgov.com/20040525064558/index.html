Ssraeshza Temple
Posted By: Aranash - 04-22-2004 06:12 PM - Replies (4)
  Well, apparently yesterday was a patch day or something. Everything was up and wanting to be slaughtered. Tant had made mention earlier in the day that the Cursed cycle trigger was up. Well, this was a good 8 hours before we ever raid. So, I figured no shot at it.

As soon as I got home from work I figured I would check it out and see what was up. Well, I found that.... Not only was the Cursed Trigger up. But, so was Emp Ssra and High Priest. Well, obviously we do not have enough keyed for Emp Ssra(I think only 2 people in the guild are keyed for him). We also did not have a key to get to High Priest. So we settled on Cursed Cycle.

We got everyone moving there and got the groups together and headed down. We had 26 people there once everyone was accounted for. Not a bad number to go after Ssra bosses with.

While we buffed we went over how to trigger the cycle and the strat for the first mob. Everything seemed like it would go perfect. And... well.... It almost did. We triggered a glyphed covered serpent and pulled him to us. We did pretty good handling his AE FD, royal pain in the ass by the way, and steadily killed him. For the most part everyone listened and we dropped the bastage slowly but surely. Then woot! He was dead!

Then came the bad part.... I screwed up. I didn't know we had to engage Exiled with in 30 minutes of killing the serpent...... We rebuffed and were getting to pull when I got this tell..... "Exiled poofed"... At this point I go on a profanity laced tirade at my computer.... Though, I don't think my computer understood a word of it.

Oh well... we learned and next time we get the entire cycle!

High Priest was still up and I had a friend on who might have the key. I sent him a tell and asked him if he did and if he could come help us out and let us in. He had key! And he came to help us out bringing over his chanter and cleric.

We made our way up to High Priest prep area. Then we sat and waited. For future reference any cleric and/or necro in the guild please get the spell Banishment of Shadows. That way next time we do not have to wait for ever and a day so we atleast have a shot at the mob =p.

We finally had everything setup, buffed, strat gone over, and CC setup. It was time to engage High Priest........ errrr so we thought. Unfotunately, probably do to sleep deprivation I do not think everyone was paying attention as the CC did not go as planned. This caused us to not even get a shot at the High Priest unfortunately. Though, next time we should be more prepared and ready and will take a shot at this punk!

All and all it was a good night all! Hopefully in the future we can start living in Ssra so that everyone can get all warm and friendly with the snakes =)
