Manaetic Prototype
Posted by: [Aranash] on 09-04-2003 @ 02:53 PM

Tonight was a good night for us. We had yet another first.... We took out Manaetic Prototypes IX, X, XI. After weeks of trying to find these guys up on a raid night we finally found. We quickly assembled outside of Innovation then zoned in and waited for others and buffed just inside the factory door. We had about 30 people there tonight, execellent turn out all! Once set we went in and took these guys out like they were moss snakes in East Commons. We then sent out trackers to Bastion of Thunder and Plane of Storms to check for Lords we could kill. Nothing was up in BoT and all 3 Lords were up in Storms. So, we ran over to Storms and reformed up in the forest area. Unfortunately though to many people had to log soon so we were unable to try the Lord. But, we were able to take out a mini before that and keep 3 people closer to their BoT flag.

All in all it was an awesome night!

BoT - Tower Lords
Posted by: [Aranash] on 08-30-2003 @ 05:10 PM

Tonight we logged on to a nice little suprise. Almost every Tower Lord was up in BoT and not taken. Though, we quickly found out this was due to Primus Exodus killing Agnarr causing lag to be horrid. We decided to form up in one of the towers and wait out the lag. So, we all headed to the NE Tower home Auliffe Chaoswind and proceeded to wait...... and wait..... and wait..... and wait.... and wait..... then finally oh we waited some more! Eventually after like what seemed like an enternity they killed him and the lag went away. We started clearing up to Auliffe this was very uneventful and easy. We got to his platform and had Enyil and Kehlantra each charm an elemental. Then we went over our strategy. We then engaged Aullife and we found out.... He is suprislingly easy. With only 2 deaths we dropped time. He left a few nice trinkets behind for us.....
