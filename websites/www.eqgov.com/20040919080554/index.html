After a few more mobs in between a few names dropping more gear for us we found oursleves standing in front of THE FINAL ARBITOR.

Staight up melee fight was pretty easy after a good min and half he was down and we started setting up Strat for the Ancients. The first ancient was fun we pushed him off the bridge And myself and sarg got some gudluvin in the water we got summoned , AFter one amazing save by our mage Damodor coh ed me and sarg outta the water the mob was back on the bride and went down.
