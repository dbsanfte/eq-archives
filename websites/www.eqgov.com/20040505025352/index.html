  Another day and another Luclin boss mob down to GoV! We seem to be making a habit of this. Though we should not forget the things in Velious we have still to accomplish.

Tonight was a glorius night! It was a night that will be remembered for a long. For tonight we killed - Arch Lich Rhag `ZaDune! We did it not with zerg forces, but with skill through working together as team and as a family. It was awesome! The joy of watching this undead snake fall beneath the power of our swords and spells was amazing.

We should hit the guy in Grieg's a few times for his Remove Greater Curse right clickie so we can cure the trap in the future.

Everyone was paying attention and knew what their roles were and everyone executed their roles perfectly! Excpet me =/ I out tuanted Pippie when building up aggro and ended up tanking Arch Lich. Though Pippie/Heiken did an awesome job tanking the rampage. Through out the fight we did amazing! The healers especially the ones in my group since I messed up! Falleni, Theramor, Izlanna, and Eclaire awesome freaking job! Nev, Damis, Austen, and Aideen you guys did amazing as well. Pippie dying was not your fault as we needed to get Pippie levi'd so he wouldn't take fall dmg from the knockback.

Everything held perfectly and we slowly drained the hps from Arch Lich the excitement as his health got closer and closer to zero was amazing.

Then we had done it! We had killed Arch Lich!!! The kill of him was by far the single greatest solo kill for GoV EVER!!!! (Well atleast while I have been in the guild, though our first Vindi kill was close!)

It was amazing everyone! Thank you all for such an awesome raid. I hope we have more of these in the future!

Ssraeshza Temple
Posted By: Aranash - 04-22-2004 06:12 PM - Replies (4)
  Well, apparently yesterday was a patch day or something. Everything was up and wanting to be slaughtered. Tant had made mention earlier in the day that the Cursed cycle trigger was up. Well, this was a good 8 hours before we ever raid. So, I figured no shot at it.

As soon as I got home from work I figured I would check it out and see what was up. Well, I found that.... Not only was the Cursed Trigger up. But, so was Emp Ssra and High Priest. Well, obviously we do not have enough keyed for Emp Ssra(I think only 2 people in the guild are keyed for him). We also did not have a key to get to High Priest. So we settled on Cursed Cycle.

We got everyone moving there and got the groups together and headed down. We had 26 people there once everyone was accounted for. Not a bad number to go after Ssra bosses with.

While we buffed we went over how to trigger the cycle and the strat for the first mob. Everything seemed like it would go perfect. And... well.... It almost did. We triggered a glyphed covered serpent and pulled him to us. We did pretty good handling his AE FD, royal pain in the ass by the way, and steadily killed him. For the most part everyone listened and we dropped the bastage slowly but surely. Then woot! He was dead!

Then came the bad part.... I screwed up. I didn't know we had to engage Exiled with in 30 minutes of killing the serpent...... We rebuffed and were getting to pull when I got this tell..... "Exiled poofed"... At this point I go on a profanity laced tirade at my computer.... Though, I don't think my computer understood a word of it.

Oh well... we learned and next time we get the entire cycle!

High Priest was still up and I had a friend on who might have the key. I sent him a tell and asked him if he did and if he could come help us out and let us in. He had key! And he came to help us out bringing over his chanter and cleric.

We made our way up to High Priest prep area. Then we sat and waited. For future reference any cleric and/or necro in the guild please get the spell Banishment of Shadows. That way next time we do not have to wait for ever and a day so we atleast have a shot at the mob =p.

We finally had everything setup, buffed, strat gone over, and CC setup. It was time to engage High Priest........ errrr so we thought. Unfotunately, probably do to sleep deprivation I do not think everyone was paying attention as the CC did not go as planned. This caused us to not even get a shot at the High Priest unfortunately. Though, next time we should be more prepared and ready and will take a shot at this punk!

All and all it was a good night all! Hopefully in the future we can start living in Ssra so that everyone can get all warm and friendly with the snakes =)

Thought Horror Overfiend

Kuurus found an interesting thing out... Thought Horror Overfiend was up. Now, we have never killed him and being like noon est when we saw him up. I was doubting we would get the chance.

Once done we all logged for a bit and came back on later in the day. I asked Kuurus to check THO again. Well well what do ya know! He was still up!

Our first plan was to gather and get a mage across the invis bridge and set up CoH. Guess what we found out..... NO COH!!!! Argh! So, we waited until we had about 4 groups at zone in and started moving through. Wow... Do those mobs die quick with 24 people there! In no time we were at THO. While we were moving back there another group of people logged on and headed over. We setup Necrovac and brought them over. In the end we had I think 28 people there for the kill.

The fight is interesting as THO 2 AE's... 1 that slows all the melee and 1 that makes it to where all the caster will fizzle and fizzle a lot! That being said there is a reported way to block the AE. Well, I got the pillar we were to stand behind wrong! We ended up all getting hit with the AE and had to get it cleared off everyone. This took some time and by then we had repops to clear again.

We cleared those and we were set. Pippie was all ready to main tank... again... But, I think he has some sort of reflect heals armor or the clerics just flat out hate him. Because he died.... again... as usual.... Maybe one day he will live!

With him out of the way we had a real tank step in... Me! I tanked him the rest of the way and we slowly and I mean SLOWLY widdled down his hps. Damn fight was like 20 minutes long. Dude has way to many damn hps.

Once we had him slowed and healing set it was an easy fight. We dropped him before repops and got our tales out of there. It was an execellent job everyone! Our only death. Was, well..... Pippie.
