More WoW wackiness
Posted By: Ewan - 10-01-2006 08:55 AM - Replies (0) - Attachments (6)
  It's been an interesting week for GoV inside of World of Warcraft. Most of the week (and weekend) effort was spent trying to get parts to spawn the "Dukes" in Silithus, and let me tell you that the "Ultra-Safe" transporter has some ... side effects:



Much fun was had, and much loot was gotten from the Dukes... but that wasn't enough to fill the plans of the hearty GOV crew. Friday night we gathered up a grand total of 7 people and headed to Blackrock Spire. We handily took down everything in our path and cleared the lower part of the instance with very little difficulty. Before long we'll be doing the same thing to the Upper area of the instace, for sure!




Saturday night we had to find a way to top the excitement we had Friday, so off to Stratholme we go! This time we took on the "dead" side, facing up with the archvillian of the Argent Dawn, the "Baron". While the Baron was unable to comment when GoV was done with him, we did get reports that his head has been handed over to the Argent Dawn for some level of reward.



Grats to everyone on a productive weekend!

GoV'ers play WoW?!?
Posted By: Ewan - 09-27-2006 05:43 PM - Replies (1)
  This just in, in an amazing turn of events we've found that there are members of Guardians of Veeshan playing World of Warcraft! Updates for the WoW community have been few and far between, but here's what we've been up to.

Our jolly band has been taking on the baddies in the Eastern Plaguelands, with very successful trips to Stratholme and Scholomance under our belts. So far we've completed almost all of the live side of Stratholme, and the dead side is in our sites.




Aranash and Bryson showed off thier Femine sides while helping Highlord Taelan Fording discover he was batting for the wrong team. Too bad he dies at the end of the script!



We've also been working on individual quests that unlock raid events. Calalene and Bryson are almost done with their Onyxia keys, with Aranash and Fazzy and Alectra not far behind!



Soon we'll be working on Blackrock Spire, and who knows where from there?

Guild XP in out pockets and Dragon Blood on our hands (again)
Posted By: Thaadius - 09-10-2006 12:56 PM - Replies (0)
  Friday night alliance raid with T(Torment) and all i have to say is WOW! We first set up to take down Lockjaw the Ancient. Let me tell ya, he's gotten WAY bigger since we last seen him 500 years ago in Oasis of Marr. We took him down with little effort. Tons of guild xp from the kills in The Court of Al'Afaz a T6 Raid zone. Due to the amount of people and the "low quality" of my PC I was unable to get any screen shots. (/e nudges Theramor) As a guild we almost achieved a complete guild level! In one night!

Saturday evening we gathered up again with T(Torment) to do our first T5 Raid Spirit of the Lost. We gathered in Feerrott and moved to the entrance of Cazic-Thule. After a zoning in and a little confusion with mentoring we got the zone to green out for us. Nishu started pulling mobs and we started the crawl through the zone. Drof lead us through quite an intricut raid zone. After killing several named mobs and clearing out the area under the pyramid we set up to take out Venekor (you may remember him from the EQ2 demo movie). We had a little trouble at first with agro and a few deaths but no wipe (what kinda raid has no wipe?!). We killed Venekor and started setting up for Varanak. Unfortunately we couldnt do Varanak. The encounter is set up so that you have to kill both Venekor and Varanak at the same time. Well get em both next time.
