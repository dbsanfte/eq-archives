Sleeper's Tomb........ Here we come!
Posted by: [Aranash] on 10-20-2003 @ 07:21 AM

I logged on this afternoon and went to get Kyllrath, Qaladil, and Kuurus summoned from out of deep North ToV. Well, while running there I noticed something..... Klandicar was up! Not many were on so I went ahead and got everyone summoned and rezzed. Thanks Raphsody and Damisela =) Well, after I was rezzed I decided I would go see if Lendiniara the Keeper was up.... and she was!!! So, I started the raid channel and waited for more to log on. Valcor logged on and when he heard he sent out the call for everyone to gather. We started gathering and shortly had 3+ groups. We buffed and and got ready for Klandicar. With Heiken as the main tank we pulled Klandicar and things went pretty smoothly even though Mraaka added in the middle. We killed both of them pretty handly and rezzed up.

Then it was off to ToV to go and kill Lendi! This took us awhile to get accomplished unfortunately. As we are learning how to get around ToV with people who are KoS to Claws of Veeshan. Though we did eventually get everyone there. Once there we ran into more problems though..... Gating mobs again.... We will work on this and get it solved and soon =) Well, after about 3 wipes and Ubergina dragging tons fo corpse's we finally got the gating thing under control.... Once we did that we owned the mobs and Lendi..... Though Lendi was a bit harder then I care for as we did lose a few people. But, in the end we killed Lendi =)

Congratulations -
Brutusk - Lendiniara's Talisman (Sleeper's Tomb Key)
Heiken - Klandicar's Talisman (Sleeper's Tomb Key)
Diagonslow - Reinforced Dragonhide Breastplate
Mirimele - Boots of Superiority
Grantilis - Velium Maul of Superiority

That's 2 more Sleeper's Tomb Keys down =) and about 40+ left to go =)

Fun, exciting, and at times boring
Posted by: [Aranash] on 10-19-2003 @ 03:01 PM

That about describes our first venture into Temple of Veeshan North(NToV).

We started off slowly as we were unsure as to were we are supposed to CoH everyone to. Then after a slight problem while CoHing people we got everyone together. Then we wiped..... We reformed...... we wiped...... we reformed...... we wiped....... we reformed.... and in all that time I think we killed 4 mobs!!! Woot! Its what you call a learning experience. Tonight we learned many things. The most important of which flurry drakes hurt!!! We also learned that this area is well within our reach. As once we got settled down we did pretty good...... Until we let one gate.... Then it was wipe time again!!!! Second most important thing DO WHATEVER IT TAKES!!! BUT DO NOT LET THE MOBS GATE!!! The guy gated... and came back with a few friends and they ate us. Could of sworn I heard one of them said "Mmmm Yummy" after eating Eclaire. After this wipe about 10 people logged. We still had about 15 people there and we kept on. We were doing great..... Until the server was locked, reset, and rolled back.... So guess what happened next! That's right! We wiped again!!! Only this time NToV was fully popped so there was no way to get us out besides Necrovac! A HUGE thank you to Damisela for arranging this. You are wonderful!!(In more ways then one =p) She and the person who plays with dead things got everyone out except for Kyllrath.... I gotta go back for him later. After that we called it. Everyone was tired! Especially the leader who fell asleep at the keyboard.

All in all it was an awesome night. No one got any upgrades but we explored a new area and had a blast doing it. Hopefully we can get into NToV again soon and this time actually kill something of meaning....
