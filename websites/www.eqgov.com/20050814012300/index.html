Blood bath in Firemyst Gully
Posted By: Dolby - 06-26-2005 07:04 PM - Replies (1)
  The scheduled raid of Cauldron Hallow was canceled due to only having 2 groups of Guardians. It was quickly decided to try one of our guild raid zones. Every one went back to their home city and got the quest needed to enter. We entered the zone and since most every one was 40+ we all mentored down to the few lvl 31s in the raid.

The level 15 guild raid instance was kind of nice. Almost every named in the zone added to the event list in the guild window. A Palladium Torque dropped used for the Heritage quest for the Glowing Black Stone. Eseliica was rewarded the Torque since she was at the step in the quest of needing it.






Although this wasnt an uber leet raid I think every one had a lot of fun. I must say every one did a great job working together and listening. A big thank you goes to Baerek for leading the raid.
 

Deathfist Citadel Raid
