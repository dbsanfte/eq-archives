The Itraer Vius
Posted by: [Aranash] on 12-21-2003 @ 02:42 PM

Well, last night the servers went down for approximately an hour in the early evening. So, I figured we could do something. Upon getting back in we head to Ssra to try and grab Rhag' Zhezum and Rhag' Mozdeth. After waiting about 6 hours and having 3 groups there. I decided to start checking other targets. First was Grieg's and PE was taking out Servitor of Luclin. Next up was Umbral Plains and there was nothing there. Then I checked Akheva and low and be hold there is The IV! So we quickly started moving everyone to Akheva. While we were moving another group or so logged on and we were up to around 24 people.

I wanted to get this done quickly so we started moving in as soon as I arrived to Akheva and gathered and buffed while we pulled his room. Now, this encounter can be a bit tricky as he has a curse blindness which can proc on who ever he hits. If the MT can not control aggro everyone ends up blind. Which..... Is bad very bad. Because, when people are blind they end up doing things they otherwise wouldn't.

We got into his room and setup on the carpet in front of him and prepared to go after him. Max engaged and we had started. We were doing good and his hps where steadily dropping. Then people started taking aggro from Max..... Then people started getting knocked into the chain aggro mobs on the side of his room.... then people ran those mobs back to the raid..... Then we wiped =(

Not wanting to settle for defeat we quickly worked on getting corpse's dragged. We were held back a little because Ubergina was have some computer issues last night. But, once we got a group or so into where we prepped we were good. We quickly got everyone dragged and rezzed. We added 4 more people and went over the strat again. This time..... we were flawless, that's right ZERO deaths, was an execellent fight all. I think we all learned some thing tonight. I know I did! =)

The Villagers are freed...... Hello High Priest
Posted by: [Aranash] on 12-15-2003 @ 02:38 PM

Tonight..... Tonight was a good night! Servers had gone down so their was bound to be some stuff up..... Sure enough there was. Unfortunately what was up wasn't our primary targets, Behemoth and Aerin Dar. We had did find High Priest cycle in CoD up and a couple of the HoH trials up. We decided to hit the Villager's trial in HoH while we waited for everyone to log on.

We started forming in Halls of Honor and groups were being made. And then the zone decided that it was going to die on us for about 30 minutes. Thankfully however, when we got back in the trial was still spawnable. Unforutanely it took us a little while to get everyone grouped up and to the trial.... (We need to work on our running with invis skills) Once in place we got buffed and got ready. This trial is very easy and we roughly 40 people there which is overkill..... We started the trial and about 45 minutes later it was over. We only had a couple of deaths from the kiters. Other then everything was good! Though the trial was stingy and dropped nothing for us!!! Grr oh well we still flagged around 40 people for that trial! Gratz BC and GoV!

Next up.....

After finishing the trial we quickly verified that the High Priest was still up in CoD. Sure enough it was. We quickly headed into CoD and followed the lead of Casien and Nerkin. They lead us quickly to the pulling of Carprin and we dropped him with relative ease. We then moved farther in for the the next of the Preist's men. We dropped him easy....... But, thanks to Mek we wiped from his pets =p His pets dying to rage started a train that eventually had 100+ mobs in it...... And I thought Heiken pulled some rough trains! Once rezzed we moved back and continued on our way. The next of the Preist's men presented little problem to us. We dropped both of them with great ease. Then...... We stumbled on this guy Raex.... Something about an unslowable mob that hits 1600hps and lifetaps for 5k hps that makes him really hard..... He dropped the raid in under a minute easy I believe. This time though we managed to get a cleric out(Good job Casien!). We got back in and this time had every healer healing the MT and then those who called rampage. And we dropped him!

All that was left was the High Priest Ultor Whatshisface =p. Once we were past Raex I knew we could kill High Priest. And we did first try no deaths. He was the easiest part of the whole cycle! Gratz BC and GoV 36 people flagged for Bertoxx!

Was great night of flagging all and good work and willingness to hang in after 2 wipes!

Grummus and Terris Thule
Posted by: [Aranash] on 12-13-2003 @ 05:45 PM

Valcor had planned it with Blue Cloak..... It was our first real flagging night in a long time. I logged on and started talking with Casien. We had Grummus and Behemoth up but not nearly enough for Behemoth. So we decided to go after Grummus. We had approximately 40 people when we decided to start. By the time we reached Grummus we were up to 66..... Val lead us there and Casien quickly went over strat...... Unfortunately not everyone listened/paid attention and the pets were over zealous. We were unable to execute the strat laid out by Casien and we wiped.

Though we wiped we were not giving up. We rezzed up reformed and flew back through the castle with a vengance. Casien reiterated every phase of the strat and then it was on. This time everything went exactly as planned. We dropped Grummus without a death I believe. Though some died to his DoT's afterward. We looted, flagged, and moved on......

Next up Terris Thule!

First I would like to apologize to the 3 or 4 who were not able to join us because of lack of completing the Thelin Maze in Nightmare. We WILL get this for you very soon.

Forming for Terris Thule took awhile. Figuring out who we could and could not get in because of lack of flags was difficult. But, it was finally done. We moved to Terris Thule very quickly and Casien again went over strat. We engaged.... and we executed perfectly with no one dying. Terris Thule went down without much of a fight.

It was a good night for all. Can not wait until we take out Aerin Dar and Behemoth!

Ring of Vulak'Aer
Posted by: [Aranash] on 12-12-2003 @ 09:48 PM

Never in my wildest dreams did I ever think I would see the day when GoV would attempt this........

Until last night.

I logged on and was told that BC was inviting us to NToV to clear the balance of named up and to possibly take a shot at Vulak'Aer. Most of the guild was game and was already in or on their way to ToV for the fun and killing. Casien and Nerkin did a good job getting everyone in, setup, and moving. Awesome job both of you!

Our first target once inside was the popcorn machine a.k.a. Lord Vyemm. And boy did he live up to his name =p I think that was the second most annoying fight in the history of my EQ experience. The 1st is coming up later =p After many had died we finally killed Lord Vyemm. Once he was dead it was on to the triplets.....

The triplets I had heard the pull was hard..... But, we dropped Cekenar, then Zlexak, and then Sevalak really really easily.... I was very suprised. With all three triplets dead it was on to the final named up in NToV.

We headed to Lady Whirl Til You Hurl..... Then I believe half the raid went afk afterwards to go and puke from spining so damn much. I will admit this is one fight where I envy every ranger with AM3/EQ. After a long dizzying battle she was dead as well. We then moved in for the Vulak.....

We made our way to the area of Vulak's Ring.... Hark setup the tanking order 5 deep for all the mobs we were about to encounter. Then we started..... And honestly we were doing really really well.... Until I had forgotten something about how the Ring was done on a previous raid here I had done. Forgeting this caused us to get 2 flurries and 2 splitters..... Which killed us....
We found out though... We can do this EASY! and we will come back and do it again!

Thanks again for the fun evening BC it was a blast!
