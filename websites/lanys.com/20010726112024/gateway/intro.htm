INTRODUCTION TO THE SUPERNATURAL GATEWAY


     I think its time to admit something to each other. Society, in general, is suffering from a serious loss of meaning in life. Alarm clocks wake us up, our lives are micromanaged and twisted awry by the insane schedules we keep up, and every day we find new ways to avoid being wrong. We are losing the spirit of brotherhood, of community, and the basic fraggin expectation that your fellow man will not stab you in the throat if it would get him a promotion or his name in a newspaper.

    There are many ways that mankind is discovering to begin to tear down the walls we have built all around us. Religious philosophies of all kinds, theories of perceptual evolution (psychic development), scientific approaches to life in virtual space or higher spatial dimensions, reaching out to universal life, and rediscovering magic in a time where we are still left with the ideological biases associated with the Industrial revolution... are all ways in which man is trying to inject hope into a society that has become very bleak and very cold. I want to help.

    These basic ideas and this small chunk of virtual space is the construct. The people who inhabit it are the seeds. This is the absolute basic framework, it began with one page and some message boards. How it grows and the directions it takes will be completely shaped by those of you who choose to call it home.

Our Journey begins.
