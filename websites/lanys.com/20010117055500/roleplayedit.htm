ROLEPLAYING
by Futunctious


Hail and well met!

We have a tremendous opportunity to influence the direction this new server will take, and I hope to encourage everyone to join myself, my guild (PKD), and the other guilds who have posted on this site as we attempt to make this server a home for roleplayers.

Different servers have different personalities; they are known by the character of the players, as well as by the characters themselves. If we are vocal, if we submit to the various public forums, hold events, encourage GM participation, wage guild wars and form guild alliances - in short, if we take an active role in our game play we will attract the type of players who enjoy roleplaying, and repell those who are not interested. And we can make much more out of our time in Norrath.

We all love the game, but how many of us play passively- waiting for GM events, camping, farming, otherwise responding to the game rather than initiating? There are a thousand or more other players on with you at any given time, and while many of those will be camping the PGT, certainly a few hundred will be left over for a decent storyline. Guilds of like mind should ally, those of opposite attitude should war. And guild leaders should be in
regular contact with one another to develop ways in which the entire EQ experience can be made more exciting for everyone.

So play well! Whether you like to solo or group, whether you want to join a guild, perhaps create one yourself, or whether you are a free-agent grouping differently each time you login, play well. Be active, and be creative. And let everyone know that there is a new home for the imaginative player who wants a little bit more out of EQ than merely an eBay sale.

If you are interested in PKD or any of the guilds posted here, by all means contact the guild leaders. They want to hear from you.

Good luck! I'll see you in-game...

Futunctious
Guilld Leader of PKD
