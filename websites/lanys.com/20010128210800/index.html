GUILD MERGER

Two mighty guilds merge.

The sun shone bright that morning, the frogs did sound & the birds did sing. Until IT began . . . then the animals grew silent, the newbies became awed, and the powerful did take notice as the sun and the moon did merge and march across the land of Norrath in an Eclipse.

Let it be known by the peoples of Lanys T'Vyl that the mighty and honourable Rune of Flame and Crystalline Knights have merged! WE ARE THE ECLIPSE OF NORRATH!

Chaos Plague
Guildleader: Mandidar Supanuka
Guild Elders: Uhgree Bugga, Zambone Steelrod
Guild Site: http://chaosplague.webjump.com
Chaos Plague is a guild comprised of people who get along well together without the typical infrastructure of most guilds on Lanys T'Vyl. Because of this, the guild has remained fairly small in size. Guild chat is a great place to keep in contact with the rest of the members even when you are several zones apart. It is very "out of character" with such information as price checks, the latest gossip, and more.
