Welcome!
Hello. You have reached my little niche in the word. Captured in Ink is a place for me to put my writings (yes, I did write everything here, every single little detail, coding and etc...).

This is version 6, Lilies. I made this layout all by myself (the third one, to be exact), I'm so proud of myself! Anywhoo...I thought it was time for a color change. version 5, Glimpse of the Past, was black-and-white; my sister professed a great dislike for the lack of color. And since summer is just around the corner (where I live it's still cold), I decided to use a gold-ish color. Ok, maybe the layout is more brown than gold but close enough. I also went back to the flower theme but this time, the image isn't the big central background.

Credits: the image used in from GettyImages, and the brushes on the top are from Celeste. Everything was made in Adobe Photoshop 7.0.
