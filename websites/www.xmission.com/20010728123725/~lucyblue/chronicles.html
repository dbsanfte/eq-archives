Welcome to the Chronicles of Ilsik Haucil.

Ilisk Haucil is a guild of honorable and noble persons who reside in on the world of Norrath. Originally Founded by Tallion avu Winden Haucil, son to the House Winden Haucil, it was formed based on honesty, loyalty, and honor. Due to years of hostility in the land, the guild fell into corruption. It was Valkith avu Winden Haucil, Tallion's sister, who lead the guild out of corruption and restored honor to the name.

Norrath is a world which resides in the computer online game Everquest. It is a place where friends can spend time adventuring, completing quest, acquiring weapons and armor, and spending time together. The environment is a rich interactive community.

Within these pages you will find the Chronicles of Ilsik Haucil. Included are a photo gallery, a member list which is updated a few times a month, as well as the guild bylaws and charter. There is also a link to the message board where the guild and their friends actively plan raids and activities. To the left you will see the status of the Everquest game servers. Ilsik Haucil resides on the Mithaniel Marr Server.





From the strength of the warrior through the firepower of the magician, the guild forms stong groups which are able to eliminate the most terrible forces on the face of Norrath. Skills honed over time and sharpened to resemble a fine steel blade are one of the defining characteristics of the guild. To become a member is an honor and a privaledge, not to be taken lightly.
