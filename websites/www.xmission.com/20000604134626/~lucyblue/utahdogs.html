Just a Note: I am sad to say that Kip passed away on Saturday, April 29, 2000, at 8:15 a.m. He will be greatly missed.

Utah Dogs is a site dedicated to dogs and their people in Utah. Within these pages you will find resources for adoption, rescue, breeders, and places to take your dogs offlead. There are many dogs in Utah which are getting involved in many activities, and their people are always looking for new and exciting ways to have fun with them.

From agility to obedience, to camping and hiking, and sleeping peacefully on the couch, there are a variety of dog related activities possible in Utah. This web site is intended to be a resource for dogs and their companion people living in Utah. Some of the features of this site include pages dedicated to dog resources such as training clubs, rescue organizations, breed clubs, agility clubs, obedience training, assistance animals, and more. There is also a web ring; if you have a web site dedicated to dogs in Utah, please join the ring! Also, there is a web forum where people in Utah can discuss various topics, post questions, and just general talk. Please explore the site, and you will find all this information and more! If you're not sure where to find information, just use the search tool located on the bottom of the page.

Utah Dogs is a new web site, created January 2000, and hopefully will grow quickly. In order for it to do so we need input! So if you have pictures or stories, information on hiking or camping areas around Utah, please submit them to me. I will get them on the site as soon as possible. The more submissions I get, the richer this site can become.

The picture on this page is of Kip and Lucy, the two dogs you will see most often throughout these pages. Kip is a shepherd mix, born in 1986, and Lucy is a smooth blue collie, born in 1999. And now they have a younger brother, Levi, who will also be in the pictures around this site. These Utah Dogs are my companions and will pose for me as often as I can convince them to keep still!
