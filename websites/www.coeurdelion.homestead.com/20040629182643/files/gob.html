The Gathering of Banners
18 February 2004

[GoB Forums]

The Gathering of Banners is an alliance of pro-roleplaying guilds. This alliance was formed in the spring of 2001 to so that players would not have to leave their (usually smaller) RP guilds in order to participate in the harder encounters Norrath has to offer.

The Gathering of Banners holds raids ranging in difficulty from Chardok Nobles raids to excursions in the Elemental Planes.

Every member of a GoB guild is automatically a GoB member, and may attend GoB raids. While on GoB raids, GoB loot and epic rules apply. GoB members have access to additional forums on the GoB boards, including the Raids forum where scheduled raids are posted and the Bank forum where the Points List and Spell Bank threads are located. PoP key progression is tracked in the PoP Flags forum. Please note that to be considered for epic drops you must post on the appropriate class thread in the Quests forum. Changes in policy are announced in the Open Council forum.

The Gathering of Banners is administered by a council known as Sub Rosa. Each member guild has two votes on the council. Coeur de Lion's representatives are Mallo and Tintari.

Mallo and Luniel are admins for the Gathering boards.
Nitika is in charge of the GoB spell bank.
Jaered (Elven Elite) administers the Points List.
