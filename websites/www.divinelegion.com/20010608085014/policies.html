Policies

Divine Legion events

Divine Legion family members should go to the events if they can.  We all try to help each other as much as we can.   But of course these events are not required to be a Divine Legion member.

Divine Legion event loot rules (NEW POLICY CHANGES ARE BEING MADE NOW 4/9/01)

In a few simple words "Need Before Greed".

1. If a "need before greed" non-nodrop item is looted, this item belongs to the guild. If you upgrade your equipment beyond the item, it is to be given to a guild officer for redistribution to someone else who "needs" the item.

2. Item hunts are to strengthen Divine Legion by equipping its members
better.

3. Only divine legion members that can use an item and will equip the item
looted can lotto for the item.

4. Members at events may not roll for another member that is not present.
You must be at the event in order to lotto the item.

5. After everyone present during the item drop that you will use/equip the
item has acquired the item that has dropped, everyone else present can lotto
for the item to do with what they please.

6. If someone is late for a DL event,  they will sit out one lotto before
they can participate in the lotto.

Divine Legion officer status

Officers in Divine Legion have duties they perform to insure the growth and success of our family.   If the tasks an officer is supposed to perform don't get done, then the duties will be reassigned accordingly.

Divine Legion spell looting

All spells that are looted by a guild member should be turned into the Guild Treasurer.  When you need a spell ask the Treasurer and when it is acquired by a member the spell is then given out appropriately.

Divine Legion donations

Any item you wish to donate to Divine Legion will go to the Guild Treasurer.  This person will validate whether someone in the guild could get some use out of the item... but if not sell it for plat to into the guild bank.  The guild bank is used for Raids Stones,  Plane of Sky entrance, and for anything else the guild needs to spend plat on to function.

Divine Legion new members

Anyone is welcome to be part of our family,  they only requirement is that the person joining has friends currently in Divine Legion to vouch for the person joining. And be at least of level 40 if at all possible

Divine Legion complaints

If anyone has any problems with a member of the guild, or the way things are being handled.  All reports are to go to the guild leader or one of its officers.   Complicated issues will be handled in the officer meetings.
