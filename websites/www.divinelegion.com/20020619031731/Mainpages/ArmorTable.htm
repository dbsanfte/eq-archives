Armor Table

In all cases, many of the quest armor components are shared between classes.

  • Plate: Warrior, Shadow Knight, Paladin, Cleric, Bard
  • Chain: Ranger, Rogue, Shaman
  • Leather: Druid, Monk
  • Silk: Enchanter, Magician, Wizard, Necromancer

The gems components are split into three groups:

  • Melee: Warrior, Monk, Rogue, Paladin, Shadow Knight, Ranger
  • Priest: Cleric, Druid, Shaman
  • Caster: Enchanter, Magician, Wizard, Necromancer

The Gems drop in the following zones: Cobalt Scar, Dragon Necropolis, Sirens Grotto, Velketors Labyrinth, Wakening Lands, Icewell Keep
