GuildStructure

1. Master/Mistress of Truth - Top Person (s) in the guild.
2. Sentinel of Truth - In charge of Recuitment.
a. Crusader of Truth - Actively search for potential recuits for Asa-Tru.
3. Ambassador of Truth - In charge of Inter-Guild Relations.
a. Diplomat of Truth - Actively serach for other Guilds to ally with.
4. Scribe of Truth - Keeper of the Knowledge, maintainer of the Guild Records (and website).
a. Artisian of Truth - Assists the Scribe with graphics, screenshots, maintenance of the website.
5. Hunter/Huntress of Truth - In charge of planning/executing Guild and Inter-Guild hunts.
6. Keepers of Truth - These are the members of the guild who are able to sponsor new members, they are not officers.
7. Warder of Truth - The main body of the guid, most members will be Warders.
8. Initiates of Truth - New recuits into the Guild, 30 day probation period, then promoted to Warder.

Rank Definitions and Descriptions

1. Master of Truth (Officer) - As stated, this person (s) head up the guild and if needed, make the final and ultimate decisions. They are the figurehead (s) for the guild both internally and externally. The Guild Masters should be the ones to contact GM's for game related information, a.k.a. when halls will be available, ideas/suggestions to the game that relate to the guild, people in the game who are harassing other players, etc. The Guild Master (s) help develop and create guild structure and make sure that it is being followed. They are assisted in this by the various Officers and members of the guild.
Member of the Council of Truth.

2. Sentinel of Truth (Officer) - As stated by definition, the sentinel is a sentry, a guard. This person guards the guild to those who would enter the guild and do not belong. They are the overseers of the Crusaders who bring in new members. The Sentinel proscribes the questions to be asked to prospective members, has oversight to all induction’s in the guild, and answers questions that may arise from the Crusaders. The Sentinel works closely with the Huntress in proscribing quests when needed. The Sentinel is of equal rank as the Huntress.
Member of the Council of Truth.

2a. Crusader of Truth (Officer) - The Crusader is the main force for recruiting for the guild. They travel through the lands finding and helping induct new members to the cause of Asa-Tru. The Crusaders and the Sentinel are the only ones who should question perspective members. If a current member has a person they want to try and get into the guild, they must have a Crusader or the Sentinel online to screen that person. This is a secondary officer's position as they are able to induct members into the guild once clearance has been given.
Member of the Council of Truth.

3. Ambassador of Truth (Officer) - The Ambassador is a master at diplomacy, able to make alliances at the drop of a hat. The Ambassador is in charge of establishing alliances with other guilds and informing the Guild Masters, as well as the general body of the Guild of these Alliances. The Ambassador takes information gathered by the Diplomats, who are his/her eyes and ears in Norrath.
Member of the Council of Truth.

3a. Diplomats of Truth (Officer) - The Diplomats are the main force actively seeking other guilds in Norrath who are similar in belief to Asa-Tru, and attempting to establish friendly relationships with those guilds. They inform the Ambassador of the guilds that they find, and assist the Ambassador in the creation of Alliances with those guilds.
Member of the Council of Truth.

4. Scribe of Truth (Officer) ­ The Scribe is the person who helps keep the histories of the guild, keeps the web page up to date, and helps keep the goings on of the members known to others. Posting pictures, stories, character histories are just some examples.
Member of the Council of Truth.

4a. Artisian of Truth (Officer or Not an Officer) - The Artisians are the artists, poets and historians, of all types, who assist the scribe with the development of the history of all the members of the guild, the guild itself, and the pictures that are found within the halls of the Guild Manor.
Member of Council of Truth if an officer, Keeper of Truth, if not an officer.

5. Hunter/Huntress of Truth (Officer) - The Huntress is the person in charge of sending out members to get specific items needed by the guild or its members. He or she will work closely with the Sentinel if/when a new member is sent on a quest. These quests will bring specific items into the guild. It is up to the Huntress to help the Sentinel proscribe these quests. If a member is in need of a certain item, they should let the Huntress know of it. This person uses the Scouts as his/her primary resource for items, but the whole guild is under his/her will when pursuant to items that are in need. The Huntress is equal in rank to the Sentinel.
Member of the Council of Truth.

6. Keeper of Truth (Not an Officer) - Keepers of Truth are those members of the guild that have been authorized by the Council of Truth to sponsor new members. No Keeper may sponsor more than TWO applicants in a 30 day period. Keepers of Truth must have been in the guild for a minimum of 60 days. They must show an excellent knowledge of the guild charter and heirarchy. Keepers of Truth will be appointed by 2/3 majority vote of the Council of Truth.

7. Warder of Truth (Not an Officer) ­ The Warder is the body of the guild. They help other members, group and fight together, help find new prospective members to be handed over to the Crusaders, when in need, help get items for the guild, and as a whole, help the guild function. As all members do, they will help tithe to the guild for a guild hall or other items that require coin.
Some may be Keepers of Truth, once the division is organized.

8. Initiate of Truth (Not an Officer) ­ The Initiate is the newest of the members of the guild. They have not yet gone through the quest process or truly proven their worth to the guild. Until that time which they do, they will remain an Initiate. They will have no privileges within the guild and are treated as any plebe would be.
New members of the guild, with less than 30 days in the guild.
