GuildAwards

**Actions known as "above and beyond" can be called Medals of Asa-Tru. We need to track these. We need a member list that will have an icon of a Medal and we can put a dot under each person's name for each Medal they earn. We are able to then track this, others can see their accomplishments, and we'll know who's ready for promotion.

OR

"Above and Beyond" can be earning awards from the guild. And we come up with 4 or 5 VERY FAST and when they earn a Medal, they get that icon (small icon) under their name and when they get the right mound, we see who's ready for promotion and others see what medals another person has received.
(I think you get the idea for both concepts.)

Medals:

Medal of Sacrifice - Those that selflessly give of themselves time and time again are recognized with this medal. This medal recognizes the members who help not only Asa-Tru, but also other citizens of Norrath. Sacrifice of oneself symbolizes this award.

Medal of Peity - Those that continuously strive to goodness, devoutness and piousness are recognized with this medal. This medal recognizes the members who have shown that giving, charity and good deeds are what drive and characterize the members of Asa-Tru. The continued search of higher ideals symbolizes this award.

Medal of Valor - Those that willingly face danger, without flinching, in order to ensure the betterment of Asa-Tru are honored with this medal. This medal recognizes the valiant bravery shown, in combat, and out of combat, which symbolizes all that Asa-Tru stands for. Personal Bravery symbolizes this award.

Medal of Honor - Those who show themselves to have a high level of personal integrity, in combat, and out of combat, are recognized with this medal. This medal shows all that the level of integrity of Asa-Tru is beyond reproach, and increases the reputation of Asa-Tru. High Personal Integrity symbolizes this award.

Medal of Achievement - Those who reach the goals that have been set by them and those who are ranked above them are recognized with this medal. This medal shows that a member of Asa-Tru does not give up, and will strive to achieve the goals set by not only themselves, but also those around them. Hard work and dedication symbolize this award.

Medal of Devotion - Those who devote themselves to working toward bettering themselves and their Asa-Tru brethren are recognized with this medal. This medal recognizes devotion of time and energy to assisting others in whatever endeavor they are attempting to accomplish. Devotion to the ideals of Asa-Tru symbolize this award.

Awards (Mostly for fun and humor purposes):

Puller's Award - For outstanding pulling on an Asa-Tru raid, given to anyone in the "Pull Team" who does an outstanding job, and gets the job done.

Healer's Award - For outstanding healing on an Asa-Tru raid, given to any healer type who does an outstanding job and gets the people healed.

Tanker's Award - For outstanding tanking on an Asa-Tru raid, given to any tank type who does an outstanding job in dealing and taking damage.

Nuker's Award - For outstanding nuking on an Asa-Tru raid, given to any caster type who manages to nuke for tons of damage without getting themselves killed.

Death Award - For taking one for the gipper. For anyone who dies, or dies multiple times, trying to do something for the guild. A medal can often accompany this award.

Transport Award - For willingly transporting members all over the face of Norrath, this award is given to wizards and druids who teleport Asa-Tru members all over the place.
