  1. Asa-Tru: A Guild established on the ideals of Friendship, Family, Valor, Honesty, Integrity, and, most of all, Fun.
    (back to top)

    1. Officers: These are the people who have the ability to invite others into Asa-Tru. All Officers are members of the Council of Truth. To become an Officer appointed to a position, you must have a minimum of 8 months in Asa-Tru, and be recommended by one or more Officer of Asa-Tru, you can express your interest in becoming an Officer by contacting the Master, and explaining what position you are applying for and why you feel that you want the added responsibility. Officers from guilds that are absorbed into or merged with Asa-Tru, will be voted upon by the existing Officers, and placed into positions based upon a majority vote.
      (back to top)

      1. Master (1): Person who holds the Title of Guild Master of Asa-Tru. Head of Asa-Tru, who leads Asa-Tru in all things. All Asa-Tru Officers and Members ultimately answer to the Master. The Master should be the one to contact the Game Masters, in the event that Game Information is relevant (i.e. When Guild issues arise; Asa-Tru inadvertently disbanded, etc.). The Master has final say in Asa-Tru policy, and is guided by the Guardians and Council of Truth. The Master is top in the chain of command of Asa-Tru. Must have a minimum of one year in Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)

      2. Guardians (2): Seconds in command or Lieutenants to the Master. These are the 2 that make up the remainder of the Top Three. The Guardians act as advisors to the Master, and assist the Master with the application of policy within Asa-Tru. All other Officers and Members will answer to the Guardians, should the Master be unavailable. Position appointed by Master, and voted upon by Council of Truth. Must be an existing Officer, and have a minimum of one year in Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)

      3. Sentinel (1): By definition, the Sentinel is a sentry, or guard. This person guards Asa-Tru from those who would enter Asa-Tru, and do not belong. They are the overseer of the Crusaders, who take potential initiates through the induction process. The Sentinel proscribes the questions to be asked of prospective members, and has oversight to all inductions into Asa-Tru. The Sentinel will also answer any questions that may arise from the Crusaders. The Sentinel is appointed by the Master and is voted upon by the Council, must have a minimum of 9 Months in Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)

        1. Crusaders (4 -6): The Crusaders are the main force for recruiting within Asa-Tru. The Crusaders travel through the lands, finding and helping to induct potential members to the cause of Asa-Tru. The Crusaders, Sentinel, or Top Three should be the only Officers to question perspective Members. If a current Member has a person they wish to sponsor into Asa-Tru, they must have a Crusader or the Sentinel online to screen said person. This is a Secondary Officer position, as they are able to induct members into Asa-Tru, once clearance is given from the Sentinel or Top Three. Appointed by the Sentinel, and voted upon by the Council., must have a minimum of 6 months in Asa-Tru.
          (back to top)

      4. Scribe (1): Self Explanatory Position. The Scribe maintains the Asa-Tru Domain, http://www.asa-tru.com/ and keeps record of all members who are actively in Asa-Tru. The Scribe provides information to the members of Asa-Tru and keeps the domain updated. The Scribe is appointed by the Master of Truth and voted upon by the Council of Truth, must have a minimum of 9 months in Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)

        1. Artisans (1 - 3): The Artisans assist the Scribe with gathering information, development of the Domain, graphic art, etc. The Artisans are recommended by the Scribe, and approved by the Top Three, must have a minimum of 6 months in Asa-Tru.
          (back to top)

      5. Huntsman (1): The Huntsman is the person in Asa-Tru that creates, approves and runs Asa-Tru hunts and raids. It is the responsibility of the Huntsman to delegate duties to the Hunters, who may also create and run Asa-Tru hunts or raids. It is the responsibility of the Huntsman to set up hunts that will best help Asa-Tru and the Members of Asa-Tru. The Huntsman is appointed by the Master and is voted upon by the Council, must have a minimum of 9 months in Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)

        1. Hunters (2 - 4): Assists the Huntsman in the planning and leading of hunts and raids, etc. Hunters are recommended by the Huntsman and are voted upon by the Council, must have a minimum of 6 months with Asa-Tru.
          (back to top)

      6. Merchant (1): Heads up the Asa-Tru Vault, maintains information regarding the items that are available to members of Asa-Tru within the vault. The Merchant is appointed by the Master and voted upon by the Council, must have a minimum of 9 months with Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)

        1. Tradesmen (1 - 3): Assist the Merchant, by making themselves available to handle items given to the vault, or give/trade items to Asa-Tru members. Tradesmen are recommended by the Merchant and are approved by the Top Three, must have a minimum of 6 months with Asa-Tru.
          (back to top)

      7. Esprit (1): Asa-Tru Morale officer. This position is in charge of keeping up morale within Asa-Tru, and getting to the bottom of any and all morale problems that are manifesting themselves within Asa-Tru. Members should go to this person, in the event that they feel that they are experiencing a problem with the morale within Asa-Tru. The Councilor is also in charge of working with the Huntsman or Hunters to create guild run events that are not hunts or raids. Councilor is appointed by the Master and voted upon by the Council. Must have a minimum of 9 months in Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)

    2. Members: This is the general body of Asa-Tru, the individuals who actually make up a majority of Asa-Tru.
      (back to top)

      1. Noble: The Nobles are members who have a minimum of 6 months in Asa-Tru. The Nobles are able to sponsor people into Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)

      2. Keeper: The Keepers are members with a minimum of 4 months in Asa-Tru. The Keepers are the first tier of members who can sponsor potential members into Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)

      3. Warder: Warders have been in Asa-Tru a minimum of 1 month. Those members who are absorbed into Asa-Tru or join during a merger are automatically given the rank of Warder.
        (back to top)

      4. Initiate: Initiates are new members to the guild, and are subject to a 30 day evaluation, during which they can be removed for any reason.
        (back to top)

    3. Non-Members:
      (back to top)

      1. Applicants: People who are interested in becoming members of Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)

      2. Allied Guilds: Guilds that we do raids, events and otherwise help whenever possible.
        (back to top)

      3. Friends: People who we associate with regularly, but are not members of Asa-Tru.
        (back to top)
