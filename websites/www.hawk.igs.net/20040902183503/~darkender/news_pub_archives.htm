General Message and Mission Statement   Posted Thursday, November 1, 2001
Dear members of Darkender Bloodline:

In these past few weeks you have witnessed growth in our guild, and diversity in our members, class, race, and personalities. What comes with this growth is accepting the responsibility that we must respect many personal goals, personal interest, and the personal needs of "everyone". If we fail at this responsibility, we will loose the very thing we are trying to accomplish, build "A Great Guild, where every member truly enjoys their guildmates, themselves, and what we bring to the Norrath World."
This is the vision I've always had, as your Guild Leader.

In this vision, I do not see this guild as having one leader, and one voice. I see Darkender Bloodline as a guild where leadership and authority "are not" practiced by one, but "shared by many". Many that are quite talented, compassionate, adventurous, and want to grow in the unity of Norrath and in our guild.
In the last officers meeting, I announced the position of Guild 1st Officer to Lord Brother Horath, And Guild 2nd Officer to Lord Brother Azzerin. In their new duties, and by rank, they shall replace me as your Guild Leaders during my absence, and assist me during times when I'm with you.

Officers Cronicx, Lady Glennda, and Officer Ommadon have also accepted and been appointed as our new Raid Committee and Commanders. In these duities they shall set up all raids, raid rules, set date and time schedules for all participation levels, and to the needs of our guild members. They will need our full support and unconditional co-operation if we are to be successful in this area of the EQ world.

As your GL, I've chosen to concentrate on this goal, and closely follow up on our progess. At the same time, I'll be working to strengthen our relationship with other high level guilds and those that make up AoT.
I want to personaly thank all members that are young in age for believing in us and joining our family. I want to also give a special thanks to the mid and higher level members for being so dedicated to the point of some placing their own immediate goals aside, and on hold to help everyone in Darkender Bloodline. You are the true Guild Leaders!

Lets continue to get organized, focus on one another, and make this happen! WE CAN DO IT!

Sincerely
Cinque Darkender
Your Guild Leader
