STORYLINE
  As is known, a weak but very intelligent human named Erud led a band of "High Humans" such as himself away from Antonica, and to the continent of Odus, where he Founded the City of Erudin, which became home of the newly born race known as the Erudites.           The 5 captains of Erud's National Guard were all Akin, and were of the Royal Darkender family, historians speculate whether or not Erud himself was a Darkender.  These 5 captains journeyed from Antonica with Erud, and each of them was a master at one of the divine arts, meaning there was a Paladin Captain, a Cleric Captain, a Magician High Sorcerer, a Wizard High Sorcerer, and an Enchanter High Sorcerer.           These 5 fought many battles to protect Erudin from evil and from the heretics in Paineel.  Legend Says that one day, the 5 journeyed to an unknown place to fight the greatest evil, and were never heard from again, and the evil they went up against vanished just as mysteriously.           Erudin grieved at the loss of their champions, but they knew there was hope, for the mighty descendants of the Darkender Champions still survived.           Today, there are still living Descendants of the 5 legendary champions.   Two Mighty Paladins, Dumarr and his younger brother Cinque, have re-united the Darkender Bloodline. Now they stand to face all evils, and to protect Erudin in the way of their forefathers from the rise of Darkness.
