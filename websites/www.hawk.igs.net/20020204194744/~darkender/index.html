A golden crown encircles the shafts of a great sword, shining stave, and powerful wand. The crown shimmers with the power of honor and righteousness, demonstrating the royalty of the Darkender clan. The gem embossed in the golden circlet symbolizes the bounty of success and loyalty in the laurel engravings. The sword holds power and strength in its mighty blade, enhanced by triumph in conquest. A fine stave contains within its glowing crystal the fortunes of inheritance derived from shared decent, and the adventure in new beginnings. The flowering wand embelishes a mysterious charm and grace, manifest with pride in the decendants of Erud's valiant champions.
