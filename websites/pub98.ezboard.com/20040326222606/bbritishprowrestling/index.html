Hello and welcome to the brand new home of British Pro Wrestling. The design is pretty basic but we hope that it’ll work well.

British Pro Wrestling is an e-fed which we hope will bring back fun and entertainment to the world of e-wrestling. We want to give you long, flowing, logical storylines and build up a roster of respectable wrestlers.

Applications are currently open for singles and tag wrestlers, and we hope that you consider joining us.
