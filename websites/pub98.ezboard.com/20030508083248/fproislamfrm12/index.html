    • It is upon us once again. Two suicide bombings in Tel Aviv occur in tandem, claiming the lives of dozens, and injuring a hundred more. Network news channels are in top form; we look at the weeping and bleeding faces of Israelis, victims of a violence that has become all too common in a land that has seen only blood and death for the last thousand years.

    It was interesting to watch all of the various functionaries, dignitaries, and politicians up and down the Israeli diplomatic food chain condemn and curse this latest atrocity against the population of Israel. However, it was even more interesting to realize that all of the Israeli politicians and leaders were stating the same thing; in fact, all of the Israeli leaders have been saying the same thing for the last two years, and probably will say the same things until this whole mess is finally over. It is evident that Israeli leaders will respond to acts of violence in accordance with a very clear propaganda policy. After two years, it is amazing that no one has taken the time to detail this.

    First of all, Zionists will all express horror at the savagery of the attack, as if the act of violence has occurred in some kind of vacuum. They are almost arrogant in their assertion of blame; they are the ones that are suffering, victims of a people that want them dead for no apparent reason. Every few weeks they must tolerate a suicide bombing, because they do not have free rein to annihilate the plague of terrorism from their free and democratic borders. Every few weeks another dozen Israelis will have to die, because the Palestinians are inherently evil. They do not mention the fact that four times as many children have died on the Palestinian side, or that three times as many civilians have been killed by IDF forces in the last two years. They fail to recall how their own policies of apartheid and collective punishment have driven the Palestinians over the edge.

    The next step is to blame the Palestinian Authority. Before anyone has claimed responsibility, before the final numbers are tallied, before any research is done into the exact specifics of the crime, the Israeli government and all of its cronies are clearly and directly placing the blame on Yassar Arafat. This is one of the most insidious strategies that Zionists use against Palestinians; they create a cult of personality around Yassar Arafat, and undermine his credibility and the significance of his leadership by making him the sole object of the focused hate of Israeli society. Say it enough times and people will believe it; Yassar Arafat is responsible for the terrorism. It does not matter that the PA has spent millions of dollars in television commercials condemning suicide bombings and begging Palestinian people not to support them. It is irrelevant that the PA routinely condemns any act of violence against civilians, be they against Palestinians or Israelis. It does not matter that the Palestinian Authority has no credible police, military, or intelligence apparatus to control or track the violent extremists that exist in their society. It is Yassar Arafat’s fault. Yassar Arafat. Yassar Arafat. It is reminiscent of Orwell’s Two-Minute Hate.

    At this point, the Zionist spokesperson will point out how Israel is a democracy and how Palestinians live under an autocratic government. They will be sure to mention that Israel is the only democracy in the Middle East, and that the Evil Arab Empire is ruled by dictators, and the Palestinians have continued this tradition by allowing Arafat to represent them. However, they fail to mention that Arafat and the rest of the Palestinian Authority were elected in ballot voting described as “open and fair” by former President Jimmy Carter, even though numerous international observers documented that Israeli Defense Force units harassed and intimidated Palestinians in Jerusalem in an attempt to prevent them from voting. They also fail to mention how the PA cannot continue its political reforms and pursuit of democracy because of the extremely arduous circumstances that the Palestinian people have been subjected to over the last few years. They don’t mention the blatantly racist form of “democracy” that Israel employs; allowing Jew-only employment, housing, or even streets. They don’t tell you about how their democracy uses summary executions and torture, or how 85% of water resources go to Jews, and the remaining 15% to Palestinians.
