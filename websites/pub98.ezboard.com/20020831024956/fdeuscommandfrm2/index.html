August 2nd 2175. Scientists operating in a top secret pacific laboratory successfully discovered, and captured the essence of life. Dubbed 'Deus', the essence of all life, that birthed the human race, the animal kingdom, and the lifeforce of earth itself was placed in suspended animation today, it was revealed by a government spokesperson. -Excerpt of the New Terran Times, August 2nd, 2175


Thus, the advent of The Deus Command is interwoven forcibly into destiny. Taking control of nature itself, the Deus' influence spreads to every continent. The Deumages, soldiers artificially infused with magical power took every part of the earth, their empire dominating even life itself. Complete renewal of nature, and the revival of the Earth their only goal.

However, the Ceraph rebellion grew, vowing to stop the Deus' plans, and end their reign. Based around 'The Heap', a huge pile of scrap metal and mines, left by a highly technological civilisation, they use their technical knowledge in the war against the very essence of Earth, their only goal to finally liberate it.
