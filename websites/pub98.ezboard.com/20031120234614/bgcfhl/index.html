GCFHL: The Great Canadian Fantasy Hockey League --- The Premier Fantasy Hockey League! Founded in 2001, this rotisserie keeper league features 2 leagues, 36 teams, 23 man rosters, 11 man farm teams, trading, free-agents/waiver wire and head-to-head scoring for 81+ games and playoffs! It's hockey for the REAL fan!
