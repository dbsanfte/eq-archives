Welcome all to the Lands of Nenyávё and Ornorond. Nenyávё is a land of the Elves, dating back to the Return of the Noldor in the First Age. Elves in seclusion from others in Middle Earth, they learned to co-exsist with the Men of Ornorond. Now the bane of Ornorond's Half Elven Queen will test the powers and loyalties of both the Elves and the Men.
All are welcome to join, and there are no book characters so go ahead and look around. This is a Tolkien based RPG however the storyline is entirely original.
