The Pre-Shintei Era

The Great War


Many millennia after the events of Dragon Ball Gt. The universe experienced a prolonged 500-year period of peace. During this time, technology and civilization as we know it advanced substantially. Computers became obsolete, and many new inventions were created in their place. Many of the inventions of old simply became items in antique and memorial stores. During this time, all the races of the universe were in a state of perpetual peace. The planets were all pretty much happy with their lives and everything ran smoothly, or so it looked.

Far off in the remote reaches of space lied the planet of Quazar. On this planet lived two extremely powerful, and vastly advanced races: the angel-like Gemanites, and the Super-inhuman Mystics. These two races had been coexisting together peacefully for centuries, that is until one of them began to get too powerful. You see the Gemanite King, Enock, was a great warrior. He had led his armies into many extreme battles in the past, all being won without fail. His power grew so vast that he one day reached the level of an Ascended Gemanite; a form that had not been seen since the ancient times. This level of power made him nearly unstoppable. With this new found power he became greedy and corrupt. He began to plot against his long time allies the Mystics. What he did not plan on was the Mystic King, Jazen. Jazen was also a skilled warrior, and had many centuries of experience in battle. When Enock began his campaign to take over the Mystic lands from Jazen, he sent in his armies to intercept Enock.

Enock merely wiped away the armies almost single-handedly. Jazen was then forced to fight Enock face to face. He hated the fact that he had to fight his one time companion, and tried to reason with him, but for nothing would the Gemanite listen. He unleashed his fury onto Jazen, but was ultimately overpowered, at first. After this occurred, the king went back to his forces and he began to plot against the Mystics. In the end, he planned to rule the entire planet of Quazar, and eventually the universe. With his power, it was not far from his reach. He decided that he needed allies if he wished to carry out war on the Mystics; for the Mystics were the only race ever to give the Gemanites trouble when it came to power.

The king first decided he would need a leader for his elite army. This warrior had to be loyal, trusting, and above all else, sworn to the king. He found the perfect warrior with unchallenged power and skill. His first born son, Lazarus. Lazarus controlled his armies with an iron fist and was a great fighter on the battlefield. He sent Lazarus as an ambassador for the Gemanite race, to the planet of New Vegeta. He planned to ally with the powerful race he heard of many times before, the Saiya-jin. He promised that when the war was won, that they could have part of the bounty, and even control of certain land on Quazar. They agreed to fight alongside the Gemanites, but did not know of the king’s true intention to enslave them the moment he knew that the Mystics were no longer a threat.

Word of this act got back to Jazen. After learning this, he sent a fleet of his best men to the home planet of the Pikkonian people to negotiate an alliance with the people of that planet. They agreed to help in the war, in exchange for lands on Quazar; and freedom to do as they pleased with their lands. Jazen agreed, and so it began. The fleets of the four races gathered, and on a tragic day in history, the battle known only as the Great War began. This war was the most bloody, disgusting, and vile battle that had ever been fought in the history of war. It lasted for years, spanning the length of the entire universe. Many different battles were fought on all the different planets. Though most of them took place on Quazar, there was always another immense battle taking place on the other planets. Warriors in this war reached power levels so high that it boggles the mind to even try and fathom the weakest of them. As it progressed, armies grew stronger, but numbers dwindled. New alliances were formed, and in only 3 short years, almost every major race in the universe was part of the Great War.


After many years of intense battles the Mystic queen, Aphelia, decided to tell her husband it was time to end this. While planning a way to approach the king and tell him to make a truce and pact with the other races, the royal castle was destroyed by Enock’s extermination squads. Although his wife had now been killed, Jazen’s son, Xain, managed to escape shortly before thanks to his mother sending him out to hide in the country in case they came. Jazen received word of this wife’s death and became uncontrollable. He found Enock, and with tears of sorrow in his eyes, challenged him to one final battle. Enock agreed, knowing that this would be his chance to kill the Mystic threat once and for all, and secure the planet of Quazar for himself.


As they began to unleash the primary forces of their immense powers, the force of it could be felt all through the galaxy by every being. As the two legendary warriors began to power up for this last battle, every planet shook with incredible force from their power. All fighting stopped, for the armies realized what was about to take place. The entire war would be settled by this one, cataclysmic, battle. These two warriors were the best in what they did. The best their races had to offer. King Jazen was of the same bloodline as the legendary Alpha Mystic, Shikujya. King Enock had the blood of the first Celestial Gemanite coursing through his veins. These two warriors shook the entire universe by merely charging to portions of their maximum power. As the fighting on the different planets came to a cease, the immense battle grew ever closer.


Then, in a sudden sonic explosion that shook the galaxy, it began. With every blow the planets shook. Their power had reached such levels that stray blasts destroyed uninhabited planets just by passing by. Their many stray attacks left all the planets in ruins. With every devastating attack the people became aware of the two great powers that were going to determine their fates. They fought for days, which then turned to weeks and those to months. They fought for a total of 9 months on end, never stopping for anything. They pushed themselves to such great heights that both reached the final and most powerful levels achievable by their races. King Jazen attained the level of Gamma Mystic. And King Enock reached the level of power destined to him by his bloodline, Celestial. At this point their power was almost god-like. They could move at speeds even greater than that of light. When they fought darkness would surround them. They exhibited uncanny skills. Tearing into each other, neither ever getting the upper hand. Their telepathic and telekinetic powers were so great that they would hit each other with meteors and comets, just by using their telekinesis. These two warriors would hit and cause sonic booms and tremors to echo out through out the galaxy. Then, after the long and intense months of fighting, the two great warriors ended it.


They both realized just how powerful the other was and came up with the idea that would destroy the other. Enock unleashed a gigantic and almost catastrophic wave of pure psychic energy that killed Jazen, but at the same time, Jazen unleashed from within himself of massive wave of pure energy that destroyed everything for miles in space: stars, comets, and meteors. As the two explosions erupted, they were heard all over the universe. The blast shook every house and planet that it seemed like the end of creation.


Then, long after the two warriors finished their battle by committing the ultimate desperation, the entire universe was left in shambles. Races and populations had taken a major decline in numbers, and many died caught in the crossfire of the insane final battle. About 5 years after the war ended the races began to go back to they way things were before. Everyone began rebuilding. The universe has now returned mostly to normal, and the War is a thing of the past, although some places are still recovering. The two races that were the cause of the war have now begun an uneasy alliance, and have gone on with their lives.


The Great War was a devastating episode in the history of the Pre-Shintei period. That was 200 years ago on Quazar when the war ended, and now there are still those with incredible power, but nothing even remotely close to what the warriors of that battle had.The year is now 5286, and though now things are looking peaceful, there are still those who would dare to harm the innocent. These people still wish that some kind of leader would rise up and take them back to this time, but there are also those who still fight for justice.


This is Dragon Ball Shintei, a new beginning, with new people and a new story. Let the saga of their many journeys begin. Welcome to the world of Dragon Ball: Shintei!

Saga 1

The Master Olympian

After being summoned to the New Lookout of Earth, the Shintei warriors were told of a great evil that was approaching. After learning that the long forgotten Dragon Balls still existed and that with them they could revive the fallen warrior Lazarath, they were insisted by the Earth's guardian, Nhala, to search for them and find them before the forces of evil were able to find them. After retrieving the first ball, they returned to the Lookout with a new companion; a woman known as Denise. After meeting everyone, Denise revealed her true nature. She stole the dragonballs and when confronted by the other warriors she transformed, revealing her true form, Harpy!

After defeating the warriors and escaping with the Dragonballs, Harpy returned to parts unknown. After their defeat by Harpy the Shintei warriors were advised by Nhala to search for the other Dragonballs, for if they could retrieve the remaining Dragonballs then the forces of evil could not use them for thier own demonic purposes. The warriors split into two teams, Akaris leading one, and Lyjian leading the other. Lyjian's team retrieved their Dragonball but were then confronted by Harpy and a new threat, Chimera. At the same time Akaris and his team had been confronted by the deadly fiend known as Titan.

The two teams now had to face the Olympians. Although each team consisted of members with unique abilities and skills, they proved no match for the extraordinary powers of the Olympians. Facing the ultimate defeat, Akaris and his team returned to the Lookout with nothing. Only the news of their defeat. Lyjian later returned with the same depressing news. They had also been defeated. Akaris departed, along with the beautiful Blade to train and increase his powers for the inevitable battle to come. Lyjian also returned to his ship and over the course of three days: he, Akaris, and Blade cominced training sessions that they thought would never have to be reverted to.

After completing their training, Akaris and Blade tracked down the Olympians and spied on them learning the true goal behind their plot to retrieve the Dragonballs. They planned to revive their long sleeping Master, the Master Olympian known as Thor, the Minator. After summoning the dragon and making their first wish they were interrupted by Akaris and Blade, who were soon joined by the newly, much more powerful Lyjian.

A fierce battle ensued between the Olympians and the Shintei warriors. The Olympians began to grow weaker with every blow. Soon the Master Olympian arose and began a thread of destruction, destroying multiple cities. After the Shintei warriors disposed of the lesser Olympians, they turned their focus to the new menace ravaging the planet. Akaris, Lyjian, Blade, and Citan confronted the Master Olympian in the ruins of the destroyed city, but proved no match for him. All the Shintei warriors confronted Thor, but were nothing to him but mere bothersome flies. Even the newly acquired techniques of Akaris and Lyjian proved nothing against the fierce might of Thor.

During the fight with Thor, Scepterz and Matthew succeeded in wishing back the now immensely more powerful warrior known as Lazarath. Upon his arrival onto the battle field, he witnessed the death of his only friend, Akaris. This was exactly what he need to unlock the new power he had acquired. Lazarath reached the level of Elemental and even the full power of Thor proved nothing for the might of an Elemental Gemanite fighting for revenge. After the defeat of Thor, the remaining Shintei warriors returned to the Lookout to tell Nhala of their bitter triumph.

Dragonball Shintei

A Divine Balance



After the events of the Olympian Saga, life continued on in the Shintei universe, for good and worse.

Quickly after the destruction of Thor, the Master Olympian, Lazarus entered into his ship and blasted off into space into parts unknown for a reason that still remains a mystery. Ever since that battle where he ascended into the Elemental Form, no trace of him has been seen throughout the galaxy.

Blade, Lyjian, and Kamajin left in Lyjian’s personal ship and have been undergoing intense training in the atmosphere of Quazar directly after the events of the Olympian Saga. Only recently have they landed. Not soon after that, Lyjian seemed to disappear into thin air without giving any such word to Blade or Kamajin. His whereabouts are still unknown while Blade and Kamajin continue to train.

In the realm of the afterlife, the trainings of Akaris and Matt intensify under the master teachings of King Kai.

During all of these new events, their has been many strange happenings upon the surface of Quazar. Soon after the Olympian Saga, a war between the Gemanite nation and a guild known only as The Black 7 took place. The Gemanite nation was quickly loosing. Their only hope was to turn to the Mystics. Unfortunately, the happenings in the Great War were still fresh in the minds of the Mystics. To the great dismay of the Gemanites, the Mystics simply reinterated that their “alliance” was just a cease fire between the two nations. Without the help of the Mystics, the capital city of the Gemanites, Zione’, was quickly captured by the ruthless Black 7 who possessed power beyond any of the Gemanite’s capabilities.

The Black 7 quickly established Zione’ as their own capital and enslaved every remaining Gemanite. The Mystics watched in horror but were unsure what to do. After The Black 7 established their empire, they soon informed the Mystic capital that they wished to keep the cease fire treaty and ensured them that there were absolutely no intentions of the Black 7 even attempting to capture the Mystic nation. Thinking that possibly The Black 7 would be a bit more trustworthy than the original Gemanite nation, they agreed to the treaty.

Now…The great story continues. As life goes on, forces beyond comprehension are hard at work shaping something that is out of any sort of fathoming. Children grow older and warriors grow stronger. The time is now. The Divine Balance shall soon be revealed!
