Welcome to the unofficial Conservative Townhall Meetup Forum. Most of you that attended the very first Meetup were excited about the meeting itself, but had serious problems with the way Meetup manages each site. One issue that has come up is how Meetup will cancel a Meetup if they get less than 5 RSVP's. Another problem mentioned was the venues that Meetup had posted. About half the folks were not thrilled with the choices. These are just a couple of problems that we as a group can work out. Conservatives are a pretty smart bunch, so making this work should be easy. See ya at the next Meetup. Email us if you have any suggestions to help pave the way for a smooth growth plan for our Meetups.
