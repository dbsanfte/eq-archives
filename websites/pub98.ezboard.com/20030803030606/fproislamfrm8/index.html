• 12/09/02: View the entire article: The Americanization of the Muslim American


    • There seems to be this unreasonable fear in conservative America with respect to the lack of Americanization of Muslim immigrants. Chief among the complainants are Michael Savage, venerated hate columnist for World Net Daily, and disgraced-but-reviewed “investigative journalists” like Steve Emerson and Daniel Pipes. These people, along with a long list of other Americans, believe that the problem with Muslims and immigrants in general is that they fail to “Americanize.” Muslims fail to learn the language, adopt the values, celebrate the rituals, or participate in the processes of American society.

    I would suggest to these doomsayers: Take a moment and think back through your knowledge of American history. Can you name one group of people of any race that failed to assimilate into mainstream American culture? Can you think of one example of a group of people that failed to learn English by the first generation born in America? It is impossible. The people that come here and that do not "Americanize" overnight are representative of the natural process of the absorption of one culture into another, or at least the creation of a new hybrid subculture that takes from both. We cannot seriously expect immigrants to concentrate on learning English so that they can enjoy an evening of Survivor with their friends of a Saturday night; the vast majority of immigrants come to this country in search of economic opportunity or religious freedom. The process of becoming American will be a slow and steady one, and will accelerate with the following generation.

    The real problem that exists is not the Americanization process, I think. It lies with something slightly more basic, slightly more sinister. The real problem with this conservative fear is not in the culture, because that is a problem that will take care of itself. The sheer vastness of America will make believers out of us all. The problem is religion. The one value no Muslim will ever sacrifice is faith, and this is the great dismay of the conservative world of America. Where are the inroads into their community for evangelizing? Where are the inter-faith marriages that uniformly bring families into the fold of Christianity? How do we approach and convert these people?

      Herein lies the fatal flaw of the conservative movement; there efforts to marginalize the Muslim American will fail simply based upon the flawed reasoning of their leaders. The average American will not expect Muslims to give up their values or religion; after all, that is the definition of who they are.  But these Muslim immigrants (and converts) all will speak English, they all will love America, and they all will honor the Constitution, as has every other immigrant community in the history of America.  Muslims are as American as the rest of America.  It is unfair to suggest that they are less American because their parents have not fully assimilated.  Muslim Americans will end up finding their voice in US politics, as have every other minority; it is just a matter of time. This will not destroy America; this will be another example of the success story of the American Immigrant.
