Welcome to New York City, year 1997, in a world completly different then we ever knew it to be. The pollution created by mortals has finally managed to almost completely blot out the sun. Now as the human race dwindles we find ourselves enslaved by the ever growing population of Vampires. Yet odd enough, struggling revolutionists plot and plan in the abandoned subways with the help of Were creatures to bring down the enslavement of the human race. What side will you chose?
