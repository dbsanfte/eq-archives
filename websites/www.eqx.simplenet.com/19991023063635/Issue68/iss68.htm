The Dwarven Bard, part three

The trip back to Kelethin was swifter than her previous journey. Though untutored and incapable of performing the magic himself, Amgho learnt the drumming song quickly, and his voice supported hers in her songweaving. They nearly flew through the countryside. The grass at the side of the road bent over in the wind of their passage. The travel that had taken her four days to complete alone, was over in two and a half. They arrived at Kelethin late in the afternoon of the third day.

Two Weeks in Rallos Zek

Despite the server problems these past weeks I was actually able to log in over 48 hours of game play in the first seven days, and this was around school and work. Since then I have been able to play a lot more. As you can tell by the title I chose to play on Rallos Zek, the PvP (although we call it the RP) server. I chose to play on this server for a lot of reasons. I also had a lot of presuppositions on what the server might actually be like, and most of them turned out to be wrong. Rallos Zek is not the most popular server so far, but I think it has merit and I certainly hope that Verant decides to keep it. Here are some observations that I have made after the first week that I thought I would share with people who are curious to know what is going on there.
