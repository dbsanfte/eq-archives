Q:  Is there any way to tell if a piece of jewelry or a stone has magical properties? There are lots of jewelers in Norrath, but none of their pieces show any thing besides weight and cost. Sense Magic will probably work, but is there another way?
A:  Unless the jewelry was made with enchanted silver, it will not be magical. And any jewelry that is magical will show as such in a right click. Enchanted silver is regular silver that has been enchanted by an Enchanter.

Q:  Does the mana stat (int or wis) increase meditation speed or casting ability? If it only increases mana capacity, that is a good advantage but not all-important. However, if a character with higher mana total meditates full just as fast, or has more powerful spells, putting points in anything else is an absolute waste. I need to know if it is safe to put points into other stats. - Mitey
A:  Mana stats affect your mana pool, and also how fast you scribe/memorize/cast spells. It will not affect the amount of dmg/buffing your spell do. Medding rate won't be affected, so far as I know.

Q:  Terminology: What is MOB? What does it mean to 'Train' a creature on someone else? What's the difference from causing a 'train' on yourself? What is 'twink'ing? What is SoW? What's the difference between somoeone with SoW cast on them and someone that has absolutely no items in their inventory? (or just food and water) Why does it say Sense Healing increases quickly? How do you practice 'Safe Fall' without feeling like a lemming? - Torch
A:  Whew! Ok here goes: MOB - no one really seems to know...Mobile? Monster On Berserk? It comes from the earliest MUDs, that much I DO know. Any of you readers out there know the Real True Origin? Lemme know if so! To "train" a creature on someone else is to attract a MOB, then run away, and by running close to another player, dumping it on them. Training on yourself is the same thing, except you don't lose the MOB onto someone else.

Twinking is any higher lvl player giving items to lower lvl players to give them a "leg-up" so to speak. It was illegal in Beta, due to the fact that Verant was trying to balance the game, and twinking interfered with the data they needed. Nowadays it is not illegal, but not really approved of either. Some items have been "lvl-rated" ie, they don't work, or work only in a limited manner for chars who aren't high enough lvl to aquired the item through normal gameplay.

SoW is Spirit of Wolf, a druid/shaman spell that increases a char's running speed. It is way better than simply running around "naked".

Um, Sense Healing? you must mean Sense Heading. I have never found it increased quickly...

The only way to practice safefall, other than at your Guild trainer, is to throw yourself of small ledges, take small dmg, repeat. Go, lemming, go!
