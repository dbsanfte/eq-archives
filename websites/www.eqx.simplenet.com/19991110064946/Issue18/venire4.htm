Venire's Promise

"You stay away from those people. That is evil magic!" he gnarled.

"But I heard they are the most power mages in the land Grandfather." I said defensively.

Red had told me about an old man who lived on a small farm outside of town. It was rumored he practiced necromancy. 		"Mages?" he laughed. "Those are no mages. They are evil liches that will take your soul." With that said Gosling declared the lesson over, and headed out the door. As he walked away he added.

"You stay away from them you hear!" 		"Sure Grandpa, sure." I thought to myself.

I headed off towards the stables to work. I couldn’t wait to tell Red.
