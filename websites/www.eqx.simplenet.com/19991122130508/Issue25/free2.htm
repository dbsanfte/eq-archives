Razor's "Free Entertainment 101"

Have you ever actually let a computer salesman, at one of these computer superstores, try to talk specifications with you?

NO?

Oh you have to try this sometime. This is the worth the price of admission. This beats Disney World any day. 		"Hi sir can I help you?" The salesman says, as he confidently walks towards me.

The typical answer at this point is:

"NO just looking."

This is where the fun begins. At this point you should smile and say: 		"Why yes. Yes you can help me. I've got $4000 dollars to send on a good computer for our household. My friend at work said we should buy a computer so we can access the World Wild WEB"

"Now will people on the world web be able to see things on my screen?" I ask.

"No that's a rumor hacker's spread around to scare people away from the Internet." He says with a laugh.
