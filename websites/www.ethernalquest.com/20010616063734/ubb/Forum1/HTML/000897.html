Free points can be done with a few exceptions... First of all, you cannot zone, camp, disconnect, or gate while it's hacked, or you will revert to your original skill.

Second, use the points all the way to ONE LESS then max on everything. Then get the last skill yourself by fighting/casting whatever, and it will stick.

And last, when you reach 0 points left, you will start to go negative. like -65 or so. Meaning that once you have gotten one skill on everything yourself, the skill will stick, but the client will remember 0 while the server will see -65... So next time you gain a level, you will see another 5 points, but have to use them the same way as the hack, since the server sees -60...

That means that you can use your extra 5 points, but again, cannot zone, gate, whatever, and have to get the last point on your own for it to stick, otherwise the skill will revert, and the client will see 0 again instead of 5... while the server still sees -60.

This hack, and most of the other mentioned ones were on Pac0 and Tel0net's F-H site awhile back... and they are working their asses off getting the site back up.

If you adjust your x,y,z coordinates client side, the client moves you to the new coordinates in a straight line. If you encounter any solid object (such as a wall), you will be stopped at that point. This has little to no use in practice.

The only way to truly "teleport" is by having the server send your client an updated position packet, a la a spell effect (summoning, gate, coh, etc).
