Zombie 		posted 06-01-2001 08:15 AM         
The keys on the CDs are completely random, a keygen can never be made. And before you go saying "well I got a keygen for <insert single player game here> and it worked great!", well, with a multiplayer only game like EQ that requires you to register your CD key, they can make them totally random because everytime a new key is made they just put it in the DB of "real keys". With a game that doesn't require an Internet connection, you have to use an algorithm unless you want to put a different exe build on every CD (not going to happen). So if you don't want to pay for EQ to get a key, you'll have to pick one of the ~1 million legal keys out of the 131,621,703,842,267,136 possible. Yes, that is 131.6 quadrillion possible keys. Good luck!
