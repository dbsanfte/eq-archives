Here is how I use 2 accounts on same comp.

EQw is not installed in the Everquest Directory. I have a shortcut to EQw on my desktop. If you right click on your EQw shortcut in the start in field I have this: "C:\Program Files\EverQuest\". That is where my everquest directory is.

Then I just start up EQw and log on my account. Connect to server and log on my charactor.

Then start EQw again and and log in as friends account. Connect to server and log onto his char.

While the Char selection screen is loading and after you select what char to use be sure to allow EQw to keep control. This just causes the char selection and the world to load faster.
