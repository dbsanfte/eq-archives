> what is the most xp you get from a
> monster?

Most XP someone can get from any single kill is something close to 10% of their current level. This comes out to roughly half a yellow bubble.

The rules are apparently different for quests, as I've heard of a few cases where more than half a yellow could be earned.

But for mobs the 10% rule is pretty close, as I've had level 1 newbies only get something like two blues of experience for killing a level 50 mob.

You are forgetting one class that although is pretty useless in groups that attack anything magic resistant based, the Necro. PL with necro you say? WTF? hehe Most of you probably know but for those of you that don't:

Necro can Mez, Snare, Root, Fear, Heal, FD and A little expensive but even Rez another character.


I would create a pet, get a mob to around half, root it, then start getting the pet to back off (Spectres were good because they move and attack slow) Then screaming terror the spectre (or whatever mobs you choose) Then send in the other char to start finshing it off.

It probably isn't as fast as Druid thorns, but you can at least build some skills while killing stuff that gives a quarter to half a bubble of xp per kill.

I leveld a druid to 50 that way, then used the necro and the druid to power level other chars. Then I leveled up a 30 enchanter and would log out druid, login enc, buff Warrior/Monk/Rogue (Fighter types worked best)

Also fun leveling was fearing the spectre with darkness on and practicing backstab, that was a fun and useful way to level because it raised the rogues biggest damaging skill.

Anyway, you probably all know all that already, but maybe at least one person will learn something.
