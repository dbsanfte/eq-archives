How is your connection Setup? i mean is your Win98 box the one with 2 NIC's or your Linux box? cause if you Cable modem is hooked up to your Win98 box then Eq sends the packets directly to the Cable connection and not the Network. and Showeq couldn't pick up the packets then.. And if your linux box is the one with the Cable Connection make sure that you have the Right NIC chosen for it to set sniff.. So
eth0 = Cable
eth1 = LAn
then run
showeq -i eth1 <Ip Address>
but if you have two different IP address's from your cable modem.. then i don't think you can get Showeq to work. Since your win98 machine wouldn't be sending the Eq Packets to your Linux machine at all. they would just be directly to the Eq servers.. not to your Lan for Showeq to pickup..
