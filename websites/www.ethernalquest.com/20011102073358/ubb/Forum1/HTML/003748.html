Actually, with the new hardline approach Verant is using against hackers(and on those who were hacked,) the best you can do is get the account banned. Any accounts used from the same IP as the thiefs will also be banned...

You will need, of course, the original CD-Key, the last 5 digits of the card that first subscribed to the service and other pieces of key information like the original name and so on. Contact a GM in game, or contact the support staff over the telephone.

Verant will not give it back. This prevents people from selling their accounts and retrieving them afterword...
