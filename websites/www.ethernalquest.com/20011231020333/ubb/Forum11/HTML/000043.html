Ok, got this working with the help of Maxzrz. My first problem was I installed RH 7.1 instead of 7.2, so I did not have a recent enough libpcap. I then downloaded and compiled the latest libpcap, but I did not remove the libpcap that was included in the rpms from the full install of RH. I just had to remove the libpcap from the rpm and rebuild libpcap and OE, then everything works fine, on RH 7.1.
