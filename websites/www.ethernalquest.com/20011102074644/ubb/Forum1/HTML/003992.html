Hacking is a lot of things. Personal definition really. It also changes over time with technology. Hacking used to be to put a bluebox up to a phone and dial in to international operators to make free long distance calls.

To me HACKING means to personaly know the ins and out of a network/program/system that you would not usualy have access to. Or of those functions. And to be able to act on that to gain access to those systems/programs/systems or functions thereof.

So IMO hex editing to gain access to functions not meant to be used by you in that fashion would be hacking.

It is like driving a beat up old VW van, Or driving a new Ferrari. Either way it is still driving.

Hacking does not mean ILLEGAL or even unauthorized IMO. Like taking a program and alter it to do as you like can also be hacking.
