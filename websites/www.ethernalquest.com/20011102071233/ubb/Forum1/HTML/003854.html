Zombie 		posted 09-03-2001 11:52 AM            
First of all it has nothing to do with Java, JavaScript and Java are totally and completely different and have absoluty nothing to do wtih each other.

Java: Created by SUN
JavaScript: Created by Netscape

Now that we got that out of the way:

They haven't built the option into the GUI just yet. However you can disable the window.open funtion this way:

1. Browse to C:\WINDOWS\Application Data\Mozilla\Profiles\<username>\<scamled stuff>

2. Open prefs.js with a text editor like notepad

3. Add the following line of text right into the middle of this file:

user_pref("capability.policy.default.Window.open", "noAccess");

You must put that right into the middle somewhere. It will not work if you place it at the beginning or end.

That's all. Now the window.open funtion will be disabled.
