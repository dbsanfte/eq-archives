Guild Advancement (updated 03/09/04)


We have several paths within our guild that one might consider applying for. Below is a list of current positions within the guild. Members may choose to apply and hold more than one position should they have the time to do so. The number of positions available for any position in the guild is based on how much guild support is needed in that area. This is determined by the High Council member that is in charge of overseeing any given group.
