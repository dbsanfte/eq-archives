    Victory!! (1/25/04)

    After several demoralizing defeats the Knights of Reverence and its allies defeated the Vindicator of Kael. Humans, Elves, Dwarves, and even Gnomes united to put down his evil reign. The battle was in peril and many of our forces were crushed when the Magician Valkinar called upon the fire to do its dirty, but noble, deed. Derakor screamed in pain as the fire came down, he stumbled around a bit, and then fell. Victory is ours, but alas, there's more work to do, as the giants of Kael continue to threaten the freedom of our allies on Velious.
