Code of Order of the Dragon


All members are bound to abide by the following code of the Guild

TO RENDER AID to other members of guild when in need and when called upon to do so. No matter the risk or circumstance. Any officer may make a general call for aid for a member. Members in need must contact an officer to petition for aid. All members, by default are to look out for the interest and well being of fellow members and render local assistance when necessary. To ignore a call for aid is grounds for probation or dismissal from guild, pending majority vote of officers.


TO FIGHT for the best interests of the guild. Members are encouraged to group together and journey the length and breadth of Norrath as much as possible.


TO UPHOLD the ideals of Truth, Justice, Honor, Strenght, Loyalty, Brotherhood, and Sisterhood.


TO DEVELOP SKILLS to be the best of the races and classes of Norrath. In so doing, the goals and ideals of the guild are furthered.


TO BE HELPFUL to any in need. This aid may take the form of guidance, money or items as deemed appropriate by the member.


Additional ByLaws can/will be added as needed upon majority vote of all the officers.
