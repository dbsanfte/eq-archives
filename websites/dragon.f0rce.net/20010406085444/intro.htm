Order of the Dragon Mission


The Order of the Dragon is a guild that represents the best of the
races and classes of  Norrath. We are commited to upholding the
highest ideals of Truth, Justice, Honor, Strength, Loyalty, and
Broter and Sisterhood. We are united together to fight for the
interests of our Guild and to uphold the ideals we represent; to
help other members in times of need; to foster an enviroment of
friendship and comaraderie; to be a guilding, helpful force to others
in need; and to serve as role models for others to emulate. We are
a neutral Guild and believe everything exists as a necessary and
integral part of the whole, each thing as a check and balance for
the other. For what is good if there were no evil, or happiness
without suffering. By maintaining balance throughout the world, we
are better able to appreciate that which we have. All who possess
these same ideals, goals, and beliefs, regardless of Race or Class
are friends of the Order of the Dragon.

So it is written, so it shall be done.

Menu Options                                                      -top-
The menu options are listed at the top of the website from left to right with a helmet located in front of each option.

Public

Guild - The guild menu option list all the issues that are related to the structure of Order of the Dragon. Topics that you can find there are Membership (how to join),  Judgement (disciplinary action), Duty (member responsibilities), our History, and allies.

Roster - The rosters menu option list all the members of the guild and their positions they hold. this is broken down into two parts, Officers and Members, as well as the sponsor points.

Resources - The resources menu option leads you to an area that has alot of different resources related to EQ for everyone to use. Some of these topics are links to other websites, guild calendar, and more.

Forms - The forums menu option is the area where players get together to share a variety of topics on the message boards or in live chat.
