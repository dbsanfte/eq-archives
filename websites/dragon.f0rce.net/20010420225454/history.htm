History


History tells us that in eons past, Veeshan, the Crystalline Dragon and Ruler of the Plane of Sky, came upon the world and found it pleasing. The world then was pure and uncorrupted. She deposited her brood and thus began the age of scale. Great dragons roamed the lands and flew the skies. They were powerful beings of great intellect, wisdom and strength. The beauty and majesty of the world was unparalleled. In time, other gods took notice of Veeshan’s discovery and came unto the world. These gods created the elder races of Norrath and in turn, evil was brought into the world as well. In time, the dragons were driven from mastery of the over-world and the lesser races soon laid claim to all the lands. They spread across the length and breadth of Norrath and with their growth so too grew evil upon the lands. The world was brought into balance. Today, there is great need upon Norrath for the revival of the virtues of Truth, Justice, Honor, Strength, Loyalty, Brotherhood and Sisterhood. A need to restore Norrath to the same beauty, majesty and grandeur that Veeshan first saw in the world. A restoration of the balance of all things. A world uncorrupted by pettiness and greed. The Order of the Dragon was formed to uphold these ideals, to maintain the balance of all things, and to stand as role models for others to emulate. To best uphold these ideals, we are duty bound to develop our abilities and skills to be the best of our classes and races and to be recognized by others as such.
