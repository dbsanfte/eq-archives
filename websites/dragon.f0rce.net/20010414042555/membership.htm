Welcome!

Welcome interested members! Please browse through our pages and familiarize yourself with Order of the Dragons people and there conversations on the public bulletin board.

Our basic philosophy is contained in the Guild Duty. If when reading these words you find yourself agreeing, then possibly we are the guild for you.

Our membership policy is centered around getting to know some of the people in the guild. The details follow, but in general you must have sponsors who will put you forth to the Order of the Dragon membership. After consideration by the membership, the officers will make the final decision on each candidate. The more people you meet on your travels, and the more often you demonstrate the values of Order of the Dragon, the easier it is to become a member.

Step by Step for Prospective Members

  1. Read and decide that you can abide by the Guild Duty.

  2. While not required, introducing yourself on the web board is generally a great idea.

  3. Seek out Order of the Dragon members (around your level is usally best as you can group together) and get to know them with the purpose of obtaining sponsors.

  4. Look to the Rosters to find people. We suggest you print the roster and keep it handy.

  5. Tell the Order of the Dragon members that you are interested in joining and that you are looking for sponsors.

  6. Once you have found a sponsor return here and make a forum post with the required information. It also is suggested that you write a brief paragraph or so explaining why you want to become a dragon.

  7. Read your email. You will be sent your status and, if accepted, will be advised on the officer(s) to contact to be invited to join the guild.

If for some reason you don't get enough sponsor points within the time allotted, or are not selected, do not dispair, keep trying to meet more people and do good in Norrath.

Keep up the great work through your probation period and try to be active in guild chat and on the boards to get to know everyone.

 

General Membership Rules

Applicants must read and be willing to abide by all Rules and Codes of the guild.

Applicants must gather 20 sponsor points.

Process - Order of the Dragon Member Roles & Responsibilities

why the candidate wants to join Order of the Dragons

Below that initial Post everone is allowed to post comments about you with the following rules. If you are sponsoring the person, please make the Subject SPONSOR if you know the person or have other information that can spead the process, include it here as well. If you are not sponsoring the person, then the subject and body of the message can contain anything that you want.

20 sponsor points are required to be considered for OOD. Sponsor points are equivalent to your rank number if you are sponsoring someone. So if you are rank 4 then your sponsorship is worth 4 points. All officers count as 10 sponsor points. Only 1 sponsorship per player, so if you have multiple characters, chose your highest ranking character. Also remember you can only sponsor every calander month (speak to a online officer for override of this rule this is done on a per case basis.)
Check the Responsibilities list for details.

The person will be on the Administration Board for 1 week minimum.

Mid-week usually on Wednesdays the Administration Board will be checked for possible candidates. If the candidate gathers enough sponsor points then he or she will be moved over to the officer board for final approval. If the candidate doesn't recieve enough sponsor points, then the post will remain on the private board to gather more sponsors. If the candidate doesn't have the sponsors within 1 month of the original post then he and she must be reposted to be considered for membership. A list of candidates pending officer approval will be posted to the Administration Board.

The candidate will be considered on the officer board for one week. If there is no officer that has an objection to the new candidate then they will be considered approved for membership.

The candidate will receive an email about his or her status and if approved the names of the officers to contact to be invited so please insure this info is correct.

There will be the typical probation period of 1 month where a member can be removed for just not fitting in to the guild. After those weeks he or she will be considered a full member of Order of the Dragon and gain all voting privledges. If the person is removed from the guild for any reason he or she must wait 1 month before being sponsored again.

Sponsor Points

Each guild member holds a rank in the guild. The member's rank can be found on the Guild Members page of the web. Each rank is worth a certain number of sponsor points. You must gather 20 sponsor points in order to be eligible to join the guild. The sponsor points (rank) is the same number as your total months in guild and must be declared on the Administration Board when the prospective member is put forth. Full description of ranks, rank levels, and rank sponsor points is given on the Roster Responsibilities page of the web.
