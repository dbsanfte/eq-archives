Neocron Beta phase 1 launched
Reported by: Marcus   0 Comments | Add  

According to Bruce Crest from German developer Reakktor Media AG, the Neocron beta test has been launched. The first 20 people can enter the persistent world of Neocron from their home PC. Most of them are Reakktor employees or their friends and relatives.

Stage 1 will be up for around 2-3 weeks, to seek and eliminate the most obvious bugs and design faults.

Stage 2 will open Neocron to around 100 people from outside the company, so stay tuned for its launch.

For the latest news and information about Neocron, check out Neocron Center.
