Magelo tags - Howto

How to to use tags

Tags are an abbreviated version of HTML. To use tags, follow the directions below
  1. To Make a Link Within a Post
  2. To Make an Image Within a Post
  3. To Make an Active Email Address Within a Post
  4. To Make a Quote Section Within a Post
  5. To Make Bold Text Within Your Post
  6. To Make Underlined Text Within Your Post
  7. To Make Italicized Text Within Your Post
  8. To Make Centered Text Within Your Post
  9. To Use a Code Tag Within Your Post
  10. To Use Several Tags Together Within Your Post
Everquest Extension Tags:
  1. To link your character profile in a Post
  2. To make an item screenshot in a Post


1. To Make a Link Within a Post

 You can just put your link and it will be converted automatically,
 or you can use the following tag:

Tag: [link="your_link"]link_name[/link]
Html: <a target="new" href="your_link">link_name</a>
Result: Magelo

2. To Make an Image Within a Post

Tag: [img]url_img[/img]
Html: <img src="url_img">
Result:  

3. To Make an Active Email Address Within a Post

Tag: [email="email_adress"]email_name[/email]
Html: <a href="mailto:email_adress">email_name</a>
Result: Magelo

4. To Make a Quote Section Within a Post

Tag: [quote]your quote[/quote]
Html: <blockquote type=cite><blockquote><hr>" your quote "<hr></blockquote></blockquote>
Result:
your quote


5. To Make Bold Text Within Your Post

Tag: [b]your text[/b]
Html: <b> your text</b>
Result:
your text


6. To Make Underlined Text Within Your Post

Tag: [u]your text[/u]
Html: <u> your text</u>
Result:
your text


7. To Make Italicized Text Within Your Post

Tag: [i]your text[/i]
Html: <i> your text</i>
Result:
your text


8. To Make Centered Text Within Your Post

Tag: [center]your text[/center]
Html: <center> your text</center>
Result:
your text


9. To Use a Code Tag Within Your Post

Tag: [code]your code[/code]
Html: <pre> your code</pre>
Result:
your code

 Note: The code tag preserves formatting and is useful for displaying programming code in the post.


10. To Use Several Tags Together Within Your Post

Tag: [center][i]your text[/i][/center]
Html: <center><i> your text</i></center>
Result:
your text



Everquest Extension tags


1. To link your character profile in a Post

Tag: [eq_profile=your_profile_number]link_name[/eq_profile]
Note: Your profile number can be found on the page where you manage profiles.



2. To make an item screenshot in a Post

Tag: [eq_item]item_id[/eq_item]
Note: The item ID can be found on the page where you see detailed information about an item.
