Privacy Information

The intent of this privacy policy is to detail the information Magelo may gather about indivudals who visit our websites and create accounts with us. Further it will discuss how that information is used and Magelo's disclosure practices.

Site Usage Information

Magelo gathers "site usage" information from all visitors to the Magelo websites. Such data is used in the aggregate, to help determine general usage patterns and fine tune layout in order to ensure that our sites are as useful and enjoyable as possible. This "site usage" information does not contain any information which would identify an individual user. Generally it contains information such as (but not limited to):

  • what pages have been visited
  • What pages referred visitors to our sites
  • what search engine was used to find our sites
  • what browsers and screen resolutions are used to view our websites
  • what country visitors to our site are from
  • How long a visitor remains on our website
This "site usage" information and statistical data may be made public at Magelo's discretion.

Individual User Information

Individually identifiable information will not be released without that individual's prior consent. Visitors are specifically asked for individually identifiable information when signing up for Magelo Eq premium services. All information which would be individually identifiable (including billing information) is only transmitted over a Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) framework which encrypts the information for your protection. From time to time Magelo may contact individual users who have provided information to us regarding account informations. It may include (but is not limited to):

  • receipts for transactions
  • password reminder
  • support
Magelo does allow for accounts to be created by children provided it is supervised by a Parent or Guardian over 18. We do not knowingly collect individual user information from minors or persons under the age of 18. Creation of accounts or submission of information by a person under the age of 18 must be done only with the involvement of a parent or guardian.

Credit Card Security

We collect credit card information for the purpose of conducting financial transactions for account holders. These transactions are conducted over a Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software which encrypts the information both sent and received. We do not store any credit card information on our servers.
