EQ Expansion - About

Magelo features some components dedicated to the popular online game Everquest:
  • Characters management (therefore roster are up to date on the fly)
  • Profiles management
  • Guild bank management
  • Marketplace component
  • Access to ressources: item spell zone and bestiary database
  • Special event component

Even if you are not a member of a Magelo community, you can register your characters and create your profiles.
