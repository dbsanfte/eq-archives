Overview

What is Magelo ?

Magelo is a solid, secured platform enabling people to envision, create, manage and use communities on the Internet. Magelo leverages webmasters and designers to build entire communities by simply stacking up building blocks.

Creating communities with all popular features like forums polls and such has never been so simple.

How it Works :

The application is a three tier system consisting of skins, components and the database backend.

With Magelo, the data are separated from the view. You hold the view, Magelo take care of your data, therefore the layout of your website is truely under your control. You can develop skins by using the technologies you know : HTML, CSS, etc...

You can customize the look&feel of your magelo skin at will. Magelo is all about pulling content from Magelo server through some Javascript tags.

Magelo provides javascript variables and arrays throught components you can place exactly where you want on your page. So you'll not have to sacrifice your current design to add magelo community to your site.
