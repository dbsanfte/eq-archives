EQ Features - About


Magelo provides an EverQuest character management tool that will let you build profiles like the ones at the right.


Key features of the Magelo EverQuest character management:
  • Multiple characters per account, you just need one account to handle all your characters, please avoid creating multiple accounts.

  • Multiple profiles per character, you can build resist profiles, dream profiles etc...
    To be able to test items with your characters is really helpfull to help you choose the upgrade that best fit your needs and it's fun too ;)

  • Detailed informations on your hp / mana

    • Hp / Mana due to your level
    • Hp / Mana due to your items (+hp/mana items)
    • Hp / Mana due to your items (+sta +wis/int items)


  • Handle pretty much everything that EverQuest provides regarding your character

    • Skills
    • Languages
    • AA points
    • You can inspect any item


  • You can customize few things like your background, its position etc..

You will need a magelo account, once logged in you will see a "Characters" link by the EQ features.

Dont forget to visit the Help, there are some nice viewlet tutorials to help you get started as well as a FAQ.
