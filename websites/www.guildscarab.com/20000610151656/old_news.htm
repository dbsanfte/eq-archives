July 8 1999
 
  The Master of Admissions, or the MoA, will be soley responsible for the screening, a ssigning, and promotion of new recruits.

The new recruitment procedure will now happen like this:
  • An Email is received to either Guildmasters, or a recruitment@guildscarab.com address.

  • Email forwarded to the MoA.

  • Email screened by the MoA, and then if approved the MoA assigns to the HC for evauluation.

  • During the pre-recruit (or candidate) stage, they (the candidate) must find a full member who is willing to sponser them to full member status. This is done by adventuring with as many people as possible. This will also give the guild members more say in the overall recruitment procedure.

  • Once the HC feels they are ready, the HC presents the pre-recruit to the MoA. The MoA can consult with the HC and the sponser to get information about the candidate. If the MoA feels they are ready, they must adventure with the MoA, and then one of the guildmasters. Once approved, the MoA awards them full Recruit status, They are added to the guildstone, and moved into /anon status.

  • Recruits are required to remain in that status for at least 14 days. After the 14 days the recruit, his sponser, and the HC member assigned to him/her will appear before the MoA, a member of the IC, and at least one Guildmaster to be verbally tested. This will no longer be done in tells. This will be done in the back of the Jade tiger. If the Recruit passes all of the questions, then the Gm's, IC, HC, and full member sponser must all vote the recruit as passing to full member status. The voting is done live, and in front of the recruit. If the vote passes, the recruit is made full member.

As you can see, the MoA will assume all duties pertaining to the recruitment of the guild.

At no time will there ever be more than 4 candidates/recruits. When one recruit is promoted, then another candidate can be accepted.

This information will be added to the membership page immediatly, and will take effect effective today.

Current pre-recruits and recruits are exempt, and will progress according to the old procedures.

By vote of the IC and HC, Lord Jayfoot Bloomrose will now assume the role as Master of Admissions.

Hail Scarab!
