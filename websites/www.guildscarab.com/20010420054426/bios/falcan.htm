Falcan's Story
   

Description

Falcan appears human, tall and well built. The only thing that doesn't fit in is his blue eyes and hair. This is said to be the result of a failed experiment long ago, but no one knows this for sure. He also radiates with a power that not many beings possess, and a strange Darkness always seem to hang over him. Not many things are known about his childhood, but a last interview is recorded in the great scroll's of Britian.

History

He was raised by foster parents on a small farm, and at an age where he was still young he started his adventure into the world. (Editor's note: There is no record of the names of these parents or what era it was.)

The first record of him is when he became a member of a mercenary band called the "Pendragons" but after a fight against a Black Wizard (where the band was nearly wiped out), he went under the teachings of a wizard called Baragon. (Editor's note: There is no Record of a Wizard named Baragon in any of the Ancient scrolls of the Conclave, witch leads me to believe that either Falcan used this name to cover for self research or this Baragon was not part of the Conclave. If it was the case that he was not part of the Conclave, he held his identity very safe and was never prosecuted for self-research and heretic acts. There is also the possibility that Baragon was a Dragon in disguise, there are several records of Dragons teaching ancient magic to the First High members of the Conclave).

A member that later on proved to be his long lost brother first enlisted Falcan into Scarab. He started out as a recruit but soon after became a full member of this glorious guild. After being a member of the guild for a long time he finally achieved the status of Inner Circle of Scarab. One of his greatest accomplishments was when he succeeded in becoming an Assistant Guildmaster a little while before the guild disbanded.

After the guild was disbanded he left his family behind and was last seen looking for a notorious keep.

Time passed and he came back, more powerful then ever. During one night he set up a house in the outer city and didn't leave it for anything other than gathering more books.

The last 2 years he collected and read all magical books known to man (and more books unknown to man). His library is notorious for its collection of spells and secrets.

The last that was seen of him in the world of Britian is a last interview made by a well-known scribe.

Current Status
Falcan was in an unknown way transported to a different world, this world being the place that The Hawkes were sent to. He is currently active with Scarab but is rarely seen doing anything else then studying and fighting the Evil of the world.
