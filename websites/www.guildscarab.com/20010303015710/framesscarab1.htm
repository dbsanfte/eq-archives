SCARAB has risen again (2-16-99)
 
 

There seems to be some confusion about us. You are probably saying to yourself, "Ok, just what the heck IS SCARAB"? Allow me to help clarify. SCARAB is a guild, and the dictionary describes a Guild as a "professional group, union or society". Out of those three terms I think the work society best describes us. SCARAB is a guild, err, a society of people who play online games, most recently of which is EverQuest, a real 3D massively multiplayer online RPG. We do more than just play the game though. We interact, we roleplay, and we let some things about ourselves come out we normally would not.

In both a roleplaying, and real life sense SCARAB was founded to promote Honor, Loyalty, and Valor. Members of SCARAB feel strongly about these issues in real life, and therefore strive to promote them in the media of an online Role Playing game. We do not promote Player Killing activity, which is the practice of killing a character within the game for malacious or unwarrented reasons. We do not activly seek out to hunt these people either, instead focusing our energies on the concepts we hold to be true, Honor, Valor, and Loyalty.

Within the game we hunt evil monsters, and play our characters to the full extent of the game, and we get to know our fellow guildmates. We become a family. If you believe that an Online RPG should be played with the concept of helping others, and having fun, without taking the fun away from others then perhaps SCARAB is what you are looking for.


Read our web site carefully, and decide. Once accepted into our family, forever will our blood be your blood!

Thank you and I'll see you in Norrath!

Lord Hawke - Co-founder and Co-Guildmaster of SCARAB
