Gregor's Story
   

Gregor, like his father chose the holy path at a young age. From the time of his fathers birth, he was chosen to be a priest, to shed light where there was darkness, and happiness where there was sorrow. Little did Gregor know that his path along with his fathers would not be able to protect us from the very horrors that we fought against.

It was the day after his 16th birthday and like every other day of his life, his father left for his daily holy crusades. On that day he chose a much more difficult task of trying to preach in a new town with an unfamiliar feel to it. The town was filled with many uncertain evils. This in no way altered his fathers holy quest. This would be his downfall and the beginning of Gregors abrupt uncertain future.

As his father preached to a small crowd of somewhat interested listeners, he noticed a group riding into town. They were equiped with swords, shields and were very heavly armored. One of the men dismounted his horse and attempted to insult and degrade Gregors father because he feared what he didn't understand. Gregors father stood tough, and defended his virtues, onlt to get run through by a bandits sword. Gregors father could not have died more honorably as he attempted to spread the principles of truth and justice!

The word of his death reached Gregor quickly, and he could see his destiny begin to unfold. He then knew then that he would take his fathers strong religious beliefs along with a few new ones of his own. He dedicated the following years to becoming an expert swordsman. Strong study and an unwavering dedication to truth and justice made him a mighty Paladin. As he grew in age he became exactly as he had forseen. His horrible tragedy that plagued him as a youth now turned Gregor into a great and honorable man. He traveled many miles in this great land, alone with my sword and the virtues as his guide. He needed more. He needed a greater purpose.

On one of his many nights alone Gregor had a guild travel across his path. They seemed very honorable and trustworthy. Gregor invited them all to enjoy the comforts of his campfire. The members of the guild gathered around and they spoke of many of the adventures they had shared as a group. The head of the guild approached Gregor and introduced himself as Lord Hawke,one of the two Guildmasters of Scarab. He too being a Paladin, could see Gregors faith and loyalty asked if he would be interested in joining their guild. Gregor has been faithful to Scarab and a member ever since.
