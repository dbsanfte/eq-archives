The Tales of Farold Falconray 's Life
  Keeper of Zorenn's heart

The wind moves slightly through the leaves of an old oak, deeply hidden in the forest. Almost invisible from human eyes, Farold, an elf of almost 500 seasons, leans against the trunk. Although slender of build his graceful movements reveal a body much stronger than it appears at first glance. His long brown hair covers his face partially, but when a gust of wind catches it his gray eyes appear. They are almost closed, but like a cat they observe everything around him. He is very alert and his leather-clad body appears to be ready to change from standing totally still to running in one second to another. This serves him very well, when he hunts for pelts or guides inexperienced people through the deeper woods during summer. His clothes look very old and worn but a closer look shows that he feels very comfortable in them and that they fit him like a second skin.

A young deer moves through the undergrowth but it does not notice Farold at all until it is almost beside him. It stops, stands perfectly still and looks at him. Then after a second, sensing no danger, it moves closer to him and he starts to stroke its flank. The young deer settles down at his feet and is very calm. A thought slips through Farold's mind: " Why can I deal so good with animals of the forest and why it is so difficult for me to be around humans?" He thinks about the great wizards daughter, who he longs to talk to but cannot find the words at all.

He thinks about this very often but never finds a satisfying answer for himself. At home in the forest among the animals and the trees he is somehow aloof around the humans. He is well liked and respected and many call him friend. But when asked to characterize him most fall silent. Many look confused when they realize that they know him not at all. He is not really complaining about this, because he likes to keep his own council. He will never be dependent on others, even though it causes loneliness at times. Of course, he never speaks with anybody about this.
