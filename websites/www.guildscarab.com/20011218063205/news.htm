Monday, December 10, 2001
  Scarab - DAOC -Sayuri
Scarab and Reckoning Raid Albion!

In a great show of force and organization Scarab and Reckoning took three Albion forts last night in a blazing hour and a half raid!

While the alliance was raiding Nottmoor, keeping the Albions busy, Scarab and Reckoning had free reign of the Albion frontier and razed half the territory.

After this, most of our force stayed behind to defend the keep near Snowdonia, where they succesfully kept the Albions at bay for several hours, while a team of Scarabs went to help the Alliance retake Nottmoor and Bledfaste. Soon Nottmoor and Bledfaste fall to us, but sadly while we were doing this Fort Scarab in Hibbieland fell to those foofy elves...

All in all a great night of raiding and counter-raiding.

Huzzah's go to:

Both Battering Ram teams! The speed, the professionalism, a sight to behold :)

Goonar, Captian of the Guard in Fort Scarab, who had only the grannies to keep him company most of the night.

Our home defense team, who despite two zone crashes kept at it, trying to retake our forts.

To Tharian and others who rallied our forces to get the home keeps back after the Alliance called the raid off!

Great job to everyone!
