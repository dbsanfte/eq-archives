Food: Jayfoot's Steak Marinade

Ingredients:
8 oz. honey
Bottle of Montreal Steak Seasoning
1 BIG bottle of Jack Daniels Green Label Whiskey

Instructions:
Take a large metal bowl and place on the stove.

Turn heat on to a medium level.

Pour in 8 oz. honey.

Pour in 2 bottles of Worchester Sauce

Pour in Soy Sauce (approx. 5 oz.)

Use half bottle of Montreal Steak Seasoning.

Keep stirring this mixture to allow the honey to blend. Heat should never be high enough to boil!!!!!

Add 1 BIG bottle of Jack Daniels Green Label Whiskey...

Get your steaks...make sure that you have tenderized the steaks...

Put each steak in the marinade for 10-15 seconds then place in large pan...

Cover each steak again with Montreal Steak Seasoning...

Pour enough marinade into tray so that the steaks are almost fully covered.

Let steaks sit in Marinade for minimum 24 hours...

Grill steaks!!! EAT STEAKS!!!

While grilling the steaks pour marinade into a pan and use flour to thicken into gravy...

Sautee Mushrooms and Onions (seperately) and then smother with gravy.

Posted by Jayfoot Bloomrose
