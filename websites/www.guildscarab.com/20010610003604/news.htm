Guild Meeting Summary
Last night we all gathered in Freeport for a guild meeting. Here's what happened:

Horammer introduced the ideas of having an official "Meeting Day" and an "Alt Day" on a monthly basis. The Meeting Day will serve as a consistent and predictable time when guild meetings will occur. Alt Day will be a day for playing alternate characters together just for kicks.

The Hate Gem Jewelry project is underway, and those who attended the last Hate raid will be contacted if they are eligible for guild-crafted jewelry created from the gems gathered in Hate.

Mountains of items and spells were distributed. A big thanks goes to Nilla for her incredible generosity!

ScarabFest is this coming weekend. If you plan to attend, be sure to check the Secure Board for travel directions.

Luxy, Corvax, and Divina all recieved a Torch of Loyalty medal and Trystelnar recieved the Spirit of Sacrifice medal. The MVP medals for the 1st Quarter 2001 were distributed as follows: Corvax - caster, Peabodi - tank, Blackrabbit - specialist, Horammer - healer. Way to go!

Finally, our most sincere condolences to Kelagry, Etsalon, and Corvax for being elected to the Inner Circle.
