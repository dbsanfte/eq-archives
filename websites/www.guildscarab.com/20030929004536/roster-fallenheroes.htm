Fallen Heroes of Scarab

The only thing constant in the universe is change.

There comes a time in everyone's life when they must move on, leaving the past behind them and pressing forward into the future. This section is devoted to honoring and preserving the memories of noble Scarab members who have moved on and are no longer with us in our journeys. Regardless of the honorable reasons that separate these heroes from the rest of the family, we will always remember them. They will always have a home in Scarab.

Their presence among us is gone, but their memories will never pass away.

Dedicated To Our Fallen Hero -Sir Blatz Burgimiester-
