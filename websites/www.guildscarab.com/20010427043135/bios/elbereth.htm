Elbereth's Story
   

I was born of royal blood...and heir to my father's throne.

From as early as I can remember I trained in the arts of magic and sword. Naturally I was to become a great Paladin as my father was. The castle life was all I ever knew until the age of 16 and thats when i joined in the holy crusades. My life long friends Dias and Ragnar had joined a guild called Knights of the Round Table. There we all fought bravely, but the evil there was overwhelming. It wasnt until we met up with three of the most brave and honorable people we had ever met...Boudica was the most talented archer i have ever seen and also the most beautiful...Cyan Bloodbane and his Brother, Cyric the Virus, also great warriors, accompanied her on there quest to destroy the evil's of our land. With a group like this we drove out the evil and killed diablo himself.

Then a voice from what seemed to be coming from inside of me said "Your Job is not done here, I send thee to Brittiania where a greater evil awaits...you will find a group of adventurers waiting for you by the river bank...treat them as if they were family...now go my son...and remember what i have tought you". That moment I knew it was my father. Then a gate poped up and I walked inside. When I made it to the other side I saw a group of people in the distance and I begain to walk to them. I introduced myself to them and told them my story.

We are Lord and Lady Hawke, Co Guildmasters of Scarab, said the Gentleman and Lady...this is Ozwyn, Ravenn, and Venom, we are all the founding members of Scarab. I then knew this was the group my father was talking about so I humbly asked if i could join their ranks and they graciously accepted me. It wasnt for a few days after then I met up with my friends from back home, and so I asked Lord Hawke if it was alright for my friends to join us in our quest for peace and he discussed it with the Inner Circle and they were accepted...then Cyric and Cyan informed me of there name change to Skie and Stout.

Now we were looking like a real guild and our name spread throughout the land.

As my father said, there is a greater evil at work...it did not take us long to find that evil. It seemed i had lost all my power from the gate trip and all I had were the clothes on my back...I quickly started to practice the art of archery since I still had my speed and no armor...it didnt take long for me to get good at it since there were plenty of monsters to fight. Over time the guild got big and I was recruited into the Inner Circle of Scarab, an honor I hold as high as any other. I started to think about my other family and why exactly i was brought here, but I didn't question my fathers purposes. I then realized why I was here...our honor and valor is what keeps this land from becoming a total chaotic world, it was then I heard my fathers voice again, he said "Now you know why I sent you - know that you can't completely stop evil, but as long as you contain it everything will work out."

Some time had past since i heard from my father...our guild had some conflicts and we went our own directions...Dias, Ragnar, and I tried to uphold the honor we once had but the evil had taken over us. I was lost and in trouble...the days got so dark that one couldn't tell it from the night.. I was on the verge of destroying everything in my path until I heard the voices of Lord and Lady Hawke say "What has become of you? Dont you remember what you stand for?" It was then when i realized what had happened... I knew then i had to do something before I lost control of myself..so I had Lord Hawke put me in a deep sleep until he called upon me to join him in another world...and so there i slept.

Now i finally awaken, and the darkness that once controlled me is lifted by the spirit of Scarab. The lands I once walked are merely a dream to me now... with the past aching in my heart i promise to uphold the honor and valor i once had... and with my family by my side I swear by the honor of Scarab I will not stray from the path of righteousness... and defend all that is good in this new world....Norrath.
