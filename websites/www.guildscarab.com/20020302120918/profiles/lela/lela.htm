The Tales of Lela Harofari 's Life
  Lela was lured into the world of Norrath by an irresistible force that was her sister, Yoldie. She follows the old tenets of the druid religion, that the gifts given to her are a sacred trust, and that she must share those gifts with those in need of them. Wanderlust found her at an early age, and she set out to discover the worlds beyond her island homeland. She remains single, knowing that the gift of the heart should be forever and true, and knowing that her own nature precludes her from this. Although, from time to time, she remembers a Paladin who for a while was a part of her life until his path diverged and he was gone.

Her path has been long, and she has made and lost many friends in this journey, but the one constant remains…. The bond shared by sisters.
