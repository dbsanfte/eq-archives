What we expect from our members
 
1) Be honestly motivated to be a member of the guild. Our guild is a large family, we take pride in the fact that every person in the guild really enjoys being a part of the guild. If you want to be a part of the guild, then be a PART of the guild.

a) Guild message board: This is the best place to get to know your fellow guild members. You can also pick up good info here.

b) Emails: The guild sends out a TON of emails. If you don't check your email often, you will miss out on a lot of important guild information. The guildmasters also include every guild member in almost every aspect of guild lawmaking, and if you dont check your email frequently you will miss out on the chance to have your voice heard.

c) Guild website news page: This is your #1 source for up to date guild news. Check it frequently.

It is very simple to remain an active, informed member of the guild. This motivation has to come from you, members who don't show they want to be in the guild, probably won't be in the guild.


2) The guild has two sets of laws which dictate our actions. The Code of Conduct and the Code of Chivalry. These codes are the heart and soul of SCARAB, and are to be followed at all times. Any violation of the Code of Conduct will result in instant dismissal from the guild.


The world of Norrath is much safer travelled in groups. When at all possible, travel with fellow guildmates. That is what the guild is for.


Read read over our requirements of membership. If you have any questions, please contact a Guildmaster.

SCARAB Mission Statement
 
At SCARAB, we strive to promote our code of conduct and chivalry throughout the lands of norrath by deed and word.

Our members strive to uphold our values to create a world where evil fears the light of honor, loyalty and Valor.

Our members will strive to make Norrath a land where all can enjoy life.

SCARAB is also commited to ensuring that all of our members enjoy their guild. Guild activities will promote the family enviornment we have created.

SCARAB was founded for our members to have fun with each other, and to have fun in the world we live in.
   
Current Guild rules and bylaws.
 
GUILD SERVER - Solusek Ro

GUILD BASE OF OPERATIONS - Freeport

RACE RESTRICTIONS - Evil Trolls, Ogres, or Dark Elfs will not be accepted into the guild under any circumstances.

CLASS RESTRICTIONS - Shadowknights and Necromancers are pure evil, and have no place in our guild.

GUILD RACIAL INTERACTION RESTRICTIONS - SCARAB will not judge a person soley on their race. We understand that Trolls, Ogres, and even the Dark Elf can cast away the evil heritage and take up the cause of honor. It for this reason that SCARAB has the following policy regarding racial interaction.

SCARAB members may have friendly relations with a Troll, Ogre, or Dark Elf who has given up his ways of evil, and is against the ways of his own race. This must be proven in deeds of honor. SCARAB members should be hesitant to take the word of any Troll, Ogre, or Dark Elf without witnessing true acts of honor firsthand. Any member associating with an evil member of ANY race shall be immediatly dismissed from the guild.

DIRECT OPPOSITION CLASSES - Shadowknights and Necromancers stand for everything that is evil and putrid. SCARAB members despise these dark classes and would never have even a passing conversation with one.

We understand that people might have friends who are roleplaying "evil". Members of SCARAB, however, while in character, despise these races.

ROLEPLAYING VS POWERGAMING - SCARAB would be considered a middle of the road Roleplaying guild. When at all possible, we ask that members try to be in character. Ooc comments are not prohibited by any means, nor do we expect people to be hard core roleplayers all of the time. We do however, do not support powergaming. There are times when a touch of powergaming is acceptable, and even needed. SCARAB members should not consider powergaming to be a top motiviation for their character.


MEMBER INTEGRITY - At NO time will a member of SCARAB attempt to hack game code. Members of SCARAB will at no time exploit bugs for any reason. Members of SCARAB will not promote bug exploiting, or any other illegial activity as deemed by Verant Interactive, or the Guildmasters of SCARAB. Any member found to violate this bylaw will be immediatly dismissed from the guild.


Player VS Player - SCARAB is supportitive of it's members and their values. The Inner Circle and Guildmasters have concluded that we should be a -PVP guild on Norrath. There are limitations in the way that -PVP players can interact with +PVP players (from the functionality of the game), and thus we need to all be "in the same boat" for optimal group interaction. There is alot of PVP interaction in -PVP, such as dueling and Guild Wars, which provide honorable PVP fighting situtations.

Phases of Membership
 
RECRUITS

When a new member is accepted, they automatically become recruits. Recruits are watched over by members of the Inner Circle and the guildmasters to make sure that the recruit not only follows the SCARAB way, but also is an active participant in the guild. Actions both out of game and in game will tell the inner circle if the recruit has a chance to become a full member. The path to full member is long and difficult. Many times the recruit will be tested. Guildmasters or Inner Circle members may, without warning, test the recruits knowedge of guild policy. When it appears the recruit is ready, An Inner Circle member or Guildmaster will nominate the recruit for full membership status, there will be a vote, if the vote passes, the recruit is promoted to full member. If the vote fails, the recruit is notified of the areas to improve in.

GUILD MEMBERS

Once promoted from the status of Recruit, the member is now a full member of the SCARAB Family. Full members are the heart and soul of the guild, and are able to nominate new inner circle members, recieve a custom guild pop3 account, and have the bragging rights of being in a tight knit family of true friends and comrades.

HIGH COUNCIL

Members who serve the guild and promote the codes of conduct and chivalry may find themselves appointed by the guildmasters to serve on the High Council. The High Council is very important because only members that have reached this position are eligible for Inner Circle duty. The High Council offers input on guild bylaw and policy decisions, but all voting for those issues are reserved for the Inner Circle. The most important duty of the High Council is that of training and evaulating new recruits before they are promoted to full member status.

INNER CIRCLE

A few members will impress the guild to the point where they will find themselves voted for a seat on the Inner circle of SCARAB. This elite group of lawmakers are the ones who vote on almost all of the key issues within the guild. The Inner Circle are the leaders of the guild, who help the Guildmasters administer the guild and help with day to day operations making sure the Scarab way is spread throughout the land.

Code of Chivalry.
 

Justice-  In courageously seeking the truth,   and the path of righteousness, members must strive to escape their own personal gain and instead be just.  Members who seek out justice without bending to temptation will be members of renown. Justice serves both HONOR and LOYALTY.

Sacrifice-  Generosity is a main characteristic of a SCARAB member.   In order to counter the weakness of gluttony, members are to be as generous as their resources will allow!!!!   Generous members are better able to walk the fine line between mercy and cold justice. Giving rather than taking makes the name of SCARAB known and respected. Sacrifice serves HONOR.

Compassion- The weak and Innocent are our charges, thier defense and safety are our duty. The might and wisdom of the SCARAB member allow those not capable of thier own well being to stand boldy behind us. The meek may rest weary heads on the strength of our shoulders. Compassion serves HONOR and VALOR.

Spirituality-  Members must seek to be masters of their own minds and bodies .   From this prowess we must seek to humbly serve those to whom we have sworn our lives and duty. Spirituality serves both LOYALTY and HONOR.

THIS IS THE SCARAB WAY!!! To these ends we strive to serve! Thanks to Lord Gregor, Who provided the model for this code.
