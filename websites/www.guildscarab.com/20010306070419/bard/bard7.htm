The Ambush...and the recovery
 
  The evening air was stale, yet not entirely unpleasant. Hawke moved with a purpose, the collection of bags and backpacks, and the contents within them, tied together seemed to weigh him down a bit and the

walls of Freeport seemed that much further away.

He had just came from Rivervale and was carrying some guild supplies back to Freeport. The guild would benefit from the items he had, as Lord Cloud and the wise and powerful Llau Chan had pulled a rare

vest from the beast known as LockJaw. "This will make a good reward to someone" Hawke though, and as he lost himself in thought.

He passed the First Inn of the Eastlands, and moved into the desert. West Freeport was just a small distance away now. The thought of having to cross the path of a vile militiaman was of little concern now, as

It was more important to safely deliver the items to the bank vault.

It had been a long trip, but Hawke knew that soon it would be over. Without warning he felt himself hit by a vile magic, and some life energy slipped away. Hawke spun around to see a group of Tier'Dal , joined by a hideous Skeleton, rapidly approaching. "Marr!" Hawke shouted, slipping the collection of bags and packs off his shoulders and reaching for his blade. Another blast of magic slammed into him, and He felt a vile disease fester within him. His attempts to draw his blade almost succeeded, but the necromancers pet had other intentions, knocking the sword free from Hawke's hands. Several more dark spells slammed into him now, as others from the group began to cast on him. The Skeleton lunged forward and pinned Hawke's arms to his side, and weakened by the disease, Hawke fell to the ground.

A sinister laugh was heard as one of the dark ones walked up to him. He was shorter than most Tier’Dal, and his hair was pure white. Blood ran into Hawke's eye, and he could not see much more.

The necromancer began to rummage through the packs and Hawke could see several items taken.

"Leave them be foul one!" Hawke muttered, "Take thy hatred on me, but leave those items alone, for they belong to my guild!"

"I will take what I want, Hawke of Scarab, Your guild does not deserve items such as these"

Hawke struggled against the grip of the Skeleton, which held him pinned down. His efforts rewarded with the hideous cackling laughter of the undead pet.

The Necromancer, finished with the looting of Hawke's packs, drew a dagger and smiled, moving towards Hawke.

"Now, Vile Paladin, your life ends, and all of Innoruk's people will celebrate!"

Hawke could see the man clearer now, his purple robes stained with blood.

"Enough is enough!" Hawke shouted. He gathered in his will and called forth the power of Marr, calling for the pet to be warded. Bands of blue light began to swirl around Hawke, then shot out and caught

The undead pet. The force of the blow knocked the Skeleton back, and Hawke was free to go. He quickly snatched up his shield and bashed the nearest Tier’Dal. Other necromancers began to cast more spells, and

Hawke was again knocked to his feet. He felt himself on the verge of death, vile poisons and disease infested him, and the world began to go black around him.

With his last ounce of strength, he again called to Marr, and Laid his Hands upon his body. In a rush his life force returned, and he stood up, ready to face his attackers.

Hawke stood alone, his attackers had fled. Hawke quickly gathered his packs and took inventory.

They had taken a Banded tunic, a mystical Skull that was rumored to give a caster more mana, a snakeskin tunic, and the mighty Vest of LockJaw.

This was an outrage, a horrible injustice. Something had to be done, and Hawke knew just what to do.

He reached under his tunic and grasped the guildstone he wore on a chain around his neck.

"Members of Scarab, I, Lord Hawke, Guildmaster, Call thee to Arms!" He thought, with the powerful guildstone sending his words to all that wore a matching guildstone.

Hawke told the guild of the ambush, and called on them to find the leader of the band of Dark Elves.

Soon after, the Dark Elf lay dead.. Lord Clouds blade dispensing final justice on the vile one.

Many members of the guild had banded together to find, and dispense Scarab Justice!

In the end, all was well.
