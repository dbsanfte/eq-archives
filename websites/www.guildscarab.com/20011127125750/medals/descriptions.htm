Medal Descriptions
Medal Of Service
This award is presented by the Master of Admissions to any member of Scarab who has proven his honesty, hard work, dedication and loyalty to the guild by surviving our admissions process.
 
Torch Of Loyalty
This Medal is given to any Scarab member who has proven loyalty and dedication in any various way. Such acts as donations, courage in battle, or a tremendous show of loyalty to the guild whether in game or out of game. This medal is received via nomination by any guildmember for ANY action. The Guildmaster and/or the Inner Circle then review the nomination and decide if the medal is to be awarded.
 
Medal Of Triumph
This medal is awarded to any member or recruit who has reached the 50th level.
 
Webmaster Crest
This award is given to anyone who has direct impact on the design and/or maintenance of the Scarab website and related projects. The dedication and commitment to maintaining the guilds website is a great service indeed.
 
Heart of Brittania
This Medal is awarded to any Scarab member who had served in the guild during its existence in the world of Brittania. The member who has earned this medal has showed loyalty to the guild by continuing to strive to further the cause of Scarab.
 
Heart of Norrath
This Medal is awarded to any Scarab member who had served in the guild during its existence in the world of Norrath. The member who has earned this medal has showed loyalty to the guild by continuing to strive to further the cause of Scarab.
 
Heart of Camelot
This Medal is awarded to any Scarab member who had served in the guild during its existence in the world of Camelot. The member who has earned this medal has showed loyalty to the guild by continuing to strive to further the cause of Scarab.
 
Heart of the Crusader
This medal is awarded to any member who, either solo or part of a group, has completed a Guild Quest. This medal is also awarded to the creators of the quest. Completion of a Guild Quest takes dedication, honor, and hard work. This medal is awarded by the Guildmaster and/or the Inner Circle.
 
Spirit of Sacrifice
This medal is awarded to any guildmember who makes an extremely large sacrifice, whether it be of time, equipment or funds.
 
Star of Truth
This Medal is awarded to the member who has taken the time to complete the Test Of Truth from the Paladins of Marr. By earning his testament of truth, this individual has earned the prestigious right to be known as a Knight of Scarab.
 
Blue Star of Scarab
This Medal will be given to any Scarab member who performs a deed of a highly exceptional nature. In order to receive this award, someone from the guild must have first nominated the person to receive, and then the Guildmaster and Inner Circle will review the actions of the individual and then pass judgement on whether the award will be given. The act must be of an heroic nature in order to be considered for the honored Blue Star of Scarab.
 
Crest of Leadership
This Medal will be given to any Scarab member who has one time served in the position of Guildmaster, Assistant Guildmaster or Leader of the Inner Circle. This Medal recognizes the guild member for the sacrifices made that come with the position, even if the member is no longer serving as a Guildmaster, Assistant Guildmaster or Leader of the Inner Circle. Once a person has earned the respect and loyalty of the guild by being voted into the position, forever do they earn the right to wear the Crest of Leadership.
 
Scarab Medal of Honor
Much like the National Medal of Honor, this ultimate award can be given to any Scarab member who goes above and beyond the call of duty. This award can only come from an Inner Circle nomination and then once the person is nominated the entire Inner Circle will vote on whether the award is given or not. The highest achievement possible in the guild is to perform an act so great to be worthy of the Scarab Medal of Honor!
 
Peabodi Award
The Peabodi award rewards longstanding and high level loyalty to Scarab. Recipients are bedrock members of the guild and are examples of Honor, Valor, and Loyalty. The IC confers this medal on a guildmember they feel has shown loyalty and and an exceptional level of support for a long period of time. The Peabodi award was named for Sir Peabodi Sinclaire, to whom it was first given in June, 2001, in conjunction with his being the first ever Scarab to reach level 60 in Everquest.
 
Most Valuable Player Award (casters)
This medal is rewarded occassionally to the person voted the Most Valuable Player in the category of Casters. This includes Enchanters, Magicians, Wizards, and Necromancers.
 
Most Valuable Player Award (healers)
This medal is rewarded occassionally to the person voted the Most Valuable Player in the category of Healers and Helpers. This includes Clerics, Druids, and Shamans.
 
Most Valuable Player Award (specialists)
This medal is rewarded occassionally to the person voted the Most Valuable Player in the category of Specialists. This includes Bards, Rogues, Monks.
 
Most Valuable Player Award (tanks)
This medal is rewarded occassionally to the person voted the Most Valuable Player in the category of Tanks. This includes Paladins, Rangers, and Warriors.
