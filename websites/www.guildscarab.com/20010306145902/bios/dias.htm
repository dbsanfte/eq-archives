Dias' Story
   

The wind blew strongly through the open door, causing the few inside to huddle tightly in their cloaks. The bartender looks up and looks at the bundled creature standing in his doorway. Layers of clothing peel away to reveal a stern faced male face. I walk across the room, sliding into a booth against the wall, facing the door.

Smiling warmly, "What would you have?" asks the waitress.

"Ale and stew if you got it." I reply.

"That'll be 5 copper."

Three coppers are placed on the table and covered with a well callused hand, "Half now.. the rest when the food comes."

I watch the waitress swish off through a door, the scent of food and warm grease drifting from the back. Looking slowly around the room, I notice that the tavern is quite busy. Two dwarves play a game of dice in the corner, a few nondescripts sit at the bar buried in their ale. Drawn to the bright colors, I see an elf adorned in bright blue sitting in a booth near the door. Picking up my ale, I walk over to his table.

"Hail, might I sit and talk? I have not spoken to anyone in some time."

"Pull up a seat, young sir. An old man, such as myself, always enjoys company." The stranger gestures to an empty chair.

Spinning the chair so it faces the door, "What is an elder doing out in such harsh weather?"

"Elder eh? I am in search of a young half-elf by the name of Dias Icewind. He is said to be a drifter from this part of the world"

Stunned to hear my name, I inquire further, "What do you need with him? I know of him, and have seen him on occasion." "I have important news of his parents, do you know where he could be found?"

Excited by the prospect of learning of my parents, almost yelling "I am Dias Icewind, what do you know of my parents?!?"

Raising an eyebrow, the elder looks at you suspiciously, "What proof have you that you are who you say you are?" Pulling my cloak and shirt off, I show him my right arm, where the tattoo of a large star floating above a large oak tree, on a mountainous landscape is placed.

"This was drawn on me as a young child, before I can even remember. It is all I have to remember my heritage by"

Looking intently at the tattoo, "Amazing, I have only seen one other such tattoo. Placed on the left arm of a half-elf that was slain in battle. That half-elf was your brother, you were twins. The name of the tree is the Ellcrys, created by long lost elven magic, locking the hordes of ravenous demons in their dimension. This is the mark of the family Icewind, destined to protect the world of Norrath from these evil forces. The time of darkness is upon us, you must seize your destiny, and destroy the evil that threatens. You are the worlds hope, but you will need help. There is a force already gathering to the north, go to them and give them direction. Prove your worth and make your ancestors proud, for you are born of the nobles of nobility. The last family left of a long line of defenders of the Ellcrys."

The rest of the morning flew by quickly as I prepare for my journey. I mount my horse, and look back over my shoulder to see the elder gazing to the north…towards the waiting army...towards my destiny
