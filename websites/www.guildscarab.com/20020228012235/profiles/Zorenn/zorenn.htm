The Tales of Zorenn Falconray 's Life
  Her Heart Belongs to Farold

I understand if my presence makes you nervous Warrior. Surely you have sensed something is not what it seems. I will take off my cloak of the gentle woman that allows me to stand in the shadows and reveal myself to you. As you have noticed I have received the last of the facial tattoos of my clan. I am the great Zore's first and favorite daughter. I am a woman not a child to dazzle. Yes I am learned in the customs and folkways of the cities of the Realm, but never forget that I am from the Clan of Dragon Falconry. I have rode the dragons and crossed into the Mystic. My medicine is strong. Many a powerful mage cannot meet my gaze. I keep the secrets of mages, warriors and kings. I am not ashamed. Shame is for fools. I rejoice in my femaleness. Come sit with me and talk if you dare, but know this Warrior, I am not afraid.
