Sir Hawke's Story
   

Hawke, as he is known now, has no real memories of his childhood. Born to a poor family, his only thoughts of his mother and father are brief images, mental flashes, smells, and sounds. One morning, around the passing of his 7th year, he awoke to find himself in the home of a saddened uncle, who refused to answer any questions about his strange arrival. His only contact to his family was a wooden crate, which contained a sword from his father, and his mothers sewing kit. His uncle would never let the young boy discuss his previous life, and always called him "boy". His uncle took care of him, and taught him to be hardworking, loyal and honest.

One day, after his 16th birthday, he was chopping wood in the clearing outside his uncles small home when a man on horseback rode up and identified himself as a tax collecter. Hawkes uncle emerged from the small home and demanded to know what was going on. Without a word the tax collecter pushed his uncle to the ground, entered the home, and emerged with several items, including Hawkes crate that contained the only links to his past. His uncle tried to stand, but was kicked in the face and dropped. Hawke, his hot temper flaring, moved towards the man, "What tax is so important that you must injure an old man!" Hawke demanded. "Mind your own life, Boy!" the collector shouted, mounting his horse. "How dare he take my items" Hawke thought, his anger at the injustice raging in him, He sprinted off after the horse and rider, running as fast as he could. The chase lasted many miles.. and Hawke was soon joined in pursuit by a local sherrif, who was curious about the goings-on.

After seeing young Hawke continue the chase, the sherrif spurred his horse along side the fleeing man, and stopped him. Out of breath, Hawke tried to explain his story, but immediatly the tax collector attempted to flee, but was quickly caught by the sherrif. "This man is no tax collector" The sherrif explained. "We have been looking for an imposter robbing homes claiming to be from the tax office for quite a while now!" The Lawman shackled him and gave the young Hawke his gear back. "You've got quite the spirit there boy, What's your name" said the lawman, in awe at the distance Hawke ran to retrieve his items. "I have no name, well, no name that I can remember." The Lawman smiled, "Well you certainly have the spirit of a hawk to chase this imposter down for your items." Said the man. "It wasnt the items, Hawke said, "It was the injustice of it." He paused, thinking about what the sherrif had just called him. "Hawk? I like the sound of that." The lawman smiled, "Well young Hawk, with an attitude like that.. You should go talk to my friend Sir Repirn at the Paladins Guild." With the criminal in shackles the lawman rode away, leaving Hawke in the middle of the road, his words about the paladins guild rolling in his mind. Hawke did just that.. he arrived at the gates to the paladins guild the next morning, asking to speak with Sir Repirn. "Whats the name, boy" Said the gruff gate keeper. Without realizing the importance of the event, Hawke lifted his chin, looked the huge man in the eyes and boldly said. "Hawke, my name is Hawke, and I would take kindly if thou would not refer to me as 'boy'". The gatekeeper began to laugh, and let young Hawke pass.

After spending several years talking and learning from the wise and noble Paldin Sir Repirn, Hawke was given a quest to spread the virtues of Loyalty, Honor and Valor. Bidding goodbye to his uncle, he took his fathers sword, his mothers sewing kit, and headed off to his destiny.

Armed with a new name, and determination, Hawke traveled across the commonlands towards the west gates of Freeport. The guards at the gate payed no attention to the young man. Hawke made his way through the city, ending up at a grassy pathway with a sign labled "Hall of Truth".

He found himself standing before a large keep, surrounded by a moat. He crossed into the keep, and made his way to the top. A large armored Paladin took him aside, and listened as young Hawke told his story.

Hawke spent many years training and learning the ways of truth. The ideals that Sir Repirn had taught him served as building blocks for Hawke's growth into a Paladin.

One day Hawke was quietly strolling towards the dock to buy some fish cakes. Unknown to him, he was headed for an encounter that would change his life. He walked into to the port authority to find the place full of races of all kinds, One Dwarf caught his attention. She was standing in line, waiting her turn to sell her basket full of fresh fish, and a rusted dagger. She was reading a book on the history of Freeport. She seemed so out of place surrounded by the Elfs and Humans, yet didn't seem to mind. He stood there for a moment, not realizing that he was staring at her.

A slight movement caught his eye as a rogue moved slowly towards the reading Dwarf, his hands reaching for her coin pouch. Before Hawke could react, The Dwarf spun, planting her fist under his rib cage. "I wouldn't do that." She said with a fierce tone, "I work hard for those coins." The rogue ran from the shop, out of breath and stunned.

The Dwarf looked up at Hawke, who stood with an slight grin staring at her. He introduced himself, and they quickly became friends. He found her to be strong willed, Loyal, honest, and just. She was a cleric, and therefore shared the same values as Hawke. Together they learned about the city they lived in, and they begin to explore the possibilitys of forming a Guild based on the virtues they share. he found himself wanting to spead and promote the virtues more and more each day that he and his dwarven friend talked, her dedication to them made him want to be more dedicated, and a friendly rivalry formed that exists to this day.

Soon they married, and teamed up with some friends, Ozwyn, Venomm, and Ravenn, to form a guild of Honor, Valor, and loyalty that would serve Norrath to promote good and smite evil. Lord and Lady Hawke would be the guildmasters, and have continued to provide wisdom and leadership to Scarab since the day it was founded.
