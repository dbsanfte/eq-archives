       Rules

No grocery-list of rules at this point. The people know what is expected of them and can use common sense.

Sublimity does not use a point system, but the guild does track prior loot and participation by using EQDKP with zeroized points. Loot is decided by a council of officers. The officers evaluate participation, prior loot awarded, and what kind of upgrade it would be for the individual.
