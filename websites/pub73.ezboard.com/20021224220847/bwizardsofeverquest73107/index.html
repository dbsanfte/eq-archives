Welcome to the Wizards of EverQuest message board! This message board is currently under construction, the contents are subject to change at any time, without notice. The primary purpose of the "Wizards of EverQuest" message board is to promote online communities for the players of EverQuest Online Adventures for the PS2. “Wizards of EverQuest” will endeavor to provide a place where Wizards in EQoA can gather to exchange information about Wizardry in EQoA with their peers.

This message board is NOT intended to take the place of "Graffe's Wizard Compilation", a popular website dedicated to both the players of Wizard Characters and their "friends" in EverQuest PC.
The “Wizards of EverQuest” message board is designed to be the nexus or single point of reference for the Wizard Community in EQoA. If you have any suggestions, questions, or if you wish to correspond with me privately, send an email to ozzymandos@yahoo.com
