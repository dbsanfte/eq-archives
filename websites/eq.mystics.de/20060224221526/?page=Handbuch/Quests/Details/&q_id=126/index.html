 Guide

EDITOR'S NOTE: We have reports this quest now is initiated by examining a pump near the Insane Peeper. We need confirmation and a loc/zone on this new start.

If anyone has any further information please submit to our staff or leave a comment.

Engineer Veebert can be found in the Thieve's Way in the big room in the middle of zone, next to the machine. He tells you that someone is pumping goo into the sewer system and that you should seek out Shift Boss Myrkle in Serpent Sewers.

Shift Boss Myrkle is a rare spawn in the same room as Crazy Peepers (most SW room in Serpent Sewers). He is aggro, but killing him is not what you want to do. You must talk to him, and apparantly you can just barely hail him from outside his aggro range if you edge forwards.

Shift Boss Murkle tells you that it is really an engineer down in Edgewater Drains who is pumping the stuff into the system and that you should go talk to him. He tells you to find Engineer Grimosh in Edgewater Drains.

Engineer Grimosh can be found way down the bottom of Edgewater Drains where all the very high level Murkwaters are. You tell him that he needs to stop pumping goo into the system and he says ok, and gives you the Key to Storage Room 303.

The quest ends here, and there is no hint as to where this storage room can be found.
