 Branch to One of Two Alternate Webpages
 
 
NOTE:  Like " Lord of the Rings " in the 'Quintessential LOTR' banner on the 'Home' page, these pages were designed to be viewed in the 'Flat Brush' font.  If your computer doesn't show them that way, right click on this link and save the file to your 'C:\WINDOWS\FONTS' folder.
 
 
I have created two different webpages to display these trailer images.  One or the other may suit you better, depending on what kind of Internet connection you have and what you want to do with them (print them, compare them or just look through them):

1.  Those who have a FAST connection, or who want to print out all the images should go to THIS page.  Every screencap is displayed, one after the other, in what was originally the largest available size (480x200 pixels), with one line of description per image or group of images.  However, for people on a slow line, this page will take a long time to load.

2.  Those who have a SLOW connection, or those who want to look at a number of smaller images together on their monitor at the same time should go to THIS page.  The pictures are displayed as thumbnail images (one, two or three/line), with one (or more) line(s) of description per image or group of images, as needed.  Each thumbnail will 'pop-up' to a larger image (480x200 or 640x272) in a separate window if you click on it.
