Phew! -- by Lohki

Last night, we killed Cazic Thule for the first time. What a great fight! He dropped a Cloak of the Fearsome(Xasraiki), Pauldrons of Ferocity(Lohki), Bile Etched Obsidian Choker(Opafaf). Too stoked to say anything more...


Dracho was also slain, giving up a Grotesque Girdle to Tawyyn and a Berzerkers Ring to Cervantes.

Few Talendors and Severilous here and there .. Green Scales to Coem and Grazskin and a Cloak of Flames to Wrekk are about the only items worth mentioning.

And of course the constant ToV raiding leaving us with some phat loot I'm not going to bother listing out and bore you all to death with.
