AoW and a visit into the new Sleepers Tomb -- by Malbadon

We started out our day with Avatar of War. We got to test out that 3 Dire Charm pets thing I mentioned yesterday, the 3 stooges performed well.



After that we decided to go check out the new Sleeper's Tomb. I would like to give a big 'ol FU to whoever decided to change the Newly Created Sent's above DC level. We cleared the zone, from Master of the Guard and Prog all the way down to Final Arbitor, and then we laid waste to the 4 Ancient Guardians. Got a few of those nifty BP's from em, nothing you haven't seen on Allakhazam's tho. Here's a few of em showing em off, Akiro's 52ac chain look and Thaldin sporting an SMR :)
