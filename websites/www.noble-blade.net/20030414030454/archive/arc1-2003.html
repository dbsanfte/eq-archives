February 2003


Tuesday, February 18, 2003

Vallon Zek meets the graveyard. -- by Malbadon

Noble has kicked it into high gear the last few weeks, playing around in Tactics killing Tallon Zek again and most recently his multiple personality brother, Vallon. Can bigboy Rallos be far behind?

We have also added some kill marks beside Emperor, Blood, High Priest (x2), Cursed (x2), Grummus, Arch Lich, and Creator, along with another visit to lovely Vex Thal.

 

Sunday, February 2, 2003

We came, we saw, we rubbed Aten-tooshie -- by Malbadon


We did it. How else is there to say it. Less than a month after our first Emp kill, we did the shard-camp from hell that is Vex Thal key and became a Vex Thal raid force. Despite the constant camping we still managed to squeeze in 2 more Emp's, Tallon Zek, Manaetic Behemoth, Cursed, High Priest, Arch Lich (or 3), King Pooh and good 'ol Dozekar. But who cares about all that! We popped off Vex Thal over the weekend! On with the loots!!
