Monday, August 5, 2002

NToV cleared, THO flattened, Burrower? oh let me get started.... -- by Malbadon

So, we went to go kill the Burrower Beast tonight, a rather long involved fight with multiple waves in a non-CoH zone and a huge megaHP wurm at the end. We got him down to 18ish% with about 4 deaths, all rezzed back and fighting fine, when suddenly I hear "WTF" from our MT. Then suddenly, "You have been summoned", and I am staring at the zonein. Then a cleric appears beside me, then another cleric, then a few more tanks, then apparently the Beast disappears leaving the rest of the guild nothing to swing at, except the 10 of us at the zonein now. The gang in the Burrower tunnel are ready for action, unfortunately the Burrower is MIA, somewhere inside the world gemotry, and when he finally decides to appear back in his tunnel he is at 50%. At this point we have a GM there watching this occur, and they get him down to 7% when suddenly people start appearing around me at the zonein again, including yet more clerics, and the fight was over.

For 3 years I have accepted falling under the world in zones, having a mob mysteriously fall under the world and summon you down there, and outright watching a mob walk through a tunnel and fall through a crack (Holgresh killers know what I am talking about), but this takes the cake. You put a raid-level mob in a tunnel, knowing all the problems you had with tunnels in Velious, and guess what? You STILL cant figure out how the hell to create a WALL. I don't mind losing to mobs when the mobs beats us, but having 3 hours of my guildmates time wasted in the last 3 minutes of a fight cause Joe-coder put a 1 where a 0 was supposed to be is a joke.

In other news.. :)

Cleared NToV over the weekend and also killed Thought Horror Overfiend before the Burrower fiasco. We got some really nice loot from everything, except Cekenar, no Blackstar was forthcoming.
