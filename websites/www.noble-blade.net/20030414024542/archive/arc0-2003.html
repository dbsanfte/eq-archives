January 2003


Monday, January 6, 2003

Emperor falls! Ssra completed! Vex Thal awaits!! -- by Malbadon


In one of the most amazing battles we've ever seen, we met, engaged, and buried Emperor Ssraeshza! After a chaotic start and suffering through a jawdropping amount of LD's we buckled down and chopped away at the Grand-pappy of snakes. His loot was, well, amazing! Grats (clockwise) Howy, Katair, Morlen, and me!



We also killed Creator and High Priest as well as tearing through Seb in about 15 minutes, plastering Trakanon all over the wall just for giggles.

 

Thursday, January 2, 2003

Happy New Years from us! -- by Malbadon

Good to see the Rathe is starting the new year off on a high note!!
