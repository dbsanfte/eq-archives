Thursday, December 13, 2001

Sometimes the best way from A to B is to stop at C and killing that mob too. -- by Malbadon
We served up a full plate of baddies tonight. Eventually these patches have to stop so I can get a real nights sleep..

The gang started the day off at ToV killing the named. Along the way through WW Myga graced the gang with her presence, grats Daio on DragonsBane!.

The Special Ops team discovered that Vaniki was up in Dragons Necropolis so they headed on in to get Lucitano a Willsapper.

After that the named fell and the troops gathered to take out Doze. Apparently Doze and the named are tired of seeing us, as they refuse to hand out the good stuff. Grats Uzumzen on backpack_01 on 5-6 orbs mediocre tears.

Our good friend Lendaria the Keeper made the mistake of saying that Dorfs made good eats, so we gave her a schoolyard beating until she apologized. Unfortunately a few people didn't hear her in time and she died. /shrug.
Grats Tigert on Ring of Relkententinar, Tillamook on Maul of Superiority, and Geddre on a ST key!



With everyone high on our success, we flew off to Kael to go visit Mr. Tormax. Our elite green beret monks spent a while perfecting their pull strategy, and much muttering of "ok..lemee try that again, but from here this time", was heard. Their work paid off as they proceeded to pull each of his guards one at a time. GG monks! We decided to stop poor Tormax from feeling lonely and brought him out and flattened him
Grats Mada on Ring of Lightening, Kanem on Axe of the Kromzek Kings, Gaglain on Crown of the Kromzek Kings, and Vincecross on Tormax's head for White Dragonscale boots!



The troops were still restless, I mean, there was still 2 hours till yet another patch, so the team headed off to go take out Zlandicar. Once dead his loots were distributed quickly, with but 20 minutes to spare till our friend "Mr. Patch" forced the gang to bed. First Brood Talisman to Berg, Zlandicar's Heart to Carden, sword to Matok, Fraka Dar talisman for Shroud of Dar Brood to Rheanna, and grats Raifin on ST key!!

and with that, NB called it a day..except Thaldin..who never sleeps it seems...
