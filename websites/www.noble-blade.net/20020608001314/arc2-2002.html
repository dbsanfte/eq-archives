Saturday, March 23, 2002

Grieg Veneficus falls -- by Malbadon

One of the other guilds on our server decided it would be very adult-like to post on their main page how Grieg hadn't been killed yet, despite knowing the guild they were drawing criticism from for monopolizing him, was at a get-together for the weekend.

We couldn't help but notice their update the next day still didn't include Grieg on it, so after we got done our 10th RingWar we went and killed him. Thanks for the heads up guys!



Grats Marrius on Jenny's phone number!! Devian on a Bone Ulak!


We really have been busy this week, I've just been a lazy updater.

We have also done 2 10th RingWars for Raifin and Fosters, and grats Falconus on finally getting his Doze Mask :)
