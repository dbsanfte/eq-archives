Wednesday, August 29, 2001

Kaboosh! -- by Lohki

Noble Blade gathered tonight to flush the big walking poo named Cazic Thule. Loot consisted of a Halo of the Enlightened which went to Teclis, and a Cloak of the Fearsome which went to Mianiel.


Dracholiche and the golems were also slain. Dracholiche dropped a Grotesque Girdle which went to Kamasu and a ring went somewhere...

We also made our first venture into Veeshan's Peak this past weekend and dispatched three dragons. Was hoping for a full clearing on our first trip but VP had a few twists we weren't used to. We'll clear it next trip!

Silverwing was first on the list...


Next up was Hoshkar....


And lastly, Druushk.


Loot consisted of:
Hammer of the Dragonborne - Gaglain
Hammer of the Dragonborne - Groggog
Executioners Blade - Grazskin

Robe of Burning Rage - Slyth
Feverblade - Rainessa
Fatestealer - Rylnor

Shissar Deathspeaker Staff - Mordicai
Sarnak Summoners Staff - Ariman

I believe I missed one or two drops .. oh well.
