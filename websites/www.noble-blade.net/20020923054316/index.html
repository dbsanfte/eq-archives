Weekend Update -- by Malbadon


We've had a busy few days. Thursday we knocked down Avatar of War (more Blades, a ring, and a mask), and then The Creator. It was a great day and we got great loot.
Friday wasn't our best moment, and I made a pretty picture to commemorate it, click here.

Next we spent a buncha mind-numbing hours in PoG clearing and dropped that high-kicking lady, Tunare. We then topped off the night by heading into Fear and killing my favorite mob, Cazic Pooh.
