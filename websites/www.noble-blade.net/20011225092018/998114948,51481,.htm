Karadin receives Serrated Dragon Tooth! -- by Milambus




After killing the named drakes in the Halls of Testing tonight, Karadin was able to complete the Test of the Tooth. Resulting in the Serrated Dragon Tooth.

Earlier in the day, a small force gathered in Cobalt Scar to kill Kelorek`Dar.

Loots:
Cloak of Crystalline Waters - Gutar
Bioluminescent Orb - Beja
2 x Kelorek`Dar Spine Razor - Vault
