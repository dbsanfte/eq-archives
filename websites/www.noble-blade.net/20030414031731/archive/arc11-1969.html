December 1969


Wednesday, December 31, 1969

Noble Blade tromps across Norrath. -- by Malbadon

Busy couple of days, dropped a couple nice mobs including Mr. Avatar of War

Verant pulled a ninja-patch with little warning during AoW causing much frustration among those of us who had been prepping all afternoon. Luckily after the server came up Statue respawned within an hour letting us get him dead. I hope the *neato* dragon in Swamp of No Hope was worth the CS nightmare you put your GM's through.

Sunday we dropped the Burrower and he dropped very nice loot for us. We dropped a few Dains tonight and finished off with Lend in ToV. It appears there's going to be another patch. Just means more mobs tomorrow!
