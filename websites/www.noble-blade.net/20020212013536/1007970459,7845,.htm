Sunday, December 9, 2001

King Tormax shouts 'Those who raise arms against me will suffer my wrath!" -- Oh ya? Meet BLADE!! -- by Malbadon
Kael Drakkal Daily -- 6th Day of October, of the year 3218 50cp

Chaos in the City! Our King lay Dead! Vindicator assassinated! Our Statue desecrated!

Kael Drakkal, Norrath (NP) -- Our great city was brought to a standstill tonight by hated enemies of giant-kind everywhere.
The dastardly Noble Blade's, friends of our hated enemies the "Dragons" were somehow able to get past our guards and somehow manage to kill our greatest Heroes. They somehow managed to sneak up to our great Arena. "The mighty Derakor fell without raising any alarms", Kallis Stormcaller was reported as saying. Our great Statue however, did hear the commotion and came out to investigate. He fought long and hard but somehow these creatures found a way to defeat our great idol leaving nothing but broken rock and metal in the arena.
With the discovery of our statues remains, our forces quickly headed to our Arena to destroy these vermin, but they had snuck out of sight. With great horror, we learned that they made haste to our King's castle, and *obviously* caught him unaware. He fought valiantly and his shouts were heard to shake the walls of the city, but with great sadness, he was felled by these "Noble Blade".


Citizens! Be aware! These individuals were made known to use as carrying off the spoils of their evil deeds! Hunt them down! Show them NO mercy! We must have our vengeance!! Cogen carried away Derakors breastplate, Our Statues chestplate was stolen by one 'Ayame', and Groggog and Maeil stole our Scrolls of War! Tormax's loot was carried away by the filthy rangers Opafaf (gloves) and Cyriakus(Crown), Lohki the Scourge(Boots), and Seji the man-thing(Bracer). As a trophy, our Kings head was carried away by a 'Daio'. Show no mercy! Get back what is rightfully ours!!
