Wednesday, December 13, 2000

Hate Raid -- by Patro
Another fun filled afternoon in Hate. Congratulations to everyone who participated and obtained items. Of note, Rogoo (EM Helm -- Full Set), Jemit (EM Breastplate), Lamani (Rogue Breastplate). Some epic items droped as well, Hand of Maestro(Lohki), Essence of Vampire(Furyous, Malbadon). There was a most glorious charge at the end to take on 6 Ashbone Drakes! Half the people went LD from the AE spells being cast but hey it was a blast!
