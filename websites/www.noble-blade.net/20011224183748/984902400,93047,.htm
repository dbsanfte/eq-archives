Monday, March 19, 2001

Plane of Hate -- by Lohki

We had a great day in Hate. Lots of drops, lots of rots. Some of the highlights... Breastplates to Dejin, Aack(Trak BP to Faldyil), Zzakefin. Legs to Erilyn (*cries*), Yanilu, Talvar, Gleeman, Lindarie, Kevlin, Gaglain, Cervantes. Also finished off Slyth's Apothic suit.

A few new items were found. Cullers to Tyrin, Kyleus, Irontrunkiam. Hate Crystal to Shandzy. Life Guard to Byen. Crown of Hated to Jemit.

Also got a few epic quest pieces: Eye of Inny to Carden, Nekron, and Mordicai. Hand of Maestro to Fonceur and Raifin. Shattered Emerald of Corruption to TigerT. Where did we get the Shattered Emerald? Off Innoruuk of course! We dropped him like a bad habit, server first woot! GM Vaylin told us that was the second time Innoruuk was slain anywhere. Congrats guys!

Cloak of Scales - Grazskin
