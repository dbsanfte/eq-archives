Odds and ends... -- by Lohki

Wasabi! Sorry it's been a while since the last update, but we really haven't done anything that exciting. We dubbed the past two weeks or so the "Get piddly shitz out of the way" weeks. You wouldn't believe how much of the smaller epic stuff we got out of the way. Let me put it this way, we have 83 epics in the guild now, and many more down to the last item. To start off, we whacked this pitiful dragon again... Drops sucked except for the White Dragon Shield which went to Rogoo.


Few Lodizals went down, as well as a bunch of Scout Cherissa's for Bracers and Idols of Benevolence. I think we dropped Talendor once or twice. Well, I know it had to be once because I still have a Tobrins Eyepatch on me. Er nevermind, that's mine I traded for it, really! Tons of epics were completed: Gutar, Akasha, Yanilu, Kamasu, Thaurn, Rylnor, Jenora .. just to name a few (Really, I just don't remember) ..

Oh ya, and we did our first semi serious ToV run. Got a bunch of killer drops. Mainly a Unadorned Breastplate which went to Falconuts. We are currently near the end of our "Piddly Shitz" rope, so for you websight fans out there (All two of ya!) I'm sure the updates will be getting more interesting in the next few weeks.

Anyway, Back to this boring camp on my other PC. ciao
