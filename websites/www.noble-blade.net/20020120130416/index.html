Wednesday, January 9, 2002

Tormax Falls Down and Goes Boom :( -- by Cyriakus



We've had a busy couple of days. Yesterday, we dropped Keeper yeilding a BP to Stake, Belt to Avian, Mace to Wrekk and ST key to Mada. Dozekar dropped an emerald tear for Darin allowing him to complete his Buckler of Insight, a ruby tear to Jenora, a black tear to Niten allowing him to complete his Mask of the Silver Eyes and a serrated tear for vault. On our way to Statue Vindi dropped an earring for Seji. Statue dropped Swiftblades to Wrekk and Galerwen, a hammer to Devian and a reaver to Thegdr. We also managed to get Myga for Kamasu to complete his Dragonsbane quest.

Today consisted of Zlandicar who left us with a first brood talisman for Gimbor, a Fraka Talisman for Darrek, a ST key to Drakkoss, heart for Mada and GAUNTLETS OF MORTALITY!!! for vault. Then we dropped Klandicar just cause he was ulgy who dropped a ST key for Seji. The main event of the evening was definately King Tormax who dropped a Horn of Hsagra for Galhad, boots for Belgarathe, a bracer for Thaldin, gloves for Falconus and Borick recieved head. We finished the night off with a clearing of ST where Jemit got a primal brawlstick.

Grats to everybody who got goodies, has been a really fun couple of days. Thank you Verant for nightly patches and terrible lag.

Thursday, January 3, 2002

Thought Horror Overfiend Slain! -- by Cyriakus



For the first time in a while we didn't have any rotation mobs to deal with so Noble Blade did some hunting in Luclin. The main event was a truly epic battle with the Thought Horror Overfiend, it took 23 minutes to down the foul beast but we prevailed on our first attempt. He dropped an Orb of Lost Souls for Mada and several Luclin spells. We also visited the Ssra Temple where we acquired Silver Lined Sleeves for Malbadon, a Focus Crystal for Ailt and quickly dispatched Rhag Mozoezh who dropped a number of level 60 Shadow Knight and Beastlord spells.
