Sunday, April 29, 2001

Gore Score -- by Seji

... and several days ago, Noble-Blade rampaged across Norrath because, after sampling some buttered toast from Kelethin, they just could not believe it wasn't butter. First, team Blade gave Innorruk a taste test last Wednesday, and he couldn't believe it either, and flopped over dead!



He gave up HE belts to Tweekie and Camar,
DE mask to Slyth,
and an Iksar cloak to Morthik.

Wanting a second opinion, Noble Blade let Trakanon try on Thursday, and his reaction was the same.



Trak parted with a mana robe for Kamasu, and a paladin BP for Giadin.



Thinking that dragons had different tastes than gods, team Blade asked for Wuoshi's opinion on Saturday, and he said, "Oh my god! This stuff is TO DIE for!" Well, he did.



Daveian received a Scimitar of Earthdawn, and Byen, Tigert, and Kardius recevied scales.

Finally, Noble Blade trudged across the snow on Sunday to talk to Gorenaire, but the big white dragon, trying to eat lunch, suffered some food poisoning and dropped dead.



Team Blade walked away with a Runed Bolster Belt (Leugim), White Scale (Saldrin), and a frost book (Vican)

And last but not least, Noble Blade spent the evening in Chardok and stomped the royals, completing two more warrior epics for Redox and Tenil!



Congratulations to Noble Blade, a fun filled, action packed week!
