Al`Kabor! -- by Lohki

So I wake up today, and login to EQ while rubbing the sleep out of my eyes. As soon as I login, I recieve a tell from Gaglain telling me to get my cute little barb arse to GD because I'm going to be smacked around by a GM. Hell, how could I refuse that proposal?

I met up with the gang in Greater Divide. They quickly explained what was going on and that we had to run to the wurm caves to kill something to get some ore. Well we finally found some elemental looking fool and he wouldn't cough up the ore, even after I charmed him with my roleplaying wits (Gimme your ore mo fakka! [edit by Patro: uhhh no, at this point Lohki was roleplaying out the rear,"Thou Al`Kabor what is to be done with this coin m`lord"!]) so we killed him..


At this point, Al`Kabor showed up to loot the ore and hand out some rewards while making some wise cracks at Gaglain, our stinky troll and rewarded him 1500 plat.




From there he summoned Daelome, Anyen, and myself out to Western Wastes to meetup with his comrade ..


Oh and he decided to bring the stinky troll also...


From there, we beatdown another golem then Daelome turned the box into Makil Rargon...


And recieved the Scepter of Al`Kabor!



Grats!
