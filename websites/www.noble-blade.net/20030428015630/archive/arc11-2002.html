Thursday, December 5, 2002

Vyzh'dra the Cursed falls -- by Malbadon


Picture says it all. Tonight, finally, we dropped Cursed. We have been attempting this guy for a few months now, changing positioning and tactics. We made our first attempt earlier this evening, wiped, made final tunings to our strat, and waited 2 hours to make another attempt. Even the evil that is ATTnet dumping 40 of us for 10 minutes right after he spawned was not enough to deter us, and we quickly grouped, pulled, and had an amazingly smooth fight!
Congratulations Everyone!!


We haven't been idle lately, just nothing neat like a first kill of Cursed till now. An Avatar of War and Tormax, a bunch of Arch Lich's, a Creator and High Priest or two, squeezed in with the leveling and aa points we've been getting)
