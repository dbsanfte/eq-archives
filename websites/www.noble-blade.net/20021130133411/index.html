Tuesday, November 12, 2002

High Priest down! Noble Blade returns to News Updates!! -- by Malbadon


Well, its been a bit since an update, there's been tons going on in the last month, especially with the new expansion. We went and dropped High Priest tonight, it was a very orderly fight. Since we were in the prep room, we decided to take out Creator too, but then the servers crashed, so who knows what shall transpire once the servers come back up. **edit** Came back in, dropped Creator, looted, left. All is well **end**


We killed my pet dragon Xanamech on Monday thanks to the gnome finally running his script correctly and actually activating the dragon.



We haven't exactly been "idle" the past month, with multiple takedowns of Creator, Avatar of War, Arch Lich, as well as Yelinak, King Tormax, a smattering of Dain's, and a few visits to the Ancient's down in Sleepers Tomb. Combine all that with Planes of Power being released and everyone xping to 65, and you have a full month.


We also had our 3rd Vegas Gathering the weekend Planes of Power was released (/rude VI on not having a release party, we timed it perfectly :P ). So while the some of the guild was exploring and pushing to 65, the rest of us were gambling and drinking it up! Here is a picture that gives you a general idea of
how the weekend went. It's truly amazing what a great time we have each time we get together and do this.



Hopefully I will be back on track and updates will be more abundant from now on :)
