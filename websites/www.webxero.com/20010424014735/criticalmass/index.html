The Critical Mass was created in February 2000 as a guild for people who like to have fun and help each other. All races, classes and levels are welcome and there are no entrance requirements. Our experiences have shown us that most of the people that play Everquest are friendly, intelligent people, and would make excellent members of our guild. Critical Mass only has one rule: "No jackasses" - and only on a few occasions has someone been removed from the guild.

 

The birthday party was a success, so - what do we do next?
