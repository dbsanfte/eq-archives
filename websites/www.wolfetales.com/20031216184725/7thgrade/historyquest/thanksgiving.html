Thanksgiving
An American Holiday Tradition
This Web Quest is worth five points

A day of thanksgiving is celebrated in many places around the world. However, Thanksgiving Day is a uniquely American Holiday. To most of us it means gathering with our families to enjoy a wonderful meal with tempting deserts. It means Thanksgiving Day Parades and a rousing football game to watch on television while our noses are tempted with yummy smells from the kitchen. Many of us attend services where church leaders meet as one to pray for peace and plenty in our land. Thanksgiving in America is a day for families and sharing, a day for remembering our many blessings,

To learn the origin of our Thanksgiving celebration, complete this web quest.

Several presidents, including George Washington, did make one-time Thanksgiving Holidays. But no official date was set until the 1800s when the last Thursday in November was chosen to represent a National Thanksgiving Holiday.
