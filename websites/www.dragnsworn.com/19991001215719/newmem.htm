Rules

Cheating is not allowed under any circumstances. Hacking is what ruined
Diablo and we would like for Starcraft to remain playable.

Backstabbing is unwelcome. If you allie with someone than your are honor
bound to remain with that ally. Switching sides in the middle of a game is
cowardice.

Disconnectors are not welcome. This is a clan  about having fun. Winning
or losing dosen't hurt or help anyones status in the clan.

Bad and foul language is not allowed.   There are many young players in   the
clan and they don't need to be subject to that kind of language.

The tag of ~Ashaman~ or ~Sedai~ is required of all members
example: YourName~Sedai~, or Yourname~Ashaman~

The decisions of the Clan Chiefs and/or Wise Ones is final.  They are here
to try and make it fun for everyone and try and filter any problems out to
make the clan better for everyone

Have fun. That is the whole point of this clan and this game.  Members
are not graded on wins or losses. We are about having big fun games and
having a laugh or two.

To keep as accurate  a list of members as possible, every member is
required to play at least 1 game of StarCraft per week, and update
their battle.net records once per week.  You can update your record
by clicking on "record updater" in the members page.
