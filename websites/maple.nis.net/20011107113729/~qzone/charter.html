Our Rules and Creed

Purpose of the guild
To offer an aspect to the game which helps to support the members of the guild in the way that a family would.

Objectives of the guild
1. Offer training and aid to newer players.
2. Develop skilled craftspeople among the guild members to make it possible for items to be more easily acquired.
3. Develop help files and guides on playing the various characters.
4. Have a great time and enjoy good fellowship.

Membership Requirements
A perspective member must have reached his 10 level of life and must be sponsored by an existing member. Application can be made to any officer of the guild. Upon being accepted by vote of 3 officers, the new member will be inducted into the guild.

Our Creed
I will by my actions bring honor to myself and to The Order of Valhalla.
