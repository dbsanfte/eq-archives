Yes they removed the poison training ability from Stanos.

My husband went from 0-250 in under 10k so I can't imagine it costing you 20k to get those last points.

Also, keep in mind you can attempt a trophy at any skill level. Yes you will have a better chance of making a trophy on the first try if you skill up but you are still not guarenteed success. So, if all you care about is the trophy, it might be worth it just to try and fail a few times rather than spending money and endless combine time on the skill ups.

My personal opinion is to at least skill up until you max out the storebought combines (275?) and then just attempt the combine. Never know when that higher skill might come in handy and it will probably cost similar amounts in pp either way :)
