Having done the same thing recently, I have a few bits of advise for ya...

You need PoP for travel. I am still level 43, so I dont use any more than that... but instant travel makes it much easier to go try different zones looking for what will work best for you.

Luclin is nice for the Bazaar - which might be a good place to look for nicer gear... I cant hunt in Luclin zones because my computer is as outdated as my character's gear and the lag is too bad. I think Luclin is the era they introduced the new character models too, which I think are much nicer...

With Ykesha you get added bank space, a better LFG tool and the map system (which is handy)

As for places to make quick pp - there are probably many others out there more able to give you advise on that one... But I'm sticking to old world places mostly so that my gear is not so out of sync with the mobs. There's always the suggestion to farm items needed for tradeskills (leather padding, essences, etc) and sell them in the bazaar for pp too.

As a necromancer, you might have decent luck hunting Specs in Oasis.


Most specs will drop a scythe worth somewhere between 2-11 plat, along with with decent coin, and some gems.

The plat drops aren't as nice as Hill giants, and it's only a 6 spawn. But if your gear is sub par, you might have decent luck. They start going light blue at 46.

My druid quadded them from 42-46. With better undead spells, you shouldnt have a problem.

Just don't let them hit you. :-)
