Excellent AMVs to keep an eye out for:

-- Memories - "Phantom of the Opera". It's a rock/techno remix of the song, excellently set to the haunting Memories short film.

-- Cowboy Bebop "Failed Experiments in Video Editing". One girl's story of trying to create the bestest AMV ever....

-- Cowboy Bebop - Verve's "Bitter Sweet Symphony". Might not last the test of time, but is pretty cool.

-- "Everybody's Free To Watch Anime". Misc clips from everything from Muyo/Hina/Ah-Megamisama to Excel to Clamp's X to Battle Angel Alita to Eva... yeah. Mostly pre-1994 stuff. ^_^ Set to the Sunscreen Song.

-- "Flowers of Love". Series: Utena. Song: "Hit Me Baby One More Time" as redone by a male punk-rock band. As much loathing as I possess for the original female singer of that song, this AMV kicks much, much booty. (The editing = excellent.)

-- "Lostboy - RhythmAnimation". High-density in-your-face on-crack clips and music. Good for those late night papers when you need a visual pick-me-up.

-- "Lord of the Animes: Fellowship of the Animes". Someone had guts to cast Goku(?) as Frodo. Very amusing.

-- MQP - "Hair". Misc clips, extolling the virtues of Anime Hair. Fun to try to recognize all the series represented.

-- "Nadesico vs Eva". Nadesico is Eva, minus the Insane Crack. The AMV points out the visual similarities between the two series.

-- Eva: Rammstein's "Engel". For a long while, THE best amv there was. Even now, simply excellent.

-- Trigun and Cowboy Bebop "Tainted Donuts", to Shakkazombie's "Shiroi Yami no Naka". SIMPLY. FRIGGING. HILARIOUS. And the song is so addictive too. ^_^

-- Van Halen's "Right Now" with misc clips. Highly recommended, very funny. Also fun to try to name all the series in there.

-- YCSTR - "Eva-Man". Takes the theatrical trailer to Spiderman and sets Eva and LoveHina to it. One of my current favorites.
