With almost 1000 shadowscream combines, I have some experience with he Hollowshade Moor war. After a server reset, or just a zone reset (rare, but GM can do it), the war is on auto pilot. One camp will randomly attack another. Often, two attacks will be in progress at once (owlbears attacking grimlings, while sonic wolves attack the owlbears). Once one camp is taken over, the automatic attacks stop. It might be that killing a wandering named will restart the auto attacks.

Its been mentioned that killing a named makes that race launch an attack. I haven't seen this, but I don't discount it completely given how buggy the whole war is. I have seen the war in a state where killing a named caused that race to be attacked. On two occasions, I was able to flip-flip control of the south islands back and forth between owlbears and sonic wolves by killing the mob at the grimling chieften's spawn point. More often, I got there ASAP after a patch, then I just waited for the correct auto-attack to occur, and kill the defenders. I don't believe you need to kill the guards (or the named when auto-attacks are happening) in the south camp, but killing them makes killing the defenders that much easier.

If you get the zone wide messages about the attacks, then the war is on auto-pilot. If the war isn't on auto-pilot, and killing the named doesn't trigger any attacks, then the war is bugged. If the east caves, or the area betwee the east cave and the river, has zero (or nearly zero) mobs, then the war is bugged. If one mob controls the whole zone, and nothing is happening, then the war is bugged.

If by "base camp" you mean the camp where the grimling cheiften spawns, then you were in the wrong place. I've never seen any substances drop there, although other have reported then dropping. I usually farmed the largest island (not technically an island since its connected to the main land). There are about 50 mobs on that island. Drops on both substances were between 1 in 3, and 1 in 4. Dual boxing 56 shaman, and 57 warrior, I could get between 30 and 65 per hour, with an average of about 45 per hour.

On petitioning: In all the times I petitioned, and /bugged this zone, I got zone resets twice. All petitioned are answered by guides, and guides can't help you. Only a GM can reset the zone. Ask for your petition to be "escalated" to a GM. Then you get to wait for a GM to actually get online, and atually get to you.

I can't prove it, but it seemed to help if I explained to the guide I was working shadowscream quest, shar val quests, and/or smithing, but the mobs I needed were bugged. When I said I was working on the war quest, I was told they wouldn't reset the zone because reseting the zone wouldn't help the war quest work.

Good luck.
