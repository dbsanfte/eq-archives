The recipes are at www.eqtraders.com, under PoP recipes. It's not a
cultural recipe, it's a planar specific... ie all the parts (basicly) come
from a specific plane. The Earth robe, for example, has its parts from
the Plane of Earth. (No, I didn't get there personally, I just tailor for
some of the guilds that DO get to play there).

The hardest part is getting the parts... I've gone 2 for 2 so far on
Earth bits, managed the legs a day or so after the robe.
