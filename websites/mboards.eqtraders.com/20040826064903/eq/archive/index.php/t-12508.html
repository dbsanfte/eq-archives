Moraganth
Another option, which doesn't require any huge amount of factioning (although it does take a bit.. but mostly time)... Neriak. Have someone help you do the wine quest in 3rd gate till you can do it on your own... then keep doign it till you get to the level you want.

Mhb's can be made in FQ without zoning, so if you're over level 20, all you need is inner guard and merchant faction (both gained from the wine quest). Under 20 is a bit more problematic, as there is a guard on the warrior faction standing next to the vendors you need, and he paths to the brew barrel... he shouldn't agro on anyone over level 20, less you sit. Bonus is, the wine quest also gives outer guard faction.. so you can make it to neriak in one piece by doing this also.
