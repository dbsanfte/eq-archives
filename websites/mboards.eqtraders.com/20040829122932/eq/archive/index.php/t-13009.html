Things I'd like to see:

1) A decent set of mid-level, tailored caster armor - something with a little int and a few other stats, something like the new smithed armors from LDoN. (Which, by the way, are nice, but did they really have to cream the old-school cultural smithing market to do them? But I digress...)

The singular point of Mules is to offer Bank space. To this end: A Mule is almost a playable NPC. A Mule is linked to a main character, in my case, Iroke, and starting city, deity, and necessary stats are determined by that main. (Irokemuleone would start in Kelethin, worship Tunare, have the faction appropriate to an unadjusted woodelf druid, and the HP of one as well.)

(Snipping section on how to aquire Mule names, it's not the most important thing atm.)

Mules enter Norrath within right click distance of a Banker, devoid of any possessions. Mules cannot equip gear or weapons of any kind, do not need to eat or drink, and do not have that noob bag on them. Mules have the standard 8 bank slots, the 8 Loy bank slots of the account is equipped with such, and instead of the 8 standard self-inventory slots, a Mule can carry 12 bags. Mules can, of course, receive bags through Shared Bank, or purchase from vendors. Mules can hold plat and interact with NPCs and the world of Norrath in appropriate non-offensive manners (buy from vendors, be killed by kos mobs, die from falling off of Kelethin, the like). Mules cannot fight, group, or hold buffs. Mules can be TLed by a wizard but not ported by a druid. Mules cannot /duel but can be killed in Arena-style PVP.
