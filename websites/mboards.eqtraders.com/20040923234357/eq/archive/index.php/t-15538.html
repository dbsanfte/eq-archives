It's worth a trip to the boat to discover what the merchants have in stock. For example, all the components needed to make a Rebreather are available from the Tinkering merchants, except for the Fresh Fish which can be purchased from a Baking supplies merchant not too far away.

The bankers are right next door to the Tinkering merchants. This is handy when you need more cash because you fail the combine on the way-trivial Aqualung. Like I did.
