Any undiscovered recipes are likely to be alternate recipes to existing known poisons.

Part of the problem is that SoE gave us no clues for any poison recipes. Once we had found a recipe or two for all of the known poison effects, we stopped looking; it is simply more efficient for us, in terms of time, to gather ingredients for known poisons than combine random ingredients together in hope for a new recipe to reveal itself.
