We may not have the fanciest armor … or weapons … Jusiel hold’s her most treasured items … the Orc Impaler and the Raincaller Bow across her back, on her hip dangles a Golden Tunare Idol a mark of her love of pottery. Laura has always been the archer of the two rangers and the bow she wields is one given to her by Jusiel. And young Meredith a powerful druidess calling the power of the thicket to protect her loved ones for battle.

This picture is special in so many ways but for the curious I will touch on two … you will never see these three characters in game together … though family in Norrath Laura and Meredith have to share a computer so only one can be awake at a time. And after many … many seasons on the hunt Jusiel has retired to the Garden of her beloved Tunare and gives her soul to her enchanting side, Koori.

But thanks to Evan’s wonderful … wonderful talent all three stand together with a glint of mischief in their gazes ready to take on their next adventure.
