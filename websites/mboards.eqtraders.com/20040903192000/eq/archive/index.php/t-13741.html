Lilosh
Ok, we ALL have our opinions about recent EQ expansions. Mine may , at times, be a bit...err... negative.


But I've come to realize something. The Dev is darned if they do, and darned if they don't.

They , for example, make new content REQUIRE you to purchase the expansion. They are therefore called "Greedy Corporate Pigs", and all kinds of nasty language, because they are forcing you to buy the whole expansion, for the sheer purpose of you getting Ubah_Item_3, or Trade_Skill_Recipe4, or even General_Feature5. (Think expanded bank, PoK Stones, or any one of a dozen others).

Or, on the other hand.

They do NOT require you to purchase the new Expansion to use the new content. Magus Transport, Armor Dyes/New Geerloks (Finished products, mind you). They get lambasted for selling "Empty" expansions that "aren't worth buying" because all the cool stuff is "Given to you already, even if you don't".


So , while many a time do I/we boggle at the truly mindblowing decision making that Sony tosses our way, I can think of at least half a dozen more situations where the devs are going to annoy a whole bunch of people either way. And it's normally due to the simple fact that most people can't see past the tip of their nose, and all they can think of is how "SOE is SCREWING ME, MAN!!".


Please, don't argue whether any of the specific items should or should not have been given free, or included in an expansion. It's really not the point of this thread.

Just next time you're debating the merits of an expansion, please think about how many people would have been angry if the pendulum had swung the other way, and you'd gotten things your way. :-)


-Lilosh.
