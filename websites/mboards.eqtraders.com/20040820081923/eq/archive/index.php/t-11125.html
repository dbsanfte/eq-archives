Do Faydwer Shakers (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=2144&menustr=080030900000) until 188. All storebought ingredients that you can find in several cities, so no running about. Cheap.

After that you are looking at doing Minotaur Hero's Brew (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=2124&menustr=080030900000) through post-200. Again, all cheap and storebought, just a ton of clicks (9 items per combine). Most other post-200 recipes are either too expensive or too difficult to obtain ingredients for to use as a skillup path. If you got your hands on a Corking Device you could do GLM (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=3901&menustr=080030900000) past 200 since it is also cheap and storebought, but really the subcombines would probably end up making Mino Brew look a lot more appealing.
