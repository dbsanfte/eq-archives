This is not in any way saying Dex, Wis, or Int is the way to go with Tailoring, but a reply to what kind of skillups you get at lower skill level. I had a Smithing Skill of 47 and was finally fed up with losing more than 1/2 of my Acrylia after all breakdowns to make Acrylia Studs (Trivial 45), so I decided to increase my skill in Smithing. I went and bought enough Lockpick components to make 60 of them. With a Str of approx. 150, Wis of approx. 145 and an Int approx. 110, I managed to increase my Smithing skill to 77. I have yet to see how my new skill of 77 will affect my combines when making Acrylia Studs since I'm hunting Rockhoppers currently, but if it's still bad, I will increase my skill some more.

I tend to agree with the others here. I do not believe that your Wis/Int/Str/Dex is all that important when your skill is so low.
