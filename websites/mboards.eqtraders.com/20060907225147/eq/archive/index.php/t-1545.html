After 65+ combines (100k is BDs) at Tunarean Soldier Stuff, my success rate is a bit above 40 percent (worst streak was 0 in 6, good thing it was on bracers and not tunics). This is with maxed skill, so not sure where 60-65% comes from. My guess is its 50 percent and I've just been a bit unlucky.

So skill:
232= ~30% success rate
242= ~40%
252= ~50%

Stuff I've sold so far:
Tunic:2
Pants:5
Belt:2
Bracer:5

Basically calculate you costs (mostly Blue Diamond cost) and factor in your average success rate, then see if you have any chance of making a profit. If no one else on server is selling stuff you need to compare to other items (i.e Belts compare to Cobalt Drake Belt - can get about 3 times as much for a tunarean one I think, etc.).
