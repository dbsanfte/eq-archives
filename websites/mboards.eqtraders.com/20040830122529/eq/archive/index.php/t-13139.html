Lothay
In order to copy all of your customized settings (text colors, Options, which character models are on, your customized UI, etc.) there are lots of things to copy over;

all files in your EQ directory ending in .ini
the contents of your UI Files directory
all the files in the EQ Directory ending in .opt


The first time you start EQ on the new computer AFTER copying these files will ask you to reselect your video card, but after that, you should be ready to play.
