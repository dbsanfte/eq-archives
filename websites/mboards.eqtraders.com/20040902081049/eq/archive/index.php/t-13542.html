When I needed silk for Wu's tailoring, I took a trip to East Karana...

Now, I know that doesn't seem very exciteing, but here's a way to keep things interesting and expedite your collecting at the same time...

If you are of a class that can PB AoE for over 600, bring along a friend (preferably bard or ranger) to pull massive trains for you...and I mean everything that spawns in the zone aside from NPCs, giants, and cyclopses (anything with more than 600hp or might ruin either of your factions).

The funny thing about the EK zone...is that all of those mobs you just wiped out are on an instant spawn timer (or very near...maybe 30 seconds) and what spawns in place of them is pretty much random (killing a lion, wolf, spider, snake, griffawn will spawn any of those).

My friend and I collected a little less than 6 stacks each in about 2 hour's time.


Good luck. :)
