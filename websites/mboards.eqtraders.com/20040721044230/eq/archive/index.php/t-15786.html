KyrosKrane
One of the tasks I've set myself is assembling the lore text behind the paladin holy sword quests (SoulFire, the Fiery Avenger, and the Fiery Defender). When I first did the quests, they seemed really arbitrary and illogical. It turns out that most existing guides are either outdated or "get it done fast" guides that skip much of the optional material. Unfortunately, that misinformation from incorrect guides and skipped material from the correct ones serve to make the quests illogical.

So, I set out to gather all the lore text I could get for these quests. Here's what I have so far:

http://www.gamesnet.org/KyrosKrane/Paladin/

I know that many of the folks here at EQTraders are into quests, so I'm hoping some paladins here have run these quests with their logging on. I'm looking for any lore text I may be missing for these quests.

When viewed as a complete story, the three holy sword quests (the latter of which is in fact the paladin epic) suddenly make a lot more sense. I wonder how much more of this rich tapestry of background material we're missing because we just want to get the quests done as fast as possible?

Dunthor Warsmith
For the Fiery Defender -> Pure Crystal, Jark has two "random" emotes (from my logs):
Jark strikes the wall with his pick. A dull clang sounds throughout the cave. 'Arrr. Nothin' here either.'Jark strikes the wall with his pick. A clacking sound echoes through the cave. 'Har har. There's something all right.'
The latter is the source of the "something" keyword for the rest of the conversation.

Note that the second line has changed - older logs have it as (note the spelling of "all right"):Jark strikes the wall with his pick. A clacking sound echoes through the cave. 'Har har. There's something alright.'The change happened sometime between June 28, 2002 and July 17, 2003.
