Enchanters get a line of spells that increase your mana regeneration. It is sometimes referred to as the Clarity line, because Clarity was the first spell they got in it (originally; they added a lower level one called Breeze when the Kunark expansion came out).

C3 is short for Clarity 3, which isn't actually the name of the spell, it is just that the spell increases mana regen and is the next spell in the line. It is really called Koadic's Endless Intellect. Sometimes people call it KEI for short also. In addition to increasing your mana regeneration, it adds to the size of your mana pool and increase your Intelligence and Wisdom by 25.

In the time you were fighting after you got that spell, you probably didn't ever run out of mana.

As far as C3/KEI *Sane spell), it is one of the most popular spells in the game. It allows you to regain 14 mana every "tick" (Which is how the game is measured. Tick = 6 seconds). This is 14 in addition to whatever you get from Sitting down and your meditate skill (Which I believe druids get at level 8 or 9).

All of this mana regaining helps you cast alot more spells without waiting for mana to come back for a while.

Just don't turn into one of those people (*blush*) who refuses to fight without clarity or c3. They stand around in the nexus, or Plane of Knowledge, and sadly shout "Is anyone casting KEI? I will pay!!"

Nothing wrong with KEI, but do not become dependant on it.
