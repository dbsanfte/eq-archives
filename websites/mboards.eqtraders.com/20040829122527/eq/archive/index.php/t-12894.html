I added a Verto GeForce FX 5900 128 MB to my machine a couple months ago and it runs like a DREAM. It was a little higher than your price range, $200 after the $50 rebate. But it and EQ have played together flawlessly so far *knocks on wood* so you may wish to consider something similar at least. The machine it's on has 2 GB RAM and is a Pentium 4 2.4, so with that card I turned all the EQ settings on full throttle and only ever have any trouble in the Bazaar or on raids. And even then it's functional, I just turn stuff down a little to smooth it out. A vast difference from the integrated video I was using till I got around to buying a card. *shudders* I used to get lag in Halas for crying out loud. Now the only place I ever pull in the clip plane is the Bazaar.
