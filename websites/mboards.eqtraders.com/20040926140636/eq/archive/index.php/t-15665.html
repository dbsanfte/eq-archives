Just in case some weren't aware, vendors used to have only about 20 slots, anything else sold to them would still be on the vendor but not be shown.

To get to the hidden items you could buy out one of the slots, exit the vendor, and re-click on him and the next item would now show up.

Then there was a patch that simply increased his space to show everything he was holding, they added about 70 iirc.

Now, anything sold past his visible inventory simply poofs.
