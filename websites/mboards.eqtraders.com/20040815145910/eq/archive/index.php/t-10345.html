Kahmon
Iceclad Ocean for Iceclad cutlassfish. You may also want to combine 3 for the cutlassfish oil and sell it that way. The oil is LORE so be sure not to make an extra one.

Cobalt Scar for Cobalt Cod. If you have brewing skill you may even want to make them into oil and charge more that way. It also insures that you do not accidentally eat any.

Plane of Nightmare if you are level 46+ for Nightmare Chiclids (or something like that)

Plane of Valor if you are 55+ for Valorium Discuss (again, or something like that)

Ocean of Tears for saltwater seaweed. This used to be no drop, but I think I heard that was changed. I'm not human so i never cared enough to check.

Sebilis for Kunzar Koi. Under the first bridge is a nice peaceful fishing spot which happens to be where I got skill (200) just before making some Crucibles of Escape with pottery.

I'm sure there are a few other good fishing spots too, but those are the ones that came to mind for me.

Originally posted by Kahmon
Ocean of Tears for saltwater seaweed. This used to be no drop, but I think I heard that was changed.

Saltwater Seaweed used to be both no-drop and not stackable. A few months ago, it was made tradable and stackable. It can be fished in both OoT and in Timorous Deep.

Whatever you decide to fish for, PLEASE remember that the folks buying your fish (or seaweed, heh) are tradeskillers who are perpetually strapped for cash. I wince every time I see a single Saltwater Seaweed or one Mug of Sea Foam for 100pp in the bazaar. I kid you not, folks, some people in the bazaar actually expect skillers to pay 300pp just for temper components (which you can lose in a subcombine!), never mind the other 100-200pp in smithing components, for a single attempt at a skillup. This is not reasonable pricing. Heck, even Mistletoe sickles become cheaper at that point.

Another safe spot in Gunthak.

From the lighthouse island. There is a chain of rocks that connects the island to the zone wall. Run out the the far rock and fish away.

Don't foget to forage if you can. The foraged stuff out of Gunthak is worth a fair bit to the NPC merchants as well.

There is also a pleasant little ogre merchant in the lighthouse that will sell you more bait when you run out.
