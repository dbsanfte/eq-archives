Hail,

While the stats might not be as amazing as the PoP armors now in game, the Engraved Royal Velium Field Plate is also within your capability, and it is definitely trivial to a GM Smith.

While not CULTURAL in nature, it is serviceable INTERIM armor that you can make yourself, and requires no faction/religion on the part of the wearer. (The smith has to have proper Coldain faction.) It seems to sell fairly well in the bazaar.

As a Barbarian Tribunal worshipper, I recently had a Night Terror Chain Tunic made that I'm much happier with instead of the Artificer's Ringmail Tunic I could have obtained.

You may find yourself (and customers) preferring to purchase the new PoP stuff in lieu of cultural plate opportunities as well even for those with the correct religion for it.
