For chromodracs, ask a friendly bard to come along and swarm kite with them. The chromodracs make excellent pets against anything but another chromodrac (the dispell breaks charm early). A 50+ bard should also be able to solo a single chromodrac with chant kiting, or possibly fear kiting.

Teaming up with a necro is also a good possibility - you can fear kite them very effectively.
