DoING the following is what I did to get my twink up to 188 for 8th Shawl Smithing. It was a fairly cost/time effective route.

Metal Plates until trivial (save these for making banded)
Javelins until trivial (give these to monks)
Banded until trivial (vendor)
Ornate Chain (gold/platinum) until 160ish (vendor)
Fine Steel (vendor) until trivial.
