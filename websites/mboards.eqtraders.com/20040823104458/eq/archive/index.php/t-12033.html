The Quick Guide

Fire
1. Molten ore: drops off random mobs, not common but certainly not rare. You can get this off mobs at the zone in area, and some classes can solo there

2. Obsidianwood bow staff: random drop, again, not uncommon

3. Fiend guts: Can only drop from fiends (the big red demon mobs) These mobs do not spawn except for the Fennin Ro event or the named demon Blazzax. So figure a big guild takes them out and they keep what few guts they get. You might see 3-4 from a run on Fennin.

Net result: you'll easily buy two parts and be stuck on fiend guts. Odds are you'll only see completed bows for sale.

Water
1. Chunk of E'cian Ice: drops randomly from mobs deeper in, mostly slarhilug from my memory. The thing is that it makes great armor - farwater plate, so it's in demand for that more than bows.

2. Bow staff: again, random drop from slarhilug

3. Slarhilug kelp strands: same thing.

None of these pieces are rare, per se. The thing to realize here is that (at least on my server) people don't just go exp in water. It's a huge pain to fight in and nobody goes in and just happens to pick up bow parts. The ice is in demand for plate, so I guess that's the sticking point.


Earth
1. Immaculate Steel: random drop mostly from rockfiend type mobs. Easily obtained by a single group near the zone in.

2. Stonewood bow staff: same, except substitute "treants"

3. Vegerog vines: ah the rare one. This only drops from a certain type of mob - sometimes called compost heaps, or vegerogs or whatever. They look like the Construct of Nightmares at the end of the hedge maze. There aren't many of these near the zone in, and in earth the zone in is really the only place without mobs roaming close by. On the far side of the zone there are lots of vegerogs on a flat platform, but it's part of a ring event and if you pull them you could mess it up for days or longer. So people rarely mess with them, unless you're doing the ring which is in fact where most vegerog vines come from.

Air
1. Wind Metal: random drop from turkeybirds and some other mob types

2. Bow Staff: random drop from a few different mob types

3. Air arachnid silk: drops somewhat commonly from spiders. But there are a few catches. First, air is arranged in a mostly linear fashion. There are no stable camps - you get roamers pathing up and down the islands, so it's not much of an exp zone. Also, the spiders are way way way up top on the last island, so the only ones you see for a long time are the ones that roam. Did I mention that the spiders are nearly invisible, and brutal mobs that will shred people quickly? When a guild is working on the avatar of dust, then they'll get lots, but otherwise nobody goes near the place.
