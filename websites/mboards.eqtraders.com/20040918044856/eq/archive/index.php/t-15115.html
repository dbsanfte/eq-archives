baldorrex
For practice runes you do not want a froglok tadpole eye (or at least they were DNC when I made some of the practice runes for research a long time ago). As I remember, to make the practice rune you need froglok eyes that drop in Guk from live frogs. For a long time they did not drop. As a result, I skipped this particular practice rune.

At some point, there was a patch message indicating that they would drop. I believe they did drop, because I saw a few for sale on vendors. The froglok eye was blue. However, I was past the trivial at that point so I did not care too much. I also have a memory of making some of the practice runes for friends, but that was probably more than a year ago.

It is probably cheaper and certainly much easier to simply purchase your research skill from a guild master. FYI, there were several threads discussing this topic in the spell research forum.
