I make no claims to be any kind of expert on smithing, but I have been surprised by the omission of the Fishbone Dart Tool from the skill-up guides.

As a high-elf paladin I looked around for what to make after Embroidering Needles go trivial at 122 and I came across the Fishbone Dart Tool which (according to EQTC) goes trivial at 140, and therefore cuts out the early part of the Ornate/Fine Steel haul.

Uses:
4 small pieces of velium
3 coldain velium tempers
1 scaler mold

Now that the temper sells for 6pp in Thurgadin, each combine costs about 20pp, plus the velium cost. The velium can be farmed in nearby Velks or CC or power-shopped from the Thurgadin vendors. This combine seems to be comfortably cheaper/faster than the parallel options involving leather padding or ore.
Leather Padding is trading at 40pp on Xegony atm.
Rewde
Which recipie are you looking at? This is the actual recipie for the fishbone dart tool, and trivial, unless it has been changed since being implemented:

Smithing Components: coldain velium temper, scaler mold, velium bits(2)
In: forge
Yield: 1
Trivial at: 146


And yes, it is a different skill up route that is now cheaper due to the lowered cost of the tempers.

According to Lucy, it is not lore, not stackable, and only has a value of 1sp 2cp. So, this is going to be similar to the EVB on which you are just throwing money at smithing. At least with the ornate chain and LDoN chain you will make some of the money spent back. But, lacking raw data on skill ups, it will be a little hard to say whether it would be a more viable skill up route.
