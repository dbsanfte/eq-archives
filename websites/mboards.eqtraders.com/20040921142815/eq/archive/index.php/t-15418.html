I think tradeskill items should be higher than normal items on average. The whole point of the tribute system is to make it so items get sucked out of the game. It only makes sense that the items that are more proliferous in the game be the ones worth more tribute as they need to be sucked out the most.

Raising the tribute on certain items might also increase the value of them. If you're hard strapped for needing tribute points, you'd want to buy the items that gave the most. Eventually, these items would become rarer and their price would rise somewhat.
