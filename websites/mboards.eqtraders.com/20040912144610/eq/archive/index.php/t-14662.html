Waterlogged Riggings drop from the zombies on the Guntak beach.

The Quills drop from some of the skeletons on that beach. Moreover, c the merchants in Gunthak usually have them: as I was working my tinkering, I ended up finding all the quills I needed on the vendors, though once that trivialed, I did farm some riggings myself.

If you frequent Gunthak, you can often make offers to the people XPing on the beach and get instantly outsourced farming.
