Taushar
I can make a couple of suggestions on items you can use. First is shield of baning warding. It's a quested item but it is droppable. It provides 20 wisdom on the secondary slot. I even had LDoN +6 wisdom augmentation in there for additional wisdom just for tradeskills. Also, check out Glittering Ring of Judgement, player made ring from PoJustice dropped items. They have 20 wisdom and not lore so you can have two. Again, you can augment with two, +6 wisdom items. If you have the shield, two rings and augmentation, you can gain 78 wisdom in 3 slots. Not too bad.

Another item for your neck, don't know what you have, for cheap is Velium Polished Quartz Necklace made by players from LDoN drop. It has 8 wisdom on neck slot. If you don't have a trophy, you can skill up fairly cheap on brewing and get the cork. It adds 10 wisdom in range slot. If you don't already have, you can get earring of solstice, it adds 8 wisdom on an ear slot.

Of course, this assumes that you have lots of pps to buy them from others.

I was in the same boat and didn't want to spend the cash for things like Glittering Rings and a Shield of Bane Warding.

Well, technically, you don't have to spend money on either items. Shield of Bane Warding can be had for a quest and nothing I remember cost money for the quest. You just have to spend time and help from a few friends. I admit that it is a long quest but it's very rewarding.

As for the Glittering Ring, I made both of them myself after farming the items I needed from PoJustice. Again, I don't remember requiring anything I have to buy for the components other than what is dropped. If your JC skill isn't high enough, you can ask a friend who is or you may have to pay someone to combine for you.

Again, the tradeskill is about tradeoffs, farming versus spending pps. In this case, I chose to farm rather than spending pps.
