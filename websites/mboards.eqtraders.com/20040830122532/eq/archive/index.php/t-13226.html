When: 9pm pst - ask on /join Guardians chat.

When I was first invited into Universal Guardians (http://legends.everquest.station.sony.com/guild.jsp?gid=65), I wanted to share my passion of being a craftsman, more than obtaining the highest level a priest could obtain. Sony themselves realized it was more than a passing fad and after thought to put in the mechanics for tradeskills, but they eventually needed to embrace the potential which many sought as an alternative path to constantly killing for experience alone.

Recall that each expansion since Norrath came to be has offered new challenges and discoveries for all of us.
There is an entire community about who have taken the time to while away hours sitting in front of blazing forges and flaming ovens to futher their desire to be the best Blacksmiths and Bakers

As a guild leader today, I truly want to mentor those interested to try, that they too can succeed too.

The best place to gather that I have found to be neutral to all races is Luclin's city of Shadowhaven. Merchants will sell what you will need to buy, given you have sufficent faction with them and when not Commissioner Henry has a few errands that certainly helps you to be a more trusted visitor. Note that the stouts have their own quarter to transact business and those who are around the Nexus portal are much more tolerant to newcomers while the rest can be won over with a few quests for faction gains.

Can we start early? Sure you can, but no need to wait for me to spawn.
I want to learn, but don't know what I need? We will be happy to show you were to start.
