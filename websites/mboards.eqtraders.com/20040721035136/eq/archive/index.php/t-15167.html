Normbow
Since I started my run at ribbons, these Steamfont and Nedaria's Landing have been my two favorite spots for foraging.

Steamfont is good because, as mentioned before, you can also get the Water from killing Kobolds. My experience is that I get roughly an equal amount from each activity. The only hassle is if there are newbies in the area trying to get exp. Not nice to go kill all their mobs.

Nedaria's Landing is great if you also want to get pelts. The forage rate of plant shoots is much lower (but ultimately, I'll just need them for 183-187). Also, along one wall there's a cave with 8 Cavern Grizzly/Magnificent Grizzly spawns. By the time I've killed the last spawn, the first one has respawned. I recently recorded the results from roughly three times through the cave.

In 31 minutes, I killed 31 grizzlies and got 7 Low, 5 Medium and 5 High Quality Bear pelts. There are also roaming Majestic Grizzlies that drop pelts
maedie
Lesser Faydark is also a good zone. You can forage both Morning Dew and Sylvan Berries there. The Steamfont entrance area seems to be a safe spot to park a forager. I've sat there for a couple hours and gotten equal numbers of Dews and Sylvan berries. (a stack of each every few hours.)

Morning Dew is used for cultural smithing, tailoring and fletching, thus sells fairly well in the bazaar. They sell at least as well as ribbon forages, so you can go to bazaar or auction and "trade" them for more ribbon dye by selling the dews. :)
