If you've never camped Dervish Cutthroats in North Ro or WC, then go check out Murissa Sandwhisper (vendor in the dirt if you're coming from East Freeport) and buy any spiderling silks she has. Grab any LQ, MQ or HQ cat pelts the vendors have, too. You can resell them for decent profit, as well. Don't bother making Leather Padding, unless you're doing them to raise Tailoring skill. You're better off just selling the silks and pelts as is.

(If you have camped Dervs and she cons Dubious, then use an alt or pay someone in the zone to buy from Murissa. She's on Derv faction.)

North Ro is great for farming spiderling silks. I'm KoS in the area, unfortunately (killed the WC tollbooth guards a lot back around lvl 28 ), so I use a Ranger alt to do it. (The Tracking comes in handy)

Don't bother farming Spider Silks, unless you want to use them to train up Tailoring. They don't sell.

Download a map from EQAtlas, get KEI and head up there, then ask if anyone is camping Tribals over near ME or GE. If they are, ask if you can join in. (If you can't get in a group, leave the zone. I didn't try soloing at all there at all until lvl 33 or 34)

And make sure you're invisible while you go to join them. You'll run into a lot of mobs that will aggro on you and will kill you fast if you do not.

Use the Earth pet if the party doesn't have at least three tanks, and the Air pet if they do (some Tribals cast, and the Air pet's stun will come in handy for stopping that).

You might be able to hunt Tribals over at Grieg's End with just 3 people. If no groups are up, try to form your own. Look for a Monk to be the main puller, since Tribals will help each other if attacked and their are Lightcrawlers there. I wouldn't risk it at ME, though. (The Tribals seem to be closer together there)

Tribals drop crap weapons (except for Polished Steel Ulaks), but sometimes they drop Acrylia, which you can sell for decent plat. I spent about 2 or 3 hours one night killing Tribals over at the entrance to Grieg's End with a Monk from my guild, and let the Monk sell the Acrylia in the Bazaar (I can't stay on all day or all night as a trader, and he can), splitting the plat from the sales 50/50.
