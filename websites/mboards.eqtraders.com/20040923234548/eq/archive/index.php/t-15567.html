Something to bear in mind is the relationship between Triv level and skill up which I believe has been documented elsewhere in these threads. Out of my last 90 solstice robe combines at a skill of 193 I got 1 skill up (Triv 335) I then did 20 Excelletn Sabertooth (Triv 216) and got 1 skill up. After this I did 67 reinforced acrylia (Triv 242) and got 6 skill ups. In total I have done several hundred CSR and am lucky if I get 1 in 50-60 combines. I was able to see this effect more clearly when I ddi baking. At a skill of around 205 I tried MTP (335) and had a horrible skillup rate, I switched to Hales Pies (226) and the skillups came flying in. I did a few more MTP at 226 and rate was still poor so switched to GoD souffles (252) and the skillup rate improved again.


Sadly I think arctic wyvern and Tigeraptor are both 335 triv combines so same as CSR which probably means cultural and acrylia reinforced are better options if you can get the bits you need. Also any other sub 250 stuff you can lay your hands on.
