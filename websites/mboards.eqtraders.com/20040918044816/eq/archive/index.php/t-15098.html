The ">" symbol means "Greater Than" in mathematics.

So, when you saw a trivial listed as being >250, this meant we knew the trivial was greater than 250.

This occurred when a person with a skill of 250 would make the combination and not receive a message stating that the combination was trivial at that person's skill level.

There is a FAQ on the use of the > and < symbols in the Frequently Asked Questions (http://mboards.eqtraders.com/upload/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=12) forum.
