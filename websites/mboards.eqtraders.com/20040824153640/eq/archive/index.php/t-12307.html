A few things to check:

Did you use a quill or your research geerlock? (been there, done that., it’s why I ask ;p )

For the swatch : Did you use the “Sorcerer’s Lexicon” obtained in Gunthak ? Did you use Salil’s Writ Pg 90 right?

For the Magi (Azia) Practice Rune, you need to use “Brownie Parts” (the ones that are used in Baking to make chocolate, NOT torso, head or leg.)

For Master (Azia) Practice Rune, that’s supposed to be the correct recipe, if it doesn’t work, /bug it in the game.

Finally, once you have a pair of Practice Runes, you have to combine them in your Sorceror's Lexicon, NOT in the Concordance of Research...the Concordance is only used to created the Practice Runes.


Good luck :)
