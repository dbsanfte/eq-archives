As far as my recommendation for increasing a stat... I'd recommend utilizing the melee buff related to the skill, and making a tradeskill suit to fit that.
In my ignorance, I made a suit that maxed out strength, spending MAYBE (probably much less than) 1k in the bazaar on items that had lots of str, but not much else. I don't think it'd be hard to find dex items the same way... Most melees go for AC, strength and stamina primarily, then dex as a secondary if necessary. Buying items with low AC/STR/STAM but lots of dex should be fairly cheap... and these, combined with a shammy buff should max you out easily.

On the other hand, many casters tend towards ignoring all stats but their mana affecting ones (at least until they've maxed out wis), so int and wis gear may cost a little more for the same increase. Hope this helps.
