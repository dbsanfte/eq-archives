Hollowshade is a zone that has an 'permanent' War going on it it. Between the 3 factions Owlbear, Wolf, and Grimlings. Now first thing that needs to be mentioned is that this war is --- VERY BUGGED

What has happened in your case is that the Wolfs have attacked and won a battle against the Owlbears. Thus the owlbear cave is now populated by wolfs.

TO end the war the entire zone must be taken over by one faction, including the Vah-SHir outpost. Once this has happened, a Named will spawn in the outpost. WHen this named is killed, the zone is reset.

I have personally killed the final Grimling and Owlbear.

NOTE: the nameds corpse lasts for like 10 seconds even if it has loot, so get those items quick. They are NODROP too.

Off the grimling i got some Mask and a 1HB (skull on a stick) both were pretty crap.
From the Owlbear i got a SWEET range item (see my magelo click my sig name) which i often get asked about as i dont think many people at all have got.

NOTE: I do not know how to control the war, how to help any of the factions or activate/trigger the battles.

I have only killed the named leaders by being lucky to be in the zone at the time.
