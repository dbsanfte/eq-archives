Steps for making GoD baking recipies
In the following, XX can be any of the 4 GoD meats

1) Break down >1lb XXmeats into 1lb XX meats.
Put >1lb XX meats into a mixing bowl, alone, and hit combine. Graphics doesn't change.

2) Convert "1lb XX meat" to just "XX meat"
Put "1lb XX meat" into a mixing bowl, alone, and hit combine.
Graphics will change to match that of a "chunk of meat"

3) Make the sauce of your choice in the brew barrel

4) Combine "XX meat" and sauce
Back to the mixing bowl. put 1 "XX meat" and 1 sauce in the mixing bowl, hit combine. You will get something like "Barbequed Cragbeast Meat"

5) Final combine.
Finally, to the grill. Put the sauced XX meat of your choice, plus additional ingrediants as needed into an oven or spit, and cross your fingers.
