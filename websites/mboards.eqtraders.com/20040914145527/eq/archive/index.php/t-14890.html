I read where now "temp" spell will give you max strength, so thats a no brainer.

Temp (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=19116) does not add to your STR. It ups your HP and AC. Spells like Fo7 (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16724) and HoS (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=9843) will increase it. There are tons of STR items out there, so it's not terribly difficult to raise up. I have a special set of gear just for smithing, which takes me to 245 with my ranger STR buff.
