Just so you know, at 65, with even the slightest care, you can easily hunt wyverns in CS while hated and detested by CoV.

Heck, to be real, I hunted CS for wyverns and drakes for exp in my low 50s with max KoS to CoV.

If you really do plan to do old school quests in Skyshrine, then by all means, keep your CoV faction.

Since you're a porter, you might find Skyshrine especially convenient. You can port right into WL, run over, killing any panthers you see on the way. Take out the tigerraptors near the entrance. I almost never see anyone else in Skyshrine when I'm there; when I do see someone, they're most often doing a quest, so I just make sure not to interfere with the quest mob they need.
