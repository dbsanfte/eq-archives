thrashette
my biggest source of BDs is and remains PoT and PoK vendors after a patch or server going down for some other reason

Here's the deal. People are continually selling BDs, but only at first server up are they visible.

Vendors keep the first items they are sold, and drop off new items. Some of the first items are always blue diamonds, so you see them to buy.

After your server has been up 2 days, vendor inventories is full. When you buy all the blue diamonds off a vendor, it creates a void that gets filled by the next thing that is sold. All future blue diamonds sold to that vendor go into poofland.

So after a few people vendor mine, no new blue diamonds will appear until a patch.
