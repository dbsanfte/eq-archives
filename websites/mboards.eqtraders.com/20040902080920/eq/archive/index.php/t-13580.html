Nex Alacer wrote:

"Is there a map like that for Misty Acorn ground spawns?"

There isn't really a map, but there is a secret, that I'll share with you since you know the super secret "true tradeskiller's handshake". If you look at a map of the Misty Thicket, you'll see that it's split east and west by the Wall. Draw a second imaginary line across the middle to split it north and south as well, and you'll find that there are four ground spawns, one in each of the four quadrants. That said, I find acorns more often near the big hill just south of the PoK book in the southeast quadrant, and among the goblin camps south of the Runnyeye entrance in the southwest quadrant. For the northern ones, I haven't noticed any particular place that's better than any other, so you'll need to canvass, but at least if you find a spawn, you can skip the rest of whatever quadrant you're currently in.
