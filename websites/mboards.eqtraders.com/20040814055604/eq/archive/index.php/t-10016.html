Perhaps this is mentioned on one of the pottery guides somewhere, but I found it quite helpful...when getting into the 222 range (where they trivial), Green Ceramic Bands are *extremely* cost effective.

Only non store-bought ingrediant are the vials of cloudy mana, and significantly unfired green ceramic bands sell to NPCs for 7p6g, which is a recoup of about 1/3rd the cost of the other ingrediants. Curiously, if you fire them, the NPC sell price drops down to 1s1c, so they are actually better unfired.
