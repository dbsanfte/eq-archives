I've been keeping logs when they're relyable during my farming of the south islands of Hollowshade and the shadow island in Twilight. The numbers should be acurratly reflect the drop rates in these two places. They are an average over all the mobs in those areas, I AoE every stinkin' owlbear an' wolf and keep the shadow island pretty clear (**** I love sense undead). Possible errors come from the mob being too far away from me when it died (DoT) causing the kill message to not show up or me missing some corpses (lost in a tree in Twilight or in some hut in Hollowshade). Here's what I've found:

2250 wolves killed, 503 substance. 4.473161 ratio
845 shadows killed, 506 swirling. 1.669960 ratio
1837 owlbears killed, 411 wailing. 4.469586 ratio
And as a complete side-note:
48 perfect owlbear pelts. 38.270833 ratio

So I'd guess 1 substance per 4.5 wolves and 1 swirling per 5/3 shadows.
