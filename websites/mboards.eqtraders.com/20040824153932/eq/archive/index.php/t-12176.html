Brildary
It's true the ingredients for Faydwer Shakers are easy to get, and there are other recipes whose ingredients are also pretty available...but make no mistake, it takes hours to skill up. I've posted some of this in the Primal Scream Room, but just to let up- and-coming brewers know, it took me 10 hours of clicking to skill up 46 points on Shakers. This isn't a complicated recipe, no earlier subcombines, no combinations of bits and pieces from some other brewing run, this was just straight ahead combining of existing, easily purchased cultural alchohols. And it still takes 10 hours! I've made inquiries, and this is a fairly common length of time...nothing high level about it, down to earth brewing. So don't be lulled into thinking it can be knocked out in an afternoon after school. You won't do this and get your chemisty homework done on time.
