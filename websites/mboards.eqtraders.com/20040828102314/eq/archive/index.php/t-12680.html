Astroshak
Quite honestly, I do not know why the best diety bows would be so low on a BotB list. 33? Sheesh. That BotB list probably only looked at DPS and stat point totals; something like a 30 38 bow with 200 int would be ranked fairly high simply because of the amount of int . . . and if that was the only stat, it'd be a VERY poor ranger bow.

For a ranger, there's a very short list of bows that I'd recommend people go for.

It starts with the diety bows. BFT/ETCB, depending on diety. This should really be the first stat bow, though the lesser diety bows are worthwhile if you're a new player. Aim for the BFT (or Karana thing) however; this is the bow that'll last you quite some time.

Once you get EQ and some level of AM, get a Twisted Longbow. Don't bother with a nightmare, its not that good from a rangerly perspective.

Once you've got EQ and AM3, start looking for a bow better than a Twisted. Considering current market prices, you might have to loot one . . . typically, it is at this point that you'll start looking for elemental bows, though the bone carved short bow (38 40 IIRC) is a decent intermediary.

Once you have an elemental bow, you've got essentially one of the best bows in the game. Last BotB list I looked at showed them all very near the top, in terms of DPS.
