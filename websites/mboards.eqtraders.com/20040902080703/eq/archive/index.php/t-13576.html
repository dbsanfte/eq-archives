Q: How good can I get at a tradeskill?

A: Of the 7 main tradeskills (baking, brewing, fletching, jeweling, pottery, smithing, tailoring) you may only have one up to 250, while the rest are capped at 200. Regardless of whether another skill is above 200 or not, other specialized tradeskills are as follows: Alchemy 130 before level 51/180 at 51+; Poison Making 200 pre51/250 post51; Tinkering 250, fishing 200.
