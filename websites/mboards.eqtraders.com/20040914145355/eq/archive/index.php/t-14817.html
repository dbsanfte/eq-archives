One time that being a dwarf really helps as the main components are right there for me. The hardest part of this brew is acquiring the corking device.


To max your skill from 135 to 250: I say spend the entire time in and around Kaladim. All the Hero's parts are sold in South Kaladim or the vendor houses just outside Kaladim in BB. The only reason to leave is to gather the few extra parts for the Constitutional (soda, cork, corking device, yarrow, CE's). Low numbers in Kaladim make lag and zoning almost invisible. There is only 1 barrel, though, and if it's camped you may have a wait.
