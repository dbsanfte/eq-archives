So far I’ve only looked over your brewing guide. I started with brewing because it’s the only skill I’ve taken to 250 thus far, and it’s likely to be the least complicated of all the guides. Just about every guide and post I’ve read has had slight variations on what’s the best path to take. For example, I made skull ale and never made Ol' Tujim's Fierce Brew. But overall I fallowed the same path as you suggest in your guide. The only point I’d argue with is your suggestion for the last two skill points.

Champagne is expensive to make and requires the services of an enchanter and a high level jeweler. I would highly suggest making Kaladim Constitutionals instead. You need to buy a corking device, which can be rather costly, but its something you’ll use over and over again if you wish to also make many of the newer drinks, including Grobb Liquidised Meat. But the nice thing about the constitutionals is that you aren’t dependent upon anyone else for your combines.

The only technical problem I noticed with your guide is some lines of text are cut off due to the width of the columns. Line 18 is a good example: “Exception to rule 2 ingredients elven wine and dwarven ale cannot be” is all I read without selecting the field.

Thanks for putting these guides together. I realize they take a lot of work and everyone trying to advance in a trade skill can use all the help they can get. At least I know I do.
