As yet, EQ Traders does not have an offical policy on .sig file size. Please do be considereate of others when constructing your .sig.

Some guidelines are in the FAQ on this topic, and I'm quoting them here.

Rules for using Signatures

Don't over-use your signature file. If the signature is bigger than your post, don't use it.

Keep your signature file small. Some people, believe it or not, still pay for internet information by the amount of bits sent/recieved, and some pay for things by time used. Not everyone has flat-rate high-speed connectivity. Be considerate. No one wants to pay to see your signature a zillion times.

Keep the signature in good taste, or at least self-moderate. If you would feel uncomfortable explaining your signature to your mother, or a small child, then it doesn't belong in something you will attach to all your posts.
