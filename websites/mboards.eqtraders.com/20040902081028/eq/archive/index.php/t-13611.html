It depends.

I have a love / hate relationship with Hobble (my lizard mount)

The mana regen and "no sit agro" are the PRIMARY reasons I bought the lizard. I have JBoots for speedy travel. (JBoots are faster than the slowest horses. Hence "Hobble" the lizard.)

If you want one strictly for fashion, then race takes a part in the discussion. Evil races and frogs look better on lizards. (I'm a dark elf, so I bought a black lizzy.)

Light races look better in general on horses. But a gnome on a huge horse might look pretty silly.

As other's have pointed out the head DOES get in the way for horses about 100 times more often than for lizards. (if you are facing UP a very steep slope even the lizard head gets in the way)

If you are LOOTING the horse and lizard BOTH get in the way. It takes a little practice to get used to how to loot from the top of a mount. (in most of my groups either the cleric wouldn't deign to loot or INSISTS on ML'ing to cover peridot costs... there seems to be no middle ground)

Targeting with F-keys and /assist are the normal way for people who aren't pullers or enchanters. (please do NOT /assist the enchanter until s/he tells you to... *spits out several feet of small intestine he apparently no longer needs*) I personally have no problem targeting and casting from the mount, even as an enchanter.

I -do- know a fellow enchanter who chose a horse cause it LOOKED better for her High Elven Female Enchanter. She repeatedly uses a minor "trick" to get the horse to disappear while she's in combat situations. I believe they have FIXED this trick, and even if they haven't I won't publish it here. (She's still got the buff, but the horse doesn't appear on her screen.)

Best bet. Let her see what each one looks like. Some people, unaccountably, like the brown horse better then the black or white one. Ditto the red lizard instead of the black.
