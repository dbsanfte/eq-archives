Heck yes switch to MTP's!

Veggies, Fruit, Brownie Parts, and eggs are the only parts not purchaseable in Shadow Haven (where I did all my MTP combines).

Veggies and Fruit can be bought in the bazaar, often for less than 1pp each. Same for Eggs. you only need 1 egg per 100 MTP combines, which means I basically used 1 stack of snake eggs to get to 250. *shrug*

Brownie parts can be problematic, mostly because of the OBSCENE price gougers in the bazaar! :steamed: On Xegony, they're all up for 50PP each in the bazaar! WTH? That's why I ported to steamfont to kill my own. If they had been reasonable in the bazaar, I woulda just bought em. Same as with eggs, you need just 1 brownie part per 100 MTP combines.

I literally prepped 200 combines in less than an hour, and cranked out those 200 combines within the duration of a single cast of brilliance. (Used to get me to 255 WIS) 45 minutes I think it lasts. /scratches head

My skill up rate was pretty average, but that's with 255 WIS. Lower WIS = slower skillups.

I'd strongly recommend MTP's for anyone skilling up past 200 in baking. Despite the subcombines, it goes very quickly, due to the high yeild of said subcombines. :)

Balkin
There's a "Haven Amulet" quest in SH.

It's in 2 parts. One is to run something to the Bazaar from Commisioner Henry and return with a piece. Then he sends you to the outpost in Fungus Grove to give something to someone out there, and bring something back to Henry. Takes about 10 minutes, and gives you a 2 CHA +faction necklace for SH. Everyone will sell to you if you wear the necklace. :)

Good luck!
