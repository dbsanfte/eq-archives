Pootle
Straightening up, her back groaning as she does... wiping her hands on a nearby rag she looks at her work. At her feet lay hundreds of unfired idols....

'Such a waste' she mutters to herself, 'all this work cast aside'

She had not been fireing the idols, far too much trouble to bother with, and she had no idea what she would do with them all if she did. She looked at them again, ugly little idols covered the floor. Deep down she was glad her work was going to be destroyed, it had felt very wrong making idols representing the god Cazic Thule, but as a tradeskiller she sometimes had to make things that she didnt want to, just to futher her skill.

At least the last batch had been a pleasure to make, idols of Tunare, she didnt mind making them. She often visited the Plane of Growth to walk amongst its trees and pools. She had even visited Tunare a few times, who was very nice, despite being an elf!

A shiver broke her out of her daydreem, she loved the city of Thurgadin, so quiet, nobody bothered her when she was tradeskilling. But it was always so Cold!

Packing away her tools and supplies, she dropped some components into the bank and sat to memorise Circle of Misty!

'Home for a beer and a smoke' she tought, 'Perfect'
