If you are interested in a better list, go sit in the bazaar for a couple hours and shop. Check prices... See what sells and what dont... You will soon find out what you can supply and what you cant. Buy some trader satchels and load them with supplies... Price your items at or near the lowest of those already for sell (Never Undercut Current Traders ... Extremely Bad Manners ... Your Karma will be completely nerfed and you will anger the Trader Gods!!!)

When you go to work or to sleep, just click off your monitor and leave your trader up. Hope for a nice surprise when you return.
