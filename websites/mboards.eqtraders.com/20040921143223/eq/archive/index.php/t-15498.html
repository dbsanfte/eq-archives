Since it has been (roughly) misstated twice in this thread, stat caps:

255 - up to level 60
+5 - each level over 60
+5 - each level of planar power (2 aa each level - one rank can be purchased per level over 60)
+10 - each level of Innate enlightenment (3 aa each level - one rank can be purchased per level over 60 - priests and int casters only)

This gives caps as follows:
level 61:
260 (default)
265 (1 level of planar power - cost 2AA)
275 (1 level of planar power and innate enlightenment - cost 5 AA)

level 65:
280 (default)
305 (5 levels of planar power - cost 10AA)
355 (5 level of planar power and innate enlightenment - cost 25 AA)

Of course, you also need to get the gear to get your stats capped once you buy the AA.

I think you've gotten some excellent suggestions here so far, especially

1) the Enchanted velium bits - Trivial 222

2 small pieces of velium + 1 Coldain Velium Temper + 1 spell: enchant velium = lore, no drop enchanted velium bit.

You can buy CVT and the spell Enchant Velium in the same shop in Thurgadin. If you're KoS, make a mule, have a friend escort it to Thurg, and use the shared bank slots.

I would add this option, once you get to 220 and have the necessary flags.

2) Aid Grimel - Breastplate Cleansing - Trivial 227

You need to have 220 skill, Bertoxxulous AND rescuing Mylik and Adier done in order to use this as a skill up path. If you do this, you can do the cleansing step, and not the whole thing, and do it repeatedly.

You do the quest part way, and then redo it again. It involves running, but is relatively cheap, and perhaps less irritating than pharming.

Start by telling Aid Grimel, "I am well versed in the art of smithing." He'll ask you to cleans a filthy bp he gives you and more. You only need to worry about that step.

Brew: 3 unstacked Celestial Essence + Jar of Acid = Acid Wash (stackable, not lore)

Combine Adic Wash and the Filthy BP he gives you in a planar forge. Rinse and repeat.

3) After 227, you have the options others have already helpfully discussed.

Good luck!
