200-222: Opal encrusted steins, roughly 24.5 stacks of combines or 490 attempts.
222-233: Idols of Tunare, 20 stacks or 400 attempts.
233-237: More Idols of Tunare, 10 stacks or 200 attempts.
237-239: More Idols of Tunare, 10 stacks or 200 attempts.

I think that works out to about 1 in every 37.3 clicks for skilling up.

At 242 I decided to screw the cheap way and dig into my bank account to finish off with Star Ruby Encrusted steins...patience is not my best quality :)
