You are already at the cap for level 29. The formula for the cap on alchemy skill is:

(Your character level minus 24) times 5

In your case that's 25. You need more levels before you can get more skill.

As to your second question on making the potions affordable, you need to get your skill level higher so that your success rate is higher. At a 95% success rate it costs something like 55pp to make a 10 dose blood of wolf.
