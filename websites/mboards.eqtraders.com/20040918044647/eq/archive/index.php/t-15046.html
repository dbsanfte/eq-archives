I woke up this morning and saw blue sky for the third day in a row. This made me so happy, that I sat down and wrote this.


I was also procrastinating on a Japanese essay and an English Poetry test. Also, in the past couple of weeks, we've gone through lots of Shakespeare in two classes (in one, I got to be crazy Ophelia from 4.v; I love playing that scene).

So, enjoy. Or run away in phear, I don't know which. ^_^ Maybe this will make someone's day a little brighter too.







------------------------------------

Ah, March, how do I love thee? Let me count the ways:
First thou art not February with cloudy ashen days.
Secondly, not January, false snow and fickle sun;
Thirdly, not December with his grueling Finals run.
Lastly, not November, al'ys the Month of Insanity,
Teachers want their papers, fifteen pages, hand in early.
Add to this, the Chain of Life, wrapped around my feet,
Seeming always wanting me to trip, then me to beat.
Seas'nal Affectation, eighteen days of straight no light
Is enough to cause my sunny self to give in to my plight.
The Four of Darkest Winter always darkly stalk my dorm room door;
But cometh March with Sun and Wind, commanding them, "Begone! No more."
E'en if another cold comes, tis curt, they're on their way,
My March will bring me flowers and more temperate sunny days.

-- Sanna
March 12, 2004
