I figured it out...

EQTC says multielement glaze + unfired medium container + quality firing sheet (2)... in a tanaan wheel. This is WRONG.

Correct recipe is: multielement glaze + unfired medium container + high quality firing sheet (2)... in a tanaan kiln.

It was trivial at 188, btw.
