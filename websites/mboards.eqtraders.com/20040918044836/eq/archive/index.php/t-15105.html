Azirux
As per the FAQ on this site:

Q: What stats are used in what skills?

A: For ALL tradeskills, the greater of your Wisdom or Intelligence will determine your skill-up rate. In Smithing, Strength also affects this rate, while Dexterity affects Fletching. NO stat changes your rate of success, as far as we can tell. (See the Stats and Tradeskills FAQ (http://mboards.eqtraders.com/upload/showthread.php?s=&threadid=19) for further details.)
