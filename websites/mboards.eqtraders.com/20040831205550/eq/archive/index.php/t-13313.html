caena
If you need that main slot, you can destroy the collapsible container.

Then, when you need a Tackle Box again, click on the collapsed, and voila!

The advantage of the collapsed versions is that you don't need multiple tradeskill containers. I have a collapsed M&P, spit, and mixing bowl in a pack, seven weight reduction bags of various types, and one open main slot, which has whatever uncollapsed container I need at that point. Without the collapsibles, I'd have three slots taken up with non-WR bags with 4-6 slots.
