No, only your highest skill modifier makes any difference on your skill, or in other words, modifiers do not stack. The only way to get 10 or 15% is with an item that has that modifier (such as the 10% smithing belt or the 15% smithing gauntlets). To my knowledge, no such item for jewelcraft currently exists in game.

Besides, the maximum *modified* skill of any tradeskill is now 252.
