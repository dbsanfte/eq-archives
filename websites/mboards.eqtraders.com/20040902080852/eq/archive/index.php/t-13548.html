If you're looking for big money fletching isnt it. Rangers buy most of the high end arrows and with endless quiver they buy one or 2 of each. The PoP bows fetch a price fo roughly twice the cost of materials. Since there is a (roughly) 50% chance of success on them you just about break even. The next expansion (GoD) may change all that but right now fletching isn't a very profittable trade skill.

How to make money fletching... ahh thats tough

There is a half decent market for arrows on the rathe, however the only ones that sell relialby and steadaly are the various substance types and/or long range silver tips. While endless quiver reduces the end game requirement for these, it does take a whack of AA points to get. Casual players still need arrows. Trick is don't overprice, make a reasonable profit and they will sell faster than you can make them...usually. Another good money maker for me has been Nightmare bows, but if i cannot get my price, i don't sell them. So you have to willing to sit for a while sometimes. No quick way..
