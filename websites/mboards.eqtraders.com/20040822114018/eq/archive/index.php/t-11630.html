I just got my trophy with a skill of 234. Feel free to critique my reasoning. I figured that the closer I got to skill cap the less it will cost on final combine, but the more it will cost for skillups. At some point there could be a break even point. From reading these boards I got the impression that at full skill (240+geerlok) there is a 50% chance of success. I think that is my biggest assumption but it is reasonable. If the success formula is valid then you lose 1% chance of success for every skillpoint lower than that. My cost for components on trophy combine is 3704p. Expected cost for total trophy combines is 3704/chance_of_success. Doing this as a table I could see that on every skillup I would save money but the higher I got the less I would save on each skillup. On the other hand the cost for skillups in the platinum range is a flat 106*36.7/20=195pp per skillup, since a combine costs 106, it takes average 36.7 combines per skillup (measured this), and failure rate is 5%. I found that above skill 234 I would spend more money on skillups than I could expect to save. It took 2 tries for trophy. L62 bard with 255 wis.
