Progress Report for 8-Jan-2004

I downloaded the beta patcher zip file yesterday evening after I got home from work. I unzipped the file into my EQ directory and started the patcher. It did a quick file scan and started downloading new zone data files. I assume traffic was very heavy since it estimated that the files would take approximately eight hours to download.

The download finished around 4:30 am this morning. I ran the Test patcher when I got up and it took about ten minutes to download some additional files. I logged onto Test and selected the Beta server. The Berserker class was not available for selection so I created a wood elf druid since I have the most experience with druids. My new character popped into the Kelethin druid's guild.

At that point, I camped and went to work... where I'm... errrr... working! Yes, that's my story and I'm sticking to it!

So far, everything has gone very smoothly. Tonight I will play with /betabuff and actually attempt to do some exploring.
