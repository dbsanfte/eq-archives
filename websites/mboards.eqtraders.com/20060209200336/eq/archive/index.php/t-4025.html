I *used* to carry enchanted ores on my bazaar mule. Unfortunately, they don't stack, they were only selling sporadically, and I was seeing far better returns on alchemy goodies.

I'd be more than happy to stop carrying enchanted clay if someone wants to pick up the production for that. I can generally sell from 2 to 20 stacks per week (many, many, many hours in that week in zombie trader mode) for 1pp per magic clay. I don't need mana vials anymore, as I'm a GM potter now (250 woohoo). However, when I was progressing from 200-250 there were 2 main things often missing from the bazaar:

Magic clay
Vials of clear mana

I could make the magic clay myself, so not a huge deal there. However, to save myself the pain I would have paid up to 2-3pp per clay, which would be a 40-60 pp profit for anyone in the bazaar. Considering you can mass enchant clay now, this would be an ez thing to do.

The vials of clear mana I was willing to give chanters a 100pp profit per stack (so sell for about 20 pp each vial). Aaastro stocks them from time to time - no idea who that is but he/she made me very happy. But...Aaastro was about the only one.

Also, if you can imbue the cheapest of the gems, those are always appreciated for skill ups. I could do emerald myself, so never worried about buying, but if I were a class that could only imbue a really expensive gem I would buy the cheaper ones already imbued in the bazaar.

From a tailorers perspective, heady kiola is always a nice thing. I HATED making that when doing skill ups, and usually bought it in the bazaar.
