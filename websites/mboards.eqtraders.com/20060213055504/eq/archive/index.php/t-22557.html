15 elemental warriors, no mercury. Tick, tock...

OK, we have (had) success. Anyone who is offered this task should grab it. The mobs are right at the zone in, and are EASILY single pulled, even by the lower levels that are heading in for exp.

At 60, about a third of the elementals turned up light blue, so there was a bit of AA to be had while I ground out the required number of kills. Less than one KEI duration took care of the 50 warriors, the trip back to FP, and back to the hole for 5 monstrosities, as well as derailing a few trains, doing a few rezzes, and some tag-along healing to help level someone's chanter.

The bad new is, the symbols (as well as the armor) don't sell to the merchants, and can't be tributed. They has a very high success rate, as well, so you get to trash them as you make them (owwww!)

Now, on to the good part. 41 symbol combines and (about) 15 LoY robes netted 4 skill-ups to 220. Not too bad considering the low cost involved. I think the expert symbols/armor take me to 222, then it's back to robes only, I guess. :(
