If yer Iksar Smith doesn't have access to Luclin/PoP I would say forget it.

I don't mean to douse your smithing aspirations but the Iksar just don't have much usefull in the way of cultural armors. I could go off on SOE for their completely brainless cultural design for Iksar but thats a post for Primal Scream. The Fearsome Skyiron (Iksar BD Cultural and only high end cultural you can make) is horrible. The AC is weak for War/SK and the Wis on some of the set is so-so for Shammies. The set is Iksar only as well. Trolls have more useful items w/ their recycled cultural IMHO.

The Acrylia recipies will make some useful midgrade armor for your Bard and smith (chain only for the Iksar.) Farming components is slow. I'm lucky to get enough for 2-3 combines in 3-4 hrs of nonstop farming w/ a 51 necro.

W/O PoP your smith will not have access to the Tanaan forges required to make any of the planar metal pieces ( Nightmare Iron etc.)

Things are bleak for Iksar Smiths.
