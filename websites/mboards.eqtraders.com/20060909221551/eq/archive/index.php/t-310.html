If you are of level to fight in Cobalt Scar, you can go there get some exp and save all the cobalt drake hides, wyvern hides and othmir furs you get. A little farming in Crystal caverns for velium(or fight in Velks to get it) and you can skill up on those.

Cobalt drake hides + drake oil(brewed water + foraged drake egg(western waste, skyshrine, or ToV)) + pattern + velium boning(number depends on pattern used)

Otter caps = 2 Othmir fur + silver thread(vendors in Thurgadin) + pattern + vial of clear mana

Wyvern hide + cod oil(fish cobalt cod from lake in Cobalt Scar brew cod fish + water in brew barrel) + pattern + velium stud(number depends on pattern used)
