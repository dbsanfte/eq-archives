I have experienced this problem in Innothule since Gukta camp about... All the mobs tended to be crowded around the dead bodies where the spores spawn...

This technique won't help much in Plane of Storms cause it'll get you killed if it works, but otherwise it can be useful. Track the mob you're after and determine to within a few feet the spot where it is trapped.
