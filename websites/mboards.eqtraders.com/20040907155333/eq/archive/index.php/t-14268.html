Well, if fletching is your first tradeskill to 250 there are two benefits. First being that you can use the trophy to summon no rent bags with 100% weight reduction, one of which can be turned in for a permanent bag with 100% WR as well (both summoned and permanent are no drop, so no sharing). The second benefit is the added stats. It's really not bad of a range slot item, gives good charisma which helps out a lot with buying and selling if you've got low CHA. The skill modifier works while equipped in primary, secondary or range, so you can swap it out with any whatever you might carry in those slots to help keep your other stats up while still doing combines.
