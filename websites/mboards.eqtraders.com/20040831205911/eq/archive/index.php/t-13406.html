Tejaye wrote:

"Now I wonder if that rotating to the right with Fishing is connected somehow to my Forage being set up with my right cursor."

It is. Based on your saying that your forage always gets bagged correctly, I assume you have the /autoinventory command in your foraging macro. When you move right, you forage, then autoinventory. When forage is unpopped, moving right just does the /autoinventory, so you turn right with a fish in hand, the system forages (I'll assume that it can't because the button hasn't repopped), waits for whatever your macro says, then does /autoinventory and you drop your fish in the bag. Test by turning right only a littl bit and then waiting, and I bet you bag your fish.

If so, then the easy answer (and the one I use) is to put /autoinventory both before and after you fish. Then, if lag keeps the second one from working for whatever reason, when you hit the fishing button again it starts by bagging your fish, so you don't get the "can't fish with stuff in hand" message. Like so:

/autoinventory
/wait 120, /doability (fishing number)
/autoinventory

Simple! Happy fishing!

Brodybeard
My Fishin' macro:

[Line 1:] /pause 125,/doability X
[Line 2:] /autoinventory
[Line 3:] /pause 125,/doability X
[Line 4:] /autoinventory
[Line 5:] /doability X

Notes:

No space after the comma following the /pause command.

The pause length seems to be affected by lag. Sometimes I could use a pause of 110 (11 seconds) and other times I needed to increase it to 130 (13 seconds).
