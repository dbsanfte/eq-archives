Calabar
The best place to farm basilisk eggs is dependent upon your level and whether or not you have tracking.

LAVASTORM
Basilisks are lower level
Every basilisk can drop an egg or two, though many don’t drop any.
Basilisks roam the entire zone, making hunting difficult unless you happen to be a tracker.

JAGGEDPINE
Basilisks are higher level
Only specific basilisks drop eggs (a basilisk genitor)
Some genitors drop no eggs and some drop several
You have to kill many (a cave basilisk) to get the genitors to spawn
Basilisks are static spawns, no tracking required
