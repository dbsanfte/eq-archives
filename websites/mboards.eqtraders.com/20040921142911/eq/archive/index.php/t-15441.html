Beatleguise
GO to one of the numerous Bat Camps, and stock up on Batwings.

Take 5 gold and go to Ocean of tears.
Make sure you have a Tacklebox, and a Skinning Knife.

Gunthak Mackerals can be skinned to get you five baits per fish.
The 5 gold is for the sole purpose of buying fishing poles.

In a weekend, you can have Fishing Maxed, and by making Fish Rolls. (One Fresh Fish and a Bat Wing) You can have Baking to 135.

Additionally, since you will not have space for everything, your 5 gold will have multiplied. Depending on your intel/wis for skillups, you can easily have 100pp by the time you are done, to put toward the next step of baking.

At this point you go to Abysmal Sea, you should have lots of bait left over from OOT. Your going to use Tuna and Crab, and follow the Barbeque Recipe line.

Using the 100pp you made, you will be buying ingrediance to make Barbeque Sauce.

Starting with Barbeque Tuna, you will make Steaks, then Rolls.
This will get you to 196 in a couple hours, and the 200pp you started with will have once again grown becoming about 500pp.

If your bored, you could spend a weak making Barbequed Tuna Rolls to build up cash, and use this money to get ingrediance to take you beyond 200 pts.
