Some skills now make use of one of three stats, in the case of smithing it is EITHER Str, Wis, or Int what ever is highest. This number is only checked to manipulate the chance of getting a skill up.

I think that Poison making, Fletching and Tailoring were all targeted to take advantage of Dex or Agil as well as Wis or Int but not sure if this ever went live.

So yes if your Str is 255 and you Int is 3 you should see the same results as if your Str is 255 and your int is 178.
