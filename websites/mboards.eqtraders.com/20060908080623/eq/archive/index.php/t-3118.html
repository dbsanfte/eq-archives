And I'd definitely consider this a 2fer recipe. My skill is 242 (adjusted) and I'm 50/50 on Storm Rider stuff (grrr no StormWEAVE stuff made yet though), so I'd still plan on doubling the recipe. Remember the hard part is the pure rain. The recipe is interesting because of the leftovers you get.

For ONE attempt:

3 sandstorm pearls
3 stormrider blood
1 raw diamond
1 stormrider skin
2 a drop of pure rain
1 blank emblem
2 bricks of electrified copper

For TWO attempts:
3 sandstorm pearls
5 stormrider blood
1 raw diamond
1 stormrider skin
4 a drop of pure rain
1 blank emblem
4 bricks of electrified copper

This is because the 3 pearls turn into 4 powders, and you need 1 powder per temper. You also get the emblem back on failures, so you only need to make the grey diamond and emblem once. Also, if by chance you succeed the first time, those extras make an EXCELLENT tip and possibly a friend for life.
