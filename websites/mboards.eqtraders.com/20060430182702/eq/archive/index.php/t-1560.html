There are 3 places I go to get drops of mercury. They are listed in the order or best to worst drops.

1.) The Hole -- Albeit not everyone wants to go down here but if you are doing a raid, these drop pretty liberally.

2.) Steamfont near the Minotaur caves -- There are wandering elementals all over the place in this area and the earth ones are green at 15+ I believe.

3.) Najena -- There are elementals here too but they don't drop very often.
