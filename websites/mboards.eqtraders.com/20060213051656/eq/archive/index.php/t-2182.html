Farm the Tae Ew Blood and Pristine Scales. (not sure if they're "Pristine Lizard Scales" or "Pristine Tae Ew Scales"

Recipies (not recently updated) are here: http://www.eqtraders.com/secrets/recipes_cazicthule.htm#smith

Scales (&amp; other components) are no longer No Drop.

To make a BP attempt, you will need 6 Pristine Scales, 3 Tae Ew Bloods, 3 Scale Tempers (Iksar cultural temper), 1 BP mold, 1 Leather Padding, 1 Water &amp; a Smith with a Hammer.

Of course, there's a nice Warrior-only BP that drops in CT too: http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=10439
