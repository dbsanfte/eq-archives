Greetings,

Having just finally hit 250 after 2 years of work, I'm posting my run from 201 - 250 in case it can help others still working their way up. All combinations were done with a WIS between 265 - 280, and using a geerlok. I'm not going to attempt to count up the money I've spent, as it would probably depress me! :rolleyes: The vast majority of supplies were bought in the Bazaar, apart from hopper hides. I farmed all of those, as the hoppers were still giving me experience. Guildmates helped out to a small extent with various supplies, for which I rewarded them with armour I could make and they could use. All tannins/studs/bonings were brewed/smithed by me - I'm not sure if that saves money, but it raised two other skills to a good level!

I passed 200 back in Oct 2003, and hit 250 earlier today (11 Apr 04) - just in case you think I sat down and did all these in one sitting! :D
