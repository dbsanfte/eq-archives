Remember Nick Yee? He used EQ as the subject of research in college and had put out some questionnaires that were advertised on a wide range of EQ sites, starting a year or two ago. He now has a website of his own: http://www.nickyee.com/hub/home.html where you can find a bunch of stuff including the results of those studies and some similar research and complete the latest questionnaire, that has now extended to include games other than EQ. A lot of it has to do with the demographics and social lives of players...very interesting stuff!
