After trying theese out for a while, here's my comments.
Most traps are named "Crab Trap" and will will give up to 3 various sized crabs. Sometimes they give rusty daggers, or nothing at all.

I belive "Battered Crab Trap" is an uncommon spawn with an uncommon/ rare drop yet to be discoered, with Cloth Caps as the common loot.
