From 202 to around 240, I made shadow tipped acrylia arrows. With 20 tries (1 stack), I failed somewhere between 40-60% of the time. This is w/ wisdom of 320 and using a geerlok.

I probably made around 100+ attempts at making Mithril champion arrows, from 240 on to GM fletching, and the failure rate was noticably larger.

When I hit 250, my results, although minimal testing, confirmed that the acrylia arrows have a lower trivial than the mithril arrows. 1 stack of acrylia arrows yielded about 80% success while the mithril champion arrows was roughly 40-50%.

The mithril arrow combine was more time consuming due to the fact that there's only 1 specific forge and one location to buy the mithril. I don't expect you getting a 50% success rate unless you are effectively GM (252) in fletching.
