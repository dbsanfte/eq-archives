I have noticed that when I went to the fletching guides...none of them went into DETAIL....so i shall try to submit one. (BTW all the other guides were great)

-1--16- Buy Field point arrowheads, large nocks, wooden arrow shaft, and round cut fletchings. (skill...not money making material here)
-16--32- use parabolic cut fletchings in place of the round ones. (big change huh?)
-32--64- upgrade to Bone shafts (maybe a little more money used)
-64--108- work on class 4 bone point arrows in place of the fletchings use wooden encasings (this may or may not be the right name, but i believe if you want to find out go to your local fletching merchant PS these are NOT shafts)
-108--122- (wrack your'e nerves cuz this is gonna cut your'e pockets if you are not rich) Ok. go back to field arrowheads large nocks wooden arrow shafts....(you thought i was going to say the fletchings?) and Bone encasings costing you around 14-17 pp depending on what your charisma is and faction standing is.
-122--up- Still working on it I will keep you posted when i make a breakthrough =^)
