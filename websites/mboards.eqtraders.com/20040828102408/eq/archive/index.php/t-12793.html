Dunthor Warsmith
It the old saying, "Having is not so pleasing a thing as wanting; its not logical but its often true." Thus it is with food in EQ - the thought of that delicious, tasty, tantilizing meal invigorates body and mind, boosting it to new heights. Actually eating it however, you realize that this god-meal you have been dreaming of is nothing more than mortal food, and the effects of the dream are gone.

Thus you force yourself to eat lesser foods to keep yourself from getting hungry, while keeping the wonderful feast sitting right there as the next thing to eat 'when' you do get hungry. So you're always savoring the dream-food, but never actually eating it.
