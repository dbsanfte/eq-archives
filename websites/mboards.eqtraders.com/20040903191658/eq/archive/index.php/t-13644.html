Valeanx Madpath
My advice for saving money from 202 to 227 if you can't do cultural fletching: GoD fletching is the way to go.

A Chunk of Taelosian Rock to Imildu in Abysmal yields 2 taelosian stones (You can do this yourself, but even after several stacks of rocks I was still only averaging 1.5-1.7 stones per combine. ) Taelosian stone + water + file = 2x serrated arrowheads. Serrated arrowheads has recipes that trivial at 212 and 227. They sell on NPC vendors for ~3pp and on bazaar traders for anywhere from 5-500pp.

What I did was pick up any stones off vendors I could find, plus buying up any in the bazaar that cost less than 150p. Once I got 10 or so saved up I'd take em to Imildu, turn em into stones, then make them into arrowheads and I'd have 2 stacks of attempts. The arrows are crap but they're cheap and every rock gives you 4 attempts. I went from 202 to 220 in about 5 sessions with these, my latest being when I spent 2200p on 22 rocks. I turned that into 84 arrowheads and from these got from 211 to 220. This was probably a lucky skillup rate, but regardless, it would have cost me 3 times as much to do the same number of darkwood bows, and that's assuming no failures.
