The first generation of mechanical mounts, the Robogoat, consumed trash for fuel. Unfortunately, the algorithm that decided what was trash had some shortcomings which resulted in some rather spectacular failures. While the loss of mining expedition 323 (after a goat ate its way into an explosive storage area) was considered to be an acceptable loss by the inventor, 2 weeks later when one of his creations consumed his prized collection of left handed wrenches he recalled and rebuilt the entire line. The rebuilt mounts were originally only supposed to eat small bits of organic trash, but determining what is trash still doesn't work well.
As mounts that eat the food from their packs is much less stressful to gnomes than if the mounts ate the other contents of their packs, the new Roboboars are considered a resounding success.
