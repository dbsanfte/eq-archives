Kritta
To recharge an item, place item + battery in a toolbox (regular, deluxe or summoned via collapsible toolbox) and click combine. No skill is needed (in other words anyone of any race and any skill [even 0] can combine with no risk of failure).

== Rechargeable Items ==
Happy Dragon: Rechargeable with saltpeter and firework
Meterological Rocket: Not Rechargeable
Necrotic Device: Unlimited Charges to begin with
Gauze Press: 20 charges, recharge with class 1
Max Device: 20 charges, recharge with class 6
Min Device: 20 charges, recharge with class 6
Clockwork Armors: 10 charges, recharge with class 5
Gnomish Vanishing Device: 5 charges, recharge with class 5

The traditional way to recharge non-tinkered items has been to find a vendor who does not stock any of the item, sell him a fully-charged item, sell him a no-charge item, then buy both back. Both will be fully-charged.

Verant is now aware of this "feature". For example, Lady Vox drops a rez stick with limited charges. To recharge this stick using a vendor now costs well over a million plat.
