I was a guide. I did NOT have Kunark. I WAS called on to Guide in Kunark zones, but I could NOT play in them till I bought the expansion.

Also someone said that they had heard that EQII and following will not be volunteer supported. True. This decision was made and carved in stone back in 2002. The EQ Guide program is the last Verant/SOE Guide program.

And with the advent of "outsourced" CS-staffing the Guide program is, so far as a former guide can tell, being shifted (I said SHIFT-ed) over to being what used to be called "The Quest Troupe" in that they will have lots more time to run "dynamic quests" as they will be handling fewer and fewer (and eventually zero) petitions.

(Someone said the Senior Guides had lost the command to change first names. *tremble* That would be scary. And the handwriting on the death warrant for the Guide Program would be clear.)

Plus, for the true bean counters one must understand that the vast majority of Guides don't subscribe month by month. So the $13/month number is high.

And most guides buy all the expansions. (For their play accounts. I was a freak. I had Velious for a year before I bought Kunark.)

And most guides have at least 2 close friends they play with on their play servers. And most guides are a POSITIVE retention factor. (Higher guide program numbers leads to higher customer satisfaction leads to higher subscription renewal rates.)

Then there is the timing factor. Guides don't get EQ -free- so much as they are not charged for renewal during their time as a Full Guide+. But apprentice guides ARE charged renewal rates. (I got re-billed during my apprenticeship, and so did other apps.) And the subscription "renewal" date is not extended for the time that one is a Guide. After I left the guide program after just over 3 months a mere 2 months later my 6 month subscription (which I had paid during my apprenticeship) lapsed and had to be renewed.

For the ones that didn't catch it.... SOE didn't lose a penny having me be a Guide. (And the washout rate for new apps and guides is horrific. Attrition at the Normandy invasion wasn't that bad. Heck Custer's Last Stand is a decent comparision. 90% of Guides last less than a year.)
