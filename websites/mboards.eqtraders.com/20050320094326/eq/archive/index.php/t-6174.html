As I tell most people, "You will end up with 2 sets of gear if you tradeskill".

For your tradeskilling gear, get wis, pure and simple. Closest to 255 is what you are aiming for. Thats an easy one, lots of cheap good wisdom items out there.

As for fighting, I know only a few shaman, and they tell me, AC is what you want! Youll want your AC and HP as high as you can, because who else is gonna be taking the hits when you are soloing, or slowing and getting agro from the slows? Exactly. Sure, wis is important too, but dont try to go both. You may want to consult the Crucible(sorry, dont know the website) to get a better informed answer about shaman though.

So in conclusion, buy some cheap wis gear to do tradeskilling, and then toss it in the bank when you go hunting.
