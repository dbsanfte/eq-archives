Well, okay.. the NDA has been lifted, so here goes: The twist to alchemy is... Throwable potions! Sadly, I haven't had as much time for beta playing as I had hoped, so I only have the recipies for the 4 base potions, but the effects you can do are: Fire DD, Cold DD, Stun (!), Snare (!!). Stun and snare I'm definitely excited about. You can see all of the effects here: http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spelllis...text=concoction

Sadly, snare is only 5 ticks (although to me right now, all 3 levels of snare appear to be the same, so maybe higher ones will get a longer duration before it goes live), and the top level stun only goes up to lvl61 mobs, but hey, this is on-demand snare and stun for shamans, or any class for that matter.

The DD potions do not appear to be worth much to me, but may be useful situationally. The level 1 potions anyway cost about 5pp to make and do 20dmg. Can't really think of where I'd use that just yet.

Here are the recipies for the 4 level I potions. You can get this from the book you get from the alchemy people there, but this is a little cleaner to just start making them from:

Concoction of Flame I: Vial, Cork, Yellow Rheum, Cicino, Acaana, Flamewort
Concoction of Ice I: Vial, Cork, Yellow Rheum, Cicino, Suranie, Seathorn
Ensnaring Concoction I: Vial, Cork, Yellow Rheum, Angurth, Kaktu, Bitterwood
Stunning Concoction I: Vial, Cork, Yellow Rheum, Angurth, Kaktu, Arroweed

Trivial on all is 82
Costs are: Cork: 1cp, Vial: 2g0s9c, all other ingredients: 1p0g4s9c each

So you're looking at 4p4g0s6c per dose for these level one potions, which seems reasonable enough.

I think I'd say my main complaint about them at this juncture is that they do not stack. :\ Hopefully with some complaining, that'll get fixed, though.
