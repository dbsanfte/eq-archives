Your best bet is to follow the pottery guide under the Learn a Skill section of the main site (sounds like you already found that one.) That will take you to something like 122, though when I did it, it was more like 100pp than 30pp.

If you click on the pottery recipe (http://www.eqtraders.com/recipes/pottery_recipe_page.php?article=55&rsa=Pottery&sub=&sb=item&menustr=080090000000) page, it will take you to all the various things you can make. You're more interested in the basic recipies (http://www.eqtraders.com/recipes/pottery_recipe_page.php?article=114&rsa=Pottery&sub=basic&sb=item&menustr=080090020000) right now.

Always remember to check for your cultural. I don't know if there is any cultural pottery, but I know for smithing, the wood elf cultural saved me a ton of time and money.
