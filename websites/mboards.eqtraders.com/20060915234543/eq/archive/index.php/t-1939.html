Just a few notes:

1) ring recipes (velium rings, enchanted rings) produce 2 rings per set of ingredients.

2) Jade is imbued solely by Ogre Shamen of Rallos Zek, as far as I know.

3) 1-3 imbued rings per item.

4) 40% success rate on most Fierce armor pieces. You'll want to have 3x materials available to have a good chance at what you're looking for. Personally, I've had as many as 7 failures in a row, and have heard of 10 or more. Usually, though, 3 attempts will come up with something.
