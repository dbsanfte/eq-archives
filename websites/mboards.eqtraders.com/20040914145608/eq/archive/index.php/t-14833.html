KanadWinterstorm
Othmirs for me drop on average 7-9 furs an hour, clearing solo with a 65 ranger in EP gear. It takes approximately 5 hours of farming time per 20 cap combines (40 furs).

It really does vary wildly though, I'll go an hour getting two or three then get 11 in the next hour. All in all, othmir fur is still the easiest progression method with farming but it is far from rewarding.
