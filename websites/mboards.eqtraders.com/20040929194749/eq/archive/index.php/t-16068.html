They are all still lore, I stock all the folded and collapsed containers on my bazaar mule. The trick I've learned is that shared bank slots are not assigned against a character so you can have a lore item on the mule and one in the shard bank slot. There are 10 types of collapsed/folded containers so I can have one of each on my trader and a spare one of each in a deluxe toolbox in a shared bankslot. That way, when I go to check my trader in the morning before I leave for work I can quickly replenish the stock. Not perfect but still better than having to log onto my gnome and halfling to make up some new ones.
