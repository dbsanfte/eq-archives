JC was my last skill to 250, 1750 finally :D

I followed Gumkak's excellent guide: http://www.valendor.org/gumkak/

WIS was 280. I used a geerlok.

So, from 201-->250, skill-up rate was 50/1292, or 25.8 attempts/point. Overall success rate was: 1214/1292, or 94%. Points gained on successes: 45/50, or 90%.

My best runs were skills after 1 attempt: skills 209, and 235.

My worst run was a skill-up after 94 attempts for skill 243.

I didn't do the math on how much this actually cost me. I'm figuring 8K or so.

Have fun with the data!
