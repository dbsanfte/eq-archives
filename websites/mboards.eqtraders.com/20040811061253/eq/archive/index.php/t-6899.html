The original thing about the shawl is that is *stacks* with aa enhancement, whereas the items like the pottery-made hammer don't. Max extension from aa is 50% and if you have shawl (only the shawl, no other items) you can add +20% for a grand total of +70% duration. Here's a link the the exact data for the enhancement on Lucy's - shows that the hammer is +15% to duration but if you have SCRM (aa for enhancement) don't bother, you can't get more than +50% without the shawl.

Well...I haven't tested that so I can't say if shawl + item stack, and in the history of EQ design, oftentimes items like that don't (can anyone say haste?)

So, I think I would test that before making the claim of +35% with no aa.

... and as far as AA extension is concerned, here are a few bits n pieces (specifically for enchanterly aa) copied from elsewhere:

Adds the +30% to duration:
Archetype (Requires Level 55, and at least 6 points in General Abilities)
Spell Casting Reinforcement [Prerequisite for Spell Casting Reinforcement Mastery]: Lengthens the duration of buffs cast by you including from items like the enchanter epic, and tranquility pants. Your friends will love long duration KEI when you run by them. Nice on raids particularly for hastes which are often a bit too short for some long fights when you factor in buff time. Side benefit of improving the effective mana efficiency of buffs. (3 Levels) Cost: 2/4/6. Benefit: Increases buff duration by 5%, 15%, and 30%.

Adds an additional +20% for a grand total of +50% to buff duration:
Class (Requires Level 59, and at least 12 points in Archetype Abilities)
Spell Casting Reinforcement Mastery [Requires Spell Casting Reinforcement 3]: 120 Minute VoQ, and 1 hour haste is a good thing. Note: I've now tested this out myself, this plus a 8th shawl takes you to +70% duration. Old CW: Why would anyone need an enchanter, KEI lasts 150 minutes? New CW: Why would anyone need an enchanter, your KEI lasts 255 minutes? (1 Level) Cost: 8. Benefit: Increases the duration of beneficial buffs by 20% (with pre-reqs total of 50%)
