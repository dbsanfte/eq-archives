Battlewagon
Some notes on above.

Guess the first thing to notice is that tailoring really doesnt start until skill 159 or so. After that, its gonna be brutal.

Celestial robes are a good cost effective method of getting to 210, then ethereal swatches to 212. But after that, 213-219 was brutal, 33% success rate. According to other sources, after 220, its 25% success rate, so you have been warned.

It took me 6months to finish tailoring (and thus finish Aid Grimel) and I collected all my own materials, with a bit of help along they way. (you need friends to get the holy water in PoN, and Saps in PoF, etc)

So how much to get to skill (219)?

~147 STACKS of tunare swatches used.
@ 15p per swatch ... 44100pp in emeralds alone. (yah its ALOT of imbueing... tip: imbue where you can forage! )

50/80 chains made (with JC3 enchanter) and used. 21600pp in chains.

And of course, the collecting of no less than 294 stacks of silk.

Has VI or Sony actually ever looked at that number and thought about it? I didnt realize it during those 6 months, but it would appear I spent no less than 150 HOURS in EK collecting silk. And of course this is the fastest route to tailoring. (and this only gets me to 219 tailoring!)

I did get a chance to use the new UI from 219 to 224 tho... and i like it!

However, despite the long stretches without a skillup, I did average slightly better than 1 in 20 skillups... and I was VERy lucky in NOT succeeding on robes.

Despite the hardships at skill 183 or so, I couldnt be more satisfied with my hard work, and my new earring.
