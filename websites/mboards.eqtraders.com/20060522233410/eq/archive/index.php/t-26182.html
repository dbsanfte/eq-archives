Smithing in general is not cheap; whichever path you choose will involve an investment in time (farming), money (buying), or both.

Fine Plate is the most efficient route to 188 or so. It's entirely store-bought except for the padding. Successful combines will sell back to vendors for about the cost of the stuff you bought, minus the padding. If you farm your own, you'll more or less break even on fine plate, paying just for failed combines. Note that all fine plate takes a single leather padding per attempt.

See also Secone's guide here (http://mboards.eqtraders.com/eq/showthread.php?t=25961).
