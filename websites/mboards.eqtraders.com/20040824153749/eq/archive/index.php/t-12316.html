Krazick
To make anything, you simply need the materials and the tradeskill container. The actual expansion would only be needed if you can't buy/trade for the materials needed... or can't reach the tradeskill container to do the combine.

For Example, to make PoP Jewelry you need a Planar Jewelery Kit... which someone else who has PoP can buy and sell to you. So PoP is not required.

But, to make PoP armor that can only be made in a Tanaan Forge, found only in Plane of Knowledge... you MUST have the PoP Expansion.

So for LoY Potions... get someone with LoY Expansion to buy them for you, or find someone in the bazaar that is selling them... and get them.
