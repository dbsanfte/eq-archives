Sylphan
Crafted chain is tricky.

Take, for example, Hurricane Chain. Both sleeves and tunic have good stats. However, the tunic has to compete with Crypt Robber's Chestplate with roughly the same stats and no required level for one tenth the price. Sleeves have no such competition, not even Tae Ew is as good.

Recommended levels probably won't matter much to twinks... they intend to level fast and grow into the armor, but required levels are a big problem. That will effectively kill the Tew Ew market. HP and Str are bound to be the favorite stats, unless dex proves to be as important for throwing as for archery.

Rangers won't pay much for gloves and helm because there are some common drops with great stats for under 100 plat. Berserkers can't wear those ranger-only items so they should buy yours... but it will be an uphill battle at first because gloves/helm prices have been depressed for awhile now.

But... I don't mean to be pessimistic. Overall, the addition of berzerkers can be nothing but good for the chainmail market.
