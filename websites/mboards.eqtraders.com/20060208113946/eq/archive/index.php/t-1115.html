What we are trying to do it to hook people up with tradeskillers who can do their combines. While a 200+ skiller with tinkering (Yay, Xenophon!) is more safe for Rebreathers and Corks, a 160 tinkerer would most likely be able to make the item (5 for 6 corks so far for me).

What is going to be the bottom limit for listing a skill?

I'm winging it really, if it's a cultural skill (tailoring,smithing,fletching) or a race/class skill (tinkering,poison,alchemy), I'll have a tendency to include everyone that posts, especially if that's the only skill they post on.

If it's a common skill (baking, brewing, fishing) I don't want to list everyone and their brother who has that skill, it should be fairly easy to find someone if you are looking. Therefore I'll tend to only post it if it's > 190 or so.
