Rissenn
..is used to create "Unfired planar stein's"

I have used these between 200-216, and noticed that I got skill-up's quite frequently. (Around 1 skillup per 15-20 combines)

The recipe is also quite easy:

Sculpting Tools (1) Storebought
Water Flask (1) Dropped Storebought
Tainted Planar Essence (1) Dropped
Ceramic Lining Sketch (1) Storebought
Block of Tanaan Clay (1) Storebought

Combine in Pottery wheel. Trivial at 216

If you farm your own TPE, this combine will cost you about 10.5pp which is pretty nice.

TPE, I have found, is most easily farmed already at the entrance of Crypt of Decay. Bring a solid group and spend some time there, you will get frequent drops of TPE and also gems enough to do a split and have money left over for the clay. And the xp is decent as well. :D

I finally sat down and did a price comparison between Unfired Planar Steins, Unfired Opal Encrusted Steins and Unfired Green Ceramic Bands.
The comparison is not 100% accurate since it is geared for the skills I have easy access to.
I valued Celestial Essence at 6pp which is a common selling price in Karana Bazaar. I won't make my own.
I valued Tainted Planar Essence at 6.299pp which is cost to buy off NPC vendors. I farm or buy from NPC vendors.
I did not include costs for enchanting services. I have two L65 enchanter friends.

Unfired Planar Steins 16.861 pp per combine.
Unfired Opal Encrusted Steins 24.157 pp per combine.
Unfired Green Ceramic Bands 31.855 pp per combine.

Considering the relatively small cost difference between Planar and Opal steins, I'm not sure that it's worth doing Planar steins to 216 unless you have easy access to supplies of TPE.
