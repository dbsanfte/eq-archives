Tinile
No, if you are level 55+ you do not need any flags to enter PoStorms. The fishing pond is immediately behind you when you zone in.

The entrance to get in the zone is near the windmill. Go up to the grass and look for a cave with an opening circled in rocks in the grassy hill. Go through the tunnel, pass the bridge and get to the really big rock with the "force field" surrounding it. Click that and you are in PoS. If you have maps from maps.eq-toolbox.com, it is marked on your map.

I haven't fished in PoS for awhile, but I do remember there was an issue with land sharks while fishing there. The best solution I found to this problem was to lev and stand right over the pond in the middle. That eliminated any nearby land sharks.

Good luck fishing. If you are planning to work on baking, you will find storm salmon very useful as you near 200. If not, be sure to save them for friends or your bazaar mule. No other unique fish are in the zone.
