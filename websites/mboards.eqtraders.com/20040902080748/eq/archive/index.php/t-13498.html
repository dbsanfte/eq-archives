Just finished my 7th tradeskill in Baking 250 plus a trophy.

I started out in Halas Pies at 184 but changed my mind quickly.

I went instead up the Justice Fruit Pies avenue and spent 70k along the way getting there but.....

Sold my fruit pies I made and got back 62k of that so was not sooooo bad.

Lets see from 184 to 250 it took:

1245 attempts

775 successful pies

470 failures

1 wurm egg (sold in bazaar)
+ 1 cup of flour (sold in pok)
+ 1 bottle of milk (sold in pok)
yields 14 clumps of dough.

1 Justice Fruit sold in bazaar
1 Frosting Sold in PoK
2 Celestial Essence sold in Bazaar

1 Muffin Tin sold in Bazaar (returned on successes and failures)

This was the easiest way to get to 250 from 184.

Hope this helps,
