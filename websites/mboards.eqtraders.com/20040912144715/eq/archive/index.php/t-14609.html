Well, it looks to my spreadsheet that:

*) Tuna and Crab share recipes, while Cragmeat and Hynid do as well.

*) Tuna/Crab do not have Jerky or Kabobs, while Crag and Hynid do not have Cakes. Crab alone has Appetizers while you can only make Tuna into a salad.

*) The trivial level, "benefit" and complexity of the recipes rather steadily increase thru the sauces: Grilled, Spiced, Smoked, Buttered, Garlic-Buttered, Lemon-Buttered, Barbequed, Beer-braised and Beer-battered whereas the resultant items similarly go Sandwiches, Stew, Soup, Steaks, Appetizers/Salad/Jerky, Rolls/Kabobs, Cakes, Pies and finally Souffles.

*) Crab and Tuna trivials are generally below Crag/Hynid recipes, altho it seems that pies and souffles share the same trivial tables across the sauces.
