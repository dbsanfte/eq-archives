I've been working my way through pottery recently, and I found a few more skins that do and don't work.

Add to the DNC list: Low Quality Coyote Pelt, Grizzly Bear Skin, Mountain Lion Pelt.

Add to the DOES work list: Puma Skin, Thick Grizzly Bear Skin.

These were tested with Sealed Poison Vials, but I imagine they work for other vials also. I'll post back here as I find and test more skins.

The vial of viscous mana is made by an enchanter using the level 12 Thicken Mana spell. It requires a poison vial and a pearl as material components
