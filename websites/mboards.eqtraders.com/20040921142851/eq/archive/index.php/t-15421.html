Originally posted by Straay
Just wondering if someone could give me some good areas to hunt for spiderling silks.

Go to the Field of Bone. If you're not an Iksar, be sure to give Cabilis a wide bearth, as Trooper Taer from the city has a fairly large patrol radius. Don't go into the pit, but instead, find a small hill slightly into the woods west of the pit. On the side of a hill facing away from the pit, you will find a tunnel sloping down into the bonebinder warrens. As a general rule, the FoB is a big newbie area, but I generally find there is nobody in the warrens. Entrance to the warrens is -300, 2600. Spiderlings also congregate between the woods and the pit, but slightly north of the ruins nearby. Not as many as typically in the warrens, though, and outside, there are a lot of annoying large/giant scropions which tend to aggro on someone minding his own business. Hope this helps.

I have a rather unconventional method of farming Leather Padding, but I find it yields a lot more.

If you kill rockhoppes, you will get LQ,MQ, HQ hides in much higher quantities, and you will get the $$ hides often as well. The hides for tailoring drop just dandy in Shadeweavers if that is all you are looking for.

However, the real money is in the luclin thread. If you use rockhopper hides, it allows you to use Shade Silk thread as well as regular thread for padding. This is especially useful because you can massacre the little silk worms in Shadeweavers for silk, which have dropped as many as 5 at a time for me...but /drumroll. A little known fact is that the black Aliens in shadeweavers, and other parts of Luclin drop silk as well, and they have TONS of it. If you go and clear the caves in Shadeweavers, I have regularly gotten 2-3 stacks per clearing, and only rarely under 1 stack.

The lower quality rockhoppper hides tend to sell for less than their old world counterparts in the bazaar on my server as well, when I am feeling especially lazy.
