I've been looking for an item with Improved Healing IV for my level 63 Cleric. I checked on augments in LDON, but there are apparently none for this focus item, so I decided I had 2 main choices to get this focus: either ornate plate sleeves or a Ceramic Totem of the Rathe. On my server, I had heard that the sleeves molds run at 80K+...assuming you can find one which I hadn't been able to do. And since my current sleeves are clockwork observer, they are pretty good as it is.

The other choice was the totem. Since I just finished shawl 8 a week ago, my ranged slot no longer was sporting the Ceramic shield of Valor when hunting and seemed like a good spot to upgrade. I'm in a small family guild, so don't have elemental planes access, nor expect to any year soon. As a result, I could either just buy a totem in the bazaar (for 60K), or look for the supplies in the bazaar and try to make one myself.

I waffled a bit after pricing everything for 2 weeks, as the supplies to make a totem run 40K+. Do I buy the totem, buy the supplies, or wait to see if an ornate sleeve mold shows up. Finally, I made the plunge and purchased everything I needed for a combine.

Put it all on my potter, and prayed to the RNG god. Oh my god, this is a 40K combine. /e Bites her nails. Oh please, oh please...

Click.

Woohoo!!! I succeeded! Okay, calm down, can still fail on the fireing...

Click. WOOOOOOOOOOOT!

My cleric is now a proud owner of a Ceramic Totem of the Rathe. :D
