Barli
If you have the GoD expansion you don't need to farm for skins at all. I was told to buy stacks of Crow's Special Brew in the vendor area on the Abysmal boat and use that in place of a skin.

Sure enough, I tried this and made the unfired vials using the CSB. For some reason, with the new UI it tells you that you learned the recipe for the vial but it thinks you are using the Mist Wolf Pelt version of the recipe.

So in this case, may be bugged not sure, but a Crow's Special Brew = Mist Wolf Pelt and can be used for making poison vials. The CSB costs only 1 silver plus change so it is well worth buying that in stacks rather than farming skins any day! Don't pay 2-10pp per skin any more!

I got my Pottery skill up to around 170 now and plan to do more with the Crows Special Brew soon.
