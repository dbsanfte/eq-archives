I think a strong group could farm silks, but it'd take a lot of work. One of the roamers that go all the way to the zone-in is a spider, but the rest are all up on the top island. You'd have to fight your way up past quite a few mobs and spiders hit like a ton of bricks. But a strong group could do it, assuming you can split the spiders, have a good tank, and a solid healer.

In my experience, bow staves are fairly common if your guild does the lower air rings, which we do a lot. Silks, on the other hand, are a pain to get except for when you do Dust Ring (i.e., almost never) or Xegony.
