Silverfish
What's a Dwarven cleric to do? You could take up smithing so I can get some decent armor...

But seriously, you can indeed make robes. And armor. All sorts of paths are open to you, so don't fret not being able to make robes for the bookworm types. Your best bet, having broken 187, is to take up the tailor-driven extermination of the rockhopper menace that plagues Luclin (there's a cave in the Dawnshroud Peaks that's nigh infested with them). Failing that, and assuming you have access to a Tunare worshipping cleric or druid, you can make ceremonial Solstice robes. Both are fine choices for someone with your sturdy fingers and the hides and blessed Tunare silk have a high tolerance for drunken dwarves spilling ale on the materials.

Good luck!
