You have to make it successfully once the 'old' way (click Experiment in the new UI, then drag the stuff in and click combine as you used to) before it will show up in the list. You will get a message saying 'You have learned the recipe for Misty Thicket Picnic!' when you do this.

Briefly: Some recipes are flagged 'autoLearn', and they appear in the list without you having to do anything. Some recipes (eg MTP) are flagged 'canLearn', and won't appear in the list till you've successfully made them once the 'old' way. Some recipes are not learnable at all (quest recipes and those they haven't got around to flagging yet).
