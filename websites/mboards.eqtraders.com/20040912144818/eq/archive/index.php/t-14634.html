Well, I recently went from zero to 200. This was jsut before GoD launch, so no freebie points for me. I wasn't overly worried about price at the time, mainly because I allowed myself a budget of 10k and no more (I was bored one afternoon before a raid, and decided that since I had decided to do aid grimel, I would knock out some of the skilling up). The point is, I sometimes skipped steps of 2 or 3 points that would have been cheaper in order to buy in bulk, so I'm sure it can be done cheaper than I did.

I went from 10 to 179 for roughly 2kpp. By skilling with geerlok equipped, I figure I saved a few plat on failures. From 179=200, it cost me roughly 7kpp, mainly because of the massive number of combines per skill point it took after 179. Losing 2 failure's worth of supplies per stack is a LOT cheaper when it only takes you a stack to gain a point then it is when it takes 3 or 4 stacks per point.

YMMV though, as I wasn't particularly worried about getting maximum value for my plat.
