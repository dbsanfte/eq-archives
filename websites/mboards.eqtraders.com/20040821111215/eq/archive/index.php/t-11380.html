Mukkul
You can sell the used probe to a vendor for 14 pp, then tinker a new one for 18 pp. Net cost 3-4 pp.

Even better, used probes can be vendor-recharged: Sell a fully charged probe to a vendor with free slots, then your used probe, buy them both back fully charged. Net cost 3 pp and no re-tinkering involved.
