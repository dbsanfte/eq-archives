There should be two listings for each one. One is to make (for example) a large brick FROM small bricks (ie going up in size). That's the list you have there.
The second (with the same name) is for going down in size eg, a large brick FROM a block.

Try making your window a bit wider. I believe there's actually a note in brackets saying (from whatever-smaller item) beside the first set of listings that you have mentioned.
You *may* have to do the breakdown combines once first to get them added. But they were working as of last night for me at least.
