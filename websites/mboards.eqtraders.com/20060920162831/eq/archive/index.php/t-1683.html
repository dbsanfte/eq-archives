Best tip I ever read here was to equip a fire beetle eye as a light source when looking for ground spawns. It makes the yew leaves give off a greenish glow and they are much easier to see. Also, the same poster (Wyrdlan I think? sp?), suggested dropping a yew leaf on the ground that you already have and 'practice' finding it. I found both tips very helpful.

Good luck!
