Frerin the Smith
In my opinion, Hurricane Plate is such a little upgrade from any cultural plate, that it's not worth making. It is a darn fine chain suit though and I sell chain tunics and leggings on a regular (1 or 2 pieces a week) basis.
Nalien
I've found that the Hurricane chain sleeves and gloves sell pretty good. The tunic and legs don't seem to compete very well against tae ew chain though (tae ew tunics go for like 3k any more on Xev), so I've always edged away from making them.

I agree with Frerin though, the plate just doesn't seem to be worth it, unless you're filling a specific order. It takes four times as many copper bricks (and double the hurricane tempers) to make a copper sheet as it does to make one copper ring. Basically, plate just gives you more chance for failures with scarce components and gives not great competition against other plate items.

my $.02

KyrosKrane
Since Hurricane Tempers are also needed for the Aid Grimel quest (albeit not too many per tradeskiller), the price on Drops of Pure Rain tends to get unreasonably high at times. This often makes it much harder to justify the cost of Hurricane Plate armor compared to cultural plate. If you can get Drops at a sensible price, though, you should be able to provide some serious competition to the plate sellers in your local bazaar.
Abazagaroth
Hurricane Plate arms are the only plate that is worth making to sell without a pre-order. You can make a greater profit on them than you can for any chain piece. They are pretty much the second best droppable arm slot for plate classes, particularly for paladins and clerics. Let me put it this way, it is extremely close to elbow spiked armplates in terms of stats. Almost negligable differences. So what do elbow spiked sell for?

I don't actively smith anymore, just buy components when I see them very cheap, but I have made like 20 pairs of the plate arms and none have taken more than 24 hours to sell. Chain arms, gloves, and coif all generall sell as well, just tend to take awhile.

Plate gauntlets and helm will sell too, but no faster than the chain and with slightly less profit.

I will post the caveat though, that on my server there are almost no high end tradeskill stuff available due to an agreement between elemental guilds. So if you are on a server with livestone plate generally available, then this may not hold for you as much.
