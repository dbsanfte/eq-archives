andaerice
Success rate should be very high, almost not noticable that you don't exactly have the skill (210 effective vs. 226). My cleric with the same 200+geerlok made these all the time. In general it looked like this...

Effective skill: 210
203: Surefall Ranger's Trail Juice - No problem
216: Grobb Liquidized Meat - about 5% failures, hardly noticable
226: Qeynos Afternoon Tea - again, hardly noticable
246: Innothule Triple Bile - will probably take 2-3 tries to make it
248: Mino Brew & Avalanche Ale - high failure rates
250+: Champagnes - chance of success 1/10 or worse
250+: Kaladim Constitutionals - almost no chance, fails 50% of the time at 250+geerlok

I made nice profit on these buying the forages in bazaar for 10pp ea., so profit would be much higher if you go and forage them yourself.
