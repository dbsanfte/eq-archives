When you hit the Experiment button, it opens up your
tradeskill container normally instead of the UI window.
You'll need to do this occasionally to add combine recipes
to your tradeskill window if they aren't showing. To get
the UI window back, close the toolbox, then click on it
again; you should now see the UI window once more.
If not, you may have also clicked on the button that
makes it so your UI doesn't open automatically, in which
case close the toolbox again and then hold the Ctrl key
while you open it with your mouse.
