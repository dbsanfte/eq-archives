The maximum for all stats is raised by five points every time you ding 61-65.

You can also buy for 2aa a pop ability that raises the maximum for all stats an additional five points. This can be bought up to five times (once per level 61-65).

You can also buy for 3aa a pop ability that raises the maximum for wis and int only an aditional 10 points. This can be bought up to five times (once per level over 60) as well.
