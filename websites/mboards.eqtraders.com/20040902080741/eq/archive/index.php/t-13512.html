After a frustrating and unnecessarily lengthy time spent fishing in PoStorms, I'm looking for tips on how to avoid getting the 'land sharks' message.

I've tried:

-leving
-taking lev off
-sitting
-fishing on the wrecks
-fishing on the grass
-fishing in the water
-fishing with inventory open
-fishing with inventory closed
-pointing deeper into the water
-looking at the sky

WHEREVER I sit (or stand) and whatever my view, land sharks seem to be a random occurrence... I finally decided to change position when I had a run of three sharks messages. I must have tried 50 different positions.

At one point I had a theory that if my cursor wasn't hovering over water (ie, interface, land, chat box) I'd get the message. But that doesn't follow through.

It's driving me nuts, can anyone help?
