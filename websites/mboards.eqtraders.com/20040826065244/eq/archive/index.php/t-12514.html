Azirux
Success% = (skill - (trivial*.75)) + 51.5

(37 - (37*.75)) + 51.5 = 61% success rate.

So you answer is; you are not doing any wrong, and yes, you need to level and skill up.
GraemeFaelban
Right idea, wrong formula.

Success = Skill - trivial + 66 for trivial <68.

So 37-37+66 = 66.

66% success rate should be expected.

Conclusion was correct, you need to get your skill higher, for which you will need to level soon, as your current maximum skill is 40 at your level.
