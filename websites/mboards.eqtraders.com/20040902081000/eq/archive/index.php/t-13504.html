Race - Deity - Extended Fletching

Wood elf Bards can worship almost anyone: Agnostic, Bristlebane, Karana, Erollisi Marr, Mithaniel Marr, Rodcet Nife, Prexus, Quellious, Solusek Ro, Brell Serilis, The Tribunal, Tunare, Veeshan, Rallos Zek.

Human Rangers can be: Karana or Tunare
Half elf Rangers can be: Karana or Tunare
Halfling Rangers can be: Karana
