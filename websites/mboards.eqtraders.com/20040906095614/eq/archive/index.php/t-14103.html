Greyhoppers are a random spawn throughout the zone. I farm my LQ pelts for paddings there, and being a mage I just send my pet absolutely everything in sight, to keep the spawn cycle going. If you didn't see any grey hoppers and were the only one in zone, it could be that someone was farming greyhoppers (only) earlier. If they were only targeting the greyhoppers, you might not see many up just on sure respawn odds. What I'd do is start whacking everything in the zone. Even when I was farming there were times that I noticed a decrease in population and then all of a sudden the whole zone seemed to be filled with them.

Hope that helps. I've farmed well around 1000 pelts between greyhopper hides and LQ pelts in mseru.

Slight corrections

There are some named hoppers in Mons Letalis (Furious, Raging, etc) that will agro on you, but the good news is that they are lower level than the rest.

If you are fighting An Adult Rockhopper or A Mature Rockhopper, Rockhoppers will join in the battle against you. Practice careful pulling, and you should be OK.

If you are fighting A Stonegrabber, A Mature Rockhopper will assist.

And watch out for the Shiknar Hatchlings...
