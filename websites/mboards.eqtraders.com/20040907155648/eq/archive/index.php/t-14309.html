Mileron
Most of the NPC buyable spells for Rangers, SKs, and Paladins are 'lower' level versions of spells from their parent class.

If you were to look through each list...

Beastlord - 13 of 37 are vendor bought, and are class-specific. 2 of 37 are dropped and class-specific. One is quested; one is LDoN. The remaining 20 are shaman hand-me-downs.

Paladins - 31 spells... Two are quested, two are LDoN, nine are class-specific. Of those, nine, seven are dropped. The remaining 18 are cleric.

Rangers - 30 spells. One quested, two LDoN. Eight class specific, for which all are dropped, the remaining 20 are druid handmedowns.

SKs - 39 spells!. One quested, two LDoN. Seventeen! are class specific, ten of which are dropped, leaving 19 necro handme downs.

So that leaves 2 beastlord, seven pal, eight ranger, and ten SK spells which all could be researched.

That doesn't even get into Bards.
Bards - 31 songs. One quested, two LDoN. Nine are dropped. The remaining 19 are vendored. I didn't even count the two Ancient songs.

I certainly wouldn't mind researching some of these, because no one really hunts PoG or FG or Velious with enough regularity anymore to make these more readily available.

But where would we start?
