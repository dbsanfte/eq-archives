Litetta
You need to be 61+ to start. Int/Wis cap goes up by 5 per level starting at 61. So at 65 caps would be 280 without any AA. Beyond that you get to spend AA's. All classes get planar power, which will raise all stat caps by another 5 points, 5 levels available. Int/Wis casters also get Innate Enlightenment which raises the int/wis cap by 10 per rank, 5 ranks. So after spending lots of AA's, you can raise cap by another 75 with those AA's maxxed out, bringing total cap to 355.
