Back to the original question: What pieces should I make?

The answer, of course, is all of them.

When I was skilling through the lower 200s, I would keep 3 of all the pieces on my trader. The only exception was the "skillup" combines (bracers, masks, etc). I felt that many people come to the Bazaar with a list of several items to buy. If I have all of them, they are more likely to buy from me, even if my prices are not the absolute lowest.

Replace the ones that sell, and after a month, you will have made more of the ones that sell on your server than the ones that don't sell. Keep the others on your trader after you are done with Acrylia, and you will eventually sell them. Even if you don't, it will be a small fraction of the ones you have sold.

This doesn't work, however, for items that are expensive to make -- unless you are rich enough to keep a full inventory.
