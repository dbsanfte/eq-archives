I was just looking over the Trade Supplies for Tinkering (http://www.eqtraders.com/sources/source_trade_page.php?st=Tinkering&menustr=110140000000) page and noticed some errors.

Fishing Pole (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=1199&menustr=110140000000): Is not an item used in tinkering nor are any recipes using it listed.

Clockwork Carapace (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=5239&menustr=110140000000): I am 90% certain that this item drops only in Plane of Innovation from A Defective Clockwork (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10141) and not in Bastion of Thunder.

Mana Battery - Class Five (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=4161&menustr=110140000000): 90% Certain that this drops only in Plane of Innovation from various monsters and does not drop in Bastion of Thunder

Gnomish Compass (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=4114&menustr=110140000000): This item is tinkered only and cannot be vendor purchased. The vendor info is for the Compass not the Gnomish Compass.

Small Lantern (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=4177&menustr=035000000000): Did not show up in the list at all.

Holgresh Fur (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=2108&menustr=035000000000): Needs to be listed on the Trade Supplies for Tinkering page under Wakening Lands

Faun Hoof (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=4166&menustr=035000000000): These drop from A Faun and A Faun Outcast (the outcasts have no faction hit) in the Wakening Lands.

Sifaye Dust (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=4163&menustr=035000000000): Drops from various Sifaye in the Wakening Lands.

Thats a whole bunch of changes, so if anyone can confirm them before the hardworking folks at EQTraders put them in the database it would be great. When most of these have been confirmed I will repost over in the database submissions forum.
