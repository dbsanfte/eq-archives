mewkus
The only way to get skill ups (past the 54ish you can get from GoD quest) is by attempting combines. Prior to the introduction of the Tanaans, only one of the seven skills could exceed 200. With each Tanaan AA you buy (at 3AAs each) you are allowed to have an additional tradeskill exceed 200. Buying the Tanaan does not provide any skill ups, it just removes the 200 cap. Whichever tradeskill hits 201 next is the one that counts.
