Actually...
Believe it or not, there was a thread on the old board a while back in which people kept suggesting the old Erudite cultural as a viable method for skilling up. I think they just kept inaccurately comparing it to the Wood Elf and Halfling cultural tailoring, which for quite a while, was the single most sane method to get 200+ tailoring skill (i.e. in the days between the release of Luclin and when Crystalline Silk was introduced). I had to keep shooting down the idea of skilling up on Erudite Cultural, but from some of the posts, I still think one or two people may have remained blithely ignorant.
So, yes, some people do think its a good way to skill up, but I don't think those people have the slightest idea of what's involved in making it.
