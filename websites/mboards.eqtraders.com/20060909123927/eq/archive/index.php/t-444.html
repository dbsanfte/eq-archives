There were a number of threads on the old boards about oak bark groundspawn locations in Butcherblock. One included a very good map.

One location I've found groundspawn is in the Northeast corner of the zone. Turn left when leaving Kaladim.

Oak bark can also be foraged in Kithicor and Warslik's Woods.

In BBM look for oak bark:

1. To the south of the path between x-roads and gfay, the whole area spawns it, all the way to the south edge of zone.

2. The cove in the northeast corner of the zone, where the goblins are.

3. The triangular area between the bandit camp, the new aqua goblins and the dock entrance, to include on the hills and in the vallies.
