I've been doing velious tailoring mostly to get skill ups. I buy reasonably priced hides in the bazaar and farm them myself being a druid. To get velium and a little cash i rip apart CC and since my farming partner is a chanter he could sell there before the faction change. In theory acrylia is the best way to go but from my experience, the actual acrylia is hard to get. Unless you can get a group in AC. The Sambata hardly drop it (I've noticed a better flawless/superb hide drop ratio from hoppers than acrylia from the Sambata) and farming in TM can be tough with all the wolves and bats that hang around.

My advice to you would be do everything you can get your hands on. On days when you have nothing to do, gather components from velious and bank them for later, farm and buy as much acrylia as you can get. I've never tried solstice robes but I hear that if you fail on them a lot its not as expensive as succeeding making a robe. But in the end it's all going to get you points.
