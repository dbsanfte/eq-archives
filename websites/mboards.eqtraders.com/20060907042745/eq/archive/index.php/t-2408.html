Acrylia doesn't drop very commonly. I have never been to AC or near it personally so i don't know what kind of drops you can get there. I farm in TM (usually the first cave ie not the laborer cave). I spent 3 to 4 hours there and usually come out with 4 or 5 bricks/blocks and a few pieces. Stick with it though. Look for umbracite and luclinite, they are stones that sell for 114 pp and 80 pp respectively. If you want to GM tailoring you're going to need the money. All the other stones sell between 2 and 12 pp and windstones drop there rarely. You can sell those to blacksmithers for nice money. The weapons that drop sell like fine steel and there is a rune that sells for 9pp. I can make nearly 2k in just a few hours of farming. If you are not KoS to Katta i suggest you bind yourself in one of the caves and use Egress to gate to Katta. Sell you crap and bank and gate back to the cave and do it all again. You get acrylia and cash and hopefully your drop luck improves.
