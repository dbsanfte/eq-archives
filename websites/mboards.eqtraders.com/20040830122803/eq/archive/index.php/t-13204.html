Easiest place to farm HQ Brute hides thusfar for me has been inside the cave in warsilik woods that leads to dalnir.

There are 5 steady spawn brutes that respawn every 15 mins (approx) This can be very boring, however I found the drop rate of hq brute hides to be reasonable, as well as some coin and various words / runes.

It's very boring, but the drop rate on hq brute hides was about 1 per every two clearings - somedays I was able to get more.

It certainly beats dealing with aggro greenies in FM or even more difficult aggro in DL -

Also, if you are using these for skillups, dont waste your time trying to make the high end cultural until you are near 250 skill as it's very expensive (blue diamonds) and failure rates for me even at 250 skill have been around 50 percent. Personally I would just /auction wtb them everywhere you go -
Good luck :)
