I wouldn't be too concerned. You will go through many periods where the RNG will bite you, but it will even out with many single digit combines/skillups. I noticed a significant skillup decline beginning in the 180s with 255wis and 180+ strength. You are now entering what appears to be the dog-days of smithing, but be patient and you'll get through it. You may even go through a few 100+ dry spells before it's all said and done.

btw, I've recently gone through a streak like this at 218
213......14 combines
214.....108 combines
215......8 combines
216......12 combines
217......63 combines
218......2 combines

The 108 and 63 are the longest runs w/o skilling up for me so far. While i've seen much nastier ones out there on these boards. So even though I curse the hammer, I'm a little thankful at the same time.
