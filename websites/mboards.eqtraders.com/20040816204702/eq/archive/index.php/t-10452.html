Bigmeeno
With PoP, you can spend AA points in New Tanaan Crafting Mastery on the general page of your AA window, and become a grandmaster in all 7 tradeskills.

There are also high end quests in PoKnowledge where the NPC wont talk to you about the next step of the quest until you have at least 220 skill in the tradeskill used for that step of the quest.

Class skills, such as alchemy, do not count against your tradeskill count. So yes, you can take Alchemy to 200, and still GM in a tradeskill. And do this before you spend any AA points like I mentioned above. Poison Making for rogues, Tinkering for gnomes, and probably others also do not apply to your tradeskill count.
