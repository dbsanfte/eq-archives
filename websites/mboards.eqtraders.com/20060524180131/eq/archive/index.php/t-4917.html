There are many scammers out there but there are also ways to protect yourself.

1. Talk to people in the guild that have purchased any of the Cultural Armor on your server. In the bazaar you can search for any of the Diety Armor sellers easily who day in and day out leave themselvs logged in and usually selling full suits of armor.

2. Join the Bazaar channel and ask in the channel if anyone has worked with said smith. Chances are you will recieve some feed back from someone who has or at least a recommendation on another smith who is available.

3. If the character is stripped down and is lower than level 30 ask the person who thier main is. Chances are that any serious smith would have no problems telling you the name of thier main. If the said smith is in anonomous and wont tell you the name of thier main account or toon chances are its a scam.

4. Take screen shots of all transactions and ALWAYS keep logs on. Most GM''s cant do a DAM thing on a normal petition but if you have screen shots and the log on you can email that directly to the server GM for action and correspond with the GM in email to make sure that something has come out of your loss.

5. Finally for combines please post here in the tradeskill section of EQTRADERS. Most smiths who are GM have the name of thier toons listed in the GM tradeskill list.

Sorry you took a loss but never ever take anyones word for face value in this game.
