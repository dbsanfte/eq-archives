I just put up some info on it on the LoY alchemy recipes page:

These potions have a 3-hour duration, and offer a 5% mana reduction on spells of the proper type and level. These focus effects stack with the focus effects on items. Awareness I affects spells of level 20 and below; awareness II affects spells of level 44 and below; awareness III affects spells of level 60 and below and awareness IV affects spells of level 65 and below

So, heat awareness I would give a 5% mana reduction on fire-based spells of level 20 or below, benefit awareness I would give a 5% mana reduction on beneficial spells (buffs, heals), etc., etc.

Hope that helps!

Just as an FYI: the term "based" means that you gain the benefits based on what the spell is resisted against.

For example: poison awareness III will give the 5% reduction for Gale of Poison (poison aoe rain spell), Bane of Nife (poison DoT) and Shock of Poison (poison DD).

This is a very important distinction that has caused some confusion. A fellow shaman was confused as to why the Disease awareness wasn't working on his slow spell. The slow spell is removed by cure disease, but it is still resisted based on Magic so it is reduced by Magic Awareness instead of Disease Awareness.
