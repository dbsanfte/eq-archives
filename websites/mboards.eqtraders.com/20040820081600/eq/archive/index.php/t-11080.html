Normbow
I stumbled onto this when I decided to use the Steel Arrowheads to get my Smithing up to 188 (I was at 135 at the time). I figured I'd save whatever I made and then make a comparable number of shafts and Fletches and work my Fletching up from there.

I took about 1500pp into Surefall and started working. I noticed that someone else was using the forge, so I alternated with him figuring he was doing the same as me. By the time I ran out of money I had quite a few Arrowheads and a skill of 171. I was just going to go get more money to finish off the trivial, but I decided to see what the vendor's buyback was.

All prices are rounded. Each small steel brick cost 6.2 pp and the buyback price on the Arrowheads was 4.75 pp per. With the recipe making 2 Arrowheads, you could make 3.3 pp on a successful combine.

I probably made about 1.5 Kpp getting those last 17 points in Smithing. It sure was nice to be able to skill up and make money at the same time. It was also nice being able to use the 16 stacks of leather padding I had accumulated (to do Fine Plate) for something else.
