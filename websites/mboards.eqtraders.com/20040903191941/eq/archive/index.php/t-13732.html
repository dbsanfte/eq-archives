I just heard from another website that two VERY interesting things are happening.

1) Phone support has been moved to India.

2) The Guide program will be discontinued, with people in India taking their place for /petition weeding. (Since for most of those living in India English is not their first language they will NOT be responding to petitions, only grading them and forwarding them to GMs for response.)

I don't know that 2) is true, and if not ... all the better.

But I decided to do a little math, since phone tech support used to be my job, and software development (a LOT of which is being shipped over to India these days) is the focus of me going back to school for my B.S. degree in Comp Sci.

Supposedly there are 1000 guides currently who will be phased out. They are required to work 6, and usually average more like 10, hours per week.

That's about 10,000 man-hours a week. (sorry, that's the gender neutral use of "man" please don't be offended, I've worked with lots of great women in phone support and the Guide Program both)

If you figure that the Guides are replaced with people working 60 hours a week (and why not? no 40-hour work week in India) that comes out to 165 (roughly) Indian workers replacing 1000 guides.

Now if we ASSUME that the guides, who currently only get free accounts, were to be "getting out of paying" 12.99 (13 for round numbers) a month...

(NOTE: I was a Guide for a little over 3 months. I had renewed my account just before I became a Full Guide for six months. When I left the guide program, surprise surprise... less than 2 months later I had to renew again. My compensation for guiding... ZERO.)

(NOTE: Most guides would have a CLUE and pay for longer terms than Month-to-Month, but we'll let that slide.)

12 * 13 * 1000 = 156,000 dollars. That's a hunk of money.

156,000 / 165 = 945 dollars per Indian guide replacement.

Hmm... need to save some bucks... so let's pay them

800 dollars a year per.

The "owners" of the outsourcing company are going to take a cut. Let's be nice and presume it's only 10%

720 dollars a year. Ouch. That's not a lot for working 60 hours a week.

720 dollars a year translates to 32,562 rupees a year. Go here to check (http://www.xe.com/ucc/)

Now according to The US Department of State (http://www.state.gov/r/pa/ei/bgn/3454.htm) the average per capita income for India is about 480 dollars a year.

And according to the first place I found here (http://www.iowaworkforce.org/trends/percapita.html) the average per capita income for the United States is closer to 30,900 dollars a year.

720 / 480 = 1.5 times the "average" Indian income.

1.5 * 30,900 = 46,350

So moving from 1000 Guides to 165 Indians "saves" SOE ( (945 - 800) * 165) = 23,925 dollars a year. And lifts 165 Indians from what most Americans would call "abject poverty" to a "living wage" while for the Indians the 165 "new employees" are living on a "princely income" nearly DOUBLE that of their neighbors.

We can presume similar savings for transfering the phone support people from California to India.

40 U.S. phone support people at 30k = 1.2 million plus benifits
400 Indian phone support people at 800 = 320,000 and no benifits

400 Indian families now live at 1.5 times the average income.

SOE turns about 1 million bucks more profit.

40 U.S. families now have to look for new jobs. And remember. They are phone support people. And I can buy 10 times as many man-hours for 1/4 the raw cost and no benifits by hiring outsourcing from India.

Excuse me while I go be violently ill. :steamed:
