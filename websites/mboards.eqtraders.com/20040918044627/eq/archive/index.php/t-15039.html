Osprey
They are nodrop. Both are the results of quests.

The vein rot sketch is a small quest. The master sketch is made by combining four sketches (including vein rot) in a sketch book.


The full details of the quests are over at .The Safehouse (Laboratory forum) (http://pub146.ezboard.com/fthesafehouselaboratory.showMessage?topicID=2485.topic) or Corinari's guide to advanced poison (http://home.comcast.net/~corinari/) which came from the research done at The Safehouse but is a lot more readable.

Short story: the vein rot sketch is one of the more troublesome ones. You need a Burning Rapier to do it, which is an involved quest in itself (unless you have one in the bank already).
