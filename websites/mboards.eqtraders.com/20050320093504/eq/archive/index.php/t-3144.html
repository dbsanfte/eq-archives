*snicker* When I first read the topic, I thought Lilosh finally went off the deep end. ;)

Robe of the Foci is a pretty nice looking Enchanter only robe. I believe it drops in Dalnir, but I'm sure you could find one in the Bazaar for not too much. Unfortunately, part of the problem with robes is that they don't show up in a lot of Illusions and you end up looking shirtless in any that can't wear a robe.

Golden Efreeti Boots are good for the yellow shoe look, but generally shoes will only be black or brown that I've found. Gloves are another hard one, but Coldain Skin Gloves are black/bluish and look fairly nice.

Hats are very limiting as well. Basically casters have a choice between no visual hat or leather hat. If you prefer a leather hat (which will give you a tail in elemental form), Siryn Hair Hood is a really nice one for Enchanters. It's a long camp (in Rathe Mts.), but still a really nice item.

Hope that helps!
