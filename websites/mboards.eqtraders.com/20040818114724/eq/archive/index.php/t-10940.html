Lilosh
Go to the Gulf of Gunthak.

Find Research Alvaro (or something similar, starting with A), and hail him.

Then say "I am an enchanter" (Or, if you're on a different caster character "I am a wizard", or whatever..)

Poof, you have the 8 slot, new research container for your class.
