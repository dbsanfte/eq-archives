You are combining in the wrong container. The initial combines for the runes azia and beza are made in the Concordance of research book which you get via a quest. You then combine the azia and beza runes in your standard dresearch book (dark bindings for you I think).

You will fail quite a bit on research combines until your skill exceeds the trivial a bit. Unfair, yes; we've all been ranting about it for some time. Your options are to buy spell in the bazaar, gather words and ask a higher skill level necro to combine, or keep plugging away.

In order to combine the materials for an Azia or Beza practice rune in the Concordance of Research, you must place all the items *plus* a Quill and Parchment in the Tome. Voila, a practice rune is born.

So, for the one you're attempting, insert:
o lapis lazuli
o bat wing
o bone chips
o Quill
o Parchment

Repeat until you are ready to order a new robe (you know, the one with the extra long sleeves that tie 'round back)...
