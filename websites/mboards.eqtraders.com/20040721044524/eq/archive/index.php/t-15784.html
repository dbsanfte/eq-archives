Last night (OK, technically very early morning today), right around 3:00am, I was finally greeted by a message I didn't expect to see.

You have become better at Pottery! (250)

Pottery was the last skill I had left, and I had been saving it for the end because I thought it was easy. Barring one hell level at 227, skillups were fast and furious, oftentimes two or three per stack of combines. The only really irritating part was finding enough imbued gems, mana vials, and magic clay to keep churning out those unfired items! =) I think I kept half of Veeshan's enchanter population busy over the last couple of days making the needed vials and clay, though I had enough imbued emeralds in the bank left over from my tailoring and smithing days.

I'd like to extend a very hearty thanks to EQTraders, particularly the DenMom and the House Ogre. Without this site and especially the new database, I would never have been able to reach this milestone.

It's been an amazing run, and it feels great to have a dream long fulfilled.
