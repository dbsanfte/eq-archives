Originally posted by Tejaye
Can you safely walk through Plane of Mischief alone at lvl 55?
If so...where is the exact entrance and exits and from what zones?

The occupants of the woods in PoM don't see through invis, so I always enter that way. To get there, go to Great Divide and follow the glowing river all the way to the South end of the zone. You will see a miniature castle in the water; click on it and you will zone in to PoM. You will find yourself in a small raised gazebo-looking thing. Face due North and click on the ground in the middle of the woods. You should see a message like "You can't reach that from here". That's the Cobalt Scar zonein. Walk toward that area clicking on the ground until you zone in to CS. You will be underwater at loc 0,0 in CS. Swim East to get to the Othmir area of the beach.
