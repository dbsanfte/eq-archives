DING 250 Baking on Povar!

I got bored of grinding out AA's early last week, and decided to do some tradeskilling. My baking at the time was 199 and I decided I was going to try doing some MTP's, and wow, it worked out great!

I had a set up in my bank, with most of it being 10 slot toolboxes for space....

I'd prepare sub combines, so that I could do 300 MTP combines at a single time. THus requiring 1.5 toolboxes for 300 of each component, except for The Cake, which required 3 total tool boxes.

I found that it cost about 500 PP for these 300 combines...and the wooven mandrake for me was the worst part, since My tailoring is so low.

So over the week I'd do 2-3 batches of 300 a day, and in a week or so, I went quickly to 250. With only 230 Wis(I'm a Ranger).

Got myself a ton of MTP's, sold them all for 10PP each and made roughly 30K just skilling up!

I love Baking!
