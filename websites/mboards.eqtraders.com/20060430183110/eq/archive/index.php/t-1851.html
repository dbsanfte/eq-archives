Go to neriak 3rd gate, find the fur shop. Put your back to it, head through the gate. On the left wall (past Maiden's fancy) you will see a portcullis gate (mold vendor and forge is there) then an opening. Go through opening, take a right, up the bridge, at the gatehouse right again (inside the gatehouse), left at the end of the corridor (2nd left), vendor is there.
