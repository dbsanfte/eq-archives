Right now, the most desirable drink in the game is Qeynos Afternoon Tea, and the difficulty to get the ingredients for this is low at best.

The GOD Teas are much much harder to acquire ingredients, and the stats simply don’t measure up (As bazaar sales have borne out.)

We either need better stats on the GOD teas, or make them a little easier to aquire.

Also we need more varied stat drinks, and they have to be able to compete with Qeynos tea. If they are harder to get than Qeynos tea, then there must be a compelling reason to make them. Simply increasing one stat to 7 from 5 and then removing 2 or 3 other +5 stats is not an incentive at all, add to that the fact that the +7 is FAR harder to get, and you simply increase the desirability of Qeynos tea.

And don’t put the ingredients in places that take a raid to get to, you simply eliminate all of the non power gamers by doing that.

Stugein
Make brewing slightly more of a challenge to GM
This may not be popular, but hear me out. While tailoring and smithing are both almost ridiculous in cost to GM with respect to both farming and/or platinum invested, brewing is their complete opposite. Brewing is the only tradeskill that one can skill from 0-240+ on a single recipe that uses common storebought ingredients that costs virtual pennies per stack. There is no sense of accomplishment from dropping a few plat on FE and MHB. When you hit 250 smithing it's like, "Wow. I achieved something." Same with most of the other skills. With brewing 250 is just another skillup.

- Significantly lower the trivial on Fetid Essence.
- Signifcantly lower the trivial on Minotaur Hero Brew.

More varied stat drinks and duration drinks at all levels of skill

- A "fish rolls" drink (longer lasting, no stats) for lower skills. Example:
~ Treghost's Sweetberry Flask
~ Trivial: 112 - Lasting Drink
* 1 x Water Flask
* 1 x Cup of Sugar
* 1 x Wild Blueberries
* 1 x Bottle

- More low-to-midlevel drinks for +stats, perhaps catering to specific classes/roles, for example:
~ Dismal Rage Black Tea
~ STA+3, INT+3
~ Trivial: 90 - Refreshing Drink
* 1 x Emerald Tea Leaf
* 1 x Elven Blood
* 2 x Water Flask

- Drinks with varied stats at the high-end of the skill spectrum. Situational drinks, just like folks have situation gear ("I'm going to be MT for this LDoN...time to switch out my +ATK drink for my +HP/STA drink"), rather than having one or two "one-size-fits-all" drink items.

Give more benefit to consuming alcoholic drinks and make benefits/detriments directly related to the alcohol tolerance skill.
Right now alcoholic drinks are for in-game RP or more of a "Gee-Whiz" than any really useful purpose (no that isn't a knock against RP'ers).

- The +STA and +STR effect from consuming alcohol should vary depending on the strength of the brew (A point or two for a simple short-beer, more for some toe-curling nostril-burning concoction from Brodder's private stash).

- The blurred vision and slurred speech should be lessened more significantly for folks with higher AT skill.

- The INT/WIS loss should be lessened more significantly for folks with higher AT skill.

- In addition to STR/STA, add +ENDURANCE as a benefit to consuming alcoholic brews. Also to scale depending on the strength of the drink.

Just ideas.

I DONT GET STATS WHEN IN MY AMMO SLOT ! This is intentional, Coas. The devs posted in another thread that the ammo slot is purely for holding stuff and for knowing which ammo to use when you use a ranged weapon. (If you're carrying three types of arrows, which should the game use when you fire your bow?)

Due to a bug with how fishing bait works, you can now get a skill modifier from your ammo slot, so you can get the brewing +5% if you equip your corker in the ammo slot. The stats will not work in that slot, by design.
