I'm not exactly overflowing with the rarer words so I assume the ribbons will virtually be a complete lost.

Tinile
I did platinum ribbons until 187 when they trivialled. I found the skill ups fast and refreshing. They may have slowed down since, I did them just after LoY came out and was at about 163 when I started. As a wood elf, I cannot say anything about making the robes afterwards.
Hibashira
Platinum ribbon is way easier IMO. Since it closer to trivial, successful combine makes it much lower cost than Solstice robe. Not to mention you have more requirement to do solstice robe efficiently (namely 250 JC, someone to enchant silver and imbue emeralds).

For the platinum ribbons, I simply sell back all the successful combines to vendor and take the lost. Its still cheaper than any other combines out there.
Kellaen
I just did gold + plat ribbons from 131-187 yesterday. I started with 168 steamfont dyes, and 59 jaggedpine dyes. 131-162 cost me 117 steamfont dyes, 163-182 used up another 40 steam dyes plus 8 jaggedpine ones. 183-187 took just over two stacks of jp dyes, 41 in total. It left me with 8 steams and 10 jp's if my figures are correct.

Total cost was just under 12k including ribon sell backs. They were about 16pp cost per golds and 87 pp for plats. I'd gladly sink another 20-30k if I could do this past 200, 210, etc. Ribbons were by far the fastest imp route for me to take. I've done wu's 5 times previous (shawl8's) and wasn't touching that crap again.

So far with robes it's 6 imps from 187-193 on 93 combines, 4 successes >< @ 305 dex 264 wis.

Definately do ribbons. Find someone who two-boxes in our guild who wouldn't mind setting up one of their foragers in a zone that has your ribbon components (can't remember your race) and have them hit the forage button while we raid or while they are exping.

Then use the ribbons you make for cultural robes; that is if you are a race that has cultural robes, since I cannot remember (DE I believe, which I think get cultural robes).

I am sure someone in the guild will be more than happy to do this for you, and you will save a lot of time and money this way. If you do not do ribbons you are going to waste alot of money and components on < 187 combines that you want to spend on 187+ combines.

Oh, and read up on the boards. Almost all the questions you will have will already be contained in these threads.
