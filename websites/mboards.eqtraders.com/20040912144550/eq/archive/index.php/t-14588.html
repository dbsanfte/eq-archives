If you use the self gate, you end up in the "safe spot" of the zone you cast it from.

If you use the group spell, anyone with GoD gets sent to Natimbi, anyone without GoD gets sent to the safe spot of the zone you cast it from, including you.

If you use the Translocate, anyone with GoD gets sent to Natikmbi, anyone without GoD get sent to the safe spot of the zone you cast it from, assuming they click yes on the confirmation box.
