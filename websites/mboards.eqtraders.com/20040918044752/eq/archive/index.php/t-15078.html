Just a thought on this topic.
Making sure all toons are lvl 1 (option to put newbie points in to fishing) and give them 15mins Fishing time in each zone you choose.
Put them in teams of 3 or so ( Makes fishing alot nicer if you are in group and have someone to chat to.)

Try and pick zones that have something unique about them. i.e OOT has seaweed, CS has Cod.

Give people bonus points if they catch a specific zone item, and say 1 point per fresh fish...Try and stick to things stackable as level 1's are limited to 1 backpack..in other words those rusty daggers and shoes get tossed back in.

Might take a bit to research where and what fish..Do a test run and fish a few areas as well as a safe place for a lvl 1 toon.

Don't forget FV has different size fish too. The bigger the fish the more points they get =D..

Nairn Niteraven
You could split it up into 2 separate contests, porting classes and non-porting, and no level/fishing level restiction. Announce it in plenty of time to let anybody get Fishing up that wants to. Give the porters 30 minutes, only restriction is no Fishing modifiers, and give them one point for each "rare" catch; the rest is worth nothing. They can go anywhere they want, but porting cost some time.

The non-porting classes would require some thought on the starting point. Start in a non-rare zone, but have as many as possible rare zones nearby with the same 30 minute limit.

With this method, you won't eliminate many at first, but you could play it like a tournament, winners advance to the next round, same rules.
