Check vendors. Thurgadin, Shadow Haven, PoK, PoT, and the Bazaar sometimes have them if you spend time checking many vendors. Unless you're in a hurry, you should be able to get all you need this way within a few weeks or so, and much more cheaply than they're sold by players in the Bazaar. That's how I got all the jacinths, black sapphires, and diamonds for my smithing trophy attempt and three attempts at the alchemy trophy.

It's amazing what you can find on vendors tucked away in obscure corners of otherwise busy zones. Blue diamonds, rare (and I do mean rare) spells, tier 3+ PoP drops etc.
