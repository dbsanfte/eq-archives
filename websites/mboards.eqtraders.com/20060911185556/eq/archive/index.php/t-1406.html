i would recommend skinning the MQ pelts to LQ, but not the HQ, either sell them, or use them to get your tailor5ing up to 116 by making quivers.

now if your ultimate goal is to up your smithing, skinning the HQ down to LQ will get you your padding, but it would be a BIG waste of money. Sell the HQ for 25-35p then buy those LQ for 15p, you'll still make 10-20 plat to finance you ore purchases.

If all you really want is LQ, and you are of a level(above 45ish depending on class) where it is feasable Stonebrunt is your answer. The Cats there drop a LQ or better pelt about 80% of the time, and about 40% of the time drop more than one. If you can sustain the farming(this works especially well for high level trackers) the numbers there can be staggering. Then just downgrade all the HQ/MQ(or better yet make a profit bazaaring them) and you have your pelt supply.
