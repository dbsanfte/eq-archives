Melinia
Snake eggs: any where you find snakes they can and will drop. Check merchants near where you would find snakes, great place to merchant mine them ( Freeport, Qeynos, Butcherblock, East Karanas, Ect.) Foraging there will help to. Snake eggs only yield 1 dough tho. good for skill ups.. bad for subcombines for the bigger stuff. Good for making noodles aswell.

Aviak eggs : Foraged will yield less dough than dropped. Kill or check merchants in and around Butcherblock and South Karanas along with Lake Rathe. Good for the sub combines. Do not waste on the skill ups.

Basilik eggs : (sorry for spelling) Yields much dough when looted rather than foraged. Kill or check merchants in and around Jaggedpine, Butcherblock Mountains, Lavastorm. Great for sub-combines but do not waste on the skill ups.

Bazaar : People may have them on sale. Dont jump at the first one you see.. some people will charge outragous prices for something they foraged up. Keep an eye out for some good prices.. they will come.

There are other eggs you can use but these are the most common and farely easy to find in your general travels. Most eggs can be foraged but will yield less dough except for snake eggs.. either will yield only 1 dough I believe.
