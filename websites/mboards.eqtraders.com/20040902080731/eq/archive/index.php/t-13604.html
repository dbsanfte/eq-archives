Sabine
Fiend guts are rarely sold in the bazaar since they only drop to raid targets, and the staves and metal are as common as the rain (to exp mobs).

Each time a guild gets 2 guts, that's an attempt at a bow (about 40% chance of success with max skill). To estimate a price for a gut, take the price of an Obsidianwood bow, and divide by 5.

If an Obsdianwood bow costs 100k in the bazaar, I would expect guts to be about 20k. 40k gets you the two you need to make a string. The other components (staff and ore) run about 100pp on my server. If you suceed at bows 40% of the time, you break even buying guts at 20k, if Obsidianwoods sell for 100k in your marketplace.

People have interesting ideas on pricing for things. Stonewood bowstaves can be 10k or 35k in the bazaar. I chuckle when I see three vendors selling vegerog vines for 40k each, since the parts to attempt a stonewood would be just under the cost of a finished bow. Somehow I suspect that the vines don't sell too quick at that price...
