I've recently found my new home for tradeskilling. This place has everything you need (I think, I've yet to prove this but so far so good) on a merchant, plenty of banks, plenty of general stuff (water flasks, etc.), and is extremely tradeskill friendly despite its out of the way location on the world map.

This place is the Abysmal Sea.

Once you get to know the layout and location of the merchants and tradeskill equipment, you will praise this zone. Silverfish
Ah, pies. As my other posts suggest, I'm quite fond of pies. The best place I've found for pies is Shadow Haven, near the square pool (the port to and from the Nexus). The alchemy merchants stand very near a baking merchant, who is near the merchant who sells lion and bear meat. There are two ovens in close reach, so you're never waiting in line to bake, and two banks. Bring your own eggs, wolf and mammoth meat, and you can find everything else without ever having to go past Shandling's Roost. Need those eggs or meats? You can hit the Bazaar a few steps away.

All in all it's a wonderful place for all of the tradeskills. Even tinkers don't get shorted here (pardon the pun). It's less crowded than the PoK (I've never had to wait for a pottery wheel or brew barrel here) and still has it all. The only down side is for you folks on PvP servers. Not a safe zone, so plan accordingly. Remember that there's one oven in close proximity to five guards, so get your faction up and they'll cover you.
