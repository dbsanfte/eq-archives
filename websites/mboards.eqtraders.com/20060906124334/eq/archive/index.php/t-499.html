DSP Sambata / Hopper Caves

If the Sambatas are up you have to fight your way to the bottom of the cave and kill the Tribal Advisor (40 - 40)

If the Rock Hoppers are up you have to fight you way to the bottem of the Cave and kill the An Age Old Rockhopper (50 - 51)

Warning, if people are killing one or the other please be polite enough to ask them if they mind if you do this and if you do switch it back for them if you can talk them into it.
