Kelaan
The muffin quest will fix all your earthside factions that were damaged in Plane of Growth, but it will never fix your faction with the mobs who live in of Plane of Growth (Faction is called Servants of Tunare). At present, there is no way to repair that faction, once you're KoS to them, you always will be.

Here is the guide I wrote for my Guildmembers on repairing factions after fighting in PoG.
______
Repairing your factions after PoG

Killing anything in Plane of Growth lowers the following four factions:

Clerics of Tunare
Faydark's Champions
Soldiers of Tunare
Servants of Tunare

Three of these four factions can be repaired in an hour or two! The fourth faction is only attached to npc/mobs within the Plane of Growth itself. If you wish to quest an item with an NPC IN PoG then you should not be hunting there until you've finished your quest because there will be no going back. However, for the rest of us who just want to be able to visit the guild trainers in our home town or complete our epics, here is the way to repair the other three factions:

In a nutshell, you need to hand in hundreds and hundreds of muffins to Pandos Flintside in Freeport West (see here: http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=611). This will raise the three Norrath-side factions, the ones that are stopping you from visiting some NPCs in Kelethin and Felwithe:

Clerics of Tunare
Faydark's Champions
Soldiers of Tunare
(plus raising King Tearis Thex and lowering Crushbone Orcs)

Pandos Flintside is on Faydark's Champions, as strange as that may seem. If you're KoS to Pandos (and you will be) you can
a) sneak if you can
b) get a druid or ranger's help to harmony him and invis-reinvis you which should allow you to do the hand in
c) Kill Crushbone orcs. Much more fun!

Killing Crushbone orcs raises the following three factions:

Stormguardians
Faydark's Champions
Emerald Warriors

One you're out of KoS to Pandos you can then do the muffin quest.

So, that's how to raise your Norrath-side factions after a slaughtering spree in PoG. There are many other ways to raise those three factions but from all reports this will be the fastest by a long way. All up, it should take a few hours at most.

Disclaimer: That said, don't forget that at the present time there is no way to raise your Servants of Tunare faction. If you have PoG quests you want to do, you will not be able to do them after killing even 3 or 4 regular mobs in PoG. If they introduce new quests or new NPCs on Servants of Tunare faction at any time, you'd better hope they introduce a way to fix the faction as well! Also, there are roleplay reasons for some people not to kill in PoG.

Hope all that helps some people.
