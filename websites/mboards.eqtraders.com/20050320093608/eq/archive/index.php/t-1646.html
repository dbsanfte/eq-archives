I don't know why I haven't posted here yet. I read more than I post anyway. This site has helped me amazingly and I give thanks to Denmom =) Thank you for helping me on the road to 250 in so many tradeskills. Now the message, which was cross posted to a few forums.

-------------
Just in case anyone cares...

I have completely lost confidence in Verant's ability to provide a fun game for my spare time. EQ is no longer fun for me or my husband. We cancelled all three of our accounts today.

I have become increasingly cynical/skeptical that anything can make this game enjoyable for me again, however if anyone is listening, here is what would help:

1) Expansions are supposed to expand the world, not shrink it. For the love of god, please make more exp zones for high level players.

2) Everyone should have an incentive to level, casters and melee alike. The parchment drop nerfs are unacceptable, as well as other issues that I know nothing about due to the fact that I do not play melees. Fixing parchment drops in tier 3 zones does not help, as most players cannot access those zones.

3) Fix the flag issues. Whether you are a member of an uber guild, or a casual player, each caste dislikes the way flags are done in this expansion.

I used to enjoy EverQuest, but it seems more like work when I log on anymore rather than fun. If many of the issues I feel are limiting my fun are not fixed within the next couple of patches I am leaving for good, and no, I am not selling my account. By doing so only gives Sony more money and to them it would be as if I had never left.

I would appreciate it if nobody flamed this post for whatever reason they feel they need to. This is just a tiny good bye. I still plan on reading the boards though. Who knows what may happen in the future?
-------

Again I have loved this community and will still read here from time to time. Good luck to all who seek the elusive 1750 and grats to those who discover new combinations. I am headed off to DAOC, where it seems to support the casual player more than EverQuest does. You can find me on Bedevere, Kinarie, level 30 scout.
