I've been quad-kiting extensivelyl in Halls of Honor, and get a lot of Hero Meat. I bake these into Hero Sandwiches which are one of the best stat foods available. I keep what I need and have the rest on my Bazaar mule.

I checked out a few sources. It seems that 70pp is considered a fair price per sandwich. I check the Povar board... There are 3 - 4 sellers all priced at 140 - 150 pp each. One trader is selling for 250 pp each.

*Shrug*. I don't usually like getting into an undercutting battle, but who would buy Hero Sandwiches at 140 pp or more? I put my price at 100 pp each.

After a week, I've only sold 9 sandwiches out of 100 (all sold to one person). I expected the other traders to drop their prices, but nope... they are still 140 - 150 pp and one die-hard is still at 250 pp.

I think I'm going to drop my price to 70 pp each. I've got enough hero meat for at least another 100 sandwiches.

What the heck is the point of having a bunch of traders keeping prices artificially high, when people won't buy at those high prices?

Lickity
The one thing you need to consider, is you may be pricing these against something like MTPs. These items are very low in price. The Hero Sandwiches do not sell to this same market. Hero Sandwiches are bought by people who force feed. These people do not buy as many as say the MTPs, that many do not bother to keep around by forcefeeding.

In short, lowering your price will not force them to sell faster. They sell slowly because of the nature of the food, they are not considered a "consumable" product, and thus the demand is diminished a ton. I have tested this with other spendy foods, and they sell as fast at 500 as they do at 50, its a matter of use.
