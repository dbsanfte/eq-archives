Silverfish
While you're hunting for spiderling silks, don't forget that shade silk thread and a LQ rockhopper hide makes leather padding too. Go to Shadeweaver's Thicket and the pit under Shar Vahl, and nuke the heck out of the xakra worms (SWT right near the PoK book, and all over the place under Shar Vahl). Once you've gotten a pile of shadeling silks for shade silk thread, go to Marus Seru and AE the greyhoppers until you have LQ rockhopper hides to match. Stitch 'em up, and you're on your way!

How is the drop rate in Shadeweaer's Thicket or Shar Vahl compared to the current Ferrott?

IMO, it's much better. You can farm shadeling silks very quickly in Shadeweavers, and also pick up some swirling shadows for shadowscream armor later on in your smithing life. There are hoppers in that zone that drop pelts, but the best one seems to be Marus Seru, where you can kill tons of hoppers for the pelts. Keep the high quality ones for tailoring backpacks, and reduce the MQ ones to LQ.

One thing to note, is that a low quality wolf pelt and a shadeling silk thread will not combine to make leather padding. You need to use low quality rockhopper pelts instead.
