I usually do my combines whereever is handy. Usually near the merchants I from whom I just purchased materials. Often this is PoK, Thurgadin or ShadowHaven.

As for buffs, they don't matter on success-rate. Stats don't affect the rate of sucess (see Stats & Tradeskills (http://mboards.eqtraders.com/upload/showthread.php?s=&threadid=19)). Stats only affect your chnace to gain a skill-increase. Since my skill is at the maximum possible, there isn't any point in trying to get a further increase. :)

I have had the occasional bad run. It happens. But, over the course of time, things tend to hit the average, and I'm saying that my experience with all things Jewel Craft shows me that nothing is broken. I'm seeing the number of successes I more or less expect.
