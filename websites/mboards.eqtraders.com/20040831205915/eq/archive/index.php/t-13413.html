Most "route" guides are geared towards 1 or 2 things: convenience (everything purchased at 1 zone) and simplicity (the fewer components, the better).

For lockpick, it is entirely about convenience. Zoning takes long. FG is 2 zones away from the nearest forge. Not to mention Echo Cavern is not a safe zone with green aggros. Molds are not stackable, so the less you have to travel to get them to the forge, the better.

One close skillup route is Scaler(41)-Dairy Spoon(74)-Fillet Knife(76). They use the same components, water, metal bits (x 1,2,or 3), and a scaler mold. All can be done in the same zone (PoK to name one).
