It's used to combine the four sketches you quest for into a single Master Sketch. The sketches are used as a catalyst (ie influence the combine without being used up) in various PoP poison combines.

If you want more details, go to the sticky at the top of this forum, and follow the link to Coinari's excellent Advanced Poisons guide, which includes details of all of the quests related to PoP poisoning.
