Verdandi
Nothing in the ammo slot gives stats. That's the way the ammo slot is designed. Its original purpose was simply to hold, well, ammo, like arrows or thrown weapons. As EQ evolved some other items were allowed in the ammo slot, like trophies or horse bridles/drogmor drums, presumably just to give people a convenient place to put those things. People who have other items they prefer in their primary, secondary, and range slots might like to put the trophy in ammo for convenient clicky access or just to show it off.
