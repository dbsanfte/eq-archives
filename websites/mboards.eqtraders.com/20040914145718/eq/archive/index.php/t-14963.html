Balkin
I did 0-250 in 3 days.

Fetid Essence to 122
Ol Tujim's Fiere Brew to 135
2X brewed 2X stout dwarven ale to 142
Faydwer Shakers to 188
******************************* The above was day one
Mino Hero's brew to 248
******************************* The above was day two
Kaladim Constitutionals to 250
******************************* Having a corker made, and doing KC's was day 3.

I then sold the 208 KC's I made (52% success in a 100 count run) for 15plat each, netting me a profit of aproximately 1.3K (after the cost of Skilling to 250, the price I paid for a corker, and the price to make 100 KC's taken into account) overnight and early into day four.

As far as knowing how many combines, and how much I spent. I meticulously wrote it all down as I went. I recorded ingredients prices down to the copper. I kept track of attempts, successes, and number of skill ups per stack (20 attempts), and partial stack when I reached a trivial on one of the recipe's I used. I Filled both sides of a sheet of paper with notes as I was going. :)

It was a bit tedious recording it that way, but I really wanted to know just how cheaply I could do it (was on a 20th level wizard). You can get to 248 for less than 300pp following the path I used above, 'IF' your INT/WIS is 255 or higher already.
