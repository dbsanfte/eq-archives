Also consider Jagged Pine. The poachers drop quality hides most of the time, sometimes 2, and they also drop plat, research components, and the occasional rare item. My cleric was also lvl 59 and I could pull singles with a nuke then beat them to death with the proc hammer. It was pretty slow. I started 2boxing with a 52 Ranger. He would bow pull, snare, and melee. I think there are 16 spawn points there, all static, no wanderers, and a number of Named poachers that can spawn on occasion. My Cleric/Ranger team could easily control a dozen spawns. Poachers turned Light Blue at 51 or so.

Do Not kill the four legged wild life here unless you have no care for your faction with the vendors in the outpost. Stick to the poachers!

After a few hour tour there, I would have 80+ hides. HQ/MQ/LQ cat, bear and wolf pelts. All pelts seemed to drop in pretty equal numbers. No ruined drop here! HQ bear and cat went to my bazaar mule, the rest got cut to LQ for padding. Everything else went to the inzone vendor, and a few hundred plat went into the bank.
