Grimlach
Earth temper:
1) mephit blood - camp ledge left side of zone and you'll get more than enough while exping
2) liquified earth - camp mud ring and kill tons of mudwalkers. I do this with a single group very often and you can too. I dont suggest 2boxing here because of the roamer dillo tho.
3) chips of granite - not sure exactly which stone mobs drop these but basically if you pull the blue con guys around the peregrin ring you'll get them very fast.

If you stick to the appropriate camp spots and pull the correct mobs you'll have more than enough tempers in no time.

Water temper:
1) gills - zone in group killing many sharks and fishes. Dont know exactly which mobs drop the gills but you'll get about 1 an hour with a decent group.
2) mephit blood - same zonein group you camp gills for; you'll get more than enough bloods.
3) soapstone - get lots of these when your guild attempts coirnav or ofassaa and kills the slarg guys. you'll also get the ice you need here too.

Fire temper:
1) mephit blood - obvious. kill either mephits that roam at zone or on their lil isle.
2) magma crystals - drops off the soldier looking guys, fairly uncommon in my experience, but then fire armor aint that great.
3) solidified magma - magma guardians by the birds. Bring a strong group with a good puller as they hit like 5x 1.1kish

Air temper:
1) mephit blood - you will get tons of these when your guild does the avatar rings for xeg keys.
2) fog fragments - smoke ring mist elemental looking mobs. Your guild will be a main source of fragments for you here.
3) condensated - forget exact name but drops off same mobs as fog fragments.

Earth tailored and water plate/chain have more hitpoints than any ornate items you could possibly make and they're tradeable. These items are the best tradeable gear in the game as far as I know; so why should their drop rate be nerfed? Personally I wouldnt bother with anything but earth items if you are trying to do this stuff with a single group instead using raid drops. Earth chain/plate bp/legs are still very good and rival ornate. Plus, they are tradeable. I personally like the drop rate and difficulty of making these items; it makes them cool =)
