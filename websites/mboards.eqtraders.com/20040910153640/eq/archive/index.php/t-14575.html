thrashette
It is my opinion that Sony messed up the order of the Aid Grimel quest from what the designer intended. It should have been wash plate, brew drink, craft ring, make urn, bake platter, fletch bow, tailor tunic.

Consider:

o You are stuck with a 35 hp 35 mana earring from the time that you do Justice trial until you kill Solusek Ro. This earring is uncompetitive to people who can kill any of the flag mobs between Justice and Sol Ro. Once you complete the Fire tunic there is a trivial farm to the 130 hp 150 mana one. The intermediate earrings are definitely out of place progresswise.

o Nobody in thier right mind kills Solusek Ro before they kill Rallos Zek. The order of progression given out by the Grand Librarian is 'Go kill Rallos Zek, then go Kill Solusek Ro.' One would think that a quest designer would run with the plot, and allow people to get to the 100/100 earring with the RZTW kill. But no....We have to wait for FIRE to do the last -4- earrings. Once there, we can jump straight to the last earring with no additional flags.

o The difficulty of farming and number of components required is higher for the Fire tunic then for any other bit. As guild banker, I've got air cams and food parts coming out my ears. Obsidianwood sap, used up at 3 per try, can't be kept in stock. Sure, it's easier to get to the spideys in Fire than the spideys in Air...but the drop rate is horrendous in comparison. One Water raid is crab claws for a month.

o The tunic seems to have a much higher failure rate than other parts, AND tailoring is the hardest skill to raise. One would think that part of the appeal of the 100/100 earring would be to have something to wear while you get your tailoring up. But nope, you got this lamo 35/35 one.

o Bristlebane's Party Platter is worth making on its own, outside the quest. Any serious water-enabled baker is going to have one or two just to use at raids. So the time from attaining the 100/100 signet to getting the 115/115 one is almost NIL in many cases. It was about 10 minutes for me, only because I failed and had to remake a platter. That ain't right. A Smart Sony would set it up so traders would be stuck with a 100/100 earring and a 75/75 earring waiting to make a robe. Again, this places the robe as Part 7.

So maybe you can sleep better knowing that Sony probably messed it up...and that once you are beyond the hump it is a coast.
