From what I understand, this is the way that all of the GoD refines work. Turning in the raw material to the appropriate NPC will give you 2 refined materials. If you do the refining yourself, you can get from 1-3 refined material based on the number of refine combines you have made. Ultimately a player should be able to produce more refined material than the NPC overall average. The NPCs allude to this in the final quest text in Abyssmal (something about 'given time you think that you can improve the process' or text to that effect).
