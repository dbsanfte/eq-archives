Just in case you wondered ...

If the previous two LotR Oscar parties are any indication, "formal" attire means gents wear suits or tuxedos, ladies in nice dresses (kinda evening-style, not necessarily elaborate).

So - nope, this does NOT need to be top-notch designer. ;)

Please notice -- this is the $60 dollar per ticket party, not the $600/$6,000 per ticket party. :p

This one is for *the fans* ... yes, the stars have been informed that it's there. And yes, they have been invited.

However, as of today... none have sent any confirmation of intention to attend.

That's not really surprising, since these would not be paid appearances. Their agents generally have them fairly well booked. A few have responded that they'd rather go to this party than to the one their agent booked them into ...

LotR Fan Club members have done other activities with various cast and crew members from the films, so there is an acquaintence level that makes this party more attractive to some of these celebrities than the "glittler" parties that are more about publicity than about enjoyment.

So there's very little chance of any celebrities being there. At least, officially. There's a couple rumors of possibilities, but I was strictly instructed to be clear -- there is nothing certain. There are always rumors surrounding events like these ...

If any big names from the film industry *do* poke their noses in, it will be as much a surprise to the organizers as to the attendees. It's theoretically possible, but extremely unlikely. :boggled:

There are, however, some fairly well-known artists in painting, writing, sculpture, music, etc. who will be attending the art show portion and offering their wares (and autographs).

The point of the party is those who enjoy high fantasy (especially if that includes Lord of the Rings) to get together and have fun watching the Oscars together. From offsite, a direct tv thing, but nearby.

So ... if you're inclined, don't be intimidated. Everyone else buying tickets, or otherwise confirmed to attend, are just people too. :)
