For those of you that do not know, when you make a grandmaster trophy, you can summon a no rent bag. You can take that bag and hand it into the gn0me in the bazaar (middle building, second floor) to get a 100% weight reduction bag that only weighs 1.2 lbs. If you have 2 or more grandmaster trophies, you still only get one bag.

Now, I personally think that each grandmaster trophy should get its owner a seperate bag. If you have grandmaster 1 trade, you get 1 x 100% weight reduction, 1.2 lb bag. If you grandmaster 4 trades, you get 4 x 100% weight reduction, 1.2 lb bags. etc etc. We put the effort in it to get the skills, and the trophies, why not let us have the reward of a few extra bags.

Help this happen. While in game, type /feedback and let them know how you feel!
