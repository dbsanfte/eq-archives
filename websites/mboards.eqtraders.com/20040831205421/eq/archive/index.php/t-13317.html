I did a bunch of skill ups making fish eggs (thunder salom + skinning knife in tackle box). In addition to the skill up chance from just fishing, each caught fish was an extra chance to skill up.

My recommendation is to not do lures for skill-ups. They're used up just like lures and you can put the silk thread to better use. (I.E. making Leather Padding to sell if you don't mind raising Tailoring a bit, or selling the spiderling silk in the Bazaar to people who do make the padding)

You're better off buying some Animated Bait from a Gnome or some Catch-A-Lot Bait from Fishing supply vendors, if you want the skill bonuses while actually fishing.
