Tilnoom,
Depends on your access to pp as well. You can camp for or buy weight reducing bags. Perhaps one of the easier is the Rallic Pack (85% weight reduction, which sell for around 1K on our server, but are lore), Archeologist's pack (from Goblin Archeologist in Permafrost, 6 slot, 25% WR), or any of a number of other 100% WR items, which run 2-5K in Bazaar. You do have to spend time auctioning for them in Bazaar or in the Auction channel.

Another method is to GM a tradeskill, get your trophy, and then you can summon as many 100% WR bags as you have slots for. This, of course, assumes you are at least 51 and can plop AA points into New Tanaan Tradeskill Mastry to GM the smithing skill as well.

Another tried and true method we smiths of old used to use when one could only purchase MQ Ore from 3 places in all of Norrath, none of which were near the mold vendors: Purchase in Bazaar, put them in the bank, and get a SoW potion and STR buffs (or STR potions) to help you lug them from the bank to the forge. Your gate back method also works well if you are a class that can gate. There are things that can make your job easier, but none that will make it simple :)
