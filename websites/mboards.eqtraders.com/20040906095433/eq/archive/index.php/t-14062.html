ChaseFV
Around 35, you're hitting the bottom end of the right level to farm the grimling caves in Tenebrous Mountains. In two nights of non-stop clearing of one cave (that's all the faster I could go solo before the repops started), I came away with less than a stack of windstones. This is also an excellent place to farm acrylia (which was my real goal), so you'd be killing two birds with one stone.

The grimlings will be tough at your level, you'll probably want a group. Make sure you have MR/FR/CR if you're facing a geomancer, and watch out for the foreman, which will be tough for you at that level. Stay in the caves, unless you're running away: bat or wolf adds can be deadly. Good luck!
