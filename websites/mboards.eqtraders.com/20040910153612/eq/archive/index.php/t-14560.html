Deedolith
Time to clear some things about the tribute system.

- You can hand any tradable item to the tribute master in your home town (right click on him / her to open the favor window), he / she will reward you with favor pts.
- You can spend your pts to get various boosts (stats / focus / effects ... , up to 5 running at time.
- Each boost is tier restricted, the tier you have access to depend on your lvl.
- To get the desired boost, you need to upgrade it untill it is as high as you want (to get FT5, you need to upgrade Aura of Clarity 5 times)
- You can only get the boosts from the tribute master
- You can stop any boost at any time (hit alt + u)
- Every 10 mins, the amount of pts used by the boosts (active cost) is deducted from your total of pts, untill you remove them all.

- The tribute master will refuse any NO DROP / summoned items, as well as some junks (to prevent abuse).

eqNichola
Yes. Although you can *down*grade your level of tribute-buffs 'in the field', ie without going to see the tribute master.

SO what you do is this:

- (whenever) give items in tribute to the tribute master, to acquire tribute points (a kind of bank, if you will)

- just before going out to hunt / raid / whatever you want some nice buffage for, go to the tribute master and upgrade the buffs you want.

- you will now use X points every ten minutes, depending on which tribute-buffs you have running

- when you have finished hunting / raiding / whatever you wanted the buffs for, bring up the 'current tribute buffs' window by pressing alt-u, and downgrade everything to 0. You are now no longer spending points. Note you do NOT have to visit the tribute master to DOWNgrade.
Feldaan
Just one more addition to this. All tribute buffs count as items, therefore they also count against all the current item cap limitations (15 worn mana regen, 35 worn hp regen, and 250 worn atk). So don't upgrade yourself beyond these caps as it just won't help. :)
