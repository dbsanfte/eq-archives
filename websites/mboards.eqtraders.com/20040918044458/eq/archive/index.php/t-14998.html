Lord Grimrose
There is a trick with the Gor Taku and Shak Dratha.

Gor Taku spawn during the Norratian "Day"
Shak Dratha spawn during the Norratian "Night"

Here's the catch, you can't check game time in Shadeweaver's because the sun doesn't move there :boggled:

If you have been killing in the caves just keep killing they will cycle once the sun goes down over Antonica.

No...

They share spawn points.

However, if the spawn points are all gor taku, they'll stay gor taku until you kill some at (or just before) night, so they respawn at night as shak dratha.
