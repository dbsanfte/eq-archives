Well it's pretty simple :
the maximum you can reach in research skill is level dependent :
at 16 you can train 1 points and you get to 16

After that you need to wait untill 19, then you can train up to (lvl-15)*5

You just waisted all of your time practicing =(

on the other end, enchanters have an advantage in this skill :
vendor farm for "faded" pages and combine them alone in your tome of endless enchantment. they trivial well over your current skill and costs a couple of gold each on merchants, giving you cheap and easy skill increases.

But you *need* to wait 19 :)

Next time, come here first and read that skill's FAQ.

From EQTC's very own Spell Research FAQ (http://mboards.eqtraders.com/eq/showthread.php?t=6718) (found in the Spell Research forum):

Q: I just put one point into research, and it wont let me train anymore! Why?

A: The formula for the cap on research is 5 * (Your level - 15). At 16, you can only have 5 points in it. At 19, the skill cap is 20. This is why it is generally recommended that even though you can train research at level 16, you wait and put a point into research only at level 20, since you will automatically get a skill of 20 and can still skillup to 25 (skill cap at level 20.)
