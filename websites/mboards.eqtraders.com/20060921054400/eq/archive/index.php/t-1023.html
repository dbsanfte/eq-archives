Needed to bang up my tailoring to do MTPs so figured I'd go the easy root and kill two birds with one stone to boot.

Had a 21 tailor skill from whatever to start with. Bought enough mandrake roots for 100 combines. Hit 66 skill at 98th combine and failed my last two combines. :(

Out of those 100 combines I had 54 success.

Int was 162 through combo of INT gear, food and drink as well as chanter buff Voice of Quellious.

So basically I averaged one skill up every other combine - total cost was about 500 plat although since I'm going to actually use all of those in my baking, cost could technically be said to be just the 46 failure - about 200 plat.

The thing that really gets me was how fast the skill ups came - my buddy had done this same root with his troll SK and it took him twice as many combines to get to the same place starting from his high 20s - it would seem that I had either hit a magic number or the Voice of Quellious buff was better than I thought.
