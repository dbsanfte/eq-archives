Incidently, on the Shaman-made gate potions: dont buy anything but a 10-dose. 1 dose potions are a waste of plat. 5 doses potions are as well just not as bad.

Why?
(assumption is 300p per dose)

10 dose. 3000p spent. Use 9 doses. Sell the last dose to a vendor for 1900+p.(!!) 1100p spent for 9 gates. Thats 120ish plat per gate.

5-Dose. 1500p spent. You can use 4 doses and sell the last one back, but the ratio is worse, and thus it costs more per gate then a 10 dose. (Dont know the exact figures, never made any before).

1-dose. When you use it, its gone. 300p spent.

If you want to spend big plat, then buy those 1-dose insta-gate Vial of Swirling Smoke from NPC vendors. If yer cheap, go camp the Lizard gate potions from outside Cazic Thule in Feerot. =)
