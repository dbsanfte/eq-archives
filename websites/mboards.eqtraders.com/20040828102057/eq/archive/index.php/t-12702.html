Purified water can also be made by combining a foraged water reed (or moss root) with a purification tablet (about 3 sp cost) in a Tracker's Water Extracter (about 1.5 pp cost, 2 slot container)The tablet and extracter are bought from some cabilis merchants, including those outside of town (like Field of Bone).

I've personally foraged water reeds at Lake of Ill Omen and the moss roots in Swamp of No Hope. The combine makes 3 purified waters and is no-fail (it doesn't increase brewing, but I decided brewing was the best place to put this).

Besides faction issues, the only problem with using this method is that the purification tablet is LORE and is consumed in the combine, so you have to stand next to the merchant and buy a new one for each combine.
