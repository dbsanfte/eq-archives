drtyaty
Tailoring does indeed require velium bits, which uses pieces of velium. Therefore, you can never have enough.

The recipe is for arctic wyvern hide armor and is one of the easiest post 187 tailoring combines to find components for. However, you will require a lot of velium; likely more than you will need to raise smithing to 222 or whatever.

Break the velium down. When you are through raising your smithing to 222 or whatever go ahead and smith the velium into bits and then studs; that way you are taking up way less space in your bank.

Forgot to add that the velium recipe does not use boning, that is acrylia armor.

Hope that helps,
