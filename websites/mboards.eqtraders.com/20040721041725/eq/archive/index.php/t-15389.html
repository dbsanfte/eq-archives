yawp64
Each chunk of condensed makes 2 arrow heads.

So, multiply 48 (the number of ranks from 202 to 250) by how many combines per skill-up you expect (I used 20), then divide by 2 (since you get 2 arrow heads per chunk).

So, the answer is 480 chunks of condensed.

Then multiply that by an integer that represents how deeply the RNG hates you. In my case, it's 2. :-)
