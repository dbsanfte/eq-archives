Well, I did some work on the Taelosian Teas tonight, after getting my Grandmaster Brewer's Coerker, i might add :)

First off, Taelosian Tea leaves are dropped not foraged, as posted in the other thread. I found some in the snpools zone, off of what mob i don't recall. Others have found them dropping off of tureptas and stonemites in natimbi. Taelosian Mountain Tea Leaves are reported as dropping in the advanced zones of GoD. I was unable to find any in the bazaar yet.

Secondly, the ingredients that are foraged (mint, ginseng, moldy tea leaves, and a handful of tea leaves and twigs) are RARE. I foraged one handful and one mint in 3 hours spent in natimbi and the hanging gardens (barindu). That's a bummer, but given that the tea leaves themselves are also rare, it balances out. Not something to be doing just for the fun of it - I would reccomend just foraging while XPing in the zones. However I did find some in the bazaar at reasonable prices.

Finally, here is one new recipe outcome not yet posted on the brewing recipe board, and three new trivials....

Taelosian Green Mint Tea, cha+3, agi+4, cold/disease+5, hp/mana+5, trivial 156

Also decided to write up a small Tea guide, based on observing the logic progression of stats in these teas ... not all this information is verified.

Tea Leaves:
Taelosian Tea Leaves: mob drops in beginning GoD zones, such as natimbi, barindu, etc.

Tealosian Mountain Tea Leaves: mob drops in advanced GoD zones, such as Kod Taz, etc.

Place a tea leaf into a brew barrel and hit combine (after being trained by the brewing quests in the abysmal sea) to recieve 1+ quantities of the appropriate type of Tea.

Basic Teas:

White Tea: Taelosian (Mountain) Tea + water
int+4(6), cold/disease+5(8), hp/mana+5(10)

Black Tea: Taelosian (Mountain) Tea + Moldy Tea Leaf + water
changes int to wis

Green Tea: Taelosian (Mountain) Tea + Handful of Tea Twigs and Leaves + water
changes int to agi

add Mint to any recipe, and you add cha+3(5)

add Ginseng to any recipe, and you add sta+3(5)

Moldy Tea Leaves, Mint, Ginseng, and a Handful of Tea Twigs and Leaves are all foraged, it seems in any of the beginning GoD zones, and possibly in the advanced zones as well.

Enjoy :)
