Dryanna
All tradeskills that are not available to everyone are capped in regards to player level. This includes - Tinkering (only gnomes), Alchemy (only shaman), Research (only int classes) and Make Poison (Rogue only).

They're restricted skills - both in who can do them, and how fast they can advance in them. This is a design feature to make sure that only characters of that class which are actually played are the ones who can advance to the upper echelons of that trade.
