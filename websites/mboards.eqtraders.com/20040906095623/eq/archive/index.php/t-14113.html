arturio emerys
East Karana is great source of tradeskill items. If you get there early in the morning when no one else is there you can farm it quite well if you have a pbaoe char. Gather the snakes, spiders and lions and wipe them all out in a single hit of upheaval. The most I've done is 40 mobs at once. Snake eggs drop frequently from the chasm crawlers there and they are place holders for the crag spiders. Here is a break down of what you can get in zone:

Chasm Crawlers source of snake eggs
Griffawns, Griffenes and Griffons source of griffon meat
Lions, Lionesses sources of cat hide in a variety of quality, expect lots of ruined though.
Crag Spiders source of silk and spider venom sack
Carrion Spiders source of silk, spider venom sack and halfling parts

Foragers can forage tea leaves and plains pebbles. Other standard forages apply too.

While you are there, if a hill giant, Tarbul Earthstrider or Theoris Bladespur spawns, take em out. Nice cash for all 3 plus fine steel for selling or to the tribute guys if they are ever brought back.

Hope that helps.

I don't know if this has been posted before but...

In Gukta, there's an NPC merchant near the soulbinder, just past the zone to Innothule. He's called the Dar Forager.

He's there maybe 50% of the time. When he's there, and no one has beat you to it, he sells snake eggs. 15-20 is the max he seems to carry.

When he's not there, as far as I can tell, he's out wandering the swamp, foraging new stock. He aggro'd my troll once when the troll was sitting down at the PoK book. I assume if he's killed, it takes time to respawn him.

It's possible that newbies are selling him the eggs, since he buys like any other merchant, but I don't think so, since the eggs are at the top of his inventory along with some unique swamp-style food items.

I don't know how long his trips take or any other info, I just keep a baby frog parked next to him with some plat. Every once in awhile, I log on the frog and buy whatever eggs he's got. Adds up pretty quick.

Anyway, that's my egg supply. I'll prolly regret posting it, but oh well. =)
