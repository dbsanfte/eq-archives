FYI - Pets generally share the faction set of the owner, and also have the special "non-aggro" rule.

"Non-Aggro Rule" - A pet which has not yet gotten aggro'ed by a mob will not cause aggro. This was implemented so that pets were more useful in dungeons. A pet which is attacking other mobs will generate aggro as normal.

To see the faction change in action, try charming a pet or doing a quest (Qeynos Invstigator) which turns an NPC into your pet. His factions suddenly match yours. A shaman friend of mine doing the quest above discovered that having a level 4 pet when you are KoS to the corrupt Qeynos quards (she's 56) is a bad thing.
