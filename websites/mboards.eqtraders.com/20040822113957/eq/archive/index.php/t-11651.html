(Yes, I watch the learning channel)
Old style, done over an open fire (smithing)
Use Oak or wooden staves, and start hammering consecutively smaller metal hoops around them (with it all places around the fire to heat the wood)
Don't need to get too deep into it. All that really matters is tools involved:
-hammer
-metal hoops/bands
-wood (oak) staves
-open fire
-water (sloshed onto the inside of the wood surrounding fire so as to keep it from burning and making it more plyable)
