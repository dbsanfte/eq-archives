One zone has it all: Shadeweaver's Thicket.

Shadeling silk from the Xakra Silkworms and Lesser Shades which wander the low level areas and it sometimes also drops from the higher level Shak Dratha (mobs which mostly drop the unrelated Shade Silk). The Xakra tend to spawn in waves, so sometimes there are a ton of them wandering around and sometimes none.

All of the various hoppers throughout the zone drop rockhopper hides of varying quality, including high quality, even the needle claw hoppers which are quite low level.

Kill the chitterlings as a bonus and make a tinkerer happy - the chitterling barbs are used in making tailoring geerloks and aren't easy to come by due to the relative scarcity of the mob.

In shade weaver thicket the worm spawn cycle is 30 mins. It goes worm for 30 mins then beetles for 30, repeat cycle. Tiny hoppers are always there and can be placeholders for both cycles and provide lq/hq hides as well. Just park yourself there and smack away. Also, and this has been a well guarded secret since I rarely find people here, the north east section of the zone has the undead spawn camp for the lesser shades/skellies. The shades drop shadeling silks, swirling shadows and the xakra kel armor parts. Skellies drop bone chips and crap weapons and some cash. Hunting there for the swirling shadows yielded me 8 stacks of silks and 4-5 stacks of bone chips in very little time. The shades and skellies spawn in the same points so kill them all and remember to loot the bodies. Have noticed that if you leave bodies they still count as a place holder until they have rotted away and spawn timer does not seem to start until they are gone so clearing bodies keeps the spawns happening faster. I stay busy killing and looting the entire time for my supplies.
