FelishannaSecretlight
Yesterday, my magician (Suzanna) set about working on her Blacksmithing, and seeing as she had 220 skill, I decided it would be worth trying the Aid Grimel route to 227.

Requirements:
Must be 220 (unmodified) Blacksmithing skill.
Must have the Bertoxxulous flag.

Preparation:
Made up 5 stacks of Acid Wash (actually made 98, 2 failed).

Set-up:
Placed Felishanna (cleric) by the forge in PoK.
Equipped the smithing geerlok on Suzanna.
Equipped Suzanna with all her +INT gear she could manage.
Took Suzanna to Aid Grimel in the library and bound next to him.
Suzanna then suicided down the lift shaft, to make a corpse.
She then looted all her items bar one and dragged the corpse to Felishanna at the forge (where she rezzed her).
Suzanna sits and med a few seconds, then gates so as to be stood next to Aid Grimel. She targets Aid Grimel (and remains with him in the target from now on).
Now, I was ready to start.

The loop :-
(1) Suzanna says "I am well versed in the art of smithing" with Aid Grimel in target. She receives athe Flithy Breastplate from him.
(2) Felishanna rezzes Suzanna (epic click).
(3) Suzanna accepts the rez, and appears at the forge.
(4) Suzanna sits down, to med faster.
(5) Suzanna does the Filthy Breastplate + Acid Wash combine in the forge. This can give a skill up (sometimes).
(6) Suzanna destroys the resulting Cleansed Breastplate.
(7) Suzanna stands up and casts Gate. Gaining enough mana to cast this only takes the few seconds sitting down. She then appears at Aid Grimel again, ready to repeat the loop.

The result:
The first 60 combines :- nothing.
The next 27 combines :- gained SEVEN skillups!!!!
This gave Suzanna smithing skill of 227, at which point this is trivial.
For reference, with rez effects on, Suzanna's INT was at 306.

Hope this helps someone.

Felishanna/Suzanna.
