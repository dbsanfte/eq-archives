The items that are both dropped/forgaged and always able to be purchased by a merchant are marked as such. To my knowledge the items that are only dropped or foraged can only be purchased off a merchant if a player has previously so that item to the merchant. In other words you are as likely to get a dropped/ foraged item off one NPC as you are off of any other NPC :)

The classes that can forage are ranger and druid. All iksar also get forage as a racial ability. Iksar cannot ever improve the skill, though. I believe they start with it at a 50.
