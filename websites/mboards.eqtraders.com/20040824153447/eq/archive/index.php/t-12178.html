It's not a question of saving some plat, it's a question of making it a remotely viable skill-up path. For example, making darkwood + hemp up to 215:

Ellis Cloudchaser tells you, 'That'll be 226 platinum 7 gold 9 silver 9 copper for the Darkwood Bow Staff.'
Ellis Cloudchaser tells you, 'That'll be 1 silver per Hemp Twine.'

Ellis Cloudchaser tells you, 'I'll give you 204 platinum 7 gold 7 silver 1 copper for the Rough Darkwood Recurve Bow.'

So a failure costs you 226.809, but with sellback a success only costs you 22.038.
