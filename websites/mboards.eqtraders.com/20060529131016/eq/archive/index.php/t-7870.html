01JUL2003 ** UPDATE *** Released v1.01
Updated a few trivials here and there
Fixed some things here and there
Added a few more recipes
LOTS of updates!!
Fixed a lot of formating issues
------------------------------------------------------------

This has been the worst ever compendium to make. I ended up having to make a lot of the items myself in-game to get them added to Magelo. Anyway, it's not pretty and there will be a few updates to come I'm sure after you guys take a look at it.

This compendium works hand-in-hand with the previous compendiums that I've released (baking and brewing so far). I recommend installing the full pack of all three to get the most out of it.

You can download the file from here:
obsidian.efrontco.com/regression/LothBake_108.zip (Baking Version)
obsidian.efrontco.com/regression/LothBrew_104.zip (Brewing Version)
obsidian.efrontco.com/regression/LothPott_101.zip (Pottery Version)
obsidian.efrontco.com/regression/LothComp01July2003.zip Baking, Brewing and Pottery in one download

Just unzip the file to your storyline directory using full paths. No files should be overwritten unless you have a previous version installed. If you have a previous version installed, you may want to delete the files associtated with it just to get a fresh install.

Have fun and I hope you enjoy the mod.
