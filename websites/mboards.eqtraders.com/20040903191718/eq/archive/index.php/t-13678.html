Most of the elemental is not worth it. (even on a guild level)
I think my guild has about 10 pieces of elemental armor in circulation for guild use. Most of this is Earth elemental because components are alot more common than the rest and a few pieces of water elemental. Even on the scale of a time or elemental guild its 1300% faster to farm actual pop elemental drop molds then it is to try and outfit your guild in the pop elemental smithed armor. If elemental armor was worth making it would be seen atleast as much as elemental pop fletching and pottery. Those are the money makers for the guild in elemental planes and time.
