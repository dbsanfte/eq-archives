KyrosKrane
Well, that's easy enough to answer. PoP is largely concerned with a specific storyline -- the conspiracy of certain gods (and goddesses) to destroy or expunge Norrath in retribution for Zebuxoruk's attempts to disseminate his knowledge.

Only the "villains" of the plot (the Zeks and Solusek Ro), the "observers" (Mithaniel Marr and Karana), and the "unfortunate bystanders" (the elemental gods, who block your access to the Plane of Time) are encountered. The Bertox / Terris Thule / Saryrn sequence doesn't seem to tie in to the main storyline, though -- I still haven't done much of it, so I don't know the lore involved.

Erollisi and Rodcet don't seem to have much to do with this sequence, though it would have made sense for Rodcet to be involved in the efforts to defeat or at least frustrate Bertox.

After all, Veeshan, Brell Serillis, Bristlebane, Prexuz, Quellious, and several other dieties and sub-dieties don't make an appearance either. There has to be something left for PoP 2! < evil grin >
