While the normal 5 trade skills are not level capped, the race/class tradeskills are level capped to some extent. A gnome cannot get any skill increases in tinkering(above the 50 they are born with) until lvl 17. At level 17 they an get to 250. A rogue can't start poison making until 18 or 19, i forget which, and it is level capped. Alchemy is also level capped and can not be started until a certain level, i don't remember the level but its around 30 iirc.

The non-tradeskill tradeskills (Foraging, Fishing, Begging, Alcohol Tolerance) are also level capped.

The big 7 (Pottery, Smithing, Fletching, Tailoring, Brewing, Baking, Jewelcraft) are not capped. A level 1 can theoretically be 250 in one of these and 200 in the other 6.
