arturio emerys
In Jagged Pine Forest there are poachers near the river. There are 3 tents with I believe 18 spawns in them and near them. They are quaddable in the high 40s low 50s for decent exp. Mages, necros and bards can farm them well too. The poachers can drop low, medium and high quality bear, wolf and cat hides and relatively frequently. They also drop other sellable loot and up to 3 or 4 plat at a time. These are a good source of cash, tradeskill drops and exp if you are in the high 40s, low 50s. By 52 the exp will be slower than other places though.

If you hunt in Jagged Pine, do not kill the bears, wolves or cats in the zones for tradeskill drops. They give several nasty faction hits, some that with the fort folks. It is easy to get kos with the fort folks, which is fixable, but the animals themselves in Jagged Pine do have a faction hit that you recieve for killing them and there is no way to improve that faction. Most people are kos to that faction starting out, but since you are Druid (I see Tejaye on EqDruids all the time) you won't be kos unless you have already trashed it by killing the animals in Jagged Pine. No reason to trash a faction when there is an easy alternative.

Well, two spots I like are:

1) west commons (all bears there can drop, black, young kodiak, kodiak) - course I also forage plains roots while there, for cultural robes.

2) rathe mountains. Near the zone from lake rathe, there are TONS of bears.
