You may be thinking of Woven Mandrake (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=1136), which uses a store-bought alchemy component, and is trivial 66. Note that each combine will cost you 4.2pp. This can be further combined with 1 Steel Boning (smithed, again all ingredients store-bought) to make 10 Picnic Baskets - tailoring trivial 76.
