I mainly wanted to offer an alternative for those skilling up near 300. You don't HAVE to have tons of advanced poison vials.

So there's pros and cons of using coffin bottles.
Con: GMAS = Very hard quest. (I helped with parts of this, so I know it's a beast.)
Pro: Owning GMAS is a significant accomplishment (that warm glow of success)
Con: Not all recipes work.
Pro: Avoid tedium of making vials.
Con: GMAV is expensive
Pro: Save money on Advanced Poison Vials
Con: Must be 46+
Pro: GMAS required to complete the Master Sketch for some of the advanced poisons.

Some folks really aren't interested in the poisons produced with poison making. (ie, someone wants to make augs, or just post big skill numbers here.) On the whole, I think they should skip the GMAS.

For those interested in using poisons later, or any rogue with the PoP ornate leggings, the GMAS is a fine investment.
