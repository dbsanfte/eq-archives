To be honest, with the AG earring causing any rare saps to drop to be sold in the Bazaar at enormous prices and with the better drop rate of stonewood saps, I would recommend looking into an earth robe instead of fire. Obsidianwood never dips before 20-25k..and I wouldn't expect it to.

Chenier
The raw skill cap is 250. The modified skill cap is 252 (regardless if you have raw skill of 250 and the shei shears vs. a geerlok).

So, you could have the raw skill of 242 and a geerlok or trophy and hit the 252 modified cap or the raw skill of 220 and have the modified cap of 252.

The new tradeskill UI now actually shows the trivial levels for all recipes. We knew before that the "super difficult" recipes had very high trivial levels (thus, a 250/252 skilled person would have a 50/50 success rate). The new UI confirms it.
