From my experiences, I have learned a few things:

If you go on the law of averages to determine how many you need, you will always need 1 more.

If you gather double what you think you need, you will finish is less than half of your supplies.

If you want to just finish off a skill to be done with it, you will skill faster.

If you want to get your skill raised to make something, your skillup rate will become closer to 1 in 100 attempts.

So, take these lessons and determine your need based on that. GL to you.
