In DSP Flawless hides are 'Not' rare.

Just have a 50/50 chance of the tibe being up, and if you don't have a high level group, you can't make the Hoppers spawn.

When they are up they drop either a superb or a flawless every 5th kill or so, very common.

Since superb are also used in tailoring, and more tailors can use them (lower trivial), their price seems to be almost identical (at least on my server) to flawless.

So collect superb/flawless, then either find someone needing superb to trade, or just sell in bazaar at 200pp each, and buy flawless for the same... zero net cost thing, doubles hides.

Last time I was there in 2 hours I had 50 hides, 10 flawless, 10 superb, 10 hq (sold as backpacks 60pp each), 10 medium (sold in bazaar 14pp each), 10 lq (now leather padding to be used later), well, 11's and 9's, but very evenly split up.

Hope this helps.

The name of the mob to be killed in DSP to respawn the Rockhoppers is called Sambata Tribal Leader Garn. His PH is a mob called Sambata Tribal Keeper. He is on a 23 Minute spawn time and can be a pain in the butt to spawn. I've seen the Leader spawn after killing the Keeper once, and been there 10 hours with a Keeper respawning every 23 minutes.

Kill the Tribal Leader, you get Rockhoppers to spawn. Kill the Age Old Rockhopper (they spawn in the same spot), and you get the Sambata's to spawn. As someone said earlier, Tailors who are working Acrylia to skill up want the Rockhoppers to remain when the Age Old spawns and tend to get irked when their Rockhoppers go bye bye.
