First of all, from a non-rogue even (but I did poisons for the database), Spine Break and Spine Rend are two totally different things.

Spine Rend is a Tier 2 PoP poison (the spell buff proc kind). From the lucy spell data base, Spine Rend (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=1185&amp;source=Live) is 55% snare (with poison counter so it can removed with Cure Poison).

Spine Break (the topic of the original poster) is an old-world poison with effect Paralyzing Potion I (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=1833), which is a 95% snare and 50% slow (with small poison counter) and lasts for 7 ticks.
