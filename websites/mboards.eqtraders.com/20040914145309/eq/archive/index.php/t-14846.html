If you have GoD you can go to Abysmal to fish. You can a) sell the fish to vendors as-is for 1-5pp each or b) you can make them more stackable by putting your 1 catch into a mixing bowl and hitting "Combine". after you have stacks and stacks of these you can sell to vendors for the equivalent of the original catch.
Hope that makes sense.
