Anyone know how to get or make a Combine Smithy Hammer? I can't seem to find any info on it.
Zopharr
12-29-2002, 09:34 AM
Go to tradeskill supplies in the home page (bottom of the icons on the left)

On supplies plage, click "sort by tradeskill," then click "smithing" to look at only smithing supplies

Assuming you are using MS Explorer, go to "Edit" at the top of the page (second in) and scroll down to "Find (on This Page)" and click

In the box that pops up, enter "combine" and click "Find Next"

And you will get, under: Katta Castellum (Luclin)

- combine smithy hammer - Granic Malicus (100, -1480)

I'm sure there is some similar procedure in Netscape. This one works for me.
