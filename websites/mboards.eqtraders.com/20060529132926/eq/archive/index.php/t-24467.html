This guide other then suggestions is more or less Dead since the revamp of Alchemy, but will leave it up for posterity sake.

a few humble sugestions on your quest to skillup.
1) Get Alchemy Mastery 3 !
2) Get wis as close to 370 as possible (check the skillup formula for why)
3) There is no single path to skillup, use all that is at your table, save the higher skillup components for when nothing else will take you to that skill. (employ a mule!)
4) Get salage if you can, fails will every now and then return ingredients.
~~~

Forages and drops:
Autumn Crocus (Foraged Ssraeshza Temple, multiple drop zones esp sanctus seru)
Deepwater Ink (drops in Kedge and SG)
Nodding Blue Lilly (drops in Seb and foraged in Seb and DL)

Stillmoon Temple (Dragons of Norrath) Foraged
blue nightwort, burnberry, cryptanthus, deadly ivy, draconic essence, firefly, luminous ivy, marsh algae, mature silkworm, nettle creeper, sky vine, silkworm larva, starshine moss, spiny fruit, silkworm, red cups, storm cloud,

Mob drop (infrequent to rare from many DON zones):
Basalisk Saliva(& mature), burnberry, deadly ivy, draconic essence, Drake Saliva (& mature), firefly, marsh algae, nettle creeper, red cups, silkworm, sky vine, storm cloud, quicklime, wood ashes.
~~~
