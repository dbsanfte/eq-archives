Yes, we can, although with the database temporarily rolled back I don't have the recipe at my fingertips. It's called Essence of Concealment and is an LDoN potion, so the herbs for it are drops.

Swamp vine is an LDoN forage and rare drop. Burdock root is sold in Shar Vahl and Gukta. Mystic ash is sold by the usual old world vendors. Empty vials are sold by a number of vendors (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=1898&menustr=035000000000).
