Tweedledea
You ought to be fine quadding in CS. But it's a very good idea to get your CoV faction to non-KoS - that way you can single pull. (I don't think you need to go all the way to ally to achieve this, but it was a long time ago...)

At 52 quadding wyverns should be very good xp for you too. You could fish while you med. And if you felt so inclined, you could kill the cobalt drakes too, for cobalt drake hides... but remember to keep out of range of their nukes... quadding is not an option for those guys.

My tips:

-Work on your CoV faction
-Take lots of bait and a mana regen buff
-Don't attack Yvolcarn (he summons, and you'll be dead in no time)
-Ditto Azureake
-Yvolcarn and Azureake will also attack you if they see you fighting their wyvern/drake buddies.
-If you turn up and it's too busy... give up and come back another time... CS is horrible when it's over-crowded.
-Yvolcarn and Azureake give CoV faction hits.... but the rest don't.
-If you see a bulthar or siren running at you... get out fast. At 52 you won't stand a chance.
-If anyone is quadding othmir in the wyvern area, remember they'll attack you if you're in wolf form.

Wyvern hides come off Arctic Wyverns in Cobalt Scar. They are on CoV faction (so best to be dubious or better) however you do not take any faction hits for killing them. (Except for the named wyvern, Yvolcarn and the named drake Azureake <sp?>) You can quad anywhere up from the druid port in to Skyshrine. If you aren't KoS you can pull whatever you want with snare and nothing assists (a.k.a. you won't get adds just by running past them). I started quadding these on my druid at 51 and went to 54 before the xp got slow. You should be perfectly ok to do it with a 52 druid. From what I remember the drop rates are pretty common. If you're looking to kill drakes as well keep in mind not to try quadding them, they're casters. Just snare/root them, run to max range to avoid their nukes, and dot them.

I'm in the middle of this too Tau, so here goes:

(Relatively) efficient farming in Velious for tradeskill occurs in the following zones:

For the skin/fur/hide:
Cobalt Scar:
- Wyvern - Arctic Wyvern Hide
- Cobalt Drake - Cobalt Drake Hide
- Othmir - Othmir Fur

Wakening Lands:
- Black Panther - Panther Skin
- Haze Panther - Haze Panther Skin
- Tigeraptor/Tigesaurous - Tigeraptor Skin
- Holgresh - Holgresh Fur

Western Waste:
- Velium Hound - Velium Hound Fur
- Ice Burrower - Ice Silk

For the tanin:
Cobalt Scar:
- Cobalt Cod - Cod Oil

Wakening Land:
- Yew Leaf - Yew Leaf Tanin

Skyshrine:
- Drake Egg - Drake Egg Oil

For the velium:
Velks:
- Spiders - Velium ore

Here's a little summary:
- Wyvern @ CS are statics but high competition from quadders and farmers, hides drop rate was nerfed so 1 round of all spawns will yield 3-4 hides
- Cobalt Drake @ CS roams in a small area (2 straight paths) but has CoV faction hit
- Othmir @ CS are statics but has Othmir faction hit (Othmir vendor is the only one that sell Brown Algae needed to make Coldain Heater, which is needed to make Coldain Velium Temper (CVT))
- Black Panther @ WL respawns fast but roams north, east and south of the lake among other hostiles (giants, holgresh, and mercenaries), skin drop rate is moderate, may be 1 in 3 has skin, sometimes 2 skins.
- Haze Panthers @ WL are uncommon, roams, and harder to kill, drop is also uncommon, skin drop rate is uncommon, may be 1 in 10 has skin, rarely 2 skins.
- Tigeraptor @ WL are both static (next to Skyshrine entrance) and roams (west of the lake), easy kill and no one farms them, hide drops rate is moderate, may be 1 in 3 has hide.
- Holgresh @ WL are casters, easy to kill if you melee but quite annoying (they nuke, dot, dispells, some has pet), their combines also require distill mana vial (the sapphire x 2 one), fur drop is uncommon, may be 1 in 7?
- Velium Hound @ WW are not easy to farm and track, highly contested mob, the fur is in high demand for shawl, unknown PH, fur drop rate is 1 in 2.
- Ice Burrower @ WW are not very numerous, they are not easy to kill with tons of HP, also highly contested, the silk gear are quite a money maker, silk drop rate is 1 in 3 but needs 2 to make a swatch.
- Cobalt Cod @ CS is high yield, easy to get
- Yew Leaf @ WL as a ground spawn is hard to spot, as a foraged item is rare as heck (1 an hour, may be)
- Drake Egg @ Skyshrine as a drop is easy but mob has CoV faction hit, as a foraged item is high yield.
- Velium @ Velks are common drops from top shelf spiders, rarer on the bottom floor, also drop by gargoyles in zone.

...
yeah, something like that, I only go farming if I'm extremely bored, I farm bazaar mostly.
...
