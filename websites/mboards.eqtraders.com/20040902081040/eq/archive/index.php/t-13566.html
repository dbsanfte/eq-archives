Yes, it can be used for anything that works in a regular mortar and pestle, and it can't be token-ized in the Bazaar (it seems that only containers with a weight reduction can be tokenized, which also left out Vale sewing kits and Fier'Dal fletching kits, much to my chagrin). Still, it's ten slots, so if you have to have a mortar and pestle, you can carry this one around because you can use it for a loot bag when you're not making poisons or celestial essences.
