Brodybeard wrote:

"I don't know if this is still true... check the new tradeskill interface for vial recipes. There was a "Crow's Brew" sold in the Abysmal Sea that could be used in place of animal pelts to make vials."

I have personally verified it for my guild leader. Crow's Special Brew is sold in the Abysmal Sea, and it works in the creation of regular, lined and sealed poison vials. I made one of each to be certain, and the recipes work fine substituting the brew for a pelt or skin. It makes doing poison vials to 188 a breeze.

For your skillup path, then, assuming you have access to the Queen of Thorns or someone who can go there for you, is to do claywater mixture to 26, then regular poison vials to 148, then lined to 168, then sealed to 188. After that, if you want to go to 200 or higher, go with casserole dishes to 199. If you're going to do casserole dishes, then instead of going to poison vials after 26, make ceramic linings until they trivial, and fire them. The fired linings stack and you'll need lots of them for the 188-199 run.
