Ixiola
Look for different items of similar stats, then consider who can wear.

The shadowwalker armor is clearly for the gnome rogues out there. Yes, clerics, pallies, etc can wear it, but why bother when the observer stuff is better.

So your market is what gnome rogues specifically (any deity) can buy that have similar stats.

And the others are correct, prices will vary by server. So you'll need to do research on where yours is at currently.

I have a good idea of what observer armor sells for on my server (usually a bit more than the Dwarf Culteral Brell only armor), but the only rogue stuff I made was to twink my rogue alt, so not sure of those prices on CT.

For example, an Dwarven Culteral Brell bracer can be purchased for around 5K. I can sell an observer bracer for about 6K with little trouble. The reason clearly is it's for any gnome regardless of deity, whereas the dwarven culteral is for small races who worship Brell only. Similar market so I use one to help price the other.
