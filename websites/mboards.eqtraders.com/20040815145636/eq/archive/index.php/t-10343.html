Axterix EnObelix
Tunareancourt is the faction, not tunare. Mobs on that faction are found nowhere but in Wakening Lands. The sifaye, the faerie drakes,the butterfly things, the fauns by the tower, and the sylphs inside the tower are all on this faction.

Feel free to kill them, doesn't mean anything other than that you need to invis if you want to go to PoG before entering the tower. And with Wakening Lands, it's always a good idea to be invis anyway.

Or you could just not bother with the sifaye and hats, since they aren't anything special anyway. For the boots, you can always kill the Faun Outcasts (not corrupted, he's nasty). They carry no faction hit and always con indifferent. Sifaye will assist them though (I believe, not 100% sure), as well as buff them and they will assist sifaye.

For the tar, just need to kill the tar goos in the geonid caves. No faction hit associated with those, but be aware that when killed, they split into two smaller tars. Kill those and they can drop the component. Since you're a fellow chanter, I highly recommend getting Dire Charm. Makes farming in WL much easier and faster.

For items you already have:

Holgresh are on Holgresh faction, but you start out KoS to them anyway.

Geonid Shamans are on geonid faction. Not an important one either unless you want to do the phenocryst focus crystal quest.

Best place I've found to camp is near the giant side outcast faun camp. 3 faun spawns and wandering sifaye, panthers, and holgresh.

As the armor, nope, it doesn't really sell. There's a lack of gnomes that wear armor other than cloth. Most of those are already decked out in Thurg armor, brellium, underfoot, or other stuff. Shadowwalker in particular is hopeless, since, like all cultural chain, the stats suck compared to easily available items. The recent increases in Ornate drops have further hurt the situation, as the gloves make it easy for warriors and rogues to get 30% spell haste.
