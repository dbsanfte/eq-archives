Currently our database does not track -WHEN- an item or recipe is added. It might be reasonable to add a couple of fields...

Entry Date
Modified Date

To the two primary tables... And from them build a couple of scripts that would generate lists of "New" and "Updated" items and recipes.

Technically it is possible...

Note however I said the two PRIMARY Tables. We have supporting tables... (Recipe Components, Recipe Alternate Successes, Recipe Alternate Failures, etc) To be complete it would require each of these various tables to be also updated.

On the surface it sounds simple. But there are plenty of tricky things to consider beneath.

And even if this is all implemented... then there is the consideration of the additional Bandwidth consumed by people browsing the "new" and "Updated" lists.

The idea is not without merit. It is a GOOD idea. But I am still way behind in getting the GoD Items and Recipes added.

And Ngreth I know is working on implmenting various fixes and truely small, but useful updates. I would not want to distract him to add this new functionality that could easily -break- the current data entry/edit forms.
