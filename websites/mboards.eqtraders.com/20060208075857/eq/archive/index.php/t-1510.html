Blue Diamond = 400pp
Diamond = 200pp
Jacinth = 180ishpp
Black Sapphire = 200ishpp
Velium Bar = 262.5pp (x8)
Vial of Purified Mana = 600ishpp

The mounted gems have a triv of less than or equal to 142, the unfired seal has a triv of less than or equal to 102. Rumours and lies has it that the difficulty of the unfired seal doesn't reflect it's trivial, I've blown up 3 in the 8 I've made, at 240+ skill, could just be RNG tho.

Cost of about 3700pp or so for the ingredients and 5 combines that should, in theory, be at a 95% success rate.
8k ? mebbe, especially as a one off where you can blow up the ingredients =(.
Check the bazaar for the Purified Mana and Thurgadin sells the Velium bars.

Oh, and as to how I did, I have 2 GM trophies on my characters. One has JC the other has Pottery =).
