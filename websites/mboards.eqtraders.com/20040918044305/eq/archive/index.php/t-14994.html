Well it depends on the guild and the server.

The real elemental guilds, those who are still working on farming elementals for gear and didn't kill all 4 elemental gods thus not in PoTime yet do make BPs and legs because elemental BPs come from PoTime and elemental legs are a rare drop from elemental gods thus they need tradeskill items for those slots.

I can tell you truthfully, in our 6 months raiding fire and the other elementals we've killed maybe a handful of the magma guardian model mobs... certainly no more than 20. With a good bard or monk you can pull any named mob in that zone without touching anything else, and the mobs that spawn during the fennin event don't drop metallic substance. This is a huge departure from going to earth and doing rings, where you're forced to kill all the various mob types in huge numbers (an average night of doing rings in earth nets us 10 elemental stones, for example) or Water where the trash sees invis and you have to kill it to pull mobs like Offassa and Hydrotha.

For raiding guilds and making armor it's all about what is killed during guild time... people exping in a zone on non guild time aren't going to give those pieces to the guild, they're going to keep them for their own use or sell them. Metallic Substance doesn't drop off the mobs we kill in fire on guild time, hence we have none in the vault and have never made a piece of Inferno plate or chain.
