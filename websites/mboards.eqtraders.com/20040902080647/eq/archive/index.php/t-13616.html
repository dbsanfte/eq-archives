"Having hunted only in Dawnshroud and Mons Letalis for pelts, I can tell you that there is no discernable difference in the frequency of pelt drops. Not scientific, just an observation.

Hope that helps,"

From extensive experience this is not true.

Hopper hunting.

Dawnshroud peaks.

Plus side.
1. Lots of hoppers. The cave can support several people or groups hunting.

2. Ease of access and least dangerous hungint spot

Down side.
1. Low drop rate of pelts
2. Often contested
3. They may not even be up if someone is questing and has changed the cave from hoppers to sambata.


Mons Letalis

Plus side.

1. Lots of hoppers.
2. Drop rate is slightly higher


Down side
1. Terrible hunting terrain
2. Lots of potential adds especially if you are KOS to the SHik Nar.
3. Since the last attempt at fixing pathing. You have the old big pile o mobs stuck under the world at 1 point problem



Scarlet desert.

Plus side.

1. Relatively safe place to hunt the hoppers.


Down side.
1. About same drop rate as DSP
2. Very few hoppers to farm
3. small but anoying possibility of adds
4. Pathing near mesa is horrible
5. Location is remote

That is from extensive hunting in all three areas.
