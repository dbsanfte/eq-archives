Magma is pretty hard to get. Killing the magma with one group is lots of work for little xp, compared to the other mobs (the ones that drop granite) in PoFire. Any group that's killing them, is probably doing it for the magma drops... or it's a raid clearing them, and it's not too common for raid-dropped tradeskills supplies to make it out of guilds. The alabaster beak is one of the more common parts from Air, so a trade, especially for an item that's used to make the Ceramic Burner (a highly coveted item), is probably unlikely. Basically, the magma = the burner. Granite is more common. :(
