Lolanae
I raised poison to 250 doing storebought. You can make Athropic Sap to about 98 skill, then calicum Rot to about 172. You can apply these to raise apply poison in the process. Afterwards, you can make Spirit of the Sloth all the way to 250. Don't apply these, just sell back to the merchant. They sell for about 9pp each.

With Sipirt of the Sloth, you never have to leave the poison merchant room in PoK. Calium rot requires a trip to the vendor in Steamfont, and I can't remember about Athropic.

Hope this helps.
