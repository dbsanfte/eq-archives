Silverfish
Congratulations on your Fisherman's Companion! Here's what I do for a long trip out. Get yourself a tackle box or collapsed tackle box, a skinning knife (which you can get your favorite smith to build or just buy it in the Bazaar) and stacks of Gunthak Mackerel. The reason is that Gunthak Mackerel can be put in the tackle box with the skinning knife and you get back five fishing bait, so one stack of mackerel is the same as five stacks of bait. Takes up much less space, so you can load up and not have to come back to a fishing merchant for a long, long time.

Happy casting!
