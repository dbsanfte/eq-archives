I like having this list--each "ad" is personalized, with contact info and other useful facts. The Karana board doesn't include the guild the character is in (which can make a big difference--someone who is in a big raiding guild may well be harder to get hold of than someone in a more casual guild). Here, you can say if you'll "just click" or if you have a specific fee or materials requirement. You can say the name of your most-played alts or give your email.

Moreover, this is the "Tradesfolk for Hire" thread. Nothin on the Karana version says that any of the folks with those skills are willing to do them FOR anyone else (though admittedly anyone who posts his skills and thinks that no one will ever contact him is pretty deluded).

The fact that the thread here is updated as posts get older is also good. There doesn't seem to be any contingency for removing people from the Karana board list when they leave the game or decide to stop tradeskilling for hire. You know that the people in the list here have at least stopped by and done something purposeful to their posts fairly recently. They are likely to still be playing, likely to still be interested in customers, likely to be findable.

People posted to the Tradesfolk for Hire thread knowing that their post would be deleted if they left it for more than 3 months. If they have allowed the post to go unedited that long, perhaps they no longer wish to advertise in this location?
