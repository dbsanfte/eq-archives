It's actually the UI_Character_server.ini file you need to edit. Under the Skills window section it's likely your settings are at 0, which basically makes it a box that doesn't exist. Depending on your resolution, it should look like this:

XPos1024x768=440
YPos1024x768=472
Width=300
Height=240
