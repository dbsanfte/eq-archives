PoK is your friend for Celestial Essence.

Near the fishing pond at the back of the zone you can buy the celestial solvent from the one dark elf vendor near the pool.

If you then stand with your back to the dark elf you just bought your CS from, go up the stairs to the right of the pond and enter the room on your right. This is the poison vendor room.

The dark elf straight ahead will sell you a mortar and pestle, and the one on your left will sell you all the Scent of Marr you could ever want for just over 1pp each iirc.

I usually sit in this room and make my CE, and then sell the mortar and pestle back when I'm finished.
