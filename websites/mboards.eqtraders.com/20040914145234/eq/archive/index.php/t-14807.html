Tudani
The speed granted has nothing to do with the level of the shaman who makes the potions.

For most potions that have an effect that varries (such as DS potions) the effect of the potion varries with the level of the person who drinks the potion. I believe (though am not totally sure) that sow potions work like this.
Azirux
Tudani is correct. Thecharacter drinking the potion is the one "casting", so it uses the drinkers level as far as spell effects and duration.
