The first question is.. what level are you?

At 30+ combines without a skillup, my first thought is... are you level 47?

If you are, you can't gain a skillup until you level. If you're above 47, i would stongly recomend you just gather components for combines as you hunt (paying for them is just going to cost you dearly, seeing the silly prices people want for components in the bazaar).

For 5k, if you had build points, you could buy it from 1-200 at a trainer. Paying 5k for 30 combines.... makes no sense. If you can gain levels, do it and use the build points. If you're level 65.. there is no reason not to farm the components from an area where lots of components drop. Not all the components you get would be your classes, but some will.

The practice research runes are also an option.
