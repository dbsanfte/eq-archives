Ooohhh.. that "large". It has nothing to do the race-related-size, TINY, SMALL, MEDIUM, LARGE, GIANT are just related to whether they'd fit in some bags.

If a human can wear it, it is medium and the correct size for what you need.

If a halfling or wood elf or gnome can wear it then you have small.

If a troll or ogre can wear it is would be large

(not all-inclusive, but just a few key races you could look for).
