Zuggwugger,

Well, let's just say that after you hit 122 (embroidery needles) it starts getting rather expensive in terms of either plat or time. To trivial out fine plate at 188 will be "relatively" cheap, 6-8k - assuming that you hunt for your own padding. If you do, say, ornate chain and electrum ornate chain to avoid doing the "Padding Quest" until after you hit 148, then tack on another 12k or so (say figure 20k total to get to 188 going that route).

The it get's really expensive in terms of either time or plat. You can go the "fast" expensive route of making mistletoe cutting sickles (about 300pp a combine) from 189 to 250 - uses all store-bought stuff, costs about 400k I'd guess to get to 250 going that route. (You'll make part of it back selling sickles, but not until you've gotten up a ways). Or you can go the less expensive route of skilling up on shadowscream, but that requires you to kill lots of mobs in their 30s or so- so not really an option for awhile.

Stick with it, though - you can still make some plat selling banded and plate, enough to pay for smithing get you decent stuff for someone in their teens and 20s. Then you can level for a bit more and then start working on shadowscream when you are a bit higher up. Use the auction channel-I've seena few people asking for fine plate, but generally don't make it any more, and will start referring them to you. Good luck!
