Galain
When you look up a recipe it tells you if it is a restricted combine. For example Tunarean Scout Boots (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=7867&menustr=035000000000) are a restricted combine. This is shown in the following line:

To make tunarean scout boots (Tailoring: Yield 1, trivial >250), (Restricted) Combine the following in a fier`dal sewing kit

If it doesn't have the restricted label, anyone can make it. Also, you can click on the restriction to find out what the restriction is. For example, some items are Race restricted, while others are current skill level restricted.

What everyone else is failing to mention is that at skill level 210 + Geerlok, your effective skill is 220, which is the required skill level to make the ethereal swatches, but you can still get skill points from 210 to 212 since they trivial at 212.

Hence, 210-212 on ethereal swatches = "freebie" so long as you have a Geerlok, which are easily purchased in the bazaar.
