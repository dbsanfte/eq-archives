About the most efficient way is to take over the grimling caves in TM. You'll get about one peice per clearing, however windstones and a variety of 10pp+ gems drop fairly often. The acrylia runes are worth keeping too since some sell for 6pp or more.
If you're quick about it you'll have a good deal of time before the grimlings start spawning again allowing you to wander around the general area and take out alien dudes for the occational 3 brick drop. Just stear clear of the wolfs and bats and be prepared to be rooted a lot.

Acrylia caverns apperantly has a good drop rate, however it's a much higher level zone with casters galore and no real good safe spot to pull too.
