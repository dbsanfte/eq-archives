I believe this is a Make Poison skill and Shamans cannot make poison. Therefore you will get the "trivial" message simply because you cannot gain skill increases in a skill you can't learn.

You would get the same thing if your shaman tried say Tinkering, or if I as a rogue were to try Alchemy.
