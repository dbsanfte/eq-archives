It is somewhat complicated... and requires a whole series of steps. I will try and layout the sequence regardless of the metal used (will call it planar metal... substitute the proper type):

1. Planar Ore + Distilled (or Purified) Mana Vial = Enchanted Planar Ore [SMITHING]
2. Raw Diamond + "Proper Liquid" + "Proper Imbue Spell (by caster)" = Colored Diamond of ... [CAST BY CLASS 61-65 Level Spell]
3. Matching Imbued Diamond + Enchanted Planar Ore + Blank Emblem = Emblem of ... [JEWELRYCRAFT]
4. Planar Essence Components (several) = Planar Temper [BREWING]
5. Brick of Planar Ore + Planar Temper + File = Planar Rings [SMITHING]
6. Planar Rings + Planar Temper + Tanaan Smithing Hammer = Planar Chain Jointing [SMITHING]
7. Brick of Planar Ore [2] + Planar Temper + Tanaan Smithing Hammer = Sheet of Planar Ore [SMITHING]
8. Planar Rings [1-3]+ Chainmail Pattern + Planar Temper + Emblem of ... (step 3) + Tanaan Smithing Hammer = Planar Chain Mail [SMITHING]
9. Planar Chain Jointing + Sheet of Planar Ore [1-3] + Superb Leather Padding + Emblem of ... (step 3) + Planar Temper + Tanaan Smithing Hammer + Full Plate ... Mold = Planar Plate Mail [SMITHING]

Emblems and Hammer returned on Failure. Emblem is consumed on Success.

I don't have drop information... and haven't made any myself, but this is what I believe to be accurate. Did I miss anything?

EDIT - Corrected Item 5. (no Hammer needed)
