AS indicated below, this quest is linked on the front page. You have to complete the quest to use the recipe, so even though I've posted the recipe below, you can't use it until you do the quest.

This is the complete text.

Make sure you turn in the items 4 at a time - 1 per window. Not in a stack of 20.

There are recipes to be gotten from the Pottery Supplies Merchants, Malkidiv and Hiloan in Abysmal. Hail them and they tell you the following:

Hiloan O`Yviania glances at you and holds your stare for a moment before speaking. 'I don't suppose you have any skill at all throwing clay or at the kiln, because if you do I could use your help with some of the more [intricate items] that the fools around here are always breaking.'

You say "What intricate items?"

Hiloan O`Yviania says 'I have been given the unsatisfying task of replacing [specialty items] for the gardeners and the folks that deal in poisons and other such things, but I just don't have time for it. I have my own research into the native clay to work on. There appear to be stone workers made out of a type of clay that was dug up long ago, when this land was much more fertile and magical. The clay of these [ancient stone workers] is more powerful and useful than the rest. If you are interested in helping Malkidiv out with lesser tasks, talk to him. He can also refine chunks of broken stone workers for you.'

You say "What specialty items?"

Hiloan O`Yviania says 'Let's put you to work. You don't have to be a skilled potter to handle this. Oh, and, well, I'll need you to get some clumps of refined Taelosian stone worker clay from Malkidiv. He can refresh the stone for you, making it into useable clay, if you can find some. I won't need the ancient stuff for these tasks. Hand me the clumps and I'll get you started. Work hard for me and I'll reward you with the secret recipe that Malkidiv and I share. Since what I need is more difficult to obtain, I'll be quicker to reward you.'

You say "ancient stone workers?"

Hiloan O`Yviania says 'I hear they roam deep in the interior of the continent. They say something about a temple, but I really don't have time to keep up with that sort of prattle. The parts of these ancient golems are more potent and I can use as much as you can bring me. If you do, I'll restore and refine it and give you the bulk of it back.'

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Malkidiv U`Ycionuz says 'Hello. Thanks for stopping by. Are you, by chance, interested in helping me with some of the more [menial tasks] I have to get done around here?'

Malkidiv U`Ycionuz says 'Well, I am responsible for keeping the ship stocked with plates, pots and other simple supplies, but with all of the research I'm working on and my experiments with the amazing local [clay], I just don't have time to keep up. Wayfarers are notoriously hard on dinnerware. Can you [help] me out? And if you have any spare clumps of refined ancient clay, please speak with Hiloan, she needs help making some of the more complicated items used on the ship.'

You ask, "Clay?"

Malkidiv U`Ycionuz says 'Ah! Yes, amazing stuff. It seems that the locals have a way of making clay that is so powerful they can use it to make stone workers! It's fantastic, and I just can't get enough of it. If you come across any, please give it to me. Parts of broken golems seem to work the best. If you do, I'll restore and refine some of it for you."'

You say "I will help."

Malkidiv U`Ycionuz says 'Great! We've discovered that this new clay makes wonderfully durable plates. Of course, there's no point in making durable plates, that's just a challenge to these people to try to break them. With this clay we can make thinner plates that are just as strong and take up less room. Here, take these bits of broken plates and this bonding agent and re-fire the plates. Bring them back to me as soon as you can. I'm grateful for the help. If you can keep helping me out, I might be willing to share with you our secret method for refining the stone worker clay.'

Turn in several stacks of Wayfarer Plates (5 stacks, I think).

Malkidiv U`Ycionuz says 'Thank you for your efforts.'

Malkidiv U`Ycionuz says 'Excellent. You're obviously too talented to be wasted fixing plates. We could use a lot of pots. Our cooks are just as clumsy as our patrons. Take these pot molds and blocks of clay and fire them in a kiln. I can use a lot of these things, we use them for storage, cooking, breaking over heads. . . All the usual stuff. We don't need quality here, just quantity. Bring them back to me quickly or we'll run out soon.'

Turn in several stacks of Wayfarer Pots (5 stacks, I think).

Malkidiv U`Ycionuz says 'Wonderful! You are certainly good at working with a kiln, and you've been a great help to me. I have a somewhat more complicated task to be done, if you have the time. We go through mugs here faster than an ogre goes through chocolate. Take this clay and these flasks of water and turn them on a pottery wheel until you have something that resembles a mug. Handles would be helpful, but are not necessary. Then take the unfired mug and these Wayfarers seals and carefully fire them in the kiln. Don't worry, those seals are stamped onto tough sea serpent skin, they won't burn. Bring me the mugs when you're done.'

Turn in your stacks of Wayfarer Mugs.

Malkidiv U`Ycionuz says 'Fantastic! You have been of great service to the Wayfarers Brotherhood. If you can help me with my next task, I'd be willing to give out one of the Wayfarers' trade secrets. This task will require more effort and skill than those I've been asking you to do. Some of our scouts use ceramic shafts for their arrows. These require some skill to make properly and have to be made well. I can't just trust just anyone to make them. Take this clay, these flasks of water and this special dye and use the pottery wheel to form them into unfired shafts. Then, take these molds and fire the shafts. The dye is important, as each of our scouts likes to have their own colors on the shafts. Something about competing over who kills the most enemies or something like that.'

Turn in your stacks of arrows.

Malkidiv U`Ycionuz says 'You've been a great help already. I'd feel very guilty asking you to help out any further. There are others that can do that work for us. For all of your efforts, I'd be honored to teach you the secret of refining chunks of broken stone worker into refined Taelosian clay and chunks of broken ancient stone worker into refined ancient Taelosian clay.' Malkidiv pulls a small text from his coat and leans forward to show you a few of the pages. What he shows you seems amazingly simple once you've seen it. You think that with practice you might be able to get better results than Malkidiv gives to those that he refines clay for. Just place a single chunk of broken Taelosian stone worker onto a pottery wheel and you will receive clump of refined Taelosian clay.'

Note, the combine works in the Kiln, not on the wheel as indicated in the quest text.
