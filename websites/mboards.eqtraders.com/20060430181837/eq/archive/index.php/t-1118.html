The only such recipie(s) exist in Troll Cultural Smithing.

Take 1 Obsidean Shard, add various stuff in the Troll Forge. Then add more stuff to the result, again in the Troll Forge.

You can get a Polished Obsidean Shard, a Hateful Polished Obsidean Shard (Innorruk Only), a Fearful Polished Obsidean Shard (Cazic Only, so why bother?) or a Vengeful Polished Obsidean Shard (Rallos Only, so again why bother?).

Given that the resulting weapon is pretty-much a "pre-epic" backstabber for Rogues, I forsee Innorruk's being the only Imbue ever done.
