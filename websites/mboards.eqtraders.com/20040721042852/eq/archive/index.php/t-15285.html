Ngreth Thergn
The real redason is not "dumbing down"

The real reason is not to make it easier on the players...

The real reason was to make it easier on the developers.

If they wanted an effect that was +2 mana regen, +4 HP regen and 10% haste... they would have to create a new spell.

Then when they wanted to add +3 mana regen, +4 HP regen, and 12% haste... They would have to create a new spell.

you get the idea.

Creating an item is easier for them than creating a spell. (though balancing an item is another matter entirely but not part of why they did this...)

This allows them to play more with what effects are on an item, without having to create a new spell for the focus effect. It still does not cure them from that, but it allows them more flexability without creating a new spell.

they could even make a new mana robe "Acursed Mana robe" it does not have the effect of +30 mana -60 hp instant with a recast... instead... it is +10 Mana regen -20 HP regen which comes out about the same without the clicking :) (or make the HP part worse say -30 HP regen to make up for the fact you don't ahve to click)

basically it gives them more freedom.
