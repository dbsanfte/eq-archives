Greetings,

From my own experience, this is what I've been selling best since regularly making higher-level combos. I've not got into planar tailoring yet, so there is obviously even more money to be made! :)

Velium Mastodon cloaks: I know others don't appear to like them, but I've sold about 8 or 9 now for around 12k each.

Fleeting quivers: Regular fast sellers at 3 - 4k each.

Black Pantherskin: Sells well on my server. I concentrate on tunics, arms, legs and cloaks. They tick over nicely at a price of 1500 - 3k per item, depending on the competition in the Bazaar!

Cobalt drake belts: Nice sellers at 700pp - ish

Acrylia studded: Sleeves and cloaks sell out as fast as I can put them on my trader. 6 - 700 for sleeves, 800 - 1k for cloaks. I can understand the cloaks, for the +10 WIS, but have been surprised how quickly the sleeves have been snapped up.

Leather padding (of course! :p ): Sells for 60pp per padding on my server (Mith Marr). I put mine at 50 and it's gone in minutes. I don't go out specifically hunting for hides for padding - more of a by-product of hopper killing!

I'm doing a bit of everything at the moment, desperate for that last tailoring skill point. Tigeraptor and Arctic Wyvern armour is tough to move, and sits on my trader for quite a while. I've not bothered with Velium hound combines at all, as they are far too expensive, and these days there appear to be better items available in the Bazaar.

Hope this helps, and I'll be posting an analysis of my progression from 200 - 250 once I hit the magic figure.
