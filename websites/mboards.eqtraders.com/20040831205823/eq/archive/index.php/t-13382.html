As an example, on Ayonae, the price for Bloodmetal bricks ranges from diddly (10 pp) to squat (40 pp). War Wraith Blood nudges 200 pp if I run out and am unlucky, 100 pp is more normal. And Highly Polished Gems run ~19k. The completed earring usually sells for 30k - 32k.

IMHO, the profit margin of buying HPGs for 19k and selling the earring for 30k pretty much sucks, if it's there at all, so I buy fewer gems and make sure not to pay more than 16k, which gives me some breathing room.
