The Grandmaster’s Guide to Brewing v1.0
By Ceryn Bwahahahahahaha of the Stromm Server

So you want to become a master brewer huh. Well then, you’ve come to the right place! I made this guide with the intention of making a quick, cheap, easy way to become a master brewer. I also made it so you wouldn’t have to forage or look for crappy ground spawns for 90000000 years; everything you see in here is sold by an NPC. Most of the stuff you can find in bars, personally I prefer the Felwithe bar that’s right above the brew barrel since everything is in close proximity to the barrel. The Faydwer Shaker supplies are sold in many places; East Freeport, HHP, and Halas seem to be the favorites. If you’re looking for where you can find these supplies, check the EQTC Brewing Supply Locations page. I hope you find my guide to your liking, please feel free to email any updates you’ve found to erock32@nni.com. Thanks, and enjoy!

NOTE: If you have the PoP you can go to the Plane of Knowledge and make Fetid Essences until skill 122. They’re made by combining 1 Fishing Grubs and 1 Water, unsure of the container. The fishing grubs vendor is named Caden Zharik and is at loc -344, 769. You must combine in the New Tanaan Brew Barrel or it won’t work. Good luck.


0 – 21 Train Away

Train Brewing to 21 skill at your nearest class trainer.

21 – 31 Mmmm Beer…

Short Beer = Combine 1 Barley, 1 Hops, 1 Malt, and a cask inside the brew barrel.

31 – 41 Mead = Fun Times

Mead = Combine 1 Yeast, 1 Hops, 1 Malt, and a cask inside the brew barrel.

41 – 51 Short Ale for short people

Short Ale = Combine 1 Barley, 1 Hops, 1 Water and a cask inside the brew barrel.

51 – 68 Why does that barfly keep winking at me? STAY AWAY!!!

Ale = Combine 1 Barley, 1 Malt, 1 Water and a cask inside the brew barrel.

68 – 135 Good Ol’ Tujim

Ol’ Tujim’s Fierce Brew = Combine 1 Barley, 1 Hops, 1 Malt, 1 Yeast, and a cask inside the brew barrel.

135 – 188 Shake shake shake! Shake shake shake! Shake that booty!

Faydwer Shaker = Combine 1 Elvish Wine, 1 Gnomish Spirits, 1 Dwarven Ale, 1 Mead and a cask inside the brew barrel.

188 – 248 The brew of champions.

Minotaur Hero’s Brew = Combine 3 Malt, 1 Yeast, 2 Flasks of Water, 2 Short Beer, and a cask inside the brew barrel.

I've been trying to level several skills at once - as a result I'm not necessarily going the "fastest" route but one that gives me easy skill-ups in conjunction with making me dyes/tempers for my 190+ smithing/tailoring:

If you are trying to raise several skills at once - particularly if your race is capable of cultural tailoring on robes, or you're a smith - you can make your own tempers, and raise your brewing and smithing skills together:

Are youplaying in LDON a lot? Then add tempers to the "quick" list. The recipe is simple -

(ingredient) + water = temper, trivial at 127 or less.
(ingredient) = sand verbena, gargoyle granite, goblin bones, pondweed, ice lichen

Working on Velious smithing? Brew velium temper:
Coldain heater = allspice, brown algae, shotglass, water flask (all store-bought, trivial 156)
Coldain velium temper = celestial essence(4), coldain heater (trivial 136)

Able to cultural smith? Brew dyes - which is especially good for forage classes/races!:
http://www.eqtraders.com/recipes/recipe_page.php?article=180&rsa=Brewing&sb=item&sub=dye&menustr=080030035000

Vials of prismatic dye are trivial at 68, all store-bought items, and you can easily sell them in the bazaar to coup your cash back and then some!

Even if you are not working tailoring/smithing yourself, you can always find buyers for your racial dyes and LDON tempers. Due to these recipes nicely filling the 160ish-220ish tradeskill gaps, they are quite heavily in demand and I find I get quick skill-ups on them. I am also brewing Ice Wine, made with Stunted Arctic Roots (new forage in the EF dungeons, I believe) which is giving me a nice set of skill-ups and produces a weak sow potion which I give away to newbies.
