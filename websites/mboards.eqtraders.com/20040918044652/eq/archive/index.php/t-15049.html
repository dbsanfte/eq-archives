Qaladar Bragollach
Who ever said there was 5% failure on trivial combines?

Raise your skill more. The failure rate will drop. There used to be a floor on success rate such that you always had a 5% chance to fail (or succeed) regardless of your skill. That doesn't exist anymore but even then you had to be somewhat above the trivial point to reach it.
Tinile
You don't drop down to the 5% failure rate until you are about 40 points over the trivial level, so keep skilling up.
andyhre
With a geerlok, a 75 skilled tinker has effective skill of 79. At 79, a 75-trivial combine has a 74.25% chance.

To reach the 95% level for a 75-trivial combine, you need to reach 95 skill. This is not that hard, making either spyglasses or stalking rpboes, both of which sell profitably in the bazaar. You can also vendor the stalking probes at a loss of only 1.5pp per success.
