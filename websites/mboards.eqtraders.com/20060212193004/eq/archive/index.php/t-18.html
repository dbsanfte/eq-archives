These is where folks who can imbue gems to be used in making tradeskill items for certain dieties can offer their services. If you are looking for someone to imbue a gem for you, see the next post in this thread for a regularly updated list of imbuers for hire. I have carried over people who had signed up on the original list on the old message boards, so don't be surprised to see some names on the master list who do not have posts providing their information in the rest of the thread.

For more information on the possible uses of imbued gems, see the Imbued Gem Info (http://www.eqtraders.com/secrets/imbued_gemlist.htm) page.

If you wish to be available to imbue for other people, please reply with your name, diety for whom you can imbue, and anything that might help people find you. Please only post if you are willing to do imbues on commission for other folks. If you scribed imbue spells for several dieties before the patch that removed this option, feel free to list all of the dieties for whom you can imbue. If your name is already on the master list from the original on the old boards, feel free to post again providing more contact information in the event that someone has trouble finding you.

(Please note: I am taking advantage of my moderator abilities to sticky this thread and am volunteering to maintain the regularly updated list. I do this only on my own initiative and as a public service for our server, not as any kind of official service of EQ Traders. If anyone would rather this be done differently or not done at all, just let me know! :))
