Hello fellow fletchers,
I lurk here a lot but never post. After trying acrylia arrows and Karana cultural arrows and being disgusted with the skillup rate, I had all but given up on fletching. After reading some of the posts here yesterday I decided to try the Darkwood bow(stave and hemp string) combines to 215 and see how it went. Here is my data:

Skillup.........Combines/...Successes/..Failures.....Cost
206.......................5/...............5/..........0........110p 1g 9s 0c
207.......................8/...............8/..........0........176p 3g 0s 4c
208.......................4/...............4/..........0..........88p 1g 5s 2c
209.....................25/.............23/..........2........960p 4g 9s 2c
210.....................19/.............19/..........0........418p 7g 2s 2c
211.....................17/.............17/..........0........374p 6g 4s 6c
212.....................30/.............30/..........0........661p 1g 4s 0c
213.....................12/.............12/..........0........264p 4g 5s 6c
214.......................5/...............5/..........0........110p 1g 9s 0c
215.....................52/.............50/..........0......1555p 5g 1s 8c

Total.................177/...........173/..........4......4719p 8g 1s 0c


I used geerlok and had 280 Dex, 181 Wis. The 181 wisdom leads me to agree that wis/int does not affect successes. But the higher of wis/int/dex affects skillups. Each success cost me 22p,0g,3s,8c and each failure cost me 226p,8g,0s,9c.
