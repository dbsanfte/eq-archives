Maddoc-the-Wayfarer
These recipes were modified slightly, to take into account the different types/sizes of eggs.

You must now make "Egg Batter" which is used in place of the eggs in the recipes. Different types of eggs will yeild different amounts of batter (just like dough).

-- Maddoc
Auricula
Thank you, Korendir :)

Apparently the recipes were changed and instead of 2 snake eggs, you need two Egg Batter.

Egg Batter = egg + milk in mixing bowl.

Snake egg yields one batter; griff egg yields 7 batter.
