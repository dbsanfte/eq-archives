Oualawouzou
Note that the trivial of an arrow combine is easy to determine: it's set by the most expensive component you are using. :)

So, take the arrows that trivial at 102 and check for the next highest component (no matter if it's the fletch, the knock, etc..., only the price matters) and keep it up. Up untill 202, it's relatively cheap (from 180ish to 202, it costs about 70-75 per 20 combines, and you can make over 50pp back by selling the arrows to the merchants). After 202, it's VERY expensive.

Currently, I have the choice of going with bows (which costs over 200pp per combine), the Luclin stuff (I don't feel like farming and I try to avoid the Bazaar when possible, a personnal thing...) and the Karana arrows (which cost around 35pp per combine)...

So I'd say fletching is not at all expensive to get up to 202. After 202... ouch.
