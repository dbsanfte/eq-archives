Claws are fairly common off the Regrua. Maybe 1 in 3 will drop claws, but each time they will drop 2. 2 claws produced 4 regrua meat which produce 8 buckets without failures. At 250 baking, I see about an 70% success rate with Claw to Bucket conversion.

The problem is, these mobs hit for about the same damage as the Avatar of War, just with less hit points. They are not the type of mob that is usually killed in a group. The only claws we see is when we are going after a mini-boss or fighting our way to Coirnav. And of course, lots of bakers ask for some to make for themselves (for platter) as well as for others in the guild.
