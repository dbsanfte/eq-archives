Arctic Wyvern armor seems like a pretty good option for you. Here is what it requires.

Artic Wyvern Hide - Drops off wyverns in Cobalt scar, can usually buy the for around 15pp from the othmir vendors as people often dump them there, or you can go out and hunt em yourself.

Cod Oil - Cobalt Cod + Water brewed. The cod is fished from Cobalt scar, good thing to do if you are recovering from soloing.

Velium Studs - Required 4 velium tempers for 3 studs. The velium tempers cost around 9pp if you brew them, 26pp if you buy them from vendor in Thurgaddin.

Pattern - duh

Hope that helps.
