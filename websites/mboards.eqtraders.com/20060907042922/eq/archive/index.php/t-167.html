So I figured, well the chance to fizzle is low (5%) and the chance for a skill up is low (5%ish), so...knowing the streakiness of teh RNG, I did it on purpose a few times. So when I went to make my shawl, I did the same thing (Knowing my skill was far below the trivial and I was in the 5% range). I started/stopped my song a few times until I missed a note, closed my eyes and hit combine...came away with a shawl. I hope this helps others in the same situation (Using fizzles for spellcasters instead of missed notes).
