No trophy, because for some reason its not considered a true tradeskill (maxes out at 200 instead of 250, and you can keep using skill points after reaching a skill of 20).

The reward for maxing Fishing is that you're able to make Blessed Fishing Rods.

I guess the Fisherman's Companion could be considered a trophy, but since all you have to do is get the bones and turn them in, even someone with 0 Fishing skill could get it.
