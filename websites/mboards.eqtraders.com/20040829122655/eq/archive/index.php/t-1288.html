Dragnet
Plane of Nightmare is easy - follow the Wall away to the left avoiding mobs and you will soon find a stream. If you watch carefully you will find that there is a spot a little way out from the zone wall that is safe from all but Trains. This is one of my favourite places to fish as I get far less junk than most fishing spots and I also pull out hose lovely crawdad for baking in Butter ;)

PoV on the other hand is very hard to get hold of being tier 2. I asked a few people in the top guild on my server if they knew any fishermen. Then luckily I heard someone asking about the quest and I made their valorian discus bones in exchange for information and some spare fish.

Needless to say I love my fishing companion.
