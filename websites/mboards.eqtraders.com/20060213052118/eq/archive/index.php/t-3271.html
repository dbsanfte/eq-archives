Found a spot to farm Iksar blood and blue diamonds. :)

The hardest pirate ship in Dulak Harbor, the Windscorn, is populated by DE casters and Iksar melee. Had a group there for an hour and walked out with over a stack of iksar blood and one blue diamond. Not to mention all the elf blood and various racial meats and body parts...

My group consisted of mid/high 50's and the encounters got exciting at times with pulls of 3 or more. Very high aggro range on that boat. Experience rate was low for tons of dark blues.


From the other boats:
2nd hardest (Oceancrasher): gems (diamonds & lower) and a decent chain bracer
3rd hardest (Dandolak's Run): dry, but we got our cleric Temperance quest items we were after there
4th hardest (Lady Dirulia): gems (like you'd see drop off Seafuries) dropped like candy. I kid you not.
Easiest (Stormwave), nearest the zone line: was already camped :(
