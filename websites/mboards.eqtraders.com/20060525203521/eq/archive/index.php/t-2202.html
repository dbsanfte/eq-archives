It's VERY hard to say.

At this point, there are a LOT of people at or above 200 in most tradeskills. There are fewer people capable of doing some specific cultural stuff (I'm thinking Troll Smithing, for example), but there's also probably relatively little demand for the items, compared to the cost to raise those skills.

As far as doing tradeskills, do what you enjoy.

As far as supplying stuff for tradeskills, take a look around the bazaar and get a sense for what is selling for what prices, then think about how much time it will take you to get an item to sell, and if you're getting exp or having other fun getting that item.
