Originally posted by Collwyn

There are no "hell levels", but 180+ is where it will start to slow down and depending on the RNG, you might go 100+ combines without a skill gain at any point after that. You might hate the RNG now, but you'll love it when it gives you 3 skill gains in a row or 5 on a stack (yes, that happens too). Just stick with it, and try to space out your combine sessions so that you don't get too frustrated from no movement during a single session.
