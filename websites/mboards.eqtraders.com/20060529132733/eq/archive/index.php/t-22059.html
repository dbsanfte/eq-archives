I went to make the retired Ceinweins foraging compendium version 5 into a story mod :D

Download Foraging Compendium (zip-file) (http://bell.dk/psy/psytoo/storyForaging.zip)

I was tired of switching between browser and game multiple times a day to check out all the items I somehow get foraged every other second - I hate carrying some no drop druid epic around, since I am always out of room 24/7 ;)

I hope a few happy helpfull foragers on EQ Traders will be interested in trying this mod and give me feedback. I am looking for feedback regarding content update, quests related to foraging, items with forage modificator, experiences with Nature's Bounty, and wishes for the setup and suggestions to improve usability.

I am adding quest info concerning foraged items, with reference to who wrote it and where I found it. I am adding items, mostly from DoN zones, and updating old content when I find new information.

Feel free to try it out and send me feedback ingame or through this board for updates and comments.
Flaming about content will be ignored, eloquent polite complaints about source of information will be validated and the text removed or updated.
