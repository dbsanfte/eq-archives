Kulyen
Zone in, you have a zone wall behind you. Turn left or right (from where you are stood when you zone in), follow the zone wall until you see the water. If you go left, you will end up near the 'outer' camp which often has people at it kiting/killing the fiends. If you turn right, you'll end up at the water with some non-aggro frogs and will probably not see a single person or aggro mob while you fish.

For nightmare I don't seem to have any trouble at all.
Zone in. Go towards creek. The spot that has the two static spawns.
As you are facing them, there is a rock to the left of them and a small widening in the creek.
I fish there. I don't even bother with invis anymore.
Just avoid the aggro range of the mob closest to the rock on your way to this spot.
