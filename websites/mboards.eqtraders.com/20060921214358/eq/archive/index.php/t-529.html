Jackoal wrote
Anyone know what it's like to sit down and make 2600 celestrial essence?

Yup. I found the most efficient way to handle the gazillion sub combines was to work on them all simultaneously. So I would sit at the kiln in the Bazaar and imbue emeralds until I was oom, sit down, make enough CE's to process those emeralds through dusts and silks, then make dusts, then make silks. Go to the bank, drop off completed silks and pick up more regular swatches and emeralds and I'd be nearly FM again. If not, make more CE's, pick up more CE supplies. Sometimes I would work with a mule next to me so I wouldn't have to stand up or move (full of CE supplies, silks, emeralds, acid...)

I also used to carry a mortar-n-pestle and celestial solvent with me everywhere - and made CE's from dropped pages and stuff whenever I could, either soloing or in groups.
