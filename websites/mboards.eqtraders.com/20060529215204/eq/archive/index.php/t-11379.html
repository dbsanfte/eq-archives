Ok, step 1 - make your vial. This is the same as all the other trophies out there and is the one that also winds up with the grandmaster's bag.

Step 2 - make your seal. You need an eyerazzia from Hate, several different poisons and the vial made in step 1 - you turn all of them into a nice lady in the Karanas and you receive your seal. You do NOT get your vial back, its lost in the turn in. You now have a seal that summons coffin bottles.

Step 3 (optional) - repeat step 1 so that you have both a vial and a seal on your rogue.
