You are probably about 2 years late in trying to get much in terms of profit from crafting jewellery using store-bought ingredients.

Nowadays, they're pretty much useless, given the massive influx of better accessories into the market.

The only remote way of generating any form of profit from JC'ing would be to reach 240 (with Trophy) and make Tier 1 or Tier 2 PoP jewellery (ie. Glittering Rings of Judgement, Valorium Rings of Gallantry), and hope you don't fail so much that you end up losing money instead.

Some of us are praying for GoD for a revival of tradeskill relevance.
