Oh one thing with the MTP combines that made things easier on me (besides doing them in Rivervale) was doing subcombines on the fly to save space and decrease the number of bank swaps needed to mule stuff around. The only thing I made ahead of time in my last pass was jum jum spiced ale because making it on the fly didn't save all that much space and premade the intermediate combines of woven mandrake and steel boning. I had made too many jum jum cakes when I started the MTP path as well so they were made ahead of time too. Anyhow I would make a stack (or a little less) of whatever was needed for the final combine and just kept swapping between oven and mixing bowl - it really saved a lot of time compared to when I made all the subcombines ahead of time and was swapping them around between banks.
