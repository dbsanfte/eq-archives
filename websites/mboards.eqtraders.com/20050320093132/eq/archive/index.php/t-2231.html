Worn mana regen stacks up to 15 points.

This rule has changed a number of times. At one point it was "FT 1 stacks with FT 2, but FT 1 does not stack with FT 1". Later it was "15 points is claimed by verant, but they are wrong".

FT gear is hard to get. Mostly they are aquired by L 60 and above players on massive raids against very tough mobs.

There are a handful of possible aquireable FT items at your level. The solstice earring is a quest that you can buy all 9 components for -- it gives you a very good earring, with FT 1.

There is a FT 2 DROPable belt from BoT, but that's probably out of your reach.

There are FT 3 boots from CT, but you can't wear those until L 50.

There is a shawl you can quest for. This quest is long and hard, has 8 main steps. The end result is a good shawl with FT 3.

A better thing to look for would be focus items, or a pony.

A 8 k pp pony will allow you to meditate while standing/casting outdoors. Not cheap, but it works out to alot of FT.

Mana Preservation items are very good to get. They are as good, or better, than most flowing thought gear, in terms of effectiveness. The ODS is one (see above), another one is a crystalline torque. There are cheaper ones (like the Black Alloy Meddallion) that only work on L 45 and under spells.

Also think about getting spell casting haste, extended range, extended enhancement (makes buffs last longer), and even affliction haste (2) items.

I know! :)

no focus effect works for Complete heal, not the druid/shaman 75% heals.

It should work for any other of your instant heals.

it may or may not (depending on the focus effect) work for your heal over time spells.

it may or may not (depending on the focus effect) work for your GROUP (more than one person) healing spells. But then... not many clerics use these because of the HUGE agro generated.


I hope that helps :)

http://lucy.fnord.net is a good way to research focus effects :) you will typically see an etry like this:

Spell Haste IV:
1: Increase Spell Haste by 15%
2: Limit: Max Level(65)
3: Limit: Spell(Complete Healing excluded)
4: Limit: Min Casting Time(3.0 sec)
5: Limit: Effect(Percentage Heal excluded)


#3 aplies to clerics.
#5 applies to Druids and Shaman :)

So for a CLERIC this spell will work for any healing spell, up to level 65, that has a casting time of at least 3.0 seconds, EXCEPT Complete Heal.
