Grimlach
drops from flayed horror and skinless creatures in stomach event in torment.

you need to get to the southern most part of the zone and start killing tormentors (not slowable and summon) then make way upstairs on southernmost building. (bring a monk bard etc to lull one of the 2 door guards on each set of guards before the stairs). Work you way up to top of stairs and attack the named yellow con guy... starts with a b forget name but its obviously him and he looks like one of the 4 mouth guys... he will proc port you into his stomach (stomach event). Since rogues are buttwipes on stealing from the mobs in this event I suggest dropping down, clearing the 6 first drop mobs (if they see invis or keep goin if they dont and you dont want to risk dying). On 2nd drop there are very few mobs in yer room and probably wont agro. There are 4 mobs in this room, a couple mobs in hall, and about 5 mobs in next room. set up camp here and clear respawns until you are blue in the face (respawn is long like 45mins or so). Repeat until you have all the putrescent bloods you want. This will take a lot of hours of farming. If you can accomplish acquiring bloods, you will have no problem figuring out the rest of the combine components.
