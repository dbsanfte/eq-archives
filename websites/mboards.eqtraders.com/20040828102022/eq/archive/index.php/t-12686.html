I've done many hard LDoN and have never seen Zephyr. Come to think of it, I've never seen it period. I have seen Scintillation on LDoN normals, though rarely.

By the way, when they say that the components aren't "entirely" zone-specific, they don't mean it's just random. In LDoN, for example, there is a very clear pattern with each theme constantly having 2-3 of the same spell components (e.g., Guk hard will always have tons of Words of Divergence and Expertise). For other components (e.g., Rune of Tempest, Words of Torment), it's spread out pretty evenly. It's not just a level thing because the NPC levels are the same across the themes.

Also, LDoN hard doesn't necessarily give you better components than normal, just different ones. Ironically, most of the components I get from hard ones are for the (junk) high 50s spells.
