Nolrog
Those are two good places. I spent a lot of time in FoB getting the silks. The Ferrott is another good zone with a lot of spiderlings.

However, I found that the best place is Shadeweavers. If you kill the lesser shades, they drop shadeling silks and sometimes swirling shadows. Keep both. If you use shade silk thread and LQ rockhopper hides, you will make leather padding too. You cannot use this thread with regular pelts though. I found that they are both pretty easy to come by. The swirling shadows are used to make the shadowscream armor, which will take you up to 250 (if you choose to farm your way up here.)

Hope this helps.

have done about 4 chars to 190 smithing now solely for the shawl, and the best method i have found for getting the padding is this:

for pelts i took my wizard to EK, used his staff of temperal flux to pull about 20-25 mobs at a time (doesnt take more then a minute per pull), then used Jyls static pulse to AoE them, i try to pull everything i see to get the zone respawning and after about 45 minutes of doing this it goes smoothly. Have found that the AoE will kill everything except for the black spiders, usually they will run after the first aoe, but most stay together so i follow them and aoe again. you can get MQ and HQ cat pelts off the lions pretty fast, i get about 50 per 2 hours of AoE, then just cut them down to LQ. Also you can get snake eggs and spider silks, the snake eggs are now used for high lvl baking so you can either sell them or give to friend/guildie, and the spider silks sell well also or (if you dont have tailoring already for the shawl make Wu's armor with these) can give them also to friend/guildie i average about 200-250 silks per 2 hour hunt. I usually just hae Po9 and KEI and my health/mana never drop below 80. he is level 61 btw, cant remember level on the AoE tho. Move between the ares, up on the mountain where tarbul roams, down in the plains.

the spiderling silks are a little harder, before they implimented the ogre newbie quests i got all of mine in feerrott, but now i use my ranger (leveled to 30 solely for farming :P) and track them in Nek forest. if you sold the snake eggs/spider silks you can use the money to buy ling silks in bazaar (did with my last and finaly char) easy way to get both of what you need.

Hope this helps as it would have helped me when i started that tedious shawl so long ago ;)
