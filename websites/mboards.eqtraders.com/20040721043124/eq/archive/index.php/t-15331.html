Froni Thunderpaws
In Jaggedpine there are two cave systems - one is full of darkpaw gnolls (don't go there) and the other one has the good guy gnolls, the zone boundary to Nedaria's Landing, basilisks, and basilisk genitors. The genitors drop eggs and usually drop a lot of them - I think I have had 5 drop at a time.

Those basilisks are a lot tougher than the others that drop eggs. The ones in Lavastorm, Rathe, and Butcherblock are lower level - but if you can kill the ones in Jaggedpine (genitors and regulars) with no down time - go there. You'll get stacks quickly.
