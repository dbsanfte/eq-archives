gnormy
I think you need to approach Gates tradeskills with a different mindset. In previous expansions - tradeskills were something where you went and farmed parts and made tons of items. Drops being pretty common overall with few roadblocks. Gates is anti-farming. Parts will come - but very slowly. If you are expecting to be able to farm parts - its just not possible. Even on guild raids killing at will - there will only be a handful of drops. This means the items that are made from Gates will retain value longer (assuming they had any to begin with). It also means that rather than saying - today I"m going to go make x number of things to sell, you'll find yourself saying, I spent a day killing in Gates - what can I make with what I have.
Yes - its different - isnt that what an expansion is about?
