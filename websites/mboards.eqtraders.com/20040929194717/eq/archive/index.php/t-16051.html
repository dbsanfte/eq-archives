Enchanted Velium Bits require a mininum modified skill of 165. This means you can attempt them at 158 raw skill plus a geerlok, or earlier if you have a skill mod item with more than 5% smithing mod on it. If you don't use a smithing mod item at all, you have to wait until 165 raw skill.
