Cheese is only sold in Jagged Pine Forest. Well, the cheese used to make patty melts is only available there. I've seen other cheeses elsewhere, but those wont work for patty melts. The easiest thing that I've found to do is make an alt and park them in the room with the cheese vendor and banker. Then when I need cheese, log out, log in alt, buy cheese, put in shared bank slot, log out alt, log in main... presto cheese in bank. This is particularly useful for my Cazic Thulian Troll who isn't exactly like too much in JaggedPine.
