Any translucent model (Garg or Golem) in Skyshrine will drop velium. Horanaf
Here is how I did my time in SS farming velium (222 smithing as of today, no idea where to go next :) ). Start at the WL zone in, kill those first 4 mobs. ten go right, and kill the 4 golems and 2 drakes that are in the 2 rooms. then clear to the bank, gettinig the roamer gargs, the 2 golems in the brewing room, and the 2 in the bank room. Then clear to the CS zone in area, clearing all the golems you see. Grab those 6 and the drakes/wurms that come with them, and kill them all.

By this time, the WL ZI golems are spawning again. So rinse and repeat until you are tired of it.

Drops are mediocre to great, really just random. some cycle i would get about 5 small pieces' worth, some cycles i would get 2 stacks worth of velium.

Feel free to add/subtract spawns based on killing time, as all this is with a 65 paladin with about 6800 hp self buffed, 41% haste and a 51/37 weapon.
