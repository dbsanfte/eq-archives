Angelsyn
In your Program Files\Everquest directory, you'll find a file named something like: BZR_Adelie_xegony.ini

In it you'll find all the prices for all the items you've ever sold. The longer this list, the longer it takes to add new items and the longer it takes for EQ to set you up as a Trader.

I went in and deleted a huge amount of the old junk I'd sold and my set-up time is much faster now.

P.S. The items are listed by the price in copper pieces. :D
