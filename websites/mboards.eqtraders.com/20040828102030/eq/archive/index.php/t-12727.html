Cerpicio
Enchanters are a lot of fun to play. There are many things you can do just screwing around. Minor Illusion is always fun to pull out while your group is medding or a guild is gathering for a raid. All of the racial illusions are fun to play with, too. Someone will make a comment about the "cute gnome" and I'll pull out the ogre or troll illusions.

We can charm anything (ie. not limited to animals or undead). I have my Preserved Chokodai Vocal Cords (clicky Voice Graft) for when I finally get Dire Charm. We're supposed to be getting a "doppleganger" spell to turn us into whatever we have targetted.

We can enchant things and make vials of mana for profit. We can sit in PoK and cast buffs, too. We don't have to worry about seperate CHA gear when buying from NPCs.

I can get into any race's home town, which is fun for exploring.

Crowd Control is fun. Stopping a 60+ mob dead in its tracks is pretty impressive. Saving the group from a bad pull is thrilling.

Faction usually isn't a problem for us, either. Got an uppity Erudite vendor? One cast of Alliance and he's your best friend!

I'm not saying Enchanters are the best class, but they seem to have a lot of potential for fun stuffs outside of groups and XPing. I'm not as familiar with other classes, but I can't think of any that have so many "extra-curricular activities."


Just sayin'
-- C.
