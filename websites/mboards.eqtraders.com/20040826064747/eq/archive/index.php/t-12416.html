Re-read the recipe.

To make the spell: minor summoning: water (lvl 16) ...

combine the following in a elemental grimoire, or arch magus grimoire :

words of tyranny
spell: elemental water
shark skin


Put the above 3 items in your book and hit combine. :)


Once you have 2 copies (one to scribe and a spare), put one copy of the spell + words of domination in your book, & hit combine, to get Spell: Lesser Summoning Water (lvl 20) (assuming no failure.)


Once you have 2 copies (one to scribe and a spare), put one copy of the spell + words of dimension + ice of velious in your book, & hit combine, to get Spell: Summoning Water (lvl 24) (assuming no failure.)




Good luck!
