Lothay
It seems a little silly to be to announcing this publicly, but here goes.

As of the end of this week I will no longer be playing EverQuest. It's been a long road. I've played the game since April 3 of 1999, and headed one of the oldest guilds in EQ, Spiritwalkers on the Tunare server.

I've played until I'm blue in the face. I've played marathon sessions lasting 18+ hours. I've tradeskilled, camped, ground, and quested until the very thought of ever playing again leaves me cold. Why then did I stay so long?

The answer is simple. I continued to play EverQuest for the people. Those I met in-game and through communities like this one and EQInterface and the tunare.tradeskills channel made for such great friends (and sometimes rivials or foes) that I was compelled to stay.

I will be continuing my role here at EQ Traders Corner as a moderator. My volunteer efforts to help Niami and Ngreth run this excellent resource will continue. You just won't see me playing the game.

I've begun playing City of Heros (http://cityofheros.com), and I'll be on the Justice server there. If you're looking for me, any member of the Super Group Heros Inc. will be able to point you to me.

I'll be logging in to EQ a few more times over the next few days to turn over guild leadership and to bestow gifts on friends and guildmates alike. Feel free to stop by and /wave or /hug.

Thank you all for a great 5 years.
