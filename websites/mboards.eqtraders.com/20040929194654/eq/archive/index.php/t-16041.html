Actually there are to alternatives to DSP. The scarlet Desert and Mons letalis.

Mons letalis hoppers have the highest pelt rate drop which includes FLawless and superb, But its has the worst hunting conditions and can be very dangerous. Not to mention the pathing had been messed up for some time. I have not been there since the recent collision changes so I don't know if things are still messed up there pathing wise or not. But it certainly was a viable alternative for me when the hoppers were not up in DSP.

The Scarlet desert drop rate is about the same as DSP. There are fewer hoppers available and they are spread out more. Pulling them is the hardest part, but once you get back to the ramp to the mesa killing them was easy.

DSP is the safest and easiest place to get hides, at least when the hoppers are up.
