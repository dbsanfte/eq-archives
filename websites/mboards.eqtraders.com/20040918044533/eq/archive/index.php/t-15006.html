Boleslav
I would feel safe guessing the trivial is 335.

As to the farm vs. try now argument… Let's assume that the base level for success at max skill (252) is 50%. Looking at the formulas derived to estimate probability of success, the probability drops by 1% per point of modified skill until it gets to 5%.

In this case, someone with an unmodified skill of 220 would have a modified skill of 231 with a geerlok. This is 21 points lower than max skill, so you would expect the base probability of success to be about 29% (50 – 21). You would figure on average that four attempts worth should get it done for you.

However, the probability of failing all four times at 220 is still 25.4%. So having four sets of components is no guarantee. You have to do 7 attempts before you have less than a 10% chance of failing them all. It can look pretty bleak sometimes.

Let's look at this from the point of view of someone buying most/all their components in the bazaar. On E'ci, you might spend 100pp per skillup attempt if you are at all careful in buying your supplies. If you average a skillup in 20 attempts, you are looking at ~2k per skillup. Getting to 240 unmodified gets you to max modified skill. So for another 44k you can give yourself a 21% better chance per attempt to make your item. In this case, the 4 combines gives you over a 93% chance to have at least one be successful. Compare that to the cost of 6 – 9 extra saps and those skillups start to look pretty cheap.
