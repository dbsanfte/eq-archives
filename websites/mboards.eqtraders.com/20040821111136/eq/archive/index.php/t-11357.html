Not to take anything away from the tinkering skill, but if you really love to fish then you must do this quest:
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2236

The quest is both simple and difficult. All you have to do is hand the barbarian NPC, standing by the pool of water in PoK, two Nightmare Cichlid bones and two Valorian Discus bones. The first trick is to be high enough level to fish in the Plane of Nightmare and the Plane of Valor. The second trick is being able to live long enough in the Plane of Nightmare to be able to pull up the fish you need. Fishing in the Plane of Valor is actually safe. Or you can search the bazaar for the bones and just buy them.

Doing this quest will get you a Fisherman’s Companion. You can use the Companion to summon an endless supply of fishing poles. The only drawback is that they are no drop so you can’t pass them out to fellow fishermen, even though they’re no rent. And each fishing pole can summon an endless supply of ale. The booze is no rent but it can be passed out to everybody around you. So if you keep your Fisherman’s Companion in your inventory you’ll always have a fishing pole. It’s just a shame it doesn’t also summon bait.

I have four characters I play on a fairly regular basis. They each have one of these.
