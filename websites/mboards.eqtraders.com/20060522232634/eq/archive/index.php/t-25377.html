On Quell the price for Midnight Stones varied from like 300p to 700p. But just recently someone has been asking like 5K for Midnight Stones and has driven up prices (to anywhere from 1.5K to like 4.5K). It doesn't look like there are any buyers at those prices though.

I'm a Dark Elf Smith at 281 in Skill with the +15% VT gloves, BM3 and Salvage 3. I basically refuse to pay more than say 500p for a Midnight Stone. Since there is no use for the skill level I'm already at (due to hitting the success cap) there is no need for me to try and skill up any higher.

I've been trying to sell Dark Elf Master's Level plate items for ages (since DoN came out) and it basically rots. But since someone has increased the bazaar price of midnight stones, I have subsequently increased my price the same (like 5 to 10X) for finished goods. (On Quell finsihed master's level armor sells for about the same price as Expert level armor.)

Anyhow... If people feel like gauging the TS people for skill-up items then I feel they should pay the same inflated price when seeking finished goods.
