ChaseFV
I have also seen ashweed drop from the camp of goblin warriors where the Erudite Necromancer spawns (can't remember her name, sorry). Ashweed also drops from lava imps (but not the sprite), and the relatively rare Goblin Scout spawn (not to be confused with the fairly common a fire goblin scout). While I really, really hated and resented the week or so I spent in Lavastorm (wall to wall greens except for the vendors), in comparison to most of my other attempts to get supplies for ribbons, it was probably one of the more fruitful.

As a ranger, you may already be familiar with Jaggedpine Forest. It is a fear-kiting mecca, and if you forage as often as the button pops (turning off attack if you need to), you can rack up a stack in a night or two of hunting (3-4 hour sessions), and get pretty good experience from the griffs and the anacondas. Stonebrunt also yielded up a goodly number of bamboo shoots by hunting pandas and foraging as often as I could, but I can't remember off the top of my head if those can be used for the golden and platinum ribbons or not.
