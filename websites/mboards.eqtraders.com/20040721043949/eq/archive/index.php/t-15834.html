It's something that I keep saying over and over again . . . don't focus so much on just skilling up. Run an appropriate quest at the same time, or be making things for someone, anything to take your mind off of skilling up. I've run the Shadowscream Boot quest many times now, and I've found that when your mind isn't watching for that "you've improved in the skill!" message, those skill ups come pretty quickly. I know I've gotten at least two to three skill ups on Heraldic Armor, and most of the time I was doing that, my friend (for whom I was making the armor for) and I were more concerned about succeeding on the armor, not on the skill up. Lo and behold, a successful item . . . and a skill up!
