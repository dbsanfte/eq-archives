Kind of aggravated here, so decided to start a new kind of list: people who are deadbeats when it comes to picking up made-to-order trade goods, leaving the tradeskiller holding the bag.

I have been pretty lucky when it comes to having people pick up items they have ordered. I have (until this most recent case) been stiffed only once, and only one time felt a need to request partial payment prior to making armor for someone. So perhaps I'm spoiled, which is why running into these cases irks me so.

So I was thinking perhaps a list of known deadbeats might be of value to the Quellious tradecrafter community - people NOT to make items to order for unless you get a deposit for materials up front. Of course, if the deadbeat pays off, their name needs to get removed from the list.

Currently I have one deadbeat:

Anyway, someone to add to your "sell to with extreme caution" list. Perhaps he will come through some day, but I've given up on it.
