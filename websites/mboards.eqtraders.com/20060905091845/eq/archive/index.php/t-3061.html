I did a lot of skilling up on wood-elf cultural, and still use excellent sabertooth tiger hides for tunarean armor.

Currently, all my needs are met by long standing suppliers, the Bazaar and the occasional merchant.

In the early days, I tried hunting sabertooths myself, but it just wasn't worth it. The hides are an uncommon drop.

As a porting druid, there is a nifty and quick way to buy from players.

1. Port to Skyfire
2. Egress to Skyfire/Overthere zoneline.
3. Zone to Overthere.
4. Auction to buy excellent sabertooth hides while running to Frontier Mountains.
5. Zone into Frontier Mountains.
6. Egress to FM/Lake of Ill Omen zoneline.
7. Zone to LOIO
8. Auction to buy excellent sabertooth hides while running to Firiona Vie zoneline.
9. Zone to Firiona Vie.
10. Auction to buy excellent sabertooth hides in Firiona Vie.

I would average 2 - 3 hides each run. As time went by, people would save them for me.

You are also on Povar, Lilosh, so I'm sure it would work for you. Wood elf cultural is excellent for skilling up. BTW, I paid 20pp per hide and never had a problem meeting my needs.
