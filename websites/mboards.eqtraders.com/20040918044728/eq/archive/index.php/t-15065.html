Silverfish
I found the pattern that Misty acorns follow, so finding them isn't bad at all, and this might save you a lot of running into trees as you stare at the ground.

If you observe the map of the Misty Thicket, you'll see that the Wall splits it into east and west halves. Draw an imaginary line across it from the west side to the east to split it again into north and south, and you'll see four quadrants. The acorns spawn one in each quadrant when they spawn, although it's rarely if ever in the same place within the quadrant. So, start your search in one corner of a quadrant, and scope across it in a search pattern. When you find an acorn, move to the next quadrant and keep looking. This way, you don't end up searching a lot of ground in a quadrant you already cleared, so you can spend more time in the others. I often find three in fifteen or twenty minutes this way when the zone is empty, and sometimes all four.

Hope this helps.

Silverfish
