The Acrylia is used in Tailoring, Smithing and Fletching. In this case, it is being used to make Acrylia Shafts.

The Condensed Ice/Flame/Shadow are used to make Arrowheads that have special properties based on the type (Ice are +4 to Cold Based Creatures, Flame are 4 to Fire and Shadow are 4 to Magic).

When put together with a shield cut fletch and a Small Nock you get an arrow that is 10dmg 125rng and +4 to the appropriate type of mob.
