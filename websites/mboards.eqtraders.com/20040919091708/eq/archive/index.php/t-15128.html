Heres the scoop on Cragbeast Souffles, and i think all others that require 2 snake eggs.
You need to make egg batter and use 2 of them instead.
If you use dragon, drake, or wurm eggs, then you need to combine the egg and a cup of flour in a mixing bowl.
All other eggs like snake, griffon, basilisk, etc you combine the egg with a bottle of milk in a mixing bowl.

So the new recipe is:
Beer-battered cragbeast meat
egg batter (x2)
clump of dough
vegetables
Good news is you dont have to use just snake eggs, any will do. Bad news is that its another sub-combine. ;)

edit: can be bbq, beer-braised, etc for the meat. I posted beer-battered which is what i make.
