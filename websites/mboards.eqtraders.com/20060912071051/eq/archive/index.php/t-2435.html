On Fennin Ro the "price" of an acrylia studded cloak is about 900pp.

I say about 900pp because I've seen a few people sell them for 700pp, most sell for 900pp, and I price mine at 1k. You'd think I'd never sell any at 1k. I sell ALL of my cloaks. It takes a bit longer (average selling time is 4 days per cloak.), I could sell more if I charged less (probably alot more), but I make a comfortable proffit at 1k. About 300pp per cloak (I usualy end up buying all the Acrylia, and I have a small supply of rockhopper hides I use and ocasionaly a friend will hand me a few hides to pay me off for giving them a cloak or some other tailored item. Most haven't repaid me, but what the heck, their friends after all. Occasionaly I buy hides when I can find them for less than 100pp. I haven't bought any for months as the price keeps going up.) That 300pp of proffit takes care of any loss I get from the occasional failure and still leaves a few coin for other things. I don't feel bad about charging more than others. My price is based on what I WANT to sell the cloak for. If anyone meets my price they have a cloak, if not then I'll have one of these for a new alt, or more likely for when a friend wants one (or the very rare gift to a newbie. I'm reluctant to give away something I made to a complete stranger instead of something that I got from random hunting somewhere.)

Well, hope my rambling post dosen't upset too many people.
