Enchanted Velium Bits (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=9525&menustr=080100020000) to 222 Skill.

After that, 3 of the options you have are as follows:

Luclin Plate Armors (http://www.eqtraders.com/recipes/recipe_page.php?article=129&rsa=Smithing&sb=item&sub=lplate&menustr=080100140000) and Luclin Chain Armors (http://www.eqtraders.com/recipes/recipe_page.php?article=128&rsa=Smithing&sb=item&sub=lchain&menustr=080100120000)
~ These will get you up to 250 (if you do shadowscream after
acrylia plate trivials at 232), but will entail either a TON of farming for
components or a lot of money buying acrylia.
~ Since Shadowscream entails mostly no drop farmed
ingredients, it is the cheapest to make.
(Edit: Thank you for the correction on trivials Balkin,
I must've looked at acrylia plate when I said chain trivials
that high. :boggled: )

Human Cultural Armor - Freeport Cultural (http://www.eqtraders.com/recipes/recipe_page.php?article=74&rsa=Smithing&rc=HUM&sb=item&sub=freeport&menustr=080040070020) and Qeynos Cultural (http://www.eqtraders.com/recipes/recipe_page.php?article=75&rsa=Smithing&rc=HUM&sb=item&sub=qeynos&menustr=080040070040)
~ These will get you to 250 skill as well, but require blue
diamonds for the infused high quality rings used for the
combines, it'll get pricy as well over time unless you're able
to sell succsess to cover the costs.

Mistletoe Cutting Sickle (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=1621&menustr=035000000000)
~ This is THE ultimate plat sink if you have cash to burn, but
it's also one of the quicker ways to skillup. (Not recommended
unless you're swimming in 200kpp+.)

At 212, you can still make enchanted velium bits for 10 more skill points. They are pretty easy to make, and cheap too (if you farm your own velium) at around 18PP per combine.
