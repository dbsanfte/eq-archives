Galain
The E'Cian Ice cams are used for Aid Grimel quest so people use those.

Actually it's the Wind Metal Bow Cams. As far as your list goes here are my feelings.

Wind Metal Bow Cams
Metal Rarity - Semi-rare plus noone exps in Air

Comments - The Featherwood bow is supposed to be the best Ranger bow. The cams are also used in the Aid Grimel bow. Lastly, the other Air recipes that use Wind Metal are pretty lousy (aside from the JC item maybe).

Price - I've seen them sell for 10k usually on Prexus

Planar Steel Bow Cams
Metal Rarity - Very common

Comments - Stonewood bows sell like crazy. I am always running out of these. The metal is also used in making studs for the leather armor that sells really well, but the metal is an extremely common drop.

Price - At most, I'd pay 250pp for one

Molten Bow Cam
Metal Rarity - So common that it rots most of the time

Comments - The Obsidianwood bow sells fast, but the string for the bow is made from items that only drop off the Fennin Ro event and Blazzax in Fire. These bows are pretty rare.

Price - At most, I'd pay 250pp for one

E'cian Ice Bow Cams
Rarity - Uncommon

Comments - The Primordial Driftwood bow isn't all that great. Also, this metal is used in making Farwater Scale and Farwater Plate armors. I do most of the combines for my guild and I only use the metal in Farwater combines.

Price - I've seen the metal itself sell for 5-10k each

Innovative Bow Cams
No clue on these

I play a not-flagged-for-anything ranger and so I shop the bazaar for my fletching opportunities. The description above matches my perception of rarity: e'cian and wind metal are simply darn rare as are the cams. On the othe rhand, immaculate steel and molten metal are not rare at all (you can grab a lot off of NPCs in POT) to the point where the grease and the tinkering skill are the harder to find items.

On E'ci, the offered prices look like this:

Innovative Cams: 100-300pp per (and they sit there unpurchased for long stretches, b/c the silk is the rare component for Nightmarewood bows)

Molten Cam: 1K
Planar Cam: Variable, 1K-5K, not available long at the low end.
Wind Cam: Not available, wind metal 5k-15K (yes)
E'ci Cam -- neither cams nor metal has been offered within the last 30 days.
