The gnomish cultural tradeskill is tinkering. They do not have their own cultural smithing; they can WEAR but not make the dwarven smithed armor.

With a high end gnome smith, you can make Acrylia armor, black acrylia weapons, and the various POP armor. You should consider browsing around the recipies pages.
