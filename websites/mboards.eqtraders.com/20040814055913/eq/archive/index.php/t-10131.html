Yes, all intel classes can make all priest spells... that are researchable.

.. As for old world research.. all of the classes had their researchable spells fall into two categories:

1) Researchable only.. about half the spells

2) Researchable, and buyable... generally spells they shared with priest classes, but not always. The idea, I belive, was to give the casters a few more recipies that they could gain skill on, without absolutely having to succede to gain their spells.

Some of the multi-class research spells could be memmed by all that could use, even if the class wasn't listed on the researched scroll. There are some notable exceptions, which were probably duplicate entries in the spell database. There is actually 2 versions of Paralyzing Earth, for example.. but the chanter researched one is not scribable by wizzies Seems like it'd be a big waste of database room.. but, it is how it is atm.
