I know this is rather well known here, but I do present it as it's possible that newer fisherbeings don't know about it yet. Since Gunthak Mackerel can be made into five baits with a skinning knife, the fishing kit can be:

Fisherman's Companion
Skinning Knife
Collapsed Tackle Box
Stacks of Gunthak Mackerel

Every stack of mackerel is (usually) 100 bait, so four or five stacks would keep you going for a long, long time. In fact, for people doing monster fishing sessions, I usually recommend doing skillup runs in the Ocean of Tears, where you can catch Gunthak mackerel and then cut them into bait to keep going.

Happy fishing, from every fisherman's dream girl (I can bake and brew and I love to fish!).
