Tudani
There's two good ways to do it that I found. If you're a caster, get a coh mage and park him at the forge. Have him coth you then do the combine then gate (have your bind point right in front of grimel) then hit your hotkey that asks for a new bp and if your mage is good he's half way done casting coth again by then.

If you aren't a caster then get one mage at the forge and one at aid grimmel.

The other way to do it quickly (without having to take the elevator up) requires you to be a caster. Bind at grimmel again. Make sure the elevator is in the down position. Get sow, and cast or click levi onto yourself. Get a bp, and levi down the elevator, then click off levi, one to the staircase near the forge building, and drop down the middle. Run to the forge, combine, gate, get new bp, levi, rinse repeat. You could probably do this by taking the elevator up and levi/drop down but it would take much longer.

Gryfalia
Hehe, I think my method is slightly faster (not too much).

Bind in front of Grimel. Speed Float down the elevator shaft. Float around the corner to the door, head to the front door. Open that door and then head forward/right into the angled wall panel near the open mini courtyard to your right. Go up the wall a bit and fly through the opening. Then fly through the other opening and turn right. Use the slanted wall there again to get a bit higher and just go over the wall and run down towards the smithy. If you time it right you can land at the door and open it, do your combine and just gate back.

No having to recast lev and, as you get better at it, you never even slow down as your ramp up the walls. Plus it looks reallllly funny.
