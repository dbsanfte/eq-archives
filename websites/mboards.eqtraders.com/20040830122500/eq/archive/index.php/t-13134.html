Tinile
At level 16, your gnome can achieve 250 tinkering. Tinkering is a bit costly. Mine is at 199 now and I've made a ton of geerloks, which I have to sell at a loss in bazaar, just to recoup some costs. Others sell to merchants. For geerloks, which can get you to 236 with LoY geerloks, all ingredients but 1 are vendor purchasable. The vendor prices for sprockets and gears isn't cheap.

You can make money skilling up on collapsibles, but due to their lore status, it's hard to make them enmasse. It is a bit easier since the shared bank was added.

Sorry can't give you approximate costs, as I don't keep track of those things, but take a look at the recipes and the costs of vendor items and you should get an idea.
