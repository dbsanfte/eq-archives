kiztent
http://www.eqecon.com/

If you do a search on 'farwater' it will give you all of the pieces for sale, along with the lowest and highest price offered, as well as the "value" which is a moving average of the lowest selling price across all servers (what a patient buyer will expect to pay for the item).

It doesn't say if the stuff will *sell* at that price. Just that it was offered. I've found it to be useful for "best guessing" prices on unusual items.
