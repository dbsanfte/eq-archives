I've tried a bit with my shaman. No luck selling it.

What I made was Ethira's Poison Antidote. Costs about 6.5 plat per charge to make. Pretty low trivial at 57. Takes 3 charges to cure the poisons, at 65 anyway. Casting time is 2 seconds, plenty fast enough. Can't sell the stuff, no interest in it. People would rather die. But at least my characters don't.

Potion of Gorging Toxin does Cure Poison. Cure Poison only removes 4 counters. It would take about 6 uses of it to remove one of the posions.

Potion of Spurn Affliction is the same deal, but for disease. The diseases don't damage you quickly, no rush on them, doubt this would sell at all. If you want to get around disease, my advice is get yourself a Shield of the Immaculate. Rocks as an enchanter, great for removing slow from charmed pets.
