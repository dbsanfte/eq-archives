Antonian Javelins cannot be made in the Freeport Forge - they can only be made by human smiths in the Qeynos Royal Forge in South Qeynos. It doesn't matter where your human smith was born, only that he/she is human. The Qeynos Royal Forge is found in the big "Town Square" area in SOuth Qeynos, right across from the big clock. Here is the link with the info on Human Qeynos Cultural Smithing (http://www.eqtraders.com/secrets/recipes_smithing_human_qeynos.htm).
