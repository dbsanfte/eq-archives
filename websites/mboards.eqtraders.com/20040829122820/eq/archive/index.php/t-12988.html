You are correct... there is not a database of recipies that were tried but didn't work.

That would be insanely huge, given the sheer number of possible combinations.

For example... some recipies that make sense, but are not in the game...

Breaded Mushrooms: Bread Crumbs + Mushrooms + Vegetable Oil.
Cheese Stuffed Poppers: Cheese (three different kinds available) + Habenero Peppers.
Owlbear Fillets: Owlbear Meat + Fillet Knife.
Sonic Wolf Fillets: Sonic Wolf Meat + Fillet Knife.

The list goes on and on.

While it's a nice idea, you also have to realize that there's only so much room in the item database. Putting in more recipies for Baking, especially more pre-200 trivial recipies, takes away space that could be used for many other things.
