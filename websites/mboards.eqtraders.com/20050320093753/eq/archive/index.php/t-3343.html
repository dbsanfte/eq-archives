Ivandyr's Hoop has 6 charges of Spirit Tap, and it NOT rechargeable. Buy it for the 6 WIS, not the lifetap charges :)

For tinkered items, there is also an option 4: The item is rechargeable in a toolbox by making a no-fail combine of the exhausted item with a Mana Battery of some kind. As mentioned, the Anizok items are rechargeable thus, also the Gnomish Vanishing Device and possibly others (check tinkering.org)
