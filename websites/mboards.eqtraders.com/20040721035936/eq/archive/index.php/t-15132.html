Azirux
I'll rephrase from the other post: What you see is what you get. There are no hidden values for Int/Wis. Your only options for getting above 255 is to get above level 60, and start spending AA points in Adv Enlightenment, Planar Power, and whatever other AAs are available to raise your cap.

And gosh, give us more than a few hours for an answer before deleting it if you don't want to look it up yourself. Some of us have jobs :-)

Full info:

Cap on all stats pre level 60: 255
At level 60 to 65, you gain 5 points to the cap.
AA: Innate Enlightenment. Requires level 61. Each rank increases the cap by 10 points in INT and WIS.
AA: Planar Power: Requires level 61. Each rank increases cap by 5 points in all stats.

Itek
INT/WIS/All are capped (hard) at 255 at level 60.

To go beyond this you gain 5 points of (hard) cap per level 61 - 65 (in all stats)

To go beyond this you must spend AA in Planar Power. This is 2 AA points per level. One level (max) per level 61 - 65. Each level of Planar Power allows 5 more points of (hard) cap (in all stats).

Pure Casters and Priests (Nec Wiz Mag Enc Sha Dru Clr) get Enlightenment AA which costs 3 AA points per. One level (max) per level 61 - 65. Each level allows INT/WIS -ONLY- to gain 10 points of (hard) cap.

Now... that's fairly clear and visible to anyone who has the PoP expansion installed and checks the alt. advancement window.

What is UNCLEAR is the actual effect of stats beyond 255. Personally I see a massive improvement up to 200, great improvements up to 255, and -diminishing-returns- for 256-355. Meaning that adding 30 points of stats going from 210 to 240 will see better results than adding 30 points from 270 to 300. Things seem to still improve, just at a slower rate.

Your mileage may vary, and I don't have "hard data" to prove it. (All my hard data was on the hard drive that went -boom- last december.)
