Having read the thread that was linked above, I note that it says that to make the Zek Bane weapons requires a smithing combine with a trivial of 182 and two brewing combines of over 145.

The final combine for the wielder has a very low trivial, but making the ingredients requires a good smith to loot the Decorin Shards and a good brewer to loot the Valorium Shards and Insanity Fangs.
