I farmed 40-50 silks this morning as a 52 cleric. Took me about 45 min, including a few afk's and medding time.

I have J-boots for the speed. Spells up are Strike, Smite, Retribution, Upheaval, Root. Also have DA/DB & Remedy.

What I do is run around and smack everything at least once with either a fast spell like strike or my 1HB until I have 15-20 MOB's. I'll hit everything, especially the Chasm Crawlers. Once I have an adequate supply of lil-greenies, I face them, back up, then move into the center of the group and cast Upheaval until it goes off and kills everything.

It helps to kill everything so that the spiders keep popping. When ever I kill 4-10 CCrawlers, I'll start to see spiders popping all around me.

I usually work from the entrance ramp to HPH over to the Halfling outpost to the Gorge & the plains in between. Yes, many of the spiders pop up on the hillside, but with other farmers out, they usually get them first. I have found this area much better odds of the spider still being there by the time you get to him... :D
