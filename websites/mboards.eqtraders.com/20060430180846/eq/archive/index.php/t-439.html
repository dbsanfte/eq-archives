Fierce Heraldic - WAR, BRD; HUM, BAR, ELF, DEF, HEF, TRL, OGR, HFL, GNM; deity: Rallos Zek, wt 3.0, recommended level 45, MAGIC

Crimson Darkscale - OGR, TRL; WAR, SHD; Rallos Zek; recommended level 45, wt 3.0

Barbarians can not SMITH any of the armor themselves. However, they can wear the human cultural from Qeynos, which is the Fierce Heraldic. However, they can not wear the Crimson Darkscale which is made by Ogres.

Armor Stats can be found off the mail page.

Recipies->Cultural->Human-Qeynos then select the link at the top which has Human Qeynos Smithed Armor/Weapon Stats page.
