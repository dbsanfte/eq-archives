DOn't do sickles at 188, that would probably make you decide to quit smithing very quickly. You'll get a success rate of about 1/20, and a stack of 20 costs about 7kpp to attampt.

Factor in the 2k or so that sickles sell for these days, and you'll be losing somewhere around 5kpp per stack.

Figure 2 stacks per skillup on average, and you're looking at 10kpp per skillup, and that probably won't improve much until you hit 210 or so.

If you can afford 220,000pp to get to 210, then by all means do sickles. If not, do shadowscream, or old cultural, or acrylia (or ideally, all three, as the materials become available).
