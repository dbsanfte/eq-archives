I thought these might be useful to someone.

Since the Oct 8th, 2003 that changed jewel craft mastery I've had the following success rates:

Tempest Earrings 11/17
Valorium Ring of Gallantry 35/50

All this is with 240+ skill and trophy equipped. I have jewel craft mastery 2. From some asking around I believe that at 240+ skill you have about a 50% chance with a PoP JC combine. JCM1 gives you a 60% chance and JCM 2 improves that to ~70%. JCM3 gives a 90% chance of success on a PoP combine. Again, these are my guesses. Just including my data for reference. It's also my experience that all the PoP JC combines are about the same success rate.
