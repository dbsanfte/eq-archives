I agree, shadow is better for fletching skillup. Go to the cave area in shadeweaver and if the brown/grey men are up you should start killing them at about 5pm game time. Any mob that pops after 7 pm game time will be the black ones that drop shadow (grey/brown don't drop any). Any repops after 7 am game time will be back to grey/brown men.

In between the spawn cycle, you can hit the forge there to convert the chunks to arrowhead and work on fletching if you brought the shafts and parts ahead of time.
