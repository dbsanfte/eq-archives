Maevenniia
Only if that spell is shared between the classes, and only with the wizard (or priest class) recipe

example: Levitation - useable by WIZ ENC DRU SHM

There are 3 possible research recipes:

1. WIZ - rune of Current, rune of conglomeration
2. ENC - part of yaeth's 65 left and 65 right
3. DRU SHM - part of yaeth's 70 left, dew clover

As a wizard you can use recipes 1 and 3 to make the spell, and an Enchanter could scribe the result. Or if an enchanter used their recipe, you could scribe the result.
