:?: I've recently found a NPC I've never noticed before in the Monk Guildhouses in Freeport and Qeynos...They give out recipes and materials to craft newbie monk armor. This armor is apparently a No Fail Combine, but the closest skill I can find is Tailoring so I'm posting the one piece, I have completed.
Bracer of the Silent Fist:
This is a two part combine. First:
1 Woven Spider Silk
2 Rat Whiskers
1 Gnoll Jawbone
1 Cloth Wristband
combined in a container provided by NPC, gets you Bracer Material which is combined in a Loom with the Enchanted Bracer Pattern provided by NPC to get a Bracer of the Silent Fist
Stats: Magic Item, No Drop; Slot:Wrist; AC: 2, Dex: +1, Sta: +3;
Wt: 0.3; Size Medium; Class: Monk; Race: Human
