Balkin
Tinkering doesn't count as one of the primary 7 trades, so, you CAN get brewing, and tinkering up to 250 on one character without AA's. Poisonmaking is another one that can go to 250 independently of AA's.

So, a Gnome Rogue, theoretically speaking, could get both Tinkering and Poisonmaking AND one of the primary tradeskills (brewing, smithing etc.) all up to 250 without AA's.

That said, I got my wizard to 250 brewing, AND a corking device for less than 800 pp. That's paying cost for the corker though. Had a friend make it for me.

So, to fund your tinkering, brewing is probably a good idea.

Good luck!
