A tracker isn't strictly needed but helps a lot. The spawn cycle mentioned contains the following mobs with a 30-45 second respawn after the last of a spawn dies.

a puma (multiple spawns, typically 3-5)
a sand mummy (multiple spawns, typically 3-5)
a dune tarantula (multiple spawns, typically 3-5)
a desert tarantula (multiple spawns, typically 3-5)
a terrorantuling (1-3 spawn, haven't seen more than 3)
Terrorantula (single spawn)

The trackers way to do this cycle efficiently is to set tracking to "normal" so new spawns are at the top. Then find which mob off the cycle is up and kill every instance of that mob, be it puma's or sand mummies or spiders. Once the last one is dead, another spawn group will pop in 30-45 seconds. Kill all of those, rinse, repeat until terrorantulings or the Terrorantula spawns.

Note: Sand Giants and the Ancient Cyclops are not on this spawn cycle.
