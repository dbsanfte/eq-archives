Haven't been to the Othmir camps in awhile(months), so can't give good info on the drop rates. Level does help in farming though. If you are a druid and have Karana's Rage lvl 62 AE spell then farming them isn't to bad. I do remember it taking awhile to get a stack even using my method.

Note: do this only when not many are in the zone, it tends to upset people who can't do it. I believe in positive PR for my guild and my class.

Single pull the shamans and clear them from the Chief's camp and the one over by Bulthars. Med up if needed. Harmony the Chief and snare pull the entire camp, including pups. Being KOS here helps as it's always easier to pull the pups in mass to snare in the open. Run over to the Bulthar side camp Snareing any of the Othmirs in between (think there are 2). Snare pull that camp. At 62 there was no need to Harmony the 2 guys there you don't want. AE at will and collect your drops from the little bodies strewn all over the beach. :)

Some AA into Fury of Magic and Spell Casting Fury makes this fairly mana efficient. KEI is a must but is easily paid by all them gem and armor drops. There are finer details of technique not discussed above, but you can only figure those out by doing, like making sure you avoid being pounded on by 19 or so Othmirs. :idea:
