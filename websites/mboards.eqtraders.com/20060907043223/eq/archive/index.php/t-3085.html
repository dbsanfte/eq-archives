Worked on soltice robes also.

- 250 JC + 5% making the gem studded chain I fail 50% of the time. Given the cost of material per combine is around 250 plat, that yields 500 plat per chain. Luckily this isnt destroyed on robe failures.

- I buy silk in bazaar for 5 plat or less (or 10 plat or less for swatches).
- Emeralds cost 13,5 plat a shot and must be imbued (make friends with Tunare cleric or druid)
- Making the sacred tunare silk involves making emy dust (needs celestial essence ~ 1,5 plat self made)

- Given the fact you loose 5% to minimum failures for the dust and tunare silk combines, U can say that a dust costs around 16 plat (13,5 + 1,5 + 5%) and the silk 29 plat (10 + 16 + 1,5 + 5%)

- U need 3 silks per combine, so that's 90 plat. Round it to 100 if you donate for imbues or buy imbued emeralds.

Given the high triv point, you will have the 5% minimum success rate up until 210 220 skill. 20 combines cost you 2000 plat, unless you farm silk. On my server (Terris Thule) robes sell around 1k. So this would yield a cost of 1000 plat per 20 combines (without farming silk), unless you are very unlucky and get zero successful combines.

PS: I neglected material costs less than a plat piece or one time investments such as the needle.
