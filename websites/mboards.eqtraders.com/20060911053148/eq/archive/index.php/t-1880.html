Merchant mine vendors for Arctic Wyvern pelts. Get your brewing up. Make a load of coldain tempers. Farm Crystal Caverns with your second computer and build up a stock of velium, while you fish for Cobalt Cod in CS.

Make studs, brew cod oil, stockpile, buy mask patterns, combine in large batches until materials run out, repeat ad nauseum until your patience - or plat - is gone.

At least, that's the way I'm doing it.

You could also do a similar thing with acrylia armour. For vendor mining read 'kill a billion rockhoppers', farm for acrylia instead of in CC, but the work on your brewing still stands as you'll be making paela stuff.

Enjoy.

I have been going the acrylia armor route. I hunt in the grimling mines for acrylia, and in Dawnshroud Peaks or Scarlet Desert for superb rockhopper hides. It is tedious stuff.

My system is to skill up using the acrylia studded masks (recipe is on eqtraders), but ocassionally make an acrylia studded cloak, tunic, or sleeves to sell in the bazaar. I generally make about 1K give or take off the piece, and I use that money to buy more acrylia and superb pelts in the bazaar.

By that method you are always farming for yourself, but once you build up 1K of "throw-away" money, you can speed things up a bit and buy in the bazaar too.

IMO there is no one route to take.

I've basicly gone the way of farming certain items, buying others and basicly just combine anything i can get my hands on.

Vendor mining is a good option but it seems to me that a regular mining of the Bazaar is by far the most superior action.

I've bought stacks and stacks of wyvern hides, paying anything from 15 pp to 35 or 40 pp (when in a good mood). In the process i kept a lookout for panther hides/HQ lion skins/othmir hides/flawless rockhopper hides.

Other than that i just mined The bazaar for silk swatches, paying on average around 7 pp a swatch. I clicked merrily away at some 100 succeeded solstice robes (staying optimistic and not listing the number of combines). I started out combining the coldain tempers/peabala temper/celestial essence myself but soon gave up for the sheer amount of clicks.

If you have access to an enchanter that has 250 skill and can make the necklaces for the solstice robes, you might be in luck in a way. I managed to do around 1000-1500 combines on the robes and actually making money to fund the mining of hides/velium pieces/coldain tempers (yes some people sell em for around 20 pp in the bazaar on my server)


Even doing it this way it has been a loooooong looooong road. At 235 skill now and ready to start erudite cultural now with a decent chance of actually succeeding at a decent cost. Planning to do some major fishing for cod as i still have around 20 stacks of wyvern hides on various mules.

If you want to get the tailoring up, look at ways of making some money on the combines, mine bazaar, in slow times farm acrylia and velium, fish for cod and keep on looking for those bargains in the bazaar. Just have a mental checklist of all items that you might want some day for combines and search (managed to pick up a stack of blue diamonds for 250 pp a piece the other day. The BD's are dropping in price quite fast but even then it's still a bargain)

I did reasonably well in Mons Letalis next to The Grey zone. The only thing that wanders by that is KOS is the walking rocks (and occasionally people training the zone line). As a cleric (someone mentioned it, but I don't see the reference), you ought to be able to make a little cash by sometimes zoning into the Grey and offering Rez-services.

Solstice Robes is a very good option if you can imbue the emeralds. Be innovative -- offer ressurections in EK in exchange for silk when you are farming -- same in Firiona Vie.

A lot of it depends on the classes of your characters. As a druid, I quadded wyverns for several levels. Quad, then med at the water and fish. Forage when I can and use the forages for Seafood Salad.

I got from skill 190-ish to 250 using materials bought in The Bazaar -- and made a profit ! But, I can understand your not wanting to use that system.
