Oh, I know the prices are largely out of whack. I pointed out in my original post that the prices were off. It was more to test the concept of a site, along with the appearance. The prices for the most part are wrong; they're just numbers I made up before I knew the actual costs.

The exception to this is the banded armor. I'm doing a reasonably brisk business selling banded for between 1 - 1.5pp per AC. I tend to auction it to new or mid-range players in East Commons; I haven't tried other areas yet. It's not going to put food on my table, as the saying goes, but the extra occasional 100pp for a full suit is a nice boost, and it gets my name "out there" in the market.

Even in the bazaar, I can consistently sell banded for 1pp per AC. Every time I put up a few pieces, they sell within a day or two.

In general, I look for 50% profit on a full suit, adjusted to fit in the scaling of other pieces and types. That puts the prices as follows:

Banded: 1 - 1.5pp per AC
(silver) Ornate: 7.5pp per AC
Electrum Ornate: 9pp per AC
Gold Ornate: 12pp per AC
Platinum Ornate: 30pp per AC (platinum is EXPENSIVE! :) )
Fine Plate: 14pp per AC

With Fine Plate at 14pp per AC, that puts it very close to the 75pp per sheet pricing.

I also offer to dye FP armor for 30pp a piece, including the cost of finding and making the dye.

Now, I'm not seriously going to include patchwork armor on the final listing. I'm realistic enough not to expect to make any sales off of it. I'm mostly interested in fine-tuning the pricing system and the spreadsheet I use to generate the numbers.

I'll update the page in the next couple of days and start referring people to it. Once I'm sure my spreadsheet ain't broke (I keep finding and fixing odd bugs in it), I'll post it up there too.

Thanks for the feedback, all! =)

OK, finally got around to updating the Web site. The new version isn't quite as spiffy as the "demo", but it's a fraction of the size to download and MUCH easier to update.

http://kyros.info

Pricewise, the prices for all tailored pieces currently on the site are final. Smithed armor prices up to Fine Plate are likewise final; acylia and shadowscream prices are made up just to fill something in. (I also have to go through and confirm the stats on the armor.) I know that some of the prices are out of line with the server standard, but my play time is limited, and it's not worth my time to make the pieces for less than this. And yes, patchwork armor is still in there. :)

On the back end, the system I've designed is based on an Excel spreadsheet. You plug in prices for things like silk swatches or metal rings, then it calculates your cost to make a particular piece. Pricing is set on a pp per AC basis for all armors. The spreadsheet then generates a JavaScript file with pricing info that gets downloaded and run locally on each user's browser. I've tested it with IE and confirmed it works; please let me know if it breaks with other browsers and I'll investigate.

And yes, I know the home page is as ugly as a ... well, as a swamp-dweller. :) I'm working on making that neater and easier to read.
