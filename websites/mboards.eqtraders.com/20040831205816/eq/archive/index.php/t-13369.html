Unless you are part of an elemental guild... you will be hard pressed to make a profit off JC combines that you cannot farm yourself. The Bloodmetal earrings sell from 22-25K on the Luclin server. The Highly polished Gems are sold for anywhere between 18-25K each. And it seems that one person buys them all up and marks up the price to monopolize the market on them.

The other day I got lucky and found a Gem for 6K... I quickly bought it and failed the combine... =X

I've succeeded on more of these for my guildies than I have those made for personal gain. I stopped making them now and even if I run accross a gem while XP'ing in Tactics, I refuse to take a chance at the combine. If I would have turned and sold the gem at 15K.. it would have sold fast and I would have made a 9K profit. It's just not worth the risk of combine, when I can make near the same amount selling the raw materials needed.

Same goes for Cloudy Eyes from PoJ, and metallic liquids. (Although I historically have had very good luck on Vings of Valorium)

Bregalad
Plain vanilla JC market is dead. About only jewelry still worth it for some is diamond/blue diamond for resists. But selling even that above cost is about impossible.

Cazicite jewelry is hot, but cazicite is dang rare and hard to farm.

LDoN jewelry - dunno, I experimented with it, and was sorely unimpressed with results, even with Velium.

I did not have a chance to check out GoD JC yet - I had very little time to play EQ lately.

PoP JC can be a great moneymaker if you have a second account that allows you to keep a permanent trader in bazaar. Xev economics are a bit less crazy than Luclin, and bargains can be found.

I can usually make Tempest Earrings for under 1kpp cost(I charm solo storms in PoS, copper is the most expensive component here), and sell for over 2.5k. Metallic Liquids cost 5-6k, and Valorium rings sell real well at 10k - with cost of everything else factored in, that's 3-4kpp profit per ring if you succeed. HPGs go fo 16-17k, earrings sell for 25k or so - even better profit margin.

Elemental jewelry without elemental access is a scary business. If you check bazaar often, you can find all components needed for say, resilience ring for about 40k. Rings go for 70-80k, so on success it is 30+k in your pocket. But losing 40k on a fail feels on par with being kicked in the groin.

Overall, high-end JC market is not about who got the skill - but rather who has access to components.
