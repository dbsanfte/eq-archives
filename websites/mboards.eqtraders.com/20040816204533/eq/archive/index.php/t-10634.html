KyrosKrane
Unless the % modifier is huge on the player-made bait, I don't think there'd be a market for it. You can buy +3% bait in Shadowhaven for about 2gp each and +2% bait in PoK for less than 1gp, I think. The forgaged bait from Gunthak has +5% and when it's sold to a vendor, players can buy it back for about 1.5pp.

There's also the unsolved question of whether modifiers on bait and poles stack. You can buy a +2% pole in Shadowhaven, and players can make pole with +5%, I believe. If that doesn't stack with bait, then you may as well use the cheapest bait you can find. Again, we have no evidence (that I know of) one way or the other on this issue.

Given that the baits require 8pp worth of silk, I seriously doubt you'll find anyone willing to pay 160pp for a stack of bait unless it has, like, +100% modifier or something.
