I don't have an exact number for you.

For me, after my gnome has bought some high-priced PoP jewelry component and my cursor is positioned over the combine button, I say to myself, "Okay, you've got at least a 50% chance of succeeding." Then I click the button and either cheer or curse at the RNG.

And, of course, there is a FAQ on calculating chance of success in the FAQ forum.

Let's look at the Bloodmetal earring, since my gnome has failed all three of his attempts to make one.

The simplified formula:

Percentage = (skill - (trivial*.75)) + 51.5

Plug in my gnome's skill and the earring trivial:

Percentage = (252 - (308*.75)) + 51.5

Percentage = (252 - 231) + 51.5

Percentage = 72.5%

So, if I did that right, my gnome should have a 72.5% chance of succeeding. However, the RNG has not smiled upon him yet in regard to the Bloodmetal earring. :(
