My analysis of High Elf Robes for skillups and cost...more or less :)

Ok currently I am at 218 skill. I have done about 20 robes and gotten 0 skillups off them. I have succeeded on 19/20

Robe, Trivial level, Research Component

robe of sorcery - trivial >211 &lt;=214 - rune of sorcery
robe of the skewn - trivial >218 &lt;=225 - nitillim's grimoire pg. 115
robe of tears - trivial >225 - Tears of Prexus
robe of slant - trivial >226 - keris's dissertation pg. 5 (right)
robe of impulse - trivial >233 - rune of impulse
robe of virtue - trivial >226 - words of virtue

Comment on Reserch rarity

Rune of Sorcery is a mid level (30-40ish) reserach component and can at times be found on merchants but not in great quantities.

nitillim's grimoire pg. 115 is also mid level and maybe slightly more common then the rune of sorcerey. I personally have 7 swatches of this left I belive.

Tears of prexus is a 44-49 mage research component and seems to drop like candy. I see this all over merchants and have at least 1 stack.

Keris' dissertation is semi rare, I have seen 3 and are for the new research stuffs.

Rune of impulse same as keris' dissertation and have seen 3 also.

Words of virtue. Rarer then sin. It is used in the mage 60 pet heal spell and I think another rare spell. Don't expect to see this for robes, though I do want to try at lest ONE robe with this.

Breakdown

At my current skill level the robe of skewn and robe of tears may be a viable path because they use silver ribbons 15p for skewn and gold ribbon 31p for tears.

Robe of sorcery didn't seem that viable because i trived it very fast and got 0 skillups on all the combines. Also the rune is used in at least 1 int caster quest so they are not as easy to find as the grimoier pages or tears.

Tears of prexus are just about the break point where i would throw money at tailoring due to not being rich. I'd rather keep making solstic robes for guild earrings and skill up that way.

Solstic robe = 50p if all self done, maybe 95p if buying from other traders. Not counting if you succeed and have to get another chain.

The last 2 robes impulse and virtue will cost 105p just for the ribbon. On top of whatever someones going to charge you for the words or rune..which can be up to 1k.

I think that at skill 218 I am close to the trivial of tears and every combine makes a robe. I would hate to see these become so flooded on the market they become worth 20p each.

The thing i find stopping me is the stupid lichenmoss and sylvian berries seeing as I do not forage and HATE having to rely on another class to do tailoring...hell that pisses me off about solstic robes and imbued emeralds.

Anyway I doubt I would buy a swatch of the first 3 for more then 25p each maybe 50p for tears but that would be pushing it as solstic robes cost about the same and i could outfit a guildmate with an earring then.

I would pay at least once to make the robe of virtue even if the effect sucks just to have one :)
