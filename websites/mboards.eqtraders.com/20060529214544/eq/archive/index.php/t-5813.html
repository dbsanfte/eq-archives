Make Poison is different to regular trade skills: The skill gain is based in your INT or your WIS or your DEX - whatever is highest. DEX is a primary stat here, not a secondary like for tailoring.

Which stat you prefere is your own choice. I don't go for DEX as I need INT or WIS for the other tradeskills anyway. In the WIS vs. INT question I went for INT, since I think it's more fun to play a rogue with more INT than some wizzes or chanters ;o)
