The reason they don't really add new armor is that it involves a lot more work and assets. To make one new chain model, you'd have to make a new model for every race, not just 1 copy of the model, since every races chain model is a little different. New weapon models, on the other hand, are much easier to do since you just make one model and it fits all characters.

Probably, the easiest thing to do that would make armor look different other then the simple tinting they currently do would be to make new textures like they did for velious. However, due to the variety of armor now, and the complexity of the models, even this is a fairly daunting task...anyway, just my humble opinion.
