I use Panicware's Pop-up Stopper myself, and while I love the program, it has one annoying side-effect that you may not expect: it will auto-close ANY new Internet Explorer window that may open, <i>even if it's you double-clicking the icon on your desktop!</i> So if you use it and find yourself completely unable to start IE, it's likely that you have it activated.

One time, I forgot the program running after I'd ended my browsing session and closed all the IE windows. A friend tried to use my PC to browse the 'Net. He was utterly befuddled when everything he tried to do to open IE resulted in zilch. :D
