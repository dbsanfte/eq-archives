Eidolon Faer
Okay, first of all...

Remember there are two different "levels" of tradeskill drops. The level 45-50 wimp mobs drop the following:

Raw Pale Nihilite (JC and Smithing)
Aligned Ore (Smithing)
Raw Dark Matter (Tinkering)
Hynid Mane (Tailoring)
Hynid Blood (Tailoring)
Taelosian Rock (Fletching)

A direcharm Enchanter can farm this junk all day long with minimal risk, and get experience and several quest drops along the way.

I have not seen ANY sources for "Shimmering Steel" or "Shimmering Nihilite". If I were to hazard a guess, I would assume that these items drop off more-difficult critters deeper in the expansion.

I would therefore also assume that weapons made from these higher-level materials would have greatly superior stats. Frankly, I'm rather encouraged that weapons made from drops off such weak MOBs have stats as good as they do. It's a far cry from the crappiness that is Fiery Ore, and is arguably a better return-on-inventment than even Black Acrylia weaponry. Akheva Blood drops off FAR tougher creatures than drop these things.

Sadly, slapping a rec. level of 50 on these weapons is going to trash the market value for them completely.

Oh, on a side note, while you're farming the ore and gems, save an (undamaged) Turepta Shell from the turtles, you'll need it to quest the reinforced JC kit for making the inlay kits. And have a friendly jeweler handy.
