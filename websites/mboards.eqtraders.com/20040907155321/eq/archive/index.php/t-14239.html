For some reason my "Favorite" for the Lemming Fur bag in the new UI got replaced by a recipe for the Large Lemming Fur Bag. So I decided to experiment and found a recipe for a Small Lemming Fur Bag and a Medium Lemming Fur Bag...recipes and stats below


Small Lemming Fur Bag
Weight - 1.0
Cap - 4
Size - Giant
WR - 25%
Trivial - 151 (per UI)
Graphic - Looks like a typical 4 slot pouch
Token - Copper Coin

1 Lemming Fur
1 Backpack Pattern
2 Silver Threads
1 Embroidery Needle (returned on success)


Medium Lemming Fur Bag
Weight - 1.7
Cap - 6
Size - Giant
WR - 25%
Trivial - 158 (per UI)
Graphic - Looks like a normal 8 slot backpack
Token - Platinum Coin

1 Lemming Fur
1 Backpack Pattern
1 Gold Thread
1 Embroidery Needle (returned on success)


Large Lemming Fur Bag
Weight - 2.0
Cap - 8
Size - Giant
WR - 25%
Trivial - 167 (per UI)
Graphic - Not sure which bag this looks like, has the appearence of a satchel, larger on the botton then on top with a leather strap running down the front.
Token - Gold Coin

2 Lemming Furs
1 Backpack Pattern
2 Gold Threads
1 Embroidery Needle (returned on success)


Caerlyna will turn any of the above 3 into tokens and back again.

I suppose this is an alternative to Wu's to get to the 160's, and at least these may have a resale value, though not much except for the Lemming Fur Backpacks. If the Large bag had only used one fur it may have had a decent value, but the fur is the major cost on these bags unless you spend all your time in Miragul's foraging.

Also posting this info to the Database Update Submission thread.
