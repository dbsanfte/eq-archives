Zyrtryx Conjureblade
Forgot to mention that I've had a few fishing droughts myself.

I always carry 2 stacks of bait with me. (I have the Fisherman's Companion, so worrying about a pole breaking isn't a problem) If I'm fishing somewhere with a vendor nearby, I usually go through at least 3 stacks of bait. The most I ever did in one sitting was about 5 and a half stacks, and that was when I first went to OOT. (I was fishing on Zachariah's island - best fishing spot in the zone, if you're not evil)

I occasionally fish for Saltwater Seaweed in OOT, and sell it in the Bazaar. I've had occasions where I get a stack or more in an hour, and occasions where I'm lucky if I get 3 an hour.

Two guildmembers once decided to go fish in OOT (on Sister Isle - second best fishing spot in the zone, if you're not evil) after I talked about fishing in guildchat, and I joined them about 15 minutes later. Both had Fishing skill that was less than half of mine, and they each caught more Seaweed than I did in the time we were all there together!

Wish I could do that with them again sometime, but they both quit EQ to go play SWG. And one of them was my best customer when I sold food regularly (I sold him a LOT of Truffles and HMPs - at a discount, of course). :(

You just have to keep hanging in there and hope for the best.
