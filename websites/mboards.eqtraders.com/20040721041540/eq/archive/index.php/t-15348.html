Kaladim Constitutionals, Grobb Liquidised Meat, Qeynos Afternoon Tea if you happen to know a forager or get lucky on vendor dives. Saleani
I used to make tainted avalanche ale (TAA) for MQ. I made about 120 of them and sold them mostly for around 2k. Some as high as 5k, some as low as 1k. And if folks blew Peffin, I gave them one free replacement.

I considered making Kaladim Constitutionals, but decided there was not enough profit in them. With a triv of 335, and a skill of 250, I fail about half the combines. So I only make them for myself, and have the metabolism 3 AA on all my upper level characters to make them last longer. There are too many subcombines on Grobb Liquidised Meats for me to bother with. My foraging characters have never managed to snag 1 leaf for the tea, so I have never made one.

This character (Saleani) was originally a model for plate armor that I made on a diff char until someone (who has since quit the game) asked me to get my brewing skill up to make some TAA for them. Due to server politics, very few high level characters were willing to associate, or do anything with the members in that guild. And I had wanted to grandmaster other things on my other characters (like tailoring on my halfling, smithing on my barb, that sort of thing). At the time I got brewing to the point I could make TAA, I next had to level up to where I could fight the wyverns, and get non kos to them. When I first started, 2 years ago, I was one of the first to sell the MQs on our server: most folks making that ale, were only doing it for guildies, and not selling the ale to outsiders except maybe for 10k+. The list of >200 skill brewers willing to press combine for others (outside their guild) was small enough that you could count them on both thumbs. There were no AAs at the time I started all this, so one could not be a master of all tradeskills, like Saleani is now. Go figure.
