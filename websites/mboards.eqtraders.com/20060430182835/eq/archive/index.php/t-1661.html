It depends a lot on the economy of your server, and how many other human smiths are working. On most other servers, prices for the BP are in the 20k-30k range, no one would buy it for 50k.

If you want to conserve money, you should hang out in the bazaar and bazaar channel, and auction that you are taking orders on heraldic armor. That way you ensure yourself a sale, and you know exactly which deity to imbue it for. If you are really in tight jamb money-wise, some customers may agree to a down payment in return for a discount later.

I'm actually a barb smith but I would have to agree that the best option is to hang out in the bazaar channel and auction that your a human smith taking orders and make the peices after you have an order. With the large number of possible imbues for humans multiplied by the potential peices the amount of things someone could possibly want is huge. Yes the fierce for the ralos zek warriors is probably going to be your best seller over all but when you need to sell something to make your money back it's best to be able to make what ever they are looking for. Probably also need to line up people or mules for the assorted different imbues or get some gems preimbued.

Bp's never seemed to be that popular of a peice when I was selling. Quite a few nice bps out there as drops and MQ's availble too. Just seemed like most people who could afford a cultural bp could get something else either as a drop of bought. Bracers for almost any class seemed to be popular and for barb warrios at least the legs and boots seemed popular too.
