kiztent
The other thing to keep at the back of your head is how you two will intereact as a group. Cleric is nice, but they tend to overlap a lot with druids and don't bring much to the mix in a duo.

On the other hand, cleric seems to be a motherly role. I know on my old server our raiding clerics were 2 grandmothers and a new mother.

Since a druid will have heals covered, consider... an enchanter. ENC/DRU is a good combo - both to duo and to build a group around.

Mage is a good choice, or BST if the idea of having a faithful pet appeals to her. A woman in our guild who likes the American Indian lifepath is a barbarian Beastlord and has lots of good emotes to support her roleplaying. Then again our MT is a woman (and **** good tank, and I'm sure Sanna will verify she's a cutie too :D), as is one of our AM3 rangers, so gender stereotypes aren't all that.

Lastly, I'd ask you to consider a shaman. While it's not the most powerful combination duoing, a shaman/druid pair is a SOLID backbone to form a group around. And once the druid gets over 58 and the shaman gets canni 5 it's a pretty powerful combination. Plus you have the advantage that you don't have to find a cleric, or an enchanter, but you can pick up all the melees LFG. I know shaman say they are underpowered, and they are correct. If you are endgame and the tanks have all their physical stats capped and worn ATK capped as well. At levels of the game below that, shaman are very solid - though a BST can stand in as a shaman in a pinch.
