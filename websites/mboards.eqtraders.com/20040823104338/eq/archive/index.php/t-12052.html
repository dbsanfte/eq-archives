There are 3 know mod items (for Research) so far:

Geerlok Automated Quill - +5% to spell research, tinkered

Thought Horror Scalp - +5% to research, rare drop in The Deep

Sanguine Robe - +10% to research, drops off Va Xi Aten Ha Ra in Vex Thal
