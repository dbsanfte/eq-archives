Or, rather than doing a search on "swatch", do this.


1) Main EQTraders page, left hand side, click on Recipes

2) At the bottom of the list, select Spell Research Swatch Recipes

3) Sort by Trivial (click on the word Trivial )

A word of advice: at 62, you have 3 more levels - that is 15 more training points.

Treasure these points, and spend not a single one until you hit 185 skill. Around 185, skillup rate slows exponentially.

I had the foresight to get my Research to 150+ over the course my leveling, and even after sinking 30+ points into it to get to 200, I still have over 50 points left.
