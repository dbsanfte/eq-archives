NobbinTook
I'm actually glad you brought this up. It reminded me to share this tip with tailors.

I am working towards 250. I'm at 199 right now. I've been switching from Solstice Robes (so my Tunarian Priest Guild Members can get a break) to Reinforced Acrylia armor. I figured I could make back some money on my purchases that way.

Some of the pieces seem to get snapped up by the new Zerkers who can wear them. But I can't figure out which pieces they like and which ones they don't.

So I decided to test out the new tribute system by turning in some reinforced acrylia pieces. I was amazed at the following:

1 Boning Pieces (Bracer, Mask) = 24 Tribute Points
2 Boning Pieces (Cloak)=**800** tribute points.

It only costs about 50-100pp to get an extra boning, but it gives you about 40x the value in Tribute points. So if you don't want to just throw stuff back to vendors (which I hate doing), you should consider making cloaks for an extra 100pp and get all the Tribute points!
