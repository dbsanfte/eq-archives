Dyllwin
Remember that you'll also need access to pottery, brewing, and smithing at as high a level you can manage, in order to minimize blue diamond losses.

That's the real trick to Haversacks. While it's painful to fail the final combine, most of the cost of failures once you get a high enough skill comes from the subcombines. You have 9 chances to lose a single Blue Diamond, and 1 chance to lose 3 of them at once. You have 3 chances to lose a single Platinum thread and one chance to lose 3 at once. If your pottery, brewing and smithing aren't up high enough above the trivials for those combines you will lose a ton of plat.

Don't sweat the tailoring combines on the bags until you can reliably make the infused threads :)
