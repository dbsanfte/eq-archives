For your Wiz before you start skilling try going to PoK or Nexus and looking for KEI... This will buy you some additional INT for what ever the caster is asking for donations. As a High elven Paladin I always look for Girdle of Karana, PE or HoS before I start smithing. I also will make some of the Str enhancing foods (Frost and Storm giant Steaks). This will normally max out my Str (255 as I am below 60).

For You if KEI does not get you to 255 then look on the baking page for the Int enhancing foods. You can get baking to 135 easy on Fishrolls (Also a good food to have, lasts a long time).

Last point, The high stat is only good for skill ups. So do things that are "within the range" if you want to succeed. Success is determined only by your skill level, everyone has between a 1 and 10 percent chance of making ANY item the higher your skill the greater the chance of making that item (i.e. Blue Diamond Cultural).
