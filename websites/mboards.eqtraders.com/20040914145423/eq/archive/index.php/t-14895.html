What is Concoction Of Flame III and what or how do I use it? What does this whole line of potions do?
KanadWinterstorm
The Concoction of * potions are a line of thrown potions with a direct damage effect, similar to a caster's nuke. They come in both cold and fire varieties and 4 levels off effectiveness (20hp at level 1 to 65dmg at level 4 per throw). To use a thrown potion you must equip the potion in your ranged item slot and empty your ammo slot, then throw the potion at your enemy using your ranged attack button.
