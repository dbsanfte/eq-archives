Astroshak
As a fellow ranger, allow me to offer my opinion.

The trophy is a nice item. 10 to STR, INT, WIS, and 50 to CHA, as well as being able to summon 100 WR bags. The 5 percent mod to fletching is moot, because you can buy a geerlok planing tool to ge thte same effect. Useless to you in combat unless used in range slot, useful out of combat for buying, selling, and fletching.

The bow is also a nice item. MUCH better than your current bow. Better stats, beter range, better damage and delay ratio. Useful in combat due to the stats (better stats than the trophy IMO) and as a pulling tool. With enough arrows, makes a fearsome bow for kiting with, even without the archery AA's.

All in all, I'd have to say, buy a geerlok and go for the bow first. The effects of the trophy (other than personal satisfaction) can be duplicated elsewhere, while the bow itself is a generally useful upgrade. The geerlok's skill mod and the trophy's skill mod do not stack, so having the one makes having the other irrelevant. Besides, I've said on the Ranger's Glade that the cultural bows are the best bows to use (though I've found one or two since that were better) from around 20 to EQ and some level of AM.
