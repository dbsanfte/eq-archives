Full Suit:

Boots, 2 Bracers, Collar, Mask. 1 Sheet Each.
Vambs, Gaunts, Pauldrons, Girdle, Helm. 2 Sheets Each.
BP, Greaves, Cloak. 3 Sheets Each.

That's a total of 24 Folded Sheets of Infused Mithril, minimum.
That's 13 pieces, so that's 13 Enchanted Mithril Jointing.
13 Enchanted Mithril Jointing requires 7 Enchanted Bricks.

So... 24 Blocks of Velium, 31 Enchanted Bricks of Mithril, 20 Morning Dew, 24 Celestial Tempers (BDs), 13 Moonlight Tempers.

I would say a 30% success rate, given the numbers you were looking for.

That means your total supplies would be:
80 Blocks of Velium
80 Celestial Tempers
105 Enchanted Bricks of Mithril
70 Morning Dew
45 Moonlight Tempers

Conservative Estimate from the resident CPA. :bookworm:
