For the first part of your question, you need to use html, so:
[code:1] &lt;center>your text here&lt;/center>[/code:1]
which then looks like:
<center> your text here </center>
For the second part of your question, this is explained in the FAQ: How to use Signatures (http://mboards.eqtraders.com/phpBB2/viewtopic.php?t=63).
