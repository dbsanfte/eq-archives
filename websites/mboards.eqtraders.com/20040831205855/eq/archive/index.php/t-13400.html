Lord Grimrose
A good average for skillups is 1 skill up in 20 combines. It's a good benchmark and many people believe this to be a consistant average. With 500 blocks using the 1 in 20 ratio you can expect 25 skill ups on the average. I will give this advise however, the RNG (Random Number Generator) can be streaky. It's also not unusual to go 100-200+ combines on a skill point a few(or a dozen) times on the path to 250.

just remember the RNG is random your personal results will vary.
