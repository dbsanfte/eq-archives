Uhm, the Koada Dal is for High Elves and needs Essence of Moonlight (from the mobs in Mistmoore). The Tier'dal is for Dark Elves and needs the Essence of Shadow from Shadow men (various zones).

Also, the Koada Dal that you are asking for is the NON blue diamond imbued armors (ie, the old cultural).

Just making sure that you are seeking the correct pieces.

Seventh Cavalry has a couple High Elf and Dark Elf smiths that I can contact to see if they are still making the old cultural.

Your best bet, if seeking the old cultural, is to obtain the various parts and look for a combine, as the enchanted ore (either mithril or adamantite) is a pain to acquire and will not be cheap if supplied by the smith.

Just some observations...
