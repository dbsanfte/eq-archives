I find the halfling bags to be quite sweet, personally.

Instead of paying 5250 pp for a tinkerers bag, you need 3 BD's and a night's worth of foraged tufts -- and less than 350 pp worth of components. My guild still does regular raids on the AOW so it's easy to get a couple stacks on a raid -- just ask folks to save them. And BD's drop pretty commonly in POP -- if we spend a night getting experience can often get enough BD's for everyone in the group to get one or two BD's. Or spend a night in POG. ;)

Plus the bags are 0.4 pounds lighter than tinkerer bags, so monks don't mind getting them at all! They're more than happy to give me a few BD's and some foraged tufts for the bags (remember, Iksar monks can forage these just fine).

Of course, this is on Test -- where folks tend to help each other out more. If you have to buy your tufts from guildmates and friends, I can see where that could be a problem.

EDIT: As noted below, it takes a bit more than 350 pp for components -- forgot plat threads are actually just over 200 pp, not 100. So total cost is closer to 650 pp per attempt, plus 3 BD's and the foraged tufts.
