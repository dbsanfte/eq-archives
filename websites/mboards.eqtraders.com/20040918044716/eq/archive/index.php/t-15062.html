I keep a mixing bowl handy and break down the meat into 1 lb sizes for ease in stacking. I have also started keeping track of how many lbs. of meat I get from 1 stack of bait. Currently, my average is 162 lbs per 20 bait, an average of about 8 lbs per cast. That equates to about 8 baking attempts for each 3 cp fishing bait. I am about 100 brewing, so I make the best sauce available for approximately 7 or 8 gp. Add about 2 sp for water and jug of sauces, I can make beer-battered crab or tuna soup for less than 1 pp each, which will take me to about 162 baking.

My point is that Abysmal fishing is still good for something: it could take you to 250 baking (Beer-battered crab souffle = 252 trivial)
