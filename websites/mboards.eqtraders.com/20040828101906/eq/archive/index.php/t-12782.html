Let's approach this from a different angle. Is there any planar temper where the ingredients drop from two or more zones? Off hand, I believe all the temper ingredients (for Night Terror, Hurricane, and Elemental tempers) all drop in one zone. The associated bricks of metal drop either in the same or in a different zone.

All the tempers follow a consistent pattern.
One appropriate blood
One rare component
One semi-rare component, of which you grind three in a mortar and pestle to yield four powders, used in the temper
