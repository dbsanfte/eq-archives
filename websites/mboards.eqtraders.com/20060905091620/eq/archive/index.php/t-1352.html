Try checking the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) forum. There is a thread there on success rate versus skill and what to expect. There are two very similar math models, one porposed by Iswar and one by Laurefin.

To sum it up, just barely over trivial for leather padding = horrible success rate.

Success rate at the trivial point is horrible for low trivials, and gets decent in the 170 skill range.
