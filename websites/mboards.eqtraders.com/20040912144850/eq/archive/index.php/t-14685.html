Nichola,

The idea of a downloadable version of the site has been suggested a few times, and each time Ngreth (or one of the other mods) shoots it down for two reasons. The download would be out of date literal seconds after it's generated, and it would horribly increase the bandwidth used by the site.

Have you visited the EQEcon.com site? I subscribed to their service recently, and they seem pretty solid.

Since they already have all the item info (I'm assuming they're somehow getting the info the same as Lucy and Magelo do), they also have the tribute value of the item stored there. I sent in a suggestion that they allow us to search by items by the ratio of bazaar price to tribute points. This lets you find the best "bargains" on your particular server, using actual prices. That might be something for you to look at.

Another alternative would be for EQTraders to add the Tribute value to the database. However, this would require extensive work, both in modifying the database and in manually keying in all the values. There's also the possibility that tribute values will vary in the future, making manual entry burdensome at best.

Just some random thoughts to chew on. :)
