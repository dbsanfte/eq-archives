Goldenly wrote:

" Hours of work getting to a fishing spot in old Seb..."

I saw this and it made my foot hair tingle. In case you're referring to finding a fishing spot once you zone in, and not actually getting there, there's a wonderful spot very near the zonein. If you can't port yourself out, take a gate potion of some kind, though, since you'll be a long way from any exit when you're done.

Right after you zone in, follow the tunnel through the long hallway down to the bridge. Face due south from the tunnel, and jump off the bridge, like you're going to the lower levels. Once you're in the water, though, swim straight south to the other side of the waterway, and climb out on the sloped ledge right there. In this spot, you're too far down to agro the frogloks above you (although you'll hear the progress of any battles that happen up there) and the ones below you are in water, and won't bother you. I've fished this spot since level 32 (yes, I got a Sebilis key at level 32) and I have never agroed anything accidentally. Getting to the bridge can be a chore if you're alone, but when I was younger I simply waited at the zonein for a group, and followed them in. Once you're in your spot, you can fish unmolested for hours if you choose. it's quite peaceful, aside from the occasional screams from the adventurers being butchered above.
