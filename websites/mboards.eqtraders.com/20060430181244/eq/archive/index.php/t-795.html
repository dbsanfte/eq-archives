I'm sure in this age of 60+ lvl chars few people bother with this anymore, but I've made leveling up my smithing inexpensive by this method so it may do for most folks to have a more accurate bit of info about the rusty->Tarnished->Ore supply route.

Namely that the yields on the smithing tables aren't exactly right, some of those conversions yield more than one piece of small ore.

I know for a fact Tarnished Spears give 2 pieces of ore, and I think there's something that gives 3. Rapiers may give 2 but that's a weaker memory. Spears are a confirmed fact (by myself anyway.)

I don't know if the yield info is to keep the box a certain size or whatnot, but I'll post all the small-piece yields here as I gather them.
