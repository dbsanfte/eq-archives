Centuri
I have found that on all my post-200 tradeskills, I expect approximately 1 skillup every 30 combines or so. I rarely go more than 40 without a skillup.

And it seems that at least once every tradeskill you get that "hump" point that you just can't get past and you'll be screaming when you hit 300 combines without a skillup.

I have not found any one item that works better than the others for skillups. Only the ease/difficulty of finding components really affects my decisons on which things to skill up on.
