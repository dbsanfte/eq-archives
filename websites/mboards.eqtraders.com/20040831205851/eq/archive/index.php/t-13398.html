To do the newbie armor quest for barbarian warrior talk to one of the men in the pit of doom. Im not positive on the name but he's the only guy in the pit of doom without the words Warrior Guildmaster under his name. Simply hail him and he will say something along the lines of "Are you a warrior here for your [training]? If not bugger off(or something to that nature).

Say 'what training?'

He'll tell you the training involves crafting of [armor].

Say 'what armor'

I think at this point you get the northern wolf plate assembly kit.

He'll then tell you what pieces you can make-[armplates] [bracers] [legplates] [gauntlets] [boots] [helm] [breastplate].

To get the mold for each one say 'i want to craft bracers/legplates/etc.'

In order to make the item you must gain the ingredients(recipes for each one at end of guide) and place them-UNSTACKED-in the northern wolf plate assebly kit and hit combine. Once you have the material put the material and it's appropriate mold in a forge and combine. I don't have the exact statistics for each piece of armor but they raise strength, stamina, and (maybe) agility. Also the breastplate is a lightsource so you can dump the torch.

The quest giver will also say at the end of telling you what pieces of armor that you can make that he has a [final task] for you to accomplish once you get done with the armor.

Say what final task and he'll tell you to travel to blackburrow and get him two perfect gnoll skins and two giant snake rattles.

I don't know at the moment what the reward is for doing this but I assume it's a weapon of some kind. That is just an guess though.

Recipes:

Legplates- 1 small piece of ore, 2 ruined wolf pelts, 1 bone chip, 1 spiderling leg, 1 patch of gnoll fur
Bracers- 1 polar bear skin, 1 small piece of ore, 3 bone chips
Boots- 2 small pieces of ore, 1 polar bear skin, 1 spiderling leg
Helm- 2 small pieces of ore, two bone chips, one gnoll pup scalp
Armplates- 1 small piece of ore, two ruined wolf pelts, one bone chip
Gauntlets- 1 small piece of ore, 1 ruined wolf pelt, 4 bone chips
Breastplate- 3 small pieces of ore, two ruined wolf pelts, 1 spider venom sac
