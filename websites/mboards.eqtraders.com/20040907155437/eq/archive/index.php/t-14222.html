Pages 69-70...

You don't need a friend, and the 'timed' nature of the quest has minimal impact. This is true if you remember to do one thing; after you attempt a repair on a dented shield, whether it fails or works, pick up new repair orders before going out. This reduces the time needed to complete this quest dramatically.

If the guard has re-spawned with the shield, simply give him the orders, hand in the repaired shield, and head back to repair the shield just received if its repair is still not trivial and for another repair order.

If he has not re-spawned, hand back the repaired shield and head straight to a guard you still have to do work for.

The only road block is when you have three repairs completed. And, that occurs if the repair fails. In that case, you must wait until the specific guard you need respawns with his dented shield. This is true whether you are doing the quest with a friend or not, and if you both fail when working toward your 4th repair, you are actually slowing each other down.

The repair requiring the highest skill can be repeated until it is trivial relatively quickly if you get lucky on the repairs so that you don't have to wait for re-spawns between attempts along with not running back for new repair orders between turn-ins.
