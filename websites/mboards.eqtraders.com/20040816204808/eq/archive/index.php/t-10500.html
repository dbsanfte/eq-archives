tatanka7th
LOL, well since I passed 236 in pottery last night, I can talk about my "secret" =)

There are 3 spots in the zone where mobs get stuck in the wall. Two are up on the wall, on each side of the canyon leading to the three eyes. One is east of there, along the main canyon from EK to RE, on the north wall. All three spots are as high on the wall as you can walk, right where it becomes vertical.

If you go to the spots, you can hear the mobs "walking" but you probably can't see them. In one spot, they are sometimes visible.

I used PBAoE spells to kill them, since it was quick and fast, and didn't require targetting mobs I couldn't see. However, if you don't have a way to PBAoE, just go to the spots you hear the mobs "walking" and sit down. They will attack you, and appear for targetting =) They may not become visible, but will attack you, so if you still can't see them, just move away from the wall, they will follow and become targettable.

I actually bound at one of the spots last month when I started working on GMing pottery. At the beginning and end of each session, I'd clear all three spots. This cleared about 20-25 mobs per day, netting 5-10 heavy clays each day.

Once you clear these spots, the mobs spawn in normal spots, and if killed relatively quickly, you can get to them before they get stuck. That's if you want to stay there and keep farming them.

And, yes, I used track to find the spots. If you track the oldest mobs, and walk slowly once you get close, you will be led right to the spots.
