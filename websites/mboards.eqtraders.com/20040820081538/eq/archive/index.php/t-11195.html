Brodybeard
You may find these three PoP recipes useful from 242 to 250:

Wok
Components: clockwork carapace, firewater, gnomish bolts, grease, metal rod

According to the EQTraders database, the clockwork carapace drops from mobs in the Bastion of Thunder.

Crab Cracker
Components: firewater, knuckle joint, metal rod(2)

According to the EQTraders database, knuckle joint drops off mobs in the Plane of Innovation.

Cheirometric Lockpick Device
Components: chainmail gloves, coiled spring, mana battery - class six, mechanized lockpicks, pinion, steel ball bearing

According to the EQTraders database, the coiled spring, mana battery - class six, pinion, and steel ball bearing drop off mobs in the Plane of Innovation. The mana battery can also be purchased in the PoK.
