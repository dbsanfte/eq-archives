My experience with Wood Elf Cultural tailoring was excellent. For some reason, I skilled up much more often with cultural than with Velious. I went up to 228 tailoring skill mainly on cultural. 228 is the trivial. Beyond that you make Tunarean armor which required expensive blue diamonds.

Oak bark is fairly easy to forage in Kithicor. My 'puter is fairly decent, so I logged out/in to reduce forage time. I averaged a forage every 60 seconds that way.

Morning dew is also easy to forage. You can also buy it by auctioning in Greater Faydark. I paid 5 pp each.

Excellent sabertooth tiger hides can be bought fairly easily. I port to Skyfire, egress to OT zone line, auction for hides in OT, zone to Frontier Mountains, egress to LOIO zone line, and auction again in LOIO. I pay 20 pp per hide and averaged 2 - 3 per run. After awhile people would save the hides for me.
