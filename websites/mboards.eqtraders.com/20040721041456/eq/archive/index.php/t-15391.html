To repeat something that's been said over and over (and over and over) for several years. Loot for a mob is established when the mob spawns.

How fast you kill, what you kill it with, what level you are, etc. have absolutely, positively no bearing on what the mob will drop.

The only thing that really can change what the mob will drop after it spawns is a pickpocket. :eek:
Chenier
The brownie guards, brownie and brownie scouts in LFay all have the above "brownie bits" (brains, torso, etc. etc.) in their loot table. Brownie outcasts do not (only drop parts and one of the arrows).

Btw, if you are worried about faction, you can just kill brownie outcasts as they are not on brownie faction. They are a rare spawn from killing pixie's (and ALL pixie's MUST die) at the pixie tower in LFay. Pixie tricksters and pranksters are on pixie faction - pixie jongleurs are not. As a goodie, don't kill the jongleurs (I think they are tied to some Tunarean-related faction, but not sure).

Oh, and if you're killing the brownie guards and the dudes inside (3 regular brownies and 1 farmer), be sure to kill the itty baby spiderlings in the brownie fort. They have insane drop rate for spiderling silks and they respawn super fast.
