I just spent an hour doing subcombines for Gruppa's Poppin Ale hoping to get the last 2 skill points I needed.
Database has the trivial listed wrong as there are two different recipes with two different trivials.

Gruppa's Poppin Ale has a trivial of 202 and uses Fizzlepop (soda; flask of fruit juice; bottle)

Gruppa's Poppin Ale (cask) has a trivial of 255 and uses Fizzle Pop (berries; bottle(2); Sylvan berries; Aerated mineral water)

I was using the printed recipe from the database.

Just didn't want anyone to make the same mistake as I.
