Lothay
The trivials are designed so that you still have a significant failure rate on the higher-end items. From a game-design perspective, this is a sound choice. Otherwise, everyone and thier dog would have only the very best gear.

The trivial values are reported by the Tradeskill Interface -- that's how we got those numbers.
mewkus
Also, the highest skill that you can have is 250. The cap of 252 means your modified skill cannot exceed 252. So if you have a skill of 240 and a geerlok (skill +5%), then your modified skill is 252. If you have a skill of 250 plus that same geerlok, your modified skill is also 252 (capped).
