Itek
Monk level 60 and under 255 WIS (max)

Monk level 61 ... 260
62 ... 265
63 ... 270
64 ... 275
Monk level 65 ... 280 WIS (max) ((and all other stats for that matter))

Planar Power AA (must have PoP expansion, if you don't.. go buy it)

Each level of Planar Power adds 5 max to all stats. You can buy one (and only one) level of Planar power each level 61 thru 65 (or any time there-after)

Planar Power 5 and Level 65 max stats (all) 305.

That's it. Non-casters (Cleric, Druid, Shaman, Wizard, Mage, Necro, Enchanter) are capped at 305 for WIS/INT.

The general Innate Wisdom AA does not raise WIS cap, it raises actual WIS.

The 61-65 level actually raises the WIS (and all stats) cap but not actual WIS.

Planar Power also raises WIS cap but not actual WIS.

Um... yeah.

To clarify.

Innate Wisdom (or Int) raises your CURRENT Wisdom, not your MAX Wisdom.

Ditto for Improved Innate Wisdom (the PoP version, which requires 5 points (10 Wisdom points added to your base) in Innate Wisdom before you can buy up to 10 additional points (which raises your base Wisdom an additional 20 points) at any time, with no level restriction, other than 51+ of course)
