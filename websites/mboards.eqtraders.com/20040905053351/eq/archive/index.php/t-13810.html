Brodybeard
I decided to try the new tradeskill interface by skilling up my halfling from 195 to 200.

He collected 5 empty deluxe toolboxes and bought:

15 stacks of malt
10 stacks of short beer
10 stacks of water
5 stacks of yeast
5 stacks of casks

for 100 combinations of Minotaur Hero's Brew.

First, he had to use the "Experiment" button to bring up the old brew barrel interface to successfully make Minotaur Hero's Brew. When he succeeded, he got a message telling him that the recipe had been added.

After that, he opened the new interface and whipped out Brews in three clicks (one click for the Combine button and two clicks to autoinventory the results).

He needed 155 combinations to reach 200 with a Wisdom of 229 (D'oh! I forgot to exchange his charisma gear for wisdom gear!) I may take a little time to skill up one of my mules to 250 since he's stuck at 200 until he levels above 50 and can earn some AA points.

I like the new interface, but I'm sad about how much more easy it has made grandmastering brewing.
