I was browsing through the recipes and stats for crafted piercing weapons and noticed that there wasn't any info on the Combine Acrylia Dagger. It isn't listed on allakhazam yet either so I decided to make one. Like some of the other daggers and rapiers it does NOT use a pommel mold. Equipped it has the same graphic as the Black Acrylia Dagger (saw a pic on Tradeskill Fashions) or Curfang's Torment. Chunky looking grey dagger with a green gem in the hilt, same style as the line of weapons on Katta guards like most of the other acrylia weapons. It was trivial to me at 175.

Skill: Piercing Atk Delay: 18
DMG: 6
