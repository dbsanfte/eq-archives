Buy in bazaar or ask your halfling druid friends (or the rare Karanan cleric) to imbue them for you - at the rate you can forage planar oak and dragon eggs it will take quite some time to get through a stack of imbued pebbles. You can certainly forage the unimbued ones as an elvish druid. No need to remake a character - the game mechanics strive to "encourage" you to work with other players to get things done. And if you were a halfling you would probably want the blessed dust of Tunare for some of the other combines.

Good luck
