I'm a Dwarven Smith and have been doing old school cultural since the May 8th patch.

At 188 skill I had great luck with 1 and 2 sheet Enchanted Dwarven Plate, never more than 2 tries for any piece. On 3 sheet items, with the exception of one bad run of the RNG, it never took more than 3 tries to make legs, BP, or cloak. The Enchanted Imbued Dwarven Plate was a little rougher to make but usually a MAX of 3 tries on 1&2 sheet items and a MAX of 4 tries for BP / Legs (never tried imbued enchanted cloak.)

Bottom line, with 188 smithing and a geerlok you have a pretty good chance of making the Enchanted Dwarven Plate Helm. Now finding a chanter to enchant all the brellium might be a different story :)
