I'm on Xev, and I haven't had too much trouble when I go to farm haze panthers for tinkering. If there is someone else in the zone when I zone in, I usually just send them a tell to see what they are there for. (it's a fairly underutilized zone.) If it's haze panthers for eyes/skins, then I just farm something else until they are finished. (and the zone area where they spawn and wander is easily trackable; usually only a section of zone, not the entire zone.)
