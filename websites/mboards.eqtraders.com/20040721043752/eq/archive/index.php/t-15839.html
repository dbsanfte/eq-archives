Well, I certainly wouldn't go for the GOD whips at that cost and payback ratio, although the success rate is nice. I detest the Solstice robe route having had nothing but horrible luck (get no points, succede on chains a LOT). I actually found it was costing me more like 70/80 plat per attempt given the chain cost and me succeeding 1 out of 7 to 10 tries. The robes are going for as low as 75 plat recently on Druzzil which is just insane IMHO. And since I am not elemental or in an uber guild, i.e., poor, I hate losing that kind of plat to get skill points. I also got tired of farming Rockhoppers, especially when they went GTM.

However, I have found something that is working pretty well for me, not sure what class you are and if you can dot it. I am working up through the LoY robes. I find Bazaar vendors or NPC Vendors selling the words and runes I need usually quite often (I pay anywhere from 2 to 25 plat in bazaar, 6 to 7 plat from NPCs). As allways, just after a patch is the best NPC shopping time. Occaisionally I sometimes even find the temper components (bamboo shoots and sea horse roe for me), although usually I have to spend a few hours and farm them myself, analogous to spider silk farming. While the low level robes also don't sell for a lot, they usually do sell for more than the cost to make them (silver ribbon, rune/word, parchment, quill, shoot and water, for instance.) Occasionally I get a nice Dew Clover or, more rarely, a Rune of the Tempest (Robe of the Tempest sells for 5,000 plat on Druzzil). I am currently at 213 and when I trivial on runes of inverse at 222 I will start mixing in words of ethereal (232) and Velious stuff as I come across it, farm it.

Now, this is not a fast way to skill up, but it is one of the most inexpensive ways. If you're not a class that can make these then you're probably stuck with the Solstice robe or the hoppers. Good luck to you.
