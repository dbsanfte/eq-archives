Flame Tipped Acrylia Arrow - bundled acrylia arrow shafts + condensed flame arrowheads + several shield cut fletchings + small groove nocks - trivial >250
Ice Tipped Acrylia Arrow - bundled acrylia arrow shafts + condensed ice arrowheads + several shield cut fletchings + small groove nocks - trivial >250
Shadow Tipped Acrylia Arrow - bundled acrylia arrow shafts + condensed shadow arrowheads + several shield cut fletchings + small groove nocks - trivial >250

Bundled Acrylia Arrow Shafts - arrow shaft mold + small brick of acrylia ore + water flask - trivial 54 (smithing)
Condensed **Substance** Arrowheads - chunk of condensed shadow + file + water flask - trivial 116 (smithing)

200-250 on arrows.... real cheap, if you take the time to farm the items yourself.

Do a search for "acrylia".. there's a couple threads that have pretty in depth discussion on where to get it.

Personally, i do AC. Mobs in the entrance section all have a chance to drop it, and it's a bit more common than what you've gotten.. probably the rng jumped on your back. In 3-4 hours, i usually fill my packs with acrylia, weapons, and shards. I've farmed roughly 280 shafts worth of acrylia, and gained roughly 12k plat doing so... mind you i've spent probably 50pp of that making those shafts... certainly cheaper than buying you're way to 250 on bows, or through the bazaar. I figure going from 200 to 250 will put me up about 60-75k minimum.. plus experience, assuming i don't sell any of the arrows i make.

Trackers might get better results in TM, but i'm not positive of the abundance of the aliens that drop it... i just usually stumble around till i find one.
