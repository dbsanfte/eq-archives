Kelador Stormwolf
As other people have said, bows require more outlay, but assuming you succeed on the combine you're not losing too much per attempt - at least until you get to shadewoods which can be a real plat sink. It kinda depends on whether losing a couple of hundred plat in a single combine makes you wince more than losing a couple of hundred plat over 100 combines...

If you want to make bows but you don't want to spend a lot of money you can do the Trueshot bow quest repeatedly (assuming you have the requisite faction in Kelethin and Kaladim (and the other places you need to acquire stuff on the quest). The Trueshot bow trivial is 235 - after that you're either going to have to make shadewood bows or switch to arrows. Cheapest way, but by far the most time consuming is to make acrylia arrows and farm the components yourself.

BTW I found that condensed flame drops in Scarlet Desert (from the Tro Jeg guys on and around the plateau) was a better return than the condensed shadow in Shadeweavers Thicket... SwT caves are fine as long as the 'right set' of aliens is up but one advantage of Scarlet Desert is there are Scarlet Rockhoppers running around that can drop flawless and superb hides - which you can sell for obscene amounts in the bazaar to desperate tailors, which you can in turn use to offset costs for acrylia if you get bored of farming the stuff.
