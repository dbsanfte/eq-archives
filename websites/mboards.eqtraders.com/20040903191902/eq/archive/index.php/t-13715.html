OK, the recipe is slightly confusing there... my fault.

If you look at the recipe for the Planar Steel Bolts:

Planar Steel Bolts (http://www.eqtraders.com/items/show_item.php?item=7214)

You will see:

Smithing Components: brick of immaculate steel, cam parts mold, file
In: forge
Yield: 1
Also Returns: file, planar steel gears
On Failure Returns: file
Trivial at: 91<x<=114

This one recipe returns the Planar Steel Bolts AND the Planar Steel Gears.

I never created the alternate recipe... and I have had someone pester me about it. Just haven't added it.

If you make the Bolts you are ALSO making the Gears in the same step.
