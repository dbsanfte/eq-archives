Not having any Electrified Copper yet I haven't tried this. But my suggestions are as follows:

1. Verify you have Electrified Copper BITS (not Bricks)
2. Check if you are using ONE Bit... If So, Try TWO Bits, or even THREE Bits (all unstacked of course). This is based on the fact to make standard Metal Studs, the recipes is THREE Metal Bits, Water and File...

If the answer is #2... The recipe needs to be updated.

Once successful, please report Qty Produced and whether Trivial or not at your current Skill Level.
