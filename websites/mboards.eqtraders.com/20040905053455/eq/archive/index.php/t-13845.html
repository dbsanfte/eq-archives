G'Day Folks,

For the info of those Gnomes considering doing some tinkering and who also like (very boring) quests...

If you talk to Skelontorim Orrthemech (one of the tinkering merchants) he will ask you to make some springs for him AND will give you the (single) ingredient to be combined. The trivial for the combine is listed in the UI as 31 - which matches the similiar Tailoring quests (they progress through a number of items until the final one which has a trivial of 54).

Sadly, since we gnomes start with a skill of 50, making 100 springs then 100 of the next item until you get to the fourth which will presumably trivial at 54, does not seem worthwhile.

If you do decide to try this out, hand the products back to Skelontorim unstacked!

Erikanudan
Yes, do the grunt work combines and you learn how to convert Raw Dark Matter to Dark Matter. And how to convert the Raw Infused Dark Matter into Infused Dark Matter.

1 Dark Matter yields 5 Darkmatter Dust, I imagine the same for Infused.

1 Darkmatter Dust + Battery Casing 1 = 1 Class 1 Battery.
2 Darkmatter Dust + Battery Casing 2 = 1 Class 2 Battery.
.... etc up to being able to make Class 5 battery.

Class 6 Batteries are made from Infused Dark Matter.

So, after doing the 400ish n00bie combines, you learn the secret to produce Dark Matter/Infused Dark Matter.

Of course, you need to do alot of your own conversions to get your conversions to yield 3.

End Result of Quest is the ability to make Mana Batteries for about 20% of the cost to buy them from a vendor (since you need to buy the battery casing).
