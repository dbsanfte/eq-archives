I understand better now. Fishing in this regard is like foraging. There's a fishing table with "basic" and "rare" stuff for each zone. The higher your fishing skill, the more likely you'll get something rare from the table (and the more likely you'll get anything at all). So, in answer to your question, the higher your skill, the more likely you'll get special stuff. Some zones don't have any special stuff to catch, but in places that do, your skill will decide how often you get them.
