Skillups and sucesses are different.

You said: "I have heard from many people that the highest of str, wis, or intelligence is what gives you your success rate "

Sucess rate is based directly on your skill rating, not any of str, wis or int.

However, str, wis and int does affect the rate at which you get skill ups. The higher skill you have the less failures you will see.
