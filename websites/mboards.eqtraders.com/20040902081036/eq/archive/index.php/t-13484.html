My druid made:

unfired opal encrusted steins to 222
unfired white ceramic bands to 231
unfired crucibles of escape to 236
unfired idols of tunare to 242
unfired star ruby encrusted steins to 250She fired all the steins and ceramic bands and is slowly selling them off in the Bazaar. I would guess that at this point, the star ruby encrusted stein sales have paid for her skill-ups.

There were some bad spots when she thought she wasn't ever going to get another skill-up point. But looking back at her records for 200 to 250, she actually averaged approximately 30 combinations per skill-up.

It really helps if you know an enchanter who is willing to enchant all those vials of mana; a priest that can imbue the gems; and a jeweler to lacquer gems.
