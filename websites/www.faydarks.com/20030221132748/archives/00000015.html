09/09/2002 Entry: "Dalimar"

Some News

Not a lot of news at the moment but some exciting news nonetheless. Our old friend Dalimar is back but with a new character. His new character is Riukie, currently a level 15 Monk. Even more exciting is that his second child is on the way! No events are scheduled as of yet but some should be coming up soon. In the events forum there is a place where you can vote for where the next raid will be.
