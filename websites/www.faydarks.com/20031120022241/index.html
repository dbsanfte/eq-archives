Vessel Repair

Bludworth Marine operations are driven by our commitment to maintain the highest standards of service and safety in the industry. At Bludworth Marine, we believe in going the distance for our customers.
Bludworth Marine has employees with decades of experience in shipyard repairs and services to barges, ships, tugs, inland and ocean going, and other marine equipment.
Give us a chance to service your marine equipment needs.

We specialize in ship repair and topside marine vessel repairs in all Texas ports. Our home office is located at
3502 Broadway just north of Hobby Airport between I-45 and Loop 610. We are proud of our rapid response repairs and offer a full range of expert repair services to the marine industry. If you are in the area, please feel free to drop in for coffee and a visit.

We built and operated "Bludworth" pushing systems beginning in the 1970's and are the manufacturing agent for the Bludworth Cook ATB Connection System on offshore tug barge units and also offer repair services.
