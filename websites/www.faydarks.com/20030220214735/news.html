Friday, October 4, 2002

For those of you that don't know Faydark's Lost Dragons under went a pretty big change last Tuesday. Most of the officer's and many others in the guild decided it was time to move on including myself (Barliman). In the 14 months I was apart of Faydarks Order of Peace and Faydark's Lost Dragons I had a great time. The only thing I regret was that I did not get enough pictures. Even though I only have fond memories I felt it was time to move on and experience some of the higher end game. I put thousands of hours into trying to get Faydark's Lost Dragons to the high end game but in the end saw little to no improvement. Many officers just didn't have the time and I too don't have the time anymore.

I don't know of everyone that left but I know for sure that Talnakk, Tansor, Youngleaf, and myself have moved on. I am not certain who else is leaving but if I remember correctly Aluiene, Ely, Gwen, Shatner, and Ripfang are among some of the others leaving soon. I'm not certain of the new officer line up since Lasslie is the only officer left. Cryingwolf is now the guild leader and I'm sure he'll appoint some good officers. In a couple of days I'll be temporarily replacing the intro movie with a video of some pics of the old and current guildees, going all the way back to when I first joined the guild. Well this is good bye for me though I'm still in the game and you can always send me a tell and ask for anything.

Sunday, August 25, 2002

Thurgadin Rejoices!

The crowds cheered as the triumphant heroes of Faydark's Lost Dragons returned to Thurgadin after their slaughter of Wurms.

"We condemn any action against the Shardwurms of the Great Divide" the mayor of Thurgadin, Durgan Bottlenip told reporters as he poured Fingles another Dwarven Ale with a smile.

"Wurms bad!" Fingles replied with a hicup. Fingles was last seen leaving the party with Kyla Frostbeard. Best of luck to you Fingles, great beard on that chick!

"You're not writing this down right? Good. None of us like the wurms in Thurg but the mayor thinks they are too strong to formally declare war on. Your not going to quote me on that though right? Thank god." Tobart Dirkins was quoted saying after his 14th Dwarven Ale. Abalene told me not to print that but it was opposite day so it wasn't opposite day, or was it?

"I want to thank that crazy couple Shatner and Gwenurthur for saving my cat Ralphe," -Yates the Butler

"Kantry and Arura are shorter than me!" -Mordin the Short

Ripfang and Eenea were hoisted onto the dwarves shoulders and carried around town. Sokath was muttering something about those blasted chat channels and that key next to L but cheered up once he saw Fingles fall off his table. Aluiene showed up a bit late but no one minded once they got their hug. I watched from the back quietly. You know me, always the quiet one.

Anyway, I'm very proud of those who showed up. We did a great job and more events are in the works. The officers are getting together Tuesday night for a meeting and we'll set up some bigger and better raids. Check back Wednesday for upcoming events.
