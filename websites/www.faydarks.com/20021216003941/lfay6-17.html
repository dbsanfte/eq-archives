Lesser Faydark Mayhem - June 17th

In the early hours of June 17th, eight brave souls ventured into Lesser Faydark on a dangerous mission. They were to rid Lfay of not only the fierce brownies but also the dangerous Tier'Dal. Aluiene, Berrysings, Elysius, Sabble, Sebab, Sioahban, Sweething, and Youngleaf were the eight chosen for this mission. They also discovered the faerie city and spoke with Princess Joleena at length. Sebab even ran into the Pained Unicorn after she battled a deadly gnome mage.
