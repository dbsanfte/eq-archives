I. LEADERSHIP

A. The Guild Leader (GL) will have all final say in anything concerning the
guild or any of its members. The GL reserves the right to edit this charter as
to accomadate anything not foreseen herein.

B. The Ruling Council (RC) will be much the same as Guild Officers as far as
invites. The Ruling Council has authority over Guild Officers. The RC's basic
duties include recruitment, organizing guild events, and answering questions from
Guild Officers or members if no Guild Officers or Class Leaders are available.

C. The Guild Officers (GO) will have the same basic functions as the RC but
will not have quite the authority. They will have the authority to invite members
but do answer to the RC. If a GO can not answer a question it will be forwarded
to a RC.

D. Each class will have an appointed Class Leader (CL). The Class Leaders will
be in charge of answering any class specific questions but will not have the authority
to invite. If a CL can not answer a question they will forward the question on to a GO.
Class Leaders will be chosen by the Guild Officers and are subject to a monthly review.
Class Leaders will be chosen after we have more of a member base.

1) The guild's minimum level requirement for new members is level 20. The officers will
choose when to invite alts though there is not minimum level. If your main has been a long
time guild member they may decide to invite your alt at level 1, if your fairly new to the guild
you may have to wait a bit longer to get an alt invited. That decision is up to the officers.
However, alts will not be listed on the member's list until they reach level 20.

2) The maximum amount of recruits for each month is 4. Exceptions to this rule may
apply in extraordinary situations but only with the consent of the GL or the RC.

3) All class specific questions should be directed toward your CL. If your CL
is not on at the time ask a GO. If no GO's are on at the time ask a RC member.
If your question is class specific ask a GO, if no GO's are on ask a RC member.


II. MERIT SYSTEM

The merity system is no longer being used.


III. LOOT

1) All loot on raids will be rolled on based on need before greed. We ask that players
do not roll on an item unless their character present at the raid needs it. If no one needs
the particular item there will be an open roll in which everyone can roll. Items will be rolled
on after the raid is over. All no drop items will be rolled for at the time by members who
need them. Any items that no one wants will be used to fund the Dragon of the Month
and Active Dragon of the Month awards.

2) If people from other guilds are in the raid they can roll on items they need unless
stated otherwise by the raid leader.

3) All spells/songs that drop on raids will be given to the highest level player who
can use the spell/song. If there is a tie between levels it will be rolled upon by those
members.


IV. RAIDS

1) If you wish to invite someone to a raid that is not in our a guild you must check
with the GL, RC, or a GO first.

2) Come prepared to the raid. Peridots, gate potions and things of that nature are
to be taken care of before joining in on a raid.

3) Any questions or concerns about a raid will be directed to the GL.

4) GL unless not present will be the main authority over the raid. If the GL is not present
it will be announced who the main authority of the raid is.

5) Most raids will be announced at least 1 week in advance. If you can not participate
in the raid for at least 2 hours please do not come. If you will be attending a raid there
will be a raid sign up on the web page. Please sign up at least one day before the raid.

V. CONDUCT

1) No Kill Stealing (KSing) is allowed.

2) There will be no flames in guild chat or on the message boards unless in a designated
flame forum.

3) We are a guild created around making Norrath a better place for all. We will not
tolerate abuse of any kind toward other members or non-guild members.

4) Selecting a new main requires the member's alt to be at least level 30. To declare a
new main you must get approval from the GL, RC or a GO.

5) Respect your fellow guild members as well as players not in the guild.

6) All persons clicking yes upon a guild invite agree to this charter and trust the judgement
of the GL and appointed RC and GO's of this guild.
