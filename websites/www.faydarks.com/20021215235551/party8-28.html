Guild Party, August 28th, 2002

We had a guild party at the Nro docks complete with a pet duel, troll death charge and a scavenger hunt. My 39 pet dueled Talnakk's pet. It was a close fight but my pet was victorious. He had about 25 percent of his life left at the end.

The Troll Death Charge was a blast. Bellicosus, Eenea, Elevenknight, Fingles, Taloc, and Ripfang all made level 1 trolls. We then had Shatner port them over to Nro and we ran to Freeport. From there the Trolls lined up and whoever made it closest to East Freeport won 500 platinum.

However, we made the mistake of sowing the trolls and as it turned out 4 of them made it all the way to East Freeport! So from there it was whoever made it closest to the docks. Fingles, died just seconds from the docks with Eenea and Ripfang right behind.

We then had a scavenger hunt in Nro. The first one to bring back the specified 5 items won 100 platinum. I don't remember the exact items but they were all found off of mobs in Nro. Eenea surprised everyone, finding them all within about 7 or 8 minutes.

All in all it was a great time. I think we should have these parties at least once or twice a month. Its a great way to get together and have some fun. You can even make some money.
