Reformation Underway
Posted by Barliman, on June 6th, 2002

Plans for the reformation of our guild are underway. Lasslie has accepted our offer to lead the guild but will be without internet for up to a month. In the mean time we have offered Talnakk the leader title until Lasslie can return. The reformation will take place this Sunday, June 9th, at 8pm eastern / 5pm pacific time. Also, there will be an officer meeting this Friday, June 7th, at 9pm eastern / 6pm pacific. We'd like to get as many people on for the reformation as possible so if you can make it please do come. The whole process could take up to 2 hours so make sure you can be on for a bit if you do show. We will all try to get online at 8pm eastern and join our chat channel (/join faydarks). When we have at least 10-15 people in the channel we will give out the password needed to join the private channel as well as the channel name. From there we will go over what needs to be done next and then Talnakk will log to sign into the chat. After that we will all need to log and go into the chat on the server sign on screen. Basically at this point the 10 or more people in the channel will have to enter a command. Talnakk will enter the guild charter and he will receive an email shortly after notifying him that our guild was approved. Talnakk would then need to get on to petition a gm to actually create our guild. Talnakk is the only person that needs to be present for the petition. That pretty much sums it up but we will go over it again at 8pm eastern / 5pm pacific when everyone is on the in game chat channel faydarks. For more in depth info on what we'll need to do and guild creation in general follow this link Guild Info

Ok now on to a few more things. For the officer meeting at 9pm eastern / 6pm pacific Friday, Talnakk will create a private chat called meeting. Talnakk will set the password so you officers will need to send Talnakk a tell on how to join the channel when you get on. Talnakk has quite a few things to go over including the guild charter so if you officers can make it please do. As far as the talks of changing the guild name I have emailed Lasslie about it and she is going to get back to me. She is very busy with real life so if she doesn't get time to respond we will probably hold a vote on keeping or changing the name on Sunday when everyone gets on. Ok I think I have covered everything, sorry for the long news update.

-Barliman
