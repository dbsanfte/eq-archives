Awards

Each month the officers will give out two awards to the members. For June we will only select an Active Dragon of the Month as June will be ending shortly. The "prizes" for each award are not set in stone yet so I won't go into exactly what the prizes are but the winners will be receiving some platinum and will be shown on the web page.

Active Dragon of the Month
Active Dragon of the Month, will be awarded to the member who attended the most guild events. Such as raids, buffing stations, guild meetings, etc. The officers will keep track of who was at each event and at the end of the month will see which member attended the most events.

Dragon of the Month
Dragon of the Month, will go to the member who the officers deem as the most helpful member of the month. Each member is asked to submit other members for going above and beyond their duties to the guild. If a fellow member has done something extremely great and you would like to submit who the player is and what they have done, enter the information in the box below. The officers will choose who the award goes to by your nominations.
