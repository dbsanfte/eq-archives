Again it has been some very exciting times since my last update. The heroes are now larger and more powerful than ever. Huntingguide 001113:

First of all, it has been some very exciting times since my last update. It all began when our glorious HoB surprisingly to us all was wiped by Verant in their "no guilds with less than 10 people" swipe of the servers.

This of course lead to immediate reaction from our fearless leader, who after a lot of emailing, finally succeeded in getting the guild recreated.

To avoid being wiped again in the next server sweep, we have begun an unusually active recruitment, which have included most of the older members, plus a number of newcomers, with whom I have confidence we will take the HoB to new heights.

This influx of new members also have the consequence that we now longer are one homogenous group of characters at approximately the same level. Therefore I will alter the format for the huntinggrounds a little to reflect the greater variation in levels.

From now on I will probably elect a certain area as huntinggrounds, instead of a more specific dungeon.

The place to be the coming week or two will be in Kunark. I have had a long list of requests to move the HG to Kunark, since our experience shows that the frequency of Pking in Kunark is minuscule compared to Antonika proper.

That being said, the HG will be Frontier Mountains in Kunark. FM are rated 25 to 40, where 25 is an absolute minimum, and only capable of zonepulling. However FM borders to Lake of Ill Omen (1 to 40), where all characters between 20 and 27 should be doing Sarnak castle or goblin ruins - tons and tons of mobs, and the chance of getting very nice rare items.

FM also contains two dungeons for those of us that might not want to hunt in the open. These are the Mines of Nurga (25 - 45) and the Temple of Droga (35 to 45). These two wil suit the great mass of HoB's that are in their 30s.

For a nice change we can take a stroll in the neighbouring forest of the Dreadlands (35 to 50) or Burning woods (35 to 55). Finally its sort of close to both the good city of Firiona Vie, and the evil city of The Overthere.

As always there are no obligations to participate, but if you want to have an improved chance to meet-n-greet with your fellow HoB's (and maybe score a descent item or two from your friends vault), Frontier Mountains and the surroundings will be the place to go the coming weeks.

Hope to see most of you.

Lt. Valenasun Rayofsun
