Guild News :

Forming of the Council
 Guild News [ 15-May-01 ] - Lt. Valenasun Rayofsun

Hail Heroes

As of today we have officially formed the Council of the Heroes of Bifrost.

The Council has participation of the 8 highest ranking members in the Heroes.

The council primarily has only one task - to vote for or against the acceptance of any new members into our ranks.

This means that the Heroes once again will be accepting new members of all levels above 15.

In order to be considered the prospective members has to make a post on the General Forum explaining who they are, and why they want to join the Heroes.

From here they will need a councilmember to sponsor them, and thereby move them to the votingboard.

We have set some flexible voting rules, and should be able to reach descision in about 1 day.

I hope you all agree that this is the correct direction to take, and that we will all benifit from a number of new members in our ranks.

Hail Heroes of Bifrost,

Lt. Valenasun Rayofsun


Eventmaster
 Guild News [ 07-May-01 ] - Lt. Valenasun Rayofsun

Hail Heroes

In order to promote the feeling of being a true Hero and part of a greater whole, we have decided to put a stronger weight on doing raids in the guild.

While this most likely will create stronger ties between the members of the Heroes of Bifrost, it has the added bonus of making it possible to get items which otherwise would be out of our individual reach.

There is a lot of work involved in arranging raids, but Frankey has generously volunteered to do the job.

To signal this new position Frankey is promoted to Grand Master of Raids.

As you can see from the menu bar, we now have a new forum - Heroes of Bifrost eventforum.

This forum is protected by a password.

To obtain your pass, please contact Frankey in game, and he will let you know the rules applying to participate in the Heroes of Bifrost raidgroup.

This group is NOT only for highlevels, and NOT restricted to Heroes of Bifrost.

I wish to thank Frankey for taking up this challenge and I hope that we will get a lot of participants in the raidgroup.

Hail Heroes of Bifrost,

Lt. Valenasun Rayofsun

Demotions
 Guild News [ 15-Mar-01 ] - Valenasun Rayofsun

Hear Ye, Hear Ye

For actions unbecoming of a true hero, for seriously jeopardizing our relationship with the esteemed Wild Hunt, and for the direct blame for the death of Frankey, the former Grand Master of the Enchanters Sionar Sun have been stripped of rank and honor.

Sionar is no longer counted as a noble of the Heroes of Bifrost, and has instead been placed at the fourth rank.

Sionar has had an official warning put against him and as such will be rejected from the Heroes of Bifrost in the case of a repeating offence.

Nerakim has recieved a warning for his partaking in the incident.
