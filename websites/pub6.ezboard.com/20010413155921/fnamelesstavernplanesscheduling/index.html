Nameless Events Calendar
  • This forum and calendar is for reserving the Altered Planes
  • Your posts are the real reservation!
  • Only the altered planes are scheduled and posted to the calendar. Dragons are not reserved.
  • You must post your guild affiliation (or that you are unguilded) in your request
  • Reservations will be accepted and posted for 1 plane raid at a time; with a 2-day limitation for the Plane of Fear or the Plane of Hate. The exception to this is the Plane of Air which does not have day limitation.
    EXAMPLE:
    Plane of Fear for 1 or 2 days
    OR
    Plane of Hate for 1 or 2 days
    If you have a Fear raid scheduled then you can not book one for Hate until after the Fear raid is completed and vice versa.
  • Once your reservation is on the calendar the reservation is locked.
