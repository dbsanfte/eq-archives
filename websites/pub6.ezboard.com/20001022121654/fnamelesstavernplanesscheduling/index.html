Nameless Events Calendar
  • This forum and calendar is for reserving any of the Altered Planes on The Nameless Server.
  • Your posts are the real reservation!
  • Only the altered planes are scheduled and posted to the calendar. Dragons are not reserved. All other raids are informational only.
  • Reservations will be accepted and posted for 1 raid at a time with a 2-day limit for the Plane of Fear and Plane of Hate. Plane of Air at this time does not have day limitation.
  • Once your reservation is on the calendar the reservation is locked.
