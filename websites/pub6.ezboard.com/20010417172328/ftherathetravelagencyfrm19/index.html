The Epic Calendar is for those guilds/alliances that have cleared Isle 6,
and have agreed to be placed on this rotation.

Guilds/alliances that are unable to take down Inny, Maestro, CT, Draco, need to invite the Epic Guild for help.
Which Epic guild to invite in is NOT left up to the disgression of the current raider.
The guild who is up next according to the posts below needs to be the one invited.

In exchange for this service, the Epic guild/alliance agrees to loot only the epic item,
leaving the rest of the loot for the posting guild/alliance.

The current list of the Epic Calendar are:

BotS - Blood of the Spider

MFH - Emef` Aitch

DBN - Dark Breed of Norrath

FoR - Foundation Rathe

CIW - Clan Icewind

KEG - Knights of Eternal Good
