Minimum number of plane-able characters:
1) Minimum number of plane-able characters:
To make a reservation your guild/group/alliance must consist of a minimum of 40 characters of 46+.
2) Guests:
You may invite whoever you wish along on your raids, as long as no more than 6 come from the same posting entiry
3) To receive a password, submit:
Your organization Name
All guilds in this organization with approximate number of Planable toons
The raid leaders names
Webpage and email and/or message board
4) Reservations:
Each posting entity may hold one reservation to each plane at any time.
Fear and Hate reservations consist of a single day.
Sky raids consist of 2 consecutive days, with the exception of Sat/Sun.
The ownership (for lack of a better word) changes from one entity to another at 2 am Pacific Time. Your guild and all their guests are expected to be out of the plane by then.
