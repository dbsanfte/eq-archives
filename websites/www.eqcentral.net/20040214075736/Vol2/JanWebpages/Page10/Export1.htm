From the MQHA Executive Office:

The 30th AQHA World Championship Show was held November 8-22, 2003.  Missouri definitely made a showing.  Erin McGuire riding Perfect Soul Mate brought home the Gold in Amateur Trail and also the Bronze in Amateur Western Riding.  Dusty Mattern riding Saintly Rogue brought home the Bronze in Amateur Breakaway Roping.  Jay McLaughlin riding Cashew 107 owned by Julie Vestal-Gibbons brought home a Bronze trophy in Open Senior Working Cow Horse.  Many others from Missouri were in the top ten or finalists.  Congratulations to all MQHA qualifiers and participants.

The MQHA Annual Convention will be held January 23-25, 2004, at the Lodge of Four Seasons, Lake Ozark, MO.  It will be a fun filled weekend, including the stallion service
auction, a silent auction, youth horse races, the banquet and a dance.  More information is available on our web site at www.moqha.com

The Stallion Service Auction will be a live auction held in conjunction with the Annual Convention.  The auction will be held in Granada Room-A at 1:30 p.m. on Saturday, January 24, 2004.  Bids may be placed in person, by phone or proxy.  A list of donated stallions is available on the web site.  It is not too late to donate a breeding.
