Configuring Your PC

Computer Settings
You should have your PC set up for DHCP. Should you require an externally addressable IP address, please contact Dave or Saqib.

WindowsXP
1) Right click on "My Network Places" and select "Properties" in the menu.
2) Right click the connection that is active (usually called "Local Area Connection") and select properties. 3) Select "Internet Protocol (TCP/IP)" and click the "Properties" button.
4) Select the "Obtain an IP Address Automatically" button
5) Press the OK button
6) Press the OK button again

You may need to restart your computer for these setting to take effect.
