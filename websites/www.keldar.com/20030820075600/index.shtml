May 1, 2003

UPS Installed
The new UPS is now installed and should suffice for keeping the entire system up for a reasonable period of time during a power outage. Everything critical to the system remaining up is now on these new UPSes including the wireless amplifier. For those of you who are not on our physical power grid, you should still be able to surf if our area loses power. Sorry for any interruptions last night. Of course if you share the same physical power circuit as me, I can't help you unless you want to invest in a UPS for your systems as well. Email me for details :).

FAQ Page in the Works
We've really been getting a lot of requests lately for service (10 requests in the last 4 days). Some are close and some are fairly distant. Some of the questions involove the antenna needed to get service. I'm working on a "FAQ" page and hope to have that ready soon. This FAQ will cover such things as: "Can I get your service where I am," "What kind of antenna do I need," and "What kind of networking equipment do I need." With any luck, I can have the beginnings of a FAQ page up tomorrow. Hopefully this will help people to understand how we work :). However, we can't cover all the questions so keep the emails coming if you need help :).
