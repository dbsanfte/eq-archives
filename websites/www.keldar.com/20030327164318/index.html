January 25, 2003

New Developments
Things have been running very well as of late. Next week, WBS Wireless will be mentioned in an article in the Washington Post about internet service providers in Loudoun County, VA. Also, we're going to be doing some long distance testing with some folks over in Ashburn Village (about 1.5 miles). We're going to be establishing a long distance connection to offer service to several people. If you live in the central to western edge of Ashburn Village, it should soon be possible to get high-speed wireless internet service through WBS Wireless. Stay tuned for information about this new development.
