The Ring of Fire

We went into Acrylia Caverns tonight and attempted the Ring of Fire. Although we didn't complete it tonight we did learn a lot about the tactics and positioning while we were in there. We're going back tomorrow and I suspect we will have dramatically better results and (/em crosses fingers) may even complete the whole thing on only our second attempt.

-The first pic is me FD'd in the ring to start the cycle with everyone against the back wall.
-The second pic is the skellies that pop in the first wave.
-The third pic is the beginning of our second attempt. As you can see we tried moving into the edge of the hallway. This turned out to be a bit crowded and we had a couple of people get banished into the pit of lava.
-The fourth pic is a little hard to make out but if you look close you can see that there are at least 6 skellies in there with us and everyone is crowded into the entrance to the hallway.
-The last pic has a couple of dead skellies on the ground and 6 or 7 skellies mixed in with the crowd of people.


These next 2 pics are me just goofing around after everyone left. This is what it looks like when you trigger the RoF and you're the only one in there. I really wanted to get some screenshots of this room full of mobs but they wouldn't cooperate as you will see.

Basically, when you FD in the ring, the grimmlings spawn and then the first set of skellies spawn. When the skellies see that there is no one to attack, they stand there for a minute looking dumb and then poof and it's all over.
