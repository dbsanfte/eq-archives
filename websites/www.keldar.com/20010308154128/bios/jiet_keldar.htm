Jiet Kel'Dar


The true way to enlightenment in this day and age of dragons is not through deceit and the resort to shiny & flashy material equipment, but through hard work & dedication		 Name: Jiet Kel'Dar

Race:  Human

Class: Monk

Info:  I was born in the hills of Blackburrow, where I soon came under the strict and difficult training with the monks of the Silent Fist Clan.  Upon learning as much as I could in Qeynos, I set out on an adventure.  This was to further continue my training to become a master of combat, in both hand to hand and also with weapons.  I continue to this day, wandering the lands of Norrath, facing new challenges to improve my skills, and add to the many experiences I have encountered. 

True wealth is not in the value of your personal items but in the knowledge that you hold in your head.
