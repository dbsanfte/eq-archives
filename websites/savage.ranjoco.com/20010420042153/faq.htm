The Savage Gang FAQ

(Tell me my spelling and grammar mistakes!)

The Basement is the home of The Savage Gang. Only the Elite have access to this sacred shrine dedicated to us. Members of this elite organization gather every weekend for various activities such as cornering the real estate market, destroying several civilizations and rebuilding it in our image, with enough time left to get exotic Chinese, Italian, or Japanese food.

The good activities include

  • Ranma 1/2 marathons (See Jackson's Homepage)

  • Magical duels of epic proportions

  • Philosophical debates over chaos and order

  • Strategic annihilation of opposing forces and their dwellings

  • Gorge ourselves in an orgy of culinary delights

The activities to avoid

  • Being in the bottom of a pyramid scheme

  • Bad alcohol (Especially bad beer)

  • Burning your CPU motherboard due to wrong wire connection

  • Having nothing to do

Where did the name come from?
It came from this old amiga game called "SAVAGE" made by Firebird. It's a crude "Golden Axe" type of horizontal platform game. Jackson is currently experimenting with playing on his PC using an emulator.

When was the group formed?
Exact time of the formation is hard to pin point. But it just happened. We all sort of met via BBS community and we started to play games together at one of the many BBS parties. The very first game that most of us remember playing, was Advanced Marvel Super Heros RPG.

What other games do you guys play?
Close to the order of appearance: MSH, Shadowrun, Boulderdash, Shogun, Axis & Allies, Mah Jong, Advanced Civilization, Magic the Gathering, Monopoly, Hacker, Nuclear War, History of the World, Politika, Honors of the Samurai, Age of Renaissance, Cosmic Encounter, and many other games that we only play a few times and games on Super Nintendo, PSX, and PCs.
