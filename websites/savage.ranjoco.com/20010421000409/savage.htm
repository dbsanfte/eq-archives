THE Charter & Rules:

 FOR THE COMMON GOOD/EVIL:

A member of SAVAGE will acknowledge the fact that they always work for the common goal. Hereby defined as the majority of ONE (or the self) and the WHOLE (or the guild). Personal pursuits and freedom is encouraged, but try not to let it interfere with the upholding of the goal of the guild or its members.  Always be lawful within the guild, while feel free to be chaotic, neutral, or lawful outside.

WITH TRUTH/LIES AND HONOUR/DISHONOUR:

These basic virtues are fundamental(or not) to anyone. Our actions should reflect this. We will always act honourable to our fellow members, but you're not bound to do so with others for that is what separates us from the other guilds. Truth is what binds us together. A member of the SAVAGE should act and speak truthfully within the guild.

TO DEFEND AND PROTECT:

We are "SAVAGE". It is our duty to defend and protect those that are in need or cannot defend themselves in the guild. We are NOT judges by divine charter, nor should we act as marshals of law. But, a member will act swiftly to aid or assist those in danger in any way they can.  For outsiders, we shall treat them the same as it will benefit our guild in the long run.

BY WISDOM AND COMPASSION:

Although the ends sometimes do justify the means, that which defines Good, is the ability to act with wisdom and compassion. These traits should be common for all true members. These are the pillars, on which we build and grow. We, of SAVAGE, will bear that in mind, always.  Even while in pursuit of personal goals.  

WE ARE ONE AND ONE IS ALL:

No one members actions are ever more important than the whole of SAVAGE. We live and die by the actions of our fellows. The chain on which we base our trust is only as strong as the weakest link. Always help a member and expect no less from them. Evil (here define as OTHER who's against us) is great and has many faces. Only together can we prevail. Such is. and final rule, and on this we pledge.

 RULES OF CONDUCT:

 General:

All conduct should reflect the rules. All members will act according to these fundamental virtues.

In-game specifics:

We will not define any specific rules of conduct for members of SAVAGE. We expect that a belief rooted in the rules should defer any dishonourable behaviour (to the fellow MEMBERS). But also, treat other fellow players, as you would want them to treat you.
