Joani's Existential Corner



Introduction

Who Am I?: The disgruntled generation of neuroticism, pessimism, and occasional psychoticism.
My Favorites: Eeyore, Marvin the Martian, The X-files, Photography, and spending time with my fiance, Jackson ;)
My Philosophy: Life sucks, but I can get by with a little positive pessimism. It goes like this..."things will probably end up sucking, but at least it's predictable." (The truth is, things usually don't suck, but lower expectations are easier to beat. :)
The Existential Piece: Life is meaningless. So, in order to create the illusion of happiness, I must create my own meaning. That's the hard part, but I start by realizing that I'm free to choose my attitude in any given situation. It makes an innately meaningless life a little more tolerable.
Music: Currently, I really like Third Eye Blind and Blink 182. Then there are those old favorites...NIN, Tori Amos, Nirvana, Depeche Mode, Disco, and Funk of the 70's.


My Other Half

This is my fiance, Jackson, posing with our cat, Mau. We've been together since '94. We met in our coed fraternity, Alpha Phi Omega. After much stalking, he finally won me over. We have fun watching cartoons such as The Simpsons, Ranma 1/2, and Southpark. We occasionally do those mushy, romantic-type things, like walking in the park, in exchange for me playing Gran Tourismo with him on his Sony Playstation.

The Real Eeyore

I really like animals because they seem to be so loving, accepting, and approachable, (unlike many people). Visiting Eeyore at Maymont Park in Richmond, Virginia is another way that I spend my days. Eeyore seems to enjoy being fed by those passing by and scratching his hind parts on the fence. Oh, the serenity! ;)
