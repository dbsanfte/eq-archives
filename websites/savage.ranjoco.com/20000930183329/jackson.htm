Console Games

We all knows that there are tons  of good Playstation games out there.  And with that, tons of webpages out there for many of the domestic psx games.  As imported Japanese games starts to spread thru out the state, I thought maybe I should put up a Uncommon Imported Playstion Games page.   Well,  so far, I'm only going to put up the games that I've seen myself as well as uncommon games.  The goal is to generate enough interest to some of the games that may've been overlooked because they are not put out by Squaresoft.  Most of the infomation are from the offical Sony Playstation softwares site in Japan.  That's the best place to go, if you know Japanese.  I do not know ANY Japanese, so please correct any mistakes that I may have made.
