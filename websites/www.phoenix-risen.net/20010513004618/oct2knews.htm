NEWS FOR OCTOBER 2000:

Guild Website Opens!

Phoenix Risen's web site is now open; welcome home to all Clan Phoenix members!  The news, roster, calendar, and gallery pages will be updated as needed.  Our message boards are now located under the "Forum" area.   A "Members Only" section will be added shortly; to access this area of the message boards, you will need to ask one of the Counsel members for the password.  As this is a private area where we discuss internal Phoenix Risen matters, members are asked not to share it with non-PR players.

 

Phoenix Risen Joins 4 Corners Alliance

Phoenix Risen is now a proud member of the 4 Corners Alliance.  Please be sure to give a warm welcome to our fellow alliance members: Wrath of Power, Order of the Illuminati, and Band of the Broken Bow.  A "4 Corners" link has been added below that will take you directly to the 4 Corners page and any 4 Corners events will be listed on the Phoenix Risen calendar.

 

Guild Hunts & Events

We are looking for suggestions for guild hunts and events!  If you have a specific place you would like to schedule a hunt for, please submit it directly to Sirenia, the guild Events Master.  Sirenia will see that it is placed into our calendar and that proper notice is given to members.
