Guild Merger Successful!

On Saturday, January 13, Phoenix Risen and The Order of Illuminati joined forces in a ceremony that was held at the Lake Rathe Arena.  Many new members were welcomed into the ranks, including some new officers whose titles will be announced here shortly.

The Order of Illuminati has a long-standing reputation for being one of the most honorable guilds on the Tribunal server and Phoenix is proud that the would choose to bring their collective talents and strengths to us.  Please extend a warm welcome to all of the new faces you see over the guild channel!

 

Temporary Recruiting Freeze and New Recruiting Requirements

Due to the sudden increase in membership, a temporary recruiting freeze has been enacted.  In addition, some new recruitment requirements have been put into place for new members only.  This freeze does not affect 2nd generation characters for current members.

PR will continue to bring in additional Illuminati members over the next two weeks, therefore, we will be unable to determine our current membership size until everyone who will be joining us has completed the change over.  Once we have determined what our current population is and our new members have had a chance to settle in, we will reopen recruiting following the new guidelines set up by our Guild Leader, Sir Lage Holyblaze, and the Executive Staff.

The new requirements for recruiting are posted on our Recruitment page; you can read them by clicking here.  Please note that ALL recruitment issues MUST go through Sir Mikimoto Haruhiko, our Recruitment Counselor.

 

Member Roster Begins Anew

With our new size and stature, Phoenix Risen is beginning an official count of members and levels.  This information is needed for a variety of reasons, including raid and event scheduling.  Without this information, we do not know our population size nor how many Planes-ready members we have.  Please note that our Member Roster page is down until we receive updates from all members.

The information needed is as follows:

  •  Full Name - first, last, any GM-awarded designations

  • Class

  • Level

  • If you have more than one character, please include all character information in  your update; please denote which is your main and which are your 2nd gens

The sooner this information is received from everyone, the sooner we can update our roster.  Please send all bios to our Web Master, Sir Drozz Z'Cannonz by clicking here.

 

Another Member Joins PR's "Fast Feet" Club!

Yet another member of Phoenix Risen has earned a pair of JBoots!  Rthiel, with the help of members Nuela, Leafwind, Fahad, Sir Drozz, Lady Sirenia, Yensil, Killiari, Brainy, and Adaiz, gathered the components needed and received his boots from Hasten.  In addition, Killiari killed the Ancient Cyclops and will soon be receiving her boots as well.

Any members who need JBoots, please see our resident JBoots experts Sir Drozz and Lady Sirenia to set up a time to complete the quest.

 

PR Victorious Over Efreeti Lord!

On January 5, our Guild Leader himself, Sir Lage Holyblaze, led a daring raid against the Efreeti Lord.  Several groups, including Yensil, Bildiben, Zeruel, Sir Bkaz Hunt'Master, and many more Phoenix members, decsended into Sol B to face down the EL.  He was successfully taken three times with new Golden Efreeti Boots going to Bildiben, Sir Swampfoxy Shadowalker, and Sir Raza Thegreat.  Congratulations to everyone who took part and to our three new GEB owners!

 

New Officer Roles

Lady Jeronath Mystfire has joined the ranks of Phoenix Risen's Executive staff and will be taking over the role of Enchanter Counselor.  Lady Ro, as she is better known, is a long-standing member of PR and is one of the most knowledgeable Enchanters in our guild, if not on the Tribunal server.  All Enchanters are encouraged to bring questions, concerns, and needs to her.  Congratulations Lady Ro!

With Lady Ro taking over the role of Enchanter Counselor, Sir Swampfoxy Shadowalker will now be Phoenix Risen's Guild Master Jeweler.  Sir Swampfoxy has by far the best knowledge of gems, jewelcrafting, and jewelry combinations; in recognition of his outstanding talents and abilities, he has been asked to be the guild's authority on jewelcrafting.  He will also remain as the Designated Guild Bachelor.  Congratulations Sir Swampfoxy!
