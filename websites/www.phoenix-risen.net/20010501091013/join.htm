Phoenix Risen is founded on the principles of friendship, fellowship, and honor above all else.  We do not have a limit on membership, however, we seek only those who would live by the defining principles we have set forth.

    If you truly feel that you can abide by PR's code of honor, we will welcome you with open arms.  If you cannot uphold any part of the code, PR may not be the right guild for you.  We are but one of many honorable guilds across Norrath; if our code does not fit your needs, another guild's may.

   Please note that our application process has been changed.  If you have any questions about our application procedure, please contact Sir Mikimoto Haruhiko, our Recruitment Counselor for more information by clicking here.  ALL recruitment issues MUST go through Sir Mikimoto.

 

JOINING PHOENIX RISEN:

  1. Nominees should thoroughly read the guild Charter so that they may better understand our rules and regulations.  We expect our members to abide by and defend these rules of conduct without exception.

  2. In order to join Phoenix Risen, characters must be at least level 14.

  3. Prospective members must be sponsored in by a current member or officer; if the nominating member is an officer, that officer will be the only sponsor required.  If the nominating member is not an officer, a second sponsor is required and must be a member of the Executive Staff.

  4. Sponsors, both officers and regular guild members, must be in good standing at the time of nomination.

  5. The sponsor(s) should spend time hunting with the nominee when possible.  Although not required, members are encouraged to bring their nominees to guild hunts.  A current schedule of events is located on our "Calendar" page.

  6. Once the nominee has fulfilled all of the above requirements, he or she MUST attend Phoenix Risen's next regularly scheduled guild meeting in order to be invited.  Exceptions to this rule will be made only for reasons of hardship and will be made on a case-by-case basis.

  7. Please note: we accept all races and classes in Phoenix Risen (including Dark Elves, Ogres, Necromancers, and Shadow Knights ), however, we do not allow evil role-playing.  If you prefer to role-play an evil character, it is highly likely that you will not be invited to join us.
