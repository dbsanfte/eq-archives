Welcome aboard !
This site is about Massively Multiplayers Online Role Playing Games (Everquest, Anarchy Online, DArk Age of Camelot etc. etc.)

First versions of this site were dedicated to a unique Everquest Guild (Barbarians Helping Hands), but with time we now are also hosting boards, albums and various tools for other guilds from the same server (Zebuxoruk).

Some of the hosted guilds are allied in game, anyway if you are also living on Zeb server, do not hesitate to join us on our channel gameshell while you are in game (/join gameshell or /autojoin gameshell). Allways better when we have friends to talk or group with

To have a full access to the site, just register, it's free and will let you access to the restricted parts of the site as the photo albums etc.

Have fun,
Meet ya in game maybe (tof & steve, webmasters)

Guild Recruitment
Barbarians Helping Hand Recruitment:

You must first register on the website to gain access to all the modules (message boards, screenshots, calendar, etc.) Registration is free, and can be cancelled at any time.

Then be sure to read the Guild Charter and the guild Code of Conduct. If you think our guild is what you are looking for, please post a request for membership HERE.

Once you've posted a request to join, our officers will look you up in the game and give you an invite at the earliest opportunity. Enjoy!
