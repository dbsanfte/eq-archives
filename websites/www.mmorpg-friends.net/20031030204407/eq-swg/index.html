Welcome !
Hello there,

Welcome on MMORPG-Friends! This site history is now a long one. So, to make it short i will just say that it's a place to share informations and discuss about MMORPG and various games.

The origin of the site is BHH, Barbarians Helping Hand, an Everquest guild of friends, playing on Zebuxoruk server. With time some members moved to other games, but the familly spirit is still there

Our members are now playing various games, Everquest, PlanetSide, Star Wars Galaxies, Dark Age of Camelot, Anarchy Online etc. But we often come here to discuss about games and life
If you want to join us, you are welcome, just register on the site to get full access.

We are playing Everquest on ZEBUXORUK and Star War Galaxies on Sunrunner. Most of us have their character names in the signature, you can see them on boards and in members profiles.

To join the Everquest Barbarians Helping Hand guild, you will have to register on the site, read the guild charter and Code of the Helping hand, and then post a request in the recruitment forum. First post in this forum will link to the mentioned documents.

Have fun, Tof.
