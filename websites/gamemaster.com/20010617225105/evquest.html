  General Reference & News Sites

AllaKhazam's Magical Realm
These folks really should be sponsored by Sony!  The site has it "all" including classifieds where one can buy/sell EQ items.

Dr. Twister
EQ's "unofficial" devil's advocate.

EQVault
Even the EQ Staff Members post news on their site. Voted #1 for their message boards.

EverQuest Stratics
A multitude of forums, databases for: spells, items and quests, also a references for: skills, weapons, armor, classes and attributes and maps (and more).

EQ "Official" Support Site
Brought to you by Sony.

EQ-Spoiler
Besides spoilers there are plenty of decent maps, quest info, key guides, and an EQ Guide & GM list.

EQ'Lizer
Better than average information on: skills, abilities, classes, equipment, quests, races, spells, and zones.  The Spawn Guides are way cool.

EverLore
Excellent searchable databases for their Beastiary, which allows searches alphabetically or by location.  Their Items database contains over 5000 listings and you can also search for Quest items in the Quest database as well.

GX Everquest
Basic Guides for: Races and Character Class. Also features detailed Early Leveling guides for: Freeport, Kelethin and Nariak.

PlanetEverQuest
Items, spells, maps, factions and more.

Maximum EQ
One stop shopping for: maps, items, quests, faction, forums and more.

Verant
The "official" website of Verant (the original creators of EQ).

 

  EQ Map Sites

EQ Atlas
Before going anywhere be sure to get a map from these folks and don't forget to use your [/LOC] command.

EQ Maps
As the name may suggest they are big on maps. They also have an items database, and you MUST be a member of  Flashlink to get access to their maps of: continents, Towns, World Zones, Dungeons and the various Planes.

Map Shop Norrath (Japanese)
These detailed maps are beautiful--true works of art! Quite a bit of information has also been translated into English.

 

  EQ Items & Equipment Sites

EQprice.com
Database of equipment with average prices. Price details and printable guides require a Flashlink subscription.

EQ Items
Searchable item database by: type, race, class, Armor Class/Damage via access to EQ'Lizer's item list.

EQLoot.com
A great item directory for just about everything and provides links to other site item databases (just in case).

EQ Weapons & Armor Database
Features a large Weapon and Armor database that can be searched by race/class. 

 

  Character Class Specific

The Casters Realm
Devoted to the spell casters of EQ. Their creation and strategy guides are a must read for the newbie caster.

EqCaster
From this site download a program that decodes the information from the EQ spell file, so that you can see all the attributes of each spell. In addition there is an excellent EQ Compendium (in html format) that lists detailed: Quest, NPC, Books and Map information...all VERY useful!

EQ Cleric
Has decent general and quest forums.

Everquest Monks
Multiple Forums about...monks (of course).

Graffe's Wizard Compilation
Great wizard class information on: items, quests, and spell research.

The Mage Compendium
Features: News, Message boards, Spells, Research, Pets, Focus Items, Equipment, Quests, Mana Calculator, and more.

Mythiran Tower
Spell Research forums and tutorials in addition to spell recipes for all caster classes.

Interealms.com
Plenty of forums and great guides on: Alchemy, Ranger, Shadowknight, Shaman and Warrior.

EQ Classes: Necro
Necromancer class specific info for: spells, equipment, research, and quests!

EQ Druids
Information on Druid: Character creation, battle tactics, quests, equipment, spells, and maps.

The Safehouse
Rogue forum and guide site.

ShadowKnight Class Site
Information on ShadowKnight: Combat, faction, quests, spells, and forums.

The Warriors Edge
Warrior: Creation, Equipment, Quests, Guides and Forums.

EQ Shadow Knights
Shadow Knight Forums and information about: Weapons, Armor, Spells, Skills, and Quests.

EQ Shaman
Shaman: Spells, Alchemy, Guides, Skills, Equipment, Quests and Message boards.

Illia's Everquest Bestiary
Beastie information (by zone as well) in addition to faction adjustments.

Seekers Of Lore
Even though many members of this guild no longer play EQ the site's library of: maps, quests, guides, beastiary and items can be useful.
