The Plane of Growth, Tunare's plane:

As you all know that the plane of growth was released upon Velious release. Before this  there were the planes of Hate and Fear. These planes were factionless hits and taught players on the ethics and style of a plane raid. Many great people like myself, Beld, Abad, Arith, Soulaw, Durron, Frieddman, even Sluggo, Isriam and many others of the old style on how to raid a plane and accept the reality of it. We did had a few whiners but at least we told em there was always a next time. During these time armor that was dropped there was very good. In fact it was the best of the best before Kunark was out and before Velious was released. Now Velious was released and it's being plauged by people who's expecting "phat lewt" and very inexperance at this raid. Also raiding Growth does come with a hugh minus.

As you all now I have been thought over 100's of planes and done so much CR to get people and thier bodies out of the planes. Now today PoG is being raided by people who skips the old school planes just to get loot. If you want loot and want to learn about the planes do fear and hate. The plane of hate is the first plane unless your armor isn't there than do fear. Hate is like easy street to break while Fear is tough love. At least you learned about breaks and the CR factor. With PoG you go in invisable and pray some named or your invisable don't break. Once your in than mobs at 52 and above will come to you. This plane was ment for people around those levels and/or with planar experance not some newbie who just go into the plane just to get loot. The planes are ment to be a risk factor not some please give me some phat lewt so I can tell the next experance person I'm better than you are.
 
My problem is that inexperance people doing planes. To me if your doing a raid its' best you do the plane of fear and hate. Once your very good at it and/or have your armor collect than move onto the tougher ones. I believe it's disrespect when some SK with non-fear gear coming in, whining for loot and rubbing it in when he wins it. To me that SK just lost respect with many other old school shadow knights like myself. If people can't assist, can't stay where they are, pull when we doing CR instead of holding the fort til CR is done, whines, and many more negative stuff than they shouldn't be here in PoG instead Fear or Hate. The Plane of Growth is ment for those who knows thier stuff and yes knows how to do a planar raid not for some armor grubbin, greedy, loot mongral, jackass, inexperance newbie.
 
Here is another thing about PoG I like. I am worshiping Innoruuk for my Shadow Knight. In fact I am a mortal enemy of Tunare. So naturally her believers invades Hate while Innoruuk's subjects invades Growth. With this there is a bunch of factional hits. With these hits the people of Tunare can do the quests that she and her loyal followers gives. It is natural for her followers to do these quests and get the armor. The problem is that some of the classes in the game following Tunare also have some mobs dropping thier stuff. The question for them remains should I kill a subject of Tunare just to get a piece of armor or quest for it. Well sadilly many high levels off these creatures just for a chance of armor. The sad part is that many of them taking the hits. Well four or so kills on a Tunarian creature your fate is sealed of not doing the quests. Verant did made a nice fix to the Cleric BP and many clerics of good aligned cleric now crying cuse of this. Well if Verant did made a quest for them to get it back it should be a long route but alas they are not. Instead these people have to suffer this choice of thier loot over thier god. When you make a choice you accept it's full responsiable of what to come. Like Growth once you do a night of it you got a long way to regain it back otherwise find another town to buy your spells.
 
Well in final Growth has factional hits, many inexperance people doing it and yes it's a fun place to get revenge on Tunare if your worshiping Innoruuk. Well that's it about my feel on Growth these days. So next time you want to do your first plane do fear and/or hate.

Dragon Raids

Normally I do not post about Dragon Raids in word from the Shadow Lord but I must get this out. Tonight my friend Arith tactic got botched up by a group of people who didn't wanna listen and take risk. When you go on a dragon raid your risking your life and yes that thing called the exp bar to go left. The problem with todays Nagafen and Vox raids are people not taking one for the team and whining about loot. I have been on over 50 dragon raids. I have seen good ones where loot wasn't an issue and I have seen it where it takes more than an hour to lotto off the loot.

When you do a Dragon raid prepare to die. When you do get invited to your first one by the Shadow Lord either you die with him or you get that dragon killed. Either way it's a fun experience and yes it's also an learning experience for future encounters more tougher than your average Old school dragons. As I said before Exp and Loot is just bonus when you go on a raid with me.

You'll make great friends and yes if he does return the favor it will be in your favor. If you do win a book from Vox or Naggy THAN you should get defaulted for the other book. My last rant about dragons is that when you kill a dragon your killing it for the glory of yourself. Yes I have killed many dragons myself for the glory of the LSH.
