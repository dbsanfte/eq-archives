Now I tried my damndest to make a Bowl.
    Thinking that that was a mixing bowl for baking.  which, by the way, I bought in N. Freeport eventually.
I tried everything.  Now if you read the book there are only 5 possible combinations of clay you can every possibly use:

  1. A store bought Small Block of Clay
  2. A store bought Block of Clay = book conversion A Small Block of Clay
  3. A store bought Large Block of Clay = book conversion A Block of Clay
  4. 4 store bought Small Block of Clay = book converson A Block of Clay
  5. 3 store bought Block of Clay = book conversoin A Large Block of Clay
If you look at the book for a S. Jar is says A Block of Clay - OK so you do the conversion and use a store bought Large Block of  Clay - EHH!! NOT!
OK then so you go the other route - 4 store bought Small Blocks of Clay - EHH!! NOT!
OK then you try what it really said in the first place - a store bought A Block of Clay - DING DING DING!
Now tell me what are the dumb conversions for if they are ignored!?!

Any way back to the bowl.  Now part of the problem may be that it was a bowl sketch and not a S. Bowl sketch.  But then the vendor also had M. and L. Bowl sketches so one can only assume plain old bowl is S. Bowl.  I tried EVERY one of the 5 combinations - EHH!! NOT! and at a cost of about 5 gold just to try to figure out the recipe.
