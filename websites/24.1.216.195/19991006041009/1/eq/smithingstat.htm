The best place for beginning smithing is N. Freeport.  Ore for bits is near 2 forges, and files are fairly close, just buy a bunch and keep them in the bank near by.
Once you are making banded either E. Freeport or Kelethin are good.  SOMs and molds are available with a forge in the same zone.  I think Freeport is a bit better since if the forge is busy in Kelethin you are SOL.  In Freeport there are 4 that I know of, I heard of a fifth but have not found it.
Another tidbit, make small items when you can, dark, wood and high elfs get weight benefits wearing the smalls instead of mediums. example: Banded Gorget AC 5 Wt 2 - Small Banded Gorget AC 5 Wt 1.5
You could even conceive of a trade in program - "Calling all elves, do you have Medium Banded?, I'll trade you for small for 5-10 gold and save you weight" - then sell the Mediums to Humans, Halfelfs, Erudites
