A few hints...
  • ALWAYS tailor with high inteligence.
  • Train your tailoring up to (21) at your guild OR make patchwork by killing hords of wolf, cat, bear. Save ALL MQ and HQ pelts for later use.
  • Also kill every (large, giant, carrion, crag) spider you see and combine the silks in a large Sewing Kit so they stack. Save them for later.
  • Once you trivial Patchwork (26), move on to raw silk and Studded.  If you don't want to bother with smith items, then stick to silk.
  • Once Raw silk trivials at (36) - don't waste silk making raw tunics and legs, move to Masks.  This is harder since you have to get Heady Kiolas.  Either Become a master brewer (2 hours and 20 plat) or find a reliable source.  You will need hundreds (3 or 4).
  • Since the hardest thing to aquire is the silk, keep advancing you skill by adding more Heady Kiolas as things get trivial.
  • Heady Kiolas can be made in 2 zones easily (N Freeport and Kelethin)
  • Spiders can be found in quantity and are easy to hunt
    • E Commons - Large and Giant
    • W Commons - Giant
    • E Karana - Carrion and Crag
    • S Ro -
  • Spiders can also be found in but tend to be in small supply or hard to hunt
    • Kithicor - Giant
    • Misty Thicket - Giant
    • GUK - Hart
    • L Feydark - Large
