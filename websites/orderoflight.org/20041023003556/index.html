Welcome to The Order of Light Headquarters!

The Order of Light originated from a group of friends who have been gaming together for a good deal of time. This group has been a part of many different types of organizations. When news of the World of Warcraft broke out, it was without a doubt on the top of the list for new games to try. So here we are, the newly formed Order of Light.

Because we have been gaming together for a while we have come to understand that gaming should be based on a few simple philosophies:

Respect- Above all else we honor the value of Respect because it is a key component to all other aspects of gameplay. Without respect you cannot hope to be a part of a team. Without respect there is no trust, cooperation or fun.

Honor- Directly related to how we act as a group in the World of Warcraft. How we display ourselves to the rest of the server is of the utmost importance. Our members are models of excellence and honorable gameplay. We strive to be the ones that people can rely on for help or advice, as well as promoting fair gameplay.

Discipline- Because no guild can survive and hope to achieve its goal in a state of chaos, The Order adheres to a strict disciplinary code. Our members respect this code and understand that through this we distinguish ourselves as a unique guild who uphold their principles.

Friendship- No game is truly a game without your friends. The Order is there to help promote new friendships and the improved cooperation between these friends to further enhance their gameplay experiences,

The Order of Light is continually looking to improve itself with the addition of new members. If you are interested we would love to have a chance to answer any questions you have. We have a section in our forums for these questions under Recruitment. This is where the initial steps to join are as well.

Thank you for visiting our Guild!
-The Council of Light-
