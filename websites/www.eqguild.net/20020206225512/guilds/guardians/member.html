Our Members

A full list of members is available at: Guardians of Truth Guild Roster

We are using www.guildmagic.com to keep track of our members. You are responsible for signing up for the guild and keeping your character information current. Since several people have had problems signing on and creating accounts, I have created the following step-by-step guide to the process:

1. Go to http://www.guildmagic.com/create_user.cfm

2. Create your private account by entering a username, password and timezone. You can use this account anytime to update your character skills, level, etc.

3. You will be taken to a login screen where you need to log in with the usename and password you just created.

4. Go to add guilds to my user and select our guild

5. Create a new character. You can also create alternative characters that are in the guild.

6. Once Crohan approves the character application, your character will be listed in the guild. Please don't panic if you don't see your character on the roster immediately.

7. If you have any further questions about the guildmagic system, please read their FAQ at: http://www.guildmagic.com/faq.cfm

8. Please keep your character information updated regularly.

9. If you are having problems logging in or creating characters, read the statement below. If you have other questions that are not answered in the Guild magic FAQ above, please email iluvia at iluvia_got@hotmail.com for help.

Please be aware that guildmagic is experiencing some ISP problems right now. You may have difficulties in creating or updating your character or creating an account until the problems are fixed. If you can not create or update your character, please try using Netscape to do it. I don't know why, but it works better for some people. Please give it a try and don't give up. If the network is busy please try to log on at an off-peak time and try again. Thank you for your patience.
