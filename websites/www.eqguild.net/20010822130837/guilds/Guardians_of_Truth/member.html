Our Members

A full list of members is available at: Guardians of Truth Guild Roster

We are using www.guildmagic.com to keep track of our members. You are responsible for signing up for the guild and keeping your character information current. Please go to www.guildmagic.com and create an account and enter your character information.

Note: When you create an account for the first time, please associate our guild with your account first BEFORE you create your character. Otherwise you're going to have to recreate it again when you add the guild. After you create your character, Crohan will have to approve it before it shows up on the guild list, so don't panic if you don't see it immediately.

Please be aware that guildmagic is experiencing some ISP problems right now. You may have difficulties in creating or updating your character until the problems are fixed. I have been notified by them that some people have had better luck using Netscape to update their information. Please give it a try and don't give up. If the network is busy please try to log on at an off-peak time and try again. Thank you for your patience.
