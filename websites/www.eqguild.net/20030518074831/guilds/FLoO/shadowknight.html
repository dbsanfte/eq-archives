Innorruk's Curse

The Shadowknight's Epic weapon has been argued to be the most difficult to obtain (though some Necro's might take issue with that!). It is a long drawn out quest that involves many subquests and journeys to several unpleasant areas that require powerful friends. Basically all you have to do is:

 

Give Kurren Ni (in the Overthere) some Darkforge Armor, then kill him;

 

Retrieve the Dusty Tome from the Hole

 

Hand in a Ghoulbane (subquest), Decrepit Sheath (kill Drakes in the Plane of Hate), Soul Leech (kill Cazic Thule), and a Blade of Abrogation (Plane of Air) to Derek Bloodpool;

 

Free and then Kill Kyrenna and Caradon in the Hole

 

Free (and kill) the Ghost of Glohner in the Hole

 

Turn stuff in to and subsequently kill Lhranc in the City of Mist

 

For more information, visit CastersRealm for more detail.
