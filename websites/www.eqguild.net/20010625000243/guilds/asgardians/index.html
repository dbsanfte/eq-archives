Jaxel's Induction:

We have another new member.  Lets all welcome Jaxel into our family.  There was an induction service for him which was quite fun =)  I'm putting up the text log and screen shot from that =)

Here is a small snippet from the text log.  Click here to read the full log.

Tymme holds the chalice to the heavens and faces North.
Jaxel says, 'I shall be sure to walk lower when in the presence of the king  '
Tymme says, 'May the strength of the north winds fill this chalice.'
Tymme turns and faces south.
Soljourn tells the guild, 'I shall only ask for silence as Tymme does a wonderful presentation here as an induction thank you all )'
Tymme says, 'May the winds of the south fill this chalice with the warmth needed to fill our hearts.'
Tymme face the west
Tymme says, 'May the colors of the sunset fill the chalice with all of its colors and joy.'
Tymme turns and faces East.
Tymme says, 'Now I face to the east to se the birth of a new day. All of this in welcoming our new member Jaxel.'
Tymme says, 'Jaxel You have been told of the things that your are expected to do in this family do you agree to share in the joy and camaraderie of this family?'
Jaxel says, 'I do agree to share in the joy and camaraderie of this family'
Tymme says, 'Then take the chalice from me and drink to the happiness of joining our wonderful family.'
Jaxel has joined your guild.

Welcome Jaxel! =)
