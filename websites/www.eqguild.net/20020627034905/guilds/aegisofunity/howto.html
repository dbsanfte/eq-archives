How to create your character profiles

Introduction

We have three different systems that you should use to update your character information:

  • AoU Web Site Roster
  • Guild Magic
  • Magelo

Each of these systems tracks different information. Please take the time to complete this information and keep it up to date.

 

Step by step Instructions

The following are the steps that you should follow. The order they are listed in is important as you'll be linking this information together.

  1. Create your Magelo Profile. This profile is a complete inventory list of all of your equipment.
  2. Create your Guild Magic Profile.
  3. Complete the AoU Roster update form.
  4. Go play Everquest, ding & get more loot, update your profiles, rinse, repeat!

AoU Web Site Roster

Our web site roster is a list of all of our members broken out by class. Using this list you should be able to get an overview of who is in the guild, what classes we have, and our levels.

To make update to this page, please use our update form.

Magelo

This profile maintains all of your equipment.

  1. Point your browser to www.magelo.com.
  2. Click the Register button on the top of the home
    page and fill in your information. You will need to
    use a valid e-mail address as their registration
    confirmation uses e-mail.
  3. After confirmed, log into the site using the Login
    button.
  4. On the left nav, under "EQ Expansion", click
    "Characters"
  5. You'll start by adding a character. Put in all the
    information you can. It'll be worth it when you see
    your profile. What you'll need are your
    Race/Class/Diety/Level, Starting Stats (Str/Wis/Int,
    etc), AC and ATK when unbuffed and naked (yea, you'll
    need to kill yourself to get this .. so duel
    somebody).
  6. You should now get a screen that has several
    options on it.
    • Update this Character ... use this to change your
      initial information
    • Manage his profile ... where you'll change your
      equipment (more on this later)
    • Manage his keyring ... what keys do you have
    • Manage his inventory ... used to manage extra gear you
      have (not what you are wearing)
    • Manage his abilities ... AA points
    • Manage his trade skills ... tradeskill levels
  7. Choose the "Manage his Profile" option.
  8. Okay, we are now ready to figure out what equipment
    you have. NOTE: If you have already added some gear,
    you need to use the "LOAD" button at the bottom to
    load a profile. I use "Standard Gear" as my default
    one. It is all the gear that I usually walk around in.
  9. Using the "Edit" links, modify all of the equipment
    locations.
  10. SAVE the profile! It's gone if you don't save it!
  11. SAVE SAVE SAVE.
  12. On the bottom of this page is a link. It will look
    like http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=90390.
    This is the link that you'll post to the AoU site.
    Click it and you can look at your newly created
    profile!
  13. Visit the other links from the Update Character
    page to change your other information. Always use the
    "UPDATE" button on those pages to save your changes!

Guild Magic

This profile is our primary roster list. We use this system to

  • Track all of our Members
  • Manage the guild bank
  • Bestow awards upon deserving guildies
  • See guild statistics such as level distribution andclass distrubition.

So, do the following to get your profile set up:

  1. Register yourself on the Guild Magic website. This process will create an account for you, NOT a character.
  2. You need to join the guild before you create your characters. Use the "add/remove guilds" button. This will bring up a list of servers. IMPORTANT: Select Torvonnilous and then add the Aegis of Unity guild to your user. Failure to do this means your characters will not show up in the guild roster!! When done, use the "back to my user" button at the bottom of the page to return.
  3. Now, create a character using the "create new character" button. The field "Character website" should contain the link to your Magelo Profile. Also, make sure that you select our guild!
  4. Optionally, use the "edit skills" and "edit languages" buttons to add more information about your character. If you do, use the "save changes" button to return to the new character screen.
  5. Press the "update character" button to save your changes.
  6. Congratulations, that's it! You can use the other buttons to add more information about your character such as skills, keys, hunting habits, and more. The guild administrator will be automatically contacted with your update and once approved your character will show up in the guild roster.

Make sure that you complete the AoU Roster form so that we link your profile into the main web site.
