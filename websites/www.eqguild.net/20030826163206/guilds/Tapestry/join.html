Interested in joining our Heroes?
All persons wishing to join Tapestry of Heroes MUST read our charter and agree with ALL of the policies contained in it.  If you do agree, then you have two (2) options for starting the process.
 
1)  Send a tell in game to one of our officers (or higher) indicating your desire to join the Heroes.

2)  Email Kortap with your request indicating your desire to join us.
