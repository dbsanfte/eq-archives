Femmes Fatalle was started by a group of friends who had a vision of something better in a guild than what they had previously found.

Our original intent was to have a guild for all female characters.   Well, that quickly went awry as more and more men wanted to be a part of what we are.  Husbands, sons, and close male friends also share our vision of what a guild should be.  Femmes Fatalle is still goverened by women, and we have no plans to change that, though we do have a few junior officers who are men.

We are rather picky about who we invite to be a part of our family.   Too many times have we seen guilds invite everyone and anyone just to have a large guild.  This is not for us.  We do not allow kill stealers, beggars, and other undersirables who would take the fun out of our game.

We are a fun and friendly guild and do our best to make all our members feel at home.  We are big believers in helping and mentoring our lower level players.  To those who have proven themselves a part of our family, we are happy to help with equipment aquisitions, camping mobs, epics, etc.

If you think this sounds too restrictive, then we are probably not the guild for you.  If, however, you would like to be a part of our family please send a tell to one of our members.   Join us on a raid, an xp group or let us get to know you in some way!
