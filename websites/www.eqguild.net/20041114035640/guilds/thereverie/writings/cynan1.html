She is the mother of the maelstrom.
The mother of life.
With immeasurable power in her hands
She drinks a thousand shipwrecks.
Yet, I would go back, Yes I would go back again.

She is Mystery. Able to destroy on a whim.
Countless ships lay in her belly,
A testament to her wrath.
Yet she can whisper to you across the waves of the ages.
I would go back, Yes I would go back again.

She beckons me daily awaiting my return
But I do not fear or tremble
For I know the Master of the sea
I have seen the caress of his hand assuage her fury once again.
I will go back, Yes I will go back again.
