The Fiery Defender

 

The Paladin’s Epic, the Fiery Defender, is a very long and complicated quest. Details on the quest and related sub-quests can be found at CastersRealm. Suffice it to say that while gaining your Ghoulbane, Soulfire, and Fiery Avenger in preparation for finally receiving your Fiery Defender, you will need to kill the following mobs:

 

Sir Lucan – West Freeport

Xicotl – Castle Mistmoore

Your choice of Nagafen, Faydedar, Talendor, or Severilous for the torn, burnt book

Your choice of Vox, Faydedar, or Gorenoire for the Book of Frost

Miragul – Everfrost

Thought Destroyer – Plane of Hate

Keeper of the Tombs – The Hole

Kirak Vil – Nektulos Forest

 

And then you get to do two turn ins in the Plane of Fear!
