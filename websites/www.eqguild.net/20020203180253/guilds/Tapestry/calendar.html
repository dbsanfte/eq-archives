Welcome to the Events Calendar
I am attempting to set up calendar for events that we would like to do.  I would like to do some smaller raids in the near future, but my last few attempts at this have been marked with tons of no shows, and most people were not prepared.  So before I get to the calendar, let me make a few strong suggestions on raid planning.

First of all, if you are planning on participating in a raid, make sure you know who is heading up and organizing it, be it me or somebody else.  Contact them via email or in game ahead of time to find out exactly when the raid is, and where it is going to be.  Then log on with enough time to get there BEFORE the scheduled time.  Do NOT log on at the time and then spend an hour getting ported and/or walking to the location.  This has killed a few raids in the past, and I for one will no longer tolerate it.  If you are going to be late, I'd like to know ahead of time.  If enough people are going to be late, then maybe we need to push the start time back.

Secondly, anyone can set up a raid, as long as they let me know so I can post something here to let everyone know.  Also, we can raid for any reason.  If you want an item from a higher lvl dungeon, and can't do it alone or at all, let us know.  There are enough 30+ people in the guild to allow us to go into some higher dungeons to kill and loot!

Okay, that being said, here is the calendar.  If you want to plan a raid, fill out the form, and I'll post it here.  You can also look at the requests here .  The results are real time, this calendar is updated when I find the time.
