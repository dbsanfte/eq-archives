Officer Agreement (Beta release)

This is what every officer of Eternal Faith agrees to follow before becoming an officer. This is to encourage only those people who are truely committed to serving members of this guild and being a productive part of Eternal Faith to become officers.



  • I agree to try to work out problems that concern guild members to achieve a positive solution. If I can't work it out I will ask another officer for help.

  • I agree to discuss problems with other officers that I come across or think of.

  • I agree to listen to the concerns of members and discuss them.

  • I agree to suggest guild improvements I think of or hear about.

  • I agree to participate in guild chat when I can.

  • I agree to attend 3/4 of all meetings unless RL prevents me from attending, or if a serious in game problem happens I must take care of immediately.

  • I agree to visit the guild message board at least every 2 days unless I am not online in any way.

  • I agree to encourage guild members to participate in guild events, participate in guild chat, help with problems members need help with, and visit the website and message board.

  • I agree to follow and know all rules.

  • I agree to not bypass procedures and to not abuse my powers as an officer.
