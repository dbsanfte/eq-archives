Tips

This is more a warning than a tip... BE CAREFUL who you trust. I have heard many stories of people that let someone "hold" something for them while they switched characters only to find that their "friend" ran off with their weapon, or people (myself included) who bought some wonderful item for a ridiculous amount of money only to find out it was NOT what promised, or that they could not equip it! ALWAYS ask someone you know to hold items, or ask a guild member. IF a guild member ever pulls a stunt like that they won't be a member long! When buying always left click on the item before paying and makes sure it's what you were promised and that you can equip it, remember to check both class AND race!

Some helpful commands

/corpse - will "summon" your corpse if within 50 feet of you. This is helpful when you need to loot your corpse somewhere safer or you are stuck in a strange place!

/consent - If you are unable to get to your corpse or get close enough to drag it, ask someone you TRUST (see tips/warning above) to drag it to a safer spot for you. Type in /consent tweehugger and tweehugger can drag it to the designated spot (in the same zone). The person given consent can then use the /corpse command (above) to drag it.

/yell or /y - yells for help, tells any in the area where you are in relation to them, EX. Tweehuger yells for help from off to the left of you. Use this, and NOT shout when you need help. Shout will do no good, noone will know where to go to help you.

/gems - if you are real bored, use this command to play a little tetris-type game!
