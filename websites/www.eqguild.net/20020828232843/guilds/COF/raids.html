RAID ON CAZIC THULE

Date: Sunday, December 23
Time:  4pm EST
Meeting Place: Feerrott/CT Zone
War Leader: Shoez 
Level Needed: 30
MOB Areas:  Court Yard Inner CT

MAPS:
Thanks to Vespis for all of the CT info and links!

Lost Temple of Cazic-Thule (Upper Levels):

1)      Avatar Pyramid- Silvered Guards (25-26) who drop Silver Wand, and Crusaders (33), middle level is water-filled.

2)      Archon Pyramid - top room spawns Tae Ew Archon (34) which drops Lizardscale Belt (common) and Ravenscale Armguards (rare)

3)      "Throne Room" or "TR"

4)      "Courtyard" or "CY"

 

Lost Temple of Cazoc-Thule (Lower Levels):

1)      Avatar Pyramid - lower level spawns Avatar of Fear (38 Shadowknight) which drops Bladed Thulian Claw (common) and Ravenscale Chestguard (rare), and Cazic Cenobite (35 cleric) who drops Lizard Blood Potion (common) and Ravenscale Boots (rare)

2)      "The Ponds"

3)      "Gator Pit" - many powerful Alligators (20-34) and Steel Golem (35-37 similar to SG) who spawns underwater and drops Golem Metal Wand (common) and Ravenscale Gloves (rare) and Lizard Champion (35) who drops Ravenscale Gloves (Rare)

 

 

Cazic-Thule Courtyard:

1)      Throne Room" or "TR" - spawns Tae Ew Templar (30) who drops Lizardscale Cloak (common) and Ravenscale Legs (rare) and Tae Ew Diviner (28 Enchanter) who drops Lizardscale Mantle (common) and Ravenscale Bracer (rare)

2)      Throne Room upstairs - spawns Stone Golem (30) who drops Potion of Stone Skin (common) and Ravenscale Coif (rare)

3)      Tunnel goes to second (middle) level of Avatar Pyramid

4)      Tunnel goes to first (top) level of Avatar Pyramid

5)      Water tunnel drops down to the Ponds below

6)      "Courtyard" or "CY"

7)      To the Maze

8)      Water tunnel drops down to the Gator Pit below

   

 


Cazic Thule Maze:

1)      Wizard Pyramid - port here with Wizard spell or Lizard Blood Potion



Clay Golem (30) spawns in the Maze somewhere and drops Ball of Golem Clay (common) and Lizardskin Tribal Mask (rare), also Radiant (32) spawns here and drops Glowing Gloves for Enchanter quest

In Southeast corner at the pyramid spawns the Lizard Ritualist (30-31 and pet), who drops Grimy Lance (? common) and Decaying Armor (? rarity)
