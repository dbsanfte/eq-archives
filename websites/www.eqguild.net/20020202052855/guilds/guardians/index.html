General Guild Policies
Please be aware of our guild rules. They are as follows:

1. All new members must have attained at least level 8 to be considered for membership in the guild.

2. All new members (and old members who haven't done so yet) must register with our forum and with our member list on guild magic. Guild officers will record the name of each new recruit and will enforce this policy.

3. Using Anonymous is not allowed. Anonymous is considered hiding your guild for ill behaviour and hides your existence from the guild roster as well. Please use roleplay if you want to go anonymous.

4. Do not beg for Money, Equipment, Power Leveling or ask to be twinked. Power Leveling, begging, and rude behavior are not allowed or approved by Guardians of Truth, and will result in intervention by the officers (i.e. you WILL be kicked out). There is nothing wrong with making reasonable requests (a TP, CR or Rezz) but if no one can, then accept they can't at that time. If no one responds to your request do not beg or spam the guild chat channel.

5. If someone in the guild gives you an item or items, DO NOT sell them. If you are no longer going to use them, offer them to a low level character in the guild at no charge.

6. Respect the guild members, we are all here to enjoy our time on the server. I'm sure we would all love to help you with every request, but if the guild is busy, stop asking for the day.

7. Be polite and sociable with your guild mates. If a guild mate makes a reasonable request, and you can help them, then do so.
.
8. Do not try to rip off your guild, If you get an item that is worth 300-500p offer it up to the guild for 300p or maybe even 200p.

9. If you are looking to accomplish something you can't do yourself, please go to the forum and post a request or talk to an officer in-game. The guild will try to organize some help for you.

10. Any in-game problems or disputes will be decided by officers. Every officer has the power to remove players from the guild and will do so without hesitation for breaking the rules.

11. All officers will anounce their arrival on line, as has been standard for some time already.

12. Officers will hold a meeting at the end of each month, on the last Sunday 12.00 PST on the chat, to discuss any and all Guild related issues.

And finally and most importantly,
13. Have fun, it's just a game. Remember that and don't stress.

Please remember to register your character with the guild and member forum

To register with the guild and maintain your character information, please go to our members page. There are full instructions there. It is vital to keep a current roster of our guild members.

Please also go to our forum to sign up and check back regularly for news. It's where all the imporant decisions are taking place!

Descent into the Hole

Over the past two weeks, our guild has had two adventures in the deep, dank dungeon known as The Hole. The first trip took place on January 5th and involved Goragg, Saeaddilzz, Milrsok, Enlil, Keldora, Featherie, Zzaxe and Irezz who succesfully camped the entrance and pulled rock golems from deep inside. During the evening they obtained a pair of Loam Encrusted Lined Shoes and many Elemental Binders (magician only item).

The second trip took place on January 19th and included Wien, Akaver, Iluvia, Nerlo, Grinderwaldrf, Saeaddilzz, Milrsok, Quantum, Featherie and Daisy, and non-guild warrior. This time the entrance was unfortunately camped, so we wandered deeper into the Hole, past the first room and pulled from the tower. It was definitely challenging, with a rock golem and 3 elementals spawning together all at once. Luckily, we did have 3! Chanters with us. This dungeon definitely takes a toll on the caster classes because the mobs hit hard and there is no place to run. Although almost all casters died once throughout the evening, I think a good time and some experience was had by all. We got two two loam encrusted gorgets and a loam encrusted sash for our magic users out of the evening.

Here's hoping for more trips to this interesting but dangerous place!

A Watery Adventure

On January 4, an impromptu trip to Kedge Keep turned into a guild raid when Goragg asked for backup on guild chat.

For those of you who don't know about Kedge, it's a completely underwater zone off of Daugnor's Cauldron, populated by fish and mermaids of all types. It's quite difficult to fight there because of pathing issues and easy to get trained if you don't pull well. Fear doesn't work there because of these same pathing issues.

Our ranger Wien has been after a particular quest item in Kedge for the past 5 levels or so. It is a lock of golden hair from the Golden haired mermaid for a quest that results in a very cool bow. Nerlo, Grinders, Goragg, Akaver and I have been there once before, but alas, the Mermaid never graced us with her presence - only some nasty fish as PHs. We made plans to come back tonight and see if we could get her to spawn. However, it turns out that Goragg was discussing our plans on guild chat and by the time I logged in an entire guild raid was in progress!

We had two full groups going, and logistics for that many people are very difficult. However, Zzaxe took organization upon himself and formed two well-balanced groups. We all followed Wien inside and cleared the first room. Then we went to the second level and went into the mermaid room. Unfortunately, only fish were present. Then Goragg started pulling fish (all blue to me, level 44) and a gloomwater mermaid or two (level 38-45). However, the crowning achievement of the evening in terms of mobs killed were two level 49 fish named CauldronBoil and CauldronBubble!

I am happy to report that there was only one death during the three hours we were there, and that was because Goragg pulled not only a gloomwater mermaid but about 4 other fish. Grinders kindly resurrected Goragg however, so it wasn't that bad.

And most importantly, the golden haired mermaid finally spawned and graciously gave Wien a lock of her hair. So the evening was a success in several ways - Wien accomplished his quest, and we all had a very interesting experience in Kedge. Everyone fought together in an exemplary fashion, supporting each other, listening to the organizers, and most importantly, assisting Goragg no matter what.

-Iluvia

Lower Guk (undead side) revisited

On December 13, a group of 6 GOT members (Goragg 51 monk, Wien 46 Ranger, Akaver 43 Chanter, Keldora 43 Chanter, Zrax 47 Cleric and Irezz 40 Cleric) had a blast in LGuk undead side. The group was good. That is an understatement. The group was AMAZING! Can you imagine the support that 2 chanters and 2 Clerics can provide to a Ranger and a Monk! We owned the zone, 6 out of 8 camps I believe! we got few good items, and an extra mantle (9ac, 7wis) that we are saving to Griderwaldf in case he needs it! We stayed about 2.5 hours, Wien never crashed and most gained 2 yellow bubs, except Goragg, who is in a bad hell level. Thanks for all who joined, and I wish many more can organize similar outings and have fun.

Asterixx / Irezz

Visiting the Misty City
In a follow up to our Lower Guk adventures, 9 hardy adventurers from our guild visited the mysterious City of Mist on Sunday, Nov. 11th. It was an interesting but sometimes hair raising adventure due to 3-4 mob pulls. Sae helped nuke and provide extensive support and our thanks go out to him. Good experience was gained by everyone and we even got some ruined scale armor for the lower members of the guild.
