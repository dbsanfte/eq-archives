The Autumn's Twilight Charter

Good day to you friend. The following is a general outline of what I wish to accomplish with our Guild as well as the Charter and current member list. I plan on running this Guild to the best of my ability. I want our Guild to be known and respected throughout Norrath. I will always try to be readily available for my Guildmates and I'd ask that we all would do the same. As a Guild we shall eventually be going on raids, as well as possibly challenging some other Guilds to a war. It will be some time before we do these things because I wish for us to become a little more established first. I ask that you bear with me and if you have any suggestions don't be afraid to make them known. You shall find out that I am willing to work with everyone to make this Guild like our gaming family. The following is our Guild Charter.

We, The members of Autumn's Twilight, Wish to work together in achieving the following goals:

  • Firstly, We shall always work together to uphold all that is good in the lands of Norrath.
  • Secondly, We shall always be prepared to help those in need, around us.
  • Last, As we grow in number, intellect, strength, and wisdom, We shall be prepared to thwart all that is foul and evil throughout Norrath.

Cyriana Autumn - Guild Leader
