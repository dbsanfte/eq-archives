While All Initiates passed the vote and are now full members, a score of 3 or less indicates that person still needs to become more involved in the guild.

If you are one of these people, please let us get to know you better!  Send an email at circleforgotten@eqguild.net so we can put you on our Roster page, talk a bit more in Guild-say, sign onto our Forum and give your opinion, and join our Guild Events!

NOTE: Folks with 3 or less rating may be removed from the guild at the next guild meeting unless we see some more activity from them. It occurs to me that a few of them may have removed themselves from the guild already.

Thanks to all of those who voted!  Thanks to Ellory for the compilation!
