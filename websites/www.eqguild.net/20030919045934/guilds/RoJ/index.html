GUILD NEWS

Events are coming up in the near future, so please register for the events on the member's webpage here.

This Saturday(5/4/02) we will be going to Maiden's Eye. This event will be limited to ONE group only. There are some excellent drops in this zone, but it is quite dangerous as well. Target will be to gather some of those elusive high level druid spells to help out the many members who could benefit from them. An e-mail has been sent out to many members with the pertinent information and links; and, the event is posted on the Events section of our member's page.

My apologies for the most recent exploratory raid into Karnor's Castle. A very bad connection coupled by less than perfect organization by myself (and the constant trains on the day of the event) led to a rather poor experience for the guild. I apologize wholeheartedly to all members involved.

A successful exploratory raid of Karnor's Castle was conducted by a full group of Guild Members on Saturday, March 30, 2002. The event was quite successful, and the zone seems to be a good place to get some excellent experience. Though perhaps the best loot dropped was a star ruby, there seemed to be rather common drops of fine steel weaponry. The rare drops in the zone, though we did not see them in this particular raid, are indeed excellent. I think this is a place the guild will visit quite often in the future. Special thanks to all those who participated in the raid!

The Tower of Frozen Shadow Raid, while it had some bumps along the way, was rather successful! An update of the raid events will be posted in the near future. On a side note, I have now been banned from weekend play by my girlfriend, so I do apologize for having to leave such an enjoyable raid, and having to forgo future weekend raids.
