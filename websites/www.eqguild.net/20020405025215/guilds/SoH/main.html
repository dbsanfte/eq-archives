Greetings!

Welcome to the web page belonging to the Shield of Honor. We are an EverQuest-based role-playing guild currently established on the Firiona Vie server, but with plans to expand to other servers as the opportunity allows.

As a quick look around will tell you, we have hardly begun to operate this site; although all of the links to the left are functional, most of the pages have no content--with the exception of the CHARTER, MEMBERSHIP, and GUILD HEIRARCHY links (please note that the Heirarchy link is only found on the Charter page). Feel free to examine our charter and Membership requirements, as well as our intended Heirarchy. If the ideas outlined appeal to you, feel free to click the link to the left leading to our Membership page or contact any of our officers (soon to be posted on their own page within the site) by logging on to EverQuest and creating a character on the Firiona Vie server.

Thanks for stopping by, and may Tunare be with you always!
