Guild Charter

 

Guild Charter

As stated by our name, we are, of course, Elven. If your heart is pure you will remain a loved member of a growing family. We must all be the best we can be, to become the elite! We must be honorable, helpful, wise and strong. Only together can we accomplish our goals. These goals are simple, but never easy. We strive to become the greatest of the guilds. Wear your guild names proudly, Ladies and Gentlemen. For the actions of one of us can spoil our good name for everyone.

Guild Rules:

Light side elves only. (Exceptions to be made only at by majority vote of ALL guild officials. And even then, the only exception possible would be a dark elf, having proved him/her self to be of good, and pure of heart and having turned his back on his own people.)

Roleplaying is of course, a choice, but not deemed necessary for EGOL. However, if you turn on "roleplaying" we must insist that you DO indeed roleplay, and not simply be anonymous with a purple name. Unless you are roleplaying, please refrain from being anonymous.

Code of Honor: Outside the Guild

We must at least ATTEMPT to answer calls for help. All too often has come the time when each of us has asked for assistance,
and even with people nearby, no one came. This, however, does not mean get yourself killed trying to help, if the person has
obviously bitten off more than they could chew. *We may NOT deliberatly run trains of mobs to another person or group
without warning. Training without warning is indeed the scourge of Norrath, and will not be tolerated. Sacrificing yourself
would be preferable rather than run a train to unwary folk. Reports of this type of action WILL be investigated, and if found to
be truthful, that member may be sanctioned. HOWEVER. If the reverse is found to be true, then justice will be
sought! We must protect our own. We may not deliberately Kill Steal. However, if someone asks for help, and one of us
annihilates the problem at hand, may to the victor go the spoils. Lastly, we must treat others with the dignity and
respect they deserve, lest they prove otherwise.

Guild Functions and Meetings

Members may request a guild meeting if there are sufficient circumstances to require it. Please, if you require that a meeting be convened, speak to an officer.
On occasion we as fun loving creatures need a break! Therefore, we will attempt to have the occasional activity for our members. Anyone who has any suggestions for activities, please feel free to pass them on to activities commitee. The attendance for such activities will NOT be mandatory. (Altough your fellow guildmates may wonder where you were!)

Our Mission:

Our Elf-Folk are to be the elite! We are to become the most powerful, polite and helpful of all the elves of Norrath. Once again, only united together can this be achieved. We must help each other to become the best of the best. This, after all, is OUR guild, and only together as a team can we make it work. If you feel this is not for you, then we will respect that. You may depart without remorse or guilt. Should you feel that this is where you wish to be then WELCOME HOME.
