The Rules of the Council






If you decide our Council is not right for you, please tell an officer before you leave. If you don't, you will not be allowed to return.

NO begging from our Knights or other players. You can ask twice, nicely, in guild chat. If we can help, we will.

NO stealing at all. Our Knights are to be trusted. We have a no-tolerance policy, if you are caught stealing you WILL be exiled.

Always wear your guildtag, use Roleplay if you wish to have your stats hidden, but never Anonymous. You will get 2 warnings, the third time you will lose the priviledge of wearing it.

No heavy cussing in guildchat. Respect for each other is the very heart of our guild.

Always be courteous to your fellow Knights. Say hello and goodbye, it doesn't take much and means more than you know.
