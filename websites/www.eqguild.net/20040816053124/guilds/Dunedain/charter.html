Guidelines

Charter of Dunedain

We are a little known group of wanderers that protect the Shire. We protect the Shire from numerous evils, yet they know nothing of our efforts. They do not even know we exist. We are the Keepers of the Flame, the Dunedain. (Lord of The Rings).

Mission Statement

The Dunedain are decitated ot honor, sportmanship and team play. We strive to help our fellow citizens of Norrath through assistance, encouragement and example. We gather and disseminate information based on the world of Norrath-both through research and through action.

Hierarchy

Thane: Guild Leader

The Dunedain Guard: officers(Knights)
Brothers and Sisters of Dunedain: Members in Good Standing.

The Great Dunedain
-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=

There's a name that you hear when they talk about men who are brave.
They used to use fire and swords for the evil they slayed.

They roamed over all of the earth,
Though not very many were they.

They were battered and stung, but they finally won.
And they seem to be out there again.

The battles they fought and the causes they fought for were grand.
Battles that bred many heroes and saved many lands.

The honor the had was bizarre,
seen only in each others eyes.

But you're glad they were there, they put faith in the air.
That evil might finally be stayed

So up go the horns to the battle,
the horses go on to the field.
The demons are high in the numbers,
and the bridge has already been sealed.

There's a name that you hear, when they talk about men who are brave.
And you'd better be happy that all of your heroes were saved.

For they're going all over the earth,
and no, not very many are they.

But they're fresh from the skies, and they won't ever die, they've come back to guard you and me.

So up go the horns to the battle.
The horses go on to the field.
But the demons are hiding in people,
and people should only be healed.

So up go the horns and the spirits,
for the good won't be hurt by the rain.
And the Grey Wizard will guide them together.
Together the great Dunedain. (LoTR)
