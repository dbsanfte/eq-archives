Welcome to the Dunedain official website. Dunedain was founded Febuary 2001 and has served the people of the Vazelle server. If you are interested in joining us please visit our message forums or send one of us a tell in the game.
 
                               

 If you have any questions about this website please email us at dunedain@eqguild.net

Dunedain is an Everquest (EQ) guild based on the values of the Dunedain (Strider or Lord Aragon) in the Lord of the Rings Books. In short we believe in the Old Code. We are open to all classes: Rangers, Druids, Bards, Necromancers, Magicians,Beastlords, Enchanters, Clerics, Warriors, Shadowknights, Rogues and Shaman.

We are not a guild to join if you expect your guild to twink or powerlevel you. We help each other out as much as we can yet you are expected to pull your own weight.
