To forward your current page to your new page, simply replace the bold text with your new site's url, then paste the entire red section into your index.html file (or main page) somewhere between the <head> and </head> tags, and the entire blue section into the main area of your page (after the <body> tag).

OR you can replace the entire contents of your index.html (or main page) with the green section:

after the <head> tag:
<META HTTP-EQUIV="Refresh" CONTENT="20; URL=http://yourguild.eqguild.net">

after the <body> tag:
You will be forwarded in 20 seconds to our new site! If it doesn't forward you, please <a href="http://yourguild.eqguild.net">click here</a>.

OR replace your entire page with this:

<html>
<head>
<title>You are being redirected</title>
<META HTTP-EQUIV="Refresh" CONTENT="0; URL=http://yourguild.eqguild.net">
</head>
<body>
You are being forwarded to our new site. If the page doesn't refresh, please <a href="http://yourguild.eqguild.net">click here</a>.
</body>
</html>
