                         ruler - more at ARCH-] (1539) 1. a: absence of government b: a state of

                         GUILD CHARTER

                         Who We Are:

                         We are the product of the world around us. Norrath is a dangerous place, where
                         anarchy rules supreme. Our goal in it, is to enjoy our existence here, and to
                         embrace the chaos around us, and reap the fruits of its presence.

                         The DoA is a band of freethinkers, brought together for one purpose...the
                         enjoyment of life.

                         We the members, of Deeds of Anarchy, believe that once in one’s life, one must
                         make a decision, a course of action that will not only change your life but also that of
                         the world around you. All great things come from a single act of chaos, an
                         un-thought decision, and an act of desperation or wantonness done without care.

                         Our place is to search the world through good and bad searching for the time when
                         our decision will make us great. To change the course of the world with a moment of
                         anarchy.

                         Our Goals:

                         * We shall let no obstacle overcome us

                         * We shall provide a place for all to explore the world around them, and to promote
                         the ideals of Anarchy

                         * We shall assist those in need, and show them that Anarchy is the way

                         * We shall not mar the image of Anarchy by intentionally wrong-doing others
                         around us
