Joining Us

For those interested in joining the Fellowship, let it be known that maturity, above all virtues is a must as beggars or whiners are not tolerated. Becoming a member does not guarntee you will be PLed or outfitted with armor and/or weapons.. Not to say you wont, just that it is not to be expected =) You can expect that you will have the oppourtunity to group with, ask advice from and have fun with some really great people.

Fellowship of Vazaelle members are helpful and are known for their generousity. but as much as we like to help, we are more likely to help those who help themselves and show the initiative or drive to get what they want done. While some people seek pleasure in filling their banks with platinum and items, or grinding day in and day out getting levels, we like to enjoy our time online and want others to have a good time as well. We have no age restriction, we only expect our members to act in a mature, respectful manner at all times.

After reading our Charter and Code of Conduct, find us in game by typing /who all 'fellowship of vazaelle' Don't forget the quote marks or it won't work =) Listed below are our officers. Contact one of them and let them know you have visited our site and are interested in joining. If all else fails you can always email me and we can work out a time to get together.

Hope to seeya in there, Mulvina Itchybottom
