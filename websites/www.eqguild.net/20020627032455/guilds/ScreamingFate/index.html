Screaming Fate

An EverQuest Guild on Prexus

Screaming Fate is a guild on the Prexus server. We are a guild based on friendship and fun, so if you have a suggestion on when to party, raid, quest, or any thing of that sort, post it in the message board with your character's name. The news section, there will be confirmed dates on parties, raids, meetings, and etc. If there are any questions, post them in the board, or ask an officer or Rink Woodhands.

 

January 21, 2002

Officers Be on Watch at all times for Players who would make a great addition to our guild.

Players if you want to become an officer talk to the guild tell them you want to be an officer , and if they support you talk to the officers, and they will report to me for a suggested promotion for you.

New rules: 

1. None under the level of 15 may join unless they are an alternative character of a player already in the guild.

2. Those who Wish to be an officer, must be level 20+, and have an approval from more than half the officers, votes from regular players help too.

3. The new rules will not effect those who joined or became an officer before the rules where changed.

-Rink Woodhands.
