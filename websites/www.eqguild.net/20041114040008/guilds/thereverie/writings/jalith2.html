Ode to a Meditation


I port over to Nektulos Forest and run to Lavastorm Mountains. I then proceed to find a tall mountain overlooking a small lavaflow. I cast a small spell to allow me to sit in relative comfort as I work. I sit down and pull out my spellbook. I begin reading over all my spells and try to decide what I would like to commit to my memory. As I read on, my thoughts begin to wander to all my friends (like a Dark Elf has many friends.) I wonder what part of Norrath they are holed up in trying to find that ever elusive "object of desire" that they are searching for. But I digress, I should be studying right now. I clear my thoughts with this ditty.

I pray to the power of fire.
Fire to cook, fire to clean,
Fire to cleanse, fire to burn.
I pray to honor the Burning Prince.
I pray to the power of Solusek Ro.
Use thy fire to cleanse my heart
Use thy fire to purify my thoughts
Use thy fire to light my pathways
Impart on me thy fire so that I may show the world your power
Impart on me thy fire so that I may impart to you the souls of your enemies
Impart on me thy fire so that through me the world can burn in your glory
My prayer burns in my heart always

I then bend to my studies, with my heart (and eyes) aglow with power and fire. I gain new insights to my spells and how to use them effectively and to smite my enemies.

Be aware, the world will burn with the glory of Solusek Ro, the Burning Prince! The Sun itself proclaims his glory!!


Jalith S'Trahl
EQG
