Guild Charter

Fate is not always kind.  Fate is blind to such things as race, chosen god, or profession.  Fate falls, and though one be powerful and strong, they find themselves alone or forgotten.  The Circle of the Forgotten is a place for folks such as these.  It is  a safe haven, a refuge, and a home.  The Forgotten of are gathered into the Circle, given aid, a voice, and companionship.  And they become stronger.  The Circle of the Forgotten is open to any class, any alignment, and any level.  The Circle asks only that its members follow three simple rules:  Honor Thy Guild, Enjoy Thyself, and Don't Be a Jerk.

Guild Rules 

Honor Thy Guild
Be willing to help fellow Members.
Serve without expectation of reward.
Attend guild functions whenever you have a character of the function level.
Never dishonor fellow Members.
Be loyal to the Guild.

Enjoy Thyself
Above all else have fun!
Please share your sense of humor!
Good-Natured teasing is always welcome! 
Role Playing is encouraged but not necessary (a mere “M’Lady” or 
“Thank Tunare” is always appreciated).

Don’t Be a Jerk
This includes:
Stealing,
Harassing other players,
Intentional train causing,
Dishonesty,
Cheating,
Kill Stealing,
Ninja looting,
Exacting revenge against other players.

In Return Members Can Expect
 A Vote in important guild matters.
To be aided when in trouble.
Some help obtaining items and levels.
Advice on good hunting spots.
A chance to make friends with good people.
