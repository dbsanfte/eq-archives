EQ Links

AllaKhazam's Magical Realm
This website is a wonderful source of information. It's been up since the release of EverQuest and just never stopped growing in size. You can find information about virtually anything but perhaps it's greatest strength is quests.

Eqmaps
Fantastic equipment and maps site. The feature new NPC updates and new equipment updates daily

Magelo
This site maintains character profiles and presents them much like the inventory view within EQ. All AoU members are encouraged to create a profile and post it under their roster profile.

EQ Trader's Corner
So you want to learn a tradeskill. This is the place to learn how. Filled with all the information you'll ever want to know about tradeskills including Recipies, Supply Locations, Prices, and even Tradeskill-based quests.

EQ Caster's Realm
They cover all casting classes with creation guides, spell lists, and strategies. They always keep you up to date on the lastest news and changes (or nerfs). Many of the questions are answered by the magic guy at Verant himself, GZ

Torvonnilous.net
Our very own server's web site. Probably the biggest reason to use this site is the message boards.

Check out the links page for other cool sites.
