Guild Rules

 

 

 

Recruiting Officer – Lyndsel

 

1)     New members - New members to the guild will be referred to Lyndsel. She will get the new member together with at least 2 officers for review and approval. Two officers must be present to invite a new member of the guild. All members will be allowed to give their input on new guild members and the Guild Officers will have the final decision. New members will serve a 30 day probation period under which the other members will get to know them and their compatibility with the guild.

2)     Removal from the guild – The Guild Officers must vote on player removal from the guild for actions unbecoming a guild member. This is not done lightly. Problems with any guild member should be referred to any guild officer for review and action.

3)     Play well, enjoy your band of Brothers and Sisters, and look out for one another… after all, that is what our Guild is all about.
