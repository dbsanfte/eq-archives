Types of Guilds

Finding the type of guild that fits your goals and desires will allow you to be satisfied with your guild choice. The different types of guilds fit different personalities and different styles. Knowing what type of guild to look for will help you make a good decision.


Family Guilds A focus on caring for others and helping others.

Raiding Guilds A focus on achieving more through raiding.

Roleplaying Guilds A focus on enjoying roleplaying characters.

Guild of Friends A focus on supporting a close group of friends.

"Guild" Guild A guild focused on nothing and usually formed for bad reasons. A guild to avoid.
