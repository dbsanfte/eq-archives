Guild Laws
1. The leader is and it's three senior officers ( S.O.) are elected buy popular votes and shall serve the guild for 3 months.
2 The Guild leadership shall consist of these four elected officials.
3. Anyone can be approved by one of the four officials to be a recruiting officer ( R.O.).
4. Membership becomes official to Noblehearts once all character information is completed on the members page.
5. You must be a member to be considered for an R.O. or S.M.
6. You must be a S.M. to be voted the leader.
7. You must help out other guild mates within reason and give fair trades or sales of items to one another.
8. Make no representations that are wrongful or misleading to others in and out of the guild.
9. A sub leader shall be appointed by the three officials for levels 30 and under, they shall be considered the immediate leader for all levels under 30. They will remain sub leader until the next election.
10. Major issues shall be brought before the officials first, instead of openly discussed in guild chat.
11. The position of R.O. is just that ... to recruit new members.
12. No anon character status under level 20 unless approved by an officer.
13. Give special preference to fellow guild mates when in grouping with others and when it comes to corpse retrieval.
14. Any changes to guild laws must be fully agreed by the four top guild leaders.
15. Past leaders become E.L.'s and retain rights as a silent officer :)

 

Raid Rules

1. We stick together, no wandering off.

2. If one of us dies then the raid is on hold till we recover the poor souls corpse.

3. Looter is the person we are raiding for.

4. All other dropped items will either go to those who can use or need them, and will be decided by a random roll. There will be a one item cap per raid for the person we are raiding for, beyond that items will be random to all who have yet to receive something. 

5. On good items we will do /randoms, if you win one you will be excluded from other /randoms until everyone gets something.

6. Time limits : when doing a raid plan on a minimum of 3 to 4 hours and a maximum of 5 to 6 hours, of course depending on how things are going.

 

 

Hail Noblehearts,
We have a very unique organization that we all agreed to create, a Democracy based on the goodness of each and every one of us. In that we hope that each of us has the chance to be a leader or an officer of this fine guild. Yet there must also be a system of checks and balances to insure those who are trusted with the guild leadership do not abuse their powers.

Unfortunately we can not assume that everyone will put the good of the guild before the good of themselves, sorry, it sometimes can be the darker side of human nature. To guard ourselves from this darkness, and to make certain that the role of leadership is very clear and precise, I believe there should be some special conditions for our leaders to adhere to. For they will be the ones who shall interpret all future challenges to our rules, our doctrine, our oaths and ultimately our guild...

EQeriously yours

Nixurian
