Tenets of Our Order

That I will provide help to those who request to the best of my ability. If I cannot provide the help requested, I will seek the answers from those who would know.


That I will portray myself truthfully and not maintain multiple personas with various conflicting beliefs. Consistency in style and beliefs will help define who I am.


That I will not belittle the performance of a fellow player, for I would not wish to receive such treatment.


That I will not prosecute another player or guild without significant provocation, nor the support of the Covenant.


That I will come to the aid and support of my fellow Knights. Fraternity is a bond we share in spirit, as well as in the flesh.
