Proper service in the Eyes of Innoruuk is expressed by three commandments. In Neriak, past the Third Gate, in the Black Library, there is a small chamber. In that most holy chamber stands a pedestal, and on that pedestal rests the Great Pact. Here is written in the Ancient Tongue the Commandments of Innoruuk by His own Hand. These are the foundations of the Church, and must be followed above all else. I set forth here, in plain and modern language, the Commandments:

THE FIRST COMMANDMENT: PURPOSE
Return to Top

All Teir`Dal were created only for the fulfillment of the Will of Innoruuk. We exist only to do so. We do not follow the Will. We ARE the Will. We cannot be separated from the Father, and free-will is not a burden We carry. We are not troubled by questions of right and wrong as are mortals. We simply do the Will of Our Father, and in this We are superior.

Each Teir`Dal is given a purpose in the Will even before their birth. We Children must follow Our purpose, for only in it will the Will be realized. Above all others, some purposes are great, and are called the High Purposes. They are enumerated in the Litany of Purpose and are as follows, in descending order:

The Queen and King: Eternal Mother and Father to Us all. They are pure, and beyond reproach.

Clerics: They are the mouth of Innoruuk and speak His Words. They insure that Children do not stray from the Father, and that the body does not falter in the Will. They are one with the Father and above all others in understanding of the Will. They are His embodiment in this world.

Necromancers: Their purpose is to command the dead in the service of the Will. Death is no excuse to be released from the Will. The dead must serve the Father as in life. There can be no rest until the End. They also bind the dead of the Hated Others to the Service of Innoruuk, and are to be revered.

Shadowknights: They are the Soldiers of Innoruuk. They are the holy guardians of the Priesthood, and the swordarm of the highest servants of the Father. They are unwavering in their purpose and are blessed in the Eyes of Innoruuk.

Warriors: They are the shield of the Queen and King. They are strong among the Children, and full of Hate. Their purpose it to cut down the\par enemies of the Will and before them none shall stand.

All purposes serve the Will of Innoruuk, but these shall be set above all others as most holy.

THE SECOND COMMANDMENT: UNITY
Return to Top

Teir`Dal shall not raise word, hand, or sword against Teir`Dal. All are Sisters and Brothers in the Will of the Father. Those that turn away from the Will, however, shall be considered Spurned, and shall be treated as are the Hated Others. The Will shall always remain open to them, however, and if the Spurned turns back to the Will of the Father, they shall be allowed admittance to all but the highest mysteries of the Church, as befits the purity of their blood.

THE THIRD COMMANDMENT: PURITY
Return to Top

The teachings of the Church shall not be kept from any who are called to the Will of the Father. Therefore, all who turn from the lying gods and turn to the True Father may be taught of the Will, regardless what race they call Mother. However, those not of purest Teir`Dal blood shall not be taught the inner mysteries, nor allowed entry into the Third Gate or the Church of the Firstborn. They shall also not partake of the Greater Ceremonies, nor be bound by the Commandments of Obedience to the Father. In short, they may be taught Our Father's use for them, but they are not to be admitted into the Circle of His Children.

Those that stand against the Father shall be damned. They are the Hated Others. All shall be reviled and destroyed. Hatred shall be spread among them and they shall perish from inside as well as from out. No mercy shall be shown them, and none shall be suffered to live.

Thus are the Words of Our Father. Thus are His Commandments to Us.
