About Me.....
 At the moment I am a magician that has seen 49 seasons. I have all 4 focus items of the first set. I like to help out the younger people when I get the chance to help them along the way a little, The world of Antonica is a hard enough place out there as it is I just try to smooth it out a little along the way for them. I have been known to get drunk at every opportunity. I was born in the city of Erudin but have been traveling ever since my eighth season. I do go back every now and then though. I have traveled most of my life but still have not seen half of the world out there, although I do take every chance I come upon to see something new. I have been a member of the Obsidian Dragon Society ever since my sixth season. I was not named after anyone or have had the chance to pick my name for myself. My parents dropped 6 sets of letters into a Helmet and pulled out as many letters as they thought my name should be long. If my name was spelled the way it came out it would have been Auridnr but they mixed the letters up and the closest thing to a what they felt was a name, that they could come up with was Irrudan.
