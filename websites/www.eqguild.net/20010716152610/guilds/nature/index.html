April 19th 2001
Tonight the guild had its first meeting, although it was a small one many issues were addressed: the web site, guild divisions, division leaders, and recruitment system.

Web Sites
We have decided that this site will not be submitted to any search engines. The reason for this is that we are trying to limit our members to serious players only. We do want random web surfers signing up for our guild. We want to get to know you in game first, make sure that you are a dedicated player, and a true EverQuest player.

Division Leaders

 Each guild division will have a leader; the division leader is in charge of inviting new members into the Covenant. The leader of each division will supervise trail periods upon joining and have the right to disband anyone from the guild at anytime. *Guild banishment's can only be done for a valid reason and must have the approval of all division leaders and guild captain before being carried out.

Melee Division Leader: Urlacher
Caster Division Leaders: Soulnexium and Terina
Hybrid Division Leader: Farramir

Recruitment System

 New members will be invited to join by current members in game. Upon invitation you will be directed to this site to fill out a membership application form. After sending the form you will receive a reply instructing you on whom to contact for invitation into the guild. Your division leader will then observe you for one week, if at the end of the week you do not follow Covenant rules you will be removed from the guild. If all goes well after the one week you will become a full Covenant member.

Raids, raids, raids, well I have one word for you all Befallen. Nothing has been planned for sure yet, but this looks like the place. Keep a look out for more information.
