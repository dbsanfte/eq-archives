Article V. ~ Duties of Centurions and Role Definition

Sec a)Any members with the rank of Centurion, are strictly given said rank to assist in the recruitment of potential good soldiers, spreading the word of their superiors (the Military Advisors), and thereby, spreading the words and practices of the Imperator General.

Sec b) Centurions are also appointed the task of upholding the laws of the guild, and are bound to hold themselves to a standard befitting of the privilege of that rank.

Sec c) Centurions witnessing or informed of any violations of guild rules, or conducts unbecoming of a Legionnaire, will reference said event, and all information regarding to their superior for evaluation. Difficult matters will be convened in a discussion between the MA, and the IG, and punishment, or choice to take no action, we be delved out by said superiors.

Sec d) As a Centurion you have the right to use the guild message of the day command to promote guild activates, events, or pass along pertinent information about other guilds inviting us to raids and such. When changing the message, be aware of the weight of your new message vs. the current one.

Sec e) you will have full access into the officers club forums.

Sec f) As a Centurion, you will be responsible to coordinate one raid that you will host, and operate in rotation with the other Centurions. You will be required to research and post about the raid. Be sure to post your raid intentions first in the officers’ forum for brief discussions. As the Centurion in charge of the raid, you will need to post times, level requirements, and loot rules for the raid.

Sec g) you are bound to all the rules and responsibilities of Centurion and Conscript.
