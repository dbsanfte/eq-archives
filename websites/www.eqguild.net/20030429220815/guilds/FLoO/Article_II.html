Article II. ~ Promotion from Legionnaire to Conscript

Sec a) to be promoted from Legionnaire to Conscript you must be nominated by a current Conscript, Centurion, Militant Council member, or the General. This nomination is an example of the sponsor showing their respect to you by nominating you to become a Conscript. They would feel you exemplify what our guild stands for. They will also recognize you have the ability to recruit new members to our guild.

Sec b) you must be at least 10th level to become a Conscript.

Sec c) You must have been with our guild for at least one month. This is a good way for you to show your willingness to assist in the growth of our guild.

Sec d) you must register your character at guildmagic.com
