General EQ Sites

EQVault
If you are looking for general Everquest information then look no further. EQVault is the place to find the latest news and events happening around Norrath. If you are an avid Everquest player than a daily hit on this site is definately in your best interest.

Official EverQuest Home Page
Game development, message boards, tech support , and FAQs from the game creators themselves. A good place to start your EQ education, or just check on current status of servers and upcoming game add-ons.

AllaKhazam's Magical Realm
What can I say, Allakhazam is the man. If you want information on quests, spells, equipment, etc then this is the place to go. I must admit, I'm an Allakhazam junkie and visit his site at least 20 times a week.

EverQuest Stratics
If you are looking for strategy guides, maps and other information then EQ Stratics is the site for you. Don't forget to check out the message boards if you need some specific help.

EQ'lizer
Here is the spoiler site you've always wanted. Race, class, equipment information is all here for the asking. Daily updates and heavy player traffic make this site a wealth of Everquest knowledge.

Tamm's Everquest Pages
Info on Items, Equipment, Trade Skill tricks and suppliers.
