Dark Revival

    Once upon a time, the world was  devoid of magic.  But now,  there is a revival of the Dark Arts -- magic -- taking place in Norrath. The gods may grant lesser beings these powers in order to use them as tools in their titanic struggles. We in Dark Revival believe the Dark Arts can benefit us, that we can use magic and magical items to help us defeat our enemies, perhaps even the gods themselves!
