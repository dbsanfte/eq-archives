CHARTER


Fate's Rival was founded on January 20, 2002 by a small group of friends with the hopes to see each and every one of us reach our full potential as members of the Fennin Ro community.

To that end, we strive to encourage and assist one another with completing our individual goals, while still keeping an eye on making a complete and well-rounded guild.

Compassion, reason, and dedication shall be the foundations of this assembly. Honor, prestige, and fulfillment shall be our reward.
