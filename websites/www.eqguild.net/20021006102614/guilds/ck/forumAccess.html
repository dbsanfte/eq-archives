How to Access our various Forums

To access the CK guild forum:
1. Create an account on EZboards. Make it similar to your ingame character name.
2. Go to the guild forum on EzBoards and you will be asked to apply for access to the boards.
3. Officers will check your name (similar to ingame) and approve your access.


Creating an EZBoard account

To create an account on "ezboards"
  1. Go to this link: EZBoard New account Link.
  2. Fill in every box that has a blue asterix next to it.
    NOTE: You don't have to tell the truth..I dont on online forms..lie about age..etc. just to confuse the database programs.
  3. EMAIL address has to be valid - They will send a confirmation email with entry information in it.
  4. Choose to donate money to EZBOARD (or NOT):
    Choose an ezSupporter Subscription
  5. Remember to CLICK OFF the checkbox:
    Yes! I want to receive messages and special offers from ezboard's advertising partners.
  6. Remeber to CLICK ON the checkbox:
    I agree to ezboard's TOU and declare that I am over 13 years old.

    ---------- PAGE #2 of signup -----------
  7. Remember to CLICK OFF on the checkbox:
    Yes! Sign me up to receive special coupons, discounts, and freebies (from the stores you trust!)
  8. Go to bottom of page...Click on the "NO THANKS" button...this DOES NOT cancel your account, just says no thanks to the email spam offers.

    ---------- PAGE #3 of signup -----------
  9. Go to bottom of page...DO NOT click on the [Sign Up] button at the bottom of the page...instead click on the "No, thank you." link underneath it.
  10. You should then see a page with: "Your ezboard Account is Ready"
    Congratulations on your new ezboard account and welcome to the Internet's most vibrant and active network of online communities!

    -------- YOUR EMAIL PROGRAM -------
  11. Startup your email program...or, if the email address you gave was a web based email, then go to your web email reaider.
  12. Wait for the email from ezboard to arrive.
  13. Read the email and follow the instructions. Basically you have to click on the link at the bottom of the email.
