Our Policies
Loot

Need before greed.

Main characters come before twinks. If nobody's main needs the item, anyone who would use the item can get in on the roll. An exception to this rule would be if the person was asked to bring along his/her twink for the good of the raid.

If you are late to a raid (by 10 minutes or more), you must wait 2 hours or 1 random before you are eligible for loot.

Class specific loot will be randomed to the people who need.
 

Epics

A epic piece that drops during a raid will go to the person who is furthest along in the quest..
..unless scheduled beforehand (doing the raid for THAT person)

Older members come before Trial members on these drops unless decided before.
 

General

Each member is to act with maturity and carry their own weight.
 

Raids

Show up on time! Be at the zone we are raiding or at some spires BEFORE the raid! 
Do not show up late and be bummed that nobody will come and get you.
During raids, keep the ooc and shout channels clear until told to use them.
While we try not to die, it happens. Please be patient, understanding, and helpful on a CR.
We will get all of our stuff back.
Only one person in the group loots. That person calls out items to the Loot Randomer.
Make an ASSIST button. I don't care what class you are. Everyone needs a button that /assist's the main assist. Use it!
 

Recruitment

(See the Join page for the classes and levels we are looking for)

You need to have your keys for Sebilis, Skyshrine and Cobalt Scar.
You need to be an active leveler.
You are expected to show up to raids and participate in the guild events.
You are expected to check the website and forums often.

We only want main characters, please do not submit an app for your alternate.

Post your join application under Applications in our forums.
