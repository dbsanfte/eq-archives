The Legion And the Cabbage...

Tradeskill Members and Non-Tradeskill Members  Needed!

 

    After reviewing the Protection of the Cabbage Quest, many of us decided we wanted to commit our time and abilities to produce items for the POTC quest as a team.  The reward is an extremely nice.  Anyone can use the Earring of the Solstice, and druid's can choose between that or Xanthe's Earring of Nature.  Please note:  This is different from the spell version of the quest.

     To say this is a tradeskill-intensive quest would be an understatement.  We need the services of folks who have or are working toward a 250 in the following skills: 

  • Baking
  • Brewing
  • Fishing (200)
  • Jewelcrafting
  • Pottery
  • Smithing
  • Tailoring

     Ok, so fishing isn't considered a tradeskill...  <Tries to track down her train of thought>  Well, we need at least 1 Grand Master for each of these tradeskills (er, and fishing.)  Multiple Grand Masters for a specific would be great!  There's plenty of work to do for this quest.  The more folks that can help, the easier this will be for all involved.  I've posted a Breakdown of the POTC Quest so we can all get a basic idea of what's involved at a glance.  Ok, so it's a rather long glance, hehehe....

 

     If you lack a passion for tradeskills, but would skill like to help (and would really like the cool earring<g>,) there's plenty of stuff we'll need to do before any combining can even start!  We'd really appreciate any assistance you can give.  There are some recipe components that can only be obtained by fighting, e.g., lotus sap and/or leaves dropped by deep cavern lotuses in FG and brownie parts dropped by...well, brownies!  At times, we'll need people willing to port folks to various locations, people who can forage in specific zones, folks willing to collect and/or purchase 39+ spell research components for celestial essence (which ANYONE can make and is no-fail), ect...

 

     The Legion has many friends in Norrath, through allied guilds and through the friends of individual members.  We may very well need to seek the assistance of these friends, but we want to start from within.  So here's what we need anyone interesting in helping to do right now:

  •     POST to the POTC folder in our forum here.  Please READ the initial message and then post as a reply.  Once we get an idea of who's willing to        help (which also means who wants the earring), we'll start organizing this massive undertaking, breaking it down into bite-sized, manageable pieces.  We don't want any one person to end up overwhelmed with work.  Make sure to include all the info asked for.  If you don't know something, just say so :)  No worries, we'll get it all straightened out eventually!

If you have any questions, you can also post to the POTC folder.  Just start a new thread, please.  Take care, and we'll all be talking soon! :)
