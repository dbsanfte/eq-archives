Notice to all members:

As of Jan 01. registering all your characters that are in the guild will become mandatory. All current guild members are expected to register by the first if they have not already. All new members will have 10 days to register. This includes any Alts that are in the guild but does NOT included any characters that you may have that are not members of BD. If you are having any difficulty registering your character please feel free to email me, my addy is at the bottom of the page. Anyone that does not register risks removal from the guild. Please include the race and class of your alts in the spaces provided and note after their names if they are officers. If you need any information changed or left anything out, please email me and let me know. Please note that once you are on the guild list, your entry in the guest book is removed.

Also please be advised that you are expected to know what the guild you are a part of is about, who the leader and officers are, and the rules of the guild. Please look over the site, there is plenty of information contained here and more added all the time.

-Maripha.
