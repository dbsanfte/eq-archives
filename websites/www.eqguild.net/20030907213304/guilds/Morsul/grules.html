The Council of Morsul is just that group. These rules not only bind us but remind us of what is the target for any individual to strive for.....blah....blah....blah :)

 

C o u n c i l  o f  M o r s u l:Rules
1. All members must live by the four virtues of the guild, Honor, Loyalty, Patients, Honesty.

2. Treat others how you like to be treated, within and outside of guild.

3. Try to group with your guild mates as much as possible, as this will make us a strong and true family.

4. Prepare to help and be helped by other guildies.

5. No Swearing in guild say pls or at neother time, as this puts shame to our guild.

6. No Begging, of any kind, again this puts our guild to shame. You want something, request to the Officers or GL's and we will see if we can get it for u.

7. Have respect for your Guild leaders and Officers. Remember that for promotion we will be looking for skills such as these.

8. Act in a mature and sensible fashion remembering you are representing this guild at all times. ( I don’t want to receive complaints from others about our guildies Kill Stealing Etc )

9. Your must display your guildtag at all times. Be proud to be with us, and help make our name known in this world.



C o u n c i l  o f  M o r s u l:Code of Conduct
Powerleveling
This is something that we as a guild should not rely on. Sure everyone likes to be leveled, naturally everyone wants to be a high level? but how you get there is the choice of, either doing it yourself, or getting someone to level you. Be proud of who you are, and be proud that no matter what your level, be it 10 or 60, that you've done it yourself! That way you can truly be proud of who you are, and will appreciate your character all the more.

Also certain problems can occure as the result of being power leveled. Yes you gain levels, stat wise, but in mind you don't. You may be able to fight more powerful mobs and enter, until then unexplored zones, but because you haven't taken the time to level yourself, the learning process has been left behind, and you may not be able to handle your now UBER toon!

With all that in mind, ppl of the guild, and even myself may offer to aid or assist a player, either buy a quick leveling push, or just a general hunting exercise. I myself will often buff up most ppl of the guild I see to give them an aid for a short while, but I stress, take only if offered. Asking to be powerleveled is the sign of weakness, be strong and proud, you will much more appreciate the guild, and the game as a whole for it.

Character Transfers
We all from time to time, have the need to transfer items from your main to your alt toons, or vise versa. If you need to do this, firstly ask around your friends for help, if u get no joy then feel free to ask the guild. It is important that care is taken with transfers, as if you let someone you don't know do it, you risk losing the items you wish to transfer through theift? ( This is also something that the GM's can't do anything about )

If you need a transfer from the guild, pls make sure you set up the following. Make sure you are in a sensible place, and easy to get to.
Asking for a transfer in the depths of an obscure zone, will not have ppl flocking to help you. Setup someone where easy like the nexus or Gfay, if KOS in Gfay then the commons are all easy zones to access, and most races
can get to them easily.

Secondly think about time constrants, make sure both characters are in the same place, so that you don't hold up the member helping you for too long. Most peoples playing time is precious and they may be unwilling to help you in the future if you keep them hanging on for hours, while running a toon over from the other side of the world.

Deaths and Resurrection
Please not for the members of the guild, that people who have the ability to rez players are not obliged to do so during normal hunting days. If you need a rez, you may of course ask for assistance for a port or a rez, to help retrieve your corpse of maybe even some experience, but this is a voluntary action by the rezzer or porter, and should not be demanded. If there is no one available to rez or port you , then ask in which ever zone you are in, or get a port to the loc of your corpes and then shout for a rez. Else its down to biting your tongue and joining the rest of us, who have to go and hunt out there own corpes.

Exceptions to this rule and policy are :
When under RAID control. During a RAID, I will make sure that any player who experience death, will be rezzed without question.

Only and if very ONLY, if it is an emergancy or completely hopeless situation. Like your corpes is buried very deep inside a dangerous zone, and you have no group to help you regain your corpes, and there is no one in zone prepared to help you. Be warned though, if you take this route, and pull ppl out of there hunting times, to form a group to make a corpes run for you, only to get to the zone, to hear shouts of a cleric offerring out rezzes, then this shall be not looked upon kindly.

Hunts and Quests
If the is a particular drop item that you need or a quest you need to complete, please email me or post a link on the message board under quests.

Once info is received, either the raid organiser or another officer or myself will organise something in due course to help you.

Please note I ask that we only help you to complete quests, not do them for you?. Additionally, if your quest or hunt gives bad faction, please warn others on what faction hits they will take before they embark on the quest with you.

Guild Bank
Members are allowed to draw a maximum of 2 items per RL calendar month. The items in the guild bank are items donated by the guild members themselves. If you have something you think would be of use to younger members or even older ones, and you don't need them anymore, then please ask myself or Xantie to log on the guildbank characters, and donate your items. These can be Armour weapons, words, research or even spells etc.

The guildbank chars will be available every night from 6 ? 7 PM GMT (1-2PM EST) if you require it before hand, then you may request special access afterwoods , I will also have the guildbank access the general meetings for use, afterwoods.
