Everquest Links
This is a page of links to sites for our favorite game!  These are sites that I have explored and/or belong to.  If you have any links to add to this, email me and let me know.
 
Allakhazam A very killer site.  The best out there (if you have the premium membership anyway)
EQ Atlas Need a map?  This is the top map site out there.  Maps to 90% of the zones.
EQ Casters Realm Lists all the spells for all casters and where to purchase them.  Very handy.
EQ Maps Another killer site.  Need to get membership to get all the perks, but very nice site.  Has ton's of maps and gear lists.
EQ Traders Good place for the skilled traders to come.  Has most of the recipes.
GU Comics Very funny site dedicated to EQ humor.  Take time to read some of the old comics!!
