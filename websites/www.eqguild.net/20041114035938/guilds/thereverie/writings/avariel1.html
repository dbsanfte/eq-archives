The Reverie


Pathways of dreams, we are together
Blades, bows, magiks and love
Against any odd, forged friendships harder than any metal
Armour is our physical protection, our bond is stronger
The Reverie...the dream...
Family, friends and fun
Like the stars, we are not alone
Many are we, though we may not seem it
Numbers is a measure only in body
Friends together, foes will quiver
Dragons flee and gods will fall
Sundered towers and burning bridges
The dream wave does come
Make way! Make way!
We come...with no fear...

Avariel
