- Behavior -

Be civil to all members of TSW and all other people you come into contact with.

 

- What TSW expects from you -

Turn up for raids, not all, not compulsary, but some (eg epic) you must attend if your online. It would be appreciated if you could attend as many as possible while you are online. While on a raid, listen to the raid leader and pullers, and follow their instructions.

 

- Have Patience -

If you require help with an epic mob or help getting an item, think on we have over 80 people in this guild, the majority of which are 50+ so some patience is going to have to be given as not just you will want something done. The more people you help, the more chance you have of those poeple helping you in return.

 

- Use of guildchat -

We keep guildchat open minded, but it is not a channel for moaning about loot. During guild events, /gu will be kept to a minimum.

 

- Loot -

The majority of loot is handed out using an 'Award' system, to benefit the guild as a whole, not the individual. This system is used on guild 'raids' (raid is defined as >2 groups aiming for one goal). The awarding will be decided based on dedication, attendance, current gear to an extent and general contribution to the guild. Certain items will be exempt from this - rare one off items my be lottoed among all who can use or a fixed number, all depending on item and situation. Handing down items will NOT be mandatory - however, if you win a droppable item on a guild raid, it would be appreciated if you handed it down to someone who could benefit from it.
