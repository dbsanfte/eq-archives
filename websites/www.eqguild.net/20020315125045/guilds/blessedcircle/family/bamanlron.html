Tales Of The

Bamanlron

BamanLron is a human ranger born in Surefall glade. He has had many an adventure and met many interesting and friendly folk in various parts of Norrath but has done most of his hunting in West Antonica and Odus. As he grows he is widening his circle in which he seeks out evil to combat and friends to make.

Bamanlron joined the Blessed Circle early in life and has recently been made an officer in the guild.
