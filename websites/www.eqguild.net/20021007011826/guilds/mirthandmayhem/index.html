News as of 9/23/02

Treasury Updated:
Please visit the Treasury to see if anything we have on hand meets your needs. (Equip those nekkid alts we all know you have out there!)

Thursday Nights:
Our first two "Thursday Night Assistance Outings" have gone quite well! The first, Qalidor requested assistance in obtaining a Bracer of the Reverent, and although we did not obtain the bracer -- the mob did not spawn for the 3+ hours we waited, Qalidor gained over a level of exp waiting for the mob to spawn!

The second Thursday Night Outing was even BETTER! -- Denaldar asked for assistance getting the last four parts for his Staff of the Wheel. All four parts were obtained and Denaldar ended the evening the proud possessor of the Staff of the Wheel! WOOT WTG DEN!

Guild Reorganization:
Recent events have prompted a "reorganization" of the Ring, leaving us with need to recruit a couple of new officers. If you are interested in serving Ring as an officer or have any questions about the reorganization, please chat with Kuli, Talarius or Chasmaneight.

Membership Reopened:
Ring has reopened it's membership rolls for interested parties. We are actively recruiting Clerics and Warriors 35+ but will consider all applicants Level 20+. If you would like to be a part of Ring, please complete the application and indicate why you would like to join and who you know from Ring of Mirth and Mayhem.
