Guild Mission Statement

Introduction

Welcome to the Brotherhood of Majestic Night site. Here you will find information about our guild and its members.

Our guild is a small tight group of friends who like to hang around together and enjoy the game of Everquest. We were founded by Alklun and Moshca who were tired of the non-personal guild playing and decided to join a group of close friends together so that they could keep in touch when they play.

Since the time of the guild' inception we have added some select friends who have turned out to be good friends and a perfect match for our guild.

Because our guild is a small, but growing one, we need to be careful who we select as members. That way the best, most family like guild, can stay together as long as possible. Let's all keep it that way by continuing to successfully add more outstanding individuals to make up our whole!
