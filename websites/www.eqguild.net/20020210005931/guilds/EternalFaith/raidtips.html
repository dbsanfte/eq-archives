Try to visit the area ahead of time so you know you way around a little- *where the zone is* and where the dangers are. Having a guide with you who already knows the area is a very good thing.
Stay with your group at all times. If someone is in trouble NEVER go out to find and help them unless you are very familiar with the area. If you're not familiar with the area you'll probably just be another casualty. If you have tracking don't use it if you're not in an open area. You'll get horribly lost and you probably won't find your target if you're in a maze like or walled off area.
Know what types of mobs you'll be facing (melee types or casters) and any special abilities they have so you can prepare accordingly. Also know what levels they are and any special NPCs to watch out for.
Make sure everyone knows their job. (healer, nuker, defense, tank, utility(helpful skills), backup)
Assign people to stay behind and group with those who are late so the entire raiding force isn't held up.
Plan on getting there 1/2 an hour early because you'll probably be late if things don't work out as planned. (waiting for someone to join you, waiting for transport, unexpectedly dying, computer crash or disconnect)
