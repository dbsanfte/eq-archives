A successful exploratory raid of Karnor's Castle was conducted by a full group of Guild Members on Saturday, March 30, 2002. The event was quite successful, and the zone seems to be a good place to get some excellent experience. Though perhaps the best loot dropped was a star ruby, there seemed to be rather common drops of fine steel weaponry. The rare drops in the zone, though we did not see them in this particular raid, are indeed excellent. I think this is a place the guild will visit quite often in the future. Special thanks to all those who participated in the raid!
