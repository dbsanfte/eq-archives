Trakanon
Name: Trakanon
Type: Dragon, necromancer
Level: 65 - 65
Location: Old Sebilis, Kunark
HitPoints: 35,000
Hits For:: 300-650
Casts: 1500 point lifetap every 45 seconds, AoE Poison DoT(150 per tick) every 45 seconds, Trakanon's Touch every 45 seconds which teleports you to another location filled with Myconids, Blindness

The goal is to quickly kill Trak before the poison kills your raid. Trak can be done with 20 level 60 players or 30+ level 55+ players. I try to set up groups like so, two tanks, one bard, two clerics, one pally in the MT group, same thing in the second group. Everyone else fends for themselves with balanced groups.

The most common way to pull trak. Have a monk in the lair to pull, have a second monk in the cave to tag the pull. When Trak/Protectors are in the back, first monk single pulls Juggs to the bridge then FDs. If the pull is good as in no Trak/Prot together then have the second monk finish the pull to camp. In the end you should get the Protector single into the cave. If you do get trak in the cave then kill him quick as he will gate.

Lastly when you're done pulling the Sebelite Juggernaughts between Trak and the cave you rush Trak. Send in the first two groups then everyone else a few seconds behind. Have melee use offensive disc at 70%, chain nuke at 50%. The trick is finish off trak quickly - do not send in waves as that tactic just gives his poison dot that much more time to do damage.
