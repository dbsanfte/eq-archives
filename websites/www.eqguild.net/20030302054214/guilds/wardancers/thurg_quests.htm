Thurgadin Armor Quests

The simplest explanation to be given to describe the requirements for the class armor quests in Thurgadin is that there are three different sets of gems and four different sets of armor.  It is to be noted that beastlords follow the druid armor information.

The three different sets of gems are:
  • "healer" gems (cleric, druid, shaman) 
  • "melee" gems (bard, monk, paladin, ranger, rogue, shadow knight, warrior) 
  • "caster" gems (enchanter, magician, necromancer, wizard) 
The four different sets of armors are:
  • corroded plate armor (bard, cleric, paladin, shadow knight, warrior) 
  • corroded chain armor (ranger, rogue, shaman) 
  • eroded leather armor (druid, monk) 
  • torn enchanted silk armor (enchanter, magician, necromancer, wizard) 
As long as you keep this in mind when a gem or a piece of armor drops, it should be easy to determine who should be allowed to loot the item.

Gems are tradable, but the armor pieces are not.
