Membership Rules

The regulations for membership have not been set in stone yet, and are still in the process of being determined. So far, Grimhold and the officers have decided the following:

This is Grimhold's guild, he has veto on any player status, based on his decision or with the officers' recommendations.

New members must have a main character level 20 or higher.

New member must be recommended by two officers/members or more.

New members must be willing to abide by the terms of our Guild Rules.
