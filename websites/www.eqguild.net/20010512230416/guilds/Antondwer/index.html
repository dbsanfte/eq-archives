April, 18th 2001

I am slowly trying to make this a new home for The Order of Antondwer, but it promising to be a slow process. I will be slowly trying to move over everything from the old site, but I can't promise that it will work. Time will tell : )

In other news, we will be having a welcoming ceremony for new members Sat., the 21st of April, as well as that is the time I will be announcing the names of the people who are going to be running for Guild Council. The elections will be held on April 28th. Please let me know if you are wanting to run by writing me an e-mail at Talanye@eqguild.net.

April, 2nd 2001

Uwei and Gwendilyn's wedding was wonderful, and there are a lot of stories that were written about it in the forums. There is a picture sent in by Thorni that I will try to figure out how to get up here on the board soon, so all can see.

This Saturday, April 7th, we will be having a Guild Meeting at 3pm PST (4pm MST,5pm CST,6pm EST and 11pm GMT). The plan is to have a Welcoming Ceremony for new members and the meeting in Rivervale, and after the meeting, a Guild Raid on Runnyeye. If you are interested in raiding Runnyeye, please contact me (Talanye) in game, or through e-mail Talanye@eqguild.net.

Topics for the meeting will include elections, what positions will be open, and what is needed in order to be able to run for the spots, when the elections will be, and any other topics that are brought to my attention.

March, 19th 2001

This past Saturday we had the Scavenger Hunt. It turned out even better then I had hoped it would, and it seems a fun time was had by all. Thank you everyone who was involved in making it so successful.

This Saturday, the 24th, is Uwei and Gwendilyn's wedding. It will be on top of the Stone Bridge to South Karana at 11pm GMT (which is 6pm EST, 5pm CST, 4pm MST and 3pm PST) If anyone needs help getting to the location, please let someone know, such as Uwei, Gwendilyn, or myself (Talanye), so we can plan for extra time helping you get to the location.

I am hoping to get the Guild Rules up soon, and also the Recruitment Form working, as some people seem to be having difficulty with it. If you are having troubles sending the form, and wish to see about joining the Order of Antondwer, please feel free to either post on the forums the information so we can get hold of you (e-mail or in game), or send me an e-mail at Talanye@eqguild.net
