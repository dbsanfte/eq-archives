What it takes to be a Officer in the guild and a seat on the council.

It is the intent of these rules to help in the development of the Guild where recruitment is important but it will only be one function of the Officers duties, that those newly recruited do not feel left out or left to there own devices. Thus it is the intent to have officers responsible for those they bring into the guild. 

First off Officers in the Dragon of Mist Isle are leaders. According to our guild rules it will be these Officers who will help influence and lead the Guild.

It is the Responsibility of the officers to To help others in the guild when they are having problems and to guide those who are new to the ways of Everquest. whether this be with info or with pointing the guildie in the right direction or lending a hand. to give the members a person to discuss issues with when there are problems so that they can be solved. 

To become a guild officer 

First of a Position must be available either due to growth or retirement of an Officer when said position does become available the High Council will let it be known.

To qualify for said position you must be a full member in good standing, must be nominated by your fellow guild members.

This list will then go in front of the High Council where the candidates will get a chance to present there case as to why they wish the position and why they feel they can serve the position . The High Council will then go into deliberation and will elect the new Officer.

As an officer you are responsible for helping your fellow guildie by pointing them in the right direction by being available for them to bring issues to you or giving advice to help in there improvement, and their development should be paramount, for it will be these very souls that you will be depending on in our various guild party's and raids (with that said it is not the officers or the elder Guild members responsibility to Power level each and every member. If and when one takes the time to do so, remember to say thank you.

it is important to list all members you bring into the guild there class and their levels. as an officer you may be requested to give that information to the council.

If there are any questions please feel free to bring them up at our Guild meetings.
