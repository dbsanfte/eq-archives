It is the deed and the sincere thought that matter more then any title...

Chaos is a guild made for comradeship. We’re here to enjoy game, adventure and have fun. Many of us have several alternate chars so that guild grouping options are more open.  You might say we are the official guild of alts. ; ) Many of us like to spend some of our time role playing, meaning we create histories and personalities for our characters both in game and on the message boards. Role play is not required but everyone will respect those that wish to role play, in and out of guild.

We don’t play to be uber… we are not power gamers... we don’t intend to be the largest guild on game nor the most uber high level guild. Our objective is as stated… to enjoy the game, have fun doing it, and for many of us… to give our characters life by giving them a personality to role play with. Of course, some of us come here to escape. = )

Chaos welcomes characters of all races, classes and levels, the evil, good and somewhere in between. We often partnered with select members of other larger guilds. We group with these folks at times, but never ever expect them to assist anyone of our members for raids, questing, etc. 

Remember, we are not an uber guild, we won’t twink your char or power them, and while some of us may do Planes raids and assist on quests, you won’t see us taking down Moushi or Lady Vox single handed anytime in the near future.

All members will respect the privacy of each other. Several of in Chaos have high lvl primes outside of guild. If you know who someone's prime out of guild is, you WILL NOT disclose that information without that person's permission! Failing this, you will more then likely be removed from guild. Chaos is primarily made up of role players, primes, and several alts of high lvls looking for a break. Respect each other's privacy!!!

We do not allow minors in guild. You heard right, we are an adult guild only. Reasoning for an adult only guild: We discuss adult issues at times and have no desire to be explaining adult issues to minors. This of course does not mean we accept crude behavior or language. We prefer folks to be 20 plus year olds.

We follow Verant’s Rules of Conduct. Chaosians represent Chaos D’Ame at all times, therefore members are expected to uphold the rules and guild’s reputation by being a player of merit.

In addition to Verant’s rules, we have a few considerations:

Item Requests. Chaos is not here to twink your character. Some of our older members may hand down items as they see fit or give items to guild bank for distribution as officers see fit, but don’t expect them to. Don’t ask them to. As a necro friend says, “If you ask or tell me to you can bet I won’t.” On that note, begging is not tolerated, in guild, to guild, outside of guild, to non-guildies, etc, etc.

Power Leveling. We all want to see our chars gain xp (okay well most of us), but like item requests, requests for powering is not acceptable. A few members of guild might get the whimsy to power a guildmate now and then, which is perfectly fine. Just remember, don’t expect to be powered, don’t ask to be powered.

Trade Skills. Many nice items in game are tradesmen made. Chaos has several fine tradesmen. If you are in need of a crafted item, you can make a request (one request for that item to that person) to one of the tradesmen. No tradesmen is required to fill that request however, for whatever reason they give or don’t give. Most often you are expected to pay for the cost of materials and any failures. A guild tradesmen may charge whatever they see fit above these costs. Payment is expected up front. Being a tradesmen is costly in plat and time, please respect that.

Class Abilities. Every class has needed abilities, some more then others. A person with needed spell abilities, be it porting, rezing, buffing, whatever, are not at anyone’s beck and call. While it is expected of all members to assist guildmates, do not expect someone to log off a current char to fetch the one you need, nor to leave a group or other involved situation to assist you. Matters like corpse decay, rez time running short, etc are considered of far more importance then porting you 3 zones away so you can do your thing.

Account Sharing. If you share your account with other people, you are responsible for their actions while playing your Chaosian character!! We cannot state this enough. Poor conduct coming from your char, no matter who it is playing, won't be tolerated. More then likely you will be removed from guild immediately.

 

Chaosian Role Play

Role play adds flavor to the game, which after awhile, gets boring. You’ve hit level 60, are like a god now, nothing you can’t do. Except for get real bored. For some of us, role play brings fun and new adventures to game. Understand that we are NOT a role play guild but we do have role players in guild. A lot in fact. No one is expected to role play. Everyone is expected to respect the choice a player makes as to whether or not to role play their char.

When in role play (in char= IC), remember that not everyone role plays, and someone that does may not be IC at any given moment. This goes for inside and outside of guild. Please do not interrupt role play in action if you are not a role player or are not in char.

Chaos guild chat is most often ooc (out of character) talk, though you will see in char chatter going on, often to everyone’s amusement. When meeting up with a Chaosian, don’t expect them to be in char, but understand that they might be. You don’t have to play along, they don’t have end their role play. Some of us never step out of character. Also know that when hunting as a group, role play rarely happens, especially in group chat. When we hunt we tend to stay ooc (usually because many of us are mega pullers which leaves no time to banter in char). Chaos works well this way because we accept these simple guidelines.

 

Guild Structure

Officers of guild and the GM generally make decisions as a group, however, in case of indecision or possible harm to the rep of guild, the GM, or one of the Ambassadors in GM’s absence, has final authority. Guild officers have been chosen by the GM for their maturity and strength of character. Officers failing to maintain such qualities will be demoted. The chain of command is GM, Officers, Members.

In addition to the title of Officer, some officers carry specific titles. Ambassador (handles inter-guild relations on reputation issues & arranging alliances), Class Leader (leader of their class).

 

Officer Duties

Officers are expected to be on game often, either as their prime in guild char or an alt in guild. (Often being at least 4 times a week… five minutes a time isn’t considered often.) Real life sometimes prevents game time... perfectly understandable. They are expected to be involved with guild matters (hunting with, assisting, use of the msg boards, participating in guild concerns and discussion, suggesting or sponsoring hunts). Since we are primarily a guild of alts, we are not overly active in planned events. At any given time there may be 2 to 15 of us on game, which makes it rather hard to do planned activities.

Officers in guild prime and their in guild alts will be given officer abilities, but only their prime will carry the title of Officer.

It is an officer’s duty to keep an eye out for potential new members… based on the uniqueness of our guild, who might be a good addition to the family. Invite such prospects to visit the guild msg board and to come hunt with us. But absolutely make sure they visit the msg board so they know what we are and what we are not! No minors!! Meaning we prefer 20 plus year olds. While there is no way to be certain of anyone’s real life age, at least ask them.

New members are on a 30 day trial period. The inviting officer must email guild at chaos_dame@yahoo.com within two days of inviting a new member with full char name, race, class, lvl, and trade skill they might wish to offer.
