Charter for Inflaming Wrath

Please be aware that you are carrying the banner of Inflaming Wrath. As such, conduct yourself in a way as to bring pride to the Inflaming Wrath. Remember, your behavior reflects back upon the rest of the guild!

1. Have fun!

_________________________________________________________________________________________________

2. While we do encourage grouping within our Guild we do not require it, nor do we require who hunts with who and when. All we can ask is that when your brother or sister asks for help, if you can provide it, do so, if not, say so.

_________________________________________________________________________________________________

3. Respect and courtesy towards each other is paramount. That means, arguments and disagreements will be held in tells or private modes of communication. This applies in our interactions both in guild and with non-guild individuals. If you can not resolve the disagreement, call in an officer to assist in the mediation. No shouts or ooc arguments across a zone will be tolerated.

_________________________________________________________________________________________________

4. Inflammatory posts towards another member of the Inflaming Wrath upon the Inflaming Wrath’s message board or any other message board will not be tolerated. One of life’s greatest challenges is to talk and negotiate before reacting out of anger. An Inflaming Wrath member strives to resolve conflict before spreading it further.

_________________________________________________________________________________________________

5. Refrain from public bashing of Verant / EQ. There are other venues that are appropriate for these matters.

_________________________________________________________________________________________________

6. The Inflaming Wrath and members of The Inflaming Wrath will not knowingly engage in any activity that is an exploit. To do so will be grounds for guild removal.

_________________________________________________________________________________________________

7. There is no place in our Guild for animosity, pettiness, Kill stealing and the other nastiness one sees in the world, we don't like it in the general populace and we will not tolerate it in our home.

_________________________________________________________________________________________________

8. No use of \anon, you will need to use \role. The use of \role will still hide your Class - Level, while allowing your guild name to be seen.

_________________________________________________________________________________________________

9. Inflaming Wrath members will honor all commitments they have made to other players.

By following these rules of common sense and common courtesy, we will all be able to enjoy the experience of playing Everquest with our online friends to it's fullest.

Thank you!

Kosak
