10 August 2002 After leaving there former guild ‘The Bane of Dragons’, Tiranathon & Aulasiel decide to create their own, and the idea of Council of Morsul was created. GuildAdvertisement:
11 August 2002 The initial guild obtains its first 10 followers and the guild is entered to the Everquest team for approval.
13 August 2002 The guild is officially born as a GM enters the game and tags Tiranathon as the Guild Leader!
14 August 2002 The guilds first alliance is formed with Orbis Amacitia.
15 August 2002 The guild embarks on its first raid in the Estate of Unrest, Joined by some members from Orbis Amacitia.
15 August 2002 Tiranathon promotes Aeristerone as our first guild officer!
15 August 2002 Our member count reaches 30!
23 August 2002 2nd Guild meeting in the glenn behind the druid ring in BB transcript
