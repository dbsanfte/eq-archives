Philosophy

We of Dark Revival  uphold the  philosophy that the ultimate purpose  of Our Guild is to enhance the enjoyment of our Everquest experience through providing membership in a group of friends who will support each others' endeavors as much as possible, protect each other in battle, and have the best interests of its members at heart.  The Guild will provide a means to communicate with each other and to organize events, such as meetings and raids.  In return, the Guild asks that its members conduct themselves in a seemly manner, bringing honor and respect upon all.
