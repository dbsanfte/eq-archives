~~~~~~~~~~Power Of The Storm~~~~~~~~~~

Power of The Storm was created in May 2002. We are a group of online friends that were all searching for the same goal in the land of Norrath. Our goal consists of fairness, peace and understanding with eachother and non guildies. Furthermore to have "Fun" in our travels to Ding 60. Our minimun level to join is level 30. Our waiting period to guild is approximately 3 days. However getting to know our members is best, as we take a guild vote for any and all applicants. Once approved we ask that you treat everyone in the land of Norrath with respect. We ask that when applying that you include your Class ,Level, when available in game and reason for wanting to be apart of the "Power Of The Storm". We will not tolerate KSing, foul behavior, thieving and begging. Please feel free to contact any one of our members with any questions and concerns and an officer will contact you when available.
