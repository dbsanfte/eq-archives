GuildAdvice:
Paludal Caverns

     Do not underestimate the importance of the Paludal Caverns for a level 16-24 player. The ammount of experince that can be had is the easiest and fastest of almost any zone.
GuildAdvice:
Soloing

     Many people hold the belief that the whole reason for playing is to group, and for many it is. But a good soloist can climb levels almost two times faster then with a full group. Good skill to know and valuable for speeding past those levels before you get a valuable skill or spell.
