A Campaigner's Dream


A Dragon's Breath, Troll's Blood and fine wine too.
A crossbow bolt, a sword slash, an enemy cut in two.

A God's wrath, a demon's rage, A vampire's lethal gaze
A wraith's touch, a fighter's punch, experience gained in a drunken haze

A lucky roll for double damage, your weapon hits the floor
A wizard's breath as the fire consumes or lightning shatters the door.

A faithful cleric's prayer, answered in the blink of an eye
A thief stealing an item while the victim walks on by

a magic weapon, a magic spell, a hunt for treasure dear
a massive fight, a simple duel, the smell of ash and fear

Armor, weapons, friends and enemies, All we find and face
Hills, mountains, plains and seas, Oh, where the hell is this place?

Villages and towns, Castles and caers, Oh the people we will see
Roads and paths, trails and caves, Oh where the fuck can it be?

Arrows whistling through the air, Bodies litter the ground
Blood and guts spewing forth, Stacking the dead in a mound

Driven by a promise, Of fortune, fame or more
We bravely go forth into battle, To face the blood and gore

Bound by an honor, That won’t let us fail
And the feats we accomplished, Shared over warm ale

oh far and away our minds will go, as we sit and play
oh we miss the glory, of those happy days.
