Omerta Info

Omerta is an Everquest guild on the Xev server. The Omerta was one of the principles the Sicilian Mafia operated on, a code of silence. Why this applies to an Everquest guild we haven't figured out yet either =/. For a bit of background on the Omerta check out this website.

The guild exists to provide its members with new challenges in EQ, working towards defeating ever more difficult encounters through teamwork and strategy. We recruit our members selectively operating on the ideas of quality over quantity. Everquest is ultimately just a game, and Omerta is out to have some fun playing it.

On this website you should find all the information you need about the guild, news, members, joining, and rules. Most of our communication is done through our EZBoard forum located here.
