Champions Of The Round will be an honest fun-seeking guild. We are all out to the help one another and others around us. We will have guild raids and other events that hopefully everyone will assist with and participate in .We are here to protect Norrath and the citizens of it. It will take all of us working together with cooperation to make a better life for us and everyone in Norrath. Friends are one of the most important things in this guild and I hope we all become very good ones.

 

 

1-The guild comes first unless you are already preoccupied.

2-Treat others with respect and decency

3-Take full responsibility for your actions and language.

4-Be completely truthful and honest at all times.

5-Protect yourself and everyone around you if possible.

6-Obey the rules of Norrath at all times.

7-Always avenge those who have perished in the field of battle.

8-And just be yourself.

 

 

1-If you violate any of the said above.

2-If you use profanity in guild chat or any other chats.

3-If you use sexual talk to anyone that is not comfortable with it, even though its not suggested anyway. That will be decided by the whole guild and not just the officers to decide on if you were out of line or not.

4-Misuse of the guilds name, functions or items.

5-Intentionaly killing, stealing or ninja looting from another individual, guild member or not.

6-Going anonymous for reasons that you cant explain to the guild.

7-If you give out any of the guilds passwords that people are not allowed to have or use.

 

 

These actions below will be taken if any of the rules above are broken:

 

First offense – You will receive a warning from the leader or an officer.

Second offense – You will be notified and so will the GM’s.

Third offense – You will be notified again and so will the GM’s and you will be suspended for 1 month.

Fourth offense – The guild leader and the officers will have a meeting determining weather you will stay with the guild or not this will be an open hearing to everyone if u would like.

Fifth offense – This would be you’re last offense and you’re automatically booted from the guild.

 

 

Ok the bottom line…….

 

We are all on here to have fun .All of us have real lives and problems in them.  Please don’t bring them to the game if you’re angry. We will always be here to listen anyone’s problems if they want to discuss them. Please don’t break the rules if you would like to join this guild. We are all exceptional people and very understanding. We all need a break from working, school and just life. This is one way to take a break, please lets work together and help one another. I’m sure that this will be a great guild, it will be like family.
