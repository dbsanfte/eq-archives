Guild Origins


Legend Urbana is the result of a group of friends from two message board communities (The Straight Dope Message Board and The Fabulous Forums of Fathom) deciding to play together on one server. Most of the members were already EQ players, scattered on various servers at various levels, but we wanted to be able to play together. We chose a server at random and ended up with Druzzil Ro, where we started new characters and began to play together. A few people from our communities were interested enough in the game that they bought it and began playing just to play with us in our little guild.
