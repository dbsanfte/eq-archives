Welcome New Members

Here is all the info a new member needs to set up and use our website. If you are not yet a member please contact an Autumn's Twilight officer in game to get tagged.

Go here to get an email account. Unless you already have an email account you wish to use. Then send me, tyga@eqguild.net, an email requesting the password to the members section on EZ Boards. Include your characters name and I will send you the password.You also need to register with ezboards to post so do that here. I suggest a "local" account with you characters name if you can't get the "global" account. This is the same board system used by many EQ websites so if you already have an account your set. Then please post an introduction message and your email address will be added to our database.
Last you need to go here and add you char to our guild roster. Make sure you select Autumn's Twilight for your guild!

Thats it! Welcome to Autumn's Twilight. Enjoy.
