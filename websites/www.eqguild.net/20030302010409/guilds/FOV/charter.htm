The Guild Charter of the Fellowship of Vazaelle

 

Salutations to the loyal members of the Fellowship of Vazaelle.

By this Agreement know that we citizens of Norrath and Luclin, being Barbarians, Dark Elves, Dwarves, Erudites, Gnomes, Half Elves, Halflings, High Elves, Humans, Iksars, Orgres, Trolls, Wood Elves, and Vah Shir, bound by the highest oath of honor for us and our future heirs until the end of time to these virtues:

  • Integrity - Earning the trust of others through responsible and honorable actions, good judgement, and genuine character.
  • Service - Supporting the growth of newer members and meeting or exceeding the needs of all that matter to us.
  • Achievement – Growing in one's profession and trade skills through the exploration of Norrath and Luclin.
  • Cooperation – Pursuing creatures with two or more members with each doing a part, humbling one self's ego for the benefit and success of the whole team.

As bound to follow these virtues, the members of the Fellowship of Vazaelle have certain and undeniable rights; regardless of affiliation, or lack there of, with a given deity.

  • Members of the guild are free to explore the width and breadth of Norrath and Luclin by land or by sea, save for a time of war. However, we find the sailing vessels of Norrath to be a hated means of transport and deemed untrustworthy a way to travel.
  • There will be no dues or fees imposed on guild members.
  • From this day forward, all members of The Fellowship have the right to vote and have their voice heard by all.

Guild Officers are individuals in the Fellowship who maintain leadership qualities and have been promoted in good faith by the Guild Leader and High Council, based on their achievements, attitude and loyalty to the Fellowship. As with aforementioned rights and liberties, the following is expected from the officers that were commisioned that position :

  • To uphold the virtues and be an excellent example of what a member of the Fellowship of Vazaelle should be and not abuse the powers of their post, for they are ambassadors of the Fellowship to the worlds of Norrath and Luclin.
  • To be accessable to all members of the Fellowship for assistance and advice within reason.
  • To carry out the will of the Guild Leader and the High Council.
  • To attend the meetings that the Guild Leader or High Council schedules to resolve challenges that face the Fellowship, being an active participant at the meetings, and to carry out the resolutions of the Guild Leader and the High Council.
  • To grant membership to a citizen of Norrath and Luclin provided that the citizen is of solid character meeting the requirements of joining the Fellowship, for the first three weeks being a mentor for the one they guilded in, ready to address and assist their charge with any challenges that arises in the new guild member's way.
  • To be on time and assist the High Council on any Fellowship functions which require their attendance, including raids where they may take charge as group leaders or need to watch over a group who are lower level to themselves, insuring their safety and success.

As with the Guild Officers, the following can be expected from the officers commisioned by the Guild Leader to a position in the High Council:

  • To meet and uphold the expecations of the Guild Officers.
  • To resolve the challenges that face the Fellowship and make the decisions for the overall good of the guild.
  • To carry out the will of the Guild Leader.

This charter was produced in good faith by the hand of Hibereas Homorousmelody.
