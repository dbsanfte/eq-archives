Raid Readiness - Assisting

How do I assist you ask?

Thank you for that question. You assist by using a "social" key you constructed before you arrived at the raid, and then dragged to your hotkey panel. Some macro (social button) examples follow:
/target <secondary assist name>
/target <primary assist name>
/g Assisting %T.
/assist 		/target <MA>
/assist <MA>
/assist off

When do I assist?

When you are instructed to by the main tank. The job of the main tank is to get the mob only hitting on him/herself and solely focused on killing the main tank. (we won't let the mob succeed, but they don't know that).

Once agro is established the Main Tank (MT) also called Main Assist (MA) will call out in the raid channel "Please assist me in killing [mob name]", at which point you type the number of your handy <assist> hotkey and begin doing whatever your class does best to the mob (or your party members), at a pace that will not get the mob's agro on yourself.
