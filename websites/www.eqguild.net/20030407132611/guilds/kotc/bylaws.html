Bylaws of Our Order

1) Officer status shall have a minimum level requirement of 40, as well as whatever qualities the server guild leader is looking for. Characters promoted prior to 7/1/02 are exempt from this requirement. Individual servers may have a more, or less, restrictive level requirement based on that server's needs.


2) The Knights of the Covenant have no class or race requirements to be invited; new members, however, must be a minimum of 30th level. Real Life friends, as well as alternate characters of an existing members, are exempt from this requirement.


3) The guild does NOT require it's members to 'twink' or 'powerlevel' each other upon demand. There is rarely a problem with members sharing equipment or investing some time to buff and heal other members. Nor does the guild require it's members to sell hard won loot at below market value. The guild DOES require that you ask a fair market price, do not gouge your fellow Knights for profit.


4) The guild requires all main and alt characters to be registered with Guildmagic.com. Guildmagic is a guild management tool that everyone can benefit from, but that means that all Knights must participate for it to be effective. Each server has its own Guild Magic setup. New member invitees have 7 days from joining to register with Guild Magic. Existing members that wish to have alternates invited must have registered the alt that same day. Members may wish to have their characters listed as Anonymous if they wish to keep their character info private.


5) All differences between Knights are expected to be handled in a courteous fashion. Failure to do so will result in both parties being placed on probation, with a second violation in 180 days resulting in being removed from the guild. It is highly recommended that you seek out an Officer to mediate your problem to avoid such circumstances. If the actions of a member is deemed to be sever enough to consider disbandment, it shall require three Officers to agree to this action. Each of the Officers must submit an email to the Guild's Leader (for that server) detailing his/her reasons for disbandment of said member. The server Guild Leader reserves the right to Veto the disbandment.


6) The Knights of the Covenant respect our members' right to privacy. We respectfully ask that when a Knight wishes to not display class and/or level that they use /roleplay instead of /anon. The reason is that /roleplay hides the information, but still shows the guild tag. Knights in /roleplay will also show up when other members use the /who GUILD all command, where /anon does not.
