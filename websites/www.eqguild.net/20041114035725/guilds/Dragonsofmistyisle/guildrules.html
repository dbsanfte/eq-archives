Guild Rules

1st Edition

Introduction :

  

Hello we are a New guild barely 3 weeks old much of our time has been in setting up the guild and establishing members but now I feel its time to get down to meeting people and start working to better the guild.

General Rules ~ These are rules and as such always subject to change

 

1.  No cussing in guild chat OR official raids... simply no need for it.  as there are minors in the guild Words like Damn and Crap are NOT considered cusswords, but I am sure you can figure them out.

 

2.  No conversations of sexual orientation in guild chat OR official raids.  That is childish, send your friends a tell and laugh there, not by disrupting and offending others, who may not welcome such talk.

 

3.  NO ripping off guildies.  I am not saying port guildies for free (though it would be nice), but I am saying like 10pp or 20pp... don't ask for 50pp.  Basically don't overcharge for services rendered... try to cut your fellow guildies some slack, but I know everyone needs money, so offering services at modest prices is acceptable.

 

4.  No BEGGING for stuff in guild chat.  That includes Power Leveling.  A good approach to this rule is to ask to pay for something like "does anyone have some bronze armor for sale".  In most cases some guildie will cough up the item at a discounted price or just give it to you.  But, it comes across a heck of a lot nicer than give me this or that.  I WILL warn onetime violators and will kick if it they do not stop. Most of us hate begging but don’t mind helping those in need.

 

5. Guild preference and Camping rules

First give your Fellow guild member Preference in Groups when at all possible. 

Though this should be covered under rule 4,  we will discuss it here Camping or Farming rules  Please don't ask those members of Elder Status to Leave there group to camp something for you or check to see if said camp is open. (I mean its one thing if they are is the same zone and you ask them to do a Camp check and another to ask them to leave there group go check on camp and hold it till you show up) the rule is you want to Farm an item Great go to the camp once your at Camp then ask for help most don’t mind helping but we are not going to farm the item for you.  the end result is this we are there to help you guide you and point you in the right direction but in the end its up to you to help your self as well. (and a good rule of thumb the more you work your self the more others are willing to help).

 

6.  Bringing friends to raids:

This is a touchy situation sometimes. you are an officer or can get an officer to "sponsor" your friend, you can bring friends to a raid.  But, by sponsoring or bringing a person to a raid you are responsible for their actions, like ninja looting, spamming guild raid channel or just not listening or getting along with guildies at raid.  If problems occur they can and will be asked to leave.  Also take note that under the guild loot rules section, Friends will not be able to acquire items listed under rule 2 > NBG items.  All other items they can.

Guild Raid Loot Rules :

 

Our guild loot rules have will go through changes and I am well aware as we get bigger they may need to be changed. Through discussions with the other officers the loot system will thus follow these guidelines

 

Guild officers and Raid group leaders will always do the looting, and will divvy up the spools at end of Raid.

  

1.  Need before greed, thus if a item is for a specific class and you are on the Raid you will have first option on the item.

 

2. After need items will be randomly rolled for with a seed of 678

Raid Leaders will roll twice once for them selves then they will announce the second roll to be for guild coffers, the second roll is always for the Guild.

 

3.  Any PP that is earned will be divided evenly with a full share going to the guild Coffers

 

4.  Agene All Quest oriented, Epic oriented, NO Drop OR Spells are Need Before Greed (NBG) meaning ONLY people that can use can roll for item. Please be honest to the guild and yourself on these items, because these are the items that will make the guild stronger as a whole if the right and more deserving people get them.  (On Epic items, no one under the level 50 can win UNLESS there are no 50+ on the raid.  On quest items, ONLY people high enough to do quest may roll on that item.  Kael armor for instance is level 45 to quest.) to be implemented at later date.

 

3. non guildies present on raids can not roll on or receive items listed in rule 4, though all else they can.  This is because we raid to better our guild's citizens, NOT the world of Norrath.  Most items that drop that we consider NBG items were the ones we were after in the 1st place. (However exceptions can be made if announced in advanced of leaving for raid for non guildies who repeatedly help the guild.

 

On raids with major drops where class specific no drop stuff drops, like in kael, and are semi rare, you can only win one piece until all of that armor class type present have each won a piece. After each has 1 piece you can win as much as you have luck to roll. An example would be in Solb kings room a RMB, a 2nd one can not be won till all others have one a RMB however other Items can be rolled on as per our normal rules (The guild will be allowed its 1 specific item as well item as well)

 

4. Split will be off and All Coin goes to guild coffers on official raids to be split after the raid. (donations are always welcome)

 

5.  All minor gems and trinkets go to clerics (and wizards if planes raid) to cover symbol gems used during the raid.
