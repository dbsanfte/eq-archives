How to join :


ANY officer with Legacy of the Lost is able to invite anyone that they feel would be a good candidate to join us. So you, at any time, are welcome to send a tell to our officers. You can figure out who is an officer by checking the guild roster (linked above.) we do require that you have looked into our guild by reading our charter(s) and reading some of the posts in the "introduce yourself" forum. This guild has a purpose. It makes no sense for anyone to join a group of people they know nothing about. Our chater is also our ruleset - So everything you need to know (besides the people) are there.

I personally suggest that once you've looked over everything, and decided that we look like what you want - that you post something on the "join the legacy" forum. The instructions as to what you are to post are listed there, on the board.
