GUILD CHARTER
FOR
SHIELD OF HONOR

Guild Start Date: 00 / 00 / 02

The Shield of Honor is a guild dedicated to the principles of Honor, Justice, Fair-Dealing, Equality, and having a damned good time, all in equal measure. This means, in brief, that Members agree to deal honestly and fairly with everyone they encounter, not just other members, and will do their utmost to see that no injustice prevails--while at the same time being as friendly and polite as possible. This includes helping other travelers in whatever way they can and to whatever extent seems necessary, as well as striving to make the EQ experience a pleasure for all..

With these precepts in mind, we submit the following Charter to the Game Masters of EverQuest.

Article I
Members are required to help any who are in need assistance, even if they are not a Member of the Shield of Honor or its daughter Guilds. While this aid would in theory be rendered regardless of class, level, alignment, race or location, it is recognized as a fact of life that some fellow travelers of opposing alignments will be objectionable to individual members, and that some locations simply cannot be entered by certain races or alignments. So stipulating, help may take any form that an individual member chooses, based on the situation at hand.

Article II
Respect for naturally created life and the works of the gods. All creatures not obviously deformed, twisted, tainted, or otherwise unnatural deserve as much respect and fair treatment as they will allow themselves to be shown. Specifically, this means never attacking natural creatures first but being allowed to kill in self defense. If a natural beast attacks a member of this guild, it is within a members' rights to defend themselves, even if it means killing the creature. What creatures are deemed natural is up to the individual member and his or her gods, but is also subject to definition by the Guild Council. As an addendum, the Guild Council realizes that some Members have professions that require the sacrifice of natural creatures (tailors and such) and asks only that the Members exercise restraint and proper respect when harvesting wild creatures.

Article III
The Guild does not accept applicants who are Evil. This does not mean, however, that there can be no Members whose cultural upbringing and racial bias inclines them to evil; the gods know that anyone who truly wishes to can change, and this Guild believes that every being deserves a chance to do so. There will, however, be no evil actions or expressions by members of the Guild, whatever their apparent alignment might be. If, as an example, a Dark Elf Shadow Knight were to desire membership and swear to refrain from all evil acts and associations, the Guild could accept him on those conditions. If the Dark Elf were to renege, engaging in evil actions and/or expressions, he would then be expelled after a consultation of officers and members and final confirmation by the Council.

Article IIII
Following the rules of EverQuest, written, unwritten, and otherwise. If there is a doubt as to what these rules are, the Guild Council will have the final ruling.
