Interested in joining our Heroes?


A few guidelines we have for our guild.  These are things that I disliked about the last few guilds I was in, so I decided that I would make my own guild and fix the things that I saw as wrong.

Guideline #1: Officers and Leader (Kortap) receive no special treatment.  I would not want people bowing before them like they are kings.  They are just normal people.  They just have the privilege of being officers.

Guideline #2:  We do not use the /anon command to go anonymous.  We use the /roleplay command so that all can still see the guild behind your name.  I figure what's the point in being in the guild if you are going to hide that fact from everyone else.  We need to advertise!!!

Guideline #3:  All local problems and issues stay in local chat.  No spamming the guildchat with useless prattle that should stay within the group you are in or the person you are talking to.  Guildchat is for talking to all of your fellow guild members.  Treat them with respect by not overusing guildchat.

Guideline #4:  We have among our members some skilled traders.  If you wish to purchase something from them, you pay for the materials (unless of course they offer it for free).  They donate their time, don't make them donate their money as well.  DONATIONS ARE NOT ACCEPTABLE!!

Guideline #5:  We do not remove members often, but if we need to, a vote of all people in the guild who are online at the time decides your fate.  Majority wins.

Guideline #6:  Everyone's opinion in this guild matters.  I take all suggestions to heart before I act on them.  So if you ever have a suggestion, please don't hesitate to talk to me.
 

We offer a 15 day trial period for new recruits.  During those 15 days you must group with one of our members so that we can get a feel for how you play.  If you decide during that time that this is not the guild for you, you can leave at any time with no problems.  If you stay, and we like you, then you are a full fledged member.  I am also very liberal with my officerships, so don't rule out being one!
 

Still interested?  Then email me (Kortap) at the link below, and I'll see about getting you in.  Please be sure to include your in game name, times that you will be on and your hunting locations so I can set something up.  Also include level, class and race for my reference.
