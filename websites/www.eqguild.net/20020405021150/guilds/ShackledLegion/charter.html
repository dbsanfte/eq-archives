The Shackled Legion, so named because we consider monks to be the true warriors of Cabilis, was created as a roleplaying guild. Beyond roleplaying, the purpose of this guild is to serve as an ingame forum for iksar monks, where they can ask each other for help, suggestions, and advice. While members are encouraged to help each other, this does not mean they are obligated to powerlevel, give out items for free, or provide loans. 

We exist to spread the world of Cazic-Thule on Norrath.  Even though Cazic-Thule is an evil god, we expect members of the guild to obey the general etiquette of the game.  In other words, don't kill steal somebody and run away screaming "For Thule!", as this will cause people to hate our guild out of character, and we don't need that kind of grief.
