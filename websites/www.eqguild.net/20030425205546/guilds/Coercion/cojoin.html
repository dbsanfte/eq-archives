[ Expectations ]

If you can only play a couple hours a night or only on weekends, this is realistically not enough. There are Guilds available for casual players, again we are not one of those.

If your Internet connection is not reliable, then we can't rely on you. With the way the classes depend on each other, a critical person going LD at a key time can mean failure for a whole raid. While we are certainly aware that occasional "Timeout and Link-deaths" are par for Internet games, constant and consistent ones are a cause to look for a better ISP!

Being a mainly raid based guild, we encourage you in the future to acquire keys to the following zones/areas: OS and HS. It is also recommended that you have completed Veeshans Peak key quest at some point along with everyone else. These keys are mainly just a bit of tedium to get, and having them will make your life much better whether or not you join the guild.

Know your way around! Train some points into that Sense Heading skill! And then work on it! Particularly when we are exploring new areas, a sense of direction is important.

You must have all expansions. Our travels regularly take us to all the known continents. If you need me to tell you that "All Expansions" means

  • Ruins of Kunark
  • Scars of Velious
  • Shadows of Luclin

2boxing or 3boxing or however many you like to play is fine, but NOT on raids!

[ Current Recruitment ]

  • Meet Level Requirements
  • Pass the time of the one week trial
  • Get 10+ yes votes
  • Cannot receive more than three no votes.

After the said person meets the level requirements for their particular class, the person will then have a one week trial where they are tagged, so they can more easily group with guild members and get to know everyone.

After the person is temporarily tagged for one week, that person must pass a vote held in the Recruitment Forum. This is determined by recieving at least 10 "yes" votes, and no more than 3 "no" votes.
