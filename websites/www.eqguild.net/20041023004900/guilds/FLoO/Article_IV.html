Article IV. ~ Promotion from Conscript to Centurion

Sec a) you must attempt to participate in guild events whenever possible.

Sec b) Recruitment of potential new Legionnaires:

1. While at the level of Conscript you can invite people you group with, meet, or otherwise know into our guild.

Sec c) Promotion of the Foreign Legion of Odus:

1. Whenever the opportunity presents itself, spread the word of the Legion. Always conduct yourself in a manner that is befitting of our guild.

Sec d) Lead by example:

1. In terms of being willing to assist others within the guild when possible, and helping to guide and inform new members in terms of training and learning about our guild.

Sec e) by following the chain of command, in turn, once you are promoted, the respect you gave to your superiors will then be revisited upon you by your insubordinates.

Sec f) for your character that is a Conscript to become a centurion, you must be registered at guildmagic.com

Sec g) in game please do not use /anon but rather /roleplay.

(Both conceal your identity, but /anon makes your guild tag disappear, and keeps other guild members from knowing that you are online. You may also elect to use neither and just remain known to all.)
