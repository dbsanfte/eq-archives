Welcome To The Guild Armory
 
Long ago one of my fellow members (sadly, I cannot remember who) asked if we had a guild armory.  Not knowing what that was, I asked him.  He told me that another guild he was in had a collection of weapons and armor that the people in the guild both contributed to and took from for the betterment of the gear of the guild.  I thought that it was  good idea, but without the website it would have been impossible to manage.  Now that we have a website, I am going to start putting things up here that are available to the guild for use.  If you have anything, please email me and let me know.  I will try to get a form or something up, but in the meantime just send it to me with a pic if you can get it (just take a screenshot of it while you are looking at it).
