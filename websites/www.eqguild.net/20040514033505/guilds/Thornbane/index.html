The noun "thorn" has 3 senses in WordNet.

1. irritant, thorn -- (something that causes irritation and annoyance; "he's a thorn in my flesh")
2. spine, thorn, prickle, pricker, sticker -- (a sharp-pointed tip on a stem or leaf)
3. thorn -- (a Germanic character of runic origin)

The word Thorn has also been used to describe both demons, and dragons in different languages throughout history. For "us" the word Thorn is a combination of all of these things, and more.
