Guild Structure

Council
The Council is made up of senior officers. Senior officers and Council members will be defined as those who have been officers since the conception of the guild or have served at least four months of faithful service. These Council members include: Xalik/Azarak, Silverrunner, Wayrepth, Duun, Arvella, and Xaevier

Officer
Officers will be in charge of keeping order amongst the ranks. They will oversee all Aegis of Unity tasks including quests, raids, and guild events. These officers include: Qandar, Burck, Denikar, Nustram,Skunkeen, Sglanton, Enamy, and Saaladin.

 

Mentor
A member who has agreed to helping out a lower level member of the guild (must be 50+).

Member
Everyone else currently aligned with Aegis of Unity

Recruit
Members with guild tag who have not been with us for over 2-weeks or people without tag that are raiding/grouping with us because they would like to join Aegis of Unity.

Refer to the Roster for more information.

Officer Responsibilities

Spell Librarian
Qandar Said officer(s) shall be keeper of all spells and is responsible for buying and selling of spells as needed. This includes spell research components like runes, words, and pages etc. Only spells which benefit the guild not the individual will be purchased buy the guild. For example, Wizard/Druid Evacuate, Cleric spell group symbol, Mage spell Call of the Hero. These spells benefit all during raids. Solo spells like pets and
regular healing spells must be purchased or acquired by the player.

Guild Ambassador
Enamy Said officer(s) shall represent our guild to other guilds and be responsible for handling conflicts when they arise with other guilds. Any alliances will be handled by this officer as well.

Recruitment Cordinator(s)
Rideinos/Duun, Arvella,and Enamy Said officer(s) shall handle the invites of new guild members. Oversee the balance of the classes. Ensure potential new members are educated on guild policy, pass the background check, and meet said requirements for membership. RECRUITMENT OF OUR FRIENDS IS EVERYONE JOB!

Epic Cordinators
Xaevier,Stacis and Skunkeen Said officer(s) will be responsible for facilitating epic quests and raids. Only members level 50+ will receive formal guild support for their epic, all of which must be arranged by said officer. Lorissa has stepped up and offered to help in with this responsibility.

Keeper of the Gems
Arvella Said officer(s) will maintain a supply in sets of all gems used in the Velious class specific quest armor. Said officer will work with raid officer to ensure an ongoing supply of these gems.

 


Raid Planners
Wayrepth and Saaladin Said officer(s) will be responsible for scheduling raids and shall work with the Epic Coordinator such that there are no overlaps when planning raids. Guild will FORMALLY raid twice per week. All requests for raids need to be handled via our website and directed to the appropriate officer. Raids will only be planned for members over level 40. Raids = events that require more than one group.

Guild Banker
Azarak Said officer(s) will be responsible for buying and selling of items via posting on guild and server websites.

Webmaster
Silverrunner and Sglanton Said officer(s) will be responsible for maintaining and updating the guild website.

Resist Gear Cordinator
Denikar Said officer(s) will be responsible for maintaining gem drops and items for resist gear and assisting members over 50 with making their resist gear using gems provided by the member or obtained during guild raids. Excess supplies may be sold to purchase supplies that are in short.

Records Keeper
Sglanton Said officer(s) will be responsible for maintaining records on Attendance and Loot acquired during guild events. Also, said officer(s) will maintain a members list. The member's list will contain: main character (race, level, class, whether or not they have obtained their epic, which keys they have, and same information for alts.

Raid Leader
Xalik Said officer(s) will be responsible for making groups and setting strategy during raids. Raid Leader will also be assigning loot based on our loot system.

Guild Advisor
Denikar Said officer(s) will be responsible for the Mentor program. Said officer(s) will contact all mentor/apprentices weekly and track their progress. Said officer(s) will also match up mentors with lower level members of the guild.
