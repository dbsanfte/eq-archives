Rank Structure

Master/Mistress of the Covenant: The Guild Leader, selected to give the Guild direction. This person finalizes Bylaws for the guild, can recruit new Members, and create new Officers.


Battle Leader: This Officer is selected by the Master/Mistress of the Covenant to perform guild raid/quest event organization and leadership. There is only one Battle Leader. The Battle Leader will assume current Guild leadership in the absence of the Master/Mistress of the Covenant.

Knight Commander: Officers selected by the Master/Mistress of the Covenant to perform the day-to-day running of the Guild and recruit new Members. There is only one Knight Commander per character class. The highest level Knight Commander logged on will assume current Guild leadership in the absence of the Master/Mistress of the Covenant.


Knight: Knights are non-Officer members that fall into the level range of 51-60. The highest level Knight shall assume Guild leadership in the absence of higher authority.


Captain: Captains are non-Officer members that fall into the level range of 41-50. The highest level Captain shall assume Guild leadership in the absence of higher authority.


Lieutenant: Lieutenants are non-Officer members that fall into the level range of 31-40. The highest level Lieutenant shall assume Guild leadership in the absence of higher authority.


Sergeant: Sergeants are non-Officer members that fall into the level range of 21-30. The highest level Sergeant shall assume Guild leadership in the absence of higher authority.


Squire: Squires are non-Officer members that fall into the level range of 11-20. The highest level Squire shall assume Guild leadership in the absence of higher authority.


Page: Pages are non-Officer members that fall into the level range of 1-10. The highest level Page shall assume Guild leadership in the absence of higher authority.
