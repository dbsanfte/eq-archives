RAID INFORMATION
If you have an idea for a raid, want a specific item, or if you want to sponsor a raid (i.e. be the raid leader), send the information to Ankalagon (Ankalagon@eqguilds.net) for scheduling and posting. Please provide as much information including at least two possible times you want to schedule the raid so that if it conflicts with another raid, one of the raids can be reschedule without difficulty. Send raid requests at least one week before the raid (we can fudge on this a bit until we get things running more smoothly). For all raids, RVSP to the raid leader as soon as possible. Include which character you plan on bringing and their level and class. Ex. Ankalagon, Druid, 48. If you are going to arrive late, please also send the time you plan on arriving. With all raids, make arrangements to get to the rendezvous BEFORE the raid begins. Once the raid has begun, no one will be available to come teleport/retreive you. We have had several bad experience when someone from the raid group leaves to go get a late arrival.
