Requirements
First, a minimum level of 10th. Those who are members before that date will be retained regardless of their level.

Second, a new sister must find a nontwinked newbie (level 1 to 5) and give them a gift of a nice item from their own inventory, stating as they give the gift that the gift is from the Sisterhood of Power.

Third, a new sister must send an email to the guildleader, Edie, with their Name, Level, Race, Class and any Special Skills they have.
