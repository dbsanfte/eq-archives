Coercion will be a high end guild located on KB. We have dedicated and motivated team members who all share one common goal, and that is the high end game. We are a raiding guild. If you are a casual player, I am sure there will be guilds of that kind, this however is not one of them.

[Who are we?]

Coercion will be one of the first guilds on its home server of Kane Bayle, and counts among its members many of the most well known players on the server. As a guild we strive to do, well everything. It is our intent to do server firsts and we intend to keep it that way.

[ What we are looking for? ]

Above all we are looking for people who can play this game and their chosen class at a level well above average. We want people who don't use the anonymity of the internet as an excuse to act like jerks in the game. We want people we can depend on. And last but not by any means least, we want people we like!
