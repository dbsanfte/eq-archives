The Raid on Beholder's Maze

December 28, 2002

Having recently completed a very succesful exploration of the ruins of Befallen, the Foreign Legion searched for a more formidable opponent. Thus we headed to the minotaur infested chasms and valleys of Beholder's Maze. The following were in attendance:

Adanya
Adenia
Aegnar

Gaereth

After getting bored with the trivial task of slaying Muddites and Minotaurs, our force headed to the throne area of King Xorb. Here we encountered a little more challenge with the Evil Eyes and unfortunately had to dispose of Liwanu's pet as it was charmed and atacking the group.
