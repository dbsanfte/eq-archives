What are Councils?


'Councils' is the word we use for the groups of officers / members that handle certain matters within the guild. We have these 'councils' organized so that things run more smoothly, and so that YOU know WHO to contact when you need something done. (Got a good idea for an event? Tell the event council leader!)

Council members are reminded to *ALWAYS* check things over with your leader before you make any big decisions.
Guild Relations


This council is in charge of anything outside the guild. Forming guild alliances and declaring guild wars.

Leader: Devlyn*
Assistants: Barretta, Villana, Berkeley, Tarash

Member Relations


This council is in charge of handling any issues within the guild, and between it's members. If you're not getting what you want out of us, or if you have a conflict with another member (or officer), we encourage you to contact on of the MRO's (preferably whomever you are most comfortable with) as soon as you possibly can. Please DO NOT handle conflicts yourself unless you can do so civilly and respectfully.

Leader: Tarash
Assistants: Barretta, Tanaquil

Events

This council is in charge of planning, scheduling, and hosting any event that is not a raid, guild-war, or recruiting event. Such as parties, PvP, role-playing events, scavenger hunts, festivals, lottos, and charity events. Please feel free to contact the event council staff if you would like something in specific planned, or if you have any ideas!

Leader: Villana (Dawnlight)
Assistants: Berkeley, Tanaquil, Georgina
Raids

This is the council that is in charge of planning and leading our raids. Please get permission to lead a raid from the council leader before you post it and make it official. If you need a raid lead for a quest or anything like that, go ahead and send one of the council members a tell!

Want to join / add / remove yourself ?


You are welcome to remove yourself or add yourself to assist a council. If you are NOT a officer, you MUST have approval from the council leader. If you are a new officer, please tell Tanaquil or Nazzie in game which council you'd like to join, and she'll set it up for you.

Be SURE to converse on the forums provided. Contact Tandie or Tanaquil of you are having a problem that the said councils can't handle. Thank you for your time!
