NToV and other scary Mofos Wednesday October 09th 2002



Well its been a while since my last update but we have certainly been busy. We had our first shot at the Velious end game in the Forum of the North Temple of Veeshan. Our start was delayed a day or so due to unexpected patches and the such but 50 odd intrepid Silent Watchers made our way into the hallowed halls of Veeshan.

First up was Ikatiar the Venom, after some spectacular pulling from our monk team we got the beast solo and promtly kicked his head in. Not bad for only our second shot at the beast


Congratulations go to:
Tulgin on his Ancient Wyvern Hide Leggings
Damaddil on his Mask of Venom 
and an Applicant on his Ikatiar's Stinger 

Next up was Eashen of the Sky who we had attempted earlier that week but due to dwindling numbers on the raid we had failed to kill. However full raid force = dead Eashen, another first for TSW /cheer.


Congratulations go to:
Gindian on his shiny new Ring of Sky
Talrasus on his Onyx Chain Sleeves

Now we came to the real guardian of NToV Aaryonar.


We had many plans for killing Aary my favorite being wedge the git in a doorway and kick him in the nuts hehe. But we the repopping hatchlings continually gave us hassle and unfortunately this was where our NToV adventure came to a halt after many attempts on the beast exhaustion finally took over and we had to call it a night.

But suffice it to say Aary will be meeting us again and next time he is toast!

We have also been killing Cazic, Gozz, Telk, Rumblecrush, Va'Dynn, Vindicator and The Dain we have also completed many epics.

Congratulations go to:
Bruenorr, Malloc, Aven, Pirmin and Yorethel on their new Click Sticks 
Damaddil on his Singing Short Sword
Terellion on his shiny new Claws
And Tomh on his Spear of Fate

Submitted by: Althalonas Cazicbane



Hell of a busy Week! Sunday September 8th 2002



Well its been a hell of a busy week we've had some good luck and some bad. But for the most part all the patches have given us alot of fun. We have killed a few old mobs and some new.

The biggest kill of the week has to be the first time slaying of Dain Frostreaver IV! Our first ever attempt on the punk and he went down faster that Mega on Rezam!


Congratulations go to:
Tarval on Frostreaver's Head
Humil on his new Frostreaver
Darkivy on her new Frostreaver's Embroided Cloak
Daldur on his Ring of Winter

Also with the changes in the Cleric epic we took the time to finish up a few epcis. Grats to Aven and Pirmin. Thanks to a clever Necro this was the best shot I got heh.


We finished up the week by doing the 10th Ring War for Jerusalem. It was a hard fight at times but with team work we kicked giant butt. But as bad luck would have it the zone crashed just as we finished and although Jerusalem still had the ring and Narandi's Crown, all the other rewards got eaten. Yay Verant!


Congratulations go to:
Jerusalem on his shiny new Ring of Dain Frostreaver IV
Terellion Lucky Dice (hehe) on his Crown of Narandi

Also during the week we took down Derakor the Vindicator, Velktor the Sorceror, Telkonar, The Va'Dynn, The Chardok Royals and had a damn good go at Sontalak.

Now a public safety announcement for the population of Karana never ever under any circumastances let Doneb pull....
