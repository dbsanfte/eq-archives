Warsilk Woods Giant Fort Event



Time: 6:00 pm GMT
Duration: Approximately 2 hours for the event itself
Place of Event: Warsilk Woods Giant Fort
Requirements for Attendance: Level Range - 20 to 30
Meeting Area/Grouping: Mudpit in The Overthere
(please arrive here before 5:30 pm GMT)
Event Coordinator:Surrussi (send tells for info not covered here)


This is the classic event on the giants. You are expected to make your way to the meeting area before the start of the event. If for some reason you are unable to make it to the meeting area before event time, you will be expected to make your own way to the event site. Escorts will be available from the mudpit to the Fort up until 6 pm GMT. Binds will be available for all at the Outpost (meet at the mudpit then request a bind). All races can be bound there (even KOS races).

The giants drop many nice items. Each group is expected to set up their rules on treasure split (Autosplit on/off, splitting of mob drops), who is the puller, who is healer, who calls med/healing breaks and etc.
