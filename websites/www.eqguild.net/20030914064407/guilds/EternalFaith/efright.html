Is Eternal Faith right for you?

If you're thinking of joining Eternal Faith make sure this is the guild for you. We hope that since you're even just considering Eternal Faith you're the type of person we'd be proud to have.


If any of the following is what you're like, what you do, or what you're mainly looking for, look for another guild.

  • Me! Me! Me!
  • Phat Lewt..., for ME!
  • Hurting others.
  • Joining only because someone you know is with us.
  • Finding a spring board guild so you can join your first choice later.
  • Being disrespectful.
Look for a guild that does not care who they invite or who you are.


Eternal Faith

If any of the following is what you're like, what you do, or what you're mainly looking for we'd like to see you with us. :)
  • Helping others
  • Having fun
  • Being generous
  • Caring about others

If you're one of the great types of people Eternal Faith is home to, and Eternal Faith is the guild for you, let us know on our message board.
