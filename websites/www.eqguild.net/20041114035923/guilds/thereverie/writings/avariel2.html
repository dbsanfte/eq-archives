Smell the Rain


The pastures dew kisses the blades of grass
A lovers embrace is the sun, peeking over the clouds
Arms of wind wrapped around intertwined bodies
Sounds of the morning, smell the rain

Forever travels in time
To distant lands and nearby streams
The kiss of my lover, arms strong with work
Tender is his touch, smell the rain

Purrs drift almost silently on the winds through the window
My voice is a flowing river, his the sound of thunder
But words speak only sounds of what we feel
Feel my heart and smell the rain

Taste of lips and eyelash flutters
Naked bodies entangled in arms and legs
Pillows fly, blankets billow
A lightning flash, thunder criews, smell the rain

Pitter patter is the rain on the roof and window
Kitty cat swishes of tails as leaves massage the walls
Boom, crackle...drowning out sounds
Blind when the light flashes, but smell the rain

Pure smell is rain
Innocent, sweet, clean
Hold a friend tight to make the future into present
Smell the rain, smell the rain
