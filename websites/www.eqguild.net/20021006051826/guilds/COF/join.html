Join Us!

four simple steps 
If you have just joined our Circle of friends or would like to, please complete the following:

  1. Go to our Home page.  Read the Guild Charter and the Guild Rules.

  2. Go to our Structure page.  See how we have set up our guild and the process established for becoming an Initiate and then a Member.

  3. If you agree and like what you see, click here: 
    circleforgotten@eqguild.net

  4. Write us an email (RE New Member)!  Tell us about your character!  

Be sure to include:

Character's Name 

Class

Race

Level of Experience

Helpful Skills (jeweler, blacksmith, brewer, tailor, etc).

Alternate Character's Names -

Then write a few sentences about your character.  What makes them unique?  What is your playing style?  Anything else we should know about you?  Anything you would like to share with the guild.  

We will respond as soon as possible!

NOTE:  Once invited, you will be given the title of Initiate to last for approx. 2-4 weeks.  This gives YOU time to get used to the guild and its workings, and US time to get to know you.  At the end of this period, if you still wish to become a full member, your character's name will be put in a Voting Ballot and your membership will be voted upon by the entire guild.  After this, you will be considered a regular Member.  

This process is only intended to ensure we remain a Circle of Friends who aid each other when ever possible.  We want to ensure EVERYONE in our guild has fun, and a large part of that is playing and chatting with their Guild-mates.  

Gaining Full Membership is easy!  Simply attend as many events and meetings as possible during your Initiate Period and speak up in Guildsay!  

Our members simply need to get to know you!  The weekly PL Events were set up just for this purpose!  Come and help lower lvls get some exp or get a little extra exp for your own mid-lower lvl.  It is held on Tues eves one week, Sun afternoon/eve the next, Tues eve the next, etc.
