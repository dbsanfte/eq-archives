Message Board The Svirfneblin, or Svirfs, are an all-gnome role-playing guild on the Zebuxoruk server in Everquest. We are dedicated to all things gnomish with the end goal of just having some fun!

Membership to the Svirfneblin is possible so long as your character is a gnome, you like (or would like to try) to role-play, you don't speak in short-hand or "chat", and you are otherwise a responsible individual who doesn't try to ruin the game for others.
