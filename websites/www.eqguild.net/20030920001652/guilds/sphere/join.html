So you want to become a Member?

It isn't difficult. You need only to have a willingness to help others, a strong sense of Honor and Morals, and a desire to join the best darn guild in Norrath! If you meet these qualifications, and are at least level 20, then you qualify! Simply post a message here on The Crystaline Logs under the "Applicants" forum, or send one of our officers a tell in game. Remeber that we are free to turn down anyone at anytime, and possibly without explanation. We never do that of course, but it could happen. Before posting your message or sending that tell, be sure to check over the Charter, and check the Members list make sure there isn't anyone on there who you KS'd or Trained in the last 30 days LOL :-P
