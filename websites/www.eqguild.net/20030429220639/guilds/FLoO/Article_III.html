Article III. ~ Duties of Conscripts and Role Definition

Sec a) Guild invitations

1. As a Conscript you have the power to invite other people into our guild. It is your responsibility to be certain the new recruit is informed of where to find these articles, our web sight, posting forums, and guildmagic.com. You will want to give them an overview of our ranks, and requirements for all Legionnaires. Further details on recruitment can be found under Article VI.

Sec b) as a Conscript you will have access to the officers’ club forum

Sec c) as a Conscript you are not permitted to use the guild message of the day command.

Sec d) you are bound to the rules of Legionnaire and Conscript
