How the Bank Works

  1. Donate any unused or duplicate items and/or a few platinum to the bank.  Everyone will be richer for it!  

  2. Examine the list of items below to see if there is something you could use.  Platinum will also be available for short term loans.

  3. Contact Jelek (Pikir/Sentosa) In-Game or via email at circleforgotten@eqguild.net RE: BANK asking for the item.

  4. If it is available, arrange with him to pick it up (Bank Mule is in Free Port).

  5. Use the item until you have it replaced.

  6. Return it to the Bank so others may also benefit.

It is that simple!  While this page will track who has what, returns and such will be based on the Honor System!  As such we ask a few things:

Please do not keep items for more than a few months and keep loans short term.  Other members will have needs also. 

If there is an item which you'd like to keep indefinitely, simply replace it with another of equal or greater value.

To protect all involved, we feel that Initiates should not participate in the Guild Bank until they have been voted in as full members. This not only helps ensure Bank items stay in the guild, but also keeps new members losing valuable items to our Guild Bank should they decide CoF is not for them.  If there is an available item an Initiate would like, they can ask the Banker to hold it for them until their membership is official.

Treat your guild well and reap the rewards!
