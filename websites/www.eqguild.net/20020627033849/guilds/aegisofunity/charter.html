Guild Charter

Introduction

The Aegis of Unity is a guild based out of the Torvonnilous server in the online role-playing game of Everquest.

We are a guild based upon the friendships aquired while fighting the denzinens of Norrath. The guild is laid-back, and not out to be the first or best at anything, except to each other.

The guild is controlled by the first council, who make all guild decisions. This council has supreme power over the direction of the guild. The council also approves all invites AND removes (if eventually that is necessary). Members are encouraged to talk to the online council members if ANY guild member is bothering you in any way. The council will talk about your complaints and deal with them, in most likely one of three ways: We will talk to the problem player and suggest changes in behaivor/action, We will if deemed necessary convine a public guild trail, or removal.

 

 

There is a min level of 46 for entrance to the guild, entrance is based upon grouping with current members and their sponsorship. While only one sponsor is adequate, two sponsors is best.

Twinking/Powerleveling/helping other characters is approved, if and only if it is unasked. For example dont join the guild thinking you will get twinked, this is only for current characters who have friends that want to make a nOOb chars life a bit easier. WE IN NO MEANS TOLLERATE BEGGING!

There are also no race/class restrictions for player admittance.

If you think you are Aegis of Unity material, apply now!

Guild Policy and Standards

Membership
Note to memberships after April 2002. We are currently only taking a very few selected players below lvl 50. Other certain classes have their min lvl set to the following lvls ( Rangers 57+ Shadow Knights 55+ Pallys 55+ Druids 54ish )

Players seeking membership in our family must be minimum level of 40. They must be someone with whom friendships exist with our members that most likely developed while grouping. These people need 2 members to sponsor them and will be on a 2-week probation period. During this 2-week period, any officer can /guildremove them at will should they prove to be dishonorable. After that 2-week period ONLY the council can remove a member after any officer has asked for a majority vote. If this recruit is a stranger (40 +), he will be directed to the recruitment officer(s) and asked to group/raid with us for up to 2-weeks before he will receive an invite. Does that mean anyone under 40 cannot join or anyone currently under 40 has to leave NO! It means that anyone under 40 must be part of the mentoring program. It also means that people seeking membership under 40 must have 2 sponsors and can only join if someone will mentor them. In any case, no one will be given an invite until the recruitment officer(s) gives us the ok having done the BACKGROUND check on the person. Background check will include a line of questioning, speaking to the recruits previous guild leaders, researching the server website boards for Rant/Flames on them, and possibly contacting a former guild or a guide to inquire about any potential issues.

Dues
Dues equal to the level of your main char will be paid to the guild banker once monthly by each member. This will help us to build a bank in order to fund major purchases and raid equipment like coffins for corpse summons, peridots for symbols, pearls for call of the hero, and gems for port to the planes etc. For example, at level 60, Xalik would pay 60plat per month to guild bank. If you have two mains on two different accounts you will have to pay for BOTH your mains.


Officers
Before you may become an officer you must be 50+, mentor at least one person to 50, have all your basic keys {(HS, OS, and Sky Shrine; later this will include other keys}, and have all the EverQuest expansions. If you meet these requirements, an existing officer may nominate you to a position. A majority vote of the current panel of officers will make you an officer. Should any of the council members VETO the vote, a majority vote of the council will be needed to become an officer then.

 

 


Mentors
In order to help our guild grow and to keep our lower level members from feeling left-out or forgotten, and promote unity, we have adopted the Mentor program. Basically this means that any member under level 40 will be paired up with a member over 50 as Mentor and Apprentice. The pair need not be the same class, as it will give them opportunity to learn one another's class. The Mentor will be responsible for helping his Apprentice more so than the entire guild. He/She will be the go to person for questions and guidance help with basic gear, camping items, learning group/raid roles and responsibilities, leveling etc. The vision for this is that the Mentor/Apprentice relationship will be one of friendship much like if the two players were friends in real life. This is not a license to expect power leveling or twinkage remember the mentor still has to get to 60 too. My recommendation is that the pair spends one or two nights a week together (Tuesday and Thursday were suggested). This gives the mentor a chance to play his/her lower level alt if so desired. During these times goals and strategies should be set. For example, where to hunt for XP and what to do when the mentor is busy with other things. The guild advisor will ask for updates and speak with both the mentor and apprentice to see how things are progressing. The guild advisor will award points for participating in this program. Also, Mentoring is one way to becoming an officer doubtful you will be considered if you haven't ever been a mentor. The guild advisor will pair up people. If you are not paired up the guild advisor will contact you and feel free to remind him/her if he/she becomes too busy.


Guild Tag
Aegis of Unity members are not permitted to use the /anon flag unless extreme circumstances are approved by Council. We understand there are times many of us do not want our class or level to be known. If this is the case your welcome to use /role-play to hide from spam tells for ports and resurrections. We want to know when our members are on and wear our guild tag with pride.


Twinking
Power Leveling and Twinking is NOT a GUILD supported practice. (We will not help you twink your alt or your friend.) If you choose to twink, you will not be frowned upon, but you will do so on your own. The guild will not help you get an item that you can not participate and contribute in getting. (No level 5 Ranger/Warrior is going to bet Tolans or Cobalt armor if they cannot get into OS and help with the kill).

Rules of Conduct

The list is Aegis of Unity's code of conduct. All members are required to read and agree to these guidelines. Please keep in mind that this guideline can change at any time. Any violation of these rules will result in a tell for an officer. Further violations will result in a tell from a council member. If the violations continue you will be removed and banned from the guild.

1. All guild members must obey Verant and SonyEQ's code of conduct. This means that kill stealing, intentional training, harassment of other players, fake trades and transactions.

2. No personal attacks on a guild member in guild chat, group say, out of character, shout, or tells. Please report all such behavior to an officer nearest to you. That said officer will group with the two members involved and try and solve the conflict. If the officer cannot help then a council member will join and help.

3. Please watch your language at all times while in guild chat. Please keep in mind that not everyone may share your views or sense of humor. Some of your guild members my be underage or from a different country and my not be able to understand.

 

 

4. Do not beg your fellow guild members for the following: ports, buffs, rezes, clarity, wolf form or Sow you. Ask first, but if a guild member is busy try to ask someone in your same zone. Please keep in mind that like you, your guild members are working on leveling or a trade skill. Please do not ask over and over again. Doing so will result in a reprimand from an officer.

5. Please understand that ANY item given to you belongs to Aegis of Unity and is not your own personal property to sell when you are done with it. You may have no more than two item from the guild bank at one time. And you may not keep these items more than one month. During that month you will be required find a suitable replacement. If you cannot, a extension on your loan may be approved by the council.

6. Remember that at all times what you do and say is a reflection upon Aegis of Unity and your fellow guild members. Wear your guild tag with pride.
