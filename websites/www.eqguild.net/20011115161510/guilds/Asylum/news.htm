Guild News

July 14, 2001 - Our first official Guild Raid in Befallen was in most part a complete flop.  It went horribly.  In future guild raids we need to all meet together FIRST and not go running off alone, so we can group up.  Single players running off and getting themselves killed made it hard for everyone to get together.  We need more organizatoin and cooperation.  Befallen was a bit difficult to take on at this point for most players and I do apologize for any deaths.  Hopefully our next guild raid will be more fun.
