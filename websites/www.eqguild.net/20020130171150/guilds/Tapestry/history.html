Read about our History
Formed in the fall of 2001, Tapestry of Heroes was my attempt at creating a guild that people would love to be in.  I had been in one previous guild, and felt frustration at the lack of leadership and smart playing that I had cultivated in myself.  I was constantly begging for officership and was continuously told that I was being considered, but we had enough officers already.  I was one of the 3 people left from the forming of that guild, and was put off by the rudeness of the guild leader.  So I decided to start out on my own.  After a few months of hacking around ideas for guidelines for the new guild, and a few ideas for the title, I came up with Tapestry of Heroes.

One of my first rules, like all rookie guild masters, was the creation of a level limit (14).  After I had trouble rounding up the needed people, I considered using lower level characters.  Then I started to think about saving the world.  It's my very nature to want to help people (probably why I chose a druid), so I figured I could also make the game more enjoyable for all by "training" new recruits as well.  So I opened up the level restrictions I had previously put on the guild, and said "I can start training the newbies to be smart players too!!!"  In this way I rounded up the needed players and started the process.

The creation night was a mess from the start.  I happened to pick the night of a GM event over at the Arena, so needless to say the GM's were very busy.  I also had trouble getting the necessary 10 people to form the guild.  Sadly to say, some of those people are no longer with the guild.  But in the end we got it created.

So from there, I made all of the original 10 people officers and told them to start recruiting.  We have grown slowly but surely over these last few months, gathering together some of the nicest people I have had the pleasure to meet in game.  And after all these months, I have only had to remove one person, which  I consider to be my proudest accomplishment.

I wish to thank all of the people who have help keep this guild together these first few months.

First on that list is my good friend Mawr.  He is my in game adviser and confidant, whom I have never met in real life.  Thanks for the help.

Then there is my friend Remie (who sadly sold his account).  His help was some of the best I have ever gotten.  Thanks bud!

And lastly, I wish to thank my brother Micos.  He is the rock that has held me together for so long both inside the game and out.  He got me hooked on this game, and on life itself.  For all the things you've done bro, thanks a ton!
