Nobility is the defining trait of Sage Council. We do not claim alliance toward Good nor Evil. Thus, we are enemies of neither. Welcoming ALL races, classes, and deities to join us in our struggles against oppression. Our fight, is one which is strong against all that is untrue within Norrath. Sage Council representatives use only their conscience to determine right from wrong. Therefore, our members must be strong not only in battle, but in character, all the while maintaining an open mind in keeping a choke hold on tyranny without passing undue judgment. Many, Sage Council members have made the ultimate sacrifice battling for what was true and right. It is in their great honor that we not only expect truth from all ranks, but demand it.
