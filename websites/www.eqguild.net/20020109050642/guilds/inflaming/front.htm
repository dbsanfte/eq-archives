12-08-2001

We had our first guild meeting in quite a while on Sunday. The meeting went well and I felt like we took a step in the right direction. Some good ideas were put up for discussion such as raids and recruitment.

In regard to raids, a few sites are being considered, but your ideas are encouraged and appreciated. Please see Jynn's message in the Forum area of our site to post your suggestions.

Regarding recruitment, we again would like to stress that everyone continue to keep an eye out for good candidates. Our recruitment efforts are going well and by continuing to bring in other good players with a mind for what IW represents, we can only become stronger. This will also allow IW more flexibility in raid selection.

Other notable highlights from the meeting were, "Jynn's Random Roll" contest, and a few spirited duels in Freeport's famed Arena.

Remember, this is YOUR guild. Without your active participation, we cannot grow and accomplish all of the things we would like to do. If you don't feel comfortable recruiting, post some ideas here or vise versa, but play an active roll in whatever way you can.

Thank you
