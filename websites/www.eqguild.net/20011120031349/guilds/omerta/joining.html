Joining the Guild

Step 1 - Please read through the guild info and rules on this site. They can be found here.

Step 2 - Applicants must meet our minimum level requirements for their class. These vary a bit depending on the current needs of the guild, and are also subject to change at any time in the future. Currently they are:

  • Level 41: NEC, MNK, ROG, RNG, BRD, MAG, WAR, ENC, SHM, WIZ, CLR, SK
  • Level 46: Druids, Paladins (we have lots)

    Step 3 - Applicants must find a current Omerta member to sponsor them into the guild. Once this step is done, applicants post their intentions of joining the guild on our Apply forum with their character name, class, level, the name of their sponsor, which guild they were previously in, and why they feel they would make a good fit into our guild.

    Step 4 - It is possible we may require more grouping with members first and/or an in-game interview, but once we as a guild are satisfied that all conditions are met and that the applicant would make a good fit into Omerta, an officer will contact them in-game for the guildinvite. This may take a few days or more from the time of applying on the forum.
