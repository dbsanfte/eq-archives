Purpose:  The purpose of our guild is simply this: TO HAVE FUN. This is, after all, a game! We will always be there to help one another, and we do realize that sometimes EQ can seem far from fun. It is the purpose of this guild to make sure that while you are getting your corpse out of a pool of molten lava, or waiting on a /petition to be answered, you will still have fun as much as is possible.

Requirements: There aren't many requirements to join this guild. The first is that you must be level 20 or higher. If you have an Alt that you want to join up, he or she must remain in /roleplay until they reach the 20th Level. Power Leveling is acceptable in this guild, though if you are a melee class I highly recommend you resist that temptation, for you will be utterly useless by the time you are done.  A select few exemptions to this rule will exist. Immediate family and RL Friends of officers can join after level 10.  Other exceptions may be made, but they will need to be approved by me. Any members under the level of 20 must keep /roleplay on at all times except while LFG.
For the next few weeks, we will be having open enrollment. Any officer can invite any player that they wish, so long as they are over 20. This is so that we can get a large base-membership set up. After this period is over, players must hunt with an officer as least once to be considered.  The only exception is if a member tells me the person is a RL or In-Game friend, and that they are of good character.  But beware, for if they are not, this will look bad on you. Not that you will be punished for it, but I may not believe you the next time if your last 3 friends had to be removed from the guild :-P Officers are free to invite anyone who is backed by a current member, so long as that member has been in the guild at least 2 weeks.

Code of Conduct:  While I certainly don't anticipate having any problems with my guild members behaving inappropriately and making the guild look bad in the eyes of non-members, situations do sometimes arise, and so I will lay out a few very simple rules here, to try and prevent as many as possible.

               1.  No member shall ever knowingly mistreat another player in any way. This will include Stealing Kills, Training, Stealing or Scamming, cursing at or generally being disrespectful in any way. You should always treat other players like they were your guild mates, and you should always treat guild mates like they are your best friends :-)

               2. Profanity is not allowed in guild chat, and is discouraged in any public chat forum as it makes the name of The Crystaline Sphere look bad.  This includes /shout,  /ooc, /auc and /say. I realize this is not the most popular rule, but I also already know of a few minors that are members of our guild. Our guild chat should not cause a member to have to shut off his monitor when his mother walks in the door. Minor cursing, using minor curse words,  is ok in moderation. But the "F-Bomb", the "P" word, the "S" Word and any other such utterances will get my attention or that of an officer every time.

                3.  Violations of these two most basic rules will result in the following:
                        First Time: Warning
                        Second Time: Final Warning
                        Third Time: Disbandment from the guild.

*I reserve the right to remove any member at any time if their actions are severe enough to warrant it, without warning*


Now, all that being said, i really don't expect to have issues with this. I recruit carefully and I have only the best people I can find in this guild, and my officers do the same.  If anyone sees a member doing something which soils the name of this guild, please let us know. Our reputation is THE most important thing we have, and we will not let it be tarnished.
