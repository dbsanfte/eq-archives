Rodcet's Fury Charter

Rodcet's Fury is a guild on Rodcet nife server. The guild was founded on Oct 18th 2001  by Kyssi Albetter, Karthis Myth, Tolump, and Salina Moonlite. Rodcet's Fury is now ran by Kyssi Albetter. Rodcet's Fury was founded as a group of friends and even though some friends have moved on will continue to strive to be a guild of friends. There for members are very much encouraged to group and talk with your fellow members. This is not a must of course but we do ask that you try.
 
 

Guidelines of Conduct

Everything a guild member does reflects the guild as a whole. There for there are rule on how you must not conduct yourself while wearing our tag. The following are things that will not be allowed.

  • Kill stealing from another player
  • Engage in Item Theft
  • Engage in verbal abuse or harrasment of another player
  • Useing bad language in open zone wide chat channels
In the event that a guild member (in any role) violates the above rules of conduct, the matter is to be discussed. Depending on the severity of the offense, the guild has three possible courses of action. Guilt and punishment is decided by vote after a discussion.

Warning: The member is given a formal warning. Two warnings result in the member losing officer status (if an officer or the leader) or removal from the guild (if a member).

Demotion: This applies to officers or the leader. The person is demoted to standard guild member status.

Removal: The member is removed from the guild.
 

Guidelines for Loot

Rodcet's Fury is a need before greed or NBG guild. Meaning if you need it and will use it and no one else needs it it will be yours. If someone else needs and will use it you will have a /random for it. Once you win a item at a raid. You will be out for any more loot till everyone else has won something. Anything item/spell not claimed while on a guild raid will be put in the guild bank. Then any member who can and will use can ask for the item and be given it at no cost.
