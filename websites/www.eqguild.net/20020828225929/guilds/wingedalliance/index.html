I am a wind across the sea, I am a flood across the plain, I am the roar of the tides, I am the stag of seven tines. I am a dewdrop let fall by the sun, I have the fierceness of boars, I am a hawk, my nest on a cliff, I am at height of my poetry*. I am the most beautiful among flowers; I am the salmon of wisdom that is both the tree, and the lightning that strikes it. Who is the dark secret of the dolmen not yet hewn. I am the queen of every hive, I am the fire on every hill, I am the shield over every head, I am the spear of battle, I am the ninth wave of eternal return. I am the grave of every vain hope that knows the path of the sun, the periods of the moon. Who gathers the divisions, enthralls the sea, sets in order the mountains, the rivers.
I inspire the peoples hope.

 

*Poetry: Magical Skill
