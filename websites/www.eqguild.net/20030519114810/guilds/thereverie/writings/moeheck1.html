She is my Queen


A heart that lays heavy with a loss never owned
A dream that haunts although dreams never come
Belonging in a world where evil and hate is hidden within
Meeting a queen by chance, through kindness, and in love, I drowned.

To be re-born into a life that’s now fulfilled
Never sensing the power that this queen could hold
A heart that lays heavy waiting for a touch from her soul
And to know in my mind the powers will unfold in her hands

The struggle to survive in a world so harsh and unkind
Without purpose, direction, no hope just to wander the lands
How one person unknown can know such a dream
To show Love, to show hate, to show silence so loud

My queen, I beg thee, show me your love and your light
Show me your strengths, your softness, my weakness, my fate
I am but a pawn with a need to belong
In a world that can love me, love us, for we are one.
