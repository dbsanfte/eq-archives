17 June, 2001
Sorry for the long delay in web site updates - real life been extremely busy.

I know we have many new faces running around Norrath, and to all of you - WELCOME. Naldiien has expressed his desire to be on an "Invite freeze" right now. We are a family guild, and we need to take the time to get to know everyone before we grow more. From past experiences, growing too fast is a bad thing.

So, my request to all of you, get together and hunt as a group. If you see someone on about the same level as you that you may not know, or barely know, make an effort to go group with that person. We are a family, we need to act like one, and do things together. Not just the big raids, but everything - camping those items, getting experience, or just plain messing around. Go out there and make JJKR what is should be, not just one of the uber guilds, but the biggest, best family in Norrath.  This will not happen on its own, we must ALL make the effort to achieve this goal.

Now, I am doing some web site updates. I am adding a page "Family Photo Album" for all those screen shots we have, and would like to share. If you have some, get them to me (Prefer jpg or gif) and I will put them up. Please provide some information about the photo - when, where, a caption, ...

I will also be working on a roster section. I hope to be able to include character photos.
