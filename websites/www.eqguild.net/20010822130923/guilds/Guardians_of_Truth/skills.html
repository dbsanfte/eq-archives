Our Skills

Many of our members are accomplished trades people and are happy to make items for the guild at reduced prices, at cost or sometimes in exchange for materials. If you need armor, weapons, jewelry, etc. chances are that a guild member will be happy to help you out. To find out what services are available, please take a look at our guild roster, available at: Guardians of Truth Guild Roster. When you look at individual members, their skills and what they offer should be available for you to see. Please remember to keep your skills current!
