Adding a chat room to your guild's homepage

To include a chat room on your guild's homepage, just paste this code where you want the chat room to appear. The blue colored text is where you can configure the colors to match your site if you prefer. The green is the size of the chat room and can gbe changed if you want as well. The red is where you name your chat room and MUST be changed. More info on that below.

See this in action: sample chat room

<applet codebase="http://klagenfurt.at.eu.worldirc.org/" code="EIRC" archive="EIRC.jar,EIRC-gfx.jar" width="600" height="400">
<param name="server" value="klagenfurt.at.eu.worldirc.org" >
<param name="port" value="6667" >
<param name="mainbg" value="#C5D5FE" >
<param name="mainfg" value="#000000" >
<param name="textbg" value="#FFFFFF" >
<param name="textfg" value="#000000" >
<param name="selbg" value="#5252C7" >
<param name="selfg" value="#FFFFFF" >
<param name="channel" value="#guildname" >
<param name="titleExtra" value=" - EIRC" >
<param name="username" value="eqguild" >
<param name="realname" value="EQGuild">
<param name="nickname" value="EQ-Guest" >
<param name="login" value="0" >
<!--param name="spawn_frame" value="1" /-->
<!--param name="frame_width" value="600" /-->
<!--param name="frame_height" value="400" /-->
</applet>

The default colors are these, feel free to change them in the code to match your site:

(mainbg)
#C5D5FE		 (mainfg, textfg)
#000000
(textbg, selfg)
#FFFFFF		 (selbg)
#5252C7

The "channel" is the name of your chat room. It should be one word with no spaces. It can contain numbers if you want.
