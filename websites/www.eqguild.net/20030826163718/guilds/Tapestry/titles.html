Explanation of Titles for Offices
This page defines the roles of the titles bestowed upon certain members of the guild.  All of these positions are filled either by common election or by appointment.

This list is in order of power within the guild.  Top of the list is highest power.  Power decreases as you go down the list.  Officials may or may not be Officers.  If a member is currently fulfiling more that one duty (i.e. an Admiral acting as Battle Master), the higher position (i.e. Admiral) is the power that the member has.

Click here for a visual flow chart of power.
 

Guild Leader - The Guild Leader's (GL) duties are as follows:
GL is the absolute authority within the Guild.  All decisions come from or go through GL.

GL is in charge of issuing removal notices for two (2) time violaters of Guild charter.

GL is in charge of issuing removal notices for anyone violating the Guild charter in an extreme way, or damaging the Guild reputation.

GL is in charge of overseeing operation of the Guild, and checking up on ALL members.

GL is in charge of calling votes for, or appointing, new Officials and Admirals .
 

Admiral - The Admiral's duties are as follows:
Admirals are in charge of overseeing guild functions and operations in the absence of the Guild Leader .

Admirals are in charge of voting on all issues that require a vote.  This includes but is not limited to rule changes, removal of persons not fitting the Guild charter, etc.  A simple majority wins.  There will always be 3 Admirals.

Admirals are in charge of reporting charter violations to Enforcer .
 

Officer -  The Officer's (OF) duties are as follows:
OF is in charge of recruiting new members.  This includes interviewing and locating as well as inviting.  OF is expected to use Recruting rules when recruting.

OF are in charge of reporting charter violations to Enforcer .
 

Officials

Lore Master -  The Lore Master's (LM) duties are as follows:

LM is in charge of reporting Raids on the Guild Calendar.  The LM gets this information from the Battle Master .

LM is in charge of posting Guild news on the webpage.

LM is in charge of tracking new recruits and their information on the webpage.  The LM gets this information from the Admirals and Officers .

LM is in charge of keeping attendance at the Guild meetings.

LM is in charge of reporting violations of grouping, meeting attendance and information submission for new recruits to Enforcer .

LM is in charge of reporting the number of violations of Guild charter to Enforcer .

Battle Master -  The Battle Master's (BM) duties are as follows:
BM is in charge of coordinating Guild raids.  He reports this information to the Lore Master . He must plan at least two (2) raids a month.

BM is in charge of taking attendance on raids.  He reports this information to the Lore Master .

BM is in charge of reporting charter violations to Enforcer .
 

Chaplain -  The Chaplain's (CL) duties are as follows:
CL is in charge of giving moral guidance to guild members on any and all subjects as needed.

CL is in charge of resolving any grievances that arise within the guild either by email or in game tells. These grievances are reported to Lore Master and Guild Leader , so that the problem may be solved if possible.

CL is in charge of reporting charter violations to Enforcer .

Enforcer -  The Enforcer's (EF) duties are as follows:
EF keeps track of who has violated the Guild charter, and how many times.  EF reports all violations of the Guild charter to Lore Master for official record.  When two (2) violations are tacked against a member, Lore Master and EF must compare records and agree.  EF recieves this information from Admirals , Officers , Officials , or Guild Leader .

EF enforces all rules in Guild charter, including suspension and removal.  EF recieves approval to remove only from Admirals or Guild Leader .  Admirals must vote before giving the approval to remove.  Guild Leader is not required to call a vote to remove.
 

Armorer -  The Armorer's duties are as follows:
Armorer is in charge of recieving and storing all gifts (items and money) donated to the Guild.

Armorer is in charge of issuing gifts to members in need of them.  Money will be handed out on a payback basis, to be decided by Guild Leader , Admiral , or Lore Master .

Armorer is in charge of reporting recieved, stored and issued items and money to Lore Master .
 

Apprenticeships -  Apprentices may be required of certain official positions.  These will be issued to any Official needing help.  The apprentice will be picked from the member pool by the Official in need of help.  These will be voted on by the Admirals or Guild Leader .

The apprentice will train under the current Official and learn the ways of that position.  The Official will then call for the Guild Leader or Admirals to promote the apprentice to full time Official, creating another position of the same title and power.
