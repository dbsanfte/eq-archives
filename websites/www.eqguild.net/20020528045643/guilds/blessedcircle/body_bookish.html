"I have heard of this place, but have never seen it," Trogg sniffed between great belches of air. Even though he was still exhausted from the battle, he spoke with an eloquence unbecoming an ogre raised in the dank swamps of Norrath. It was obvious he had spent much time in the more refined regions of this world. Just as obvious, he disbelieved the words of his savior, the troll that for some reason had rushed to Trogg's rescue in the heat of an ambush orchestrated by a lone hill giant. "Matter of fact, I have never found reason to believe these tales."

"Believe me or no," the troll grunted, his disposition heated further by Trogg's disbelief. "Frottleg been to this place in different form, seen with two eyes. Land called Xev exist, and there also be what called brotherhood of the Blessed Circle."

Frottleg tugged a pouch of silver coins from the giant's side and trudged once again down the grassy path, with never a look back at the ogre. Only a word of explanation to fill the void left by Trogg's unspoken gratitude.

"It was Circle folk that saved Frottleg from beast of Kanara. Why you think me save ogre hide? Me returning favor, just like Blessed Circle said do when Frottleg offered gold payment. Frottleg save gold that day, today he save some silver from ogre purse."

Welcome to the Tales of the Blessed Circle, an Everquest guild on the Xev server!

The Blessed Circle is a fellowship of Everquest adventurers dedicated to making the world of Norrath a better place by aiding friends and destroying the evil that creeps across the land. What better way, guild members, to begin your explorations of Norrath than through the explorations of these pages?
