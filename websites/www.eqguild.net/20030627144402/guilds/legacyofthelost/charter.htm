Guild Charter

 

From all of the corners of Norrath we've come. We were
once lost, once without a home. Without a leader,
without a place. Guilds, friends, and family we had, but
our hearts yearned for more. A connection missing...
It seemed that the world lacked respect, lacked love.
All the things we needed. Maybe they seemed to be
there, but in the end, it was all an illusion. After
the many pains endured, and the many travels spend
wandering lost and alone, frustrated and confused...
Our paths met, and a spark of hope filled our hearts.
There was one... Who with a smile, could assure you
everything was more than it seemed, and we were
worth something. Our leader she became, and our path
will forever endure. Together we will build a Legacy,
and together we shall endure anything that comes.

We pledge to help those in need, to share our knowledge
and wisdom without a grudge or a second thought,
to all those who ask.
We pledge to never stop learning, and to keep our minds
open of all things.
We pledge to respect every living breathing creature,
even when we disagree.
We pledge to meet your hello with reply, and to meet
a smile with another in return.
We pledge to put our friends and family above all things,
and that the only true wealth is love.
We pledge to participate in events and raids with our
friends and partners, whenever it's possible.
We pledge to always keep morale and a sense of right
on the tops of our minds, and not to purposely
offend another creature.
We pledge to keep our mouths open, and to speak
whenever the chance is given. To share ideas and
praise, and to make conversation when our time is free.

and above all

We pledge to have fun, with whatever we do.
