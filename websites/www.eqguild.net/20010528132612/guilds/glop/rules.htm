Guild Rules

These are the the Codes that We as Guardian Lords of Peace have agreed to live by:

Statute I:
- First and Foremost, We are built on the Foundation of Fun and Friends,

> You Will have fun in our Guild or we will Hunt You Down and Spank You till You ENJOY IT!

> We Strive to make the game Enjoyable for everyone in and out of the Guild so that those who wear the tag "Guardian Lords of Peace" will be Proud of it and that those who do NOT will speak of how great the Lords are and wish they were a part of us.

> Also, Remember Your fellow guildies when you acquire new Armor, Weapons, Jewelry, Ect. someone could use that Item and not have to pay the OUTRAGEOUS PRICES that you see in Auctions

Statute III:
- New Membership Policy,

> All candidate names must be submitted to the "New Memberships" board so officers are in the know. The names will remain there along with their sponsor until approved by three or more officers.

> He or she must have a sponsor who's hunted with this person and must then be referred to the officers for approval.

> This person must get registered on the EZBoard and Send E-mail with Toon Info to GuardianLords@Hotmail.com so that we are able to keep up with everyone's potential for raids and such

> Lastly, once the person becomes a member of Guardian Lords of Peace, he or she will be on a probationary period of two weeks, When Probationary time is over that person's name will also be removed from the "New Membership" board

Statute IV:
- Children Player Characters

> Due to the nature of the Internet and hostile nature of some of EverQuest's characters, children will not be allowed in Guardian Lords of Peace unless approved by ALL guild officers.

> Submissions for minors entering the guild will be reviewed on a case by case basis. Any officer can be contacted regarding this inquiry.
