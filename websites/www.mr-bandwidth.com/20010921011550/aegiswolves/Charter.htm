Original Charter

This charter was originally written buy the first 10 founding members of this guild.  the charter was written for Everquest's world. but with a  small amount of modification can be any world. as the guild grows out of the single game single server realm we will move to a single code of conduct that will be universal. All wolves will be of one mind and spirit when it comes to their conduct with others and each other. anyway here is the original charter.

It is rumored that long ago there was a clan. They were a clan that stood for honor and justice. They defended the weak and hunted the wicked. They were known as the Wolves of Norrath. Since those days the Wolves have been forgotten, yet their names are still whispered throughout the most remote regions of Norrath. Unfortunately, their once proud name was now only used when describing a threat to small children to get them to finish their meals or to get to their beds at night. "Mind me, or the Wolves will get you!" Well the world of Norrath has changed much. Racism and persecution could be seen like a virus engulfing all of Norrath. There seemed to be no one who remembered the ideals and beliefs of the original Wolves. This was seen by several young and idealistic adventurers, and they decided that the revival of the Wolves of yore would be the best way to help rid the land of this vile threat. They decided that instead of just standing by and watching it further engulf Norrath, that they would do something about it. This was the day the Aegis Wolves were born. These brash youngsters decided to live by the old ways. They founded the Aegis Wolves with the same tenants and ideals the original Wolves.

Like Wolves, they have few rules, but the rules that they have are to be taken seriously and upheld by the guild members to their best ability.

1. Religious beliefs can and will be kept to personal choice. However members with opposing beliefs will keep them secondary to their membership in the Aegis Wolves, in order to keep inside conflict to a minimum.

2. Members are to protect each other from danger. If a member is seen to be in danger, do not hesitate to offer assistance to the member. The exception to this is if the person tells you otherwise. "Kill stealing" is looked down upon, especially with other member’s kills.

3. All members must do their best to represent the guild in good light. This requires that they try, to the best of their ability, to follow the guild’s charter. This rule is especially true when traveling with people out side the guild. If they see the behavior of the member, they may want to join the member's guild.

4. Never do anything to bring dishonor to the guild. This will include, but is not limited to, "Kill Stealing", Stealing from members, bug abusing, and irritating/inappropriate behavior to others. These actions could result in in disciplinary action up to and including removal from the guild.

5. All members of the guild will be considered trustworthy. This is what the Aegis Wolves were founded on. If they are a member of the Aegis Wolves they are your brothers/sisters in arms. Trust them as you would trust yourself. If a member is found to be untrustworthy then it will be handled at that time. And in a manner appropriate to the breach of trust.

6. The Aegis Wolves will not discriminate against any race or class. In this guild, it is a common sight to see a High Elf Paladin fighting alongside a Dark Elf Necromancer. As long as the person proves to be a loyal and valued member they are welcome to join the Aegis Wolves.

7. The Aegis Wolves are a role-playing guild. As such all members of the Aegis Wolves will develop a character and must try to stay in character while playing.

8. Guild Wars. Guild Wars can only be initiated by a 50% vote of the of the Elder Wolves Council. Once the Elder Wolves vote to begin such a war notice must be sent immediately to all members. All members must be notified at least 24 hours in advance.
 
Code Of Conduct

All members of the Aegis Wolves are expected to live by a code of conduct. this code is what sets the Aegis Wolves apart from many other online gaming guilds.
