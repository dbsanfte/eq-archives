Our Mission

The mission of the Aegis Wolves is to journey across gaming worlds. All the while making friends, assisting those in need, combating the forces of darkness, growing stronger and, more importantly, having fun.

 

 

Guild Profile

The Internet is an incredible thing that man has created. It allows vast amounts of information to flow from one place to another in just a few seconds. It allows instant communication with others and it allows new imaginary worlds to be born. but this comes with a price. the price is that you never truly know who you are dealing with.

No where is this more of a problem than in the online gaming community. players will use that anonymity the internet allows to perpetrate crimes on fellow players. often ruining the other players evening. So how do you protect yourself? How do you know the person your dealing with can be trusted? How do you know that this player lives by the same standards and beliefs as yourself?

The answer is simple: Become an Aegis Wolf.

becoming a member of the Pack means far more than having a group to talk to and swap tales. It is a Way of Life. it is something that comes from with in and it permeates everything you do. If you are unsure if you know what it takes to be a wolf then seek out a member of the pack they will show you the way. For those who have that Wolf already in their heart then i say to you... 

Welcome to the pack....
