The Honored and Scorned of the Aegis Wolves

This is where those members of the Aegis Wolves who were considered the best are Honored
It is also a place to honor those outside of the pack. people who have gone out of their way to assist the guild or its members. people who have shown the spirit and heart of a Wolf.

It is also used to show Guilds who have proven themselves to be with out honor. Guilds whose individuals are to not be trusted.
