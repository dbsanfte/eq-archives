The Aegis Wolves present awards to the members as they perform services to the guild both in general and to their specific server. These awards are general guild awards. Some servers/games may have additional awards not recognized here.

The Aegis Wolves also bestow Titles on those members who's actions in game Warrant them.

Awards

Award Symbol Description
Wound Badge Awarded to those who suffered severe deaths and loss in a guild war or defending a guild member in a guild sponsored event or duel.
Order of the Valiant When a Guild member does something beyond the ordinary in game. usually to assist another and putting themselves at either great risk or sacrificing game time to do it.
Valor of the Pioneer Awarded to those who start new chapters of the Aegis Wolves on new servers or new games.
Glory and the Honor Given to those players who have been outstanding members of the guild and who have retired from the game.
Golden Aegis is an award symbolizing protection. It is given to members who go out of their way to protect the well being of fellow Wolves, whether that be corpse retrieval, assisting in a quest, or other similar, selfless acts.
White Aegis is awarded to members who have organized quests, plots, events, weddings, dungeon raids or other similar things that require much personal investment for the enjoyment of all.
Event Shield Given after a member has developed and supervised no less than 5 in game guild events. these can be role-playing or competition events.
Cub Shield
Colors of Vigor given for two years service with the guild.
Colors of Vitality given for 5 years service with guild.
Silken Paw awarded to members who show outstanding courtesy, assistance and guidance to other members.
The Sharpened Claw For participation in a guild dungeon raids and guild events. Multiple awards are designated by gold bands for each additional award and a platinum band for five awards.

Titles

Title Symbol Description
Knight of the Wolf Carries the title of Sir an formal functions such as councils and meetings. These people have gone out of their way and risked great peril to assist guild members, guild allies or just other players multiple times. They have shown great dedication or valor during a hot trot. Their dedication has brought pride upon themselves and Honor to the guild.
Lord/Lady of the Hunt
Barer of the Shield The pack masters personal bodyguard. During time of war will be beside the pack master at all times. this requires a level 30 at minimum. You are the pack masters champion and a champion of the wolves. Due to playing times and real life there may be up to 3 in the chapter at any point in time.
