These are a list of the current guild chapters that have formed or are forming. as additional packs form the contact information will be listed here along with any general information about that guild that needs to be known.

Tunare Pack - Everquest

The Tunare Pack is the Aegis Wolves Chapter that exists on the Tunare server in Everquest. 

The current pack master is Leetah Puppypaws.

You may contact her via email at aegis_wolves@hotmail.com or by contacting her directly in game via a tell. if she is not on line then contact any member of the pack and they will contact her for you.

Erollisi Marr Pack - Everquest

The Tunare Pack is the Aegis Wolves Chapter that exists on the Erollisi Marr server in Everquest. 

The current pack master is Paladium Soulseeker.

You may contact him via email at adept_wizard@hotmail.com or by contacting him directly in game via a tell. if he is not on line then contact any member of the pack and they will contact him for you.

 
Sawtooth Pack - Diablo 2

The Sawtooth Pack is the Aegis Wolves Chapter that exists on the servers of Diablo 2.  Is named after the Sawtooth pack of wolves in a Wolf repopulation project. In honor of our brothers in spirit and the beauty they represent.

There is no current pack master the wolves are organizing there at the moment.

You may contact them via email at aegis_wolves@hotmail.com .
