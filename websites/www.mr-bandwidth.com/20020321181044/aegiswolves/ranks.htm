The Aegis Wolves have a very general Ranking system. this system is designed with the realization that there will be many worlds and games the pack will be in. So for ease of use by both pack leaders and the members the original ranking system has been simplified.

Some servers may adopt special server ranks that will be in addition to those listed here. these will be for special occasions and be relevant only on the server where they were created.

Ranks

Rank Description
Grand Pack Master the leader of the Aegis Wolves across all games/servers.

(a) responsible for making sure all guilds obey the guild charter
(b) approves choices for replacement pack masters
(c) does not interfere with basic running of a guild as long as the charter is followed.
(d) initiates charter ratification’s and confirms changes with all pack masters.
Pack Master the guild leader

(a) all officers duties and responsibilities
(b) calls votes and meetings
(c) Final authority in disputes or tied votes
(d) Over all responsibility for the pack and it’s well being
Grand Elder This rank is an Honorary title rank bestowed upon the first 10 Elders of the Aegis Wolves. It is used to give representation that the Individual so Ranked was one of the First Wolves. Pack Masters may on occasion issue this rank so that All members of their server know that this individuals words carry a great deal of weight in guild and should be heeded.
Elder an officer of the guild

(a) has full guild officer duties/capabilities
(b) has voice and vote in Pack Elder meetings
(c) has other duties and responsibilities as determined by the Pack Elders and/or Pack Master
(d) mediates problems or disputes.
(e) responsible for the packs well being
Umbra Wolf a former Elder of the guild who

(a) has limited elder abilities to assist officers or mediate.
(b) has voice in the Pack Elder circle
(c) can not vote as an Elder, tag cubs, have positional responsibilities (i.e. treasurer)
Wolf a full member of the guild who

(a) can serve as a mentor to potential cubs
(b) has full member access to guild resources
(c) eligible for officer (elder) promotion
(d) assists with event formulation, running and playing having fun
Den Pup These are children of guild members or close friends of the guild that have been approved by the elder circle to be allowed in guild for protection purposes. This rank serves as a warning to members that patience will be needed.
Pup a junior member of the guild who is tagged but  

(a) has limited access to guild resources
Cub a candidate member of the guild who is under mentorship or working on his quest to achieve Pup status.
