IMPORTANT: With respect to the other Amazon tribal boards, The Olympian Gods (TOG) guild board is run much different. ONLY TOG Olympian registered SN's can post on the TOG Ezboard. If your character is not a God or Goddess, they can posts. This keeps the TOG members from being confused over who is playing Hermes for example. If we have 2 or more Hermes, then storylines get confusing. If you post a God character on the TOG ezboard and not a member of TOG, your post will likely be deleted to relieve confusion. If you want to become a member of the TOG board. Please check out their website at www.mythicalodyssey.i-p.com/tog.html Thank you, Admin: Hippolyte
