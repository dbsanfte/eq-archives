PLEASE, when filling out ezboard profiles...state if your character is MORTAL or IMMORTAL. If immortal, please state powers so that they will be recognized. You MUST have those powers in profile to be recognize if you are not an a typical myth god/dess. This helps other's know who you are and what you can do. But, please note...there is to be no killing off of other's characters. You may injure or place a spell on another character. But that character chooses how long or how devistating the wound or spell is to their OWN character. Attacks and such can be fun...but remember to allow the mun's to control their character in the end. Thanks~ Hippster
