Do not attempt to sharpen a weapon ye need. Use those rusty daggers to practice on. 1 sharpening stone (3 cp) and 1 rusty dagger combine in forge for 1 tarnised weapon. If it does not work stone and weapon are gone! To make metal bits add 2 small ores separately and one water flask to the forge hit combine and hopefully ye get metal bits. 1 metal bits + 1 file mold + 1 water flask = 1 file (with luck)
