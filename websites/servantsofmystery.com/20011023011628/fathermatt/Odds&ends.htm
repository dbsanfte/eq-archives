FatherMatt's Odds & Ends

Batwings and fish scales--along with tiny daggers and bloodstones for the enchanters--can be bought in unlimited quantities at a hut at the inn in East Commons that is right on the border with West Commons.  (This is also true in the Arcane Academy in West Freeport and in Ogguk, but the East Commons site is the closest to a teleport.)

Always have a location macro (/loc) handy.  If it looks like you may die, hit this macro button repeatedly.  If you do die, you can scroll back to get the location of your corpse.  If you forget and can't find your corpse, enlist the help of a bard (level 4+) or necro (level 1+). 

In the middle of the sea, you can enter a boat by getting in front of it, ducking down (use the "d" key), letting the boat go over you, then standing up from the duck (hit "d" again") when the boat is over you.  Doing this will place you inside the boat.

If you are sitting, /camp desktop will take you fully out of EverQuest.

If you have a pet, press F1 twice to target it.
