Posted by Cassiopeia on February 10, 1999 at 13:53:59:

Greetings,

As some of you may know, an Ultima Online player in the LS Shard had her account hacked by use of the Netbus Trojan Horse. Please read the following link to learn more about BackOrifice, Netbus, and other Win95/98 Trojan Horses. This particular link is an ad for BOClean 3.x, a virus protection program, but about 1/3 the way down they have links that explain many known trojan horses AND how they are transmitted.
The best defense is to at least be informed of these risks.

That I have seen, most guilds/people draw the line at personal attacks of this nature. It's no longer just an inconvenience "in game", but an attack on a real person's person, privacy and property.

To this end, DSS and FuQ (the two guilds involved in and bragging about the hack) have become "kill on sight" guilds. Although your guild may or may not be a PvP guild, I encourage anyone who sees DSS and FuQ (esp. in dungeons or outside of town) to report them to others who are willing to engage in PvP combat.

Thanks for your attention

Cassiopeia of TNO (The Nameless Ones) - Lake Superior
