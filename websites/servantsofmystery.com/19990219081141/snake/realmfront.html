Who are the Servants of Mystery?

The Servants of Mystery are the followers of Mystra, Goddess of Magic in the Forgotten Realms. Our Lady of Mysteries is the origin and fountainhead of magic and its energies, the One True Spell from which all others flow. Our Order is dedicated to the teaching of magic and to its practice in service of the common good. The Servants of Mystery was founded in March, 1995, as a non-sanctioned guild in Neverwinter Nights (NWN), the original official online Advanced Dungeons and Dragons role-playing fantasy adventure game on America Online. The Servants of Mystery attained Sanctioned status in NWN on September 30, 1996. The Guild began operation in Dark Sun: Crimson Sands in May, 1997, and received formal recognition as a DSO Pack on July 16, 1997. On July 19th, 1997, a Temple was formed in The Realm.

Where can the Servants of Mystery be found?

The Servants of Mystery currently have Temples in Dark Sun: Crimson Sands, Panterra, Ultima Online, and The Realm. The Neverwinter Nights Temple was destroyed on July 19th, 1997, when the Awful Over Lord (AOL) completed the destruction of Neverwinter and scattered the Neverwinter Community throughout the Cosmos.

Mystra, Goddess of Magic

Mystra is the Goddess of Magic in the Forgotten Realms. She is the Goddess of the power of magic itself, not the Goddess of spellcasters, although many spellcasters number themselves among her followers. To her alone is given the power to see through the eyes of the other gods and goddesses of the Forgotten Realms, that she may see how their strength of purpose colors even their perception of reality. Our Lady of Mysteries is the greatest of the Greater Powers of the Realms. When the word came that Neverwinter would be destroyed, the Servants of Mystery fled through an Astral Portal, transporting their Tower to the world of the Dark Sun, Athas. From there the Guild has spread to many new worlds, carrying the word of the One True Spell. Join us as we teach Mystra's wonders to those who would achieve greatness in service of Good!

St. Talia the True

Saint Talia the True epitomized the spirit of Mystra. This Paladin and Mage was the finest of the Knights of the Mystic Fire. She spent countless hours traveling The Forgotten Realms, aiding those too weak to survive on their own and questing to find items of power for use in combating evil. She served as Mystress of Apprentices during the peak period of growth of the Guild in Neverwinter Nights. Shortly after she was chosen to serve as Assistant Guild Mystress, she was taken to Mystra's side. The Servants of Mystery revere her as Saint and Protector of all who use magic in the service of Good. The Shrine of Saint Talia was consecrated in the Temple beneath the town of Longsaddle, where she dwelt before her ascendance. New Shrines will be consecrated in each world in which the Servants of Mystery travel. May her shining spirit continue to inspire us wherever we roam!

The Realm

The Realm is a multiplayer online AD&D game playable by Internet. The Realm is a product of Sierra Online. In The Realm, players can advance to high levels as any of four character types: Adventurer, Rogue, Warrior, or Wizard. Highly customizable player sprites, rich graphical detail, and an easy-to-use interface make The Realm a very popular spot for roleplayers. Join us in The Realm as we fight the never-ending battle against evil!

Sierra Online

Sierra Online is the creator of the popular King's Quest line of computer fantasy roleplaying games. These games are known for their rich visual content, and The Realm is no exception to that tradition. One cautionary note: America Online owns a significant portion of Sierra Online, so the situation bears careful watching, considering the way Neverwinter Nights players have been treated by AOL.
