Other Useful Sites:  

Attributes/Abilities:   For descriptions of the effects the attributes (strength, wisdom etc.) have, see attributes by EQ Stratics, EQ Insider's attributes page and abilities by EQlizer.  
Bind Locations: See Binding in Norrath by EQ Atlas for the locations where non-casters may be bound.  

Buying/Selling Items:

Allakhazam has classified ads and links for trade/auction sites.

Chat Abbreviations: Ever puzzled by what someone says?  Check Allakhazam's chat terms or the EQ Glossary by Talies the Wanderer.  
Class Titles: A simple chart of class titles (for example:  druid, wanderer, preserver, hierophant).
Con Messages: Allakhazam provides a list of con messages (threateningly, warmly etc.) by level.
Deities:   The official site has a list of major and minor deities, and EQ Stratics has a Deity Reference Chart.  
Disciplines:   For info on these special abilities of melee and hybrid classes, see the Treatise on Disciplines by the Seekers of Lore. 

Emotes/Commands:

EQ Stratics has a printer-friendly list of emotes and commands (/bow, /time etc.), and Allakhazam has separate emote and command lists.
Epic Quests: For information on the epic quests, see Epic Quest Adventures and Everlore's epic quest boards 
Equipment Effects:   Though not complete (where are the Elder Spiritist Vambraces, for example?), EQlizer offers a list of equipment spells/effects and which weapons and armor produce them.  
Etiquette:   Yes, etiquette counts.  See FatherMatt's own guide to EQ etiquette.  

Experience Tables:

To tell how many MOBs to kill for that next level, see these experience tables.  
Faction Improvement: If you are evil and want to improve your faction, see Anezka's Faction Guide for specific strategies.  (See also Faction in the chart above.)
Guilds:   Everlore has a guild database which lists all the EQ guilds, and Playervault has an EverQuest guild search.  
Hunting:   To see where to hunt for your level, try Allakhazam's zone level chart or the hunting guide by level from EQ Atlas, or the EQ Insider hunting database.  

Jewelry:

Casters Realm has the jewelry chart and jewelry/metal vendors list.
Keyboard Commands: GameGuides.com lists the default keyboard settings (scroll down).
KOS chart:   To see which races will kill you if you enter their cities (unless you have changed your faction), see this EQ Vault chart.  
Leading a Group: See FatherMatt's own tips for leading a group.  
/loc Command:   For info on using the /loc command, see the EQ Atlas /loc Guide.  
Locations: EQ Maps offers a list of key locations (zone entrances, rings etc.) with coordinates; you may want to print this and keep it handy.

Macros:

For basic info on macros, see Everlore's Hotkey/Social Bar Guide.  See The Library of Talies the Wanderer for commands for macros; these are also available from EQguild.net.  (These lists are perhaps more macro-oriented than the lists in "Emotes/Commands" above, though only the latter list the all-important /cast command!  Bah!)
Mana Calculators: Casters Realm offers mana calculators that calculate mana based on intelligence and on wisdom.  
Manual:   You can download the official manual for EverQuest here.  
Melee Attack Odds: EQlizer offers a Melee Attack Odds chart!
NeverWinter:   For information on NeverWinter Nights, check out FatherMatt's NeverWinter Nights Tower.   (No, this is not related to EQ, although it is AD&D.)  
Newbie Help: Have a friend just starting EverQuest?  Make sure they do the CD-ROM tutorial and change the auto-attack key from "a" to something else, then point them to Allakhazam's newbie guide, the EQ Atlas newbie guide, the Newbie Zone's Newbie FAQ and the EQ Stratics beginners guide (and possibly to "Chat Abbreviations", "Etiquette", "Keyboard Commands", "/loc Command" and "Tips & Hints" in this chart). 
News: For EQ news, try Everlore's headlines etc., the EQ Stratics news or the official Sony EQ news.

NPC's:

EQ Maps offers an NPC database searchable by type, race, class.
Official (Sony): Oh, all right:  here's the official Sony EQ site.
Patch History: For the latest patch message, see Sony's patch message page; CastersRealm provides a patch news history listing all the patches by date (click on each date for the details).
Pet Commands:  Planet EverQuest has a list of pet commands.
Ping/Trace Use the EverQuest Trace utility to check the speeds of your connection. Also see The Meaning of the Lag Bar, by Markemp the Wanderer.  
Raids (Planes): Sign up for Plane raids at Rordan's EverQuest Event Planner.  Also see the EQ raids boards at www.eqraids.com/personal; for this one you will need to get a password from your guild.  
Research:   Check the Mythiran Tower at Allakhazam for information on research; Casters Realm has research information as well.
Resistance Gear:   See Jewelry Based Resistance Suits by Tashia of Xegony.
Roleplaying:   Scrolleth down to "A Guide to Conversational Britannian" to learnst how to speak in the older tongue and roleplay!  (Yes, I know this was done for UO but it works well for EQ too.)
Search Engine: Jibantik offers a search-engine devoted to EverQuest.  See also "Site Search" below.  

Server Status:

Everlore offers the ever-popular server-status site, and Sony lists the network status and the  scheduled downtimes.  
Site Search: Everlore also offers a full site search, as does EQ Atlas on the left side of its home page.  (You'd think they'd all have this!)  See also "Search Engine" above.  
Skills:   Everlore's skills list describes all skills (combat, magic, other), and it offers a chart of when each class acquires a skill; there are also listings of skill caps by class at Casters Realm where you can also click a specific skill and see its cap formula.
Specialization: See the Casters Realm specialization page for recommended specialization areas, and Everlore's article on specialization for procedure (be sure to read the comments below this article). 
Spells:   See the Casters Realm's spell lists (by class) and the Everlore spell lists (by class).  The EQ Stratics spell database is useful to see which classes have a particular spell.

Text Colors:

EQ Stratics offers instructions on how to modify your text colors. Casters Realm offers a color wizard which is more automated.  
Tips & Hints:   For tips and hints, see Allakhazam's Gameplay Tips (although the first one is a bit odd) and FatherMatt's Odds & Ends.  
Tradeskills:   At EQ Traders you can learn a skill, see the  "recipes" for each of the trade skills, and track down elusive supplies with the trade supplies finder.  (Also see "Trade Skills" in the first chart above, and "Trivial Tables" below.)

Trivial Tables:

Trivial tables for trade skills can be found at the Casters Realm Trivial Charts.  

Utilities:

Everlore has a list of links for EverQuest utility programs. (Also see "Ping/Trace" above.)  

Velious Armor:

EQ Druids has a list of Velious armor quests/gems; locations where the Velious armor gems drop are at Guardians of Veeshan and Retrofied.  Also see Allakhazam's page on Velious Armor information.
Voice Commands: Yes, believe or not, EQ can be operated with voice commands, listed by Casters Realm. 

OK, I wasn't going to list Class-Oriented Sites because they come in and out of existence so quickly--but here they are.   
For additional class-specific information, see CastersRealm's FAQs for each class and class strategies.  
For additional class boards, see Everlore's class boards, EQ Stratics's class boards and  Casters Realm's class forums.  
If you know of other class-oriented sites and boards, shoot me an e-mail!
