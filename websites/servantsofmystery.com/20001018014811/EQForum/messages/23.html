ooopps .......
to get to GUK from Freeport;
travel to NRO and head for the water
follow the water till you zone
this will be the Oasis region follow the water till you reach the mountain follow it to the right till you reach a gap - enter it
you will zone to SRO
this zone is good to hunt also - dervish bandits
head right once you zone
follow the wall into the green area (leaving the desert - still in the same zone)
you will travel along the wall avoiding the orc camps stay close to the wall
you will encounter a passage (its green and hard to see unless you stay on the wall)
this will be kitty corner to the spot you zoned into SRO from
enter this passage and welcome to the Troll Swamps (zone)
turn left and follow the wall in the swamps
the entire zone is a large circle with hills marking the edge
once travelling left along the wall you will encounter a passage up a bit on the wall but very obvious
enter this and zone into GUK
warning dont travel too far past the entrance to the dungeon - the troll city is along the same wall further down (5 minute run past it)
good luck
