There are new battlefield areas for levels 20-35.  For a map of the new battlezones, head to www.daatlas.com/midgard/murdaigeanmap.html.  The map is apparently identical for all three zones, although which realm holds which fort changes with each of the three battlefields.  One caution:  if you print the map in black and white, it tends not to show the third portal fort (a fort where one realm teleports in).  The third portal fort ("pf") is  south of  the main keep in the middle, up against the mountains there and is fuschia; the other two portal forts are shown in pink.  (The main keep in the middle can change "ownership" depending on which realm successfully takes it in battle.)

In these battlefields (as in all Realm v. Realm combat areas), you do not suffer any experience penalty if you are killed by the enemy.  (You DO suffer this penalty if killed by a monster, as is usual.)  Note that unlike in non-battleground areas, guards will NOT protect you from monsters--even if they are the guards to your own realm's fort!  You cannot bind in the battlefield zone, so if you release you will wind up at your last realm bind point. 

To get to these battlegrounds, you need to find the teleportation pad (with a medallion merchant standing close by) in one of the gate keeps that is an entrance from your realm to the frontier territories.  (In Midgard, this pad is in the Uppland gate keep; I do not know which are the correct ones for Albion and Hibernia--shoot me an e-mail if you know.)  Buy a medallion from the merchant, stand on the pad and wait for the next teleport. 

When you arrive (which is inside the portal fort ("pf") for your realm), the first thing to do is to get into the battleground chat group, which generally includes several player groups.  Ask anyone you see (from your realm, of course!) to ask that you be added to the chat group.  If you can't safely locate anyone, use /yell to ask to be added.   Once added (you'll see the chat message start), use /c to talk to the chat group and use /cg who for a list of who is in the chat group.  Once you are in the chat group, if you are not in a player group already, ask which groups are available to join.  

And here's a reprint from the friendly folks at DAOC:

"We have added two new restricted RvR battlefield zones, which brings the total number of Battlefields up to 3. Each is restricted by level. The lowest is for levels 20-24, the second for levels 25-29, and the third is for levels 30-35. The intent of these battlefields is to provide a training ground where lower-level characters can practice and learn RvR tactics without having to worry about higher-level players marauding them. Each Battlefield has been configured to allow each player approximately 100 enemy "kills" before you will no longer be allowed to port there.

The Battlefields are:
- Caledonia - levels 30 - 35, Realm rank 1, Realm level up to 9;
- Murdaigean - levels 25 - 29, Realm rank 1, Realm level up to 5;
- Thidranki - levels 20 - 24, Realm rank 1, Realm level up to 3.

The medallion merchant's speech has been updated to reflect the changes within the Battlegrounds. Should you have questions on the function of the portals or which battleground your level/realm rank can travel to, please seek him out."

See you on the battlefield!
