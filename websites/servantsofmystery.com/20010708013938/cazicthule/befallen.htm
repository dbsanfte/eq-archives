7/25/99 - What has Befallen the Servants?   Sunday evening served up a most interesting adventure for the assembled Mystran host.  Ashnal, a Lady of Mystery, received a request for help from an elf warrior named Hyunkel.  Hyunkel had travelled into the ruins of Befallen and had promptly managed to leave his belongings behind him.

Ashnal send out a call to arms to her fellows in the Servants of Mystery.   Many responded to her call and fell upon Befallen in a determined formation, among them Neranek, Adon, Gleau, Nall, Eynon and the paladin in St. Talia's service, Cassus.   Stalking throught the ruins, Cassus was backed against a deep well by an undead skeleton lord and he toppled to his doom.  In the face of this double rescue mission, Mystra smiled upon us by allowing Hyunkel a relatively easy time in finding his belongings. 

Attempting to spot the broken body of Cassus, the magician Neranek overbalanced and also fell down the well.  He arose from the fall and was beset upon by a foul necromancer and his undead minions.  The Servants were determined to affect a rescue to the bottom of the well.  For many hours, these stalwart Mystran faithful attempted to overcome the odds and reach the third level of the Befallen ruins.   Finally, as the battle weary and bloodied Mystrans were nearly overcome with exhaustion, a most desparate plan was hatched.

Cassus bravely dove down the well and taunted the necromancer before dashing off into the tunnels below.  The enraged necromancer and his undead roared off in pursuit.  Hearing the sudden silence at the bottom of the well, Neranek once more plunged down the well.  He quickly gathered up the belongings of Cassus and his own, and cast a gate spell to deliver him back to the top of the well. 

Through the brave sacrifice of Cassus, he and Neranek were able to reclaim their belongings.  Weary, the Servants departed the ruins and camped for the night, vowing to return in the future to rout the evil.
