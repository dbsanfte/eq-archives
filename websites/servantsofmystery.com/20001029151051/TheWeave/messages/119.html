Bardic and Archery Contests

[ Follow Ups ] [ Post Followup ] [ TheWeave ]

Posted by Thranduil on May 14, 1999 at 09:14:33:

The elves of tel'Mithrim are hosting two contests next week.

Sunday, the 16th, at 3 pm (Central) will be a bardic competition at the Silver Arrow tavern.

There will be three divisions of competition : Dramatic, Comedic, and Limerick.

The contest will be scored by three randomly chosen judges.

Please keep the length of each entry to five minutes or under, and make sure your entry is in the common tongue of Britannia.

Prizes include a massive silver crossbow of might, various colored sets of armor, floor harps, some gold, and assorted oddities.

For more information on the bardic contest, contact Camris (camris@grey-company.org).


Thursday, the 20th, at 6 pm (Central) will be an archery competition at the tel'Mithrim archery range.

Advanced registration is free by sending a message to Glorfindle (glorfindle@grey-company.org). Last minute registration begins one hour before the event, and will be at the Silver Arrow tavern. Last minute registration will cost 500 gold.

The contest is a bracket event, single elimination, blind draw.

Each game will consist of three rounds of five arrows. The archer with the highest average score for the three rounds will advance.

Exceptional bows are allowed. Magical bows are not allowed.

Prizes will be awarded for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place.


Questions on the archery contest should be directed to Glorfindle.

Anyone needing directions to the Silver Arrow can contact Camris, Glorfindle, or me.

Further information is available at our event Calendar.

Hope to see you there.

Thranduil,
Nikerym tel'Mithrim
