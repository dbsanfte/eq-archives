SOM Conduct Guidelines

With respect to everyone:

Do not use foul language (there are kids playing the game), make racist or sexist remarks (such as commenting on a female character’s physique) or call someone "gay" or "fag".  If there really is a need to insult someone, find a more creative way to do it.  Don't use the word "rape" to describe what you did to that MOB:  many women players  justifiably take offense at this. 

With respect to guildmates:

! Guildmates are civil to each other, even if you disagree--and even if you don't like the other person. 
! Guildmates do not insult one another. 
! When there are discussions, guildmates stick to the objective issues and do not engage in personal attacks. 
! More specifically, say a) what the objective behavior was (in-game action, post, speech), b) how it made you feel and c) what you would like done about it in the future.  Do not make accusations.  
! Guildmates never assume that another guildmate has acted out of malice.  If you're hurt or offended by something, ask the other person about it, but do not accuse them of intentionally acting badly.
! If you have an issue, complaint etc., DO bring it up, but do it in accordance with the above.  If it involves a specific person, however, start with that person first, rather than taking it public first!  Alternatively, talk to the Guild Master or other Guild officers.
