FatherMatt's Tips for Newbies

*First, go to http://runescape.com/worldmap.html to see the main map for Runescape.  Bookmark this site and put an icon for it on your desktop. Always have this open when you play (even if it is minimized or in the background), because you will be referring to it constantly.

*You start in the town of Lumbridge. By clicking the scroll icon near the upper right of your game-view window, a small map of the area you are in pops up.  As you travel, go to the red dots and see if there’s anything worth picking up and reselling. 

*As best you can, buy chest and leg armor, a helmet, a shield and a weapon--even if you are a wizard.  Upgrade as you are able.

*There is combat practice room in Varrock across from the bank.  Go there to practice combat safely. You can only earn a certain amount of combat experience here, and will see a message saying so after you max out. 

*Eat food between battles to heal, and gather food prior to battle.  One free source:  the cabbages from the Draynor Manor field; these not only heal one hit point but increase your defense by 1 point for a while. 

*In any battle, be sure to run if it looks like you may die--and keep running until you are sure you aren't being pursued.  A good place to begin hunting is across the river from Lumbridge; goblins are the target.

*You WILL die from time to time.  When you die you lose all items on your person except the three most expensive items, so be sure to always leave money in the bank to recover from deaths. 

*Pick up and bury bones to raise your prayer ability.  Remember to renew your prayer power by clicking on altars. 

*As soon as you can get some gold together, do a silk run as set out in Money-making. Put 60 back in the bank when done, then go buy better armor, weapon and shield.

*Watch out for scams!  They are common and can seem innocent.  Read the Scams section and say "I'm not interested."
