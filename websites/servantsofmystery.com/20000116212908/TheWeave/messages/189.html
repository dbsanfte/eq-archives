Posted by Lluwen Ranger of the Faydark on August 27, 1999 at 09:16:58:

Hail to all.

I had the honor of adventuring with some of the Servants last night in PAW. While for the most part It was good fun, runnibg naked from Qeynos to South Karana is something I hope I do not have to do 3 times in a row again .

Alas the blade of a foul Dark elf (no slur to you Ren) did me in early on. Later is luck would have it (or the lack there of) I followed the group on a underwater trek. Just a short swim, one I actually made easily. However, upon exiting the water, I must have slipped and fell back in...Taking over 1/2 my hits from falling Damage.

Attempting to swim back to the surface I got caught under a ledge or something and could not reach the surface. Alas one really feels stupid when he drowns. To make matters worse, my foolishness got me demoted to 15.

Again making the run back to Paw, I had the worst luck possible unable to forage any water my run was dragging to a walk. Then at the worst possible time, my camoflage spell wore off and I was set upon by nasty ogres.

Setting out again from Qeynos I again could not find someone willing to help a naked elf with a SoW or even a bit of food so I could at least keep my strength up. I arrived very late back at the Paw. My comrades having long since retiring for the night. Myself having an early call was way past a time to camp. perhaps this evening I'll attempt to reclaim my body If you are around, hail me I know I would enjoy the company.

Lluwen Ranger of the 16...15th Degree
