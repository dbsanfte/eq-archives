FatherMatt's Odds & Ends

To automatically increase your Sense Heading skill, adjust your keyboard in the game.  First, if you haven't already, set up "Sense Heading" as a macro key on the left side of your screen.  As an example, let's say you put it in the first slot.  Then press the Options button, then keyboard.  Flip until you get to the screen that contains User Identified #1.  Right click on the secondary slot for this key, then press your Up Arrow button.  Now, whenever you use the Up Arrow key to move forward in the game, it will automatically do a  Sense Heading.  (A tip of the helm to Wylyth for this hint.) 

Batwings and fish scales--along with tiny daggers for the enchanters and many gems--can be bought in unlimited quantities at a hut at the inn in East Commons that is right on the border with West Commons.  (This is also true in the Arcane Academy in West Freeport and in Ogguk, but the East Commons site is the closest to a teleport.)

Always have a location macro (/loc) handy.  If it looks like you may die, hit this macro button repeatedly.  If you do die, you can scroll back to get the location of your corpse.  If you forget and can't find your corpse, enlist the help of a bard (level 4+) or necro (level 1+) or, if desperate, get at least two Chipped Bone Rods, which can be used to help locate your corpse. (These can be purchased from any merchant that sells potions; most cities have them; cost is 3 to 10 plat apiece.  Be sure to target yourself before trying to use it.) 

In the middle of the sea, you can enter a boat by getting in front of it, ducking down (use the "d" key), letting the boat go over you, then standing up from the duck (hit "d" again") when the boat is over you.  Doing this will place you inside the boat.

If you are sitting, /camp desktop will take you fully out of EverQuest.

If you have a pet, press F1 twice to target it (or once, if you yourself are the current target).  If you are in battle with multiple monsters, pressing F8 often will switch among them. 

Strongly consider reserving one bag for Charisma gear--and put these items on before buying and selling.  (Don't forget to put your regular gear on afterwards!)  Over time, this can save an enormous amount of plat.  One possible item:  a Silk Evening Tunic, +5 CHA.  This is available from Iala Lenard (?) just south of the Lion's Mane Inn (outside, under the tent) in South Qeynos for approximately 6pp.
