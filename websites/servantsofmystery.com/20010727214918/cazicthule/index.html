8/01/99 - Revolt of the Undead King!  Apparently Cazic-Thule (the god) doesn't much care for being beaten on by 30 heroes around the levels of 45 - 50.  (see http://www.cazic-thule.com/ for the story there)   He sent his minion, the Undead King, to invade the Oasis of Marr.  The Undead King was a towering skeletal presence who appeared to dwarf even the regions Sand Giants.   He summoned a small army of ghouls, zombies and spectres to slay any hapless travellers that they came across.  Once the field was littered with bodies, the Undead King disappeared.  Presumably, he returned to report back to Cazic-Thule.
