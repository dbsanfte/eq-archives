Tacvi Flag = 2 days work
Friday, April 01, 2005
Posted by: Akanay
We came, we saw, Rayvenn went down on it. Thats pretty much the story.

It all started with Peoni deciding we were going to get keyed for Tacvi.

Wednesday Tacvi key mobs were up, so we offed them with a minimal amount of difficulty, and got keyed for the final
Txenu mob, Zun'Muram.

Thursday was the big task, the goal was to learn the encounter so next time we could blow through it. The problem was,
no one really wanted to go through that stupid shit another time.

There was much fighting, and rezzing. There was sharing, and caring, and even people discovering their sexual identities...


But in the end, all it took was Rayvenn going down on the mob.



We all crept in tenatively as Rayvenn blew the leader, Zun'Muram, out footsteps muffled by the slurping noises... and just as we heard Rayvenn say "don't cum in my mouth" we sprung it, giving the mob everything we had, and discovered the strategy that worked, thats right, the element of surprise!

And in the end, we remembered what was important and teamwork, and that made all the difference






Aegis of Discord to Haabx

Shroud of Ceaseless Might to Kausali

Wristband of Chaotic Warfare to Eollis

Kaftan of Embroidered Light to Clearpath


And that children, is the story of how Defiant (and apps!) broke into Tacvi in two days.
Hate Down
Sunday, March 27, 2005
Posted by: Akanay
down and to go!!

 Thats right, three more trials to go for CoA access. I think we locked down our strat for this trial.

Basically, we were in Riftseekers warming up before we did the Hatred MPG trial, and a ring war seemed to spawn at the entrance. We quickly mustered our forces, thinking the 50 Hate running around must have to do with the trial of Hatred right?

It was weird though, where as hatred is strategic and tricky til you get your strat, the ring at Riftseekers ent was chaotic and not hard at all. Those involved had no coordination or strategy. However, once we cleared this ring with only 5 losses, one of those two a mob, we entered Hatred trial, and were able to clear it easily.
Having mastered our strategy, I see what people mean about this being easy, the trial and the ring of Hate
