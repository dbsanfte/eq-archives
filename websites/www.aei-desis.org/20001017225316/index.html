Chardok Raid last week

We had an incredible Chardock Raid last week. Thanks to Lamhantar for pulling it all together. I should have taken screen shots. We ended up going along with a group from another guild to the Sarnak Royal family hideout. =P Course knowing how evil those Sarnaks are, we immediately began to dispatch them! Flip was vailent with his flashing sword and shouting above the battle .. To Arms! Assist me! Don't fear my friends! What at battle it was too. Once we entered inside the palace, we were swarmed by it must have been about 30 sarnaks defending the room. We charged the room. It was incredible how many we killed just to have a new group spawn immediately behind it. We battled until our mana was exhasted and our arms worn out from lifting our swords. And still the sarnaks continued to stream in. We tried to retreat to a different room to no avail. I think Tawney was the last standing with about 15 sarnaks grinning evilly at me. I must say we are very good at CR. =) We all got our corpses back very fast and got res'd and none the worse. Thank you to everyone for a fantastic raid and a very fast recovery from the deaths.

Fear raid a complete success!

Fellow Guild Members,

I'd like to thank everyone for the amazing turnout in Fear yesterday. Out of all the people that signed up, all but one was able to make it. We even had extra people show up. This just proves how successfull we can be when motivated properly.

The entire raid was done fairly fast and with minimum downtime. Also, a lot of nice items dropped. This is the way things should be and are going to be for a long time to come.

There will be as many planes raids as it takes until all our characters are fully equiped in plains gear. Also, Deafoe is being recruited to lead us thru some Sky Plane raids in the next month or so. He has vast experience in Sky, I think they named a cloud after him or something.

We have had a great guild for sometime due to the way we recruit. Now it's time to enjoy ourselves without all the bickering that goes on in most large guilds that form quickly solely for the purpose of obtaining Uber loot.

I'd like to ask that everyone check the raids category on the forums section of the web site frequently. Lets keep the thunder rolling and not let this new attitude die.

Special thanks to Allistair, Lerinya, Durham, and Sunitte for stepping up the pace when me and Nemeweh were LD for so long.

Each and everyone one of you should be very proud of yourselves. We acomplished a lot yesterday.
