Near the docks in butcher block, you will find a dwarf named
Dru Razbine.  Talk to him eventually he will give you a combiner
with two slots.

You now need the Chalice of Conquest and the Candle of Bravery.
To get the Candle of Bravery you need to get Soil of the Underfoot
You also need a Honeycomb (Bixies drop them in Misty Thicket).
Next head to Lake Rathe, there find the green orc camp.  There
kill off the orcs then an Abandoned Orc Shovel should be found
on the ground.  Now head to Rathe Mountains find the Paladin
Camp (Same as Armour of Ro's), ask Nicholas about the
Candle of Bravery, eventually he should give you part of the
candle (I'm unsure of this but I believe he will give you Klunga's
Braclet as well).  Find the Unkempt Preservers camp, keep
killing them eventually you should get the base of the candlestick.

Run to Halas and enter Dok's Cigars.  Ask him of the Candle
of Bravery, give him the required pieces (base, stick, soil and
honeycomb).   Dok should then give you the Candle of Bravery.

Now travel all the way back to Faydwer and in Dagnor's Cauldron
go to the Orc Scout camp, keep killing the scouts until an Exiled
Orc Legionaire spawns (Probably every few EQ days).
You should then receive a Bloodstained Note.  Now find
Ghilanbiddle Nylwadil, give him 100gp the bloodstained note
and Klunga's braclet.

Race back to the Orc Camp (pray that no one kills him), and give
Captain Klunga the Abandoned Orc Shovel.  Follow him (pray more
that others do not kill him), follow him to a remote spot.  A bag will
appear at his feet, pick it up immediatly.  Captain Klunga will then
attack you, kill him or get away quickly.

Combine the Candle of Bravery and Chalice of Conquest together in the
bag Dru gave you.  Take the result to Lord Datur Nightseer Kaladim's
Paladin Guildmaster.  Datur will then give you a Cape of Underfoot, take
this to Dru Rzbine and he will give you the Holy Partisan of Underfoot.

The Holy Partisan of Underfoot was found by Jiae Soulmender of
Veeshan.  This was taken from his Exploits.
