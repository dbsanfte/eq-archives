Shining Star of Light

    In Qeynos near the Temple of Life (also known as the flying
saucer), Astaed Wemore will ask you if you would Travel Afar,
tell him you would.  (Note : if Astaed will not give the quest he
will also not reward you, and possibly kill you to boot.)  He will
send you to Rivervale to talk to Kizzie Mintop.
    Upon entering Rivervale find the Fool's Gold (Octagon shaped
building 3 or 4 stories), walk around it and one way will have two
houses, the back one is Kizzies'.  Get 3 Honeycombs (bixies drop
it, buy it for 5gp or so from PCs) and 30gp and give these to Kizzie
all at once.  She will give you Honey Jum in return.
    Run back to Astaed Wemore give him the Honey Jum.  This is an
odd part, there are currently two theories on this :
    1) If you take to long Lepeck sucums to the disease and kills you
    2) Lycanthrops is a night-time disease and if you give it to him at
    night he kills you (or tries)
    I've done it twice and was never attack so I don't know...  Anyway
To find Lempeck enter WKaranas from QHills and head on an angle to
the right (more right than straight), once you hit the river follow it and
before long you should find fields there.  In approximately the middle of
these fields you will find Lempeck's House, normally he is inside, yet
he has been known to run on the roof-top (possibly people kill him for
exp).  He will then give you a No Drop Lore version of a Rusty Scythe.
Return this back to Astaed Wemore for your reward, the Shining Star of Light
