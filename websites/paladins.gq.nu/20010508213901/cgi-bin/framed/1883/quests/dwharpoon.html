Deepwater Harpoon

    One Serna Tasknon in the Temple of Marr in North Freeport
will give you a potion of Marr, take this to 7 the Sentries in Alphabetical
Order (Andlin, Boris, Gallius, Janeal, Meighan, Theo, Warren).
    Next go to the Ocean of Tears, and get off and Sisterhood of
Erollisi Island.  There ask the leader, "Where is Sentry Xyrinn?".
Sentry Xyrinn will then spawn on an Undead Isle (straight off the docks,
to Oracle Island, go 1/4 the way around the island to the right, now swim
straight to the Undead Isle).  At the top of the island there are 4 spectres,
Kill the spectres and keep them down, then give Sentry Xyrinn the Last
Potion of Marr. She will run up the hill to where the spectres would be,
they will agro on her if they spawn so you get them first.  After approximately
10 minutes, Sentry Xyrinn will thank you and drop the Deepwater Harpoon
on the ground (literally).  Be VERY careful of poachers I have heard many stories where
someone steals it.

Mob Level Range : 35
Reward : Deepwater Harpoon
Other Loot : Box of Abu'Kar etc
