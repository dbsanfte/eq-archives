Zimel's Blades (Soulfire)

    Enter the Temple of Truth (Freeport), go right,
then first right, up the stairs, left then left.  There
should be one Kalatrina Plossen. Talk to her,
she will tell you of one of our spies in the militia
house, and give you a letter to give to him.  He
will then give you a piece of paper   Wait around
for a few moments and he will be attacked by
Krazen (from the Bank in NFP).  I don't know
what happens if you save him, probably end up
with the militia house on your head.
    Kalatrina Plossen had instructed you to take
what this spy gives you back to her.  At this point
if you do the quest seems to be broken, DO NOT
give it to her, take it to Groflah in Groflah's forge.
    He will instruct you to meet him in Sea Farer's
Roost in EFP (on the docks).  He will show up
around 1-3am (/time), give him the note back
and ask him now who Zimel was.
    After Groflah leaves a beggar named Tykar
Renlin is nearby, give him 4 Drom's Champagne
(these are Lore), if your faction is not high enough
you must get another to buy them for you.  In
exchange Tykar will give you a blanket.
    Try Brownloe Bakery (near monks) and see if
they have any Edible goo, if not you need to make it.
Find an Oven and place in it a Rat Ear and a Fire
Beetle Eye.  Now unless you know a Brewer you must
also make some Bog Juice.
    Take these to Zimel underneath the Arena, give him
the Blanket, Bog Juice and Edible Goo.  He will give
you H.K. 102.
    Now run to High Keep and give the Key to the Bank
Cleric.  She will give you a note in exchange.
    Make towards Splitpaw in SKaranas, within it you will
find Brother Hayle, give him the note (you may have to be
kindly at this point).  He will tell you what you need then
give you the note back.  (You need a way out of his cell,
as it closes. A friend outside or a nifty Gate potion 1300pp!)
    Complete the Test of Truth in Freeport, next head to
Mistmoore (just off of Lfaydark), find the evil shadow
knight Xicotl (level 35ish).  Now we're in the home stretch
Race back to Freeport and complete the Brilliant Sword
of Faith quest.
    Make sure your faction with Brother Hayle is up to kindly,
(Qeynos factions), then give him the items.
    You may now proudly wield the holy sword Soulfire.

Found by Unknown, Dwarven Paladin.

Mob Level Range : 35-48
Reward : Soulfire
Other loot : Coins, Misc.
