Orc Runner

    In Felwithe talk to Lord Jyleel, he will send you on a
quest for the Orc Runner.  Quickly run to Butcher Block
the Orc Runner is normally found near the two buildings
across from one another.
    Kill this runner and return what you find upon him to
Lord Jyleel.  Lord Jyleel will then give you the Falchion
of Koada'Vie quest.

Mob Level Range : Submit
Reward : Potions, etc
Other loot : Coins

Note :  Some have experienced becoming KoS with FP militia though I had
to complete it 2x due to a bug and was not KoS.
