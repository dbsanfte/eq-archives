Bandit Sisters

    The High Elf Guildmaster will ask you if you are
"ready to serve the Clerics of Tunare", reply and he
will ask you if you are new, or experienced.
    Answer new, he will then tell you that you need
to talk to fallen paladin named Tandan Nybright,
otherwise known as the 'a drunkard'.
    Hail the Drunkard as Tandan Nybright (normally
found wandering Kelethin), he will tell you of
his four daughters who, ran off and joined the
dervish cutthroats.  He wants you to kill his daughters
and return their necklaces and return them to him all
at once.

Mob Level Range : 10 to 13
Reward : Ringmail if your faction is good enough
Other Loot : Bronze & rusty weapons
