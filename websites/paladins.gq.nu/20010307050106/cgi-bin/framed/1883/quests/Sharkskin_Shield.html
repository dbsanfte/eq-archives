Sharkskin Shield

    A knight in the Temple of Truth needs shark skins
to make a new shield.  He will give you a two slot
container to fill with shark skins.
   You can kill sharks in the harbor or even buy them.
Although its probably not worth it.

Mob Level Range : Submit
Reward : Potions or even the Sharkskin Shield (3 ac,
                 yuck!)
Other Loot : Shark pieces
