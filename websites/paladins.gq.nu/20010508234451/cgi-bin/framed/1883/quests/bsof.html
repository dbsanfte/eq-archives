Brilliant Sword of Faith

    For this part of the quest you need to gather a group (or two!)
to kill Sir Lucan D'Lere.  Lucan spawns every 12 hours in the
Militia house.  You need to pull him out of the house to the newbie
area (give them warning not to touch Lucan or cast on your party).
Kill Lucan and his undead self.  Loot his Testimony and return it to
Valeron Dushire, the paladin guildmaster of Freeport.

Mob Level Range : Submit
Reward : Brilliant Sword of Faith
Other Loot : Boots of Brawn?
