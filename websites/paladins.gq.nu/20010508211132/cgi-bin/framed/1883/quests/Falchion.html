Falchion of Koada'Vie

Complete the Orc Runner's Quest then Lord Jyleel wants
you to [prove yourself a faithful paladin of the court].
For this you need to kill Ambassader D'Vinn and take his
Black Heart, next you need to kill The Prophet and
and take his blue orc head.
Now go out into the Ocean of Tears, and you will
soon see rocks with sirens upon them.  Abandon
Ship and swim to these, apparently if you kill them
Brawn will spawn (Apparently level 22), if not he
spawns at 5 am Norrath Standard Time.  Unforetunately
he has two Dark Elf Dragoons (18-22) with him.
Kill him and take his Crate.

Return these to Lord Jyleel for the Falchion of
Koada'Vie.  Basically its our Langseax though
slightly weaker and luckily its undrop =)
