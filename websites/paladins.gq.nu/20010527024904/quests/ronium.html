Ronium

    Do fool yourself, the making of Ronium is every bit as hard
as obtaining the molds, in some cases much harder.
    Talk to Lord Searfire the Dwarven smith in the Temple of
Ro and ask him about Ronium, you need high faction which
can be obtained these three ways :
   - Kill Shadowed Men
   - In South Ro give the erudite Rathamana 3pp in coins, use
   this only if you have money to burn.
   - In South Ro give 4 Dervish Cutthroat Insignia Rings to
   Ortallius, a named friendly orc.

    Once you have the faction required (amiable), you'll need:
      - Sands of Ro, from Sand Giants in Ro & Oasis (34-8)
      - Mistmoore Granite, from Flouting, Jeering, and Leering
      gargoyles from Mistmoore.  (30-38)  No Drop
      -  Melatite, from Clockwork Gnomes CWG and MC, in
      Solusek A (lowers Ak'Anon factions).  (30+)
      -  Soil of the Underfoot No Drop
      -  2 bars of Enchanted Platinum from Enchanters

    It should no longer be required to be a Master Smith to
combine.

Mob Level Range : 30 - 39
Reward : Ronium
Other loot : Misc.
