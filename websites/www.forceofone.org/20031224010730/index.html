The Albanian hip hop nation is growing every day here in the states & overseas, Albanian groups have adopted a level of skill that will give give any western counterpart a competition.  Some of these Albanian Groups will make it big here in the states, one example we have is the group Bloody Alboz, and we feel as if these groups will be recognizable like the famous "Albanians" the Belushi brothers remembered by their famous Blues Brothers appearance.  They defiantly deserve it like the Albanian rapper "Rebel" his already seen by many Albanians as "The Albanian 2 PAC", and this is just one example of a talented rapper that we have discovered, there are many other talented artists and groups based in the Detroit area. 

Also in the other hand we can't forget the rappers overseas and other countries outside the U.S. There are many groups that are really famous Ritmi i Rrugës, which has been herd by almost every Albanian, and some of the other groups well known are WNC, Tingulli 3, Etno Engjujt, Danny Boy, Gold Snakes and many more that we didn't mention.  Most of these Albanian Rappers have produced English songs as well.  Even the rappers here in the states started making rap music first back at their homeland, one perfect example is "Rebel" a.k.a Unikkatil making the first explicit songs at age twelve.  This was the reason why this Artist wasn't herd all around the Albanian regions, however other rappers which weren't ready to break the ice gained much fame.  We first recognized this talented group when someone from the former Prishtina Clan renamed one of "Rebel a.k.a Unikkatil's" song, and as pathetic as it sounds we even contacted that person, via AOL IM and he took the full credit for it.  And using his "claimed song" for talent he recalled the reason for leaving Prishtina Clan, referring to himself as the only one who had the skillz of being the only good rapper in his group. Now the former Prishtina Clan is known as "Albo Holics" and is gaining ground and respect in the Albanian Rap Community after kickin out that pathetic loser who stole Unikkatil's song. Recently they were taken under Conqueror Records and Rebel a.k.a. UniKKatiL is their new producer so expect the unexpected, just wait till you hear some of their upcoming awesome songs.
