GUILD CHARTER

INTRO:

We are a guild of friends who are loosely organized so we can have fun while we play. The guild was born out of another, which followed heavy politics and had all the internal struggles associated with some guilds. Hey, most of us have enough stress in real life! We pay to play this game to relax and have some fun! We try to maintain a family type attitude with each other and would ask that you do the same.

POLICIES

Members may be of any race or class.

We have no level limitations.

We ask that you know a character (have hunted with them) before you invite them.

Treat your guild family with Dignity, Honor, and Respect. We like the virtues of life rather than the avarices.

We like to help others when we can; even characters outside the guild.

We don’t like being taken advantage of just because we are nice. We will defend what we believe in.

If you need help with something let your guild mates know. This is not an open invitation to be greedy though.

We all help each other with game mechanics and leveling. This doesn’t mean that we will do everything for you. If you want something, show some initiative.

If you come into this guild and act like a jerk, you will be removed. No offense.

Play fair and try to follow basic game etiquette. We all understand what this means: No intentional kill stealing, no training monsters on someone intentionally, share loot with your friends who helped you, etc, etc.

We will have leaders for Raids and Quests. Lets face it, it is impossible for many characters to achieve a single goal without leadership. Cooperate with them.

We have officers to maintain stability in the guild. Remember, they were chosen because they have demonstrated that they are leaders, have wisdom, and follow the guild policies to the best of their ability.
