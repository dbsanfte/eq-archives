1: What's up with Hearts of Heroes anyway?
 Hearts of Heroes was formed in February of 2002 when a number of members of another large guild began coming to terms with difficulties and tensions present within.  The decision was made to spawn a new Guild built on principles and ethics of the highest order. Please take a look at the Guild's Charter and see what these principles and policies entail.  We seek only to play EQ with integrity and honor, and have a good deal of fun in the process.

[Top]

2:: How big is this Guild?
Hearts of Heroes is currently a small guild consisting of approximately 50 characters.  Our roster will show you exactly who and how many belong to our Guild family, so check back and watch us grow!

[Top]

3: Why should I join Hearts of Heroes instead of another, bigger Guild?
Huge and Uber Guilds offer their advantages ~and~ disadvantages.  Weigh carefully what your expectations are from a Guild and research it thoroughly before choosing.  A wrong choice can lead to frustration and disillusionment  Hearts of Heroes stands for quality before quantity, for success through honest effort, and for caring for others.  We foster a family type relation within the guild.  Members look out for each other and help out to the best of our ability.

[Top]

 4: What must I do to become a member?
Seek out an officer.  Do a "/who all Hearts" to find a member and simply state your desire or interest.  Someone from the guild will put you in touch with an officer.  The officer will likely ask you some down to earth questions and may well wish to observe  you fight with one of our groups to observe your methods.  There are no character restrictions in place to join Hearts of Heroes, but we seek to accept members who are skillful in both game-play and in getting along as a team member.

[Top]

5: Do you class-balance this Guild?
No, there are no class, race, or level requirements.  Your main and alts are all welcome regardless of their types.  Note that during a Raid, you may be asked to balance a group if you have an especially needed alt available.

[Top]

6: How do I find out about Guild meetings and Raids?
Guild events are announced in advance using the Guild Message of the Day (type "/guildmotd" ) and on our Webiste's Calendar of Events.  Sometimes, in-game Word-of-Mouth is also in progress if something special is in progress (i.e. a GM event).  E-mail notification of special events is possible, but not set up at this time.

[Top]

7: How do I make a suggestion or Request to the Guild Leaders?
Post your ideas or suggestions on the Forum, or sign the Guestbook and leave your comments.  You may also email the Guild directly.
