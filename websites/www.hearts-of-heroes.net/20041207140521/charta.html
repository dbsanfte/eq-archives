POLICIES

Members may be of any race or class.

We have no level limitations.

We ask that you know a character (have hunted with them) before you invite them.

Treat your guild family with Dignity, Honor, and Respect. We like the virtues of life rather than the avarices.

We like to help others when we can; yes even characters outside the guild.

We don’t like being taken advantage of just because we are nice. We will defend what we believe in.

If you need help with something let your guild mates know. This is not an open invitation to be greedy though.

We all help each other with game mechanics and leveling. This doesn’t mean that we will do everything for you. If you want something, show some initiative.

If you come into this guild and act like a jerk, you will be disbanded. No offense.

Play fair. Try to follow basic game etiquette. We all are adults and understand what this means: No intentional kill stealing, no training monsters on someone intentionally, share loot with your pals who helped you, etc, etc.

We will have leaders for Raids and Quests. Lets face it, it is impossible for many characters to achieve a single goal without leadership. Try to cooperate with them.

We have officers to maintain stability in the guild. Remember, they were chosen because they have demonstrated that they are leaders, have wisdom, and follow the guild policies to the best of their ability.

Guild Laws:

These are the basic rules that guide the behavior and organization of our Guild. Compliance and Understanding with these precepts are vital to the organization and continuation of the guild.

1. Guild Members must comply with Sony’s Nice Play Rules

No Kill Stealing Mob's, intentional training, harassment, ninja looting, game play interference, unethical activities, exploits, revenge and/or maliciousness.

2. Guild Guidelines & Rules

All Probationary Members and Guild Members are expected to conduct themselves within the general guidelines of etiquette provided under our Expectations of Etiquette.

All Probationary Members and Guild Members that wish to participate in Raids/Events/Operations must comply with our Guidelines that govern those activities.

3. Guild Members will never willingly behave in a self-serving way to the detriment of other Guild Members

No Overcharging, Fraud/Cheating, begging (for items, money, power leveling), and/or dishonorable activities (cowardice, dishonesty, causing death/danger to group or guild mates due to irresponsibility, etc.).

Accountability: Use of 'Anonymous' (/anon) should be avoided. Please us 'Roleplay' (/role) mode if you do not wish to show your current Class and Level.

4. Guild Operations

The Guild Spell Bank and Guild Mules are herby created to store extra items our members wish to donate for the betterment of the Guild. They shall be located in the Bazaar Bank or where ever he or she chose's.

Guild Coffers are hereby created. The Guild Coffers shall be held by characters created for the sole purpose of holding guild funds and/or equipment in the Bazaar Bank or his or her chose.

5. Guild Hierarchy

The Guild Creator has final decision on all matters within the guild. Being considered beyond reproach at institution of this document, his decision in all matters is considered final. The Guild Leader may create Officer positions to fulfill any task that Guild Leader feels is necessary. His decision to create these positions is final, and he may terminate such position at any time.
