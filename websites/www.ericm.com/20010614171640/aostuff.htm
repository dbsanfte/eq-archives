Ok, this page is not going to be anything special.  You won't see any fancy stuff here... and very little formating on top of everything else.  This is a hidden page, meant just for us currently in BAD.  I wanted a place where I could show you all a small piece of a very cool game. 

So, why is Takumi never in EQ anymore? What the heck is he doing that he has no time to visit Norrath?? Well... all of my time is now spent in a game called Anarchy Online.  It is currently still in beta testing, but the release date has been set for June 24, 2001.... thats coming up very soon.   Ultimately what I would like to see is as many of you as possible playing Anarchy Online, because I know that once you try it, EQ will be easy to forget about. 

So far, there are still quite a few bugs in AO.   They have some real problems with crashing and such, but they are working hard on it... and even with the problems, AO to me is far more fun, exciting, and addicting then EQ ever was.  Below is some links, some to screen shots and to a couple movies.   Take a look...  and seriously give AO some thought.  The game is going to be great... and it is just made for clans....
