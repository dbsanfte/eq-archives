Some engineers from Freeport were on a survey mission and found that the Everfrost/Blackburrow border must be changed. They stopped to inform a barbarian that he was no longer in Everfrost but in Blackburrow. After a long pause, he grunted and said, "That's good. I couldn't take another one of these Everfrost winters."

A Ranger on vacation from Qeynos was visiting Grobb, and decided she wanted to pick up some gator skin boots. After haggling with a troll merchant who wanted over five platinum for a pair, the ranger finally exclaimed, "Well maybe I'll just go out into the swamp and get my own alligator so I can get some boots made at a reasonable price!" The merchant indicated that she was free to try, and that she might even run into a pair of warriors who had the same idea earlier in the day. The ranger heads into Innothule and comes upon a pair of burly barbarians standing waist deep in the water, obviously the two warriors. Suddenly, a huge gator swims towards one of the warriors, jaws agape. Just as it's about to attack, he grabs it with his bare hands and chokes it to death. The two warriors pull the carcass onto shore and flip it onto its back next to a huge pile of dead gators. One of the warriors exclaims, "Damn it! This one doesn't have any boots on either!"

Gonk - by MadMonk

A troll shadowknight named Gonk was attacked by a swamp alligator, chewed horribly, and killed. The Grobb shaman called in Gonk's two best friends, Zeke and Bufu, to identify the body. First, he brought Zeke in and showed him the body. Zeke said, "Yup, it look like Gonk but him chewed up sum' bad. Roll him over." The shaman was confused but rolled Gonk over. "Nope dis ain't Gonk" said Zeke. The shaman then decided to bring in Bufu and show him the corpse. Bufu said, "Yup, it look like Gonk but him chewed up sum' bad. Roll him over." The shaman did so. "Nope dis ain't Gonk" said Bufu. "How can you be sure?" asked the shaman. "Cuz Gonk had two arseholes" replied Bufu. "Two arseholes! How you know that?" asked the shaman. "Well, every time wez a coming into Grobb, all the guards all says 'Look! There's Gonk wit them two arseholes.'"

Bumping Bottoms - by Qinimil

A troll couple are sitting out back in the swamp one evening. Popa Troll looks over at Moma Troll and says, "Durn, yah gotz ta hab da biggest arse I hab ebber seen."

Mama Troll shrieks, "I du not!"

Popa Troll retorts, "Bah, yu du! I bet hit iz biggern anywun in da hoe dang swamp."

Getting even madder, Mama Troll sneers and says, "I don needer."

Popa Troll continues, "Betcha yer arse iz evun biggern me grill I use ta kook dwarfis in."

Mama Troll can't stand it any more. She jumps up and bashes Popa Troll over the head, "It iz not. I proove hit ta yuh."

Popa Troll grabs a stick and measures them both out and sure enough, he is right. Chuckling he lets the issue drop before he really gets her mad. A couple of nights later, ole Popa Troll is in a frisky mood and rubs up against Mama Troll, "Mama, wut say me and yu barsh bottums?"

Mama Troll rolls over and looks at him with that evil grin, "Yu specktin me ta fire up dis big ole grill fer wun teeny weiner? Nuttin doin, uh uh."
