DBN Guidelines :: Revised 06.02.01

General
 
  • All members are expected to read the guild message board at least once a week. It is preferable that you read it each time before logging in to play.

  • Only sponsored recruits shall be /guild invited for raids or events. NO exceptions.

  • Only designated raid leaders are permitted to schedule guild events on the calendar.

  • Members are not permitted to /guildremove themselves and just expect back in. If a member has a problem it is to be taken to a member of the grievance committee. If a member does /guildremove and wishes to re-join the guild, they must be put up for and pass a vote.

  • We are expected to post our MAIN characters items and keep this list up to date.

  • All dropped spells are to be saved for the spell vault currently run by Evendara and Immac. Please give them to them when you see them.

  • All velious quest gems are to be given to Bramev our gem toon in EC. Contact Jairn or Baldruss if you have some to drop off.
 
     
Recruitment
 
  • A potential recruit must have a primary sponsor and four secondary.

  • A sponsor (primary or secondary) cannot sponsor more then one recruit at a time.

  • New members cannot sponsor a recruit for a period of 1 month after joining the guild.

  • A minimum of 35 votes must be reached before any decision is made on a recruit.

  • A minimum of 30 YES votes (85% of the 35 required) for a recruit to be considered for membership.

  • If a member votes NO on a recruit they MUST state why in a post in the vote thread, or the vote becomes void.
 
     
Mains vs Alts
 
  • Everyone is allowed ONE main character. Any other character the player owns will be considered an alt no matter of level.

  • If you wish to change your main character to one of your alts. You must inform Jairn of your new main. Jairn will then inform both loot leaders and officers of both classes. Your new main will start at the bottom of loot tables for that class.
 
     
Respect, Behavior, & Conduct
 
  • We are expected to respect each other. /gu is to be respected this way. No personal attacks etc on this channel.

  • Please remember you are wearing a DBN tag when doing /ooc or shouts etc. This is how we are perceived by the public and shouldn't be confused with /gu *evil grin*.

  • There is to be no attacks, slander or profanity through /ooc or /shout directed at any other player or guild.
 
     
Officers
 
  • Officers are put in place for a period of 2 months at which time new officers may be chosen
  
     
Raids
 
  • Raid leaders will be listened to and respected. Once a raid leader is named any invites need to go through him/her. He/she will assign who is doing groups, call charges etc.

  • Even if you are capable of leading a raid yourself, you are expected to show the same curtsey as anyone else in the guild. You must follow the raid leaders instruction not contradict them. If another raid leader is present they will not undermine the authority of the original.

  • Loot leaders and officers are to also be respected. If you have problems talk to them or to the grievance committee but their decisions, as given to the raid leader are final, and not up for debate over /gu etc.

  • Only full guild members will loot mobs during a raid. ONE person from each group will be declared master looter and loot all kills for that group.

  • All vendor loot and gems are to be given to the cleric presently running the dot fund (currently Evendara) or whomever the raid leader appoints in their absence. Current high end jewel crafting gems such as diamonds and blue diamonds as well as Velious quest gems are to be given to the raid leader or Jairn.

  • Once a raid has been posted or named, it is not up for debate. Very rare exceptions to this such as rare spawns maybe tolerated, but once a raid is announced it is not in the best interest of the guild to try to get the guild to go somewhere else.

  • Cost encored for spell components used on raids are reimbursable, contact Evendara for dots, pearls, etc.

  • Do not send a raid leader a /tell unless very important, they are already doing quite enough.

  • Group leaders are responsible for their groups (moving camps etc). Make sure non-guilded people know what's up as well as keeping track of link-dead and afk people.

  • All dropped spells are to be given to people/person running the spell vault (currently Evendara & Immac) or someone designated by the raid leader in their absence.

  • All droppable loot of value is to be given to a Loot Leader.

  • If you receive a upgrade to a item at a raid (loot), it is asked that you pass whatever item it replaced (if its droppable) to you class loot leader for further distribution.

  • We are expected to always bring mains on big raids, unless alt is needed (need as defined by raid leader).

  • If you have a problem with how the raid was lead or run, take it to the grievance committee.
