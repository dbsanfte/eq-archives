JENOLEIC SELLMAGNUS

44th level Dwarven Cleric of Brell Serilis
Keeper of the Treasury
Member of the Stormseekers
Mentor of Healing

I, Jenoleic, have supported my guild, the Stormseekers, for a long time.  It is second in my thoughts, next to Brell Serilis, whom I serve as a cleric.  Brell must like what I am doing.  I know, because He has blessed me with the ability to make lots and lots of Brell's Blessed Stout, which I give out freely to all that need it.  It's important to be well-supplied with Stout.  It helps keep people happy.

Also, I like to sell things.  For profit.  Dwarves may be known as greedy, and some are of course, but I like to think I am generous.   At least to other good people.  But I will buy and sell things, as a service to others.

I am the Keeper of the Treasury for the Stormseekers.   I hold the items people donate, and sell the excess ones for a contribution to the monetary treasury.  Don't worry, I get paid for it.  If I sell something for a LOT of money, I get to keep the excess over what is expected.

 Caprina is a friend of mine, who I introduced into the Stormseekers.  We still like to group, so we can speak to each other easily and to be safer when killing bad things.  I try not to let her die.  I think it has worked.  But even if she dies, I can make her better.

She moves me all over Norrath at my command.  Well, my request.  Alright, when I beg her.  But she is nice, and helps me a lot.

Healing?  Oh, HEALING!  Yes, being a cleric, I heal people.  That nasty orc cut off your foot?  No problem, I just magically make a new one for you.

Naturally, a cleric does more than heal.  I heal FRIENDS.  I know SOMEONE made it necessary for me to heal them, and whoever or whatever did it may feel the righteous wrath of Brell, delivered through His humble instrument, me.  Every once in a while it feels good to keep muscles limber that get stiff from too much meditating, and I go whack something.
