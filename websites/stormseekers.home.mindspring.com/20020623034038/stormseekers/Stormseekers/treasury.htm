Jenoleic has volunteered to keep some items for the Stormseeker's treasury.  I, Arrk, will still keep the actual funds for the Stormseekers, until a need for an actual treasurer presents itself.

The Value column is the price at which the contributing member values the item fairly.   This is the price at which the contributing member would be comfortable selling the item to a stranger.  If no one in the guild needs such an item and someone outside the guild needs it, the non-guildmember should pay this amount for the item.  Of course, such prices are subject to change, with most changes downward, but when in doubt, ask an officer or the contributor.

Those still under probationary status may not necessarily be eligible for ALL items at the stated guild price.  This must be in place to protect ourselves from those that seek to join us merely to withdraw from the Treasury.  The problem has not yet occurred, but we need not learn by "being kicked by the donkey."  If a probationary member requests a withdrawal, the treasurer is fully empowered to use his judgement in granting it.

The Member column is the price a member should pay to withdraw the item from the treasury and make it his/her/its own.  This price also should be set by the contributor.  Non-free prices are set to discourage "twinking," since the Stormseekers is not meant to be a "twinking" guild.  Nonetheless, it is expected that members would normally pay less than non-members for items.

CONTRIBUTING GUIDELINES:
•   Contribute items you think other people in the guild may need, for which you cannot get a reasonable price at auction.
•   When contributing an item, specify an approximate value, both for members and non-members.  If you do not specify a value, Arrk will set one anyway.

WITHDRAWAL GUIDELINES:
•   Do not withdraw an item just to sell it.  These items are to be used for guildmembers.
•   Try to replace an item withdrawn with a superior item.  This can be done much later, though the later it is done, the better the item should be.
•   Things that cannot be reused (spells, potions, etc.) should be used by the person needing it.
•   "Twinking" is not condoned; learn to progress through equipment on your own as much as reasonable.
•   Of course, the contributor can withdraw donated items at any time for free.

TREASURER PRIVILEDGE:
If a particular item in the treasury has not yet been claimed, the treasurer is permitted to try to sell the item for the listed value.  The amount designated under Member would then be returned to the contributor, and the difference between the listed value and the member value would be contributed to the Treasury balance.  Any amount over that the treasurer may keep for himself, as payment for his treasury duties.  In the case of items listed as Free such as expendable items (spells, potions, etc.) the treasurer must be SURE no one in the guild currently needs such items to sell them outside the guild.

If you have anything you wish to contribute to or withdrawal from the treasury, see Arrk or Jenoleic.
