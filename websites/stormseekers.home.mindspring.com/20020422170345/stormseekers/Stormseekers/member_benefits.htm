Member Benefits


Other than the priviledge to proudly display the name of one of the oldest E'Ci guild names, one can enjoy the following benefits:

•    Free access to Clarity, Gift of Magic, Insight, Swift like the Wind, etc., any time Arrk sees a fellow guildmember.  Other guildmembers freely give the Spirit of the Wolf and teleporting when available, too.

•    Discounts on magic jewelry of any sort, for cost.  For example, a Wis+6 ring would normally sell for 175pp to 200pp, but guildmembers can buy them for only 106pp.

•    Assistance of other guilds for accomplishing things, such as the Ancient Guardians, Amber Order,  Sacred Legends, Norrath Guard, Armie uf Oggok, and Norrath's Crusade, with whom we have working relationships.

Guildmembers should be very free about providing services for other guildmembers, such as Spirit of the Wolf and teleporting.
