The StormSeekers of E'Ci was founded by Ailine and Reaveline.  The original history is recounted below.

CHARTER

The goals of the Stormseekers are as follows:
•     HAVE FUN with each other!
•     Support each other in tasks we wish to accomplish, such as quests, tradeskills, teleports, etc.
•     Develop our skills as groupmembers, to be as effective as we can.
•     Gain a reputation as effective and important people throughout Norrath
•     Take every opportunity to fight evil, as per Aku Zoku Zan (See Evil, Kill Evil).

Secondary goals:
•     Roleplaying is strongly encouraged, since it makes the game more fun!
•     Keep the game at a PG rating.  Young people play it, too.

When Stormseekers are in the same zone and one shouts out "Aku Zoku Zan," which is our motto ("See Evil, Kill Evil"), the proper response is to shout Aku Zoku Zan or See Evil, Kill Evil back.

History (last updated: SEP 23, 1999 )

In the beginning, 2 founders: Founder Ailine and Founder Reaveline formed the Stormseekers. It is a guild formed on the belief of "Aku Zoku Zan" ("See Evil, Kill Evil"). The idea to form the guild came about when the Founders, Ailine and Reaveline, met Warlord Bagary and Royal Wizard Rapun’eth. It was about 1 year ago, when we first met these Legends; Warlord Bagary was a half-elf and very skilled sword fighter with a shield named Stormseekers. The King bestowed the Stormseekers upon Lord Bagary in the human city, Freeport. The Royal Wizard Rapun’eth was a high-elf, an intelligent man with a great vision, he mastered the Arcane knowledge at a level the commoner can only dream of achieving.

The two was on a journey, a journey to not only improve themselves but also to eliminate any corruption and evil that roam throughout the world of Norrath. When Lord Bagary and Wizard Rapun’eth were in the Greater Faydark, we studied under the Warlord and the Royal Wizard for more than a year. Founder Reaveline decided to struggle on his physical side, while Founder Ailine learned the secret of magic and the Arcane Knowledge.

After one year passed, it was time for both Legends to carry on with their quests and journeys. We were not allowed to follow them, but their dreams and visions inspired us to form this guild. Stormseekers has one and only principle, "See Evil, Kill Evil", we DO NOT protect the good; but we WILL kill evil when we see one. This belief was passed down to us by Royal Wizard Rapun’eth. He also mentioned that in the events where two parties are fighting for something that is evil/wrong, we would not take sides, for if we did we would have to fight and kill both parties.

About 1 month after the departure of the Legends, the Founders received a parcel sent by the King’s Royal Guard. Inside we found a badly burned shield and a book. The shield was the Stormseekers owned by the Warlord Bagary and the book "Way of the Seekers" was written and the only belonging left of Royal Wizard Rapun’eth. Later, the Royal Guard informed us that both the Legends were slain by fire dragons. But their last quest was not in vain, it seemed that the Royal Wizard Rapun’eth managed to destroy the cavern where the dragon clan lived, killing many dragons in the process.

Founder Reaveline repaired the Stormseekers and preserved it in our Guildhall as our emblem. The book itself contains the law, principle, and knowledge of the Royal Wizard. The book is being used as the guild’s holy book.
