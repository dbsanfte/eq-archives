Guild Charter
  Charter
Our guild is designed to not only help members improve and advance their characters but also to Create a Community of true friends who wish to bring their Dreams into Reality, of people who value and strive for honesty, integrity, dignity, respect, compassion and generosity. We hope you enthusiastically and humorously role-play these qualities, creating a place for all of us to creatively express ourselves, our beliefs and visions for the future. To adventure and explore new realms that test our group's unity, strengths and weaknesses, that test our flexibility to meet with craft, guile, intelligence and bravery all challenges that face us. Our motto is: "All that you see or seem, is but a dream within a dream. Turn your dreams into reality." 		012/12/02 12:30 pm  
Clan Bloodtalon Rules of Conduct
  Rules of Conduct
In General: Thou shall not Steal Kills. Thou shall not Take Over Other’s spawns (camps) without allowing others to kill the spawn first or without asking to share first. Thou shall not use foul language. Thou shall not scam other players to take their stuff. You should treat others how you wish to be treated. Remember, guild members you help may quickly be in a position to help you! And always try to group with family. In raids and in groups, family comes first. Raiding Rules: Raids are established for a purpose... usually experience/gear or for Quest Items. Know why you are on the raid! Raids will be shown on Calendar, Announced in Weekly Newsletters, Posted on the Message Boards and Posted in the Guild M.O.T.D! If you wish to join a certain raid, make sure you are on site before the raid is scheduled to begin. Listen to your Raid Leader in the Channel He or She has set up!
