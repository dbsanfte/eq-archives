Sony removes MB from their links.

Nathan Pearce, Director of Web Presence, Sony Online Entertainment made the following statement today.

"We've been watching the Monkly Business events closely and we are removing the link." " I expect several new monk boards to pop up in the coming weeks in reaction to these events, and until one or two are busy and have the 'endorsement' of a good portion of the Monk community, we won't be linking to them."

Looks like Sony is cutting all ties to MB. Can anyone really say they did not expect this to happen? MB is now officially dead to Sony. It really is a sad thing that people actually thought Sony would continue any ties to MB with the current sponsorship. What amazes me is people actually believe Sony does not care about this sort of thing since as long as an account is paid for, it is money on their bottom line. I imagine a lot of people are in for a rude awakening regarding this.
