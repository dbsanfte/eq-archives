  Clerics, Clerics, Clerics...  Rommy ~ Thursday, December 4th, 2003

So here we are. Not much to report really... been clearing Time a couple dozen times, doing LDoN raids, and of course the obligatory Elemental scraping to equip alts. This brings me to the main reason for even making an update...

We're looking for Clerics (not Druids).

When I say we're looking for Clerics I don't mean that you, a level 62 with a VEX THAL KEY(!1!!). I mean YOU, a level 65 Plane of Time enabled Cleric that's not going to pussy out of LDoN raids or only login for Time. YOU also have to play a lot, I mean hardcore. None of this 1 day a week bullshit or going soft after you're done with trial... we will boot you. Why should you pick Triton? Well, we're a talented group of players that enjoy raiding and having fun. If you can't tolerate swearing or take abuse, this guild isn't for you. If you like hitting on manginas though... well, I'll let Pssycho field this one!

Want a shot at the amazing stuff PoP end game has to offer but your current guild cannot? Let's put it this way...

So, if you're interested we're accepting a couple x-server TIME LEVEL HARDCORE Clerics. Head over to our application section and fill one out. If you're on Povar don't fret, you can still apply... but you better be either close or damn close to being Time flagged (and for your sake AT LEAST have Elemental + Fennin flags). Just don't send a tell to Llapheria about recruitment...

So yeah, requirements. Well, first of all you need a bare minimum of 150 AAs (including CR, DA3, UD3, MGB, SCM, SRC, SCRM, ND3, bonus points for defensive AAs!). After that you must be hardcore. When I say hardcore I mean HARDCORE. You must be able to raid from 7:30/8:00pm EST until around 1am 5-6 / 7 days a week. You must know your class, and you must be ready to be drilled by our existing Clerics to make sure you're Triton quality.

As always all applications are kept confidential and nothing is seen by any non-officers until you are trialed. We treat our Clerics VERY well, so remember that when you apply. Oh, one last thing...

PS: For those that have applied we haven't forgotten you, we're just trying to get as wide of an array of Clerics to pick from as possible. ;) Be patient, fuckers.
