  Pancake Rabbit to the rescue!   Rommy the Vicar, Sep 21, 2001

Ok, what the fuck!  I'm sick of being so secretive about our tactics.  Yes, we have a lot of secret things we do to get our leet pulls, one is to make diagrams and maps of how our pulls should go.  We call these diagrams and maps DSSA3YOCD maps.  I've been authorized by Triton Command Central to share two with you on the condition you understand that these tactics take much skill to pull off.

First we have the Sontalak Strategy - drawn by Nurben... note the gay people out of range asking for Gift of Brilliance, THIS IS NECESSARY TO TAKE DOWN SONTALAK!

Next we have the diagram of what a Nene Pull should look like - drawn by Nuvian.  Note the Orc Pawn from the West Freeport area.  THIS IS NECESSARY!  If you do not pull every single mob in the zone and AT LEAST one mob from another zone, you cannot possibly recreate a Nene Pull...

There you have it, our extremely leet D(iagrams)S(o)S(imple)A3Y(ear)O(ld)C(ould)D(raw) maps.  Use their great powers to take down Sontalak and recreate a Nene Pull!  Results may very, consult your guild leader before attempting these strategies, all sales are final.

On to the deaths!  

We hit up ToV West in an attempt to kill time while North repoped.

Telk, as usual, was defeated in a matter of seconds.  His death brought about some of the worst loot we've ever seen - a Black Rock Maul of Crushing for Dreadwar and an Emerald Bastardsword of Purity for Gruntath's backpack.  

Gozzrem, however, was a different story.  Gozzrem is a formidable adversary (LMAO!) so Team Ice special forces were called in to assist with the kill.  Enter: Pancake Rabbit.  Pancake Rabbit used his special confusion attack to subdue Gozzrem into a trance like state.  Once this state was achieved, Triton Demolition Teams lined Gozzrem's body with dynamite and proceeded to destroy her.

Gozzrem's remains left behind a Shovel of Harvest for Farmer Saolor, an Unopenable Box for Munwin, and a Wand of the Black Dragon Eye for Brownivy.

Knowing he was on a role, Pancake Rabbit lead us to Keeper where he used his extremely powerful SURROUND attack on the Keeper.  Confused, dazed, and phased Keeper fell to the ground and dropped a Mask of Superioirity for Gorador, a Silver Girdle of Stability for Alas, and a pair of Gloves of Stability for Archie, and finally a Sleeper's Tomb key for Coren.

The day was over for the Pancake Rabbit, realizing he had done much good he scampered back to his den located inside Brodie's storage closet.

Before the night was over we decided to drop one final mob.  Brodie sensed an evil presence, however... it sounded, like a cat.. a very evil cat...

Congratulations to Armok on your Sleeper's Tomb key and several alts on your newly completed spell book!

 

In other news, we decided it was about time to start our monthly clearing of Veeshan's Peak, Triton's zone.  In a frantic attempt to destroy Hoshkar, Heihachi trained Silverwing on us forcing us to kill him.  The death, as usual, resulted in nothing worth mentioning.  The upside was that Kastrina got to destroy yet another Druid Staff!  Grats Kastrina!

You could tell Hoshkar was next, Heihachi was bouncing with excitement!  As always, Hoshkar fell with little problems and put out a Feverblade for Yanik Gaggas, a Fatestealer for Fuedar, and some Grey Suede Boots for Genni.  Yes yes, no Lure or Gheal Sticks... why for are you so mean to us, Hoshkar? :(

So we knew Triton Luck™ had followed us to Veeshan's Peak when we pwned (!!1) Xygoz.  Congrats umm, who gives a fuck, on two Smoldering Cudgels (ooooh!).  Pulled Druushk and following the pattern he dropped shit.  Maelstrom for Yanik, not sure who got the rest of the stuff but who cares IT'S VEESHAN'S PEAK!  Oh yes, and I got a Shissar Deathspeaker Staff!  I was so happy!!

Pulled Nexona and to our surprise he actually put out something worthwhile.  Grats Genni and Soffit on the Bo Staves of Trorsmang and a Star of the Guardian to Adie... I think.

Finally finished off the night with Phara Dar.  Her loot was disappointing.  Congratulations Nuvian on the Crown of Rile (stop spamming me now, k? thx), Yanik got a Claw of Phara Dar as did I.

So umm, that's Veehsan's Peak for ya!  We'll be back next month to get more asstacular loots... 

 

That's about it for our update!  Stay tuned for this week is ToV North ownage. ;)  Wrapping up today's update with quotes as I forgot to take screenshots.. AGAIN.

We were greeted by an arms opened wide (350 version!)
