  Can you hear me now?... GOOD!  Eom Rombus Zethon ~ Saturday, May 25th, 2002

Just shut the fuck up... I'm finally doing one.

So it's been awhile since my last update, I guess persistency pays off.  I can't tell you how many fucked up stalkers I got on Povar requesting that I do an update.  Here it is, in all of its glory, just for you - my little short bus riders!

 

We finally went back to Vex Thal.  Yeah, that's right, I said finally.  I don't get it, you work your ass off to get your god damned key and then you don't show up?  What the hell is wrong with you people!?  That's like going to Abercrombie and Fitch to buy a pair of $100.00 jeans that have the 'dirty' look so you can look cool when in reality you just look like a scum.  Anyone know what's up with that stuff!?  Annnnyway, we played around a bit tonight and dropped good old Kaas Thox Xi Ans Whatever who will from this point be known as 'Flip.'  Flip was kind enough to put out some Shoes of Constipation or whatever (I'm to lazy to look at the name on the picture) for Irontaail and a Shade Rock Amulet for Wizury.  Not to bad, and a fun encounter to boot.  Best way to describe this guy would be a smaller version of the Avatar of War, rooted, with an un-resistible flux.  FUN!

'BAM~  Triton and Emeril kicked you bitch ToVN dragons up a notch!!!  I need some dragon owning music from the Emeril Live band now pls k thx!'
