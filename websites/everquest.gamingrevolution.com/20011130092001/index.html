EQ Guru Heads Out
10-15-2001 | poster: Sherlock | Everquest
Sony Online Entertainment (SOE) has announced Brad McQuaid, one of the original creators of EverQuest, will be leaving the company after five years of service.

John Smedley, currently chief operating officer as well as a partner in the conception and production of EverQuest, will take over McQuaid’s duties.

“After five years of building the EverQuest franchise for Sony Online Entertainment, it’s time for me to explore other opportunities in the creative development of massively multiplayer games,” stated McQuaid.
