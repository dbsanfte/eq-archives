THE STORY BEHIND THE BAND

July 2000

    Our story begins when Desmond and Nick, at the time employed as lowly file clerks at a private law firm in southern Orange County, meet for the first time. Desmond, a recent college graduate from Cal State Long Beach, realizes that he cannot let his college education go to waste, hence, he decides he wants to be a rockstar. Nick, has also come to the same realization. He has had musical training since the 2nd grade and he strongly believes that this entitles him to rockstar status. Never since Lennon met McCartney has their been such a union of musical minds. Having large amounts of time on their hands as a result of their boring, yet well-paying, summer job, the two cleverly devise a plan to start a band. They call their band Access Denied (since changed to Prime Syndicate, but we'll get to that later).

   Desmond and Nick first decide to look for a singer for Access Denied. Not really having any idea what sort of music they want to play, they begin looking for someone who can sing. They search low and high. Finally, they find this guy Dave and he has always wanted to be a rockstar. He definitely looks like a rockstar, so after talking with him, Desmond and Nick agree to go over to his house for a jam session (that is, if a bassist, guitarist, and singer getting together with no drummer, no songs, and next to no equipment can be considered a jam session). The first meeting went well, so the three agree that they should begin looking for the next ingredient commonly found in a decent band...a drummer.

August 2000

   Some guy named Mike finds a "drummer wanted" flyer at the local music store. Mike is 19, just out of high school, and ready to become a rockstar. He calls the number on the flyer and finds out the situation. "Partially formed band in need of a drummer." Mike likes the sound of this. Only one problem, he doesn't play drums. The members of Access Denied decide to meet with him anyways. Mike brings along his guitar, the instrument he does play. After the meeting, all parties involved decide that having two guitars would be better than having one (more noise), so Mike is adopted into the band.

Late September 2000

   Nothing much going on with the band at this point. Weekly practices, a few songs written, and many sleepless nights dreaming about becoming rockstars. Dave is seeing this hot Brazilian girl (who will go unnamed) who has a friend that has just come to California from Brazil. One practice, Dave explains about the Brazilian who has just come to California. The other band members think to themselves, "Who cares...some Brazilian dude..." However, this Brazilian plays drums. In fact, he KICKS ASS on drums. "Big J", his given nickname by the band, is inducted into the band. He does not yet know the meaning of or how to stay rockstar in English, but already he knows that he wants to be one. It is at this point that the band, now fully formed, takes the name of Prime Syndicate.

April 2001

    The immigration authorities find "Big J" and he gets deported to Brazil! Just kidding. In reality, he decides that he wants to go back home to his family and girlfriend. He is a kick ass drummer and he is sorely missed by all who know him. What happened with the band? They decide to take the summer off. Well okay, they kind of disband for the summer. Okay okay, they break up. But don't worry, it is only temporary.

December 2001 - present

    Still in contact with one another, the four remaining members of Prime Syndicate decide to get back together. Only one problem...no drummer. In steps Greg, who had previously played in numerous bands. He is amazed when he meets this group of highly talented musicians. Well, maybe only mildly amazed. Okay, in truth, he is shocked at how badly these so-called musicians suck. But for some reason he always comes to practice and wants to jam with them. Greg, while he has experienced a taste of success in his previous bands, knows he is not yet a full-blown rockstar and must join Prime Syndicate to achieve this goal.

And so, the syndicate was formed. Like the uniting of Voltron, these members came together in order to bring this world quality music (and to become rockstars in the process).
