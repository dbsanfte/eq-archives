AOS welcomes players of all classes and races. If you are interested 
in joining a guild full of great people that have tons of fun playing 
EQ on the Cazic-Thule server then post a message in the General forum 
on the message board and a couple of our members will make arrangements 
to meet you in-game and get to know you. If you have any questions about 
guild membership, events, policies or anything else, just post your 
question in the general forum on the message board and one of our members 
will be glad to reply with an answer.

All prospective new members are strongly encouraged to read all of the 
information (including guild policies) on the About AOS page on this website.
