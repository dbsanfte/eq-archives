Let it be known that Destiny herself shines with pride on this day as the founding laws of her guild are laid forth by myself and those who stand with me. By this doctrine do we stake our fortune and seek our fate; by this path shall we earn our namesake.

ENJOYMENT AND CAMARADERIE
We are all on this path because it provides us enjoyment; we combine paths because we treasure the company of our fellow Crew mates. While the creeds we follow are significant and the standards of behavior expected of us even more so, in the end let us not forget that we are here for fun. If something within the guild is bringing a Crew mate sorrow, it is of concern to us all.
EQUALITY & DUTY:
We are as different as the faces of Norrath and yet we are equal on our path toward Destiny. One single Crew mate’s actions are never more important than the whole of The Crew. Together, united and bound under one name, the good of the guild and its members comes first. When a guild member requires assistance we shall respond with haste. Only the member in need of assistance rises in priority.
VIRTUE & COMPASSION:
We are obligated to help those in need and to alleviate the distress of others, when doing so will not jeopardize our lives or the lives of fellow Crew mates. The weak and innocent are our charges, their defense and safety our duty. The meek may rest weary hands on the strength of our shoulders. In our path toward Destiny we are bound to confront many evils, let selfishness be not one of them. The good reputation of any one member for his assistance to others will grow and will reflect directly on our guild. At the same time we shall recognize the need for self preservation and allow Crew mates to continue on their path without feeling required to constantly and consistently assist others: if that were the case we could never find time for our own destiny.
HONOR & VALOR:
We are heroes, and our actions should so indicate. We honor our names and our guild by behaving in ways that reflect well upon both. We are gallant; we are polite; we treat others with respect when deserved. No member shall engage in dealings that conflict with the these interests. Nor shall anyone engage in dealings that will taint the guild’s reputation. Such offenses include, but are not necessarily limited to, theft, participating in a fraudulent transaction, price gouging, kill stealing and looting. Pending an advisory review by the Great Council, such dishonest actions shall be grounds for removal from the guild.
LOYALTY & DEDICATION:
We will stand by each other at all costs. An implicit expectation is that members will keep a certain level of participation in the guild, although we are all cognizant of the need for vacations and breaks from Norrath. We will fight any threat to the Guild or our Crew mates with full intensity and at no time abandon our companions. No member is to partake in the slandering of the guild or any member, and we are all expected to actively protect the guild should others slander our reputation. No one is to ever abandon another member in a hostile situation.

But above all walk with dignity, my Crew, and take these words with you: YOU are the people that make Norrath great, YOU are why the fights are worth fighting; YOU are the reason we call this guild our home. Take pride in our guild, as I take pride in calling you all family.
