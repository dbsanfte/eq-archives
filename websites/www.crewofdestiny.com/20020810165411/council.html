Please note that we are in the process of re-working the officer structure and induction guidelines and policies. We will update the page when the changes are complete. In the meanwhile, this is our present guild structure:
 

The decision making body of The Crew is a council. The Council has final say in all matters concerning the guild. They are responsible to the rest of the guild to provide adequate leadership and a sense of enjoyment to all and to consult with other members when discussing matters of importance. Only members of the Council may add or remove other Council members. This in no way should stand for the proposition that others within the guild have no say on guild direction or decisions. The Crew is a guild of equality and the Council shall listen to and integrate all opinions on an issue before making a final ruling. If any ruling is heavily contested, in that two or more non-Council members feel a larger opinion base is required, the issue shall be opened up to all guild members and all members shall have a chance to vote.

The Council is made up of nine positions. They are involved in all the day-to-day tasks of the guild. The council members are as follows:


Taloz Winterwolf - Responsible for all that goes on in the guild. Sets policy, appoints officers, oversees the officers.


Dyamond Fanal - Responsible for The Great Council, coordinates with Taloz, is second in command for guild issues, and will be the tie break vote if needed for council issues.


Iroqmun Koa - General for all guild sponsored events. Coordinates with the master of Strategy for times, places, and who. Establishes loot priority with the Master of Strategy for all guild sponsored events.


Loradin Wolfryder - Plans and implements strategy to be used on raids. Coordinates with the Master of War for loot priority, group planning during event and will assist the Master of War during the event.


VACANT - Responsible for the protection of all guild members. Will oversee and coordinate to help others in dire need.


Kizzestra Bonerender - Responsible for forming alliances with other guilds.


VACANT - Responsible for member recruitment, setting up sponsors for new applicants, will coordinate for full membership voting.


Lingsurfer Nukonater - Responsible for the guild vault, makes policy for the vault, has final say on who gets what from the vault, and also will maintain a published list of what is in the vault.


Nanae Sunspirit - Responsible for the guild webpage and message board, updating and or changing of the pages.
