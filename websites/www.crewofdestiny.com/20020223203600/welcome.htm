Subject:  Enduring Beloved, Sang'Carus

The day after Valentine's Day, Dindawen and Vigdis gathered before a small group of friends and pledged their lives together. Faendrill, the perfect melding of warrior and druid, did o'ersee the ceremony and his words were lovely:

"Bear WITNESS to these words, all ye, friends of this pair. Vigdis, steel-bright maiden, dancer of swords; Dindawen, earth-muted man, weaver of dreams; Children of Gaeia, who would share a path, and light the darkness of their lives. Many have wandered the forest, though few have seen the Lover Trees. A bound pair, twisting, twining about each other in gentle embrace, holding each other, sharing the light ... and the darknes, sharing the gentle raid ... and the lashing storms which so often uproot the weak. Bear witness to these, the Lover Trees, uplifting, binding, sharing, holding. May they ever grow to one another, and shelter the seedings which are sure to follow."


Thank you all for sharing with us the day that we begin sharing our lives together.
