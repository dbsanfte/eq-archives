Application For Membership Into Guild

We are very happy to take applications for membership into the Nomadic Knights.  We ask that you fill out the following information and click on the Submit button.  An e-mail will be sent to the guild leadership with that information.  Shortly afterwards, you should receive a reply from a guild officer.  If you have not heard back within a week, please e-mail Garwynn or Mystykal so that we can find out what is going on.  Thanks!
