Information
Nomadic Knights has expectations to accomplish great things.  However, we do realize to do these tasks we cannot do them alone.  We have forged alliances with these guilds so that our combined resources and manpower are strong enough to overcome obstacles that we as individual guilds would have difficulty accomplishing or being unable to accomplish at all.

Members of Nomadic Knights are encouraged to welcome the members of these guilds into your plans, whether it be grouping, raiding, or simply just getting in a chatroom and saying hello.  Please remember if you attend an event hosted by another guild, you will be subject to their event policies and rules, and will be held individually accountable should a problem arise.

Members of allied guilds are encouraged to contact Nomadic Knights members if they should want to attend raids, join groups, or even just say hello.  We ask that if you attend an event hosted solely by Nomadic Knights that you follow our policies laid out by the one leading the event.

If your guild would be interested in forming a guild alliance with the Nomadic Knights, please contact Mystykal or Garwynn and they will contact you after receiving an e-mail from you.
