Application For Membership Into Guild

As of this moment, Nomadic Knights is recruiting on an exception-only basis.  All new recruits into NK will require a minimum level of 40, and grouping with current NK members.  To apply, click on the Guild Boards link, and place your application in the folder marked Applying to Nomadic Knights.  Please remember to do this after you have read the rules and policies on this website.

Once you have applied on the board, you should autojoin the nkchat channel.  This channel was specifically set up for new applicants to group with current Nomadic Knights.  We hope that this new application process will result in a smoother application process than before.

Should you have any questions, please contact one of the officers ingame through the nkchat channel.
