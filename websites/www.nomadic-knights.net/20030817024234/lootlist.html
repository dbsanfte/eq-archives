How a Loot List Works - by Erki - taken from the message boards

Note: We use loot lists when armor farming in Kael and HoT.

Loot lists are composed by level and armor type; silk, chain, leather, or plate.

Example: On the raid are 4 people who can use a silk drop.

Antony lvl 63 chanter
Benjamin lvl 59 wizard
Cristina lvl 56 magician
Daniel lvl 51 Necromancer

Silk gloves drop.
Raid leader asks Antony if he wants them, Antony passes.
Raid leader asks Benjamin and he takes them and moves to the bottom of the list.

Later, silk arms drop.
Raid leader asks Antony if he wants them, Antony passes.
Raid leader asks Christina if she wants them since Benjamin got the gloves and therefore went to the bottom of the list. Christina takes them.

For the next silk drop the list is:
Antony
Daniel
Benjamin
Christina

All chest items will be god loot as well as drops from Vindi if we should dare to attack him.

If you have questions, please contact an officer!
