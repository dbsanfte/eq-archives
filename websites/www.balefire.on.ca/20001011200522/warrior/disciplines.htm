Skills: Disciplines
This is a listing of the Skill Disciplines that were released with Kunark.  They are post 50 skills.  If I have made any mistakes or missed anything please email me with the corrections.

[ Submit Info ]  
 
 

Skills: Disciplines
 

Evasive
Level Dur. Re-use Description
52 3 Min
15min at 52
7min at 60
The warrior is hit by his opponent less often but also will hit his target less often. This discipline lasts for 3 minutes and has a base reuse time of 15 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the warrior raises in levels
 

Charge
Level Dur. Re-use Description
53 14 Secs.
30min at 52
23min at 60
When using this discipline the warrior will automatically land all attacks against his opponent. This discipline lasts for 14 seconds and has a base reuse time of 30 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the warrior gains levels.
 

Mighty Strike
Level Dur. Re-use Description
54 10 Secs.
60min at 52
54min at 60
When using this discipline all landed attacks will automatically be either Critical Hits or Crippling Blows. This discipline lasts for 10 seconds and has a base reuse time of 60 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the warrior gains levels.
 

Defensive
Level Dur. Re-use Description
55 3 Min.
15min at 52
10min at 60
The warrior will take less melee damage but also will deal less. This will not affect damage taken  from any form of magic, damage shield, or weapon proc. This discipline lasts for 3 minutes and has a base reuse time of 15 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the warrior gains  levels.
 

Furious
Level Dur. Re-use Description
56 9 Sec.
60min at 52
56min at 60
When using this discipline the warrior will automatically riposte every attack landed by the opponent. This discipline lasts for 9 seconds and has a base reuse time of 60 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the warrior gains levels.
 

Precision
Level Dur. Re-use Description
57 3 Min.
30min at 52
27min at 60
The warrior will have a greater chance to hit his target but will also have a greater chance of being hit. This discipline lasts for 3 minutes and has a base reuse time of 30 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the warrior gains levels.
 

Fell Strike
Level Dur. Re-use Description
58 12 Sec.
30min at 52
28min at 60
When using this discipline all hits landed by the warrior will be for at least four times the base weapons damage.  This discipline lasts for 12 seconds and has a base reuse time of 30 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the warrior gains levels.
 

Fortitude
Level Dur. Re-use Description
59 8 Sec.
60min at 52
59min at 60
When using this discipline the warrior cannot be hit in melee combat. This does not affect the ability for the warrior to have damage inflicted by magical means. This discipline lasts for 8 seconds and has a base reuse time of 60 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the warrior gains levels.
 

Aggressive
Level Dur. Re-use Description
60 3 Min.
27min at 52
27min at 60
The warrior will do more damage with every blow landed but will also take more damage as well. This discipline lasts for 3 minutes and has a base reuse time of 27 minutes.
