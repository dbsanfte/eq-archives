General Reference
  • Allakazam's Magical Realm
    An extensive collection of searchable databases including but not limited to quests, strategy, equipment, skills, and more! Also includes the option to store your character's stats, equipment, and history in their database. Be sure to check out their awesome collection of links, guild and otherwise.
  • EverQuest - Official Site
    The official site for EverQuest, this Sony Station site features tons of information straight from the game designers. A definite bookmark to keep up to date with upcoming changes and additions.
  • Caster's Realm
    Great news site for Norrath residents. Contains information on skills, factions, maps, and areas specific to each class.
  • Epic Quests at Caster's Realm
    This ambitious site provides walkthroughs for all the epic quests of the game which makes it useful to anyone of any class or race.
  • EQ Vault
    Another general information site for EverQuest players. EQ Vault distinguishes itself with a sizeable collection of player-created artwork.
  • EQ XP
    Easy-to-use page used to calculate the amount of experience needed each level given a particular race and class. Also includes the approximate number of mobs of a particular level one would need to kill in order to level.
  • Everlore
    General information. Contains message boards and searchable databases for quests, trade skills, and more.
  • EverQuest - Official Manual
    The EverQuest manual is now available online and in an easily-printable format.
  • Planet EverQuest
    Great general reference site with several useful resources for any player including an emote guide, character, class, and race guides, information on trades, and very much more!
  • EQ Spoiler
    This is a paid Flashlink site similar to Allakazaam showing quest, spell, equipment etc by zones class etc. Also has maps and tradeskill information.
  • http://pub43.ezboard.com/b7thhammer
    The most active Seventh Hammer forums. An excellent opportunity to communicate with other players out of game.
  • EQ Links
    This website contains other links to various EverQuest sites. Sorted by class and skills etc.
  • EQ Stratics
    This site is full of articles, images and news about EverQuest. This is also an excellent site for UI downloading
  • EQ'Lizer
    This is a paid Flashlink site similar to Allakazaam showing quest, spell, equipment etc by zones class etc. Also has maps and tradeskill information.
  • EQ Portal
    This website is simply an engine that give you various EverQuest site based on different properties such as race class etc.
  • Mobhunter.Com
    This Site includes members articles and EverQuest links.
  • EverQuesting with Glory
    This site is set up by a personal player that has chock full of information check it out.
  • EverQuest Raids
     A site that I do not much about since I have not bother to log on to it but I am assuming it pertains to raiding in EverQuest
  • Gumkak's Stuffz an' Tinkz
    This website is dedicated to the MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game) EverQuest It has a collection of other website information that is either still available or has been removed from postings. Has good armor dye information and gem information.
Maps and Zone Guides
  • EQ Atlas
    The largest and most comprehensive collection of maps and zone guides available online. Contains tons of information and detailed maps for most zones in EverQuest An invaluable tool to the unseasoned and experienced traveler alike.
  • EQ Maps
    A hodgepodge collection of maps and zone guides by various authors. Lacks consistency when compared with EQ Atlas but sometimes has additional information not available elsewhere.
Fun Stuff & Utilities
  • Magelo
    This website is simply awesome! On the site once you have created a free account you can set up your character(s) profiles and have others view them with all your equipment and stats. Our guild website incorporates this feature under your characters information page if available.
  • EverQuest Log Analyzer
    This website has a wonderful program made to decipher your EverQuest log files to see what you have done while playing the game.
  • EverLogs
    This is a character analyzer and statistics program for EverQuest Information is collected from the log file generated by EverQuest. This program is designed to be as complete as possible and include all possible data that you would like to see about your time in EverQuest
  • The EverQuest Uber Item Generator
    Want to knock the socks off that braggardly group member of yours by proving once and for all that your equipment is far more uber than his? Why not show him your brand new 60/20 1-handed slashing weapon with +10 to all stats and +20 to all saves? The EverQuest Uber Item Generator will let you do just that! Uber!
  • EQ Watcher
    This is a website with a wonderful utility designed to run in the background as you play EQ -- it interprets the chat log, and notes file.
Trade Skill

  • Aoenla's Fletching Guide
    Excellent resource for anyone interested in learning how to create the best bows and arrows with the greatest cost efficiency. Very detailed with stats, prices, and trivial levels of all components.
  • EQ Traders
    A large site dedicated to several trade skills including Alchemy, Baking, Brewing, Fletching, Fishing, Foraging, Jewelcraft, Make Poison, Pottery, Smithing, and Tailoring. Guides are written by various authors in varying levels of detail. Of particular interest is the message forum.
  • Info on the Various Mana Vials
    This is taken from the EQ Trader site from a post about information on Mana Vials
  • Kiky's Poisonous Pages
    A useful resource for those practicing the art of poison-making. Contains information on poison recipes, ingredients and where they may be found, and the effects of various concoctions. Also a useful section on the different vials necessary for storing poisons.
  • Lokari's Tailoring Guide
    A simple yet informative guide to tailoring almost anything. Even includes information about Tailoring in Velious!
  • The Shaman's Crucible
    Gorgeous site for anyone who plays a shaman (regardless of class). Features include class guides, equipment listings, message forums, and an extensive guide to the shaman-only skill Alchemy.
  • The Tal'Kor Jewelry Academy
    Fully-illustrated guide to the art of jewel craft. Contains several different jewelry guides based on gem-stone, metal, attributes, and type.
  • The Tinkering Compendium
    Tons of information about the gnome-only skill of Tinkering. This site is well designed, easy to navigate, and absolutely invaluable to both master and amateur tinkerers!
  • Trader's Workshop
    This site focuses on the cheapest methods of improving one's skills in any number of trade skills.
UI Modification
  • EQ Tradeskill Companion
    A must have for any trade skiller, the EQ Tradeskill Companion features recipes for Baking, Brewing, Fletching, Jewelry, Pottery, Smithing, and Tailoring. Tabbed for easy reference!
  • EverQuest Toolbox
    A variety of resources for EverQuest, many of which revolve around customzing the user interface.
  • Mez/Spawn Timer
    Replaces one of three windows (based on the version you choose) with a working timer, which can tick seconds, minutes, and hours depending on its configuration. Highly customizable, you can even dynamically create a timer to match your preferences.
  • Muse's GUI Creator
    This program allows the creation of a replacement help file containing maps downloaded from EQAtlas.com.
  • Remelio's Mods at Transcendance Forums
    Several useful modifications of the EverQuest UI, many of which are based upon the work of T.King.
  • T.King's EverQuest Art
    Indisputably the most beauitful spellbooks, spell icons, and spell gems available. These mods are a must have for any caster.
  • UI Timer
    Allows you to keep track of up to eight different recurrences simultaneously. Fully configurable!
  • Vert UI Modifications
    Clean, clear, and sleek, Vert's line of UI modifications (available in blue and green) are the best I've seen. I highly recommend this set for anyone who isn't necessarily partial to ornate, fantasy-like interface elements.
  • EQ Interface
    A site that has many old and current UI modifications. Registration is required to download UI files.
Guild Alliances
  • Silver Moonlight
    The Silver Moonlight Guild is a family orientated guild on the Seventh Hammer Server. They do not focus on Hate-raids and Power-leveling only, but to create a family atmosphere.
Guild Sites

  • Glory of the Glade on Guild Portal
    The guild first website set up by Divine Healer and later edited by Talamont Trailfinder before Talamont decided to make one himself. This site is going off line on 12/31/2002 but still can be viewed just not used as an active site.
  • Guild Portal
    This website is a hosting service for guilds playing massively multiplayer online roleplaying games (MMORPGs) such as EverQuest, Anarchy Online, Dark Age of Camelot, Asheron's Call and Star Wars: Galaxies. They provide a fully-functional community management system for free with additional content options for the price some other providers charge for just a forum.
  • Guild Magic
    This is a website that allows others to host there guilds website on for free much like www.guildportal.com.

  • EQ Cleric

Druid
  • The Druid Pages
    An extensive, earthy toned site dedicated to the druid class. Among its many features are equipment and spells listing, maps, class/race guides, and message forums.
  • EQ Druids
    The premier site for Norrathian druids, this site has more information than you can shake a moss-covered twig at! Contains information on tactics, quests, spells, guides, equipment, maps, and more. Highly recommended for any player of the most popular class of EverQuest.

Magician

  • EQ Mage
    A supplemental site to playing the mage class. Well designed but currently short of frequent updates. Contents include spells, forums, and a unique guide to magician pets.
  • The Mage Compendium
    Masters of elemental magic, the mage is one of the best solo classes of Norrath. But how much do you know about these mysterious, powerful loners? Learn more by visiting the Mage Compendium where you'll be able to peruse guides for equipment, quests, and of course spells!
  • The Magician's Tower
    This is a very useful message board that is sponsered by the Mage Compendium. Find information on Mages or ask a question about them here.
  • Rohane's Ultimate Magician Epic Quest Guide
    This is a detailed step by step record of how to obtain the mage epic and includes photos and information of epic and items needed for the quest.
Monk
  • Monkly Business
    Monkly business is a nice site which provides a collection of guides on how best to play this melee class, a selection of rants and stories from other players, as well as a fair collection of humorous pieces.
Necromancer
  • EQ Necro
    Feel an inclination toward the darker arts of necromancy? EQ Necro will help you on this path toward lifelessness and disease by providing you with tons of useful and informative guides on how to best play a necromancer. Also features message boards as well as character and guild listings.
  • Necrognomicon
    An adorable personal site dedicated to Morty, a gnomish necromancer on an undisclosed server. Includes extensive guides to tactics, necromantic recipes, and just about anything else you could think of for a necromancer. Very impressive.
Paladin
  • Paladins of Norrath
    Sponsored by Casters Realm, this site focuses on the cleric-warrior hybrid, the paladin. Much can be learned of this holy warrior and noble companion from the detailed spells listing, class and race guides, and quests and equipment catalog.
  • The White Cross
    An in depth compendium of information related to the holiest of warriors, the paladin. Features include spells, armor, skills, quests, and more.
Ranger
