GUILD RULES AND REGULATIONS v 3.0
 
hese are the rules and guidelines for membership in the Glory of the Glade . They are to be followed at all times without exceptions and breaking of the rules you will be placed under review and possibly a vote to be subject to dismissal from the guild. Please Read them and understand there meaning if you have questions ask you Ring of the Glade a.k.a. Class Leaders or a Order of the Glade a.k.a. High Council member for clarification.
 
RULE 1: Guild Invitations
  1.1 GLORY OF THE GLADE invites all Classes, Races, Sexes and Deities characters to the guild that have accomplished minimum Level 30.
  1.2 Alternate characters of Guild Members are invited to join with your main character as long as they have acquired a minimum level of 20. Some Exceptions may be made.
  1.3 Guild High Council Members , Guild Class Leader, Sergeant at Arms and Guild Raid Manager have the exclusive right and power to invite new members to the guild. Please use your best judgment when inviting a new member. Once invited please announce there invitation to the guild note there name, class, race and level along with the date of invite and report it to the guild here please newmember@gloryoftheglade.com
  1.4 Once invited you will be introduced to your Guild Class Leader either by name or in person if applicable. He or she will be your guide in the guild and act as your mentor and provide you help if needed.
  1.5 Once you become an approved guild member you will be placed under a 30-day review and at the end of that time you will be accessed by the High Council Members to determine if you are worthy to remain a member in the guild. Exception to the 30-day review rule Alternate Character of Main Characters already in guild and accepted past the 30-Days.
  1.6 You will be asked to fill out your character's profile under the membership application on this site within the first 30 days of membership. If this is not done within 30 Days you will be automatically removed from guild during your review.
  1.7 You will also be required to make a Magelo account of your character. This is free and will be posted with a link under your character's profile. Go to www.magelo.com to find out details and forward me your profile link in membership application. Here is a link to my profile http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=461742

RULE 3: Guild Rank
  3.1 The guild is run by the Order of the Glade a.k.a. OotG or simply put High Council Members. These members were created by the guild leadership at its infancy and the members we either asked to join by other OotG members by deeds done in the guild or were original members or the guild. The OotG have the finally say in all guild matters by vote of majority rule. The OotG is made up of a minimum of 3 members and a maximum of 5 members. They are appointed into the OotG by other High Council Members. They are responsible for making guild class leaders and other leaders i.e. sergeant at arms, guild historian and raid officers. They handle guild business, guild invites, votes and disciplinary actions.
  3.2 The next forms of leaders in the guild are the Ring of the Glade a.k.a. RotG or simply put the Class Leaders. There are 2 class leaders for each Everquest class. i.e. Mages have 2 class leaders, Rangers have 2 class leaders etc. They act as mentors for there class and are asked to help member of there guild specially related to there class. That is if a person of a certain class is looking for something or has a question in regards or related to their class they should try to help them out. They also act as mediators for any guild related issues that has created a problem concerning someone in there class. They are the person who appears in front of the High Council with you for any disciplinary action you may receive from the OotG and they will also advise the OotG about your acceptance to the guild after your 30 day trial period. If you have any problems with anything please speak to one of your class officers before bringing it up to the OotG. Get to know your class leader! (see Rule 1) Please note not all Classes have a leader yet you may apply for the position with OotG you must be level 50+ certain circumstances are accepted.
  3.3 The next forms of leader is the Sergeant at Arms a.k.a. SaA. This person is perhaps the most important person in the guild. He or she is to be a guild's eyes and ears, they moderator and will try to keep order in regards to guild events, chats and rulings. They will only answer to OotG officers and reports to them on a regular basis. The SaA is appointed by the OotG members. The SaA must be level 45+.
  3.4 The next form of leader is the The Old Glade a.k.a. OG who is the Guild Historian. They are appointed by the OotG and record guild events and hold the guild records. They report to and on the OtoG and other guild officers i.e. how a guild raid or event did. This position is open to a level 40+ member of the guild.
  3.5 The next form of leadership in the guild is the Guild Raid Manager. They are the head Guild Raid Officer in charge of all the Raid Officers and tries to coordinate guild raids. The Guild Raid Manager will be of level 50+ and is appointed by the OotG members
  3.6 Guild Raid Officers vary by levels. There will be 4 Guild Raid Officers and there level determines there position. They will be as follows level 1-20 another from 21-35 another from 36-50 and another from 51-60+. These Guild Raid Officers have no guild powers like inviting people to the guild. There sole purpose is to plan and coordinate guild raids with the Guild Manager and Guild members.
  3.7 We are also have set up a guild bank this position is not officer related and anyone that wants to hold items for the guild is welcome to set up a mule for this purpose. Please if you are interested in becoming a guild bank you need to play with regularity so we can access the items.

RULE 6: Kill Stealing
  6.1 There will be no Kill Stealing allowed at all in the guild. If you Kill Steal a.k.a. KS and the OotG, SaA or one of the other officers hear about it you are placed under review and put up to a vote to be removed from guild permanently. Officers and OotG members are not exempt from this rule. If you are in a group and they KS another group and you didn't know about it or you knew about it but didn't help in the KS then you are exempt and will be taken into consideration in your hearing in front of the OotG. If you KS by accident i.e. you were argoed by a mob that was rooted by other and it attacked you please promptly apologize and do not loot the body.

RULE 9: Guild Chat
  9.1 No Spamming, Flaming, Harassing or Begging will be tolerated in guild chat. Guild chat is a way for the guild to communicate with other guild members please keep the lines of communication open for guild matters only. Spamming is the typing of things over and over again; flaming is being disrespectful to others and the guild. If you have any guild problems please send a tell to your Class Leader (See Rule 4 above) Harassing in any form will not be tolerated and is grounds for immediate review and dismissal from guild. Also no begging for things over and over again is allowed politely ask for assistance or speak to your Class Leader for help. You may also speak to a Raid Officer for assistance in acquiring an item need i.e. epic or quests items. No Smartasses in the guild chat please! If someone is asking where he or she need to hunt or something be firm and straight with him or her ok. We can have our times of joking around but there are those times to be serious.

RULE 10: Guild Problems
  10.1 If you and another guild member are having problems then try to settle it out of the guild chat. Call on your Class leaders for some help in resolving issues related to the guild. i.e. if two warriors are having problems call both Warrior Class Leaders for help; if a warrior and a ranger are having problems call a Warrior Class Leader and a Ranger Class Leader for help (always have 2 officers present and 1 of each class) if both of your class leaders are off then as a last resort ask a OotG member for help straighten up things or the SaA may fill in for a missing class leader. Guild Raid Managers and Officers are not to get involved in matters like this because they are too busy planning a cool raid somewhere!

RULE 15: Guild Participation
 

15.1 In order to be an active member in this guild and help keep it strong you must participate in guild functions, events and meetings when applicable. You are required to attended at least one in every three guild raids or events for your level that is scheduled. You are also required to attend at least every other guild meeting or officer meeting if applicable. Exceptions: Real Life Issues and status of voluntary inactiveness. If you have RL issues and or want to become inactive for a period of time please inform a OotG officer of this decision and post your declaration of inactiveness on the guild Forum Page.

  15.2 If you fail to come to events you will placed on the inactive roster list for 30 Days. After 30 days of inactiveness you will be removed from guild with or without warning by a guild leader.
  15.3 Once you become inactive (30 Day) you will immediately become active again once you attend a raid or guild event. Continued inactiveness from missed guild raids and events may be also grounds for immediate removal by a vote of the Order of the Glade based on lack of guild support.
