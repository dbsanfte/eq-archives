  Barbarian Profile
Size Special Power Disadvantage Starting City
Large Slam No Night Vision Halas

A hardier variety of the Human race that comes from the cold and rugged northlands. Because of the harsh environment and their warlike culture, they are better suited to being Warriors than Humans are, and generally are unwashed and rugged -- possessing very few social graces. Barbarians are uncommonly tall for humans, with a girth that matches. They don't care much for Elves, Trolls, or Ogres, but deal passably well with most other races.

Barbarians are mighty warriors regardless of class and can deliver a Slam that will stun creatures.

Racial Modifiers: Since the Barbarians come from a harsh, unforgiving land, they're stronger and taller than their normal Human brethren. Warriors receive bonuses for Strength, Stamina, and Agility to reflect their warlike lifestyle, while Rogues are more Agile and Dexterous and Shamans are naturally Wiser and more Charismatic.

Class Limitations: Barbarians don't have much trust in magic or the mystical arts, they tend to be Warriors, pure and simple, or Rogues. There are Barbarians Shamans, but they're more spiritual than mystical, calling upon the power of the land to assist them.

Starting Areas: Barbarians begin in Halas, north of the frozen reaches of EverFrost on the main continent of Antonica. Barbarians are seldom seen outside of Halas and EverFrost at lower levels because they face several difficult challenges in order to reach the plains of Qeynos.

Redcloak's Thoughts: The Barbarian is the strongest of the "good" races, but simply can't compete with the overpowering Ogres. Their STR stats match up well with the Trolls, but their STA is 14 points lower. Their DEX is 5 lower than the Troll, and their AGIs are near the same. However, the Barbarian is higher than the Troll in INT, WIS, and CHA. Basically, if your looking to be a Warrior (which by the way is about to be upgraded by Verant because of complaints), and you don't want to be an evil Ogre, than the Barbarian is the race I would select. They have the higher CHA and INT needed for being a Shaman even though their STA is slightly lower. For the Warrior their STR, STA, and AGI, are all comparable to that of the Troll, and finally the Barbarian Rogue is the least "gifted" of the race. Their AGI and DEX while decent could be higher, and I would probably go with a different race.
