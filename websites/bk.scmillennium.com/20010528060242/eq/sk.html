Summary of information on the Shadow Knight as well as links to where you can find more in-depth coverage.
  Shadow Knight Profile
Important Attributes: STR and STA are important for battle, while WIS and CHA are used for the Necromantic arts.

Races: Dark Elf, Human, Ogre, Troll

Specific Skills: Along with the special Necromantic spells learned at level 9, SKs earn several skills. including Dodge at level 10, Bash at level 12, Parry at level 17, Double Attack at 20, Riposte at 30, Hide at 35, and Disarm at level 40. Every Shadow Knight begins with Harm Touch, the evil inverse of a Paladin's "Lay of Hands", but similiarly can only be used once a day. SKs learn the magic related skills of Abjure, Alter, Channel, Conjure, Divinate, and Evoke at level 9, and then Meditate at level 14.

Redcloak's Thoughts:Many people have commented on how hard it is to play as a SK. I've never done it myself so I can't say much except your in for a challenge if you take this path.
