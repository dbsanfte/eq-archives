Summary of information on the Druid as well as links to where you can find more in-depth coverage.
  Druid Profile

Important Attributes: STA for building hp, WIS for learning new spells.

Races: Half Elf, Human, Wood Elf

Specific Skills: Along with the spells that a Druid learns, they also learn the Ranger skill of Forage at level 5, the ability to Dodge at level 15, and the oppurtunity to specialize in skills at level 30. Additionally, Druids need to learn tha magic-related skills of Abjure, Alter, Channel(level 4), Conjure, Divinate, Evoke, and Meditate(level 8).

Spell Listing:
 Level 1:

  • Skin Like Wood - Add AC and MAX HP
  • Glimpse - Brief Telescope sight
  • Minor Healing - Healing
  • Endure Fire - Adds to fire resistance
  • Flame Lick - AC on Target and some damage
  • Lull Animal - Lower animal reactions
  • Snare - Lower movement rate of target
Level 4:
  • Burst of Fire - Direct damage
  • Cure Poison - self explanatory
  • Camouflage - Invisible
  • Grasping roots - Root w/damage
  • Invoke Lightning - Area Effect Direct Damage
  • Feet Like Cat - +Agi
  • Thistlecoat - Personal Armor

Level 9:

  • Enduring Breath - Water breathing
  • Ignite - Direct Damage
  • Ward Summoned - Direct Damage to summoned creatures
  • Harmony - Calms animals outdoors
  • Skin Like Rock - Add AC and max HP
  • Bind Sight - See thru target and fatigue loss

Level 14:

  • Bind Affinity - Set home point
  • Summon Food - Summon food item
  • See Invisible - Take a wild guess
  • Summon Drink -  Summon drink item 
  • Invigor - Restores fatigue
  • Befriend Animal - Charms animal
  • Levitation - Levitate
  • Cascade of Hail - Area effect rain damage spell
  • Skin Like Rock - Add AC and max HP
  • Stinging Swarm - Persistent direct damage
  • Spirit of Wolf - Add to speed and fatigue
  • Halo of Light - Light, Add AC
  • Expulse Summoned - Direct damage to summoned only

Level 19:  

  • Healing - I wonder...
  • Superior Camouflage - Invisibility
  • Cancel Magic - Another riddle to me...
  • Feral Spirit - Add to pets AC, STR, and speed
  • Endure Disease - Add to disease resistance
  • Endure Poison - Add to poison resistance
  • Shield of Barbs - Damage shield
  • Careless Lightning - Direct damage
  • Calm Animal - Lulls animal
  • Terrorize Animal - NPC animal run away
  • Barbcoat - Personal armor
  • Dizzying Wind - Direct damage with 2 second stun
  • Ring of Karana - Teleport to N. Karana
  • Ring of Commons - Teleport to the Commonlands
  • Ring of Toxxulia - Teleport to Toxxulia Forest
  • Ring of Butcher - Teleport to Butcherblock Mountains

Level 24:

  • Pogonip - Area effect damage spell
  • Resist Fire - Add to fire resistance
  • Ensaring Roots - Root w/damage
  • Creeping Crud - Persistent direct damage
  • Dismiss Summoned - Direct damage for summoned only
  • Sunbeam - Area effect blindness
  • Spirit of Cheetah - Increases movement speed for 18 seconds
  • Charm Animals - Animal follows you till the spell wears off, at which point the animal will most likely attack you.
  • Tremor - Area effect radius direct damage
  • Skin Like Steel - Add to AC and max HP
  • Wolf Form - Turn self into Wolf
  • Ring of Lavastorm - Teleport to Lavastorm
  • Ring of Ro - Teleport to Southern Ro
  • Ring of Feerott - Teleport to the Ferrott
  • Ring of Steamfont - Teleport to Steamfont Mountains 

Redcloak's Thoughts: The Druid receives teleporting earlier than even the Wizard. They are very in tune with Nature, and at later levels can charm animals. The Druid is very hard to pick a race for much like the Bard, but my choice would be the Wood Elf. It's stats are slightly higher than the others in the important sections overall. Second I would choose human, and last Half Elf.
