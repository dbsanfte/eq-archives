  Erudite Profile
Size Special Power Disadvantage Starting City
 Medium  n/a No Night Vision Erudin

     The final variant of the Human race. Being the exact opposite of the Barbarians, the High Man prides himself on his intellectual abilities and social graces to the almost total exclusion of the physical diciplines. Erudites are almost as self-involved as High Elves and take a narrow view of most other races. As a result, Erudites will find it hard to get good service from merchants outisde of their own cities. In fact, city guards in otherwise peaceful human towns have been known to take up arms at the sight of an Erudite.

Racial Modifiers: The High Man shuns common tasks and menial labor, finding overdeveloped muscles to be grotesque. Their natural intelligence is the highest of all other races in Norrath, but their other abilities suffer from their views toward physical efforts.

Class Limitations: Mental disciplines are the most prized aspects of the Erudite race, particularly those in the areas of the magical arts. Erudites are possibly the most formidable Necromancers in all of Norrath. Any class that uses magic(excluding naturalistic powers) are open to the Erudite, even those who feel the calling to take up arms and become Paladins.

Starting Areas: Erudites begin on their small island west of Antonica in their main city of Erudin.

Redcloak's Thoughts: Erudites have the highest intelligence of any race, a STR that is slightly higher than the High Elves. Why then are they not the best spell casters in all cases? Because they lack 15 points of agility that the High Elves do have. Their CHA and WIS are also lower than the high elves. Why should I play as an Erudite then you ask? Because the stats that they have are the most important to the spell casters. INT is the mana for pure spell casters (Wizards, Necros, Mages, Enchanters). I wouldn't recommend being a Paladin or a Cleric because their STR like the High Elves is still to low to melee fight well. Another thing to be weary of is that Erudites are not loved among the good races. While usually not killed by guards on sight, they face highly raised prices in places outside of Erudin. IF you are going to play as an Erudite I would recommend the Necromancer. Sure, you will be hated by everyone, but no other Necro's can reach the full power that you can as an Erudite. If you want a class/race combo that is challenging with great rewards that is your pick.
