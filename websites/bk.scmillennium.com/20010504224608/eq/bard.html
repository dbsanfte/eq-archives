 Bard Profile

Important Attributes: Dexterity for Rogue skills, Wisdom for spell casting, and Charisma for influence.

Races: Human, Half Elf, Wood Elf

Specific Skills: Bards can learn magical Songs. There are four different types of instruments that Bards can purchase to enhance their Songs' effects. Percussion (level 5), stringed (level 8), wind (lvl 14), and brass (lvl 11). Bard's also learn a number of Rogue skills — including Disarm Traps (lvl 30), Pick Locks (lvl 40), and Safe Fall (lvl 24) — Ranger skills — Dodge (lvl 10), Forage (lvl 12), Sneak (lvl 17), and Track (lvl 35).

Redcloak's Thoughts: The bard is like the rogue in Diablo. A mixture of the spell caster and the warrior. Only a bard is for Everquest :). Bard's are very good in groups, but can struggle alone. If you've ever heard the phrase "Jack of all trades, master of none." It applies here. While, you will have a broad variety of abilities, you won't be able to compare with the specific types in their element, but then they won't have your vast knowledge will they? It's hard to say what the race best suited for Bard-dom is, but I think I would select Wood Elves. Closely followed by Humans and Half Elves. Half Elves have higher DEX than the others, but lack much in Wisdom. Humans are the middle of both with good DEX and good WIS, but not the best in any category. Wood Elves are lowest in DEX, but highest in INT. They are all equal in CHA. It's a tuff decision, but you probably won't be disappointed with any selection.
