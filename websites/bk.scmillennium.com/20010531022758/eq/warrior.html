Warrior Profile
Important Attributes: STR, STA, and AGI are the key aspects of a Warrior. Put all your bonus points into them. STR allows you to carry more loot and determines how hard/often you hit your opponent. STA is your hits per level and endurance. AGI (also DEX to a point) is your ability to dodge attacks and run away.

Races: Barbarian, Dark Elf, Dwarf, Gnome, Half Elf, Halfling, Human, Ogre, Troll, Wood Elf.

Specific Skills: Kick ,taunt(can save your spell casters life), bind wound(lvl 5, works like healing, but only below 50%. Requires bandages, so talk to a mage), dodge(lvl 6), Bash(lvl 9), parry(lvl 10), duel weld(lvl 13), double attack(lvl 15), riposte(lvl 25), and disarm(lvl 35).[Note: in the upcoming patch the Warrior is most likely going to be improved in numerous areas, so keep an eye out for that.]

Summary:Warriors are the true tanks of Norrath. Paladins and Shadow Knights have alot of hit points and good abilities and spells, but they can never have the hp of a fully buffed up warrior. Most races of Norrath allow warriors. The only rases who don't are the High Elves and the Erudites. The best warriors are the Ogres but they're evil. They have Strength that is unmatched by any other race though. Barbarians all around make the best warriors.

Redcloak's Thoughts:When it comes down to hardcore tanking, the warrior is the one who can handle the task. His abilities only enhance his powerful standing as a tank. A Warrior a few levels lower than a caster still works as an effective tank just because of their raw power(even at low levels).
