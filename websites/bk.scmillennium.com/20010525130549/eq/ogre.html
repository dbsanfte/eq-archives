  Ogre Profile
Size Special Power Disadvantage Starting City
Large Ultravision, Bash Evil Oggok
The tallest and most massive of all the humanoid races, Ogres generally stand about ten feet tall and weigh well over four hundred pounds. Ogres are a brutal and misshapen race, with projecting tusks and gnarled faces. Ogres have eked out a small measure of society on their own as amazing as that seems.
Ogres are definitely trying, however, While Rallos Zek was more free with muscles than brains when creating the Ogres, they have come to covet some of the benefits that obviously come along with civilization (more food, more wars) and are attempting to mimic Human culture in some ways. There is a definite hierarchy evident within their home base of Oggok, but it's not exactly the pinnacle of society. Travelers of Oggok, pleased to have escaped with their lives, have returned with strange stories of Bouncers, explosive and violent mating rituals and the most convoluted and stupid Shadow Knights conspiracies hatched upon Norrath.
Other races have learned to be cautious around Ogres (especially the smaller ones who tend to be used as footstools and appetizers), for their glimmers of intelligence and apocalyptic levels of violence make them almost as deadly an ally as enemy. Ogres are very sensitive about their intellectual shortcomings and take the view that actions speak much louder than words, especially when those actions result in the quick and thorough pummeling of the person speaking those words.
Ogres start with the ability to Bash, which makes a certain amount of sense as does their Ultravision which lets them see clearly in their dark and cavernous hideouts.

Racial Modifiers: Due to their size they are the strongest of all the races and are tied with their Trolls in pure STA. Conversely their lives consist mostly of smashing things and eating them.

Class Limitations: Obviously, Ogres are not producing a great deal of spell casters. In fact, the Ogre's dabbling in magic has been so spectacularly unsuccessful that the race as a whole has opted to leave it alone. Their Shamans are able to tap the life-forces of the world around them (one mage, who was later pounded flat by an Ogre who overheard, remarked, "They're as smart as dirt why shouldn't they be able to draw STR from it?"). Woe betide those who anger an Ogre Shadow Knight for your horse may one day awaken with your head in it's bed.

Starting Areas: The Ogres have carved themselves a home, Oggok, in the southern reaches of Antonica not far from the Troll city.

Redcloak's Thoughts: If your looking for sheer strength you found your race. Far surpassing any other race including the Trolls the base stats are over 20 points higher in strength. Their stamina matches that with ungodly high values while the rest of their skills reflect that. Their charisma is the lowest of any race, and their intelligence is only better than the foolish Trolls. Their Wisdom is slightly higher than the Trolls, and their Agility and Dexterity are below average. I have to discourage anyone from becoming a Ogre Shadow Knight(SK) because while they are very high in STR and STA, they are very low in WIS and CHA the other two important charistics of a SK. If you want to be a Warrior you can't find a better race. Unrivaled STR and STA allow them to literally pound most things into the ground. I would recommend putting a bunch of your bonus points into Agility as that is the other stat important to Warriors. As for the Ogre Shaman I again have to shy away because of the terribly low charisma and Wisdom, but they are very high in STA the other important trait of Shamans.
