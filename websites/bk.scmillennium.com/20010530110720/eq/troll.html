Troll Profile
Size Special Power Disadvantage Starting City
Large Infravision, Slam, Regeneration Evil Grobb
Trolls typically stand about 8' tall, are extremely strong, ugly, stupid, dirty, and green of skin. They will eat anything that holds still and cripple anything stupid enough to move. Trolls are big and green and like to kill living things in unpleasant ways. Unlike the Ogres, Trolls haven't made much of an effort to embrace civilization in any way. Their homeland is still little more than the swamp it started out as. Trolls can see in the dark using Infravision and can Slam opponents.

Racial Modifiers: To call Trolls ugly is a great understatement. Their hideousness has gone out the other side, approaching a negative inverse of attractiveness that's nearly as striking. Intelligence has nearly been bred out of the Troll race it's still an ongoing project.

Class Limitations: Trolls are in a very similar situation to Ogres. Too dumb to know better, too strong to be stopped. Trolls are mainly encouraged to be Warriors, but can also be Shadow Knights or Shamans.

Starting Areas: Their swampy refuge, Grobb, lies at the southernmost expanse of Antonica. This distance is greatly appreciated by other races.

Redcloak's Thoughts: Flat out looking at the statistics. Your best choice of a Troll would be the Shadow Knight. Beyond that it depends whether you want to be a more magical Shaman or a brute Warrior. I'd recommend the Shaman over the Warrior, but I'm biased to spell casters.
