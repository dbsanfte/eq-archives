  Human Profile
Size Special Power Disadvantage Starting City
 Medium Gains Levels Faster No Night Vision, One Racial Language Freeport, Qeynos

     The youngest of all the races of Norrath, Humans have quickly because the singlemost dominant faction in the world, much to the anoyance of the older races like the High Elves. Humans are related to the similar Barbarian race of Everfrost, but are rumored to have been the last race created by direct divine action before the various deities were forced back to their respective Planes.

     Humans have been hard at work trying to catch up to the rest of Norrathian society and have even approached the High Elves in their knowledge and understanding of magic. Sionce the founding of Qeynos by Antonius Bayle, Humankind has been the fulcrum that moves Norrath.

     Humans are not deterred by any odds, and being told they cannot do something only makes them try all the harder (and usually succeed). A brash and passionate race, Humans are quick to rush into any situation without pasing to consider the options. This may be the cause of their unusually short lifespans (when compared to Dwarves or Elves).

     Humans have no remarkable addition abilities or powers, but do seem to Level up faster than other races.

Racial Modifiers: Humans are considered the norm by which all other races in Norrath are judged. As such all their abilities ar considered "baseline".

Class Limitations: All classes are open to Humans, with the exception of the Shaman class (Humans are not as in touch with nature a some of the other races). Humans have no particular strength or weakness in terms of classes. Humans are the only race that can be Monks though.

Starting Areas: The two major Human cities, Qeynos and Freeport, have become the hubs of trade and commerce in Anotonica.

Redcloak's Thoughts: Humans are not the pinnacle of power in any such race, but rather more average than anything else(excluding Monks of course). If your looking for a balanced character Humans are probably your choice. If your looking for a specialized character than I'd go with another race.
