A detailed listing of all the trade skills available in EverQuest.
  Trade Skills

Alchemy: Allows Shamans to concoct potions.
Example: Charming deceit and Troll's essence.
Requires: Medicine bag.

Baking: Allows people to produce a wide variety of baked goods.
Examples: Bixie Crunchies, Dwarf Chops
Requires: Oven

Brewing: Allows any inhabitant of Norrath to produce potent alcoholic beverages.
Examples: Mead, Short Beer, Kalish
Requires: Still

Blacksmithing: Allows people to manufacture valuable metal items.
Examples: Lockpicks, Iron Boots, Muffin Tins.
Requires: Forge

Fletching: Allows anyone to construct a wide variety of bows and arrows.
Examples: Elm Linen Bow, Class 1 Point Porcelain Arrow.
Requires: Fletching kit

Foraging: Allows nature orientated classes to find food and water.
Examples: Grubs, Roots, Rabbits
Required: Nothing

Fishing: Allows people to pull fresh fish from larger bodies of water.
Examples: Fish (deep eh?)
Requires: Pole and Bait

Jewel Craft: Allows anyone to fashion jewelry, gems, and precious metals.
Examples: Golden hematite choker, Engagement rings and Wedding rings.
Requires: Jewelry kit

Pottery: Allows people to craft clay into pottery.
Examples: Small bowl, small clay container, clay deity.
Requires: Pottery wheel and kiln

Research: Allows the spell casters to assemble spells from component pieces.
Examples: N/A
Requires: Class specific (Ex Wizards: runes).

Tailoring: Allows anyone to sew fine leather items from hides and pelts.
Examples: Raw hide tunic, Backpack, Tattered Belt.
Requires: Tailoring kit

Tinkering: Allows Gnomes to fabricate mechinical items.
Example: Collapsible fishing pole, Gnomish fireworks, and a Spyglass.
