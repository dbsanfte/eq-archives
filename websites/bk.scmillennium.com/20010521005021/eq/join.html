Apprenticeship Form
Becoming an apprentice to an Order Knight is simple and fun. After you hit submit the email will go to our staff, and they will contact your Knight to confirm the apprenticeship. At which point you will be an official apprentice. You must be an apprentice for an undetermined amount of time(usually one to two weeks)before officially becoming a Knight.
