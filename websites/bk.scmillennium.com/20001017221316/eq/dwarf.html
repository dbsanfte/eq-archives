  Dwarf Profile
Size Special Power Disadvantage Starting City
Small  Ultravision, Sense Direction n/a Kaladin

     Dwarves are short, but extremely strong(much more so than Humans) and dexterous. They appear to be small, burly humans and are never seen without beards and mustaches. Dwarves are a stout and hardy people and take life very seriously. Whatever their course of action, be it fighting, drinking, or digging for gold, Dwarves operate with a singlemindedness that belies their size.

     Since Dwarves spend much of their time undergrounds, they have developed ultravision which allows them to see into the ultraviolet radiation spectrum. They also possess a preternatural skill at finding their way through caverns and mine shafts, which translates to a heigthened Sense Direction.

Racial Modifiers: Like the ground they come from Dwarves are a Strong lot, but not particularly Intelligent. Their Agility is limited by their stature, yet they are surprisingly Dexterous. The Dwarven ideals of beauty are unlike the other humanoid races; facial hair on a female is considered not only normal, but attractive.

Class Limitations: Even though Dwarves have a long natural lifespan, they prefer deeds and actions to thought and meditation - Paladins, Warriors and Rogues are more preferable lifestyles than magic users. they feel closely connected with their deities, whose existance is obvious to even the most closed-minded of Dwarves and so they make great Clerics.

Starting Areas: The underground homeland of the Dwarves is located in Kaladim on the smaller continent of Faydwer, on the coast furthest away from those pesky Elves.

Redcloak's Thoughts: The dwarves may not be the prettiest race in all of Norrath, but they make up for that in their unusually high STR, STA, and DEX. Their AGI is average, but they really hurt in their low INT and extremely low CHA. When you look at their stats the Dwaven Cleric is simply the best possible selection for a Cleric. Lots of STR, STA, and a decent WIS, that could be raised with your 30 bonus points. In my opinion Clerics are already one of the most powerful classes, but putting STR with WIS makes this race/class combo extreme. You might be lead to believe that the Paladin would also be a really excellent combo for the Dwarven race, but I would have to say no on it. Your WIS needs to be raised, but so does your extremely low CHA. Both important traits of the Paladin. Moving on to the Dwarven Rogue, while the Dwarven race supplies the high DEX required to be a Rogue their AGI is just average. Luckily for you, you still have 30 points to add in wherever you like. You can forget the CHA here because Rogues aren't exatcly a popular race :). Finally, is the Dwarven Warrior. Lower in STR, AGI, CHA and STA than the Barbarian, the Dwarven race only excels past Barbarians in DEX and WIS. Unfortunately, the most important stats for a Warrior are AGI, STR, and STA, so I would have to pass on being a Warrior with this race.
