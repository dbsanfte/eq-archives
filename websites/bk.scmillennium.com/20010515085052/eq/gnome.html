Gnome Profile
Size Special Power Disadvantage Starting City
 Small  Tinkering,Ultravision  n/a  Ak'Anon 

     -Incomplete-

Racial Modifiers: Not quite as burly as their cousins, the Gnomes aren't a particularly tough race. They are fast and agile though and their innate tinkering abilities show through in their high INT.

Class Limitations: Gnomes tend to be a positive race and are not drawn to the relentlessly evil lifestyle of the Shadow Knights. They do make excellent mages and clerics, and their small stature gives them an advantage for Roguish behaviors. Their size also presents their Warriors as small targets harder for large monsters to hit.

Starting Areas: The clockwork Gnomish city of Ak'Anon is located on the southernmost tip of Faydwer, in the high regions of the SteamFont Mountains.

Redcloak's Thoughts: Gnomes are evil little creatures. Actually I haven't had enough time to look into them just yet, but I will update this when I do.
