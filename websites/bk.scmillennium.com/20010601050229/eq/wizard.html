  Wizard Profile

Important Attributes: STA and INT are both important for the type of magic that Wizards use.

Races: Dark Elf, Erudite, Gnome, High Elf, Human

Specific Skills: A Wizard's powers come almost entirely from his spells. Wizards also need to learn the magic-related skills of Abjure, Alter, Channel, Conjure, Divinate, Evoke, and Meditate(level 4). Wizards begin to Dodge attacks at level 22, Specializing at level 20, and can conduct Research at level 16.

Redcloak's Thoughts: For pure offensive spell casting you can't be beat. That is until you get to level 4. You may find yourself doing 13 points of damage while a Cleric could do more. Once you hit level 8, you will be able to cast much more powerful spells(35 and 45 bolt). At level 49, the Wizard gains a spell that does 1100+ points of damage. Show those newbies whose boss :). Anyway, Wizards can't do melee combat, they can't heal people, but what they can do is transport people. Starting at level 24 as a matter of fact. I will be writing a guide on this later so I won't spend to much time on the Wizard.
