This page is a FAQ of sorts on the Order of the Blood Knights.
  OBK Information
  • What server does the OBK operate on?
    OBK is currently operating on the Rodcet Nife server.We are considering also playing on a PvP teams server.
  • Could I start OBK on another server?
    Possibly you will have to speak with Redcloak regarding that.
  • What races and classes can join OBK?
    We accept all races/classes because we don't want to limit your role-playing experience.
  • What are the goals of OBK?
    See the charter for a wonderfully detailed list of goals.
  • Do you have a member limit?
    Due to the nature of EQ we do not have a member limit.
  • How do you join OBK?
    Via the application page which has more information regarding joining.
  • May I use the graphics/text/info from this site at my site?
    Sure, just make sure you tell us, and give us credit.
  • Do you have meetings?
    Not at the current time.
  • Who can I contact with more questions?
    Redcloak at redcloak@sclegacy.com
