MISSION STATEMENT

When I had initially sat down to write out a mission statement for this particular section of darkimes.org, I had begun by drafting a list of bullet point objectives that we wished to accomplish with our web presence...then something occured to me. Who cares? Really and truly, do you want to read some grocery list of chest beating and "rebellion"? Of course you don't, and the last thing I would wish to come of this is for it to come across as some half-sincere manifesto of catch-phrases and other assorted jargon. Instead, I'll write what I really feel, and what I really want.

I want you to be pissed off... I mean, I want you to be really pissed off. I'm tired of being angry alone, and I'm tired of people around me ignoring the crap going on around us. I don't like having my money pulled from my pocket and being used to fund some politicians pet project for re-election. I don't want Uncle Sam telling me what my child can watch, or what I can read. You know what? It makes me sick to my stomach that the American public can sit back and watch its government murder a building full of men, women, and children who were guilty of nothing more than having "strange" religious beliefs, and say that it was okay. I'm sick of the government telling businesses who they can and cannot hire, and I'm sick of a justice system designed to protect the rich. I'm sick of Feudalism being passed off as Capitalism and Democracy when in truth Democracy is a farce.

As more and more time goes by, the government has grown more and more like a giant parental figure, spanking our bottoms if we don't do as they say, and spoon feeding us their beliefs and wants. More and more lawsuits go to court everyday that make us all seem like childish little brats fighting in the playroom (and we are), sueing away while society rots around us. Schools and education are shit due to overstaffed administrative positions, while good teachers are being squeezed out. In short, society as a whole is going to hell, and the people we elect to solve these large-scale problems throw their patchwork solutions at us to keep us quiet while they pad their retirement fund.

So, you're wondering, what exactly is the mission statement, aside from some incoherent rambling that really hasn't gone anywhere. Well it's this: I want you to be as pissed off as I am. I want you to be as annoyed as I am. I want you to be informed, educated, and I want you to be an opinionated yet open-minded, loud-mouthed slob, just like I am. Even more importantly, I want you to take what you learn and harness what you feel, and do something.

Have a nice day.
