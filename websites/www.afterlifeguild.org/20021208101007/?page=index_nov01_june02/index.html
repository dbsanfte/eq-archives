Tuesday May 14, 2002
 

Posted by Thott
Cross Server Applications
Apparently I wasn't clear.  We are accepting very limited cross server applications, i.e., we may accept as many as...three.  Anyone with less than 80 AA (except for clerics), need not apply.  This offer exists only to allow polygonal dieties a means of making the transfer.  It is not a call for general recruitment.

Clerics are a special exception.  Since we prefer to have real, live, focused people play clerics, and not someone's left hand whapping a keyboard connected to a second computer, we are always looking for more.  Competent clerics, on MM or off it, are encouraged to apply.
