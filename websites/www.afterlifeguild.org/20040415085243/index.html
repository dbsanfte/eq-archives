GoD
When someone asks me "what kind of things do you do in Everquest?" and I respond, "I fight bugs", they get completely the wrong impression.  Nevertheless, we have prevailed!  Mostly.  Random loot shots:

Most recent advancement boss, with loot:

He wasn't difficult.  This expansion has three types of raid content, all three about equally common:

  • Easy (bugged?)
  • Impossible (bugged!) Example: Ikkinz, Chambers of Trancendence v1.0 and v2.0
  • Impossible (bugged?) Example: Uqua
NPC's are also hungry this expansion, and we've had several of our nice key items eaten by famished fiends and friends.  We petitioned for a GM, Jesus and the Easter Bunny, and I expect all three will arrive at around the same time.

For anyone progressing through the expansion, I suggest carefully testing each stage, to avoid item loss.  For example, instead of directly giving your good item to a NPC, first, test the NPC with a different item.  If the NPC gives it back, then move on to stage two: have someone else give the real item.  I used Hobben.  He has now lost both his Icon of the Altar and his Sliver of the High Temple, while I safely have both of mine.  I highly recommend this technique.
