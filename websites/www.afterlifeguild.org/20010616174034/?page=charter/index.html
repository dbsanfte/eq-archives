Distressed world
 


Destruction
Flames leaped from the remnants of a village, giving the smoke an eerie orange glow. The cries of women mourning echoed through the near empty streets as loved ones were discovered to have been slain in the wake of the latest destruction left by feuding gods. This was the scene of devastation replayed all over the lands of Norrath. But from the ashes arose a new generation, determined to no longer be blinded by their differences, all races were now equal since they all shared a common threat, their creators. Friends became foes, foes became friends. Undaunted they accomplished feats which were said to be impossible, the victim's were becoming the victors. Crossing the boundaries of reality, Gods could no longer run and hide from their children and as they fell, the remaining ones either disconnected their grasp upon the lands, or flooded the lands with more of their minions in order to protect themselves. However the Afterlife, as they came to call themselves, will never rest until every god is vanquished, every threatening creature destroyed, and peace settles over the land like never before, because then, and only then, they will be free.
