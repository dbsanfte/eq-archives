Bard Song Classifications

By Thott

Discuss
A few times in the past the subject came up, how many offensive songs do bards have?  How many defensive songs?  So I sat down and classified all of them.

The only questionable classification is slow songs.  I classified them as offensive because they're used on the mob.  If you think they're defensive, just subtract the number of slow songs from the offensive column and add that number to the defensive column.
