Joining Afterlife
 


Yes, Afterlife is recruiting, Afterlife is ALWAYS recruiting.   We just don't add very many, or add very often.   What kind of player is right for Afterlife?  We want players that know everything about the game.  Players that have a desire to do the impossible, and are willing to die plenty to obtain it.  Players that don't give up or complain when things get tough.  Players that play a LOT, every day, because that's how we play as a guild.  

We raid every day, usually starting at 5:30pm PST. If you can't play from that point until after midnight, then AL isn't for you.

We play on Mithaniel Marr exclusively, your characters must too.

For players with the right dedication and attitude, there are also level and item requirements:

To join Afterlife a player must have one character that meets the above table level requirements, and a second character that is at least level 20.  Preferable is two or more characters that meet the above level requirements.

Desireability is a combination of class usefulness for what we do and how many we already have.  Check back periodically for desireability updates.

You must also have the following equipment:

  • Innate Run Speed 3 (AA)
  • Sebilis key
  • Charasis key
  • Some form of permanent enduring breath item
  • Resist gear, MINIMUM of 100 fire/cold/disease/poison unbuffed, while simultaneously having 100 unbuffed in magic resist.  We have members that can reach 200/200, 100/100 is bare minimum.
  • Jboots (yes, run3 AND jboots)
  • Shrunken goblin skull earring (5k or less in FP *Edit - price is higher since adding this as a requirement*), and/or velium coldain ring (for warriors and monks, both).
  • 100+ sense heading
  • 185 swimming
  • At least one VP (Veeshan's Peak) key piece, perferably the full key.
  • Cobalt Scar key (Shrine Key)
  • A solid non-winmodem 45k+ connection, or ISDN+ (DSL/Cable).  If you always go LD during raids, or even just fall back in time, we can't use you.
  • P3 750, 256MB, and a fast video card, better hardware preferred.
  • 5 Golem metal wands.

Once you meet the general requirements, then it's best if you have a sponsor.  A sponsor will teach you how to work well within Afterlife, what rules we have, what techniques we use, and answer any questions you may have.  A sponser must be a full member of Afterlife that knows you and can vouch for your playing ability.  Having a sponsor is a significant advantage.

If you can't find a sponsor, all is not lost. Fill out the app, and most likely a mentor will be chosen for you.

When you're ready to join, and meet all of the requirements: Apply Online

Direct any questions or comments to Recruiter@Afterlifeguild.org. Be sure to include your character(s) name, class and level.
