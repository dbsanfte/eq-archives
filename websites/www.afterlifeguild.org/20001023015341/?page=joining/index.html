Joining Afterlife
 


Level Requirements

To join Afterlife a player must have one character that meets the above table level requirements, and a second character that is at least level 20.  Preferable is two or more characters that meet the above level requirements.  The level requirement for Paladins is not a typo.

Desireability is a combination of class usefulness for what we do and how many we already have.  Check back periodically for desireability updates.

You must also have the following equipment:

  • Sebilis key
  • Charasis key
  • Some form of permanent enduring breath item
  • Resist gear, MINIMUM of 100 fire/cold/disease/poison unbuffed, while simultaneously having 100 unbuffed in magic resist.
  • Jboots
  • 100+ sense heading
  • At least one VP (Veeshan's Peak) key piece.
  • ISDN or faster Internet connectivity.

Once you meet the general requirements, then it's best if you have a sponser.  A sponser will teach you how to work well within Afterlife, what rules we have, what techniques we use, and answer any questions you may have.  A sponser must be a full member of Afterlife that knows you and can vouch for your playing ability.  Having a sponser is a significant advantage.

If you can't find a sponser, all is not lost. Contact Gospadin, our Initiate Handler, and he'll start you on the road to joining Afterlife, even though you have no sponser.  It won't be as easy, but at least it's possible.

Once the entrance requirements are met, and Gospadin has been contacted, the first step is an interview in /tell. If the interview goes well, you will be invited to guild events as an initiate. The initiate period lasts anywhere from one week to more than a month, depending on the activity level and playing ability of the initiate.

Assuming all goes well, after the initation period you'll be permanently invited into the guild, and be a full member of Afterlife.

Direct any questions, comments, or requests to join to Gospadin.
