Singing Short Sword Quest Completed!

Today, October 1st 2000, at 1am PST, Thott finished the bard FA quest.  As far as we know, this is the first Singing Short Sword to exist on any production server.

Here's the quest completion text:

Here are the item stats:

The Dance of the Blade effect is a proc that affects the entire party.   Unfortunately the range on it is extremely short, such that only those very close to the bard (30 units, roughly melee range) are affected.  The proc gives 55% haste, 30 str, 30 atk, and lasts 2.5 minutes.  The haste component does not stack with bard haste, so in effect this adds 10%, not 55%, haste.

The weapon can be wielded in either hand, but will not go in the ranged slot (unlike the rogue FA weapon), which means it's useless when the bard us using an instrument.

Compared to Breath of Harmony (which is primary hand only), it randomly adds some haste, atk, and str, which is quite nice, but at the cost of losing a reliable 10str and 7hp/tick regen. The weapon ratio is 10% better than BoH, but the weapon is 44% slower, which means less damage for the ratio when held in primary hand.

I haven't done any real tests, but I'm assuming this will do more damage than BoH.  Since BoH doesn't go in the offhand slot, and this weapon does, it may be that putting SSS in the offhand (23% better than crystaline short sword) and BoH in the primary will do the most damage.
