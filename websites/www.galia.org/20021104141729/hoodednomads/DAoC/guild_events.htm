The Hooded Nomads had always enjoyed events and activities that triggers their excitement. Challenge, camaraderie, enjoyment, friendship and amusements are always the essentials ingredients of how a Nomad have FUN!! For us, we make it a point that everyone is having fun, getting experience or sometimes have a new armour, weapon or accessories to bring to their vaults. Below are the list of activities and events this guild enjoys alot...

NEW!!! Level Up Contest: A few months ago, Ogjellomanjr suggested for the guild to have an event that everyone can enjoy. He suggested to have a LEVEL UP CONTEST which we think would be an exciting activity for the Hooded Nomads members. Below are the details:

* Title of the Contest: Level Up Contest

* Objective of the Contest: To be able to motivate and enhance the members to play their MAIN characters and level up quickly. Likewise, to encourage comradeship between the Nomads.

* Duration of the Contest: October 13 - 19, 2002 - Time Start: 1:00 am EST (Sunday) October 13, 12:00 mn EST (Saturday) October 19

* Rules & Regulations:

- Qualified members should be level 15 – 40
- Character should be their MAIN character in the Galahad server – Midgard Realm.
- All Officers and their alts are not qualified for this event.
- The member who gets most level within the duration of this contest will win prizes accordingly.
- The member should get 2 levels within the duration of this contest to be eligible to join the finals.
 There is a Point Systems for each level:
15-20 = 1 pt
21-25 = 1.5 pts
26-30 = 2 pts
31-35 = 2.5 pts
36-40 = 3 pts

* Registration Procedures:  Registration will start on Saturday, October 12, 2002 at 5:00 pm EST. Guild Vault Clearing: This is a weekly event that the Hooded Nomads have for the low level members. This is an event wherein all the High level members (UBER ones) gather their stuff from their vaults and then hand it out to low level Nomads who can use it. We usually gather during Thursdays, 8:00 pm at the Gna Hut Vault. 
Guild Realm Vs. Realm: This is an event wherein most guildies gather and head out to the Hibernia or Albion Frontiers. We kill, smash, mezz and do different stuff to this people from different realms. The guild  receives Bounty points and the Nomad receives realm points to obtain a realm ability point.
Guild Hunts: This is another gathering of the Nomads, depending on their levels, to form groups and head out to experience hunt. 
Guild Character Portrait Screenshot Sessions: This is a session for the Hooded Nomads wherein they dress up on their fine and lovely armour for a character Portrait screenshot to be posted on this website. Check out our CHARACTER PORTRAITS page and see the gorgeous Nomads.
