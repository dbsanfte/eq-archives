A Fellowship of Friends...

Welcome to the official website of the Hooded Nomads...

Let me introduce myself and the guild, I'm Ynnos, bard, mage, berserker, and whatever the flavor of the day may be, at your service. The Hooded Nomads is a group of friends that have met both in RL (real life) and online via different RPG (role playing games), the latter being a common interest/passion among us.

We've started our merry band back in January 2001, on Verant's online RPG - EverQuest. Since then, we've also started up a chapter on Mythic's Dark Age of Camelot.

In addition, some of us also dabble in other online gaming interests, like RTS (real time strategy) games and the like. This site will be an index site that will link you to the respective websites that host those different guild chapters and games.

As there is nothing constant but change, this site will be continually evolving and changing. Please drop by for a visit sometime in the future.

Thank you for your time, and once again let me bid you... Welcome...!!!
