Below is the table outlining our current guild ranking system. Beside the rank name is the description of the rank along with the duties/responsibilities and guild abilities for that rank.

Also included after this table is the List of Officers dedicated to the support of the guild.

RANK NAME

RANK DESCRIPTION
Guildmaster Person who oversees the whole guild.
Guardian Officer. Deputy Guildmaster.
Council Officer. Focused on guild management, maintaining charter ideals and goals.
Protector Officer. Focused on guild events coordination (i.e. raids, tradeshows, etc). Usually a group leader during Alliance raids/events.
Warder Officer. Focused on maintaining membership and other member-oriented functions, i.e. recruit verification
Nomad Member. Dedicated member who embodies ideals of the guild.
Adventurer Member. Verified/confirmed recruits to the guild who regularly hunt with guild members and attend guild functions. Member of guild's email group and/or guild message board.
Traveler Recruit. Active recuits, still new to the guild and hunts regularly with members to get to know them, working on getting verified/confirmed.
Wayfarer Recruit. Non-active recruits, still new to the guild, BUT hunts regularly with members when available.
Wanderer Recruit. New recruits to the guild.
