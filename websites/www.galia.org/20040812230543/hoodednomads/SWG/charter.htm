PURPOSE

This is to document our missions, goals, and the code of conduct that we abide by. Let it be known that these aren't laws that are set in stone that we follow to the "T", but rather we treat them as guidelines by which we govern and organize ourselves.

 

MISSION STATEMENT

We are the Hooded Nomads. We travel the world, both near and far. We are a hardy bunch of friendly adventurers. Woe to those that fight against us. Peace to those who ally with us. From the depths of our hearts, our creed rings true...

..."MIGHT FOR RIGHT"...

 

FAQ

Why "Nomads"? - because we are travelers and adventurers...

Why "Hooded"? - because we like to remain nondescript... the "anonymous benefactor"... "a friend"...

Is this a "hardcore" or "powergaming" guild? - No, we started as a close group of friends that date back a long time and have been gaming with each other. Some of us know or have met each other in RL. We don't "frown" on powergamers, we have a few members that are hardcore, and are still dedicated to the guild. But we don't want to "crimp your style" and not live up to your expectations should powergaming be your cup of tea. For the most part, we're pretty laidback and relaxed.

Do you roleplay? - As much as possible. Our members come from a variety of backgrounds, some have been roleplaying for years, and others are new to roleplaying. We encourage our members to develop their characters and experience the fun in roleplaying. However, we would not discriminate against you if you don't wish to roleplay, but we ask that you respect people that do strive to stay in character and roleplay and not demean them or diminish their experience.

 

GOALS

Our Main Goal is to build friendships and enjoy the online roleplaying experience to the fullest capacity possible.

Another goal is to promote camaraderie and teamwork.

Last but not the least, we strive to help and support fellow guild mates and other peace-loving citizens of the galaxy.

 

MEMBERSHIP QUALIFICATION

Once you've heard and understand our charter, all we ask is that you accept and abide by it. We would often hunt or group with potential recruits for a bit, in order to determine if they would be a positive addition to the guild.

New Recruits

register in our forum
please post in the Wanderer's Lounge to request for membership
at least (3) members need to vouch for you by replying to your post
participate in forum discussion and in-game activities with guild members for at least (1) week

We also expect the following from our members:

communication - actively participate in discussions with members
support - actively join in guild functions, events, and goals
respect - be a role model to your fellow guild members and to others

 

OFFICERSHIP REQUIREMENTS

We only have two requirements:

  1. Must be sponsored by (3) existing guild officers
  2. Must demonstrate outstanding qualities reflecting the embodiment of our charter and our goals.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES AND CODE OF CONDUCT

  1. Guild Member
    Support and abide by guild charter and goals
    Actively participate in guild activities and events
    Seek out and encourage potential members

     

  2. Guild Officer
    Actively support and look out for the well being of the guild and its members
    Be a role model and mentor to fellow guild and realm mates
    Resolve disputes and promote harmony among guild and/or realm members
    Maintain open, consistent, and accessible communication with other officers and leader
    All member responsibilities

     

  3. Guild Leader
    Provide leadership and direction
    Maintain an updated roster of members
    All officer responsibilities
