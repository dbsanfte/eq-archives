Eastern Wastes - yes, the whole zone

I've been eyeing this place for a while now. There's a Ry' Gorr fort in the middle which those enviable armor drops at. Not to mention a couple of quests require some rare items from this zone, i.e. snow griffon eggs for the Coldain Prayer Shawl quest (which starts in Thurgadin - start with Frost Giant Toes or the Burlap shawl).

Also, a major part, if not all, of Garadain's Coldain Insignia Ring quest (start with Garadain's Blanket or the Copper ring) is in this zone. I'm still researching on the 9th or 10th level of this quest which has been said to start an epic battle between the giants and the dwarves.

Stonebrunt Mountains (on the isle of Odus) - <no date set - may become a semi-regular raid>, Pick-ups start 12:00 NOON - lvl range: 15 to 40

Description from Eqmaps - "The entrance to Stonebrunt Mountains can be found in the back of the Warrens, midway between the Huntmaster and Mighty Bear Paw rooms. The zone's population consists mostly of Kerran, gorillas, bunnies, snakes, pandas, Kobold, and predatory cats. For each type of animal, there is a "boss" mob around level 45."

Note - certain, if not all, BOSS mobs have faction hit with Kerrans. Killing "spirits" while a wandering Kerran is around will also have a faction hit.

Castle Mistmoore - (off Lesser Faydark zone)

Nearest bind point is Greater Faydark, you will need to pass through Lesser Faydark to the southwest corner to zone into Castle Mistmoore.

Be very careful of the BROWNIE SCOUTS in Lesser Faydark...!!!
