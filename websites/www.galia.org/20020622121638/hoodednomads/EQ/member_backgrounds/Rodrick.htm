Subject: I have an idea...... you should all be scared now

My friends,

This is the first that many of you have actually heard from me, and some
even heard OF me. The reason I speak up now is to bring before you an idea.
An idea that stumbled out of the deep, dark recesses of my mind. An idea
that came to me as I was singing and dancing my around Greater Faydark on a
clear, beautiful day. An idea that... oh, sorry. The bard in me was
starting to get the better of me again.

Now.. back to my idea. Everyone knows how Verant (the creators of EQ for
those of you who didn't know :-P ) has always said that they wanted this
to be a world created by the players. So far, this development by the
players has come in the form of ideas for spells, songs, items, trade
skills, area suggestions, etc. All seeming to be focused on the future of
Norrath. Well, I bring before you a way for the players to develop the
HISTORY of Norrath... a particularly bardic way, mind you, but a way that is
still open to ALL characters. Okay, okay. You want me to get on with
stating my idea.

So.. here it is. *handdrumroll* How about organizing a songwriting and
storytelling competition? It would be open for anyone to make submissions.
They could write anything they wanted (just so long as it pertained to
Norrath and was in good taste ie.. no sexual content, etc). For example...
stories about how the druid rings were built, or stories about some great
hero of the past, or cheesy love songs about star-crossed lovers that got
killed when a tree fell on them during a lover's tryst. The typical kind of
stories that different races and classes would tell and sing.

Now, I already understand that this is going to be time consuming reading
all the stuff that might be submitted, so judges would be in order.
Probably break the submissions into categories and have a couple of judges
per category. We would probably also have to have a message board of some
sort for people to make their submissions. Right now, this is still just in
the scheming phase, so there is still a lot of work that would have to be
done if I/we go forward with this.

As rewards, we could ask high level players to donate items for rewards.
Also, I was thinking about trying to ask Verant if they would be willing to
post the winning stories and songs on the main EQ website. Since this IS
going to help develop the game, I think they "might" actually be willing. I
also want to ask if they would be willing to provide some prizes so that
high level characters are also interested in submitting entries.

Okay.. now that I have thrown my idea out there, I am prepared to take any
constructive criticism, be it negative or positive. Remember, this is just
an idea. If this seems like a feasible, fun, and exciting idea, I am
looking for volunteers to help me. I was also thinking that we might want
to have it guild sponsored. It might be a good way to get our name out
there and maybe pick up some new members.

Okay... sorry for the long post, but I think the idea warrants at least that
much.

I leave you to your thoughts and response writing now.

*bows and, with a flaring of his cape, turns and walks off the stage*

Rodrick
Half-elf bard of the 15th song
