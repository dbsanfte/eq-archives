GOALS

Our Main Goal is to build friendships and enjoy the online roleplaying experience to the fullest capacity possible. Another goal is to promote camaraderie and teamwork. Last but not the least, we also strive to help and support fellow guild mates and other realm mates.

 

MEMBERSHIP QUALIFICATION

Once you've heard and understand our charter, all we ask is that you accept and abide by it. Also, we would often hunt or group with potential recruits for a bit, in order to determine if they would be a positive addition to the guild.

 

OFFICERSHIP REQUIREMENTS

We only have two requirements:

  1. Must be sponsored by (3) existing guild officers
  2. Must demonstrate outstanding qualities reflecting the embodiment of our charter and our goals.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

  1. Guild Member
    Support and abide by guild charter and goals
    Actively participate in guild activities and events
    Seek out and encourage potential members

     

  2. Guild Officer
    Actively support and look out for the well being of the guild and its members
    Be a role model and mentor to fellow guild and realm mates
    Resolve disputes and promote harmony among guild and/or realm members
    Maintain open, consistent, and accessible communication with other officers and leader
    All member responsibilities

     

  3. Guild Leader
    Provide leadership and direction
    Maintain an updated roster of members
    All officer responsibilities
