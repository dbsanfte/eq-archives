Below is the table outlining our current guild ranking system. Beside the rank name is the description of the rank along with the duties/responsibilities and guild abilities for that rank.

Also included after this table is the List of Officers dedicated to the support of the guild.

RANK NAME

RANK DESCRIPTION

Officer Ranks
High Council These are the people that have "GM" abilities and rights. They head up teams which are responsible for different areas of guild duties. Below, you will see the areas of responsibility, or "teams", that members of the High Council have been assigned to.
Guardian Officer. Deputy Guildmaster.
Council Officer. These people have been chosen by their appropriate High Council and will serve as the "assistant" or 2nd in command for that specific team. There will usually be only 1 or 2 people in this rank per team.
Protector Officer. These are the veteran officers. They will be part of a specific team that is in charge of an area of responsibility. This will be the main body of officers.
Warder Officer. these are the newly inducted officers. As with all the officers, these individuals have stepped up to the plate and have committed to helping out the guild by shouldering the duties and responsibilities which make our guild run smoothly. Note: this rank will also contain officer alts. 

Member Ranks
Nomad Member. Dedicated member who embodies ideals of the guild.
Adventurer Member. Verified/confirmed recruits to the guild who regularly hunt with guild members and attend guild functions. Member of guild's email group and/or guild message board, has Alliance chat access.
Traveler Member. Active members, still new to the guild and hunts regularly with members to get to know them, main char. is still under lvl 20, restricted to Guild chat only.
Wayfarer Recruit. Active recruits, still new to the guild, BUT hunts regularly with members when available, working on getting verified/confirmed.
Wanderer Recruit. New recruits to the guild.
