Below is an excerpt from our charter regarding membership qualification:

MEMBERSHIP QUALIFICATION

Once you've heard and understand our charter, all we ask is that you accept and abide by it. Also, we would often hunt or group with potential recruits for a bit, in order to determine if they would be a positive addition to the guild.

You can contact any of our members in game to express your desire to join our guild. Being a new recruit, you will start off in the rank of "Wanderer". As you get more active with the guild, and verified, you will progress through the ranks.

To verify your membership, please do the following:

Join the guild's YahooGroups Email List
This is what we currently use to send out announcements and maintain communication amongst members


Click to subscribe to HoodedNomads_DAoC

 

After your subscription gets approved, you should get a confirmation and a welcome message from us. Please write a short note to the group (HoodedNomads_DAoC@yahoogroups.com) and tell us who you are so we can get to know you better and /roleplay a bit more if you like.

That's it!!

Hope to see you soon in our merry band...
