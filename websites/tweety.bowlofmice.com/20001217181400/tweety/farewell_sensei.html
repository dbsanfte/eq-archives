THE FAT LADY SANG AND BROKE A WINDOW

The link to The Dojo goes nowhere, because The Sensei, aka Chai, closed his site down. For those of you who never played a monk, this was THE site for anyone who either played a monk, or wanted EQ news from someone with the cajones to tell it like it is.

Some of y'all started reading my hissy fits from the EMarr message board, where you may have seen a reference to Gerome (now The Asshole Formerly Known As Gerome, since he sold his account and put a down payment on a new house) being the inspiration for Tweety. This is true, because Gerome the Peeping Gnome (yeah, yeah, he was an Erudite, but that doesn't rhyme, shut up) had a special way with flames and rants. He's one of two people that could make me laugh so hard I wet myself.

The other was Chai.

Full disclosure: I am in the same player guild as Chai. I use the present tense, because the Tarsis Shriners are not an EQ guild, they're a group of friends who have been playing games together since the text MUDD Arctic. In order to join, you need to be a real life friend of one of the members. Therefore, once a Shriner, always a Shriner. Look for us to be delivering a smackdown in The Next Big Thing.

A fair number of people discovered this sucky ass site through a link from The Dojo. If you find me half as funny as you found him, you're giving me an enormous compliment.

In his farewell message, he pointed out that "The Sensei" was not the full description of the man writing the rants. I can't say I know him any better in real life than most of you, jeez, the guy lives practically on the other coast from here. I'll tell you this, though - the guy was one of the first monks ever to level to 50, he could run a raid like a god, he gave advice to junior monks that made the difference between a good night and a bad one, and he really is as funny as his rants.

The Dojo, and the psychotic personality behind it, will be missed. Sayonara, Sensei.
