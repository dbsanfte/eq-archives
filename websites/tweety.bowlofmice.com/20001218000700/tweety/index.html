WEEKLY UPDATE - 12/14/00

I'm touched. I really am. It seems your favorite angst-ridden bird has made the new issue of Computer Games magazine. Sniff. Of course, the author apparently didn't know I had a website, didn't know that while letting of steam appeals to her more than lawsuits it still got my player account banned, and finally, didn't know I was female. I, being the helpful citizen I am, called her and informed her voice mail of my gender. I do hope she gets pointed to my site someday, because I really, really want the screenshot that went with her article. It's a dragon, flaming the crap out of a little fighter. What the hell is it with me that make outsiders assume I'm male? It must be my charm and demure demeanor.

Wow, a whole lot of you have kids. Yowsers. The email bag was loaded with parents writing in response to the most recent hissy fit. I hereby offer up a prayer to the small god of rational people that reads, "Thank you, small god, for encouraging smart people to breed." I cannot express how happy I am that some of you (gasp) monitor your kids' exposure to gaming and television.

No rant today, love muffins, I'm working on something for my other gig (writing for the Corporation, duh, go drive up the hit counter). Catch you tomorrow, I promise you I'll have something pissing me off.
