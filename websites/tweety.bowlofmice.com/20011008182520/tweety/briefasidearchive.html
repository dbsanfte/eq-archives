Who Is The Brief Aside Guy?

No, it isn't Tweety. The Brief Aside guy is six feet tall, a professional artist, male, and a Republican. You cannot GET more different than Tweety.

His name is Alyxz. He would be considered an avid gamer if his attention span was long enough. We tried to start a religion once in our undergraduate days. It didn't work because neither of us had the money for the post office box we needed. Also, we ran out of beer.

Now he's that friend everyone has in their life, that guy who you don't see or hear from in a year or more, and suddenly he shows up with large amounts of alcohol and funny stories, and like a complete idiot you find yourself drinking heavily and saying things like "Whoa, dude, I never thought of that." In the morning he's gone, you're got a headache, and he left his extra booze in your fridge. It's usually good beer, too.
