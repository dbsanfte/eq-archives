WEEKLY UPDATE - 11/28/01

For those of you who like to keep track of such things, the world ended a few weeks back at 10:30 PM on November 15. How do I know?

I, Tweety, foul mouthed ranter from hell, bane of all that is polite and good in the world, champion of the sardonic and bitter cubicle dweller, and all-around puppy kicker, essentially told the entire Vault board to shut up until they could be polite to each other.

I didn't turn into (insert Other Game Company Rep's Name here) as so many feared, I turned into my freaking mother.

In my world, we spell that I-R-O-N-Y.

The last two weeks have been a test of my self-control that I never want to experience again. Between the foil hat wearing nimrods of the internet and people making half-assed attempts at eliciting a Tweety Rant TM on the Vault, I was about ready to set a plague on both their houses, until I took a step back and realized - this is really and truly hilariously funny.

So anyway, my rants lately have been unprintable if I wish to remain employed. I hope to have a new one up soon. This fast and furious update was mostly to let the three of you left know that the bookmark will change to simply http://tweety.bowlofmice.com. My fabulous host is moving the site to an in-house server, so there may be delays and temporary outages.

Remember, you're always free to email me with questions and comments.
