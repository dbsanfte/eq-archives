According to America Online health news today, "the world is rapidly growing fat and out of shape," experts say.

In what could be the funniest commentary on the human condition I've seen since we all read about the blue dress that never made it to the dry cleaners, experts are saying that the number of overfed people has come to equal the number of underfed people and that soon the time will come when the majority of the earth's population will be too fat.

The article does not mention what standard is being used to define the phrase "too fat," nor does it specify what task these people are too fat to achieve...it merely poses the possibility of too many fat people on the planet in generalized terms.

Word is not yet in as to whether the mere fact that someone wasted time and energy on such a moronic point is sad, or just funny.

(We at Brief Asides™ feel that wasting the space on writing about someone else's waste of space is BOTH sad AND funny.)
