The Brief Aside

Reborn! New and improved!

After getting Tweety drunk enough to believe we are not a complete moron last night, she agreed to revive the Brief Asides™ here. I thought about opening with a small history of the column, as it has had a three year lifespan on various other sites of note that you most likely don't care about. Then I decided that it just wouldn't be in keeping with my long time dedication to the short attention span format the asides have always had.

So, for the sake of argument, lets just say it doesn't matter where the asides came from, they are here now, for your pleasure/annoyance.

I will be attempting to send them weekly (weakly?).

Enjoy.

By now, some of you may be wondering whether or not the brief asides are written by nice guys. Are we the sort to tip sacred cows? Are we merely the apathetic anonymous trying to cash in on the MTV soaked generation X penchant for anything and everything remotely humorous yet bite sized enough not to cause any truly major thought?

To be honest, we have been known to romp through the sacred fields pushing a cow or two over, for no better reason than to hear what a sacred "Moo" might sound like. Can you say you're not curious? Have we pushed the analogy too far yet?

Doesn't truly matter since we're going to lose half our audience if we dwell on this for longer than three paragraphs so we'll just leave it as is. Our own cows are still hung over anyway and unable to "moo" properly even if someone does manage to tip them today.
