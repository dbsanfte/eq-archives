  To become a member of the Cubs of Norrath, there are a couple things to consider
  • The Cubs are a 'newbie' guild.  That means that no member of the Cubs of Norrath can be over level 20.
  • There are no class or race restrictions.
  • Potential members must be nominated by an existing member.
  • You must be 'interviewed' by a guild officer.
