The Royale Knights of EverQuest

Sub-Charter

NeverWinter, the second age. A time of warfare and strife where the strong take what they want and the weak stay close to the protection of their homes for fear of being molested. Until Now... Out of the ashes of warfare and strife has arisen a new order of Knighthood. An order dedicated to protecting those who cannot protect themselves. While helping those who would travel the dangerous paths of NeverWinter. This order would be known as the Royale Knights of NeverWinter. In time all the inhabitants of NeverWinter were driven from there home by an Evil and powerful Orcish army. Their only hope of survival was to find new homes and spread the knowledge and love that they had found as Royale Knights. Hoping that someday they could return to their home they so dearly loved, NeverWinter.

 

Statement of Purpose

The main purpose of the Royale Knights is to establish a guild made up of members who are willing to form a Brotherhood and Sisterhood beyond that of the friendships of others in the online Role Playing Games. Our intent is to put fellow guild members above all others, while still remaining helpful to those in need. The purpose of this Brotherhood/Sisterhood is to fight the evil guilds that run amuck in the online RPG's and to show that evil can be fought while still maintaining Honor and Dignity. We are a guild that takes our loses with Dignity and our wins with Honor while still protecting those who have not acquired the knowledge that we posses as Royale Knights. Our goal is to share our knowledge with all those willing to abide by our rules and to take the steps we deem necessary to train one to become a Knight.

Guild Guidlines

 

  • You must be of good or neutral alignment.
  • You must maintain dignity at all times.
  • You must swear to protect the innocent.
  • You must hold honor above all else.
  • You must follow all guild guidelines.
  • You are allowed only 2 characters in the guild to have guild tags. (see guildlines below)
  • You must send a letter to the Scribe for roll call as stated in the requirements of membership.

 

Conduct Unbecoming of a Royale Knight

Kill stealing, and ninja looting are strictly prohibited.
 Refusing to help a new member is unacceptable.
 Everyone wants to have good magical items, but being Greedy is not necessary.
 Harassing a new player (otherwise known as a newbie) is strictly prohibited.
Consorting with other guilds in a manner that would be unacceptable to the guild.
 Doing anything with the intention of causing trouble, destroying or splitting up the guild is unacceptable.
 Any violation that a game guide brings to the attention of the guild.
The Royale Council may add additional rules, stated in the sub-charter of a specific game.

Any violation of the above rules is subject to disiplinary action by the Royale Council.

 

Rank and Requirments for Rank

Neophyte Royale - {A recent convert, A beginner} This is the first rank available to a new member of the Royale Knights.

Adept Royale - {A member that is in study of our Beliefs}

Acolyte Royale - {A Follower of our beliefs, and student of our History}

Guardian Royale - {A protector and teacher of our History and our Faith}

Squire Royale - {The beginning of the path that leads to Knighthood}

Knight Royale - {A proven Champion, Protector, and Zealot of our Principal Beliefs} A member with this rank has achieved full status in the Royale Knights. This member has shown that they are worthy of the title of Knight, and upholds the principals of Honor, Dignity, Sacrifice, and Kindness, that which the guild is built upon.

First Sargeant- This is the title given to the assistants to the captains.

Knight Royale Captain - This person will hold the positions of Preceptor, Scribe, Requisitioner, or Quest master.

Knight Royale Lt. Commander (Assistant GM) - This is the second highest rank bestowed upon a Knight in EverQuest. There will be two(2) Assistant Game Master's (AGM). This rank is next in command after the Game Master (GM). EverQuest AGM will be the official abbreviation for this rank.

Knight Royale Commander (Game Master)(GM) - This person is the leader of the guild in EverQuest. This is the highest rank bestowed upon an individual in the EverQuest charter guild. EverQuest GM will be the official abbreviation for this rank.

 

Questing for Rank

 

In order to gain rank you must meet the following criteria for each rank, and have completed the requirments for the previous rank.

Adept- you must be at least level 5, and you must donate 20gp's to the guild fund.

Acolyte- you must be at least level 10 and you must donate 40gp's to the guild fund

Gaurdian- you must have completed the requirements for the previous ranks, AND have recruited one person into the guild.

Squire- you must be at least level 15, and you must donate 75 gp's to the guild fund. You must have also completed the requirments for Gaurdian.

Knight- you must be at least level 20, and you must donate 150gp's to the guild fund.

You must complete all the requirements for the rank previous before you can quest for the next one.

When you have met the level requirments for each rank, you may then quest and recieve that rank. For example, when you reach level 5, you are elegible to quest for the rank of Adept. Each week the guild will have a Quest Night. The Quest Master will post a guild wide quest. As long as you meet the requirments for that rank you can attend and gain rank. For the rank of Guardian the only requirment is to recruit one person into the guild.

When you are ready to quest for the rank of Knight, you must notify the Quest master of your intent. He will assign you a quest to complete. You have two weeks to accomplish the quest. You cannot gain the rank of Knight by attending the weekly quest night. You must have at least one Officer, and one non-officer attend your Knights quest.

 

Titles

 

Once a member has reached the rank of Knight Royale, he may then have a title bestowed upon him by the Royale Council. The title can be placed in the surname after the rank of Knight Royale. All titles will be abbreviated to fit the games tag restrictions. The following are a list of guild titles.

Founding Knight - These members founded the guild under the original charter of the Knights of NeverWinter. This title shall be bestowed upon those knights only, and never upon any other member.

Charter Knight - These are the Knights from NeverWinter, that helped to restart the guild after the destruction of NeverWinter. This title shall be bestowed upon those knights only, and never upon any other member.

EverQuest Charter Member - These are the original members that started the Royale Knight charter in EverQuest. This title is limited to the first 10 members of the EverQuest Royale Knights guild.

Preceptor-General- This person will be in charge of all new members. They will be given the authority to give out tags. They will make sure each member has read and understands the charter. They will also do their best to give any help a new member may need. They will compose a list of all new members that enter the guild in a two week period And send the list to the GM. This person will also hold the Rank of Knight Captain. The preceptor will also be able to appoint one person to help him in giving out tags. This person will hold the rank of First Sargeant. Each person will be allowed one character in each starting town. This character must stay level one and cannot be played. When picking this second person, care should be given to consider a person that is on opposite to you. So if you are on in the day time, then the other person should be on at night. The Preceptor-General will be responsible for holding an event once a month. This event should be geared towards helping new members.

Preceptor-Knight- This person will be in charge of instructing the guild on how to best serve the guild, and a group, as a tank. They will develop tactics and procedures to help everyone in the guild become better tanks. They will be responsible for holding a training session once a month.

Preceptor-Bishop- This person will bein charge of instructing the guild on how to best serve the the guild, and a group, as a healer/buffer. They will develop tactics and procedures to help everyone in the guild use there healing and buffing tactics. They will be responsible for holding a training session once a month.

Preceptor-Magus- This person will be in charge of instructing theguildon how to best serve the guild, and a group as a caster. They will develop tactics, and procedures to effectivly uses magic in a group. They will be responsible for holding a training session once a month.

Requisitioner - The function of this character is the storage of guild items as well as reporting all items that have been donated and withdrawn from the vaults to the GM as well as the general assembly. This person will hold the rank of Knight Captain. Also the Requistioner will be allowed to have one person to help him in his duties. This person will hold the rank of First Sargeant. Each person will be allowed to have extra character to keep stuff in as required. The names will be RK Vault followed by a number or letter if the game allows.(I.e., RK Vault1, RK Vault A, etc....) This character is not to be played. The Requisitioner will be responsible for holding an item drive once a month.

Scribe - This title will be given to the individual who is willing to take care of the official list of members. This list shall include the rank of all members, the day they entered (if known), the day they left, the day they made the rank of Knight. All requirements for advancement would also be noted here. This position will require some work, so the scribe should be someone prepared to take this responsibility seriously. The scribe will also be allowed one helper. This person will hodl the rank of First Sargeant. The Scribe will be responsible for having a roll call once a month.

Quest Master - This person will be in charge of assinging quests. He must post a quest each week for Quest Night. He must also assign quest to those people who are attempting to attain knighthood. Also he will assign quests for people who request something from the guild vault.

White Knight Royale - Every six months the whole guild will vote on the member who has done an outstanding job or service for the guild. This title will be bestowed on the winner of that award and his name be scribed upon the Honor Rolls.

 

Guild Vault

The guild keeps a vault so that members can upgrade their equipment as items are donated to the guild. We keep this active for the use of all guild members. We ask that when you get something from the vault you do not sell it. When you are finished with the item you should either give it back to the vault or to another guild member. If you sell an item for personal gain, you will never be allowed to take an item from the vault again.

The Requisitioner is in charge of the guild vault. When he gets an item in he will post it so that everyone may see what the guild has. When you see an item you would like to have, notify the Requisitioner. If the item is worth over 100pp he will have the Quest Master assign you a quest to complete. Once you have completed the quest you will be given the item. The Requistioner will give a higher level player first dibs on any item. If they choose not to complete the quest, then it will go to the next highest level player.

Please remember the vault is here for everyone to enjoy, do not abuse the privalege.
