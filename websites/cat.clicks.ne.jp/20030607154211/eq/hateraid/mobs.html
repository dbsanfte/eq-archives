Cleric of Innoruuk

Cleric - (Level 49-51 - 10,350 HPs)

Aggro範囲は普通で、ダークエルフ・ガードのような格好で、魔法抵抗がかなり高く、かなり寝にくい。その上、Complete Healingをこよなく愛し、彼自身または周囲のMobに掛けまくるので注意が必要です。Stun,Bash,Slamを駆使し、Stun状態でキープしながら殺すべし。また、DeathPactを含むCLR-Buffを周囲のMobに掛けるのが趣味です。 They look like a Dark Elf guard. They have high magic resist, so harder to mez, and they love to complete heal themselves or other mobs in the area.  Keep them stunned (bash/slam/stun) while fighting and kill them fast. They will buff other mobs with cleric buffs including Death Pact. They have a normal aggro range. Also drop Prismatic Shield (Cleric only)
Ire Ghast
Undead Shadow Knight - (Level 49-51 - 10,250 HPs)

Aggro範囲は狭い。魔法抵抗は低く、とても寝やすい。HarmTouchを好んで使う。
They look like an oversized ghoul and have low magic resist, so easy to mez, but love to HarmTouch, and do it a lot. Undead magic works on them. They do not see rogues, but do see normal player invisible (although invis to undead would work). They have a small aggro range.
Revultant Rat
Undead Warrior - (Level 49 - 10,250 HPs)

魔法抵抗は低いが、Proc 'Deadly Poison'を持っている。CLR/NECのLv60スペルBanishment of ShadowsでDeathTouchできるらしいｗ
They look like a giant diseased rat and have low magic resist, but proc Deadly Poison. They do not see rogues, but may or may not see normal invis. They have a small aggro range. Can be DTed with Cleric/Necro spell Banishment of Shadows.
Haunted Chest
Warrior - (Level 48-52 - 14,400 HPs)

Aggro範囲及び他のMobに対するLink範囲も狭い。固定Popで、'Nullify Magic'をProcで持っているので、Buffが剥がされる事あり。動きが遅い為、Pullの際に離れすぎると帰ってしまうので注意
They look like a bog box with teeth. They will proc a Nullify Magic, so they will strip your buffs. They do not see a rogue, but do see others. They have an extremely small aggro range and are very slow. When pulling, the puller has to stay close, or the box will forget and go home. They even aggro other mobs at a very short range. Boxes spawn only in fixed locations.
Loathing Lich
Undead Wizard - (Level 51 - 14,4000 HPs)

Aggro範囲は狭いが他のMobに対してのLink範囲はかなり広い。魔法抵抗はかなり低いが、HPは多く、スパムDS(2dmg)を使用する。CLR/NECのLv60スペルBanishment of ShadowsでDeathTouchできるらしい
They look like a spectre and have very low magic resist but more hp than most mobs in Hate, plus are undead so undead magic works well, so usually easy kills. But they will cast the wizard damage shield (the annoying 2 damage spam shield). They do not see a rogue. They have a small self-aggro range, but will pass chain-aggro other mobs a very large range. Can be DTed with Cleric/Necro spell Banishment of Shadows.
Abhorrent
Rogue - (Level 49 - 10,250 HPs)

Aggro範囲はかなり狭く魔法抵抗はかなり低いが、BackStabを好んで使うのでPullerは背中に注意
They look like gargoyles and have very low magic resist, but love to backstab, so make sure the puller is careful pulling them. They do not see rogues or normal invisible. They have a small aggro range. Also drops the Shrieking Ahlspiess (Rouge Weapon).
Kiraikuei
Undead Monk - (Level 48-52 - 11,250 HPs)

Aggro範囲はかなり広い、但し視線Aggroは狭い模様。魔法抵抗は低目だが、拳にProc 'Strong Disease'を持っている。
They look like a mummy and have low magic resist. They will proc Strong Disease. They are undead, so undead magic works well on them. They do not see rogues, but do see normal invis, though invis to undead would work. They have a very large aggro range, but a small pass-on range, so they will aggro with anything close, but not aggro the mobs around them... usually. They also drop monk throwing weapons, Shuriken of the Tranquil.
Male Forsaken Revanant
Undead Magician - (Level 49 - 10,250 HPs)

魔法抵抗は普通で、DSとAE-DDを使う。
They look like male vampires and have normal magic resistance. They also cast a mean damage shield and nasty AE direct damage. Undead magic does work. They drop mage gear. They do not see rogues, but do see normal invis. They have normal aggro range.
Female Forsaken Revanant
Undead Enchanter - (Level 49-51 - 10,250 HPs)

魔法抵抗が高い、またCharmは使用しないがDSを使用する。Maleと違いかなり痛い思いをするので注意
They look like female vampires and have high magic resistance, but they do not cast charm. They do cast a damage shield that must be canceled. Undead magic works. They do not see rogues but do see normal invis. They have normal aggro range.
Scorn Banshee
Undead Warrior - (Level 49-51 - 14,500 HPs)

Aggro範囲は狭いが、魔法抵抗はそこそこある模様だが、拳にProc 'Strong Disease'を持っている。
They look like a female zombie and have low magic resist, but proc Strong Disease and hit a ton. But undead magic works. They DO see rogues and all invis, including invis to undead, regardless of what con says. They do have a small aggro range, though. Also drops the Martune Rapier for Bards.
Ashenbone Drake
Undead Necromancer - (Level 51 - 15,750 HPs)

Aggro範囲はとても広い、視界を横切っただけで反応するらしい。魔法抵抗はかなり高く、Fearを好んで使用する。
Looks like a skeletal dragon or a small DracoLiche from Fear. They have high magic resist and love to cast Fear. They do not see rogues, but do see normal invis. They have an insane aggro range and have been known to aggro stuff across the zone. Be extremely careful on this one's aggro.
Spite Golem
Shaman - (Level 51 - 16,100 HPs)

Aggro範囲は広く、魔法抵抗に関しては正気ではない高さを誇る。毒、病気、WizのLureがなんとか入るかも知れない。絶対寝ない。'SoW'と'Alacrity'を使用する
They look like a rock golem. They have insane magic resist (although poison, disease, and the wizard Lure spells will usually stick). This is the most important target most times. If you get 2, one warrior, usually the backup assist, will keep second one busy while the group kills number 1. It is possible to kill several, but very, very tough, so be careful. They love to cast SoW and other Alacrity on everything close to them. They do not see a rogue, but see everything else. They have a large aggro range. Also drops Barbarian Shaman and Druid Shields.
Maestro of Rancor
Bard - (Level 53 - 32,000 HPs)

Melee範囲のAE Lifetapを使用するので、Casterから離れた所で戦闘すること。また、両手に武器を持っている場合は攻撃がとても速いので注意。通常２つのアイテムを落とす、大抵はBRD用だが、MNK用やPAL用盾(ERU only)を落とす事もある。また死にそうになるとオルガンホールの入り口に向かって逃げ始める。
He hits a TON and has an AOE lifetap on everything in melee range. And if he has dual weapons, he hits extremely fast. While fighting him, you must keep him pended in and away from the casters. He normally drops 2 items, mostly Bard stuff, though a few monk items and a nice dropable shield. He sees rogues and all normal invis, but his back is usually to the door so no problem. He is the lesser boss of Hate. He will run near the end of the fight and will head toward the door of the Organ Hall.

Innoruuk
The God of Hate - (Level 65 - 300,000 HPs)

Hateの大御所で、DeathTouchを使用する。Gravity Fluxを使うので戦う際は壁を背にして下さい。素晴らしい性能のアイテムを沢山落とします。
He does not DeathTouch anymore, but loves to cast gravity flux. When fighting him, always keep your back to a wall. He has a long list of drops, and most of it nice. He will run at near the end of the fight.
