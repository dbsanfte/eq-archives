Barbarian Shaman Levels 5 - 20
by Keltek the Barbar - Fennin Ro Server/Nameless Server


5-6:
Kill gob scouts at the Caster igloos (sw corner of EF). If you're having trouble at 5th doing this, then grab a friend (a warrior prefferably in case the caster beads for your gavel ever drop....warriors have no use for em so won't fight you for em)

7:
Group up and take out Polar bears near the 1 Guard (Brendyl). Keep a Polar Bear Skin or two on you in case you ever see Karg for your cloak.

8,9:
Solo or group the polar bears. Fight in the BlackBurrow entrance (stay outside the BB zone for now) and if you need help run to Arnis or the BB zoneline.

10,11:
Get a group for Mammoth Calves and farm on the edge of the tundra in front of the 1 guard spot.

12:
Hit the Wisps on Eruds Crossing. Be sure you have a magic weapon for em, cause you can't schmackem without one =)

13:
Wisps still prefferably, but Guards in BB will also do.

14,15:
Do the BF quest if you haven't gotten a magic weapon yet and get the Testament of Vernear quest started in Qeynos (you just got Bind, use it often!). Hunt Wisps solo or the Elite Room grouped (called the green rm). Excellent loot either way. Might want to head to Highpass Hold at 15th. (on your way to HH give your greater lightstones (one at a time) from the Wisps to the Gypsy woman whose name starts with an "M" (make sure you have an open inventory slot every time you give a GLS) in the Gypsy camp in N Karana. She'll give you a research book which you can sell for 8-9pp)

16:
Head to HighpassHold and the Gnolls (Get a map!) if solo. HH Orcs if in a full group (you'll do better in a full group with the Orcs). Camp Dyllan for the Testament page or if you see an SK (or is it a Palydin quest?)camping him for the letter you can ask to loot the page when they get him.

17:
Hunt E Karana solo or grouped. Pull reavers (not undead yet), Gorge hounds, Dark Stalkers, and Highland Lions/Lionesses to the very edge of the river.

18:
Same as 17th or if in Everfrost-Orc Shamen solo or Mammoth Mammas in a FULL group. Also Orcs in HH (grouped) or Gnolls solo.

19:
Undead Reavers, Gorge Hounds, Dark Stalkers in EK if solo. Orcs in a group in HH or Gnolls solo.

20:
I went to Dagnors Cauldron but had a hella time solo hunting Bosuns and Casters near the NPC camp. If you get someone else to group with you you'll get more than decent experience.

Hope this helps you guys, and good hunting!
