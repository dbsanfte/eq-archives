Shaman Player Guides

Troll Shaman Levels 5-9
by Dawnrose

Well me got to level nin at last. It took ages cos I was stoopid. So me gonna tell you how not to be stoopid lik Dawnrose. First me tell you wots you want to do first.

Spells
So you now level fiv. You can get loads of gud spells! Now me understand you not rich so me tell you wot you shud buy. Well you can get da feet like cat. Dats a gud buff spell that helps you bashing and makes udder trolls lik you a lot. Dis you shud buy. You shud also get frost rift. It much better spell dan burst of fire. Drowsey is ok me tink. It maks tings bash slower so you cast it on dem froggies an dey cant hit you as often. Fleeting fury is no gud if you lik bashig tings on your own. But wen you wit udders it gud to cast dis on da bestest basher in da group. It makes dem into a death machine! But it only last 20 seconds. A great spell is gate. It brings you back to where you were last bound. Me liks dis cos I could go running around den jus cast one spell an be back in da swamp. It becomes really gud when you get bind affinity but dats not for ages.

Don't buy scale skin. Its stoopid an you need snake skins to mak it work. You don't need edure fire and spirit pounch yet so if you got no money den leave dem for later. Summon drink ok but me a lazy troll an milk is so cheep me jus buy dem. Now all dem spells can be bought in Grobb but udder gud spell is sicken. For dat you need to find a troll that is standing on a big hand in da swamp. Now you gotta be careful cos der are two hands in da swamp. On one is da troll dat sells loads of shaman spells but the udder has shadowmen. Dey very bad mojo. Dem da worst tings in da swamp an dey lik bashin silly trolls that run up an ask to buy spells.

Sicken is a gud spell dat can mak tings sick. It's a DOT wich means it hurts dem over an over again till it wears out or dey fall down ded. Wot to fite Okie you redy now. At level fiv an six you want to hunt da froggies an da weaker froggy fishermen. Also dem walking mushroons are gud for experience. If you are wit som udder trolls you can kill da tougher fishermen an the two froggy guards outside guk. On your own you shud wait till level seven before kiling the tougher fishermen an guards. Now me only found dis wen I was nearly at level nin but it would be really gud for level seven and eite. You goes to the Ferriot. Dats de Ogre newbie zone. In one cornor der are loads of skeleton camps. Dey great! No nasty spells an dey don't every run (me hates da froggies dat always be running away, me dont lik fast food). Gud fast spawn rate means you shud shot up to level eite. Dis is greet level. It greet cos you get meditaion! Loads of mana now an da skill shots up real fast.

 *** Remember da gloves?***
Remember wen I said to keep all dem majik gloves and to buy any you see on da merchants? Well now is da time to make sum reel money. You head to the Northern desert of Ro an you auction dem for 4pp. Just auction dem as majik gloaves dat let you bash magik tings. You get loads of monks an hummies dat want dem. Me sold six of dem da first time I did dis. Now da clever bit dat I forgot wen I did dis. Der is anudder spell called flash of lite. No troll or ogre sells it but da clever hummie clerics can use it an dey sell it in der big cit. But we cant get der cos hummie guards kill trolls just cos we eats hummies. So wots you do is you asks a nice hummie to buy it for you (dont eat da hummie as dis can ruin da deal). Dey get da spell an gives it to you. Now you can eats dem if you want.

Level Nin
You gud and strong now.
