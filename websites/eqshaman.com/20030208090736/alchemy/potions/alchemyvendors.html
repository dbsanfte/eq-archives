Old Word Vendors

Halas: (All Herbs)

Vendor: "Dargon"(Herbs for Stat, Aura and Blood of Wolf potions)

"So, young Barbarian, ye wish to taste my Herbs?"

Vendor: "Spirita" (Herbs for Utility potions incl Sandlewood)

Location: Six-feet to the right of Dargon
