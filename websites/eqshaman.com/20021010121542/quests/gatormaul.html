Gatorsmash Maul Quest
by The Mighty Thantu of Mithaniel Marr

updates

Hope this helps all those interested in getting themselves a maul. First of all let me state that the maul was gotten by a high elf wizard who is agnostic and owns a guise of the deceiver (good luck recreating that unbelievable luck out), however, I believe this quest to be doable in other ways if you are willing to take the time. Namely, either have a wizard or druid gate around your newbie ogre warrior to get Dvinn's heart, or, try it invis (don't see why it won't work long as you don't right click on anything).
Hoakay, let's get down to details:

1. Go to crushbone, blast the crap out of Dvinn and get his heart (easy enough). Please be sure that the room is empty or if it is camped offer them a dragoon dirk to loot his heart (you'll get another, no loss here). Ogres and Trolls will have to have someone pull Dvinn from the tower of levitate to it (as Verant seems to not like fat people , crackmonkies).

2. Catch a ride or foot it to Oggok (pretty safe these days, guards are camped 24/7).

3. GET A CRUDE STEIN ( so easy to do, guard killers leave them all over the place). This step is crucial, you must have the stein in hand to do the quest.

4. The third bottom hole of five (the one on the right) leads through the shaman guild, stick right until you enter a room with maybe 6 hallways leading off from it. You want to go down the one in the middle, should be 3rd from the right I think (there will be a bed in the room).

5. If you happened upon the room at nighttime, have a sit big guy (start sweating if you are invis, heh). At 8am, the Bouncer in the room should unspawn, and at 10 am (like 5 min later), Ambassador K'Rynn should spawn.

6. Nuke K'Rynn (do not let him run!). He has a heart and a Goon Dirk every time.

7. Put your crude stein in hand and head to the warrior guild. Give both hearts to Lork (we started as dubious and moved to amiable, hehe, little bit of xp too, which makes up for all the time spent getting those mauls, rofl).

8. Admire new Gatorsmash Maul (worked every single time for god knows how many times now, this would be a good time to run like hell you invis people).
Hope this helps you all get your own maul , if not, feel free to look us up to purchase one on Mith Marr, be glad to get you one. Good luck and Godspeed brothers and sisters.

Give me Gatorsmash or give me death!
Oh, one last thing, don't get one unless you have quickness at 29, or plan to bank it until then, and the damage is capped until 30, then again till 40, then one more at 50, happy hunting.
