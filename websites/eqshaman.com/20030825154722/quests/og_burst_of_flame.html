Burst of Flame Quest - Oggok

The Oggok Burst of Flame spell quest is obtained from Zulort in the Shaman Guild area.  The items required are four (4) froglok tadpole fleshes.  If you give Zulort the four froglok tadpole fleshes he will give you the Burst of Flame spell and some faction.  Be careful to give the 4 fleshes to Zulort in this way:  take one of the fleshes and give it to Zulort.  A window with four slots will come up showing the one froglok flesh you gave to Zulort.  One at a time, place the other 3 froglok fleshes into the slots, one flesh per slot.  When you have done this all four slots should have one flesh only.  Click on Done or Give (whatever the button says).  BAM!! You've got the spell and some faction.  I have done this quest about 4 times and I always get the same spell, so don't waste your time trying to get something other than the Burst of Flame spell.
