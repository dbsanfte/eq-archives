Alchemy Recipes


I am working on confirming all of the old alchemy recipes personally. As for the trivial, that is up to you guys to confirm. Email me with updated information at j.nesta@shaw.ca!

  • Note: Trivial means the skill level at which you cannot increase your skill any longer from that particular recipe. The trivial can be used as a difficulty rating for the potion. For more information, view the "Starting Alchemy" section.
  • Note: The duration for all potions is approximate and you may experience a decreased duration if you are under level 25.
  • Note: All potions stack with all other potions/spells except for Rejuvenation, which does stack with any potion that effects strength and/or attack speed.
