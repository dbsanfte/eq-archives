Why make a Barb Shaman?

Well I read the Why to make a Troll Shaman and they have a lot of good points but I think Barbs have some great points too. I decided to make this in a pro/con format.

Pros
The biggest pro would have to be travel. We are welcomed in all port cities and all others except the 3 dark cities. This can be a great benefit while traveling Norrath.
Racism - The other races in Norrath might think twice before allowing a Troll or Ogre in their group. The DO eat most of them if you havent noticed! Whereas a Barb is just like their big brother ;)
Halas has a great newbie area followed by outer Everfrost or BlackBurrow.

Cons
We dont see well. We do get an infravision spell though
Halas is not that easy to get to when you are ready to by a new set of spells
