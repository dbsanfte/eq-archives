Totemic/Dufrenite Armor:

Notes: Everyone can do the quest (Barbarian, Troll, Ogre). The crushed, ground, powdered gems are obtained by giving one good gem to the Ogres across the lake (talk to them first).Also the Banded Pieces must be MEDIUM only.

The Ogres are also located in Lake Rathe, right before you get to the Docks at the Mtn. Rathe Zone. Look for a grassy cliff face and climb up, there tucked back outta sight is the Ogre Shaman Camp. They /Con indifferent to all races. 
Watch out when you are going to get your Dufrenite crushed/powered/ground.. There are 3 different ogres who do 3 different things.. I found out the hard way .. well not so hard.. I noticed after i got a gem crushed the the other ones said different things so I ground the other one I had.. Anyway be sure you get the right ogre. -Maddcovv
