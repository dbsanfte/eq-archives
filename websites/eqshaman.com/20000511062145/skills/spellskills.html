Spell Skills
Skill Name Level Analysis
Skill capacity: (lvl x 5) + 5
Abjuration 1 Resistance, AC, and quickness buff spells.
Alteration 1 Healing and many buff/debuff spells.
Conjuration 1 Summoning spells and death over time spells.
Divination 1 Vision spells.
Evocation 1 Direct damage spells.
Channeling 4 Increases chances not to be disrupted while casting, even when hit. Eventually you can even run while casting certain spells. You must put 1 practice point into this before it will start going up on its own.
Meditate 8 Allows you to regain mana faster, and increase the speed of memorizing and scribing. You must put 1 practice point into this before it will start going up on its own.
