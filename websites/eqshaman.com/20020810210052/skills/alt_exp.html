General Archetypes (Level 51)

First Aid Passive Increases Healing Rate from Bind Wound - This ability increases the amount healable for a player using the bind wound skill.
It is especially useful to those classes who cannot heal up to 75% of a players life with bandages.
This skill grants a 10% increase in the amount healable for the first point, 20% for the second, and 30% for the third.
In practical numbers this means a normal player who could heal only heal to 50% of total life with no skill can heal up to 80% of total life with 3 points in the skill.
Improved Lung Capacity Passive Increases Duration Player Can Hold Breath - This ability increases the duration a player can hold their breath.
It is very useful for players who do not have easy access to an enduring breath item or spell.
It improves capacity by 10% for the first point, 25% for the second, and 50% for the third.
Improved Natural Agility Passive Increases Base Agility - This ability increases the base agility by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Agility is important for increasing your chance to avoid being hit in melee combat. It does not increase your damage mitigation.
Improved Natural Charisma Passive Increases Base Charisma - This ability increases the base charisma by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Charisma is important for merchant prices (although it is far easier to get items for this than points).
It is also of some importance to Bards and Enchanters for spells and songs which use secondary rolls.
Improved Natural Cold Resistance Passive Increases Base Cold Resistance - This ability increases the base Cold Resistance by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Cold Resistance is important for increaseing your chances of avoiding taking maximum damage off cold based attacks.
Improved Natural Dexterity Passive Increases Base Dexterity - This ability increases the base dexterity by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Dexterity is important for increasing your chance to proc with a melee item, or to use passive abilities such as Assassinate.
It is especially important to bards to avoid missed notes on songs.
Improved Natural Disease Resistance Passive Increases Base Disease Resistance - This ability increases the base Disease Resistance by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Disease Resistance is important for increasing your chances of not being affected by disease based spells

Improved Natural Intelligence Passive Increases Base Intelligence - This ability increases the base intelligence by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Intelligence is important to Intelligence based casters: Necromancers, Enchanters, Wizards, Magicians, Shadow Knights, Bards. It increases the mana pool for these classes.
Improved Natural Magic Resistance Passive Increases Base Magic Resistance - This ability increases the base Magic Resistance by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Magic Resistance is important for increasing your chances of not being affected by magic based spells.
Improved Natural Metabolism Passive Reduces Characters Need for Food and Water - This ability reduces the rate at which food and water is consumed by a player.
This ability is probably one of the last ones players will want to fill up as its usefulness to a player is really very minor.
1 point reduces hunger and thirst by 10%, 2 points by 25% and 3 points by 50%.
Improved Natural Poison Resistance Passive Resistance Increases Base Poison Resistance - This ability increases the base Poison Resistance by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Poison Resistance is important for increasing your chances of not being affected by poison based spells.
Improved Natural Regeneration Passive Increase Hitpoint Regeneration Rate - This is a very useful ability for any player. It increases the speed at which they recover hitpoints.
Each point increases the base heal rate by 1 per tick. I.e. 1 point is 1 hitpoint increase per tick, 3 points is a 3 hitpoint increase per tick.
This regeneration stacks with all other forms of hitpoint regeneration in the game, including base regeneration bonuses which Trolls and Iksar get.
Improved Natural Run Speed Passive Increases Base Running Rate - This ability increases base movement rate for a player.
It does not stack with any other speed enhancement such as Spirit of Wolf, Journeyman boots or a horse.
This is primarily useful indoors in areas where these buffs cannot be used.
Each point gives around a 10% boost. A player with 3 points runs just slightly slower than a person using Journeyman boots.
Improved Natural Stamina Passive Increases Base Stamina - This ability increases the base stamina by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Stamina is important for increasing the maximum number of hitpoints a character can obtain. At high levels this can amount to a significant amount.
Improved Natural Strength Passive Increases Base Strength - This ability increases the base strength by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Strength impacts the carrying capacity of a character, and is one of the primary stats which affects maximum damage.
Improved Natural Wisdom Passive Increases Base Wisdom - This ability increases the base wisdom by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the 255 stat cap.
Wisdom is important to Wisdom based casters: Clerics, Druids, Shamen, Rangers, Paladins, Beastlords. It increases the mana pool for these classes.
Priest Archetypes (Level 55)
Channeling Focus Passive Improved Channeling Ability - This ability reduces the chances that a player will have their spell casting interrupted due to damage or movement.
The first point improves channeling by 5%, the second improves that to 10%, and the third raises it to a final 15%.
Healing Adept Passive Improves Instant Heal Spells - This spell increases the amount of healing done by instant heal spells (not Celestial heals spells or regrowth etc).
The first point improves healing done by 2%, the second point by 5% and the third by 10%.
For example, 3 points in this skill would make a 438 hitpoint remedy heal 482 hipoints instead.
Healing Gift Passive Chance to Critical Heal - This ability gives the healer a chance to critical heal. A critical heal gives twice as many hitpoints as its base.
The first point gives a 3% chance, the second a 6% chance, and the third a 10% chance.
This means that a person with 3 skill points has a 10% chance that a 438 hitpoint remedy spell will heal 876 hitpoints.
Mental Clarity Passive Improved Mana Regeneration - This ability increases the base mana regeneration of the player.
Each point increases the mana regained by 1 point per tick.
This mana regeneration stacks with all other forms of mana regeneration in the game.
Spell Casting Fury Passive Chance to Cast Critical Strike Spells - This ability gives the player the chance to score critical damage when casting damage spells on enemies.
This is especially important for classes such as Wizards and Magicians who rely heavily on their direct damage spells.
Critical damage spells hit for twice their normal damage.
The first point adds a 2% chance for this ability to be activated. The second point increases this to a 4% chance, and the third to a 7% chance.
Spell Casting Mastery Passive Increases Chance of Specialization Activation - This ability gives the player the chance to score critical damage when casting damage spells on enemies.
This is especially important for classes such as Wizards and Magicians who rely heavily on their direct damage spells.
Critical damage spells hit for twice their normal damage.
Spell Casting Reinforcement Passive Increases Duration of Buff Spells - This ability increases the duration that a buff will last.
This allows enchanters Clarity, Speed and Mana Pool buffs to last much longer.
The first point increases the duration by 5%, the second by 15%, and the third by 30%.
Shaman Archetypes (Level 59)
Alchemy Mastery Passive Improved Alchemy Skill - This ability reduces the chance of failure when making Alchemy based potions.
The first point reduces the chance of failure by 10%, the second by 25% and the third by 50%.
Cannibalization Activated Massive Cannibalization - This ability takes a massive amount of life from the Shaman and replenshishes their mana.
It takes 1924 hitpoints from the Shaman to give them 1200 mana back.
It can be re-used every 69 seconds, but must be used in relatively safe circumstances.
Mass Group Buff Activated Area Effect Buff - This ability allows the Shaman to cast normal group buffs in Area of Effect form.
This means that the cleric can buff an enormous number of people at once. The drawback is that spells can cost up to twice as much mana to cast in order to do this.
There is a 72 minute re-use time on this ability.
Pet Discipline Activated Make Pet Non-Responsive - This ability allows the Shaman to control their pet better. It enables a new command '/pet hold', which prevents the pet from moving or attacking under any circumstance.
The pet will break from this hold mode once any other command is issued to it. You will need to re issue the hold command after any pet encounter.
Quick Buff Passive Reduce Casting Time on Buff Spells - This is a very useful ability for the Shaman as they are normally the primary buffers in any group or raid situatuon.
This ability reduces the casting time on buff spells by 10%, 25% and 50% respectively for each point invested in it.
Rabid Bear Activated Transform Shaman into Bear - This ability allows the Shaman to transform into a Bear increasing all of their melee skills.
It has the following bonuses:
Increase ATK by 200
Increase Attack Speed by 99%
Increase Strength by 100

Increase Dexterity by 100
Increase Cold Resist by 50
Add Disease Proc - 35 Direct Damage, 25 damage per tick for 10 ticks.
Spell Casting Reinforcement Mastery Passive Increase the Duration of Buff Spells - This ability increases the duration on all buff spells.
It has a pre-requisite of 3 points in Spell Casting Reinforcement.
Investing in this ability raises the total duration from 30% (with 3 points in SCR) to 50%.
This means that a Shamans Group Focus Spell will last 2 hours 30 minutesas opposed to the normal 1 hours 40 minutes.
