Iksar Shaman Quests
From Allahkazam's

As an Iksar one thing I noticed fast was most quests give you a container. HINT: These are also good as backpacks! - Madd

Bone Chips:  Trooper Mozo, outside the gates to the Field of Bone, promises to give you 10 sp for 4 Bone Chips.  however, you will instead get laughed at and get between 1 to 7 sp.  The xp is good though and you gain faction with LegionOfCabilis, CabilisResidents, ScaledMystics, CrusadersOfGreenmist and the Swifttails. (Submitted by Muse)

Scorpion Pincers:  In the Field of Bone, Warlord Zyzz desires the return of four Scorpion Pincers off of Scorpions.  Return them for xp, some coin and increased faction with LegionOfCabilis, CabilisResidents, ScaledMystics, CrusadersOfGreenmist and the Swifttails.  (Submitted by Muse) (GREAT NEWBIE EXP - Madd)

Curscale Shield:  Talk to Drill Master Dal in Fortress Talishan and Mention that you are a new recruit and he will hand you a Partisan Pack, a 10-item backpack.  He wants you to fill it with one Giant Blood Sac of the Giant Leech, Scout Beads from Goblin Scout, one Sabertooth Kitten Canine, and three Bone shards.  Combine and return it to him and he will reward you with a Curscale Buckler.  (Submitted by Muse)

Curscale Skins:  Klok Mugruk gives you an 8 item backpack to fill with Curscale Skins from Scaled Wolf Pups and Cubs.  Combine them and return the pack for small coin and a piece of Curscale armor, which is excellent armor for a newbie. This quest gives very good experience (almost a bubble of experience at low levels).  The following factions improve: CabilisResidents and LegionOfCabilis.  (Submitted by Muse)

Shestar's Scaled Coif:  Trooper Shestar, inside the gates to the Field of Bone, wants 5 gp and three loose scales.  In return, he gives you Shestar's Unfinished Coif.  This gives no ac or other bonus, but he tells you that you can blacksmith it to fix it up if you use 3 scaled wolf scales.  So run out and get three more loose scales, combine them in a forge with the unfinished coif (no water needed), and you will get the Shestar's Scaled Coif for your troubles.    (Submitted by Muse)
