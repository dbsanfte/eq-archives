Shaman Skills


Shaman recieve a nice blend of both combat and casting skills, as well as a ton of useful alternate advancement abilities.

  • Note: Formula means the formula used to calculate the skill cap for your current level. For instance, the formula for Alteration is "5 + (Level x 5)". So, if you were level 4, your Alteration would not be able to rise past 25.
  • Note: For Archetype, Class, and some PoP Ability AA, the second/third rank will cost more than the original, using the formula "Base Cost x Rank Number". For example, quick buff costs 3 points at rank one, 6 points at rank two (3 x 2), and 9 points at rank three (3 x 3).

    Combat Skills

    Name Cap Formula Description
    1H Blunt 200 4 + (Level x 4) The skill of wielding one handed blunt weapons, like maces and clubs.
    2H Blunt 200 4 + (Level x 4) The skill of wielding two handed blunt weapons, like hammers and staves.
    Piercing 200 4 + (Level x 4) The skill of wielding piercing weapons, like spears. The shaman epic is a spear.
    Hand to Hand 50 5 + (Level x 5) The skills of using your fists for combat. This skill is utterly worthless for a shaman.
    Offence 200 Level + 5 The core skill of dealing damage via melee.
    Defense 200 Level + 5 The core skill of defending against melee attacks.
    Dodge 75 5 + (Level x 5) Allows a change to dodge each time you are attacked. You have to train a point into this skill to learn the basics before it will go up on its own. You cannot train in this ability until level 15.
    Bind Wound 210 5 + (Level x 5) This skill heals you for a minor amount of HP based on your skill level. There is a delay associated and you can be interupted by attackers. Requires a bandage for every use. You can only bandage to 50% of your total HP unless your skill is post 200, in which case you can bind wound to 70% of your total HP.


    Other Skills

    Name Cap Formula Description
    Alchemy 200 (Level - 24) x 5 This is a trade skill that will allow you to create potions that increase statistics as well as grant special effects, like Spirit of the Wolf and mana regeneration. For a complete guide, check out the alchemy section.
    Alcohol Tolerance 200 5 + (Level x 5) The greater your skill the more alcohol you can consume before the effects kick in. As for advantages, being drunk increases your strength. The downside is your that intelligence/wisdom decline, decreasing your mana pool (and causing the illusion that you are gaining mana).
    Beg 200 5 + (Level x 5) If you have a high skill in begging you can recieve a copper from a merchant/banker. If you have a really high skill, you can even get a silver. You can increase your beg skill from any NPC in the game. This includes your pet, animals, and even monsters that are currently attacking you. Overall, this is useless skill and takes an eternity to get up.
    Fishing 200 5 + (Level x 5) Fishing is like a mini trade skill. You need to have a fishing pole equipped to and bait for every time you cast. Fishing can not only turn up Fresh Fish, but items like Tattered Sandles, Rusty Daggers, and Fish Scales. Some zones have unique fish/items.
    Forage 50 None needed Only Iksar shaman recieve Forage and start with 50 skill. This skill gives you free food, like Vegetables and Berries.
    Safe Fall 50 None needed Only Vah Shir shaman recieve Safe Fall and start with 50 skill. This ability gives you a chance to dodge fall damage.
    Sense Heading 200 5 + (Level x 5) This ability will provide you with the direction you are facing (North, Southeast, etc.).
    Sneak 50 None needed Only Vah Shir shaman recieve Sneak and start with 50 skill. When sneaking you will move slower and are less likely to have NPC's attack you when traveling near them.
    Swimming 200 5 + (Level x 5) The higher your skill the faster you swim.
