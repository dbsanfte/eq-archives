Where to find Shaman spells

Basic Spells
Halas
The shaman guild in Halas isn't hard to find, there's a large broken table outside of it. and there are several Shamans perpetually casting spells outside of the guild halls. If you've gotten lost in Halas, here are some simple instructions: Go to the Ferry dock in the city side, turn around and face away from the water. Now move forward and take the left split. Pass the bank, and then take the street on the right just before the Inn. The Shaman guild is down that street on the left.

Trolls and Ogres are not welcome in Halas.

Grobb
Grobb is an easy city to get around in, but your first moments will be very confusing. You will want to find the courtyard where the hanging dwarf parts are on display in a torture device. This is near the bank, and directly in front of the Shaman guild. Some Shaman leaders are mingling outdoors, and other are inside the guild hall.

Barbarians are not welcome in Grobb. Ogres are welcome, and can buy freely, but cannot train in the shaman guild.

Oggok
Oggok is so sparsely populated that it can be difficult to find the Shaman guild. Upon entering the city you will see a small guard shack. Ahead you will see a large, white tower. Still further in, on your left, you will see a series of holes in the mountain. Enter the last hole and keep right at any splits as you navigate this dark cavern. At the top you will see two rooms filled with Shaman leaders.

Trolls are welcome in most parts of Oggok, but may find some embittered Ogres who do not tolerate Trolls. Proceed with caution.
Poison and Disease Spells
Barbarians: You'll need to wind your way through Everfrost Peaks and get out onto the tundra. Take an immediate left, and walk along the tundra and ice border but stay close to the mountainside on your left. Eventually you will see a ruined temple. Out on the platform you will meet someone who sells the poison and disease spells. Beware, the tundra is not for the young Shaman! Make your trip quick and efficient.

Trolls and Ogres: Innothule Swamp is home to a strange troll who appears upon one of the Hands of Innoruuk. This hand is a huge, brown statue reaching out of the ground. Legend has it that a colossal statue sunk into the depths of Innothule Swamp. Rumor has it that Barbarians are also welcomed by this vendor.

Vision Spells
Serpent Sight and Spirit Sight (among others) can be found in the Oasis of Ro. This is quite a jaunt for Barbarians but an easy trip for Ogres and Trolls. Note that Barbarians can buy Serpent Sight in the Enchanter's guild of Qeynos.

Barbarians should enter the oasis from North Ro along the beach. When you come to the first great rock spire in the water, turn immediately right and head inland, being wary of deadly sand giants. You come upon the gypsy tents from whom you can buy the vision spells.

Trolls and Ogres have an easier trip than Barbarians, but suffer the uncanny misfortunate of having too low of a faction with the gypsies to buy the spells on their own. You will need a trusted friend to buy the spells for you. You will enter the Oasis from South Ro. Head immediately to the right, but skirt the one orc camp that separates you from the beach. Run along the beach now until you hit the second rock spire on your right, in the water. Now head inland to your left and you'll happen upon the gypsy camp. Be warned, they do not like Trolls and Ogres.

Spells available in the Oasis Gypsy tents
Cure Blindness Serpent Sight Spirit Sight Vision
Ultravision Assidious Vision Sense Animal  
Summoning Spells
Along the rivers bank in the west part of West Karana, is a quaint Barbarian village where vendors sell the Summon Wolf/Spirit line of spells.

The easiest way for Barbarians to find it is to turn immediately right as you enter West Karana from Qeynos Hills. Follow the mountain edges to the river banks and then travel along the river until you reach the village.

Trolls and Ogres will most likely come from South Karana across White Bridge. Beware, once you enter North Karana, you must jump off the bridge and swim diagonally to the shore on the left to avoid the guards of White Bridge. Travel along the river bank (keeping the river on your left) until you reach West Karana. Once in West Karana, just follow the river to the Barbarian village. Be warned, watch your factions with these folk. Assume they do not like you, and keep them at a distance until you're sure they will receive you properly.

Spells available in the West Karana Barbarian village

Befriend Companion Spirit Vigilant Spirit Guardian Spirit
Frenzied Spirit      
Miscellaneous Spells
There are a few spells which can only be found in the guild halls of other mage classes, or at another Shaman guild in a different city.

For Barbarians this might mean a trip to Qeynos is in order. Some spells may be acquired in other cities, but you may find your faction needs adjustment before anyone will deal with you.

For Trolls and Ogres, this might mean a cautious visit to Neriak. You cannot buy the spells directly as most mage guilds will kill you on sight. Make friends with a Dark Elf.
