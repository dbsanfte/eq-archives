Velious Quest Armor:

Notes: When you zone in to the city go across both bridges and when you hit the intersection take a right. He is in the armory on your right 1 or 2 buildings down.

It would appear the gems drop in many velious locations and the Corroded armor can be found in Kael of giants in the arena

 

Terman Underbelly
 location: Thurgadin
He makes:
Boots: 
three crushed flame emeralds and a pair of corroded chain boots
Tunic : 
corroded chain tunic and three pieces of black marble
Sleeves: 

corroded chain sleeves, 3 juandiced gems 
Gauntlets: 
corroded chain gauntlets and three crushed lava rubies
Coif:
corroded chain coif and three pieces of crushed onyx sapphire
Leggings:
Three chipped onyx sapphires a pair of corroded chain leggings
Bracer:
corroded chain bracer, 3 crushed opals
