Multi-charge potions

To make 5 charge or 10 charge potions you will need to have a Bear Skin Potion Bag. I keep one in the bank at all times. The bag is made through tailoring, here is the recipe:

2 spiderling silks = silk thread (Tailoring Trivial 15)
3 silk threads = silk cord (Tailoring Trivial 26)
thick grizzly bear skin + silk cord = bearskin potion bag (Tailoring Trivial 68)

The bag is tradable so if you have no tailoring skill get a friend to make one with the materials you gather. The skin is a semi-common drop off grizzlys in West Commons and other zones.

Now make 5 or 10 single charge potions and put them in the bear skin potion bag. The combine is a no-fail combine , meaning you will allways succeed.
