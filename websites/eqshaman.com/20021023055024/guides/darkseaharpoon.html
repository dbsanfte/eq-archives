Darksea Harpoon Guide
by stusser (sam)



1) You must be level 49, or you will not be able to do it solo, period. Cold spells (like our DD) are 100% resisted in kedge.

2) If you don't have a fishbone earring or rebreather, you're in for a WORLD of pain. Without a fishbone you simply cannot go AFK or you'll die...

Oh, and you remember those serverlag spikes? The ones where the RCV packetloss goes to 30%, back down to 0, back up to 30%, and nothing happens for up to a minute or two? Well, enduring breath's duration is clientside, and so is drowning. That's right, EB can go out during serverlag and you can drown before you can cast it. You can be sitting at your computer and just watch it happen.

So anyway, if you don't have a fishbone or rebreather try your DAMNDEST to borrow/buy/beg/steal one. If you can't pull it off, buy a couple (5 or 10) water-breathing potions from your local potion vendor for emergencies. (not your local shaman, the vendor has em cheaper =/)

Also buy two entire backpacks of fish scales. Yes, I realize that's 320 scales.

3) In kedge, ALWAYS fight with your back to the wall, and fight in a corner if possible. This is to prevent spell interruptions; every time you're hit underwater you move backwards.

4) Make a level 36 pet, the one that hits for 51. You can test it on lancer swordfish, they're really easy. Park your pet at the spot marked "1" on the map.

5) Your home for the next X hours (hopefully X<65!) is marked "2" on the map. Wedge yourself into this corner.

4) The fierce impaler's placeholder spawns in the very back of the room, at the spot marked "3". It then follows the arrows to "4", "5", and then back to "3".

The placeholder has a spawn time of eighteen minutes and can be either a piercer swordfish (level 33 to 38, mostly green at 50) or an impaler swordfish (level 38-42, all blue at 50).

5) How to solo-pull the placeholder.

i) First, make sure that there are no lancers visible behind the pillar. They'll show up at 6 or 7. Usually 7. If a lancer is there, just stand up in your home corner and slap it with togors insects. Your pet will attack the fish, so root it and swim over to 1 and med until it dies. It shouldn't take long.

If a lancer is present right behind the pillar there, it will aggro on you when you go to pull the placeholder.

ii) Alacrity your pet. Make sure your pet has talisman up.

iii) From your home at 1, stand up, look straight up and swim to the ceiling. Then, let yourself float down. You should be about 2 feet behind the ledge and NOT that close to the pillar. As you float down, check 4 and 5 for a piercer or impaler. It will be fairly high up-- remember kedge is a three dimensional dungeon. If you don't see either one there, it is either at 3 (and thus inaccessable) or dead already (so wait). Target the impaler/piercer placeholder by clicking on it.

iv) Turn around and face back to the zone. Swim forward until you hit the wall at 2, then pop togor's insects. The second togor's insects finishes, swim over to 1. With a bit of luck (and some practice) the placeholder and the placeholder alone will follow you to 1 and attack you.

Your pet will instantly attack it and take its attention off of you. If the placeholder is a piercer, root it, swim to 1, and med.

If the placeholder is an impaler, ebolt it, root it, swim to 1, and med. After 42 seconds, ebolt and root again. Watch your pet's health, sometimes you need to toss a gheal on it. If the impaler breaks root and attacks you at 1, root it again and med at 2. ALWAYS STAY IN A CORNER!

6) How to solo-pull and terminate the fierce impaler.

i) The fierce impaler is a nasty mother. Before I pull him, I make sure that my pet and myself have talisman, guardian, and chloroplast up and especially that my pet has alacrity. Yes this will take a lot of mana and yes you will have to med when the fierce impaler has actually (oh my god!!) spawned... but it's worth it. Get full mana just in case.

ii) You can try to pull the fierce with togors like the placeholder, but usually he aggros on you as soon as you get to the ceiling and start floating down. He has a HUGE aggro radius. So, give it a shot... I managed to pull with togors 3 out of 6 fierce pulls. Regardless, the fierce should be on you and you should be at 1 with your pet hitting it.

iii) If togors isn't in, togors. Then plague, ebolt, root, swim to 2, and med. After that, it's the same as an impaler placeholder-- watch your pet's health and ebolt/root a couple of times, always staying in the opposite corner. I have never had to zone a fierce impaler.

7) Loot your gloomwater harpoon (heh heh), set your timer for 18 minutes, and repeat.

Hope that helped!
