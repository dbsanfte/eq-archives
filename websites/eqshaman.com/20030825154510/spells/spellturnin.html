Shaman "Trade-In Reward" Spells

(thanks to Mannra Mystikclaw for information)

In both Firiona Vie (barb hut) and Overthere, there is a person you can turn in these 4 spells to:

Talisman of Jasinth (level 51)
Spirit of Scale (level 52)
Cripple (level 53)
Cannabalize III (level 54)

When you turn them in (one at a time mind you) you will get one of these 4 spells randomly (no way to predict which you get, i should know now with 3 insidious decay and no Shroud of the Spirits yet..... sigh):

Insidious Decay (level 52)
Talisman of Shadoo (level 53)
Shroud of the Spirits (level 54)
Torrent of Poison (level 55)
