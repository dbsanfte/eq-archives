Weapon Skills

Weapon skills will max out at (Your level X 4)+4

Slam -
Slam will never go up it will always be 1 point. Its primary use is to stun casters so you can interrupt their spell casting

1H slash-
There are a few 1H Slash weapons a shaman can use. The irony is we can not train in 1H Slash.

2Hblunt-
Lots-o-Damage

1Hblunt-
Nice to use if you want to equip a shield. Not like it will show up on a barb anyway.

Piercing
