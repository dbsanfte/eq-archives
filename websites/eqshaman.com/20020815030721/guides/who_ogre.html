Why choose a Ogre Shaman?
from Devan

When choosing a shaman, the first and most obvious choice you have to make is: Good or Evil. You should only be reading this if you can accept the fact that evil races are only welcome in 3 cities to start with. I emphasize the welcome because there are places in these cities where you will still be KOS.

Ok, so you are going to be evil. Why choose to be an Ogre Shaman over a Troll? Starting Stats Ogres start with 28 more stat points that Trolls.While most of those go into stamina and strength, its still a significant number of extra points. Wisdom Ogres have more wisdom than trolls to start. Since wisdom is THE defining stat for a shaman, this is quite significant. Newbie Area Say what you like, but the Feerrott is a pretty good newbie area. The roads close to Oggok will raise you from levels 1-5 or so. You then move to lizard camps for a few levels, followed by skeleton and zombie camps. This will get you to about level 10, where you can go to the temples outside Cazic Thule and kill Lizard Men until 14 or 15. The connecting zones are Innothule Swamp (Troll newbie area), Rathe Mtns, and Cazic Thule(CT). If you like the troll newbie area, its an easy enough run to the swamp. For later levels, Rathe Mtns can carry you from level 10 to 50! Cazic Thule is also one of the best mid to high level dungeons. Regeneration is a spell Trolls, as a race, regenerate HPs faster. That is great for warriors and Shadow Knights, but not nearly as important for a shaman. We get regenerate and chloroplast, which are quite adequate at the later levels. Agility/Dexterity/Charisma Ogres have lesser numbers in these 3 stats, but look at how it affects them All three of these stats can be raised to more than adequate levels through spells that all shaman get. While charisma is nice for charming animals above level 29, the few extra points will not make a difference in your overall EQ experience.
