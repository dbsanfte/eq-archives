Other Skills

Dodge - Level 15 - helps you avoid what would have been a sucessful hit

Fishing - Seems silly at first but I have found it a great way to get scales for the spell enduring breath, fish for food (I know we can summon food) , and some cheap free items like daggers, sandals, and other misc things.

Beg - You run the risk of NPCs aggroing on you if you beg to them too much. I have heard you can increase it safely by begging to a pet during downtime.

Bind wound - Skill capacity: (lvl x 5) + 5
Some people swear by it but I dont think they were shamans. I haven't found much use for it.
Once you get the skill up to a respectable level, you can heal 30+ points a bandage. Even though we can heal with spells, saving mana for other things, DoTs, buffs, is important especially if you do not have tons of mana to expend.
