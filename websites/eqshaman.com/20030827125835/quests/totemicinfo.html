Totemic/Dufrenite Armor:

Notes: Everyone can do the quest (Barbarian, Troll, Ogre). The crushed, ground, powdered gems are obtained by giving one dufrenite gem to the Ogres on the east side of the lake near rathe mtn zone (talk to them first). They /Con indifferent to all races. Also the Banded Pieces must be MEDIUM only.
Watch out when you are going to get your Dufrenite crushed/powered/ground.. There are 3 different ogres who do 3 different things.. -Maddcovv

Oculys Ogrefiend a Level 44 Cyclopse in Rathe Mountains drops totemic armor. He spawns after killing a large number of clops, and pretty close to the lake rathe zone.
