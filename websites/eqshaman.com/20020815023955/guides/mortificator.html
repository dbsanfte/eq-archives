My Theory of Mortifer Syythrak’s Spawn


As quick note I have been in the Rathe Mountains for over two months now studying this. I have read every single post that I could find. I think this will be a guarantee to my Shaman Brothers out there that this information will force the spawn of Mortifer. Just like other known camp spots of Named MOB’s where you have to keep clearing spawn till the spawn Tables provide the random Named one included in the spawn cycle.

I have been successful at killing Mortifer right at the Exact Spot he spawns twice in a week’s period. My first kill produced the Monk only vest and left me not very sure I had the key to his spawn. I the second time I killed him I got what I had set out for, the Staff.

Location: Rathe Mountains Skell Tower Area
1) I first noticed that there are two types of Lizard faction, Lizard Man, and Allize Taeew
2) Mortiy’s faction is Allize Taeew

The main premise to applying my theory revolves around Faction as the key to his spawn location.
Randomly in this area lizards spawn with Tae faction and Lizard Man faction
There is a very large spawn table involved here.
Lizards, Orcs, Obs. Drakes, Large Skells, Small Skells, Possibly Bears,

So the fun begins. I started killing all random spawn in the area with the exception of the two Haunt spawn spots, and the two other camps (Orc, Lizard). The spawn table I mentioned above has to be killed off. Random lizards will have the Taeew faction. It is my opinion that full spawn table has to be killed off to get the Mortifer randomly included in one of the spawn tables. As a side note to what I have noticed is that one Lizardman Scout spawns at the end of the valley near towards Feerott. IM not sure if this signifies the beginning or the end of the spawn table but I think it is significant. Also the spawn will come to a crawl unless you kill Balsaks as you go. This seems to keep room open for more randoms to spawn.

My first night I tried this on my own for 5 or 6 hours when Mortifer spawned right at the red spot I have on the map. Following weekend I invested 12 to 16 hours killing the spawn tables. Again right at the red spot on the map he spawned.

The map I have included shows some general spawn locations in yellow. Red is the spot twice I actually saw him spawn. The blue spot is roughly where the Liz Scout spawns and he travels up the valley to the hill. The big green spot is the small hill that exists. Around the hill on it, and through out the valley random spawn occurs.

I am hoping this to be the one of my most significant contributions to our community. I was determined to figure this because it seems so unfair that we do not have some really significant weapons available that other classes do.
