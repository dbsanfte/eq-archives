Spell Research


When venturing through Norrath, you will have probably noticed various runes, words, and pages dropping from creatures all around the world. These items are used by the intelligence casters (Enchanter, Magician, Enchanter, and Necromancer) to produce spells in a unique trade skill called research. Not only can they make spells for themselves, but are able to combine components to create spells for the other classes, including shaman. Any intelligence based caster can make shaman spells, the class does not matter.

To be able to make these spells, the caster will need a special research book. The new book can make everything the old book can in addition to wisdom based caster spells. To obtain the new book, you have to have a skill of at least 101. Find Researcher Alvarik in the Gulf of Gunthak (at location +1367, +80). Hail him and say, "I am a Wizard", "I am a Necromancer", "I am a Enchanter" or "I am a Magician", depending on your class. He will give you the new book, which is no drop.

Keep in mind that there is a chance at failure when making spells so you should try to find a player with the highest skill possible. It is also a good idea for the caster to have an Automated Quill, which gives +5% research skill. The quill is made by tinkering and can usually be bought in the Bazaar for relatively cheap.

But what about obtaining the spell components themselves? Research items can drop from too many creatures to list here and there is no specific way to get them. If you want to get a level 51 spell, hunt things that are around level 51. For instance, some people have had luck obtaining Levitation components in the Fungus Grove. However, research drops are so widespread and random that it is best to just purchase the components you need from the Bazaar.

The following is a list of the shaman spells that can be made from research.
