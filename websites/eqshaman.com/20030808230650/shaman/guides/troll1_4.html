Shaman Player Guides

Troll Shaman Levels 1-4
by Dawnrose

Okie me her to give you da lowdown on being a troll shaman.
Now me be just a lill troll shaman now so me only talk abut wot I kno. Befor you start you better lern about da trolls. Our big god is Cazic an he da biggest and bestest god der is. Sum trolls say Innoruuk is badder but me jus bash dem till dey not be talking. Cazic was de god who made us from the nice gook dats in de swamp, he de best!

LEVEL 1 - 4

Hokie, dis da first step in da troll life. You has two spells dat you get free so learn dem now before anyting else. You get inner fire, the bestest buff for now and you get a silly healing spell ting dats no gud. You now bring your note to da shamans place were all da biggest shamans met an stuff. Der you give it to the big troll (me not say name cos stinky troll say Innoruuk is da best god grrrrrrr). He gives you a tunic. Now you needs to train. Dis is up to you but me want to be gud at bashing so me put two points into 1h bashing, one point into 2-h bashin and 1 point into peircing. Me also put a point into unarmed but me now tinks dat was a waste.

Now you gud an ready for da swamp! Off you goes but you better stay near da bashers in da swamp. You bestest hunting da froggy tads and decaying skeletons. You keeps all da froggy flesh an bones you gets cos dem handy for quests.Once you gets to level two you can hunt dem lesser kobolds. Dem make me mad, dey called me a filthy troll so me bash dem gud.Now dem froggies can carry gud stuf. Lots of netted armor wich me kept till I was level 8. If you wants you can sell da loot for gold but don't sell any gloves but more on dat later.Now me got lucky an got a cracked staff an rusty shortened spear as loot so I was able to get gud at 1h bashing an piercing.Now you is getting loot an money its time to see wots you can do wit it all.

Quests
Get dat spell
Stoopid boss shaman troll wants two bone bits an two froggy flesh (I knos you want to eat dem tasty froggies but dis is orders of da boss shaman). Bring two bone bits an two froggy flesh a give dem to boss shaman, you gets nice spell an experience. Dat spel is burst of fire wich is kinda okie if you liks your froggy cooked (yuke! Me lik it nice an fresh an raw, yummy!). You can use it for pulling tings to you. No more swimming over to clobber dat froggy now yous just cast an it com running to you! An if you are bashing it so hard it runs den you can just cast the flame burst till its cooked and not running.

Make money

If you gets lotsa bones you can go to the big troll dat runs da shadowknight and give him four he den gives you a rusty two handed sword dat you can sell for real gold (over two gold each!) Or if you gots froggy meat and some froggy flesh you can give it to da biggest basher around. He gives you a gud rusty battle axe if yous give him two fleshies and one meat. Dis axe gets you de same as da rusty two hamded sword.

Buying spells

Okie now yours got loads of money you ant to get your gud spells. You shud hav three now, inner fire, burst of fire an minor healing. So nows you shud buy dexteros aura an strenghten, dey make you stronger an fsater at hitting tings. Also gud to cast on udder trolls you lik. Don't buy cure disease and edure cold yet cos you don't need dem yet. Me ben told dat sense majik is gud only for getting your divination skill up but its very gud at doing dat becos it so cheep in mana.

Bak to da swamp Now you cooking wit gas! Time to hunt da bigger frogges dan the tads. You moving a bit away from da bashers so keeps an eye out fir tings dat are too tuff (for now!). You can thump tings dat are blue an white mabe even yellow. You hunt da frogloks (but not da froglok fishermen cos dey nasty). An you get better an better till you get to level 5! Hurrah me congradulate you, you a gud troll!

Me bring you next section, from 5 to 9 soon as me gets to 9!
