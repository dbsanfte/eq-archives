Wolf Callers Amor (SkyShrine)
Slightly less Armor Class than Spirit Caller's, but has the highest combined Wisdom, Hit Point and Mana buffs with a nice smattering of resistances that add up admirably if you have a full set. In order to do the Wolf Caller's Armor Quests in Skyshrine, you will need to raise your faction with Claws of Veeshan to ALLIED status.
Quest Begins: Skyshrine
