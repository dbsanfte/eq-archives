*Levitation Changed*
Due to the implementation of casting times on activatable items, the code that disallowed refreshing of the Levitation spell while it was active is no longer necessary and has been backed out. The Levitate spell may now be refreshed while the spell is in use.


     Postings -

Gordon made another post in response to the Shrink item issue on the official forums today. Here's the scoop:

    Clarity is a defining spell for the Enchanter class. A clarity item was rejected because it would devaluate the Enchanter class. Than the item is supposed to shrink everywhere (dungeons and outdoor zones) making it better than the Shaman spell. Now there are 2 possibilities, its either an item that's been dropped or quested. Or its a potion that shaman can make with alchemy. In the last case how can a shaman get a spell in potion form that's more powerful than the original spell ???

    One of the reasons that the Shrink spell is dungeon only is that we wanted it to be a utility spell, not a "look at me, I'm a miniature troll" spell. By limiting it to dungeon only, we at least have it someplace where there is a context for it.

    The new shrink item will be a potion that is created through alchemy. Since only shamans can do alchemy, there's no worry about taking something away from them. We went ahead and decided to make the potion "shrink everywhere", since its cost will dissuade people from using it as a "show off" spell.

    -Gordon
