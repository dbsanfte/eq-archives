GM Event 02/21/2002 Tsuda

There was a GM event over in Lake of Ill Omen last night. Hele and I were over in Frontier Mountains taking down a mountain giant when we had a few adds and had to run to the lake. When we zoned, the sky in LOIO was a funky purple/blue color. We headed down to the windmill to see if anything was going on and found some mean looking blue goblins. They /con red and indifferent, but attacked when we got close. The goblins were then swarmed by a bunch of other players and despawned before any of them were killed. Badnewss, Bandeko and Perelandra joined us a few minutes after that, but Badnewss and Bandeko had to leave before the second group of goblins spawned.
