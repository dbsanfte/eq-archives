A brief summary of the charter, as interpreted by Binnen

Lodestar is a lawful/neutral guild.  It is based in friendship first and everything else a distant second.  We aren't a power leveling guild, an "uber guild," or a particularly active raid guild.  Our only cast-in-stone rule for new members or alternate characters is that they must be at least level 10.

We are comprised of mature people of a wide age range (from 18 - 41 as far as I know).  Since we are comprised of mature people, vulgar language in /guildchat is not allowed.  Only children use "adult" language where it's not appropriate, and we feel that it's not appropriate in guild chat.   Please don't get the impression we're prudes...I use the "f" word when I feel the need to express my feelings that way to friends, including the other Elders (guild leaders), and they're not particularly shy with the word either.    We're not Thought Police, we just want guild chat that any mature person would feel proud to view and to which to contribute, whether they curse like Navy Seals or they're offended by somewhat risqué jokes.  What you say in /tells is your own business.

Becoming a member:

It's not difficult to get a character of yours fitted with the <Lodestar> guild tag.  It simply requires two full members (those who have made it out of "Recruit" status) to sponsor you and we'll tag you immediately.

Keeping the tag is another story.  You will be a "Recruit" for your first month.  During that month you have a "quest" to complete; you must get at least 1/3 of the members to like you well enough to vote for  full membership. Since friendship is THE thing upon which we are based, being friendly with ONLY  your two sponsors CANNOT be enough.  Here's some tips (for those who don't like quest "cheats," don't read any further ;) ):
Visit, read and post to the Message Board.
Speak up in guild chat (say hello to folks logging in, and goodbye to those leaving, answer what questions you can, or simply be pleasant)
Group with guild members you don't know very well.
