Tomb Bloodfists
< dust an inch thick stirs as he opens the door >

This place doesn't get many visitors does it?

< silence, the barkeep snores, slumped on a stool in the corner behind the bar >

Don't mind if I help myself do ya?

< grabs a couple bottles of Flaming Clurg from behind the bar, slipping a few plat into the barkeeps pocket >

Ah, not the best, but better than most.

< takes a seat in a dark corner and sits back to relax for a while, chugging the first bottle of Flaming Clurg, propping his feet up on the table and leaning the chair back against the wall >
Tomb Bloodfists
< stirs from his latest alchol induced nap and walks over to the bar >

So, what else we got behind that bar to drink?

the still dozing barkeep doesn't reply...

< helping himself once again he notices everything is gone, including the Bog Juice and Ogre Swill >

Well, looks like the bar has dried up, time to be movin on.

Don't spend all that platinum in one place good sir.

< walks out into a dark deserted street and fades out into the shadows >
