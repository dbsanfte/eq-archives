Ghuarboktumawg
OOC: Hi all, i'm trying to develop my character a bit further with this. Any suggestions are welcome, there'll be more to come if y'all like this.

&nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp When me wuz born, wuz jus' a runt, tuu big ta thro, but de rite size ta punt. Meshurin' up ta me brudders me alwaez wuz smaller, an' up ta me's sistahs, ebryun uv dem wuz taller. So me's mudder sed "Sun, dis wut me's gunna do, gunna gib yuu's BIG name ta help ta gro yuu. BIG names makes fer BIG critters, so you's Ghuarboktumawg. Dat's shure ta grow you's BIG (least mo den enee frog)." So dat wuz me name, an' mudder tol' me it means, 'idee-biddee teenee-weenee, but still bigger den de Greens.'
&nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp So eneewaez, growin' up in Oggok wuz purdee gud, playin' wif de uvers in de Feerot wud. We's played fun games like Croceegader an' Frogs, but den ebryun'd barsh on dis poor hed uv Mawg's. But me did no mind, eben tho dey wuz ruff, me's lurned ta fite bak, an' dat made me's tuff. Yar, tuff as nales 'cept me still wuz no big, but me also gruu cleber, an' into de mojo-force me lurned ta dig. Cuz Zekee had mark'd me's, dis wuz gunna be fun, me'd lurn ta do mojo t'ings like bery fas' run. But me still wuz no redee, me's still be tuu small, cuz ya can't werk fer Zekee, 'less you's can barsh wif de Maul. So me kep' on bein' tuff, an' fin'lee un' day, all me blud an' swet it started ta pay. Me started ta get bigger, me gru an' me gru, an' me's got bery bery big a'for dat yeer wuz t'ru. Me wuz still a liddle small, but dat's a'rite, me's almos' as big as me brudder de Shadee-nite.
&nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp So me's feelin' all big, barshin lizzees an' den, Zulort he sez ta me "Yuu's gunna be a Shamen." Me wuz so happee dat me jumped in de air, den' trundled off wif Zulort tu ta War Shamen lare. Der me spent seb'ral yeers jus' lernin' de arts, uv barshin' an' mojo-in', an' for'chun tellin' wif farts. Me werked bery hard ta lern all dose t'ings, like no-slo an' mojo-ice, an' on-yuu's-feets-wings. Me werked an' me werked an' me lurned an' me lerned, an' befo me knowed it, into a truu Greenblud me'd turned. Den de bigboss Zulort, him says "Now yuu's all set," go an' barsh in Zekee's name, show dem dey's gods is all wet." So me's went roun' de wurld followin' Zekee's werd, until un' day, uv da Simmer'i me herd.
&nbsp &nbsp &nbsp &nbsp So me's went der ta meet dem, an' tu me's surp'ise, dey looked tuff fer darkees, an' sum bery wize. So me said ta me's sef "Sef me'd bedder show dem de way, ta barsh t'ings fer Zekee, an' make yer en'mees pay." Cuz, tuff or no, dey still wuz liddle darkees, bet dey'd radder reed buks den rassle wif sharkees. But me's t'inkin' "All de darkees at un'ce, dat's allot ta du, mebee me'd bes' start wif un', mebee tuu." So me's startin' wif Janee, me's Bes' Barshin' Buddee, gunna teech him ta barsh' ware it's dry or id's muddee. Me's happee now wif all me's Simmer'i frend, yuu no mess wif dem or you's meet yer end.

Now it's de end, see you's roun' de bend.

Mawg
Nu blud uv de Cimmerii
