Vendui, city council members,


As dicussed in the meeting held last eve, in The Third Gate, many of the communinty are still outraged with the opening of our gates to the Trolls. I for one, am in full favor of this as it only strengthens our forces.

Recent developments have arisen that have widened the rift between Teir'Dal for and against. Mainly the dwindling supply of food and fresh game. True that the addition of the Troll population has seriously effected food supplies, we need to find a way to support all that now make Neriak their home. Not just point fingers and toss our friends and allies to the forest.

My Family has constructed a plan to answer the growing need for food here in Neriak. There are remote farms in the Karanas. These farms provide enough food through the spring and summer to feed all of Neriak.
These farms are far from the reach of Antonious Bale. Even if he was to attempt to protect them he couldn't begin to drag a reasonable force that far from Qeynos without risking the cities safety.

The Darkness will sweep into the Karanas and solve to problem with food supplies. I leave the other matters of our city in your hands.
