View Thread: Sceptre of the 3rd moon

Solstaar Darkfyre
Solstaar cleared his mind as he sat in his study deep in the heart of Neriak.

"Everything is working so perfectly.....there is no more reason to worry about the Knights destruction. Now there is just one final piece, and it will be complete."

The mage turned a page in an aged leather-bound book. He blew off the dust to find a picture of a golden sceptre. Under it in elder-Tier'dal it was titled...

"Sceptre of the 3rd moon."

Solstaar stared at the drawing for several moments, and breathed heavily.

"The Storm are unaware of the powers posessed by this artifact, yet they hold it in their vault of treasure. If my spies serve me correct...."

Solstaar reached for his dark robes and exited his quarters in quickness.

"The Knights have signed on an ogre of Oggok to their ranks, now to find out how incredibly they trust this one...."
