Mr Bodie
As Dwarkan says if you are persistent enough, you can probably make it happen. However, if you do not have a legitimate reason for your info not matching the original info in some way, you will very likely be denied. In fact I have read of cases where the transfer person tried it and got banned(probably for being an @#%$ about it and catching the wrong customer service person at the wrong time) If the account has a lot of ownership changes or red flags of any kind due to past disputes this can also cause you major problems.

Also, if you info doesn't match expect to be forced to jump through many hoops including TONS of questions about the original owner's personal info, which can include CD keys and a host of other crap, like birth date, last 4 digits on various credit cards used etc.

You can get lucky and just get greenlighted with no effort, but the vast majority of people run into sticking points at some point during this process. I buy and sell assloads of characters and stuff, and I've talked to a lot of people who have tried this.

To sum it up, it's about 10 percent likely you will get a clean, no problems transfer. 70 percent likely you will need original owner info, and 20 percent likely you will get a CS rep from hell and the account could be banned. So make sure you remove all droppable gear first to another account, because you are taking a risk.

Added to all this is another 20 percent chance that Verant will @#%$ up or lose your request entirely, and you will end up contacting CS anyways, and any time you spend Verant's customer service dollar's is when they get aggressive.

Your absolute best bet here is to have the original owner move the character for you to a brand new account, also signed up by that owner.

I get requests all the time to do it, but I've given up after about 5 or 6 abortive attempts.
