Attention all those cast out from society, for deeds unspeakable and crimes so unforgivable that even your dark bretheren shun you. I offer you a brotherhood made up of such like you. Non shall ask of your past, nor would you be condemed if it were know. Only one thing I ask of you, to be willing to work for anyone for a price. This shall be a brotherhood of mercenaries, and we shall be like brothers, protecting eachother from all others. You will owe no one, not even gods allegience, but you cannot betray your brothers, for you will be punished for that and only that. If you would like to be apart of this please inform me here or if you happen to see me in your town.
