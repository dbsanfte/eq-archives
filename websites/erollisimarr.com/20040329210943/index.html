The Poacher of Everfrost Peaks!
- by Galvanid on Mar 03, 2004 - 7:10 PM
Today there was an honest to god GM event in Everfrost Peaks.

An Ogre Poacher Rama was being chased by a Warden Chas for slaying the mammoths of everfrost needlessly.

Many helped Chas track down the Ogre female and trap her in Miragul's caves, but the surprising thing was that of the people who came to the aid of the poacher. The poacher, Rama, lured two druids and a ranger to her side. They accepted her bribe of 25 platinum and defended her against the righetous Warden Chas.

A sad day it is when two druids and a ranger side with a poacher... vile villans they were. Of course they lost their ill fight and the Warden succeeded in capturing and banishing the poacher.

I am thankful for that. The people of Halas have enough to suffer through with the Lady Vox, the fiend Miragul, and the constant assault of goblins and giants, than to worry about filthy poachers unbalancing our valuable Mammoth populations. Once again, the White Wolves of the North were triumphant!

The New Style
- by Ogmuk on Feb 22, 2004 - 5:00 PM
It's not yet finished but it's getting close. As you can see it's not the style that the majority voted for because I really disliked reading yellow and orange on black/grey. I hope that everyone agrees that this is a good alternative.

I'd love to hear feedback about what you do and don't like about the layout and of course of any errors you encounter.

I've also added an option for an expanding/'liquid' layout for those who wish not being restricted to a layout based on 800 * 600 although I advice to use a fixed width because the styles were designed that way.

You can find the style chooser at the bottom. It also has an option to change back to the default looks for those who really hate it.
