Mawg's Ode ta eetin'

Wen eber me gets a tummee rumble,
It spins me's hed an' makes me's tumble,
But wut alweyz be der ta pick me up?
Dose mos' tastee t'ings on wich me's sup!

Mebee it be time fer frogee legs,
Dose nice saltee treets fer wut me'd begs.
Or p'raps it be pikkul'd lizee,
Dat tuff chewy stuff put Mawg allina tizzee.

On fis' filays me lubs ta naw,
Jus' gib me's mo' ta cram in me's maw.
Hrm, how 'bout sum sharkee staak,
Shure, sum mo' uv dose me'd luv ta taak.

Ooh, de toze uv haf'in feet,
Now fixin' dose, it be quiit de feat,
Dey's such a spicee squishee treet,
Dey's sumt'ing dat me lubs ta eet.

If mee's wants sumt'ing ta munch,
Me'll hab bixee crunchees wif me's lunch,
Dey gots sucha luvlee crunch,
Me lubs ta eet dem a ho' ho' bunch.

An den ta drink der's Oger swill,
Ta drink dis stuff be sucha thrill,
An' if me's t'irstee den me will,
Drink de stuff straat out de still.

Ooh, me'd no sed 'bout usin' beer,
dat sizzle is muusik tuu me's eer,
Rub de staak down gud wif ale,
Dat'll maak it tastee in sun or hail.

Den der's dat bred dat Charm'me maaks,
An' jus' sum mojo iz all it taaks.
It no's tuu bad if yuu's in a biind,
An' eetin' food liik wud ya no doo miind.

Uhoh, dat wuz a liddle uglee,
Hope Charm'me no chaas me's up a tree.
Soree Charm'me it no dat bad,
It no be de wurst stuff me's eber had.

De wurst stuff, dat be elfee cukin'
No'un can cuk hoo's dat skinee lukin'
Me ate der food an' me wuz shukin'
Eben sandulz taast better jus' aftur hukin'.

Now get bak tuu de tastee stuff me shud,
Furgit alla de stuff taast liik wud,
Der's a bery gud cuk in me's neyburhud,
Ansur tuu de naam uv Dooga she shud.

She maaks dat wunderul HEHE Meet,
Dat's tart an' tangee an' such a treet.
De stuff is so much fun ta eet,
It eben be tastee if rapp'd ina sheet.

Den uv cors der's choculat,
Me tummee sez, "dis ware it at!"
Yuu make dis frum de brownee t'ings,
Dat dans aroun' de muchruum rings.

Den de choc'let yuu uus ta maak,
de cukeez an' de birfdaa caak.
Dese t'ings dey jus' iz so graat,
Dey's perfec' fer a dinnur daat.
