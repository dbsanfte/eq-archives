Krual
ooc: an oldie, but goodie. Reposted in memory of my beloved troll Krual and his mogud nafoz : Draz, Ednay, Mornak, Zee, Kzan and Ugdugy, as well as the KHS and Shadow Guard. This was Krual's beginning on his path of Evil with the Cimm's.




Da toree uv Krual, az teld bi Krual.



Krual uz lev fo ded wen Krual wuz namobig. Krual liff onlee bi eeteeng frogee tadz dat wer almo az big az Krual wuz. Neburdelez, Krual liff. Un namuun, Krual heerd voyzez uv tuu mojomen. Krual neber seed dem befo, so Krual hyde bi cuvureeng Krualz eyez. Emayzenlee dey fine Krual! Krual tink dey wil bash Krual so Krual skard. (Krual iz namobig, memer?) Dey nabash Krual doh, ensted dey speke Trol tu Krual. Dey azk iv Krual ar lozt. Krual say Nar, Krual lif en de swamp. Dey luuk nahapee at de nuuz. So dey tel Krual dat dey tak Krual ober Namobigwatr tu dey hom. Krual hapee, Krual phinlee hab a famlee.

De tuu mojomen wer Ooeelar n Ullitii. Dey Krualz nuu famlee. Ooeelar wuz Rudenz mopowurvul wizurd. Hez wive, Ullitii, praktized a dark, sekruativ mojo. She mak bondedz, n diz wuz forbidun by ebereeun, but Krual n Ooeelar natel aneebudee.

Dey sen Krual tu skool so Krual git mobig n motuff. Krual lern dey wayz uv da paledin. Krual gro mobig n tuff but et momoklank on Krual, Krual neber had anee nafoz. Dey all mak phun uv Krual, ebeen Krualz teechurz mak phun uv Krual, say dat Krual wil alwaz be steekee Trol pheecee, wot ebur pheecee meenz. Diz phil Krual wit momomuch hayte n raj. Krual azk Ullitii tu teech Krual de sekrutz uv her mojo. She duz n Krual lern mofazt.

Un namuun Ullitiiz sekrut git owt. Ebereeun raze der voyzez, caleeng fo her tu be ded! Ooeelar wud na alow diz. He kazt mojo so dat Ullitii n Krual hab tyme tu xkape. We reech de dokz wen yu heer Ooeelarz skreemz uv deth. We namohapee. Ullitii say silunt prayur tu Innruk n tell Krual tu prepair tu lanch de boat. Krual nadid see de Lord Paledin kumeeng tuwurdz uz. Ullitii nadid see him eder. He bash her on de syde uv her hed n she fal ober, blud gusheeng owt uv her wund. Diz fil Krual wit moraj n hayte den Krual eber fel befo. Krual skreem Innruk Krual gib Krualz sol tu yor Eturnul Hayte! n Krual bash de Lord Paledin wit Krualz hand.

De stajezt ting happen nex. Krualz hand glo wit a blak lyte. De Lord Paledin skreem en payn n fald down klucheeng hiz hart. Krual pik up Ullitiiz bodee n sayl awae. Ullitiiz dieeng breth teld Krual dat Innruk haz blezd Krual wit Hiz hayte. She tel Krual tu seek owt udderz dat hab ben blezd tu n diztroy all nabeeleevurz.

Diz iz how Krual wuz guyded tu The Cimmerii but Krualz toree iz far fum phinushed.
