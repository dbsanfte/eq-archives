Dawnen
Some things that MIGHT protect you when buying accounts:

1) Only buy with someone with a high Playerauction or Ebay rating.

2) Ask for the original discs when you buy the account. Agree to pay $4 extra to have those discs sent to you priority mail. Don't send payment for the account until you have the discs. That way, if the person is indeed a scammer, even if they were to try and scam you, you could get the account banned by Verant, which would only leave the scammer with the same amount of cash as they would have gotten just by selling you the account. If the person isn't the original owner, then be very cautious.

3) Deal with high level people in well known, well respected guilds. Then talk to other people in that guild about the transaction.

4) Don't deal with people that seem to be in a rush to complete the deal. A good deal today is a good deal tomorrow.

5) If the deal seems to good to be true, then it is probably a scam.

None of these will protect you 100%, but if you are careful, you most likely won't get ripped off.
