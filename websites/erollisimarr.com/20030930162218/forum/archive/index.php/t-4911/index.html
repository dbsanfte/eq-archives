I have a friend that has obtained accounts successfully without issue. "Know who you are buying from" is his motto.

Ex.
1. Never use in-game funds for an account.
2. Get a signed agreement faxed or mailed to you with photocopy of their driver's license.
3. Make them sign for the money... what is an extra $xx.xx for a piece of mind.
4. Get their phone number.
5. Make sure they are of age and if not, deal with their parents.

People that are not willing to do these steps are either
a.) Are Scammers
b.) Paranoid
c.) Other
