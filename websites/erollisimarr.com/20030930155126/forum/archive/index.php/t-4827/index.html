~~~~~~~~~~~Chapter 1~~~~~~~~~~
~~~~~~~~~~~Betrayal~~~~~~~~~~
...in the year 1429 a war was brewing between the Humans of Qeynos, Lead by their General Desuka, Loyal Shadow Knight, and friend to all Humans, their enemys were the Great Barbarians of Halas lead by the Vicious Olint, Warlord of the Barbarian Army, their war had been rageing long and hard for a great many years, but in the year 1457 the two sides made peace, the reason was not explained, all the people know is that one day Olint waved the White flag and gave up, and so there was peace in the land for a great while, but what the Humans did not know was that the Barbarians did not give up they simply bought themselves time to prepare and make a deal with a new threat which faced not only the Humans of Qeynos, but the entire world of norrath,The Iksar had come forth from the far away continent of Kunark & they wished to make all the land just like thier own, but they knew of course that they could not do it alone, so they made a deal with the barbarians, The Iksar will help them Defeat the humans, but only if the Barbarians would help the Iksar, defeat the rest of Norrath, Starting with Antonica & continuing to Faydwer, Odus, and lastly the newly discovered Velious.
The Barbarians agreed to this deal hoping that they could finally beat the Humans that they hate so much, although all was not as they thought, many Barbarians were against the war and wished for the fighting to stop, but more than that there was one that had many human friends before the war and did not wish to fight them, he avoided it by becomeing a guard to the General Olint himself, this way he did not have to go to war and could avoid fighting his friends but when he heard this plan he knew that his human friends would have no chance of survival this Barbarian was named Valden and although he was a Rogue by trade he had much knowledge of the Ways of the Warrior, and made his fellow Barbarians beleive he was one so that he could remain in the army, for Barbarians did not admit Rogues into the army only warriors.
So during the night Valden Rode away on his horse to Blackburrow the only passageway between His people and the city of Qeynos....after many hours of riding he came to the gate a Qeynos where he was immediatly captured, being a Barbarian and brought before Desuka who asked him,
"What are you doing in our Land"
Valden replied,"What dont you remember me old buddy?"
"What are you talkings about! I have never met you before in my life!"
"That hurts man, we grew up together, dont you remember me, the young barbarian that used to play with you as a child?"
At that Desuka had a flashback, suddenly he remembered playing with his human friends in Blackburrow before it became a city for Gnolls, there was a Gnoll Scout that found him and his friends, and had them cornered, it looked like they would never get out of there, but then all of a sudden the gnoll ran away like a cowardly dog, Desuka and his friends were confused, and when the turned around they saw a huge Barbarian and although he was the same age as them, he was much bigger being a Barbarian and all, and they became friends all of them, Desuka, Xeerex a high alf and only a child then but still training to become a great wizard even at his young age, Lahn a half elf and a decended of great druids of the past and studying to become a great druid himself one day, and the last boy that was with them that day was quite unusual, he was the only one of them that was not grateful for Valden's help, for he was very self dependant, and belived that he could beat the gnoll by himself, he was rather a cocky little punk, his name was Strykker a human and an orphan with no home or family, he had become a ranger at a very young age, seven years old he had decided to become a ranger, ever since his parents left him to survive on his own.
But even though they didnt get along at first Valden became good friends with Desuka, Xeerex, Lahn, & Strykker and as children they grew up together during good days, before the Barbarian/Human war, but when the war began they lost contact each of them went home to their villages, and saw each other no more until the war ended, this in fact was the first meeting of two of them in more than 10 years,
Desuka then stood up and said,"cut his ropes this man shall not be held prisoner he is our guest"
The orders were soon obeyed and Valden and Desuka were left to Desuka's Quarters to discuss the reason of Valdens Journey.
Desuka says,"Well my old friend what brings you here on such a dark night?
Valden replies,"I wish i were here for happier reasons but that is not the case...the war is not over, not like you thought, Olint was not surrendering simply buying himself time to make a deal. The Iksar have come forth, and they wis to take over the world. That is why Olint surrendered to you, he is makeing a deal with the Iksar to take over first you then the rest of Norrath, I tell you this because you are my friend, and i have been against this war for a great while."
"This is indeed i'll news my friend, but i owe you a great deal of gratitude now, for now that we know of this we can be ready, the war will not catch us by suprise we shall call for reinforcements,and we shall be victorious. I ask you my friend to join us in the fight."
"Aye and that i will my friend, that i will, but my weapon will kill no Barbarian, only Iksar Blood will stain my weapon, for i cannot kill one of my own."
"That is understandable my friend, and tommorow we will have much to do, but for now you should sleep, I will send a messenger tonight to Freeport, Where Xeerex is currently studying at the Academy of Arcane Science, he should be here for this, Lahn is with him and will be sent for as well, as for Strykker, he is currently residing in Surefall Glade across the Hills, no more than 10 minutes journey walking, I shall send for him tomorrow, and we will fight side by side once more, as we all did as children, but for now sleep my friend, if you need anything just ask the servants they will bring you whatever you need, but for now I must do some prepareing."

Valden is given a room within the great city of Qeynos, at last reunited with his friend, and soon to be united with all of his childhood friend, he begins to think of his own people in Halas, he knows now that he shall never be able to return there that he has done this, he has betrayed his people to help his friends, yet somehow he believes everything will turn out alright in the end, but for now he must get rest, he has much to do in the morning...

~~~~~~~~~~Across the Karanas~~~~~~~~


The next day the four travelers departed from Qeynos to begin their journey, but bad luck befalls them less than an hour from departure. As they enter the Plains of Karana from the Qeynos Hill they encounter a Werewolf hunting small prey, as it catches sight of them it stops.
Xeerex say,"Whats it doing, why doesnt it attack?"
Valden says,"I dont know maybe it knows it cant take four of us."
Strykker says,"No thats not it"
Lahn says," Then what is it?"
Strykker says,"Its not alone.
Xeerex says,"What? what you mean?"
Strykker says,"Over there in the bushes, another one, and a third on its way probably."
Valden says,"What do we do then?"
Strykker says,"We attack and quickly before more get here."
Lahn says,"Ok then lets do it."
Strykker says,"Hold on, Xeerex you and Valden take the one up ahead, Lahn help me with the one in the bushes."
Strykker calmly draws his bow, but keeps his sword ready.
Valden slowly approaches the werewolf, it slowly walks up and looks him straight in the eye...suddenly Valden Disappears. The Werewolf looks around confused. Then without warning Valden Backstabs the werewolf before it knows what hit him, Valden drew back his Crystal Grinder and struck at the wolf many times. The wolf blocked and parried his attacks and he blocked and parried the wolfs. Neither could seem to land a blow on the other until without warning an arrow hits the wolf through the head, it slumps over, dead.
Valden says,"Hey I had that!"
Strykker says,"no you didnt he was gonna kill you."
Lahn says,"you would think that a Werewolf could take more than one arrow."
Strykker says,"They can."
Xeerex says,"Then why did that one die?"
Strykker says,"It didnt."
Xeerex turns around and sees the wolf staring at him right in the eye. An arrow sticking out of his head.
Suddenly a Crystal Grinder is thrust forward, and pulled back slicing its Jugular ear to ear....Xeerex falls to his knees.
Xeerex says,"Thank you!"
Strykker says,"Told you."
Valden says,"What happened to the one in the bushes?"
Strykker says,"I made a mistake."
Valden says,"What you mean?"
Strykker says,"It was a rabbit"
Xeerex says,"So what happened?"
Strykker says,".....whose hungry?"
After they had a decent lunch of rabbit, the travelrs continued
on their Journey through the Plains of Karana. They encountered few problems on their journey and eventually decided to camp a few miles off of The City of Highkeep. For saftey they decided to sleep in shifts, two by two. Strykker and Xeerex had the first shift.
Strykker says,"Its been a while man"
Xeerex says,"Yeah, ten years i think since I last saw you."
Strykker says,"So what have you been doing these past ten years?"
Xeerex says,"even though i do not support it I have been studying to become a wizard."
Strykker says,"You mean you dont want to be a wizard?"
Xeerex says,"No what i really wanna be is a Warrior."
Strykker says,"Your young why dont you become one?"
Xeerex says,"My parents wont allow it. When they first asked me what i wanted to become, I told them Warrior, but they would not allow it. My mother was a High Elf Enchanter, and my Father a Human Necromancer. They wanted me to become a caster just like them, so i picked Wizard, that way I would be able to Translocate them away from me and they could stop running my life."
Strykker says,"....seems like a plan, where are they now?'
Xeerex says,"Dead. both of them, they had to go on a trip to Rivervale one day, passing through Kithicor Woods my father cast Invis to Undead on both of them...but somewhere they got seperated, my father searched long and hard, and when he found my mother, her Invis had failed and an Undead solider had ravaged her body."
Strykker says,"Wow thats rough man...what about your dad?"
Xeerex says,"He returned to Freeport a week later."
Strykker says,"Ohh Freeport that place is a hole!"
Xeerex says,"Tell me about it, well anyway, after my mom died my dad could not go on. he was real quiet alot and he was never the same...one day his body washed ashore on the bank underneath the Academy of Archane Science."
Strykker says,"Wow man that sucks. but now that there gone why didnt you become a warrior like you wanted?"
Xeerex says,"I dont know man, by that time i was already so far into my studies, giving up and becoming something else just didnt seem worth it."
Strykker says,".....hmm well that sucks man....hey i think its our turn to sleep lets wake these guys.
Strykker and Xeerex switched with Valden and Lahn, and went to sleep. The next morning after a quick breakfast the Travelers continued on their way...at the end of the day they arrive at the great city of Highkeep, where their journey takes a new twist...
