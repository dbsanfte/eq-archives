Please follow these rules or your post will be deleted, continue breaking the rules and you will get banned from the Erollisi Marr message board:

- This forum is meant for roleplay, and only roleplay. Non-RP posts will be deleted.
- If you post out of character, OOC (e.g. to tell someone you liked the story) please mark it with OOC.
- Please do not post violence or adult material in this forum. This is a public forum and thus kids are reading this too.
- Do not flame or fight with someone OOC, those posts will be deleted.
- 'Roleplaying' an asshole is not allowed. You are meant to roleplay and not be yourself :P


I'm looking for a few moderators:
- They'll delete threads and posts that should not belong here.
- I hope they encourage roleplaying by posting stories themselves.


If you're interested in being a moderator, please leave a post here or in the general forum. Thanks :)
