Our Vision for the future of the guild is to try and become a closer group of Dwarves, Gnomes & Halflings; to hold guild events that benefit all, and bring glory to the Granite War Machine. I would like to see the guild remain strong and prosper.

  • We are a Dwarf, Gnome & Halfling only guild on the Tarew Marr Server.
  • We are a small guild that likes to have a lot of fun.
  • We do drink a lot of ale. (Honey Mead if funds are tight.)
  • We do partake in drunken brawls from time to time.
  • We do not have set rules.
  • We are not a Power Guild!
  • Our Guild Events usually revolve around copious amounts of drinking.
  • Our main objective is to help one another out, be it with info or directly.
  • Our secondary objective is to help those truly in need.
  • The Guild is based on kindness to others, fellowship, and fun.
  • Did I mention we drink lots of ale?
Recruiting Policy
  1. Must be at least level 15. (this will hopefully insure the commitment of the prospective member to the character)
  2. Must interview with an officer of the Guild. (said officer shall insure that the prospective member is amiable, and has the granite it takes to be a member of the GWM)
  3. Prospective member must participate in a guild event as an honorary member, this includes all loot rights. (this is to introduce the prospective member to the guild, and to raise his/her alcohol tolerance)
  4. Prospective member must participate in a second guild event where in he/she shall be invited into the GWM. Note: upon invite the new member will be required to drink one half stack (10) of ale or comparable substitute.
  5. Exceptions*: a) Alternate characters of current guild members; b) Previous members that left the GWM on good terms that wish to rejoin; c) Alternate characters of previous members over level 15 who left the GWM on good terms.
* Exceptions can join after an interview with an officer, and approval of the Guild Leader.
