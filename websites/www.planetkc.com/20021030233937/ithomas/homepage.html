Neil Campbell "Itinerant String Section" CD (Freedom From) - "Unreleased LP from a few years back capturing Campbell in pre-New Age mode, carting his tiny violin around his tiny country. Gritty precursor to his new job trying to conduct the Vibracathedral Orchestra."  >>>
