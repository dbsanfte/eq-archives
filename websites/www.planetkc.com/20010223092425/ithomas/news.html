January 4, 2001
Magnetic Breakthrough unleashed...
The newest CD from The Terrifying Experience titled "Magnetic Breakthrough" is now available directly from Mental Telemetry mail-order!  Check out 2 full-audio MP3's from "Magnetic Breakthrough" by visiting our sounds page!

---The long awaited full-length follow up to 1998's "Supreme Radial" (AAJ), "Magnetic Breakthrough" marks The Terrifying Experience's triumphant return to the raw deconstruction of traditional rock.   Mitch Mitchell & Co. dish out a full-on temporal assault, using disfigured pop melodies, fuzzed out geetars & some of that good 'ol rock 'n roll panache as their weapons of choice.  It's quickly made obvious that Space and Time are irrelevant in the microcosm of The Terrifying Experience...from the first arena inflicted power-chords on "Woodshed Fist Fight" to their tongue-in-cheek stab at electronica on the closing track "Underground Pressure", "Magnetic Breakthrough" effortlessly breaks down all barriers of the known rock universe and gives birth to something new. ---

Whew...I've not only survived 2000, but M-Tel has successfully lurched onward to celebrate it's 1st anniversary!  My deepest thanks go out to everyone who has made this first year possible.  2001 is already looking to be both busy and productive.  With a new CD in the works, a new CD from The Terrifying Experience coming out in just 4 days and a new 7" in the pipe as well, this will be an exciting (yet thoroughly maddening) year for M-Tel indeed.  More updates are coming in a few days.  Until then, thanks for stopping by and have a great new year! 
-Ian
