F(requently) A(sked) Q(uestion)s
DOES ANYONE ACTUALLY READ THESE THINGS?
We don't think so, but Matador Records has one, so here we go...
DO YOU ACCEPT DEMOS?  WHAT ARE YOUR SUBMISSION GUIDELINES?
Yes, we do accept demos, thanks for asking!  However due to the usual madness around here, an answer to your submission is unlikely.  Ian does listen to all demos sent our way and tries to personally reply to each one of them, too.  If you happen to totally blow some minds, you can bet we'll be in touch!   Send all demo submissions to Mental Telemetry PO BOX 46643 Kansas City, MO 64188-6643 Attn: Demo Box.  Please save yourself (and us!) the trouble and do not send MP3's or web links via email!   Honestly, our punk-as-hell 26.6k dial-up connection makes viewing and downloading somewhat difficult and we'll probably just wind up hating you for it.
HOW DO I GET PROMO COPIES OF M/TEL RELEASES?
Promo requests from print journalists/editors are normally filled, however, we would like to know when and where the article, review or write-up will appear if at all possible.  Unfortunately, if you're requesting promos on behalf of an e-zine or other web type publication or if you're a college radio DJ, we probably won't be able to fill your requests (for the latter, please send us a current play list and we should be able to do something for you - especially if you've spun our stuff before).  It's nothing personal, but M/tel is terribly dedicated to helping all of the fine people we work with and we're simply not financially or logistically able to continue doing that without your support, too.  For more information on requesting promotional copies from M/tel, click here.
WHO DO I TALK TO ABOUT BOOKING A BAND ON YOUR LABEL?
Typically, we do not handle booking for the bands on our label.   However, circumstances do vary from artist to artist and occasionally, we may book a show or help set something up.  In most cases though, the bands themselves book their own shows, so we suggest getting in touch with them first.  Check out our artists pages for specific contact info or if you can't find what you need there, drop us a line and we'll forward your inquiries on to the right people.
