BIO INFORMATION
Founded in 1997, Straphanger hail from Grand Forks North Dakota.  Straphanger's sound is best described in their own words as "Midwest Loud".

STRAPHANGER FACTS
*Straphanger's tour van "Old Paint" was formerly owned by punk agriculturist Farmer P, who used the van to transport bails of hay on his North Dakota farm.

*Straphanger drummer Isaac "Ike" Turner, currently living and training in Norway, is rumored to be studying advanced polyrhythmic percussion under some guy named Hellhammer. He also played drums in Imipolex G and runs Genuflect Records.

*Straphanger bassist Brian Schill's bass rig has been called "ridiculous" by many a sound man (who knew a Fender M-80 120w combo amp w/ 15" speaker, slaved to a 300w Fender BXR 300C combo amp w/ 14" speaker, driving a Ampeg B25B cabinet w/ 2 x 15" speakers was "ridiculous" anyway...eh?). He's still trying to fit the lyric "I cut like Malarchuk" into a song.

*Straphanger guitarist Jason Lay is the undisputed World Champion of Hip Hop Scrabble. He can be reached c/o Odin's Belmont Service, Grand Forks North Dakota.
