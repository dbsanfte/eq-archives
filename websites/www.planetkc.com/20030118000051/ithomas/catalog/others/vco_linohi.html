The debut CD from VCO, after a string of self-released CD-Rs pits Neil Campbell, Julian Bradley, Mick Flower, Adam Davenport and Bridget Hayden against one another in a room full of guitars, drums, synths, violins, lutes, zithers, horns, bells, and all manner of things to bang on. Conradian violin dream-thrust, folk riffage, and commune drum-pummel collide with a tranquil ambiance.

Wondrous, wandering & wailing early droneage ('98-'99) from the VCO gang, that somehow slipped past the most of us.  Lino Hi is a true classic, just as essential as Versatile Arab Chord Chart & limited to 500 copies.
