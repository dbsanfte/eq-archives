PAYMENT ONLINE:
If you'd rather keep it ONLINE we recommend visiting ZEROTEC to purchase our stuff with a credit card (via secure server).  Of course, if you'd rather buy direct from MTEL with a credit card or you're buying titles from other labels, we can do that using PayPal...

USING PAYPAL:
To order titles online via PayPal, simply calculate your order total from the prices listed in our catalog + postage and handling, then send your total amount to our PayPal payment address at 'ian@mentaltelemetry.com'.   Once your payment comes through, we'll send you a confirmation email & get your order out ASAP.  Please write if you have any problems or need further assistance!

OTHER TITLES:
Other titles in the catalog are available through direct-mail order OR for purchase on-line using PAYPAL only.  Prices of all 'other' items will vary from title to title, so if you're ordering/paying by mail-order, please check the catalog prices before sending payment.

Keep in mind that we stock very limited quantities of 'other' titles, but do our best to stay current with stock on hand.  If a listed title is out of stock and we're able to get more copies of that item, the title will be noted as 'backordered' in the catalog, occasionally followed by the date that title is expected to return to stock.  If the title is completely sold out & we're not able to stock any more copies for some reason, you will be notified through email or post and your order will be canceled.  With that said, it's always a good idea to nab what you want while it's in stock.  You are definitely welcome to list alternatives as well.

PLACING TITLES ON HOLD:
We will gladly hold back items on your behalf for 1 week, but please be sure to send us an email (or give us a call) confirming that you wish to have said item(s) placed on hold.  We cannot guarantee your item(s) will be held unless we've received confirmation from you.
