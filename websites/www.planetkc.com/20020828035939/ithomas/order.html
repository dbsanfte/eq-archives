To order titles online via PayPal, simply calculate your order total from the prices listed in our catalog, add postage & handling, and send the total amount to our PayPal payment address at 'ian@mentaltelemetry.com'.  Be sure to tell us what you're ordering, too.  Once your payment is processed, we'll send you a confirmation email and put your order in the mail ASAP.  Please write if you have any problems or need further assistance using PayPal, etc.!

OTHER TITLES:
Other label releases listed here are only available from us via direct-mail order or via on-line purchase using PAYPAL.  Prices of all 'other' items will vary from title to title, so if you're ordering/paying by mail-order, please check the catalog prices before sending payment.

Keep in mind that we stock very limited quantities of 'other' titles, but do our best to stay current with the stock we have on hand.  If a listed title is out of stock temporarily, the title will be noted as 'backordered' in our catalog.  If the title is completely sold out or we're unable to further order/stock copies of said title, you will be notified through email and your order will be canceled (if necessary, your money will be promptly refunded).  With that said, it's always a good idea to nab what you want while it's in stock.

PLACING TITLES ON HOLD:
We will gladly hold items on your behalf for 1 week, however, please be sure to send us an email (or give us a call) confirming that you wish to have said item(s) placed on hold.  We cannot guarantee your item(s) will be held unless we've received definite confirmation from you first.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

DO YOU ACCEPT DEMOS?  WHAT ARE YOUR SUBMISSION GUIDELINES?
Yes we do accept demo recordings, thanks for asking!   However, it should be noted that it'd be a fairly rare occasion for us to release anything from unsolicited demos.  And this is not to say that your music isn't any good or of any value to us of course, but under the circumstances, we're just not financially able to take on anything we haven't instigated ourselves.  Because of our busy work schedules and the usual madness around here, an answer to your submission is not likely, but we do listen to all demos sent to us (Ian tries his best to personally reply to each and every last one of them).  If you totally blow our minds though, you can bet we'll be in touch!  All demo submissions should be sent to our PO BOX ATTN: Demo Box; please save yourself (and us!) the trouble and do not send MP3's or band websites to M/TEL via email!  Honestly, our punk-as-hell 26.6k dial-up connection makes viewing and downloading somewhat difficult.

HOW DO I GET PROMO COPIES OF M/TEL RELEASES?
Promo requests from print journalists/editors are normally filled, however, we would like to know when and where the article, review or write-up will appear if at all possible.  Unfortunately, if you're requesting promos on behalf of an e-zine or other web type publication or if you're a college radio DJ, we probably won't be able to fill your requests (for the latter, please send us a current playlist and we might be able to do something for ya).  It's nothing personal and your interest is greatly appreciated, but M/TEL is terribly dedicated to helping all of the fine people we issue records for and we're simply not financially or logistically able to continue doing that without your support, too.
