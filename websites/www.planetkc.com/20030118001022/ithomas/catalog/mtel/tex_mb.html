Mitch Mitchell and his Terrifying Experience tear apart the rock paradigm, bringing all of its raw eccentricities into full view: garage, psychedelia, punk and the cosmic spaces in between.  Mitch Mitchell played guitar in the "classic line-up" of Guided By Voices, while other TEX members have been in semi-legendary Ohio underground bands such as: The Method, Pica Huss, Vibralux and Monster Truck 005.  Psych damaged, full-on, rock 'n roll - make no mistake.
