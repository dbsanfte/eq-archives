6.18.02
::Updates!::
 Trighplane Terraforms, our much anticipated 3-way split compilation CD featuring The Magic Carpathians, Six Organs of Admittance and Vibracathedral Orchestra, is well underway and we're working extra hard to have it out before Summer's end.   We're also very happy to announce the involvement of our friend Nemo Bidstrup, who has joined on to design the artwork/sleeves for Trighplane Terraforms.   You may recognize Nemo from the gorgeously hand crafted LP/CD jackets he has created for his own label Time-Lag Records (Charalambides, Drona Parva, Matt Valentine, Songs from the Entoptic Garden) and trust us, his work here is just as remarkable.   Yep, this one continues to amaze and surprise even us!  Stay tuned.


Trighplane Terraforms artist submissions.

M/Tel will have a new 7" out real soon, put together with our North Dakota friends Straphanger.  This limited edition single boasts two exclusive tracks ('The Victory Lap (Goddamn the Circumstance)' b/w 'Glad') of the 'straps uniquely noisome brand of anvil pounding punk rock.  This single was originally scheduled as a tour support release for the 'straps happening-as-of now Midwest tour, however we just couldn't keep those guys away from the Econoline long enough...no matter, we dig it anyway!  Sadly, this single will mark the trio's last outting before drummer Ike Turner (also of Imipolex G fame and heir apparent to the 'socks of Bonham') ships off to teach in Norway.   An official July release date is right around the corner kids, so hold tight.  In the meantime, you can jump over to our mail order catalog and check out a few samples from the 'straps recently released album The Grain Belt.

M/Tel Mail Order is keeping fairly busy these days and we've got a batch of new items in for your listening enjoyment, all accessible now via our on-line catalog.   Recent arrivals and restocks include: Ashtray Navigations 'Tristes Tropiques' CD [Betley Welcomes/Blackbean & Placenta/Rhizome], Ego Summit 'The Room Isn't Big Enough' LP [Old Age/No Age], Guided By Voices 'Universal Truths and Cycles' LP/CD [Matador], Screamers 'In a Better World' DBL CD [Extravertigo], U-Sound Archives, Vol. One DBL CD [Parallelism], Vibracathedral Orchestra 'Lino-Hi' CD [Giardia] and Lexicon Devil 'The Fast Times and Short Life of Darby Crash and The Germs' Book [Feral House].  >>>

Mitch Mitchell and The Terrifying Experience are currently holed up in an undisclosed Ohio basement, writing and recording songs for their next album.  TEX guitarist Duane Hart has formed a new band called Gypsy Witch, who will have a track featured on the upcoming Columbus music CD Cringe.com/pilation Volume #3.  Axle Rose phones Gypsy Witch front man and asks for his lungs back.  TEX bassist Geoff Ortlip has opened a vintage guitar shop in Columbus, OH.

Matt Middleton & The Aesthetics have re-formed under the band's classic My Right to Riches line-up.  They've already played a handful of live gigs around  New Zealand and are rumored to be recording once again as well.  Thurston Moore promptly soils his britches over the news (...it's OK buddy, we did too!).
