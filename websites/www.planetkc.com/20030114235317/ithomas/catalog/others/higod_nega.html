"The Hi-God People make improvised music which draws from sources such as space rock, musique concrete, experimental electronics, and Balinese and Middle Eastern music.  The band consists of Dylan Krasevac (Snawklor, Fong), Dion Nania (Panel of Judges, Golden Lifestyle Band), Greg Wadley (New Waver, Thou Gideon, Tedium, Spill label) and Julian Williams (Solids, Above Ground Pool, From the Same Mother label).  These diverse backgrounds make for an unpredictable outcome.   Instrumentation is sometimes guitar, bass, drums, vocals, percussion and sound effects, but diversifies frequently."

The Hi-God People are the terminally brain-damaged descendants of Van Der Graaf Generator.  In fact, had they existed 30 years ago, HGP would've been mindfucking the very same audience.  These cats know the terrain. Nega the Eight Headed Serpent is a soundtrack for a film that doesn't exist, and by my estimation, it's the greatest film ever made.
