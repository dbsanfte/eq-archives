Columbus Ohio's punk rock progenitors Mike Rep & The Quotas started blasting down their bedroom walls in the early 70's - their debut LP "Rocket to Nowhere" was released in 1975 & marked one of the very first Columbus punk records released.   Mike Rep has since been at the epicenter of Columbus' burgeoning underground music movement & is also well known for 'engineering' (or as Mike prefers 'Lovingly Fucked With') some fantastic records by the likes of: Strapping Fieldhands, Guided by Voices, The Yips & many others.

Featuring two long lost Quotas classics from 1975 & 1976 respectively, "Mama Was a Schitzo/Daddy Was a Vegetable Man" b/w "Rocket Music On" available on this 7" for the first time ever - making it a true piece of Columbus punk history.  This Swedish only release includes liner notes penned by Mike Rep, as well as the legendary Tommy Jay on drums.  All in glorious 100% MONO, too.  Oh yeah, believe it!
