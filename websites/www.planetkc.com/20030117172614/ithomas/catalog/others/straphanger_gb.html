Through the far-off recesses of North Dakota's vast fields of grain, Straphanger glows & sputters like a brand new John Deere Tractor (mean & green).  Of course, it's quite possible this toxic trio's aforementioned 'glow', is an unfortunate bi-product of living in an area that boasts more missile silos than McDonald's...but then again, this could be the very essence of their mutant charm.  Straphanger's first full-length The Grain Belt is packed with noisy 'birds-on-a-wire' guitars, heavy handed thud-stick blues and the hyper-energized rhythmic sprawlings of drummer Ike Turner (of Imipolex-G).  Lovingly crafted with Midwest pride!
