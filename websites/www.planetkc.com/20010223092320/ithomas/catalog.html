The Terrifying Experience (TELE002)
Magnetic Breakthrough
Compact Disc/$12 (post-paid)
The long awaited full-length follow up to 1998's "Supreme Radial" (AAJ),  "Magnetic Breakthrough" marks The Terrifying Experience's triumphant return to the raw deconstruction of traditional rock.  Mitch Mitchell & Co. dish out a full-on temporal assault, using disfigured pop melodies, fuzzed out geetars & some of that good 'ol rock 'n roll panache as their weapons of choice.  It's quickly made obvious that Space and Time are irrelevant in the microcosm of The Terrifying Experience...from the first arena inflicted power-chords on "Woodshed Fist Fight" to their tongue-in-cheek stab at electronica on the closing track "Underground Pressure", "Magnetic Breakthrough" effortlessly breaks down all barriers of the known rock universe and gives birth to something new.

The Electrophonic Foundation
Two Trick Pony - MINWAX00B/CDR - $6 (ppd)
The Reverend Midnight Payne, Meanie, Johnny Pharaoh & Jeff Gargoyle are Kansas City's Electrophonic Foundation: perpetrators of dirty blues influenced Rock & Roll, curators of the street sound, the last great basement evangelists smashing rock testament into microphones and nickel-wound vibrators.   "Two Trick Pony" finds the Electrophonic Foundation chipping away at rock 'n roll's crusted exterior, revealing what's been lying beneath all along, proof that rock itself was born on the Delta.  This compact disc features 25 electrophonified tracks served up LP style: side 1 contains 12 newly recorded "slick ass" studio tracks, featuring foundation favorites like "Mashed Potatoes", plus brand new songs like "Warm Water Method", "White Negro", and "Rock & Roll Motherfucker".  Side 2 contains 12 tracks of bombastic sludge via 4-track demos and live recordings, many of which are being released here for the first time.  Beautifully packaged in a gatefold sleeve including a 7-page booklet, all lovingly assembled by hand just for you.  Limited to 150 pieces, LP style.

The debut CD-R release from our sadly "de-funkt" sister label Minutewax.  A few copies remain and are available through mail-order only.  Features the original EF line-up in all of it's sick & depraved glory.  Due to our low stock on this release, please email for availablity!  Cretin 66 & Turbonegro fans take note!
