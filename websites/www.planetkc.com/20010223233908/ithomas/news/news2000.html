November 20, 2000
TEX - Magnetic Breakthrough release date:
I'm very excited and pleased to announce The Terrifying Experience's new CD, "Magnetic Breakthrough", will be released & available directly from M-Tel on January 4th!  This long awaited follow-up to 1998's Supreme Radial (AAJ) has The Terrifying Experience burning through fifteen new tracks with intense fervor & an unapologetic raw excess.  "Magnetic Breakthrough" will also be available for on-line ordering through Zerotech, plus a few fine distributors & record stores (list forthcoming) as well.  More updates are coming soon...(revised 11/28)

October 16, 2000
Our new Virtual Store-front is open:
Now you can order all Mental Telemetry releases on-line through our virtual store at Zerotec.  Zerotec offers simple & secure (SSL) on-line transactions, so you can shop safely with your credit/debit card.   Zerotec carries releases from quite a few great independent labels like Estrus, Tiger Style, Gearhead & others...it's also ran by independent musicians (our favorite martians Man or Astro-man?) so you know it's alright!  Visit our virtual store by clicking here.

Mental Telemetry Radio/Streaming Audio:
Our streaming audio feed is back on-line & features audio from both M-Tel and Minutewax releases.  You can now access the radio feed through our sounds page.  A sneak preview from the Terrifying Experience's soon-to-be released CD "Magnetic Breakthrough" is now playing, as well as the B-side from their "Anvil Jumper" single.  Minutewax artists Electrophonic Foundation have two cuts from their new CD playing on the feed too!  Note that not all of the bugs have been worked out of the audio feed, so if you have any problems please let me know.  I've tried to optimize the feed so everyone can access it (so the songs are noticeably lo-fi), but I will look into a higher quality stream if any problems are reported.  Thanks & enjoy!
