ORDERING ON-LINE
SHOP @ ZEROTEC

Zerotec is a great way to find and purchase M/tel releases on-line (via secure server).  In addition to our records, Zerotec offers a huge catalog of independent/micro-label/direct-from-artist records (i.e. Amish, Estrus, Kranky, My Pal God, Touch 'n Go...just to name a few!), zines and digital file downloads for your perusal.  Zerotec accepts all major credit cards and the people behind this burgeoning operation (perhaps Man or Astroman? rings a bell?) are really nice, too!  Buy stuff from them often.

M/TEL ON-LINE: If you'd rather use a credit card to order M/tel releases directly from the source, or if you're purchasing some of the OTHER TITLES featured in our catalog, we can do that using PayPal.  We now offer two very simple ways to send on-line payments via PayPal:

PAYPAL SHOPPING CART
By far the easiest and quickest way to shop through our website:

1. Add catalog items to your shopping cart by clicking the ADD TO CART button.
2. Add postage to your shopping cart by using the Postage Chart below.
3. Follow the remaining steps through PayPal check-out.

Example: if you are ordering 1 CD/7"/Magazine or Cassette for shipment via US First Class Mail:
1. Add the CD to your shopping cart by clicking the ADD TO CART button located on the catalog page for that title.
2. Refer to Postage Chart and click the ADD $2.00 button to add appropriate postage to your order.
3. Check-out.

Example: if you are ordering 4 items including an LP/12" for shipment via Europe Air Mail Post:
1. Add the 4 items to your shopping cart by clicking the ADD TO CART buttons for each title.
2. Refer to Postage Chart and click the ADD $7.00 button to add appropriate postage to your order.
3. Check-out.

PAYPAL BY E-MAIL
To order titles online using PayPal by e-mail:

1. Calculate your order total from the prices listed in our catalog (on a piece of paper or in yr head).
2. Calculate your total postage costs using the Postage Chart below.
3. Add it all up and send the total amount to our PayPal payment address "ian@mentaltelemetry.com".
4. Be sure to tell us what you're ordering and where we need to ship it!

For orders containing an LP/12", the LP/12" should always count as the first item.  Double items count as two items (except where noted), and so on.  Once your payment is processed through PayPal, you will receive a confirmation email from M/Tel and your order will ship out immediately.  Write to us if you have any problems whatsoever or if you need further assistance using ordering on-line, using PayPal, etc.  We're always glad to help!

ORDERING BY MAIL
Ah, the lost art of ordering records by mail...still our favorite way to shop!  Must be yrs, too?  Right-on then...let's go!

ORDERING/ORDER FORM: You can use our shopping cart system to order by mail as well!  Simply fill up your cart, add postage/handling, print out the 'shopping cart' page once you're done shopping and send it along as your order form (be sure to include your mailing address, too!).  Of course, if you'd prefer to keep the mail ordering experience purely nostalgic: print out this order form, fill it out completely - totaling up your order, adding postage/handling charges (see Postage Chart) and including your shipping address - and mail it, along with your payment, to us at the address below.

FORMS OF PAYMENT: We accept money orders, personal checks (US funds only), cash (sent @ own risk) & major credit cards via PayPal.  Checks should be made out to Mental Telemetry.   We do not accept international checks, please use a money order instead...thank you!

MISSOURI RESIDENTS/STATE SALES TAX: Please add an additional 6.75% to your mail order total - 'cos the taxman says so!  If you order using the PayPal shopping cart, sales tax is added automatically.

PAYMENT/SHIPPING INFO
PAYMENT INFO: Please allow 1-week for personal checks to clear, send well concealed cash at your own risk, money orders are preferred for the quickest turnaround.

DELIVERY TIMES: Allow 4-6 weeks for delivery!  Under normal circumstances, mail orders are filled within 1-week upon receipt/clearing of payment and PayPal orders typically ship out as soon as we receive your order (1-3 days).  It rarely takes us much longer, but please allow a maximum of 4-6 weeks.  If you need to contact us regarding your order status, write or call (816-454-5953).

POSTAL INSURANCE: All orders are packed with tender loving care, however, Mental Telemetry cannot be held responsible for damage caused by the Post Office (bent corners, cracked cases, etc.) or for packages lost by the USPS.   Insurance is available upon request and comes highly recommended for LP shipments or larger/more expensive orders.

OTHER TITLES
Other label releases in our catalog are available from M/tel through direct-mail order or on-line purchase via PAYPAL.  Prices of all 'other' items will vary from title to title, so if you're ordering/paying by mail-order, please check catalog prices before sending payment.

Keep in mind that we stock very limited quantities of 'other' titles, but try our best to stay current with the stock on hand.  If a title is out of stock temporarily, it will be noted as [backordered] in the catalog.  A [backordered] title simply means that we've sold-out of all copies on hand for that particular title and we're currently trying to get a hold of more copies.   If a title is completely sold out or we're unable to further order/stock copies of that title, you will be notified by e-mail and your order will be canceled.  If necessary, your money will be promptly refunded or you can opt for an M/tel credit note as well.  With that said, it's always a good idea to nab what you want while it's in stock.

PLACING ITEMS ON HOLD: We will gladly hold items on your behalf for 1 week, however, please be sure to send us an email (or give us a call) confirming that you wish to have those item(s) placed on hold.  We cannot guarantee item(s) will be held for you unless we've received definite confirmation from you first.
