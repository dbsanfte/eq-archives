How to Register on EZ Board

  • Click register your free account.
  • Choose global account.
  • Fill out the form and its required fields.
  • Activate account by clicking on the link sent to you in your e-mail.
  • You will need to enable your cookies.

How to Enable cookies

  • On your browser Menu bar - Go to privacy.
  • Choose Manage websites.
  • Type in ezboard.com
  • Hit ALLOW.

 

 

How to get into Lodgings once you are a member

 

  • You must be logged in to do so.
  • You must have cookies enabled.
  • An EF administrator must grant you rights or someone must tell you the password.
  • Send your EZBoard login to ursi_major2002@yahoo.com to get this process going.

How to set up a Magelo

 

  • go to http://www.magelo.com.
  • Hit register. Fill out the form.
  • Send form and activate it by email.
  • Login.
  • Hit Characters on the left hand navigation bar.
  • Follow instructions from there.

How to Set up a signature/icon etc.

  • Have cookies enabled for this website.
  • Click on my control center at the top of the page.
  • Go to My Account
  • Click on Account prefrences.
