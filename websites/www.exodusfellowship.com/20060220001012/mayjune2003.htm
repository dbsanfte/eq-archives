6/18/03 - The End of an Era... And the Welcoming of Friends

I am both saddened and excited to bring you the
following news. It is with a heavy heart that I tell
you that Circle of Shields is disbanding. Many of
them have fought and died with us, and many of us have
joined them in their battles. The guild has made it's
mark on Norrath, and it will be missed.

As an ally, the High Council feels that we should do
everything in our power to assist them through this
troubling time. If Exodus ever disbanded, my biggest
grief would be knowing that friends have split apart.
We wish to help Circle of Shields avoid this.
Therefore, it is with open arms that Exodus Fellowship
welcomes many of our allies into our own ranks.

As a celebration of the merger, we will be meeting in
the Lake Rathe Arena on Friday, 6pm EST. At this
time, many of our allies will become our guildmates.
Please try to attend and join the celebration.

The period following the merger may be difficult for
several of the new members. I would ask that each of
you make a special effort to get to know everyone and
make them feel welcome.

Thank you, and ALL HAIL CIRCLE OF SHIELDS!
