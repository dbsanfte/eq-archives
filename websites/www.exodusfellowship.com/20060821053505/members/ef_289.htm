Below is a list of members with screenshots that they may add to their pages on Guild Magic. To add your picture to Guild Magic go to their website and proceed to your "My User" page, then click on "edit" in the character section. Enter the exact web address into the "Character Image" box, scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on "Update Character". Your picture should now appear when you click on your character from the Roster.
You may also create a link from Guild Magic to your profile page (if you have one, that is!). To find out the address of your page, go to your profile page and then do the following...

If you use Internet Explorer for your browser, right click on a blank part of the page, (not over a picture) select "properties" and a box will pop up showing you the correct address for the page.

For Netscape users, go to your page, right click and select "Open frame in new window". The address for that page will now be displayed in the address box at the top of the newly opened page.

Enter this address (or URL into the "Character website" box on your page at Guild Magic. Then yer done!
