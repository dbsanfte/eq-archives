1/03/02 - Exodus Fellowship initiates new Awards program And announces the Exodus Honor Guard!

Ursi receives Medal of Honor...

In recognition of her tireless devotion to this guild and her fellow guildmates, Ursi Major is our first recipient of our highest honor, The Medal of Honor. Not only has she always been available to lend a helping hand, she often puts the needs of others before herself.
She has also been involved in putting together many guild events as well as recruiting quite a fair share of new members! Ursi has always gone above and beyond the call of duty, and she is most deserving of this medal.

Ursi is also the first member of what will be a second tier of officers, known as the Exodus Honor Guard. More news will follow on both the Honor Guard and our awards program over the next week. So keep watching this space for the latest details.
And please congratulate Ursi and wish her the best when you see her in Norrath.
