4/28/02 - The best guild event ever!

I have planned out the biggest guild event to date! (i hope) I have designed it so that all levels (16+) will be able to participate, with the proper group set up. Everyone will get xp, and there will be a buffing/rez station to assist everyone! The main goal will be to spawn the Ancient Titans. I need some of their drops to complete a quest, and ursi wants a robe from the Panda Titan.

Here is my plan....

We will all travel to stormbrunt mountains. The mobs in the zone range from levels 15-45. Most of the High Council and other high level members will form a safe base. The medium level members (30-40) will form at least two groups known as Death Squads. The lower level members will form up groups known as Brute Squads. Each group will be set up to allow all members to recieve xp.

The safe base is there for you to come to for buffs / heals / rezes, and to bring any unwanted mobs you may have agroed along the way. The two Death squads will circle the Safe base killing pandas and monkeys to get the two Titans to spawn (monkey and bear) that i need for my quest. some of these placeholders were blue to me at level 40. The brute squads will camp the kobold camps for xp, and can pull higher level mobs from around them if needed.

Because the Death squads will be on the move alot, i feel they should be led by rangers to track down their prey quickly. This is important because the Death squads will be the driving force behind the Titan spawn, by killing the placeholders. I would like to hand pick Tanluin and Thallus as group leaders for the two death squads, and let them set up their groups as they see fit. Remember, everyone in your groups must get xp! Contact them prior to the event if you want to be part of a Death Squad!

Talk among yourselves to decide who will form up a Brute squad. Post here the groups that you decide, not to mention support for this event!

I have made a map of the zone, with some drawings to help illustrate the plan. just cklick on the link. As you can see, everything is near the river so it wont be hard to find each other. Just follow the river! I will be porting the day before and the morning of, i will also be escorting people through the warrens for an hour or so before the event starts. This event is scheduled for May 12 (Sunday), at 11:30 EST and should last about 4 Hours. If there are any suggestions or comments/questions, post here or send me an email at cobokelvar@yahoo.com
