Welcome New Members...

1. By applying to our guild, we assume you have read and agreed to our Bylaws. If you haven't please take this time to familiarize yourself with them

2. You are entitled to our tradeskills special. Namely out tradeskillers will make an attempt at an item if you come to them with ALL of the components necessary to make that item. Example: You bring Herbdiva a HQ bear pelt and pattern and she makes an attempt at creating a 10 slot 10% weight reduction backpack. We are not responsible if you lose your ingredients in a failed attempt.

3. Guild vault items. If you receive an item from the vault – please pass it on to another Exodus Guildie or return it to Bagaloot when you are through with it.

4. If you have an issue or complaint with another member of this guild, please keep accusatory remarks off of our message board.
