There may be times when you have an urgent or personal matter for one or all of the members of the High Council or another Officer of the guild, be it a Sage or Honor Guard. You may not be able to catch up with them in Norrath and the issue may be too sensitive to be in "open chat" in the forum. Please feel free to contact any of the High Council Members ingame. Please know that your issue may be discussed among all of the HC members unless you request that it not be. In any event, the HC will do their best to help you with whatever problems you may have. Remember, this is your guild, and everyone should be able to have their say.
