Exodus Fellowship is about unity and freedom.

Inside its ranks, all are equal and all are free. Each member is just as important as the next and all are protected by each other. This is what makes us strong. Guild representatives fill our leadership positions and our important decision making processes are left to a majority vote by the High Council. The High Council is entrusted to listen to the concerns, ideas and suggestions of the general Fellowship. Everyone gets a say in the activities of the guild and everyone is equal. All members are encouraged to bring forth their ideas for the betterment and growth of the guild. This fundamental principle is what our guild was founded on, and by upholding this principle we will ensure our strength in the future. Now, we must move forward to spread freedom and liberate the oppressed. The goal of Exodus Fellowship is to band together against tyranny in any form.

If you desire to join our Fellowship, we ask that you have attained your 25th season, and have familiarized yourself with our Bylaws. We also ask that you register with Guild Magic and select Exodus Fellowship as your guild, so that you will appear on our roster. Feel free to contact any of our officers with any concerns or questions.

Signed - Exodus Fellowship
