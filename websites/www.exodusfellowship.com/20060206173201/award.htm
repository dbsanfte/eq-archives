These are the awards that can be attained by members of The Exodus Fellowship. Some are difficult to attain, while others are easy to receive. In order to receive an award here, one must be nominated by another member of the Fellowship, once submitted it's then presented to the High Council for approval and voting. Awards can also be received directly from the High Council Leader.

 

 

High Council Leader

Speaks for itself, is only worn by the Guild Master. Awarded ONLY by Guild Master in change of Leadership.

Awarded to: Herbdiva

Honor

This is attained when a member has gone above and beyond the call of duty in all facets of guild life. This is our guild's highest honor and shall not be given out lightly. To be awarded by the High Council Leader only. (Rare)

Awarded to: Ursi, Tanluin, Kamdar

Leadership

This is attained when a member shows exceptional leadership qualities. This award can be given ONLY by the High Council Leader. Officers only. (Rare)

Awarded to: Arri, Kamdar, Cobo, Arathain

Sacrifice

This is attained when a member has sacrificed his/her life for that of another member or non-member so that he/she may live. If a non-member was spared, that person must post it on our message board of the situation in detail.

Awarded to: Macolic, Sereni

Excellence

This is attained when a member has shown him/herself to be an exceptional Role-Player. It can also be awarded when a member has demonstrated a high level of proficiency in his/her class and has shared their knowledge with their fellow guildmates.

Awarded to: Chonn, Agradeleous, Arathain

Recruiter

This is attained when a member has shown his/her outstanding ability to recruit quality new members into the The Exodus Fellowship.

Awarded to: Ursi

Conduct

This is attained when a member shows a continuous attitude of kindness towards others unselfishly, and a willingness to help those in need. It can also be awarded to members that have shown great enthusiasm and a winning attitude. And for the ability to follow all orders set out by the Guild Leader and its Officers and doing so without dispute.

Awarded to: Herbdiva, Rathgard, Nilknul, Warden, Chastisy, Ursi, Gilgamesh, Daliddar, Sindee, Boffle

Participation

This is attained when a member has gone "above and beyond" in attending raids and events, especially those events that help their fellow guildmates.

Awarded to:

Trademaster

This medal is awarded when a member has demonstrated a high level of proficiency in a trade skill such as Jewel Craft, Fletching, Tailoring, Pottery, Smithing, Cooking, Tinkering, Brewing, Alchemey or Poison Craft, and willfully helps others with their skill with a passion unsurpassed by a god.

Awarded to: Thallus

Contribution

This medal can be attained by donating items to the guild armory or to other members. This can also be attained through the act of donating your time and energy to specific guild duties for the benefit of your fellow guildmates or by contributing money to the webmaster for the upkeep of this website.

Awarded to: Tanluin, Arri, Mantrena, Arathain, Kamdar, Macolic, Herbdiva, Niniane, Tacomir, Ashmael, Boffle, Chonn

Rescue

Awarded for pulling member's bodies out of extremely dangerous stituations, for recovery purposes, with little or no concern for his/her own safety.

Awarded to: Ashmael, Xxax, Chastisy

Epic

This is attained when a member has completed his/her epic quest. This medal is awarded by High Council Leader ONLY. If you receive this award you have reached a very high level of Mastery and will be respected by all.

Awarded to: Xxax, Ursi, Cobo, Rathgard, Kumtha

Dragon Slayer

This is attained when a member has killed a Dragon (grouped). Must be witnesses to this event and screenshots. This medal is awarded by High Council Leader ONLY.

Awarded to: Thallus, Mayla, Daliddar, Ursi, Sindee, Berina, Ashmael, Jobeman, Matthan, Cobo, Xxax, Tacomir, Kumtha, Magdilyn, Timmai, Niniane, Ursi, Taniana, Rathgard

Lemming

This medal is attained by a member that repeatedly walks blindly into situations that get them killed. To be given to members with a good sense of humor only! Members may nominate themselves for this award!

Awarded to: Chonn, Gilgamesh, Chastisy, Ashmael, Tanluin
