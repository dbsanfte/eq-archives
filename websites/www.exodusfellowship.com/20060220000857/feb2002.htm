2/19/02 - Guild Magic Wants You!

Or rather, I want all of you in Guild Magic! I don't know if any of yop are aware of this site, but it's a pretty good one, and many guilds use it. It's a complete guild and character database that you can update yourself. When you ding or improve your level in a skill, you can log on and change the info yourself. This thing keeps track of everything from awards to alternates, and you can even get your characyer's picture on there. Hopefully down the road this will save me untold hours of updating the website. Eventually I hope to incorporate Guild Magic into our own site as many other guilds have. The only trade off is, we have to adapt to those crazy Guild Magic colors of blue and pink! But, they may have a fix for that in the future.

So here's what I need eveyone to do...
Go to www.guildmagic.com
Create a username and password. This does not have to be your character's name. Then Add your characters to Guild Magic. You can put in as much detail on your character as you wish... the more the better. Then you can add him/her to a guild. Uh, pick Exodus Fellowship, ok?! You won't appear on the roster right away, I have to accept you first. Then yer done!

I know this is a little extra work to ask of you all, but the website has become a rather monstrous undertaking, and I'm hoping to make it easier for all of us down the road.

2/03/02 - Get your votes in!

Hail Exodus Fellowship! I thank you for your participation regarding the nomination process of the initial class for our Exodus Honor Guard. In no particular order the finalists are as follows: Arri, Ashmael, Macolic, Herbdiva, Niarki, Lanashay, Qural, Foreest, and Chonn.
Please email up to two votes to Tanluin_Atrackin@yahoo.com.
The role of the Exodus Honor Guard is to assist the High Council in expanding our ranks (ie recruiting). By installing the Exodus Honor Guard we are allowing ourselves to reach more classes and levels within Norrath in an effort to make our Guild the best it can be. The High Council will remain the governing body of the guild and as always remains open minded to your concerns and opinions.

If you are a finalist and you are not interested in the position please send me an email or send me/other High Council member a tell in-game stating such. You will not be considered regardless of the voting results if this is the case. The High Council respects your feelings and would not want to place you in a position you do not want.

The voting period will be about 2 weeks in duration and dependant on your participation. If I'm still recieving votes the polls will remain open. We will post in Forum and on MOTD when the voting period is closed.
