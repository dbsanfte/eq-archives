Slip me some green backs, Jack,

Drop that napalm.

Carcinogens pay for my rent and my beer.

My collar is blue and I'm poisoned, I fear.

Industrial blessings,

Turkey in his dressings,

Lepers Kiss

The social machine has rusted pipes,
Emitting steam in a loathsome mist.
In your head the day resounds
Like a prize fight fist.
The stadium gawks as lepers kiss.

The mallet pounds
in verbs and nouns,
Cowering as the leopards hiss.
Cock a brow as lepers kiss.
Stagnant, repugnant,
Total abyss.
Tongue the gun as lepers kiss.
