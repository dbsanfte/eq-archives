MISSION

The Underground Crawlspace Review is a non-funded, independent literary forum for social change.  This e-zine does not pander to corporate entities for handouts, fame, power, or status. 

The Underground Crawlspace Review prefers to publish unknown or little known artists, with a definite bias toward anything noncomformist, controverisal, or abrasive. The Underground Crawlspace Review runs counter to and seeks to wreck the prevailing trends of religious conservatism, corporate conformity, 'alternative' hucksterism, and ecological suicide.
