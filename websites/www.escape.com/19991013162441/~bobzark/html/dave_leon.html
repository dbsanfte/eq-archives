the ashen me


the ash in me
is piling up
i am so burned out
i am so full of it.

the ashen me walks down the street
to where and what i cannot say.
it must be all the ash in me
that causes me to wander this way.

in between the love i have watched my world die
hit my head against the dash doing nearly sixty-five
stared at those pictures of you until i nearly went blind
trying to drown the fire with a bottle of stolen wine.

the ash in me
is smothering me
and i am thinking of getting out
maybe somewhere south of this hell.

the ashen me talks to a prostitute
with bleach blond hair and a missing tooth
and when she smiles i look for you
but shes not you so i just make do.

the ashen me
oh what a tragedy.
arrested in alleys for pissing without caution
into the careless wind.
with the ash in me i know i can never win.
and the ashen me
is even giving up on losing gracefully.
 

untitled


tonight the high school class is graduating
and i am feeling weak.
i can hear them cruising main
the girls sloppy drunk on two wine coolers.

i am going to bed bloated on beer feeling sad
that some of them will turn out like me
or my golden whores who scam champagne
and do what they have to do to survive.

what a let down its going to be
for them to be in our company

my face

my face
eyes of whorehouse doom
a crooked smile that embraces the moon

i am sorry that
i am so ugly but i
can not help it.

oh and baby, if i could
i would be your james dean forever,
but scars like criminals
rule my face
and fight for their turf
and eyes hide a scared soul
in the attic.

cigarette after cigarette
i try to forget
without success

the mirror reminds me
everytime i
stumble to the john to take a piss
