Ideas B4 Censorship: Spring 99
 

Don't be scared. 

Things new, novel or strange do scare people.  OK, some people.

But your not one of those, are you?  Otherwise you wouldn't be here, right?

Well now that's not true is it?  You could be anybody.  You could be a really obtuse sonofabitch, even worse than the prosecutor down in Largo Florida. 

Then again it'd be hard to find anyone anywhere more backward than that.  Except for maybe the armed garbagemen postercops, back roaming the streets of New York. What to do if you get fined for postering.
