Baby,
there's nothing to be afraid of
in the dark
'cept me.

Your blood
is like red red roses in September
so pure and
so sweet.

I'm
going to bleed you
like fine strawberry wine
into a clean cup.

My hands
urn for you like a lover
at the midnight hour
so alone.

I'd love
to know the way you are
with death around
your neck.

Your face
pale as a weak winter's day
drained of all life like
fine Christmas ash.

Your body
pulled tight like heavy cable;
bloated white worms writhing
beneath you skin.

Your voice
shattered and splintered
caught like broken glass
in your throat.

I'm nobody special
just that guy who's gonna
cut you wide open and leave you
like Thanksgiving.

Just that guy
Nobody special.
