Catalog Overview
 

The Panic Button Pr_est online catalog lists books, cassettes, stickers, t-shirts, underground ephemera.

A lot of crud which now passes for alterna-significant is made by teenagers who live with their parents and who can afford to fling their bohemian loogis. Who cares? We sure don't.

These mixtures of music, illustration and words render frightening, unwholesome realities... these are the stories of people and places usually associated with the dark side. 

This online catalog is organized in 3 sections: yeah you got it, books, cassettes, and order form. Each page gives you the low down on an individual item; hit the next button or the lydia icon to advance to the next page.

Happy Motoring!
