Old Geezer Party!
Tuesday, May 29, 2001 9:03 AM PDT by SlimWit
Come one, come all to the Final Old Geezer TFC party. Help us wish our good friend, TFC, a happy future as we go our separate ways (competitively, anyway). Sunday, June 3rd, starting at 5pm Pacific.

Drop by our IRC channel (Instructions) to get the final Server Details. We'll be playing on a wide variety of maps which we've put on the Web site for you to download.

Even if you don't/can't play TFC, please stop by the IRC channel and give us grief. We'd certainly enjoy your company.
