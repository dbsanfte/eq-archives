Cautiously like a predator, while our enemies killed themselves. After a few minutes of silence we move out on the final attack. Cint got shot but was able to communicate with me where he was. Lt.Sam killed the enemy while Dent and Wild Flanked the other side. With 2 CGI and no AOW left alive, the Geezers attacked.
