The Geezers met URF on the field of battle for another STA face-off. We've met URF before and they whupped us. Since old men have nothing much to do in life (except shine their dentures), we practiced a little more for this match.

Round 1 started out with both teams in a mad rush for the flag. URF and OGzr both got early touches. These touches continued and many a cap was had by both teams. As the round wore on, URF once again showed us they were in charge, ending the round with a 190-160 loss.

Round 2 was where the gloves came off. Then the Geezers put some ace bandages in their place, 'cause hey... we're old. Anyway, the Geezers started with a 3 scout, 1 medic rush that blew through their defense. URF sent a similar team our way. This round had a nearly continuous stream of caps for the Geezers, and a slightly slower stream for URF. The Geezers gummed their enemies to a round win at 250 to 130, for a match total win of 410 to 320.

Thank you URF for the fun match. You were all gentlemen, and a delight to play. We look forward to meeting you again.
