BOOYAH!!!!
November 28, 2005 - 7:57AM by Stilgar

11/27/2005
OGzr met 57th on the battlefield of Karkand... and OGzr kicked some butt.



Taking the USMC side first, OGzr attacked en masse at the entrance to the MEC city. 57th stood their ground, but not in enough numbers and OGzr quickly overwhelmed the defenders. OGzr pressed their advantage and 57th responded; but to no avail. OGzr was eventually victorious by attacking and overwhelming 57th defenders, 25-0.

After a brief respite and the always enjoyable TK spree, OGzr donned the gear of the camel jockeys, errr... MEC force. With a set strategy in mind, OGzr blasted away at the determed 57th force. The USMC squad was bound and determined to sneak around the OGzr flank and force a multi-dimensional conflict, but OGzr would have none of it. Though OGzr surrendered the Market Place CP, with an unmatched ferocity and acts of attrition, they blasted 57th 45-0.

It was a glorious victory for OGzr. A hard fought match against a determined, skilled foe. We are honored to have performed at such a level versus 57th and look forward to next season's play!



Proverbial Red-headed Stepchild
November 3, 2005 - 2:20PM by Stilgar

11/2/05
OGzr met up with MA (Mature Asskickers) on the streets of Mashtur City. MA proved their name fitting as they methodicially, and with a mature sense of gameplay, kicked OGzr's ass all over the town.



OGzr had a couple of peeps drop and the TS server connectivity cease right aas the match was to start. MA was gracious enough to let OGzr get their act together, thus the "mature sense of gameplay". Once the match started, OGzr jumped out early and took key SPs... bu MA proved the 'A' in their name fit, and they beat the ancient ones like they owed them money! Round one was actually close for a very long time, but MA ground OGzr down for a MA (USMC) 112-0 victory.

After some incredibly enjoyable, thoroughly therapudic TK'ing between rounds, it was OGzr's turn to assume the role of USMC. This time, the Geezers didn't start so fast. They got peeps in position, but the tactics got crossed... emphasis was placed on setting up defense, rather than taking the SP THEN defending. It cost OGzr dearly; MA swooped into position and took the SPs. With spawn points throughout the city, MA assaulted OGzr wherever they popped up their silver-haired heads (chrome-domed, in the case of Anim8). It was ugly. MA took ALL SPs, and slaughtered the stragglers... no mercy... MA (MEC) takes an easy win in short time, 162-0.

GGS MA! You proved to the Geezers why you're numero uno in the BEML rankings. We'd stand and salute you, but our brittle old arses are crushed.



V Is For Victory!
October 26, 2005 - 9:40PM by Stilgar

10/26/05
OGzr locked & loaded, took to the battlefield of Kubra Dam against a determined =AO=. The fighting was fierce - in many cases, hand to hand.



OGzr took the USMC role first and quickly took control of the MEC Intake base. This gave the Geezers the advantage of holding BOTH airfields. The ancient ones then swiftly attacked and took the Lower Dam; insuring a hold on the primary landmark. =AO= was determined to take back their airfield, and they sent wave upon wave of cannon fodder into the fray. The Geezers mowed them down, but made the mistake of pulling away from the flag - giving it back to =AO=. The Geezers were back on the offensive, and eventually took the intake once more, but at the expense of the lower dam. Still, it was enough to insure victory: OGzr 139, =AO= 118.

After a brief respite, the roles were reversed: OGzr took the role of defending MEC, while =AO= became the agressor USMC. =AO='s initial approach was an all-out air assault, but it quickly became apparent that OGzr was dug in too deep... it would have to be a war of attrition. =AO= shifted tactics and soon they were mounting combined armor and infantry assaults on the OGzr defenses. The tide waxed and waned, but eventually OGzr was able to hold on for a very slight victory in round two: 122-118.

For the match, OGzr was victorious: 261-236. A tough, close, well-fought game. Many thanks to =AO=! We had a blast. You guys are always a great opponent.



Old Geezers Battle 57th at the Dalian Plant
October 20, 2005 - 4:35PM by Stilgar

10/19/2005
OGzr boarded their aircraft carrier (USS Geritol) and set way for the Dalian coast off mainland China, where 57th awaited. Once in position, OGzr launched jets and helicopters to assault the PRC defenses.



The initial attack for OGzr went well - they took all but the main entrance SP and jumped to an early 30+ point lead. The 57th PRC forces rallied late and repelled the OGzr aggressors. Once pushed back out to sea, OGzr had a rough go of retaking the land bases. In the end, time ran out before OGzr could manage the ticket bleed. 57th wins round 1 64-16.

After a short respite, it was 57th's turn to assault the defending PRC OGzrs. Turns out that's not the ancient ones' strength. 57th jumped on OGzr early and never let up.

57th's helos were swarming, their armor was concentrated, and their artillery accurate, keeping OGzr pinned-down. The ticket bleed was tremendous and in the end, it was 57th victorious 116-0. Ouch.

Overall it was a very fun and exciting match. We look forward to our next battle with 57th! GGs
