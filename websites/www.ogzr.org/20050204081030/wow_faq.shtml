World of WarCraft FAQ


Q. What server?
A. We play on the Argent Dawn server.

Q. Alliance or Horde?
A. We are an Alliance only guild.

Q. Why role-play server?
A. The expected level of maturity by WoW's creator, Blizzard Entertainment, is higher and on PvP or PvE and enforced by Game Masters. We feel this server type best matches our Charter and helps us get more qualified recruits. See Blizzard's Role-Play Server Policy for more information.

Q. You guys seem fun but I am younger than 25. Can I join you?
A. Yes, but not as a Guild member. When you're on our World of WarCraft server, if you want to meet or group with us, check the "ogzr" channel (/join ogzr). If any of us are playing, we will also be on that channel.
