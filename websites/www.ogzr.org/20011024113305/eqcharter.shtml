Guild Charter
  • No Begging.
  • If you see a fellow Guild member online, chat and join with them as a group, if possible.
  • Any items that may benefit others should be put up for auction to the guild first before the general populous.
  • Everyone needs at least one Trade Skill.  If you ask another Guild member to make an item for you, do as much as you can to help.  For example, get the material for the maker for  the item you want.  If the maker fails:  Hard
    Luck.  Try again if you have more material or go out and find more.
  • All new members must meet the Old Geezer prerequisites found on the Join Us page.  Membership will be offered when all existing Guild members agree to the new membership.
  • New members will be subject to a two to three week probation period.
  • Every week there will be an update in the Forum to see how everyone is progressing.  If you have not played or have not gained a lever, it is not necessary to post.
  • If you find any good quests, record them in the Forum with as many details as possible to enable others to attempt the quest.
  • At all times, we will strive to demonstrate to our all, the following qualities:

    Nobility of heart - so that dishonor never taints our reputation.

    Gallantry of spirit - helping those in trouble, defending the weak, and putting others first.

    Respect for others - always remembering that people's time is precious, and never forgetting the person behind the character.

    Courtesy - treating other people as we would wish to be treated, never nagging or making repeated requests for assistance, and taking the time to be polite.

    Integrity - being honest in our dealings with others, both inside and
    outside the guild.

    Loyalty - Devotion to the guild/clan and our guild/clan mates

    Fun - That is what the clan and the guild is all about so go out there and have fun!
