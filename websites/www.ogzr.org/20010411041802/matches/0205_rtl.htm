Ouch. Ride The Lightning used to be in Silver and tonight they showed us why. We were looking forward to playing them because we had heard about their new "just for fun" attitude, and we thought we might actually put up a decent showing. Oops.

Round 1
RTL scored a very steady stream of caps, taking a commanding lead early and never letting up. We struggled against their D, but were finally able to drag out a cap. Yay, matches like this make you happy for the small things. Their O was very efficient, and our O never found a rhythm. Round 1 final: 15-195.

Round 2
I think RTL eased up a bit on us as they didn't score nearly as much this round. That was nice of them. We managed to pull one flag to their balcony but they got it to return. Grr. We did finally get another flag out for a cap. Round 2 final: 10-45.

GG RTL. I think we learned a few things.
