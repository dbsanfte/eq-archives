Join Old Geezers


The Old Geezers Clan is about maturity and fun.  A great way to get to know us and our gaming goals is to visit us on our IRC channel or register on our forum and post.

In order to join, you need to meet these simple requirements:

  • 25 years old and/or act like it on a consistent basis

  • Do what you say, and say what you can do

  • No whining

There may be additional gaming requirements (i.e. practice attendance, meeting participation, etc.) to be part of a gaming squad as defined individually by each squad.
