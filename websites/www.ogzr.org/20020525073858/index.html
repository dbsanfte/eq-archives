Another scrim under our belts...
May 9, 2002 - 8:13PM by Sentry Killer

...and we get a lesson learned in the process!

Our scrim partner was (MA), Mature Asskickers, and an ass-kicking is what we got! The scrim helped us to welcome MA into the TIW program with BEML, as well as to get some good games going!

Started on Harvester(x), where the MA Offense just kept coming and coming... old bodies tried to defend as best they could, but apparently our Depends gave out, and MA took the map by a shutout / cap out.
Next was Sanctuary, and we were warned that MA was good at capping out this map. The old ones held their ground, and only allowed a few caps after the bomber of doom hovered overhead. This map goes to MA also.
Final map was Slapdash(x). After losing a few Geezers to sleepiness, the game was on. With a fresh set of Depends and Viagra in our veins, the old ones were able to pull off a series of caps and get a shutout of their own.

Great games were had, and we appreciate MA allowing us to host on our server. It got a workout! We look forward to our next matchup with them.


Another TWL victory for the Old Geezers
May 9, 2002 - 10:37AM by Aarvid

OGzr Celebrates first TWL Victory!
April 30, 2002 - 7:42PM by Tusk

The Old Geezers faced off with EW (Elite Warriors) Tuesday Night for thier first TWL Match. Maps played were Quagmire - x and Sanctuary with Riverdance as the scheduled tie breaker.

The OGzr Leadership proved that one can teach Old Dogs new tricks. The modified Quagmire map worked well with the Old Men launching out a fast assault while the Defense deployed and parked a Juggernaut on the flag to stay! The persistant EW Offense managed a few grabs, but couldn't pull off the full carry home. OGzr capped a shutout on EW 6-0.

The next map was Sanctuary and once again (after a few Geritol pills and Ensure chasers) the Old Farts were up to the task. EW came in hot and quick, taking down OGzr's gens and sending the D scrambling. EW was kind enough to return our flag after taking home for a moment. Not bad for being a few players down. The caps however, ended there. OGzr Offense redoubled efforts and pulled off 4 complete flag carries before time ran out. 4-1 OGzr win.

Thanks for coming out EW!
