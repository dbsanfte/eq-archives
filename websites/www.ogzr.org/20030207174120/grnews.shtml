]RS[ Dominates RRBridge and Caves
January 30, 2003 - 9:10PM by Tracker6

The Geezers met up with Recon Battalion ]RS[ for some 6v6 combat tonight...early indications suggested the wheelchair-bound gunshooters would command the battlefield...but alas, the Geritol/Viagra/Motrin wore off fast!

[OGzr] managed a tactical win on its choice of maps for Round 1: RRBridge. Coming off of a very productive training camp with Clan FR last week, the Geezers immediately put into action its position strategy. The squad went up 1, then went even; went up 1, then went back to even; and finally, went up for the third time of the round, and managed to win on time. JR had an impressive 3 kills (2 shots, one grenade).

The following two rounds of RRBridge went progressively worse for the Geezers...in fact, Round 3 was a genuine slaughter (all six Geezers killed, all six Recon Battalion shooters sill alive)! Indeed, Recon Battalion adapted well to the opening round, and ended up dominating the map.

The final two rounds played on Recon Battalion's choice of maps, Caves. Round 4 was ugly; the Old Ones had poor spawn placement, and Recon Battalion showed their true mastery of sniping from what seemed like all directions (PaleRider scored the only kill). The final round was much more even, but [OGzr] was no match for the enemy snipers.

The high quality of their Clan webpage (http://recon.rabidd.com ) turned out to be quite indicative of the high quality of their Ghost Recon game play. Recon Battalion is a professional team, we look forward to a future firefight soon.


*AP Serves OGzr
January 11, 2003 - 11:30PM by Tracker6

The Geezers were handed a plate of ownage by the American Patriots *AP tonight. The first two rounds on *AP's choice of maps (Village) went dreadfully fast. Only [OGzr]Viagra showed any promise with the sole Geezer kill during Round #1, and staying alive for the longest in Round #2 (though, all five Geezers took a dirt nap after less than two minutes of gameplay!).

Round #3 on OGzr's choice (Plantation) showed signs of promise. The Geezers were able to cap 2 Patriots, and movement/control of the map was better coordinated. By Round #4, [OGzr] was truly warmed up...the squad locked down the map and [OGzr]s Viagra and Cint commanded a 2v1 advantage for most of the round. Again, [OGzr]Viagra's patience paid off, as he scored the final cap to put *AP to rest. Unfortunately, *AP came back with a vengeance for the final round, and slowly picked off the Geezers one-by-on, until only [OGzr]LTSam was boxed in and finally gunned down.

American Patriots *AP was definitely a class act...though [OGzr]sfpops would likely disagree with their political courtesy! Hey...Bush did win, 'ya know?! GG *AP.


GR Match: 1.9
January 10, 2003 - 8:31AM by Tracker6

The Geezer recently met up with [JAG] to spar for 5 rounds of 5 v. 5 Ghost Recon on our own trusty Walker Wars server. Though [JAG] claimed victory with a Final Score of 3-1-1, both clans enjoyed a spirited, heart-pounding match.

Round 1: All 5 Geezer bodies litter the street in under ten seconds!!!! OMG, what have we gotten into! [JAG]Joey was unstoppable, killing Pale, Cint, Cart, sfpops and T6...and even killing one of his own in the rampage. Thankfully, the Geezers got their heart-pacer batteries recharged during the intermission.

Round 2: Geezers score two kills during a much more even match. Pale Rider stalked to the second floor of a corner building to score a sniping kill, and Tracker6 put his 7.62 carbine to work at long range while an enemy ditty-bopped down the street; but in the end, [JAG] came away with their second win.

Round 3: Geezers choose Plantation as the next map...and it pays off. sfpops scored a very solid 3 kills, and Pale Rider turned in another 2 to finish off [JAG]. Geezers worked a good checkerboard pattern to cover all avenues on the map, and used team communication well to identify enemy locations.

Round 4: A heartbreaking tie, where a Geezer 2 v 1 advantage was lost with one second left in the round!! Pale Rider and T6 went down quickly...then sfpops began to snipe like a king! Unfortunately, he went out at the last second, leaving Cint as the lone Geezer.

Round 5: Another well played and even match, with some [JAG] players claiming it to be the most nerve wracking match they'd ever played; they come out with a close win.

It was a good battle, we look forward to our next run-in with the classy folks from [JAG].
