We met clan [IDC] tonight for another lesson of how to win in a league match, at all costs. Both rounds were played on Walker Wars as [IDC]'s server was unavailable. The map was cs_docks with a 30 minute time limit.

Round 1
Geezers played CTs, [IDC] played Ts. The Geezers came out guns a-blazin'. Apparently, with old age comes bad eyesight as we couldn't seem to put them [IDC] boys in their place. [IDC] camped the roof exclusively and it gave them a strong edge tactically. Ended with a 13 to 1 lead for [IDC].

Round 2
Time for the Geezers to take advantage of the Terrorist friendly map.  One problem, however: it was as if the CTs didn't come for the game. [IDC] decided to take advantage of a league technicality and not bother trying to rescue the hostages. The Geezers just sat there, protecting hostages, getting older with every passing minute. This was the longest, slowest, and most boring round we have ever played. While we find their tactics to be fully legal, we feel they are not in following with the spirit of the game. It's a shame the game played out that way. But in the end, we won 6 to 1, for a total of 14 to 7.

On the plus side. Rice (of [IDC]) came to our IRC channel and apologized after the match. I doubt they will change their ways. We should probably expect more of this style of play from our opponents in the future.
