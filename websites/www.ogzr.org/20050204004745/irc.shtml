Contacting us on IRC

Feel free to join us on our dedicated IRC channel.  It's a great way to get to know us and our gaming goals. Here are the channel details:

Server: irc.GameSurge.net
Channel:  #ogzr

To talk to us, first you need to get mIRC. When you have it:

  1. Start mIRC
  2. Add the connection to irc.GameSurge.net
  3. Selected the added connect and click "Push To Connect"
  4. Dismiss the displayed dialog and type "/join #ogzr" without the quotes

That's it. It's very simple, extremely quick, and very reliable.

Additionally, you are able to connect to the channel through your browser from this page.
