Round 1: [OGzr] 70 -- [WW] 10
Round 1 got started a bit late, but not a big deal. We got warmed up a bit while we waited, so it worked out good. When the gates opened, it was a mad dash for both teams to get to the bridge and their perspective positions to get set first. The [WW] guys got to the bridge first, and before we could get our defense in positions and set, they grabbed our flag and made a mad dash for home. They got the early cap they were looking for and started again. By that time our Defense was ready and waiting for them. Our offense started making steady progress with their flag, by this time, and the spam on the bridge was in full swing. Our offense started making successful runs for the flag, getting several caps in a short period of time. Toward the end the [WW] guys were getting closer and closer to capping, moving the flag several times, but had to carry the flag through the base giving our defense a chance to close in and stop them. Through it all both sides were going all at it. The [WW] guys did great being one player short. I think it would have been a whole different match if they would have been fully staffed. They are a group of highly skilled, especially their soldiers, and classy group to play against.

Round 2: [OGzr] 150 -- [WW] 10
Tonight's second round pre-match started up with the usual stress relieving game "Spam Your Respawn". Similar to the fantastically popular game "Mine Sweeper", there was carnage everywhere! Oh the humanity!! Then the match started with a ROAR! The Geezers headed across the bridge with guns blazing! Unfortunately, [WW] was well on their way to the Geezer flag already. They got the quick and fast cap but then the Geezer defense set up shop, but they weren't selling entrance tickets. The Geezer Offense set about penetrating the [WW] defenses. The [WW] soldiers were whupin' on the Geezers as they tried to get thru, but the Geezer Offense managed to snake by a few times.

Kudos to the [WW] team members who stuck it out thru the whole match to the end. Great match all around. We look forward to our next encounter.
