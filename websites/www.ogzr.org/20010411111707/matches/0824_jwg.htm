Tonight, we played clan [JwG]. The map was de_prodigy. We came into tonight's match, wanting and needing a win, after a few disappointing weeks. The match started a few minutes late, waiting for a few of our players that had difficulty connecting. [JwG] was gracious in waiting.

Round 1
On Walker Wars. We were T's. The whole round stayed close, staying within one or two of each other. Came down to the last round, with us trying to plant the bomb, and time running out before the timer did. It showed with the final score 10 - 9 [JwG].

Round 2
On their server now as CT's. The fighting was fierce, with them wanting to hold their lead from the first match, and us needing to win by 2. They split up and hit us from both sides, ever vigilant with their grenades. The score stayed close like the first round, until the last eight minutes, when they decided to hang back a little, forcing us to rush. Final score: 12 -7 [JwG].

Overall Score: 22 - 16 [JwG]

It was a very close match, and very enjoyable. Good game to all the guys in [JwG], and all the Geezers. Coder, thanks for Walker Wars: it ran good tonight.
