The Geezers girded their loins on Monday for some Openfire action against clan [DIE]. The Old Ones felt quite confident about this old favorite map…perhaps too confident!

Round 1: Geezers pull a quick cap with some conc’ing magic…then, another with some coordinated spy work. Defense holds tight with excellent communications, able to shutdown [DIE]’s Offense when the flag started moving. In the heat of furious spies and lights, the score ties…Only to fall in OGzr’s favor with a third and final cap. Final score—30-20!!

Round 2: Geezer confidence set in while we patted each other on the back in mIRC…meanwhile, [DIE] was hot and heavy on their server improving their strat. The round starts much tighter than the first, with the Defense earning their sweat and blood. SG’s go up, SG’s go down. [DIE] pulls two caps, then [OGzr] brings it within one. The bad guy stepped up his spy employment, and pulled a third, to end the round…30-10. Final score, 50-40.

While the competition was stiff, [DIE] remained true professionals, a fun clan to do battle with. GG [DIE] and GL!
