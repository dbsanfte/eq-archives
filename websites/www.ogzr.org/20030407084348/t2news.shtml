TWL Win vs -(ZR)-
July 15, 2002 - 8:54PM by Sentry Killer

The Old Geezers were out to play again tonight, defending their spot int he TWL ladder against ZULU'S Revenge. The first map of the match was Firestorm.

ZR worked their way to a 3-0 lead before the Geezers could manage a cap, and pulled ahead 4-3 with just minutes remaining in the match. ZR regrouped and pulled out one more cap before time ran out, ending the map at 4-4 and forcing a sudden death overtime.

Both teams came out flying, with touches and returns on both sides. Finally, DTK came home with the flag, and a strong contingent of ZR's hot on his tail. Map goes to OGzr.

Next up was Death Birds Fly. This map turned into a shutout for both teams, as capper after capper was turned aside by the Defenses of both teams. At the end of 30 minutes of gameplay, the score remained as it started: 0-0.

Sudden death #2 started with a bang. ZR came with the heavy artillery, but were sent packing by a tough OGzr defense. Meanwhile, the OGzr cappers were getting popped early and often. Finally, with just a sliver of health left, Spookious made it past the 50 yard line, to lose the flag. MechMan came in and carried the flag home, for the second win of the night.

Our shouts of "Good game!" go out to ZULU'S Revenge, for the hard fought games and great sportsmanship they displayed. We look forward to our next meeting with them.

BEML Tribes 2 Match vs 57th
June 6, 2002 - 8:56PM by Sentry Killer

The old ones took to the battlefield again, and started Season 4 of the BEML against a new team to the league, 57th. Although a few persons short, 57th showed their resolve and played on through some great battles.

First map: Katabatic. Starting off with a flourish, 57th got the first touch on the flag, and the race was on. A tough 57th Offense made the Old Ones have to backup a step and put the brakes on the flag grabs. Some good midfield work on the part of both teams made the match a good one. However, when the dust settled, the Old Geezers were able to pull an 8-2 win.

Next was Sanctuary. The 57th Defense made our cappers earn every step of our attempts to capture the flag. 57th pulled one cap, but then our D held solid, and was able to get the flag back each time it disappeared from the stand. By the time the clock ran out, the Old Geezers had again won the map, but not without a hard fight. OGzr 2-1.

Last was Drifts(x), a new map to the BEML. After making sure that everyone was ready to go, the final map started, and 57th came out charging. Flying cappers everywhere. The Defense pulled up their Depends, and held fast to the flag. Meanwhile, mortars were flying around the 57th base, and our cappers were stopped numerous times at the flag stand. The OGzr Offense stepped it up a bit, and was able to pull a few flags out of the base and back home. OGzr 4-0.

Our hats off to the 57th group. They fought well, and are a great addition to the BEML. We wish them well, and hope to play them again soon.

First TWL Loss
May 30, 2002 - 2:46PM by Aarvid

Well, it had to happen sometime. OGzr got their first loss on the TWL 12man ladder vs =W= Warlords. It was a well-fought match by both tribes. The maps played were Harvester(x) and WoodyMyrk(x).

First map was Harvester. =W= came out strong and got the quick cap on us. OGzr battled back and the score was tied at 3-3 for some time. =W= hit our gens hard and crippled us. They took advantage and pulled out a 6-3 victory on the map.

Second map was WoodyMyrk. Both teams came out with mortars flying! Lots of grabs were made and the score stayed close the entire map. Ogzr pulled ahead with an early 3-1 lead. However, the lead was short-lived and =W= pulled out a 5-4 victory on the map.

Great games =W= and well-fought. Looking forward to the next matchup!

Another scrim under our belts...
May 9, 2002 - 8:13PM by Sentry Killer

...and we get a lesson learned in the process!

Our scrim partner was (MA), Mature Asskickers, and an ass-kicking is what we got! The scrim helped us to welcome MA into the TIW program with BEML, as well as to get some good games going!

OGzr Celebrates first TWL Victory!
April 30, 2002 - 7:42PM by Tusk

The Old Geezers faced off with EW (Elite Warriors) Tuesday Night for thier first TWL Match. Maps played were Quagmire - x and Sanctuary with Riverdance as the scheduled tie breaker.

The OGzr Leadership proved that one can teach Old Dogs new tricks. The modified Quagmire map worked well with the Old Men launching out a fast assault while the Defense deployed and parked a Juggernaut on the flag to stay! The persistant EW Offense managed a few grabs, but couldn't pull off the full carry home. OGzr capped a shutout on EW 6-0.

The next map was Sanctuary and once again (after a few Geritol pills and Ensure chasers) the Old Farts were up to the task. EW came in hot and quick, taking down OGzr's gens and sending the D scrambling. EW was kind enough to return our flag after taking home for a moment. Not bad for being a few players down. The caps however, ended there. OGzr Offense redoubled efforts and pulled off 4 complete flag carries before time ran out. 4-1 OGzr win.

Thanks for coming out EW!
