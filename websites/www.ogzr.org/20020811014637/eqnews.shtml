Home coming
July 19, 2002 - 9:29AM by Dremarian

Sebilis, ancient capital of the Iksar empire, destroyed long ago by the dragon Trakanon and his followers.

Last night, joined by a groups of friends, Chracner returned to this fabled city. Sent to retrieve a once powerful artifact by Cazic Thule himself, they would not be denied.

After a quick run to the crypt area, final resting place of the Sebilis rulers and those that guard them, they set about clearing the area. There were many titanic battles that took place clearing the crypt and the surrounding area in preperation for the final showdown with the fallen Iksar master. The warriors faught with great execution and spells flashed and flared seeming to rip the very fabric of time and space. In the end, all lay dead before this mighty band of Ancients, incuding the fallen Iksar master. Having retrieved the piece they were sent for, the group, very pleased with thier combat prowess, vowed to come agian perhaps one day freeing this ancient city of the dragon Trakanon.


Grats go out to Chracner for the quest piece, Graphyte for the Blood Ember vambrace, and all others that won items. It was great fun and a honor to fight at your side.

Do not fear us for our numbers, fear us for our courage, for one fearless warrior can overcome ten cowards....Dremarian Darkheart


Ancients night on the town
July 11, 2002 - 12:07PM by Dremarian

Last night a well balanced group of melee, casters, and priests journeyed deep into the heart of Karnor's Castle. With no working knowledge of the zone or the monsters that lived there they made thier way to top of the battlement before having to evac and regroup. Thier second run netted them many failed attempts at breaking the hand room and a final resting place just south of the jail. All was going fine until a bad pull cost Drgo and Sab thier lives. Some ports were done, some rezz's were done, and it was time to go to bed for the night.

Before they left Chrac sent Sax a tell.
Chracner: Hey Sax wanna go turn in that item.
Sax: alone, can we do it.
Chracner: Dunno, but what the hell, we can try, I will pull them through and clear a path you get the item and port out.
Sax: So no port for you?
Chracner: Nah, I'll be alright.
Sax: Ok.


LOL, Chracner thought to himself, this guy is crazier then I am. After some great buffs by Drgo and Sab, Chrac prepared himself for what was sure to be a one way trip. Chrac turned to Sax and with out hesitating said lets go do this........Dremarian


Fall of an Ancient foe
July 5, 2002 - 6:47PM by Dremarian

A well organized group of Ancients entered kael Drakkel last night bent on avenging the death of friends.

They entered from the Wakening lands and started killing giants at the entrance to Kael. The first big test was a 55th level giant named Korakaz, it was a great battle and in the end the Ancients dropped Korakaz. They then made their way to the bank area and cleared a path to the dreaded giant Captain Bvellos. They got Captian B on a single pull and proceeded to beat on him for what seemed like 5 minutes. In the end the well balanced group, the perfectly timed spells, and the fearless nature which has become a trade mark of the Ancients won the day.

I would like to thank all that came, all that faught, and all that died to make this the biggest guild kill to date.

Dremarian

And they all fall down
June 8, 2002 - 6:36PM by Dremarian

A mighty band of Ancients and friends entered Kael Drakkel from the Eastern wastes last night. They faught bravely and killed all that stood before them. They made their way from the zone all the way to the throne of Iceshard keep with little mishap. Turning to leave they decided to have a go at Captain Bvellos.

The pull was fine the tag even better and the group engaged as if it were of one mind. At first it appeared as if the Ancients had everything under control. Then a back spawn added. The Captain began raining blow from everywhere. They came so fast and so hard it was if Hell had opened and were wanting to swallow them whole. They fell one by one til only one remained, he ran but was summoned back. Again and again he ran and was summoned like some sick cat and mouse game until finally he too fell.

This is the sight that Chracner saw from where he lay bleeding in the center of the commons, too weak to stand, too tired to fight, too confused to know wrong from right, he lay there......weeping

Dremarian

Fall of the Ancients
May 26, 2002 - 10:34AM by Dremarian
