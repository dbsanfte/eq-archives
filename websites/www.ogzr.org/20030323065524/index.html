OGzr 3 Year Anniversary Party Wrap-up!
August 28, 2002 - 9:35PM by Stilgar

This comes from Tracker6 who was a very active Geezer during our glorious stint in STA Team Fortress League. *sigh* Those were the days... we were so young (for being old) and we had so many good times. Anyway... here's T9's wrap-up:

August 23, 2002: OLD GEEZERS CELEBRATE (BELATEDLY) THREE YEARS OF PROSPERITY AND GROWTH

As the flurry of Geezer-gaming continued in to its third year of existence...we collectively felt a pang of shock and regret upon reading SlimWit's post on the Forum: HOW COULD WE FORGET?!! Imagine the personal tragedy that befalls those unforgiven spouses who have committed the egregious sin of overlooking their wedding anniversary. Quickly, upon realizing that our founding date had passed, the Old Geezers resolved to recognize the momentous occasion with a most befitting event, an All-Comers game of the saga that started it all three years ago: Team Fortress Classic.

And so, they came by the dozens (about two-dozen, to be more precise!). Well before game time, Geezers were found loitering on our mIRC channel, waiting to check in and bid each other "Happy Anniversary". [OGzr] mAdmOnk is reputed to boast the record of at least six-hours prior to game time (of course, he had the UK advantage working for him!). After catching up on old news...who had new kids, who had a new job, who had been in jail...it was off to our very own Walker Wars server, graciously administered by [OGzr] JackhOle, and loaded with all our favorite maps.

'Lo...this was no organized, disciplined practice session in the traditional Geezer vane! While the initial rust of re-learning old moves, positions and strategies was highly evident, the skills, camaraderie and teamwork seemed to come back quickly. By the time "Well" was loaded, Geezers seemed to be coming back into old form, with strong defenses set-up around the FR, and routinely ferocious movement of the flag once it had been dropped to the bottom. Crossover and Shutdown2 proved to be as enjoyable as always (though the concs just didn't seem as refined)...and 2Mesa3 proved as frustrating as it was the first day we practiced on that map! So, it was fitting that with the clock approaching midnight for our East Coast bretheren, Geezers took their leave back to mIRC to talk smack about who crowbarred and wrenched who, and to say their goodnights.

We've come a long way: from TFC, to CS...to EQ and Tribes 2 (and don't forget Gang Wars!). It's been a hell of a ride. We can be proud that after three years of prosperity, Old Geezers from all of our Clan's generations returned on the Anniversary to experience what makes us unique in the Gaming community: maturity, camaraderie...and our trademark canes and wheelchairs! See you next year.
