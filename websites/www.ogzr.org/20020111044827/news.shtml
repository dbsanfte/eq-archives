On the EverQuest Front
Wednesday, January 9, 2002 11:43 PM PST by SlimWit
Ripped shamelessly from the Forum, posted by Dremarian: In breaking news today, the Ancients made a strategic insertion into the Rathe Mountians to secure a pair of boots for the cold feet of a comrade. Through no fault of their own, a member was lost when they were set upon by a horde of Giant kind. After much persuading, they convinced a Vicar to work his Magic on the fallen friend and bring her back to her former self.

After reinforcements arrived, the Ancients set about avenging the loss of thier friend. Many Giants and Cyclops were lost in the exchange and the Ancients suffered no more casualties.
