This is the second time the Old Geezers have faced off against the Attack Doggs in the past three weeks, and we were looking forward to seeing them again. They're classy, fun guys. They beat us last time on Canalzone, and we hoped to exact a bit of revenge on 2Fort. Alas, 'twas not to be.

Round 1
Attack Doggs server, pings pretty even, favoring A-D about 128-107. Out of the gates, A-D scouts came flying over our battlements as the Geezers did the same. However, the A-D rush was successful with a few quick caps, while the Geezers managed a touch early, but couldn't get it out of the basement. This trend continued. A-D went heavy on D, or at least it seems that way because everywhere we looked we found soldiers and demos waiting. A-D brought home a few more caps, and our Offense couldn't crack their D. We managed I think 3 touches, but no scores. Our D was scrambling, but those Doggs were just too quick. Final, 50-0.

Round 2
Old Geezers Walker Wars, again pings even, favoring A-D about 102-72. The Geezers spent some time in IRC getting our heads together, and shuffled the O and D lineups a bit. Still had to wait a few minutes for our subs to appear, so thanks for your patience. Scout rush again, and again A-D brings it home quickly. Geeers not so lucky, because A-D locked down even more on D than the first round. [OGzr]VRMan managed to grab their flag and get into the open. A-D pounced on it, but eventually [OGzr]JackhOle was able to bring it home. A-D grabbed another flag, then pretty much went all D, and had a sniper and Demo holding the bridge. OGzr kept pushing, but could not break through their many defenders. Final, 20-10.

GG Attack Doggs. Nice deadeye soldiers.
