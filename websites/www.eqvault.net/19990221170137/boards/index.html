Message Boards

General Message Board
This board is for general discussions about the world of EverQuest. Here you'll find people talking about gameplay and roleplaying issues, bugs and solutions, and the latest events in the world of Norrath. Come join our hearty band of companions around the warm fire and share your tales with us!

Developer's Board
Here players can address questions to a mystic group of beings known only as The Developers. Please be sure to read the FAQ parchment before posting here, to make sure that some poor soul has not already asked the same question and incurred their great wrath. Irrelevant postings will be smitten.

Bug Board
Multiplayer RPGs will always be works in progress as new features are added and the world evolves. Unfortunately, because of this bugs will be introduced from time to time. This is the place to report these nasty critters so the development team can squash them as quickly as possible.

Tech Board
EverQuest is the first MMORPG to demand a 3D-accelerator in order to play, and as such many people have questions about whether their current card, or even whole system, is capable of running the game. Here's the place to discuss with other fans what works and what doesn't.

Magic Shop Board
This is the place to discuss all things magic in EQ; from levitation spells, to the magical songs of Bards. Please try to stay on topic as much as possibe or we might have to dust off the Wand of Off Topic Post Smiting.

Player vs. Player
Although many players have banded together in mutual cooperation to survive the dangers of the land of Norrath, there are those who choose a different route, and choose to prey upon their fellow travellers. This is the forum to discuss the issues associated with Player Killing, and hoe to make it work in EverQuest so that it's fair to everyone and enjoyable to those who choose to do it.

Tales of Adventure
Gather round the fire, my friends, and listen to the tales of other travellers who have crossed these lands. Be warned, however, that many of the tales might contain spoilers for those who choose to follow a similar path, to read at your own risk. Please try and avoid replying to tales on this board unless it's really necessary - we'd like to leave as many of the tales up for as long as possible so others can read them and enjoy.

Court of Guilds
Intrigue. Danger. Adventure. Such is the life of those who join a guild. Here, we present a forum for all guilds to gather, announce events, and search for new members.
