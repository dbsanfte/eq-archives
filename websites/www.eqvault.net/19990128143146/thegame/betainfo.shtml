Beta Information

EverQuest is now in the Second Phase of the Beta

 NOTE: This is information taken from the www.everquest.com Homepage, and in no way shape or form is there a way for us to get you into any phase of the Beta. Please read this whole page, and then if you still feel the need, I guess we won't mind the mail from you about Beta. But please do not e-mail us about being in the Beta, we have no hand in it.

About the EverQuest Beta Tests:
The EverQuest beta tests will be broken up into several phases. The last phase will be open to the public, but the phases before that will be closed and only involve those people whom we accept. We will begin the first phase with a small number of external testers, and then as we begin new phases, more and more people will become involved. An   application form has been posted on EverQuest.com site. Applications will no longer be accepted for the first phases of the beta test. The applications form will still be up, but you will only be considered for future phases of the beta period. It is important that if you wish to be involved in the beta that you fill out the application. The last phase before the public beta will be open to everyone who has filled out the application.

Some Questions and Answers

I've heard that the closed beta will be open to just a small group of applicants... why should I apply?

There will be just the one application for all phases of closed testing, so if you want to be involved in any of the tests, you'll need to apply. Also, the last phase of closed testing will involve everyone who applied, regardless of qualifications and experience.

Are you referring to the open beta?

No. The last phase of closed testing, prior to the public beta, will be open to everyone who took the time to apply. We feel that even if we don't choose you for whatever reason for the first phases, you did take the time to fill out an app, and you deserve something for that.

Speaking of qualifications and experience, what kind of people are you looking for?

For the first phases, we'll be pretty picky. We're looking for people who have a lot of experience playing online RPGs, single player RPGs, and MUDs. Also, experience in previous commercial beta tests will be a plus. But keep in mind that with each successive phase we'll relax the qualifications more and more.

How many people will you be accepting in total?

We don't have a specific number determined as of yet. The very first phase will probably involve less than a hundred, yet the final closed phase will include whomever applied during the time period we are accepting applications. Each successive phase will involve more and more of the applicants.

When can we fill out the application?

You can fill the applications out now. Click Here, remember this application is for all phases of Beta Testing.

When does the actual testing for the individual phases begin?

We're not committing to any dates at this time, simply because we want to make sure we're ready. Please be patient, we certainly don't take any pleasure in having people wait and we are working as hard and as fast as possible.
What if I fill out the application, but later change my email address, or other pertinent information?
Just fill out the form again.

So do I lose my spot as a result?
First phase beta testers will maintain their spot regardless of any change to their application. However, those beyond the first phase will find their application adjusted each time they re-submit an application. In other words, don't re-submit your application unless you have to. Please do NOT send any adjustments to the webmaster, or anyone else; all adjustments must be made in the form of a new application. Sorry.
