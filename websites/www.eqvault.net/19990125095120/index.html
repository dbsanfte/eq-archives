Brad On The EverQuest Hint Book
RedEye Interactive's Vice President, Brad McQuaid, made a post on the General Board concerning spoilers in the EverQuest Hint Book. Here's the post:
Will the EQ hint book that EBworld is selling be more of a listing of race/class stats, entry level maps and price/spell guides or will it be one big mess of spoilers? I would like to buy it if it is only to aid gameplay like, for example, how expensive it is to buy bone chips in freeport. If it is comprehensive maps of dungeons and hidden areas along with lists of unknown, questable spells and other such things, I want to stay as far away from it as possible.

The official EQ players guide will not have spoilers in it -- we made sure of that. The intent behind it is to really be a player's guide, or a strategy guide. -Brad

Guild System Update
The EverQuest patch server was updated with new information concerning guild commands and the first guilds to be approved in Everquest. Here's the information:
Guilds who have met the 10 member limit and whose charters have been approved will begin to be contacted for initial set up on 1/20/99. The following guilds have been approved: Vale Patrol, Underground Warlords, The Risen, The Crimson Rose, Tal-Kor, Mystical Order of the Brotherhood, Asu-Tru, The Covetous Crew, and Clan Delaf. This list will be updated as more guilds are approved. If your guild is not listed above, please DO NOT petition with questions.

If your Guild is listed above, the leader of the guild should petition for a RedEye GM to assist you in setting up the guild.

Guild Commands:
/guildinvite - Invite a person to join the guild, if this person is to be an officer, include the argument (as in oscar) for the rank. Usable by Guild Leader and Officers, but only the Guild Leader can make officers. You would also use this function to promote a member to an officer.
/guildremove - Remove a member from the guild. Only the Guild Leader may remove Officers.
/guilddelete - Disband your entire guild. Only the Guild Leader may use this command.
/guildstatus - Will return information on whether the named person is a member, an Officer, or the Guild Leader.
/guildleader - Transfer guild leadership to.
/guildsay - Sends a message to all guild members.
/guildwar - Initiates a guild war against the guild of Guild Leader which appears in the field. sets the duration in real days of the guild war. This defaults to one real day if left blank. The opposing Guild Leader must send the same command with the of the other Guild Leader to fully initiate the conflict.
/guildpeace - Ends a guild war before the duration has expired. is the name of the opposing Guild Leader. Both sides must send this command to end the war prematurely."
