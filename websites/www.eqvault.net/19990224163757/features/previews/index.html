Previews

GameSpot TV Preview
November 30, 1998
Requires Real Player to view. Was broadcasted on ZDTV on January 23 and 24.

PC Fan Preview
November 30, 1998
In this preview, we follow Jason & Chris as they journey throughout the land of Norrath.

CDMag Preview (Part 2)
November 7, 1998
This is part two of a three part preview. It focuses on EverQuest's races and classes.

PC.IGN.COM Preview
November 17, 1998
This preview of EverQuest will be updated regularly with new content.

CD Mag Preview
November 7, 1998
Computer Games Online has jumped into the ring with it's preview of EverQuest. Of particular note is a new map of Norrath (along with 14 other new screenshots).

Voodoo Mag 989 Studios Profile
November 7, 1998
Although this isn't a preview of EverQuest it has some information on the people who make EverQuest.

PC Accelerator Preview
November 7, 1998
The November issue of PC Accelerator magazine features a two page review of EverQuest. Their review includes all new screenshots, five questions with the developer, and four sure signs of a closet role-player

Games.net Preview
August 13, 1998
This preview is basically the web version of the PCGames article with new screenshots.

GameZine Preview
August 5, 1998
This preview contains no new information or screenshots.

PCGames Preview
August 1, 1998
PCGames (The September Issue) has a 5 page EverQuest preview full of info (not new) and new screenshots.

Exscape Preview
July 30, 1998
Another preview with no new information or screenshots.

Desslock's EverQuest Preview and Interview
June 12, 1998
Gamepen finally got around to posting Desslock's EverQuest article, and as usual it's worth the wait. Included in the comprehensive coverage is an interview with Brad McQuaid, new screenshots of the game, and a brief preview.

Short Preview in This Months PC Gamer
June 7, 1998
PC Gamer has an EQ preview in this months magazine, along with 2 screenshots.

BetaZine Interview With Charles Flock
June 5, 1998
BetaZine posted this informative interview with Charles Flock the Test Manager at the San Diego Studio. He will be overseeing the beta test with Brad.

Vaultnetwork Interview with Brad
June 5, 1998
Our very own Mahrin Skel, gave us this great interview with Brad after visiting with him at E3.

Games Planet Preview
May 8, 1998
Games Planet released this preview of EQ which includes several screenshots and a brief list of features from EverQuest.

OGR Preview from CDGC
May 6, 1998
OGR has posted a great rundown of information gleaned at the CDGC, included within is some new info, and confirmation on some old.

GameFan Preview
April 30, 1998
Game Fan Online put up this quick preview of EverQuest. It doesn't have too much information about EQ, but it includes 12 screenshots.

Game Revolution Preview
April 3, 1998
Game Revolution put up this informative preview, revealing some of the best features of EverQuest.   Also included are 2 exclusive screen shots at the bottom of the article.

Game Revolution Interview
April 2, 1998
Game Revolution posted this interview with Brad discussing such things as lag, roleplaying, and the development of EverQuest.

PC Fan Preview
March 30, 1998
A very limited preview, hardly worth much attention.

UGP Preview
January 7, 1998
A completely uninspired preview, although there appears to be a new Quicktime movie of the game.

OGR Preview
December 30, 1997
OGR.com has an excellent preview of EverQuest that gives readers a great overview of what this game is all about.  Of special note is a series of production drawings of characters from the game, and a few new screenshots.

AllGames Interview
December 27, 1997
AllGames Network has a RealAudio interview with the producer of EverQuest, Mr. Brad McQuaid.

EGS Preview
December 26, 1997
Eidelon Gamers Society has a brief article on EverQuest, along with a new Quicktime movie.

Sony's PC Adventure
December 9, 1997
Computer Games and Strategy Plus offers readers an in-depth look at Sony Interactive, and discuss how the company is slowly expanding into the PC gaming market with a strong emphasis on online gaming.

A 3D RPG With an Online Attitude
December 2, 1997
A rather brief summary of EverQuest from Computer Games and Strategy Plus that does a decent job of summing the game up, but doesn't tell us anything we didn't already know.

Sony Goes Deep
October 28, 1997
A short overview of EverQuest in Computer Gaming World.

The Future of RPGs?
September 4, 1997
Another summary of EverQuest, this time with an interview with the producer of the game, Brad McQuaid.

Sony's Online Realm
August 27, 1997
A massive preview of EverQuest from the folks at Gamecenter. Check this one out!
