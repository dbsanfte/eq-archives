A New Charge in New Lands

Posted by Morphite on August 21, 2000:

Much of what I know to be, I withold my little vassel. I shall impart what I want you to know now and what I still need to know, you will discover for me, do you understand?

The dragon's voice boomed at him from out of the skies. Morphite looked around, half believing he'd actually see the voice's source.

Fool! Did you not hear me? You struggle to use your primary sense ignoring all others. Will your kind never mature?!

The words stung, and Morphite struggled to maintain some sense of control as he stammered, "I understand, Lady Vox."

Ah . . . the little one remembers my voice, that is good. Then listen, I charge you to gather a small force of fodder, call them 'friends' if you wish, and make your way to the newly discovered lands of the Iksar. There, you will find the lands of the giants. It is the lineage of these abominations who defiled the time known to your people as the Age of Scale and brought forth the Elder Age. Thus I charge you, 'dear' Morphite, bring forth your newly found powers, which I have chosen to allow you to learn, and smite many a giant that I may know how to better destroy their kind. Our suspicions in the Ring of Scale are now known to be true,

"What are those suspicions?"

The chuckle ran up and down his spine as the gutteral moan of Vox taking in a breath passed over him. That some giants had escaped our intial wars and are now residing on . . .

The pause was excrutiating for Morphite and he waited as long as he could . . . "Vox?" he half whispered. There was no reply . . . "Vox?"

"Vox?!"
