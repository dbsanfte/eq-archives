Something Befalls the Wizard...

Posted by Keldore Silverwand on July 18, 2000:

They met at the entrance, the large and small warriors, the green ranger, and the pale wizard. With swords, spells, and claws, the four prepared to deal heavy damage to the undead and their animators in the lost elven city of Befallen.

It was not long before numerous bones had been cracked, ghoulish fleshed had been ripped to shreads, and necromancers had been left writhing in agony on the chilling stones. Many fine items had been found as well, and Balkin managed to collect a bundle of bone chips for Shivre.

All was going well, there was little threat to the health of the small group. Suddenly, however, there was an slight decrease in temperature, barely noticable in the place where death walked. Balkin, Trespare, and Krudd had left the open cavern to do battle, and so escaped the spell cast by an unseen warlock.

Paralysis took over Keldore's senses and a deep chill set on his bones. He was held in place, unable to move, unable to speak. For nearly ten minutes he was nothing more the a statue. The others could not fathom his location, the spell was coupled with invisibility. This was powerful magic at work.

Fortuately, someone or something broke the warlock's concentration and Keldore was realized, shivering with fury and chill. He decided he had better leave the dungeon and get some rest, departing from the others, who were more than capable of handling themselves.
