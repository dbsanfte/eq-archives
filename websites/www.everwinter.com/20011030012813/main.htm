8/27/2001 ~ Site and Guild News

Well folks, the summer hasn't been kind to our little guild.  Most members have been busy, as was the case last summer.  For those interested in raids and higher level guilds, we ask that you leave an alternate character within the guild so we can continue to use our chat channel in EQ.

In other news, I've been growing increasingly excited about the prospects of a Neverwinter Nights campaign.  For those that aren't familiar with the software, Bioware's new NWN software is essentially a 3rd Edition D&D game in the style of the Baldur's Gate series.  It is *NOT* a massively-multiplayer online game like Everquest.  Instead, it allows one player to host a server and serve as a conventional Dungeon Master does with up to 64 players (server and connection permitting).  You can find more information at {Official Neverwinter Site}.

I intend to run a regular campaign, using the web site to support online game-play.  For those that are quite happy with Norrath, consider joining us in addition to your normal EQ gaming.  It should be noted, I currently have no intention of giving up my EQ account.  While Shar and I don't play often, we still play now and then :)  

~dirge, Webmaster
