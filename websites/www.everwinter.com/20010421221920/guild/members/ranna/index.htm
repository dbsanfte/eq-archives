Ranna was found in the grand city of Qeynos.  She was left at the Temple of Life as a baby.  With no parents, she was raises as a disciple of Rodcet Nife, listening to the preachings of high ranking members of the church day and night.  She knows most of the priests and priestesses rather well, and follows the rules of Rodcet Nife, the prime healer... save for one thing; she's a tad violent.

Her home is constantly under attack by gnolls, from the scrawny pups to the powerful adults.  Indeed, even as a child in the temple she had problems with these humanoid dogs attacking her.  If it wasn't for her fellow clerics, she might not have survived to this day.  These trials have left a mark upon Ranna.  She despises gnolls and gladly walks to the area of Blackburrow and either slays the gnolls there or heals and assists in any way those that attack them.

Also, as a Cleric of Rodcet Nife, she fights the minions of Bertoxxilous--the undead and the diseased to the best of her ability.  But despite her aggressive side, she's a good friend to have.  No matter the race (ask Amorsaylnn) she will be a friend to all.  Amorsaylnn, Tier'dal Magician, noted "You're a good sniper!" when Ranna called down the power of Nife to smite the gnolls they were chasing.
