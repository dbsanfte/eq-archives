Battles to be Fought...

Posted by Peony Furfoot on August 19, 2000:

Peony glanced over her shoulder and out the window to where her husband, Dolgo, was making the last minute arrangements for their mounts. She was frustrated, tired, and more than a little nervous about the upcoming journey that would change their lives forever.

Her brave and stout Dolgo... dressed as he was for battle, she couldn't help but wonder if they were making the right decision. It had been but three days since Rackin had mysteriously disappeared, and only two since the Call to Arms for all hobbits and their allies to return to Rivervale.. The Tier'Dal were advancing it seemed, somehow they had come up with majiks that allowed them to creep through Kithicor undetected.. even by the undead armies that had been plagueing that blasted wood since time out of mind.. Dolgo and Peony had spoken long into the night when they first received the missive.. their first thoughts of course were that Rackin had gone to join the Teir'Dal armies.. that Najena had once again worked her spell on him.. Peony and Dolgo had so wanted to give Rackin the benefit of the doubt however.. to believe that he had simply fled from the propaganda that was sure to have reached his ears.

Whatever the case may have been.. they both firmly felt that they had to return to the place of their birth and stand alongside their relatives against the hideous foe. They made plans to tie up their affairs in Kunark and return to Antonica. It took them just one full day and evening to procur the needed supplies.. Dolgo had made one last forage out into the mountains.. gathering his poison componants. Peony had wandered, almost in a daze behind, gathering the few rare herbs that might assist her in her healing. Mostly she meditated and read her psalms, gathering energy for what was to be a long and dangerous path.

She watched as Dolgo gave a sharp nod to the stable boy, dropping a healthy amount of gold into his surprised paw.. with a snort she returned her attention to paying the lass behind the bar, handing over almost three times what the beer and board had cost. Knowing that she had caught the ladies attention, she pulled from her pack one of four identical notes.. The missives were written on cured hide and then closed from prying eyes with her own personal wax seal. She waved the rolled leather before the womans nose.. "I'll pay ye more than this if ye promise tae hand this over tae anyone that bears the same crest that looks like this..\\v//§\\v//... Specifically a woman that can identify herself as Rhianna, "Queen" of Winterborne..

The woman snatched the missive out of Peony's hand and quickly tucked it up onto a shelf behind the bar with promises to see that it was properly delivered. Peony, fairly dubious about the womans "good" intentions saw fit to hand the promised coin over anyway.. She then shouldered her pack and made her way from the bar out to where the laden beasts and her husband awaited her.

Along the journey towards home, three more of the same missive were distributed.. most, thankfully to trusted hands.. Peony and Dolgo both knew that they could count on the letters making it to the right people..

Dear Rianna and Winterborne,

I trust that this finds you in good health and high spirits. It is with a saddened heart however that Dolgo and I do not bring the best of news. War has called us home and after searching our hearts we've realized that what Winterborne once meant to us.. is no more. Those that we truly called family, those that we knew and loved have perished, become lost, or simply faded away from the "Clan". We wish for there to be no hard feelings and hope that should we survive our battle, a friendship can be maintained..

Fair Winds and Weapons High..

Always,
Peony and Dolgo Furfoot
