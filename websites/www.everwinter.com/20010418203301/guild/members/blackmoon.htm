History: Blackmoon actually has a long and varied history, dating back to the prehistory of the Isles. One constant has been the influence of dr’Shivrehel Despana, known as Dirge. Clan Blackmoon stems from his beliefs and dedication to the House, and it has endured through the Isles, Neverwinter, Sosaria, and now faces Norrath.

Dogma: Why are you bothering me? You seek what? Knowledge! Knowledge is only as good as the mind that wields it, but I will indulge you. Perhaps you can see past your own limitations. The other clans consider us isolationists, reclusive and bitter hermits. It is true that we prefer our own company; we value our privacy greatly and resent intrusions. When we must deal with those in the light, we tend to be quick to anger. Our senses are far more acute and this makes us sensitive to what most do not see… the ceaseless approach of death; the reign of decay.

How can you be so blind? Our survival is paramount. In the darkness, there is solace from the piercing brightness. In the silence, there is opportunity to contemplate, plot and dream, freedom from the cacophony of the fools. When we emerge, it is to gather power, to hunt and make known the sanctity of our dwelling. If safety allows, we prefer to bless our enemies with a slow and painful death. Sometimes a tortured man left to live is more useful than a field of corpses, but a thousand heads on pikes can also leave a lasting impression.

We are a cunning people. Risks are for fools. We use the shadows to protect our secrets and delude our foes. Some of our rank use the arcane magic of necromancy to create undead servitors that do not question our will; they obey because it is our right to give orders-another sign of our mastery over that which others fear.

All is not bleak. What is not to laugh at? All around us, death embraces the sightless. There is nothing more entertaining than the dawning recognition that there is no hope… only decay. Most crumble under the realization they will be rotted flesh and bone one day. We learn to dominate it and make the fallen remains of others dance to our whim. Speaking of whim, would you care to dance?

We are sensual creatures. We see what others do not, the beauty of pleasure and pain. Rather than turning away from the rot, we embrace those who see the world against a dark moon. We are the enlightened. We are the teachers of what is beyond life. Embrace our way now and become more than you ever dreamed of; choose a different path, and learn in your passing that more than life can be lost. You can sacrifice your soul to our greatness.

Code of Conduct:

  • Betrayal of your Clan and the Clan's ambitions is unthinkable. Never betray a true friend. Acquaintances however, are another matter, and how common are true friends?

  • The truth is a delicate flower. It requires just the right parts of light and water. Too much of either will kill it. By that token, revealing secrets can be your death; use the truth carefully. Your word is important to you only because it might affect how others trust you. Give your word freely to anyone you disrespect - who cares if they will think badly of you? Sooner or later, they'll end up dead.

  • Protect your clan's interests, protect your interests, and manipulate others' to work towards your interests. It doesn't matter if they understand them or not. Anyone who can’t benefit you is meat.

  • Laws are established by those with the strength to enforce them. If they aren't strong, substitute your own laws; otherwise, adhere to them.

  • Killing an innocent, or many, can help spread word of your name. If fear is what you desire, then make their deaths as offensive and public as possible.

  • Torture is a useful and sometimes fun tool. After all, your victims will probably serve you better in death.
