"Arturiss me old friend! Tis good to see ya. Have a seat me friend and let’s get to work." Exclaimed Brombeard as he shook Arturiss’ hand. "What is this you want of me? Some sort o history?"

"Yes my short friend. I have been collecting histories of my companions so that their tales will last til the dragons return." Arturiss stated while pulling a seat to the table in the crowded pub.

Brombeard reached into his sack and pulled out a beaten old box. The scent of ale spilt forth as if he had just opened a fresh bottle. Pulling two musty bottles from the box, Brombeard gnarled the tops off with his teeth, setting one in front of each of them. "Where shall I begin? Tis an odd tale, but a good one at that." Brombeard said after a long drought from the bottle. "From what me pops told me, I just showed up one day. He had been out huntin fer ingredient fer his new ale when he found me crawlin around. I guess I had thought that I could scale one o the Butcherblocks that day. He said I was half way up the mount when he saw me." Brombeard chuckled when he remembered the day his father had told him. "From the beginin I was awnry, or so I have been told. Stickin me nose into places it nae belonged. I remembered days in my youth where I was getting into fights all the time. Finally, me pops decided that nuff be nuff and sent me to the warriors academy. This is where all me life training was shown to me.

"Several months o trainin ensued. Finally, the guild master told me to go out on me own to further me trainin. Me thinks it was due to the beatins I was giving the other students. This is where I met me best friends Hustrun and Tobbin. They took me to places that I thought I would ne’er go to. Places I have only heard of from stories that me pops told me. Places such as Unrest and Crushbone, those be the trainin ground fer ol’ Brom." Brombeard paused for a moment to take a long pull from his ale. Looking up he noticed that half of his friends brew had been finished and a slightly glazed look in Arturiss’ eye. He began to chuckle.

"Ah. Then there was me first time o’er to Antonica. This is where I met Lady Trespare. And where I got me favorite axe!" He exclaimed holding his tomahawk. Arturiss noticed that Brom had a distant look in his eye. Reading his lips, Arturiss could swear Brom said Rianna. Brombeard took another swig before beginning again. " After the Pass at HighHold, I found me self all over. Oasis to Qeynos, I was transportin people all over the world. It was good fer me to see the entire world. Finally, I got me fill of portin people around and took up me axe fer me own reasons. This is where we end. There are some stories in there, but let those be fer another time. Now be the time to drink me brew. May Brell bless ye on all your journeys." Toasted Brombeard as he quickly finished his ale.
