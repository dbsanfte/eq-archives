Cazic-Thule

Posted by Morphite on August 22, 2000:

The lizard men had been fight back all day. Much of the air was filled with their dull, gutteral chants. Each chant, they hoped would bring the blessings of thier lord, and, of course, their champion, the Avatar of Fear.

Many of the lesser lords in the temple had been winning thier battles against the mobs that attempted to vanquish them. Though more for their hoards, "loot" as the Rivvin called it, then for any real moral victory. Still, it was nice to be able to reap the benefits of the hooligans every now and then.

This day brought forth Morphite and Balkin to do battle within the Silvered Temple. A prescouting forray by Morphite found the silver temple burned and in shambles. A strong party of adventurers claimed ownership of the entire area. Morphite and Balkin discussed their options amid the shouts for others to join the fight against the hoards, elsewhere in the temple. Finally the pair decided upon the Archon Temple, a larger, more fortified temple past the maze of the defenders. The temple had some large open areas and seemed to be available.

Soon after arriving at the hunt spot the pair were again asked to join a solid group and agreed. The hunting was fast and furious and all seemed well. A monk went to go bring the group more and whilst drinking and talking the group did nae see her die. A quick death, nonetheless, but a sad one. She never returned except to reclaim her items. In the midst, Rianna did arrive and lend a hand as well. In fact, played healer she did, till the lovely Miss Kimber showed to lend her magiks of healing.

It was about that time that the overhunting of the area took its toll and soon the group hungered for more. Into the upper reaches of the temple is where they would hunt . . . and die.
