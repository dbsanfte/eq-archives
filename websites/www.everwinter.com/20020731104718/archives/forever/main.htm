Many fantasy stories read like the spaghetti westerns of the 60's: black hats for the villains, white hats for the heroes. As a reader of fantasy, I have always enjoyed those stories where the line was not clearly drawn between good and evil and in which the character's personal demons and flaws are as critical to the story as the antagonists.

While this is a fantasy story, I've tried to stay away from the cliches that predominate the genre and focus on a character driven adventure that I hope will take you down a new, strange road. I am currently attempting to find a publisher for this work and appreciate any creative criticism or comments; please e-mail me.

Thank you!
Mark Serrahn
