(Terms of Service)

All posts in the in-character section *MUST* be in-character.  In short, in-character posts are, at the minimum, the spoken words of your character--a fictitious, fantasy persona. All out-of-character posts will be removed at the discretion of those maintaining the board in question.  The following are various OOC postings that will always be deleted at the earliest convienance.

  • GIFS/JPEGS & Other Graphics Files: The House strictly prohibits the transfer or posting of sexually explicit images online. Nor does the House allow posts on the topic of exchanging such graphics; any posts soliciting graphics of this nature will be *DELETED* without notice!

  • Private E-Mail: Please do not post private electronic-mail authored by someone else without explicit   permission.

  • Phone Numbers and Street Addresses: The House does not allow members to post messages that include either a voice phone or a street address.

  • Confidential Information: Any post containing, referencing, intimating or requesting any confidential information will be subject to removal without notice. Any post containing specific information, such as (but not limited to) passwords for web access also subject to removal and disciplinary action.

  • Member Conduct: Conduct policies will be enforced on the message boards, as per our charter. These polices include:

    • Harassment: Posts accusing someone of being a hacker, listing other names of someone's characters, insisting they delete their name because it is close to someone else's or otherwise obviously harassing another member will be *deleted* without notice and may receive a disciplinary action.

    • Vulgarity: Save your sexually explicit descriptions and personal experiences for private electronic mail with consenting members. We're sometimes put in the unenviable position of having to delete postings for inappropriate language and/or content. Posts with inappropriate language will be deleted without notice. This includes masking: if you use asterisks to "blank out" letters, but you can still tell what the profanity is, it is still a violation. Erotic situations are acceptable, but graphic sexual content is not.


  • Story Line Enforcement: All new threads *MUST* be designated open or closed. Open threads are the default and are titled without any special indication of their status. Closed threads are designated with an asterisk (*); the first post also indicates the "originator," the future contact person for the message thread (via e-mail).  Closed story lines may NOT be posted on without the express permission of the originator.

  • Open Story Lines: In an open story line, no character can be killed, changed in form, or otherwise incapacitated without the explicit and mutual consent of the involved characters.

  • Closed Story Lines: In closed story lines, all outcomes are determined by the originator of the story line.
