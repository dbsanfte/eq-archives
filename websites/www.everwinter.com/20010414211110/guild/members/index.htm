The House has several tiers of duty.  Some take the task of planning and diplomacy, but all are committed to assisting Winter's children.  Some members of the House travel in more than one guise.

An asterisk (*) designates a primary character.
Low level is between first (1) and fourteen (14)
Mid level is between fifteen (15) and twenty-nine (29)
High level is between thirty (30) and fifty (50)
