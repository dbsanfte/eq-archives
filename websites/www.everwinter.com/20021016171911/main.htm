8/21/2002 ~ Site News

After a year of hibernation, the Children of Winter have awoken!  We have joined up with the Neverwinter Consortium {HERE} and look forward to returning home after so many years wandering.  What is the Consortium?  In short, the Consortium is a group of players from the original Neverwinter Nights game (1991-1997 on AOL) that have banded together to try to recreate that setting with the help of Bioware's new version of Neverwinter Nights.

For those wondering what Neverwinter Nights is, visit their site {HERE}.  Basically, it is an updated version of Baldur's Gate/Baldur's Gate II that allows people to design their own adventures with a relatively easy-to-use tool set.  Individual players/DMs host their own servers so there is no monthly fee.  The Consortium is building a persistent world by linking multiple servers together.

The site is being updated daily.  The menu system and front page was revised.  The message board link has changed.  Virtually all of the "Bleak Bastion" (guild section) has been updated.  The forums from November 2000 and all of 2001 were archived (about sixty-five new story lines).  I also took the time to add twenty new fonts to the Fantasy Font archive in the Desolate Muse section (library) and provided a selection of new music.

~dirge, Webmaster
