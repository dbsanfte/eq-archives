Seeking at the End

Posted by Trespare Winterclaw on April 26, 2000:

Walking stiffly away from the revelers outside Qeynos, Rianna blended in with the crowds going into the city and made her way slowly to the far end of town. Her thoughts whirled in a mixed maelstrom of confusion. Her soul fairly shivered in this new form, only now starting to understand and learn what it was capable of. Leaning back against the cool stones of a half wall near the fountains, She slipped a hand across her eyes to hide the sudden tears that threatened to make thier way down her face.

"They do not know...I did not know...Please, Celestial One, guide me here." Her voice emerged from these new lips, strange and yet the same, filled with the power of her prayer. Leaning her head back, Rianna peered out at this strange new darkening sky, and placed her hand over Trespare's unknowing gift.

"Why?" She cried into the winds, "Her honor knows no limits, her sacrifice no bounds, must she also suffer this loss?"

The quivering of her stomach and the queazy rush of heat to her head did not give her an answer or an absolution.
