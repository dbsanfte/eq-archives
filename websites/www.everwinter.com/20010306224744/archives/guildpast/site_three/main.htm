Shrouded in the peaks of the Magdelen Mountains, an ancient dragon's lair has been converted into a formidable fortress.  Ringed with a maze of illusions, magical traps, and guards--both mundance and fey--the Aerie is virtually impossible to find, let alone siege.

Ye make thy way through the heavily guarded gates with a nod of acknowledgement from the Brokenblades--a glance that assures ye the masters of this place know why ye've come.



The Aerie opens into a huge chamber with a vaulted ceiling.  Ye can make out several runed portals that lead off into the distance.  The age of the place is concealed in recent remodeling.  Within, ye find many places of interest.  What do ye wish to explore first?

Ye may relax in the Starlight Tavern or enter the Grand Hall.  Ye may visit the Clans, where the members of the Conclave dwell.  Or ye may enter the Darksong Spire, where the Triad resides.  You can also journey to the Tower of Auspice if you wish to apply to Conclave.
