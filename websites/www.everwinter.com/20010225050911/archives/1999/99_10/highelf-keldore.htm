A High Elf in Human Lands

Posted by ag'Keldore on October 9, 1999:

It had only been a day since Keldore had arrived with Raezel, Fera, and Vijnana to Qeynos. It had been a day since the Silver Dragon trapped in his seeming of a High Elven Wizard left the solace of the Greater Faydark, with its lofty branches and sinister orcish hordes, and still he was having trouble adjusting.

Taking Vijnana's advice, Keldore threw in his lot against the gnolls of Blackborrow immediately. They reminded him of the Crushbone Orcs from Faydark in many ways, down to the racial slurs they shouted at him and the comraderie they shared with one another. This was his problem.

At first, he had found another with which he could practice his skills and magic to futher advance the cause of the Conclave. Abruptly, his dog-catching partner had vanished, leaving him outside what he had nick-named the Pound. It was downhill from there.

In Faydark, everyone was eager to help everyone, and there was a closeness between those who would combat the scourge of orcs. In the Qeynos Hills, however, everyone seemed to have a rather selfish attitude. Perhaps it was the difference between the humans and the other Races of the Sun on Norrath. Perhaps the humans had higher standards and valued solitude. Whatever the case, he had found it very difficult to find someone to aid him in his training.

Of course, this led to death, after death, after death at the hands of the gnolls, who seemed to enjoy large parties where elf meat was served.
