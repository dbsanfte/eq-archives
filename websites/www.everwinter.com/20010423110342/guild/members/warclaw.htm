History: Warclaw stems from a militaristic clan of sapphire dragons from the Isles, but holds special importance to our history. Clan Warclaw seeks to uphold the tradition of the warrior, honoring the memory of Grax Gorin, Valator of the Star Riders.

Dogma:  Under Construction

Code of Conduct:

  • Betrayal goes against your word, and your word is your honor. Give it very carefully. If someone makes an assumption and suffers the consequences, that’s their problem. Falsehoods should not leave your lips, but that does not mean the whole truth need come out if it is not asked for.

  • Laws are what keeps us separated from the animals and beasts. There is a purpose in them, but those that hinder your objectives should be circumvented.

  • Protect your clan's interests, protect your allies' interests, and protect your own interests. Be ever vigilant.

  • There is no need to kill innocents unless they are stupid enough to stand in your way once they have been fairly warned. Innocents often die in war. Their death sometimes preserves the order.

  • Torture is unnecessary, but a good beating often reveals the truths.
