Hail! I am Zentinlert, a Dwarven cleric of Brell, in my 19th season.

I was born in Kaladim to a kind family. My father was a smith who's specialty was making Dwarven platemail armor. My mother, well.... she was o' bit strange. I grew up around Dwarves and hadn't even seen another race until I stepped out of Kaladim for the first time.

I was stepping outside for o' bit of hunting. "Hunt only bats, son," my father had said, for I was only but six years old.

I crawled over a hill an hour later as I drew my trusty mace. The bat was hanging from a tree as I sneaked closer. The bat's eyes shot open. It let out a screach as it attacked. Well, me and my platemail armor, I thought I was invulnerable. So young and stupid I was...

The bat attacked with such force, it knocked me to the ground. As I was knocked unconscious, I felt a spell of healing race arcoss my body as a Feir'dal cleric beat down the bat. " Are you alright lad?" asked the elf.

"I'm fine, now who-and what- are you?" The high elf let out a hearty laugh.

"I am Moinester. High elven cleric, at your service." The elf bent at the waist, slightly. He walked off.

That was the day I decided my destiny as a Cleric.
