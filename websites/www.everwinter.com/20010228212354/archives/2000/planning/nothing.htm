Dreams Of Nothing

Posted by Trespare on December 2, 1999:

Gods but it had been years since she had been warm or seen any life in this mist-shrouded realm. Too long, too many whispers of evil, and too damn many drinks from the skin of old, stale wine on her hip. A shiver shook her form once again, and she paused in her questing magic to perceive herself again. Small of stature, but graceful of form, her limbs were still deeply tanned from a sun too long gone. The leather armor she wore was worn and patched in places, certainly no suitable cover for who she was. But then again, who was she?

An elf certainly, that much was apparent from the lilting ears and slightly slanted eyes that she could feel with her fingers. There was something else, however, a past and a history she knew was hers, but that did not seem to come to her calling mind. Great magics were there, sliding and writhing in a glorious, roiling cascade through her mind. What to use them for? Who knew? Where was she?

Damn that Black Death that had taken her! Oh yes, she knew what it was that caused her to be here, in this infernal shadow realm of mists and ghosts and slavering beasts of evil. The real question was why? And why did she know only that and nothing else? Sighing softly, she trudged on, in no direction at all….
