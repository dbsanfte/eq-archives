Entering the Aerie:

To enter an area of the Aerie and visit said dragons, select the appropriate letter on the map. For example, to visit li'Shar, White Highlord, select the "O" cave and enter the White Caverns.  Note, the Grand Lords are accessible through any Caverns that fall under their rule or the Celare Triad Quarters in the Grand Hall.  NOTE:   The Grand Hall of Dragons contains many of the areas not listed in the menu, including general message boards, the Temple of Io, the Tavern of Nathair's Mirth, the Throne of Dragonkind, and the Athenaeum of Chronepsis and the Chambers of the Triad and Officium and all their operations.
