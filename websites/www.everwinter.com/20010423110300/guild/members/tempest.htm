History: Clan Tempest, though named for a group of heroic dragons of the Bronze Cliffs, is an amalgamation of several similar outlooks, and the heroics of several individuals. Among these are the valiant human warrior Verro, who shed his blood for what became the House in Neverwinter and Sosaria, as well as the canny tactician and organizer White Leopard, who tutored our Queen Rianna, now known as Trespare, back in Neverwinter.  While the legend Olam must split his time between commitments to the House and the Conclave, Aspect N'Viathon--a powerful high elven wizard--leads the Clan.

Dogma: Dogma: Ah, of course you're drawn to Clan Tempest! When given the choice, who else would you join? That's rhetorical! Just look at all we've done, the wars we've won, and the beauty we've wrought. It's no wonder you envy us. What a lucky person you are, to be sitting there, listening to me!

What can I tell you of our Clan that is not blatantly obvious to those with eyes? We love beauty, both in form and function. Our cousins in Justice often shine in a field of bloodshed, but on the lapping waves of the sea, when peace reigns, they are like statues.

We relish the resplendent nature around us. And, when time permits, we love the sea best. We know the songs of the whales and dolphins; they sing to us of things they see and treasures they have found. They are able to report on the activities of pirates, the hyenas and jackals of the trade currents, whom we stop whenever they cross us. Preying upon the weak or ganging up on the strong is typical of these bottom feeders. If killing is the only way to stop them from preying upon the innocent, then so be it.

Ah, the ocean. Water is beautiful, in all it's forms, salt, fresh, and mist. Even the storms show the beauty of the water in the crashing of thunder and roaring of waves. There is purpose and power in that, and balance, for the tides come and go in regular cycles, the rivers swell and freeze and ebb with the seasons, and the calm of a lake surface brings peace and contemplation. Still, we are often called past the shores.

When we travel the land, we do so in harmony with nature. The forests are green seas to us. While we have few true druids among us, we are warriors who appreciate the outside world. Yes, we are warriors, almost all of us… though we fight with flair and stealth, when need be. The magnificence of a muscled and graceful body is a virtue among us. Our senses, acute from the time spent observing that around us, make us fine trackers, hunters, and archers. There is a time for arrow, but also a time for axe and sword, too, or perhaps a spell of obvious power and elemental mastery… we are accustomed to battle.

We of Clan Tempest prefer to think of ourselves as friendly. We find it amusing to watch others, perhaps even joining in their adventures-fighting for the right side is not only helpful to your friends but promotes peace and quiet! Perhaps you think our way placid, dull. Not true! We are the storms of the House. Our temper erupts from time to time. Despoilers earn most of our wrath-vandals, pirates, thieves, and worse. How could they possibly compete with us?

Code of Conduct - IC:

  • Betrayal is base. Give your word and stick to it, but don't be stupid about who you give it to. There are those would abuse it.

  • Lying? Well, exaggeration is perfectly fine. Beauty is rarely clear to those outside the Clan.

  • Bend or break the laws if necessary. Laws should promote happiness and peace. If they don't, do what is right.

  • Protect your own interests. Your family is of great importance. Be true to your clan.

  • Innocents are a thing of beauty. They are goodness in a tangible form. Protect them.

  • Torture is a crime. Face your opponent down in battle, by ambush if you must, but do not abuse those you've captured.

Code of Conduct - OOC:

  • Maintain a stature when in the company of others.  You are not expected to get along with all the people, all the time.  If you strongly feel you are in the right, rather than going public, try to resolve it within /tells with the party in question, if this becomes apparently not an option, please do not hesitate in contacting a Guide to mediate in a private environment.  NEVER lose your composure in public as this reflects poorly upon House Winterborne and the Clans within it.

  • Always make every effort to adapt to your group, and not only make your experience enjoyable for yourself, but for those around you as well.

  • Kill Stealing is strictly forbidden.  Never intentionally take a creature or mob that another is camping or fighting.  If an occurrence does take place that was unintentional, do everything in power to rectify the situation and demonstrate that House Winterborne and Clan Tempest does not practice in this type of play. Some examples of rectification are, but not limited to:  Let the other player loot the mob in question OR if possible, utilizing magic, increase that players stats, or advantages within the game (otherwise known as buffing).

  • Vulgar or offensive language will not be tolerated (either in groupsay, shout, ooc, guild, or otherwise).  Vulgar and offensive language only demonstrates a lack of maturity and posture within a RPG game.  This is also a violation of the Sony/Verant policy for playing as well.

  • House Winterborne and Clan Tempest Chain of Command.  The proper chain of command shall be used for clan/guild issues.  The clan/guild chain of command will be made known to all new recruits.

I. General By-Laws

  • A. Thou shalt represent thy Clan/House well.

    • 1. All guild members are required to enact their characters in an in-character, role-playing fashion when speaking aloud (/say) or on the guild channel. Out-of-character comments must be designated as OOC, either through "OOC:" or double parenthesis surrounding the comment.

    • 2. Roleplaying is central to House Winterborne and Clan Tempest. We are willing to teach those with an interest, but we seek skilled role-players as recruits.

  • B. Thou shalt nae create discord among thy fellows.

    • 1. All guild members are expected to behave in a mature fashion.

    • 2. The EverQuest terms-of-service will be strictly enforced.

    • 3. No harassment, profanity, or vulgarity will be tolerated.

    • 4. No hacking will be accepted.

    • 5. Dynamic role-playing is acceptable, but consider the interests and welfare of your guild-mates.

    • 6. Arguments are good sources of character development, but petty bickering adds nothing positive to the gaming experience.

  • C. Thou shalt reply the wishes of House officers promptly and to the best of thy abilities.

  • D. Electronic mail from guild officers (indicated on the roster) should be answered promptly and with as much detail as needed.

  • E. Administration functions should be secondary to the role-playing; officers who donate their time to these tasks should be treated with respect.

  • F. If a player is going on a leave of absence of more than one week, we request notification.

  • G. Thou shalt nae steal the victory of another character. Should an accident befall thee, have the dignity to make amends.

    • 1. House Winterborne tolerates no kill stealing.

    • 2. We define kill-stealing as follows:

      • a. Taking a kill that somebody has already engaged, or taking a spawn that somebody has been camping.

      • b. If accidental kill-stealing occurs, we recommend you compensate by a buff spell or allowing the other character to loot.

      • c. If you are a victim of kill-stealing, we have trust in a member’s ability to gauge whether engaging in kill-stealing in compensation is acceptable.

  • H. Thou shalt be permitted inheritance and assistance from the wealth of t’ House.

    • 1. House Winterborne allows what is commonly referred to as "twinking", giving items to other characters that are beyond their means. We prefer these cases of gifting aren’t exorbitant.

    • 2. Banded armor and a fine steel weapon, enchanter-bought jewelry, these are acceptable, with perhaps an item of real-value or two. On the other hand, full rubicite for a first level character is excessive.

  • I. Treasure ye gain shall be offered first to the benefit of thy House mates.

    • 1. When items are found, before auctioning them through the general channels, make certain the guild members have a five (5) day period to purchase them for a fair price. For example, a savant’s cap might fetch 25 PP to 40 PP on the open market, depending on auction location. Offering this item to another guild member for 25 PP would be acceptable.

    • 2. Gifts are always welcome, but we understand characters earn their "living" on their finds.

  • J. Combat with other power-groups is permitted, as well as duels and Arena battles.

    • 1. PvP+ characters are welcome and dueling is acceptable.

    • 2. Since the PvP system of EverQuest entails consent, duels between guild members are also acceptable, providing both are in agreement.

  • K. New recruitment into Clan Tempest

    • 1. All new recruits MUST have an existing sponsor within the clan or house.

    • 2. All new recruits MUST be provided a full background, by-law, and code of conduct to Clan Tempest prior to being invited into Clan Tempest.

    • 3. All new recruits MUST submit a personalized biography and character photograph to Dhirge prior to an invite.

    • 4. It is strongly advised that all new recruits be notified that a 30 day trial period will commence upon acceptance into Clan Tempest, at which time the officers of Clan Tempest will make a decision upon the new recruit.

      • a. Be it known this decision will be held privately, and in the strictest secrecy, no one officer shall be held accountable for denial into Clan Tempest.

      • b. The basis will be a majority vote among officers, and the Clan Leader shall count the votes, and notify the Master at Arms to notify the new recruit of the decision.

  • L. Grouping with other Tempest Clan Members.

    • 1. All Clan Tempest members shall make every effort within their power to continually group with other members of Clan Tempest if they are online.

    • 2. No Clan Tempest member shall ever, at any time, engage another Clan Tempest member in combat.

    • 3. Only upon Clan Leader Approval shall any Clan Tempest member engage another Clan Member of House Winterborne.

    • 4. Under no circumstance shall any two or more Clan Tempest members publicly defile or degrade one another. This WILL be held privately and strictly between the two members.

      • a. If a solution cannot be attained through private tells to one another, then a hearing before the Master at Arms shall take place.

      • b. The final decision of the Master at Arms SHALL be honored by both parties, and honored then and thereafter.

      • c. If this decision is not honored, The Clan Leader will impose the Trial of Fang & Claw.
