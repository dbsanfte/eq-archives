Out-of-Character Note:  The House history is a work in progress.  Frequently, it makes reference to events that occured within the Council of Wyrms or Conclave of the Wyrm.  The Council was a Neverwinter guild that existed between late 1995 and late 1997.  Due to a potential trademark infringement, the guild changed it's name to the Conclave of the Wyrm.  The Conclave is still active, a sanctioned guild on the Xegony Server of Everquest, but under different leadership.

Citizens of Xegony, heed the eldest of heralds--the biting wind of the north, the raging storm from the plains of eternal frost. It bears a tangled tale from the nadir of the Lair of Lady Vox, a legend of exiled dragons and the spawn of an archmagi.

Long ago, an erudite of great magical prowess, a peer of Miragul, formed a pact with the rogue white dragon. His experiments led to an immortality quite unlike the Lich—fame through his creations. He constructed a race of automatons—he termed them animations—using the combined might of enchantment, sorcery and necromancy. The first generation was promised to Vox, the lesser known as Ice Goblins, and the greatest were veritable giants of frost and ice. Her servants forged an empire in the north, outside the rule of the Ring of Scale.

The second generation is the stuff of myth. It is said that this archmagi created artificial life. Taking the forms of all the races of Norrath, these "animations" were identical to their emulated forms, be they human, elf, or troll. When the archmagi died, his vassals hid the second generation within many cities.

Twenty four years ago, a dragon from a different land fled a God of undeath and the wrath of his own kind. dr’Shivrehel Despana—called Dirge among men-- found his way into Neriak, emerging from his world as a Teir’dal. During his studies within the Third Gate, he came to be the guardian of two of these animations when their "mother" was cursed by the clergy of Innoruuk as a Blasphemer. He fled to the land of Neverwinter, and his kind survived there, the origin of his charges forgotten.

After the fall of the city of Neverwinter, he returned to Norrath only to find tales of other animations… and their "malfunctions." The archmagi—Winter—had not achieved the immortality he dreamed of. His children were failing to diverse magical maladies… crippling pain, blindness, even insidious madness. His adopted daughter and consort was not immune.

One eve, the Lady Vox came to him in a lucid dream. Her army was failing, falling to the same short-comings. Renewing the ancient pact, he drew upon the assistance of his old companions from Neverwinter to establish a noble House in Qeynos. Mercenaries were hired and the family, friends, and lovers of animations mustered to banner. Aided by Vox’s agents, they began their search for the other children of Winter—the Winterborne—and a cure to their impending doom.

Recently, a momentous change within the House occurred. Queen Rianna Blisterclaw, who once led Dirge, Olam, and many other members of the House in Neverwinter, was summoned from her ghostly state and bound into the form of a wood elven ranger. Pleased to work from behind the scenes and gather intelligence without the burden of political maneuvering, Dirge stepped back into the more staid role as Assistant House Master. Mistress Trespare Winterclaw, as she is known in the land of Norrath, struggles to learn the past struggles that have fallen upon her friends, and understand the ways of this new world… while Lady Vox continues to exert more influence over the young House.

OOC: Interested in a role-playing guild on the Xegony server? House Winterborne was formed from several founding members of the Conclave of the Wyrm, known as the Coterie of Wings (CW) on Tanarus, and the Council of Wyrms in Neverwinter Nights. We have been active, in one form or another since 1995, and seek to bring our brand of role-playing and camaraderie to EQ. We are interested in mature players with the desire, initiative, and patience to build a enclave of epic role-playing and adventure. Please visit our site if interested. Class / Level / Race are unimportant. We also value interaction with other role-playing guilds and individuals. Allies and enemies for RP events are welcome.
