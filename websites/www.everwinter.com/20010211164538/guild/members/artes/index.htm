Working the wheat fields of West Karana, Artes takes a moment to wipe his brow and rest his aching muscles. His eyes fixate upon the setting sun recalling the lesson taught to him this very morning.

Master Tisquane, "Artes, today ye shall help with the fall harvest in the Karanas. Think not only of this task as work but as a chance to practice your monk skills. Focus mind and body on the tool as an extension of yourself. This is the lesson ye shall master until the fall harvest is complete."

Artes picks up the scythe with both hands mumbling, "Tis true I have never learned to wield an item which requires both of my hands." He begins to cut the wheat with an awkward swing while his mind concentrates on the perfect way to wield the device. Remembering the teachings from his master recharges his will to complete the work this day. As nightfall descends upon the land, Artes prepares his camp falling asleep the moment his head rests on the makeshift pillow.

Many moons pass before the next entry is scribed.

The master has traveled from Qeynos to oversee the final days of the harvest. From a hilltop, Master Tisquane observes young Artes handle the scythe with confidence. He controls the tool with such grace, his motions form an elaborate dance as he harvests the wheat.

Master Tisquane walks down the hill approaching Artes, "Young lad, I am most pleased in your skill development. The time has come to send you on a journey of self-identity. Many seasons ago you joined the brotherhood after your parents' were slain by the foul gnolls of Black Burrow. What you do not realize is the parents lost on that dreadful day were not blood, but foster parents. You are truly a son of Lady Vox, a Winterborne."

Pausing a moment, "The time has come for you to travel the land seeking out other children of Winter. Learn of your past to master the present and future. Only together can all the children of Winter comprehend and dispel the maladies which strike many children now."

"The training I have given to you over the years has prepared you for this day. All my wisdom is already bestowed and I can no longer further your training of the body. Take this raw silken robe and Wu's gauntlets as a gift in celebration of your becoming a man."

So this is goodbye, Artes thinks as he wipes his tear filled eyes. "I accept this quest and am bound to live by the honor and code of the brotherhood as I leave my homeland in search of answers." Compiled by a scribe of the Qeynos monk guild.
