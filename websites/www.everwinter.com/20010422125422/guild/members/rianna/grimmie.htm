Grimmie is a classic ogre shaman, niave and stupid, but vested with massive powers she has trouble controlling.  She stays close to her home, and will strike up conversation with anyone who passes by, often bestowing them with her magic, asked for or not. Born in Grobb, she was raised by her parents to be a 'bug smusher' like them. One fateful day, Grimmie happened to be out practicing her 'smushing', when Vox was inducting a different person to her ranks nearby.  Mistakenly thinking that Vox was speaking to her, and somehow inbued with the magic intended for the other, she has pursued her calling with religious fervor.
