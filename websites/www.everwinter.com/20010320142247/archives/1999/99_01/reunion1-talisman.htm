Family Reunion

ag'Talisman - Sunday January 3, 1999

Talisman flew toward Grand Master Jabbuk's lair. As he flew his eyes searched the ground. High above the rocky hills belonging to the copper celares he glimpsed a form lying below.

He banked and circled.

*Fagan?* he thought then plummented down to his friend. Flaring his wings he halted his fall. Shifting his half-elven seeming as his feet touched the ground.

He checked her pulse. Cursing silently, Talisman unsheathed Caliburn and searched the area. *No one, only a few boulders and rocks*.

Bending down he carrassed Fagan's bloody face. *Someone is going pay for this outrage but first I will do what I can for Fagan.*

His other hand drove Caliburn into the ground. It parted stones and sliced through rocks. Talisman knelt and placed Fagan's head his lap. Stroking her hair he started the spell.

Serle waited. Talisman's eyes could not see him as long remained part of the shadows. He left the shadows and watched.

All of Talisman's concentration was be focused on the spell. He crept up and slide the collar around Talisman's neck.

Serle - Tuesday January 5, 1999:

Serle slipped the torc around Talisman neck. A brillant silver light flashed as the spell took effect. Serle's laughter boomed as Talisman's own spell ruined. He rolled his eyes when Talisman fought the torc's influence. He knew. Talisman's battle was lost. It was useless to resist. The torc was created using the same principles and magic as the dragon orbs.

Long ago, Talisman was held under the torc's spell. It would not any different today. HIS father owned Talisman. It was fitting that Elwin Whitelock's son inherit his father's belongings. Yes, indeed he would have all of his inheritance. This miserable creature had stolen his father's love from him. It was his fate to even the balance.

"Toad". Serle said.

Talisman's form blurred. It refocused into a giant toad. Serle laughed. "Good, my slave. Human, open a portal home".

Talisman shifted to human form. Dark hair, hazel eyes stared lifelessly at the world. His hands wove the air. Time and space latered and stretched until they split apart. A portal stood before them.

"Dragon, you will take me home".

Talisman shifted to his true form. Held out claw and gently raised Serle to his back. Wings flared out and they lifted. Entering the portal which abruptly closed.

Pinnacle - Tuesday January 5, 1999:

Solon took Fagan advice. He had a cot brought to Aidan's room and was prepared to sleep on it. Before going to sleep he looked at Aidan. She was silent, so silent. Bowen and Fagan stated it was a matter of time before she would wake. Be patient they advised.

He was tired of being patient. He could not help shake the feeling that something was going to happen. They were balanced on a pinnacle and he could not tell which way they would fall. Casting his troubles to IO. Solon drifted to sleep.

His eyes closed.

"TALISMAN!" Aidan screamed....
