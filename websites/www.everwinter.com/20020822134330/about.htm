Welcome to EverWinter!  No doubt the first question on your mind is precisely what we have in store for you.  EverWinter is the community that serves the Winterborne gaming group; it is an attempt at providing useful resources for fantasy role-playing.  Specifically, we cater to players from the Advanced Dungeons & Dragons "pen and paper" game and the Neverwinter Nights online game.  The following detail the completed sections of the site and what they hold.

~dirge



The Bleak Bastion is the home site of the Neverwinter Nights role-playing guild known as "Winterborne"; located on the Neverwinter Consortium; for more information on the Consortium persistent world visit our links section.
Included is a vast history and ongoing journals of the group.
Allies, enemies, a current roster, and message boards complete the site.
Enter the Bleak Bastion through the Menu on your Left.



The Desolate Muse is an archive of fantasy information.

It includes the message board RP from the Conclave of the Wyrm guild (1997 - 1999) and Winterborne guild (2000 - Present).

The archives also hold fantasy fonts, languages, AD&D netbooks, and even a complete original novel.

This section also contains a Labyrinth (movie) fan page, and will be the future home of image galleries and other fantasy-related files of interest.

Enter the Desolate Muse through the Menu on your Left.



The Frozen Forge is an affiliate store provided through Amazon.com.
AD&D products, fantasy novels, and fantasy videos are targetted.
All proceeds go towards maintain the EverWinter.com site.
Enter the Frozen Forge through the Menu on your Left.
