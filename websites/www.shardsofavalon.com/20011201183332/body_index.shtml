Vulak and co
Thursday, November 29, 2001 by Zammel

Another patch comes and goes, another vulak does the same. He put out a Mrylokars Dagger of Vengeance for Tass (IS), a Viik's Pauldrons of Pain for Neero (VS), and a McLear's Boots for Kohnan (VS).

King Tormax also died a swift death, and he dropped a Horn of Hsagra and a Crown for Illuminati Sancti, and a Boots of the Storm (Ranum) and Wristguard of Thunder(Shandoa) for SoA/VS.

Before the patch, we took down Vindi/Statue, which results in a vindi earring, a Breastplate of Eradication (Pherawk), and other crap i cant remember. We even decided to take a quick shot at The Avatar of War, for fun. We were going along nicely, but 1 minute into the fight, Sober got hit with a bad round + a bad flurry, and took nearly 6,000 damage, through defensive in one round and subsequently died.... there wasnt much hope after that. But well, it was fun, and we managed to get AoW to around 85% on our first try.

Sontalak also died somewhere along the line.


Tunare slain!
Sunday, November 25, 2001 by Zammel

In an attempt to fill the void between patch days and respawn, we decided we were bored enough to clear PoG and try Tunare. The clearing was long and boring, there are seriously too many mobs in that zone. One by one, sometimes ten by ten, the mobs fell. Sometimes it was 30 by 30, and we fell. But in the end, the zone was clear, and we buffed up for Tunare.

Our first attempt was pretty shocking. We made some strategy adjustments and vowed to stop sucking. The vow was broken on our second attempt, so we all camped and came back. The third attempt was the clincher... Tunare had 1 pixel of red left in her name, and we still managed to lose. Incredibly pissed off, we ressed in and returned with a vengeance. On our fourth shot, around 5:30am est, Tunare went down with ease. We had a total of two deaths, one being the puller, and the other being Sober.

Tunare truly is a tough, yet fun fight. She is definately one of the harder mobs in everquest at the moment, and her loot reflects this.

Sorry about the inventory stuck in the screenshots, blame Lemieux!

Tunare put out a Braid of Golden Hair for Lionelies



A Girdle of Living Thorns for Sitten(IS)



An Essence of Nature for IS (So sorry i cant remember who, been a long day!!!)



And an Earring of Orgasm for Sanili (VS)



History was made, the first slaying of Tunare on Xegony.... and about the 5th in the entire everquest community. Congratulations to you all.


Vulak times two

Second Vulak had a Ssra's Bloodstone Eyepatch, which went to IS, aswell as the servers first Abashi's Rod of Disempowerment, which went to Taehoon(IS), and the servers first Jaelin's Katana, which our very own Frunkle recieved.

Congratulations to all the winners.


Furball Power.

Today was a fun day. Our combined IS/SoA/UN/Ook Ook/Machin Shin/Venator Sapentia/Forgive me if i forgot you, ToT3 team "Team Furball", dominated the server and took out the title. First up we faced a formidable team from Inner Circle, but with a bit of luck and effort we prevailed, with an 18-0 victory. Our next battle was against the combined Death Knights/Undaunted team who put up a good battle, but were also beaten flawlessly. We all recieved Golden Championship Rings and look forward to doing the best we can on test server. Wish us luck!

We also cleared out a chunk of NToV before the ToT, as well as the first 3 warders in ST, Dain, and other junk.
