Test of Tactics II Update
Tuesday, February 13, 2001 by Kellen

SoA/DK fielded two teams, one team led by Ranum (with Binnon, Zelena, Calek, Kellen, Ceinwen, Lemieux as members) and the other team led by Fuego (with Megolien, Citron, Newlaen, Nadilx, Zerax, and Balas). Ranum’s team bowed out of the first round losing to “Havenscrubs”, however, Fuego’s team did very well advancing to the Final’s match, even beating Inner Circle’s “House of Pain” team along the way. Fuego’s team faced off with Havenscrubs during the Finals, but the server unexpectedly went down during the battle, and was thus postponed until the Inter-Server Test of Tactics competition to be held on Feb 23-24. Havenscrubs were ahead 3 kills to 1, but unfortunately for them, the battle will start with a clean slate to determine who will represent Xegony.

Good luck to Fuego and his team!! You guys did our guilds proud no matter what happens. *cheers*
