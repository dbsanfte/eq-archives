ToV Weekend
posted Sunday, April 22, 2001 by Kellen

We set out to explore and learn how to survive in the West Wing of the Temple of Veeshan (as far as we can go anyway being KOS to everything in there and we had no mage to lug us around.) and while we did manage only to go deeper than usual on the second day, it was a good couple days overall. There were also lots of CRs hehe, kudos to our monks and rogues for doing such a great job, once under the feet of Gozzrem, a white dragon that is probably Gorenaire's Big Daddy. J



The Gozzrem fight was fun even though we got clobbered bad. Not knowing what to expect, we charged his room and beat on him for a good minute until we got another roamer wurm. This guy has a lot of HPs and the AE damage from him and the wurm was just too much, so we ran away like chickens back to our staging spot with Zammel, the head chicken, leading the way. J

Loot was decent, we didn't get much on the first day since we just stayed at zone in but the West Wing mobs had a nice drop rate (too many wurms though). Congrats to major piece winners Ceinwen for Ancient Leather Tunic and Nadil for Ancient Silk Robe and Boots.

Other winners for those interested: Julien(leather boots, cap), Sober(plate helm), Kellen(plate boots), Vandu(silk gloves), Citron(chain coif), Gradie(silk gloves), Seleron-DK(leather boots), Gininea-DK(leather boots), Megolien-DK(chain sleeves), Calren-DK(plate gloves), Wink-DK(silk sleeves, bracer), Lesbrina-DK(chain coif), Bebin-DK(chain sleeves), Dyne-DK(plate bracer), Ogarok-DK(plate bracer), Sebasttian-DK(plate gloves), Raethen-VS(silk sleeves), Frakis-VS(chain coif), and Crazdem-VS(silk sleeves).


Trakanon and PoG Treants Slain
posted Saturday, April 21, 2001 by Kellen

Another server crash and Trakanon is up, once again providing us the opportunity to obtain more Trak Teeth required to gain entrance to Veeshan's Peak. Afterwards, we all went to Plane of Growth and beat on Treah and Fayl before calling it a night. Congrats to all winners!

Trakanon: Borniel(Donal's BP), Brilinnia-VS(Singing Steel BP) and Kevork-DK, Lesbrina-DK, Crazdem-VS for Trakanon's Teeth.

Cazic Slain Yet Again
posted Friday, April 20, 2001 by Kellen

For the third time in five days, Cazic-Thule lay fallen on our feet, however, he didn't go down quickly this time around as he was able to fend off our shaman attack debuffs for two battles before finally succumbing to our forces. Thanks to all who stuck it out and stayed until we nailed him.



Treasure Trove: Bile Etched Obsidian Chokers, Halo of the Enlightened and Robe of Inspiration

Congrats to Aerylin, Binnon-DK and Myrnia for winning the choker, halo and robe respectively. Other good drops for the night went to Raethen-VS (Zombie Flesh Bracers) and Julien (Grotesque Girdle)



Cazic Thule Dead Again
posted Thursday, April 19, 2001 by Kellen

What was supposed to be another day of "boss" mob killing-spree turned into a frustrating night of boring and repetitive killing of PoF mobs due to a couple of server crashes -- first one occured while we were buffing up for the Cazic-fight and the second one while we had dracoliche's health in slivers. The server finally stayed up long enough for us to clear the plane again and defeat Cazic-Thule for the second time within four days.



Treasure Trove: 3x Bile Etched Obsidian Chokers

Congrats to Oumobim-VS, Grimbone-VS and Kevork-DK for winning the chokers
and to Aedalare and Myrnia for winning the Zombie Flesh Bracers off Dracoliche.



Two orders of Trak and a side of Velketor please...
posted Wednesday, April 18, 2001 by Kellen

Taking advantage of an emergency patch after a quick Trak raid, we were able to do a repeat of killing Trakanon when servers came up a couple hours later. After that, we were off to Velketor's Labyrinth and do battle with the big man again and refine our battle tactics. We were very successful this time around, killing him on the first attempt, even with a bad case of link-deaths at the start. Congratulations to everyone who participated in the 3 raids...it was a long but glorious night indeed!!



Treasure Trove: Boots of Bladecalling (Sable), Vermillion Orb of Torrefaction(Aedalare), Bracelet of Frostbite (Auen) and some spells.

Trakanon Drops: 2x Manna Robe (Vandu and Cabar-VS), Cloak of Piety (Aerylin), Poisoned Mantle (Zwei-VS), and 10x Trakanon Teeth (Keenan, Newlaen, Belain, Zwei-VS, Raethen-VS, Zortek, Lemieux, Calren-DK, Neero-VS, Aydindril-VS)


Trakanon and Zlandicar Slain!!
posted Monday, April 16, 2001 by Kellen

A routine Trakanon raid was quickly followed by a trip to Dragon Necropolis just to see how we would fare against Zlandicar. With an army of 36 people and only 4 clerics, we didn't really expect to win but thanks to the brilliant tactics researched and formulated by Aedalare and Zammel, we were amazed ourselves that we killed him on our first try!! Great job and congratulations to all SoA/DK/VS folks who participated!!

Trakanon's Treasure Trove: Tolan's Darkwood Breastplate (Lionelies), Elder Spiritist's Breastlate (Damas-DK), and 2 Trakanon's Tooth (Sober and Megolien-DK)

Zlandicar's Treasure Trove: Zlandicar's Heart (Aedalare), Dragonclaw Shard (Aerylin), Cracked Claw of Zlandicar (Kellen), Zlandicar's Talisman (for Sleeper's Tomb key quest) (Lesbrina-DK) and Frakadar's Talisman (for Harla Dar's quest - Shroud of the Dar Brood) (Sober).

A Dragon-Killing Spree
posted Saturday, April 14, 2001 by Kellen

Gorenaire a no-show -- Talendor, Kelorek'Dar, and a bunch of CoV dragons die in her stead

Too bad Gorenaire didn't show, and for the first time ever it looked like we had enough of the right classes to pull it off...perhaps another time, maybe in a month or two :P

Talendor was up so he became our first victim of the night, kinda weak on loot but I guess that's expected. Congrats to Newmoon and Brillinia-VS (sp?) for Selo's Drum of March, Balladar-DK for Kavruul's Mystic Pouch, Alopezer-VS for Gauntlets of Fiery Might, and Sebasttian-DK for Red Dragonscales.

Then some of us went to Western Wastes and played with the dragons a bit..couple nice fights against Von -- at least we got our revenge before we left. Too bad he only had a crappy 5ac talisman for loot. We also got a bunch of talismans, dragon chain armor, and dragon head quest items from various nameds all over the zone.



Just as we were about to call it a night, we heard that Kelorek'Dar was up so we rushed to Cobalt Scar and quickly killed the giant fish. Once again, loot wasn't very good -- 3 Spine Razors, a couple seadragon scales, and a Bioluminiscent Orb that went to Gradie. At least it was a good closer for the night. Great job everyone!
