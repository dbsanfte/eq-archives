April 3, 2001

Monday Night Raid Marathon

It was a long night - first we slew Severilous (who had nothing good unfortunately) - no green scale :(  just an Orb of Tishan (Gradie), McVaxius Horn of War and 5 spells.  Having the groups set already, we then decided to do a sneak attack on Wuoshi as we teleported directly to the Wakening Lands dragon portal to fight him.  It was kinda fun and saved us some running hehe :)  His drops were a Skydarkener (Norl-VS), 3 Emerald Scales (Klaget-VS, Moosius-VS, Marlana) and 4 spells.   After that, we were off to Chardok to try and push forward some epic quests -- we got a Manisi Herb (Auen?), Prince's Head ( Myrnia), 2 singed scrolls (Klaget-VS, Cheevers-DK), and an Ancient Blade for (Sober).  Hopefully some of these quests will be finished soon!  Congrats to all who got something tonight!

March 27, 2001

Innoruuk Slain!

Shards of Avalon and Venator Sapentia forces marched up to the Plane of Hate to do battle with the all-new and improved Prince of Hate.  The raid was mildly eventful (plenty of unneeded loot decayed while clearing up to his lair as usual), until Innoruuk was aggro'ed and pulled inadvertently.  Unprepared, caught in a bad spot, and unable to slow down his attacks, we still battled him down to 50% HP until all clerics ran out of mana and tanks started dropping fast.   The second attempt was very successful and was a near perfect assault (3 or 4 deaths at most), largely attributed to getting him positioned properly and being able to successfully land a slow debuff on him (Thanks to Kinna of Cognatus for providing Malo services).  Good job to everyone involved!


Innoruuk battles SoA/VS

Treasure Trove: Bloodstar Pendant, Brewer's Mesh Cloak, Skinner's Belt, Ring of Pureblood, 2x Eye of Innoruuk

March 26, 2001

Weekly Highlights

Sorry for the lack of updates lately as I haven't been involved much in recent epic-related raids or dungeon crawls, (and no one bothers enough to send me any type of news or screenshots to publish hehe)

Anyway, belated Congrats to Damas of Death Knights for completing his druid epic quest after we killed Venril Sathir last week after a painful 6-hour wait for his respawn.  Too bad he didn't drop the Gnarled Staff that Nadil needed to complete his epic weapon, would've been neat to complete 2 epics in the same day.

We also went on a Hate raid last Friday to explore the second floor and to try to make a run at Inny, unfortunately, we didn't get to him before people started logging.  Next time we'll probably skip clearing the first floor and go straight to second since there must've been about 150-200 mobs in that floor alone.   Congrats to all armor winners and to Sober for getting his Hand of Maestro. (2 more quest pieces to go!)

     
Ranum? Is that you?                 The Hate Council  

Last Sunday, after an aborted ToV raid, SoA went on a Western Waste dragon-killing spree.  Lots of fun and good loot, a couple Matchless gear dropped among others, and everyone who needed a NoS-type necklace got one (including twinks!).  Also met Strong Horn for the first time, which dropped a nifty horn .  It was a fun fight too, that knockback proc was nasty hehe.
