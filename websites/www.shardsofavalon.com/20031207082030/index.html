Alliance is recruiting
Alliance is recruiting! What is Alliance? Alliance is a long standing partnership of three guilds, Illuminati Sancti, Shards of Avalon, both guilds strongly raiding oriented with their own personalities, and Venator Sapentia, a more family oriented guild with a raiding segment and invitation only membership. Alliance has been a long term partnership, stretching back to the Velious era, and has been at or near the top of the End Game ever since.

Where is Alliance at the moment? We are an organization on Xegony that raid the Elemental Planes, within the EST time zone. Now that's not too rare a thing at the moment, to be raiding the Elemental Planes, but we are also one kill to Time, and need a few extra key people to help fill in the recent gaps that have sprung up in membership.

Recruitment is open for almost all classes! High need on Cleric, Wizard, Magician, and Rogue. Medium need on Ranger, Necromancer, Druid, and Beastlord. Low need on Enchanter, Bard, and Shaman. No need on ShadowKnight, Paladin, Warrior, and Monk, but don't be afraid to post an app, any exceptional player will be considered, no matter class! Recruitment requirements can be found here. For higher need classes, we may bend the minimum requirements.

Flags are not an issue to us at the moment, we will flag any and all apps we accept. Our goal is to kill Coirnav and get into Time. We are looking for exceptional players on Xegony and any willing to transfer to Xegony, who play in the Eastern time zone
