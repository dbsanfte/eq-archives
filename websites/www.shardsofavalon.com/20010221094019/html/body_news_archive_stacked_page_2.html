Week Ending 2001-01-28

January 28, 2001

Weekend Highlights

It was a relatively quiet weekend, perhaps because it was Superbowl Weekend, or maybe due to a bit of burn-out doing successive dragon raids last week.  Nevertheless, there were still a few activities that happened over the last couple days. The scheduled Plane of Growth raid was a mild success as well as some epic quest activities.

Congratulations to those who got new equipment from PoG:
Glador for Silver Moon Chest Wraps and Zammel for Spirit Weaver's Greaves.


SoA/DK vs. Tunarean Earthmelder

 

January 25, 2001

So  which dragon tonight?
 Severilous dies on an impromptu raid initiated by VS

Unfortunately for this Emerald Jungle resident, he was picked as the main attraction for tonight's entertainment.  A quick assembly of 30 people results into the death of Severilous and his pet dog.

SEVERILOUS FIGHT

Treasure Trove: Runed Bolster Belt, Treasure Hunter's Satchel, Green Dragonscales and 5 spells - Legacy of Thorns (DRU), Entrapping Roots (DRU),  Emissary of Thule (NEC), Winds of Gelid (WIZ), Voice of the Berserker (SHM).

Congrats to Zammel for receiving the Shaman spell, and to the rest of the winners in DK and VS!!

 

January 24, 2001

Trakanon Dead Again

SoA, with the assistance of friends from Death Knights and Venator Sapentia, took down Trakanon one more time in a quick and glorious battle.  It was a one-sided encounter as Trakanon didn't stand a chance of surviving the onslaught of the powerful dragon force. Very few casualties have been reported.

TRAKANON FIGHT

Treasure Trove: Elder Spiritist's Breastplate, Blood Ember Breastplate , Trakanon's Guts (bard epic quest item)
3x Trakanon Tooth (for Veeshan's Peak Key quest).

Congratulations to Ceinwen for winning the Elder Spiritist's BP and to Newmoon and Zammel for receiving the Trakanon's Guts and Trakanon's Tooth respectively. *cheers*
 Venator Sapentia won the Blood Ember BP this time around. J

January 23, 2001

SoA/DK Undead Army Owns Ragefire
Borniel gets the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh!

It was an amazing (or perhaps hilarious) sight to see as a bunch of skeletons fought Zordakalicus Ragefire seeking to obtain its Heart, which was the final piece required to finish Borniel's quest to earn the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh.  The battle ended with only a single casualty, that of a Troll who chose to remain true to his native form and refused to be subjected to the magics of necropotence.  Perhaps there is a lesson to be learned here folks -- when fighting dragons, who do you think they'll eat first --  skinny and bony skeletons or fat juicy trolls?
