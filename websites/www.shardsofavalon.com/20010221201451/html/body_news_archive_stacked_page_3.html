Week Ending 2001-02-04

February 4, 2001

Dragon Necropolis

Although we didn't tackle any big mobs at the end, the dungeon crawl was quite interesting as we fought through the Chetari tunnels and also visited the Paebalas.  We also reached Zlandicar's Lair (sorry no screenshots) and saw the big guy himself which I'm pretty sure we'll meet again.  Not much else to report besides getting a few nice toys such as the Velium Warlance (forget exact name but it's a 30/37 2H piercer) which Sober got,  a Chetari Wardstaff which went to Zortek, a 10/19 Velium Warsword (forget exact name also) that went to Calren of DK and a couple of Wrapped Entropy Serpent Spines, one of which went to me (Kellen). 

 

February 3, 2001

Severilous and Kelorek'Dar Slain

Severilous earned a swift death, once again giving up his green dragon scale which is highly sought by warriors these days. Also, while passing through Cobalt Scar en route to Dragon Necropolis, the sea monster Kelorek'Dar made a mistake of rearing its ugly head and thus fell beneath our blades and fiery magic.

Severilous Battle

Treasure Trove: McVaxius' Horn of War, Green Dragonscales, Burnt  Book for Paladin FA quest, 5 spells (Winds of Gelid (Wiz), Vengeance of Al'Kabor (Wiz), Legacy of Thorns (Dru), Form of the Hunter (Dru), Banishment of Shadows (Clr/Nec))

Kelorek'Dar Battle   Kelorek'Dar Dead

Treasure Trove: Bioluminiscent Orb, Cloak of Crystalline Waters, Kelorek'Dar Spine Razor, 2 Sea Dragon Scales and 2 spells (Aegolism (Clr), Arch Lich (Nec))

and Binnon's twink for the crappy rogue dagger!!
 

February 2, 2001

Whew! What a Night!

Trakanon didn't die so easily this time, putting up two good fights before finally succumbing to the tired and battered forces of SoA/VS/DK.  Some unlucky breaks and the less than optimal conditions proved to be our undoing in the first couple fights but we persevered and nailed him on the third try.  Thanks to all who stayed throughout the raid and next time, perhaps it will be a routine killing as usual.  Below are some screenshots from each battle and be sure to check out the "victory shot" and see our guildleader in action.
