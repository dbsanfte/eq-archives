ELP System put in effect
Thursday, September 20, 2001 by Kellen

As we continue to be involved in high-level encounters where a raid usually lasts for many hours, we have finally come to realized that we needed an impartial system to keep track of member participation as a direct factor in determining loot distribution. Effective as of today, all SoA raids (whether it be a multi-guild venture or not) will be subject to the ELP Rules, as determined by the officers of the guild or the raid leader.

Welcome to the Ghetto
Thursday, September 6, 2001 by Aedalare

Two weeks ago we killed Aaryonar for the first time on Xegony. In those two weeks we have ripped through the North Wing to become yet another server that has cleared every dragon from Temple of Veeshan.



Today we finished what we started. In our second ever Vyemm fight (without his guards in assistance) everything came together in unison and Lord Vyemm, the biggest badass in ToV hit the dirt. Congratulations to all involved. Vyemm is a fast and hectic fight where the slightest errors can spell death. Thankfully all went according to plan and he was executed flawlessly, leaving the way clear to proceed to ToV's resident loot dispenser - Vulak' Aerr. Vulak is a looong fight. He has an assload of hp, with his AE he becomes a solid war of attrition. Even calling in support at the last moment from two drake buddies we still saw him off in what is currently the most fun fight I've had (I'm sure melee don't feel the same way hehe)

The end result has seen us clearing ToV way ahead of the curve. I don't think anyone expected us to be able to come together this hard and fast, nor to conquer each of these dragons in so fast a succession. To all those people who said Xegony would be the last of the servers to clear North Wing - BZZZZ. Wrong.



Yes there was loot to be had. Congrats to Sybian(VS) and Hazenson(IS) who each got a left eye from Vyemm (freak dragon..). Lomark scored his Right eye. Vulak put out a Kelciferous Armband which went to Loric(IS), a pair of Sirrans Boots of Insanity which I (Aedalare) won and a Zeriphra Dagger of Blood which Thalin(IS) was forced to take!
