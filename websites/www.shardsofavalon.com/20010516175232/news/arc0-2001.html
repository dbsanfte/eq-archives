SoA/DK Undead Army Owns Ragefire
Tuesday, January 23, 2001 by Kellen

Borniel gets the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh!


It was an amazing (or perhaps hilarious) sight to see as a bunch of skeletons fought Zordakalicus Ragefire seeking to obtain its Heart, which was the final piece required to finish Borniel’s quest to earn the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh. The battle ended with only a single casualty, that of a Troll who chose to remain true to his native form and refused to be subjected to the magics of necropotence. Perhaps there is a lesson to be learned here folks -- when fighting dragons, who do you think they’ll eat first -- skinny and bony skeletons or fat juicy trolls?

PLANE OF SKY RAID - A Success!
Sunday, January 14, 2001 by Kellen

Continuing on from where they left off, the SoA/DK group started the day with battling drakes and sphinxes to have a confrontation with the Sister of the Spire and ultimately the Eye of Veeshan on the topmost isle of the Plane. The newfound knowledge and experience of battling the creatures of the lower isles have proved invaluable as it seemed almost routine when the 7th island of the Plane of Sky was cleared easily and methodically....

The battle raged on with the Sister of the Spire.....


....and finally on to Isle 8! (seen here posing with the Eye of Veeshan and some shots of the ensuing battle)




After an arduous climb with plenty of exciting fierce battles along the way, we found ourselves victors and can claim to be one of the few guilds in the server to ever reach the top!

However, on a more somber note, a battle somewhere else would affect the end of our journey unexpectedly. Another guild (Havenlight) had defeated the Overseer of Air in Isle 4 as we rest and recover from our battle with the Eye of Veeshan. While we were aware that this was happening, we did not realize that the Hand of Veeshan which appears after the Overseer of Air gets killed, would attack us on sight, hence we were caught unprepared and got killed. We did attempt to fight him again but due to lack of keys and the lateness of the hour, we were unable to do so. Perhaps another time.....
