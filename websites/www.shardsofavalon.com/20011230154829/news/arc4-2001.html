Veeshan's Peak!
Sunday, May 20, 2001 by Kellen

Silverwing Slain, Hoshkar Victorious

Our last several Trakanon raids had garnered us enough keys to start a decent attempt at Veeshan's Peak, one of the zones we have yet to visit, until last night...

We set out with the mission of killing Silverwing while learning as much as we can about the zone, and also possibly make an attempt at Hoshkar. It was a bit anti-climactic however as Silverwing fell down faster than Calek does when he plays with the cocks in Dreadlands.

We got a few nice items from Silverwing also, albeit more of just "trophies" nowadays. Congrats to the winners: Eevia-DK and Raethen-VS for Robes of Smothering, Ceinwen for Dragon Spine Staff, and Lesbrina-DK for the Hammer of the Dragonborn (nice right-click buff!)

Giants > Dragons
Tuesday, May 15, 2001 by Aedalare

3 DRAGONS FALL, BARD EPIC WEAPON QUEST COMPLETED

Well I think it is fair to say we put in a full day's work yesterday. First we went and paid a visit to everyones favorite poison breather Trakanon and after a few exchanged words he handed over one Donal's BP (congrats Moosius-VS), one Mrylokar BP (grat's bank) and 6 teeth that went to Myrnia, Vandu, Julien, Klaget-VS, Balladar-DK and Bodan-DK. Now go get your keys guys!

After Trakonan was tucked in we decided to pay a long overdue visit to the terror of the dreadlands Gorenaire. The last run in we had with her resulted in a win to the dragon. Eager to put one up on the scoreboard for us we geared up and let rip. When the smoke cleared the old white lay in the dust (or in my case suspended in the air - for some wierd reason the dragon was a human...)



CONGRATULATIONS to Newmoon on the completion of her epic! We were also lucky enough to garner one of Gore's prized tomes which went to Fenar for the continued glory of paladins everywhere! (see I even said that with a straight face. J) Also congrats go out to Sebasttian-DK who got the drums she dropped. The rest of the loot (warhammer/Orb of Tishan) went to twinks, so they dont get a mention J

Still eager to lay the smack down we did yet another patented hot-drop in to our favorite Wakening Lands scaley Wuoshi. The high point of this fight being Fuego zoned so slowly he missed the whole thing. Surprisingly the drops weren't total crap for a change. We got a bow, crescent blade and 2 shields, as well as 1 scale. Unfortunately at the time I was checking for Severilous in EJ, and only got back in time to find out that Orbin-DK won the emerald scale J

By now it was getting on but we decided since Kael was only the next zone over we would go and see what we could do about making Derakor the Vindicator's life interesting. Unfortunately against Derakor we hit a brick wall. After three tries we called it a night, but we learnt alot from him. Our best effort getting him to 40% with all our warriors dead in the first 20 seconds lol. Still we are pretty sure we have it sussed now and will be returning in the very near future to prove our point. Derakor, mark it off on your calendar, your time is due...

Overall a very busy night, unfotunately a little overshadowed by that giant pain (^ ^) Derekor. But any way you slice it, three dragons in one night is a good effort. I'll see what I can do about getting the drops from Wuoshi posted tomorrow too hehe.

Stay Safe all, see you in game.
