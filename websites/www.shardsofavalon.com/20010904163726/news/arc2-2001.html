Innoruuk Slain!
Tuesday, March 27, 2001 by Kellen

Shards of Avalon and Venator Sapentia forces marched up to the Plane of Hate to do battle with the all-new and improved Prince of Hate. The raid was mildly eventful (plenty of unneeded loot decayed while clearing up to his lair as usual), until Innoruuk was aggro’ed and pulled inadvertently. Unprepared, caught in a bad spot, and unable to slow down his attacks, we still battled him down to 50% HP until all clerics ran out of mana and tanks started dropping fast. The second attempt was very successful and was a near perfect assault (3 or 4 deaths at most), largely attributed to getting him positioned properly and being able to successfully land a slow debuff on him (Thanks to Kinna of Cognatus for providing Malo services). Good job to everyone involved!

Patch Day Mayhem
Tuesday, March 6, 2001 by Kellen

Havenlight leapfrogs ongoing SoA/DK raid

As usual, 3 SoA/DK groups at Ilis jails heading towards Trakanon’s Lair was not enough for Havenlight to back off on their plans to kill Trakanon, and thanks to Grigg, Callain, and some others for the extra bunch of frogloks and *gasp* even a Sebilite Golem at lower level!! Their reasoning? See Callain’s response when I talked to him about it.



So basically, since they don’t want to give up Trakanon unless a guild is already at Trakanon’s Lair with 30-something people, should we expect to be bulldozed by Havenlight members each time we want to do Trakanon same day as them, even if we form and got rolling on the Juggernaut break earlier than they do??

We ended up dying eventually, not too many people can survive ‘shrooms and ilis when healers and chanters are at 20% mana, and since we aren’t the kind of people that enjoy fighting and training others to get a spawn we want, we just gave up and left. This is the second time this kind of dispute happened and as usual, no response from Gwidion, guildleader of Havenlight, when asked to settle issues peacefully. Had the situation been reversed and we found out that HL already had a break-in group at Ilis jails headed to Trak, you can bet we’ll leave ‘em alone.
