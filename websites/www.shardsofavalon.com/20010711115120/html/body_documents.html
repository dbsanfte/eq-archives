Guild Profile

The Shards of Avalon guild was founded on May 26, 2000 by 8 charter members with Calek and Kellen as officers, with plans and dreams of conquering the new world offered by the then recent discovery of the Ruins of Kunark.  It became an official guild in Xegony on May 29, 2000 and started to recruit a few more members mostly from the recently disbanded guild Austere Dream.  While the guild was intent on becoming one of the more prominent guilds in Xegony quickly, it was still very much unprepared to tackle the many challenges ahead.  With several of its members still lacking a lot of equipment, and a lot of strategy and tactics to learn, the first few months that followed were very much focused on preparations, like raiding Planes and practicing raid tactics on occasional high level encounters.

By the end of the year, the guild has grown to about 30-member strong, and has developed a strong alliance with the Death Knights guild to supplement each other during raids.  So far, the SoA/DK alliance has conquered most of the tough zones and won battles against several gods, dragons and other dragon-caliber mobs like Cazic-Thule, Innoruuk, Ixiblat Fer, Overking Bathezid, Faydedar, Venril Sathir, Severilous and Trakanon.  During that time, the SoA name has become popular, and although the guild does not have many high profile members, its deeds are being noticed.  After the many months of preparations, SoA has become a force to reckon with in the world of Norrath.  The discovery of the Scars of Velious, new challenges came about just in time for a guild ready and eager to harvest the fruits of its efforts.

January 7, 2001
