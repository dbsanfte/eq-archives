Well you just wait buddy, you just wait.... cause your gonna get yours, you mark my words!!

While we're at it, anyone wanna buy a BE bp? hehe.

Next day stuck with naught to do, we were going to try Dain with IS, but we had a shortage of people and no real puller so instead we decided to pay a visit everyones favorite gimp giant (no not Derekor the other one). Despite a total lack of any real effort by anyone he still dropped like the gimp he is and disgorged his usual trove of rather sad caster loot. Today saw us returning once again to the Halls of Phara'Dar and giving Veeshans Peak another run. We've had a slight shortage of keys lately so we needed to get a few more done, now that we have enough it's back we go. Well first up is, as always, Silverwing. Can someone explain to me why it wont stack with agility? Congratulations also to Norl (VS) on his uber Dragon Claw Main Gauche and Emile on his Blue Flower Katana. Thanks Silverwing, while your loot is ass, it's always a pleasure killing you.

Being the lucky recipient of the first post-gate summon I got to watch Hoshkar with NO red left in his name summon me and kill me. he then proceeded to wipe others out one by one. By this time it was too late to actually finish him off, but you better believe we will be back there tomorrow, getting past this mofo and seeing if we can't pay a visit to a few of the other denizens of this dragon infested hovel. Tomorrow the saga continues.

SONTALAK - suck it down.....
Friday, July 13, 2001 by Aedalare



Well, if you had told me a week ago I'd be standing over Sontalak's dead corpse today I'd have laughed so hard I'd rupture my spleen. But here we are and there he lay. At around 11pm EST Sontalak had assumed the position and coughed up his talisman. A combined effort by Shards of Avalon, Venator Sapentia and Illuminati Sancti exerted their authoritah and broke this pug faced freak in half.

It was never going to be an easy fight, took us three tries. First time 'round we weren't really prepared for the amount of sheer damage output he can pour into a buffless warrior and before he had lost a fifth of his health Sitten went down squealing. It wasn't a few seconds before Sontalak turned and made a BBQ our of the rest of our poor melee and casters (discretion being the better part of valour I ran away into ToV, shrieking the whole time like a little girl...).

Second time round we were better prepared but it seems we forgot one little rule, and it's truly amazing how quickly your tanks die to an enraged dragon. Funny to see 10 people fall over hehe. However my humour was short lived as Sont took this oportunity to pay me back for my previous cowardice and current state of blasting him with Lure of Ice and I dropped to the pavement. My dismay turned to incredulous disbelief to zone back in and see Sontalak walking back to his guard post with NO red left in his name!!!

Gentle reader this catastrophe could not be allowed to stand. Regrouping and rebuffing ourselves we were soon ready to engage once more. Tragically the pull went horribly wrong when Sontalak failed to kill Calek at the start but we quickly recovered from our horror. Getting stuck in like the angry midget he is Sitten took charge while our clerics pumped every little scrap of mojo they couldn into his beaten body. All was going well when disaster struck. Realising that this was his final chance Sontalak invoked the dreaded lag gods and cause a server spike that threw everyone out. Combining with his enraged attack Sontalak made a final last gasp effort, slaying many brave warriors. But it was too little, too late for the mighty red beast as with a final few swipes to take some casters and rangers with him he came crashing to the ground for the first time ever on Xegony...

Let the Temple of Veeshan farmers rejoice! For no longer must they suffer under the heel of this tyrant when farming for phat lewtz! The way to Temple of Farming is clear for all and will undoubtedly be packed by people who would normally never have gone while Sontalak lived.

It was Sontalak so hopes were never high for the booty haul. In a remarkable show of good taste he dropped his Talisman for Thuel (IS) to finish his sleepers key, as well as a Exquisite Velium Battlehammer which went to Frunkle. Also a couple of bits of dragonhorn, worked boots and matchless gauntlets. Oh and spell, can't remember what, sunstrike and some others. Demi-lich rotted as I was about to loot it...

All in all a constructive day. Later all...
