Joining Shards of Avalon

The first step for anyone interested in becoming part of the guild is to familiarize themselves with our charter.  If you have not done so, then please read it now.

SoA has class and level restrictions and certain item requirements for anyone applying to the guild.  These requirements and restrictions often vary as the game and the guild demography evolve.  Please read the following requirements carefully and ensure that you have satisfied them:

Minimum Level: 54

Class Desirability (as of August 2001): 

  • VERY HIGH - Cleric, Warrior, Rogue, Monk
  • HIGH - Necromancer (57+ preferred), Magician
  • MEDIUM - Shaman, Enchanter, Bard
  • LOW - Wizard, Paladin, Shadowknight, Ranger, Druid

Required Items:

  • Old Sebilis Key
  • Howling Stones Key (if non-evil race/class),
  • Velious Expansion and Cobalt Scar Teleportation Tooth
  • Average quantity of planar armor or better
  • Resist gear items that enables you to have at least 100 unbuffed Magic Resistance while simultaneously having at least 75 unbuffed resistance on one of the following: Fire, Cold, Poison, Disease

Optional Items (expected but not enforced):

  • Journeyman's boots
  • Item with See Invisible Effect
  • Item with Enduring Breath Effect
  • Item with zero-cast time buff effect (if you do not have Jboots) like shrunken goblin earring.

In order to improve our ability to add quality members and give them the opportunity to meet existing members the following procedure will succeed the previously existing application process:

1) The applicant must meet the posted requirements for membership before advancing to the next step.

2) The applicant must find 2 people to sponsor him/her.  The easiest way to accomplish this is to either have prior friendship with members or to group with members in a group environment.  It will be the responsibility of these two members to provide scheduling information for the applicant so that he/she has access to members in a raid environment.  Both members must maintain communication so that the applicant is properly informed.

3) The sponsor will either forward an application to the officer in charge of recruitment (Zammel) or post it to the "SoA Recruitment" board and have a board admin lock the thread.

4) For approximately 1-4 weeks at the discretion of the sponsor, the applicant will need to group with members and attends raids with sponsors support.  The applicant may be added to guildchat for raid coordination though it is expected they will remove themselves shortly after each raid.

    a) This period is similar to the previous period of temporary membership and/or the pre-application period.  Temporary membership is no longer an option on voting thus it is important to group and raid together frequently during this period.

    b) Often grouping with others improves the chances of entering the guild after voting is completed so it is advised the applicant and sponsor give sufficient time before opening the voting process.

5) Once a satisfactory period of grouping has occurred the sponsor will have the application unlocked by an officer and a second person/sponsor will need to second the application before anyone else responds with comments.

6) After sufficient time for comment, all guild officers will make the final decision on the applicant and post the result to the thread and contact the applicant with the result.
