Weekly Highlights

2/15: ANOTHER RACE FOR THE PLANES ON PATCH DAY

As usual, patch day means the week-long spawns are up and this time SoA/DK rushed to Plane of Fear for the chance of getting the quest pieces from Cazic Thule as well as to obtain the final piece necessary to complete Wlog's (DK) epic particle weapon quest.  Unfortunately, a dispute for the spawns with another guild occured and we had to settle with splitting the quest mobs with them.  We ended up taking Cazic Thule who dropped the skin which Nadil won for his epic quest, and a Bloodfire which Keenan got.  Also congrats to Aerylin for getting an Umbral BP.

2/14: MONK'S LUCK AT VELKETOR'S LABYRINTH CONTINUES

A return trip to Velk's Lab resulted to another dead efreeti, and this time he coughed up an Icicle Wristband and Frozen Ereeti Boots which Lemieux and Shandoa received respectively.

February 13, 2001

Lord Doljonijiarnimorinar

Finally getting the hang of killing this bad boy, as he went down easily this time.  Congrats to Glador for winning the Efreeti Ice Staff and Zerax for receiving Glowing Red Stone.  We also got Kobold Bone Gauntlets from the Brood Master which Calek received.  Good job to everyone involved in raid.

 

Test of Tactics II Update

SoA/DK fielded two teams, one team led by Ranum (with Binnon, Zelena, Calek, Kellen, Ceinwen, Lemieux as members) and the other team led by Fuego (with Megolien, Citron, Newlaen, Nadilx, Zerax, and Balas).   Ranum's team bowed out of the first round losing to "Havenscrubs", however, Fuego's team did very well advancing to the Final's match, even beating Inner Circle's "House of Pain" team along the way.   Fuego's team faced off with Havenscrubs during the Finals, but the server unexpectedly went down during the battle, and was thus postponed until the Inter-Server Test of Tactics competition to be held on Feb 23-24.   Havenscrubs were ahead 3 kills to 1, but unfortunately for them, the battle will start with a clean slate to determine who will represent Xegony. 
Good luck to Fuego and his team!!  You guys did our guilds proud no matter what happens. *cheers*
