Joining Shards of Avalon

Membership to the guild is usually done by invitation only.   Simply put, if we do not know you, there is no chance that you can become a member.   People who are interested in joining us will have to put forth a lot of effort in getting to know and play with as many members as possible and this does not mean simply trying to get involved in our raids as they are usually stricted to SoA and its allies only (with some exceptions).

However, we do realize that we can't possibly know all good players out there, and we are always looking for people that play their classes well, familiar with group tactics (in dungeons and planes) and have the dedication to overcome the toughest challenges in the game. 

The first step for anyone interested in becoming part of the guild is to familiarize themselves with our charter.  If you have not done so, then read it now.

Just recently, SoA had enforced new level restrictions and certain item requirements for anyone applying to the guild to cut down on paperwork as we've had a lot of turned down applications lately.  Before submitting an application, please read the following requirements carefully and ensure that you have satisfied them. 

Level Minimum: 54
Class: any
Required Items:

  • Old Sebilis Key
  • Howling Stones Key (if non-evil race/class),
  • Velious Expansion and Cobalt Scar Teleportation Tooth
  • Average quantity of planar armor or better
  • Resist gear items that enables you to have at least 75 fire/cold/poison unbuffed resistance, while simultaneously having 100 unbuffed magic resistance

Optional Items (expected but not enforced):

  • Journeyman's boots
  • Item with See Invisible Effect
  • Item with Enduring Breath Effect
  • Item with zero-cast time buff effect (if you do not have Jboots) like shrunken goblin earring.

All submissions will be subject to voting of the current members.   If no one has a substantial reason why you can't join our hunts or raids, then you will be placed on an "Candidate List" which is used by the guildmembers during raids or experience hunting groups to fill up spots.   This will not earn you a /guildinvite, but for those who received a bit more favorable response (but not enough to fully get in) a tempoary membership may be granted which will allow you to have access to our guild chat and Events Forum on the message board.

There is no set time period how long a person will be in the Candidate List as it varies greatly due to difference in online time, number of raids, and other factors.  Temporary Membership usually lasts for a month, but may often vary as well.

The guild's recruitment officer will be the point of contact throughout the whole recruiting process.  However, please be advised that while occasional questions about current status and opportunities to join raids are expected, excessive inquiries are frowned upon and may result to your removal on the Candidate List.
