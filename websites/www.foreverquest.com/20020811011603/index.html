Trime Ayedareya

8-8-02 -- 09-Aug-02
T'was a cold night accross the lands of Norath this evening, and there was no place colder that the Royal chamber of Kael Drakkal. There in his throne room the vile King tormax once again sat upon his throne brooding and planning the demise of Dragonkind civilization as it is known to us. Upon getting a tip from a spy tracker we stealthily placed in his chambers the Mighty Forces of Fires Within rallied to confront the giant king .... a cold chill filled the air as King Tormax was ripped from his lair he engaged us and was dispatched in a bloody battle. The FIRES WITHIN are ever burning, take heed Norath.
