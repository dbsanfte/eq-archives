Rank System Explained

Good vs. Evil
I wanted to take the evil out of the guild, without taking the members out of the guild.
The only way I could see doing this was to split the guild into two armies, one of good and the other of evil. Starting with this concept, I had to start a system in which characters could be determined as 'good' or 'evil' after evaluation. I came up with a three point system that looked at a characters race, class, and deity. After coming up with a characters alignment, they are ready to be placed into the heirarchy of the army the belong to. Determining where a character should be places with this system, it seperates good and evil, without making one army stronger than the other, or making them unbalanced as far as skills go. Both armies have their tanks, as well as healers, and powerful casters. After a character is placed, they will advance thru the ranks of the guild, as they adventure and advance their character.
