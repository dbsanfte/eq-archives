We Gather in Light
to form a Pure Order.
Evil we fight
against all disorder.

The weak
 and the needy
we'll always seek out,
but the evil and vile
we'll always leave out.

Our journeys are vast,
far and wide.
but our Light of Pure Order,
we do not hide.

Its evil we seek,
not glory or gold,
that's why at times,
this duty is cold.

So come forth all evil,
and everything vile.
This Order will fight,
against all defile.

Its darkness we fight,
for the Light to win.
Our glory is our God,
our Country, our men.

Daemc Pureheart,
Guildleader,
Light of Pure Order
