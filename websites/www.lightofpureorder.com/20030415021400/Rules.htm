Code of Conduct

There are not many societies that can survive without some form of rules and codes of conduct to keep justice and peace. I wish there was some way to keep from having to make rules for the guild, but there isn't. SO, without further notice, here are the rules set forth for the guild, Light of Pure Order. These rules are subject to be changed and revised as seen fit by the officers of the guild.

Guild Rules:

 1.  There is to be no profanity in the Guild Chat. Please, there are some minors that play EQ
        and there are even some in the guild. Please be kind and do not use profanity.
 2.  There is to be no begging or bothering of guild members for equipment, cash, ports, or
        power leveling in guild chat. If you need a favor, then you can ask for help, but do not
        bother other members in guild chat.
 3.  Power Leveling is not condoned by the Light of Pure Order. If you wish to seek someone
        to PL you, please do not spam the guild chat.
 4.  Finally, all "Play Nicely" Rules and regulations set forth by Sony and Verant should be
        followed without a second thought. If there is something that you don't agree with that
        Sony and Verant have ruled against, leave it as something you don't like. Don't use the
        guild to try to overthrow the rules.
 5.  All members should act in ways that will bring only a good reputation to the guild.
        Everyone that sees you, sees the name of the Guild. Anything you do that is harmful to
        your character's image, is also harmful to the Guild's image.

Bank Rules:

 1.  All donations are welcome, and are not subject to re-embursment.
 2.  All items sold to the bank are subject to be sold at anytime after they become property of 
        the bank.
 3.  The bank will not buy any items unless the item has a value of no less than one platinum. 
        Anything valued less than one plat will be considered a donation and will not be
        purchased by the bank.
 4.  Loans are subject to be paid with interest according to the Interest Rate Chart below.
 5.  In order to borrow a loan from the bank. An item valued at half of the loan value must be
        held by the bank. Upon the loan being paid in full, the item will be returned to the owner.
        If the loan is not repaid, this item and the penalty items become property of the bank.
 6.  The maximum time for a loan is three months. After three months have passed, if the loan
        cannot be paid, then an item or items of equal value to half the loan must be paid to the
        bank at that time. Failure to payback the guild bank will result in reporting action to a
        GM, as well as a guild trial.

Loan Amount: 1 Month 2 Months 3 Months
<100 plat 5 plat 10 plat 15 plat
101 - 500 plat 15 plat 20 plat 25 plat
501+ 25 plat 30 plat 35 plat

All of the above rules are strictly enforced. Anyone caught breaking the rules will be given one verbal warning. After a verbal warning has been issued, another violation of the rules will be removal from the guild. If a member feels that they are being treated unfairly, then a council of members will meet and discuss the situation and a decision will be made. The ruling of the council is final and no one will change what the council decides. Serious violation of the rules will be subject to notification of a GM for assistance, and possibly, removal of the violator.
