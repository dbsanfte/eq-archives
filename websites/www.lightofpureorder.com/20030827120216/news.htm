June 30, 2003
        In response to a few emails, I will answer the question as to why we are starting to recruit for the guild once again. I have received numerous messages and complaints that there isn't enough active members online at various times. The only idea that I have to solve this, is to recruit more active players. I want this guild to become one "very large" family once again, but also, I would like for us to start coming up with a purpose for our guild. Some reason to stand out among the rest of the guilds in Norrath. I am not wanting this guild to grow too large, too fast, but rather a small contest to see how large we can become for this moment. After the contest is over, then we should take time to get to know our new members, work together on the guild, and figure out what the next step the guild should take is. If anyone does not like this idea or has other ideas and suggestions, please let me know. I am always open to ideas and comments from other guild members, after all, the guild is more than just a few people, it is a whole family. Please, everyone help make this guild a family again.
