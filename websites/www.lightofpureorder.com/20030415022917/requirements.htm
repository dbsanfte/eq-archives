Application Rules

Membership Requirements:

 1.  Level Requirement: Level 10
 2.  Must play Guild character at least once a month.

Promotion to Officer:

 1.  Level Requirement: Level 25
 2.  Must Recruit 5 New Members into the guild.
 3.  Must be a standard guild member for 1 month.
 4.  Must pass majority vote of current Officers.
