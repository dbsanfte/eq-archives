Guild Bank

   If there is an item that you wish to sell, but don't want to deal with the hassle of trying to find someone to buy it, and you don't want it taking up space in your bank? Then I've got the answer for you. Sell that unwanted weapon and armor to the Guild Banker. It keeps you from having to go all the way to the Bazaar, plus you get paid on the spot instead of waiting for someone to come and buy from you. The rules of the bank are listed under the guild rules page.
