What we stand for: 
1. Loyalty to our brethren. 
2. Honor in combat. 
3. Respect and helpfulness to all deserving citizens of Norrath. 
4. Strength through unification of diverse classes and races into an organized group. 

Our Main Goals: 
1. Create a family of heroes that each member can depend on in time of need, helping with quests, etc. 
2. Make each member as strong, skilled and knowledgeable as possible. 
3. Exploit all facets of the game including quests, trade skills, and events. 
4. Advance to the point where we can someday raid the planes, slay the dragons, and become TRUE heroes of Norrath.
