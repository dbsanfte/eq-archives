To put it simply, our ultimate goal is power.

We gain power through respect and esteem, not through overt brutality. This is not to say that we will not raise our swords in defense of the families of this guild, or those guilds we are allied with. We understand and relish the need to draw blood of the Lighter Races to defend the hatred of the Father.

We are an organization of families that will defend itself against anyone, regardless of numbers, or situations. We believe that our honor is power and that power will dictate who rules Norrath. Our families must chose the only honor, the honor of evil, which will lead them to the true power over the false rulers of Norrath.
