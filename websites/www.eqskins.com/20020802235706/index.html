Welcome to EQskins.com
EQskins.com was created so the Everquest community would have a place to post pictures and info for multiple characters, and have those characters linked to a single account.

The downloads section is now up. If you have a mod for the new Everquest User Interface, goto the downloads section and click add to submit your creation.

If you want to create character profiles, or post in the forum, you need to sign up for an account. Sign up to begin adding your characters, information, and begin posting in the forums.
