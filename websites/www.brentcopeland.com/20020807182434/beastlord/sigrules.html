Signature Rules:

  1. No Java
  2. Flash/Gif/Animated Picture shouldnt be any larger then 30k(this is -plenty- to work with)
  3. Images that distort the format of the forums
  4. Images shouldnt be larger then 550x250

Reasoning:

  1. Java requires large downloads typically, and every time you post someone shouldnt need to download/install stuff to see your sig
  2. Keep the pictures under 30k, this is double of what The Safehouse uses, and they have some of the best sigs you'll see
  3. These forums are for discussion, not showing off large sigs, sigs are just "ID's" not a measure of power
  4. 550x250 i think is very generous, it might even be reduced later, but we wanted to limit it to this now, 250 pixles high is a tad under half the -entire screen- in 800x600, which is the minimum we should try to support.. 550 pix's wide is to keep the forums from needing horizontal scrolling at 800x600, no one likes to scroll through the entire page left and right because of one or 2 people's 1250x500 sig
