Section #4 - The Beastlord


4.0 What is a Beastlord?

Beastlords are essentially hybrid class with a heavy reliance upon pets. More information will be available as we finalize the details.

4.1 What races can be a Beastlord?

At minimum, the Vah Shir will be able to be Beastlords. It is very likely, though not guaranteed, that we will open the class up to other shamanistic races.

4.1 Will we need to purchase Luclin to play a Beastlord?

Yes. Even if we make the Beastlord available to existing races, you will need Luclin in order to play one.

1. What pets do the beastlords have?
Ogre - Bear
Vah'shir - Tiger
Iksar - Scaled Wolf
Troll - Alligator
Barbarian - White Wolf

2. What restrictions do beastlords have?
So far, we have no weight restrictions.

3. What special abilities do beastlords have?
Class abilities - AC bonus (has nothing to do with weight, but isn't as good as monks)

4. What is the deal with our pet? We only get it at the first rank of spells!?
At 9th level, we get a hotkey called "Summon Warder". This summons a pet for you. The pet will zone with you, and will even be there when you leave and re-enter Everquest. When you zone, your pet will lose all of its buffs. Each spell rank, you will get a new spell to "Buff" your pet and make him a higher level pet.

5. Can the Beastlord's warder/pet be dispelled?
No.

6. Can the warder/pet's level buff be dispelled?
No

7. When I summon a warder/pet, I get an icon in my buff window. Can this buff be dispelled?
No. That icon IS your pet, and you can click it off at anytime, and this will dispell your pet.

8. Can a Beastlord's warder/pet be charmed?
Unknown at this time, though most likely it can.

*update from message board*

"I play on sullon zek (pvp) and I can verify that the pet is very much charmable =) Both enchanters and bards have charm poofed my pet in fights."

9. Does my warder/pet get Call of Hero'd(CoH) with me?
Unknown at this time, though most likely it will not, which means you will need to remove your warder before being CoH'd. Or if you are high enough level - Have your pet "Guard here" or "Sit down" and then you can summon your pet to your side.

10. Can I give equipment to my warder/pet, the way a Mage or Necro does?
It won't do anything, but you can give it whatever you want.

11. Does my warder/pet get bigger as I level?
Yes, it gets bigger with each of the Beastlord's level buffs.

12. Can the level buffs be stacked?
No.

13. Can I level buff someone else's warder/pet?
Not with your "Pet buff" spell line, but you can buff them with your other buffs, such as strengthen, or inner fire.

14. Can other PCs buff my warder/pet?
With most buffs, such as Damage shields, Regeneration, SoW, and other Shield buffs.
