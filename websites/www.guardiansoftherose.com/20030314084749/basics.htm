Most people have this HUGE list of requirements for being in their guilds.  In my opinion that just puts too much pressure on everyone and takes away from everyone's fun.

But this is what we expect from our members.

1. Use your common sense.

2. Know the rules and play with good etiquette.  Kill-Stealing and being a nuisance will get you reprimanded and/or removed.

3. Try to help out your fellow guildies.  (we do have community property, and low characters can be assisted, please assist if you have extra equipment)

4. Play nice.

5. Make an attempt to participate in guild events (you don't have to do all events, it's not a requirement, but it helps the rest of us if you try).  Raids are fun, but not alone hehe.  We know you have lives, so if you want to raid, please work with us all to set up times that work for everyone.  If you cannot come, please give notice so we can work things around the open space.  Other members need raids to help them get their Epic pieces, you will too eventually.

7.  Members MUST register with our message board.  All raids and guild events are posted there a week ahead of time.

6. HAVE FUN!  This is a game and that's what it's about.
