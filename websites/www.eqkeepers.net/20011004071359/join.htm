People interested in joining KoF must fulfill the following requirements:

Fill in the form below expressing your interest in our guild.  Please include you primary character's name, class, race and level as well as any secondary characters which you play frequently.

We strongly encourage people wanting to join our guild to have a good reputation among the general populace of the server.  If we know someone to be generally disliked by most people, it makes us wary of giving you any serious consideration.  This also applies to characters that have been bought or traded for, on Ebay or otherwise.

We prefer your main character to be a minimum of 20th level.

After receiving your application, you will get a notice through e-mail from our interviewing guild officer that will set-up a time to meet with you.

Also, please know all new members are placed on a probation period of 30 days to evaluate their performance.
