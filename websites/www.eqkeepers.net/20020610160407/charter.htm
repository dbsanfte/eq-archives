We, Keepers of Fortitude are a united people made up of the citizens of Norrath.  We hold this charter as true within our hearts, all having an equal say and all in agreement with this established pact...

Let us always be loyal to one another and ensure the continued growth and prosperity of the members of the Keepers. There exists other groups of people who are only loyal to two things… money and power, this should not be… Our blood and our peoples are what makes us powerful when we band together.

Let us always maintain a noble countenance and strive to do what is just, valorous and right. We maintain a code of honor, as there is no purpose in life if there is no honor. Honor brings courage to defend ones homeland, to defend ones family and to defend ones friends.

Let us always seek to grow in our knowledge of the land in which we live. This should be the basic principle upon which our adventures are organized and designed. We strive educate ourselves and obtain any information that we can to further our cause.

We take upon this as right and true.  As we band together as one powerful force, born from Felwithe, soldiers of truth and honor, may we all bring pride upon our name!  We are the Keepers of Fortitude.
GENERAL LOOT RULE INFO
You are NOT qualified to roll or be awarded loot until you pass your probationary membership... unless noone else on the raid can use the dropped loot or everyone in the raid from that particular class has received something. The probationary membership lasts for approx 3 weeks from initial invite to the guild. What this does is attempt to keep out the guild hoppers who are interested only in loot. We are a guild built on fairness, friendship and family... we know everyone loves a nice piece of loot, but this should not be a determining factor as to why you are in the guild... if it is then this is not the guild for you. If you are willing to come, help and be a part of a great family of friends, then waiting a few weeks won't be too much to ask.

If you are not in probationary period the following loot rules apply to you.

OPEN RAID
An 'OPEN RAID' is basically a raid where non-guildies are invited to join us... in the case of an open raid; guests and probationary members will have the same rights to roll on items as full members would (see below for 'full member' rules). Prior to starting a raid it would be announced if open rules apply to that function.

NEED BEFORE GREED
All members will qualify for loot following these guidelines. Once a member has won a roll, they will then drop out until the next round. In the case of class specific loot... all members in attendance who CAN use the item and WILL use the item, are allowed to roll. If none of the specific class wish to use the item, any others who want to roll for said item may do so. In the case of members not wanting the item, the guild will keep for either a person unable to attend, put in guild bank or to use to boost guild funds. (please see exceptions on rolling for loot below)

WILL USE the item is defined as having an item of lesser worth in either usefulness or value than the item in question. While we want to equip our members with the best equipment, we also do not want to have items going to members who will bank/sell the item at the first opportunity, especially if there is someone who can and will make better use of the said item.

BP's and LEGGINGS
The guild assigns ALL BP's and Leggings. This is due to the rarity of their drops. These items are to be awarded BY the officers in charge or the guild leader.

PLANES LOOT
Class specific loot will go to the appropriate class attending the hunt. If more than one of the same class is in attendance they will then role to decide the winner. The winner will then drop out of the roll until the next round. Only exception here is with BP's and Leggings as described above.

THURGA QUEST LOOT
In the case of a Kael raid where the Thurga quest armor drops, all classes able to use said piece of armor will roll... you MUST have at least amiable faction with the Coldain in order to roll on a piece. Only exception here is with BP's and Leggings as described above.

BENEVOLENCE QUEST LOOT
The Benevolence quest in Western Wastes... all folks in attendance are able to roll on this great piece of gear but you MUST have Ally faction with Claws of Veeshan to roll on it. If you do not know the quest check here for more info.

AWARDING CERTAIN LOOT
For the most part only BP and Leggings will be awarded... on occasion the raid leader may decide to award certain items in the case that it would better benefit the guild as a whole.

CASH LOOT
All cash loot that drops will be looted by your group leader... then all coin and gems that drop will go to the raid leader at the end of the raid. This cash then goes to the guild bank to help pay for stones, peridots, coffins, etc.

UPGRADING GEAR
If you are 'awarded' an item we REQUIRE you to donate the item you upgraded from so another guildie will be able to upgrade from what they have. If you win a 'roll' on an item you are NOT required to do so, but it is always appreciated. (EXAMPLE) Soanso the Enchanter is wearing a Siren Scale Robe and is on a Hate raid... a Insidious Robe drops and is awarded to them... Soanso is then required to hand over the Siren Scale Robe to a guild officer in order for it to be awarded to someone else in the guild who is in need of an upgrade.

Also if you have gear that you no longer need and would like to donate it... we do have a couple guild banks that store gear, spells and such for these purposes.

ALTS
If you are playing your alt and are on a raid (i.e. you are not hydraing) your alt will have all the rights and privileges as a full member. If you are hydraing an alt on a raid, you are automagically moved to the end of the loot list, which means you are not allowed to roll on loot until all mains have received something. Also, please do not ask if your alt can have a specific piece of loot that drops, as it is most likely a main in the guild will be able to use.

On occasion, the raid leaders may ask for you to bring your alt on a raid instead of your main, due to needing that class more... in this case, if you so wish, you can opt to be there for loot as your main. (example: we ask player_01 to bring their warrior on a raid instead of their druid... player_01 has the option of being there for rollable druid loot instead of warrior... which means they would not be included in the roll table for warriors.) This would be taken on a case by case basis and only at the request of the raid leaders... this would also be announced prior to raid.

TROUBLE WITH NON-GUILDIES REGARDING LOOT
While the guild will do all it can to protect its members, we will not get involved in loot issues while members are grouped with other individuals. Ninja looting, training and Kill-stealing are looked upon with GREAT distaste by the community as a whole and we will not tolerate any individual within the guild carrying out such acts. If you find yourself the victim of such an act, then the guild officers or leader will act on your behalf to try to resolve the situation as fairly as possible to all parties concerned. If you find yourself accused of such acts as detailed above then be aware this is grounds for immediate removal from the guild, however we would need proof of such an act being carried out be it in the form of a screenshot or substantial, eyewitness testimony.

PLEASE NOTE: We like to keep such things out of open forums such as the Saryrn Tavern and if you are brought into a flame fest on the Saryrn boards... don't fight back as that just fuels the fire and makes everyone involved look childish. If such a situation occurs, notify a guild officer and they will try to take care of it.

TROUBLE WITH GUILD DECISIONS REGARDING LOOT
If you are upset with a decision regarding loot distribution please take your grievances to a guild officer or the guild leader and it will be discussed at that point. Lets keep such things out of open forums or guildchat as most problems can be resolved privately.
