Monday January 14th, 2003

Out with the Old, In with the Smelly!
Airelyn steps down as guild leader, all welcome Bumraz to take her spot.

Well folks.  I am stepping down as your guild leader.  Now don't be sad, I still wub you all and I'll still be around.

I am sure you have noticed I haven't been around in about a month... well due to RL stuff I am just way too busy and will be for quite a while. It is in my opinion that a guild leader needs to be around a lot in order to run things properly so this is my reasoning for stepping down. I care very much for the well-being of KoF and I am so proud of how far we have come... through good times and bad we have kept pushing forward making us a little powerhouse guild. Thank you all for following me for as long as you all have, it has been a pleasure. Also know... I will still be here if you need to talk or anything, just send me a message on the boards, I check everyday. I will also still be running the website.

Now, Bumraz will be taking over the reigns.  There are some great times to be had by the Keepers and some wonderful new things will be happening... I forsee a great future for the Keepers.  The day when I am able to return full-time, I expect you all to still be here!

So all welcome your new leader Bumraz, please be nice to him and don't be worried... I know it is scary thinking a troll will be running things, but he promised to pick up some new deodorant.

Good luck Bumraz and good luck my Keepers.
