People interested in joining KoF must fulfill the following requirements:

By filling out the join form you are agreeing that you fully understand these requirements.

Membership Requirements:

1. You should be level 40 or higher.
2. You must have someone from the guild who is willing to sponsor you.
3. Once you have a sponsor, fill out the join form here. (make sure to include your sponsers name)
4. Once these requirements are met, we speak to your sponser, and you have been approved by our head recruitment officer... you can then be tagged for a two week trial period. Within that two week period is the time to get to know your fellow guildies by grouping with them and raiding as often as possible.

If you do not know anyone in the guild and need someone to be your sponser... try to hook up with someone in the guild and get to know them.

Also note family, real life, or longtime EQ friends are exempt from this process.

After receiving your application, you will get a notice through e-mail or through a tell from our interviewing guild officer that will set-up a time to talk with you.  Please allow 2-5 days to recieve a response.
