People interested in joining KoF must fulfill the following requirements:

By filling out the join form you are agreeing that you fully understand these requirements.

Membership Requirements:

1. You must be level 51 or higher.
2. You must have someone from the guild who is willing to sponsor you.
3. Once you have a sponsor, fill out the join form here. (make sure to include your sponsors name)
4. Once these requirements are met, we speak to your sponsor, and you have been approved by our head recruitment officer... you can then be tagged for a three week trial period. Within that three week period is the time to get to know your fellow guildies by grouping with them and raiding as often as possible.
5. You must have the ability to make thurgadin gate potions and carry one on you at all times (excluding port classes)
6. You must read, understand, and accept the guilds charter and loot rules
7. The guild is on Dorf/CoV faction and you should either be on this faction or willing to work towards getting it.

If you do not know anyone in the guild and need someone to be your sponsor... try to hook up with someone in the guild and get to know them prior or join us on a couple raids.

Also note family and real life friends are exempt from this process but are still subject to the probationary period.

After receiving your application, you will get a notice through e-mail or through a tell from our interviewing guild officer that will set-up a time to talk with you.  Please allow up to 2-5 days to receive a response.
