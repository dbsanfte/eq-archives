Guide to making the Thurgadin Gate Potion.

Well Keepers, as you know it is REQUIRED for you to have the ability to port to Thurgadin... the ONLY exception to this requirement is if you are a porting class.  The reason we require this is for a few reasons:   If we need to mobilize the guild fast, we can get everyone together no matter where they are...  If you go LD in a bad spot and aren't able to get in far after the guild has left the area, this will save you and us from a hard CR.  This is a quick and easy method to gain the ability to port.

These were introduced into the game as a part of the 4th step of the Coldain Prayer Shawl Quest.

Vial of Velium Vapors
MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP
EXPENDABLE CHARGES: 1
Effect: Thurgadin Gate
WT: 1  SIZE: Small
ALL/ALL

First off, you need a pottery skill of around 120, where these are trivial ( See guide at bottom to get this skill up )

Go to Crystal Caverns and start farming!  You need Crystilline Silk, Small Bricks of Velium,  Small pieces of Velium.  You will need these on a ratio of 3 : 1 : 2.

The components you need for each potion are as follows:

Velium Vial
Combine on a pottery wheel:
3 Crystilline Silks (from spiders in CC)
1 Small Brick of Velium (from orc miners in CC)
2 Glass Shards (from left-most vendor in Thurga bank)

This results in an unfired vial.

Combine in Kiln (oven right next to wheel):
1 Unfired Vial
1 High-quality Firing Sheet (from left most vendor in thurga bank)

This results in a velium vial.

Now, go out of the bank take a right and head towards the church (stay left at the fork).

Inside the second merchie building on the left is Frundle Frenkler.  Hail her and say "What velium vapors?"  She tells you the recipe and gives you:

Velium Purifier
Container
LORE ITEM  NO DROP

Combine in Velium Purifier
1 Velium Vial
2 Small Pieces of Velium

The purifier will poof and you will be holding the Thurga gate potion... woot!

The potion will gate you onto the bridge you cross when you enter Thurga.

Easiest way to always have one in inventory is keep a few velium vials in the bank at all times and everytime you use one, grab your velium vial and 2 pieces of velium and go make another one.



OK HOW TO GET YOUR POTTERY SKILL FROM 0 to 122 in ONE HOUR!

0-102 Skill -
Combine in pottery wheel:
1 small block of clay
1 bowl sketch
1 water

When this skill gets trivial at 102 switch to making Medium Bowls:

103-122
1 medium bowl sketch
1 block of clay
1 water

So there you have it!
