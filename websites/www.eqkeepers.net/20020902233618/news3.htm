Wednesday, October 10th, 2001 Shadows of Luclin
Shadows Of Luclin:  It will feature new adventure zones, items, creatures, and spells. In addition, it will include a new playable character class, a variety of new quests, and a greatly enhanced 3D graphics engine.  The expansion will take players to Luclin, a satellite floating above the world of Norrath. The satellite is divided into a land of light, which is ruled by Discord, and a land of shadow ruled by Order. Each side of Luclin will feature a number of new treasures and unique monsters.  Oh also don't forget about the horsies!  Release is due mid-december!
