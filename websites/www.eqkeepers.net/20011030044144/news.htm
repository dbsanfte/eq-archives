April 19th New Expanison:  Shadows Of Luclin
Shadows Of Luclin:  It will feature new adventure zones, items, creatures, and spells. In addition, it will include a new playable character class, a variety of new quests, and a greatly enhanced 3D graphics engine.  The expansion will take players to Luclin, a satellite floating above the world of Norrath. The satellite is divided into a land of light, which is ruled by Discord, and a land of shadow ruled by Order. Each side of Luclin will feature a number of new treasures and unique monsters.

Okies now that is the "official release statement" now let me tell you about this in more detail.  First off... it is gonna really change things, the new 3D engine is unbelievable, featuring enhanced player-character models and texture maps, and the new wearable armor textures are much more advanced.  Visually, Luclin should raise the bar higher than either of the first two expansions did, with all the enhancements made possible with DirectX 8 support. (see pics to the left)

The new playable race will be the Kerran (you know the kitties) and the new class is called Beastlord.  This will also add 25 new zones (including a new starting city), suitable for players of all levels.  They will not be raising the 60 level cap but they are including new avenues of character advancement for high-level players.

The exact release date has not been specified as of yet but it will be released in the Fall of this year.

April 14th

 Saiferus & Airelyn Divine's Wedding

photo by: Kaneril		 On Saturday the 14th at 6pm cst Saiferus Gemdancer and Airelyn Divine were married in Felwithe.  After much discussion it was decided to go with Airelyn's last name of "Divine".  Airelyn's family was killed by the Crushbone orcs when she was young, leaving her the last to carry on her family name... out of respect for her beloved parents, Saiferus has decided to honor them by becoming a "Divine" and carrying on the family name.

Immediately following the wedding, Colmir, our leader announced the addition of two new guild officers... Airelyn & Cremson.

April 1st

 GM Event rocks the Commonlands
It was quiet night in the East Commonlands on April Fools Day.  Ayrlia was twiddling around doing nothing important when suddenly the sky turned blood red and the world around her got quiet and still, she knew something evil was brewing.

Suddenly without warning the commonlands were invaded by literally thousands of undead.   Trying to make her stand at Inn 2 she was almost defeated by a train of at least 40 undead.  With the spirit of the wolf at her side she made a run for NRO tunnel, but found yet another 100 or so undead in battle with many others.  She made a valiant stand along side her fellow players until there were no undead left.

Alot of people died this day, but in the end we were victorous and made the undead rue the day they decided to raid our gentle commonlands!
