We, Keepers of Fortitude are a united people made up of the citizens of Norrath.  We hold this charter as true within our hearts, all having an equal say and all in agreement with this established pact...

Let us always be loyal to one another and ensure the continued growth and prosperity of the members of the Keepers. There exists other groups of people who are only loyal to two things… money and power, this should not be… Our blood and our peoples are what makes us powerful when we band together.

Let us always maintain a noble countenance and strive to do what is just, valorous and right. We maintain a code of honor, as there is no purpose in life if there is no honor. Honor brings courage to defend ones homeland, to defend ones family and to defend ones friends.

Let us always seek to grow in our knowledge of the land in which we live. This should be the basic principle upon which our adventures are organized and designed. We strive educate ourselves and obtain any information that we can to further our cause.

We take upon this as right and true.  As we band together as one powerful force, born from Felwithe, soldiers of truth and honor, may we all bring pride upon our name!  We are the Keepers of Fortitude.
We use R.A.P (Raid Attendance Points) as our method of loot distribution. A system based upon the idea of DKP, but with a simpler touch that is customized for our guilds needs.Points are awarded for guild function raids and used to buy drops.

The theory behind this system is to give all members a fair chance at choosing what loot they wish to get. A member would save up their points and get awarded items based upon their work. This system was built around the idea that not only full-time members, but also part-time members would be able to have a shot at getting whatever loot they desire. It may take a bit more time for a part-time member to get something, but they have more of an option to do so then previously. This system is to promote fairness and take away the burden of "awarding" drops based upon the officers decision.

This system DOES NOT include quest items or spells (i.e. epic quest items, spell parchments, etc.) those are all to be awarded by the raid staff. The ONLY quest items included in the point system are the Velious quest armors. Your current total points will be updated no longer then 24 hours after a raid has finished.  Points earned on the day of a raid will not become available for your use until the next day when the chart is updated.

Full details are provided in the members section of the website.
