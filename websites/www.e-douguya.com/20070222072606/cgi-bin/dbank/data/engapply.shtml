Recruitment in non-Japanese speaker


- About the language
We are not basically accepting ppl who can't understand Japanese.
We mainly use Japanese for raid. We have English channel, but it's limited translation.
There is a few exception for acceptance, if you are non-Japanese user and you have friend in our guild already, you may apply our guild.
But this case, you should have both English (or applicants' native language) and Japanses speaking sponser who is Vane active member.
And you should speak English at least, and ppl are useally speaking Japanese in your chat channel and you have to accept it.
You may not understand what we are talking about.
Some class is not accepting non-Japanese speaking ppl at all. It's up to the class members.
