    Exceptions to the /Movelog
    11/1/01, 9:38 PST | Kwill

    The move to the Antonious Bayle European server is on, but there are some exceptions to who can move there, according to this update from Absor on the Developer's Corner:
    Characters from special rules servers will not be moved to the new server, nor will characters be moved from Test. This includes the Zek servers as well as Firiona Vie. The rules for play on those servers are different than on the standard servers, and we want to preserve that uniqueness. Also, gaining levels and equipment is going to be either easier or harder on those special rules servers, so to maintain a level playing field we don't move characters across rulesets.

    IMPORTANT! Loot any corpses you might have before the move date! While we will be moving all equipment and banked items and cash and all that, we won't be moving corpses. So if you leave something on a corpse you will lose it.

    Absor also reminds peopple about names:
    If two characters are moved to the server with the same name, one of them will randomly have an X appended to their name. You can then ask a GM to change your name if you don't really want that X appended. Character names must be unique, so someone has to lose their name. That's one of the unfortunate necessities of moving characters.

    European Server is /Movelog
    10/31/01, 8:14 PST | Kwill

    We reported some time ago that the SoE staff was not sure if they would make the Antonious Bayle server for European customers a /movelog server or not...looks like it will be. Here is the official word from Absor, from the Sony Developer's Corner:
    As you have already seen by the announcement on our EverQuest Live site, we will be opening the Antonius Bayle server for our European players on November 1, 2001. We will be opening a move log from all servers to the new server on November first. We will be giving priority to European players when we move characters, and we will only be accepting a limited number of moves. Obviously we want this server to be used primarily by our European players and we don't want to move so many characters to the server that it becomes too crowded.

    Here is a schedule for the movelog.

    11/1/01 – Thursday at 8 am (pst) - Movelog opens.
    11/5/01 – Monday at 8 am (pst) - Movelog closes.
    11/6/01 – Wednesday – Moves begin and characters start becoming available to play.
    11/7/01 – Thursday – We expect to finish moving the characters and have them all available to play.

    Due to the sheer volume of players that we expect will be putting in a request to move, we expect to have to cut the list short. Again, priority will be given to European players on a first come, first served basis.

    If you want to have your characters moved to the new server, here's what you should do.

    Log in on the account and server that you want your characters to be moved from. Pick any character that you have on that server and enter Norrath.

    Then all you have to do is type "/movelog yes" (without the quotation marks) and Enter and you will be on the list to have your characters moved to the new server.

    Good luck to all those who are moving...your friends on the American servers will surely miss you.
