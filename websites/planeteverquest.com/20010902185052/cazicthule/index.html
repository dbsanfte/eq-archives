    Belt...too tight...arggh
    8/31/01, 9:17 PST | Kwill

    Earlier this week we reported that humans would now be able to wear the Belt of the Cenobite. Not after the next patch. Absor writes on the Developer's Corner today:
    Recently we made an error with a change to the Belt of the Cenobite. A player pointed out that there was an error with the belt, that it was usable by Monks but not by humans. So we changed it to make it usable by humans.

    However, the belt is a small belt, intended only for the use of small races. The error on the item was not that it was unusable by humans, but that Monk was listed as a class that could use it.

    So with the next patch we'll be removing both humans and Monks from the list of folks that can use this item. We just wanted you to know.

    European Fan Faire Cancelled
    8/30/01, 10:25 PST | Kwill

    Here is the official word from Sony:
    Sony Online Entertainment and Ubi Soft today announced the postponement of the European Fan Faire. Our European community of EverQuest® players has seen much growth in the past year and we are excited about all of our initiatives in this market and beyond. While we regret the delay in bringing what has been an incredibly successful and one-of-kind event in the U.S., we know that all of our international initiatives will enhance the player experience, increase our international fan base, and lead us overseas in the coming months.

    We want to thank all of the fans who had planned to participate in this inaugural event and will be sending a full refund in addition to a special gift from our Fan Faire team. While we are disappointed that we cannot meet you next month, we hope that our presence in Europe continues to grow. We will be able to better serve you by postponing this event to a more suitable time and place for everyone. We apologize for any inconvenience.

    Stonebrunt Mountains Go Live
    8/29/01, 7:49 PST | Kwill

    A new zone has been added to the game today! Use the "optional patch" to dowload the files. Here is what Verant has to say about it, from their press release:
    Located on the small continent of Odus, the Stonebrunt Mountains are rich with vegetation and distinctive hills that give way to the towering Mount Klaw; the largest mountain in Odus and home to the Kerran village of Kejeka. The zone of Stonebrunt is designed to be a mid-level, outdoor zone similar in scope and scale to the Lake of Ill Omen on Kunark, which has proven to be a very popular zone in EverQuest.
    Find more information at www.everquest.com

    Test of Tactics 3
    8/28/01, 9:07 PST | Kwill

    Start training and getting that uber-gear together...the Test of Tactics Three has been announced!

    Dates:
    September 17-October 1. During this period one team per guild per server will be allowed to sign up. If after October 1 there are slots left, more than one team per guild can sign up. A "guild" is seven or more players from the same guild on the team. To sign up you must have the minimum level requirement. All entries close October 8.

    Teams:
    Each server will have a maximum of eight teams. Each competing team will have a maximum of 18 players. No more than 3 players of any class are allowed. The total team level, arrived at by adding the level of each member of the team to make total level, must be at least 1060.

    Cost:
    25,000pp if you want to win prizes, free if you do not want prizes.

    Objective:
    The object of each team will be to eliminate as many of the opposing team members with the fewest members lost of their own team in a 20-minute battle.

    There will be no spectators at this event.

    For a discussion on the rules, head over to The Harpy's Head Tavern.

    Tidbits from the Developers Corner
    8/28/01, 8:39 PST | Kwill

    Not a lot to report from the Sony Developer's Corner lately, but here are a few notes from Absor:

    Fear and Change Form Spells
    Fear does overwrite all change form spells. It is not a bug.

    Quest with Hala Dar
    The VI Team cannot seem to get her to spawn, so for the time being this quest is broken.

    Faction Messages
    The faction messages should be fixed now, although it was not announced as it was part of an emergency patch last week.

    Enchanter Spell Issues
    Enchanter mezzes do not overwrite the weaker necro screaming terror spells. Lawrence Poe was working on getting this fixed by the next patch, so it may be fixed at this time.

    Enchanter choke lines do not stack with the shaman epic, Poe notes, but he is working on the spell messages that notify players of the spells being overwritten.

    From the Quality Assurance Boards:

    Ester reminds people that you can bind in Skyshrine, but not everywhere--so you should keep looking for a bind point if you get the "find a city" message.

    She is checking on the haste component of Brother Xave's headband in Temple of Veeshan, which at this point has no haste component.

    Tomb of Ages Opens
    8/26/01, 23:09 PST | Aracnakat

    The Tome of Ages staff would like to announce that we are officially open for business.

    Tome of Ages is a site and forums developed by roleplayers, for roleplayers. The Tome of Ages team have spent many days making sure that this site should, hopefully, rewrite the book on roleplay in Everquest.

    The site and its forums aspires to become a home and reference point for the roleplayers of Fennin Ro. The forums aim to provide a friendly and accessible environment that any roleplayer, new or old, should enjoy.

    The web site itself contains what we hope you find to be excellent resources to help you develop your character, no matter how long you've been playing as them. Dictionaries of fantasy languages such as Elven, Dark Elven, and Thieves Cant are available, with more on the way. Our "How To.." guides aim to aid you through many aspects of roleplaying, such as "How to develop a character", "How to stay in-character in Everquest", and much more.

    Please come visit us at http://www.tomeofages.com . We hope you enjoy your stay!
