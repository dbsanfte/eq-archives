The Freedom Fighters

Freedom Fighters are the essence of the name: we fight for freedom from the evil that walks this vast land, of which none is worse than our own Freeport Militia. The good people of Norrath are under constant assault from the forces of evil which desire to rid the world of people like ourselves. There are many challenges to be faced and met by the Freedom Fighter.

We are not the hardest guild to get into, but hopefully, we will be the most united. I feel, and it is my dream, that we all work as one unit to achieve a common goal. You may ask, "What is that goal?". The goal for Freedom Fighters is to help the needy, heal the hurting, have compassion for their fellow man, but in the same breath do everything in our power to spread light to each corner of Norrath - destroying the evil that walks our land.

In order to do this, we must know each of the other Freedom Fighters. This is not to say that each Freedom Fighter must attend an invasion, a fair or event to remain a Freedom Fighter but in making this guild great we must be united. A guild is only as good as its members.

We each must think about the challenges that lie in front of us. There will be zone invasions. There will be dragon hunts. There will be trade fairs, duelling contests, races, giveaways, and a variety of group things on the agenda in the coming weeks, months and years. It is our vision to have a fun, exciting, role playing game that people look forward to being a part of.

Triton Truelight
Guild Leader
