Rules
  1. Dueling is permitted in the Freedom Fighters. We are aware many like to do it for fun. Nobody will be caught killing just to kill. ie..picking on people, hazeing and such evils. We live off the creed of a Paladin and we must act as such. All punishments for offenses will be dealt with on a case to case basis.
  2. There will be no kill stealing in the Freedom Fighters, except in areas which are considered a "free for all". Anyone caught kill stealing will be punished, and possibly dismissed from the guild.
  3. You may have as many characters in the guild as you like. We feel if we have several different classes in the guild it only makes us stronger. There need not be any interview for this, just find an officer and tell them who you are. Sorry, no dark races until rule 4 has been met.
  4. Dark races maybe allowed into the guild after much work and dedication. If a dark race wants to join the Freedom Fighters they must first prove themselves worthy. All dark races in order to come into the guild must be amiable to the factions with the guild. ie....North Freeport, Kaladim or Felwithe.
  5. There will be no drinking at guild meetings. It distracts from trying to take care of business. There will be no weapons at guild meetings. It is not necessary to go through bowing to everyone at the guild meetings. They are informal in that manner. Just say hello and that is fine.
