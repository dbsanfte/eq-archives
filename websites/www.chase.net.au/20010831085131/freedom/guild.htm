The Freedom Fighters

Hello brothers and sisters of Norrath. I am Triton Truelight, leader for the Freedom Fighters guild. Our guild is filled with lots of exciting folks who are interested in meeting others and adventuring throughout our vast lands. We hold nobody back in the Freedom Fighters. All are welcome to come who have the desire to rid the lands of evils. We feel all creatures no matter what race or class can have good in them.

Our guild is not hard to get in if you know someone, if not then you may have your work cut out for you. Nonetheless it makes us a tight knit family that way. We want to truly be brothers and sisters fighting for a common cause. We have many members who wander the land, if you want to know more about our guild, I am sure your paths will cross.

We each must think about the challenges that lie before us. There will be zone invasions. There will be dragon hunts. There will be trade fairs, dueling contests, races, giveaways and a variety of group things on the agenda in the coming weeks, months and years. It is our vision to have a fun, exciting, role playing game that people look forward to being a part of.

 

Triton Truelight
Guild Leader
