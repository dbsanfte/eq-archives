Rules
  1. There will only be role play talk in guildtalk. We do not feel everyone needs to hear how your day was. You have your friends and you can send them a tell. So, please keep guildchat, exactly that.
  2. There will be no speaking badly of another guild member publicly in guildchat. If you have a problem with someone in the guild, tell a member of the high council and they will deal with it. Anyone who is trying to report someone from the guild for doing something wrong should be directed to an high counciller. This will keep a lot of the rumors down.
  3. Anyone who is reported for doing wrongs will be dealt with on a case by case basis. There are some things that will need to be addressed in council and others that I will deal with at the time the issue is raised.
  4. There will be no zone recruiting for our guild. We feel that the best quality people will come by word of mouth. If there is someone you are fighting with and you like them then send them here. We want quality above quantity.
  5. We feel it is necessary to try to play this as much like roleplay as we can. In saying this when you sign up to be a Freedom Fighter, you agree to our punishments and decisions in a roleplaying manner. Despite our current society this was not the way it was back then. We really want to have fun in the guild, but keeping within roleplay, I must say that my word is final judgment no questions
