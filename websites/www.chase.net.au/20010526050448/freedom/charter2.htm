Rules
  1. Guildmeetings are in various locations throughout Norrath depending on your lvl. This evening is dedicated to your guild. After we have a guild meeting we will do an invasion. Let me give you an example. Levels 10 through 25 may all meet at Befallen or another spot picked out for that lvl within the guild for there meeting place that week. Lvl 25 and up may have there guildmeeting at SOL A, or somewhere else designated for there invasion. Both designated spots will meet and wait for the guildmeeting over guildchat. After the guildmeeting they go on there invasion. These meetings will be held every other Sunday evening. Officers will meet at 7:30, regular meeting is at 8:00 (all times Central). These meetings are manditory, no exceptions. I know everyone has a different schedule that is not the issue.  The issue is if you are online then you will be at the meetings.
  2. There will only be role play talk in guildtalk. We do not feel everyone needs to hear how your day was. You have your friends and you can send them a tell. So, please keep guildchat, exactly that.
  3. There will be no speaking badly of another guild member publicly in guildchat. If you have a problem with someone in the guild, tell an Officer, Sgt, or the Guildleader and they will deal with it. Anyone who is trying to report someone from the guild for doing something wrong should be directed to an Officer, Sgt, or Guildleader. This will keep a lot of the rumors down.
