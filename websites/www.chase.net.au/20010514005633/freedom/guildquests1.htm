Guild Quests

This is not manditory but will weigh in your favor if you strive to be an officer or Sgt in the guild.

  1. All quests must be completed in front of an officer, Sgt. or Guildleader. The reason they must be done with an officer, Sgt. or Guildleader is that while they are a challenge, we do not want anyone to die. At any point during the battle the officer, Sgt. or Guildleader may step in and save your life. If your life is saved , you may not question this. I would rather your life be saved then you loose experience over it.
  2. To qualify to be a Sgt. in the guild, you must be of Apprentice status in the guildquests or higher.
  3. To qualify to be an officer in the guild, you must be of Journeyman status in the guildquests or higher.
  4. The quests shall be done at the convenience of the officer, Sgt. or Guildleader, not a pledges convenience. I say this in full belief that the officers will do the right thing.
