Rules
  1. Dueling is permitted in the Freedom Fighters. We are aware many like to do it for fun. Nobody will be caught killing just to kill. ie..picking on people, hazeing and such evils. We live off the creed of a Paladin and we must act as such. All punishments for offenses will be dealt with on a case to case basis.
  2. There will be no kill stealing in the Freedom Fighters, except in areas which are considered a "free for all". Anyone caught kill stealing will be punished, and possibly dismissed from the guild.
  3. You may have as many characters in the guild as you like. We feel if we have several different classes in the guild it only makes us stronger. There need not be any interview for this, just find a member of the high council and tell them who you are. Remember to update your photo in the family album.
  4. There will be no drinking at guild meetings. It distracts from trying to take care of business. There will be no weapons at guild meetings. It is not necessary to go through bowing to everyone at the guild meetings. They are informal in that manner. Just say hello and that is fine.
  5. Guildmeetings are manditory and will be posted on the message board. Manditory means if you are online then you are there.
