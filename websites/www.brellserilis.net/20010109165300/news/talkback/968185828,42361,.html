Uber Class Weapons Coming
Posted: Tuesday, September 5 by delsonora @ 3:30 PM EDT


Noted by Gordon today, commenting on a players concern that Paladin's shouldnt be the only class to get "FA Style" Quest weapons.


First, there will be no Fiery Avenger 'Clones'. There will be items however that are of similar stature.

Second, they won't be just for tanks, but for all classes. Lastly, the quests are in the development and testing stages right now. They'll go in game soon.

- Gordon
