Delsonora Blackthorne aka William Smith

About the Character

Delsonora is a gnomic necromancer, currently of the 32nd season. Her development as a character started as part of a dream I had while on vacation last year around Christmas time. Originally, she was supposed to be a gnomic magician but necromancers are just too cool. Her roleplay story is that she belongs to a rich and very social family within Ak'anon. While her parents wined and dined living high on the hog, they paid little attention to Delsonora and her two sisters. To spite their parents and attempt to "get their attention" the sisters ventured into the Mines of Malfunction. The trio met the wise and old gnomic necromancer Fuzzlegrump, and forever their lives were changed.
