An Everquest Movie Script

Source: Unknown
Author: Unknown
Originally found on Eqvault.com

PLAYERS:
Lombard Setheriah, High-Elf Paladin
Dobin Kinkle, Halfling Druid
Harrybalz, Human Warrior

(skip first 1.5 hours of movie which are spent killing rats and snakes)

FADE IN: 1. EAST KARANA.

Rolling landscape with a few hills, and trees with branches going in only 4 directions. Animals scamper across the screen, vanishing occasionally, only to re-appear a few feet further away moments later. The sun is setting in the distance to the West, and the moon is rising to the South. LOMBARD SETHERIAH, a Paladin, enters from the right. He is clad in shining orange armor of Bronze and wielding a halberd, weapon of the Paladin. Behind him strolls his faithful companion, DOBIN KINKLE, wearing the finest in leather attire and wielding a large magical staff.

LOMBARD
Ahh, looks like a full moon!

DOBIN
Yes, just like last night. And the night before. And the night before.

LOMBARD
This new land is foreign to me, but I have been told it is cursed with vile undead reavers which appear at dusk. We must prepare for them. Do you have...

LOMBARD is suddenly interrupted by a loud clap of thunder. Suddenly, a thick bank of fog rolls quickly in and the rain begins to fall.

LOMBARD
I cannot see more than 20 feet ahead. This will provide a challenge. I shall attempt to sense the undead around us.

LOMBARD throws his arms around and suddenly flecks of light sparkle from his eyes.

LOMBARD
I sense undead, but they appear to all be... dead... hmm... I appear to not be able to find any animated undead due to all the dead ones lying around. Friend Dobin, your tracking skills will be most useful now.

DOBIN
Very well, I shall attempt to find some.

Enter HARRYBALZ, a low level warrior decked out in light blue armor and wielding a powerful looking axe.

HARRYBALZ
cn u give me sow?

LOMBARD looks the warrior up and down. He realizes that this warrior will need substantial aid in an environment such as this, as his skills are far too weak to handle the creatures which roam about EAST KARANA.

LOMBARD
Dobin, let us assist our friend the warrior here.

DOBIN
k

DOBIN waves his arms around, and a brief lupine aura settles around HARRYBALZ.

HARRYBALZ
thx dood!

LOMBARD
Be safe, frie...

Before LOMBARD can complete his sentence, HARRYBALZ goes running off and quickly disappears into the fog.

DOBIN
I'm not tracking anything.

LOMBARD
That is well in any case. The fog makes it far too dangerous to be hunting at this time. Let us journey to the wooden bridge to the north and rest ourselves as we await this storm to pass.

LOMBARD and DOBIN run north and soon a large wooden bridge looms into view. This structure is huge, and has been rumored to have been built by Giants long ago. The two men sit down at the edge of the bridge. Suddenly, the screen is filled with the image of a Tomb of Magic.

DOBIN medding 10 minutes pass, and finally the book comes down and we see that the storm has passed.

LOMBARD
Excellent, we can now resume our hunt. Let us...

LOMBARD is interrupted by a loud shout which rips across the land.

HARRYBALZ
BROON TO BRIDGE! MOVE YER ASS!

LOMBARD
Broon? What beast is this?

HARRYBALZ suddenly runs past our heroes and down the bridge, behind him runs a large bald CYCLOPS.

HARRYBALZ vanishes as soon as he gets to the middle of the bridge, but the CYCLOPS has spotted the heroes.

LOMBARD
Ahh, a beast to vanquish at last! DOBIN err, we should zone.

LOMBARD readies his halberd, and swings at the CYCLOPS as it tries to pass. His halberd meets air as the large clumsy CYCLOPS is surprisingly agile enough to parry and dodge his blows. LOMBARD manages to pierce the Cyclops with his razor sharp halberd several times, but the CYCLOPS shows little damage. Meanwhile, the bare fists of the CYCLOPS are tearing into LOMBARD, causing him visible injury.

LOMBARD
This beast is stronger than I realized.

DOBIN zone

LOMBARD and DOBIN realize their plight and head down the bridge to escape the CYCLOPS. As soon as they get to the middle of the bridge, the screen goes blank and we see the message:
LOADING, PLEASE WAIT...

2. NORTH KARANA. Rolling hills in this open land. Our heroes emerge on the other side of the bridge. Behind them, they can see no trace of the CYCLOPS. HARRYBALZ is sitting down on the bridge in front of them, greatly injured.

LOMBARD
It would appear that it is far too dangerous to hunt in that land. This is a new land I see before us here, perhaps we should try to...

LOMBARD is suddenly interrupted by a loud clap of thunder. Suddenly, a thick bank of fog rolls quickly in and the rain begins to fall. LOMBARD and DOBIN look at each other, and then begin to laugh as we FADE OUT

During blackness, and before ending credits roll

HARRYBALZ
Dood, cn u heal me plz?


ENDING CREDITS
