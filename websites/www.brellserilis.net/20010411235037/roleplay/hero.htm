To be a Hero
It grows dark in the southern desert of ro. The smell of rotting flesh blows by my face as continue my journey through the sands, passing by scarabs and men who stagger with a drunken stuper. I see the silouette of someone...something...in the moonlight just ahead. I walk closer to investigate. To my surprise, this being that once was man is now no more than wraps and bones with occasional rotting flesh on the hands....the cheeks...the shoulders. It staggers toward me. I unsheath my sword and raise my shield.

'HALT!' I yell. 'Stand back, or I shal smite ye down with the force of the gods!'

The beast continues to stagger foreward.

'Stand fast!' I warn. I can feel my blood pumping through my body. My hair raises on the back of my neck. I clinch my sword tighter now.

The beast continues. It walks with a limp. I see it in more detail now. It wears rusty, seemingly cheap jewlery on its wrists and fingers. It has a tattered belt on with pouches that have maggots crawling out of them. Worst of all, and most disturbing, a large spider crawls out of its mouth.

I yell, for a final time, in hopes that I do not have to fight today. I just wish to get to the ruins of the druids to meet a druid friend. She is to take me to the newfound lands of Kunark.

My cry for this evil to stop in its tracks does no good.

The beast takes a swing at me, and misses. Dust from its rotting hand and its decaying wraps blows by my face, causing me to caugh. Calling upon the force of the gods, I stun the creature. He could do nothing but stand there, in awe of the power that had just struck him. I then raised my sword, the mighty ghoulbane, and struck at him. The force of my blade severed his arm. Surprisingly enough he did not seem phased by the fact that he had just lost a limb. I want to laugh, as it is a funny sight to see someone stand before you with no arm and with no emotion about it. Before I could even do so, the crumbling, and now one armed, beast staggered foreward again with what seemed to be a smirk on his rotten face. He lunged foreward again, hitting me with his forearm across my brow followed by a kick to my groin. I collapsed. Sickened by the blow i had recieved and surprised by the power this beast seemed to have, I shook my head and looked up at the beast. He was coming for me, yet again, surely with the intent to send me to my maker.

I throw back my hair, wipe my brow and rise to my feet. I parry his attack with my shield and strike him with the hilt of my sword. This staggers him. I run in and swing my ghoulbane at his chest. Not only does it split him in two, but the sword emits a horrid scream and causes the body of the mummy to ignite in a magical flame.

I stand over the burning heap and yell, 'Rotting keepers of the crypts, decaying flesh of norrath, none of ye shall strike me down! For I am Gunganus! I have made gods kneel to me! Dragons have begged me for mercy! You shall soon do the same!'

I sheath my sword and continue my walk. There are new lands to explore.


-- Story originially posted to the Brell Serilis.net Roleplaying Forum by Gunganus
