New Beginnings in an Endless Cycle (Forlorn) (1/2)

The fiery giant toppled to its knees with a groan of protest. He slipped back into a shadowy corner as the mighty band of adventurers rejoiced in their victory. The night was young, and the sojourn into the dragon's lair was going well... normally he would have partaken in the revelry, but this night he was silent. It was one of his dark times.

The wrong emotions were raging in him. Nothing had happened to spur this melancholy, but his usual facade of charm and easy humor would not slip into place. Angry at himself, he attempted to enter his meditative trance to rest and regain his energy for the trial ahead. After some time, too much time, peace found him and his mind started to clear. He started to relax, and remembered that he was here among friends. This... feeling of impending doom was just that; a feeling. Nothing could harm him, especially now - his eyes opened to slits as he glanced at the familiar faces around him. Vumil, dark in his robe of the Arch liches. Mufat and Napzard, busy keeping the younger members of the raid from pulling the entire castle down around their ears. Veranon, calmly receiving and discarding potential crises as they arose.

Miyke and Luna, comforting in their union... so many others, resplendent in the trophies they had won through long and hard fought battles. Tonight, among this company, he was invincible. Why then, was he afraid?

Fear... he only now realized that is what was troubling him. It was an unfamiliar thing; his dark heritage had somehow eliminated the relevance of that emotion to his existence. But he tasted it now, and again fought to master it. He was not afraid of the dragon; this company, under this leadership, would overcome it... it would be easier than when he, Daablo, Dracoh, and Kaiyn defeated the great red wyrm Infuranya all that time ago. For the first time this night, he smiled as he recalled the times spent with those old friends...

With a jolt, he came back to himself. His meditation was broken beyond recall. He had just thought back on fond memories... that he had never experienced. He was in turmoil. Those memories... could only have been Taruen's... could only have been his father's. He growled under his breath. The time had come to seek answers... Kaiyn... he must seek out the Lord Darious, and get some answers.
