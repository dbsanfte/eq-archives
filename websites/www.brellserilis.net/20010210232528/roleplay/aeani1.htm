Tales of Aeani Fairblade (Chap 1)

A heavy-plated helm and sword flings into the ground. Aeani gets up from the lava scorched dirt, staggers, and picks up her sword, kicking away her helm.

"Give it up, Aeani, and submit to your fate," a Dark Elf Shadow Knight calmly says while pointing her sword at Aeani. They both stood on a land completely surrounded by lava, flames, and steam. Aeani never spoke a word but only cries as she tightens her grip on her firey sword and charges after the Dark Elf.

The two begin to sword fight, both exchanging parries and dodges while never successfully hitting each other. It only becomes a struggle to see who gets the first blood, and a fight that neither want to lose. Aeani quickly gains advantage with undodgable swings yet able to be stopped by the Dark Elf, but she finally pushes Aeani's last swing aside and hops away to hold her sword in one hand, pushing her long hair aside with the other. Aeani took this as a taunt and charged her again. The Dark Elf managed to get a few swings that Aeani couldn't dodge and forced to push her blade against hers. Aeani hopped and staggered back in fatigue. They both stop to stare at each other, each knowing who will win this fight. The Dark Elf relaxes her arm and lets the tip of her two-handed sword touch the ground.

"Well, we know who's the best now, don't we?" Aeani could barely be able to stand up strait and still wield her two-handed, firey sword. She then twirled her sword and tucked it away in its sheath. She still could not stand up as easily as she could before, but she clenched both her fists infront of her (knuckles facing the ground). The Dark Elf gleamed the biggest smile of victory on her face, "So you do know! Prepare yourself. This fight is--"

Aeani starts to shout, "This fight... is not OVER!" She began to cast a spell, screaming louder and louder as a flurry of red sparks begin to circle around her and out of her fists. The ground began to tremble, and the air started to circulate around her. The Dark Elf took a step back in complete shock!

"You fool!" The Dark Elf closes her eyes with a scorn look on her face in concentration and held out her left palm at Aeani. A black cloud complete engulfs Aeani, who then gripped with agony and collapsed. Aeani loses her concentration and struggled to stand up from her hands and knees. She barely lifts up her head only to see the Dark Elf holding her two handed sword high above her.

The Dark Elf starts to swing toward's Aeani's head.

"AEANI," screamed a familiar voice in the distance.

"... Aeani? Aeani, suit up and open this door with haste! You're already late for training!"
