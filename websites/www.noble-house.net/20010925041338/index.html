Tuesday, August 21, 2001

Operation: Save Thurgadin. -- by Lohki

Noble Blade gathered in Greater Divide tonight to save Thurgadin from Narandi and his army. The first wave fell no problem. Second wave was a little more resistant but fell but we had a lot of casualties and link deads the last wave. Our brother guild, Knights of the Iron Cross, dropped what they were doing and came to assist us with the third wave.

Several linkdeads later (thanks 30 spectators!) we came out on top. We only saved two of the dorfs, as half our forces were linkdead the whole time so dorfs got the beatdown.

I(Lohki) completed my 10th Coldain Ring.


We also recieved Narandi's Lance which went to Katair...


A Choker of the Wretched which went to Erilyn...


and lastly, Narandi's Eye which went to Dejin...


Again, Special thanks to Knights of the Iron Cross, Noble Blade, the individuals for lending a hand, and the people who graciously left the zone for us to complete this war.
