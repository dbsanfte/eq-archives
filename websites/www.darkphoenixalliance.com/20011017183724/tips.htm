• Which way is North
    If you are young and have not developed your sense heading skill yet or do not have magical means to find North there is a quick way you can. Take a sword and drop it on the ground. The sword will always point North.

  • View, edit or send previous sent chat message
    Did you know that you can edit or quickly repeat a message that you sent. To few the recent history of message that you have sent hold down your SHIFT key and then press the UP ARROW key. This will allow you to scroll through messages that you have sent. Once you have found a message you can quickly edit it by holding down the Shift key and pressing either the LEFT or RIGHT ARROW keys to move within the message. Once you are at the desired location edit you message and press enter to send it.

  • Purchase and sell in bulk
    If you want to buy a stack of 20 of a particular items hold down the SHIFT key when you click on the purchase button. To sell a stack of items that you have again hold the SHIFT key down when you click on the sell button.

  • Learning a skill
    If you are trying to learn a skill increase your INT as much as possible. This will allow you to learn the skill at a faster rate.

  • Add an emote or message to an action
    If you want to add an emote or custom message to an action (casting a spell or using a skill) you will need to creat a HOT KEY. Below is an example to use on how to creat a hot key to send a custom message to your group that you are casting a heal spell on John Doe.

    1st line: /g Incoming heal %T
    2nd line: /cast 8

    Your group members would see the following in group chat if John Doe was targeted.

    Incoming heal John.

    The second line in the hot key cast what ever spell is in the 8th gem slot. In this case it was a heal spell.
