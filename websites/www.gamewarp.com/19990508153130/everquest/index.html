Thursday August 27th, 1998


  EQX changes

By: Stone Time: 12:00AM PST

Source: n/a
  I am sure many of you have already noticed a few minor changes here at  EQXtreme. For the most part, we plan to keep the site as you have become accustomed too. You will however notice slight changes as Seros and myself setlle ourselves in to our new environment. Pardon our recent shortage of updates and feel free to submit any worthwhile suggestions, since we are more apt to try them out/use them during this short remodeling stage.

  Rest assured however that EQXtreme will continue to be the leading source for professional information about Everquest. If anything, we hope to improve upon the excellent standard already set by Zen.

Thank you for the continued support and we look forward to our future together in Norrath!


Monday August 24th, 1998


  Intel announces three new processors

By: Seros Time: 07:11PM PST

Source: n/a
  Yesterday (Aug 24, 1988) Intel announced three new processors, the Pentium II 450 and two new Celeron CPUs ‘Celeron 300 A’ and ‘Celeron 333’. The Celeron processors are very cheap and could be a viable choice for some of you out there. Check out Tom's Hardware for a nice review of the new chips.

  EQX Site Update

By: Seros Time: 07:17PM PST

Source: n/a
  Unfortunately, the Everquest Xtreme site had a "mishap" of sorts this past weekend. Months of data were lost, lots of hard work went down the drain. However, the site will not just fade away. We have a new staff, and over the next few days (weeks?) there may be a design change. In any case, you can still count on EQX to bring you the best EverQuest coverage possible, and in return, we simply ask for your continued patronage.

Thanks!
