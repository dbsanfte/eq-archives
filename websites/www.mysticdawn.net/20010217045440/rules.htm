Basic Rules

 

It is most important, and above all REQUIRED, that all members keep in mind that everything you say and do, is now associated with the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn. Also, this is a Role-Playing guild. When faced with a decision, do what Role-Plays best. Even if that means dying to aid one of the house.

Common Courtesy Rules

1) Profanity is unacceptable.

2) Killstealing is not tolerated, and will be grounds for dismissal from the guild.

3) The training of guards and other creatures for the intent to harm another player is also intolerable, and will be grounds for dismissal from the guild.

4) This is a role-playing guild. Therefore, you will show the respect due to your guild leaders at all times. If in group, or they say otherwise, first name basis is acceptable. However during official business, or in the public's view, the leader's title shall be used.  If a member constantly refuses to show respect to other members of the guild they will be issued a warning.  If it continues, they will be asked to leave the guild.

Guild members Encouraged to Follow

1) Since we are a Role-Playing guild members should stay in character as much as possible.

2) Show respect to other game players.  There may be a situation when faced with aiding a person Out of Character, when In Character you would not.  Remember you might need their aide at some point in time. 

3) Try to travel with other members of the guild as much as possible. It is known that this is not possible to do at all times, but try to remember that we are a guild battling for the forces of good and need to set a good example.  If at anytime you feel it necessary to not role-play and just kill a few monsters, please turn off your role-play tag and go anonymous.

4) By being accepted as a member of the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn, you  swore an oath to stand by your new family above all others.  Travel with your fellow guild mates, for there is strength in numbers and together we are strong.  

5)While adventuring, if any equipment such as weapons, armor, runes, etc., are obtained, and not useful by your current party, please offer them to the members of the guild before selling it to a merchant or auctioning it off.  As you may be searching for a specific item, others may be doing the same.  This does not mean that we condone twinking.  As a matter of fact you should not give an item to another member that would not be in his abilities at that point in time.  In addition to this, any equipment obtained by another member of the guild is theirs to do with what they desire.  At no point in time is any member of the guild required to give the item they have earned to another in the guild.  If they wish to make a gift that is their choice.  ALSO, asking that higher seasoned members obtain items for you that you could not possibly get yourself is unacceptable and could be grounds for dismissal from the guild.  We are not a guild that is here to powerlevel or twink other people.  If that is what you are seeking, seek elsewhere, you will not find it here

6) Please stop and role-play with any that wish to role-play with you.  This speaks highly of both you and the guild.  We want to promote a good role-playing impression at all times.  

7)Help out those that are less knowledgeable than you.  That includes stepping into a battle if they are about to perish and request help or showing them the way to a bank or guild hall.  Again this can only reflect good upon us and shows that we are here to bring good across the lands of Norrath.

8) If you get into a situation where another player is downright rude and disrespectful, do not lower yourself to their level.  Do not resort to using the /shout or /ooc  to resolve the issue, this will only reflect badly upon us.  Please advise the guild of the situation and allow us to assist you to resolve the situation..

9) Have fun!  We are here to enjoy adventures and have fun.

Dueling Rules

1) No looting from your defeated opponent. The only exception to the rule is if it is a predetermined guild quest, and it is intentionally put there for the purpose of the quest.

2) Absolutely no outside interference during a duel.  Any spells or buffs must be in place prior to the start of the duel, unless they can be casted by the individual that is dueling. If these rules are broken it may be grounds for dismissal from the guild.

It should be noted, that these rules apply to all duels. However, as the forces of evil are bound by no such rules, it does not mean that you should break any of the other rules in dealing with the situation. If the situation gets out of hand, please get one of the council involved immediately.

Guild war Rules

1) No training at any point in time.  If it is done report it immediately.

2) Looting is limited to coin only.  NO items may be taken.  Violation of this rule may be grounds for dismissal.

3) After you are killed, there is a one hour "no play"  rule.  You are required to turn off your Role-Play and go anonymous.  At this point you may not do anything that can affect the outcome of the war.  This also means that additionally  nobody else can do anything directed towards you. Only turn off your role-play tag and go anonymous if you have been killed.  The one hour time limit  is in place to prevent the abuse of Lay Hands or Harm Touch to the perspective  classes.

4) During a guild war at no time except due to death, are you allowed to turn off your Role-play tag and go anonymous.  If during the duration of the war you do not wish to participate then you should advise your Superior and log off.

5) During  a Guild War you are required to Check in and out with your Superior.
