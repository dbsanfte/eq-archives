Thank you for your interest in the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn.  We are a small, role-playing guild, with limited membership.  In order to be under consideration for membership, you will need to be recommended by a present guild member.  This means that you must know someone in the guild well , they must be either a good friend, or you have hunted with them on many many occasions. After a present guild member recommends you, a leader will contact you for an interview.
