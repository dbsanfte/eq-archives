As you enter the room of a nearby inn you see four figures sitting around a table studying a parchment.  A Half Elf female looks up at you, letting a single white lock of hair fall in front of her face as she takes a swig from a glass of ale.  Another Half Elf stares at the parchment in silence and you can see on his face his desire to be in the wilds.   A Gnome and Barbarian seem to be in great debate over the single meaning of a translation on the parchment and the entire room seems to be in a slight discomfort.  

With a nervous clearing of the throat, you try to speak up over the noise of the room.  "Excuse me, I am looking for the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn.  I am here to join the cause." 

Every person in the room goes silent except a female Feir'dal in the corner sharpening a great axe, who turns to look you in the eyes.  Your face flushes with excitement and embarrassment, but your resolve, and the fact that your legs don't seem to be working anymore, hold you in place.  What were you thinking  when you decided to take this course, this chance at fate to be one of the elite members of Norrath? 

Then suddenly, a sense of tranquility comes over you as you see a male Feir'dal approach you.  He places his hand on your shoulder and you relax a bit.  You feel a family here and you now desire not only to be here as a part of its cause, but as a member of this unity.  And as the door closes behind you, you can see the smile of a blonde haired Feir'dal with a staff in his hand mystically fade from view.
