Sorry for the lack of pictures, but they take quite a while on my uber 28.8 connection.  I wanted to get an update done sometime this week =)

Ahhhh, hello Brethren!  Once again it is time for my semi-regularly scheduled update of the happenings of the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn from a short, balding halfling cleric's perspective.  Well, the first thing you may notice is that I decided to use a different pen for the scrolls.  People been saying that writing in that scripture style and all that jazz is hard on the eyes for long reads, so I decided to use my #12 Arial pen!  Whaddya think?  I have decided to post some pictures that were taken in this edition, here is my favorite:

 

Typical Falkieri, eh?  Bah!  Get back and fight, ya pansy!  I'm just kidding, Falkieri.  I know that deep down in your heart, you could have taken them!

Anyway, a lot has been going on in Norrath as of late.  I know that the Justicar, Alysandir completed another part of his epic quest!  The troops marched into Castle Mistmoore and slayed the Black Dire Wolf with ease. Congratulations, Justicar!  I hope to aid you in the end!

On the seasonal side of things, Alysandir, Deral, and Falkieri all have achieved their 50th seasons!  Thats a lot of seasons, in my opinion.  Personally, I just gathered my 49th season and boy oh boy am I loving clericdom!

As far as other quests are concerned, I have been briefly updated on a few involving our dearest members!  Iianae has completed her master's quest.  She is now a Grand Master Fletcher!  If ya be needing some arrows or a nice bow, mayhap the fine, wood-elven lass can make ye some or one or both or whatever.  Our monks, Ryszard and Aedwy have completed their Lost Circle quest!  They now don nifty looking robes that make them look like finger wigglers.  Just don't tell them that or ye might take a kick in the tooth.  I know Greyna has completed her quest for the tiny coffin from Neriak for Bucco's studies.  That filthy city stinks of inkie, I am impressed by the savviness and cunning of Greyna in her attaining of this item!  Squire Wingulf has succeeded in attaining his Ravenscale armor off the evil lispards in the Lost Temple of Cazic-Thule.  He also achieved many seasons there!  Way to go all!  I salute thee many times over!  If I forgot a quest, send me a mystical message and I will update it next time!

Now for the hunting!  I noticed many of us spending time slaughtering the foul giants near Kael Drakkel.  These giants have been at war with the noble Coldain dwarves for years and it has come the time to help put an end to their actions!  The dragons of Velious also have a bone to pick with the giants.  I suggest we learn a bit more of this war between the three races before we get ourselves in too deep.  Never know if we are being conned into helping the wrong race.  I shall research the histories a little more and give an update later.  We assisted Chazzmirh, from the Harbingers of Death in his quest for the Coldain Ring.  I took pictures, but for the love of Bristlebane, man!  I lost them somehow.  Me thinks I pressed the * key instead of the - one.  Oh well, I will just post these pictures in honor of the epic battles we had with the orcs and dwarves.  There was blood all around (mostly orcen blood) and no casualties during the dwarf - orc battle and it was truly epic indeed.  We continued in finding Icefang, a big-arse wolf.  He had teeth as long as m'arms and his breath smelt of Santhar's!  But he was a nice puppy, until we tracked him to an orc outpost and he turned on us!  We lost one comrade in this battle, yet we won the skirmish rather quickly.  Chazzmirh had almost finished this quest at the time of my departure.  I wish him well, and great job to the Brethren for supporting him and his noble cause.  Oh, yeah, the screenshots:

02/10/2001

Howdy, folks!  It has come time for yours truly (Jeppo) to begin his semi-regularly scheduled updates to the news involving the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn.  I am hoping to update two or three times a week and I would like to include such nifty things like hunts, quests completed, seasons gained, etc.  I will include screen shots of grandiose things, funny quotes, and other swell things that make our time in Norrath so special.  So please, when ya run across me in Norrath, send me a tell informing me of stuff in your life that you would like to see reported.  DON'T MAKE ME HUNT YA DOWN FOR A GOOD BONKING for lack of information.  You can also e-mail me at jeppo@mysticdawn.net.

So, without further ado, here is a brief update of what has been going on from the Jeppster's perspective!  Well, we tackled the Plane of Fear recently and I am fairly certain many of our members got some planar armor.  I couldn't stay for the entire raid as I began to notice QWERTY on m'face.  The lovely Avatar presented me with a Girdle of the Diligent the next day (Thanks Chrysania!).  With the aid of the Alliance, Cazic-Thule and the undead Draco dragon thingy did fall!

With the assistance of many Brethren members and some of the Nameless Templars, we journeyed into the depths of Nagafen's Lair to take on the Efreeti Lord Djorn!  After a brief setback, we regrouped and made it past all the imps and lava guardians and faced off with the Efreeti himself.  Shoryuuken turned everyone into skeletons, except me (>boggle<) and we took him down rather quickly.  Come see me if ye ever want to check out what treasure the Lord Djorn had on him (lets just say m'feets are yellowier and wiser).

Some of us spent some time in the Plane of Hate this past week as well!  With the leadership of Illucia, from the Decadent Few, we were able to fight off the minions of Innorruk for a couple of evenings!  Also, a big, fat salute to Ryszard for his learning of how to pull that filthy place!  If ye never attended a hunt with Illucia as the leader, I highly recommend it.  It is very memorable, so I suggest to try it at least once!

Hmmm, what else...  Oh, yeah, many of our Brethren achieved the necessary seasons to go on planar raids recently by hunting in the City of Mist!  I'm looking forward to going on more journeys with you all!  Congrats City of Misters!

Oh, I spoke with Squire Wingulf recently and asked him about his Knighthood quest that I gave to him.  He is doing well in the Lost Temple of Cazic-Thule and is achieving many seasons in his quest for the lost Ravenscale armors from the Tae Ew Templar and Tae Ew Diviner.  If ya get a chance to see Wingulf, give him a good luck hug, or hi-five.

Once I figure out how to do these updates better, I intend to organize it a little more.  Please be patient!  I would like to add screenshots, good quotes, and some kind of daily Jeppo-ism so that all of you people get some good reading while taking a break from work, hehe.  Until I write again... be well!

Jeppo Curefinger
