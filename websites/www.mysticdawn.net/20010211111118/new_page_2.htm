The Brethren's Chat Room: Mysticdawn Chat

02/10/2001

Howdy, folks!  It has come time for yours truly (Jeppo) to begin his semi-regularly scheduled updates to the news involving the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn.  I am hoping to update two or three times a week and I would like to include such nifty things like hunts, quests completed, seasons gained, etc.  I will include screen shots of grandiose things, funny quotes, and other swell things that make our time in Norrath so special.  So please, when ya run across me in Norrath, send me a tell informing me of stuff in your life that you would like to see reported.  DON'T MAKE ME HUNT YA DOWN FOR A GOOD BONKING for lack of information.  You can also e-mail me at jeppo@mysticdawn.net.

So, without further ado, here is a brief update of what has been going on from the Jeppster's perspective!  Well, we tackled the Plane of Fear recently and I am fairly certain many of our members got some planar armor.  I couldn't stay for the entire raid as I began to notice QWERTY on m'face.  The lovely Avatar presented me with a Girdle of the Diligent the next day (Thanks Chrysania!).  With the aid of the Alliance, Cazic-Thule and the undead Draco dragon thingy did fall!

With the assistance of many Brethren members and some of the Nameless Templars, we journeyed into the depths of Nagafen's Lair to take on the Efreeti Lord Djorn!  After a brief setback, we regrouped and made it past all the imps and lava guardians and faced off with the Efreeti himself.  Shoryuuken turned everyone into skeletons, except me (>boggle<) and we took him down rather quickly.  Come see me if ye ever want to check out what treasure the Lord Djorn had on him (lets just say m'feets are yellowier and wiser).

Some of us spent some time in the Plane of Hate this past week as well!  With the leadership of Illucia, from the Decadent Few, we were able to fight off the minions of Innorruk for a couple of evenings!  Also, a big, fat salute to Ryszard for his learning of how to pull that filthy place!  If ye never attended a hunt with Illucia as the leader, I highly recommend it.  It is very memorable, so I suggest to try it at least once!

Hmmm, what else...  Oh, yeah, many of our Brethren achieved the necessary seasons to go on planar raids recently by hunting in the City of Mist!  I'm looking forward to going on more journeys with you all!  Congrats City of Misters!

Oh, I spoke with Squire Wingulf recently and asked him about his Knighthood quest that I gave to him.  He is doing well in the Lost Temple of Cazic-Thule and is achieving many seasons in his quest for the lost Ravenscale armors from the Tae Ew Templar and Tae Ew Diviner.  If ya get a chance to see Wingulf, give him a good luck hug, or hi-five.

Once I figure out how to do these updates better, I intend to organize it a little more.  Please be patient!  I would like to add screenshots, good quotes, and some kind of daily Jeppo-ism so that all of you people get some good reading while taking a break from work, hehe.  Until I write again... be well!

Jeppo Curefinger
