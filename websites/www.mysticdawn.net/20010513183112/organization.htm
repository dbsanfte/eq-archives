Rose of Faydwer

Deep within the heart of Faydwer lies the knowledge of Trade and Battle.  The Rose of Faydwer is dedicated to fighting with blade, bow, wisdom, or cure, whether it is on the front line of defense or the support role from supplies, healing, and the gathering of useful knowledge.  Behind the cover of the title of a merchant or tradesmen, we are also the eyes and ears of the Brethren.  It is our duty to gather information and allow the Brethren to be better prepared for any foe.  We are not spies, though some may see it that way.  We are Scouts of the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn, but we travel under the auspices of Master tradesmen.  We are the gatherers of the knowledge that will prove us stronger than our enemies.  We are a pillar of the Dawn, and thusly it is the information that we gather with our network of stealthy Scouts, that helps to strengthen the Brethren and further our fight for good.

This order is lead by the Templar.  It falls upon the Templar to ensure the order runs smoothly and the ranking among the Rose of Faydwer is as follows:

Grand Master of Faydwer

In order to be a Grand Master one must accomplish the duties required for advancement to the position of Master of Faydwer.  Additionally they must achieve the status of Grand Master in one trade skill (200) and the mastery of two non-trade skills.  Finally, this individual has rendered valuable services to the Rose of Faydwer that are recognized by the Templar.

Master Jack-of-all-Trades of Faydwer

In order to be a Master Jack-of-all-Trades, one must accomplish all the duties required for the advancement to the  position of the Master of Faydwer.  Additionally they must achieve the status of Master in two trade skills and Master in two non-trade skills.  Finally, this individual has rendered valuable services to the Rose of Faydwer that are recognized by the Templar.

Master of Faydwer

In order to be a Master one must accomplish all of the duties in the two ranks below and achieve the support of the Avatar and Templar.  Additionally, one must master one trade  skill and one non-trade skill that benefit the Rose of Faydwer. (This does not mean the giving of free made materials, weapons, or such to guild members; we are a Role-players guild not a group of twinkers)  While the skills can range from tailoring to conjuration to One-Hand slashing, or any others including fishing, it must be a MASTER SKILL. Again, proof must be submitted in the form of a screenshot e-mailed to the Templar.  Finally, this individual has rendered valuable services to the Rose of Faydwer that are recognized by the Templar.

Journeyman of Faydwer

In order to be a journeyman in this order one must accomplish all the duties of the rank below and achieve the support of the Templar and a Master.  Additionally, one must raise one trade skill and one non-trade skill to to the level of "good".  To prove the achievement a screenshot of the skill in question must be e-mailed to the Templar benefit the Rose of Faydwer.  Whether this is in spell casting, prayers (clerics), or weapons it must be a mastered or excellence achieved skill.  Journeyman are dedicated to upholding the values of the Dawn, while striving to master their skill in order to benefit the cause.  Finally, this individual has rendered valuable services to the Rose of Faydwer that are recognized by the Templar.

Apprentice

Apprentices are beginning in their profession and as such require instructors to help them on their way.  The Templar is responsible for making sure assignments or quests given to the apprentices by the Master are completed.  Knowing that apprentices will someday master the skills and prove their value to the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn, makes all the efforts worthwhile.

Special positions within Faydwer

Purveyor of Faydwer

Leader of all the peddlers of Faydwer, the Purveyor oversees that all the peddlers maintain a pricing standard.  He is also in charge of investigating the standard market prices  for items that the Brethren wish to liquidate .  The Purveyor may be called on by any in the guild for appraisal of their assets and may also be employed by other outside of the guild, for a small fee.

Peddler of Faydwer

The Peddlers of Faydwer are the liquidators of the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn.  They are responsible for accepting a contract from others in order to sell items that fellow Brethren wish to commission them for.  They are to confirm pricing with the Purveyor of Faydwer, and maintain fair pricing.  They are awarded a commission percentage per contract.  As peddlers, they have access to many travelers across Norrath, and as such, are in the prime position to gather information.

 

Rose of Antonica

Antonica, the largest mass of land known on Norrath and the center to two powerful lands Odus and Faydwer is also home to the evil of heart.  Where ghastly deeds of torture and inflictions of disease are performed for pleasure by endless races.  On this piece of land, one can find just about anything in the dark, but where there is dark a Mystic Dawn rises and with it the bloom of the Rose of Antonica.

Rose of Antonica is only for those with the courage and the stamina for a life of war.  The Rose of Antonica (also called RoA) is called in for the special assignments, when it is life for life situations.  This is an order of hardcore individuals of any class (spell casters, warriors, clerics, paladins, ranger, bards, druids,  etc.) dedicated to the preserving of the lives of innocents and others of the Dawn.  Only the  strong of heart need apply.

This  order is lead by the Justicar.  It falls upon the Justicar to ensure his order runs smoothly and the ranking among the Rose of Antonica is as follows:

Knight Commander

Knight Commander is the second in command, and is the right hand to the Justicar and helps lead and plan the moves of the RoA.  His responsibilities include giving out training assignments (quests) that are attainable to the group.  This is the highest rank within the RoA and is considered one of great respect.  This is the only rank where a new Justicar can be selected from and must have the approval of the Avatar.  The second in command would not be activated except upon the request of either the Avatar and/or a member of the council and the Justicar is not present.  

Knight Prelate

A staff position, serving as the Justicar's assistant and aide in all matters relating to the RoA.  This is an advisory position in matters before the RoA and would never assume command unless both the Justicar and the Knight Commander are absent and is so requested by the Avatar.

Knight Exemplar

Squad leader , reporting directly to the Justicar.  They will be assigned members who will serve under him, and are responsible for all in their squad at ALL times.

Knight Rose Croix

An honorary title given once a Knight of the order of the Rose of Antonica achieves the Twentieth season.

Knight

The Knights of the RoA, carry out the the orders of the Justicar and all their superiors in the field.  A Knight must follow orders of their commanding officers at all times.  A member of the Rose of Antonica receives the title of Knight once they achieve what is required of them.

Squire

Squire are the grunts of the operation and are the most deadly of positions within the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn.  Be prepared to be given special assignments.  This is where the initiates prove themselves.  Promotions are given for discipline, courage under fire, and valor.

Rose of Odus

The Rose of Odus is dedicated to restoring the former glory of the arts, through training and study, dedication and discipline. We help our fellow brethren fight the evils around us, while seeking to uplift and find kindred spirits who wish to bring order and beauty back to the arts of magic.

In this pursuit, we have set the following ranks for persons studying in the Rose of Odus:

Magistrate

The first rank is that of the Magistrate, who is the one who keeps the Rose focused and together. His duties sometimes include acting as a sage to younger members of the Rose, as well as the organizer of events related to the Rose of Odus. He will also report to and advise the Avatar directly on the status of his Rose and it's members, as well as determining promotions within the Rose of Odus. The current Magistrate is Bucco.

Magus

The Magus is the second in command of the Rose. There is only one Magus at any time. The Magus is tapped from the ranks by the Magistrate, and by permission of the Avatar. Much of the responsibility for the well being and safety of the Rose falls upon the Magus and his Sorcerer Protectus, and therefore the Magus is not required to be a pure caster. The current Magus is Adon Ironhouse.

Sage

The next rank is that of Sage, or Sorcerer Primus, and is rarely filled. Few find this lifestyle possible in these times, and so Sages are sometimes not available and the Magistrate will be called upon to fulfill this position when it is called for. The role of the Sage is to simply research and be a source of knowledge to the Brethren. He has no obligation for quests or administrative duties, and is not bound to the Rose of Odus strictly. Any of the Brethren may call upon him for information, and may seek his advice and wisdom in times of trouble. He answers only to the Advisors and the Avatar for direct commands. No other members of the Rose report to him, although often many previous students continue to offer personal loyalty and service. A sage will spend much of his time in solitude, disregarding much that goes on around him. The requirements for the rank of Sage are known only by Sages, the Magistrate, and the Magistrate's Magus. They retain all rights and privileges granted by prior ranks.

Mage

The next rank is that of Sorcerer Altus, or Mage. This rank is reserved for those that have demonstrated extreme loyalty and skill in their art. The few who achieve this rank are revered highly by their peers, are quite knowledgeable in a large variety of subjects, and are virtually unparalleled in their talents. They are an elite, and may be called upon at any time by the advisors or the Avatar to assist in a difficult situation. Their duties include assigning tasks to subordinates and fulfilling major quests set out by the advisors and the Avatar, They retain all rights and privileges granted by prior ranks., including of Sorcerer Praelium. One may achieve this rank only by arduous work and long hours of study, by proving themselves on the battlefield, and by recommendation by the Avatar.

Master Sorcerer -- 24+ Seasons

The Sorcerer Praelium are the teachers of the Rose of Odus. Attaining this rank is a difficult process requiring submission of a thesis upon a specific area of magecraft in Norrath  demonstration of knowledge of tactics both in group and solo adventuring, spells, and spell research methods, for all three of the casting professions of Wizard, Enchanter, and Magician, and testimonials of teaching ability from former students and apprentices which the Journeyman had tutored under the guidance of a current Master.

Once this rank is achieved, the new Sorcerer Praelium is expected to begin acquiring students and apprentices. He is expected to be available to them for tutoring on a regular basis. He may assign tasks and quests to them, and may allow Journeymen to assist in tutoring under his guidance. He is responsible for their safety, their learning, and their behavior. Note: Master Sorcerers are knowledgeable in the tactics of all three pure casting professions, and therefore may have apprentices from any school, rather than just their specific profession.

Sorcerer Praelium is a voluntary position, and while each Sorcerer a Palaestra is encouraged to follow this path, some may prefer not to teach. It is not required for the rank of Mage, although it is a useful step along the way and much more likely to lead to promotion to Mage.

Journeyman Sorcerer – 20+ Seasons

The Journeyman Sorcerer is the first rank with a title, Sorcerer a Palaestra. A Sorcerer a Palestra has graduated his apprenticeship, and is considered capable of self teaching through experimentation and research from this point on. Granting of this rank is given by the Magistrate after two assigned Masters have tested the candidate’s knowledge of magecraft appropriate to this rank. These tests are threefold – First, a demonstration of research ability shown by presenting the Masters with two new spells the candidate has researched, which must be of the sixth circle (level 20 spells); Second, a demonstration of proficient spell use in a solo situation; and Third, a verbal examination of the tactics used in hunting within a group, and working efficiently with other professions.

Duties: Journeymen Sorcerers assist the Brethren with their magic while continuing their personal growth. Occasionally, they will be assigned a task or quest to be completed by a superior, but generally they are self-directed. They no longer require permission to hunt anywhere. They no longer are subject to the commands of the Master, although personal loyalty and friendship often remain, and they may choose to remain in the Master’s employ. They may teach students and apprentices under the guidance of a Master.

This is the highest rank at which one may enter the Rose of Odus. To enter at this rank, one must complete the tests as stated above, and must have two members of the Brethren to testify to the Magistrate and Avatar as to your worthiness to join the Brethren. Either the Magistrate or the Avatar will most likely require other tests, and there is a one week probationary period for non-apprentices seeking membership at the Journeyman level..

Apprentice Sorcerer – 12-19 Seasons

The Apprentice may or may not have completed a training period as a Student, but in any case must demonstrate proficiency at the Bind Affinity spell and pass a verbal test on spell tactics in his specific field of study, to be administered by the Master who proposes to accept the candidate as an Apprentice. Only Masters may have Apprentices. Once the Sorcerer Praelium is satisfied as to the candidate’s worthiness, he will inform the Magistrate who will seek permission from the Avatar to induct the candidate as a full member of the Brethren. Acceptance as a Apprentice and as one of the Brethren go hand in hand, and must be granted simultaneously. Either the Magistrate or the Avatar may require other tests of worthiness, and there is a one week probationary period, which may have been completed while a Student.

Duties:  The Apprentice owes service to the Master, and the Master is responsible for the development and training of the Apprentice in all aspects of magecraft. Personal tutelage will be received from the Master, and the Master will assign tasks and quests to the Apprentice. The Apprentice still must be granted permission to hunt in a new area. He may bind himself in any city, but to bind in another area will require the permission of the Master, even if permission is granted already to hunt in that area.

Student – 4-11 seasons

 Not officially a member of the Brethren, Students apply to a Master Sorcerer for training in the arts of magic while their worthiness to become an Apprentice Sorcerer and a member of the Brethren is ascertained.  Demonstration of the Gate spell is required for acceptance as a Student. The student may be assigned minor tasks to complete for the Sorcerer Praelium as payment for the tutelage received.  The Sorcerer Praelium may assign the apprentice to a Sorcerer a Palestra for tutoring, should that Journeyman Sorcerer be willing. This assignment must be agreed upon by all parties. The Student should have permission from the Master (or Journeyman if so assigned) to practice his skills in a new area. The tutoring Sorcerer may choose to accompany the student, or to take a group of students, to a new area and personally watch over them for some time before granting them permission to hunt freely in the area. This is for the student’s protection.

OTHER RANKS

Sorcerer Protectus

Sorcerer Protectus are the Physical Guardians of the Rose of Odus. They are responsible for the safety and well being of the members of the Rose of Odus.  Their duties are directly assigned by the Sage and they answer directly to him. All professions other than pure casters who belong to the Rose of Odus are Sorcerer Protectus. They may also be granted ranks of Mage or Sage, or be tapped by the Magistrate as his Magus.

 

Tunare Talon's

It is said that long ago Tunare sent spirits from the plains to help her children without pride or prejudice to help those who walk along the path of life.  So Tunare would give these spirits the ability to bond with her children and they would have all of their strengths and weaknesses.  They would be able to travel through the spirit world from one place on Norrath instantly, they would take the form of animals and run the wilds, they would be the Talon of Tunare and would bring her wisdom or become her vengeance.

Tunare Talon's is made up of a pack of tight members whose sole purpose is to guide the Brethren of the Mystic Dawn on their journeys through life and death, although they fall under no direct leadership except Tunare.  They are there to help the Avatar or the three heads of the Rose in all their actions including the members within each order.  They sometimes babble or speak in riddles, other times they take the form of nature herself and cannot be understood.  Looking in their eyes one can see the look of the wild.

Tunare grants membership to the pack through invitation and the bonding of a spirit.
