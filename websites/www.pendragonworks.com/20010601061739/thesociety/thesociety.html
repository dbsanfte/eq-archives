"MISSION STATEMENT"

We are "THE SOCIETY"

Our mission in this world is to stand strong among our brothers and sisters.

To be honor bound, To be honest, Never to scam, lie, cheat, or otherwise hurt another person in this world....We DO NOT Kill Steal, but we WILL protect our brethren and our Kills with our very lives!!!! We will help others when needed, and we will always be there for our brethren when ever it is humanly possible....We live by one code and one code only "our honor is our life"

 

      SECTION 1 - Leadership of Guild
        Run by two leaders and a council. See membership pages for leaders and officers names.
          Notes: Once a member is made a officer, all of that members characters are officers.


      SECTION 2 - Guild Justice & Review
        Any violations of the guild rules must be reported to a guild officer. Violation/Complaint will be promptly reviewed by a Leader AND at least TWO officers, who will hear evidence from both the accuser and the accused. Repercussions can include any or all of the following depending on the severity of the infraction, Verbal Warning, Fines, Loss of Officer status/Expulsion. Decisions made by the required Leader and Two officers will be explained to the accused prior to any action being taken and will be announced to the entire council at first chance.


      SECTION 3 - General Rules
      The Society is based on brotherhood, it is expected that we all help our guild and guild brothers and sisters.
      Use of guildchat- Never use guildchat to complain against another members. Complaints and reports of violations should only be made to guild officer thru /tell. Otherwise guild chat is at your discretion.
     If A member has an item for sale or donation please give consideration to fellow guild members before going public
      As for other forms of assistance the basic rule is simple: All members if safely possible to render aid to a guildmate should do so, not limited to time or zone restrictions.
      NO Kill Stealing- Defined as intentionally taking someone or some parties pull or not following a fairly established rotation at a camp.
      Members are responsible for those they are hunting with. If a member of your party is behaving against the guildlines of "The Society" then you could be considered guild by association.


      SECTION 4 - Guild Funds
        Guild funds will be maintained for now thru donations only. If you can donate to guild fund great. There will be mules (equipped with backpacks) in or near each major area to whom you may give the donations. Records of these donations will be updated at officer meetings. These guild funds will be used to help out members in need at the discretion of Guild leadership, also for guild events and possibly quests.


      SECTION 5 - Guild Recruitment Policy
        "The Society" will be looking for new members. Membership policy forthcoming, in the words of one of our leaders "I'd kind of would like us to start recruiting heavy not only Higher levels but worthy newbies also".
      


      SECTION 6 - Guild Alliance Policy
        "The Society" is always for and will always support helping others which includes other guilds. However we will NOT entertain "mergers" with any other guilds. We believe simply that we would lose our identity as "The Society" if we were to dilute ourselves with merger after merger. We value being the "family" that we have come to know and appreciate.
