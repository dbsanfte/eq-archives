Custom domains and registration now available Thursday, October 02, 2003 - 9:55:31 AM

I just deployed a patch that allows you to register a custom domain (www.yourguild.com), and use it instead of the semi-private guildwizard (http://yourguild.guildwizard.com) address.  You can click the `Domains` button for more details, there is a FAQ link there too for more details. 

If you already have a domain registered, and would like to use it, it can be used as well.  You have to point your DNS to our server and inform us of your intention to do so, so that we can add your domain to our accepted domain list. 

Next patch will take care of the issues that have been reported in the last couple weeks.  Raid, Encounter, Item grid sorting is up first.. I am also going to do some searching in certain areas where the cache isn`t updating immediatly, namely after raids are entered.

Sometimes it`s better to be smaller! Thursday, August 28, 2003 - 10:53:03 PM

Sometimes it`s better to be smaller ... well when it comes to image file size anyway :)

This is a note, ok a plea to people posting pictures for news items, profiles, gallery items etc.. OPTIMIZE YOUR IMAGES!  Now as you know I have been really tightening up Guildwizard... and I am running a lot of analysis on how much bandwith is being used etc.  I am noticing that images are being posted in excess of 125kb per image, which is quite large.  I, for fun, optimized the same image and got it down to 35k.  Thats a huge difference.. 

So..  this is where its at.  I am going to politely ask everyone to optimize their JPEGS a little better...  you dont have to have the settings at Maximum for general web-browsing, for the most part.  You can easily get an image to be a third of the file size by setting down to Medium or Medium Low, instead of Maximum.

In the next few weeks I will see if people are doing it themselves.. if not, I am going to write an automated service on the server which will `Do it for you`.. I will probably enforce smaller uploads too, if need be.  I don`t want to do it, as its about 5-6 hours of work that I could spend on other guildwizard features, instead of playing bandwith cop :)
