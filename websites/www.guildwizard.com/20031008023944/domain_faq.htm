Q: How much does it cost to register a domain with GuildWizard.com?
 A: The cost is $19.99US for the domain registration.

What if I already own my own domain?
 If you already own a domain, and have DNS set up, you simply have to email me for instructions. I will need to know the domain name, and your guild's name. You will also need to point the DNS to the guildwizard server.

Q: If we didn't register through you, is there an additional charge to use our domain?
 A: No, not at this time. The only requirement, is that you are a paid GuildWizard subscribing guild.

Q: Is DNS hosting included in the registration fee?
 If you are registering the domain through GuildWizard.com, I will host the DNS at no charge. This is subject to change. If you registered your domain elsewhere and need DNS hosting, the cost is $10US. Email us if you need this set up.

Q: Who 'owns' the domain when I register it?
A: You own the domain, I am simply registering it for you.  You have full rights to the name, and full control over the domain account.

Q: Can we use both our custom domain AND our guildwizard semi-private address?
A: Yes, both will stay active.

Q:What if we stop using GuildWizard? Can the domain be used still??
A: Yes, since you have control over the domain, you can point it to another service very easily.
