Guild Charter

We are a guild formed around friendship and trust. We are dedicated first to our fellow guildmates, and next to all those in need. Above all, we are a family. We follow a few simple rules:

  • Always respect others, regardless of class, race, sex, level, or deity.
  • Aide guildmates at all times.
  • Aide those around you whenever possible.
  • Do not intentionally cause the death of another player, outside of friendly duels.
