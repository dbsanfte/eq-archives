Being mostly of the elven variety, you might think that my little help section this time on how to be a troll is way off base.  Wrong-O my friends.  Having adventured much throughout the lands I've seen trolls... plenty of them, and I know what they all do to be the best characters they can be.  However, I cannot promise that this information won't be hard hitting and accurate, so I must put forth this notice:

Right, now with that out of the way, let's continue.  If you're not quite catching on to the troll-like ways, this will surely give you some good ideas.  The first rule of being a troll is that you are your own best friend.  Sad, but true, you're no Cindy Crawford or Tom Cruise, and with the B.O. of a dumpster on trash day, you just aren't going to attract flocks of folks with your charisma.  So since you don't have to worry about impressing others, let's see what you can stop doing now to start saving time and gold and start getting on with your adventuring life.

Tip: Soap is VERY overrated.  You don't need it!  In fact, some trolls I know have contests going to see how long they can go without touching water.  The dirtier the better!  It might even keep the baddies away from you if their eyes start watering within 100 feet of where you're standing.   Never swim and for Pete's sake if it starts raining go inside.  Water's for drinking, and even then if you can find some nice Ogre Swill instead that would be much better.

Soap, Lose it, don't Use it!

Next Tip:  Scratch your butt - A LOT!   It could be just evolution that has made the brains of the larger folks move south the the rear end region, causing the instinctual "scratching of the head" when puzzled effect.  Or it could just be that being so large trolls and ogres are always picking pieces of squished dwarves off of their pants.  Whatever the cause, butt scratching is a MUST.  Do everything in your power to get an itchy rear.   Whether it be using a lot of public restrooms or playing "shake your booty" with a festering hag, do it!  It's the law.

You might be saying now "But Bixby, how can trolls be so repulsive yet still look so suave in the eyes of our fellow race?" -- to which I say homemade plastic surgery.  Yes, remember your little elven mothers and fathers saying to you "Don't run with that or you'll poke your eye out!"?   Troll parents tend to give knives, ice picks, and sharp sticks to their babies instead of rattles and teddy bears.  If you can run around with a short sword and poke your eye out, scar your face, or give yourself accidental open heart surgery, then you, my friend, are one sweet looking troll!  Look out for those hot troll chicks -- they will come running, I guarantee!

Lastly, but certainly not least of all, eat.   You cannot be a troll and be as thin as a dark elf.  It's not going to happen.   Eat anything that comes into range.  Chunks of meat, berries, bread, gnomes.   Slightly more than a mouthful, those gnomes are good fat sources -- and tasty when served with BBQ sauce.  Save the nose for that special someone... all that fat and crispy goodness... mmmmm.  Pardon me :)  I know what I'll be cooking tonight ;)

Start using these tips and the rest should follow quite nicely.
