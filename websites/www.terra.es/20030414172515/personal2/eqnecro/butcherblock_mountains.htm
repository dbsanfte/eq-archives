ButcherBlock Mountains

Ok now, ButcherBlock is mainly the dwarves newbie zone. But since we necros are baddies, lets kick some dwarven asses  : ) 

Like in any other newbie zone, there are several guards spread all over the zone. I have pointed out  their posting cottages on the map. Some of those guards are low level who will con green for you. Some others are level 45 (the ones by Kaladim gates) and they are tough fellows. But there are some lvl 40 ones who spawn by two of them. That will be the target you are aimming to. I do not remember which of the guard spawns are actually the lvl 40 ones, so you will have to check that out by yourself : )

First of all, I would advice you to bring over a small party with you. Maybe three of you would be perfect. Why? Because these guys spawn by couples, and because they have a very high Magic Resistance. Main issue is that you would indeed have some problems to brake up the spawn if either Darkness or Fear are resisted, and that will most likely happend. Lets face it : you cannot handle two of these guards at once. That being said, and considering you are a cautious fella  : )  and will bring some guildmates along with you, read on.

These guys havent got many HP ( about 3250 ) so they should not get you into any problems. Once you have broken the spawn, your group will run the spawn easily. In order to brake the spawn, get some distance and Fear one guard away, sick pet to the second one and Darkness-Fear-Dot him. Even DD him with lvl 44 ignite bones spells (check spells list if needed) because sooner or later the Fear effects will wear off of the first guard and he will madly head his ass towards your very face !!  Always keep an eye up on the first guard and be ready to recast Fear on him in case it wears off before you take the other one down. Once the spawn is broken, just use your timing sense so they wont spawn together again. If by any chance you are in a 6 guys full group, you might want to try to run 2 spawns : killing the 2 guards in one certain hut, and afterwards running upon the next hut on the road to reapply the process. If your group is good enough, this can be very exp beneficial

And thats it. An easy way to level from lvl 45 up to 50 without taking a big risk (again, if you are in a small party). They drop 22 plats worth at vendors axes, but apart from that loot is not anything to dream of. And of course, no need to say that you will be as popular in Kaladim as Slodovan Milosevik in a NATO congress. But since you were already KoS there, who the hell cares.
