Necromancer epic quest

The epic quests are class specific quests that would reward the owner with the most outstanding item in game for each class. They are very hard to get (that's why they are epic), and oftenly include killing stuff like gods, dragons or simillar uber mobs. Of course, these epic quests can only be achieved with the help of many post 50 guildmates / friends. At least no less than three or four high level balanced groups.

The necro epic weapon is called Scythe of Shadowed Souls. It's a 1 hander with awesome stats. But, of course, the less important thing about epic weapons are the stats. All of them have got a certain right clickeable mana free effect, which is supossed to be a class defining spell (example : chanter's epic effect is ultra melee haste, cleric's is 56 exp rez, mage's is a uber pet, etc). Well, Necromancer's epic effect is called Torment of Shadows, a 1200 hp DoT in 1.36 mins with 65% snare factor. Basically, is a mana free lvl 49 Cascading Darkness (check spell list for more details). Casting time is 9 seconds and resistance rate is somehow high.

In my opinion, the necro epic is one of the worst of them all. Stats are meaningless -by the time you are able to get the scythe, your main stats will be pretty much maxed-, and the effect is, well, just not epic. We high lvl necro already use lvl 29 Dooming Darkness in Stead of lvl 49 Cascading (check spell list for more details) due to the long casting time and similar snare factor which is Darkness spell's main point. Besides, I do not belive Darkness to be the necro class defining spell - /bonk Verant's design team -. In my opinion it should have been a Lifetap (maybe lvl 59er touch of night?) since lifetaps only belong to necromancy realms. 

Anyway, to complete your necromancer epic quest weapon, do as follows :

  • Head to Nektulos Forest and find the female dark elf NPC by the river loc -1000, -700. Talk to her to get the introduction of the quest.
  • Head to Lake Rathe and find Kazen Facae loc 340 -1600
  • Kill Sir Edwin Motte (level 33 paladin). He is easiest to catch in tavern by the docks in East Free port where he is on a rotation with the Tumpy Irontoe.
  • Bring head of Motte to Kazen and recieve a Symbol of the Appretice.
  • Bring Symbol of the Appretice to the DE in Nektulos Forest, she'll return to you Twisted Symbol of the Apprentice.
  • Go to Najena and camp Flowing Black Robe (or just purchase it for 200 pp)  and when you get it go to Nektulos Forest and give FBR to DE female and get some moss.
  • Head to Lake Rathe and give moss and Twisted Symbol of the Apprentice to Kazens apprentice, Emkel. Get Symbol of the Serpant.
  • Go to Swamp of No Hope and find Ssessthrass loc 3800 1600 give him Symbol of the Serpant, receive Scaled Symbol of the Serpant.
  • Head to Chardok. Kill Royal Herbalist and loot Manisi Herb (found on way to overking area, right before the secret door which leads to overking room). You'll need few full groups to raid Chardok.
  • Go back to Ssessthrass and give him Scaled Symbol of the Serpant and herb, and he'll return Refined Manisi Herb.
  • Head to Lake Rathe and give Emkel the Refined Manisi Herb, he will give you Symbol of Testing. Have about 12-15 people ready (clerics and tanks are a must) and give Symbol of Testing to Kazen. 3 mobs will spawn. Kill these, last only will drop symbol of Insanity.
  • Go to Timerous Deep, give Symbol of Insanity to Drendico Metalbones (near where evil raft drops you off, on hill) and he gives you his journal.
  • Gather Cloak of Spiroc Feathers (quest in Air given by Drakis Bloodcaster. To get one, you have to get a silver disk, a spiroc feather, and a black silk cape. Give all three to Jzil G'Six), Eye of Innoruuk (before used to be dropped off Inny himself, but now it is randomly dropped by a mini boss mob in second floor in Hate), and Blood of Cazic Thule (before used to be dropped off Cazic Thule himself, but now it is randomly dropped by the golems in Fear). Bring all 3 along with Drendico's journal to timerous deep. Give to Drendico, he returns to you a prepared reagent box.
    Take prepared reagent box to Kazen, and give to him...you get back a tome of instruction.
  • Go to Plane of Air, give Thunder Spirit Princess 3pp (it will trigger Gkzzallk in the Gorgalosk isle windmill). Then clear isle to 3rd (Gorgalosk) isle. Give Gkzzallk the tome of instruction. You get back Gkzzallk in a box. (no battle.)
  • Go to lake Rathe, give Kazen the Gkzzallk in a box (no battle). Get back Scythe of Shadowed Souls !!
