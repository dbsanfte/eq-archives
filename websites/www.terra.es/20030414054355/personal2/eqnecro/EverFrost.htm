Ever Frost Peaks

Right behind the newbie zone BlackBurrow, a vast and frozen land can be found. Its Ever Frost Peaks.

This zone is mostly a high teens zone, where mamuts and orcs gave me a nice exp loooong ago  : ).  Anyway, we havent come here in a turism trip, have we? Ok then, leave the photo camera and the hawaiian design T-shirt aside and mem your spells up.

We will be headed towards the east wall of the zone. Exact loc is +1900, -6200  -take a look to the map if you get lost-. Watch out for the barbarian NPCs, they scowl at you and they are damn tough. I was once hanging around there doing some buttscratching when I was lvl 52, and I decided to drop even more my Halas faction just for the hell of it. So I walked up there and pulled a barbarian guard. Well, he resisted my darkness, so I sent pet to him, and he resisted my darkness again. Hhmmm... a chilling nervousness ran thru me when the third cast got resisted. My pet was getting beaten badly so I decided to fear the bastard. Guess what? Exactly, got resisted. I Feigned Death right afterwards just to realize that they are almost 100% magic resistant. Same applies for the lvl 30 named polar bear (Iceberg). So Gather Shadows yourself all the way till your destination. 

Now, we will be looking for some Ice Giants hunting. They are somewhere between lvl 40 to 45, have around 5000 hp and hit for 110, so nothing really that you havent seen before. There are three spawns, all of them by the Permafrost zone entrance -check map-. 

You can make awesome exp in this zone if you get the twin spawn at the fortress entrance. Spawning time is 6 mins, so its fast indeed. I know of one necro friend of mine who made his way from 50 to 53 in 10 days !! no bullshitting here. Unfortunately, they are camped almost every second day. There is a third spawn inside the corridor too (blue crossed on map).

I would recomend you to not let your pet to get kill, as opossed as what I told you in the Burning Woods zone section. Why? Cose you have to wait anyway 6 mins for the Giant to respawn, meaning no non-stop pulling here, so try at least to get full exp from each kill. In order to do 51%+ of the damage, and considering they have around 5000 hp, Cascading Darkness, Splurt and Bond of Death should be enough. Leave the rest for the pet while you sit down regenerating mana up with Lich. This specially if you got the twin spawn, and hence you dont want to burn too much mana.

Lets say you got the twin spawn (red crosses on map). Dont worry to have them both aggroed on you cuz they are far enough to not to. Also, when you fear them off, they will tend to run thru the entrance towards the open fields, so no train risk at all.

What about loot ? Giants in antonica have always been known for their excellent cash loot. Well, the Ice Giants top them all !! Up to 40 pp cash !!  The only thing is that you ll be soon overloaded with coppers, silvers and golds. But dont worry, they is always a banker around (read a newbie who will run to Halas bank for a proffit).

Im telling you, if you manage to get the twin spawn for your own, exp will definitely fly, and your platinum resources too. If, alternatively, only one spawn of all them three is available, Id rather be looking for a TP anywhere close to Burning Woods.
