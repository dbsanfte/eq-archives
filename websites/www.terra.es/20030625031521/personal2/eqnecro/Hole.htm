The hole

The hole is high level dungeon situated off paineel newbie hunting zone. The entrance is blocked by a big rock, and the key can be purchased at paineel vendors (the one in front of the bank) for 100 pp. Alternatively, you can duck thru the small hole between the rocks, pressing continiously the 'd'  key. Anyway, I recomend you to buy the key.

There is something very special about this dungeon. It has not been dessigned, either on porpouse or not, like any other high level dungeon in the game : It ain't got 'rooms' to camp, it's empty most of the times, people don't gather at the zone in entrance looking for pick up groups, etc. In my opinion, the main factor to explain The Hole's lack of adventurers is that it's frigging apart from the rest of the world and you must have a gater/evac'er in your party. In the other hand, Verant has implanted a 25% exp bonus here trying to atract player to the Hole. All this characteristics have made the Hole the perfect raiding dungeon where guilds come and practice their organization, team work, etc.

Ok now, there are two hot exp spots in the Hole :

  • The entrance : Just right after zoning in, you will see a corridor which leads to the depths of the dungeons. By the side, there are 6 elementals spawning points guarding the way. This elemental start turning green at lvl 55, so they are around level 40 (my guess). They hit for 100 and don't have many HPs. They drop fast indeed, so the exp is very good (top that off with the 25% exp bonus and you got a serious exp boosting oportunity).

In my opinion, there is no need of a full length party at the entrance. Grab a 3 or 4  members party (heavy melee prefered, supported by a healer and a snarer) and pull away those elementals (blue points in the map) and also from the corridor. Whatch out for the Rock Golem though. He is lvl 52-54, hits for 140 and has got tons of HPs.

This spot is very good for a small exp party due to the closeness of the spawns, the 25% exp bonus and the zoning line border is just 30 feet away.

  • The courtyard : If you got a balanced full party, you might want to jump off the cliff down to the courtyard. Please, note that once you do the jump down (yellow arrow on the map) there is no way back unless you raid thru the whole dungeon. Of course you can't do that with one group, so make sure you bring a teleporter along so he can port the melees back out.

The mobs here are bit tougher, and an enchanter is a must. There are plenty of roamers in the courtyard, and chances to have few adds on the pull are high. You better have an experienced puller too, or else the whole group will be in deep....

I assure you that as long as you control the situation, you'll have non stop pulling and exp will indeed fly upwards.

What about loot? the main armour set you can loot in the Hole is the Loam encrusted armour. It's got few nices pieces that can sell for good cash. Of course, I don't think you would get them if you camp the entrance, but you might have a chance at the courtyard.
