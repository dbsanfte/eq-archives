Dread Lands

The Dread Lands zone is another huge high level outdoors zone of the newly discovered Kunark.

The hunting thru lvls 39 - 44  in Dread Lands will be most similar to Lake of ill Omen (lvl 20 to 25) and to the Overthere (lvl 34 to 39) expiriences : One certain spot where you should have you pet to guard, and a pulling area where you will pick up blue conning mobs from. Also, same Darkness - Fear technique applies here. As you see, you will not face anything that you havent already mastered  : )

In Dread Lands, there are two hot spots where you can hunt fairly safe. Such areas are marked on the map with a blue coloured square ( see map of Dread Lands). The red arrows mean that you should pull the mobs towards one very spot (where your pet faithfully waits for you). 

Either the snowy area and the Karnor castle whereabouts should both be fine for your needs. The icy area will most likely be absolutely deserted and uncamped, so there is plenty of preys around there. So dont be fool and dont combat and fear the mobs as soon as you spot them. Drive them up the hill so no other monster will jump on you while fighting another, ok?.

In the other hand, the Karnor castle s backgrounds are indeed camped by players in their 40s. If you preffer to team up, you will easily find a gang there, but if you would rather stick to your own and you get into problems you can always  /yell out and somebody will help you promptly. Besides, SOWs and clarities are very easy to get here, and that is another bonus to count with, aint it?

So thats it. If you hunted in the Overthere zone few levels ago and you enjoyed it, then Dread Lands should be your next destiantion.
