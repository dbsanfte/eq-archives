BurningWood

This is anothere large high level outdoors zone in Kunark.

There are several creatures you could hunt in your high 40s here, but I would advice you to go to the Forest Giants hut (check map for the location) since this zone is heavily forested and hilly making visibility indeed limited, being the Giants outpost just a bit more open and hence less risky.

These giants are similar to the ones you were hunting in Frontier Mountains at your early 30s, just far tougher. There are four kinds of Giants in BurningWood: Arbor (lvl 30-35), Verdant (lvl35-40), Ancient (lvl 40-46) and the named Giant (lvl 44-47). The first two are easy to solo and I dont think your spells will get much resisted. The ancient may also be taken down by a high 40s necro, but it will burn most of your mana bar and might not be worthy. The named one is a bad ass : do not attempt to solo him because he has got loads of HP and max his blows up to 139 !!

Also, few dragon-alike wurms, hornets and undead apes are roaming up and down thru the zone. I soloed my first wurm at level 47, and I was sooo close to kiss the dirt that my blood still chills when I remember that fight. They are not actually very dificult, nor they got high Magic Resistance, but they are well over 5000/6000 HP, and you can easily run out of mana in the middle of the combat. My advice it to leave alone both apes and wurms. Also, you will find some sarnaks and high level skelletons you can pull too, but ensure before pulling away that there is no other mob by, specially the apes since they tend to spawn within the same area.

At Giants, everything is reasonably safe. You will be pulling the giants off the gate or even from inside the hut and deal with them few hundred feet away (check red coloured squares in the map). As long as you stick to Arbors and Verdants, or Ancients once you have hit lvl 49, you will be fine. Of course if you decide to group up, pull everything around ( wurms, ancients and apes included ).

The loot is great here. They oftenly drop items worth up to 50 pp at vendors (axes, tunics, swords, gems, etc.). Only concern is that the closest vendor is in The Overthere zone, and their prices sucks. My tip is : Once you have looted several of these items and you are getting badly encumbered, ask to any wizard or druid hunting at that moment at Giants to TP you to Commonlands or Oasis where you will definately get much more money from the vendors. If you spend few levels hunting here, you will be few thousand plats richer, and thats always nice, isnt it?
