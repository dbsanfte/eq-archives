Karnor's Castle

Karnor's Castle -KC from now on- is settled in the eastern part of the vast DreadLands (check DreadLands map if needed).

KC is, toghether with Old Sebilis, the most popular post 50 dungeon in game, specially for player in the low 50s range. Once you hit 56 or higher, some of its inhabitants (lower level dogs and some undeads) will turn green so either you venture deeper inside the dungeon or you might want to move your headquarters to Sebilis.

So, what about KC? This dungeon is one of the wildest zones around, with plenty of furious doggies traing the hell outta people. In a way, this place reminds me of BlackBurrow : outdoors dungeon, similar canine mobs and a central train station  : )  And the loot? well, being honest there are not many necro orientated items in this dungeon. Most of 'em are melee gear (Jade Mace, Cobalt, Mrylokar, Blood Ember and Deepwater armour pieces, Noctavigant blade...)  and wis casters gear (Donals, Jaudiced bone and Elder Spirit armour pieces, Springwood club...). The only necro armour set is the Flyed Skin armour, but you probably have better stuff by the time you can hunt here.

Anyway, the main reasson to group up in KC is to build up some social faction with your server's high level guys comunity in case you hadnt been grouping much pre 50, like me. Why do I say this? Because post 54 soloing in dead, and you will most likely level up those post 55 levels in Old Sebilis, and if you dont build up a nice reputation with the rest of the players you will have a hard time trying to find group. So my advice is to come here moreless oftenly to group and let yourself pratice your skills in a high level group. That being said, if what you really want is fast exp, go solo in Burning Wood, or at least I'd personally leveled up quite fast in there.

KC follows the same structure like many other dungeons : Find a balanced group, /shout for a camp check, see what rooms are open, and camp them for both exp and loot with the tank pulling the whereabouts. KC has got lots of "rooms" to camp, each of them with a certain type of mobs and loot. You will find this information in the KC map.

Overall, I'd say hunting in KC is pretty safe because SoW and J.Boots can be cast here (remember, it's an open dungeon), so basically if you see yourself in deep poo, just hit the HarmShield hotkey (check spells list if needed) and run to zone as fast as your SoW'd feet alow you. When I say that KC is safe, I mean that you are not supossed to die here as oftenly as you would in, say, Lguk or Sebilis where the exit is miles away and without any SoW or J.Boots. But in no way in hell I'm qualifying this zone as cake : trains are nasty here, and you and your party will probably have to zone over to DreadLands ( " /shout Train to zone !! " hotkey applies here ) quite oftenly.

Sometimes, if you are bored of grouping or if you can't find any, you might want to try to solo here. Even though it is not exp feasible (you will not get even half exp than in a group), I find indoor soloing extremely funny  : )  You got two ways to solo in KC :

1.  Root - DoT solo (mainly because, as I said, with SoW up is fairly safe as long as you pull your preys to a quite spot). Procedure in the following order : Paralizing Earth, few DoTs, Dooming Darkness (for safety reassons), renew PE, sit down and lich. Reapply spells as needed. 

2.  Undead charming solo : Charm an undead ( Thrall of bones spell, check spells list if needed) and toss it to another mob. If you see that your new pet is winning the battle, don't do anything apart from rooting the other mob and renewing the charm every while (our charm spells dont last long, or at least in my personal experience). If, alternatively, our pet is dropping fast, land one or two fast DoTs on the mob. The point of this technique is to let your charmed pet kill the mob while leaving him very hurt himself. Now, you just FD (to brake charm), stand up, root and land Splurt. That should be enough to kill him. Basically, you got two kills with very little mana. You can find plenty of undeads to charm in both courtyards and upstairs in the towers -see KC map to locate it-.
