The Ocean of Tears

The OoT is the see between Antonica and Faydwer, and the boat can be taken both at East Freeport docks or at Butcher Block Mountains. This time, you will be headed towards the seafury cyclops isle (see map if needed).

The seafury cyclops are lvl 40ish and they are somewhere around 5000 HP, but they hit amazinly gently : )  They max their blows for 76, which is what you were used to 10 levels ago !! Only concern here is a 45ish named cyclop, Quag, who hits for 130. So, dont mess with him and hunting here will be a piece of cake.

Since the isle is quite small and quite packed with plenty of clops, I would tell you to get into a non-full group, maybe 2 or 3 of you will be enough. Darkness-Fear gets a bit train-risky because there is not much space to perform your deathly combo. Best guys to team up with are ubdoubtly mages with their root-casting earth pet. So grab couple of friends an sail off to this island. I am sure exp will boost upwards. If you cannnot find any mage up to hunt seafuries, shop around for root casters such a wizzies and druids (and yourself among them). Root is very important because Fear gets way too risky here, so actually root-dot is the only feasible technique here. You can try to the druid kitting along the sea side, but I personally believe that root-dot is better. And always pull towards the beach, or I assure you that a second clop will join in.

What about the loot? Up to 6 pp in cash and also some jewels that you can sell to vendors for good money. 

Most important of all is that after Kunark expansion was released, high level players have fleed over there, hence leaving this lovely area mostly uncamped. This means that if you are in a well balanced group with an experienced puller, you will never lack of preys to pull.
