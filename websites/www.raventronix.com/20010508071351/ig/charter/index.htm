Our Charter

1) IG is open to all Classes, Races, and Levels

2) The Guild Leader and Vice Guild Leader will have final say on all guild matters.

3) Becoming an officer is a reward. Only Guild members who are 25 lvl may become new Officers. (NOTE. I’m aware that some of our current officers do not meet this level requirement. They will still be officers. The new level Requirement only affects new Officers.)

4) All members must make a HOTKEY for trains. Here is an example.

5) All members are to follow the "Play Nice Policy" set forth by Verent. We will not allow KS’ing, Stealing, Begging, or any type of personal attacks.

6) All members must help out other Guild Mates in need. I.E. If you are close to someone that needs help, then help them. This doesn’t mean that you most leave a group, or give up a camp. If you are free and can help then help. Now there are a few no-brainers to this one. If you are in Halas, and you want a SoW or other buffs, and the only guild person who can cast them is in say Freeport, don’t ask them to come up there and buff you.

7) This Charter is not carved in stone and can / will be updated as needed.
