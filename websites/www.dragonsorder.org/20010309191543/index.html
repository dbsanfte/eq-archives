February 7th, 2001 - Veny

    Thank you to all of you who were able to show up for the guild meeting this monday. I apologize for the length of the meeting, but I was very happy to see everyone express their true feelings about the guild.

    Bele has is leading three raids this month, so please show him your support by RSVPing and attending. I will be sending out emails shortly to those of you who signed up for the buddy system raids. I posted the assigned raids on the event board but havent gotten any event submissions so an email might promt some of you = ) Remember if you dont know much about the raid you were assigned, it is your job to research it. Start at Alakhazam's and then try some of the other links under our "Misc Links" section. If you are still in need of help ask some guild members, but remember this is a raid that you need to lead, not them.

    We have a few guild events in the works, still not sure when each of them will be: Tanag is planning a scavenger hunt is to incorporate players from out side the guild so that we can make new friends and get the guild name well known; Aranel is planning a beer bash to improve moral; and Thaywulf is planning a Trade Skills Fair.


February 1st, 2001 - Veny

    I am sorry to say that we have recently lost some members of the guild. But good-bye and good luck to each of them: Riffraffs, Woolf, and Steemer. Please come and visit us often.

    On the up side we have a couple great new members that just joined the guild! Welcome to Damballa/Renic as well as Thaywulf. I am glad you guys could join us and I hope you feel right at home.

    Our guild lottery from last month was won by Steemer. Congratulations! He has asked that the money be given to Pedlz, so congrats to her too! The total sum raised for the lottery was 340pp.

    This month's guild event seems like it will be a scavenger hunt planned by Tanag! The goal is to incorporate players from out side the guild so that we can make new friends and get the guild name well known.

    I dont mind taking suggestions for next month's guild event a bit early. Remember we have these once a month and they are designed for all levels. It is an oppurtunity for us to just have fun together not for items and loot.

    The new event color (grey) is for canceled events. Please re-submit an event listing if you want to reschedule the raid. Thank you.


January 15th, 2001 - Veny

    There was a member list update today... I finally moved the council and officers into the correct positions!! I also removed a few members that we lost over the weekend: Bthin, Ardor, Gealayenea, and Fingurina. Sorry to see them go, but I wish the best of luck to them.

    I also added a few more events to the calendar. The new pinkish color is for "item camps". Take a look at the event and you will see what I mean. The new greenish color is for regular/montly events.

    This months guild event is going to be a lottery. For 5pp you will get a number, or 10pp two numbers, etc. You must purchase all your numbers by by the 30th. On the 31st I will /random 1 30(i.e. the total of numbers bought) and if your number comes up, you win! I am strongly encouraging that guild members buy at least one ticket, just cause it will be fun for everyone. = )

    I am looking for volunteers for nexts months guild event. Please keep in mind this is not a raid, but an event that all levels can participate in. Feel free to email me if you have any ideas and want to show the guild a fun time.

    I am planning a buddy system, where you plan a raid for me, I plan a raid for you sort of thing. If you wish to participate please read the events board for more information.
