Introduction
Mozaic Fawnheart became a brewer for the simple reason that she is the kind of druid who forages every time the button pops. So she quickly had a bag (or two) full of berries, vegetables, and fruits. Brewing is a great way to liven up a guild meeting, but it is also needed to make tempers for Shaman Totemic Armor and Cultural Armor. This guide is meant to be helpful for the starting brewer, but is by no means the definitive discussion on the skill.

Brewing is one of the easiest trade skills to become a master at; it is not level, class, or race dependent. Begin by training some skill points into brewing. You can start with as little as one point, but I would recomend the full 20 if you have points to spare. Brewing is fairly inexpensive so saving practice points and doing it yourself wont hurt your bank account too much.

Many of the items used in brewing can be foraged. Casks, bottles, and grapes can generally be found in a tavern. (Note: The Gnoll Cask found on the Gnoll Brewer in Blackburrow cannot be used for brewing.) Malt, barley, and yeast are generally harder to locate. To brew something, place your ingredients in a Still and click "combine". If it works you will get the drink; if you fail that's ok, failing can increase your brewing skill too.

There are books in game such as the "Book of Limericks" or the book of formulas that is obtained through a quest from the Seafarer's Roost bartender in Freeport. However, their recipes are somewhat hard to decipher, so I will try to list all the formulas here. I also recommend the website: http://everquest.allakhazam.com/Skills/Brewingr.htm

Mozaic currently has a skill of 135 and plans on working with some Tempers soon. She also offers her skill to anyone who is need of a good drink. Any questions or comments, please e-mail her at: zhaan27@bellsouth.net
