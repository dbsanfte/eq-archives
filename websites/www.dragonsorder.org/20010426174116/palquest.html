The Shining Star of Light
Given by: Astaed Wemorcan - Qeynos, near path leading to the Temple of Life
What you need to do: Bring 3 Honeycombs and 5gp to Kizzie Mintiopp in Rivervale. Return the bottle she gives you to Amstaed. Take the Healing Potion Amstaed gives you to Lampeck Hargrim, a farmer in West Karanas. Give the rusty scythe from Lampeck Hargrim to Amstead.
The Brilliant Sword of Faith
Given by: Valeron Dushire - North Freeport, Hall of Truth
What you need to do: Kill Sir Lucan (he is found near the Freeport Militia House) and return his Testimony to Valeron.
Knight of Truth - Testimony
Given by: Merkos Quentos - North Freeport, Hall of Truth
What you need to do: Take the note he gives you to Altunic Jartin in East Commonlands (hut across from the 3rd Inn just before the West Commonlands zone). Kill the rogue, Narls (lvl 6ish), who is found standing outside the house. Give Narls head to Altunic, and he requires a Cloth Shirt (this can be purchased frm several merchants). Give Altunic the Cloth Shirt, and he gives you the "Token of Generousity". Tell Merkos "I have earned the token of generosity" (notice the spelling, it is VERY important). Merkos then requires a spider venom sac. When you give him the spider venom sac, he will then give you the "Token of Bravery". Give Merkos the tokens one at a time. After the second token, you must go to the sewer and defeat Guard Willia (lvl 12ish). You must move very quickly to the sewer, where the canoe is, and to the left is the Guard. Take the "Token of Truth" from the guards corpse and return it to Merkos.
Zimel's Blade - The Soulfire
Given by: Kalatrina Plossen - North Freeport, Hall of Truth
What you need to do: Take the note she gives you to the spy in the middle of the Freeport Militia House in the Courtyard on the first floor. When you hand him the note, the Militia swarm to the spy and begin attacking him! He hands you an old piece of burned parchment and yells to return to the Hall of Truth. DO NOT give Kalatrina Plossen the Note, she sends you to Rashinda Elore at the Office of the People. Rashinda sends you to Groflah Steadirt of Groflah's Forge. Hand Groflah the Note you received from the Spy, he gives you the note back and askes you to meet him later. Meet Groflah at the Seafarer's Roost between 1am and 3am. Ask who Zimel is, and hand Groflah the Note and he attempt to decipher it. Groflah tells you that Sir Lucan D'Lere and Pietro Zarn are searching for Soulfire as well. While still in the Seafarer's Roost, ask Tykar for information about Zimel. You will need to buy him a Drom's Champagne four times total. Tykar informs you that Zimel has been thrown into Prison by the Militia near the Arena. Zimel will require some items: Bog Juice, Edible Goo, and a Blanket. Bog Juice can be found froma Brewer, Edible Goo can be found in Bakeries, and Tykar gave you the Blanket. When you give these items to Zimel, he will give you a key marked H.K.102. Visit the bank in Highhold Pass and hand the Bank Clerk the key. She will give you a Note to deliver to Brother Hayle. You find Brother Hayle in Splitpaw and hand him the Note. Brother Hayle hands you the Note once again and asks for the Hilt of Soulfire. The Hilt is on Xicotl in Mistmoore Castle. Bring Brother Hayle the Hilt, the Brilliant Sword of Faith and your Testimony. Once you return thses item with a "warmly" faction, Brother Hayle will thank you and grant you the Soulfire!
Armor of Ro
    All Ro Armor quests begin at the Paladin camp in the Rathe Mountains. In additon to the quested Molds listed below, you need Ronium to make the armor. Ronium can be made by any smith with a skill of 20 or higher in a Cauldron of Ro (Given by Lord Seafire in Temple of Solusek if you have a faction of "kindly") and requires these ingredients:
2 Bars of Enchanted Platinum
1 Soil of Underfoot (Lore Item- Turn in 4 Fairy Dust in Kaladim)
1 Sands of Ro (South Ro from any Sand Giant)
1 Mistmoore Granite (No Drop- Gargoyles in Castle Mistmoore)
1 Melatite (Model Clockwork mobs in Solusek A)
    Place all the ingredents inside the cauldron. Make sure you place the enchanted plat bars in the cauldron sperately, so all 6 slots are full. Combine, and with a little luck you will have a bar of Ronium.

    Take the bar of Ronium and the Mold for one of the armor pieces to a forge. Place the mold and the Ronium in the forge and combine them. This will produce the end product: your new armor!
