Allows anyone to sew fine leather items from hides and pelts. Making your own armor can produce better and cheaper armor than can be found in the stores of Norrath. Examples are Raw hide Tunics, Backpacks, and a Tattered Belt.

Requires: Tailoring Kit, Patterns and Pelts

Summary

Tailoring is the skill of making items out cloth and leather type materials. Tailoring is one of the more popular trade skills because of it's simplicity. Unlike the blacksmithing skill it does not require a vast amount of knowledge in order to make leather armor out of pelts. On the other hand the pelts are rather difficult to get because they can not be bought, they must be hunted, and this can be time consuming.


Tools of the Trade

Small Tailoring Kit - The tailor kit is a container in which you place your materials. Whenever you are attempting to create something put your materials in this container and hit the combine button. After hitting combine you will either succeed or fail. If you have succeeded the new item will appear under your cursor for you to place into your inventory.

Large Tailoring Kit - Same as the small tailoring kit however the small kit only allows you to make patchwork armor while the large kit will allow you to make studded and reinforced leather armors.

Patterns - In order to make anything you must have a pattern first. If you are making a tunic you must have a tunic pattern, etc. Size doesn't matter here, the size of the item is based on the type of pelt used.

Pelts - In order to make your items you will need pelts, and lots of them. They go pretty fast either from failures or from sales. There are always plenty of newbies around that need patchwork armor so you should probably start by gathering as many ruined wolf pelts as you can.

Studs - Studs are used in the construction of studded leather armor and may be obtained from a blacksmith or a shop that sells them.

Steel Boning - Steel Boning is used in the construction of reinforced leather armor and may be obtained from a blacksmith or a shop that sells it.


Item Information

Pelt Sizes List:

Small Sizes - Typically smaller pelts make smaller sized armor. The only small sized pelts that I have found that will make small sized armor is the wolf pelts.

Medium Sizes - Medium sized pelts will make medium sized armors. Medium sized pelts include cat pelts.

Large Sizes - Larger pelts will make larger armor. Known large pelts include bear pelts.

Pelt Quality List:

Ruined Pelts - Patchwork Armor

Medium Quality Pelts - Studded Leather Armor

High Quality Pelts - Reinforced Leather Armor
