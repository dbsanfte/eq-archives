• New applicants must read and agree to the Guild Charter and following rules prior to joining the guild.

  • New members will not be brought in to the Order until they have reached a ranking of the 15th level.

  • ALL new members will join the guild as a probationary member for two weeks. After that time, if both the applicant and guild is satisfied, the applicant will considered a member, with all privileges there of.

  • In addition to participating in the probationary period mentioned above, unknown applicants must group with guild members of their level a minimum of 5 times prior to guild invite.

  • Applicants who have the endorsement of an existing member, will be invited if the supporting member can provide sufficent history on the person in question.

  • Existing members may only endorse new applicants after they have been with the guild for a minimum of two months. Supporting members must also be in good standing in order to endorse an applicant.

  • A majority council vote will determine guild invites, warnings, removals, and termination of probationary periods. All council votes are equally wieghted.

  • It is recomended that everyone attend a guild event prior to guild invite to meet all of the existing members and experience.
