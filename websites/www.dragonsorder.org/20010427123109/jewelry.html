Jewel Craft allows one to fashion jewelry items by combining gems and precious metals. To begin you will need a Jewelers Kit and "The Legacy of Jewel Craft" manual. It is also helpful if you are an Enchanter or know someone who will enchant metals (silver, electrum, gold or platinum) for you. The enchantment spells are located in the Library of Neriak.

     As you start to combine gems and metals, your skill will rise. I recomend training as many points into Jewel Craft as possible before attempting your first combination. Each attempt is costly so starting with a higher skill will save you money. You will be able to increase your skill without enchanting the metals, however you cant make much money this way since the items will not be magical. Merchants will buy these items from you at a slightly lower price than it cost you to make them though.

     Having a high intelligence will increase your skill faster, high wisdom will reduce failures, and charisma helps reduce the cost of components. The components can be found at vendors and/or may drop off NPC's. Start with the cheapest metal and gem combination until it is trivial, then move on to the next.
