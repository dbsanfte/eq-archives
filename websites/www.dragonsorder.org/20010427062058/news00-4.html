September 16th, 2000 - Veny

    Well, it is official. Torlon is back in command! The available council members and officers had a long meeting last night and made an unanimous vote to bring Torlon back in the guild... since the vote was unanimous not even the guild master could turn it down = )

    Another issue addressed in this meeting was our charter. We (the council and officers) are working on a new one, but we really want to try to bring in more member participation. This is why we are also creating a new message board for feedback, this is where council members can post a topic (something that we have already discussed and want to make a change to) and everyone can reply with their opinions. The first topic will be the new charter, which will also include some cardinal and loot rules that didn’t exist before. Please make any suggestin on this board once the revised version is posted.

    Topic number three... Many thanks to Tamerlane who is putting in a lot of thought to the guild event on Saturday the 23rd. It will be a great opportunity to have fun and see everyone. It is encouraged to bring friends, family, cows, chickens, strangers, etc. but the council is going to continue with the temporary halt on inviting new members. We know there are problems that need to be resolved with in the guild right now and that has to happen first.

    Yet another subject discussed, is that of the guilds size in proportion to the council. If you haven’t noticed, we have grown – a lot. This is why we will be increasing the number of people holding officer positions. There have been a few people nominated for the positions and we are just checking with council members who were not present last night before revealing them. We are trying to optimize the council and officers to best reflect the guild. We also reviewed the responsibilities that both council and officers should maintain, and we will ask that everyone agree to them before resuming (or starting) their position.
