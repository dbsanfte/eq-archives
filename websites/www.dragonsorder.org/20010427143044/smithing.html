Smithing is the ability to shape ore into valuable metal items. The items crafted by a blacksmith are of better weight and utility than those sold in stores. To begin you will need Ore and a Forge.

Train all 20 practice points into blacksmithing, and start to gather as many rusty weapons as you can. Sharpen Sharpen Sharpen. 2H swords will take you all the way to 30 skill. At Skill Level 28-30, Purchase as many small pieces of ore as you can and convert all of them into metal bits. Buy Scaler Molds outside of Kalidim and follow the directions in your smithing book to make Scalers until you reach 40 skill. After 40, move onto large lanterns until it is trivial. You will have a decent success rate at lanterns, and it's the first item you can make as a smith that is actually wanted by players! Selling them back to shops will gain you a bit over a gold piece per lantern, but players will buy them for up to 3 gold pieces in places like Freeport, or anywhere races without infravision live. Lanterns should take you well over 55 skill, and if you've got the extra money to purchase some metal sheets you'll notice that you can start making banded gorgets around 55 skill with about a 60% failure rate. Other items of banded, will take much more skill to be successful at a decent rate, and you're going to saturate the market with banded gorgets here, so travel, sell what you have made, and have patience. Tunics, Pants, Arms, Boots and things of that nature are going to take around 100+ skill to make, so I wouldnt be wasting money on them yet.
