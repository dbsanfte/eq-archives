    Regarding the GM incident in Kedge Keep last Saturday, Abashi and Lokkison issued the following statements...

Abashi:
Regardless of the events that lead up to the incident, our customers always deserve courtesy and respect. They weren't given that in this particular case, and we'll continue to work at providing better customer service for our players.

Lokkison did want to say a few words:
I would like to take this opportunity to address the situation that arose on the Xegony server last weekend (2-12-00) in Kedge Keep. This incident has caused many debates over the roles of GMs and Guides as CS representatives.
While both sides have their points, ultimately it is my responsibility to treat each and every customer with respect and dignity regardless of their treatment of other CS personnel or myself. We are responsible for maintaining the playability and stability of the game as well as good customer relations. The last of these is the point that I have failed on and for that I apologize to the players involved. I am not going to offer up any excuses or make any statements about what happened and why. The bottom line in all of this is that a group of people’s enjoyment of the game was lessened by an interaction with me, and that is unacceptable. I will consider this an experience for me to grow from and I hope the players do as well.
Our main goal here at Verant is to provide the players with a gaming environment that we hope they will enjoy, sometimes things we have to do to maintain this game upset people. I assure each and every one of you that not one person working here at Verant would do something that intentionally detracts from your enjoyment of the game.

Thank you,
Lokkison
Night Lead-GM/Server Monitor
Verant Interactive

February 12, 2000 - Veny

    This is a post that can be found on EQ vault reguarding my experience with one of the Xegony GM's:

    In the early hours of February the 12th, my group experienced perhaps the most disgusting and distasteful use of GM power I have ever witnessed. We were insulted and belittled by the very people that are supposed to be dedicated to helping us.
    As the Brotherhood of Norrath guild started their invasion into Kedge Keep, our group (consisting of myself - Veny Tier'Gristal, my husband - Apenance Tier'Gristal, and two of our good friends - Serieux and Wolfen) had been fighting and maintaining the spawn on the second level for over two hours. As BoN got closer to defeating Phingel, they realized and petitioned that there were some spawns that were stuck in the depths of Kedge. GM Lokkison, Tarskok, and Nabben came to analyze the area. When they came to a conclusion to re-pop the entire zone, neither the GM's nor BoN had taken in to consideration how this might affect the people who had been fighting there all night. As we all know, a zone or server re-pop can be very devastating. Anyone fighting high level mobs has to struggle to work their way into the dungeon and secure a spot to pull to. As well as spread out the spawns so one can safely pull to their party. This can take hours. This is what our group would now have to attempt again if the whole zone was to re-spawn. As you might expect, we contested the zone re-pop. Yet we did so in a rational way despite our anger for being overlooked. We requested that, since Lokkison had fixed BoN's problem by creating one for us, perhaps he could TP us out of the zone for re-pop and help us break the spawn once again so we could continue to play. According to GM Lokkison, we were "clearly ignorant as to what was going on in the zone" and needed to keep our "uninformed opinions to ourselves". Apparently this type of request is unacceptable, and he "does not take crap from whiney little players like you who are selfish and ignorant and cry when they don't get their way."
    Yes, that was a direct quote from a GM on Xegony. GM Lokkison resorting to immature name calling of the players he has vowed to assist.
    After being verbally abused, we made sure to log, report, and take screenshots of the incident, which we considered unfit GM conduct. We also stated to Lokkison that we would be doing so. At this point, for no apparent reason other than to perhaps show his 'power' over us, he told Apenance to "take a break, fool" and disconnected him from the server. "Anyone else wanted to play?" he sarcastically asks.
    Shortly after Apenance returned to the server, we were summoned into a 'safe room', in which we could not move or access any of our belongings. In addition to this, Apenance was 'silenced' the entire time. Lokkison took this time to explain to us that he doesn't have to explain his actions to anyone and that we were "wasting his very valuable time" because he had to "explain this to a group of immature individuals". He told us that we, the players of Everquest who put in over 200 hours a month of time into exploring dungeons, slaying beasts, and tactically maneuvering to a camp, "have no idea how this game works and why {a GM} would need to do something."
    We were eventually dropped back into Dragnor's Cauldron and forced to leave Kedge Keep for the night.
    This entire event was completely unacceptable. After any comment we are either ridiculed or disconnected from the server. I fail to see any of the justice, which GM's are supposed to provide, in Lokkison's tactic. I think all players on Xegony as well as other servers should be aware of the GM's that watch over them. I consider GM Lokkison to be unjust, short tempered, and certainly not fit to be a GM in Everquest. I also would like to see appropriate action taken against Lokkison for his actions against us. I will not tolerate paying to play a game to be harassed by the staff.
    For a full log of this incident, visit this page. I have posted Apenance's log from that night as well as added personal tells and such from the other players involved.
    I know we have all had bad experiences with GM's but personally I cant stand back and let such blatent abuse of power go on. Please post feedback and other experiences you have had perhaps with this GM or others on this thread (Abusive use of GM power) at the EQ Vault.
