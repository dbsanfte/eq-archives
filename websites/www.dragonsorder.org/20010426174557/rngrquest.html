Short Sword of Morin

     Hail Morin Shadowbane in the Kithicor Forest. She will tell you that her rangers cant fight off all the attacks and that they are underequiped. She says that you can either get her a Ebony Blade to help or kill a Ghoul Messenger and give her the secret documentations. The ghoul travels between Kithicor and the ghoul camp in South Karana. Once you return one of these items to her, she gives you her personal sword: the Short Sword of Morin.
Cloak of the Jagged Pine

     Larsk Juton wants you to kill a gnoll by the name of Gnasher Furgutt, he is about lvl 10 and can be found in Qeynos Hills. After you return Gnasher's head to guild leader Lark Juton, he needs your services in exterminatiing gnoll brewers. Return three Black Burrow Casks to him and he will rewarded you with the Cloak of the Jaggedpine, a Ranger and Druid only cloak, Magic Item, Lore Item, No Drop, 6 AC +3 DEX 1.5wt. The casks are found on "a gnoll master brewer" and on "a gnoll brewer" which are rare spawns at the end of the bottom bridge by the barrels in Black Burrow.
