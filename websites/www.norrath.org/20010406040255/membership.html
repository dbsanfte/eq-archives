Admission Policy

MEMBERSHIP FREEZE. For now no new voting threads are being allowed for the for seeable future. There are a lot of great people we would love to see in the guild still but because of raid considerations (we never turn away a member) the freeze will stay in place for now. 

Old admission policy:   

 Talisman is primarily a high level guild with a tendency to large, epic raids against the so called uber mobs of Norrath on the Tunare server. Talisman does not have requirements per se since everything is based around a voting system. In practice though this means that people not 51+ will be unable to group with and meet the people necessary to reach the required number of votes. To start the process any full member of Talisman can sponsor you for voting. At this point you have two weeks (with extensions available at the officer's discretion) to obtain the necessary votes.
    Currently you must reach 45 yes votes with zero no votes, with the voting cap periodically adjusted by the officers to help keep the guild at a reasonable membership volume. A major tenant of Talisman is that we never turn away people in the guild from a raid unless they are too late and we are already charging. To continue this policy we must carefully monitor the size of the guild, too many people just equates to everybody going LD.
    Votes are strictly confidential and while we try and work out no votes, sometimes an arrangement can not be made and the applicant is denied membership with no real means to address the situation. Once 45 votes is reached the member is permanently guilded and given access to the guild board, vault, and voting forums.
    The voting system insures that the majority of the members know each other and feel very comfortable with each other. We do not guild people just because they are level 60 and a needed class, you must also be an outstanding member of the Tunare community to get the required votes. The converse applies too, we frequently guild classes and level's that offer no real value to our raiding expeditions because we already have almost a dozen of higher level. With that said popular classes and higher levels will likely get you more votes than not, but a fun spirit and a good attitude will earn you more.


        Any questions about the process can be directed in game to the guild leader and officers or via email.
