Tunare's words beckoned me...

Posted by Mandon Ashenwood at 24.237.17.0 on August 11, 1999 at 13:36:18:

I was lying in bed one eve, and a beautiful voice chimed inside my mind. I knew at once it was my goddess, our Mother. She questioned my will to depart the family that I've been a part of for so many years. I did not have an answer for her.

I knew, by my inability to answer, that I had erred. And I spoke with our King the very next morning. I asked him to grant me a place in the family once again, for the final time, and in the generous way he has, he granted my request.
I have missed you all, those that I have known, and those that I haven't. Those that I did not have the pleasure to meet before I departed have shown me such generosity and kindship, so much, that I will never have to leave. Most prominent on that list is Aakar. That little Dwarf has the BIGGEST heart. I came back to this world nearly as naked as the day I was born, and was trying to get some gear together. Aakar gave me something that I KNOW isn't cheap, and it will be something I will use for quite some time, a Combine Longsword.
I was taken aback with his generosity. Last night, I had the pleasure of teaming with him. What a sturdy companion he is.. *grins* I am glad to see that there are several members in my level range now. When I left, I was in between several people. I would like to get a group together and kill some disgusting Orcs or Undead. I am here for the long haul, ladies and gentlemen. Count on it.

My eternal thanks for all of your warm welcome's, even those of you whom I've never had the pleasure to meet.

Regards,

Mandon Ashenwood
-As Korfallen would put it "The Nature Boy"
