(Here is a list of locations in the Star Wars Galaxies universe. Here again, this page will be added to after the game goes "gold")

Corellia

Corellia is home of the Rebel Alliance with forested land that also has swamps and gold beaches. Sounds like some interesting intrigues will be playing out on this planet. Also, for those of you interested in selling your wares, this is a great place to establish your business.

CITIES

  • Coronet, starting city.
     
  • Tyrena, starting city.
     

Dathomir

This is a dangerous planet with two warring factions and an extremely dangerous land to navigate due to the steep mountan paths and dense jumgles. You will need to choose a faction and will be able to trade and visit witht he faction you choose. You may choose to align with Nightsisters, clans of witches, or Singing Mountain Clan. Rebels beware, there is an Imperial prison on this difficult planet.

The land consists of canyons, mountains and is forested. You can arrange for transportation by special charter transports to this planet.

CITIES

  • Unknown at this time

Endor

Part of the "Endor System", this planet is covered in forests with lots of natural resources for the player interested in Tradeskills. Endor is home to the Ewoks and you will find their villages here admists the forest treetops.

While Endor is a wonderful place to visit, trade with the inhabitants and explore, it is a wilderness that can not be covered with settlements or be anything other than what it is, a great place to visit and explore.

CITIES

  • Unknown at this time

Lok

Pirates and criminals call this planet home, not that anyone else would want to. The planet is terrain is basically dead with lava pits, sulfur pools, dangerous mountains and deserts marking the terrain.

Pirates use this planet as their home base to plan attacks in the Mid-Rim. Great place to do missions. Lok is part of the Karthakk System.

CITIES

  • Unknown at this time.

Naboo

Humans populate this planet that the Emperior has dubbed his vacation playground. The Naboo, peace loving humans, and the warlike Gungans, have a history or fighting and cooperating with each other when threatened by the Trade Federation invasion.

Naboo offers a variety of terrains including forests, swamps, grasslands and rolling hills and is in the Naboo planetary system.

CITIES

  • Moenia, starting city
     
  • Theed, starting city

Rori

Part of the Naboo system, this moon has mountains, hills and grasslands, yet, they are harsher looking than many of the other planets.

There is a secret city here along with many great adventures on this moon that can sends chills up your spine.

CITIES

  • Unknown at this time.

Talus

Talus is home to the Dearic spaceport and part of the Corellian system. You will traverse fields, forest, seas with mudflats and ravines thrown in. This planet is ruled by The elected Federation of the Double Worlds with regels and Imperials residing here.

CITIES

  • Unknown at this time.

Tatooine

Tatooine is the miners dream planet even if it is a desert planet. Jabba the Hutt resides on this planet and if you maintain good faction with him, you will find considerable quests here. The Empire and Rebels can both be found on Tatooine along with Fort Tusken now being held by the dangerous Sand People, Mos Eisley, Mos Espa and Jabba's Palace.

CITIES

  • Bestine, starting city
     
  • Mos Eisley, starting city
     
  • Mos Espa, starting city

Yavin 4

This moon is part of the Yavin planetary system and covered with jungles, lakes and streams. In order to explore this extremely dangerous planet, you will need to avoid either the Imperials or the Rebels, depending on your faction and the dangerous mobs that roam this planet. The Imperials currently rule Yavin 4 but there are many adventures awaiting both factions. If you manage to explore this planet, you might find the Great Temple.
