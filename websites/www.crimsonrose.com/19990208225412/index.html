Well we knew it would come eventually, just before 11:15pm AST the Phase 3 Beta test officially ended. Though many players logged off when the first warnings began coming , a few of us (291 last I checked) stayed in until ther bitter end. I'm sure I speak for all CR members who were involved in the P3 test by saying thanks the EQ team for a wonderful time and for letting us test their amazing game. See you all in Phase 4!

EQ vs. UO (karsus 10:46am AST)

What's this, EQ Producer Brad McQuaid posting on the UOVault's Message borad? That he did, in response to untrue statements about EQ made by "Gryphon of Great Lakes". Here are the actual messages;

a: I don't have the techincal support for EQ. When your poor like me, you can't afford the MINIMUM SYSTEM REQUIREMENT of a 3d accelerator. Even with a 3d card, it is going to lag like hell.

(Brad)Nope -- a game like this being 3d or 2d has little to nothing to do with lag.

Right now, in beta, you have what...a few hundred people with a graphic engine near quake II quality? Imagine when it releases 1500 per server (the max...to low in my opinion), with 28.8 connections, low rate 3d card, lag everywhere.

(Brad)Nope. During phase 3 of testing we had 1700 people on a server, with all sorts of connections, 3d cards, etc. There was NOT 'lag everywhere'. In fact, people are pretty blown away as to how lag resistant EQ is.

: B: UO is a rpg. EQ is a rpg. UO is faster, original, has more of a rpg aspect (Can we say virtual economy? I haven't heard that word from anyone in EQ).

(Brad) Depends what you mean by a 'virtual economy'. We have a 'player-driven economy' that depends on tried and true principles such as supply and demand. 

Plus i've already devoted enough of my time to this great game so if you want to go and complain about Uo and how EQ is god, go to www.eqvault.com...we don't want your kind here. 

(Brad) Well, I can respect that, however I can't stand by and let misinformation about EQ float around either.

 -Brad

 Looks as though Brad doesn't take kindly to false rumors drifting about that speak poorly of EQ.

Phase 4 CDs are coming (karsus 6:21pm AST)

February 10th will be upon us soon, as I'm sure many of you know. Many people have already received there CDs and others should expect there's soon. The Everquest team sent out the following e-mail yesterday to all the Phase 4 testers...

To: All EverQuest Phase 4 Testers

Hopefully by now you will have received your EverQuest Phase 4 Beta disk in

the mail. At the bottom of this email you will find your Account Key for

Phase 4. Phase 4 will officially begin Wednesday February 10th at 12:00pm

PST. If you have any questions, you can call 1-888-Games02 for help. Please

be advised that the disks have been completely mailed out, so you should

receive your disk soon if you haven't already.

We are really looking forward to seeing you in Norrath!

The EverQuest Team

Apparently most of the CDs were shipped without their account keys on them and this e-mail was sent out to over 25,000 players with their key numbers.
