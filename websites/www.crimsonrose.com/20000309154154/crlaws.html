Laws of the Guild

Below are the core rules of the Crimson Rose which have remained the same for many years. The laws are the basis for all the rules of the guild. An infraction of a guild law is the only type of crime that is taken directly to the Justicar. Punishments for breaking guild laws may include, but are not limited to: Death, Exile, suspension or revocation of guild membership, and excommunication.

Thou shalt strive to uphold truth and justice in all aspects of thy life. Therefore, thou shalt not lie, nor steal, nor cheat. And being so, thou shall not allow such crimes to go unnoticed by bringing them to the attention of appropriate authorities.

 

Thou shalt notcommit murder. Do not engage in unprovoked attacks on anyone. Exceptions may include; enemies due to war as decreed by the High Lord or High Lady, in defense of a guild brother/sister, in defense of an ally of the guild and in defence of the innocent. In addition, thou shalt not engage in combat on the behalf of any other orders unless thy superiors have approved such an action.

 

Thou shalt respect and obey those of higher rank than thyself. Respect also includes making use of the chain of command. Questions, complaints, etc., should be directed towards the officer directly superior to thyself. Remember that High Lord is the highest member of this chain, followed by the law itself. When issued an order, thou shalt obey it without question, unless an officer superior to the one issuing the original order countermands the order. If thou dost believe that an order is in violation of guild laws or rules, thou mayest report it after the order has been carried out.

 

Thou shalt be loyal to the Crimson Rose, and its High Lord, Lord Rithak. Thou shalt behave with honor at all times, reflecting the standards of the crest you wear. Every word thou dost speak, every action thou dost take reflects on the guild. If thou dost bring dishonor to thyself, thou dost bring dishonor to all thy brothers and sisters. To demonstrate thy loyalty, thou art required to wear the appropriate uniform of the realm at all times while in a game, unless directly ordered not to do so by a superior officer. Thy loyalty shall extend to the allies of the Crimson Rose as well. Thy loyalty shall be absolute, even unto the point of laying down thy life for a member or an ally, for surely they will do the same for thee.

Lasted Changed the Thirtyith Day of January, in the year Two Thousand.
