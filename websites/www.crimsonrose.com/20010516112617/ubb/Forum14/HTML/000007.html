For some odd reason when I pay $50 for a game I expect to be able to play it without too much difficulty. I may be old fashioned, unhip or dare I say it down right square, but I think it's the least a multimillion dollar corparation can do for me, the humble consumer whose purchase pays their checks.
Now I realize it's not a perfect world, mishaps do occur. But the blindingly assenine idiocy (yeap I said assenine idiocy, meaning more stupid than the inventor of the word's son from wich he got the idea times 15) of Electronic Arts is beyond comprehension. In a span of 10 days not only have they managed to cancel the most anticipated game of 2001 (wich means it would have been released upon the 10th return of Christ to Earth after the 103rd hell freeze) Ultima Worlds Online upon the basis that it would compete with Ultima Online( a 3 year old game that looks as if it could successfully run on a Gameboy Color)and they would rather improve upon that glorious nugget of excrement (instead of moving into the new milenium), and incredibly have shipped beta cd's of Black and White along with the retail ones. Of course this was all accidental (and Stalin was just trying to cure lice) and not done to meet shipping agreements, I'm sure.

Now zoom into last night, I made the awful 7 mile trek to the hell that is Electronics Boutique and picked up my advanced purchase of Black and White. I sped home (mowing down 3 children and 5 bicyclists in the process) and popped the cd into my computer and immediately installed it. Ok everything is fine, install going well, wow not taking too long. Install is done and I reboot my computer like a good little boy always should and upon startup close down all applications (as the manual suggests) and launch the game. Ok movie crap, logo crap, woohoo the game! The graphics are amazing and the voice acting is extremely well done! When like a bitchslap from the hand of god after having a few too many brewskies the game freezes up. Oh crap, thinks I, what should I do? thinks I. I try to alt-tab out, no luck. I try to ctrl-alt-delete out, again luckless. So I manually restart my computer (The Devils way of doing things) and upon startup close all applications and try again. Logo crap, movie crap, the game! Alas it (like Hitlers conquest of Russia) was not to be... this time the mouse pointer in the game dissapears. So now Im panicking, but I can alt-tab out and close it down with ctrl-alt-delete.

Come to now when after 27 hours of attempting to find a solution I am at wits end and ready to murder anyone whose initals are E. A. If I had nuclear weapons at my disposal Eastern Arkansas, Alaska, Arizona, and Alabama would be no more. In a word... Im pissed. I spent $50 (American, you know... real money) on a bunch of useless code, some carboard, and a shiny coaster.

Oh well, thats what I get for spending my hard earned money buying a product from a multimillion dollar corporation with the intent of bringing some modicum of joy into my otherwise depressing life. Oh well, instead of playing computer games, or having sex I'll do the next best thing... kill people! Or not.
