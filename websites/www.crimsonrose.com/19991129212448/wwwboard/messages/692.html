Gratitude and heartfelt thanks...

Posted by Aamana at 208.167.54.78 on August 10, 1999 at 01:00:33:

My family and I would like to thank each and every one of you for your condolences, support and prayers. I feel as if many of you have carried me in the trying times of her near death beating and stabbing incident and hospitalization, and now this. You epitomize the true concept of family and friends. As some of you know, us girls and boys had horrific and unimaginable lives until Mom found and took in each and every one of us over the years, and then gave us the love, dignity and respect that every child deserves. She asked for nothing, and gave everything, and lived a life second to none, wanting no more than to feel joy every day, by giving love and joy wherever she could.

She survived her own hell as a child. When she turned her life around, she made a committment to assure that when in her power, no child would live with the fear, lonliness and pain she had to. Is she a saint? Heck no! Is she special? Heck yeah!

Your comments and thoughts reflect the far reaching affect of her beauty, grace, wisdom and ability to share her joy. I will miss her for she healed a heart that I thought could never be healed. It's not that I can't live without her... I couldn't have survived without her. I shall carry her grace in my heart forever. Her soul, a part of my soul, and yours. I am eternally grateful for you, my extended family. And I am eternally grateful that she was my Mom! Thank you all!
