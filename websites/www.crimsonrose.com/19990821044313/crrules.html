Rules of the Guild

1. Your attendance at guild meetings is mandatory. Guild meetings are held every Sunday evening at 7:00 PM Pacific Standard Time. If you are unable to attend a meeting, you must notify your superior officer within 24 hours prior to the meeting.

* Guild events and news are shared during this time and it is critical that all members show. Not only will you learn about guild activities but you'll be a part of a great roleplaying experience. Do to security reasons the guild channel and password are given upon membership to the guild.

 

2. It is your responsibility to keep informed of guild activities, events, and other news. You should check the web page as often as possible for new information. Also pay attention to bulletins and newsletters sent to you via e-mail or other means.

* Once again, knowledge of current events in the guild is the most important part of being a good member. The strength of CR is only as strong as it's weakest link.

 

3. If you are changing e-mail address, or ICQ accounts, please be certain to notify your superior officer and your section leader. This is one of the only times you may bypass the chain of command.

* For obvious reasons.

 

4. You must remain active in the guild. If you have not been heard from or seen for a period of 14 days or more, you may be assumed to have perished, and your name will be stricken from the list of members. If you are going to be unavailable for an extended amount of time, you must notify your superiors.

* To be a member one must... be a member. CR wants members who are active and helpful, being absent for half a month is neither.

 

5. Promotions are at the discretion of the section leaders and they're duly appointed assistants. As always, any promotions and appointments to guild offices are subject to approval by the King.

* Members are promoted by actions and skill. Roleplaying and involvement are key points to ones promotion, as well as keeping true to the guilds virtues.

 

6. Occasionally the guild may request your attendance in official actions within the game, such as a war or ceremony. If you are unable to attend you must notify your superior officer as soon as possible.

* Once again for obvious reasons.

 

7. The Crimson Rose must be your only guild. Unless you have been given express permission to do otherwise, you must not maintain any memberships within other guilds that The Crimson Rose is officially a part of. The only person who may give such permission is the King or Queen only, however requests for such permission must be routed through the chain of command.

* Being a member of another guild would lessen your ability to follow the above guidelines.
