Getting Started

Visit our Getting Started section which provides a wealth of information on how to set up your Web site.

Uploading Your Pages

Using the FTP client of your choice, connect to www.crimsonrose.com (or 216.219.195.207 until your domain is active), and login with your username and password. Once logged in, you will be in the document root directory of your Web site - this is where you should place your default page (index.html). If you have any scripts, change to the "cgi-bin" directory and upload them there. If you wish, you may create sub-directories and place documents in them as needed.

How Do I Access the Control Panel?

The ValueWeb Control Panel is at http://control.valueweb.net/control.
Select Site Utilities to:

  • Alter your account settings.
  • Configure your e-mail.
  • View your Web site statistics.
  • Find utilities to aid you with the installation of forms, guestbooks, counters, and more.
  • Access the many additional features available from ValueWeb.

Important Things to Remember

  • Please be careful when modifying any of the existing files in your directory. Deleting or modifying certain files may disable your site.
  • It's recommended that you have at least one current version of your files backed up on your local hard drive or storage device.
  • Secure pages (SSL) must be named https://... (Note the ‘s’).
  • Server Side Include (SSI) documents must have a .shtml extension.
  • E-mail sent to anything@crimsonrose.com will be forwarded to your account. See E-mail Configuration for more information.
  • If you are using MS FrontPage, you may begin publishing to the URL http://www.crimsonrose.com/ once your domain is active. Your FrontPage extensions will be enabled if you made the request during sign up. If not, you may turn them on yourself, in your Control Panel>Site Utilities>Site Information. In Account Options, select FrontPage Extensions ‘On’.
  • This Welcome page will be replaced by your home page when you upload your files. You may access this information at any time at www.yourdomain/stats/welcome.html.
