What is a Shadowrun PBEM (Play By EMail)
The Shadowrun PBEM is an online role-playing game put out by FASA. We have taken it to another level of playing it via email and have been pretty successful in this endeavor. Although PBEM games have been around since the beginning of email, you can find alot of sites out there that give you differant versions of what are called, "House Rules". Here is a link for you to get an idea of what type of game setting this is. FASA is the company that puts this game out.
