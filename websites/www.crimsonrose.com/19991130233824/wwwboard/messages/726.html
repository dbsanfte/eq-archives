Hail brethren of the Crimson Rose, I bring news of my recent travels in the world of Dereth. The world is simply beutifull. Climbing atop an especially high hill (Or mountain in one case) the average adventurer can see for miles on end. Zones are virtually non-existant, the only exceptions being a two-second pause as a village appears just over the horizon. I have yet to see anything that can qualify as an actuall city, however. The Asian city of Shoushi is surrounded on all sides by a thin 3 meter-high wall. Shoushi is definately larger than many other towns in Dereth, but it is still dwarfed when compared to such oddities as Qeynos. Keep in mind, though, that this world is huge, and all attempts to reach the Aluvian cities in the far north have ended in disaster for me. Perhapse greater things can be found there.
The land overall feels much more...real, than that of Norrath. My adventures between the portals have taken me to several extremely different lands. I have been in wide open plains, deserts, forrests, swamps, and even to the scourged Direlands. I have seen massive breathtaking waterfalls, been atop a mountain so high I could not see the lands below. I have fought Shreths, Drudges, Skeletons, Wood Golems, and Black Rabbits. Most amazingly, however, is that the spawning points have spawning points. Drudge camps move, and Drudges only spawn around Drudge camps. When the sun rises, and sets again, the camps have moved to a new location. My Blademaster, Talyena, has been adventuring with an AC guild known as the Red Wizards, lead by Zhentarim. Perhapse if/when a CR presence is established in Dereth an alliance could be negociated with the Red Wizards. Anyone currently in the beta, feel free to add "Dorionious" (Me) to your Zone friends list. Or contact either Dorionius or Talyena in-game.
P.S. OOC-There IS a first person view, but your weapon tends to get in the way of targeting. You'll find yourself changing the camera views often, and the controls are so easy, it becomes second nature almost immediately (If you didnt catch that, this is a good thing).
-Scrivener
