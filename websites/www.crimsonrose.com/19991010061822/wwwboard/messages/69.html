I've run into this problem building computers many times before. Having all IDE drives not show up, and the light not turn off is due to having the IDE cable upside-down on one of the drives. Flip the cable on your CD-ROM or hard drive, and try again.

Whatever you do, just don't get mad at the computer and chuck it out the window, it doesn't make things any better :)
