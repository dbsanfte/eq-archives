Welcome to the Crimson Rose Cantina, where Dantrag is the proprietor of a small bar. He also has his stores of "goodies" around back. We are not a guild per-say. We are just a group of friends banding together for a common good. The Crimson Rose is a drinking establishment with no alligiences to anyone, save a few. This cantina is a place for you or your friends to come, to meet for pleasure or business, as we have several private rooms for which you can go to discuss privately.

So come on in, order a drink, sit back in the main lounge area, and relax. We cater to all......even droids.
