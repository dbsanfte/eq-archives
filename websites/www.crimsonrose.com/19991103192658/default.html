Dearest Members,

As you well know, there are some changes that are going on within our ranks. These changes are not uncommon as we try to better ourlseves as a group. Some accept the change, and some fear it. History has taught us that when people fear something, they tend to destroy it, or at least make the attempt. As such the case here and now.

I have given a list of a few names to the leaders recently to have a few members removed from our ranks. These reasons I have shared with the leadership, so there is no need to go into them here with you now. Suffice to say, the leadership will be available to listen to your complaints and 'arguments' on why you wish to return to us. But there are a few, that i will not allow back in. Under any circumstances.

I fully acknowledge, that I can not please 100% of the people, 100% of the time. So I must please as many as I can whenever possible. So, with this said, there are going to be rumors, hate /tells and the like coming from some people. Most of this is about me. I have dealt withthis before, and I am sure that I will deal with it in the future. I also fully know and accept, that whenever I am not around, the wind or whisperings happen about me, about how I rule this guild, and the like. I also know that this wind can be harsh at times. Believe me when I say, I have a pretty thick skin and that I am a big boy and can handle it.

Many of you know, that I rarely ever respond to 'hate mail', whether it be on the message board, or sent via email. However, this one stricks me odd, and as such, was surprised to read it. I am encouraging a discussion to begin on the message aboard about this. I am open to your thoughts. And believe it or not, I can have my mind changed.

This was a letter sent to me by our own Sir Rogan. Mind you, let me say up front, I am not upset in ANY way towards him whatsoever.

Member Submitted (Scribe 4:50am PST)

Here is something that was sent in for posting...

Hello there. This is the player of Kurgan on Karana. I was wondering if you would mind posting the following message in the news section of the CR site. I think this petition is a good one and stands a good chance of working.

Thanks for your consideration...
Kurgan

Greetings fellow EverQuesters!

I currently represent a large number of players who would very much like to see the addition of a role-play only server. A few players will claim that the server they play on is for role-playing. However, it becomes obvious, after a time, that they are truly all the same. The reality is that the role-players themselves are the minority on any given server. If we had a server that was tagged as "Role-play only," it would give all of us (with this particular style of play) an advertised place where we could fully enjoy our EverQuest experience. There is no question that a role-play only server would be used to its full extent since it would draw from this minority rather than have us spread out over all of the other servers. That is essentially what we would like to prove to the
staff of EverQuest with this petition.

However, this petition is not just for role-players. The Hack 'n Slash style players would also benefit from the addition of a role-play only server. The most obvious benefit would be the virtual elimination of arguments and resentments between the players with these drastically differing styles. I am referring to many situations that include, misuse of OOC, Shout and Auction commands, begging people for "buffs," open discussion of inappropriate topics (i.e., that aren't genre specific), et cetera. A role-play only server may also eventually lead to a reduction of strictness or necessity concerning some of the controversial "Play Nicely" rules.

If, in fact, the goal of the EverQuest staff is to ensure an enjoyable gaming environment for all of the players, a role-play only server would seem like a very logical step toward accomplishing that goal. If you are in agreement with us or you simply feel that this suggestion is reasonable, please email us at EQPetition@aol.com and let us know about it. We will forward all of the responses (along with this petition) to the EverQuest staff in the hopes that they too will see the logic behind our request.

Thank you all for your consideration.
- Sky

 

Patch Update (Scribe 9:54am PST)

Not much with this patch - heh

November 3rd, 7:00am
--------------------

Listed below are the enhancements introduced in this patch.

*Item Enhancements*
The entire set of the Armor or Ro (Paladin class specific armor) and Darkforge (ShadowKnight class armor) has received an increase in AC.

*Class Enhancements*
Rogue Specific

- The minimum backstab damage is now same as their level.
- Rogue poison now procs on first chance after it is applied - it is no longer random.

*Zone Enhancements*
Following the Halloween event which took place on Sunday night, the Plane of Hate was officially opened with new unique armor, weapons, and items.

In addition, the EverQuest patch program has been altered such that there is no longer a seperate "Optional Patch" request window. The Optional patch may now be selected via a button on the standard patch window.

- The EverQuest Team
