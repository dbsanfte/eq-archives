What is the Crimson Rose?
The Crimson Rose is an online role-playing guild for graphical MUD games based in fantasy settings. The guild was established during the glory days of "Diablo (beta)" and moved on to the lands of Britannia for "Ultima Online (beta)". Currently the guild is involved in Verrant Interactive's "Everquest", a massive multi-player fantasy online game based in the lands of Norrath. The Crimson Rose is now making a transition into Star Wars Galaxies, or SWG, due to be released in April 15, 2003 (date supplied by Electronic Boutique`) and Planetside due to be released May 20th, 2003 (date supplied by Electronic Boutique`). We have a few members in these games beta tests now.

The guild is designed as a playing environment whereas a player can come and be free of k3VVL dOOdZ, profanity, cheating and general lack of the role-playing that seems to be enveloping the MMOG gaming community. Focused mainly on the Role-Playing of good character types, the guild is helpful to all players in the actual game that are not rude, belligerent, known cheater/hacker or k3VVL dOOdZ. The Crimson Rose is "Anti-Player Killing" for purpose of killing for loot, though player killers are permitted into the guild for defensive purposes only but have their actions closely monitored as to keep the Crimson Rose's good name.

Member or not, the Crimson Rose looks forward to meeting all role-players in game and sharing good times during our online gaming experience. We are currently exploring other games to become a multi-game guild and to expand our time online to more than one game theme. We encourage you to come join us in this endeavor and explore the worlds we reside in to the fullest of your imagination.


I want to become a member!
Good to hear! The Crimson Rose is always looking for valuable and mature members to add to its membership. We do recommend that you read and understand the rules and laws of the guild before you submit your application. Once you have read our guidelines, and agree completely with them, send an email to Dantrag. You should receive a response back from one of our officers no later than 72 hours after sending it in.

Do you allow Evil Races?
Yes, but only as long as the character "role-plays" him/her as a good-aligned person. Just because a race has a bad reputation does not mean all members of the race are the same. However, we ARE a group of role-players and you may have to "win" the respect of other members in the guild. This may lead to some excellent role-playing encounters as well. Elves and Dark Elves just have a hard time getting along...

Do you allow Evil Classes?
Yes, providing they abide by the laws of the realm. These classes may not be treated with the "best of terms" based on the way the other classes role-play their character. But that is part of the role-playing experience.

What is the benefit of joining?
Fun! We are a group of good friends who play for the enjoyment of the game. Protection is also a major factor as well. Nothing gives you more pride than saving a member in distress and receiving his or her gratitude as reward. We do not have specific level, gear or other requirements to join our guild. We only expect that you be friendly and are looking to have good clean fun. We look out for our own and try to ensure that all members are having a great time!
