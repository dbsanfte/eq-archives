Info From A Tester (Scribe 1:49am AST)

Here is some inside info on the new Online game, Asheron's Call,

My first few hours...

Last night, as I finished the installation of the client, I began to wonder if I would feel the same way.. as I had with EverQuest. I entered the
character creation screen knowing nothing of the strengths or weaknesses of the 3 races available.. ( a European based race, an Asian based race and an Arab based race ). After looking at the various modifications that you can make per face, per race, I chose Aluvian ( the European based race ). I then was able to chose the starting clothing. I chose no headgear, a puffy shirt and a puffy pair of pants and leather boots.. ( NOTE: STARTING EQUIPMENT DOES NOTHING AS ARMOR ).. This means that I was able to really choose the look I wanted.. I tried on differing colors and settled for an all black ensamble. Then, I chose a name. I chose.. Kirel the Redhand (
grins ).

The next thing to do was to chose my skill set. They have various skill sets from your basic warrior.. a blademaster.. a healer.. an enchanter..wizard/mage.. rogue and some others. Also, if you are so inclined, you can create a totally custom skill set. Anyway.. I chose blademaster. I then modified the best stats ( Quickness, Coordination and Strength ) as much as I was able to.).. The system is set so that you can modify them in any way, but for every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.. ( hehe )..
For instance, To raise Coordination and Quickness, I had to sacrifice my sence of self and my focus ( the two things needed for good magic skills ).

Once done there, you have your actual skills. I chose 4 skills to be my "special" skills and left the others alone ( later I learned that you can really do whatever you want here.. but there is a caviat. Let us say that you chose as I did.. a blademaster.. and sacrificed your focus and sense of self : In your skills, you can choose to have healing or magic as a training skill.. but because you have such low focus and self ( no mana ), being able to train in magic does you no good. You can't use it....

Anyway.. from there I entered the world into a pre-defined town/area. I went to the newbie trainer.. learned how to fight, how do pick stuff up.. how to use the camera... ( very well thought out and the interface seems very intuitive.. you can use standard quake keys for movement... as well as the arrows. I played for about 1 hour .. just learning how do do stuff.. emotes... running around.. got into a battle .. ( attacked a cow.. Hahahahaha )... found an experianced player who wanted me to assess his weapon ( one of my primary skills )... I did so, it was a basic sword, nothing special.. He was disappointed and just gave it to me.. ( YAY!.. a real weapon )..

Over all.. I enjoyed my time there.. but I was disappointed in a few areas.

Only 3rd person view. The camera angles are great, but I really do not like the semi - Tomb Raider view Graphics.... While the spell effects.. the fire.. stuff like that are undoubtably Awesome, I was not to impressed with the over-all graphics... The houses were good, the tree's looked.. like background paper .. the falling leaves confused me at first ( thought they were flying monsters that I could not hit ).. but they were a nice touch.. The monsters... looked good enough ( I saw 3 types.. one was a greyish.. almost classic alien
looking thing.. another.. a cross between a wolf and an ape.. ( mind you, I was running for my life and the camera angles only let me see just a bit of this one ).. and the cow.. *giggles*.. If any of you have played Microsofts Monster Truck racing game... you know what the cows look like.. ( I got steaks when I killed the cow!!!!! ).

Some might say that my system was a contributing factor in my distaste of the graphics... well. Here is what I have..

- 56k modem ( good enough for this )
- Riva TNT 16mb card ( NOTE: at this time, AC would not support my Voodoo2 cards )
- P2 300
- 128mb PC100 ram
- 400mb dedicated and defraged daily swapspace on the HD

I will give more info as I am able... and.. I am not leavin you all for AC..
( just a diversion at this point ).

 

A Poem (Scribe 2:04am AST)

Here is a short peom sent to me by an anonymous individual. I thought it pretty deep!

 

If I knew it would be the last time that I see you walk out the door, I would give you a hug and kiss and call you back for one more.

If I knew it would be the last time that I'd see you fall asleep, I would tuck you in more tightly and pray the Lord your soul to keep,

If I knew it would be the last time I'd hear your voice lifted up in praise, I would video tape each action and word, so I could play them
back day after day.

If I knew it would be the last time, I could spare an extra minute or two to stop and say "I love you," instead of assuming, you would KNOW I
do.

If I knew it would be the last time I would be there to share your day, well I'm sure you'll have so many more, so I can let just this one slip
away.

For surely there's always tomorrow to make up for an oversight, and we always get a second chance to make everything right.

There will always be another day to say our "I love you's", And certainly there's another chance to say our "Anything I can do's?"

But just in case I might be wrong, and today is all I get, I'd like to say how much I love you and I hope we never forget,

Tomorrow is not promised to anyone, young or old alike, And today may be the last chance you get to hold your loved one tight.

So if you're waiting for tomorrow, why not do it today? For if tomorrow never comes, you'll surely regret the day,

That you didn't take that extra time for a smile, a hug, or a kiss and you were too busy to grant someone, what turned out to be their one last
wish.

So hold your loved ones close today, whisper in their ear, Tell them how much you love them and that you'll always hold them dear,

Take time to say "I'm sorry," "please forgive me," "thank you" or "it's okay" And if tomorrow never comes, you'll have no regrets about
today.

- Anon
