(These are the races of Star Wars Galaxies that a player may be. This list may be added to at a later time as more races become available)

 

HUMANS

Humans are born on either Corellia or Naboo. They have the choice of fighting for the Empire or becoming a Rebel and will have a "normal" range of skills. Human's language is called "Basic" and the galaxy's official language. Because Humans reside all over the galaxy, they are usually referred to by their home planet.

TWI'LEK

Twi'leks are native to the planet planet Ryloth and have above average hearing along with their own private language that can not be learned by other races and species. Twi'leks are humanoid in appearance but have a rather large, drooping "hornlike" protrusion on each side of their head that contributes to their unique cognitive and communication abilities.

WOOKIEE

Wookiees are extremely loyal and honorable, strong, extremely over sized, smart with regenerative powers, understanding Basic language yet only able to speak their language, Shyriiwook, using animal-like sounds and actions to speak this language. Wookiees live in treetop villages on on Endor.

ZABRAK

This horned and tattooed race can be found on Talus and Corellia. They speak Zabraki and Basic but can learn other languages and have special abilities and attributes from their horns.

BOTHAN

Bothans make excellent spies and tend to spy for the Rebel Alliance, however they are also excellent poets and scholars. Humanoid with a furry appearance, they are fluent in Basic and their own language.

RODIAN

Not one of the nicer looking species, the Rodian also have nasty personalities as a rule preferring to be bounty hunters. They excel at "hunting" and trade skills involving weapons and equipment. Rodians are quick and agile.

MON CALAMARI

This peace loving species can survive underwater for a much longer time than other species and races. They do better on Cooler, wet planets than harsh, hot planets.

TRANDOSHAN

Hated enemies of the Wookiees, Trandoshans are a lizard-like species that have few friends and more enemies. Excellent Warriors, Trandoshans are strong with great regenerative powers.
