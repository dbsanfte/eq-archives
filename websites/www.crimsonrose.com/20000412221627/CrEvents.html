CR Calender of Events

 

* DONE February 26th, 2000, 8pm (PST), Meeting at the Xroads in ButcherBlock - Lord Rithaks' Bachelor Party. It is BYOB (bring your own beer). Once everyone is assembled at the Xroads, it is a march to Unrest where we set up shop outside along the left side of the House, next to the first tree by the side door. Where we will commence to get drunk, and send the Bachelor into the house for pulls to the group outside. There MUST be a Designated Runner (DR) for brew. The DR will be selected at the Xroads and they MUST be able to gate (for faster service *smiles*). Our Goal is to get the Ghost at some point to get boots for members and non-members alike. But if we dont succeed, WHO CARES. 'cause we will have one HECK of a headache the next morn! The goal is to be Drunk at ALL times while fighting. A bunch of spinnin' fools fightin and have'n a good time! :)

* DONE February 27th, 2000, 8pm (PST), North Ro Derv Camp #1 (along the water) - Lord Rithak and Lady Lilea's Wedding day! ALL are welcomed to share this great event.

*March 11th, 2000, 7pm (PST), RiverVale Bank - An excursion to Clan RunnyEye. Our army will be leaving at 7:15pm sharp to start our march to the caverns of Clan RunnyEye. Bring a Hearty appetite for adventure, fun, and the "won'ton" slaying of the dreaded Goblins of Clan RunnyEye!

*March 12th, 2000, 7pm (PST), Guild IRC - The Guild Meeting in the guild IRC channel.

*March 14th, 2000, 9pm (PST), Docks at Oasis - Meeting at the Docks in the Oasis to march on the Orc Highway and from there to the depths of Upper GuK for Spider hunting. Bring a sewing kit to make more money while hunting spiders.

*March 18th, 2000, 5pm (PST), White Bridge in East Karana - Lake Rathe Hunting Party Forming - Qualifications: Must have own weapons/armor and/or spells, must like the outdoors, must know how to swim and most of all, must not be afraid of blood and guts OR to get a little scraped up. If interested, meet at the white bridge in East Karana at 5pm.

*March 19th, 2000, 7pm (PST), NO GUILD MEETING - There will be no guild meeting UNLESS Lady Lilea or Lord Cirith call for one. Dantragyth will be on vacation to a far off land known as California for the weekend.

*March 23th, 2000, 8pm (PST), Estate of Unrest? - Meeting at the zone to Dagnor's Cauldren at 8pm (PST) for the march to Unrest. Once assembled there, we will enter, and commence to slaughter Undead and beetle alike to send them BACK tot he 9 Hells' from which they spawned!

*March 26th, 2000, 7pm (PST), Guild IRC - The Guild Meeting in the guild IRC channel.
