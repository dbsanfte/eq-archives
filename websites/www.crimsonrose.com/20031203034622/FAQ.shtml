A.1  Who is the Director General?

The Director General of the Crimson Rose is Dantrag

A.2  Who are the rest of the leaders?

The officers of the guild, and chain-of-command is as follows:

    • Director General (guild master) - Dantrag
    • Commandant General (assistant guild master) - Belwar
    • Governor (political leader) – Lady Vespacian
    • Deputy Commandants (a members immediate “supervisor”) – Dangarok, Skorpiun

A.3  How does the chain of command work?

The Crimson Rose’s chain of command functions exactly as a “real life” chain of command would. If you have questions, suggestions, complaints, or anything at all, you would first contact your immediate supervisor - your Deputy Commandant. If your Deputy Commandant is unable to give you a satisfactory conclusion to your query, you would then continue up to the Governor, and so on. Breaking the chain of command is highly frowned upon, as well as being disrespectful to your Deputy Commandant. Let’s say, only the Commandant General is online at the time, and your question/problem can not wait, you, at that point, may bypass the chain-of-command and go to the Commandant General.

A.4  How do I find out who my Deputy Commandant is?

You should be notified upon joining The Crimson Rose, who the chain-of-command is. If there is any question about this, you should check on the website/message board. There, you will find a complete listing of all the leaders and members.

A.5  Can I start my own guild “section”?

No. It is the Director Generals decision when/if a new "section" is to be formed. A “section” may also be created via the process outlined in the Guild Charter (Governor gets enough votes, etc).

A.6  How do I get promoted?

Promotions, if available, are determined by guild activity, demonstration of role playing and leadership ability, etc. If you are interested in such, it is often a good idea to let it be known to your Deputy Commandant, so he/she can make mention of it to the Commandant General, or even the Director General.

 

B.1  Where can I get more information about The Crimson Rose?

www.crimsonrose.com

B.2  How long has The Crimson Rose been around playing online games?

The Crimson Rose has been a guild, for just over 8 (eight) years now. We were formed during the beta testing of Diablo I, and from there, went into the beta testing of Ultima Online, Tanaras, EverQuest, Dark Age of Camelot, and a few others. Generally speaking, we get to test the guild functions of these new games. At present, we can not discuss if we have any members that are in beta testing games at the moment.

B.3  Who is the founder of The Crimson Rose? And Why?

"I created The Crimson Rose as a restaurant for the table top rpg game, Shadowrun, back in 1992. When I started to test Diablo I, I was surprised at all the cussing, abusive nature and general disregard for another person, not to mention the lack of role-playing, in just about everyone there. So I decided to make a guild and named it, you guessed it...The Crimson Rose. I wanted to have a playing environment that is honest, where good gamers could come to and not worry about the "k3vvL d00dz", cheaters, hackers or the generally rude person. So, being new to the "guild business", and after getting about 10 (ten) people to join my new guild, I decided to get some experience and merge with another guild that was much larger and had what appeared to have a lot of experience in the "guild business". After about a month, I saw the in-fighting, mayhem and "won-ton" destruction that large guilds possess. So I decided to break away and have CR be by its lone-some. After having about 15 (fifteen) more people come with me when I broke away from the "mother" guild, we decided to do our own thing. From there, everything just "snow-balled" into what it is today. I can not take sole credit for the present day CR. I could not have brought it this far without the exceptional help from its members. To them, I owe my thanks!" - Mike (aka: Dantrag)

B.4  How do I get the password to the private areas of the message board?

Your Deputy Commandant  will supply you with the password, following the completion of your probationary period.

B.5  How long am I on probation for?

Approximately 2 (two) months. Probations will end at the first guild meeting following your 2nd month anniversary of joining The Crimson Rose. This is to ensure your “seriousness” in wanting to be with us and to help us prosper as a whole.

B.6  What is a "daily pic"?

This is a screenshot of something (anything in game) that you like and send in to be posted on the website for others to see. Send pics to: dantrag@crimsonrose.com

B.7  Can I turn on/be PvP?

Becoming PvP must be approved by the Director General only. If you wish to activate your "PvP flag", contact your Deputy Commandant for consideration. Reason for this, it may not be in the best interest of The Crimson Rose that you specifically be PvP as we may need you for a specific "job".

B.8  Can I "duel" other people?

You may duel anyone outside The Crimson Rose that you wish, so long as you have a good role-playing reason for it. Dueling another guild member is not allowed, unless permission is obtained from your Deputy Commandant. (note: if you duel and lose, the guild will not seek retribution. Dueling is a personally thing between you and your opponent)

B.9  I thought The Crimson Rose was only a merchant/trade guild, why is that Bounty Hunter here??

There are no racial/profession restrictions within The Crimson Rose. We believe every class/profession has it purpose in some way, shape or form.

B.10  Will I be “micro-managed”?

To a certain extent…yes. The leaderships responsibility is to watch it’s members closely to make sure that everyone is in keeping with The Crimson Rose’s policies. The leadership will not  tell you what to do and where to go 24/7. They may ask your help or direct you to help a member out if your in the general area.

 

C.1  When are guild meetings?

The Crimson Rose official meetings are held every Sunday, at 7:00pm PST (that’s 10:00pm EST for those mathematically challenged). They are held at a place determined once game is released.

C.2  I can't make it to guild meetings, is that ok?

No. Trade Organization meetings are mandatory for all members. If you are to miss a particular meeting, you should contact a Deputy Commandant in advance, and let them know you will not be in attendance. Emergencies happen and exceptions will be made for those unfortunate times.

C.3  What is this Messenger thing?

Microsoft’s Instant Messenger is in simple terms, a form of instantly contacting someone else. If you do not already have a client to connect to Microsoft’s Instant Messenger, you can download one at www.msn.com. Near the top of the page will be a link titled People & Chat. Click that link and you should see in red letters, MSN Messenger, and Download Here. It will need to be configured according to your tastes and you will need a hotmail account in order to connect. Hotmail accounts are free. Personally, I never use mine save only to have MSN Messenger. Contact a Deputy Commandant for details.

C.4  Can my non-guild friends come to the message board?

This may be allowed, but is up to a Deputy Commandant. At times we get people that won’t like us for some reason and may want to find out where our site/message board is. This leaves us open to spam/flames on the message board. And we do not want that to happen.

C.5  How often do guild events happen?

There is almost always a guild event in the background being planned, how often they come to completion and managed are determined by whoever is creating the event. No specific time frame is available for this, for obvious reasons. Most times, the event will be posted in the private areas of the message board.

C.6  Can I hold my own official guild event?

Absolutely. Contact a Deputy Commandant for approval on the idea (just to make sure nothing is planned when you want to have your event), and if they give you the green light, then the next step is to contact Dantrag (dantrag@crimsonrose.com) with the details so he can get it posted on the web site news area.

 

D.1  Can I get powerful/free equipment (driods, cash, ships, resources, etc.) from The Crimson Rose?

No.  We do not condone, twinking, in the games we play. Also, it is highly frowned upon to ask for such items in the guild channel, and may gain you a speaking to by a Deputy Commandant or another member of the leadership. There is a clause to this rule. You get what you put in. If you give to the bank, whether that be in the form of cash, resources, blueprints, droids, etc., then when a time comes that you need help with something, you will get it. And it will be in the form of your choosing. Do not expect to come into this organization and get equip'd, or loaded out just because you are with us now.

D.2  Does The Crimson Rose offer transportation services?

Generally, Yes.  If there is a special occasion, such as you accidentally forgot to clone yourself, and then die, reappearing across the world, then it may be ok to ask someone for a transport. Asking simply because you are too lazy to wait 14 more minutes for transportation to arrive, and your friend is knee deep in some mine, and your in town across the world, etc, is not acceptable and is not allowed. Our members that own transports, would like to play the game also, not be everyone’s private taxi service all the time (which is what happened as soon as we got members to that level of play, they found themselves literally unable to play, hence this rule)

D.3  Does The Crimson Rose encourage quest information sharing, help or even "spoilers" to quests or missions?

That is up to the individual. If you would like quest help, then it needs to be conducted via private /tell. Discussing quests once solved, or 'spoilers', in the guild channel or message board, is frowned upon, but allowed. There will be a spoiler’s forum for those that feel they need to share this info with others.

D.4  Can I name my business what I desire or must I have the same name as The Crimson Rose?

You may have the name of your business be anything that your heart desires, as long as it is in the genre of the game and is tasteful. You may also name your business, The Crimson Rose, as well if you desire. The choice is yours. But we would like our initials in the name somewhere (ie. Black Jack Alley (CR) or (CR) Black Jack Alley ) so other member of our guild will know that we have a “safe-house” in the area.

D.5  I have a screenshot for the daily pic, who do I send it to?

Email Dantrag (dantrag@crimsonrose.com ) and he will post the information on our site ASAP. There is also a link off the main site, "Summit News", that you can use as well.

D.6  How does The Crimson Rose communicate outside of the game?

The primary mode of communication is Microsoft’s Instant Messenger, and this is a required program for all guild members. We also have a guild message board, which is linked off our main site.

D.7  What are the rules of the message board?

No quest information may be posted on the message board (save for the spoiler forum). It is highly preferred that you do not post your entire question in the subject field of your message, or that you post "NT" (no text) follow-ups to messages. Subjects in all caps, quest info, that contain profanity, etc., will be deleted from the board without notice or explanation. This is a public forum; you are expected to present a good showing of both self and Trade Organization, for anyone who views it.

D.8  Can my friend join The Crimson Rose as well?

If you know someone who wishes to join The Crimson Rose, they will still need to complete a Petition for Membership. A Petition for Membership is simply an email with, Why you want to join The Crimson Rose, What trade are you bring to The Crimson Rose, and a brief summary of themselves. Let a Deputy Commandant know, and they will have the Governor be on the lookout for your friend’s petition, which will be given special consideration. However, just because you are recommending someone does not guarantee they will be accepted. But the chances are good.

D.9  Can I move into another profession without having to tell anyone?

Yes. The proper method for doing this is to contact Deputy Commandant and let them at least know you are interested in moving into another profession..

D.10  Do I have to 'thee' and 'thou' all the time when I talk?

No.  This is not a universal form of RPing, everyone has their own style. It is, however, strongly encouraged to Role-Play your character as much as possible.

D.11  Does The Crimson Rose anticipate expanding onto another server?

Not at this time.

D.12  Someone is being abusive to me in game, can I retaliate?

No.  Acting childish in response to someone else acting childish just makes you look as foolish as your antagonist.  Just put the person on ignore and go about your business.  If it is something persistent, petition a GM (game master) for help.  Do not resort to "OOC" (out of character) cussing and name calling with someone who is a jerk that just gives them satisfaction and lowers you to their level.

D.13  What is The Crimson Rose's stance on cheating or bug abuse?

If you are caught doing either, you will be immediately removed from the guild and we will report you to the appropriate gaming officials.  Simply don't do it!
