Noble Kirel's latest deeds of greatness

Hear ye! Hear ye! I bid thee all to hearken to mine tale for 'tis one which involves one most pure of heart and action. Verily, I speak of non other than Kirel Redhand, noble paladin and Servant of the Rose.

Twas yesterday eve that I had the pleasure of travelling with Kirel for a short time. We had decided to rid the Butcher Block Mountains of some of its more scurvy inhabitants, the Smeltpot Bandits. During a pitched battle, it was clear that I was nearing death at their hands. Despite his wounds and overwhelming odds, Kirel did a most noble act. He used his Diety given powers to heal mine own wounds instead o' his own. The bandits, seeing that I would overcome them quickly at that point, turned in fury upon Kirel Redhand and soon cut him down. In a blind rage, I made short work of the remaining three but it was already too late. Kirel's broken corpse lay next to those of his assailants and mine heart was filled with grief...

But wait! Mine ears didth detect footsteps nearby. And LO! Twas Kirel, naked as the day he was born, coming to return to gather his things. I asked him how was this possible, for twas certain he was most dead. He simple shrugged in humility and mumbled something about earning his Diety's favor...

Milords and ladies of Crimson Rose. I do not subscribe to this belief in a higher power being but a simple warrior of a fridged land. But now I wonder, having seen such a wonder! Truely, Kirel is blessed and his heart most pure among us all!

Carrai En Ellisande most noble Kirel Redhand! May ye live ferever with the power of yer Diety and the strength of yer giving heart!

Korfallen Ardgael of Halas
