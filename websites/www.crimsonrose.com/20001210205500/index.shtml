Welcome to the Crimson Rose Gaming Hub. As you might know, we were once a online gaming guild that spanned a course of over 5 years. Since the arrival of more online games, we decided to move into them all. After several attempts at this, we decided to move to something a bit differant. As of now, The Crimson Rose is no longer a guild in the sense that you know of. We are now just a hub where anyone can come and connect up with other online gamers to set up games of various makes by the use of the extensive message board we have. If there is a game that you wouldlike to see on the message board, contact Scribe or Ares and give 48 hours for it to be posted for you.

This site will no longer worry about offending those that have weak hearts in the aspect of language, jokes, or anything that may be offensive. If you are easily offended or have a weak heart, we encourage you to not bookmark this site or frequent it in anyway. This site will not endorse pornography of any sort.

If you have something that you would like to see posted for others to see on the main page here, email Scribe and he will see that it is posted for you. Over the next few days, links to the left will be changing to reflect the change we are going through, so mind your step for a little while.
