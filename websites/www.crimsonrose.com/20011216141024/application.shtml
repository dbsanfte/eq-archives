Our guild has members from all over the world. We try to get you familiar with other people in your time zone, so that you always have someone to call upon. We also have members who play for 2 hours a week, and some who are in game about 25+ hours a week. We do not hold this against you, no matter how much time you can put into CR. We only ask that you spend 90% of that time with your fellow guildmates. We ask to know what state/country you live in so that we can see if any of our members live close to you. This helps for real life gatherings and other events.
