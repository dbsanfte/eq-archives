What is a Profession?
A Profession is a group of disciplines grouped together. Disciplines are small skill trees (groups of similar skills) which you can work your way from novice to master. The number of disciplines per profession may vary, but if you manage to master them all you are given the chance to master the profession. Some professions require that you have mastered certain disciplines (or even entire professions) before you can start training them. For instance, to become a Miner, you will likely need to finish the Scout's Resource Specialist discipline first.

 How do I raise my skills?
Unlike many other MMORPGs released at this time, you climb the discipline skills by using your skills. For instance, to gain blaster skill, you must use a blaster in combat. To become a better Medic, you must heal people who have been hurt. You get XP (for lack of a better word) towards a skill when it is used, and when you have obtained enough of the required xp, you can train at the next level of that skill. There is no general XP, so you cannot use a blaster to kill a creature and then put that XP towards cooking. Crafters have a special way of gaining xp towards their skills. Instead of gaining it when they successfully create an item, they instead gain it when somebody else uses an item they've made. So if a Chef baked a dozen pies, they wouldn't get XP towards their cooking disciplines until somebody else ate their pie. Some skills can also be raised by completing certain quests, or by having another character or NPC train you in the skill. Each character has a set number of 'skill points' they can use at once. Each skill costs a certain amount of skill points. You may train in as many skills as you wish, as long as you have enough free skill points, and the prerequisites, to purchase them.

 What are the Professions?
There are basically two types of Professions in Star Wars Galaxies. The six Starting Professions are the basic type - they have no prerequisites and you must chose one at character creation before entering the world. The other type is "Advanced Professions" - these are Professions which require certain disciplines or entire professions be mastered before they can be explored. Different professions require different prerequisites, and some even require that two or three skills be mastered. Currently, all we really know about each professions is their name and one or two of their disciplines. We can guess what kind of prerequisites they may have, but we are not sure as of yet.

 What are the Basic/Starting Professions?
They are all listed below for what is known so far. This page may be added to at some point.

Professions
Star Wars Galaxies will include over 30 unique professions.

Starting Professions
When you begin playing, you will choose from one of these starting professions.

Artisan
This profession includes skills from the basic crafting and surveying disciplines. A player in this profession is well versed in using schematics to build things. The basic crafting profession. All of the other crafting professions, such as Tailor, Armor Smith, and Industrialist, branch from these disciplines.

  • Disciplines Required: Basic Crafting, Basic Surveying.

Brawler
This profession includes skills from the unarmed and melee combat disciplines. A player in this profession can be extremely dangerous in close combat. The basic melee profession, proficient in hand to hand combat. Other melee professions, that is professions dealing with close ranged weapons, branch from these disciplines.

  • Disciplines Required: Unarmed, Melee Combat.
    •

    Entertainer
    This profession includes skills from the dancing and musicianship disciplines. A player in this profession can play musical instruments and perform dance routines for the benefit of others.     The basic 'buff' profession. Entertainers (and later, Dancers and Musicians) can offer dances and music which can empower your character or heal wounds.

  • Disciplines Required: Dancing, Musicianship.
    •

    Marksman
    This profession includes skills from the ranged combat disciplines. A player in this profession is extremely dangerous in ranged combat.     The basic ranged combat profession. All other professions which require use of a blaster or long ranged weapon require some of this professions disciplines as prerequisites.

  • Disciplines Required: Ranged Combat.
    •

    Medic
    This profession includes skills from the wound treatment and medicine crafting disciplines. A player in this profession is a valuable ally to any combatant.     The basic healing profession. Players investing in these disciplines are able to heal damage and wounds given to players in combat. The Doctor and Combat Medic will most undoubtedly require much of this profession be trained.

  • Disciplines Required: Wound Treatment, Medicine Crafting.
    •

    Scout
    This profession includes terrain navigation and organic resource harvesting disciplines. A player in this profession will be able to set up campsites to protect players from wildlife.     The basic light-weight profession. Scouts are initially proficient in movement and fauna identification and gathering. Advanced scouts can look forward to the professions of Creature Handler and Ranger.

  • Disciplines Required: Exploration, Hunting, Survival, Trapping.

    •  

    Elite and Hybrid Professions
    The following professions require some level of mastery in one or more of the Starting professions.

    Architect
    This profession includes skills from the blueprints and building production disciplines. A player in this profession can build structures including player venues, installations, and other structures.     This profession allows players to construct buildings and other structures. It will likely require some background in the Artisan profession, and perhaps be a prerequisite for the Industrialist profession.

  • Prerequisites: Unknown
  • Disciplines Required: Blueprints Production, Building Production.
    •

    Armor Smith
    This profession includes skills from the deflectors and armor crafting disciplines. A player in this profession can create armor for players.     This profession is similar to the Weapon Smith, and will allow players to construct armor for other players to use. It will most definitely require background in the Artisan field.

  • Prerequisites: Some Artisan Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Deflector and Armor Crafting.
    •

    Bounty Hunter
    This profession includes skills from the investigation and tracking droids disciplines. A player in this advanced profession can hunt down other players with bounties and is an accomplished combatant.     The ultimate track and kill profession, the player bounty hunter will be both a certified scout and expert combatant. You will likely require some backing in the Scout profession, as well as Marksman.

  • Prerequisites: Some Scout Disciplines, Some Marksman Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Investigation, Tracking.
    •

    Carabineer
    This profession includes skills from the carbine accuracy and speed disciplines. A player in this profession is extremely accomplished in the use of ranged carbine weapons.     This advanced Marksman professions specializes in the use of ranged carbines.

  • Prerequisites: Some Marksman Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Carbine Accuracy, Carbine Speed.
    •

    Chef
    This profession includes skills from the cooking and general crafting disciplines. A player in this profession can create all kinds of foods. Foods are not necessary but are quite useful for temporarily improving a player's attributes.     An accomplished Chef can create foods that will temporarily improve player's attributes. A chef will most likely have to already be an accomplished Artisan.

  • Prerequisites: Some Artisan Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Cooking, General Crafting.
    •

    Combat Medic
    This profession includes skills from the medicine crafting and ranged healing disciplines. A player in this profession can both hold their own in a fight as well as heal those who are injured in the field.     This profession allows players to heal others and fight in the field of battle. To be a combat medic, you will require skills from the starting Medic profession.

  • Prerequisites: Some Medic Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Medicine Crafting, Ranged Healing.
    •

    Commando
    This profession includes skills from both the thrown weapon and heavy weapon accuracy disciplines. A player in this profession is dangerous both at range and up close.     A commando has the combined skills of a Brawler and a Marksman, and is deadly in both melee and ranged combat.

  • Prerequisites: Some Marksman Disciplines, Some Brawler Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Thrown Weapons, Heavy Weapon Accuracy.
    •

    Creature Handler
    This profession includes skills from both the creature taming and training disciplines. A tamed animal can be sold to other players who have sufficient skill to control the creature.     The Creature Handler is an advanced Scout profession, and allows players to tame and train creatures to obey their commands.
     

  • Prerequisites: Hunting Specialist (Scout), Exploration Specialist (Scout)
  • Disciplines Required: Creature Taming, Training, Empathy, and Management.
    •

    Dancer
    This profession includes skills from the dance ability and knowledge disciplines. A player in this profession can heal mind wounds of other players with this talent.     This profession is an advanced form of the starting Entertainer profession. It allows players to perform dances that heal and/or increase the abilities of all those who watch.

  • Prerequisites: Some Entertainer Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Dance Ability, Dance Knowledge.
    •

    Doctor
    This profession includes skills from the wound treatment and general healing disciplines. A player in this profession has a greater capability to heal damage in a controlled environment.     The Doctor is an advanced Medic who has a greater capacity to heal then his starting profession counterpart.

  • Prerequisites: Some Medic Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Wound Treatment, General Healing.
    •

    Droid Engineer
    This profession includes skills from the droid blueprints and production disciplines. A player in this profession can design droid components, instruments and droids themselves.     A Droid Engineer is an advanced Artisan profession which allows players to construct droid components and combine them to create droids.

  • Prerequisites: Unknown
  • Disciplines Required: Droid Blueprints, Droid Production.
    •

    Farmer
    This elite profession includes skills from the livestock production and harvester management disciplines. A player in this profession can grow plants and animals for foods and medicines.     This profession stems from the basic Scout profession, and allows players to grow plants and animals for food and medicine.

  • Prerequisites: Some Scout Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Livestock Production, Harvester Management.
    •

    Fencer
    This profession includes skills from the sword speed and accuracy disciplines. A player in this profession can be quite formidable in a duel.     The Fencer is an advanced Brawler proficient in pointy one-handed weapons. A fencer is fast and deadly in melee combat.

  • Prerequisites: Some Brawler Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Sword Speed, Sword Accuracy.
    •

    Image Designer
    The Image Designer profession includes skills from the body customization and hairstyling disciplines. A player in this profession can modify various aspects of the way other players look.     Players who invest in this profession, which stems from the Artisan (and perhaps leads from/to the Tailor profession), can alter the appearance of other players - tattoos, hair styles, etc.

  • Prerequisites: Some Artisan Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Body Customization, Hair styling.
    •

    Industrialist
    This profession includes skills from the factory maintenance and management disciplines. A player in this profession can affect the speed at which factories turn out items.     Industrialists can enhance the abilities and longevity of buildings. This profession likely requires background as an Architect.
     

  • Prerequisites: Unknown
  • Disciplines Required: Factory Maintenance, Factory Management.
    •

    Jedi
    Jedi is a special case profession that requires considerable prerequisites (such as being Force sensitive). A player in this profession has access to considerable Force wielding skills, but they would be wise to keep that information to themselves as the Galactic Empire aggressively hunts the Jedi.     This elite profession requires a character be Force Sensitive. It contains all the Jedi skills.

  • Prerequisites: Force Sensitivity
  • Disciplines Required: Force Wielding.
    •

    Merchant
    This elite profession includes skills from the venue and vendor management disciplines. A player in this profession can operate vendors and run player-venues.     his profession allows players to create shops and shopkeepers and sell/buy wares. The merchant profession will likely require previous experience in the Entertainer and/or Artisan trees.

  • Prerequisites: Unknown
  • Disciplines Required: Venue Management, Vendor Management.
    •

    Miner
    This elite profession includes skills from the mineral and liquid extraction disciplines. A player in this profession can pull valuable resources from the environment.     The miner is proficient in removing minerals, metals, and other valuable resources from the ground. Requires Scout.

  • Prerequisites: Some Scout Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Mineral Extraction, Liquid Extraction.
    •

    Musician
    This profession includes skills from the musical ability and knowledge disciplines. A player in this profession can heal mind wounds of other players by performing different songs.     The musician is similar to the Dancer in the fact that their abilities heal and/or increase the abilities of others. Likely requires backing in the Entertainer skill tree, if not dancer as well.

  • Prerequisites: Some Entertainer Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Musical Ability, Musical Knowledge.
    •

    Pikeman
    This profession includes skills from the pole-arm accuracy and technique disciplines. A player in this profession is accomplished fighter with blunt weapons.     A Pikeman is an expert in long, blunt melee weapons. He is fast and accurate up close. To be a Pikeman, you will likely need experience as a Brawler.

  • Prerequisites: Some Brawler Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Pole-arm Accuracy, Pole-arm Technique.
    •

    Pistoleer
    This profession includes skills from the pistol speed and accuracy disciplines. A player in this profession can be extremely deadly with handguns.     Similar to the Carabineer profession, except with smaller and more accurate (though less damaging) ranged weapons.

  • Prerequisites: Some Marksman Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Pistol Speed, Pistol Accuracy.
    •

    Ranger
    This profession includes skills from the tracking and terrain transversal disciplines. Some of the most accomplished explorers are master rangers.     An advanced profession that extends directly from the Scout profession. A ranger is proficient in movement over rough terrain and tracking. Some of the Ranger's disciplines are likely prerequisites for a bounty hunter profession.

  • Prerequisites: Master Scout
  • Disciplines Required: Tracking, Terrain Transversal.
    •

    Rifleman
    This profession includes skills from the rifle accuracy and speed disciplines. A player in this profession is extremely dangerous at a long distance.     The rifleman is extremely skillful with long ranged and accurate weapons. Will require a background as a Marksman, for sure.

  • Prerequisites: Some Marksman Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Rifle Accuracy, Rifle Speed.
    •

    Smuggler
    This profession includes skills from the smuggling disciplines. A player in this profession can move "illicit" materials past "faction police" with greater ease and are useful to players involved in commerce.     A smuggler is proficient at moving illicit materials past security or 'faction police'.

  • Prerequisites: Unknown
  • Disciplines Required: Slicing
    •

    Squad Leader
    This profession includes skills from the combat and defense leadership disciplines. A player in this profession provides combat and movement bonuses to anyone that is grouped with them.     A squad leader will likely be both an accomplished scout, medic, and Marksman. This profession specializes in skills that aid their group in movement and defense.

  • Prerequisites: Unknown
  • Disciplines Required: Combat Leadership, Defense Leadership.
    •

    Swordsman
    This profession includes skills from the heavy sword speed and accuracy disciplines. A player in this profession excels at the art of cleaving.     This profession contains disciplines related to the use of two handed swords and other such weapons. Requires backing as a Brawler.

  • Prerequisites: Some Brawler Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Heavy Sword Speed, Heavy Sword Accuracy.
    •

    Tailor
    This profession includes skills from the patterns and tailor techniques disciplines. A player in this profession can outfit other characters in style.     A proficient tailor is capable of creating new and unique clothing for players. A player can change their entire look with the help of a tailor. Requires Artisan skills.

  • Prerequisites: Some Artisan Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Pattern Techniques, Tailor Techniques.
    •

    Teras Kasi Artist
    This profession includes skills from the fist accuracy and technique disciplines. A player in this profession is extremely dangerous in melee combat. Teras Kasi, or "steel hands", is a Bunduki martial arts discipline focusing on weaponless combat.     Some of the most deadly unarmed Brawlers become Tera Kasi Artists, capable of rending a great deal of damage with only their fists.
     

  • Prerequisites: Some Brawler Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Fist Accuracy, Fist Techniques.
    •

    Weapon Smith
    This profession includes skills from the melee weapon and firearm crafting disciplines. A player in this profession can create the best weapons in the galaxy.     The weapons profession contains disciplines for the creation and maintenance of weapons. Will require backing as an Artisan.

  • Prerequisites: Some Artisan Disciplines
  • Disciplines Required: Melee Weapon Crafting, Firearm Crafting.

    •
