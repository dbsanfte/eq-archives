Thursday - May 11, 2000

Note About Updating (Dantrag 2:13am PST)

The site is going through some changes at this time. Please forgive the lack of updates and hopefully this will change soon. As many of you know, my health is going down a little each day and the last few weeks has been pretty bad for me. I ask for some help. What I need is someone to help out, if not take over the updating of the news for me. I will help out as much as I can, but I can not do it all myself. Please send me an email with your experience and if possible, some web-page sites you have done previously.

Next, I am changing the guild format again. We are going back to the Royalty motiff. King, Queen, Duke...etc, etc...The titles and the changes are noted on the Hierarchy of the Guild page. This is taking effect immediately.

We are as well changing our outlook on others as well. We are going to revert back to our original ways of old. Which is, there is good in all of us, and as such, until we are treated without honor or treated with disrespect, we will show honor and respect to all that ask for it. You have your own personal beliefs and I respect each and every one of them. However, you are a member of this Kingdom and as such, you are a subject of the Crown. Which in short means simply this: Everything we do, say or think...is for the betterment of the Kingdom and it's subjects. Your actions reflect on everyone of us.

If at any time you feel you do not like what is transpiring around you while you are here with us, I ask simply that you send me an email to me stating you wish to leave and then go in game and de-tag yourself. Please give me this courtesy. There will be no hard feelings whatsoever.

Please watch for more minor changes as time goes on. As we all know, to make somthing better, you must fine tune it.

There will be a short, but very important meeting in our guild IRC channel this Sunday at the normal time. I ask that you all be present.

King Dantragith Gryfonsbane -
