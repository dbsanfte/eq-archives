Wednesday - November 3, 1999

Oh Oh, cont'd (Scribe 2:25am PST)

You will see that the Kings response is in the light blue and Sir Rogans is in Grey...The emal addresses have been left out to ensure privacy...

Sir Rogan wrote:

I must say, this came quite out of the blue. All of a sudden.. you've ordered me to be REMOVED from the guild without even bothering to say why? Jinni contacted me with the news yesterday, but I miss-read what the message due to spam, and thought that she was asking me to remove her tag. I told her she could /guildremove herself and went about my business. Earlier today, Cirith came on and also informed me of your order.. at which point I got a teleport away from my group deep within the Mino side of Guk to sort out this issue. Cirith and I had a discussion, which turned more into me ranting about you and how I was dissatisfied with your actions as Guild Leader. Cirith listened to me, but he still was bound to your order to remove me, so I went anon for the time being while I continued to talk with him. Jinni came on, and I confirmed with her what message she had sent me yesterday, and I realized my error in reading it.I then proceeded to bid the two of them farewell, leaving some choice parting words about you. I will try to restate them now so you can hear them from the original source.

What really has perturbed me about this whole situation is that NO ONE ever mentioned a bit that I may not be doing things as you wished. I'm sorry if I wasted 3+ weeks on my Armor of Ro, which was broken for a week, and then finally realized as the laughing stock of Paladins.I bid my time then alone, and only asked for assistance in taking down a Nightfall Giant. After this, I became frustrated with EQ and began to play less and less. I withdrew from the game temporarily and resumed some other interests. I came back periodically to try and hunt in Sol B, but was trained out so many times I gave up yet again. Recently, I began to quest in earnest for Soulfire, and did not ask anyone for help because no one who was on at the time stood a chance of even harming Sir Lucan, or Xicotl. I will agree that I have not spent a lot of time recently helping members level, or giving items down. I have, however, very little in items in my bank account that I can give away in the first place. I have not gotten a new item in nearly 4 weeks because of combine questing for Ro/Soulfire, and previous disgust with the dungeons. If members wanted to get my help, they could have come to me when I was on, but no one even approached me to ask for a sliver of assistance, nor was anyone near to my position. On top of that, I was not playing Rogan much at all because I realize Verant's obvious bias against Paladins, so I played Rogan much less, and started a Gnome Magician to play to try and make EQ fun again. I had reached that point and was ready to go get Soulfire, and good items for the guild... but you kicked me out of the guild. Now, I understand that you expect me to help the guild, but if I'm not living up to your satisfactions, wouldn't TELLING ME be a good idea instead of just kicking me out? No one, and I stress.. NO ONE expressed dissatisfaction with me in any way prior to your order to remove me. I have been at every guild meeting that I can remember, and you had numerous opportunities then, in game, or via E-mail that you could have expressed your feelings. If you want to go on a power trip and strip people's tags without warning, and go removing people on a whim, that is your business as the Guild Leader. I promise you, however, you will find your membership quite low if you continue to act as a Tyrant with absolute power.

I expect to hear back from you on this matter, and in a timely matter. If one week passes and you have not satisfactorily answered to my charges, I will raise a stink about this. I will let the community know of your actions, and the kind of ruler you are. I will let people know that you are intending to turn Crimson Rose into Combine, which seems to be your current objective. And lastly, I will damage, if not destroy your reputation, and that of the whole guild as well. I don't want to hurt the people who serve you, but any actions I take upon you will surely fall upon the guild as a whole.

Sir Rogan Almot,
Knight of Truth

Well... I am answering to your "Charges"...And you will find that my decision was fair AND just. Lets start at the beginning...to refresh your memory a little....

Remember a few months ago...I was holding lotto's in Rivervale? You were one of those there that got an item. You remember this item? A +9 Str shield. I asked for nothing in return...HOPING that the guild would catch on to what I was doing to in turn do the same....However, this was for nought.

Now...speed up time a bit....

Remember me coming to you several weeks later and asking if you had an FBR (as someone in the guild said you did)? You said yes...but that it was for a friend, and that if I REALLY needed it, I could have it. I, in turn said, Great, Where can we meet? You then replied with something along the lines of, Why do you need it? Your a Paladin, Paladins cant use FBR's. And you kept on wanting to know why I wanted it. Remember this? First of all...I have several high level characters, I think, as well as many of the members of the guild, know, that an FBR is not for a Paladin, and secondly, as the "King" of CR, I owe no explanations to you, or anyone else in this guild about my day-to-day activities when it pertains to a deal I am doing. IT was for a REALLY good deal I was brewing. Now...do you remember me saying....Nevermind....I dont need it.....?

Ring a bell so far? If not, let me jar another memory....

Since you have been about L35+, you have flaunted your "power". I have sat and watched this and as well, have been the recipient of this banter. i.e..."I can whoop you with just my shield...", "Want me to come over and teach em a lesson...", "How would they feel if a L40 Paladin showed up...."....etc etc....This is not the type of behavior that I want within CR. This has been said to members within Guildchat. If this is said within Guildchat, How is it reflecting to non-members?

But wait....Still not done....

Remember Hoffy coming into Mistmoor the other day...? I asked if you had room for another? Your Reply. "Yes, we have a slot open, but we have 3 Druids already. Not sure if we want another...". Remember this? I then went into camo mode. You were with, hmm...honestly cat remember, With AoD or Combine....cant remember which one. Anyways....the point being....you turned Hoffy down....you were in there and saw Hoffy advertise for a good 30 minutes for a group....You never gave another reply.

Ringing any bells now....?

There are a few others...but these will suffice. As for, your comment of "What really has perturbed me about this whole situation is that NO ONE ever mentioned a bit that I may not be doing things as you wished.", Correct me if I'm wrong here, but didnt I, for the last 3-4 weeks, hound people about the changes going to
be made? Did I not hound people about grouping with the guild? Didn't Korfallen warn people about this? In your own words..."I have been at every guild meeting that I can remember...", So you knew of everything that I have said so far. So again, your comment of, "but if I'm not living up to your satisfactions, wouldn't TELLING ME be a good idea instead of just kicking me out...", really has no baring here. You are a graon man. Part of the reason for the age limit within our ranks....so the leadership doesnt have to micro-manage you. People get upset if they are micro-managed. You know this.

Now, to the meat of your letter to me....I was going to have a talk with you...becuase of the beginning of your letter to me. However, once I got to the bottom, I have to say, this is what I expected from you. You are forcing my hand. And as you well know, I will not back down. So, I will hold to my belief. You will remain out of CR. Although, we will still like to have you as a friend, it is my belief that you are not suited for what we are trying to do here. It falls on no ones shoulders but my own. No one is to blame but me for this decision. I have spent 5 years getting this guild where it is now, there are many people within EQ that know how we are, and know what we are about. Your threats to me, to this guild, and lastly, to it's members, do not worry me. I am trully sorry you feel you have to resort to these measures,
but we have dealt with disgruntled ex-members before, and I am afraid to say, we will deal with them in the future as well.

Reading the last paragraph, made me think, mayhaps, we are better without you in CR. But again, I could be wrong. And I am open to hear more on this. But understand this well, Rogan. Your threats to me is not what prompted me to respond. What prompted me to respond was the closed mindedness with which you are showing here. To think that I would just one day, out of the blue, kick someone out of the guild on a whim. And to think, that you could be as arrogant to think, that you have done nothing to spur this action? I think you know me better then that. :(

Rogan, times come, and times go. You stated, "I will let people know that you are intending to turn Crimson Rose into Combine, which seems to be your current objective...", I must say, I whole-heartily disagree. We all know Combine, and what they are about. You know our policies, and what we are about. The words you speak to me, are words of anger, not reason. I urge you to rethink your words. Take some time away and think about this a bit.

I am not angry at you. I dont hate you. And I hold no malice towards you at all. i trully wish things could be differant. Maybe they can in the future, who knows.

As they say, "The Gaunlet is thrown down".

I shall post this for other members to read. I want everyone to know what has transpired here between us. I look forward to hearing from you again. But if you should decide not to, that as well is ok.

In either case, I bid you farewell, and may the Gods guide your path well.

King Dantrag Gryfonsbane,
