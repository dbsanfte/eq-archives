Guild Relations and Alliances

These are the allies of The Crimson Rose. Here you will find the guilds that The Crimson Rose holds in the highest regard. This list will grow with time as well as effort from Ambassador Ardikan, and members alike. The Crimson Rose will uphold any and all alliances held here and if need be, die for them. They are one with The Rose.

The Crimson Rose does not at any time wish to engage in more the 3 alliances at a time. Once an alliance is made with another guild, no further alliances will be made for the period of one month. This gives both parties engaged in the contract, time to settle in with each other. After one month has passed, another alliance may be entered with us. The time with which an alliance was secured will be posted under the guild name.

Non-Agression Pacts may be entered into as many times as necessary. There is no limit on these. All other prospective alliances fall under the rules above.

If you are interested in an Alliance with The Crimson Rose, contact our very own Ambassador Dhuras Songblade for details.

 

Types Of Alliances of CR

Non-Aggression Pact - Parties of this pact will agree not to bother or hinder the other in any way. Parties of this pact tolerate each other with no aggression towards the other in ANY way. Parties agree to post a link from thier web page to the others, and visa-versa.

The minimum existance of guilds wishing to enter this type of Pact is one (1) month. A Guild must have been established for at least one (1) month before we enter into a Pact of this type with them.

Treaty of Friendship - Parties will agree to all the above mentioned as well as the following; Parties agree to accept the other into thier "turf" without reprisal of any kind. Parties agree to trade and conduct business with each other fairly and if at all possible, at a discount.

The minimum existance of guilds wishing to enter this type of Pact is three (3) months. A Guild must have been established for at least three (3) months before we enter into a Pact of this type with them.

Full Alliance - Parties of this pact will agree to all the above mentioned as well as the following; Parties will assist the other in times of war, peace, or anything else that is needed ( providing it does not hinder the one helping ). Parties of this pact will give discounts on all goods and services provided by the other. (Guild MUST be sanctioned by Verant Interactive)

The minimum existance of guilds wishing to enter this type of Pact is six (6) months. A Guild must have been established for at least six (6) months before we enter into a Pact of this type with them.

Most Favored Guild - Parties of this pact agree to all the above mentioned as well as the following; Parties give and recieve free services and merchendise from each other. This is the Most Coveted of all Alliances, as the Parties involved believe and trust each other without hesitation. Guilds wishing to enter into this type of Alliance will be researched GREATLY before any decision is met. (Guild MUST be sanctioned by Verant Interactive)

The minimum existance of guilds wishing to enter this type of Pact is one (1) year. A Guild must have been established for at least one (1) year before we enter into a Pact of this type with them.
