THe Evolution of a Baroness...

Jinni Silverwing
...coming over a small ridge on her way to the Greater Faydark, the Faeiry Cortier saw a broken cart and bundles of wasp wings on the path with two young Woodelves dead nearby. They were obviously Druids, one male and one female, their scimitars bloodied and lying on the ground, one broken. "What is this she thought? They must have been victims of a bandit attack, or worse..."Noting the location so she could send a burial detail out, the Courtier started to leave when she heard a small squeak from the pile of bundles. Moving one bundle, she discovered a child, wrapped in a wolf-hide cape, the Courtier returned to the Faeiry town and presented the child to the Princess, saying "milady, the child's parents were slain, I was not able to find any identification other than the scimitar's carried by her parents, Gypsy crafted Combine Scimitar's from the land of Oasis." The Princess said "Ahhh, Druids...I have many friends from the Woodelf city, I will inquire to the leaders there about the child, perhaps she has relatives there. In the meantime, as the child has not a name, I shall name her Jinni, which means ‘magic one'."
The leaders from Kelethin had finally, after a long silence, sent a pigeon to the Princess saying that the child had no other relatives and that the leaders would place the child into the orphanage. The Princess had grow fond of the blond haired, blue eyed child, and sent word to the leaders in Kelethin that the child would remain in her care. Jinni grew into a strong, bold young elf, and having been tutored in the Druid ways at the order of the Princess, had developed strong spell casting powers. She said, "Princess, I am ready to travel our land...I want to discover the world!" The request was accepted with a smile from the Princess, who then held a ‘coming of age party' for Jinni, and announced to all that henceforth, Jinni would have the surname Silverwing, the Princess's royal name, then handed her the lone remaining scimitar that the Cortier had brought from the site of her parents death. "Take care Jinni, and may Tunare watch over you, young Druidess." Watching Jinni walk among the mushroom fields, the Courtier thought, "did the Princess always plan to give this...this......ELF, the royal name of Silverwing?" Shaking her head, the Courtier looked at Jinni with jealous envy.
Jinni began traveling over all of Norrath, meeting and helping many peoples. She thought "I must search out a group that has made evil their enemy...I know I can help them fight." One day while battling Skeleton Pawns at the "chessboard" in the Butcherblock Mountains, she met Kingdantrag and his Queen Jenna; they accepted her into their group and soon became friends. Many meetings of the three occurred and then one day, Cirith, the Duke of the Crimson Rose, asked Jinni "why don't you join the Rose? When approached, the King was overjoyed, and shouted to all " Jinni, welcome into the CRIMSON ROSE!!!".
