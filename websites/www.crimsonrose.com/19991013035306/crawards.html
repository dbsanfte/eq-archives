Awards of The Crimson Rose

These are the assorted awards that can be attained by members of The Crimson Rose. Some are difficult to get, while others are easy to recieve. In order to recieve an award here, one must nominate another fr the deed required for the award. That nomination goes before the members Baron for him to gather the needed information required for said member to recieve the award in question. Once the Baron agree's that the member nominated should recieve said award, the Baron will contact Duke Cirith for confirmation and thus the King or Queen will be informed. At the next guild meeting, the award will be presented to said member and will be posted for all to see his achievment.

The list before you is a detailed description of each medal. How to get it and what it is for as well as how many times one can recieve such a medal.

The Medals

-Medallion of Excellence - This is attained when a member has shown him/herself to be an exceptional Role-Player. THis medal can be awarded more then once. Dukes and above may award this medal.

-Kings Medal - This is attained when you show absolute self-sacrifice to other members or non-members. This is given more then once, but is RARE! This medal is VERY difficult to recieve.

-Medal of Attendance - This is attained by members coming to guild meetings, functions and special events. This medal may be attained more then once. Barons and above may award this medal.

-Master Composer - This is attained through the endless pursuit of composing various ballads, and poems for the guild. This individual has the rating of "Excellent" in 3 of his Bard skills with at least one of those skills being his instrument. This may only be attained once in a Bards career. Dukes and above may award this medal.

-Good Conduct Medal - This is attained through the pursuit of following the laws and directions, as well as orders given the member by the leaders appointed over him/her. Members may recieve this upon the end of thier 2 week probationary period. This medal may be attained more then once. Barons and above may award this medal.

-Medal of Contribution - This is attained through the act of donating to the Guild Fund. One may attain this medal after he/she has given, above his/her regular taxes, an amount of 100pp or greater either at one time, or over a period of time. All donations will be logged in the Ledger of the Treasury by Filgur for you to keep track of. This medal may be achieved more then once and the King and Queen alone may award this medal.

-Giant Slayer- This is attained through the pursuit of extreme excellance. One must slay either alone, or in a group, a giant from one of the outlying areas of Norrath. A screenshot is required of this feet and a 50pp reward will be given at the award ceremony to the member who achieves this medal. The King and Queen alone may award this medal. This medal may be achieved more then once.

-The Heart of Sacrifice - This is attained when a member has sacrificed his/her life for that of another member or non-member so that he/she may live. If a non-member was spared, that person must post it on our message board of the situation in detail. This medal may be given more then once. And Dukes and above may award this medal.

-Medal of Leadership - This is attained when a member shows exceptional leadership qualities. The number of these medals will be looked at when The Royals decide on inducting a new leader. This medal can be attained more then once. Dukes and above may award this medal.

-Medal of Kindness - This is attained when a member shows a continuous attitude of kindness towards others unselfishly. This medal can be attained more then once and can be awarded by Dukes or higher.

-Medal of Mastery I - This is attained when a member has reached his 10th level of Mastery. This medal can only be given once and is awarded by Dukes or higher.

-Medal of Mastery II - This is attained when a member has reached his 20th level of Mastery. This medal can only be given once and is awarded by Dukes or higher
-Medal of Mastery III - This is attained when a member has reached his 30th level of Mastery. This medal can only be given once and is awarded by Dukes or higher.
-Medal of Mastery IV - This is attained when a member has reached his 40th level of Mastery. This medal can only be given once and is awarded by Dukes or higher.
-Medal of Mastery V - This is attained when a member has reached his 50th level of Mastery. This medal can only be given once and is awarded by Dukes or higher.

-Master Merchent - This is attained when a member has reached an "Excellant" rating in 2 merchant skills. This medal can only be given once. Dukes or higher may award this medal.

-Medal of Quests - This is attained when a member has completed 10 quests. Each quest completed must be written up and summited to the CR.com staff for posting in the Arcanum's Library. This medal can only be awarded once. Dukes or higher may award this medal.

-Master Tracker - This is attained when a Tracker has reached an "Excellent" rating in his tracking skill. THis medal can only be given once. Dukes or higher may award this medal.

-Medal of Holiness- This is attained when a member of the cloth has reached his 15th level of mastery. This medal can only be awarded once. This medal may be awarded by Dukes or higher.

-Spell Master - This is attained when a spell caster has reached an "Excellent" rating in 2 specializations. This medal may be given more then once. Dukes or higher may award this medal.

-Master Traveler - This is attained when a member has traveled the world of Norrath and has master 4 regions spanning 2 continents. This member will know everything about each region when asked (all quests, special items, all major NPCs, etc). Dukes or higher may award this medal.

-Weapons Master - This is attained when a member has reach an "Excellent" rating in 3 weapon styles (example:1h slash, 2h slash, 1h blunt). This medal may be given more then once. Dukes or higher may award this medal.


-Medal of Negotiation - This is attained when an Ambassador has achieved either a Full Alliance or a Most Favor Guild Alliance. Thhis medal can only be given a total of 4 times to any number of Ambassadors. This medal may be awarded by the King or Queen.
