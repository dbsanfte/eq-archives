Thursday - June 1, 2000

Site Update (Dantrag 12:12am PST)

Vampire: The Masquerade - Redemption has went GOLD and has gotten GREAT reviews. This was sent in by Kirel and there will be a link up top to it on its release date. Here is a little of the review. Feel free to click the pic and read the rest of it.

"White Wolf's Vampire: The Masquerade is an extremely popular pen and paper role-playing game set in its World of Darkness, a dark and foreboding alternate reality based on the real world, but one in which vampires exist and wherein the threat of evil is omnipresent. In this fascinating campaign, vampires are not the stereotypically Dracula-like creatures too often seen in movies. Collectively known as Kindred, they are instead part of separate bloodlines called Clans, which are distinguished by their unique vampiric powers and by often subtle pseudo-political agendas. Nihilistic Software's upcoming Vampire: The Masquerade - Redemption represents the first attempt to bring this complex, intrigue- and conflict-filled world to the computer screen. It arrived with relatively little fanfare at E3 1999, where despite showing beside the much-heralded Quake III in the booth of publisher Activision, it garnered tremendous attention and won numerous accolades. Since that time, it has retained a very prominent spot in the computer gaming public's eye, and it approaches its impending release with considerable fan interest and anticipation."

As always, if you know the release date of this game, please sned it in to me for update. Thank you Kirel greatly for the info on this GREAT looking game! :)
