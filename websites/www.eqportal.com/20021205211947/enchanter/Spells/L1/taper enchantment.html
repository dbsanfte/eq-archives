Taper Enchentment
Spell Rank: Rank 1 - learnable by enchanters at player level 1

Spell Effect:

 Increases the speed at which spells wear off.
Area of Effect: single targeted creature.
School of Magic: Abjuration

Cost in Mana:

 5

Component/s/ Needed:

 --

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: The first in a line of spells meant to remove enchantments.  It is intended to be used primarily to remove spells, such as root, cast on you by other spell casters.  It works by increasing the speed at which the spell decays so it will wear off sooner.

It will only work on spells, so effects such as poison, rabies  and disease which are not spells will not be hastened by this spell.  You must wait out the duration on them or find some friendly cleric/shaman type to cure you.

Realistically you will rarely, if ever, really need to use this spell for its intended purpose.  By the time you really need to be worrying about removing spells you'll have better spells, like Cancel Magic, to do the task quicker and more efficiently.   The best use for this spell is as a practice spell to raise your skill level in Abjuration when you level.
