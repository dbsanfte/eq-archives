From Rathorme


Twas the night before Christmas
in the City of Trees.
And I thought to myself,
"I need a hit of that Breeze."

It was rainy in Kelethin
the platforms were slick.
So I pulled on my cloak
and went to find that Caster chick.

I strutted up to a High Elf
and casually said "Whassup?"
She turned and she whispered,
"Can I hook you up?"

I slipped here five gold
(not all things are free)
She casted her spell.
"Ah, sweet Clarity!"

With senses renewed,
I needed drinkin' time
so I went to the bar
and I ordered Elf Wine

After 10 drinks or so
the bartender said "Out!"
Then I left, mighty angry,
with a screm and a shout.

"My life is worthless!"
"This town really blows!"
So I went to the Fletcher,
and I bought me a bow.

I readied an arrow,
and I aimed at my head.
I shot-Missed completely!
though, the Fletcher was dead.

Then a guard entered.
He put a sword to my chest.
He shouted, "What happened here?"
So I had to confess.

"It was a Dark Elf!,
umm Drizzt is his son!
Zaknaifien-he did it!"
and then off I did run.

I ran to the lift.
Trees were all I could see.
I jumped to my death.
Wrong-I landed on a newbie.

He was crushed flat,
but I was okay.
So I decided to leave suicide,
for yet another day.

I went back to my hut,
and I turned off the light.
Well, Merry Christmas to all,
and to all a good night.

From Zaknaifien Do'Urden


Every Elf Down in Elf-ville
Liked Velious a lot...But the Gnome,
Who lived just North of Elf-ville,
Did not...
The Gnome hated the velious release date!
Oh but there was so much more then the date to hate...
No one quite knows why the Gnome hated it.
Could it be the lack of warm places to sit?
But I think that the most likely reason of all
May have been that his heart was three sizes too small.
But what ever reason
The Gnome hated this season.
The people in Velious getting the phat lewt.
The icy air making the elf women so cute.
"And they're getting new spells!" he snarled with a sneer.
"Tomorrow is Velious! It's practically here!"
Then he growled, with his Gnome fingers nervously drumming,
"I MUST find a way to keep Velious from coming!"
For, in Velious, he knew......All the Elf girls and boys
Would wake up bright and early. Open the mailbox and get the loot!
And then! Oh, then they would loot! Oh, the loot! Loot! Loot! Loot!
That's one thing he hated! The LOOT! LOOT! LOOT! LOOT!
The bards would sing! And they'd sing!
AND they'd SING! SING! SING! SING!
And the more the Grinch thought of the Bard-Velious-Sing
The more the Gnome thought, "I must stop this whole thing!"
"Why for fifty-three levels I've put up with it now!
"I MUST stop Velious from coming!
...But HOW?"
Then he got an idea!
An awful idea!
THE GNOME
GOT A WONDERFUL, AWFUL IDEA!
"I know just what to do!" The Gnome Laughed in his throat.
And he made a quick FedEx hat and a coat.
And he chuckled, and clucked, "What a great Gnomey hex!
"With this coat and this hat, I'll look just like FedEx!"
"All I need is a truck..."
The Gnome looked around.
But since FedEx trucks are scarce, there was none to be found.
Did that stop the old Gnome...?
No! The Gnome simply said,
"If I can't find a truck, I'll make one instead!"
So he got his tinker kit. Then he took some red thread
And he made a big sign, that simply said,
FedEx Express Delivery.
THEN
He loaded some bags
And some old empty sacks
On a ramshakle truck
And he set off following the FedEx truck's tracks!
All their windows were dark. Quiet snow filled the air.
All the Elves were all dreaming sweet dreams without care
When he came to the first house in the square.
"This is stop number one," The old Gnome FedEx man hissed
And he went to the front door, empty bags in his fist.
Then he slithered and slunk, with a smile most unpleasant,
Around the whole yard, and he took the present!
And he stuffed it in his bag. Then the Gnome, very quietly too,
I might add, took the Velious box, and gave a great "WHEW".
Then
He did the same thing
To the other Elves' abodes
Leaving nothing
Besides a young toad.
It was quarter past dawn...
All the Elves, still a-bed
All the Elves, still a-snooze
When he packed up his truck,
Packed it up with their Velious! The box! The wrappings!
The Cds! And the free toy offers! The maunal! The trappings!
Three tousand feet up! Up the side of Mount Delown,
He rode to the tiptop to dump it down!
"Pooh-pooh to the Elves!" he was gnome-ish-ly humming.
"They're finding out now that no Velious is coming!
"They're just waking up! I know just what they'll do!
"Their mouths will hang open a minute or two
"Then all the Elves down in Elf-ville will all cry BOO-HOO!"
"That's a loot," grinned the Gnome,
"That I simply must get!"
So he paused. And the Gnome put his hand to his hearing kit.
And he did hear a sound rising over the snow.
It started in low. Then it started to grow...
But the sound wasn't sad!
Why, this sound sounded merry!
It couldn't be so!
But it WAS merry! VERY!
He stared down at Elf-ville!
The Gnome popped his eyes!
Then he shook!
What he saw was a shocking surprise!
Every Elf down in Elf-ville, the tall and the small,
Was looting! Without any Velious at all!
He HADN'T stopped Velious from coming!
IT CAME!
Somehow or other, it came just the same!
And the Gnome, with his gnome-feet ice-cold in the snow,
Stood puzzling and puzzling: "How could it be so?
"It came without boxes! It came without wrappings!
"It came without packages, boxes or plastic trappings!"
And he puzzled three hours, `till his puzzler was sore.
Then the Gnome thought of something he hadn't before!
"Maybe Velious," he thought, "wasn't made for loot to sell in an E-Store...
"Maybe Velious...perhaps...means a little bit more!"
And what happened then...?
Well...in Elf-ville they say
That the Gnome's small heart
Grew three sizes that day!
And the minute his heart didn't feel quite so tight,
He whizzed with his load through the bright morning light
And he brought back the game! And the free toy offers!
And he...
...HE HIMSELF...!
The Gnome was the first to port and loot Earring of the Foffers!
