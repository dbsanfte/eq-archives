Handbook of the Bane Sidhe


3.1 Membership

What does Membership in Bane Sidhe mean? What do you want to do? Where will you go? How will you live?

Membership in Bane Sidhe focuses on one primary thing - the Guild. It is Gestalt theory - the whole is greater than the sum of its parts. Putting together a bunch of random people may create a more powerful force than just each of those people alone. Putting together a bunch of people where everyone works together for the benefit of everyone will be more powerful than several groups of the other bunches.



You need to be willing to put others before yourself, realizing that in helping them you are helping yourself. You need to be concerned with being a part of something greater than just yourself, and you need to be constantly aware that every action, every word, every breath of yours will also reflect on the Guild.

Individually we may become powerful. Together we will become unstoppable.
