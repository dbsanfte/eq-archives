1.2 Guild Ranks

Dark Age of Camelot has 9 fully customizable ranks in the guild. 9 is the lowest and 1 is the highest. The Guildmaster is Rank 0, and the only thing that can be customized for Rank 0 is the Title.

Each Rank has several abilities that can be set as a yes/no. These options are:

Hear Guild Chat
Speak Guild Chat
Hear Officer Chat
Speak Officer Chat
Hear Alliance Chat
Speak Alliance Chat
Invite into the guild
Remove from the guild
Promote/Demote those of lower Rank
Wear the Emblem

Upcoming abilities will be whether a person can deposit to the guild bank and withdraw from the guild bank, and more may be in the works.

Each Rank can also have a Title, which is what appears on the player's Character Sheet (the one with your stats, etc.).
