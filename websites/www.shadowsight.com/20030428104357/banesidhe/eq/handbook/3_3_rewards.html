Handbook of the Bane Sidhe


3.3 Membership Rewards

This isn't the section for all of you selfish folk out there... the "What's in it for me?" people. The honest answer to that is "Nothing." We will find you and remove you - if you're lucky enough to get in in the first place.



No, this is the section for the people who think in terms of "one for all and all for one!"

And that's the biggest reward right there...

An entire guild to help you in life. There will be regularly scheduled events where the Guild helps classes to gain their class-specific armor. We will work together to benefit all of us.

We are a family. If we act as a group of individuals each using the others for our own benefit we will all lose. If we work together to help all of us, we all win.

Do you really need more than that?
