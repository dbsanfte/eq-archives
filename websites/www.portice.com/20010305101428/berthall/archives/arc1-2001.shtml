News Archive: February 2001
2-10-2001
Zlandicar Slain!
[ 0 comments] Submited by Radek

 Last night Prophecy traveled deep into The Dragon Necropolis and after one failed attempt the mighty Zlandicar was slain. The fight was one of the most fun battles I've ever been in. I would like to publicly thank Server GM Rhuyai for delaying taking down Dragon Necropolis till after we were finished. Dragon Necropolis was one of the many zones experiencing a guild database issue and it needed to be rebooted. In other news congratulations Anad on his epic weapon! It is the first bard epic completed on Bertoxxulous.

Loot was:

  • Frakadar's Talisman - awarded to Obsidia for a Quest

  • Zlandicar's Talisman - awarded to Drizzlorn for a Sleepers Tomb Key

  • Zlandicar's Heart - awarded to Xytras for a Jaled Dar's Tomb Key

  • Two Gauntlets of Mortality - awarded to the Rogues Julya and Raidek




   




-Radek the Elementalist
