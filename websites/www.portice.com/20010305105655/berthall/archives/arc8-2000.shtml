9-18-2000
Trakanon and Venril Sathir slain a few more times.
[ 0 comments] Submited by Radek

 Trakanon was slain two more times and Venril Sathir has been killed one more times since the last news update. Ironchef became the first Shadowknight on Bertoxxulous and the second Troll SK in EQ to complete his set of Blood Ember Armor. Crag also became the first Paladin on Bertoxxulous and the second Dwarf Paladin in Everquest to complete his set of Deep WaterArmor and become a Golden Nugget.

Trakanon Loot was:

  • Cloak of Piety AC 12, +12 str +12 wis +12 int +10 sv fire +10 sv magic Effect: Haste - Awarded to Fryedegg.

  • Cloak of Piety AC 12, +12 str +12 wis +12 int +10 sv fire +10 sv magic Effect: Haste - Awarded to Dragosani.

  • Donals Breastplate AC 40, +12 wis +12 dex +12 agi 80 mana Effect: Complete heal - Awarded to Boradek.

  • Deepwater Breastplate AC 40, +10 Agi +10 Str +10 Wis +50 Mana Effect: Symbol of Pinzarn - Awarded to Crag.



Venril Sathir loot was:

  • Nightshade Scented Staff 12/40 +11 int +55 mana Effect: Scent of Darkness - Awarded to Nezereth

  • Elder Spiritist's Greaves AC 10, +6 str, +6 wis, +55 Mana. Effect Skin Like Diamond - Awarded to Mahtra



   




-Radek the Arch Magician

9-11-2000
Trakanon Slain two times.
[ 0 comments] Submited by Radek

 Trakanon has been slain two more times in the past week. Congratulations on Iena and Mahtra completing their Veeshan Peak quests and being the first two people to enter the zone on Bertoxxulous. Also Fryedegg becomes becomes the first ranger on Bertoxxulous to complete his set of Tolans Darkwood. Nokraptor completed his set shortly after. Ironchef also has every piece of Blood Ember except helm now! Loot Was:



  • Donal's Breastplate - AC 40, +12 wis +12 dex +12 agi, 80 mana, Effect: Complete heal. Awarded to Liferaiser

  • Tolans Darkwood Breastplate - AC 35, +10 str, +10 dex, +10 wis, +10 agil. Effect: of Ring of Faydark. Awarded to Fryedegg

  • Tolans Darkwood Breastplate - AC 35, +10 str, +10 dex, +10 wis, +10 agil. Effect: of Ring of Faydark. Awarded to Nokraptor

  • Blood Ember Breastplate - AC 40, +12 str, +8 dex, +8 sta, +8 agil. Effect: Major Shielding. Awarded to Ironchef.
  • 8 Dragon Teeth - Awarded to various people in the guild.



In other news please welcome the following people to the guild!
Helor - Shaman
Brlaen - Necromancer

-Radek the Arch Magician

9-2-2000
Trakanon slain by Prophecy for the first time!
[ 0 comments] Submited by Radek

 Today is a great day for Bertoxxulous! Trakanon was slain by Prophecy for the first time on Bertoxxulous. The battle was strong and fierce but in the end Prophecy emerged victorious with only 5 deaths.
Loot was:

  • Mrylokar's Breastplate 35 ac 10 str 10 dex 10 sta 5 cha -10 wis -10 int 10 agi 50 hp Effect: Invigorate - Awarded to Kadafi.

  • Cloak of Piety 12 ac 12 str 12 wis 12 int 10 sv fire 10 sv magic Effect: Haste - Awarded to Grailyn.

  • 5 Trakanon Teeth - Awarded to Xytras, Radek, Rinen, Elarinx and Iena.




   





In other news please welcome the following people to the guild!
Lorelie - Druid
Drizzlorn - Necromancer
Gorignak - Wizard
