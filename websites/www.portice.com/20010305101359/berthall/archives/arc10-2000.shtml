Trakanon, Venril Sathir killed countless times along with tons of epic quests completed.
[ 0 comments] Submited by Radek

 Over the past few months Prophecy has been quite busy with Trakanon, Venril Sathir, various other minor gods and epic quests. Sky was also cleared up to isle 7 for the first time.

Epic Quests completed:

  • Clerics - Eloren, Liferaiser, Boradek, Iena, Sakit, Dardain, Arra.

  • Monk - Xytras, Rinen.

  • Ranger - Nokraptor, Grailyn.

  • Druid - Mahtra.

  • Warrior - Elarinx.

  • Wizard - Walaskin.

  • Enchanter - Llarn, YIlyen.

  • Paladin - Crag, Atherion.

  • Rogue - Julya, Destael.

  • Shaman - Katerin.

  • Deepwater Breastplate - Atherion.

  • Donals Breastplate - Eloren, Iena.

  • Mrylokar's Breastplate - Raidek, Kelcea, Kelyna, Julya, Huppoy.

  • Blood Ember Breastplate - Beasley, Jarlax

  • Elder Spiritist's Breastplate - Mahtra, Lorelie, Nuffles, Farnoosh.
