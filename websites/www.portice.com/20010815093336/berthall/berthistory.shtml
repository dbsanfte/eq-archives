History

Every world has it's own history, and every person their own. These are the collection of discoveries we all share. As with any mystical and fantasy lore, these facts are handed down by word of mouth from one generation to another. Events and names are often open for debate and the discussing as such is always healthy.

The players of the Bertoxxulous server may or may not owe a bit of gratitude for the hard work and labor these individuals or groups went through, but there is no denying that the lexicon and history of our world we know as Bertoxxulous was forever changed after these events took place.

So share with me in retelling of our great world's past and honor those who have shown ingenuity or luck in becoming known for their actions and discoveries. Our world is far from running short and the list will grow as time passes.

New worlds are yet to be discovered.
