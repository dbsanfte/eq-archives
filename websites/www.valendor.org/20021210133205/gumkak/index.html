This website is dedicated to the MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game) Everquest.

In my search for information about this game I found a lot of good information and at the beginning I collected URL's to that sort of information. In the passing of time however I noticed that a lot of the best information available "vanished" from the internet. The reason for this was that a lot of the information was maintained by individual players on free webspace providers. As time passed, the individuals would loose interest in the game, cancel their accounts and eventually close down their webpages. That information was then lost forever.

When I became aware of this problem I started "collecting" the full content of several webpages and this is what you'll find at this site (amongst other little things =)) so at least this information will be available to myself for as long as I need it.

All "collected" content of this site has reference links to the original owners / authors of the content. No intention of violating the rights of relevant owners of this content is intended.

Tradeskill succesrate formula found.
13 april 2002  -   Gumkak

"In the extensive and wideranging "Truth about WIS and INT" thread here, Laurefin seems to have done nothing less than discover the formula for determining success rate in tradeskills. (OMG!)

Success rate = (1.15*Skill) - (Trivial - 70)

She and several other people have plugged their collected statistics into it and it seems to work. It also explains many previously observed oddities about success rates, and it even works for the cultural plate armors with very high trivials."
