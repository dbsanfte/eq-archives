Blight is one of the newer guilds on Vazaelle, established Feb. 14th 2002. This is a fairly detailed set of level / gear / spell requirements needed to join. Our raid times will be geared toward 330-430pst start times. If you are online you will be expected to participate with the raid that is in progress. This guild will be utilizing a DKP / IDKP point system for loot distribution.

Level Requirements

Class Minimum Level Desirability Special Requirement(s)
Cleric 55 Very High Special requirement(SR) See an officer in game
Enchanter 58 High 220+ Int (SR)
Monk 58 High Fungi Tunic
Rogue 58 Very High Ragebringer; Fungi Tunic recommended
Paladin 58 Moderate Wave of Healing (SR)
Wizard 58 Very High Epic near completion (SR)
Magician 55 Very Very High Call of the Hero
Shadow Knight 58 Very High 1100+ AC
Shaman 58 Moderate Malo; Fungi Tunic
Warrior 58 High 4k Unbuffed hp; See Rula in game
Necromancer 57 Very High Improved Summon Corpse
Bard 57 High Epic near completion (SR)
Ranger 58 High Fungi Tunic
Druid 55 High 220+ Wis
Beastlord 55 Very High See an officer in game.


To join Blight a player must have one character that meets the above table level requirements. This is subject to change check back periodically for desirability updates.

You must also have the following equipment:
  • Sebilis key
  • Some form of permanent enduring breath item.
  • Resist gear, MINIMUM of 100 fire/cold/disease/poison unbuffed, while simultaneously having 100 unbuffed in magic resist. (BD Earrings / Bracers and Velium Diamond Earrings recommended to accomplish this).
  • Jboots
  • Shrunken goblin skull earring and /or velium coldain ring. (Indoor right click buff).
  • 180+ swimming (Yes you Goober).
  • A Solid Connection (If you repeatedly go LD you will affect the raid in a negative manner).
If you have any questions feel free to contact me in game, thanks. --Nukuki
