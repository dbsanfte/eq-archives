How do I get started with research at all?  

If your character belongs to a researching class (MAG WIZ NEC ENC :) you will at first need to buy the specific tome for your class (see this FAQ :). This Tome acts like a container. All you need to do is put the spell ingredients in there, hit combine and you have a new spell (or not :). However, there are a few minor details you need to know. Read the whole FAQ for the best start :)

What's the deal with research at level 16? It only allowed me to put ONE practice into the Research skill when I made level 16! How can I improve this skill?

Sorry, folks, but at level 16 through level 18 you are only allowed that ONE practice. Something to do with that spell circle. But never fear! Once you break level 19 you can put 4 or 5 in there (depending upon your magic class), then once at 20, the skies the limit, and then at each level thereafter.

What do I combine my stuff into to create the spell?

Wizards use a Lexicon, Mages use Elemental Grimoire, Enchanters use Tome of Endless Enchantments, and Necros use Book of Dark Bindings.

What languages are the Wizard recipe books in?

Wizards Canon - Old Erudian
Wizards Doctrine - Combine Tongue (giant)
Ipsor's En. IIV - Dragon
Ipsor's En. IV - Elder Dragon

Where can I find all these runes, words, and pages?

It's all random. You must attack critters level 16+ and on occasion they will be carrying your research item. There generally is not a SPECIFIC creature to hunt for a SPECIFIC item.

Is the quill and parchment needed to create a spell?

No! It must NOT be included or the creation will fail.  These items are used with the Concordance (practice research)

Can I research any spell at any level?

With EXTREMELY limited success. I would suggest just sticking to your own level or below.

Do I have to know a particular language to research a spell?

The answer is yes and no. If you want to read the research recipe book, yes (although all are posted on this site). However, language is not factored into success or failure when using the research skill.

Who uses which ingredients?

Wizards use Runes, Necros use Words, Enchanters use pages from various grimoires, writs or tomes. Mages use a combination of Words and other special items.

Where can I find the recipes in game?

For each class the first research books up to about level 29 spells are sold on vendors.

I cannot train a language I need to read a research book. How do I get the proper language?

You get a wider selection of languages to learn as you advance in level.  Or learn the language by having a person who already know it spam you to death (in tells).

How do I know which book is written in what language?

There is a book in the game called "On languages". It gives a rough picture about the languages needed. The translation of the book is posted on this website.

Where can I find the Gloves of Rallos Zek?

On evil eyes in the East Karana and in the Gorge and got a report someone found one in High Hold Keep.

Do I have to research my level 16 and 20 pet spells for magicians already?

No, they can be bought off a vendor on an Island in the Ocean of Tears. Not sure about levels above 20...

Should I research my spells that are available for purchase also? Is it cheaper?

Depending on the ingredients and how many you have at your disposal it is usually A LOT cheaper to BUY your spells.

Which is the fastest way to learn a language?

Group up with the person teaching. Have him create a 5 line of text hotbutton and then spam the groupchat. Depending on his connection and clicking skill a language can be learned from awful to excellent in only 5 minutes.

I have been taught a language (using the above method), but it still says I am awful in it. Why?

Yet another bug. You have to zone or log out to make the changes go into effect.
