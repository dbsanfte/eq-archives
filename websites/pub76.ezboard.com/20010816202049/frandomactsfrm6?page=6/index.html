...Greetings Seekers! To become a member of Random Acts we ask you to do the following:
1) Put a message below with your name and vital information, and explain why you wish to join us.
2) Go to our website www.eqrandomacts.com and read The Call and The Charter.
3) Seek out members of Random Acts to get to know them and find a Sponsor among them.
4) Your Sponsor will help guide you in the completion of your Fledgling Quest.
5) Your Sponsor will notify the council when you have finished your quest and you will receive your guildtag at your first attendance of a First Aid camp (FA). Full membership induction at the following regularly scheduled FA.

... Check the Fledgling and Prospective Members Message Board forum for your name on the latest approved members list...

...... Please check the Fledgling Quest information on our website (www.eqrandomacts.com)...
