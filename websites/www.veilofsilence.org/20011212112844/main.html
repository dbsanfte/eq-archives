We are a group of people built on family and friendship, not the greed that has permeated much of Norrath. We are built to help everyone in the guild to grow strong, not a few.

If you are looking for a group of people to become friends and family with, please make a post in the Join VoS forum and tell us about yourself. Currently we are looking for people levels 53+ of various classes. But if you are not of 53+, but have an interest in us and are of 50+, you are welcome to come with us sometimes to see if we fit each other. The dedicated have come to enjoy what we have to offer and made the minimum requirements.

But our activities are our own, and much of it is not talked outside of it. That is our primary rule.
