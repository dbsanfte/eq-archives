At Level 22, Rangers get the spell “Panic Animal”.  This spell works best when the mob is Snared and you are able to follow the feared mob as it slowly tries to get away from you.  This technique is called “Fear Kiting”.  Here is a list of Animals by Zone that you’ll find useful.  Keep in mind that not all mobs which you’d personally consider an animal, is an animal in Norrath..  best test of this is to stand just out of agro range of your target and cast “panic animal”.. if you see the message “this spell only works on animals” then it’s not going to work.. However, if the spell starts to cast, DUCK immediately to interrupt the casting… then you know it’s time to snare and fear-kite.
