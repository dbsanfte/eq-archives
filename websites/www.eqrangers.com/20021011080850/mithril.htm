The Fier’Dal Forge is located only in the city of Kelethin.

 

·          Take the “Newbie” Lift to the platform

·          To your South East, is a Ramp leading to the Standard Forge

·          To your South, is a Ramp leading to the Fier’Dal Forge.

·          See this Map, Platform #22

 

 

 

Mithril Ore is only found in the City of Felwithe.

 

·          Enter Felwithe, across the bridge, take a Left.

·          Passed the Travelor’s Home, Travel East

·          Inside the “Shop of All Holds” on the 2nd Floor.

·          Merchant Tanalin Silverlake has the ore.

·          See this Map, Building #4
