The Short Sword of Morin

 

Western Plains of Karana

 

  • Track down and kill the Ghoul Messenger
  • Loot the "Detailed Map of Kithicor"

 

The Haunted Woods of Kithicor

 

  • Give the Map to Mornin Shadowbane
  • He gives to the Short Sworn of Morin

 

Make sure to have SoW cast on you, the Ghoul is very fast and runs all over the zone.   He's more commonly found (by tracking) around the Undead Camp near the South-Eastern zone line.

 

The Ghoul hits quite hard for his level, so if he /cons blue to you, you may want to bring a friend along for extra melee, or healing.
