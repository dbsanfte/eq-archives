HISTORY LESSON:

            Long before the lands of Kunark where discovered, each class had a specific set of Quest Armor, this was the equivalent of an Epic Quest back in the beginning days of Everquest.  Paladins have Ro Armor, Bards had Lambent, Clerics had Reverant… and Ranger had Ivy Etched…  Time has passed, and these quest armors are still sought after, and can very well lead you into your 40’s or Low 50’s and still be effective.
