The Helm of the Tracker

·          Ranger’s Plane of Growth Quest

·          Starts with “Tunarean Earthmelder”

·          Minimum Level: 55

 

Turn in 4 Named Kromrif Giant Heads:

·          Frost Giant Overseer, Frost Giant Overseer, Wakening Lands

·          Smashed Protector of Zek, Protector of Zek (1), Kael Drakkel

·          Bloody Protector of Zek, Protector of Zek (2), Kael Drakkel

·          Kallis’s Head, Kallis, Kael Drakkel

 

Kallis and the two Protectors spawn behind the Kael Arena, in front of where the Vindicator spawns.

The Frost Giant Overseer spawns in Wakening Lands, in front of Kael Drakkel.

 

All four Giants have a lot of Hit Points, so make sure to bring friends.
