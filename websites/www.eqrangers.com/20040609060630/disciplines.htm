RANGER’S DISCIPLINES
  • To activate your disciplines, simply type /disc then the name of the discipline you which to activate.
  • If your re-use time has not been met, a message will show, telling you how much time is remaining
  • Re-Use Time refers to the amount of time between uses of any Discipline, this time must be met before using any disciplines
       » Example.. if you use your Trueshot Discipline, you can not use Any other discipline for 72 minutes.

    NEW!!! - As of December 18th.. Disciplines are Purchased in Plane of Knowledge.
  • Go into the 1st Floor of the Library to Keshyk Wardorn
  • Purchase a Tome for each available discipline (1 copper each)
  • Go visit "Warder Elwysaie" on the first floor of the building next to the Qeynos Stone.
  • Give her each Tome to gain the discipline.
  • Hit "Control+C" to bring up your new Combat Abilities window.. Right click on a hotkey and set your disciplines.. you now have a timer bar that'll count down your disc use. You may move this hotkey to your normal hotkey window by holding a click and dragging it into place.

    RESISTANCE: Level 51
  • Adds +10 to all resists
  • Duration of 5 minutes
  • Re-use time of 30 minutes

    FEARLESS: Level 54
  • Immunity to all Fear Attacks
  • Duration of 1 Minute
  • Re-use time of 15 minutes

    TRUESHOT: Level 55
  • Doubles Critical Hit Probability
  • Doubles Bow Damage
  • Duration of 2 minutes
  • Re-use time of 72 minutes

    WEAPON SHIELD: Level 60
  • Parries all melee attacks
  • Duration of 24 seconds
  • Re-use time of 72 minutes
