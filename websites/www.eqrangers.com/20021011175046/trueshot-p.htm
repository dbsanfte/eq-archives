THE TRUESHOT LONGBOW

 

Basic Walkthrough

 

Start in North Freeport, if you have participated in the paladin’s “Soulfire” quest, and unable to enter West Freeport safely, this could get very tricky.

 

North Freeport

Ø         Fill a backpack with “Small Lanterns”, which can be purchased at the Jade Tiger Inn.

Ø         Travel to West Freeport

 

West Freeport

Ø         Go to the 2nd floor of the Hogcaller’s Inn

Ø         Look for Lyle Windshot in one of the rooms

Ø         Give Lyle your Small Lanterns, one at a time and collect the Wooden Shards

Ø         If he gives you a “wooden heart”… Keep it, it’s used for the Rain Caller

Ø         Travel to Ocean of Tears

 

Ocean of Tears

Ø         Jump ship at the Isle of the Sisters of Erollisi

Ø         Find the Merchant in one of the Huts and get a stockpile of Kiola Nuts

Ø         Hurry back to the Boat and travel to Kaladim

 

Kaladim North

Ø         Seek out Trumpy Irontoe in his tavern

Ø         Give him 1 Kiola Nut & 1 Flask of Water

Ø         You will get a “Trumpy Tonic” in Return

Ø         Repeat until all your Kiola Nuts are gone.

Ø         Travel to Kaladim South

 

Kaladim South

Ø         Seek out Trantor Everhot, at the Everhot Forge

Ø         Hand him 2 Trumpy Tonics at a time

Ø         You will get “Dwarven Wire” in Return..  you need 2 at least

Ø         Repeat until your Tonic’s are gone

Ø         Travel to Steamfont Mountains

 

Steamfont Mountains

Ø         Around the Minotaur Caves, you will see “Rogue Clockworks”

Ø         Kill the Rogue Clockworks until you get a “Micro Servo”

Ø         May as well get a bunch while you’re here… just in case.

Ø         Travel to Kelethin

 

Kelethin

Ø         Kill Spiderlings and get a bunch of “Spiderling Silks”

Ø         See Maesyn Trueshot in the Ranger’s Guildhouse

Ø         Speak to Maesyn, follow the text, until he gives you a container

Ø         Place in the container, One of each of the following: 

Micro Servo, Dwarven Wire, Spiderling Silk & Wooden Shard

Ø         Click “Combine” and give the resulting bag to Maesyn

Ø         You will get a Treant Bowstaff in Return.

 

In your Fletching Kit, Combine the Following:

Ø         Treantwood Bowstaff

Ø         Micro Servo

Ø         Planing Tool

Ø         Dwarven Wire

 

Your Result, if your Fletching Skill was good enough, will be:

 

TRUESHOT LONGBOW  

LORE ITEM

Damage: 20
Delay: 45
Ratio: 2.25 Excellent
Range: 100
