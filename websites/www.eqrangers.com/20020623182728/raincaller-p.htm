THE ENCHANTED LONGBOW aka The Rain Caller

 

Basic Walkthrough

 

Kinool Goldsinger, in the Enchanter’s Guildhouse in South Felwithe, needs the following items to make your Enchanted Longbow:

 

Ø         Your Current Trueshot Longbow

Ø         3,000 gold pieces

Ø         Treant Heart – see the Trueshot Bow Quest

Ø         Fairie Dust

 

Above all, the Faerie Dust is the most difficult of these items to get…

and here’s how you do it.

 

Ak’Anon

Ø         Seek out Sanfyrd Featherhead, across the river from the Necro Guild

Ø         Tell him  “I am your friend”  follow the text until you receive a 10 slot box

 

Freeport – west

Ø         Outside the gates, in the newbie area, you’ll see a gnome merchant named Ping Fuzzlecutter

Ø         Purchase 10 of his “Fuzzlecutter Formulas” and place them in the box

Ø         Click “combine” and bring the resulting box back to Sanfyrd in Ak’Anon

 

Ak’Anon

Ø         Give the new box of hair tonics back to Sanfyrd and he’ll give you his Topee which he wants repaired… here comes the fun part.

 

Feerrott

Ø         Kill the Gorillas outside the Temple of Cazic Thule and loot 2  “Gorilla Hairs”

Ø         These apes have been given higher levels since the update to CT, so be careful.

 

Kedge Keep

Ø         Seek out and Kill the Golden Haired Mermaid and take her Lock of Hair.

Ø         You’ll need a good 40’s Group to do this, and it doesn’t drop all the time.

Ø         Kedge Keep is completely underwater, so you’ll need Enduring Breath

 

Freeport – west

Ø         Give Ping the 2 clumps of Gorilla Hair, Lock from the Mermaid and Sanfyrd’s Toupee

Ø         You will receive the “Mane Attraction” from Ping

Ø         Travel to Ak’Anon

 

Ak’Anon

Ø         Give Sanfyrd the Mane Attraction

Ø         You will receive a “feather” from him.

Ø         This is the wing of Princess Jolena, of the faerie court.

 

Lesser Faydark

Ø         Travel west to the Faerie Ring, you’ll see “Gearheart”, a clockwork gnome.

Ø         Wait for Princess Jolena to spawn and give her the Feather

Ø         You will receive a “Bag of Faerie Dust” in Return.

 

South Felwithe, Enchanters Guildhouse:

 

Give Kinool Goldsinger, the following items:

Ø         Your Current Trueshot Longbow

Ø         3,000 gold pieces

Ø         Treant Heart – see the Trueshot Bow Quest

Ø         Bag of Fairie Dust

 

You will receive the following:

 

RAINCALLER  

LORE ITEM MAGIC ITEM

Damage: 20
Delay: 45
Ratio: 2.25 Excellent
Range: 200

Str: +6 Dex: +6

Effect: Firestrike (5 charges)

 

*Firestrike is a 302 direct damage ‘fire based’ spell*
