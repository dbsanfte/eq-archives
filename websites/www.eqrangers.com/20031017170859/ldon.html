The Lost Dungeons of Norrath - Important Ranger Information..

Note.. Although there are items to be found at the adventure merchants which are generic, such as augment stones and lessor armors and weapons.. Any item found at an Adventure Merchant which is noted with a Dungeon catagory, must be paid for with points earned from that specific dungeon and only that dungeon.
