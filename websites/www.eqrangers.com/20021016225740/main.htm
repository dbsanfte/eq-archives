Jaggedpine Forest is here!!

That's right, you half-elf Rangers (and druids) of Karana.. you now have a place to call yourown!!

Jaggedpine Forest is entered by clicking a gem inside the bear caves of Surefall Glade.. once there, characters levels 35 and Up will be entertained by a variety of mobs to hunt.. most notably, the Darkpaw Gnolls (level 48-50?).. Griffons (45+, can be feared), Willowisps (35+, always drop GLS), Wolves*, Bears*, Treants*, Cougars*.. heck, it's like the Karana's on steriods!!

I would recommend that you visit Allakahzam's site and view some of the items dropped, quested for, etc.. there are some new Ranger/Druid (karana only!!) items available.

Good luck and Good Hunting!!

* killing these mobs will lower your faction with the residence of Jaggedpine Forest
