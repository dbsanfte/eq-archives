THE ETCHED IVY ARMOR – RANGER ONLY QUEST

Starts In:

Kithicor Woods

Req. Level:

30-60

Quest Item:

Etched Ivy Armor

 

Note:  All of these Quests can be “Chained” – meaning the items may be quested for by different people, and turned in – in succession, make sure turn in the correct items for each quest – the Ivy Runes are not marked as to who you got it from, and they cannot be used in the wrong quest…  Grimfeather’s Rune must be used for the Sleeves, etc…

 

All of the Following Items are Ranger Only – HUM, ELF, HEF – Magic Items, Lore Items

 

 

1.      ETCHED IVY TUNIC

[ AC19, Str+3, Wis+3, HP+30 ]

 

-          The Skull of Meldrath the Malignant (35) – Steamfont Minotaur Caves

-          The Ivy Rune of Thistle Underbrush (25) – Lesser Faydark

-          2 Rubies (purchased)

-          Returned to Gandari in Kithicor

 

 

 

2.      ETCHED IVY LEGGINGS

[ AC12, Agi+5, SvFire+5, SvCold+5 ]

 

-          The Quiver of Kithicor

a.       Travel to Seafury Isle in the Ocean of Tears

b.       Defeat Wiltin Windwalker – (Buccaneer Placeholder) – 90minute Spawn

 

-          Named Brownie Arrows

Acknowledgment, Bravery, Defiance, Gallantry, Fortitude and Recognition

 

a.       Travel to Lesser Fadark’s Abandonned Tower

b.       Kill Pixie Pranksters (5) and Tricksters (5) to Spawn “Outcast Brownies”

c.       Loot the Arrows from the Outcast Brownies (33)

d.       Place all 6 Arrows into the Quiver and “Combine”

 

-          The Star of Odus (purchased in Erudin)

-          Returned to Leaf Falldim in Kithicor

 

 

 

3.      ETCHED IVY HELM

[ AC12, Wis+5, SvDisease+5 ]

 

-          Charm of the Cyclops “Tarskuk” (33) in Rathe Mountains

-          Rune of Ivy from Deathly Usher (29) in Mistmoore

-          1 Sapphire (purchased)

-          Return to Gandari in Kithicor

 

 

 

4.      ETCHED IVY BRACERS

[ AC9, Dex+2, SvPoison+3 ]

 

-          Ring of Water – Aqua Goblin Tidal Lord (22) Dagnor’s Cauldron

-          Rune of Ivy  -  Redwind (27) of Everfrost Peaks

-          Fire Emerald (purchased)

-          Return to Gandari in Kithicor

 

 

 

5.      ETCHED IVY SLEEVES

[ AC10, Str+4 ]

 

-          The Heart of Fire – Inferno Goblin Wizard (26) in Solusek’s Eye

-          The Ivy Rune of Grimfeather (37) in North Karana

Warning!!  Grimfeather Flies faster than SOW and Hits HARD!!

-          2 Star Rubies (purchased)

-          Return to Gandari in Kithicor

 

 

 

6.      ETCHED IVY BOOTS

[ AC10, Stam+5 ]

 

-          Mistmoore Granite – Grinning Gargoyles (27) in Mistmoore

-          Solusek Romium (Smithing Result)

a.       Bars of Enchanted Platinum (2)

b.       Mistmoore Granite – Other than above

c.       Sands of Ro – Dropped from Sand Giants in Oasis and Ro.

d.       Melatite – Rare Drop from “CWG” (clockwork gnomes) in Solusek A

e.       Soil of Underfoot

-          Gather Pixie Dust (at least 4) – Buy it, Kill Pixies for it, whatever…

-          Give Pixie Dust, one at a time, to Priestess Ghalea in the Kaladim Cleric Guild Until she gives you the Soil of Underfoot.

-          2000 Gold Pieces

-          Return to Gandari in Kithicor

 

 

 

7.      ETCHED IVY GAUNTLETS

[ AC11, SvFire+7, SvFire+7 ]

 

-          Retrieve pieces of the Bow of Kithicor

 

-          PART I

-          Hunt Maldyn Greenburn (30) in Rathe Mountains

 

-          PART 2

-          Hunt Slaythe the Slayer (13) in Lower GUK

-          ( This is NOT the same “Slaythe” from Misty Thicket )

 

-          Returned to Leaf Falldim in Kithicor

 

Note:  It has been said that this particular quest may not be worth the trouble of traveling into Lower GUK.  Other Gloves or Gauntlets, which may be the same Quality or even Better, may be easier to obtain or purchase.
