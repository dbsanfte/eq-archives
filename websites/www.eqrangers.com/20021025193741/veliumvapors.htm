VELIUM VAPORS:  Pottery (122)

Magic Item, Lore Item

Charges: 1

Effect:  Thurgadin Gate

 

This is a Gate potion that anyone can make…

 

Unfired Velium Vial

ü        Small Brick of Velium

ü        Three (3) Crystalline Silks

ü        Two (2) Glass Shards

ü        Combine in Pottery Wheel

 

Velium Vial

ü        Unfired Velium Vial

ü        High Quality Firing Sheet

ü        Combine in Kiln

 

Find Frundle Frenkler in Thurgadin and ask her

“What Velium Vapors”

 

She will hand you a “Velium Purifier”

 

Place the Velium Vial and Two (2) stackable pieces of Velium into the purifier

Click Combine and you have your potion.

 

The Velium Vial is Not Lore, so you can have as much in stock as you wish, along with a supply of stackable velium pieces.

Once you use the potion, simply see Frundle and make another…
