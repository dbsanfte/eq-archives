AUTOFIRE UTILITY:
- Information and support can be found at EQ Hunter
- Program author is Moos Sherwood, of the Prexus Server

Installation:
- Download the zip file and extract to your Everquest Directory
- You may want to make a shortcut to your desktop for easy access.

CAUTION!!!
- Once autofire is enguaged, it will not stop once your target is dead
- You must manually turn your bow off but hitting your assigned hotkey again.

UPDATE Tuesday, January 27, 2004
- Edited the Input and Output entries so you are able to chat while autofire is working.

These instructions are how I personally got this program to work, your individual tastes may vary..

1, Run program.. setting the Input to F12, and Output to F7


2, Run Everquest.
3, Set your Ranged Slot as Hotkey number 1


4, Open your Options Window and click the tab for Keyboard, and choose Macros


5, Set macro for Hotbutton1 as number F12 with an alternate of F7.

6, Set macro for "Target Nearest PC" to *anything but F7*

You can now hit F7 for a single shot, or F12 for autofire.
You MUST hit F12 again to turn it off.

Adjust your Update Delta to syncronize your bow attack to reduce
spam of Melee Warnings (too high/low, too far, too close, etc..)
but remember, if you choose number too high, haste may throw off your timing.
