EQRangers’ Guide to Mastering the Needle

 

Mastering Tailoring is not the easiest of tasks, but was made significantly easier after the PoP release; it requires a lot of hunting and patience, and skills in other Trades.   If you don’t have a lot of time on your hands, you may not want to do this.

 

You can find a really nice Pelt and Silk drop guide at EqTraders

 

 

Level 1 though 15

§          Silk Threads (Small, Spiderlings, Hatchlings)

o         2 Spiderling Silks

o         Combine in Sewing Kit

 

§          Silk Swatch (Large, Carrion, Spiders)

o         2 Spider Silks

o         Combine in Sewing Kit

 

 

 

Level 16 through 21

§          Silk Bandages (used to “Bind Wound”)

o         2 Silk Threads

o         Combine in Sewing Kit

 

 

 

Level 22 through 26

§          Patchwork Armors

§          Each recipe requires a ruined cat (S) wolf (M) or bear (L) pelt and the appropriate pattern.

§          Usable by all classes, except pure casters.

 

 

 

 

Level 26 through 82

§          Cured Silk Armors

§          Each recipe requires the appropriate pattern, number of Silk Swatches and Heady Kiolas (brewed)

§          Monk & Beastlord Usable Armor

o         Cap (AC 4) - 1 silk swatch, 3 heady kiolas, pattern

o         Cloak (AC 4) - 2 silk swatches, 2 heady kiolas, pattern

o         Gi [tunic] (AC 8) - 3 silk swatches, 2 heady kiolas, pattern

o         Gorget (AC 3) - 1 silk swatch, 2 heady kiolas, pattern

o         Handwraps [glove] (AC 4) - 2 silk swatches, 2 heady kiolas, pattern

o         Mantle [shoulderpad] (AC 3) - 2 silk swatches, 2 heady kiolas, pattern

o         Mask (AC 2) - 1 silk swatch, 1 heady kiola, pattern

o         Pants (AC 5) - 2 silk swatches, 3 heady kiolas, pattern

o         Sandals [boot] (AC 4) - 1 silk swatch, 2 heady kiolas, pattern

o         Sash [belt] (AC 3) - 1 silk swatch, 2 heady kiolas, pattern

o         Sleeves (AC 4) - 2 silk swatches, 2 heady kiolas, pattern

o         Wristbands (AC 3) - 1 silk swatches, 2 heady kiolas, pattern

 

 

Level 82 through 115

§          Handmade Backpacks!! (88 triv)

§          10 Slot, Large Capacity, 10% Weight Reduction

o         High Quality Bear Pelt

o         Backpack Pattern

 

 

§          Tailored Quivers !! (115 triv)

§          6 Slots, Arrows Only, 10% Weight Reduction

o         High Quality Cat Pelt

o         Quiver Pattern

o         Combine in Sewing Kit
