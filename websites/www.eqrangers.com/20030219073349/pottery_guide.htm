EQRangers’ Guide to Mastering the Kiln

 

Pottery is, perhaps, the easiest Tradeskill to Master… And the Cheepest.  It is recommended that if you have the Planes of Power, get yourself to the Plane of Knowledge for all your Tradeskill needs… Otherwise, Thurgadin is the place to be.

 

Blocks of Clay are purchased, or can be hunted from Muddites in The Gorge of King Xorbb (aka Beholder’s Maze), in between Runnyeye and East Karana.

o         Skewer Sketch
