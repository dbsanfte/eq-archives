Strat - Phlashbak
    Here is a little strategy I recieved. I can't confirm this, but it sounds very very useful.

    You might already know but here it is anyways It involves casting the invisibility spell. If you cast it on someone with a pet the pet will disappear, but the person would turn invisible, so be sure to keep the person as your target because you can still attack him. Also if you have see invisible spell you can still see him. Also I believe you can cast the cancel magic spell on them to get rid of this(might have to do it a few times to get rid of other enchantments first). Also if you have the person targeted and have attack on, you will attack them if they are in front of you. There are probably many ways this can be to your advantage.

Welkome to Grobb - Phlashbak
    Here is a little trick for raising levels in Grobb. Around level 6 you can fight Froglok Prisoners that are being held in Grobb. Here is where they are located

    1. Right past the Priest of Disorder to the right.
    2. Past the first evnder to the left against the wall.
    3. 2 prisoners at the very top of the shaman guild.
    4. 3 prisoners in the tunnel located in the Shaman guild area.

    There is also one prisoner that you can talk to, and is higher level. Don't make the mistake of hitting him and having to run all the way to the zone (atleast that is what I have been told, I have never made that stupid mistake, nope not me)
