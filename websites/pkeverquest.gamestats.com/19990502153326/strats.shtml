General Advice


1. Have plenty of food & drink on hand, and be sure to not go hungry because that is sure to decrease your player killing ability.

2. Killing your races' enemies (such as Trolls are enemies with Humans) will allow you to move freely within your races' towns and you might almost become a hero to your people.

3. Jumping makes you go faster than simple running. So jump if you want to get somewhere fast.


Looting Tips


1. Since you can't loot bags, be sure to put everything you have in one. If you are going to die, try and get your weapon in there also.
