Thursday, September 19, 2002

Tuesday Concordance beat us to Vindi, so after tagging him for faction we moved to TOV for Ikiatar. This was our first try at either of the guardians, and the pull took some deaths and time to figure out, but it was fun doing it. Once we got the pull down, we lost a cleric at the start of the fight, but with 27 people still managed to beat him down a fair amount. Sadly he finally kicked us back, and we didn't have time or numbers for another attempt, but it's great to be attempting those mobs!

Wednesday no Ikiatar so we didn't get to hone our tactics more, Vindi was around (for all of two minutes). Congratulations Shaari on the Breastplate!
