May 2, 2003

GC has been going through a lot of changes in the past month. We are now a guild of about 35. We are more oriented to human relationships, having fun, and retaining the values that GC always has had. We "play how you like" in terms of doing raiding, quests, levels, tradeskills and lots of other things. About half of our folks are casual players and the other half play a lot. We are no longer a major raiding guild. Since a lot of information on our web site is incorrect (we're working on upgrading it!) please check our forums for the latest information. Applicants should look on the Applications board for where to send their applications. We want to thank the many friends of GC who have been helpful to us during this transition. If you have any questions, contact the interim Guild Leader (me) at gcxevbusiness@aol.com -- thanks.
