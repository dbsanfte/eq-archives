New Member Consideration Form

Information:

Friends of members: Our guild is not considered exclusive. However our general policy is to only recruit friends of members unless we need more of a certain class and are looking for them. If you are a friend of a guildie you are automatically qualified. If you are a friend of one of our guild members and wish to join simply ask your friend to sponsor you then have your friend ask in guild if you may join and one of our guild officers that are on will try to put you in our guild as soon as possible. This is the easiest way.

Without a guild sponsor: If you have heard of our guild through different means and currently have no friends in our guild but still wish to join, we require you be level 20 or higher, nice, polite and a social person. Our guild is very social in guildchat and prefers to hunt with guildies.

Guild Recruitment Officer: If you were referred to us by our Guild Recruitment Officer Lasterfill please read our guild policies and information and if you believe our guild is for you, please choose the Referred by GRO option in the drop down box on the recruitment form below.
