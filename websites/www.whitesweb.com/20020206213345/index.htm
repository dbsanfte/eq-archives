What we are about       

We believe in working together to accomplish our goals. We believe each member must not only be respectful to their guildmates but also to others in the game as outside actions reflect upon us as a whole. We all try to not only be guildmates but friends.

Our guild benefits include: 

Friendly Guild Members
High Exp Guild Hunting Areas
Good Loot Guild Hunting Areas
Guild Events based on level ranges
Dungeon Raids for higher level members
Experienced Officers with good in-game knowledge
Friendly & Non-Demanding atmosphere
