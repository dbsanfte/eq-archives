What we are about       

We believe in working together to accomplish our goals in a friendly non-demanding atmosphere. We believe each member must not only be respectful to their guildmates but also to others in the game as outside actions reflect upon us as a whole. We all try to not only be guildmates but friends. Rudeness and bad attitudes are not tolerated!

Our guild benefits include: 

Friendly Guild Members
High Exp Guild Hunting Areas
Good Loot Guild Hunting Areas
Guild Events based on level ranges
Dungeon/Dragon Raids for higher level members
Experienced Officers with good in-game knowledge
Friendly & Non-Demanding atmosphere
