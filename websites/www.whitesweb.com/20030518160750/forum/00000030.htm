By the way, I would also note, you do not have to be an officer to get a raid going :) If you have something specific you need to do, research it and check the Members page to see what other members are in your level range. If you miss the boat, you may need to figure out how to get there yourselves. As Saif pointed out, there are a *ton* of things to do and places to go... check the Everlore, CastersRealm, Allakhazam, etc. sites for quests / zones appropriate to your level, research them, and get some people together )
