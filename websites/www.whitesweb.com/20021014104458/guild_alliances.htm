Friends of Draconis Arcanum

 

Allied guilds of Draconis Arcanum are friends , raiding partners , hunting partners and questing allies of our guild. We will try to help them with their requests , buffs , tps and generally help these friends when we see them and the same is done in return. When you need help with a raid or quest and there isn't enough of our guild online to help you, do a /who all name_of_allied_guild and send a tell to someone in their guild of the appropriate level and ask if their guild would like to help.
