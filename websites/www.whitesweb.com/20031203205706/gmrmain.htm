Welcome to the city of GMR Deveraux!

GMR Deveraux is a city founded by the GMR Deveraux corporation in the middle of the planet Dantooine orbiting Corellia.  On the Bria server

Populous:

GMR Deveraux is a growing city housing only the finest and friendliest merchants and adventurers in the galaxy. The city monitors its population closely and only allows citizenship based on personality requirements. It is rumored that the GMR Deveraux Corporation controls the size of the populous to ensure it keeps majority votes in its favor due to the corporations heavy investment in the city. The population is almost exclusively rebel aligned and although it is not required it is greatly encouraged by the GMR Deveraux Corporation.

 

Economy:

Being the headquarters for GMR Deveraux Inc. many master crafted items can be purchased here from them at a lower cost than anywhere in the galaxy. The city specifically encourages economic and industrial growth in its citizens, highly prizing its crafting population by granting tax incentives and housing reimbursement.

 

Public Amenities:

Currently most of the resources of the city are geared towards its inhabitants. Exceptional crafting stations, open public housing and citizen accessible factories make it easy to craft and heal anywhere in the city. GMR Deveraux Inc. constructed a guildhall to make its wares more available to its citizens and is building a luxurious cantina to rest and heal weary townsfolk and travelers, many city beautifications to improve the lifestyles of its citizens, mission terminals and skill trainers. The city hopes to have a cloning facility placed sometime in the future as well as faction terminals and recruiters.

 

Travel:

Traveling to and from the city of GMR Deveraux takes awhile due to the fact it isn't on any of the planet's shuttle routes. However being located in the middle of the planetary map it is not a very long walk to or from just about anywhere.

 

Government:

The city of GMR Deveraux is primarily controlled by the GMR Deveraux Corporation and is influenced by its anti-imperial sentiments.  The corporation while benevolent maintains a dictatorship like hold on the city and fends off any of its competitors with brutal force. The opinions of GMR Deveraux Inc. are expressed through their spokesman and one of the founding members Gaborra who holds the recurring position of mayor over the city.
