Guild Raid on Permafrost

 

11/28/01 -- The all levels raid on permafrost went a little ruff this weekend with massive casualties for our side. The extreme range in levels made it difficult for healers and spellcasters to heal the low levels from hits from high level mobs and heal the high levels from fighting their mobs. It cost too much mana for the high level casters to maintain all the groups. However this was not a disaster. This was a predicted result from experimenting with a raid which all levels were allowed to participate in. As results from this raid would show Toryen will now be organizing guild raids based on level ranges.
