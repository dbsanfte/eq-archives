Bard Epic

Baldric Slezaf in the Dreadlands old Combine city is the start and end of the quest. He wishes to play a symphony like no other, but needs a few things:

1) The top half of Page 24 of a composition by "The Maestro"
2) The bottom half of Page 24 of the same composition
3) Page 25 of the same composition
4) Mystical Lute made by Forpar Fizfla in Steamfont Hills
Turning in all four items to Baldric results in the Bard epic weapon:
SINGING SHORT SWORD
1HS
16/26
Wgt 2.0
+10 Mana +100 HP +20 Cha +15 Str +10 Dex +5 Sta +10 All resists
Effect: Dance of the Blade (must be 50+ for proc to work) Increase attack speed by 55%, +30 str, +30 Attack. Duration is 2.5mins and will effect all party members in a 150' radius.

Also when sword is held in primary hand it will boost all songs used (see eqsongs.com for details.)

The Top Half of Page 24


The top half of Page 24 can be obtained by running a relay race for a bard (Konia Swiftfoot) in West Karana. Konia can be found in a Guard Tower near Qeynos Hills.
Konia Swiftfoot in WK asks you to deliver torches for her. You must run to Misty Thicket and hand it to Fajio Knejo right at the wall. Then run to South Ro zone entrance to swamp and pass it to Andad Filla and finally run to Lake Rathe near Arena zone where you get Proof of speed. Bring back to Konia and she only gives you the top half of the page

Reward: Top half of Page 24.


The Bottom Half of Page 24


Konia will keep the bottom half of Page 24 because it contains a signature and has a high value. She will trade it for a Mahlin's Bongos.

Go to Baenar in South Karana. The trigger for this quest is "What Doll?", but "Real" also pulls up some more information. - Bring tissues

Take the Invoice he gives you to the Tinkerer's Bag salesman in Sol A.

Take the mechanical doll he gives you to Serra in Unrest, found next to the Spelunker.

Take the note she gives you back to Baenar.

Take the note he gives you to Maligar in West Karana. (1245, -10900). Maligar will more than likely be KOS. Use charm, DE Illusion, other illusion, or pull and kill the surrounding mobs and use sneak to turn in the note.

Maligar and Maligar's enraged doppleganger will attack. Kill both and loot Doppleganger's Head.

Give head to Baenar, receive Mahlins Bongos.

Return Bongos to Konia for the bottom half of page 24

Reward: Bottom half of Page 24.


Page 25


Page 25 is received from Kelkim Menkia (Found along the Eastern zone wall) in South Karana. She has broken her lute and needs new strings for it. The strings are made of the following:

Chromodrac guts (Eldig the Old, Skyfire).

Red wurm guts (Nezekezena and/or Phurzikon, Burning Woods)

Huge onyx drake guts (Blackwing, Rathe Mountains)

Reward: Page 25.


Mystical Lute


Forpar Fizfla (Found in one of the Windmills) in Steamfont does not like adventurers, and will not speak to anyone unless you visit his bard friend, Vedico Windwisper, at the Butcherblock docks and help her retrieve her horn by killing Quag Maelstrom in OOT. Loot the NO DROP Horn and return it to Vedico. She will give you a note to give to her friend Forpar Fizfla. Forpar will then offer to make you the Mystical Lute.

Step one:
Head and Neck of Mystical Lute A backbone of an ancient fishman (Phinigel Autropos, Kedge Keep)

A strong tentacle from one of the long lost amalgyms (Amygdalan Tendril-Amygdalan Warrior, Plane of Fear)

A petrified skull of a lycanthrope (Drolvarg Warlord, Karnor's Castle)

The note he gives you or he might forget who you are.
Reward: Head and Neck of Mystical Lute (one piece).

Step Two:
Body of Mystical Lute
Red Dragon Scale (Nagafen, Talendor, or Ragefire)

White Dragon Scale (Vox or Gorenaire)

"Some" Metal Bits (1 Smithed Metal Bits)
Reward: Body of Mystical Lute.
Final Step:
Mystical Lute
a. Undead Dragon Guts (Trakanon, Old Sebilis)
b. The completed Head and Neck.
c. The completed Body.
Reward: Mystical Lute.
