Longsword of Ethereal Energy

This is the quest for the Longsword of Ethereal Energy. It's a sweet sword, and I suggest you all go do this easy quest now, before Verant nerfs it.

1) The quest starts at Katta Castleum. Speak to Yurrdle The Caretaker inside the crypt. On your way inside, be careful to stay on the path, if you touch the grass, undead will spawn that the guards will not help with. Ask Yurddle about grave robbers to see if he'll give you the quest.

2) Go to Shadow Haven. Inside the magic guild, go up the left stairwell, and into the first hall on the left. Inside the second door in the hallway, you will find Pietro Guccini, a High Elf. Give him 1000 gold (not 100 plat) and you will recieve a portrait.

3) Go to Paludal Caverns. At loc -1424, 1374 a named bandit Maricella Slithra spawns. She is on a 12 minutes spawn clock, with a recondite bandit as a placeholder. This is a long camp. It took me 3 hours, and a friend 4 hours. She is not an uncommon spawn, but the drop you need is. Kill her until you get an Ornate Ancient Combine Dagger.

4) Head for Fungus Grove. Inside the outpost, step into the bar and you'll find a Half-Elf named Ebben Quickblade. This is the toughest mob in the entire quest. He's level 40, but a pretty weak 40. Just kill him there, none of the other patrons will defend him. Get the Antique Platinum Medal he drops.

5) Go to Twilight Sea. On the island with the dock, adjacent to the castle proper, spawns three named NPCs. The one you want is Fierdo, the Halfling. He cons blue to a level 39. Unfortunately, his friends jump you when you attack him. Once you kill him, loot the Ring off his corpse.

6) Bring all 4 items to Yurdle the Caretaker. He gives you a box and a ghost spawns to your left. He is invisible, except for his name. Give the box to this ghost, and you'll recieve your longsword.

1hs, 16/29. +5 Magic Damage -5 STR, -5 STA, +12 DEX, +10 Magic Save, +15 AC, +2 Ability mod: Divination.

Sweet sword for how simple the quest is.

Armadon Dupree-Rallic Backpack Walkthrough

(this is one of the few quests actually written by our guild)

You need atleast a 20 tailoring to attempt this quest.

Ok this quest involves these pieces

Grimling Runner Pack (no drop pack off grimling runner in Tenebrous Mountains)

Perfect Owlbear Pelt (rare drop off owlbears, there is a cave in Hollowshade Moor that has plenty of these, odds for a perfect pelt are 1 n 30)

1 Shadeling silk swatch (combine 2 shadeling silks in tailor kit)

1 Shadeling silk cord (combine 3 shadeling silk swatches in tailor kit)

(shadeling silks can be found in Shadoweavers Thicket zone off lesser shades and those black worms)

Rallic backpack pattern (you get this from turning grimling runner pack into Rallic, Rallic is in the large church in Katta, through the main door, down a hall to your left and in a room to your left , behind a door)

Combine : Rallic backpack pattern, Perfect owlbear pelt, shadeling silk swatch, and shadeling silk cord in a tailoring kit (you need a 20 tailoring skill or higher to combine)

combine those items in any large tailor kit and poof

you can sell or trade these for about 2000pp currently
