10/9/2002 7pm CST Raid Initiator/Leader: Daolin <DA> & Phalnobor <EG>

Minimum Level: 45

Time Required: 5 hours

Number of People Needed: 30min to 42max

Location(s): Plane of Fear

Objectives: Infiltrate plane of fear and clear all mobs with the possible exception of Cazic Thule. 

Loot Rules: NBG <Need Before Greed> , Drops will be given to highest level DA or EG member that needs them the most. After NBG item is rewarded everyone will roll for the item that was replaced by NBG item, including the person who got the NBG item. Daolin or Phalnobor will decide who needs which NBG items the most. Items of low value or items that can be used by a large range of classes will be /random 0 420 rolled for.
