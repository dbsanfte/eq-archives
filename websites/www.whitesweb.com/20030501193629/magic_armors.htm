Bracer of the Reverant

 

To obtain the quest say to Gavel the Temperant: "Tell me about the Reverant"

Gavel says: "Regis the Reverent fell in love with a gypsy girl named Lianna Ferasa who lives in the Rathe Mountains. Ask her what has become of him. Bring me the Ingot of the Reverent, the Icon of the Reverent, and 2 Enchanted Platinum Bars and I will forge them into a reward for you."

1. Ingot of the Reverant (Kill Zahal in North Karana to spawn the corpse of Regis who drops the Ingot and a flute)

2. Icon of the Reverant (Give Lianna Ferasa (Rathe Mountains) the flute and she will give you the Icon)

3. Two enchanted bars of platinum

 

Chestplate of the Constant

 

To obtain the quest say to Gavel the Temperant: "Tell me about the Constant"

Gavel says: "Althuryn the Constant was brutally slain by two aqua goblins. The one called Sludge fled to Runnyeye, the one called Bilge lives in Dagnors Cauldron. Bring me the Ingot of the Constant, the Icon of the Constant, and 2 Galvanized Platinum Bars and I will forge them into a reward for you."

1. Ingot of the Constant (Kill Sludge in Runnyeye.  He can be found by going down to the river between the Warlord and King rooms, go left underwater.  He's at the end of the underwater tunnel.)

2. Icon of the Constant (Kill Bilge in Dagnor's Cauldron.  He's a mid 30s shaman, has SoW, and is heavily camped due to his other drops (Kedge Totem - +18 Wis, and the Reed Belt - +5 Wis).)

3. Two Galvanized bars of platinum (For each Enchanted platinum bar you hand Genni you will receive one Galvanized bar.)

Shield of the Devout

To obtain the quest say to Gavel the Temperant: "Tell me about the Devout"

Gavel says: "Nebbletob the Devout was once a slave in the mines of Meldrath, the Minotaur Sentry there was particularly cruel to him. He was in the expedition to Everfrost when Brother Theo drown. When trying to rescue Theo he came upon some caves under the river and narrowly escaped death when a great white beast attacked him. He never saw what the beast was, but it shredded his pack where he kept his Icon. Bring me the Ingot of the Devout, the Icon of the Devout, and 2 Vulcanized Platinum Bars and I will forge them into a reward for you."

1. Ingot of the Devout (Dropped by Minotaur Sentries in the Minotaur Caves in Steamfont)

2. Icon of the Devout (Dropped by the giant Glacial Bear under the river in Everfrost Peaks)

3. Two Vulcanized bars of platinum (For each Enchanted platinum bar you hand Villisia you will receive one Vulcanized bar)

 

Boots of the Reliant

 

To obtain the quest say to Blaize the Radiant: "Tell me about the Reliant"

Blaize says: "Turgan the Reliant lived in a cabin in the Kithicor woods until there was a great fire. Searching the burned cabins there may turn something up. He always loved Lake Rathetear as well. Bring me the Ingot of the Reliant, the Icon of the Reliant, and 1 Enchanted Platinum Bar and I will forge them into a reward for you."

1. Ingot of the Reliant (StumpWatch '99 -- The ingot spawns on top of a stump in Kithicor Forest.  To get on top of the stump run up the log next to it and jump.  The /loc of the stump is (-90,3300).)

2. Icon of the Reliant (CabinWatch -- A diary spawns in a burned out cabin in Kithicor Forest.  The /loc of the cabin is (1700,1100).  Give the Diary to Turgan in Lake Rathetear who will give you the icon.)

3. One enchanted bar of platinum

Crafted Armor

All crafted is gotten from 2 centaurs in South Karana in the centaur camp, named Skakm and Ulan Meadowgreen. Here's what's available from each:

Skakm: Helm, Boots, Gauntlets, and Vambraces

Helm-14ac, 6wt, +5 cha, +5 wis; For this you need a Fire Totem off of the goblin shamans in Solusek A and 2 rubies . 

Boots-12ac, 6,5 wt, +5 agi; This requires a Frost Totem from the goblin shamans in Permafrost on the northern side of Antonica (next to Halas) and 2 sapphires.

Gauntlets-12ac, 5 wt, +5 str; You need an aviak charm off the avocets in South Karana (they have their own city so you should find plenty of them there), and 2 star rubies.

Vambraces-12ac, 6.5 wt, +2 sv disease, +2 sv poison, +1 str; These are a little tougher to get than the gauntlets; you have to kill a griffon (lvl 31-33) for a griffon eye (ONLY drops off griffons contrary to some other stories), and 2 fire emeralds.


Now for Ulan's pieces-Bracers, Breastplate, Greaves, and Pauldrons

Bracers-10ac, 4 wt, +2 dex, +2 int; For this you need a bunch of optic nerves off the Evil Eyes in the gorge, East Karana, or Runnyeye (any of the zones will have these), and 1 sapphire. Most of the eyes are only around lvl 24-25, so you can handle this pretty easily provided you have a high sv magic to avoid getting charmed.

Breastplate-22ac, 10 wt, +5 sta, 25hp; For this you need a Werewolf Talon (or claw) from any werewolf (try Mistmoore at night , all the gyspies turn into werewolves, and 3 rubies. 

Greaves-14ac, 7.5 wt, +2 sv fire, +2 sv cold, +1 str; You need the stolen circlet off a necro named trizdain (sp?) in Najena up in the Lavastorm Mtns, and 2 star rubies (different from rubies).


Pauldrons (shoulders)-12ac, 4.5 wt, +2 sv magic, +2 str; For this you need the 3 pieces of the "Rune of Dwarves" and a fire emerald. The Rune of Dwarves pieces drop off the Evil Eye prisoners in Runnyeye I believe (sometimes might drop off of gobs in RE, but I haven't seen that happen yet).

Ivy Etched Armor

All of the Ivy Etched Armor Quests begin with one of two NPC's Leaf Falldim or Gandari who are found at the Rangers Guild in the Kithicor Forest.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ivy Etched Helm
To complete the quest for the Ivy Etched Helm you will need to obtain the following three items.
A) One Top of the Rune of Ivy (from a deathly usher ) in Mistmoore
B) A Cyclops charm from Tarskuk (a named cyclops in the Rathe Mts.)
C) One Saphirre
Ivy Etched Helm is 12AC +5Wisdom +5svDisease 3.0wt

Ivy Etched Sleeves
To complete the quest for the Ivy Etched Sleeves you will need to obtain the following items
A) Heart of Fire (from an inferno goblin wizard in Solusek aka Sol A)
B) Left Rune of Ivy from Grimfeathers (named NPC griffon in the Northern Plains of Karanas)
C) Two star Rubies (Can be purchased in Firiona Vie)
Evy Etched Sleeves are 10AC +4Strength 3.7wt

Ivy Etched Bracer
To complete the quest for one Ivy Etched Bracer you will need to obtain the following items
A) The bottom of the Rune of Ivy from Redwind (a named orc in Everfrost)
B) A watery ring from a tidal lord in Dagnor's Cauldron
C) A Fire Emerald.
Ivy Etched Bracer is 9AC +2Dex +3svPOIS 2.3wt

Ivy Etched Gloves
To complete the quest for the Etched Ivy Gloves you will need to obtain the two parts of Morin's Bow
A) Retrieve part A of Morin's Bow from Maldyn the Unkempt (a fallen ranger) his faction is with outcast brownies, and Unkempt Druids. Maldyn is the rarest spawn in the Rathe Mts. and is on a strange spawn cycle that goes thru "a drake, the generic place holder, Hasten Bootstrutter, Brother Zephyl, and Maldyn the Unkempt" I have read that Maldyn will usually appear after Hasten has been killed, and that, Hasten usually pops after 3-5 drakes. Complicating matters even more is that fact that Maldyn is a roaming NPC. I have read reports that his place holder is at the round rock near the Freerot zone near where the drake and Hasten usually spawn. After Maldyn spawns he will then proceed North towards the Druid ring. Of further note Maldyn is KOS to Giants and Cyclopse, and often the Giants kill him, causing the players camping him much frustration.
B) Retrieve part B of Morin's Bow from Slaythe the slayer, a froglok NPC found deep within the Lower Guk on the Live Side. Turn in both parts of the bow to Leaf Falldim and you will recieve the nice resist gear gloves.
Ivy Etched Gloves 11AC +7svCOLD +7svFIRE.

Ivy Etched Tunic
To comlete the quest for the Ivy Etched Tunic you will need to obtain the following
A) The Skull of Meldrath (in the mino cave in the Steamfont Mts.)
B) Right side of the Rune of Ivy from Thistle Underbrush (named brownie in the Lesser Faydark)
C) Two Rubies
Ivy Etched Tunic is 19AC +3STR +5WIS +30HP 6.0wt

Ivy Etched Leggings
To complete the quest for the Ivy Etched Leggings you will need to obtain the following
A) Star of Odus (purchase in Erudin jewlery Shop)
B) The Quiver of Kithicor from Wiltin the Theif (a very rare spawn [long camp], found in the OOT found on Seafury Isle) Wiltin Windwalker's place holder is the Buccaneer who can be found at the small camp of pirates where the tents are set up around the campfire. Once the Buccaneer is killed the next spawn time is 45 min to an hour.
C) Six Named Arrows from the Brownie Outcasts in the Lesser Faydark. Names of the six NO DROP arrows are; acknowledgement, bravery, defiance, gallantry, fortitude, and recognition.
*additional information* Once the six named arrows have been obtained combine them in the Quiver. Then return the combined quiver and the Star of Odus to Leaf Falldim.
Ivy Etched Leggings are 12AC +5AGI +5svFIRE +5svCOLD 4.2wt

Ivy Etched Boots
To complete the quest for the Ivy Etched Boots you will need to obtain the following
A) Ronium
B) Two pieces of Mistmore Granite from the gargoyles in MM. (one piece of granite is used to make Ronium [see below], the second piece should be given to Leaf Falldim, when the Bar of Ronium and the gold is turned in)
C) 2000 gold (very encumbersome)

*Additional Information* The Bar of Ronium is not a drop item, but rather is a side quest.
To Make Ronium you will need to do the following
1) Obtain the mold from Lord Searfire [Dwarven Smith], in the Temple of Solusek Ro. He must consider you amiably before he will deal with you. To raise faction with him you can either hunt the shadowed men or you can give gold 30gp at a time (very expensive) to an Erudite NPC in the Southern Desert of Ro until he cons amiable to you. Once your faction is high enough return to the Temple of Solusek Ro, and hail the Dwarven Smith and then say "What Ronium" and then he will give you the sol cauldron, and then he will tell you the ingredients that you must acquire to make the metal ronium.

The ingredients to make Ronium are the following
a)Melatite (Dropped by MB and MC Clockworks in Solusek's Eye aka Sol A) sometimes you can buy this from the gnomes in Sol A because many players sell them to the NPC's. This will prevent you from taking any faction hits with Ak'Anon
b) Two Bars of Enchanted Platinum [side note] you can buy the Platinum Bars in the upstairs of the Blue building in North Freeport, then hire an enchanter of at least lvl 24 to enchant the bars for you using their enchanter's enchant platinum spell
c) One Mistmoore Granite (Pieces of Mistmoore Granite can be found on Fouting Gargoyles, Jeering Gargoyles or Leering Gargoyles in Mistmoore). The granite is a rare drop off the Gargoyles outside of the castle, but is a common drop on the Gargoyles inside of the castle.
d) The Sands of Ro (from Sand Giants in the Oasis of Mar)
e) The Soil of Underfoot (Get from the Priestess Ghalea in the Kaladim cleric's guild for 4 fairy dusts [need at least amiable faction for this], you can raise your faction with Priestess Ghalea by turning in skeleton bones (lot and lots of them), to Gunlok Jure an NPC dwarf in the paladin's guild in North Kaladim. Also remember not to kill faeries because you will need good faction with them later to complete the Epic quest to Enchant your Trueshot longbow into the Raincaller. I would advise buying the Fairy Dust or hiring another character to kill the fairies

Once all of the ingredients have been obtained place them in the cauldron and hit the combine button and you will get one bar of ronium. You do not need any blacksmithing skill to combine these. Turn in the bar of Ronium, with the Mistmore granite, and the 2000 gp to Leaf Falldim to recieve the boots.
Ivy Etched Boots are 10AC +5STA 3.6wt

Silent Watch Shield

I'm posting this for one reason and one reason only. All the other walkthroughs about this quest that I've read have been lacking in what might be considered some rather important areas. So, I'll start at the top.

In order to do this quest you have to be considered kindly by the Clerics/Paladins of Tunare. The easiest way to do this is to do the muffin quest in FP.

While in FP turn in orc picks four at a time to Hallard in Hallard's Resales until he gives you High Hold Keep lotto ticket #16568.

Next thing you want to do is to collect the following:
1 Sands of Ro - Found on Sand Giants in the Deserts of Ro.
3 Faerie Dusts - Found on faeries in both Lesser Faydark and Greater Faydark.

Next head to South Qeynos and talk to the woman in under the canopy tent in front of the Lion's Mane Inn. She sells a Silk Evening Gown, purchase one, cost is around 5pp.

Now you need to head off to HighPass Hold. Go to the Golden Rooster and up to the second floor. Follow the hall upstairs and you'll go around a curve, it'll be the last door on your left. During the daytime the gypsy Wyle Brimlin is inside of this room, wait until after dark and Marlin Shirtov will take her place. Give the silk evening tunic to him and he'll give you a brass shackle key.

Next, head into High Hold Keep and give lotto ticket #16568 to Treasurer Lynn, she's in the room behind the Jailer and the Banker, she'll give you a royal suite key.

Now, head up the third floor and open the locked door with your new key. Inside is Tearon Bleanix. Just a word of caution so you don't jump out of your skin like I did, he's a skeleton. Tell Tearon that "All is not bright above the clouds" and he will give you Tearon's Bracer.

Now head up the fourth floor and go into the hidden area behind the painting that you face when you come off of the stairs, in this area you will find Tyranna Slil, she's a DE with a whip. She'll pop in the room next to the guard with the table and overturned chairs. Give her some time and she'll move into the other room, by herself. Just a note here. This hidden area is considered "outdoors" which means that a druid can use all of their spells, including Harmony. When you kill her she drops a Fish Food Container. Put the 1 Sands of Ro, and the 3 Faerie Dusts (Unstacked), into this container and combine it to get Fish Food.

For the next part, a tracker is a MUST. Go to Lake Rathetear and find "a royal fish", it can be found swimming against the shores in varius places, including around locations: 3600, 2500; 2500, 2200; and occasionally swimming against a little island at 1450, 1900. Give the fish the food and Princess Lenya Thex will spawn at the aforementioned island.

Now, the Princess likes to swim, she will swim from that little itty bitty island South until she hits the edge of the zone, and then teleport back to that little island. My suggestion is to sit on the south side of the island and watch underwater for her to pop, as soon as she does, hail her and she'll stop swimming. Give her the brass shackle key and Tearon's bracer and she'll give you an amulet of Thex.

Now, trot yourself back to High Hold Keep and go talk to Tearon again. Now, what you'll want to do is open his door, step inside and give him the Amulet of Thex, then leaving the trade window up, step OUT of the room and put the suite key into the trade window and hit Give. He'll proceed to give you the Silent Watch Shield. The reason you step out is that if that door closes, you're stuck.

Now to clear up some myths about this shield. No, having it on your back doesn't allow you to bash when using a 2hs. And yes, it is a light source equivelant to if not better than a Greater Lightstone, for us poor blind folks.

Okay, that's my story and I'm sticking to it. It took me awhile to do this quest and alot of frustration as sometimes Lenya will spawn and immediately despawn. So be careful and if that happens, just petition it. Also, Lenya will stick around for awhile until you give her the items or someone/thing kills her.
