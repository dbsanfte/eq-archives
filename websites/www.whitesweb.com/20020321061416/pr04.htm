Deadside Guk unorganized raid

Small Guild Raid on Deadside Guk

A great start with a bad ending.

12-15-01 - This unplanned guild raid on Deadside Guk seemed perfect at first. 5 of us started in on the this part of the dungeon. Although we tried to invisibility to undead past most of them we wound up fighting them off instead. This turned out to be no problem as we worked our way further and further. We stopped by the Gargoyle room to pickup a carapiece for one of our members quests. After getting the piece of the first 3 the pit stretched before us. Deciding to take the alternate way down we headed to the right to try and locate the stairs. However our group fell down the hole not far from the passage and dropped all the way to the bottom level of Guk. There were a few decent items down here that we could use so we headed for the executioners room. We started fighting our way through the maze of stone walls and undead frogloks. When we were almost to the room one of the undead froggies we pulled ran the long around the maze to get to us and pulled a large group of froggies with more on the way. The evac started almost immediately. We all appeared in Butcher Block Mountains, a little ruffed up but all alive. As more members were now on we decided to turn this into a legitimate raid. Members started joining up and by the time we got back to Deadside we had 2 full groups of powerful nukers, healers and crowd control. The groups pressed on in Guk blasting everything to dust before the mobs really got to hit. Then there it was again. The Pit. The pit almost mythically calling out to the stronger members of the group, daring them to defy death. Everyone agreed to face the challenge and they dove fearlessly into the pit. The first wave of froggies was met with a barrage of high damage AoE spells and nukes bringing the froggies corpses raining to the ground. However the froggies were not to be underestimated, 2 large waves of froggies seemed to pour out of nowhere and start beating on the mana deficient groups. Evacs started across all the groups but the determined froggies managed to interrupt all of them. Doom closed in on us all. The main wizard and enchanter went down with blinding speed. Those that could manage it gated out, yet most stayed to their bloody death in the bowls of the Pit. Hopefully this has convinced all but the strongest of us to stay away from the god forsaken Pit.
