Foundation Rules
Guild Charter:
“Draconis Arcanum” is a fellowship of Individuals dedicated to exploring and adventuring throughout Norrath.
Guildmaster:
Monarc Amir – Also known as the “Big Bad”
Structure:
Officers are held in the highest esteem and can forecast events, invite/kick, individuals at their discretion. They have complete authority in Guild matters.
Officiership:
Once the initial birth of the guild has passed you cannot ask to be an officer, the HONOR will be bestowed upon you. The criteria is level 45 and a reasonable term of service with the guild, or an original member of the Protectors of the Unknown with whom I or an officer is acquainted (They have a standing invitation)?

 **Depending on the size of the guild there may be two lowbie officers to run lowbie events.
