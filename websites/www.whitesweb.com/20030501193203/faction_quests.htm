Freeport Militia & Corrupt Queynos Guards

Freeport Militia, Corrupt Qeynos Guards
OK PEOPLE, I will tell you the best way to improve faction with the Freeport Militia, AND NOT TAKING ANY FACTIONS HITS! First, go to High Pass Hold. There are orcs at the entrance coming in from Kithicor Forest, they drop orc scalps. You can loot these, and also buy them from the people camping this spawn. After you have collected alot, go see Captain Ashlan on the bridge. Turn them into him, and you will gain faction with the Freeport Militia, Corrupt Qeynos Guards, High Pass Guards, and a couple others. NO FACTION HITS!!
The other way to improve faction with the Freeport Militia without taking any hits is to loot left goblin ears in Highhold Keep. Find the bank, then hang a left, and go down to the basement. Goblins are there, ranging in level from 22-35. Kill goblins, loot ears. People will also sell them to you. Turn them into Captain Bozec, at the top of the stairs. This will improve faction with the same exact people as the orc scalp quest... taking NO HITS!!!
Or kill Knights of Truth.

Knights of Truth

KNIGHTS OF TRUTH
If you don't care about your faction standing with Opal Darkbriar or Dismal Rage, here is a way to improves faction with the Knights of Truth. Locate the Hall of Truth in North Freeport. To the left just before you hit the bridge to the Hall of Truth, there are pirahnas. Jump in the water and kill them. Loot the fresh pirahna. On top of the roof on the Hall of Truth, hail around, somebody will give you a bag to put them in. Once the bag is filled with four fresh pirahna, hand it in.
Or you can do what most people do, and kill orcs.
If you don't care about your faction with the Freeport Militia, keep a look out for people killing guards in West Freeport. They will usually let you loot the damaged militia helms, which drop of the guards, and are no-drop. Turn these into Theron something on top of the Hall of Truth. (hail around, i'm don't remember exactly)
Or you can kick a guard being pulled to the tunnel in the east commons, this won't taunt them bad enough, when he dies, you get faction.

Queynos Guards

QEYNOS GUARDS
Quite simple to improve faction. You can kill Karana bandits, or gnolls in Blackburrow. Alot. There are other easy ways to improve faction. Wandering around in the docks side of Qeynos, there are two guards. One wants muffins, the other water. Give one muffins, and the other water. This will hurt Corrupt Qeynos Guards faction, though.

Felwithe Bone Chips

The Cleric Guildmaster in felwithe asks you to bring him 4 bone chips (I think), which are easily obtainable from the skeletons out in greater faydark. Return them to him for some faction, and a minor item (I got a low-level cleric spell, which I sold on).

Temple of Ro Faction

Ortallis is an orc. If you zone in from Oasis, hang a right, walk along the zone border until the zone makes the corner, and the orc and his merchant will be near. The tent is a one-man tent, and an erudite merchant sits in there, his name is Rathmana Allin.

You can hail him (I'm a wood elf, thus a good race, don't know if evils can do it) and he gives you a couple of things you can do, but one in particular, he asks you to give him 30gp and you can reach into his bag and get a spell. So, give him 3pp and you'll get exp, an item (which I let fall to the ground) and sol ro faction.

I was dubious to those guys in the temple, it cost me 21pp (7 tries) to get indifferent with Rathmana AND teh temple of solusek ro. At indifferent I could do the quests.

Shadowhaven Faction

NOTE: This is a quest on Luclin, but the Luclin zones are not listed as options on Everlore yet.

This quest begins and ends in Shadow Haven, with a short trip to Dawnshroud Peaks in the middle.
Find Mistala Glimmerblade in Shadow Haven:

You say, 'Hail, Mistala Glimmerblade'

Mistala Glimmerblade says 'Hello there friend! I am Lady Mistala Glimmerblade of the House of Fordel I reside over this great city of Shadowhaven along with my husband Daloran. I take great pride in knowing that our city has come so far and became a place of free trade and freedom for all of its inhabitants. We also are lucky to have some of the finest guardspersons in the land. Which reminds be I need to collect my [report].

You say, 'what report?'

Mistala Glimmerblade says 'I have a report that I collect every few days from my first Lieutentant Broansas. This report contains information about each guard his performance and usually notes on suggestions upon how to limit the number of smugglers passing through our town. However, Lieutenant Broansas is currently on assignment investigating a smugglers ring. I need someone to retrieve his reports of the latest information that he gathered for me. Will you [retrieve the daily reports]?

You say, 'i will retrieve the daily reports'

Mistala Glimmerblade says 'Many thanks friend. The last I heard from Broansas he was heading for the Dawnshroud peaks please seek him out there and present him with this notorized letter so that he will know that I have sent you. Return to me with his report as soon as you can. I await your return.

Take the note to the Dawnshroud Peaks. Lieutenant Broansas is located on a hill on the western side of the zone. Give him the note, and recieve his journal. Take the journal back to Mistala for experience (not noticeable at 43) and a faction boost with Shadow Haven.

After doing this quest once (you can do it multiple times) and the Trade Commissioner Henry quest once, the merchants in Shadow Haven would deal with me.
