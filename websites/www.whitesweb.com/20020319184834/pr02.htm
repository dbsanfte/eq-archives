Guild Raid on Kesora

High level raid wipes out the dungeon Kesora.

12/05/01 -- The high level guild raid on the dungeon Kesora was like shooting a cannon at a straw house. For one, more high levels showed up then was originally intended. We had atleast 2 full groups with levels ranging from 35 to 52. From the moment we walked into Kesora we owned the whole dungeon. Anything that got in our way got mowed over instantly. We ended it off camping for 30 percent weight reduction bags which half of us got one and kinda relaxed for the rest of the night after a job well done. This raid was a great success.
