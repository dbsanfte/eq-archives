CHARTER 'the Order of the Gauntlet'

The Order of the Gauntlet solemnly vows to do everything in her power to seek out and destroy the powers of Chaos and turn it into Order. Set up as a guild where honourly virtues are held in the highest regard, we will help our brethren and sisters in the guild to the best of our capacity. Besides that, our services into combatting Chaos are for hire to any Order-oriented guild or freeman in the world of Norrath. Quests to further guild improvement or ranking are important to us and will be given as much aid as possible.

RULES for members

Members and the Gods

 A member shall respect all deities within the Pantheon.
A member respects the Balance between Good and Evil deities.
A member shall use the gifts of his/her God to further the cause of Order and Balance.


Members and the Order of the Gauntlet

 A member shall show respect to fellow brethren and sisters
A member shall respect the ranks within the Order of the Gauntlet
A member shall attend all guild meetings:
This includes Guild Meetings, New Member Meetings, Hearings, Guild Quests, and Guild Wars
Exceptions to attend Guild Meetings or New Member Meetings can only be made when other events take precedence 1)
A member shall aid fellow brethren and sisters when it is requested:
if such aid is feasable - meaning you have the skill to give aid or you are in the vicinity
if it does not compromise other rules
A member shall not take undue advantage of the generosity of fellow brethren and sisters
A member must report any offenses to the rules to his direct leader or to the Guild Leader as soon as possible
1) Meaning: events in Real-Life. You will not be excused if you are in the Game.


Members and the Citizens of Norrath

 A member must offer his aid to all citizens of Norrath
A fee may be asked for this aid
Unless the aid is not withing the abilities of the individual member
Unless it compromises one or more of the rules
A member must come, if so asked for, to battle-aid of a citizen in problems
Unless it endangers fellow brethren
Unless it compromises one of the rules
Subpoint a. can be voided if redress has been agreed to beforehand in the form of cash or products
A member shall not take vengeful or retributive action against those deranged citizens who have wronged him
Unless this is in the scope of a special event or guild war
A member will always take time to give information about the Order of the Gauntlet when asked for from a citizen


General Roleplaying and in-Game rules

 Unless otherwise required (by virtue of the Guild Head or Order Leaders) every member must roleplay in aspects that are externally visible (e.g. emotes, /say, /shout, /guildsay). Out of character discussions may only take place via /group and /ooc.
Every member must make a short bio to be submitted to the Ceremony Master for inclusion in our Book of Brethren and Sisters
we will adhere to the Common Law
When hired for services by a freeman or a guild, the member must truthfully inform his direct leaders about the nature of the service and the agreed price. If a price is in cash, the member must submit a 5% tax to the treasury of the guild.
Services that require four or more members of the guild to complete must be passed by the direct leader (or interim) of the members for approval.
