the Order of the Gauntlet

dressed in armor
dressed as knights
following the Bishop
to heaven's delight

as man and woman
with honour and respect
fighting the chaos
with no neglect

setup as brotherhood
setup as sisterhood
being strong together
in surviving mood

structured as a gauntlet
with fingers different
being strong together
strong in structured difference
