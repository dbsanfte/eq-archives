Robe of Elements
The Bishop has a very humble quest right now, and that is to get a new robe for himself. Since he is now representing a group of formidable people, he needs to dress a bit better than the raven-black he is wearing now. In time, he has to extend his wardrobe significantly, but to start it of he is looking for a so-called 'Robe of the Elements'.

He spoke with a Lady in the Temple of Ro, who told him she could make a robe for him if only he brings her:

1. a Frost Goblin Skin; After a successful adventure in Permafrost with many of the guild members, Udaai managed to get his Frost Goblin Skin .
2. a Fire Goblin Skin; The Fire Goblin Skin is supposed to be on one of the goblins in Solusek A, you know, the ones that chant "Die by the Lava, Die by the Flame, Fire Peak goblins kill and maim ". Hmmppppf.... " Die by a sword, die by a spell, the Gauntlet slays you very well!" *cackle*. Udaai is lurking around Solusek A now, as you can imagine.
3. a twicewoven cloak
