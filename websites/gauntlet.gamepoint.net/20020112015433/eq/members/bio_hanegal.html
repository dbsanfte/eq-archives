Hanegal Thunderflite

Race: Human
Profession: Wizard, Leader of the Order of the Brass Gauntlet, member of the Crystal Gauntlet.

Description:
Hanegal, a handsome lad, is an optimist. He likes to fight, and is always in the persuit of knowledge. Being a wizard makes him disliked by the majority since they feel he is a threat to their position. But Hanegal wishes no one harm. He can be harsh and lose his temper, if you were to wear down his patience with you. Some might say he is arrogant, but he knows better.

Background:
Hanegal was born in the outskirts of Freeport as the firstborn son of Hannah and Galvur Grapestomper. When Hanegal was only a child his parents were abducted by a gang of unknown origin. Overwhelmed by an uncertainty of what would become of him, he reached out for a strong hand to guide him. The 'challenge' was picked up by the barbarian Laerer Frasaj of Clan Frasaj, who took it upon himself to teach Hanegal the ways of Mithaniel Marr.

Later on, now a worshipper of Mithaniel Marr and no longer uncertain, Hanegal delved into deeper into the mysteries of the arcane. He went to the Academy of Arcane Science to study wizardry. With his new education he set as a goal for himself that he would find out his parents fate, even if he would have to search every nook and cranny of Norrath.
