The Walk

The Walk is a formal method of moving to and from important events. The setup is that the Crystal Gauntlet is at front (with the Bishop and any important guests in the middle), with the Holy Circle in the middle and the rest of the members at back. At the sides of this setup are guardians for protection and at the front is our flag-bearer.

If the Walk is performed in dangerous areas, special scouts can be assigned to explore in front and to watch the back. Also weapons may be drawn in dangerous areas. The Knight will guard over the safety of all and will tell if weapons may be drawn. (Please don't keep weapons drawn in safe areas such as cities, especially when we are meeting a friendly alliance!)

As the name says, the Walk means everyone is walking. Certain circumstances can call for a run. If so, it's called by the Knight when to start running and when to slow down again.
