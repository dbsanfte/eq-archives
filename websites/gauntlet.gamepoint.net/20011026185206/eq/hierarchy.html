At the head of the Order of the Gauntlet is the council that consults about the bigger things in live and for the guild. This council is called the Order of the Crystal Gauntlet. This Crystal Gauntlet has the Bishop and 4 members which are called fingers.

Every single Finger has it's own Order. We have the Order of the Golden Gauntlet, Silver Gauntlet, Bronze Gauntlet and Brass Gauntlet. These Orders have different visions on how to pertain Order, Honour and Respect. Choose your Order wisely when you join!

Beside these Orders is the Bishop's Order or Canton. These are the wise men and women through-out the Guild (therefor someone in the Order of the Golden Gauntlet also can be in the Bishop's Canton!).

The 4 Main Orders can be divided up when numbers are growing to pertain Order and not let Chaos rule in our own Guild.

the Order of the Golden Gauntlet - GUARD -:
Head: Lovidamo Gaidin'Maconar
Description: "The Guardians"

Main task to guard and protect our guild.

the Order of the Silver Gauntlet - FUN -:
Head: Balifor Thistleknot, also 'Ceremony Master'
Description: "The Advisors and Scribers"

Main task to give advice and to scribe events.

Ranking:
Head Advisor; Gives advice to all and uses his pool of advisors as a thinktank.
Advisors; smart intelligent people taking part in the thinktank.

Head Scribe; Head of all scribes who decides what areimportant news or events.
Scribers; the people that scribe news and event concerning the guild or even the world.

Ceremony Master; Keeper of Ceremonies, gives information about how the ceremonies should go from the walking path to what to wear. He/she has helpers to aid in the ceremony.
Ceremony Helpers; the helpers for the ceremonies; setting up a ceremony and cleaning up afterwards.

the Order of the Bronze Gauntlet - LIFE -:
Head: Ashai Silverheart
Description: "Protectors of Life and Spirit"

Main task to protect our divine lives and spirits.

the Order of the Brass Gauntlet - BURN -:
Head: Hanegal Thunderflite
Description:

Main task to support in battle and tactics.

Ranking:
The Merlin - Leader, answers only to the Crystal Gauntlet.
1st Oracle* - Second in command, answers to the Merlin - Holds the responsibility for several Sr.Apprentices
Sr. Apprentice - Senior member, answers to his or her appointed Oracle.
Jr. Apprentice - Junior member, answers to his or her appointed Sr. Apprentice
*Oracles are numbered as they are appointed.

The entry level is always Jr. Apprentice. A robe for official guild use, will be handed out at the inauguration ceremony. Advancement within this division is possible by examplary conduct (or by necessity) (No one will stay a Jr. Apprentice forever)

the Bishop's Canton - HOLY-:
Head Bishop: Udaai Fateweaver
Description: "Holy Circle"

Main task to bless people and to lead ceremonies of all kinds.

Ranking:
The Bishop - head of the Bishop's Canton and the only one who has direct guidance from the major Gods and Goddesses.
Godly Enforcer - the senior staff of The Bishop. Only a few are strong enough to grasp major deity's and force concrete answers. Not Gods though, these beings are just too powerful.
Deity Questioner - junior staff of The Bishop with teaching and guiding responsibilities towards the Spirit Listeners. Mind strengthened, they can now hold a deity's attention long enough to direct feelings or very general 'questions' towards it.
Spirit Listener - pupils who are taught to listen to minor spirits and natural forces. The mind is a fragile thing, and needs to be prepared and strengthened for interaction with spitits and later deities.
Novice - applicants just entering the Bishop's Canton and waiting for a space to be accepted into the next rank
