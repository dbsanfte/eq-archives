Council Hearing

A Council Hearing is a discussion meeting in order for the Council to form decisions that are to be made. It can be seen as a less formal Guildmeeting.

The setup for this meeting is different from other ceremonies:

The open space is used when you want to tell something.

Rules that has to be followed during a Council Hearing:
- No speaking until called forward. Speaking is only allowed when you stand in the open space, unless stated otherwise.
- You can ask the discussion-leader for a moment to speak (different from a Council Speaking).
- The discussion-leader will be the Ceremony-master unless stated otherwise.
- The discussion-leader keeps track of the people that want to talk and calls them forward one by one (or per group). So put your hand up (and a quick tell with subject to the Ceremony Master).
- The discussion-leader also can announce open discussion whereas the members can speak freely without asking first. (Keep in mind that an open discussion has to be announced first!)
- It is allowed to wear all armor during this session (except helms and hats).
