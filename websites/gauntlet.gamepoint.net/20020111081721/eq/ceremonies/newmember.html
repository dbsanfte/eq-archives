The New Member Ceremony

In the next pictures is shown how the new member walks forward to the Crystal Gauntlet, pays respect to the 4 fingers and the Bishop, choosing his order and getting accepted (or not), and leaving the ceremony for celebrations or mournings.

Protocol to be followed during this ceremony:

- The new member holds the rules for lower rank members and therefor doesn't wear any armor better than leather. (All robes are allowed.)
- The new member is asked to come forward by the Ceremony Master; "[name of candidate] come forth and kneel before the Crystal Gauntlet."
- The new member walks (no run and no SOW allowed) forward to the Crystal Gauntlet and pays respect (by bowing) to the Brass Gauntlet, the Bronze Gauntlet, the Silver Gauntlet and the Gold Gauntlet in this order.
- The new member then walks toward the Bishop and kneels (/kneel + stay crouched).
-

The Bishop then talks: "Be it known to all men and women that I, in name of the Crystal Gauntlet, am minded to raise [candidate] by virtue of his honour, loyalty, valour and skill, to the high rank of the Order of the Gauntlet." (Note that low rank means the footfolk or common people, peasants if you like.)

"[Name], do you swear and acknowledge the Order of the Gauntlet, and the Crystal Gauntlet to be your true and lawful lead?" (The candidate now has to answer affermatively.)

The bishop now puts his hand on the candidates head while doing a small blessing and continues with the ceremonial words: "Repeat after me. I [name] do solemnly swear and pledge my sword and mind to the Order of the Gauntlet, to defend and obey the order until the order is no more or death shall take me, and to uphold the honour of the Order." (The candidate repeats using his own name.)

"And I, in name of the Crystal Gauntlet, do swear to defend and honour [candidate] as befits a true member of the Order."

"Arise, Brother/Sister, and choose you Order to follow."
- After getting the Bishops Blessing and after being told to go choose his order he/she will stand up to the center of the Crystal Gauntlet. There he/she asks the specific order if he/she may join: "I kneel before thee in order to be accepted into your Order." The Leader of that Order will then say "I accept your offer, and you may join my Order." OR a simple "Nay!".
He/she may also ask every Order 1 small question before deciding, but it's common that you inform yourself of the different orders before the ceremony!
- After acceptance he/she will take leave by walking away and accepting cheers and bows from the other members. After that the new member may sit down in the corresponding group as shown in the pictures.
- If not accepted he/she may try only a second time with another order. He/she will accept the outcome when not accepted and leave peacefully and quietly. In this case the Bishop will say the following: "Let it be known that [Name] has not been accepted. Let him/her leave as a friend and we will hope for the day that he/she is ready to join our Order." (Not accepted means you'll have to work harder to earn your respect of this guild. It doesn't mean all your chances are gone!)
- Listen closely to the Ceremony Master when this ceremony is over, because he/she will tell if another ceremony will take place or if there is time to celebrate.
