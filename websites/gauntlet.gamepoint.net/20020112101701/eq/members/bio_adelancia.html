Adelancia Lavastorm

Race: Human
Profession: Knight / Guardian

Description:
Adelancia is a muscled lady that isn't afraid easily. She also has the habit to bonk people on the head and get violent very easily. But she upholds the Knightly manners so the fights will always be fair. Although she keeps reminding people how much she hate certain things, she is always welcome as she brings a certain "safe"-feeling among others.

History:
Adelancia had a tough childhood and was raised among thieves and burglars. Although her father was a true Knight, she never has met him as he saw his fault when he found out that Adelancia's mother, Siradoru, was a unfamous rogue. He let her live and left heart-broken. Siradoru raised her only child back at her home in south Freeport among the many rogues and beggars. Because she told Adelancia so much about her father, Adelancia was destined to become a knight. Only the other side of her childhood caused her also to become known with black magic!
