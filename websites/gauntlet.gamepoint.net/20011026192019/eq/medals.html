Medals

Guildmembers with outstanding qualities are role models for the rest of our members. As such they are to be honoured respectfully by the giving of a Medal.

The following Medals are known:

 Medal of Order
Highest Medal you can get. This medal is given when you have earned all other medals!
Medal of Bravery
When showing more than average Bravery (not Stupidity!), you will easily earn this medal.
Medal of Honour
When showing more than average honour, you will easily earn this medal.
Medal of Respect
When showing more than average respect to your elders but also to the younger ones, you will easily earn this medal.
Medal of Spirit
When showing more than average spirit, you will easily earn this medal.
Medal of Wisdom
When showing more than average wise decisions/resourcefullness, you will easily earn this medal.
Medal of Leadership
When showing more than average leadership qualities, you will easily earn this medal.
Medal of Loyalty
When showing more than average loyalty, you will easily earn this medal.

Medals are decided on when brought before the Crystal Gauntlet. The Forwarder is entitled to give the Medal and do a speech. The Medal is only given after it is blessed by our Bishop.
