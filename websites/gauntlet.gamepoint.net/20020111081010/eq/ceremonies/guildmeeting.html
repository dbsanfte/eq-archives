Guildmeeting

Guildmeetings are the most common meetings of the members of the guild. The guildmeetings can be open discussions or more formal held decision-taking sessions. In all cases does the Crystal Gauntlet lead the meeting.

Rules that are to be followed are:

- Don't talk before your turn. Raise your hand and eventually you will get your turn to talk. If you are seated in the lower ranks you should tell your direct officer you want to say something (this because your raised hand might not be seen from far). Your direct officer will ask you forward to speak up.
- Always act with respect to your superiors.
- To speed up meetings it is better to speak things out in your own order before discussing it at the meeting.
- Decisions made at guildmeetings are official and therefor in direct effect after the meeting or on another date when explicitly stated otherwise.
- During guildmeetings a raffle can be held to collect money for the guild (be it for the building of the guildhouse,or the other tasks to be done by the guild). This money goes directly into the treasury along with any items that are given during the raffle.
- Pay attention when the Ceremony Master anounces something, because a guildmeeting can easily transform into a formal ceremony (such as for example a new member ceremony).
