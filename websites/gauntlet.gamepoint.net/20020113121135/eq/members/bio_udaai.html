Udaai Fateweaver

Race: Human
Profession: Bishop, Battlemage, Guildleader

Description:
Udaai normally has a friendly and outgoing personality, but at times he can be very withdrawn and even forgetful. This is not because he is bored with what happens around him, but actually because he is chatting up the beautiful and luscious godesses again. Hmmm, make that: he is intervening on behalf of our Guild with all the known deities (and quite some more!) to grant the Guild and its friends safety in travel, good fortune in the hunt, and luck with the women (or men).
Although Udaai is a mage, he likes to throw a punch now and again and often joins in the fray. Until he gets his delicate hands bruised, at which time he takes a step back to 'supervise' the battle and throw a spell here and there.


History:
Udaai always fantasises that his mother was an angel, and as such he is partly immortal as well. But alas, he will probably never know for sure for his father died during the voyage on the ship to Freeport. Udaai was only 3 years old at the time. His father was a practisioner of magic himself too, and just returned from an extensive trip to Kunark to look for rare tomes. Supposedly, Udaai's father met his wife in Kunark, where they conceived Udaai. What happened with Udaai's mother is not known, for his father returned alone.
In Freeport, Udaai was taken in during the first years of his life by Opal Darkbriar, and was trained in the arcane arts of magic. When he was sixteen years of age, he left the mage's guild after a big argument with some of the older mages in the guild (which he called 'old farts' behind their back). During his travels through the lands, he came in contact with distant family of his father. Balifor Thistleknot, an intrepid bard, and Lovidamo Gaidin'Maconar, a big brute of a barbarian, were his travel companions for a long long time.
It was during these travels that they decided to form a guild and battle the chaos they saw throughout the lands of Norrath.

Likes:
Dark-elf women, big and stong familiars, chocolate cakes, surprise treasure, Udaai

Dislikes:
Rain and fog, ugly Troll/Ogre women (which means _all_), beggars, nasties that stain Udaai's robes
