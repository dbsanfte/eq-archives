Lupen Reports:

I got Egress a few days ago by turning in a Spirit of Scale. It's a self-only teleport spell that takes you to a supposedly safe area in your current zone.

Egress info:
Level 52
500 mana
5 second casting time (same as Gate)
Outdoor only
Clears hate-list

This table shows the locations of where Egress drops you off in a bunch of zones. Hopefully Succor (lev 57 same-zone group teleport) will go to the same locations. I didn't check every single zone in the game, but I got most of the Kunark ones, including the 2 outdoor dungeons.
