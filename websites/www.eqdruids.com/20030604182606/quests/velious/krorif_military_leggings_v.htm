Kromrif Military Leggings (Great Divide)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                              Armor, Legs

Armor Class                           14

Boosts                                   +6 STR, +5 WIS, +5 DR, +5 CR

Effect                                     None

Weight & Size                        WT: 4.0  Size: ?

Class                                      ALL

Race    ALL

Talk to Vores the Hunter who can be found in the Great Divide behind the wurm caves. He will tell you to get him a Blood Wolf Harness, which drop off Blood Wolves.

Once you have obtained the harness, head off to the Crystal Caverns and start killing all the orcs you see until you obtain a Bottle of Karsinic Acid. Now head off to Erdarf Restil in Thurgadin, in the bar near the Temple. Hand him the acid and he will hand you back a gem.

Go back to Vores and hand him the gem and the harness he will reward you with a Control Harness. Now go and locate the Broodmother, in the shardwurms caves and hand her the control harness. Two named giants (Grakl and Fergul) will spawn outside the fort, and she will run up to them and start attacking Grakl. Fergul will start to run to the zone, kill him and you will be able to loot the Kromrif Military Leggings.
