The following is a helpful chart for use when using the Armor Tinting system within Legacy of Ykesha.

How to use the new tinting system:

To use dyes, you do the following steps.

From the Erudite vendor in the Gunthak Lighthouse, purchase an unfired dye vial and a quality firing sheet.

Combine these in an Kiln.  If you are successful you will have a dye vial returned to you.

Now from the same vendor buy a prism and a jar of lacquer.  Combine these two items in either a brew barrel or a shaman's medicine bag.

Now take that dye, and combine it with the fired dye vial.  If successful you now have a dye reagent.

Each armor slot will take one dye reagent to change the color.  So you will need 7 reagents to do a full set of armor.

Now that you have your reagents, open your inventory window and you will see a button at the bottom that says "Dye Armor".  There is where you choose what armor slot and what color combination.

Enjoy!
