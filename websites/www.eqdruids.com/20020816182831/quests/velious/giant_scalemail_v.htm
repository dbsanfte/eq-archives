Giant Scalemail Armor (Kael)

Talk to Slaggak the Trainer who can be found in Kael Drakkal. He will ask you if you are ready to serve the Kromrif. Tell him you are and then ask him "what coldain". He will tell you about the ice dwarves and how he longs to kill them again. Bring him four Coldain Heads and he will reward you with a random piece of Giant Scalemail Armor.
