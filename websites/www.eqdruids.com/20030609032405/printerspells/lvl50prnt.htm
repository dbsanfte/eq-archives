Level Fifty Spell

Improved Superior Camouflage:  

Effect:  Invisibility

Skill: Divination
Allowable Targets: Self

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 75
Spell Duration: 100 ticks (10.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 5.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: You vanish.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> fades away.
Spell fades: You appear.

This is a self only invisibility.  It has a fixed duration of 10 mins per cast.

 

Reported Drops from Crystalline Wurms in Great Divide

Also reported to drop in Velketor's Labyrinth and Icewell Keep and the Shardwurm  Matriarch
