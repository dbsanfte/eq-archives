Drake-Hide Mask (PoA)

 

Properties                            MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                            Armor, Face

Armor Class                          4

Boosts                                  +10 AGI, +10 DEX, +10 STR, +10 WIS, +10 MANA, +7 MR

Effect                                   None

Weight & Size                       WT: 0.4  Size: ?

Class                                     DRU

Race                                     ALL

 

Talk to Strandar Pinemist who can be found in the Plane of Air. He will ask you if you are ready for the Test of the Wolf. If you say yes, Will Treewalker will spawn and you can then talk to him. He will ask you to retrieve the following three items for him.

·  Azure Tessera

·  Black Face Paint

·  Worn Leather Mask

These items are obtained off various monsters all of which live in the Plane of Air. Hand Will the three items and you will receive the drake-hide mask as your reward.
