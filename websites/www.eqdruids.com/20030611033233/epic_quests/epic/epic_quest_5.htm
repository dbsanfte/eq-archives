The Stones
 

Go Back to Misty Thicket and hand Ella Foodcrafter the hardened mixture and the rune crested bowl. She will then make you a softly glowing stone.

 

 

Ella now will tell you to find one such as yourself.

 

She is referring to Foloal Stormforest in Firiona Vie.

 

Ella Foodcrafter smiles slowly as she works the mixture in the bowl and it starts to shine with power. 'This is... a piece of a lost magic item known as Nature's Gem. When restored to completeness I've heard its healing powers will be extraordinary. Take it now, I will take the bowl and hopefully do some good from my end. From here, you can go many ways but I can't guide your path step by step. The only advise I can give you is to look for ones such as yourself'

You can find Foloal on the wall that circles the city of Firiona Vie at the north end.

 

Ask her "are you one such as myself?" "what answers?" and "what about Venril Sathir?"

 

She will tell you to bring her the stone you already have from Ella and the gem that can be found on Venril Sathir.

 

(luckily she means the spawn of Venril Sathir’s remains not the true Venril Sathir!)

 

Ask 'Are you one such as myself?'


'You must be the one that has been spoken of. Yes, yes I am one of the circle. There are many of us in the lands right now, all seeking answers to the ills that have befallen our homes'

Ask 'What answers?'


'I have heard whispers of new Iksar movements in the land. The name of Venril Sathir is on the scaled lips and sharp tongues of every cold blooded lizard in this place'

Ask 'What about Venril Sathir?'


'All I've heard are whispers of connections between the undead lizards and the dark god of hate. There's some kind of history behind the two. It might be that Venril is the main focus of Innoruuk's will in the land. Then again, maybe not, but this is the best we've gotten so far. If you think you can do it, then find him and remove him from the face of this earth. Bring me the half of the stone I know you have and whatever conduit of power you find on his rotting corpse

Venril Sathir is found in Karnor’s Castle, in the Dreadlands. Venril Sathir’s remains will spawn twenty four hours (approximately) after Venril Sathir is killed. 

 

There should be two spawns of Venril Sathir's Remains, then Venril Sathir will respawn.  However this spawn seems to be acting differently lately so be forewarned it may not be this constant any longer.

 

When the remains spawn, go into Karnor’s Castle and hail him. First he wants a Globe of Fireflies.  Use the spell Dance of Fireflies to make a Globe of Fireflies to turn in.  This is a level one spell for those that forgot they even had the spell.

 

This spell can only be cast at night.  You might want to go ahead and make yourself a globe ahead of time, so you aren't having to wait at the spawn for night to fall.

 

Hand the Remains of Venril Sathir the Globe of Fireflies.  This will spawn Spirit of Venril Sathir.

 

Now hand Spirit of Venril Sathir a copy of the level 49 Cleric spell Resurrection (this spell is purchased at any of the Cleric guilds).

 

This will spawn yet another Venril Sathir who will attack.  Don't worry he is not as tough as the "normal" Venril Sathir.

 

Kill this spawned Venril and loot the Pulsing Green Stone.  The good news here is that the loot normally is two Pulsing Green Stones so you can help another druid/ranger with one kill.

 


Take the Softly Glowing Stone and Pulsing Green Stone back to Foloal Stormforest. She will give you a warmly glowing stone.

 

Foloal will now tell you to take the stone and walk your path. The path she is referring to leads back to Ella Foodcrafter in Misty Thicket.

 

Foloal Stormforest blinks and carefully takes the stones from you, cautiously scratching the jagged green stone against the other. Its skin seems to shiver and flake at the contact and eventually begins to shine brightly. 'With the gem nature's balance is complete. Take the stone and walk your path. Blade or lightning caller, burning sword or the mother's walk'.


Give Ella the warmly glowing stone and you will receive the Elaborate Scimitar (10/30 +9 wis, +10 mana, +5 sv poison and disease).
 


She will then ask you to collect the Cleansed Spirits of Antonica, Faydwer and Kunark and hand them along with the scimitar to Xanuusus.
