Mask of the Silver Eyes (ToV)

 

Properties                           MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                            Armor, Face

Armor Class                        25

Boosts                                 +12 STA, +12 WIS, +12 INT, +20 HP, +80 MANA,

                                            +5   CR, +5 DR, +5 FR, +5 MR, +5 PR

Effect                                  Flowing Thought I

Weight & Size                     WT: 0.5  Size: TINY

Class    CLR, DRU, SHM, NEC, WIZ, MAG, ENC

Talk to Lendiniara the Keeper who can be found in the Temple of Veeshan. Ask her "what request of the arcane" and she will tell you to bring her the following items to prove yourself. You will need to find the White Tear, the Black Tear, the Runed Tear, and a Glowing Orb. All the items are dropped off random mobs in the Halls of Testing, within the Temple of Veeshan. Once you have obtained all four items, return them to Lendiniara and you'll be rewarded with the Mask of the Silver Eyes.
