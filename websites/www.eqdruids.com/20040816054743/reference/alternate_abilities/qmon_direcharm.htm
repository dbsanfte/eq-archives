Prepared by Elder Qmon of Brell Serilis – Last Updated July 18, 2004

 

This guide lists some of the “higher level” animals that can be Dire Charmed (DC). This list does not include most lower level animals since most Druids are probably not going to DC them anyway. Remember, you can always use Sense Animal to determine if a mob is an animal.

 Druid DC is an AA Archetype ability that costs nine AA and only works on animals, level 46 and below. Since some animals on this list can exceed level 46, there is a chance your DC attempt might fail. If that happens, when you cast DC you will agro the animal but the 72 minute recast timer will reset to zero so you can try again.

 Of the animals listed below, there is a chance you might come across one that is actually higher than level 46, so you might have to hunt around before you are successful. I once made over 20 attempts to DC a Brontotherium (rhino) in Western Wastes before I found one that worked.
