White Dragonscale Cloak (Rathe Mtns)

 

Properties                            QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                             Armor, Back

Armor Class                          10

Boosts                                  +9 INT, +9 WIS, +25 CR, +75 MANA

Effect                                    None

Weight & Size                       WT: 0.0  Size: ?

Class                                     ALL

Race    ALL

Talk to Karam Dragonforge who can be found in an Inn, in the Rathe Mountains. He will make a special Dragon Armor for you if your faction with him is high enough. He will tell you that he can make the White Dragonscale Cloak but he needs the following items in exchange.

·  Diamond Platinum Ring

·  Black Sapphire Platinum Necklace

·  White Dragon Scale

The Diamond Platinum Ring and Black Sapphire Platinum Necklace are both created through the Jewelry Skill, and you will need to be a master jeweler to create them. For the White Dragon Scale, it is dropped by Lady Vox.
