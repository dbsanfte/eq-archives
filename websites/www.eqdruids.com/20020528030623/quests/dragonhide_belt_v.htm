Dragonhide Belt (Kael)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                             Armor, Waist

Armor Class                          6

Boosts                                  +10 STA

Effect                                    None

Weight & Size                       WT: 0.0  Size: SMALL

Class                                      WAR, CLR, PAL, RNG, SHD, DRU, MNK, BRD, ROG, SHM

Race                                      ALL

 

Talk to Vorken who can be found in the Iceshard Manor within Kael Drakken. He will tell you a story about when he was a youngin and that he was humiliated by Blizzent. He wants revenge and would love to see the beast dead. Blizzent is a rare spawn in the Great Divide, and drops evidence of his death. Return to Vorken with the evidence and you will be rewarded with the Dragonhide Belt.
