Level Thirty Nine Spells

Pack Regeneration: 

  • Alteration 

  • Mana cost 200 

  • Effect:  Group Regeneration

Great spell for groups, one cast, all people within range now are regenerating.  I would only use this with 5-6 people in a group however, any less and its more mana efficient to use single regeneration.

Also the regeneration will only last half as long as the single spell.

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Pack Spirit:  

  • Alteration 

  • Mana cost 120 

  • Effect:  Group Sow

Group Sow!  Again, I would use this for 5 or more people in the group, otherwise it is more mana efficient to use single cast Sow.

As with regeneration, the effects last half as long as single sow.

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Skin Like Diamond:  

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 200
  • Effect:  Upgraded AC and Hp buff

Next spell in the Skin Like... line of spells.  This one increases your 14 and your hitpoints approximately 200.

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Firestrike:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 155 
  • Effect:  Direct Damage fire spell

This is your latest fire blast spell.  The damage it does is 282 damage + 4 more each time you level.

This spell does some nice damage.

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Avalanche:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 200 
  • Effect:  Area Affect Direct Damage 

This is an upgrade to our rain line of spells. 

 As with all rain spells, they last from 1 to 2 rounds and hit a maximum of four times.  Each rain does about 125 dmg.

The range is about a 2 person radius meaning that the Mobs have to be standing side by side for them to be hit.

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Shield of Spikes: 

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 100
  • Effect:  Upgraded Damage Shield

Upgrade to the Shield of ... Spells.  This one will do 14 points of damage when the protected person is hit by mob.

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Spikecoat:  

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 110
  • Effect:  Upgraded Personal Damage Shield

Upgrade to your personal damage shield spell.  This spell will increase your AC by 19, and will damage a mob 4 every time he hits you. 

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Enveloping Roots:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 80
  • Effect:  Upgrade to root spells

This is a double effect root spell, like previous version of it.  This means that the chance for resist is really high.  Damage is 88 points at level 39 and increases by 4 points every level up to 44

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Ro's Fiery Sundering:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 50
  • Effect:  AC and Fire Resist Debuff

This is a nice spell.  When it sticks it reduces the mobs AC and Fire resistance.  Which of course makes it easier for your flame spells to hit.

Only downfall is this spell will normally cause INSTANT agro on you, if the tanks haven't been building enough agro.  So practice with it before you use.

Purchase From:  Drops from Wurms in Great Divide

Share Wolf Form:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 120
  • Effect:  Group Wolf Form

Group Wolf Form!  This will give wolf form to your group, equal to your first wolf form spell.

Make sure you tell the tanks, that they will not be able to bash or kick when in wolf form.

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Ring of Cobalt Scar:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 150
  • Effect:  Teleport self to Cobalt Scar

Finally we receive self ports to Velious!  Ring of Cobalt Scar ports you to Cobalt Scar.  You must have a Cobalt Scar tooth for this spell to work.

Purchase From:  Kelethin Druid Guild (Soldiers of Tunare) Srendon Glademender

Ring of Wakening Lands:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 150
  • Effect:  Teleport self to Wakening Lands

Ring of Wakening lands is a self only port to Wakening Lands.  You must have either a Wakening Lands tooth, or a Cobalt Scar tooth for this spell to work

Purchase From:  Kelethin Druid Guild (Soldiers of Tunare) Srendon Glademender

Circle of Misty:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Teleport group to Misty Thicket

As it says, this is your group teleport spell to Misty Thicket.  Its a nice spell to use when group needs to go to bank.

Purchase From:  Toxxulia Druid Circle

Circle of Great Divide:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Teleports group to Great Divide

Teleports group to Cobalt Scar.  Each person riding must have tooth from either Great divide, Wakening Lands, or Cobalt Scar.

Purchase From:  Philip the Fawn in Wakening Lands

Winds of the North:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Teleports you to Skyfire

Be very careful when using this spell!  It will teleport you into the Skyfire zone, which is FULL of dragons, drakes, and worse.

The good news is, the druid port is reasonably safe when you port in.  You normally have time to invis up.  The wizard port is in a different location, and is almost a guaranteed death!

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

Winds of the South:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Teleport to Emerald Jungle

This is your self/group spell to teleport to the Emerald Jungle.  

This spell is different, it drops you in a location in Emerald Jungle, not at a druid circle or dragon ring.

Again, be careful porting into this zone, there are lots of mobs wandering near.

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

Succor Ro:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Group Evacuation

Another evacuation spell.  This spell takes your group to South Ro.  Same limitations apply as before.

This Evac has a nice feature to it for if you have evil folk in your group, there are no druids standing at this circle so you don't have to be worried about evil's being attacked.

Purchase From:  Toxxulia Druid Circle
