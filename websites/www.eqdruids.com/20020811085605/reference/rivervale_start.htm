Welcome To Rivervale, the city of halflings!

Here is the EQ Atlas map of the city of Rivervale and of Misty Thicket.

The marked areas are the druid guild, your starting point, the bank, and your initial bind point.

Descriptions of the other buildings within Rivervale can be found on the EQ Atlas map, here.
 

Descriptions of Misty Thicket can be found here.

(Click on picture to see larger)

You begin your adventures in front of the druid guild (loc -104.00, -347.00, -9.81).

(Click on picture to see larger)

Open up your inventory, and read your guild note (right-click on it).

(Click on picture to see larger)

Turn to the right, and you will see the Storm Reapers building, and the adjacent farm.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Go through the doors into the main hall of the Storm Reapers, and locate Hibbs Rootenpaw.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Turn in your guild note ("a tattered note") to Hibbs Rootenpaw, and he will provide you with a starting tunic.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Equip your tunic, and exit the building.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Using the map of Rivervale, make your way to the bank. The zone to Misty Thicket will be nearby.

(Click on picture to see larger)

When you are directly facing the bank, turn around, and you will see the tunnel to Misty Thicket.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Go through the tunnel, and enter Misty Thicket. You will see two guards guarding the entrance.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Exit the tunnel, and you will see your first view of Misty Thicket.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Begin hunting the creatures present in Misty Thicket.

Should you die, you will re-enter the zone at your initial bind point (see map of Misty Thicket above).

When you do re-enter the zone, your view will be similar to the image below.

(Click on picture to see larger)

It is not too far from the guards and the Rivervale tunnel. Simply turn a little to the right, and you will be able to see the tunnel.

Good luck, and happy hunting!
