Greetings fellow druids. I am Bokken Stokken of the Vale (Innoruuk server).
I have come to inform you of a way to control and dispense of the critters
you be fighting. Make friends with a Wizard (or someone else with good
flash-bang stuff). Then head to a nice open place with good prey. SoW up
both of ya.

Let the Circle Of Death begin.
You as the Druid go out and find a nice yellow/Red critter (that won't
be resisted... VERY important! ;-). DOT him and run him back to the waiting
blaster, Let him know what is coming and when close to the blaster range
snare the critter. Then run in a circle around the blaster with the critter
after you. If everything works as it should the critter will be nuked to
death by the blaster. If the blaster nukes it until it's sufficiently pissed
off at him to turn on him, he just starts to run a circle around you while
you bang it with a new DOT and DD spells. Either that or he runs the critter
close to you so you take a hit, it usually stays with you after that and you
can start running circles with it again. Most often it just takes a
lap or two before the critter is dead.

NOTE: The blaster should stand still... it is the runners responsibility to
stay in range.

VERY nice exp and low downtime as the blaster spends the most mana then just
sits and meds while you pick up stuff. Just make sure to conserve your mana
for DOT's and the finishing DD spell. You should be able to kill 2/3
critters before you have to meditate. I have done this with great success on
beetles in NK and Crag spiders in EK.

My friend Bopper was the one that originally came up with the tactic
and showed me the ropes. Honor to the one who deserves it.

This is Bokken Stokken signing off.
