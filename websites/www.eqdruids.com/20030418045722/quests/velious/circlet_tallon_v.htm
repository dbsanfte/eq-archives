Circlet of Tallon (Kael)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                              Armor, Head

Armor Class                           18

Boosts                                   +9 CR, +9 MR, +9 PR

Effect                                     None

Weight & Size                        WT: 0.6  Size: ?

Class                                      ALL

Race                                      ALL

Talk to Gkrean Prophet of Tallon who can be found in Kael Drakkal. He will tell you about the temple of tactics and he will ask you if you will serve the temple. He will tell you go out and retrieve the different books of tactics that the Coldain use and return them to him. There are various rewards to this quest, depending on the book you hand him. The book you want to locate for this quest is the Tactics of the Frostreavers. Hand it in to Gkrean and your reward will either be the Bracer of Sacrifice or the Circlet of Tallon.
