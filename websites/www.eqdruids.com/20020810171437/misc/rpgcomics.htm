RPGComics and their Custom Artwork

(RPGComics and Phantasy Workshop have merged.  So the below link takes you to Phantasy Workshops site now)

I want to make sure that I let as many folks know about RPGComics and their custom artwork as possible!

These guys do FANTASTIC custom artwork.  You send them a screenshot of your character, and what you want the picture to look like and they will send you some of the nicest artwork you could ever imagine!

Here are some thumbnails of their work:

The first picture above is actually work they did for my wife and myself!  We've ordered two more times from them since!  The detail they put into these pictures are just incredible and their prices are very very reasonable!

For those that notice a resemblance to ElfQuest artwork, that would be due to the fact that these artists used to work on ElfQuest comics!

I highly suggest checking these guys out!  You won't be sorry!
