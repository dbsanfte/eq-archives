Level Thirty Four Spells

The port spells are available at the West Commons Ring.

Animal based spells are available at your guild hall.

Resist Cold: 

Effect:  Increase Cold Resist from 39 (L24) to 40 (L25)

Skill: Abjuration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 50
Spell Duration: 360 ticks (36.0 minutes)
 Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel resistant to cold.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is resistant to cold.
Spell fades: Your cold resistance fades.

This spell will increase your cold resistance.  It will add 40 points to your CR.  It can be cast on yourself and others.

Regeneration:  

Effect:  Increase Hit Points (HP) by 5 (L24)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 100
Spell Duration: Unknown
Duration Formula: 10
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You begin to regenerate.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> begins to regenerate.
Spell fades: You have stopped regenerating.

This spell causes the target to regenerate 6 hps a tick for about 15 minutes. It is great for when you are low on mana and seriously hurt as it consumes less mana then greater heal and lets you regain your mana while you heal. It does stack with the bard healing song and a troll's regeneration. This also stacks with Treeform.

 

I keep regeneration on at anytime I am kiting.  Allows you to heal as you run around.

 

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Lightning Strike:  

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 172 (L34) to 184 (L38)

Skill: Evocation
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: Target Based AoE

Range to Target: 200 feet
Area Effect Range: 10 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 25

Mana Required: 149
Spell Duration: Instant
Area Effect Duration: 5
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
 Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: Lightning surges through your body.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> has been struck by lightning.

This is an outdoor only spell. It has a small area of effect and does 173-185 pts of damage to all creatures inside that range. 

The range is about a 2 person radius meaning that the Mobs have to be standing side by side for them to be hit.

 

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Earthquake:  

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 226 (L34) to 246 (L39)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: Point Blank AoE

Area Effect Range: 30 feet
Pushes Target Back: 1 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 5

Mana Required: 375
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
 Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 24.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel the ground shake.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is smashed by the heaving ground.

The area of effect is quite large so you might accidentally pull monsters that you didn't mean to. 

 

The damage is 300 pts but it consumes a large amount of mana. 

 

The Tremor ---> Earthquake line of spells are point blank area effect. Monsters must be extremely close for this spell to hit them. The advantage is that there is no max target limit, I.E. you can hit an infinite amount of Mobs using this spell.
