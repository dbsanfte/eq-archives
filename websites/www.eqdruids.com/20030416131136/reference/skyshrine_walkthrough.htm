How to reach the Skyshrine Armor Quest Mobs

 

This guide assumes that you have teleported in from Cobalt Scar.

 

At zone in go across the fountain (the direction you are facing) and go through the gate.
 

Take the first right doorway as you go down the hall.  You will see the following.


Go forward until you see a room with a left and right hallway. Take the right hallway

Follow the hallway until the doorway on your right. Go in that doorway and down the hall

You will enter a room with a dragon statue, and gargoyles

 

Behind the dragon statue is a stairway up, take that stairway.

 

You will now be in a room with a ramp up. Take the ramp up and then go left.

 

At the top of the ramp, you will see a hallway with a ramp up to your left. Take that ramp up.


You will go through a hallway with LOTS of dragons and other mobs.  Keep following the hallway until you see a ramp going up to your left. Go up that ramp.
 

Keep following the hall until you see the Dragon Gate. Go through that gate.

 

Continue up the next ramp in front of you.

 

On your right, you will see a tunnel with large gem looking sides. Continue along that tunnel

 

 

Go up the ramp, and at the top you will see a teleport pad, step on it.

 

 

Now follow the ramp ahead of you up.


You will soon see the beginning of the maze area.

 

Go into the tunnel, and follow it until you reach the maze.

 

 

In the maze, go to the first right hand passage.  Keep to the right hand wall.

 

Keep following the right hand wall until you see a teleport marker

 

 

Step on the teleport.

Now stay with the left wall until you teleport again

After you teleport, continue to move forward until you teleport again.

Now you want to turn around 180% and go straight forward into the last teleport.Go through the gate in front of you, and click on the spinning ball.

 

This will teleport you again.


Before you reach the stairs, on your left is a door. Open that door

You will see the room with the quest mobs through this door

 

 
The mob you want is Qynydd Fe'Dhar (he is on the right side of the room).

 

When finished, click on the open book on the pedestal

 

and you will be returned to your starting point (zone in)
