Silver Bracelet of Speed (ToV)

 

Properties                                     MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                                      Armor, Wrist

Armor Class                                  38

Boosts                                           +10 STR, +10 STA, +10 WIS, +10 INT, +100 HP, +5 CR, +5 DR, +5 FR, +5 MR, +5 PR

Effect                                            Haste

Weight & Size                               WT: 0.0  Size: TINY

Class                                              ALL

Race    ALL

Talk to Lendiniara the Keeper who can be found in the Temple of Veeshan. Ask her "what test of the platinum tear". She will tell you to return to her once you have found the four items she needs. These items are the Black Symbol, the Serrated Symbol, the Runed Symbol and the Platinum Tear. All of these items are dropped by various creatures who can be found in the Halls of Testing, in the Temple of Veeshan. Hand her all four items and you'll be rewarded with the Silver Bracelet of Speed.
