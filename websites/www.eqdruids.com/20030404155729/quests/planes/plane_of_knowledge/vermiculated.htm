Vermiculated Armor Turn In

 

In the Plane of Knowledge there is an NPC named Hierophant Trilawyth.

 

He wants Vermiculated armors.  Hand him Vermiculated Armor pieces, and he will reward you with a high end gem (reports are blue diamond, diamond, sapphire). 

 

One gem per piece of armor. 

 

A level 61 said that he turned in 4 pieces of armor, and receive 1/4 of a blue in experience also.
