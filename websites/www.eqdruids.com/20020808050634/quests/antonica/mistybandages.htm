Bandages for Honeybugger (Misty Thicket)
Recommended level: 1

 

Find Joogl Honeybugger in Misty Thicket. Give him a bandage and he'll thank you with some faction adjustments.

For each bandage, you'll receive the following faction adjustments:

    Factions Raised:

  • DeepPockets
  • Guardians of the Vale
  • Mayor Gubbin
  • Merchants of Rivervale

    Factions Lowered:

  • Coalition of Tradefolk Underground

This is an excellent way of gaining faction with several groups in Rivervale.
