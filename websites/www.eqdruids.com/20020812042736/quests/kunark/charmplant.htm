Charm Plant Spell

You get this quest after finishing the handy shillelagh quest. Shrub Marwood requires a few items and will give you the Druid's Charm Plant Scroll in return. Bring him the following:

Sarnak Farsight crystal (Found on Sarnak Revelers in Sarnak Fort, Lake of Ill Omen);

Sarnak night dust (Found on Sarnak Partisans near Iksar statue, Frontier Mountains);

Strathebone Healer silk (Found on Healers in Kaesora);

Powder of Tsu (Found on Froglok Tsu Shamans, Swamp of No Hope/Firiona Vie
