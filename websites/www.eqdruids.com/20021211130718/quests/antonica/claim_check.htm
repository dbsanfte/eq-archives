Claim Check

 

Vesteri Nomanoi, in the JaggedPine Treefolk shop wants you to take a claim check to Nesiff's Wooden Weapons in South Qeynos and pick up a statue for him.

 

Take the claim check to Nesiff and you will get some xp and he will give you a small wooden statue to return.

 

This step gives the following faction changes: Merchants of Qeynos, Antonius Bayle, Guards of Qeynos and Coalition of Tradefolk get better and Circle Of Unseen Hands gets worse.

 

Return the statute to Vesteri and he will give you some money and experience and the following faction changes: Jagged Pine Treefolk, Protectors of Pine, Guards of Qeynos and QRG Protected Animals get better and Unkempt Druids get worse.
