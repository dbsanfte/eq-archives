Coldain Prayer Shawl Quests

 

I was going to write up this 7 part quest for EQDruids but I found an excellent resource for this quest already written.

 

Its on EQtraders.com at the following link Verdandi's Thurgadin Shawl Quest guide 

 

Enjoy this quest.  But be ready to start working on your tradeskills!  Its a fun quest over all, but you will need to be ready to take some time to finish it.  It won't be finished quickly!
