Lesser Succor

 

Lesser Succor

Decrease Mana by 300 (L18)
Evacuate to the zone's safe point
 

Classes: Dru (L18)
 

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Group Buff
Beneficial: Beneficial
 

Area Effect Range: 100 feet
 

Resistance Check: None
 

Mana Required: 150
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 10.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
 

Spell cast on someone: An orc centurion creates a mystic portal.
Spell fades: The portal shimmers and fades.
 

Group teleport to "safe" point in a zone.  For locations of port check the Succor/Egress chart here

 

Spell is sold in all Druids guilds including Shadow Haven
