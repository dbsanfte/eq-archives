Recent News :

(5/10) The NEW item ranking section has now completely replaced the old equipment section and Vamphiri wanted to say this:

At long last, the final Druid-Equipable weapons have been added to the Item Rankings lists.  All the weapons known in the lands of Norrath and equipable by Druids are now listed. 

I’ve also gone through and deleted a few of the Religious Jewelry items from some of the armor sections.  Even though All/All could have equipped the items I deleted, Druids can only worship Tunare or Karana.  So, there really was no point of them being included in the Item Ranking.  It just made sense.

New or updated items in the rankings will now appear in RED text, so they can be easily spotted.  (Ranking number, Points and Name of item only will appear in RED)  You will notice lots of them in this update.

I’d also like to thank all of you that have emailed me with praise and suggestions on the rankings.  You’re welcome!  Yes, it took an enormous amount of time to compile.  And yes, I will be keeping the lists as current as possible.  You can expect, at a MINIMUM, of two updates per month.

Many of you have also been contacting me for any and all Druid related questions you may have.  I try to answer these questions to the best of my ability and believe I’ve done so thus far.  Bear with me if I don’t get to your emails within a few days of you sending them though.  You have no idea how many I get each day.  Perhaps there should be an “Ask Vamphiri” section here… =)
