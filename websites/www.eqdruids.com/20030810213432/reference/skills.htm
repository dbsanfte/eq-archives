The druid has a mix of skills of pure caster, melee, and some of the ranger skills.   Each skill has a name, description, recommended point investment, movement, needed for, and max formula.  Except for spell skills - you will not be able to go over 200 until you reach level 51!

If you see anything that needs correction, please let us know so we can change it - Spiritual / Tesiux

Spell Casting Skills

Name Description Investment and Level Formula / Max Level
Channel Ability to recover from casting interruptions 1 point - Level 4 (Begins at skill 4) (level + 1 ) x 5 - cap 220
Meditate Increases the speed at which you recover mana, and scribe/memorize spells 1 point - Level 8 (Begins at skill 8) (level + 1) x 5 - cap 252
Abjuration Abjuration is the discipline of Shielding. All personal AC and HP buffs and damage shield spells are based on this skill. None - Level 1 (level + 1) x 5 - cap 235
Alteration Alteration is the discipline of changing matter. Spells of this type are the basis of all buffs and debuffs, as well as teleportation spells. None - Level 1 (level + 1) x 5 - cap 235
Conjuration Conjuration is the discipline of summoning. It is the basis of many DoT, all pet summoning, and all item creation spells. None - Level 1 (level + 1) x 5 - cap 235
Divination Divination is the discipline of inner change. Spells based on divination include illusions, and those spells which enable you to identify or locate things. None - Level 1 (level + 1) x 5 - cap 235
Evocation Evocation is the discipline of destructive magic. Evoked spells are the basis of most Direct Damage spells, Banishments, and Rains. None - Level 1 (level + 1) x 5 - cap 235
Specialization (Alt, Evoc, Div, Conj, Abj) Increases your mana efficiency upon a successful "roll" 1 point each - Level 30 (Only one skill can be above skill 50 - the rest are capped at 50.) (level + 1) x 5 - cap 235

To get a head start, you may increase your skill using practice points up to skill level 20.

For more information and recipes on tradeskills, visit EQ Traders.
