Succor:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Teleports group to safe area of zone

This is the group version of Egress.  Takes you to the same teleport points, but allows you to take a group with you.  It will use 700 points of mana from each person if available (if no, it drains all that person has available).

Great spell to go places like across Siren's Grotto to Western Wastes zone.
