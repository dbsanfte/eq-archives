The Crude Bone Alter

The Thulian High Ritualist drops a Crude Bone Alter. This is a 4 slot container with a combine button. You need to combine the following in it:

1. Tear Encrusted Quill -- Brown lizards near the golem in the pyramid area

2. Black Powdered Incense -- Brown lizards in the Avatar Pyramid.

3. Flesh Bound Tome -- Brown lizards in the temple area

4. Black Ooze Filled Phylactery -- Brown lizards in the temple area.

This will create a Phobonomicon of Thul Tae Ew.

You can do one of two things with it.

The first choice is to turn it into the Aqaar Aluram in Feerott. This will result in the Medallion of Arcane Scientists.

The second choice is to give it to the Living Void in Cazic. He will then despawn and a Horror Construct will spawn in his place. It's tough, but drops some good loot.
