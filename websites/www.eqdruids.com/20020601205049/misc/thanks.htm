Thanks.  That word doesn't begin to cover what I feel.  When I took over this website I knew I was going to be in for a challenge.  I took a look at the structure of the website, and thought "well, here we go, time to start the job!". 

I put in I think five straight nights of about seven hour days each, changing things around, adding content, and making updates.  Afterwards, I told my wife "after all this work, you know if they don't like it, I might just have to scream a little!"  Luckily my wife is a very understanding woman, and looked at me and said "I know honey".

So when I put up the first update, I was really nervous.  I was afraid you guys wouldn't like what was being done.  Then the first e-mails started coming in.  Some had issues they wanted to discuss, but most were very nice e-mails.  No one wrote anything that I felt was anything but helpful.

This encouraged me.  Maybe I'd be ok with all this!  Then as time continued, more people wrote.  And I have to tell you, some of the e-mails I have received, have been some of the nicest things you could ever want to hear.  I've saved them all, so if I ever do start to get discouraged, all I have to do is reread those letters!

You all have not only continued to encourage me, help me, show me my errors (which I also do appreciate!), you made me realize that this group of people is one of the greatest groups of people you could ever want to be around.

So all I can say is, Thanks!

  Spiritual Thistlethorne - 11/16/01
