Handy Shillelagh

Shrub Marwood (in the Druid guild in Firiona Vie) needs a stout adventurer to go to the wilds and find a few items. He needs two tump stumps and a mantrap root.

The stumps are: one Kromdul (lore)(forest giants in Burning Woods), and one Kromdek type (lore)(forest giants in Frontier Mts). The mantrap root is from erollisi mantraps in the Emerald jungle).

Give him those three items and he will give you a Dark Oak Shillelagh, some faction, and some exp. He will also mention the "fertile crop" spell, which is the lead in for the quest for the Charm Plant Scroll.
