Animals and Summoned Mobs   Lists

The number of mobs that can be affected by the Summoned and Animal line of spells is bigger than most people think.

The majority of this list was compiled from information on Illia's Beastiary.

If you see anything that needs to be corrected or added, or have any suggestions, please contact me at Tesiux@eqdruids.com, or Spiritual at Spiritual@eqdruids.com.

If you are submitting information about a mob, please include the exact name of the mob, along with the zone and mob type.
 
Spells That Affect Animals:
(not including pet summon or buffs)
  • Level 1:   Lull Animal  /  Panic Animal  / Sense Animals
  • Level 9:   Invisibility vs Animals
  • Level 14: Befriend Animal
  • Level 19: Calm Animal /  Terrorize Animal
  • Level 34: Beguile Animal
  • Level 44: Allure of the Wild
  • Level 51: Repulse Animal
  • Level 52: Call of Karana
  • Level 53: Glamour of Tunare
  • Level 55: Tunare's Request
  • Level 63: Command of Tunare
Spells That Affect Summoned Beings:
  • Level 5:   Ward Summoned
  • Level 24: Dismiss Summoned
  • Level 34: Expel Summoned
  • Level 44: Banish Summoned
  • Level 55: Exile Summoned
  • Level 60: Banishment
  • Level 65: Destroy Summoned
