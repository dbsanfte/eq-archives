Level Twenty Four Spells

Resist Fire: 

Effect:  Increase Fire Resist from 34 (L24) to 40 (L30)

Skill: Abjuration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 50
Spell Duration: 360 ticks (36.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel resistant to fire.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is resistant to fire.
Spell fades: Your fire resistance fades.

This is your first Fire Resistance spell.  It increases your fire resistance up to a maximum of 40 points of resistance.

Nice spell to use, due to the number of fire based casters in the game.

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Spirit of Cheetah:  

Effect:  Increase Movement between 99% (L24) to 115% (L40)

Skill: Alteration
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: Self

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 20
Spell Duration: 8 ticks (48 seconds)
Duration Formula: 1
Casting Time: 0.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 600.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel the spirit of cheetah enter you.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is surrounded by a brief feline aura.
Spell fades: The spirit of cheetah fades.

This spell takes 15 mins to memorize, and 15 minutes to recover after casting.  

Why use it you ask?  Because this spell causes you go into jet powered mode!  It only lasts for 30 seconds, but 30 seconds is enough time to normally leave any mob in the dust.

It is a self only spell.

Purchase Spell:  Kelethin Druid Guild

Tremor:  

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 112 (L24) to 122 (L29)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: Point Blank AoE

Area Effect Range: 30 feet
Pushes Target Back: 1 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 25

Mana Required: 200
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 4.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 10.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You hear the ground rumble.

Tremor is a powerful spell that if used wrong will cause you to be killed in a heartbeat!

This is an area affect spell that radiates out from you in a circle.  So you can do up to 112-122 damage on anything standing near you.  The only problem is, if you don't kill all the mobs, you have probably just upset multiple mobs, and they will turn on you and smack you.

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Area Effect Range: 20 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 25

Mana Required: 40
Spell Duration: 3 ticks (18 seconds)
Duration Formula: 5
Casting Time: 3.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are blinded by a sunbeam.

Wolf Form:  

Effect:  Illusion: Wolf Elemental
Effect:  Increase Movement between 42% (L24) to 55% (L50)
Effect:  Increase Attack (ATK) from 25 (L24) to 30 (L29)

Skill: Alteration
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: Self

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 60
Spell Duration: 720 ticks (72.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 4.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 8.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are now a wolf.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> turns into a wolf.
Spell fades: You are no longer a wolf.

Alteration buff/form change - 60 mana - NEVER let this one down - auto cast SoW, more attacks, more damage, and you look really cool. - Gives Infravision. 

You will move a little faster than sow in this form.  You will also con indifferent to many things that might have once wanted to kill you.

Some mobs do not like illusions, so be careful at first when you enter a new zone e.g. guards at Thurgadin
