Gauntlets of Dragon Slaying (Kael)

 

Properties                           MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                            Armor, Hands

Armor Class                         30

Boosts                                +9 STR, +9 WIS, +9 INT, +75 HP, +75 MANA, +9 FR, +9 DR, +9 CR, +9 MR, +9 PR

Effect                                  Haste

Weight & Size                      WT: 0.0  Size: TINY

Class                                    ALL

Race                                    ALL

Talk to King Tormax who can be found within the giant city of Kael Drakkal. He tells you that he rules his land but has a problem, which he wishes you to solve for him. He wants you to eliminate Lord Yelinik or Dain Frostreaver. Killing Yelinik and returning with his head will result in the Gauntlets of Dragon Slaying.
