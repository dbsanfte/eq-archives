 Ricshaw's Guide for Druids to Trep's Shopping List Quest
(may also work for wizards)

First of all, if you aren't level 35, Trep won't give you the quest, so save this for when you're "old enough". :o)

If you are at least level 35, you may find this to be a very worthwhile quest. My level 41 druid is able to complete the
quest twice in 30 minutes, yielding 3 blue bubbles of experience (12% of a level) and about 60p for a half hour's work. After completing the quest several times, I have worked out the method that is most efficient for me, and that is what I am sharing here. While it is intended for druids, it may work as well for wizards, since they too can port to PoK. But to do it in 30 minutes, a wizard will need to get SoW from someone or use a potion. The nice thing is, SoW lasts just long enough to complete the quest, so getting it right at the start should do the trick.

Briefly, the quest involves purchasing 6 LORE items in different zones throughout the world (The Bazaar, Thurgadin, Shadeweaver's Thicket, Qeynos, Freeport, and Firiona Vie), combining them in a box provided by Trep (the quest-giver), and giving him the combined item. The rewards for the quest are 60p, and experience. At level 41, I get about 1.5 blue bubbles (about 6% of a level) for each turn-in, and you have to spend about 30p on the items, so the net profit is only about 30p each time. Since this 30-minute walk-through involves TWO turn-ins, you would get 12% experience (varies by level) and 60p profit in 30 minutes.

We begin with Trep Thilpin, a gnome NPC sitting on the grass at n430, p865 in the Plane of Knowledge (PoK). This is VERY close to the druid port-in point for Ring/Circle of Knowledge. To get the full quest info from Trep, you hail him, then say "I will do you a favor", then say "I am ready to begin". For this streamlined version, you will only need the last phrase--you can skip the preliminaries. The first time you do the quest, it will take a few minutes longer, unless you happen to already be bound next to Terman Underbelly in Thurgadin. :o) You will need to travel the "long way" (port yourself to Great Divide and run to Thurgadin, or use the PoK portal to Great Divide and run to Thurgadin) and then bind yourself next to Terman. If you don't want to bind there for some reason, and would prefer to have the quest take an extra 3-5 minutes, just port to GD and run to Thurgadin every time the instructions say to cast Gate.

Spells you need to have memorized: Gate, Ring of the Nexus, Ring of Knowledge, Scale of Wolf (faster than SoW, though it has to be recast a couple of times)

You will also need about 60p in cash to purchase the items as you go through the quest.

1. Target Trep and say "I am ready to begin." Place the box he gives you in your first inventory slot, for most efficient use of your time, as this will make the items you purchase automatically go right into the box.


2. Port yourself to the Nexus, cast Scale of Wolf on yourself, and run to the Bazaar. In the Bazaar, go into the merchant building (between the two banks) and upstairs to the bar. From Gaelsori Heriseron (the female NPC) purchase a Case of Meat (about 5p).


3. Cast Gate. (The first time, you must get yourself to Thurgadin some other way, and once you get all the way inside the city, turn right (NOT the way to the bank) and enter the first shop on your right. Terman Underbelly is there, by the wall rather than behind the counter. Bind yourself next to him, so you can gate in the next time around.) Purchase an armor assortment from Terman (about 5p).


4. Port to PoK, and cast Scale of Wolf on yourself. (It went away when you cast Gate to get to Thurgadin.) Run east to the area where the portals are to all the locations for this quest, and use the Shar Vahl portal to go to Shadeweaver's Thicket. Run to Saren Kaja (merchant in north building of encampment, loc n1426, n2795) and purchase a large ball of twine (about 5p). Run back to the PoK portal and return to PoK.


5. Use the next portal to the south and go to Qeynos. Run in through the north gates and go to the shop that the Priest of Discord is facing (Galliway's Trading Post). From Tanlyn Galliway purchase a keg of beer (about 5p). Run back to the PoK portal and return to PoK.


6. Follow the path straight south to the Freeport portal stone, and use it to travel to Freeport. When you appear by the book and pedestal, turn to your right and run out of the secret passage (through the "stone wall") and continue that direction until you reach the west gate of the city. Enter the city, and go to Krystin Charcoal (p102, p6). Puchase a purification tablet (less than 3s, the only item for this quest that is NOT just over 5p). Run back to the portal (make note of the location of the secret passage when you come out of it) and return to PoK.


7. Go to the portal stone for Firiona Vie, just north of the Freeport stone. Use it to travel to Firiona Vie, which will drop you in some caves there. Face east (away from the book and pedestal) and run though the caves until you can make a left turn. That tunnel will take you outside, but a number of drachnids (nasty spiders) will be around out there, so watch yourself. If you just run full speed, you'll probably be fine. The outpost and docks are southeast of the cave entrance. Run to the bridge (about n2400, p2000) and cross it to the outpost. Turn right at the other end of the bridge, and go to the second hut along that shoreline to find Dom Pathfinder (n2820, p2680). Purchase a bundle of weapons from him, and while the trade window is still up, combine the contents of your quest box. Then buy a bundle of weapons again. (Since the items are all LORE, you can't buy the second bundle of weapons until the first is eliminated by combining it.)


8. Repeat steps 2-6, purchasing another one of each of the quest items, so you'll have all 6 items again, as well as the combined set to turn in. After you make your last purchase, in Freeport, port yourself back to PoK instead of running back to the portal.


9. Run over to Trep. Give him the combined set, and you'll get your first reward. Tell him "I am ready to begin" again, and he'll give you another box. Fill it with your second batch of the quest items, combine it, and give the combined set to Trep for your second reward.

You have now completed this quest twice in 30 minutes or so. If you have another half hour to kill, or you need the experience and/or the cash, you can now start at step 1 (Trep's right there in front of you, after all) and do the whole thing again.
