Emerald Cloak of the Faydark (L. Fay)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                             Armor, Back

Armor Class                           5

Boosts                                   +10 CHA, +25 HP, +25 MANA, +10 DR, +10 PR

Effect                                    Fay Gate with 1 charge(s).

Weight & Size                        WT: 0.0  Size: MEDIUM

Class                                      ALL

Race                                      ALL

Talk to Talisyn Stormwing who can be found in Lesser Faydark. He will tell you that he is in a very foul mood this day. Ask him about it and he will mention about the new evils that are descending upon the land, such as the Tier'Dal near Mistmoore and some of the Tunarian followers were corrupted by evil powers. Equestrielle has become a blood thirsty beast as well as many others, like the pixies, or frightlings as they are called now. Tell him that you will assist him and he will give you your task. He will tell you about Bilewretch, the leader of the Frightlings and Bloodboil, the Undead Pixie. Head out into Lesser Faydark and kill Bloodboil to obtain Bloodboil's Wing. Hand this wing to Talisyn and you will be rewarded with the Tiny Skull Earring, or also the Emerald Cloak of the Faydark.
