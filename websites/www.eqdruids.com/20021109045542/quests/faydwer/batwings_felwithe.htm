Bat Wings (Felwithe)

Recommended level: 1
 
Find Niola Impholder in the magician's guild. She requires four bat wings for a spell she is researching.

For each four bat wings, you'll receive experience, a minor spell scroll, and the following faction adjustments:

    Factions Raised:

  • Keepers of the Art
  • Faydarks Champions
  • King Tearis Thex

    Factions Lowered:

  • The Dead
Some players will start out dubious to Niola. If this applies to you, bring a friend (who is not KOS and can cast invisibility) along. Initiate the trade with Niola, and before you click the trade button, have your friend cast invisibility on you. Once this is done, click the trade button and you should receive the faction adjustments.

If your faction is worse than dubious, cast Harmony on her prior to initiating the trade. Then use the above invisibility tactic, and continue until you can hand in bat wings without your friend's assistance.
