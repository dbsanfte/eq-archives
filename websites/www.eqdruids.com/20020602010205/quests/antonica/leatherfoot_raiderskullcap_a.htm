Leatherfoot Raider Skullcap (Rivervale)

Race    HFL

Talk to Marshall Anrey who can be found in Rivervale. Say "I am from Rivervale" and he will give you a quest in which you must bring him the following skins.

·  Polar Bear Skin (from polar bears in everfrost)

·  Grizzly Bear Skin (from grizzlies in the Karanas)

·  Shark Skin (from sharks, or buy from vendor at the Rivervale dock)

·  Alligator Skin (from bull alligators in Innothule Swamp).

The polar bear skin is dropped by polar bears in Everfrost. The Grizzly Bears who roam the Karanas drop the grizzly bear skin. To get the shark skin, either buy it from a vendor at the docks in Rivervale, or from Sharks who can be found in the Ocean of Tears or Erud's Crossing. For the alligator skin, head over to Innothule Swamp and kill all those Bull Alligators.

After you obtain all four skins, hand them in to Anrey and he will hand you back the Leatherfoot Raider Skullcap, but this is not "real one". Now Marshall Anrey will tell you that if you give him back the cap and a Dragoon Dagger, he will reward you with the real cap. Head off to Crushbone and kill D'Vinn, a named Dark Elf who lives in the tower. Hand in the dirk and cap to receive the true Leatherfoot Raider Cap.
