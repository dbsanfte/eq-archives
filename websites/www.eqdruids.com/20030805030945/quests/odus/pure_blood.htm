Grennik Neltrin can be found at the Gunthak Lighthouse area loc 1600, 60

 

You say, 'Hail, Grennik Neltrin'

 

Grennik Neltrin says 'Hello, Spiritual.  Have you been exploring the island?  I came here to discover the secrets of the Broken Skull trolls but I have yet to even explore the island.  Rumors that their high priest Nadox held the power to cure any disease have been drifting around Norrath for some time.  I've come to see if the rumors are true.  Unfortunately the island is much more dangerous than I had imagined and I haven't found anything useful.  If you find anything having to do with the troll High priest, please bring it to me.'

 

In a brew barrel combine A Blackened Vial + Broken Skull clan blood + Gunthak Gulf Water (foraged).  If you are successful you will receive a Black Potion

 

Now combine in a brew barrel a Black Potion + Pure Blood.  If you are successful you will receive A Glowing Black Potion.

 

The combine should be fixed now, to a no fail combine!

 

Hand this potion to Grennik Neltrin and you will receive the spell Pure Blood.

 

Broken Skull Clan Blood drops from Furious and Vehement Blackhands

Blackened Vials drop from Broken Skull Initiates and Adherents

Pure Blood drops from Luggald Abominations and Initiates

 

All the items for this quest are No Drop items
