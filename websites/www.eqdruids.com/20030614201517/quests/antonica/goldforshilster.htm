Gold for Guard Shilster (Qeynos)

Recommended level: 19
 
This quest begins in North Qeynos, and is actually initiated by doing the first part of the Investigator's Badge quest.

Find Vegalys Keldrane near the north gate. He will be at the top of the guard barracks overlooking the north gate. Tell him 'I am truly serious' and he will give you instructions to kill Robbie Shilster in North Karana and bring back his helm.

Teleport to the North Karana druid ring. Guard Shilster will be near the treeline north of the wooden bridge to East Karana. When you find him, do not kill him. Rather, hand in 5 gp.

Each time you hand him 5 gp, you'll receive some Blackburrow Stout, some experience, and the following faction adjustments:

    Factions Raised:

  • Corrupt Qeynos Guards
  • Kane Bayle
  • Circle of Unseen Hands
  • Bloodsabres

    Factions Lowered:

  • Guards of Qeynos

This is an excellent method of raising the Bloodsabre faction, without lowering a lot of good factions.

Guards of Qeynos faction is easily recovered by killing gnolls in Blackburrow.

This could be done at level 1, but is much easier to do at level 19 with the Ring of Karana spell.
