Level Fifty Five Spells

Annul Magic: 

  • Abjuration 

  • Mana cost 75 

  • Effect:  Upgrade to Nullify Magic

Nice upgrade to Nullify Magic.  This spell will remove two buffs/effects at a time instead of one.

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

Chloroblast:  

  • Alteration 

  • Mana cost 175 

  • Effect:  Healing

Very fast casting, healing spell.  Will heal 400 hit points a shot, with low mana cost.

Purchase From:  Rushka Deklamoor in East Commons Tunnel (near the Shady Swashbucklers)

Exile Summoned:  

  • Evocation 

  • Mana cost 250 

  • Effect:  Upgraded Direct Damage to Summoned

Upgraded Direct Damage to Summoned creatures.  Does 675 hps of damage per cast.

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

Girdle of Karana:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 105
  • Effect:  Strength Buff

Strength buff that adds 42 to your STR.

Dropped from NPCs

Nature Walkers Behest: 

  • Conjuration
  • Mana Cost 600 
  • Effect:  Summon Pet

Summons a tiny bear pet!  One of the druids at the NK druid circle normally has one if you want to see it.  Its about the size of a giant rat.

Reported drops in Plane of Growth and Temple of Veeshan

Tunare's Request:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 350 
  • Effect:  Charm Animal

And yet, another upgrade to charm animal.  There is a major difference in this one however.  When the charm wears off, the pet will not attack you.

Dropped from NPCs
