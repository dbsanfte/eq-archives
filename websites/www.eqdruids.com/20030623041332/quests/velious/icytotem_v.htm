You say, 'hail Keref Spiritspear'

Keref Spiritspear says 'Bark! Lo furless one. My people are Snowfang. Once mighty tribe, now snowfang home lost to vengeful winter.

You say, 'what vengeful winter'

Keref Spiritspear says 'Our tribe once many strong, then vengeful winter come. Tribe grow lost in storm of ice. Many moon, no food, no water, no home. Many snowfang die, many snowfang leave to find new home.

You say, 'what new home'

Keref Spiritspear says 'Many go north, not see again. Many family go, Keref family go. Aroooooooo. Someday Keref become brave, like mighty hero Groumshak. Keref go find pack to north.

You say, 'who is Groumshak'

Keref Spiritspear says 'Groumshak mighty snowfang hero. Groumshak make battle with furless one fifty paws high. When Groumshak return to tribe, vengeful winter fall. If you bring Keref mighty harpoon like Groumshak, then Keref become hero like Groumshak. Will give totem of great protection for thanks.

To get the spear Keref Spiritspear wants, you need to talk to Grizlin Bloodfang in the Snow Fang camp. He asks you to bring him:

1. Strong Wood (a Runed Totem Staff is what he seeks)
2. Strong Bone (a Whale Bone looted off sharks in Cobalt Scar)
3. Strong Rope (a Tinkered Rope from Ritap, bring him a Frost Giant Scout Beard)

When you hand him the above items, he hands you back a spear to give to Keref. At this point, however, he went insane and attacked me (even though I conned
Warmly). It's advised you gate or have SoW ready.

After you clear the hate list (by gating or outrunning and camping), bring the spear to Keref Spiritspear. When you hand him the spear, he gives you an Icy Totem of Protection as the reward. However, like Grizlin when you handed him the stuff to make the spear, he goes insane and attacks you. Again, be ready to run. Even though he attacks you, you still get the reward.
