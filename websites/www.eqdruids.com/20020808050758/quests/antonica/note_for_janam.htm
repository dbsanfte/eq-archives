Note for Janam / Note for Rebby / The Frikniller Family (Freeport)
Recommended level: 1

 

These are good quests to raise your faction with the Freeport Militia and Corrupt Qeynos Guards and not lower any other factions. These all start in the East Freeport sewers.

Locate Nestral T'Gaza. She wants you to take a note to Rebby. Rebby is located near the Theatre of the Tranquil in West Freeport. When you deliver the note, you will receive Rebby's rat whiskers (not NODROP) which you deliver back to T'Gaza for experience, faction, and some money.

Locate Harkin Duskfoot. Tell him "What message" and he will ask you to deliver a message to Janam near the Theatre of the Tranquil in West Freeport. When you deliver the note, you will receive a note to return to Harkin. You will receive experience, faction, and a Used Merchants Gloves for giving the note to Harkin.

Locate Gren Frikniller. He will ask you to deliver a letter to his sister Falia near the bank in North Freeport. When you deliver the note, Falia will give you a broken heirloom to return to Gren. Giving the heirloom to Gren gives you experience and faction.

For each return deliver, you'll receive the following faction adjustments:

    Factions Raised:

  • Carson McCabe
  • Coalition of Trade Folk
  • Coalition of Tradefolk Underground
  • Corrupt Qeynos Guards
  • Freeport Militia
