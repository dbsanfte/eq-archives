Twilight's Hunting Guide

I've got 5 level 50+ toons now so I thought I would post my preferences for hunting.  Always keep your next few series of spells on you if you are traveling deep into Kunark and will ding soon...

Please note im assuming a tad of twinking here - some of this might be tough on a new non-split server

Under Level 20

Level 1-6 Right outside your starting town...you need level 6 so your corpse wont rot super fast.

Level 7-19 Field of Bone - Bone pit and Grassy area between Kurn's and Kaesora  (Misty Thicket is dang good from Level 1-12)

Level 1-12 Misty Thicket is great

Level 11-19 Kurn's Tower (leave the penguins alone they cast)

Level 18-23 East Karana, gnolls

Level 20-30

Level 19-24 Swamp of No Hope - Bandit camp and bugs/frogloks running around the obelisk

Level 20-31 Lake of Ill Omen Sarnaks and Goblins

Level 25-33 Sol A

Level 25-35 South Karana, aviaks and other mobs

Level 27-32 Dalnir lower floors

Runnyeye, Unrest, Crystal Caverns, and Mines of Nurga are decent dungeons too at this level

Level 30-40

Level 29-36 Tizmaks in Great Divide

Level 29-33 Frontier Mountains Giant camp and various brutes

Level 32-46 Overthere - anything starting from thorny succulants ending up with tigers

Level 30-35 Ulthorks in Eastern Wastes

Level 30-35 Various mobs in Firiona Vae

Level 35-48 Lower Guk - either side various camps

Level 35-48 Sol B Kobolds

Level 34-48 Dwarfs in BB

Level 29-35 Cougars in Iceclad

Kaesora is a decent dungeon with a good exp bonus at these levels

Level 40-53

Level 46-53 Bats in Sol B

Level 48-53 King and Frenzied camp in Lower Guk

Level 49-53 Front parts of Karnos

Level 45-53 Dreadlands outside of Karnors and along Northwall of zone

Level 49-55 Burning Woods outside giant fortress and other areas

Level 51-60 Skyfire

Level 49-58 Raptors in TD

Level 40 - 52 Eastern Wastes

Level 39-47 CoM and Emerald Jungle

Level 49-54 Wyverns and Drakes in Cobalt Scar

The Hole is a decent dungeon with a good exp bonus at these levels

Level 53-58

Sebilis, Howling Stones, Raptors in TD, Chardok

Buthar in Cobalt Scar 

Othmir in Cobalt Scar

Sentient Suites of Armor in Wakening Lands

Geonids in WL

Level 59-60

Sebilis, Howling Stone, Chardok
