Miranda's Dice

In Kejek, the kerran city in Stonebrunt, there is a small girl named Miranda near the firepit. Hail her, and she will say "Im not supposed to talk to strangers but if my parents let you in here I guess it's ok. Do you like [candy]? I would give you some candy to play a game with me but my [dice] are gone now." The dice in question are in the Warrens, and they drop off of Prince Bragnar, and are a fairly common drop if you are in the correct level range. To finish the quest, give Miranda the dice.
