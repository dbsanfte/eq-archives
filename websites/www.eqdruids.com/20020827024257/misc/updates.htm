Ok some information on the future of EQDruids!

There is going to be a LOT of work going on around here, so be prepared!  Some of the things I think you'll like, some you might not even notice (things to make my life easier)

Some of the plans at this time:

  • Adding a chat room

  • Putting a lot of the information (spells, items, hall of fame info) into databases.  Makes it much easier for me to update.  Along with this, there are plans to add the ability for the users to add items.

  • I will be adding some banner ads.  This is purely to help with the costs.  I will not be putting "just anything up".  It will be companies that I feel are worth using.  Don't worry, they will not be plastered all over the place.  It shouldn't interfere with anyone's enjoyment.

  • There will be a Donors Wall added.  Those that donate will have their names added acknowledging their donations!

  • All donors will be given their own @eqdruids.com e-mail address.  This will be a permanent address, no renewal needed.  A single dollar donation is all that will be necessary to receive this e-mail address.

  • EQDruids will also be doing website hosting.  Again to help with the costs.  This will be your own domain name, not off the EQDruids.com domain, hosted on our server.  The price will be very reasonable.  So if you are considering a guild or personal web page, wait for our prices.

  • A lot of the information that I inherited is becoming out of date.  There will be a major push to not only update the information we have, but to continue writing new guides.

  • There are going to be auctions run on E-bay also for different items that are being sold to help support EQDruids.  NO, they won't be items from in EQ!  It will be anything from trading cards, comics, antique computers, unusual items etc.  There will be link to our auctions so you can browse them, and if an EQDruids visitor purchases an item, there will be a discount on the purchase price.

  • The Epic Quest section is about to have a major overhaul.  You will the option of reading several different guides, including specific guides for such as Faydedar tactics.

  • There are more things that are in store for the EQDruids visitors, but some of them I want to keep to myself for now :)  I promise you will enjoy them all!
