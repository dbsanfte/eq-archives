All the below spells are found by turning in a Spectral Parchment to Wanderer Astobin in the Plane of Knowledge Library.  They are completely random in which spell you will receive.

 

Blessing of Replenishment:

Allowable Targets: Group Buff
Beneficial: Beneficial

Area Effect Range: 60.00 feet

Resistance Check: None
Base Difficulty: 5%

Mana Cost: 650
Spell Duration: 10 minutes
Duration Formula: 19.9 mins @ lvl 63 to 20.5 mins @ lvl 65
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
