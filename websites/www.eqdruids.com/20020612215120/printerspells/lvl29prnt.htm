Greater Healing: 

Effect:  Increase Hit Points (HP) from 288 (L24) to 300 (L30)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 150
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 3.75 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel much better.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> feels much better.

This is the last upgrade to your healing spells that you will receive in a while, so enjoy this one while it is handy!

This spell will heal up to 298 hps per cast.  At lvl 29 this is fairly decent, when you are in your upper 40's this spell doesn't do much when healing tanks.

This is where the cleric spells start to pull ahead of hybrid healing spells.  You are still useful as a healer, but prob not with tanks.

Scale of Wolf:  

Effect:  Negate if Combat

Skill: Alteration
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: All

Spell Duration: 450 ticks (45.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 3.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel the spirit-scale of wolf enter you.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is surrounded by a dark lupine aura.
Spell fades: The scale of wolf leaves you.

This is an upgrade to SoW in a way.  It is faster than SoW by about 2 times.  But it has limitations.  It can only be cast on yourself, or someone in your group (it overwrites sow).

Succor: East:

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Full Group

Area Effect Range: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 300
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 9.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> creates a mystic portal.
Spell fades: The portal shimmers and fades.

This is your first Succor spell.  They work a little different than your normal teleports.  The succor spell will take you and your party to the appropriate druid circle.

The difference is, the cast time is half the normal group teleport, but you can leave some of your party behind.  So don't use these for normal teleporting, only in emergency situations.

The above Succor will take you to East Karana.

Purchase From:  Toxxulia Druid Circle
