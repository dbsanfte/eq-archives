Coldain Military Wristguard

 

With the Coldain Military Bracers quest, you must have a least

dubious faction to the coldain and to the Kromrif/Kromzek also, if you do not have this, the starting faction for a Troll Warrior with the religion of Rallos Zek sets coldain to apprehensive and giants of Kromrif/Kromzek to dubious and makes the quest possible.

In The great Divide, kill a Coldain War Wolf, (PH's being the coldain tracking wolves around the coldain areas). Coldain War wolf drops a war wolf choker neck slot item magic/lore.

Bring the choker to a giant wolf tamer under the bridge to the King Tormax faction areas (do not get near them... as they are usually kos to everyone), just jump down the side of bridge carefully for 20dmg fall.

After giving him the choker he gives you a wolf harness that you could alternatively get by killing Drakkel Blood wolf in great divide if you do not care about giant faction (such as using another character).

With the wolf harness now in your possession say "I am looking for more work".

He tells you his friend Erdarf Restil in Thurgadin (he is in a bar near cleric fort) needs a shipment bottle of karsin acid.

Bottle of karsin acid is no drop , and is looted off of random easy kill orcs in Crystal Caverns, about 1 out of 15 drops a bottle.

With your troll's faction, or if yours is good with both giant and dwarf then give the karsin bottle to Erdarf, and he gives you a gem of persuasion.

Now, with Gem of Persuasion, you can do the Kromrif military leggings OR you can do this.

Go back to giant wolf tamer, give him gem of persuasion, he gives you a giant's version of the gem.

Give him the wolf harness and the giant version of the gem, and he gives you his giant control harness.

Bring this giant control harness to the Shardwurm Broodmother in Great Divide.

After giving Broodmother the giant control harness, it heads to the Coldain Mine camp in the south east part of the zone where if you have tracking, you can track the 2 dwarfs outside the cave, these 2 dwarfs were originally deep inside the cave doing nothing, and drop nothing, and Korf being lvl55 when not being in the quest.

Wait for Broodmother to come, she suddenly comes running to the side of the cave and starts attacking, 1 second later, the dwarf with the coldain bracers (Korf Brokenhammer) starts running southwards (right of the cave entrance), hurry and root him , if you miss him you may miss chance on the bracer.

Now, the broodmother had became Korf's pet and would hit me if i was too close to Korf, so i stayed back and just root/dot, the broodmother never runs to attack you even if she is agro as a pet, she just sits there.

When Korf is dead, broodmother depops, and the coldain military bracer is on the corpse.
