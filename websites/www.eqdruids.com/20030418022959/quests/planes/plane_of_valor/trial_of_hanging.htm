Trial of Hanging

 

Talk to Mavuin in the basement cellblock. Hail him and follow the dialogue. He will ask you to plead his case before the Tribunal, aggreeing will grant you a character flag.

Head to the area with The Tribunal, and ask them about Mavuin. Following the dialog, saying "I am prepared" to each Tribunal will give you description of the six trials.

To do this one, say "I am ready to begin the trial of hanging." Make sure you are actually prepared when you say that because your group will be teleported to the event area where you will only get a few seconds before things start.

Two mobs + Spirit of Suffocation spawn at the beginning, kill Spirit then get on a mob, another spirit will spawn, kill the spirit right away. Basically each wave will be 2 mobs and 2 spirits, but only 1
spirit can be up at one time. Razorbeasts are unmezzable, air elementals are mezzable. There will be messages to tell you how long you have to kill the spirit of suffocation before you lose. If one of the 3 hanging people falls over on the ground, KILL IT FAST , otherwise you will lose. After the spirits of suffocation are dead in the 4th wave, the boss will spawn. When we spawned the boss we had 3 mobs up that were mezzed still from previous rounds. Boss is an enchanter, hits for 720 max and rampages. He's about as tough as all the other ones.

Finishing this gives you the Mark of Hanging.

Now talk to the Tribunal and say "what evidence of Mauvuin" and they will tell you that they have consented to hear Mauvuin's case. Head back down and talk to him and he will ask you to go see Karana and Mithaniel Marr. This will flag you to be able to enter the Plane of Storms and and Plane Valor
