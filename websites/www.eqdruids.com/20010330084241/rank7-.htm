1) Bramblecoat. This spell is an upgrade to the druid damage coat line of spells. It adds 17 AC and does 3 points of damage to any creature that lands a successful attack. The spell lasts approximately twice as long as the Skin Like Steel spell and should be kept up at all times. It is a self only spell so you cannot cast this on other people.

2) Circle of Butcher, Commons, Karana and Tox. These are the most desired spells of druids and their friends. With these spells you can teleport groups (must be in groups) to other far distant zones. You cannot unfortunately teleport someone without you going with them though. These spells take a very long time to cast, longer than any other spell; therefore they should not be treated as emergency escape spells. These spells also take a whopping 300 mana to cast. There is a downside to these spells also. You will be sent many /tells by others asking you to gate them to such and such place. Get used to being harassed often.

Butcher -- Takes you to the skeleton ruins near the docks in Butcher Block. There is a druid vendor there.

Commons -- This spell takes you to the druid ruins in West Commons near the dervish camp. There is a druid vendor there.

Karana -- This spell takes you to the druid ruins in North Karana near the bridge to South Karana. Be careful as sometimes when you zone here there are griffons and Hill Giants about. There is a druid vendor here.

Toxuul -- This spell takes you to the druid ruins in Toxuul Forest, right outside the city of Erudin. There is a druid vendor here.

3) Combust. The druids next fire based direct damage, this spell does a nice 171 points of damage to a single target. The damage for this spell increases by three points up to level 34, where as it peaks at 182 points of damage. The fire damage spells available to druids cast quicker than any other direct damage spell and are excellent in combat.

4) Counteract Disease.This spell is a more powerful version of the cure disease spell. It cures more powerful diseases that cure disease cannot. A decent spell but I have never used it personally.

5) Counteract Poison. An important spell to get by level 30 because in several more higher level dungeons, you will be poisoned often. There are a few poisons that do not do much damage and can easily be waited out but some can do several hundred points of damage if left untreated.

6) Ensnare. This spell has been fixed - now lasts over 10 minutes!!!

7) Extinguish Fatigue. This spell wipes out all traces of fatigues by restoring a person's entire stamina. This is very useful in Kedge Keep and long melee fights for your tanks.  

8) Greater Healing. This spell is the last in the series of instant healing spells. It heals a nice 298 points of damage but be careful. You can really piss off a lot of MOBs because they consider as you doing 298 points of damage to them. If you have several monsters attacking the group at once and you cast this on one person, the entire MOB will target you....deadly if your group does not have a back up healer.

9) Immolate. This spell is another Damage Over Time spell that can be stacked with the Swarm/Crud/Drone/Drifting Death spells. It does about 250 points of damage in half the time as the other DoT's. It has about a 25% chance of being resisted though. I usually use this as my starter DoT followed by Drones of Doom after combat has started. It is wonderful if both stick though as the MoB will lose about 500 hps (400 if you are using Creeping Crud).  Spell lasts about 60 seconds. You must have a fire beetle eye in inventory to cast this spell and it is not consumed

10) Ring of Misty. The last personal druid teleport spell and the one most anticipated by most druids. This spell teleports you to the small druid ruins just outside Rivervale in Misty Thicket. It takes a long time to cast but is very useful.

11) Shield of Brambles. This is an upgrade to the druid damage shield line. It is an excellent spell to cast on your tanks before battle but is a short enough duration where it is not efficient to keep up all the time. The shield does 10 points of damage whenever a creature lands a successful attack. This damage increases to 11 damage at level 30, and 12 damage at level 34. A must have for druids.

12) Succor: East. This is the first emergency escape spell that teleports the party to the East Karana druid ruins. There is no vendor here. The spell does take a long time to cast, but not nearly as long as the normal group gate spells. It consumes 300 mana so make sure you have enough mana to get this spell off. In the higher level dungeons, this spell can save your parties' lives as it has done for my group. Example: A poor pull caused 6 higher level spell casting MOBs to jump our party shortly after battle commenced on the intended pull target. With this spell I was able to cast it at the first sign of trouble and pulled all but one person to safety (only because this person was running too far away and was out of spell range). Get this and keep it ready if you are in dungeons.

 13) Beguile Plants.  should charm some of the Kunark plant life. This spell is available thru a quest  - Talk to the little halfling in FV

You hail him, and he'll give you  a quest for a weapon....you can either do
that quest, or simply say "What Fertile crop?"  and he'll give you the quest
for the Beguile plants spell....

See the Quests section for more detail on this spell and quest

14) Scale of Wolf.  Faster than Sow but drops  if you attack - will overwrite wolfform or sow. Located by docks right off the Kunark boat.  This spell does NOT drop if you are attacked in melee - but drops if you are cast upon or hit with a ranged weapon

15)Imbue Plains Pebble - creates a magic plains pebble that is used for smithing armor.  The spells is located in the North Karana on Tak Whistler.  The resultant Pebble is tradeable. The spell is limited to worshippers of Karana.

16)Imbue Emerald - creates a magic emerald that is used for smithing armor.  The spells is located in the Clerics Guild in Felwithe - its says cleric only but is writable.  The spell is limited to worshippers of Tunare.
