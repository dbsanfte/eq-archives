Reinforced Cobalt Drake Hide Armor

This armor is not a "simple" armor to make.  It is one of the Velious armors.  You have to collect several different pieces to make this armor.  The trivial on this armor is 250+

The cobalt drake hides drop off cobalt drakes in Cobalt Scar.  The velium blocks drop in the Crystal Caverns and Velks Lab.

Drake egg oil is made by combining a drake egg (foraged in Skyshrine) and a flask of water in a brewing barrel.
