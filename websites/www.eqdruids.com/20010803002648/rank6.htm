1) Resist Fire - Abjuration Buff - 50 mana - Adds to your resist fire save (+40)- great in lavastorm and NPCs

2) Skin Like Steel - Abjuration Buff - 100 mana - Adds AC & max HPs as per the skin line of spells - always have this on you and the main tank.  Long lasting but expensive to cast.  

3) Spirit of Cheetah - Alteration Buff and Movement - 20 mana - kick butt ass hauling speed spell for 30 seconds...great for that emergency zone run with a werewolf on your butt or another SoW speed MOB. This spell refreshes after 15 minutes real time.

This spell will overwrite wolf form and give you some time to make for a zone.

4) Dismiss Summoned - Evocation DD - 90 mana - Summoned blaster for max 157 damage slow casting but useful in groups.

5) Pogonip - Evocation AE - 125 mana - casts Rain spell at 62 x 3 damage - limited use

6) Tremor - Evocation AE - 200 mana - casts a large scale AE - Radiates from yourself - If you can have some mobs close buy this does significant damage to all of them. 112-122 damage

7) Creeping Crud - Conjuration DoT - 100 mana - the upgrade to Stinging Swarm - good starter and kiter - 210 damage over 13 ticks

8 ) Ensnaring  Roots - Alteration Root - 60 mana - an upgrade damage root

9) Sun Beam - Divination AE Blindness - 40 mana - Blindness spell - use before combat - also can be used as Kiting Variation

10) Wolf Form - Alteration buff/form change - 60 mana - NEVER let this one down - auto cast SoW, more attacks, more damage, and you look really cool. - Gives Infravision.  If you have SOW on this will not cast - remove SOW first.

7/1/99 - in wolf form you are what you wear - if you were a metallic piece you will be solid - see through for any other - the green scarab boots make you have green feet ;-)

11) Charm Animals - Alteration Charm - 120 mana - this give a better chance of charming higher level monsters

12 ) Ring of Lavastorm, Ro, Steamfont, Feerot, combines - Alteration RING - 150 mana - takes you to the respective ring...Ring of the combines is sold in the kelethin druid guild
