Belt of Dwarf Slaying (Kael)

 

Properties                         MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                          Armor, Waist

Armor Class                      20

Boosts                              +15 STR, +15 WIS, +15 INT, +100 HP, +100 MANA, +5 CR, +5 DR, +5 FR, +5 MR, +5 PR

Effect                                 Aura of Battle

Weight & Size                   WT: 0.0  

Class                                  ALL

Race                                   ALL

 

Talk to King Tormax who can be found within the giant city of Kael Drakkal. He tells you that he rules his land but has a problem, which he wishes you to solve for him. He wants you to eliminate the old dragon Yelinak or the pitiful Dain Frostreaver. Killing Dain and returning with his head will result in the Belt of Dwarf Slaying.
