In Cobalt Scar, there is a creature by the name of Bungre Crawcrusher. If you talk to him he will tell you that he can make a great shield but you must bring him a Piece of Lodizal's Shell, which is dropped by Lodizal in the Iceclad Ocean and 10000gp. Handing him both items will result in this shield. This item can also be equipped in the back slot.
