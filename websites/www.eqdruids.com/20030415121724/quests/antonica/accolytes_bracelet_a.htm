Acolytes Anklet

 

Talk to Gapeers Johhanis who can be found in the cleric guild in Rivervale. He will tell you that he needs three samples of cursed and drowned flesh. The three items that he needs are the Algae Covered Flesh which drop off the Zombie Sailors in Erud's Crossing, Swollen Flesh found on a Greenblood Zombie in the Feerrot, and Waterlogged Flesh which drops off the Drowned Citizen. Hand in the three pieces of zombie flesh and you will be rewarded with the Acolyte's Anklet.
