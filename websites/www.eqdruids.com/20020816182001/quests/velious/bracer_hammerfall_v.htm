Bracer of Hammerfall (Skyfire)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                              Armor, Wrist

Armor Class                           6

Boosts                                   +5 STA, +3 WIS, +25 HP

Effect                                     Summon Throwing Hammer

Weight & Size                        WT: 4.1  Size: ?

Class                                      WAR, CLR, PAL, RNG, SHD, DRU, MNK, BRD, SHD

Race                                      ALL

Talk to Sentry Kale who can be found in Skyshrine. He will tell you to go and pick up some Velium in the Dwarven mines, and he then hands you a note. Take this note and 50 pp to Ungdin, a dwarf in the mines under Thurgadin. He will in turn hand you the velium shipment to return to Kale. Return to Skyshrine and hand the shipment to Kale and you may sometimes be rewarded with the Bracer of Hammerfall
