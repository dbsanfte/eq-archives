RPGComics and their Custom Artwork

I want to make sure that I let as many folks know about RPGComics and their custom artwork as possible!

These guys do FANTASTIC custom artwork.  You send them a screenshot of your character, and what you want the picture to look like and they will send you some of the nicest artwork you could ever imagine!
