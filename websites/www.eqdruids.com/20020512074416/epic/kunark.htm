4. The Dragon Faydedar in Timourous Deep.  This will be the longest camp for most of you.  He spawns at the Firepot room which is under the wizard spires in the southwest corner of the zone.  There have been many suggestions on how to kill this baddy and I will only mention the one I used here.

        - Buff your druid with 5 dummy buffs.  NO buffs like thorncoat or anything that does DMG to a mob if it is attacked.  This is because Faydedar is a 50+ Epic MOB and he summons if you do damage to him.  We would like to prevent the summoning so you stay alive =).  Examples, See invis, resist poison, resist cold, resist disease, and one other.  Then load up with these buffs:  SKin like Nature or better, Resist Magic, Resist Fire, Levitate, SOW.  The reason for the dummy buffs is because the dragon dispells you and you must recast these upon yourself so he does not take down your good buffs which are also on you.  Med him to full and camp out on the wizard spire in Timourous Deep.

        - Start a level 1 character in freeport and get him a sow and a levitate.  DMF would be good sense you have to run a long ways.  Run him to Oasis and take boat to TD.  Run him to the spires and sit him down there.  Now you leave your computer on for what might be the next 200 hours. LOL.  That is the longest known camp thus far.  I recomend letting several of your friends know so they can watch your "Bait" while you are away from your PC.  I carry a cell-phone so this part was not that bad as I did not have to be home the whole time which would have been impossible.

        - When bait is killed you have 2 hours to login your buffed druid and start kiting this little guy.  Do not be surprised, he is a very little dragon.  Keep recasting your buffs as he dispells them.  Kite him around and rally your forces.  Once everyone is in the zone you can pull him to the chessboard and let them take him down.  If you die do not worry as he will return to the spires first.  Then you have 2 hours once again before he wanders to the iksar master in the zone that will kick his butt.  Thats right.  If his AGRO list is clear then he will only live for 2 hours.  After 2 hours have passed he will run into the Iksar master and the Iksar MAster wins....go figure.
