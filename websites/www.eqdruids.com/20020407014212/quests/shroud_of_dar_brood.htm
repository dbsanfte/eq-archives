Shroud of the Dar Brood (WW)

Type, Slot                             Armor, Back

Armor Class                          14

Boosts                                  +8 STA, +9 WIS, +9 INT, +20 HP, +50 MANA, +10 CR, +10 FR, +10 MR

Effect                                    Haste

Weight & Size                      WT: 1.0  Size: ?

Class                                     ALL

Race    ALL

Talk to Harla Dar who can be found in the Western Wastes. He has lost a hatchling named Frakadar and will reward you if you can Frakadar for her.

Well as it turns out, Frakadar was eaten by Zlandicar, a huge dragon who lives in the Dragon Necropolis. Zlandicar will sometimes drop Frakadar's Talisman when he's killed. Return this item to Harla Dar and you will be rewarded with the Shroud of the Dar Brood.

Note: This item can be worn on the back and the chest slots.
