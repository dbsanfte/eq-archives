Level 54 spells are available either off the Druid Spell vendor in Firona Vie, or are dropped off various mobs.

Blizzard

Blizzard: 

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) by 490 (L54)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: Target Based AoE

Range to Target: 150 feet
Area Effect Range: 20 feet

Resistance Check: Cold

Mana Required: 332
Spell Duration: Three Waves
Casting Time: 3.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 12.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are caught in a raging blizzard.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is caught in a raging blizzard.

Cold Based Area Effect rain spell that does 3 rains of 490 per hit.

Dropped by NPCs

Regrowth:  

Effect:  Increase Hit Points (HP) by 15 (L52)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 300
Spell Duration: Unknown
Duration Formula: 10
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Form of the Howler:  

Effect:  Illusion: Wolf Elemental
Effect:  Increase Movement between 62% (L54) to 65% (L60)
Effect:  Increase Mana by 2 (L54)
Effect:  Ultravision

Skill: Alteration
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: Self

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 100
Spell Duration: 1440 ticks (144.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are now a wolf.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> turns into a wolf.
Spell fades: You are no longer a wolf.

Upgrade to wolf form.  This spell increases your movement rate by 65%, increases your mana by 2 and gives ultravision.  Nice speed increase.

Dropped by NPC's
