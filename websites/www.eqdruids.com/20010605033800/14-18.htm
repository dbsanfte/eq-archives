Some choice level 14 zones :
Field of Bones
The Karanas

Hail to the new level 14 spells!!!!!

At this point you should have been conjuring your butt off and have now purchased at least the first 5 or 6 level 14 spells.

Hence you now have a choice: To Kite or not to Kite

Kiting used to be cool - but now its basically snare and nuke - a weaker version of a wizzies root and nuke (nuke bombing).  Basically Sow yourself, snare the target, run off, cast a nuke (DD spell), run off again, repeat - very boring but you take no damage.

Through level 20 ( and 39+)you can do combat as follows : Start with Swarm, cast Shield of Thistle on your self, Melee for a bit, burn to death if it bothers you too much.  The recovery time is more than with masterful kiting...but its not nearly as boring.

If you picked a half-elf or human you might not have enough mana at 14 to kite the mean stuff...give it some time and stay near the zone walls until you have got your tactics down.

At this phase you also get the 'beggar buffs' - specifically Spirit of the Wolf and Bind Affinity.  You can be anonymous or charge for it...or give it away for free and waste a lot of time.  Just be ready for the begging.
