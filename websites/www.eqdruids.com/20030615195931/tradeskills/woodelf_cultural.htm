Cultural Woodelf Armors Components

Oak Bark Tannin - oak bark (foraged in Kithicor Forest & Warslik's Woods, flask of water combined in a brew barrel (trivial 102)

Mithril Boning - small brick mithril (purchased in Felwithe), file, morning dew (foraged in Faydark's) (trivial 68)

Infused Mithril Boning - celestial temper, small brick mithril, file

  • Celestial Temper - blue diamond dust + purified water

  • Blue Diamond dust - blue diamond combined with acid in a kiln.

  • Purified water - 2 water flasks + celestial essence

  • Celestial essence - celestial solvent + high level rune/word/page in a mortar and pestle

Mithril Bits - 2 small pieces mithril, morning dew

Mithril Studs - 3 mithril bits, file, morning dew (yields 2)

Feir'dal Sewing Kit - mithril bits, needle mold, thimble mold, flask water

Embroidering Needle - needle mold, metal bits, celestial essence, water
