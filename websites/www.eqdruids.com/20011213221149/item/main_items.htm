PR MR DR FR CR - Any poison, magic, disease, fire, or cold resist

WT - Weight of item

WP - Weight Penalty

 

This is the ranking system we used, on the ranked items (not including the weapons)

.

Each point of WIS = 10 pts

Each point of Mana = 1 pt

Each point of AC = 4 pts

Each point of AGI = 2 pts

Each point of STR = 2 pts

Each point of CHA = 2 pts

Every 2 points of fire/cold/magic resistance = 1 pt

Every 4 points of poison/disease = 1 pt

 

For armor items, every 5 lbs of weight we subtracted 2 pts. 

 

If the item had an effect we added 30 points.  I did not do any categories to effects.  An effect that I think is very important, someone else wouldn't care about.  So I gave a bonus for effect but no ranking of the effects.

 

The weapons are ranked on the damage/delay ratio.  Also we ranked weapon penalties at every 3 lbs of weight, subtract 2 points due to folks stamina lost with weapons.
