Raiding with LDoN 7/29/03

From EQLive:

Lost Dungeons of Norrath - Raid Content

In designing Lost Dungeons of Norrath, we haven't forgotten about those who enjoy raiding.  In addition to the multitude of rewarding single-group adventures, LDoN also includes exciting and challenging events targeted at six groups or less.

For the first time, raids take place in truly private zones, where your groups can test their mettle and focus solely on the task at hand.  Instead of limiting the availability of content by who can get there first, this new system only limits participation based on whether or not you have recently succeeded or failed at each event within a given period.  This allows us to place a heavy emphasis on skill, while remaining in control of the influx of new items into the game.

While our primary focus for LDoN is the single group experience, we are working diligently to ensure that these new raids are centered around the principles of organizational tactics, individual skill, and most importantly - fun. 

We feel that the core content and rewards in the Lost Dungeons of Norrath expansion will entertain and benefit even the most experienced players.  We are aware of the desire for high-end raid content and will continue to address and meet those needs over time.

- The EverQuest Team

New Spells on Test Server 7/24/03

Let me make sure I'm clear at the beginning of this announcement these spells are ALL still on Test Server only!  Very possibly for the Lost Dungeons of Norrath.  I do not have any more information than this so far, but I thought I would bring them to folks attention.

Circle of Cooling - Level 29 spell that increases fire resists by 39

Circle of Warmth - Level 39 spell that increases cold resists by 39

Talisman of Purity - Level 49 spell that increases poison resists by 30

Talisman of Vitality - Level 49 spell that increases disease resists by 30

Spirit of the Shrew and Pack Shrew (level 24 & 29) - Increases movement speed by 20% and gives infravision.

Spirit of the Mongoose and Pack Mongoose (level 34 &39) - Increases movement speed by 30% and gives Ultravision

Remove Minor curse and Remove Lesser Curse (level 19 & 24) - Removes curses

Form of the Predator - Level 65 spell that gives Wolf form with 67% increase in speed, ultravision and decreases your attack speed by 20%.

 

For those reading this and thinking "that sure looks like sow on the shrew and mongoose" the important thing to know is those spells can be cast INDOORS.

 

For more details you can visit Lucy for the full spell details

 

 - Spiritual
