Banishment: 

Effect:  Destroy Target

Requires Star Ruby as a reagent

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: Summoned

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 5

Mana Required: 500
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 4.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 3.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 120.0 seconds

This spell will only work on a summoned creature.  When you cast this spell if successful the following will happen:

 

The mob is instantly killed, and their corpse vanishes.  You will receive no loot and no exp for kill.

 

Great for emergencies, don't use on something you actually need loot from.

 

Dropped by NPCs

 

Reported to drop in Old Sebilis

 

This spell can also be researched

Mask of the Hunter:  

Effect:  Increases your size by 25%
Effect:  Increase Magnification by 115%
Effect:  Increase Mana by 3 per tick
Effect:  See Invisible

Skill: Alteration
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: Self

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 100
Spell Duration: 3 hours @ lvl 60 to 3 hours 15 mins @ lvl 65
Casting Time: 3.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: Your features sharpen.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> features sharpen.
Spell fades: Your features return to normal

Shrinks the caster, magnifies the casters eyesight and lets the caster see invisible.  Nice for scouting out new areas.

 

Includes:

 

Regen 3 mana/tick

 

It is an outdoor only spell.

 

Dropped by NPCs

 

Reported to drop in Old Sebilis and the Royals in Chardok

Also have reports of dropping off of Talendor and Guardian Wurm in Skyfire

 

Has dropped off of Servelous in Emerald Jungle
