Level Fifty Five Spells

Annul Magic: 

Effect:  Cancel Magic + 9 levels
 

Skill: Abjuration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 75
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 4.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel annulled.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> feels annulled.

Nice upgrade to Nullify Magic.  This spell will remove two buffs/effects at a time instead of one.

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

 

Chloroblast:  

Effect:  Increase Hit Points (HP) by 428 (L55)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 175
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 3.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are blasted with chlorophyll.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is blasted with chlorophyll.

Very fast casting, healing spell.  Will heal 400 hit points a shot, with low mana cost.

Purchase From:  Rushka Deklamoor in East Commons Tunnel (near the Shady Swashbucklers)

 

Exile Summoned:  

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 675 (L55) to 725 (L60)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: Summoned

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 5

Mana Required: 250
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 5.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> staggers.

Upgraded Direct Damage to Summoned creatures.  Does 675 hps of damage per cast.

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

 

Girdle of Karana:  

Effect:  Increase Strength by 42 (L55)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 105
Spell Duration: 720 ticks (72.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 5.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 7.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel the strength of Karana infuse you.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> looks stronger.
Spell fades: Your Karanic strength fades.

Strength buff that adds 42 to your STR.

Dropped from NPCs

 

Nature Walkers Behest: 

Effect:  Summons a Druid Pet

Skill: Conjuration
Allowable Targets: Self

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 600
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 14.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 28.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You summon a spirit of nature.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> summons a spirit of nature.

Summons a tiny bear pet!  One of the druids at the NK druid circle normally has one if you want to see it.  Its about the size of a giant rat.

Reported drops in Plane of Growth and Temple of Veeshan

 

Tunare's Request:  

Effect:  Charm Animal

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Animal

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 5

Mana Required: 350
Spell Duration: Unknown
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 8.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 24.0 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> blinks.

And yet, another upgrade to charm animal.  There is a major difference in this one however.  When the charm wears off, the pet will not attack you.

Dropped from NPCs
