The Plane of Knowledge:
---
This zone appears to be non-kos to all. I safely conned an othmir and the necro/sks didn't aggro me either.

Seems to be layed out in the following fashion:

###################
#----#-------#----#
#----#-------#----#
#-----------------#
#----####-####----#
#----#-------#----#
#----#-------#----#
#----#-------#----#
###################

You could also think of it maybe as a three-leaf clover without the stem -- I'll let some map guru figure out the details.

The zone is laid out into two main courtyards. There is the upper courtyard and the lower courtyard. The upper courtyard contains the library which contains spells and various undetermined merchants. The lower courtyard contains all of the usual merchants and a bank as well as the portal pedestals to all of the other Norrath zones.

Pedestals were found to the Nexus and to the Great Divide. I did not find any to Cobalt Scar or Iceclad or Eastern Wastes or any other zones in Luclin except for the Nexus.

In addition to Norrath zones -- there is a pedestal that takes you to the Plane of Tranquility.

The druid spell vendors can be found on the entry-level floor of the library. One of the vendors sells some of our new spells -- the ones that are pre-61: Pet shrinking spell (i forget the name); Ring of Knowledge, Circle of Knowledge and one more that I forget.

The bank was at either n440, n400 or p440, p400 - I forget.

Your entrance to the other planes is via the Plane of Tranquility.

Pedestals you click to enter them.

---
Plane of Tranquility
---

This is a big island. Many planar entrances will not let you enter unless you 'are worthy'. My guess is to keep us newbies out until we are well ready to zone in to die. If so, kudos Verant -- rez hell on opening day is just no fun.

Planes that appear to be open from the get-go include: Plane of Disease (big sludge spewing pipe), Plane of Nightmares (bent rusty empty kiddies-pool looking thing in-between one of the two sets of buildings), Plane of Innovation (in the windmill), Plane of Justice (the scales) and possibly Plane of Fire (I didn't have the guts to click it after talking to the dragon -- who 'might' have made me 'worthy').

First looks indicate that the Planes are agro-free at the zone ins so long as you don't move too far in.

Since the folks I saw in the Plane of Justice were naked as jay-birds when I zoned in I decided not to wander. Plane of Disease I have yet to explore at all, but I hear that there is a disease affect that sucks your HP to some degree unless you have disease resists.

---
Plane of Innovation
---

Entry mobs hit hard to me (a druid with 850ish AC) -- 230s to 240s. They double attack and kick, bash. They appear to be warriors.

There are roamers, albeit sparse that I noticed.

These can be root rotted easily. They have high HP - probably near an Elysian's.

They did not summon at the entrance.

I tried to quad kite them but there is hardly any room to do so, as such I was kiting right at the zone in and probably scared the bejesuz out of everyone zoning in. Agro appears to stay with you well so shouldn't be much of a problem unless you zone leaving ensnared mobs there to greet people.

There is a little lamp at the zone-in that takes you out of the zone.

Very cramped space to try quading in here -- occasional roamers can really mess it up (you start out in a little 'gully' of sorts)

They drop NO DROP tinkering type stuffs which still have an unknown use to me.

Don't even think of quading here unless you have a huge mana pool, kei, mask, potg, and some flowing thought. You will probably @#%$ people off too if you do it at the zoneline as more people get there.

I did not get any faction hits for kills.

-- No phat loot on entry mobs

Plane of Nightmares
---

Much like fear's look, but darker. Nothing like Kithicor dark though -- more like Burning woods dark. Trees are much further spread out than the heavily forested areas of Burning woods.

You zone in near a building. The zone out exit is something you click in the building.

Fire spells seem to stick well here.

Zone-in is agro free. I did not get any faction hits for kills.

Mobs hit harder - 230 to 270s, double, have MORE hps. None summoned at the zone in area.

Killed spiders, bats, Treants (ya i'm a druid -- no faction), ravens.

-- No phat loot on entry mobs

Is more efficient to root/rot here than quad kite I found (even with all my gear I had to med-kite).

Plane of Nightmares
Go to Plane of Tranquility and talk to Adrona Jezith at (neg 257, neg 1424) Tell her... "What about being tortured by nightmares?"

Plane of Fire
Go to Plane of Tranquility and talk to Miak the SearedSoul at (pos 272, neg 2254) Tell him... "What about the portal's destination?"

*Warning Spoilers*
PoI:
To zone in click on the gear that isn't turning on the clock (look up). Zone in is usually clear, but there are roamers and people fight here. Didn't go too far in, but there are alley ways that you have to follow, with mobs spawning along those. The only mobs that saw my invis were Archaic Models (or something like that), I was also told they summon, and someone else said they are immune to speed changes. The mobs hits for 200-250ish, and are easily root rotted. The spaces can get a little cramped, and there are roamers, but they don't break root very often, so I had 3 going when I got adds. The only drop I got was a Moonstone Crystal, and no PP. I also foraged a clockwork grease, that the elder talks about needing in the windmill, but neither him or his assistant would take it. To zone out click on the box looking thing near where you zone in.

PoJ:
I hated this zone, but it got better. I just explored it, didn't kill anything, but its like a prison, with harder prisoners on the lower levels. The lower mobs are KoS (threateningly, at least to me) and aggroed when I opened the doors. They aren't really hard, but the guards that wonder around will assist them and they hit like trains. Most things don't see invis. I found a secret door in a wall, that lead to a tunnel (escape tunnel?). It had mobs that looked like Geoids, some that saw invis, and "Mindless tunnelers" that did see invis, all were scowling. After more exploring I found some High Guardians of Justice(5), all level 60, that seemed to be guarding a door. I opened it and a punisher and a enforcer who both saw invis and were scowling came after me, and the Guards helped again. I think one will have to fight through there (probably the really bad prisoners) to get to the 7th hammer. I did find the tribunal though, they look awesome. I wanted to see how hard they hit so took a swing, they DT and hit me for 550, and 709 if anyone is interested, but my friend said the others didn't agro(I think I died too fast). If you can't find your corpse, don't worry, after 10 mins it will reappear in the graveyard( the zone in). There was a group hunting there that said they were getting faction hits (guards/prisoners) but the exp was amazing. The zone out is a prison door near the first ramp to get into the prison (I'll post directions/locs when I get them).

PoD:
Seemed kind of boring to me. There were already exp groups set up there, so I went exploring and died a bunch. Pestilent or something like that rats were too high to charm. Spider things were necros (at least the ones I got) and liked to cast, alot. Couldn't find anything fun to do here so I didn't stay long. No loot, no pp =-/ Decent exp (using the charm solo thing someone else suggested)

PoN:
Where my most recent corpses have been resting, luckily there is a graveyard here too, so its like a free summon. Things here like PoI, but a bit harder. 260+ish hits, lots of hps (Wrath+DoD+WD with extended affliction took to about 72ish health, I don't feel like doing the math so figure it out yourself =-P. Didn't last long here, seemed like a fun zone, but once you get a couple of adds it hurts. Nothing summoned, just tons of hps and hit hard, seemed easy enough to root rot, but I'll let you know when I live longer than 5 mins. Zone out is in the building, stone sign just like PoK ports.
