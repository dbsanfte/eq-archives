Bat Fur

There's a shop called "The Herb Jar." If you sit there for a while Caleah Herblender says to one of her co-workers, "We're out of bat fur again?" Ask her about bat fur.

She will then tell you she is a wizard of the circle of three, and that they need components to develop new spells. She asks for your help in obtaining rat whiskers, a beetle eye, and a piece of bat fur so that she can work on a spell she is trying.

Giving all 3 items at once gives you the following faction (she attempts to cast a new spell, but fails): +OrderOfThree, -BloodSabers, +GuardsOfQeynos, -OpalDarkBriar.

You don't get any other reward other than exp and the satisfaction of helping out the wizard’s guild.
