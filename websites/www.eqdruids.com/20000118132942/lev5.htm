I would continue soloing blues for a few more level.  Check out the spell section for what order to buy spells in as you are probably still piss poor at this point.   The haul to level 9 is very tough.  With the addition of meditate at level 8 - life is better.  Just put one point in meditate - it will max on its own and always use it when resting.  if you have a good group to play with - its fun.  But begging for a group or a bunch of chumps just bites.

At this point the spell snare becomes useful...if you want to find a runner then you start the combat by snaring it and beating the crap out of it.  You get some pretty decent spells.  If you have a little cash when you make 5 you should be able to purchase all the level 1 and 5 spells by level 5.  If you wasted time by killing non treasure creatures...you probably can't afford a pot to piss in.  Continue saving all your cash for level 9 spells...all of it! 

From Delc:

If you want to group at these levels, run around your local dungeon
and snare Gnolls, or Orcs to keep them from running from people.  This will
illustrate your worth, much the same way SOW, affects people at 14th. Also,
perform random acts of healing and buffing.  Before you know it, everyone
will want to group with you.  Your only drawback will be your long down
time, until eighth.  You can overcome this by minimizing your burns and
relegating yourself to just heal and snare.
