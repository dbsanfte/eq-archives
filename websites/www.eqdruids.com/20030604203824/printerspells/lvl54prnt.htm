Level Fifty Four Spells

Level 54 spells are available either off the Druid Spell vendor in Firona Vie, or are dropped off various mobs.

Blizzard: 

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) by 490 (L54)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: Target Based AoE

Range to Target: 150 feet
Area Effect Range: 20 feet

Resistance Check: Cold

Mana Required: 332
Spell Duration: Three Waves
Casting Time: 3.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 12.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are caught in a raging blizzard.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is caught in a raging blizzard.

Cold Based Area Effect rain spell that does 3 rains of 490 per hit.

 

Dropped by NPCs in Old Sebilis (Juggernauts) and Named Cycle in Skyfire

 

Regrowth:  

Effect:  Increase Hit Points (HP) by 15 (L52)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 300
Spell Duration: Unknown
Duration Formula: 10
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You begin to regenerate.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> begins to regenerate.
Spell fades: You have stopped regenerating.

This is an upgrade to Chloroplast.  You will generate 15 hps per tick now. 

 

Purchased From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

 

Spirit of Eagle:

See Invisible + 3 levels
Increase Movement between 57% (L54) to 60% (L60)
Levitate + 3 levels
Infravision

Classes: Dru (L54)

Skill: Alteration
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 125
Spell Duration: 600 ticks (60.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: Your body pulses with an avian spirit..
Spell cast on someone: Soandso's body pulses with an avian spirit.


Spell fades: The avian spirit departs.

 

VERY nice spell.  Combines Levitation, Sow, and Infravision all in one cast!  You'll love this spell!

 

Can be found off mobs in the following zones:

 

The Maiden's Eye

Fungus Grove

Akheva Ruins

The Deep

Grieg's End

Umbral Plains

Effect:  Illusion: Wolf Elemental

Effect:  Ultravision

Casting Time: 6.0 seconds

Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds
