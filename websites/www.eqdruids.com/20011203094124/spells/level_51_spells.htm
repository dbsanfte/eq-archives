Circle of Winter: 

Effect:  Group Increase Fire Resist by 45 (L51)

Skill: Abjuration
Allowable Targets: Full Group

Area Effect Range: 60 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 150
Spell Duration: 360 ticks (36.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 3
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are surrounded by a winter haze.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is surrounded by a winter haze.
Spell fades: Your winter haze clears.

Nice spell for groups.  Increase the fire resistance for everyone in your group by 45 points with one spell cast.

These Spell drops of Tatterback Gorillas, Greater Warbones, Wurms, Scoriae Hornets, Forest Giant Ancients and Sarnak Avengers.  Higher the Mobs are the greater the Chance of a Spell dropping.

Repulse Animal:  

Effect:  Fear Animal

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Animal

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 25

Mana Required: 100
Spell Duration: 8 ticks (48 seconds)
Duration Formula: 8
Casting Time: 1.75 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 4.0 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> is utterly repulsed!

Nice upgrade to fear animal.  It lasts fairly long, is a fast cast, and isn't resisted very often. 

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

Legacy of Spikes:  

Effect:  Group Damage Shield by 20 (L51)

Skill: Abjuration
Allowable Targets: Full Group

Area Effect Range: 60 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 300
Spell Duration: 6 seconds (L1) to 360 seconds (L60)
Duration Formula: 7
Casting Time: 3.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are surrounded by a thorny barrier.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is surrounded by a thorny barrier.
Spell fades: The brambles fall away.

Group Damage Shield spell.  Puts a Spike damage shield on everyone in your group that does 20 hps damage each time someone is hit.

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 250
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 4.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel much better.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> feels much better.

Upheaval:  

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 648 (L51) to 718 (L58)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: Point Blank AoE

Area Effect Range: 35 feet
Pushes Target Back: 1 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 625
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 5.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 24.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel the ground scream and heave.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is mauled by the moving ground.

Area Effect Direct Damage spell for 648 hps a shot.  Just like all the other "earthquake" type spells, it radiates out from you, and will anything within your range.  

You receive this spell at random from Firiona Vie Druid Quest.
