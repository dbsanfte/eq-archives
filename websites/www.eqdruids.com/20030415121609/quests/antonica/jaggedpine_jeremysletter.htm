Jeremy's Letter

Jeremy Leafrunner by the camp fire will eventually ask you to deliver a letter to his grandmother if you follow his dialogue options. He gives you a letter to take to Elishia Leafrunner in Felwithe. She can be found in the second Felwithe zone, in the part with the caster guilds. Delivering the message gets the following faction adjustments.

+Keepers of the Art
+King Tearis Thex
+Faydarks Champions
-The Dead

Continuing to talk to Elishia gets you another letter to take back to Jeremy. Delivering this letter gets you more faction.

+Residents of Jaggedpine
+Jaggedpine Treefolk
+Protectors of Pine
+Guards of Qeynos
