To begin this quest you will need to talk to Ralgyn who can be found in Skyshrine. He will tell you that he is terribly worried. He will tell you that he made a promise to Falinkan a long time ago to keep her talisman safe, but alas half of the talisman has gone missing along with my son. He will tell you that his son was in training by Kardakor to become a member of the Patrol. Head off to the Great Divide and locate Kardakor, in the wurm caves. Ask Kardakor "I am looking for Glanitar", and he'll tell you that he has been missing for some time now. Head out to and find Taskmaster Aboytt and kill him, as he will drop a Piece of the Talisman, and a head. Return to Kardakor and hand him both items, you will receive the talisman back. He will tell you to go and seek out Rolandal.

Talk to Rolandal and he'll tell you to bring him three rare treasures along with your talisman and he'll tell you everything you need to know. The rare items he needs are the Head of Staff Sergeant Drioc, which is dropped by the Staff Sergeant Drioc who can be found in the bank within Kael Drakkal. The Teachings of GKrean, which drops off GKrean who can be found in the Arena within Kael Drakkal. The last item is the Chipped Fang which drops off Vilefang, a named serpent in Dragon Necropolis. Once you have obtained all three items, hand them in with your Talisman to Rolandar and you'll receive the Imbued Talisman. Return to Ralgyn and hand him the new talisman and you'll be rewarded with the Circlet of Falinkan.
