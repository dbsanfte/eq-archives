Qmon's Dire Charm Guide

 

Prepared by Qmon of Brell Serilis – Last Updated February 4, 2003

                 

This guide lists the known “higher level” animals that can be Dire Charmed by zone and is updated from time-to-time.  This is not a list of all animals that can be charmed, only those animals that can specifically be Dire Charmed.  Dire Charm is an AA ability.  Also, this list does not contain low level animals since most Druids are not going to Dire Charm a low level animal.

 

Druid Dire Charm only works on animals that are level 46 and below.  Some of the animals within the same species are above level 46 as well as equal to or bellow level 46.  That means there is a chance you could attempt to Dire Charm an animal on this list and it will fail.  If that happens, you will agro the animal but the 72 minute recast timer will be set to zero so you can try again on another animal.  You might have to hunt around to find an animal of that species that is level 46 or below.

 

The name in parenthesis after the animal name is the name of the person who submitted and tested Dire Charm on that animal.
