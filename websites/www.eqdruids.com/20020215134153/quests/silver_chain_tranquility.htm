Silver Charm of Tranquility (ToV)

 

Properties                            MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                             Armor, Neck

Armor Class                          35

Boosts                                  +10 STR, +15 STA, +100 HP, +100 MANA, , +8 CR, +8 DR, +8 FR, +8 MR, +8 PR

Weight & Size                       WT: 0.5  Size: TINY

Class                                     ALL

Race    ALL

Talk to Lendiniara the Keeper who can be found in the Temple of Veeshan. Ask her "what test of the ruby tear". She will tell you to return to her once you have found the three symbols and the Ruby Tear. These symbols are the White Symbol, the Silver Symbol, and the Ruby Symbol. All the items are dropped by mobs in the Halls of Testing within the Temple of Veeshan. Once you have obtained all the items needed, hand them to her and you'll be rewarded with the Silver Charm of Tranquility.

Note: This item can be equipped in the Range, Neck and Wrist slots.
