Bone Chips (Kaladim)

Recommended level: 1
 
Find Gunlok Jure in the paladin's guild in North Kaladim.

Give him four bone chips and you will get experience, money and an item (usually a torch, lantern, rusty weapons, or leather armor).

For each turn-in, you'll receive the following faction adjustments:

    Factions Raised:

  • Clerics of Underfoot
  • Kazon StormHammer
  • Miners Guild 249
