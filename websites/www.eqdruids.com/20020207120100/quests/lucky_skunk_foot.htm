A Lucky Skunk Foot (Plane of Mischief)

Properties                           MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                            Armor, Neck

Armor Class                         5

Boosts                                 +5 CR, +5 DR, +5 FR, +5 MR, +5 PR

Effect                                   None

Weight & Size                      WT: 0.1  Size: TINY

Class                                    ALL

Race                                    ALL

 

Kill Sniffles who can be found in the Plane of Mischief. This will cause Stomples to spawn, kill him to obtain his foot. Now bring this foot to Debbis the Fish (who is really a skunk) and you'll be rewarded with a Lucky Skunk Foot.
