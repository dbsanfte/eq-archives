Spells are why a druid exists...you have the coolest spells in the game and can cast in any given situation.  Direct damages, buff, travel, utility - druids have them all.   Remember that most spells are hardest to cast the level that you  receive them and grow in power for 3-5 levels.

THE MOST IMPORTANT thing to do at all spell levels is to MAX each skill at EVERY level if possible.  The first one to focus on maxing is conjuration.  Whirling Wind can be cast in a small group turning the monster to you and damaging it. If you do not keep your Conjuration maximized, when you hit level 14 you had better get ready to summon a pile of water.

Alteration - healing and some buffs
Abjuration - some buffs and shields
Evocation - burn baby burn
Conjuration - wind , swarm (DoT), and summon items
Divination - camouflage line of spells

DD - Direct Damage
AE - Area Effect
DoT - Damage over Time
OD - Outdoor Only
SO - Self Only
DS - Damage Shield

Spell Locations:
Rank 1,2,3,4 all druid guilds - Kelethin, Surefall Glades, and Rivervale
Rank 5 and 6 - Karana Ring
Rank 7 and 8 - Steamfont
Rank 9 and 10 - Butcherblock
Rank 11- Lavastorm
Ring Spells - West Commons
Circle and Evac Spells - Tox Forrest

 The best way to buy the rank 5 spells is buy the ring of commons before 19. When you cast it at 19, buy Karana port spell at West Commons. Use Ring of Karana to port and buy up everything else you need, before heading back to WC for your last two spells if you’d like.

The Rings generally contain Rank 5 on up spells.  The guilds do still carry some of these as well as a gypsy or two.

I haven't included any of the Shadows of Luclin spells as of yet, because the actual spell effects are not known for fact yet.  When SoL is released, I'll add the new spells.
