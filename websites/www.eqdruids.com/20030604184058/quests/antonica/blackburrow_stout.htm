Blackburrow Stout Shipment

The Fish's Ale bartender may mention that he needs someone to pick up a shipment for him.

If you accept, you are given a note to give to Gnasher Furgutt.

Hail Gnasher, give him the note, and he will give you a shipment of blackburrow Stout.

Take it back to the barkeep, and you get some experience and a free bottle of Stout.
