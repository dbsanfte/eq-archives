Muffins for Pandos (Freeport)

Recommended level: 1
 
Find Pandos Flintside, a dwarf in the Hogcaller's Inn. He is Lady Shae's guard.

For each muffin, you'll receive experience and the following faction adjustments:

    Factions Raised:

  • Faydarks Champions
  • King Tearis Thex
  • Clerics of Tunare
  • Soldiers of Tunare

    Factions Lowered:

  • Crushbone Orcs
Some players will start out dubious (or worse) to Pandos. If this applies to you, bring a friend (who is not KOS and can cast invisibility) along. Initiate the trade with Pandos, and before you click the trade button, have your friend cast invisibility on you. Once this is done, click the trade button and you should receive the faction adjustments.

If your faction is worse than dubious, cast Harmony on him prior to initiating the trade, and initiate the trade from the maximum trading distance. Then use the above invisibility tactic, and continue until you can hand in muffins without your friend's assistance.
