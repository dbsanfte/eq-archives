Burst of Fire:  

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 12 (L5) to 15 (L10)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Fire + 25

Mana Required: 15
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 1.75 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You feel your skin singe as the Burst of Fire hits you.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> singes as the Burst of Fire hits them.

A burn spell that replaces Burst of Flame. It costs 15 mana to cast and does 12-15 points of damage. It is the next progression of burn spell and you use it identically as you would Burst of Flame.  Useful till about level 10-11 - then switch to ignite unless you need a faster cast.

 

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Ward Summoned:  

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 35 (L5) to 41 (L11)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: Summoned

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 25

Mana Required: 30
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 2.10 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> staggers.

This is a pretty specialized spell, as it only does damage to summoned creatures like Elementals or pets.

 

However, when you are fighting those types of creatures, it is by far the best spell in your arsenal at this point. The main point for this spell, is it is more mana efficient when attacking summoned creatures (does more damage per mana cost) than your normal attack spells.

 

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Invoke Lightning:  

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 21 (L5) to 25 (L9)

Skill: Evocation
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: Target Based AoE

Range to Target: 200 feet
Area Effect Range: 10 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 25

Mana Required: 32
Spell Duration: Instant
Area Effect Duration: 5
Casting Time: 2.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: Lightning surges through your body.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> has been struck by lightning.

Although it can only be cast in outdoor zones and it is pretty expensive mana-wise (32 mana), it does good damage, 25 pts dmg in a small area of effect. This spell is at its best when you are grouped with a fighter. Have your fighter friend attack a group of monsters and then back up several steps.

 

The backing up will cause the monsters to group tightly together as they chase after him. It is important to note that the spell will only effect things in a 1 space radius around the target. So I like to say the monsters need to be "shoulder to shoulder" and then I cast it on the central one.

 

Be careful though as this really irritates the monsters and they will often make a run at you. 

 

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Grasping Roots:  

Effect:  Decrease Current Hitpoints by 10 (L5)
Effect:  Root

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 25

Mana Required: 35
Spell Duration: 48 seconds
Casting Time: 2.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: Your feet become entwined.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is entwined by roots.
Spell fades: The roots fall from your feet.

It is pretty expensive mana-wise and it is resisted often. But if its not resisted then it does 10hp of damage to the target and keeps them frozen in place for maybe 10-30 seconds.

 

Because it has a damage effect, it will also cause the creature to agro on you normally when the root breaks.  Good for when you need to run away from a mob. 

 

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Camouflage:  

Effect:  Invisibility

Skill: Divination
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 15
Spell Duration: 20.0 minutes
Casting Time: 5.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: Your body fades away.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> fades away.
Spell fades: You return to view.

A very good spell.

 

It's effect is to make you invisible in outdoor zones.

 

The best feature is its low mana cost (only 15). So even if you have to cast it a couple times to get it to "stick" it is worth it.

 

Make sure you cast all other buff spells BEFORE this one, as any spell casting or combat action dispels it. 

 

Always use this when running or about to meditate solo.  Just cast and sit and meditate. 

 

Spell casters can see through it but the instant death mobs like Griffons and Sand Giants can not. 

 

Be careful to watch for the message 'You return to view'.   When traveling in a group, use this spell in conjunction with See Invisible to travel safer.

 

Also any undead creatures will be able to see through this invis.  Make sure you don't think you will be safe from them.

 

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Harmony:  

Effect:  Change Frenzy Radius to 15 feet
Effect:  Change Reaction Radius to 15 feet

Skill: Abjuration
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: Target Based AoE

Range to Target: 200 feet
Area Effect Range: 40 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 25
Spell Duration: 2.0 minutes
Casting Time: 3.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 12.0 seconds

Harmony is basically an area effect lull spell which works by decreasing the radius of the NPCs agro range. 

 

In short, if you harmony a group of NPCs and then attack one, the rest will not attack when they otherwise would have. 

 

It is very handy in that it allows you to pull NPCs in a controlled fashion (one or two at a time), allowing you or your group to handle camps that might have otherwise overwhelmed you at the first pull. 

 

It is one of the spells that make druids such effective solo artists in the game, and nice for group work at tough camps

 

This is a spell I highly advise practicing with.  It will be one of your handiest spells for many levels!  It is outdoor only.

 

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Gate:

Effect:  Gate Home

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Self

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 70
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 5.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 8.0 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> fades away.

This spell is very simple.  It returns you, to your bind spot.  For those that don't know, your bind spot is where you appear when you die.

 

You can have another player bind you in a town, or at level 14 you will be able to bind yourself.

 

Purchase at:  Druid Guild
