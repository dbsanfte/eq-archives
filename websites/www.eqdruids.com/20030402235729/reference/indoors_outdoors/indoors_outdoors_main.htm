Indoor - Outdoor Zone List

Druids are known for their hunting prowess in large, "outdoor" zones.

However, a large number of Druid spells, such as Spirit of Wolf or Harmony, can not be cast within the confines of "indoor" or "contained" zones, such as certain underground dungeons (e.g. Befallen, Guk, Kedge Keep, etc.).

So here is a list of the zones found in the game, and if they are considered an indoor or outdoor zone.

If you see anything that needs to be corrected or added, please contact me at Tesiux@eqdruids.com.
