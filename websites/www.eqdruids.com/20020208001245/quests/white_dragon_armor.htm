White Dragon Armor (Skyshrine)

 

Talk to Lord Yelinak who can be found deep within Skyshrine. He will tell you that he has a task for you to prove your trust. He will ask you to retrieve for him the head of King Tormax, and then return to him. King Tormax can be found deep in Kael Drakkal and it will take a mighty force to bring him down. Once you have his head, return to Lord Yelinak and hand it to him. He will hand you the Clawed Griffin Sword as your reward. You can hand the sword to Yelinak and he will instead hand you the White Dragonscale Boots. Now hand back the boots and you'll receive the White Dragon Helm.
