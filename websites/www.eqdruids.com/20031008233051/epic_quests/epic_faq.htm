At what level can I start my epic?
The epic can be "started" at any level, really.  The items that you are required to collect drop off various mobs from various zones.  However, in order to actually start the epic or do any turn-ins, Sony has stated you are supposed to be level 51 or higher.  There are some who have done some key parts (i.e. turn-ins, etc) at level 46, but you run the risk of losing your items if you try this.  Of course, you also need proper faction with the various turn-in mobs to ensure success.   If your faction or level are not at the appropriate level, the NPC will keep your items.

What factions are required throughout the epic?
The following factions are required if you are to successfully complete your epic:
 

  • Faydark's Champions
  • Soldiers of Tunare
  • Storm Guard
  • Kaladim Merchants (for Kenlo Strongarm -- Jade Reaver)
  • Keepers of the Art
     

Factions can be adjusted accordingly by following the EQDruids Faction Fixing Guide

Why won't Telin Darkforest wont talk to me?
Telin Darkforest will not talk to you unless you are of level 51 or higher.  Also, you have to make sure your faction is at the right level before trying to talk to him.  Some say they can be dubious and he will not talk, but saying "What Action" to him still results in the start of the epic.  If you hail him and he will not speak to you (or says something rude), you need to do the Muffins for Pandos quest in Freeport.  NOTE: If you have done the Jogle Honeybugger quest ("the band aid quest") in Misty Thicket, you might have problems purchasing muffins in Freeport.  If this is the case, simply buy the muffins elsewhere., such as EC.

Niola Impholder won't accept my batwings.  What should I do?
Niola Impholder is on the Keepers of the Art Faction.  She will not accept the batwings from you if you're not at least apprehensive.  If you are dubious to her, however, you can simply use Wolf Form.  Wolf form raise you one "faction level" (i.e. if you're dubious, you will be apprehensive in Wolf Form).

How many batwings or muffins can I hand in at a time?
You used to be able to turn in 16 batwings (or muffins) at a time; 4 stacks with 4 in each stack.  However, Sony recently changed the turn-in process for NPCs.  NPCs will now only acknowledge one of the stacked items in each "give" slot.  For example, if you turn in 4 stacks of 4 batwings to Niola Impholder, you will only see 4 sets of faction messages; thus wasting the other 12 batwings.

Where does Faelin Bloodbriar spawn?
Faeline Bloodbriar spawns at 1500, 500 in Greater Faydark (near Orc Hill).  It was believed that you have to kill every orc pawn in the zone in order to get her to spawn but this is untrue.  All you have to do is kill on orc pawn that spawns on this very spot.  He is on an 8 to 10 minute time and will faithfully spawn on that spot.  It is unclear if Faeline will spawn at the end of the timer or not.  Some have seen her spawn as soon as 3 minutes after the PH is killed.

What is the spawn time on Dolgin Codslayer?
Dolgin Codslayer (the Faydedar trigger in Timorous Deep, hidden Oasis) is on a static 12 hour timer; give or take an hour.  There was some question as to his timer based on success or failure of a Faydedar encounter, but this is not true.

What items in the epic can be multi-quested?
The general rule for multi-questing is that anything requiring NPC-only turn-in can be multi-quested.  The only consideration to be aware of is the person MQ'ing an item with you must have the same correct faction with the turn-in NPC.  Most of the items in the Druid Epic are single turn-ins (i.e. only one item required) so there's not much chance for multi-questing.  The following items can be multi-quested:
 

  • Softly Glowing Stone (results from hardened mixture turn-in)/Pulsing Green Stone (dropped off VSR).  NPC is Foloal Stormforest in Firiona Vie
  • Cleansed spirits.  Each of the cleansed spirits requires drops off "corrupted" mobs in various zones.  These items can be multi-quested to receive the one item which eventually results in the cleansed spirit.  NOTE:  The Cleansed Sprit of Kunark turn-in requires that only the items off the corrupted mobs in Kunark be turned in.  There is no "intermediate" item (like the gleaming unicorn horn).

Where do I find the Human Skeleton?
The human skeleton spawns in Frontier Mountains.  His actual spawn spot and time are unknown.  He has been spotted in various parts of the zone.  The best thing to do here is to get a cleric or necro (someone who can Sense the Dead) to locate him for you.  It can be tough to find him, however, because his pathing will march him straight into a hill and he tends to get stuck.  You will also have to deal with adds along the way so using camo/invis is the way to go.

What do I do with the Shiny Tin Bowl after I make the hardened mixture?
You can simply destroy the bowl after the mixture is completed.  It is not needed for any other part of the Druid Epic.  It IS needed, however, for the Ranger epic.  Ranger epic is the same up to this point and Rangers should have this bowl already to trigger Jahled the Wretched in The Hole.  BUT, if the encounter results in failure, the shiny tin bowl is lost and the Ranger must start the epic over.  Druids who have this bowl can simply re-trigger Jahled so the Ranger doesn't have to repeat the whole epic.

Does the "spawned" Venril Sathir drop the Pulsing Green Stone?
No.  The pulsing green stone only drops off the triggered Venril Sathir (the one "resurrected" from Venril Sathir Remains) but he drops two.

Does the triggered Venril Sathir proc a life tap?
The triggered VS will proc a life tap, but it is very infrequent and not nearly as much of a hassle as the timed-spawn.  There were mixed reports about whether or not he actually did do a lifetap; however, it has been confirmed that if the fight lasts long enough, you have the chance of seeing him actually lifetap a couple of times.

Why won't Farios Elianos talk to me?
He doesn't usually speak at all unless you hand him the crushed pot (which you receive from Arlik Farsight in Timorous Deep).  He's pretty rude and sarcastic so you wouldn't really want to speak to him anyway ;).  He is on The Keepers of the Arts faction, as is Arlik.  if Arlik gives you the crushed pot, you are ok to do the turn-in with Farios.  If not, you need to do more batwings with Niola Impholder.

Which brownie will spawn the tainted brownie?
There are 3 brownie scouts who wander the Lesser Faydark zone.  They spawn at 0,0 and each walks his own path.  Killing any of the 3 will spawn the tainted.  In fact, it is entirely possible to have 3 tainted brownies running in the zone at the same time.

Where does Faydedar spawn?
Faydedar spawns in the water, near the ramp in Hidden Oasis (click here for a picture).  Once the turn-in is complete, he will spawn and swim around a little, then come out of the water.  It is best to have your main tank(s) sit near the bushes next to the ramp.  He will exit the water and immediately attack the sitters.  All others on the raid should be standing.

How come my nukes do not land on Faydedar?
In order for any nukes to land, you must be within melee range.  Otherwise, the spells will be resisted.  He is a fire-based mob so the likelihood of him resisting fire-based spells is quite high.  It is best to use cold-based spells.

Why did Faydedar immediately attack my casters and ignored my sitting tank?
Two possibilities can cause this to happen: 1) He hit the tanks, causing them to stand but there were casters sitting at the time of the spawn.  2) You had people on the raid who were fairly low level (less than 46).  Faydedar is identical to Naggy (except he looks like salamander with no hind legs...lol) and will attack anyone lower than level 46.  He will definitely attack all those who are sitting, so everyone must be standing when you do your turn-in.  There is also some indication that he hates illusions so Wolf Form or elemental illusions (used by Enchanters and Mages) will most likely aggro him as well.

Can my raid use pets to kill Faydedar?
Yes.  The only consideration here is to make sure the pet's taunt is turned off ("/pet taunt off").  Other than that, pets are great for this encounter, specifically necro and mage pets (certain pets have the ability to stun).

Does the "real" Faydedar drop the seawater?
Yes.  The timed Faydedar will drop the seawater.  However, the chances of you finding him are slim to none.  He spawns in the firepot room and will sit for a little bit, after which time he ventures out.  His pathing takes him close to the Iksar Master (he sits atop a big rock near Chessboard) who proceeds to run down to him and literally beat him to death.  The Iksar Master loses about half health but he's highly magic resistant so the damage he takes is purely from melee.  If you have the chance to see this fight, do so.  It's been reported as being "fascinating".  Just make sure you don't get too close and stay invis'd -- everyone except Iksar Monks are KOS to him.

Why didn't the corrupted aqua goblin shaman drop the water?
You can sometimes get many tainted/corrupted aqua goblin shamans to spawn at this camp, however, they don't all drop the water.

Why didn't the tainted barracuda spawn the corrupted barracuda?
Same thing as the aqua goblin shaman...the corrupted barracuda will spawn many times, but will not drop the water off every one.  However, you can tell which one will drop the water by pulling the tainted out of the lake.  If it follows you on land, the corrupted will spawn and drop the water.

What is the spawn time on Athele in East Karana?
Athele is on a 2 hour timer (real-time).  This is whether or not she gets killed.  If the Dark Elf Reavers reach the druids and kill her, she will return in approximately 2 hours.  If you turn in the fleshbound tome to her, she will despawn and return in 2 hours.

I've killed the Millers.  Can I still do my epic?
Absolutely.  Being KOS to the Millers will not hurt your epic in any way.  However, you might have some issues in various zones (i.e. Qeynos Guards, etc).  Being KOS to the Millers WILL hurt the Paladin epic because they are on the Priests of Life faction (Qeynos Pally guild).

I am KOS to Kaladim.  How do I turn in my jade reaver?
You can invis yourself and get to Kenlo Strongarm in Kaladim with no problem; however, because Sony nerfed the ability to turn items in while invis, you might have a problem here.  One thing you can do is have an enchanter charm him so you can do the turn-in.  The best thing to do is find the crazed goblins in Butcher Block Mountains and camp them for a while.  You should be able to raise your faction enough to where you are dubious (and dubious is all that is needed).

Where does the tainted gorilla spawn?
The tainted gorilla spawns in the northwest corner of Emerald Jungle, right around the area at the top of the stairs.  He has a tottering gorilla as his placeholder, but only a live one.  The undead ones (who look really gross) will not spawn the tainted.

Neither The Withered Treant nor Xanusuus Treant will respond to my hails.  What do I do?
If you want to hail these two, you have to get VERY close to them -- almost to the point where you see nothing but treant bark on your screen...lol...they are old and hard of hearing. ;).  But you can simply hand in the items to them and you will be fine.  Dubious or better faction has been reported as all that is needed for these turn-ins (druids should rarely have lower than indifferent faction with these two unless you've been naughty ;) ).

Can I just have an enchanter make me the Enchanted Clay?
Unfortunately, the enchanted clay used for the epic is not the same that is the result of an enchanter casting the Enchant Clay spell.  Although they have the same name, the one for the druid epic is completely different (for one thing, it is NO DROP)
