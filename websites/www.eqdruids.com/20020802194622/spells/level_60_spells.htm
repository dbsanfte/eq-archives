Banishment: 

Effect:  Destroy Target

Requires Star Ruby as a reagent

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: Summoned

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 5

Mana Required: 500
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 4.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 3.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 120.0 seconds

This spell will only work on a summoned creature.  When you cast this spell if successful the following will happen:

The mob is instantly killed, and their corpse vanishes.  You will receive no loot and no exp for kill.

Great for emergencies, don't use on something you actually need loot from.

Dropped by NPCs

Reported to drop in Old Sebilis

Shrinks the caster, magnifies the casters eyesight and lets the caster see invisible.  Nice for scouting out new areas.
