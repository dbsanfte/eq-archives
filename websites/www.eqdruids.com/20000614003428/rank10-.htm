Level 44

Allure of the Wild:  An upgrade to the charm animal line of spells, it has a smaller chance of resisting by higher level animals.  As with all charm spells, care needs to be taken as the charm does not usually last long and the target will attack the caster as soon as it breaks free.   These spells suck at every level...I had an elephant calf last about 45 seconds at level 45.

Banish Summoned:  This is a direct damage spell against Summoned Creatures.  This works great against elementals and any caster pet.  Damage is 585 so far.

Chloroplast:  This spell works like regeneration but it heals 10 pts per tick for 15 minutes.  This also stacks with Treeform and Bard songs

Drifting Death:  This is an upgrade to the damage over time spell line.  It has a low chance of resist and deals about 650 pts dmg in just under a minute.

Form of the Great Wolf:  This spell is an upgrade to the Great Wolf Form spell and adds an extra 62 to ATK at level 45. Also grants ultravision and is faster than Sow

Nullify Magic:  An upgrade to the cancel magic spell, it will strip the caster of all enchantments or remove a single enchantment off another target. Has a lower chance of resist.

Resist Disease:  This spell adds about 50 to your disease resistance.

Resist Poison:  This spell adds about 50 to your poison resistance.

Savage Spirit:  This is an upgrade to the Frenzied Spirit spell. It adds a little attack to the animal pet thus making it hit more often.

Storm Strength:  This spell adds 30 to 35 pts of strength to the target.

Succor Lavastorm:  This is an emergency escape spell for druids that teleports the group to the druid ruins in Lavastorm.

Calefaction - fast casting nuke with 45 second recycle - sold off the docks in Kunark 444-450 damage for 250 mana
