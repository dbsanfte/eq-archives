Once you make level 14 you have available to you a technique that I have mentioned in other places...but decided to devote special attention to it.

Kiting IS NOT AN EXPLOIT!  This has been verified by Verant and MANY GMs online.   Other players may whine about it - but it is not an exploit, simply a use of our Karana given skills.

1. Make sure you have maxed your Conjuration with Whirling Wind through level 13
    If not conjure a pile of food and water once hitting 14
2. Purchase Spirit of Wolf and Stinging Swarm - put them in your book and memorize them
3. Go to an area that is really wide open - like North Karana or South Karana (NOT a dungeon)
4. Run around camo'd until you find your target - usually an even or yellow that runs slow
5. Make sure you have a fresh SoW
6. Cast Swarm on the offending MOB and run like hell
7.  After about 45 seconds, stop and cast Swarm again when in range
8.  Run Like Hell some more
9.  Either it dies or you run out of mana
10. You might want to melee the last bubble of health to keep your skills up
11. If you run out of mana...go for the zone
12. Takes a long time to do this - up to 10 minutes

Kahlan's Variation

1. Fresh SOW, SLR, and Barbcoat, hell even Firefist if you want. Med up to
get the mana back
2. Cast Summon Swarm on the mob...
3. Run like hell for about 13 seconds...
4. This should put you far enough away from the mob such that you can turn
around and cast Dizzying Wind on it, turn around and run away again without
even getting hit if it's done right. ( this takes a bit of practice to get
the timing right. )
5. Repeat steps 3 and 4, about 4 times.
6. By this time your mob should be dead or very close to it ( Depending on
what it is I suppose. I usually use this technique on Griffawns in North
Karana )... I usually just melee the rest. Of course I cast shield of barbs
on myself before doing so...

Ping Pong Kiting from Mordikal

  1. Hook up with my friend. Normally a wizard but this will also work with other casters or someone with a good bow skill.
  2. Cast a fresh SOW on both parties
  3. Meditate back the mana lost form the buffs
  4. Pick the target and make sure both parties know which target.
  5. Position my partner about 5 seconds run away
  6. DOT the target and run towards my partner
  7. When the target is within range your partner blast it with their best DD spell
  8. Break off at 45 degree angles from each other and the monster will follow one of you (normally you).
  9. Run about 7 sec and pull a 90 degree turn toward where your partner is. He should do the same.
  10. Whichever one of you is not being chased needs to blast the monster as it approaches.
  11. Repeat this process until the monster is dead.

This combined the Ping Pong technique with the Kiting technique very effectively. As a level 14 Druid running with a level 13 Wizard we routinely killed red ghouls in West Karanas. This despite not having a Magical Weapon. 3 passes would normally kill the critter. And all our mana would be back before the critter respawned.
