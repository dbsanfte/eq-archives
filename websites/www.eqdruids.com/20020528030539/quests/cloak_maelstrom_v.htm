Cloak of the Maelstrom (Kael)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                              Armor, Back

Armor Class                          11

Boosts                                   +3 STR, +10 DEX, +6 STA, +5 INT, +55 HP

Effect                                    None

Weight & Size                       WT: 20.0  Size: ?

Class                                     ALL

Race                                      ALL

 

Talk to Dlammaz Stormslayer who can be found in Kael Drakkal. He will tell you that the dragons are the greatest threat to Kael Drakkal and that he will reward you for every head you bring to him. Dragons in the Western Wastes will sometimes drop heads when killed. Return with a Lesser Dragon Head and you'll be rewarded with the Cloak of the Maelstrom.
