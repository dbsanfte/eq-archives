The druid has a mix of skills of pure caster, melee, and some of the ranger skills.   Each skill has a name, description, recommended point investment, movement, needed for, and max formula.

An important note about skill points at the guild.  When you add to a skill that is 0 (like tracking at level 20), you actually get your level in points.  So when you go to Rivervale and add that one point at level 20 - your skill is now 20, not one.   This is not applicable for TRADE SKILLS! This can be very useful with neglected things at higher levels. 

Enjoy!
