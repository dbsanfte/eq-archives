Please note I do not want to be a generic quest site and have only listed proven druid (or very desirable for a druid) quests here.  I feel that Allakhazam's site is the best for questors and should be used for general questing.

Kunark Druid Quests:

Handy Shillelagh: Shrub Marwood of Fironia Vie needs a stout adventurer to go to the wilds and find a few items. He needs some tump stumps (found in Frontier Mts on Giant Tumps), one Kromdul (found on healer spider in Kaesora), one Kromdek type and a mantrap root (from erollisi mantraps in the Emerald jungle or Trakanon). Get me those items and he will give you a Dark Oak Shillelagh. You will also get the quest for the Charm Plant Scroll.

Charm Plant: You get this quest after finishing the handy shillelagh quest. Shrub Marwood requires a few items and will give you the Druid's Charm Plant Scroll in return. Bring him the following: Goblin Plague beads, Sarnak night dust, Strathebone Healer silk (found in Kasorea), and powder of Tsu.

Druid Spells: Samitha Lightheart of Fironia Vie is looking for four scrolls that she has not been able to locate. They are the scroll Circle of Winter, the scroll Circle of Summer, the scroll Spirit of Scale, and the scroll Form of the Howler. If you bring either one of these back, she will give you one of four very rare scrolls that she has.

Spirit of Scale results in Upheaval or Glamour of Tunare , Egress so far  I am getting the idea the spell you get is related to your level...

Rathe Mountains - White Dragonscale Cloak

NPC name is Karam Dragonforge.

The blackened sapphire and diamond are from level 48+ NPCs and monsters - they then must be made by a master jeweler.

White Dragonscale Cloak:  To acquire the armor, you need a wedding ring and a wedding present for his wife.  For a ring, he wants a platinum and diamond ring(From OOT or PoF mobs). For a present he wants, a black sapphire and platinum necklace (From Plane of Fear mobs).   He also needs the white dragon scale(From the Dragon Vox).

Rathe Mountains : Solvedi Scimitar and Sap Sheen Staff Quest

Since the patch, there are now two new quests out of the druid circle in Rathe Mountain.

Sap Sheen Staff is given by Tbrinn Ember, and requires Gazhak the Berserker's Mithril Plated Girth as well as King Xorbb's Slime Crystal staff. 

Solvedi Scimitar : requires Timber the Tiny's Laceless Sandal and Groi Gutblade's Lionhide Backpack.

Observer Staff Quest

This isn't much of a quest but yield a great item.  Kill Borxx (level 34) in Runnyeye and loot his lens.  Take it to Brona Frugin in Steamfont.  Give it to her with at least indiferent faction.  Yields the Staff of the Observers - one of the best 2 handed blunt weapons in game.

Testament of Vanear (quick version)

Go to Qeynos and find Moodro in the first section near the monk guild and bar.  Ask him 'what testament of Vanear' - or just give him 2 gold.  He will give you a 2 slot book container. Then start giving him 4 gold until he gives you a jester card.  (took me 200 times+).  Give the jest card back for the first page.  Then go to High Hold Pass Smuggler area (underwater corridor gets you there).  Kill Dyllin Starshine (level 15 or so)  Whack him until you get the other page - NOT the envelope.  Combine and wahlaaa!  +10 wis +10 mana off hand book.

Shakey's Stuffing

USE THE FORGE RIGHT BY THE DRUID FARM AND YOU DON'T NEED SMITHING SKILL.  HOWEVER YOU CAN FAIL WITH NO SKILL OR MASTER SKILL  - YOU ARE WARNED!

UPDATE: after you talk to the priestess in the temple of life in Qeynos she tells you to tell Nemsoe Jusgata "brother Estle needs the blessed oil" if you tell him that he just stares at you. what you need to tell him is"I will deliver the flask" than you get the flask of the oil of life.

First off... the quest details wont be given to you by Reebo if you aren't 4-14 levels. This does not mean you cant do the quest. Simply follow my instructions and it will work fine.

Ok the quest starts at Rivervale in the Druid Farm. (Love that place)
Go here and Hail Shakey the Scarecrow. Ask Reebo what is wrong with Shakey. He will tell you Shakey needs stuffing and a head, but as I mentioned, you really don't need to chat with Reebo anymore to proceed with the
quest.

Go to West Karana. There where the Scarecrows spawn you will find the Miller farm. You must get the no drop hay from the ground. You need one hay to continue the quest. 

After you have obtained the hay go speak with Priestess Jahnda in the Temple of Life in Qeynos. Tell here, "I need the blessed oil for Estle". She will direct you to another priest who's name has slipped my mind. This priest gives the oil out. Ok so now you have the hay and the Blessed oil.

Make sure ya have 66gold and head to the Ferrott. Might want to use invis on this one depending on your level. Look around the Cazic-Thule Temple entrance for an NPC Lizardman named Roror. Hail him then tell him, "bless my hay". Give him the 66gold and the hay and he will return to you some cursed hay and some experience back.

Now finally go back to Rivervale, in the Druid farm is a forge. Put the cursed hay and the blessed oil into the forge and combine it. You should get some Writhing Hay. (It looks like a pouch, ?!?) Ok give the writhing pouch...er hay to Shakey the Scarecrow and walla! You either get a neato magic weapon or a real nice magic belt for halflings. Repeat the quest as many times as you like to get both. Or more for a nice profit. The stats for each are Below.
