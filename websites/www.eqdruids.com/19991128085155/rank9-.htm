Level 39

Succor : Ro - Fast party escape to South Ro

Avalanche:  This is an upgrade to our rain line of spells.  As with all rain spells, they last from 1 to 2 rounds and hit a maximum of four times.  See Cascade of Hail for exact natures of this type of spell.  Each rain does about 125 dmg.

Circle of Misty:  This is the group teleport spell to the druid ruins near the entrance to the halfling city of Rivervale.

Enveloping Roots:  This is a double effect root spell, like previous version of it.  This means that the chance for resist is really high.  Although the damage hits about 80% of the time, the actual root works only 25% of the time.  Damage has increased to 88 pts at level 39, and increases by 4 pts every new level up to 44.

Firestrike:  An upgrade to the fire-based direct damage spells, this spell deals 282 pts damage and increases by 4 pts every new level up to level 44.  This spell is resisted a lot in the Solusek Eye but not as bad in Nagafen's against the Kobolds.

Pack Regeneration:  This spell casts regeneration on all members of the party for 200 mana.  The spell duration is about half the time of the single target version but still reduces down time a lot when many casters have to meditate.

Pack Spirit:  This spell casts Spirit of the Wolf on all members of the party.  Its duration is half that of the single target version.

Share Wolf Form:  This spell casts Wolf Form on all members of the party and its duration is about half that of the personal druid spell Wolf Form.  If the druid has Greater Wolf Form on and casts this spell, it will be replaced with this lower level version.  If they have SoW on it will not take.

Shield of Spikes:  This is an upgrade to the damage shields that druids are well known for.  At level 39 it deals 14 pts damage each time the druid is hit. It increases to 15 pts at level 40 and to 16 pts at level 41.  A great spell to use before battle.

Skin Like Diamond:  This spell adds about 15 armor class and 180 hitpoints +10 hitpoints per additional level up to level 44. 

Spikecoat:  An upgrade to the coat line of spells, this spell does 4 damage to any attacker that lands a successful hit.   Not sure of the armor class improvement, but I believe it is only about 5 AC.
