Rusty Weapons Quest for Gukta

 

This quest starts in Gukta:

 

You say, 'Hail, Shin Supplier Nugrud'

Shin Supplier Nugrud says 'You there, listen up. Though the battle for the Outpost of Marr was won, there is much work to do before we can rest. We must rid the swamps of Innoruuk's foul influence. Trolls still lurk in every shadow, and endanger our existence. It is my job to see that we can beat them back. However, we seem to have run into a bit of a [problem].'

You say, 'what problem?'

Shin Supplier Nugrud says 'Problem indeed. If you had not noticed, our supplies are growing thin. The militia is in desperate need of weaponry to equip our recruits. I would fetch some myself, but I am too busy here to leave my post. This is where you come in. Acquire a rusty dagger, mace, spear, and axe. If you return them to me, our smiths will then reforge them into passable weapons.'

Shin Supplier Nugrud flips through several pages of an inventory ledger. He shakes his head and the corners of his mouth curl downward to form a miserable frown. 'Blast! We are long overdue to receive another shipment of weapons. I cannot send our people into battle armed only with moss-covered stones and harsh words.'

Hand him the 4 rusty weapons

Shin Supplier Nugrud takes a few moments to examine the wares you brought, before speaking, 'Splendid. This is exactly what we need. Our smiths will sharpen and polish these for use in battle, posthaste. You have done fine work to further our cause.'
