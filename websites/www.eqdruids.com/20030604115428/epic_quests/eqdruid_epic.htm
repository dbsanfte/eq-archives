EQDruids Epic Guide

Research has shown that you can begin the Epic quest at 46 if you have high enough faction with Telin.  Some have also reported that you can do the final turn ins before level 51.

Since below 51 is not the original intention of the epic quest creators I don’t want to say for sure you can do these turn ins.  I will say that people have done them, and if you want to risk your work you can try it too.

You can however do a lot of the epic pre-51.  You can forage the different items and also can probably kill some of the different creatures needed.
