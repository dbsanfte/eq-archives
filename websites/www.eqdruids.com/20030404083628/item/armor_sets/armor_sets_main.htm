The ranked list for the above armor is as follows:

 

The rank number is calculated by taking the following calculation:

 

All added together

 

AC*10

Str

Wis*10

All Resists added together

Mana points divided by 6, multiplied by 10 (equivalent of 200 wisdom at level 50)

Effects*5

 

This gives the "base" stats that are most useable to a druid, with additional weight given to wisdom and mana.  This was done so the rankings had a base to work with.  That doesn't mean the rest of the stats aren't important.

 

IMPORTANT NOTE - Some of the rankings may be different than expected due to armor sets not all having the same number of items.  Please take that into consideration when viewing the list.
