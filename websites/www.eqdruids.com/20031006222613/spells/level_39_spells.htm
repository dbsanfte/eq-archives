Pack Regeneration: 

Effect:  Increase Hit Points (HP) by 5 per tick

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Full Group

Range to Target: 100 feet
Area Effect Range: 50 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 200
Spell Duration: 8.8 mins @ lvl 39 to 14.0 mins @ lvl 65
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You begin to regenerate.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> begins to regenerate.
Spell fades: You have stopped regenerating.

Great spell for groups, one cast, all people within range now are regenerating. 

 

I would only use this with 5-6 people in a group however, any less and its more mana efficient to cast single regeneration on each person.

 

Also the regeneration will only last half as long as the single spell.

 

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Winds of the North:

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Full Group

Area Effect Range: 100 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 300
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 9.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> discorporate in a portal of wind.
Spell fades: You appear in a gust of wind.

Be very careful when using this spell!  It will teleport you into the Skyfire zone, which is FULL of dragons, drakes, and worse.

 

The good news is, the druid port is reasonably safe when you port in.  You normally have time to invis up.  The wizard port is in a different location, and is almost a guaranteed death!
