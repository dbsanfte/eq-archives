Eyepatch of Plunder (Iceclad Ocean)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                             Armor, Face

Armor Class                          10

Boosts                                  +10 STR, +10 DEX, +50 HP, +6 CR, +6 DR, +6 FR, +6 MR, +6 PR

Effect                                    Captain Nalots Quickening

Weight & Size                       WT: 0.5

Class                                     ALL

Race                                     ALL

Talk to the lost pirate which can be found on the first island in Iceclad Ocean. Once you hail him, he will mention the map he drew a long time ago and how he lost all the various pieces to it. Tell him that "I want to find the pieces" and he will hand you the cover of the book. He will tell you that once you obtain all four parts, put them in the book and combine them.

The first part of the map drops off the Dire Wolf Stalker in Iceclad Ocean, who roams near the dragon bridge. The second part of the map is dropped by Stormfeather, a named griffon who spawns in Iceclad Ocean. To obtain the third part of the map, head over to the Great Divide and locate the cave with Bloodmaw. Hand Bloodmaw four Tizmak Meats and he will despawn, now say to the gnome "I want the map please" and he will hand you the third piece. The fourth piece is dropped by Lodizal, a giant sea turtle who also lives in the Iceclad Ocean zone.

Combine the four pieces of the map in the book and then hand it to the Lost Pirate. He will hand you back the Completed Iceclad Map. Now run over and hand your new map to Captain Nalat and you will receive the Eyepatch of Plunder.
