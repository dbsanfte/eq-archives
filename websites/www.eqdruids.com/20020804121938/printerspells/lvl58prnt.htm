Level Fifty Eight Spells

Fist of Karana: 

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) by 812 (L58)

Skill: Evocation
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: Target Based AoE

Range to Target: 200 feet
Area Effect Range: 10 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 357
Spell Duration: Instant
Area Effect Duration: 5
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You have been struck by the shocking Fist of Karana.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> has been struck by the shocking Fist of Karana.

This spell does 812 hps of damage as an Area Effect.  Again, the mobs must be very close to each other for this spell to affect them all.

Outdoors only

Dropped by NPCs

 

Regrowth of the Grove:  

Effect:  Increase Hit Points (HP) by 15 (L58)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Full Group

Area Effect Range: 60 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 600
Spell Duration: Unknown
Duration Formula: 10
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You begin to regenerate.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> begins to regenerate.
Spell fades: You have stopped regenerating.

This is the group regeneration spell you've been waiting for.  One cast, and all your party is regening hps at 15 per tick.

Reported drops from Juggernauts in Old Sebilis and Guardian Wurms in Skyfire

 

Shield of Blades:  

Effect:  Damage Shield by 32 (L58)

Skill: Abjuration
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 100 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Required: 150
Spell Duration: Unknown
Duration Formula: 8
Casting Time: 3.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are surrounded by a barrier of blades.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is surrounded by a barrier of blades.
Spell fades: The blades fall away.

Upgraded Damage Shield spell.  Each time a mob hits you, you will cause them 32 points of damage.

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor
