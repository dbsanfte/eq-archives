Epic Submission

To have us add you to the Druid Hall of Fame you need to submit the following to our e-mail

The name of your Druid

The name of the server you play on

What level you were, when you received your epic

And to allow us to verify your epic, you need to submit a screenshot holding your epic weapon.  We cannot post a graphic of each person, but this allows us to see that you have obtained the Epic.

 

Once this is received, and verified you will be added to the Druid Hall of Fame!
