Faydedar Techniques

How to Kill: Faydedar

Heya.

This is *my* guide on how to kill Faydedar. I have attended several raids versus this Dragon (Including two for my own epic). I also have borrowed information from Brewall's guide at www.crimsontalon.com and http://www.pucksworld.com/fay.html

I would suggest that someone create a alt - and stash them at the oasis. This will allow a check of the trigger so a entire raid force is not moved to the oasis to not have the trigger available to battle. The trigger is a Dwarf Fisherman named Dolgin Codslayer, his respawn is 12 hours. YOU WILL NOT KILL HIM. In fact - just stay away from him entirely.

You will need between 18 and 30 people.

What level do I have to be?:

I would strongly recommend high 40s - 60. Faydedar has a Area of Effect Fear spell known as Dragon's Roar. Granted a high magic resistance is helpful for resisting this, nothing can beat being a higher level to improve your chances of resisting the Fear attack and Lava Breath Weapon.

What do I have to bring?:

Resist Gear. This Gear should make your Save versus magic A LEAST 50. I would go as far as to suggest 75. You will have a *unbuffed* Fire resistance of as near 100 as possible. Low resistances and you can plan for a early death. If a Mage is handy - resist gear can be summoned for you.

Getting There:

Getting there takes time - and normally lots of it. People should effectively begin moving to the raid location 1 hour PRIOR to the start time.

Melee should bind in East Freeport. Casters should bind at the Oasis docks (there is no Binding in the Timorous Deep).

Once people are bound, and in Timorous Deep you will need to run to the oasis.

The Oasis is hidden behind to thick stone walls, the only way threw the walls is by locating the underwater entrance in each. I recommend using EB, or EB items.

The location of the OUTER Ring is: Neg 11520, Neg 3776

Once threw the cave, you will see another wall, the location of the SECOND Cave is: Neg 11333, Neg 1352.

Once threw the second cave - you will be on a beach with a large pier. This is the correct location - wait for the rest of the raid to arrive. Seriously. This is a long run without being CoH'd. If you even think you may be late - camp here.

Information on Faydedar

Faydedar is a level 55 Dragon with a 300-500 AoE Lava Breath Weapon. His Breath Weapon will dispel one buff. Right Click items are handy. Faydedar will dual hit in the low - mid 300s. To land spells on Faydedar, you must be in Melee range. Anyone sitting during the battle will be instantly moved to the top of the Hate list. No one sits - period.

Buffing:

When you show up, you will dispel and drop ALL buffs. Due to Faydedar's dispel, you will have 3 or 4 Junk Buffs prior to the battle beginning. All Priest Classes will Obtain a Mod Rod from a Mage if present.

Buff Order:
Caster - See Invis
Caster - Resist Cold
Caster - Resist Poison
Shaman - Talisman of Altuna
Shaman - Harnessing of Spirit
Cleric - Shield of Words
Cleric - Heroism | Druid NatureSkin
Cleric - Symbol of Marzin.
Enchanter - Rune V (MA Only)
Enchanter - haste (Anya's Quickening)
Druid/Shaman - Regrowth
Druid - Circle of Winter OR Circle of Seasons
Mage - Boon of Immolation (+ FR!)
Ranger - Call of Earth
Paladin - Divine Glory

Positioning and Battle:

The MA will be near the pier. The Monk Puller will be in the water.

EVERYONE Else - will be near the (NOTE: NOT ON) the wall by the dwarf. NO ONE will be sitting. No one will be CASTING, and No Bards will be SINGING. THERE ARE NO PETS.

The Druid will then turn the Froglok Essence into the dwarf - this will trigger Faydedar (You get the Froglok Item Back). Faydedar will Dragon Roar. Monk will pull Fay to the shore and FD, MA will then gain Aggro. If MA is 56, and you have a large number of healers - MA should use Furious Disc. Priest Classes will use Remedy, Devine Light, Super Heal, and Chloroblast to Keep the MA Alive (No Cheal Rotation).

Once MA Has aggro, he will call attack. NOW the other Tanks leave the caster area and assist on the Dragon. If you have never seen Faydedar, he is not Large, he looks like the Velium Crawlers in Crystal Caverns - LOOK DOWN. When the tanks are sent in, Pet users may NOW summon pets and send them in. This is so they don't aggro upon the Dragon's Roar Faydedar opens the battle with.

Bards will not sing UNTIL they are in Melee range of Faydedar.


Shamans and Druids will also help toss smaller heals on people caught in Faydedar's Lava Breath.
Bards should be playing Elemental Rhythm/Chorus

At 60%: Slower/Tasher/Debuffer Should Enter Melee Range.
You Have *one* Shot at this. Tash, Attempt to slow, Malo line, etc.

At 50%: Int Casters enter Melee range and Nuke.

At 40%: Use Offensive Discs, Including Deadeye, Charge /Mighty Strike, Inner Flame.

Continue until Dead.

Faydedar ONLY Drops a Pod of Seawater for the Druid Epic. This item can be Multiquested. The Timer on this mobs body is 30 minutes.

IF YOU FAIL:

Faydedar will despawn in about 40ish min of NOT BEING AGGROED. So, head back to Oasis, and WAIT outside the outer wall. Trackers will give the all clear to enter the oasis for rezzing.
