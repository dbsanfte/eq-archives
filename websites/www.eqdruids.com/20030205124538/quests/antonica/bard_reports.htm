Bard Reports

The Bard Guild's needs your help in keeping an eye on the town, if you wish to accept their assignment.

Talk to Juseanus and he will give you two scrolls to deliver. Take one to the North gate and another to the docks.

You may be given scrolls to return as well.

Each delivery is worth a little bit of experience.

Also, the following factions will improve: LeagueofAntonicanBards, KnightsofTruth, GuardsofQeynos. RingofScale and MayongMistmoore go down.

Because you can do this quickly and get a faction hit for each delivery, this is a good way to improve faction.
