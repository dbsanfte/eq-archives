Resist Magic : The top and final resist buff.  This adds to the all important magic resist.

Skin Like Nature : The final Skin spell - this is better than the level 44 Cleric spells as far as buffs go. This also adds some regeneration - adds 2hps standing and 3hps sitting +250 hps + 16 AC

Engulfing Roots : another weak ass root spell - however this does more than CL (160 at level 50 damage) and should be used in its place in hot places

Fire : Column Fire - AE 392 damage - fire based

Ice : DD Column - indoor / outdoor - 612 damage - reduces fire resist

Lightning Blast - 477 OD only DD

Shield of Thorns - the pimp tool - 24 damage shield for a measly 120 mana....make those guild members happy!

Thorncoat - Personal Shield and last in the line - keep it up at all times for AC +32 and 5 points reflect damage.

Starfire - AE but used as DD a lot - 612 damage

Pack Chloroplast - Manastone junkies unite! this is a group spell that you will be casting often

Succor: North - Escape from those nasty places  - this spell sucks as all evils will get tore apart by the locals
