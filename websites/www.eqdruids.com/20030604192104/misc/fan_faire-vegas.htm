Fan Faire Vegas

 

Ah Fan Faire.  How shall I sum you up.  Lets go with the good, the bad, and the ugly.

 

The panels were very very worthwhile.  The developers had a lot to say.  The transcripts will be out soon, so I'm going to hit a few of the things they said.

 

One very important thing to remember when you read the below and the transcripts.  The team that is working on EQ is completely new for the most part.  So they can't just go in and "make a quick change".  In a lot of cases they are having to completely rewrite sections of the game (like adding the extra bank space).  So trust me when I say there is a TON of stuff they want to do, and just may not be able to.

 

Yes they would love to add more spell slots, it is just a question of resources.  Many of them play casters, they understand the pain.

 

Melee is going to have more changes coming to them, some things were over done, some under etc. 

 

The Rathe encounter is being looked at.  They realized they may have made that one a touch too hard.  However, plane of time is finished no matter what rumors are running around.

 

The Druid Bear pet was put in as a toy for druids, not a full pet.  I think we all knew this :)

 

They are going to continue to try and communicate changes as fast as they can to the public at large. 

 

More will come out with the transcripts.  The EQ2 talk was AWESOME.  If you want to read more on that, go to my EQ2 info.

 

The costumes at the costume contest were just fantastic.  Some incredible stuff there (yes, pictures are coming)

 

That was the good.  Seeing old friends, meeting new friends was also of course a good thing.

 

The bad.  Underutilizing the space they had available!  You walk into a hall that (I kid not) is about the size of a football field, yet they are only using a corner of it in rooms.  SPREAD out!

 

Listing our races on our badge?  Really would have been better to list our classes.  Makes more sense to this poor tired druid.

 

Showing us the EQ2 demo at the Friday night reception was a great idea, having to turn the lights up and down and up and down cuz they couldn't see the keyboard.  Tsk tsk, get a flashlight! :)

 

The ugly.  No one even if they had a blood hound was going to be able to find me unless they had tracking.  Finding anyone in the Fan Faire was a major problem.

 

Please please PLEASE in future Fan Faires, put up more signs!!  80% of all the confusion people were having could have been stopped by having just a few more signs explaining what was where!

 

And lastly, this goes out to that special waitress at Quarks who told myself and my party, that we couldn't sit down with another group of people that had invited us to their table, because we hadn't waited in line.  Not because we were going to take a table from someone else (there was a ton of empty tables) but because we hadn't waited in line. 

 

Overall am I glad I went to Fan Faire, sure.  Do I see room for improvements in the execution of Fan Faire in the future, yep.  But hey, everyone gets better with age.

 

Now lets get back on with life, tomorrow I'll get the pics up I promise.  Until then, enjoy the night.

 

 - Spiritual
