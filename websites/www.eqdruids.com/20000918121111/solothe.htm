Solo the Dino in OOT? YES YOU CAN!
 
If you own a manastone and have the patience to try it a lev 50 druid can solo the Dino in OOT.
 
My first success: 1 hour 20 mins approx of kiting. In time and practice i'm sure i can get this time down to about 40 mins of kiting.
 
How to go about it:
 
1) buff up med up and start with fresh SOW & CHLOR
2) use snare cause root just don't stick and when it does it don't last long enough
3) snare the dino, dot the dino, sunbeam the dino (sunbeam gives the effect of fear spell hence monster runs from you DOT does more damage), jog ahead and med while using F9 to see where the dino is at in relation to you when he gets close jog ahead and med or repeat the snare dot sunbeam as needed.
4) while your dot is still good you can sunbeam after sunbeam to keep the dino on the run.
5) very important to allow enough mana saved in case your regen or sow starts to fade so you can recast them.
6) another thing to watch out for is where you med don't med near the goblins on the island cause well we all know what them green buggers do when you sit near them they like to take cheap shots and smack you and become a prob while kiting the big guy.
 
Thats about it I dunno if it's worth the time to do it vs the reward which could be from nothing but lizard scales upwards to 2 rare gems in one shot. If anything else it should be something to try to just be able to say yea i soloed the Dino with my druid.
