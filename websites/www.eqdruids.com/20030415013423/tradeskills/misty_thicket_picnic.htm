Guide To Making Misty Thicket Picnics
 

Misty Thicket Picnics are used both because of their amazing stats, and in the Protection of The Cabbage quest.  Due to the many components that go into a Misty Thicket Picnic they can be complicated to understand.  This guide should help with learning how to make this item.

First you must make The Picnic Basket. You will need one picnic basket per attempt at the finished product.

 

To make a Picnic Basket you need 1 Woven Mandrake and 1 Steel Boning.

 

To make the Woven Mandrake you need to purchase two Mandrake Roots from Callowolf MacFuller in Shadow Haven.  The combine is made in a sewing kit and has a trivial of 66 (note, this can also be used to raise beginning tailoring).

 

To make your boning you will first need to make yourself a file.

 

Port on over to Great Divide and head into Thurgadin.  You are looking for Wevil Doughbeard.  From Wevil purchase a file mold.

 

Now you need metal bits.  Take two small pieces of ore and combine them with a flask of water in the forge.  If successful you will have a metal bit.  Take this metal bit, your file mold and a flask of water and combine in the forge.  If you succeed you will have your very own File.  The trivial on files is 22

 

Combine in a sewing kit 1 mandrake root with 1 boning.  If successful you will have made 10 picnic baskets.  The trivial is 76

 

Now it is time to move on to some baking.  You first need to make Apricot Marmalade.  Take 3 frostings and 1 foraged fruit and combine together to make Apricot Marmalade (yield is 6).  The trivial on this item is 99.

 

Now that you have your marmalade, it is time to make a Marmalade Sandwich.  Take 1 loaf of bread and 1 apricot marmalade.  Combine, and if successful you will have made Marmalade Sandwiches (yield is 12 if successful).  The trivial on the sandwich is also 99.

 

Now what type of picnic basket would this be without drink?  That’s right, it is time to start your brewing.

 

You will need to make JumJum spiced beer.

 

To make the beer, you need to combine 1 barley, 1 hops, 1 spices a flask of water, a bottle, and 1 jumjum stalk.

 

The hops can be bought at Tobart Dirkins in Thurgadin, the Spices can be bought from Perkins Doughbeard also in Thurgadin.  The flask of water and bottle can be purchased all over, and the jumjum stalk can be purchased from Daleen Leafsway in Rivervale.

 

Successful combination will yield 10 beers, and the trivial is 162.

 

Now it is time to make your JumJum salad.

 

Combine in a mixing bowl 1 foraged vegetable, 1 lettuce, 1 JumJum stalk and 1 fennel together.  If successful you will make 10 salads.  The trivial on this is 142.

 

Let’s make an after dinner mint now. 

 

Combine in a mixing bowl 1 frosting and 1 eucalyptus.  If successful you will make 10 Royal Mints.  The trivial is 76.

 

Eucalyptus can be purchased from Polson Medicinal in Shadowhaven.

 

It is time to add some cheese to the mix.  But first you need to make yourself a spoon.

 

To make a spoon combine 1 scaler mold, 2 metal bits, and one water flask in a forge.  If successful you will make a Dairy Spoon.  This item is trivial at 86, and is a reusable item.  It will not be destroyed on your combines.

 

Ok it is time for the cheese.  Go to Gragner Kenforth in Shadowhaven.  Purchase milk and rennet.  Combine 2 milk, 1 rennet and your spoon in your mixing bowl.  If successful you will have made 10 Mature Cheeses.  The trivial on cheese is 84.

 

It is time to make desert!  You will be making a JumJum cake.  But first we need to make some dough and then some frosting.

 

To make dough you need to combine 1 flour, 1 milk, and 1 egg in your mixing bowl.  Now depending on what type of egg you use, is what will decide how many clumps of dough you will make.

 

A snake egg will yield 1

An Aviak egg will yield 5 if foraged egg, 7 if it is an egg looted off an aviak

An Alligator egg will yield 5 if it is a foraged egg, 10 if it is an egg looted off an alligator
A cockatrice egg will yield 5

A griffon egg will yield 7

A basilisk egg will yield 5 if foraged, 15 if it is looted.

A dragon egg will yield 20

A drake egg will yield 12

A Wurm egg will yield 14

 

Be aware as you are gathering these eggs that they are considered food.  If you leave them in the wrong spot in your backpacks, you will eat them.

 

Now you need to make some Winter Chocolate Frosting for your cake.  To make Winter Chocolate you need to combine 1 brownie part with 2 frostings in your mixing bowl. 

 

The easiest place to get brownie parts is Nillipus in Rivervale, and also the brownie scouts in Greater Faydark.  I don’t recommend the brownie camp in Lesser Faydark until high level.

 

Now lets make the cake.  Take 1 clump of dough, 1 frosting, 1 winter chocolate, one jumjum stalk and a cake round and combine in an oven/spit.  If successful you will make 20 cakes.  The trivial is 166 for these cakes.

 

With the cake made, it is time to slice it.  This is the easiest part of this entire recipe.  Put 1 cake into an oven and hit combine.  If successful you will have 10 slices of cake.  The trivial on this is 152.

 

Now that you have all the parts made, it is time to make the Misty Thicket Picnic. 

 

Combine 1 picnic basket, 1 marmalade sandwich, 1 jumjum spiced beer, 1 jumjum salad, 1 royal mint, 1 mature cheese, and 2 slices of jumjum cake in an oven.  If successful you will make 4 of the following:

 

Misty Thicket Picnic

 

Miraculous Meal

Str 5, Sta 5, Wis 5, Int 4, HP 20, Mana 30

Wt. 0.1

 

The trivial on the Misty Thicket Picnic is 250+ (meaning you will never be trivial on this item)
