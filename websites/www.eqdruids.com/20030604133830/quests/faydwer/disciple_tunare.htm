Disciple Symbol of Tunare

You must first do the initiate quest.

Say to Yeolarn, 'I will slay the Necromancer'

Yeolarn Bronzeleaf says 'The Fier'Dal rangers that inhabit the Lesser Faydark have spotted a courier making deliveries to the Teir'Dal camp near Castle Mistmoore and to a necromancer that lingers near the ancient obelisk. We believe that the crates he carries are supplies needed for the creation of more Ghasts. Another shipment should be arriving soon. Seek out the necromancer at the obelisk and take his head then take the head of the courier and return them to me with the crate that the courier carries and your Initiate Symbol of Tunare.

The courier is making deliveries to the Teir'Dal camp near Castle Mistmoore and to a necromancer that lingers near the ancient obelisk. We believe that the crates he carries are supplies needed for the creation of more Ghasts. Another shipment should be arriving soon. Seek out the necromancer at the obelisk and take his head then take the head of the courier and return them to me with the crate that the courier carries and your Initiate Symbol of Tunare.

Yeolarn Bronzeleaf says 'I require the head of the necromancer, the head of the courier, the crate the courier carries and your Initiate Symbol'

The necromancer you are trying to spawn is Larik Z' Vole (a dark elf Necro).  He spawns at the undead Obelisk in Lesser Faydark.

 
The location of the undead Obelisk is 100, 2200 in Lesser Faydark.
 

Now Larik can take some time to spawn, so be prepared.  There will be two undead creatures that spawn on the Obelisk, one at the top near the Obelisk and one down the steps.  The one down the steps is Larik's placeholder.  He can only spawn at night also.

 

I found it worked best to stay bound near the Obelisk, and when game time was getting close to night I would gate back over and start killing the undead in the area.  It took three "nights" to get him to spawn, but he will eventually spawn.

 

When you kill Larik you will spawn the Dark Elf Courier.  The way I handled it was simple.  I looted Larik's head, then started to track the Priestess at the Dark Elf camp.  The courier is running from the Greater Fay zone area to the Dark Elf camp.  By running to the Dark Elf camp I was able to get the courier on track and chase him down before he reached the camp.

 

Some have also had luck binding near the Greater Fay zone and gating after they looted the necro's head.

 

Kill the courier and loot his head and the crate he was carrying.  Now you should have:

 

 

 

Bring the two heads and the crate back to Yeolarn.
