Feskr's Supplies:  hail Feskr Drinkmaker and he says 'Erh?  If you not be here to help Feskr with his [problem] then Feskr wants you to leave. Say, 'what problem?' and he says 'You wish to help?  Orrderrr of supplies I order not arrive, ship sink.  Now Feskr needs supplies.  Feskr need bottle of Tunares Finest (buy in Felwithe), Thunderhoof Mushroom (forage in SK), Tea Leaf (forage in EK), and Handmade Backpack (tailoring skill).  Rrrr, strange items but Feskr need to sell.  You bring Feskr give you nice bag."  Give him the items and you get the rough leather sack, a weight reducing backpack. (30 percent reduction)

Fish Dinner:  Roary Fishcatcher,  in the hut immediately to your right upon entering Kerra Isle, is an excellent chef and loves to prepare different types of fish.  He wishes you to venture to Nektulos Forest to hunt the Darkwater Pirhanna and return it to him for a reward.  The fish swim in the Nektulos Forest River (they are a very rare spawn) and sometimes drop a Raw Darkwater Piranha Fish when killed.  Bring back this delicacy and your reward is a Kerran Fishing Spear.

Gnomish Toy:  The Kerra Iseqs are children who beg you to bring them a gnomish toy. There is a clockwork in Steamfont called Nilit's Clockwork which spawns near the mino cave and drops a gnome tinkered toy.  This is likely what is needed for this quest.  Your reward is a Kerran Toy, which goes in the ranged slot.

Health Potion:  Errak Thickshank has a sick friend and needs the ingredients to make a potion to cure her.  Bring her a giant snake fang, an Aviak Charm, and a Tiger Skin not  from Kerra Isle.  Your reward is a Bone Talisman, which goes in the off hand.

The Luck of Allix:  Allix feels that in order to be a bettter warrior, he needs a good luck charm.  He asks you to venture to Eastern Karanas and bring him back an Arjah Leaf from a tree there.  I assume you have to kill a treant to get this.  I don't know the reward for this.

Rat Teeth:  Kill wharf rats that spawn in the warehouse near the docks and collect their teeth (not the ones in the water).  Turn them in to Thalith Mamluk, who stands by the docks, for a fishbone necklace.

Razortooth:  You can find Feren on a boat in the wharf, fishing.  He says he enjoys fishing but is forever haunted by a vicious fish named Razortooth that continues to nibble at his boat and eat his catch.  He says he will reward you if you kill him and return the remains.  The fish is a very rare spawn.  Your reward for this is a fishing pole (1HB, 3/17).  It seems likely that the pole is needed for another quest somewhere.

Shiny Rings:  This is to get the rings for the Paw of Opolla Quest.  Melixis is a merchant that sells supplies for shaman-made potions.  When you hail her she claims to have three [shiny tings] that she found all over the world.  These shiny tings are [a dull ring] (the copper ring) a [kinda shiny ring] (the silver ring ) and a [really shiny ring] (the gold ring).  For the dull ring she wants a bracket mildew, which drops off of the Fungus Mutant in Upper Guk.  For the kinda shiny ring she wants faerix spores, which drop off the Mutant Fungus.  And for the really shiny ring she wants degenerated guk weed, which also drops off the Fungus Mutant in Upper Guk.

The platinum ring drops from Gynok (STILL the only way to get it)
The gold ring is the reward for bringing the degenerated guk weed (Mutant Fungus in Uguk) to Melixis.
The silver ring is the reward for bringing the Faerix Spores (Mutant Fungus in Uguk) to Melixis.
The copper ring is the reward for bringing the Bracket Guk Mildew (Mutant Fungus in Uguk)  to Melixis.

Silvani Leaf:  Urkath wants you to bring him a silvani leaf, which are rumored to drop from brownies in Lesser Faydark.  Thisdrops from Ivey Bramblefoot in lesser faydark, who has a placeholder.  The actual spawn point is somewhere south of the pixie tower (the loc escapes me) but can be found as follows.  On the west wall of the zone near the south corner there is a 'valley'.  near the north end of this valley you can find an Orc Centurion stuck in place turning in circles.  He is the placeholder.  Kill him and every 6 or so minutes he will come back.  Each time you kill him you can watch him approaching and track him northeast a bit to a small hillside, then east a bit to his spawn point.  After I found the placeholder it took me about 30 minutes to get to the spawn point and about 6 hours of killing the orc to make him spawn.  The orc is about level 10, Ivey is a level 20 druid.   The reward is Worn Leather Shoulderpads, which are 4ac +4str. 

Test of Kejaar:  There is a Kerran named Shazad Asad who tells you that you can prove yourself a Kejaar by helping destroy their Erudite enemies.  He first wants you to destroy the Erudite Emissary (approx level 20).  The emissary runs around Tox Forest.  Kill him and bring back his head for a Kejaar Totem .  You will then be told to to kill their most hated foe, Sentinel Creot (approx level 30), a roaming guard in Tox Forest.  If you kill him and return his head you will become a full Sejah warrior, and receive the reward of a Sejah Ghulam Bracer.
