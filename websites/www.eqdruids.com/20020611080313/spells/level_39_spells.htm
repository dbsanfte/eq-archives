Pack Regeneration: 

Effect:  Increase Hit Points (HP) by 5 (L39)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Full Group

Range to Target: 100 feet
Area Effect Range: 50 feet

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 200
Spell Duration: Unknown
Duration Formula: 9
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You begin to regenerate.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> begins to regenerate.
Spell fades: You have stopped regenerating.

Great spell for groups, one cast, all people within range now are regenerating.  I would only use this with 5-6 people in a group however, any less and its more mana efficient to use single regeneration.

Also the regeneration will only last half as long as the single spell.

Ro's Fiery Sundering:  

Effect:  Decrease Fire Resist from 37 (L39) to 42 (L60)
Effect:  Decrease Armor Class (AC) from 6 (L39) to 8 (L60)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Fire + 5

Mana Required: 50
Spell Duration: 50 ticks (5.0 minutes)
Duration Formula: 1
Casting Time: 3.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are immolated by blazing flames.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is immolated by blazing flames.
Spell fades: The flames die down.

This is a nice spell.  When it sticks it reduces the mobs AC and Fire resistance.  Which of course makes it easier for your flame spells to hit.

Only downfall is this spell will normally cause INSTANT agro on you, if the tanks haven't been building enough agro.  So practice with it before you use.

Resistance Check: None

Mana Required: 300
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 9.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

Spell cast on someone: <Target> discorporates in a portal of wind.
Spell fades: You appear in a gust of wind.

Be very careful when using this spell!  It will teleport you into the Skyfire zone, which is FULL of dragons, drakes, and worse.

The good news is, the druid port is reasonably safe when you port in.  You normally have time to invis up.  The wizard port is in a different location, and is almost a guaranteed death!
