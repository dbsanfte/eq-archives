In the Iceclad Ocean there are a bunch of pirates who talk about a lost key, and a lost case of rum. Talk to Qarrgy Scallopgobble, a named Otter who can be found on the beaches in Cobalt Scar. Hand him two Barracuda Livers which drop off Cold Water Barracudas in Cobalt Scar, and you will receive the Bladder of Acidic Ooze. Now head off to the Wakening Lands and forage some Fresh Tree Sap. Run back to Pythic Urson and hand him the Sap and Ooze, and you'll be rewarded with the Bottle of Dissolving Liquid.

Now off to the Great Divide and hand the Bottle to the Gnomish Deserter who can be found on the top of the mountain near the wurm tunnels. He will in turn hand you back the Locked Rum Box. Now run over to the Eastern Wastes and get four Fresh Rhino Meats, which drop off the Wooly Rhinos. You will also need a High Quality Dire Wolf Fur which is dropped by Kael Drakkel Wolves. Run off to the Wakening Lands and hand the Rhino Meat to the Mist Panther on the south shore of the Plane of Growth tower. You will receive the Broken Key in return.

Now back to Iceclad Ocean and locate Errgriz, a named Snowfang Gnoll. Hand him the High Quality Dire Wolf Fur and you will receive the Wolf Fur Ear Covers. Run off to the Gnomish Pirate Village and hand Sojan the Sleepless the Earmuffs, and you will be rewarded with the Fine Metal Chain. Hand in the chain and the broken key, and you'll receive the Golden Key. Now hand this key and rum box to Captain Nalot and you'll be rewarded with the Bottle of Captain Nalot's Triple Strength Rum. Run down to the docks and hand Nelet Durzit the bottle and you'll be handed the Rough Silver Chain in return.
