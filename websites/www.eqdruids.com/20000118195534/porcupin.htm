Porcupines...Ya know? The furry critter that gets in ball and lets thing bash themselves to death against its spines?

Although this tactic is used at lower levels - it is MOST interesting to note its a viable tactic for level 39+ when soloing.

Precombat Buffs:

Skin like Diamonds (nature at 49)
Spikecoat (Thorncoat at 49)
Chloroplast
Form of the Great Wolf (+62 atk + sow)
Storm Strength
Firefist

Weapon choice: (delay is most important)

1HS - Well Balanced Scimitar
1HB - Black Enameled Mace
2HB - Shovel

Combat Spell Order:

Cast Shield of Spikes (thorns at 49)
Snare target
DoT (Drifting Death) if desired for faster kill
Melee until down to 1.5 bubbles of health
Run off for about 10 seconds
Cast 2 Greater Heals on self
Return to melee and repeat until dead

Although this is MUCH slower than nuking down your foe - its a great way to increase Offense, Defense, and weapon skills.

Please note that grouping is better EXP and SKILLS - but this is fun too =)
