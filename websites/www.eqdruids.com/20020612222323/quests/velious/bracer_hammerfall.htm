Bracer of Hammerfall

Sentry Kale wants you to pick up some Velium in the Dwarven mines.

Take the note he gives you and 50 pp to Ungdin in the mines under Thurgadin.

He will then give you a velium shipment to return to Kale. Give it to Sentry Kale and he will occasionally reward you with a bracer of hammerfall.
