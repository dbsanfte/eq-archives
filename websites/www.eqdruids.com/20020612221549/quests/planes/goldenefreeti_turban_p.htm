Golden Efreeti Turban (PoA)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                              Armor, Head

Armor Class                           10

Boosts                                   +5 STR, +6 FR, +6 DR, +6 CR, +6 MR, +6 PR

Effect                                     None

Weight & Size                       WT: 4.5  Size: ?

Class                                      ALL

Race    ALL

This item is dropped by the great genies, Noble Dojorn and the Overseer of Air. They require many groups of adventurers fighting together to bring them down and even then they can easily kill all those that are unprepared. They both have many different attacks and great spells that can be used to slay all who try to obtain their treasures.

Most of the items that drop from Noble Dojorn are needed to complete the different quests given out in the Plane of Air.
