Druidic Skills Guide

The druid has a mix of skills of pure caster, melee, and some of the ranger skills.   Each skill has a name, description, recommended point investment, movement, needed for, and max formula.  Except for spell skills - you will not be able to go over 200 until you reach level 51!

If you see anything that needs correction, please let us know so we can change it - Spiritual / Tesiux
