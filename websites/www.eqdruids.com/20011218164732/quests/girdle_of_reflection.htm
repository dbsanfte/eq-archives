Girdle of Reflection (Kael)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                              Armor, Waist

Armor Class                           12

Boosts                                   +6 WIS, +35 HP

Effect                                     None

Weight & Size                        WT: 2.3  Size: ?

Class                                      CLR, PAL, RNG, DRU, SHM

Race                                      ALL

Talk with Lieutenant Krofer who can be found in the Wakening Land. Say to him "I am prepared". He will give you a Letter for War, and will tell you to give it to Staff Sergeant Drioc who can be found within Kael Drakkal. Drioc will sign it and hand it back to you, saying that the troops are ready to move. Return the note to Krofer, and he will hand you yet another note, which he will tell you to give back to him after the battle.

Four giants will spawn and will run over to the Sifaye camp [1750, -275]. Now issue the command, "troop attack" and the giants will charge into the Sifaye camp. High level Sifaye's will spawn to defend their camp, basically you are meant to lose. Now return to Krofer and hand him back the letter he gave you. He will hand you the Failure Report and tell you once again tell you to go and see Drioc. Hand Drioc the report and he will tell you to go and see his financier, and then hands you back the Failure Report.

His financier is Noble Helssen who can be found next to Wenglawks in the Noble's building. Give Helssen the failure notice and he will tell you that it was not your fault and that Drioc was to blame. He then will divulge that he seeks to not only control the wakening land but the very force of Growth itself, and he needs your help. He will hand you a Tablet inscribed with special runes and tell you to seek out Corudoth, a named sea turtle, in the Iceclad Ocean.

Head off to Iceclad Ocean and hand Corudoth, who can be found under a chunk of ice [-6000, 6000]. Hand him the tablet, he will reward you with a Lock of Hair. He will tell you to travel to Thurgadin where you will have to look for Petcas, who owns a small bar. You must kill him to obtain the Ancient Rusted Key. From here you will travel to the Tower of Frozen Shadow and locate Abrams [Incoherent Spirit on 6th Floor]. Kill the spirit to make Abrams spawn, and then hand him the Lock of Hair. You will be rewarded with the Arcanum.

Now head back to Helssen and hand him the Arcanum and the Ancient Rusted Key and he will hand you a voucher. From here, there are three different rewards that you can receive, depending on who you hand the voucher to. Hand the voucher to Kellek to receive the Collar of the Storm, or to Wenglawks to obtain the Girdle of Reflection, or lastly hand him back the voucher and he will hand you the Helssen's Prismatic Trinket.
