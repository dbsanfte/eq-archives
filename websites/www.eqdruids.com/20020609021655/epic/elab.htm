2. Find Venril Sathir in Karnor's Castle.  

   **Note: If you are not in one of the larger guilds that kills Venril Sathir at spawn time each week, do not worry.  There is a new and improved change that works to your favor.  Venril Sathir must be killed first.  Within 22 - 26 hours later the Remains of Venril Sathir will spawn.  The remains DO NOT spawn in the same room as Venril Sathir.  They spawn in the prep room.

    A. Hand the Remains of Venril Sathir a summoned "Globe of Fireflies".  This is a low level druid/ranger lightsource spell that is self only.  This will spawn the "Venril Sathir Spirit"

    B. Hand him the level 49 Cleric Ressurection Spell.  This will spawn a new Venril Sathir that is less difficult to kill.  This Venril Sathir will drop TWO Pulsating green stones.
