There is an NPC in Planes of Knowledge that will tell you what Character flags you have so far.

You say, 'Hail, Seer Mal Nae`Shi'

Seer Mal Nae`Shi snaps from her meditation, now aware of your presence.  'Greetings traveler, I am Mal Nae`Shi.  I have come here to heighten my awareness through meditation, much can be learned through a brief exploration of one's self... Assuming there is something there to learn.  While reaching inner peace is a journey each spirit must undertake on its own, I can begin your enlightenment with a short [guided meditation], which will aid your memory, when you are seated and ready.

You have to be seated when you reply "what guided meditation" for her to tell you the flags.

If you do not have any flags yet, you will see:

You manage to recover some images from your childhood, but no recent events spark a memory.

Otherwise she will list the different flags you have completed
