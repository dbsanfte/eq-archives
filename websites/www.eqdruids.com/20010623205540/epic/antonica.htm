2. Find the Corrupted Aquagoblin Shaman in Lake Rathe.  He is located underwater on top of the deepwater goblin tower.  His placeholders are deepwater goblins and are the outermost spawns on the top of the tower.  Wolf form will prevent agro and allow you to pull singles on top of the island.  Many tainted should spawn but only a certain one will spawn the corrupted. Best method is to pull them one at a time on top of the island and kite them so the other goblin shaman below do not heal the damage you are doing.  It is very easy to hide behind trees so his arrows do not interrupt your casting.  Kill him and loot this:
