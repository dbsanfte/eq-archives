Along with these gems, you need to find leather drops from the following zones.  Each zone has listed which type of leather armor drops you need to complete the quest.

Zone to turn in Leather Type of Leather Where to find
Thurgadin Eroded Leather In Kael Drakkel (near arena)
Kael Drakkel Ancient Leather Temple of Veeshan (non-testing halls)
Skyshrine Unadorned Leather Temple of Veeshan (testing halls)

Your faction is going to help decide which of the armors you do at any given time.  To do any armor quest, you need to have ALLY faction with the group.  So to make sure every understands what you need, you need three of the gems listed above, and the corresponding piece of leather listed above.
