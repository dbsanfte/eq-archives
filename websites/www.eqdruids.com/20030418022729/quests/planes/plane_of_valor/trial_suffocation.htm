Trial of Suffocation

 

Head to the area with The Tribunal, and ask them about Mavuin. Following the dialog, saying "I am prepared" to each Tribunal will give you description of the six trials.

To do this one, say "I am ready to begin the trial of suffocation."

Make sure you are actually prepared when you say that because your group will be teleported to the event area where you will only get a few seconds before things start.

The object of this trial is to keep the prisoners from suffocating.

You do this by killing the Spirits of Suffocation (also Asphyxiation).

The prisoners die quickly so this mob needs to be the first one to die (3 others spawn with it).

There is no time to rest between waves, unlike there is in
Execution, so once this starts expect a non-stop fight till the end.
