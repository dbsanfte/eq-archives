Find Faelin Bloodbriar in Greater Faydark.  She will be located around the northern part of the zone just west of Crushbone.  Faelin spawns at Pos 1500 Pos 500 and then runs in a circular pattern around that location.  She has one orc pawn as a placeholder.  The placeholder is on a 8 minute timer.  Hand her the note and you will recieve this ring:

3. Find Giz X'Tin Kithikor in Kithicor forrest.  He can be found roaming along the path anywhere in the zone.  I recommend doing this in the day time as there are many high levels mobs at night.  Hand him the ring and you will recieve this coin:

(Note: After you hand in the ring a lvl 50 Dark elf reaver will spawn but you can flee from the zone without worries if you are sowed or wolf-formed.  It is not necessary to kill the reaver and only causes a faction hit that might prevent you from running this part of the quest again.  If this does occur you can have a enchanter charm him and you will then be able to turn in your item.)
