EQDruids Guide to Dungeon Keys

 

Several of the high level dungeons require you to have a key to enter.  These keys are all found through various quests.  Below are listed the dungeon that the key lets you enter and the quest to receive the key!

 

(Quick Jump to Dungeon: Howling Stones - Skyshrine - Sleeper's Tomb - Veeshan's Peak - Grieg's End - Ssra Temple - Vex Thal - Plane of Air)

 

Old Sebilis Key

 

In return for the Medallions of Kunzar and Nathsar, Emperor Ganak will award you the Idol of Trakanon, which is the key needed to enter Sebilis.

The medallions of Kunzar and Nathsar drop from the Froglock Hunter and Forager in Trakanon's Teeth. These Mobs spawn one after another, so they are fairly easy to get.   Be careful they are undercons.  Make sure to keep them snared, and at a distance if you are going to solo them.

The medallions that are dropped by the Froglocks are no-drop and lore.  So anyone in your party will have to grab one of each.  Doesn’t take too long, the hunter/foragers are very common.

The idol is now soul bound and will stay with you even after you die.

 

Once you have both parts of the medallions, you now need to turn them in to Emperor Ganak.  You will need someone that can cast invisibility to undead with you to do this part.

 

If you zone in from Emerald jungle and follow the wall to right, go all the way south till you find his temple at approximate loc neg 2700, pos 2800.  Now follow down the ramp and you should see him at the bottom.

 

Turn in both pieces to the Emperor and you will receive the Idol of Trakanon.  This is your key into Old Sebilis.  It is now a soul bound key, so if you die you will keep the key in your inventory.

 

Howling Stones (Charasis)

 

The key to Howling Stones is also in two parts.

 

One part is found in Kaesora off the Field of Bone.  Down in the area by the church you will find a spawn named Xalgoz.  He is a twenty minute spawn and has a Guardian of Xalgoz as his placeholder.

 

Kill him, and loot the xalgozian fang (he normally drops two at a time).

 

Now travel to Lake of Ill Omen.  In the Sarnak fortress you need to find Chancellor of Di’Zok.  He is in the back of the fortress.


Kill him and loot the Jade Chodakai prod.

 

Now take the Jade Chodakai prod and the xalgozian fang to the Swamp of No Hope.  In Swamp of No hope you are looking for an NPC named Durgoz (a Froglock).  You can find him near the froglok town at -267, -1772.

 

 

Skyshrine Key

 

To go through Skyshrine into Cobalt Scar you must have the Skyshrine Key.

 

To receive the Skyshrine key you have two options.

 

Option one is to do a quest involving Kael Drakkal.

 

Talk to Ziglak (he is a named Drake that is to the right as you enter Skyshrine from Wakening Lands).  He will tell you that he has a spy in Kael Drakkal and he needs you to deliver his payment of 200 plat and a note to their spy (you have to come up with the 200 plat).

 

The spy is near the nobles in Kael Drakkal.  You give him 200 plat and the note you got from Ziglak and he will give you back the information Ziglak wants.


Return this information to Ziglak for the no drop, lore, soul bound key to Cobalt scar.

 

You must be dubious or higher with Claws of Veeshan to complete the quest.

Option two is even simpler.  If you are not worried about your Skyshrine faction, just kill Ziglak.  He drops the very same key.


This key acts like a Group gate, if one person in a group uses the key all group members near him are zoned into Cobalt scar.

 

Sleepers Tomb

 

The dragon Zlandicar has the key for access to Jaled Dar's Shade (Veeshans Peak), you can perform a small task for him in Necropolis in return for the key. You can also kill him to get the key.

Jaled Dar's Shade wishes you to wake the Sleeper, who is encased within his tomb. He needs a talisman from any of 5 different dragons of the first brood in order to open the door.

These Dragons are Lord Yelinak, Sontalak, Lendiniara, Klandicar and Zlandicar himself.

These are all HIGH-level encounters.  You will not be able to do these solo, and you will not be able to do this without high level support.

 

Veeshan’s Peak

 

To receive the key for Veeshan’s Peak you need to complete 3 different medallions and also receive a tooth from Trakanon (not the Trak’s tooth from Trakanon Teeth zone, the one off the dragon Trakanon).

 

We’ll start with the Medallion of Jarsath.

 

First go to the Swamp of No Hope.  Go to location 52, 2935.  One the ground will spawn a bag.  If it is not there, wait for the spawn.  When bag appears, pick it up and you have the first piece of the Medallion of Jarsath.

 

Now head to Firiona Vie.  Find the Ancient Jarsath.  He is low 40’s and shouldn’t give you to many problems.  If he isn’t up his placeholder is the Drolvangs that wander the area.

 

He is an undead creature, so someone with sense undead to help track this creature comes in handy.

 

When you kill him you will have your second part of the Medallion of Jarsath.

 

The third and final piece can be found on a Bloodgill Marauder in the Lake of Ill Omen. He spawns at the entrance to the underwater ruins in the middle of the lake (under water). He is lvl 40. His placeholders are the Bloodgill goblins.  Just keep killing them until he spawns, and loot the last piece of the Medallion.


Now you travel to Timorous Deep.  Go to the chessboard island and find Xiblin.  Hand him the three pieces and he will make you the Medallion of Jarsath.

 

Next is the Medallion of the Kylong

 

You now go to Chardok and deep inside find Niblik.  Hand Niblik a ruby and a black sapphire and she will give you the first part of the Medallion of Kylong back.


The second piece can be found lying on the ground upstairs in the library of Kaesora. Just pick it up and you will have your second piece of the Medallion.

 

The third and final piece can be found on an NPC by the name of Verix Kylox who spawns in Karnor's Castle. Be sure to bring a decent group for this one also.  Kill Verix and loot the third piece of the Medallion.

 

Now that you have the three pieces head to Lake of Ill Omen and find Professor Akabao (He’s a Erudite in a tower).  Give him the three pieces of the medallion and he will create the Medallion of the Kylong for you.

To obtain the Medallion of Obulus:

First you need to either buy or loot a Burnished Stave which drops of random mobs in Chardok.

 

Once you obtain the Stave take this to Warslik's Woods. Search out an NPC named Sselot. He is on a small island north of the zone. He is an Iksar so you may be KoS.  If you are KOS to him, have an enchanter or a bard charm him (mez won’t work).  While he is charmed give him the Stave and in return he gives you a piece of the Medallion of Obulus.


Second you must kill an NPC by the name of Pained Soul in Trakanon's Teeth. He is a fixed spawn and spawns near the ramp to Old Sebilis. He spawns in the doorway to a broken building. His placeholder is a Spectral Champion. Pained Soul is a VERY rare spawn.

 

Once he has spawned, kill him and loot the second piece of the Medallion of Obulus.


Finally head to Dreadlands and find the Rotted Skeleton. He is a static spawn so he doesn’t wander.

 

As you come in from the Frontier Mountains/Dreadlands zone take a left and continue along the zone line into the mountains.

 

His place holder is a skeleton, so keep killing him until you spawn the Rotted Skeleton.  Kill the Rotted Skeleton and loot the last piece of the Medallion of Obulus.


Now head to Burning Woods and find an NPC named Slixen Klex. His location is -681.59 -1120.76. He is lvl 50 and is indifferent to both good and evil races. Give him the three pieces of the medallion and he pieces them together to form the Medallion of Obulus.

Now you must kill Trakanon (the dragon).

 

Trakanon is one of the hardest dragons in the game.  You will need lots of friends to take him down.

 

You want the no drop tooth off of Trakanon.  This is the last part of the key.

 

Now that you have all the pieces of the key, you return to Emperor Ganak (same guy as Old Sebilis key).  Give him the three complete medallions and the tooth and he will give you in return the key to Veeshan’s Peak.

 

Grieg's End

 

To obtain the key to the servitor and Grieg in Griegs end:

Kill Prast Inaitor in Grieg's end, he drops a NO RENT key which will open the area to the servitor and to Grieg himself. Keep in mind this key is NO RENT, so make a key corpse or kill Prast every time you want to kill Grieg.
 

Ssra Temple

 

Obtain the following to make the key to the emperor in Ssra Temple:

Idol & Ring of commanders in Ssra Temple (random spawn on first and second floors, any snake can be PH's). Get a taskmaster pouch off the basement taskmasters, then go to the third floor and farm until you get a Ssraezian (spelling?) insignia. Combine the idol, ring and insignia inside the taskmasters pouch, you receive:

Ring of the Shissar
ac 8, wis 4, int 4, sta 4, hp 80, mana 65. All/All. Wearable as a ring, even though it's the key to enter the emperor area.

 

Vex Thal

 

To make the Key to Vex Thal:

1. Gather 10 different shards from all over Luclin.
2. Obtain a Scepter Frame and combine the 10 shards to reassemble the Scepter. This Scepter is now a 2 slot container.
3. Kill the Emperor of ssra and looting his staff. This Staff has 2 charges of Summon a Shaded Sphere. *one charge - 20 sources, so each staff produces 40 power sources.*
4. The fourth part is the re-energizing of the Scepter by combining the Orb of Luclinite and the summoned Shaded Sphere (power source) in the reassembled Scepter.
5. Use key to enter Vex Thal!

The Shards can be found in the following, you'll need one of each:

1. SSRA --- Basement mines. Can be off any skeletons but high drop rate on skeleton drudges, which are in the back half of the mines. (uncommon)
2. Sanctus Seru --- all the legionnaires etc. At zone is good for solo, go in a bit and go up via telepad to kill around the ring wall is good for group. (rare)
3. Fungus Grove --- Warriors/Royal (lvl 60 only) (rare)
4. Dawnshroud --- Rockhopper/Sambata cave, either can drop (common)
5. The Grey --- Named Rockfiend, Sun Revs at NE corner around lake. (uncommon)
6. Scarlet Desert --- Sun Rev/Reavers (common)
7. Akheva --- Shaded Rock, Roudy Ruble, Xi Vius, Xi Xiau, Shadow Reaver etc. Either camp Va Dyn room or IV prep room. (uncommon)
8. The Deep --- Any though horrors. Best drop rate is pass the invis bridge near THO area before Elder Thought Horrors. (uncommon)
9. Acrylia --- 2 Skullcracker/Runed at the city gates. So far only seen shard drop from those 2 spawns. (uncommon)
10. Maiden's Eye --- Goranga Prophet, Battlemaster, Seer, Forager, Savant. Not 100% drop. PH for the goranga named are Goranga Scouts. (very rare) This is the bottle neck shard.

After you have your shards, get a glowing orb of luclinite (off almost any high level raid mob in Luclin, rhaq1 &2, Grieg's, tho ect). Then get your scepter made...

Head to Akheva. From the zone in, run straight, past the fallen pillar. When you get to the next room, head right. Going left will take you to Va'Dyn. Go down the next set of stairs. To your right is IV. Go left, you will see a large "gate" with two "shades" standing on the left and right edges of this gate. Clear them out. Then drop down from the stairs and you will see a number of green con "Sacrificed Remains" and a "Shimmering Presence."

From there you will need items from a mage and a Necro to continue the script.

Plane of Air

 

Faerie Island - Starting island. Kill Faeries - Drop a Tessera, 3 swords, 3 Rabbits Feet each. Kill all the Faeries to get the swords and feet and then kill the princess to spawn Sirran the Lunatic. Hand swords and feet to Sirran the Lunatic for Key of Swords and Key of  the Misplaced. Talking to Sirran other than hailing is a bad idea.

 

From here you can use the Key of Swords to go to Noble Dojorn (Island 2) or Azaracks (Island 3)

 

Island 3.  To get the key from Island 3 for Island 4 you need to kill Azaracks.  They will drop broken mirrors.  This is what you will turn in for keys.

 

Once you have the broken mirrors you will need to kill the Protector of Sky (lvl 55).  When he is dead, Sirran the Lunatic will spawn and you can turn in the mirrors for keys (key of misfortune).

 

On island 4 you need to collect Animal Figurines.  These will drop off any of the mobs on Island 4.  Kill the Gorgalosk to get Sirran to spawn again, turn in your figurines and you have the key to island 5 (Key of the Beasts)

 

On island 5 you need to collect Bird Whistles off the different mobs.  To get the boss mob to spawn you need to kill the harvesters.  Killing the harvesters will spawn a mob named Ekim Nottap.  In about one hour Ekim will despawn and the Keeper of the Souls will spawn.    The Keeper will stay up for aprox 80 mins.  Kill the Keeper and up Sirran pops.  Give him the bird whistles and you will receive the key to island 6.  The Keeper also drops 4 of the total 24 Bird Whistles that drop on this island.

 

On island 6 you need to collect Noise Makers.  The spirocs will drop them when killed.  You need to kill the Spiroc Lord to cause Sirran to spawn.

 

Island 7 you are looking to loot Dull Dragon Scales off the Bzzazzt.  Once you have killed Bzzazzt Zzzt (the boss) Sirran again will spawn.  Hand him the Dull Dragon Scales to receive the key to the 8th island.  Before you go, have someone say "?llaw eht htiw eno I ma"  This will spawn the boss mob on island 8.

 

Island 8 you need to kill the drakes.  They will drop Replica of the Wurm Queen.  Kill the Sister of the Spire and Sirran will appear again.  Turn in the Replica of the Wurm Queen to him, and you will have the final key to island 9.
