1. Find the Black Reavers in City of Mist.  The Jade Reaver is a semi-rare drop off of the Black Reaves.  

A Black reaver ranges from 50 - 53 and has almost 100% Magic Resistance.  Once killed another Black Reaver will spawn on its corpse and will be a little tougher than the prior.  Although there are only five reavers in the zone and they are on a 2 hour timer, you have more than five chances for a reaver due to this respawning.  

They spawn behind a door that must be picked by a rogue of 51 or higher.  You can pull them to the archway with a group of six if done correctly.  Best group I have used was an Enchanter, Cleric, Rogue, Monk, Warrior, Warrior.  All 52+ (And the druid stands alone...LOL)  Black Reavers will drop this item:

(Often at the end of the Black Reaver spawns they will spawn a ghostlike creature of some kind.  There are several that spawn but there is one in particular that you should avoid if you are using one group.  Rak Ashiir is a floating spectre that can Complete Heal and 2K Rain Damage so if he spawns stay away.  He will be indifferent to you and even allow you to keep killing all other things in the zone.  Just make sure nobody hits him with anything.  I have done CoM several times while leaving Rak Ashiir standing next to us.  For more detailed info on how to kill Rak Ashiir please visit my friends at everquest.allakhazam.com)
