Druid Foraging

Foraging Table

Druids excel at foraging. Of all the classes, only Rangers surpass us in this ability.

Only three classes receive this ability: Rangers (level 3), Druids (level 5), and Bards (level 12). Wood elves and the Iksar have the innate ability to forage, so they receive this skill at level 1.

The ability begins at a skill level of 50, and only Rangers and Druids can advance in skill level.

There are an amazing number of things that you will forage as you explore each zone. Recently Verant has reduced the skill level to forage some of the rarer items, so don't be surprised what you find.

Below is a list of the items that are known to be foraged in the game. Items will continue to be added as they are found.
