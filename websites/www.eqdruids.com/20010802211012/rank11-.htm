Resist Magic : The top and final resist buff.  This adds to the all important magic resist.

Skin Like Nature : The final Skin spell - this is better than the level 44 Cleric spells as far as buffs go. +250 hps + 16 AC - this also has a slight regen effect of 2 points per tick

Engulfing Roots : this does more than CL (160 at level 50 damage) and should be used in its place in hot place...other than that its a long root spell

Fire : Column Fire - AE 392 damage - fire based  - 50 cold resis

Ice : DD Column - indoor / outdoor - 612 damage - reduces fire resist - will stack with malaisement for -90 fire resis

Lightning Blast - 477 OD only DD

Shield of Thorns - the pimp tool - 24 damage shield for a measly 120 mana....make those guild members happy!

Thorncoat - Personal Shield and last in the line - keep it up at all times for AC +32 and 5 points reflect damage.

Starfire - 612 damage DD - fire based - when in SolB use ICE

Pack Chloroplast - Manastone junkies unite! this is a group spell that you will be casting often - last for 15 minutes and is 10 points a tick.  

Succor: North - Escape from those nasty places  - this spell sucks as all evils will get tore apart by the locals
