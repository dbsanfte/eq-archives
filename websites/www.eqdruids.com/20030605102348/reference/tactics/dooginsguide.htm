Doogin Hobblefoot’s Free Leveling Guide (05-60)

Ok here is my list of mobs you can kill from your newbie levels around 5 to the end game level 60. The guide is lacking in some areas but can be added to if needed. I tried to keep level ranges for blue mobs and above even though light blue do give some exp. I also left out most dungeons since I rarely fought in them while leveling myself. Hopefully this will be helpful to those playing the game.
