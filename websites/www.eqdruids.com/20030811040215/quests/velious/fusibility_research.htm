Western Wastes

  

 

This quest starts in Western Wastes and is given out by Markil Rargon. When Markil is hailed he will talk about the fusibility of elements and ask you to bring him 4 items to combine in a box he gives you. After you combine the 4 items and hand the combined box back to him he will give you the Strength
of the Elements. The 4 items that are needed are:

  • Fusible Coral Ore - Off Phinigel Autropos in Kedge Keep

  • Fusible Earthen Ore - From Master Yael in the Hole

  • Fusible Igneous Ore - Ground spawn by the large volcano in Sky Fire mountains around 900, -1500

  • Fusible Velium Ore - Off a large air elemental named Guardian Kozzalym in Western Wastes.
