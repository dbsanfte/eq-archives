The first meeting of the Rathe Druid Council went very well. We have screen
shots available. This organization is being developed to set standards of
behavior for Druids and standards of expectations for everyone, as well as
to add strength to the class through unity.
Some of the things we accomplished:
We have started a list of people whom members of the Druid Council are asked
to NEVER help as well as a list of "Good" people too.
We set standards of cost of Druid powers and
We discussed the Council's reaction to future changes to the class.
