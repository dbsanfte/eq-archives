Cloak of Jaggedpine (Surefall)

 

Properties                            MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                            Armor, Back

Armor Class                          6

Boosts                                 +3 DEX

Effect                                   None

Weight & Size                      WT: 1.5  Size: ?

Class                                    RNG, DRU

Race                                    HUM, ELF, HEF, HFL

 

If you talk to Larsk Juton in Surefall Glade he will ask you to retrieve three Blackburrow Casks. These casks can be found off Gnoll Brewers and Master Brewers in Blackburrow. Return these casks to Larsk and you will receive the Cloak of Jaggedpine.
