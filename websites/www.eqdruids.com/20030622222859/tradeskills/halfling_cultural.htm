Cultural Halfling Armors Components

Acorn Oil - flask water, Misty Acorn (Misty Acorns can be foraged in Misty Thicket)

Steel Boning - small brick ore, file, flask water

Infused Vale Boning - small brick high quality ore, file, celestial temper

  • Celestial Temper - blue diamond dust + purified water

  • Blue Diamond dust - blue diamond combined with acid in a kiln.

  • Purified water - 2 water flasks + celestial essence

  • Celestial essence - celestial solvent + high level rune/word/page in a mortar and pestle

Metal Bits - 2 small pieces ore, flask water

Metal Studs - 3 metal bits, file, flask water

Vale Sewing Kit - high quality metal bits, needle mold, thimble mold, flask water

  • High quality pieces of ore - small brick of HQ ore, smithing chisel

  • High quality bits - 2 small pieces of high quality ore, flask of water

Embroidering Needle - needle mold, metal bits, celestial essence, water
