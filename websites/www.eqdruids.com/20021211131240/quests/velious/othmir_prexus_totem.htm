Othmir Prexus Totem

 

Hail Bloogy Shellcracker and he says 'Hello, strange one. I am Bloogy Shellcracker, the preparer of meals for my people. I am not familiar with the culinary tastes of your kind but you are welcome to purchase some of my supplies. I will also barter for ingredients to some of my exotic dishes.

You say, 'what ingredients'

Bloogy Shellcracker says 'The rarest treat I can prepare for my people is saucy salted seadragon steak. I wish to barter for some water dragon or sea dragon meat, saltwater seaweed, and fish eggs. The rest of the ingredients are collected locally by our collectors.

- You need one Sea Dragon Meat. It can drop off either kelorek'dar (Faydedar clone in Cobalt Scar ocean) or Faydedar in Timorous Deep (not the spawned Fay, the "real" Fay).

- Saltwater seaweed is fished in the Ocean of Tears. You need two of these.

- Fish eggs are made by combining Thunder Salmon (fished in the Karanas) and a skinning knife (the one that player-smiths make for tailors), combined in a tacklebox (check the Trade Supplies section of the website for merchant listings for the tacklebox). This yields two fish eggs.

Give him two fish eggs, two seaweed and the seadragon meat for your reward.
