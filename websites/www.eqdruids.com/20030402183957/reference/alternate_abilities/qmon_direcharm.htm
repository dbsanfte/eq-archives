Qmon’s Dire Charm Guide

 Prepared by Elder Qmon of Brell Serilis – Last Updated March 20, 2003

 This guide lists the known “higher level” animals that can be Dire Charmed (DC) and is updated from time-to-time.  This is not a list of all animals that can be charmed, only those animals that can be Dire Charmed.  This list does not include low level animals since most Druids are not going to DC them. 

Druid DC is an AA ability and only works on animals, level 46 and below.  Some animals within the same species are above, equal to or bellow level 46.  So there is a chance you could attempt to DC an animal listed below and it will fail.  If that happens, you will agro the animal but the 72 minute recast timer will be set to zero so you can try again on another animal.  You might have to hunt around to find an animal of that species that is level 46 or below.  I once made over 20 attempts to DC a brontotherium “rhino” in Western Wastes before I finally found one that worked so don’t get discouraged.

If you have personally Dire Charmed an animal that is not on this list, please send me your character and server name (so I can give you credit), along with the zone name and actual in-game animal name (don’t say something like rhinos in WW.)  Send your updates to dire%charm@eqdruids.com, leave out the % in the email address, its just there to mess up the stinking spam bot scanners searching for email addresses.  Death to spam!!!

Finally, the name in parenthesis after the animal name is the name of the person who submitted and tested DC on that animal.  Thank you to everyone for helping to build this list.
