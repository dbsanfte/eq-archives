Welcome To Kelethin, the Elven city!

Here is the EQ Atlas map of the city of Kelethin and of Greater Faydark.

The city itself is not a separate zone, but rather located centrally within Greater Faydark.

The marked areas are the druid guild, your starting point, the elevators, the bank, and your initial bind point.

Descriptions of the other buildings within Kelethin can be found on the EQ Atlas map, here.
Descriptions of Greater Faydark can be found here.

(Click on picture for larger view)

You begin your adventures in the back of the druid guild (loc -698.00, 224.00, 77.06).

(Click on picture for larger view)

Open up your inventory, and read your guild note (right-click on it).

(Click on picture for larger view)

Go through the doors into the main hall of the Soldiers of Tunare, and locate the Heartwood Master.

(Click on picture for larger view)

Turn in your guild note ("a tattered note") to the Heartwood Master, and he will provide you with a starting tunic.

(Click on picture for larger view)

Equip your tunic, and exit the building.

(Click on picture for larger view)

Using the map of Kelethin, make your way to the nearest elevator. The easiest route is to use the following points on the map:

  1. start at 21
  2. turn left and go to 16
  3. turn slightly right and go to 13
  4. turn right and go to 14
  5. go straight to 15, then turn left to get to the elevator *OR*
  6. turn left and go to 11, then turn right to get to the elevator

The ramps are not always visible, so use your PageUp and PageDown keys to adjust your view. Use caution!

(Click on picture for larger view)

Once you get to the elevator, get on it, and left-click on the spot marked in the image below to activate it.

(Click on picture for larger view)

Make your way down and get off. You are now in the area known as Greater Faydark.

(Click on picture for larger view)

Begin hunting the creatures present in Greater Faydark.

Should you die, you will re-enter the zone at your initial bind point (see map of Greater Faydark above).

When you do re-enter the zone, your view will be similar to the image below.

(Click on picture for larger view)

It is not too far from the guards. Simply turn a little to the left, and you will be able to see the elevators and the main city.

(Click on picture for larger view)

Good luck, and happy hunting!
