Summoned Mobs List

The number of mobs that can be affected by the Summoned and Charm line of spells is bigger than most people think.

The majority of this list was compiled from information on Illia's Beastiary.

If you see anything that needs to be corrected or added, or have any suggestions, please contact me at Tesiux@eqdruids.com, or Spiritual at Spiritual@eqdruids.com.
If you are submitting information about a mob, please include the exact name of the mob, along with the zone and mob type.
