Level One Spells

Skin Like Wood: 

  • Abjuration, 

  • Mana cost 10 

  • Effect:  Add 3 AC and 11 HP

This is an excellent buff spell. It improves slightly as your skill increases. Its effect is to add about 3 to your overall Armor Class, and 10 + (1/your level) hps to your hit point total. A must have spell. It is eventually replaced by Skin Like Rock.  Always keep this up to buff yourself and your party with it.  

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Burst of Flame:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 7
  • Effect:  Direct Damage (DD) for between 3-5 damage

A minor burn spell. It costs 7 mana to cast and only does 3-5 damage, but it’s similar to the other classes first burn spell. Comes in as a useful supplement to your melee attack. You have to time it so you begin casting immediately after the monster attacks to improve your chance of not being interrupted. A must have spell at this level.  This spell is great as a lure spell. It doesn't do much damage but it sure beats chasing down things.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Minor Healing:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 10 
  • Effect:  Heal 10 hitpoints

Again, this spell is pretty much the same as other 1st rank healing spells. It does a fine job for the first five levels or so, but after that it becomes less useful to heal people in combat, because the monsters can dish out so much more damage than this spell can heal. Unfortunately Druids don’t get anything better until 9th level. Its still useful from 5th-8th as an after combat heal spell, although you have to cast it many many times. Once you get Light Healing you won’t use this spell much. This spell heals 10 pts of dmg.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Flame Lick:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 10 
  • Effect:  Reduces AC of mob by 4 (-4 AC) and for 10 rounds will damage mob at one hp per round (DOT and Debuff)

A very minor DOT (Damage Over Time) spell. It doesn't do much damage (10 points), but its most beneficial effect is to reduce the AC of the target by a few points (-4), which can make a difference at the lower levels. It requires a fire beetle eye to cast, but the fire beetle eye is NOT consumed after casting. It is best to use it as an "opener" to draw the monster to you, so it's AC has been lowered before you engage it in melee. Another benefit of this spell is the target is prevented from regenerating hps during battle in case you happen to miss a lot (For low level mobs under 10). In addition to this, the lower a target's ac the higher chance you have of hitting for max dmg more often.

A quirk of this spell is, that for Woodelf druids it is almost unusable due to the fact that nothing drops fire beetle eyes anywhere near Greater Faydark.  This has been addressed to Verant, but no changes have been made at this time.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Snare:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 15
  • Effect:  Lowers movement rate of mob.  Movement is reduced 41% at level 1, and increases to 55% at level 15

This is a very good spell. . In effect it halves the speed of an adversary and it only costs 15 mana. Once the monster is ensnared you do the "Run, turn and burn" routine until it is dead or injured enough to take out with melee. This spell is also an excellent "finisher" against monsters that have a tendency to run when they get low on hit points. Rather than use an expensive burn spell to kill them, cast snare. Once the monster is wounded it slows down to a creeping pace, and once it is damaged to 1/4 health it stops moving altogether. A beneficial side effect of this is even though the monster is no longer moving it is still in "flee" mode and will not attack you. You can finish it off with melee attacks at your leisure.  Always have this ready - costs little and should be a prime job of the druid in groups and solo.  Snare monsters BEFORE they start to run.

This is a must have spell in your spell book.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Endure Fire:  

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 20
  • Effect:  Adds to your Resist Fire (up to +20)

This is another long lasting buff spell, so feel free to cast it during your "buff" phase prior to going adventuring. It only costs 20 mana so its pretty reasonable for as long as it lasts. This spell is very hand for going up against casters that attack with fire.  It will not make you safe against fire, but will reduce the amount of damage you will take from an attack.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Lull Animal:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 10
  • Effect:  Lowers Animal Frenzy and Reaction

Lull Animal does NOT work in combat. It is essential to cast it BEFORE combat, which reduces its effectiveness because you have to guess which animals are going to potentially gang up on you before you start combat. 

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Panic Animal:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 10
  • Effect:  Fears the animal, so it will run away

Same mana cost as Lull Animal and you can cast it in combat. It makes the rats run away, which is good. I kept this one memorized instead of Lull Animal.  Remember they come back eventually and attack.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Sense Animals:  

  • Divination
  • Mana Cost 5
  • Effect:  Points your body in the direction of nearest animal, and targets that animal.

When cast it spins your body around and points you in the direction of the nearest animal, and targets that animal as well. Unfortunately its not like tracking where you get to pick from a list of potential targets, it automatically selects the nearest one.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Dance of Fireflies:

  • Conjuration
  • Mana Cost 10
  • Effect:  Conjures a ball of light with that gives light equal to a lesser lightstone

This spell creates a globe of light that you can place in an inventory slot. It works well if you do not have Infravision.  Night Only casting

Purchase at:  Druid Guild
