Many different playing cards drop in the Plane of Mischief. There are four different types of cards that drops, the Throne Cards, the Crown Cards, the Knight Cards and the Squire Cards. Collect the following cards and combine them in the Deck of Spontaneous Generator to create the Green Flower of Functionality.

·  White Throne

·  Red Throne

·  Red Knight

·  Red Crown


The Throne cards are usually found in the Alice area of the zone and drop off Big and 22. Knight cards are dropped by the four halflings in the forest near the chest and on mid level npc's like Gorillas, Guards and 86. The crown cards are dropped by 86, Fenj and My Hand. The squire cards are dropped by mobs found in the forest area.
