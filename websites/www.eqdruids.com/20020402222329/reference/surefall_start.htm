Welcome To Surefall Glades, the home of the Jaggedpine Treefolk!

Here is the EQ Atlas map of the city of Surefall Glades and of Qeynos Hills.

The marked areas are the druid guild, your starting point, and your initial bind point.

Descriptions of the other buildings within Surefall Glades can be found on the EQ Atlas map, here.
Descriptions of Qeynos Hills can be found here.

(Click on picture to see larger)

You begin your adventures in front of the druid guild (loc -207.00, -301.00, 3.31), facing the Jaggedpine Treefolk building.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Open up your inventory, and read your guild note (right-click on it).

(Click on picture to see larger)

Turn to the right, and you will see the druid guild building.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Go through the entrance into the main hall, and locate Te'Anara.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Turn in your guild note ("a tattered note") to Te'Anara, and she will provide you with a starting tunic.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Equip your tunic, and exit the building.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Using the map of Surefall, make your way to the zone to Qeynos Hills.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Go through the tunnel, and enter Qeynos Hills.

Exit the tunnel, and you will see two guards guarding the entrance, and a small campfire where Chandra and Baobob Miller are resting.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Begin hunting the creatures in Qeynos Hills.

Should you die, you will re-enter the zone at your initial bind point (see map of Qeynos Hills above).

When you do re-enter the zone, your view will be similar to the image below.

(Click on picture to see larger)

It is not too far from the guards and the Surefall Glades tunnel. Simply turn a little to the left, and you will be able to see the tunnel, guards, and The Millers.

(Click on picture to see larger)

Good luck, and happy hunting!
