1. Find Ulump Pujik in Swamp of No Hope.  He can be located near the froglok village at location Neg 100  Neg 1770.  He is a level 55 warrior with almost 100% Magic Resistance.  Lull will still land but every other spell I have seen will not.  Two good groups can do this very easy.  2 Clerics and 10 tanks.  He double attacks for 250DMG and has 30k Hitpoints so it will take a bit to kill him.  He has also been known to run at 30 % health.  Kill him and loot this item:

2. Find Corrupted Gorilla in Emerald Jungle.  He can be located near the Northwest corner of the zone and has tottering gorillas as placeholders.  Tainted Gorillas will spawn the corrupted gorilla but only the live ones.  You will know because the undead tainted gorillas look nasty.  The live one will bring you the corrupted.  Kill him and loot this:

3. Find the Corrupted Barracuda in Lake of Ill Omen.  He has one Deepwater barracuda as a placeholder at Neg 900 Neg 900.  I found that the easiest way to do him is by fearkiting him.  Many tainted will spawn but only the Blue colored tainted will spawn the corrupted barracuda.  None of the fish can be pulled out of the water except the blue tainted.  If you pull one out then you have the tainted that will spawn the corrupted.  They are very easy to kill once pulled on land.  He is on a 6 minute timer.  Kill him and loot this:

4. Find Dolgin Codslayer in Timorous Deep.  He is a naked dwarf in the hidden oasis at location Neg 11300 Neg 1400.  Hand him your Froglok Essence to spawn the mighty Dragon Faydedar.  Use standard Nagafen dragon tactics for fighting him.  Set up your groups on the inner most circle of the hidden oasis.  When you hand in your item the naked dwarf will despawn and give you your Froglok Essence back.  The dragon should immediately come out of the water and your groups should be there waiting.

Kill Faydedar and you will receive this:
