Poacher's Head

 

Talk to the first ranger you see when you go into the ranger guild house.

 

It turns out that there is some poacher hunting the animals of Surefall Glade and he wants you to end this dastardly deed and poach the poacher instead.

 

Bring his head back as proof that the animals are now safe and you will get experience, faction and money or an item.

 

You get the following faction changes: Protectors of Pine, Jagged Pine Treefolk and Guards of Qeynos got better and Sabertooths of Blackburrow get worse.

 

Any poacher head seems to work here, including those you get in Tox forest. However, if you kill the Gnoll Poacher and turn in his head, I am told that you will get a rough elm recurve bow.
