Gauntlets of Iron Tactics (Kael)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                              Armor, Hands

Armor Class                           2

Boosts                                   +7 STR, +7 DEX, -5 CHA, +7 WIS

Effect                                     None

Weight & Size                        WT: 1.0  Size: SMALL

Class                                      CLR, DRU, SHM

Race    ALL

Talk to Gkrean Prophet of Tallon who can be found in Kael Drakkal. He will tell you about the temple of tactics and he will ask you if you will serve the temple. He will tell you go out and retrieve the different books of tactics that the Coldain use and return them to him. There are various rewards to this quest, depending on the book you hand him. The book you want to locate for this quest is the Brell's Divine Strategy. Hand it in to Gkrean and your reward will be the Gauntlets of Iron Tactics
