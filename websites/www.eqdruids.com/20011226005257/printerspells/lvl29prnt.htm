Level Twenty Nine Spells

Greater Healing: 

  • Alteration 

  • Mana cost 150 

  • Effect:  Healing Spell

This is the last upgrade to your healing spells that you will receive in a while, so enjoy this one while it is handy!

This spell will heal up to 298 hps per cast.  At lvl 29 this is fairly decent, when you are in your upper 40's this spell doesn't do much when healing tanks.

This is where the cleric spells start to pull ahead of hybrid healing spells.  You are still useful as a healer, but prob not with tanks.

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Counteract Disease:  

  • Alteration 

  • Mana cost 50  

  • Effect:  Upgraded Cure Disease

This is an upgrade to cure disease.  This spell will cure the higher lvl diseases and won't take as many casts.

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Counteract Poison:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 50
  • Effect:  Upgraded Cure Poison

This is an upgrade to cure poison.  The spell will cure the higher lvl poisons and won't take as many casts.

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Extinguish Fatigue:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 35 
  • Effect:  Upgrade for removing fatigue

This is an upgrade to your fatigue removing spells.  This spell will remove more fatigue, and do it faster than the previous spells.

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Immolate:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 120 
  • Effect:  AC reduction and DOT 

This is a nice spell to use.  When cast it reduces your targets AC by 10 and does a Damage over Time of approximately 250 in 60 seconds.

These spells have one drawback however, they tend to cause major agro on yourself.  So make sure to keep that in mind when casting.

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Combust:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 100
  • Effect:  Direct Damage

Upgrade to your offensive spells.  This spell will do between 171 and 182 hps of fire damage.

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Shield of Brambles:  

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 80
  • Effect:  Upgraded Damage Shield

This is another upgrade to your damage shield spells.  This spell will do up to 12 hps damage each time someone hits the protected. 

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Bramblecoat:  

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 75
  • Effect:  Upgraded Self Damage Shield

This is your upgraded self damage shield spell.  It now increases your AC by 17 and will hit an attacker for 3 hps each time you are hit by the attacker.

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Ensare:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 35
  • Effect:  Upgraded Snare

This is an upgrade to your Snaring spells.  This one will last longer than previous, and be resisted less.

Purchase From:  Steamfont Druid Ring

Scale of Wolf:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 60
  • Effect:  Faster Sow

This is an upgrade to SoW in a way.  It is faster than SoW by about 2 times.  But it has limitations.  It can only be cast on yourself, or someone in your group (it overwrites sow).

And if you are attacked, or cast a spell, the Scale is remove (same rules as camo).

So when you need to run a long distance, use this spell.  But it is not for everyday use.

Purchase From:  Firona Vie Druid Vendor

Imbue Emerald:

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 200
  • Effect:  Imbues an Emerald

This spell imbues an emerald with the essence of Tunare.  Used by smiths and jewel crafting for Tunarian based skills.

I've also noticed, that if I imbue an emerald it will sell for 10-15% more at a merchant.

Purchase From:  Felwithe Cleric Guild

Imbue Plains Pebble:

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 200
  • Effect:  Imbues a Plains Pebble

This spell imbues a plains pebble (foraged in the Karanas) with the essence of Karana.

Used in Karana based smithing and jewel craft.

Purchase From:  North Karana Druid Circle

Ring of Misty:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 150
  • Effect:  Self only teleport

This is a great spell!  Takes you to Misty Thicket, which is only one zone to a bank!  

Very handy when you are in need of banking, and don't want to run far.

Purchase From:  West Commons Druid Circle

Circle of Karana, Commons, Toxxulia and Butcher:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Group Teleports

Its finally here, the ability to teleport groups!  You're life will never be the same again!

Circle of Karana takes you to North Karana Circle

Circle of Commons takes you to West Commons Circle

Circle of Toxxulia takes you to Tox forest Circle

Circle of Butcher takes you to Butcherblock Circle

Let the taxi services begin!

Purchase From:  Toxxulia Forest Druid Circle

Succor: East:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Evacuation Teleport

This is your first Succor spell.  They work a little different than your normal teleports.  The succor spell will take you and your party to the appropriate druid circle.

The difference is, the cast time is half the normal group teleport, but you can leave some of your party behind.  So don't use these for normal teleporting, only in emergency situations.

The above Succor will take you to East Karana.

Purchase From:  Toxxulia Druid Circle
