Talk to Miranda who lives n the Surefall Glade, she will tell you about her problem. Her brother, Ronn Castekin, is missing and she needs you to find him. Ronn's skeletal remains can be found wandering the Catacombs of Qeynos, near the North Wall entrance. He is a very rare spawn, kill him and bring the news to Miranda to complete this quest. You may receive either the Ring of Pine or the Band of Tunare as your reward.
