Engraved Bone Pauldrons (Kael)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                             Armor, Shoulders

Armor Class                          12

Boosts                                  +3 DEX, -20 CHA, +5 AGI

Effect                                   None

Weight & Size                       WT: 2.5  Size: ?

Class                                     ALL

Race                                     ALL

Talk to Wenglawks Kkeak who can be found within Kael Drakkal. He will ask you if you are a mercenary, to which you respond yes. Tell him you are looking for work and he will ask you if you could deliver some packages for him. He will then hand you a note and tell you to take it to Kellek, who can be found in the next room. Hand the note to Kellek and he will in turn hand you a Toolset which he says must be given to Sojon. Sojon can be found in the Iceclad Ocean near the gnomish pirate camp. He will hand you back a receipt once you give him his toolset. Return to Wenglawks with the receipt and he will reward you with the Engraved Bone Pauldrons.
