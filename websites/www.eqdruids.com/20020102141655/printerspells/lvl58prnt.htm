Level Fifty Eight Spells

Fist of Karana: 

  • Evocation 

  • Mana cost 357 

  • Effect:  Area Effect Direct Damage

This spell does 812 hps of damage as an Area Effect.  Again, the mobs must be very close to each other for this spell to affect them all.

Outdoors only

Dropped by NPCs

Regrowth of the Grove:  

  • Alteration 

  • Mana cost 600 

  • Effect:  Group Regrowth

This is the group regeneration spell you've been waiting for.  One cast, and all your party is regening hps at 15 per tick.

Reported drops from Juggernauts in Old Sebilis and Guardian Wurms in Skyfire

Shield of Blades:  

  • Abjuration 

  • Mana cost 150 

  • Effect:  Upgraded Damage Shield

Upgraded Damage Shield spell.  Each time a mob hits you, you will cause them 32 points of damage.

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor
