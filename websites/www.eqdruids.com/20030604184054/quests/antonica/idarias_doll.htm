Idaria's Doll

Indaria, located in the white building behind the bank, apparently had her doll eaten by sharks.

Kill sharks in the sewers or by the docks or in Erudin harbor until one of them drops a doll and return it to her for some experience and a nice item (usually a Velvet Choker).

Faction: +Guards of Qeynos, +Antonius Bayle, -Corrupt Qeynos Guards, -Circle of Unseen Hands, +Merchants of Qeynos.
