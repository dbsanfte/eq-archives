Druids are known for their hunting prowess in large, "outdoor" zones.

However, a large number of Druid spells, such as Spirit of Wolf or Harmony, can not be cast within the confines of "indoor" or "contained" zones, such as certain underground dungeons (e.g. Befallen, Guk, Kedge Keep, etc.).

So here is a list of the zones found in the game, and if they are considered an indoor or outdoor zone.

If you see anything that needs to be corrected or added, please contact me at Tesiux@eqdruids.com.

 

Outdoor-Only Spells:
  • Level 5:   Invoke Lightning  /  Camouflage  /  Harmony
  • Level 9:   Starshine  /  Treeform
  • Level 14: Spirit of Wolf
  • Level 19: Dizzying Wind
  • Level 24: Spirit of Cheetah  /  Sunbeam  /  Wolf Form
  • Level 29: Scale of Wolf / Harmony of Nature
  • Level 34: Lightning Strike  /  Greater Wolf Form
  • Level 39: Pack Spirit  /  Share Wolf Form
  • Level 44: Form of the Great Wolf
  • Level 49: Lightning Blast
  • Level 53: Spirit of Scale
  • Level 54: Spirit of Eagle  /  Form of the Howler
  • Level 56: Breath of Karana
  • Level 58: Fist of Karana
  • Level 59: Spirit of Oak
  • Level 60: Form of the Hunter  /  Mask of the Hunter
  • Level 62: Karana's Rage / Flight of Eagles
