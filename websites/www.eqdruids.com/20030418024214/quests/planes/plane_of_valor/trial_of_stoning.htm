Trial of Stoning

 

You can only do this quest with a single group. Completing this quest (or any of the other trials) will give you access to the Plane of Storms and the Plane of Valor

Talk to Mavuin in the basement cellblock. Hail him and follow the dialogue. He will ask you to plead his case before the Tribunal, aggreeing will grant you a character flag.

Head to the area with The Tribunal, and ask them about Mavuin. Following the dialog, saying "I am prepared" to each Tribunal will give you description of the six trials.

To do this one, say "I am ready to begin the Trial of Stoning". Make sure you are actually prepared when you say that because your group will be teleported to the event area where you will only get a few seconds before things start.

In this trial 4 mobs spawn, they can be rockmen, earth elementals, or spectres. When mobs are up, so are two archers (forget exact names). They are untargetable and shoot you for like 90-100 damage while fighting. Rockmen of course are unmezzable, and spectres are mezzable. The boss is the same as all the others -- melees for 700s, slowable, has an AE dot that also has a snare component to it.

Finishing this gives you the Mark of Stoning.

Now talk to the Tribunal and say "what evidence of Mauvuin" and they will tell you that they have consented to hear Mauvuin's case. Head back down and talk to him and he will ask you to go see Karana and Mithaniel Marr. This will flag you to be able to enter the Plane of Storms and and Plane Valor.
