Druid Epic Walkthrough

EQDruids.com

 

The Druid Epic starts with talking to Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods.

 

He's hidden in a grove of thick trees near to the Skyfire zone usually around 3200, 2900.

 

He’s very easy to find on tracking if you zone into Burning Woods from Skyfire. Start up your tracking and you should be able to find him easily.

Ask him "what action" and he will give you a note. If he won’t respond to you, you need to do the Muffin Quest (see Epic Faction Fix guide).

 

Hail Telin

 

'Hello, wanderer'

Ask 'What are you doing in this forest?'
'Mighty this place must have been, once. Now scarred by hatred and suffering. I have been sent here seeking answers to problems not yet understood'

Ask 'What answers?'
'A great evil walks across the land. The hand of some demon we don't yet know is twisting and polluting forests'

Ask 'What evil?'
'If I knew that, I wouldn’t be here looking for answers. I would be elsewhere, looking for solutions. For now, I suspect much but know little'

Ask 'What do you suspect?'
'The lands are changing. Beasts, large and small, have turned upon those who watched over them. Druids and rangers, worshippers of both Karana and Tunare, have reported a feeling of hatred throughout their once peaceful lands'

Ask 'Perhaps it is Innoruuk?'
'Innoruuk? Perhaps. Aye. It must be. Perhaps I knew and didn't want to consider the possibility. It must be this though, and some action will have to be taken'

Ask 'What action should I take?'
Telin Darkforest looks at you consideringly and then scribbles something quickly on a small, worn paper. 'You seem powerful enough. Perhaps you can help those who seek to protect nature's peace. Take this note. Deliver it only into the hands of Faelin Bloodbriar. Where you will find her, I cannot say. She is a friend of mine and a wanderer. She has been seen from one end of the lands to the other. Seek her out, and quickly'

 

Now that you have the note, you need to give the note to Faelin Bloodbriar in Greater Faydark.

 

She spawns around 1500, 500 near Orc Hill and the entrance to Crushbone.

 

She has an Orc Pawn placeholder that is standing between two trees. An easy way to make sure this pawn is killed is to sit.  The pawn will immediately attack you when it spawns.

 

Make sure that you continue to SoW yourself. When Faelin does spawn, she immediately SoWs herself and takes off running!

 

When you see her on tracking take out the note and start running to her. She runs in a circular pattern and will not stop if hailed.

 

Run up behind her and give her the note. Once you have given her the note you will receive a ring and she will despawn.

Bad news, eh? Take this ring. There is one elf, dark elf even, who we must go to in order to gather insight into Innoruuk's plans. Giz X'Tin, he goes by most commonly. Kithicor seems to be his favorite haunt and most likely you will find him there. Don't ask how I know him. Just take the ring as my sign to him and he will speak to you. We don't need to speak again, so don't seek me out, just go to Telin. I must leave to investigate these events'.

Your next step is to go to the Kithicor Forest.

 

Once there, head towards the entrance to High Hold Pass.

 

Start your tracking and find Giz X’Tin.

 

He is a dark elf, but will not attack you.

 

Hand him the ring you received from Faelin and he will give you in return a dark metal coin.

 

Handing him the ring will spawn a level fifty Dark Elf Reaver but with SoW or wolf form you will be able to run away without a problem.

 

It seems we have a mutual 'acquaintance.' The coin repays an old debt. You don't have to worry about what that is. Luckily, Lord Innoruuk has informed me of your coming and your desires. Fortunately, I have all the answers you require right here at the end of my friend's blade. I think I'll be paying my old 'friend' a visit'.


 Now return to Telin Darkforest and give him the dark metal coin. He will give it back to you as worn metal coin and tells you to find Athele.

Faelin sent you to Giz? Surprising. This coin speaks to the outcome of your talk. He would hardly give this bit of metal to a stranger, but that's a story for a different time. So, you say Giz did confirm Innoruuk's involvement, and something about undead minions? Here, take this coin back. Find Arch Druid Althele in the Karanas and show her that. Tell her what we have discussed. She will look kindly on an ally in this matter'

Now is when you will need to bring a few friends along. You are about to do several trades in a row which will cause a spawn of three Reavers.

 

You should not need a huge group of people, only enough to keep three NPCs busy (they are all about level fifty). Park your friends up the hill from the Barbarian Farm, about neg 1500, neg 1000. 

 

You need to make sure to do all the trades in order. 

 

If you do not complete all the trades you will have to start over.

Go to Althele. Athele is in East Karana north of the barbarian fishing village. Give her the worn metal coin and receive a Braided Grass Amulet.

I see. The story of this coin speaks much to me as do the words you have given me. Telin sent word that you would arrive. The tidings you bring are ill indeed. Here, take this amulet and find Sionae. She is nearby. We will speak more on this matter when all are present'.

 

Now track Sionae. Give the Braided Grass Amulet to Sionae. From Sionae you will receive a Frayed Braided Grass Amulet.

 

I see that the time has come. Take the amulet and give it to the third of our kin, Nuien. I will meet you at the gathering'.

 

Take the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet and give it to Nuien. You will receive the same amulet back.

 

'So be it. Do as you have done before and find the next. Teloa is the last'

 

Finally hand that amulet to Teola. Teola will say to follow her, instead run/gate back to your party.

 

'Follow, friend. Hold that amulet for yourself for the time being'.

 

(If you were to follow Teola back to Althele you would hear her say)

 

'Great mother of life and father of sky, growth and spirit, Tunare and Karana. Innoruuk once again schemes and we have failed in our duties to protect our land. We give our powers in sacrifice for your help. Heed our call and send us your wisdom'.

 

'Innoruuk's brood is upon us. Go, find the spawn of hatred before they reach this point and destroy them!'

 

A Dark Elf Corruptor and two Dark Elf Reavers will spawn near your party. The Corrupter is your target.

 

If he is not attacked within ten seconds of spawning he will despawn (and you will have to start the epic over).

 

Easiest way to handle this, is for you to take care of the Corrupter and have your party take care of the two Dark Reavers.

 

The Reavers will try to kill all the druids/rangers that you were just dealing with. You do not want this to happen!

 

If the NPCs are killed before you finish killing the Corrupter he will despawn.

 

Kill the Corrupter, loot the Fleshbound Tome from his body, and then kill the Reavers. (suggestion here, an enchanter can make this VERY easy, as they can mez the two Reavers until the Corrupter is dead).

If the Reavers kill Athele wait one hour of real world time and Athele will spawn again. Give her the tome and she gives you an Earthstained Note.

Althele hands the book to Tholris who reads through it with lines of concern etched on his face, then whispers into her ear. 'Dire news, indeed. This cannot be allowed. I must keep this book but you, [your name] , must not allow Innoruuk, to seed the land with his hatred and filth. You have only just begun your quest. The path you are guided upon will be difficult, if not impossible, but someone must finish it. Please, take this, read of it, follow its instructions. Tunare bless your path and Karana watch over you'.

 

(The Earth Stained Note reads)

 

'We cannot speak again. Our circle is now known and must seek sanctuary. In case this note were to fall into others’ hands. I cannot tell you plainly the next steps of your task. All I can say is to seek she who walks the path of the mother, she who walks the lands in service to her kin. Give her this note and she will know'.

The note is referring to Ella Foodcrafter in Misty Thicket.

Like Faelin, she constantly runs, so you have to chase her.

 

Give her the Earthstained Note and she will tell you that she needs a mixture made. She will give you her mixing bowl.

 

It is a container so make sure you have a free slot in your inventory. She also mentions some kind of ancient bowl that she needs.

 

The mixing bowl she hands you is the Shiny Tin Bowl.

 

Hmmm. I've been waiting for someone to bring me this. I'm sorry such a young one as you has taken up this task. Maybe the sky god blesses ya though. Aye, ya have that look in your eye. Our task is to focus the blessings of the gods and cleanse the filth from the land. I'll need two things from ya'

Ask 'What two things?'


'Well, ya ask before ya act. That's a good sign. What I need isn't easy to find. First, I need a mixture. Plain sounding enough but it's a special one. Cleanses the mind and focuses the earth's energies into a forest walker's mind. I don't know how to make it. I don't even know what it's called. I merely know it exists and we'll be needing it for our task. When ya do find how to make it ya can mix it in that bowl. Keep it when youre done, it's a good bowl'

Ask 'What is the second task?'


'Good, ya didn't run off without finding out what else ya need to do. Second, I need a special type of bowl. An ancient type of bowl, which strengthened the food placed inside it, once made by the elves of old. Again, I cannot tell ya how it is made for the making was lost to the ages. Seek this knowledge and these items out in the lands. Come to me when ya have found both'

 

You need to forage four items from different zones to combine in the mixing bowl. The items are:

Chilled Tundra Root from Everfrost
Sweetened Mudroot from Misty Thicket
Speckled Moldy Mushrooms from Innothule Swamp
Ripened Heartfruit from Greater Faydark

You can forage these items at anytime.  A good way to start on your epic early is to go ahead and save the above items if you forage them.

 

An easy way to know if you have a piece of the epic foraged is all the above are lore and no drop.

Once you have all four items, combine them in the mixing bowl and you will receive the Hardened Mixture. You can now dispose of the mixing bowl.

 

The Runed Bowl


 Now you need to go to the Chessboard in Timorous Deep.

 

This is a much easier trip now than it was before.  Go to Butcherblock and on the docks find Translocator Gethin.  Hail her, and follow her lead and you will be instantly transported to the Timorous Deep.

 

Once you are in the Timorous Deep cast either Lesser Succor or Egress and you will be teleported to the island that Alrik is on.

 

When you find Alrik, hail him then say "How do you make an ancient bowl?" and "I can take the artifact."

 

He will then give you a crushed pot and send you to Felwithe.

 

Say 'I seek an ancient type of bowl made by the elves of old'


Alrik Farsight rummages through his tattered bag and throws aside a towel. 'To many towels. Oh well, someone told me they were good to have once. Here it is, a bowl pattern. Interesting bowl. Looks almost like a magical bowl but I’m no potter. You need it, you say? Well, it is interesting. Part of my research here is for the wizard guild in Felwithe. I’m sure they’d put a high price on this one, the enchanters guild being so interested in trading for magical paraphernalia and all. Of course if you helped me out on a chore I have, I’d be happy to give it to you. I need to deliver an artifact to the guild and I’d much rather stay here exploring. If you wish, you can take the artifact and return with the receipt and I will give you the pattern'

Say 'I will take the artifact'


'Hah! Okay then. Here take this to Farios Elianos in Felwithe. He will give you the Receipt'

In Felwithe you need to find Farious Elianos. He is in Felwithe South at the merchant building near the caster’s guild (you can find him with tracking).

If your faction with him isn't at least Amiable you need to  refer to the Epic Faction Fixing page under the Keepers of the Art quest (batwing quest). You can also gain positive faction by killing Kizdean Gix in West Commons (dark elf near the inns).  He probably will not respond to your hails, but if your faction is amiable or better you can turn in the items.


Once Farios cons amiable and will talk to you, give him the pot and in return you will receive a grocery list.

 

Why in the nine hells would I need a crushed pot? Perhaps I could use it to not hold water! Why did you even bother me with this? Are you mentally deficient? Apparently. Oh, well, you're just a servant boy. Well, then, we'll make you useful, if just a little. Take this shopping list and go get my groceries. Take it to Nora in the tavern. She'll know what I need'.

 

Take the grocery list to Merchant Nora in the Shop of All Halos in Felwithe north, near the cleric guild. She will give you a bag of provisions when you hand her the grocery list.

 

You hardly look like the type of errand runner Farios usually sends out to fetch his groceries but here they are. I hope he chokes on them. Oh, and tell him he still owes me five gold for his last order!'

 

Bring the grocery list to Farios and he will give you a receipt for the ancient bowl.

Ah, good. Here's a few copper and a receipt for services rendered. Now be off, I'm sure citizens across the land are in dire need of broken pots at right this moment'.

 

Now return to Alrik Farsight in Timorous Deep. Give Alrik the receipt and he will give you an ancient pattern.

 

'Excellent! Was he pleased with the artifact? Oh, that's not even worth answering. I'm sure he was. He's always happy with the things I send him. That's why he honored me with this position of esteem, searching for useful and powerful items in this newly discovered land'.

 

(The Ancient Pattern reads)

 

.........x..........w...............iea.......Pla.......Sp......Powder....’Encha......lay.......’This ........attern


 The pattern instructs you to combine the pattern with a piece of enchanted clay and platinum speckled powder in a pottery wheel.

 

Don’t worry if you don’t have any skills in pottery there is no skill check.

 

To receive the platinum speckled powder you must first forage a Rose of Firiona in Firiona Vie and give it to Merdan Fleetwood in Surefall Glades.

 

The rose is not no drop, so if a druid/ranger happens to have one of them, you could buy or be given it.

When you give the rose to Merdan you will receive back an Old Wooden Painting.

 

'Hail Merdan Fleetfoot'


'Yes Niera! Oh. Erm. Hi. I mean, uh. How are you?'

Ask 'Who is Niera?'


'She is, uh.. I don't know. I haven't been able to speak with her. She has many suitors, and I'm just another poor ranger. I spend my days here pretending to fish, to carve bows, but really I'm just waiting to catch a glimpse of her. I thought about adventuring into the world to find some kind of token to give her, to show her how I feel, but I cant tear myself away from here'

Give Merdan the Firiona Rose you previously foraged.


'This is perfect. Thank you! I'm going to go over to her now. Erm, well, maybe I'll wait a week to let the uh.. beauty of the flower.. uhm.. blossom. Yes, that sounds good. Here, you take this painting. I don't need it now. I made it myself, she wouldn't like it anyways'

 

(Go over to Niera Farbreeze and when she starts to hum say the following)

 

Ask 'What tune is that?'


'Ohh. Just a little song my mother taught me when I was young. It speaks of the earth, the sky, and the sparkling stars above, always there'

Ask 'Why are they always there?'


'Aye, always there. Like this small bag I wear around my neck. The last gift of my dying mother. It holds a fine dust, a dust she collected during her short life, that she believed brought her luck. It's speckled, flecked as the night sky is with stars'

Ask 'Where did she find the speckled, flecked dust?'


'I've had this little bit of dust, all I have of my mother, for many years now. It's the only reminder I have of her. She said it was special, magical, a warmth to help me through the nights. Even if my life were in the balance, I would have a hard time giving it up'

Take the old wooden painting the human skeleton in Frontier Mountains. He will show on tracking a “A Human Skeleton”.

 

Give him the painting and he will crumble into a pile of dust (this is what is supposed to happen!) and you'll have a silver chained locket in your inventory.

 

Return to Surefall Glade and give the locket to Niera Farbreeze.

 

When you give her the locket she will hand you the platinum speckled powder.

This, this is my mother's amulet. I cannot believe it. Please, take this powder you say you need. Perhaps it does have powers, I don't know, I don't care. Thank you, my eternal thanks for this'.

 

Next you need to find the enchanted clay. Go to Kaladim and find Kinlo Strongarm in Kaladim's EverHot Forge.

 

Once you find Kinlo ask him "what mud" and "do it for me" Kinlo will tell you that he would like to have a colored axe for his service.

 

He wants a Jade Reaver that can be looted off the Black Reavers in the City of Mist.

 

Be forewarned the Black Reavers are a serious mob to deal with.

 

They are approximately level 50-52. One will spawn, and when you kill him either he will drop or another will spawn immediately in his place.

 

This can happen up to seven times! (there have been recent reports of the spawns going on for many more than seven so be prepared just in case) 

 

Make sure to bring a nice solid party with you for this mob. They reside behind a locked door that must be picked by a rogue with over 200 lock picking.

There is an upside here. The Jade Reaver can be purchased from other players. So if you are unable to put together a group for City of Mist you do have that alternative.


Hand Kinlo the Jade Reaver and you will be handed the enchanted clay in return. Now that you have the clay, combine the pattern, clay and powder in any pottery wheel to make the Rune Crested Bowl.

 

Ask 'What Ancient?'


'The ancient smiths, for what I think yer askin about, ain't been around fer a long time, laddy. Last anyone heard o' the ancients was 'fore my time even. There be more than a few tales about 'em'

Ask 'What few tales?'


'There be some who talk about how the ancient smiths went down to work fer the duke o' below, sayin he be needin some fine dwarven smiths fer makin some castle or some such. There be tales that the ancients were so good at smithin that the duke took 'em down and made 'em head smiths over his minions. There's even tales that some o the smiths' souls were trapped in their hammers, stuck there by some necromancer or somethin, as punishment or retribution'

Ask 'What hammers?'


'Aye. Some says their souls were bound into their hammers by an Erudite necromancer and enchanter. They say they used the power of the hammers ta profit by makin ships, boats, and houses better than any other merchants. Sad tale, if'n it be true'

Ask 'What Erudite Necromancer?'


'I'm not knowin more about the story, lad. Everythin I know is already in what I said. Aye, Erudites. I suppose if yer wantin ta run after shortbeard tales ya can look over on that plague island they call Odus'

Ask 'What mud?'


'Eh? Oh, the clay. Arrr. Lots o that mud round 'ere. Mostly, a good dwarf not be needin somethin so soft 'n squishy but the womenfolk, you know, the elves come lookin fer it. If ye be one o' those pansy elves wantin some mud fer workin into fairy statues, flashy trinkets, 'n pig sticker arrers then I can get it for ye. Course I'd be lowerin meself, a good dwarf, ta be diggin that kinda mush outta the ground, but I can do it fer ya'

Say 'Do it for me'


'Arrr. Shaddap. I ain't doin it til ya bring me somethin. A fee, I guess yed call it, fer lowerin meself ta elf woman work. Go get me a good strong axe and I'll do it fer ye. Mebbe somethin with a good tinted polish on it. I got plenty o' axes but no tinted ones. Har har! Colored axe. Somethin to go with the color left on the blade after I be crackin open a gobbo head'

Give Kinlo the Jade Reaver you got in the City of Mist.


'Arrrrr. Ooooo. Arrrrrr... Yah, now that's what I be callin a good lookin axe. I be crackin a few skulls with this one. Oh, here's yer block o' mud. Our priests o' Brell use the mud pit fer relievin themselves after really hard ale blessin ceremonies, so ya ain't got ta be enchantin it in holy water or anythin cause they already have. Har har!'

 

The Stones
 

Go Back to Misty Thicket and hand Ella Foodcrafter the hardened mixture and the rune crested bowl. She will then make you a softly glowing stone.

 

Ella now will tell you to find one such as yourself.

 

She is referring to Foloal Stormforest in Firiona Vie.

 

Ella Foodcrafter smiles slowly as she works the mixture in the bowl and it starts to shine with power. 'This is... a piece of a lost magic item known as Nature's Gem. When restored to completeness I've heard its healing powers will be extraordinary. Take it now, I will take the bowl and hopefully do some good from my end. From here, you can go many ways but I can't guide your path step by step. The only advise I can give you is to look for ones such as yourself'

 

You can find Foloal on the wall that circles the city of Firiona Vie at the north end.

 

Ask her "are you one such as myself?" "what answers?" and "what about Venril Sathir?"

 

She will tell you to bring her the stone you already have from Ella and the gem that can be found on Venril Sathir.

 

(luckily she means the spawn of Venril Sathir’s remains not the true Venril Sathir!)

Ask 'Are you one such as myself?'


'You must be the one that has been spoken of. Yes, yes I am one of the circle. There are many of us in the lands right now, all seeking answers to the ills that have befallen our homes'

Ask 'What answers?'


'I have heard whispers of new Iksar movements in the land. The name of Venril Sathir is on the scaled lips and sharp tongues of every cold blooded lizard in this place'

Ask 'What about Venril Sathir?'


'All I've heard are whispers of connections between the undead lizards and the dark god of hate. There's some kind of history behind the two. It might be that Venril is the main focus of Innoruuk's will in the land. Then again, maybe not, but this is the best we've gotten so far. If you think you can do it, then find him and remove him from the face of this earth. Bring me the half of the stone I know you have and whatever conduit of power you find on his rotting corpse

Venril Sathir is found in Karnor’s Castle, in the Dreadlands. Venril Sathir’s remains will spawn twenty four hours (approximately) after Venril Sathir is killed. 

 

There should be two spawns of Venril Sathir's Remains, then Venril Sathir will respawn.  However this spawn seems to be acting differently lately so be forewarned it may not be this constant any longer.

 

When the remains spawn, go into Karnor’s Castle and hail him. First he wants a Globe of Fireflies.  Use the spell Dance of Fireflies to make a Globe of Fireflies to turn in.  This is a level one spell for those that forgot they even had the spell.

 

This spell can only be cast at night.  You might want to go ahead and make yourself a globe ahead of time, so you aren't having to wait at the spawn for night to fall.

 

Hand the Remains of Venril Sathir the Globe of Fireflies.  This will spawn Spirit of Venril Sathir.

 

Now hand Spirit of Venril Sathir a copy of the level 49 Cleric spell Resurrection (this spell is purchased at any of the Cleric guilds).

 

This will spawn yet another Venril Sathir who will attack.  Don't worry he is not as tough as the "normal" Venril Sathir.

 

Kill this spawned Venril and loot the Pulsing Green Stone.  The good news here is that the loot normally is two Pulsing Green Stones so you can help another druid/ranger with one kill.


 Take the Softly Glowing Stone and Pulsing Green Stone back to Foloal Stormforest. She will give you a warmly glowing stone.

 

Foloal will now tell you to take the stone and walk your path. The path she is referring to leads back to Ella Foodcrafter in Misty Thicket.

 

Foloal Stormforest blinks and carefully takes the stones from you, cautiously scratching the jagged green stone against the other. Its skin seems to shiver and flake at the contact and eventually begins to shine brightly. 'With the gem nature's balance is complete. Take the stone and walk your path. Blade or lightning caller, burning sword or the mother's walk'.


 Give Ella the warmly glowing stone and you will receive the Elaborate Scimitar (10/30 +9 wis, +10 mana, +5 sv poison and disease).
 

She will then ask you to collect the Cleansed Spirits of Antonica, Faydwer and Kunark and hand them along with the scimitar to Xanuusus.

The Cleansed Spirit of Antonica

Go to North Karana and find the Withered Treant. It is at the far north end of NK, at the base of a mountain wall.

 

 Hail the Withered Treant and ask him "What Sickness" and he will respond that he wants you to cleanse Antonica of its sickness.

 

He is referring to the Corrupted Mammoth, Corrupted Aqua Goblin and Corrupted Hill Giant.

 

Yes. My bark is blackened and cracked by sickness. If only I were the only one to be afflicted this way. Throughout the lands this is happening. Agents of some dark god are twisting the lands and their creatures'

Ask 'What dark god?'


'Not long ago, a human passed through this area. Around him swarmed some aura of sickness. Few could see it but with my age I could recognize a bringer of the plague. He seeded the area with pestilence before I could lift a branch to stop him. I believe it was he, and others in other lands, who has helped bring about this sickness'

Ask 'What sickness?'


'I have kept in touch with friends of Tunare and Karana. It seems something is building amid the darkness. Some pact between gods to corrupt. All I know for sure is that you can stop the sickness in this land. I can feel it, vaguely. Somewhere, in the mountain and lake areas, and far north amid the cold mountains is where the land hurts most. Go, find the sources of corruption, and bring them back to me. Three should be their number'

 

All three mobs start out as a tainted mob first.

 

When you kill a tainted mob you have a chance for the corrupted mob to spawn. The corrupted will spawn right on top of the tainted corpse.

The Tainted/Corrupted Mammoth.

 

He is approximately level twenty and spawns in Everfrost Peaks. Wooly Mammoth Calves are his place holder. If he isn’t up kill Wooly Mammoth Calves to make him spawn. Kill the Corrupted Mammoth and loot a Chunk of Tundra.


 The Tainted/Corrupted Aqua goblin Shaman.

 

The Aqua goblin is found in an underwater tower in Lake Rathe (loc of the tunnel is 2831, -912, -221 and is underwater). You should keep enduring breath on yourself and also be in wolf form (less agro problems).

 

(I HIGHLY recommend the following quest for not only this part but for Kedge Keep.  Makes your day MUCH easier - Amulet of the Grey Wastes)

 

It is easiest to snare the mob and run back up to the island (above water) to fight.

 

You can hide behind trees to keep his arrows from interrupting your casts. Only bother to kill the goblins at the top of the tower.

 

If the Corrupted is not up keep pulling/killing the goblins in the tower and eventually the tainted will spawn.

 

Don’t bother Deep! She is a major nasty and will spoil your day. Use harmony to single pull and you will be fine.

 

Kill the Corrupted and loot a Clean Lakewater. The Clean Lakewater looks like a globe of water.


 The Tainted/Corrupted Hill Giant is in Rathe Mountains.

 

You want to hunt in the hill giant area near the hill with the Guard Tower, where Cyclops can also be found.

 

As with all the others, kill hill giants until the Tainted spawns.

 

Kill the Tainted to spawn the Corrupted Hill giant.

 

Kill Corrupted and loot an Ancient Rock from it.

 

Give the three items to the Withered Treant in North Karana and receive the Treant Heart.


 'My heart cleansed of Corruption can interrupt the Flow of the corruption between the priest and his dark god, force it upon him quickly, I can sense him, he is in the Karana's somewhere'

 

Now go to Yeka Las in South Karana. He is normally found to the east of the bridge and along the water.

 

Now travel to Lesser Faydark and find the Pained Unicorn.

Ask him "What Corruption" and he will ask you to collect items from the Corrupted Seahorse, the Corrupted Cyclops, and the Corrupted Brownie.

 

The Corrupted Seahorse is located in Kedge Keep.

 

The Corrupted Seahorse is a challenge. He is approximately level fifty two, hits for 150ish and is also a Cleric (yep, he can complete heal).

 

Bring lots of extra fish scales for Enduring Breath and some strong parties. An Enchanter or Bard is almost a necessity here due to adds.

 

Bring strong tanks, and healers also!

 

He will normally spawn near Estrella. When you kill the Corrupted Seahorse loot the Kedge Keep Crystals.

 

The Corrupted Seafury Cyclops is next.

 

The Corrupted Seafury Cyclops spawns on an island west of Sister Island in the Ocean of Tears.

 

Take the boat from Freeport to Butcherblock Mountains. Get off on Sister Island, and head west.

 

The first island is the one with a mage spell vendor in a tower. The next island to the west is Sea Fury island. There's around a half dozen Sea Furies, which are Cyclops.

 

You can pull them to the shore or other island until a Tainted spawns. Be careful some of these mobs do summon.  Having a tank or a "fear" caster here can make a HUGE difference.

 

Kill the Corrupted Seafury Cyclops and loot the Ocean of Tears Seavines.

 

The Corrupted Brownie is in Lesser Faydark.

 

He is a druid so be prepared for a serious fight.  Again having someone that can fear the Brownie can make this fight a much easier fight.

 

His placeholders are the Brownie Scouts.

 

Use wolf form to reduce your agro with the Brownies. You can find these scouts at loc 0,0.

 

Like the others, kill until tainted/corrupted spawns and then loot the Green Heartwood Branch.

Hand the Gleaming Unicorn Horn to the gnome Silox Azrix in Ak'Anon to get the Cleansed Spirit of Faydwer.

 

He is near the mines at the far north of the zone, on the left side of the green river.

 

If your faction is bad with Gnomes, you still should be ok going in invisible.

 

Find Ulump Pujik in Swamp of No Hope. He can be located near the froglok village at location Neg 100 Neg 1770.

 

He is a level fifty five warrior with VERY high Magic Resistance. 

 

There have been changes to Ulump's resistances.  He is still very hard to land a spell on but you can hit him sometimes. 

 

DoTs seem to be the best bet to land.

If you have a solid upper level group, or two groups of 50's you shouldn't have any problem with Ulump.  Just make sure you have a good Tank with a Cleric ready to complete heal.

 

He double attacks for 250DMG and has 30k Hitpoints so it will take a bit to kill him.

 

He has also been known to run at 30 % health.

 

Kill him and loot Froglok Essence.

 

He can be found at 2300, neg 2300

 

Give him the heart, and he will give you back the Cleansed Spirit of Antonica.

'Yeka Ias grabs the heart as you toss it to him before realizing what it is. As soon as it touches his hands, his body convulses, a sickly light emanating from his eyes and oozing cracks appear in his skin. As he melts into the ground, a small glowing ball floats where he once stood'.

Now travel to Lesser Faydark and find the Pained Unicorn.

Ask him "What Corruption" and he will ask you to collect items from the Corrupted Seahorse, the Corrupted Cyclops, and the Corrupted Brownie.

The Corrupted Seahorse is located in Kedge Keep.

 

The Corrupted Seahorse is a challenge. He is approximately level fifty two, hits for 150ish and is also a Cleric (yep, he can complete heal).

 

Bring lots of extra fish scales for Enduring Breath and some strong parties. An Enchanter or Bard is almost a necessity here due to adds.

 

Bring strong tanks, and healers also!

 

He will normally spawn near Estrella. When you kill the Corrupted Seahorse loot the Kedge Keep Crystals.

 

The Corrupted Seafury Cyclops is next.

Now its time for more corrupted mobs!

 

The three corrupted mobs of Kunark are the Corrupted Gorilla, Corrupted Barracuda, and the Dragon Faydedar.

The Corrupted Gorilla can be found in the Emerald Jungle. Place holders for the Corrupted Gorilla are the Tottering Gorillas. Keep killing Tottering Gorillas until the Corrupted spawns and then kill Corrupted.

 

There are 2 different tainted gorillas.

 

Only the live version will spawn the corrupted.  If you have a tainted gorilla that looks like a zombie it is the undead version and won't spawn the corrupted gorilla.

 

The Corrupted drops the Green Tree Bark.


 The Corrupted Barracuda is found in the main lake of Lake of Ill Omen. You can normally find the barracuda spawns at neg950 neg900.

 

Kill the Deepwater Barracuda, until the Tainted spawns.

 

Be warned this mob can take a LONG time to spawn. An easy way to see if the tainted will spawn the corrupted is to pull the tainted barracudas onto the land. If the tainted follows the Corrupted should spawn after.

 

Be warned the barracudas are not an easy kill! Make sure you are conservative with your mana and a KEI doesn't hurt. 

 

Once you kill the Corrupted Barracuda loot the Pure Lakewater.

 

The last mob is Faydedar. This is not an easy dragon. He however is spawn able.

It's time to go back to Timorous Deep again.  Go back to the docks in Butcherblock and have your party Translocate on over to the Timorous Deep.

 

Now is a great time to have a high level mage friend. If you have such, he can go to the Oasis where Faydedar spawns and Call of the Hero you all over.

 

If you don’t have such you need to swim or levitate/run to neg 11300, neg 3500. This is a wall in the water. Swim down underwater and you will see an entrance to the Oasis.

 

Once inside you have to find the second entrance through the second ring. The second entrance is on the north side of the inside ring at loc 2000, neg 1100.

As with many dragons Faydedar dispels. So load up everyone with “junk” buffs (resist poison, resist disease, see invisible etc.

 

You will want resist fire, so don’t use that yet). Once everyone is buffed up then cast the beneficial buffs like Skin like Nature, and Resist Fire.

 

You will want your Fire and Magic resists as high as possible! Bring some bards along they are great help here.

Now remove your SoW and turn run off (Faydedar can also fear). The most important thing for people to realize here, and I can’t emphasize this enough. DO NOT SIT DOWN. EVER.

 

If you sit while the fight is going on, you guarantee agro from Faydedar.
 

If you are unfortunate enough to have someone sit down during the fight, tell the master tank to IMMEDIATELY sit down.  This normally will pull agro back onto the tank.
 

To spawn Faydedar go to Dolgin Codslayer. Give him the Froglok Essence you looted from Ulump.

 

Before you do the turn in, make sure you have people track Faydedar (the non spawned version). 

 

In the rare cases that he is up as a normal mob, the hand in will not spawn the "spawned" Faydedar. 

Either one is useable for the epic however, so if the normal mob is up, you can kill him for your piece also.

 

Dolgin will give you back the Froglok Essence  and then despawn.

Be ready for Faydedar next.

 

Don’t laugh at him (he’s tiny) but has all the power of a full size dragon!

 

Have your master tank continue attacking until Faydedar is at 70% health.  Once he is that low have the casters and additional melee start their attack. 

 

Casters must be close to Faydedar for their spells to land.  If Faydedar is resisting all your casts you aren't close enough.

 

When Faydedar is at 50% health pour it on!  Dump everything you have on the dragon.


Once you have killed Faydedar loot the Pod of Seawater

Now travel to the Overthere.

 

Hand the Froglok Essence, the Green Tree Bark, the Pure Lakewater and the Pod of Seawater to Nekexin Virulence in Overthere.

 

When you hand him the four items he will despawn and you will have the Cleansed Spirit of Kunark.

 

He is by the water at the far north end of the zone, to the east of the evil outpost.

Be very careful here. The guards of Overthere are nasty with a fairly large agro range.

Easiest way to avoid them is to either come along the zone from the Frontier Mountains/Overthere zone and keep going along the east side of the zone.

Or take the Bloated Belly (the evil boat to Overthere that leaves from Oasis docks) and as soon as it zones into the Overthere jump off and swim to shore tracking Nekexin.

 

The End

You’ve done it!

 

You have finished all but one task to obtain your epic!

 

Now go back to North Karana, find Xanuusus the Treant and hand him the three Cleansed Spirits and the Elaborate Scimitar.

 

Once you hand him these items you will be given in return the Nature Walker’s Scimitar!

Xanuusus pushes the spirits in the air, making them dance around the scimitar. With wave of his hands they melt into the blade and the hilt of the weapon. 'With this the spirits of the lands are now free of the corruption of the dark gods. Through your strength and dedication was this possible, [your name].
