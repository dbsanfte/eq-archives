Bearskin Gloves (Kithicor Forest)

 

Properties                          QUEST RELATED

Boosts                                 None

Weight & Size                    WT: 2.1  Size: ?

Talk to Ged Twigborn, a wandering NPC who lives in Kithikor Forest. Ged will tell you that he can make different types of gloves if you bring him the following two items.

·  25 Gold

·  High Quality Bear Skin
