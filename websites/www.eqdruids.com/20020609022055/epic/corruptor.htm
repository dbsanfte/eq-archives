Make sure when you start this part of the quest you have your group ready and waiting at (Neg 1500 Neg 1000).

All of the following druids can be found near the south river in East Karana but might not all show up on track from the spot where you start which is Athele.  I have also seen these folks walk to meet the others where you can hand them the item at that time but I would not rely on them coming to you.

1. Find Athele in East Karana.  She will be located near the river just west of the barbarian fisher village.  Give her the coin and recieve this amulet:

2. Find Sionae in East Karana.  He can be found around -2300, -930 but starts walking to the Athele sometimes after he spawns.  Hand him the amulet and you will recieve this:

3. Find Nuien in East Karana.  He can be found around -3650, -300 but also has a tendency to wander to the gathering that is taking place at Athele.  Hand him the item and you will recieve the same item back:

4. Find Teloa in East Karana.  She spawns somewhere around -3800, -2860.  Hand her the item and you will recieve nothing but she will begin walking to the other NPCs which you have already triggered.  At this point you can gate back to your group waiting at the location given at the top of this page.  Wait for the DE corrupter and 2 darelf reavers to spawn which will take place within a minute.

The reavers will start running to the druids and the corrupter will despawn within 20 seconds if not attacked.  Kill him fast and loot his corpse before the reavers kill the druids.  When the last druid dies the reavers will shout out what they have done and then despawn themselves and the corruptor.  Even if you have already killed the corrupter the CORPSE will despawn as well, so loot quickly.
