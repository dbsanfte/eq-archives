Level Forty Nine Spells

Resist Magic: 

  • Abjuration 

  • Mana cost 85 

  • Effect:  Increases Magic Resistance

Increases your magic resistance.  Adds 40 to your magic resistance stats.

Purchase From:  Cleric Guild

Skin like Nature:  

  • Abjuration 

  • Mana cost 300 

  • Effect:  Upgraded AC and Hp buff

The final Skin spell - this is better than the level 44 Cleric spells as far as buffs go. 

+250 hps + 16 AC - this also has a slight regeneration effect of 2 points per tick.  Will stack with Regen/Chloro

Purchase From:  Lavastorm Druid Circle

Engulfing Roots:  

  • Alteration 

  • Mana cost 100 

  • Effect:  Upgraded Root spell

Upgraded root spell.   Lasts longer, isn't resisted as often and does 160 damage.  Nice spell.

Purchase From:  Lavastorm Druid Circle

Fire:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 260
  • Effect:  Area Effect Direct Damage and Debuff

This spell does and Area Effect damage of 392 points, and also reduces cold resistance by 50 points.

Purchase From:  Lavastorm Druid Circle

Ice:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 250 
  • Effect:  Direct Damage

Direct Damage spell for 612 hps a shot.  Also will reduce Fire resist for a small amount of time.

Purchase From:  Lavastorm Druid Circle

Lightning Blast:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 234 
  • Effect:  Area Effect Direct Damage

Nice spell for quad kiting.  Fast, and does some nice damage (477 per cast).  The only problem is that the range is SMALL, so make sure you have the mobs walking basically on top of each other, otherwise you won't hit all at once.

Purchase From:  Lavastorm Druid Circle

Shield of Thorns: 

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 120
  • Effect:  Upgraded Damage Shield

Another upgrade to your Shield of ... line of spells.  This one will do 24 damage to the mobs each time they hit protected person.

Even at higher levels the 24 damage adds up fast, its nice to keep on yourself when soloing.

Purchase From:  Lavastorm Druid Circle

Thorncoat:  

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 175
  • Effect:  Upgraded Personal Damage Shield

Upgraded person damage shield.  This upgrade will increase your AC by 32 and damage attackers by 5 points every time they hit you.  It is stackable with Shield of Thorns. 

Purchase From:  Lavastorm Druid Circle

Starfire:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 250
  • Effect:  Direct Damage 

612 points of damage using this fire spell.  Its my main Direct Damage spell at this level.

Purchase From:  Lavastorm Druid Circle

Pack Chloroplast:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 400
  • Effect:  Group Chloroplast

This is your group chloroplast spell.  As with all group spells, I wouldn't use unless the group is more than 4 people.

Also like all other group spells, this spell lasts approximately half as long as single chloroplast.

Purchase From:  Lavastorm Druid Circle

Improved Superior Camouflage:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 75
  • Effect:  Self on Invisibility

This is a self only invisibility.  It has a fixed duration of 20 mins per cast.

This is a rare drop off Velious Creatures.  If anyone knows any specifics please send it in!

Succor North:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Evacuation Spell

This is your last evacuation spell.  This spell takes you to North Karana Druid Circle.

Purchased From:  Toxxulia Druid Circle
