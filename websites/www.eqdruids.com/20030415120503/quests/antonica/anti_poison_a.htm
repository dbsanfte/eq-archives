Anti-Poison Amulet

 

Talk to Reebo Leefsway and he will tell you that he wants you to teach Nillipus a lesson for stealing Jumjum. Kill Nillipus to retrieve the stolen Jumjum stalks. Return four of these stalks to Reebo to complete the quest. You get a variety of different magical items upon completion of this quest.
