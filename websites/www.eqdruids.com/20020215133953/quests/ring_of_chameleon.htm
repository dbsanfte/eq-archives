Ring of the Chameleon (Skyshrine)

Class                                      ALL

Race                                      ALL

Talk to Sentry Kale who can be found in Skyshrine. He will tell you that he is looking for someone to perform a task for him. If you ask him "what task" he will explain that his food supplies are very low and he wants you to kill the spiders that have been stealing from him. Kill all the Crystal Spiders you see in the zone and bring back their legs. Bring him back the four crystal spider legs and he will sometimes reward you with the Ring of the Chameleon.
