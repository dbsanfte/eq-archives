Intermediate Magic Manual

 

You must have completed the first magic manual quest to continue with this quest.

 

Cadelid Etord says 'I see by the beginner manual you carry that you are on the right track.

 

Let us continue your lessons, shall we? Magic can be a susceptible, yet volatile force sometimes, especially when wielded in the hands of dark beings.

 

I fear there is one such being in particular that seeks to damage the very foundation of our mystic abilities, an ugly warrior by the name of Vhaksiz the Shade. He calls the Plane of Nightmares his home.

 

You must seek this foul creature out and acquire proof of his demise: his dark, sinister heart. Bring it to me along with your Beginner's Magic Manual.'

 

Bring the heart and your beginner's manual back to Cadelid and you will be rewarded with:
