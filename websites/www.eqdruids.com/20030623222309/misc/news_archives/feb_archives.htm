Tens of thousands of people play EverQuest every day, carrying out fantasies from the vantage point of an alter ego in a virtual world inhabited by elves and dragons. New message board for Luclin Quest Armor 2/6/02

I don't know anything about these folks, but thought you all might enjoy knowing about it :)

Announcing the Twilight Exchange!

For those who have been working on the Twilight Sea quests, with the 5 zones and hundreds of rare quest mobs & drops, I have started an ezboard dedicated to being a pan-server marketplace and discussion area for the quests. The idea is that most people will end up with more unneeded drops than needed, so the board is there to make it easy to arrange trades with people that have what you need and need what you have.

There is a board for each server, plus areas for discussing the quests. Of course, it will only work if people use it, so I'm trying to get the word out to as many Everquest sites as possible. Please, if you are working on the quests and want to make trades of drops, come to The Twilight Exchange and post what you've got, and what you want!

http://pub59.ezboard.com/btwilightexchange
