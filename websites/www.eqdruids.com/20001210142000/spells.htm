Spells are why a druid exists...you have the coolest spells in the game and can cast in any given situation.  DD, OD, buff, travel, utility - druids have them all.   Remember that most spells are very hard to cast the level you make them and grow in power for 3-5 levels.

THE MOST IMPORTANT thing to do at all spell levels is to MAX each skill at EVERY level if possible.  The one to focus on maxing is conjuration.  Whirling Wind can be cast in a small group turning the monster to you and damaging it. If you do not keep your Conjuration maxxed out - when you hit level 14 you had better get ready to summon a pile of water.

Alteration - healing and some buffs
Abjuration - some buffs and shields
Evocation - burn baby burn
Conjuration - Wind , Swarm (DOTS), and summon items
Divination - Camouflage line of spells

AE - Area Effect

DS - Damage Shield

Spell Locations:

Rank 1,2,3,4 all druid guilds - Kelethin, Sure Fall Glades, and Rivervale
Rank 5 and 6 - Karana Ring
Rank 7 and 8 - Steamfont
Rank 9 and 10 - Butcherblock
Rank 11- Lavastorm
Ring Spells - West Commons
Circle and Evac Spells - Tox Forrest

Best way to get Rank 5 spells...buy Ring of Commons before 19...cast it when hitting 19...Ring to WC, buy Karana Ring, Ring to Karana - buy Rank 5, Ring back to WC to buy other 2 rings if you want...

The Rings generally contain Rank 5 on up spells.  The guilds do still carry some of these as well as a gypsy or two.
