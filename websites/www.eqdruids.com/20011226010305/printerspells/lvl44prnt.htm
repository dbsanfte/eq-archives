Level Forty Four Spells

 

Storm Strength: 

  • Alteration 

  • Mana cost 85 

  • Effect:  Increases your Str

Upgrade to your strength buff spells.  This spell increases your str by 32 points.  Very nice, and lasts a long time.

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Back to top

Resist Disease:  

  • Abjuration 

  • Mana cost 50 

  • Effect:  Upgraded Resist Disease

This is your upgrade for disease resistance.  This spell will add 50 points to your disease resistance.

Purchase from:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Resist Poison:  

  • Abjuration 

  • Mana cost 50 

  • Effect:  Upgraded Resist Poison

This is your upgrade for poison resistance.  This spell will add 50 points to your poison resistance.

Purchase from:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Allure of the Wild:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 220
  • Effect:  Upgraded Charm Animal

Next upgrade to your charm animal spells.

Purchase from:  Druid Guild

Savage Spirit:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 100 
  • Effect:  Pet Buff

This is an upgrade to the Frenzied Spirit spell. It adds a little attack to the animal pet thus making it hit more often.

Chloroplast:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 200 
  • Effect:  Upgraded HP Regeneration 

This is an upgrade to Regeneration.  You now will heal 10 hps per tick for about 15 mins.  Definitely replace Regeneration with this spell.

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Banish Summoned: 

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 225
  • Effect:  Upgraded Direct Damage to Summoned

Upgrade to your line of Direct Damage to Summoned spells.  This spell will do 585 damage per cast on summoned creatures.

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Drifting Death:  

  • Conjuration
  • Mana Cost 200
  • Effect:  Upgraded DOT Spell

Another upgrade to your Damage over Time spells.  This one does 650 hps of damage over approximately one minutes time. 

Purchase From:  Butcherblock Druid Circle

Form of the Great Wolf:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 135
  • Effect:  Upgraded Wolf Form 

This is an upgrade to your wolf form spells.  This is self only.

The spell adds an extra 62 to attack at level 45. Also grants ultravision and is noticeably faster than Sow.

Purchase From:  Kelethin Druid Guild

Nullify Magic:  

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 50
  • Effect:  AC and Fire Resist Debuff

An upgrade to the cancel magic spell, it will strip the caster of all enchantments or remove a single enchantment off another target. 

Has a lower chance of resist.

Purchase From:  Rivervale or Surefall Druid Guild

Fixation of Ro:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 100
  • Effect:  Area Effect AC and Attack Debuff

This is an upgrade from Ro's Fiery sunder.  Does AC debuff and also reduces the mobs Attack.  It is an area effect spell.

Spell drops from Shardwurms and in Velketor's Labrynth in Great Divide

Calefaction:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 250
  • Effect:  Direct Damage Spell

This is a nice spell, casts fast!  Does 440-450 direct fire damage.

Downside is, it takes 45 seconds for it to be ready again, so make sure you have another spell ready.

Purchase From:  Firiona Vie Druid Vendor

Circle of Cobalt Scar:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Teleport group to Cobalt Scar

Teleports group to Cobalt Scar.  Each person riding must have a dragon's tooth from Cobalt Scar, otherwise they will be left behind.

Purchase From:  Philip the Fawn in Wakening Lands

Circle of Wakening Land:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Teleport group to Wakening Land

Teleports group to Wakening Land.  Each person riding must have tooth from either Wakening Land or Cobalt Scar

Again, be careful porting into this zone, there are lots of mobs wandering near.

Purchase From:  Philip the Fawn in Wakening Lands

Succor Lavastorm:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 300
  • Effect:  Group Evacuation

Another evacuation spell.  This spell takes your group to Lavastorm.  Same limitations apply as before.

Purchase From:  Toxxulia Druid Circle
