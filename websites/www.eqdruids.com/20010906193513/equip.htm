Equipment is a prime motivator for playing.  With armor limitations and cost of items, it is important to maximize your equipment usage for your level.  Save that money at all times.  Spells are hideously expensive starting at level 14 and only get worse. However by your early twenties you can make enough cash to buy some toys...make sure you have enough to buy your spells!

Items in BOLD have a + wisdom component, trade only means its value is in excess of 20,000pp on most servers and will only trade for a like valued item...

These are the formulas at level 50 and 200 defense:

Item AC * 1.55 = Addition to Total AC
(round to nearest whole number, 0.5 round down)

Item Agi / 3 = Addition to Total AC (round as above)

Item Sta * 5/2 = Addition to Total HP (round as above)

Ex. Putting on a Foreman's Tunic (AC 10) boosts total AC by 15. Azure Sleeves (AC 12) boosts total AC by 19.
Putting on Agi +6 or +7 item only adds 2 to total AC.
Putting on Sta +2 item adds 5 to HP.  Sta+5 item adds 12 to HP.
The above results are based on my own testing, with Agi 100 and AC in the690-700 range.
