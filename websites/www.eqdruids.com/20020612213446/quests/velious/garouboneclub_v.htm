Garou Bone Club

Belix Misteyes, a gnoll located near the Tower of Frozen Shadow talks about the many dangerous creatures in the area. If you ask him about them, he will tell you that the most dangerous of all the creatures is Garou. If you keep asking for more information, Belix tells you that if you bring him the skin of Garou and a cougar bone, he will reward you.

The cougar bone is called Bloody Cougar Bone (MAGIC NO DROP LORE) and drops off a named cougar named Midnight. Midnight is black rather than the white of normal snow cougars and is a pretty common spawn around the tower of frozen shadow.

Garou's Skin can be found on a named spawn named Garou, who appears to be some sort of gnoll effected by lycanthropy. (He dropped a dire wolf pelt in addition to the quest item when killed). He was near the Tower of Frozen Shadow when I saw him. His skin looks like a cap (AC 4 and positive and negative stats) - not sure if it can actually be worn or not.

Returning the Garou Skin and Bloody Cougar Bone to Belix results in the Garou Bone Club.
