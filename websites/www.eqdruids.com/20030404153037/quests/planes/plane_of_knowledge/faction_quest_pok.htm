Experience Quest in Plane of Knowledge

 

Trep Thilcan gave this quest when I hailed him.

Collect 6 items and place in the box he gives you.


1) Purification tablet
Freeport from Krystin Charcoal


2) Keg beer
Qeynos from Crow or from Tanlyn Galliway. She is in North Qeynos  In Galliway Inn


3) Ball of twine (large ball of twine is what I bought)
Shadeweaver from Saren Kaja


4) Bundle weapons
F.V. from Dom Pathfinder


5) Assorted Armor
Thurg from Terman Underbelly


6) Case of meat
Bazaar from Gaelsori Heriseron

Each item cost me around 7pp with the exception of the purification tablet.


I combined in the box Trep gave me and handed this to him.


He gave me a small experience hit and 60pp. (no faction)
