Bladed Weapons

 

The Druid guildmaster, "Heartwood Master," wishes to rid the world of bladed weapons, because they're forbidden by Tunare.

Bring him a Rusty Short Sword, a Rusty Long Sword, a Rusty Broad Sword, and a Rusty Bastard Sword, and he'll reward you with faction, around 6 gp, XP, and a random weapon.
