Silver Gilded Tiny Skull Earring (L. Fay)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                              Armor, Ear

Armor Class                           5

Boosts                                   +20 HP, +20 MANA, +6 DR

Effect                                     Force with 5 charge(s).

Weight & Size                       WT: 0.2  Size: TINY

Class                                      ALL

Race    HUM, BAR, ERU, ELF, HIE, HEF, DWF, HFL, GNM

Talk to Nojas Blackfist who can be found in Kael Drakkal. He asks you to obtain for him three Sifaye Heads and one Sifaye Knight Wing, all of which can be found in the Wakening Lands. Once you have obtained the items, hand them over to Nojas and you'll be rewarded with the Silver Dragon Tattoo.

Note: This item can fit in the Arms, Shoulder, Face and Wrist slot.
