I wanted to clarify some items about the various spells in Velious.

PORTALS

The teleports to ICECLAD are available in the North Karana (and other) Gyspsy Camp

The teleports to Great Divide, Wakening Lands, and Cobalt Scar are available in the Fawn Tower in Wakening Lands on the second floor...I believe the Western Wastes portal is mob dropped

Iceclad Teleport is not safe safe and you will be attacked by some level 30ish dervishes and will need to run off from em

Great Divide Teleport is fairly safe - there are some mid 20s tizmaks close by

Wakening Lands teleport is NOT SAFE.  Woushi (a dragon!) is up every 24 hours even if killed...it is advisable to find out if Woushi is up before porting.  You can port in and run like hell - but she might get you =)

Cobalt Scar port is NOT SAFE as there are wyvern very close to the ring and generally agro - you can port invisible people in with no problem.

PORTAL KEYS

In each 'dragon ring' there is a fang on the ground that respawns every minute.

Before you can port to a Velious Portal you must have the fang or a DEEPER FANG than the portal.  This must be in each party members inventory

This means that to portal to Great Divide you must have the Great Divide Fang (and everyone else) or a deeper one such as wakening lands.

You do NOT need any lesser teeth - so if you have the Cobalt Scar tooth you can destroy GD,WL, and Skyshrine...

GENERAL SPELLS

Fury of Air is sold in Thurgadin and by the gnomes in Iceclad

Fixation of Ro drops off of guardian wurms in Great Divide

Fixation of Ro and  Improved Superior Camoflage drop from Crystalline Wuems in Great Divide

All the other spells drop off level 55+ mobs in any Velious zone.

If you find a mob that is always dropping a certain spell please email it to me!
