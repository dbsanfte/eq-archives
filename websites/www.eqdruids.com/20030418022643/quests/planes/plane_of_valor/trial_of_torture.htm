Trial of Torture

 

You can only do this quest with a single group. Completing this quest (or any of the other trials) will give you access to the Plane of Storms and the Plane of Valor

Talk to Mavuin in the basement cellblock. Hail him and follow the dialogue. He will ask you to plead his case before the Tribunal, aggreeing will grant you a character flag.

Head to the area with The Tribunal, and ask them about Mavuin. Following the dialog, saying "I am prepared" to each Tribunal will give you description of the six trials.

To do this one, say "I am ready to begin the Trial of Lashing". Make sure you are actually prepared when you say that because your group will be teleported to the event area where you will only get a few seconds before things start.

When you start this trial, you're staring at a T intersection, if you run around to the left you'll see a cubby, and you'll realize that you are in a hallway surrounding a center room. There is a door to the center room on the opposite side from where you started the trial. 4 mobs spawn and two come from each side doMl the hallway. Mez and kill one at a time as fast as possible. These were shades and razorbeasts and hit for 280-300ish. After you kill all 4, if you were fast, a Wraith of Agony will spawn in the middle room, kill wraith (similar to ~e other mobs) and an ae heal spell will go off. Rinse and repeat for 4 rounds to reach the boss. The Boss is similar to the boss of execution, hits for 700s with an added twist of a 100 hp AE every 9 seconds that has a small knockback to interrupt spells.

Finishing this gives you the Mark of Torture.

Now talk to the Tribunal and say "what evidence of Mauvuin" and they will tell you that they have consented to hear Mauvuin's case. Head back down and talk to him and he will ask you to go see Karana and Mithaniel Marr. This will flag you to be able to enter the Plane of Storms and and Plane Valor.
