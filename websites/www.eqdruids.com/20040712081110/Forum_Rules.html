Forum Rules

 

These are the rules for posting and maintaining an account on EQDruids forums.  This list is subject to change without notice.

 

By posting on the EQDruids forums you agree to the following:

 

No personal attacks

No spam (messages with no value other than to increase post count)

No advertisements.  If you have something you would like to announce to the EQDruids community please send it to

SirAzrael999@hotmail.com for review.

Please don't post links in an attempt to gain value in some online game (e.g. outwar)

Posting of pornographic or obscene materials is not allowed.
Signature size should be no bigger then 550 wide by 250 high, and a max of 150 kb for the file size, though smaller is always better :)

 

Decisions on the operation of these forums are at the discretion of the administrators.  Those decisions are final.

 

Anything that is not covered by the above will be dealt with on a case by case basis by the administrators. 

 

Violations of the above will be met with by warnings up to removal of posting privileges on the EQDruids forums.

 

Any questions or needs for further clarification can be sent to SirAzrael999@hotmail.com
