The expansion spells are either sold at the FV vendor or are rare random drops in the specified dungeons.  The Druid Quest in FV is giving a spell to the druid there who wants one of 4 spells. Can result in : Upheaval, Glamour of Tunare, Egress, and Call of Karana
The following zones drop spells:
Old Sebilis, Howling Stones, Chardok, Burning Woods, Skyfire, Trakanon - only 55+ mobs can drop the high level spells
