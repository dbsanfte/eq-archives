Journeyman's Boots (Rathe Mountains)

Recommended level: 35

 

Haten Bootstrutter in Rathe Mountains wants a few items in exchange for his extra pair of journeyman boots.

He wants the following items:

  • a shadowed rapier (NODROP / NORENT)
  • a Ring of the Ancients (NODROP)
  • 3,250 gold coins

The Ring of the Ancients can be looted off the Ancient Cyclops. He can be found in two zones: The Ocean of Tears and The Southern Desert of Ro. The AC in OOT is a more regular spawn, but also more highly contested, and of a higher level than the AC in South Ro.

The shadowed rapier can be looted off Shadowed Men in various zones. The best one I've found is the Shadowed Men camp in Innothule Swamp, since it's only 2 zones away. The rapier is not a common drop, and is NORENT. Thus, it is highly recommended that this is the last piece you attempt to obtain.

Hasten wants 3,250 gold pieces. He will not accept 325 platinum pieces. You will be encumbered carrying all that gold, so try to get a Spirit of Wolf and a strength-buff before the final hand-in.

Hasten shares a spawn table with three other spawns: a Drake (lvl 24-27), Maldyn the Unkempt (lvl 33-35) and Brother Zephyl (lvl 60).
