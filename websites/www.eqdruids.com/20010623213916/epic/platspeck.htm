2. Find Merdan Fleetfoot in Surefall Glades.  Give him the rose and you will recieve the wooden painting.

3. Find the Human Skeleton in Frontier Mountains.  The best way to find him is get a Necro or other class that can Sense Dead.  He is the only one in the zone so a necro or such class can lead you right to him.  Hand him the painting and he will give you a Silver Chained locket.

4. Find Niera Farbreeze in Surefall Glades.  Hand her the locket and you will recieve the platinum speckled powder.
