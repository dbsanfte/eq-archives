Level Sixty Two Spells

Printer Friendly Version of Spell List

All the below spells (with the exception of Imbue Storm) are found by turning in a Ethereal Parchment to Wanderer Astobin in the Plane of Knowledge Library.  They are completely random in which spell you will receive.

 

Imbue Storm

 

1: Summon Gray Diamond of Storms

Classes: Dru (L62), Enc (L62)

Requires Raw Diamond as a reagent
Requires Storm Rider Blood as a reagent

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: Self
Beneficial: Beneficial

Resistance Check: None

Mana Cost: 1000
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

When you cast: You draw the essence of storms into the gem.
 

To receive this spell, you need to do the following:

 

In a Planar Jewelers Kit combine:

 

A Raw Diamond, a scroll of Imbue Diamond, and Storm Rider's Blood together.  No Fail Combine.

 

The scroll of Imbue Diamond is purchased from Locarme Zennix in the Temple of Marr (North Freeport).

 

The Storm Rider's Blood drops off Koka`vor Giants in the Plane of Storm
