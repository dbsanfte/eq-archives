Eye of Trust

Talk to Examiner Malikin who can be found in Sanctus Seru. He will tell you about how they keep the city safe and secure, and if you want, he can give you some jobs to do.

He will hand you a Heavy Coin to take to Percontorius Ontu [-125, -700]. Hand her the coin and she will return it to you and tell you to bring it back to whom gave it to you.

Return to Examiner Malikin and hand him the coin. He will smile and will then hand you back the same coin and tell you to bring it to his other friend. Hand the heavy coin to Consis Sinsolus [675, 775] and you'll receive the Mark of the Hand in return.

Return to Examiner Malikin and hand him the Mark of the Hand and he will return the coin to you as well as a vial of poison. He then tells you that you've passed his first two tests perfectly and that now he needs your help. He tells you that there is a traitor within the ranks, named Ornis Tranum. He tells you to enter Ornis' house, and pour the poison into his ear. He will be unable to move, so just place the Mark of the Hand on his body and leave.

The best way to gain entrance to the house is using invis. Hand the poison to Ornis and then hand him the coin.

Now return to Examiner Malikin and hand him the empty poison vial. He will tell you that he has one last task you for, and hands you another poison vial.

You must locate Erallic [175, -1950] in Katta Castellum and hand him the poison vial when he's sleeping. He will die and then run back to Malikin and hand him the empty poison vial and this time you'll be rewarded with The Eye of Trust.
