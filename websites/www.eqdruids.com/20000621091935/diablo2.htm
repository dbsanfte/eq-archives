My quick thoughts on D2 ST

First Impressions:

Great Interface to Battle.net and D2 itself
Warrior class might actually be worth playing (unlike original)
Very fun to play and fast paced
Items are much more varied and detailed
Graphics are so so - I guess Im used to games that make my Geoforce cry like a beat wolf in Qeynos Hills...D2 doesn't even make it think about it (640x480 is only mode)
Battle.net servers have a ways to go...doh!
The game engine is SUPER stable
Trade windows and vendors are much improved
No need to carry gold in stacks - its in its own column now
No need to carry TP and Ident scrolls - they stack in this 2 slot book thingy
Waypoints are gonna rock
Quests actually seem to matter
The mercenary/helper thing is quite cool
More players makes the game monsters a little tougher - 8 players makes them alot tougher
Can put level limits in open games
The skill system is so cool - will mean that 2 level 30 warrios are gonna be completely different.  Check out www.diabloii.com for some more good info.

Compared to EQ:
Fast game play - great rewards, no down time - EQ is gonna loose a lot of players for a few months - but I don't think they will actually have cancellations.  Diablo 2 is more like the ultimate 'thumb cheese' game...the guilds, massive servers, economies, great developer communication, blah blah blah will still make EQ the main OLRPG for a while...until a new one comes out of the same or better scale.
