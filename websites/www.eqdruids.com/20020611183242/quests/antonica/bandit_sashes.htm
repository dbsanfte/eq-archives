Bandit Sashes

Chesgard Sydwen, in the house of thunder, is willing to reward those who help eliminate the bandits in the Karanas.

Bring him a bandit sash and he will give you exp, faction, some decent gold and an item (often bronze).

You get the following faction changes: Knights of Thunder, Priests of Life and Guards of Qeynos go up and Bloodsabres goes down.
