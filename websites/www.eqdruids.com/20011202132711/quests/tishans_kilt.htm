Tishan's Kilt (Temple of Sol)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                              Armor, Legs

Armor Class                           5

Boosts                                   +4 STR, +4 STA, +20 HP

Effect                                     None

Weight & Size                        WT: 0.8  Size: ?

Class                                      WIZ, DRU

Race    HUM ERU HIE DEF GNM

Talk to Vilissia who lives in the Temple of Solusek Ro. She says that you must search out and find a Shadowed Ball and bring it to Trankia. To obtain the Shadowed Ball you must kill the Shadow Men who can be found all over Norrath. Remember that the Shadowed Ball will disappear from your inventory if you log out! Once you receive this item head to Everfrost and talk to Trankia. Give her the Shadowed Ball and you will receive a note. She now wants you to find evidence of her brother's death. Head to Upper Guk and kill the Shinta, he is found in the mine shaft past the zone to Lower Guk (Dead Side). Kill him to get the Barbarian's Head. Give the head back to Trankia and you will receive another note. She tells you that Martar Iceberg betrayed her brother and she now wants him dead, kill him and grab the Warthread Kilt. Hand in the two notes and the kilt to Trankia to receive the Tishan's Kilt.
