Level Fourteen Spells

Invigor: 

  • Alteration 

  • Mana cost 20 

  • Effect:  Restores Fatigue over time

Adds to you fatigue bar - great for underwater excursions and running around - works over time

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Skin like Rock:  

  • Abjuration 

  • Mana cost 60 

  • Effect:  Adds 7 AC and HPS

This is a very nice buff.  Adds 7 AC to you or your target, and increases hps 54 at lvl 14 up to a max of 90.  Keep this on yourself and main tank at all times.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Spirit of the Wolf:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 40
  • Effect:  Increases your speed and decreases your fatigue.

Many players will flat out say that this is the best spell in the game. I will not disagree. It adds SIGNIFICANTLY to your overall speed. No longer can monsters run you down! You will run circles around them. Better yet, groups which were pretty indifferent to adding a Druid to their ranks now get considerably more excited once they have been exposed to the benefits of SoW. This spell probably adds more to your character's survivability than any other spell.  This spell has an extremely long duration, longer than any other buff spell we have yet. It’s a good thing to because if you want to cast it on yourself and several party members it takes the majority of your mana. However it is an outdoor casting spell only. 

This spell is one of your main spells.  Keep it handy at all times!

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Expulse Summoned:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 60 
  • Effect:  Direct Damage spell - Summoned only

This is your upgraded DD spell against summoned creatures.  It will do 94pts of damage per cast.  Much more mana efficient again, when going up against summoned creatures.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Cascade of Hail:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 62 
  • Effect:  Area Effect Rain Damage 

An Area of Effect RAIN spell. Does about 60 damage over a span of 10 or 15 seconds. Like Invoke Lightning the area of effect is very small and you need to make sure the monsters are "shoulder-to-shoulder". Oddly enough the graphic for the spell looks more like a rain of fire than it does a cascade of hail. It costs 62 mana to cast, so it is more mana efficient that Invoke Lightning, but it takes much longer to recover from casting. However, unlike lightning it is not an outdoor-only spell.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Stinging Swarm:  

  • Conjuration
  • Mana Cost 65
  • Effect:  Damage over Time (DOT)

A Damage Over Time (DOT) spell. This is an awesome spell. This spell does about 117 pts of damage (if you have your Conjuration skill fully trained) over about a 54 second's worth of time when cast on myself. It only costs 65 mana to cast, so the mana:damage ratio is very good. 

Combined with Spirit of Wolf this spell is wonderfully effective. You can cast the spell on a monster and lead it on a merry chase, stopping every 30 seconds or so to recast it. It is absolutely essential that you max out your Conjuration skill before you get serious about using this spell often however. At 80 Conjuration skill it still fizzles 5-10% of the time. 

Note some sites list this spell as outdoors-only, but that is NOT true. It can be cast indoors as well. 

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

See Invisible:  

  • Divination
  • Mana Cost 25
  • Effect:  See invisible players and mobs

This is a very simple spell.  When you cast it on yourself, or others, you will be able to see invisible players or mobs.

The way you know someone is invisible, is their name will be in parentheses e.g. (Spiritual) vs. Spiritual

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Levitate:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 30
  • Effect:  Levitate yourself and others

This is really not true levitation.  This is more of a slow your fall kinda spell.  You will stay floating above the ground, but if you step off a cliff or such, you will slowly float down.

It is a very handy spell to have though.  You will use it in many situations.  It does need one batwing per cast, and the batwing is used up.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Halo of LIght:  

  • Conjuration
  • Mana Cost 40
  • Effect:  Conjures Head armor

Another simple spell.  It conjures a halo that can be worn on your head.  It has AC 3 and is a light source.  It is no drop.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Befriend Animal:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 70
  • Effect:  Charm Animal (make pet)

This spell is one of those hard to love spells.  It does charm animals, and makes that animal your pet for a short duration.  Unfortunately the duration is short, and when the charm breaks, the animal normally attacks you.

Try it out on a green mob before thinking it will be of great use to you.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Summon Food:

  • Conjuration
  • Mana Cost 10
  • Effect:  Summon Food

Simple enough spell.  It will summon stacks of food (bread).  This food is no rent, but can be given to other players.  It will disappear from your inventory 30 minutes after you logoff.

Its a nice spell in the fact that you will never go hungry, but its more convenient normally to forage food, or to buy some rations in my opinion.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Summon Drink:

  • Conjuration
  • Mana Cost 10
  • Effect:  Summon Drink

Simple enough spell.  It will summon stacks of Drink (water).  This drink is no rent, but can be given to other players.  It will disappear from your inventory 30 minutes after you logoff.

Its a nice spell in the fact that you will never go thirsty, but its more convenient normally to forage water, or to buy some water in my opinion.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild

Bind Affinity:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 100
  • Effect:  Set Bind Point

Finally you have the companion spell, to Gate.  You can now cast this on yourself and set your bind point.  Self casting, you can bind almost any area.  Casting on another person in your party (must be in party) you can bind them in towns only.

You will use this spell a lot.  But don't memorize it, until you need it.  Make sure when binding that you bind in a safe area also.  Nothing worse than being killed, and zoning in on a Sand Giant.

Purchase at:  Druid Guild
