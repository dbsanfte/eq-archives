Orc Runner

 

This quest is given by Dill Fireshine after you do the orc hatchet quest.

 

You need to kill the orc runner and return his damaged pouch to Dill Fireshine. He will also ask you for a note which can sometimes be found on the orc runner.

 

The orc runner can be found in Butcherblock. He runs around a lot, but the easiest place to find him is by the two cabins hunting area.

 

The faction changes are as follows:

 

Faction with Faydarks Champions, King Tearisthex, Clerics of Tunare and Soldiers of Tunare gets better, and Crushbone Orcs gets worse.

 

You sometimes get a pretty nice reward for this (sometimes worth several platinum), and a decent experience boost.
