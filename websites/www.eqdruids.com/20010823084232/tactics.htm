This section covers some the spell, combat, and combination tactics that are much more efficient than bashing a monster and healing up afterwards.

Please take careful notice of each of your skill levels and keep them maxxed as best you can through the various levels.  A higher INT helps skills go faster - but NOT at the loss of a more important skill such as Wisdom or Agility.

One of the most important concepts in leveling to me is insurance - after you make a level, keep killing EXACTLY what you were doing until you have a full bubble in your new level...then you can move on and try new things.  The quickest way to die is to rush out at a new level and try to thwack a new mob in a new area.
