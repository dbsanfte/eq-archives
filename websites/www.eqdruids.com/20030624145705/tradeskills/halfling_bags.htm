Leatherfoot Bags

 

Reinforced Cobalt Drake Hide Armors

  

Halfling tailors have a wonderful new set of bags they can make.  All the below bags are 100 % weight reduction!  Which means whatever you put into the bag will weigh NOTHING!

These bags are useable by all, but Halflings are the only ones that can do the combine in the Vale Sewing kit.

The recipes are as follows:

Wrist Pouch - 1 woven fur, 1 infused platinum thread, acorn oil (trivial 160+)

Leatherfoot Wrist Pouch, Wt: 0.1 2 GIANT slots

Small Pouch - 2 woven furs, 1 infused platinum thread, acorn oil (trivial 160+)

Leatherfoot Small Pouch, wt 0.2, 4 GIANT slots

Shoulder Bag - 3 woven furs, 2 infused platinum threads, acorn oil (trivial 200+)

Leatherfooot Shoulder Bag, wt 0.4, 6 GIANT slots

Haversack - 4 woven furs, 3 infused platinum threads, acorn oil (trivial 250+)

Leatherfoot Haversack, wt 0.6, 10 GIANT slots

Woven furs are made by the following process:

You need 3 foraged dire wolf fur tufts.  These can be foraged in several places in Velious including Kael.

Combine the 3 foraged dire wolf fur tufts and your embroidering needle in your Vale sewing kit to receive a patch of dire wolf fur.

Now take 3 patches of dire wolf fur, combine them for a woven dire wolf fur.

To make infused platinum threads you do the following:

First you need to make a celestial temper:

  • Celestial Temper - blue diamond dust + purified water

  • Blue Diamond dust - blue diamond combined with acid in a kiln.

  • Purified water - 2 water flasks + celestial essence

  • Celestial essence - celestial solvent + high level rune/word/page in a mortar and pestle

Then combine the celestial temper and platinum thread to make the Infused Platinum Thread.
