Replenishment

 

1: Regeneration for 25 hitpoints per tick

Classes: Dru (L61)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All
Beneficial: Beneficial

Range to Target: 100.00 feet

Resistance Check: None
Base Difficulty: 5%

Mana Cost: 275
Spell Duration: 19.3 mins @ level 61 to 20.5 mins @ lvl 65
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: You begin to regenerate.
Spell cast on someone: An orc centurion begins to regenerate.
Spell fades: You have stopped regenerating.

 

Purchase from Wanderer Astobin in the Great Library of Planes of Knowledge.  He's on the first floor, loc is 10, 1050
