Validus Scout Cloak

Celris Shadetracker asks for a skin of liquid being transported from the Vampyre Aerie in Tenebrous Mountains to a Vampyre Settlement in Maiden's Eye. There is a Vampyre named Vellar Toranal (approximately 54th level) in Maiden's Eye who drops a blood like substance wineskin. Turn that in to Celris and he will reward you with a cloak.

Nice item for a ranger, not so nice for a druid
