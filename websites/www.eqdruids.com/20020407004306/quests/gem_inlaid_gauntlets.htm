Gem Inlaid Gauntlets (Qeynos)

 

Type, Slot                              Armor, Hands

Armor Class                            8

Boosts                                   +3 DEX, +3 STA

Effect                                     None

Weight & Size                        WT: 1.0  Size: ?

Class                                      ALL

Race                                      ALL

Talk to Umvera Dekash who can be found resting at the Crow's Tavern in North Qeynos. He will tell you that he is a librarian and is in need of help. He wants to learn more about the Tesch Val Gnolls and why they moved to Split Paw. He asks you to return with four scrolls and he will reward you with your efforts. Hunt the gnolls around and inside Split Paw as they all drop these scrolls as their rare loot. There are four different volumes so make sure you get one of each. Run back to Umvera and hand him all four scrolls, he will translate the text, hand you back the translated note, and then ask you to locate Jheron Felkis so that he can bind them together.

Head off to Freeport now. Jheron can be found on the third floor at the Velith and Bardo's establishment in East Freeport. Hand him the bag with the translated note and he will return the Splitpaw Tome to you. Now head back and hand the book to Umvera and he will reward you with a note, and tell you to hand it to Durkis Battlemore.

Head off to the Butcherblock Mountains and locate Durkis [2075, 400]. Hand him the note and he will in turn hand you the Gem Inlaid Gauntlets.
