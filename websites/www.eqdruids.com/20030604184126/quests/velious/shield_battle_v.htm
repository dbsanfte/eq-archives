Talk to Semkak who can be found within Kael Drakkal. He's trying to locate some of the lost scrolls of ancient knowledge. These scrolls can be found on various Dragon Constructs which live in the Dragon Necropolis. Obtain the Scroll of Scaled Tactics and return it to Semkak and you'll be rewarded with this shield.
