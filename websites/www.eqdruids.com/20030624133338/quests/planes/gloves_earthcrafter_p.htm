Talk to Tunarean Earthmelder who can be found within the Plane of Growth. He will tell you that the giants are trying to gain control of the lands of Tunare and that he needs help. If you agree to help him, he will tell you to kill the three priests that are trying to setup a new temple for their evil god, and return with their heads, as well as the one who is helping them. The priests you need to kill are Priest Grenk who roams near Kael Drakkal, Priest Bjek who roams near the Plane of Growth tower, Priest Delar who can be found roaming near the Dragon Circle, and Derakor the Vindicator who is deep within Kael Drakkal.

Note: This item can only be equipped by those that worship Tunare.
