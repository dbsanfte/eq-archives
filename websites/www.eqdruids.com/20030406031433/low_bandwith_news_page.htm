He just sent me this e-mail and I'm putting out the world to all of you :)

[Online MMORPG Study: New Findings and Surveys]

 

Over the past 3 years, I have done research on the psychology of MMORPGs. You can find my past research at: http://www.nickyee.com/hub/

http://www.nickyee.com/daedalus/

Important news for people doing spell research! 3/16/03

I do not have personal verification of this, but I have heard it from several reliable sources.

Some of the spells may be easier to obtain than we originally thought.

If the spell crosses over different classes (such as Levitation) you do not need to have the druid specific combines for this spell.

Word is that any of the different INT casters combines for this spell, also include druids on the scroll!  So if you can find the pieces for the Wizard version (to give an example) a wizard would be able to make the combine and then you could scribe that version of the spell!

Now before people get TOO excited at this thought, I would like to have someone actually verify this.  I know for a fact that the spell scrolls are showing druids for one of the classes, now someone just needs to verify that it can be scribed by a druid.  More than likely it is, but I want to know for sure :)

If you need a list of the components used by INT Casters, you can find a very good one at EQTraders.

The spells that this would effect are:  Everlasting Breath, Levitation and Banishment.
