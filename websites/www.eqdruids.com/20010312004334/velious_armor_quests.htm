There are tons of questions about the armor that can be made in velious. Ill try to shed some light on what is known so far.

Each race can make the armor - the dwarf faction armor is considered the easiest to make due to where the pieces drop.

So far the gems can drop from any level 55+ monster in Velious.  The most common place for these are Icewell Keep, PoG, Veliktor's, Dragon Necropilis, Sleepers Tomb, and the back parts of Kael and Skyshrine.  In addition the Haunted Sea Chests and Wyverns drop them in Cobalt Scar.  The sentient armor suits in Wakening Lands also drop gems rarely.

Currently the armor pieces drop for the dwarf armor deep in Kael.

See below for which gems go with what class of armor

For all intents and purposes this armor is for 55+ characters although large raids can get the pieces too...
