Cloak of Leaves (PoA)

 

Properties                            MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                             Armor, Back

Armor Class                          12

Boosts                                  +5 WIS, +30 HP, +30 MANA

Effect                                    None

Weight & Size                       WT: 0.0  Size: ?

Class                                     DRU

Race                                     ALL

 

Talk to Strandar Pinemist who can be found in the Plane of Air. He will ask you if you are ready for the Test of the Eagle. If you say yes, Fenalla Moonshadow will spawn and you can then talk to her. She will ask you to retrieve the following four items for her. 

·  White tipped Spiroc Feather

·  Acidic Venom

·  Ethereal Ruby

·  Spiroc Elder's Totem

Hand Fenalla the four items and you will receive the Cloak of Leaves as your reward
