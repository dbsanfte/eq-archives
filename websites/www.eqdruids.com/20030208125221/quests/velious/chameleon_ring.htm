Chameleon Ring

Sentry Kale is looking for someone to perform a task for him. Ask him what task and he will explain that the spiders have been eating the food supplies. Go out and kill the four crystal spiders that spawn in the zone and bring back their legs as proof. 2 are near the bank, 1 is in the fountain, and 1 is in a bedroom. They are on 2 hour spawn timers.

Most of the time you get some faction and coin, but sometimes he will give you a Chameleon ring.
