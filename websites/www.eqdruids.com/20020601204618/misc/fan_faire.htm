Fan Faire Seattle!

First the pictures!  I had taken a TON of pictures that I thought people would enjoy.  Mainly the different folks in some very very nice costumes.  Unfortunately there was an incident with the digital camera and all the pics were lost!  So for those that wanted to see their pictures here, I am sorry for that.  One of my server mates has promised to send me some copies of his pictures, so I will put those up, and anyone else that has some good pics of fan faire that they want to share with others, please send them to me and I will post them!

Now on to the thanks!  I was overwhelmed by the support for EQDruids that I was seeing at the Fan Faire.  From the folks that found me to tell me how much they liked the site, to all those that cheered when EQDruids was announced at dinner, to the folks that I was talking with at the airport waiting for our plane to be fixed.  You guys all make doing this worthwhile!  I wanted to make sure you all knew how much I appreciated your support!

Now for the fun stuff!  There were some companies that had demos of their new products, new companies coming out, panel discussions etc.  I'm going to try and remember them all, but if I forget anything important make sure to let me know!

White Wolf and EQ RPG - White Wolf was there doing a demo of the upcoming EQ Rpg.  The game is a hybrid game, based somewhat in the D&D rules.  I was very impressed by what I saw so far, and talking to some of the White Wolf people there, I think people will be very happy with this game when it is released.  Yes, there will be Druids in it!!  One of the more interesting ideas in it I thought, is there will be quests that will start in the dice game, then you will go into the online game to get information from an NPC, and then return to the dice game.  You won't have to be playing online at all to enjoy the game, but for those that will be doing both this is a nice feature.

To find out more on the EQ Rpg go here  There isn't a lot of info there yet, but more will be forthcoming.

Cymouse by Maui Innovative - This was a VERY cool item.  It is a new way to control your mouse in EQ, using your head.  You put on this head mounted control unit, and the sensor on your computer follows which way you are looking and allows you to navigate along EQ while still having the ability to type!

Maui Innovative is going to keep us informed as they get closer to a release date on the product (should be out in a little while).  For more info on this product, go here

There is going to be a new quarterly magazine for Everquest!  I talked to the authors of this upcoming magazine, and have made sure they know to let us help spread the word on it.  When more is available I will make sure you all are informed!

One of the best parts of the Fan Faire for me was being able to see the documentary Avatars Offline.  This documentary was done about the effects that the online gaming industry is having on people, business, the world etc.  Very interesting movie, and I would highly recommend everyone see it when it becomes available.  The producer is currently working on a deal to put it on DVD, and will be letting EQDruids know when it is available.  So keep an eye out! 

You can find more info on this documentary at their website here

Some EQ info.  They are still working on the caster's balancing issues.  VI wants to make sure that they changes things properly the first time, instead of having to fix things as they go along (I agree with this idea).  So don't think they have stopped working on such, they are working hard on things still!

The next Fan Faire has been announced for June in Boston!  So for all you East Coasters, start getting ready!

That is all that comes to mind at the moment.  I am fairly certain I have forgotten something, but I'm still recovering from the trip!

If you were at the Fan Faire and have some info that I haven't posted that you feel should be included, please feel free to send it to me!

Thanks again all!

 - Spiritual
