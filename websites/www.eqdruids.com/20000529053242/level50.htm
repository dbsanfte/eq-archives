PLANES OF FEAR AND HATE—

 SPELLS TO MEMORIZE

 Starfire- In the planes you nuke a LOT.

 Pack Chloroplast—Cast this one, so you can use a Manastone if you have one, or other part members can, two it cuts down on the healing job of a cleric after battle.

 Share wolf form—Casting share wolf form on your puller/s lowers the aggro range of the monsters. A must for a successful raid.

 Greater healing—Clerics have to heal a LOT in here, so they are lom or oom quite often, you need to make sure you are a safety net. (You may not think gheal is worth anything on the planes, but when my group pulled a dracoliche and tentacle terror in PoF, our cleric was oom and my heals were the only thing that saved one of our tanks =P)

 Shield of thorns- Great way to do extra dmg to monsters, should always cast this.

 Skin like nature-Cast this  on everyone, because they’ll all get swung at one time or another and you want as many hp/acs as possible.

 Resist Magic- A lot of the mobs in fear like to darkness (nightmares), and stun (gorgons), and fear (turmoil toads). This makes pulling for tanks VERY risky. However it has been my experience that when you’re without an enchanter to do group resist magic, casting resist magic on all of your party helps substantially

 (Note: Why no snare or dot, you ask? Well, snare is useless, because these mobs don’t run, and if they try, they have like 8 casters or so throwing their best nukes at them so they won’t get very far. No dot? Nope. There is a dot bug that sometimes causes monsters to poof upon death. That’s bad. No dots!)

 PLANE OF HATE—

 One would think that all of a druid’s skills would need to be utilized in a plane. Well that just isn’t the case =P Usually you have so many people, you can afford to make some mistakes. Basically, as long is no one is getting beat on, you nuke. Also its your job to keep the pullers in your group wolf formed. This also reduces aggro range, and in some cases if the person is agnostic makes them non-kos. Also keep pack chloroplast up at all times. (Note: Don’t try nuking stone golems, they resist everything but shaman dots)

 PLANE OF FEAR--  

 Same deal here.  I highly suggest binding to the safespot however. This has some really useful purposes.  For example, when my party pulled the dracoliche last night, we had 23 people, which was a skeleton crew.(no pun intended =P) We all barely managed to kill but, but most of us died. Now in the midst of the battle a tentacle terror also wandered over and was having its way with us, everyone was near oom or oom already, situation looked bad, BUT, about 5 of us were bound to the safespot, so as soon as we popped back into existence, we memmed our nukes and heals (heals if we had em) and raced over to the group, and were able to squeeze by and kill the TT. Then a couple wanderers came over and slaughtered us while we were all rezzing and oom, but that’s PoF for you =D Also, if a party member falls asleep or something, or goes LD, you can port back in and get them out. Sucks to be a tank stuck in the planes. But I’m sure the thought, “What if I die, and something is at the safespot and I just die over and over and over?” Well 9 times out of 10 there is nothing there, if you just pop up, gate out someplace safe its no big deal. In the unlikely case there is something there, don’t try to run, just /q if something is attacking you that way you don’t respawn and let it kill you again. Wait about 5 mins, login, port out. On a side note, you need to be prepared for a lot of death in the planes. Personally, my record has been 6 deaths in PoF. CT snipes people, pulls go bad, you get feared, and train stuff on the group. However let me say it was a LOT of fun, and with the 49 rezzes death is not so bad.
Oh yeah one last thing. If there is a bad pull. you CAMP. You do NOT under ANY circumstances evac. If you do so then you need t get everyone in again. It is easiet to just have the cleric camped there log in and rezz everyone. DO NOT EVEN MEM EVAC OR ANY GATE SPELL.
