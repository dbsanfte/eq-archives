Currently we know of only 4 spells that require a spell component.  Newbies keep those bat wings!

There is a vendor in a hut in East Commons that sells some of these spell components

Spell Component Where found Usage
Flame Lick - level 1 Fire Beetle Eye Fire beetles, klikniks, newbie area vendors, beetles keep one in bag - never used up
Endure Breath - level 9 Fish Scale fish :-) - some general vendors one per cast
Levitate - level 14 Bat Wing bats, newbie vendors one per cast
Immolate - level 29 Fire Beetle Eye Fire beetles, klikniks, newbie area vendors, beetles keep one in bag - never used up
Wake of Karana - level 56 Opal killing stuff used once per cast
Banishment - level 60 Star Ruby Jewel Vendor used once per cast
