Rohan's Brandy (Qeynos)

Recommended level: 1
 
This is an excellent and easy quest to raise factions with the good guards and merchants of Qeynos.

Captain Rohand is located in the Mermaids Inn by the docks in South Qeynos.

Hand him a bottle of brandy and you will receive some experience and the following faction adjustments:

    Factions Raised:

  • Antonius Bayle
  • Guards of Qeynos
  • Merchants of Qeynos

    Factions Lowered:

  • Circle of Unseen Hands
