Warden Symbol of Tunare

 

 

This is the third Tunare quest. You must do the first two before you can do this one.

You say 'I am ready to become a Warden of Tunare.

Yeolarn Bronzeleaf says 'The crate that the you recovered from the Courier contained this black candlestick that radiates an aura of great magical power. I request that you deliver the candlestick to Lady Trilani who is studying with the High Men in Erudin. Perhaps she can divine the nature of the candlestick and offer aid in defeating its power. The remaining contents of the crate have been given to Crusader Swiftmoon to be delivered to a gnome in Steamfont that is purchasing them on behalf of the Eldrich Collective. When you return be sure to present your Disciple Symbol to me with anything that Trilani may ask you to deliver.

 



 

Take the candlestick to Trilani Palrone. 

 

 

She is inside the Erudin Palace on the second floor in the Tower of the Craftkeepers.

 



Trilani Palrone says 'I sense a great evil power in this candlestick. I will need you to concoct a divinatory aid for me before I can discern more. Take this suspension and brew it in a brew barrel with one white hellebore (dropped by Gnolls in Split Paw or SK), a pouch of the red dust created by the Fire Peak Goblin Wizards, and the caustic substance used by the Dark Offerers in Mayong Mistmoores wicked castle.

Your faction standing with KeeppersoftheArt, KingTearisthex and FaydarksChampions got better. Your faction standing with TheDead got worse.

 

The pouch of red dust seems to drop off random goblins in Solusek's Eye. It seems like every goblin has a chance of dropping it.  This pouch is no drop.

The Caustic Substance drops off Dark Offerers in the castle in Mistmoore.  They seem to be the only ones that drop the substance.  The Substance is no drop.

 

The White Hellebore definitely drops inside of Split Paw.  It is a fairly rare drop so be prepared to do a little killing.  The White Hellebore is droppable, so you can ask people hunting inside if they have seen any drops also.

 

At this point you should have:

 

 

 

 

 

Take the ingredients and combine them in a brew barrel.  I am unsure if there is a skill check here or not.  You will receive back the Divinatory Concoction.

 

 

Take the result back to Trilani. She will give you Un-animation Powder.

 

 

Take this powder and your Disciple symbol back to Yeolarn:

Yeolarn Bronzeleaf says 'Praise Tunare!! I will have our sorcerers examine this power immediately to see if we can reproduce it in quantities enough to eliminate the undead plague. I award you the rank of Warden of Tunare, the All Mother smiles upon you Illilliae !'
