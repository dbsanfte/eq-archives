Reset Casting Specialization

In the Temple of Solusk Ro (off of Lavastorm) there is an NPC named Ostorm.

If you give him a ruby, he will reset all your specializations in spell casting back to 49.

When you are done, go outside, and start casting a spell that uses whatever specialization you want to master in over and over until you increase that specialization to 51.

Now you are done resetting your specializations.
