Careless Lightning:  

 

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 94 (L19) to 99 (L24)

Skill: Evocation
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 25

Mana Required: 70
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 2.75 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: Lightning surges through your body.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> has been struck by lightning.

Finally we get an update to our venerable Ignite spell. This direct damage spell can be cast indoors or out, and it costs 70 mana to cast. It does a range of damage from 2-99 pts. Basically if your target does not resist it will do 94, but if the target resists it will do significantly less. When it does max damage it is an awesome spell with a damage-mana ratio of 94:70, which is pretty good. This is also the spell used in Sol A and B until you get Ice at 49.

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Dizzying Wind:  

 

Effect:  Decrease Hit Points (HP) from 110 (L19) to 122 (L25)
Effect:  Stuns target

Skill: Conjuration
Casting Area Restriction: Outdoor
Allowable Targets: All

Range to Target: 200 feet
Pushes Target Back: 2 feet
Throws Target Up: 2 feet

Resistance Check: Magic + 25

Mana Required: 80
Spell Duration: Instant
Casting Time: 2.75 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds
Recast Delay: 12.0 seconds

Spell cast on you: You are slammed by an intense gust of wind.
Spell cast on someone: <Target> is slammed by an intense gust of wind.

This spell is an upgrade to your Whirling Wind spell. It costs 80 pts mana to cast but does a whopping 122 pts of damage. Unfortunately it keeps the weaknesses of its predecessor. It either does max damage, or it does NO damage. Unfortunately if you cast it on monsters that con white to red, there is a very good chance it will be completely resisted. Unlike Careless Lightning, it will not do some lesser amount of damage when resisted, it is all or nothing. It is also an outdoor-only spell. The advantage of this spell is if it lands, it includes a short stun. 

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

This is your improved invisibility spell.  This spell will work indoors, and lasts a great deal longer.  Be careful though, this spells duration varies greatly!

Spell Duration: Instant

Ok, you've made it to 19!  Now you start receiving your ring teleports.  This is a great time to be a druid.  You will save so much time, you won't know what you used to do to go places.
