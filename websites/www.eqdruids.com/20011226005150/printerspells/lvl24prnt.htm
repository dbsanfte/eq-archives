Level Twenty Four Spells

Resist Fire: 

  • Abjuration 

  • Mana cost 50 

  • Effect:  Increases Fire Resistance

This is your first Fire Resistance spell.  It increases your fire resistance up to a maximum of 40 points of resistance.

Nice spell to use, due to the number of fire based casters in the game.

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Skin Like Steel:  

  • Abjuration 

  • Mana cost 100 

  • Effect:  AC and HP buff

This is your next spell in the Skin line of spells.  AC is increased 10, and your hitpoints are increased 74 (maxes out at 100 at lvl 50)

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Spirit of Cheetah:  

  • Abjuration
  • Mana Cost 20
  • Effect:  Speed speed and more speed

This spell takes 15 mins to memorize, and 15 minutes to recover after casting.  

Why use it you ask?  Because this spell causes you go into jet powered mode!  It only lasts for 30 seconds, but 30 seconds is enough time to normally leave any mob in the dust.

It is a self only spell.

Purchase Spell:  Kelethin Druid Guild

Dismiss Summoned:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 90 
  • Effect:  Direct Damage to Summoned

This is an upgrade to your direct damage to summoned spells.  This spell does 157 damage per cast, and is again more mana efficient when attacking summoned.

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Pogonip:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 125 
  • Effect:  AE Rain Spell 

For such a weird name, this is a fairly simple spell.  Its an Area Affect Rain Spell that will do three showers of damage up to 62 hps each time.

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Tremor:  

  • Evocation
  • Mana Cost 200
  • Effect:  AE Direct Damage

Tremor is a powerful spell that if used wrong will cause you to be killed in a heartbeat!

This is an area affect spell that radiates out from you in a circle.  So you can do up to 112-122 damage on anything standing near you.  The only problem is, if you don't kill all the mobs, you have probably just upset multiple mobs, and they will turn on you and smack you.

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Creeping Crud:  

  • Conjuration
  • Mana Cost 100
  • Effect:  DOT

This is your upgraded Damage over Time spell.  This one does 210 damage over time.  

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Ensnaring Roots:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 60
  • Effect:  Root target plus damage

This is your upgraded root spell.  This one will be resisted less, and also hold onto the mob longer.  The damage it does is 34 damage at lvl 24 and increases slightly as you level.

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Sun Beam:  

  • Divination
  • Mana Cost 40
  • Effect:  AE Blind

Simply put, this is an Area Effect blind spell.  If the spell hits, the targets will all be blinded, and run about without attacking you.

Be careful though, when blind wears off, you will be attacked normally.

Purchase Spell:  North Karana Druid Circle

Wolf Form:  

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 60
  • Effect:  Take the form of a wolf

Alteration buff/form change - 60 mana - NEVER let this one down - auto cast SoW, more attacks, more damage, and you look really cool. - Gives Infravision. 

You will move a little faster than sow in this form.  You will also con indifferent to many things that might have once wanted to kill you.

Some mobs do not like illusions, so be careful at first when you enter a new zone e.g. guards at Thurgadin

Purchase Spell:  Kelethin Druid Guild

Charm Animals:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 120
  • Effect:  Fears Animal

Updated Charm Animal spell.  Charm will last longer, and you will be able to charm higher mobs.

Purchase From:  Druid Guild

Rings of Ro, Feerot, Steamfont, Lavastorm and Combines:

  • Alteration
  • Mana Cost 150
  • Effect:  Self only teleport

More teleport spells!

Ring of Karana takes you to North Karana druid circle

Ring of Feerot takes you to Feerot druid circle

Ring of Steamfont takes you to Steamfont druid circle

Ring of Lavastorm takes you to Lavastorm Mountains druid circle

Ring of Combines takes you to Dreadlands druid circle

Purchase From:  Purchase Ro, Feerot, and Steamfont in West Commons.  

Ring of Combines can be purchase from Kelethin Druid Guild
