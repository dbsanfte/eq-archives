New message board for Luclin Quest Armor 2/6/02
I don't know anything about these folks, but thought you all might enjoy knowing about it :)

Announcing the Twilight Exchange!

For those who have been working on the Twilight Sea quests, with the
5 zones and hundreds of rare quest mobs & drops, I have started an
ezboard dedicated to being a pan-server marketplace and discussion
area for the quests. The idea is that most people will end up with
more uneeded drops than needed, so the board is there to make it easy
to arrange trades with people that have what you need and need what
you have.

There is a board for each server, plus areas for discussing the
quests. Of course, it will only work if people use it, so I'm trying
to get the word out to as many Everquest sites as possible. Please,
if you are working on the quests and want to make trades of drops,
come to The Twilight Exchange and post what you've got, and what you
want!

http://pub59.ezboard.com/btwilightexchange

Media Advisory

Sony Online Entertainment expands subscription offerings for EverQuest.

Sony Online Entertainment is pleased to announce EverQuest Legends, a new Premium service available for EverQuest subscribers. With over 400,000 monthly subscribers playing EverQuest, SOE is confident that the EverQuest Legends service will appeal to a select group of players who are looking for an incredible new take on the world of Norrath. Some revolutionary features include the ability to track your player’s history, items, and an amazing map of Norrath that allows you to keep track of your location and the location of guild mates. The EverQuest Legends server will be rich in quests, unique weapons and armors that are not available on any other server, hosted by a dedicated and exclusive assembly of GM’s.

For an exciting preview of this service head on over to the following link and reserve your chance to make history as a member of EverQuest Legends.
