Level Sixty Four Spells

All the below spells are found by turning in a Spectral Parchment to Wanderer Astobin in the Plane of Knowledge Library.  They are completely random in which spell you will receive.

 

Savage Roots:

 

2: Root

Classes: Dru (L64)

Skill: Alteration
Allowable Targets: All
Beneficial: Detrimental

Range to Target: 200.00 feet
Area Effect Range: 30.00 feet

Resistance Check: Magic

Mana Cost: 75
Spell Duration: 45 ticks (4.5 minutes)
Duration Formula: 8
Casting Time: 2.50 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.50 seconds

Spell cast on you: Your feet become entwined.
Spell cast on someone: An orc centurion is entrapped by roots.
Spell fades: The roots fall from your feet.
