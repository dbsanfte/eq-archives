I don't want to make this quest page an "every quest in Everquest is listed here" type quest page.  I think Everlore , Eqmaps , and Allakhazam's do a very nice job in being the main source for all quests.

What I've tried to do is make a quest area that would be of particular interest to Druids.  All the quests listed below give an item that is druid useable.  I think that gives us a nice reference for when you are trying to upgrade some of your equipment, or when you are looking for a quest that has a reward that you can use.

I am sure, I have missed some quests.  There are a LOT of them out there and I don't think by any means did I find them all.  So, if you know of a quest that I haven't listed below that you feel would be a good addition for all of us, please submit it to me so I can add the quest for everyone!

One of the most asked for quests  I will link to here :)  It is the Velious Armor Quests.  The page shows all the armors, what you need to get them, and what stats they have!.
