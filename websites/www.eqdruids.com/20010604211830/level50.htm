PLANES OF FEAR AND HATE—

 SPELLS TO MEMORIZE

 Starfire- In the planes you nuke a LOT.

 Pack Chloroplast—Cast this one, so you can use a Manastone if you have one, or other part members can, two it cuts down on the healing job of a cleric after battle.

 Share wolf form—Casting share wolf form on your puller/s lowers the aggro range of the monsters. A must for a successful raid.

 Greater healing—Clerics have to heal a LOT in here, so they are lom or oom quite often, you need to make sure you are a safety net. (You may not think gheal is worth anything on the planes, but when my group pulled a dracoliche and tentacle terror in PoF, our cleric was oom and my heals were the only thing that saved one of our tanks =P)

 Shield of thorns- Great way to do extra dmg to monsters, should always cast this.

 Skin like nature-Cast this  on everyone, because they’ll all get swung at one time or another and you want as many hp/acs as possible.

 Resist Magic- A lot of the mobs in fear like to darkness (nightmares), and stun (gorgons), and fear (turmoil toads). This makes pulling for tanks VERY risky. However it has been my experience that when you’re without an enchanter to do group resist magic, casting resist magic on all of your party helps substantially
