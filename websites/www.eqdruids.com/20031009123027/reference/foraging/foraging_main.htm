Foraging Table

Druids excel at foraging. Of all the classes, only Rangers surpass us in this ability.

Only three classes receive this ability: Rangers (level 3), Druids (level 5), and Bards (level 12).

Two races have the innate ability to forage, the wood elves and the Iksar, so they can begin using this skill at level 1.

For most, the ability begins at a skill level of 50, and only Rangers and Druids may advance in skill level.  Bards begin at skill level 1 and cap at 55.

There are an amazing number of things that you will forage as you explore each zone.  The skill level to forage some of the rarer items has been reduced, so don't be surprised by what you can forage early on.

Below is a list of the items that are known to be foraged in the game. Items will continue to be added as they are found.  For more detailed information on what these items can be used for, visit EQ Traders Corner.

If you see anything that needs to be corrected or added, please contact me at Tesiux@eqdruids.com.
