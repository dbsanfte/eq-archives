A Giant Laceless Sandal

Type, Slot                           Armor, Shield

Armor Class                        15

Boosts                                +5 STA, -7 CHA

Effect                                  None

Weight & Size                     WT: 5.0  Size: ?

Class                                   WAR, CLR, PAL, RNG, SHD, DRU, BRD, ROG, SHM

Race                                   ELF, HIE, DEF, DWF, HFL, GNM

 

This item is dropped by Timber the Tiny, a named Hill Giant who is a very rare spawn in North Karanas. He drops this sandal as his rare loot and the Giant's Reminder String as his common.

This sandal is also used to complete the Soldevi Scimitar quest.
