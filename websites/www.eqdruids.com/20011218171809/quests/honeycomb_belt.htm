Honeycomb Belt (PoA)

 

Properties                            MAGIC ITEM  LORE ITEM  NO DROP  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                             Armor, Waist

Armor Class                          6

Boosts                                  +2 CHA, +8 WIS, +75 MANA

Effect                                    Haste

Weight & Size                      WT: 2.0  Size: ?

Class                                     DRU

Race    ALL

Talk to Strandar Pinemist who can be found in the Plane of Air. He will ask you if you are ready for the Test of the Bee. If you say yes, Fenalla Moonshadow will spawn and you can then talk to her. She will ask you to retrieve the following three items for her.

·  Efreeti Statuette

·  Divine Honeycomb

·  Wilder's Girdle

Hand Fenalla the three items and you will receive the Honeycomb Belt as your reward.
