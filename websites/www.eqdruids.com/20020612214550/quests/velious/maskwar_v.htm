Mask of War (Kael)

 

Properties                           MAGIC ITEM  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                            Armor, Face

Armor Class                         9

Boosts                                 +2 STR, +5 STA, +9 WIS

Effect                                   Yaulp II

Weight & Size                     WT: 0.2  Size: ?

Class                                    ALL

Race    ALL

Talk to Captain Bvellos and tell him "i will work". He will tell you of his suspicions that Wenglawks are trading information with the Coldain and Dragons. He would like to do something about this but he has no proof.

Head over to Dragon Necropolis and start killing the ratmen couriers. They will drop the proof needed to complete this quest. Return with the item and you will be rewarded with the Mask of War.
