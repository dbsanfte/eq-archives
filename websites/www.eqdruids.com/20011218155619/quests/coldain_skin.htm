Coldain Skin Gloves (Kael)

 

Properties                             MAGIC ITEM  QUEST RELATED  

Type, Slot                              Armor, Hands

Armor Class                           12

Boosts                                   +9 STA, +6 WIS, +6 INT, +20 HP, +20 MANA

Effect                                     None

Weight & Size                        WT: 1.5  Size: ?

Class                                      ALL

Race                                      ALL

 

Talk to Yetarr who can be found in Kael Drakkal. He will tell you that he does not trust the Kromrif to protect us against the Coldain. He wants to hire some mercenaries to seek our and slay key Coldain Leaders. He will reward you if you return with either the Royal Scribe's, or the Royal Huntsman's head, both of which are located within Icewell Keep. Turning in the head of the Royal Scribe will result in the Coldain Skin Gloves.
