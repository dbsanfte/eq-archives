End of another year...

Well we made it another year….

I wanted to personally say a few words to all the visitors here, and tell you what your support has meant to me.

For those that were not aware, this has been a very rough year for me.  I lost a friend to a suicide, marriage went south, had to have a fairly major surgery, found out I have a chronic illness, and a few other things.

What I don’t think enough folks realize, is that running EQDruids for all of you helped me through those times.  Knowing that we were doing something that people appreciated, and found important gave me a reason to keep moving along, instead of wallowing.

Letters of support like:

“Hi there. My main is currently a 19 Human Druid (don’t laugh, i like humans) and wanted to say that your site is the greatest. I’ve gone to other sites that are basic on classes, and none match yours. The best part of eqdruids, in my opinion, is the spell listings. You give us where to get them, what they do, etc. I visit the site al least once a day. Keep up the good work man, its great.”

“Thank you for all the hard work on your website.  It has helped me tremendously.  I am a first time Everquest player, and decided to play a Halfling Druid.  While playing the other night, another druid came up to me and gave me a few items.  I started to ask her some questions, and while answering them, she gave me the web address for eqdruids.

 Am I ever glad she did!  The site is perfect for me.  I have learned all about kiting for when I get the ability, which spells are more for show and which are functional, and good areas and tricks for leveling.  I also really benefited from that piece about sharpening the sword.  Now that my skills are maxed for the level, I really do notice a difference in fizzles, which saves my mana and health.”

“I'm usually too lazy to write something like this but in this case I felt I needed to do it.  Your web site is the BEST source for Druids, much more Everquest, that I have ever come across.  I used to use Everlore, but it is hard to navigate, confusing, and outdated.  EQDruids is simple to navigate, to the point, and an invaluable asset.  I really appreciate that there is someone out there like you who is dedicated to the site and its upkeep.  Thanks again and keep up the GREAT work!!”

Letters like these helped to make me remember why I run the site (yes, I keep all the letters!).  That constant support from people kept me going over a rough year.

We’ve seen some amazing growth in the site and the forums also!  Currently the site sends out approximately 50,000 web pages a day!  With between 8-10,000 unique visitors a day!  The forums have grown into around 18 thousand views a day!  That is over 2 million views of web pages and forum pages a month folks!  You have no idea how many times I have sat there looking at the numbers and just shook my head in amazement!  And it is all due to you, the loyal visitors!  The folks that have helped spread the word about the site, and who have contributed time and effort (and even sometimes cash!) towards our continuing operation.  EQDruids would not be here without all that support you have all shown!  I will never be able to properly show my gratitude for everything that has been done to help out.

2003 is here.  We have big plans!  Major upgrades are in the works.  I think you will all be very happy with what is coming.  Trust me, we here at EQDruids are never happy; we always find ways to improve things!

So this thanks goes out to all the visitors of the site.  All the wonderful folks that I have met at the various Fan Faires.  All the people that take time out of their day, and write us notes!  Thank you all!

The above is all from me, but on top of that, myself, Naila and Tes would like to wish you all a wonderful New Year, and hope that you all have an incredible 2003!!

 - Spiritual
