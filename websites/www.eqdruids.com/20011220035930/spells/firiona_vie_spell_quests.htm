To receive any of the following spells:

  • Upheaval

  • Egress

  • Call of Karana

  • Glamour of Tunare

Go to Firiona Vie.  Find the druid named Samitha Lightheart.  Give her a copy of any of the following spells:

  • Circle of Winter

  • Circle of Summer

  • Spirit of Scale

  • Form of the Howler

And she will randomly reward you with one of the spells from the first list.  You may want to have multiple copies of the spells to turn in, if you are looking for a specific spell.

The spells to turn in are dropped by random mobs.

Good luck with her!  She always seems to give me a copy of the spell I already have, and never the one I need! <grin>
