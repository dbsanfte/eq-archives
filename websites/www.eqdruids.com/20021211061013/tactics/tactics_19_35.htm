The 'Ring of XXX' spells just rock...lets take a look at some spell strategies...

Lets say you are in a dungeon.  You need to sell.  Your party needs to sell.   Instead of gating, running, and generally wasting time - you can bind yourself to the dungeon, Ring to Butcherblock or Tox (safer than the others - no griffins), sell to the merchant there, gate back to the dungeon.  Although this might not yield the maximum amount of cash due to factions...it saves usually 30 minutes or more of solid playing time.

Tactics haven't changed much at this point.

Melee is getting to be difficult and the life of magery comes apparent.  HOWEVER you have enough hit points and melee ability to contribute in a small or large group..including periodic tanking to keep damage off the main tanks. I moved a bit more to Swarm, thistle, melee and burn the mobs.

I found that I really couldn't melee a mob to death at level 20...but I could burn the hell out of it and not take much damage.  Also some of the new spells are not very efficient right at level 19...however heal and barbcoat should be immediately used...I would wait a few levels to use Barb shield.

By level 21 you should enough cash to go back and buy every spell you might have skipped at the lower levels.  BEWARE the next ring of spells is going to run 200 - 240 plat...

At these levels I ran around with SoW, Barbcoat, Rock, SoE, and Flamefist on...Swarm is always my opener - I also Careless'd a lot on the way back while pulling.

Be careful - monsters can get really upset at you and sometimes tanks have a hard timing pulling them off you.
