Boston Fan Faire

How to describe this Fan Faire.  It was a huge amount of fun!  First I want to say thanks to some of the folks that were there.  Sony of course for throwing it!

Gus the Baahd (That is Boston speak for Bard) for donating all those beers to the website (laugh)

The Terrible Trio from Druzil Ro (you all know who you are!)

To all the gm's that we met and partied with.  To Tabitha and Marlina!  To PF Changs, to all the pubs we crawled through!  It was a lot of fun! 

To all the other website owners that came out of the woodwork!  To those that didn't (and missed out!)

To Cindy, who put us all on the spot saying a few words in front of 2 thousand people and CBS, without warning ahead of time (I must have sounded so foolish up there! <laugh>)

Also to all the gms and Sony employees that take the time to dress up in costumes and run the Live Quest, you guys all make this so much fun!

Ok, now on with the news, pictures and more.

This was definitely the largest fan faire so far.  I showed up on Thursday night and there was already 200+ people there (and I think we were all in the same bar!). 

Friday things started moving.  Found Tess standing in line waiting for his registration, and later we started the social setup for the Fan Faire.  Wave to all those Nameless folks at our table, and all the others I met at that part.

Saturday we were introduced to the crowd, and heard all the latest news including the fact that the next fan faire is probably going to be San Francisco sometime end of November (and yes, I will be there!)

The energy from everyone was fantastic, all the people that stopped to talk to us really made Tess and my days.  Thank you all for the nice words, and support of the website.

That night is when the awards were given out (we remembered to bring a few!) and the fun continue long into the night.

For anyone that is on the west coast or near, I highly recommend that you consider the San Francisco fan faire.  It promises to be as much fun as this one!
