An elderly, one-legged elf is seen approaching the gates of the Court. Exchanging words, the guards lead the Elf unto the Council. Shortly thereafter, he is again seen, leaving the Court, with a smile upon his face. A week later, builders are seen on the Lands of the Court, and a large, two story building is shortly erected. All is quiet, while the building stands, empty. Then, a few traders are seen making their way to the building, delivering various supplies. Windtalker the Shaman is seen disappearing frequently into the building, as are the Ladies Javanna and Faelyndrea. Eventually, all is in readiness. A large sign is posted over top the entrance, proclaiming the Crosswinds Festhall is now open for business. Enter, and enjoy.
