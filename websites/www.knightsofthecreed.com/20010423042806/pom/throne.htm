This is the Throne Room that comes from My Right Hand after going through Picture Hall. The only way out of this area so far is to click on the main picture due South .. the same one you came through in the Picture Hall. This will lead you back to the Main Hall. There are no known NPCs in this room. Although nothing in this room appears clickable or anything but eye candy. This is a very cool looking room with piles of gold and chests all over the place.
