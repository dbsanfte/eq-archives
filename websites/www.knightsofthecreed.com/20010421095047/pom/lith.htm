This room is beyond the Shakespeare room, past the secret door in the Dining Room, which comes from the Main Hall in the West wing. This area holds the 2 NPC's needed for the Tunarean court crest quest. They respond with many different saying, that can be found on Coladar's website links. You must kill Lithiniath and take the horn and give it to the White Stallion to complete the quest section. This is one of the few NPC's in the zone that drops anything at all. Lithiniath also drops high level spells.
