These are combined in the Deck of Spontaneous Generation. This is a container that holds 4 slots and is received by obtaining a King Cod Card, found on "Dinner" NPC, or fishing. The "Throne" cards seem to be coming from the "Alice in Wonderland" area. Other cards are obtained by killing different NPC's throughout the zone.
