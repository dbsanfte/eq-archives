This room is the first room to the left in the East wing off the Main Hall, across from the Sphinx Room, up the hall from Pinky ... II, and the Library. Theses NPC appear to be dancing eye candy for the zone and will respond to hails.  The exact hails can be found on Coladar's sight using any of the NPC links. Of interesting note, though, every so often, no specific time frame as of yet, when you hail a Gorilla Scholar it sometimes agros .... very odd. Normally only one of the front 2 do this ... but very rarely.
