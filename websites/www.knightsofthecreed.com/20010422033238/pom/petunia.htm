This room is the room at the end of the West wing off the Main Hall, down the hall from Chuckles, Treasure Chest, the Dining Room, and Bozer, next to Sniffles. These NPC appear to be a little odd, but do not respond to hails. Petunia is one of the only "GREEN" NPCs in the zone. Please also note that Ricoculous is a "HE", unlike most things in PoM which are "IT's".
