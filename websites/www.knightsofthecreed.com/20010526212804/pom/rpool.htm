This room is the room down the Guard Hall,  which goes past the Mees Room and also down the hall from Donk, you can swim across the drain from the Left Pool . This is where the infamous EightySix lives. This NPC used to hit for 1s, and have huge hps. Irontaail was initially kicked out of PoM for attacking this NPC, as some GMs felt the NPC was broken. It was later determined that although the NPC is broken, it was not considered an exploit, because of the oddity of the zone. EightySix now hits for 120s and can still drop Crowns cards and Knight cards.
