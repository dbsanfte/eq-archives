Please note that the Treasure Chest in this room is not agro and neither are any of the halflings or Numbered npcs. There are 3 main exits from this room, and many of the teleporters send you back to this room. The 3 exits are the S exit which leads to the Forest entrance area, the East exit which leads to the East wing and the North West exit through a secret wall to the left of "Two", which leads to the West wing.
