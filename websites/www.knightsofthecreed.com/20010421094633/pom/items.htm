QUEST BEGINNINGS 

There are CARD combinations used in the Deck of Spontaneous Generation. This is a container that holds 4 slots and is obtained by saying the phrase "take turn" to the female halfling vendor in Rivervale, at the fruit stand in the garden. She gives you a "sack of turnips". You then give that to the NPC in PoM "Ferjeneror" and he gives you the Deck of Spontaneous Generation container. The cards are found by killing NPC's throughout PoM. The "Throne" cards seem to be coming from the "Alice in Wonderland" area. Other cards are obtained by killing different NPC's throughout the zone.
