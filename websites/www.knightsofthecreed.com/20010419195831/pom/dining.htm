This room is the second room to the right in the West wing off the Main Hall, down the hall from Chuckles, and Treasure Chest right next to Bozer, Lithiniath, and the Shakespeare Room, up the hall from Petunia and Bozer. This NPC appear as annoyances. They are both casters on the ceiling and nuke anyone who walks in the room. They wont chase or leave their spots on the chandelier, easily, but can be coaxed down. They seem to guard the Lithiniath room, which is located through a  secret entrance on the East wall
