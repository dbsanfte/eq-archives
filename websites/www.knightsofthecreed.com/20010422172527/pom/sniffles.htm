This room past a secret door, past Petunia at the end of the West wing off the Main Hall, down the hall from Chuckles, Treasure Chest, Bozer, the Dining Room and Treasure Chest. This NPC is one of the oddest NPCs in PoM. It does not hail and appears to do nothing. It has almost no hp and is level 12-16.
