This room is the room down the Guard Hall,  which goes past the Mees Room,  you can swim across the drain from the Left Pool to the Right Pool. This is where the infamous Donk lives. This NPC used to hit for 1-4s, and have huge hps. Irontaail was initially kicked out of PoM for attacking this NPC, as some GMs felt the NPC was broken. It was later determined that although the NPC is broken, it was not considered an exploit, because of the oddity of the zone. Donk now hits for 120s and can still drop Crowns cards and Knight cards.
