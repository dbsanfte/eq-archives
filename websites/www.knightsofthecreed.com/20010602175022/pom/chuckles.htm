This room is the first room to the left in the West wing off the Main Hall, across the hall from Bozer, and Treasure Chest right next to Dining Room and up the hall from Petunia and Sniffles. Chuckles appears to do nothing and does not seem to hail. There is an invisible trap Five, that is in the corner that can not be effected, but hits for small amounts 1-4 damage. This appears to be for flavor. This room exits to the Treasure Chest room by clicking on the picture of Bristlebane behind Chuckles.
