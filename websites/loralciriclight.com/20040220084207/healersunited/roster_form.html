Guild Roster Form

This script will generate your own guild roster using Everquest's in-game guild roster dump. Follow these steps to use this program:

  1. Enter Everquest, Press ALT-G to bring up your in-game guild roster, press "Dump" in the upper right corner of the guild roster window.
  2. Locate the guild roster file. If your guild is called "Your Awesome Guild" it will be called Your_Awesome_Guild.txt.
  3. Post this file to a website and note the URL where you can find it.
  4. Enter the URL of the location of the file and the name you wish to give to your roster page in the form below.
  5. Hit submit and link your guild's page to the URL of the roster page you created.
