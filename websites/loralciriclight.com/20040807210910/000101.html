Tales of Loral

About This Site

Tales of Loral Ciriclight is a collection of stories based around a character in the game Everquest. Loral is hosted on the Quellious server, and was created in May of 2000. He is a member of the prestigious guild, Healers United. This group prides itself on seeking a higher goal than the typical hunting and gathering of powerful beasts and magical weapons. We seek to help all the citizens of Norrath in their time of need. Read more about the author.

This site was written to work on any device capable of rendering HTML but looks best on a browser that supports Cascading Style Sheets including Netscape, Internet Explorer and Opera. For more information on why you should update your browser to one of these, read about web standards.
