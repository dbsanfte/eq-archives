Tales of Tarlin

About Tarlin of Tunaria

Tales of Tarlin follows the adventures of a young ranger of Telethin, the elven city in the land of Tunaria. These stories follow the adventures of this character in the world of Everquest Online Adventures, a massive multiplayer role playing game for the Playstation 2 video game console.

Tales of Tarlin is set 500 years before the Age of Destiny, the setting for the current Everquest PC game. It follows the adventures of the father of Loral Ciriclight, cleric of Tunare in the Age of Destiny.

We begin our tale at the beginning of our young hero's adventure hunting the Hatebone orcs on the outskirts of Telethin. And now...The Orc Scout.
