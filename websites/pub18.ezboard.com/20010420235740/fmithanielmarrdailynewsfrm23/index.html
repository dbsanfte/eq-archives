Purpose of the MMDN ITR Forum
This forum is not a formal scheduling or reservation system; it is an intent-to-raid (ITR) forum, and a sign-up forum for open raids. Please be advised that there are some guilds on our server who will not share or respect your ITR posts here, who may even jump your raid if you do post. That said, many guilds on MithMarr will respect your ITR, and will cooperate to make our community a friendly one in which everyone gets to have fun! To help ensure that everyone gets full, fair use of the forum, and help the moderators do their jobs consistently, and also to help users navigate the forum quickly, we have a few guidelines we ask that you follow. Disregard of any of these guidelines will result in your post being locked and/or deleted, allowing others to post ITRs for that date and location. So please pay attention! :) (We'll also be moving old raids to the ITR Archives to help reduce clutter.) Rules are subject to modification by the appropriate staff as necessary.
Please Read the Current Guidelines Before Posting!
7-Day Rule: Please don't post raids more than 7 days in advance. By 7 days we mean a full week; if you wish to post an ITR for an event on Saturday the 15th, you must wait until Saturday the 8th PDT to post it. Our EzBoard functions on PDT time, the same time zone in which Verant is located, and that is the timestamp we will check.
Give Details: Please post the full date, location, and group in the subject of the thread. In the body of the post, please indicate whether the raid is open to people outside of your guild/group. Feel free to use the thread to recruit raiders as well. If the details of your raid change, make sure to change them in the subject/body of the original post as well, so as to avoid unintentional conflicts.
Be Courteous: Check all of the other posts first to make sure you're not stepping on anyone else's ITR. Also, don't be a zone hog; it's inconsiderate to repeatedly post ITRs for the same in-demand zone multiple days/ weekends in a row.
Diplomacy 101: If there's a misunderstanding or conflict between posters/raiders, try to work it out before ranting at the MMDN or ITR system.

Thanks for your cooperation! Happy raiding!
