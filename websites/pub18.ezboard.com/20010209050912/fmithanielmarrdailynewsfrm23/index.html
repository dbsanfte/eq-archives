This forum is NOT a formal scheduling system; this is an intent-to-raid (ITR) forum, and a sign-up forum for open raids. Please be advised that there are some guilds on our server who just don't understand respect and sharing, so they won't respect your ITR posts, and may even jump your raid if you DO post. That said, many guilds on MithMarr will respect your ITR, and will cooperate to make our community a friendly one in which everyone gets to have fun! We do have a few requests, though, for posters to this board: 1) Please don't announce raids more than 7 days in advance. 2) Please post the full date, location, and group name in the subject of the thread. 3) In the body of the post, please indicate whether the raid is open to people outside of your guild/group. 4) Check the other posts first to make sure you're not stepping on anyone else's ITR. 5) Don't be a zone hog (ie, don't try to hog the same zone multiple days or weekends in a row.) 6) Don't use MMDN as your holy book -- if there's a misunderstanding, try to work it out before coming and ranting here. 7) Posts which violate any of these guidelines will be banished by the staff -- don't take it personally :). Past raid posts will also be deleted, to reduce clutter and confusion. Happy raiding!
