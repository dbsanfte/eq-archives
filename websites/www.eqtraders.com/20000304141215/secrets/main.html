EQ Trade Secrets

While I'd originally planned on never providing "cheats" or "recipes" on the site, the overwhelming number of requests have made me rethink my decision. HOWEVER, I leave the caveat up that there may be some errors contained within here, especially on the items that I have not had a time to personally research. Feel free to let me know when you find a correction or addition, but leave your flames and complaints at the door. <g>

Baking - all known recipes, plus some queries on "goes well with" recipes that are still undetermined

Brewing - all known recipes

Fletching - arrow chart, including damage, range, delay, average cost to make, and some trivial listings

Jewelcraft - gem/metal combinations, plus a link to a better trivial list

Poisons - several recipes from a rogue

Pottery - translation of the basic pottery book

Smithing - translation of the Blacksmithing, armor and weapons guides
