Planes of Power Pottery Recipes

A temporary spot for PoP recipes found in books and by hailing various NPCs in the Plane of Knowledge. Note that some of the NPCs will not give out their recipes unless you meet certain requirements (such as high skill in the tradeskill in question.) Some of these recipes indicate that they are to be made using the pottery wheels and kilns in Plane of Knowledge (New Tanaan) only - when reporting in verified recipes, please note if it can be combined in a non-PoP pottery wheel and kiln.

The glazes are a no-fail combine

The first column ("V?") indicates if the recipe has yet been verified. All recipes that do NOT have a "V" in that column are our "best guess" based on the recipe text. In many cases, naming and exact recipe are guesswork based on an interpretation of the book/hail text. If there's no "V" listed, then there are no guarantees that the recipe will work as listed.
