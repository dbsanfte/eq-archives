Smithing Prices

Prices listed are using best charisma and faction. They are listed in platinum pieces in decimal form preceded by a dollar sign: $9.524 would mean 9pp 5gp 2sp 4cp. The cost of components that are themselves made as a tradeskill recipe assumes no failures making them; your mileage will vary.

Note that "Max Cost" is not truly the highest price you can pay to make an item, but the minimum cost to make the most expensive of several items grouped together. For instance, the banded gorget and bracer are grouped together because they both have a trivial of 102 and are made with only one sheet of metal; however, the gorget only costs $1.091 to make at best charisma/faction, and the bracer costs $1.375 with best cha/faction, so $1.091 is listed as "Min Cost" and $1.375 as "Max Cost". For all single items, these two costs will be the same, of course.
Cultural items and most Luclin recipes not included in this list yet.
