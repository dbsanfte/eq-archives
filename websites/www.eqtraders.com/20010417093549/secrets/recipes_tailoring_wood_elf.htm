Wood Elf Cultural Armor & Weapons

Please make sure to post corrections/updates to the tailoring message board. Note that these recipes are ONLY to be tried by wood elves, and the smithing portion MUST be combined using your racial fier'dal forge. The tailoring portion cannnot be verified until the fier'dal sewing kit recipe is fixed.

At the current time, incorrect recipes mixed in a cultural forge will NOT eat your items. Close the forge, open the forge, and your items still should be there. Failed skill checks will, of course, still eat your items, just like with any other trade skill.
