Akanon Shadowknight Armor Quest

This is one of many new youngling armor quests. This one originates in Ak'Anon and results in armor for gnome SKs. The armor should be lore (other than the bracer), no drop, and GNM/SHD only.

Similar structure to the other evil melee class-specific quests - you get a note from the quest giver (Garret Zethkog) which you have to give to a secondary NPC (Mimtia Zethkog) who will then give you a kit (a Mail Assembly Kit). She will then tell you the recipe for each item and give you a mold (Crude [xxx] Mold) when prompted. The primary quest giver gives out the class-specific weapon quest.

Go to Mimtia, she's in the Mines as well, though more difficult to find. Gave her the letter(missed the text), she lists the kinds of armor available.

You say, 'I wish to craft a plague knight helm'
Mimtia Zethkog says 'To assemble a Plague Knight Helm you will need to obtain two bricks of crude bronze and smelt them in a forge with a Water Flask and this Crude Helm Mold. Once that is done combine the Crude Bronze Helm with a Ruined Cat Pelt and two Clockwork Spider Eye Lenses in the Mail Assembly Kit.'

You say, 'I wish to craft a plague knight bracer'
Mimtia Zethkog says 'To assemble a Plague Knight Bracer you will need to obtain a brick of crude bronze and smelt it in a forge with a Water Flask and this Crude Bracer Mold. Once that is done, combine the Crude Bronze Bracer with a Ruined Cat Pelt and a Clockwork Rat Leg Section in the Mail Assembly Kit.'

You say, 'I wish to craft a plague knight gauntlets'
Mimtia Zethkog says 'To assemble Plague Knight Gauntlets you will need to obtain two bricks of crude bronze and smelt them in a forge with a Water Flask and this crude Gauntlet Mold. Once that is done combine the Crude Bronze Gauntlets with a Ruined Cat Pelt and two Clockwork Rat Back Plates in the Mail Assembly Kit.'

You say, 'I wish to craft a plague knight boots'
Mimtia Zethkog says 'To assemble Plague Knight Boots you will need to obtain two bricks of crude bronze and smelt them in a forge with a Water Flask and this crude Boot Mold. Once that is done combine the Crude Bronze Boots with two Ruined Cat Pelts, and two Clockwork Rat Belly Plates in the Mail Assembly Kit.'

You say, 'I wish to craft a plague knight vambraces'
Mimtia Zethkog says 'To assemble Plague Knight Vambraces you will need to obtain two bricks of crude bronze and smelt them in a forge with a Water Flask and this Crude Vambrace Mold. Once that is done combine the Crude Bronze Vambraces with a Low Quality Cat Pelt and two Clockwork Spider Leg Sections in the Mail Assembly Kit.'

You say, 'I wish to craft a plague knight greaves'
Mimtia Zethkog says 'To assemble Plague Knight Greaves you will need to obtain two bricks of crude bronze and smelt them in a forge with a Water Flask and this Crude Greaves Mold. Once that is done combine the Crude Bronze Greaves with two Low Quality Cat Pelts and four Clockwork Spider Leg Sections in the Mail Assembly Kit.'

You say, 'I wish to craft a plague knight breastplate'
Mimtia Zethkog says 'To assemble a Plague Knight Breastplate you will need to obtain four bricks of crude bronze and smelt them in a forge with a Water Flask and this Crude Breastplate Mold. Once that is done combine the Crude Bronze Breastplate with a Medium Quality Cat Pelt, a Clockwork Spider Thorax Plate, and a Clockwork Spider Abdomen Plate in the Mail Assembly Kit.'
