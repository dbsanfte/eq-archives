Kelethin Trade Supplies

Kelethin does not have its own zone, but sits in the midst of the Greater Faydark. Your intrepid editor almost met her match here. This city is all unenclosed platforms of varying height, and after one bad batch of vertigo, I fell to my death. I've since learned to cope with it, and initial exploration has finally been completed. Many thanks go out to Bryndal for added research.
