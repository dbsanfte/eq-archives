Iksar Cultural Smithing

Please make sure to post corrections/updates to the smithing message board. Note that these recipes are ONLY to be tried by iksar smiths, and they MUST be combined using your racial iksar forge unless otherwise noted.

At the current time, incorrect recipes mixed in a cultural forge will NOT eat your items. Close the forge, open the forge, and your items still should be there. Failed skill checks will, of course, still eat your items, just like with any other trade skill.

Cabilis scale mail

appropriate section mold, flask blood water, appropriate number of skyiron sheets and scales:

  • Coifs, Masks, Gorgets, Mantles, Belts, and Bracelets require one Skyiron Sheet, and one set of Skyiron Scales.
  • Gloves and Boots require 2 Skyiron Sheets and 1 set of Skyiron Scales.
  • Sleeves require 2 Skyiron Sheets and 2 sets of Skyiron Scales.
  • Coats, Cloaks, and Leggings require 3 Skyiron Sheets, and 2 sets of Skyiron Scales.

 

pieces are 1 AC higher than banded, and lighter

trivials: bracer, gorget - 175, coif, mask - 182, belt =>182, BP =>184, mantle - 188, sleeves =>188, legs =>188, boots =>188, gloves - 195, cloak - 202

  • Sleeves require 2 Skyiron Sheets and 2 sets of Skyiron Scales.
  • Coats, Cloaks, and Leggings require 3 Skyiron Sheets, and 2 sets of Skyiron Scales.
