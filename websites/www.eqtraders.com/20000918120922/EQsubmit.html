I need your help!

You'll find several areas of this site that have large gaps in the information.  I can't do/learn everything at once, so I'm asking for help. The more folks who help, the more useful this site is for everyone.  Additionally, if I get enough active participation in this site, I'll be more likely to make available to the serious tradesperson some of the more closely-held information such as exact recipes, gem/metal combinations, etc.

  • Basic trade skill information: Articles on any trade skill are welcome, but I'm especially looking for articles on alchemy and tinkering at the moment..  Please send email that contains the submission, as well as your character name and server, and email address.  (Please let me know if you'd rather I didn't link in your email address-- I'll be happy to oblige). Additionally, please send me any corrections/additions you may have to posted information on the other trade skills

  • Locations for trade materials in each city and zone:. If your favorite zone is missing items in my listings, and you'd be willing to explore/document for me, please send email.  I'd like to have up-to-date lists for all zones that have merchants, and this has already been a huge undertaking.  Even if you can only list information for a couple shops, every little bit helps! Where possible, include NPC merchant name, building name, and /loc, as well as what trade items are available.

  • Merchants wanted! You want to make money, I want to help you hook up with players who need your goods. If you've got trade items to sell, or a skill to offer (i.e., wedding services, weapon sharpening, guide services) send email with your ad, and I'd be happy to include you in the list. Please be sure to include all relevant information, including server name, specifically what you are offering (i.e., not just "I'm a tailor", but what types of tailored items you can/will make), your in-game name, and a note to me as to whether you would like your email address linked in.
