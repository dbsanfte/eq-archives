Tinkering Trade Supplies

While your main tinkering items are located in one specific spot in Steamfont, it's come to my attention that there are other items needed. As time permits, and I am doing full re-exploration, I'll list all the locales for these items that I can find.

Ak'Anon

  • gnomish spirits - clockwork barkeeps inside bar at 1258, -901
  • bottle - clockwork merchant at 1264, -773

East Commonlands

  • lockpicks - Ponila Quickfingers (first building on right when entering zone from West Freeport)
  • water, large lantern, bottle - Romya (335, 4058)

East Karana

  • fishing pole, large lantern, bottle, water - Bradly & Julie (shop at -833, -109)

Erudin

  • water, large lantern, bottle - Borge Finleather, Teria Finleather (Erudin Surplus)

Eruds Crossing

  • fishing pole - Renna (Kerra Isle, near dock)

Freeport

  • water, large lantern, bottle - Alec & Ran Greenglade (blue building next to bank, N. Freeport)

Halas

  • water, large lantern, bottle - Greta Terrilon (outside McDaniels Smokes), Leopok Terrilon (bank area), Dok (Dok's Cigars)

Kelethin

  • water, large lantern, bottle - Merchant Iludarae (next to first lift), Merchant Alanya ("Goods" shop on platform between bank and Priest of Discord lift)
  • bottle - Merchant Tilluen & Merchant Aluuvila (Packwearers Goods), Merchant Aildien (-492, -133), Merchant Kwein (153, 332)
  • gnomish spirits - Barkeep Aanlawen ("Tavern", 603, 688)

Kithicor Forest

  • bottle - Eithne Walker (loc 1429, -828)
  • lockpicks - merchant in building at -768, 3081

Lake Rathe

  • lockpick, water, large lantern - Dreana (gypsy camp, -418.49)
  • large lantern, water, bottle, fishing pole - Turga (ogre camp, NE corner of zone)

North Karana

  • lockpick, water, large lantern - Romella (gypsy camp)

Oasis of Marr

  • Large lantern, water, lockpick - Isslana (gypsy encampment)

The Outlands

  • metal rod, cork, grease, steel lined gloves, metal shaft, spricket, metal twine, gears, sprockets, reflective shard, static orb, gnomish bolts, toolbox, silk lined steel helm, Journal of Gimblestan XIV, Journal of Gimblestan XV - Tin Merchant V, 2615, 2537
  • water, large lantern - Tin Merchant I, 2722, 2577

Paineel

  • large lantern, water - Geomor Hapera, Rallia Hapera (Superior Supplies, 938, 757)

Rathe Mountains

  • lockpicks - Lianna Ferasa (gypsy camp, 3979, 1469)
  • compass, large lantern, lockpicks, water - Chandra Kali (gypsy gamp, 3925, 1399), Susanna (paladin camp near Feerrott zone entrance, 2916, -764), Viira Bali (gypsy camp near Feerrott, 1643, -867)
  • large lantern, fishing pole, water - Guzzla (ogre camp, 1749, 1289)

Steamfont Mountains

  • Toolbox, metal rod, cork, bottle of grease, metal shaft, spricket, metal twine, gears, sprockets, reflective shard, static orb, gnomish bolts, Journal of Gamblestan XIV, Journal of Gamblestan XV- Bom Knotwood (windmill at /loc 165, -881)
  • fishing pole, large lantern, bottle, water - Glen Garginbur (1st building on right from entrance to Ak'Anon, -1788, 3142)
