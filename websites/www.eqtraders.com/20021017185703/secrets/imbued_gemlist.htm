Imbued Gem Information

Healers (cleric, druid, shaman) can call down the blessing of their deity upon a gem with the use of a level 29 spell. (For Solusek Ro, it is a wizard spell, for Veeshan it's four foraged items that have to be combined in a kiln.) Each healer can only scribe and cast the imbue spell for the gem specific to their deity, after which a tradesperson can use the imbued gem in crafting deity-specific items. (The tradesperson does not need to worship a specific deity in order to craft items for that deity.) Some gems currently only function in imbued jewelry and pottery idols.

May 9, 2002: You must be of the proper class as well as religion in order to scribe an imbue gem spell.
