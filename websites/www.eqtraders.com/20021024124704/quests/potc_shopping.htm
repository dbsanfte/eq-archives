"Shopping List" for Xanthe's Earring of Nature/PoTC Quest

There are many, many items that need to be collected if you are making these items yourself (or if you are providing supplies in order to have someone else make the items for you). Gladlyn, with the aid of the EQTC message board community, has kindly put together a "shopping list" for the items needed for this involved quest.
