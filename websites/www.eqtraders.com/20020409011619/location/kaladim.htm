Kaladim Trade Supplies

City Evaluation: Trade skills appear to be excellent in this area. In this and the nearby Butcherblock zone, you can find the only known book on brewing, plus one of the few locations for smithing ore. The only thing I have NOT found are the ingredients needed for brewing. All other trade skills look good.

(See also Butcherblock Mountains listing for items in the next zone)
