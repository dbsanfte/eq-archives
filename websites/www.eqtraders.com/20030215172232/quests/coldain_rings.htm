Coldain Insignia Ring Quests (Tradeskill portion only)

This is only the tradeskill portion of the first and third coldain insignia rings. For a complete write-up on the quest, please see the Garadain's Rings writeup on EQ'Lizer

Ring 1: Copper Coldain Insignia Ring (+1 AC +1 Str +1 Save vs Magic, All/All
Garadain Glacierbane, a dwarf in the huts northeast of the Ry'gorr fort, is looking for a blanket to keep him warm. In return for this blanket you receive the Copper Coldain Ring. The blanket is made by combining 4 pelts in a tailoring kit. Two High Quality Tundra Kodiak pelts(unstacked) along with two High Quality Snow Cougar pelts. This produces a nodrop Coldain Hunting Blanket. The trivial level is between 36 and 44.

Tundra Kodiaks in the Eastern Wastes drop the High Quality Tundra Kodiak Pelts. Snow Cougars in the Eastern Wastes and Iceclad Ocean drop the High Quality Snow Cougar Pelts.


Ring3: Gold Coldain Insignia Ring (+3AC +3 Str +3 Save vs Magic, All/All
Garadain Glacierbane, a dwarf in the huts northeast of the Ry'gorr fort, is looking for a hunting knife. This is made by combining a Skinning Rock, a High Quality Walrus Hide, and a Wooly Rhino Horn in a forge. This produces a nodrop Hunting Knife. This is trivial at a smithing skill of 41.

Walruses in the Eastern Wastes drop the High Quality Walrus Hides. Wooly Rhinos in the Eastern Wastes drop the Wooly Rhino Horn. Skinning rocks are found in the Eastern Wastes as ground spawns, they look like a rough black rock on the ground and an egg shape in inventory. Skinning rocks are no drop
