Barbarian Cultural Smithing

These recipes have been tested and verified. Please make sure to send all corrections/additions to the DenMother. Note that these recipes are ONLY to be tried by barbarian smiths, and they MUST be combined using your racial Northman forge unless otherwise noted.

At the current time, incorrect recipes mixed in a cultural forge will NOT eat your items. Close the forge, open the forge, and your items still should be there. Failed skill checks will, of course, still eat your items, just like with any other trade skill.
