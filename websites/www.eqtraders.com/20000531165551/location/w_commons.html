West Commonlands Trade Supplies

Zone evaluation: This is not a major shopping zone, but rather a spot to supplement supplies as you run through, dodging hill giants and griffons. You'll want to hit East Commonlands/Freeport for more serious supplies

Baking

  • bread - Innkeep Roster (inn near toll gate), Innkeep Olissa (inn near lake)

Fletching, Pottery, Smithing

  • none found

Brewing

  • bottle, grapes, cask, shot glass - Innkeep Seke (inn near toll gate), Innkeep Redthorn (inn near lake)

Fishing

  • fishing pole, bait - Maula Fishcatcher (lone building next to lake)

Jewelcraft

  • silver/electrum/gold/platinum bars, Legacy of Jewelcraft, jewelers kit, star ruby, fire emerald, sapphire - Naresa Sparkle (building near entrance to Befallen) - this human woman is afraid of a human monk of good intent, so I am not sure of her faction. Make sure to consider her before buying or selling to her, as you might end up with some very bad prices.

Tailoring

  • small sewing kit - Sorsha Hobbitfriend (inn near toll gate - her name is misleading - she dislikes halflings who have not got Freeport faction adjustments), Peron Threadspinner (inn near lake)
