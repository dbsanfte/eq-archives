dwarven plate

appropriate small plate sectional mold, dwarven smithy hammer, earthen temper, leather padding, brellium chain jointing, and appropriate number of folded sheets of brellium:

  • Visors, collars, bracers, and boots - 1 folded sheet of brellium.
  • Helms, pauldrons, girdles, vambraces, and gauntlets - 2 folded sheets of brellium.
  • Breastplates, cloaks, and greaves - 3 folded sheets of brellium.
