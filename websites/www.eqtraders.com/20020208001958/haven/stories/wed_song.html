Ruldari & Joril's Wedding Song


By Ellen

Face to face is love uncovered,
Through expression, souls discovered,
Each the other's state of grace,
Love has brought us face to face.

 

Side by side is love united,
Strength and will, we're undivided,
Through each storm, each rising tide,
Love will find us, side by side.

 

Back to back is love defended,
Past and present pain transcended,
Let life launch it's worst attack
Love will shield us back to back.
