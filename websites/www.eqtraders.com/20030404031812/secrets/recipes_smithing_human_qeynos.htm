Human Qeynos Cultural Smithing

Human cultural smithing is now broken into two pages - one for Freeport, one for Qeynos (below). Any human smith can make any of the items for either city, but they must use the proper cultural forge, in the proper city, for each recipe. Note that, as with all cultural tradeskills, cultural tradeskill containers are not fooled by illusions.

Stats for these pieces can be found on the Human Qeynos Smithed Armor/Weapon Stats page.

(Note: enchanted large bricks of HQ ore are made using the level 49 enchant steel spell. The enchanted large bricks are then formed into various enchanted rings, folded sheets, etc. as listed below)
