West Karana Trade Supplies

Zone evaluation: Until the expansion pack, this was the largest zone in the game. Merchants are spread out in odd places, and you'll want to watch out for roaming creatures as you shop. It's a good place to pick up basic supplies while passing through the zone for baking, brewing, fletching, pottery, smithing and tailoring.

Baking

  • bread - Innkeep Danin (-1514, -12998), Innkeep Rislarn (327, -4419)
  • spices, vinegar, sauce, garnish, frosting, flour, milk, 13 recipe books - Chrislin Baker (1036, -1990)
  • fish - Misla McManus (barbarian fishing village, near -4020, -2343)

Brewing

  • barley, hops, rice, malt, yeast, wine yeast - Cleet Miller (-2252, -4118 ... but he wanders off his farm a lot, so it may be hard to find him)

Fishing

  • tacklebox, fishing pole, bait, catch-a-lot bait, dry hooks, treble hooks, circle hooks - Misla McManus (barbarian fishing village, -4020, -2343)

Fletching

  • arrowheads (3), shafts (4), fletch (3), vane (3), groove nock (3) - Brenzl McMannus (-1965, -8964)

Make Poison

  • old delivered book, suspension, mortar/pestle, constrict suspension, larent suspension, poison vial (3), coyotetail, thorny ergot, alocasia root, lactera, delphinium - Gindlin Toxfodder (-2989 , -13399)

Pottery

  • blocks of clay (3), firing sheets (3), glass shard, Basic Pottery book, vial sketches (3) - Tarnar (1002, -2079)

Smithing

  • large (banded) armor molds, lantern casing mold, Armor & Weapons Guide, Basic Blacksmithing, About Northman Ringmail - Sera McMannus (-4021, -2224)
  • file mold, container molds, lantern casing mold, Armor & Weapons Guide, Basic Blacksmithing, weapon molds (pommel, hilt, dual-edged blade, heavy steel blade, spiked ball), Dirge of Forgefire - Spinner (-1454, -12841)
  • needle & thimble molds - Alysa (425, -4362)

Tailoring

  • large sewing kit, mask, gorget, belt, cloak, wrist, and shoulderpad patterns, backpack, whip & quiver patterns (2), How to Sew (3) - Alysa (425, -4362)
  • small sewing kit, How to Sew (Medium) - Silna Weaver OR Minya Coldtoes (847, -1824; NW corner of the zone, a tent behind the buildings) -- these women seem to take turns manning the booth, and sometimes nobody will be there.
