Velious Tailored Armor Fashion Guide

For those master tailors who want to challenge their skills to the utmost, the frozen continent of Velious brings with it pelts, patterns, threads, tannins, and recipes geared towards highly skilled tailors. The components are rare and/or expensive, and the armor is very difficult to make (not trivial at skill 250). Huge thanks go out to all the brave adventurers who risk life and limb to gather pelts and brewing components, as well as the struggling master tailors who repeatedly hit the combine button in hopes of a success or skill increase.

If you have a screenshot of some Velious tailored armor and would like to share it, click here to submit it!
