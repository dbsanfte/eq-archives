Brewing Prices

Costs listed are using best charisma and faction. They are listed in platinum pieces in decimal form with no currency label: 9.524 would mean 9pp 5gp 2sp 4cp. Unconsumed tools are not included.

"Product" is the product made. Yields are given in parentheses when other than 1.

"Min Cost" is the price of all dropped items gathered yourself (farmed), and any subcombines performed yourself. One subcombine is to gather the research materials used in celestial essences. The cost of components that are themselves made as a tradeskill recipe assumes no failures making them; your mileage will vary.

"Max Cost" is the price of all dropped items purchased from vendors. This is only used where options apply. Some Max Costs in brewing are nearly irrelevant, as for example you will likely be fishing your own zone-specific fish for brewed essences.

"Discrepancy" will note differences between Min Cost and Max Cost when they exist, usually noting farmed ingrediants and/or cost saving subcombines. Buying dropped items from players will obviously impact the prices greatly, usually to the costlier side. All indredient costs are listed after combines for your own comparisons. NO DROP and NO RENT ingredients are not accounted for, as they must be gathered in the field. An asterisk (*) denotes an ingredient which is not NO DROP, neither will it sell to vendors, so it has no vendor cost.

Technical Exceptions: Discrepancies will not be given for lootable items which are found normally from a vendor.
