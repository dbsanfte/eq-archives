Freeport Paladin Armor Quest

This is one of many new youngling armor quests based out of Freeport. Armor pieces are lore (other than bracer), no drop, and restricted to HUM/PAL only.

This is a list of the recipes for the new low level armor quests gained from Edward in North Freeport ontop of the Paladin guild. The items needed are found in Northern Ro, East Commons and West Commons.

If you talk to Edward he will give you a 'chest' which you use to combine the parts in. This will produce a Metal Sheet which you combine with the mold in a forge. Edward gives you a mold for each peice of armor you ask him about.

You do not require smithing skill to forge these items.


Helm
2 x Bricks of Crude Iron Ore
1 x Young Kodiak Paw
1 x Ruined cat Pelt
1 X Hops


Bracers

1 x Orc Prayer beads
1 x Red Wine

Greaves

Gaunts

Notes:
The crude ore drops of most low level mobs in the above zones. I found the Orc Prayer beads on Orcs at the camp in WC as well as the DeathFist Skull.
