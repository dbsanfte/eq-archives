Ogre Cultural Smithing

Please make sure to post corrections/updates to the smithing message board. Note that these recipes are ONLY to be tried by ogre smiths, and they MUST be combined using your racial Oggok forge unless otherwise noted.

At the current time, incorrect recipes mixed in a cultural forge will NOT eat your items. Close the forge, open the forge, and your items still should be there. Failed skill checks will, of course, still eat your items, just like with any other trade skill.

ogre war plate armor

large plate sectional mold, smithy hammer, leather padding, adamantite chain jointing, blood temper, appropriate number of folded sheets of adamantite:

  • 1 sheet for visor, bracer, collar, and boots
  • 2 sheets for helm, pauldrons, girdle, vambraces, and gauntlets
  • 3 sheets for breastplate, cloak, and greaves

some pieces come out a reddish brown in color, others are black

trivial: >=198 on collar, visor, boots; >250 on breastplate
