Crystal Caverns Trade Supplies

All these merchants are in the dwarven outpost. If you have done no faction work in this area, you will need to use sneak or a faction-adjusting spell in order to shop here.

 

Baking

  • milk - Grabble Coldheart (dwarven outpost, -262, 172)
  • short beer - Gilliad Coldheart, Hardat Gemshard (dwarven outpost, -241, 185)
  • 13 recipe books, vinegar, spices, jug of sauce, garnish, frosting, flour, milk - Praps Gemshard (dwarven outpost, -260, 367)

Brewing

  • water, bottle - Grabble Coldheart (dwarven outpost, -262, 172)
  • cask, shotglass, brewers limericks, short beer, dwarven ale - Hardat Gemshard (dwarven outpost, -241, 185)
  • short beer, shotglass, grapes -Gilliad Coldheart (dwarven outpost)

Fishing

  • Fishing pole, bait - Grabble Coldheart (dwarven outpost, -262, 172)
