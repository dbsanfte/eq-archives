Tradeskill High Fashion

Oftentimes, folks will ask "what does x cultural armor look like?", or "what does the 3-resin blue look like?" and other such questions. As time goes on, this section will be used to display player-made armors that come in a variety of colors and designs.

This section of the site has been taken over by EQTC's very own Fashion Editor, FeistyGirl. Please send screenshots to fashion@eqtraders.com, and include information in the email including item specifics (enchanted, imbued [deity]), etc. as well as information on the smith who made the items.

  • Cultural armor
  • Velious tailoring
  • Dyed fine steel plate (Currently only dyed FS on a dwarf is available. this, too, will be expanded as time goes on)
