Velious Tailoring

A lot of discussion, some of it very heated, has been stirred up due to the addition of new tailored items in Velious. Many folks who are totally new to this site are coming here in hopes of getting some of the amazing new items that can be made, and really have no clue how difficult a task it can be to craft these lovely items. The path to grandmaster tailoring via these items is difficult, painful, and quite expensive, and not one to be undertaken lightly.

As time goes on, it is hoped that there will be more tailored items added to bridge the gap between the old non-cultural tailoring maximum of 115, and these new Velious items, most of which are in the 220+ range of difficulty. In the meantime, the crystalline silk armor is the only set of Velious tailored armor that trivials in the 100s. (You can get to 131 skill by tailoring crystalline silk armor.)

I've tried to include links in the recipes over to information on Velious tailoring drops. Hopefully this will help answer some of the questions on ores, pelts, etc.
