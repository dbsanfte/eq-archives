midasear@my-deja.com replied:

Obviously, Rush is a rare spawn in Innothule, who occasionally pops up
in place of Dark Deathstinger the Bard. He's a shaman of approximately
the same level as Dark. Beware though, he is defended a large swarm of
Dittoheads that spawn whenever he is attacked. They are numerous, very
high level, and are aggressive to all PCs. A Dittohead train is worse
than a Mistmoore train, and can clear a zone full of L50s. Its not fair
to the newbie trolls to spawn one in their newbie zone, the swamp.

 
The interesting thing about Dittohead faction NPCs is, that unique among
MoBs, they never refer to a PC's race when attacking. Instead they
substitute the word "Liberal" into the standard stock MoB phrases i.e.
"Liberals like you have ruined your own lands..." or "Liberals like you
are better left dead...". Occasionally, they also use the battlecry
"DittoDittoDitto", but only immediately after Rush finishes a section of
his monologue.

 
Rush will despawn on his own after 3 EQ hours if he is ignored and left
undisturbed. During this time, he will recite a white text monologue,
similar to the bards in certain inns and taverns in parts of Norrath.
"Listening" provides an insight into the culture and beliefs of some
trolls, and hints regarding certain troll-only quests and items.

 
The wand is not so hot. It's no-drop, troll shaman only, and adds +25
charisma, but only with respect to other trolls. Killing Rush to get
the wand makes you KOS to the Dittohead faction group and gives you
positive faction with a few other troll factions. This is not worth the
bother, because Dittoheads turn up in surprising places, and are often
disguised as other NPC races, or even as PCs. And trolls have terrible
faction problems to begin with. If you actually bother to get the wand,
you can no longer sell or train in Grobb without being attacked by a
wandering Dittohead. And visiting ANY zone can be very dangerous.
 

My advice is to leave Rush alone and ignore him. The danger presented
by Dittoheads make even discussing him dangerous. And only Trolls will
really be interested anyway.
