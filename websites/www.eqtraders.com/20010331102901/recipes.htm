Secrets of the Trades

There may be some errors contained within here, especially on the items that I have not had a time to personally research. Feel free to let me know when you find a correction or addition, but leave your flames and complaints at the door.

  • Baking - all known recipes, plus most trivial levels and yields
  • Brewing - all known recipes, plus most trivial levels and yields. Also see the new brewing recipes for smithing tempers.
  • Fishing - fishing lures, both successful and unsuccessful combinations
  • Fletching - arrow chart, including damage, range, delay, and some trivial listings
  • Jewelcraft - gem/metal combinations, plus imbued (deity-specific) jewelry
  • Poisons - some recipes, including translations of books in several languages
  • Pottery - some trivial levels included, but there's still a lot of blanks
  • Smithing - new and improved pages full of smithing recipes
  • Tailoring - all known tailoring recipes, plus most trivial levels, plus Velious tailoring information and recipes
  • cross-skill items: dyes for smithing (made via alchemy or make poison), a dye color sample chart, and a clue sheet for dye ground spawns

I refuse to try to improve on a good thing , so here are links to recipes for other trade skills not listed above:

  • Alchemy - visit the Belgar's Ultimate Guide to Alchemy and/or the Shaman's Crucible for more information on this shaman-only skill
  • Spell Research - while not technically a trade skill, it's exotic enough for questions to be raised by visitors. Mythiran Tower is a good source of info.
  • Tinkering - Visit Fulcrum's Tinkering Compendium for recipes and more (while the original Fulcrum is gone, the site is still being maintained)
