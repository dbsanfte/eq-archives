Zone Specific Foraged Goodies

Items such as fruit, berries, vegetables, pods of water and fishing grubs may be foraged in any zone. However, druids and rangers with a forage skill of over 100, and iksar (with their racial foraging abilities) can often find that various zones will produce a few extra surprises, many of which can be sold for money, some of which are used in quests, and some of which can be used for brewing, baking or cultural smithing.

An asterisk (*) indicates that the item is not stackable

Kunark foraged items

Unlike items in the "old world", unique foraged items in the Kunark area do not require a high forage skill to find them. Also unlike the rest of Norrath, many of these uniqe foraged items do not have any resale value to the merchants. Several are used for quests.
