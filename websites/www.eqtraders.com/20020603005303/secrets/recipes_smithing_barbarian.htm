Barbarian Cultural Smithing

Please make sure to post corrections/updates to the smithing message board. Note that the smithing recipes that are listed for the Northmanl forge are ONLY to be tried by barbarian smiths (you cannot use illusion to fool the forge), and they MUST be combined using your racial Northman forge. The May 8 upgrade to Barbarian Smithing has been added in, and the NPC text for the new recipes can be found at the bottom of the page. Stats for the weapons and armor can be found on the Barbarian armor/weapon Stats page.

NPC text for the May 8 recipes:

You say, 'Hail, Maggie Shawnasy'
Maggie Shawnasy says ''Good day, Tazzax! You look cold indeed; come and warm yourself by the forge. Frostbite is a cruel foe for the unprepared!' Maggie winks at you, 'Tell me, do you much about our special [Halas armor]?'

You say, 'What halas armor?'
Maggie Shawnasy says ''Oh, Uncle Kevan has been making Northman armor since the dawn of time, it seems. He's ancient!' She lowers her voice, so that only you can hear, 'Well, recently, he's been experimenting with some [new types] over the Northman forge, though he refuses'

You say, 'What new types?'
Maggie Shawnasy says ''You're a curious one!' She laughs, and begins to whisper in your ear. 'He's been telling me about [ringmail], [platemail] and [boned leather] armour. April saw him talking to a strange fellow in a dark, hooded robe recently, she thinks that may be f'

You say, 'What ringmail?'
Maggie Shawnasy says ''With a smithy hammer, large patchwork or tattered armour piece, frost temper, the appropriate large chainmail pattern, a little magical ivory and some [infused rings], he's been making some strange ringmail.''

You say, 'What infused rings?'
Maggie Shawnasy says ''With velium rings, I saw him work in a large brick of medium quality ore with him smithy hammer. He used a strange temper that I've not seen before. April thinks that she overheard the Dark Stranger mention Celestial Temper. I don't know what it may'

You say, 'What platemail?'
Maggie Shawnasy says ''What heavy armour this was! Even Uncle looked to have a hard time lifting it. He used his smithy hammer, some [jointing], frost temper, a large plate mold and some [infused rings]. I think I saw leather padding, and some magical ivory too.''

You say, 'What jointing?'
Maggie Shawnasy says ''Everyone knows how to make jointing! Uncle was using medium quality rings, which he used his smithy hammer and file upon, and a little water.''

You say, 'What boned leather?'
Maggie Shawnasy says ''Uncle gave this armour to Peppes - you know, the roaming beastlord that has been entertaining everyone here in town recently? He made it in the same way as he made the [chainmail], except with some [special boning] instead of rings.'

You say, 'What special boning?'
Maggie Shawnasy says ''With velium boning, he worked in a small brick of medium quality ore, using some of that curious new temper, and his smithy hammer. It seemed to glow with an inner light, it really is odd stuff. What can it be?''
