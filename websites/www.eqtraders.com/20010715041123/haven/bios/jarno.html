Jarno Wiggletoes - halfling druid

 

Jarno was born to a typical halfling family in a typical halfling village on a typical day with the typical amount of fanfare that this typically accompanies.

As he grew, his parents much to their disappointment realized that Jarno was anyhing but typical. He was loud; boisterous; given to impulsive action; and generally not proper(Jarno's mother blamed his father for these bad traits saying that it was from his side of the family...but then, you know how families are).

Jarno's future path was really set when he was only 10 years old. He was out with his family working on the farm as they were getting the yearly JumJum crop ready for harvesting, when all of a sudden some goblins appeared. The goblins had chained black wolves as attack pets with them. They let the wolves go to attack the family, but when the wolves came to Jarno they stopped in their tracks. They wouldnt attack him. In fact, they let him pet them. When the goblins came to drive their wolves to attack, the wolves turned on the goblins and chased them away. With his family dead, the village druid took Jarno to his Aunt Niami (who is related to him through her cousin and his father Rupert). The village druid told her the story of what happened at the village and Niami passed Jarno off to the Training Druids.

Thus has Jarno, friend of wolves and bears, has begun his journeys through the Lands of Norrath.
