Planes of Power Recipes

A temporary spot for PoP recipes found in books and by hailing various NPCs in the Plane of Knowledge. Note that some of the NPCs will not give out their recipes unless you meet certain requirements (such as high skill in the tradeskill in question.)

The first column ("V?") indicates if the recipe has yet been verified. All recipes that do NOT have a "Y" in that column are our "best guess" based on the recipe text. In many cases, naming and exact recipe are guesswork based on an interpretation of the book/hail text. If there's no "V" listed, then there are no guarantees that the recipe will work as listed.

Imbues - the spells themselves

These spells are now made by players, using the recipe below. The imbue diamond spell needed can be found in the Temple of Marr in North Freeport from Locarme Zennix (92, 287). The spell combine is a no fail combine and can be done by anyone. Yes, I repeat, the spells are obtained by a recipe, and it is how you make the actual piece of paper that needs to be scribed.
  spell: imbue ____ raw diamond, spell: imbue diamond, PLUS component from below list
  • imbue disease: bubonian blood
  • imbue storm: storm rider blood
  • imbue fire: fire mephit blood
  • imbue valor: metallic liquid
  • imbue justice: alkaline etched stone
  • imbue nightmare: nightmare mephit blood
