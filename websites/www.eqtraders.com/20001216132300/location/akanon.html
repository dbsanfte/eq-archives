Ak'anon Trade Supplies

Many of the merchants in this city are clockwork, and so do not have individual names. A /loc will have to do for the ones not near recognizeable landmarks.

City evaluation: Baking, fishing, basic fletching, basic tailoring, jewelcraft and smithing are the more accessible skills for a young gnome. Brewing and pottery are difficult without additional travel.

More items can be found in the nearby Steamfont Mountains
