Tailoring

Tailoring is the art of working with pelts, hides, and silks in order to make armor and various containers. It is one of the easier skills to get started, but one of the hardest tradeskills in which to grandmaster. Three races (halflings, wood elves and erudites) also have tailoring as their cultural specialty.
