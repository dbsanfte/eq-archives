Spell Research Recipes

For quite some time, EQTC has resisted the coaxing, whimpers, and sometimes outright demands for a spell research section on the site. It's always been an odd duck, with some folks arguing that it was a calss skill, others arguing that it was a tradeskill, and, frankly, too much doggone work to sort out all the mish-mash of information out there. It was left to other sites, such as Mythiran Tower and EQCasters Realm, that specialized in spellcaster information.

Times change. Tradeskills change. With the advent of the LoY expansion, spell research combines are required for some new tailoring recipes.

We won't promise to have a ton of information up here in any great hurry. We won't even promise to make a new navbutton for it, given that we're trying to devote our energies to the new database. However, we will gradually add links to the listing below:
