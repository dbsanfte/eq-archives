Oggok Trade Supplies

(See companion area Feerrott for items available in the next zone.)

City Evaluation: Alchemy, baking, fishing, fletching and brewing (bog juice only) are the only skills that can be performed with travel only between Oggok and Feerott.  However, travel to the neighboring city of Grobb allows you to also try out more serious Brewing, as well Jewelcraft and Tailoring. Pottery will require travel to multiple zones (Grobb has clay, but you need to go to Neriak for firing sheets, and to Oasis for sketches). My thanks go to Krobuu of E'Ci for some corrections and additions to the below information.

Alchemy

  • medicine bag, mercury, comfrey, sumbul, fennel, bladderwrack, eucalyptus leaf, clubmiss, echinacea, lady's mantle, hyssop, jalamasi, hydrangea, mystic ash, elderberry, clover, dhea, heliotrope, oakmoss - Garakbar (unlabeled room with skull floating in the middle of it)
  • medicine bag - Rora Dirtstamp (unlabeled room with skull floating in the middle of it)

Baking

  • bread, fish - Chef Dooga (Meet and Drink Tew Bie) - note that Dooga will KOS non-ogres, so beware
  • bread, short beer - Crunga & Erung (unmarked shop down hall from armor merchants)
  • oven - next to Crunga & Erung in unmarked shop down hall from armor merchants
  • bat wings - Hrak (shaman guild)
  • (main cooking ingredients in Feerrott)

Brewing

  • cask, bottle - Brokk Boxtripper (near Priest of Discord)
  • bottle - Angrog (next to tunnel leading to Feerott)
  • brew barrel - next to Clurg (Meet and Drink Tew Bie)
  • snake scales - Hrak (shaman guild)
  • ogre swill (needed for smithing temper) - Clurg (255, -247)
  • (main brewing ingredients in Grobb)

Fishing

  • fishing pole, bait - Angrog (next to tunnel leading to Feerott), Sinsaal (inside first tunnel on left in the wall of tunnels)

Fletching

  • arrowheads (3), shafts (4), fletch (3), vane (3), groove nock (3), fletching kit - Praak (Bouncer Keep)
  • (bowmaking supplies in Feerrott)

Jewelcraft

  • peridot - Hrak (entry to shaman guild)

Pottery

  • pottery wheel, kiln - inside first tunnel on left (facing the wall of tunnels)

Smithing

  • forge - inside Metil Armer
  • imbue jade [Rallos Zek] spell - Barsk (shaman guild)
  • Oggok Forge (cultural forge) - 141, 931 ("end of let of 3 tunnels" or so says the ogre who sent this in)
  • large chainmail patterns - Pugla (98, 849)
  • information on ogre cultural weapons [hail him] - Varrok Neckchopper (321, -206)
  • information on ogre armor and shields [hail him] - Zarg Knightbane (245, -194)
  • large plate molds, large shield molds, smithy hammer, Ikthar's Fine Steel Skin, Firepride's Guide to Shieldcraft - Bubbgrib (291, -206)
  • (other items in Feerrott)

Tailoring

  • patterns, small sewing kit, How To Sew (3) - Juk Hidebeater (Lether Armer)
  • small sewing kit, How To Sew (large size only) - Duuga Tearstopper (Lether Armer)
  • (lg sewing kit in Feerrott)
