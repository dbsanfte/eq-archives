Freeport WIZ/MAG/ENC Armor Quests

One of many new quests for young adventurers, this one is for human Wizards, Enchanters and Mages in Freeport. Pieces are lore (other than bracer), no drop and WIZ/MAG/ENC, HUM only. Robe is orange, other pieces are a red leather graphic. Please note that while the bracers are not lore, the bracer materials often are, so make your first bracer before you get the parts for a second one

Shana Liskia in the Academy in West Freeport gives out quests for Mages, Wizards and Enchanters. She gives you an enchanted sewing kit and a pattern for each of the pieces that you can get. Molds are given as you ask her about the specific armor piece. These items are reported to look like reddish leather when worn.

Combine each of the following in and enchanted sewing kit, then combine the result and the proper mold, in the loom, to get the proper armor piece.
