Here are a few of my favorite EQ links. More sites will be added as I have more time to websurf

General links:

  • www.everquest.com - Sony's main EQ website is good not only for anyone just starting out, but also contains news of interest to the serious gamer (scheduled downtime, policy changes, etc.)
  • www.eqvault.com - This site contains some of the most recent news about the game, player guides, maps and more. Their message board may be slow, but it's quite active, and a good source for information.
  • eq.stratics.com - This is the best site I've seen yet for depth of coverage on a myriad of topics, up-to-date news, and more.
  • EQ'lizer - chock full of information on quests, abilities and much, much more.
  • EverLore - offers a deeper look at EverQuest including news, guides, discussions, quest info, etc.
  • AllaKhazam's Magical Realm has merged with EverSTOMPED to bring you information on cheats, quests, and more.
  • EverCrest - a semi-daily comic strip that any die-hard EQ'er can empathize with
  • EverQuest Map Shop - some amazing maps of the various zones. While the majority of the site is Japanese, it is a must visit for anyone looking for good maps.
  • EQ Atlas - another good spot for maps and travel guides. If you need information before visiting Kunark, be sure to read their zone guides!
  • EQ Maps - contains not only maps and travel guides, but item and NPC listings as well
  • EQ Portal - Are you positive there's an EQ site out there that contains information you need or want, but can't find it anywhere? You may be able to find a link to it off this site.
  • Still can't find the site you're looking for? EQ's Best Link Site may have what you need.
  • Seekers of Lore - a guild that seeks out knowledge of the world of Norrath, their site contains maps, quest information and more
  • The EQ Links site looks like it's fast becoming a good reference material to find links to guides, quests, items, etc. A lot of research has gone into linking to some of the best EQ information on the net. Additionally, they provide direct links to some game-specific news posts that should be of interest to many.
  • Maximum EQ is chock full of information on armor, weapons and more, including known prices for various armor and weapons in the game. (It's a good spot to check before you sell that rare item, and before you buy something that sounds neat from another player.)
  • EQ Convention is a new site where tradesfolk can set up "booths" to advertise their player-made wares.
  • NEW! /gu comics - I can't believe I've had this site on my bookmarks for this long, and had forgotten to include it here. This pithy comic often produces maniacal laughter in my house, in my guild, and sometimes even makes it to my message boards when it's tradeskill related. Now you too can say "been there, done that, got the /gu t-shirt to prove it."

Specialized links:

  • NEW! The Ogre Forj is a smithing site dedicated to ogres and other dark races.
  • Everquests - a site for listing player-run events on each server. Just the spot to list your wedding or recruitment drive, and/or look for afternoon diversion in the form of a quest, etc.
  • Everquest Monks is a monk-specific message board.
  • Monkly Business is a great monk site that I just discovered with up-to-date news and information of interest to monks (added 11/9/00)
  • EQ Casters Realm has great information on spell research, etc. for pure casters. They also have some tradeskill info, but since these aren't the prime focus of the site, you might want to check here first for up-to-date recipes.
  • The Shaman's Crucible includes a lot of stuff for shamen, including some hot-off-the press information on alchemy
  • Belgar's Ultimate Guide to Alchemy is great alchemy and shaman site.
  • Gillaen's Alchemy Page also contains good information for you shamen out there.
  • Mythiran Tower is another site that provides recipes for spell research, among other things. They also have some very active message boards for those trying to get niggling questions answered, trade words for runes, etc.
  • Fulcrum's Tinkering Compendium contains a lot of wonderful information for tinkers
  • Oggok - The Ogre City has much information of interest to all you misunderstood ogres out there.
  • The Tal Kor Jewelry Academy has some great information for the starting jeweler, including cost estimates and breakdowns of jewelry by gems, by attributes, etc.
  • Aoenla's Fletching Guide contains a lot of wonderful information on making bows and arrows.
  • Xyriel's Guide to Brewing has some great information for those interested in brewing.
  • Order of the Black Rose is home of information geared towards rogues. Their poison guide is wonderful.
  • The Rangers Glade is a lovely resource for rangers.
  • The Smithy is a site dedicated to the smiths of Norrath. It's still getting off the ground, but it's looking good so far.
  • The Povar Trades Association is a guild on Povar for trade skills practitioners.
