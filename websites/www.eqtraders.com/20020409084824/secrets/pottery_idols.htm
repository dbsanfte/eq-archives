Pottery Idols

Pottery idols are deity-specific items for primary, secondary or ranged slot and can be only wielded by worshippers of the proper deity. However, any potter can make these items. (The person who imbues the gem must worship the proper deity, the person using the item must worship the same deity, but the potter's deity is not checked.)
