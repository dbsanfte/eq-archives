Imbued Gem Information

 

Healers (cleric, druid, shaman) can call down the blessing of their deity upon a gem with the use of a level 29 spell. (For Solusek Ro, it is a wizard spell.) Each healer can only scribe and cast the imbue spell for the gem specific to their deity, after which a tradesperson can use the imbued gem in crafting deity-specific items. (The tradesperson does not need to worship a specific deity in order to craft items for that deity.) Some gems currently only function in imbued jewelry and pottery idols.

Below is a list of imbued gems, and their known uses (plus links to the recipes).
