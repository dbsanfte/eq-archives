February 17, 2002 - Another day ... another patch?
Lo and behold, when I logged on this morning, there was a patch message! Nothing was mentioned about the Butcherblock HQ ore vendors, or about the hilt mold vendors, but mention was made of the Bazaar water and HQ ore vendors. Early reports are back from someone who purchased 150 large bricks before the Bazaar merchant ran out, and "about 300 or so" small bricks, and the merchant had still not run out. This is happy news indeed for our smiths! And yes, three water vendors now in the Bazaar!

In other news, reinforced rockhopper armor, which had been in a recipe book, but bugged, since Luclin launch, is finally working. Stats are slowly going up on the Luclin Recipes page.

February 15, 2002 - More from the patch ...
While I'm still not fully unpacked, I managed to verify a few things, and folks on the boards verified a few more with regards to the February 13 patch:

  • Three baking recipes that were introduced when Luclin launched (bug bites, batwing pie, deadly medly) are verified as now working. Yields have been determined, and some initial narrowing down of the trivial has occurred
  • The Shar Vahl Figurine step of the progessive Vah Shir pottery quest has been mostly fixed. The figurine can now be made, but the quest tools are lost on a failed combine.
  • One of the brewing recipes we received from Brodder's Diaries, gnomish cleaning fluid, that has long been a perplexing puzzle, is finally functioning.
  • Only the Qeynos merchant for high quality ore has been located. Many pairs of eyes have searched high and low in Kaladim and the Bazaar, and not managed to locate the promised vendors. (The merchant that sells HQ ore in the Bazaar on Test has not shown his face on the live servers.) We also cannot locate the promised water vendors in the Bazaar. Initial reports on the "limited" supply of HQ ore on the Qeynos merchant seem to indicate that he sells 75 blocks of high quality ore before he runs out.
  • In the "please, sir, might we have another?" list, the non-visible pieces of black panther armor are finally BST wearable. Unlike the visible pieces, however, they did not expand the races that could wear these items. While we may have some happy iksar beastlords around, folks like my kitty are still waiting to be able to wear more of the set. Hopefully this will be resolved in the next patch ...
  • The "jury" (all the folks on the message boards) is still out on whether drop rates for cultural temper items were increased. Some say yes, many say no. I'm not sure if we'll ever know for sure, but there are a lot of hopeful smiths that have gone back to farming ...
  • I've yet to hear verification on if the owlbear feather darts portion of the progressive Shar Vahl fletching quest are now returning the proper tools. If anyone has confirmation since the patch, please post on the message boards. (Balidil is hard at work on a writeup for this quest, and we hope to have something in the Quests section of the site for you in the next few days.)

Addendum: Has anyone been able to find new sources for hilt molds? While it was mentioned in the patch, we've been unable to find any new vendor sources for these yet. I've the sinking suspicion that the only vendor-change from the patch message that actually made it into the patch was the HQ ore vendor in Qeynos. I'd be more than happy to be proved wrong, however! Please post to the message boards if you find hilt mold vendors that are not already listed on the Smithing Trade Supplies page.

Addendum to the Addendum: It has been confirmed that several zone-related tradeskill items were not patched as intended (Bazaar water vendors, two of the HQ ore vendors, etc.). The most up-to-date information we have can be found here.

 

February 14, 2002 - Happy Valentine's Day!
In a sure sign that Bristlebane loves to see one of his High Priestesses of Mischief scamper frantically to and fro, there were a LOT of tradeskill changes put in via yesterday's patch ... while the House Ogre and DenMother were away on a mini 2-day holiday. Please have patience with me as I verify various things before listing them all here. (I really hate just listing a patch message verbatim, then finding out that things are different than expected, etc., so I like to make sure things are verified before they're posted.) I hope to have a decent-sized update tomorrow listing verified patch information. In the meantime, here's a couple tidbits:

  • The trades-impaired trolls have been out shopping! Many tradeskill supplies that the light races have taken for granted, have finally made it to Grobb. The Grobb Trade Supply listing has been updated accordingly.
  • High quality ore has been added to vendors in the Bazaar, Qeynos and Kaladim. Supplies are rumored to be "limited", and more details will be available soon (hopefully after I wade through the many, many posts that piled up over the last 2 days). So far, I've only managed to get details on the Qeynos merchant. The trade supplies pages will be updated as I find information on the Bazaar and Kaladim merchants.
  • clay and firing sheets for pottery now stack ... it's the little things in life that mean a lot to us sometimes
  • enchanted/imbued dwarven plate armor now has the same AC as enchanted dwarven plate (several pieces had lower AC values before)

Expect more updates to sneak onto the site over the next few hours, and a more detailed front page update tomorrow after I've had a chance to wade through everything.

January 28, 2002 - Farewell Rat Ear Pies
No longer can we make jokes about "who ate all the rat ear pies?" (Or maybe this will be the cause for more such jokes now that they're gone?) Lo and behold, a startled cook announced today that their stockpile of rat ear pies had become ... rat ear turnovers? I scampered in game to check, and sure enough, our old friend the rat ear pie is no more. I've not had a chance yet to see if the quest version of the pie also underwent a name change or not.

In other tradeskill news, the addition of grimling blood to the drop tables last week was welcome news to questing potters ... sort of. The final step of the Vah Shir Figurine quest still appears to need some kinks worked out of it. (It's been reported in, but with the Fan Fest over the weekend, etc., it may take time before it's tackled.)

EZBoards are acting a tad slow lately, so please be patient if you're trying to surf the message boards for information.

December 27, 2001
The December 20 patch, which advertised some tradeskill changes, may not have had as many tradeskill changes as we thought. At least some of the rings (such as some of the quality rings) are reported as still not stacking. Other reports state that bait and fishing flies are not able to be equipped in the ammo slot, as advertised, and if there is a bonus to the fishing skill available from player-made fishing flies, it's not yet visible. On the positive side, new tradeskill merchants have been added in several spots, and now that I'm back from traveling, I'll be doing more zone verification to make sure we catch them all.
