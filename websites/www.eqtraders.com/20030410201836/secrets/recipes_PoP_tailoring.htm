Planes of Power Tailoring Recipes

A temporary spot for PoP tailoring recipes found in books and by hailing various NPCs in the Plane of Knowledge. Note that some of the NPCs will not give out their recipes unless you meet certain requirements (such as high skill in the tradeskill in question.) These recipes must be combined using a planar sewing kit sold in Plane of Knowledge. There are also indications that some of the combines may be skill-restricted, as a 193 tailor was unable to make the armor combine attempts without the used of a 15% skill modifying item. We suspect that a 220 skill (raw or modified with a +tradeskill item) will be required to make these armor sets.

The first column ("V?") indicates if the recipe has yet been verified. All recipes that do NOT have a "Y" in that column are our "best guess" based on the recipe text. In many cases, naming and exact recipe are guesswork based on an interpretation of the book/hail text. If there's no "V" listed, then there are no guarantees that the recipe will work as listed.
