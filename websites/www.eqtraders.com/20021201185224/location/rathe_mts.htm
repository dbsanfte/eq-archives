Rathe Mountains Trade Supplies

(Note: Remember to /con before you approach. There are merchants here for every race/religion, but nobody will be able to shop from all these folks. As far as we can tell, the innkeeps will sell to everyone, the rest are standard for their races.)

Alchemy

  • empty vials, resin, lacquer, acid, How to Dye and Stain - Ukla (ogre/troll camp, 1730, 1300) used to make dye

Baking

  • short beer, water, bread - Innkeep Serge & Innkeep Troy (4295, 840), Sven Drinn & Philicia Drinn (inns near paladin camp, 3985, -1376. Note that the Drinn's are badly overpriced on selling and don't pay well on buying)

Brewing

  • bottle, cask - Roshmael Tandre (gypsy camp near Feerrott, 1560, -883)

Jewelcraft

  • 18 gems, jeweler's kit, Legacy of Jewelcraft - Darfumpel Zirubbel (side building next to inns, 4100, 900)

Make Poison

  • empty vials, resin, lacquer, acid, How to Dye and Stain - Ukla (ogre/troll camp, 1730, 1300) used to make dye

Smithing

  • small/large bricks of medium quality ore, block of medium quality ore, smithing hammer, empty vials, resin, lacquer, acid, How to Dye and Stain - Ukla (ogre/troll camp, 1730, 1300)

Tailoring

  • patterns, small sewing kit, How To Sew (large sizes) - Chizzlik (ogre camp, 1723, 1301)

Tinkering

  • lockpicks - Lianna Ferasa (gypsy camp, 3979, 1469)
  • compass, large lantern, lockpicks, water - Chandra Kali (gypsy gamp, 3925, 1399), Susanna (paladin camp near Feerrott zone entrance, 2916, -764), Viira Bali (gypsy camp near Feerrott, 1643, -867)
  • large lantern, fishing pole, water - Guzzla (ogre camp, 1749, 1289)
