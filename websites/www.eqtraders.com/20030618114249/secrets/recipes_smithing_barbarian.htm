Barbarian Cultural Smithing

Please make sure to post corrections/updates to the smithing message board. Note that the smithing recipes that are listed for the Northman forge are ONLY to be tried by barbarian smiths (you cannot use illusion to fool the forge), and they MUST be combined using your racial Northman forge.

Stats for the weapons and armor can be found on the Barbarian armor/weapon Stats page.

Trivials for many "old cultural" recipes changed with the June 5 patch. Please post any trivial updates to the Smithing Trivials thread.
