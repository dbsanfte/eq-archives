Silver visible pieces are about the same shade as shaman totemic armor, electrum is a brighter green (close to the 3-resin dye), gold is a golden color, and platinum is a medium bluish/purple (darker than mithril, lighter than cleric planar) - so far the only differences noted with using different metals in the non-enchanted version has been the color of the visible pieces, as well as the difficulty of the piece

Armor pieces have the magic item tag.
