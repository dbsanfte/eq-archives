Xanthe's Earring - The Gifts of the Elves

The first three gifts come to us from elven lands. Original writeup courtesy of Xanthe Ravenwolf. Known trivials are now included, as well as links to the various sources for ingredients.

First Gift of the Elves

Demi-Sec Champagne

  • Demi Sec Champage = 1 Champagne Magnum + 1 Soda Water + 1 Wine Yeast + 2 Grapes (unstacked) (brew barrel, brewing trivial >250)

Other combines that you need:

  • Champagne Magnum = 1 Enchanted Gold Bar + 1 Opal + 1 Bottle (jewelers kit, jewelcraft trivial 195 or 196)
  • Soda Water = 1 Soda + 1 water (brew barrel, brewing trivial 58)

 

Second Gift of the Elves

Mistletoe Cutting Sickle

  • Mistletoe Cutting Sickle= Blessed Sickle Blade + Blessed Sickle Hilt + Blessed Sickle Pommel + Smithy Hammer (forge, smithing trivial >250)

Other Combines that you need:

  • Blessed Sickle Blade = Sheet Metal + Smithy Hammer + Emerald + Curved Blade Mold + Celestial Essence + Blessed Dust of Tunare + Mistletoe Temper (forge, smithing trivial 183)
  • Blessed Hilt = Sheet Metal + Smithy Hammer + Ruby + Hilt Mold + Celestial Essence + Blessed Dust of Tunare + Mistletoe Temper (forge, smithing trivial 180)
  • Blessed Pommel = Sheet Metal + Smithy Hammer + Sapphire+ Pommel Mold + Celestial Essence + Blessed Dust of Tunare + Mistletoe Temper (forge, smithing trivial 182)
  • Celestial Essence = 1 Celestial Solvent + 1 High End Research component (mortar and pestle, no-fail combine)
  • Mistletoe Temper = 1 Celestial Essence + 1 Elven Wine + 1 Mistletoe (brew barrel, brewing trivial 136)
  • Blessed Dust of Tunare = 1 Imbued Emerald + 1 Celestial Essence + 1 Jar of Acid (kiln, pottery trivial 103)

 

Third Gift of the Elves

Golden Idol of Tunare

  • Unfired Golden Idol of Tunare= 1 Gold Bar+ 1 Idol Sketch + 1 enchanted block of clay + 1 water + 1 Celestial Essence + Sculpting Tools + 1 Vial of Distilled Mana + 1 Imbued Emerald (pottery wheel, pottery trivial 242)
  • Then combine Unfired Golden Idol of Tunare + Divine Crystalline Glaze + High Quality Firing Sheet (kiln, firing trivial will be considerably lower than trivial on pottery wheel)


Other combines that you need

  • Celestial Essence = 1 Celestial Solvent + 1 High End Research component (mortar and pestle, no-fail combine)
  • Divine Crystalline Glaze = 1 Permafrost Crystals + 1 Iron Oxide + 1 White Lead + 1 water (glaze mortar, no-fail combine)
  • White Lead = Lead Sulfide + Limestone (mortar and pestle, no-fail combine)
  • enchanted block of clay is made by having an enchanter cast the level 8 Enchant Clay spell on a large block of clay
