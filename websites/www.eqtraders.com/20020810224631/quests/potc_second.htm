Xanthe's Earring/PoTC - The Gifts of the Humans

The second three gifts come to us from human lands. Original writeup courtesy of Xanthe Ravenwolf. Known trivials are now included, as well as links to the various sources for ingredients.

First Gift of Mankind

Blessed Fishing Pole

  • Blessed Fishing Rod = Branch of Planar Oak + Celestial Essence + Blessed Dust of Karana + Spool + treated silk fibres (combine in tackle box, fishing trivial >200) ... please note that this fishing pole, like every other fishing pole in the game, can break if you fish with it.


Other combines that you need

  • Celestial Essence = 1 Celestial Solvent + 1 High End Research component (mortar and pestle, no-fail combine)
  • Blessed Dust of Karana = 1 Imbued Plains Pebble + Celestial Essence + Jar of Acid (kiln, pottery trivial 103)
  • Spool = 1 Sandalwood + 2 Velium Bits (unstacked) (forge, smithing trivial <=109)
  • velium bits = 2 small pieces velium, coldain velium temper (forge, smithing trivial <=50)
  • coldain velium temper = vendor sold *or* coldain heater + 4 celestial essence (brew barrel, brewing trivial 136)
  • coldain heater =water, allspice, brown algae, shot glass (brew barrel, brewing trivial 158)
  • treated silk fibres = Silk Cord + Dragon Egg Oil (sewing kit, tailoring trivial 150)
  • Silk Cord = 3 Silk Threads (sewing kit, tailoring trivial 26)
  • silk thread = 2 unstacked spiderling silk (sewing kit, no-fail combine, tailoring trivial 15)
  • Dragon Egg Oil = Dragon Egg + water (brew barrel, brewing trivial 150)

 

Second Gift of Mankind

Star Ruby Encrusted Stein

  • Unfired Star Ruby Encrusted Stein = Lacquered Star Ruby + Large Block of Magic Clay + Sculpting Tools + Ceramic Lining Sketch + water + Celestial Essence (pottery wheel, pottery trivial >250)
  • Then combine: Unfired Star Ruby Encrusted Stein + High Quality Firing Sheet (kiln, firing trivial will be significantly lower than the pottery wheel trivial) = Star Ruby Encrusted Stein

Other combines that you need:

  • Lacquered Star Ruby = 1 Star Ruby + Jar of Lacquer (jeweller's kit, jewelcraft trivial >110 <=157)
  • Celestial Essence = 1 Celestial Solvent + 1 High End Research component (mortar and pestle, no-fail combine)


Third Gift of Mankind

Ceremonial Solstice Robe

  • Combine: Gem Studded Chain + 3 Sacred Tunare Silks (unstacked) + Tunic Pattern + Embroidery Needle (sewing kit, tailoring trivial >250, gem studded chain and embroidery needle returned on failure, embroidery needle returned on success)


Other combines that you need

  • Gem Studded Chain = Worked Silver Chain + Sapphire + Ruby + Emerald (jeweller's kit, jewelcraft trivial >250)
  • Worked Silver Chain = 1 Enchanted Bar of Silver + 1 Metal Bits + water (forge, smithing trivial 111)
  • Sacred Tunare Silk = 1 Silk Swatch + Celestial Essence + Blessed Dust of Tunare (sewing kit, tailoring trivial 135)
  • Celestial Essence = 1 Celestial Solvent + 1 High End Research component (mortar and pestle, no-fail combine)
  • Blessed Dust of Tunare = 1 Imbued Emerald + 1 Celestial Essence + 1 Jar of Acid (kiln, pottery trivial 103)
  • Embroidery Needle = Needle Mold + water + Metal Bits + Celestial Essence (forge, smithing trivial 122)
