High Elf Cultural Smithing

Many of these recipes are as-yet unverified. Please make sure to send all confirmations/corrections to the DenMother. Note that these recipes are ONLY to be tried by high elven smiths, and they MUST be combined using your racial Koada'dal forge unless otherwise noted.

At the current time, incorrect recipes mixed in a cultural forge will NOT eat your items. Close the forge, open the forge, and your items still should be there. Failed skill checks will, of course, still eat your items, just like with any other trade skill.
