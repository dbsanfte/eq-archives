Sylvan Initiate Armor - Felwithe Cleric Quest

This is one of several youngling armor quests originating in Felwithe. All armor pieces are lore (except bracer), no drop, HIE/CLE only. They are no-fail smithing combines. (This information has been obtained from several sources, so please excuse the variations in formatting ... it's going to be ugly for a while)

The Felwithe Cleric newbie armor quests are initiated with Terren Starwatcher (right behind the cleric guildmaster in the cleric guild in Felwithe) and the pieces are called Sylvan Initiate ___. Terren is your source for the mail assembly kit, as well as the molds needed for the various pieces of armor

Breastplate

1st combine:
four bricks of crude bronze
Water Flask
Crude Breastplate mold
IN FORGE

2nd combine:
Crude Bronze breastplate
Giant Sylvan Bat Fur
2 Mature Arborean Bark
IN MAIL ASSEMBLY KIT

Yields: Sylvan Initiate Breatsplate (AC 12 STR+3 WIS+5)

Greaves

1st combine:
2 bricks of crude bronze
Water Flask
Crude Greaves mold
IN FORGE

2nd combine:
Crude Bronze Greaves
2 Giant Sylvan Bat Fur
2 Arborean Sapling Bark
IN MAIL ASSEMBLY KIT

Yields: Sylvan Initiate Greaves (AC 6 STA+2 CHA+2 WIS+1)

 

Helm

2 bricks of crude bronze, water flask, crude helm mold - combine in a forge

Once that is done, combine the crude bronze helm with a large sylvan bat fur and an arborean sapling bark, in the mail assembly kit.

Yields: Sylvan Initiate Helm (AC 5 WIS+2 HP+5)

Gauntlets

2 bricks of crude bronze, water flask, crude gauntlet mold - combine in a forge

crude gauntlet + 1 large sylvan bat fur + 2 arborean sprout twigs - in your mail assembly kit

Yields: Sylvan Initiate Gauntlets (AC 5 STA+2 WIS+1)

 

Boots

2 bricks of crude bronze, water flask, crude boot mold - combine in forge

crude boots + 2 large sylvan bat fur + 2 arborean sprout roots - in mail assembly kit

Yields: Sylvan Initiate Boots (AC 5 STR+2 WIS+1 HP+5)

Bracer

1 brick of crude bronze, water flask, crude bracer mold - combine in forge

crude bracer + 1 large sylvan bat fur + 1 arborean sprout bark - in mail assembly kit

Yields: Sylvan Initiate Bracer (AC 4 STA+1 WIS+2 CHA+1)

 

Vambraces (arm)

2 bricks of crude bronze, water flask, crude vambrace mold - combine in forge

crude vambraces + 1 giant sylvan bat fur + 2 arborean sapling barks - in mail assembly kit

 

Final test: Need the amulet from the named orc, Gurleg Bribgok. He spawns in the NW corner of the zone near a large log, along with an orc arsonist.

 

Shopping list:
Molds (from Terren)
16 bricks of crude bronze
8 flasks of water
6 large sylvan bat fur
4 giant sylvan bat fur
2 arborean sprout twigs
2 arborean sprout roots
2 arborean sprout bark
5 arborean sapling bark
2 mature arborean bark
