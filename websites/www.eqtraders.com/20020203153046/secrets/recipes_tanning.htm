Known locations for Tanning Agents:

  • yew leaves - ground spawn in Wakening Lands
  • drake eggs - foraged in Skyshrine, Temple of Veeshan, Western Wastes, also rare drop off named drakes in Skyshrine
  • misty acorn - ground spawn in Misty Thicket, foraged in Misty Thicket
  • oak bark - foraged in Kithicor Forest, Warslik's Woods, ground spawn in Butcherblock
  • cobalt cod - fished in Cobalt Scar
  • aviak eggs - foraged in Butcherblock Mountains
  • paeala bark - sold by merchants
