EQ Baking Recipes

As with ALL trade skills, be very sure that a) you do NOT stack any items when combining, and, b) you use water flasks (from a merchant), not summoned or foraged water

NOTE: baking trivial levels seem to have changed recently for many items. Please do send feedback if you find that one varies from what is listed here, but leave the flames behind. Thanks :D Also note that while we had one report of beetle eye pie going trivial above 148, everyone else is still finding it trivial at 142. We do not know if some servers out there have different trivial data right now, or what, but please be aware that things seem a bit flakey.
