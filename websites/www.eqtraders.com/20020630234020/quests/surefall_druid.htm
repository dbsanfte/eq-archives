Surefall Druid Youngling Armor Quest

This is one of several new youngling armor quests appearing throughout the lands. This quest, which originates in Surefall Glade, results in DRU only armor and a DRU only weapon. All pieces of this set should be lore (other than the bracer) and no drop

Salmekia is standing right next to Te'Anara (right in front of you when you port in to Surefall). I am not a human druid, but she still gave me the quest, though I doubt I'll be able to use the rewards...
