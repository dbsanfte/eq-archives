Poison Secrets

Basic information by Chazon Shadowstalker, with many updates by Niami - note that MUCH was added on July 5, that does NOT yet have full details. Rogues using this list should realize that the Poisons that Blind, Poisons that Cripple, Poisons that Kill,Miners Poisons, Silent Weapons of the Black Mask, Cures for Undeath, and Kaxon's Beatuy Tips are taken from initial information on new poisons from the June 29 patch. If you have more details on the items that are unclear, please let me know.
