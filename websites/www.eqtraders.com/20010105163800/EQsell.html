A place for listing player-made items that are for sale, various services (songwriting, weddings, etc.) folks would like to provide, and a spot to post if you're trying to find player-made items, and haven't found anyone on your server able/willing to help you. If you would like your trade, services or wishlist to be included on a list, please email me and I'll be glad to include you. If you use any of the player-merchants listed here, why not tell them that you saw their ad on the EQ Traders Corner? The more word gets out that this area can bring in business for tradesfolk, the more likely they are to list their offerings here, and to tell their friends to get listed as well!

 

***If you do NOT see your server listed, then I have no submissions for your server. Nudge your favorite tradesfolk to get themselves listed and known. <g>***
