When they go live, merchant inventories will also change ... across the entire world. Anyone who wants to "adopt" a zone to re-explore, please remember the following. If you do NOT give me all the correct information, then it will take me even longer than normal to run around and re-verify stuff -- which means it takes longer to be posted on this site. For ANY report of new merchants, new items on merchants, etc., I need 1) zone name, 2) merchant name, 3) merchant /loc, as well as any landmarks, 4) exact item names. Sending me something that says: "The windmill in the Lake of Ill Omen has poison stuff" just means that I have to spend time to get one of my personas over to that zone and then figure out where this doggone windmill is located. ;) It's still nice to get the head's up, but it adds a couple days of delay onto anything being posted, as I have to add it to the pile of things "to do" for the site ... and for those not familiar with the force behind this site, it's a single-person operation that costs me a lot of my personal time and pp to maintain. If you find the info on this site useful, and you want to contribute, jotting down that extra information requested above and sending it to me means that I can free up more of my time on some of the more difficult or more expensive information to verify.

1999

December 10: This will be the last update from the Den Mother until mid-January, as I will be away on travel. Yes, there's someone watching the mailbox, responding as able and posting Marketplace updates, etc., but most stuff WILL stay on hold until I return. That having been said, here's a few other tidbits.

Alchemy: Finally, shamen can train in it properly, as of the patch this week. Supposedly skill increases can now be gained in it as well. It's a start, though the potions are still far too expensive, in my opinion, for what you get in return.

Marketplace: We've added several new player-merchants. Based on email from the last month or so, please remember a few things when sending in email: If you don't include your server name when sending in an ad, it's rather hard to post it. <g> Let us know if you want an email link in the ad, or if you only want potential customers to contact you in-game. Last but not least, if your server isn't listed in the marketplace, it's not because I forgot it, but because nobody has sent in an ad for that server.

Trade supplies locations: Please remember to eyeball the appropriate page before you send email saying you found something, as I've gotten a lot of mail lately for things that are already listed on the site. Please remember to provide enough details on where you found something that is not listed so that I can find it in order to get the details not included in the email.

Stance on recipes/formulas: This site has always left the posting of cheats/recipes/formulas to other sites. My reasoning behind this was that, without player experimentation, we don't find the new and undiscovered recipes. I've provided some links here and there throughout the site for recipes and such for those who want to learn things without costly experimentation. When I return, I might directly link in some of the recipes that I, and other contributors to this site, have found. However, this is a 1-person site that has tried to stay ad-free (other than the remotely hosted message board) and low-maintenance. On top of all other considerations, the amount of extra time I'd have to spend per week on handling corrections/additions to those recipes, handling often irate and incoherent email asking "why do you have these recipes and not those recipes that I really want?" and a myriad of other issues, makes me very hesitant to expand the offerings of this site. I love helping folks learn new things about tradeskills, but I love playing even more. Thanks for your understanding on this.

I hope everyone has a safe and happy holiday season.
