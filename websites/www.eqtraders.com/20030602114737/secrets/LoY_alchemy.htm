LoY Alchemy Recipes

All recipes must be combined by a shaman using their medicine bag.

While two of the steps for the new armor dyes can also be combined via alchemy, they are only being listed on the main LoY recipes page to prevent duplication of information. Further details and exact names, especially on the focus effect potions, will become available as folks are able to work with these new recipes. As always, if you have questions on where to buy various components, your first spot to check should be the alchemy trade supplies page. :)

Focus Effect Potions
These potions have a 3-minute duration at level 1, and a 3-hour duration at level 65 (and scale up, in between). They offer a 5% mana reduction on spells of the proper type and level. These focus effects stack with the focus effects on items. Awareness I affects spells of level 20 and below; awareness II affects spells of level 44 and below; awareness III affects spells of level 60 and below and awareness IV affects spells of level 65 and below. Note: the shaman turgurs/tigirs/tagars line of slows are MAGIC based, not disease based. Complete heal is NOT included on the benefit awareness potions.
