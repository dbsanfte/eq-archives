Felwithe

City evaluation: Fishing, jewelcraft and tailoring are easily practiced by young high elves, as well as basic fletching. Baking is troublesome without a run out to Butcherblock for spices and such. Smithing, pottery and brewing are bad ideas for a young high elf unless you're willing to travel quite a bit.

Baking

  • oven - next to Bait & Tackle (N. Felwithe)

Brewing

  • cask, bottle - Merchant Issynal (Shop of All Holds, N. Felwithe)
  • cask - Merchant Muselender & Barkeep Songwhisper (Tovaniks Venom, N. Felwithe)
  • brew barrel - basement of Tovaniks Venom (N. Felwithe)

Fishing

  • fishing pole, bait - Rolyn Longwalker (Beyond Faydark, N. Felwithe), Merchant Sorintal (Shop of All Holds, N. Felwithe), Fishmonger, Jassa (Bait & Tackle, N. Felwithe)

Fletching

  • arrowheads (3), shafts (4), fletch (3), vane (3), groove nock (3) - Merchant Longarrow (Faydark's Bane, N. Felwithe)
  • (many more fletching items in Kelethin)

Jewelcraft

  • 18 gems, jewelers kit, Legacy of Jewelcraft - Merchant Tyslin (Shop of All Holds, N. Felwithe -- also same exact merchant located in building just inside entrance to S. Felwithe)
  • metal bars (silver, electrum, gold, platinum), Legacy of Jewelcraft, jewelers kit, star ruby, fire emerald, sapphire - Merchant Silspin (next to Bait & Tackle, N. Felwithe)
  • cats eye agate, bloodstone, jasper, peridot, star rose quartz - Merchant Lyssia (Enchanters Guild, S. Felwithe)
  • peridot, wolfs eye agate, malachite - Merchant Jewyln (outside mage guild, S. Felwithe)
  • enchant silver, enchant electrum, enchant gold, enchant platinum spells - Alicia Starshimmer (Enchanters Guild, S. Felwithe)
  • imbue: emerald spell [cleric/druid: Tunare] - Allia Moondancer (cleric guild, N. Felwithe)

Pottery

  • pottery wheel, kiln - behind Faydark's Bane
  • enchant clay spell - Alicia Starshimmer (Enchanters Guild, S. Felwithe)
  • imbue: emerald spell [cleric/druid: Tunare] - Allia Moondancer (cleric guild, N. Felwithe)

Smithing

  • mithril bricks (small/large) mithril blocks, small piece of mithril, sheets of mithril, elven smithy hammer, chainmail patterns - Tanalin Silverkale (upstairs, Shop of All Holds, N. Felwithe) (also hail him for information on mithril chainmail)
  • shield molds - Mearchant Tearlin (Emerald Armor, N. Felwithe)
  • weapon molds (dagger blade, thin blade, short blade, axe head, halberd head, curved blade, mace head, throwing knife, javelin, shuriken, throwing axe, long blade, hammer head, spear head, pick head), oak shaft - Opal Leganyn (Emerald Armor, N. Felwithe)
  • plate armor molds, Ikthar's Fine Steel Skin, Firepride's Guide to Shieldcraft - Nerisia Irontree (upstairs, Emerald Armor, N. Felwithe)
  • imbue: emerald spell [cleric/druid: Tunare] - Allia Moondancer (cleric guild, N. Felwithe)
  • enchant: mithril [enchanter] - Merchant Lyssia (enchanter guild, S. Felwithe)
  • koada'dal forge - inside Emerald Armor
  • (other molds and books in Kelethin)

Tailoring

  • patterns, sm. sewing kit - Merchant Tissan (Faydark's Bane, N. Felwithe)
  • sm. sewing kit - Merchant Silvenspin (Shop of All Holds, N. Felwithe), Merchant Moonthread (building just inside entrance to S. Felwithe)
  • 10-slot loom - next to Merchant Tyslin (S. Felwithe)
  • (large sewing kit in Kelethin)
