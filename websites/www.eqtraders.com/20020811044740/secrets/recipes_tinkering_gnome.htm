These tradeskill enhancers give you a percentage increase to your skill with regards to success/failures, but are capped at a modified 255. They do not affect skill gain, only your chance to succeed on item creation.

  • darkclaw claws drop off saurek darkclaws in Shadeweavers Thicket
  • porous mineral block drops off netherbians in Netherbian Lair
  • belt of leathery fungus flesh drops off plerg phlarg fiends in Paludal Caverns
