Before you volunteer to "help" with this section

For those of you who've been following this section for a while, you know we've been through a LOT of volunteer fashion editors, but that little in this section has changed (it's been made tons worse by the change in graphics due to Luclin models). A lot of folks have sent in images, only to have them not show up here, even after waiting a couple of months. Unfortunately, I can't handle the load of this section myself, and any volunteers that I get are, well, volunteers. I can't force them to put things up. While I apologize for the lack of updates, I'm likely far more frustrated about it than you folks are, as I invest a lot of time into getting new fashion editors settled in, getting them access, forwarding them whatever backlogged images I've saved, playing mail-tag with questions and answers, etc ... time I could have spent working on the site. I understand about real life getting in the way, really I do, but as sometimes months go by with nothing happening, it makes me want to cry, scream, and/or just give up and delete this section of the site.

Please, please, please, don't volunteer unless you are positive you are willing and able to commit several hours per week, a lot of mailbox space, and some solid knowledge of image editing and HTML coding, to the effort. Otherwise, you're wasting my time, your time, and the time of all the folks who go to the trouble of getting screenshots of their hard-made armor. (Additionally, after some of the files I've seen in my mailbox recently that claim to be images for the site, you'd better have a very good virus program in place, and know how to spot suspicious files.)

If you're still interested in helping, feel free to send me an EZBoard private message and make SURE you include an email address in the message ... then expect it to take at least several days for me to respond, if it doesn't get totally lost in the flood of spam that's hit there lately. :(
