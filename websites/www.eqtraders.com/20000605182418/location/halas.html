Halas Trade Supplies

City evaluation: Cooking, fishing, pottery, fletching and tailoring are great here, with the caveats listed below. Smithing seriously needs imported goods from other lands, and you can totally forget about becoming a jeweler without relocating, or taking repeated shopping trips. Many thanks to Lucian on the Bristlebane server for locating a few "missing" items for me.

Caveats: Many of the cooking recipes that are "common" recipes to start with in other lands are simply not feasible in barbarian lands -- there are no bats, rats, fire beetles and the like to give up the needed pieces/parts for things such as "Edible Goo", "Batwing Crunchies" and "Fish Rolls."  However, with a bit of recipe research, there are plenty of other things you can bake in this area.  Tailoring is great, with quite an abundance of wolf and bear pelts to practice on, and there are many larger spiders out on the plains with which to make the new silk armors. Fletching will need a quick trip to Surefall for a fletching kit, but you can then sit and make arrows all afternoon long in town.
