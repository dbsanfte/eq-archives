Tradeskill High Fashion

A lot of times, folks will come to the site, asking what a certain type of player-made armor will look like when worn by a specific race. Others want to know what the various shades of dyed fine plate armor will look like. Attempts have been made to gather images from many race/class combinations for popular armors. Please note that these images are using pre-Luclin textures, and things will look different for those of you who have your Luclin textures turned on for the race in question. While we hope to display the Luclin textures in the future, it will not only need many submissions from you folks who are wearing/using/making this armor, but it will also need significant image editing and web publishing time. It will be tackled only as time permits.

We have a new Fashion Editor as of late December 2001, and she'll need a bit of settling in time before she's caught up from the backlog caused by having no steady editor for this section of the site for many, many months. Submissions for cultural armor and Velious and Luclin tailoring should be sent to Irishcreme57@hotmail.com.

  • Cultural armor (smithing and tailoring, also includes Wu's Fighting Armor)
  • Velious tailoring
  • Dyed fine steel plate (dwarven model). For color samples on other races, see the new fashion table built by Darmer. Once the holiday dust settles, I'll be chasing him down to see if he needs it hosted over here due to space/traffic issues.
