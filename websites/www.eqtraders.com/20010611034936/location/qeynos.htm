Qeynos Trade Supplies Locations

City assessment: life is good here for most tradesfolk. The exceptions are smithing and pottery, for which I've not found all items needed. The Qeynos area is the best area yet for those who wish to pursue advanced fletching, spell researchers have materials on hand, and there is an abundance of pelt-bearing animals in the hunting areas for tailors. Bodies of water abound for the fisherfolk, and jewelers and cooks will find their needs well met as well.

  • oven - inside Voleen's Fine Baked Goods (S. Qeynos)
  • milk - Sneed & Tanlyn Galliway (Galliways Trading Post, N. Qeynos)
