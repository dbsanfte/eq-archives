Just why did I make this site anyway? Partly for myself, since I was gathering some of this knowledge anyway; partly for others trying trade skills, and partly for those trying to buy made items.

 

For myself: I spent a lot of time trying to find everything I needed to practice my trades in my home city of choice ... and failed. After risking life and limb to get to another city, I spent quite a bit of time hopping through 5 different zones to find everything I wanted. It made sense to make notes, just like it makes sense to share that information in hopes that other exploring traders will return the favor for other zones..

For others trying trade skills: There's a great feeling of accomplishment when you successfully make your first trade item, but there can be a lot of frustration getting to that point. I don't want to give you all the secrets of your trade, which is why you won't find recipes, gem/metal combinations, etc. readily available. What you will find are things to give you a helping hand -- basic information on the skill, perhaps a tidbit or two to get you headed in the right direction, some knowledge of how readily you will find trade items in your area, and an opportunity to sell your finished product to a wider audience.  Trade skills will grow and change as the world grows and changes, merchant locations will come and go, but at least I  hope to give you a fighting chance at getting that feeling of accomplishment as you hold your first homeade edible goo, that first piece of handmade patchwork leather armor, those first metals studs, etc.

For those who are simply shopping: Welcome! I hope to have more merchants listed soon. Once I've got a better listing of folks for you to shop from, perhaps you'll be able to squelch all that auctioning going on in your head in-game.  I know that other than the shopping page, most of this site might bore you. At the same time, a bit of reading might give you a better understanding of why you hear folks auctioning things in your head for prices you might initially feel is high. Once you do the math, you may find that they're barely making a profit. Then again, they may just be greedy, but you'll probably have a good feel for which it is when you see the myriad of trade items they have to find for their craft.

I'm always open to suggestions and submissions -- the more people actively participate in this site, the more use it will be to us all.
