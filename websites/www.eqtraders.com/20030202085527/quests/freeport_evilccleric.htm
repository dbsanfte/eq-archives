Dismal Priest Armor - Freeport Evil (Innnoruuk) Cleric Quest

This is one of several armor quests for younglings that was recently added. This one is for evil Freeport human clerics, and is HUM/CLR, Innoruuk, no drop and lore (other than bracer). Please note that while the bracers are not lore, the bracer materials often are, so make your first bracer before you get the parts for a second one

Questgiver is Zhem Xecia in East FP tunnel. The "Dismal Priest" armor is a matte dark rust color of fine plate, and the helm has little horns near the forehead.

You say, 'Hail, Zhem Xecia'

Zhem Xecia says 'What is it maggot? I have only time for new clerics who have chosen to [serve the Dismal Rage]. If you are looking for meaningless banter, than I suggest you find a bar wench.'

You say, 'I serve the Dismal Rage'

Zhem Xecia says 'The clerics of the Dismal Rage must be strong and familiar with battle. There are many beings across Norrath who oppose our deity and our dogma. Take this note to Jerra Renlock and she will aid you in obtaining a suit of armor to protect you from the physical threats of our enemies and the creatures of the wild. Once you have been properly outfitted return to me and will offer you [further instruction].'

Jerra Renlock is outside the building, near neg 314, neg 334. Hand her the note.

Jerra Renlock says 'Ah, so Zhem wants me to assist you with some armor. You will surely need it if you are an associate of the likes of him. This Mail Assembly Kit will be needed for you to construct your armor. The materials required depend on the armor section you plan to construct. Do you plan to construct a [dismal priest helm], a [dismal priest bracer], [dismal priest gauntlets], [dismal priest boots], [dismal priest vambraces], [dismal priest greaves], or a [dismal priest breastplate].'

You say, 'I plan to construct a dismal priest bracer'

Jerra Renlock says 'To assemble a Dismal priest Mail Bracer you will need to obtain a brick of crude iron and smelt it in a [forge] with a Water Flask and this Crude Bracer Mold. Once that is done, combine the Crude Iron Bracer with a Ruined Coyote Pelt and a Sand Scarab Hatchling Sternite in the Mail Assembly Kit.'

You say, 'what further instruction'

Zhem Xecia says 'So you think you are ready to face the enemies of the Dismal Rage? Be careful to not be blinded by your arrogance or anger. You must focus the rage in your soul and refine it into patient and insidious hatred. A cleric of the Sentries of Passion, Ulia Yovar, has been attempting to uncover our allies among the citizens of Freeport and is believed to be in hiding in the sewer tunnels under the western quarter of Freeport. Seek this enemy of the Dismal Rage and bring me her head.'


Final quest reward unknown at this time. There have been reports that Ulia may not be dropping her head when killed.
