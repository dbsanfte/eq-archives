Muette Wavedancer ("Grasshopper") - human monk

 

A lithe woman practices katas at the edge of the ocean. Her movements are sure, graceful, deadly. The surf is high this day, but she doesn't seem to be swayed at all as the pounding waves break against her.

"Spirits grant me strength. Spirits grant me courage. Spirits grant me wisdom."

Her body appears to be a finely-tuned weapon -- what is not as apparent is that her mind is just as finely tuned. Her voracious desire for learning is curbed only by access to that learning. At last count, she knows 23 languages to varying levels of fluency, and it is not enough. She is near master-level in jewelcrafting, plus journeyman level in baking and pottery ... and it is not enough.

The only time she can be pulled from her studies, is when there is someone in dire need, or her friends ask her to hunt.

She has made it her personal crusade to aid Sonoroud, an honorable dark elf of long acquaintance, on his quest for acceptance in the bright worlds. While they are nearly inseparable, refusing to go adventuring without each other, it appears to be only friendship. She has oft been overheard, with many a sidelong glance at his necromancer's robes, to state that she can't conceive of being romantically involved with someone who wears a dress!

The two of them are often joined by a cleric friend of theirs by the name of Janus, and the monsters oft tremble at their approach!

Muette is generous to a fault. Not only are her own needs simple, but she remembers well when she was an orphan, begrudged of even the rags upon her back.

One thing has remained true since her childhood. Never will she willingly let another fall if she can take her place.
