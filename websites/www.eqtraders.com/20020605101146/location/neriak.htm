Neriak Trade  Supplies

City Evaluation:  For the evil races, this is a passable spot, tradeskill-wise. Baking, brewing, fishing, jewelcraft and tailoring all look good. (Though for advanced tailoring, you will need to travel for a large sewing kit.) Pottery will require some travel to the Oasis of Marr for sketches (or a friend to get them for you from Freeport). Smithing looks good, though there doesn't seem to be easy access to skewer and other baking molds.
