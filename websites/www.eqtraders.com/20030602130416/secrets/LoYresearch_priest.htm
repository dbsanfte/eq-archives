LoY Priest Spell Research Recipes

These research combines can be performed by ANY researcher, regardless of which caster class they are. They must be combined in the appropriate advanced research container, as explained in the LoY Caster Research Recipes page. Note that hybrid spells that are not usable by any priest or caster class are NOT available via research. The parchment and quill mentioned are standard spell research components. The other items (words, pages, runes, etc., etc.) are now random mob drops off higher-level mobs throughout the lands.

WARNING! Some 50-60 dropped spells are LORE. Be very careful to check whether a spell you already have is Lore before making another one. The spell scrolls you research are identical to the ones that are dropping, so having a dropped version in your possession can still pose problems even if you are researching it for the first time.

Note that the names of enchanter pages have been abbreviated for the sake of space and printability. Finnok's = Finnok's Treatise, Toharon's = Toharon's Memoire, Yaeth's = Yaeth's Compendium, Zeannor's = Zeannor's Thesis.
