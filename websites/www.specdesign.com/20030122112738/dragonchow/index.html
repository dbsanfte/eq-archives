Mon Nov 18th 2002

Weekend Update

Teroth returns to Chardok

CHARDOK--After a less than successful first attempt on Wednesday, and a forced raid cancellation Saturday, Teroth finally had the numbers and the talent to launch his invasion of the Korucust room Saturday night in his quest to obtain the elusive group DMF spell. While DC ruled the day, neither Korucust nor the DMF spell made a showing.

 

Remii calls his first raid

KARNORS--An impromptu raid was called on Saturday by Remii when he spotted VSR up. A strong raid forced was assembled in good time and the dead emperor was once again put to rest to the benefit of Remii and Feanorr, and the enjoyment of all.

 

Indra leads raid on Neh Ashir and the Reavers

CITY OF MIST--Responding to a thread request on the chat board, Indra called an impromptu raid on CoM Friday night in an attempt to assist Wolfwoode who needed to speak to Neh, and Feanorr and others needing Jade Reavers. Many reavers were killed, but no Jade was found. Neh on the other hand was dispatched with incredible ease.

 

Hazard Team Down

FEAR--Responding to a distress call that Indra was trapped in Fear, Teroth put together a rescue team Saturday afternoon. Teroth, Indra, Iilae, Rezzana, Sarenia & Eldeberon made the jump into Fear intent on summoning, rezzing and porting. Seconds later, only Eldeberon, Sarenia, Teroth and Rezzana, came out. Once more the team entered. Seconds later they were all trapped inside Fear. DC gathering for the Chardok raid responded in droves rallying to the support of their trapped brethren. However, two other guilds were forming for a joint raid so it was decided to wait for the zone-in to be cleared before going in for another attempt. All bodies were recovered safely and the assembled DC forces were soon diverted to KC by Remii.

Grouping Channel already working
As a tool to help guildies find fellow guildies to group with a standard channel has been established--and appears to be working.
