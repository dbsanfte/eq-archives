Kinney  (Human, Mage) 6/5/02

 Kinney Shooz

Kinney grew up in a small town with other magicians. The Arch mages would teach the younger ones about Gods and spells, but Kinney never believed in the Gods. She chose the way of the agnostic. When the Arch mages found this out, Kinney was cast out from her beloved town. Teaching herself in the way of the magicians and wandering the lands looking for a place to call home, she wondered to herself that maybe she should believe in the gods. Then she met a handsome man named Exavier, who she fell in love with right away. He took her to his home called Dragonchow. They took her in with open arms. From that day on she knew she did not have to believe in the Gods, she believed in the love of the people.
Lindsay  (Barbarian, Shaman) 6/5/02


      
    

Lindsay

Lindsay was a beautiful shaman who loved her warrior husband, Faladaor. They fought side by side in battles for good and love. One night Faldaor was called to battle without her. Months passed and there was nothing from him. One day a man came to her door with a letter from him. The last one he wrote before he was killed. He wrote "my dearest love you must sign up in Dragonchow and fight for the good and remember that I will always be with you. With tears in her eyes, she packed her bags and said her last good byes. From that day on she walked the earth helping others who are in need.
