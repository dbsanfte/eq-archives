Freyana  (Wood Elf, Bard) 3/11/02

      
It's been a good long while that Freyana has had the privilege of singing her song. In the beginning, in the high treetops of Kelethin, she learned what it’s like to fall to your death. Urm…I mean she learned the value of knowing when to play an instrument and when to sing. Ah but life was simple then, gather up the bat wings and prance around like a cute little bard without a care. An orc was just a thing out of legend.


Not too much time had passed and many nighmares had come to pass, when she met up with the likes of Dharamsala, a filthy little gnome following the even more repulsive path of the necromancer. Although he was the most unlikely of companions for this elvish bard, they became a fierce duo. One day the fire kinda left the old gnome though. Freyana never knew if it was all the fishing, the tinkering, or the plain drunken stupor that overtook him but Dharam’s essence somehow passed on to an upstart cleric of the Tier’Dal.


It was not long before the tired muse woke up to realize that this upstart had become a Scarypriest. It seemed as though little had changed when the pair were united, except of course that a cleric and bard have a much easier time finding companions then a filthy little gnome and said bard.


It was through this steadfast and mighty companion of Freyana’s that she had the pleasure to meet the likes of a group known as Dragonchow. The good fortune and friendships that were formed have been sung all over Norrath.
Even after a couple bouts of indecision by the apprehensive wood elf, when she had trouble deciding whether to go on adventuring or retire to the warmth of a good orc burning, her new companions still welcomed her back with open arms and ready smiles.


Such is the good fortune of this minstrel. Giggling her way through life, trying to inspire others to perform to their best.
