Dragonchow is a guild that is a collection of folks that simply wish to have fun playing.

EQ is our recreation, not our job. We come here to laugh, to cry, to shout, and to blow off steam. We are NOT an UBER Guild nor do we have the desire or time to become one. We instead seek to become a highly competent collection of players, who can get help from other folks or join up with other folks in order to complete some of the higher level quests or fight some of the higher monsters.

If you seek ph4t l3wt, uberness, twinking or powerlevelling, this is not a guild for you.

If you are thinking I need to join a guild because of what I can get out of them as versus what I can contribute to it, this is not a guild for you.

If you wish to be a member of a family of folks, who have craftmen and traders, newbies and know it alls, cynics and psychos, and even the occasional optimist, then this might be a home for you.

Liberte, Egalite, Fraternite mais toujours Fraternite
