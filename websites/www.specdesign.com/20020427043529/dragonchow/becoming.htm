You want to be a Chow, but don't know how?

 

Step 1:

Carefully read our Guild Description , Code of Conduct and Recruitment requirements, to be certain you are considering the right guild and that you are eligible.

 

Step 2:

Look for Dragonchow members in the game that are of a similar level, let them know you are considering joining the guild and ask to group with them. This allows you to see first hand what DC members are like, and lets us evaluate how good of a fit DC would be for you.

 

Step 3:

After grouping with one or more members, if you still feel you want to be a Dragonchow, ask one of those you grouped with if they would be willing to sponsor you.

 

Step 4:

If the member agrees to sponsor you, the member will then need to post their request for membership for you. After which they will contact a guild officer who will officially invite you to the guild.

 

Step 5:

You will then be on probation for one month. If, after that time, you are still a member (assuming you haven't done, or failed to do anything that would cause the guild to reconsider your application for membership), you can consider yourself a Full Member.
