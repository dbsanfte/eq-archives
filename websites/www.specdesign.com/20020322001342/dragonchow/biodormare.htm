Avengaline  (Human, Enchanter) 1/29/02


      
    

Raised in a well-to-do family , she has always been a bit spoiled but still good at heart. At her parents behest she went to enchanter school at an early age. There she prospered well. Learning quickly and competently. she was always a bit flighty and easily distracted but her intelligence was always high. if bothered enough by someone they could easily pull her away from one task to help them at another before she was finished with the other. viewed as odd she tended to be a bit of a loner. While on the circuit given to her by the Enchanter School she found those she crossed were often won over by her generosity and big heart. knowing that she really cared about what happened to them. one day as she came upon one of the many villages she tended, she witnessed the massacre of the inhabitants of the village. among the inhabitants of the village was her lover and some distant family members.
