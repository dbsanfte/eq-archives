Terms

Glace S'arne- The Grand Mother. This title is bequeathed to the generations "chosen one". She that has been chosen to help the others in the world to learn about the DIvine Light and help them hear it's beautiful teachings.

Glace Ambad- The ambassador of the Divine Light. This is the voice of the Divine Light. A very high honour as the Glace Ambad speaks for the entire Order as it has been established. Glace Ambad must do whatever can be done to keep peace between factions dealing with the Order, and units those that are secluded from the Divine Light.

Trauwn- The office of Treasurer. Trauwn is resonsible for collecting revenues from current guild members as well as any debts owed from outside factions. Typically a wanderer, the Trauwn must sometimes travel vast distances to obtain the financial tool used in the world to advance the goals of the Order.

Enjune- The Order's coordinator. This job is what keeps the order organized. Enjune must keep records, and help coordinate events. The nexus of the guild, the coordinator knows at all times everything that is being done in the guild and reports all news to the Glace S'arne and the guild council. Only the strong of mind can complete the tasks of Enjune with out leaving the world at an early age.

Ne'trat- Ne'trat is responsible for welcoming new initiates in the order. This officer will assign a mentor, catalog the initiates information, and see to that all of the initiats questions are answered to the satisfaction of the Ne'trat. This is usually the first contact an initiate will have upon entering the Order.

Ortis- Founder. Every cycle that the Order repeats itself the Glace S'arne gather's up the most open minds and creates the Order. The founder's that accept the idea's of the Glace S'arne are bound by a spirit link and thus become Ortis. The Ortis form the council that govern all guild laws and oversee all ceremonies.

Quisan- The Quisan is a member of the Quest Council. They use their experience in conjunction with the whispers spoken to them by the Glace S'arne to delegate quests and trials to new initiates and members wishing to prove themselves to the guild.
