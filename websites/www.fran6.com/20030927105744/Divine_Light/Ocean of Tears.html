There are 8 islands/rock collections that are visible from the ship. I will start at the Freeport side and describe what I found moving towards Butcherblock mtns. Note while documented elsewhere, it was still a bit interesting that while we think of the continent of Feydwer being to the east of Antonica, this is incorrect based on the sense direction skill used during the trip, it appears that in the Ocean of Tears, Feydwer is to the west of Antonica.

Now on to the islands,

Sisterhood Island, first island to the north: This island is filled with female warriors who /con red to me, and would not talk to me either, from what I have read about the island there isn't much here of interest, except that the ship stops here, so it's important for people who fall off the boat.

Isle of K'Arnon: first isle to south: This island was populated by sea goblins who /con green to me. I have read that there is an oracle on the island, but I missed talking to him.

Gray Isle: second isle to south: I didn't see anyone on this isle, but then again at this point in time I was running quickly between sides of the boat. In addition I can find no writing talking about it.

Mudtoe Isle: second isle to north: On this isle I saw a single NPC called Boog Mudtoe who /con black, and non-agressive to me. I have read that there is a merchant selling magic scrolls on the island (I don't know what class the scrolls were for)

Pirate Isle: third isle to north: On this isle there are various red aggressive people and monsters, the two I saw were Gornt Giant and Capt. Surestout.

Twin Tines: (also called the peaks Cyrith and Gorgor by a fellow traveler) the twin rock croppings in the middle of the journey: These rocks had various Alluring Sirens on them who /con blue aggressive

Zachariah Isle: the last isle on south: This island has various NPCs who /con red to me. The only one who would have anything to do with me was the innkeeper, who would sell me food, water, etc. Some other things of interest are some runes on the island, and that this is another stop for the boat, so important for people who jump off the boat.

Siren Rocks: the collections of rocks at the end of the zone near Butcherblock: These rocks had numerous green Aqua Goblins and blue Alluring Sirens.
