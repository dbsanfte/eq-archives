Spiritwalkers Guild Member Bio

Name: Tasha Reefshard
Race: Erudite
Profession: Wizardess
Likes: Water, half-elf men, and shiny things.
Dislikes: Scrubs and men that haven’t noticed my tiara.
Quirks: Beauty, mischief, and fashion (not necessarily in that order)

For as long as the races have existed on Norrath, there have been those who live by the seas, leading simple lives harvesting the bounty of the oceans.

On the shores of Odus, the island home to the city of Erudin, is the Reefshard family, a family of two daughters, Tasha and Coral. Deep is the faith in Prexus in this family, and Tasha, though not called to priesthood, learned and believed the truths of the oceans. She has built many a castle of sand in homage to the great power of the seas.

Being the oldest child, Tasha developed a fierce and protective love for her younger sister Coral. When Coral was called to a sacred duty for Prexus, it was Tasha who promised her powers of magic to Coral's protection.

A long and hazardous journey from Odus to Faydwer was called for, Coral to carry a sacred vial from a holy shrine to the dark citadel of Castle Mistmoore. The journey involved many dangers not to be spoken of now, but Tasha was equal to the task. She guided her sister, protecting her from the many fell creatures who would prey upon such a young priest. They come at last to Mistmoore.

Slowly they advance upon the gates to the dark citadel, fearing its foul reputation; its dark powers kept barely in check. Closer they come, and Tasha feels herself weaken, her strength insufficient to hold back the darkness. She sees Coral, face paled and emotionless, raise the sacred vial above her head, and enter the courtyard.

Mists swirl and coalesce, a dark figure appearing. He is the color of death, carries the stench of decay, and wears a smile of pure, unnatural bliss. His hands perform arcane motions, and the gate seals itself by growing strangely translucent flesh at a blinding pace. He beckons to the young Coral, and she moves to him, emotionless, like one in a trance.

Tasha calls to her sister, vainly trying to pierce the gate's unnatural flesh with her powers, but Coral does not respond. In horror Tasha can only watch.

"How good of you to bring me the price your mother agreed to pay. The vial is your spirit, and its power shall give me flesh once more!" cries the dark figure.

"Grant me life, Dark Powers, as I drink the spirit of the young woman. Give me that which I have bargained for, and the Reign of Blood will be yours!"

Coral lifts the vial, dropping to her knees, proffering her very essence to the beast of un-life.

Horror reaches new depths in Tasha's soul, and she draws strength from it. Taking her enchanted knife, she strikes the flesh-covered gate, tearing at it. A gash is opened, and the foul stuff bleeds a filthy ichor on to Tasha's flesh. An opening present, she pours the last of her remaining power into a spell. It is a small pitiful thing, but it is enough to shatter the vial, but only after some of its contents have drained between the foul thing's lips.

Swirls of light and the clean fresh smell of an ocean breeze escape, and Coral's face again shows signs of life. Parts of the dark beings body seem to have grown whole, and the desiccated flesh which covered its face reveal fangs and the beauty which was once a fair Teir'Dal man.

"Trickery! Deceit! Betrayal you seek to commit! Tonight I was to be made whole! You have stolen it from me creature, and I curse you for my revenge!"

Coral rushes to Tasha's side, lifting her from where she has collapsed, holding her.

"Lightning's strike and thunder's bay, I sunder you with night and day! Until my thirst for life you slake, one must sleep whilst the other does wake!"

As the dawn rose and Tasha's strength returned Coral collapsed into a deep slumber.

And so it is done, a curse upon the sisters, for only one can walk awake and they are divided, never to meet again until it is broken.
