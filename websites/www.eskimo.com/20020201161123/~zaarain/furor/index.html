Today Furor Poeticus
Something happened...

Wednesday, January 30, 2002 Gruffydd
A few weeks ago Gitt & Dunnan decided to move to Wings of the Chimera. Shortly after the rest of us decided it was time to move on as well. It was a good run and we held our identity longer than I think most of us ever expected to.

I still remember doing bandits in West Karana, getting used to our characters and roles. - Tennyson

Ah the good old days....Millay getting lost, Tenn bitching about why in the hell the tanks let him die, Millay getting lost again, Kera whining about how Paladins suck, Faulk sic'ing his pet on a red DE merchant in Sro because he had a few too many drinks at some parade that day, Millay getting lost again, Coleridge benevolently putting up with us lower level newbs after doing uber raids on Quellios, Gruff keeping it all together and eloquently smoothing things over by saying "imho..." ....good times...good times.... - Kerawack

Yeah, sometimes I miss the excitement of the lower levels. When we'd get totally wiped out and have to run across 2 continents to do CR because Gruff didnt have group ports. And then we'd come back and get our revenge. Or being eally jazzed about upgrading our crappy equipment to something like the Runed Circlet, because we'd killed the mob that dropped it ourselves and it was challenging. - Millay (Hey, I didn't get last THAT many times!)
