About us

Furor Poeticus was formed for the purpose of having a regular, permanent group. No spending half your time shouting for a pickup group, no squabbling over looting rights, no idiots or twits to get you killed, just solid, dependable people. You know their skills and they know yours. Each person can count on the others to watch thier back and to support them whether it's a special raid to get some quest item or some extra platinum to help get that trade skill over a hump. Our group makeup was designed from scratch based on the skills we thought we needed and the classes each person was willing to play. We have been formally grouped since level 5.

This is without a doubt the best way to play the game. We have often been the lowest people in the zone by two or three levels yet we're doing better than the higher level groups. We get regular compliments about how well we work together. Best of all is the fact that your groupmates are your friends.

We are not a guild as such and we are not actively seeking to expand the core group of six at this time. However we're always on the lookout for good people to fill in when somebody can't make it.

Furor Poeticus plays on Sayrn.
