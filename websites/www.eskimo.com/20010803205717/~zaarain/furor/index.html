This floor has 2 parts: to the left is where the ice shade spawns, which has to be killed to cause the keyholder (Val Shera) to spawn. He spawns in a room to the right, through a one-way mirror. The shades aggro through the floor, though, so if they haven't been cleared, they'll swarm while you're trying to kill the keyholder. The best strategy is to take 2 groups (or split in 2 groups, if everyone is high enough) and have one group clearing the bottom floor and looking for the PH while the other keeps the upstairs cleared in preparation for the keyholder spawn. We're rather proud of the fact that we did it with a single group of 44-50.
