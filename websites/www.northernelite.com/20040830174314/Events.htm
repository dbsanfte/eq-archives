National Tax & Practice Development Institute
At NPDI – an expanded version of the popular fall tax institute – you can get the tools you’ll need for the upcoming tax season and explore growth opportunities to build your practice. This seminar covers important practitioner topics such as basis, depreciation, ethics, reasonable compensation, and what’s new for the upcoming filing season. As an added bonus, practice-building workshops will offer valuable insights. These workshops provide a unique and priceless opportunity to hear what your peers are doing, share ideas with your colleagues, and get answers to your specific questions and client issues. This year the NPDI is scheduled for November 15-16 in Las Vegas, Nevada.

NSA National Accounting & Tax Symposium

The fast-changing pace of the economy, changes in tax laws and other factors are affecting your clients and your practice. Don’t sit on the sidelines and watch—this is your opportunity to continue your professional development, stay abreast of the latest trends and network with your peers to continue to grow with the profession. Join NSA and your colleagues at this milestone event, earn 16 hours of CPE, and gain valuable insight on major accounting and tax issues and skills to service today’s clients. Topic highlights include Professional Ethics, Limited Liability Companies (LLCs), Accounting and Financial Statements for the small Business Accountant and much more. Session presenters include some of the industry’s leading national education providers, such as the National Center for Professional Education (NCPE). This seminar will be September 27-28 at the Baltimore Convention Center. The registration fee is waived for NSA members ($100 materials fee required; $50 rebate on attendance and participation). The fee for non-members is $575. For more information, visit National Accounting and Tax Symposium.
