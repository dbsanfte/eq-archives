Top 10 EQ Tipsters

 

10 Learn EQ Etiquette

9 Be considerate to fellow EQers
8 Share loot
7 Don't kill your own race
6 Talk to everyone and make lots of friends. Be diplomatic
5 Try to stick to a distinct EQ role (good, evil).
4 Don't play EQ like you would Quake. If you do, you will be branded an immature child.
3 Join a good guild.
2 When you are good, start becoming closer with friends and enemies (makes game more dymanic). When you are good, having some enemies can be really fun, esp. when you whoop them.
1 Don't approach EQ as if it was Ultima Online because then you will get screwed in many ways. EQ is much more structured and recquires players to act more civil and help eachother out. It is a harder game, and recquires teamwork amongst races. Your race is like your greater guild. Remember that. Unless the player is a real jack ass, be friendly with your race because when you geet attacked by 200 trolls in Nektulos Forest, you won't want to be left on the battlefield all alone to rot.
