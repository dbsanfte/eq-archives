Game Overview


Everquest is the newest most innovative online game out. It is the first game to provide a true 3D environment with literally hundreds of miles of land to explore. Everquest is a "massively multiplayer" game, which means that each person plays over the internet on a specific server with up to 1000 other players per server. This allows for a great deal of social interaction with other real people that play. For each server you are allowed to create up to 8 characters who can differ in race, diety, and skill. You can anything from a brave warrior to a powerful wizard.

The variety of skills and races make each character unique. The purpose of Everquest is to make your character advance in the levels and skill of his class (wizard, monk, rogue etc.) so that he can explore new places, kill bigger monsters, become more famous, and just simply kick ass. When you become pretty good (around level 30), you can start your own guild and recruit real players into it. Guilds are ideal for going on quests, having wars with other races, and just hanging out. Some of the more rewarding aspects of Everquest are the humungous castles and houses that players with alot of money can buy.

The trick to the game is diligence and patience. If you expect to play out this game for a month or two, you are wasting your money. Everquest like most online games (Ultima Online), is an investment of time and money. If you pay your 10 dollars a month and work hard at your character, you will enjoy the innumerable benefits that this game possesses. Everquest is a revolution in the gaming industry, and you will find that one you get the hang of it (which will take at least 2 weeks) you will be hooked.
