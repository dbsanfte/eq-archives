Everquest Races

There has been limited information regarding the races and the particulars of their culture, but here is what we know: there are 12 player races, and there are also NPC races with which you can interact, such as Orcs and Gnolls. You can be either male or female, but not all races can be all classes! Traditionally, after all, Halflings have been less proficient in the magical arts, as have Trolls...

  • Humans This is the normal, average of all the races, by which other races are compared. They will undoubtedly not get innate special abilities, but will have a wide variety of classes available.
  • Barbarians This race is an offshoot of the normal humans, a much more hardy and physical variety. They are less cultured than the average human, and more warlike. The rugged cold of the Northlands makes for a strong people.
  • Erudites (High Men) This variant of humanity values their intellect over their brawn, and have attained great status with magical ability. Founded by their leader, Erud, they originally settled the island of Odus, and pride themselves on their social and mental abilties.
  • Woodland Elves These elves are forest dwellers, and have more pointed features than humans, including pointed ears. Their intellect matches that of a High Man, and their speed and nimble reflexes are superior to those of men. Their charms and comeliness are greater as well, but their physical strength and prowess is not as great as an average man.
  • High Elves This is the noble variant of the elves, the most lordly, although the Dark Elves would challenge their "royalty." As a more intellectual race of elves, they are thinner, paler, and more attractive than their woodland cousins. They can be found on their continent of Faydwer.
  • Dark Elves This race is the master of the undercaverns, where they can see perfectly well in normal darkness. Their race is devoted to evil gods, and they presumably seek to overthrow their surface cousins. Their hair is stark white, while their skin is bluish black.
  • Half-Elves This is the union of a human and a Woodland Elf, having some of the characteristics of both parents. Their features are slightly pointed, but less than an elf's.
  • Dwarves An extremely hardy and also short race, the dwarf prides himself on his beard or moustache. They are also quite agile, and skilled.
  • Trolls This race averages around 8 feet in height, making small entrances and most beds out of the question! Their size is equalled in their strength, but their green skin is often smelly and unwashed. They are not the most hygeinic race on Norrath, nor are they extremely bright.
  • Ogres This is arguably the strongest race on Norrath, and equal to the Trolls in endurance. Their height averages 10 feet, and their weight averages 400 pounds! They are, unfortunately, extremely stupid, and it is a cause for celebration if they remember to cook their food...
  • Halflings They are a rather new race, like Humans, but are shorter than men. They are quick, agile, and stocky, being first encountered in their home of Rivervale. They love a good meal and good conversation.
  • Gnomes This race spends most of its time underground, where they have intricate homes. They are much like the Dwarves, but their skin is more of a brown hue, and their heads are sparsely covered with white hair.
