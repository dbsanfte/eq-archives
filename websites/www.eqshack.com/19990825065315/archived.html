Wednesday, March 31 1999


Racial Issues in Norrath
Special News [2:38 AM EST] Finklesworth

This message was sent to me last night, and since it was about two pages long, I was only able to post a few exerts. Hope you find it interesting because I sure did.

"Hey there fellow Norrathians. My name is Finklesworth the human, and I have noticed something disturbing in my lands. We are being constantly raided by those vile Dark Elves from Neriak, and there has been very little resistance by us humans. My friends and I were slaughtered yesterday in East Commons, and when we went to pick up our bodies, we were killed again by a bunch of hoodlum elves. I am sick of the lack of unity and support in our race, and I feel that we need to come together and fight off the elves instead of sitting on our asses looking at our corpses. Personally, I sometimes envy the unity of the elves and feel that I might join their side if the human race remains as weak as it is now. Fellow humans please come together so that we can prevail instead of fail."

We are currently working on a Racial Issues page, which will address the issues explained above and also explain the strengths and weaknesses of each race.

 
Tuesday, March 30 1999


EQExtreme
Everquest News [10:58 PM EST] Gandar

There has been rumors of the first Everquest hack program, known as EQExtreme. Sound familiar? Well, there was a program with a similar name for Ultima Online, called UOExtreme. However, the difference between the two is that EQE is a great deal more dangerous because it has the ability to act as Trojan Horse Program, which can secretly infect your computer, and retrieve your login information. This entails possibilities for your account to be used by any sick minded individual that likes to screw people over. And unlike UO the login information is not stored in your hard drive, however a simple keylog can be made with EQE to find out everything you type into your computer, including your Evequest password. So beware of what you download because you might be a victim of the EQ SCAM O' The Month.

Friday, March 26, 1999


EQShack Introduction
site updates [10:48 PM EST] Orgetorix

Welcome all to the opening of EQShack. The staff here has been working for months to make a comprehensive, useful, and comical Everquest site. Our goal is to reveal all the things that go on in EQ, and positive and negative player feedback. We hope to use this to keep EQ members aware of what they are paying for, and what their 10 dollars a month is being used for. We do not wish to draw anyone away from EQ or any other online game, however, we feel that the players should be informed about the different parts of online gaming. Online gaming is a new and exciting trend, which we will be touching upon greatly in editorials and articles throughout our site. Since this is a new site, we would really appreciate any submissions of editorials, essays, news, or screenshots related to EQ. Thanks for your support.
