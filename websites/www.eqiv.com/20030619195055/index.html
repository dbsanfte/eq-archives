Our journey towards the elemental planes shortens with the fall of Agnarr the Storm Lord. May 30, 2003
A Long and Overdue update...

Recently IV has been continuing its clearage of VT everytime it repops. We pretty much have it down, clearing VT in one late night...The late nights have really brought the team together though, and the loot is superb. If you thought clearning VT in one night wasn't enough we also added the Emp and Cursed into the mix in one night.

After SoE decided to end its patch-fest, we finally got some time to leave our homes in VT/SSRA and try something new. We headed into the Temple of Mithaniel Marr not fully knowing what to expect. Our first attempt brought Mith Marr to his knees at 12%, but he got the better of us and sent us back to the poorly developed graveyeard. We kept our heads high and decided to pay him a visit a few days later.

Well, after much strategizing and discussion, and dealing with the some what annoying flag situation, we went back in. We had perfected our strategy and it was time to test it out. We were successful, and let me tell you it was one of the most beautiful displays of teamwork I've ever seen in this game.

The list of barriers grows thin to the elemental planes... we'll see you there soon =)

Mobs deaded along the way:
Shei Venitras, The Itraer Vius, Emperor Ssraeshza, The Cursed Cycle, Tallon Zek, Vallon Zek, BoT Tower Bosses, Avatar of War, Idol of Rallos Zek, statue of Rallos Zek, Aten Ha Ra (and all of VT).. the list continues...

April 4, 2003
For whom the bell tolls

Emperor Ssraeshza has been dethroned! On our first attempt, with only 3 deaths, we killed the infamous Emperor.

The crowd control teams did there job flawlessly and everyone rose to the occasion. It was one of the easiest and most enjoyable raids I have ever been on which I can directly attribute to the excellent display of leadership!

We have been working on this for months and are finally seeing the fruits of our labor. We did this alone without riding on the coat tails of another guild! We all knew that it would make us or break us and I am proud that it was the former!

My many thanks goes to the guilds that zoned in and did not attempt to race us to the Emperor. Once again there is proof that an Everquest server can be a civil place to play without having to cut each others throat in the name of fun.

It's a great day for us and I for one am proud to have the tag Infinite Vision at the end of my name!
