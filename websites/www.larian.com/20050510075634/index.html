Larian licenses physics engine for multiple projects
April 1, 2005 by Lynn

Larian Studios has chosen Meqon to provide its future next-generation games with realistic dynamics and physics. Thanks to Meqon, projects at Larian Studios can look forward to ragdolls, liquid surfaces, rigid bodies, lifelike explosions, and overall enhancements in animation. For more information on meqon, visit www.meqon.com

Kirill does it again !
January 13, 2005 by Lynn

After capturing IGN's RPGVault "Outstanding achievement in music" award in 2002, our beloved composer Kirill Pokrovsky has once again been honoured with this prestigious award for the music in Beyond Divinity. Well done Kirill!

If you haven’t had the chance to enjoy Kirill's music yet, or you simply want more, head over to our online shop where Divinity series soundtrack has been made available for purchase. You'll get a signed version of "The Child of Chaos" novella written by Rhianna Pratchett, and a Divinity series audio CD signed by Kirill.


Gamebryo and Speedtree to power Larian's next-gen titles
January 7, 2005 by Lynn

We're currently very active on our next generation titles and are pleased to announce that Gamebryo is our 3D engine of choice.

Also powering our future titles is Speedtree, which has been licensed to provide realistic and stunning vegetation in our upcoming games.
