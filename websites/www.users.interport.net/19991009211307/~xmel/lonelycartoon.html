Merrilyn and Lena "In A Lonely Place."


It wasn't until the Fourth of July that anyone knew we were friends. I'd gone with Aunt Trudy to the town picnic. Merrilyn came and sat beside me, and I couldn't tell her not to, because everyone was welcome in the park that day, the first Independence Day of the war.
