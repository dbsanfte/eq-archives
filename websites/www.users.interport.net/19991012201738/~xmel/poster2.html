The point of this site is not to sell stuff. The point of this site is to bring stories to readers.

Letting readers know about the site is a job in itself, however, and it's particularly tough reaching people outside the U.S. That's why I offered this 24" x 36" poster to cybercafes overseas.

Right now, it's on display in over a dozen countries, including England, Australia, New Zealand, Poland, South Africa, Hong Kong, Mexico, Canada, Switzerland, Malaysia and Slovenia. I had to check an atlas to find out where Slovenia was.

A limited number of signed and numbered posters are still available, and I'm not above trying to make some dough off them.
