Atrocities

What I am looking for here are personal stories regarding health care or lack there of. Were you abused by a doctor, HMO, health care facility, hospital,insurance provider ? I want to hear about it.
Also seeking essays on current or past events that are atrocious. Some examples of which may be the recent outbreak of kids killing fellow students, elderly abuse, mercy killing,you name it. A wide range of topics will be considered. Submit atrocites

GENERAL GUIDELINES

Please use "Submission" as the subject of all submissions.
Send attachments as either plain text or word docs.
If in doubt - send the work to me - we'll discuss it.
I wish that it were different - but we can not pay you for your work.
However the main goal of this publication is to help writers - while shaking up the reader and providing a new avenue of thought for them. Your goal as a writer should approximate the same - so here is your forum.
If you make a submission and have heard nothing from me within a month please resubmit or write an inquiry.
