THE JAMES DEAN STORY

Ronald Martinetti
44 years after his death, James Dean appears as ever- larger than life. A cultural icon for 3 decades of American youth, Dean's biggest Fans, still know little about the REAL James Dean. Ronald has authored a hard hitting, fact-finding, truthful portrait of Dean in THE JAMES DEAN STORY.
Ronald has had access to people who were prominent in Dean's Life, people who had never agreed to speak openly about the Star. One of his sources was Dean's biggest support as well as his lover, Who agreed to talk with Martinetti with the provision that none of the facts He revealed would be published until after his death.
Sometimes ugly, sometimes endearing, but always truthful- this portrait of James Dean is a must read for all Dean fans, and should be required reading material for the study of cultural trends during the last 40 years.

Adventures of the Teen Furies
MaryJanice Davidson ©1999
ISBN 1-58200-072-7
A very accurate and on target portrayal of the emotional conflict that today's teens face.
The characters are well developed, and very believable, so much so, that I could mistake several of them for my own nieces!
Once I started reading it- I couldn't put it down until I was sure the young heroines in the work had made the "right" choices and were going to be ok. I cared about these characters. Even though they could at times be smart-mouthed and rebellious- what teen isn't?
Ms Davidson writes a cohesive, telling and endearing story of the lives of the 6 central characters.
And she does so with a special flare and expert craft. Highly recommended for young adults and their parents, aunts, grandparents.
Well done!

FATAL FORTUNE.... Elizabeth Delisi
A romantic suspense novel. Elizabeth has woven a fine mystery with well-developed characters. A book you'll read cover to cover- without putting it down. Lottie Baldwin has a special "gift" of knowing, aided by the tarot, tea leaves, and her urgent need to help a friend. You'll find yourself immediately drawn to Lottie Baldwin, and like Lottie you'll find many ideas flash through your mind as to who the culprit might be. Eternal Life…Bobby Right makes an auspicious debut in his first novel. It is neither an archetypically " male" or "female" story. It embraces aspects to entertain and draw both sexes. Set in the exotic country of Dubai it blends both the physical and spiritual aspects of life and death. An adventure novel that will heighten your awareness of the new wave of spirituality alive in today's hectic world. The story winds through loss, rebirth, and most importantly leads the reader to a true understanding of love. A wonderful read for fans of inspirational fiction. For more information VISIT HERE






Between Absolutes… David Hunter Sutherland - An amazing voyage into contemporary poetry, David's command of the language, his cadence, and flow all combine to paint a portrait of one today's best new poets. David was named the winner of the The Hudson Valley Poetry Festival Award. Between Absolutes is highly complex in its seeming simplicity, David takes a hard look at reality and then as if he is the shapeshifter you'll find your perceptions shifting and offering a multitude of viewpoints and emotion.
