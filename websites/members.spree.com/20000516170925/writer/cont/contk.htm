Kim Kline

I was born in "South Philly" Pennsylvania and now reside in Southern New Jersey. After high school graduation I became a mother and a wife. At the time, my dream career was to be a lawyer. I figured the best place to start would be as a paralegal so off to school I went for certification. A friend of mine gave me a job right after school and I worked in the legal field for a few years. It was very upsetting when I realized that I didn't have the stomach to handle the career which was my dream for so long. There would have to be some other way to achieve the justice that I so desperately searched for in my life.

After my divorce I drifted around for a few years trying to find myself while taking care of my young daughter. I was stuck and just barely getting by. Then I met a wonderful man while waiting tables at a diner and a beautiful relationship was started. We married after 6 months knowing that we were meant to betogether. He has given me that chance to look inside myself and concentrate on all the healing that needed to be done. Our 6th wedding anniversary is June 26, 1999.

I have had several years of therapy to deal with all the horror I "put away" during my childhood. The process has been very enlightening. Out of all the pain and sorrow came a spiritual, caring, forgiving and centered woman. The gift of creativity has always been part of my life but I learned how to use it to heal. Writing and drawing about my pain taught me that I could be less and less afraid of it. I always worried that some day the ability to create would leave me. Now I know that it is a permanent part of me. In all the growth that has occured, I found a passion for living and I now see beauty in this world, a world which was once filled only with pain, confusion and a huge sense of injustice.

I hope that my writing may instill a sense of hope in those that feel hopeless and a glimmer of trust. Trust in that the world can be a truly wonderful place for all of us
