Jerome Mandel sometime Chair of the English Department at Tel Aviv University, is the first-place winner of the P.E.N.-UNESCO International Short Story Competition for 997. Profits from Heritage Blue, scheduled for April 1999 publication by PoetWarrior Press, will be donated to Heritage House for restorations.

Eric Morrissey I'm a 51-year-old with three children and three grandchildren. I'm originally from Cwmaman, South Wales, U.K., but now living in Cardigan, West Wales. I didn't start writing poetry until well after my fiftieth birthday. I have not had any tuition in creative writing, so am entirely self taught in this respect. I've traveled fairly extensively, whilst in the Royal Navy and since leaving in 1968. My hobbies are fairly diverse, from Falconry to marathon running. When training for a specific marathon I run between 120 - 130 miles per 6 day week when fit. Sundays I rest, unless there's a race (of anything up to half marathon distance), in which case I run that too.
Victoria Mosley

" My poems are really about relationships , the times of transition in one's life when there's death , divorce, and everything seems to be falling apart . But things are never unremittingly bleak , I write intense love poems too "

Performance poet Victoria Mosley was born in Kent , and grew up in Singapore and Hong Kong .

Returning to Britain to read English Literature at London University , she dropped out after a year to work as a production assistant on short films and rock videos .

Moving to a clifftop farm on the Isle of Man she bred Arabian horses- , and helped produce a literary magasine. She then lived in France , and later Devon , where she joined the Company of Poets and gave readings of her work at the Dartingtom Art Gallery ,Wessex Poetry Festival,Cheltenham Festival , The Bug Bar , Brixton . She makes regular appearances at the Poetry Cafe Covent Garden , Poetry bookshop Brixton , has appeared at the Comedy House Stockwell .

She is a founder member of Poets of the Pulse and will be working closely with them , ( Philip Law ,Adam Horovitz, Ian Winn, Michael Groce , John Citizen ) over the next year , touring Theatres , schools , hospices festivals , wherever poetry is needed .

She now lives with her three children in Dulwich .

She has published in numerous small press mags and anthologies .

Victoria Mosley's first collection of poems The Dry Season will be published on November 2 1998 by Odyssey Poets at £6 .95 . To order a copy or for further information about Victoria Mosly contact : The Creative Umbrella 0171 323 5993
