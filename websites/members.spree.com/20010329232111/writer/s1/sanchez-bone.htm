 What Can You Expect From Bone & Joint Problems?
© Lena Sanchez

When was the last time you went for a walk without pain? What prescription did you doctor give you to rid you of join pain? Did that prescription correct the problem?

Bone and joint problems are the number one problem that causes disability. Our chemical laden medical world treats symptoms not problems.

According to medical experts one in seven people are experiencing joint problems. For those over 65 years of age it soars to 80% of people plagued with join problems. There are several reasons for these problems. Traumatic overuse or under use of the joints as well as diet, infection, immune factors and heredity all play a role here.

Post World War II the pharmaceutical companies began to adulterate and try to reproduce the properties that had in the past rebuilt the bones and joints but with dismal failure.

Basic science teaches that our body regenerates itself when given the proper tools to do so but that has long been forgotten and no longer taught in our medical schools. It also teaches that the proper nutrition can repair our bodies as well. If regeneration was still taught by our medical schools the pharmaceutical companies could not sell so many chemical laden body-destroying drugs, which line their pockets with wealth.

Our sedentary lifestyles also contribute to the joint problems. The stiffness and soreness due to inactivity frequently start as minor symptoms, often initiated by bad weather. Before too long the stiffness leads to agonizing symptoms which stop any kinds of movements and propels you to the doctors office. When right the opposite would be the rule to regeneration. Exercise has proven to regenerate the bones and joints but our society has been brain washed to think that pain should stop you from moving! So get up and move when joint pain hits!

Diet is another factor, especially as we age, more commonly poor eating habits are found among people with joint and bone problems. A diet lacking in vegetables and omega-3 fats can lead to the production of sticky, watery joint fluid that is unable to properly nourish cartilage and other joint structures or flush out the toxins that build up. It is important for people with joint and bone problems to eat whole foods, including fruits, vegetables, grains and nuts and drink plenty of clean, clear chemical-free water.

Have you ever known a person taking medications such as the common NSAID's to regenerate their bones and completely cure the problem? None, in the twenty years I worked in medical offices I only saw a spiral of downhill health problems that can only be attributed to the drugs prescribed for those problems. On the other hand I have since seen people go off all NSAID's and corticosteroids and regain total regeneration of their joints and bones. Myself included in that group.

Unfortunately traditional drugs play a major role in the continuing decline of the joints and muscles. If you are taking one of these: Aspirin Orudis Ibuprofen Tandearil Aleve Rufen Bufferin Datril Indocin Nuprin Asolid Liquipen Nafon Butazolidin - Tylenol Oxalid Advil Tolectin Excedrin IB Clinoril Midol 2000

Although you may relieve the pain and cover the symptoms you are probably accelerating cartilage damage and worsening your condition. You may also be experiencing Headaches, Diarrhea, Constipation, Drowsiness, Sore throat, Sensitivity to sunlight, dizziness, ulcers, ringing in the ears, nervousness, kidney/urinary problems, swelling of hands/feet, cramps nausea, rashes hypertension, stiffness, weight gain and colds. All are commonly known side effects from these drugs. Then one of the major side effects is death. More than 10,000 people die yearly from the side effects of these drugs with hundreds of thousands hospitalized. The JAMA (Journal of American Medicine Association) documents this.

Your body was made to take in food -- not chemicals. Drugs are unnatural to the body and create poisons to deplete the system of nutrients, which destroy the body. They may also subdue pain temporarily but these drugs are damaging to you. Clinical tests have shown that arthritis and rheumatism are the result of various nutritional deficiencies but when was the last time your doctor told you that your diet could make you healthier? In order for the body to repair itself it must be fed the building blocks that can restore it to the natural state. Along with proper diet and exercise there are much-needed nutrients and herbs that can regenerate and restore joints and bones when taken over a period of time. True chemicals tend to give you that "right now good feeling" that this generation goes for but in the long run is ruining any chance of regeneration.

I wish you all good health and a long life to enjoy it!
