Submission Guidelines for A
Writer's Choice Literary Journal 

 Publication Dates and Submission Deadlines for 2000


March 31st - Submissions accepted until Feb. 15th


June 30th - Submissions accepted until May 15th


Sept 30th - Submissions accepted until  August 15th


Dec 30th- Submissions accepted until Nov 15th


Any submissions after the dates above will be held over for next publication,
or returned to the author.




GENERAL:
1. Please send all submissions as attachments (any MS word processing 
 program or plain text) to the email address below.

2. No overt sexual content, no racism or prejudice of any kind will be
accepted.

3.Please type "Submission" as the subject of your email.

4. If the submission has been submitted elsewhere please state that in
the body of the email. If I find the same work on another website at the
same time that it appears in A Writer's Choice - the work will be 
removed and we will not consider any future submissions from you.

5. If it is apparent that you have not read these guidelines- your submission
will be returned unread.

6. DON'T send me the url to your homepage
and ask me to consider work that appears there. Any submission of this
type will be returned.

If you want to be published by AWC make the effort.

7. Previously published work is O.K. just let me know so that I can list
the publication information.

SPOKEN WORD

1. poetry, rap, slam, essays, etc.

2. Please send an RA file or a WAV file.

3. If you want your work to be read as spoken word
but don't have the capabilty of producing sound files on your
computer- you can ask that I have someone else read the work,
or contact me and I'll provide an address where you can send an 
audio cassette. The only length limitation is download time.

POETRY

1. I accept VERY LITTLE RHYME. If you submit rhyme
please be sure there are no forced rhymes in the work.

2. Nothing trite, cutesy, overly sentimental will be considered.

3. No length limits.

4. No limit on number of poems you can submit- 
but if submitting more than 1 please do so as an attachment as
detailed above.

5. I am seeking poetry that  will take the reader into the
poet's mind and challenge the reader's perceptions.
Poetry to take  them where they have not yet been, or to
a new level of understanding and seeing the commonplace.

6. Any style is acceptable.

FICTION

1. I seek quality work. That being said, quality is a subjective
term. You will think your work fits the
description, while I may or may not agree.

2.Specific needs at present - I'd like to see a  wider range
of offerings for those with varied taste. 

3. Any length

4. Any subject

5. Any genre

6. Any style 

7. Please list a one line description of your story and the 
genre you wish to be listed under

DRAMA- PLAYS

1. 1-3 act plays.

2.  Prefer minimum of stage sets, etc.

3. No length limit. 

WRITING ARTICLES

1. Advice, tips, etc for authors.

2. Discussions on writing such as poetry, fiction, etc. I.E. "Is
Poetry Dead?" 

3.Interviews with or articles about other writers and poets.

4. Literary criticism

5. No length limitation.

Atrocities non-fiction 

1. Personal stories regarding health care or lack there of.

2. Were you abused by a doctor, HMO, health care facility,
hospital,insurance provider ?

3. Any other true story that would be considered atrocious

4.  No length limitation.

Adversity non-fiction

1. Stories of triumph

2. Overcoming abuse or rape, dealing with disability, beating
an unjust system, overcoming
personal demons, sobriety stories, etc. 

3. No length limitation

ESSAYS 

1. Any subject considered

2.  No length limitation.

ANYTHING ELSE

1. Just what it says. Essays, political commentaries,
rants, philosophical items, spiritual items, etc.

2. Anything that is thought provoking.

3.  We want the reader to question the status quo, take
a look at their deep held belief systems that
have been instilled by society, shake up their false 
sense of security and make them ponder the
"what-if's" in their existence.

4. Any length.

ORIGINAL ARTWORK

1. Submit for approval.

2. No files over 500kb.

VISUAL or CONCRETE POTERY

1. Image size limitation 500kb.

2. No other restrictions.
