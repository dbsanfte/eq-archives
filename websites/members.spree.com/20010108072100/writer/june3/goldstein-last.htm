 Last Stop: Redemption
© Allison Goldstein 

	I live in a state 
		of ridicule
	and false pretenses
	my murder would be 
		justified
	squashed
		explainable extermination
	I am not
		that different
	     from you,
	   but I guess its just
	easier to pretend
            I could be the echo
		of your self-destruction.
	I'm not a virus
		you don't have to
	kill me.

	Cats in the alley
		suddenly sick
	I am blamed for you
		black plagues
	       and bad luck
	and a slaying 3,000 miles away,
		yes,
	      my alibi will confess
	but he wears black too
		society's discard
	     beaten like a bruise.
	Hey chocolate night
		I'm still a baby
              in your eyes,
	only man defines me
		regardless of anything.

	The "lesbian witch" is
	   on the brink of disaster!
	She would tear us to 
		shreds
	with her hellish intent!


	Last Stop... cont.'

	They will never lack
		preconceived notions.
	My trial was over before
		I opened the door,
	when I look into
		the mirror
	I can't see my reflection,
	   can't see my good intentions,
		no,
	       not anymore.
