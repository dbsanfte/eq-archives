Vacant
© Laura Johnson



Ponder pen,
and mind in hand
emotions carved
in stone await.

The shattering stick
turns blood to ink
exposed for reaction,
each violation, of mind
in pursuit of a poem.

Finding in the thick,
superficial inward rift
Pacing on the borders
of insanity,
mortality,
reality and God.

Watch my soul's tears
bleed pathetic trickles
across a silent page,
In attempted escape
from the agony,
of that insanity,
mortality,
reality and God.

Vanity.
In death
the final poem
of my remains,
held captive
to literary vacancy.
