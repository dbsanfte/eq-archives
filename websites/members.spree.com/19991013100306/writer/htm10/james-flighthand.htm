Flight in Hand
© M.E. Janes





I have spent the majority of my painting life in Canada working from the east in New Brunswick to the province of British Columbia in the west where I now reside. During the 1970's I spent approximately eight years living in Australia and while there worked with the attention and assistance of three outstanding watercolorists. Winnifred Beamish of Goolagong, N.S.W, Jack McDonough of the University of Bathurst, N.S.W and Peter Constant-Ellis in Sydney, N.S.W. Since returning to Canada and British Columbia I have enjoyed working with Mae LeBlanc and trying her rather unique and enjoyable method of water color pours. In addition much time recently has been spent exploring the fantasim of digital art using my background of varied experience and preferences for the watercolor effect.
