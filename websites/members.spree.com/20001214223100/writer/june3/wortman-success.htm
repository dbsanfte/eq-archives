 What is success?

© Tamara Wortman

Have you ever wondered...

Why are some people more successful than others?

Why do some people make more money, have more friends, enjoy better health and get greater satisfaction from life than others do?

All Successful people have certain traits and habits in common. Use them in your life to access your personal power and realize your full potential as human beings. Our goal is to empower you to control your thinking, attitude and outlook while providing you the steps needed to ahieve your goals. Use these proven methods to get what YOU want and bring about dramatic improvements in your life.

What is success?

There are basically six requirements for success.

1) Peace of mind. Without peace of mind nothing else has very much flavor. Peace of mind means freedom from fear, freedom from anger and freedom from guilt. Going to sleep at night completely in harmony with ourselves and waking up the same way.

2) High levels of health and energy. No matter what we have in our life, if we don't have our health or we do not have the energy to enjoy our life we do not get much satisfaction from it.

3) Loving relationships. The ability to enter into and maintain long-term intimate, mature relationships with other people.

4) Financial freedom. It means that we have enough money so that we don't worry about money. The amount will vary from person to person, when you are not preoccupied with where the money to pay your bills is going to come from. We can't enjoy life if we're preoccupied with money.

5) Worthy goals and ideals. As human beings we are not capable of functioning at our best unless we have clear goals that we are committed to and worthy ideals to believe in. Our need for meaning and purpose is the greatest single drive in human nature.

6) A feeling of personal fullfillment. The feeling that we are becoming everything we are capable of as human beings, fullfilling our full potential in life.

We all measure our success relative to our ability to enjoy more of these six ingredients of a well rounded life.

So, why is it that so few people can be described as successful? A full 95% of all people reaching retirement age in our society today end up flat broke and dependent on others for survival. Only 5% or less acheive financial independence in their lifetimes.

There are precise steps that all successful people use, consciously or unconsciously, everyday to gain all six ingredients to success in their lives. Once you learn to use these methods everyday in your own life you will see your dreams and wishes turning into reality.

Ask yourself this question, "If I could do one thing in my life knowing that I was guaranteed to succeed, what would it be?"

Know that whatever it is, you CAN do it!

Think about this question seriously. It will help you to focus on what it is you really want to do with your life.
