RIC MASTEN SPEAKING
A truly wonderful book from my friend Ric Masten. The work in this collection is largely autobiographical in nature, yet we see ourselves in the trappings of the poets rich expressions. The words combine with some amazing photography by social gerontologist Marianne Gontarz and provides us with a sensitive work of art. This book is a masterpiece in the truest nature of the word. Bound by Red Clay © Neca Stoller
An extraordinary first volume of poetry from the accomplished Neca Stoller. I've read through it several times and enjoy more with each new reading. Southern writing and literature has always been my favorite and Neca does a superb job in bringing that southern flavor to the peak of perfection. As you turn the page you'll recall Gatsby, you'll catch glimpses of Blanche provoking Stanley, and you'll hear echoes of Maggie complaining about her sister in law's "little no neck monsters." An outstanding work!
