 What is Reality?
© Roy Cook-

How can we really judge what is real, and what is not?

How do you base your perception of Reality?

An amazing thing that I have just learned is that we do Create Our Own Reality!

I've always thought that things just seem to be different from time to time, and it wasn't just my Imagination!

Do you know how time seems to Speed Up, or Slow Down?

Is it just your imagination?

I don't think so, It happens too often. Is it just Our Perception of time?

I really don't think it is Just our perception that changes. I think that we are Making Our Own reality, by thinking about what we want, while energizing our thoughts with Emotion!

I have been reading a lot about Negative Events that are supposed to happen in our Near Future, and that were Supposed to have happened already, and they aren't, and haven't!

Why? There is a group of people on this planet that like to call themselves Light Workers. These Light Workers are changing the negative predicted events into Positive Events just by Thinking about what they want to happen, Instead of the Negative event, and It Happens!

Do you know what Real magic is?

Real Magic is the manisfestation into reality that which the person Wants To Happen!

Magic is really Creativity!

How Creative Are You?

Do you control your own Reality?

In actuality, we all control Our Own Reality by thinking about what we want to come to be, and mysteriously, it becomes Real!

If you think about something that you want often enough, and charge those thoughts with emotion, they take on a life of their own.

These things can be either Positive, or Negative.

Please, don't spend hours, and hours thinking hatefully toward your fellow people. Instead, think about how you want them to think about you, in the most positive way possible.

If your neighbor has wronged you in some way then think about how you would like your neighbor to undo the wrong in a positive manner that you can both feel good about.

This one method will help prevent many fights, battles, and wars!

Love, and kindness are much better than Hate, and Hatefullness, for everyone!

Let us all live together in harmony, and love.
