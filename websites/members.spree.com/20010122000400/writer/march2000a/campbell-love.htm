 The Way Love Grows
© Donald D. Campbell 
As a seed finds its way into the ground,
 A new transformed life begins as it were lying in wait.
 It grows as the rain and sun feed it daily, 
It sprouts roots grounded deep in the soil,
Growing stronger each day.

So it is with a love of a man and woman. 
It must start from a small seed,
 Much like that of a mustard seed. 
Then their love plants deeply into each others hearts,
 It is here that they receive their nourshment,
As the plant grows with the sun and is fed with the rain,
So are two fed by each other.
 One as the sun, while the other is the rain.
