 How To Overcome Adversity
© Dave Cole

Condition yourself to be positive. Being positive is a mental state that you can develop. Your attitude and your mood are always under your control.

Block out any negativity that surrounds you. Negative people will drain your spirit and your desire to be successful. Read about successful people, this will inspire you to also become great.

Napoleon Hill wrote, "In every adversity, there is a seed of an equal or greater benefit." Learn to begin looking at adversity and stumbling blocks, not as obstacles in your way, but rather as learning opportunities.

It's all in how you look at things.

Before you make a million dollars, you must first become a millionaire in your mind.

If you cannot picture yourself reaching a goal, then the race is over before it starts.

Always dwell on what went right - always look and try to find the good in yourself and in other people.

A great majority of troubles are minor annoyances, but if dwelt upon, lead to a pattern of annoyance and worry. Feed your mind on little worries and it will develop quite an appetite for big worries.

Sit around and think about your problems or worries, and you will automatically attract to them their older brothers and sisters. You will soon be a dead duck.

The best destroyer of worry is to do something constructive - go into action - MAKE YOUR MIND FOCUS ON THAT ACTION

Anticipating a no response from someone else is the surest way to get it. The top notch salesman must first sell himself the product before he can ever expect to sell it to any one else.

There is an amazing field of energy that is flowing throughout the universe, You attract to you from it what you want.

Think about your lack and you attract lack.

Dwell on whats wrong and you only attract more wrong and more problems. Did you ever know any one who just seemed to attract good things to them? It just seems like everything they touch turns out right.

You can learn how to have that "Midas touch."

You too can become like a magnet that attracts to it success and prosperity in every area of your life.

Read Emerson's essay on Compensation.
http://www.jjnet.com/emerson/compensation.htm

Great minds and great people fill their lives and minds with great thoughts. Majestic thoughts, thoughts of grandeur and plenty.
