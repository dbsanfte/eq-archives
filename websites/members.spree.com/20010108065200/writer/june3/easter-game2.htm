 The Waiting Game II

© Luke Easter 

  
  
Joyce La Royce,
 Yes that's my name,
I too am playing,
 The Waiting game,
I play the game with,
 Shari Danielle,
Just look at my stomach,
 Can't you tell?
We've been playing this game,
 For thirty weeks,
And during that time,
 It's been tough to sleep,
Six weeks ago,
 Six months were gone,
Baseball, basketball or football,
 Doesn't last that long,
Shari every time you move,
 I feel the pain,
Can't we play something other than,
 The Waiting Game?
I know it's cozy,
 And warm inside,
But it's tough for mommy,
 To sleep at night,
It's even tough to sleep,
 During the day,
I never imagined,
 It would be this way,
If I would have know this,
 28 weeks ago,
I would have told your daddy,
 Oh heck no,
How dare your dad,
 Give me the line,
A year from now,
 It will again be time,
That crazy idea,
 I don't where he's gettin,
But I'm not gonna spend,
 Another nine months spittin,
It would be nice for you,
 To have a little sister or brother,
But not at the expense,
 Of making a looney toon mother,
Everyday's a battle,
 Going up hill,
How come I never,
 Took the pill,
All this loosing sleep,
 And fighting pain,
It's tough to play,
 The Waiting Game,
But I'll get my revenge,
 Just wait and see,
Next thing you know,
 I'll be a grand mommy,
Then you'll be fighting,
 And wrestling with pain,
When your turn comes to play,
 The Waiting Game,
Untill that time,
 I'll have to grin and bear it,
When it comes to the pain,
 I wish your dad could share it,
He's running around laughing,
 And calling me fat,
The nerve of that man,
 What do you think of that?
Oh well ten more weeks,
 That's all that's left,
Then it's time for you,
 To take your very first breath,
Take it easy on mommy,
 When it's time to pass,
Or I'll grab you from the doctor,
 And really spank your ass,
So quickly your body,
 Will come to life,
You'll be ready to go home,
 That very same night,
Your dad and I love you,
 We really do,
We just can't wait to put,
 Our arms around you,
Well the time has come,
 To say good night honey,
Let's have a restful sleep,
 You little Easter Bunny,
This second little rap,
 With a story to tell,
Is brought to you by the mother,
 Of Shari-Danielle!
