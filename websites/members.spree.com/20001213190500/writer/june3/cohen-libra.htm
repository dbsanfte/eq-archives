 Why can't a Libra decide?

© Debra Cohen, LCSW

A libra client the other day mentioned that she has been reading different diet books and can't decide between "Protein Power", "Eat Right For Your Blood Type", "Pritikin" "Macrobiotics" etc etc. She said each one seems convincing while she reads it. Jenny also said that at the same time she brings in other points of view to not feel so "swallowed up" by what she's reading. I think this illustrates two tendencies of libras:

1) The ability (or handicap) of being completely entranced by something.

2) The tendency to "relativize" or bring in alternative positions in order to make everything equal and not have one view be superior over others.

We were talking about her desire to find the ONE diet, self-help method, fashion statement, philosopher etc. Jenny was saying she hopes to find the one cleaner to throw away all cleaners, the one recipe book to throw out all her other recipe books etc. It often seems this happens- ie she saw an infomercial for quick n brite where the woman threw away all her other cleaners and just used that one. It claimed to clean mirrors, cars, the bathroom, dirty clothing, shampoo the rug and much more.She got excited and ordered it. It didn't seem to work in every application the way the infomercial said and she felt this great disappointment.

But, she also wasn't sure if she might not have made the concentrations up correctly or had the patience to wait . (For example,in trying to get out stains in her jeans she didn't have the patience to let it soak in overnight so she immediately put it in the washer). This of course brings up the meta-level point about how the libran can't "wait" often or think of steps and stages but needs the experience to be NOW.

I think everything is summed up in this example: There's the desire to finally get rid of all the "multiplicity" , "choices" , alternatives", "options" and just find the "one" solution. Is this an attempted antidote to the libran love of possibility? This also explains her attractions to Virgos who seem to be able to assert ONE view , product or philosophy rather than be tortured by the many vying for the position.

The desire for finding the "one" is our desire to find what the mind can rest in finally- the self-sufficient. This is a universal search and can be seen illustrated in religious literature- The One without a second, the Infinite . We also can hear it in all the love songs on the radio: "You are my only one", "I just want to be your everything". The libra at the same time doesn't want to exclude what else exists PLUS doesn't want to get stuck, swallowed up or merged completely by something. Other signs often get frustrated because while explaining the virtues of one diet to a libra, the libra starts saying, "But what about how x says this".

In returning to the diet books example, we can see how the libra mind-form is at play: Jenny liked reading the macrobiotics philosophy because it did seem to cover different foods in each meal- a little bit of beans, grain, seaweed, seeds etc. But, she objected that it left out fruit plus seemed restrictive. She had a similar reaction to "ProteinPower"- what about eating carbohydrates? What she felt most attracted to was "Eat Right for Your Blood Type" because it seemed to take account of individuality.

Types Os have one diet, Type As have another etc etc. What Jenny liked about this diet was that it didn't say there was only "ONE " way. It said some people will benefit from being vegetarians, other people benefit from eating meats and high proteins etc. She also found it explained why some people with heart conditions do well with Pritikin principles and others doing well eating like Eskimos.

It seemed like this was a mirror of what a libran can like: The book that says there is diversity of different types without recommending just ONE method or solution. And can Jenny stick to this diet? Her comment was interesting: "I'm a Type O but I wish I was a type A". When we went into why she replied, "because type O's are supposed to be carnivores and I don't want to make everything into me". Again, we see another illustration of the libran mind-form: If I eat other creatures then I'm violating the principle of equality because I'm saying that I should live and they should die."

The amazing thing about all of this is that we can get a sense of wonder in watching how our minds evaluate, make choices and get excited about one trend or another. We use our own inner images all day to look at the world and keeping a notebook outlining one's eating habits, romantic attractions , favorite writers etc helps us to uncover our mind-forms. And since the world is populated by other signs than libra and we all are doing this think of the complexity of our interactions.
