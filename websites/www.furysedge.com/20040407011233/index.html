Gates of Discord - The first few weeks     -update by Sasarella 03/01/2004

Gates of Discord

The first few weeks

I truly wish I had a screenshot that would allow you to see the insane train that was on us. It was so unbelieveable all we could do was sit there with our chins to the floor instead of simply clicking the zone out that was less than a foot behind us.

Gates has proven to be very different. It's full of challenging riddles and new encounters. Yet some minor things from previous expansions have carried over into this one. Lets take the Shissar for example. Did SOE create a bride for the snakes we came to love in Luclin? I'd say so...
How about zones? They are very nicely designed with many new creatures and events. But I couldn't help but cringe when I compared these two screenshots.

Overall most of us are pleased with the design of Gates. This expansion is definately seperating the men from the boys. Each new challenge tests your skills and prepares you for the next step.

I'm not going to bore you with loot or corpse screenshots that you can see off of any guild news update, instead I will say that Fury's Edge is advancing quite nicely and there is much more to come. So, stay tuned in for the next update.
