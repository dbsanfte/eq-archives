Changes in the Wind     -update by Cosmodeous 05/18/2004

Update!

Fury`s Edge has undergone a massive amount of internal changes in the last few weeks, these are often painful for a guild yet through it all there is one thing that has remained constant:

Fury's Edge is a raiding guild, banded together to tackle the toughest challenges Everquest has to offer, both current and future and to allow its members the opportunity to participate in extraordinary encounters.

This has, and will continue to remain our primary focus.

Both the Gates of Discord expansion and Spring have taken its toll on us. Like many other guilds, several long-term members have taken breaks or have decided to leave for real-life issues. Unlike many other guilds, our core members have come together, become much more focused and want to continue taking on the most challenging encounters this game has to offer us. We have, and will continue to farm the Plane of Time and press deeper into the Gates of Discord expansion.

What does all this mean? It means that these changes have created several vacancies that are now to open for applications. Any questions you have regarding applying should be directed to Medusarexei in game. Please note: Exceptional members of any class will be considered on a case-by-case basis, characters already flagged for Time and/or Kod-Taz preferred (but not required) and server transfers are also welcome to apply.

We have also had several previous members return to us recently. Welcome back! We`ll get you back up to speed and raiding with the rest of us as soon as we can get you caught up with flagging. It`s good to have you with us again!

With all of the changes, new members, returning members and VERY successful recent raids, many of us can honestly say, we are once again having fun, despite many of the bugs we`ve had to deal with, and it`s long overdue.

News updates will be much more frequent in the future as well, be they informative or just entertaining.
