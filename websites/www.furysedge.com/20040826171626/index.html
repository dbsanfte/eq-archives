Dogs & Goats & Dragons

Oh my!

Pressing forward into the Gates of Discord continues to be our primary focus. As evidence by our recent meeting with this fellow. Some of the themes used in GoD are interesting, but we are glad to be out of the cookie cutter dungeons and into something different now.

Some of these encounters offer new and interesting problems and challenges, some are just more of the same but with harder hitting mobs and AE's that make parts of your anatomy pucker. We'd continue to press on through them like we always have. We can say this though, back-flagging in GoD makes you want to kick a family pet at times.

Fun and friends is what it's all about, well, that and making fun of Solthan's Luclin gear. Which, much to our regret he seems to be finding ways to replace.

We can honestly say this though, revamps like Veeshan's Peak are are great thing. Many of us had forgotten how nice it is to actually get back to the fantasy aspect of the game. It's that magic that we somehow lost along the way. Giants, Dragons, Mythical Creatures of all size and shape. Let's hope the next expansion or two after Omens is released brings us back to the world of fantasy and myth.
