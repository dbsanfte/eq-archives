    Tuesday, 18 April 2000
     Word from Dereth Defense Council

    From Lepton:
    Fellow citizens of Dereth, soldiers of the Dereth Defense Council, it is my duty to report that the counter attack upon shadow forces resulted in a stalemate. Some groups met with success, while others did not. Before I discuss the specifics of the attack, I would like to congratulate all soldiers and civilians who joined the attack on their bravery and performance during the battle.

    At approximately 7:00 PM CST, all of the battle groups except for the Lin group moved out. Each group contained, from my understanding, approximately twenty to forty soldiers each.

    The northern armies met with victory after victory. Despite minor casualties, the attack groups pressed on and eliminated nearly all the shadows sighted on their advance to Stonehold.

    The attack force from Lin met with heavy resistance and was forced to retreat back to Lin several times. However, they reformed and pressed on with the attack, and, from what I understand, returning to Lin only when there were no more shadows left to fight.

    Runemaster's group, the Qualaba'r force, met with initial success. They broke through the shadow lines around Qualaba'r, killing many shadows. However, they then swung west towards the south Direlands, where they met with heavy resistance. The attack force suddenly found themselves surrounded by shadows, at which point the attack disintegrated into an unorganized melee. Heavy casualties were taken.

    Despite these minor setbacks, we must continue to fight. We must never give in, nor ever surrender. I strongly urge all of you to fight the shadows wherever and whenever you can, whether it be organizing your own small attack parties, or providing soldiers with supplies and aid. No matter what you contribute, you are still contributing to the war effort.

    In the future, I urge all citizens to stand tall with us, to help fight the shadows. I urge all citizens to join attacks upon the shadows, as lack of manpower was one of our primary difficulties. I urge all citizens to defend their homes. I urge all citizens to cooperate with our soldiers, as we can only prevail by standing tall, standing united.

    I assure all of you; we will prevail. I give you my word; we will emerge victorious. The Qualaba'r attack force's defeat was but a minor setback. In the future, I promise the Council will lead victory after victory against the shadows. However, we can only emerge victorious with your help.

    -Lepton, President, Dereth Defense Council(Leafcull)


    Thanks Lepton!

     Retirement party (Harvestgain)

    Halken the Bold, Monarch of the Harvestgain Allegiance known as the Guardians of Dereth has announced that he will retire on the 19th day of this month. He has long been known throughout the land as a generous and helpful leader, not only to his followers, but also to all travelers of Harvestgain. Leadership of the Allegiance will be handed down to three high ranking current members: Dregorn the Fierce, Feng Liang, and Son Gojin. Dregorn the Fierce will be the new overall Monarch. Thagoris the Joker, whom is to be married to Aberleigh next Saturday, will host a feast honoring Halken the Bold after his ceremony. The wedding will be held in the Hebian-To meeting hall Saturday April 14th at noon PST (2 CST) The feast will commence afterwards at an location to be determined. If you are one of many whom Halken has helped along the way, I encourage you to drop by and send him off with celebration. * In real life Halken is taking on a mission for his church that requires him to leave the U.S. for two years. This is the reason for his retirement from the game. We wish him all the luck and success in the world in his new endeavor*

     Gaming Globe Awards 2000

    The movie industry has the Oscars, the music industry has the GRAMMYs, and now the gaming industry has the Gaming Globes!
