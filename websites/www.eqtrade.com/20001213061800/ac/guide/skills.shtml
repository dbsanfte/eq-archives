Skill Formula Description
Assess Person (Focus + Self) / 2 Helps you figure out humans' attributes.
Melee Defense (Quickness + Coordination) / 3 Helps ypu evade melee (hand-to-hand) attacks.
Sword (Strength + Coordination) / 3 Helps you wield swords and similar weapons.
Appraise Item Focus Helps you figure out non-magical info about items.
Appraise Weapon Focus Gives info about magical and non-magical weapons.
Dagger (Quickness + Coordination) / 3 Helps you wield daggers, knives and similar weapons.
Jump (Strength + Coordination) / 2 Helps you jump higher and fall farther.
Leadership Self / 4 Affects the experience gained from your vassals.
Magic Defense (Self + Focus) / 10 Helps you resist magic from spells and magi items.
Run Quickness Helps you run faster and jump farther.
Appraise Armor Focus Gives info about magical and non-magical armor.
Appraise Magic Item Focus Helps you figure out information about magical items.
Arcane Lore Focus / 3 Helps you learn how to use spells and magic items.
Assess Creature (Focus + Self) / 2 Helps you figure out creatures' attributes.
Axe (Strength + Coordination) / 3 Helps you wield axes, hammers and similar weapons.
Bow Coordination / 2 Helps you fire bows and similar weapons.
Crossbow Coordination / 2 Helps you fire crossbows and similar weapons.
Deception (Focus + Self) / 4 Helps prevent others from seeing your attributes.
Loyalty Self / 4 Affects experience given to your patron in an allegiance.
Mace (Strength + Coordination) / 3 Helps you wield maces, clubs and similar weapons.
Missile Defense (Quickness + Coordination) / 5 Helps you avoid damage in missile (long-range) combat.
Spear (Strength + Coordination) / 3 Helps you wield spears and similar weapons.
Staff (Strength + Coordination) / 3 Helps you wield staffs and similar weapons.
Thrown Weapons Coordination / 2 Helps you use weapons meant for throwing.
Unarmed Combat (Strength + Coordination) / 3 Helps you punch, kick, and wield punching weapons.
Alchemy (Coordination + Focus) / 3 Lets you prepare gem components, potions and oils.
Cooking (Coordination + Focus) / 3 Lets you make and modify foodstuffs.
Creature Enchantment (Focus + Self) / 4 Allow you to cast spells that affect creatures' abilities.
Fletching (Coordination + Focus) / 3 Lets you make and modify arrows and quarrels.
Healing (Focus + Coordination) / 3 Helps you heal injuries.
Item Enchantment (Focus + Self) / 4 Allows you to cast spells that affect items' properties.
Life magic (Focus + Self) / 4 Allows you to cast spells that heal and protect creatures.
Lockpick (Coordination + Focus) / 3 Lets you analyze locks and pick them open.
Mana Conversion (Focus + Self) / 6 Reduces the Mana consumed by spells and magic items.
War Magic (Focus + Self) / 4 Lets you cast spells that damage creatures.
