Wicked Bone Atlatl-5 AC +6 Sta +10 Wis +10 vsPos +10 vsDis +30 HP +30 Mana

Effect: Focus of Mithaniel

1: Increase Spell Haste by 20%
2: Limit: Max Level(65)
3: Limit: Spell(Complete Healing excluded)
4: Limit: Min Casting Time(3.0 sec)
5: Limit: Spell Type(Beneficial only)
6: Limit: Effect(Percentage Heal excluded)

For those of you that spent time in VT, or are allready in the elemental planes... this doesn't apply to you, but the rest of you should seriously look into this item.

Talisman of Benevolence was my old range slot, and seems to be pretty common among shaman. Switching to this increased my HP by 1(woot!) and I lost about 50 mana. I'd gladly give up 50.. even 100 mana for this focus effect. It reduces time for all buffs obviously, but also reduces Torpor and Tnarg's. I've got QB2 right now and my Torpor has a 3.3 second cast time. If I got QB3, that'd be 1.8 second Torpor! I think this is a must have for anyone that doesn't have a ridiculous item in their range slot.

It drops in CoD, off of what... i'm not sure. If you're looking to buy one, you can normally pick it up in the 30-50k range.
