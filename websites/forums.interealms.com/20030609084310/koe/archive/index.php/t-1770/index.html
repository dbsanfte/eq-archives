In answer to your question..

IMHO, it depends on what you get.. 4-6 groups (depending on levels, class mix, etc). I recommend no less than 3 clerics (prefer 4) and 3 chanters. The chanters must be at their best and I think it is one of the hardest tests as far as crowd control goes in the game. (As a side note, I thought the chanters did awesome the other night. Take a bow, you deserve it). After talking to several people, we came to the conclusion that the chanters did most of the tanking the other night.. :) A raid breaker is you need a rogue with 201 lock pick. A monk is very much a plus for pulling. With the changes to CoH (not sure if they are in or not), you need some type of FD puller to break each of the royal rooms.

Other things to take into consideration are wipe outs and what to do. It happens.. a lot. I would have some plans in place to deal with this contingency. (Coffins and necro/sk). If you get to the safe hall, CR can be done there. If you get nailed before then, it could be hard to find a spot to start up CR as the dogs see invis and see through rogue sneak.

Hope this helps and good luck.. :)
