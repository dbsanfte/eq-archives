Grafsha McDaughts
Cleric is the perfect toon to pwrlvl with a druid if you have access to one. At 23 head to Unrest, wolf form, regen, Nature hp buff, ds, str and head to house. Pull and you will get adds. Use the AE nuke (not sure if that is 24 or higher for first level of AE spell.)

You can go thru to 34 working Unrest and go thru fast. Melee skills will max as will offense/defense. Casting skills will lag so you will spend some time working those back up in PoK.

Afterwards head to Sol A, rinse repeat tactic (without wolf form of course, indoor dungeon.) Camp King room for cleric epic drop.

After this head to Qeynos Catacombs at 36 or so and do the 4 spectre spawn. Can pull single using pacify. This will take you to 42-44 (gets slow 43ish.) Other option is Gunthak beach killing undead from 36-44. Same tactic, pull groups and let ds kill them and you pop a couple AE nukes.

If you don't have access to a druid, DS potions can substitute though the cost would be lots.
Juventus
If you are 65 THE best way to powerlevel a cleric to the 50's is the same way a druid would, except you use damage shield potions.

Drink potion, aggro as many mobs as you can heal yourself through and let them beat themselves to death. Before they die and after they are sufficiently pissed at you, take the cleric in the middle of the pack and cast his lowest level PBAoE nuke. He will get the exp for all kills. It works like a champ up through 50ish. GuK worked great for it through the 40's for my cleric. Beyond Guk it becomes obsolete as the mobs start to hit too hard to pull a big enough number to make it worthwhile.
The potions really are not that expensive (30pp a dose?) and if you actually loot the mobs you will probably break even. At 65 they last a good while. 15 minutes or so.

Past 55, I am stumped myself as to a PLing method. I usually just solo and let the cleric watch. Any suggestions would be great as it is very slow grouped with me.

Clerics BIGGEST strength is PBAE ...

Got my 2nd account Cleric to 39 in 16 hours played by using the following ....

Shammy DS potion + regen + FT15, pull as many mobs as you can find in Mistrmore with Malosi (low mana cost) .... pull from Front yard, Grave yard and Pit .... let them eat selves on DS, Cleric load up the best 2 PBAEs for level .... Very fast XP rate ....

At about Cleric level 40, you can move the same tactics to Karnors Castle or Chardok or HS or Velks or The Hole or SSra temple or Inner Acrylia or well, ya get the picture ...... you are looking for relatively low HP mobs that don't hit ya shammy that hard and are relatively non resistant to magic (cleric PBAE) ...

PS. You can get all the way to 65 with this tactic, just gotta pick ya zone and modify tactics accordingly .... DS Cleric and Shammy, have cleric heal shammy, then Shammy heal cleric for aggro switching ... at this level, start pulling with slow ;)
