-This would be nice, but since it's not Fo7 that has the problem but rather any upcoming Shaman buffs, why not go ahead and change those instead?

14. Improve Shaman Debuffing Capabilities
-New spell lines for this would be great but not likely this far into the game development. As has been said before though, increasing the effectiveness of the cripple line could be a start for sure.

15. Group FA
-Start at #2, work from there.

16. new bear form for higher level
