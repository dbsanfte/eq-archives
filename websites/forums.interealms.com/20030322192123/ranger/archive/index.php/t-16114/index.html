First - /bonk. Welcome to the Glade

Yeah, level 5 is a bit young - especially with your first character who probably doesn't have much in the way of money. Better to just do some basic fletching and get the hang of it, make a basic bow and some arrows to start with. But you could easily go to Freeport and Kaladim and do those two parts of the quest, although for the Kiola Nuts you'll need a bit of coin and a few containers to carry them in. You should be able to get the Spiderling Silks, too. Only tough part at your level, come to think of it, will be getting the Clockwork gears. And having the money to spend on Fletching, of course.

For training points, general advice is to only put one point into new skills as you get them - Kick, for example, at level 6 or whatever. Put one point into it, and then use it - it will go up naturally. Same for Sneak, Dual Wield, Double Attack. Then, when you get to a higher level, you can use them for trade skills or for a weapon skill you've neglected.

At your level, as far as grouping or soloing, I'd just hang out in Faydark and take the groups as they come. Maybe solo until you get some spells at level 9, and see what you can do with them in a group situation (Snaring, for example).
