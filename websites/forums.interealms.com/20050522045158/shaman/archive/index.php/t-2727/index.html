Just for reference (at level 49 Togors gives 59% slow, Tagars gives 44.5% slow)

Walking Sleep
Decrease Attack Speed for between 23% (L14) to 35% (L60)
Slow % = 20 + (level / 4)

Tagar's Insects
Decrease Attack Speed for between 34% (L29) to 50% (L60)
Slow % = 20 + (level / 2)

Togor's Insects
Decrease Attack Speed for between 49% (L39) to 70% (L60)
Slow % = 10 + level

Turgur's Insects
Decrease Attack Speed for between 66% (L51) to 75% (L60)
Slow % = 15 + level
