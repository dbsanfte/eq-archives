i'm not in a big guild, dont' have many friends in game,not high lv.

with a small group of friends i attempted to get sow boot for quite a few times.and i got it nights ago.so just want to share how i did that.perhaps some of you might find this useful.

group was 53 shaman(me),53 monk,54 cleric,51 warrior,50 sk.

i really did tried many ways to do it,but i'll just tell you how the succeed attempt goes.

1.clear the valley with bloodwolves,there are a few DE and 2 garg that will arggo on you.main force set up here.

2.get my monk tp train adviosr(and 10+ DE) down to the valley,kill them all while we leave adviosr alone.just let him hit our tanks.no biggie after slowed.

3.casters med up while melee keep the bloodwolves killed upon spawn.

4. when we're set ,trigger black dire and kill him, at lv 53 my first cast was malosi,got resisted but slow landed without it.it's very easy kill after it's slowed, but before slow landed it drop my monk from full to half hp in around 10 secs( then my monk use all-ripost disc)


i think it's actually doable with a shaman,druid and a tank.just with care.the most difficult part is to clear the blood wolves.others aren't too difficult.

hope this helps

Grippa
SoW boots are the Black Fur Boots. They are one of the rewards from the shaman epic quest, to get them you have to kill a wolf in mistmoore castle.

JBB could indeed stand for Jaundiced Bone Breastplate, which is something that Trakanon drops, but what people are actually talking about is the Jaundiced Bone Bracer. The nice thing about this bracer is it has a right click nuke spell on it - ie mana free damage.
