there are fun guilds out there, mostly far and very few between, but I had the luck to be in two of them on my server.

of course, this was all way back when, before kunark way back when. and the guild was more of just a big group chat and if we could we would group together and kill things. some people wanted more, but most were just in it for the social. sure there was drama, but with any group of people over 1 you are going to get some sort of drama. it is the guild leaders jobs though to stop as much of this from seeping into the guild chat and trying to solve the problems with the people included.

anyway, I agree heartily that this game was not meant to be a race to get to the high level guild to do epic encounters. I did a few hate, fear, vox raids (with a friends account) and honestly, I have had more fun with one group of people back in the day doing runnyeye.

sometimes it helps to just drop out of everything and sit back and just think about what part of the game gives you the most fun. some people it is doing trades, some it is seeing how much plat they can make in the bazaar, others it is a race to get to the top. the goals of the game are different for everyone.

it is like one of those old choose your own adventure books. you read through a section, choose your response and never read the same story twice. one thing about those books though, there is usually only one ending that you are really happy with.

EQ is much the same, you have plenty of choices starting from the character selection, and the options broaden from there.

make of the game what you can, but don't try to make yourself happy by following others footsteps. do your own thing.
