Resist gear is tricky.
Unless you have a bottomless pit of money, it is unrealistic to buy all the resist gear that you would like to have. You have to be selective. Either go for items that have very high resist on one or two particular resist, such as stone of morid, or shield of the green dragon, or go for items that have some resist on all resists, such as plyer crafted diamond or blue diamond jewellery.

Blue diamonds and diamonds are quite common these days. My suggestion would be to buy some player made resist jewellery. A diamond mask and 2 blue diamond earrings would be a good start and fairly cheap. I wouldn't bother with blue diamond bracers - you should look towards getting bracelets of sense and / or fortitude which have good resists anyway. You may wish to get some resist rings too.

For raids it is also well worth knocking up a few resist potions - since negative stats have been removed from potions, they are actually quite good and cheap to make, and they stick with normal cast resist buffs
