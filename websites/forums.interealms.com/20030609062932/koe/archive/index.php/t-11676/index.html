If you want a plat farm place then try to get the ice giants in everfrost right outside the entrance to Lady Vox's lair. They give exp up past 55, drop anywhere from 15 to 65 pp and fine steel weapons. There are 3 spawn points, the first two and best camp to get are in there own little rooms at the gate you can sigle pull them np, then futher in is another one that guards the entrace/zone as well. I camped these for quite a few lvls once gnomes went green and made a pretty penny.
