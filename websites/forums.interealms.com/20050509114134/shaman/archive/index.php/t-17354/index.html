The point of a range is that it's not instant-effect. It takes place in its area over time. Calling our AE slow a rain is ridiculous.

For more clarification, AE stands for Area Effect. It effects a particular area. Regardless of how many it can effect in that area or how large/small that area is. PBAE - point blank area effect, typically infinate creatures in a tiny area of effect - centered on you.
