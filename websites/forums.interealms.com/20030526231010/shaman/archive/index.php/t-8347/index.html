All the DoT spells are not sold in Halas. They are sold in Everfrost.

If you follow the left hand wall when you zone out of halas you will (eventually) get out of the canyons and onto the Tundra - you will know when you have reached the Tundra because there are 3 barbarian NPCs guarding the entrance to the canyons, and also usually a Wolf NPC called Snowfang

Keep following the LH wall along the edge of the Tundra, but keep looking to your right - you should eventually be able to see a large ruined temple. Head to the temple and have a scout around on the raised area of the temple - you will find a Barbarian standing behind a fallen column - he seels a lot of our DoT spells, including the ones you mentioned
