Beeg
Personally when I twink I tend to avoid buying the BIG items such as the Flayed Belt. They're expensive and aren't really that much of an improvement. High levels tend to pay far too much for stuff that isn't that much better.

The example I always use is Wisdom rings. For about 200pp a pop you can get Platinum rings (+6), but to get Velium Rings (+7) is another 100pp each. Is one wisdom really worth 100pp? Not if you're trying to stretch out your money it isn't. I understand the cost of components and stuff... but a 16% wisdom increase for a 50% price increase???

Personally, using prices from Innoruuk server, I'd sell the belt. Only seen a few go and they were for around 2k if I remember right. With that I could pick up a Smiter's Girdle (~500pp), JB Boots (~600pp), JB Arms (~500pp) and a Slime Coated Harpoon (~300pp). That'll help you a LOT more than the wisdom on the belt (Smiter's Girdle is 10AC, +3 all stats too. So you're only losing 2wis/3dex). People don't like selling JB cheap though... but at the same time no one buys it. Give them your price and not a penny more then let them get frustrated. If they find a sucker that'll pay more... meh. Just play the waiting game. You could wear banded and level to 15 without breaking a sweat.

Charred Guardian shields are worth diddly too. People killing CWGs in SolA practically give them away. It'll tide you over til you get 30-ish and can grab the Epic shield (FREE!). Stay away from stuff that looks like a "book" though.

That's just my opinion though.
