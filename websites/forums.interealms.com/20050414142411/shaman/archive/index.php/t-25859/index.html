Macnair
Are there any other expansions that have content that might interest us? Any suggestions would be welcome...keeping in mind that I am the tank for our group of two...What they are telling you about Omens is correct.

The biggest weakness in your magelo is augments, introduced with LDoN and out of reach for those who solo / duo all the time.

That's why I think you should consider Gates. You can do Natimbi at your level, and pick up 20 hp augments for you and 20 mana augments for your wife. There are a few other nice drops there as well. You will get pretty decent experience there. Sometimes it will be challenging with the adds, but with an enchanter to mezz you should be able to handle that.

Since you are the tank, you need to work on your hp more. Bag the Lodi shield for when you need enduring breath, and get an iksar hide cape. Several of your pieces have available bazaar upgrades that will get you another 20 hp or so for that slot.

If you can get to 2700 unbuffed hp, you will be able to tank a lot of things that are iffy right now.

Good luck!
