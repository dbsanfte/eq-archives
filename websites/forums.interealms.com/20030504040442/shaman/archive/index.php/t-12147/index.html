Jorlaan
Is your defense maxed for your level? If not, then you should be in there swinging! :-) [or in there sitting on your horse, letting the mob hit you instead of dogdog.]

How long is it taking you to kill something? You are using a disease resist debuffer and two disease dot's. These are really slow working. Is the mob dropping before they wear off? Have you tried to do it without the disease debuffer? Are you getting resists without the disease debuffer?

You are using a group str spell on yourself and then casting it again on dogdog. That's a lot of mana for a barely noticable difference in str. I'd use Furious strength. It should be about 1/2 the cost in mana for about the same strength and about the same duration. (Bih'Li is group also but it's probably worth it for the increased attack for dogdog, but since you apparently aren't swinging yourself, you could save mana and just use SOW for yourself).

I read somewhere that STA doesn't affect pet hitpoints, so you can probably drop the Health on the pet.

Are you feeding dogdog his alpha food? If so, you might consider a dex buff depending upon what type of alpha food you buy him! :-)
