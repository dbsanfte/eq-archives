I just wanted to get an idea of some of the better ways to arrange spells in my book. As of now, they are just in there as I got them. Really messy.
Mamutoi
I group similiar type spells on the same page/pages. All of my buffs get grouped together with the group version on one page and single target versions on the opposite side of the page. My dots/dd spells are together an another page, utility spells on another (Gate, Enduring breath, shrink etc), heals and regens on another, debuffs and roots on another and so on. Move all of your lower spells that you don't use to the back of your spell book and out of the way. I also put all of the pre 60 buffs on a page after all of my main ones, that way if some lower level asks for buffs and I can't use my higher level buffs on them I don't have to go digging for the lower level versions.

Calabar
I played my ranger and druid to level 51 and never arranged my spell books. Rangers don’t really have enough spells to have to worry about, though some organization certainly wouldn’t hurt. With my druid, being my first character, it just never occurred to me to do so until I was already used to where everything was. Kind of like if you cleaned up your room you’d never be able to find anything again. *grins*

But when I started my shaman the spell names really threw me. So many of them have nothing to do with what the spell actually does. So I decided to group things together. My first page has attack spells, my second page has buffs and the next few pages have utility spells and such, things I think I’ll use and don’t want to have to dig for. Starting about page 20 I put all my other spells, arranged in alphabetical order by to level. When I organize, I organize.

Tenjhai
I can't remember how many times I have re-arranged my spell book, but what has worked easiest for me is my most used spells at the back. In general, your newer spells are going to be used more often, so if you put them at the back you can just add your new spells, and not have to worry about changing your whole book around again. Of course, I don't have all my spells yet, but when I do, I might consider moving them to the first page. One of my pages is set aside for the spells I mem after I die and I have them in order of which they go on my spell bar. This makes it really easy and I don't have to think (usually you are occupied enough after you die, and don't need to think about any more things :P)
