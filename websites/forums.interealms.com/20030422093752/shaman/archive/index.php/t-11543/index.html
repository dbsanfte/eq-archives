Weyalan
65hp > 7wisdom

The latest thinking is that hp is the most important thing, followed by AC, then mana, then wisdom.

Wisdom (and +mana items) determines the total size of your mana resevoir. But the secret is, not how much mana your resevoir can hold, but how quickly you can refill it.

i.e. Mana regeneration is more important than total wisdom. Unless you have lots of flowing thought stuff (and most of us do not), your main source of mana regeneration (apart from sitting on your a$$) is to cannibalise. So, your rate of mana regeneration is determined by both your total hp and your rate of hp regeneration. Spells such as torpor, used in conjunction with cannibalisation, basically giver you loads and loads of hp for conversion to mana, thus keeping your rate of mana regeneration high regardless of your wisdom.

Hp have the added bonus of keeping you alive.

that, my friend, is why hp > wisdom

You're level 58, right? Using can IV to regen mana at 148 a pop? Surely you can see the value of 65 hitpoints when you are casting Can IV over and over again.

Plus, you're going to keep leveling and eventually want Can 5, an incredibly powerful way for shaman to regen mana. It takes off 1924 hitpoints every time. It's a good idea to plan your gear goals around what it would take to solo with your own buffs while using can 5.
