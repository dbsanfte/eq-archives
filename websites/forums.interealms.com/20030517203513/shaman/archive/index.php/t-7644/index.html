Both as a necro and as a shaman I have hunted here a great deal....I expect I have killed somewhere between 500 and 600 plaguebones....in that time i have never had an add....

park your pet at the edge of the pyramid facing the doorway...take a few steps forward and f8 till you pick a mob...cast a slow on it and step back behind pet...as soon as mob hits the door tell pet to attack...then I would usually drop a dot on it, if it agros, turn on melee and poke it a few times, then step back and let pet get agro and give it a good nuke, if I didnt pick up agro on the dot I would generally sit and med till pet got down a quarter or a third, then nuke...

The nuke will bring agro to you and you wont shake it till the bones die, but by this point it wont be long...when the mob begins his walk sit down and med while pet finishes it off...

I would then generally run and loot because they can drop several plat...if you are rich just sit and med...by the time pet shows up you should be back to full mana and ready for another...

there are eight bones in the building and two that roam, you probably won't be able to clear them all till you get to your high 40s...
