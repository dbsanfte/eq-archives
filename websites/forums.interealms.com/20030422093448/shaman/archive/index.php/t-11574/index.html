Neh is a spawned mob. Neh isn't ever up because the only way for Neh to be up is for someone to specificaly go to the deepest part of the zone and kill the reaver that spawns neh, and since this normally only happens when a shaman is going for their epic, chances are you wont find neh just sitting there.

The reaver that neh spawns from is on a 2 hour spawn cycle i think, and unless city of mist is a highly contested zone on your server, you have a 99% chance that the neh reaver is up.

To answer your question, yes you can max your faction, and skip neh, but since it takes all of 15 mins for 1 group to go and kill neh, then I highly recomend that you just go kill her.

You can probably do a neh run from start to finish before you can find the wondering spirit one time, and you would have to do that 3 or 4 times to max your faction from where you are now.
