This may be apples and oranges, as I’ve yet to start on my alchemy skill, but I’ve found similarities in many of the trade skills I’ve worked on. And the main similarity is the complete randomness of it all. There are some general truisms, such as skill increases are faster at low skill levels and slower at high skill levels and that for skills that go to 250 the range from about 180 to about 210 are often hell levels. But the randomness factor is HUGE. For example: I’m working on brewing right now, which is thankfully inexpensive, and it took me just over 180 combines to go from 192 to 193. But I hit 194 and 195 within two combines of each other. I know you can average all the numbers together, but sometimes you can hit several dry spells in a row and on high plat combines it can be a killer. So honestly I think everyone will end up with different price totals in the end and some of them are going to differ by vast amounts.
