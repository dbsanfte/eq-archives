Drinnar
The idea behind using canniablize is this: If you aren't getting hit (and you shouldn't really get hit that much unless soloing) you might as well turn in the hps you're regenning and don't need into mana. I used to canni to 80% and then heal back up to full, and canni again. At level 40 (as per magelo) you should look into the canni2 quest, you're still a long way from canni3, and canni1 really doesn't do all that much. To those of us who leveled in the age before 4 hour KEIs for cheap as dirt in the nexus, canni was a VERY powerful form of mana regen in the lower levels.

At higher levels KEI makes less of a difference and canni becomes more and more a necessity.

Hope that's the answer you're looking for.
