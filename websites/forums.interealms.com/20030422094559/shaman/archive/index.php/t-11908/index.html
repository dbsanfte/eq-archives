Below is a quick guide to these rings:

http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=19651
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=19665

These quests were designed so that good aligned and evil aligned classes could get a similiar ring, with the introduction of illusion potions good classes can do the 2nd ring, which originates from an NPC in Neriak Commons. Costs about 400p in illusion dark elf potions (which modify your faction).

These items require Legacy of Ykesha and cannot be multiquested.

Expect to spend around 5 hours on average per ring, with a fight in Toxxulia Forest for each ring.

I suggest when camping the Luggald Flesh (Dulak) and Fecund Luggald Flesh (Nadox) to get 6 of each, 3 are needed for each quested ring.

If you have a melee twink or friend helping out you might want to check out these weapons, which can also result from the above quests.

http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=19854
