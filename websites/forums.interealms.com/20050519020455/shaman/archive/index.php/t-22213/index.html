The Netstat monitor shows everything's OK, but fights stop, spells don't cast, and I can do nothing but move until BAM everything starts back up again. Used to be that the monitor would show lag spikes like that. Evidently, the server is sending the EQclient data... it's just not usable (or believable?) data.

- Spells insta-casting ONCE, then never refreshing.

- Disciplines having all sorts of odd refresh times. We once believed this was due to the client PC being radically off in date or time from the server, but my PCs sync up with the national time server, and Brenlo's stated that the EQ servers do as well.

It's not limited to certain zones, although there are some zones that are notably worse (such as Sanctus Seru).
