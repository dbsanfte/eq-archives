Solariis
Well if you are looking for body armor then here's a rough progression of lowest to highest in terms of AC:

Totemic -- > quested all over the world
Spirit Weaver's --> drops in PoG
Rune Etched --> drops in PoH
Rune Crafter's --> Kael drop Thurg quest
Wolf Caller's --> ToV drop Skyshrine quest
Spirit Caller's --> ToV drop Kael quest

Rune Crafters, Wolf Callers and Spirit Callers all require 3 gems and enough faction(kindly+ for thurg and ally for Skyshrine). Just hand in the armor and gems to the appropiate NPC. All these quests are listed on allakhazam.

Best bet is to start getting in on some Kael arena raids for the Rune Crafters armor. Chain drops aren't common though so be prepared to go on several raids.

Jaradis suggested these 2 Quests on another Topic. They are some very nice pieces.

If you are looking for some nice sleeves and shoulders you should look into these quests.

Quest Info - Fusibility Research (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=1292)

Item Info - Strength of Elements (+100 HP Shoulders) (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6989) -- Requires getting these 4 elements:

-- Fusible Coral Ore - Off Phinigel Autropos in Kedge Keep
-- Fusible Earthen Ore - From Master Yael in the Hole
-- Fusible Igneous Ore - Ground spawn by the large volcano in Sky Fire mountains around 900, -1500
-- Fusible Velium Ore - Off a large air elemental named Guardian Kozzalym in Western Wastes.

Now if you are Ally with Chardock you can do this one. This is a very nice piece. It will fit in the Arms, Shoulders or Wrist. This would be an item you probably wouldn't replace for awhile.

Quest Info - Spirit Wracked Cord (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=1889)

This requires you do the Regal Band of Bathezid (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=1888) quest.

Yah I know trying to be ally with Chardock is the suck. If you are KOS it requires going to Droga or Nurga and killing close to 3000 GREEN mobs. Well less than that if you do the turn ins to Harold from the items they drop to get additional faction hits but you need a faction adjustment of about 3000. Info for how to change your faction comes from the Chardock Quests EZ Board (http://pub64.ezboard.com/bchardokquests). My faction is toast from all the Sarnak killing I did when I was a little cub in Lake ill Omen and the King/Queen Kills I have been on. But this is something you might want to look into.
