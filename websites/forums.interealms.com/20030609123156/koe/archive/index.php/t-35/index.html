Cyrssi
As I sit in this lonely field of darkness
Dwelling on a past I should have never known
Ever do I contemplate different paths
Never have I followed a path different than my own.
As I sit in this lonely field of darkness,
the light begins to engulf me.

A Star's light glimmers in the Darkness
As I approach it I wonder if it will welcome me Home
Of all the things which I have done
Leaving the Star's light seems to be the worst
I step closer yet stumble away
Falling further than I could possibly climb
Yet I walk on towards the Star's light
Wondering if it will welcome me Home.

I take new steps toward a brighter future
My oaths to myself, promises never to look back.
I see my light.
My light is my own, and it will always light the path in front of me,
Leaving the Darkness behind.



Shades of Light and Darkness align to determine my fate
yet all that matters is the grey...
