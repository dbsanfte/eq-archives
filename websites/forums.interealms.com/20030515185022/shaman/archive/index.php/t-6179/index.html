I did a post on Kedge Keep. You might want to try soloing there one 2nd floor (east cubbyhole is safest, or you can try south if a little more brave). Just need alot of fish scales or an EB item to do this and be sure to read up on everything you can about Kedge before doing it. There's an "ultimate guide to kedge keep" I found off one of the big EQ sites but I forget the page offhand. Find that and read it, and reread it. The preperation may be kind of a pain but once you go in knowing what's there and how cautious you need to be, the reward (of xp) is well worth it in my opinion. Lately though the south cubbyhole in Kedge (at least on mith mar) has turned dangerous for some reason. XP was nice at 53 to 54, so I bet at 52 you could do 2nd floor as well. - Warmag of MM
