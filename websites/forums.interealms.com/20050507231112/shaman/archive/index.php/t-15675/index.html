Konnor
I have seen this too on low level alts. It seems the damage is rounded to a whole number and it can be rounded so the extra damage is zero, in which case you don't get any activation message.

Disease dots to do less damage per tick than poison ones of the same level, so there would be more chance of the whole number being 0. Similarly Scourge is lower level than Plague so it does less base damage and hence has more chance of the addition rounding to zero etc.

As a quick test I tried casting sicken and tainted breath. I didn't get any extra damage at all from sicken and didn't get any message either. For TB I only got a message on the 9dmg ticks, but not on the 8dmg ones. (When you consider these spells you see why there is no minimum of 1 extra damage, although it wouldn't really break the whole game).

From your observations it sounds like the extra damage might be being rounded down, which would suggest scourge (base damage 24) should miss about 1 tick in 5. If the extra were being rounded to the nearest whole number it should miss about 1 tick in 10. The way to check would be to see if it ever did 29, since 24 boosted by 20% gives 28.8.

It doesn't really seem like a big deal.
