soupypoopy
Huh? He'll get his ass handed to him by an experienced PVP necro. His pet would walk through your pet and his dots would quickly drop you as the pet disrupts your casts of Torpor.

Here's what I'd do:

A. Buff Order. Buff in this order: inner fire, cold resist, fire resist, sow booties, stamina, agi, dr, mr, focus, pr, regrowth. When done buffing, click off the first two slots so that his dots don't end up at the end of your buff list, or Pyrocuror will make short work of you.

B. Spells Lineup....
1. annul magic (in addition to the pumice that you're going to buy, you need this spell memmed)

2. malo (for his pet so that you can root it, for him so that you can land your dots)

3, 4, 5. nife, ebolt, venom of snake (sound counterintuitive against a necro, right? But here's the trick. His PR is going to be high becuase he'll buff DMF, but alas, it's only a buff. Necros get real cocky vs. shaman because they think that DMF renders them immune. The nice thing about DMF is that it's a VISUAL buff -- if he's got it he'll float, so right out the gate you'll dispell his ass until he's no longer floating. Then you malo, then you start stacking your poison dots on him.

6. immobilize (you need that pet off of you fast and with an offensive strategy the fight won't last that long)

7. torpor

8. chloroblast (you need a quick heal -- if you get below two bubs he can life-tap you down real fast).

C. Fight ... The poison strategy is pretty offensive. If after you've dispelled DMF and landed malo you're still not landing anything, go defensive and run him out of mana. You can dispell EVERY SINGLE one of his dots while retaining yours so long as you are religious about spamming your booties after you dispell each time.

D. Dirty tricks. These are fun, if a bit cheap. See if you can convince him to fight you while the two of you are grouped. If he falls for this, you can torpor his pet right off the bat (it's an unresistable snare, remember?) That's often REALLY confusing to the necro because the pet isn't rooted, which is what he expected. While grouped with him, you can also INVIS him at a critical moment -- his pet will poof. Also, while grouped with him you can ALWAYS target him much more easily by hitting F2 -- this allows you to click his pet, root, then get back to your main target very quickly. Another dirty trick -- summon your pet before the fight then HIDE it somewhere in the fight zone. If you're in the bazaar, you can park your pet in the bank, buff it with Focus, Stamina, Resist Magic and Resist Poison long before the fight and then use "summon companion" at a critical moment to surprise the hell out of him. Your pet can make a real difference, but usually it'll get blasted down pretty quick by his pet if it's nearby at the beginning of the fight. And finally, you can invis yourself -- he probably wont' have a see-invis item because he's relying on DMF to provide see-invis. Once you're invis you can move around behind him and possibly get a spear dot on him or just plain melee him.

Oh, and one more tip: make sure it's 2/3 or 3/5 fights to declare a win. That way he can't just manaburn you and declare himself winner for all time.
