Giac
What Wulf said. Use Divine Stun AA from Paladins to move them.

That said, The Rathe Councilmen aren't really immune to disease, but you will not be able to land any Shaman disease based spell on them.

The SK debuff Aura of Darkness lands fine after Malicious, which will bring all resists down by another 10 points. This still won't be enough for Malady or VP.
There's another trick to move them if you don't have paladins though. Make melees equip non-magic weapons and melee push them. The Rathe Councilmen are immune to non-magic attacks and mezzes won't break.
