Mozeley
Soloing in PoN is possible at level 54. Although I don't think that you could call it soloing as such, as you will almost definitely need a C3/4/5 and an Aego/virtue.

I have only tried the spiders so far, I'm not sure if the trees/ravens have more HP or are more magic resistant as yet.

I pulled with Malise, let puppy get aggro and then slow, let puppy do as much as he can and then pull him off after rooting the mob.

Letting puppy tank is not an option, as even when they're slowed puppys health will go down fast, and it just turns out to be a major mana suck trying to keep him healed.

I then cast E-Bolt and leave it to stew for a bit. When root breaks, it's best to let puppy get aggro again, root and then back off.

I've got a JBB, but I've found that it's a bit risky using it, as 50 percent of the time it'll break root. I usually just cast E-Bolt and let it rot while canni'ing my mana back up and letting puppy regen some of his health.

It will take a while, and you'll have to weigh up the whether it's worth the amount of of time/risk involved compared to how much XP you're getting. I was getting at least 3 percent AA xp per kill. I never tried normal Xp to see how that was affected. But I'm betting it's something like 1 percent per kill or even better.

It's hard, and you will die a couple of times. But if you can get the hang of it, then I think that it is worth it. As with C3 and Stoi the down time in between fights is minimal.

Oh yeah, by the way, reslow half way through...or keep a timer.

I don't know whether what I'm saying here is a bit stupid as loads of people have soloed them at 54 before, but I do know that I've had major problems in the past soloing at all in the places (other than PoJ). And now I've found a way I'm quite pleased:)
