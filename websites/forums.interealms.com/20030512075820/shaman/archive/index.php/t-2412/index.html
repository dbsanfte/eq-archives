Zyphyr
They do work from inventory. Anyone can use them from inventory. You will NOT regain substantial amounts of mana from it.

The mana returned from Reclaim Energy is 1 point per 10 HP the pet has at the moment you reclaim it.

There is no Shaman pet that will return enough mana to break even on the casting cost (even with a Summoning Efficiency III item), let alone come out ahead.

Consider it to be a way to recover a little bit of the mana you spent when it comes time for you to log out or otherwise move on.
