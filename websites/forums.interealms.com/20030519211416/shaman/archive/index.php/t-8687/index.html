Tallanas
I tried a comparison of Scarlet Desert and Echo Caverns last night. I am level 42, fairly nice gear, and had KEI.

Echo Caverns - spawns either too slow (corridor), too many (back room, and Fireclaw is a pain) or too camped (TM). Mobs are generally very soft, and worth a little over 1% per kill at 42.

Scarlet Desert - spawns are plentiful, you can single pull a variety of creatures. But they are MUCH tougher, take longer to kill (more mana), and do more damage by far. And they are still only worth a little over 1% per kill at 42. However, I imagine that this stays as good xp all the way up to 50 and maybe beyond. No loot though!

Very annoying that two recommended zones both have such flaws...
