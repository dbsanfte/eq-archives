These two guys are King Tormax's personal bodyguards in Kael Drakkal. I have been working on trying to break Tormax's throne room with single group of fellow crazies on Quellious (while Tormax is dead of course).

We have done pretty darn well in our last two attempts. We took out Dlammaz, Kyenka, Yetarr, and all of the Sentinels leading up to Tormax's bodyguards.

All of these giants were fairly easy to slow using Malo/sini, and then Turgur's.

However, Vkjen Thunderslayer & Irrek Bloodfist have been our undoing. I had no luck landing malosini or Turgur's even after both Tash and Malo were in place on these guys.

I checked the "slowable and unslowable mobs" list, but these two are not on it.

Can any shaman with experience raiding Tormax comment on these two? Are they able to be slowed, or should they really be considered 100% MR?
Chutoi
They are slowable, but it can be a pain to land. If you have a bard (especially one with AA points in instrument mastery), it can help a lot. Get Tash, Malo, Occlusion of Sound on there, and you can probably slow them pretty easily.

Good luck.
