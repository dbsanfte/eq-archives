Non-Uber 50s gear;

Get into Kael Arena raids for main armor slot replacements; better and just as easy to get normally as Hate armor.
Other suggestions;
Jaundice Bone can be bought or gotten in drops from KC & Seb for Head, Arms, Feet and Hands rather easily.

Shield; SBS - get groups in Chardok, plus it's good XP

Neck; Ivory Imbued Collar - again, Chardok.

Belt; Fear - one raid tends to get you the Toad belt

Legs; Jarsath Scale - no drop from KC, usually rot. Down in the basement area. Xp camp

Shoulders; work on that shawl quest more, or get a Ranger and a couple friends and head to Trak's Teeth. Pull the Hunter/Foragers until you get a Meditative Blanket. Xp, but a bit slow as the puller has to find the mobs.

Upgrade Rings to Velium Wedding Rings. One day of HGs will give you the plat easily to buy them.

Back & Face - Artic Wyvern Hide; not too bad stuff for those slots. Camp Artic Wyverns in CS to collect some hides to lower pirce. Again, XP!
Bigguss
Neck: Another option is the Shrouded Medallion (AC 12, +25 Mana, +15 vs Magic, +5 vs Fire, +5 vs Cold, +5 Str) - drops in Maiden's Eye off Xi Xiau's- it's NO DROP, so you'll have to camp it, but it's fairly common.

Neck (again): You might want to think about a BA Medallion. Stats aren't bad (ac 6, +5 wis, +5 int.) You'll also get "Mana preservation II", which will cause all spells you cast under level 45 to cost 10% less. This should also be pretty reasonably priced (few hundred PP, about the same as your chryso talisman.)

Face: Ditch the velium ruby veil and get an Arctic Wyvern Mask (AC 6, +5 Wis, +6 Cha, +3 vs Cold) - you might even make a few plat on this deal. You get a nice balance of AC and WIS with the mask.

Head: Replace the ry-gorr with a JB helm. Cheap and great AC.

Feet: JB boots.

Wrist: Swap out one of your bracers for a CBB (chipped bone bracelet). Same stats as the RMB (minus the 4 str) and you get "Affliction Efficiency II" (25% less mana to cast any level 44 or lower DoT - I still use it for stacking VoS on top of EBolt.) If you swap out the RMB, you should even make a bit of cash on the swap.

Shield: Replace your charred guardian shield with a Clay Guardian Shield . You can probably break even on this one. You'll lose 9 WIS, but gain 15 raw AC and 50 HP - this is a good trade. A general ratio for deciding on equipment is 1 WIS = 1 AC = 10 HP- according to this the char guar is 19 points, the clay guar = 30 points.

All of these changes shouldn't set you back much, and will give you a nice increase in stats. :)

Good hunting!

When trying to upgrade gear do this:

Look at your gear, pick out what you have that is best, what is average, and what blows. Work on getting good gear for your slots that blow, will be the best way to improve your stats overall.
