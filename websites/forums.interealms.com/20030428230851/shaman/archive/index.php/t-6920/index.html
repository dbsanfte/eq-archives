Nytmare
Originally posted by Haleice
Does anyone know if Pop lets' you get alchemy over 200 now (without leveling to 64/65) as they said that they were raising the caps on all trade skills to allow you to Grand Master in more than one.

No. Alchemy is level capped and is a race/class specific trade skill. Grandmaster refers to the 7 general trade skills -- baking, brewing, fletching, jewelcraft, pottery, smithing, tailoring. In the Oct 16 patch, a new ability called "New Tanaan Crafting Mastery" was added to AA general abilities that can be trained only 6 times, which is what allows you to grandmaster more than one of the seven tradeskills.

Alchemy is separate, and should now be trainable to 205 at level 65 (if there are any new potions with high enough trivials).
