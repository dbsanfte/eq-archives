Rains are considerably good... especially since in tier 2 it is very risky to slow without malosini... since the malosini is used for the slow and/or cripple, I don't consider that as mana wasted for rain dpm.

Three resists, 450 mana for 0 dmg is 0 dpm (rare)
Two resists, 450 mana for 650 dmg is 1.44 dpm (uncommon)
One resist (or two resist one crit), 450 mana for 1300 dmg is 2.89 dpm (usual)
No resists (or one resist one crit), 450 mana for 1950 dmg is 4.33 dpm (usual)
One crit no resists, 450 mana for 2600 dmg is 5.78dpm (uncommon)
Two crits no resists, 450 mana for 3250 dmg is 7.22dpm (rare)
Three crits no resists, 450 mana for 3900 dmg is 8.67dpm (incredibly rare)
