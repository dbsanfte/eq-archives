South Karana should be pretty good at 24th. The centaurs, aviaks, and gnolls all drop pretty reasonable loot and should be high blue - yellow. My 24Sk solos there fairly easily and his best twink item is a blackened alloy bastard sword.

If you find SK to be too difficult, then Lake Rathe is a bit easier and is right next door. NK is also right there with some slightly lower level stuff.
Andrewe
Once paludal caverns greens out, there are a number of options in luclin very near by.

- Neth Lair -- anything blue to your monk friend would be fine (probably Netherbians). I don't really recommend you soloing in there (Trogs/Fungoids) because the regen often keeps pace with our DOTs, meaning you have to mix in some nukes.

- Marus Seru -- easy soloing for you here on rockhoppers. Would probably be fine to duo as well (on rockhoppers, possibly stonegrabbers).

- Dawnshroud Peaks -- better when you both get some levels under your belt, but Zelinaks here will be good once your monk friend gets to about 35 I think.


I'd check out Neth Lair and Marus Seru, then think about trying old world hunting spots if those don't work out. (Exp at these levels seems insanely fast on Luclin however, even after being spoiled by the exp in Paludal).
