If you're serious about going all the way to 8, you are going to need dire wolf whiskers for #6. And these things can be really really rare (streaky RNG).

I'd suggest farming GD dire wolves until you get 4 whiskers. At the same time, save giant heads and toes (since you'll need them for shawls 1 and 2). Once you have your 4 whiskers, see where your faction is. Odds are you'll be non-KOS. It will take that many wolves if you're as unlucky as I am.

And dogdog can tear through the wolves -- load a low level nuke to pull and let him tank away. Can give him a couple of those velium weapons the giants have for added dps. :)

If you are still KOS, then the arena is a good option. Another option is to buy up a bunch of giant helms and turn them in for COV faction bumps in Skyshrine. If you kill a few dorfs in ThurgB first and get the amulet with faction modifier first, you may be able to get high enough to do turn ins. If not, get a chanter friend to go with you, pull him to the zone and root -- and zone out. Have your chanter friend pacify him, now you can stand just inside turn-in range without him attacking. Give him 4 helms but don't click yet -- have the chanter invis you first, now click and you should get the 4 faction hits. Helms are a nice bump, a couple backpacks full should be enough to get you to dubious.
