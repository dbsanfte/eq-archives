Kryscha
You *can* solo Va'Dyn at 65, but it's a pain in the butt. Really not worth the hassle. If you want to single pull him, get a 62+ bard to help you out. Pre-62 bard won't be able pacify the guards. (I hope I'm remembering my song levels correctly) You still need to clear some junky rock guys, but other than that he's pretty easy. Super easy to magic slow, but he's got a very high PR/DR; don't even bother trying to dot him, just frost nuke and magic dots.

Definately recommend Rhag1/2 farming as they're easy, can drop RGC (saves some adventure points) and may offer some upgrades for your members. I was going to suggest Xerkizh the Creator, but I don't think he's doable with your current regular makeup. You'd need more healers to keep your tank alive, and a dedicted GRC spammer, not to mention 2 offtanks which need healing. A single shaman can keep slow on the two guards + XtC as well as healing the two off-tanks, (assuming you use the off-tank method) but you still need probably 2 more clerics, and maybe another shaman/druid for GRC.

I don't know if you're only looking for easy sources, or will consider challenging targets as well, so could be worth a try nonetheless; you know your crew better than I. However, be prepared for a 30min - 1 hour snooze-fest. You might also consider Thought Horror Overfiend (THO). Only need like 2 clerics to keep a tank up (we used to have a single cleric holding warrior up, with everyone else just nuking etc) but he is a very boring fight as well, and you're time limited by repops within an hour of 45 min or something. (been a while, memory fading)
