Here's a screenshot of what I've done with my UI, and using a bit of other people's graphics and code here and there as well for the timer and compass, etc.

Anyway, I wanted 800x600 so I don't have to move the mouse acres across the screen to click on stuff.

I put my pet in his own full bar under my health bar, and made stamina a small khaki-ish sub-bar of my HP bar in the playerwindow.

My group window I made the bars for players full height and added a nearly full height pet bar right below each group member's bar so I can click and target them or their pets easier, plus the F# keys beside name, etc.

In my target window, since I solo a lot, I wanted to have one place to look while solo'ing to show me all "critical" info in one spot, so I put my HP, my mana, and my pet's HP all stacked neatly with % indicators beside them right in the target window just to the right of my target's health.
