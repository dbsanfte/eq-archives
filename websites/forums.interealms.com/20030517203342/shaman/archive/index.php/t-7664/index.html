Well, the biggest easy to get hitpoint upgrade I see is a pair of velium fire opal rings. That would add 130 hitpoints.

Also:

Neck - ivory collar from Chardok

shoulders - obulus death shroud

Naagruz
It really depends on the amount of cash you have to throw around, but playing conservative and adding to Glori's suggestions I would go with:

Shoulders: Tattered Mantle, Chodkai Hide Spaulders, Giant Scalemail
Rings: Jade Velium, Coldain quest, Golem Tear
Head: Kylong, Totemic
Ears: Electrum black sapphire
Boots: Iksar Hide, Tribal War
Arms: Giant Scalemail

Just a few ideas to give you a bit more mana/hp. JB armor is really something I would try to limit to a single slot if possible because it is pure AC and nothing else. There are quite a few items from Luclin that might be cheap and fit the bill as well so search that Bazaar! :)

Feet: Black fur boots (epic quest stage 2)

Shield: Crystal Chitin (less AC than SBS, but good wis / mana)

Range: Howling harpoon (ridiculously cheep for like 6wis 40mana in range), Ceremonial smudging stick (easy camp in hollowshade, but long).

Edit: P.S. You say you are going OOM in groups, it is probably not a "wisdom" thing per se - your wisdom is quite ok for your level - you are probably trying to do too much. If all you do is slow mobs, haste and altuna tanks and regen / tuna self, you are doing your job - anything else is icing on the cake. Remember, in a group, just because shaman can do everything, doesn't mean you must do everything!
