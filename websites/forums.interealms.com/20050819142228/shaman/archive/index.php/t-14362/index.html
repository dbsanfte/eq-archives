If you want plat, farming greens is not the best way to go about doing it. Sure, it's safe, consistent, steady... slow...

Go fight in dungeons for experience. I'm not 60+, I've only got a 53 shaman and a 57 ranger, so I go to Karnor's and camp the WL/jail area, which is still good xp for me duoing. I can clear the jail + WL room + wanderers before respawn with that duo. A good day will produce a monk epic pipe MQ and a t-staff. There's 20-25k or so, via the bazaar. And in that time I could earn a good yellow or 2 of AA or regular xp.

I also have a 55 wizard that I take to Cobalt Scar and quad with to make cash. It's not near as "hit-and-miss" as fighting in dungeons and winning phat lewt, but it's consistent. The wyverns drop velious gems and hides, all of which sell for 50pp... gems 50pp to vendors (with high charisma), and hides 50pp in bazaar. A 2-3 hour session of quadding (assuming the place isn't flooded with dr00ds) can produce 1-2k pp at the end of the night. The wyvern hides sell like hotcakes to tradeskillers in the bazaar... I'm not kidding --- within 15 mins of putting up yer wyvern hides for 50pp each, they're sold in bulk to someone. I can usually get 1 stack of hides in a 2-3 session. That's 1k. Mix in another 20 gems or so, and there's another 1k. Of course, the hard part is raising up a wizard to be 50+ so he can quad in CS... but I don't regret at all the time I spent on my wizard, especially when he's able to fund my cause like that.

The money starts to flow the higher level you get and the tougher things you are able to take on. Go to the Hole if yer in yer 50s and fight down in the city... go to KC and grab a good camp... go to Seb and grab a good camp...

Also, if you have 2 accounts and are able to duo these places, it makes it even better since you are guaranteed to get any loot that drops. ;) Cash loot, too.
