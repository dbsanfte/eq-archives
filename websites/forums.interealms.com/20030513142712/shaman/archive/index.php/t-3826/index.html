Shh, secret: at the temple, go up the interior ramp to the top and jump onto the flag, then jump onto the wall. Large races may find lesser bear form helpful for standing on the flag. It takes a bit of practice, requiring some patience and coordination for a large party. Once you're all up on the wall, you have an unbarred path into Ghiosk's castle.
