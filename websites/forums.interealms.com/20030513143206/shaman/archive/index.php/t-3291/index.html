Qeynos Sewers...all races can hunt here (there is a secret entrance in the wall near qeynos gates) but probably best suited to Barbarians as they can buy / sell nearby.

At level 7 kill thugs...can probably do these a little earlier if you have decent equipment. When Thugs go green move onto smugglers...from memory these are good til about 14 or so. After that you can kill the sharks at the shark pit...these stay blue til about 17 or so.

Best of all there is an experience modifier for this zone :)
