Thorzion
You've got a few days; run some adventures and try and get every resist aug that you can. THere is usually one or two on every adventure. Resists are key in PVP. You cant do as much damage as quickly as some classes. A shamans strength is his ability to last. You said you have Torpor; that puts your survivability near the top of the list.

You say its a free for all, so take out any INT caster that you can first. Let the melees worry about the other melee's for a bit; your main concern is any class that can take large chunks of your health away at one time. Make a pet and get some mage weaons for it. Interupting the casters with procs is important.

Warriors, aside from having a large number if starting HP, arent as hard as you might think. Aside from potions or item effects they have no way to heal any damage done to them. Paladins and shadowknights, asside from being fearsome damage dealers through melee and spells, have the ability to heal themselves. Carefull.

And GL. Staying alive is the most important, not your body count at the end.
