Bigguss
I'd say you're doing pretty well! I agree with Hen that the JBB is your next big upgrade- it will increase your power more than anything else at this stage. You could probably get one (with cash left over) by trading away the JB greaves, if you needed to. The greaves are nice (I dream of them myself) but won't provide nearly the power the bracer will.

I'll take a quick crack at upgrades by price:

I'd upgrade the shoulders to a Tattered mantle (ac 12, +20 hp, +10 vs disease) - couple hundred plat tops, dropped in Burning Woods. If you get lucky, you might find someone selling one of the lesser dragon mantles (11 ac, +25 hp, +25 mana) for cheap

Clay guardian shield (ac 25, +50 hp) - these can often be found for a couple hundred pp, and are a great bargain for the money. 25 ac + 50 hp = good !!

Goranga idol (ac 8, +25 hp, +3 sta, +4 dex, 500 pp?) - nice range slot alternative that provides AC and HP. I use it instead of the harpoon when soloing.

AC 6 / 65 HP rings (400-500 pp each) - outstanding value for the money.

Those are the cheapie upgrades... next ones are gonna be more pricey:

Sarnak Battle Shield (ac 25, +40 hp, +10 fire/cold/magic, +5 sta, +5 str) - around 2-3k- nice upgrade to the clay guardian shield... similar ac/hp, and resists.

Ancient seahorse hide cloak (ac 20, +25 hp) - around 3-5k on Karana.

Crystal chitin gauntlets (ac 20, +10 str, +35 mana) - again, around 3-5k... very nice step up from the JB gloves.

Shadel bandit ring (ac 6, +55 hp, +55 mana, +10 vs disease, +5 vs cold) - prices vary widely. I've yet to have anyone offer to sell me one for under 3k, though.

From here, it just gets more expensive...

The Bracer - 10-15k- this will make a HUGE difference

Bracer of sense (ac 12, +15 wis, +6 to all saves) - currently around 10k on Karana, but prices are falling.

Bone Braced Buckler (ac 25, +10 wis, +55 hp, +55 mana, +10 str) - 10-15k

Mask of War (ac 9, +9 wis, +5 sta, +2 str, right-click Yaulp 2) - 10-15k

Hope this helps. :)
