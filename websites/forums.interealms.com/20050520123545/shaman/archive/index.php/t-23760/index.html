After being used to watching that AA XP bar fill up so fast these past months, recverting back to full time regular XP is a semi-rude awakening. But its doable if you ignore the XP bar and focus on fun and adventure.

To answer your question directly, yes, you can get a group of casual friends and get some spells. WoS offers a DL style zone with lots of nooks along the walls to pull to, and named spawns in the yard-trash areas that drop runes for 66, 67 and even 68 spells, as well as solid, VT quality gear. Deeper WoS offers more spells, and near-EP quality gear, and excellent XP.
