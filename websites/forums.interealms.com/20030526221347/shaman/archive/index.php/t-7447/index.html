"You begin casting Tirgir's insects"
"<X> Resisted"
"<X> Yawns"

"<X> Resisted"

Get ready to root or recast Tirgir's fast, alternatively ask for a heal early and then recast Tirgir's to help save the healer long enough for you to heal them....
At 60+ Pre-torporing before casting Tirgir's can help in tough spots (like PoJ trials).

I am looking forward to being a higher level soon and getting even less resists.

On trials (or elsewhere), if you've maxed evocation, let rip an AE rain just after the AE slow :)
