Over a month ago, VI added new mobs and quests in a few newbie zones (true newbies, lv. 1-15---not the Luclin twilight armor).

Examples--In Misty Thicket there are many new mobs (Drakelings, Fruit Bats, Undead Halflings, etc.) for Cleric, Ranger, Paladin, Rogue, and Warrior armor. The stuff is class and race specific. In Nektulos, new orc camps dropping pieces of stuff for newbie dark elf armor. Same thing in East Commons, lots of new newbie drops. Quests geared for lv. 1-15 or so. Decent AC for those levels and has stats (str, wis, sta, etc). These quests similar to the Vah Shir newbie armor quests that were released with Luclin.

That said, they did NOT revamp all the newbie zones. I have heard that Barbarians, trolls and ogres got nothing (future patch?).
