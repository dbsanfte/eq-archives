I have a Innoruuk worshipping Troll Shaman. Here are my findings on faction work:

56 Explorer Knives (14 faction hits)
Legion of Cabilis - from threatening to kindly
Scaled Mystics - scowls to threatening
Crusaders - scowls to scowls
Swifttails - scowls to threatening
Residents - scowls to scowls

900 Bone chips (225 faction hits)
Legion of Cabilis - kindly to warmly
Scaled Mystics - threatening to dubious
Crusaders - scowls to threatening
Swifttails - threatening to dubious
Residents - scowls to scowls

32 Explorer Knives (8 faction hits)
Legion of Cabilis - warmly to warmly (max)
Scaled Mystics - dubious to dubious
Crusaders - threatening to dubious
Swifttails - dubious to dubious
Residents - scowls to scowls

208 Scaled Curskins (28 faction hits with Residents)
Residents - scowls to scowls

1 Penance Quest and 4 Cudgel Quests
Scaled Mystics - dubious to apprehensive

I did the knives first. It took only a couple of bone chip turn-ins to get to warmly with the Legion. Another interesting thing was that I hit dubious with the mystics after ~520 bone chips (130 faction hits), yet I was still scowls with the crusaders. I didn't get to threatening with the crusaders till ~800 bone chips.

Residents which include the bankers are a pain in the rear.

I went to apprehensive with the Mystics after the Penance quest before I had done any Cudgel quest turn-ins.

So far I have attained my Iron Cudge of the Prophet. I have had to use Essence of Iksar Potions three times. The first time I had the skulls for the first two quests and the doll from the hextracer for the third quest before starting the turn in.
