Jaruden
Supple black gloves drop off Wrank in torg mines. He's the boss guy of the zone. First you have to kill 20 guards and get keys (they don't all drop keys). Then you have to find 20 resistance miners to give the keys to (there are on average 2-3 up at a time, 10-15 minute respawn'ish). Figure about 6 hours to get the keys and turn them in. Killing other slaves can help speed up the resistance miner spawn process a bit.

Once you've turned in 20 keys, the overseer spawns. There are 6 guards in his room, but you can pacify it all to clear them easy. Overseer himself hits for 350's I believe, has a 500 dmg fire AE.

IIRC he drops 2/3 of his loots every time you kill him. I got the gloves, our cleric got the ring (70hp/mana, 10 sta/wis) when we killed him. The loot is pretty nice, but a ridiculous amount of time to spawn the guy.

So uh, no, you can't solo it =)
