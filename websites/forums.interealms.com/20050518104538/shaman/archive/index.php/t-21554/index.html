Yes, you can buy all 9 items for the earring. On my server people auction the package of items for about 5-6k. Thats usually about what you can buy them for individually if you shop around. If you're patient sometimes tradeskillers will dump a bunch of sickles or robes on the market just to get rid of them.

The flowing thought effect works at any level, but the stats will be reduced until you reach the recommended level. Every bit of flowing thought helps, though, and the stats are a bonus.
