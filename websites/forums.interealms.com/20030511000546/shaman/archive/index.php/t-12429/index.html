Without PoP getting to Nexus is a challange, but not impossible.

I've done that run many times with my iksar characters and never had a problem. Running through the FV zone is (imo) a far more dangerous proposition, as the spiders tend to hang around on the zone walls more than any other mob I know of. (haha.. get it? hang around?)

However, if you want to go this route DO NOT EVER hunt in crushbone, as it will just make raising the DE factions that much harder.
