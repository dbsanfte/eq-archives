I think it's pretty much held on it's own merits. The people above who hate it probably won't pay any attention to it. The people who do may or may not get a slightly skewed view of someone's rep by the dots. It is handy to give a rough idea on someone's previous contributions (or lack thereof) though. The biggest improvement I could suggest is to echo the removal of anonimity. At least if someone was being an ass you'd know a) who it was, and b) could ask an admin to intervene if it mattered to you, but in reality it would probably just make people think twice about doing so.

For the newcomer, it's handy to see who's thrown out good stuff in the past and who hasn't, it kinda gives more value to what they're saying. That's the only downside with the 'traditional' way of building your rep - you don't get that instant value appraisal. That said, it doesn't take too long to see who's who and which end they're speaking out of, so maybe we wouldn't miss it greatly. I just think as it's in and functional it would be more productive to try and improve it rather than just bin it. *shrugs*
