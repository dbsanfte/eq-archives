Well, here was my strategy the two times I've killed him... luckily there was never anyone in the zone, so I was able to pull him to the zoneline for safety.

Ranger pulled with ensnare, pulled him to the zone. Necro slapped a couple dots on him, but he resisted some of them or else the fight would have been much shorter. Ranger fought with my necro's pet a bit until he was low health, backed him off while my necro healed him a bit and tapped Dire to death.

I had to zone the first time I tried it, and my necro ended up zoning with low hp and got dropped in the middle of Lfay with no buffs... and by the time I got him back and buffed almost ten minutes later, Dire was still sitting ensnared at the zone, with only about ten-fifteen percent health regenerated. So, you could always pull him to the zone snared (so he won't move if not aggroed), then zone out while you let dots rot him... just make sure you make it back in before he dies or snare breaks. His regen is very slow, and it's a way to do it without needing a melee/cleric.

The second time I just had my necro FD-rot him since it was a lot easier than using melees. It felt cheap, but it was very easy.
