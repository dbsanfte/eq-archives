snapdragn
Overall advice: Dump all your other chars and just play the troll shaman to 60 :) Then you will have the thousands of plat necessary for tradeskills, plus any gear you might want.

Specific advice:
If all you need is the Imbue Jade spell - geez, just send a few /tells to the ogres on your server. I'm positivly pleased when I get a chance to help someone out with that spell.

On a(nother) shaman:
Make sure your DEX and AGI are both above 75. I don't know how much money you have for twinking (especially if your highest char is 45 AND you are spending money on tradeskills), but you can make up for you stats either way.

If you dump all your points into Wis, a single ac 6/65 hp ring will get you more Hps at the lower levels than the 20 odd Sta pts.

If you dump all your points into Sta, a +35 mana/+25 hp earring will make up the difference in Wis. BUT - your skills will suffer greatly. The dumber you are, the harder it is to gain skill levels.

You should remember that Alchemy has a level based cap. I would suggest having your Troll make your potions.

Final advice: You've already got a troll to 45, you should know how YOU like to play your Shamans :D
