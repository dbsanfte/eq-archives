Personally I wouldn't work on Critical Affliction for a long time yet, you have other stuff that I think would suit you better. Level 1 fired (from memory) very infrequently. Maybe one or two ticks would crit on a Tactics mob when soloing with CritAff1. The percentage of a crit increases dramatically with CritAffliction3. If you aren't getting the other levels soon, I would wait if I were you.

If you are still tanking quite a bit, finish CS&ID.
Also, you are losing out on potential free mana and hitpoints any time you have Boar & KEI (or better) on because your stats are capped at 280. A few levels of Planar Power would give you immediate (though small) boosts in total hp/mana when grouped. I'm also a big fan of Suspend Minion2, but if you don't solo or raid much, it might not matter to you.

There are still lots of good AA's for you to work on before picking Critical Affliction in my opinion. But at this stage there are no real clear 'must have AA's' the way the first hundred went, as playstyle and opinion can vary substantially.

Take care,
