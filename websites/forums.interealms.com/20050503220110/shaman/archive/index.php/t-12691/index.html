I really missed this level of shamanhood. I gotta say though, i really miss my aggro. In high levels when i want aggro for a offtank its not hard to do at all, at this level i need to Slow and stack DoTs then sit and if im lucky ill get aggro and be able to stand and whack on it a few times to convince it to stay on me.

Its amazing to me how much KEI has changed the game at this level. Its a curse and a godsend all in one. On one hand you have a large part of people that just blow though the levels and never group with you till 55+ when PoP comes into play, which makes grouping hard at this level ( curse ), then you have the ability to change the one thing in EQ everyone hated ( downtime ) for a relitivly cheap cost. Its kinda intresting how KEI worked out like that, how everyone went to DAoC and came back to EQ saying " *** is up with EQ's downtime". Was kind of a fix for the issue.

Anyway, on my 3rd shaman i get my puppy again and enter into the world where the shaman becomes truly fun to play. Frogs dont make bad lil shaman i must say.


So whats this post ? A open rejoicement of the wonderful class that i love above all others....
