Jaruden
You cannot negate a mobs regen, that's an ancient myth due to the fact that players regen 2hp/tick and rabies did 2hp/tick damage, so a lot of people just assumed it stopped your regen altogether.

No spells stop mob regen, if they regen 100/tick, you'd better stack enough dots to do more than 100/tick.
