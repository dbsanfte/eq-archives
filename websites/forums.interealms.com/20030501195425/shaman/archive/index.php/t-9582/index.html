First of all, conversation does not have to occur immediately following a turn-in. You can converse days or years after the turn-in, as long as you know what to say.

Secondly, conversion is usually just background info and therefore completely optional. Exceptions are when it's necessary as a trigger to receive an item or start a script. Since you don't need any item from the Sentinel before handing in Black Dire's pelt, no conversation is necessary here.
