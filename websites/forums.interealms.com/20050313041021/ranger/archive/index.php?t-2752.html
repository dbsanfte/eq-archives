Now, I've heard a lot of stuff about how Kendrick favored rogues, etc etc... but I'm not so sure I believe any of it anymore. Last night I was leading a Sleeper's Tomb raid (-kendrick zone-) and contemplating whether or not to buy a Salindrite dagger should one drop. (I did... a small mushroom is struck by a thunderbolt, arrrrr)

But that got me thinking, here 5 months after Luclin's release I'm still using mainly ST weaponry, it was truly a great zone for rangers. Primal, priceless, great bows, and SoDs/Salindrites. Furthermore on the warders... they are ripe candidates for archery, very low AC and you can sit out of range easily and plink away. And heck, even for the "non uber", most of the better bows in the game come from Dragon Necropolis (-kendrick zone-).

ST is one of the few zones, as was DN, that paid attention to bows and from the get go included rangers on almost all the good drops. Furthermore the design of the warders and the AC in general in this zone lends itself very well to archery. NToV? Our Vulak weapon blows, 15/21? Sad... and the bows all suck. AoW? Warbow of Rallos Zek sucks. Ssra? Up until the reitemization, this was pretty sad too.

It's just too bad freeing the sleeper makes this zone mostly worthless and unused, heh, as it is on most servers. That wasn't such a hot idea, but as a whole, the zone is truly a great one for rangers.
