Camps in Katta include:

1. Back of bank, pulling both tunnels
2. Front of bank ("Courtyard" on The Tribunal), pulling 6 statics in courtyard and the guard up the first stairs.
A well equipped shaman will be bored doing just the courtyard, but will not really keep up with both tunnels and the courtyard, so on my server it is usually treated as two camps. A 60 monk/necro/mage can do both of these.

3. Nathyn's guards (two sonic wolf guards - no negative faction to Katta)
4. Twilight Sea sone in - 6 statics and 2 roamers, easy to split the statics.
5. Tunnels - zone in from twilight and go right, follow walkway to first tunnel. pull on line of sight - if only one guard can see you, only that one will come. a roamer, 5 static, and another roamer from zone in if no one is killing them.
6. barracks - or "the other tunnels" go left from twilight zone in.

7. graveyard\gargoyles. the gargs are higher than the guards, and much more resistant to poison and disease. better for level 60, but not necessarily for shaman.

There are plenty of guards on the upper level (around zone in from tenebrous). I have seen wizzies quadding up there. Not usually called as a camp on my server.

Go - look around (nothing sees through invis).
