Csin Csity
Hs skulls mostly drop in the south wing. You will need a rogue to open the door.

The mobs in the south wing all HT, and they can swarm up on you in groups of 4 and 5 if you're not careful.

I suggest getting a chanter/bard and a rogue for this endeavor.

As an alternative you can solo the entrance and basement a while (or group), and simply wait until an AOE or fully equipped group comes thru and pick up skulls off their kills.

It is POSSIBLE to complete each of the previous sections, get the key, and proceed to the next section until you are in the south wing. This would take a dedicated group because the key mobs aren't always up and may need to be spawned. With proper crowd control it should be a fun afternoon though.

Good luck
