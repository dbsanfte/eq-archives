Dunwut
Ok just going by memory here are some good upgrades for you

Fingers - Dump those rings! Get some velium fire wedding rings or shadel bandit rings. VFR rings go for around 400 each I think. Shadel a bit more at 1k-2k each.

Neck - Ivory Imbued Collar would help ya here. Should be cheap now.

Hands - Jaundiced bone gauntlets are a cheap upgrade

Shoulders - A tattered mantle would do ya well here. Or if you worried about losing wis get a chokidai hide spaulders.

Belt - A chitinous belt give great ac and around the same wis. Got one last night for 800.

Legs - Gatorscale is for druids. :D Get ya some brown chitinous legs.

Feet - Several options for ac and hp for feet. I keep my fur boots in inventory and hotkey em for use.

Shield - If ya got the plats get a Crystal Chitin Shield or Skull Marked Shield. Both around 1500 and good upgrade on ac without too much loss in wis.

Chest - Barbed ringmail may be an option.

Hope this helps ya.
