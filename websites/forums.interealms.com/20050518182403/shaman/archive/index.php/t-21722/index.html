One of the things that all casters and mellee have had a problem with is how should spells be distributed. Little recap of how things have been done so far.

Original EQ
Priest could buy their spells, all of them.
Pure casters could buy all of their spells up to lvl 16 or so, then could only buy certian ones and had to make their others from drops.

Kunark
Some spells were sold, some were drops, and some you got in return for turning in others. Some spells were rare....ok thats not quite right, they were RARE! And spell quests for 4 or so classes.

Velious
Some spells were sold, most were drops, all were very common in comparison to Kunark spells Except for the Ancients which were beefed up versions of other spells for the more uber out there and were no drop. And a spell quest or two for wizards.

Luclin
Some spells were sold, most were drops. Some had rare drop rates, and some were in questionable places.

PoP
Some spells were sold, when pop came out the ones that were sold were no drop if I remember. Three tiers of quests for spells in which you hand in an item and got a random spell out of that tier, unsure if some were weighted more than others but for all we know was totally random.

LoY
Each class gets 2 spells, both spells require a quest to get.

LDoN
Purchase the spells with the gratitude you get from the wayfarers brotherhood. Included was a spell from luclin that it was felt more people needed.

Gates
Spells randomly drop off of named mobs and afaik rare. Spells are no drop.

I think we need to help SoE figure out a good way to distribute spells in the next expansion, there have been problems with all of the above ways. In some cases true hardcore legitimate problems, and in others well people just liked to complain.

A couple points I think that spells distribution should follow.

Spells that Make a class should be easy to obtian, if its a spell where if you don't have it you can't do your job and therefore your not going to get groups in that expansion well there should be a way for you to get it very simply.

If its a spell that is rather nice for you to have and is not 70% necassary then you should earn it, but also being able to buy it from other players is perfectly acceptable.

If its a slightly beefed up version of a spell thats out there and it really doesn't add all that much to you, but its really cool to have just to have it haveing it no drop and off of a tough mob sounds fine since 90% of the players aren't really going to care. The ancient spells from Velious is a good model for that. The ancient spells from gates IS NOT.

The above 3 points are just my opionion, whats yours?
