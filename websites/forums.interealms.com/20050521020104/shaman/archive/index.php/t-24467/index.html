Thorzion
Leadership takes 20% of the exp that you get from every kill. It doesnt matter if you have the AA meter set at 100% or at 0. You have to be the leader of the group to be able to gain leadership AA.

If you plan on doing leadership AA, I would suggest running either full AA or full exp when you do. The math gets funny when you are trying to subtract a % from reg exp and a % from AA, and you often times will lose exp (at least as far as Ive seen). So go all or nothing to AA when working on Leadership.
