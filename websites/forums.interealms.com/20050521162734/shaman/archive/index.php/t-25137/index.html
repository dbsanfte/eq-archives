Well other than Samanna's site and the AA listing there and the semi accurate listing at EQCastersrealm I dont really know of any such animal. I can, however, offer some good information on AA paths to take.
For your SK friend, for sure is defensive AA, defensive AA, defensive AA...sure its nice to have the Unholy Pony, but no one gives a rats ass about that if you tank like a wet noodle, HT crits are nice on raids for Boss mobs, but dont do a lot in groups. He should go for AT LEAST his Luclin defensives and Hp raisers before looking at anything else, then from there Weapon Affinity 1-2, Theft of Life 1, then from there crit AAs both nuke and melee based. Here is a link to the "official" SK.org thread on "Your first 100 AA"
Tanking SK AA path for first 100 AA's (http://shadowknight.org/forums/showthread.php?t=23691)

For a shaman, go straight for Cann 5, from there go ahead and finish up the buff extension AA's (SCM/SCRM), Spell Casting Mastery (flat reduction on spell costs), VP 2 at least (1 just doesnt push the mob far enough back), and Puppy Pack 1 (good to help snag adds sometimes, others for push when you need it)... also finishing up to LR5 is worth the time.
Lots of good stuff to look at after that, so many that its really hard to look back...once I hit 200 I really had gotten all the ones that I "wanted" and started screwing around with crit nukes, Rabid Bear, etc.
For a "cap" and just a last sidebar, I have to say skip regen 3 and get Metabolism 3....its much more return on your investment, especially if you snag some of your favorite foods :)
