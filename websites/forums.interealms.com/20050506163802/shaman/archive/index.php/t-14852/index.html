Kazim
You could make use of the memblur effect of Atone. Pull the mob with malo, let your pet beat it down to almost nothing. Root the mob and back the pet off. Then have the cleric cast atone until it sticks (if the mob is naturally non-KOS to the cleric, have the cleric get initial agro with the pet by tossing some gimpy level 1 nuke or tapping it with a hammer - you'll know atone stuck when the mob goes from KOS to non-kos). Then either finish it off with a hammer or a nuke. I figure if you find a dungeon with mobs a couple levels above the cleric and a good ZEM you'll get pretty decent xp per kill. Not sure if this is going to be faster than just grouping cleric with a melee and PL'ing using the shaman though.

I've found that grouping my cleric with a rogue and having the cleric "root tank" for the rogue also works quite well when the mob is pulled/slowed with an out of group shaman.

Oh yeah. I got my 2nd box cleric from 30 to 44 in nurga & droga (I wanted the faction hits anyhow, so seemed like a decent spot). Seemed to get about 1 level per few hours on the cleric.
