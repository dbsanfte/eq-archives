moved to pit . in the pit there are two tunnels out on the west wall . it is best to go to the south tunnel as the stampede event pathes through there . when soloing in those tunnels you will not have too much room , you will have to keep a timer ( a wanderer pathes by every 25 mins no matter what tunnel you are in ) , but you will have a chance at more spectrals and very nice loot ( random stampede event / stampeding pigglet [ spectrals + nice loot ..] , frenzied initiates [spectrals] , enraged boar [ bracer patterns / spectrals etc] ) coming from mobs you can pull from the pit .
to survive in the pit , first don't try to jump from the top down ( ok i had to try it the 1st time .... ) , even monks with safe fall maxxed don't make it down there . use levi . there is a zone out in the NE corner of pit if you have to make a mad rush . with Run speed 3 you should make it even without sow , just don't pass through mobs .
in pit use invis , most mobs don't see through , except the undeads ( war wraith , tormented souls ) and nameds ( enraged war boar ... ) .
best thing is to check around going invis , hunt for a named , wait for pulling path to clear and bring it to tunnel for some one on one fun .
