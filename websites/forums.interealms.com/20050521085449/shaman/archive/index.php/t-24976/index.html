Base spell granted automatically is apprentice I or apprentice II.

You can buy apprentice II from merchants in cities.

Apprentice III scroll, rune, or essence are made by crafters.

Adept Is are uncommon mob drops and are tradeable.

I hear adept IIs can be given as quest rewards. Never seen one.

Adept IIIs are made by crafters, but the components for these are incredibly rare.

Master I is a very rare drop.

Master II is not in game i believe.

Master III is an artifact and there will only be a few per server, if any.

You don't need to upgrade in sequence.
