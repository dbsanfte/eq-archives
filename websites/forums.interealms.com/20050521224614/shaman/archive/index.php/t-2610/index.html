Along the Damage Sheild vein of thought:

Traditional damage sheilds were cast on players, surrounding and protecting them.

Cleric damage sheilds became known as the "reverse damage sheilds" because they are cast on the mobs not the players. Mark of Karn heals a whole 8points per main hand attack on any player that attacks a mob with said DS on it. Mark of Retribution damages 15points, per successful attack of the mob on a target, on any mob with said DS on it.

MoK's true purpose is overwriting damage sheilds rather then actually healing people, though it does help rogues and monks. This is a wonderful tool when you really dont want to dispell that uber mob of all the debuffs, slow, dots everyone smacked on it. Of course these are both magic so there is quite a high resist rate. :(

I assume it is called a Reverse Damage sheild because the God would be casting it upon his foes, not himself.
