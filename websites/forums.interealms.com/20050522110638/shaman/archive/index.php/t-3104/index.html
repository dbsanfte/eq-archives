I have the following items:

Girdle of Magnificence - Affliction Haste 3
Coldain Military Wristband - Extended Affliction 3

Affliction Haste 3

The Girdle has cut down all my Dots casting time. Here are the results with Bane and Pox:

Pox was 5.0 sec cast time now 3.4 sec cast time
Bane was 5.0 sec cast time now 3.4 sec cast time

So the differnce looks to be about 5.0 x .32% or 32% not the 33% advertised but hey that looks like a rounding issue.

Extended Affliction 3

Pox unlike Plague is 18 ticks or 1min 48secs

I have timed at least 12 castings of this and the spell always runs to 2mins 6 secs. So I am getting 3 extra ticks for 333 dmg (3x111)

According to EQcaster stats should be an extra 20% duration. 18x.20=3.6ticks but looks like they aren't rounding up..rather down to 3 ticks.

Bane runs 7 ticks or 42secs. After about 8 trials I have gotten consistant 48sec runs. So an extra 214dmg (1x214)

So in a fight where I cast both Bane and Pox at least once (Katta Guards) I am doing an extra 547dmg. Not too shabby.

Where the extended affliction 3 really appears to shine as you can tell is with disease and longer duration dots, which makes sense.

I also tested Plague and got these results:

Was 2m 6secs (21 ticks) was getting 2m 30secs (24 ticks) so an extra 220dmg (55x4ticks).
