Sudedor
An excellent question.

One thing you have to keep in mind with LDoN gear is slots. Many of the nicer augs are very slot specific, both in type and location. LDoN purchased gear typically has a LOT more slots than dropped. Look at your current chest piece vs the Ghastly Chestplate. The stats and AC are almost exactly identical. The difference is the slots.

Drop a Regen 5 aug, a +2 to all saves or whatever else in the Ghastly Chestplate and compare again.

And the fact that you can now sell it back? It seems like a really good deal to me.
