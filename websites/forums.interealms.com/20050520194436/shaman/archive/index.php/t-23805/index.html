Macnair
The steamfont spring water have minor value in tradeskills, you can probably make a few plat on them in the bazaar.

If you want to level quickly, see if you can get a teammate and go to Crushbone till level 10, then go join a group in Paludal Caverns to level 22 or so.

To get better weapons and armor, kill spiderlings in Everfrost or Feerot and loot the spiderling silks. You won't get exp from the spiderlings and it gets boring pretty quick, but the silks sell well in the bazaar. Three or four hours of spiderling hunting will probably get you a few hundred plat worth of silk, depending on your server's economy. For 300 plat nowadays you can outfit yourself decently well.

Good luck.

Sell the Skins and the Teeth to vendors in Halas. Then attempt to sell the spring waters in the bazaar. If they dont sell, just sell them to a vendor.

Then you can start hunting spiderlings and saving their silks. Once you get a good stockpile of around 100 (will take awhile), you can go to the bazaar and sell them for some good money. That should be good to get all the spells you need for the upcoming levels.

You can use a spear (Piercing), or a hammer (1hblunt). It does not matter, use what has the best stats of what you can afford.

Always check the bazaar to see if people are selling an item you have. Some bazaar tips. Type /baz to bring up the general search window. If you see something you want to buy, highlight the item and click the button "Find Trader", it will illuminate a path to the vender. If your going to setup a trader you need to buy a traders satchel first, then stand under an overhang and type /trade.

Good luck!
