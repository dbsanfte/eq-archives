I came to CoM solo as a 45 shaman. With KEI, I was absolutely able to own the bridge area. Once you break the spawns it's cake.

I start by parking dogdog just short of the first two spawns at the entrance. Both will come when you pull so just let dogdog engage, root one and call dogdog back to you.

The next spawn area is a trio across the bridge. I found that if i rooted the the left one, only the center spawn would aggro (usually). From there, there are 2 spawns on both the left and right sides that you can grab one at a time.

With KEI, I was able to clear all 9 and still have time to pull an extra or two before they repopped. I'm currently 46, have all 6 drops for the book (did turn in), and will probably stay there until at least 49. Plus a couple of raids to get my other epic pieces. :)

steveo
I am currently hunting in CoM. I'm almost level 47 now and came here at 45.

There is normally very few people in the zone (most of the time I am the ONLY one). The exp is great and getting the epic items is easy as they drop frequently.

Most of the pulls here will net you at least 2 mobs, and sometimes 3 if there is full spawn and noone else in the zone. Most 2 pulls can be handled easy enough by rooting one and moving out of melee range while killing the second.

When pulling the trio past the gate (at the entrance) a good method is to cast root on one, then pull the other 2 back to the zone line. Root another one of them there and then zone out. When you zone back in only the rooted one will be there (or on his way back depending on your zone speed) and you can engage and kill him alone. You can repeat this for the duo pulls also if they are getting the best of you.

Takes a little longer this way, but its safe, and breaks the camp spawn up nicely so that you have enough time to kill one before the others spawn from then on.

As I said earlier, the exp is great. So CoM from 45 - 50 (or so) should definately be checked out if you havent been there.
