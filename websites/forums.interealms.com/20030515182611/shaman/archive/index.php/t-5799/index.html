I personally love COM. At 53 I usually start out my evening by running to Seb and making my presence known. After 15 - 20 minutes of LFG if I don't get a group I head to COM. Do to it's proximity to OS I find this to be ideal - has a wonderful list of bonuses in my opinion that make soloing here really easy - 1. decent ZEM 2. No Wanderers 3. Mobs don't run 4. Good amount of dark blue cons - though with the ZEM the light blues actually seem to affect my xp bar. And when soloing gets boring it's a short jaunt down to Seb to LFG.

The goos are dark blue 11 times out of 12 and can be single pulled with slow. I then aggro pull the house to the east (2 spawns in each wing) which are most of the time lt blue. The only time things get hairy is when I open the door to Lhrancs office - there are two spawns in here that have always been dark blue to me. This gives me 3 goos, 4 mobs out of the house and two out of the office. If the goo's still havn't popped by the time I finish these I can run out to the courtyard and find a lt blue to kill while I wait.

I plan to start looping around the area in front of the castle next, though I have found that I still can't single pull from the gazebos. But I've gotten pretty used to two. Overall though nice safe xp.
