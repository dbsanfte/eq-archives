Kbok
Because Verant hates us funloving Ogres.

From a game mechanics point of view, what does this do?
Discourages "evil" characters from hunting in Karnor's (which is
conviently located in a zone with both wizzard and druid ports)
and Howling Stones. We're "allowed" to hunt in either Chardok
or Seb.

Our Jaundiced Bone stuff drops in Karnor's and Seb. This
virtually forces us to hunt in Sebilus, where only half of our
Kunark gear drops. This forces us to trade with people who
are "allowed" to hunt in Karnor's. I guess this is an attempt to
stimulate the economy. Fortunately for me, I was outright given
the misssing jaundiced bone pieces.

Since it's such a long haul to get from Seb to Chardok, Seb
becomes the most desirable hunting grounds for "evil".
Chardok never took off on my server until they overhauled it,
but I suspect the idea was that "evil" was supposed to be
segregated into either Chardok or Seb. The non-gating "evils"
would probably stay in Chardok, or have to spend half a night
traveling to Seb.

As an Ogre, I could carry enough loot until I could group with a
porter who would take me back home to sell and bank. About
twice a week, and I'd just gate back to my Seb bind point. Don't
know what the weak dirt elfs and non-gaters did. Still, I did make
Seb my home. If I was a warrior or sk, I probably would have
quit over this.

This was a thorn in our sides, however with the introduction of
the port idols, it's a non-issue for us now. The new regieme at
Verant isn't so spiteful. Port idols, binder NPC's, horses, etc.
