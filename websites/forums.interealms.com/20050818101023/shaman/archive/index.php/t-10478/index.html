Alt enter to put EQ in a window. Then alt + shift + R and that will free your mouse to minimize EQ. Then open up something else like internet explorer. Press alt tab to switch back to EQ. The cursor should be gone. Alt tab back to internet, close it, this should bring u back to EQ. Alt Enter again and you're back to normal.
It's just a work-around. They should fix that bug imho.
