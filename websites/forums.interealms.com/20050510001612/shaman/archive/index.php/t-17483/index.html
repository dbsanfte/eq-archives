Perelandra
Pets who don't duel wield will equip MOST 2handed weapons. These weapons will only increase max hit damage of a pet IF the base damage of the weapon is 2x greater than the max hit of the pet.

For Mage pets, this is only convienient up until level 16 or 20. For shaman pets, I'm not sure this would ever be realistic.
