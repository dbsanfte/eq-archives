Not everyone wants to pay to look at stuff at Allakhazams as well. For me the quest has been easy to far; this is what I can recall:

1. Find DE near beastmaster trainer in Grobb, get Perrin Zexis note quest

2. Deliver note to Perrin in 3rd gate cleric guild

3.) not enough faction yet so kill halfings in nektulos and get four beads and as many leatherfoots caps as you can. It took me about 10-13 caps to get enough faction to do the quest.

4.) Turn caps into Perrin until faction lets you get the initiate symbol quest from him. Then turn in 4 beads for the necklace

5.) Go to Dran N'Ryt (spelling?) in the Neriak caster guild and get the halfling mask quest. Kill master whoopal in Nektulos at halfling shrine and take head back to N'Ryt for halfling illusion mask (save it you need it)

6.) Talk to Ithvol in cleric guild he sends you get get a note from wallin slyfoot in WC, use mask to talk to him and get note. I can't remember the exact phrase.

7.) Take note to Dran N'Ryt to spawn halfling spy in Neriak tunnels. Kill spy and turn head and initiate necklace to Ithvol for disciple necklace

8.) Tell Ithvol I will deliver provisions to get last part of quest

9.) See slug Rembor in Neriak Foreign quarter to get next part, say Ithvol sent me. He wants two steel bonings to make crate

10.) take crate to Dragoon Sszorn in LFay for reciept, he sends you to Steamfont to take to Ennixy (sp?) who gives a voucher and who spawns a paladin. Kill paladin. Take receipt, voucher and head and disciple symbol to ithvol for Regent necklace.

Double check all this at some of the online sites, but I am pretty close (recalling it all from memory, not notes). I have not finsihed the quest...waiting till 24 and new DoT before trying the Pally in steamfont. I also need to work smithing up to make bonings.

I finally got my necklace. I was 26 before I got around to going to Faydwer, but I am glad I waited. Swiftmoon conned yellow and was very had at 26 and I had to zone twice. The only assistance I had was a skin like steel from a druid hunting gnomes.

Fight 1: did not know where swiftmoon would spawn. Ran around like an idiot for 5 minutes until he arrived at ennixy. Started with malaise + root + drowsy + affliction. Root broke a lot. Finally got EB on him. He was hitting in the 52 range. At 25% health he did lay on hands. I had to zone.

Fight 2: repeat first fight after medding and buffing

Fight 3: Got SLS, started over. This time Swiftmoon did not lay on hands for some reason. I root rotted him to 30%, cannibalized half my health for mana then nuked him the rest of the way.

FWIW, the necklace is really handy. When grouped, I will typically use the necklace to snare if we lack a snarer or as a little extra mana free damage when I am main healer. I hardly ever use it solo since the cast time is 6 seconds, but being able to snare does help getting into some groups at my level.
