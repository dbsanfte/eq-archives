Sciek Spellbottler
Get flagged for BoT and xp there as much as possible in the upper wings. Named wing bosses drop runes. OR if you are Tactics flagged there are a few places like the kitchen that have named spawns that drop runes as well. No reason to farm gimpy mobs for cash to buy spells at all IMO. I know quite a few shaman that have all their runes and got them in xp groups.

As far as order to buy them in if you have to... If you are soloing a lot and want to increase your efficiency there, get BoS first since I think you said you have Torpor and Malo already. After that I'd go for Q and Malos. lastly Fo7 and FA.

Now if you group fairly often, Q is damn handy as it allows you to be sole healer in many spots without gimping your melees, and IMO FA is a noticeable boost for all those melees in groups.

It all kinda depends on your playstyle, but I'd be camping those wing bosses in BoT and/or Tactics to get the runes on your own while you're making xp to get to 65. Save ya a ton of cash at least. ;)
Landragoran Heronwing
Q is definitely number one priority. It will seriously change your life and make you a MUCH better healer. There's a night and day difference between Q-equipped shamans and Torpor-equipped shamans.

Second would be Fo7. It's a must have for raiding, and raiding is the name of the high-end game. Nothing says luvin like MGB Fo7!

Third is FA. No avatar is nearly as good as this spell, and it will make a lot of MAs and group melees very very happy. Which increases your reputation as a shaman. Which helps you get groups and raises people's idea of your power. So I rate this number 3.

4th is Malos. Having this for raids to more fully debuff difficult resistant mobs is kewl. However Malo will also work if the enchanter remembers to tash. I dunno.

BoSaryn: this spell is really nice and will greatly increase your soloing ability. However, the game is very little based on solo ability, and much more focus placed on grouping and raiding. Thus, this is the last spell you should shoot for.

Currently, I have Q, Fo7, and FA. Still need the last two.
