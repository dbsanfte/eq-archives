Barm McLir
I got in a argument with Wildaar last time this came up, but honestly, shrink should have an icon. Before you get upset please hear me out. I know the majority of players don't want this because of how precious the buff window is.

IMHO, the only reason shrink has no icon today is because it began life as a hack. I suspect that all the player models are the same height in their native file formats. So, when you shrunk a player, instead of occupying a limited duration buff slot it just changed the scaling factor within that character's temporary, online profile. Which would explain why shrink didn't wear off until you camped or zoned and why when someone used an illusion before you entered the zone you'd see high-elf sized gnome in the bank (this was fixed a while back). You were getting the updated model but the race's scaling value.

This is also why grow only lets you reach barbarian height. I'm not positive but there seems to be four valid character heights (as you can shrink dwarfs slightly).

They *should* re-work it to act like a spell and not a hack. Which includes giving it an icon and a duration. Then you could target anyone and they could just click if off. Or group. Or MGB. Yes, I know most of would consider it no longer worth the slot. Yes, I feel like the kid who reminds the teacher to collect homework. Yes, it would make the spell less appealling BUT more consistant with game mechanics. The only compromise I could suggest is adding additional buff space to the UI.

My opinion, grain of salt, standard disclaimers, etc.

-Barm
