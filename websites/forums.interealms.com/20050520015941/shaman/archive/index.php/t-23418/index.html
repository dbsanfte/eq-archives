Here's another DPS idea that's a new spell line that I thought might work to aliviate problems:

summonable pets that only last a predetermined number of minutes more than 3, but not more than 15. Each pet would be the spirit of a warder and have a differing function. Each of these "timed" pets would have a component of a meat part from that animal (most meat can be bought from vendors now)

Level 45 Summon: Wolf Spirit
Summons a rogue wolf with approx 750 hps and deals approx 20 dps
Mana cost (depending on how long it lasts) about the same as pet

Level 50 Summon: Chokdai Spirit (or Scaled Wolf Spirit, but chokdai is more what they look like)
Summons a ranger chokdai with approx 800 hps and deals approx 25 dps, casts snare

Summon: Aligator Spirit
Summons a Paladin Aligator with approx 800 hps and deals approx 25 dps, casts stun

Summon: Tiger Spirit
Summons an SK Tiger with approx 800 hps and deals approx 25 dps, casts cripple (or something like that)

Level 55 Summon: Bear Spirit
Summons a warrior bear with approx 3K hps, 1000 AC and deals approx 30dps, taunts (well)

Level 60 Summon: Spirit of the Great Wolf
Summons a great Rogue with approx 1.2K hps and deals approx 60 dps

Level 65 Summon: Spirit of the Great Aligator
Summons a great Paladin Aligator with approx 1.5k hps and deals approx 40 dps, casts a stun

Summon: Spirit of the Great Chokdai
Summons a great Ranger Chokdai with approx 1.5k hps and deals approx 40 dps, casts a snare

Summon: Spirit of the Great Tiger
summons a great SK Tiger with approx 1.5k hps and deals approx 40 dps, casts cripple (or a Debuff like it)

Level 70 Summon: Spirit of the Great Bear
summons a great warrior bear spirit with approx 5k hps 1200 AC and deals approx 30 dps and taunts (well)

Level 50 Summon: Essence of the Spirit Realm
summons a spirit hammer
30/70 1HB Magic Damage: +5 no rent

Each pet when summoned would cast an insta spell buff called "Materialization" or something like that, that spell would wear off 1 min before it was about to poof with a wears off message like "So-and-sos spirit begins to wane"

I'm not an elemental player, I'm - I guess - pre elemental. But I do see the disparity with Shaman and other classes desireability in groups (hence why a 7 months ago I was never lfg cause everyone wanted me, and now I have to go try and get my self killed solo in hard to get to locations in order to not be bored) But because I'm not in the upper crux of shamanism I'm not sure how best to tune these spells, but I think you get the jist of them. These would be better than normal DoTs for 2 reasons, first is they could go from mob to mob in a fast pull group, doing their damage across multiple MoBs. The other reason is of course the ultility. They have a vulnerability that DoTs don't have, and that's that they can be killed, thus wasting mana. I want the Bear pet to not replace a tank so I dunno where to put its stats, but I do think it should be able to taunt semi ok (ie get something off us after a slow with a taunt or two) Lemme know what you think of this and http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=23370 maybe one of the devs will pick something like these up.

Thanks for considering my ramblings
