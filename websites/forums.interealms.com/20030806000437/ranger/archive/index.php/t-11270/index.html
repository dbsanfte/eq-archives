Lauranthalus
I don't have it down to a science yet even though I've used it numerous times. But, given your account of two instances, one succeeded, one failed, it may be possible that you had to get too close to get a good shot. If you are in front of the mobs, you need much greater distance than if you are behind them.

Another tip:
We don't get confirmation messages like rogues, but there is a way to check to see if sneak worked. You can verify that your sneak attempt worked by sitting down and standing back up. If you hear the associated sounds for sitting and standing, sneak did not work.

Using camo, hide, sneak and lev, it's possible to pull off some fairly miraculous things. I still enjoy using the OT bank with the General standing in front of me the whole time. You get some strange comments from the evils when they show up to bank and a wood elf is standing there in front of the general and he's not attacking. :)
