Goldark
Jagged Pine - hunt gnolls, camp in the pit trap area and pull gnolls from the cave. By far the easiest option for someone without a JBB or epic for mana free damage, pretty safe and reasonable loot too.

Gorangas in ME - much tougher, but a chance at getting a Goranga idol. Might want to wait another level for this, or at least until you get some better gear. Don't touch the hunters at 55.

Personally I went to Seb from 53-56 or so tried to get groups in the crypt or juggs areas whenever I could. Crypt = jbb(very rare), ODS (very good chance of getting one if you spend a week or two getting in the crypt), hiero cloak, plus spells and other loot for selling. Juggs = spells, and if you can take Prot and Jumpy then you have (small) chance of landing malo or torpor. But horsey won't serve you well there, and its not for soloing, just something else to consider :)
