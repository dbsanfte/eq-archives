Tidykat
To add to what Nuggruck said..

Quarm is slowable, with Turgurs/Nihil/Grummus/etc. And each time you chop one of his 4 heads off, he must be fully re-debuffed and reslowed. Each head pops off at 25%, so at 75%, 50% and 25% he will lose a head and requires reslow/re-malos/etc.

As well, i *think* that he also loses an ae or 2 each time you lop off a head. Might be wrong, but last time I was around to wax him,I swear I was curing less ae's the closer he got to dying.
Kahn
Quarm is stripped of debuffs when heads are chopped off, but he retains his hatelist, so there's little chance of stealing aggro when reslowing at 75%, 50% and 25% :)

And yeah, he does lose one AE for every head lost. Yay! I wish we could get a group version of RGC for that annoying Epoch Conviction.
Grizlor
Balance works nice on him, if you have decent DPS it shouldnt wear off on its own, you'll lop off a head and it'll get removed for you.

Oh yeah, Blood of nadox is your friend. So are Abolish Poison potions from Kaesora. Regardless, you want to be with DPS, and have clerics and nukers hide around the corner, running out to nuke and running back in.

We had some problems with quarm due to 1) Lower numbers and 2) Inefficient curing. Your groups should look something like this:

1) Rogue
2) Rogue
3) Rogue
4) Rogue
5) Shaman
6) Paladin

then you stick with the rogues. Keep your group together so you can keep em all cured. Make similar groups for monks, rangers, BL, etc and STICK CLOSE to your groups. Keep 1 shaman (if you have one to spare) near casters to cure the Curse, cause that's the only thing that actually goes through the wall.

Oh yeah, you want 1 sham in the MT group for nadox also. 4 Wars, a bard, and a Shaman.
