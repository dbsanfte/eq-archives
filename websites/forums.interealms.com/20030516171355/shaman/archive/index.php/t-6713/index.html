Chulain
Felwithe guards. They are Pallys, so you have to kill them 1.5 times, but they drop decent cash and the 2HS they drop is worth 20pp with good faction/cha.

Zone is usually pretty empty too, only people there will be newbies asking for buffs and a couple of people screaming at you for killing their guards.

I bound in Firepots, took the Kelethin portal, ran to Felwithe, killed guards until my backpacks were full. Then gate to firepots and take Halas portal. Sell and repeat until bored.

Some guards are still light blue at 60 I think. Only minor exp, but pretty easy cash.
