Here is the formula as I understand it for max possible damage:

main hand:
(Weapon damage * modifier) + damage bonus

offhand:
(weapon damage * modifier)

The modifier goes up with level. not sure where, or why, but I do know it goes up at level 60. At level 60 the modifier is ~ 5.7; at 59 it is ~ 5.4. And Ive heard that at 65 it is ~ 5.9.

We can figure out your modifier because we know your max damage (88), your weapon damage (13), and your damage bonus (12)

(13 * x) +12 = 88
x = ~ 5.84

This is only conjecture, but the way Ive seen damage, it seems to me that the weapon damage is like a die roll. You roll 1 - 13 on a die, and then multiply it by your modifier which ranges from 1 - 5.84. The modifier I presume is where your attack and the mob's AC is calculated, among other things. Then you add your damage bonus at the end.

So your minimum damage will be 13 (1*1 + 12) and your max will be 88 (13*5.84 + 12)
