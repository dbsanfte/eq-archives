Grippa
If fact we could go into possibly too much detail here, and work out the efficiency of some spells factoring in the resist rates.

Take Bane of Nife - mana cost 425 but increase that by 7% to account for resists, gives an efficiency calculation of (214*7 + 150) / (425*1.07) = 3.62 hp/mana

And Torrent of poison, 3 waves gives (540 * 3) / (380 * 1.23) = 3.47 hp / mana

If you can arrange to hit with 4 waves of ToP (ie 2 mobs, 2 waves each) the efficientcy soars to (540 * 4) / (380 * 1.23) = 4.62 hp / mana

But given the awkwardness of using the spell (hitting yourself, mezzed mobs, mob moving our of rain etc etc) I think the resist rate or something should really be tweaked with these rains.
