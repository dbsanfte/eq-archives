LaughingWoman
I think a lot of people are going to get a bad impression of this game because it's so very unfinished right now. Gotta remember, it's not even in BETA. The interface is horrible (I don't think it's finished by any means), they said themselves that the movement was still a bit awkward (I agree). What I was interested in was
* downtime
* rate of play
* character development depth
My warrior/ranger had a real blast. Trying to ignore the carpal-tunnel effects of not having a friggin autorun key... I found a knockdown attack and followed it up pretty often with a power attack while they're getting up - had some nice results.

Then there's the adrenaline system... as you fight, your adrenaline INCREASES, allowing you to access particular attacks. They only work in the heat of battle. Nice concept. Knockdown was one of those.

I'm looking forward to trying out the instanced PVP zones - where suppoedly your group of friends (or up to 4 groups?) can enter an instanced (read: LDoN-style) zone paired up against an equal number of PC's to whack at each other until one team wins and gets exp or prizes, or whatnot.

The game's got a lot of potential. And without a monthly fee, you can about guarantee I'll TRY the final release. I hope they're getting around to the interface issues.
