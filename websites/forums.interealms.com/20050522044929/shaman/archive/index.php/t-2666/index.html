Sciek Spellbottler
Figure out what the mobs resist most often, cast that. :p

If you can get root to stick, that's a surefire way to take aggro. Root and get in the mobs face. As long as you're the closest to the mob, it will stay on you while rooted. Chain cast the 30 mana 'Root'. Paralyzing Earth seems to work well for me when I want to park an add, but doesn't seem to hold any better than regular Root if someone is beating the mob, so don't waste the mana when trying to hold aggro on a mob you're beating down.

Other than that, the Cripple line of debuffs seem to taunt pretty well for me, use a lower level and chain cast it perhaps. *shrug*
