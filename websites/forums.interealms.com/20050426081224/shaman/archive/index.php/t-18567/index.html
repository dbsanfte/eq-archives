Jaidin
Normal PoP tier2 and tier3 solo kills will give you about 5-6% aa per kill and using Epic, BoS, and BoU will take 6-7 mins per kill. Your targets should be mostly golems in PoV, bees in BoT, either Ogres (Diaku Warriors, Scouts, and Death Knights) or the "pit" (anything you can pull) in PoTactics, and very careful pulling anywhere in the PoI factory. Watch out for the Death Knights, they have a large harm touch and tend to resist root frequently but will give you 7% or so aa since they are 63-64 I think. Also, if you are in the pit, the Diaku Oracle's are 64 and very resistant. I think there is another Ogre type up top but I don't remember the name. You could also do frogs in PoS, they are still good xp if you can get them to yourself and plenty of spots in HoH if you don't mind managing an add sometimes.

Droga is a terrible place for xp at 65, but not bad if you are there for the loot also. If you plan on continuing to solo get vp3 asap. It is probably your best soloing tool other than BoS and BoU.

I very highly doubt that more than a handful of shaman are getting an aa in under 90 minutes solo in tier3 or under anymore. We used to prior to the solo xp nerf though. Elemental planes are an entirely different matter and if you have access to them, get vp3, then don't ever look back at t3 or lower :)

Why you are only getting 20-25% of an aa per ldon adventure I can't explain. Are you in groups that are 63+ average level? Are you winning the adventures so you are killing plenty of mobs? I get a *minimum* of 40% aa per adventure even if we finish in 30 minutes, sometimes up to 60% if it's a longer adventure. I tend to make my own adventure groups and invite level 65 players as often as possible.

If you decide to group in PoV, BoT, or HoH expect an aa every 60-75 minutes with a good group. Less if you are in BoT with a charmer or at inner caves in valor, for example, more if you aren't chain pulling, don't have good dps, etc etc. Basically 2 hours for an aa in tier2+ PoP is pretty slow from what I see.

Anyway, good luck and have fun! If you decide to go to tactics though, beware as it's a fairly empty zone most of the time and can be difficult to get a rez :)

we are not really tailored for soloing for exp. Our slows and mana regeneration make us capable of soloing incredibly powerful things, but we still have downtime, because our mana regen cannot be done *while* soloing, at least not half as efficiently as, say, a necromancer can.

papiraul
I do 1 AA every 30-35 mins at Tables..

This is how I do it:

If you dont have Dot focus items get a Ceramic Gavel of Justice (Will save mana when dotting).
You will also need Affliction Haste IV and Burning Affliction 4. You can get this at your local tribute master.
Easiest way to get trib points is by making Forged Picks. Material for combine costs 2pp, it's trivial smithing at 49 and will give you 320trib a piece.
8 backpacks of Picks will yield about 38000trib points.

Be sure you buy VP3. Do this as early as possible cause AA's at tables is easy to get. You will be able to buy all the other stuff you need pretty quick afterwards.

I got 2 spellsets I use.
1: Solo Debuff
-Bane
-Anathema
-Odium
-Malosinia
-Malicious Decay
-Quiencense
-Pox of Bertoxxulous (this breaks root.. I'll explain further down)
-Canni IV

2: Solo Burn
-Bane
-Anathema
-Odium
-Breath of Ultor
-Blood of Saryrn
-Quiencense
-Pox of Bertoxxulous (this breaks root.. I'll explain further down)
-Canni IV

Make 2 hotbuttons for them:
/loadspellset solo debuff
/loadspellset solo burn

K, this is how you optimize the kill.

Load "Solo Debuff" spellset.

Keep max distance to target.
Cast Malicious Decay (This will lower dis resists to be able to land VP3, and it will guarantee VP to hold its full duration. It will not get resisted)
Cast Malosinia (This is rarely resisted. Max amount of times I've got resists is about 5 times in a row. There is no need to malos before, you will only waste mana that way, and precious dot time.)
Cast Bane
Cast Anathema
Cast Odium
Press hotkey to load spellset "Solo Burn"
Cast Breath of Ultor
Cast Blood of Saryrn
At this stage Bane will run out.
Cast Bane
Cast Anathema
Cast Odium
Now press Canni V
Target yourself and smack on a Quicense.
Now be sure to click VP3 now and check how long time is left until it refresh. You got one tick to reapply it after it refreshes.
At this stage Bane will run out again.
Cast Bane
Cast Blood of Saryrn
Now reapply VP3.
Cast Anathema
Cast Odium
Etc. etc..

When you feel safe and got all this timed in you can add in the Epic from start of the dot chain. It will wear of just before you VP3 the mob the second time. So reapply it after that if you want added dps.
Also if you feel like risking it some.. If you time it right and keep an eye out for how long VP3 got left before it breaks, you can add and extra dot. Pox of Bertoxxulous. This will break VP3, so be sure to cast it when VP3 only got 5 sec left before refreshing. And be sure to be at max distance before you do it. Pox will break VP3 but since VP3 refreshed during the time you cast it you will be able to reapply it directly after.
With BA4 and Crit Aff 3 AA I usally dot the tables mobs down in 3min 30sec.
Canni up as fast ass possible after kill and you should be able to DING an AA every 50 mins or so. (Or even faster if you got good manaregen and stuff.)

/Cheers
