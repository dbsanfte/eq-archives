Kianor
I don't think there's a right or wrong answer, and I don't think you'll regret it whichever way you do it.

Personally, I did the AAs for Canni V between level 61 and 62. (I started at 61, then when they opened up the tier 3 zones, I levelled to 62 before carrying on with AA).

I never really had a problem getting a group, though it probably is a little easier at 65. It doesn't really take long to go through 60-65 though in tier 2/3 of PoP. One factor is - at 61/62 I was getting groups quickly, but more in PoV, only getting groups in HoH and BoT when there were no higher level shamans lfg. At 65, I was more likely to get a group in tier 3, where spectrals drop, and runes can sometimes.

You do get a bonus for fighting yellows/whites, and you might find this a good reason to stop at 60/61 to AA. I know I did an AA in 50 minutes at IC in PoV at 61 in a flying group, and have fought there at 65 with a similar pace of kills (keeping both caves clear) and the exp rate wasn't as good.

When I hit 65, I didn't have any 65 spells, and precious few of my 63/64 ones. I did notice fewer resists, though, and I had a few more HP and mana - so I'd recommend getting to 65 straight after canni V if not before, regardless of what spells you have. Once you have the level, the spells will be easier to get.
