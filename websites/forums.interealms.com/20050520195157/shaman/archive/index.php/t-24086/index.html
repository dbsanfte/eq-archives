afreeman
Correct me if I am wrong, but tap tanking != Agro kiting ?

That is correct, but what I'm talking about is kind of a hybrid. Standard tap-tanking is just that: you stand there and tank while you chain-tap; I'm sure you know what aggro-kiting is.

Neither of these strategies works against harder-hitting summoners, so you incorporate both of them by snaring the mob and then kiting it as much as possible to minimize damage, while also timing taps in between each summons cycle.

I've seen this referred to as 'summon-kiting' but I'm not sure how widespread this is as a definition.
