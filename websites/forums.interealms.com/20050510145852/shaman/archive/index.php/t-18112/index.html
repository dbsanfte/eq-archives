Denn,

Try this to fix:

1. In IE, click on the menu "Tools"
2. click "Internet Options"
3. click on the "Advanced" tab.
4. scroll down till you see the category "HTTP 1.1 settings"
5. put a check box in the option "Use HTTP1.1 through proxy connections"
6. hit ok, etc.

That should fix it. Keep in mind those instructions are for IE6, and may vary slightly with other versions of IE. If you are using another web browser, look for similar options...
