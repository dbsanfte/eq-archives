Necro avoids getting hit. Thats the whole key.

BL sends pet, sits and heals pet, or goes in and melees with pet. Either way, BL AND pet are getting hit. Necro snares, fears, dots, sends pet, and neither necro or pet ever gets hit.

The secret to solo high level is avoiding hits. Any class that can do this can solo high up. Enchanters druids wizzies all can solo high level because they can avoid hits. Mages cant avoid hits (or their pets cant) so they have a disadvantage.

Well, 60+ shammies can solo in PoP. With the 61 pet fully buffed ya can kill rats/goos/raves/spiders/treants/trash no problem in PoD. Pet tanks, you dot, pet takes 1 CH. mob dies./ Repeat. Its actually really safe 61+. These mobs max at 250ish per hit and double. We can easily mitiage this. When mobs quad for 400 is where we have a problem. In PoV or PoS you are gonna have to root rot. Unfortunately that means ya cant get the damage out of the pet, and there is more mana wasted on roots, and time wasted running around and re-rooting. Its not ass effecient as pet tanking, but still is plausable. Its also alot more dangerous.

The real question is can ya get the same or better exp/hour compared to a group.

If you are 61 and do not have access to PoV or PoS you can solo nearly as well as a PoD casle group. You solo much better than a crap CY group, and nearly as well as a CY group that is pulling your CY and MCY. Its about the same exp as a PoN Hedge/docks group. Gargs/Spidies/Dooms/Gobbos are better.

It really boils down to kill speed. At 61 you will be getting 2% aaexp per kills solo. Sometimes 3. So at say the CY, you only get 1% grouped. You kill much faster though. So you gotta kill 100% faster at 1% to make up for the lesser exp. Since shamans kill so slow solo, its entirely possible that ya could. In castle ect, Mobs are teh same % groups as the trash solo kills. And ya kill at least as fast. Therefore its better exp.

PoV PoS you get 5% ish. And ya kill just as fast. So, its obviously better exp.

So yes we CAN solo. However Necors and chanters can solo stuff that we cant. Therefore, they can solo or group and get the same exp. We need to group to get the higher level exp. But we do have the option to fart around while we ait for a group.

At 63+ this all changes. The trash goes lb/green. Your only option is to root rot with more risk. Necros and chanters keep chugging along though.
