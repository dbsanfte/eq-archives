Most definitely. A shaman really is the preferred bot class for PoP in my opinion. I use a cleric, which works, but getting in an early slow really makes things more efficient.

You don't necessarily need to get keyed for PoS/PoV immediately, either. Up to level 63 you'll get very good xp in first tier zones - PoD, PoN are good 2 box zones, and good "intro" to how PoP mobs hit :). At 63 and above, PoV is doable, PoS is hard work & not really efficient for me but likely easier & more efficient with a shaman. Another possibility is CoD (Crypt of Decay) which is easily accessed via a key quest - I do a lot of XPing there, as the zone is often empty on my server.

On the DW vs. 2h question, I have tried both. The problem for me using 2h was slowing, so I experimented with a Swarmcaller - fights ended up taking too long, and I required more heals as a result. I did notice damage taken over time is more predicable with 2h, versus dual wield. I seemed less likely to get hit with the really bad streak of 3 successful hits and 2 ripostes for 1.5k+ HP total damage taken, in a matter of a couple sends, while using 2h. With a shaman bot, 2h is likely the best choice.
