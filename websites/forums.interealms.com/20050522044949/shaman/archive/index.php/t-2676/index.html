Yes the staff stacks with the tunic. Since the staff effect is a spell efeect (takes a buff spot) it will stack with any "constant regen" item, like the fungi tunic/iksar BP/etc.

Yes, you have to equip the staff (and right-click it to trigger the effect) - meaning only SHM, DRU, BST can use it

The staff effect won't work until level 35

The staff effect lasts around 5 minutes

It also snares you and overwrites SoW

It also overwrites any regen type spells (Regeneration, Chloroplast, Regrowth...) you have on you
