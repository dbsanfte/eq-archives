I posted the following to the developer site. I dono if others agree with me but I hunt quite a bit with harmony. I am a hunter. I dont sit in a camp and wait for a monk to bring back prey. The harmony changes and proposed harmony changes have seriously hurt.

begin post >

I don’t know exactly who to send this to so I’m trying whoever answers the class specific ranger problems because I view this as such. The recent change to harmony has seriously damaged the ranger class. We are hunters of the outdoors. Outdoor zones are the only places that we can still “hunt” rather than sit in camp waiting for the monk or shadowknight to bring the prey back in. For most indoor zones, track is just a “rare spawn indicator” but in outdoor zones, the combination of track and harmony made the ranger the puller of choice.

While the scroll for the spell harmony of Nature still has not been found on any server, the details of the spell are available via Lucy and Eqcaster and have been posted numerous times on the web. The spell is severely lacking. Rangers, especially half-elves, do not have large mana pools. The combination of an increase in mana cost and the “upgraded” harmony being single target, completely destroy its usefulness. I cannot cast the spell three times on each pull. I simply don’t have the mana to pull at the rate most groups demand. Because I would be pulling I also have no time to meditate between fights. Couple this with the fact that the spell is easily resisted, SvMagic minus 10 vs. the average damage over time spell resist of SvMagic minus 100, and rangers simply can not pull anymore. We do not have any method of feign death. If the spell is resisted the mobs will agro and the ranger will die rather than lead 5 mobs back to their camp and slaughter their friends. Because it is NOT an AOE spell it must be cast once per mob with each having a chance to resist.

We have also been encouraged to fight mobs above our level. That is suicide with a spell like this. Rangers have no way of splitting off second, third or fourth adds on a pull like a monk or SK. Four red mobs will eat any full group alive even if they have an enchanter. The rangers only choice is to stand and die then be pulled back to the cleric for a rez. Without real harmony, we are nothing more than extremely poor warriors that can sow and snare. We are not hunters of the forest.

The stated reason for the change was players were able to single pull in Cazic Thule. The average level of mobs in Cazic Thule is 55. Why then is the level cap lowered all the way to 40? I would suggest the following changes to harmony. The creation of a harmony line of spells.

Harmony 25mana up to level 30 mobs
Calmness 50 up to level 45 mobs
Serenity 75 up to level 60 mobs
Tranquility 100 up to level 65 mobs

Range 200
Casting time 3 seconds
Recast time 30 seconds
Outdoor only
Resist SvMagic minus 100

The reasons for these changes are as follows. The range is identical to both the old and new. Mana cost is reasonable for someone that never has a chance to meditate. Casting time is the same as the old harmony but more than harmony of nature. I think 3 sec is completely acceptable. Recast increased from 12sec to 30sec. The recast time should be increased to make it more difficult to harmony very large groups of mobs. The spell should be kept outdoor usable only. Resist magic minus 100 is the same as DoT based spells. This will allow the spell to be resisted yet still be reasonably useful. Duration should continue to be the same, 2 minutes. Mobs should not agro when the spell wears off as is happening with harmony now.

I accept that monks and shadowknights are much better pullers in indoor zones. Rangers however should be the great hunters of the forest. If harmony isn’t fixed, rangers should be given an “Elude” skill, similar to feign death but only usable outdoors. This would allow a ranger, with 4 mobs on them after a bad attempt at harmony, to loose their pursuers. I died 5 times attempting to pull today. I will not be pulling again unless this is fixed. Even in the forest I will be letting the warrior pull. At least he can take the hits. I am no longer a hunter.

Muas Bringer of Storms
