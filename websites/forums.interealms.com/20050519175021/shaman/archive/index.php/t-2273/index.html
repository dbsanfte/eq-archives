The secret of the cycle......

ok.. i know this cycle by heart, and with danger of inflation of this items.. (I hardly do it anymore anyways :)here it is:

The cycle includes the 3 corners of the zone, SE (fort), SW (BW ent) and NW (droga ent) in that order... Track is almost a must. First thing is to patrol the 3 corners, preferably bind at SE corner to be able to speed up travel time.

The mobs thats in the cycle are:

SE corner: blugtugin/A goblin raider/goblin herbcollector/Rockwolf/wandering stonehealer/goblin trailblazer/goblin chieftain/goblin iksar slayer

SW corner: Joojooga/mountain giant hillock/mountain giant prospector/Goblinstomper/overseer Miklek/gromlok/(krenlek(might be sarnak cycle though.. havent dont it in ages))

NW corner: Dustback/a burynai miner/Burynai grand cenobiter/gemeye/eboneyes/burynai treasure seeker/stonegrinder

usually the next one after the kill is on top of track in the next corner (as its the latest spawn), so its easy to pinpoint it even if its a common mob...
In the cases that theres no special named mobs on track its usuallly the raider, hillock or bury miner that are up... In the case of the gob raider in the se corner make notice that the one in the cycle are spelled "A" goblin raider in comparison with the others that are without A in front... burynai miners are common in zone, but not in nw corner... if u only got one on track standing close to nw corner that prolly it (as a druid... im not sure of the trackrange of rangers and (bards))... The hillock is the tricky one.. it doesnt stand out from the rest in any ways... but if you just kill of the 2-4 hillocks in the area around the spawn"pit" in SW corner you prolly got him, and can proceed to NW corner...

and voila.. youre in the game... happy huntin
