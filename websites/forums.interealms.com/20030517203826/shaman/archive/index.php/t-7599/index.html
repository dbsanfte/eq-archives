Naagruz
Killing dragons in WW will get you non-KOS with zek giants pretty quickly. From there, I would go to the dorf camp farthest south in EW and repeatedly flatten that camp. If you are not yet 61, you can prolly make some decent exp out of it and get a ton of heads + riff faction. You can even get the books off of the missionaries for some decent quest rewards if you stay there long enough.

Killing Royals and Dain in Icewell is by far the fastest way to faction max and riff and very worth it timewise if you can get a decent group together.
