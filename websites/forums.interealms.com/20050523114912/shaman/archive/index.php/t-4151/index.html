I've found high 20's to low 30's tough to solo with several of my characters.

In the short term, LOIO is excellent xp until 30, even 31 if it's not too crowded. My high-level spot there is on the beach on the South edge of the Lake. There's a big mountain there were you should find plenty of single wandering blues. Easy to pull them off the mountain and down to the beach for easy killing.

From there, I think I had some success in FM killing burynai miners, but the adds there can be brutal.

stormrazor
I leveled from 30-34 pretty much at the Golra Iceberg in Timorous Deep. No competition for spawns, and there is a exp modifier in that zone 20% I believe so the exp should flow like water.
Loot isn't great but its not too shabby either, occasional pp and words are dropped by them and they will help work on your melee skills and defense. No roamers so adds are not a problem.
Here is a link to a map of
Tim Deep (http://eqatlas.com/kunark/timorousdeepmap.html)

#12 on the map is the Golra Iceberg, here is a link to the beastiary:Golras (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?zone=72)

The only real problem is getting there is a pain, and always have Gate memorized. Also Crushed Diamonds for Cannibalize II quest iis a ground spawn on that island at location: -8840, -6080.
Good Luck hope this helps.
