Weyalan
Yes, you do take a big jump in soloing power at 34....but only if you have prepared for it!

The major factor (and it is, in my opinion, one of the class defining factors) is the puppy, fifi, dogdog - call it what you will - the pet. The pet will, when used correctly, provide your most consistent and reliable source of damage output...BUT (and there is always a but)...

...although the pet does dish out excellent damage (especially with alpha), it cannot take damage for peanuts....so... you need to be able to tank mobs in teh face, and let dogdog do its damage without getting hit to much.

In order to achieve this, you, obviously, are going to have to be able to survive the pounding being handed out by the mob that you are tanking, so your armor class and hp will suddenly become very important. Plan your gear upgrades with emphasis on AC and hp, even if it is at the expense of wis/mana. Also, bear in mind that defence skill ups = free AC increases, so make sure that your defence skill is maximised for your level. So make sure that your defence skill is maximised for your level. So make sure that your defence skill is maximised for your level. So make sure that your defence skill is maximised for your level (are you getting the subtle hint here?).

Having said all that, for the mid-level shaman, tanking for dogdog is certainly not the only way to go. But take it from me, it is the most fun way to go...Muahahahahahahaha!
