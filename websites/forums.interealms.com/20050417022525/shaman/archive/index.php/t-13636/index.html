By request, I've taken the time today to clean up my scoring system to bring it into accord with the PoP age and have begun the process of creating a list of tradeable items, sorted by score, as a guide for shaman. I am including only tradeable items, those that are the result of popular quests (e.g. Earring of the Solstice) or popular sets of armor (e.g. Velious quest, Ornate armor), and a very few select no drop pieces that would be obtainable with the same degree of difficulty as the other items here. Generally speaking, only items that rate about 50.0 or more are listed, although I have made a few exceptions.

In the interests of open knowledge, this scoring system is as follows:
AC*0.5 + HP*1.2 + MP*1.0 + STA*3.0 + AGI*0.5 + WIS*2.5 + (PR+MR+FR+DR+CR)*0.25. Additionally, an item earns 3 points per point of HP regen/tick and 10 points per point of mana regen/tick (usually FT). Clickable or focus effects that have a major impact on the value of the item are figured under a seperate system and added to the value of the item - if people are interested, I will later post that here (with the number of foci in game, the discussion is lengthy).

EDIT: First 3 slots are completely done, working to fill in the rest. I'll be going back when I can to fill in missing spots, especially on the 3 sets of Ogre Cultural.
