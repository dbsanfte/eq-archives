I have a suggestion for a camp. Bind yourself at timorous firepot room. Now click on the Grobb pot to inny swamp, run across the zone and go into Guk. Go to Lguk live side and go camp the evil eye till it pops (40 min spawn time . . 50% odds of named). Now /join market (or whatever the auction channel on your server is), and gate to timorous. Find a buyer for the EE bag you just got and sell it, should sell for upwards of 500pp. The bonus is that Guk is a good solo place for u. Since the spawn takes 40 minutes, you can whack the ph and go to a different part of the dungeon for some blue cons and exp.
The EE is rarely if ever camped, and it's an easy lewt to get. People dont sell them much because they are busy using them and the bag is LORE. I know I use one of those, thus I don't sell them.

The firepot room gives u access to most of norrath to meet prospective buyers, and also access back to Guk in a hurry. Repeat this a few times till you have what you need for cash. Also avoid outdoor zones. They won't teach you to be a good player, and the biggest reason is that you don't see droods soloing indoors. Just zone into a dungeon for loot and exp. Get to know dungeons and you will always have enough pp. They offer more risk, but way more reward as well. And truthfully, once you learn how to deal with the challenges of these dungeons, they become really easy.

Keep referring back to these boards if you have questions about dungeon hunting or tricks to use while soloing. Also remember that a duo can be a lot of fun and VERY profitable. Look up a monk or ranger or SK for dungeon work. Even a paladin or warrior.

Wobbs
First you need to decide whats more important to you at the moment a) exp. or b) loot.

If your answer is a) then take everyone elses advice. If your answer is b) then spend the next few weeks camping the most plat lucrative zone in the game - bazaar. Many many people hate buying and selling, but a select few are patient, responsive and stupid enough to spend virtually tens of hours a week just sitting their ass in the bazaar with the auction channel on.

With the amount of time I've spent playing on my new account I should be somewhere in the low 40's in terms of levels, but right now I sit at the grand level of 25. Equipment wise I am very well off, I'd say well off enough until 50. How did I make my cash?Some friends gave me some junk to sell when I restarted my naked toon and spending so much time just running around the bazaar allowed me to do 3 things.
1) Sell my stuff. Duh.
2) Buy other stuff. I constantly spend time browsing others wares looking for hidden gems and great deals. It's really just dumb luck but I've been able to snag an ODS for 3k, a rune etched icefang for 3k, a drakkel icegrinder for 75pp and an embalmers skinning knife for 50pp. I recently sold my REIF for 7k, icegrinder for 800 and the knife was used in a big trade for a rubi bp. woot dumb luck.
3) Get a feel for prices. /bazaar is a godsend. It allows you to get a good idea for how much an item is worth. Knowing how much items cost stops you from overpaying for something you know you could get for less.

If anyone decides to try this out remember some golden rules.

You want to make profit? Only buy underpriced stuff, don't even buy at market value.

A *trade* that you initiate will almost always net you a bigger profit than if you just go /trader and /afk for the night. I almost always have auction channel on just scanning what people are selling/buying. If someones selling something nice, I shoot them a tell saying, " Hi, would you be interested in trading _____ for _____?". Most of the time they say no, but sometimes you'll get lucky.

Plat is King. I can't stress this enough. While items are assets that are sometimes hard to get rid of, Plat is always liquid. It will get you anything.

Don't take big risks. Just like in real life playing the stock market, don't sink all your plat into one big item hoping it will pay off. Its just too big a risk and can ruin everything. I have a friend who does this actively however, trading everything he has in exchange for something lucrative like a CoF, then selling it for profit. He has made it big doing this, getting a fungi among other things at lvl 30. But its just too risky if you ask me.

And finally the number one and most important rule: HAVE PATIENCE. I don't know how many times I've seen some bright eyed, bushy-tailed entrepreneur zone into bazaar with a sack of items and plat thinking they are going to play EC trader and then see them leave the next day wearing a barrel. It usually starts with someone auctioning that they want to sell ____ for 5k. Then an hour later they are getting impatient so they auction for 3k. Then finally the poor schmuck is tired and bored and wants to go gain exp so the settle for 1k. I'll tell you right now, if you've got a fancy item worth some coin and you can't sell it immediatly, just bank it and try again some other day.

Hope this helps anyone interested in their EQ day trading. :D
