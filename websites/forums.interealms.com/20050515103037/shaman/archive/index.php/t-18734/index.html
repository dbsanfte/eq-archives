Toktuk Wartalon
Iksar Shaman have the Cudgel quests, rather than a set of armor. The end result is much better in the long run, trust me. You can also work on getting Scaled Mystic armor, which is comparable to Totemic and can be obtained between levels 20-35 or so.

Klemydia
For low level newbie quested armor, hail the merchant inside the Haggle Baron (can't remember his name) and follow the textual clues. He'll give you a satchel that needs to be filled with scaled curskins. Fill it, click combine and give it back and you get a random piece of curskin armor (not too good) and some coin.

Or.

Hit the bazaar and buy some cheap armor. You want AC and HP above all else.
Zatriz
No no no. Iksar Shaman DO get newbie armor, and it can last you into your 40's. As you zone into Cabilis from Field of Bone, run like you are heading to the Shaman guild. Instead of turning right (at the new tailoring merchant) go straight to the next house on the left. Might be 1 house farther to the left, but it is right there.

You are looking for Zazlan Furscale and Shezlie Furscale. The armor is called Scaled Mystic.

Have fun and good luck.

Iksar Shaman don't get "newbie" armor like other race/class combos. They get curscale which is sort of like patchwork armor.

They do get an equivalent to Totemic but that won't help you at level 10 and, really, Ry'Gorr is better and probably a lot cheaper to get in the long run.
