Shoendesae
Between the introduction of the Berzerker epic and the half-azz patch today, we are seeing quite clearly the problem with SOE.

Back when Verant ran the EQ show, items had limited effects, damage was low, speed was high, and you were lucky to see one stat improved let alone ALL of them. Kedge or Hate were the scariest places.

Then Kunark came out. Ten new levels to master. Items saw little change for the most part. yes, they became more desirable, those Wurmslayers and Yaks were the sh...well you get the point. Classes were given other abilities to make them overall more useful like the Rogue and Shaman (tugurs/topror). Veeshans Peak was the scariest place conceivable.

As the playerbase aged and maxed out the level cap, Velious became necessary to appease those who were bored with Seb and HS and Velious was good. Well implemented and balanced for level 35+. Plenty of raid level content and only moderate item scope creep. But the seeds of evil and destruction had been sown.

The talent started to balk at the gigantic corporate structure that started to notice the revenue that this little bit of entertainment was generating for Daddy.

Bean counters started making policy. Talent left, and the true Vision(tm) as convoluted as it was, left with them.

Then came Luclin. While it may have had some small merit in the keying tiers to some of its zones and a general nice overall boost to xp granted, it single handedly killed EQ. UBAR became required. This is where EQ stopped being a MMORPG and became truly victim to blatant Scope Overrun. I wont ever forget the first time I saw some of the Vex Thal gear, how while it was cool for the people that took the time to get it, really blew balance out of the water. Coupled with the relative ease of travel for all classes with the spire ports, the game couldn't ever hope to retain its original Vision(tm). Oh and then there was the introduction of the Beastlord, and the 3 days it took to get one to 60th level, and the whopping 2 weeks it took for them to find their overly strong, non planar required epic.

Then Planes came, and it had its merits , but mostly contributed to everything wrong with Luclin. Books made travel trivial. I remember running from Qeynos to Freeport to catch the 20 minute boat ride to Butcherblock to run to Mistmoore to get quest pieces for whatever quest it was I was working on at the time. SOE has made sure that the world of EQ is now gone. EQ is now ever only 5 zones large from any port point or book. Gone are the days where one would have to contend with encounters on the way to where they wanted to be. To me this marks the loss of one of the most important elements of any RPG. Items were once again blown out of statistical proportion with their old world counterparts simply to drive sales of the expansion.

Then far to rapidly, so rapidly in fact I can't honestly remember which came first, Yekesa and Lost Dungeons. More scope overrun. But I must say that at least Lost Dungeons had the right idea. To make content for smaller groups or raids. To make it time based so that one could spend an evening after work doing one run and be happy with the progress that was made, and most importantly at ANY level. Well done there SOE I give you much credit for this.

now

What the hell happened with Gates? Did you loose all the LDON talent too? It was fubared from the get go. I can understand the need to feed the ravenous lvl65 200+AA raid set, but at what cost? Then for it to be so shabbily done that it requires the patches we have seen in the last month, and then to pat yourselves on the back for fixing GRAMMAR problems? And to do so while ignoring so many other payers ~er~ players requests to fix the playability of the game? Scope Overrun is as rampant as ever, the "story" of EQ has gone to the dogs, and the classes are still not balanced.

Here is a clue SOE the classes can never be balanced until content for all of them is. This means you have set yourselves on a path that does not allow you to do what it is that you must do. Balance the game. Content is king and the coronation is done with balance.

Until you can look at this game with gamers eyes, and understand all I have said EQ will always head down this wrong path.

meanwhile, all the talent you once had is drifting away and coalescing at Sigil.

SOE, EQ meet your match.
