Ximix the Troll
Zaspacer asked me to explain Bard / Shaman synergey in another thread - so here it is. Seemed to deserve a new thread.

These observations come from my regular hunting group:

War War Brd Cle Wiz Shm (me).

The bard that I group with is one of the very best EQ players I have ever met. She is our puller, and uses numerous bard tricks to pull as well as any Monk I have seen:

Lull song pulling to get 1 mob alone from a tight group

Bard Speed / Lazy Aggro splitting (outrun the additional mobs that you don't want such that they give up the chase and return to spawn points, while bringing your target to camp)

Gate Song splitting (gate unwanted adds back to their spawn points - after having pulled them far enough away to be out of chase range).

She also makes great use of Singing steel helm eyeball, tracking, and bind sight to completely scout an area and plan the pulls.

She fanatically studies every zone to learn which mobs are bard mezzable (55-), and which mobs are not 56+.

All of this lets us usually avoid having more than 2 56+ mobs in camp at one time. Since we have 2 good tanks, we can tank 2 big mobs easily. In a pinch, I can Torpor tank one mob if we have 3 56+ in camp. When things go bad, we evac to zone and try again.

In terms of synergy with a shaman -

Shaman debuff resists (Malo), and Slow better than enchanters do. Bards debuff magic resists (OOS, Orb of Tishan) too. This allows us to take on mobs that have very high magic resists and are hard to slow without an enchanter to Tash.

Bards have superior Mana Draining ability to enchanters. The level 60 Bard mana drain song is dramatically more effective at stopping Complete Heal and Gate than anything an enchanter can do (especially vs. 56+ mobs).

With Bard/Shaman - all the crowd control in your group is being handled by people with good AC and hitpoints. Your group does not die when one paper-thin enchanter goes down too fast due to a resist or two.

Heck - Our cleric does not even bother to keep remedy memorized. No need to use such a tiny, fast heal when everyone in the group can take a few licks (except the wizard - and she is smart enough to avoid aggro).

Bard Epic Haste is practically the best haste a group can have. Add the new Luclin "over the cap" haste songs to that, and bard haste is much much better than enchanter haste. It pays to keep our bard melee-ing, so that Dance of the Blade is always up on our melees. If we get too many adds in camp, though, and our bard can't melee - I just buff Celerity when Dance drops.

Cantata is a superior buff to C3. It regens health, mana, and stamina. The mana Regen is faster than C3 (I believe), especially with all of the things a bard can get to improve song effectiveness.

An enchanter grouped with a shaman frequently has full mana with almost nothing to do but buff occasionally (depends upon the camp and skill of the puller, but happens a lot).

Grouping with a good bard is like having one person performing the functions of both an enchanter and an extra melee damage dealer/uber-puller. Consider it a group of 7 that fits into a 6 person group! :)

Glencannon
Bard > all others, as far as duoing with a Shaman.

Shaman with regen tunic, regrowth, bear form, natural regen= anywhere from 28-50 health per tick. Bard regen stacks, increase depending on which song and what instrument is used. Let's just be conservative, and say 40-62 regen per tick now.

Shaman can Slow (duh). 70% dmg mitigation.

Bard sings Rune Song (lvl 49 dmg absorption--20pts per tick singing, 40pts per tick with instrument).

Slowed mob + shaman regen + rune song + bard regen (twisting) = very small amount of damage taken while tanking.

I've done this with a guildie one time, and it was amazing. Of course, he switches from weapons to a Lute during downtime, and continues to twist Rune + Cantata while I cannabilize. While he's doing this, I can't get my health bar to go down (still using Cann 3).

Try it out some time. We had basically zero downtime, and were able to clear all Sentient Suits before repop. (I have yet to try it with a C3 added on top of all this...)

Oh yeah, bards can mezz adds, snare runners, and are much more willing to charm a beastie with me there to bail him out (sow + heals).

I only have Epic and Ikky regen, btw. Fungal + JBB going for 100K on my server, and both mobs are perma-camped because of it. Probably will never see either.

I'm currently leveling a bard on a second account. Gonna get him to 40, and have him group with me, just sit there and sing Cantata all day with a good Lute. I figure he might get 5% of each kill (I'm lvl 57).

Just my 2cp.
