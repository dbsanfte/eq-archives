The boats in Dulak are a really good spot but often the ones you can do solo at 55 are camped up. A spot I liked a lot is a little hard to describe but I'll try. Using the Eqatlas map of Dulak, find boat #1 and follow the point of it to a large building that's kinda shaped like half an "H" and it has a smaller rectangular building next to it, with an ally in between the two. Set up toward the back of that ally (but not against the walls of the bigger building as you can pull aggro from inside). The smaller rectangular building is the treasure sorter warehouse and is where I suggest pulling from. There is a spawn outside that sometimes spawns a named in it's place. And the spawns inside should be easily single pulled. Just be careful as there is one guard that wonders down the alley from time to time that you will need to kill. The cash is good, the xp is not too bad (some might be lt blue at 55 i don't remember) but it's never camped.
