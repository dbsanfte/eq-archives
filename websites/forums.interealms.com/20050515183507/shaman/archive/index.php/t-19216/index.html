Shadrak
I do a LOT of 3-boxing. I started with a druid/shaman/warrior or monk and have lately been playing enchanter/mage/cleric. Both work pretty well, but I know what you mean about dps. Shaman pets are too weak to really help much in your trio, and dots take too long to work for LDoNs where you're always in a big hurry.

If dps is your problem, don't get rid of the mage. Mages are some of the easiest dps you can find for 3-boxing. Hot key the pet attack macro and hit a few nukes - it's easy to handle on your second or third character. It seems that your shaman, with slows, should be able to keep up with pet heals. If the shaman just debuffs, slows, and buffs the pets, then you shouldn't need the cleric. Mind you - I understand how nice it is to have a cleric around. But if you're feeling the bite from not enough dps, I'd replace the cleric with another easily bot-able (not sure if that's a word) character.

My choice? Another mage. Think about it. 2 mages with a shaman. One mage uses the earth pet or air pet for taunt, the other mage positions his water pet for backstabs. That's a little tough to accomplish, especially in LDoNs where you're moving all the time. But in dungeon or zone camps, you could position your pets with /guard so they'll approach a mob from different directions. Wait a sec before sending in the water pet, to let the primary pet build up a little agro with a root or stun.

Or, have both mages use the air pets. Two air pets could mitigate a lot of damage with the stuns they generate - it would make it easier for your shaman to keep up with heals when mobs are A) slowed by 70% and B) being stunned by two air pets.

Another option would be a druid. Druids with dire charm can add some serious dps to your 3-box, and you could snare-kit with 3 pets whacking on the mob while your druid runs around holding agro with low-cost snares. Not necessarily the easiest 3-box option, in my opinion, but another choice if you don't feel like playing up another
mage.

A wizard would also be a good choice for a 3-box bot. Instant damage via simple hotkeyed macros. Ideal for 3-boxing.
