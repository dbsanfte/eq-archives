Juujuu
SCRM is 8 points. You read that right. It's still very much worth it.

Get QB2 and some quick buff focus item. That, or get QB3 and never think about needing to get a quick buff focus item again.

Your magelo AA list is out of date. It's hard to tell what you want to go for as such. Try searching for info about different AA routes to go. It depends if you do a lot of healing, or tanking, or want utility clicky AAs, or if you raid.

Assuming you have: run3 regen3 mc3 c5 scr3 scrm qb2
What you absolutely MUST HAVE is RC1 before you go on to anything more. It's amazing, and only 2 points.
You'll probably want to go for SCM3 to save yourself a whack of mana regardless what route you go down.

then this is what you might want to go for:
raiding: MGB, CA3, LR5, CS3, ID5, ND3
healing: HG3, AHG3, RC3, HA3, AHA3 (whichever gives you critical heals first, then RC3, then bigger normal heals)
clicky: SC1, VP1, SM2, Pet Disc, then finish working up to SC3 and VP3

Don't bother with Rabid Bear. It blows.
Don't bother with better nukes until you don't have much else to buy. Shaman don't really nuke much to make it worth while.
RC1 is an absolute necessity for so much of POP. Higher levels cure more counters, but also give you a better chance of removing annoying effects like Malo and stuff.
Don't bother with higher int/wis or increasing your int/wis cap or your physical stats until you have all of your raiding/healing/clicky AAs bought. You just don't get much benefit out of it compared to the other stuff.

Personally, I basicly only had run3 regen3 mc3 c5 mgb before POP.
I went the clicky route a bit to get sc3, vp1, rc1, then jumped over to the raiding path to get LR5 and CS3, then jumped back to the clicky where I'm working up stuff now. I most recently bought VP3 and SCM2 because I personally always had enough mana for what I was doing, and I like clickies! I've liked the path I've gone down. I'm going to finish the clicky path, then finish the raid path, then start working on the healing path before I get the "fluff" AAs like rabid bear and bigger caps on my stats.
