Gloriana
Every six seconds is an EQ "tick." Mana and hitpoint regen goes up on the "tick" bard song pulses on the "tick" and dots do damage on the "tick." The little dreamweaver in your inventory screen will spin on the tick.

So, in your example (I guess this is the level 19 Affliction?):

30 damage + 126 (6 x 21) = 156 damage for 75 mana

Compared to:

130 damage for 100 mana for the DD spell Ice Strike (level 24)

You can see that dots are more mana efficient (as long as the mob lives throughout the full duration of the dot).
