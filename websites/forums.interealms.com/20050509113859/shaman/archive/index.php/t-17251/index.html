taraddar
There are times when raids become bugged even when you just formed em. Had that happen a couple times rescently where we formed up a raid outside PoAir or Fire or someplace, zoned in and none of the unflagged could zone in. Reform raid and they could get in. Your raid may have just been bugged if no one at all could get in.

As far as sol ro's chamber goes I've never seen us not be able to get unflagged into his chamber. Back when we first started killing him there was some confusion because the 5 mini's flags disappeared when you got sol ro flag and people thought it flagged them to enter his chamber. It doesn't and you ended up with people unknowingly using up unflagged spots. Not as confusing anymore since the mini's show up even after getting sol ro flag now.

As far as ad goes the only reason i could see doing him for an elemental or better flagged guild was if you lost enough old members that you couldn't reliably field enough people to do marr and needed to do him for reflagging. His loot is just almost total junk and he's rather hard for his level. Best solution is to try and buy flagging spots with lower tier guilds or flag share with them so you can get a few people flagged here and there.
