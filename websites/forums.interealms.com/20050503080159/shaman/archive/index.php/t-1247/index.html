stormrazor
You should absolutely work on your other skills. You won't get your epic at level 1 and early piercers are nothing short of pathetic. Seeing as how Shaman only have 3 combat skills to work on it won't take you very long to max all of them. I'd be kicking myself if I wouldn't have work on my 2 hand blunt seeing as I just got my hands on a Drakkel Icegrinder. You never know what kind of weapon you're gonna get your hands on during your journey to your epic. So work on your other skills as well, otherwise you'll be kicking yourself later for not building your skills. It is much easier to build them up earlier than later.
And some will even tell you that they use their epic for its DoT and switch it out for another weapon while meleeing. For meleeing I'd personally use a Rune Etched Icewurm Fang over the epic. So work on all your skills...
Just my 2cp
