If you are talking about PL by 2-boxing I might have some useful info for you. I have been 2-boxing for a while and started out with a monk but that is a very one-dimensional character and the numerous monk nerfs didnt help either. Now I am duoing with a ranger. A much more interesting class to duo with but now he is 61 and it seems like its getting more and more obvious that he is not a warrior/shadowknight/paladin when tanking darkblues. I too play odd hours and being able to play my own tiny group has really added a lot of life to an otherwise rapidly-approaching-boring game.

darkpaw
Meelee's are definately the way to go if you are looking to PL and watch levels fly by. A shaman's spell line up naturally helps them much more then casters, although at higher levels some of the casters would make as good or better partners.

My suggestions would be a SK or Pally. I have doue'd almost my entire carrer with a monk, but they are useless as tanks in PoS/PoV. My little leveling group is now held up slowly by a lack of proper tank.

However, if you are not planning on playing them seriously 60+ and just want to have some fun a ranger or monk are still pretty nice choices. I like the extras a ranger adds to the mix and monks still have a nice balance of power/pulling capability although imho no where near as good as a well equiped SK now.

As far as the other part of your question, I don't really have any suggestions for soloing. I have always only solo'd as a last resort and have never been a big game hunter.
