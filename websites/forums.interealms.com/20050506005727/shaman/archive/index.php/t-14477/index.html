finaljustice
YOu spent dyes to make your armor look that color. Therefore, you have to spend dyes to make it the original color again. You can glimpse the true color by dyeing the armor neutral and then cancelling the dye, but perminent takes reagants.
Anjar
Set the color of the slots to white (255, 255, 255). That will remove all tinting.
dmb08
thanks anjar. that wont just dye it white though?
finaljustice
There is no white dye. All dye is like placing a Gel in front of a light. Put a red gel and everything looks redder.... Put a white (i.e. clear) gel and everything looks its proper color.
