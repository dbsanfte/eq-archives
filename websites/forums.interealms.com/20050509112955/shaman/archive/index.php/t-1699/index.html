Wolvenborn
I've done quite a few guardian wurms with the guild actually....and to me the hardest part is actually FINDING them up. I believe they are 8 hour spawns, so they are kind of hard to come by.

I'll try to answer your questions below. Every time I've fought them, I've used my monk....who is only lvl 52. I am usually the puller as well.....of which one time I had to pull across the entire zone (that was a fun one) :)

1) do guardian wurms summon?
-- yes, they do. you have to do some dmg to them first though. maybe ~300+ before they actually summon you. I was able to wack one for about 100, then pull it across the entire zone w/out being summoned.

2) can they be single pulled w/o (or even with) harmony?
-- They are usually single just walking around in the zone. you can just walk up to one, wack it, and run like mad :) Usually have to walk them back to camp though...they aren't all that fast and will lose interest if you get too far out of range.

3) can one group of 58-60's do it? or 2 groups? or 3 groups of 53-60s?
-- Well, put it this way....this is the latest group we did it with:
60 war
56 cler
45 cler
54 sham
52 monk
53 monk
That's it! We were really happy with being able to take one down with the above group. The key was the Shaman slow and the warriors Defensive Disc. You will probably need to chain CH the MT until a slow is landed.
4) Is it safe to set up at the vp door and pull to there? do they see invis and is that out of aggro range?
-- Hmm, just got to this question and assume that you are talking about the guardian wurms INSIDE VP.. oops :) they are the exact same thing as the ones outside from what I"ve heard, so hopefully some of this info will be helpful :)
5) Spell resists?
-- not sure, but our lvl 54 shammy never really has a problem landing a slow. Be prepared to die in about 5 secs once you DO land one though. hehe
6) Anyone confirm that they hit for 550?
-- yep, they sure do. Put it this way.....our lvl 60 warrior using his def disc went down in about 10 seconds because the cleric didn't get the first CH off in time. VERY VERY nasty hitters. The key word here is SLOW.....land that and you are golden. They do have a ton of HPs as well. Very fun fights.
