Seredith
Can't be done. All you can do is create a hotkey that tells the group / raid etc that the mob is slowed and hit it when you see the slow take hold.

I only call slows on raids, I dont do it in groups, the people I group with are competent players who invariably have spell messages for the group turned on and can see slows when they land, the only time I'll comment on slows in an exp group is when it won't land or we're in danger of wiping and the group needs some reassurance.

I never call that I'm about to slow or trying to slow something either, nothing more annoying than spamming people with irrelevant information over and over again.

I do call torpor and kraggs in groups due to the negative side effects / cast time, I dont hotkey quick heals.
