Zyphyr
Of the items you have listed, the Horse will help the most : Siting mana regen while standing/casting in outdoor zones is godly useful. And it will help you imediately upon purchasing it. Buying a cheap gluepot (8-9k version) is the best money any casting class can spend. As to using it with bear form : unfortunately, that is a no-go. Horses only work with the various models that you turn on under options before loging in (and with skeleton form).

Fungi staff isn't nearly as good as it used to be, since they nerfed/fixed the stacking bug on it. It will stack with the Ikky BP effect. It won't stack with the regen spells you can cast on yourself.
