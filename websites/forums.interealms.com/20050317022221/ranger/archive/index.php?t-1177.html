If you see a gent paying all kinds of rent on a flat that could flatten the Taj Mahal, ain't it grand, ain't it funny, but its better than even money that the guys only doing it for some doll.

Frank Sinatra as Nathan Detroit, running the oldest established permanent floating crap game in New York. Marlon Brando showing he could do it all, even kind of sing and dance, a little.

The musical was based on the works of a very talented columnist and author named Damon Runyan. If you have never seen the live show, watch for it next time it comes to town, community theater can usually do a good performance, high schools even have a chance at it. The movie is one of those rarities, almost as good as the live show.
