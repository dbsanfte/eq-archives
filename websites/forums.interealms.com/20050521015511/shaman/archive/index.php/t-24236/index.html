Thunderwolfjr
Actually, I believe that Manson's point is this; PD debuffs both poison and disease resists by 55 for 250 mana. Using Malos and MD you get the same poison and disease resist debuff of 55, but it costs you 550 mana and takes at least 9 secs to cast both vs. 3.5 secs for PD. So, if you are doing root/rot, and are not worried about slowing (so you don't need the MR debuff) then PD would be a better choice, as long as the -200 resist check is good enough to ensure that it will land.

So, the question remains, was he unlucky, or is there an issue with PD. If not, then PD should be the clear choice, unless you feel more comfortable slowing the mob just, in case.
