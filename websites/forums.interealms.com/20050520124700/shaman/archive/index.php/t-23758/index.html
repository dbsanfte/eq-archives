Sharok
I am against somehow 'trading' anything for the top 10 list (will we ever see ONE single thing on it done by the way? It's like 2 months now, sheesh).

The top 10 list is in most points a list about actual class issues. It's more a list of fixes than anything - no need to give up anything for a reasonable fix request. God knows, we will see little of it implemented anyway :( .

Having said that, there is a point about rebalancing mana costs against our canni ability.

Throwing out some completely theoretical numbers:
Let's say some average shaman (incl. canni V) is canniing for FT50, that is 500 mana per minute. Just to have a number. On the other hand mana costs (or mana effectivity for healing or dps) are calculated so, that shaman needs 300 mana more per minute to do his job.

So what is the point about canniing this 300 mana and then paying higher mana costs to begin with? Keeping us busy for zero advantage doesn't make a lot of sense imo.

A second point: the whole dependance between canni and our mana consumption is also decreasing our effectivity in short term fights. Sure we can canni back the spent mana relatively quick after the fight, but when sh.. hits the fan and everyone is chain casting to keep group/raid alive, our mana costs skyrocket. To state the obvious: Canni has zero use for us, when there is no time to canni ...

When i would have any confidence, SOE could do such changes (reduce canni AND mana costs) in a fair and professional manner, i would hesitantly agree with it.
