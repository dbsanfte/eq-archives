BlargX
Yes, windblade would be great; so would a fast 2hb. At 51, he can use the tae ew war maul, 33/36 and 65 hps; quick skill builder and decent weapon and cheap.

Frostbringer is nice, and procs very low level -- I think already at your level, can't remember.

I'd get more HP gear too. The collar of undead protection was a good suggestion.

Crystal spider eyes for face; some dex items would be nice to swap in when you're wanting to max offensive/weapons skills -- shissar scaled headpiece comes to mind, I think it has 12 or 15 dex. Helps to make up for dex minuses of the fungi.

But what's going to help most of all is doing damage faster, which is probably more a matter of haste than weapons, once you have at least reasonable weapons, which you more or less do, some better than others. If you're keeping him out of group to level, especially, cuz then you're waiting for him to do damage to 51% before you can sic the doggy on the mob or whatever. RBG is only 27% haste; there's plenty faster haste out there. RBB, at 31%, would be nice whenever you can afford it; it's about 20k to 25k more expensive than RBG, but you'll definitely see a difference.

Haste and hitpoints are going to be your gods overall with a melee, though hitpoints are a lot less vital when you have a high level shaman around to slow and heal for a twink. Then, the quality of weapons leaps up in importance.

But any melee has to save for something like an RBB or COF or COM just like a shaman has to save up for torpor(unless you get lucky and loot it -- not likely for most of us). If you aren't prioritizing haste now, do it soon -- and the sooner the better, really. You'd be surprised what a difference 5% makes in powerleveling.
