You are right people die to a lack of hp. However, groups die bexause a lack of mana to heal the ma or to slow the mob. Like I said both hp and mana of extreme importance.

But here is the example you gave:
A lvl 60 shaman with 170 wisdom, he has 2160 in mana. Now make this shaman have 215 wisdom (what I normally sit at). At 60 with a wisdom of 215, the shaman has 2607 in mana.

Now let us review some beneficial spells.

Can III 75hps:36-38 mana Cast time 1.25 sec
Can IV 148 hps:82 mana Cast time 2.50 sec Delay 3 seconds

Malo 350 mana Cast time 5 sec Delay 10 sec
Turgur's 250 mana Cast time 3 sec Delay 6 sec
Krag's Mending 400 mana Cast time 10 sec
Topor 200 mana Cast time 6 sec

Ok now for the scenario, shaman x is at a raid in Kael. Target is the statue of Rallos Zek. Ch cycle is every 3 seconds, and shaman x has been casted with druid x as the same ch number in the rotation. If shaman x had 2160 in mana he could ch 5.4 times withoutmoding or canning. However, with 2607 he could ch 6.52 times without moding or canning. Also keep in mind this shaman has 3500hps fully raid buffed. IMO mana is the more important factor.

Henora
Hlaan, level 60+ with torpor is completely different than level 55 without torpor. Raid situations are, overall very different than general exp groups.

There are certainly times when a large mana pool is a very good thing. Overall though, for shaman, HP is more important. If you are having trouble with your mana pool I might suggest the AA skill Canni5. You need to have a minimum amount of HP to be able to use that skill effectively.

I would argue that the goal of most shaman at the high end, 60+ is to increase their hp and mana, while maintaining some AC level that they are comfortable with... bah, and getting focus effects they like.

No where in that equation is people wanting to raise their wisdom. Wisdom raises naturally when you get better gear. You would be working against yourself and backward to a future goal, to start trying to get wisdom gear at this point.

Certainly you are free to disagree. Personally, I feel our spells in POP (with high mana costs and short durations) are balanced against the fact that we can regain mana quickly through the use Cannibalize 4 and our AA skill Cannibalize.
