New Shaman Heals on Test! (http://69.93.210.2:8080/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=20323&mode=linear&perpage=40&pagenumber=2)

Hey gang,

Sorry I didn’t post about this earlier. I was waiting for the numbers to be on test server, which I thought was going to be a lot sooner then today, but anyway…

The new spell parameters should put shaman heals on the same ratio that they were for Planes of Power spells:

Supernal Light: 2750
Nature’s Infusion: 1750
Tnarg’s Mending: 1300

Comparatively for HP/Second:
Supernal Light: 100%
Nature’s Infusion: 64%
Tnarg’s Mending: 47%

With the new Daluda:

Holy Light: 3275
Sylvan Infusion: 2100
Daluda’s Mending: 1550

for the same comparative HP/second as the PoP heals:
Supernal Light: 100%
Nature’s Infusion: 64%
Daluda's Mending: 47%

Likewise, the Quiescence and Breath of Trushar changes should make the HoT balance equal from PoP to Gates between Clerics and Shaman.

Still looking at the slow mitigation concerns in several gates zones.

Again, sorry I didn’t get this info out earlier, and for letting the bad mana cost on Breath of Trushar go live. There’s really no excuse for that blunder.

Brenlo does listen, and even responded to my email - please read. (http://69.93.210.2:8080/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=20190)

Just to expand a bit on what Brenlo said:

The healing adjustments that are in development on test will most likely include an increase in the power of shaman Heal over Time spells. The healing changes in general are aimed at significantly reducing the number of situations where a cleric is the only viable healer in 6 man group situations. While we certain maintain that clerics should be the best at healing, it is unfair to expect them to be the only adequate healer in a single group setting.

The frequency of slow immune NPCs in Gates is also being looked at, and there is the possibility of some adjustment in certain areas if we find it necessary. Although this will likely only apply to a few specific areas where the concentration is especially high.

-Ryan "the Spell Guy"
