You could buy Tae Ew bloods and have someone craft you a bp/legs/shield, or get a group to farm them.

Get a group and hit the crewmen in Nadox for a coral ring.

Camp the Nadox zoneline that leads to the Mines for a Bracelet of the Spiritcharmer. You can do solo, easilly. I did it solo at 53, and got a charm of the deceiver, too (adds 6sta 10cha 10dex when in bear form).

Chitinous belt (cost me around 800p)

Silver hammer earring (6k) or the glassy frog eye (5k) to replace forest loop.

Hobgoblin surprise - 300p. Use that as the turn in for the second part of the beginner's magic manual (for wisdom) or melee manual (for stamina) quests. Easy quest, just a little time consuming unless you grab someone to track.

Maybe replace your boots, but the AC on yours is decent.
