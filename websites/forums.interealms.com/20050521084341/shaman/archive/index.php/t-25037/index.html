Halrog
We attempted the event several months ago. We used 5 groups of Ssra/early-VT geared.

the key as I remember it was to engage the sacrifices with 2 groups and not aggro the upper warder (thereby not getting the aoe to go off) until you are ready to burn him. DPS was key - especially rogue/ranger melee dps without having to get spells cast time messed up. Once it was dead, these groups ran downstairs to help in the cages.

Downstairs used 3 groups 1 in each cage and the other split between. Chanters & necros mezzed the skelies, everyone assisted the MA in each cage. We had an MA just taunting the warder (was it mezzed? was a long time ago), he engaged as soon as it popped. I did quick heals on warder MA to make sure the c-heals landed.

Once that was dead, we headed down into another room and killed the mobs handily, though they were pretty high level (60+), where the door seemed to be bugged. First time we got to this point, the door never opened for us. Second time we got the door opened.

We went in and fiddled around with the bridge for about a half hour. I think we finally found that someone had to jump into the lava and click on the bridge or on a spot to get the bridge up (no lev in that zone).

Going across, a turn or two later we found a room of mobs. Having no map of this area, we were kind of winging it. That was where it went wrong. Lots of higher level mobs for that era, we got a bunch of them at once. I remember there being a door in the floor at that point, which we went through... and aggroed Khati & the whole room. I think if we had known the area better, we might have been able to get a good couple pulls out of there, but the monks might get deaded a few times. Well that was the end of our raid, it wasn't pretty... but we got to see it.

I think after that disappointment, we found that VT was a much more efficient use of our time as we geared up for killing Rallos Zek and getting into the EP's.
