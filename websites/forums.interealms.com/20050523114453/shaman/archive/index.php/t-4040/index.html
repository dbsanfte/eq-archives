Youichi
Get yourself to KFC in South Karana, and kill some aviaks.

Start with rooks, then move up to harriers and avocets in that order, just slow, dot and melee, and you'll be fine. they run when low on health giving you a chance to skillup weapons for no damage. Also try to hunt down the solo roamers, as these seem to carry more gems.

Also keep your DEX buff up at all times, it really helps with weapon skillups.

Try to max all your weapon skills, swap out that piercer for a 2HB. If you don't have a good 2HB, get up to the bazzar and do a search for 2HB shaman useable, and buy the best one you can.

Spell skills go up as you use them, so try to use which ever spell class is lowest, e.g. conjuration or evoke.
And practice your spells whilst waiting for the spires/your pet druid to turn up.

Hope that helps, Remember most importantly, HAVE FUN !
