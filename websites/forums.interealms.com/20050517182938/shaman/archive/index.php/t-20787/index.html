The Hyena Shaman
New description of Shaman Class from the test server!!!

Shamans serve as priests to the tribal races of Norrath, able to draw upon the power of the spirits and focus it for powerleveling alts. Shamans are primarily a magic-using class, able to wear chain armor and wield blunt weapons and spears.

Shamanic magic has two main focuses - Healing pets and buffing low level alts of real classes, and royally pissing off enemies with spells that have no other noticable effect. A shaman's enhancement spells don't do jack to squat, but light up numbers on your inventory in a pretty green color, and put our paintakingly hand-painted buff icons on your buff window. Shamans also are able to heal their allies for miniscule abounts, and have excellent health regeneration spells that couldn't save a gnat from a feather.

The weakening and damaging spells in the shaman's arsenal are tremendously effective in battle, provided the battle lasts half an hour and is in the snowy lands of Velious. The shaman attacks his enemies with diseases and poisons that cause massive aggro for very little damage, while using cold and ice magic to inflict damage directly to their metacarpals. A brutal line of crippling spells can appear to slow a monster's attacks, hamper their resistance to spells, and lower their physical prowess, but only on paper.

Shamans are a well-rounded class with strong abilities in both group and solo situations, but nothing truely useful for either. An experienced shaman can turn the tide of any battle, by gaining sufficient aggro and cannibalizing far down enough so that their deaths disrupt mob positioning and wipe the raid. The shaman is an excellent class for players who enjoy working in groups, but not working well. They are also the only class able to solo dragons in Velious!
