Hullk
Both of those items drop off of the Manaetic Prototypes. I think there are 3 of them total, and each have a chance of dropping them.

They prototypes aren't too difficult. A guild mini raid did them with less than 3 groups of 60-65. They hit for 450ish, quad, flurry, and enrage. They also do an AoE oil spray, I think it's the same AoE as the clockwork dragon. Curable with radient cure 1.

They also tend to banish the tanks into the other Manaetic prototype rooms, which can be quite annoying. Each Prototype room has 2-4 of the clockwork golems in the same room serving as guards.

They can be slowed relatively easily at 65, but it's only 'partially effective'. Hope that helps.
