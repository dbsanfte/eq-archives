I had a fun 3 days playing this game. I made a monk/ranger. Monk has many shaman like skills, and the ranger 2ndary allowed me to have a pet warthog to take with me...not the same as old wolfie, but fun.

Things that I immediately liked:
How they handle their buffs, mana regen, spell casting.
In game cut scenes for missions makes it a bit more involving/fun.
Pet warthog leveled up from killing, just like PC's.
Fast paced gameplay, little/no downtime, super regen when all nearbye monsters are dead. Cosentual PVP.
Travel is instant between "areas"....friends are just a click away

8 skill limit is a blessing and a curse.....hard to decide what role to take before going on a mission. Can't change on the fly once you have started. Makes for a lot of strategic decisions.
