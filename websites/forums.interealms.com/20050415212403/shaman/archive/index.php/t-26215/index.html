Just wanted to tell people, if some didn't already know, that you can get an improved healing V aug solo or duoed in Stillmoon Temple.

To get to the camp invis up and head North upon zoning in, it will be the temple directly in front of you. You will enter the first room with stairs run past this to the first tunnel straight ahead. It will split left and right, you want to head East (right) and follow that around to an open courtyard. The courtyard is filled with wandering drakes and stationary goblins.

The first room on the right is where to set up an easy camp. There is one drake in there and nothing else. The mobs mostly hit for about 200 to 300 hundred. Some drakes dispell and heal, so keep that in mind, but they are casters so really makes the fights easy. They do wander so the pulls are usually single. I believe these mobs are the PH for named, but can't confirm it.

Stillmoon Basalt Mage (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=17586)

The named mob is Stoneskin and can pop at any of the stationary mage spots. Just keep the whole room clear. The wait time between mobs can be a bit, I think the timer is the standard 20 minutes. He has two drops in his loot table I have seen.

I believe the average camp time is between 2 to 3 hours. Augment is 20% healing increase. Hope this little guide helps you save some of the crystals. I would say to effectively solo the named you would need to be at least EP geared and can be done easily in Time gear. If you don't have either just bring a friend.

Good luck great item for a shaman to have and fits on many pieces of armor.
