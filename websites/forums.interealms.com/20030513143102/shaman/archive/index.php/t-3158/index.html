If you can get 3 or 4 friends of level 45 - 50 or thereabouts, you can get the mantle yourself - it drops of a girrila thingy in Burning woods.

Inqubus
Tattered Mantle drops off Gullerback in Burning Woods. My regular group of a 50 Druid and 50 Rogue took him down twice last night. From what I could tell, he is Very magic resistant.

First time, I tried to Malosi and Turgur's him while Rogue meleed straight up, never got either to stick, was a tough fight. Second time, I just dotted him up with Plague, VoTS and EBolt and kited him around while the rouge BS'd and Druid dotted, was a quick and easy kill. Something to keep in mind if you're thinking about soloing him. He conned blue both times we killed him, and after they left, I saw a white con Gullerback.

So I don't think he would be soloable at all due to his MR, unless you're going to kite Druid style with no melee to help out.
