Doing this restore tells us that the problem is probably NOT a software problem in your computer's operating system.

If other programs are running fine -- email, word processing, internet browser, other games -- then you probably have a software problem in some Everquest file. Glauri's comment about copying all the EQ files from the good computer to the bad computer could be the easiest way to fix that.

If you are getting problems in other programs, you could have a hardware malfunction. DXdiag or your computer's own diagnostic software may be able to detect that; run those and check. Also, follow the suggestions about vacuuming out the dust and replugging your connections. Those are good general practice anyway, they won't hurt anything.
