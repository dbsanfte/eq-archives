I wore a Ceremonial BP until 57 or so when I got a fungi, the advantages of the regen outweighed the loss of ac for me personally. The equipment game is game of gaining items and then balancing them. A high ac item in one slot, a regen item in another. Choose your goal and work towards that, make a magelo profile then make different versions with items that you can realistically obtain and have a play to see what you can get.
