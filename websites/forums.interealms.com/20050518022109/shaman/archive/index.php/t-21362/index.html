Einnod
you got some fairly nice gear, but don't have many AAs.. for pvp, I suggest that you get spirit call, at least level 1. level 3 rocks. Free damage and interrupts.

When you fight a pally, chances are this will end in a draw, unless he gets a lucky root on ya and you can't get away. Keep yourself quiescenced, don't use dots that he can cure.

Ranger is kinda tough. Keep moving so his bow damage is mitigated. Dots will work on him. Start with mal decay and then VP. get close enough so he can't bow you, but far enough away that he can't hit you, then malo him and dot him. spirit call when he's at 50% and then chain nuke, pray that he can't channel heals thru the pets.

druid is alot the same as ranger. keep yourself quiescenced, dispell his dots/debuffs, but avoid doing dots. Melee him down with a high damage weapon. also use nullify magic(or pumices) on him so he loses his pot9 and self mana regen buff. Nuke if you're FM.

Mage, slow the pet, and just tank it and his nukes. Keep yourself quescenced and tnarg a lot till he's oom, then go to town. DoTs work very well here.

Wizzie, keep yourself over 75% so he doesn't manaburn you, or you're dead. Watch out for the lures followed by chain draught of thunder(dd/stun), followed by manaburn. Watch carefully at what he's casting, try to land tnarg right after his nuke lands. Chloroblast can also be your friend, it's a little quicker and can keep you above 75% health.

Good luck.

Weyalan
Unfortunately, you are probably going to get raped by just about anyone. If your opponent has goo dresists, you are toast. Even if there resists are not uber, you are probably toast anyway. However, here are a few tips that might help a little...and if your opponent is clueless, you stand a chance

1. Get yourself a minimum of 2 insta clickies, 3 is better and 4 is best (SoW booties dont count if you are fighting indoors). Expect to get dispelled. Use the instaclickies to fill first 4 slots then click 2 off to give you your dispell slots, but still retain 2 in case of cancel / annul magic

2. Make yourself some of the 33 point damage shield potions for use against melee classes who are dumb enough to use 2 weapons. Ideally get JB BP for pre buffing

3. Make yourself stinging wort potions - the more the better

4. Expect to be unable to stick any magic based spells.

5. Cast annul magic on your opponent as regularly as you can.

6. Get the divine aura totem thingy from HHP

7. Dont bother trying to kill anyone fast - expect to take a long time to kill anyone

8. Against casters, try to run them out of mana - you are the king of mana regen, so if you can get them oom, you will win.

9. Do get spirit call - as high level of spirit cal as you can

10. The AoE level 64 posion spell (Tears of Saryn?) is strangely easy to stick after Malos in PvP (whereas it is resisted like crazy on mobs)

11. Get plenty of mage summoned pet stuff - muzzles, belts and proccing weapons

12. People often have weaker disease resist than other resists
