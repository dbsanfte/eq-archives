Chutoi
Biggus, there used to be a bug whereby resists over 255 rolled over. Thus, a resist rate of 256 was equal to a resist rate of 1. Needless to say, when this came to light, they changed it pretty quickly.

You do get to a point of dimishing returns on resists, though. In my experience, most resists over 250 work about as well as they are going to work. I haven't noticed myself resisting anything more around 300 that I didn't already resist at 255, though, of course, this is very hard to check and I'm only operating on my observations.

The bad news is that most big mobs (heck, even yard trash in ubermob zones) tends to be higher level than we are anyway. Between that, and the fact that VI has a tendency to code a lot of their AoEs at lure resist levels makes even obscene resist levels less effective. Annoying, but there it is.

Pardon my morning rambling! :)
