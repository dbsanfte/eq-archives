Your epic weapon is a spear, so by all means keep piercing up. There are also a lot of nice weapons for mid-to-high level shaman that are either 1hb or 2hb, so I worked to keep those up as well (see Blight, or Poison Wind Censer).

Keeping 2hb up is easy--go to Lake Rathe, get the Abandoned Shovel, and when you are in groups, just whack at the mobs--you won't do any damage, but your 2hb skill will go up pretty quickly. 1hb--just find the fastest 1hb you can get (damage is irrelevant) and do the same.

The experience necessary to go from Level 1 to 10 was recently reduced, so there's no way you're going to keep all your melee skills up during those levels. Eventually, especially if you solo a lot, all three weapon skills (and maybe HtH, if you're insane) can be maxed out.

I'd say which weapon skills to focus on depends on two things: (a) whether or not you'll be soloing, and (b) whether or not you're twinked. 2H Blunt is great when soloing, and there are a number of good staffs and the like. If you're grouped, a one-handed weapon with a shield is best for the extra AC and any stats conferred by the two slots. The reason why I brought up twinking is that, if you're untwinked, it's really difficult to find good stat-boosting items in the low/mid levels and you may therefore want to focus more on 2H Blunt. Also, low-level Piercing weapons are awful.

Another thing to consider is that Piercing is generally the easiest to raise at a later date, because of the lower delay on Piercing weapons, while 2H Blunt can be difficult because most of the weapons are slow.

Just try to always have one maxed (your best weapon) and the other two within a reasonable range of max, and you should be okay.
