Thank you for visiting the KOE Recruiting page

Looking for a full time raiding guild? Then this is not the guild you are looking for. Instead, try this one http://www.circleofeternity.org/

The majority of KOE is comprised of mature gamers who have RL responsiblities but still find the time to log on, kill stuff, and have a great time together. We average 2 to 3 raid events per week with smaller events in between dependent on circumstances.

While the majority of us live in North America, across all time zones, we have a players in Europe and Australia. You can find keepers on line 24/7.
We do not seek specific classes but we do welcome new people into our family of friends.

We wear our tag with pride and do not grant it lightly. As such, KoE uses a recruit process.

To become a KoE recruit, players must meet the following criteria:

The player must be at least Level 55
The player must be a registered member of this message board (please complete the email confirmation)
A KoE guild member must request recruit status for the player.

To become a member of KoE, players must meet or exceed the following criteria:

The player is a current KoE Recruit
The player is at least Level 59
The player must demonstrate skill in their class and the ability to perform in a raid environment
The player must display social and communication skills compatible with KoE values and standards
The player must read, understand and agree to compy with our charter http://www.interealms.com/koe/charter.html
The player must obtain a KoE sponser

It is not easy to become a keeper, but the journey is well worth the efforts.
Thank you for your time and interest in Keepers of the Elements
