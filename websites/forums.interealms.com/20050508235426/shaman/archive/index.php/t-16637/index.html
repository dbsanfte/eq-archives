Kriel Darkbane
Are you going to be mostly solo'ing your way to 65 or grouping? If you're grouping, I think there is a huge advantage for the races with no exp penalty. Whether you have frontal stun immunity, or higher AC, or even regeneration, is going to have very little effect on how fast your GROUP earns exp - which will determine how fast you gain exp. Your regeneration or stun immunity - or JBB for that matter - won't give your group the ability to kill 20% more mobs and earn you 20% more exp. You will take longer to make L 65 due to your exp penalty.

Now, solo'ing, that's a different question. Will your racial advantages make up for the 20% experience penalty? As an iksar, I'd say the answer is definitely no. Or, at least, you'll level slower than the troll. A JBB at L 45 is HUGE. It remains huge at least to the mid 50's. My shaman is 58 now, and it's definitely not what it was any more. Still useful, but not godlike any more. I do far more DOT damage than I do nuke damage now, when solo'ing. But from 45-52, solo'ing with most of the damage coming from the JBB was the fastest thing possible. So an iksar has no real advantage over the troll. JBB >> some extra AC for your overall leveling.

But that still doesn't answer the question - is the extra regeneration a troll gets worth the 20% exp penalty over a frog or a vah'shir? I discount the ogre entirely, as frontal stun immunity will NOT get you exp 15% faster. I'm not sure if the regeneration is worth the penalty when solo'ing or not. But I'm sure it's not if you're going to group a lot.

Now, having said all that: that's what you want to consider if your goal is to get to 65 as fast as possible. That's an entirely DIFFERENT question from "what race makes the best shaman?" The races are balanced enough, especially considering the exp penalties, that a different race is best for different circumstances.

Do you want the most powerful shaman when you're L 65 and uber, no matter how long it takes? I'd say ogre, for the stun immunity. Do you want the shaman who'll be L 65 first? I'd say Vah'shir or frog, due to no exp penalty. Do you want the shaman who'll have the most mana regen and least downtime? Then do Iksar or troll. If you're a twink and know you'll have a JBB young, go troll. If you're going to have to earn your JBB by L 45 to have one at L 45, then go iksar, as you won't have one ANYWAY, unless you spend several levels worth of exp time farming plat to buy one. And in that case you could be L 49 in the same playtime you would need to get 45 and the JBB, and be at least as far ahead in the game.

But you've already decided to keep the iksar, so go for it. The real key is to pick the race you like the most, so you won't regret it later on, when you're looking at that ugly assed troll you picked cause he was "best". No offense to those who love their trolls. ;)
