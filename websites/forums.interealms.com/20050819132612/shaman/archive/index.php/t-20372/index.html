Lohknar
With Ryan checking our boards, I wanted to post something about two spells that are not working properly right now:

Tiny Companion: This is supposed to shrink our pet, but it does nothing to him. We can't even shrink our pet using the regular shrink spell. This bug has been around for a long time, and shaman have just given up on it ever getting fixed. It's probably an easy fix too, since every other pet class can shrink their pet.

Tears of Saryn and Torrent of Poison: This is a wierd one. Both of these spells behave in the same way.
- For 1 target in AE, it does 3 rounds of damage.
- For 2 targets in AE, it does 2 rounds of damage to each target.
- For 3 targets in AE, it does 1 round of damage to each target, and one extra round to one of the three targets. I'm not sure how it decides which of the three targets gets the extra round.
- For 4 targets in AE, it does 1 round of damage to each target.
- For anything greater than 5 targets in the AE it choses 4 and behaves like the 4 target rule.

I really believe since these are AE Rain DD Spells, it should do 3 rounds of damage to all mobs that get hit. In other words, if there are 4 mobs it should do 3 rounds of damage to all 4 mobs which is 12 rounds. Which is exactly how the Wizard Rain DDs work. Why do our Rains work differently?

Post any more bugs that you know of, and maybe we can get them fixed.

The 4 target rule goes back to the early days of the initial release. Unlimited rains were WAY over powered for us. They also had the annoying tendency to target your own pets and generate instant death causing agro.

The rules for them are probably buried deep in the code and are a special case just for that spell type. Changing it would probably be a major pain. And as far as I know, shamans are the only people that actually have Rains as their major AoE attack.

It might be a fun thing to see Rains adjusted to be a more powerful spell for us, but it's hardly going to be high on anybody's list.

A more consistent behavior would be nice. Like a doing 4 waves to a single target so you always get 4 hits per cast.

Of course, removing the old 'shaman wave' rules and just letting us play by AoE rules would be fun... I'd bring along a cleric to Rezz you after each cast though, if I were you. :)
