Yes that will work perfectly. Here's why:

I'm 99% certain the games works from top to bottom then left to right when it comes to food consumption:
1 5
2 6
3 7
4 8

This is true both for your inventory slots, and then again for items within containers in those inventory slots. If you have a 10-slot container it's:
1 6
2 7
3 8
4 9
5 10

You can put your food and drink anywhere, and you will still get the stat bonuses for it. You could put it in slots 9 and 10 of a ten slot container that's in your 8th inventory slot, and you would still get the stat boosts. However, if any other food or drink is ahead (say you forage a root and drop it in a bag in inventory slot 4) you will get the stat boosts of that item instead (which for a root is absolutely nothing). So if you want to be certain of getting the stat boosts, just put your food and drink in slots 1 and 2 of a bag in inventory slot 1 (it doesn't matter whether the food or drink comes first).

As others have pointed out, you can save money by buying rations and milk or some other cheap food and drink, putting them in an inventory slot, right-click eating/drinking them until you are full, and then moving them back in to your inventory somewhere behind your stat food. This way they won't contribute their (non-existant) stat bonuses, but they will prevent you from consuming your expensive stat food. However, with Halas meat pies selling as cheap as they do now, I don't even bother with force-feeding anymore.
