Grouping/Lesser content

This gets into the "ideal group" type debate. Which, of course, depends on the encounter/content. In general though, the raid top 5 = the group top 5.

Tank: Normal grouping, I'd prefer a Pally to other tanks, simple because the tank has guaranteed instant aggro. However, for vxed/tipt level, I'd prefer a warrior still. Defensive discs are unmatched.

Healer: Again, if you want this role filled, you can't go wrong with a cleric. But, if the content isn't hard, I too would prefer a Druid for the greater utility.

CC/Slow: If you don't require backup healing, OR, if you require tremendous dps, Enchanter is the only choice here.

Pulling/Mob aquistion: For tracking+pulling+utility, again, Bard is better in a group than a monk or rogue. The bard, while not as much dps, provides way to much utility to not have one in the group. However, if you don't need that utility, then of course monk or ranger is better for added dps.

DPS: In grouping situations, it all depends on the character AA and gear. There have been proven log posts of necro's peaking at 500 dps, but they are rare. Wizards and Rogues are the same way. I can't really pick a top dps position for slots 5+6 because there are too many variables. Usually I'll pick the last couple spots based on what other abilities the group needs. Do we have doors to pick? Do we need evac? Do we need FD backup Rez?
