At the zone in to the warrens from Paineel is a male erudite NPC , forgot his name ,but anyways when hailed he asks you if you are looking for work. Follow the quest dialogue key words and on the last phrase you have to say either I will help the merchants or i will preform the mercenary service .I forgot which one or the other though.
Anyways though he asks for 2 badges (actually insignias) from each of the 5 ships the trolls use to raid the mercahnts at sea.And gives you a 10 slot container to place them into . These 5 ships are in Dulak Harbor (considering the boats are always there I dont see how they can get much raiding done)
Again I didnt memorize the names exactly but its something along the lines of the Stormwave , The Lady Dirulia , Doldorans Run , Oceancrasher , and Scornwind. Kill the mobs that share the boats name , IE ~ A Scornwind Templar , A Stormwave deck hand , etc etc.
The mobs on each boat vary in level Stormwave has the lowest level mobs then progressively the Lady Dirulia , Doldorans Run, Oceancrasher , and Scornwind with the highest level mobs on it a good deal of the scornwind mobs were blue at 61 Oceancrasher and Doldorans Run mobs were all lt blue at 61 and the final two ships were all green at 61.
Anyways kill kill kill until you recieve an insignia bearing the ships name . A Scornwind Insignia , Oceancrasher insignia etc etc. These are NO DROP items. Youll need 2 from each ship average seems to be about 1:40 pirates for insignia. Although theyll also rarely drop a diamond or blue diamond to make the camp a little less painful along with the deepwater insignias used in paly spell quests i believe.
After youve finally collected 2 insignias from each ship place them in the box you were given and combine .The box should ID as a sealed crate.Then give the sealed box back to the erudite in paineel at the warrens zone line.
For this youll recieve :
Symbol of Loyalty to the People
4ac
10 charisma
30 hps/mana
12 save disease / poison
Magic , Lore , No drop
Range slot
all races all classes

didnt say this quest was worthwhile just that its out there. =p
