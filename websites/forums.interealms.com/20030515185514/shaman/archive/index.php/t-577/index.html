3. Rings. Get a Shadel Bandit ring and have the best of both worlds

4. Wrists. That supple scale armband sux the big bikkie. Even a Jarsarth bracer is heaps better. Get Jarjar or just about anything else for that matter. Personally, I would be spending heaps of time grouping in the arena in KD and getting plent of the corroded chain drops

6. Back. Cloak of the Icebear or even a totemic cloak might be better than this.

7. Belt. Cant you get some belt stuff from VL?

8. Legs. Kromriff Military leggings might be marginally better, and are easy, but likeI say, only marginal

9. Boots. You do have the dire wolf booties dont you?

our tactic was as follows:

Drood = sit down and look at book with all wisdom gear on (i.e. poor man's cleric)

Rogue = pet number 1

Shaman pet = pet number 2

Shaman = main tank (a.k.a. the olympic runner)

Shaman pulls with best debuff and trys to channel a slow thru first hit(s) then Shaman takes off running in a big circle around druid

Guller will aggro on shaman (they dont like malxxx debuff and togors much) and chase shaman, but should not actually be able to catch you. Rogue and pet run along behind guller chewing his ass off or nipping at his heals

Guller WILL turn on Rogue (pet "no taunt"), in which case Rogue starts running in big circles around druid, shamn stops and as Rogue runs past lets rip with another dot. Usually this will be enough to get aggro back on shammy who can then run around again, if not, let the rogue run another lap and then launch another dot and then you WILL get the aggro back...


...shaman continues running around druid with pet and rogue chewing Gullerbackside...when Guller aggroes to Rogue repeat as above....

...Dead Gullerback

So, the druid is there to keep everyone healed and to read their book heh heh

It sounds complicated, but it isn't particularly hard really once you have got it down pat (but, yes, we did ALL die the first time we tried it!)

Sow him, haste him.
Pull gullerback with insid decay (lvl 54? been a while)
Channel plague.
Run around a bit to let regrowth build you back up in hp, he hits hard.
Almost outrun him, stop, keep trying to cast eb on him til it sticks.
Start running again.
When you can, stop and canni2 a coupla times.
When EB wears off, repeat line 6.
When plague wears off, repeat line 6 with plague.
Run over to pet.
Pet will attack cause he's already beaten on you while you were channeling your first plague.
Run round in circles (all this near a wall so you reduce adds. If you get an add, root it and run away - lazy agro so powa). Woofer will chew on guller. Make sure pet no-taunt thing, and if he by chance draws agro, cool. you can EB him again without getting beaten on.
