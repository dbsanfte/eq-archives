DarkPhase
I use my gear to make money in this kinda way:

1. I buy something nice and pricey that I can use and has high demand for as low as I can get it for.

2. Constantly auction the high priced items I use for more than I got them for. If I can't sell it I can still use it :)

3. If I sell I repeat all the steps. This way I end up with lots of money in the bank and the spells I need before I'm 60.

I'm currently level 58 and have Fungi, JBB, Lodi Shield, Malo and Torpor. All of that came from nice trades.
My best trade so far was OOiV for FT3 boots. Got OOiV for 28k and sold FT3 boots for 43k :)

Trades like this can get you rich, but keep in mind it has to be high demand items. But I never dared to trade Fungi and JBB in the same manner. They are just too darn useful for that.

Hope my tips helps for future shamans in need of spells. Start your search early and you will not regret it.
