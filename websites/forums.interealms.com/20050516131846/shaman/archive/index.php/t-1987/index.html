Welcome back Talamay! Long time no seeum u on these forums. Glad to have one of the old guard back. :D

The usual path to AA skills has been
1) regen x 3
2) run x 3
3) Mental Clarity x 3 (takes all 12 points of arch skill . . . only worth it cause it's a prerequisite for Canni5.)
4) and CANNI5

After that whatever u wanna do will probably help you equally. The path I suggested is the "generally agreed upon" path to the best and most notable skill Canni5.

Other notables are Spell Casting Reinforcement, which can make Torpor last one tick or two ticks longer than normal, depending on how many damn points you put in it. 21 points total needed to max this at 50% longer buffs. *wince* However, only 2 AA points are needed to make Torpor last that extra tick almost every time.

Spell Casting Mastery is supposed to be very nice once you max it (12 points), lowering the costs of spells by about 30% on average.

Channeling also might be good, if they fixed it, which I dunno if they have or what.

Mass Group Buff makes yer guild love you, not much else to say about it tho. Pisses off clerics tho . . . they got that uber self buff it overwrites . . . tuff **** tho haha. MGB is too expensive at 9 points tho for what it does. I'll probably get it anyway just for a boost in the guild faction area.

Um, o, check out the new CT! It's loads of fun, but highly dangerous. That extra hp/stam u like will help u a lot there, as things hit hard and fast. New lewt from the zone is impressive as well, or some of it is anyway. Mostly tho, the impressive thing is the exp hits. In a full group of lvl 60's I get a quarter blue per kill. In a hard driving fast killing group that makes for an AA point every 4 hours approx.

Newbies are even more fun to twink and level these days. Droods get succor at level 19 or something. Pallies get exp rezzes starting at lvl 30. Clerics get additional exp rezzes of varying percentage, 0% rez starts at like lvl 12 or something. Pally 0% rez is at lvl 22. Oh and lvl 55 pallies can now cast 75% rez, which is quite serviceable and worth having over trying to find a cleric for an hour or whatever.

newb casters no longer have to med while looking at a book. 1-10 lvl chars respawn after dying fully clothed with all their equipment. <== abusable.

Epic can still be factioned to complete all but the tear (which can still be MQed by an ally faction shaman).

That's all i can think of atm. Hope i have enlightened you in the manner in which you requested.
