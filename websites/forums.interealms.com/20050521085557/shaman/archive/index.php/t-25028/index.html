Hello all,

A while back an NPC was added to the Plane of Knowledge. This NPC Kraylor Nalsiv, expresses an interest in rare items and artifacts. Show him your epic and he will give you a flag that the PC Title system will look at when determining which titles you are allowed to have.

This will prevent the problem the caused your title to disappear when you die, or have your Epic in the bank etc...


Unfortunately there is a downside to the system currently, you will need to remove all augments from your epics
before you hand them over.
There are plans to change the functionality of that NPC in the near future, but currently we do not have an ETA on when this will be completed.
