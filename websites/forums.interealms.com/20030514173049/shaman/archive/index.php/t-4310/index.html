Xatrei
heheh - i used to 2box down there, but the place gets so boring. I used this spot to level through part of 58, all of 59, 2 golds into 60, and then my first 10 aa points. During that time, my necro went from 54 to 57 and picked up 3 aa's. This place is insanely boring, but awesome experience. A couple of notes:

if you die, you're screwed. it's very difficult to get a rez in this zone unless you happen to catch a vox raid or BST epic raid as they're moving through. I see lots of people in that spot with pocket clerics. I've only been able to get a rez one time down there after a LD mishap, and that was from a guildy of my wife's that lives down there with his mage and cleric. I also burned through about 6 essense emeralds on my necro providing rezes after various mishaps (first time i encountered the ice witch after she was added for example).

things are on a roughly 20 minute timer (i've never kept an exact count). with a good partner or when 2 boxing my necro, I could hold between 15 and all of them down before repops started, depending on the partner. solo, depending on level and the spells I had available, I could do 6 to 10 before repops began.

when travelling to this spot, be careful. occasionally some of the giants that you have to pass will see invis. I usually nose around the corner to target them, con them to see if they see me. if they do, i'll pull them and then move through. 80% of the time, however, you can safely move through the area without being seen.

you can pull singles MOST of the time at 57, but you will get an occasional add until 58. the level range for the bears / spiders / wolves is 43 to 47 i believe.

these mobs have fairly hefty cold resistance. debuffing them with malosini helps, but you'll still get lots of partial resists (i never really tracked it, but i'd say somewhere around 60% of max jbb damage over all). on occasions where i was by myself down there, and not 2boxing I made a pet, buffed him with haste and pulled a mob with slow. epic dot followed by a disease dot (eventually pox once i got it). then jbb blasted away while the pet chewed on him. I'd canni a few times along the way to recover from the mana expenditure, and cblast / torpor myself as needed.

most of the spawn locations in the area will pop EITHER one or two mobs. making the number of mobs per cycle variable between 13 and 22, not including the ice witch or the roamer that pops at the top of the ladders leading back up to vox's lair.

this is a very popular spot for rangers since they can fearkite VERY well in this spot. AA farming druids like it to since they can DC a bear and go to town.

pulling the mobs near the ladders can be tricky with the addition of the ice witch. one of them tends to stay in one spot, the other roams. pull the one that stays close to it's spawn point first. then try to catch the roamer when he's as far away from the witch as possible. most of the time, he's within aggro range of the witch, so you'll get her too unless you time your pull with when he wanders away from her a bit.

at 58 you occasionally get a light blue. at 59 i'd say about 25% of them are light blue. at 60 the number seems to fluctuate around 50%, depending on how lucky you are with the pops. just as with the mobs in high keep, the light blues make for some tasty exp. the zone has a decent ZEM, and the mobs die fairly easy.
