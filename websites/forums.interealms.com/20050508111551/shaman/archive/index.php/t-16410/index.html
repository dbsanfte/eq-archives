Kianor
I haven't done a lot of LDoN dungeons yet, so I'm mostly asking what people's experiences with various themes are.

I've tried 3 Mistmoore and 1 Rujarkian theme dungeon, all at "normal" level.

The first Mistmoore one was "kill 55 mobs in 90 minutes" which a group of monk, rogue, shaman, druid, cleric, wizard did easily, with 40 minutes to spare. We explored a bit after that and found a miniboss to kill.

The next two Mistmoore ones I did were Assassination ones. We had warrior, paladin, necro, shaman, cleric, bard, all 65, for the first and the paladin was replaced with a druid for the second. Both times we succeeded easily, with 40 minutes to spare the first time, and around 60 the second time, and used the extra time to go kill any minis that were up.

For those who haven't done one, the assassination adventures involve killing 60% of the mobs in a dungeon, at which point the boss spawns. You get emotes every few minutes indicating in which direction he is, so a tracker isn't necessary (although desireable).

Because we wanted to try something different (and I wanted to collect Rujarkian points too), we went to EC for a new challenge. Bear in mind that this was the same group that did a Mistmoore assassination in 30 minutes. We were assigned another assassination adventure, so we went for it.

We found the Rujarkian adventure was *much* harder than the Mistmoore one. We were expecting to find things a little harder, simply because as a group we were well suited to undead and these were alive. However, the mobs hit harder, they had more HP, and seemed to be more resistant.

They also had a larger agro range, and were more likely to come in big bunches (up to 8 at a time). We ended up having to pull with FD split or lull where previously we'd mostly pulled 2/3 and worked with them. We had several deaths in the process (due to a 6 pull), but were getting into the swing of things and getting used to it being harder.

Whether or not we would have succeeded at that point is uncertain, for I crashed out and got bugged so I couldn't log my character back in for 30 minutes (crashed out repeatedly, until I figured a way around).

We failed, but carried on for the lesser reward of beating it within the 30 extra minutes. We did this within about 10 minutes, so I believe that had I not been bugged we could have done it in the time, though it would have been close.

Anyway, my point is this: even taking into account necro/cleric being suited to undead, etc, the Rujarkian adventure seemed to be at least twice as hard as an equivalent Mistmoore one with exactly the same group. Has anyone else had similar experiences?

If this is typical, unless they tweak it somewhat, I can see people wanting to go to the easier themes, which will be unfortunate for those of us who need spells in the harder ones.
