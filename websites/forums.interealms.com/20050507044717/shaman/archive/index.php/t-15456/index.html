Bigmeeno
No, put the Halas 10 lb meat pie in your bags as the next item you will eat. Then gorge (manually eat) your cheaper food, like patty melts. Since you force fed yourself, the meat pie wont be eaten for some long period of time.

You only get stats from food/drink if the stat food/drink is the next item to be eaten by the automatic food/drink consumption system.

Innate Metabolism 3 reduces your need for food and drink by 50%. When sitting on a mount, that basically takes you back to what you used up before you had a mount without Innate Metabolism. With Innate Metabolism 3 and not on a mount, your food and drink will last twice as long.

When I first got my mount, I thought I was going to go bankrupt buying food. Dropped 3 points into Innate Metabolism, and worry about it about once a week now on my shaman, and every couple weeks on my rogue.
