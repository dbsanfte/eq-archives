I suggest you solo in the SolRo tower for ornate. On my server it's usually empty, might be different on yours.
When you zone in, you'll notice that there are 2 roaming mobs. You can single pull them easily. Just wait till one walks away and get the other(the assist and aggro range is fairly big). You need an ER(Extended Range) item to cast/pull and be outside of the aggro range(that's if you are level 65). They are fast, have fairly high MR(slow should stick 95% of the time after Malos) and are immune to movement altering spells like root and snare.
Pull with Malos(don't forget your ER item or they will aggro before you start casting), run back to the zone in wall while spell gems pop back up and slow it. Use HoTs for healing and Tnarg's/Daluda's for emergencies(you might get low sometimes). The mobs have around 40 k HPs, so use DoTs and pet.

The respawn is fairly long, so you will have to split the pairs to the sides sooner or later and here's how to do it:

Bind outside at the stones in PoT. Zone in and pull one of the 2 with Malos, both will come. Run in circles and through the middle intersection to make them split(pathing will allow you to split them depending on how skilled you are in positioning yourself).
Once they are somehow split, move them as far as possible from their spawn point(without aggroing the other pair on the opposite side) and gate(you want them to take as long as possible to reset). Zone in immediatelöy and grab one. If by a mistake both of them reaggro, just try to split them again and gate.

Rinse and repeat till you get one solo.

Exp is decent. The best before elementals.

Tsuyoi
There are several reasons SolRo isn't packed:

0) You have to be flagged to get in
1) Mobs cannot be snared or rooted
2) Ornate drops are uncommon
3) No ZO, you have to gate
4) XP is poor for the effort you put into it

The ZI and courtyard areas have the protectors that are all level 65, have huge agro ranges and too many HP. Once you get away from the ZI you will have to split pairs on almost every pull. For the time invested to solo here you would probably walk away with more plat and bazaar loot from Droga.

Your best bet for farming here is to get into the Xuzl caves. I'm not sure how easy it is to solo, but it is very common to find people boxing here. Spend enough time here and you very likely can follow a guild who is going to take out Xuzl. Respawn is an hour so you can follow them up quite a bit after they have moved through. Your camping spot is right where you are when you use the cup to click up.

From my time here I expect a full group to get an ornate drop every hour. That is chain pulling and killing fast. So, expect that a soloer will see on average 1 piece every 6+ hours. I have not done the Xuzl camp myself, so the drop rates may be slightly different up there.

Hope that helps,
