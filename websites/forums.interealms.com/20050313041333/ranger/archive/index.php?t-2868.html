Some personal experience regarding skilling up on mithril arrows:

Frankly, I found it impractical, at least in the 202-230 range.

First, you have to do a LOT of legwork running around getting mithril and doing the smithing combines. I already had more than enough smithing skill so that wasn't the issue, but if you don't that's one more factor to work in.

Second, it's expensive. You're blowing in the range of 15-18pp per combine. Compare that to doing rough darkwood bows, where I was blowing 25pp per combine on success (and 225 on failure). Yes, it seems like less, but it's a time vs money thing. I can stand in Shadowhaven and do darkwoods like mad, but it takes a couple hours to set up for some stacks of mithril.

Third, I found the skill up rates to be terrible. No I didn't keep statistics. But the failure rates were very high, and the skill ups few. I have heard (but not confirmed) that you stand better chance of a skill up on a success than a failure, so these are not a good idea from a skill up perspective for low 200's fletching.
