Calabar
Yes, East Karana is one of the best spots for spider silks. The only problem is the majority of players needing spider silks know it too. So the poor spiders can be hunted to extinction. If this happens start killing the snakes, hounds and lions to get the spiders to spawn again. You can sell the snake eggs, wolf meat and HQ, MQ and LQ cat pelts in the bazaar too, so it’s not even a waste of time.

The problem with hunting silks in Shar Vahl is that they are shade silks, which are not the same as regular spiderling silks. You cannot use them to make leather padding from low quality wolf, cat or bear hides. So these will not sell as well as normal spiderling silks. However, you can use them to make leather padding from low quality rockhopper hides, as can normal spiderling silks. So if you have a lot of rockhopper hides in the bank you can use the shade silk to make padding and sell that in the bazaar.
