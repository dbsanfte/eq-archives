With all the foci added to the game, I can't help but wonder if the best way to balance casters is to allow them disciplines similar to what the melee folk get.

Short-duration (1-2 mins), high-recycle time "uber" effects would give us the flexibility to complete with some of the amazing stunts that our peers are doing now.

They'd there'd be some overlap amongst the classes, but each class would get something unique, too.

For example, as shamen:

Gift of the Spirits: Spells take good percentage less mana
Haste of the Spirits: Cast much faster
Rage of the spirits: Hit much harder
Spirits of Decay: Dots, do more per tick
... and the like.

I find it amazing what the meleers can do innately that we need to rely on items to approach.
