Basically I would just go to the bazaar and do searches on each slot for hp. Your wisdom is overly nice already so you could stand to lose some of it for more hp.

Here are a couple of specific suggestions:

For next to no cost I would replace your Imbued Ivory golden earring with a black sapphire electrum earring, gaining 35hp and losing almost no mana.

The shoulders strike me as weak, and would probably look for dragonwing mantles, might find a good deal on one, the +45hp ones and lower are usually fairly cheap.

You could get a better BP for fairly cheap, I still use (/ugh) a Polished Tin Mail shirt but it has suited me fine, would give you 5 more wis but more importantly 25hp and 7sta. Optionally you could save a little more money and upgrade to a Tae Ew BP (running around 10k I think) which would give you even more hp and a lot more ac.

Replacing those 3 slots (weakest on you in my opionion) with my suggestions would run about 6k on Prexus and would get you around 125 more hp, about 10 more ac and a little less mana but you are hardly hurting there imo.
