KEI of course is C3. It is a 60 enchanter spell that lasts for 150 minutes, gives you 14 mana per tick regen, 25 wis/int, and increases your mana pool by 250.

Tranquility would be considered C4. It is a 63 enchanter spell that lasts for 80 minutes, gives you 16 mana per tick regen, 26 wis/int, and increases your mana pool by 260.

Voice of Quellious (VoQ) would be considered C5. It is a 65 enchanter spell that lasts for 80 minutes, gives you 18 mana per tick regen, 28 wis/int, and increases your mana pool by 275.
