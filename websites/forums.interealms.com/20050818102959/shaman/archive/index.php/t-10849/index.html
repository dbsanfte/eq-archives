In the mid 50s you are likely doing pop. I have yet to notice a difference from 800 to 900 ac. I am told if you cross the 1000 ac threshold there is a big difference. I was doing my shammy/warrior in kael arena one day, pulled a veteren, slowed it and proceeded to cann 5, and then torpor, after which, I sat the shaman down.

Right after my shaman sat, I took a rampage from the vet....203, 203, 85. My shaman lived for one reason, and one reason only...the fact that i had more than 500hp left over after I cann 5'd. AC doesn't help in that situation, and wisdom doesn't help in that situation.

AC is damned useful, but HP is useful in many many more ways...AE's, nukes, cannabalize, hits you can't avoid, etc. HP is the way to go. Follow it up with AC, and I think you're going to have a hard time running out of mana, which makes wisdom kinda pointless.
