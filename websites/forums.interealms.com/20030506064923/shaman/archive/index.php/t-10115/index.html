Two good CoV quests you can do with one group or less are the Circlet of Falinkan and the Talisman of Benevolence. There's lots of posts on the board on those two. The other common suggestion would be the Coldain Military Wristguard that you could solo at your level.

Other than Jaruden's suggestions which are all good, you might want to upgrade your belt at some point for one with some hp. Barbed Rubicite Belt would be an xp spot, Artificer's and Girdle of Mithaniel are other alternatives but costly.
