The first level of Grieg's End off of Dawnshroud Peaks has been empty each time I've been there with a group of friends (or near group, because we occasionally have a slot open). We don't really have a typical group makeup because we often think that nearly any group is a good group.

Casting Malosi and Turgur's Insects combo rarely works in Grieg's in my experience. That is, neither has a good chance of landing at all and I'm likely to find myself OOM for trying. But the disease slow, Plague of Insects, seems to land a good 90-95% of the time without trouble (and is a darn sight faster to cast). In the latest run we made, we actually had two shaman players on the run and we commiserated on the lack of success on landing either of these line of spells. In the end, I stuck to the disease slow route and he nuked because that seemed to be the only thing that worked.

Question 1: Is this everyone's experience or just that I'm at a point where my spells are starting to be insufficient for where the group is hunting? (No info here at EQshaman or in Alla's that I could find and I'm tending to say this is normal because I can slow just fine in other, sometimes more difficult, locations.) Am I not considering something here?

Question 2: Is this normally such an underutilized zone? It seemed pretty straightforward for a walled zone. And with the right group, it seemed like running through the maze of hallways was just a matter of knowing which side of the room or door a mob would be standing on and where the traps were. It lacked a certain...I don't know...randomness that happens in other zones. Maybe it's because of the low agro radius of some of the mobs. I'm hesitant to even mention the zone because of how blessedly empty is has been when we've been there, but my lack of success with the "Patented Shammy Two-shot Combo" (TM) makes me want to find out the answers more.

Thanks in advance,
