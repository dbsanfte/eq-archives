BlargX
The quickest way to make money, by far, is to level.

Put a few levels on you and you'll wind up spending one hour killing or camping stuff of equivalent value to the stuff you would take five or ten or twenty hours camping and killing when you were younger.

You'll have the same amount in plat or much better, but all that time will have been spent on leveling, not trying for loot, so you will be way ahead of the game -- same loot, plus levels. And...leveling isn't anywhere near as boring as just hanging around camping stuff or killing greenies for fine steel or whatever.

Guarantee you will like the game more if you take care of your money worries by leveling instead of burning yourself out at low levels camping and killing garbage.
