------------------------

Agility/Strength/Dexterity: Finally they got agility right - put in the effect intended, not a useless stat. This spell should have no stacking issues with other spells. Strength/Dexterity should maintain its current status of +5% dmg mod, however, it should also allow for slightly more procs/crits. It should also not have any stacking issues.
------------------------

Avatar: Pick one of two versions for this spell: long duration, short duration.

Short: 250atk, 10-15% damage modifier, 5% strikethrough/hate modifier, significantly more crits/procs. High mana cost, long recast so you can keep it on 2 melee max.
Long: less atk and lower damage mod than short. Make it group castable (and mgb'able).

Both shouldn't have stacking issues with any other spell, especially our own spells, with the exception of agro increasing spells. Yes, this spell should raise you on the hate list, above and beyond additional hate from damage mod. Not castable on charmed pets. I would rather keep this in short duration form despite the hassles.
