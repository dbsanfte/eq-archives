LaughingWoman
First on my list is flying. It REALLY IS all that and a box of cookies.
Obviously, the fun will wear off a bit as I get used to it, but DAYUM ... I've been waiting for a video game to incorporate flying this well for a long time. I'm not disappointed. It's kind of the same feeling as I got when I scribed torpor. Except I didn't have to suffer waiting for it.

Air Superiority - got this with the scrapper, and knocking people into a flip that lands them on their bum is something I'll never regret having the ability to do (that and it interrupts casters, and things that are trying to blow up on you).

Hover - on ranged-attack types. Once I found that holding down the space bar (or whatever you remapped your jump key to) will make you float up a good deal, I get a HUGE kick out of watching the frustrated little guys staring up at me mournfully with baseball bats in hand. Gives you a nice vantage on the fight if you have crowd control abilities. Bonus height - jump and immediately hit the hover key to start out a good ways up... this works particularly nicely if you're wierd like me and also have the super jump power.

I'm interested in any other powers people are getting a big kick out of. Not necessarily the Monstrously Powerful Superbuild stuff, but the ones with good cheezy fun factor...

Jinheim
Recall Friend is a really good one. When I was low level and no one had any travel powers, I would hover on top of the tallest building I could find, recall someone on top of it, and then hover back down. This is really great if they are afk and you leave them teetering on the very edge. If you're pissed at someone you can also drop them from a really high height into a group of mobs: guaranteed death.

I also like teleport. Whenever I see a group of mobs I am going to attack, I pick my target out of that group, and then teleport directly on top of him with Shadow Maul queued up. Basically I appear out of thin and and unleash a flurry of attacks on my target. It's a really cool looking gank.

I also really like the animation for siphon life (basically its lifetap). My favorite is when the enemy starts to run away just as I am casting it. My character makes this gesture with his hand (if you've ever played mortal kombat, it is like scorpion's "Get over here!") and the villain just drops dead.

Propel is also one of my favorites, even though I haven't played a character that has it. The first time I saw it I was like "did someone just throw a sink?" and the guy is like "yeah" and I was like "cool".
Jenavyra
My current favorite so far (my highest char is only level 8, so I can't speak for the future) is combat jumping. I'm not sure why, I just get a kick out of being able to jump over big stuff. Planning to get super-jump at 14, and I can't wait for that either. Hover is fun for getting out of melee range as well - there's just something about a hovering robot throwing down ice blasts on the poor thugs that gets me giggling every time.

The animation for the Spines scrapper power Impale is really funny too, at least on some characters I've seen. My husband plays a character who looks similar to Tezrian (he's a full-fledged demon), so watching this evil looking black and gold demon-guy wind up to throw a pitch is pretty funny. The sadistic part of me grins when he hits with it too, as the fleeing thug gets it in the back and falls over with this huge spike through him. (What was that about *arresting* criminals again?) *ahem*

Anyway, I've been pretty impressed over all with the animations and general concepts of most of the powers. It's been *really* hard to try to settle on a character, because there are just so many options that are cool.
