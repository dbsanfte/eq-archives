Gojirax
I ran to Freeport for the first time on Saturday.

The guards outside amazingly enough were neutral and didn't aggro me, although when I hailed them they all said the same thing. "Death to the enemies of Lucan."

When I clicked on the doors to zone into Freeport I got a message that said only citizens of Freeport were allowed inside Freeport.

I would wager it's impossible to get into the guildhall of an opposing city. Or buy food, or bank/broker/tradeskill.
Hodur
You cannot get inside the city proper, but you can get into the refugee suburbs as a member of the opposing faction. Everybody cons as aggressive, but they won't attack you that I've seen. You are able to hail them and get quests from some of them. However, as soon as any guard gets near you, they'll port you to outside the gates of the city.

All the docks that I've explored have guards on them, so its difficult to get very far in the city without being detected. A scout of high enough a level to sneak past the guards might be able to get around more of the suburbs than I was able to.

It should be possible to reach the guild hall, yes, but that's all you can do in an enemy city. Sadly no way to gain or spend status points, neither for your own city nor the guilds city. Because of this I would rather recommend betrayal if you can stand being a different subclass possibly.
