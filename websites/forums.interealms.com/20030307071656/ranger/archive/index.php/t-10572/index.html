Just want to correct a few things on Haldir's Guide.

Journeyman Boots do not make you immune to being rooted. They're a lesser, toned down, movement increasing item. Regardless of class, they are essential to PVP on Rallos-Zek. Without them, and sow dispelled, you're done for.

Kick doesn't interrupt casting, it has no stun effect ever for rangers (unlike warriors at higher levels). With fast weapons, a level 20'ish caster should never continue casting anyway.

This guide is geared towards fighting magicians... and one of the most useful things to know about a magician is that MAGICIANS NEVER GET A ROOT/SNARE SPELL.

Their pet can root, just root it then snare it... snare the magician and beat the snot out of him, very easy to do. Once a magician gets epic, things are a bit nastier as their pets are basically Gods with great magic resists :/
