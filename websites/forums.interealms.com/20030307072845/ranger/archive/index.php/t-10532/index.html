AFAIK, harmony is never resisted.

Unless you get a message saying "that spell doesn't work on this type of target" (or something like that), it will always work.
Lanearas
I would add that when using Harmony, it is a good idea to keep your spell effects turned ON so that you can visually verify what mobs are actually effected.

Additionally, keep in mind that as an AE type spell, this can sometimes be cast on oneself to Harm a mob on the other side of a wall, around a corner, etc, when you cannot directly target the mob.
