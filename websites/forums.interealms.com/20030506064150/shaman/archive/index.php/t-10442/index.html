I wear a Piranha Tooth Necklace that I bought in the bazaar for too many pp, after I never saw one drop even while camping fish in the Blue Pool several times. In the "Pools" camp, the "Blue Pool" is the one full of fish, while the "Green Pool" is the one full of oozes, with a twisty hallway full of oozes between them.

Just last week, we were headed down to kill Ring of Slime (An Acidic Mass), and a couple named fish were up. We cleared the Blue Pool and one of the named piranha had the necklace. It went to a guildee's shaman twink, since I already had mine. Too bad I spent all those pp!

But anyway - yes it still drops. The Blue Pool is the right spot. It seems like a rare drop in that camp though.

I do not believe that the correct named fish can spawn in the "Planks Pool", which is the pool on the way to the FT3 Boots camp.
