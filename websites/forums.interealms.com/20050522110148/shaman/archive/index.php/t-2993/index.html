All of the current Pet poofing problems (and some of the pathing crap) happened with the Luclin graphics change.

When they changed the graphics they made the pets Z axis too low. (as in to close to the ground) In chardok if you summon a pet and move through the place to The exit ramp, you can watch a real pain in the ass bug that is reproduciable and no dev wants to look at.

Pet will get stuck on the steps and will go under the world and "warp" to his bind point (the location where you summoned him originally)

When pet cannot path to you he does one of two things. Poof (as in 32K himself) or warp to spawn point. Sadly with the damn graphics change to our pets this happens a hell of alot.

Watch your pets closely and beware of steps, mutt was made alot more stupid with luclin.
