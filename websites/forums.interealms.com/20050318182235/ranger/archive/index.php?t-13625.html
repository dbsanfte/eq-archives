The puslings drop parchements fairly often. There's a camp I like to take that I call 3 hut. If you keep running along the right wall, you'll get to a point where there is a Wretched pusling hut in front of you, a corruptor hut a little behind and to the right, and just a little further to the right is another hut of wretcheds. With really good groups, you can pull all 3 huts and have constant pulls. With decent or mediocre groups, I stick to just the wretcheds, and take my time to make sure I don't get adds. They have never resisted my harmony, although the pulling is a little wiggy and you'll get the occasional add when you shouldn't.

Hunted there 3 times, and have always had parchements drop. Had 3 in one camp which lasted about 3 or 4 hours or so, including 2 of a single mob. In my experience, the puslings is MUCH better on parchement drops than castle.
