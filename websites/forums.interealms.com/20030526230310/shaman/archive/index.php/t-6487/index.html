Guys....

You're WAY over doing this.... I've killed the shroom more than 20 times (have him timed after server resets) and have received the spell 4 times now.

He's very easy...

Invis into the room and move to the right. There's a spot where the floor plane moves up to the wall. Sit there... I'll explain why in a minute.

Summon dog. Cast alacrity/celerity on dog. Maybe regrow if you feel spunky. No other buffs needed.

Turn and face the saprophyte. To your immediate right there will be a cultist. Edge down the 45deg plane (from floor to wall) till you are very close to him. Pull him with JBB. Do NOT sic the dog on him as that will agro the second cultist to your left. JBB and melee the cultist down. Easy kill - 1K hps roughly.

Pull the other cultist near you next and kill.

Pull the cultist from the other side of the entrance and kill.

Pull the last cultist with JBB. Shroom won't agro. Kill last cultist.

Med and heal if necessary.

Sic dog on shroom. Wait for two taunts and then nuke him down with your spell nukes - not JBB. No need for slow, his melee sucks. Let the dog taunt twice between each nuke and you won't be summoned. Dog will live. You will be at full health and 50mana.... and hopefully you'll loot a new spell. :)

EDIT: Gah! Forgot to mention the reason for sitting in that spot... you are just out of reach of the AE nuke of the shroom - and something about that 45 degree plane keeps the cultists from adding... maybe a z-axis 'sploit? ;)
