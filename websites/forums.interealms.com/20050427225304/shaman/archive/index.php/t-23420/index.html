I just got a mage to level 41 in 18 hours played (includes 2-3 hours traveling and buying gear and spells) using a ds method.

Basically I ds my shaman run around and gather as many mobs as I can handle (3-10 depending on zone and level) Let the mobs beat on the shaman and kill themselves (best to find non-running zones).

With the mage it is easy, I just launch one rian or ae to put minor damage on all mobs, then sit down and make sure my shaman stays above 50 health.

Some non running zones when you get higher levels=
city of mist (went from 26 - 40 in 2 nights played maybe 6 hours)
velks (40- xxxxx I should be able to stay there until 46 when my alt can group with my shaman and I can go solo in pov or bot)

So if you are looking to get a toon to 65 in 7 days, it is possible. Just need to find the right zones.
