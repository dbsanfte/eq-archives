Waran
A newly reborn Warrior took his steps out into Everfrost and began his journey in Norrath. His hunting partner Xirius, a Barbarian Shaman and he walked the lands of Everfrost looking for adventure whereever it took them :) (with the experience of a Level 6 Warrior from Phase 4 Beta, well, not really exp, but I knew how to fish really really well in Beta :P)

The path he followed was long and rough, taking him through many difficult times, but still he remained strong and vigilant, meeting tons of new friends and adventurers that he fought with every step of the way. Most of all, he's considered himself very honored to have and been fighting along side his guildmates past, present and future who are the most honorable and noble on the Veeshan Server.

Tonight, that Warrior reached the pinnacle of the Warrior class.

Waran Rhinelander, <B>WARLORD</B> of Halas.
