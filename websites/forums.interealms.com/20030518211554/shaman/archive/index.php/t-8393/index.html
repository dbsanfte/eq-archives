Mirabilis Dictu
Phase Spider Carapace is 28 AC, +6 Str, +9 Sta, +8 Agi, +5 Dex, +6 Wis, +7 Int, +6 Cha, +9 vsMg, -3 vsFire, +5 vsCold, +8 vsPos, -4 vsDis, +25 HP. Classes: Bl Ra Sm C P Sk B Mk Ro Wr

Polished Tin Shirt is +28 AC, +7 Sta, +8 Dex, +10 Wis, +25 HP, +25 Mana, LORE MAGIC Classes: Ra Sm Ro. All Races.

The tin shirt is substantially superior for a shaman because it has both hps and mana on it, and increased wisdom. You want as much hp/mana as you can lay your hands on. Don't sacrifice wis/mana for hps tho. You need them all.

The price is irrelevant here. The shirt has a lower price because it's not wearable by as many classes. Monks like the PSC because of the weight, and that drives up the price. Leather and chain classes like it because it doesn't look like leather or chain.

Count your blessings and get that shirt if you like.

What would be better is if you could get your SS breastplate. I know it means a lot of tedious (and probably open) raiding, but it's a very good breastplate.

Same with the legs, btw.. you'll likely be wearing those into Vex Thall when you're ready :)

OK.. let's try this again :)

Don't sacrifice wis/mana for hps :). You still need the hps because mobs tend to slap you around some, but your mana pool is still your biggest asset.

I thought a lot about what aura of battle does for us in the last little while (because I've been trying to rate gear.. the process sucks hardcore btw because of the complex relationship between our gear stats and how we use that gear).

I use the kind of arbitrary AC=3 wis=6 hp=3 mana=6 agi=1 sta=1 aob=100 ft=100 system. I say kind of arbitrary because you also have to factor in focus effects in deciding if something is a good upgrade, and there isn't really a good way of quantifying those.

AoB. 2hp/tick. 20hp per minute. That's a miniscule amount when you have 2400 hp. Even if you have 1200 hp. AoB=FT1 or pretty close, when you cann it down to mana again. AoB would be a nice thing if it was just a freebie and not a goal in itself :).

We eat 148hp every time we use cann4 for something like 84mana (I'm working on cann5 atm but the hp-mana ratio isn't much different as far as I can see). That means that one mana is worth about 1.75 hps to us. The other way, 1hp = 0.57 or so mana. That's why raw mana is so much more important to us than hps. Also why 20hp/min is just silly. It would take us 7 minutes to regen enough hps to cann once.

Oh yeah.. I didn't make a mistake above :)
