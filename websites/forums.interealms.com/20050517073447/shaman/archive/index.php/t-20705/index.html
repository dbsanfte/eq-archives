Gruog
First of all you might have problems getting a new 8x card to work in that older motherboard. I am not saying you WILL have problems, just you might. I have tried to put a FX5200Ultra in a A7V133 and it flat out failed to work. Your A7A266 is an old Ali chipset that was notorious for compatibility issues. If your running DDR in the a7a you might want to consider a nice cheap DDR 8x agp board before upgrading the video card. Anything KT600 will work great.

Now that said, you need to determine a budget and get as fast as possible for that price. Someone said don't get a 256mb card and I cant stress how accurate that is.

You will want to look only at DX9 accelerated cards so stay away from the Geforce4. Games (especially BF:V and Far Cry) look soo much better on a GeforceFX or Radeon9xxx.

Your best bet in a budget card is a Radeon 9600XT ($150-190) or FX5700Ultra ($160-180) or GeforceFX5900XT ($180-210).

If you want another step up you can get a Radeon 9800Pro for $220-260
