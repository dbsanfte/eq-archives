Hail,

Here's my advice. SELL it NOW. Do not wait, do not pass go. Go to the bazaar and put it up for sale. I had one a few months ago, and wore it for about a month or so.

I was very happy with it as it was an upgrade over the Hate BP I had at the time. I sold mine for cash and along with selling my Bane Warding Shield I was able to buy an OoIV and have some cash left over to buy spells. I put the Hate BP back on for about a week or so, and then went to a Night Terror Chain Tunic.

The new smithed PoP BP's are dropping in price rapidly (and the Tae Ew BP) and are much better for you anyway.

Sell the Iksar Regen BP AND the Emerald Dragonscale Tunic. Depreciation on the EDT is happening rapidly, but you can likely still find a cleric who will want it for the pure wisdom (and MR) willing to pay a good amount for it. The Ikky is a popular BP that has held its value, and you can likely sell it for what you paid for it.

I recommend you buy the Polished Tin Mail Shirt (from PoI) or even the Engraved Royal Velium Field Plate BP. Either are decent enough for interim wear for you, and you'll likely come out with more cash for other purchases as well.

SO, order a fine steel BP from a smith, make the EDT, and then sell it asap. Sell your Iksar once you find a replacement BP. The regen effect isn't that significant on the Ikky. If you have enough cash, a Fungi would serve you well until you hit 60 and get Torpor, if you can find one to buy. BUT, you won't have enough cash to do that merely from selling your Ikky and EDT alone.

If you are on a mature server, you'll see the Night Terror Chain or the Tae Ewe Chain BP for sale for only a few kpp more than you can sell the EDT for. Selling the EDT and the Ikky could put you in a 40ac (or more) BP with good stats, which is a significant upgrade for you.

That's my advice. Take it or leave it. :)

Good luck,
