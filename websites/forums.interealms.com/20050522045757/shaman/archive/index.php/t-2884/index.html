Maboun
Two spots not mentioned that are very good exp. Akheva Ruins and The Deep.

Akheva is moderately camped on fennin, maybe one wizard kiting at zone in or a monk soloing and maybe one group in norht hall and one in east. Loot is pretty much just junky centi weapons but the exp is golden. I regularly do this with friends as 5 melee and one healer (me) and we keep entire east room and most statics / pats in both halls up to rockman area clear. Exp is simply nuts. Only maybe in Seru have I seen better exp but that place is a damn deathtrap if you actually go inside and don't camp zoneline.

The Deep - I have had less exp here but you can camp fiends and shrooms at zoneline. Would reccomend duoing with a melee partner here as shrooms have a ton of hps and are immune to poison so killing is sort of slow. Exp gain is very noticable per kill. And with a single group you can camp deranged underbulk and get the no component lev spell if you are bored.

Ssra which was already mentioned is really good but you can get yourself in trouble quick with single group if you get multiple elites. I highly reccomend FD puller here !

Scarlet Desert - Plateau kill reavers which a lot are still blue at 60 and seem to be decent exp, maybe since they are casters? The red aliens are a pain in the ass since they have that freaky 200+ dd proc that can sometimes proc 3-4 times in a row. Would reccomend root and rot on these, they can be pretty resistant too.

ME and The Grey - Poopy exp, trains, and the chance for a few nice rare drops (15 ac 20 wis gloves come to mind).


Tenebrous Mountains - vampire fort here is fun, can be done with shaman/melee partner and will be amix of light blue to blue. The named have a ton of hps and are casters but seem to drop blue diamonds very frequently as well as a few decent items. finding a good pull spot outside castle is essential as the pathing here is beyond bad. You can kind of crawl up and down in castle some once it gets broken but there are a few flying pats with fairly fast respawn so if duoing I'd reccmoned pulling to left or right of castle zone where wolves/bats do not path.
