What the other posters are trying to explain to you is that the Ulak is a "Hand to Hand" weapon. Go here : http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=26802

Notice the Weapon Skill is "Hand to Hand" What that means is that the wielder of this weapon will us their hand to hand skill, i.e. their attacks will look the same as barehanded. In our pets case, barehanded means it bites.

cattywumpus
Best way to prove that the pet is weilding the weapons is to give him one slashing, piercing or crushing weapon then watch his hits. You will see that he no longer bites but will only have the attack type of the weapon you gave him.

Now give him a hand-to-hand weapon for his off hand(off-paw?) and you will see that he starts biting again in addition to his other attack type from the first weapon.

I started saving some secondary only weapons when I find them so that puppy will dual wield with only one weapon and save myself some inventory space that way. There are several secondary only weapons from luclin zones that you can find on merchants for under a plat. One of the easiest to find is the Master's Eitchatka (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7152) . It drops in Tenebrous Mountains and can be found in Katta pretty regularly.
