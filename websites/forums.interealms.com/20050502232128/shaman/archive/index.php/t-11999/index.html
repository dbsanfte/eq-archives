Now for shaman the numbers with canni 5 (but not canni 4 to make it simple should be like this)


Chorus of marr: 21FT 20hp

This gives us a total of 128FT and 4 hp regen. This is not practical however, because the probability of casting canni 5 everytime on the second it pops is VERY unlikely. Furthermore, it is often dangerous to allow your hitpoint to regenerate via natural terms and therefore mana is spent to offset this (Torpor/Q).

Canni 4 gives you 82 mana for 148hp every 5.5 seconds including recast.
Over a 180 second period, assume you can cast this 32 times and you get. ~87FT and -158HP Adding this to our totals we get 215FT and -154hp per tick. This should fill up pretty much any shaman mana pool out there. Now the downside is that
a) You cannot cast anything else at all
b) There is no point in sustaining this past the 3 minute period since you cannot deplete any mana while gaining it.

I would say in most practical situations necros have us beat or at least matched in mana regen simply due to the fact that their's is all passive and incremental vs the active and sudden mana regen of the shaman. Not to mention their dots/poison nukes are far more mana efficient. Of course they can't slow or buff or heal quite as well so it's a tradeoff.
