Litchebane
Dunno how many of you do this but thought I would share a little tip we use on some raids. When you know you are going to be pulling multiple mobs and they all need to be maloed before slow, we usually have one person on malo hitting them all then the other Shamans slowing. Especially when you get mobs with the same name this works well. The problem you run into is that malos is a 10 second refresh and I want the mobs maloed NOW. So I loaded up malo in addition to malos, I mean they are close enough right? And I made a hotkey that goes
/cast (malos)
/rs Casting MALO on %T
/cast (malo)

That way no matter what was the last one you just cast, the other one will fire off if the other isn't ready. No waiting on malos refresh. Works for me anyway, the 10 point difference isnt normally a problem.

Enjoy!

Caanus Grey
If you have 8 shaman, then you can say screw tactics. But if you only have 2 shaman on the job of slowing, then when one hits the Malo hotkey, the other hits a slow hotkey along the lines of..

"/assist Malo Shaman
/cast turgur
/announce SLOWING %T"

And if turgur lands/resists they then plug the appropriate calling hotkey. This way with only 2 shaman you can slow all the mobs MUCH faster than if each shaman chose their own targets (and quite likely double Malos'd at least 1 of them thus wasting time).

a little off topic, but here is another tip:

you can also use the double casting for your heals..
