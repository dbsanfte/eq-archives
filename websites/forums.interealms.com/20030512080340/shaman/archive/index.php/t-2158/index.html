Gloriana
I hate to break it to you, there is no way to "plan" on getting that ring unless you're in a group that's ready to clear Sky multiple times.

IIRC the three drops are:

* Spiroc feather - Island 5
* Bixie essence - Island 6
* A ring from the Sister of the Spire - Island 7

Remember, the sister only drops one quest component at a time and there are 14 classes (not sure if BLs have any sky quests now or not). So, yes, you could get incredibly lucky, or you could be like me, I've had the spiroc feather in the bank for over a year and have cleared Sky multiple times (farming Mage crowns) and have NEVER seen the other two pieces.

There is no way to "do" any of the Sky quests, you have to be in the right place at the right time and be able to win a random (if you're guild does randoms) over several other interested parties.

Sky is NOT fun! Your guild will probably go for necro cloaks and ranger globes and warrior blades and mage crowns, but after clearing it a few times, they aren't going to want to go just for "fun" or the chance of completing a hellishly rare upper island quest. It's long, boring, bringing more than 24 people is incredibly time consuming and cost prohibitive.

Bleh, Sky. Rusty daggers, indeed.
