We are a new guild forming, tentatively named The Old World, with goals focused on that exactly, exploring EverQuest as it was in the old world. I am one of the many seasoned EverQuest players to look back longingly on the days of old when camping the FBSS was a rush, and seeing a pair of GEBS drop was a dream. This goal is forming with intent to step back in time, and explore EverQuest as it was when it was new.

This guild will have many self imposed limitations, which are required of every member. To begin with, we will live in the "Classic Era." This means that we play the game as if we were at a point in which Kunkark had not yet been released. We will be limited to level 50, and will wear only equipment available before the release of Kunark. We will adventure in any and all of the old world zones, including and especially the exciting dungeons. We will aspire to vanquish dragons and to conquer planes.

If this is something you would like to be a part of, then we would love to have you as a part of our family. Please visit our boards at http://theoldworld.kicks-ass.net/boards/ .
