I've finally gotten the Silk Coldain Shawl!(number 5)

Last night I got tailoring to 123 and couldnt wait any longer to try the combination :) So I equipped my newly purchased tailoring gizmo (the plus 5% thingy that tinkerers can make), said a little prayer, and hit that little click button.

Cha CHING

For those of you not familiar with this quest, i'll sum up the 5th part as best I can.

Combining 5 crystalline threads(unstackable off of spiders in velks)
will get a crystalline fiber

three fibers plus a glowing wurm bile from wurms in GD makes a glowing silk piece.

five glowing silk pieces plus my 4th coldain shawl yields the 5th shawl.

so basically i need 75 crystalline threads (I actually needed 85 due to failures in combining them) And 5 wurm biles. Those wurm biles took me 4 days of camping 3-5 hours for. Big thanks to Seran for helping me get those last two and thanks to vaemas for the first two :)

After that it's all a matter of getting tailoring up to and past the trivial of 115 that the final combination requires. If you fail, you get to do allllll that work all over again. You do get shawl 4 back, but you have another 75 threads to get and 5 more wurm biles... not exactly a fun position to be in.

Well I got lucky so now I have this masterpiece gracing my shoulders :)

Silk Coldain Prayer Shawl: +5 AC +5 Wis +5 Int +5 Cha +5 Save vs Fire +5 Save vs Cold

WHEEEEEEEEEE!
