weissblau
BW has a great solo spot with no wanderers but lots of mobs nearby, including Gullerback. Not sure if I can describe how to get there, but I'll try.

Put your back to the giant fort ramp. Now go about 90 degrees around the giant fort to your RIGHT and move down the hill - you should see a burned-out area facing a steep hillside. The mobs don't wander in the burned out area, at least not on the left side of it, but lots of them are wandering the hillside and the green area to the left of it. Gullerback does wander here.

Other good solo spots at that level are Chardok, Skyfire, maybe Veksar(?). KC sewers and Seb would be nice but I found them very hard to single pull, so duoing those spots is better.

Zzang
If you do not care about the Katta faction, then Katta will be great place for solo xp.
The xp per kill is not superb, but you gain xp faster because of the quality vs. quantity.
Bank yard was my favorite spot, because the area is huge and there are 11 guards that I could pull for a kill and they spawn FAST.
These guards have about 3-4k in hp and hit for average of 70ish.
They have very low resistances so you do not have to waste mana on debuffing, plus when you slow them your pet can tank them with ease.
I usually single pull them with slow then lure him to the area where I want to kill him, then root him and stick pet on him. I also dot them for faster kill.
They also drops okay loot(merchant junks) and you can bank there even your faction with katta is totally destroyed. Just make sure to invis yourself and sneak in behind banker to avoid contact with bank guards.
