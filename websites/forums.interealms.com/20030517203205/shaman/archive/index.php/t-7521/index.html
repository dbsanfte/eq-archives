Get an earring of twisted leaves if you want some hps, but if you're going to replace your wis rings with a shadel and a velium, thats a loss of 14 wis and if you're not over 200, that can hurt a bit, so you might want to try and get an ivandyr's hoop for good ac and 6 wis. Go with an SBS as a shield if you're low on cash, or if you have a little more and like to be eccentric like me, you can get a shield of the white dragon and look like a high roller. For your face you should grab crystal spider eyes or an enshrouded veil. Neck, just be simple and stick with a ivory collar for resists if you want, or get something with a use, like an amulet of insight, so you can procrastinate on rebuffing for another 5 minutes.
