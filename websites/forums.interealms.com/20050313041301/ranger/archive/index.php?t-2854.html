Flurry - Extra attacks against current target. If a mob flurries, heal more :p Cripple and slow are both youre friends here if the mob isnt immune.

Rampage - Attack to an extra (or several) targets in an area. Controling rampage is difficult, and basically only experimentation will teach you about it. Dont have people sitting when pulling or aggroing mobs that rampage. Cripple and slow are both youre friends here if the mob isnt immune.

Enrage - Riposte discipline most high end melee mobs use when low on hp. To counter, either turn off attack completely until you see the "no longer enraged" message, or attack from the rear. Be careful of the mob spinning on killing people. Usually safer to just wait it out unless youre good at positioning.
