Chutoi
I'm actually rather fond of Avatar as well. It seems a logical next step in the progression with our various gods. Shaman, mystic, luminary, oracle, prophet, avatar. . .

With each step we get closer to our god. A shaman (unless talking to Rytan) is a priest. A mystic is someone who relates to religious mysteries or occult rites and practices. A luminary is a person who gives inspiration to others (a bit of a stretch to imply it's from our gods, but c'est la vie). A oracle is a person through whom a deity is held to respond when consulted. A prophet is a person who speaks by divine inspiration or as the interpreter through whom the will of a god is expressed. Each step is a step closer to our diety.

Seems to make sense that our next step is to allow our diety to manifest through us. Hence . . . avatar--1) temporary manifestation or aspect of a continuing entity, or 2) The incarnation of a [Hindu] deity, especially Vishnu, in human or animal form.

Most of the above are from the dictionary, btw! :)
