Good poll, Murtak. Don't worry about asking people's opinions, we're mostly not too shy about saying what we think.

Need to say something about DoT duration, though. Shaman DoTs seem to envision a fight that is 1 to 4 minutes long. They work well for such encounters; and they work well for fights that are longer than that, simply by being reapplied.

To be relevant in new game content, shaman DoTs need to envision fights that last 15 to 40 seconds. That means a long-running DoT would be Bane, at 30 seconds. Retuned DoTs would need to hit and do their damage in less time than that.

I also agree that future DoT's need to run faster, similar to LDoN Bane but they *must* have a resist adjust on them of -50 or more so they actually *land* most of the time.
