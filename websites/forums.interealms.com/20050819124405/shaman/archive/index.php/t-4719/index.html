One of the recent changes on the Test Server has been greatly increasing the exp received by a post-50 player who kills an even con or higher mob, the rationale being that the greater risk should net a greater reward.

I think that if anyone can make this into a viable exping tactic, it's the shaman. Personally, it's also the type of fight I prefer. I'd love to be able to engage in a long, difficult fight that leaves me at 0% mana and 20 health to gain half a blue bubble instead of having to mow down half a dozen blue mobs.

So, I'm starting a list of white-con or above mobs that shaman have a crack at, along with some minor details. I don't have much so far, so I'd appreciate any contributions.


60th:
Emperor Chottal - Level 60 NPC in Sebilis crypts. Hits for high 100s, casts often. Fairly standard fight, slow, burn him down with epic, jbb, and DoTs, while keeping up health with Torpor. Keep resists up so his spells just bounce off.

Stomples - Rabbit in PoM. 32k hp, can triple for 350, need more details.

A Vigilium Cohortis, A Lictor of the Heart(plus various other SSeru mobs) - Duel wielding warrior and paladin respectively. Need more info on these.

There are some other mobs I know that are soloable at 60, although I'm not sure of the level. Are any of the soloable Umbral Plains named, Cliff Golems, etc. For that matter, anyone know of any decent camps where a well equipped shaman(epic, jbb, horse, full spellbook) can take on an even con or above mob?

You can solo Shiknar warriors in fungus grove easily, provided you have torpor. They are level 60, less than 32000 hp I believe, more like 20 k or so. They hit for max about 183, and are pretty easy to slow with malo in.

They have very low agro range, so can pull them from the other shiknar warriors without agro, unless Warlord_Tk`kik`tthik is up. Can pull single then too if you watch when the Warlord starts casting. Be sure to root them before they run though.

These are the easiest level 60 mobs I know of, but sucks to loose the faction if you like to hunt in the back part of Fungus Grove.
