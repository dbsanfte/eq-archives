Yes your goal profile shows you're approaching the shaman like a shaman and not like a druid, except perhaps the RMB and the Black Pearl Velium Ring. I am also a new Shaman in my 38th season but I'm new to EQ as well (only been playing since 3/3/03) However there is a really great mask you can get by doing a pretty simple quest.

Here is the mask: http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16394

and here is the quest: http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2203

The only hard part of the quest is going to Temple Droga (I did this quest when I was lvl 30) but what I found to work is cast Invis on your self and then find the guy in the temple. Once you complete your interaction with him simply gate out of the Temple. To make it even easier you can even bind yourself if PoK near the NPC you have have to give the book to in exchange for the mask.

For the time (it only took me about 1 1/2 - 2 hrs to complete) and the price (free) its a GREAT upgrade! Good Luck!
