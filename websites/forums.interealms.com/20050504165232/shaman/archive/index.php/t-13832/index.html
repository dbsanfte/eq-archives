I've made 2 trips up there since the patch and thought I'd offer what little knowledge I have and ask if others could post what they've learned.

The zone seems to be more of a typical zone that the existing PoG,Fear, etc. Meaning, your not gonna clear the zone of mobs. The common ones respawn on a 15-20minute timer. I'm guessing the named are on some kind of cycle, but the common yard trash respawns.

You can take 2 approaches here - Bring a group or 2 up and camp the zone in or take that group or 2-raid and crawl around. You won't have a monk pulling things back to the ZI.

Most raids have invis'd before going up but I'm leaning towards it not being necessary anymore.

Zone is fairly safe - You zone into a little alcove with a well. Stay behind the well. The walls in that area seem to be safe - we haven't gotten adds through the walls.

The zone now looks like your outdoors in a town, but lizards/horses don't work, nor does sow

Drops are far more rare than in the past. I'd say 80% of mobs drop pp only.

Quite a few drops are lore only (ghast fingerbones, kuraiki flesh, abhorrent powder ). no stats, probably all quest stuff

We've only crossed the bridge on the right and headed west. We haven't explored to deep. Haven't found too much in that direction.

Wondering if anyone has gone off in a different direction and found any of the new golems or higher level clerics (we've found clerics of hate but there are supposedly some templars and high priests?)
