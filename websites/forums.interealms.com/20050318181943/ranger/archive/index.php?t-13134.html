Has anyone taken the time to read the books available at the library in the Plane of Knowledge?
I spent the entire night going through all the books and learning about the history of certain races and events in Norath’s past. I found it very interesting and mesmerizing.

My favourite was the story of how the Frogloks came into being. I was so sad reading how these once noble, selfless and brave warriors came to the defense of the captured Mithaniel Marr and how they fought against insurmountable odds at the cost of thier lives.
My second favourite, was the story of the Unkempt Druids.

If you have not taken the time to read the stories, I would suggest making the time, as your opinion about some of these creatures may change and your eyes open to the fantastic and mysterious past of Norath.

Did you know that Ogres were not always fat, slobbering, bum itching simpletons?

Prior to Planes of Power, I enjoyed reading the lore of EverQuest.

I enjoyed reading about the fabled Elldar forest, the first home of the Elves, and how the wrathful gods burned down the forest, which is now the southern desert of Ro and how the Elves fled to their present home in the Faydark. The Druid rings are all that remains of these forgotten ancestors of the Elves.

Another of my favourites prior to PoP, was the story of the beginnings of Necromancy among the Erudites and the ensuing epic battle against the outcast Heretics. The history of Necromancy spanned from Qyenos to an ancient place in the snowy coverings of Everfrost.

If you look for it, there is a whole epic story that can be gleaned from various places in the world of Everquest.
