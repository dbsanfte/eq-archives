Zaug,

A couple of places I hunted (and still hunt) which are steady xp, and may be a change of pace for ya....

Sanctuary Room in Crypt of Nadox. Invis up and work your through the tunnels in Dulak, all the way to the Castle in the back of the zone. The zone in for Nadox is in the back of castle. When you zone in, this first room is the Sanctuary room. Mobs are spread out enough for single pulls, and is things get hairy for ya, safety is a few steps away. Decent loot, 3-8pp / weps that sell for 5pp / occaisional gem drop. There is a rare spawn that pops in this room - High Priest Grumsalot, occaisional A Cloister Sentinel will pop as well. Can get some decent mid lvl gear out of here that could make ya a few pp in baz. I still hunt here at 63, but mobs are starting to go LB. If you haven't done your Blood of Nadox quest spell yet, I urge you to think about spending some time in this zone.

I also recommend (as others have) Nurga. Xp may not be as great now that it's no longer a hot spot, but still a place I liked to hunt around your lvl, and still do once in a while as there are still DB mobs at 63. I hunted here before it was a hotspot, and xp wasn't too bad.

Not sure what your wearing, or if you have all of your lvl60 spells, but another option may be PoJ Basement and the enforcers. I solo here a lil bit once in a while. Just need to be careful... mobs don't come alone and they repop pretty fast. I usually just pull to my pet parked outside the doors from the first enforcer and pull to there. When you get an add, slow it and park it away from your camp.

Hope this helps... and if not, just peruse the Hunting Grounds threads. Lots of great suggestions that may be burried in here =)
