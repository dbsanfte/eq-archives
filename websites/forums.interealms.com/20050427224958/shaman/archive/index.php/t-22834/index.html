Unfortunately I haven't been into PoM in a while, so my advice won't be the greatest, but at the least I can hope to trigger other people replying with better knowledge ;)

When you zone into PoM you will be standing on a blue dias with a forest in front of you. Invis yourself since the denzens of the forest are all quite KOS to everyone last I heard. Head through the forest to the moat.

At the moat you will see a bridge leading up onto the first wall of the castle, that bridge doesn't actually exist and you will fall through to the water. Head to the left or right as you prefer and look for the first bend in the wall. Right at that bend is the actual ramp up onto the wall. Walk straight toward the wall just before where it bends and you should catch on the ramp. If you fail, back up and try again, lev helps so that you can adjust a bit before falling to the water each attempt.


The halflings you want to fight are right there on the first wall as I recall with the revamp. Supposed to be pretty easy kills, so just head to one end of the wall and take em down. I'd imagine that with mage gear and mini-torpor you can handle them from 58 on up relatively easily if skilled at the solo.

As for mischief, it has all been systematically removed from the zone. The place is completely boring now :(
