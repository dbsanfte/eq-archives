Tsuyoi
Lots and lots of places you can visit:

Droga - Poor xp for the trouble, but easy kills and decent drops. At 61 you may stick close to ZI. Near Nurga will be mobs that better fit your level. Get to know this zone, though, since it will be a moneymaker for awhile.

Inner Acrylia - About the same as Droga, but a little further out so likely less competition.

The Deep - Almost no-one goes here so it's fairly safe. Not great drops, but the XP isn't bad. You would need to be proficient at handling 2 or more mobs for break-in. I'd recommend invising somewhere out of the way to avoid the occasional ZI train.

The Hole - Things are lite blue at 65, but at 61 you may have a good time hunting down in the city. Some of the named drop things here worth 5k in bazarr easy. Need to gate out once you get into the courtyard.

Fungus Grove - I think you are high enough level now to single the mushrooms. Extremely boring but not much to worry about.

Madien's Eye - Should be able to find a nice corner to pull the roamers to. Never really camped here so no idea of the XP/ Drops.

The Umbral Plains - Same as Madien's Eye but a bit higher level.

Akheva Ruins - Should be able to camp the entry hallway for decent xp.

LoY - Many good spots in LoY. XP isn't bad and drops are decent. Out of the way so very little competition. Try any of the camps in Nadox, ZI at Hates, Nadox ZI at Torigan, luggald caves in Dulak.

Plane of Hate - ZI mobs are easy. Also some nice places close to the ZI where you can hunt. The marsinger quests are well worth doing. CRs are a pain here though.

Siren's Grotto - Stay away from the ZI/ZO and you can do well here. XP isn't great but the place is easy to get to now that PoM is in the GD. This is a bit rougher than most of the other places I mentioned since a lot of it is under water.

Chardok - You could probably do the entry hallway / ZO area. ZO is a bit harder to break but easier since you can run to ZO when in trouble.

Skyfire - Lots of wanderers here that should be in your range. I hunted the Chrono's for the essence of sunlight that sold very well. You will be debuffed while doing this.

City of Mist - You might still get some xp from some of the mobs near the castle. Was decent XP and not too dangerous.

Old Seb - Several camps here you should be able to do. Heard that the jem drops were nerfed so may not be worth the effort.

Veksar - Entrance gobbies are in your range and the faction for the non-Iksar is good. Be sure to do the weapon turn-in quest. Resistant to the Poison/Disease dots so LDoN curses work best.

Plane of Mischief - Easy access now since it is in the GD. Do the halflings along the castle wall and the yard trash. Be sure to figure out where the ZO tree is in the courtyard before you agro anything. Lots of spell component drops here so get an idea of what you need and watch for it. You can help fill your spellbook as you get xp.

Plane of Innovation - ZI camps or factory possibly.

Enjoy,

Tsuyoi / Kany

I suggest Veksar - not just entrance mobs, but basically as deep as you want to go. You will need plenty of IVU potions, but there are heaps of named deeper in with good bazaar loot in the 1k - 20k range.

Basically, from 61, you can solo almost anything in Veksar. I did it with just melee / pet / Epic DoT (most mobs in Veksar have insane poison / disease resists, making most of our DoTs useless). If you have any of the LDoN dots, these will stick fine (Odium, Anathema, Bane), but you certainly don't need them.

Veksar is slow soloing, but will be much faster as a duo. Some of the named are very hard to single pull, some are very easy.

As a final note - Words of Agony drops moderately regularly in Veksar - DO NOT sell this to a vendor - it is still worth around 10k on most servers!

Another suggestion, although certanly challenging, is perhaps SolC - the kobold side zone in should be a do-able duo option. Single pulling is tricky, but using your spirit of the howler pet, you can use the standard SK or BL pet-pulling technique to get singles (top tip - use spirit of shrew on pet). You might even be able to clear all the way to Spiritmaster Heili, although you will have to be on top of your game to take him, perhaps, but the rewards are woth the effort - he drops a 2hs which is something like 40/43 with great stats / resists or a 1hs which is 15/22 with good stats / resists, or, rarely, an very very good ring with a nice extended buff 4 focus.

Caves in PoN is also an option - snare kiting is very viable - you can either let ranger keep aggro (not that hard with the right combo of very low level low mana ranger spells) while you stack dots and send pet, or you can just let ranger snare and play ping-pong with your dots and his bow, with whoever has aggro doing the running until the other person gets aggro back. I emphasise that snare is the key here - you cannot afford to get smacked up much at your level - you should probably pull with malo, and run past the ranger so he can snare - you really do need snare to stick first time, every time.
