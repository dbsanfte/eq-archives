Besides the methods already mentioned (root & camp, root & gate, sacrifice pet & gate), you pretty much suck up a death if you mess up badly. The learning curve from this school of hard knocks (w/ no easy getaways) will make you a better player in my opinion.

I see the difference in game philosophies by class all the time. I'd rather die trying to survive something that might be too much than run & live never knowing if I could have won out w/ a little courage & some skill.

We were fighting in PoV our way in to IC (instead of just invising through) from OC yesterday, and after respawns caught up with us halfway through. We had 4 mobs aggroed and one member already dead. The other people were ready to train to zone as soon as the first person died. But I convinced them to stick it out, and we came out on top & had a great time doing it. Was MUCH more fun than invising through just to get back to the pull-kill-pull-kill grind. We had to root park, mez, purposely shift aggro, manage mana, battle rez, watch positioning & adds, etc. It was great fun. :D

Good luck!
