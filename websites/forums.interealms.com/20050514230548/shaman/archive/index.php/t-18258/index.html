Tordail Farstryder
I think you're going to get several different replies. Seems like there really isn't one good way to do AA points. I was told, as a young shaman, to do what seemed best for the character, the guild (if you're in one at the time) and your play style. Not in that order, necessarily.

That said, I took care of Innate Regen and tossed a couple points in Innate Wisdom (it was either that or Innate Run, I can't remember). I got Baron at 59 and dinged 60 the next day. I held off on more AA till about 40% into 62 (enough to die doing stupid **** and not worry about losing the level) and ground out Mental Clarity 3 (nicely noticeable difference in mana regen). I'm told to hold off on Canni 5 until 65, though I may stop once I'm into 64 and grind out the 5 AA points ... simply because I don't yet have many of my 64 spells and I like to take my time leveling until I get all the tools in the toolbox.

I'd tell you to hold off on more AA till you hit about 40% into 62. Between BOT and HOH you will be amazed at the experience you get, and once you turn it to 100% AA (if that's your fancy) I'm told you can get 1 AA per hour or so. In PoTac, it's 1 AA about every 45-50 minutes.

Hope that helped.

Vigdis
Get 65 first. There are many reasons:

1) You will get less aggro.
2) You will be more resilent.
3) You can mem those nice spells you might pick up during the exp and AA grind.
4) You can land those spells easier on more mobs.

I also heard something about lower level people in groups with higher level people generate somewhat less exp than a group of max level people.

With that said, you might also consider the follow philosopy a friend of mine preaches:

Get all the boring stuff first (ie exp to 65 and all the boring aa's) then when you have those you can reward yourself with lots of fun stuff without having to go back.

I feel that getting 65 first is the wisest choice. I've explained my reasons for this many many times, and they've already been expressed by other posters on this thread.

However, if you absolutely must do AA, then get to 62 first. The exp will be the same, but you will have tnargs, new focus, and access to BoT.
