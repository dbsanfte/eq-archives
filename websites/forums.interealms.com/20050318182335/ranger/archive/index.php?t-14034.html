Ditto the bard thing....

I had tried Execution with a variety of groups and never succeeded. Then we got a 64 bard (who had the 62 ae slow song) and we did it perfectly, twice.

Without the bard we generally had issues with somebody getting aggro and dying. With the bard, we barely had to heal. So you need the bard, one decent healer, and DPS.

My first successful group was: 62 ranger (me), 60 warrior, 60 rogue, 62 cleric, 62 wizard, 64 bard.

Then we swapped out the 62 wizard for a 60 paladin (that's gotta be a massive cut in DPS) and suceeded again! This time was a lot closer than the first time, due to lower DPS. We were also lacking any enchanter/shaman haste on the second run (just had bard haste when he could sing it, which probably wasn't 100%).

Bard + healer + DPS seems to be the key. Just make sure your MA latches onto mobs quickly, no wasting time.

Definitely load the level 61 Exile Summoned -- nuking for 725 damage is great. And have somebody watching the progress of the executioner and call out NUKE if it is getting close. I had Calefaction, Firestrike and Exile Summoned loaded (along with Abolish Disease and Abolish Poison -- for curing slows and tashes), and needed them all.

I used TS against the boss mob, more for fun than because we actually needed it.

The cleric in my successful runs did more nuking than healing, I think.
