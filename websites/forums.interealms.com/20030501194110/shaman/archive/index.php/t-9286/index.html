Thurdar
The short answer is yes, you can easily avoid Rak on your way to the 2nd floor. You'll have to kill the Giosk reaver just past the first locked door but the other 4 reavers (and Rak) are up a locked elevator. No way that you can accidentally agro them on the way through.

For both of them, they'll spawn after you kill the appropriate reaver, they spawn at the spot that the reaver is killed.

In case you're interested, we tried to duo Rak a few days ago with a 65 SK and 61 shaman and failed miserably. Only got him down 1 bubble but I died at half mana , I wasn't paying attention to positioning and I had to get into AoE range to heal which got me killed. SK wasn't wearing any resist gear and didn't have FR buffs (but did have enchanter haste). If we'd buffed up correctly and let our 52 cleric help we probably could have taken him but we didn't want to risk wiping the group so only 2 of us engaged. I was pretty much chain casting my 2k heal to keep the SK alive. Didn't really have any time to canni and heal myself in between.
GraemeFaelban
You can heal from outside the AoE range, I know, I was doing it. We had Rak spawned where the first Reaver spawns, the one that spawns Ghiosk. The healers were all just inside the room, or just outside the room at the door. At that location, you are out of AoE range, but within healing range for Cheal, Kraggs, and Tunares Renewal.

If the 65 SK had had decent FR gear, and you had stayed out of AoE range, I would expect you could duo him.
