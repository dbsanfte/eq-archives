Yes, at least of the start of October, you could skip Kirn and Neh. No, it's not recommended. It may be fixed at any time and if you have problems and complain, you'll be SoL.

Yes, all you need is MAX ally, the tear, kill rak, turn in scale.

If your guild can do fear, they can do the hole. Really. Honest. The hole is just a long grind down, depending on how cautious your pullers are. I've done the hole six times in the past two months, and yeah, it's boring, but it's not that deadly.

As for Fear, you've got the following issues:

1) whether your guild kills on the same cycle as the golems are on (IE, 3 days after they were killed last)
2) how many other shamans want the tear
3) how many other guilds are camping fear.

With PoP out, your chances of getting fear to yourself are much better nowadays. Hitting it "everyweek" for 6 months isn't the answer.... it's strategy. Find out when fright dread and terror went down last, count 3 days, go then. Otherwise you'll just be doing fear raids with no golems. Or plan to go on the night after a patch.
