Cirin
Mobs in HF are pretty tough for thier lvl. They only hit for a max of 170 or so, but have a ton of hps. Troll casters arent that bad, but Luggald caster can be a royal pain the ass...priests will ch 3-4 times a fight, arcanists like to use ice comet, etc...so watch your mana and your health.

Now, if you want to get a feel for HF, I'd recommend hunting at the zl. There are 7 static mobs, and one roamer that comes down the stairs from time to time. 3 spawn on the lft side, 4 on the right. Start by pulling the rat just inside the door to the right, then root split the 2 trolls in the middle of that side, clear, then get rat near the stairs. Nothing on other side should aggro, but watch out for the pat if it comes down the stairs. If one of the luggalds on the left side is a caster, you may just want to leave them all alone, becasue he will just stand there and fry you while you are sorting out the other 2. You can actually get some decent drops here...lots of twink gear drops randomly in the zone, but they are fairly rare.

I've been to the "new zem" Hate's fury. Don't bother. This is from a 65 perspective. A better place to go is in nadox, if you come to the "bridge" in nadox, and are facing west, the left hand tunnel leads to hate's fury, the right to the back part of nadox, which is far and away better bang for the buck (if you insist on LoY). I myself stil lspend a healthy bit of time there, as aa xp isn't horrid. It's dangerous, and quite a ways in the boonies for a CR. I usually bind in gunthark, and carry a swirling mist on me (instagate). If you an keep up the dps, work your way to the back of the luggald's, heading to the right, in the back is a room of undead, with a mob that con spawn that has some so-so loots. nothing specacular, and not worth much. on the upside, that room has mobs wiht less hp than HF, hit about the same, but are also less resistant to spells. the difference in hp (and still being the same levels as zi HF) makes it much better for the solo or duo shaman.
