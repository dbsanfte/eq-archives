After finally getting what I needed to smith Shadowscream, I found out I can't store up on smithing items and have a big long smithing session, because one of the components is NO RENT. So, I have to spend half an evening farming the NO RENTs so I can smith the other half.

Last night, after farming the NO RENTs, I put everything together for a smithing session. I put all the components in the forge, but by mistake hit DONE instead of COMBINE which closed the forge. When I opened it back up, the NO DROPS were gone....including the MALLET AND THE ANVIL! I petitioned, but it never got answered, so I'm having to do the lengthy noob quests all over again to get the mallet and anvil back.

The other option is smithing Acrylia. MO FARMING MO FARMING MO FARMING! I have to farm the Acrylia, which is actually a common enough drop, if I'm not in competition with tailors. I also have to farm the components to make the temper, which is rare stuff that drops in Tenebrous with its wonderful complement of just-barely-green aggros. On top of that, I have to give an enchanter a poison vial and an emerald to make clear mana. CHA-CHING!

Between the May and June patches, I went up 30 pts in smithing. 3 weeks after the June patch, I've gone up a total of 1 point in smithing. My 202 skill is nowhere near the level it needs to be to reliably do cultural smithing.
