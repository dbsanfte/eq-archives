Gloriana
No, it doesn't.

Extended enhancement doesn't work on torpor either.

AA which affect torpor - SCM, SCR, SCRM, Quickbuff

Focus items which affect torpor - Mana preservation, Spell Haste, Haste of Mithaniel

Items with Improved Healing III:

1. Idol of the Underking - rare drop in the Hole
2. Mask of Fall - NTOV
3. Shroud of Veeshan - Phara Dar in VP

The cog encrusted epaulets which drop in POI have haste of mithaniel, very nice!

PS http://lucy.fnord.net is great at looking this stuff up. You just type "improved healing III" in the Spell Search box and it returns all the items with that effect, very handy!
finaljustice
No, Torpor is a Heal over time, not a heal. Get SCR3 and SCRM to add ticks to the 300 per that Torpor gives you. Torpor with no AA's gives you 4 ticks of healing. With SCRM, you get 6 and sometimes 7 (depending on where your cast falls on the tick boundries. So it starts at 1200, and you can make it 1800 with an occasional 2100 HP's (and is ALWAYS overwritten by Cleric HoT's).
