Qhan
The VahShir newbie area is one of the best I've seen in game. As a vahshir, you will hit level 2 just doing the citizenship quest, and learn your way around the city in the process (after you know shar vahl a little, you can do the quest in about 5 minutes).

The pit around Shar Vahl is awesome all the way up to about level 7. There are various quests down there for cloth like armor, etc. There are guards in the pit you can run to if you get in trouble, but I only had to use them a few times, the mobs are cake. Its not crowded, and there are lots of mobs with little searching required.

From there, you can move on to Shadweavers thicket and stroll up to level 9 with little difficulty. The mobs aren't quite as closely packed as Shar Vahl, but there is no shortage of them.

At level 9 (or 10, to be on the safe side), you MUST go to Paludal caverns. Its a kinder, friendlier blackburrow. The exp is awesome. The mobs you'll fight at first are little shrooms and crabs, and they are indiff with no faction. They will assist, but they are spread out enough that this is no problem. With minimal (very minimal) twinking, i was taking out 2 or 3 at a time...and there are tons of them, no shortage, and they respawn fast.

I'm currently about to hit level 15 in paludal, and I consistently see people in their 20s exping in there as well, so I imagine it will take me a good ways.

Hope this helps. I'm an old time shaman reincarnated as a Beastlord, but I still love these boards. :)

Finally, this sneak skill thing. If it works the same way as monk sneak/ranger sneak then I have a definite use for it.
PULLING.
A skilled player can use Sneak to single pull mobs that would otherwise not be able to single pull. How it works is this:
1)Turn on sneak
2) chuck a hammer/cast a spell at the mob in the middle of those other mobs
3) Book it away until you are out of aggro/assist range of those other mobs.
4) you just single pulled.

If you get hit by a spell or melee while still in assist range of those other mobs then it won't be a single pull. The others will come too.

I perfected this with my monk, and at lvl 52 can single pull almost any spawn in Howling Stones without using FD to split. Runspeed3 would help a ton with this, so I'm gonna do some AA next with the monkie.
