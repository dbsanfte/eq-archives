Jinheim
What is it i need the faction for?
Do barb shammies care about OT faction at all?

As a barbarian you start out KOS to OT and you will remain KOS to OT. You have absolutely nothing to lose by hunting in KC. Frogloks will also be KOS in OT, since they worship mithaniel marr. I don't have a clue how OT feels about the vah shir. Iksars are also hated in OT. All of these races are welcome in FV, though, so they have a base of operations in kunark (except iksars though, who of course have cabilis). Back during kunark and velious days, having a place to sell at and a guildmaster to visit somewhere on the continent were important. Now, though, it doesn't matter if FV, OT, and cabilis all hate you because you can just take a book to pok.

You also asked about some spells you bought in FV. Well, there is a corresponding evil vendor (who I believe is located in ocean of tears) who sells the exact same spells. I think there is another one in cabilis who, again, sells the exact same spells. There are a few spells you get via a turn-in quest in the barbarian hut in FV, and there is an NPC somewhere in either OT or OOT who does the exact same thing. Again, I think there is an equivalent NPC in cabilis but I am not sure.

So basically, to sum it up, I'm a barb and I hunted in KC. Been kos to OT my entire life and have never been bothered by it, except for when dragoons used to kill me.
