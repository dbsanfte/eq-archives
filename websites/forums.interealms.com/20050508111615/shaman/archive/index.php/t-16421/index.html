Bigmeeno
Items wont haste above your level... So a 40% haste item on a level 30 toon is going to be limited to 30% haste. As he levels, the haste will increase.

Spell haste doesnt have the same limitations.

However, there is an overall haste cap for each level. I dont remember the number for all the levels, but I know at level 60 its 100% haste maximum (ignoring bards here). Seems like the limit was somewhere near 50% at level 50, but I could easily be wrong on that.
