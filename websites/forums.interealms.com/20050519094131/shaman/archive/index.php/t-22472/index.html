Well, I play the decreasingly popular shm/monk duo. Shaman is at 3000+ hps and 4100+ mana, while the monk will be approaching 4k hps with a few more AAs (ND3 and PE). Shaman has 150ish AAs, while monk is pushin 90.

I guess I could put it this way --- damn near any non-pure-caster can tank decently with all their defensive AAs. Hell, my shaman can solo in Tactics, straight up tanking a slowed mob and keeping Q on him with little concern and he only has LR5 (currently working on ID5). So if a shaman can do it... well even a monk with their vaunted mitigation nerf can do it. In fact, mine does it all the time. He's got LR2 and that's it... on the rare double pulls, he will successfully tank 2 Tactics mobs at once with merely a Q on him.. sometimes throwing a Tnarg's to recover from spike damage. But it's not like it isn't manageable. I end those fights with plenty of health and mana.

Yes, level and AA and gear makes a difference... I won't lie. But eventually, you'll have that, so in the end, whether you have a SK or pally or monk or whatever --- you'll be able to do what you wanna do. The monk does some nice DPS while providing slightly less tanking ability, but then again a monk can mend for over 2000 hps ever few mins or so. A monk has some nice discs that can come in real handy. On those rare double pulls, I nail my auto-riposte disc and proceed to lay the absolute smack-down on 2 mobs at once for about 15 secs. Surprisingly, the monk keeps decent agro once the mob is past 80% health or so... not like I have a ton of mana left after I malos, slow, cripple and throw a Q on the monkie, but I can usuallyl throw a DoT or 2 and not worry 'bout losin agro. If I do.. the monk has it back in no time. Monks do nice DPS that the mob doesn't ignore. Even tanking and with a weak 2-hander, I exceed 100 DPS.

On top of that, dungeon crawling is a blast with a monk simply for lull and FD... there's not much I can't split and quickly with those 2 monk tools. When the ****e hits the fan I can at least FD the monk and gate the shaman. There's no zone I can't get out of with my monk using fast sprinting, FD, mend, regen and lull.... monks don't get stuck. :)

The combo does have it's limitations... I crave snare and stun... I wish the monk could taunt just a little better but not a huge issue... but other than that, it's a fun combo.

What's great about the combo is when I 2-box in a full group... I don't have to worry 'bout my shaman duties AND tanking... and the monk is always welcome for his pulling and DPS.
