I spent a lot of time writing this up only to realize that it was buried in a thread on the General forum, lol. So here it is in this forum.

I did this at 63 with Torpor and Bane of Nife. It was hard. At 65 with Quiescence and Blood of Saryryn it got MUCH easier. Now at 65 with those spells plus some really nice focus items and 1k more HP, it's almost trivial.

THE CAMP: Run to the guardhouse, up to the plateau in PoV; take a LEFT outside the door and invis across the WW camp to the zoneline (watch out, a few golems in WW see invis). Follow the zone edge to Lever. Go past Lever and to the NE corner of the plateau. Single pull golems easily here, just watch for wanderers, especially unrootable wandering spiders that are VERY hard to see.

Spell lineup:
Tnarg's
Root (level 14, cheap)
Turgur's
Malosinia
Malos
Quiescence
Canni4
Breath of Ultor

Buffs:
SOW
Focus
Ancestral Guard (yes, I use it)
Boar
Wrulan (yes, I use this too)
Acumen (makes it easier to see the spiders)

NO PET. Useless, he just dies anyway and you burn mana trying to keep him healed.

Start at max cast range, cast malos and turn your back to the mob while casting. When it lands, take off in as straight a line as possible. When spell gems refresh, stop and turn around, cast root when you have successfully turned around (the time to turn around is about equal to the time needed to guarantee no interrupt due to movement). Root WILL land after malos, or if not just run, rinse, and repeat until it does. Hit it with malosinia at this point to reduce root breaks, and Turgur's just in case it gets in melee range. Retreat to max epic cast range if you plan to use epic DOT, or to max cast range for regular spells if you are not using epic.

Now cast Breath of Ultor. While this is refreshing, unmem malosinia and mem Blood of Saryryn. While BoS spell gem is refreshing, cast epic. Retreat to max cast. Refresh root. Cast BoS. NOTE: I don't have Bane. If you have it, I'd have it up instead of BoU and cycle both BoU and BoS through the other spell slot. Cast BoU first, you'll only need one of those and then you can leave BoS up the rest of the fight.

Canni 5 if it's up. Quiescence if necessary. At this point typically I have not been touched, I'm at 80+ mana and quiescing up from 60 health.

At this point, you can go the patient route and just refresh root/BoS/BoU/Bane as necessary (without Bane this will take around two full durations of BoU but will never break root), or you can accelerate things a bit by using the DOTs that break root. Turn your back to the mob while casting the DOT that breaks root and run until root is castable, then turn and root, just like you did with malos. I like to load it up with disease dots because if they break root, they only break it once every 2 minutes :-) so generally I might have three disease dots and 1-2 poison dots running, plus epic. Bane would help a LOT, I really need to get that.

There are variations - sometimes I don't use the epic because you have to get so close to cast it, and the damage for a whole epic run is worth about four ticks of BoS. Sometimes I do throw a second disease dot just to speed things up but no other root-breakers because I'm being lazy. I do throw swarm pets in there occasionally but the damage is negligible, it's more for fun than anything.

I canni4 constantly and canni5 when available. If I get hit, I reroot, back off, and Quiesce; I VERY rarely ever use Tnarg's, in fact I am about ready to boot it off my spell gems when soloing. No horses, they are too awkward if the mob starts running at you and you have to retreat.

I am short a lot of nice AA's, but I have some great focus items to make up for them. No, I don't have Burning Affliction, but I have a DOT extension focus that makes them last longer, excellent for soloing; a buff extension focus plus SCR1 that together make Q last an extra tick; mana cost reduction foci; and range increaser focus. Although I did all this before I had those, it's a lot easier with them.

AA's that would help on top of what I have would be to finish out the SCM and SCR/SCRM lines; Quickbuff 2-3; maybe channeling focus; LR and other defensive AA's; and for dungeon soloing where you invis between camps, Suspend Minion 2.
