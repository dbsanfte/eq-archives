Ximix the Troll
I have done Gimlik a dozen times or so on Quellious, usually with one group.

Tips for Gimlik - you want 2 tanks, a mezzer, cleric and shaman (in my opinion). I have only done this at level 60, I have not attempted it since PoP and leveling to 65 - so it is probably easier now.

The biggest keys to gimlik are recognizing the ambushes that he spawns and reacting appropriately asap. Learn where things will spawn and have your tanks standing in the right spot to get initial aggro. If the ambush jumps on the wrong person first, your success chances go way down.

Ambushes he can spawn:

5-6 mobs level 55ish (enchanter AE mez handles)
4-5 mobs level 56ish (enchanter AE mez does not handle)
3-4 mobs level 57ish (enchanter AE mez does not handle)
1-2 mobs level 60ish (unmezzable at least with pre-PoP mezzes)

All of the mobs have the same name and spawn on top of each other - so targeting for mez and slow is a pain.

Tigir's Insects is fantastic for this event, I suggest keeping it mem'd. Group elixer from your cleric right as the ambush spawns helps a ton also.

The ambush's with 5+ mobs are the tough ones. If you control those with Tigir's, mez, and group elixer then you will have no trouble. Which ambushs you get seems totally random. We have gotten 1 mob ambush's 3 times in row, and have also gotten 6 mob ambush's multiple times in a row. So - you can get lucky on this event or get unlucky.

As shaman, my job was also to aggro Gimlik with Turgur's and tank him for most of the fight. This prevents him from wandering off and gettng killed while we are still fighting. Atone or Memblur once fight is complete.

Pre-clearing his path through the maze is advisable.

You only get one item as your reward. We seem to always get the 10 wis/100 mana earring or the FT1 range item - which sucks because we were always hoping for the 100 hp earring or the gloves that gate to nexus.

Feel free to give me a tell on Quellious if you would like to discuss.

A tip for those interested in tradeskills, as the brown lizards he spawns drop items at a greater rate.

You can respawn the ambushes by telling him "I am ready". This will port him back to the spire and he will begin walking. Also handy if you fear you screwed up horribly and want to try again, as it wipes his aggro list.
