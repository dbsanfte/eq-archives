Caanus Grey
I had read your question to mean "If I have the spell, can ANYONE'S group spells buff MY pet, or can MY group spells buff ANYONE'S pet?"

To answer that version of the question, it is the first option. Group spells begin to hit YOUR pet if YOU have the AA. Other caster pets in your group will NOT get the spell benefit unless THEIR owner has the AA.
