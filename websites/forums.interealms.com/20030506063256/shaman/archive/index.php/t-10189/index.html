Do you plan on slow-tanking, with the rogue attacking from behind while you hold aggro with slows, etc? If so, you will want to focus on HP/AC, because you will rarely be FM anyways... a larger mana pool is not as big of a deal to you.

Gearwise, I'm not familiar enough to make recommendations... i just run bazaar searches with the HP stat selected.

I personally have decided that a medium-AC approach with focus items works best for me. I sometimes take hits, but mainly support my 56 ranger with haste, slows, heals, etc. I have enhancement extension, affliction extension, affliction haste, mana preservation, and extended range focus items.

Affliction haste would be a good choice for you if you do plan on tanking and keeping aggro for the rogue (shaves a third of the time off). It'll let you get off dots and slows better while being beat on.

Gwain Buffmonkey
Sell flayed turmoilskin and buy a spider fur. Net + in cash.

Sell crystal chitin shield and get a SBS. Also a net + in cash.

Ears....electrum black sapphire are 2ac, 35hp, 25 mana....I consider them better than gold.

Dusty soriz boots....12ac, 40 hp, and good saves too. They're like 800 or 1k on terris.

Cloak...dire wolf hide, coterie general, shimmering terrorhide are all pretty cheap, andhave good hp for their cost.

You may not want to make all these swaps, as that'd drop your mana a whole lot, just pick and chose which would be the most effective or something.

I was comfortable as low as 149 wis, couldn't get any lower with the gear I had (when I switched from druman gear to shaman gear), so I can't speak for having lower.

Since you're both twinked, getting to the level where you can actually canni and use that HP shouldn't be a problem, even with lower wis.
