if you want to go to LoY zones, go to gunthrak and invis, go down the corrodor to the back door of nadox (not the front entrance by dulak zone).

there is a two spawn of guards that pops probably about every 15 minutes or so, and one of them in a PH for a SK guard that drops a necklace thing. once these are down, go pull singles from the path area. the diseased things have a chance of dropping a shroom looking thing, that has a one shot cast of clarity. it dropped twice for me in the 2 hours I was hunting there. money is not great, but don't just leave the drops from the mobs, one of the stackable things sold for almost 9pp to vendors with my cha at 110.

there is usually no one around this area (except for higher levels going into nadox) and the errant train from people that know about this area right now.

there is a spot that you can sit though that is far enough away that if someone trains they won't aggro on you, and usually only one or two of the critters come after anyway, and you can have them from there :)

I zoned into nadox a few times right here, and there are some elemental things that are over there, that conned white to a 42.

you can also try out dulak, or the zone in area to dulak (there is a little town like thing there). probably better drops than the area I was at....
