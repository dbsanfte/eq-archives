Naagruz
You are not using pathing or aggro range to your benefit it sounds like. Watch where the guards path and poke around inside of buildings to see if mobs will aggro on you from inside a building then pick a safe spot to fight and pull the mob to there.

You can fight safely in many different spots in Dulak and get the drunken pirates out of middle and end houses if you watch pathing a bit. You will do best if you run around as little as possible while fighting and try to stand just outside of melee range once you root a mob. If you are JBB tanking then you don't need wiggle room at all.

As far as "safe" spots go, you can fight easily on every dock entrance to the right of zone entrance, on the strip of ground just outside the porch at the bar, in spots behind the first row of houses to the right of the zone ent, and around the center lighthouse with the 2 trolls in it.
