I have worked in both ToFS and at the poachers camp extensively. ToFS at 47 solo is not a good idea. you need a healing partner in ToFS a cleric or shaman is ideal. Invis up to the wedding and make a ton of xp and cash. Keep in mind that in ToFS level one and 2 are undead, level 3 and 4 are alive. With one or two rare spawn exceptions. So fight thru one and two. (the mobs on 2 will follow you thru mirrors, level one will not)

The wedding is one of the best camps for pre 50 and it is almost always open. but you need a slower with you.

I have a friend who is 2 boxing a 49 druid and 42 ranger at the poachers in JP right now. He is rolling in the xp. When he gets board he fear kites griffs.

I think fear kiting griffs in JP is a good idea at 47 just remember to keep them close to town unless you have wolf form.

Good solo spots at 47
fear kite in eastern waiste and great divide.
Fear kite raptors next to sky zone in wakening lands.
