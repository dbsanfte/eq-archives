It depends on your goals in my opinion.

If you plan on leveling as fast as possible to 60 w/o stopping to explore and have fun, play an Ogre. The ability to keep from being stunned from the front would be nice to have later in life.

If you plan on leveling fast by soloing to a certain point without stopping, then taking your time to reach 60 while enjoying mid-level dungeons, Naggy/Vox raids, etc, I'd say go Troll for the Snare necklace.

If you want to be able to have an INITIAL leg-up on your gear, I'd recommend Iksar. Eventually, just one or two pieces of gear (if you play a non-iksar) will overcome the AC advantage, but I'm sure it will probably make a difference early on.

If you want to be able to travel and accomplish many quests without alot of faction work, I'd say choose a kitty or barbie shaman. The advantages of the other races aren't very important in the overall scheme of things. If SoL zones are available, the initial faction woes (inability to buy food except at home, horrible faction work just so you can use a vendor) are immaterial, as you will have Shadowhaven and the Bazaar available from Day one.

I personally would probably STILL choose Barbarian, with Froglok a second choice if that race is open for you. The aesthetic (barbies are beautiful!) & roleplay value of a Tribunal Shaman for me are worth more to me for entertainment value than any of the race-specific abilities.

Good luck with your Shaman regardless of the race you choose!
