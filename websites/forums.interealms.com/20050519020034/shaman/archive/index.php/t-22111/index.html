I'm proposing to start an ez-board called The Balancing Chamber ( http://p214.ezboard.com/bthebalancingchamber ). Please let me know what you think about the idea. Here is my rough concept for it:

There are plenty of boards out there for each class separately. The Balancing Chamber is a class balance board. No ONE class is more important here than any other class. If you think so, then this is not the board for you.

If you get your kicks flaming people that disagree with you in a civil manner, yet again, this is not the board for you. Incidentally, if you do flame and keep doing it, you'll likely just be precluded from coming back.

I will be looking at the class community sites and will try to pick a few representatives from each board to approach for moderators.

The goal is to get enough ideas that can be approved by enough of the class-wide player-base across the board that SOE will not need to worry about class balance. This would get done in what I call versions or stages. Each of these would have a clearly defined schedule of suggestion, rebuttal, suggestion, modification, final rebuttal, cutting out the losing suggestions and writing the final joint proposal. Then we would do the next round.

The process will likely be long and sometimes painstaking, but I feel like if we came up with joint class proposals that had a decent amount of thought put into class balance, and if we could get someone like Brenlo to read the final reports, it might just yield something. Hope to see you all here. And thanks for checking it out.

PIE!

Well, I'll throw my 2c in like usual.

First off all classes are not equal. When you look at the total EQ population Clerics, Warriors and Druids each have about 12-13%. Bards, Shaman, SK, Beastlord, etc. have (and I'm just going to round) about 5% each.

People like to conveniently forget about this when they talk about class balance. Also, given the way Sony has decided to balance the most difficult content, raids are usually dependant on the # of clerics available. That's the cold hard facts of the situation. And clerics are balanced for the most part (and correctly IMHO) by their healing power and not their utility or dps.

I think I have flipped positions on the "Priest" balance since I have read all sorts of posts and really thought about the "healing dilemma". My current take is what's good for the game > than what might be good for Shaman regarding healing balancing. And what I think is good for the game is a good strong healthy cleric class. Clerics should be clearly the best choice (and maybe the only choice) as a primary healer. When and if Sony takes that position, they need to re-tune shaman and druids to effectively compete for one of the 4 slots remaining in a group (excludes tanks as well, lol). How they tune Shaman? I don't care. Just do it. And make it fun, hopefully.

Would it be nice if every class had a similar population and place in the "overall balancing of the game"? Yes, I suppose it would be but that's not reality.

Think about if you were balancing Everquest. First you balance tanking and healing and the damage output of the mobs. Then you work on balancing the player DPS and the hit points of the mob. Then you work on the crowd control and the placement and agro range of the mobs. And then.....taa daa..you work on the utility classes to fill in the gaps - that be Shaman in case I lost you.
