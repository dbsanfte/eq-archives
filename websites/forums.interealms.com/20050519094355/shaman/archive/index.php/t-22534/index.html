One of the things that has been discussed has been an ability for shaman buffs to go over the stat cap. Looks like they listened...but were concerned that giving that ability to an obsolete class would limit it's impact on the game. So they made it a tribute effect instead. :D

Ok no flames! Anyways, there are some interesting tribute effects on test...I wonder if you will be able to have more than 3 running at once.




From the necromancer forum:

Theres new tribute choices on Test server.

As always this may/may not change and may/may not go live.

Arm of Power
Level 50
Tier 1 cost: 40 (yes, these are all pricey)

Tier 1 increases your max STR cap by 2.
Tier 2 increases your max STR cap by 4.
etc..etc..etc..

Theres one of these for each stat.

Fortune of the Staff
Level 50
Tier 1 cost: 30
Each tier increases your chance to crit with blunt weapons.

Theres one of these for each type of weapon. (Slash, blunt, peirce, h2h)

There is one for archery, but it doesnt increase crit chances, but it increases accuracy.

Theres also one that increases general melee crit chances, any weapon type.

Fortune of Suffering
Level 50
Tier 1 cost: 50
Each Tier increases your chance to crit each tick with DOTs.

Theres also one that increases Nuke crit chances and one that increase heal crit chances.

Second Chance
Level 40
Tier 1: 60
Each tier increases your chance to ressurect upon death.

Interesting.

REPEATING: As always this may/may not change and may/may not go live.
