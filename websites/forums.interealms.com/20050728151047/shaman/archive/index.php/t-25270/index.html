Aanbuffer
If you are familiar with the hole he isn't too tough to find. After you make your way through the city.......Instead of going into the tunnel heading toward Jaliel (ranger epic) and the old Kirn location, you head straight and go through the house and to the left. This will wind you through a couple halls and into an open courtyard. On your right you will see an entrance to another building, at the top in one of the rooms is Kirn. You should see ghosts, a couple of ratmen and a named imp (roams on the first floor) in the area.
This is about the best directions I can give you, but they may sound confusing if you arent familiar with the hole. It took me 4 tries of wandering around with my guild to find him, I had the same issues finding him after the revamp. ><
Anyhow hope it helps more than confuses you.
