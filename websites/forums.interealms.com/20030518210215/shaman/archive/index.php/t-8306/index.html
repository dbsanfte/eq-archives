You must do all the steps of the quest leading up to the handing in of Black Dire's pelt FIRST. The quest is faction based and if you hand in the pelt before doing all the preceding quest parts, the spirit will eat the pelt because you didn't have enough Truespirit faction. But don't worry -- all the steps before Dire's pelt are very easy, just involving some time on your part.

The epic quest is not bugged.
