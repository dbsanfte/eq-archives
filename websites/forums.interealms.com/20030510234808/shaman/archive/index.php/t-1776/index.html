Fright Terror and Dread are 3 of the minibosses in PoF... 4th been the Dracoliche. One of them has a DT which means the first pull needs to be done by a useless player (we use dorf pallies, and purposely fail so we get to watch them die again). Another has a nasty cold AoE and i cant remember the third ones ability. Anyway, Iksar Broodling spawns immediately at feet of these golems corpses, and drops the Tear (corpse lasts 8 minutes like normal corpses with items on)

We are talking about a pretty large and well equipped guild raid to get to these guys quickly and nail them... 5 groups minimum i would say for a Non-ToV armour and equivalent weapon equipped guild.

Did this last night.
Closest to the portal was Fright. He DT'ed (we sent in a low level ranger to take the DT for the raid, then had someone else pull him, as his DT has a longish recast time. ranger got to the foot of the hill before Fright Shouts "Aregorne!", instant death.)and afterwards the broodling spawned on top of his corpse. Next was Dread, he also DT'ed. No broodling.
Last and furthest was Terror, he's twice as tough as the other two...bring your cold resist gear for this guy, and buff up-- he has a 500pt AoE cold spell that hit me for between 180 and 250 with my CR at 202. Oh yeah, and I think he dispells buffs too. he spawned the broodling as well.
We did this with 4 or 5 groups, but only 2-3 clerics and 2 chanters.
