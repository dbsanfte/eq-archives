"To clarify...

During tuning, we took a look at how Sleight of Hand fit into the bigger picture of the expansion and the overall reduction in aggro that would be possible with current items and new ones being introduced with Omens. The end result was that in order to be balanced within the big picture, Sleight of Hand would have to be an insignificant amount of aggro reduction. Passive aggro reduction abilities have always been problematic since it's hard to see a concrete effect except when the overall reduction is large. The extremely minor reduction that Sleight of Hand would have offered would have been unnoticeable to most players. Instead of releasing an AA with no appreciable effect, I chose to cut it from the list.

Rashere"
