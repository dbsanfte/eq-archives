SisterLi
Don't sell the giant toe potions to the venders!

Use them. Or share them with friends, or sell them at cost to make.

They have great effects.

Ice giant toe potions are a heal in a can.

Fire giant toe potions are a very handy haste in a can for melee's.

Hill giant toes are a decent attack buff with small amount of regen. I've found it to be very handy for me young monk.

These effects are worth far more than the paltry amount the merchant venders are going to offer you.

As to your question -- is it worth it -- it depends on what your goals are.

1. If your goal is to make money, it is not worth it. There are steadier ways to make money.

2. If your goal is to have a handy skill on tap, and to make potions for personal and friend's use, I have to say it is very much worth it. I carry all sorts of useful potions around, most of which trived at over 100.

It is very nice being able to take your group deep into a dungeon without worrying about having a porter to get the magic impaired back out.

Gate potions, Regen potions, haste potions, damage shield potions, sow potions, inviso v. undead, and Ice giant toe potions all have slots in me bags.

At some point it might be revised enough to make it a marketable skill, but currently I do not find it so to be.

SisterLi
I'm not sure what the going rate would be. Cost varies. Herbs to make run around 33 plat per attempt. Toes off a merchant are 21 plat with top charisma. If you have to mine the merchant to get to the toes, the price goes up quite a bit. Triv is high, so you won't get to the 95% success rate until around level 57 or 58. (Assuming you've maxxed Alchemy). Bottom price to make a 10 charge, assuming you collected the toes of the giants yourself would be around 396. If you buy the toes from, price to make would likely be 648. Both figures assume 12 combines to make a 10 charge. Add in a profit margin, and some room for a bad run, and I'm thinking 1k per 10 charge sounds about right. If players charge you more for the toes (I've seen as much as 50 plat a toe), or you get unlucky on a merchant mining fest, then it could easily cost you 1k to make a 10-charge. At which point I assume you'd sell at 1250.

But to me they are too valuable to sell. It's something of a pain to get ice giant toes -- I have to buy them from people camping the giants or mine merchants for them.

Each toe makes one potion. Really need a 10 charge potion for it to be that "save your arse" trick up the sleeve that you need. My standard policy is that everyone should have 1 full 10 charge, and 1 partially used 10 charge on them. That's enough for a couple of emergencies, or one really nasty one.

I give them to the folks I reguarly group with, and we use them for emergency situations, or sometimes as a planned part of some crazy strategy.

Course we have quite a few "emergencies" in the normal course of exploring.

As a result I'm always looking for more toes, and think the potions are way to valuable to sell. I give them away to friends, but you nay can pay me enough to make it for you. Or perhaps you nay will pay me enough, so I'll keep it for meself. =P

To quote me rogue friend, "They're gold mate! Solid gold!"
