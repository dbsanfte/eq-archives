Only been playing 2 months.. so take my advice with a grain of salt.. I was around 41, and I took the battle shammy route of the velium fire wedding rings+sanark battle shield+ anything that could give me ac like all the jaundiced/totemic items I could find. At 41, I could root/rot with slow+vos+scourage+kei+debuff+regen+dog and take out frost giants scouts very easily. I did it newb style by just zoning in through pok book and going a little south. The further down you go the more of them there are and the more adds you get, but I would start there and as you progress go further down.
