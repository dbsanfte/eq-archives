Here's my ideas for what I would change in EQ at the present time, just my opinions...

1. Eth/Spec Parchment = Runes.
I'd like to see it possible to turn in 10 Eth Parchments for 1 Spec Parchment, and the possibility turn 10 Spectral Parchments for 1 Rune.

2. Shaman Slow vs, Enchanter/Beastlord/Bard.
I'd leave everything as it is (75/70/65/50, cast times, etc.) but I'd add a simple negative resist check on the Shaman line, like a -100 resist check.

Well just my ideas...
