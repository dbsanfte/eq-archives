Jinheim
Ok, here is what happened:

Used to, you could cast a spell, click an insta-cast item (i used sow boots) and then all the spell gems would refresh instantly and you could cast a spell. For example, you could cast tnarg's, click your sow boots, and then cast tnarg's again without having to wait the 3 seconds.

A couple months ago, they fixed it so that even if you clicked your insta-cast, the real spell that you just cast would remain greyed out for 3 seconds, as it is supposed to. However, the other 7 spells that you cast were ungreyed and you could cast them immediately. For example, you could cast quiescence on someone, click your boots, and then cast tnargs without having to wait the 3 seconds.

In the last patch, they fixed it so that clicking an insta-cast item does not refresh the spell gems at all. You have to wait 3 seconds to cast a spell, which is how it was intended. It was really annoying the first day or two, but I have gotten over it already. It really isn't that big of a deal.

What kozul said about rage refreshing before it was supposed to, thanks to a clicky item, is something different. There was a bug where if the spell you had in your #1 gem slot had a long duration, then using a clicky would make it come ungreyed before it was supposed to. However, even though it was ungreyed, you still couldn't cast it until it refreshed. I noticed this a lot because I always put avatar in my first gem slot. They have fixed this, though, and the spell will remain greyed for the proper duration.
