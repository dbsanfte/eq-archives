Maybe this will help - my 'default' and 'red' layouts. With no 'color shift' pressed or activated, this map gets used:

http://morelight.org/samanna/n52/EQ.n52.default.bmp

I hold the red-shift button to cast any AAs; any unmapped keys will do the default action. AAs are macros, like '/alt activate ##'; the rest are mapped to the keys that activate hotbuttons, or control movement.

http://morelight.org/samanna/n52/EQ.n52.red.bmp

The keys listed on the left are the 14 keys, top L -> R, mid L -> R, bot L -> R.

I haven't figured out a need for green and blue shifts, except that the blue matches my fragbox and keyboard lights. :D

And this is just another USB device, plug it in wherever. Extra keys!
