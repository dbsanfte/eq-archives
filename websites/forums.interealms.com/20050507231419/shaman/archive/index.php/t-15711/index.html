/set mode = pedant

Even if you assume that you melees have no haste items, you don't reduce the groups DPS by 10% by using ToA instead of SLTW.

Base damage = 100
SLTW Damage = 160
ToA Damage = 150

ToA damage is 150/160 = .9375 = 93.75% of SLTW damage (6.25% reduction in damage)

If you melees have 41% hate items, it becomes :
Base = 141
SLTW = 200 (cap)
ToA = 191

ToA = 191/200 = .955 = 95.5% (ToA is only a 4.5% reduction from the SLTW damage)

That doesn't include any damage from procs or spells. Neither of which is going to be reduced by a lower haste.

/set mode=normal

That being said, of course SLTW is more effective than ToA and I would (usually) chose SLTW to buff melees.
