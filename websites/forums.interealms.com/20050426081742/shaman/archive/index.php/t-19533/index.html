Biggest issue inside the castle or down on 'the ledge' (the deck and stairs far off to the right of zone-in) is not the mob difficulty; its the adds. Many of the mobs inside the castle even aggro thru doors and walls, and there are a couple of wanderers, which means you'll be doing CC along with your root-rotting.

I cleared the entry hallway and cul-de-sac a few dozen times and also solo'ed a bit on the ledge (there are a couple of safe spots) but my experience is that exp and loot don't compare to other places like PoTactics or Droga. Most of the loot that drops there is stuff like Sarnak Battle Shields, Staves of Shielding and the like and these are all 500p-1k items on Xegony, whereas Droga and tactics have 10k-20k drops.
