Largus
Merry, you have just hit my two favorite zones post 50. CoM is great exp at 53-55 (and I did the last coulple of blues there at 56). HHK may be better but I did not want to ruin my faction. After ZI, you will follow the corridor to a gate (there are 2 spawns behing a wall before the gate so bring up dog before you head in). Kill the two close to the ZI and the three behind the gate ( you should be able to single pull the gate). Now you are basically going to keep going back, you will have to turn at different spots to get to/go through the doors but it is in same general direction. Once past the large courtyard, you will come to 4 spawns, that is the u-turn and this is where you want to be. Start pulling and have fun. Gazebos are around the u-turn and you may not be able to solo pull them yet. I know it is kinda confusing but best thing is to get a map at eqatlas or other places. There is another u-turn on the other side (one on the north wall, one on the south wall) that you can di but it is tough getting the dog to follow you in there. If you get in trouble, run to the courtyard back wall and camp out, pathing in there is terrible and I always got camped before they found me. SoW does work there as well.

JP is great exp once you get your epic or hit lvl 56-57. I am there now at 57 and pull about a blue every 45 minutes.

Hope this was not too confusing and good luck.

Roxxanne
I found the gazabos to be great exp at 53. Using a JBB and horse and doggie, was able to keep both gazebos clear (all DB) and pull some of the U-turn mobs on both sides while waiting for repop. Non-stop pulling if you can get to camp both gazebos.

Breaking the 3 spawns in each gazebo can be tricky. I usually cleared the right side U turn and park dog there. Go stand on the wall/railing to target one of the 3 right gazebo mobs that is facing you; pull with immobilize; if the other two both come, go to corner lampost to your left and park one far from the pooch, then run the other down to the pooch. I seemed to be able to single pull the left gazebo just with slow from a distance, but the same techniques would apply.

If you bind at the entrance, you can gate and zone to clear agro then run back. This sometimes was needed when I botched the spawn break and couldn't handle the 3 pull.

One you have the spawns spaced out, move to the central bridge going into the reaver house and pull to there. That spot helps make it clear you are camping both gazebos.

Never any good loot, but lots of experience and epic parts.
