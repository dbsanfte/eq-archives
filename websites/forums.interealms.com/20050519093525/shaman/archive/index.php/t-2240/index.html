Gloriana
The way I understand it, FT adds mana regen ticks. So, a FT1 item would add one point of mana regen per tick.

Clarity 1 - 7 points a tick
Clarity 2 - 11 points a tick
Clarity 3 - 14 points a tick

Each FT item provides a certain number of mana regen points per tick as indicated by the FT number associated with the item. The items stack. For example, if you had the FTIII boots from CT and the FTIII earring from Tunare and the FT1 Eye of Cazic Thule you would gain an additional 7 points of mana regen per tick (equal to clarity). The FT mana regen stacks with innate mana regen (ie medding), clarity type spells and bard song.

With the AA skill, mental clarity, you can earn 3 points of innate mana regen that also stacks.
