Here are some easy "fixes" that will raise your defense significantly.

Legs: If you can wear Jasarth, get a pair from KC (free), if not do the Kromrif Military Leggings quest (easy) for a pair of Ac 14, +6 wis legs.

Bracer: do the Coldain Military Bracelet quest (see my post in the Quest and special item section on how) That will be a HUGE upgrade.

Arms: Get rid of the Ulthork and add some JB arms. Cheap and very nice AC.

Range: get a Medicine Totem

I would bet with these changes, you will break 800 Ac easily without breaking the bank.

Ok I wrote a response and then clicked you profile.. it killed it...

Simply put - you have some good gear there you'll not have to replace for a while.

Have a look at my profile - in game I have 950+ac unbuffed and only recently added the shoulders and earring. My main chunk of AC comes from the Skyshrine armour. My wis is kept at ~230 because with KEI and/or bearform I still hit 255. This wis will drop as I change bits :)

I'd suspect these profiles for 900-1200ac will be for shaman that have velious armour. For 1100+ac I would suspect kael armour.

There are three types of velious armour that make the basis of most Oracle's gear:
Thurgadin: chain look, basic AC and basic stats. need faction with dwarves. Drops in kael arena etc
Skyshrine: dark blue chain, higher AC than thurg, highest stats of the three armour sets. need faction with Skyshrine. Drops in HoT (east ToV)
Kael: yellow chain, highest AC of the three, higher stats than thurg armour. need faction with Giants. Forgotten where this drops lol..

This is ignoring PoM etc (PoM boots are 45ac to give you an idea)

I'd start thinking about raiding in kael, at 55 I'd think of HoT.

I would say invest in some resist gear for HoT as the AE mobs will cold/fire AE for 300-350dmg.
West ToV mobs AE for 500 (telk) and north tov mobs will start AEing with some fun 1200dmg..
An if you annoy a nToV mob that's unslowed... well I got flurried for 950 a pop and was turned into mush by Lady M...

HP rule for shammies. AC comes a close second (softcap at ~1300ac), resist are important but as with wis can be maxed out with buffs.

Hope that helps.

A few gear upgrade suggestions:

Replace your legs with Brown Chitin Legs, more AC and better stats.

Replace the belt with Chitinous Belt, more AC and better stats as well.

Replace the arms with Crystal Chinin or Gladiator Chain. Either of these has a much higher AC than what you have.

Replace the Ry'gorr BP with Seb Scale or Froglok Scale BP

All of these can be picked up relatively inexpensively while waiting to get the quest armors that people have mentioned.

I have quite a few of these pieces and can hit 900 AC buffed at level 46 and still maintain a 180ish wisdom.
