Ticktockman
I'd recommend a 2nd enchanter, 55+, to help with the break. 3 enchanters will make it a cinch.

Zone in, turn left, move to west wall by the crooked tree. If you get aggro from a see invis mob, bring it. They'll be coming anyway. However, if you get feared, stand and die. You don't want to bring anything to west wall that didn't come from the portal area or points in between.

Highest level enchanter faces the portal and mezzes mobs coming from that direction. 2nd highest level ench covers north. 3rd ench/bard/necro covers south. Main tank calls targets and everyone assists.

Best bet with the above group makeup is to head north and go for Terror. We use a ranger to pull Fear; I find they get DT'd far less than monks do. Of course, they also bring more mobs in at once, so you must be prepared to deal with a train. Group a CotH mage with your monk and he may live longer.

Buff cold resists for Terror. He's not hard, but have the ench ready to charm/mez his 2 amy knight buddies. If you can get some wizards to come you might give Dread and Fright a try; you need decent damage for them or the DT will do you in. Chances are good that the iksar child will spawn.

Make sure any friends you invite can pay attention and listen to orders. Pick up Fear raids are recipes for disaster. Good luck.
