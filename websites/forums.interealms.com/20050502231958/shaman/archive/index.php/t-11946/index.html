Eatbugs
Pets make great xp group tanks, if you're pulling only one or two mobs at a time. (or more, if you have an all pet group - those are fun, but tend to be chaotic.) Pets can't:

1) grab aggro on a mezzed mob
2) choose the right target to kill next
3) pull and tank on the way back to the group
4) choose the right place to die so the rest of the group doesn't get aggro from an overpull
5) taunt things off the damage dealing classes when they're in melee range
6) switch targets reliably
7) reposition mobs reliably (A good pet handler can do this, but it doesn't work well when the pet is MT for a group.)

If you're willing to give up all those things and spend a lot of mana healing the pet (pets have tons of hitpoints at upper levels, but never get the defensive skills/spells a decent tank will have so they take more damage) go for it. Personally, if I'm in a group I prefer to have a warrior, SK or Paladin who knows his job. (If one isn't available, then sure, I'll use a pet for MT.)
