Brohg
At 46 as a shaman, you're going to have to be extremely patient and careful to solo anything in PoP. It's not, as a rule, very worth it until you're in your high 50s at least.

If you'd like to solo out a bunch of levels quickly, Lower Guk is recommended, and High Keep Nobles&Bards. Either option can take you to 55ish in a week or so of dedicated exp'ing. Be aware that killing in HHK carries a faction consequence, if you care about stuff like that.
