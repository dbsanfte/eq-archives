Cappi Aprile
From what I understand catform doesn't use the weapon dps. Its all factored by level + talents. The reason it reports less is because your special attacks like BS make up for the defecit. Druid BS is 250% base damage vs Rogue 150%. The reason being we get the bonus from our held weapons.

I wouldn't take the warrior bashing too much to heart. Everyone here has different experiences, and warriors go through several periods of evolution. The fact that they can wear plate, and have different spec templates means that while a PvE warrior might be easy to kill at lower levels wearing chain, a fully spec PvP offensive warrior is a totally different challenge.

I don't believe any one particular class sucks over all others when it comes to PvP. Since it's a rock paper scissors situation there is always going to be a few classes that you will outshine one on one. As a Rogue I find Mages, Warlocks, and Hunters to be easy kills, and Paladins to be a real challenge. I bet that same hunter I can kill 100 times in a row hates rogues, but love fighting paladins since they have to range defense. It's all from the perspective of your class.
