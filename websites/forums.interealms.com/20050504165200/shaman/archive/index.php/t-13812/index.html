With bard song and maybe some slow procs (ranger epic, willsapper), both effects will stick to the mob, however only the higher % slow will have any effect. Haste works the same way. Talisman of Alacrity and Vallon's Quickening might both take hold on the same person, but only VQ will have an effect.
