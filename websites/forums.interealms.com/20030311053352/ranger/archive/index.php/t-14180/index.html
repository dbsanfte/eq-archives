In HHK i try to get KEI if I can - KEI in Bazaar - Get to PoK port to Misty Thicket/Rivervale, to Kithicor then sprint to HHK (takes about 5 minutes). If no KEI I usually bribe youngins in the Keep to give me clarity or at the worst Breeze. Every little bit helps for mana regen
Items I use that help me with free mana
Earring of Woven Bark - Free snare
Tolans Arms - Free Stinging Swarm
Swarmcaller - RangerShaman - Free Slow when it procs

The strategy ive been using is
Root
Stinging Swarm
Snare
Drones of Doom
Immolate

I have Force of Nature and Chloro on me as well as a damage shield (19hp DS per hit). I regen 26 hp per tick with Iksar BPand Regen 3, so I will attack til i get to 60 or 70 hp on me, then back away and root again so I can med a little and let dots continue to rot them. If you need regen time you can hide and sneak to sit without fear of getting attacked by Isa as she circles the Noble room. Let her pass and she'll return shortly again.
Nobles are on a 6 min spawn, and Isa is just as quick from the Bards room. Isa stops by the little card table, says"I love this place", at that point root her in place. If you are doing well pull her early from othe room. At 55 I can single them all out as long as I stay around 10 feet away from themto avoid agro. I find if i root away from the spawn points in far corner I can control them easier, so sometimes i snare first to yank them off spawn point.

Not going to work for everyone but I find it to be quite successful
