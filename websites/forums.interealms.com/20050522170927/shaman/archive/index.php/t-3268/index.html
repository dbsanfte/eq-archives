stormrazor
I'm currently in RM hunting HG's for cash. This is how I solo them.
I make a pet buff him with haste, Talisman, and SoW then I turn taunt off. I buff myself to the hilt...Agi, Dex (need it for proc on my hammer), Str, Talisman, Haste and Shifting Shield.
Pull out my Ugly Stick (Drakkel Ice Grinder)

Find a giant and pull with Malisement.
Slow him with Togors and DoT him with Venom of the Snake.
Me and Cujo pummel him into the ground. Depending on how the fight goes I occasionally have to heal during battle but, this method has been very effective for me.
I tried Root/Rotting but that took too long and took too much mana.

Exp is slow but I've made over 600pp there and gotten half way to 42.
