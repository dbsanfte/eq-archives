I find that it's not really comparable to EQ KS'ing. You know *instantly* at the beginning of a fight whether you're going to get credit or not (as opposed to EQ's 51% of the damage rule) - so you're never... say... laying in the majority of your mana on DoTs, just to have some asstard wizard steal it by nuking at 60% health. *laugh*
