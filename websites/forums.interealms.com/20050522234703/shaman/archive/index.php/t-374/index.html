Bigguss
At level 51, +20 wis adds 200 mana to your mana pool (240 at level 60.) It adds nothing to your mana regen rate.

A fungi adds 9000 hp/hour (15/tick, 150/minute, 9k/hour) - which can be cann'ed for a heck of a lot of mana (3000-4500 or so depending on which Cann, what level you are, etc.) - and this is an increase in mana regen, which is what really counts.

I'd seriously have to think of whether I'd take an Emerald BP over an Iksar BP (Iksar = 19 AC, 3000 hp/hour regen) - but with a fungi it's no contest.

Regen > WIS

Fungi > Emerald BP

Happy hunting!
