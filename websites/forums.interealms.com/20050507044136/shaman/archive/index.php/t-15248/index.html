Well bit more info about your pet and the weapons we feed them....

Any 1 handed weapon that is droppable = Pet will use
Any 2 handed weapon = Pet will NOT use
Any No Drop weapon = Pet will NOT use
Armor* = Pet will use ( Head, Face, Hands, Feet, Chest, Back, Wrists, Shoulder )

Equipment slot restrictions do matter ( example: Sarnak Parrying Blade (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6840) in Secondary slot only)
Any Class or Race restrictions on weapons/armour do not matter.


Dly - Does not effect
Dmg - Does effect (Only if greater then natural damage modifier)
Effect - Does effect (Only if pet level is higher then weapon Proc level)
Str** - Does effect (Increases Pets Average damage per hit)
Dex** - Does effect (Increases Pets Proc rate)
Agi** - Does effect (Marginal depending on the amount increased to effect the AC ussually not really worth it but can be at times)
Sta** - Does not effect (By most popular Opinion it has ZERO effect)
Int** - Does not effect (By most popular Opinion it has ZERO effect)
Wis** - Does not effect (By most popular Opinion it has ZERO effect)
Chr** - Does not effect (By most popular Opinion it has ZERO effect)
AC** - Does effect (Marginal depending if it pushes pets AC over a AC threshold)
HP** - Does effect
Mana** - Does effect (Our pet really does not use any mana so no real use for our pet)

*NOTE: I know for a fact pets equip Mage summoned armor. (Huge tanking ablity increase) With that information I would hazard to guess they would follow the same rules for equiping armour as they do for Weapons. (Example: Will not equip no drop armor)

**NOTE: It really does not matter what causes the change in stats be it weapon, spell, or Armor they affect the pet the Same

The biggest thing to keep in mind is our pets unlike all other classes pets will not duel weld unless forced to by making them equip a weapon in thier secondary slot. The easist way to do this is by giving them two cheep weapons Rusty Weapons work very well for this. Though if you gave them a secondary only weapon they would equip that weapon and would duel weld from there out. Just be careful about giving them Primary only weapons as they will not equip them unless that is the first weapon you gave them. From the best of my knowledge will not unequip a weapon to equip a primary only weapon.
