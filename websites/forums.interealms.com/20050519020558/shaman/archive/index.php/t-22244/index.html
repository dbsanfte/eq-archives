When I first started reading this board during Luclin era (when I made my shaman), it was full of lots of useful information on spells, quests, and general things about the game as it relates to shaman, which helped me immensely in figuring out our class. For a while now, though, the percentage of useful posts has decreased, to the point where I am actually surprised when I read something informative. The majority of these threads that Garf mentioned all say the same basic things, none of which are new to anyone, including Sony. Now I'm not talking about posts by people who parse stat buffs to prove their uselessness or threads which are aimed at compiling positive improvement ideas for the shaman class, such as the polls by Murtak recently. I'm talking about the posts that demonize Sony for destroying the shaman class without offering any suggestions for a solution.

We all know that shaman have serious class balance issues, especially stackability on raids (which we share with quite a few other classes). Instead of repeating this fact over and over in reworded threads which accomplish nothing, our complaints and desires should be forwarded to Sony through a class representative - apparently they felt the same way. This way, we can use the board to disperse new, useful information to our fellow shaman without cluttering it with complaints. Also, remember that being reasonable in our requests for our class that we send via our class rep will go a long way toward seeing them realized - i.e. things that Sony might actually implement, rather than things like giving shaman rez, making Daluda a 4k heal, etc.

Anyways, I'm just hopeful that having someone through which we can channel our discourse will lower the amounts of trivial threads on this board and turn the forum back into what it used to be - the most informative and useful class board for EQ on the Internet.
