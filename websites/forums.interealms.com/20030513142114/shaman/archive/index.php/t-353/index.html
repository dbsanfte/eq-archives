It is, I'm sorry to say, impossible to get the tear alone now. In order to get the Iksar Broodling to spawn, you must kill one of the named golems (the bodyguards of Cazic-Thule). I can never remember which of the three spawns him, but normally he will spawn twice if you kill all three.

The little iksar no longer spawns on his own and is only going to spawn after one of the golems is killed. So you'll have to go along on a raid that is capable of killing the golems. They're not TOO difficult, but it will take a sizable force to take them down.
