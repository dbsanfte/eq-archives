My favorite two cash spots are plane of growth and the NG (guard) camp in sebilis. The latter is usually camped but PoG generally has plenty of mobs for me. Both camps drop alot of gems and such that you sell to vendors, so make sure you have a pair of opal-encrusted steins (25cha each) and buff your charisma when selling. Also, some vendors don't pay as much as others so be sure to 'shop around'.

Zhorn
I used to "buy low/sell for higher" in the bazaar.
I.e. buy an iksar regen BP for 4kpp, sell for 7kpp.
or buy Malo/Torpor for 5kpp and resell for 30kpp. $$
Obviously you may need to start small depending on how much plat you have. Also never be afraid to send someone a tell offering EVEN LESS than what they are asking....

Takes awhile to learn the prices of stuff.
It used to be more fun in the old days of the East Commons Tunnel.

Now days I make plat from selling Tae Ew Shields/Barbarian Cultural Armor/etc/etc.

However it took me 2 months of no exping to get from smithing 188 to max mod skill (thankfully I got a +10% smithing belt so 230 skill is max mod skill for me).

You could pharm Ice Giants in Permafrost or you could make plat from doing low level tradeskill combines (i.e. Celestial essences cost like 2 plat to make and sell for 7 plat each... sell 1000 = 5kpp profit). Lots of clicking involved though!!
Other stuff you could learn to make easily are: Leather Padding or maybe Heady Kiola's.
Just make sure you get a decent number of skill points (like maybe 20 or 30 for low skill items) over the item's trivial to cut down failures.

Goldark
Find a rogue, and duo the crypt in Sebilis. Only worth while if you can stay a good while (3-4 hrs maybe), but you have the chance of getting some really good stuff, including malo and torpor from the Emperor if you are really lucky.

ODS - ~10k
JBB - ~20k
Heiro cloak - ~12k
Numerous other bits for a few hundred pp each
Gems to sell to vendors

A lot depends on luck, but go there a few times and your time will come. And when the rogue can't make it, NG and DHall/Disco 1 offer good cash from gems. I made about 2600pp from one KEI's worth of soloing NG.
