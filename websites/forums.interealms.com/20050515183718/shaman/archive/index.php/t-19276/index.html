Jinheim
I want a better heal because healing is one of my primary roles. I enjoy being the one to keep my group alive through raids, and I enjoy being the primary healer in LDoN adventures. It is a lot more fun than slowing each mob then just casting a nuke or two.

As for better dots and nukes, I am not very concerned about it. I'd like to have them, but I am not going to make a big deal of it. Some shamans feel that their secondary role after debuffing is to do damage, whereas I feel it is to heal and ensure that the rest of your group/raid stays alive.

As for better slows, I think turgur's is as powerful of a slow percentage-wise as we are going to get. Slow is already extremely powerful, and even a 5% upgrade to turgur's would be a 20% increase in damage mitigation. It would be unbalancing for the game in general if our slows got any more powerful. What I have wanted for a long time, though, is a fast-casting slow with a resist check that can be used as a supplement to turgur's. We are getting exactly that in GoD, so I'm a happy shaman.

A disease-based slow with a bigger %-slowed would be good, but I hardly ever use grummus so I'm not overly worried about it.
