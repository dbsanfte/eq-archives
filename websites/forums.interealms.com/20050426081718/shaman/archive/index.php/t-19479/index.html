Juventus
The drops are painful, and you won't be able to hold down more then a couple of the named spawns in tier 4 alone, most likely just one. Your best bet is to bring a group with an enchanter or ballsy cleric to pacify pull the spawns, then all you have to kill is phers. Doing it that way, you can easily hold down all of the tier 4 named mobs, whis will net you a full set of quest pieces in an average of 40 hours or so. The tome of dark healing is the hardest of the drops to get. The aforementioned website says it can drop in any tier off named priests, but I have only seen it drop from the Archon and Evangelist. To give you perspective, I got mine finished with the help of friends and during the questing earned roughly 35AA's at a similar XP rate as 1 group fighting in Tactics. It can be a hellishly long camp. My monk was there the whole time too, and he still lacks 2 pieces.
