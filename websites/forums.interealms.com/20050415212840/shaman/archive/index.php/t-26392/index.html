Tordail Farstryder
CardPlace LDON Search (http://www.cardplace.com/eq/ldon/list.cgi)

That's a site I've always found useful for LDON stuff.

As a shaman, you'll want to hit Everfrost hard. That's where you'll find the Incorporeal (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/itemlist.html?searchtext=Incorporeal) stuff. That's the "top end" LDON armor, the 1492 armor. It's pretty sharp when it's augged fully. Next down is Black Mire (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/itemlist.html?searchtext=black+mire) gear, that comes from South Ro (read: Guk) and is around 760 points per piece. The Ghastly stuff from Butcherblock really isn't worth the trouble; better to save up for *at least* Incorporeal, IMO.

For the paladin, I think the best (read: 1492) plate stuff comes from North Ro (Takish-Hiz), which I think is called "Royal Attendant ____ of Sand."

You can use the link above to search for augments for various foci, HP, mana, and the like. It tells you which camp the aug comes from, how many points it is, whether or not it's LORE, and what slots it's available for.

Good luck -- and keep up with your hails for the various camp NPC's to increase your adventure stone stats.

note on ldons and losses.... heres how to get one one for one or two people without the others taking a loss.

1/ form a three person group and request the LDON

2/ those not going then leave the group BEFORE the first person zones in. They will NOT take a loss as long as no one has zoned in yet.

3/ those still going will still have the mission and can zone in even if the group only has 1 or 2 people in it now. Now you can solo or duo it and no one takes a loss cept you if you fail :)
