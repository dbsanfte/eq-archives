GandarrTheletch
I solo in there quite often, it's really not much harder than regular guardian wurms in Skyfire. The zone in is "practically" safe, the g-wurm that roams to the zone in rarely gets close enough to agro and even more rarely sees invis, just zone in invised to check things out. I suspend minion and invis past the pairs of racnars, as they always come in pairs for me, and just stick to the g-wurms. I've never had one hit for more than 530, but some of them do have AEs ... some are fear, some are DD + dispell, and one that I've always resisted was some thing with the word "Mind" in it (forget the full name, sorry). Just have your resists up, a clicky buff in first slot, and be careful when they start to run so you don't get adds.

Spell drops are usually junk, even inside VP unfortunately, I've managed to get a couple Voice of the Berserkers, couple of Form and Mask druid spells, couple wizard nukes, nothing much that sells for over 10k and lots of junk spells in between. The cool thing is the g-wurms with the AEs aren't much more difficult than the regular ones, yet they give 11-12 percent AA exp versus the regular 5 percent AA exp. Heck of a lot easier than soloing the Magmas in PoFire, almost the same exp, and you get spell drops to give to friends, hehe.

Have fun!
