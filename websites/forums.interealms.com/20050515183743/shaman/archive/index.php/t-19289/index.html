I am sure that many who are in Time enabled guilds are aware of the recent rash of bugs that have crept up since the GoD beta went live. Now, if there is a correlation or not, who knows, as the dev team at this point isn't giving any information besides that they are working it.

Over 3 weeks ago, our guild received a bugged Time instance, meaning that after clearing phase 1 and expecting every subsequent phase to be available, we were greeted with an empty zone and got ported out 5 minutes later.

We petitioned but received no help at all that night, and decided to petition the next day and let our Instance ID expire as has been suggested on numerous websites now.
