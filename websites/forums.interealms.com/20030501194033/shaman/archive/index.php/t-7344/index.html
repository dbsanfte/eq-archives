The formula for success rate is reportedly:

Success Rate = (1.15 x Skill) – (Trivial – 70)

so for SoW with a skill level of 40:

Success Rate = (1.15 x 40) - (37 - 70)
Success Rate = (46) - (-33)
Success Rate = 79%

So...

If you had a 40 out of 46 rate = 87%
If you had a 40 out of 47 rate = 85%

Either way, you did better than the norm.

As your skill gets higher, trivial actually starts to mean "success". The higher the skill level and the trivial level, the more likely you will reach the 95% success rate cap.

For example, a shaman with skill of 165 attempting to make port potions (trivial at 170) would have a better success rate on a NON-trivial potion than you with a skill level beyond the SoW trivial. See:

Success Rate = (1.15 x 165) - (170 - 70)
Success Rate = (190) - (100)
Success Rate = 90%

Hope that helps. :)
