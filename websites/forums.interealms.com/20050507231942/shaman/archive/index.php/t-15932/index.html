Hey Guys/Gals,

I really enjoy using Spiritcall and don't really find any limitations on it. Mana free damage is always good.

There is the concern of push but all you do to overcome this is run a circle around the mob as soon as you cast it. That way you get pets all around the mob and you can actually assist in pinning a mob quite well. Of course on a raid with lag to deal with you cannot always do this so you have to use your judgement a bit.

On Rizlona in Sol Ro Temple we had someone running a parser of all melee damage and I was quite pleasantly surprised to see that my Spiritcall pets pumped out 71DPS for the duration they were up. Stack that on top of n00ks and my DPS for that fight would have been reasonable which is rare for a shammy :) . Also quite funny was that 71DPS on my pets was enough to beat some of the pallys ROFL!

Cheers,
