Grippa
Also, it's possible to get good faction in N FP reasonably easily, and you can then walk around most of the zone unscathed (but beware of the bards).

The easiest way, if you can do it, is to find a paladin who's killing Lucan for the soulfire quest. A single tap to lucan (assuming he dies) will get you a massive positive faction hit in NFP.

If you are of sufficient level, it's great fun to take a few friends and hunt the guards in east Freeport (I think it's east - the zone with the docks). As these are corrupt militia guards, you get positive faction with the paladins who control north freeport. On the role playing server, I imagine this could provide excellent RP opportunities - evil trolls invading freeport etc :) Just watch out for the occasional jerk player who will train every guard in the zone onto you.
