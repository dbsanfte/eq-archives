Grimthor
I did 59 in a week, played probably about 20 hours. Mainly doing longish sessions in upper spiders in Velks and Juggs/Crypt in Sebilis. You need a group for both - go heavy on melee in Velks, you will need some form of crowd control in the Sebilis camps. The reason you need to do long sessions is that it will take you some time to get the camp, and to get bedded into a good rhythm.

If you can get there, a duo/trio in Juggs can work wonders (with a rogue and monk ideally). Get the monk to pull, then you slow, and root tank for the rogue and monk. Once they are slowed they don't do that much damage (I have a fungi, and was buffing with bear form and regrowth) and since the monk and rogue are both attacking from behind, they take the juggs down quite fast. You can also throw some DoTs in if you have the mana for it.

If you really feel brave grab a strong monk and go duo in the castle in Velks. I was getting 1% of level 59 per kill. We broke in with a group, and then camped in Velketor's room (he was not up) and pulled to there. We handled single and double pulls easily, triples with a little bit of thinking. There is a passage behind the throne that leads to the Crystal Eyes camp, so you can get to the zone fast if you need to.

I found 59 MUCH easier than 58. But I was racing against a rogue guildie to get to 60 first. You have a similar incentive - go for it, and good luck!

The Grey (solo or group): Skellies in the southwest corner, stuff at the temple or sun revs at the pool (always lvl 55, fairly high hp)
