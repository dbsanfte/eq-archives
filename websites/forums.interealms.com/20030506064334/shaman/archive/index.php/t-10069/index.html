Grayskies
You will be able to single pull everything in both the ZI and the ZO tunnels in Chardok. Pet tanks for me and one Ebolt and one BoN generally drop any opponent in either tunnel.

ZO is a little harder to break, but is doable. I generally buff up at ZI, invis myself then head to ZO. There will be a mob on either side, I generally root the one on the left and then slow the one on the right. After these two are dead, I bring pet up. When using this strategy, it is good to con the dogs as you go, as some of the Chokidai Sniffers will see through invis.

Some people like to work their way from the fort/gate side of ZO tunnel towards the actually ZO, but I find that fighting too close to the fort/gate to be problematic, and having to zone right after you've given puppy 2 precious Gnoll whips really sucks. :)

At 59 I am not able to single pull from the fort/gate yet.

There is also a very good guide to soloing in Charasis posted on this board.

At 58 Katta is starting to get light blue - still real easy exp and pretty nice cash flow, if ya loot/sell the weapons. With horse ya don't even need KEI. Since PoP came out, Katta is no longer crowded.

You can also do the archers in Echo Caverns. Two archers each drop a pair of bows, so 4 bows that I sell for 23 pp each every cycle. The archers and the 4 scoundrels each drop plat as well, up to 6. At 58 you probably will not get to the skeletons in the tunnel (arisen soldiers?) unless you have KEI (no horse here). If you do try the skels, at 58 I would root one in the middle so that only one would come. First time I got two, and had a tuff time finishing them off. Once they are split they are cake. skels can drop up to 8 plat.
