Bindamel
I have yet to see 100 pp/hour in EF. I play mostly U.S. prime time, so there's a lot of competition. If there's an average of 1 tusk per mammoth, I have to kill one every 6 minutes to make 100 plat per hour. I haven't been that fortunate on spawn or drop rate to do that. On the other hand, my RL brother has done them during off hours, and has gotten close to that.

You can do the highland kobold camp in Stonebrunt for 100 pp/hour if you get KEI. You'll still do decently well without it, but you'll have downtime.

You can do bandits in WK, as well. They probably are the best plat for their level when you add in the sash quest in Qeynos with Chesgard (?).

Never been there, but Aquagobbies are easy cash in Butcherblock, but my understanding is that they are permacamped as well. I always look when I'm there, so I can "loot parse" it, but it's always been camped, and never by anyone of an appropriate level.

Another no fuss option is the warrens back door (i.e. Stonebrunt side). At 32 you should be able to kill non-stop and just sell the junk. Not as profitable, I would guess, but very easy.

If you're willing to do the bazaar trader thing, and patient, then spiderling silks, spider silks, pelts, and just about everything you forage is sellable in the bazaar. You can get 10 pp for a morning dew on a lot of servers.

If you want xp too, fear kiting in Stonebrunt leads to the Wak quest, which sells for 200 pp regularly on EMarr in the bazaar. You'll get the occasional jewlery and gems, as well, and pelts.

Your probably just getting to the levels where you can hunt heretic invaders in Stonebrunt. Four heads yields a Swiftclaw Sash (rare, worth 1-2k), or a Kejekan tribal headband (70 pp in bazaar). They spawn once a game day, though, so it takes a while.

There are some guides in the New guides section you may want to check out as well.
