Landragoran Heronwing
Go fool around with it for a while and then come back with some real questions. I suggest PvPing in the arena with all comers. Try two or more at once to get a grip on your skill level.
With melees, strip their buffs first and then Malicious decay them. Then see if you can Virulent Paralysis root them, disease slow them, and disease dot the snot outta them. If you can do that, you can win. Rangers are a special case, I'd just recommend standing near a wall to avoid arrows. Melee paladins for the most part, with a potion-derived DS on yourself and Quiescence at all times. Heal yourself mostly when fighting an SK, and strip his self buffs as much as u can. Disease slow and let the pet do the work. Dogdog is our greatest asset vs. most melees.
I'd recommend keeping the pet spell memmed too so if Fido gets waxed, you can make another one to harrass them.
Kylt
My standard pvp spell line up is:

1) Blinding Luminance - fast, use for crowd control and because being blind sucks.
2) Malos
3) Turgur's Insects - pvp server so we dont generally fight in the arena.
4) Quiessence
5) Tnarg's Mending
6) Scourge of Nife
7) Blood of Saryn
8) Petrifying Earth

Always always go poison. Why? Because you only have to debuff once, and the dots are *way* faster. Malos will debuff both MR and PR, but not DR.

Use radiant cure. Get it.

Use spiritcall to interrupt casters. This is handy for a number of reasons - you can kill clerics if they can't heal themselves. Ditto for paladins. Casters can't dish damage, etc, etc. Spiritcall lasts 1 minute so time it well.

Max damage shield is handy also - DS potion and 2nd chardok ring alone is a big ds.
