Thurdar
I'd head to City of Mist. It's not the easiest place to solo, I don't think you're guaranteed single pulls at that level but it's a nice quiet place to work on your skills like handling an add while solo, pet control, etc. Nice thing is that the mobs there don't run and you'll have to hit the zone anyway if you ever want your epic. Might as get your pages while all the mobs are still blue to ya. Dunno if you can handle the courier spawn spots solo at that lvl but definitely doable if you can find a partner.
