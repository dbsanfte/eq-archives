1) do their slows and haste spells stack?
2) does the bard act as tank?

A bard can slow and a shaman can slow; they don't really block each other, although once the shaman slow is in place, there's no point in slowing with the bard unless it's for other reasons (agro or snare or aoe mischief) since the highest slow just takes effect (75%).

A bard haste and shaman haste stack as well, but only the highest one will take effect. A bard also has overhaste, which stacks with both.

The bard is a much better tank than the shaman, especially with reflexes and defense, although in many cases the shaman can tank as well. I've seen my brother tank better than a paladin and much better than a ranger with good gear.

You'll want the bard to tank so that your hp and mana are protected.
