Well I've been reading through the message boards lately, and seeing all the talk about how we need this & we need that to fix the Shaman class. So I started thinking about other things that Sony could do to help the game progress somewhat and in turn be able to make the appropriate changes needed to many classes.

From what I see Sony is having a difficult time balancing new content encounters with Old World encounters. Every time they add in a new expansion they are making the old world encounters more trivial. Well what if instead of making stuff harder, why not spend some time revamping some of the older content. To make thing more inline with what player’s can do with today’s items/gear.

I personally love the older content. I still have not bought GoD and probably won't. I’ve spend most of my time lately exploring my old hunting grounds (everything pre-Luclin). I have a blast walking thought these old hunting grounds & seeing the mobs that use to scare the **** out of me. I mean it is mostly trivial now, but it would be great if we could some how find a way to make these zones useable again. My last trip to LGuK had like 3 people in there (2 65's & one anon).

I would really like to see them revamp most of the old world content to upgrade the drops to be something close to or equal to the loot found in the current day zones. This also means that I would like to see the mobs matched with today’s standards as well. When I start my alts I try my best to use these older would zones, but have a difficult time finding groups. Seems everyone is stuck in Luclin. Maybe it’s just me (Left the game for the entire release of Luclin), but I just don't know what the appeal is.

I know that this probably won't happen has there would have to be a lot of time and effort put into revamping the older items & zones to keep them inline with today’s standards, and that means less money in it for Sony (so it’s really not going to happen).. But I think it would really nice for the older players to be able to pick up some of their favorite old items and have them actually be useable or considered as a useable item, instead of vendor trash.

Well back to my dreaming, and continuing to explore every aspect of the old world..
