I would have to suggest QB2 and Pet Discipline. Anybody who tells you that pet discipline is not useful has not considered the ability in very much detail. When a mob enrages, you can instantly make your pet stop attacking. This is great when soloing or when grouping with tanks that do not turn the mob. In addition, when combined with /pet guard me you can actually control the positioning of the mob during the fight (to avoid adds). As if that were not enough, you can even use pet discipline to prevent your pet from running around when it gets hit by an AE. There is really no end to the usefulness of this AA ability. At 5 points, its a bargain.

I would avoid QB3 though -- you can get QB2 + a spell haste item to hit the maximum. I'll get QB3 someday, but for the moment Haste Of Mithaniel is more than good enough.
