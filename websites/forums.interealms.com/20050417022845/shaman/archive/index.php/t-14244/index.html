There are a variety of mob types in the gnoll caves. I haven't been in there in a while, but if I remember correctly the following classes are present:

Necros
Clerics
Warriors
Enchanters
Shamans

I may be off on some of these, but I am 100% sure of the necros, warriors, and clerics. My info isn't solid on the others because during my few visits here with my paladin, I duoed with a mage who armed his pet with Gnoll Hide Lariats. Between my stuns and the pet weapon procs, the casters didn't get many chances to cast any spells and my resists were pretty high, so I can't say for sure on the mobs outside of the three I mentioned.

Also, there are several wandering mobs in the cave system and they can make life miserable for you quickly. Make sure you have your crowd control spells up and ready.

What I have seen many soloers do here is pull from the caves and kill outside below the entrance of the caves.

Kozul
i spent a decent amount of time in JP recently (about 3 nights...maybe 8 hours total...solo). Invis when you get to caves, they dont see thru invis, keep making rights till you fall through trap. Theres one right there, but if you back up all the way against the wall you can summon pet and buff and stuff and he wont agro you. (by the way, make sure you root when low on health, they run pretty fast and they will go through wall and you wont be able to loot...pet can still kill him though)Most of the gnolls that spawn are the warriors, so no worries about casters usually, there are necros, shaman, clerics, and one more casting class that do spawn once in a while (roughly I'd say one in 8 or so). I've only had fear cast once, it was on dog, so in my opinion they dont cast it much. I send dog in, slow, root till dog gets agro, cast ebolt, let him chew for a while and canni some mana back, then go in a finish off (of course make sure you position pet behind so no parries,etc). Kill the first one...go further...at the opening pull the one back and take care of him. Then theres one on the hill just ahead of the last one, when your done with him, theres one on top of the hill. Next is one more on the right side of the wall above the hill. By the time you do these (all easily single pulls), the first one should have repopped, I'd say go back and do it over again, or those 3 near the hill might aggro if ya hang out there. If you duo with a pet class, like mage, you could clear the place fast, its probably better xp, only did that once, and only for a short time so not sure.
