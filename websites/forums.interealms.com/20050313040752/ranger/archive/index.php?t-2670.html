Maybe it's time to, as a class, start pestering VI not so much about balancing our class against other classes, but our class as a whole, within itself. As a Half Elven ranger of Karana, I'm getting pretty disparaged here. All over the place I'm seeing things that favor Wood elves, and, or, followers of Tunare specifically.

Cultural trade skills: Half elves get NONE, and now, Wood elves get the best fletching, while non-Wood-elf rangers get jack squat. Half elves as a race have been left out of all the nifty cultural things. Yes, I know, because we have no culture, but I don't feel that exscuse is good enough anymore.

Deity restricted items: Helm of the Tracker, need anyone say more? Followers of Tunare are blessed with items that followers of Karana can only dream of, and have no equal that is as easy to obtain.

With such things now known, what reason is there to be a Half elf, or ranger of Karana, let alone a Half elven Ranger of Karana. It has yet to be seen if Humans have cultural fletching, and I doubt Halflings do, since they have never had fletching vendors, although that may have changed quite recently.

What does everyone else think? Is it time Verant did something to address the issue of Race/Deity balance?
