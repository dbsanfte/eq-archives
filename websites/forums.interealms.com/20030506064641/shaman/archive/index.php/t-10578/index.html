Just a little up date of faction for this. You CAN complete this quest for the earring with "Apprehensively" faction. I did this last night and got my earring.

You can also go see Trade Commissioner Henry in SH, run a few reports around to vendors for him and raise your faction to all the merchants in SH. The Commissioner and Rebecca Cabbageleaf are on the same faction table (IT WOULD APPEAR), so if you want to know your faction just con the Commissioner.

Making the earring, Put all nine items in the Forge (there are too in the bazaar) and click combine. It is fail safe, I have no smithing skills. You get the earring (DRU Only) and a spell, take the earring to Rebecca Cabbageleaf in FG, give to her, get your new kick butt earring.
