I cannot suggest the Tae Ew stuff enough. They sell in the bazaar for outrageous prices. So the solution? get it yourself. There are probably about 100 or so 250skill smiths on your server, and you're looking for one of the 5 of them that isn't greedy and does combines for free. Iw as lucky enough to form a friendship with a 65 ranger in my local Uberguild and he has done 2 tae ew combines for me so far and to quote him "ask me if you ever need another smithing job done free of charge."

At level 65, with the 61 pet and all, soloing the Tae ew lizards will be a walk in the park. there is a guide on this page. Read it. From looking at your equip it seems that you have the breastplate category covered. I would craft a Tae Ew Shield (stats damn near as good as the BP) and possibly the greaves or armplates. Really I farmed all of the ingredients for BOTH my BP and Shield in under 5 hours of work. However it did take me more like 10 or 15 hours to find a smith willing to work with me.

So once you get yourself some tae ew stuff, It really comes down to a choice. you can either farm money and buy yourself good equip, or join a raiding guild and fight for uber equip. its up to you and very dependant on how much time you're willing to invest in the game.
