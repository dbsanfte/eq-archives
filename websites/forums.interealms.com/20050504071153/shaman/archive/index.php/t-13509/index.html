Jinheim
Anyone ever looked at DAOC's customer support system? It's awesome. They have what are called "team leads." There is one of each of the 39 classes, and 1 for tradeskillers. These are all real live human beings who actually PLAY THE CLASS (holy ****!!!!!) that they are assigned to. Every time there is a major patch, they write up a team lead report that address the major and minor concerns of their classes. Most of the TL's are reasonable people, so they have reasonable requests and reasonable solutions. Each class has its own forum, where people can post questions and suggestions directed at the TL. And, get this, the TEAM LEAD ACTUALLY RESPONDS DIRECTLY TO THE POSTER. I mean, holy mother of god, is that cool or what? When I first saw it, I couldn't believe it. I had been trained by EQ to believe that all MMORPG's have awful, unresponsive customer service, and I couldn't believe my eyes when I actually saw a functioning, interactive, informative system.

On top of that, they have something called the friday grab bag (www.camelotherald.com) where they post answers to the most commonly asked questions they've received that week.

Mythic is just trying so goddamn hard to listen to their customers and make the game better - something EQ gave up on a long time ago.

I just wish mythic's CS department would come to EQ, and we might have a damn good game on our hands.
