Reave
Many people have pointed out that there is no bug, but rather the display will only list a certain number of spells per search. One additional note I can add is that the stuff that displays on larger searches is the stuff that has been put up for sale most recently. Test this out for yourself by selling a bunch of armor yourself, then just choosing 'armor' as your only search criteria - your stuff will make up a large part of the list, and bump someone's stuff that has been up for sale for a while.

I constantly use this 'feature' to scan the armor, focus effect, all effects, haste, etc lists to see what bargains have just gone up for sale, to beat others to em. You most definitely have to search by specific item name if you want something from a category that has more than the max displayed number for sale, like a shaman spell. Putting in minimum price #'s is also a good method for shaman spells, but you do risk missing a true bargain when someone who has no clue puts up a rare spell for less than a few hundred pp. It does happen occasionally.
