Server: E'Ci

1 - friendliness to new shaman - If they know what they're doing they'll do fine. Paludral Caves is the new Mid-teens to mid-20s playground. Which is nice imo, good dungeon experience.

2 - mid level game - The LOIO/Oasis-Otherthere/S.Karana-Dreadlands loop is still very popular. However; SolB, LGuk, Jagged Pines, Scarlet Desert, Twilight Sea are still pretty reliable for pick up groups. The LOIO/FM/OT/KC loop is definately populated with idiots. If you're going to do KC, get a group, get inside and turn off ooc/shout.

3 - high level game - 50+ Xp; Seb and Velks are very high level weighted. Almost impossible to get a group under lvl 55 if you're not a cleric. Fungi shrooms are perma camped after 5pm est. However, you can usually find a fighting spot in HS, Chardok, Hole, DN, SG and other more out of the way places. People on E'Ci seem to hate to walk anywhere.

As for the Raiding game; E'Ci has a strong 'public' raiding force. Fear, Hate, and PoG have a monthly raid schedule (however, it's common for Uber guilds to jump in to get to the Plane Bosses and Epic mobs). There are public raids on Chardok Royals, Plane of Air, CoM, Hole, Kael Arena and even HoT. There is also a public end game raiding group which takes out Kunark dragons, Vindi, Dain, Tormax, VP and Sleepers.

There are probably 4 large 'Uber guilds' in the EST/PST range. There are 2 Asian 'Uber Guilds' on JST. Only one guild that I know of does Vex Thal, though one of the Asian guilds might be as well.

4 - fun factor - I love E'Ci. It has it's share of whiners and idiots. But most of them seem to shake out of it by the time they make it to the high level game.

5 - economy - Bazaar has killed low/mid item prices on E'Ci. However, high end items have increased in price. Hiero cloak goes for 25k, Lodi Shell for 35k, Fungi Tunic 70k+. Potions have dropped in price.

6 - are there lots of high level shaman who are nice to us who are still learning? Hard to tell, having not been a new shaman in a long time. However, there are plenty of nice shaman on E'Ci. We'll give you this address. ;)
