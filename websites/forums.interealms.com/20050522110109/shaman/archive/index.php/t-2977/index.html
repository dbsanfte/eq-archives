Grotte
Troll or ogre.

Troll get regen and a nice, nice nice snare necklace if you worship Innoruuk. I have found that necklace to be one of the most useful items any of my "non-snaring" characters have. troll stats are OK and the old models look really good.

Ogre get better wis and are non-stunnable from the front. The get good melee stats. Old model ogres look like they swinging telephone polls with a 2hb staff, which I think is neat. They get access to cultural smithing of really really nice items if you have the gumption and the plat to work the skill up to 250.

Both can wear JB armor. I don't have the bracer yet, but I've found the JB items I do have to be greatly useful for buffing group or pet and reducing my downtime.
