The interesting thing is about the faction is that you do not lose it when you spend your points; i.e, if I earn 500 pts at Miraguls, I have locked in forever a spending authority of at least 500 at Miraguls, even after those points have been spent. What he's refering to is how with Dex and Using an item with the same proc rate (same purchast enhancement on both a fast and slow weapon) you will get the same number of procs per minute.

Quick example....
SK 1 has a windblade (50/44) and casts his life tap proc on himself.
3 minute fight the proc went off 6 times.

SK 1 Upgrades to a Hammer of crushing waves (19/20) and casts his life tap proc on himself.
3 minute fight the proc went off 6 times.

*shrug* keep the Str/Attack/Dex/weapon skill level at the same for both fights and these procs go off the same number of times per fight.
