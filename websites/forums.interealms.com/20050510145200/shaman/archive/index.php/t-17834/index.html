Flufy
I've only tried Mistmoore and Rujark raids so far. Mistmoore was a bloodbath, see-invis 900-hitters stacked like flapjacks at the zone in. We found Rujark with its generous mob spacing to be much more forgiving.

Trash mobs will hit for 900+, triples(?), and will mitigate slow a little. Worst thing about them is that they run, fast, and they don't seem to wait until they're low-hp. If they get away, they bring back friends. Lots of friends. That's the worst thing about em, best thing is their resists suck.. They are a lot worse than you would expect for how hard they hit. Still, you'll want to cast malo first anyway, because you don't want to get any more attention from them than you have to. Being two-rounded in full raid buffs is embarrassing.

My guild is pre-VT, pre-Emp, mostly ssra and pop bosses and we did pretty good in Rujark till we came to the named. Named was guarded by a few tough guys, don't recall the name atm. They were hitting 1100+, either not slowable or mitigating it a lot, hard to single pull, and you gotta kill em in like <60 seconds or they summon their friends and the named. It gets ugly fast then.

I'm confident my guild can complete a Rujark raid as-is with maybe some slight group changes, but if you have elemental-equipped people I would strongly suggest you bring 36 of your biggest dogs.
