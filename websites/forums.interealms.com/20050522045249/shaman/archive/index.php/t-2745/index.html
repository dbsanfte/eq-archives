I believe it speeds up the casting of all of your DoT spells up to level 44, so it a GREAT item to have at level 13. I wore one of those until my 40's even without the focus effect :).

You can see which spells it affects by rightclicking on your spell gems to see which ones have a reduced casting time. It will say Casting time (5.0) Your casting time (4.1) or some such. The I, II, and III on the effects only signify the level cap for affected spells, not the magnitude of the effect.
