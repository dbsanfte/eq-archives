Urdegish
Im open to suggestions and questions regarding this.

1. Get PotC/Aego/Virtue and KEI

2. Get to WL, run around like a headless chicken until you find PoG shroom (cant remember loc sorry!)

3. Bind outside shroom

4. Get HP buffs on, Resist Magic and the usual selection. Load puppy into spell list, and prepare buffs for it. Also have gate loaded.

5. Invis and run in

6.a. If the Whorl doesnt see invis, run past it avoiding all horses towards south wall. You can run up the hill to the top, onto a plateau and then stand still, for about a minute, to make sure you havent aggroed anything. Gate if you have.

b. If whorl sees invis, run to the east (not too far, but away from zone in) and gate. Immediately invis again (heal if you need to) and run in. Whorl should be far enough away for you to make it safely to south wall.

7. Summon pet, regen/haste/talisman it, and guard it on plateau.

8. Spell list should be similar to:

1) Canna
2) Root
3) Gate ( NEVER EVER drop this... there is no zone out, so no hope without it)
4) Poison dot
5) Sense Animals/Malosini
6) Swap out slot for buffs/nuke
7) Slow
8) CH or Chloro

9. Use sense animals. This spell is the greatest gift here. All mobs (that are fightable) are animals except Horses (odd!) and treants.

10. Most likely you will have a gale wolf first of all. Find it, and bring with slow, dodging adds. If you get one far away from camp, root it and continue to camp.

11. Malo the mobs. Resists on mobs with sow when fleeing can end fatefully.

12. You can dot and fight ITF (in teh face) or root/dot and let pet melee them down. Because they hit so small, pet can handle with only one CH. Try to keep mana high for efficient fighting

13. Continue. Horses are sometimes paladins, so watch out for LoH. All mobs summon. Treants gate, and are very hard to interupt. Work your way along left wall towards waterfall. By the time you reach waterfall you have probably spent up to 6 hours here, so log off and do something in RL!!! Hehe. Once you get to the waterfall, go back to plateau, and head towards lake in the east. I dont recommend fighting anything at lake unless you are 60+... mobs are annoyingly resistant.

14. I think thats about it. Gem drops are almost 100%. You get the odd Crystallised Sulphur but dont cry!

I did this with epic (a must, sadly) w/o horse and w/o JBB. With horse and JBB this would be sooo much easier and faster.

Notes on mob abilities:

Spirit Flux wolves: Sometimes unrootable.
Bears: All druids or shamans. Watch for nukes and dots.
Gale Wolfs: Rangers. Oh run in fear of the rangers!
Horses: (White) Paladins (Black) Not sure
Treants: Druids. Bastards. Avoid.
Another note on treants: One paths to the back of plateau. Unless you are FM and ready to fight, i wouldnt hang around.
Spirit Stalker: Namedish bear, 200+ hitter summons and casts... probably like a Yeldema fight but with some healing on the mobs side, so shouldnt be too hard methinks for 60+... at 58 i wouldnt try because my mana pool would just be too small.


There we go! Hope this helps, and like i said im open to suggestions and questions. Good hunting!
