PoG would be cool if armour dropped more frequently. It's better than Kael in the sense that the latter requires gems to obtain the end result; PoG also looks much better. But the drops are too rare if you're fighting non-nameds. Plus like rangers, druids and pallies are shafted.

There's another thing I really don't like about Growth, and it's the layout. As you zone in, you generally have to clear surrounding mobs on west wall. Not only is their drop rate ****ty, but you won't find bp's or legs on them. Basically, those pieces drop on the other side of the river off serenes, sylvans, thiflings, etc. Unless you wanna risk running your crew invis there to skip the foreplay, you'll have to clear non-bp/legs-dropping mobs for quite a while. Those PoG's are annoying too and does slow down the clearing process.

There are some pros to PoG though. For Tunare worshippers, there are some really nice quests like helm of the tracker. It's a nice place to farm gems, not only for resist gear, but also to cover guild expenses such as peridot/coffin costs.

Finally, some nameds you're sure will drop a piece of armor, and there is one in particular worth mentionning that roams close to zone in: Galiel Swiftfoot. He always drops a random pair of leggings, and all ya gotta do to find out if he's up is have a non-kos druid check. Quick and easy way to make one guild member very happy (unless it's bst leggings which would prolly suck :D).

Anyways, hunting in PoG will only affect your faction with tunare guilds in Faydwer. There's a couple treehugging npc roamers close to gfay bank, but they're like level 20 max and the guards don't assist. I think you can fix your faction with them too by doing some sort of muffin quest, but I could be wrong, I'd never be caught dead doing a muffin quest - sounds too much like Little Red Riding Hood.
