I would not like this setup at all. HP is great advice for the those aproching 60 and beond but I would trade out a lot of your gear for AC at this time.

You should do a little thinking instead of just asking for advice. All the big post sixty shaman say go HP and that is great advice for post 60 shaman. It does very little for a young shaman growing up.

Why is HP nice in the end game?

a) High attack ratings on PoP mobs reduce the effectiveness of AC
b) Can V
c) Very hard hitting mobs require a greater safty margin in the form of a large HP pool.
d) compleat heal spells are more efficient

Before you reach the 50s and start grouping in PoP then having a large HP pool is no more usefull then having a large mana pool. Both provide an aditional level of safty but do nothing with how efficient you shaman is.

This is not rocket sciense.

The only things that effect how efficient you are at gaingin exp,

a) How fast you regain Mana
b) How fast you regain HP
c) How fast you use Mana
d) How fast you use HP
e) Damage output

Efficency must be balanced with safty wich is basicly the size of your Mana and HP pool.

You can improve how efficeint your shaman is by improving any of the 5 factors and having a big chunk of HP at level 15 does nothing to make you kill faster.

a) How fast you gain mana
FT items
Mana Regen buffs
AAXP

b) How fast you regain HP
HP regen items
HP regen buffs
Canibalization

c) How fast you use mana
Choice of spell / buffs
Focus Items
AAXP

d) How fast you use HP
Combat damage
Canibalization
Spell Damage

e) damage output
Spells
Click Items like JBB and Epic
Mele weapon
Hast Item

When you have a look at a an item of equipment you should consider your situation and evaluate it benifits in comparision to other items you can pick.

The best items you can get are those that dirctly effect your efficency like the Iksar Regen BP and a hast item and a good weapon. Some Focus items can also make a significant differance. Before level 50 or so, AC is great to have because it improves how well you mitigate damage.

How much mana or wisdome or hp an item has is almost meaningless untill you get to the point where you are fighting mobs that make it meaningfull and trivialize other more usefull stats like AC.
