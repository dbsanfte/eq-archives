Kasch
Was messing with the HO stuff last couple nights.

I found that soloing you get 2 different HO results from the exact same trigger and spells used that alternate. This is up to lev 10. I dunno what happens past lev 10 yet.

First I'd hit the trigger for a HO to start. Then all I can do is cast Smite to advance it and Smite again to finish it. Id get either an extra divine judgement nuke ranging 20-40 additional damage OR this Piety buff that adds +10 to inspirations making both nukes and heals and anything in the inspiration line more powerful for 3 mins.

Next is a combo with a lev 9 fighter. They would start the HO and then I would heal (there are 2 choices, heal and smite but smite doesnt seem to have a finisher right now in that chain). Im a little fuzzy on how this one ended but gotten it to work a few times and it seems either me or the warrior can both end it the same way and whoever does gets the buff cast on them. The buff reduces the attack rates of any creature to hit you for 3 mins. I dunno if that means attack speed or attack skill so that they dont hit you as easy.

We also pulled out a HO that used our weaken/slow spell as a step in the trigger and when you used that there would end up with a crippling type slow on the creature that in theory is more powerful than the lev 10 slow and I even did this move at lev 8 using weakness as the middle step before we even got a slow spell.

Hope this helps, we need more testing and feedback. The whole thing is alittle confusing, but at lev 10 at least you can get into some fights that last long enough to where you can test the chains. It was just too hard to test them lev 5-8 when the creatures die too fast.
