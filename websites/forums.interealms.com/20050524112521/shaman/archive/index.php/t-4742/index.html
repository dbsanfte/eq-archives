Torelorm,

The dots will continue to stack. They said that the SAME dot would not stack with itself and that they'd take measures to prevent exploits that would make this happen.

Chaining Nife has always been an aggro issue. If you stack a bunch of dots on a mob, it'll come after you. Each person attacking a mob has a "hate value." Example:

Nife Hate: 300
Epic Hate: 100
Pox Hate: 150
Plague Hate: 100

During a long fight versus a high-HP mob... at 6 minutes into the fight we've got the following hate list values:

Main Tank is just barely holding onto the hate lead with 1450.

Shaman1 casts: epic, nife, nife, epic, nife, nife. Total hate = 1400.
Shaman2 casts: pox, plague, epic. Total hate: 350.
Shaman3 casts: nife, nife. Total hate = 600.

The first shaman has the most aggro and chances are he's gonna get smooshed. The third shaman has generated less than half the aggro of the first shaman. He's much lower on the hate list. The MT's next taunt fails, dropping him below the hate generated by Shaman1... Shaman1 gets dropped.

These are all just made up values... but illustrate the idea.
