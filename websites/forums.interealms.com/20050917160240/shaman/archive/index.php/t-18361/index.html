Spritabe
At work, so I'll just share the essentials:
If you have tactics flag, soloing the Diakus is more fun, in my humble opinion.
Now that's out of the way, there's really only 5 things you need to know:

- Root-rotting is the way to go, unless you're Uber I guess.

- *A Valorian Sentry* is immune to changes in its movement speed. So either prepare for some real excitement. If you see one of these, just don't bother with him, is my advice.

- Nothing I've XPed off here mitigates slow, but that's kinda off-set by the fact they usually come in pairs. It does make it less of a hassle to re-root them.

- Most guards come in pairs. If you get more than 2, you are probably in the wrong place. This makes soloing and splitting fun - I recommend using malos on both targets to decrease the chance on root breaking. VP is your friend here, as in so many other places.

- Invis is your friends. Except for the odd suit of armour and the house in the NE corner, nothing sees invis. Con as you go.

Hope this helps =)

Einnod
mobs around the entrance mitigate slow. A good camp spot for you, if you're just starting out in hoh would be:
Zone in. Go straight to the 4-way split. Turn right. Follow the path to the end, where it T's. Take another right. Follow that path, and it'll curve left(past a spot with 2 buildings, 1 on each side of you with wrulans in front) Keep going and you'll see a building with 3 wrulans guarding the entrance. Go inside that building, downstairs. Don't bother with the first room, as they are all unrootable. Go into the next room. These are all rootable and slowable, but they come in pairs(all mobs here come in pairs.) solo away, root/dot is your friend.
