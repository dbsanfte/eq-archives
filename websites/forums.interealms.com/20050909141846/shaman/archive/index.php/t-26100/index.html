shamuofxev
The problem with a satelite connection is distance. The satelite is in a 36,000 km orbit. Which puts it about 1/10 of a light second away. Your signal has to go that distance 2 - 4 times (depending on if you're using phone uplink/satelite downlink or satelite up/down), adding 200 - 400 ms to your ping time for pure distance travelled.
