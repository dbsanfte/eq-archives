Making these zones excellent xp zones was a huge mistake. Noone cares if one class is almost required for a progression trial (sewer 2 rogues, etc), but when the trial turns into a xp zone rivalling anything but Fire and KT, it becomes wrong. However, the overpowered state of charm in those two places shouldn't really make it underpowered in the rest of the game.

2) Charm soloing hasn't been very unbalanced in a long time. With the nerfs that came around during PoP, even the best solo chanters I know only average an AA every 40-45 minutes solo. Even though I consider myself a very skilled charmer, the fastest I have ever got was 30 minutes. Note that I am talking about the current state of charm, not the exploit that was tactics pit war, or the wildly unbalanced pre-nerf PoP charming.

3) The main gripe most chanters have with this change is that the risk, not only to the chanter but to the group, is so huge compared to the dps put out by the pet, and that the change is sweeping. In outdoor Dreadlands-like zones, even the nerfed charm pets will be quite viable, unless they summon. In any tighter spot though, charm will be pretty much completely impossible, because to make the charm pet deal enough dps we have to buff it enough that it is impossible to control in close quarters. Tell me the last time an unhasted charm pet (again, outside of vxed or tipt) was unreasonably powerful? An unhasted pet will be possible for the chanter to control pretty much solo, like it is in LDoN, instead of having to build a pet control unit instead of a group.

4) I hate repeating myself, but don't judge charm by it's nature in vxed and tipt. Those are only two instanced zones out of a zillion in the game, and we all agree they were a mistake (in their current form).
