Chutoi
You can do Rak just fine with a traditional heal circle, but you have to calculate his damage and set the c-heal chain accordingly. Rather than have several people engaging him directly, I'd recommend a single tank, heavy HP buffs and FR, and a heal circle on him. Everyone else gets to use range weapons and spells. It's messy, but it does work if you can keep your tank alive. The AE DoT that Rak has sucks, but Rak doesn't hit very hard. Were it not for the AE, he'd be soloable.

*edited* Sorry, I should probably speculate a bit more and see if it helps. The guy above me said 3 manaburn wizzies--so that's probably about 25k hp tops. I'd have a minimum of 5 clerics if you want to be REALLY safe, though four would work. Set up a c-heal circle at a delay of 20, and a second circle at a delay of 30. Start out at 20, and if your tank isn't taking that much damage, drop to a delay of 30. Don't use defensive on the tank--Rak's melee is low, it's just his AE that sucks and defensive won't stop the AE. Additionally, if the tank has defensive on, he won't taunt as well since his damage is lower. Everyone else is on blasts. Debuff after 10 seconds or so to lower resists on Rak. Rangers with bows do good damage. Rogues can also use the bows. A second thought is to use a pally as the MT. Since the tank shouldn't be using defensive anyway, and (I think) Rak is flagged as undead (please confirm), a pally with some AA skills would probably excel at holding agro. This will allow all the casters to blast more easily. Agro control is going to be vital. I'd STRONGLY recommend that you have the clerics well away from the casters, so that if the casters screw up and get agro, the clerics don't also eat the AE while the tank is getting agro back. MAKE SURE THAT ANYONE THAT GETS AGRO KNOWS TO STAND STILL UNTIL THE TANK REGAINS AGRO--EVEN IF THEY WILL DIE BECAUSE OF IT. If they run around like an idiot, you will almost certainly lose. The only reason I suggested 5 clerics, btw, is because I don't know what kind of DPS your guild can put out. You may need a few minutes to do that much damage, and, depending on the speed of the heal circle, you may not have enough cleric mana to maintain the circle long enough if you are short on clerics. With all level 60s, 2 non-manaburn wizzies, 2 druids (with moonfire), a pally MT, 1 shaman, and 5 clerics, I'd expect something under a 2 minute fight for sure, unless the agro get screwed. Obviously, you know your DPS better than I would, so just plan accordingly.
