the frogs have around 8k or so hp.. takes me 2 bane's, 1 pox, 2 epic's and alpha dog with 2 fists of ixlabat to kill one.. and at the end of killing im at 90m and full hp.. all you do is pull with malosini, because they have high resists, then i bring it back near zone and sick pet on it, slow, bane, pox, root, epic, cann cann and torpor, to regain mana, ect.. pet usually needs a torpor every 2 fights or 3.. frogs go down very fast, pulling and killing constantly i could only barely keep 3 of the 4 spawns clear.

exp at 60 is 1% normal exp per kill.. every once in a while i get 2% for 1 kill though so i guess its something like 1.2% xp.

try it out guys, great spot.
