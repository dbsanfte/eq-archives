Weyalan
Ivory is a relatively common drop from the Ice Giants that guard the Entrance to Permafrost Keep, in Everfrost.

Obviously, at level 29, you aren't going to be killing these beasties any time soon...but do not despair. The Ice Giants are a favorite camp for groups of level 40+ and soloers of level 45+. Generally, these players will end up selling their Ivory to vendors for around about 5 plat, so if you ask nicely to buy their Ivory for 15plat (say), they will almost certainly oblige.

It is also well worth checking with Starn Bearjumper (the guy who sells the DoT spells at the ruined temple on the Tundra), as he is the closest vendor to the Ice Giants.

Hell, I give the ivory away to any young shaman who is well enough informed to know that (a) it drops at the IGS and (b) it is necessary to make the Tribunal earrings.

100 to 200 for a pair of earrings would be about right, you may ask jewelers if they have imbued ivory in stock.
If in stock, and they dont have a price, offer 500p for a set of the 3 items. (i sell mine for 400, but if they have it in stock you dont want to irritate em)

If they dont, get 5 imbued ivory on your body and ask for a GM jeweler. Ask if they will make 2 gold plus ivory combos and one plat plus ivory combo. Thier cost of materials will be 130p, if they dont fail. Offer thier cost of materials (which will be 105 more for each platinum they fail) and 100 to 200p for the combine. This should be fair if they have the metal on them and are not busy. If they are busy, expect them to be bothered by the offer.
