MekuDeku
Do not pay more than 50p for this spell. I think I have used it twice from 50 - 60. It does stack with haste spells but the duration and effects on Spirit Quickening aren't worth the mana cost. You might be able to find SQ for sale on a vendor in Thurgadin for 30p. That is how I got mine.

If they changed it to add hps and higher haste I would probably use it, but as of now it isn't worth the scroll parchment it is printed on.
Kazim
Your haste spell and spirit quickening will both stick (i.e., you'll see both buff icons), but the haste part does not stack with your haste spell. Just do a search. A number of people have done log parses to prove this.

If you're buffing your pet with your "real" buffs, spirit quickening is nearly 100% useless.
