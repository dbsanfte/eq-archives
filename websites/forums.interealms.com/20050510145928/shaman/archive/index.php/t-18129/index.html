DarkPhase
Once entering factory go straight forward and you get to a cross-section. Prototypes are in 3 separate rooms linked to this cross-section. I think all Prototypes can banish their main target into one of the 2 other rooms. And all Prototypes have AE spells. 800dmg dd AE or something.

Prototypes can drop a nice shoulder item with beneficial haste. I am still using that shoulder item, and our guild has killed Quarm.
Kamidari
They're really easy to get to... In the factory, you'll first enter a big room with a gnome. Straight ahead (south) is a hall with Clockwork Golems. you need to clear into there, and then the golems are in 3 rooms there - one to the east, west, and south.

I haven't really noticed much difference in difficulty between the three. They all banish, so be prepared to go through a few thanks. ;)

Can't comment on difficulty vs Xanamech, as I've never fought him.
