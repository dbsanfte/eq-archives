Jaradis
Go ahead and get MGB. When doing buffs, do the group stamina first, then just as you hit enough mana to cast Fo7 pop MGB and cast Fo7. You will get done much quicker that way than doing MGB first. Doing MGB Fo7 first is bad because it will wipe out all of your mana.

From your Magelo I see you got about 4k mana, so doing MGB Fo7 first will take 3800 mana leaving you with only 200 mana. Now if you first cast 4 casts of Tali of Boar (525 mana each) you'll use ~2100 mana, leaving you 1900 mana, which is just enough to cast Fo7, which is all MGB checks for. So cast ToB, pop MGB, cast Fo7, then hit Canni V bringing you up to about 25% mana. Shouldn't take more than 6 mins or so to get everyone full buffed and you back to full mana.

Reuse time is 72 minutes.

Slissia
A hint for anyone who may not have noticed it, to maximize the reuse time on mgb....

The MGB reuse timer starts when you press the button, not when you cast the spell. Whenever I get to raid setup point, or get rezzed after a wipeout, I try to hit the mgb button right away. Depending on circumstances, by the time everyone is actually gathered and/or rezzed for me to cast the buff, the timer has already been ticking for a while, and it gives me a much faster refresh. If you're in a situation where you won't be casting any group buffs for an hour or so, you can pretty much do two mgbs back to back, ie for Focus and Talisman of Epuration/Tribunal.
