Hmmm, I can't quite remember what I had at level 27, but here's a good rule of thumb:

1) Heal - never demem
2) Swap spot - regen, nuke, dot, other buffs/debuffs as needed
3) Swap spot - regen, nuke, dot, other buffs/debuffs as needed
4) Swap spot - regen, nuke, dot, other buffs/debuffs as needed
5) Root - never demem
6) Slow - never demem
7) Swap spot - regen, nuke, dot, other buffs/debuffs as needed
8) Cannabalize - swap out with SoW as needed

For 27, it could look like this:

1) Healing - never demem
2) Swap spot - Regen (with a 10 minute duration, better to just keep it up)
3) Swap spot - envenomed breath (swap with stat buffs: agi/sta)
4) Swap spot - affliction (swap with stat buffs: str/dex)
5) Root - never demem
6) Slow - never demem
7) Swap spot - frost strike (swap protect and with haste later, if you're not grouped with an enchanter)
8) Cannabalize - swap out with SoW as needed
