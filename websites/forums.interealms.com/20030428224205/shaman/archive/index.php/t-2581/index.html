Definitely say Gornit. Gornit is a 2 ebolt kill, or a slow and some bracer blasts. Ice Giants resist or take limited damage from JBB, and are typically just as camped in my experience. Camping Gornit while pulling Seafuries for exp is much better in my opinion.

I've been getting a yellow (or more) a day while killing seafuries AND camping Gornit at 53 AND earning over 1kpp each session. (This is actually a low estimate, as all the seafury gem drops go to my spouse who is an enchanter. You would likely earn ~2kpp a day selling all loot.) YMMV

If Gornit is already camped, politely ask the camper how long they'll be there and ask for a tell when they are about to leave. Check in every hour or so with a friendly comment just to keep your name in their mind. Kill Seafuries (or the Allizewsaur) until then. If the Gornit camper isn't leaving soon, ask them if they will let you buy the toes for a few pp each. They sell for around ~1pp to a vendor.

Once you have the camp, save a few of ALL the variety of toes. I've been camping Gornit for some time, and sold different toes to mages and other questers for ~100pp for two toes several times (their offers, not my greed). I've also GIVEN away toes to those who ask nicely.

My alchemy is at 102. Eventually I'll max it with Grow potions. I have a mule (named Toeholder) who is stuffed full of toes (all bank and inventory bags) right now, so I no longer keep them myself. I sell them to vendors, after asking any other fellow Mystics in the zone if they are seeking out toes.
