Hiya Targar =)

The only links I've ever used as my Shaman have been this board, EQ Caster's, and Allakhazam's. There really isn't a lot of info for Lizard Shamans specifically out there, although Champs of Cabilis might have something aside from the Cudgel quests.

As far as armor progression goes, I started off in a suit of the quested Iks/Shm armor (Scaled Mystic). A tip now: don't bother trying to get the sleeves. I gradually upgraded it until I'd reached the 200 wisdom mark, and since then I've been gradually swapping out armor as I obtained larger wis per-piece for AC/HP gear. Some simple pieces you can get for him are: PMM, Forest Loop, 6/65 ring, Chitinous Belt, Shadel Bandit ring, etc.

If it'd help at all, feel free to check out the link I've got in my sig. As far as your gear goes pre-Velious armor, you should (imho) aim for having mostly Barbed Ringmail and various pieces of Dragon armor (from WW). Great AC/HP, and the bracers especially combine a lot of AC with nice +Wis.
stormrazor
As a newbie Iksar you'll spend a good portion of your levels in the Field of Bone. There you can do the curscale armor quests, very easy quests and it will yield you some free armor. It's not that great of armor but its free. go to this link if you want more info on the quest: Curscale Quest (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/qsearch.html?zone=96)
You can essentially level all the way to 20, I don't recommend it but it is possible. I leveled to 17 in FOB and went into Kurn's tower until 19 or so. I bought a full suit of banded at 19. I started the cudgel quests but got frustrated because they bored me so I gave up. But I will go back to finish them. The last cudgel is quite a nice weapon.
I began the Scaled Mystic quests at 22 (Iksar equivalent to Totemic). The quests are not too difficult as I had nearly a full suit at 24. I asked to loot the no drop items I needed and turned them in. I didn't bother getting the helm or the sleeves, not worth the effort IMO. I got Rygorr instead.
You can wear Scaled Mystic Armor for quite a while.
You can get various gear all near your home of Cabilis. I got lucky and got a Forest Loop to drop in the Warslik Woods Giant fort at 21. I got my other one in Frontier Mountains at 29..FREE ! I also got a Strawspun Belt in FM...FREE !
