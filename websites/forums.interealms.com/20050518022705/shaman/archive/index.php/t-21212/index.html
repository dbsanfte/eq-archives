Ryam
If you think he ninjaed the item, one thing you can do is inspect him every now and then to see if he is wearing it. If you don't want annoy him too much, you can do that in situations where inspecting can occur accidentally (looting corpses in tight areas, next to bankers etc). Or to avoid possible disputes you can just use a low level alt(s). Since many new players like to look at the gear on level 65 chars out of curiosity that sort of thing often gets ignored.

This should lead to one of the three possible results:

1) Guilty: You catch him wearing it. He deserves whatever the guild decides to do with him.

2a) Innocent: Not wearing it, since he did not take it.

2b) Guilty: Not wearing it, because he doesn't want to get caught. Won't get you the item back, but at least he is not benefiting from stealing it.

Maybe this option is a bit paranoid, but might help in figuring out what happened (or just make him keep the item bagged if he did take it).
