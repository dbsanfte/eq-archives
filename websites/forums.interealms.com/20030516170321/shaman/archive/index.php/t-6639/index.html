First off, I'm jealous of your nest egg. :D But no matter, here's my advice.

Ear(Left): Imbued Golden Ivory (Jewelcraft made) (Bring 2 gold bars and 2 imbued ivories to your Jewelcrafter enchanter friend.)
Amulet: Chrysoberyl Talisman until 49 is fine.
Face: Crystal Spider eyes
Helm: Jaundice Bone helm
Ear(Right): Imbued Golden Ivory
Ring(Left): Shadel Bandit ring
Bracer(Left): Barbed Ringmail Bracer
Vambraces: JB Arms (cheaper than CC, good enough until 50)
Gauntlets: JB Gauntlets (" " " " " " )
Bracer(Right): JBB (buy this sooner rather than later)
Ring(Right): 6ac/65hp ring (vel)
Shoulders: Tattered mantle if funds running low OR Obulus Death Shroud
Breastplate: Fungi (50-55kpp, but you won't be taking it off, this should be your priority purchase.)
Cloak: Arctic Wyvern Cloak
Belt: Chitinous Belt
Leggings: Rygorr for now, improve later. Maybe dragon legs if you have enough cash left over (JB legs are too pricey)
Boots: Jaundiced Bone Boots
Primary Wep: Ruined Etched Icewurm Fang
Shield: Sarnak Battleshield
Ranged: Goranga Idol or Gantru Charm.

I'm recommending Jaundiced Bone (except for legs) because you will enjoy the AC until your mid 40's, then enjoy the clicky effects from 45-50. After that, you'll start wanting to improve those spots. The added cost of CC for the limited stat benefits aren't worth it to you at your level in my opinion. JB armor for these slots is cheap, and CC prices are falling not rising, so waiting would be to your benefit financially.

The Fungi Tunic and JBB are 2/3 of the Holy Trinity for shaman. Get them now and you'll be set if you persevere through some of the rough spots in your 30's.

With my lineup, I'd expect you to run out of money before getting everything. The JBB is a rare find, so don't expect to get one right away. ODS mana preservation effect will help you all along the way though, and you can usually find one for sale in the bazaar, and prices on those are coming down slowly. Chokidai or Tattered Mantle would be fine in shoulders if you don't want to lay out the cash for an ODS. Imbued Granite Spaulders are another choice, but rather pricey in my opinion. They are campable for you later easily, so may not want to spend the cash on IGS.

I'd set my priorities like this:
1. Fungi Tunic
2. Runed Etched Icewurm Fang
3. Buy everything else listed except ODS & JBB.
4. See what cash you have left & make some choices from there. Runed Mithril Bracer is a fine interim bracer, and you'll likely be swapping it out for a mage focus (no rent) extra damage bracer much of the time anyway. Tattered mantle is fine for interim shoulders.

My 2cp anyway,
