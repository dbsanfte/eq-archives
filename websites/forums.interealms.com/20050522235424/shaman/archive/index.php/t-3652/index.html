Grippa
Seru faction is easier to raise, that's for sure. Kill thought leeches or spire spirits in Mons Letalis, and you'll raise many (but not all I think) Seru factions.

As far as I know, there are actually 2 opposing factions within Katta itself, and you can possibly raise one by killing the other.

And Shadowhaven is independant, to raise SH faction you just need to do a delivery quest. This is well worth doing, as you can buy almost anything in SH, especially useful things like fish scales (for trips to the Grey).
snapdragn
There are a ton of factions for each city.

Seru:
Seru
Hand Legionarries
Hand of Seru
Heart of Seru
Eye of Seru
Citizens of Seru
Shoulders of Seru


Katta:
Nathyn Illuminious
Katta Castellum Citizens
Validus Custodus
Magus Conlegium
Concilium Universus

And they are really interwoven. Ex:
Killing the Seru Guards in Marus Seru and Nether Lair will give you Positive hits with 2 katta factions and 2 Seru factions but Negative hits with 3 Seru factions.

- Hand Legionnaires
- Hand of Seru
+ Eye of Seru
- Seru
+ Heart of Seru
+ Validus Custodus
+ Magus Conlegium

There are tons of quests and mobs that give out different faction hits...but without a lot of research you're likely to get jumped by another NPC on a different faction in the same city.
