Chutoi
I love HS. I've spent untold hours there, in all the wings.

There are, and have been, rumors of a master key quest with a key that isn't 'no rent', but either no one has figured it out yet or (more likely) it's not working.

Yes, the entrance is cakewalk, no question.

For those of you interested, this is a quick spoiler--and I'm doing this from memory, so please excuse all the mistakes. In each wing, the mobs get a little harder. The dungeon is designed as a crawl (or you can use a rogue to pick the locks as you noted). You kill a sperm up top or in the basement, and it will always drop a candle. One quick note on the wings (edited since I didn't think my post was clear): there are 2 keys in each wing except the east--one is a ground spawn and opens the door to the 'boss's' room, and then the boss will drop the key to the next wing.

The candle is the key to the north wing. One of the rooms in this wing has a ground spawn (broach) to open a door where the 'boss' mob spawns. I think the 'boss' for the north wing is the crypt keeper (easy kill, just a named, not really a 'boss' mob). The keeper has a placeholder, so it may take several clearings to get him to spawn. I believe one of his drops is the calendar. The calendar is the key to the west wing. Generally, any no-rent item will be a key for one of the wings. The keeper spawns on the platform or dias with the sparkles (you'll know it if you see it). In a side room, just to the right of that dias, is a porter back to the entrance.

The calendar should open the west wing. Actually, there are 2 groundspawns in this wing. One is a claw of some sort and the claw is a 4 spot container (thought to be part of the broken master key quest). The claw is NOT the key to the back part of this wing. Keep looking in the rooms before you drop down the stairs. I think the groundspawn key for this wing is a coin. You can get back up the drop-down with levitate, but it's annoying. :) Also, the keyholder (I forgot which spawn) has a placeholder, so it may take a while. The keyholder spawns in the room with the porter back to the entrance. I believe the actual key is a broken dagger or dirk (again, no rent).

The dagger opens the south wing. South is great for exp and pretty good for drops. I have yet to find a way to get through the south wing to the east without a rogue. Unfortunately, the portal out of the zone is also on the other side of the rogue locked door to the east wing.

At the entrance, the east wing stairs are an illusion. Don't stand on them, or you will fall. There are only 2 ways to get across to the east wing--either through the south wing, or by dueling someone with grav flux, getting knocked onto the ledge over the entrance, and levitating down to the east wing platform. The east wing door is not locked.

In each wing, there is a trapped rug that drops to the basement. If you don't know which rug it is, just stay off all of them. If you have a rogue let you into a wing, these drops are a good way to get back to the entrance. I have a druid twink that I park at the entrance if I duo with a melee. We fight in the wing of our choice, then drop to the basement and fight back to the entrance. Then I can log in my porter and get the melee out (if you don't have a druid or wizzie in the group, or the tanks don't have a gate potion or overthere hammer).

CRs suck in HS. They suck badly. You will need either a rogue or a necro (or SK) for a CR in a wing. I would NOT recommend duo'ing with a tank in the south or the east wing. Even with careful pulling, you may get a train of 5-6 (there are some fake walls and things agro easily). Most of the mobs in south and east wings have a HT. (At least 50% and probably 70%). The HTs in the south wing hit as high as 701 I believe. Six of those will take out most classes. Three of those are usually enough to get low HP agro on a self-buffed shaman, at which point you won't channel through 4+ mobs and you are dead.

HS is a GREAT spot for a group though, especially the south and east wings. I'd take a chanter to the east wing. You can get by without a chanter in any of the other wings.

Trains of 7-8 are not uncommon in the east wing. Drusella (who is easy to kill) has a number of mobs in her room who are tougher than she is. Don't try her without a good group. :)

Hope this helps!

Heck. I went back and read the original post and didn't really answer the question. :/

With the exception of the north wing (key is a candle and drops off all sperm), no key to the wing you are in will drop from a mob in that wing. The dungeon was designed to be sequential--the prior wing has the key to the next wing.

If you get a rogue to let you into the south wing, the only way to get out is to drop through the rug trap into the basement and fight out that way. The North, West, and South wings also have drop-offs with a 1-way wall shortly after you enter them.

Again, you can get out by going through the pit to the basement, but the only way to get back into those wings is with a rogue or by camping the key.

Corpse recoveries REALLY suck. Since there is a 1-way drop in each wing, and the no rent key (if you have one) will stay on the corpse, the only way to get your body is by summoning, or by having a rogue try to sneak in and drag your corpse into the basement through the rug pit trap. I don't think you should try duoing south, especially without torpor.
