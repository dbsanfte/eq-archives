House
I too would suggest doing at least some of the Totemic quests (which it sounds as though you are beginning). They offer good AC, all of the MOBs you need to kill will give you experience, and they'll show you some zones that you probably haven't been to. After you get the Boots completed, I would suggest you work next on the Bracers, as they are not difficult to obtain, and it seems in looking at your gear that you would really benefit from a pair of AC 9 bracers. You might also want to drop into the Bazaar every once in a while and see if you can't find some cheap Ry'Gorr pieces.

As for combat tactics, it may be that kiting is now actually efficient (with 100% DoT damage to moving MOBs), so that may be best for reducing downtime. However, it is very important that you keep up your Defense, and that can only be done by engaging the MOB. Try this, just to see if it makes any difference: melee the MOB, and use just enough mana (slow, a DoT or two, whatever seems to work for you) so that you finish the fight somewhere around 2 bubbles of health. You should have more mana than health and can then heal yourself. Cannibalize just isn't very efficient initially.
