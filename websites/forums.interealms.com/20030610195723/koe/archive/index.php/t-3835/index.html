Sometimes, you trip over the words of a person who seems to have a special rapport with you - with how you feel, what you think etc.

Well...I'm beginning to think Steffen Brandt from the Danish band TV2 spends half his life inside my head ;)

First it was this (http://forums.interealms.com/koe/showthread.php?s=&threadid=3808) thread where I posted a translation of one of his songs that struck a chord with me.

And now I've found another one! Again, this is my rough translation and it doesn't really do it justice - but somehow I think a Knarlzlation (tm) would be a little out of place...


It's night and it's storming and hailing and snowing

ever feeling so unreasonably abandoned

There are plenty of chances but no matter what you think
just save yourself all those fancy words
it's your back against the wall and not even the funniest line

In any decent popsong a girl would walk in
sneaking kind words into my deranged mind
float away in a slowmotion moviefantasy
in a puff of paintbox and new technology

But there's static on the line, it's pure white noise
and the dream has sought refuge underneath warmer skies
The sound of her wings leaves no visible trace
the compass is wavering around the icy cold north

And I've had it up to here, I've figured it all out
it's too much, it's too little, it's too cruel, it's too harsh
I yell at the skies and I swear by God
This is not how my life was supposed to turn out

And then it happens that I finally come home
and find you waiting again and again
you're not saying anything but I can see it in your eyes
we need to act fast before we slide away

It's not very much, but sometimes it's enough
just sitting, saying nothing till the sun it comes up
Tomorrow becomes today and I belive that I know
that someone needs to go out and shovel some snow.
