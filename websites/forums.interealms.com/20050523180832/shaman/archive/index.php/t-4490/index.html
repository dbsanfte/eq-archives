Monks? They're just unreal now.

Garrison's or Elnerik's? Okay, bad examples.. those spells really are overpowered. Any spell that can Crit for over 5000 is just insane.

Then, I looked at my spells.. and Gear.

Pox... Yeah... Had that since Kunark.
