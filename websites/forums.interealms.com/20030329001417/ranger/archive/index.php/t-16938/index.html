I've been thinking about POP more recently and... well, now the novelty is worn off and I'm used to it I still think it isnt a patch on velious. It seems to be much more 'arcadey' EQ without any of the sense of immersion or depth of previous expansions... the whole random linear progression through 'levels' you have to beat to get to the next level, end of level bosses, random events that really dont tie into the rest of EQ that well.. its just kind of beginning to break the spell for me.

Add that to the insane XP incentive to crowd everyone into the same few zones and... well... I'm kinda beginning to think it sucks. Its way too XP happy massively reducing my sense of achievement (since pop I have got AM3, Innate camo, GOTF, a few other things, and I really dont grind XP much) to the point I now think 'bleh, another AAPoint, no point getting excited, I'll have ability XXX in a few weeks' and dying is just 'hmmm I lost my buffs, thats annoying.'

Does anybody else miss.... old EQ? EQ that wasnt this linear predictable enforced route of progression, levels, bosses, and the like?

Feels like I've gone from an open ended adventure to a childrens space invaders game. Is this just burnout after 3 years or does everyone see this too?

Sui... pondering.
