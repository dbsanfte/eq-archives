Stomples - Long as you have torpor/Q, this is an easy fight once you learn how to handle him. Kill Sniffles with your pet, while you are WELL across the room. pet kills sniff, and Stomp is out of aggro range, so you get time to prepare. I reccomend equiping pet with 2 broken Blam sticks prior to the fight though since that increases your pet's max hit to 72 and helps (unless you have mage proc weapons, then use those).

Pet will do the majority of your damage if dual wielding, but keep epic and 2 dots on him as long as you maintain the mana you need to heal yourself

Lithiniath - You have to use Disease based slows on this guy, and I hate to use pet to tank for me, but sadly on this guy I do reccomend it. If you have a J-stick you can probably get his MR down low enough to stick on a Turgur, in that case he is cake. MR buffs are important as well.

An alternate method of doing him is one that I use for tough enchanters (like BB himself). First off, you take a HUGE risk because to do this right you have to leave all heal spells but torpor off mem. Debuff your own Magic resist, and put on ALL the regen that you can (Fungi, regen rings/range items, Replenishment, and keep Torpor/Q up at all times). Lay on all the DoTs that you can land on the mob, and pray for it to charm you. Stick to disease DoTs primarily for longevity. While you are charmed you will be regening nice and quick, and the mob will drop Hps from the DoTs. Of course, if your DoTs all wear out at any point, then the NPC loses all aggro on you and regens at normal NPC rates (quick :() When mezzes break, refresh Torpor, slow and DOTs as needed.

Ricoculous - In all my time in PoM I never tried him. Wasn't much point in my opinion.

Puppets - You can position yourself so that they can't hit you, but you MUST MUST MUST stay in melee range. If you get knocked back out of melee range by them hitting you, they summon you on top of them and thus in range of the other puppets. Start off killing Tunare from in front of the curtain, then stand on the Tribunal and work your way across the KOS in backrow. I can normally clear all but 2 puppets before having to go kill Geb again.

Ferjenor(sp.) - Yes, but jester cards are still worthless to my knowledge.

Posie the librarian - Never tried this one either, again saw no real reason. Shouldn't be too hard.

Chessboard - All monkeys assist. Chessboard sucks.

My Right hand - This guy is an SK, so aggro initially with pet, to soak the HT, but heal your pet, or dispell him cause his DS is DANGEROUS for your little dogdog. Also drops the useless jester cards, but easier kill then Ferj.
