Viscerator
Curse counters seem to work like disease/poison counters. The comment that it's all or nothing is not entirely correct. You can chain cast and remove the offending spell once the total has been met.

Aka remove 9 counters, cast again remove another 9 counters, if that is more then the offending spell, it will be removed. So although having to cast more then one time is time consuming, it will cure whatever ails you.
