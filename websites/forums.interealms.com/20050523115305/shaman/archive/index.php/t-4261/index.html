Grippa
The Hole entrance isn't bad, in my experience. I solod there on and off from level 50 ish, if I remember right. There are 6 elementals near the entrance, and usually I could single pull them with malosi (or whatever), then slow, send in the pet, and start poking. I didn't have my epic or JBB at the time either. If you get a double pull and you can't handle it, just swim to the zone.

I'd rather do the Hole than Maiden's Eye at level 51 - ME is dangerous and people are always training bad mobs onto you if you're anywhere near a zone line. However if you can find a safe spot, it's not too bad.

Mind you if you are just grinding to 55 because your guild told you to, maybe bloodgils are the best ...
