Great Story.

Reminds me of a fun local one here as well.

Robert Asp was a Minnesota man who built a Norse Longship and sailed it from Duluth to Norway to show that indeed Leif Erickson's trip to North America some 500+ years before Columbus was very much possible. He was greeted in Norway by throngs of fans, including the King of Norway. His ship the Hjemkomst is on display in Moorhead, MN. I actually saw it under construction in Hawley, MN when I was in high school.
