Hail,

I wouldn't perform ANY non-trivial alchemy combines with less than 255 wisdom.

There is a plethora of wisdom gear available. Much of it is very inexpensive. Some of it will cost you a pretty penny, but will also be full-time upgrades since they come with other stats that are nice. Like the new Glittering Rings of Judgement. 10ac, 20wis, 50hp! :D

Somehow I've reached 255wisdom (w/ KEI) without even trying too now. :p I used to have a full bag of wisdom gear I had banked just for tradeskills. Now I don't need it.

If you do a search for wisdom gear in these forums, I'm sure you will find multiple threads listing lots of cheap wis gear.

If you consider 2kpp a LOT of money, you might want to work on gear before spending any more money on alchemy, which is the biggest money pit we have as a class. (Our spell scroll costs aren't a money pit, as we actually get a nice benefit from them at least, even if they are horribly over-priced.)
