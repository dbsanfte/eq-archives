1) Where exactly is Neh, and how do I know which is her reaver?
Neh's reaver spawns in the 3rd (final) building on the Skybridge.

2) Where should we fight it - at its spawn point, or do we pull it somewhere else?

You don't fight Neh's reaver at its spawn point. The building is too small and has a hole in the floor. There is also a gap in the 'bridge' just before the building, so your puller (a rogue - the building is locked) will need to have levitation. Pull the reaver to the 2nd building - much more room to fight, and no way for the reaver or Neh to fall down to the ground.

3) How do we get past that hut - do we just steam in and kil everything, or do we try to get someone to pull the mobs somewhere else (like the corner room before the ramp)?

I know 2 relatively low risk ways to get into/past the first sky building.
a)Have your raid in the hallway before the transparent ramp. Puller brings 2 or 3 mobs (or more if desired) from the building down the ramp into the hall. Kill. Repeat until the building is nearly empty. Move up into the building, finish the rest.

b)Bring some people along who have experience in AE killing. A few wizards/mages and an enchanter or two. Charge into the building. Agro everything all at once. Fry the mobs in under a minute.

In either case, do NOT sit around in the building. If you have heavy deaths and need time to rez/rebuff after clearing the building pull the corpses out in the walkway and do the rez session there - far fewer spawns out there and less risk of adds. Just make sure no fools walk over the edge and plumet to their deaths.
