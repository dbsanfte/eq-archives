It is a shame you do not have LoY because Dulak is the perfect zone for you to solo. Once you get that, as you intimate you will soon in your post, you should move along there and play the boats. There are many posts to give you some ideas in this section so check them out.

I just finished level 52 in Burning Wood at the giang fort. There is a 2 spot at the front that is pretty easy to break up and there are 6 more that spawn inside. The forest Giant Ancients have roughly 4500 hit points and are the toughest of the spawns. The Arbors, after slowing, can be solo'd by our dog. Verdants are a little tougher than Arbors. Spawn time is 5 minutes. The loot is usually up to 7 plat and some vendor stuff which sells pretty well. Ancients drop spells too and there are chances for spell drops. Hope this helps.
