Grippa
Nothing wrong with using 1HB - Ideally you want to keep all 3 of your weapon skills at a decent level.

The only way to raise weapon skills is to buff your dex and hit mobs, and I don't think you'll find many piercers much faster than 25.

The only one that springs to mind is the beetle stinger - 8/23. I personally used slower weapons (spine chill spear, jade prod) and I still maxed my piercing skill before level 50.

One thing you could try, if you have the cash, is making Greater Null potions - they give +25 dex, and stack with Rage (last time I tried anyway).
