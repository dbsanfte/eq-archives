That really is the root of the problem, there's just too many classes for not enough roles. Most of the time class balancing involves robbing Peter to pay Paul. There's really no way around it. I talked to a few people about this at the summit briefly concerning healing. Honestly I think that having shaman/druid/cleric all being able to heal more or less the same is good for game balance, as long as the rest of the classes abilities balance out to make them equally desirable. The problem really is how many players actually want to see that happen? How many players would be frustrated and/or quit over a change of that magnitude? Tough questions that don't have a simple answer, but then again, that's my job.

And with as hard as these mobs hit, most groups need them slowed ASAP.

I know as well as anyone how little changes to things sometimes have huge effects in-game. But Ryan has failed to make any needed changes to Shaman for far too long now. Its time to give shaman at least as good a healing spell as druids have. Its time to put in some very high damage, very short term, low aggro DoTs. Its time to give shaman an improved SoW spell. its time to give shaman a THIRTY MINUTE BASE DURATION Avatar. Its time to forget the useless stat boost buffs. Anyone in elemental+ gear should have STR/STA/DEX/AGI maxed out or very, very close to being there. Its time to boost Shaman nukes SIGNIFICANTLY.

Its well past time to step back Rytan, and say, "Yes, shaman have slow. But we have reduced the effectiveness of slow two expansions in a row now, and we have increased mob DPS by incredible amounts. Its time to give shaman the option to do other USEFUL things with their mana when they are done or cannot slow the mobs being killed."

Sadly, Shaman are very, very close to being in the same position today that necros were in a couple of years ago. Its Rytans job to correct that problem. And lets pray he does so before the majority of shaman follow the knights.
