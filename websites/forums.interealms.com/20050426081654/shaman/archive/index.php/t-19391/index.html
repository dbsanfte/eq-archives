larns
I used a single 65 Druid to PL my bard. My method should work just as well for your wizzy.

What I did was ungroup and start buffs. I gave him Skin Like Nature series while I used Pot9 on the druid. Regen'd us both. Hit myself with Brackencoat and gave us both a DS. I'd sit him next to where I will be pulling to. I memmed Bonds of Tunare for snare and quick heals.

I switch over to my druid and start pulling. I usually start my first pull with the noble spawn side. Grab close to 10 kobolds (or less depending on druid gear). I would run by them and proximity aggro. Sometimes I have to sit there for a couple of seconds before they notice me. Once a sufficient amount of kobolds have been gathered, I would run back to the camp spot and position myself against a corner wall (so they cant push me all over).

To gain aggro, I Bonds of Tunare them about 3 times and spot heal myself. When the mobs are down to about 50-75% health, my bard(wizzy with a low level pbae in your case) would sing a single AE dot song and back off immediately. It does about 30 damage to each mob. The less damage the better so bard doesnt gain aggro and die. The bard needs to do at least 1 damage to each mob to gain exp. Once bard damage is done, I would switch back to the druid and heal as nesessary and make sure snare doesnt fade. If any try to run, finish them off with the Bard.

I stopped pulling greater kobolds when they turned light blue and concentrated on solusek kobolds, sonic bats, and lava beetles. Lava duct crawlers have too much hp to be worth it. I stayed in Sol B until level 50.

Since you have a 65 enchanter, you can also try the enchanter + druid combo. The enchanter can use arcane rune and legacy of the bracken. Enchanters get hit more often and gain amazing aggro with ae tash, but the damage shield on them would be lowered since brackencoat is a druid self buff.
