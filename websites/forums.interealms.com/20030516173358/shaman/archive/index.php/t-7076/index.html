Ok, its been said before and will be said again...
1) Go for AC and Hp rather than wis (search the forum for more info on this, but trust the community on this one)
2) Search the forum for threads on equipment.

Having done the standard response, here's some more concrete advice:
Jaundiced bone arms would be great, 350pp.
Totemic helm (quest, costs 160pp) or Jaundiced helm (350pp)
Rings: Replace with 6ac 65hp (velium fire wedding rings, 800pp for a pair, jeweller made). Keep the wis ones for tradeskills.
Polished bone bracelet - helps your dots, not sure on cost

If you've still got cash to spend:
Sarnak Battle shield 1kpp
Iksar Ceremonial bp (regen bp) 5kpp (very very nice item)
Jaundiced bone boots (detecting a trend here? :))
Crystal spider eyes

An important thing at your level is to start working on defence, and try to keep it maxed. Later on you will get a lot of aggro from slowing, and its important then to have a high ac. Working on defence at 35 is a lot easier than at 55.

Once you've got totemic there's not a lot of stuff you can quest - just keep working on xp and cash will come in eventually. The geos in crystal caverns are a good farming spot if you take a strong group of friends with you.

Edit: Just saw your "soloing" profile with totemic arms - jb arms are still an upgrade but not quite so important with totemic :) Really, I'd keep the totemic on all the time and sell that armband, 0ac on arms is not good, grouped or solo!
