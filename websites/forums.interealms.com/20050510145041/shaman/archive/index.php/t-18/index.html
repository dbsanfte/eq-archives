Drexzil
My personal favorite 24-30 solo spot is in Warsliks Woods at the entrance to Dalnir (pos 3500, pos 3950). When you go in you'll see three forks, left is a dead end, center is a three spawn of Skulking Brutes, right is a two spawn of Skulking Brutes and the entrance to Dalnir. The Brutes should con blue to even at 24, maybe a rare yellow. They will not aggro, even though they con KOS, and you can kill them right infront of each other because they do not assist. Pull with Walking Sleep, then Malise (they tend to be quite resistant at times), E Breath, Affliction (optional), Root and step back and med/regen while the dots go to work, repeat dots/root as needed. These things hit hard for their lvl, you will not be able to melee through the whole fight. Just remember, if you get into trouble, the zone to Dalnir is right there. Just be ready to zone back out of Dalnirs quickly, there is usually a lvl 30ish caster mob right inside.

There are a couple of advantages to fighting here:
1) They drop good loot (commonly drop gems worth 1-5pp each 2) This is an underused zone, usually the only people in it are
Grachnist farmers, and maybe an occasional giant fort group. The best thing about this obviously is you are far away from the ignorant newbie /ooc and /shout spam of LOIO. :p

Good luck and safe journeys
