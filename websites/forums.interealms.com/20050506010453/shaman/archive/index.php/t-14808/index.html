Do the totemic quests. They are extremely good quests. Indeed a different time, are the totemic quests worth it? It definitely was worth it for me at the time, but it was the best I could get and it was really fun and felt like an accomplishment.

The parts arent that hard save for the bp and Grizzleknot, if you do decide to do them, I wouldnt waste much time on the bp, I ended up buying the drop from someone and multiquesting it.

Would I do them again if I started fresh on another server? I started a warrior on another server (and wife started cleric) and we quested all of that armor except for the bp which I found a great price on, so my answer is, probably. Would it be the best equipment you could get your hands on? No, with the bazaar, you probably will find better stuff.

I would say try them, but dont waste a rediculous amount of time on any one piece, do them when they are level appropriate and you will get experience as well. My totemic served me well up to my high 40s and low 50s before I started replacing it with Jaundiced Bone.
