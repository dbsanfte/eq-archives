weissblau
Bear in mind that as a rough rule of thumb, 5 AA's between 60-65 are about equal to one level. If you have zero AA's now, you are looking at 27 to get to Cann V. 27 AA's for a level 60 will put them at level 65, EASILY. So it comes down to a choice between 27 AA's and level 65.

So...

If you ever LFG, you want level 65 first. Nobody picks a level 60 with Canni V over a level 65, although maybe they should.

If you raid, you want 65 first. Fewer resists and you're not the mob magnet.

If you ONLY SOLO, or group with friends about the same level as you, and if you really can't wait, then it's up to you.

Epilogue:

This was a very helpful thread that I've read several times. I thought it fitting that I come back here and provide an update.

About three weeks ago, I finally saw cann4 for sale in the bazaar at 7k. It was painful (I had seen it for 1k at level 55), but I borrowed 5k from an ex-guildmate and bought the spell. I ground cash and paid my friend back two days later.

This weekend, I dinged 60 with my shaman. The next day I caught the guildmate online and he gave me the malo, topor and several other spells I was missing. I had not asked for the spells up until that point in time... Some had suggested getting the spells ahead of time in case my guildmate changed his mind... I didn't see that as likely, but in the event that it happened... I valued the friendship more than the spells.

In doing the research on what's next for me I've found that the decision on 60 vs 65 is less painful than it used to be. With LDON, the increased exp range, and plenty of "revamped" zones having higher level mobs, there are plenty of places other than POP that a 65 can grind at. My concerns about having to equipment grind in green zones was dealt with by noticing that most of those situations were either already green at 60, would continue to be dark blue after 60... or the equipment was obsolete and I no longer wanted to camp it anyways!

My current plan is to go straight to 65 with my paladin, and stop at 62 with my Shaman to grind AAs for cann5, as well as to look for a place that I can 2box and get spectral parchments for my 63/64 spells. POJ basement has been good to me, and I already have the important 61/62 spells with 11 extra parchments banked.
