Mamutoi
Beastlords get some of the same spells as a shaman, but the shaman get them at a lower level then the beastlord.

Shaman get a 75% slow at level 51. Beastlord get a max of 65% slow and not until level 65.

Shaman get the Malo line, Beastlords do not which makes it harder for them to land the slows.

Shaman get the Focus line of spells, Beastlords do not.

Shaman have a better line of heal spells including Torpor. the best heal spell beastlords get is Chloroblast.

Beastlords do not get the Cannibalize line of spells, they do get a mana/health regen line of spells though.

When someone brings a Beastlord into a group or a raid it is usually not to play the same role as a shaman, unless it is for slowing in absense of a shaman.

I am not saying shamans are better.. just beastlords are different and the role they play is different.
