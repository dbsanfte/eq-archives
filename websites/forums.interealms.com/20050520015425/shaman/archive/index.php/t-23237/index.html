Dasha, I would recommend that you look in the bazaar for HP gear. You have a lot of WIS gear, and I think you are selling yourself short because of this. I follow the philosophy of HP/STA > WIS.

First off, I would replace your rings. Valorium Ring of Gallantry would be a nice addition to your equipment ( HP +65, STA +8 as well as give you some much needed AC ). These could be pricey on your server. If you do not have a lot of cash, I would search in the bazaar for rings by HP. If there is something that is in your price range, I suggest you get it. OR, you can farm the pieces in PoV. The Golems drop the Metallic Liquid, the Razors drop the talon, and you will need a brick of valorium. I am sure you could find a jeweler to help you with the combine for a nominal fee.

Second, earrings. Bloodmetal Earring of Engagement. ( HP +70, STA +8, and AC ). These could be pricey on your server. If you do not have a lot of cash, I would search in the bazaar for earrings by HP. If there is something that is in your price range, I suggest you get it. As with the Ring of Gallantry, you could farm the components to make the earrings in Plane of Tactics. If you are not Tactics flagged, you can do the side quest in PoS by killing Diaku. There are four Diaku that drops a quarter piece of a medillion. Once you collect all four, go to Wembly and turn it in. Make sure you bring a group with you that is not flagged for Tactics. I say this because this flags your whole group for tactics.

Third, gloves. Leaf Scale Gloves. Again, it has HP and better AC than your current set.

Forth, Neck.

Fifth, Face.

Sixth, Boots. For now, I would switch to your epic boots. It has some STA on it as well as some resists.

Seventh, Primary. I would replace your current weapon with something that is more stat friendly. If you have your epic, I would suggest that for now. If not, I would work on it. It seems that you started it because I saw the Fur Boots in your inventory.

Eighth, Secondary. I would look at getting a shield. The Tactician's Bulwark would be a nice addition to your gear. It drops in Tactics, or you can purchase it in the bazaar.

Since I see that you hunt in BoT ( you have the ring in your magelo ), you can camp various areas to try to win some ornate for you. In BoT, you could get Gloves, Sleeves, Bracers, Pants, and in the Towers, BP. An alternative to Ornate would be LDON's. You can work toward earning adventure points in Everfrost to gear yourself. Also, you can work towards earning runes for your level 65 spells. The two biggest spells I would try to get is Malos and Q.

Overall, you went heavy on the WIS gear. To use the power that SHM possess, you need to beef up your HP. I am sure you can get decent upgrades for HP in the bazaar.

Also, make sure that you have canni iv spell memmed and cast it til your fingers bleed.

Finally, for cash, if you are keyed for Sebilis, this will be a nice zone for you to play in. It will give you some experience in dungeon crawling as well as earning cash to either: 1) buy spells or 2) replace gear. Another good area, and it has some decent upgrades for you, would be Droga. There are quite a few threads on the boards about Droga, and I would take the time to read them.

My 2pp.

Enjoy playing your Shaman!
