No group chats, no zone chats, no direct tells of any kind unless its through rare magic items, spells, or familiars.

Make shouts have a longer range than /say obviously, but not throughout a whole zone. This would make spying on others viable, browsing through a bazaar more fun, chat spam less of a problem, and overall make things more realistic.

Of course, this would annoy a lot of people and make it harder to keep track of friends, so maybe it would be better suited to a roleplaying server or something.

Some interesting things could come from this though.

Mail system: Bards/rangers/rogues could have a profession, delivering messages to people. Tracking would be essential!

Familiars: You could send your familiar to speak with someone else, different familiars would travel at various speeds and differing intelligence would affect how many words/letters you could send. Druids/rangers/magic users would benefit greatly from this.

Spying: Stealthy characters could eavesdrop on other people/guilds/groups.

Travel: if you get lost, you cant just shout for directions or /tell that friend with a second comp to go look up a map for you. This would be a good thing for roleplaying rangers who could get to know a zone and check their tracking for people who may get lost and show them around.

Best of all: Nobody shouts DING!!!! and if they do, youre probably too far away to hear it.
