After grinding to warmly and helping a few friends grind up to warmly, I think I know the best way to get faction.

You need to get a full group (or almost full)...just sit in DS and /ooc that you are doing a faction group and I've never had a problem finding takers.

Go to the fort that blocks the NC zone. Go up the ramp that goes on top of the fort wall. Make sure you have dps and a healer (even a half-assed shaman healing worked for us ;) )...then slaughter back and forth on the wall, working from one end to the other. Went from kindly to warmly in about 2 hours....just slaughtering back and forth. Every mob up there gives a faction bump.

Also, xp is better here than in NC. All the mobs are light blue, but they die so fast that you get better xp ;)

Thats my advice =)
