Bandicoot
As a 61 shaman I PLed a mage up to 55 in about 4 weeks. I found that the mage died a lot earlier on but that this was due to adds. From 30 - 50 was spent mainly in the old Nurga and Droga and then PoJ.

I thought the mage was pretty weak until 50 ish, but at 55 he is great! Sirocco is a 3 wave rain that does a lot of damage and is very mana efficient. Also being a long recast means not spending that much time on mage. Great for 2 boxing!

The air pet (I forget level, early 50's) can tank the PoN maze well enough (in combination with the 61 shammy pet plus slow and CH or torpor). These are level 60 mobs! I'm certain the level 60ish mage pets would be considerably better...

Not to mention at 65 the mage can charm elemental mobs...

So the answer is yes, shaman can PL mages. I would stick to zones/mobs without the risk of many adds, as the adds will make a beeline for the mage and kill him. But once the mage gets closer in level to the sham he will be great!!!

I am sad to say that the 55 level mage seems a better match than my 61 monk (RIP). Already higher dps and more versatile.

EDIT: By nature mages are cheap to run as well. They don't need much in the way of gear... as long as you buy spells which seem pretty cheap, a badly equipped mage does almost as much DPS as a well equipped one (until you get into FT items). otoh a warrior needs decent gear to be a good warrior... just another reason to go for mage.
Jinheim
Mages are AWESOME partners up until 60, or even past that if you are exping in the old world. Before pop came out, mage was my favorite partner. The reason for this was because mobs pre-pop hit so weak that the mage pet could easily tank them, and did insane damage. All you needed was slow and the pet could handle it. In pop, though, with mobs tripling for 544 or even harder, the pet just can't handle that. Maybe if you stuck to poi pod pon poj the mage would be okay, but anything past that isn't going to work too well. I am not saying that it isn't possible, it definately is, but it is really more trouble than it is worth.

From your sig, I see that your highest character is 61, with most in the mid to high 50's. In this level range, mages are EXCELLENT partners. In pop though they just aren't as viable as they were earlier, because they just can't handle the damage. Ditto monks. You can still do it, but it isn't as good as duoing with a tank, imho.

Either warrior or mage will work, really. Warrior is a solid choice from 0-65. Mage has his weak levels where it will be a struggle, and he has his strong levels where it will be a breeze. In the end, though, warrior wins out. That's just my opinion, though.

Either way, good luck with whatever you choose.
