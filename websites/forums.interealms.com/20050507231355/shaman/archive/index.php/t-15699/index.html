Rorgg
this is from a Shaman-FAQ document I'm working on:

How should I spend my AA points?

Of course, everyone has their own opinion. It's pretty widely accepted though that there's nothing that even touches the Cannibalization (aka Cannibalize V) ability for utility. This means your first 23 points are pretty well scripted:
3 points in Run Speed
3 points in Innate Regeneration, though some people like Innate Metabolism here. (Baron title)
12 points in Mental Clarity (Venerable title)
5 points in Cannibalization

Immediately after that there are a number of useful abilities, so many that there's no clear path. Among the most commonly suggested are:
Spell Casting Reinforcement 3/Mastery
Spell Casting Mastery 3
Radiant Cure (RC1 is only 2 points, it's a good take for early, maybe pick up 2 and 3 later)
Virulent Paralysis (level 1, though level 3 has its uses too)
Spirit Call (level 1; especially on PVP servers)
Mass Group Buff (if you raid a lot)
Natural Durability 3
Combat Agility 3/Lightning Reflexes 5
Tallanas
Yes, I would confirm that the vast majority of shaman say Canni5 is the best bang for the buck.

But I can also say from experience that spending 10 points in one go on MC2 and MC3 is heart-breaking!
