me and a RL friend r duoing a sham and necro (me the sham) and i know where to go to about 15 or so.. where would be some places to duo 15+?

My suggestion would be Kurn's Tower (Cabalis stone into the Field of Bone, then a short run) followed by the Estate of Unrest (Kaladim stone in to Butcherblock, run to Dagnor's Cauldron, then follow the beach/cliff to the tunnel on the right). Both have good experience modifiers and lots of undead Mobs. You'll collect enough bone chips to last a long time. These two zones beat the heck out of the typical PC/HHK route as far as I'm concerned. There are enough people to group with if you so desire, but neither is a zoo, at least not on my server.

In Unrest you start out with the yard trash just inside the zone, move into either the front hall or back door. Then work upstairs for a couple levels and finally move into the basement.

necros have never had an experience penalty (unless they're iksars, in which case they have the 20% racial exp penalty for their innate ac bonus, swimming ability, and the outdated regen bonus).

shadowknights had, a long long time ago, an exp penalty for being a hybrid class. those days are long gone now, and like necromancers they have no experience penalty (unless they're iksars, trolls or ogres).
