Not having a shaman or mage high enough to have tihs problem....still here's some possible suggestions....

Ask the mage if he/she is willing to not chain cast the pet. The mage has pet heal spells. You have heal spells. Heal the mage pet.

Mage pets no longer dispel, that was taken out. Trust me, I've been playing a mage for awhile. At one time, Epic pets used to dispel, and so did fire pets, but Verant stripped that spell from them. I have never seen them cast it.

The Muzzle spell is Muzzle of Mardu. 10-15% haste. Get one for your doggy if you have one too :)

If mage is using epic, try to get the mage to give the pet 2 Gnoll Hide Lariats also, that stun is a HUGE benefit.

Chaining the epic is NOT a good idea also, make sure said mage knows that. IF you chaincast, you do it solo with an earth pet :)

But yeah. Let pet get aggro, do your normal stuff. Treat the pet like the Tank it is if you are using it as a tank. Depending on the playstyle of the mage he may or may not be used to it.

FYI, Epic has ~ 4k hit points, so healing it is a good investment.

But yeah, if you are using hte pet as a tank, treat it like one. Drop your biggest buffs on it. Give it your biggest regen (Epic pet regens 35 hp/tick already, your regen helps quite a bit more). The epic pet is quite a beast, though. IF you are duoing with a mage, you would be MUCH MUCH more efficient wiping through masses of lower dark blues rather thanone higher dark blue.

I love duoing with a Shaman...like I said, its why I made one of my own. A Shaman and a mage were virtually MADE for each other :)
