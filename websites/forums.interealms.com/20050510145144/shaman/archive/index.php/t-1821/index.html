Sometimes I 2box a rogue. It is always in a dungeon, like seb or sometimes I make poor roguebot camp Verix with me.

In Seb I use Root religiously. Root keeps agro on me and since I can't snare (rogue has no snare items /spank) it controls runners. Easy to see when root is off cause mob starts hitting rogue. Using root you also dont have to worry about using evade much, if you get agro it's just cause the rogue is too close, back up a step. Spam backstab...

I used insidious decay to keep the mob agro on me there rather than root, it was a mega taunt. I think now that they fixed/nerfed/changed the disease counter thing it might not work so well though.

Rogues are very fun to group and duo with. They do amazing damage and as a shaman you should be able to tank many of the slowed mobs we kill for exp (froak!). :)
