Malevant Faeshadow
I did fine at 56. I am now 58 and still do rats in PoJ on occasion. Its the fastest solo xp I can get for the minimum amount of danger and hassle.

There is a spot you can pull the rats to that makes working on them highly easy due to fear pathing. While they can resist, if you start out with Snare and simply back off when they resist repeatedly, you can avoid hits easily. In fact, the only reason not to camp the rats in PoJ at 56 would be the loot. They can drop one thing, a starter for a quest that begins in PoT, and that rarely. No money, pelts, cash loot, etc, hence why I am in WW these days when I solo.

As far as agro difficulties, anyone who has hunted PoJ guards and prisoners can attest to some seriously bizarre agro there. I can be attacking a Sentry or rat and have one of its brethren run over me with no problem, but toss in a bard and suddenly we have 4 rats under mez and the cleric is rooting like crazy to keep clear of melee. As near as I can tell its partly level based and partly based on what buffs you cast on other folks during the fight, although I won't pretend to understand it other than to say that its not hard get around. I have never gotten additional agro from rats soloing there, using the standard method of snaring, fearing, beating down, and reapplying every 10 seconds or so, personally.

EDIT: Lyntz, I believe you are thinking of the rats in PoD. The ones in PoJ can hit for up to about 236ish, but I have never seen a feeble rat on track there.
