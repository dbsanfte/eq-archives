***************************

**********************************
"The bug is actually with all pet proc buffs, but the only ones we really feel need changing are the panther line of spells. So we'll be fixing the problem with them procing more and then upping the specific pet spells to compensate and retain the same rate as before."

********************************

I am also told that it is highly likely this change will take place on April 6th . That is all the Information I have regarding this issue at this time . I hope it answers some of the many questions you guys have .

Just wanted to keep you guys informed . As allways thanks for listening :)

I'll translate it since I can afford being more explicit than Rytan:

All modifiers(I assume it's just Dex) that increase procs for PCs are currently doing the same in an inappropriate magnitude for pets. This is true for ALL procs, but only Panther deserves being adjusted due to the magnitude of the proc damage.
Changing the proc rate of the spell itself makes no sense, as this would result in PCs proc'ing less than they currently do as well, and we feel the rate at which PCs are proc'ing is perfectly balanced, thus we will alter pets to proc less OVERALL and subsequently ONLY raise the proc rates for pet only proc buffs to the current state.

This will result in Panther proc'ing less for pets, but all other proc buffs remaining unaltered in their power.
