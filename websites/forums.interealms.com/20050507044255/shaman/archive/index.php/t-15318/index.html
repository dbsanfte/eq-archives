Keep in mind that not all people that sell batwings or water/food for 1k+ are scammers or idiots.

It's a system used to transfer money between mules, when the mule owners aren't online at the same time for a few days.

Example:

Batwing: 10,000pp
Water: 1,000pp
Food: 100pp

You want to transfer 12,500pp, you buy 1 batwing, 2 water and 5 food from that player.
