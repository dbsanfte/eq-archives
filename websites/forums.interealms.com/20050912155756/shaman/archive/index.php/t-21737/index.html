LaughingWoman
These 3 are very *FUN* zones, IMO. When we finished these, a large part of our fun in EQ dried up. They aren't your typical grind zones.

Veksar - tons and tons of fun, raise cabilis faction, too!
Chardok B - easier to get there with good chardok faction, but not necessary. The run is so long you'll probably want to camp out there a while, but we didn't really mind it. Great ZEM, good drops, good bazaar fodder, too. Duo-able quest for a NICE range item.
Droga - was already mentioned, but this zone is so much fun. I think I just love those goblin models.

Scalia
If the Shaman is planning to work on his 8th Shawl at some point, you may want to visit Velks. Not the best XP in the world, but very easy, mostly single pullable, almost half the mobs should still be dark blue at 62 and you can kill them non-stop. Grab all the Velium that you can carry and seel it in the Bazaar, not to mention the nice NO DROP earring from the new named spider on the first floor and the Bazaar drops from various named spiders.

Oh yes, and the PoJ jail area on the way to Mavuin. At 62 it's no longer the great XP it would have been for you in the 50s, but it's still decent, fairly easy if you camp in the safe spot in the main hall immediately on the right, and the diamonds/BDs/EPs plus VT drops worth 10-42-71-100pp do add up.

LaughingWoman
Veksar is a quicker run than FG, I'll wager (without a port to TS). FV book brings almost on top of the LOIO zoneline, then a half zone run into the lake. It's very easy to navigate the dungeon, too, once you've crawled through it a handful of times.

Droga - with a sow from outside, or RS3, getting to your choice named is pretty quick with invis. I'd typically risk it (even tho my partner would greater invis down and CoH me) because
1) the aggro range in droga is ridiculously small (unless they changed it).. it's truly tiny
2) there are mob-free spots at very regular intervals to stop, double check your buff, and continue on
3) there are very few spots along the way where you're likely to have more than 1 or 2 gobs to contend with. A couple fast slows and you're safe (assuming they're not ice comet guys, those are dangerous)

Fun fun drops to be had, and if I remember right the h2h we got out of the jail area is the best droppable h2h you can get. Or 2nd best... *grin*
