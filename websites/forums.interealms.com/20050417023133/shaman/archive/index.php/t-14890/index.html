Grixx
The Grey used to be great exp, prior to PoP and before they nerfed the zone a little out there. You're correct in saying that exp is "ok" but not phenomenal. It's on par with most other zones of that level. As far as loot is concerned, most of it centers around the pyramid in the middle of the zone. Common practice is to set up on one side of the pyramid and pull Shissar Revenants and Golems there. Shissar uncommonly drop scales which sell for ~18pp each (stackable) and also shissar organs, which are used in an EB item quest in Katta. Occasionally, a named Shissar can spawn (I believe there are 2 different ones) which have 2 items each in their loot table - some boots, cloak, BP, and um...I forget the other item. They're nice items for the difficulty. Be careful with the named - they do not cast, but they do hit for ~170 if memory serves and fast. I prefer to root, DoT them. Golems also have a chance of spawning a named golem (again I forget his name). He's a beefed up version of the regular golems and can drop some nice arms and a necklace I believe. These named can spawn off of ANY of the spots where the regular golems/revenants spawn around the temple - the static ones. There are also a couple other named mobs in the zone - a Hierophant skeleton and one other I believe, which have nice drops, but I've never pursued those, as I hate the traps and prefer to sit in a set spot.

Good luck.
