Grizlor
You can probably do NC named with the Root/DoT/Back Up/Heal/Get Summoned/Repeat strat. You are going to want to get Affliction Haste 5 tribute though so that you can get your DoTs in between summons.

1) Pull the mob with malos.
2) Root
3) Get Cripple, Sinia, Turg, and Malicious in
4) Click your 1.0
5) Cast Breath of Wunshi
6) Cast Curse of Sisslak
7) Get summoned, back up
8) Click your 1.5
9) Cast Bane
10) Continue getting summoned and backing up, apply heals as needed. Sic doggies if you get in an emergency situation, as they will "Tank" if the mob is rooted.

This was a generally viable tactic back in Gates for soloing Sewers named, sort of our variation on necro Snare/Summon/Mujaki kiting that necros can do. Back then Quies only healed 300 a tick, so straight up tanking partially slowable 950 hitters wasn't that feasable.
