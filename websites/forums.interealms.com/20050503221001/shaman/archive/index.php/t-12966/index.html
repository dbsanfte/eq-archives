I looked at my bank account and some guildies and I realized I'm rich ... and didn't even know it. Your post started me thinking about how I made all that money.

Most of my career, I duoed with a monk.

In our 30s and 40s we spent a lot of time killing giants in FM/DL. They drop a lot of items worth 10pp to vendors. We probably averaged a few hundred pp a day doing that.

In our late 40s to early 50s we spent a lot of time in CoM. We accumulated a bunch of trance sticks, staff of the scorned, and the other stuff that drops randomly off any mob in that zone. Over christmas of 2001, when we had a lot of time off, we made 30k from items there. Of course, the prices have dropped, so you really can't do that now.

In our late 50s we killed time in sebilis, ME and UP. Sebilis had good cash loot, while in ME we got lots of drops of stuff that we equipped, thereby letting us sell some of our gear for cash. In UP we got lots of items off the mini-named (Raldukan etc).

My point is that we never really spent much time "farming" for cash, like going to PoG or HG in Rathe or anything like that. We just made sure that where we were hunting for exp also had a steady cash source. After enough dead mobs and saving your pennies, we wound up with a lot of cash in the bank.
