Ogreshm
Pull the mobs near the Grieg's End zone, and you'll see some decent drops there. Many of the named seem to wander near there. Half of them drop silly quest items, half drop usable items.

You will also learn quickly what to pull and what not to pull. Don't pull sun reavers/revenants if mana is a concern, they have really stupid resistances and can be mana sinks.

Don't pull the red aliens (Tro Jegs?). They have a REAL nasty 250 DD fire proc on their attacks. Just about everything else is fair game. Pull the cavemen at your own risk. They are not particularly tough, but they range widely in levels (maxing out at 45-47ish). Using their caves as pull spots is an advantage.

Good luck.
