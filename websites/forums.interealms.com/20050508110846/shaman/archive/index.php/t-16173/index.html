Tiorr
Personally I feel the best way to find a good guild is not to worry about it for one and just group more.

At your lvl you should be "looking" for a guild in DL or CC (what I mean is grouping with people for exp and meeting other people, not soloing)

When you group you will find someone that you like to hang with.

More often than not they will be in a guild

After grouping with them a few times ask them what their guild is like

Ask them how often it raids, what the guild leadership is like, do they have a web site, do they feel included, is guild chat friendly etc...

The reason this is preferable, besides getting a guild "members" perspective instead of a guild leaders perspective, is that now not only do you have someone you will know in the guild, most likely you will continue to group with them.

And lastly chill... relax... don't worry about it...

Trust me it is much better to take your time and find a great guild than rush it and find a crappy guild.
