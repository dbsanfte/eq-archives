Sigh... i guess i am getting the childish responses... some info on Lineage for Litgruv.. .its a MMORPG already released in Korea, it went through Beta in Japan and Taiwan already, not sure if released. It is a PvP as well as Clan v Clan and Guild v Guild game. The object of the game is to hold castles, which allow you to tax people selling items.. ie if Sony put a 10% tax on everything sold in bazaar. You can also affect the respawn rates of mobs to allow faster leveling by your allies.

It is a PvP game but what people dont' realize is you take a HUGE PENALTY for GRIEFING... you get 240 "karma" points for the first killing of a person that doesn't fight back, 480 for the second 960 for the third etc... when you have karma points you turn RED... when you turn RED you very easily lose items. You see very few griefers.. definitely less than on Sullon Zek, or any of the other Zeks for that matter (yes i've played for a bit on all).


Gear > levels > knowledge on this game as far as i can see... 14 dark elf warrior and 15 orc mage.

The Good.

Awesome graphics.. much better than anything i've seen before, including WoW screenshots.

Awesome gameplay.. though its mostly hack and slash.. clans are already using teamspeak and working out pvp strats, boss strats and castle strats... think being one of the first to play EQ and trying to figure out how to kill mobs...

Awesome guild/clan system... you can create a clan by yourself at lvl 10 but if you want to have your guild name and symbol near your name.. you have to pay in both adena(plat) and sp(kinda like exp points)... basis of clan and guild warfare.. 5 clans make up a guild(i think) and you can make alliances.

Shamans get lifetap and rangers are overpowering... a great change from EQ.

The Bad

Horrible, Horrible Login server... you basically have to spam your login to get in.

Ummm the Exp grind is worse than EQ.. think 50-60 all the way through from 10-60 on this game.
