Rorgg
There are two purposes to the trials:

1. To open later zones. There was a designed progression for these zones. For instance, the trial opens Valor and Storms. In Valor, you kill Aerrin'Dar, which opens Halls of Honor. In Halls of Honor, you pass 3 trials there which opens HoHB (Mithaniel Marr's Lair) where you kill him as one of the prerequisites to the elemental planes.

There's a similar progression from Storms. Now even though Valor and Halls of Honor can be entered without flags at 55 and 62 respectively, you will not be able to get the progressive flags for the later zones without passing the PoJustice trial.

2. Charm&AA. When they opened zones, so make passing the various flags more enticing and reward those who did it "legit," there's now a charm you can get for free in PoK that gets better with each new plane you get access to (well, kind of). It's pretty nice and since a lot of people had no charm, it's essentially free stats. Also, for each one of these, you get a free AA point.

IIRC you get this bump from:

1. Passing PoJ trial (opens Valor/Storms)
2. Killing Behemoth (opens Tactics)
3. Killing Terris Thule (opens Torment) (also can be done by killing Bertoxx, but TT is much easier)
4. Killing Grummus (opens Crypt of Decay)
5. Killing Aerrin`Dar (opens Halls of Honor)
6. Completing Askr's quest (opens Bastion of Thunder)
7. Entering Temple of Solusek Ro (requires various kills)
8. Entering Elemental Planes (similar to above).

There are a lot of sub-steps in there too. It's something close to that. I have 5 of the first 6, still need Aerrin`Dar.
