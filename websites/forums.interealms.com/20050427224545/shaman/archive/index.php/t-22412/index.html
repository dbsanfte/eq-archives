Jhaiisiin
Something to consider is that each AA uses the same experience value as level 51 does. AA's are therefore far easier to acquire at higher levels when the mobs are worth more raw xp per kill. When you start is up to you. Some people put a trickle into AA's from the get go (10 or 20%), while others wait at least until 60. Personnally, I started the trickle at 55, and I killed it as I was getting close to 56 (REALLY wanted that group regen, darnit). It will seem to go a lot faster and easier if you're at least 60 or higher though, which is what's been pounded into me by my friends. The final decision is really personal preference, and what you want your shaman to do. If you feel you're lacking in a given area, and an AA will fix that, then by all means do so. If you're good as you are, then consider leaving off until you can bust out an AA per hour like the 60+ toons can in places like HoH or PoF.

Hope I wasn't too confusing.
