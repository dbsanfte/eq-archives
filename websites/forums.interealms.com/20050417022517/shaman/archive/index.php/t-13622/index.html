Soulstripper drops from a mob named 'an enraged soulstealer'. To get to the camp, from the dulak zone in, go thru the hidden wall place to the luggald caves. You can fight all the way down to bridge area, where it will split off, take the right path, and the soulstealer, or PH will be in the middle of the hallway. It's a static spawn, the soulstealer is lvl 51, conned blue to me at 62. Just have disease/poison/magic resists up, and hes easy to kill. If he has the soulstripper, you can visibly see him wielding it. Also there is a wanderer that will add while fighting if you don't kill him first. I just cleared my way straight to him.

How to break in luggald caves:

im a 51 shammy and the following been easy for me solo.
I go in invis until i come to the bridge. 1 mob in the middle of the bridge, thats the part where i make pet.
First off, root the mob and slow him. step back and make pet, buff up and kill the mob. Head doen the right part of the bridge there are 2 mobs as static spawn, can be pulled solo even as 51 shammy.

Kill em and head in, the next mob on the left side is the Named spawn. Usually i pull the 2 mobs at bridge and the one in the middle of the bridge. they drop gems / jewelery. My best result was 4 soulstripper in 1 day, which is 16k plus gems and exp, prolly one of the best loy camps.
The Soulstripper is lore so i use my everfrost stone, run to bazaar, hand soulstripper to mule and gate back to Dulak.
