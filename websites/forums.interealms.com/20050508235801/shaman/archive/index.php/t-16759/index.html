I have a few theories of my own...
1. I'm thinking that maybe the NPC who gives out the missions may not be updating frequently enough. Such that if you ask the Barbarian (Perlac?) Adventure Recruiter in EC for a mission, someone else may have either recently accepted (or maybe even rejected?) that same mission, which inadvertently locks that Adventure out as unavailable for a group.

2. There are simply just a limited # of instances available & once the limit is reached you have to wait until a spot opens up. How those instances are broken down (by map or zone would be most likely, but I guess it could be by either mission type or theme) I'm not exactly sure of yet. How busy those instances are determine whether you would be able to get an adventure for that specific map associated with your mission.

I personally would much prefer a menu of options to choose from based on what's available from each NPC. So a group set on just trying a Slaughter mission but didn't care about the theme could bounce around Wayfarer camps checking menus until they find one available. Other groups prioritizing the theme (Rujarkian for Bane for example) and didn't care about the mission type could just grab anything available in EC so they could get started on their adventure. Even better would be a more detailed window that would show how every instanced dungeon already active is faring.

So your menu would look something like this:
Adventurer tells you:
Greeting Frekkels, perhaps you would like to help blahblablah:

8.Collect 21 Orc fangs
9.Slaughter 56
(and so forth for each Rujarkian entry zone)

The more detailed version of my idea would reveal something like this:
There are some Adventurers already engaged in battling evil! They are:
N. Ro
1. Rescue Mission (46 minutes)
2. Collection (23/27 72 minutes)
3. Slaughter (43/56 39 minutes)

(and so forth...)

That way even if all of the types of missions you want are taken, you could at least get an idea of when the next might be available. The third slaughter mission has won and is somewhere in their extra 30 minutes probably going after named or playing with chessts.

I don't know how doable any of that is from a code perspective, but all the information seems like it would be readily available serverside. If even just a simple menu option was given it would be much better.
