House
If you are not twinked (and I'd guess you aren't, as you care about low/mid-level quests), you might want to look at the Totemic Armor quests. I soloed for the Boots at Level 22, though you'll need to wait a few levels, or find a small group (duoing with a Ranger is perfect, as they have a similar set of quests, Ivy Etched, and you can help each other out) to go for the other pieces. Check out http://www.eqshaman.com/equipment/totemicinfo.html or Allakhazam's (look for quests in Lake Rathetear) for info on them. Unfortunately, each piece requires Dufrenite, which runs about 150-175pp, depending on your Charisma. For some of the armor pieces, you could probably buy Ry'Gorr for the same price when you also add in the time spent traveling the world and looking for the necessary spawns. But the quests are a lot of fun, and the armor is good enough that I'm still wearing the Helm, Breastplate, and Boots at Level 55 (yeah, I know my gear stinks). And, if you choose to do all the quests, they can provide you some focus until around Level 30, plus they require you to hunt in new zones you've probably never been to before.

As for spell prices, I think most of the Level 24 spells (or at least the good ones) run about 15pp each.

Just a note. I solo'd the Glacier Bear at lvl 30. My heart was pounding the entire time, but I killed him easily enough. Just don't waste your mana nuking with cold based spells - he will resist. Keep him rooted and double-dot'd and you'll be fine. Only hits for around 34 or so if memory serves me right.

Also, beware the Lich of Miragul who follows a similar path as the bear. After I killed the bear, I suppose I spent a little too much time reveling in his defeat (and taking screenies), and the Lich sneaked up behind me and killed me with about four swings of his boney fist (100+ dmg). Luckily I'd already looted the bear... had just sat down to memorize Gate. D'oh. =)

Glacier bear pelt is for the Totemic Helm 12ac 5wis, in case it wasn't stated above.

The Totemic Boots (10ac 5str) quest is also fairly easy to do. Just need a terror spine from Najena (can be found not far from the zone entrance) and a orc chief's tongue from Lesser Faydark. Only thing dangerous about the Orc Chief part is to watch out for Brownies and that mean unicorn in Lfay. Also, be sure not to wander into the shadowed man camp... they hit pretty hard for their level. Think I was still lvl 30 when I killed the Orc Chief (could be wrong, tho). He was green, just like all the other orc's at the camp, and I single pulled him without agro'ing the other orcs. Also made a fool mistake here, too (I'm good at that, sometimes). After I killed the chief and looted him, I sat down to med up, and must've sat too close to the camp because all the other orcs agro'd on me as soon as my butt hit the dirt. Oh well, at least they didn't hit as hard as the Lich, I survived that fowl up.

As far as the other Totemic quests, I have no idea. The Helm is the only thing with wis on it, so I did it and the boots (because I was wearing ringmail boots at the time) and decided I'd buy some wis gear in EC instead of dufrenites for the other pieces.

Also, you should do the Testament of Vanear quest if you haven't already (which I recommend to all wis casters). It's a nice 10wis 10mana item for your off hand. Look it up on eqshaman.com. Quest is easy, just can be a pain waiting for Dyllin Starsine to pop. Think he's on an 8hr timer, tho some people say it's closer to 6hr. You should have no problem at all with it at level 24, as I did it at lvl 16.

That's the extent of the quests I've done in EQ, as they were the only ones that seemed worthwhile. Now that I'm a bit higher level, I have plans of doing the Coldain Prayer Shawl quests and eventually try for some Thurg or Skyshrine armor, and of course the Spear of Fate.

Hope this helps some, and highly recommend Testament of Vanear and Totemic Helm. They will serve you well for many levels.
