ok, you CAN solo there, but it can be very difficult when you are the only person in the zone. With the bad pathing, the total number of mobs and thier assist range, it is difficult for a lower level player to single pull the mobs. Harmony is the only saving grace to make it possible. The graveyard is another great spot to attempt to solo. It is an outdoor zone, so you can have sow up, etc. At night, the mobs become undead, and see through invis. During the day, they are regular and do not see through invis.
Expect to zone out alot when you get multiple adds until you get used to it. If you have a decent group, the xp is great as you will not be standing around waiting for mobs to spawn, and won't have to go far to get some new ones.

05-09-2002, 10:25 PM
I would suggest getting a group up for your first trip to this place.

Most servers are BYOG.

If you try to solo here, depending on level, start slow and learn your way around. You can get lost here, and CR can be tricky if you are by yourself.

Some mobs see thru camo all the time, some only at night.

Learn the traps, some are in the hidden room behind the graveyard. You will want help your first time thru here, as you can have a hard time finding the secret doors.

Mobs are underconned. Casters can kill ya quick. Agro is rampant in MM. If something runs from ya, you can expect a HUGE train coming back.

Learn how to run up to the crosswalk to the tower, and jump off the crosswalk, which if you take a left leads you right back to the zone line. The tower is seen down at the end of the ramp near the lake.

Be prepared before you go here, you will more than likely die, and you want to make sure you can get your corpse.

DO NOT wander down in the valley near the front of the castle. There are lots of mobs down there, one of which can be the shaman epic mob wolf mob. Very tough. Lots of HPs.

There are some traps in the castle that will strip your camo, be prepared. Sucks to run thru camo'd, and wham! you lose camo and the whole castle comes after you.
