FWIW, debuffs seem to be assigned X amount of aggro based on what family of spells they come from, rather than how strong the specific spell you're casting is. Cripple generates the same hate as Weaken, Turgurs generates the same hate as Tagars, Festering Darkness generates the same hate as Clinging Darkness, etc, so you probably won't wind up getting less aggro by using a lower level slow spell. Still, there's a lot to be said for using a faster casting, lower mana spell if the net effect is going to be the same anyway.

Gmork
Mob_01 is Partially effected means your spell did approximately 30% of it's total ability (ie 30% of 75% for tugurs or ~26%)

Mob_02 is Somewhat effected means 50% of 75% (37.5%)

Mob_03 is Moslty effected means 70% of 75% (~49%)

using a lower lvl spell will only make the slow less. It's all a percentage game.

the mitigation messages not exact as are the percentages. It's just a basic rundown of how the system works.
