Valya
The issue is Brells Mountainous Barrier ("BMB") vs. Strength of Tunare ("SoT") vs. Spiritual Vigor ("SV), three nice buffs that don't stack. I am trying to get a definitive strategy worked out for my guild to incorporate SV (we have our first Beastlord applicant).

My take is that ideal buffs would be the following:

SoT (125HP, 60ATK, duration 1 hour) for those who want max ATK and don't care so much about max HP (rogues, rangers mostly... unless they are using Gob Earring as clicky, it won't stack.)

Spiritual Vigor (225 HP, 40 ATK, duration 1 hour 12 mins) for everyone else (unless they are using Gob Earring as clicky, it won't stack.) (I guess monk's epic effect won't stack either)

*except*

BMB (225HP, duration 2 hours 30 min) for Clerics (YaulpV bounces off SV and SoT).

BUT-- all are group buffs -- and (from what I have gathered):

BMB overwrites SoT, SoT bounces off BMB
BMB overwrites SV, SV overwrites BMB
SOT overwrites SV, SV bounces off SoT

At one point on a recent raid most of my group had BMB, which was overwritten by SV, which was overwritten by SoT... my entire group had SoT for a while, involuntarily. The difference in HP doesn't make or break the raid but it was irritating.

For the life of me I cannot figure out a logical buff order that would satsify everyone.... including rebuffing when people die, and rebuffing when various buffs wear off (all have different durations).

In practice, I usually try to get folks to ask for SV, and single-target the clerics with Divine Strength so as not to overwrite any SoT's in the group. But, with only one beastlord on the raid, and the relatively short duration of SV, folks do ask me for Brells, leading to annoyed SoT users :(

Two questions:

Is my interpretation of the overwrite/bounce order correct? I can't see a way to check this using Lucy.

How do you folks handle it?


Thanks for your input!
