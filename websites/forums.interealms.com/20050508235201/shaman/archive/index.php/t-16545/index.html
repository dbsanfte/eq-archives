Mirror - Gives a chance to reflect spells cast on you back onto the mob that cast it... 5%, 10%, 15%, and 20% chance variants (i ii iii iv). Lasts 18 sec.

Guard - Absorbs 5% of melee damage you take until it absorbs X damage. X is determined by the level of guard. Lasts 5min.

SpellGuard - see Guard, except for spells.
