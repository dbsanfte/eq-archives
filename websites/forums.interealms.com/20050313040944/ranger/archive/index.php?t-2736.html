[using the cultural forge -- Trivial Unknown]

-Enchanted Large Mithril Brick
-Large Brick of Acrylia
-Smithy Hammer

Celestial Temper was made from the following:
[using the brewery -- Trivial @ 165]
- Blue Diamond Powder
- Purified Water

Blue Diamond Powder was made from the following:
[using the pottery wheel -- Trivial @ 112]
- Blue Diamond
- Jar of Acid (found in the Bazaar)

Purified Water was made from the following:
[using the brewery -- Trivial @ 165]
- Celestial Essence
- 2x Flasks of Water

Celestial Essence was made from the following:
[made inside a mortar and pestle]
- Celestial Solvent (found in the Bazaar)
- A Rune / Word / Page for higher level research (I used Words of the Ethereal)

This could not have been achieved without the help of my Bard friend, Cevinzol who had the necessary skills in Pottery, Brewing, and Smithing. I only combined the bow, but we both worked hard tonight getting all the components and things together.
