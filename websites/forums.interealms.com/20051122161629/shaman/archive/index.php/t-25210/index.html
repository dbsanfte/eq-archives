One of the things to consider here is how the populations on the servers evolve in situations like this.

The PvP servers start with all the griefers, but also with a fair number of decent players who are (possibly naively) hoping that PvP wont degenerate into a griefer-fest this, the latest new game. Over time the proportion of griefers go up as the decent players get fed up of being griefed and either quit the game or head to a PvE server. Eventually pretty much the only non-griefers left on PvP servers are either noobies who haven't realised what they are up against, or a few high level players in good guilds who are protected to some extent from the griefers. So at this stage the PvP servers are - if nothing else - doing everyone on the PvE servers a favour by attracting the griefers.

The next stage comes when the number of victims available on the PvP servers falls to such a low number that the griefers find they are either eating each other or else failing to find victims at all. At this point the griefers start heading to the PvE servers which start seeing a significant rise in all the nonsense these idiots pull - scamming, tricking noobies into duels etc.

OK, thats a cynical view based on past experience. It will be interesting to see if WoW follows the norm.

Thought I would share a story:

(cut-n-pasted from an email to my buddies. I was lvl 26 at the time.)

On Saturday I was back in Ashenvale and I ran across a 25 rogue who
was all by himself fighting mobs. I immediately decided to gank his
ass. I went catform and prowled around behind him as he was
fighting...tried to right click and attack but it didn't work.
Previously I had read that attacking a PvP flagged player would flag
you, but apparently they changed it so you now have to turn your own
flag on first.

So I right click a few times, nothing. Damn now I'm getting nervous
and he sees me just 2 feet behind him. I remember that "/f" was a
shortcut for /pvp so I try that a couple times...Nothing. I'm getting
frantic at this time, the element of surprise is gone and he's
starting to maneuver around me. Finally I "/pvp" and immediatly open
with a special attack.

His PvP flag had wore off.

Damn. Now I'm the one who is flagged PvP all by myself in the middle
of nowhere... I give him a few seconds to see if he is going to flag
but he is at 40% health. I decide that I need to find a nice quiet
spot to wait out the 5 minutes so I get off the main path and find a
spot to pull a few mobs. The mob is higher level than me so it's a
decent fight when lo and behold I see an invisible rogue sneaking
around near me. Guess he was pissed that I tried to ruin his day.

He gets the jump on me (although he waited until I had killed the mob)
and immediatly starts shredding my health. In the 20 second fight I
managed to root him and back away. Heal nuke nuke reroot nuke etc.
He breaks root and gets close and stuns me. I see him moving around
to my backside and I'm sure this is the end. I manage to live through
the backstab and I spin around and start in with the beatstick. He
almost had me but a well timed heal potion turned the table. He died
and I was left at ~20% health and mana. My heart was racing but I
was whooping it up at home. :)

The graveyard was right across the road and I knew that he would be
able to run back 15 seconds or so...Even freshly rez'd he'd have more
health/mana than me so I high tailed it into the hills to rest up.
The hills were full of dead ends (which were full of lvl 30 spiders)
so I ended up running around frantically trying to out race monsters.
By the time I was patched up and back to the spot the rogue was gone.
