Amise
I'm pretty sure that at least some of the gems you need for Ivy armor are only able to be bought, for example the Star of Odus can only be obtained from an Erudin merchant. You could check the quest listings on Allakhazam, they may have more information about that.

With the new NPC system items that they don't want are just handed back, but you might want to identify them anyway just to avoid confusion. All int casters can cast identify from about level 16 I think, and there are a couple of cheap ID items you can get, one being the Silvery Mask. There's also a quested ID item, The Questing Ranger (http://www.questing-ranger.com) has a walkthrough for that quest.
Myrdale
