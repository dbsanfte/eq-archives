Go the the control panel, then go to display. From there click on advanced to bring up all the options for the card. Click on the tab that says GeForce 6800. In the picture of the nvidia logo or whatever, what does it say for the BIOS version? If the number ends in a 14, you may have a bad bios. You can "flash" the card to the right one, but I'm not sure how comfortable you would be doing that.
Gorg Grimm
go to guru3d.com and download rivatuner.
install it
load it up
click the customize button under your video card. a few graphic tabs will come up. click the one that looks like a mini video card.
click the agp tab
turn fastwrite to off.
hit apply.
your set.

you will need to do this everytime you reboot the system.

alternetavely you can go right into your bios on boot up and look for agp fastwrite and turn that off.
