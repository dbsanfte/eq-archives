Northerner
There is only one thing that matters in the Executioner trial, killing a Mob before the timer goes off. I know, that's kinda like saying "get MobX's HP to zero before your HP" but it really is kinda key.

This means you need some DPS. You can get it in steady amounts (Rangers, Rogues, Warriors, Mages, Beastlords etc) or in bursts (Wizards, Druids, Mages, discs etc). Burst damage is great if people know the timing, nothing kills a trial more often than a bad streak for all the melee at the wrong time. Steady damage is your staple as otherwise folks tend to run out of mana. Damage Shields are invaluable as they are casted ahead and potions, poisons and other bufflike things can make a big differance. Keeping transition times to nearly zero is absolutely key. Your biggest threat in any wave is the first Mob, think about the spawns and position everyone properly.

Now, the only other thing to worry about is keeping people upright. I've done this through CC (Enchanter/Bard/root) and healing or through slowing/healing/DA/kiting. ANY method is just fine, you just need to keep folks (and hence damage) alive. AE slows/tashes/HoTs and such can be very potent in some groups while in others you want more traditional methods. It really is not a normal camp situation, think about getting HoTs in ahead of the waves and consider using runes and non-tanks to focus the healing properly. A Shaman tanking all the time in an exp group is a recipie for disaster, in a trial it might not be.

Whatever you do, think about who you have with you. I've now been dragged to this trial more often than I like to think about and I've seen wins with the oddest combonations of players. Everyone can contribute, it is simply a question of solving the basic premise. You must do X amount of damage to any of Y Mobs in Z time. Then you must repeat this without getting overwhelmed.

As to the 'boss'? When in doubt simply kite him around until mana permits a good beating. He is trivial at that point as long as one healer and one damage dealer survive.

Viken
1. Everyone has to be able to target and assist lightning fast. Don't wait for an assist call. Just mash your /assist key until you get a target. Use /assist on. When the mob dies the MA needs to hit F8 and start attacking the next mob within 1 second. Everyone else needs to assist within 1 second. If that's too fast for you then you aren't meant to pass.

2. Have all six people create a campfire around the mob so that when the mob switches (and they will seeing as how you dont have the time to taunt before breaking mez or cast agro spells such as ensnare/flamelick) they don't go running all over = lost dps.

3. As a ranger, at least get level 61 so you can use your new anti summon nuke which does 725 damage I think.

4. Have the cleric cast elixer before the waves starts to avoid a bit of agro.

5. If you get to the 5th round then kite the executioner. A lot of teams will be oom at this point.

The real key is #1 and #2 above.
