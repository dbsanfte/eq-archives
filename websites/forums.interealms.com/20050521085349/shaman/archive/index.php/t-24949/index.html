Definitely quests are the way to go. Xp'ing with quests is way better xp than straight mob killing. For lvl 10-13, do the mites for some decent armor (to wear or sell), and then hit the Knight Lt. for his quest killing snakes, rats, and mites for sp and xp.

Allakhazam's is definitely worth it for quests and equipment in the lower levels. Surf through all the zones and pick out the ones you want to equip your character. That way, you'll have a decently equipped shammy. So far from the looks of it, Shamans are going to be a good solo and group class. No BL's to steal our thunder.
