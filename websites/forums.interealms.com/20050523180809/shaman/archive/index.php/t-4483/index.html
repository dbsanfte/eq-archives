Fascaser
There are a couple things that you can do around 14.

But first thing is to start working on getting your skill up to speed. Don't depend on any one skill but take a hour or two and do greenies to work them up. You will see the differance in the long run "Believe Me".

For example ... work on One Hand Blunt then switch to 2 Hand Blunt. Keep your skills maxed to your level. Next level (15) adds Dodge as a skill ... which will help some on damage recieved. Good thing to do as you jump major skill cap's is to work on them doing greenies. It's also a good time to work on your melee skills.

Next work on casting skills. Do True North till you are blue in the face to build divination skills NOW. It will pay in the long run. Try to keep your skills even with your level rather than letting any one of them fail behind. You can also work on casting skills during fights ... so throw a Burst of Strength or Inner Fire on tanks.

Then ... work on your parking skills. You have root now ... so work on crowd control skills. Parking adds is a skill that you will use throughout the rest of your playing if you are a grouping Shaman. Creating the macro's, working on timing, and testing holding/or lossing aggro are important skills to learn and work out with each set of spells. Soon your slow will start adding major effects to the fight ... and likely drawing aggro to yourself ... "root is your friend" here.

Last ... if possible work on gear. Get as much AC as you can get along with HP. And you don't have to buy everything. There are places that you can pick up OK gear off drops. Check out the area's that higher up's solo' or do mass AA'ing. Lot's of times gear is left to rot when fast pulling groups are working it in a area. Plus there are a lot of bargin's in the Bazaar nowadays ... which are perfect for early levels gear wise.

Hope this helps ... hang in there ... it will get better.
