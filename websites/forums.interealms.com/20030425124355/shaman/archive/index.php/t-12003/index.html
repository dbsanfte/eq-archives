Shiuna
After realizing I was spending too much time dancing and not enough time fighting, I decided to do the quest last night.

I found the strange ochre clay in bazaar for 50p and snapped it up - 1 down.

I made my first trip to Dreadlands and Karnors Castle to try the "/ooc loot bone chips" request as recommended in the walkthrough. What a place...just getting to the castle was intimidating. I zoned in to Karnors Castle and there were 12 corpses at the line....yeesh! I hoped they had been dragged there. I asked in ooc if anyone was killing the Skeletal Warlord and someone said "yes". I asked if I could trade shaman buffs for the bone chips and was told to wait at the entrance until the warlord spawned. About 25 minutes, and 2 trains, later I got a tell to come to the well and loot. I checked the eqatlas map and nothing named the well. I told him I dont see the well on the map. He told me to just come to the LCY. Nothing like that on the map, either. Then he told me to come all the way to the back and turn left. I said ok and then all my buffs started blinking....can it get any worse? Time was running out, so I made sure gate was memmed, invised myself, and ran for the back left corner. The room was empty, then my guy appeared and led me to the next room. I looted the bone chips and breathed a sigh of relief. Before I could buff him he ran off, so I sit and memmed the usual melee buffs. He came back and asked for strength and dex. I didn't realize he was the puller and had just pulled, so I started buffing and got aggro from the skeleton he just brought in! Only got hit twice before the group got his attention and I moved to another corner. After the fight I finished the buffs, thanked him again, and gated out - 2 down.

Next I made my first trip to Trakanon's Teeth. This place was four times spookier than Dreadlands. Of course, the hunting spot for Yun Shamans was all the way across the zone. After zoning in from Swamp of No Hope, I went south along the wall until I was even with the gate to Old Sebilis, then went east. It was night...and raining...and I could hear things. Got to the gate safely, went through, summoned pet, buffed, and took a deep breath. Then the rain stopped and the sun came up...ok, maybe a good omen. Went back out the gate and headed west. Within 30 seconds I saw movement, targeted it, and it was a Yun Shaman, moving fast! Sent the pet in and waited until he taunted, then cast the first DoT. Pet gets jumped by Yun Knight and I can't heal fast enough. I root them both and scoot. Then I notice about 3 new icons in my effects window, none of them good. I healed myself a couple of times, medded, summoned another pet, buffed, and took another deep breath. I know they're both out there waiting for me. Headed back over to first fight area and get jumped by the shaman. Pet engaged shaman and I backed off and rooted him. Then I get jumped by the knight and I rooted him and backed off. I nuke several times with new poison blast spell and shaman goes down just after the pet. I try to move over to loot....what the ?...I'm rooted! I looked at the knight, he looked at me, and my root icon started to blink! I rooted the knight again for good measure, looted the shaman powder, and gated out - 3 down.

Now for the easy one, the crushed diamonds. I headed to the Firiona Vie docks to get on the boat. There's a Translocator guy there that told me the boats aren't working and he can transport me to Timorous Deep. I asked him to and I appeared on the dock at Timorous Deep. I ran to the south end of the island and found a large stone bird statue. From there I levitated, invised, and ran straight south to the location in the walkthrough, picked up the bag, ran back to the dock, and got transported back to the Firiona Vie docks - 4 down.

I walked over to Marlyn McMerin's shop, gave her the 4 items, and pressed "Give". The bone chips appeared on my mouse pointer and I saw a message that she had no use for these. Then I got all the quest completion messages, experience, and faction adjustment messages. Then I got 2 messages from her listing the items she wanted. I checked and the spell scroll was in my inventory. I sat down and added it to my spell book and all was ok.

So I submitted a /bug report, but I got the spell! Yea!

Note to self: make sure you know what's going on around you before you start casting buff spells.

Shiuna, Barbarian Shaman of 44 years (but looks 10 years younger)
