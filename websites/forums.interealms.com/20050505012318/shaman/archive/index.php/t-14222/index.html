Jinheim
Ok, here is the proper way to do it.

First of all, where the bridge starts. Off to the right of the fake bridge, if you look closely at the cliffs edge, there is a corner where the cliff bends. It is a very obtuse angle, but it is there. It would kind of look like this ___________/ except the angle is wider and its upside down. If you are facing the opposite side, the edge of the bridge is on the corner and the width of the bridge extends to the right.

Once you think you know where the bridge starts, go in to bear form. After you have done that, click off sow and then duck. When you move, you will move very slowly and slide. Start sliding off the edge of the cliff. If you are in the wrong spot, your model will start to go over the edge and will be suspended at a very akward angle, almost completely upside down. If you are in the right spot, your model will dip for a second and then right itself again, because you are once again standing on solid, yet invisible ground.

Once you are on the bridge, you are going to want to run between the two big pillar things. Looking across the bridge lines you up almost exactly with one of the primary compass directions. I think it is west. If you turn about 1 tick to the right from whatever the primary direction is, you'll be on course to stay on the bridge. Go slowly at first, and use the bear form trick. If you are about to go off the edge, you'll start to dip down.

Once you have done it a few times, you can just look across the gap and see where the other side of the bridge will end, and can just go straight for it. I just autorun now I know it so well.

Also, someone said that rogues survive the fall: There is a small chance that anyone can survive. I have survived it before. In general, though, no one lives. Last time I was in the deep we had an invisi-bridge virgin with us, a rogue. I told him "There's a trap in the middle of that bridge that needs to be disarmed" and sure enough, he died when he fell. After I finished laughing, I felt kinda bad =\.
