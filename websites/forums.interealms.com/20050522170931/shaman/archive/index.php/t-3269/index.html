snapdragn
you have some nice gear for your level in several slots. I would suggest balancing your HPs (it appears that is your main focus) with a bit more Wis/Mana. I personally shoot for about 150 Wis, but it's personal preference.

A chipped bone bracelet is very nice as well - AC 4, 5 Wis and reduces the cost of your DoTs by 25% or so (cant remember name of focus). Makes what mana you have last a bit longer.

Don't listen to anyone that recommends Jaranth Armor from KC - you can't wear it.
