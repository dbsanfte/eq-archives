I play a shaman, and ONLY a shaman. If it weren't for the fact that I had NOTHING to do except EQ on a friends computer for 2 months straight, I never would have played this game for more than 5 minutes. As a result of this, I have no alts. Every group I have ever been in has had a shaman in it (me). I want to know just how many groups exist out there with NO SLOWERS. Is it really that common to run around without a slower? I was honestly of the impression that this NEVER HAPPENED. If I am right and people only fight unslowed mobs if their group's slower died before landing it, why are our heals balanced on the assumption that no other healer in the world is willing to have someone else slow for their group?

I guess if mobs existed in the game at the level our heals start to fall behind that you could actually fight unslowed with clerical healing it wouldn't matter, but I don't see any post 60 group ever going anywhere without a slower in my experiences.
