Baldaorm
I believe I did it with shaman/cleric/paladin/monk/ranger/ranger. Cleric 64, rest 65. Might have been 2 monks and 1 ranger, it's been a while.

We had no level 65 spells (I think one ranger had the summoned DD proc buff spell) at the moment but were all in Vex Thal gear with huge amounts of AA.

Do not use 1h weapons, evil damage shield on these things. Group heals are good but have huge agro so be careful with them. The cleric was mostly doing group CE/group CH while I'd keep peoples hitpoints even so we could fire it off with everyone around 30%. Divine Arbitration and Quiescence would be huge for this but it was too soon after release so we didnt have these.

Other than that, My job was to add cold damage to the rangers first (kill theirs so they could use bows on the others) then the monks, then the paladins.

I'd dump the wizard and add another tank, a monk with a caens type weapon would be ideal. If not that, a shadowknight would be very good too. If you want to keep the wizard you'll have to be quick on the root parking. I don't remember which type could be rooted but one of them could.

Finally, yes, the rest of the trials are simple compared to this one I think.
