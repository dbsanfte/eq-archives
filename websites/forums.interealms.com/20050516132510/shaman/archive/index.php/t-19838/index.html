[edit: Updating based on what I know first hand]


Spell Awareness - 6 points.
Passive, granted to the whole group.

Give you the ability to recognize ALL spells being cast in your area, including both PC and NPC. This ability simply changes the text "Henora begins casting a spell" to read "Henora begins casting the spell [Turgurs Insects]".




Inspect Buffs - Level 1 - 4 points.
Active, group members can create hotkeys from the Leadership Window.

Adds a hotkey ability that allows you to target a group member and get a list of their buffs. It displays exactly like /pet report health.


Inspect Buffs - Level 2 - 6 points.
Active, group members can create hotkeys from the Leadership Window.

Enhances the Inspect Buffs 1 ability to display a timer that shows the time remaining on each of the buffs reported.

Mitrian
From what my guild has discovered so far:
- Offensive Enhancement adds 10 ATK per level to your party members. (Max 50 ATK)
- Mana Enhancement adds FT1 per level to your party members. (Max FT5)
- Health Regeneration adds HP regen at 1 per tick, per level to your party. (Max regen 5)
- Health Enhancement - the first level adds 10 HP to each party member. (I'm not sure how much for additional levels yet)

Unfortunately, the group leaders and raid leaders points are totally independant of each other. You can't earn points as a group leader and buy raid leader abilities, and vice versa.

Henora
Just an update. I've purchased Spell Awareness (6 points) and Inspect Buffs 1 (4 points). I really really like spell awareness and so does my group. I am made leader like instantly now, heh.

Spell Awareness is innate for your group. It works on both PC and NPCs.

In the past you would see:
Henora begins to cast a spell.

With Spell Awareness you see:
Henora begins to cast the spell [Turgur's Insects]

Bard songs are shown without their name and simply come up in the brackets. For example, rather than seeing:
Rizlona's flames pulse through your body.

With spell awareness you see:
[Rizlona's Call of Flame]

You can also see when people are using AA abilities, like spirit call, suspended minion, cannibalize, bestow DA, Lifeburn, etc.


Inspect Buffs is a bit less useful in the first form, I am really looking forward to the second one though. You make a hotkey in the Leadership window (any one in your group can make the hotkey once you have the ability, by opening their Leader window and pressing the button). Then target a person in your group and press the hotkey you just made. It gives you a list of their buffs (and debuffs), similar to how /pet health does.

Level 2 adds a timer feature, that shows you the time remaining on all buffs (and debuffs).

I'm working on level 2 next. Will be very nice to just quickly target a group member and see how long until the chanter haste we bummed before we started is out. It's easy enough to time our own buffs, if we must, but this will time others too. Your cleric pals will know at a click if they should do HOV or just refresh one person individually.
