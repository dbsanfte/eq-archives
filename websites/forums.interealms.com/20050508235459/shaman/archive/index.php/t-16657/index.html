Roxxanne
In a Ruj Hills normal adventure the other night, this was our one gear drop:

Spaulders of the Slavemaster
Slot: SHOULDER
AC: +8 Cha: +8 Wis: +5 Int: +5 Disease Resist: +8 Cold Resist: +8
HP: +40 Mana: +20
Classes: All Classes
Races: All Races
Slot 1: Type 1
Recommended Level: 63

Since I won the roll, I set about to try to sell it and discovered 6 available in the bazaar at prices down to 1500pp. Thinking about it, the big problem is the crazy recommended level of 63. I mean, how many level 63 players would wear this? Nice enough item for a level 40 player, but 63?

I am sure Sony knows this, so what are they up to with these LDoN drops and their recommended levels? My theory is that Sony is using these drops and the player economy of the bazaar to establish a certain minimum level of gear for, in the case of this drop, level 63 players. Since these will obviously be readily available and cheap, Sony can tune the LDoN dungeons on normal with the knowledge that even the most casual player of this level will have gear of at least this level. It sets a "lowest common denominator" for gear.

Any other theories why the recommended levels seem so out of whack?

thRover
I think there are a couple things you need to keep in mind when considering this issue. This is not the first expansion to have "questionable" suggested levels on drops. This is the type of equipment they THINK you should be using at those levels. They know many can and will do better but this is their way of limiting the amount of people who have access to really nice stuff. Remember when they used to put negative stats on stuff? This is the new, and I think, better way of accomplishing their goal. I don't neccessarily aggree with their point of view but they didn't ask me before they made their decision.

Secondly, if you notice, over the course of the last 3 expansions they have increased the amount of "usable" items being dropped and the ease with which you can acquire them. In just one day of soloing in LoY, I can come up with several pieces of equipment, some of which I may actually want to use. This is in response to all the, I think valid, complaints that it is just too hard to earn equipment through honest effort. One of the checks on this is the fact that many ( actually all that I can think of ) are lore, meaning you cannot bring an absurd amount of them into the game, some of which you use, the rest of which you sell in the bazaar to people who may never have been in the zone you got it from. Secondly is the whole recommended level thing. That just makes that equipment appealing to a smaller number of people.

I don't always agree with the decisions they make, but I can at least understand them. There are some items that no one in their right mind would be using at the recommended level, because there are better/easier to acquire items somewhere else in the game. Its a big game and they sometimes overlook stuff like that. Personally I would like it if they would spend more time fixing things like this that are wrong in the game and less time selling us new expansions, but they would not make as much money if they did that. By the way, I love LDoN, I would just like the things I've already payed for to be right before I give them money for something new.
