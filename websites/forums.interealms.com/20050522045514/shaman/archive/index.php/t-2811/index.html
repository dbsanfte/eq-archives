How do deal with a the runner....

1. Get a fungal snare gland thingy. This has saved my life 2x while I was camping him for a few hours.
2. When he gets down to about 10% health, try to keep him permarooted.
3. When he gets down to about 30% health, try to get pox, plague, ebolt, and bane all stacked on him before he runs.
4. Nuke him with your ice nuke when he's low on health.

Basicly, the key is to kill him as fast as you fugging can when he's about to run, and to keep him rooted as best you can while he is running.

Gloobonious
You can use the pet.

You just need a LOT of control over it. IE: Back it off the second Chottal casts on you if he's not in camp. If your pet runs into his room before Chottal reaches you, you're toast from skeleton aggro.

I always use a pet as a DOT. It can do surprisingly high amounts of damage during long fights when hasted. Just make sure it is behind the mob to avoid enrage/riposte damage. It's also good for holding a mob (possibly the emperor for 10-15 seconds) off that you aggroed with Malo, to get it debuffed. (Can taunt an incoming mob off you if you only used Malo/Malosini.)
