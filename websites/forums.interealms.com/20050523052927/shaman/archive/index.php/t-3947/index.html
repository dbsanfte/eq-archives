SoW does not increase your swim speed, that was demonstrated quite conclusively in a series of posts before the wipe. Somebody ran some good experiments with stopwatches. Try it yourself - SoW up, go UNDERWATER, click it off - no noticeable speed change.

However, if you have Levitate on, and if you are on the surface, then you are RUNNING, not swimming, and SoW does have an effect.

Bloodgills in LOIO work like this, too - if you try to outswim them you are out of luck, but if you go to the surface with lev and sow you can outrun them easily.
