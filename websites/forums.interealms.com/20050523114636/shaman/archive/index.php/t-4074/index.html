finaljustice
I don't think you'll be able to Duo Droga at that level. Your most effective Duoing will be with one mob and possibly one addition. Droga will give you LOTS of Zerging and you'll die very quickly if you get 5 or so Gobbo adds. All the gobbos run if they're the last mob you're fighting, and they run along the chain aggro path, so if your root breaks, you'll be in a lot of trouble. Also, Droga starts out as a dropdown, so your monk buddy's a long way from the exit, if he finds things are a little too hot for him.

Kaesora's also got a quick dropoff and so you have the same problem of easy entrance but slow exit. However, as to your particular effectiveness or problems there, I wouldn't know... I only hunted there for my HS key item.
