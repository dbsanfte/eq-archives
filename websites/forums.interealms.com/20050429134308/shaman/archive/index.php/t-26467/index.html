"I heard Auberdine got attacked! I'm going there to fight back the horde!"

"Cool, can I come? I hate the horde"

"Sure, we always need more pallys. They rock - great tanks - and the can heal"

"Ya being a pally ownz."

I couldn't help but just chuckle. So different than PVP servers. Loved the change of pace.
