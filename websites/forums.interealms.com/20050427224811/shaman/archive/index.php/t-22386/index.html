Got a couple of my friends together last night and we were really bored, so we went lookin around for some dragons/big nameds to kill. Klandicar was the only one up. We figured that trying him would be more fun than anything else we could do at the time.

Group makeup:
Me(65 shm, 2 insta clickies)
65 war(ele gear, 9500hp, I'm 2 boxing, 2 insta clickies)
65 Druid, elemental gear, 150ish AAs, no clickie
65 cleric(crappy gear, 19 AAs) no clickies
65 chanter(2 boxed by cleric, 300ish AAs, but cleric has never played him and hates 2 boxing) and no clickies

We started out doing great, we decided to pull kland towards the west shoreline so we wouldn't get any adds. I went around and ended up pulling 2 dragons that may have added during the fight and some of the burrowers nearby.
Now the path to Kland is clear and I pull him towards the shore, pulling with malos, and kite him around till the group is fm. I get him to the group and immediately agro him with the warrior. the chanter tashes and then I start slowing. I got lucky and slowed him on the second attempt, and it was all goin good. about 4 minutes later I started reslowing him, which took me about 5 or 6 tries. The druid emptied his mana bar early in the fight with his nukes, and by this time Kland is about about 55%. The cleric then says he's oom, without any fore-warning, and I start blasting heals away at everyone. By the time I was getting lom, we evacced, and kland was at 40%.

Last night was a fun night, and we plan on going back till we kill him.
