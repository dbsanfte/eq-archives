I'll come down on the other side of the coin.

It isn't worthless, by any means, but it is prohibitively expensive.

Will you make money in bazaar selling potions? No, not likely. Why bother? The potions, expensive as they are, provide some utility that is quite nice.

There are potions to raise resistances, potions to gate people to their bind points, potions to shrink groups of people, potions to allow an MT to spam-heal himself in an emergency, and more. While all of these potions are expensive, they serve a useful purpose and when you are looking at it from a raiding point of view, spending 400p on potions to shrink people is an insignificant expenditure in the grand scheme of things.
