Just one little bit of Advice (assuming you are NOT deleting you 34 Shaman {I sure as heck wouldn't}). Farm silks (get about 100), and sell them in the bazaar for about 5pp each. 500pp will set you up quite nicely in a full suit of Ry'gorr Armor, with some Giant Scalemail Armor thrown in. The only reason I am saying this is because you can easily farm the silks Nekid, and go right to the Ry'gorr stuff.

One of the other poster mentioned something about Farming Ry'gorr, well at 34, it ain't gonna happen!! Do not go there thinking that you'll be able walk out with Ry'gorr Battle Mail. It drops off the chief, who usually comes with 3 guards. They are light blue to me at 57, so I need to assume they would be red to you at 34 (or yellow at the very least). I think the Chief may still be Dark Blue to me (not 100% sure though). You CAN do the entrance guards, and inner guards (prolly). They can drop Ry'gorr Arms, Legs, and Coif, as well as Orc Fang Earrings.
All in all, that will get you started.

dams
once you get some coin saved up I have some recommendations that might make your shopping more efficient in the bazaar. i assume that you might still be under the impression that wisdom is > than all for a shaman. the general thought now is that hps > all. i am a firm believer in this myself. when you get to the bazaar you can filter your gear searches in a number of ways. what i would recommend doing is filtering it like this.

pick and item slot
select hps from the tab below the item slot
select your race
select shaman

then put in a maximum of 50 or 100 plat or what ever you want. then the items that are available to you that add hps will show up. if you search from the giant list it can be overwhelming. you can of course do it anyway you choose but i think you find if you can increase your hps substantial your survivability will increase greatly.
