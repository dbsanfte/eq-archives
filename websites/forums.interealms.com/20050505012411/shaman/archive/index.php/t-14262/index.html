Grippa
If we assume that spell durations are handled by the server in seconds rather than ticks - 20% extra on the base 42 seconds for Bane of Nife gives 50.4 seconds. Round this down to 50 seconds.

Now also assume that the "ticks" all happen at the same time. By this I mean, the first tick of damage of a DoT will not happen 6 seconds after you cast it, it will happen when the server schedules the next tick, which could be anything from 0 to 5.9 seconds after you cast the spell.

So if you cast the spell 1 seconds before the next tick is due, the ticks of damage will happen at these intervals into the duration of the spell: 1, 7, 13, 19, 25, 31, 37, 43, 49 and then the spell will wear off at 50 seconds. That's 9 ticks of damage.

Of course, I'm only guessing it may work this way. If the server keeps track of spell durarions in ticks rather than seconds, you should always get (7 * 1.2) = 8.4 ticks from an extended affliction bane of nife, presumably rounded down to 8.
