Csin Csity
CoM can still be really nice exp.

For Goo killing without clearing the whole damn city Make a pet at the zone-in and park him. Dive into the water at the entrance and swim all the way to
the end of the tunnel on the left.

You pop up INSIDE the goo house. Swim around to the far side of the small 'island' to jump up onto it. Once you're up on top you'll have to root/slow the goo in the middle..then hop across to the door, let yourself out, and go set up camp out on the path outside the door (don't forget to summon doggy!). Once root breaks your goo will take a bit of time to cross the water and come get you..since goos have a notorious problem hopping without any legs..

Kill goo as you normally would. Having an already buffed pet should make this trivial. While I typically encourage melee'ing..these really lend themselves to root/rot/doggie killing for some reason.

The advantage to camping outside is not only can you pull the goos inside the house, but by 54 you should be able to single pull the higher-level goos from above you on the path when they patrol to the lower area. If you get an add, there is plenty of room to split them in the generously sized courtyard there.

If you're feeling adventurous you can run over to the ramps down to the gazebo area. The gazebo mobs should all be blue to you, along with most of the mobs along the way. Keep an eye out for the Army Behemoth for ochre clay for your lower level shaman friends. There are also some good random drops, and pieces for Epic here which made it my favorite spot at these levels.

Overall I spent hours here solo. Cash wasn't half bad, and the ability to go eat, talk to RL people, answer the phone, and basically relax was a welcome change. I kinda miss it.

Oh..and did I mention that in case of emergency the entrance is only a short run away, and that SoW works here? :O)
