I believe it was when they finally changed it. I was level 51 or 52 and still got it even though I would get Canni III at level 54. Here is the basic stats that you get (info derived from EqCaster)

Cannibalize I - 2.5 to 1.78 hp for every mana point
Cannibalize II - 2.23 to 1.86 hp
Cannibalize III - 1.89 to 1.80 hp
Cannibalize IV - 1.83 to 1.80 hp

*first number when you get the spell and 2nd number when you reach level 60.

So judge for yourself it if is worth the hassle.
