Syren
First off, unless the bard is under 49, not likely if torpor is an issue, selos lasts 2.5 minutes so they are not missing out on singing needed songs to keep it up.

Second, it makes pulling much faster, reducing the amount of time your puller is not doing damage or reducing the time the group has nothing to attack. In both cases, increasing damage output.

Now if the song is causing problems with your healing, explain it to the bard. So often minor problems can be solved with a little communication. Not one way communication either. Ask why they need to keep it up, often I am asked to by the puller if I am not pulling. Ask if sow or bih li is enough, usually a bard will enjoy having the extra song slot freed up.

Make a brief speech in group about making sure to click off selos if you are using torp as your main heal. Then if it doesn't hit, it's the targets fault. Or use group sow as someone previously mentioned. Either way, you can have the bard make a quick 'selos' call in group so everyone knows when it is refreshed. Really, at 2.5 minutes there is plenty of time between pulses if sow wears off to just click selos off.

How a group works together is up to the whole group. The people who say 'He shouldn't use X simply because it interferes with my Y' are poor group members. There is usually always a solution that works for everyone.
