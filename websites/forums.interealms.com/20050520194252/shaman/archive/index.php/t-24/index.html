Delirios
I've done most of my AA exp in same place with similar groupage.
Gone to Sebilis, to the hallway that leads down to Disco, and stop in the middle where the shaman frog spawns, just before the triple spawn a little lower. Stay here with a 60 warrior and 60 rogue, and make AA so fast that you would think you'd still be doing lvl 15. Pull everything from zone out to the disco bugs and sebilite golem.
The group setups have changed, somedays I've done it with a monk and a ranger, sometimes 2 rogues etc.. but never take more than 3, because otherwise the pulls can get slim and exp slows down. Best exp was with 60 rog and 60 tank. That was plain amazing. The rogue assasinated every 3rd mob and the tank had another mob ready when other was fleeing.
Maybe 2 lvl 60 rogues would be even better here because I have no problems tanking the slowed froggies, or keeping the aggro with Bane and root.
After I dinged 60 I've done maybe one regular 6 man group. The thing is, I dont need a cleric in most exp camps anymore when I can use torpor. I can haste tanks, slow mobs and CC in sebilis or chardok etc. without any problems, so ench is kinda useless, and torpor keeps me FM all the time anyway so no problem with that.

I've also done a lot of trioing with a Paladin and Ranger friends of mine in chardok, and we actually rock. 2 of us can heal very decently, pally can res and has great buffs. Ranger has track, harmony, snare and limitless supply of very deadly arrows, so what else can you need?
I used to hate paladin class, but 2 close Eq friends of mine have totally changed my mind on this one. They can do amazing things, at higher levels at least. They have become one of my favourite duoing class.
Also I cant understand why people bash rangers so much. My lvl 60 ranger friend controls his aggro amazingly, he never needs heals, he pulls like mad, and I love that he's so independant. He can heal himself, he can invis, buff, sow, levi, you name it. Plus he does tons of damage! They might not be too uber at lower levels, but with a little AA exp at 60, there's no class that I'd like to partner more at outdoor zones. I have been so impressed by the things they can do that the next twink I am going to make is a Halfling ranger, as soon as I get my second Pc up and running.

So my recipe for good AA exping and lots of fun is instead of taking the holy trinity, cleric, chanter tank etc. just grab 2 melees and crawl deep into chardok mines or other dangerous and desserted place and make better exp than any group can do. And that's a proven fact:)

I love being 60 so much. For 2 years I read at these boards about the amazing things an oracle can do, and now that I've reached the level I am still in awe about the power we have at 60. I love being able to duo efficiently with just about any class in the game. I love being able to turn a disaster pull into mass exp blast with my slows and roots and heals. I love seeing people get disappointed when I need to leave the group because I have so much to offer for them. I love to have the best buffs in game, and I love being able to do things that other classes consider suicide or just impossible. I love this class so much that I dont know what else I could play, so maybe I'll just concentrate on getting AA exp for those nifty mass cannibalizations, channeling bonuses, quickbuffs and reinforcements. There's so much to do now at 60...
