SCM is great even if you aren't doing a mana burn as you put it. When you don't it's still very useful because it effectively increases your mana regen since it reduces the mana you use.

Lets play with some fake numbers.
Lets say we've got a shaman with 4000 mana casting a 400 mana spell.
Obviously he can cast it 10 times and then he's compleately oom.
Lets say this shaman regens 80 mana a tick so it takes him 50 ticks to regen that 4000 mana.
Lets now give him SCM. That 400 mana spell now cast 360 mana to cast.
He can now cast it 11 times and still have a little bit of mana left over.
It still only takes him the same 50 ticks to med back up.

Now lets compair that shaman to a shaman that just added 400 more mana.
He now as 4400 mana and can cast the spell 11 times exactly but not it takes them 55 ticks to med up.

So the shaman with scm not only can cast as many spells and even have a little mana left over but he's back to fm sooner.

Same holds true if you just cast spells as soon as you have mana to or just cast when you get near full mana. Since the spell it's self costs you less mana it takes you less time to be ready to cast again.

SCM is a great aa for any caster. I may not do a mana burn and I can't remember the last time I went oom but this still allows me to cast more spells over a given period of time.
