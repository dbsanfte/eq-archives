NO right-click item can be considered a balancing tool PERIOD .... EVER.

A shaman that can take down a big mob without the JBB is real class. A shaman that claim to do it but used a JBB is not.

But it feels great knowing you gave the clerics time to camp out by holding agro long enough and the CR changed from 2 hours to 10 minutes (Thanks to 5 PoZ's being trained into us)

Now what balancing features do you propose ?

1) I would like the epic boots to be permanent run-speed 4 or 5 instead of click-sow. Either that or allow us to cast it on others as well. ( Not likely to happen)

2) Fix the Alchemy prices and effects . I want to have some fun with my own trade skill. (VI are probably just too lazy to fix)

3) VI to test their changes before they implement them. (Even less likely to happen than 1 above)

4) Fix the money drops on mobs. Higher levels should drop more money. Then you wouldn't have mindless money grinding to buy that Torpor or Malo. Instead you could group or solo and gain the rewards. Example is the over camped HG's. Some people say you must try organise those higher levels spells in your early 50's, but some of us don't want to spend 500 hours camping HG's and other non-experience mobs.
