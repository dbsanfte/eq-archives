Goldark
The shaman epic is fairly unusual in that you get 3 items from it, which are progressively better. They are the shield of falsehood (not very good), black fur boots (pretty nice clicky effect) and of course the spear of fate.

The sow effect on boots is in fact instant, you can cast it while running, while attacking, while dying - whenever you like. Its great for a dispell junk slot, and great for a quick escape when sow or scale of wolf drops at a bad time. I keep them handy in a bag - some people even use a main inventory slot to have em ready on a hotkey.

For a walkthrough of the epic see http://www.eqshaman.com/epic/

Harvyst
The boots are called black fur boots, and you can get them, with a group, in your 30's or 40's, depending on the number of people you bring.

They are the result of the Test of Might, and involve killing Black Dire in Mistmore.

They're very nice, I keep them in an inventory slot and click them all the time. It's just so useful. Totally instant cast.

But in order to get your epic weapon, you'll end up with the following things: the epic shield of falsehood (really crappy), the black fur boots, and your epic itself. Only the boots and the epic are worth keeping :)
