Sciek Spellbottler
There is no level based xp penalty. What does exist is an xp bonus for killing mobs near your level (within 5 levels of you) or higher level.

Levelling to 61 before the recent xp change patch used to make a lot of old xp grinding spots go light blue/green due to the decreased blue range, which further diminished the xp you could get for killing the same mobs at 61 vs. 60.

If a mob cons light blue, the xp you get for killing it is reduced from it's "normal" value. Normal in this case being a mob that cons dark blue and is more than 5 levels lower than you. If the mob is dark blue and 5 levels or less below you, then you get a bonus to xp. The closer to your level, the higher the bonus and it continues to increase as mobs become even con and higher.

Every time you level you will get less xp for killing the same mobs as the prior level. If a mob goes from 5 levels below you to 6 levels below you, the drop may be quite noticeable. If the mob goes from a dark blue to light blue, it will most definately be very noticeable.

The idea being that as you level up, you should be fighting harder mobs since you're gaining in power. Think of it as incentive to challenge yourself and not bottom feed for xp.
