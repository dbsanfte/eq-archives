Grixx
Plane of Mischief is still a viable place from 65 to 67...maybe even all the way to 70, especially if you're not flagged for the elemental planes and want a change of scenery over HoH, etc. I've been hunting in the Alice in Wonderland area where all the mobs are lvls 62 to 65. Each mob can drop up to 20pp with occasional gems. I'm getting 1% regular XP every 2 kills in level 66. Sometimes I'll get 2% exp for every 3 kills. Certainly not bad XP. Mobs have low to average resists, root easily, and there's a ton of room (deeper in AiW you go, the more room you have) to maneuver. The zone is empty 90% of the time I go there, and is a safe, easy place to get experience for the casual player. Download a map from http://www.planeofmischief.com and learn your way around the zone (it's tricky the first time - but now I can do it almost blindfolded). I've been at this camp spot for probably 30 AAs and 2 levels. Nothing sees invis except for named mobs.

For the daring shaman, I'd suggest the Theater camp, as 2 named there can spawn within easy reach - An Obsessed Patron and A Method Actor. The camp spot is on the back side of either of the staircases going up to the stage. The named here hit for ~600, are partially slowable, summon (that's the daring part), and have quite a few HP. I can take them down though with reasonable effort. The great thing about doing the named in the theater camp is the experience you get from the named. You see, where most mobs from the theater to Alice in Wonderland will net you around 5% to 7% AA per kill in lvl 65 to 67, all named in the zone will net you 4 to 5 times that amount. The named give me 26% AA (or 3% regular XP) per kill. It's wierd, but it's the truth. The named drop some mid-level gear which probably won't benefit you a lot (it's no drop except for on my server, Firiona Vie, where you can sell it). I prefer the Alice in Wonderland camp, as mobs are a tad higher and give a bit more consistent XP.

Anyway, if you want a quiet, safe place to experience that doesn't require elemental access, this is a good option.

Not a great exp spot, but for the lower end 66+ shammy Drooga can be a decent hunting ground. The key is to grab the task from overthere (I believe several taskmasters give it out) that asks you to kill 10 flame masters (I forget the name of the task, but the opening instructions tell you to go to frontier mountains and tell some NPC next to the BW zoneline that your codename is blue beetle or something). With this taks in hand you can then run around Drooga hunting flame masters for so-so exp (3 or 4 kills per % point IIRC), and then once you've killed 10 you get an additional few % points of exp. You also get an extra 100 plat for completing the task (for me this just covers the cost of buffs) and while you are in Drooga you can stop off at the spawn points of your favorite nameds to see if they are up (and possibly make some decent plat depending on what they drop).
