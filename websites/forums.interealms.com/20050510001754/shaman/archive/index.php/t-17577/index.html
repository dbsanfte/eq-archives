650 mana (pet cost) + 600 mana (fos) + 0 mana (right click ornate BP haste) = 1250 mana
Your pet does 30+ dps for as long as he's alive, and as long as you constantly have a mob in camp (which is quite possible with good pullers in ldon, or in regular xp sessions)
Adventures go from 30-90 minutes in length.

30 minutes * 60 seconds per minute = 1800 seconds
90 minutes * 60 seconds per minute = 5400 seconds

1800 seconds * 30 dps = 54000 damage
5400 seconds * 30 dps = 162000 damage

54000 damage / 1250 mana = 43.2 damage per mana efficiency
162000 damage / 1250 mana = 129.6 damage per mana efficiency

Our level 65 dot in comparison does 320 damage per tick * 7 ticks = 2240 damage.
Burning affliction IV will increase damage output on dots by approximate 10% according to parses done.
2240 * 110% = 2464 damage
Affliction efficiency IV will decrease mana by 25%.
535 mana cost * 75% = 401.25 mana

2464 damage / 401.25 mana = 6.14 damage per mana efficiency

If you can keep your pet in combat for a solid 30 minutes, and estimate a conservative 30 dps for him, your pet will be more than 7 times more mana efficient to cast than dotting things with our level 65 dot.
If you can keep him in combat for the most part of a 90 minute adventure (very doable if you are doing a hard adventure or something) then the pet is approximately 21 times more mana efficient than dotting things.

Explain to me again why you aren't using your best dot you have?
