We went in for both mine and the ranger's epic mobs. Had no problems getting to ranger's mob, and the trip there was a blast. Did his turn in, got a little hairy when we got a few adds and one of our druids accidentally nuked the non aggro version or ranger mob, but we won out with just 1 death.

We continued on to Kirn. This dungeon design is great, as the mobs spawn time keep you moving but don't overwhelm you. We get to Kirn and start clearing out the revenants around him.

Had some fun when what's her name the cold showed up and our necromancer, for some reason only gnomes know, blinded her and she took off running! Luckily our pallies caught up to her and rooted her until blind wore off.

Cleared out room, asked if everyone was ready, and turned in idol. Magician was still sitting so he became the lucky recepient of Kirn's HT. :) After that he went down very easy with no other deaths.

Highly recommend anyone doing the quest to not skip this part. I am in a far from uber guild(just ask anyone on our server) but with some help from some friends, we went in and had a blast. Definitely my high point of EQ to date.
