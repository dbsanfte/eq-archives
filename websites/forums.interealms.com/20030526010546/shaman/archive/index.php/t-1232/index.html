Chutoi
Kafeen, any of your slow spells is a check against magic resistance (MR). The cure disease is ONLY to get rid of the slow (ie a mob slows you). It was implemented because, prior to the change, the only way to remove a slow was to dispell it (along with all of your buffs). Since slow is particularly crippling for melee, and no one could get rid of it, they made it cureable as a disease. Again, that is ONLY the cure. To LAND the slow, it checks against MR, so a disease debuff will not help you land slow in the least.

As to debuffing the mobs, my only comment is this: if you are having trouble landing slow (resisted a lot) you might consider debuffing. Otherwise, I'd conserve mana and accept the occasional resist. I didn't solo much as a young shaman, but when I did, I all but never used debuffers.
