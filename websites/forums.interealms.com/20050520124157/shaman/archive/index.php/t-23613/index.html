While puttering around at work, I came up with a new zone idea. I am guessing it might work best as a instanced zone. SOE seem to really like to go towards the fast kills, and while I'm bored nothing more fun I like to do then go to an old zone and slaughter a bunch of greenies. A whole lot of light blue type mobs, or mobs with low hp's and don't hit insanly too hard, but a lot of them. In order for this to work a few tweaks would have to be made for them such as.
1. Either mobs are unmezable or have a innate -1hp every 6 seconds to break mez.
2. AE spells would have to be tweaked, something similar to our rain spell effecting only 3 mobs or 4 mobs max at a time. Possibly even our tigirs.
3. Harmony type spells would most certainly have to be out of the question.

This would require a completly new strategy.The harder zones could be tweaked a bit more easily, adding a few more hit points and making the mobs a bit more harder makes a big difference when you have 6-8 mobs on you. The mobs obviously wouldn't give as much exp, but you'd be killing alot more mobs so it would come up even.

One problem I would see is lag having 10-15 mobs coming at you would bog down a bit. Perhaps have mobs poof if they don't have anything on them when you kill them.
I'm sure it would need to be tweaked a bit. It would feel nice to rip through mobs sometimes.
