BattleAxe
At the moment there are two different implementations of horse.

If you have models on you get:
1) Horse startup, running, and stopping physics.
2) Innate safe fall.
3) You cannot duck to interrupt spells.
4) Aggroless spell swapping.
5) Continuous mana updates.

With Models off you get:
1) Your character's movement physics.
2) No safe fall.
3) You can duck to interrupt spells.
4) You do not have aggroless spell swapping.
5) Spikey mana updates that can be manually refreshed by casting a spell.

Both versions include meditation and a huge increase in the amount of food and water consumed. Neither horse cancels any other movement buff, but both conflict with illusion buffs.

Neither has been determined to be the true horse by SOE. Neither has been determined to be an exploit by SOE. They are just two different options available to players, like whether you turn your clipping plane down in laggy zones or not.

I can't believe there are posts attempting to measure Sony's intent and therefore judging people as exploiting or being 'honest' based on the models setting they choose. The fact that there are two implementations of horse doesn't make using one 'right' and the other 'wrong'. That's just nonsense.
