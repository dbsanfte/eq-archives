Mobs that hit for a reasonable amount for the intended level, don't have crazy procs or AC, that at the very least drop money. Save the overinflated crap for the raid zone(s) and nameds in higher-end zones. Make it so that a group can exp in one zone while another group raids in the same zone again (Remember someone camping Crypt, another doing the Jail, while a third group cleared out the shrooms to help make way for a Trak raid?)

But, since I doubt OoW will have 1 - 70 content, a better model would be Velious. Tough stuff, but still reasonable, with some juicy quests even if you don't raid, and plenty of mouth-watering stuff for the raiders, and some nice stuff for singe-groupers and small guilds as well (One big guild does NToV, another guild is doing HoT, and the single group is killing off WW dragons)

Some very basic things OoW will need...

1) Good solid quests. GoD has exactly one quest, BiC. There's a few smaller ones, yes, but tell me, does anyone wear their hynidskin boots? Velious and Kunark both had tons of productive quests. Even Luclin and LoY do!

2) Sane mobs. I don't want to take my group into a zone and need a Shaman, two clerics, a defensive tank, and a DC'er just to do single pulls, Sony. Nor do I want a mob to last so long that I start wondering if my account will expire before its dead.

3) Loot all around. Even if it's just cash drops or quest / tradeskill items. If no loot, at least give us some insane exp to make up for it (Paludal Caverns and PoP, for instance, little loot, great exp) Have some random gear drops (ala Ry'Gorr or Obulus Death Shroud) in addition to named "real gear" mobs.

4) Make raid content that gives everyone on the raid something to do, instead of just the guild's MT and the Cleric chain. Spawned adds are a good touch. Wierd spell effects (Such as that procced charm that gives itself as a proc to whoever it charms...) are good, too. Perhaps interactive quests, where one part of the raid keeps the main boss busy while another part goes around the zone and destroys mobs that are giving it power.

5) Give us some AA variety, and give them costs that make sense. i'm not going to spend 20 AA just so I can have a window where my pet can catch BSS, got me?
