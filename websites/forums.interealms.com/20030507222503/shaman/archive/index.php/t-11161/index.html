Definately lose the Plat/Jasper ring. Even a Velium fire wedding ring would be an upgrade. The obvious ring for that slot is, IMO, a Shadel Bandit Ring, unless you are very rich and can afford the Valorium ring of Gallantry

The Jarcrap wristguard has to go. The obvious thing to do would be to complete the Coldain Military Bracer Quest - you can solo it easy at 54, but if you don't have Kromriff faction, you need to make a RZ alt..there are detailsed walkthroughs for this quest elsewhere in these forums. The Coldain bracer has better stats and AC thean the Jarjar, and also has Extended Affliction III which gives extra duration to DoTs, Roots, Debuffs, Cripples, etc

Ok, here are a few possible upgrades for other slots:

Ears: Earring of Cleansing, Earring of Purity, Earring of Twisted
Leaves, or if you are rich, Earring of Solstice

Face: Golem Jawbone, Deathmask of the Elysians

Arms: Barbed Ringmail sleeves

Soulders: Tattered Mantle, Elder Wolfhide, Imbued Granite Spaulders, Obulus Death Shroud

Back: Cloak of Truth, Lodizial Shell Shield

Waist: Chitinous Belt, Girdle of Mithaniel, Silvery Belt of Contention

Legs: Kromriff Military leggings, Jarsarth leggings

Primary: Rune Etched Icewurm Fang, Howling Harpooon

to be honest, you would be well advised to concentrate on on camping for Velious Quest armor: If you are on Dragon / Dwarf faction, camp KD arena (you can do that with a single grp these days) for the Rune Crafter's armor quests - Rune Crafters would be an improvement in any slot. If your guild is good enough, doing HoT in ToV will get you Wolf Callers quest armor, which is as good as you are gonna get until your guild can cap Derek the Vindicator
