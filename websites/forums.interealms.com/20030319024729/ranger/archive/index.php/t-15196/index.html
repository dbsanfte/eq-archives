Eorlin
I personally stayed in Iceclad long enough to get two Cougar Claw Earrings (*shudder*) and the Cougarskin mask. I suggest that you at least go to Everfrost and kill mammoths until you get 4 pieces of meat (they will con green to you), then stop by Iceclad and find a pair of HQ Cougarskins. Sometimes merchants sell them, so check the gnomes at their igloos. Then run to the island with the Tower of Frozen Shadow (keep camo up) and track down "Ergrez Shortpaw." Give him 1 HQ skin and 2 (unstacked) mammoth meat. He will give you a mask that is pretty darn close to the Sarnak Hide Mask from KC. And don't panic if he gives you no message like "Here is your mask." He did give it to you.
If you already have better masks, don't bother, but otherwise the mask is a great piece of equipment for the risk and time involved for any fear-kiting Ranger.

[EDIT:]
I just noticed that, for some reason, merchants in freeport sell the HQ Cougarskins. You might find it easier to grab a pair of them from about anywhere; bear that in mind if you want to run in and out of Iceclad instead of spending any time there.
