Ughbash,

In your case I would recommend Level him up first. The aa's are very important, however by leveling him up you will gain free Hp's and mana or at least I did. Then you can also use the runes when they drop.

Being that my main is a 70 shaman Time/Kt geared with just over 300 aa's I know that the aa's are important, but you cant get groups without the spells. Yes you will be a Tissue Paper shaman for a while because he is not your main, but you will have a chance to change that with levels.

If you plan on swapping him out for your main at that point is when I would focus on aa's.

Here is the priority I would place on aa's for a raiding shaman.

Canni 5 ( your not a real shaman until you **** a clerics pants)
MGB (well duh)
Spell casting reinforcement Mastery (need SCR 3 for this)
Radient cure 3 ( get 1 at least to start then work up)
Natural durability ( 10% extra hps does help)
Spell casting sublety 3 (especially if you plan on using the GoD Slow)

At this point you can pick two routes Healing or defensive. I chose healing AA first, then defensive because I like to be able to keep a tank up when the cleric dies. Healing would be a good route for you if you duo a lot, just keep in mind you will be a paper shaman for a while longer.

Healing Route:
Healing adept 3
Healing Gift 3
Advance Healing adept 3
Advance Healing Gift 3
Healing Boon 3 ( if you heal much you will love this aa. I can heal for an MPG group without much trouble by useing trushar and blasting when necessary)


Defensive Route:
Combat Agility 3
Combat Stability 3
Lightning Reflexes 5
Innate Defense 5

From there its pretty much open to how you like to play your class. I like to assist healing a lot so i spend a lot aa's on the healing. If you are soloing a lot spend time on getting a piece of gear that will make your dots last longer, it will be a huger mana saver. With the new augs out it shouldn't be a huge problem.

There are very few AAs worth giving up levels for. Obviously Canni 5 is one of them. For me the only other AAs worth levels are the extra spell gem (which you have), the AA Rez (which you can not get until 70 anyways) and Virulent Paralysis (especially for soloing and duoing).

You will get more HPs from leveling then ND3 gives you. You will take less damage if you level then if you get CA3 and CS3 (Going by what other classes experience - I have not tested this myself). You will have more mana from leveling to your new cannibalize then what SCM saves you. And son on . . .

Basically leveling to 70 gives you ALL of your list and much more on top of that. Those 5 levels are worth at least 200 AAs and that is only if you regard the parts you can duplicate with AAs. AAs can not for example give you less resists on your spells or a +damage modifier for your monk.
