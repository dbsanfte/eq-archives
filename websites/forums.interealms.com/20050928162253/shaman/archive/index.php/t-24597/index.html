All I can say is WOW!

World of Warcraft 3 for the PC is AWESOME. For those who haven't played, the campaign game involves a lot of history and the coming of the Chaos (Shades of Warhammer 40k) forces.

You start off watching an Orc (Thrall) lead his forces to and thru the Human lands. One episode only.

Then you switch to the Human side as a Paladin named Arthas.

After that (10 scenarios) you switch to the Undead (8 scenarios).

Next up is the Orcs (Thrall again), for I don't know how many scenarios.

Fun, fun, fun.
