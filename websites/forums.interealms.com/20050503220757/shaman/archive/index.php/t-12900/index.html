Invis vs animals is enough for Erud's crossing, as Shamu said, keep it up the entire time otherwise you run the risk of getting eaten.

Both the paladin/SK in Rathemnts are kiteable with dogdog, load up with resist magic gear for the SK as getting snared is bad =)

The paladin just takes ages to kill due to the amount of healing he can put out.

Turn /pet taunt off, keep agro, and keep running and be efficient as you can against the paladin and you should be able to kill them both np (I did them at 39/40ish).
