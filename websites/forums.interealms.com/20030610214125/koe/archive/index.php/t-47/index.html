Top 10 Signs You Might Be Addicted to EverQuest

10. Computer monitor and keyboard relocated to next to bathroom commode.

9. You start to closely examine the jewelry of people you encounter on the street to see what kind of attribute benefits they are getting.

8. Your co-workers lovingly start referring to you as "Ferris Beuhler" when you are absent yet again.

7. You find yourself faced with the dilemma of either buying yourself that new laptop to play EQ on while away from home, or replacing your 15 year old son's two front teeth that he broke off while sliding into home plate - and eventually coming to the noble decision that a little humility and affliction can only serve to help mold your son into a stronger, better human being.

6. At any given time there are more than 6 used food dishes/containers lying around your computer station.

5. Your mother informs you that she is dying from a horrible, degenerative disease, and your first thought is, "No worries - my buddy is a 49th level Cleric..."

4. The EQ chat server goes down for an expected six hours, and you can't think of a single thing to do to pass the time other than try to connect to the chat server every 5 minutes.

3. You find yourself nervously humming the EQ theme song over and over whenever you are forced to engage in Real Life activities away from your computer.

2. You notice the striking resemblance between Barshemgud (your 37th level Troll Shadow Knight) and Barbara, your 58 year old mother-in-law.
