it's a 6 second cast, duration is 30 seconds. It's good enough to snare if you don't have anyone else around to do it, but it does allow mobs to move when they enter the fastest "run mode". I also used it early on mobs just to strip sow, even if it would wear off before they were anywhere near death. Resists were never too bad with it, but give yourself time for an extra cast just in case it's needed.
