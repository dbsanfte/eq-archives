Horomen
Gnome Quest-

I dont have the piece names memorized but ive done this quest 7-8 times between now and beta so I know the locations all pretty well.

Piece 1: Look on the map for a big tree near the undead fighters/orc cave area. Its near the base of the tree between some roots on the side near the cave.Looks like a big gear...double click on it to collect piece #1
*NOTE* Named mobs spawn at the top of the tree, so if youre feeling froggy hop on up with a couple of friends for some loot.

Piece 2: Look on the map for the two bridges over the river. Underneath the south bridge just above the waterfall is the second place to click and collect a piece.

Piece 3: From the island the gnome is on, look straight out towards the ocean...swim straight out towards a couple of islands (that you will eventually find out you cannot reach since theyre just out of bounds). It's underwater and looks just like the place you collected the item from #2.

======================================================

Underneath the above mentioned waterfall (in the lagoon) you will find some ruins....there is a statue lying in the sand. If you right click on it you will have to option to accept a quest. Accept the quest, kill 10 razortooth sharks, and you get a waist slot item for your troubles. When you accept the quest you are given EB so it isnt too bad.

While you are here, keep an eye out for Bladefin. Named shark, 1 arrow up even/blue to level 6. Ive soloed him with every class except mage (havent tried), but due to adds youll need to try hauling ass to the beach and fight him there unless you have a lot of space around you in the water. Drops some random items and can also drop another piece that starts a collection quest- this quest yields a 2int bracer I believe.
======================================================

At the big goblin camp, named goblins drop 6 slot bags, and rarely drop "Goblin Attack Plans" or something similar. These start a quest to kill goblin saboteurs, which are near the stairway at the lagoon (stairway leads to the skeleton/cave area). Finishing this quest yields a head slot item.

Monjay-Tunare
Spells are not automatically added to your hotkey bar. They appear in your book for you to place yourself.

And heres my noob hint for being a healer in a group:

* keep the main tank targetted the entire fight, do not switch at all. If you cast a heal, it will land on the tank (obviously) and if you cast any detrimental spell it will automatically land on the mob the tank has targetted. This way you can contribute dots/dd and even melee without switching your target back and fourth between the mobs and the tank for heals.
