LaughingWoman
As far as cripple goes, cripple critters that proc. ESPECIALLY cripple critters with lifetap procs. I'm remembering partnering the vamps in ME a while ago with a warrior. Since I can't dot them, and since warr DPS isn't what it could be, the fights were pretty long. Add in their life sucking proc, and it was positively hellish. Start crippling those critters and the difference is very very noticeable.

Outside of that situation, I rarely crippled unless it was some kind of miniboss - say, named pops around the temple in the grey. Makes a lot more of a difference against higher level mobs where you're less likely to dodge/mitigate.

Against regular exp mobs, seems to be a huge waste of mana.
