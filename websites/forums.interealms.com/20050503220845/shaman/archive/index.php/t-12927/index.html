Turley
There have been weird bugs concerning specialization usually involving people who didn't train theirs right away. But in your case (from what I gathered from other posts on the subject) it may be that 2 specializations reached 50 before one or the other reached 51. My suggestion would be either have a GM help you or do the quest to get them back to 49. Then cast what ever one you want to be the highest until you get to at least 51 in it.

Another thing I've heard is that you have to train in all the specilations to get one over 50. Have you put one point into each?

Most times a GM will fix you. Many people have had problems with this. Hope this helps.
finaljustice
As I recall, you can't get your spec over 50 until you train it from 50 to 51 at the trainers. That way, you don't accidentally cast your way to the wrong specialization (after all, not all casters want their spec to be their most cast class, they might want it to be their highest mana cost spells).
