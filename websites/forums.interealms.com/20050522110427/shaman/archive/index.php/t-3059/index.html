OK, rather than give you a biased account, cough cough
I am 51 shaman, my wife is 51 enchanter.

The advantage to the shaman would be healing abilities as well as general utility.
IMO shaman and enchanter are very similar classes to play, in so far as you support others doing damage, you have to be aware of adds, and you keep certain buffs on your party mates.

The problem i think you would run into is that if you are duoing, you need healing, so the clear advantage is for shaman there.
If you 2 are finding groups... shrug, if you pick chanter, you always need a healer in your group, so that is one spot that will always be needed. If you were shaman, you fill that role, and just add 4 melees, or whatever.

The advantage of the chanter is that more is possible. Adds are controlled without ghetto mez (root) and group effieciency is more helped by a chanter than by a shaman.

So, in summary, IF duo, shaman. If grouping, shaman still more usefull than chanter, but only slightly
