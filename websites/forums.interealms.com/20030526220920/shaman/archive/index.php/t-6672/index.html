Zyphyr
There is a vendor in the basement of the Cabilis Shaman guild who sells all of the Poison/Disease spells.

To get to him : walk into the flashing walls on the main floor. You will be transported to the roof. Turn left, go into the tower, go all the way down. Wander around until you find him. You will also find a vendor with all of the Pet spells inthe same room.

Nytmare
It's confusing for young Iksar Shamans because Vessel Kirtokk on the main floor sells most of the poison/disease spells, but not all. Grazik Plaguebringer in the basement sells all of them.

Also, this same Vessel Kirtokk sells most of the VISION spells, but not all. Gadallion in the Oasis gypsy camp sells all of them.
