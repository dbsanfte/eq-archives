Well, I have a 51 shammy and an almost 45 ranger. I solo with shammy and group my ranger with my 52 shamaness wife.

We haven't really experiments though I have duoed on the shammy with SK's and Monkeys. Dps if buffs and mobs slowed are good but I love the Ranger/Shammy duo my wife and I have. Monks can put out some High dps but they lack one thing that kills shammies. Taunt.

With my ranger I do some good dps damage.
With my ranger and slowed mobs I can tank very well.
With my ranger, slowed mobs and buffs I am dangerous.
With my ranger, slowed mobs, shammy buffs and haste I am a flippin frenzied weedwhacking fool on the edge of insanity.

It's awesome how a ranger with good toe to toe dps to begin with gets paired with the best post 50 solo class mix will with some buffs and debuffs and we solo a ton of stuff.

I mean at 44 I am able to somewhat tank ME gorangas. DL is a joke honestly. SLow lands and buffed and hasted and I take any drachnid giant or drolvarg apart in under a min (never really timed it).

So it's good exp plus rangers can track to named mobs for cash purposes.
Spiritblood
I have levelled up a halfling (!) ranger with my 60 shaman. This is an excellent duo. Anything that can be slowed can be tanked by a ranger. I duo a lot in Kael. So far the toughest giant killed was Korakaz and that was trivial after slow.

Also started killing Xin Thall Centiens in ME when ranger was 54. Could get close with the level 58 ones but now its very doable.

Snare, tracking and some decent buffs hps/ac/ds is a very nice addition to what a shaman can do. Only problem is that we both use chain armor LOL. Got lots of plate armor in Kael that we cant use :(

Shaman + ranger = fun

PS. Ranger is 56 now and using a black acrylia halberd (until he can get the first epic probably). That seems like a very fine weapon but rangers have lots and lots of weapons to choose from. And you can do the Bow of the Huntsman quest in Kael if you need a pretty decent bow.
