I'll jump on the bandwagon and say that my favorite duo partner at the high end is an SK (I felt that way before monk nerf). Not particularly efficient at the low end, the duo really starts to get good at 50+ and peaks at 65. Sks (with an insta-click circlet of shadows) are amazing puller at the high end. With a bit of room to maneuver, I've seen a buddy of mine pull things with his SK that I could never do with my monk. He'll invis down to a named spawn, agro then insta-invis, bring back a few mobs, snare the one he wants to park, feign, and I'll peel while the others wander back. No way I can pick a particular mob to pull like that with my monk. Combine that with a plate tank, snare, FD, fear and summon corpse and it's a winning combo imo. The only downside is relatively low DPS but I love the sheer flexibility of the duo.
