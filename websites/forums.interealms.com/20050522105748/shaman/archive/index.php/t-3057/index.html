If you don't have 2 clerics in VP:

Leave real death corpses outside VP (preferrably in the same spot in a city for everyone), then when ready to leave (must be under 3 hours total time in VP) get rez'd out. If you plan on staying longer than 3 hours you'll need to refresh the corpse before 3 hours by making a new corpse.

If you have 2 clerics in VP:

Bind in same location, when ready to leave, one at a time: Take a real death in VP, take a real death at bind point, get rez'd into VP, loot gear, get rez'd out of VP. Clerics need to work together to make sure they get rez'd in and out in proper order.
