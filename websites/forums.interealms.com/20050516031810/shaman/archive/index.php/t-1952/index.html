Grippa
"A good group of 50+" can mean different things to different people.

If you and your friends have similar equipment to the people I hang out with (eg kunark armour, not many epics, no Kael / Skyshrine / ToV gear at all), then a single group is going to have problems doing the next stages of your epic.

The Hole you can probably do with 2 groups. Same for Lord Ghiosk. Neh Ashir takes quite a raid to get to, 3 or 4 groups I'd guess. Rak Ashir takes 2 mages and a cleric, but a minimum of 2 groups to actually get to him and kill the reavers in the way.

On the other hand, I've heard about all these encounters being "possible" for a single goup. But probably a single goup of level 58+ people in ToV armour.

Also, players with exceptional skill can obviously do things with less forces. I don't count myself in this category :)
