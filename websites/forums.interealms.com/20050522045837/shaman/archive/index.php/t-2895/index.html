Decide you want to get your faction one way and do it. With consistent effort, it doesn't take that long.

When I joined CE, I was +dwarf +dragon. After a couple months of just raids (no independent work on faction), I had it reversed completely. Riff took the longest to fix, but once fixed it doesn't move too much, even though we do the occasional Vindi raid. Zek would have taken alot less time, but I wasn't concentrating on fixing it, and I lost out on alot of zek hits in ToV.

To fix Tmax, one or two dain raids, combined with maybe a couple hours of royal guards in Icewell, and you should turn it.

As a sidebar, my warrior friend who was +kael reversed his faction enough in about 10 days to get a shroud of the dar brood. Instead of hunting like he normally did, he concentrated on faction. Didn't take much time at all.
