Shazzog
I know it's not the popular choice, and it probably isn't even the best choice, but I am dual boxing a cleric with my shaman and I love it.

Granted, neither of us are Uber DPS, but our buff combos rock, we can both gate (and in a week or so, the cleric will be over 50 so our spirit\faithstones will take us to the same place), and on the rare event when one of us is going down, I get the cleric out first...then rez my shammy as needed (very rare. My Shaman can channel a gate through almost anything). Plus, my Cleric can, with KEI, toss off 500pt nukes damn near all day. (He rarely has to heal mid combat, because the Shaman has slowed the mob and tossed regrowth on everyone)

One of the things I love most is being able to help out random players. It's always a blast to get a rez request on the cleric, then show up with both for after-buffs. "There. That'll keep ya from dying as quickly next time.)

The cleric can pull with Pacify just fine, root and atone adds if needed, and has slightly better AC and Attack than my Shaman does, when they are both buffed up.

Now, as I said, I know the cleric probably isn't the best choice...but I am enjoying him and he works just fine. He started as a Utility Char, who was just supposed to level to 50, get his Epic, then get filed away till I needed him. Now, I am already feeling bummed that I am going to have to shelve him long enough to bring a mage up on that account.

That's they key...play what you want to play on your second box...any class can work with a shaman. I think a pure caster will probably be tougher until you can get it to a level that it can group with your shaman and get XP, but it is still doable. (I'll find out soon)
