What's DKP?

Dragon Kill Points....

its a system by which you gain a certain amount of credit/point at each encounter you attend......each item has a value in points and you make a bid along with anyone else in your guild for that item.

Most work on the fact that who ever has more DKP's at the given time of the bid...wins the item. Although some may use seniority/attendance in the decision as well.

Its a pretty fair system in most cases actually.
