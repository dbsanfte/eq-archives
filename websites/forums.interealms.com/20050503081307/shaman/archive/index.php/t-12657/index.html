Last night, I was partnering with a mage in PoS. It's the 61 stunning pet so it tanked at all times. The scorpions, snakes, and cactii we were killing all have some variant of poison, which was of course hitting the mage pet. At the end of the fight, if the pet had gotten poisoned, he'd attack me. If he was still dotted after the fight, he'd re-aggro on me every tick, as the poison damaged him again.

We ruled out everything through experimentation down to:
* pet must get poisoned at some point
* I was dotting every mob with at least bane (didn't try without this, as it takes too long to kill em otherwise)
* I healed the pet at some point, same results no matter which of the 3 heals I used

and bugreported it.

Pet doesn't hit for that much (quads, though!), so it wasn't a worrisome thing, but it was VERY VERY funny. Most we've laughed in EQ in a long time (/gu Geker WTS 2 stacks of bear meat). Just curious as to whether anybody else has done much testing with this?
