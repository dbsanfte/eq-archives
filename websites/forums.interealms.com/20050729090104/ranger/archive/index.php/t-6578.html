Open your eqcclient.ini file and do the following...

Locate the line that says 'Showinspectmessage=TRUE'

Delete the part that says 'TRUE" and in its place, type 'False'

Close the file, click 'Yes' when asked if you would like to save the changes you made.




Problem Solved.

Enjoy your new-found peace of mind.
