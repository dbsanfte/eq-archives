Since Strength increases ATK, it affects chance to hit, not just damage done. (This one conflicts with your recent Dex/Str post, but Str DOES increase ATK. Is ATK only related to damage?)

No. ATK and AC, despite the fact that they are represented by one number, have several different factors that make them up.

AC, for instance, is the combination of two factors:

* Damage avoidance
* Damage mitigation

ATK is comprised of two different factors:

* Chance to hit
* Damage potential

Some of the beta people will remember when AC and ATK were presented with the two factors listed seperatly. This was confusing to a lot of people, so the two seperate factors for each rating were combined into a ratio versus 1000.

So, in other words, just because more STR increases your ATK, it doesn't mean that your chance-to-hit is also increased.

Since Dexterity was a Primary Stat for Monks, I think we should at least know how it affects us.
