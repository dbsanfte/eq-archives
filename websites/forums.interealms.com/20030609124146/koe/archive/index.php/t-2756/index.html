**Sorry, very short notice, but just got back from out of town. If we can make this work, great, if not, I'll post one for next week shortly as well**

This raid will be limited to Four Groups. Though the level limit is set at 55 and up, Level 54 healers are also encouraged to signup. We will rotate people in on a standby list as people leave. The raid will likely go until after midnight, but we will keep going as long as we have a capable force.

Requirements: Attendee's are encoraged to bring some DoTs for Symbol/Rune. Able to commit to at least 3 hours.

Resists: CR/FR near 100 unbuffed should be your target. MR is a secondary resist to look at as well.

Objectives: The main goal here is to move to the Halls of Testing and pull MoBs for challenge and loot.

If you cannot make the start time, please sign up anyway, but include the time you will be able to get on. We will rotate people in and keep the raid going as long as there remains a viable force present.
