My mouse used to stop moving and then I'd have to reboot the computer.

Problem was my cord had been pulled on one too many times and it began to come loose, which caused the red laser light to flash for just a second, although it was enough to cause me to have to reboot every time it happened.

Solution: I took the mouse apart and played with the wires until it looked like I had a stable connection, then taped the wires in place and put the mouse back together.

Caution: Intelli-Mice are HARD to take apart. I pried at the thing with a couple of screwdrivers for about an hour and a half before I managed to take the top off. Now my mouse has a few scratches and dents, but hey, it works. Better than having to buy a new one.
