Talamay Crystalspirit
I must say, I'm really impressed.

Last night I participated in an LDoN adventure for the first time. I can't even imagine what the game would have been like if that were how it began. Can you? Think about it...

- No need for lists. People could just grab a group and go exp. Over and over and over.

- No camping for items. I haven't done enough adventuring in LDoN to buy anything, but it certainly doesn't seem like the sit in one spot for 8 hours or more waiting for a named to spawn with the rare drop you need... a la Paw of Opolla quest.

Thats just two possibilities. Would there have been a raid element to the game? Perhaps, I hear there's multi-group adventures. I certainly don't have the time to do any of that, but it probably would have leveled the playing field for "ueber" guilds that are competing for spawns.

It's good stuff at any rate. Fortunately for me, I have a life that I rather enjoy, so I won't play much, but also just as fortunate I can play a couple hours at a time and not invest my life to the game.

Two thumbs up.
