Faern
I wish Zorlac were here. He'd have a field day with this.

Here's ol Faern's interpretation of what happened:

A long time ago, in an expansion far, far away, casters of all types were able to defeat the greying out of spell gems (designed to prevent chain casting) by using an 'insta-cast' item (think J Boots, Gobbo earring).

In response to this bit of ingenuity on the part of the player base, the dev's and programmers coded in something which did not allow for such action.

I'm willing to be that something coded in the past 2-3 months or a problem with client - server synch has revived this situation like The Mummy Returns .

I do think there is some credence to the fast casting spells (think Tash a 1.0 second casting spell) playing a part in it. I get it when trying to cast slow and tash or tash and mez back to back.
