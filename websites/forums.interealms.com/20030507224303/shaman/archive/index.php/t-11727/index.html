ZI/ZO Tunnels in Charok got me through 56-58 fast, plus I would always walk away with a decent amount of cash, FS from the Chokidai and lots of drops for my Bazaar mule.

You can single pull these tunnels at 56. Howling Spirit had no problems tanking these. Pull with Turgurs, cast BoN and EBolt while the mob is in your face, root and then back up to Canni. Pet can take care of the rest. IIRC, mobs will not run if you fight near Herald Telcha.

Unless there is a raid on the Royals, chances are you'll have the whole zone to yourself.
