Ungkor
Think its your imagination.

And on another note, casting your pet buff, then alacrity is not the best buff sequence for your pet. They appear to stack because the pet buff has str/dex etc.. on it. Much in the same way that our haste and enc haste can both be on a player. However, only the more powerful haste component sticks (alacrity) in your case.

Cast alacrity on your pet, and then buff him like you would a player (minus agility and stamina). If he's not going to be tanking anything, put IoS and your dexterity buff on him, as well as your strength buff. Maximizing his dexterity will help with his procs alot. If he's going to take some hits, use a hp buff like HoS.
