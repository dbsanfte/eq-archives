The advantage to doing AA exp before level 60 is that you don't have to fight level 45+ mobs in order to find blues. 1/4 of the mobs in uper sebilis seem to be under level 45 depending on what camp you are at. The risk becomes higher, as you have to fight and FIND higher level mobs, and you loose out on some great solo spots like city of mist and CS wyverns.

I still think that you should wait until 60 to get AA exp, but I think it would be POSSIBLE to earn aa points at a faster rate at lower levels.
