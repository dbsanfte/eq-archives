Hi Hester, as a lvl 60 you can accept PoS/PoV group requests, but not BoT requests unless you get flagged for BoT.

To get to Valor, go into the tunnel with the port stones at the end and click the second from the right.
To get to Storms, go through the other tunnel, past the waterfall & click on the wierd watery monument on the first istland you get to.
I'm guessing you know how to get to tier 1 planes, so I wont patronise you further.

Oh, and Crypt of Decay is the tunnel just past the Plane of Disease pipe & click the orb at the bottom.

Hope this helps
