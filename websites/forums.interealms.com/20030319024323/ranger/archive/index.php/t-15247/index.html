I'd use Strength of Nature in both cases. Once you get SoN, you shouldn't use NP until you get to 60, and WP.

Other than that it looks about right - CoE for tanking, FoN for damage dealing is the main difference.

I'd also use Call of Sky in both cases - you lose nothing to keep this up if you're tanking, and you gain agro. Indeed, if not tanking, if you get agro often, you may find it helps to drop this, maybe replace it with Jolting Blades, depending on how good the tank is at keeping agro.
