There is some parsing going on now by (mostly) the SK's to determine what causes aggro and how it actually works. Dots give aggro equal to the damage caused over the time it is in effect, but give it all at once.

That means that managing aggro for a Shaman is not casting a lot of dots all at once if you don't want the mobs attention. Let the MT pick up some more aggro after you throw on a dot or 3.
