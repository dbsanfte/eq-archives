Info from EQ Druid posts -

My guild last night went into the new PoM with about three groups. Was fairly eay going with some ok loot drops. We eventually happened upon a red con (at 65) mob that hit for about 1200. We called in a few reinforcements and with 5 groups proceeded to drop The Mischevious Jester. To our dismay this mob dropped absolutely no loot. After a check of track thought it as discovered that Bristlebane the King og Thieves had been spawned.

A few stats on BB:
Hits for 1800 max non defensive, about 1k defensived (dont remember exact, am not a tank)
Slowable, our bard was pulling with slow
Nasty, nasty, ae, illusion halfling + 40% slow to all spells.

We attempted him twice before calling it a night, neither time did he drop under 90%, he has a ton of hps and seems to have a very big regen.

The skunks in the wooded area are charmable with Command of Tunare, I did not try a lower level charm. unnamed mobs in the wooded area don't summon but I did come across a named bixie that did, unfortunately charm broke at about 10% and I got owned. my experiences so far...and what i have read on other threads

similar to a tier 1 plane, forest mobs seem to have a small aggro radius, harmony works well. snare root dot summon your horse...typical.

i would say off hand you can prob quad kite there assuming you have enough mana.


there is a portal out that is invis in the middle of the forest..good luck finding it.


to get up on the rampart there are 2 invissed bridges one on each side of the visible ramp. the second arch to the right and left of the visible ramp indicate the location of these bridges. No mobs, that i know of yet, see invis either in the forest or on the ramparts or down the first hallway of the castle. from what i have read, including some new quests, there are only a few mobs that will see invis. Exodus is our friend....that and innate camo.

the guards (dastardly rascals?) on the ramparts are an easy kill..just root snare dot away.. they will break root more often then the forest mobs (btw i have the first root AA so that may help me some) I also used winter's storm on them and no resists. they have slightly more hp's than the forest mobs.

there is a quest npc along the glass fence that wants 4 shroom spores....there is a quest npc in the chessboard room that gives a quest in which you may very well need a raid force..but who knows a good lvl 65 group might be able to do it no prob.

the named bixie drops items i got some range bauble that a small group left rotting. i think the stinger may be the rare.

i only had time to kill a few beasties, the forest mobs that i did kill, about 8 of them, dropped only plat. of the 2 guards i killed, one dropped a yaeths pg 80 left and plat of course.

there is a good map of the castle that i think has been updated but i dont know for sure over on eqmap.

the castle is huge so i imagine it can handle a number of raids going on simultaneously and i imagine there will be a number of guilds headed there in the next few days.
