Ummm... as far as CT goes I think your problem will be the warrior only being 53 and the chanter only 55. A high level slower make things alot easier in there, and a Def disc on the named mobs is really nice too. If you can get mostly singles then you would probably be ok... but if you get adds or a lot of resists on the slows... yikes! I would suggest trying the maze area, nothing spawns where you port in at... and nothing close sees thru invis. If things go bad, just get a port back to CT, rez, and try again. Also judging from your gear, I think you might be better suited to tank. You might consider getting a kei for the cleric before you start and a BoA/focus for the MT.

PoP zones? Well that all depends on your cleric... things hit hard and fast. Once they get used to the pace of healing you will be fine... if not, hehe, at least there are graveyards ;)
