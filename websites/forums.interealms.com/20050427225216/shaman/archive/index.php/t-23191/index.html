I've done this a couple of different ways. With a rogue for the hand in it takes about 25 mins, with a suicidal monk it takes about 35, with you as the trigger it will take a while longer.

You will fight your way to the end, I wouldn't bother with a COH mage to be honest, it's just someone sitting there who could be helping out. Just invis/fight your way to the end room and skip as much as you can. When you complete the hand in 90 percent of the dungeon will de-pop. At this point you can either invis run back to the first room, or you can have 5 people quit the expedition and wait 1 minute to be kicked, then /dzadd them back in, wait for them to zone back in, leave yourself, get the /dzadd and rejoin them. I would prefer that option to having one person sit at ZI for the duration, but whatever works for you. (It means you don't need a COH to do it at least......).

Ok, so you have the dungeon de-popped... simply kill everything in the entrance room until you get the emote. Then invis up, run to the room on the right and do the same. Then run back and along to the third room and repeat until the final emote. Logistically speaking I would do it this way because this room is the closest to the end.

Invis/Run all the way back to the end room again and do the hail/handin.

The MOST important thing to do here is give strict instructions to your group before you enter. This instruction will make your life a whole lot easier:

"NO ONE IS TO LOOT ANYTHING IN THIS DUNGEON, AT ALL."

Trust me, looting corpses is bad, just in this expedition.

Hope this helps, good luck.

WOOT! Finally beat this dang trial! After wiping 3 times at the hallway entrance just after the swim, we tried again last night. This time after the invis swim, we stopped to the right of the steps just outside of the water, killed the static spawn and pulled all the roamers/statics at our leisure to that spot (there are easily 6-7 roamers in that hallway plus 5 or so static spawns). Not really sure why we felt it neccessary to rush the ghost inside the hallway on the first 3 tries, but once we realized that we just couldn't handle the roamers and respawn fighting inside the hallway with a low DPS group, we relocated and did well from that point on. Key is to find a spot outside of any hallways to pull all roamers too, but also don't want to stay at that spawn due to the quick respawn time. If you fight in the hallways without clearing the roamers you can find yourself with 5-6+ adds fairly quickly and one very uptight enchanter. Of course I like making my enchanter buddy work for his flags, so a few adds isn't a bad thing :p

Our setup (FYI minus the chanter guildie and cleric friend, this was a pick-up group) was Shaman (me), chanter, cleric, warrior, bard, ranger.
