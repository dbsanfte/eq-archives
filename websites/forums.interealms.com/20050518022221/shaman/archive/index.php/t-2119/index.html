Naarlina
Done Lodi duo with both a monk partner and an SK partner...both fights are pretty much guaranteed wins if done correctly (My gear is not uber by any means...self buffed I run about 1100 ac and 2500 hps)

First, pull the turtle far far away from ToFS to avoid KSers.

Second, kite slow him...Radius has alot to do with Lodi's agro, so this isn't too hard.

Typical resist rate on slow after Malo is about 1-2 resists before slow lands...worse luck I ever had was 9 resists in a row...barely won that fight.

Third, time your slows (if you aren't already). Buy a cheap stop watch, start your re-slow after 4 minutes.

Everything else is straightforward...keep you partner torpored...oh, and make sure partner is using a 2 hander instead of 2 fast 1-handers...otherwise the reposts will eat him alive.

Have fun! It's a very exciting fight...mine lasted 10-15 minutes. One last warning, if you pull him far away to a small island, he will warp when he runs, so its best to keep the melee levitated so he can chase.
