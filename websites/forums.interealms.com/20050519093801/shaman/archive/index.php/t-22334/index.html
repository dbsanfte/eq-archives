Spirit Call 3 (9 AAs, 4/3/2):
5 wolves, L58, double for 60, enrage and push a fair bit (clones of the L61 pet).

Advanced Spirit Call 2 (10 AAs, 5/5):
3 wolves, L60, double for 135, proc a 640 lure DD, enrage and don't push as much. The wolves aren't translucent either.

ASC2 gives a noticable DPS boost over SC3, and the fact the wolves are L60 rather than L58 should improve their hit rate as well.

Is ASC2 worth spending twice as many AAs on as SC3? For me, yes, as I tend to send in the wolves as often as I can, although due to it being painful to get aggro in hard LDoNs, Tipt etc it's of less use there.
