I wouldn't even worry about run 3. Levels > AAs imho.

Yes, canni5 is nice, but it is still possible to get 65 without it. Thats the way I did it at least. :D

And besides, unless you have a steady group of friends that you exp with all the time, higher levels will get you more pick up groups too.

Now don't get me wrong, AAs do make a HUGE difference, anyone who has had the chance of grouping with someone with 100+ AA can obviously see that they are better hands down than someone with no AA. But because we are offensive casters generally speaking, and our resistance is checked against the mob lvl, I still have to say that for shamen, levels > AAs.

The only situation that I would consider AAs is if you really are not interested in the 60+ end game, which is understandable. In that case, I'd just go all out AA, get everything your heart desires, then come back to leveling when you feel like it.
