The original shaman classes have experience penalties. Barbarians=5%, Ogres=15%, Trolls 20%. That means that if you are an ogre for every mob you kill you will get 85% of the experience that say a human would get. (Human would get full 100%). First expansion shaman race (Iksar) also has an experience penalty of 20%. Vah Shir and Froglok's are not burdened by the exp penalty (and therefore are not real shaman)
