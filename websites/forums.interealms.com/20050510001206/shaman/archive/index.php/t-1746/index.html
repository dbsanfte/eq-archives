Ears = Forest Loops = 250p

Face - Arctic Wyvern Mask = 300p
Helm = JB Helm = 600-800p
Neck = Ivory Imbued Collar = 1k or if your a Troll do the Regeant Symbol quest.
Wrists = Pair of Rygorr Bracers = 100-200p Nathsar Bracers 400p
Arms = JB Arms = 600-800p
Hand = JB Gaunts = 600-800p
Shoulders = Tattered Mantle =300-400p
Belt = Flayed tumolskin(sp) belt for 600-700 or get a straw spun belt cheap for 30-50p.
Feet = JB Boots = 600-800p
Legs = Rygorr = 200-400p
Back = Cloak of Rygorr Orcle = 300p or Mammoth hide cloak = 600p
Shield = Clay Gaurdian Shield = 400p
Weapon = Lots of choices here - Slime coated harpoon is nice 500p and howling harpoon for 1k, either go in range, also Jade Prod about 800p and for 1hb wraith bone hammer is decent - 200p

This gear should last awhile, I would save what ever you have left for spells personally, maybe upgrade face to crystal spider eyes 1k and your JB gear for Crystal chitin gear but big price difference from JB to CC.
