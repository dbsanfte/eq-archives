Ok, there are 4 types of haste.
V1 - Item haste
V2 - Spell/Regular Bard song Haste (cloak of dark sky is v2 I believe)
V3 - Bard Song which goes over 100% haste cap/New Guardian of the Forest Haste

And finally Quiver haste which stacks with all of the above.

The haste type on Guardian of the Forest started off as spell haste, meaning it would not stack with enchanter/shaman haste lines, and at the higher level of the AA skill, it would over write their haste spells (99% > VoG). Not only would this have made the haste percentage on Guardian more or less useless, it would also piss off your enchanters/shamen because as soon as the skill's duration was up, they would have to re-buff you.

As it is now, the guardian haste stacks with items, and normal spells, but doesn't stack with bard songs.
Its better this way, albet the issue of 15% haste being too low to make it worthwile, as the first bard song already grants a 20% haste over cap.

Hopefully, this will get changed as time goes on.
Imo the haste should be 15%, 30%, 60% for each level of the AA skill
