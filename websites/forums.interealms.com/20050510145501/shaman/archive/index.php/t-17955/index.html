Harwel
Reasons I love to duo with a Ranger:

1. Aggro control. Rangers can take aggro, or ditch it, pretty much at will. Sometimes it's preferable for the ranger to tank, sometimes for the shaman to tank while the ranger shoots or plays rogue. Either way, it's extremely easy to manage.

2. Buffs which stack with your own. Maybe not the best, but can add a few hundred HP, some AC, a Damage Shield, etc. They can also emergency-heal you... not for a lot but it can be a lifesaver.

3. Snare and DPS are among the only things a shaman lacks... Ranger brings both.

4. Tracking is awesome, nuff said.

5. Can pull single outdoors, can help root-park multiples as well when hunting indoors or when multiple pulls are preferable.

6. Slumming in places like Droga, ranger's stun proc is actually pretty effective as well, especially when they're at max haste and dex.

7. Forage. Laugh if you like... a ranger can easily feed themselves, me, and my horse. Saves on stat foods, since I have a limitless supply of forage to gorge myself with. Also some Alchemy recipes (LDoN) require foraged ingredients... all free kthx~

I spent most of levels 1-55 duo with a warrior. While it's very effective, a ranger just brings so much more to the table in a duo, it isn't even funny. Shaman + Ranger = synergy.

Much <3 for Rangers.
