Jinheim
The reason your root breaks so much is because of your level and the level of the mob you are fighting.

Every 6 seconds, a mob gets a chance to break root. It is the mob's level and MR vs. your level, with a random number tied in. Since at 24 you are very near the same level as the mob you are fighting, and you are not using any MR debuffs (or small ones if you are), then the mob has a pretty good chance of breaking root.

Also, dealing damage to a mob with a spell (a nuke or one of our dots, thanks to their damned DD component) gives it a good chance to break root.

Luclin wasn't around when I was 24 so I can't really give you much advice on needlites, but the Lake of Ill Omen in kunark near the iksar city served me well at your level. At 24 you have some powerful dots that should serve you well. It isn't your gear that is holding you back. It isn't easy being untwinked, but it is doable. You just have to more cautious and precise.

Your heals are too low ratio to help with canni. Canni-dance is a method of getting mana back a little quicker at your level than just medding. The reason it works is that you are regenerating health and mana when meditating. Sit, watch for the health and mana increase. Stand, Cast canni, and sit as fast as possible. I believe at this stage I was hurting for funds. I used to hunt mammoths in EF and if that was too crowded I'd go to the Gorge and solo the eyes.

Both are good sources of PP and OK xp. I know I could take an even con mammoth solo in banded/black alloy using a slime coated harpoon.

The eyes in the Gorge are easy once you break the spawn and can engage them before they cast their annoying animations. The trick with them is to pretend you're a wizard and nuke. Don't bother slowing or dotting. This is one of the last levels you'll feel your nukes are up to snuff might as well enjoy it.

Mammoths I'd just slow, dot, and meele. If the fight was going bad I'd root, back off, and med.

Mammoths and Eyes are both trivial encounters by level 29 but between levels 24-28 are fairly challenging fights with good loot for an untwinked shaman.

If you have a group available to you there is always the third level of Runnyeye. Every good shaman should die to Battlewizard Unak at least once in his/her career. Runnyeye has some nice loot for it's levels if you don't have a rich benefactor.
