Naala
Echo is where I did 48 through 51.

48: Underbulks and Fiends near the Deep Zone or Underbulks,Fiends and Grabbers next to the taskmaster room.

49 & 50: TM camp. You can single pull him with slow, then work on the skellies, sometimes they come as a pair but you can root park one.

51: Gnome camp. Not a lot of people know about this little gem of exp. Perfect for 51. They are in an area not far from TM camp (take a right before u get to TM camp and you'll see 2 gnome guards up a ramp) Single pulls, ez kills. After dispensing with them, move in a bit. Ignore the gnomes to the right (they r light blue at 51). Head NW and u will see a drape. Kill the 2 guards there as well. Past them is the throne room with the 2 gnome kings and his posse. I only pulled Harold the Herald and the Court Jester, (single pull with slows). Ignore the others, especially if kings are there. You'll get multiple pulls.

FYI, kings are like lev 45 but I soloed them a few times when they were by themselves. Someone must have pulled them and the kings tend to stay where they were pulled to instead of returning to spawn point.

So you have 6 gnomes total, all on a 20 minte timer. I had to clear all 6 about 6 times to get 1 gold bub (maybe 7 times). So thats about 2 hrs to 2.5 hrs per gold bub. Not bad IMHO.

Also, you can kill the Haven Defender who patrols the hallway, she's a hotty but I whacked her good. Be careful not to kill her too much cauz u lose faction with defenders of shadow haven, Im dubious there now. Also, the gnomes are on kingdom of above and below faction, which only exists in there camp, so slaughter away.

Good Luck,

And hope this helps.
