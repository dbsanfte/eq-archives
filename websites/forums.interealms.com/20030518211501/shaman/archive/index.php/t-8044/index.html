I think bringing your 50+ group to UP would be a good idea, if you want to try updating your face/shoulders.

I got a Death Watcher's shroud from a Corrupted Elysian Elder (+15 AC +5 Sta +7 Wis +5 vsMg +5 vsFire +5 vsCold +15 HP +30 Mana +0.90 Wt ), and a reasonably nice Skull Marked Shield from a Fallen Sergeant (+22 AC +7 Wis +50 HP +50 Mana +3.20 Wt).

These two items would be in addition to your chances at getting the Death Mask of the Elysians.

The Obulous Death Shroud and Golem's Jawbone are probably superior to these two, but are also in two different zones.
