Amise
Fear-kiting: pull an animal, snare it (can pull with snare if you wish), then cast panic animal on the snared mob. Run behind it and WACK it until it falls down dead. :D You'll need to re-apply fear every 10 seconds or so.

I started fearing in my late 20s, killing mammoths in everfrost. These are great cash and xp until 30, then if you want to continue fearing, Stonebrunt (cats and other animals) or Iceclad (cougars, wolves) would be a couple of good places.
magimix
That pretty much sums it up for me :-) I ground my way from 30 to 32 at Everfrost, fear kiting mammoths. The XP was fairly low risk, and I made about 1000PP as well.

Fear kiting is great for the simple reason that the mobs are running away, not hitting you. My own preference is to pull the mob with snare, unless it is a contested mob, in which case I may pull with an arrow, and snare while they run over to me. I would suggest you make sure the mob is snared before you cast panic animal though - some of these mobs can run damn fast, and if they are social they could bring back some unwanted buddies to help them out.

Of course, fear kiting works best in large, expansive outdoor zones. Because you may do this while soloing, it is vital to have root and tangling weeds memmed as well, to quickly manage any adds that may turn up.
