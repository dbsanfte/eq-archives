Kianor
Places for armour drops in Kael are:

Arena - best dragon faction, best all round armour drops.

Tormax Arch - good faction and good armour.

Bank - not bad faction, occasional armour. (Seems to be mostly bracers).

Iceshard Keep ('Rif House, Chanter house, plate cycle) - good for plate drops, occasional other bits and pieces, including Rune Etched Icewurm Fang.

If you're going for armour or dragon faction, and you have enough people, go to the Arena. If you don't have enough manpower for that, try the plate cycle or the Tormax arch (particularly if Tormax himself isn't up). A small group can do the bank and surrounding area, with a small chance of armour drops.
