I still use Togor's sometimes. In my opinion, the biggest benefit of Turgur's is the duration and not the extra slow percentage. If things are low-level and are dying really fast (i.e., the other day we were having to get through light blues on a CR) than I'll switch to Togor's.
Ciele
One huge advantage to Turgur's Instects (51) vs Togor's Instects (39) is that Turgur is a 3 second cast versus 5 second cast for Togor. Those 2 secs really make a difference if you are getting beat on.

Though as the previous poster pointed out their effects are nearly identical at 60th, with the 39th level slow enjoying a significant mana discount. (250 vs 175, notwithstanding specialization effects).
