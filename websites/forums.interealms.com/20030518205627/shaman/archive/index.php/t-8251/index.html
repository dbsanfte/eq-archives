go to EQmaps and find one of them that points out North Camp. nothing wanders by there, and I can make it up to the camp without being seen by anything easily with proper patience. Move up, kill all the see invis between you and gate, then you can invis and move in a tracker and leave him there for as long as you need. For ranger, max skill and he can see the golems (pretty sure) for a druid, just use bind sight on mobs till you get close enough to visually verify the golem spawn.
