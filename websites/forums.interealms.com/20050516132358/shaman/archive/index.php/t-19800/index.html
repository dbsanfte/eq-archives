To be honest, I am quite surprised and amazingly pleased to see that Sony has added a new server called Morden Rasp. While I will agree that there has been many wailing about the demise of EverQuest, the product continues to move along based on the "New" vision of Sony Enterprises, i.e. more about the money instead of the vision.

I do not want to sound naive, as adding a new server does not solve the numerous issues within the game. But given the continuing rate of expansions for EverQuest Online and other EQ branded products, who know where Sony would be willing to invest next. The bottom line is the bottom line.

In closing, many lately have questioned the game direction. From my standpoint, the Dev team appears to be heading back to its roots. Dev posts are popping up across numerous boards (regardless of ownership/ad issues). EQTraders has had very strong interaction from quest/trader skill Dev?s (just check out any one of the skill forums to see numerous interactive posts). And EverQuest Online Forums is starting up new sections as well. All of this appears (to me) to show that the management of EverQuest is stretching itself to meet its community base.

Here's looking forward to greater interaction on Shaman issues with the development team. Better to look forward than remain in the past.
