My advice is to make sure all questions are prefaced properly so that the door is not left open for a half assed response.

For example: If you were to ask " will shaman healing be upgraded ?", you might get the answer " we feel that shaman healing is adequate in most situations".
That answer tells us little, but you are hard pressed to say that your question did not get an answer.
If however you were to ask the question " it is clear to all of us that shaman can not function effectively as a backup healer in GoD, let alone primary healer. What do you intend to do to redress this shortcoming?" , then the same answer is glaringly inadequate, because you are not asking about "most" situations.
Same principle applies to dots and buffs/debuffs.

Leave no doubt as to what you are asking and leave less room to dodge the issue.
