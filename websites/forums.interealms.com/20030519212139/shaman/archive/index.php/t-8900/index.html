The Cudgel Quests For Good Races

This quest has been completed by a barbarian shaman of the tribunal. Total estimated time for the cudgels 1-8 is about 30 game hours depending on your faction with the Mystics of Cabilis.

Faction

To even start this quest you need to be at least amiable to the Shaman guild in Cabilis and the maximum faction you can get from turning in bone chips or explorer knives will get you to indifferent. I personally have turned in more than 3000 bone chips and 120 explorer knives to get my faction from scowl to indifferent. I suggest not doing the explorer knife quests if you are below level 60 because you will eventually become KOS to Firiona Vie and you won’t be able to buy anything from there until you fix your faction again. If you’re not concerned about Firiona Vie faction, the explorer knives do give a lot more faction than the bone chips do.

If you are KOS to the troopers of Cabilis, you need a friendly enchanter with project illusion to cast illusion iksar on you until you become non kos to the trooper you hand your bone chips into without iksar illusion. The troopers do not see invis, but if you hand something in to them you will automatically become visible. It took approximately 1500 bone chips to get from scowl to dubious to do the bone chip quest without having to get iksar illusion. Same goes with the explorer knives quest. If you are not at least dubious to the trooper you hand your items to, you would need illusion iksar cast upon you. You can start the cudgel quests once you become at least amiable in iksar form to the Mystics of Cabilis which may be still KOS without iksar illusion, but I suggest becoming non KOS to them before starting the quest just in case. Dubious faction without iksar illusion should make you amiable/kindly with it.

A good way to test your faction without having to go inside Cabilis to con the shamans is to stay outside away from the Crusaders and con them. When you do the explorer knives or bone chip quests, you gain 5 factions with Cabilis which are Mystics, Troopers, Crusaders, Merchants, and Monks. People tend to get Trooper faction up quickly because they take faction hits from hunting in Old Sebilis. Even though Troopers may con a higher faction to you, crusaders/mystics/merchants/monks will still be KOS to you. Since you will be taking the crusader faction hits at the same time you’ll be taking the mystic hits, your faction with crusaders and mystics should be about equal unless you have killed iksar crusaders before.

Without doing the explorer knife quests I have been told it takes at least 5000 bone chips to get from scowl to dubious. Your friendly enchanter will need to help you with all 8 of the cudgels, so make sure he/she is in a safe place in Cabilis before casting iksar illusion on you for your quests. Even with iksar illusion it is still possible to be KOS to all of Cabilis.

Iron Cudgel of the Clairvoyant (Cudgel 1)

This part of the text has been taken from the first cudgel quest off of <a target="Allakhazam's" href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/">Allakhazam’s</a>:

Talk to Hierophant Oxyn in the Temple of Terror. After you return complete the Curse Wafer's Quest, ask Hierophant Oxyn "What lost skulls?" and he responds, "You must have heard of the Trilac Brotherhoods disappearance. They are the skulls of three soon to be ancients. They were taken from this temple by a silent intruder. Crusaders are always on duty. I do not know how they got into our vault. Every petitioner is ordered to search for the three skulls and return them to me along with their petitioner cudgel and then they shall become clairvoyants."

When asked "What Iron Cudgel of the Clairvoyant?" Hierophant Oxyn shakes his head and waves a finger in your face. If you are looking to be handed the Iron Cudgel of the Clairvoyant then you are sadly mistaken. Perhaps if you were to gather a few [lost skulls] for the temple we may find you worthy to wield one. So you obviously need to gather the skulls to find out how the get the cudgel. The three skulls are named Waz, Morgl, and Logrin. The intruder can be found in the "sewer" area of East Cabilis. (I have been told he has been spotted in the Swamp of no Hope as well).

The way to get to the sewers from the Temple of Terror is to swim past the bridge then to the left and under one of the docks that the gondola stops at. If you find an underwater tunnel that has barracuda swimming in it, you have found it. Follow the tunnel and you'll occasionally come to some open areas that you can jump out of the water. Any jump-out point is good, however the last one allows the greatest access to MOBs to kill and cause the Iksar outcast to spawn. In this area spawns mostly mosquitoes, and scorpions with the occasional decaying skeleton. The rare spawns are the Iksar outcast and the giant leech.

Collect all 3 skulls and return them to Oxyn along with the “Cudgel of a Petitioner” you received when you gave your guild note to the guild master (NOT THE WOODEN ONE). You receive some faction, coinage, your very own Iron Cudgel of the Clairvoyant and the second skull quest.

Estimated time: 3 hours

The way I did it was I had a friend create a newbie iksar shaman and turned in the note to get the Cudgel of the Petitioner. Go to the sewers in Cablis where this quest explains to go, and kill everything that spawns in that one square area until you get your 3 skulls. It’ll seem like you can’t jump up from the water to the ledge but you can. Once you get the skulls have, ask the enchanter to project illusion iksar on you and have the newbie shaman turn in the Cudgel of the Petitioner. Then you turn in the 3 skulls and you get your Cudgel of the Clairvoyant.

Iron Cudgel of the Seer (Cudgel 2)

This part of the text has been taken from the first cudgel quest off of <a target="Allakhazam's" href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/">Allakhazam’s</a>:

After completing the first quest, Heirophant Onyx sends you on a two part quest. The first part is to get two Cleansers of the Outland skulls and return them to him along with your Iron Cudgel of the Clairvoyant. He says when you have done this he will tell you where to get the next two skulls. Skull with I is found on a stuffed giant barracuda in Lake of Ill Omen. In Warsliks Woods, a Rogue Shaman Spawns. He drops a Jawless Skull and a note. The note tells you about the barracuda. You need to go to Field of Bones and kill another rogue shaman to get the Mandible. The note also says that you can buy Mendglow Clay in Cabilis, but nobody can find a place that sells it. It can however be foraged in the Burning Woods by a master forager (with much effort). Take your Jawless Skull, Mandible and Mendglow clay and combine them on a potters wheel (make sure you have a decent pottery skill). Do NOT add water. The combination will result in Skull with II. Turn Skull with I and Skull with II along with your cudgel of the Clairvoyant to Oxyn and get some xp, coin and an Iron Cudgel of the Seer (4/21). When you finish this, Hierophant Oxyn says, 'We are in your debt. You are truly one who shall collect all the lost ancient skulls. Take your weapon. Go to Hierophant Zand and he shall guide you further. Tell him you are [the chosen saviour.]

Estimated time: 3 hours

You might need the help of a druid/ranger for this part of the quest. You need to kill the stuffed barracuda in Lake of Ill Omen which is always up for the Skull with I. The rogue shaman in Warsliks Woods spawns in the first ruins when you enter Warsliks Woods from Field of Bone. There are 3 placeholders in those ruins which you kill to make the rogue shaman spawn. It took me about 20 minutes to get the shaman to spawn. Loot the jawless skull from him and the note which tells you about the rogue shaman in Field of Bone. The rogue shaman in Field of Bone spawned for me at 3:00 a.m. game time. You need to keep clearing all of the spawns around Kurns Tower for him to spawn. He will aggro on different random NPCs which could kill him so keep an eye out for him or ask a druid or ranger to keep tracking for him. He drops the mandible for the second skull. Mendglow Clay is foraged in Burning Woods. You can either have your nice druid or ranger friend spend the next hour or so foraging in Burning Woods, or you can find someone in bazaar usually selling it. You need to combine the Mendglow Clay, the mandible, and the jawless skull in a pottery wheel to get the Skull II. My pottery is 140 and was trivial. People have had lower pottery and combined it no problem.

Iron Cudgel of the Mystic (Cudgel 3)

This part of the text has been taken from the first cudgel quest off of <a target="Allakhazam's" href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/">Allakhazam’s</a>:

Tell Hierophant Zand "I am the chosen savior" and he closes his eyes and bows before you. "I am honored to meet the one who shall pledge his life to the return of the Skulls of the Ancients. However, I must see proof of our prowess as of yet. Go to the outlands and retrieve one Froglok Hexdoll, and no, they are not found on Frogloks. They are shaman dolls made by the goblin tribe." The doll can be found on a Goblin Hextracer in Warslik's Woods. After you turn in the Hexdoll, you get a note to deliver to Crusader Quarg in Field of Bones. Quarg gives you a six slot container and tells you to go to the Tower of Kurn and bring him the Skulls of the Caste of Bone Bretheren, a caste of powerful shamans who perished fighting undead in the Field of Bone several decades ago. You are to get the skulls of the 5 Caste members along with the Skull of the Leader. The Leader Skull is a Lore skull with an "X". The others are non lore Skulls with an "X". (When Identified, they say Caste of Bone Bretheren). They all off the Crusader in Kurn's Tower. Put them in the bag and combine it. This turns the bag into "C.O.B.B box" Take that back to Crusader Quarg, along with your previous cudgel, and he will give you the Iron Cudgel of the Mystic.

Estimated time: 6 hours

This by far has to be the most boring part of the quest. I can personally vouch for the 6 hours on this quest. The Goblin Hextracer is always up in Warsliks Woods, so ask your friendly druid/ranger to help you track him and kill him. Loot the Hexdoll and then you ask your friendly enchanter to illusion you in cabilis for the note and in field of bone to get the box. Note: You have to be in iksar form for any turn in you do or you will lose that part of the quest. I have spent almost 4 hours completely clearing out all the skeletons in kurns many times over and gotten one undead crusader to spawn. I am not sure if this is luck or if the undead crusader despawns if you don’t kill him in a certain amount of time, but the only place I have seen him spawn is on the stairs between the third and fourth floors in kurns. The loc they give on Allakhazams is -98, -223, 122. I am not sure if the sitting makes it spawn more often, but I have gotten 5 skulls in 2 hours just by sitting in that spot. Even if it’s just luck, it won’t hurt to try.

This part of the text has been taken from the first cudgel quest off of <a target="Allakhazam's" href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/">Allakhazam’s</a>:

After completing quest No 3, Crusader Quarg says... "The temple shall be pleased. As instructed by the Hierophants, here is your Iron Cudgel of the Mystic. You have done well that I must ask you to [collect the Crusaders of Rok Nilok]. Take this chest (6 slot). Inside you shall combine the skull of their leader and at least five of the caste members (sound familliar). You then will go to the Swamp Garrison and deliver the full chest and your Iron Cudgel of the Mystic to Mystic Dovan. Go to him now and inquire of the Crusaders of Rok Nilok." Mystic Dovan spawns after 9pm on top of the building with the warlord in Swamp of no hope and says when you inquire "Mystic Dovan lowers his head in sorrow let us have a moment of peace. I cannot find the words to speak of the unspeakable act of the gods which took the lives of the legendary Crusaders of Rok Nilok. You must find the answers for yourself. All I can do is wait for their return." The legend of Nilok says that he and his followers were shrunk down by a magical rain and that nilok's head burns red. You need to go to the first goblin ruins in Warsilik's Woods (when entering from Field of Bone) and hunt the Goblin Bloodtracers. These drop the 5 glowing skulls you need. There are Goblin Bloodtracers all over Swamp of No Hope, but the easiest place to find them is in the goblin fort. Just kill all of the goblins in there and the bloodtracers will randomly spawn. They always drop either a glowing skull or the lore skull.

Iron Cudgel of the Channeler (Cudgel 5)

This part of the text has been taken from the first cudgel quest off of <a target="Allakhazam's" href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/">Allakhazam’s</a>:

After completing quest 4, Dovan tells you about the skulls of Di Nozok. He says "bring them to me, I shall wait within Zand". Go talk to Heirophant Zand about the skulls for more information.

The first one is the Skull of Die Nozok. It drops in the Sarnak fortress in the Lake of Ill Omen off of the Iksar Adherant.

The second one is the Skull of Dai Nozok. The details of this are still sketchy, so I can't vouch for all of them. You need to kill Overseer Dlubish in Nurga and get the Dai Nozok Canopic. You also need to get the two keys of Zai, which drop in four pieces as froglok ton totems (I think there are three ton totems and one orange totem -- use identify to be sure of what you get) off of Froglok Ton Warriors in the Swamp of No Hope. I think that these need to be combined with some mendglow clay to create the two keys. You then hand the keys and the canopic and 400 gp to Galdon Vok Nir in the Overthere to get the Skull. Be careful of your faction here. You may need an enchanter to help you do this part.

Turn in both skulls and your Cudgel of the Prophet to Heirophant Zand and you will get the next cudgel.

Estimated time: Days

This cudgel took me about 3 hours. You can kill 2 birds with one stone here if you have a druid or ranger along with you. Yes, you do need mendglow clay again, but this is the last time you’ll need it. In Swamp of No Hope there is an iksar brigand camp at 2400, 0. You need to clear this camp and the entire surrounding area of mobs to make the Froglok Ton Warriors to spawn. The warriors are kos to everything else that spawns there so you need a ranger or druid to track him and kill him before the other mobs do. It took me about 1 hour of clearing the area to get 1 whole key of zai and 3 fragments from the ton warriors. You need to go to a pottery wheel and combine the 3 broken pieces with mendglow clay to get your second key of zai. My pottery skill is at 140, but I have heard other people doing it with less skill.

The skull off the Sarnak Adherants in Lake of Ill Omen is fairly common. Just go to the Sarnak Fort and kill the Adherants and one in two will probably drop a skull. You might want to have your ranger or druid friend to tag along and track for a Sarnak Dragoon also. There’s a helm you need from him for a future cudgel. Be careful though, this helm is no rent so you’ll have to do that cudgel before you log off or you’ll have to look for another dragoon in the fort.

The Dai Nozok Canopic was pretty easy to get. Overseer Dublish can spawn more commonly in Nurga but I have also seen him spawn in Droga also. Have your ranger or druid friend track in Nurga for him and stonechanter goblins. They drop chant rocks for your next cudgel. You need the one that looks like a skinning rock or a sharpening stone or dragon egg graphic. It’s tan in color and identifies as slate chant rock. Have your ranger and druid friend forage for you also because you need a sponge mushroom from there also for your next cudgel.

Now that you have your 2 keys and the Canopic, head to Overthere with your enchanter friend. Have him/her cast iksar illusion on you before you head into the outpost. The npcs here are on venril sathir faction, so if you killed in karnors, kaesora, or howling stones before I suggest invising before heading inside. Galdon Vok Nir is pretty easy to get to. He’s in the corner away from all of the other npcs in the outpost so you shouldn’t have a problem turning in your 2 keys, canopic, and 400 gold to him to get your second skull. Turn in the skull from the Adherant and the skull from Galdon plus your fourth cudgel to Hierophant Zand and you get your next cudgel.
