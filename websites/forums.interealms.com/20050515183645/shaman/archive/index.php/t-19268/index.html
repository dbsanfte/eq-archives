Jalelin
One of the things that I always seem to see mentioned with Shaman healing spells is how they change the balance of our mana regeneration abilities via cannibalize. Giving Shamans a bigger and/or more efficient healing spell allows them to regenerate mana more quickly. This was supposedly the reason why our mini-CH was only 1950 rather than the 2925 that Druids got.

If increased healing power is something that Shamans, as priests, should be getting, why not simply flag the spells as "others only"? Group heals, single target heals, AE heals, or whatever could be added but they would just not land on the Shaman casting it. Obviously, two Shamans together could take turns casting the more efficient spells on each other but that would be an exception rather than a common occurance. By flagging the spells this way, Shamans could get some healing spells that put them back in line with Druids in the group healing department but do not change the balance with regards to mana regeneration.
