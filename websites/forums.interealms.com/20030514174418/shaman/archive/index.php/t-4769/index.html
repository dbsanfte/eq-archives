Work all 3 skills. Do it NOW, dont wait to max 1.

In order to get skill raises you have to be fighting light blue con mobs or better. as you get higher level, the mobs hit harder and harder, and if you are trying to raise weapon skills from a low base, you are gonna spend a lot of times sewing your butt back together.

cattywumpus
You definately want to keep up all your weapon skill as best you can. I used a Bloodstained Fang from level 8 when someone gave it to me (thanks Teem) till 18 when I found a Groaning Mace on a merchant for 134pp. That's when I realised how important it is to keep all your skills up. Here I had this great new weapon and couldn't hit the broad side of a barn for 5 levels.

Some weapons you should keep an eye out for that haven't been mentioned are a Bogling Chieftains Spear (10/26, fast for leveling skill and rare but cheap), Staff of Living Sand (sell for 200pp but hits like a groaning mace with a proc), or a Skull-Shaped Sacrificial Hammer (6/24 with a nice damage shield proc, great for skilling up).

Of course, if money isn't a problem get a Rune-Etched Icewurm Fang. Amazing little weapon but never less than 3kpp.
