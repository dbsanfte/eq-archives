Going to go out on a limb here.

A Berserker.

You have a class thats a better tank and does more DPS then rangers, does more DPS then a Knight, you have Stuns and Snares. Once you get to a high lvl, they have some seriously nice AAs and can do close to Rogue lvl DPS when tanking.

Grumblin
Necros are great, i agree. All the way up to probably 60ish they outperform mages in duo. But when the mobs start summoning, the necro pet is going to go down faster, and not have nearly as much aggro.

On the plus side for necros is non-summoning mobs, shaman necro duo becomes just amazing. Pet split can be done with a shaman pet and coth as contingency. But the other advantage of a necro is definitely FD rez. Mage shaman combos just tend to have to not die :p

With melee: I see melee as a serious downside, because gear directly affects the difficulty of the mobs you can duo. With an int caster though, gear has MUCH less bearing.

So aye, if mage is out of the question for you i'd definitely say necro, but will also plead for you to reconsider for the high end's sake! :)
