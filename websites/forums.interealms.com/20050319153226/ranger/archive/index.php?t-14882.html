Like they said, there is no minimum to delay on a weapon.

As an FYI the formula for calculating your reduced weapon delay (from haste effects) is as follows;

Delay/(1+Haste) = Hasted Delay

For example, using earthcaller (14/24) with Swiftwind (40% haste), the calculation would look like this;

24 / 1.40 = 17.14

So the dmg/dly of Earthcaller with haste effects is 14/17



Using a BFT bow with swiftwind (40 %), speed of vallon (66%), battlecry of the vah shir(25 %stackable), fleeting quiver(~20% stackable), and Guardian of the forest3 (15% stackable) a ranger could attain a haste of 160 % (SW+SoV=100% +bard song 25, quiver 20 , guardian aa 15).
So the formula for this one looks like this;
38 / 2.6 = 14.62 effectively making BFT a 30/15 weapon.
