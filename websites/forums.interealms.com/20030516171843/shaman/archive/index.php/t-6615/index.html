Velious plate helms are customizable, chain is not. He won't customize the Wolf Caller's Helm. It has no graphic on Iksars. Since Velious does not differentiate between races, Iksar warriors and SK's can customize their helms. This is why you see the "Crab-Head" helms on Iksars sometimes.
