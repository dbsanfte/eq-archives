According to the dev responsible, the lack of Ammo slot on this was an oversight that will be fixed next patch.

Relevant recipes are:

Grandmaster's Medicine Bag = Artisan's Seal + Potion of Mystical Aptitude

artisan's seal = mounted blue diamond, mounted diamond, mounted black sapphire, mounted jacinth, vial of purified mana, small block of clay, flask of water - combine in a pottery wheel, then fire with an HQ firing sheet in kiln (triv less than 102 pottery, not no drop)

mounted [gem] = 2 bars of velium, [gem] (diamond, blue diamond, black sapphire, jacinth) (in jeweler's kit, trivial <=142 (diamond <=128))

More info on Tradeskill Trophies in general can be found here (http://www.eqtraders.com/secrets/recipes_trophies.htm).
