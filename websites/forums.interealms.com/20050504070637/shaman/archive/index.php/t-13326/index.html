Weyalan
To do Exec, you need a good focus point Tank: Ideally a Paladin, next best a good SK or Warrior. The tank's job is to try to keep aggro on all the mobs at once.

If you have a ggod tank, it doesn't matter too much what class you take (I have seen 1xPaladin +5xDruid stroll thru trial).

The important thing is short term DPS, and although some classes are better for DPS than others, provided you havegood teamwork and everyone is ont the case, you can get through (I did the trial with 58druid, 59shaman, 59shaman, 58cleric 65Paladin 63Ranger, which is not exactly optimum class selection or level selection, but we worked well and got it done.

As a shaman in the trial, forget everything you know.

You will NOT have time to slow (except final boss)
You will not have time to DoT

You will use your inefficent Direct Damage spells
You will send pet (remember you can make pet, buff pet to the max, etc before entering the trial, and then cast "summon companion" before trial starts).

Basically, if you can get your hot keys set up as listed in an earlier post, you are set.
