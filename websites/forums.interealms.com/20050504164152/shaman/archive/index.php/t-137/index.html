Chutoi
There are 2 ways to do this.

The cudgel quests require some item (ie a box that combines the right ingredients) and the cudgel. What you need is to say the words, get the box, get the items, and do the combine. Then have a friend create a new shaman, hand in the new shaman's cudgel, and you immediately thereafter hand in the other item. You will receive the next cudgel back.

Alternately, I'm pretty sure that there is a now way to get a new cudgel from the Heirophants. Something like, "I lost my cudgel" or something. They put that in for iksar that lost their cudgels. I'm not sure of the trigger words though. Allakhazam.com may have something.
