Kianor
I was under the impression that DogDog didn't dual wield *unless* handed weapons.

Maybe that changes with level, some other casters' pets progress up the levels - lower levels never dual wield, mid levels dual wield if handed weapons, higher levels dual wield by default. It's easy to see this with a mid level enchanter animation - it's a sword and shield by default, but hand it 2 weapons, and you can see it dual wielding.

As for the level thing - originally, you could summon a pet that was in the 5 level range that the pet could be. (e.g. for the level 49 pet spell, you could get a pet anywhere from 32 to 36.) However, a while ago (maybe a year?) they changed it so that you would always summon the maximum level pet you can. The change removed a lot of tedium, and made a lot of sense, because most pet classes (except most shamans because of the low level and high mana cost of their pets) simply cast their pet again and again until they got the highest one they could.
