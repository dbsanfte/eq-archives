I can clear to the rev tower with just me playing 5 chars. The thing you need is a level 60 puller. This keeps adds to a minimum (but still expect to see up to 5 golems at once). It is alot easier now that all the mobs down there are mezzable.

What I do is this: Set up group at the entrance to the right of the entrance to the city (largish area with no mobs). Clear the 2 entry mobs, and the one directly inside. Puller goes in (staying to the left, but not entering any buildings). There will be one golem to the left and a broken wall to the right and a hallway straight ahead (refer to this as point A). Use aggro radius pulling to get the golem. Depending on level of spawn, you will get 2-5 golems on this pull. Kill them. Then everyone to in front of the More Than Mushrooms shop (this is if you passed point A without going to where the golem was). Pull group goes in the door and hugs right wall. There will be the hall to the docks. Kill the roamer patrolling that door and the 1-3 rock golems (don't go into dock, thats 3 more mobs to fight). Once all the adds are cleared, have rest of the group come to you, again hugging the right wall.

There will be a doorway to your right which leads to another part of the city. Make sure puller can get to that doorway without any aggro (kill any that do aggo). Once it's clear, the entire group needs to charge into that next room. There is 1 elemental in the little pool there and 2-4 roamers. Kill all these. Then puller starts clearing toward the tunnel. Expect 2-3 mobs at a time in camp as well as the rev roamers which will probably be repopping. Once all is clear, then move group into the tunnel.

Speed is of the essense. Respawn in the hole is only about 20 min, tell everyone to not plan on any afk's. If a non-vital person goes LD, leave them behind and have your hafling rogue scout drag the corpse to you once you get into the tunnel.

Once you've dropped down and cleared the courtyard roamers, move the raid to the first pool room, kill what you brought. Next move down the tunnel till just before the cave with the first building. Kill those roamers and move into that cave away from the building, over by a pool. kill the other few mobs that agro and pull two roamers from the tunnel further in, a ratman and a revenant.

Now you get to the map labeled city1 and it gets kinda hairy. Drop into the first big room and kill the 2 guards and the roamers that path to you. Hug the right wall and move to the next cave. Kill roamers.

On the map, you'll see a building label with Polzin Mrid (quest npc), move the raid to the back of that little dead end ally in front of the building. You won't get any roamers there. Now clear the first few mobs inside that building, one will be a healer and you don't want him CHing rock golems. Now you have your puller clear the way to the dock. Preferably have a feign puller here and someone to peel off him, this is the only place I know where you can get a massive pull by mistake. Once the way is clear to the dock area, rush it and kill the mobs in there. You can get to this point in about 20 minutes once you get the hang of it.

Now you get to the fun part. Jump off the waterfall and kill the 4 mobs at the bottom. Start moving up the tunnel, fight when you get to a mob, there's only about 4 total. Now you're at the tower entrance. FROM HERE ON, NO AOE SPELLS! If you have 2 groups, I'd only take one inside. Invis to undead and move up the ramps until you get to the last loopback before the top floor. If anyone looses ITU on the way, tell em to stand and die. If your cleric looses ITU, drag him back outside and let the cleric from teh 2nd group rez him. Pull the guys around kirn to here then move up into Kirn's room. Take him down (easy fight) and port out.

Assuming nothing went wrong, you can be done in well under an hour.

Rathelon
The Hole used to be much harder when epics were first put in. Several times we tried fighting down to Kirn's tower and ended up wiped out in the city, thus commencing a 2 or 3 hour corpse recovery. It's much easier now, or so it seems.

Best thing you can do is learn the zone so you know exactly where you're going and what's in your way. If you can do it, get the raid leader and puller to do some exploring ahead of time.

You can make halfling rogues of Brell and get a druid to take them to Toxxulia and bind them outside the Hole entrance. Any rogue of Brell would probably work, but I usually make halfling since they have hide and sneak at 1st level. Many of the earth mobs used to not attack Brell worshippers (but this could have been changed). Either way, a hiding and sneaking Brell character can still move anywhere in the zone safely. These 'hole moles' as I call them are the best way to explore and learn the zone, and also handy in case you need corpse recovery.

For the actual raid, you will need people who can follow directions quickly and stay alert. Getting to earth city isnt that difficult, and once you are through earth city, it's much easier. Inside earth city you have to kill fast and move fast so you vacate an area before respawns start. If you get stuck in respawn, you will probably die.

Get the highest level people you can and you should be able to do it with about 2 groups (or less). I wouldnt try it with more than 18 people, because then you are just asking for unneeded aggro trying to pack more people through the city.

* A good group of 55+ can do this no sweat. Make sure the puller is 60 though. (Might need more if you don't have a lvl 60 shaman.... Usually I handle the slowing so it's kinda hard to know what resists <60 will get)
