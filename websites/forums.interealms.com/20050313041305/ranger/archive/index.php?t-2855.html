Tonight my guild took down Ulump for a guild druid in Swamp of No Hope. I got ported to EJ then went through TT to the river zone area that goes to SoNH. Well upon zoning, in that dark cave with the river inside it (same cave where Dugroz spawns for the Howling Stones/Charasis key quest) I noticed something vastly different. Rather than seeing the several low level Iksars that normally stand on the shore, they were all dead on the ground, and several new monsters called "a black ravenger" had replaced them. They look like the other ravengers from Kaesora (yes, the little spermy things with teeth) except they were black. Note that the spelling "ravenger" is intentional, as the Kaesora ones are called "ravener".

They were red to me at 52, and yellow to a 57 warrior in my guild, who accidentally drew their agro and was subsequently shredded by them. They apparently hit for around 200-250. They do not see invis, however. Out of curiosity I put up camo, ran into the cave where Dugroz is supposed to be, but he wasn't there. Instead there were three black ravengers. I looked on track for Dugroz, and as I had suspected, they moved him somewhere else in the zone. He now spawns just outside of the froglok city toward the center of the zone.

I asked around a bit, and from what I heard, some guild fought them for 2 hours without a single drop coming off them, not even a common. Interesting. These things came into the cave, killed the Iksars who guarded the TT zone, and don't drop anything. My guess is that they haven't been itemized yet, but I think they must be a quest of some kind, or else a rare spawn must come off of them.

The Iksar corpses, when targetted, make your target field blank, and when conned, they con green and return " regards you indifferently", with no name showing up. They also show up as blank spaces on my track list. There are about 14 ravengers, however, many of which spawn somewhat close together.
