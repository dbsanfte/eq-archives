Jinheim
Use invis and you can run past 90% of the dungeon. The cubes see through invis, and there are ghosts which require IVU. Run to the named you found and hail him. He's labelled on the eqtoolbox maps. Then, run past a few more rooms, go down a hole, swim west out of water. Turn left and go to the southmost room. It is labelled "stonemite room" on eqtoolbox maps. Use levitate to get up on the ledges above the water. Pull the stonemites one at a time. Leave the last one alive and med up to full and make sure everyone has all the buffs they need. Once you kill the last one, the named spawns. He is unslowable but doesn't hit too hard. Time geared tanks have no trouble tanking him at all as long as the cleric isn't asleep, so I imagine any group with a decent tank and a competent cleric + blast healer can kill him. Once you kill him, go back to the scribe and say "what issue" then talk to high priest again.

Henora, there is no possible way to get into the aged stonemite room without invising past those last set of stonemites. They just respawn the instant that you kill them. The reason that you didn't see the Aged Stonemites is because you got the quest from Gamesh. Gamesh is used solely for exping purposes. If you want to see the aged stonemites, then get the expedition from the High Priest. He is the one that starts the flag progression in GoD. He starts you off with going to the plant. I can't confirm this as true at the time, but I think that if you go into the sewers through HP, then you won't see the named mobs up that can drop the items/spells. At least, i don't recall ever having seen one in the bunch of times I've been in there. Most of the time I asked someone where they got their expedition from, it has always been Gamesh.

Remember that there are four different sewer zones. There is Plant, Crematory, Lair, and Pool. Plant is easily the hardest, it's where the mobs hit double 1200s. More than likely your friend was in another sewer zone. I know that the Crematory especially starts off with 300 hitters and slowly progresses up to about 700 - 800s. Most mobs in all of these zone are fully slowable too.
