veeshanMurtak
70 gets you some amazing AAs and straight numeric bonuses that you do not see but which are clearly there - you resist more, get resisted less, get hit less and so on.

Of the AAs you listed only VP is not a lesser version of just leveling. So grinding AAs for LR is not worth it compared to levels. Grinding AAs for VP and other abilities you can not get merely by leveling might.

Ok, that is for the straight up "what gives me the most power over the next level/next 5 AAs?" answer. If you are interested in long term benefits of gaining AAs at a sweet spot it gets more difficult. Personally I don't think it is worth it to stay lower level to gain more % per kill. Odds are you will be getting experience at least as fast at higher levels due to being able to kill the same things faster/for less mana or to kill similar things that are higher level at more or less the same speed.

Depending on what zones you have access to and how much you duo/solo/group that may vary some. Generally speaking though I do not know personally anyone who has gained AAs faster at lower levels. There may be the odd person who did but overall AAs come faster the higher level you get.

Now after all this rambling my advice is: If you have any game altering abilities that fit your playstile (Canni 5, Spiritcall, VP, Radiant cure) left get them now, maybe while having a little experience going to levels. Then level to 70, possibly stopping to get your 9th spell gem along the way.

Alternatively set your experience to 50/50 and get some AAs while leveling. I recommend getting new abilities rather then numeric bonuses. If you are going to take the scenic route (slow and probably not optimal) you might as well get enjoyment out of it and new abilities are usually more fun then slight number increases to old ones.
