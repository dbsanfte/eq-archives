I can't believe people still believe that the slugworms/trean flies effect the quality of the focus....lol

but seriously, whether you need slugworms or trean flies is solely dependant on what kind of stone you are trying to add an effect to.

post above me says it, Trean Flies for gemstone augs (Citrine, Titanite, Tourmaline), Slugworms for flowstone augs (Voidflow, Bloodflow, Mudflow)

what kind of inlay you have makes the effect on the aug greater. Black Gold Inlay for Focus 5 effects, White Gold Inlay for Focus 4 effects. Black Gold Inlays are made with "Discordant Scoriae", White Gold Inlays are made with "Dust of Discord".
