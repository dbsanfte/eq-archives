Methinks you'd find some pleasant surprises if ye check out the aforementioned list of recipes: here (http://www.eqtraders.com/secrets/recipes_alchemy.htm). The Greater Purity potion is available as both Minor Aura of Purity and Aura of Purity. There is a potion of gate to Cabilis, called Potion of the Bone Field. I strongly suspect that the Innothule potion is meant to serve both Ogguk and Grobb...it's only one zone away from Feerott, after all. (And yes, the Innothule one dumps you in the shadowman camp, but if you're high enough to be able to afford those things, you should have ways of escaping the shadow men....)

I had not yet started alchemy when the revamp occurred (see A Brief History of Alchemy (http://www.eqtraders.com/learn/learn_alchemy4.htm) for more on that) but given the speed with which people tested all possible combinations for the new Luclin herbs when they were released, I would be very surprised if similar tests were not run at that time.

Not, of course, to discourage any further explorations...by all means, test away! You never know what may have slipped through the cracks or been stealth-added since. But given the cost of the herbs in question and the considerable time that's passed for others to have run tests of their own, I think the chances of finding anything new are slim.
