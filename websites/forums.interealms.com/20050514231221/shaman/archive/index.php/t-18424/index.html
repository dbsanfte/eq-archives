If you don't raid, then you do not need malos AT ALL. It is by far the least important rune spell...if you have malo. Malo is easier to channel, cheaper to cast, works with focus III effects, and the difference in 99.99999% of XP situations between malo and malos is essentially nonexistant.

Q is the most important, easily. After that it does become a playstyle issue. If you solo a lot, BoS, if you group extensively, Fo7. Feral Avatar is good for soloing (on pet), and groups (on melees and pets), the issue there is how willing you are to put up with the recast. You might want to not consider looking for FA until after you have SCR3 and/or SCRM...an extended enhancement IV item wouldn't be too far out of line either.

Jaidin
I don't understand why is Q important for raiding?
Why do you want Q if you are soloing in the face? (specially since supposedly you proc more with torpor than q)..
Why is Q important if you are in regular xp groups and don't need to heal much. (tnargs > q in this case).

I only use q when I canni on raids (majority of the time)... and Q is nice when you are worried root will break soloing and want to run like hell..

Q allows a shaman to become a healing station for anyone in the raid. Druids do not have a viable heal over time to use to counter ae's and such and they may likely be busy direct healing tanks. Q enables a shaman to very easily keep a rage tank alive for many lower PoP and pre-pop encounters.

While soloing, the difference between torpor and Q isn't huge if you plan on tanking all the time and never moving. However, it's not practical to tank many xp mobs in PoP solo and you do not want to be snared when moving away from said mobs. Also, if you are meleeing at 61+ while soloing...uh...ya

In xp groups, Q makes it very easy for a shaman to be the *only* healer in tier3 and below PoP zones, or even tier4 with a huge amount of aa as well :) Basically Q makes it so you don't need to use direct heals in xp groups unless you have a huge pull. Also, in adventures where mobs and players typically move around a lot, you won't be snared while you and your groupmates are moving around.

However good you think torpor is for you, Q allows you to give that and more to anyone.
