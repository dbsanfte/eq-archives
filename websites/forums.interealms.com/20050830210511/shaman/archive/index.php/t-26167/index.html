Brohg
You can do zone-in solo at 52. Zone in with your pet up, Invis on, with Root memmed. There are two wanderers pathing in a circle around zone-in, they don't always agro immediately. Both are fully slowable and rootable, max hit around 200, with a couple thousand hp.

Once you kill those two, you pull the pairs of golems from North, West, and South doors. Root one, cast slow on the one that charges you. With the two wanderers, that's 8 mobs, plus you can do any number of wanderers downstairs that are easy to single-pull. It's a nice quiet place to get exp.

Entering the wings solo at that level is pretty much a no-no.
Hangurin Strongbear
If you find you have plenty of mana and time after the 8 entry mobs in HS, and if you get a sperm to drop the candle, go to the north wing. There's 4 mobs in that room that are single-pullable as long as you target the first mobs in the room. Fight them in the corner by the door and you should be good. However, once that room is cleared, DO NOT go up the steps...it is a one way drop off. Once the room is cleared, go back out to the entry and wait for respawn.
