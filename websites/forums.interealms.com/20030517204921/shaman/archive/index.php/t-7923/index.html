Fighting from the back right corner of the room helped my attempts because it kept people from running around wasting time getting to mobs and kept the mobs nice and condensed for switching. Anything that puts seconds back on the clock is great.

I slowed very little and used PR nukes to finish mobs. Kraggs was great for the last fight too.
Patito
I think Ice Strike is better to use here than Blast of Venom. BoV is 680 dmg for 340 mana, while IS is 675 dmg for 250 mana. I've very rarely had an Ice Strike get resisted there. If you can, get some mage focus items before the trial.
Aruman
1. AE Slow ONE time after all the mobs are bunched. This gets aggro on you and reduces the number that jump the cleric when they heal. Don't waste the mana for another cast.

2. Cast ONE Torrent of Poison or Tears of Saryn, whichever you have.

3. Assist the melee assigned as MT and hit each mob with ONE or at most 2 faster cast DD. Blast of Venom works nicely.

4. On the 4th mob of each wave, cast Blast of Venom once and then start regaining mana.

On the final wave(4th) try to be as stingy as you can with mana, you will need it to help heal. Only nuke when it looks like one of the mobs might not die fast enough. Be ready for the Boss.
