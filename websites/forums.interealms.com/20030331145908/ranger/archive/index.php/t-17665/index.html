Muvandog
I grinded about 30 AA in CoD, because it was always so empty, it was good for PL/2-box at the zone. Getting a little more crowded now on 7th, but still rarely any groups beyond the zone-in. XP is decent, most mobs are level 60, some unslowable "Greater" versions of the mobs which are level 61 & have so-so item drops. You will get a rare ethereal parch but you will never see a spectral parch XPing in this zone.

BoT was.. really cool when I first got in. Normal mobs range from 60-64, can rarely drop spectral parch and stones for making some jewelcraft items. Named (64-66) can (could, at least) drop runes, ornate armor patterns, weapons, spectral parch. Problem is on 7th the "race for the named" often turns this zone into a ****hole of people training & claiming wings, and just having a general disregard for fellow players. I kinda dread going there now, and probably wouldn't ever choose to XP there, but I still go race for the named sometimes.

Hands down my favorite XP zone so far is HoH. Nice big spacious zone with many snarable and non-summoning mobs that hit like my grandma when slowed. Specs & decent items drop rarely off XP mobs. Invis works in most places so you can run around and explore.
