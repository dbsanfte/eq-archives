Hail,

I'm not active in the weapon acquisition market myself, but I try to stay abreast of general pricing.

I have a DE on my twink cleric. I believe I paid ~800pp several months ago. Since then, prices have fallen. I would guesstimate anywhere from 500-1kpp would be an accurate price.

The Jade Prod generally sells for about 1kpp if memory serves, generally for use in the HS key quest, not for combat use.

I personally would recommend a REIF (what I use now) if you come into some cash, or a Slime Coated Harpoon, Darksea Harpoon, or Howling Harpoon as alternative piercers if you don't.
Slimey pricing has really fallen lately, down to 300-500pp on E. Marr.

The Howler works great in range slot (6wis 40mana) and can be switched quickly to primary for the stun proc against casters. Mine is sitting in the bank since I now have a Gantru Charm in range. (AC+resists > stun proc/~50mana extra)

I never worked any combat skills seriously except for piercing and defense (which I regret), so I don't look at shaman blunt weapons often. Shaman 2hb's seem to be priced really low on my server, as I think most shaman (like myself) failed to keep up their melee skills in more than one area. So you may want to keep that up too. Most priest 1hb go to clerics or druids, and I usually decline to be included in the rolls unless they are shaman only.
