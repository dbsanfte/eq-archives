Shandy Orcbane
Alteration if you're planning on grouping, or having any sort of a life around other people (or even a partner). The mana savings here will far outweigh Conjuration.

However, if you solo more than 75% of the time, the mana savings between Alteration and Conjuration begin to balance out. If you solo over 90% of the time (and rarely buff friends, etc) then Conjuration becomes a viable alternative. Bear in mind, though, that you're still buffing yourself and your pet. Alteration specialty as soloer is still very effective. Only if you solo 95% or more and completely stop buffing other folks does it truly even out.

BUT, if you solo a lot, and are looking for mana savings NOW, the hell with down time, Conjuration can be your friend.

What are you going to cast more often? Just as this can change, so can you choose to respecialize (a minor nuisance) (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=711) if you decide that you want to try something different.
