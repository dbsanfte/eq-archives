Bih'Li is not in FV/OT. It is sold in Shadowhaven and Shar Vahl.

In Shadowhaven, find the room with the alchemy and fletching vendors. In the back of the room there is a symbol on the ground. Walk onto it. You will be teleported to a room with a tree in it. Around that room a rooms with Shaman, Druid, Ranger, and Beastlord vendors and trainers. To get back out, click on the tree and you will be sent back to the alchemy/fletching vendors.
