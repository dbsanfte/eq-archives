I've seen centi warhammers selling for as low as 50pp. That would be an excellent upgrade, so keep your eyes open for a bargain (usually 200-300).

I've seen bogling chief spear for sale pretty cheap. It drops in echo caverns on the easier side, so you might be able to bring a group and camp it. If not now, then perhaps in a couple levels.

I've seen crystal grinders selling for as little as 20pp. They're 9/27, much better than your current weapon also.

I'd go to the bazaar and use the search feature. right click on anything in your price range and take a look at the stats. Try for something with at least 1 damage to 3 delay ratio. If you get lucky, you might be able to find a 2 to 5 or better in your price range.
