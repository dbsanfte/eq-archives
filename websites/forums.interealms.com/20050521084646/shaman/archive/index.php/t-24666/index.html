Gloriana
Hey guys, I'm currently at the New Orleans Fan Faire. The server BOTB competition is going on and it's pretty fun. I'm typing on a non ergonomic keyboard and it blows, so apologies for any typos.

1. Pet Dual Wield
Our pet is going to be changed to innately dual wield! This came straight from Rytan during the spells/AA panel discussion. He also said our pet should have around 4200 hitpoints. Very good news!!

2. Pure Spirit
Rytan said it had come to his attention that Pure Spirit is not acting as intended. It should remove a detrimental effect without requiring 5-6 casts. Expect this spell to be fixed shortly!

3. Spirit of Might/Champion stacking
Should be fixed soon.

4. Dot aggro
I brought this up at the Guild Summit and I brought it up again at Fan Faire - Rytan says he's done a bunch of research and can't figure out why we are getting such terrible dot aggro. He said he has tried numerous scenarios to try to duplicate the aggro.

5. HOT/DOT crit messages
Rytan said he wasn't opposed to the concept of crit messages, but he said it would be difficult to implement. He said it would be really difficult to return the message to the caster, but it might be possible to have the crit messages displayed to the recipient.

6. Out of group shrink confirmation box
Rytan isn't opposed to the concept, but he says it's really difficult to implement.

7. Nadox not working out of group
By design, no group cures work out of group.

8. Endurance Regen
Shaman aren't likely to get any spell/AA to regen endurance, by design. Rytan said he didn't want melees to be dependent on a buff, so he built endurance regen into melee characters.

9. Pet melee range
Improved! Someone at the panel said that pet melee range had been tied to the "art" previously (and their tiny paws).

10. Group haste to match our single target haste
Rytan's only comment was "no comment" :(

11. Spiritual Channeling
I talked to Rashere and he says that SC shouldn't check to see if the shaman has mana and he's going to try to get that fixed.

12. Call of the Wild
Rashere also said that Call of the Wild is not supposed to return a player to their corpse without rez effects - expect that to be changed.

I want to be clear here, it was a very good conversation. We were in a room full of tons of people, so I was lucky to pester Rytan and Rashere about so many questions. There's still one more night of the Fan Faire, so I hope to still get a chance to ask Rytan about slow mitigation and improved healing.

Expect another update on Monday! (I get in super late on Sunday night).

I'm pretty sure that the dot aggro issue is actually a couple different factors or levels so to speak and not just 1 single thing.

1) Counter aggro. Primarily poison which hit shaman worst since we have few other options. Those that have been playing long enough know counters have aggro from when they added disease counters to slows and subsequently went in and changed disease counters to be less/no aggro.

Thats actually where tigir's got it's neg aggro mod from. If we assume that the neg hate added to it as accurate it's 200 aggro for 16 counters or about 12.5 aggro per counter. Not huge but still a source of a couple hundred points of initial aggro aggro.

Disease and curse counters are little to no aggro as far as I can tell. Many other dots for other classes are counterless. As I said this issue seems to be really only with poison dots.

2) Debuff aggro. This is one that doesn't effect shaman but does effect other classes. Things like druids flame lick line have ac debuff component which from what I can tell seems to generate a fair amount of aggro. Cripple seems to get it's aggro from the ac debuff and weapons like frostbringer and jagged onyx staff seem to generate aggro from there ac debuff proc.

3) Dmg delt. From testing performed by one member with an sk they found that the aggro generated was based on the dmg dealt and that Dot focus and crits generated additional aggro.

Initially this doesn't start off too bad compared to nukes. Both seem to generate about 75-80% of the spells dmg in aggro. Problem is that nukes aggro is constant. Resist, full dmg, focus or even critted for double dmg and it's all the same amount of aggro. Focus's and crits for dots on the other hand seem to increase aggro.


Personally I've pulled aggro off my roommate who's a well equiped warrior with just bane and blood of yoppa. He has a very solid aggro setup with a darkblade of the warlord and either a jagged onyx staff or a double augged epic 1.5 now.
