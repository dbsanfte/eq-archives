Buy a new hard drive. Set it to Master. Set your other HDD to Slave (I'm assuming you will install the new hard drive on the same IDE controller as the old one).

Partition and format the new hard drive. Install XP. Once XP is up and running, copy and paste everything you want to keep to the new hard drive. After that is finished, format your old hard drive.

That is probably the only way to save your data unless you are can connect to another PC over a local area network. If so you could share your hard drive and copy them over the network to the other PC.

Good Luck!

*edit* Since you say you're "terrible" at these sort of things, I'd recruit a friend that isn't. It's not that hard, but a mis-step can lose all your data.
