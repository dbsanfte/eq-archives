Gloriana
At the time the ancient spells first became available, level 60 was the highest level possible and the ancient spells were better versions of existing spells.

Ancient Feral Avatar > avatar and primal avatar
(+120 to stats vs. +100 to stats)

Ancient Scourge of Nife > Bane of Nife
(254 a tick for 7 ticks vs. 214 a tick for 7 ticks)

If you use Lucy to compare the spells, the ancient spells are superior to other spells available at that time. The ancient spells were intended to be rewards for big raiding guilds, dropping off targets like Lord Seru and VT named.
