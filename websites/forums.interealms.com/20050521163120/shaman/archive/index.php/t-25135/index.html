Here is how you win:

- Root with Petrifying Earth
- Cast Malos
- Cast Slow
- Cast Malosinia
- Cast Crippling Spasm(Cripple)
- Cast Putrid Decay

Get FM

- Re-root with Petrifying Earth
- Mem Breath of Wunshi, Breath of Ultor
- Cast Breath of Ultor, Breath of Wunshi in that order
- Immediately cast Spirit Call(not Advanced Spirit Call!)
- Mem Blood of Yoppa(if you have), Blood of Saryrn, Curse of Sisslak(if you have), Bane, Anathema(if you do not have Sisslak
- Cast as many as you can before the pets die
- Cast your best HoT on you when only 2 of the pets are left and be ready to get summoned
- Reapply all DoTs as they drop
- Keep the HoT up on you, if you can add epic or Ancestral Aid even better
The mob will be dead in no time.

This is for people who are a little undergeared and can't or don't want to tank for long.

RedChief
I've killed Ornia the Foul about a dozen times have yet to see a 69/70 rune. One thing about killing her is that you need to do it very fast from the initial pull. She is a caster and will spend a lot of time casting spells. During this time her melee is pathetic (ranger tanking her w/ no healing needed beyond regen). When she goes OOM then her melee dps really goes up. I would pull w/ Malos and do sinia asap. From there, start laying on the dots because she will move to you but stay out of melee range while casting some spells. When she does her pet, root/slow it. I would save pet swarms until after she starts getting low on mana.
