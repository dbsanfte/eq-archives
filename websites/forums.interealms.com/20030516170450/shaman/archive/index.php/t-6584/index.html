Yay, horray, it's OVER, thank god, etc etc etc :)

I got my toothpick of fate last night. It's shiny. It's flashy. It smells of catnip and has a nice feather.

I just thought I'd give you a report on the fight.

My attack crew took time to assemble. At first, it was just me, 60 shaman, a 60 mage without epic, 60 necro, and a 60 cleric. No KEI. We soloed reavers with ease, took down Ghiosk, and popped rak. Then we ran to the other side of the courtyard and brought down Neh.

At this point a 58 mage arrived, plus a 56 cleric, so we were now 2 mages, 2 clerics, necro and me. We took out Neh. She's doing some unusual tricks, compared to the fight we did on her 5 weeks ago. For starters, she mezzed me. Ouch. I was unable to do things for about 2 minutes. Then she healed herself. Not a cheal, but about a sup heal I think. If she wasn't doing it, then something was, we're not sure who, because her health would go up now and then. But she was beaten into submission, and dropped her diaries on cue, and, like clockwork, the torch for the mage epic needed by mage #2.

I think this took about 5 minutes. She also tashed me. Oh boy :)

This is the second time we've done neh for a shaman epic, and the second time she's dropped both the torch and the diaries. I turned in the diaries, dog humped my leg.

Then mage #3 arrived, 55 mage. We didn't have a cleric for her (miscommunication), but we got everyone ready for rak.

Mage #3 attacked rak... Ack! We all camped, except Mage #3 who died. Logged back in, rezzed mage #3, got everyone rebuffed and back into position. I did the turn in. Rak aggroed (thank gods, thank gods, thank gods). At this point, my autorun key got stuck (oh NO!) but the fire pets were engaged and even though I got pushed into the raid party, everything was fine. I got my client back under control... you know, this is the second CoM raid where this has happened, autorun being stuck on, it's very odd, and "c" would refuse to type unless I switched boxes in the UI.

I had about 150 fr, and I had about 60% health when I got out of there.

We had a pile of Mod-rods... yes, our last chance to use this tactic on Rak before Wednesday's nerf. I, still alive (yay!), healed the clerics while they chealed 2 of the 3 fire pets. the 3rd died quickly, and was resummoned 3 times because of the lack of healing on him, but the other 2, ageo'd and focused, survived the whole fight.

Rak died in about 8 minutes, IIRC. We ran out of mod rods at the very end... gulp, mages and I OOM... but he died :) Dropped the scale and the MOSS.

And here's my last bug from this buggy epic from hell....

I turned in my scale.

"You have gotten experience!"
But no text message, nothing about the story. Now Kirn gave me his text, so did Neh and Rak, so this is the first story no-show. I checked my inventory... thank god, I have a spear of fate. :)

So I feel a bit let down about the no text in the ending, but I'm glad it's all over. I ran into bugs on the test of patience, test of wisdom, had to do kirn 3 times do to his pet being up, lead a failed raid of 30 people to their death in CoM on our first ever reaver raid... and then had the resist changes on me... I was so scared of Neh and Rak being broken :) Luckily, they worked, even if the dog didn't. No, I wasn't invis for the turn in, either of them :)

I'm very happy about my new toothpick :)

Thank you all for your help, your answers, your encouragement, even when I wanted to give up on this buggy trip from hell :)
