Slurm
Softcap is a misnomer.

There is no point at which AC stops having a function, but there is a point of diminishing returns. That point seems to be where your AC meets the mobs ATK value. This varies from mob to mob, but is relatively consistant within regions. The average ATK value for NPCs has increased with each expansion, so (for example) an 800 ac may get you to the point on most mobs in kunark, vs 1000 in velious, or 1200 in luclin, etc.

Disclaimer: the above does not take into account avoidance ac vs mitigation ac, nor class specific skills and modifiers. The discussions on the warrior and monk boards go into these details and are rather math intensive if you are truly interested.

I have a raid buffed AC of 1710 and was worried about hitting the softcap if one exists in PoP.

I spoke with a warrior in my guild at length about the idea of a pop softcap - with an ac of around 2k he *still* sees a difference when he groups with a bard playing AC song - so estimates going around are that it might be in the 2200-2300 range (or who knows might be higher).

His opinion is backed up by tests run, here's just 1 example:
Last round of AC tests for me are in (http://steelwarrior.xwarzone.com/showthread.php?threadid=145)
Same mob - Level 65 A Sentry of Ro, Sol Ro zone in.

1694 AC (Unbuffed) = 329.4 Average "hit"
1815 AC (Unbuffed) = 311.7 Average "hit"
1917 AC (Unbuffed) = 295.0 Average "hit"
2112 AC ( Buffed ) = 288.6 Average "hit"


Just for reference:
1694 AC = 666 hits, 219374 total damage from hits
1815 AC = 813 hits, 253380 total damage from hits
1917 AC = 507 hits, 149544 total damage from hits
2112 AC = 752 hits, 217004 total damage from hits


Alot of the argument for AC "caps" really depends on the mobs that you're fighting (within a given expansion). If you're tanking mobs in Plane of Disease, then you might not see much of a difference between 1500 and 1700 AC - whereas if you're tanking Plane of Fire castle mobs, you will.

Shaman soft-cap is probably lower than pure melee but I dont think its anything that we need to worry about hitting anytime soon - speculation for sure but I've yet to see much evidence to the contrary.

Anyways to summarize in basic speakage - it all depends what you're fighting. Generally for the hardest mobs that you can xp on in groups or solo, or for raid trash that we as shamans can actually tank (with healers/without) - we are nowhere close to the point where AC will cease to make a difference.
