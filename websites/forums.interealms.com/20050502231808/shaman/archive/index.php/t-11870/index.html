Solariis
Their pets are just as good as mages except they lack a DS to slap on them. At 60 they have a 50% slow to back you up in case you get in trouble and a 65% slow at 65. Their mana and hp regen combo line helps a lot with cutting back downtime. They can melee or play pet cleric with their effecient pet heals. They have weak nukes and don't get a good dot till 61 so most of their damage is melee oriented.

Downsides are that they can't debuff resists like a mage, can't root nor bind anywhere like we can which can be inconvenient. For instance if you are duoing in some faraway place and you get some bad luck adds and both have to gate the best he can do is have a thurg gate potion or the talisman thingy which gates him back to PoK making for a lenghty trip back whereas a mage and you can bind somewhere nearby. Beasties also can't summon muzzles and 150pt procing weaps for your pet like a mage :)

Overall they are good partners but share a lot of the shaman spells so they don't complement us as well as a mage, enchanter or necro do, IMHO.
