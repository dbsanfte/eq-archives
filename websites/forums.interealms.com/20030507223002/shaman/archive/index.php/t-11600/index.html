Although i only pretty much solo in Plane of Fire currently, when i use to solo in HOH, i figured out a trick to splitting mobs while in the Trail area.

I simply pull one dude with slow, root him, train the other about 100 feet away, root him and then camp out.

When you come back, they will both still be rooted, but you will have no aggro, so you simply just engage the one closest to you, and the other one will eventually return back to its spawn point.

There are two very good spots for shamans in fire, the first is just in front of the two birds on the bridge (don't do this if they are up). Pull the lvl65 mobs from the castle, 11% aa exp per kill and they have pretty low hp.

The second spot is past fennin trigger on the ramps there. Pull the red con guards from the area near fennin trigger and root - rot on the ramps. Yields 18% aa exp per kill but they have a bit higher hp than the even cons, approx 40-45k hp so every mob takes around 6 min. Fennin trigger have to be down of course.
