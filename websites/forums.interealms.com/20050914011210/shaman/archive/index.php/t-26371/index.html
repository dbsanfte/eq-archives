Death Somes Swiftly
38 crystals
Average time 40 - 55 minutes depending on group
healer, slower, dps, dps, dps, solid tank (in other words make a group and a shaman can heal in here w/ some grunting - cleric or druid makes it a happier group)

Kill 10 crevice spiders
Kill 5 warder spiders
Kill Lava mistress

Zone in

Clear large room in front of you by pulling to zin. With tank pulling, you can expect a few 2 pulls (pumas, leopards, crevice spiders). OT or if the tank is hp heavy (11K above), have them hold agro on both.

Move to the right up the ramp and hold.

Clear up to next level area. Same pulling - some 2 pulls.

Pull up to the first cave entrance on the right (archway with 2 spiders guarding). Pull to entrance until you can move to the big area.

Clear this room.

At this point you now have either completed the 10 crevice spiders (or are close like 8) and a couple of warder spiders. This room will hold the rest of the spiders.

When cleared, move into the room. The Lava Mistress is in small alcove on the right. Pull her out of the room (zone out is there and you can accidently get zoned). Has a small annoying dot. Fixed with RGC or 2 blasts of Nadox.

Kill Lava Mistress - receive token and crystals.

Move into the Lava Mistress alcove. On the right cave wall is a dark looking area (like it should be a tunnel but isn't). Invis up ... Click on dark area .. zone out. Or TL to Lavastorm, sell, split, turn in token and repeat process.

My static group has farmed this enough so that I currently have 4 of the focus aug's that I wanted. Take friends, catch up on news, joke, enjoy. This one is a snap.

Now someone give us a new one to enjoy. We have done a good portion of them, but find ourselves coming back to this one for the ease of grabbing 38 crystals. Creator is ok - but overall takes more time.
