A week ago I found Ancient Scourge of Nife for sale in the bazaar. I have never seen this spell for sale on my server (FV) and I check the bazaar for spells OFTEN. I hastily ran to the bank to gather my coin only to discover that I was short of the asking price by several thousand platinum. I quick phone call to my father in law got me to the 20k asking price. Seconds later I had the spell in my book. I was still on the phone with my father in law and he asked me what other spells I was missing. I told him of the few that I had interest in and he said he would let me know if they ever showed up for sale.

Yesterday I got a phone call at work from my wife. She was all giddy because her dad had just found Pox for sale in the bazaar. This was all well and good but there was no way I would be able to buy it. I was in debt 12k due to my last spell purchase so no Pox for me. I wasn’t too unhappy. I figured it would turn up again.

A short time later I get another call from the little lady, this time she tells me that her dad bought the spell for me. “Oh great” says I, thinking that now I’d be 30k in debt. Oh how wrong I was. It seems good ol’ dad spent 60k for the spell. He sold his BP and his Lodi shield to finance it. While I am very grateful for his selflessness, I am very unhappy at the prospect of spending the next 2 months in debtors prison farming plat to raise the 72k that I now owe.

So hear is my quandary; Is the spell worth 60k? Should I scribe it or try to sell it to get my father in law his gear back? If I scribe it where is the best place to farm plat for me (62 shaman) and my wife (61 warrior). I was thinking Seb. She and I can normally make about 1200 plat an hour there. More if we are lucky. But good grief, it’s going to take at least a month to raise the coin that way.

Help me out folks, which road to I choose?

Thanks,
Ice.
