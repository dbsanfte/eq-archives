I just wanted to post something real quick so that at least WE know what is going on as shammies :)

Every single slow spell in the game that players cast EXCEPT Cload of Grummus and Plague of Insects is resisted by MAGIC.

Seems almost like I need to ask an Admin GM to spam the serverwide announcements with this one somedays :P



Ever since they added disease counters to the spells so that you can cure slow without dispelling buffs, I get pestered by people for disease buffs cause "I keep getting slowed." I used to think it was just other classes not knowing the game mechanics that WE are kings of. No problem there IMO, I don't mind covering for them.

But lately I have noticed that a lot of shaman who were lowbies when the change happened grew up listening to those people and believed them :*(. So, for the record, DISEASE COUNTERS are not the same as disease RESISTED. They are something to let you cure a spell, not to let you resist it.



So, cure disease, Blood of Nadox and other such disease curing spells will remove slow, however disease resist buffs will not help you to resist slows any more than Malicious/Insidious Decay will help you land them.

If you see someone who isn't clear on this in game, please try to take the time to inform them before they confuse another beginning shammie :)
