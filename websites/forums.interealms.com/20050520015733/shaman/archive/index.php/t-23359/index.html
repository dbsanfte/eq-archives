Blazz
There are lots of threads about the AA path for shaman of all styles of play, from "mostly solo, never raid" to mostly group, raid sometimes" to "mosty raid, rarely solo" styles.

Some of my favs:

VP3 - the bomb for both soloing and grouping. At level 3, it will last 5 seconds longer than the refresh time, meaning when it lights up, the mob will break in 5 seconds. Many times you need to de-buff disease for the full time, but Mal Decay is a simple spell to get, casts fast, and is un-resistable.

LR5 - Very noticable differene in the times I get hit when summoned. Sometimes on "lesser" raids I like to play a game with my group called "Lets see who can get summoned first". heh. Nothing like having a quadding mob miss 3 of 4 some rounds cause you have LR5

ND3 - Hitpoints = good, especially with canni 5. You DO NOT want to have Canni 5 put you into "low health aggro" if you can at all help it. Also, naked, with self buffs and ND3, I can canni 5 and not die! THATS a bonus by itself, cause you can have lots of fun freaking people out at your bind point by going to 1% heh.

SCRM - longer buffs means less memming and re-memming.

RC3 - nasty thins like Malo, Tash, and most poisons and disease stuff can be cured with this wonderful AA. The trees in Earth, for example, have REALLY brutal snare thing. I have saved TONS of pullers by running out near them and RCing the snare off before the tree catches up and waxes them.

MGB - as a raider, this is a must. Most raid level guilds require it, as well they should. I really wish we had more than 1 buff to use it on these days though (well 2, since I am playing in areas where we generally MGB ToT as well as Focus). It should be fixed to work on Shrink, with a confirm box like TL, and I wish it worked on Blood of Nadox. We really have nothing "Battle Friendly" to MGB currently, but it looks like OOW is giving us something. I do know that you can almost NEVER have too many druids on a raid vs big AE bosses, because SotW is SO froakin amazing. And the more BLs who can Paragon can REALLY bring back the HPs and Mana when a raid is on the edge. Those 2 MGBs alone can make or break some events.

I know I forgot a bunch, but these ones are cool. Another cool one, believe it or not, is Innate metabolism 3. Food lasts forever, and since there aer so many cool +stat foods (I use Misty Picnics and Kaladim Constitutionals) it saves you a ton of cash and time, not to mention a couple inventory slots. No need to carry more than 1 stack of each, since it lasts so long. And if you use a mount its EXTREMELY noticable.
