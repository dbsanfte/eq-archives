RaR!!!

I became member of the Oracles last night while soloing in PoI. My first 60 char despite haveing 4 or 5 over 50 in the past 3years. That was easily the most anticipated Ding I've ever had, I even turned the speakers up to an obnoxious volume at 5am before the end of the last 59th fight. I thought it was and would forever be my happiest EQ moment. Then I scribed Torpor which had been in my bag since I was 57. I reorganized my spells and pulled another mob. This fight quickly replaced my ding as my happiest EQ moment.

***?!?!?!

How can 1 friggin spell make that much of a difference to a class? I'm not complaining since I have it but Jesus H Christ on rubber crutchs! It isnt even fair or right or anything justifiable, but it sure as hell is fun :D. When soloing some of the tougher PoI Ent mobs at 59, I was ending the fight on average with 60% health and mana (I like to keep them even in most cases). But after my transition into the world of Super Shaman, I ended Every fight full health and mana, and was using more mana, blasting and tanking the whole time. The difference is just sick, and I couldnt be happier about it. I've gained a whole new love for my shaman, Cana5 here I come :).
