Viken
Every two minutes the server checks to that these two conditions are met:

a) Zone-wide "chanting" emote
b) Dark Masters are dead

If those two condition are met then Doomshade will spawn. Respawn on the DMs are ~15 minutes. The zone-wide emote starts occuring 0 - 12 hours after a patch, stops when Doomshade spawns, then starts three days later (regardless if Doom was killed or not). Doomshade poofs two hours after being spawned.
