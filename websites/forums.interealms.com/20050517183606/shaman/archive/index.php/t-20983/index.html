Hya,

I am playing a druid / necro duo. This duo is quite fun in outdoor but very limited indoor and quite hard to play.

The 14 min snare is invaluable with a necro. But you need ton of space and ton of skill to dual box using Agro kiting in end game. Fear kiting is easier but fear is limited after 60.
Mana regen on druid is a problem until 55+ if you cant afford or find a KEI. After 55, the lack of slow vs living mob start to hurt. But you can live without it using agro kiting method or Charm.
Druid Teleportation is really nice and i use it all the time.
Plus, Necro still the best farmer of the game.

If i would reroll i would probably choose a Necro/Shaman instead of my actual necro/druid. With Pok you can live without Teleportation but you cant live without slow.

In my opinion Necro/Shaman is the best duo in EQ. High DPS, 2 pets, Mana regen on both, Summon corpse, 93% Rez, Ton of buffs, Haste, Slow, Ton of dots and DD, Resists debuffs, Fear, Snare, Some tanking abilities with the shaman + 2 pets. You cant find better.

Followed by SK / Shaman in a close second with less DPS but better tanking abilities.

But if you really want to try something different on both account then i suggest :
1) BST/CLERIC (easy to multi box and always in demand in group. Cleric hp buffs on both PET + BST help a lot)
2) BST/Druid (better DPS but less defensive)
3) Necro/Druid (A challenge to play but fun and powerfull. Less powerfull than necro/Shaman)

Chanty/Druid is a mistake in my opinion. To wait 60 lvls to shine is way to long.

Goldark
Depends a bit what you want to do, but to be capable of fighting in a variety of places you would want

My favoured classes excluding the monk/shaman are probably beastlord, druid, necro, SK.

Beastlord brings slows+dps, and paragon at high levels is great.
Druid brings healing, porting, buffs, charm, and respectable dps
Necro is just extremely versatile, and against undead can slow/cc. At high levels their health and mana taps are significant.
SK is imo the bset choice of the tanking classes. Summon corpse, snare, FD, taps, HT... all good.

Which combo of those to take is tricky.

Beast/Necro could be real fun but limited in some areas. Massive DPS, slows, rez, fd, snare, mana regen... lacks big heals and tanking ability

SK/Druid probably gives the most flexibility but you miss any slowing abilty. Druid charm is a big factor later on.

SK/Beast has some healing (bst gives paragon + small heals, SK lifetaps, and bst pet heals too), slow, tanking ability and good dps. Drawback is lack of decent heals for tough mobs or oh crap! situations.

I quite like the druid + enchanter combo but if they nerf charm further then you're a bit stuck.
