Dissonant
My thoughts aren't terribly organized here, so bare with me, small rant inc.

Why is everyone so concerned about wisdom all the time? The only reason people care about wisdom is that it gives us mana. Why not cut out the middle man and go straight for mana?

I'm not saying that wisdom isn't important at all, but it is at MOST the 3rd, and imho the 4th stat that you should worry about in an equipment upgrade.

HP > Mana > Sta > Wis

With Cann5 and Quiessence(Torpor is just as good) I can only think of a few times that I've gone OOM in grinds/raids except when I've been buffing or when I get rezed in(obviously). With a shaman's ability to regen mana, there is absolutely no reason to focus your gear around mana/wis items.

Some of you are gonna say, that it's different for me cause I'm 65 with 2.7k hp and 3.9k mana with FT11, and up untill recently, I might have agreed with you. But now, I'm slowly seeing the light. Ditch that crappy +wis gear. Base your gear around overall HP and Mana increases, with HP being primary of course. Use magelo, it works great for figuring out what would be viable upgrades. The only beef I have with magelo is that you can't incorporate buffs, which can skew some of your end results because of hard/soft caps.

Finally.... the next shaman I see using a Lodizal that isn't swimming or using it in a +resist situation, i'm gonna slap.

I'd like to hear input from other shaman, high level and low level. I'm I way off base? Does this make sense?

I mean, think about it, if you are like me, I tend to buff every person that it's my group that asks for it with whatever they want (bar Avatar, and I don't happen to volunteer that I have that spell). When it comes down to it, with low wisdom and low mana items, you will only be able to perform one function, and that is to slow the mob. I enjoy being able to perform more than one function. I like being able to say that I can tank a mob if slowed and if it's needed to enable the group to concentrate on the other mob in camp...especially if it happens to be non rootable. I enjoy being able to hear someone tell me that "Hey, you tank better than some tanks that I know" or "Hey, you heal better than some clerics I know".
