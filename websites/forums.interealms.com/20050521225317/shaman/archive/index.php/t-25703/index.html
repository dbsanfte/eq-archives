Hi,

First post here, but felt i had to reply to the above poster on two boxing.
I two box a 70 necro with 350 and a 68 shammy with 125 aa, my gear isn't great at all but have both 1.5's. necro has like 7 k hps buffed normally and we can kill summoning mobs no problems with a tiny bit of space.

The thing is you don't use the shammy to tank. Necro pulls with lowest damage snare as you do not want mob to fall below 98 pct when it starts summoning. Root it, malo it, reroot it, spam some more snares for agro, slow it, crippling spasm it, crippling claudication it, spam some more snares at it, root it again, unleash dog dogs, army of the dead, send pets in, activate both epics and stuff like ancestral aid, grip of mori it then commence lifetapping the named into oblivion whilst spamming necro with heals.

Key to success is fast killing and at present with plenty of kiting corpses around i can spam the mob with 13 pets. Can kill any named in Rcod with no problems at all now, usually don't fall below 50 pct. You just have to be quite precise and not engage until fully debuffed which is no problems with malo and root as the mob won't summon till your dps goes in (took a lot of deaths working out what i think is a good strategy). Going to try and duo some mpg named soon. Anyhow point of the post is that necro/shammy combo is actually one of the best combo's for a lot of named imho. Sure there are others but we can hold our own with equipment that leaves much to be desired.

Butogz
Necro and Shaman is probably one of best duo combinations I can think of other than a druid & enchater charming duo. I duoed a lot with a 70 necro before she server transfered, and it was some of the funnest times I have had in EQ.

Once mob is slowed necro pet can easly tank most any "one groupable mob" (we would fully buff 2 pets and suspend one before we took on harder named). If it is a bit to much for the pet even when slowed the necro can life tap FD flop tank wonderfully. Leaving corpses for wake the dead is godly. This is expecially easy to do for all the BoT named since you can clear tons of fast kill giants giving corpses and makeing a huge clear fighting area. Also in griegs end it is so easy to make fast large corpse piles before duoing the servitor of luclin.

We never did any WoW camps before she transfered, but as a solo Shaman I can take down NC named with ease, and RCoD with some effort. But with a necro FD pulling RCoD and the added insane DPS that a necro brings to the table, you could get both characters nicely geared (or super rich) and all spells 66-70 in no time.

Also, when we did experiance grinding in PoFire we could kill the mob before VP3's duration ran a single course, which means we could get 2 blue AA every 1.5-2 minutes without going below 80% mana. Which is another point to make, Necro and Shaman have the best mana regen in the game so down time for this duo is almost null.

I say do the duo!
