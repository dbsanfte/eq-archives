Michael J. Kaspar
If you kill one mob you get X exp.
If you kill one mob with a friend in group you get half of X exp.
If you kill one mob with 2 friends in group you get one third of X exp.

...and so on.

To maximize exp, you want to find the highest rate of kills with the fewest number of group mates. For example...

Solo you kill 10 things in 20 minutes.
With one partner you kill 22 things in 20 minutes.
With two partners you kill 28 things in 20 minutes.

Here, the optimum group is the second... work with one partner. Adding more people may make it easier, and provide a cushion if a pull goes bad, but in the long run it hurts exp. rate.

Furthermore, hunt indoors if at all possible. The exp bonus from dungeons adds up quickly.

Good dungeons to two man...

SolB.
Guk.
Chardok.
CC and ToFS.
Hole (near the top).
Areas of Permafrost.
