McRoc -
yes, to buy Bane, or anything else, you will have to earn enough adventure points at the camp to buy it.
good threads on that elsewhere, but a brief recap:


You have a cap on how much you can spend on any one item. That cap is the number of adventure points you have earned (lifetime) at that camp. you can spend points that were earned elsewhere, but you cannot spend more than you have earned there.


So - if you want to power up to Bane, and ignore the spells for detecting and disarming traps, etc., then you would want to do all of your adventures at the camp where you plan to buy Bane.

If you spend any points on cheaper items/spells, then you could finish raising your cap and then go "replenish" your points at another camp if you wanted. Eventually you will have fairly high caps at ll of the camps, and can spread your adventuring around based on other criteria.
