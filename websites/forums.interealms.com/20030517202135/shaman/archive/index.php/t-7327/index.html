Belinor
Its the EQUI and EQAnimations files together causing the problem. They added a few new items to EQAnimation on the 21st and then added the two new lines to the EQUI on the 31st. If you can, download a copy of Beyond Compare (Scooter Software (http://www.scootersoftware.com/))and run a side by side comparison of the default copy of EQAnimation and your copy in your customized folder. Using Beyond Compare you can then just highlight the new sections from the default EQAnimation and copy them into your customized version.

You can then either edit your customized EQUI with the two new raidwindow entries or just copy the default one into your customized folder
