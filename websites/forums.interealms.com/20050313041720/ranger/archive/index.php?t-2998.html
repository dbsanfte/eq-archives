If you can do Velk/Lord Bob, you have a realistic shot at Vindicator. I know several Guilds have used him as training ground for Vindi. If you can do those mobs, go to Halls of Testing and get Skyshrine armor, farm it until everyone's eye's bleed and you are geared up.
Any old kunark dragon should be doable with a bit of strategy. 32k hitpoints isn't much anymore. Gorenaire might still be a stretch, she's a cast iron wench even these days, unless you cheat with Manaburn wizards. (Ok so I'm jealous of the 4 wizard nuke team :))

Any epic mob should be easily doable. I'm not sure on Luclin, haven't had a chance to play there much yet.

As far as a scale goes, I'm not 100% sure any would be accurate. Some mobs take more melee then wizards. Some require certain classes/levels to make it easier etc.

I know guilds that wipe doing things we consider easy and succeed on things my guild considers hard *shrug*

I tend to lean towards if you can handle this mob, you can probably handle these mobs and go from there.
