Harvyst
Four hours.

On my third hole try (his pet had been up but not him previously), we arrived at the hole and found through a mage's eye that Kirn not up. Another group said he had been killed an hour prior. We decided, on a whim, to try him anyway, as we might get lucky.

It took an hour for the raid to get organized. (It was a bad night). It took an hour and a half to get down to the tower. It took us another thirty minutes to fight our way up the tower. Just as we neared his room, we saw "High scale kirn begins to cast a spell."

It was four hours almost exactly since the first group killed him.

Needless to say, he died quicky, we were happy, and I got my ring.
