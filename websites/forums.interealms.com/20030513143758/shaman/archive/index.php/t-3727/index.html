I did this quest about 9 months ago, including the Orc Runner part. Though I once saw him around the entrance to Neriak (while playing another character, a couple years ago) I would recommend you look for him in Butcherblock. As you zone in from Greater Faydark, go left and sit on the wall before you get to the large tree. The Runner comes out from around the zoneline, goes to the big tree, and then turns back towards the main path.

I don't know of any way to make him spawn, but he appeared about every 30 minutes or so. I had to kill him three times before he dropped the quest piece (I can't even remember now, but I think it was a letter).

He is a very easy kill, and as I recall gives Indigo Brotherhood and Crushbone Orc faction hits (if that's important to you).

Hope this helps.
