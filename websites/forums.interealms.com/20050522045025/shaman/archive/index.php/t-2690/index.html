Azzikai
Once you're logged in you should be able to see an option on the left hand menu that says "characters". That gets you to a screen where you there should be a button that says "add a character", do that. You'll add in all your basic info etc into it and when you're done hit "add this character".

You'll now find yourself back at that same "add a character" screen but your newly added character will be listed too. At the bottom of your new characters "sheet" will be these links (gender specific of course):
Update this character
Manage her profile(s)
Manage her keyring
Manage her inventory
Manage her abilities
Manage her skills
Manage her languages

To add items to your character you need to click on the "profiles" link. That takes you to a page with all the slots listed and the word "[edit]", click on [edit] for any slot. You now have a drop down box, go through it and pick what you have in that slot then hit "update". Repeat that for everything. When you're done, click the little "this is my default profile" box, type a name into the "save current profile as" box and then update.

Now there should be that profile's name with some numbers in () under the "stored profile" box. As well as a link at the bottom of the page. You'll notice that link ends in the same numbers that are in the (). That's the link to your profile.

You can create numerous profiles for that character from this screen, just give them different names before updating. They'll all be assigned different numbers but be linked to one another. Meaning if you put your first profile in your signiture when someone clicks on it they'll see "resist", "future", "if I was a god" (or whatever other profiles you've also created) on the right hand side of the information box. It's kinda hard to explain but I think you know what I mean from looking at other people's magelos.

You basically do the same thing to add keys.

Binding stuff to your inventory is a bit different and a lot longer to explain. Once you get the basic profile done, mess around with the inventory part and you'll figure it out eventually :D
