Eolair Strongbo
Corenia, the people who make comments about rangers sucking are just people who don't know much about the class.

Up till the 30's and such, rangers can tank pretty much as well as any class out there. In fact, many mid 20's groups will take rangers over warriors simply to have a tank with utilities.

As you start getting into your 40's and 50's, you can't tank as well as SK's, Paladins, or Warriors, but you will outdamage them for the most part. Offensive nukes, better atk rating, and duel wielding will usually make it so you have a higher DPS than either of these 3. Unless they have some really sweet weapon, but that's not really a fair comparison.

When you get into your late 50's and 60's, and you get some AA's, you will never look back. A ranger with EQ and AM aa's will outdamage a warrior, paladin, or SK easier than you can say "Rangers Own You". And a ranger will pretty much always outdamage an SK, Paladin, warrior, melee wise, as long as you have somewhat equivalent weapons.

Rangers are NOT tanks. Comparing our tanking ability to a Warrior, Paladin, or SK, you might as well compare a rogue, or a bard. Rangers are really secondary damage dealers. We "can" tank if needed, but if you let us do our thing, we dish out lots of damage, have some nice utilities like snare, root for ghetto crowd Control, easy aggro management *gaining or losing it*

A good ranger can probably save a group in trouble better than any other melee class out there. We have our place, unfortantely some people just haven't learned what it really is and make stupid comments like, Rangers Suck.
