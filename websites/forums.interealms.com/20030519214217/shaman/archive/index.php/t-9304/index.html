if you are starting from level 1 with nothing, spend it all on a fungi if you can find one for that price on your server.

If you can afford it after, pick up a Rune Etched Icewurm Fang, a haste item like silver chitin handwraps and fill in the rest. If you can't afford a fungi then a iksar ceremonial chestplate is good too.

If you play an iksar you can't use jaundiced bone armor, so you will have to be a bit more creative than the standard for other races.
