The epic consists of 2 claws. Both called claw of the savage spirit.


Primary
13/22

effect: sha's vengeance (35% slow)

Secondary
13/22
5 str, 10 dex, 15 sta, 125 hp, 5 fire resist, 5 cold resist, 5 poison resist, 10 disease resist
effect: fury of the vah shir (90dd)
