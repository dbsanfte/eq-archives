Gloriana
Now that the day has finally slowed down a bit, I have a few other non shaman specific things I found out at Fan Faire:

1. SOE Guild Tools
I don't know if you guys have had a chance to check out the really cool Guild Management tool that comes with EQ 2. During one of the panels, a developer stated they were going to share that functionality with EQ 1 within the next 6 - 8 months. It is TOTALLY COOL.

Guilds will have website space, forums and a roster. All info will be automatically fed (4 hour feeds or daily feeds, depending) from SOE. Individuals can automatically update their character info (including a very cool Magelo like interface) and choose to share the info with anyone or just with their guildmates.

As items are discovered on servers, item info will be available (kinda like Allakhazam's). Also, to help improve the size of uber e-penises, the first person to discover the item will be listed.

I asked if there was any sort of limit on space and the guy said no, but he did say hosting movies would be too big. He also said that SOE would not be policing the forums (of course, any real life threats/harassment would not be tolerated) and anti - SOE posts were fine.

It was VERY cool.

2. GoD AA point reimbursement
Rashere said that there would be an AA reimbursement for some of the pricier GoD AAs. He said the person would still have the AA, they would just receive the AA difference in their AA bank (between the former price and the new price). This change is expected to go in by the big patch in early December. Rashere also said he is going to tune some of the AA abilities. I mentioned Rabid Bear and he said it was on his list to be looked at.

3. Fun Event in Dranik's Scar
Rashere told a bunch of us about a super cool event in Dranik's Scar. There is a named murkglider that spawns as a tiny little guy and he wanders around the zone. If he isn't killed, he gets hungry and eats things and gets bigger. The bigger he gets, his level and loot table changes until he ends up a raid group (3 groups, suitable for DS). Rashere says he turns GINORMOUS which I think is totally hilarious.

4. EQ 2 stuff
Saw a flying carpet, which was really fun looking. Argued with the EQ 2 guy that barb females are hideous with ugly upper lips and man hands, he liked em, go figure.

EQ 2 server names will not be released until launch date. EQ 2 server names will not use EQ 1 server names in order to facilitate cross game tells. The intention is that you will be able to send tells back and forth between the two games. They wanted to use the existing inter-server tells, for example ;tell erollisi.gloriana will work between all servers in EQ 1 and EQ 2.

5. Backflagging
Backflagging was a huge issue during the Guild Summit and came up at the Fan Faire. The plan is to extend the 85% rule to Time and some GoD zones. It may also be possible for "piggy backed" individuals in the zone to loot a quest item that will flag them for the zone.

6. Next Expansion
Everyone was totally hush hush about the next expansion, even Rashere after several amaretto sours wouldn't spill any info. I did hear someone whisper the word "dragons."

7. New models
Okay, this one is kinda hazy because I'd been drinking margaritas during this conversation, but basically they are planning to change character models to make them more efficient. Once they are changed, they will be able to stuff more things in the global file (like new armor sets). Also, I remember the guy saying that they really wanted to put the new rat models into Freeport but that the Freeport zone is hard coded to only use the old rats, fun fact, eh?

8. Fan Faire in General
This was my second Fan Faire and I had a GREAT time. About 15 people from my guild were in town (although only 3 of us were registered for the Fan Faire). New Orleans is a great place to visit, but it was way too hot for me. The hotel was in a perfect location, easy walking distance to great restaurants and fun things to do (lots of shopping!)

After living in Seattle for 5 years, I was really surprised by all the smoking everywhere. It was pretty miserable for those of us who were sensitive to smoke. There were people smoking at the dinners, in the panels, you just couldn't get away from it.

I thought the turn out was pretty good, but I heard it was about half of the last Vegas Fan Faire. I went to a bunch of the panels and enjoyed asking questions and hearing what other people had to say about the game. I had an awesome time at the server BoTB championship, Brenlo did an amazing job MCing the contest, he was really really funny.

The final dinner was great, but the sound system was a little messed up, hard to hear what was going on. They passed out some great prizes, Vierge from my server won a spiffy keyboard. I got right in front for the costume contest so I could hear everything. I was really impressed by the costumes (I wish my pictures had turned out better).

I had a great time, can't wait for Vegas. My write up isn't that great, but Loral from Mobhunter wrote a really good one you can read here:

http://mobhunter.com/000039.html

Been kind of a crazy 2 days at work recovering from my 2 days not at work. I'll add to this list if I think of anything else!
