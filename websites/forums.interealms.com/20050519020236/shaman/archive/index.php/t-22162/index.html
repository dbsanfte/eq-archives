These are examples; every class has them, and more. As such, while SoE is addressing various class issues, they have a lot of issues to sift through. Patience will be needed. I'm sure eventually the shaman class will get some SoE loving. Whether it's too late for most people, or too-little,-too-late I am uncertain. But rather than putting down other classes for gains they get, we should be cheering them on, just like we'd want them to cheer us on whenever we get some changes.

I'm not a big fan of whining. I try to post only when I feel I have a point to make, or to seek clarification or advice. Yet even I get upset from time to time.
