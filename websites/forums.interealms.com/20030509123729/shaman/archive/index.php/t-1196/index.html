Donar
Soloing multiple mobs at once was difficult for me at that level. I never planned on fighting multiples, but adds do come along every now and then. If your health and mana are good when the add comes along, you can probably take two blues, but you'll probably have to sit a while when it's over. If your health and mana are low when you get the add though, you'll probably be better served by running to the zone or calling for help. In general, I wouldn't recommend you try taking on two blues at once if you're after experience, unless the spawn times allow for you to heal/med back up before the mobs respawn. I have an easier time fighting 2 mobs at a time these days, but in general, I try to avoid doing so unless there is no other way. If root was more reliable, I'd say try dotting one mob, park it and then pull the other mob out of casting range of the other and then tank/dot/nuke until it was dead and then go finish the other. Root was never that reliable for me back then, so I always had to melee/dot/nuke throughout the entire fight, rooting only so that I could heal myself if necessary. Experiment a bit though and see how things go. Some of the most enjoyable fights happen when you get some unexpected guests. When you come out on top, it's a nice feeling. It's also a good way to test your capabilities. However, it will result in quite a bit of downtime which slows down the 'good old experience grind.' Good luck to you! :)
