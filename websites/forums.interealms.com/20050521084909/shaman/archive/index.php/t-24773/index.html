Moglyzok Moogleman
Lately Ive seen this guy up alot So I thought I would throw a jab at the main way to killing him for those who might just pass him and shrug. Ok he is the last murkglider on the way to Wall of Slaughter, loc is about 1400, -800.
The named himself is up alot actually, as many people will either ignore him, or try him and wipe. He spawns adds at around 50% and up to 5 or 6 spawn, they are Murkglider Pups. (Mostly Slowable, hit for 300's not entirely sure though, relatively low hit points.) The Murkglider himself hits for 500's, mostly slowable, average hitpoints (16k~)? He will cast Numbing Touch (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=5992&source=Live) . Its a very annoying spell, but with around 250 Posion resist with Tribunal I resisted it 40-50% of the time he cast it.

Ok on to how to kill him, he is NOT soloable, unless you can manage to split adds off I seriously doubt any shaman could do it. Any class that can snare and/or kite the guys around is good, but snarer is best. A wizard works for AE snare, but ranger is good too as they can pitch in a heal or two and hit the Fecund Murkglider with arrows. I could tank him with a virtue and self-buffs (6kish HP), but every once and a while he would take the advantage. I was using quescience with tnarg's thrown in, and with BoSaryn, BoWunshi, and the rangers occasional damage it was easy (Pet died early on).

He drops minor, lesser, and muramite runes always, and usually an OK item. Again if you can get the adds under control you will be fine, so if you ever need runes and happen to see him up grab and friend and enjoy, ive won 2 runes or so from these fights, hope those who need em will find this information useful.

P.S. sorry is there is already info on this guy, I just figured posting this would give those rune-hunters like me insight.
