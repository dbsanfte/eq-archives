Michael J. Kaspar
You can do the first few parts, up to and including the Shield of Falsehood, in one afternoon. The toughest parts will be avoiding killer sharks and tracking a spirit. Both of these may require a touch of assistance.

After that, work at your own pace. Eventually you will end up at Kirn, and that will require a fair amount of power (3 good groups) and a few hours to do.
Gloriana
I would also work on collecting the 6 pages from the CoM while the area is still really good experience for you. 5 of the pages are very very common and drop off random mobs, the 6th page (student's log) drops only from the Spectral Courier and does not drop every time. Some folks have an easy time getting their Student's log, for some people it takes days, so definitely go while you can get into a good exp group and rake in the exp, random loot (wu's stick, GJBS etc) and epic bits!

Also, while you're hanging out in CoM, watch for any raid groups that come in to bag the reavers. There's a chance they may have no need for Ghiosk books and would let you loot.

Yes. I agree with Gloriana too.

Exp in CoM. While there collect the six reports and pick up an Icon of the Scale.

If a raid comes through get the books off Ghiosk if you can. They often rot.

After you have the six reports and the icon, start the quest. You can do all through test of wisdom solo in about 3 or 4 hours.

This is exactly the way I did it. In this way I did all through the shield in one day. The next day I went and killed black dire with a friend. Then went and got the boots and turned in the reports and the Ghiosk books all at once.

This leaves you with one trip to Fear, one to the Hole, and one last one to finish up in CoM.

Saves alot of running around.
