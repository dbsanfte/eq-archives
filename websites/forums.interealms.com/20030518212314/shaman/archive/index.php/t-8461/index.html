Aselus
Wis will come naturally as you get better items. My gear is soley hp/ac gear and I've got 249 wis unbuffed (how I wish I had put those points into stamina :().

Velium Fire Wedding Rings are a great long-term investment, as are Black Sapphire Electrum Earrings (35 hp/25 mana). Orc Fang earrings are a cheap substitute for BSEEs, and you could probably get a couple of your own soloing or grouping Centurions at the orc fort in EW. They're very common drops.
See if you can round up some help from your guild and work up to your Shield of Falsehood-- it's free and it's something you'll have to do anyway. If you can't do that, Clay Guardian has a buttload of HP and is usually quite cheap. Sarnak Battle Shield is also very stylin and not terribly expensive-- again, a good long-term investment.
Also look at doing the Kromrief Military Leggings quest. You're still a bit low, but in the late 30s and early 40s it's doable with one group.

Hang in there. You'll have a tough time getting any reasonable amount of cash until you're approaching 50, but then things will switch around and you'll be harder pressed to find good XP than good cash. =)

Eriadoc
I agree with the Black Alloy Medallion. As mentioned, it increases your mana pool. If you have PoP, you may look the PoK quests up. There are several that yield different Focus Effect 2 items. The mask is pretty decent, gives spell haste 2, and the sarnak blood for the quest can be bought for about 10pp. Any piece you can quest that easily should be looked into. It will have to be upgraded at some point anyway, so you may as well save money on it. The Jasper Kundan Earring can be quested with a few friends, and it is not bad. It's basically a forest loop with STR 3 added to it. Chipped bone bracer will give you affliction efficiency, which improves your mana pool. Those are cheap. A Batfang Headband gives 3 wis and soem other stats, and has extended enhancement 2. The fewer times you have to buff, the less mana per X amount of time spent. A chrysoberyl talisman may be a cheap upgrade, and has affliction haste 2 on it, which is nice.

Good luck =)
