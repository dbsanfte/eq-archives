Moebeus
Recent video driver update? If not what driver version are you running?

A complete hard lock like that sounds hardware related. Perhaps an IRQ conflict or something else that is affecting the video card.

If it continues, remove all non essential cards from your system just leaving the video card and modem/NIC to access the net. See what that does. If its ok then start adding cards back in one by one while testing in between. Make sure you put the cards back in the slots they were in before. Slots used on most motherboards affect the IRQ used.
