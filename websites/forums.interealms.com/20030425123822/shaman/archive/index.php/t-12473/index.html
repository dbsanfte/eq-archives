When looking at your gear I would recommend losing some wis... perhaps bringing wis down to about 200 because mana/wis ratio is halved when wis is over 200. Recommend the following items for lowering wis and increasing hps/resists. Remember this is just my opinion :p.. and we know what they say about opinions.

Neck: Ribbon Choker, Large Smoothmetal Torque

Finger: Circle Toe Bones, Coral Ring

Arms: Cursed Armplates or Deteriorated Arm links

Wrist: Barbed Rubicite Bracer

Back: Justice Cape

Belt: Chitnous Belt, Spider Fur

All purchasable in the Baz at a reasonable price.
