Krenk
I'd have to say you're just not used to being a Shaman yet. That's all I can think of really, and I'm not trying to be mean. At 38 I was out soloing in BW, doing anything from blues to light reds. Melee on blues, aggro kite on anything tougher. Even though reds are killable, it's just not worth it in terms of downtime. The xp you'll get from a light red will not be much more then a yellow.

At 39 you'll get a real attack speed slow, Togor. Tagar's is pure ****, and for some reason SOE never took a look at that spell and made the correct changes. Walking Sleep is STILL better then Tagar's at 38, and at 39 Togor's is a lot better then Tagar's. The small percentage Tagar's has over Walking Sleep does very little for the amount of mana and cast time, and it lasts exactly the same time. Don't use tagar's it simply eats up mana for no real benefit.

You need to use every buff that grants you HP and AC if you want to melee. That includes keeping your berserk spell up, stacked over your STA, AGI, AC, and Tailisman buffs. As well as Regen and Quickness refreshed per uptime. Yes, Fury (or whatever you have) is worth keeping up. It lasts as long as regen, and adds AC. AC is GOD (the EQ mantra).

If you need the exta 1hp/tick, use your bearform. Slam is just a fancy trick when you aren't facing casters, you won't miss that 1 point of damage. (Contrary to what some Iksar posted, bearform regen does stack on top of Troll regen. 4 for Troll, 2 for sitting, 5 for Regen, 1 for bear. 12hp/tick at your level.)

I was taking, and still can take, 2-3 blues per up time. But that's because I know instinctively how my class works and the buff times. I've been playing SHM a long time, and this is my fifth SHM, second Troll SHM.

At 38 my WIS was around 130ish, AC and HP FULLY buffed at 800/1350ish. My weapon skills just don't go up even though I melee a lot, so I had maybe 150ish skill.

You've got better gear for the most part then I did, a much larger mana pool. So it's just lack of experience on your part.

Root and rot is for dungeons, outdoors you can aggro kite. (ALWAYS turn taunt off of your dog! Taunt and shaman pets is for advanced aggro kiting and needs to be switched on the fly. You don't need to learn that for at least 11 levels yet.). Or you can just melee. Remember melee for a shaman always means using slow and a dot. Root and rot takes a lot more mana then an aggro kite. Use MalX/Decay only when you need it.

(One perk to Decay is that is is a disease. Which means that it slows down natural regen. So any LONG fights it prevents the mob from healing as much as it normally would. You can test this on yourself, it does in fact slow your natural regen.)

If you've got the snare necklace, then you use it when the mob turns to run, and thus prevent any adds in case the runner takes too long to drop.

Always find a spot with little to no pathing. Always pull back to that spot. It only takes one fast acting (poison) DoT to get aggro away from your dog. And it's generally best to leave your dog at the spot (guard here) anyway. (Always haste your dog, it's useless without it. Always SoW your dog, otherwise if you kite, it might not be able to hit your target. STR/DEX/HP buffs are optonal, Regen it if you think it might take some beating. Other buffs do very little to nothing for your pet. It's not a pet, its a walking DoT with hit points.)

There's a lot more I am sure. This is about all you need to know for your level range.
