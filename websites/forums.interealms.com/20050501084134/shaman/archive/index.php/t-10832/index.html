Has about 2500hp buffed with FoS and seems to absorb those hits a bit better and regens much faster even without replenishment. Also really fun when someone in your group is 57 and is shocked that puppy cons yellow to them :)

So while Malo and Torpor are a long way off I'm extremely happy with what I have right now. Replenishment is total ownage, 25hp per tick on top of the 61 dog regen is pretty amazing. He recovers so damn fast after a fight with that on that he's ready way before I am :)

My next target is to solo PoG to build up cash and hopefully get more 4r/emp groups going in Seb and pray I snag a JBB and or Torpor/Malo :)

Fun fun stuff.
