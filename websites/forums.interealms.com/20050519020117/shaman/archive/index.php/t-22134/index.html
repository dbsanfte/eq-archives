i put up an idea on the druid board for the priest covenant thing .. it's in a post so i figured i'd post it here to see what you guys thought:

how about a recurve triggered by a slow mitigation message.

an example would be something like a partial slow message would cast a recurve buff ala sk's and necros that would add a 20% increase to shaman healing, a slight slow would add 40% healing and a mostly slow would add 10% increased healing that stacks with all other healing focus.

this could last for say .. 30 seconds ..

that way the healing solution is directly proportional to our slowing problem on short fights with mitigation

this would also probably increase shaman healing on raids .. where most bosses are slow immune or resist a ton or are only slightly slowable
