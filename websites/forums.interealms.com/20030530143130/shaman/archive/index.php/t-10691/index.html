Jormal
I don't think any of the traps are visible in ME like they are in the Grey. When I camped shards here I usually pulled and I think I know where you're running into trouble. All the traps that I have found are on the plain between the Scout camp near DSP and one closest to the Coterie camps, in fact there's at least one, if not more, traps right in the latter camp. To get those scouts without setting off any of the traps try pulling from up on the ridge behind the camp as far from the plain as possible. It's a pain, but I've had more luck getting shards from that camp than any of the others.

You can probably skip the DSP camp as well. With just two of you pulling the 2 camps near Akheva and the one on the plain you should have enough to keep you busy. If not, there's always the camp near Umbral that doesn't have any traps near it either. Hope this helps.

The trap mobs there used to annoy me too, until I became one with the traps, and decided to take advantage of them.

The trap mobs tend to be higher level on average than the other stuff in the zone, L55-56 fairly often, so they are decent exp. Most of the named camps can be pulled without running into traps, and when those are cleared, just head out randomly for more, and if something pops on you - free mob to kill.
