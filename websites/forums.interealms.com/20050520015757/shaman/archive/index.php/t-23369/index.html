I really like this AA, don't get me wrong, but it needs a tweak. Our hybrid clearly has a superior version of this AA, which is nice for them and everybody who groups with them. No gripes here.

But in addition to the 400hp/tick and endurance regen on Ancestral Aid right now, I really think it should allow for our melee friends to rain down critical hits. Not sure what each level of Cleave adds, but if suddenly, the melee in my group start seeing a good portion of their swings become criticals when I pop this, it would not only be handy and practical as a means to finally group heal, but it would also add some measure of additional damage. It wouldn't have to be overwhelming, but if I raised the dps of my group by a bit with this AA, while at the same time tossing them a small heal over time, that to me, would make it a beefy AA. And unless you're a vegan, who doesn't like beefy?

Beastlords regen health and mana, while we regen health and amplify melee dps. I'd choose criticals over ferocity or strikethrough and the like, simply because it's eyecandy. Regardless, the melee see their dps increased, but now you get to hear the oohs and ahhs of everybody in the group. Everybody wins.

Just a late night idea.
