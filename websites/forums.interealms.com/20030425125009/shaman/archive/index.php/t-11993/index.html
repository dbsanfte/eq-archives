toradorm
Find a low level melee, usually a level or two below your enchanters level and PL them together.

I just PL'd my enchanter to 29 with my 56 Shaman. I think we did a newbie zone for the first three levels, then moved to east commons until like level 7 or so, then into Paludal Caverns until 23, then to Frontier Mountains at the giant fort until 29.

Up until I got the memory wipe, I would just sit haste Mr. Gnome and his pet, sit back and heal. Of course I bought him a fast weapon, and buffed him to the hilt. Then when the mob got to like 40% health, step in and smack em with my hammer.

When I got memory wipe, in PC and FM, I would just slow and melee them myself down to like 10% or less, root them and back up. Memory wipe with the gnome, then cast a couple spells, and melee them with the gnome. Went pretty fast, especially in PC. I'd say got 4 levels in 2 hours, maybe more. In FM, I'd get a level every 1-2 hours or so. I picked the giant fort because I was comfortable with the area, and could get some good loot while we were there. I bound myself there, when I got a load of the lore weapons, I'd use my Everfrost port, sell them and gate back for more.

Free clarity for all!

damage shield method is ok for low levels to get the chanter some def skill ups but I would ask a druid to do it. Potions way too expensive. teen levels, group with a melee and sponge exp. Easiest pay to powerlevel was to /ooc Shaman 65 looking for powerlevel a ranger, no charge. People always wanted to do this even only getting 1/2 the exp.

after i got mem wipe, I pulled with malo line, and used dogdog to pound the mob then enchanter mezzes. this step is crucial. Once mezzed, pet stops attacking, and you have time to mem wipe which can take 2-3 attempts. Without mezz, you usually lose the mob to the dog. Until the wipe occurs the dog is still high on the hate list. I would also position the dog and the enchanter at sharp angles so you can see the mob turn toward the chanter. This means you are now on top of the hate list and memwipe worked. then nuke or let pet finish mob off. remember to do 1 pt damage with chanter to get full exp.

Ran the enchanter from 1 to 35 in a few weeks doing this then lost interest.
