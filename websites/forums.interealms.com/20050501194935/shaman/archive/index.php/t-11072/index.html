Kriel Darkbane
If your choice is coming down to either troll or iksar, go troll. The AC bonus on an iksar just isn't worth as much as the potential to use a JBB. You're already accepting the big exp penalty in exchange for the regeneration. You might as well get the full joy and go with a troll for the JBB eventually. They are not as hard to buy as they used to be (witness the recent thread about all the L 65 shamans selling their JBB's because they aren't much use at that level).

I say this because you say you want to be able to solo. Being able to use a JBB will make a huge difference in your soloability in the high 40's and low-mid 50's (assuming you can manage to buy one by then, of course). Once you get togor's at L 39 your AC (the iksar's only advantage over troll) just isn't worth very much when solo'ing. The mob will be slowed and hardly hurting you faster than you are regen'ing anyway.

JBB and a horse is just FAR too fun a combination to miss out on -no downtime solo'ing makes playing a shaman fun!
