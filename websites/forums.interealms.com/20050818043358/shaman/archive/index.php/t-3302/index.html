I've never killed any really uber mobs, but even on my sort of raids, the xp you can get being in the "KS group" is significant. A way to share that xp with everyone on the raid would be excellent.

I think it would encourage the more casual players to come on raids a bit more - when you play only a couple of times a week, a few deaths on a raid, even with a 90%/96% rez, can eliminate a whole weeks xping.

Also being able to share xp would make "mini raids" a viable option for xp hunting - for example earlier this week I took 2 groups of guildies to Akheva, mainly to have a look round. I think this is such a good idea it would be a waste to only implement it on uber mobs - it should be a system available to all players - with chat channels this would effectively allow >6 person groups.
