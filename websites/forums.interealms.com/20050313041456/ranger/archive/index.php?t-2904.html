You will only need a couple of people around level 55ish to get this done. I'm not sure how difficult it is to mobilize a couple guildmates but if I were you I would check tracking at the zone inside Kedge and ask for the assistance of 2-3 people over guildspam if she's up.

Alternatively you can go there with a group ( level 35-45ish) for fantastic experience (assuming people dont kill themselves every 20 min when enduring breath wears off). Once your in the zone let your guild know that nobody else is in kedge and that if anyone would like to make some killer experience then they should join you. A small group of level 40ish people can easily handle the first room in kedge, just leave the Impaler alone unless your well balanced.

If you want to find the mermaid, you need to head down the tunnel in the first room. After going down a short distance, the area will open up into four rooms (North, South, East and West). I cant recall which one leads to the Mermaid, but it will be one of the rooms with a hole in the ceiling. Head up through the hole and you will find the mermaid a short distance ahead. Make sure she is dispelled a few times and she wont be much trouble. She is a druid so expect damage sheilds. Good luck with the quest : )

Here is what we did: Get a druid of at least a level that the mermaid cons blue. Bring yourself and another melee friend and the druid. Invis and swim down to where she is and pull her....swim back to zone and go ahead and zone once you know she is there behind ya. Have the druid be invis and when she and her train come target her and root her and zone himself (he can have an assist on you so can be casting while she coming in). Now when you zone-back, she will be rooted and other mobs gone and you can then solo her at zone...have the druid remove the damage shield she has and nuke while you melee her.....easy!
