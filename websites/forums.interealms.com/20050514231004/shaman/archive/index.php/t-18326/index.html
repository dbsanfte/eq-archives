So, shamans are definately going to be in demand as healers, they will not be the SOLE healers. The horde faction that shamans are exclusive to also has a couple of races that are capable of being the "Priest" class, which will be the generally more powerful healer.

The flexibility looks good...the trick will be in the balancing. Which, I suppose, is why Blizzard is anticipating an 8 month beta cycle.
