I've been meaning to feedback corpse dragging for a while; I'm not sure if your problem was the 2-corpse thing, but I've noticed that when you bring the Z axis into play while dragging a corpse, you can have a problem. I imagine this could happen easily in Kedge.

Basically, if you drag a corpse while levitated, and you get to high off the ground, if you drop the corpse, the Z axis position of the corpse can get bugged. So, if you're on the ground at position Z=0, the corpse's position might still be at Z=300 (the last place you summoned the corpse to). So, you'd be out of range to pull the corpse even though it looks like you're standing right next to it. I've been able to fix this by hovering over the corpse while spamming /corpse, but it's not a guaranteed success.

Happened to us in The Deep as well -- dropped a corpse off the invisible bridge, and we couldn't pull it any more, either from the bridge or from the pit. Had to just rezz in the pit to fix that.

So, if a toon died on phinny's ledge, and the corpse sank, it might have bugged the position of the corpse.

Just a thought...
