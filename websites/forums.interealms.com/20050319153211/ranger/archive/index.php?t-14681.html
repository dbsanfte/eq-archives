yay spoiler

-The quest is started by an erudite npc by the name of Eremor(or something like that begins with E). He's located on the other back corner of PoN behind the large crypt'ish area filled with wraiths and gargoyles.

-Theres a maximum of 24 people who can attend this event.

-Mujaki is untargetable during the first part of the event.

-There are 3 gravestones that spawns a pair of "Servants of Mujaki" every 30 seconds or so from what I remember.

-The Servants of Mujaki hit for around 600-700'ish from what I remember. They have an average amount of hp for their level.

-You need to stop the servants from reaching the center platform, otherwise the result is they become untargetable and become mounted and will later be able to attack you when the waves are finished.

-There are about 3-4 waves, i dont remember the exact number.

-They major key to this whole event is organization. Each group needs to be independant and be able to handle killing 1 servant and tanking/mezzing the other. I dont remember if they were mezzable. Since there are 3 gravestones that spawn a pair of servants, and you have 4 groups, use the extra group to help out any group that is in trouble. My group was actually the first to wipe out on the event since our gravestone had some evil horses that attacked us when moved there. While we were fighting them, the servants spawned on us and it got pretty messy. The rescue group came and ressed us while we picked up and it just went pretty smooth after we got back on our feet.

-Mujaki the Devourer himself isn't too hard. He's your typical boss, hits up to 900 i think, has AE fear. I never fought Mujaki with his servants since we managed to kill every single servant before they reached the platform.

Thats about it =)

This is a 4 group event and it can be bugged. FYI there are actually 3 Mujaki encounter areas and there can be up to 3 Mujakis up simulatanously. I think what happens is someone (or a group) goes and hails Etumer in the GY and ends up getting ported to the Mujaki event area. They trigger the event and die/port out or whatever. The next 4 group raid comes along to try the event and ports in. The first 3 groups get ported to the first area (3 groups + 1 earlier =4) and the 4th group, which the game thinks is the 5th, gets ported to the next encounter area. The problem is that this never gets reset, so once screwed up it lasts till the zone/server is reset.

If this happens (one group ported to wrong area) you can lev and run over the wall to the next area and join the main force. Only thing is that this will also begin the encounter in the 2nd area as well and that mujaki will go to waste.

Another bug that happens here is that there may be 2 mujaki's up (3 encounter areas) and you get ported to the one area with no mujaki up. They need to fix these bugs.

As far as the encounter goes, there are 3 gravestones that the mobs pop out of (so on double spawn waves there can be 6 up at once). I employ a high DPS group at each tombstone (slower and off-tank mandatory). Once slowed, the mobs are easy to tank, any decently geared ranger can do it. I use the 4th group as the "roving group of death" to assist groups that fall behind or get low on mana. Once you get it down, it is an easy (and fun) encounter with some decent gear.
