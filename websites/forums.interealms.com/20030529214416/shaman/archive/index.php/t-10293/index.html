PIT in POT is Soloable. As others are saying. Adds are a problem. Don't really see how without BOU/BOS. Someone will claim "Oh but I did it." I'm sure you did on one, maybe. If you did it, it wasn't for long, With wanderers/a resisted root/root breaking every time you land a dot. You just aren't going to last long down there. Nameds aren't popping like they were before, so I'd question the reasoning behind being down there at all at this point. It is a really nice 3-6 man exp camp though. You do get good exp soloing, but as said by others and myself, you just aren't going to stay in the zone long enough for it to matter.
