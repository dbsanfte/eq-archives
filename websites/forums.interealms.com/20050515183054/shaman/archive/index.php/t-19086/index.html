Frozen Wind 700hp/3.5seconds + Brushfire 610hp/3.5 seconds = 1310hp/7 seconds.

So at least in short bursts rangers do out nuke shaman. While I do not think that this is a sign that hybrid’s dd needs to be nerfed, it is an indication of how poorly shaman dd is. Yes a Ranger would have to chain dd spells because of long recast times, and as Tarthug pointed out, even if a ranger did this, it would most likely be a decrees in his dps, compared to output with his bow and melee. The point is not that ranger’s spells are over powered, but that shaman’s dd is underpowered, and seriously cannot keep up with dps of a hybrid.

This is true, but only to a point. With canni shaman can make manna through canni/healing. But of course canni is not passive. I cannot have a good dps if I’m canning (0 damange = 0 dps) but all my spells are very manna and time inefficient compared to other casters, on the basis that I could canni. My knuckles ache from canni iv, but because of the inefficiencies in shaman spells, I’m always canniing.

Before the other hybrid classes start to worry that shaman are calling for a nerf to your nukes, don’t panic, we’re not. What I think some of us shaman are complaining about is that the traditional way that shaman do dps (dots) has been eclipsed due to the fast pace of the game, and our dd and heal spells are, well, gimps.

Just speaking for myself, I think shaman absolutely need either a better canni, better heals, or better dd. Two out of the three would not be over powering, but one of those three need to be done to keep shaman a viable class for groups. As it is I’m amazed that any Ldon group would get a shaman over a BL, since our dps is so horrible.
