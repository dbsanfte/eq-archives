Drops needed for various Items


Arms = Holgresh Fur (Holgresh/Flying Monkeys)
Legs = Holgresh Wings (Holgresh/Flying Monkeys)
Bracer = Glob of Tar (Tar Goo)
Helm = Sifeye Dust (Sifeyes)
Boots = Faun Hoof (Fauns)
Gauntlets = Living Granite (Geos)

Gnomish Vanishing Device = Haze Panther Eye (Haze Panthers)

All of these mobs are in WL.

The Sifeyes and Fauns are on Tunarean Court Faction, so if you want to ever go to PoG without the intent of killing things, don't touch them (learned that the hard way)

The easiest way to get stuff for tinkering is to camp the Holgresh in their cave at appox neg 3000 pos 2000 in WL. There are about 12-15 spawns in there and are mostly green to me at 55. the problem is that unless you can nuke the hell outta them, you cannot solo them. They have been known to chain nuke 10 times before moving to a position where you can safely melee them. a group of 3 would be ideal. Any more than that would be over kill and you would run out of spawns.

The Living granite is ok, but they don't stack like the Holgresh Wings do, and plus the cost me an extra 15pp per attempt for the gauntlets. Although I will not turn them down =)

Haze Panther Eyes are the most sought after. They are pretty rare. Plus the Gnomish Vanishing Device is the hardest thing there is to make. It trivializes at 250 and from what I have seen on the tinkering boards is that Gnomes with skill 200+ are still only makeing them at a 5% success rate. That wouldn't be so bad if one of the components wasn't a DIAMOND. So I guess if you get a Haze Panter Eye PLZ save it, but I will not attempt one of these until my skill is 180+

Globs of Tar. Again, I won't deny someone offering me them, but the Tar Goos are way too hard to kill and the Bracer I can make from it is a peice of crap! (Summons a 6/25 1HB weapon) although it is still good for getting skill increases.
