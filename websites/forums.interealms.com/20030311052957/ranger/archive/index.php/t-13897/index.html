I'll do the best I can from Memory only..
Level 51 for General abilities
Level 55 for Archtypes
Level 59 for Class Skills

Run3
Regen 3
These 2 can be attained at level 51, and are general abilities. Most people recommend getting run3, and then leveling to 60 before worrying about more AA points. You must spend 6 points in General before you can get any Archtypes. The above represents 6 aa points.

Combatfury1 (2 points)
Finishing blow 1 (2 points)
Mental Clarity 1 (2 points)
Natural Durability 2 (2 + 4 = 6 )
These are Archtypes and can be attained at level 55. I think all Archtypes cost 2, then 4, then 6 for the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd level of the skill. So the 4 skills above represent 12 points. You must spend 12 point in Archtype before you can get any Class skills.

Endless Quiver (9 points)
Archery mastery 1, 2, 3, (3 + 6 + 9 = 18 points)
Ambidexterity (9 points)
Innate Camo (5 points)
These are Class skills, and can be attained at level 59. The above skills represent 41 points total.

ND3 (6 points for 3rd level in Archtype)
Combat stability 1 (2 points for first level in Archtype)
Combat agility 1 (same)
Physical enhancement (5 points, Class skill, pre-requisite of at least 1 level of NDurability, CStability, and CAgility, it gives 2 percent more in each of these 3)

Level > Gear, and Level > AA skills. I got my first 6 AA points before level 60 to break the monotony, but looking back, I could have skipped all AA until level 60.
