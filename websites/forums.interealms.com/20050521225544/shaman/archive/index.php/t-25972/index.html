I've been rather impressed with the Drakes in Lavaspinner's Lair. At level 70 I'm getting up to 3% AA per kill, which isn't bad when you consider how easy they are. The max hit seems to be around 300, and often I'm getting hit for under 100. They don't summon, are spread out enough to easily root/rot, and drop some gems and other items that can allow you to earn a little cash.

I figure at 65 they'll be fairly decent experience, and not too hard to solo.

I solo'd NC Murks for a while for AA EXP, but then I tried WoS Murks.

WoS murks see-invis, run FAST, but they don't summon. I got 2-3x the EXP I got in NC per kill, which really wasn't any harder.

Soloing works, but its not as efficient as grouping. get OoW if you don't have it already, start killing in those zones, its easier to get groups there. if not, you're pretty much stuck in BoT for group EXP to 65, until you can get into higher zones.

PoP tier 1 & 2 are pretty much soloable by a 65 shaman, Tier 1 you can just plow through melee with DB mobs almost non-stop, not great EXP per kill, but you can kill fast. Tier 2 is root-rot for a bazaar geared 65 shaman, doable, but slow and can be painful if you aren't careful. you'll get more EXP per kill in tier 2+, but don't expect it to be fast.

grouping is always best exp.
