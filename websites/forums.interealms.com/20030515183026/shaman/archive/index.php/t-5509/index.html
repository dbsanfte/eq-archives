warmag
From 53 to 54 ("good" race shaman) I spent most of my time soloing in Kedge Keep on 2nd floor. "Evil" race shaman might want to try High Keep as mentioned in other posts. If you do solo in Kedge at level 53 don't pull the golden haired mermaid. Waste of time because of potential adds in her room. Just stick strictly to 2nd floor in kedge and you can get to 54 fast soloing. Optionally Umbral Plains in a group. You'll level fast there too, even with the occasional death due to your slowing the mob and getting aggro'd. I made the bad mistake of getting over confident after the ding to 54 in Kedge and pulled the golden haired mermaid and got a pirhanna add. Root didn't last on either unfortunately and my pet went down fast. By the time I tried to gate I was getting pounded so hard I turned to swim up to zone and died anyway: unding, welcome to 53 again! If soloing kedge on 2nd floor, just be careful to pull only from that floor. The spawn cycle is like 18 minutes I think, so by the time you clear out everything you have just enough time to med to full and start again. It's a perfect solo spot. Scarlet Desert I tried also at 53. Waste of time. Faster XP in a dungeon. Get a fishbone earring and try Kedge Keep. Pull cautiously. Just don't die and you'll do great there. The setup is: invis yourself and swim in to 2nd floor in the south cubbyhole and setup a pet and plant yourself and pet along back wall of cubbyhole. Buff the pet with HoS and spirit quickening. Give yourself a HP buff in case root drops off something, and inch out into the doorway and you should get aggro'd right there for first pull. Inch out to the left or right just a bit to get 2nd pull. Have a "/pet calm down" macro button set so when you get hit you can back the pet up to the cubbyhole. It's very bad to fight otuside that cubbyhole or you will get adds. Pull with slow, or cast root on the mob after you get in and then slow it, and then toss an E-bolt. Add a 2nd E-bolt on sharks when it wears off, and keep root up with pet on the mob. For pirhanna you might want to toss in a disease dot because they seem to regenerate or go down in hitpoints slower than other mobs. Just root and rot, med and repeat. Fast from 53 to 54. Clear out about 3 to 4 wandering fish outside the cubbyholes before trying to pull the shark or pirhanna. Also clear to the left and/or right cubby hole (depending on if you are pulling from south or east of room) before pulling the mob across the room from you because the farthest mob from you will bring in adds along the way otherwise. It takes practice to get a system down in a spot like this but the XP is worth it I think. Root and rot and med up. I tried melee on the mobs too with my 2hb that does over 100+ dmg a hit, but it's alot faster with root and rot method because these mobs will take your HP down which equates to more downtime and less HP for canning back mana. I only perfected the teqnique in my last yellow in 53 in Kedge, so hopefully these tips will help others so they don't waste time. (And by all means, please don't kill the tiny tiny fish or you spawn bad things in that dungeon that you don't want to know about) - Warmag of MM
