Goldark
Level 60 is like half a level, so xp flies past. 61-65 is much tougher (though not as bad as 59 imo). 60 really is twice as easy, quite literally.

As for LDoN xp, it depends who you go with. If you can bump up the levels to get a 63+ average, you will get 10-12% in one adventure. You can also manage this by selecting a hard mission with a lower level crew - just be sure the group's average level is below 63 before you do this! :) As you saw, grouping with others under level 63 you net a pretty poor 5% or so.

LDoN xp won't compete with a good steady PoV/BoT group due to all the downtime between adventures but it certainly can make a decent dent in the xp bar if you group with higher level folk.

As it seems with everything that pays off early, it seems SOE has hit LDoN with the nerf bat a few patches before GoD came out. With the same group, I used to be able to count on that 10-12% reg xps figure others have seen and that was even at 65. When the nerf bat hit, it seemed to all of us in our regular group that multiple things happened:

- reg xps dropped to about half of what it was
- Named mob spawn rate in dungeons dropped considerably
- Stat augment drops dropped considerably
- Gem and coin loot dropped considerably

Prior to the nerf we had multiple dungeons with 2 or 3 named in it which was great for getting us all decent upgrade gear. After than nerf, we could go 5 adventures before seeing one and we never saw another adventure where there was more than 1 named. In addition, we all could usually count on bringing out 150-300PP each consistently in zones like MMC or Miraguls. After that patch, our money dropped off considerably. If we were LUCKY we got 150-200PP if the gem god was generous but more than likely we came out with 45-60PP per adventure.

I thought I remember reading somewhere that when brenlo asked the developers if the LDoNs had been nerfed, he was assured they werent but I have YET to see any of the drop/spawn rates returned to what we used to have. I LIVED in LDoNs with some friends for about 2 months straight when it first came out.
