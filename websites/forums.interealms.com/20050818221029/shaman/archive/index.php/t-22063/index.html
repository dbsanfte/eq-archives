One other thing to remember is if you send in the pet and it's getting beat on 3' from it's friends you can heal it all you want.... but the second that pet dies all it's friends are Agro on you. There was a big post on this also because of the pet pulling techniques you can use.

But the main points to remember are:
1- You can heal your pet all you want w/o gaining agro. Ever see a BL play Pet Cleric? They jump in and beat on something and then quickly duck out of range so their pet tanks while they regen up and CH the pet then they jump back in for more damage later.

2- If the pet ever dies you will automatically draw the agro from any mobs with in agro range even if you are using the low level pet pulling tricks.
Tsuyoi
This is different than the pet pulling technique that is detailed. In the pet pulling you have to use a lower level pet since a mob's friends will join in on the fight if the pet is blue to them. The idea there is to pull a single mob from a cluster back to the group. In this case you want to use the toughest pet available so that it can adequately tank the pulled mobs while it is being healed.

If the pet dies the pet's owner gets all the agro but the healer doesn't. If they are far enough apart the mobs should rush the pet owner and the healer should be left alone. If the owner can feign then the mobs should go back to their spawn point as long as no-one else gets agro.

I can see where this can be useful for breaking spawns or packed rooms. For a single target you do want a tank to have agro, but for multiple targets this feature gives you the ability to break a cluster of mobs without endangering the entire group.
