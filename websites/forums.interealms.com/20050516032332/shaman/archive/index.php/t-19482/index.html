Redhenna
Gratz on getting Yeli's head, GoDS are awesome(my warrior main has them and loves them). The mines in the SE area of GD are the best place to farm dorf heads for turning into Cpt. Bevlos(sp?). Be warned that some of the guards are DB to 65...I simply skipped them untill I was KoS, doing just the minors. Resist the temptation to turn heads into The Kromrif Recruiter, as you get no KT faction hit from him.

In addition to saving dorf heads, there is a simple quest you can run in Kael for KT faction, delivering packages to mercenaries(1 in KD itself, 3 just outside KD in WL, follow the zone wall to the right). I did the quest each time I went and turned in heads, it is a small faction hit, but takes almost no time(tho the mercenaries despawn when you turn in package to them).

Also, you can get nice sizable KT faction bumps from killing royals in Icewell Keep. These are the dorfs around Dain himself. Best to take a group along to do them if you can. Lastly, get in on Dain and Yeli kills, as they both provide nice, sizable faction hits(and if you can get Dains head to go along with yelli's, it makes the eventual reward even better...Belt of Dwarf Slaying is awesome as well).

Redhenna
I read somewhere that the best way to raise faction is to go kill in Skyshrine, wait for a faction change on Bevelos and then turn in the heads.

Skyshrine is a great place for Rif and Zek faction. You need KT faction for your quest, but you also need Zek faction to turn in heads for KT hits. You can get Zek faction by killing dorfs, by turning in heads to A Kromrif Recruiter, by killing in SS and I beleive WW.

I strongly recomend first just killing dorfs and collecting heads. If I remember correctly, once I had dubious Zek faction so I could turn in heads to Cpt. Bevelos, it took 70ish heads to get my KY faction(this after 2 Dain kills and 1 Yelli kill). So untill you have that many heads, you still need to kill dorfs anyway, so keep killing for the Zek faction hit.

From there you can kill more dorfs, and turn those heads into the Recruiter for Riz and Zek hits, or kill in SS and WW. I chose dorfs. Note that as mentioned by someone else, it is posible to AE dorfs for mass faction hits, and then pick thru the corpses for heads. I actually did this with a cleric who needed faction as well. I would run around just outside Thurg, agroing all the dorfs and wolves, then when I had a crapload, would run over to the cleric...Dorfs all hit me, ensuring my faction hit, and 2 or 3 cleric AE's and it was over. Loot up, take a small break, and do it again. A better AE class(or a chanter and better AE class would be ideal) and it will be even better.
