Solemneyes
I don't know of any shaman usable h2h weapons but there is a small trick you should be able to use to level it up. I think you can still hit a beastlord pet with just barehanded attacks. So what you need to do is find a beastlord buddy with pet hold and get him to summon a pet, tell it to hold, and then duel him. Since his pet is on hold it regens at a rate equal to its non aggro rate which should be more than fast enough to offset any damage done by lowly shaman h2h damage.

Alternatively, the only thing I see is to find a mob that is at least LB and does not require a magic attack to hit... not sure I can offer any advice on finding one of those though :o
