Mudcrush Durtfeet
Well, first off I don't yet have spell casting fury 3, nor a spell haste item (though at raids I wear a Naki's studded ring).

I have rarely gotten more than one crit from torrent of poison, which I only use if either a) we are getting just solo mobs and/or b) we don't have a mezzer.

Yeah, I am uber healer. Basically, let's say Chloroblast heals for 500 base. This makes the math a bit easier.

My aa skills increase that to 550 and crit heal is 1100.

What healing focus 3 does is do a random 1 20, then add that percentage amount of healing regardless of aa skills or crits.

So I heal for 505-600 with no aa skills, or 555-650 and crit for 1105-1200.

Of course chloroblast is a bit less than 500 base, so my crit heals are generally a little higher than 1000, occasionally lower.

Often I wear a crystalline torque or talisman of evasion. With the torque, all my spells cost 10percent less mana.

With the talisman all my dots and debuffs cost 25 percent mana. When xping this is often the better deal.

I also have affliction extension and haste 3 items so my dots and debuffs cast in only 2/3 the normal time and have +20% endurance. It owns for xping, at raids it's good to have longer lasting turgur... :-) I figure that's a bug and debuffs will eventually cease getting these benefits but for now life is good.

I also have qb3 so I have 1/2 cast time on torpor, celerity, and most buffs.

So although I often don't have spell haste, this usually only affects PA, chloroblast (already fast), and my cold dd.

If I want to NUKE a lot with cold dd, I have a white dragon scale cape I can use for improved damage 3.
