Well, yes, that was one way to gain the faction needed to start the Symbol quests. Obviously, it won't work any more since we were kicked out of Grobb 5 weeks ago :(

However, there has always been another way of raising your faction with the DE Clerics. Go to the East Freeport underground tunnels and find the Necro guild. They should be indifferent to an Inny worshipping Troll. Go upstairs and through the breach in the wall. Go through the closed door in the room that emerge in. There you will find Opal Darkbriar (sp?) and yet another sister of the DE Cleric that you need to get on the good side of. Talk to her and she will give you a note for him. Deliver the note and you go from apprehensive to amiable in .3 seconds.

Supposedly, you need to do this FedEx quest twice to get to a higher shade of amiable before he gives you the first Symbol quest. I can't confirm it since even though I have done the FedEx quest twice, I haven't started the symbol quests yet.

By the way, if you try to get to the Freeport Necro guild from the Freeport PoK book and walk on the pool ledge, you will probably fall off and get ambushed by 3 Drowned Citizens (level 11-13?) like I did. Wolf Form definitely helps there, but I suspect that levitation will also work.
