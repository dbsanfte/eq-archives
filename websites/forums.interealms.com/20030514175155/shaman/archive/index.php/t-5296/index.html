I believe I was the one that suggested SD. I solo'd there during several levels around 50's and can even find Dark Blue's around about and for sure on top of Plateau. I also duo'd with my wife with her Ranger and pocket Druid.

There are numerous reasons to exp in that zone ... for one blocks/bricks of acrylia drop off Bloodtribe's which sell well in baazar ( or use them for smithing yourself). Then there are ton's of drops, quest pieces, etc in SD. True most drops/gear is 40's type stuff but at least they sell in baazar. The main thing is not the pure exp per kill but could we do number of mobs, easy killing , safe spots to hunt, and loot last. SD has all of these for the casual gamer ... and it's a popular spot on my server ... so it's best to check camps.


My choice was to do Bloodtribes and animals for a hour or 2 camp(Cheetah, Lightcrawlers, and Sunflowers). If top of Plateau was open and I had time ... would do Aliens making sure to keep Magic and Fire resist's up.

** Bloodtribes **
Bloodtribe range from lvl 35 to 46. The are mostly warriors and shaman. Slow the warriors and they are cake. Shaman are bit tricker but they tend to use the same spells ... poison rain, frost strike, slow, and debuffing. I would slow them nonetheless because VI loves to give caster's good melee ability ... and they can hurt if allowed to hammer on you. Best spots for them are on the back of the zone closer to the Grey zone. There are numerous caves in the zone that spawn named Bloodtribes after killing in them for awhile.

**Animals**
Animals range a bit lower than bloodtribes but Cheetah's are the best ( lvl 35-42). Animals are mostly warrior types with Cheetah's being very fast with SoW like speed. Lightcrawlers are a bit slower than Cheetahs and Sunflowers even slower. With slow ... single fights with Cheetah's are hardest. Lightcrawlers and Sunflowers easy.


A few tidbits (taken off other boards)

1. Wolf Form is KOS regardless of faction.
2. If fighting aliens and sun revenants, use Magic Resist buffs. Some aliens do fire attacks. Sun Revenants do curse and necro spell type attacks.
3. The walls are better than the center. Green mobs will aggro (sunflower, lightcrawler, etc.)
4. Choose a faction. Sun Revenant hate everyone but don't kill both aliens and bloodtribes unless you want to aggro everything in the zone.
5. There are 4 factions of aliens, but 3 of the factions are at war with the one other faction. The Tro Jeg are at war with the Grol Baku, the Vas Ren, and the Cral Ligi. So if your faction with one side becomes KOS then go hunt the other.
6. Most things cannot see through invisibility but some Sun Revenants will see through invis ... check them to be sure.

Answers

Q. Loots wasn't very good
A. Nah, it's not going to be great but if you do bloodtribes they drop both Plat, weapons, and ore. The ore is the money maker ... save up enough and sell it to smithers for nice profit.

Q. Can you camp inside any of the caves or are they too full or too fast respawn?
A. I'm not a big cave soloer. With it being a cave ... it's hard to escape which is always on the back of my mind while soloing for efficiency. You also build up a huge agro radius after killing bloodtribes after awhile which makes it hard to single pull inside a caves.

Q. What is the respawn time inside the caves? How many wanderers inside the caves? I tried pulling from inside the cave to my camp outside, but the mob just got lost and never showed up. I will try moving inside one of the caves, I just didn't have time this morning.
A. Other problem with caves is the crappy pathing for most mob's. Some will warp to you ... others will wander off a bit during the fight and warp back to you with all their friends. Worse is letting a cave pulled Shaman gate which will always guarantee that he will return with remaining mob's from the cave.

Q. Are the Spiney weapons just the SD version of Fine Steel? Do they sell OK or are they a waste of time to haul back to town?
A. Hmmm ... since we have huge STA and ability to buff it up ... even getting a couple plat per spiney makes it worth topping off your packs before selling. And you don't have to return to town to sell ... there is a vendor in the tower in TS that will sell/buy from you even as a evil (unless you have messed up faction by killing them).

Here is one you might try since I seem to have done okay soloing as a 53 shaman here: The Grey. You'll need an enduring breath item or lots of fishscales for this. I would probably only try the Shissar Stalkers there though. I couldn't solo some of the other mobs I experimented with near the zone line because a few of them are resistant to dots. The shissar stalkers don't seem to be resistant to cold nuke so I pulled with slow, used cripple, and no bear form (for the barbarian bash, which I needed to solo these). My soloing basically consisted of me hitting the thing with a hammer of rage (2hb bonus, and 190+ in the skill, plus str & rage up for maximum strength points added to damage formula so nice 100+ hits from me), using alacrity to nix some of the delay, and bash to stun the mob. Throw in a few cold nukes but save a couple bubbles mana for healing the pet and yourself. Pet used for taunt and save most of your mana for heals. With a druid, this would be an ideal place because of the damage shield to help the battle out. Buff up good though, and have full mana because a 53 barb shaman soloing these is going to have a close battle, really close. I don't have jbb or epic or anything fancy gearwise, so it's workable, but the downtime for mana regain is unacceptable to me so it would be perfect for two boxing. - Warmag of MM
