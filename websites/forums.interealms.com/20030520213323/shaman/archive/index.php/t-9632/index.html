basically you have to think of how can you regen mana the fastest - so theres basically 2 views -

1. youre under 60 or dont have torpor - fungi probably the best for you since its a constant source of hp regen that can be used to can 4, and also helps you tank and solo mobs at lower levels

Bigguss
The fungi is the best droppable tunic a shaman can wear period - until they get Torpor at level 60. The extra hit points (and through cannibalization, mana) the fungi brings outweigh any other stat, until you get torpor.

Once you get torpor, even the regeneration from the fungi pales in comparison- so you can concentrate on other stats. As more and more shamans are 60+ with torpor, you see less and less that wear the fungi.

Of course, before 60 with torpor... it's often difficult to find the means for a fungi- they're quite pricey.

Hope this helps

Bigguss the fungi-wearing pre-60 shaman. :)
