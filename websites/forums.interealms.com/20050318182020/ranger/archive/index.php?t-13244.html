17,5% crics->82,5%non crics.
Damage from non crics: 100*0,825=82,5
Damage from crics: 0.175*1.7*100=29,75
Total damage:29,75+82,5=112,25
<b>% damge from crics</b>(<I>not extra damage from crics!</I>): 29,75/112,25=26,5%

17.5% cric chance would amout to 17.5*0.7=12,25% extra damage from criticals.

I am using 1.7 multipler for damage here i think it might be a little higher. But a 17.5% cric chance where crics deals 1.7 damage should make 26,5% of your damage come from cricc.

-------------------------------------

If 17.5% crics yields 25% of total damage with crics, cric multiplier would be be 1,57.

let X be avg damage
let Y be cric multiplier

3*(0,175*X*Y)=0,825*X (3 times cric damage = non cric damage 25% cric 75% normal)
0.525Y=0,825
Y=1,57

This would indicate that either.
a. The parsing has some faulty numers.
b. cric damage multiplier is not always ~1.7 but varies. And average cric modifier is some where around ~1.57.

You have to remember that criticals makes a regular hit into a higher hit(1.7*regular), so if you hit 17.5% critical hits the damage that you gain from those crits is not 17.5%.

The damage gained by having 17.5% crits compared to no crits at all is 17.5*0.7=12.25%

If crit rate is 22% (for the sake of argument assuming my parse is a good indicator), then dmg increase over no crits is ~15.4%.

Say your average bow hit is 200 pts. So you shoot 1000 shots with 0 crits.

1000 * 200 = 200,000

Then say you shoot another 1000 shots, but this time your crit rate is 22%. So 780 of the shots are normal, and 220 of the shots are for 1.7x normal.

780 * 200 = 156,000
220 * 340 = 74,800

156,000 + 74,800 = 230,800 dmg total with a 22% crit rate. So...

230,800 / 200,000 = 15.4% dmg increase. I'd say doing a little over 15% more dmg with your bow is well worth the expenditure. Ya, its not as much bang for the buck as AM3 was (100% more for 18 pts, compared to 15% more for 21 pts), but worth it for those interested in doing as much damage as possible.
