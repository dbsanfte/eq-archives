Copied from EQLive under Future plans:
Shields and blocking


We're planning on reviewing the possibility of giving an innate chance to block when using a shield. We've been hoping to do this for a while, and it should be getting a realistic review in the near future. We'd like to increase the value of shields, and make it a worthwhile choice for players who might normally dual-wield weapons or use a two-handed weapon. We plan on balancing this against classes which use shields by default, and therefore wouldn't be making any trade-offs to equip a shield.

Im truly hoping that shaman fit into this category, but unfortuantly Im not optimistic.

Ive carried a shield for 60 of my 61 levels now. There was one level where I used a 2hb, but that was mainly an accident as I flew through level 53 so quickly (bout 4 hours). Because most 2HB weapons dont give the stat increases that a shield and spear/blunt combo does many shaman feel required to carry a shield of some sort.

Because of our role in groups/raids, Shaman also take a beating. Its pretty much unavoidable. Thats why we can wear chain armor and use a lot of good AC items. But our Dodge skill, like all wis casters, is capped at 75, which means nothing. I can count on one hand the number of times that Ive actually seen dodge work for me, and I dont have to use three fingers (Thats two times for you ogre shaman out there...).

Id like to see this innate ability given to the shaman class. Most of us carry shields so large they cover our upper torso's and heads; why not make it so that we can dick behind the shield and mitigate a round of combat or two. Is it unbalancing to save the shaman in your group a little pain? I really dont see how. In fact, I would like to see clerics and druids included in the ability. I think the time spent in implementing this would not be unappreciated by any wis caster.
