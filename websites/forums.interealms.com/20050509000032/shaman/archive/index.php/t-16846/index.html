I've always pulled for our group, and my HPs suck. :p Caanus started a thread on how to do it and it was pretty bang on. Can easily get a controlled 3-pull by rooting one, sneezing at the second and bringing the last in. While the party kills their mob, go back and nuke the sneezed one and it should break and head in. Tank grabs agro and you have the choice of helping them drop it then moving to the third, or going and breaking root on the third to bring that in too. The only times you maybe get a nasty pull is if there's a crowded room and you've got mobs behind corners that you can't target, but even then that's usually doable if you can get a Q off before you pull and a Tigirs off once the leftovers are in camp.
