Nevvyn
Biggest change to these guys is aggro range.. Harmony made this encounter trivial, but now the aggro range makes it......... not so trivial.

Honestly, I'd suggest getting a rock solid group together including a good puller, evac'er, and balanced rest of the party. Your combo may be able to fight just her if you got just her.. but getting there is half the fun.

Our guild was doing this a ton back before the aggro range change, and got extremely overconfident.. Next thing we knew, tried pulling and wiped. Not because we weren't tough enough, but because we were completely expecting something different.

And this was an entire GUILD!

Note- Small guild, yes. Small unsuspecting guild, yes. Guild that next time was prepared and didn't suffer a single death, yes. ;)
