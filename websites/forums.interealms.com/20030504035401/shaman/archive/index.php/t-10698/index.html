Stuff that's single group doable (or less)

Guardian wurms in skyfire - Friend of mine got torpor that way. All I ever got was Talisman of the raptor here.

Emporer in Sebilis - He's a umcommon spawn, and doesnt always drop spells. But this is where I got torpor.

the Protector and Jumpy in Sebilis - This is where I got the rest of my 59 and 60 spells. Never really tmied the spawns but seemed like they usually spawned every 2, to 2 and a half hours. We'd take a single group to Traks Lair, kill them both, pull juggs and frogs until they spawned one last time, then head out. For a really long haul, we'd stay for another round. I got 3 spells off of the Protector (malo included), and 1 spell from Jumpy. Besides Malo, I dont know which was which. But, the other 3 were votb, avatar, and pox.

I've seen malo, pox, raptor, torpor, and votb all drop from Sev and Talendar as well. Gorenaire drops em as well, but I've only seen her die 2 times.

edit - Ok, one thing I did not mention. I spent ALOT of time farming for these spells. Over 30 guardian wurms killed, seen the Emporer's placeholder spawn more times than I care to remember, and if someone drags me back to Traks Lair one more time, I'll pass out. These are something most people have to work, and work hard and long, to get.
