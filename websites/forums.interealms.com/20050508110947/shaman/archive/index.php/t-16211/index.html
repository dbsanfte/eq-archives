At level 34 you get a summon companion spell that summons a pet much like a mage/necro/beastlord/sk. The charm spell is left over from way way way back (like summer of '99) when Shaman had the actual spell Charm and there were no charm level limits. Then it changed to the animal deal and then level limits went in. The level 29 Befriend Animal is the only charm spell now for shaman, only druids get the higher level versions.

As someone who went up against NPC shaman before the charm changes, it was a 'good thing'(tm).
