The coldain in Crystal Caverns (Froststone Keep) are on their own faction. They're dubious, period, and I don't know of any way to raise faction with them.

If you have an enchanter/bard, they can faction song, and anyone with sneak can likewise get past this. As long as someone in your group can sell, you can unload all the (nice) cash items you'll get consistently. If not, fill up a bunch of bags, gate back to Thurg, sell, and repeat. But being able to bank in CC means you can go a lot longer.

Here's how I'd do it...
Bind out front of the fort, gate out when sow wears off and reapply. Know that if you jump down the middle and run for dwarf town (assuming you survive it, which should work, with sow), you'll need to camp/gate aggro, as there'll be at least a half dozen orcs who got stuck pathing into a wall, and will come to kill you if you get halfway down the tunnel from the dwarves back toward orcs.

My shaman didn't get to play here, but my monk did. Since she can carry exactly zero loot, it was easy to work out nice PL'ing deals with people farming ore. They were more than happy to help me exp on the orcs, and I had no ability to take any of the loot. ;)

It's a great little place. People farming ore pretty regularly /ooc rotting herbalist packs and crystalline belts (gotta love lore). The exp bonus is fantastic, and the cash is darn good. Especially if you're selling off the ore to the farmers, and even more so now that you can break down the larger bricks into small, useful stuff.
