There seems to be milestones for most character classes. For my druid, before PoP, it was reaching level 19 and getting my first port spells. For my ranger it was hitting 30 and getting SoW. And for my shaman it was reaching 34th level and getting my first puppy dog. And I must say, I’m not disappointed.

I’m hunting in Eastern Wastes, battling the evil Ulthorks and the surprisingly evil Walruses. I have my transparent little companion sit in a safe spot and I go off to persuade some evil monsters to follow me back to where he’s waiting in ambush. I leveled from 34 to 35 there today and the system seems to work really well. My puppy may have some growing to do, but the extra damage he does really helps me save manna. And he’s good at distracting the monsters when my root doesn’t stick. It’s just a shame that even buffed he can’t take a lot of damage. But from what I’ve read, he’ll get stronger as he gets older.

Anyway, I no longer have to solo by myself. I have a faithful companion at my side and I’m one happy shaman. Heck, he doesn’t even hold the fact that I’m a cat against me. What a great dog.

Grats on the doggie! Treat him right and he'll serve you well. The first few doggies don't have many hp, so don't let 'em tank too much or "bye-bye doggie".
