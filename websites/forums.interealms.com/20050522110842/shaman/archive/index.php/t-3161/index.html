Shoulders: Dragonwing Mantle - the bigger the better (~13ac+ for like 1k+)
Helm: Jaundiced (19ac i think, dirt cheap)
Ranged: Idol of the Thorned (forget stats, ~5k) or Goranga Idol (cheap)
Boots: Barbed Dragonscale (19ac 30hp 9sta i think for ~5k)
Shield: SBS is fine. If you are rich, go for the stretched fungus shield (32ac 10wis 15sta) or the OOIV (even better 35ac 75hp 75mana ~20k my server)
Arms: Barbed Ringmail Arms (20ac, 3dex 3wis, 3kish)
Bracer: Velious Quest. MQ of thurg bracer will run you like 1k or so.


If you do all this you'll be better off than me, with my 940ish AC unbuffed.
