I've played my character for about 2 years and I'm level 56. Which probably means I have no business responding to this post. :p

However, I did just recently stumble upon some XP which may or may not be a good spot for you either solo or duo. In the North West corner of The Grey is a camp of Revenants. They're undead and have the amount of HP you would expect from Luclin mobs. However, the camp is always open (at least on Zeb) and the xp was decent at 56. It's soloable, the mobs are melee only, the spawns are static, and you can avoid the wanderers fairly easily. It never hurts to swing by the Ssra temple and see if the named golems are up either. ;)

I have to admit though, I'm amazed you aren't /role most of the time so that you won't be barraged with requests to slow mobs in the Planes. I get at least one invite a night if I'm not /role.

Oh, and I've done healing in PoI and PoN. Not fun. Not fun at all. If nothing else, a group at least needs a CH druid. Maybe if they are busy healing they won't be charming even-con mobs just to have them turn on the group at the worst possible time. :mad:

Don't forget, you need some type of EB spell/item for The Grey. No atmosphere and all that.
