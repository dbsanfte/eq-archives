Calabar
No, dying your helmet slot will not generate a graphic for armor that does not already have one. It’s dying the slot, not the armor. So it doesn’t affect the armor in any way whatsoever. But the next helmet you put in that slot that does have a graphic will be “tented” that color. I tried it with my druid, who’s helmet doesn’t have a graphic, so I know it doesn’t have any effect.

However, because you’re dying a slot there can be an interesting side effect. My druid’s leather tunic is low cut, revealing part of his chest and shoulders. When I dyed his tunic slot dark green it also colored his exposed skin dark green. I don’t care for this side effect, but I can live with it.
