Malo over pox, malo is always mana spent well, it cannot be resisted, only overwritten by malosini, pox will need insidious decay memmed as well to make the most of it. It is, in my experience, a highly resisted dot.

Going on your profile I'd look to upgrade shoulders and range as a priority followed by neck and shield.
