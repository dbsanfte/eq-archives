Jaggedpine is really great cash. Do the wisps, 10-15pp per kill and a few are still light blue at 57. Griffon/Anaconda meat sells for 5-10pp each in the Bazaar. You may want to keep it as well, Baking is obscenely easy to grandmaster, 500 meats will take you from 191-250. For ~6k plat (1k to GM baking and 5k for the artisan's seal) you get two 100% WR bags, a great deal.

Stay the hell away from Rathe Mtns unless you are prepared to KS other people's giants and get into KS fights over the ones you aggro yourself.
