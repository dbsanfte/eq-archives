Hail,

Getting your spouse to play is a GOOD thing in my book.
Yes, you should get a router and a second machine ASAP. :)

You shouldn't have made her a shaman imo, though your bias is perfectly understandable. ;) If she isn't hooked on playing a shaman yet (probably is though, lol), try to find her another class that will complement you better. My spouse is an enchanter, and I'm never without KEI now unless I'm too lazy to get it. :D I made several characters for my spouse (on same account initially) and it took awhile until we found one that "clicked". An enchanter was the ticket.

My better half started playing at least 6 months after I started Frekkels, and now we're within 2 levels (62 vs. 60, though granted, I'm 50+ AA ahead :p) of each other. It didn't take long at all to get us to a point where we could group together for exp, and that was with no powerlevling & only very mild twinking.

Husband/Wife teams are excellent. I think they usually make great partners, and I'd much rather have half a dozen RL pairs on a raid than 12 individuals. Fewer miscommunications occur, and there are generally less AFK's that halt the raid, since one person can autofollow and cover their partner while the other grabs sandwiches, takes care of kids, let's out the dog, smokes, etc.

Most of my closest IG friends are other husband/wife teams.

Aside from all that, getting my life partner to play the game eased most of the stress that EQ was putting on our marriage. The whole "come to dinner/bed/etc" issues became less of an ordeal because my spouse now understood what a raid entailed. The "I can't quit & leave until I get my body" excuse stopped causing a fight over "that stupid game". (Of course afterwards, my spouse learned when I was tradeskilling in a city that I COULD just stop at any time.)

We started out on the same computer on the same account. Eventually, it became an issue where we HAD to get another machine and do a character transfer to a new account. Save yourself the trouble (and xfer expense) and just buy her a new account now and another machine. New systems are much cheaper to buy nowadays, and if you shop around you can get a deal that will likely run EQ better than your existing machine.

If EQ is a hobby that you can enjoy sharing with your RL partner, I think it is great. You'll be happier spending time together and get fewer complaints that all your wife sees is "the back of your head" while you are home at night. :)

Take care,
