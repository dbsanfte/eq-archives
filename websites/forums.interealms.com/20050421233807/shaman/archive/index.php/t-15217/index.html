I have fought both in Nurga and Droga extensively since the change. One thing I noticed is that if you take the droga entrance from Nurga the mobs are alot lower lvl. You can move in slowly and they get progressively harder. This way you can work at your pace and take chances as you see fit. The mobs will all be blue to you but they were light blue to my 65 druid. The mobs at the FM entrance area are all blue to him. The other advantage of going to Droga is there is a decent chance at some good drops, even from some of the lower lvl mobs.

After spending more time in both Nurga and Droga I'd have to say Nurga is better solo in the 50s.

I've soloed the easy part of Droga and have found 5 named in the easy part I can solo. There should be a 6th but I can't find the shaman goblin named for this region. The loots on these guys is nothing special except for "Blood Ritualist Taprom". He drops the prismatic necklace of peace, which is a nice drop. The lower Droga MoBs do not seem to drop gems very much though (unless I'm just really really unlucky). They drop about the same cash as the Nurga MoBs you could solo efficiently at these lvls.

Nurga has many more named I can solo. Nurga seems to drop gems more off the same lvl MoBs too.

For grouping I would suggest Droga. You can fight the higher stuff and get good drops and cash loot.
