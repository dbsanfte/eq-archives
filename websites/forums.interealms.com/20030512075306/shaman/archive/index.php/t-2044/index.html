One good group of mid- 50s is enough... basically, if you can make it to him, you can kill him :) Don't remember those skele's, but Kirn will not aggro you until you give him the icon... so if there are mobs around him, feel free to kill them.

Best bet is to park your party in Kindles room (Chanter Epic mob) and kill the Reverent on the left hand side of the room. Kindle and the other Revrent won't aggro you. Hand in icon, and dash back to your party. Let the battle begin!
