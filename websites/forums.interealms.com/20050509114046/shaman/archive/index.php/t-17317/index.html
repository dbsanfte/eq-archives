This is pointless, and purely just for fun, but then again, I'm easily amused. ;)

The pet alterations they did during the patch weren't completely undone. They put BL warders (particularly kitty BL tiger warders) back to normal size, but the trick they used to get them so gigantic (65 kitty BL's could make their warders the size of a small building) wasn't completely removed...and it works on dogdog.

Simply put: summon dog, and Grow him 3 times.

The result: a dog about the size of a large horse. If you shrink yourself, you can stand completely underneath your pet.

Though it has absolutely no impact in the game outside of serving as an obstruction for line of sight spells, it's kinda fun to super-size the dog and sic him on things, usually done while I sit outside an LDoN zone-in and wait for the group to set up for the crawl.

Seeing a pet larger than a Desert Madman chewing on his head amuses me. ;)
