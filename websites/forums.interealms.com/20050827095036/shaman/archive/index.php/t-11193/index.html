If you remember your account name and password, I believe it should be as simple as logging in and setting up the billing again. If you've forgotten the password, I suggest you get in touch with Sony (start from the website) and ask them what you can do. Any answers you get here will be anecdotal, and you'd have to go to them about it anyway. :)

For AA, you can start earning points at 51, but most people say it's not worth thinking about until 55, and then only for run 3. The general consensus is to get 60, then work on AA. Of course, with PoP and level 65, opinions are divided as to whether you should stop for AA at 60, or go on to 65 first. It mostly depends on what you do and where you hunt. (Most non-PoP places are green at 65).

As to how the AA system works, well, that would take a long time to explain properly, but basically, you can put your exp earned into levelling or into AA, and each AA "ding" (equivalent to level 51 worth exp), you get an AA point which you can spend on certain abilities. For Luclin AA, you need to spend 6 points in General before you can work on Archetypes, and you need 12 points spent on Archetypes before you can work on Class AA. Some Luclin and PoP AA has prerequisites too.

For a rough description of shaman AA skills, you could read this: http://eq.crgaming.com/archetype/default.asp?Action=Cat&ClassId=4 which isn't brilliant, but hopefully gives you an idea, or you could look at the AA screen in game.
