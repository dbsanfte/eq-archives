The minimum system requirements for World of Warcraft have not yet been announced. Because the game is still under development, the changes implemented by our programmers and designers will cause the program to run faster and slower on the same hardware over the course of our beta testing. Until the game is near completion, we will be unable to offer minimum or recommended system requirements for the final version of the game.

The current minimum system requirements for the World of Warcraft Beta and Stress Test are listed below. If you are running below any of the requirements below, then you could encounter graphical issues, crashes, or slow down which we will be unable to address.

- Your computer will need a 56K or higher modem with an Internet connection.

Windows® System/OS:

- PIII or AMD Athlon 800 Mhz or higher.
- 256 Megabytes or more of RAM.
- GeForce 2 graphics card or better.
- 3.3 Gigabytes or more of available hard drive space.
- DirectX® 9.0c or above.

Mac® System/OS:

- 1.0 GHz or higher, G4 or G5 processor.
- 512 Megabytes RAM or higher. DDR RAM recommended.
- ATI or NVIDIA video hardware with 64 MB VRAM or more.
- 3.3 Gigabytes or more of available hard drive space.
- MacOS X 10.3 ("Panther") or better.

Until we can provide details for the final version of the game, please trust in the fact that it is our goal to provide our customers with as rich a gaming experience as we can without requiring upper-end hardware.
