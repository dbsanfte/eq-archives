Jukax
If you're 60-62 then guardian camp in seb is the way to go. You can make decent xp in 60 or so-so AA xp at 61 or 62 there - most mobs in the camp are still dark blue at 61.

The cash flows from the three sepsis scarabs in the camp, every single one will drop a gem or piece of jewellry of some sort, ranging in value from 70pp to 600pp for blue diamonds. Maybe 1 in 20 you'll only get a worthless crystallized sulfur. A rare spawn is level 51 necrosis scarab (very easy, not a caster despite the name) who will usually drop 2 gems and either Jaundiced Bone Arms or a Cane of Tranquil.

Once you kill the scarabs, pull the frogs down the hall and then pull the golem up on top of the ziggurat thingy above the door to crypt / PLA. The sebilite golem is a placeholder for a sebilite guardian, who will either drop a crappy Larrikan's Mask or a Lamentation.

If you're level 60 with torpor and decent gear you should be able to clear it all plus pull some mobs from zone out area as well - obulus death shrouds seem to drop more often off of them than anywhere else in zone - I've had 2 drop in the last week that I had to let other people come loot because they're lore. On Drinal ODS's run 20k.

The most difficult part of this camp, which isn't very difficult at all, is when you get a roamer krup wizard or enchanter. The krup wizards can cast ice comet, and enchanter mobs are a pain in the ass to kill, but if you keep your resists buffed you should have no problems.

I sell everything to the vendors except for the blue diamonds, ODS, Lamentations and Cane of Tranquils. And yes, I mean sell the Jaundiced Bone Arms to a vendor - they sell for 340pp or so. I manage to pull in about 1k per hour there not counting the rare drops from the named mobs. I did the camp this morning for 3.5 hours and walked out with 28 gems, 3 of which were blue diamonds which I sold for 600pp each, and netted myself around 5.2k plat total. If lammy dropped for me I woulda done better but I got 3 guardians in a row who dropped masks. Made 3 yellows of AA xp while I was there to boot.

Jukax
I'll explain as best I can. I go across the bridge immediately across from the zone out path, and move down to the very start of the tunnel, summon pet and buff up. You can med safely in the corner with the torch, almost falling into the water - roamers will just path by if you need to med. Once the mob is nearly dead it will start to run if you don't have it rooted, so have a nuke memmed or just root it. I prefer nuking since I have SCF 3 and always have a mage summoned damage bracelet, and if you root a running caster mob you stand the risk of it gating.

At 61 I still can't always pull singles, but it's an easy matter of root parking mobs. Always root the melee mob if you pull a casting mob as well - casters can make your life difficult, so kill them first.

I've been able to solo the entire camp all the way down to and including guardian at level 60 (when I finally got torpor, that is) using just self-buffs. With KEI I've been able to pull up to the third golem of the zone out mobs as well.
