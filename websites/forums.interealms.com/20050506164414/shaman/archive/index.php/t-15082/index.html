I chose Troll and while the regen is what I would consider "ok" to have, I wish I had chosen Barbarian or Ogre. Having played a barbarian shaman to 60 before and now doing Troll (and having played an Ogre SK to 65), I would choose Barbarian for shaman most likely, if for no other reason than the 20% exp penalty that Trolls have. At high levels (my troll shaman is 55 now), I haven't been that impressed with regen. It was useful at lower levels for downtime, but more than anything, KEI has made more of a difference from 45 and on. I don't even have a Fungi tunic. If given the choice between being lvl 60 with a Barbarian or being 55 and Troll (I'm sure I'd be 60 by now if barb), I'd choose Barbarian. I also think Barbarians have a nice model.

The Ogre frontal stun immunity is a nice feature for sure - especially for tanking in teh face with a JBB and a pet alongside. But again, they have a 15% exp penalty I believe, and I'm not sure it'd be worth it.

And don't stick to the regular exp zones you see the "Good" races in, go eat the guards in their cities because there is no exp penalty in a city zone.
