Huurgh
I suggest hunting at the mammyruins in EF. I started there at lvl 24. Tag a mammoth with your DOT, and run at SOW speed over to the Icy Orc temple, kiting the mammoth behind you. When the DOT runs out, stop, hit the hotbutton till its activates, renew the dot and start running again.

A mammoth takes about 3 DOTS till its turns to flee, then pounce it, and melee it till dead.

Rewards are good XP, and mammoth tusks, which you carry in a spirit pouch till you sell them to Starn Bearjumper. Max Price being 10 PP (at ally status and CHA 110+) and some change.

Im soloing gnolls at the spires around paw in South Karana. Whites and reds fall easily, although I recomend a KEI, and the fine steel weapons bring a fair price (4-7pp each) when sold. I found tagging with a monk the best way to do it. no, it's not a solo, but we were killing gnolls so fast it hardly mattered. In no time I'd picked up half the exp to make lvl 26 and a nice amount of cash as well. There is no bank near, so I gated to nexus, sold, banked, then took the portal back to karana and started over again. I preferred this zone to HHK even with the banking advantage HHK has. What I dont recomend is letting someone take you into Echo caverns. I did it, and wont do it again anytime soon.
