The Hyena Shaman
This is what I do. And with only 128 RAM, and a voodoo3, it works just fine. Only lag I get is the occasional thirty-second freeze... but i get that everywhere in luclin. Painful when running through Mons Letalis.

Anyway...

1) Set clip plane to at most 15. This allows you to still see, but cuts out most of the background.

2) Particle Effects, old and new, OFF. There's always some jerk bard in the middle of a pile of merchants singing their food song, and half the time a cleric or enchanter spams the zone with a sanctuary spell (Those peoploe need to be killed)

3) Either look at the floor, or use the overhead view. Most of the lag in this place comes from the 50 people that think being in the very front of the stalls is going to help them sell more. When the opposite is true - if nobody can get to you, you don't sell squat.

4) PC names OFF. See above reason.

6) Sound volume muted. No particular lag thing, but there's usually some guy who got pushed to the edge of a stall and is contunially making a "thumpthumpthump" noise.
