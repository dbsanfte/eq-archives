Wulfbane,

These days, everything in shaman epic is very doable with a single competent group. If you break down the epic into small bite-sized chunks, it is do-able in 3 hour pieces.

Your group needs to be 60 - 65 and reasonably well equipped and reasonably on the pace.

The boots are solo-able by any level 61+ shaman.
The hole is a pain because Kirn is so deep, but a single group can crawl it in about 3 hours.
Fear can be a pain to break into cleanly, and it is a nuisance to clear enough space to take out a golem without adds. Nevertheless, with a good tank and a good cleric + shaman heals, single group can take out golems

All the books and pages in CoM can be soloed easily - and aren't that hard to get.

Neh is easy to take, but you do need a rogue for the locked doors. A decent rogue can pull Neh all the way from her room down to the safe area before the reavers

Rak is also quite doable for a single group. Either kill the last reaver until it stops respawning, or have someone off tank it with 1 healer while the other 4 of you take Rak - with modern gear, resists and AA, his HT is not so scary, and his AoE doesn't really stick much.

If you can get a single group of good people you can do it.

And everyone else, thanks for your congratulations. The Spear is the Uberest thing I have ever had, and I will not be replacing it in the foreseeable future. I was kinda tongue in cheek in my earlier words - not being in an uberguild, I dont have acces to VT / EP gear, so the Epic will last me a long time. And, as has been said before, it looks just too damned cool
