Not the best combo. The Shaman / Monk paradigm is still the way to go, Shaman / Necro, Shaman / Mage also great.

Reason we don't blend well with a wizzie is how they fight. Wizzies quad kite for x minutes, then spend y minutes medding. We can't help them with mana regen.
