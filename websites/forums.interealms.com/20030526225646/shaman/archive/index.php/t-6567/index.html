You should use whatever works for you, of course, but I only swap out buffs when I need them. I would probably swap one of those str/dex buffs for root. Root can be a great tool.

In addition, 24 marks a very exciting time in a shaman's life, you get regen and cannabalize! Your days of sitting on your butt like a robe wearing caster are over.

Keep whatever line up works for you, but I would recommend:

Heal
Regen (has a very short duration, I like to keep this one memmed in my fighting line up)
Nuke (swap slot for buffs)
Dot (swap slot for buffs)
Root
slow
Swap slot (SoW/gate/serpent sight)
Can

I never demem heal/root/can/slow unless I'm dead, I use the swap slots for buffs and spells which are not critical.
