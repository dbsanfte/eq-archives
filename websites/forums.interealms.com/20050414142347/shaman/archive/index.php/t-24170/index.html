Valgoth Ulv'Sagart
Just went through the same dilemma. Decided to stick with CRT over LCD because of known "native resolution" issues most LCD's have with quite a few games.

If the game will run in the LCD's "native resolution" you won't have any problems. Most NR's are > 1280x1024 on 17" and above. Some older games (like Diablo 2) wouldn't do anything above 800x600, so would look like total crap on LCD's.

Granted, most games coming out these days support higher res modes, but we still like (and play) some of the older games that don't.

We went with 19" flatscreen Viewsonic P95F+B (http://www.newegg.com/app/viewProductdesc.asp?description=24-116-232&depa=0) from newegg.com for $250 each.

You'll also get more bang for your buck sticking with CRT's over LCD's.

G'luck with whatever you decide to purchase.
