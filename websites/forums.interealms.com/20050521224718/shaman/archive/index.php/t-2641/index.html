If you'll look again, torpor is listed as being dropped by juggs. Torpor has been reported to drop off of a regular juggernaut a couple of times, but I've never seen it happen first hand. If torpor drops, one would assume that malo could since they're on the same drop table. It has been said that occasionally a jugg will spawn that is 58 which has a *chance* to drop a 59 or 60 rare spell. Again, just something I've seen reported here, not anything I've actually witnessed...

The real opportunity that exists for 59 / 60 spells while camping juggs / reets comes from 2 other mobs that spawn in the same area - Tolapumj and the Sebilite Protector. If you're doing juggs / reets you'll be killing these two also. This is why the juggs / reets camp is generally considered to be a source for rare 59 / 60 spells.

At the risk of whacking a dead horse that's already been flogged to the brink of oblivion, there are a number of mobs out there that can drop the rare spells. Jumpy / prot, juggs, guardian wurms and emporer chottal can all drop the spells - rarely. Your *best* chance to acquire these spells, however, remains killing kunark dragons (plus certain velious dragons), chardok royals and the like. That very simply requires a strong guild or raiding group that most people can't rely on having available. Often because their guild lacks the raw strength for the encounter or because they're unable to assemble a sufficent force quickly enough for the mobs or a myriad of other reasons.

Telling those people to go out and kill dragons and they'll get their spells easily is like telling me to run out and scrape up $300k & buy that Ferarri I've been dreaming of.

Sciek Spellbottler
Fastest way to get any spell, IMO, is to have as many friends as possible. ;) Smartass reply? Not really, but I suppose it could be seen that way. Talk to the shaman in as many raiding guilds as possible and politely let them know what spells you are looking for. As rare as Malo/Torpor are, chances are that there are at least a couple guilds with spares in the bank, 'just in case'.

Find out which guilds farm VP and/or kunark dragons with any regularity and target them especially. On our server, I know the Australian time zone guild had a recent string of good luck on Kunark Dragons and ended up with some spares to sell, which I managed to buy at 5k a pop. :D

Camp Juggs/Prot/Jumpy. Take the rare spells you get and instead of selling them on open auction, approach those guilds that are likely to have/get the spells you need and offer to trade the rarer spells you have for the ones you need.

Basically, be proactive. Don't expect to find Malo/Torpor for sale on your server auction channel often. Even if you do, chances are the price will be stupidly high. Direct inquiries almost always yield better results for me than just waiting around for someone to /auction whatever I'm looking for. ;)
