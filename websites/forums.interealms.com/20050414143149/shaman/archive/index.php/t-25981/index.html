Can the raid tasks be repeated - basically making them raid targets for any given night.

So if I had finished the Calling Emoush raid quest, could I still trigger it again so the guild could do it over and over? The stones that drop for the spell/AAs , are those applicable to anyone on the raid, or only people of the same faction, who are at the same stage of the quest?

Yes and no. All raids can be gotten by any one person up to that point in the raid at any time that they wish, unless that person or anyone in the group/raid is locked out, meaning that once the raid is beaten, you are given an invisible timer upon which you cannot redo the quest until it's back up again. I'm not exactly sure how long this is, but it's long enough that you can't do it back to back days. This messed us up when we were locked out on the emourash raid and then were not able to progress the next day doing the next 24 person raid because of it.

Think of it as any old regular expedition. Once completed you get a lockout timer for a certain amount of time, that no matter what you do, that mob will not be up in the instance. This is the way that the raids work, except you can't physically see the timer, you just know that you have one when you try to get the task again, it will tell you that you can't yet.
Kozul
The lockout timer on the raid events (emoush,volkara, etc) is three days. But, it's only on that particular event, and you are still open to do others. For instance, did emoush one night, the next night did volkara ( with just about the same batch of people), tonight we just did the dojo one.
