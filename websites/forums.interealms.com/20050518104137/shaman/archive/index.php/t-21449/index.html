The fact that everyone here is so caught up in heated discussions about the current state of EQ, is proof that everyone wants this game to succeed and continue.

Graphics: New graphics we've always wanted. Graphics that fully utilize DX9 and are on par with other games. I heard something about a revamp of the PC models, the models need more individualism. What if all our new items glowed like the Beastlord epic?
Character Progression: 5 more levels and a bunch of aa's, nothing like getting people into this game again by giving them more levels to achieve. Make everything hard to achieve, but not impossible like GoD before the retuning.
Spells: With a little imagination, OoW could come up with some really cool spells for each class. Spells that weren't just version 2.0 of old ones, but completely new spells that were interesting and didn't break game balance. This is a tough task, but that's why Brenlo and Ryan get paid the big bucks.
Items: New Epics, remember how proud you were to get your Spear? Remember how it made you feel like you were finally a true shaman. Epic 2.0, especially with the unique (solo, group, raid) aspect sounds promising. Every shaman on this board is going to want the epic 2.0 even if it looks like a Vah Shir on a stick ( I hope they change the graphic ).
New Content: At first I hated GoD, but lately I've been raiding Ikkinz and having a blast. Instanced zones are a really good idea. I hope OoW goes in this direction. Instanced zones mean no guild wars, and no KSing. It's a simple formula really, just put together some of the most basic things and EQ will be around for a very long time.
