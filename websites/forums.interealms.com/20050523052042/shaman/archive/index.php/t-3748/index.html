To stormrazor -

First, edit your EQClient.ini file. In the [Defaults] section add the line:

NewUI=TRUE

Save the file, then run EQ as normal. You should get the new interface.

Go to the Bazaar. Type /bazaar to see what's for sale. You get a search window, it's pretty straightforward to use. For example to find a hat for a troll shaman, set the "Slot" menu to "head", se the race menu to "Troll" and set the Class menu to "Shaman". Then click find.

When the list of found items appears, right click an item's name to see its stats. Right click a vendor's name and you will turn to face that vendor.

To sell items:

First, buy a trader's satchel, from Merchant Trekama or something like that. Put the items you want to sell in the satchel, and go and stand in a traders stall.

Type /trader. The items in your satchel(s) are shown, Click each item in turn and enter a price for it. Remember to click "Set" after you enter the price. When all prices are entered, click "Begin trader". This will list all the items you have for sale, along with the price, so you can double check. You are now in trader mode. You can still speak etc, or you can go afk and hope someone buys your stuff :)
