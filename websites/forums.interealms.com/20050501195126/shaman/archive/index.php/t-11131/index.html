Being both of both a good race and deity, it's my sad duty to inform you that you may still have a ways to go. This link on the paladin boards (http://pub148.ezboard.com/fpaladinsofnorrathtomesofknowledge.showMessage?topicID=66.topic) shows that a Brell worshipping dwarven pally had to turn in more than **7500** bone chips to gain good Cabilis faction. Perhaps it will be easier as a shaman ... but it's difficult to say, and I wouldn't hold your breath.

There IS a much easier way. *evil chuckle* There is a warrior-factioned npc in Lake of Ill Omen named Trooper Vaurk (he stands alone about halfway between the entrance to Cabilis and the Lake) who will give you the same factions as the bone chip quest - only MUCH faster - for 4 Explorer Survival Knives.

The catch? The knives are dropped fairly commonly by the explorers in LOIO, which are set up in a hut near the lake. They are on Firiona Vie faction, and killing them gives rather substantial hits with most Faydwer cities. BUT! The quest hand-in itself has no negative faction. So, if you can find an evil guildie/friend willing to kill the suckers for you, you can faction the easy way. Beats the hell out of bone chips, and I wouldn't do it any other way.
