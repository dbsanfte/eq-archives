Jestice
I have determined the AC/HP vs Wis level that I am comfortable with for specific situations. For groups where I find myself as the main healer I like to have my wis up to about 160 and often put on my bear suit to bump it higher. For soloing I go pretty much the other way and get my AC/HP to their highest while dropping my wis down. For normal groups where I am the slower/buffer/backup healer I split the difference and put my wis at about 150.
I also love to work on my melee skills - so often I find myself replaceing other items to make the difference. So this leaves me with a full bag of gear that I swap out based on different situations. This leaves me with a very full inventory prior to actually adventuring.
My question to everyone is do you find that you keep a lot of gear on hand and switch out or do you pretty much have a set of gear that you use standard? Looking at peoples magelo profiles I see a lot of people have bagged items - but how much do you use them, if at all.

Hrendra An'ei
All of my characters have multiple sets of armor available. One reason is that I'm a tradeskiller, so I typically have very little available bank space, meaning I keep the "raise INT/WIS gear" on my actual person.

Probably the most gear dependent person is Hrendra, and she maintains both her normal AC getup and her resist gear. Keeping it in an Evil Eye bag is handy. She has very little room for loot, as she keeps spell components, sow potions, food, telescope, and so on with her at all times, plus leaving a main inventory slot free for CoS. I used to keep a variety of weapon types in there as well, but now that most of my skills are maxed or fairly high, I no longer do so.

Hrendra having evil faction means I tend to travel with "whatever I need" because I don't know when I'm going to be close to a bank or city again. I suspect a lot of the evil shamans here are similar.

Conversely, Hraina's the least concerned with swapping out her equipment. She has her optimal setup, and since she pretty exclusively quads for experience, she really doesn't need to change anything. If I ever planned to raid with her, I'd get resist gear, but for now she doesn't require it.

Her bags are very easy to empty; if it wasn't for her carting tradeskilling stuff around, she'd easily have a bag of food, a bag of spell components, and nothing else.
