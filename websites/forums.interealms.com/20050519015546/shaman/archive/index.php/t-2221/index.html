Athame
If these are the classes you want to play, I would definately go for the Necromancer.

Wizard - no. Awesome class, but not very efficient in gaining experience as a duo with a shaman. Expect much downtime. You could however do some rough encounters together, but the wizard getting aggro will probably cause you two problems.

Mage - maybe. With the new changes to pets, I could see this work. But your haste is wasted on the mage pet since they can do it themselves. They have nice pet heals, so not too much need for that either. As you have not too much to offer to the mage besides slow, the mage does not have much to offer to you either besides his pet. Again, not saying anything bad about mages, but I don't think it will be the best duo you've ever played.

Necromancer - yes. Granted, monk or shadowknight make a nice partner for a shaman because you can unleesh all your uber melee buffs on them, but I know from personal experience that a necro-shammy duo should not be frowned upon. Basically the shaman can stay alive and the necro can throw all the dps he can scrape together on the mob. Aggro is not too much of a problem, because a necro can always fd to lose aggro. The necro can heal you when you are in trouble, and vice versa. Necro and shaman spells complement eachother very well. Even though some dots are the same between the classes, you can usually work something out that leaves you both with a good set of dots to cast. A necro will add mez, snare, fear, fd pulling and awesome dps to your duo, all things a shaman does not have. A necro will not cause you to have downtime like other casters might, because their mana regen is as crazy as a shamans. You will be amazed to see what a good necro can do to a couple of slowed mobs. I do not agree with the above post that the shaman would have not much to offer to the necro. Mobs that would be nearly undoable for a necro alone simply become doable because of slow. Malo/malosini will help a lot, especially for spells like mez. Regen/torpor will help dearly not only in danger but also for mana regeneration. HP buffs will always make necro's happy. Also, the recent change to pets will make this duo become even more powerful for gaining experience.

Xatrei
Of the choices you listed, I'd say a necro. Of the choices everyone else is adding, I'd still say necro ;-)

Necros are my favorite partner when duoing, and it's the class that I use when I'm 2-boxing with my shaman (although I also sometimes 2 box with my monk or SK). Just in terms of raw damage dealing, a necro / shaman team is pretty incredible - Tons of DoTs stacked on, pets flailing away (in situations where pets are easily managed - even more now with the new changes), necro FDs if he draws aggro, all while the shaman melees / JBBs away.

This combo makes for very little down time also. You almost never have to wait for mana since the necro has lich / demi lich / arch lich on throughout fights, regrowth drastically reduces the HP drain, necro tosses a BoD / Vexing when needed and the killing continues. Other benefits - if things totally go to ****, the necro can do a decent job of emergency healing, or he can pump mana in a pinch.

In my experience, there's very little downside to duoing or 2boxing a necro.
