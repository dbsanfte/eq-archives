Well what should have been the high point of my EQ life turned out to be the most disappointing. We have been Potime flagged for sometime now, however due to retirements and MiA's we have failed to push past CT.

Our guild decided to make an alliance with 2 other guilds playing in the same timezone (Euro) as us, but who did'nt have the power to get past Rathe and Corniav to be PoTime flagged. We decided rather than struggle with low numbers and never get Potime finished we would make a powerful alliance and finish it before the next expansion came out.

So after several weeks of back flagging on the above PoP Gods we get the 2 other guilds PoTime flagged, with just 2 Potime raids we get to Quarm! Now here is where the **** hits the fan for me, based on RA we have a fixed number of places for the raid as do the other 2 guilds, now my RA is way above any shaman in the guild and is amongst the highest in guild. Now don't get me wrong this is'nt an attack on my guild they have done everything for me, and I have worked hard to help repay them and I love them all!

Sunday arrives and we gather in PoTime A and make groups for tier 1, we cleared to tier 4 the Friday before, after several minutes I get raid invite only to be told after several minutes to drop raid, which I do, several more mins pass, and I see in Guild chat that the raid is now full! After sending a few /tells I'm told that my class is'nt deemed critical! I don't have DPS. But you know what what really disappointed me, was that one of the other guilds who were now Potime flagged with help from my guild had 2 shamans in the Raid!! 70%+ RA PoTime flagged since before Xmas 2003 and I get asked to step down from 1st Quarm attempt whilst 2 shamans from the other guild get a shot at Quarm after just a few weeks in PoTime, because my class is'nt deemed critical!

You bet your ass I was pissed off! but more at Sony than my guild, they made the decision based on what they thought best for the guild and I can handle that, but to weaken shaman to the point where they are over looked for EVERY other class makes me want to give up! I'm sure I'll get my shot at Quarm and it's lootz soon, but somehow the victory will tainted with this memory. I'm still stunned typing this that I should have played 100's of hours to get to this point, to be told that we are not needed, I cannot tell you how disappointed I am /sigh
