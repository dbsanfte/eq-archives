Conas
Hierophant Grazan is an undead skell in the north east corner of the zone. There are lots of skells there. Keep killing them until Hierophant Grazan pops. Not a roamer. He has three drops. Clattering Bone Necklace is common, Bone Handled Scimitar is uncommon, and Bone Braced Buckler is rare. You can see him wielding the scimitar or the shield when he has them. He spawns, like all the skells, in a little pile on the ground. You cant see any of the items he is holding until he agros and stands up. Druids like to kite here so you better have a tracker to help or you likely wont get him. Druids love this guy since the shield and scimitar are both good druid items. I got mine duoing with a ranger for exp when I was 54.

It is IMO superior to the shield of Bane Warding for a shaman because of the hp. It is also less commonly seen for sale.
