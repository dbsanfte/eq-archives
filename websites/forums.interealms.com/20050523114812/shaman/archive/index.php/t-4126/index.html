The perk:

You’ll need a pet you can command for this trick. Either summoned or in the

Enchanter’s case, charmed. All you have to do to see if a rare spawn is up is

type the command: ‘/pet attack (name of monster)’ For example: ‘/pet attack

yelinak’. Then assist your pet by targeting it and typing: ‘/assist’ you’ll now have

Yelinak targeted (if he’s up)! This is a great way to look for rare spawns at the

zone entrance!

The catch:

You need to know the names of the spawns you’re looking for.
