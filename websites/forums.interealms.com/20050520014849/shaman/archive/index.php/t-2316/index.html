Warador
Pull with malise, and then root. Just keep him rooted/dotted 'til you're too low on mana, send in pet and run him in circles while pet (with taunt off, very important) bites him in the arse. Don't waste mana on a slow at this level, you don't wanna tank him at all anyhow. Wait 'til 39-44 to actually use Togor's and do some tanking.

p.s. Once you get him rooted and dotted, get out of casting range. When root breaks just stand up and keep trying to cast root/enstill until he's in range and he'll never touch ya. Malise is important because it helps root last longer imo.
