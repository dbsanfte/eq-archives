Snugglegutzs
I PLed a Mag to 55 on my second account awhile back here is a general run down of where and how I Pled him.

Gear: Good 2hb wepon, Fbss, HP gear, DS's are a pluss but not needed.

Pl him where ever till lv8-9. Then goto Kerra ridge this zone has a XP modifire so that helps. Start at zone in there is hardly ever anyone here which is why its a good place to PL. Give him full buffs and pull a cat for him. Dont worry about casting to much the point is to beat the mob down. If he casts he has to med. Have him cast on the mob while its inc to get some aggro but thats about it. This way pulling is only depened upon your mana. Pet is opptional I didnt use one at this point. Stay here till lv25 move deeper has needed.

25-40 Highhold Keep the guards. Pull with slow hit them with cripel and be ready to root or finnish it off with a nuke. DS's are a big help here. Onec agine Mele is the main key. Pet is opptional here to I never really used one much becuse of the XP it took. The guards have pretty much no HP's (1kish)and give great XP. You will trash a couple of factions but if your both evil races thats no biggie. I used the guards on the top floor.

40-46 there are a ton of places you can go, Lguk, SolB, TOFS, Karesol ect ect. At this point you will problly have to use his pet and spells more then mele beat down on the mobs.

Becuse of my lv at the time my mag had to be lv46 to get xp with me. With you being lv54 once he gets to about lv38. I would kill one mob grouped and see if he gets xp. If not disband and go for the next lv. The sooner he can group with you and get xp the sooner he will catch up.

Thorzion
One of the greatest disservices you can do when PLing a caster class is to treat them as a melee. If they get to level 25 and have 5 to 10 skill in thier casting skills it will lead to a very frustrated caster that has to send a lot of time casting on corpses or themselves to up their skills.

Your son decided to play a pet class; that works very well in your favor. Have necro summon a pet and then buff the snot out of it. Pets have no level requirement for buffs, so toss on HoS, guardian, Regen, haste, sta, whatever. Then let the pet go wild on mobs while the caster casts on them. If the caster gets agro, just root the mob and back the necro off.

I only pled a mage to 35 this way, but when he hit 35 (14 hours played; I went slow) he had maxed casting skills. At higher levels a string of fizzles on a high mana spell means dead caster and negative exp gain.

Just my opinion of course
