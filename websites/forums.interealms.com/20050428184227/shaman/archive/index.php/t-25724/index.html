Just got the first rank of the Flurry talent and it seems that it is getting canceled out when Windfury procs. Am I just imagining this or does this happen to everyone? It's kind of hard to notice because you crit alot when Windfury goes off and Flurry gets reactivated.

Juujuu
Yes. It cancels Flurry. I wondered about that at first as well.
What happens is, you crit, and you get flurry icon.
If you then proc windfury, you instantly get 2 additional attacks on top of the one that procced windfury.
Flurry only lasts for X number of seconds, or until you take 2 additional attacks (or is it 3? can't remember. I'm tired from being awake 28 hours).
So, every time you windfury you eat up flurry buff.
It kind of sucks, but oh well.
Krup
Flurry lasts for 3 attacks. When you proc windfury you get the two extra attacks plus the one that procced it. So it's not exactly true that windfury cancels flurry, but the impact seems the same.

If you get a critical strike during windfury, it resets the flurry counter back to 3 (and 15 seconds).
