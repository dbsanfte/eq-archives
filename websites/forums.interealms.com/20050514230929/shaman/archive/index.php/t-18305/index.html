Cannibalize 4 - 2.5 second cast time, 3 second recast, for 5.5 seconds to regain 82 mana. So it is roughly 82 mana per tick. For this we pay the price of 148 hitpoints (1.8 hp per mana) and 4.5 seconds of being unable to cast anything else AND a spell slot we always have to have canni4 in. The necromancer lich spell is the only real similar line. Their trade is basically 63 hp for 50mana or 1.26 hp per mana. However, I have no issue with the necromancer mana regen. The point of all this is that I do not have time to chain cast cannibalize. Give us a cannibalize with a very short cast time (like yaulp) and a longer recast time (20 seconds?) that does significantly bigger chunks. I feel the lack of passive manaregen most keenly when doing serious healing or debuffing a large number/continous stream of mobs. In terms of healing, I feel, with our heals being so small that most of the time when we are healing we are blast healing. Just casting tnargs over and over. During times like these we cannot devote the 4.5 seconds of casting time for a canni anymore.

Right now it is possible for us to get 820 mana back in one minute (10 casts of canni4) doing NOTHING besides casting canni4. Say we want to get a maximum of 1100 mana back in one minute but with only 5 casts of Canni6... 220 mana for 375 hp, .5 second cast, 20 second recast.
