Not sure - but modifiers usually do NOT stack, don't know why these would be different.

I would advise against the GEB's, personally. Your WIS is already quite high for your level, and you could use more ac in several slots. At level 30, one point of WIS gives you 5 points of mana. and you need to be tanking to kill mobs and get your defense skill up (oops, I meant IN TEH FACE)

ac vs. wis vs. hp is an old, old topic on this board. do a search for some of the old threads if you want some long reading.

key question I am partial to - do you run out of mana very often? or do you often start your fights with full mana? WIS' main purpose is to increase the top limit on your mana pool (it does help with increases in trade skills, and maybe with fizzle rates). It doesn't matter how high your mana cap is, if you are not running out and do not med to completely full before starting thte next fight.

At level 30 you have begun to cannibalize for mana. As your regen spells upgrade and your hp pool grows you may come to see why many shaman are more focused on generating mana thru regen/canni than on the upper limit of the mana pool. That leads to ac and hp gear. ac to avoid losing hp to the mob's hits.
