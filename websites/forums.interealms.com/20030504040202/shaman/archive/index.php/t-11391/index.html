Just finished this quest tonight, after some brainstorming with Elyda from the prior evening, we're in the same guild /wave Elyda . all the different bloods had gotten me thinking from a brewing aspect like the 7th shawl quest and such. Here's the deal:

In a brew barrel combine A Blackened Vial (drop in Nadex) + the troll skull blood (drop in Nadex off the Blackhands) + Gunthak Gulf Water (Foraged and dropped in Gunthak). You get A Black Potion.

Then combine A Black Potion + Pure Blood (dropped off a Luggold Initiate in Nadox) to recieve A Glowing Black Potion.

You may be able to hand in the glowing black potion all by itself for the spell, but I handed in the "A Torn Page" (no-drop, all/all, Nadox drop) along with it to the npc and I got my Pure Blood scroll! Sadly the scroll is no-drop as suspected, I had extra parts and was hoping to hook up some guildees.

No idea if you need to turn in the torn page along with the glowing black potion or not, or which one you need. for the record my page identified as pg 129, and was given back along with the spell.

Islowem
Had to do this bloody quest three times, before I finally got my spell. Here is what I have learned, hope it helps someone.

1) Gunthak Gulf Water - Drops off any "pirate" around the troll fort in Gulf of Gunthak. Uncommon to Rare drop.

2) a blackened vial - Drops off "a Broken Skull adherent" in Nadox (least the three I got). Shaman type mob, dark blue at 61.

3) broken skull clan blood - Drops off any "furious" or "vehement" Trolls in Nadox. (50 / 50 on light blue to blue, at lvl 61).

4) pure blood - Drops off the luggald over the bridge, in Nadox. Got all three of mine from the initiates, but I imagine any of them can drop it. Dark blue at 61.

Combine gunthak gulf water, a blackened vial, and broken skull clan blood in a brew barrel. Take result and combine with the pure blood in a brew barrel. Give the final result (glowing black potion)to the quest NPC (Grennik Neltrin).

Some important notes !!!
1 - The combine is NOT trivial at a 98 brew skill (first combine, i failed, so didnt know if it was rotten luck, or not triv. Second attempt, I succeeded on first combine, but did not get the trivial message, and then failed combining result with the Pure blood). Went and raised my brewing to 155, collected stuff again, and got trivial message for both combines.

2 - The drops are tagged Magic, and No Drop, but NOT Lore... so I would suggest if you get extra stuff drop, loot it, in case of failure (I did not, assuming Sony gave it a low trivial. I know better now :) ), will save having to go back to some of these camps

3 - I had AA exp on 100 percent, and when I did turn in, I got exactly 20 percent (or a full yellow) of AA for final turn in/ spell reward. (Nice bonus)

4 - The hardest mob/ part, is the luggald. They dont cast, but have a ton of hit points, and sometimes hard to single pull at 61.

Good luck to you all, hope this helps.
