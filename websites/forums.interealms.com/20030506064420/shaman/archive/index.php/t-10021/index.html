Kaninfrenzy
A Large Smoothmetal Torque is an easy to obtain and fairly cheap neck upgrade. Crystal Chitin Armplates are fairly cheap but have a plate look. Deteriorated Arm Links are nice and shouldn't be too hard to get or buy but I don't know how they look. You should be able to get a pretty even trade on the Coldain Skin Boots for Polished Tin Mail Boots or Tribal War Boots which would be much better. If your group of friends are around your level, you may be able to get the OoIV fairly easily. Other shield alternatives are the Bone Braced Buckler and Skull Marked Shield. Both have good hp vs. the kitty shield but look nowhere near as cool ;). As a matter of fact they are two of the ugliest looking shields. One is the yellow round n00b shield and the other is the red n00b kite shield. Both make you look like you just mugged a mummy in Nro. Personally, I'd rather use a Goranga Idol than an Eye of the Griffon because it has no AC. Talisman of Benevolence should be an easy quest depending on your CoV faction.
Meecham
Not sure what faction you are on, but if you have CoV faction I would highly recommend the Circlet of Falinkan. Fairly easy quest, the whole thing can be done with less than 1 group. Hp and ac on it arent the greatest but it gives a good boost to the mana pool, and the effect is great (2 second Turgurs rocks :) ). Has no visible graphic btw.

Boots I guess barbed dragon would be good for hp, or SS/Kael if you can get em. If your guild does vindi his boots are rather nice too.

Not sure about the arms, maybe crystal chitin or grey flesh, or again SS/Kael?

For neck slot, the smoothmetal torque suggested is quite nice, or if you are after hp maybe you can pick up a ribbon choker for a few k. Best droppable I can think of is the tracking wolf collar suggested, but those tend to be abit pricey on my server at least.

Range slot, again the talisman of benevolence is pretty good if you have CoV faction, can be done with 2 or 3 people. If not, maybe Divine inspiration from ME would be good, should be solo campable by a 60+ shaman I imagine. Sharp claw ahlspiess is another good one, great focus (Affliction efficiency 4) but again pricey.

Hope that helps a bit.
