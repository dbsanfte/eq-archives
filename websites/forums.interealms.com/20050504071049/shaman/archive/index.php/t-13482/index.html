Is nice aa, I have suspended minion 2. Not everyones taste to buy it but for my style of play it was well worth it. Its pretty quick cast time, well in comparison to summoning a pet from new. You can suspend any time as long as pet is not distracted. Also you can have a pet out and a pet in suspense. The pet in suspense with suspended minion 2 will hold the buff timer in there, so for example you could cast haste on it suspend it, and when it bring it out again the haste countdown would rebegin. It is highly useful to me but again depends on your style of play it could be 100% useless and it is a fair few points.
