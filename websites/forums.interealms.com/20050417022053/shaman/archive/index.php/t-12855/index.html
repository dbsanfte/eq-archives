I have gone to many places old new and otherwise. CC, EC, LoY, BW.

none, I mean flat out none of them can match the XP potential you make in SOL B. when you reach 40, yes I highly recomend you go to these other places people recomend, BUT when you have fought there for a few blue bubs, and see how it goes, then move to SOLB, pre 45 do the window/pool room and however much more you can, then after move in to bats and bugs. The XP flies, and I would say that unless you get an unlucky pull it is very safe, and even if you get an unlucky pull (if you have enough HP to take the beating while you run) you are going to be close to a zone line.

this is the jewel of EQ folks, if you want to get up quickly, go here.

bring a friend to make it go even quicker.
