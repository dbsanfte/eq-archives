Crystalflake
Felt that having a 53 beastie, I could interject a lil info here.

It appears to be pretty commonly understood , that at least for beastlord pets, their weapon skills mimic that of beastlords themselves. (there are parses to support this, I'll try to dig em up later)

Basically, what this means for Warders is that their h2h skill caps higher than their pierce/slash/blunt, etc. This means that a warder using a Fist, vs say, a Kedge blade, will have a higher skill in wielding that weapon than it would the blade. This equates out to more frequent hits and for higher damage, which of course, means higher dps than when weilding non h2h weapons.

My conjecture with Shaman/Necro/Mage pets, is that they are all based as warriors, since none of their parent classes are a melee class, so..their weapon skills shouldn't matter, as they would all be capped at the same as wars get...and while I'm not positive what those caps actually are without looking them up , I believe they are a lot more equal than the discrepancy between say, a beastlord and warder's caps in their h2h skill vs their other ones.

This is what I've been lead to understand both from rabidly reading this, as well as the beastlord boards, and my own experiences in game, having a high level shaman, high level mage, and a midrange beast, and experiencing the differences in pet weapons firsthand.

Of course, it's possible it's all a pipe dream, and your mileage may vary =)

taraddar
Ok Decided to do some quick testing today since I was bored to get actual numbers. So I went out and actually parsed several fights from our trusty sidekick fido. All parses where around 5 minutes long on the exact same npc and with SLTW as the only buff. Did a base test with no weapons give but it was only 2 minutes long.

With out furthur delay on to the numbers.

Initial 2 minute base test. No weapons just SLTW
Pet did 18 dps

5 minute test with single hand to hand weapon and SLTW
Pet did 18 dps

5 minute test with single piercing weapon and SLTW
Pet did 21 dps

5 minute test with single slashing weapon and SLTW
pet did 20 dps

5 minute test with dual hand to hand weapons and SLTW
pet did 30 dps

5 minute test with dual peicing weapons and SLTW
pet did 31 dps

5 minute test with dual slashing weapons and SLTW
pet did 29 dps

5 minute test with single off hand only weapon (chill dagger) and SLTW
Pet did 33 dps


Given that these tests are a bit on the short side I do expect some variances. Looking at the 1hded tests you might take it to indicated that the piercing/slashing helped out but I beleive that was just random variance. I also have a 2 minute log for the hand to hand where it did 20 dps.

Looking at the 2hded tests it's pretty clear that the weapon type doesn't make a difference. It's also clear that a single off hand weapon like the chill dagger can be as effective as 2 normal weapons.

So there you have it give fido what ever frigging weapon you want it doesn't seem to matter and a single off hand only weapon will get him dual wielding.
