Smylee
Not to rain on the parade, but PoG armor is RARE. ie. expect to do a whole bunch of "west of the river" clearings before you get 1 or more pieces. It is entirely common to wipe out all the easy mobs and get only 1 or 2 armor drops. East of the river the mobs are much harder, and have the chance of dropping BP/Greaves, but again, rare as heck.

The blue armor you see is either the WW dragon stuff or Wolfcallers (skyshrine) armor. It's not too hard to get providing you have a couple of groups (well balanced) of lvl 60's. However, the PoG armor is still very nice, just rare. My PoG legs are the same colour as my fungi tunic (in new models on an ogre) and are, shamefully, seethrough. I get a lot of degrading comments cause of that reason :D.


Breaking PoG isn't too bad, just get everyone to zone in invis, and run straight to the west wall from the zonein. More than likely invis will drop on someone, and the Protectors of Growth will be on them in about 30 sec's if they are kos. If invis sticks, run to the wall and get main tank to uninvis and wait for the 2 little pog's to come up and whack on em. Once engaged and agroed properly, start killing, and time their death so that you are ready in 26 minutes (i think) for their next spawn. The agro from across the zone so as soon as they pop, you'll know about it.

Then just keep killing everything that moves. Simple :)
