Talsondar
I found 40-45 to be quite easy in DSP on Zelniaks, Large Zelniaks and wolves. Easy, Zels are non social so very few adds. Only real danger is getting multiple wolves on ya at one time, and that is very managable with Enstill. Very big annoyance with Lightcrawlers who aggro even if green and can occasionally blind ya.

So in short, DSP provides great exp, just about 0 loot on Zels and Wolves, is close to NExus/Bazzar for KEI/supplies so it is a great spot to head when you just feel like hunting alone.

With KEI I pulled non stop with Slow, sic'd pet, occasionally Dot'd and meleed with BSH, dropped everything there very quickly. Could do at least a yellow an hour and sometimes quite a bit more if there weren't alot of hunters around.

Good Luck!
