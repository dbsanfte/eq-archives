Everyone wants something - fine. Here's my thoughts, just brainstorming.

First off - assumptions:

Clerics are very irate about even a PERCEIVED step on their toes without them also gaining some other benefits to make up for the other priest classes being able to heal a little better.

AAs:
-----
Give Clerics, Druids, and Shaman a double attack series of AAs. 5 levels @ 40 pts equiv. / level. so rank 5 = ~200 skill.
Give Druids and Shaman a 85% rez AA ability on a 90 minute timer.
Give Clerics an AA pet ability to make their hammer pet persistant like a chanter pet. Let them buff it if they want to.

Spells:
------
Give shaman Spirit of Eagle.
Give clerics a 10 second cast, self-only, SoW/Lev/Enduring Breath/Invis combination spell for travel-only.
Give clerics an 8 second cast, 120 second poison DoT.
Give druids and shaman an 18 second Divine Aura/Barrier type spell.

Direct Heals:
-------------
Give Shaman a 2100 pt, 3 second cast, Direct Heal
Give Druids whatever type of direct heal they want.

Combo Heals: HoT with direct component built in:
-----------------------------------------------
Give Shaman and Druids 6 second cast, 1000 pt. direct, with 500 pt Heal over Time heal.
Give Clerics a 4 second cast, 1500 pt direct, with 600 pt heal over time heal.

And call it done.

backup-healer (shm/dru)
