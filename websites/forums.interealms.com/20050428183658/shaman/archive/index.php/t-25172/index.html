Wandering and exploring are my guides to where I should be hunting.
Talk to npcs and get every quest you can.
If the quest is yellow, you should be able to complete it solo at your current level, or in about 1 more level.
The quests tell you where to travel to accomplish your task.
Listen to the NPCs and new hunting grounds will open up for you quickly.
