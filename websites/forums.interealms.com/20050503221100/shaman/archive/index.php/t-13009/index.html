Kurns/FoB and or paludal to 20th (the mobs don't run when Low on life). The way I found was fastest to pl a cleric was Buff him out the wazoo so he doesn't get plastered. Have a druid cast a ds on him. Cleric runs up to a mob, attacks till he does 11 points of damage to get the xp, then he runs to the next mob, does the same (red mobs of course) When he has about 5 mobs killing themselves on the Damage shield, he runs them back to you in case he might need a heal. Repeat as necessary. Level 1 - 17 in one sitting, things slow down around 18 or 20.

After 20th, I suggest switching to having a melee beat the stuffing out of the mobs and the chanter mem blurring. The Monk Fd tactic doesn't work, I tried doing it in various ways, even during the recent Fd change. Mobs forget about the monk, start regenning hp fast and walking away but the cleric still wasn't getting the xp. Didn't matter if the mob was dead man walking or just at 1 bub of hp.

For the skills the first approach has the advantage of maxxing melee skills (damage shields raise offense for some reason and 5 mobs beating on you have a definite effect on defence, weapons skills are a perk since you're meleeing rather than casting). The spell skills are easy to max and you'd have to practice them anyways even without pwrlevelling (from personal experience) so I suggest making sure you max his melee skills up to level 40 or so when they pretty much cap.
