I have a question about is the efficiency of a shaman as a healer

A Shaman, First has to Malo (depending on the Mob) Then Slow, then start healing - and usually on harder mobs ect, Chain healing. Meaning for the first 22 secs, the mob better not kill the tank, where as a Druid or Cleric has 10 secs with which it be casting their heal. a Cleric and druid, because of the size of their heal, can also wait longer to cast it, to increase its effiecency, where we have to cast it more frequently to keep the same amount of damage healed (mitigated by slow obviously (unless the mob mitigates slow ;-) )) So, we loose 12 seconds of time in the beginning of the fight in which we cannot aid in DPS or CC or what have you, and then we can be typically casting heals more frequently, which lessens how much time we help the group. Add to that the fact that we have to spend 700mana+ in addition to the 12 secs to even be able to keep up with the heals as a druid or cleric would. if only 2 heals are necessary (approx 32 secs) for an xp mob, then we have contributed nothing to DPS and heals (if using Cheal) 4thousand damage approx, for 1600+ mana. Where a cleric would heal the same in 1/2 Cheal, meaning probably only 1 cheal is needed (depending on Tanks hps) allowing the Cleric ~1200 mana and 22 secs to aid in DPS to keep up with the shaman. And a druid might need to fire off 2 (costing a mere 900 mana for the 8K hps healed). A group with a cleric or a druid in it would also kill the mob faster, as they have more time to aid in DPS (in the druids case, to spend the same as a shaman, they can spend 700 mana on nukes) Beyond even that a druid or cleric would have more room for error being able to increase the Tanks hitpoints and AC to a greater Degree than we are.

I'm not saying that shaman should be as effective healers as a druid or a cleric... but for the first time, which thinking about our healing in that way, I've begun to think, what's the point of being penalized for all my versitility, if I can't use any of it? not because I don't have it, not even because I don't have the mana for it, but merely because there isn't enough seconds per mob to USE it.
