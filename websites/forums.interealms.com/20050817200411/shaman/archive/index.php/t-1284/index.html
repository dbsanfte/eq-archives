Bigguss
Most of my suggestions are going to be around swapping out pure wis gear for ac/hp/wis gear... and I'll try to keep prices comparable so you don't have to dip too much into your piggy bank. I'm an ac/hp nut (my normal wisdom at 49 in solo mode only hovers around 130) - so take that into consideration... :)

Swap out your charred guardian shield for a clay guardian shield (ac 25, +50 hp) - you might even make a few pp on the deal.

Swap out your velium ruby veil for an arctic wyvern mask (ac 6, +5 wis, +6 cha, +3 vs cold) - you could make a couple hundred plat on this deal, too. Masks are increasingly cheap, since people often use them to train their tailoring skill.

Ry'gorr provides a nice set of cheap upgrades for you. Don't spend more than a couple hundred pp per piece.

Specifically, I think the gloves (ac 12, +2 str, +1 cha, +5 hp), the helmet (ac 13, +15 mana, +3 str, +3 vs magic), and the leggings (ac 13, +2 Sta, +2 Dex, +3 vs magic, +5 vs cold, +5 HP) make a lot of sense.

The arms would add 3 AC and take away 3 wis- not worth swapping in my opinion. Same with the bracers... you'll lose 5 wis and gain AC... not worth it.

Overall, you've got very nice gear right now... you should be able to make these changes with little if any cost.

A couple notes:

Remember that AC tends to work in intervals... your goal should be to get your AC just over a 100 marker (700 or 800) is possible, over a 50 marker (750, 850) if not.

And of course, just in case you haven't been doing this, make sure your defense skill is maxed each level. Each point in defense is free AC, and they get much harder to raise the higher you go. If it's not maxed, I guarantee you'll kick yourself later.

Hope this helps.
