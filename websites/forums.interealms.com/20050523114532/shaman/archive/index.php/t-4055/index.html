Warscar
Gosh, will people quit beating this dead horse! (Hehe pun intended)

If you are not moving or meleeing you get the med effects from the horse. You can cast or JBB and still be medding. Let there be no doubt about this.

If you are not casting or JBB'ing your mana bar will not update. You merely have to cast a spell, right click or normal, and the mana bar will update. If you doubt my word chain cast heal till out of mana, cast horse and wait 5 mins. What you will see is the mana bar empty. Now, cast any spell. What you will see is your mana bar fly up.

I fight Nathyn's guard's in katta. The only spell I cast on the guards is Slow. If I look at my bar I am down 11 percent. At the end of the fight I will be FM. I can't make it more plain than that. If you still don't understand, you need to sell your horse back because you don't deserve the mana regen ability of the horse.
