Ximix the Troll
I am flagged for Tactics, and I did it through the Diaku quest. The Diaku raiders are in the Plane of Storms desert. They are around level 60 and are an easy kill for any group that would be there. They are KoS to all, so will aggro on you. Everything in the PoStorms desert (Snakes, Scorpions, Cactuses, etc.) will assist them. Regular desert mobs appear to be placeholders for Diaku.

We took a single group to the desert and did exp there for many many days. We ultimately killed enough Diaku to get all 6 of us keyed, and we have now moved on to Tactics. FD pulling (or some other way to ditch unwanted aggro), and tracking are highly desired to attempt this.

This is a very long road, since less than 50% of the Diaku actually drop a key piece, and you need 4 distinct key pieces.

Unless you have a very committed and talented regular group, I would not bother with this quest. You will need to bring your own group to Tactics, since it is not a pickup zone (at least not on Quellious).
