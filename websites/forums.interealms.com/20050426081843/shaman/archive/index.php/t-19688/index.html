Rascalan
I soloed right off of Riwwi, in the castle there is another zone called Coliseum of games.(spelling??) Anyways I used Assidious Vision to explore the zone. Mobs seem to be stacked thick in these zones. Things in this zone resisted all disease based spells, including VP3. Only poison dots and epic stuck on them. As I was exploring invis, I sprung a trap. It was a dead corpse on the ground. When I ran over to it, about 18 mini mobs popped. Luckily none of them saw through invis. Personally I thought the mobs were a bit weak here. They were easily slowed and didn't have any summoning ability. They did mitigate somewhat, but with malos root landed fine. I could root rot them in reasonable time about a one minute fight. Didn't run into any named mobs at all. Not sure exactly where to head to as all mobs were pathing. Seemed that if you found a camp you would have to deal with more than one wanderer. Mobs were hitting for about 300 max, but oddly enough most hits were under 100 and some even in the 70's. Mobs seem to path and path over each other. Meaning would be hard to find a decent camp that you could get away with going afk. There were some really crazy places where there were about 30 hidden mobs above your head. There were only 4 mobs visable. If you pull one you pull all 35. Seemed that they are trying to really push the envelope here on what we think as far as usual gaming dynamics. I would be very careful if you explore and take someone that can summon corpses. :)
