How I got my frog shaman to 55: Powerleveling with a 45-55 Druid
The 2 Box Perspective.

Befallen 5-19 ~This is probably the easiest and fastest place i can think of. This took several hours

Sol A 19-35 ~I powerlevel all tank types here, my frog started out slow, but after several levels it became very easy, only a few deaths overall. This took several days

Jagged Pine Forest (poachers) 35-51 ~ This is not my recommendation, exp was very slow, but controlled and very safe. This took several weeks

Dulak Harbor 51-55 ~I've been here for several weeks now. Exp is god awful slow for my frog, compared to my druid. The loot is great, sometime I'm able to pull in 1500p a KEI session after selling gems.

Befallen is almost always empty, enjoy your time here.. you wont be here for long. You even get a free 1 charge summon horse!

Sol A takes some getting used to, but after you've been there for awhile you'll come to realize this is the fastest place to level for 19-35. I find the best place to camp is the fireplace room in the northern part of zone. (25-35) Loot here should pay for KEI and you'll beable to get some (ok) items that will sell for 50+plat in the Bazaar.
I would strongly advise NOT to use a pet in this zone.
Druid should pull until later levels, unless you your equipment is really good. I'm poor, I went with the highest AC gear i could find for the cheapest price. I didn't bother with spells until level 30 or so.

Jagged Pine Forrest poachers camp is where I got my AA exp for my druid. I know the zone very well. I couldn't think of another place to go. Loot here is decent, but exp was just too slow. If I had to do it all over again, I'd have choose another place. 51+ must pull here. anything less you'll get aggro.

I would head for Dulak harbor about level 45, mobs hit hard and fast here. Shamans are wanted heavily in groups. This is where poor people (myself) start to get decent equipment

Hope this helps

Kyot
Upper Guk is one of the single best spots to power level, I know I have used it to power level quite a few characters, including my froggy shaman.

Upper Guk has a phenomal experience bonus to it, and you can easily level to 35 from your low 20's in a good day of fighting. Just head in and venture up to the ancient croc spawn. The croc spawn is a great place to fight; it is easy to control runners due to the long tunnel, it has several shins spawn to pull (3 frogs on either side at end of tunnel, and the three, two and three spawns near zone to lower guk), well fighting at the croc you are bound to see the ancient (whose arm drop still sells for about 250, legs 350 easily on Tarew Marr anyways), and if somehow something goes wrong there are two zones right beside you.

Things will start turning blue around level 29, but trust me on this, you will not care, just keep fighting to level 35 here. The exp bonus is that good. And the money is really decent, you have the ancient drops, and the frogs drop quite a bit of fs.

You can easily keep KEI up here, because there is a book to POK right outside guk.

If the ancient croc is camped, most people will not mind if you ask to just pull frogs there. And when they leave the crocs are all yours.

Give it a try, I gurantee you will not regret it.

p.s In case you were not aware, guk is not on the same faction as gukta, I have killed thousands of frogs and they still love me in Gukta
