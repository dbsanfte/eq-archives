xebthazu had some good suggestions.
some shaman prefer wisdom or flat mana gear,
others tend more towards HP/AC for cannibalizing and tanking.

not sure how you stand on the issue,
just remember that wisdom's effect on your mana pool
decreases fairly sharply after 200.
i like to keep my wisdom around 190 or so, 200 in great bear form.

anyway, my suggestions if you're interested in more HP/AC:

- a silver hammer earring in place of the forest loop.
- try the coldain military wristguard quest for an easy
extend affliction III item with decent stats. the quest
requires a little reading, around 2 hours and is soloable.
- i just bought an amulet of insight for 2500pp, extended
enhancement III is worth the cost. if not the AoI, try a large
smoothmetal torque, they're cheap and pretty decent.
- swap in a shadel bandit ring and a velium fire wedding ring.
- sarnak battle shield is usually buyable for 700pp or less, nice AC
HP and saves. if you have the cash and want wisdom, go for
a skull marked shield.
- metal linked crowns are all over the place, can probably buy one
for under 200pp.
