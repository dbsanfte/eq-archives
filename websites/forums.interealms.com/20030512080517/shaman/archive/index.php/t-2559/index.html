I used the Jade Prod from about 20-41, and recently acquired a Spine chill Spear camping the Orc Fort in EW. Basically, the same stat buffs (+3 STA, +6 WIS, but extra +10 CR), a tad less dps (10/28 vs. 11/30) but a very nice 38pt Ice Proc that averages about 5-6 procs per fight, so I think overall damage is higher. Even the Drakkel Wolves and Frost Giants don't resist the proc (very rarely) so hunting them for Coldain faction is much easier.

Drops off teh Chief Ry'Gorr, so bring friends :D
