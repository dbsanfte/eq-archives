I debated whether to write a goodbye post but somehow it didn't feel right to just hang up Thurdar's Spear of Fate without a nod to the Crucible.

It actually came as a surprise when I hit the cancel button. In many ways, OoW had put the spark back into EQ and I was looking forward to lvl 70. ... then WoW came along and managed to extinguish that spark without me even realizing it.

So to those that are left in EQ, the best of luck to you. And to everyone here at the crucible that showed me the way to being a better shaman... /salute
