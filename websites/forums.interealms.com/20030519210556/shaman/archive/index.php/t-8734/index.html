Ciele
According to Lucy, Haste of Mithaniel (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3531&source=Live) is 20% beneficial spell haste.

It will stack with the Quick Buff AAXP ability up to a maximum of 50% spell haste. It will work with torpor reducing it down to a 3.0 sec casting time.

It will also stack with the clerical buff Blessing of Reverence (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3472&source=Live) which is 10% spell haste.

It will work not work on heals or buffs that are less than or equal to 3.0 seconds. i.e. Chloroblast (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=1290&source=Live) and will not work with Kragg's Mending (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=2435&source=Live) our percentage heal.

It does work Tnarg's Mending (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3233&source=Live) our 62nd level 1300 hp direct heal.
