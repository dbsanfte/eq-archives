Boinshar
First of all, mobs don't start to summon until you've done a bit of damage to them, so as long you don't use a nuke/dot to pull, it doesn't matter how much agro you get, you won't be summoned.

His pet has around ~2000 hps, so basically just park him somewhere near the door away from the Emp's room, and it'll die after 2 epics. He doesn't really represent a problem.

The Emp himself, unless recently buffed, doesn't have too much hps, around 20k if I remember correctly. It's a necro, so prepare to have Ignite Blood casted on you, which is quite annoying since it's 99.999% unresistable and does ~1200 damage in 90ish secs. His hits are rather low, 178 I think, and doesn't quad as far as I can remember. His pure melee damage output is lower than Yeldema.

He *may* run while low on health, personally I had him rooted for the last 10% of the fight. Yes, I took more damage that could have been avoided, but having him flee through the door, then gate, is something that should be prevented at any cost.

A pet is a must, it increases your damage potential by quite a bit, and that shortens the fight considerably.

A few things you may want to look into:

- Banshee Aura, you may wanto dispell it. It'll kill the pet *eventually*.
- Pet pathing. The pathing in the room's pretty bad, make sure your pet doesn't get close to the doors leading towards the crypt, he may agro the mobs out there.
- Pet positioning. Watch where your pet is pushing him, you'll have to adjust from time to time to make sure he remains between the coffin and *his* door.

Raytes
The Emperor is a pretty basic fight. I was surprised how easy it was actually and I have killed the Emperor solo 5 times now. Best tactic I have found is to pull the pet with slow, then malo and slow the emp. He will cast on you during this time so no fear of being interrupted. Get your magic resistance up so he can't stun you with that Invoke Fear2 spell he throws around. One Bane and one Jbb blast on the pet and it is dead. Then just take the emp to town like you would Yeldema. Conserve mana and torpor yourself constantly.

He will gate if you fight him outside of the two torches next to his door. Also of note, he WILL run so keep him rooted.

I have solo'd all of 6r (minus the 2 ICGs which aren't worth the energy expended in killing them) and it is a very nice place to hunt. Hire a rogue to run down from guard and pick the lock every few minutes and you're set.

Raytes
The chances of doing Emp without Torpor is slim to slimmer. I would be very surprised if someone could do this and not resort to using potions.

Yes once you are in the Blood room you can camp out safely since the blood spawn will not aggro you unless you step inside its coffin.

The Emp is a spell-caster and thus will cast on you non-stop for the first few seconds of the fight. Why bother pulling him with slow when you can slow the pet first THEN worry about the emp while he is wasting his mana on you?

One more thing to note is that the Emp is no pushover although he does get easier once you figure your strat out. He hits in the high 200s to low 300s and hits really fast, and also has a badass pet that can wreak havoc on you if you don't take care of him.
