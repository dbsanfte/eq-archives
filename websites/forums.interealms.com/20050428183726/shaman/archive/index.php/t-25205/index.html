Instructions:
Extract to your EverQuest\UIFiles folder.

Then in game, type: /loadskin custom 1


Details:

You are probably going to want to change the clickies in the hotbutton window. So here's how.

First of all, you need to know the EQType for the item or bag slot you want displayed in the clicky window. All the EQTypes are displayed inside the file we are about to open, so you don't have to alt-tab back n forth.

You need to open: EQUI_HotButtonWnd.xml in your EverQuest\UIFiles\Custom folder (I suggest using TextPad or NotePad)

When the file is open, I suggest searching for "CUSTOM SLOT 1", and it will take you to this section. All the EQTypes are listed RIGHT above this line.

This is what it looks like in the file:


<!-- CUSTOM SLOT 1 -->

<InvSlot item = "HB_Inv_InvSlot1">
<ScreenID>HB_Inv_InvSlot1</ScreenID>
<!--<Font>3</Font>-->
<RelativePosition>true</RelativePosition>
<Location>
<X>75</X>
<Y>1</Y>
</Location>
<Size>
<CX>23</CX>
<CY>23</CY>
</Size>
<Background>A_SquareBtnPressed</Background>
<EQType>22</EQType>
<Style_VScroll>false</Style_VScroll>
<Style_HScroll>false</Style_HScroll>
<Style_Transparent>false</Style_Transparent>
<!--<TooltipReference/>-->
</InvSlot>


Basically all the slots are in order. 1 being at the top, and counting down from there, up to 8. There are 12 total slots, 8 clickies then your Pri, Secondary, Ammo, and Range are horizontal across the bottom. (Ammo and Range are much smaller and stacked above and below each other as you can see when using the UI)

In Custom Slot #1, I use EQType 22 as you can see above. EQType 22 is the top left bag in your inventory. You can set the EQTypes for the things you want, in Custom Slots 1-8. I suggest leaving 9-12 alone, unless you really want to change them. I've found having access to the Pri, Sec, Ammo, and Range slots pretty handy as a Shaman, especially if you have a bag open close to the window so you dan click your epic for added dmg.

So just go down the list, and replace the EQType's, then save and reload the UI. The great part about this, is you can alt-tab out of EQ, make the changes, save the file, then type: /loadskin custom 1 and you'll see the changes once it loads.
