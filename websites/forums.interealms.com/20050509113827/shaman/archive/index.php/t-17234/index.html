Originally, it didn't drop at all. Not even from one single mob. There wasn't a single mage on any server who had ever possessed a scroll of that spell.

Then one day they put out a patch saying "we have added a new magician spell". That patch was some time during the Velious era.

The spell they 'added'? Aegis of Ro, which had been in the spell data files since Kunark (and if I remember correctly was mentioned in the generally useless Prima strategy guide).

Since that patch, it has dropped like candy - just like all other Magician Kunark spells. It was vaulable for about 2 weeks.
