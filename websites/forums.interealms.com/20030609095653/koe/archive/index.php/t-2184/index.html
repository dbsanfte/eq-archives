Bobs,
I collected a ton of these when I was farming in Nurga. The goblins drop them a lot, and there is at least one camp spot I know of that is relatively safe and easy. I believe the Nurga goblins are also slightly lower level than the Droga ones. Nurga, like Droga, is a "one pass" dungeon, where they expect you to walk in, drop down the waterfall, and then fight your way thru. If, however, you are a small race (I think you would be small enough, but not sure) or if you shrink yourself (and I'll be glad to provide you with the potions if you'd like), you can swim up the waterfall that goes up to where you would normally exit. This path is much easier and simpler if you need a quick escape.

Two things, mobs are usually paired up, so be careful on pulls, but I had no problem getting singles at 54. There are some casters; plaguebringers, firedowsers, rockchanters (what I was hunting). Also, if you go deep in, be aware that the Sleeping Ogre spawns sometimes. He ran me out once, because I knew what he was and not to mess with him. He's part of the monk epic, so don't mess with him. If he spawns, run or hide.

I think in a very few hours you could get a stack of salts here.

Hope this helps,
