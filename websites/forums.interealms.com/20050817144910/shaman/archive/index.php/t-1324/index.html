Horses are purchased in the Bazaar zone in Luclin (right off the Nexus.) There is a stable with vendors that sell the various horse types and colors. To assure the best prices, make sure your CHA is at least 110 (anything over 110 doesn't affect prices).

I don't know about the fungi staff snare... that's a great question. I've got them both, but the first thing I did when I bought my horse was to turn off the horse/elemental models. You get lots of benefits from this:

You keep your sow speed instead of the horse speed (the speed of the cheapest horse is slower than SoW- turning off the model removes the horse speed effect... so you can cast SoW on yourself, click your epic boots, get snared by the fungi staff, etc.)

You can duck (to interrupt spells)

You don't have the acceleration effect

You don't have to see the horse head all the time

You also keep the automed feature. Basically, when you're standing still and not attacking, you're medding (you don't need to sit down... and you can med while casting/JBB'ing/etc.. If you swing a weapon or move, it doesn't count... but if you're standing still, you're medding... this is huge. One quirk, I've noticed that sometimes the mana bar doesn't update from automed until you do something to trigger it. For example, I'll stand still for a minute and the mana bar won't move. I'll refresh my SoW, and my mana will go UP two bubbles (because it's just now refreshing from the past few minutes.)
