WM231NIKE
Get a port or run to Dreadlands, Great Divide, North Karana, Toxillia Forest, or Greater Faydark. Hail the nexus Scion there in the center of the wizard spires and say "I will journey to Luclin." She will give you a shard that you should keep in your inventory. Now you wait up to 15 minutes for the spires to activate.

The most safe spires for a troll at that level are Tox, Greater Fay, or Dreadlands because of guards or high level mobs.
Megumi
No one can teleport you to the moon. You must travel to one of the Nexus Scion's at the wizards portals in GFay, Tox, North Karana, Great Divide, or Dreadlands. Hail the Scion, follow his directions, and wait a few minutes.
Widda
I went to the moon at level 15. It's a great place. At any of the large Spires (DL, GF, NK, Odus, GD) there is a white robed Scion that you can ask for the shard (" I want to go to the moon"). Now which of the spires is best for a low level troll? Well, there are two that can be gotten to relatively easy. First choice is of course the one in DL. Ask any Druid or Wiz to port you to DL. Just don't forget to ask the druid to invis you before porting. I had VERY limited funds, so TP was out. I choose to run to the NK spires (with invis through HH). Unfortunately, there is a wandering guard that will path right throuhg the spires... they don't like Trollies. So for about 3 minutes, i kited two guards around the spires. No fun.
