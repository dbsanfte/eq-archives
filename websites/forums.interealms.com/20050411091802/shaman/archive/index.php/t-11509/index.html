There is a problem with the BadWord filter in the bazaar: some /auc and /ooc's have an error that freaks out the badWord filter.

You can use:
/filter badword

to toggle the badword filter and turn it OFF.

This solved the problem of getting disconnected from the Bazaar for me - was happening on a regularly IRREGULAR basis since LoY came out.
