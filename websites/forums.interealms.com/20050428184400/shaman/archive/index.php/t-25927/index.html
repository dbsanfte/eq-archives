Paladins, on the other hand, suck horribly at single target aggro. They have an aggro seal and that's pretty much it. Oh, and when you run the seal your dps drops through the floor which means, you guessed it, you lose aggro regardless. It's one thing to lose aggro to a rogue or mage, it's quite another to lose aggro to a paladin 3 levels below you running a dps seal and doing a mere 30 dps more than you.

Where paladins shine is on huge pulls, esp. huge undead pulls. If you go down the retribution (dps) talent tree, you can pick up an instant cast AE (consecration). This makes tanking 6-8 pulls a snap, far, far easier than on warrior. If your crew contains multiple paladins like mine does, this adds up to some significant dps, esp. on undead. And unlike warriors, you can fire off the AE as soon as mobs are in camp, not wait until your pathetic rage generation catches up.
