eqphreak
I would have over 100MR / 100 FR / 100 CR when fighting either of those dragons at the very least. This is before you put on any buffs.

Typical resist gear is as follows:
Golden Diamond Mask (optional)
Velium Blue Diamond Bracer x2
Velium Diamond Ring x2
Electrum BD earring x2
Froggy Crown (optional)

Basically, when setting up you need to buff at least 4 crap buffs before you buff anything else. And I do mean anything else. If you have KEI click it off (hurts I know but you will lose it anyway).

Once the craps are out of the way buff the main tank with your best buffs (Stamina, HoS). Let the druids cast Circle spells for more resists. Buff everyone else with HoS EXCEPT casters, but you probably already knew that =p

Potions ... not really necessary unless you want to make a DS potion for the main tank.

Ok buffing is out of the way, now concentrate on your main task which will probably be patch healer. Run in after the charge is called and start spamming heals on the MT.

Move around the back of the dragon to avoid most of the AE - this works nicely with Vox not sure about Naggy but most likely will work with him also. Keep spamming heals, don't worry about debuffing the mob.

On a side note, for Vox it is almost necessary to have a few enchanters to mana drain her so she doesn't CH. If you are in this group ignore the MT, heal the chanters. Make sure they are also behind the dragon.

Oh, one last thing, expect no loot! Roll if asked but generally don't even worry about it, just have fun! Take lots of screenshots when the dragon is dead =)
