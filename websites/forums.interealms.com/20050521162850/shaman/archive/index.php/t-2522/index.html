Hey Guys/Gals,

Being a poor shammy and never seeing more than 10kpp in my characters life I have never been able to get a Fungi. I have used a Fungi a few times that was leant to me by a guildy in an EXP group but thats about it.

I have made it to level 60 without a Fungi (not that impressive) and now that I have Torpor I am a mana machine. As most Oracles will tell you once you have Torpor shamans never need to med. I have gone 4 hours soloing chain pulling shrooms in FG without sitting down and I usually pull for most groups I'm in since having the melee pull just wastes time. It's much more efficient to have all melee in camp hacking away at mobs without having to stop to pull. As a shaman I usually pull with slow or malo (depending on the mobs MR) and then just tank til the melee finish with the previous mob.

What I'm getting at in this post is that I have no Cannib 5, I have no Fungi and I have no JBB (since I'm Iksar) but I never run out of mana thanks to Torpor and I solo just as competently as someone with a JBB.

Infact I was lucky enough to solo shrooms in FG next to a Barb Oracle (think he had a Fungi but I didn't inspect him) once who was using his JBB. I thought it was interesting to see whether his speed in keeling was faster than mine but my keel rate was consistenlty faster than his. The difference in the times was because we would both pull with slow and we would both epic dot but from then he would depending on his mana use BoN or EB (I couldn't tell) and then chain JBB whereas I would use BoN and Pox and then spend my time Cannib 4ing down to 1 bub of health and torporing back up to full health while meleeing inbetween (I always keep VoB and Celerity up too). The overall effect of this was that I had a lot more mana available and I pulled and keeled consistently faster than he did.

Would I bother spending 60K or whatever the rate is for a Fungi to acquire one now that I have Torpor? I definately would not unless I was considering making a Monkey twink. Sure a Fungi would have rocked pre level 60 but Torpor OWNS now.
