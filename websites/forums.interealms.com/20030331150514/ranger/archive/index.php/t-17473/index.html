Astroshak
It is very easy for multiple rangers to do that part of the epic in rapid succession.

When you get down there, Jaeil the Wretched should be up. No one should ever kill him, and if someone accidently gets aggro with him that person should be left to die and get a rez. He should be dubious to you, and non kos to everyone.

Each ranger, in turn, hands in the Shiny Tin Bowl you'd used to make the Hardened Mixture. At that point, Jaeil the Insne spawns and the raid kills him, leaving Jaeil the Wretched alive for the next ranger to do his hand in.

Note, it may be necesary to sue sneak to ensure good enough faction. When I went I was dubious, and I used sneak. All the rangers who got Jaeil when I got him used sneak. I have never heard of anyone not using sneak, just in case, so I do not know if it is really necessary. However, I (and the rest of them with me) was unwilling to risk the loss of the bowl due to bad faction.
