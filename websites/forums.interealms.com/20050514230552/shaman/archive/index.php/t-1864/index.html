Twitching is only useful for reducing downtime. Granted, against mobs that are basically immune to magic there's not much else to do but twitch...but that's pretty darn rare.

The last two twitch spells arrive at 44th and 56th level. I'll use the 44th for comparison purposes...the ratio gets a little better at 56, but not dramatically.

Complete Heal takes 400 mana.

The 44th Twitch gives 100 mana (costing the necro 300) in 3 seconds cast time with a recast delay of 8 seconds.

That means that in order to transfer enough mana to cast a CH, the necro has to waste 1200 mana, and it takes 3+8+3+8+3+8+3, or 36 seconds (with another 8 seconds recast before another twitch is possible).

The point? If you're running short of mana during a fight...you should have brought more healing. Twitching isn't a substitute.

There's also all the other things the necro can do with 1200 mana in 36 seconds that I'd argue generally outweigh the usefulness of twitching, but that's a lot more situational...
