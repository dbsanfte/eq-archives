The clockworks are great and are very easy to kill. The exp is very good and if you don't mind being kos in ak'anon is good exp until level 41-42. But I would suggest stopping killing them at level 39 if you want keep dubios faction in Ak'anon (which I did).

The best spot to pull to is due west from position 9 on the eqatlas map. There's a bridge that goes over the lava. If you are running to position 9 from lava storm, when you cross the bridge just go left into the corner there, kill the wandering clockwork and work your way west killing the single clock work (theres also a single pull gnome east), and another 2 spawn of clockworks. at level 39 these things are cake to kill.
