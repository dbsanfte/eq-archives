Bigguss
It's tougher to come up with a gear upgrade list for shamans than for most other classes due to our versatility and different roles. Our roles change by level, by hunting type, and by playstyle.

The upgrade path for a level 25 shaman can be very different (even for the same money) as the upgrade path for a level 55 shaman.

The upgrade path for a grouping shaman can be very different than the upgrade path for a solo shaman (which can be different than a raid shaman.)

The upgrade path for a "wisdom" shaman can be very different than the upgrade path from a "AC/HP" shaman.

Even with all of those things resolved, it's still not always clear (level 60 shaman with a full spellbook and lots of AA will be different than the upgrade path for a newly dinged level 60 shaman missing torpor/malo, etc.)

Throw in racial and diety differences (snare necklace, etc.), server price differences, price differences over time (the bazaar threw many upgrade plans out of whack), and focus effects in there and you have a mess. It's a problem that could be tackled, but it would require a bunch of disclaimers (this piece of gear is one of three possible upgrades IF you play this way AND are this level AND are this race, etc.) and a lot of work.

Also, I'm not sure if anyone feels comfortable with their knowledge of gear at every level. The been-60-for-two-years shamans don't know what's good at level 20 anymore, and I'd probably get the upgrade path wrong once I ventured a couple steps above my current gear.

I've dabbled with the idea of trying to create such a list a couple times... but creating it would be a big chore, maintaining it would take time, and there would always be philosophical differences. Instead I just try to answer the equipment posts I have some knowledge of (and time to answer.) Definitely not an ideal solution for the lurker... but better than nothing. :)
