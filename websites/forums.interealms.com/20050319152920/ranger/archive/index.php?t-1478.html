In general, if your computer halts before trying to boot from the hard drive you have a hardware problem. This problem is most likely a failure of the in one of the major subsystems (CPU, Video Card, Memory, BIOS, Main Memory, Network Card, Hard Drive Controller, etc...) There are also a few virus programs that can affect your BIOS.

It would help to know a little more about the problem before attempting a solution. Specifically can you answer these questions:

1. When you say BIOS boot-block. Do you mean the computer will not complete a power on self test (POST) or do you get that actual error message?

2. If you are not getting the BIOS boot-block error message but you do get an error message can you post the exact text of the message?

3. If you are not getting a text error message do you hear a sequence of beeps during the boot process? If so what is the beep sequence? (i.e. long beep, short beep, short beep, short beep)

4. Who made your computer? More specifically who made your motherboard?
