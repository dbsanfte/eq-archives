Boogahz Dokupuppy
Breath of Karana is probably one of the nicest DD spells for Druids that I have seen so far. Mana cost of scoriae (250 mana for 688 dmg) vs BoK (250 Mana for 772 Dmg, Knockback, minor Gflux, and Significant stun). It may be outdoor only, but it is always memmed for me when outside or in the planes.

The only drawback seems to be the Knockback if you don't use it wisely. In PoA it doesn't knock the mob back, but it knocks the mob UP. Elsewhere, you could knock something off of a ledge or into a wall you don't want it in (in Hate for example). If you ever find that you DO need to move a mob, this is about the best way. Just step in behind it and give it a shove :)


As for Form of the Howler, the Mana +2 = Mana regen equivalent to clarity cast by a level 29 Enc. They stripped the ATK bonus off of the previous wolf forms for that. I have no problem with it at all. It is also a trade in spell in Firiona Vie as well.
