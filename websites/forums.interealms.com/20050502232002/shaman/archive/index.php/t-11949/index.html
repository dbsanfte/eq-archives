did my first successful trials group with me and another shaman as healers. Inefficient? /shrug. We had mana at the end and it went very smoothly.

But mostly, I haven't seen such a glut of shamans on my server that there is enough to have two shamans per group.

And besides, people just feel "safer" with a cleric around to rez their sorry asses when they screw up.

Braxton
Having more than one shaman in a group is not inefficient in the least, at least not since our DoTs now stack with one anothers.

A while back I had the opportunity to group with 5 other shaman for about 2 hours before it got broke up and people had to log.

I realize this is super rare to be able to do, but one Shaman Malos, one slows then we all Used either Pox or Breath of Ultor and started chain casting JBB's. Whichever Shaman was tanking, he had 5 others to heal when needed.

Do the math, if you had the chance to have a 6 Shaman group, all with Breath of Ultor.

3354x6 = 20,124 dmg every 2min 24 sec or so.

now add an easy 6 JBB casts per minute x 6 Shaman = 18,000 dmg by the time the DoTs wear off.

So, 6 shaman could pretty readily dish out 38K dmg in a little over 2 minutes, not counting Pets.

Also, they are all pretty much FM withing 30 secs after fight.

Seems pretty efficient to me.
