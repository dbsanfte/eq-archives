Being on a pvp server, I can say this:

Pets do 2/3 dmg in melee, but spell dmg(procs) are not reduced.

With melees, if they are NOT linkdead or charmed, will do the same dmg they would do against mobs. Of course, misses and mitigation here are based on AAs/AC.
When melees are linkdead or charmed, the absolute max they will hit for is 2x their weapon damage. Once again, procs are not affected by their being charmed or LD.

imo, the best way to deal with the birds is to have a bard pull while having only instruments equipped.. he gets charmed, then MT gets agro. 60 seconds later(or whatever refresh is on the charm), the MT gets charmed, and next MT steps up and does AE taunt and gets his agro. A chanter or bard will then rapture or ancient mez the charmed warrior, and will continue to do so until the charm wears off. This mitigates any interruption the charmed tank could cause on the clerics in CH line.
