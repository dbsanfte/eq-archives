Upcoming June 2004

We have an update tentatively scheduled for June 16th. Some of the highlights include;

- Increase Gates of Discord spell drops through a spell quest.

- Correct pathing issues in Txevu.

- Correct an issue with the berserker epic that caused some berserkers to be unable to complete the quest.

- Improved the Eagle Eye series of spells.

- Increased the drop rate for epic quest items in the Plane of Hate.

The Shattered Emerald of Corruption.
Decrepit Hide
Staff of Elemental Mastery
Essence of Vampire
Eye of Innoruuk

- Evaluate the weight on Gates of Discord monk items.

- Corrected an issue with pet shrink.

- Necromancer pets will no longer despawn after 2 minutes when their master is feigned.

- Berserker snare now has a set duration.

- The Plane of Time will now shut down unused and empty instances.

- A portal stone has been added to the Plane of Knowledge to take adventurers to Gunthak.

Just wanted to give a head's up on these changes since they came up at the summit. This is currently on test and should go live with the next patch. This is just related to BiC quest drops, not other loot on the NPCs.

The Qinimi and Barindu events now drop 3 pieces, rather than just 1. The Barindu event was lowered to a 24 hour-ish respawn.

Sewer rares now drop their piece 100% of the time. The difficulty will be just in finding and killing the rares now.

The cragbeast and kyv bosses in Tipt now drop their piece 100% of the time.

Drop rates in Vxed were increased.

The Primals and Mindrider in Yxtta will now drop their page 100% of the time. In addition, their pages are available off basepop as well, albiet at a much lower rate, to allow a single group path to this part.
