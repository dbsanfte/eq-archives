Yea it happens. Here's some of my suggestions-

// in game things to do
-good music when playing. Spinning some of my favorite DJs is a lot of fun when gamin.
-grinding solo in one spot can get boring, break out and explore the world
-farm

-raid
-dungeon crawl

-read class message boards
-muck around at allakhazam, post stuff
-anything else you do in RL
