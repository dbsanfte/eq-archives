Xev_Grammar
Hrmm, Ixiblat Fer is a tad tough if he's unslowed.

I'll tell you about one duo that works, if you can get the people: 65 shaman + 65 bard.

Have the bard run Ixi around til he's good and angry, then malos/turgur/cripple Ixi. Run around til Ixi is back on the bard. Then have the bard dot kite Ixi, in an orbit around you. He will get summoned, but you will also be in range to Quiescence him. Since Ixi is slow, he will only get a few hits in every time he summons (and you can use your Q's and tnarg's to heal those hits), but every other time it's just like a dot kite.

We used a similar strategy before we tried the duo, when we brought a raid force to kill Ixi and wiped because I couldn't get slow off. Our second try was successful because we had the bard run Ixi around while an enchanter tashani'd and I tried to malos/turgur/cripple it, and then we engaged.

The whole issue imo is getting that sucker slowed and crippled. If you get resisted and can't run off having someone else get agro without getting hurt, you're in trouble. Unless your tank is uber. Once he's debuffed, he's like a baby with a manual stapler, rather than a baby with a staple gun.
