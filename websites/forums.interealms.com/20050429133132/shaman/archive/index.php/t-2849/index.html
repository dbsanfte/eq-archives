Actually, I was soloing the whole entrance area (2 mobs before the bridge, 3 after the bridge, and 2 on each balcony) at 45 with a JBB and 850ac. The trick is to break up the spawns slowly.

My method is pretty slow at first, but once you get the pops set up right it's non-stop exp untill you go LD or the mobs turn green.

Re-pop time is 27 or 28 minutes, I was too busy killing stuff to watch the second hand on the clock. :)

First round: Enstill one mob at the entrance, pull the other one to Doggy who is parked at the zone line, kill, keep the second one enstilled for 3 minutes while you cann/med. At this point I was usually pretty beat up and couldn't get back to full hp/mana in the next 3 minutes, so i waited it out till the next re-pop.

Round two: First 2 mobs are spaced perfectly, now for the fun part. Cast enstill from as far away as you can, and 2 of them will come running at you. Enstill the second one, pull the third to Doggy, kill, keep both enstilled for 3 minutes, pull # 2 to Doggy, kill, wait 3 minutes, do you see the pattern yet? After the 3 mobs past the bridge, I was even more beat up than after the first two, so again get to full hp/mana and wait for a re-pop.

Round three: As long as you timed the kills in the first 2 rounds at 3 minutes apart from each other, from here on out you're in there like swimwear. For the pairs of mobs on the balconies, enstill the one directly at the top of the stairs and the one on the balcony will aggro, pull him to Doggy and even if enstill breaks on the other one, it won't hunt you down that far away.

Other important info:

AC > WIS for soloing like this. (If you want to kite, play a Dr00d :P ) Use Chloro, and every AC, AGI, STA, and HP buff you have on YOU, as well as Doggy. If you have 800+ ac you are a much better tank than your dog.

The lvl 44 Doggy has very good regen, but he's still an awfull tank, even fully buffed. Let him get beat on at the beginning of a fight so you can DoT and JBB if you've got one. But when he's at 1/3 HP it's more efficient for you to tank more and let Doggy regen on his own. 3 minutes is more than enough time for him to go from 1/3 HP to full without wasting your mana on heals.

Buff Doggy the same as yourself, but add STR and Alacrity. Don't ever let his Alacrity stay down for a full fight, he does a TON of damage when hasted.

Your /pet back off hotbutton is VERY important in round 1 and 2. Mash on it fast to keep Doggy as far away from the fight as possible BEFORE he starts getting beat on by 2 mobs at once.

With that in mind, park Doggy as close to the zone entrance as you can, it's almost impossible to split mobs up if they have him to beat on.

You can still get excellent exp here if you duo instead of solo. Just make sure your tank partner lets your Doggy get beat on at the beginning of every fight while you cast your DoTs and whatnot, and take advantage of his hella fast HP regen. The ammount of mana this saves you is amazing.

You are NOT your pet's cleric untill you get Topor. (Actually, I can't say that for a fact, but that's what it's looking like so far at 47.) I don't even keep any heal spells memmed when I solo.

X# of spawns divided by the mobs repop time = the time you need to wait between kills. After you get that down, never worry about adds, just non-stop exp.
