Earthbind Totem - slows down enemy movement speed. Cast this totem shortly before an enemy is about to run away.
Stoneclaw Totem - Taunts nearby creatures to attack it. This is one of your most useful Totems. When the monsters are attacking this totem they are not attacking you. Frostbrand Weapon - Chance of causing additional frost damage and slowing the target's movement speed. This ability is great to use in addition to the Earthbind Totem against monsters that are going to run.
