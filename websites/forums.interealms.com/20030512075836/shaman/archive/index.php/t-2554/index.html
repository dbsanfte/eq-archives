Khayla
Definitely one of my favorite zones in the game from 30-40. I highly recommend trekking out there.

The only annoying thing is sometimes all the orcs are dead from high level farmers...but this is rare.

There are several types of camps...orcs, tentacles, spiders, and geos. Geos are ALWAYS camped my server, and I wouldn't recommend trying them solo anyways until 40's. Spiders are lots of fun, crowd control is helpful here though as they can easily gang up on you. Terrors and orcs are the most soloable mobs for xp, and they all drop cash, velium weapons, and velium pieces/bricks for tradeskills.

And on top of it all, there is a bank and merchants to sell to :)
