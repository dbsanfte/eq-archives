Quiinn
Here is my problem with collection missions:

I believe each dungeon has approximately 100 - 120 mobs in it. At 120 mobs, if i take a 26 item collection, then 39 trinkets are placed on those 120 mobs. That means 1 in 3 mobs has a trinket statistically (120/39 = 3 basicly). If 1 in 3 has a trinket than math shows i have to kill 78 (26 * 3 = 78). I can do a slaughter mission and only have to kill 52-58. Why make myself kill an extra 20 mobs. Even if the number of mobs in the dungeon is lower, say 100, then you still have 1 trinket per 2.5 mobs. 26 * 2.5 is 65 mobs, which is still 8-12 more than what a slaughter mission requires. For the collection mission to work, based on the system SOE described at the fanfaire, the dungeon (for a 26 trinket dungeon) would have to have 78 mobs in it (39 Trinkets itemized allowing a drop rate of 1 per 2 mobs for a kill requirement of 52 mobs). I know for a fact that the dungeons have more than 78 mobs.
