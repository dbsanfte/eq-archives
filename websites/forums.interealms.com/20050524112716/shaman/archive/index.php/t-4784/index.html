Shanelle Heartogold
This is written a tad tongue-in-cheek after reading another thread... but, as some have noted, many new shaman are unaware of the benefits of cannibalization. I figured we could all post our hints/tricks here and then link to guild pages where they may do more good (since conjecture has it that the unknowing ones don't visit these boards with any frequency).

I (as a 58 Shaman) have been cannibalizing for many a level... and wouldn't trade it for the world! When the rest of the group's healers are LoM or OoM, I am normally able to cover heals if we get an add. It's rather a nifty ability, and one it would do the young shaman well to learn. Yes, at lower levels, the return is, indeed, much smaller... yet you still end up on the positive side.

Rule #1...... ALWAYS regen yourself first... that way, as you gnaw on your arm, you are actually regrowing that flesh.

Rule #2...... ALWAYS watch your hitpoints. I have a guildie who canna'd himself to death! As you get higher levels of the spell, it becomes even more important to WATCH YOUR HITPOINTS!!!

And a little trick... pay attention to your mana bar. Canna once, then sit until you see that mana bar bump up on the tick... stand quickly... rinse, repeat. You'll regain mana twice as fast, as you get the mana from canna... and if you're sitting on the tick count, you get the mana as if you were medding as well. I have a guildie who's a troll shaman who has this method PERFECTED!
