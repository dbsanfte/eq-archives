House
Don't try to solo the yellows, and possibly the whites as well, as you'll get too many resists for it to be worthwhile.

Don't root something and then try to drop two DoTs on it: more than likely you'll break the root. Ideally you (or a tank) should act as the root, but if you can't stand being beaten on by the Mammoths (they do hit very hard), pull with slow, cast Affliction and Envenomed Breath through the hits, and then root and back off. Assuming the root lasts a decent amount of time, you can then melee once it breaks.

Takk
yes all poison and disease spells stack. The main question is how useful the damage is. As you get better poison and disease spells, the mobs have a lot more hp and it is no longer practical to use some of the earlier ones. At most I would go back one earlier version.

For example
TB costs 40 mana and gives at most 66 damage ( 10 dam intial, 8 x 7 (56) DOT)

EB gives for 100 mana 212 damage max. ( 30 intial dam, 26 x 7 (182) DOT)

Adding DB as a dot will give an additional 66 damage. So why do it? Well the higher the spell the more it is resisted. Whereas TB might stick and give full damage EB might get resisted.
