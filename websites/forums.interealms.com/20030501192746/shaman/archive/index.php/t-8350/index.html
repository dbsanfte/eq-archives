Azhdarkhos
All the tradeskills work the same way regarding success rates and trivials, and it goes something like this:

At the low end of a given skill, the trivial point comes a good bit before the max success rate (95%) point. The higher up in the skill you go, the closer the triv point and the max success rate point get to each other, until they pretty much meet at the upper end of the skill.

There are, however, items at the upper end that have trivial points higher than the maximum skill you can obtain (the various blue-diamond cultural smithed armors, for instance, are estimated to have a 'trivial' point somewhere around 300, and the most smithing skill you can have is 250) so the high end stuff generally doesn't get to reach that happy 95% success rate.
