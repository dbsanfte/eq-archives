For free anti-virus you can't beat AVG. Get it at www.grisoft.com, and make sure you download the free version not the trial. It is free for home use on one computer.

It may not be a virus perse, but spyware/adware. I consider them the same as virus infection because it is still unwanted software on your computer. If you have "date manager", most free download games or "kazaa" then you have spyware. To get rid of it download Ad-Aware at www.lavasoftusa.com (along with the latest update) and clean it all off. Kazaa won't work after removing the spyware so get Kazaa-lite instead.
