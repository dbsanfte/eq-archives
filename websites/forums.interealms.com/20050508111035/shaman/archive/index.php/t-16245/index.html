Shamsham
Bards. Use them and love them. Two can be used, but preferably three will work best. Put a bard in the main tank group and have him sing Psalm of Veeshan and Guardian Rythms while twisting Amplification (If he isn't at the singing cap), this'll put your tanks at 500MR and give them a nice chance at resisting fear. Put your other bard with your clerics and have him do the same thing. You can have your third bard singing Elemental Chorus (AOE MR/CR/FR with their drums equipped or puretone /disc). If you lack a third bard, have whatever bard is closest to all the fighting (depends on your positioning) sing Elemental Chorus in his twist to get the raid MR buffed. This should help.

Please note: Whichever bard is in main tank group needs to pulse his songs accordingly to avoid rampage. Once RD pops he can stop his pulse and fade (AA ability) while the other tanks grab rampage and then immediately start up his songs again (bard needs to know what he's doing so he doesn't screw timing up and let songs drop).

Jinheim
You need three special groups.

1) MT + Bard + whatever
2) Clerics + Bard
3) Clerics + Bard

The bards sole purpose is to sing MR song. The AE is not difficult to resist, but it is impossible to avoid unless you're a ranger. I usually resist the AE with about 280ish buffed MR, so 500MR is more than enough.

When custodian starts to get low, start the CH loop on both RT and MT. A cleric might get on rampage and die, but hopefully you have enough clerics to replace him. If you wait for RD to spawn before you start the CH loop, you'll fail.

As for positioning, you generally want to have the clerics stand near the little tomb looking box thing, and you want to pin RD in the corner by the door, with the pillar. Before the fight starts, test and make sure that all your clerics are standing so that they are in range to cast CH on a MT in that spot.

As for your role, land disease slow first off, then blast heal whichever tank you want. Chain cast tnargs, and only duck out if the MT is at full health. I have never gotten MR slow to land, and slow only slightly lands on RD, so it isn't really worth trying turgur's. Your time is better spent healing.

Beyond that, there are really only two important things:

1) Lots of people. This is really a zerg fight. You want to get as many people as possible and just wail on RD to kill him as fast as possible. Melees disc early. Casters chain nuke. Someone said that this fight should take no longer than a minute, but I think that is an exaggeration. It usually takes us just under 3 (unless I am confusing him with MM).
2) Beefy tanks. If your tanks just cannot survive even when the CH loop is successfully established, then they just might not be good enough tanks. Get them some better gear and some more AA, and try again.

Good luck.
