Jaradis
Don't expect the Legends transfer to work at all.

Except for 2 or 3 items nearly all of your gear is droppable. If you had the cash to blow you could just as easily put all those items on a mule, get him sent to legends. Create a new character on Legends, put the gear on him. Cancel Legends and have him transferred to the server you want (characters created on Legends can be move to any server with gear intact). Then transfer your shaman over to the server you want, and give him back his gear. You'll lose the no drop stuff but will have the rest.


Honestly though you would be better off liquidating the gear you have on Ayonae Ro and seeing if you can trade plat for plat (or even gear for gear) with someone on the server you want to go to.
