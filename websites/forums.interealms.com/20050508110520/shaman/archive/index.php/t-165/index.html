Naagruz
Being KOS is prolly the biggest joy of playin a troll. You will be best off if you assume that every single frikkin mob outside of Grobb wants you dead. You will want to learn yer way around sewers to get thru zones like FP and Queynos and tricks like swimmin thru the front of the boat to board it. NK is the only druid ring you really need to invis for and it don't hurt to always have invis up when travelling. There are mean azz wandering NPC casters that can make you miserable.

You can pretty much bank in any evil town and in neutral places like CC, Thurg, and OT. It really isn't hard to expand yer banking options thru faction work, but it does take time.

Just being aware and conning everything will save you the grief of dying many a time. Best example I can think of is opening day of RoK when hordes of elfs, dorfs, and barbies kept trying ta get off the Bloated Belly at the OT dock. They were dying in droves repeatedly because they had no clue that you get a false con from a boat. A day or two experience as a troll cuda saved them so much pain :p

Troll Tips:

1. If it is short kill it.
2. If it scowls at you kill it.
3. If it is named after vegetation or animals kill it.
4. If it kills you, bring friends back and kill it.
