Shabby
Kromriff Military Leggins (14AC +5 Wis) are decent leggings for a Shaman. You may be a tad low to do the quest at lv. 33, but you could certainly do it in your low 40's if you had a bit of help.

You'll need to do three things

1) Kill wolves and Giant Scouts in GD to get Coldain Faction up.

2) Go to Crytal Caverns and hunt orcs until you get a bottle of no-drop Karsic Acid (you can kill Spider Queen there and use her Sceptre but that is a tad harder because the queen a tough mob for you).

3) Kill a Blood Wolf in GD and loot his Blood Wolf Harness, or buy one in the bazaar.


You can read about the rest of the quest on www.everquest.allakhazam.com, just check out Thurgadin Quests.


For the final step, you'll need some help to kill Fergul, one of the named giants in GD unless you are 50+ or so.
