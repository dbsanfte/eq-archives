Burst Agro and Shroud of Stealth Fixes Incoming

There was a problem introduced with the patch on 4-8-03 that caused unusual amounts of agro to be created by those doing large amounts of damage in a very short time (including things like crits, Manaburn and Trueshot). We are aware of the problem and we're working on a fix for that right now. We hope to patch to repair the issue some time in the next 24 hours.

There has been an increase in the number of NPCs that can see through Shroud of Stealth. We are aware of this issue and are working on a fix for it. I can't say for certain when it will be fixed, but we are aware of it.

Thank you for your patience while we correct these issues.
