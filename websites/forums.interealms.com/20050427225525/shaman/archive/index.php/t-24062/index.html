It's not the XP per mob that is important, it is the XP per hour that is. I went to Nurga at level 66 to test it as a hot-spot. Many of the mobs were light blue to me so I wasn't nearly getting the XP that I could have. But I was still getting 2% of regular xp an hour duoing. This is around 30% of an AA an hour. About what I get in a zone where I am only hunting dark blues.

Thing was, I was pulling 5-6 mobs back to my team and killing them there. Even in these numbers they didn't provide much of a threat at all. Much of the time I was running around looking for more mobs to kill.

So, I don't recommend Droga post-65. But, if you still see a lot of dark blues there it could be a very nice place to grind. Duoing for 40-50% of an AA per hour should be very possible. Plus you get good plat, good faction, tribute items and run very little risk.
