snapdragn
Kalamari: I'm not yet 60th lvl, and I've never been into HoT, but I'm pretty sure if I say something wrong some of the other Shamans here will correct me (gently, I hope).

If you are going to be main slower at lvl 56, you are going to get beat on, a lot. You will probably be hanging out right up there next to the MA so that when you get aggro the MA doesn't have to chase the dragon all over the place. You need to be tough enough to take some hits. So Gloves with an AC of 2 aren't really desireable.

You make a good point about how you don't need to max your HPs because you can get it back quickly and you are usually at 80%. If you aren't at full hps, it doesn't matter if you have 1700 or 2000 hps.

Now apply the same logic to mana. If you are at 80% mana, it doesn't matter how much your max is...only how fast you can get it back. The extra mana capacity from 20 pts of Wisdom is only going to matter if you can refill it quickly. Which we can do, but only by sacrificing HPs.

AC/HPs are better than Wisdom for 2 basic reasons:

1) if you aren't using them (converting to mana) it will help keep you alive.
2) HPs regen is *extremely* easy to get...everybody has some sort of regen, and there are plenty of healing spells from many classes including CH. Available mana regen is much lower.

I suggest upgrading without trading away any of your current setup. Try 2 or 3 raids as main slower with your current gear, then do 2 or 3 with your new gear setup. If you decide that you want to go back to 225 Wis you can just swap the gear out of your bank. But I don't think you will :)

These points are moot if you are a low agro shaman, mainly because then your AC sacrifices are for nothing, but if you are the highest shaman, you are THE high agro shaman in those situations, and as such, should tailor your gear for it.
