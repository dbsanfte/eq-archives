We also got a look at the Plane of Justice, an interstellar prison for some of the universe's worst criminals, held prisoner by the divine Tribunal (a trio of three gods of justice and a selectable religion choice for player characters). From what I remember the Tribunal was 6 not 3, although I suppose I could be remembering incorrectly. To fight the 7th hammer you must appease each of the 6, by facing a challenge from each of them. Having down all this you will then be allowed to face their champion-the 7th hammar. He was a straight up melee mob but the 6, if I remember correctly will cast beneficial spells on him during the fight (no heals that I saw though).
He hit for as high as 1620 during the fight I witnessed.

Also, I don't believe the zones are staggered or stepping stones to get to the next zones. They really seem to be trying to make all the zones accessible to everyone. One major change is that there is no longer a need for corpse recovery in any zone (according to those I talked with). An example of this is the Plane of Innovation which is based on I assume, gnomish technology, in which there is a series of sewage type pipes that lead back to the beginning and explain how your corpses come to be dumped back there.

Anyway, I wouldn't worry too much about faction yet and the expansion looks really well done. How it will play is another story.
