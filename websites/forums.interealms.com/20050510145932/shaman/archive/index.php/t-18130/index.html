Actually, you are both correct, even for American pronunciation.

Acceptable pronunciation for catch is "kech" or "kach" with the e being used as in the word "pet" and the a being used as in the word "pat".

Acceptable pronunciation for patch is "pach" where the a is used as in the word "pat".

In the UK the a format is used, as in many parts of the US. I think the e format was probably added to save US citizens from having to define an entirely new language for some folks in the South. :)
