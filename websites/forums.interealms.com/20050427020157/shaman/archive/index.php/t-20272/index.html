Goldark
BoT is great xp, the other place to look into getting access for is PoTactics. You've 2 choices:

1) Raid MB in PoI Factory
2) Collect the 4 different "quarters of a diaku emblem" from diaku in the desert area of PoS, turn these in to get "quest access". This means you can xp in the zone, but it doesn't give progression beyond it.

Tactics has some nice soloing spots but you'll want level 65 and some AAs first. You won't find pickup groups there like you do in BoT, but if you can put a group together yourself, xp is on a par or better than BoT and you get the chance of some ornate and SP drops and a few other goodies.

For Sol Ro you need to be in a serious raiding guild, there are many many PoP bosses you need to kill and there is no quest access for Sol Ro.
