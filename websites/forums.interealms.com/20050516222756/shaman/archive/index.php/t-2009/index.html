If you can find one, a sentry bracer (ac 9, +2 wis/int, +15 mana/hp, +5 poison/disease, +2 magic, +3 dex) would be a nice upgrade, and won't cost much more than the RMB you'd be replacing.

Good news is that the clay guardian and goranga swaps could actually leave you with more cash than you started with.

While the mentioned upgrades will help a little, the stuff you seriously will get the most benefit from are exactly what you'd expect: Iksar regen BP / Fungi staff (mana free healing), JBB (mana free damage), or a horse (increased mana regen).
