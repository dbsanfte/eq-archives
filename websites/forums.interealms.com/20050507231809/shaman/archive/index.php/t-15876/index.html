Xev_Grammar
The absolute best crawler (but more likely better 60+) is a bard with some AAs, Flowing Thought items, and some toys. With eye of zomm, bind sight, tiny cloak, and pacification for exploration, mez, charm, dawn (gate to bind), for crowd control, fading memories (64+ AA) for pulling/splitting/aborting pulling, decent tanking ability, decent melee ability (armed with salindrite dagger and fleshgrinder), decent damage ability (melee or dots or DS or charm), decent DS ability, decent utility, decent resist debuff/slower, best resist buffer, good healer-over-time/mana-giver, snarer, etc., etc., etc., etc., etc.

Bards can run and click/sing at the same time and unless they're bash-interrupted or fizzle, they'll always get something out. For instance, take the annoying Dragon Necropolis trap that spawns 5-6 tiny shadowstep-proccing spiders. There's no way anybody can channel anything through that-- but get a bard with his melody of mischief and you're golden.

Bards can even escape with you. Hand them a 10 second gate potion and in an emergency watch as the bard deft-dances and gates with 10 mobs on him.

Bards can take a mob, charm it, and have it fight 2-3 adds, while the shaman maloses and roots those adds so that when charm breaks, the bard simply has to recharm and send it back in-- you can virulent paralysis and the bard can mez to push mobs in position.

Bards and shamans can even take out unslowable summoning mobs by simply having bard kite and shaman quiescence the bard.

Bards and shamans can do sooo much. Bards can go nonstop if they want to, and so can shamans. It may take the bard/shaman combo a little while to kill something, but they will succeed more often than not-- hehe, my brother and I even got Vhakiz the Shade down to 90% before dying a horrible death :) Had a bad double-hit in the thousands that killed the bard and then me.

Bard can take a mob, attack another mob through a locked door (pacifying others first), break charm, get summoned, then fading memory. Then bard can control the summoning mob and let you agro it with your doggy and then you can get summoned too, then mez mob while both of you camp to clear agro (i.e. how to get your spirit wracked cords without asking for a rogue).

Oh yea, and since bards aren't affected by mana songs, KEI, etc., they have another unique advantage-- they simply cannot be manadrained.

Bards + Shamans are made for each other.
