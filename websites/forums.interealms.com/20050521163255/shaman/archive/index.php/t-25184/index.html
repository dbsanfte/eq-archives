Yes, call it OPES. The Organization of Potion Exporting Shamans.

Cartels are bad in general, because it's a known consequence from Game Theory (invented by John Nash, Jr. - the subject of "A Beautiful Mind") that cheating on the cartel, by lowering prices, is always beneficial to the cheater. Cartels with many members are even worse, because it becomes impossible to monitor every member to see if they stick to the agreement.

In short, this idea wouldn't work.
