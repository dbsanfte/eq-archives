If you insist on soloing (as I have in the past), then here's some of the fastest experience I personally have found...and believe me, I've looked long and hard...

1. The Deep. Go to the Deep and pull the first 5 or 6 mushrooms. Spawn time is about 20 minutes. I get 1% regular experience every 2 kills there, and that was as a Troll with a 20% exp penalty. So, if you're anything but a troll or iksar, you'll get even better exp. Mobs hit for ~130 max and occasionally (but not always) run on low health. Keep em rooted. As far as tactics are concerned, I pull with slow, root them, put the pet on them, and use Epic, Bane, and then JBB them to death. Even if you have no Epic or JBB, I'm sure you can still do this - just will take a bit more mana. Just get a KEI before you go. The spot is safe (right at the zone line), always quiet - as people rarely come here, and is good exp. Loot is non-existent unless you get a lucky named. (I saw one named in about a week).

2. Plane of Justice. Go to the 2nd cell block and turn left. Go to the end room and you'll see 2 NPCs - a dark elf (Captain something) and a wood elf. Sit on the bed in the corner opposite of the wood elf and pull them - they will come single. Use the same tactic as described above. Make SURE that you root these, as they will run. Once you kill those two, go across the hall and pull the 3 Wraiths (I think that's what they are called - anyway, you'll see what i mean). There are also 2 gnome casters in the room with the Wraiths but I left those alone. The wraiths do not run. Make sure before you pull that the hallway is clear of rats or Sentries - it usually is. After the wraiths, you may try to pull a rat or sentry from the hallway. Again, pull them into the room where you parked the dog and make sure you root them. Might want to pull rats with Annul magic to get rid of their SoW. Experience is 1% every 2 to 3 kills. Loot is a bit better, with an occasional Diamond or Blue Diamond.

Ive looked far and wide for good experience and this is so far the best I've found solo. The best experience overall of course is going to be grouped in Tier 2 (or even Tier 1) planes. It's less downtime and less work, but I do understand the desire to solo.

Good luck.
