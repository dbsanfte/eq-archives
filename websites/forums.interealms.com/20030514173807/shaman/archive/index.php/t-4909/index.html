Highly Useful
Mental Clarity 3 - Prereq for Canni 5
Cannibalization 5 - Uber uber uber. Uber. Should be the first thing you go for, especially with the new cheal i.e. you don't need torpor to use this skill.
Quick Buff 3 - This skill will save your life more than any other. The ability to torpor in 3 seconds instead of 6 (without focus items) is unbelievably useful. Shamans catch aggro alot, and being able to tab to yourself and quickly torpor comes in handy constantly. Plus without it you can write a book in the time it takes to cast group focus.
Spell Casting Mastery 3 - Doesn't matter what your playstyle is, less mana for spells = good.
Improved Natural Durability 3 - Free hps are > all.
MGB - Pain in the ass to save for but worth it in raid situations.
Combat Agility - Get hit 10 percent less, its nice.

Medium Useful
Channeling Focus 3 - One of those "hard to say when it actually works skills", but I guess it all adds up.
Spell Casting Fury 3 - Depends how often you nuke.
Spell Casting Reinforcement/Mastery - Good to have. Buffs last longer and having SCRM will make Torpor last for 7 ticks. Getting SCR1 will make Torpor last for 6 ticks (1800hps).
Combat Stability - The worst of the 3 (agility/stability/dura).
Healing Adept - Depends how often you heal out of group, doesn't effect the new heal tho.
Healing Gift - Again depends how often you heal out of group.
Rapid Bear - Um.
Alchemy Mastery - Um.
Pet Discipline - I guess...
