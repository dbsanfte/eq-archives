I'd go 25 to wis, 5 to stam. Max starting wis cause the more you have to start, the less wis gear you have to wear. Most of the time wis gear lacks in other important stats like ac and hps. So the more you have to start with, the more options are open gear wise if you still wanna maintain a decent wis level. I'd say if you got over 150 wis with gear, work on ac/hps from there. Velium Fire Rings (6AC 65hp) are good. regen bp of some sort if you can snag it. GL!
