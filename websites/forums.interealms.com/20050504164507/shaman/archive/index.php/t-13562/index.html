Guardian Wurms tend to split about 50-70% of the time.
A single split from the wurms is possible, but that only happened once.
Usually 2 mobs split out of the wurm.
A split wurm never split though.

Only once did we encounter one of those bugged super regeners.
Our group at the time was me, other shaman, SK, wizard, necro, and bard.
We managed to kill it, but it was a rough fight.
Everyone was nuking hard.
Wizard was almost oom.
We got him down to about 30% before we finished him off with a HT.
They are a real bitch to kill, since they regen about 3-4% health every tick.
I estimate they have something like 20k hp total or maybe a bit less.
Three percent HP regen per tick means you have to do at least 600-800 damage per tick just to start beating it's regen rate.
Suppose it regens 4% per tick, and you are doing 5% damage in total per tick, it means you will be doing 1000 damage per tick, and you will kill it in 100 ticks, or 10 full minutes, and end up doing 100,000 damage in total to kill it. They really suck. You have to kill them as fast as you can so you avoid them healing. If it regens 4% per tick, and you do 9% damage total per tick, you'll have it dead in 20 ticks instead, and you'll end up doing 1800 x 20 = 36000 damage in total to kill it instead. Much less mana spent to kill it then.

One main thing to watch out for, well 2 things, is Hoshkar and Silverwing.
They path down to the end of the bridge from the zone in, and have a HUGE aggro radius.
Their social aggro seems to be a lot bigger than their plain old aggro radius.
If you fight a mob behind the blue glowy thing on the ramp down to the lava, you should be safe from adds from the Dragons.
I really want for my guild to kill those 2 dragons to get them out of the way so we can actually use the zone for xp.
The super fast respawn time is nice to be able to pull mobs for xp.
