Tauran
I do 4c regularly and can confirm whats been said above. Adept spawns in front left (common lizard bone, rare fleshgrinder), Ritualist spawns in back right (common mask, rare lizard tooth), and Terrorclaw ph is the back roamer raptor (common necro shoulders, rare raptor tooth). The camp isn't that hard if you have an all 60 group with good enc, clr, shm, war/sk/pally, rog, rog (or rog/monk). The tough parts are break in and pulling the corners. Just make sure your enchanter is using rapture on the browns and raptors (kill raptors and Thul defenders first, leave the Thul Justicators last as they have unresistable ch- you gotta kill these about 4 times and they have a ton of hps). Have your MT pull the corners and make sure to wait for the wandering mob on the wall you're not on, he will add if near the corner. Also, Malo every mob before slowing, or you'll get alot of resists from the lizzys.
