jaggedpine forest in the gnoll caves are great exp for that lvl. There is a spot that is just past the drop off or pit that you both could work really well. Durdend
You could try Vampyre Bats in Maiden's Eye. I was douing those easily at lvl55 with a lvl47 paladin. From the Dawnshroud Peaks zoneline, head right. There's a hill that overlooks a Coterie Castle, don't pull the coterie, as they are a real pain in the arse.

Set up on the hill, back up near the zone wall so you wont get any roamer adds. Then pull the wandering Vampyre Bats from below. Slow, EB and dogdog - not too hard at all. Exp isn't too bad, but there's TONS of bats so there's plenty to pull.
