Grippa
Seems to be a little confusion here. There are two parts of the quest that involve gathering "books" from CoM. The first part is where you gather 6 items and combine them in a booklet container. This is given to the spirit in the tower.

Next you have to kill Lord Ghiosk and loot from him 3 "books". These are given to the underwater spirit, who then tells you to find the High Scale Icon and give it to Kirn.

Neither of these book stages gets you any items from the spirits, they just give you instructions as to what to do next.

I'd suggest getting the books from Ghiosk and handing them in again, just to make sure you are at the stage of the quest you think you are. It would be a shame to get to Kirn in the Hole (not easy to get to) and have him eat your icon.
