Jinheim
This is the formula for stamina:

( Level / 20 ) * ND modifier.

After 255, it is halved, so:

[ ( Level / 20 ) * ND modifier ] / 2

At 65 with ND3 you get 3.575 hp per stamina, after 255 it is 1.7875 - Don't ask me how it rounds b/c I have no idea.

At 61 with no ND you will be getting 3.05 HP per stamina.
