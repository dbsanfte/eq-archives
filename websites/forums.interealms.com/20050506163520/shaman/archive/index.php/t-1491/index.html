There are vague categories of them:

Chipped/flawed (common off CS wyverns) are the cheapest: ~200pp

Crushed (drop off tougher mobs like Sentient suits)
up to 500pp

BP gems (Sirens and DN drops)
1-2kish (3k for plate one?)

Prices are extremely variable on all of these but this is what I see them going for on xegony on average.
