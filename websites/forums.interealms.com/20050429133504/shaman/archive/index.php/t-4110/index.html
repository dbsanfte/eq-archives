Knowsow
Nope nope, yer all wrong. I know how to make the plat fast :) So much of it, that at lvl 48 as an ogre after a MONTH of soloing I made enough to pay 37k for my JBB and continue to be fully equiped (255wis was 2500 mana before mana cap went away)

Do the Coldain Military Bracer MQ.

There is a great thread on Allakhazam about this.

Things to know is that if you aren't a Rallos Ogre/Troll (but what Shaman ISNT a rallos ogre? ^_^) just make and alt and get him a port to GD, then find sow, lev, and invis and walk to CC and camp him. He will loot everything for the quest, just log him on when something drops.

Another good thing to know is that you can buy a Blood Wolf harness in the bazaar for like 100pp. You will only need ONE harness to make cash...quest is bugged and quest dude gives you the harness back. Don't petition/bug this plz :)

You willl want to have the alt bound in Thurg so when you get the Acid out of CC, you can jump down the ledge (get the acid by farming orcs...you will also get some lowbie drops off the Herbalist, Foreman, etc...) and die, free port to Thurg!

When you initiate the quest by giving the Broodmother the Giant Control Harness, camp out and log on your Shaman, who WILL be camped out at the shardwurm cave ent OR the Coldain Mines, depending on how fast your connection is, I would recomend the mines to be safer.

The first time you do the quest loot the bracer for youself, it's UBER. As in better then a JBB by FAR. Stats are 10ac 5str 6wis 5svc 5svm Extended Affliction:3 (EA:3 is found on only one other droppable item, be sure to advertise that too :) andthat the item sells for 40k :P)

Now you know everything about this quest, and how to get a JBB.

Sell the bracer for 5k MQ, after a day or two you will have enough money to buy a JBB...I've done it for a month and now I'm worrying about buying an ODS, Golem's Jawbone, Raptor Hide Cap, and Lodi Shield. This quest is INCREDIBALLY good for cash. You will sell them fast and well.


Anothering thing I am seeing work is selling MQ Earring of the Solstices for 10k. You can usually pick up the MQ for 8k if you shop around, but hell, who cares. Just buy them for 10k. But if you don't want to do this quest (this quest WILL work, HAS worked, and WILL get me another 16k min) then you could sell these.

Other possibility is Shawls. Someone is selling the 8th Shawl MQ on my server for 35k, if you want to be a tradeskiller then this is a VERY good idea, since it only costs about 16k to buy ALL of the items needed to do the quest. 35-16=19k gain for each quest. They are very hard, but they CAN be done. And when you do them, you will make a LOT of money.


With any of these Cash schemes you will have your JBB in a week, even if you only play 4 days a week for 3 hours a day. They work, and I hope none of you have to pay as much for your JBB as I did. (21k cash a Bracer MQ and an Emerald Dragonscale BP - 20wis BP ><.....41k total)
