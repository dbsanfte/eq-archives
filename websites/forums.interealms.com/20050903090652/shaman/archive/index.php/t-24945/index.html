LDoNs are great XP at your level. Get a group together, with temp if possible, and try to clear the dungeon. You'll get close to a whole level of XP from it. At that level you'll only get a point or two from an adventure and nameds at that level won't drop better gear than you hae already. Apart from that, you could head to Castle Mistmoore or High Hold Keep basement. Both those places are great spots up to around 30.
