Chutoi
RC is kind of a strange beast. It has removes poison counters, disease counters, and curse counters--though even at level 1 it seems to remove curses that have more counters than it is supposed to cure. However, in addition to the counter removal, RC has a chance to remove a detrimental effect--ie, some kind of bad effect that doesn't have counters. A good example of this would be the plasma decay DoT that is cast occasionally by grummus, or malo/malos. Basically, any damaging/debuffing spell icon that doesn't have counters (and that you would normally remove with the dispell magic line) is a detrimental.

RC1 has a chance of removing a detrimental effect.
RC2 has 3 chances of removing a detrimental effect.
RC3 has 6 chances of removing a detrimental effect.

Thus, even 5 casts of RC1 are not as effective as one cast of RC3 for detrimentals.

Since malo/malos isn't cureable (ie, only dispellable), it falls under the check for detrimentals. It's a crap shoot as to whether it will be taken off with RC. I've failed to strip a malos off myself with RC3 before, too.

Dunno if that helps.
