Zazra
Well, after reading this post I decided that I needed to FINALLY visit this zone. I'd peeked inside before but hadn't really explored -- didn't have the confidence without a cleric buddy tagging along. Well yesterday was different : tired of raiding I decided I'd head on in a solo as deep as I could :) . First of all I was overjoyed at how empty the zone was for the entire evening. A few people came and went but the zone is large enough and separated enough to not worry about someone walking over you. I could see some train possibilities in and around the entrance -- but that's the same with any zone I suppose.
I had a simple time getting to the rooms outside of the Pool/ Hottub with just some dog-dog action and Anathema ( still don't have Bane
:( ) -- the Poison and Disease line really was pretty useless against these mobs.
The first time that I got more than a single was in the room where the Feral Lord (#5) spawns. He wasn't up -- but I guess the mobs were just too close together for a single. Called up some Spirt Call pets to offtank while I took down the other. The mobs in the Pool looked to close together to single -- and it was just enough mana drain for me to think twice about doing it agian. So I moved my way into the Golem Yard over by the Shops. The golems were a breath of fresh air -- easier to root and they succumbed to my Poison and Disease DoTs :cool:
I then moved into the Shops and took out the first shopkeeper -- by this time I noticed that the first golem had respawned in the golem yard so the pop time seems to be pretty quick down here ~17 mins? So I took a lil` Shammy Med break and ploughed forward.
I quickly killed my way forward to the PHer for the Rotting Shopkeeper (#13) -- again in this room there were three mobs and I got the two nearest on my pull; luckily not the Named/PHer. Again, dog-dogs, tanking in teh face and Anathema were my tactics. I then moved back out killed Golems in the Yard ( and had the named pop there woot! ) and then moved my way back over to the Ballroom.
The mobs here were much more of the caster variety -- not just casting fear and the occasional boil blood -- but malosi and some dots. Once again the aggro range was very small and it was possible to single pull everything with a little patience ( due to the wanderer ).
I set up home-base for the rest of the night in the Ballroom and made my way back to the Stores(#13) and through the Gollem Yard to keep killing off the PHers. By the end of the evening (~4 hours ) I had seen (1) Rotting Shopkeeper (Kunzar Deathguard Shield (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=21544)), (2) Iksar Bohemoth (Bracelet of Lost Souls (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=21574) & Kunzar Ceremonial Death Shroud (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=21442)), (1) Warlok Dirloz (Soulscream Bracer (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=21633) ), (1) Lord Sasil(Ring of the Lost Soul (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=20860)), and (1) Champion Karmak (Blood Infused Stone Necklace (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=21434)).
I had quite a lot of fun there and expect that I'll be back with some friends to explore a bit deeper and hopefully walk off with some nice loots!
