Shayne716
I used the Punishers/Enforcers in Justice on the way down to Mauvin to PL my Paladin. They are really easy for lvl 52ish mobs around 10-12k hps. Hit for 202 max they will flurry and rampage occasionally. The spot i used to use was out in the hallway(can go afk without agro) but not in sight of the high guardians as they will add. Mobs do not run at low life which is a bonus however sometimes you will get pulls of two. Anyways at first i could only clear 4-5 spawns between respawn as i neared 65 on my paladin I could clear the entire room just as respawn occured. At 65 last time i was there about half the mobs were lt blue so probably leave ranger ungrp'd. There is a wraith in the main room that you can sneak by or single pull if you want. I used to use pacify to get singles as i'm sure the ranger could too. Loot is decent and etherals do drop here. Xp is weak here at 65 but that is where i take high 40s to 60ish lvl players to PL.
