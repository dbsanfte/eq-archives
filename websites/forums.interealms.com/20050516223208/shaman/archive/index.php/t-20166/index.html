I put this idea in another thread and the more I thought about it...the more I liked it, so I decided to create a new thread to discuss it. Here is a cut and paste of what I put in the other thread:

------------------

What if they gave us a new type of CH? We're shaman...let's be different. How about a blast CH?

Here are some possible thoughts and you can fiddle with the numbers to balance...

Name: ??? (I'm not creative)
Mana: 500
Casting Time: 3.5 seconds
Description: Heals up to the target to 65% (note it works different then current 75% CHs...see examples) of it's max HPs up to a total of 5000 HPs.

Example1:
Warrior has 10k HP. He is at 50% when heal lands. Total HP after heal would be 6500 for a 1500HP heal. <--- notice cap at 65%

Example2:
Warrior has 10k HP. He's at 25% when heal lands. Total HP after heal would be 6500 for a 4000HP heal. <--- notice cap at 65%

Example3:
Warrior has 10k HP. He's at 5% when heal lands. Total HP after heal would be 5500 for a 5000HP heal. <--- doesn't hit the cap in this case

Effectively this type of heal is the oh **** heal that heals for more the more dire the circumstance is. This is exactly the type of heal that I want. The tank is never at full HP with this heal so Q or the new super-Q spell is always at work. This is the perfect type of backup healing spell.

Change the numbers around for balance. Make it 60% or 70% or make it a 3 second cast time or 4 seconds or make max heal 6k or make it 400 mana...whatever. But it's this type of spell that improves the more dire the circumstance that will set Shaman apart, but is not overpowering in other circumstances!!

Come on designers....be creative!!! Make a new type of healing and give it to shaman.
