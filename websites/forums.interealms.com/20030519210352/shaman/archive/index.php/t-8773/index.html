Donar
Shamans wear chain armor. Jaundiced bone looks like plate but isn't. Banded armor is a type of chain armor, so yes, shamans can wear it, but there are many varieties of chain armor for shamans to wear. :)
Rorgg
Shamans can wear up to chain-class armor. Banded is chain class. However some technical plate armors are chain-class, for example the Hate stuff and Jaundiced Bone. Those are ones Iksars can't use.

This was the original reason Iksars couldn't use Golden Efreeti Boots -- they were plate, but cloth-class. I hear that's been changed, though.
