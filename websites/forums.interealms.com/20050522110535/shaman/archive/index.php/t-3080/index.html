Asta Khaan
I would say that mr is more important than anything until you can get it unbuffed to at least 80, even higher if fighting enchanters. Use your resist gear anytime you fight a caster. Mr based spells usually mean death if they land, and every caster has at least some of them... eg root, blind, snare, stuns etc.

If fighting pure melees, just use your hp/ac gear.

I wouldnt sacrifice much hp/wis to raise fr/cr/pr/dr unless its a very big boost. Froggy crown i would definitely use over totemic helmet or arctic wyvern. Would I use 8vs all rings or 6ac/65hp, well depends what mr you are at. 65hp is alot.

Also depends if you have access to 'twink gear' or not, and what Zek youre playing on. If playing on SZ or RZ it changes a bit due to large level differences and item loot (you want no drop gear).
