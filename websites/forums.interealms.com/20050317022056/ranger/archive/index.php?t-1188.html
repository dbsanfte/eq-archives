I can think of a few places you might want to try. Cobalt Scar is good experience, when it's not filled with quad kiters (as it usually is these days). The back part of City of Mist is a good spot. You could probably do well in Lower Guk with that combo, if you can get a camp like the hand or the sage that's not too hard to break. If you have keys, the top of the Tower of Frozen Shadow is great experience, but it is a bit out of the way. It's rarely camped, though. These are all places I've duoed with my wife, who's a druid, so I'd expect a ranger/cleric duo to be effective as well.
