Gloriana
This guild has put together an awesome flagging resource:

http://www.mysticalorder.org/flagmap.htm#PoValor

Basically, the PoP zones are arranged in "Tiers."

Tier 1 -

Plane of Nightmare
Plane of Disease
Plane of Innovation
Plane of Justice

Tier 2 -

From Plane of Nightmare - complete Hedge maze quest (max 18 people) get access to Plane of Nightmare B. Kill Terris Thule, get access to Plane of Torment

From Plane of Disease- Kill Grummus (or do quest), get access to Crypt of Decay

From Plane of Innovation - Kill Magnetic Behemoth (there is also a quest involving Diaku raiders in PoS), get access to Plane of Tactics. One person in the raid must have completed the "follow the gnome" quest in PoI, defeated the dragon and received the flag to open the factory door.

From the Plane of Justice - complete any one of 6 trials and receive access to Plane of Valor and Plane of Storms

Tier 3 -

In the Plane of Valor, kill Aerin Dar (or complete quest) and receive access to the Halls of Honor. In the Halls of Honor, there are 3 trials to complete (raid needed). After completing three trials, you are flagged for HOHB, kill Mithaniel Marr.

In the Plane of Storm, complete quest and receive access to the Bastion of Thunder. In the Bastion of Thunder, there is an event (requiring raid force) to defeat Agnarr and release Karana.

In the Crypt of Decay, complete the Carprin event (raid needed) to receive access to the Lower Crypt of Decay (this shows up on your key ring). Kill Bertoxx in the Lower Crypt of Decay.

In the Plane of Torment - Get key to Saryn's tower, help out Tylis, kill the Keeper of Sorrows and Saryn. If you've done everything listed previously, you get access to Sol Ro's tower. Killing the 5 flag mobs here and Sol Ro gets you flagged for Plane of Fire.

In the Plane of Tactics - kill Vallon Zek, Tallon Zek and Rallos Zek (very tough fight!)

If you do everything listed, you will be flagged for all the elemental planes. Kill each boss and receive a key. Kill all four bosses, and earn access to the Plane of Time.

Eryslai, the Kingdom of Wind - Xegony
Vegarlson, the Earthen Badland - The Council of the Rathe
Reef of Coirnav - Coirnav (Avatar of Water)
Doomfire, The Burning Lands - Fennin Ro

Easy as pie, right?

Oh, another important thing to know, is the fact you can do some flags out of order. Once 32 flagged raid members are in a zone, raid members without flags can zone in, based on a percentage of flagged individuals within the zone (I don't know the exact percentage). I was able to kill zone in and kill Agnarr before getting my BoT flag. This doesn't work for the Carpin key for Bertoxx, each person must have it (it's a key, not a flag). I also heard (not personally confirmed) that you must have every required flag to zone into the elemental planes.

Gloriana
The goal of the maze is to release Thelin who is trapped in Terris Thule's nightmare. Once you've completed the quest, you are able to enter Plane of Nightmare B (Lair of Terris Thule) and fight her.

If you aren't in a raiding guild, probably no rush to complete this event. PoNB is not a good experience zone.

What happens is you go to the Hedge in PoN, and get into a 3 group raid. The leader of each group will have some dialogue with Thelin and each group will be ported inside. (multiple events can occur at the same time)

Enchanters are key for this encounter, there are many multi mob encounters and Thelin must be constantly mezzed or mem blurred. You will follow Thelin about (sowing him helps greatly) and various monsters will jump out at you. When Thelin gets to a cleared out space, there will be one monster. Kill it, and 5 will appear which must be locked down and killed. This kill one mob, 5 appear thing happens 3-4 times.

At the final fight, a Construct of Nightmares will appear. He is slowable. When he dies (he can drop a decent pair of shaman pants), the raid leader will loot a dagger hilt and hand it to Thelin. Terris Thule will appear (non targetable) and yell at Thelin for a second. Hail Thelin to receive your flag and to be ported outside of the event.
