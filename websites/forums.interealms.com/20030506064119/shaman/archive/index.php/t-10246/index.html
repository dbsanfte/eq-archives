First thing I would do is drop the jasper ring and pick up another velium fire wedding ring.

Then I would get rid of the earrings, and get black sapphire electrum earrings, similar wisdom (well, maybe abit less), a wee bit less AC, but decent HP.

I would keep my eye out for JB Boots, most definitely, and JB Arms. Ghetto, but the solid AC makes it worth it. If you can't find a pair or don't want one, Crystal Chitin Armplates are decent, if abit expensive at this point.

Leaf Scale Gloves aren't much of an upgrade, but they go for 100 plat on my server, if that. You might also consider shissar scaled gauntlets, crystal chitin gauntlets, or coldain skin gloves.

Crystal Spider Eyes would be worth looking into for your face. Under 1k on my server.

I really don't know how you prioritize, but hopefully I've given you a few ideas. Good luck.

Zyphyr
Arms : Giant Scalemail (AC 11, Wis 3, Int -3, HP 20). You gain AC, a few HP, and lose 1 Wis. The STR loss is meaningless, and the STA loss is compensated for by the raw HP. Currently goes for 300pp on my server.

Earrings : Gotta go with everybody else in suggesting Electrum Black Sapphires (AC 2, HP 35, Mana 25). You lose 1 AC, gain 35 HP, lose a few point of mana and the 4 CR. Fairly cheap.

Rings : Again, have to agree with the majority here : Velium Fire Wedding Rings (AC 6, HP 65) and maybe a Shadel Bandit Ring (AC 6, HP 55, Mana 55, DR 10, CR 5, Summoning Haste II).

Shield : Sarnak Battle Shield (AC 25, Str 5, Sta 5, HP 40, FR/CR/MR 10). Same AC, some nearly meaningless STR, slightly more HP overall, and some useful resists. Pretty cheap these days.

Range : If you have PoP, buy some Hobgoblin Surprise and do the Beginner's Magic Manual quest (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2218) (AC 7, INt/Wis 6, HP/Mana 15) or buy some Planar Blood Brew and do the Beginner's Combat Manual quest (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2214) (AC 7, Str/Sta 6, HP/Mana 15). You could instead get the Planar Peridot Encrusted Stein to do the Beginner's Stealth Manual quest (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2216) (AC 7, Dex/Agi 7, HP/Mana 15) but it really isn't that great and unlike the others wouldn't really be an upgrade. On SolRo you can get the Hobgoblin Surprise or the Stein for under 200. Can't find a blood brew in the bazzar at the moment, but I expect that one wouldn't be all that much more.


Wrist : Whatever you do, do not follow the advice to get a 2nd chipped bone. Overall, the only truly redeming factor of that bracer is the focus effect. The AC is just to low to be real Shaman equipment.
