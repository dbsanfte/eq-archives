Jukax
City of Mist is great XP for your low to high 40's, I think there is quite a large ZEM (zone experience modifier) there. Personally I hate water zones, so I'd stay away from Kedge myself, but there is a big ZEM there too since it's like the least visited zone in EQ behind maybe Kerra Isle hehe.

Other places I can think of that aren't too crowded: The Hole is the zone with the highest ZEM in the game at around 40% bonus I believe, XP there is amazing. Karnor basement when you get your monk to high 40's, the XP will be rockin' for your monk and if you want to duo him there instead of PL I think it's still decent XP at lvl 60. Hardly ever camped anymore I think. Yeti caves in Dreadlands are good XP as well for your 40's, and should be lotsa mobs around.

Fungus grove is also a pretty nice choice, since your monk is level 40 now he'll get XP while group with your level 60 main, so you can either just duo shrooms with him or have him FD while you solo them. It'll be slower for him than straight up powerlevelling, but your shaman will still make decent XP.
