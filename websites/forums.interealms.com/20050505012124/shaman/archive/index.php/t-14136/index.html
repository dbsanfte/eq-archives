Ssra temple is annoying to get. Best thing to do is get your whole guild out there and do AE pulls to clear the whole mine in minimal time. Get the melees there too for banes.

Deep is easy to get. It's just getting there that's the annoying part. Estimate a 30-60 minute crawl to get across the bridge to the good shard droppers based on whether you have a full group, or are just doing with a single melee. With a full group clearing the horrors, estimate about 1 shard per half hour to an hour.

FG can be easy or can be annoying. If you've hunted in POP, going back to kill the bugs in FG will make you laugh at how sissy those level 60 mobs are. I was there yesterday with a level 61 bard who needed the shard, and I brought along a level 51 shadowknight to tank for us and soak up xp. LOL. Estimate about 1-6 hours per shard here with a full group. Quite variable time frame.

Sseru shard is annoying to camp too. I've seen people try for them down on main floor, but never seen a shard drop there ever. I always camped them up on the 2nd floor. Unfortunately the pathing up there is BAD and your pet is useless cuz he'll 32k too often unless you have summon companion memmed at all times. Estimate hours of camping this solo.

DSP is really easy to get. What you might want to do is bind near the rockhopper caves, buff up all the noobs who are killing them anyway for xp, and ask if anyone can let you loot. Do that while you are nearby in sseru doing that shard. When a shard drops in dsp, you can gate out to loot up and reward the noobs with whatever you want for the help. This shard drops often. Estimate no more than about 2 or 3 hours to see one or more drop.

Akheva is pretty easy to get as well. Any rock or cloak mob can drop them. They have a decent amount of hps, but are easy solos. Bring some help for more dps if you can to save time. Estimate about 1-3 hours per shard with a full group.
