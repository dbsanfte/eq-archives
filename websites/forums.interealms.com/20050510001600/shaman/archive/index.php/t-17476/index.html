Jinheim
6813HP raidbuffed, but I remember just a few months ago I was pretty much in your exact position. I didn't really have any focus effects at all, expect for coldain shawl. Now, I have pretty much every focus effect except some form of improved damage. I have found that for the most part, the problem pretty much takes care of itself because all of the items with nice focus effects also have awesome stats. However, if I had to choose between a focus effect and a few HP (less than 50) I would easily choose the focus effect. Any kind of mana savings or spell haste is going to help you a lot more than enough HP to survive 1/20th of a hit from a elemental trash mob. For example, I would rather wear a suit of ornate that a suit of something else with more HP but no focus, such as player smithed earth chain or something along those lines. If I had to rate focus effects I'd rate them like this, in order of desirability:

Detrimental Haste
Beneficial Haste
Beneficial Mana Pres
Improved Healing
Detrimental Mana Pres
Flowing Thought
Burning Affliction
Improved Damage

Note that I made this list with maximum raiding efficiency in mind. If you solo or try to do DPS in groups often, detrimental mana pres would probably be closer to 2nd on the list.

As for how much HP is "enough", that is a bit harder to say. With 5500, about to break in to elemental planes, you are doing fine. I was right around 6k when we hit EPs, but the other shamans were pretty much where you are and everyone did fine. If you get aggro from a boss you are going to die. If you get aggro from a trash mob you will probably live unless your healers just suck. More HP helps, but if you just use a little care when you are debuffing and make sure that the mob is tanked or mezzed before you try to slow, you'll be just fine. More HP really only gives you more room for error. Just try to make no errors to begin with and you'll be fine :)

Nowadays, I find a larger mana pool and more FT to be more useful than HP, because it allows me to chain tnargs on the MT during the boss fights for longer. I'm just sitting there, not getting hit, only using canni5 every 3 minutes and just casting tnargs nonstop. I am not really making use of my HP in most situations. Like I said, though, the problem solves itself because all the gear that is about to become available to you that has nice focus effects will also have nice hp and mana.
