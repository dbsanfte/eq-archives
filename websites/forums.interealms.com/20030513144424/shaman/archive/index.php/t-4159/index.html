Juventus
Use training points at the guild to get them as high as you can to start out.

Get piercing and 2hb up first. As far as melee damage output, we don't get a main hand damage bonus so weapons with better ratios are always going to do more damage (sans proc damage). 2hb weapons tend to have much better ratios than the faster piercers, so if you plan to melee a lot, 2hb is a good option. You do lose the ac and stats of having a shield though.

To practice 2hb, go snag an abandoned orc shovel from the orc camp in Lake rathe. It is a ground spawn and a low delay 2hb.

Lower delay = more swings = more skillups. Keep dex buffed too.

Any dark blue mob will give the same chance of skillups probably, although this is debatable.

Another Skill that tends to lag badly on druid-geared shamans is the defense skill. It is very important to get this maxxed out, so let mobs beat on you often.
