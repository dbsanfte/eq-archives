Ok for the LoTR Ranger thing, in Tolkien's world a Ranger was a Dunedain (branch of human race..descended from Numenoreans or High Man).

They basically hid their culture in a secretive group named Rangers of the North, there to help protect the peoples of Middle Earth. Aragorn was their leader, being the King basically.

Legolas was an Elven Warrior. And yes, Elves were expert bowmen and fletchers, and trackers and hunters. The Rangers were simply bad-ass mobile huntsmen.

For gaming purposes a ranger class is simply a lightly armoured, highly mobile warrior class, more skilled in tracking, hunting (and hence bowmanship) than the plate wearing toe-to-toe meatshields.
