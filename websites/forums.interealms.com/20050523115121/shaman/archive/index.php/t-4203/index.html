soupypoopy
These do NOT drop in KD randomly. They drop of one very specific type of mob in Iceshard keep (where the 9th Coldain ring cycle is). Some servers call this the "plate cycle" some call it the "9th ring cycle" some call it "iceshard keep" some call it the "small throne room".

The mob that drops the REIF can spawn in the throne room along the sides or can spawn in the first room opposite the throne and up the stairs. The mob that drops at the spots marked "I" on the map I've linked to below. He seems to rarely spawn with the spear -- you'll be able to see it if he's holding it.

The good news is that you can do the 9th ring cycle while you're here. It's not very difficult at all, and can be quite rewarding in terms of faction and drops (but only quest plate drops in this area).


Read <a href="http://www.mikeshea.net/everquest/iceshard.html">THIS EXCELLENT GUIDE</a> for info on how to do the quest and how to get to Iceshard.

Can be done with a single group of 60s, or two groups of mid to low 50s.
