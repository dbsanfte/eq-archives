Of course, the obvious choice is the Jaundiced Bone Bracer (ac 14, right click Winter's Roar every, 8 second cast, no recast delay), but it's pretty expensive.

I think the common progression goes something like this:

Totemic (+9 AC, +5 Dex)
Ry'gorr (+9 AC, +2 Str, +2 Cha, +5 HP)
Jarsath (no drop from Karnors... +8 AC, +4 Wis, +4 Cha)
Sentry (+9 AC, +3 Dex, +2 Wis, +2 Int, +2 vsMg, +5 vsPos, +5 vsDis, +15 HP, +15 Mana)
Coldain military wristguard (quest item)

----big gap here- everything above is pretty cheap, everything below here is expensive----

Dragonhorn bracers (ac 12-15, +8-12 wis, +4-8 cha)
Bracelet of sense (ac 12, +15 wis, +6 all saves)
JBB
Barbed rubicite (+13 AC, +5 Wis, +5 Int, +35 HP)

At that point you're thinking about Velious quest stuff...

By the way, I'm currently weaing the JBB and a sentry bracer.
