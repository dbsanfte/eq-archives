I remember getting my level 65 spells.
I had to farm bot named mobs for ages to get most of my spells.
This was back before zones opened up to everyone due to level, and before they had their drop rates adjusted.

Most any groups nowadays can kill bot named. They spawn every 6 hours. You can time when they die, and get a group ready to jump them when they spawn to increase chances of them dropping for you. There are many times pickup raids on targets that drop runes. Brell has pickup raids on some elemental plane targets in fact, and anyone regardless of guild status can generally get ele plane flagged. Also, runes do in fact drop in Sol Ro tower off named mobs, if you go into mini bosses sections. Or, you could just farm stuff for cash and buy the spell, or if a high end guild person has runes they are trying to sell, see if they want some good tribute items in trade.

The eril rune isn't much harder to get than the runes from pop. If you can farm a bot named, you have a group capable of hunting in the sewers in god. The sewers are in fact decent xp, compared to say tier 3 xp zones, and can be quite comparable to ele planes.

Jelki is harder to get, but mainly because you have to find 5 other people willing to go through tipt. Once you get KT flagged though, getting a jelki rune shouldn't take you long if you hunt in the zone at all for xp. The named here are beefy, but they have extremely low hit points and die in about 30-40 seconds of max dps.

The rune that gives us our slow spell is the really hard spell to find. I don't know where it drops for sure besides very very few KT named. I've so far seen my own spell drop, and 1 rune drop.
