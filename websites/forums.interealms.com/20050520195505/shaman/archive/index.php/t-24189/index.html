People don't seem to realise that shaman can single pull using pet too - and indeed, can single pull a little bit better than SKs.


The secret of pet pulling single pulls is to use a pet that /cons green to the mobs that you are hoping to avoid aggro-ing. i.e usually not the level 61 spell pet. Usually the "Spirit of the Howler" pet is fine for this.

1. Summon relevant pet
2. Buff pet with as many hp as you can and best SoW you can give him - indoors, even Spirit of Shrew is better than nothing
3. Immediately before sending pet, cast Quiesence on pet
4. Send pet to attack desired mob
5. As soon as puppy engages mob, start mashing the "back off" key repeatedly and hope that puppy is listening.

If the pet dies before it gets back to you, you will get aggro of any mob that is in aggro range, hence the need to Q the pet before pulling

Here's a list of facts with pet pulling.

1- Reason why Necro and SK pet pull so well is that they have low level pets with more hp than our higher level pets. The pet has to be green (or light blue I guess, very hard for me to know exactly the con of the pet to the mob) to the mob you are pulling for it to work correctly.

3- If pet dies during pull, any mystery agro that you should have gained but didnt by healing or by the pet proxy agroing a mob right next to another mob, auto agros everything that should have been agroed to begin with.... hard to explain. Let me give an example.

If you are in LDoN and there are 4 mobs in a room, you sick pet on a mob and spam /pet back off (or use /pet hold, if you have the AA).

A- If the pet comes all the way back, you drew no agro and everything is good with the world.
B- If the pet runs in, gets one rounded and dies. It's the same as if you had invis on yourself, stood next to these mobs and attacked. They will All come charging. Any mob that has Proxy agro will come charging you and anyone else that healed the pet.
C- Pet runs in, gets beat to 2% as a heal lands, pet lives keeps running back to you but pet dies 3' in front of you. Somehow the mobs 50' back in the next room that you pulled from will charge you same as if you drew agro on them. Even though you are well outside their agro range.

By necros and SK using FD then pet pulling all they are doing differently is they are putting zero risk in the pet dying. If it dies, they are FD and the mobs agro wipes and it goes back to it's spawn point.

If I made a mistake please correct me, but I have a lot of experience with pet pulling... my bro is a SK/Necro and we do it all the time.
