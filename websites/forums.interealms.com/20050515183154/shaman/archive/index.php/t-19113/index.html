Thorzion
As far as grouping goes:

As long as one party member does at least one point of damage to the target mob the pet takes no exp. Pets from Dire Charm will still take exp, though since I'm not a DC capable class I never paid attention to the percentages.

Now, as you are not grouping with the SK, you need to make sure that the SK does more damage than you. If he does 51% you are good. Be carefull with burst damage at the end of a kill though: on more than one occasion I nuked a mob at 20% health and the person I was PLing got no exp, even though they did 80% of the damage. I think its a problems with the server mechanics (read lag and latency in reporting totals) that screw it up, so Im always carefull not to do that too often.

I PL'd a monk friend of mine from 45 to 52. I never used dog dog. I did my damage with either JBB and/or epic. Dulak seemd to be the best spot, boat 3 or 4 worked fine if your subject is around 52. Using dog dog is less work but the jbb/epic is more exact. Once you get it down, your subject will fly through the xp.

One more thing, if you decide to use dulak as your PLing grounds, (I know there is better but I thought I'd add this), don't go into the caves until around 57+, mabey lower. Go to the second to last room. As you enter the room, go to the far right corner, that is the safe spot. The mobs in this room have aprox 5k hp's but they don't cast. At 60, 2 kills = 1% reg xp, 1 kill = 1% aa. It's slow but doable but it's the best xp in dulak at that level.

Good hunting...
