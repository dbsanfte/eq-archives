Shaman Equipment - Common Upgrade Path

Many questions posted on these forums pertain to suggestions on equipment upgrades. We have decided to create a list of the most common equipment upgrade paths by slot for our Shaman brothers and sisters.

I will be continually revising and updating this list according to the corrections and changes of the contributors that will be posting below.

This list is NOT to be considered a full list of Shaman equipment, just the most commonly available and commonly used options.

Notes: I don't want this thread to turn into a AC/HP vs. WIS debate, so let me mention right from the beginning that items will be listed on this thread by usefulness, lowest to highest, with a strong bias towards AC/HP. I have seen effective "Wis" Shamans, and I understand and agree that Wisdom is important, but only in a secondary nature in my opinion. I will be more than happy to listen to suggestions why one piece of gear is more useful than another, but ultimately the order of the items listed here will be based on my opinion. The buck has to stop somewhere. When an item "does" have additional Wisdom, the Wisdom bonus will be bold in the item listing.

Giant Scalemail Sleeves (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=3942): +11 AC, +3 Wis, -3 Int, +20 HP Races: All Races

As time allows, I will also be linking the items to their entry on Allakhazam's as shown above.

**************************************************

Equipment Criteria

Droppable
To appear on the list, an item must be droppable. No Velious quest armor, for example.

Value
The monetary "platinum" value of items is being ignored for the most part. Server prices vary, and trying to track those variations would be futile.

Resists
Resistance gear is important, but specific to certain situations and not generally used for everyday experience hunting, thus you will not find resistance-specific items on this list, i.e. Blue Diamond gear.

Commonly Available
Items on the list must be commonly available.
