Jaruden
Go right coming in and hug the wall, keeping it on your right until you hit the first portal.

Go straight a little and take the first left turn. Go straight once you've turned until you hit the 2nd portal.

Go straight ahead, very short distance until you hit the 3rd portal.

Turn around (180 degrees) and run straight ahead (a few steps) to hit the 4th portal and you're out.
