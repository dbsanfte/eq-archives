Buff yourself with as much AC and agility as you can. The more AC and agility the more chance of missing (AC) and the quicker your defense will raise (AGI).

Get a fellow shaman with a 61 pet and duel him.

Get fellow shaman to haste his pet with 60 percent haste.

You cripple his pet so he will hit for less and potentially miss more then.

Get fellow shaman to attack you with his pet.

You heal yourself with torpor.

Do this for about an hour and you should be set.

Any pet that is dark blue can give you offense and defense skill raises.

In addition make sure that the pet has weapons so that its duel wielding.
