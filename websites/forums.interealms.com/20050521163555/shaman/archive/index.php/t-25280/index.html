Giac
Persistent Casting has never enabled you to resist a stun, it basically did what Valsuvious described.
When you shake off a stun, the same thing happens as if you were an Ogre. You finish casting your spell and then you get a channeling roll which decides whether you regain the spell or not.

The are three problems with Persistent Casting which I have noted here in a thread the first week of GoD:

1. It does not kick in on a bash or kick. Ogre's stun immunity autimatically does that 100% of the time from the front allowing the player to always finish their cast and have a channeling roll

2. It goes off like 5% of the time

3. You are locked up for the whole cast time of the spell. If you cast a 5 second spell, get stunned 1 second into it and Persistant Casting kicks in, you will not be able to move, run away, duck out or whatever till the spell has finished casting. If you got stuned this would only be the case for ~2 seconds. This is not a *huge* problem for Shamans, but for classes who rely on CHs and evac, which are rather long casting spells, it can defintely be annoying.
