Just clicking the bridle again refreshes it (and gives you the "you are already mounted on a horse) - also SoW boots are another popular clicky :)

weissblau
Go to eqinterface.com and download the "3tabpet" mod for the pet window. On the "pet" tab, your AMMO slot item is always visible. Right-click on the bridle in the AMMO slot any time you want to see your current mana level.

Cannibalizing works, too, of course.

It was at one time very easy to not have the horse. You could have all the new models but just not show horse.

SOE changed this in an atempt to keep people from exploiting the benifits of the horse without the consequenses, but people figured out that if you turn all the new models off then you can still make it work.

I would just recomend shrinking.

When you need to get around fast just click the horse off and run... then click the horse back on when the situation permits.

This works fine with a couple of shrinks and you will know that you are not doing somthing dishonest.

You do not need to shrink after each recast of the horse buff.
