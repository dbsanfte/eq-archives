weissblau
What I think I have observed... all subjective, of course... is that skill goes up faster if you're within a zone of about 30 points below trivial to 5 points below trivial, then it slows down dramatically.

If someone wants to try this, I suggest buying fresh fish and batwings in FP - both available on vendors - and combining in a spit or oven to make fishrolls, then examining the log to see where the skill point increases occurred. Fishrolls trivialize at like 135 or so. Unfortunately, this is a long slow boring haul and would have to be done multiple times with identical characters/buffs to be valid as a test. Sounds like a lot of work to me.

One rumor is that skill increases are more likely after a successful combine than an unsuccessful combine. As you are more likely to be successful if you are trying to make a potion that has trivial level 10 above your current skill than a potion that has trivial level 30 above your current skill, then you would be more likely to get a skill increase with the lower triv potion.

Not sure if this is true, but it does seem to be the conventional wisdom.
