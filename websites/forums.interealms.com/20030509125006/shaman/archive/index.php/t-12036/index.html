Saryrn

Katta guards are okay. I'm there at 54 with JBB and mount and I get about 3 blues an hour. Pulling is fairly easy. 6 mobs (8 if you pull the cave guards), come in pairs. If they're not broken up yet, you might get double pulls. All dark blue to me at 54.

My usual battle goes like this: Come in invised, set up near wall between tavern and staircase. Pull tavern guards, then staircase guards, then bank guards. By the time I'm done with the cycle, the mobs are up again. So you get a steady stream. I generally pull with Turgur's, then land an ebolt and click away on JBB.

In the map below, the general area is where the bank and tavern are (items 10 and 11 on map)

http://www.eqatlas.com/luclin/kattacastellummap.html

They drop some nice loot. Custodus breastplates sell in bazaar for ~200pp (or 16pp to vendors) other weapon loot sells for around 4-5pp to vendors. Plenty of weapons to save for/give dogdog too.
