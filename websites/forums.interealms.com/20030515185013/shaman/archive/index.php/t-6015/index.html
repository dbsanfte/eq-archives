Tarthug
I did ME on toiler hill and the entrance to Akheva as well.

ME: pull only toilers, use whatever tactics best suit you, but personally I mixed up the IN THE FACE with the root n'rot. These guys hit in the 140's and are chimps when slowed.

Akheva: doing the entrance, the transient patrollers are doable, but a pain in the butt, the centi's are the meat and potatoes. Stick to the entrance.

Chardok: zoneout, zone in and lev tunnel are all doable solo and CAN be excellent experience. pull with slow and you should be able to single pull most of the mobs. with zone out there are 2 mobs that spawn in aggro range of you so be careful of how you break it.

Suits in WL: At 56 these mobs were doable with epic and JBB with doggy and could be single pulled. They are doable before this, but its harder to single pull them. They are immune to poisen and disease, use slow, malosini and melee mixed with JBB shots. They hit for a max of 117.
