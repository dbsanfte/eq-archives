Dread
I use different tactics depending on the situation.

xp group where mob requires malo: I move out of the camp towards puller a little. I hit assist key and spam malo until mob gets in range. Once malo hits ( while Mob in still inc ) I back up some and get to the spot where we want to kill the mob. This helps overcome the long casting time of malo + 2.25 sec spell gem refresh. I slow the mob as soon as the gem comes up, which is usually after the tank takes 1-2 hits and still has >80% health. Moving towards the puller some basically allows me to cast malo ( and thus slow ) sooner, but requires more attention as I have to know where the puller is. I use this tactic in places like PoD castle or ledge.

xp group where malo isn't required: I slow on inc. I laugh at the mob if it get pissed at me and just torpor. I stand beside the MT. If the mob resists slow and is borderline dangerous ( example PoI entrance group with me as only healer ) I root the mob and back up just a little to get out of melee range and slow again.

On raids: We have a set dispelling, debuffing order. By the time mob has optional dispell, malo, OOS, tash, and optional malosini, I won't get agro by the time I slow. This works well in places like HoT.
