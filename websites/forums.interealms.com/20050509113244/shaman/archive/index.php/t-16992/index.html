Low Level tradeskills are also a potential good source of money.
You need to get your skill to roughly 100 or so for most of the easy ones. Some more, some less. You want to be a good amount (50+ above the trivial + gearlok)

Smithing:
Medium Quality Folded Sheet Metal
Pottery:
Ceramic Linings
Tailoring:
Sacred Tunare Silks
Generic
Celestial Essences

Farm for the drops:
silks, pelts, acrylla, etc

Lots of old world stuff like Cobalt Scar can be very good cash when you figure cash drops, quest gems ((even if sold to merchant)), crusty armor ((even if sold to merchant)), and pelts ((never sell to merchant))
