Azhdarkhos
Tradeskills work differently from other skills. No matter when you put training points into them, they start at 1 (assuming you didn't get any skill ups beforehand by doing combines), and you can only train up the skill to 21. If, for example, you get to 6 in a particular tradeskill by doing combines before putting any training points into it (tailoring spiderling silk into threads or some such), you will only be able to put 15 training points into it at your trainer. If you skill up to 21 before putting any points into the skill, you won't be able to put any training points into it.
