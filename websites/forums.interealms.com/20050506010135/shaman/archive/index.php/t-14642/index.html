Xev_Grammar
This is just my opinion, but you are totally fine at the moment. When you get to Plane of Power mob level (or fight some of the nastier older world things like Tawro and Radir, or harder Ssra mobs), you will want to lead with Malosini-- even though Malosini and Turgur share the same resist chance, if you fail with Malosini you don't generate much agro at all, and tanks/your pet can easily pull it off of you. If Malosini does land however, you're almost guaranteed to land Turgur's.

Once you get into tier 2 type mobs, using Malo or Malos (the unresistable versions) starts to become more of a must. I was doing okay in PoV with just malosini but it was scary and risky sometimes-- once I got Malos, everything got pretty trivial.

Regarding Cripple, it's actually effective-- I would only use it on really tough mobs that proc or rampage or frenzy a lot, however, where the person has good agro control or if you can keep it rooted. It works ok on weaker mobs too, but there are better spells you could be casting in that case. Be very careful with Cripple if the mob is nasty... it is *tremendous* agro.

In that case, Malo then slow makes a ton of sense, if for no other reason, because Malo is low aggro and unresistable, so you never have to recast it, and it drops the average amount of aggro you get.
