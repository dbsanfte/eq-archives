Hail,

If you are a 65 Shaman & moderately equipped it is my experience that you can pretty much survive anywhere in Kael except for Vindi & the AoW cycle mobs. I typically fight my way to the Arena solo, starting from the EW zoneline. On the greenies I generally don't even slow them - I just cast Blood of Saryrn & sic dogdog, then keep moving to the next mob. Be sure to loot all the giant warrior helms & frost/storm giant toes for faction turn-ins later. Anything light or dark blue should be slowed. :)

Honestly if you are duoing I wouldn't even bother with the plate cycle area or mobs by the bank. Head straight to the Arena instead, and just kill & move everything there that you see. Remember that most of these giants are rootable, so as long as you don't damage them they aren't going to summon you if you park 'em. In the arena, pull with Malos from max range, then sic dogdog while you are casting Turgur's.

If you have more hp than your SK, I'd just treat him like another pet. :D Don't forget to alpha both your pet and the SK's with mage toys, they make a big difference on your DPS.

Be sure to inform your friends and guildmates to move their twinks over to Kael, and park them there to loot the NO DROP pieces. Believe me, you will rapidly fill your inventory with that junk otherwise (or it will just rot).

Take care,
