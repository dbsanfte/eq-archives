Zarniwoop
Although I haven't looked at the bazaar prices for spells in a while, I imagine the level 61 and 62 spells should be dirt cheap; you should be able to afford them all. The level 63 and 64 will be a bit more expensive, because the spectral parchments are generally found in more restricted zones. My priorities for grouping would be: Blessing of Replenishment, Talisman of the Boar, Talisman of Alacrity (if you hate refreshing SltW), Velium Strike and possibly Tears of Saryrn. My priorities for soloing would be Breath of Ultor and Petrifying Earth.

The only significant outlay will be for your level 65 spells. I would keep an eye on prices as you level up and try to pick up bargains. If your plans are to join a raiding guild at some point, they might be able to provide you with some or all of your 65 spells.

Jaradis
You should be able to pick up most 61-64 spells dirt cheap. I've seen many of those spells for as low as 100pp in Bazaar cause there are just so many Ethereal and Spectral parchments out there now.

If all you do is group/solo and don't raid, get Quiescence first. Its really the only one you need, you can live with Primal Avatar and Malo for groups. The rest just pick up as you can afford them.

If you do buy OoW, I'd suggest going there the first few weeks and seeing if you can pick up some new drops in the beginning zones and sell them. New expansion items, even stuff from the beginning zones usually nets you a good high price at first before people farm them. Look at some of the Natimbi stuff and how high it went for at first. Even if its something that would be an upgrade for you, just sell it if you get a good price, you can always go back and get another one later when prices aren't as high. A lot of plat can be made in the beginning of an expansion when stuff is new.
Danemoth Spiritconvoker
Definately get all your group buffs if possible (Talisman of Boar, Wrulan, Diaku, Alacrity, and Tribunal) it will save mana and time for buffing. Dont waste too much plat on Tears of Saryrn, as its only like 150 more damage a wave than Torrent of Poison.

If you solo a lot, get Breath of Ultor. Its about 3k damage in 2 minutes, so it helps quite a bit IMO. If you group more than solo, dont waste huge amounts of plat on it. The regen spells are good since they were upgraded so getting Blessing of Replenishment would be good too. Keeps those Necros happy and quiet. :)

For level 65 spells, get Quiescence first (as many have said), then afterwards its really up to you for what to get. Blood of Saryrn is an awsome DoT spell, especially in BOT, as it should run its full duration even after Malo + Turgurs. Ferine Avatar would be a good third if you can afford it. Malos and Focus of the Seventh should be the last things on your list of spells to buy if you have Malo and Focus of Soul. They are almost the exact same thing, except Malos reduces resists by like an additional 20 points and Fot7 is just a group version of Soul.

Hope that helps.
