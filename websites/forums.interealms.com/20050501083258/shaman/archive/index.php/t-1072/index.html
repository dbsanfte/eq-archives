I was hanging around in Trak's teeth last week with my 46 Shaman, a 50 Ranger, a 53 Wizard, and a 55 Magician, doing the Forager cycle for a couple hours. We had pretty bad luck getting named to pop, but we did get a "hangman's noose", which we of course defaulted to the ranger. The ranger already had a swiftclaw sash, which I currently have on loan until I can hand it off to the ranger's rogue alt.

So, I gave up the 8 ac I had on my belt slot (yes, I have crappy gear) for the 2 ac + haste on the sash.

I swing my hammer more frequently, which should up my somewhat dubious melee damage a bit when I'm tanking for the dog (or at full mana when fighting red mobs in a group). Seems like when soloing, a haste item makes some sense. if you're gonna fight the mob anyhow, why not do 20 percent more melee damage?

But when I see these "AC vs WIS vs HP vs REGEN vs resists vs whatever" debates, I rarely see haste items listed.

So, to make a very long question short, in situations when you are actually meleeing, where do you put a haste item in the AC/WIS/HP/etc hierarchy?

I'm hoping for something akin to the formulae people have posted for the other stats. Something like:

5AC = 1 WIS = 10HP = 4MANA = 5% HASTE = ....

You get the idea, though I'm sure my numbers are completely wrong (they were intended as illustration, rather than my actual opinion of value)

Henora
I know I have never melee'd enough to justify using a haste belt for haste alone all the time.

Since I was level 55 or so, I carried an FBSS in my bag for when I felt like swinging at things, but I never wore it when I wasn't meleeing. I have to be pretty bored in an exp group to get in and melee and practice weapon skills.

Currently I wear a spider fur belt for the hps, or
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/equipment/graphics15/silverybeltofcontention.jpg

That belt has decent stats and haste. I vote you get one of those! They drop in Kael off random mobs. It's 21% haste, same as FBSS. It is rare though.

I think it would be hard to put it into a heirarchy as everyone has different equipment goals. My equipment goals revolve around HPs. I can't even say I have a definitive ">" type scale for evaluating my own gear.

Trying to keep unbuffed AC over 1000, while maximizing HPs and STA. I can honestly tell you haste fits at about the same level as other "unimportant" stats such as dex for me.

Yeah, so in conclusion to my rambling. If you have a crappy belt and can get a better belt that happens to have haste, then go for it. Does your 6ac drop push you below a 50/100ac threshold? If not probably not a huge deal. If you have fun swinging your hammer, go for it.

Some other reasonable belts you could look into would be the Spider Fur Belt, ac/hp or Smiters Girdle, lotsa stats.
