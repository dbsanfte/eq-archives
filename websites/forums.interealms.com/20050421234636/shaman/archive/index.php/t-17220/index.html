I just want to expand on why the chromadracs are a pain, in case you get bored and feel like trying them. They dispell your buffs, nothing more crappy than having your fresh kei go away. It used to be the top buff would be removed, but I believe it is now random. From my experiences hunting there, most of the time it was indeed the top slot, might have been luck though, don't count on it. To be safe, make sure you put some junk buffs in the top four slots. If you have your black fur boots, have sow in top slot, so you can instantly recast when it gets dispelled.

Also, I tried having pet tank so his buffs would get dispelled and not mine, but they still were dispelling me.

There really is no need to hunt them, they don't drop anything good, so definitely stay away.
