SneakyB
I have done the tunnels quite a few times. My group consists of me, cleric, chanter, wiz and 2 dps. I'm sure you could use a druid or bard also for the 2 roles also. The dps can be anything pretty much. We've used most dps combos and they all work nicely.
The tunnels are a little bugged (no pun intended) and you can get aggro through the walls, therefore an evaccer is essential, as well as the chanter for mezzing.
Fight your way to the bugs and set up camp there. The main problem is the aggro-through-the-walls that can, and probably will, happen. When that occurs you will most likely get 4-6 adds. If your mezzer can deal with, then stay and line em up, if not, evac and try again.
A fun place.
