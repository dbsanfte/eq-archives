Tsuyoi
Well, it looks like the full group is the way to go. Found out some things about how LDoN dungeons are formed:

First, the dungeons are always fully populated. It doesn't matter if you have 3 or 6 people.

Second, the adventures are given based on the average level in your group. The number of people don't matter.

So.... This means I'd have very little chance of successfully duoing an adventure with my toons. :( I was hoping that the adventures would be scaled down for the group composition.

Maybe I'll try once I break 200AA on both and have better gear than what I have now. Course by then I won't need LDoN most likely :P

Hail,

Well, all hope is not lost. You COULD do this:
Get 2 level 60 people to join your group temporarily for the adventure, but don't have them zone-in. If both you and the monk are 65, this will make your average group level 62.5, which means the mobs will be hitting for less because the dungeon spawned set for that level of difficulty. You will take reduced points even on a win, but I found it VERY easy to tank mobs at this level on normal adventures. I would imagine at this setting that your monk/shaman combo would stand a very good chance of winning adventures if you were careful about what kind of adventure you accept. A friend's BoT cleric/enchanter would be perfect for this.
