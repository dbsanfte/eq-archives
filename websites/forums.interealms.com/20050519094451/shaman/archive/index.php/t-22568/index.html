I 100% agree with you from an agro stand point, too. The problem is that DoTs generate agro from each tick. If a tank has a bad round, we can be pushed to the top of the hate list. A nuke, by comparison, does a single hit of agro. Coupled with the fact that DoTers tend to DoT earlier in the fight to try to get maximum efficiency from their DoTs and shamans have no way of lowering themselves in the hate list, you have the source of the problem. I don't mind DoTs generating hate. If they didn't DoTing would not be a challenge and necros would lose some ability to agro kite. However, I do think our poison DoTs generate too much agro.

I think a -20 or -30 to 60+ DoTs would not be out of place. That means, after a debuff, we're in the same ball park.

If SOE claims that we don't need the increased resist debuffs, I'd put it to them to reduce the resist rates on necro and druid DoTs since the higher resist levels are "not needed".
