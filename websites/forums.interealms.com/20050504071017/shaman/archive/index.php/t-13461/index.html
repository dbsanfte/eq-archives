1) As I noted in the other thread, I seriously doubt that this will remove the need for the "planar progression quest mob" kills to continue past Tier 2/3. I think its quite likely that you'll still need Aerin'Dar, for example (along with the Honor Trials) to enter the Temple of Marr - even when HoH-A becomes an open plane.

2) The reward cannot be an item. By the time the patch with these changes hits, I'll be in Tier 4. The "frontrunner" guilds are in Tier 5. Any gear that would be likely useful to someone in Tier 2/3 would be beyond useless to them; any gear they (or even I) would wear would be unbalancing to people not at my part of the game. Also, unique gear would be perceived as highly unfair - SOE *did* learn the lesson about putting in good items and then making them unavailable to later customers with the whole Sleeper Script nightmare (okay, okay - there was that mage root earring in CT and a couple other things ... but you know what I mean, I hope ...). For what its worth, though, you can equip a charm without owning LoY. In addition to the FV server, there is one tradeable charm (no, its not worth getting - and is a pain to camp ... kind of like using Torment, actually).

3) The reward cannot be more planar access. It would be impossible to divide fairly among the flags. For example, would HoH-A access be worth, say Tower of Sol Ro? Would Torment + Tactics be worth Air? And again, this would not reward anyone who already *had* further planar flags; the reward will be something that applies to everyone.

4) AAXP is almost certianly what we're getting. I expect 1 AA per flag; I'd be pleased and a bit surprised to get 2. We won't get more. Keep in mind that SOE feels (correctly) that people got more than just flags from flagging encounters - which is true. Even Terris and Behemoth drop some pretty nice loot (Knucklebone Beaded Veil and Self Contained Force Barrier come to mind as worthwhile, respectively). This will benefit everyone who does not already have their AA tree complete (and it wouldn't surprise me if they add some new AA in which you can spend the same number of points as the maximum that can be awarded in this process, just because - if they do, note that it will suck). This should give 6 AA, assuming 1 per flag and counting Valor/Storms as a single flag (PoV/PoS, HoH, CoD, Torment, Tactics, BoT) - which is enough to buy a 3rd rank Archetype AA, and is therefore a "strong reward" in SOE's eyes. Not what a lot of folks want, I am sure - but really, its not *that* bad. Having done these flags anyway (and see point 1), I'll take 6 free AA for the trouble.

5) They could surprise me and do something stupid - like a title, name color, etc. This would make me cry, but I'd go on with life.
