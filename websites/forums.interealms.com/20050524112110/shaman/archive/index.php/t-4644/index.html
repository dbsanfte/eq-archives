Boinshar
Ranger + chain horse (or god forbit, ornade chain horse) is ungodly. Ever seen a EQ3 + TS ranger take down a MB wizard BEFORE he finished casting? Remember, they get to double attack with bows on stationary targets, and crits = PAIN.

If he's a newbie to pvp, you can kill him easy. However, any well-equipped ranger in a high level guild would have enough DR/PR to make any shaman cry. Not to mention, the range of 90% of good bows is greater than our spell range. If he knows what he's doing, the only thing you can do is decide how much time passes before you die.

However, if you really want to try, then duel him in VERY hilly areas preferably full of trees. When he gets on your tail, run up/down a hill, first turn one direction and run half a circle, then immediately turn 90% to the oppisit direction and run the oppisit way. Example, if you are running downhill and turned right, then you should turn left and run uphill. If there's any lag around, this usually confuses the EQ client on his side, making it seems like you've continued turning that circle. The hills should prevent line of sight for him to shoot you, and the trees can (sometimes) block an arrow.

Now, after you've lost him, stand still and heal(if damaged)/chain tap whatever spell you want to cast. Eventually, he will run in range and get hit, and if you do this enough, he'll get bored and zone.

That's about all you can do.
