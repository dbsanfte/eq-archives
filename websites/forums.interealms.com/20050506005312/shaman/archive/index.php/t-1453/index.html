Darksea is definitely a nice piercer. Here are a few more (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?s=&threadid=1323) possible choices.

As for totemic vs cultural... my answer is... Ry'gorr!

At least on Karana, most Ry'gorr pieces can be had for the price of the gem needed to complete the totemic quest. Ry'gorr generally has one more AC than totemic per piece, and some nice other stats. Otherwise, just head to EW and get in an Ulthork group.

Honestly, given prices and power levels, I'd skip totemic altogether. Cultural should be cheaper, and is almost as nice (not enough of a difference that it will affect play. In fact, for the minor slots (gloves, boots, helm, arms) you might be able to find Jaundiced Bone for a similar price... and for pure AC it's a great value !!

Hope this helps

I had a Dull Wooden Spear in my range slot for a long time, simply because I never bothered to get anything better. It is very, very much an "old-world" item. I think it's something like 7/33, Wis +2, Agi +2, Sv Cold +5. It's okay for the range slot, but not good enough to be a melee weapon.

As for whether or not to get Totemic, based on your server I'd have to disagree with Bigguss' assessment. On a blue server, it's true that Ry'Gorr sells for little more than a piece of Dufrenite, and that you can buy Jaundiced Bone for reasonable prices. But on Firiona Vie, if you are having trouble buying Dufrenite, you won't be able to afford Ry'Gorr, and there's no way you could pay for Jaundiced Bone (not that you'd even find it for sale). Cultural items are much rarer and more expensive on Firiona Vie than on blue servers, because nobody has huge amounts of money to dump into tradeskills. Basically, in my opinion, you should do the Totemic quests and possibly fill in some of the missing slots with cultural armor.

The Fierce Impaler is the Bane of a great exp camp for the 34-39 Shaman. During the course of leveling there I saw him spawn 3 itmes. When he is up you cannot pull anything else as his aggro range is huge!

I highly recommend this spot for exp, and if you have some higher lvl friends who can stop by on short notice you may get a great piercer. However, the gloomwater is his common drop.

Some tips to fight in this spot:

Pull with aggro not with a spell, easier to single that way.

Use invis to animals to check spawn positions, can be difficult to tell which fish is closer in a 3D environment.

Always wedge yourself into a corner to cast spells, fewer interuptions.

Dont make a pet, instead use charm animal (pet can work fine too, I just found it slowed me down due to frequently haveing to zone out)

Bind at the bank in Khaladim and have fish scales there, although since you will be fighting right at the zone you shouldnt die.

Fish are on a 18 min respawn cycle, EB after this although it wasnt until 37 or so that I could kill all 6 spawns.

If you want to spawn the Fierce Impaler you must kill the farthest back spawn, can be difficult to tell which is which if it is a lancer, but usually it is a piercer, or impaler place holder. Conversely if you want to avoid this spawn don't. Remember you will not be able to exp here if the Fierce Impaler is up. He will aggro from all the way across the room.

I believe there is a guide at www.eqshaman.com for this spawn with alot more info.

Good Luck !
