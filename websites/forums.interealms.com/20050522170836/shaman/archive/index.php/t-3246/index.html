Fudapuda
As a situational weapon, I LOVE my Poison Wind Censor. It's 2hb, and hits in the 60-80 range, but it procs a 122 DD poison.

The drawback is that the DD is an AoE effect, so it hits you too, which leads to this item being referred to as a "suicide stick".

However, I have found that for fighting very large mobs, such as giants, this item is awesome. You stand at max melee range, and you are out of range of the proc, while the giant takes full damage, and it procs A LOT! I typically get 6-8 procs per giant with the item, and that's with an entire raid beating on him at the same time. I've often taken aggro away from our MA with this weapon (which gets me some rude /tells).

Its just great fun to wield :)
