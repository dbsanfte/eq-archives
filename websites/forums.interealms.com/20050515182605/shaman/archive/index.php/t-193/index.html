Jing
We also use Eye of Zomm to navigate the bridge. However, some people will fall, guaranteed. To prepare for that, have a cleric use Divine Aura and fall off on purpose. Then they can res anyone that falls off, and hopefully drag their corpse up the far wall. Most mobs on the far wall are not kos, so just skip them. I've heard they don't like certain illusions though, so treat them like Jaled Dar and don't use any illusions at all. I think they aggrod our magician who was using an AA skill illusion.
Landragoran Heronwing
It's tricky.
The best method I've heard of was to have a monk Instill Doubt a mob across the bridge (mob will take the correct path) and have the monk follow along dropping hammers (thx bracer of hammerfall). Then the raid goes across one at a time, following the hammer trail.
Bonestorm
Jump on the pillar before the illusion bridge and slide down, then run across the bottom to the other side to the far right. Thats if you dont want to fall or want to res/drag your friends that have.
