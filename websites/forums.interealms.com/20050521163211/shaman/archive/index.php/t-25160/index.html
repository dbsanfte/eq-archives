For each of the 4 major classes (scout, mage, priest, fighter), there are 2 evil classes, 2 good classes and 2 that can be either good or evil. If you wanted to pick your home city first rather than your class first, this chart might help you see your options:

Freeport Priests - Defiler (shaman) or Inquisitor (cleric)
Qeynos Priests - Mystic (shaman) or Templar (cleric)
Either Priests - Warden (druid) or Fury (druid)

Freeport Scouts - Assassin (predator) or Brigand (rogue)
Qeynos Scouts - Ranger (predator) or Swashbuckler (rogue)
Either Scouts - Dirge (bard) or Troubadour (bard)

Freeport Fighters - Shadowknight (crusader) or Bruiser (brawler)
Qeynos Fighters - Paladin (crusader) or Monk (brawler)
Either Fighters - Berserker (warrior) or Guardian (warrior)

Freeport Mages - Necromancer (summoner) or Coercer (enchanter)
Qeynos Mages - Elementalist (summoner) or Illusionist (enchanter)
Either Mages - Wizard (sorceror) or Warlock (sorceror)

So, each city can support 16 classes - 8 of which are unique to it and the other 8 they share.
