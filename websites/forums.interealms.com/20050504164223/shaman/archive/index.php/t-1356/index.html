Bigguss
As far as training points go, you don't want to use them on weapon skills if you can help it. While Henora is right (that at the upper levels you'll find you have a ton of unused points) - at the lower levels it often seems like there are never enough points to go around. I might drop one point in each weapon skill (1hb, piercing, 2hb) to get them going.

As for weapon priorities:

Conventional wisdom says piercing shuld come first. Your epic is a spear, and many of the commonly used mid-range weapons are piercers (slime harpoon, spine chill spear, jade prod, rune etched icewurm fang, etc.) You could have a long and happy career using nothing but piercers.

Two hand blunt definitely has it's uses (maximum melee damage, great for swings between casts, jousting in PvP, etc.) - and it's considered to be the second most important weapon skill to train.

One hand blunt is the "red headed stepchild" or shaman weapon skills- it doesn't get the respect it should. There are planty of nice one handers- even at the end-game level. Most people will tell you, though, that this is the least important weapon skill to train.


Hope this helps.
