Trevurg
My main concern with attempting trak is all the adds.

What adds?

Step 1) Have your mage cast sense summoned. That will get you Tolapumj's pet from the prep room. Use Vision to hop to the pet, then Vision again to bind your view to Trakanon. You need to watch him, but if you know where he is and where the protector is anyone can pull the lair.

Step 2) When Trakanon moves to the "cubby" in his lair is when you'll do all your pulling. Wait for him to move and the protector and wandering jugg to path all the way to the left. Have your necro pull tolapumj from range, juggs don't assist him. Don't let him gate.

Step 3) Grab the 3 juggs at Tolapumj's spawn point when trak prot and wanderer are in the right spots.

Step 4) Grab the wandering jugg or the protector, whichever's convenient, when trak's back in his cubby.

Step 5) Get the leftover.

Step 6) You now have a mostly empty lair with a dragon in it, go kill him.
