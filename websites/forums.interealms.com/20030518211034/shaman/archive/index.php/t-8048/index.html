Harvyst
A spiritstone is a pottery made item that has a 30 second port to your home town. There is one (now) for each shaman race, and you have to get your specific race. It ports you to your race's home area.

It has unlimited charges, and trivials > 250 in pottery, and requires components which can be very expensive to gather such as distilled mana which takes 2 sapphires, and iron oxide which is hideously expensive if you do not farm it yourself.

Last night Verant put in the Kitty spiritstone after months of saying they would never add one for the kitties :) So that's why we're all going crazy :)

There is a companion stone called a faithstone for all races of cleric, as well. Nothing for Druids.
