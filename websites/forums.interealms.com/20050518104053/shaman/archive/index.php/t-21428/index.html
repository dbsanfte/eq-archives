Tordail Farstryder
It is just as forbidden for dragons of different colors to mate as it used to be for High Elves and Wood Elves to mate with humans. Vox and Nagafen are bound to their lairs for their indiscretion, and lore holds that Half Elves are shunned almost completely by both the High Elven and Wood Elven societies, and are forced to take refuge with whoever will provide them one. Wood Elves are, of course, more accepting of their Half Elf cousins, but are still a bit standoffish.

Gabus
Vox and Naggy DO indeed say something when the sleeper is released, but Kera is NOT their offspring.

As stated before, the sleeper is much older than Vox and Naggy, and the rules governing dragons of different colors getting freaky were put in place because of Kera. The rules were already written, which is why Vox and Naggy are imprisioned when we discover them.

Vox and Naggy DO run a script when the sleeper is released, but it is more to the effect of how now that Kera is out, he will destroy the forces that keep them contained. (IE the ToV dragons) It is a side effect of the release. Not because they created them. (cause they did not)

There is some good lore on the EQ official site, as well as the Role Playing Game books, which do a nice job of pulling the lore together in a cohesive fashion.
