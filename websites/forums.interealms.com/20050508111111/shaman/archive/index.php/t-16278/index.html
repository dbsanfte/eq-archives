My standard Pet Pack includes at a minimum a girdle and muzzle. That's assuming I can find a mage to summon me some. There's usually a mage or two selling summoned items at the bazaar.

However, you really have to be careful with the walnan swords and ixi fists. The proc on those breaks root constantly which can be fatal in a dungeon when a runner is dangerously close to aggroing others. When soloing I find it much more safer to just stick with rusty weapons and if I can find an SBD then all the better. In groups or where mobs don't run then I prefer the walnan/ixi weapons for max damage.
