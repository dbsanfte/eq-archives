Here is my advice.

Why? Because it is all stuff you can do yourself. The only guild participation would be on 8th shawl battle which doesn't require a lot of people, Giant Captain in WW and possibly Juggs although a duo/solo *can* do these for undead dragon sinew. Painful faction work and yes farming but very doable...

This is all gear that you can either buy or get yourself. I care mostly about HP and am not a fashion freak so take this with an ounce of salt as you might not like the look. Although the arms match Vindi BP colour!

Left Finger

Valorium Ring of Gallantry

Liquid metal (rare drop off of Golems in PoV..not that hard to get if you have Castle camps)

Right Finger

Regal Band of Bathezid

Can do this alone solo...very easily..bottleneck is Angry Goblin but the warlord spawn is a chump solo.

Left Wrist

Spirit Wrapped Cord

Bottleneck is HS mob :( but again doable if you check at the right times...

Right Wrist

Bracelet of Fortitude

Can camp or buy it for 5-10k depending on server

Shoulders

8th Shawl

Can do all of this except the final battle...But some nice drops for those who help...

Range

Talsiman of Benevolence

Can do this with 4 people and ally to CoV. Shaman/Cleric and Warrior/Rogue

Hands

Shissar Scaled Gauntles

Easy camp solo or can buy for 1-3k depending on server...

Arms

Hardened Gray Flesh Sleeves

Can camp or buy for about 5-8k depending on server...


Based on this you would net the following stats:

45 Stamina (135hp)
273 HP Directly

So a net of about 411HP with no real AC loss.
