I think I prefer the 65 hp hat over the new 7 wisdom one - based upon my formula for Shaman gear evaluation (I convert every stat to some number of HP to rank items):

Current Formula:

1 Sta = 3 HP
1 Agi = 1 Hp
1 Wis = 3 Hp
1 AC = 4 Hp
1 Mana = 0.05 Hp
1 Sv Magic = 0.5 Hp
1 Sv Fire = 0.5 Hp
1 Sv Cold = 0.5 Hp
1 Sv Poison = 0.25 Hp
1 Sv Disease = 0.25 Hp

So -

Slime Covered Rubicite Coif: (16 AC x 4) + (6 Agi) +(4 SvF x 0.5) + (4 SvC x 0.5) + (65 Hp) = worth 139 Hp

Tae Ew Spiritcaller's Coif: (15 AC x 4) +(7 Wis x 3) +(5 SvP x 0.25) + (45 Hp) +(40 Mana x 0.05) = worth 129 Hp

Accordingly, I should keep the Slime Covered, right? (at least until I change my formula again! )

Tyrace
if i were you, i would change the value of mana. i would set it at 1 mana = .5 hp, not .05.

from your scale 1 wis = 3hp, and 1 mana = 0.05 hp. but for wis over 200, 1 wis = 6 mana (by most calculations).

so to compare mana to wis, it would take 60 mana to equal 3 hp which is equal to 1 wis. but 1 wis does not give 60 mana.

if you use 1 mana = .5 hp, so that all of your equations balance out (ie 1 wis = 6 mana = 3 hp), then you get the following values:

slime covered: 139 (stays the same since there is no mana on it)
spiritcaller: 147.25 (15x4 + 7x3 + 5x0.25 + 45 + 40x0.5)
