Kriel Darkbane
My rating of the dungeon difficulties, easiest to hardest:

Rujarkian Hills (East Commons)
Mistmoore (Butcherblock)
Miragul (Everfrost)/Takish'Hiz (North Ro) (not sure on these two)
Deepest Guk (South Ro)

Rujarkian has few casters, and those are usually not that devastating. Most of the pulls are relatively easy. Takish has a lot of casters, some of whom are nasty. Guk is just ugly. It's got everything that makes dungeons tough grouped together. The pulls are harder, the rooms and passages are smaller, the mobs cast more. My favorite in Guk so far was a named who enraged, then gated away while enraged, and then summoned the tank. We still killed him easily enough, since everything was clear back to his spawn point, but it was a good trick. Mistmoore is relatively easy, especially with one or more classes who specialize in undead bashing.
