Karshak_rn
The soulstripper is a great weapon for a shaman and I am always seeing groups of 50+ wipe on it. I never understood why. I have camped it solo 5 times now and been unfortunate to only get the cloak every time, no weapon, but it can be solo'd and should be solo'd by a shaman. Here is how to do it.

Invis to the spawn spot, usually the placeholder is up (Servant of hate). Head down the wall opposite him to just past the small outcrop of rock, this will put you out of agro range from the PH and the two mobs guarding the entrance to the pit room. Pull up dog dog, give weapons, hos, regrowth and haste. Take out the placeholder. Easy with slow, dog, jbb and eb. Stand on the spot the PH spawns on and set pet to guard here. Then move back to the spot you started from.

You can solo pull the two mobs at the pit if you want, depends how confident you are. They are not hard to kill but you need full mana to beat the soulstealer (possibly) so dont be killing one when the next pop is due. Also, the two mobs at the entrance to the tunnel from the bridge are solo pulls at 52. The servant of hate is a pansy, but the archon of malevolance will complete heal twice. Anyone find it annoying that you dont get 3 x the xp. lol.

After 10 minutes self buff with hos, regrowth, all resists, rage, guardian, stamina, and any other things you want. If the soulstealer pops he will cast on you immediately. Engage pet and malosi him. Then slow him. At this point he will most likely be beating down on you. He double attacks for around 200-250. Root him and run to his spawn spot. This will ensure you do not bring agro from adds. ebolt him and jbb until he comes at you again. root and switch positions. He will possibly run under 20 percent so root him and jbb to death.

It can feel a little uncomfortable in the beginning but hang in there and get him malosi'd, and slowed, then stoicism and canni as required. I have usually finished the fight with 80 percent mana, and 80 percent health. I will post how close it is if he has the soulstripper on him because I assume the proc could make it more of a challenge but still doable.

I have been lucky every time because Chester Steelblad has been up and he is a wandering spirit that cons indifferent and will not add. I have been told that if he is not up a wandering mob will come down the tunnel and attack so be wary. If Chester is up just let him pass.

Good luck and please ask any questions, I think I have this covered, and it is always good to solo a level 51 mob comfortably so do it for the self kudos.... :)

I don't know the /loc but here are the directions. When you zone into Dulak head up the passage to the main courtyard. Hug the left wall as it turns into the courtyard. Head straight out from the wall through the invisible door in the wooden wall ahead of you. This takes you into the tunnels. Follow the tunnel down and around until you come to a huge stone bridge. Hope this helps. Use Invis, the tunnels are fulll of mobs.

Karshak_rn
Yes, you could clear the way from the entrance to Dulak if you wanted to. It would take a little while but is doable. Just bear in mind that you can invis all the way to the tunnel so if your server is busy you might find yourself piggybacked, or leapt over to claim the camp.

If you are fighting from the entrance, just fight up the corridor to the main courtyard, then take a left along the wall. When you get to the wooden wall you can agro pull the nearest mobs outside to kill them by just standing in the hidden entrance for a few seconds. Then it is just a case of crawling down the tunnel until you hit the bridge. There are some rooms along the right of the tunnel but if you hug the left wall you will head straight to the bridge. At the bridge you need to clear to the center, then turn to your right. That is the tunnel that the soulstealer spawns in.

If you are not too well equipped I would recommend you have kei to keep downtime at a minimum. Spawn time on mobs is around 18 mins so you do not need to immediately vacate the position of the last mob you dropped and can usually sit there to med up before moving on.

When you get into the tunnel you do not need to worry about the mobs at the entrance once or at the far end of the tunnel leading down into the pit. If you sit opposite the placeholder you can safely kill him (or the soulstealer) on re-pop. They run on low health so I would recommend root to stop adds.

For the soulstealer I it is worth popping your resists onto yourself and the warrior (Easy to do by timing the pop and giving yourself time to re-buff and re-med prior to the pop). If chester is up and wandering the tunnel leave him be, he is indifferent and will not add. If he is not up you are prone to seeing a wandering mob that likes to wander as you engage the soulstealer.

Horse/lizard is a good addition if you have one. Otherwise slow, malaisement (if you have resists on dots), listless power (if you have trouble keeping up with heals), dot, heal warrior, add pet in and you should have no real issues.

Is a good idea to do the stoicism quest as well if you haven't already done so, will make this crawl a whole lot easier on the mana pool... :)
Best of luck!
