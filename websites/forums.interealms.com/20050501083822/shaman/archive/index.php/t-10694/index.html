Thurdar
Just to expand on your 2nd question a little bit. The mob that you have targeted when you use spirit call will autmatically be attacked, but if there's another mob beating on you at the time odds are that the pets will switch to that one. If you want to switch them to a 2nd mob, you can't until the first one is dead and you have to have caught agro from the 2nd mob _after_ the pets were summoned. It works basically like enchanter pets in that regards.
