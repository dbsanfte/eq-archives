If you ever need to gather a lot of snake scales, this is the MOB. Gems drops are nice too but not too frequent. But solo, that place's a pain.

With no tracking but ghetto-track, you may have to run quite a bit each time the PH spawn before you can kill him. Then, when the lizard spawn, it spawns randomly anywhere on the island. Island isn't that big, but again... lot of running around involved. Plus, you may have to kill anywhere between one to a dozen PHs before dino shows up. And then, only to have it drops a bunch of snake scales.

Maybe fun to solo once, but a haul of loot it ain't unless you are unaccountably lucky.
