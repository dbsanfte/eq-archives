This is something thats been a bit of a problem with people in my guild. We have a few people who know Chardok well, and everyone else that know nothing at all, so explaining where to go can be difficult.

Easiest way i've found, is lev and invis, run to the lev tunnel, follow it all the way down and to the right (you'll be at the betrayer camp) now we all know theres 2 bridges here, and yes you can jump off either. I've found its easiest to just jump off the platform turning to the right and running straight for the waterfall.

Explaining it seems to be a lot harder than actually doing it, I'd suggest just follow someone else down there once, try to remember where you're going and you'll be fine =) And with the zone being fairly popular atm you shouldn't have trouble finding someone running down there at most times.

note: the *easiest* way is to have a chardok mage CoH you down there =P but we all can't have life that good.
