A tip on improving your skill levels with weapons -- start each level with your one-hand weapon that you have the most skill in or your fastest one-hander weapon. Now go max skill in offense first so you at least have one reliable weapon, but more importantly so that your offense is maxed. You get 5 skill raises in offensive per level, and being even a couple or a few behind can make a big difference.

Once your offense is maxed per your level, bust out the two-hander. It will now be more effective in your fights, and that matters a lot if you are soaking up hits with no shield on because you're using a two-hander. You want to be overcoming your vulnerability when using no shield by racking up the hits as much as possible. It's hard enough surviving with no shield in melee without having a crappy offensive skill too.

So max offense first, worry about lagging weapons skills after.
