My opinion would be that it depends on whether or not you plan to invest time into your shaman. If it's just a twink you might not want to spend such a large sum on it, however the fungi WILL make leveling your shaman a breeze. If you really want to balance out your shamans equipment nicely, you could get a full suit of good ac\hp gear for the amount of money it takes to buy a fungi.

I guess to answer your questions:
1. For a shaman, Yes.
2. Even to the 50s, yes.
3. Depends on whether or not you'll be playing your shaman long term (versus a flavor of the week).
