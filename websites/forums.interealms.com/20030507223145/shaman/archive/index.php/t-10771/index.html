Valsuvious
Whoa....slow down...then the hell did anyone say or even think that shaman are not good soloers. Basically, it comes down to how efficient you are and how good of a player you are. Yes, grab KEI (C3) at any opportunity because that will up your exp rate dramatically. I am serious when I say that shaman are GREAT soloers. Even though some may think that their exp rate is better while grouping, it all depends on where you are going at at your level. Read the forums on where to go at your current level and it will help you decide on where the best place to go is. I mean, HHK group exp is pretty good. But it does no good (for me anyways) when you have a set spawn time and have to sit there and wait for it to spawn again. I got better exp it seems around those levels to solo in Splitpaw or around the spires in SK. I did 44-49 in about 15 hours of playing while soloing in DL, TM, and FV. Prior to PoP, I think that the best place that I saw for exp to get to 60 was katta. The mobs were just too easy and not enough of them to be doing this with a full group, but one person was excellent.

Seriously, it's a lot of fun to have a shaman. I even enjoying doing it while soloing, partially because I always had the mob in my face, and partially because I had the serverwide crucible keeping me entertained. Talk to druids and wizzies and ask them how bored they get while soloing.

Take this for example, and remember that at that time I didn't have the best equipment and I didn't have my epic. At level 57 I was in the jaggedpine caves fighting gnolls back near the room where the head gnoll pops. Back here is a big open area that you can set up camp and have about 20 - 30 mobs to choose from. I could theorectically kill 10 - 12 gnolls before the first one popped back on me. I watched a druid quad in there (it's big enough to quad in there) and watched him kill 4 then med, then grab four more after he was full mana. By the time that the pops were coming back...he was just getting ready to start his second quad.

Just remember, that you will not have to med after every kill. With canni, kei and especially a horse (I didn't get mine till lvl 59, although I would suggest saving for one asap), you should be able to keep your mana pretty high, and therefore be able to keep your mana up high enough to make a huge difference in soloing vs. grouping. I suggest that you solo as much as possible, but don't forget to group a bit to make sure that you are aware of your roles in a group type environment.
