Chutoi
All other player-cast spells check again MR except the two Klaum noted above. However, I don't think that Kami is entirely correct in noting that most mobs' slows are MR checks. That may be true in the old world, but in later expansions, most notably SoL and PoP, that isn't really true. It seems most of the newer slows for mobs are stacked with direct damage effects and/or damage over time spells. The resist check on them seems to vary with the type of mob. You'll frequently see mobs checking against cold, fire, disease, and poison as well as magic. Best defense is to get your resists up when you have the chance, though many of the mobs in PoP have lure checks on their procs and AE's that make them essentially unresistable. For the most part, any mob with a resist check of -350 or higher can be considered unresistable. Player resists max out at 500. Checks of -350 may miss occasionally, but not often.
