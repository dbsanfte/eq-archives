Ximix the Troll
I find myself using Pox instead of Ultor in many situations, even at level 65.

Suppose that you are fighting in East BoT or in the Water Tower. Giants there are immune to poison and cold. So Disease is our only way to add some DPS.

Suppose you are killing Krigers, so the fights take about a little longer. For the sake of discussion, assume your DoT will last 1 minute after it gets cast on the mob.

My guild conducts small raids on the BoT Water tower frequently, so I am in this situation a lot. Which spell is better in that situation?

Pox of Bertox: 430 mana for 90 initial + 111 damage per tick. Duration is 1.8 minutes, so a little less than half of the spell's duration is wasted. Damage dealt is 90 + 111 x 10 = 1200 for 430 mana (about 2.8 damage/mana)

Breath of Ultor: 550 mana for 131 damage per tick. Duration is 2 .6 minutes, so most of the spell's duration is wasted in a 1 minute fight. Damage dealt is 131 x 10 = 1310 for 550 mana (about 2.4 damage/mana)

My conclusion is that on short-ish fights where you will not be coming close to using the full duration of your disease DoT, Pox of Bertox is the better spell.

On really long fights where you will be using the full duration of your disease DoTs, you want to stack both Ultor and Pox - since they are both excellent in that situation.

Pox is still a very useful spell at level 65. I paid around 30k (or maybe it was only 20k - I forget) for it (before the days of priest spell research) and have never regretted it.
