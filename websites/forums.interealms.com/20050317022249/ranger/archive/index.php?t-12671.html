the basics of PoV

you zone into the graveyard(like every zone), unlike the earlier zones, waiting on a corpse in PoV is 30 minutes.

Larvae, Frogs, Soldiers are not KoS.
Skeletons, Razorfiends, spiders, golems, crawlers are
The forest area near zone in is littered with traps that pop two skeletons that will chase you until you zone or they die, you can run from one corner of the zone to the next and they will still follow you.
Zone out is the giant tombstone in the corner of the graveyard.

Weakest mob there is 59,60 and will hit for 450ish, most mobs hit for 500-600.

oh and yes, and most importantly, things are soloable, if you know what your doing
