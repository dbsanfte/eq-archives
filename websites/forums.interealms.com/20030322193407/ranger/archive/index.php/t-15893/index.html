I used to kill these guys a lot - and I mean a lot. Strategy is pretty simple really. Head down, there are several camps of them, I used to like do the one where the Maurader spawns (I never killed him mind you when I was doing them), but any of them are about the same. Swim down, cast EB if you don't have an item with the effect. Bring lots of arrows. Load up a DoT, DS, Harmony, Snare and Root. I recommend fighting them down there, just faster that way. Harmony one, snare pull the other, swim out a ways, root him, shoot him, lay down a DoT, get your DS up, when his health starts lagging, melee the fellow - they have reletively few HP and no particular resists so to speak, which is why they are good ecperience really. I used to run this cycle a lot and would med/heal every 3 BG's. They hit in the 80's I think, maybe not even that hard, and have maybe 1800-2200 HP I'd guess.
