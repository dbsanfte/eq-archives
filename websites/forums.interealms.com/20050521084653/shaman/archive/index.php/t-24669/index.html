Tugela
The 7% is actually a focus type so 0 to 7% with an average of 3.5%. Or an average of 1 additional miss in about 30 hits theoretically.

It's not a focus type. The description is "Increase Chance to Avoid Melee by 7%", which is the same as some melee discs (just of smaller magnitude). When you fight something your chance of avoiding a hit is determined by the values of your avoidance and the mobs accuracy. What the spell does is increase that chance by 7%. So, if your unbuffed chance to avoid was 50%, the buff would change that to 50*1.07 = 53.5%. Likewise, if your chance to avoid was 5% or close to that then the buff would change it to 5*1.07 = 5.35%, ie it would appear to do nothing.

For most mobs it will reduce the damage taken by the tank by about 3-4%.

Giac
Mobs have a set to-hit rate. I remember the early GoD mobs having a hit rate of about 90%. This hit rate used to be modified by your agi, by avoidance mods, but most importantly by your LEVEL. So yeah, as people have said already, Sense will redeuce the chance to be hit by 7% of whatever it was.
Don't mistake this with dodge, parry, block etc. Those skills will only kick in when the mob already had a succesful to-hit roll.
