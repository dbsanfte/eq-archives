I only AA in LDON if possible. With the right group, I get 40% AA per LDON, average 40 minutes or so. Anyone that says LDON sucks is crazy. Decent AA, very good gear (if time consuming), moderate loot (150ish pp per LDON), easy to find groups as a shaman. Best recent expansion by far (since Velious). Bitch all you want about the repetitiveness of it, this is EQ, the whole game is about doing the same thing over and over.

Outside of raids or key camping, I haven't left LDON.

I ignore the NPC. One time, for kicks, I started the script (inside the Hedge) just to see how far I could go. I made it to the second wave and died there. Not recommended. :D

There are plenty of static spawns that will respawn before you can clear them all out. Basically you just cycle through them. I marked each static spawn on my map w/ a label so I could find them again easily.

Last time I was there I picked up a ribbon choker to sell as well. The drops won't make you rich (a diamond, peridot or other gem here & there, some bloodraven parts, etc), but it is steady exp that is SIMPLE and you have no concerns over pacing yourself, getting trained, or have to deal with any outside interference. There are only 1-2 spots in the whole maze where you can't easily (w/o any effort) single pull. Getting to the maze is more difficult than soloing inside in my opinion. :p

I would imagine it would also be an excellent spot to help a lower levelled friend with no flags get some great exp.
