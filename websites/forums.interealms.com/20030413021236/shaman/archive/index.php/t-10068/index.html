OK, I understand that the components needed for this awesome ring (AC10 WIS20 HP50) drop in PoJ. From a post on Allakhazam:


glittering ring: prisoners first floor of plane of justice. Reccommend being 58+ so the guards and rats dont assist the prisoners. If you have your 61 or 62 pet use it, they are lvl 58 and the guards wont assist and agro your pet. Drop rate is about one per 25 prisoners on average.

white diamond of justice: give an alkaline etched stone and a raw diamond to a shaman with imbue justice spell, 1k mana later he'll hand you a white diamond of justice.

crystallized cloudy eye: drops only off scarabs, beetles, and locusts in the tunnel behind Priest of Tumagal on the 3rd floor (same floor punishers / enforcers are on) Click wall behind the dwarf priest ghost and it will open passage.
Drop rate is about one in twenty off scarabs / demented locusts... bout one in fifteen off dread scarabs and enervating beetles if you go deeper in tunnel.

alkaline etched stone: only list this because you need it to make the diamonds. drops off mindless tunnelers, shadow ravegers, eyeless gnawers, and rampaging burrowers. Its possible they can drop off the named mobs in the area but the named are too rare to concern yourself with. These worm type mobs are in the same general area as the beetles and locusts. Drop rate is pretty decent on the entrance worms, and plentiful... Reccommend camping the entrance area with a group till you get enough stones and raw diamonds.

Raw diamond: used as a component for white diamond of justice... you can get these off the grolloks in the same tunnel behind priest, but punishers and enforcers are a lot easier to kill. Drop rate on raw diamonds is pretty high on the grolloks tho, thats where i get mine.

Combine: White Diamond of Justice, Crystallized Cloudy Eye, Crystallized Cloudy Eye, Glittering ring -- in Planar Jeweler's Kit. (yes thats 2 cloudy eyes, you'll get dnc message with just one)

Not trivial at 250 jewelcraft, however while combining with 280 wisdom and 250 dex I have made 6 out of 8 so far. I would recommend getting a focus, avatar, and kei before combine. Or if you are a shaman like myself just use your voice of the berserker spell or rabid bear... or both.

Good luck with your rings friends. I'd estimate it taking you at least 3 days to get 2 rings if yer lvl 65. If you are below and need a group, it will probably take you a week.
