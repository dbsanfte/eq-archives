Sorry, HSM = Hollow Shade Moor (maybe Hollowshade Moor) on Luclin. This Wolfhide is an easy camp - dropping of a named wolf that spawns in the cave on the East side of the zone. It can be combined with camping the Ceremonial Smudging Stick, which drops off the grimling chief on the south side of the zone, which is even easier, but long and dull.

Just a quiet word to consider on the earring of purity versus earring of cleansing....If I recall correctly, all the Velious quest legs (Thurgy, KD, Skyshrine) all have a right clickable "abolish disease" on them - so you may wish to get the cure poison earring and let your legs do the walking....The earrings are not that hard to get if you are on a blue server - drop in Cardok, however, im on a red server, and it is quite hard for my non-uber guild to zerg Chardok and hold it for long enough to get em heh heh.
