They are not worth the coin to make, unless you have a ton of money burning a hole in your kilt :-p I have max skill (180) and buffed charisma well over the 112 or so cap - over that, herb prices don't get cheaper. I want to say the success rate is about 40 percent, but since I can't afford to make 100 or more of them to do a more accurate percentage test, that is very approximate (although I probably have made close to 10 ten chargers in total since I maxed out skill, never more than one at once, and I have not written down and tracked my exact success rate - but 40 percent is close enough). It appears to run me about (about being the key word heh) 1500pp per successful 10 charger. And they are not clarity - the lesser one is breeze (2 mana per tick), and the greater one is 3 mana per tick. I have only ever made the greater one.

Now, for the duration - here it comes, it sucks - 15 MINUTES!!! Man, nothing like making a trade skill recipee worthless. 3 mana per tick for 15min at an approx cost of 150pp per drink heh. Again, only time I have made them is when I had about 1500pp laying around and nothing better to spend it on, and then I used them when soloing. I have never sold any, no one wants to pay what it costs. As 1500 is very approx (one successfull 10 charger might run you 1200, the next 1700) and you want to profit, really 2k per 10 charger would be minimum retail sale price to someone I didn't know. Who is going to pay that? That's right, basically no one. So, the moral of the story - they are not even worth making, unless you are rich and they are for soloing. Even then, it is questionable imo. Hope this helps.....
