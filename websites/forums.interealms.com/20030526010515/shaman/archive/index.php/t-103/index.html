Cragael
Okay.. here are the hard numbers:

Root: last 48s, 2s cast, 2s recast, 30 mana.
Enstill: last 96s, 2.5s cast, 2s recast, 60 mana.
Immobilize: last 60s, 1.75s cast, 3s recast, 80 mana
Paralysing Earth: last 180s, 2.5s cast, 2.5s recast, 100 mana.

Beside the hard numbers, the other thing to consider is the way root works. Every root spells works the same way: the target get a MR check when the spell lands, then gets another MR check every tick AND every time a DD lands (including the DD components of our DOTs). This means that ...

... when rooting, Malise/Malisement/Malosi/Malosini/Malo is your best friends. Doesn't even need to land BEFORE the root. Casting Mal* will actually help the root last longer even if cast AFTER the root land. With Malosini and PE on the MOB, the MOB is pretty much stuck in place for 2 to 3 minutes, unless it has unbelievably high MR. Incidently, this also pretty much makes the shamans the best rooters in the game.

... If you're playing wizard and are rooting/DD'ng, you may as well not bother with anything but the lowest, cheapest Root. Chances are that it will never even last 30 seconds anyway, isn't it?

... if you're doing crowd control, Enstill or Paralysing Earth are the way to go.

The last thing that everybody should know about root is the dirty little secret of Enstill. You see, that root is not like the other roots :D Every other root apply more or less the same modifier to the MR check made every ticks and with every DD that lands. Not so for Enstill... It's both durable and fragile. The chances of it breaking over time are low but, to compensate, the chances of a DD breaking it are fairly high.
