taraddar
RC1 is one of the AAs that I am considering getting next, so I would love to know which afflictions it will cure and which ones will require RC2 or RC3.

Personally I've found that rc 3 doesn't really cure much, pregod, that radiant cure 1 wouldn't cure. Radiant cure 1 cures 9 poison/disease/curse counters and has 1 chance to remove a detrimental spell with out any counters. RC push it up to 16 counters and 6 chances to remove a detrimental spell.

The biggest thing seems to be that remove curse, counteract disease/poison etc cure 8 counters. So anything that would be curable by them is already covered by RC 1. Remove greater curse on the other hand is 54 counters. Abolish poison/disease etc cure 36 counters. So cure significantly more than rc 3 will cure. So any spell with counters set with these spells in mind is either going to be curable by rc1 or well above rc3.

GoD did seem to add a number of things that RC3 gets but RC1 doesn't. The increased chances to remove a detirmental spell is more useful on RC3 than the increase in the counters. from 1 chance to 6 is alot better improvement than 9 to 16 counters. Once again the resplendant cure aa ups the counters cured to 25 yet coming up to 15 chances to remove detirmental.
