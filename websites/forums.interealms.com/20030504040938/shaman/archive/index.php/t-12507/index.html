Roxxanne
I can confirm that MD stacks with HoS but not FoSpirit or FoSoul. I suspect Sony may have just accepted this state of affairs, based upon the fancy new stacking rules you can see in Lucy for these spells. The rules seem structured to prevent HoS from overwriting a "better" dex or "better" str buff, by stacking with them. But one you move up to FoSpirit, the single target dex is no longer better, only the single target str, so only the str stacks with FoSpirit. Then when you move up to Focus of Soul at 62, neither the str or the dex buffs are stackable.
