Sithrax
I do hard missions whenever I can. Normal missions are very dull IMO.

My ideal group:

Tank (any plate tank, well geared and AA'd)
Enchanter (as long as they have CoD)
Snarer (RNG/DRU)
Cleric (druid can fill in as well, but needs to have skills)
Shaman (I am not absolutely needed, but hey, it's my dream group)
Monk (for controlled pulls, pacify/lull get a decent number of resists)

The core of this group is the death machine, i.e., the CoD pet. Monk brings in a charmable mob, which gets mezzed by chanter. Tash, Malosinia, and Ensnare on the soon-to-be pet buy you all the safety you need. After that, pet gets SoV, FA, and 2 weapons handed to it. These pets are absolute monsters, and with full MR debuffs, charm lasts a long time. DPS from the pet is truly obscene - I have read posts claiming over 1k dps. I have not parsed one, so I am not sure it is quite that high.

All the debuffs last a long time. Malosinia goes for ~20 min, Tash for 14, and Ensnare for 14. Every 10 minutes, break charm intentionally to redebuff. I have never lost a chanter to the pet of doom when using this strategy.

The downside is that not all mobs are charmable, so sometimes you have to hunt around for one. Hard adventures are definitely winnable without the use of charm, but they take lot longer to complete. With pet, they run around 40-45 min. usually... without, it's more like 70-80.

As for dungeons, Guk is definitely tough. Very long and packed full of casters. Ruj is probably the easiest.

Yeah, some of the mobs are not snarable... I always forget about those wolves in Ruj and end up trying to root the first one of the night. Snare is handy, though not central to the strat (which works in all kinds of fun places, not just in LDoN).
