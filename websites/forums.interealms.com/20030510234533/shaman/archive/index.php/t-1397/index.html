As far as doing your epic early, the epic quests were designed so they could be done over a character's life...do the Freeport gem quest at 29 (invis is good), pick up your Shield of Falsehood in the 30s, grab your black fur boots in your late 40s. If you do the quest correctly, it can't be obtained before level 46 since it requires a quest bit from Fear (and you must be 46 to enter the planes).
