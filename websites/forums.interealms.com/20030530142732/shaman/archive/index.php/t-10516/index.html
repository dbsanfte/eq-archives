It will work but its not a very good duo. It is better to have two people of different classes who compliment each other's abilities than two of the same class. Shamans can do a lot of things very well, but one thing we suck at is damage. Thus, we're better off partnering with a class who does a lot of damage, but is not very self-sufficient. Hence, the traditional shaman + monk duo, as well as many others.
