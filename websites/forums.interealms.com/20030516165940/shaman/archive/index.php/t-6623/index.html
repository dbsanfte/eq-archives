Goldark
Firstly, Shaman use wisdom not int.... so forget about int items :)
At lower levels you get little benefit from extra wisdom - so you may find mana items more beneficial. Perhaps more effective are focus items though.

I would suggest getting a cougar claw earring and mammoth hide cloak (100pp and 300pp, depending on server) for focus effects.

A spine chill spear (350pp) makes a good weapon, not sure when it starts to proc though. If you are a barbarian look at the tribunal necklace quest. You can do it at a very low level and it summons a nice hammer to get you past the first newbie areas.

Sarnak Battle Shield (1k) is very nice and will last you a long time (I use one at 55) - but would put a big dent in your finances. Charred Guardian Shield and Clay Guardian shield are alternatives, depends how much you want to spend.

Jaundiced bone armour has great AC and is very useful at 45 when the effects come into play. The common pieces are 300-400pp each. If you want to pay less, totemic is worth considering.

Something or other bone bracelet is good too - can't remember the exact one I think there's polished and chipped versions.

Once you reach the middle levels don't worry so much about mana items. Go for regen, AC and HP, and then worry about wisdom after that.

Hope that helps.
