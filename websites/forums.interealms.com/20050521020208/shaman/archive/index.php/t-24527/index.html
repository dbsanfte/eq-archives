Valkargen
I agree he needs innate DW.

He also does need more hps, a 3k base would help.

In concerns to damage I don't think raising his hit levels is the means to go about it...

From melee sources once DW is activated puppy does 55 to 60% the dps of a mage pet from every parse I've run. Mage/BL pets hitting for 92 while dogdog hitting for 70.

The main difference isn't the baseline stats of the pet but the fact that Mage/BL's utilize foci and haste their pets to 85% with BL's adding a 5% damage mod, and necros haste their pet to 70%.

Even in a group with a chanter for haste and arming pet with a muzzle nets dogdog 78% haste...a mage or bl in the same group is netting 95% haste and the necro 80%. Sans chanter puppy drops to 70% haste. On overage doggy loses 10 to 15% due to haste differential or lack of foci.

If puppy needs a DPS upgrade I would like to see a 1hour duration 70% pet only haste...Rune of Spirit or such...and pet foci made available to us. Pet AA that allows puppy to crit could also be an option.

Not all shaman utilize their pets to the same degree, however for those that do it would allow a shaman to net appreciable gains in puppy powah while not stepping on the toes of other classes, and in a manner that any shaman can attain if they choose.
