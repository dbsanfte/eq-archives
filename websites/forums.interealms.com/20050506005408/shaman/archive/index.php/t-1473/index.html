Don't know any Beastlord sites, but I do recommend Luclin - IF you have the computer to play it. It does take more computer than the other expansions. Transportation is one key - the Nexus can get you to any of the conitnents on Norrath. The Moon also has some great zones for leveling up (Paludal for early to mid-teens, for example) and a Troll has a lot fewer faction problems there. I got to ally with the merchants of shadowhaven by running a delivery quest maybe 30 times while waiting for rezzes. One time through the quest is enough to be able to buy and sell in shadowhaven (no, not all vendors, other end of town needs a different quest). The vendors in the Bazaar start off as indiff anyway.

The Tribunal server is pretty lowbie friendly, in my opinion.
