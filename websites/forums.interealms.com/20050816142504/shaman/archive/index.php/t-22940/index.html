It is a touchy issue. Clerics have the best healing in the game by far. DPS was ramped up in GoD as a means of reinstating difficulty. More DPS means more healing needed. Slow was mitigated, destroying our ability to heal as a cleric when slow was available.

I'll say this about the Shaman class... from another person's perspective, it may seem that since we have Slows, we need little else. Problem is, if you have a decent cleric, you DO NOT NEED slows, especially in mitigated content. In the groups in which I have Main Healed, slow is simply a tagging spell... I.E. %target has been tagged to allow its DPS to be healed. Without tagging it first, I cannot keep up.

In such a situation, Turgurs is merely "The spell you must cast before you can adequately heal" and little else. Sure, it helps with soloing, but only because our pet is so feeble that unmitigated slows are required for it to last more than a few seconds.

So let's forget about slow and heal for the moment... they completely own us in buffs, which is okay, to an extent. I'd be happy with Druid-level buffing capabilities... Focus is nice, but my problem is that it seems every friggin tank and mage out there already has Shield up. Tank Ornate makes me want to scream! Try NinjaBuffing with Focus in PoK, your spell will bounce off more than slows on an un-malo'd mob.

Which brings another thing to attention... our spells have zilch for resist mods. Malo is merely "The spell you must cast before you can cast other spells."

So in a group, you have to cast "The spell you must cast before you can cast the tagging spell that you must cast before you can adequately heal," then "The tagging spell that you must cast before you can adequately heal," and THEN you can start to work your healing magic as a Cleric could without having to throw two spells off first.

Let's not forget that you're blowing around 550-600 mana on those tagging spells, which makes Cannibalize less of a luxury and more of a curse. Cannibalize is now "The spell you must cast in order to cast the spell you must cast in order to cast the spell you must cast in order to adequately heal at the level a cleric can without any pre-requisites."

Canni, Malo, and Turgur's look nice on paper, but they are merely hindrances. This is why we thrive on long fights. We have to get off our chain and if we cannot before the mob dies, we are pointless.

The DPS we have to fall back on is very low, and comparable to clerics'. Thing is, we fall back on ours much more often because we have to. Clerics have a one shot spell that solidifies them in all groups and raids. Clerics have a simple role.

Now then, how do you satisfy a Cleric's justifiable hunger for more diversity (as in, less simplicity) and, at the same time, satisfy a Shaman's justifiable hunger to, with all the tricks he must turn in the first 10 seconds of every fight, heal ENOUGH in a group to keep a tank alive?

I don't know. What I do know is that I would like larger heals, or larger nukes. I don't care what you give me, as long as you give me some group desirability. Go nuts, put something (GASP) ORIGINAL into the game, like Endurance regen or percentage avoidance, but make it UNIQUE to us.

I'm done babbling now :)
