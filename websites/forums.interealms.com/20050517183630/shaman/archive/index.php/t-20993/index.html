I dunno if you saw the threads on this shortly after GoD opened, Henora, but the general consensus at the time was that it was a waste of AA. I believe the (parsed) determination was that you:

(a) had to be casting
(b) get stunned
(c) pass a check against persistent casting

then you could possibly channel through the stun. Thus, the AA adds no benefit to channelling, merely a chance to cast through a stun.

All in all, that seems a pretty low chance. Probably a 15% chance of casting through a stun if you are already mid-cast and get stunned.

Perhaps it's been changed somewhat.
