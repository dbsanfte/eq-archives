Asta Khaan
PvP servers have everything non-pvp servers have, and then a little more. The main difference is that constant feeling of insecurity, ranging from holy hell run fer yer life to just a small twinkle of looking over your shoulder. I guess you could say it's similar to what you felt whenever you entered a new zone as a newbie, but stronger. You won't shake your pursuers by zoning here.

Of course there will be the bad seeds, particularly in the form of low lvl overtwinked gankers who smacktalk, but this is also the beauty of it. Sooner or later you WILL get even with them, something you simply do not have the tools to accomplish in a non-pvp environment.

Most important of all, go in with an open mindset and remember it is a game. Don't play on a pvp server striving for character progression. Play to progress your own skills at fighting others and enjoying it, even in defeat. Obviously a couple gear upgrades (and clickies!) and more levels will help out, but this should not be the focus of your time.

As for shaman pvp, stay below lvl52ish.

If you ask really nicely I'm certain some high levels can jumpstart your careers. There are very few true newcomers to the pvp servers, so you should be able to find someone to help get you started. If I was still playing I'd hook you up for certain.
