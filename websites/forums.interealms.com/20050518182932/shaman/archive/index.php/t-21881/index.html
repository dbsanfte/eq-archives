Siroro
I understand the fact that asking for totally new line of spell is out of the Question but in most short fights we dont get the time to cast everything we want so i have been thinking of a New Type of Debuff

A Multi Debuff

A debuff which contains of many different aspects that stacks with most other debuffs and saves debuff slots on raid encounters well here it is!

Slot 1 : Slow - 65%
Slot 2 : Decrease Atack by 75
Slot 3 : Decrease AC by 42
Slot 4 : Decrease All Resists by 50
Slot 5 : -20 Accuracy
Slot 6 : Decrease Enemy Max hit by 15%
Resist Adjust - 75

I believe this Spell should stack with everything. (if a better slow EG Turgurs is to be cast it should remove the slow factor from this spell)

This spell should come with quite a hefty Mana Cost to compensate for all the factors it gives and should become very useful for very quick encounters like LDoN mobs as no other spell should be required.

I believe this spell should be 100% immune by raid encounters and only used on one or few group encounters to make us useful!

I am thinking the spell should be along the lines of 600 Mana cost and 5 Second Cast Time.

Please leave your comments here in an orderly way ! tell me what you thing about this kind of proposal for a one group encounter!
