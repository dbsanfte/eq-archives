Woolwich
WW is VERY feasible at your level. I've been soloing there off and on since 57. The exp is NO where near anything in POP, but the cash is great! In an hour or two there I usually get 3-4 vellium hound furs (800-1200pp in bazaar), 5-6 forged dragon eggs (150-200pp), 7-8 drake eggs (20pp) and 10 or so wurm eggs (10pp).

Watch the Ice Burrowers; they'll kill you quick. Get an extended range item also. With it you can snare the hounds before they aggro you. I use one of those croc tooth neck thingys from PON. You MUST be pro-COV or WW is way too hard to do.

Sow yourself before entering SG then invis - zone in and head straight ahead towards the shelf to the right of the seahorse tunnel- climb up. Now look down and you can see that if you drop down you will fall behind the seahorses. Do so and start swimming thru tunnel - stay as close to the left wall as you can since there is another seahorse in the caves on your right. Even if you are being hit just keep swimming up the tunnel and fall over the waterfall. Stay to the left and swim to the ledge - get out and you can re-ivis there. Find the tunnel to your left and just follow on the left wall until you zone into WW.
