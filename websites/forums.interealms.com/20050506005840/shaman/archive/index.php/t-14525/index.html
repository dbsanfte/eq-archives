Valsuvious
Yeah....just my opinion....but the CA/LR line is one of the best AAs that we can get. I did love the extra 250hps from ND. I do love some of the other aspects of the other abilities. But...next to Cannibalization (which is undoubtedly easily the BEST AA ability that we get) CA/LR is prob second best. As a shaman, you will be getting hit alot. With this ability your survival chances increase dramatically. Having this ability also helps in alot of the other abilities. If you are casting and the mob misses you, then you have a better chance of not being interrupted while casting something. If the mob misses you, then you won't be spending the mana and time to heal yourself when you could be healing someone else or doing some dmg to the mob. All in all, this ability will save your life more times. If will also enable you to go toe to toe with some things that you might not have been able to before. All of the hps in the world aren't going to help you if you are getting hit every time.

Basically......get CA3 then work on Lightning Reflexes right after that. It is another one of those game-changing abilities IMO.
