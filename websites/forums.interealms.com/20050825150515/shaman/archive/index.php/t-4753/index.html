Boinshar
Soloing, torpor >>>>>>>>>>>>>>> 75h

More efficient, less cast time (3 sec if you have quickbuff 3), and it's not like you expect to do a lot of melee damage.

Besides, would you rather channel through a 6(3) second spell or a 10 second one? Also, 75h only heals up to 75%, which means you need to have ~2700 hps for it to be useful if you drop down to like 2% health. If you heal at 40% health (to avoid low health agro or an unlucky round), that means you'll need to have at LEAST ~6000 (!) hps for it to achieve it's maximum efficiency. If you heal at 20% (1 bubble) you'll still need to have ~3900 hp for it to achieve maximum efficiency.

Grouping, torpor >> 75h

Sure, torpor slows down melees, but it's shorter cast time gives you time to canni at least once. Again, less time for you to channel, and again, if you heal someone at 40%, they need to have ~6000 hps for the 75h to achieve it's maximum effectiveness, and if you heal at 20% they'd need to have ~3900 hps.

For raids, group only, torpor > 75h

You should be healing someone at 40% minimum to prevent low health agro, so that means your target needs to have ~6000 hps else you are not healing the full 1950 points. Only wars, extremely well equipped monks and hybrids can reach that.

For raids, out of group 75h>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>torpor

Since torpor can't be cast out of group, this is a no brainer... but still, your target needs to have ~6000 hps for full efficiency. However, since when it's time to spot heal nobody cares about mana usage, 75H's going to be a hellovalot more useful than Chloroblast in most raid situations when healing out of group.

Of course, this only goes for level 60s who has torpor.
