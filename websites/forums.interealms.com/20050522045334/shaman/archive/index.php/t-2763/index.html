BlargX
For what everyone recommended, you should come in well under 20k.

Use the extra to buy him an iksar beserker club and a drakkel icegrinder. The IBC starts proccing at level 10 a very nice spell that adds to defense and offense, making you temporarily much harder to hit, which is a nice way to get out of trouble; plus it's just a good weapon at 7/18; the fast ratio will get his 1hb up very fast and it puts out a lot more damage than you would think because of its speed. They're less than 1k on my server.

The drakkel icegrinder 2hb is nice and fast for a non-uber 2hb and proc's a DD I think starting at level 1, and quite often. I've gotten it to proc 5 times a fight; the DD hits for 30, if I remember.

Shamans can do very well with ALL their weapons, so it's wise to build them all up, and pretty inexpensive. The rune etched icewurm fang is pretty expensive and it doesn't proc at low levels, but at 12/19 it will last your shaman up until he gets his epic. Goes for around 4-8k on my server -- prices fluctuate wildly. Don't overpay, but get it sometime.

Remember that shamans melee very well in early levels(heck, mine meleed with only 120 wisdom all the way to level 52), so don't hesitate to encourage your friend to get in there and mix it up physically. That will raise his defense too, which the standard shaman powerleveling ruins. He will have a terrible time soloing and a rough time playing in general if his defense gets too far behind.
