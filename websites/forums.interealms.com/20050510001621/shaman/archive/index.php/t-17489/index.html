Perelandra
Shaman pets can use any of the mage summoned weapons starting with the level 61 pet. Prior to this, the pet can only use Fists of Ixiblat. The fists of Ixiblat require the pet to be level 10 or greater to use while the other 2 require level 40 (the pet prior to 61 is level 39).

All 3 weapons do exactly the same damage - the only differences are a) skill type (blades are 1hs and fists are h2h) and b) resist check on proc (Walnan = magic, Fists = Fire, Kedge = Cold).

The damage the procs do is based on the following formula:
Level*2 + 20 = Damage.
The level is the PET LEVEL not the player level. If I remember correctly, the 61 shaman pet is level 58 so the proc damage is 136.

Beastlords have claimed that their pets have higher DPS when using the fists than with other weapons - claiming their pets have higher H2H skills. I have seen no proof of this one way or the other, but you may want to test some things on your own with shaman pets to determine if they have higher skills with a certain weapon type.

Rules for PCs do not necessarily apply with NPCs. Most pets are flagged as warriors including BST pets. Exceptions being certain necro pets and the post 50 Mage Water pets which are rogues. Don't assume that since 1hs on a PC warrior is higher than H2H that it will also be higher on NPCs. You'll actually have to do some parses to test this out.
