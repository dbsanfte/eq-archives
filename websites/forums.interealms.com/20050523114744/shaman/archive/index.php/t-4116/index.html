Graal the Dorf
Increased AGI does NOT help you dodge, parry, or riposte more.

However, increased AGI will reduce the chance that you get hit in melee. There are 2 components to AC, as has been mentioned, avoidance and mitigation. The mitigation portion of AC does exactly jack and **** above a certain level. The avoidance portion of AC can only be increased by increasing Defense skill or by increasing AGI. Once Defense skill is capped, that means AGI is the only way.

Not getting hit == GUUD.
Not getting hit >> getting hit for lower damage.

Anyone who is going to possibly taking rampage damage, ripostes, or getting aggro should get AGI.

As far as xp grouping goes, adding AC beyond a certain point doesn't lower average damage per hit. Adding AGI does decrease the amount you get hit, which is essentially the only way to lower damage taken by a tank without modifying the mobs attack speed or debuffing its atk.
