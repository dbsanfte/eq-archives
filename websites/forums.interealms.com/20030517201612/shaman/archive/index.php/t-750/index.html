Ulthorks are located in Eastern Wastes in Velious. Just north of the bridge past the Coldain camp, just follow the shoreline north. You'll come to a clearing where Walrus, Ulthork Warriors, Ulthork Hunters, and Whooly Rhinos.
Stay away from Rhinos and Ulthork Warriors until 39 or so, they hit very hard, very fast and have a mountain of HP's.
Good exp and loot I've been hunting them since 35, I'm 39 now.
