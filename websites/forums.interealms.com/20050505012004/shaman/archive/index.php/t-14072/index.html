deria
Until you have Malos, you will find that sometimes Malo is not enough. Even with Malos, actually, you will still get some resists.

The only advice I can offer is to get Malosinia (if you do not already have it) and use it. Malo on incoming, then malosinia, then slow. Yes, you will be wasting some mana but in the long-run it should work out to less mana than you will be wasting by bouncing slow 5 times.

I suspect that you just had some amazingly bad luck, though. In general when I first went to BoT (63 at the time, just malo and no malosinia -- pretty much your situation) I had some trouble but nothing quite on that order. We are unfortunately all at the mercy of a very streaky random number generator.

weissblau
What I do if I'm having a bad run on resists... lead with Malo; try Turgur's; if that fails, cast Malosinia while Turgur's recycles. Alternate Turgur's and Malosinia until slowed or until shaman is dead :-)

As has been pointed out, Malosinia is lower aggro than Turgur's, so an alternate is to chain Malosinia and then do a single Turgur's when it sticks. I have rarely had to cast a second Turgur's after Malosinia is in. You get the added benefit of reduced resists for snare, nukes, cripple, etc.
