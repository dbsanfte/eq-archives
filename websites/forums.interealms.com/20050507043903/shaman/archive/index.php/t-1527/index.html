Eoin MacManus
A brief summary (cause some people are confused):

unfired spiritstone = idol sketch + large block of enchanted clay + water + celestial essence + vial of distilled mana + Everfrost essence + sculpting tools combined in pottery wheel

spiritstone = unfired spiritstone + divine crystalline glaze + HQ firing sheet in a kiln

idol sketch - vendor sold
large block of enchanted clay - large block of clay + level 8 enchanter
water - vendor sold
celestial essence - celestial solvent (Bazaar vendor) + (apparently) any level 39 and over spell research component combined in a mortar and pestle
distilled mana - 2 opals + poison vial + 39 enchanter
Everfrost essence - frost turnip (foraged in Everfrost) + water combined in brew barrel
divine crystalline glaze - white lead (lead sulfide and limestone combined by a rogue in a mortar and pestle; components are vendor sold) + iron oxide + permafrost crystals + water combined in a glaze mortar
