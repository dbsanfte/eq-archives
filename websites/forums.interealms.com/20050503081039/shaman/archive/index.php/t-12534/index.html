Trevurg
I have a 46 bard :)

Track is a 35th level skill. You get a track button, just like druids / rangers. There's no "Track Song" just in case anyone was wondering.

Skill cap is 100, or 25 less than a druid's.

Range at 100 is... hmm. Hard to describe. More than visible range. Very useful in zones with lots of trees or uneven terrain.

And yes, latest spawns on top of the list always. Very useful in catching things just as they pop, not very useful in finding specific spawns.
