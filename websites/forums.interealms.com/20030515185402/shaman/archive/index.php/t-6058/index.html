FWIW,

I tandem the bank easily with 60 shm and 54 war. I slow and epic, while he just attacks with my dogdog.

They take a bit, but they dont run, and if you pull to the hallway, there is only one roamer that goes that way.

Might be harder if your slows dont land like mine did due to level.

Also not sure on your pulling of singles or not, would have to try it.

I could clear the 2 roamers, the two bank guards out front, the two in pit, and the 2 on the way to arena, with time to spare. Sometime even pulling from up the Area tunnel to get 2 of those before repops.

The kills take a while, and I am unsure how a ranger would do there, but once slowed the giants are pretty easy, they just take a while.

For the record, I fought there over 5 days approx 20 hours total and got Chain Sleeves, Plate sleeves and Plate gloves. About 1 drop every 4 hours or so.

At 60 its easy to tell, any mob dark blue can drop armor, but the bank area is around 30% blue the rest are light blue, so the chances are armor dropping isnt real good, but it is there.

Once special note, is Capt Bellevos (sp), he can whip some serious ass to a tandem, so if you accidently aggro him, just die where you stand, and dont train your buddy.

Good news is you can leave one thing on body, and get a rez pretty quick if you are playing in primetime, there is always clerics running around.
