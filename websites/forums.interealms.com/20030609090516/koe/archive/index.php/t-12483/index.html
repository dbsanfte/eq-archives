My first memories of KoE, sitting about on Sister Isle in the Ocean of Tears
with Togashi and Jinxter, we were in our 20's. They were telling me about
this guild called, The Legion of Tears.

A few months prior to this LoT had merged with KoE, and taken the former
guildname, while Bobcats (I think, who may have been inactive at the time
for a short while) ensured the KoE name was kept secure.

I ended up in LoT. As I did, the guild was falling apart, under the leadership
of Outerlimit (?) (a human wizard!). Rizz being the "nice guy" had agreed
to be a mere officer during the merger. The guild fell apart, with most of the
original LoT eventually moving to Saryn (the first ever server split).

Meanwhile Rizz began to rebuild KoE. I stayed in LoT, Cambridge the druid
took command of LoT. LoT continued to fall apart. Rizz told me I could
jump over anytime I wanted. I eventually had to go through the recruitment
process though, with Jinxter as sponsor.

Hope you enjoyed a bit of history.

Cheers.
