Hello all - A few bits of news and things I wanted to mention regarding the crucible.

New Server
Sunmoon recently moved the boards to another server, as you may have noticed from his posts. If you notice any problems, please let us know... but looks like this one went pretty smoothly.

Forum Changes
We have modified the descriptions of the forums to make them a bit more clear, please make your posts in the correct forums. If you haven't looked in these forums lately, check them out! Lots of good info in there. Notably, we tried to clear up the focus of these forums:
Equipment - All gear related questions.
Quests - Shaman quests, including epic, cudgels, cloaks and shaman gear quests.
Spells - Shaman spell discussions, spell drop info, spell quest walk throughs.

Custom Titles/Avatars
There have been some questions about user titles/avatars. The inability to edit these is a result of a using a beta version of the boards. Sunmoon will upgrade us to another build/version when that is available and there is time. In the meantime, if you have 1000+ posts and just can't live without a title you can PM me and I will give you a title. I will only do this once per person, so choose wisely.

serverwide.crucible
I've recently received a few cross-server tells in game and PMs on this board complaining about the serverwide.crucible channel in-game. The moderators on this forum have no moderation access to the serverwide.crucible channel, nor do they have any obligation to try to police that channel. I never even visit the channel, myself. I would suggest the use of the /ignore, /report or /petition features if someone is bothering you in the channel or in-game.

Crucible website
I am told my FTP access has been restored and so hopefully I will finally be able to start the overhaul of the main crucible site. Sorry for the delay!

New Site Moderator
I would like to congratulate you all on gaining Gloriana as our latest full site moderator, joining myself and Mojo. Now you have yet another person to complain about when your thread gets moved to The Grey! ;) Guard your signatures shamans, because she has much less tolerance than Mojo and I!

Signature Rules Reminder
Speaking of, please remember the Shaman's Crucible signature rules are:
Size: no more than 100 pixels high, approx 250 pixels wide.
LESS THAN 25K in File Size.
NO java, flash, shockwave, javascript, etc.


That's all for now. Thanks everyone for your contributions and for making the Shaman's Crucible a great board and community. :)

-Henora
