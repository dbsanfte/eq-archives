Beawolf
I've spent a little while soloing at the entrance to Ferubi, it takes a bit of work to get mobs split and debuffed, I typically malos and zone agro. Zone in, root the non malos'ed mob and park the other semi close to the zone line, zone again to clear agro. Re-root and debuff and then root rot. You have to be careful as the mobs hit for around 1k double plus proc differing DD's. Not really worth the effort for the xp, about 4-6% aaxp per kill, I'd put it on par with tactics ogre's for mob hp. Fun to try but like the rest of GoD I've seen, the xp isn't all it could be.
