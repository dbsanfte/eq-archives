Zyphyr
Assuming you make use of Canny on a regular basis (in other words you actually use your full abilities), 5 HP/Tick of regen is aproximately equivilant to FT2 (slightly better than 2 actually). Assuming you are able to use the HP for Canny and don't have it chewed away by a mob.

The 11 extra AC on the new BP may well save you from taking enough damage to offset the 5HP regen of the Iksar. That depends on how much time you actually spend tanking, how hard the mobs hit, and how high your AC already is.


The other options you have mentioned (compared to what you have now):
Belt is : +6 AC, +5 Dex, +30 HP, +30 Mana
Shoulders are : + 4 AC, -2 Sta, + 5 Wis, +5 Dex, + 30 HP, +30 Mana, -2 Fire
Mask is : +4 AC, +30 HP, +30 Mana

Of those 3, the mask is the least advantageous. Belt vs. shoulders depends on just how badly you need more AC. I personally would go for the shoulders, since I do think that the 5 Wis is (slightly) more useful than 2 AC (despite the fact that I prefer AC to Wisdom in selecting my equipment). Unless those 2 AC would push you over a multiple of 50.
