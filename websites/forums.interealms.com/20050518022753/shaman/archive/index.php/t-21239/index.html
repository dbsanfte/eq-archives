Spiritblood
The best thing you could ever do is find someone to play with, a melee character preferably, but other class can work very well too.

If you are not planning on racing to 65 then your buffs can and will be usefull for a long time.

Maybe find a druid or wizard to play with too to get around easier and escape from bad situations. And a cleric to round out your group. You can do a lot of thing with that setup.

If you have to solo some of the time be sure to keep your defense up by going head to head with some melee mobs as you level. This should keep your weaponskill up too. From 1 to 50 shamans can contribute some to fights by meleeing. And with the weapons available for cheap in the bazaar nowadays its even better.

Find some quests that will keep you busy. When you get high enough you can do Nurga and later Droga where there are lots of items available through quests and killing there will get you good faction in Chardok which in itself opens up some interesting quests.

Most importantly have fun - and dont grow too attached to your shaman because there is a big risk that you will someday feel like you are not contributing enough hehe.
