nornez
Sick of HHK & looking for an XP solo spot, I read a great post here on the Crucible giving great detail on XP spots thruout the game.

I decided to head to FV & try the valley in the NE corner of the zone that funnels into the ZI to the swamp of no hope.

Very safe place to pull to with a great number of mobs to pull from. I avoided the shammy frogloks at first (shammy's are bad ass' if ya didn't know :P) but they drop as easily as any of the other frogs & drolvargs... just try not to pull 2 in a row as I usually come away malaised.

Anyway in the time it takes for 3 KEI's to burn off I have gone from 34-36 easily. Some of the easiest soloing I have ever done.

Anyways thanx for the post that I got this little xp spot info from & I totally recommend anyone 32+ if you don't mind solo without dogdog to prolly about 37... although some of the frogs are starting to turn lo-blu at 36. Maybe time to head to DL.

BTW I haven't seen a single sole from FV ZI to the camp spot (good 5 min run) so it looks like a way under camped spot/zone.
