Actually the Luclin engine did make some significant changes to framerate for me (and all of you thought in a way you may not overly notice) . The most notable change was occlusion. What this means is that if there is geometry blocking your view, then nothing else behind it is rendered. So go into PoK and stand behind a tree and face the bank and strafe out and see your framerate drop. This occlusion is conditional, as some geometry doesn't block the view frustrum, but man, over all this made a big difference.
Now a lot of people didn't really notice because they thought the engine upgrade would help raids etc, which it didn't because you still would have 60+ new world models on the screen, which brings even my machine to its knees. Hopefully this more recent engine upgrade will bring things up to speed.
