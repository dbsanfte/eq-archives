I'm thinking the mage or the necro. Mage for focus items and pet weapons, necro for snare. Alpha pet with 150dd proc is tight! It could be a misconception, but I think the mage, necro pets tank just as well as a soso equipped warrior, but the dps of the pet is higher, especially without babysitting the tank class (aka casting all the quick dd's that a SK has). Mage/Necro pets get aggro FAST without having to cast clinging darkness or stun a couple times at the beginning too. That and the mage pets never complain about us Torporing them for heals, and forgetting to buff them. Not having to cast a haste spell on the tank every 10 minutes alone is worth it! If the tank dies, they can always recast another one. If the sh** hits the fan, and you need to get out of there, cast Gate.
What about pulling. This is a toss up. If you are going strictly for max exp, then the shaman pulling works pretty well. Malo on the pull, and then cast slow as the mob is coming to the group. If you get really good at duo boxing a SK, then you could do zones duo style that you couldn't do without the feign death pull. These are probably limited, and if that's the case you can always join a group and go from there. First thing that comes to mind though is The Deep for farming your levitate spell. I think a duo mage/shm could do that just as well though.

If it were me, I would go Magician between the two caster classes. If you want to get into a full group, magicians stack really well since all their damage is dd, and folks love the focus items. But also, magicians are primo money makers. Their summoned items become highly valuable later on. 10pp a pop on muzzles, fists of ixiblats, girdles for pets, naki rings. Set him up overnight and watch the plats roll in. Go to work, come back the next day... I'm assuming a good 100pp each night on average at least. Hey - it adds up!
