Korelen
Droga might be a spot you want to try out. I PL my rogue there a lot. The loot and PP [ I think the avg drop is around 10-13pp ] is nice, and I think most mobs will be in your level range. You can move around freely with invis. For the most part, the mobs are evenly spaced, so you should not get adds. Named hit a little harder than the regular trash mobs there. I usually camp the named sk room. There are various maps you can download that point out the camps.

Casters like to gate, so I suggest you fight near their spawn point. Be careful of roamer casters or you will have a train on your hands.

Do you have stoicism? If not, I suggest you work on the quest because it is a nice spell until you get Torpor at 60.

Also, you can get faction if you ever want to do the Cord quest in Chardok.
