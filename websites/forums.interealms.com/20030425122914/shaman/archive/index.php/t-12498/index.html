Phinger Bang
The best place to farm weapons for pet is in the bazaar. I don't want to sound sarcastic, but it is really convienent. You can get velium procing weapons reallly cheap, well under 100pp each. I just do a search for either 1hs, 1hp, 1hb from 0 to 100 pp. You can usually find plenty of neat little procs. As far as what procs are the best, stun procs of course are great against casters, snare procs against mobs that flee and damage proc (like velium) for dps.

Some examples are gnoll hide lariat and serrated bone dirk.
