I hestitate to post this here because I don't want this place to become crowded, but the Plane of Mischief is BY FAR the best xp in the game. The best place for xp and lewts is AIW(Alice in Wonderland). AIW is a series of 4 rooms that repeats itself 4 times, each time getting bigger(Along with getting bigger, the mobs get harder).

The first room contains a rat named Grink, the second room contains a Gorilla named Glonk, the third room contains 2 halflings named Brenn and Grenn, and the 4th room is empty. All of these NPC's are non-kos, but will assist each other. All mobs will run when low on health. Most mobs run at around 15-20% health, but AIW4 mobs will usually stick around till about 5-8%, enrage, then take off running. Running is BAD. Unless you know the area is clear, keep them rooted. Also, several times I've had mobs disappear on me when they start running. Walls and pathing in PoM are a little messed up, so it's best to keep them locked down.

After you go through the fourth room, you end up in a hallway the leads you to the next area of AIW with the exact same layout, just bigger rooms and harder mobs. The 4th area is where you want to be. AIW4 mobs hit up to 180 and can triple, the all summon and they all enrage. I haven't estimated HP's on em, but i'll do that now.

When i'm soloing it usually takes:
3 Epics-1200each (Roughly, don't know exact number)
4 Bane of Nifes-1648 each
3 Envenomed Bolts-1132 each
2 Poxx of Bertoxx-2088 each
1 Plague-1215 each
+1-2k in jbb damage

This calculates to about 20k hp per mob. The fights last roughly 5-6 minutes and you typically get 3-4% alt xp per kill(IE 3/4 to a full blue).

After you clear AIW4 you can move to stomples room which contains 3 archers, perma-rooted, won't assist. These guys aren't very tough at all compared to AIW4 mobs, but have similar hp's. These give 2-3% alt xp per kill.

All mobs in this area are on a 70 minute timer. You can pretty easily clear all of AIW4 and Stomples room, plus you might even be able to hit AIW3.

If you clear JUST AIW4 and stomples, that equals 5-6 blues per hour. Throw in part or all of AIW3 and you're looking at 7-8 blues per 70 minute cycle. If you've spent more than 10 minutes grinded in your life, you know that this kind of xp is practically unheard of, much less SOLO!

The vendors are called a jovial white knight and a maniacal black knight. They're vendors per se, but black knight only sells bone chips and the white one peridots. However, for the price they charge, unless you're really desperate, I wouldn't recommend it.

Inversely, they offer you ridiculously low prices for what you might wanna sell. But they serve another purpose and it's black/white squire turn-in. Hand in black squire to black knight, he'll give u some exp and a couple bone chips, and give white squire to white knight and he will give u some exp and a couple dots.

Exp you obtain turning in the cards isn't negligible either, maybe the equivalent of one rat kill, so if you're letting these white/black squires rot, no reason to, you're kinda wasting extra exp.

As for the exp in PoM, it is amazing. This coming from a person who's done UP and a whole panoply of other zones solo and/or partnered.

First of all, aparts from AiW4, there are 4 majorly overconned mobs which I managed to solo with extreme ease when I undinged 60 this one time: Donk, and the 3 archers, Doobis, Abbis, Dabbis. All around level 55 I think, don't hit harder than 110 double, no spells, just straight up melee and pretty weak at that. Only catch is relatively high hp, but extremly easy encounter.

As for AiW4, exp is simply incredible too. No casters, just hp and melee, and low resists at that.

/shrug You look at UP, there's some mobs I don't like pulling because of their resistances to dots ie assassins, netherbian ravagers, as well as fallen soldiers if I'm not mistaken, so then I'm stuck with having to JBB and epic nonstop (much prefer burning them down with dots). Dots work pretty well on the moktors I'll admit that, but quite a few mobs in UP where I don't have the luxury of dotting.

In PoM, it's almost always the same, straight up melee. Few exceptions like TC's, dancing sphinxes, Pinky, Bernice and her PH's, Lith (couple others maybe, but not much), but out of all those, TC is about the only mob I'd recommend soloing as a shaman, so yeah, PoM is pretty easy exp. Low resists on most mobs too.

Add the fact that you start out indif in PoM (except mobs like rooted wizzies and pansies), and mobs don't drop faction hit (except Debbis, Stomples, and Lith (his own faction)), PoM is very ideal for exp.

If you play it sensibly, with torpor, you incur practically no risk of dying in places like AiW4, gotta factor that in as well. UP is a tad more risky because of dark assassins that can roam around, sometimes see invis, backstab, and practically have line of sight aggro. So overall, I'd pick PoM over UP any day of the week.

Not to mention the armor you can quest in PoM, which makes it even more worth your while, and even though it may take months to finally achieve a piece of armor, you can nonetheless obtain a piece of armor (boots) which is superior to Vindi Boots, and I think that's pretty remarkable.

So that really only leaves me the choice between UP and PoM. I do both to tell you the truth, but I still prefer PoM, just do UP when I get bored of the former :) Not to mention PoM is generally not that crowded and trains don't come that easy (unless you're in forest and someone triggered pansies).

PoM all the way :D
