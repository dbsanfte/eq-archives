Youichi
Orecets,

"Mudflation" is the rise in player power because of better weapons/armour being added to a Mud, causing the original mobs to become much easier to kill than they were in the original game.

E.G. compare luclin weapons with say, an Axe of the Iron black.

And compare how hard/how many hit points mobs have in Luclin compared with the original mobs (Antonica/Fawder/Odus).

Therefore killing original game mobs, in this instance Seafuries, with Luclin era weapons/armour becomes much easier.
