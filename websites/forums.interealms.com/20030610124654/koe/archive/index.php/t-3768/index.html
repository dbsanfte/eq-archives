I am glad you are interested in Keepers of the Elements. We are a very open guild to people but this does not mean we allow anyone to join. Our recruiting process is long at best. Personally, it took me two months to be accepted into the guild even having some Very close friends as members.

Before you look into any guild in Everquest, I urge you to play the game more. KoE is a guild primarily of long term players, tho this does not mean we will not recruit those of low level it will hinder your hope of joining. I honestly cannot think of a member offhand in which their primary character is not at least level 50 or close. Finding members to group with to vouch for playing skills would be extremely difficult as the low end game isn't a good guideline. Finding members in general to group with to show who you are would not be easy either as most of us are high level. We do not have a level limit for recruitment, but in all honesty those of low level would probably not fit into this family.

Play the game, get to know people in all guilds before you decide which is right for you. KoE is a wonderful guild, but it's not for everyone...nor do I feel that you really know who we are just by visiting our website. Do not let this discourage you from KoE, just learn more about the guild before you make such a decision about us. Never know...might get to know ppl like Coatie and get scared off :eek: hehe jk :D
