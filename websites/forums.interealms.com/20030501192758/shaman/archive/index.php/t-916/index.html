Sengal
Like Snapdragn said: you really should wait until 29. At level 25 your alchemy skill is capped at 5. You won't be able to make anything worth selling reliably at this point. Once you get to 29, dump as many training points as you can into alchemy (21 is the max) then start on SOW potions.

It is VERY expensive. I just started alchemy the other day and I am barely breaking even so far. No "mad plat" yet :) I can see how it becomes easier to make money once you can reliably make SOW potions - there are buyers everywhere on my server.

I would say sit down with 500pp at level 29 and start then.

Dandi
You will not make money by doing alchemy. It can be a lot of fun, but only in the sense that doing anything else that's expensive and purely recreational can be fun. You can find more information at the EQ Traders Learn Alchemy Guide (http://www.eqtraders.com/learn/learn_alchemy.htm) although the "Getting Started" guide is not yet updated for Luclin.

If you are absolutely set on starting alchemy right now, I would say that you should start with the Dye Extracts (http://www.eqtraders.com/secrets/recipes_alchemy.htm#dyes) which are very cheap if you are hunting creatures that drop the components. But remember that you can train your skill up to 20 for free if you wait until level 29. And once your skill is at 20, even if you got it there by making potions, you cannot put training points into it. No tradeskill can be trained by your guild beyond 20.
snapdragn
Short description of my trials with potions:

waited until 29, trained at guild for first 21 pts, then started making sow potions. Have no idea how much money I spent, but it was a lot. Once SoW potions trivialed out at 37 (?) I thought I was good to go. spent maybe 1k or so.

But my success rate was only 60%...for every potion I sold at 100 pp I was making maybe 10 - 15 pp. And any time I sold to a guildie at 75pp I actually lost money. I decided to make more difficult potions...I made Cure Poison, Damage Shield, and a few Shrink Potions. Price per combine jumped from 5plat (Sow) to 7plat (Poision) and 11 plat (Shrink and Damage Shield). After spending about 2K more, my alchemy was 49. My success rate on Sow potions was barely over 70%, so I was making *20* plat per sell. Yippe :(

To add insult to injury, I couldn't sell ANY of the potions that I made...and I tried for a long time. In fact the cure poison is still in the bank.

Highly discouraged I stopped making potions, and gave up for a month. Once I got about 3K in the bank I decided that I would start throwing away (oops I mean 'investing') a hundred plat or so at a time on Sow Potions...

Ended up getting an order for 3 10dose Shrink Potions, and 2 10d Levitate potions... So I took the plunge, took out 1k plat and just plowed my way through the failures and made the 50 potions he asked for. At the end of the session my Alchemy was 70.

My alchemy is still at 70 (trivial for Shrink) and suddenly I can make Sow Potions with 95% success...now I can turn 105 plat into 200 with a bit of work. However, at my level (42) I am better off heading to TM and getting xp and loot.

If I ever get an order for a higer difficulty potion, I'll make them and hope for skill ups...but I'm not making any more potions just for the 'fun' of it.

Hope this helps.
