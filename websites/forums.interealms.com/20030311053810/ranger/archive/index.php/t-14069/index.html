Dar is the surname of all the Second Brood dragons. You will see it with the name (e.g. Kelorek'Dar, Jaled'Dar, etc) in all cases, except for Nagafen and Vox, who have had their Dar surnames stripped because of their little experiment to create another prismatic dragon a la Kerafyrm. And no, Kerafyrm is not the child of Nagafen and Vox; Kerafyrm's parents' names have been erased from history by Veeshan.
