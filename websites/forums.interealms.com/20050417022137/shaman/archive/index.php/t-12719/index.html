I did this off and on to get two bloods (which I used to get my tunic :) ) zone in and invis. open up the double doors take a right or left, it doesnt matter. there will be a ladder there. there should be two doors on the right and left side with two lizards in front of them. take you pick of which door you take, it doesnt matter. there will be a little leveling off point I set up camp here. Buff up completly summon a dog alpha him and you're gtg. /guard your pet and run up to the door. aggro the lizard and run back, let pet take aggro so you can mal*** and slow it then just smack up the lizard =P. once the first is done just run out and tag the second, nothing should aggro from below. Ive never gotten this bored but I suppose you could break the entrance mobs and then take those two to try to get as much blood as possible. torpor would probably make it more doable, I doubt I could keep all 4 dead lol.
