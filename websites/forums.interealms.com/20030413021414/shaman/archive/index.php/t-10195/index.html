Goldark
I did the Kirn part of my epic just the other week. We had two groups, average level 59. The enchanter was level 55.

We completely breezed through to the tower - so much so I thought everyone needed a challenge so we pulled the tower (61 monk pulling), and just killed everything. It wasn't very hard, but obviously the high average level helped a lot. The hardest part we had was just before the docks where we got about 6 to 8 mobs at once including a couple golems, but with an enchanter to mezz it wasn't so bad. If your 2 groups are more like 50 - 55 in level, then go the IVU route to the landing, and as mentioned above pull kindle's room to there, then kirn to kindle's room. Its worth noting that some ghosts are not KoS. Therefore its important that the puller gets the KoS mobs first, the non KoS ones will not add. If you want to kill them later you can, but there's no need. Kindle is also non KoS.

To get there, we took the waterfall route. 2 people died - one didn't listen and went to far out, missed the pool completely. The other one, strangely, died and his corpse was at the bottom of the pool - right against the wall. I can only imagine he actually hit the wall of the waterfall on the way down - he was a monk but safefall couldn't save him on that size of drop. No idea if Yael was up but if he was he didn't aggro. From what I hear, just keep left after the dropoff and you should be ok, but there are some horror stories about Yael coming out.

No idea about the tombkeeper.
