Setantha
As the originator of this thread I felt I should add a little more now that I have hit level 29 (nearly 30).

Firstly..forget Marus Seru unless you are grouped. From 25 on you can solo in MS, but the mobs have a lot of hps and invariably add. Experience isnt great unless you are grouped and have a decent puller.

Now...Echo Caverns is where you want to be !!! :)

When u zone into Echo Caverns, go left and thru the sliding doors. Just past the doors is a 4 spawn bogling camp..if you stay in the centre of the room you wont aggro them. Take the first right past these doors and the next right again. Here you will find the needlite cave. There are 6 static needlites with the occasional roamer and rarely the queen spawns (Last I saw her she was yellow to me at 27). They are for the main part single pulls (the two right in the centre of the room will come together). I found this a great camp and am still there at 29...I plan to stay til 30. The Needlites attack very fast, which is great for DEFENSE, but they usually only hit for 2 points. However, it makes sense to pull with Slow..then you can usually channel your dots thru. At 29, I now pull with Envenomed Breath and just melee them :) There are 6 Needlites, all on a 20 minute timer. 3 are on the left wall, 2 in the centre and 1 in a recess near the wormspore tentacle at the entrance. Camp is easily broken up into a kill every 3 or 4 minutes.

If you're feeling particularly industrious you can pull the bogling scouts at the sliding doors too though the Needlites should be plenty til u hit 29.

A word of caution...there are wormspore tentacles that will root and lifetap you in this zone. I keep Endure Magic up at all times and resist 33% of these. At 29 these are more of a nuisance than a danger. When they do manage to lifetap me they only take about 1/2 bubb hps and root only lasts a few seconds. To be honest at 29 this camp is just plain silly...almost too easy..but each kill still nets me over 1/2 blue bubb of exp.

For all you Shaman who have been neglecting your defensive skills...GO HERE !!!

Kinring
just a few quick things about Mseru mobs

all the mobs are social so they will add if you are fighting another of their kind.

Zelniaks and Lightcrawlers are the only mobs that wil aggro you for no particular reason, and are also the only ones that the guards will help out with if you train to the zone lines.

Greyhoppers and Stonegrabbers never aggro unless you are fighting one of their own and the guards will not help out on them, but if you train to zone they won't aggro other players near the zone after you zone out.

Recuso give positive faction in Sanctus Seru but one negative hit with Shadowhaven Smugglers. They start out non-aggro but the more you kill you eventually become KOS to them.

I hunt here from mid 20's -low 30's grouped, never soloed here because the mobs have a lot of HP and just suck out your mana, too much downtime for the xp you get and the loot is decidedly subpar.

Great for fast levelling in a group though.
