Naagruz
Stamina and resists are the only real weak spots I see. I would prolly ditch one of the glittering rings, the shoulders, the shield, the neck, Goranga, and maybe the gaunts. Possible replacements could be a Shadel ring, Barbed Dragonscale pauldrons, SBS, Sabertooth Amulet, Bathezid idol, and Coldain Skin gloves.

Best way to upgrade IMO is to slap stuff in your Magelo and find out what suits you best. Having a L62 cleric always by your side dramatically effects the type of gear you will need and you may never fight anything that has a manapool. Things like resists are useless to some players simply because of playstyle.

Just look at where and what you fight to get a bearing on upgrades. :)
