Perelandra
While as a Mage I appreciate the sentiment expressed here, there is quite a bit of info here that isn't accurate.

Mage DPS:
We rank below Wizard, rogue, and (rangers - I think). It is true that on very long fights our sustained DPS is great - but we haven't really seen those since Luclin. With the addition of Protection of Calliav, pets now survive most raid fights. However pet dps goes down significantly as the level of the mob increases.

Mage Nukes:
Mages continue to be the second in all out nukes. While it is true that Druids have nukes equal to most of ours, we have a couple of nukes which they can't touch: http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=5474&source=Live. If you look at PoP spells - you'll find the exact same situation between Druids and Mages that you are seeing in OOW.

In summary, as a primary damage dealer mages are a little below where we should be right now. However, we usually don't look at Druids when comparing to another class.
