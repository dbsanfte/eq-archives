tangurena
About once a year, one patch or another breaks the deity check on imbue spells. The deity check is done only when you scribe the spell, not when you cast it. So, my barbarian shaman has imbue ivory, imbue jade and imbue amber spells scribed.

Imbue sapphire is a cleric spell.

So, what you can do, if you are a tradeskiller, is to obtain the spells you cannot scribe due to deity, and after each patch, sit down and try to scribe it. One of these patches will break the deity check again. At that time, all druids should run to get the imbue spell that is from the other religion, and occasionally, clerics will get imbue spells (there are a lot of those). If you notice the check being broken, and you have some clerics in the guild, get the imbue gems for the rarer faiths to make tradeskilling easier (bertox, quell, prex, rodcet).
