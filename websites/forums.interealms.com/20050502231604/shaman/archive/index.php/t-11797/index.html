Hogcaller's Inn

This is an excellent way to build up faction with several groups. Find Pandos Flintside, a dwarf in the Hogcaller's Inn. Give him a muffin, and you'll get faction and a little experience. You can buy muffins from Pincia Brownloe, near the Theater of Tranquility. Raises faction with FaydarksChampions; King Tearisthex; ClericsofTunare and SoldiersofTunare. Lowers faction with Crushbone Orcs. Very useful faction adjustments for a 6 cp muffin. You can give him up to 4 groups of 4 muffins at once. He also says some things: "What no milk?", but giving him milk doesn't seem to make a difference. He will say "Where did you get these muffins?"

If you tell him "I got the muffins from the Brownloe bakery" he'll go there! As soon as he leaves, this ward comes out and will talk to you. This dovetails into the Hogcaller Inn Quest.
