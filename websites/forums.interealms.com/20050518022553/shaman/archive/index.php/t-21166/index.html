Ok, no clue who this poster is, but the kid came up with 2 very nice ideas that I haven't seen anyone here toss out yet. Post count is low enough he could be new to using web boards, or he might be a quiet troll type, who knows.

Spell Ideas from EQ Live post (http://eqforums.station.sony.com/eq/board/message?board.id=shamanbalance&message.id=608)

The basic summary of what all he brought up so far (fair chance he thinks of some more later, or others follow suit), is a reverse Malos and a /disc haste.

The reverse malos idea is that we get a buff which adds a resist modifier to all spell cast by that player. If the modifier was set at the same level as malos that would mean that you can have an approach to malo which matches the idea of dogdog = DoT. It lasts from mob to mob! This would mean that we can save a LOT of time on casting Malos and get straight to casting slows. It would also mean that our spells WOULD have resist modifiers on them (and our Nihil/endgame roots would have LARGER mods)... as long as we remember to buff ourselves.

The other notable idea was a spell that lowers the re-use timer on melee disciplines. My take on this is that since we suck at healing HEALTH, why not let us heal END and also toss in a haste spell that makes END running out an actual concern. This would not only give us a new role, but would also return us to the fabled ULTIMATE MELEE BUFFER status. It'd be the VoQ/KEI of the melee world.
