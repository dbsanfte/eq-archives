Shabby
Hey again!

I can't read those profile links, but you say your defense is at 130 at level 51?? That is probably your biggest weak area. That is EXTREMELY bad defense skill for you level.

I'm not good with numbers, but I think it is a fair guess to say that those missing 70 defense skill points are costing you well over 100 raw AC (and I think that is a low esitimate).

I repeat my original suggestion, go to Ocean of Tears and melee the Seafury Clops. I spent quite a bit of time lv. 48-52 on those clops, and found I got more defense skill raises against those fellows than any other mobs in that level range. Many other shaman have commented on this board that they also used the clops to raise defense skill.

The seafury clops, once slowed, are pretty much wimps. Their max hit is around 70-75. Your doggie will chew them up as you melee. I recommend simply pulling with slow, sic'ing doggie on them, laying on a DoT (one of your lv. 49 ones), and then melee'ing them to death.

Save the seafoam that drops from them. It is used in cultural tailoring (human) and a guildie might appreciate it. Also, the clops drop some pretty nice gems/jewelry that sell for 50-100 pp each.

If you are determined to chose a Horsey or Ikky BP now, well your play style should dictate which you choose. If you spend most of your time in outdoor zones, without a doubt you should get the horse. But if you spend the majority of your time inside, the Ikky BP will help you more.

Good Journeys to you! And good luck!
