Hi There Ouga,

I did this recently and being an iksar was also KOS to most of Qeynos. The easiest route is to use the Qeynos catacombs underneath the city which eventually brings you out in the port area. A map is essential as its a maze down there! Once you zone in Qeynos you need to swim to the pier and wait invised until the boat arrives. I find that the boat actually arrives and waits at the pier before the one you zone near too, so you may need to swim round to it.

As an alternative I suppose you could just run through Qeynos invis to the port area and just hope that invis doesn't drop!! hehe

The killer sharks are a worry I had 5 swim past me whilst I was waiting. Luckily I took along some guildmates to deal with them. The sharks are dark blue to level 50 :D. Also it was black as pitch down there so I hope you underwater swimming is good!

Good Luck!

I only needed one, count 'em, one fish scale. Batwings, on the other hand, I needed about a dozen. Go to the ogre, hand her the gem, Lev, SoW, and run to -1600, +4200...above the water. Stand around, refreshing lev when necessary. You will 'hear' the ghosts arrive, and you will hear them leave. When you hear Abe leave, turn on EB and Invis vs. Animals, and jump in the water. Be it that I just started EQ after Asheron's Call and UO, I don't have uber friends nor loot. All I read is how hard the Epic is to do solo. Well at 39 I started and by 40 I had the Black Fur Boots and stopped because I was happy. Here is what I've done for Test of Patience.......

Make hotkeys of ALL responses you must spurt out.


1 Port to Tox (Either Odus Scion in nexus or player port)
2 Run through Erudin (invis if KoS) to the dox area
3 Take the boat to Qeynos (Broke atm but teleporter lady will send you to Erud'd Crossing on the Island.
4 Hand Ogre Lady the Gem from Freeport
5 SoW, Lev, Invis yourself.
6. Run out to the loc from every guide out there.
7. Put your feet in the water....(this is the key because you can HEAR everything that goes on with NO risk of shark attack)
8. (REINVIS)Float there for about 30 minutes until Abe the Abandoned spawns and says something. (Debate on his usefulness to quest) and get the broken arrow from him.
9. Return to the same spot on surface until you see something like this " a greater spirit begins to cast a spell " You'll actually hear the noise of it if done right
10 (REINVIS) Swim down and converse(prolly will have to uninvis due to new changes to invis rules
11 GATE out quick



Whole time I saw total of 4-5 sharks. But you need not be afraid of this test if you have the above spells. No uber friends needed.

The black dire on the other hand may take you a group if you lack the uberness to take on the 5 mobs.

My 2cp. :p
