I think the guide or GM had said it clear, that "proximity aggro" is over rule by "melee agro". That means, who ever first damaged the mob, or cast a spell directly on the mob has the right to finish off the mob, unless they wiped, then others can move in.

In your case, the bard clearly stated that he only aggro the room, but he didn't shoot at the statue directly to land the first direct attack. When the statue is pulled pass the other raid force, their melee/caster landed a direct melee attack on Statue and gian agro. Since they landed the FIRST DIRECT ATTACK on statue, they become the rightful raid force to claim statue.

That'd been VT's policy for quiet a while. I know it isn't fair, and a lot of raid force isn't very happy about it. But wasting your breath argue with the GM won't get you anywhere. :(
