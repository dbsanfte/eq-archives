Healing - emergency heal
This is the first poll to this thread (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=21999). As I am limited to 10 options I will post this as three polls, please vote on all of them. I will edit this post and add the links to the other polls.

Shaman need help in the following areas:
* an upgrade to Daluda's. It is simply too small.
* some sort of emergency heal to recover from a resisted slow.
* some sort of group or AE heal for raid usage.

That said these seem to be the options we could ask for. When voting on them please keep in mind that simply asking for the biggest numbers in each category does not solve anything. Ask for what you think shaman need to be competitive and no more.

Daluda's
a) improve amount healed to 1800, improve OoW heal to 2100
b) improve amount healed to 2000, improve OoW heal to 2500

emergency heal[/B]
a) targetable stonestance
c) single-target divine arbitration
e) spirit shifting - drains HPs from shaman and gives them to the target

[U]AE/group heal
a) group HoT, Torpor style
b) group HoT, Quiescence style
c) direct group heal
d) AE heal, heals 4 injured players in AE range to target
Obviously the HoTs would be MGBable which SOE does not seem to like. The AE may be hard to keep your group alive with but being able to cast direct heals on a cleric group or tank duo may have it's own advantages on a raid.

I am wondering how many people actually know the difference between the single-target Divine Arb and Spirit Shifting...the difference is actually very subtle imo.

I'll try to explain it, I guess.

The Spirit Shifting AA originally suggested by veeshanMurtak has a 1 minute reuse time, takes 20/30/40%(3 AA ranks) HPs from the Shaman and heals the target for the exact amount. (A 10k HP Shaman would take 4k damage and heal his target for 4 k). The suggested cast time was 0.5 seconds.

The single-target Divine Arbitration I suggested was never really specified, so I won't do this now(since this isn't my poll :p ). However, Divine Arbitration(cleric version) works like this:

When triggered, the ability balances out the HPs among *all* group members(in the case of the single-target Shaman version this would only affect the target and the Shaman using the ability). To facilitate this we assume a Cleric(10k HPs) is only grouped with a Warrior(15k HPs). The tank gets down to 50% health(7.5k), the Cleric triggers Divine Arb, which does 1250 Damage to the Cleric and heals the Warrior for 1250((10k(Cleric) + 7.5k(Warrior))/2 = 8750). Doing the same when the Warrior has 1k HPs left, would bring both to 5.5k resulting in a 4.5 k heal/loss.

Just thought I'd clarify, don't mind me!
