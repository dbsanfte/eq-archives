Seanus
I wrote (then deleted) a rather lengthy post on the dynamics of the bazaar economy, but to sum up I've noticed a change in the way things are swinging there, and what seems to be evolving.

When first put in place, there were many folks with banks full of items who couldn't be bothered by the EC fiasco, but took advantage of this neutral medium of sales to dump there wares. At first sales were brisk, but as folks finished twinking out and rounding out there characters, money supplies dwindled, and the bazaar probably slowed down in sales.

Recently I've noticed a drop off in quality of wares offered in the bazaar on my server. Yes, there are a few representatives from the uber guilds selling their guild cast-offs, but for the most part the mid-upper line items have disappeared from the market.

Another thing I've noticed is that the lower line items are starting to sell again. I've had my old totemics for sale there a few times over the past months (all quested myself except cape and helm), and last night without a price change I rapidly sold the gloves to one person, the bracers to another, and the legs to a third. Giant heads went within 30 minutes as well to yet another buyer.

These things have been on sale at different times over the past month. I don't frequently put up my bazaar mule (formerly my main, but paladins are ... rather flat to roleplay), but I found last nights shift noteworthy.

My guess is the bazaar was a flooded economy that is now beginning to dry out/recover a bit.

Share and enjoy...
