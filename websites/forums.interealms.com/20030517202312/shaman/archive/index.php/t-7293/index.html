Patito
If you go to the spot where everything spawns, cast lev on yourself and swim straight up to the surface of the water. There, look straight down and position yourself, in first person view, so your view is just slightly under the surface of the water. If you do this correctly, you'll be able to see everything under the water clearly, without the underwater visual effects. You may be bobbing in and out of that, but you can still see what is spawning at the ocean floor and if there are any sharks nearby. That is, if you have clipping plane set high enough. The sharks won't aggro you at the surface, so you'll be safe.
