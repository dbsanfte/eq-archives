If I remember correctly, the right most figure on the "lag-o-meter" is the amount of data being sent to you by the EQ server.

This seems to fluctuate from around 500 (bytes per second I assume) in a quiet zone, to 2000 in a raid type situation, and possibly as high as 6000 when you loot your corpse.

Assuming an average figure is 1000 bytes a second, that's 60k a minute, or 3.6 meg per hour, or 86.4 meg per day.

Obviously these figures are a total guess, but I reckon they're in the right order of magnitude.
