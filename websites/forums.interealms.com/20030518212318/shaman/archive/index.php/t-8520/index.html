Just looked at your equipment. I highly recommend you get better AC and a really good weapon if you plan on PLing up through the levels. With KEI on, you don't need uber HP at our level but you do need as good an AC as you can get for "in the face" action. If you can get it, pick up a Granite Face Grinder for around 800-1000pp or a Hammer of Rage for 300-500pp. Don't worry about maxing out your wisdom until much later (and arguably never). At 43, my AC is about 810 buffed. That's with the same spells as you and only one level advantage in Defense (maxes out at 40).
