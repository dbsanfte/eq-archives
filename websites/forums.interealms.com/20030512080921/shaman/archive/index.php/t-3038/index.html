Gloriana
Here's the quick and simple version I used. Allakhazam's had a pretty good write up as well:

There are 4 things:

1. TRAKANON'S TOOTH --- Dropped by Trakanon who usually averages about 3 drops per death

2. MEDALLION OF KYLONG --- Give all 3 portions to Professor Akabao in the Lake of Ill Omen
(Top portion)In Chardok there is a mob called Niblek, give him a black sapphire and a ruby and he gives you the top portion
(Middle portion) Dropped from Verix Kylox in Karnor's Castle (He's located in the basement area-follow the moat to the right hand side and go into the underwater tunnel, he spawns around that area)
(Bottom portion) Found in the Library of Kaesora (its just lying in the corner of the 2nd floor inside)

3. MEDALLION OF JARSATH --- Give all 3 portions to Xiblin Fizzlefutt in the Timorous Deep (His loc is -5800,2950)
(Top portion) Its just lying around in the Swamp of No Hope at loc 52, 2935, -6
(Middle portion) Dropped by an Iksar monk in Firiona Vie named "an ancient Jarsath" (wanders near the Drolvarg camp-check tracking around the loc 270,-1900)
(Bottom portion) Dropped by the Bloodgill Marauder who guards the entrance to Veskar at the bottom of the Lake of Ill Omen (His loc is -380,-50,-190)

4. MEDALLION OF OBULUS --- Give all 3 portions to Slixin Klex in the Burning Woods located at -681, -1120, -560
(Top portion) Quest mob Sselot Dnaas who spawns in an island in Warsilks Woods at loc 3900, 300(Give him a burnished wooden staff from chardok, have an enchie charm and do the trade)
(Middle portion) Dropped by a rotting skeleton at loc 2100, -5100 in the Dreadlands
(Bottom portion) Dropped by the Pained Soul in Trakanon just outside the Old Sebilis
Portal
The Pained Soul (and its placeholders) spawn at Loc: -1857, -4373.

To get there:

Facing out of the Seb tunnel, take a right and go through door.
Take a left and move up a bit. Then turn to your right and the pained soul/spec keeper/spec champion should be in front of you.


Take all 3 "completed" medallions and the tooth and return them to Emperor Ganak in Trakanon's Teeth to get the Key to Veeshan's Peak
