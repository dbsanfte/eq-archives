I've heard rumors you can get your epic earlier. I have never seen it done. I'm not even sure there is a level requirement on the scale turn in. Has anyone ever had this happen to them? I've heard rumors of level 30's walking around with their epic, it just won't click for you till 51.

Rumors, though, I've never seen one.

If you MQ your tear, make absolutely sure you have MAX MAX MAX ally. As in, your faction with truespirit cannot get any better. If you don't see that, don't turn in the scale.

I also used the guide from eqshaman. There aren't any pictures, but pictures aren't needed. "Spice", well, it's a walkthrough. Not a magazine. Locs are given for those mobs who aren't obvious - such as the wandering spirit in EK (who is on a set path), Ooga in Erud's Crossing, and the guys who give Woe & Envy & Marr's Promise. Rak and Neh are pretty much known spawns, as are Ghiosk and Kirn. If you and your guild don't know where they are, you shouldn't be heading down to them :)

You can skip the Hole & Neh, but it means more faction work. Just a heads up. Rak will still have to be done, which means you will do Ghiosk anyway, as he's the first reaver pop.

I'd personally wait a few levels and do the whole quest as it is intended, as if you loose anything, a GM will not be sympathetic to you.
