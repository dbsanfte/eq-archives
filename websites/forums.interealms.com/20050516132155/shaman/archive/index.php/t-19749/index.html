Worlaan
...about the strength of the shaman class.

Tonight, I was in a group caming Upper Sand wing of Bastion of Thunder. The group, when I got there, consisted of myself (65 sham), 65 cleric, 65 warrior, 65 monk, 65 mage, and 65 beastlord. Soon after I got there, the monk was replaced by a Ranger (65). About an hour into the group, the BL decides its time to log, and we cant find a replacement. No biggie, we're fine on DPS anyway, so we keep on. About half an hour later, Cleric says she needs to go, and tells us that she has been trying for 1/2 hour to find a replacement and couldnt get one. No biggie, we're in a safe spot so we sit there for a min trying to decide what to do.

At this time, the group is now me, warrior, mage, and ranger. I get a wild hair up my butt and say, 'screw it, im FM, pull one.'

Ranger pulls, I toss a Q on tank, mage 'sinia's, I slow, and toss a Tnarg's on tank toward end of fight. He ends at full health, and im only down 10% mana or so. So we continue to pull, looking for a healer and a replacement DPS for a while but never finding one. Eventually mage has to go so he gets a replacement mage. By the time we finally find a cleric, an hour had gone by. That's right, an hour of chain pulling, sometimes 2 mobs and occassionally 3 if I had the mana, with no breaks in between with only 4 group members.

So we get the cleric, rebuff, and continue pulling. After about an hour, cleric goes LD. Fine, np, we start pulling again. After 2nd pull, my KEI drops. No biggie, we'll just have to slow down pulls a bit, I say. So we continue pulling for about another half hour, pretty much at the same rate as before, no breaks in between, chain pulling, sometimes 2. Eventually we get an enchie and cleric to fill last two spots.

All in all, I had 4 AA dings in roughly 5 hours in that group. And a good chunk of the time, I was slowing, debuffing, healing, and nuking. Hell, we even took out Rorek Steelthorn with just the 4 of us.

Never again, after tonight, will I ever say the shaman class is lacking in any way. Just thought I would share that with the rest of the community.
