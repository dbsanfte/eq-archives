..I am finally flagged for Plane of Time, so a shameless 'woot me' post was in order ;)

Anyway, I joined my guild back in November 2002. We are part of a three guild alliance, known collectively as WAP (watchkeepers, aurora polaris, phoenix alliance)

PoP had just been released and they were about a month out of Halls of Testing, doing luclin minis (vadynn/DS) and tier 1 pop stuff. We slowly worked our way into kael/ntov (first aary kill late nov02), while working PoJ trials and wiping/working on lower end pop stuff like factory/xanamech/pos keys. I'm still wearing my trusty Vindi Bp which was my first loot ;)

I remember grummus and behemoth being teeth-breakingly hard, and how satisfying it was to see them fall ;)

We worked first broods for ST keys, ST for primals, we banged our heads against the wall until we could finally farm EB weaps from kael/ssra (farmed ssra till we all got REALLy tired of snakes heh), to help us break into tier3 pop. (we skipped emp/VT)

We did all HoH trials when they were damn hard to us, RD made us tear out hair on many occasions. Also, getting people aa became a priority, so we had a 3 week raid break when Ldon launched and everyone adventured, bought Ldon gear/augs and got aa.

Summer 2003 saw us get into sol ro tower, this was a nice step up, farming the minis for solid loots and a lot of ornate dropped. Again we had a gearing and strengthening period as we worked on RZ etc.

RZ died in october 2003, solro in november 2003 which saw us fully EP flagged.

The first few months in EP were hard, but lots of fun as we worked air and earth rings, learning new strats. Then you reach a point in EP's where everything clicks (was probably 4-5 months ago for us), the balance of skills/tactics/gear/aa etc and you begin to mow through the content.

There is a great feeling of satisfaction of hammering through a full fire spawn, or the night we completely cleared PoEa (stone, mud, vine, croc, dust, arb) in about 4 hours ;) You know you are ready to move on when you start doing Dust ring in Air for a laugh. (spiders anyone? ahahahh)

The last few months have been working EP Gods, farming EP content along with some GoD progression. Think we got Xeg in may/june, fennin shortly after, rathe dropped in july, and finally coirnav died last night (we had over a minute spare at the end ).

Anyway enough rambling from me, just wanted to share some of my journey with you all ;)
