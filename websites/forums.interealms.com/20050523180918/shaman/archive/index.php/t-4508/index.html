Roxxanne is correct. You need to have your agi buffed, and your defense will rise faster(not dex). Get it as high as possible--maybe buy a couple of rings. Find a light blue mob, take it to a zone line, and let it beat you down to 2 bubbles--then zone, heal up, and come back. You can practice channelling and heal yourself in battle if you like, but you can get dead that way by accident. Better safe than sorry! :)
