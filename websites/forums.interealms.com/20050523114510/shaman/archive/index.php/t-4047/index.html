Anomalos
Not sure what your money to spend is, but i'll list a few cheap upgrades.

Ears - Imbued Golden Ivory (cheap, and more ac, and 4 wis to boot)
Neck - Ivory Imbued Collar
Arms - Froglok Scale Chestplate

Those off the top of my head, and you'd get more AC and Wis out of the deal. Your HP is great for your level. Should be able to get the above for under 3k, maybe 2k.

BTW, are you insane? Why would you bag your Coldain Skin Gloves for JB Gloves? You're losing 9STA/6WIS/6INT/20HP/20MANA, for 2AC more on the JB Gloves. Switch those back. Look at the profiles of avg geared non-uber 60 shamans. They mostly have coldain skin gloves.

Just thought of a few more upgrades.

Back - Arctic Wyvern Cloak - it blows away your current cloak, and it's not very expensive.
Head - Arctic Wyvern Helm or Iksar Hide Cap. You lose a few AC but you gain alot in return.
Legs - Kromrif Military Leggings - more ac, and you get some WIS out of it.

That's all I can think of right now, and none of these are expensive. You can do these all for under 5k, and you'd upgrade your AC, and your mana pool significantly. You're not very high level yet, so your mana pool doesn't need to be that big, but getting WIS to like 160'ish while maintaining roughly your current hp/ac would be ideal. GL.
