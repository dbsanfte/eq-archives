Jinheim
On paper, it might look better to stop at 61 and do AA for a long time, then get to 65 later. In reality, though, 65 is much better. Those 4 levels really do make a big difference in your overall effectiveness. In addition to this, people are more reluctant to group with you if you are only 61 or 62. You are not really carrying your weight. 65 spells are all over the market now, and for reasonable (I laugh at the people that said "I will never have my 65 spells because I am not in a big guild" a few months ago) prices. You want those spells. I had canni5 and MGB before pop came out, but not even canni5 would be worth stopping for. Canni5 is 23 AA, assuming you start with 0. That is about like going from 61 to 64, I believe. You ultimate goal should be: "level 65 AND canni5" and you will reach that goal faster if you do the levels first.

Also, I would like to add: I have NEVER met someone who stopped at 61 to do AA, and has anywhere near as many AA as me. Most of them have in to 20's or 30's. If you are level 61 and have over 102 AA (that you mostly earned since pop came out), please tell me.
