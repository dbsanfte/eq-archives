Hmmm,

I've never hunted here, but I can offer some advice from the times I've done research for the Earring of Veracity quest.

Pay attention to how the guards look and observe them for a bit. The casters will actually cast spells on a fairly regular basis. As long as you stick to the guards at the zone-ins, however, that shouldn't be a problem. If you decide to go further in, like to the four major buildings around the Seru building, you'll *want* to be prepared for casters. There are enchanters and pallys at the very least inside those buildings, and I've heard of groups getting sputched by g-fluxing 'chanters. On some of the upper floors there are "boss mobs" to watch out for, with a bad tendency to port groupmates to a jail area in the city, well away from the fight.

The zone in from Marus Seru should actually be a better place to pull and hunt, as the long hallway there prevents major adds. Just be aware of your faction; the guards in MS at Neth Lair are on the Seru faction. Kill thought leeches and recuso bandits to maintain faction if you really feel the need.

Just my $0.02. :)
