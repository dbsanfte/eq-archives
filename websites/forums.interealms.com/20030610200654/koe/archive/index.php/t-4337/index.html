Cross-post from Members Forum:

Well last night I logged Blaydde on and started the journey to Luclin. Kindo was kind enough to port me to DL and, as I waited at the DL portal for the Nexus to open the portal, I was chatting in guildsay. In the midst of this chatter, Wulfran auctioned in guildsay "WTT Kedge Robe to a dorf" or something like that. As many of you know, Blaydde was a Kedge Robe short of his epic. So, assuming Wulf was teasing, I offered in guildsay to trade Kilrain to him, knowing of Wulf's attachment to dwarves. Wulfran responded that I wasn't offering enough (*ducks and waits for Kilrain to start throwing bottles of ale*). So I offered to throw in Drolgin. Wulf promptly accepted. Next thing I know Wulf tells me that he is serious - that he has a Kedge Robe to multi with me and that it is mine since it is the last piece that I need. I was STUNNED.

So for the next couple of hours a group of me, Wulf, Lady V, Doku, Meiamour, and Serlina travlled the countryside finishing the rogue epic, and Blaydde now proudly wields Ragebringer.

I want to thank all of you for all of your help and encouragement along the way. I owe particular thanks to Rizz (who insisted that Blaydde loot his General when Blaydde screwed up his turn-in), Wulfran (who supplied the elusive Kedge Robe), and Aseid/Dexail, who deferred on the Kedge Robe so that Blaydde could finish RB first, being higher-level. The generosity displayed by members of this guild is extraordinary.

Speaking of generosity - umm, Killer, umm - yer now Wulf's slave. You too Drollie. 8P
