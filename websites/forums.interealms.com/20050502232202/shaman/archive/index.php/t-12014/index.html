1. JBB
You can use a JBB anywhere. At level 45 it helps immensely. It stays very usable up to level 60 and beyond depending on your spells you have acquired. And once you outgrow it, you can still sell it for most of the cash you payed. Going rate on them has been about 20k for ages and ages (on Brell)
2. Horse
Get this next. It adds HUGE mana regen, assuming you hunt outdoors. If you have a JBB, you can med while you shoot stuff with the bracer. You can also be a better healer since you keep medding while you care constantly casting. You don't miss a med tick doing the cannidance. Also, you don't get sitting aggro when memming different spells.
3. Earring of the Solstice (already have robe for it)
This is an ok earring. You can get them for pretty cheap nowadays. If you spend much more than 8-10k you are paying too much for the whole earring.

don't forget:
4. Fungal tunic.
Permanent 15hp regen owns. Nuff said.

Turley
As far as Nurga is concerned, when I was level 39 I was able to solo Nurga easily. I had a a fungi and always had KEI, but it was real easy. Invis and go up the waterfall. Run to where the sleeping ogre is. From there you have 4 pairs of mobs to pull. Root one and kill the other however you usually do it then get the other. If root breaks just reroot and keep them seperated. If they start to run let them, mobs are really seperated and their fear paths dont bring them near each other. BTW, I was able to do Nurga at 30 with a lvl 30 monk who had fungi and Bo stick so you should have no problem. Its intimidating at first, but the best solo xp for us shammies at those levels are dungeons that no one goes to anymore. You may screw up a few times trying to figure it out, but its definatly worth it when you do. I remember I did Sol A, Nurga, Labor camps in TM, Jaggedpine Forest, Kaesora, Scarlet Desert, etc. at those level all solo. When you figure it out, 3% a kill is normal and the levels fly. At any rate, your group should do well. Nurga has like a 14% ZEM and nice loot. I definatly suggest it. Dont get discoraged if you die... It gets better everytime you go back.

Hope that helps.

The second ring is 5ac 5sta 2wis/int/cha 85hp 45manna ....20 more hp, 1 less ac,45 more manna, 5 more stamina and +2wis/int/cha than the 6/65 rings...plus a 25point damage shield thats insta click and lasts 25 minutes to really piss off that mob that thought he wanted to hit me till i stood up and clicked it.
