Lyzzt
My advice is to stick it out. With your regen buffs, stacking dots, and slows, combined with a good dps pet, snare, more dots, etc you can be a truly fearsome duo. You two will have root rotting options, shaman tanking options, pet tanking options, or fear kiting with the spell lineup you have.

Stick it out until you get cana, a decent slow, and regen (somewhere around 29 +/- 5 levels). Your dps and mana regen which translate to exp rate should be quite sick.

As for cash, collect everything and sell it. After you've cached up quite a few bonechips for the necro, you can still sell them for pp to PCs occasionally. Also, don't go for a full spellbook right away. Prioritize what you buy. If you never get that uber level 9 charisma buff, so be it.

When I was a lowbie in FoB, I targetted skeletons carrying weapons. They could sell for several gp each which will get you to your level 9 spells fairly decently.

Gormadoc
When I was coming up we didn't have the bazaar and people were actually scared of kunark so twinking was out of the question.. so I, like Lyzzt targetted mobs wielding weapons that I could sell :) I also did the curscale quest a ton of times and sold the armor to merchants and other players.

To sell in the bazaar you just have to buy enough trading satchels to hold your items. The merchant that sells these satchels is in the very big building right as soon as you zone into the bazaar.. I don't recall his named but he's like the first merchant you see as you enter the building. The bags weigh 10lbs so definately not something you want to keep on your character.. but are nice to use to store items in the bank. Once you or your dad has placed the items in the satchel.. simply go to the 'trader area', type trader, set a price for the items.. add.. and begin trading.. =) Also, better to go far away from the banks for your selling since not as many people are there.
