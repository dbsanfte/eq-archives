Where can I get ingridients to make stingin wort potions?
Shandy Orcbane
Herb component is sold by gypsies in Rathe Mountains and the like (and a vendor in Shadowhaven in the Fordel Quarter), and the Ice giant toes are on, well, Ice Giants. They also drop off Gornit, on the Sea Furby island.
Torgo
At lvl 40 you won't be able to solo IG's but if you just ask the IG campers most will give you / let you loot the toes. They take up a whole slot and only sell for a few gp's so most IG campers (at least the ones who don't know about stinging wort, and thats most of em) let them rot.
You can also occasionally find them on the venders in everfrost and halas, they cost about 20plat a piece though so that ups the cost per dose significantly.
