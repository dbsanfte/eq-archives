Well, I blew a morning of good shamaning and ran Goner (our lev 61 pet) through his paces.

3 hours in PoI, 3 hours in Chardok, and I'm tired.

First 90 mins in PoI Goner had Focus, celerity, replenishment and a pair of velium weaps. I fought my way to one of the little rooms and stayed there fighting a mix of critters, all dblu at 61. After 90 mins, I gated out, and did the exact same thing but added Shroud of the Spirits. Goner actually took more average damage during the 2nd 90 minutes, fighting the same mix of critters. Max hits were compared by critter type and were identical. Average damage was 2% higher.

HOWEVER, Chardok was a different story.

Parked myself in a nice undisclosed location, summoned Goner, and did the same thing. 90 mins without AC, next 90 mins with. This time he took roughly 4% less damage the second go around. Max hit by critter type was again identical.

What does this mean? It's all within the realm of "normal effect of 29 AC," and within "This AC buff has no effect."

So, it was inconclusive here.
