PoJ is also really good if you can avoid trains.
Enforcers and Sentries of Justice give around 2% AAexp each and the enforcers drop parchments and BD's and well as some nice VT loot.
Guardians of Justice are tankable at 60 with torpor and probably root/rotable if you dont have torpor. I have only found one spawn of the Guardian so far in my short stay but they give around 6% AAexp per kill. Guardians hit for 350 max but their damage varies wildly. Im guessing they are around lvl 56-58.
The High Gaurdians of Justice are level 60 and hit for around 450 but their MR sucks and can be slowed rooted and DoTed easily without malo (malosini helps root duration so use that). I just cleared the area of any gaolers of justice and pulled the single one right before the enforcer room. They have a LOT of hp so torpor is required if only for the mana regen. Just keep 3 or 4 DoTs up on him and keep him rooted. Watch out for gaolers as they will assist. I do not know how much exp they give since each time I have tried them I hae either been trained or had a gaoler add. Im guessing it should be in the range of 8 to 10% solo.
