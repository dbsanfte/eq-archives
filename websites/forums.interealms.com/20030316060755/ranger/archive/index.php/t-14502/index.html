Get a cleric and trio the entrance mobs to the hole. Once you guys have that under control, add a druid, ench, and rogue and go crawl. Best damned xp in the game at that level. Your entire group will be 55 in a few weeks if you manage to stick together and play 3-4 hours a night together.

*If* you get sick of the hole ( I never did ), my 2nd favorite dungeon is HS - Start with North wing and start crawling. some of the best fun in the game. Chardok is also a great dungeon to crawl.


Most important thing I can pass on to you is to find 4 other people that play the same hours you do, and form a normal nightly group.

However if you strictly want to Duo , Hole Entrance mobs, HS Entrance mobs (you have a gate potion right?). Chardok zone-in/out mobs, as you get closer to 50 Akheva can be a blast also.

I never really soloe'd so can't help you much there.
