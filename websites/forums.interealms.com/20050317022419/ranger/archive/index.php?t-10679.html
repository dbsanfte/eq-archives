Maybe you can make this into a guide sometime, just add your tips here?

FORAGE SKILL - you don't have to stop the attack mode to use this skill ! Sure you won;t forage anything, but the skill check will work, and you will be able to raise the skill without bothering to press attack off, forage, attack on.

Not only it makes raising the skill easier since you are often times in combat, it is also a good trick when you want to work the skill but dont want to bother dropping or storing the foraged item.. just press attack or sit, forage then attack off or sit off, quickly.

CATCHING UP SOWED NPC's, BEFORE YOU HAVE SOW -- This tip is just for when your prey runs off at the end of a fight with a sow. Just run after your prey with attack off and fire arrows ! Eventually your prey's health will be low enough that it will slow down and you will be able to catch it up and finish it :)

RAISING SENSE HEADING -- put it say in the top left hotbutton. Go to options and add the Forward Cursor Key as a secondary key for the hotbutton on which you put Sense Heading. Now everytime you press the run forward key you will de a Sense Heading Skill Check. You can forget about it and it will raise in no time :)

Here are some common pulling tips that I'm sure everyone already knows but hey, it's my dime.

Pulling tips:

1. Archery builds very little agro. So if you are pulling but not tanking, pulling with an arrow will let the tank get the mob off you quickly.

2. If you are pulling something that runs as fast as you (or faster!), pull with snare. This may seem obvious, but hey.

3. If you are pulling indoors, and there are 2 mobs that you wont be able to split, root one. Assuming the root sticks, you will have one rooted mob and the other one pulled. This gives you a short period of time to either dispatch the first mob or get some crowd control lined up for the inevitable INC of the 2nd.

4. If you are pulling or running away from something that is as fast as you, run diagonally forward. This seems to keep mobs of kilter enough as to where they can not beat on you. Also, jump from hills. This motion builds some distance between you and the mob.

5. If you are pulling mobs with SoW or DS's or other buffs, pull with cancel magic.

6. If you are pulling something that summons, use a no damage spell like snare or cancel magic. This prevents the (somewhat unlikely) chance of you getting summoned while pulling.

RAISING TRACKING SKILL & THE NEW UI

I find the Track window quite annoying when I just want to raise the skill. What I used to do in the old UI is to quickly press the iventory key two times this would remove the track window, without the need to actually go and piint 'Cancel' with the mouse. You dont't want to do that hundred times..

With the new UI, I set Normal Transparency of the Tracking window to about 75, and Faded Transparency anywhere between 0 and 50. This will bring the tracking window more opaque when you hit the TRACK hotkey, but after a few seconds the window will almost disappear (fade off) and only the text will remain so you can leave the window on and hit the TRACK key often times during fight or walks without the need to Cancel it away and you keep a good view ahead.

FRIENDS LIST

Just found out that you can type a space after the name of your friend, and then additional characters up to I assume the lenght of name & surname.

When you type /who all friend they will show up as long as the first name is correct, anything after the space is up to you.

For example :

/friend Ninaldiu theranger

or

/friend Enoni war54

etc..

In other words, you can change the surname to something to help you remember who is who, the /who command will only use the name.

HIDE MESSAGE:

Otherwise, if it's unsuccessful, you'll target yourself. Works every time.
