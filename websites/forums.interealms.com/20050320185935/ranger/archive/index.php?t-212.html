Eldowin,

If you're stuck on the idea of fear-kiting AND getting pp then the Everfrost mammoths are going to probably be your best bet.

However, if you want to make pp AND xp for the tradeoff of slightly more downtime then how about trying Sol A or the gnolls at the Spires in SK. Both are relatively easy and the drops usually add up quick. (I still go back to Sol A for plats once in a while. I can usually make close to 100pp/hour solo. ;) )

The gnolls may be green to you by level 40 (haven't been down there in a while) but there should be other mobs wandering the zone that you can get xp off in between gnoll spawns.

The other benefit with going to SK is you're only 2 zones away from Rathe Mts which is a cash/xp cow at your level. I've never had trouble getting in a HG group there. Also, I don't know if you've completed all your Ivy Etched quests (or even if you WANT to lol) but if you do go to RM keep checking track for Maldwyne(sp?) the Unkept. He is a rare spawn that drops a broken bow component for the Ivy Etched Gloves.

Anyway, food for thought. Good hunting! :)
