Instead of malos/slow, malos/slow, you just nihil one... it's slowed, guaranteed... now you have plenty of time to malos/slow the other mob... in a situation almost devoid of danger. It addresses most of the primary problems of the shaman class, by making it so the shaman doesn't ever fall into the "crap I can't slow the mob and now I have to chain tnargs" cycle.

I also think it has lower aggro than Turgur's by a significant margin, but this is hard to tell with Time-geared tanks' great aggro.
