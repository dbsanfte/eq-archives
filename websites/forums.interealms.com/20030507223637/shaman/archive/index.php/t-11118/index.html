I actually really like my BSH. Self buffed, my STR is only 140ish. With offense at 200, and 2HB skill currently at 67 I've seen myself hit for 47. Remember that base max damage is weapon damage rating *2. You will go above that as your weapon skill increases.

I do a lot of in the face melee right now, so what I usually do is switch to the REIF until Piercing is maxed out, then work on my 2HB with the BSH until I ding. The DPS is significantly lower with the BSH, but I just love to see that 263HP proc land. :)
