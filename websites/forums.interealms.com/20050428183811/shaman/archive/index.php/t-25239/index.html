Barm McLir
Not sure how many of you are familar with the 'unique' band Oingo Boing. You've probably heard lead vocalist Danny Elfman's musical scores to movies like Men in Black, Sleepy Hollow, and Spiderman and TV shows like The Simpsons, the animated Batman, and Tales from the Crypt. I sound like Troy McClue there.

Anyway, I started an undead warrior and one of the quests sends you down to a wagon and tent with the follow four NPC's. Doing this from memory so the spelling might be off.

Daniel Ulfman - Danny Elfman (leader, songwriter, lead singer, rhythm guitar, co-arranger)
Steven Bharteck - Steve Bartek (lead guitar, co-arranger)
Samuel Fipps - Sam "Sluggo" Phipps (horns)
K-something Grayves - I *think* the fourth npc is a reference to early band member Carl Graves.

If you stand next to Daniel for a while he'll say:

"Where are the rest of the guys?" -- Oingo Boingo has performed with as many as 20 people.

"Every bodies is coming..." -- From the song Dead Man's Party.

"He didn't mean to do it..." -- From the song Only a Lad.

Boingo broke up a few years back but I really enjoyed running across this small tribute from the WoW design team.
