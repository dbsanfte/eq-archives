XeroOmega
think about what LDON should have been....

thats DoN.

there are zones that are completely unique in design, however not only can you go to them "as is", but when you get an instance (mission/raid event) you have them all to yourself.

the EXP mods on the instances is VERY nice. probably some of the best zones for AA exp. ( say AA exp, because you're routinely rewarded with AA exp)

you recieve crystals for doing missions or events, tasks (solo missions) take place in non-instanced zones, but don't yield anything but plat, faction, and exp.

there are three camps. Wayfarer's, Dark Reign, and Norrath's Keepers.

Wayfarer's offer tasks to get faction up for Dark Reign or Norrath's Keepers, but will stop offering tasks when faction gets sufficient for the faction you're working on. they will, however, still offer tasks for opposing factions.

DR/NK will start giving quest tasks once your faction gets to indifferent. quest tasks are for progression, and can only be done once. there are several benefits for progression, stat cap increases, HP increases, buffslots, etc.

the progression rewards for both sides are identical, so there really isn't any difference to choose one side over the other.
