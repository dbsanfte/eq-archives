I use EQW, and have had up to 4 instances of EQ open at once. DO NOT ALT-TAB out of EQW. First make sure you are running 232 EQW. Second, when trying to get out of EQW hit CNTRL-ALT-R, this will free your windows mouse cursur. Then go up and minimize your window that way. Now start your 2nd instance of EQW, once it goes through all the hoopla and 2nd toon is loaded use CNTRL-ALT-S to switch between the 2 instances of EQW. If you have more than two going at once you'll have to CNTRL-ALT-R and minimize to choose the EQW you want to access. If you freed your mouse this way once you get back to EQW that you did this in you'll have to CNTRL-ALT-R again to regain your EQ mouse. I'm not running any high speed machine either. AMD 1.2 althon, a gig of SDRAM, and a G-force 4 generic video card. Try changing windows the way I say above, and it should work.
