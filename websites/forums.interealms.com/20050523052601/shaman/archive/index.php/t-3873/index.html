Here are some suggestions, I'm not real keen on Luclin but, here goes. I hunted in LOIO from 21-29, exp wasn't too bad. Warslik Woods giant fort is another place you can hunt until about 28. I hear Paludal Caverns in Luclin is great exp up to 29-30. Not sure about any places in the "old World" as I've spent most of my time in Kunark or Velious.
Lake is a great place to solo or group the choice is yours. Sarnak fort was fun grouping for me at 24, when I got regen and cannibalize. You should group for a little while to gain enough exp, in case you get whacked you won't lose your level. Then solo to max your skills Defense, Offense, Piercing, etc, etc, etc.
