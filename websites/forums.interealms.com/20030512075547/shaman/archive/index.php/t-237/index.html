Gettinn
Soloing in Luclin is great exp. I find it to be well worth it. I soloed toilers in ME and sun revs in SD from 55 up to 60. Before I got epic and JBB it took a lot more work, but it wasn't too difficult.

I used same tactics for both.
Cast pet (most mana efficient dot you have if you keep him alive - stay in melee range to keep pet from getting hit unless you need to back off for heals.)
Pull with slow to where you have pet parked.
Malise if necessary (yes, level 19 Malise. It's low mana and makes enough difference on these mobs to be useful)
Stack VoS and Ebolt (re-apply when needed)
Melee that sucka down (channel heals thru as needed - it's not that hard with Chloroblast)

I used a LOT of bandages doing this. Worked bind wounds up to 210 so I could bind to 70%. Downtime consisted of cannidancing and binding until I was 70 mana, 70 health. I would then med until 80/80 and then pull again.

Once I got a JBB, I replaced the melee portion of the battle with chain casting JBB. Just channelled it right thru the beating.

Soloing was better exp than groups a lot of times, only thing really better was partnering with a monk or rogue. With a rogue partner, we were tearing up toilers and the only time I had to stop for a bit was when buffs wore off and needed reapplying.
