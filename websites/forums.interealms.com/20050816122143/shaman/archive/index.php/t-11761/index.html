talas213
two DoT related 'bugs' are the biggest ones that come to mind.....

1) DoT agro.

Our DoTs generate more agro per DPS than anything else I've seen. My guess is that all the agro generated from the DoT damage is generated at first. That is, casting a 2k dmg / 7 tick DoT generates agro equivelent to a 2k dmg DD. A DD has the advantage that it can be cast near the middle of the fight and still does full dmg. The DoT has to be cast near the beginning of the fight for it to run full course. However, casting at the beginning of the fight means you'll draw agro off the tank.

2) Double resist checks on DoTs.

DoTs with an initial DD component have a double resist check (one for the DD and one for the DoT itself). If either check fails, the spell is resisted entirely. Thus DoTs with a DD component are inherently inferior to DoTs with no DD component due to having a higher resist rate.
