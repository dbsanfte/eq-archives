Azhdarkhos
The known change to the quest is that it is now possible to have too high a faction standing to do at least one of the middle steps (Ghiosk's books hand-in), perhaps other middle steps. The result of this is that it is no longer possible to repeat some of the steps endlessly to max-out faction in order to skip other parts. It may still be possible to do earlier parts to max faction (I haven't seen reports on those steps yet), but it would take a good bit longer that way.

Basically, if you intend to go the 'max-out faction' route and skip Kirn and Neh, there is now a roadblock or two you would have to deal with.

As to the 'forces necessary to take on Rak' and Kirn, I've seen claims of others doing it with one group, and that's not exactly farfetched. The only tricky bit is getting to the point where you can IVU up the tower to the Kirn/Kindle area (and yeah, you can do Rak with just two people - cleric and mage - it just takes forever ;).)
