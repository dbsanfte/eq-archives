some cheap things that you can work towards:
Velium fire rings(400pp ea)
black sapphire electrum earrings(200pp ea)
goranga idol(100pp)
sarnak battleshield(400pp)
tattered mantle(200pp)
barbed ringmail(2k, or iksar bp much better and 5k)
spider fur belt(200pp)
shimmering terrorhide cloak(200pp)
shissar scaled gauntlets(1k)
shissar scaled headband(1k)
crystal spider eyes(800pp)

those are all relatively cheap upgrades that you can make 1 at a time as you get extra cash. This will get you a ton more hps and a bit more ac, which are the areas that you really lack.

When you feel the need to farm cash, save up 8400pp and buy a horse, it is the best investment that you can make and you will not regret it!
