House
My advice is to never, ever waste time going specifically after plat.

In Tier 1, try to form groups for Consorts in PoN (silks sell for 250-300pp), Mephits in PoN (lots of gems), or castle in PoD (lots of gems).

Keep leveling and get your 61/62 spells for free as you gain experience. After that, get into BoT/HoH groups and win some spectrals (most people don't even roll anymore) as well as loot you can sell for cash, such as windblades and ornate. Do whatever you can to get flagged for Tactics as the loot there is insane and spectral/rune drop rate is better than BoT.

PoP spells will supplant most of the 50-60 spells so I wouldn't recommend spending much time or money on acquiring them.
