gangaskan
There are random Mobs in the zone that drop library cards. Give one to Posie the Librarian and she will give you a 4 slot container called the Book of Mischief. Also dropping randomly are four pages, top left and right and bottom left and right. Combine these four pages in the book and you will get a Words of Wealth, which unfortunately can't be equipped.

Now talk to Bob the painter and give him your words of wealth and you will get a pot of gold (2 slot container). You will need to create an item using the deck of spontaneous generation. Only 2-card combination items work (i.e. Green Writguard, Vambraces of Distraction, Mask of Melodies, Crown of Distraction). Combine it in the pot of gold with a Words of Wealth. This will give you a funny money.

Give 2 funny moneys to Peachy and she gives you a colored Gift Box. The box has 5 charges. If you click on the box, you will summon a doll. The type of doll seems to depend upon the color of the box. The doll has 5 charges of hug, which is a regen spell. As far as I know, the color of the box you will get is completely random.

Each doll is named after a specific class, and there is one colored box per class. When this Doll is combined
with the 5 main Armor of Distraction types (Gloves, Breastplate, Legs, Arms, Feet -- This is gotten through the Deck of Spontaneous Generation Quest) you get the Class-Specific Doll Quest Armor.

Kill Slice and Dice whenever you can to get pet weapons. Broken Blam Stick is LORE, so you can't stockpile them, but you can keep a spare so you only need 1 kill of slice/dice at the start and again at the end of your hunting session.

Not true. Certain areas of the zone only drop certain ranks of cards, plain and simple (though some with more frequency than others).

In terms of the feasibility of PoM- I spent 2-3 months there farming almost constantly and got the Boots, BP, and a cloak of confusion (and sold cards that totalled about 100k in the end). I also got about 30 AA overall.

The xp was actually some of the best in the game after the higher level xp changes so I would say it was worth hunting there, but with PoP out I wouldn't bother with the zone, there are upgrades that arent too difficult to get for the quest items, and you won't be able to get 63+ spells if you spend all your time there. Plus, it is extremely boring after the first week or so.

Oh yeah, and the boots take 3 of the most rare cards in the zone (I was super lucky and got a white crown/throne on my first few days of the zone, don't expect odds like that though).

If you do decide to do the quests, however, I would see if you could bring a friend or two up with you, having somebody to duo/group with makes it a whole lot more tolerable.
