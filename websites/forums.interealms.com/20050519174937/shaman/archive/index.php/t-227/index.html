Bigguss, For someone still in mostly banded they can't afford to plop down 200pp for a weapon. They would be much better off getting better armor.

If you have Luclin there are a lot of nice weapons and armors dropping in zones your level that would be a nice improvement for you. I'd highly recommend Netherbian's Lair. The xp is insane and with all the coin that drops there you will make enough cash to buy new gear fairly quickly. Also its a very safe zone to get to, its right next to the Nexus and The Bazaar and there are roaming guards that will help with some of the creatures in there if needed.
