It's not bad at all with the animals. There is a large clearing on the other side of the hill next to the poacher camp, where you can park the pet in the corner, on the hill, and pull Griffons to it. Just use the third-person camera, zoomed way out and looking back at you on the hill, to watch for incoming animals, and move out of their way (none of the wolves, bears, or panthers are social with Griffons, so they'll ignore your pet). When pulling, just keep your eyes open for animals, and switch frequently between first and third person so you're looking in different directions. If you get aggroed by an animal far from your pet, just root the thing; it will give up once you move far enough away. Even if you are attacked by an animal, only the panthers are any threat, and since they're low blue or light blue you're getting experience anyway, hehe.
