Donar
Actually, you can't train defense/offense. To raise defense, you're just gonna have to take a beating. Try to find mobs that are blue/light blue and at the same time, don't hit too hard. If you were still in the 40s I'd suggest you head to Echo Caverns and solo on the side that leads to the deep. The underbulks hit fast, but not that hard (up to 70ish? -- lighter than IGs and HGs). I finished raising defense there. I just slowed the mob, cast the current cripple-like debuff (forget the name) and meleed them (hasted of course) with my trusty dog, occasionally nuking if I was taking much more damage than the mob or just healing myself -- whichever seemed more appropriate at the time. It was kinda slow, but I'm glad I got my defense up cuz as you noticed, mobs don't like to be slowed. Since you're in the 50s though, not sure what a good type of mob would be for skill-ups. :( If you're gonna continue duoing with the monk, slow early so you are guaranteed some aggro so you'll take a few hits. Eventually your defense will raise.
