Thunderwolfjr
OK, I'll bite and give my 2 cents. I also have an army of alts, all in the 15-40 level range. This includes a Pally, Warrior, BST, SK, Ranger, Monk, Necro and Enchanter.

- Ranger, one of my favorites. Bow, Track, Dual Wield, Double Attack and some nice spells like SOW, damage shields and wolf form, make the Ranger a great class. Not to mention that in the end game you will be one of the premier dps characters. Did you notice who the top damage dealer was when they killed the Sleeper, a Ranger (she did 50% more damage than the next person).

- Pally, nice heals, decent buffs, moderate dps with a 2-hander, lay on hands is a life saver. Stuns are nice but not very sexy. Got bored.

- SK, good dps, nice offensive spells, snare is great, so is FD and Harm Touch. No real healing (life taps too weak), so not a good solo class. Too much down time solo, which I tend to do more than group. Played him before I started 2-boxing, would be different story now, maybe best 2-box partner.

- Beastlord, great at levels 1-39, gets tough in the mid 40's thru late 50's (due to low level pet), and then gets great again 60+. What can you say, great pet that quads, slows, haste, heals, pet procs and CH, dual wield and double attack (aa), good buffs, Paragon, etc., etc. Can't rez :-) Great solo and in group. Similar enough to a shammy that I lost interest and I did not like playing pet cleric.

- Necro, great against anything undead, very good against everything else. Has to use a Peridot to summon pet at higher levels (sux), but has the full bag of tricks against undead. Charm, fear, mez, slow, dd's, dots, lifetap, etc. Not sure how a Necro would ever die, with a pet, snare, gate, FD, and shadow step. I guess anyone can get careless. Kings of solo, no doubt.

- Enchanter, just played into the 20's, so limited opinion. Nice being a "pure" caster, but animation was irritating (no control until aa's) and low dps unless you are charm killing. Charm killing was fun, but very quirky, either feast or famine, great exp or you die a quick and horrible death. Doing CC in a group not really my idea of a lot of fun, but solo charm killing is exciting.

- Monk, nice dps, just got bored with him. May be a different story now with the new skills. FD is nice and you jump around like a damn jack rabbit. Can solo OK at higher level with high bandage skill.

- Warrior, my current alt. Want to try out the new skills. Love tanking and not worrying about mana or spells dropping. Am also a gear whore, so I love having my choice of the best gear in the game. Dual, double, new skills, great gear. What's not to love. However, if you don't 2-box, lack of heal is a challenge, no real solo, have to group.

Since I 2-box, the Shaman/Warrior combo has been a lot of fun and easy to play. You can take down some nice mobs. As has been said before, an SK is also an excellent choice due to the complementing spells, like snare, but the War is just so much simpler.

Anyway, my 2 cents.
