Kalenth
It requires a fairly high skill in tailoring (they triv. at 222 I believe, though I've heard of successes with a skill as low as 170).

Here's the recipe:

(1) fleeting quiver pattern, (1) aviak egg oil, (1) high quality lion skin, (1) vial of distilled mana, (1) griffon feather.


A few points:

The "high quality lion skin" is the only semi-painful item to get. They are rare drops off the Highland Lions in North Karana (don't make the same mistake I did, and spend countless hours in EK or SK killing lions, they don't drop there. Only off the Highland lions.)

Distilled mana is made by a 39+ enchanter using their distill mana spell, and a poison vial and (2) sapphires.

Aviak egg oil is brewed, and is simply made by combining an aviak egg + water flask in a brew barrel.

Fleeting quiver pattern can be purchased in the Bazaar inside the stone building, off the tailoring vendor.

Griffon feathers drop fairly commonly off griffons (I believe the lesser griffenes and griffawns can drop them too, not 100% sure though).

Good luck!
