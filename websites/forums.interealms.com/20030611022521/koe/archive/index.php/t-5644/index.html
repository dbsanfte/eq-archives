Don't like hearing about people leaving KOTE, but if you feel strong about this good luck. There is no single task in the game more frustrating and difficult than leading a guild properly. It is also more rewarding then anything else when it all goes right. Here are some words of Advice for you:

1) Be Patient- It will take time for you to build a solid core of people you can use to raid. Make sure they understand this too. In the meantime, make sure you group together constantly so you form the core of a guild that knows how to play together.

2) No Matter what you do, someone is going to get pissed off- Maybe this is more true for me (hehe), but fact is every call you make is going to disappoint someone. Don't let it bother you, just try to be fair and constant in what you decide.

3) Think Long Term- Don't grow too fast or reach beyond your limits too often. Keep the guild growing steadily and things will happen on their own. The guild will also be more stable. Guilds that double in size overnight are doomed to failure.

4) Make sure YOU are having fun- This is the hardest one and the most important. IF you are not having fun, your mood will permeate the guild and bring everyone down. MAke sure no matter what you are doing, you are enjoying yourself. If the Boss is happy, then the guildies are happy.

Good Luck
