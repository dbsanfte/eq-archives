It might sound odd to you, but I view the use of lame abbreviations as a lack of respect; phrases like "can u buff me," "where r u," or "can ne1 sow me." In-game lingo like lol and other EQ-isms don't really bother me, but if you want something, I feel you should be polite and actually type out your requests. The number of keystrokes saved is minimal at best.

As to the original question of this thread, the grass is rarely greener. There is no utopian server. The main differences tend to be bazaar prices (older servers have lower prices of old items, but higher prices on high-end items and spells due to too much pp in the economy....newer servers tend to have higher prices on tradeskill items since there are fewer master crafters) and population, with some more subtle factors being the dominant timezone, the number of alts, and level of twinking.

If your main reason for wanting to leave is server stability, I would document outages (both on your own notes and /feedback when the server is back up), encourage others to do the same, and email SoE support (after every few times keep pushing it up to higher level supervisors). At the very least, it the stability issue is really bad, you might get a free character transfer out of it. If it's a very serious problem they've been ignoring, enough noise from the player base could bring in entirely new hardware for your server.

-Jovec
