Weyalan
Your bracers need upgrading. Do Coldain Military Bracer quest (from memory, 10AC +5str +5wis +5cold Extended Affliction III). Go to Karnors Castle and ask politely for looting rights on a Jarsarth Bracer. Buy a Sarnak Battle Shield (approx 500plat, depending on server) and keep paw of oppalla for use in primary hand when on large raids and you are just abuff whore and spot healer. You can buy earring upgrades reasonably cheap: Earring of purity / cleansing / twisted leaves are the obvious upgrades, or if you have more money to spend, skull charm or silver hammer earring
