Nektar
At first glance, looked like a pos, but I gave it some more thought and changed my mind. It's not right-click, that sucks, but it's a pretty high level spell proc at least, that's cool. However, in almost every situation it's safer and more efficient to cast slow on inc mobs, don't wanna rely on the randomness of the proc.

That's why at first I thought it sucked, because in that respect I told myself the proc is worthless. But say you pull a train, and assuming you have someone for crowd control, you can use mana on slowing all the incs once. But sometimes, a fight lasts longer than 6 minutes, and some mobs need reslowed. You could then proc the weapon afterwards, thus refreshing slow at the cost of 0 mana as the tank is picking on them one by one.

But overall, I'd say this weapon is kinda situational. By the time you reach 51, you'll get a better slow, and you'll probably stick to that one as it slows more and takes 2 seconds less to cast. And problem is, as long is turgurs on the mob, this proc will be absolutely worthless.

You *could* continue using togors insects post 51 tho, it's still a high % slow. Tanks won't see the difference either, and like that you could continue refreshing togors at the cost of 0 mana, but again this is assuming the fight will last long enough that slows need refreshed, which might not happen very often. But at 350pp, best to keep bagged along with the resist gear.
