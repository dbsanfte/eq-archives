SolipsisSaVatar
bring up your options by pressing alt+o

set your keyboard so that an unused key will activate your 3rd and 4th hotkeys.

put kick in your 3rd hotkey and forage in your 4th.

Now everytime you kick in a fight you should attempt a forage.
It will say " you cannot forage during battle" but I raised my skill from a measly 20 to over 100 in a couple weeks by doing this.
Legran
At work now so I can't give specific instructions (they are here somewhere else on the board),
but your sense heading skill and foraging skill can stay maxed out by mapping your top two hot bank buttons to the keys you use to turn left and right.
In the options screen I map my left turn key to the #1 hot bank slot and my right turn key to the #2 hot bank slot.
Place sense heading in #1 slot and Forage in #2 slot.
Every time you try to turn you will forage and sense heading.

SwordMage
Another tip: if you get into the situation where you have something on the cursor, there is another command most people forget about: Use Screen Center. This activates whatever you are facing head-on.

So in that previous example when you click on the pyramid and get a drop dialog, just hit the key for use screen center and you will activate it. I have it mapped the space bar and use it a lot (e.g. when I successfully forage when leaving a store, click on the door, hit space bar to open, and then clear dialog while I am moving on).
