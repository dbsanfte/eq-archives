I love soloing in PoG.
Its true the exp is very slow. At 90% AA I get 1% AA per kill which isn't horrible.
However the loot is great. Very rarely have I killed a mob that didnt drop a gem/jewelry. It would drop Cystalized Sulfur or something. About 1 out of 5 mobs drops a lvl 50 plus spell.

I'm lvl 61 w/o Epic, Malo, Pox, Torpor. I do have JBB tho :)
I can solo these particular mobs: (sorry if misspelled)
A Gale Wolf
Spirit Flux Wolf
Glade Stalker
Sanguine Kodiak
Rolling Plain Steeds
A Feral Amalgam

I've avoided Galiel Swifthoof and Farstride Unicorn.
I've also seen a giant bear named Spirt Stalker, avoided him as well.
And as the above posters mentioned, avoid the wandering Tranquil Treant. I've seen him wander all over, and when Im aggroed I let pet get on him and I run far away and sit/camp.
I've never had to gate out thus far with any trouble I've ran into.

I pull with Malosini, then I slow. I apply Bane of Nife, Plague, and Cripple. I tank in the face (all mobs summon). They hit for a max of 160 or so I think. Meanwhile I just chain cast JBB with pet (61 True Spirit) biting on his rear. If the mob is not a caster I use my Barbaian Spirist Hammer and swing that between the JBB casts. I reapply Bane of Nife maybe 2 more times if that. Mobs are fairly easy, but just have a lot of hps.
Sometimes I do get adds, and sometimes I can handle an add that comes in during the middle of the fight. This all varies on the type of mob and if its a caster or not. Usually I can just root the add and be ok for awhile. I've only died once while soloing in PoG and thats because I went LD after pulling a caster mob and I coudlnt get back into the server cause of the "Active character" message for like 15 mins. When I logged back on, was naked, and I went back to PoG and couldnt find my corpse. I looked all over and no trace. I ended up buying a Necrotic Divining Device (ammo slot) for 600pp in the bazaar and going back to PoG and found it stuck in a tree, hehe.

Anyways, I keep up all buffs and resists while fighting in PoG, it helps to resists the casters, as they almost always try to cast Ensnaring Roots. They seem to land Drifting Death on me quite easily and they usually have a Thorns damage shield on them which seem to tear away at my dogs hps very fast. I haven't tried pulling with a Cancel magic type of spell, it may help though.
I've seen a lot of various armor for many classes drop and rot. I have a near full set of Assassin armor for my Rogue (minus the Boots and Gloves) that I have got from soloing there. The mobs corpses take 7min approx to rott which is plenty of time to camp and log onto an alt hehe.

I've literally almost completely cleared the zone of all these mobs:
A Gale Wolf, Spirit Flux Wolf, Glade Stalker, Sanguine Kodiak, Rolling Plain Steeds when soloing for a couple days at 8 plus hours at a time. For the past week or so, I been logging on and hunting there and its almost always nearly empty. I usually don't even pull to a spot, I just kill them on the spot if I find a mob.

I've seen guilds and some groups come in the zone and they give me a tell asking where all the mobs are. I tell them that the mobs east of the stream, such as the Thiflings, Sylvan Protectors, & Serene Forest Spirits should be up. I'm not sure of the spawn time, but I believe the mobs are 8 plus hours.

I make around 1.5kpp to 2kpp per hour. Great place to farm for pp, as now I avoid Sebilis and RM when trying to get some fast and easy money.
Hope this info helps. :)
