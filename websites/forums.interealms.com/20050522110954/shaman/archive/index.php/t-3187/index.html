Something it took me 15 levels to figure out -

if fighting multiple mobs, and you back off to root-park one while the dog attacks the other, make sure to use /pet back off followed immediately by /pet attack. This clears the pet's aggro on the parked mob. Thus, when the first mob dies, the pet doesn't go charging off in a half-healed state to attack the second mob.
