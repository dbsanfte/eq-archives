I've still only seen two charms in-game:

1. Stats raise as you have more money.
2. Stats raise as your weapon skills increase.

And the final stats are laughable. Someone with 100k gets something like 2ac and 3cha from charm 1. Someone with every weapon skill maxxed gets 5dex and 5agi, or something similar to that, for charm 2.

I suspect many of the charms have yet to be added to the game yet. Maybe some of the yet-to-be-added ones will be useful. One clue is the fact that you can search for charms in the bazaar window. However, both that I have seen are no-drop. I haven't even heard anyone claim to have seen a droppable charm.

At this point, spending time camping a charm dropping mob is a waste of time.
