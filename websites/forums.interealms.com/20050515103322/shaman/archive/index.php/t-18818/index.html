Sunmoon
Well, I read every post that was written on the subject, fielded over 50 PM's and emails, talked with other members using both services, consulted my fiance, did some soul-searching and I have finally made a decision.

I'll be advertising with IGE. The offered me a SUBSTANTIAL sum up front and offered me a monthly fee as well for just doing what I've always done here. I don't have to give up any rights, priveleges, design changes, add new sysadmins or anything. Everything should stay exactly as it was, except for the addition of advertising. With the latest talks I've had with them, it may actually be a banner, but nothing more than that. I'll be discussing further with them to get it to text only on normal pages and perhaps only a banner on the main forum page and static site.

I'll also be moving to a better server, a dedicated one, on which I should be able to do some of the things I've been hankering to do that I couldn't on my current server. To get my fees so low during our donation time frame, I had to cut back on the hardware since the bandwidth was so much. With a new server, I can finally get on the static site and do what I've wanted to. Remember back awhile ago when I had the dynamic guides and forums? I hope to rekindle that project.

I chose IGE over Yantis for a number of reasons. Most of you know and loathe Yantis for his practices and past and I took that into consideration.. and I've got some new stories if you want.. geesh. He also effectively wanted to merge this site into his network of sites and I didn't want to relinquish that much control. IGE is simply asking (and paying) for the right to advertise here. It's a win-win for both parties and I hope you can understand my thought process on the matter.

I'm sure some of you will not like this decision; some of you may even leave, cursing me the whole while. I will understand though and can only try to assure you that with the exception of minor advertising, nothing will change! For over four years, I've run these sites without asking anything in return (and enjoyed it the whole time) and it's nice to see that I can actually make some money doing something I enjoy. In addition, I'll never have to grovel for donations drives again.

I hope you all can understand the entire process this has been and if not embrace my decision, at least tolerate it.

In the meantime, I'll try to continue to make this the best site I can!
