PennyAntics
So my guild assembled to kill Dain Frostreaver yesterday. We had more than enough healing and firepower so I decided to have a little fun. I memmed Bane of Nife, and Blood of Saryrn, and chain cast them, and used EQ companion to get the exact numbers on what happened.

~11,000 damage over 3:05 fight
averages to about 60 DPS
Many resists - very rarely had both dots running at once
Got summoned at least 3 times, banished once (dain proc)

So today we killed him again (triggered with shawl) and I decide on a new strategy. I mem Tears of Saryrn and Torrent of Poison, our two best DD poison rains. Because of the long reset time chain casting one rain isn't the best or highest damage way of doing it, and you need 2 memmed. During the fight I was very impressed with the difference over DoTs even though I still got a very substantial amount of resists.

~19,000 damage over 2:45 fight
averages to 115 DPS
.25 hits per second, meaning every 4 seconds on average I would land a wave from one of my rains
average hit of 487 damage

the last two lines show how dain really is pretty resistant to poison, and yet twisting these rains together still puts out far better DPS than the DoTs. However what impressed me the most was the lack of aggro. I was constantly hitting this mob for around 500 damage every few seconds; boom, boom, boom, boom, boom and yet I got no aggro. zero. I didn't get summoned once and on top of that, I sat down near the end of the fight just to test and I didn't get an ounce of aggro. It was great.

I really think that putting these two rains together might be a much faster -albiet less mana efficient - way of doing damage when compared to dots. Feel free to draw your own conclusions though. I would guess that on a non-poison resistant mob 200+dps would be average.
