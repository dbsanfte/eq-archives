At level 45, you can do all of the quest leading up to the black dire part solo. Doing the black dire part early is impossible if you don't have high enough True Spirit faction, and if you haven't done any of the epic quest, you probably don't have it high enough.

Go do the quest. There are tons of spoilers out there, or you can just start in Rathe Mountains. There is a guy in the cyclops valley that starts the quest, I think, been about 2 years since I did it.

If you can get a friend druid that can port and track for you, you can have the quest completed to your fancy new boots in a few hours. If you bind yourself at the firepots, you can do it in maybe 4-5 hours, assuming you get help finding the wandering spirit in west karana (that zone sucks so bad!).
