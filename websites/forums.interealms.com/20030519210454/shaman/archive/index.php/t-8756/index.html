Grippa
To expound on what my learned green friend said :)

The outpost in OT is pretty much all on Venril Sathir faction. Venril Sathir is a Undead Iksar Dude (UID). You will see lots of UIDs around Kunark :)

You can trash your VS faction (and become KOS in the outpost) by hunting in several zones in Kunark, including:

Timorous Deep (the ogres)
Charasis (aka Howling Stones)
Kaesora
Karnor's Castle (Venril himself hangs out here)

There is only one way known to raise VS faction - that's to hand in 4 sarnak war braids to Captain Rottgrime, who lives in the outpost. You need to be at least non-KOS to do this quest. That means if you lower your faction so far that you are KOS, there is no way back.
