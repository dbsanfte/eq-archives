finaljustice
Pain medding becomes more efficient as you gain levels, so you can look forward to a better relationship of lost "red" to gained "blue." Cann Dancing refers to waiting until a tick begins (which you can see in the update of your HP's), standing, canabalizing and then sitting back down before the tick's over. This means that for that tick, the game still thinks you're sitting and won't stop your sitting regen. I never strained my eyes looking at the bars (when I was your level, we didn't have HP's displayed on the main screen), deciding when the tick was and acting upon it. If you Cann Dance pefectly, you'll hit every regen tick. If you stand, Cann and sit, statistics say you'll hit half of the ticks anyway.
