Now to the reason behind this post.

Why don't the boxing federations just come out and admit it. They are NO MORE than entertainment JUST like the WWF. The sport is a joke. With so many bogus wins, with stupid DQs, stupid rulings, it is no wonder that people in general have stopped supporting this great sport now.

I know it started years ago, people in fits over some of ALI's tactics that he got away with. Holding, rope-a-dope, etc. Then Tyson's crap, biting ears and such instead of fighting like a pro. And with so many bad calls, bad referring, and the money exchanged, not to mention Don King's antics, I am utterly amazed that they can make any money at all off of serious sports fans.

Pay per view?? lol. Right, to watch a bogus sport?

Maybe once people start realizing what they are paying for, like 500 dollar tickets to watch a pre-ordained outcome fight, then maybe they will decide that the entertainment they are receiving just isn't worth it.

I mean to me, if a TRUELY gifted athlete makes lots of money, doin what he can do, in a legitimate sport, okay. But when an undereducated guy who's only gift is his body, becomes part of a prewritten script like a talk show, then why is he worth all that money?? I mean, heck, alot of guys can read a script and get out there and box around some, and do it for alot less cash.

I wish people would quit making people like Don King richer.
