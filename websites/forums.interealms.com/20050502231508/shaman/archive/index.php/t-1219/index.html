Initially they were NO DROP, but I believe they changed it recently so they're now tradeable.

I have never seen them for sale, and wouldn't expect to anytime soon- they're too good for too many classes. :)

If you're looking for something similar AC wise, you might want to try for the Western Wastes dragon leg series (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/adv_search.html?stn=dragonleg) - prices are finally starting to fall a little on them. I got lucky and snagged a pair of the Heavy (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=4450) leggings right after the silver chitin handwrap upgrade (traded a level 60 bard who had just upgraded to velious quest leggings for the new handwraps straight up- a great deal, I thought.) Another alternative is the barbed ringmail leggings (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6306), you get +7 sta and good AC with them, and they're becoming more widely traded, too.

I think the barbed rubi are the best tradeable HP shaman legs (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/listitem.html?sort=Armour+Class&invslot=Legs&cfilter=9&statfilter=hp) - and you need to get as uber as ancient wurm/wyvern hide (+10/15 sta, +50 hp) to even approach them if you're looking for lootable stuff.

Good luck!
