Howling Haze appears to be a direct replacement for Wailing Haze - a single target slow.

Grim Lethargy appears to be a group slow.

So far so good. In EQ1, the group slow was worse than the single target slow, so it was rarely used.

But Grim Lethargy gets an upgrade in the 40's. I forget what it's called, but the point is - is Grim Lethargy equal to Howling Haze in terms of slowing capability, or is the replacement as good as Howling Haze?

Which do the upper level shamans use? Howling Haze or Grim Lethargy or the 40's Lethargy? Which is the most slowest slow?
