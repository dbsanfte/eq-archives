It was QUITE a difficult camp at times; but for reasons other than I expected. I thought the big problem would be the Necrosis Scarab, but he was quite easy once I landed malo on him. The biggest problem for me was the roamers that go up and down the path, and are always krups so they've got tons of HP's. It got really nasty a couple times, but I was always able to channel a heal or root in time. At one point I was actually knocked unconscious by a rooted krup shaman's poison DD, but regained consciousness and got a heal off in time to save myself.

The only time I died was when a froglok enchanter came wandering along, which I was completely unprepared (ie no malo memmed and only about 30% mana) for and couldn't land a slow on him and his pet seemed to be immune to my JBB blasts.

Anyways, once I finished up, which was literally when I ran out of room in my backpacks, I gated to Nexus and ran to the bazaar. Self buffed my charisma to 139 and started to sell. Ended up around 13000 plat from common gems and jewellry, then went into trader mode with my blue diamonds while I ran out to grab some dinner. I put the blue diamonds up at 1750 plat each and by the time I got back to my computer 20 minutes later all 15 were sold :p

All told, I figure it to be around 38k or so from 16 hours of camping. Also made about 8 yellows of AA points, so now I've got some more saved up that I'm either going to put towards mental clarity 3 or natural durability. Not bad at all =)
