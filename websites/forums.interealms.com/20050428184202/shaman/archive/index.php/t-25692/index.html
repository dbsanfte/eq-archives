My apologies if this has already been posted. If you want to twink a char on the same server as your main but on the opposite faction you will need the following:
1) access to 2 accounts (because you cannot buy your own auctions)
2) 1 mule character slot open on each account

Step 1: Run a mule to Gadgetzan on your main's faction, main account.
Step 2: Run a mule to Gadgetzan on twink's faction, second account.

Okay, now you're all setup. To xfer coin:
Step 1: Use mule on second account (twink faction) to put a piece of bread or wherever for sale in the Gadgetzan Auction House for X gold * 1.15, where X is the amount of money you want to transfer to your twink.
Step 2: Mule on main account buys the item, thus tranferring the gold minus the 15% transaction fee (hence the 1.15 markup) to the mule.
Step 3: Twink faction mule then mails your twink the coin.

To xfer items:
If the AH is empty (very common), you can eliminate the hefty 15% cost on high value items by taking on a little risk:
Step 1: Mule on main account puts item to transfer for sale dirt cheap (such as 30g item for 1s).
Step 2: Mule on 2nd account immediately buys out the item and mails it to your twink.

If you're worried about your item getting ganked when you xfer, simply put the buy out price higher than the item's value and eat the 15% transfer fee on the sale price.

The hardest part to this is running that L1 mule through the Barrens and 1k needles to GZ- expect to spend an hour getting killed along the way. The beauty of this, for me at least, is that the Alliance AH (my main faction) has 3x the items for sale as the Horde AH (twink faction). It makes the early levels so much easier when money is no object, not to mention you can bank on having a mount at 40 because you can earn the gold easy on your high level main. Another benefit is that one artisan profession can equip both factions (assuming it's droppable unlike enchanting or engineering).

This has really made restarting an alt on the opposing faction so much easier. If you don't like twinking then obviously ignore this post. :)
