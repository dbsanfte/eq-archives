Ok, you can camp this and get very few adds... The trick is where you camp. If you zone in from the swamp, follow the river till you hit the big lake thing. Set up shop at the end of the river on top of the hill. Camping this bp for 2 weeks I never once got an add here. I had a ranger to snare, which is important. Otherwise, root then when they run and fight it out.

Here's the downside: there are some lvl 50 named mobs on the forager cycle. A lvl 50 necro will be hard to handle at lvl 41 and the lvl 50 sk will be down night deadly. He harm touches for somewhere around 650.

I hired a lvl 37 ranger (he was 38 when we finished) to pull with snare, and then did all the fighting myself at lvl 53. I had a jbb, but no regen/epic and pretty lousy gear exept for the jbb. With a 50's druid and 40's shaman, you're gonna have to kite, and some of this stuff is gonna be brutal to kite, esspecially if your magic resist is low (getting adds while being fear kited sucks).
