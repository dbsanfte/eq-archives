You gain AA's slowly until you can advance to harder zones where the xp rate scales upward. Lower-end players can "max out" an AA line, but with the rate of xp, they will have to pass on other AA abilities. A low end shaman may heal better than a druid, but their buffs may not be maxxed out, their dps may suck, and they may not have defensive AA's. Hence, where the balancing comes in to play.
