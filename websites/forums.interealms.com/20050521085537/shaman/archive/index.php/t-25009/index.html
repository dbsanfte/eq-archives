I just found an easy way to get lvl 10 maybe even farther...

Fishing lol.. you can fish for flounder, sunfish, or froglegs.. At least thats all I have caught..

Just go fishing for a couple hours most spawns of all resources.. And do baked froglegs. Then Herb encrusted baked frog legs.. 2 for the price of one resource.. although its a lil bit more expensive maybe.. Then do Sunfish Steaks. Then pick one of the things sunfish steaks can go into I did Jerky.. again 2 for one.. Then just roll baked flounder...

This got me to lvl 10/160%.. 5 hours total..

Again maybe not the fastest or cheapest but deffinatly easy...

I would run around and fill up 6 4-slot bags full of harvested/mined/fished/etc items, then tradeskill everything in the pack. The artisan levels went quickly plus it got me skill in the harvesting/mining/etc skills in addition to the various artisan skills. Keep in mind that even though there are artisan levels, you still have seperate skills for armoring, baking, alchemy, etc.
