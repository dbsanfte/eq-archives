Ruatonim
my baby chanter (33 currently) hunts the tizmaks in GD. You could probably solo somewhere inside the cave..walk around and check things out as you are non kos to start. The entrance would be hard but doable, my chanter has pacify but root also works here. Of course with the chanter i'm charming a coldain or giant, but its decent exp per kill (nearly a blue per on the higher level tizmaks). They hit for 60s so its not too rough, and they have around 800-1000 hp. Watch out for the shamans as they run at sow speed and like to cast sow on their buddies. Jboots speed is enough to outrun a sow'd tizmak though..so you should be fine.

You could also try cougars in iceclad. I did this with my shaman. Pulling during the day is a bit rough, and you have the chance of running into rude druids charming (they seem to like to pull trains on you for some stupid reason :/ )

Marus seru is an option..stonegrabbers should still be dark blue to you and plentiful. Recuso's are definitely dark blue up to yellow and red..although i've never hunted them, but they make great chanter pets :)

You could try The Overthere...are some mobs at your level range, but you need to pull them to the sides of the zone or face tons of adds. Rhinos run at near sow speed..so careful (faster than jboots anyhow).

Once you make it to 34 pretty much all these zones are great, and become a heck of a lot easier.
