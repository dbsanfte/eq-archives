On Tuesday, January 15th, at 3AM PST, (11AM GMT), all servers will be brought down for a scheduled patch. The estimated downtime is 4 hours. ****The Patch Was Canceled**** What this means is that Rhagamuffin may not be up...

So this may in fact just be a spell farm/exploratory mission. Seran and I will explore the possibilities when we arrive in Ssra tonight.

What this means is that Rhag'Zhezum will be up for a short time until we burn his scaly hide to the ground.

This is a sign up raid. Slots will be filled on a first come first serve basis. If you sign up for this raid please make every effort to be there or give advance notice if you cannot so adjustments can be made.


Requirements for this raid:
Minimum Level: Ideally 55+ but min level 51

3 Defensive Capable Tanks, 4 Clerics, 3 Wizards, at least 1 rogue with a lockpick of 210+, at least 2 enchanters for crowd control, and at least one monk for FD pulls. This leaves 16 slots for folks of other classes for signups.

Ideally I would like to start at 7 PM EST but when you sign up please state the earliest time you would be available so that I can know if this can kick off a little earlier.

As you sign up I will add you to the roster below. The goal will be the takedown of Rhag'Zhezum and then a move to either the second boss that pops or further exploration of areas we have barely touched in the past. There is no restriction or preference for the other slots beyond the ones I mentioned specifically below.

I will be able to lead the raid until 12:00PM EST but if there is sufficient participation the raid could conceivably continue. Once the portion with Rhag'Zhezum is done we will succor back to the zone in to take on reinforcements and or additional folks that would like to come... the 5 group restriction applies only to the boss take down. If you would like to be placed on the reserve list or would like to join after the initial take down go ahead and post your desire... I will add you to the list of reserves.
