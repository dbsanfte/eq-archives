My opinion...there's nothing wrong with sharing. If you walk up and start pulling, yeah expect to get a not-so-nice tell, but if someone asks you to let them share, you will NOT burn in Ranger hell if you say yes.

I spent from last Thursday until this past Monday camping bodyguards in EW. Every time another group came by and asked to share I said yes. Hell, I can't keep them all clear myself yet, may as well let someone else benefit from what I can't kill.

The only time I got anal was when a wizard insisted on getting the whole camp because "killing 3 mobs ISN'T quadding". I told her I'd be more than happy to petition for a Guide/GM to come tell us to share nice, and she decided it wasn't worth the trouble. Heck, I consider myself as having been generous, giving her 3 of the 4 guards, since I was there first. We actually became kinda good friends after that, somehow.

I view a "camp" differently in every scenario. I don't acknowledge that they exist beyond player courtesy, and 99% of the time I don't say anything. However if there's a place I want to hunt and know I am fully capable, I'll ask whoever is camping static_spawn_00 to share. This is solo only. I won't bring in a group and ask to share someone's "camp"....why? Well, there are a lot more places I can exp in the game with a group than places I can exp solo. I can take my group to any one of a plethora of zones, whereas I only have two real gold mines for solo exp, and only one involves a "camp".

With the exception of the above-mentioned wizard, everyone has said yes when I asked (and the wizzy is the one who asked me for the camp, not the other way around). Sometimes being nice about things gets you places. Gabbing back and forth with another poor guy/gal stuck soloing just like you makes the night go faster, in my opinion :)

I shared with a Warder who was PLing his enchanter alt at BGs on Monday night. Most Warders can take all 3 "main" BGs in a row, but this guy was quite nice about sharing and even slipped me a free clarity from his twink as a thank you. I was able to meet my exp goal for the day a whole hour early, and he had a whole extra hour of having the area to himself when I left.
