Diabalein
Tactics solo Guide,
note this assumes you have run3, disroot, kei and BoU/BoS, epic is handy in here as well.

buff up, invis and head up the stairs, go straight at first intersection, turn left when you arrive at the "pit" (pit is impossible to miss, its a giagantic gaping hole) head into the room thats now infront of you, this is your pull spot unless there are kiters in here,

fresh replenishment is a good plan, as is lev if you are worried, there is a click out in the bottom ne corner of pit, with lev you can run there and get out easily.
spells
canni
gate (I play pvp server, gate is always up)
decay
malos
BoU
Bos
root
fast heal
there is a spawn just outside each door, move to max spell range and pull with decay, follow imediatly with disease root, then BoU, charge to close range and malos, epic, Bos.
back out canni up and load a spell set with BoN and Pox in place of malos/decay. reapply BoS and check timer on VP, root at about 15secs or less on Vp timer, cast pox, if root doesnt break cast BoN and then reapply VP. rinse and repeat.
guardians are cake but are also .5blue aa each, warriors and Death Knights are 2blue aa each. the death knights are the only ones who ever give me trouble, RC will cure blood of hate, they tap and some times it breaks root, they are the only ones who will resist VP after decay, I use Dogs of Zerg to get rid of HT,
the wall is your friend, just run around that sucker to gain ground and root as you need to, if there are kite groups Do NOT root inside the room, use the hall outside and just stay near the wall and you are safe from wanderers. rooted Diaku will happily gank anyone who comes close. takes 8-10 min per mob and if you grab pot9 you can finish most fights at 70% mana.

note I have ft2, standing hp regen of 42, mana pres4 and AE4. no can5 and I just grabbed mc2.

I also have little time to spare, I make up the pois/dis awareness pots and use those as well. log in, gank a few fatties and camp. its solid, safe (haha) exp. if you dont mind mobs that can kill you in seconds, not useing a pet, and fighting w/o snare or sow you can yank some damn nice exp in here in your short play times.
