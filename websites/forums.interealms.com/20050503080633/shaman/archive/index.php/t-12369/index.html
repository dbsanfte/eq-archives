Best thing to do is wait until tank has good aggro. So wait 5-10 secs or wait until mob is 95% before malo/slowing. PoP is all about aggro management. This was something I had to learn early back in Valor when I was 60 and had just grinded there for the first time. I was one of those Shamans who as soon as I saw a target, I'd slow it asap. That doesn't work to well in PoP.

Usually, what I do now is:
- I stand next to, besides, or behind the tank always to avoid ping pong and so Tank can get aggro back again.
- As soon as tank engages(mob at 99%), I Malos mob. There's not much aggro at all for me. Also, Malos is a 5 sec cast, so this gives time for tank to get aggro.
-By the time I cast slow, mob should be anywhere around 90-95%. Sometimes I do notice mob turns to look at me, but most tanks regain aggro immediatly, so I don't even get hit. Again, I always stand next to main tank so they can regain aggro.

Just time your slows...make sure you give MT enough time to get aggro. About the early aggro...I think I have come across this a few times...but not enough to say theres a problem. =/
