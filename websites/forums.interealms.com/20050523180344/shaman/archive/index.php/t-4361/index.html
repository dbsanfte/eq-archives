Very nice gear choices. :)

Your magelo AC isn't working- at this point the only gear changes you might need to make would be those that put you over an AC marker (for instance, if your commonly self-buffed AC is 890, it might be worth it to swap some WIS gear for AC gear to get you over 900- howling harpoon for goranga idol, Totemic helm for JB, etc..)

Other than that... looking real good.

I'd keep your eyes open for the JBB now. Getting to 45 won't take you long with that gear, and they don't get sold all that often. If I was liquidating your gear for JBB money, I'd probably start with the gloves (replace with JB), dragon bracer (to be replaced with JBB), legs (replace with ry'gorr/jarsath) - each is a good money item that you won't lose that much by replacing with something cheaper. If I had to, I'd replace everything but the fungi, REIF (and maybe even that), and JBB to get the horse once you get the JBB. JBB + Horse at 45-52 = godlike.

Happy hunting!
