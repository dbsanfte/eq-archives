Gromble
When issuing the Pet hold command the pet will immediately stop what it is attacking or going after and do the command u had issued to it before that. If u had it guard before giving it the pet hold command, it will go back to guarding. If u had it following you, it will return to your side.

A thing to note, you cannot issue the pet hold command during a cast. (like u are able to send in the dog to attack during casts)
