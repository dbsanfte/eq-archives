Yes our pets do Dual Wield.

Use the search feature and look up Project Alpha. Project Alpha is the "Code Word" we gave this undocumented feature when we were not sure if SoE meant for our pets to have it or not. We feared that if it was unintentional that SoE would nerf it. Still have not gotten word from SoE, but ability is fairly widely known so we hope that SoE will let us keep it.

From what I have read, Project Alpha will give our pet 15% more damage. As the other poster mentioned, you can test this by giving your pet 2 weapons of different types. The weapons MUST be of the Primary/Secondary type and one handed obviously. Damage and Delay have no effect, but the weapons will Proc.

I have not noticed any benefit from armor, though the Mage summoned Muzzle of Marduk(sp) will grant pet haste. I also have read that buffs other than direct HP, haste and maybe AC buffs have no effect on pet.
