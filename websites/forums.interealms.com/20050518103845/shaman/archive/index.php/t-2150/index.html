If you're levl 60 or if you have more than 50k cash buying something worth 5k for 3k isn't worth the trouble but if you're lvl 50 and have 7k it is. Never ever daytrade with low margins, you are doing them a favour selling teir itemm 40% profit as in my example should be a minimum.

When someone tells you stories about what they've seen item X for and such pretend to agree that your price is too high and send them to meet you in the most out of the way place you think is plausible. When they arrive simply tell them that you found someone paying more. This can also be done to those guys offering bags of crap for items in the auction channel if they annoy you enough. Even to the lots of line spammers allthough they're less likely to fall for it.

When they ask to see something you've told them the stats of or when they ask to see a very expensive and widely known item just say no.

Your customer is NOT the guy auctioning to buy the specific item you're selling. He's wants to buy it because he cant afford it in 90% of the cases.

If someone is for a very long time and often auctioning an item for a very high price, which is their right of course, but should this annoy you make repeated low offers and have your friends do the same. Increase low offers in increments of about 1% of his asking price start out at half the normal server pricce of his item.

When someone PCs an item toomany times in a row, especially obscure ones, just make something up. This is better if you have friends supporting the price.

Any overuse of abbreviations or caps is grounds for screwing with the perpetrator as much as you bloody well feel like.

I've had a lot easier selling stuff made much more money and had much more fun since I decided to be a jackass to anyone who annoyed me and even to some who don't.
