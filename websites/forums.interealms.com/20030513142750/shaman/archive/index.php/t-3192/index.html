Hiho,

most of our epic can be done with only 1 group. Just keep looking on your server boards for fear raids as soon as you can visit fear. This is the one big problem getting the tear.

For The Hole make a brell halfling rogue, sow and bind him near The Hole and get to know the zone. You might get aggro from some ghosts .. just die and go again .. know the zone . know the way through it. If you know it, it can be done with 1 group (most Lvl 60 though). Important is a Lvl 60 puller to trim down adds and a good enchanter (or team).

For Neh Ashir you need 1 group that is able to kill reavers and a rogue with lock picking skill of 201 or above, Rak Ashir can be done with 2 mages (fire pet), 1 cleric and the same rogue as above.

Rest is a cakewalk .. be happy that our epic is so easily done.
