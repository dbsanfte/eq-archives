Hiho,

corroded chain will drop in Arena or King area. Kael is a good training ground for some raiding tactics, Giants get tougher as further you go into Kael. So assamble a raid force (try with 2-3 groups) and see how far you can get.
You might be able to hand around zone in (from WL) and wait for a pickup raid to assemble, but I would not advice to do so. I got some bad expirience with pickup raids of any sort (not just Kael). But if you don't have the guild to do it it may be your only chance.

Money is hard to come by. I know that many tell you to hunt HGs or stuff like that, I wasnt much into that because you don't get XP. If you dare you can try the evil vendors in Echo Caverns. Go from Shadowheaven to Fungus. Directly after zoning in take the right tunnel. At the wall click at it, go through, keep near wall on left side and go till you see a fort. 2 guards are down at the entrance, 2 archers are at the top. As Lvl 60 I can pull them single, you might run into problems. But they are pure melee mobs with low resists, so you might be able to just root them. They have about 7k HPs. After you killed the guards at entrance try an archer. They summon you up there, so be ready for that. Those archers drop each 2 bows that sell for around 18pp.

So long
