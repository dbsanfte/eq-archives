Binter
I experimented with soloing, found the murkgliders in Noble's Causeway good targets. You can single pull in many places, and slow lands quickly on them. They're pretty good xp soloing, and the murks in NC can very rarely drop an EE5/FT2 EP-quality belt (which is why I experimented with soloing in NC, hehehe.)

Only drawback is they have an AE proc that DoTs dogdog and they have a TON of hp. Soloing them is mana-intensive, but perfectly doable for anyone who can buff to 4khp. Just expect downtime between pulls.
