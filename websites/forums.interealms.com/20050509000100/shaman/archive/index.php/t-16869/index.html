I highly recommend a level of Radiant cure and VP. Maxed VP is nice because it knocks stuff back alot and can last long enough that the aa refreshes before the root wears off. The first level of radiant cure is nice because it's a quick and pretty effective way to cure the entire group. Especially handy in LDoN with traps. The later levels cost alot more and don't add much curing power for poison, disease or curses (only goes from 9 counters to 16 counters) the remove detirmental on the other hand does go up fairly well (1 chance 3 chances 6 chances).

SCM is a nice aa too. Saving ~10% mana on everything you cast is pretty nice.
