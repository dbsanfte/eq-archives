Gloriana
Except for Replenishment (61 version of regrowth) all of the 60+ spells are the result of quests.

Various mobs in PoP drop three types of parchment, ethereal, spectral and some third type I don't know the name of. Shaman turn in the parchments to an otter in the Library of PoK. Ethereal parchments turn into level 61-62 spells, spectral parchments turn into 63-64 spells and I guess the third type become 64-65 spells.

So far, it's a pretty frustrating system since all casting classes (including bards) use the same type of parchments to get spells (they just turn them in to different people). The parchments are no drop, but the spells are droppable. There is also no way to guarantee after winning a random to get a parchment that you will turn it a parchment for a spell you actually need.

Currently, the shaman level 63 spell SLTW is bugged. This spell is actually purchasable from Enchanter guilds, but you can also receive it as a spell turn in. Yay.

Parchments seem to have a greater chance of dropping off named mobs, but I've also seem them drop randomly from various mobs.

I'm sure as more people start to fill out their spellbooks, duplicates will eventually be for sale in the bazaar.
