This isn't always the case. Sometimes it's a way of transferring money between friends when they are not online at the same time. When I borrow/lend money I will buy or sell a bone chip, or other junk item for the appropriate amount. It's much more convenient than having to meet up with a friend if you are both busy.

Another tip is keep all your Plat in the shared plat bank space rather than the chars money space, that way any of your chars can easily get to all your money. I know it's obvious but you'd be surprised how many people don't do it.
