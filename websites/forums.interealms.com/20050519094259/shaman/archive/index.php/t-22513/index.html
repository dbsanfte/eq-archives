junaper
The problem is that the Dev team doesn't look at Slow like it does it other spells. When you use a Nuke spell you can get a resist, partial damage or full damage.

Why can't slow work the same?? When you cast slow on all mobs they have a chance or resisting, mitigating (different levels), or full slow for the amount of the spell. This could be put in place for all mobs not just some. This way if you cast slow and get a mitigated message you can decide if it is good enough for your group to deal with or if you need to recast slow to attempt to get a better percentage slow. I could see making fully slowing some mobs (Named, Boss mobs) harder to help tune encounters. Why does mitigation on mobs have to capped or predetermined (this mob can only be partially slowed)? It should always be possible to do fully slow a mob even if the odds are against you.

Recasting slow on a mob that does mitigate is not exactly feasible in today’s EQ with the fights getting shorter, but its a start and could sometime mean the difference between life and death.

I've also read ideas from others about giving us and AA or innate ability to reduce the mitigation of some mob. I think that this could also be a good idea (and possibly easier to implement) if done correctly. These numbers are probably no where near being correct but here is how I see things could work to make shaman more desirable for the role of slowing. I’m not sure how many stages of mitigation are currently in place but I am going to assume 3 for my example (Slightly, Partially, Mostly)

Innate Ability – Shaman get 50% chance to lower mitigation level by 1 (Eg. Shaman cast slow mob would normally be partially slowable, but shaman mostly slows the mob.

AA Ability – Increase chance to lower mobs mitigation level. Level 1 – 15% chance, Level 2 – 30% chance, and Level 3 – 50% chance.

Again these numbers are probably not very accurate, but give an idea of how it could be implemented. This would help Shaman regain a role as the primary Shaman. It also wouldn’t make it so that content couldn’t be done without a shaman. It would only provide the group a chance to better slow the mobs if a shaman were available. This also would not affect immune to slow mobs.

I would love to see this done, but I'm not going to hold my breath.

The current implementation of mitigation is too narrow minded. SoE really needs to look outside the box and come up with a better implementation for the mitigation.
