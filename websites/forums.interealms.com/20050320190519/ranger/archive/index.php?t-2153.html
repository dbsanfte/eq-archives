Looks like I'm probably going to be in charge of all of those systems now, approximately doubling my current responsibilities. But wait! It gets better.... I designed and taught a class in that technology to a group of my peers a few weeks ago. Most of the people that were in that class will now be reporting to "L" and working with that technology.
