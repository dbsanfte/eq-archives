Few notes about this cycle:

-The hunter and forager have around 5800 hp
-Neither frog flees when low
-They hit for around 116
-All mobs on the cycle run at slightly slower then sow speeds
-Harbinger Dronik is a wizard and will nuke you to the ground when you try to pull him
-Silvermane drops a nice shaman neck piece with 15mr and always on see invis effect
-The hunter cycle has about 15 different mobs and 3 drops. It sucks.
-Sooner or later they will spawn near the archway to the seb area. Seen a lot of people who thought the spawns were broken when this happened.

I've given up on camping this guy. I soloed the forager cycle with a druid for weeks and I've seen every single rare except zoglith. I hope you have better luck then I did.

For some great info on doing the TT Cycle look here:

http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=90

You will want to do the Forager cycle.

I play a 52 bard, and I can track and single pull any mob from the zone.
HOWEVER, the only way I can kill most the spawns in the cycle is by chant kiting them (run in circles and stack 3 low damage, 3 tick dots on the mob), and this is very slow.

So get your rogue, grab a bard, and have the three of you do the cycle.
I suggest you and the rogue set up camp just outside the cave zone-in from Swamp of No Hope.
Since your grouped, the bard can use track to bring the mob back to you, and kite it until you land a slow, etc.
