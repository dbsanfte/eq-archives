Astroshak
The Hole entrance should be right up your alley then.

You should be able to single pull (pull from the max distance possible) and then have the chanter tash/slow the elemental, while you beat on it. You should have no problems with the first 6 spawns; and you might even be able to take on the crusader/warrior pair down past the first flight of stairs (something I could not do solo). If you try the pair, remember that the chanter has a small, componentless rune spell line, that buffs str and debuffs agi (remember the Polished Granite Tomahawk? it proc'd the first spell in the line); the crusader hits harder than the warriors.
