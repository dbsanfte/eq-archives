Grimborn
In order to get Neh'Ashirr you need to fight through the reaver house and out onto the "sky ramp" where there are 3 huts. The very last hut has a single reaver, this is the one that will spawn Neh'Ashirr once killed. If youre looking at a map perhaps they wil have the enchanter epic mob labeled at the last hut, that is the same hut with the reaver you want.

-Grimborn
