IIRC The player name/surname is text part of the HP guage.

try changing the TextOffsetX to a negative number. The bigger the better

so the first occurance of <TextOffsetX>0</TextOffsetX>

becomes <TextOffsetX>-500</TextOffsetX>

This will still display it but outside the area of the box.

you'll then need to add your own Player name label EQTYPE 1.
