Not at all so...

I'm amiable to Validus Custodus and non KOS to the Heart Consilium atm. The first thing I did was hunt the Coteries in ME. They don't give very good faction hits and are very tough. However they have awesome drops and are fun mobs to kill to push yourself to the limit. I got a 10ac 7int/wis/mr/fr 50mana ear ring out of it too. Got a lot of other nice junks for guildies, there are few pops so it's best done duo or trio.

Then I killed guards at SSeru entrance for a while, I now have sufficient factio to do the ear ring of veracity quest #2, however that ear ring sucks and I still need to build a lot of faction for the third. I'm ONLY non-KOS to Validus Custodus in Katta and Heart of Seru in Sseru atm. But the other factions will come as I hunt within the consiliums for the reports to get ear ring #3. Also the primary quest givers for the three first ear rings are all on Validus Custodus which is the faction you get from Coteries and entrance guards in Sseru.

Note however that none of those mobs are slouches, cteries have about 12khp and proc a 200 lifetap very often as well as flurry pretty much. Optios who roam around near the entrance of SSeru have about 16khp and quad for 175 very very fast and often hit for max. However the Coteries have awesome loot and getting help there shouldn't be too hard, and the guards in SSeru drop a lot of pp 12 to 18 per mob usually.

I spent approximately three days playing relatively much doing each thing. But both were challenging amusing and worthwhile camps. It is slow at the start just like velious factions were but once you get the hang of it it's not so bad.
