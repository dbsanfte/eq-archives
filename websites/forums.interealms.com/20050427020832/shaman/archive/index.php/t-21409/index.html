Mobs in here partially - mostly slow and hit for around 1k so it's definitely dangerous soloing. The nearest bind point is nedaria so if you die it's a long run. Despite this however, thus far I've soloed a yellow con for 8%aaxp and I'm assuming 7% from white cons.

To get one of the mountain trials for those that don't know, there is an NPC in Barindu named Apprentice Udranda whom gives out the tipt and vxed expeditions. There is a mastruq that paths in agro range of her so you need to time dropping invis and getting trial right so he doesn't agro you but it's not hard at all, just watch his pathing. Say Vxed to her to get the trial and then zone in at the end of the mountain pass which is just NE of the pyramid where the sewer trials are. The apprentance is standing just north east of the pyramid in some water.

You DON'T need to have done any of the sewer trials to get the mountain trials. I have not yet completed any sewer trials and the apprentice gives me the trial with no questions asked hehe.

Pulls inside can be tricky so utilize the zone line. I typically pull with malos and then root one of the 2 entrance mobs away from their spawn and zone agro. I come back in, debuff and re-root the mob and then proceed to dot it up. You can use a mount in here also which is always handy when soloing.

Anyways, good luck to those of you that wish to solo here, maybe you'll get lucky and get the Chanting Stone Mask that can drop rarely from any trash mob.

When you enter the zone you are in a winding canyon. Following the canyon leads you to a paved circle with statues, then up a ramp to the first intersection. There are two spawns in the intersection, one at the back left and the other at the back right. I was never able to single pull them. The right-hand path has a single pull up the hill. The left-hand has a double, and straight ahead has a wanderer that stops just before it enters the intersection and a single further up the path. If you enter the flat area of the intersection, you will probably aggro both of them, so you have to climb the walls next to the ramp to target and cast.

The mobs ranged from dark blue to yellow, with dark blue yielding 8% aa exp and the yellows 10% (I have slider set to 90/10 aa exp). I only have Blood of Saryrn and Breath of Ultor and BA4 tribute effect, so dark blues were taking 7 minutes to kill and yellows about 9 minutes. (I REALLY need to get off my butt and get the LDoN dots!)

As mentioned before, the 2 mobs at the first intersection are a mix of mountain pookas, venomous stonemites, and hynid ravagers. The pookas have low DR so VP3 never broke early. The stonemites and ravagers have high DR and, even after MD, VP was resisted. I had to use root and magic debuffs. Respawn seemed to be 16-18 minutes, I will try to get a better number.

I used root pulling to break the intersection. I had petrifying earth memmed and greater immobilize on pants, so cast PE on one and run like hell, turn and target the other one and cast GI. If both roots stuck, then I debuffed and cast PE on my victim, then zoned to clear aggro. If it was still there when I got back, then started root-rot (VP for pookas, PE for ravagers).

When I remembered, I would slow (mostly successful) and cripple so I could probably survive a couple of hits if things got out of hand.

I did try one stonemite, just to see if I could survive the summoning/bashing cycle. I rooted, debuffed, slowed, and crippled it, then Q'd myself and dotted it. After getting summoned twice I made it to the zone with 1% health....don't bother.

I'm going to spend some more time to see if I can get a rhythm going. 8-10% aa exp is drool-worthy for me, but a couple more deaths will probably cause me to move on. On one occasion I was able to clear the intersection and pull the single in the right-hand path before respawns, but they were all pookas.

So once you've passed this trial can you instance it again for exp and if so how?

Actually, I haven't passed any GoD trials at all. I simply say "vxed" to Apprentice Udranda and he gives me a trial. He's located northeast of the temple standing in a small pool on the left side of the entrance to the deadend canyon on the east side of the zone. Once you get the trial you run east then north through the canyon to the deadend. There's always a pile of corpses there because it's where you eventually get dumped if you die in a trial. On the right side of the deadend you will see a crack in the wall next to the Golem Stoneshifter (indifferent con). Run into the crack and you will zone into your trial instance zone. You can zone in and out multiple times. I don't know how long the zone lasts because I only get 3 or 4 kills max before I have too many stonemites as entrance spawns.

Nothing in the canyon normally sees thru invis, so you can run up and down safely, but during prime time quite often there are groups pulling stuff into the canyon and fighting there.

There's a 6-minute lockout timer for a trial, which means even if you disband from the trial, you can't get another one until the timer expires. The timer starts when you get the trial. Alt-z will display the expedition window with the timer. If the little display area in the middle of the window is empty, then the timer has already expired.

You have to uninvis to talk to Udranda. If you stand in the pool against the north wall you shouldn't aggro any wanderers.

Good luck and let us know how you do.

p.s. I haven't tried this, but I understand you can talk to Gamesh and get sewer trials for exp only. I understand you can select the one you want. Gamesh is standing in a pool in the southeast corner of the temple area.

OK, I've done about 24 aa's here, and it's the best place I've found so far to get solo experience without elemental flags.

I have about 3350 hp self buffed with bazaar gear, VP3, BoS, and BoU. If they catch me, I survive the first round about 50% of the time when I'm facing them, and die 100% of the time with my back to them.

I only kill pookas and ravagers, and average about 11 kills per aa point with 90% to aa exp. With BA4 tribute, it takes about 2.5 BoU plus all the BoS that will fit during that time to kill one. I estimate they have 30-35K hps. Respawn time is about 14 minutes. Both pookas and ravagers run at 10% health.

Pookas are easiest to kill because VP never breaks after MD, and they are slow runners. Ravagers have much higher DR, and occasionally BoU is resisted even after MD. I use GI on ravagers for its cast time, but even after Malosinia it's not reliable.

My strategy depends on what's at the entrance. If I have 2 pookas, then I pull one with MD, run to the dead tree, VP it, zone out/in, and kill it. Then I pull the other one with MD, run to tree, VP it, and kill it. If there's a pooka straight ahead or right, then I pull ir with MD, run to tree, VP it, and kill it. During this fight the first entrance mob will respawn. As long as I keep getting pookas, I'm golden.

If I have only 1 pooka at the entrance, then I Malos the other one and zone out/in, then root the other one, target pooka, run to tree, MD, VP, zone out/in, and kill it. If the other mob is a ravager then I GI it, Malosinia, BoS, MD, BoU, run to tree and wait for root to break, re-GI as needed and kill it. If GI breaks before I get to the tree, I just zone and get another expedition. If the other mob is a stonemite and there are pookas straight ahead or in the right passage (and I'm feeling lucky), then I will GI and Malosinia the stonemite, pull pooka with MD, etc. This will get your heart rate up quickly.

If there are no pookas at the entrance, I wait 6 minutes and get another expedition.

My best time for an aa was about 90 minutes, but 2 to 2.5 hours is more typical.

I didn't get the mask. :(
