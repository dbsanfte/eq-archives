Guntar Ghostdancer
This is how I was told to kill him but have not tested it...

Pull all of Griegs guards to romm below (port in room) and kill them.

Move Raid force up steps to just before Griegs room. Place a SK at the bottom of the stairs in the corner apposite the port room door. Have a ranger aggro Grieg with a bow or spell.

Pull Grieg PAST the raid force to the SK. Ranger uses weapon shield while SK gains aggro. Ranger will prolly die.

Once SK has aggro, all melee attack from the rear. Its important to keep grieg facing the corner.

All healers stay on the steps and heal out of range/direction of the AE. Casts duck out from the corner in between casts to blast.

Trick to this whole strat is successfully getting grieg past the raid force to the SK at the bottom and then keeping him there.

Like I said, I havent tested this but the person who has done this was the Ranger pulling and has done it several times.
