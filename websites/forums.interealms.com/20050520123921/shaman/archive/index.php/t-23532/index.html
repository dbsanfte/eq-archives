I'm also concerned about the spell system. Frankly, I think the PoP-style rune system was poorly implemented. There was no differentiation between high-end spells and standard upgrade spells in PoP. A rune got you a 65 spell, be it Quiessence or Fo7. The only reason those two spells sell for similar amounts of money is the difficulty involved in getting them. Fo7 is not the same caliber spell that Q is by a long shot, yet you essentially have to be a raider to get Fo7 without being severely gouged in the bazaar. It grants the same effect as FoS, just to more people. But you have to kill EP minis or better to get it. That makes no sense to me. Hopefully in OoW, the relative power of a spell will relate directly to the difficulty in obtaining it.

That said, I am looking forward to custom titles, instanced xp zones, upgraded epics and the task system (somewhat). The level cap bump is ok, though I haven't hit 65 yet, so the carrot of a faster moving xp bar (AAXP) just got 5 levels farther away.
