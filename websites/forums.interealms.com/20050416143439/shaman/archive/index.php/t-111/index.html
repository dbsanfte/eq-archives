This is how I made my Fortune.

Go to OOT and Solo Gornit for 5-50p and FS Weapons per kill, when you hit 48 you can start to do SF's I believe, maybe earlier and they drop Jewels that sell for 40-100p.

While killing SF's you can kill Gornit inbetween pulls, at 54 I am making 2k every 4-5 hours chain pulling with JBB, with just gornit you should pull in about 1k every 5 hours or so.

If he is camped then start a mule, give him 1kp to start off and bring him to EC (or your auction zone on your server) and start to BUY low and SELL high while you wait for the camp. Make sure you give the person who is currently camping him your name and ask them to give you a /tell when done.

You will get encumbered fast, I always destroy the copper and silver, make sur you bring some bags to carry the FS, WR if you have them.

You are a good race bind in NFP so you can just bank and hop back on boat, if you are evil bind in neriak, if you have the Hammer that Teleports you to OT thats even better, bind at gornit. I dont like to sell however in OT as they only give you alittlre more then what it is worth so if you do this, dont sell the jewels, bank them instead until you find a well paying merchant and make sure to have your charisma buff up when selling.

At 54 half the SF's are Blue so I am still making nice experience off of them, my time is done when I ding 55.

Good luck and hope this helps
