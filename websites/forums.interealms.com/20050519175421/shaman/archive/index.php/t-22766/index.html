eqphreak
When going to the high priest for trials you're right, he will give you the highest expedition that you have not yet beat. BUT, if you fix you flags by talking to the scribe and saying 'issues' he will give you the lowest trial you have not yet beat.

For example: You have the flag for #2 but have not said Issues to the scribe. High priest will give you #3.

Example 2: You have the flag for #2 but you said 'Issues' to the scribe and fixed your flags. High priest will give you #1.
