Try to bring all 3 of our main weapon abilities up; Piercing, 1hb, 2hb. 2hb will take the most work, there really aren't a lot of fast 2hb (Kechata Koro Mis 7/30, and "shovel" which is delay 27).

There are some truly awesome weapons in all 3 categories. I chose to stick with piercing, and came late to 2hb, neglected 1hb for a long time. Skill slow to come now that I'm repairing them.

I use epic for piercing, I'm getting hits in the 60s. Rune Etched Iceworm Fang is an awesome and fast piercer. I used the Spine Chill till I got my Epic, tho.

2hb with my Tae Ew War Maul (33/36) lets me regularly smash things for 100-130s, at skill 185. I was late starting this one, and am still suffering. I carry Blight and the Barb Spritist's Hammer for variety.

1hb is still underdeveloped on me, so I haven't concentrated on high-damage weaps here yet. Using a Noble's Walking Staff (8/19) until skill improves.

I'd concentrate on piercing, 2hb, and 1hb, in that order.
