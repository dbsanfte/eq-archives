Jinheim
All focus effects are different. Some of them, like Enhancement Haste 4, only work on spells with a minimum duration for 72 seconds. The 8th shawl effect, Zephyr of Brell, only extends buffs that last over 48 seconds to begin with. Since Q and torpor have a 24 second base duration, neither enhancement haste or extended enhancement will work with it to make it better.

There are some focus effects, though, that will work with it. Haste of Mithaniel (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3531&source=Live) does not have a minimum duration. It works an all beneficial spells and gives you 20% spell haste. Thus, it works for quiesence. QB2 + Haste of Mithaniel will give you a 45% haste, which really is good enough - with blessing of reverence you would hit the cap. A lot of people opt to just stop at QB2 and not spend the whopping 9aa to get constant 50% haste for quiescence, which for most is a perfectly good decision. However, the only easily attainable spell haste items go in your range or shoulder slot, and I have excellent items in both of those places that I am not willing to give up. For that reason, I decided to just spend the 9aa and get QB3, and I am very happy with my decision.

They don't stack, the better focus effect takes precedence. Since the affliction haste on the bracelet of the sky is a savings of 33%:

http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=2362&source=Live

you would notice a significant savings over the spell haste III on your hand of the master which only shaves 15% off the casting time:

http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=2341&source=Live

Focus items never stack with focus items (for example Mana Preservation III with Preservation of Xegony) but focus items CAN stack with AA (for example Quickbuff + Speed of Mithaniel or Mana Preservation + SCM). Focus items can also stack with AA AND spells, for example the cleric spell haste stacks with Quickbuff and focus spell haste.

I also have a spell haste III item (Essence Lens) and Affliction Haste III (bracelet of the sky) and I've never seen both items "activate" for the same spell cast. I also have a Mana Preservation III item (Sky Watcher's Monocle) and an Affliction Efficiency III item (boots of the dead dream) so I've had a lot of opportunity to observe how focus items interact: the better focus item always takes precedence.
