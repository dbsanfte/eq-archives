fudorf
While I prefer the HP/AC over Wis route a couple things pop out.

1) off-hand - Get to work on your epic. While the shield of Falsehood is a drop of 5 in wis from the ToV, it is AC, so you are getting a semi-balance.
2) boots - get a bunch of your good friends together, and head to Mistmoore (after you have completed the quest to the shield) and do the black dire. Those boots will give you wisdom, and change your solo life.
3) Helm - For once, this is where I do go Wis over AC... get yourself a totemic helm. +5 wis, -4 on the ac (the tradeoff for the shield). There are better, but assuming you are probably on a budget.
4) Legs - the bane of all Shaman... best advice, go camp zone line to KC, buffing for free, etc, and asking for looting rights to Jarsath Scale... better ac and wis than gatorscale, and usually left to rot.
5) Belt - Crystaline Belt... AC 5, wis +3... not great, but cheap. 500pp max in EC

There are a few other options to work on... here is the link to my magelo profile... nothing that anyone would call uber, or even great, but more than enough to let me solo most successfully at 53.

http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=62502

As you can see, not too bad a balance of AC and Wis. I do still have my ToV, and another CBB, in the bank, as well as a couple of other higher wis, lower ac items banked, for tradeskill purposes, but I think the general concensus that AC/HP -> Wis, especially in the high end (and once you get Turgurs, and get summoned the first time, you will understand) is absolutely the truth.
