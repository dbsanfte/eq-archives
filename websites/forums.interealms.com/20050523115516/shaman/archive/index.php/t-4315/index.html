Tanilin
Just because I'm a nitpicker:

The hp/second is a bit more complicated than is being indicated above.

In the following examples I assume recast delay is 2 seconds and that you cast instantly after that time is over.

If you cast ONCE: Chloroblast at 142.7 hp/s vs Sup Heal at 129.6 hp/s
If you cast TWICE: Chloroblast at 107 hp/s vs. Sup Heal at 106 hp/s
If you cast THRICE: Chloroblast at 98.8 hp/sec vs. Sup Heal at 99.9 hp/sec

(the asymptotic results are 85.6 hp/s vs 89.9 hp/s)

The differences come about because you only care about recast delay inbetween casts, NOT after the last cast.

Given these numbers, it should be clear that Superio Healing is not as good a spell as Chloroblast unless you cast superior healing at least 3 times in a row.

Most of the time if you have to do that, things are going to pot anyway. :P
