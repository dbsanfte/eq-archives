Faction needed to complete all three = Ally

Max Ally Faction from Max KOS = 3700 faction hits
Droga/Nurga goblin kill = 1 faction

Took me 18 hours to get kos to ally. And for me it broke down like this.

14 hours killing 160 gobbos and hour.
4hours(when i was with rogue) 340 gobbos an hour.
Salts/skins 89 skins and 230 salts. Total turned in.

thats 3600 gobbos. That means the skins/salts were next to jack and squat for hits.

**********************************************
Ring 1

What you need:
Level 50
High warmly faction (100 points below ally, 1000 points in absolute terms.)

Ring 1 Steps:

Talk to Herald Telcha in Chardok and say "I serve the sarnak". You will be rewarded with: Di`Zok Signet of Service (ac2 1cha 1int 1wis 65hp 45mana LORE NO DROP)

**********************************************
Ring 2

What you need:
Ally faction
Ring 1
The head of Warlord Skargus.
Drogan Warlord's Insignia

Ring 2 steps:

1. Talk to "a goblin traitor" (2-3 hour spawn) in Nurga and say "what evidence". He will give you a note.(near the ramp to zone out)

2. Take this note to "a Sarnak spy" in Frontier Mountains. Follow the dialogue and you will eventually say "I will get the report." He will give you a forged note.(1880,3637)

3. Take this forged note to "a shady goblin" in Warsilik Woods near Cabilisk along with a Drogan Warlord's Insignia (random drop in Nurga.) He will give you the "report."(n1647,n1116 on floor 2 of the building)

4. Take this report to "an agry goblin" (5 day spawn) in Nurga. This will spawn Warlord Skargus. Kill Skargus and loot head.(150,1950 between dining room and chief room)

5. Take ring 1 and the head back to Herald Telcha in Chardok and he will reward you with: Regal Band of Bathezid
(ac5 2cha 2int 2wis 5sta 85hp 50mana, 5 charges of 25point ds LORE NODROP)

**********************************************
Cords Quest

What you need:
Ally faction
Undead Dragon Sinew (Trakanon, Juggs)
Urn of Spirits (Drusilla Sathir)
Ring 2

Trakanon is at least a 2 - 3 group effort, more if under 58 - 60 range. He has aoe poison that is nasty dot. He is 32k hp dragon from Old Sebilis, and spawns every 3 days or so. He has a spawned version for the bard epic that in all reports so far has not dropped the quest piece.

Drusella is reported to be 2 - 2.5 day spawn, and level 55 mob in Howling Stones. One group is recommended if 58 - 60 range, probably another group for under. She has a 250pt. lifetap proc, ontop of the melee damage she does. (not sure, but probably around 200 - 280 / hit)

Both of these are in keyed dungeons.


Cords Steps:
Hand Undead Dragon Sinew, Urn of Spirits and Ring 2 to Queen in ChardockYou get ring 2 back and the cord.

Spirit Wracked Cord (Lore, No Drop)
Shoulder, Arms Wrist
AC12, Sta5, Hp100, Svc10, Svm5
Effect: Aura of Battle(regen for 2 hps per tick and +10 atk)
All/All Weight 0.0
