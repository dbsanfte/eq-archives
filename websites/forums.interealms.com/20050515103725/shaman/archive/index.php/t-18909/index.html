Chutoi
Honestly, I'd say it depends on what you do. If you are a raiding shaman, I'd give the edge to crits. Let's face it--we're not uber healers. When we need to heal (especially on raids when raid buff characters are nearing or at low health agro and mobs hit like speeding mactrucks), we need a good heal NOW. Those crits usually come at a good time because when we heal, it's usually needed badly.

If you're not as much of a raider and just exp a lot, then I'd probably give the edge (just barely) to the % increase over the crits. It makes your heals marginally more efficient, and generally speaking the damage taken in those situations makes crits a bit less important. The additional (permanent) efficiency is probably more useful than the crits in experience groups.

This is kind of a switch for me, since before I got both skills, I would have said go with the % increase all the way!

Tudani
I personally used to be of the school of thought that a reliable increase was better than a random one. I've since changed my mind drastically. Gift is way more useful because it can make a difference. A 3k+ heal (well 2600 base with no focus) makes a sizeable difference on a tank or anybody. But more importantly it saves time. If I crit a heal I'm saving about 5 seconds worth of casting time which is a huge amount of dmg done to the person taking dmg. Yes it fairly often gets wasted, but it also fairly often saves someone's life when stuff goes sour. And unless you're the type to use tnarg's when the person you're healing is near full health (I generally use Q if they're not in danger of dying fast and are only around 1300 hps from full, or even a few k from full) you wont' often end up wasting a crit except on spam healing a MT or whoever on a raid...which is also when crit heals are most useful. So I'd personally recommend hg, then either ahg or ha, then the other, and aha last. Also with HA the difference isn't that noticalbe till you get quite a few AA points invested into it, whereas with gift you notice right away (though the amount of crits sucks a bit at level 1 and 2, but is very usefull at hg3).
