Soloing at 63 will be rough. I'd so head to droga or chardokb for some nice quiet solo camps. Not the best xp, but good loot and chardokb is almost always empty.

PoHate tier1 and maybe 2 would work well for ya. Do you have torpor? Tanking PoP mobs will be tough without the defensive aa's, but you can root rot in a lot of tier1 zones. Tier2+ root rot will be very risky and take a really long time without DoT's that don't break root (BoS, BoU, epic, Bane, Anathema).

For PoP, your best bet might be in the hedge in PoN. There's a couple threads on the subject here already, try searching for it. The mobs do summon, hit hard and can tripple, but are fully slowable and you can use a horse. Just keep bane, pox, dogdog, and any other dots you can going. Expect to use some direct heals on youself also.
