Jaruden
Those 2 work out about the same in stats, the DWS has a little more mana and ac, but the hp work out about the same. Mana pres 3 is very nice too. If you're actively working on your coldain shawl (i.e., you intend to actually finish it), you might be better off getting a Crystalline torque for neck slot. It's 12 ac no stats, but it's a lot harder to find a good neck slot than a good shoulder (especially if invest a lot of time in the coldain shawl).
Radag
I am an AC oriented shaman but I would gladly take the ODS over the DWS. The mana saving is noticble and my kill rate improved when I first equiped the ODS.

Jaruden's comment about the Crystalline torgue is also very good and somthing to consider.

A good way of thinking about the ODS is that the 10% mana savings on every spell is the same as making your mana pool 10% bigger without having to also increase your mana regeneration rate to fill it up. So if you have 180 wis and a bunch of +mana items then ODS is worth perhaps 20 adition wisdom or more in effectiveness.

I will also be replacing my ODS down the road with a thurg shawl ( working on 6 now ) and I wish I had the Crystalline Torgeu instead of the ODS.

Hope this helps.
