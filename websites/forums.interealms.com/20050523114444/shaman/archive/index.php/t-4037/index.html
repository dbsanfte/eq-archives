Not bad stuff, you've got a nice balance between ac/wis/hp going on there, wish I'd been that mindful at that level.

Do you have Luclin? The Bazaar is a great place to pick up mid level items. If not you'll have to try and buy the traditional way.

Keep an eye out for any Imbued Northman or Arctic Wyvern stuff especially if you can use the bazaar, you may find people selling off items they've made while raising their skill when they used to just sell it to vendors. For neck look for an Ivory Imbued Collar (not to be confused with the Imbued Northman pieces which are player made), semi rare drop in Chardok and a lovely neck item that wont need to be upgraded for ages. Not sure about your server but they're very cheap on mine. Shaman only item, don't pay more than a few hundred pp for one.

Good luck!
weissblau
IMO you have pretty balanced gear. I wouldn't stress over the WIS level. You can take an upgrade in many slots, so just keep your eyes open and take what comes along. Some stuff to watch for:

JB boots and gloves are obvious choices and the click-buffs will help the mana situation a lot at lvl 45. Should be buyable for around 350 in the bazaar.

Imbued Golden Ivory Earrings would be nice for a barb - AC3 WIS4, so you'd lose 15HP and gain 4WIS. I bought one for 50p in the bazaar, but that was a steal.

The polished bone bracer is probably due for an upgrade - maybe a Ry'gorr chain bracer for the AC.

Straw Spun Belt gains you 4 AC at the cost of a few HP; Flayed Turmoilskin Belt would be an awesome upgrade.

Howling Harpoon is a nice weapon upgrade with +WIS.

Arctic Wyvern Mask would be super for the face and is not that expensive any more.

Black Alloy Medallion for the neck - better AC and good WIS as well.
