Isazo
If you get lucky and find a mob that doesn't resist Poison or cold on a raid (Say some HoT wurms), you can cast torrent of Poison (540 x 3) and about the time the last wave hits you can get off an Ice Strike (675).

So... that's 2295 damage from nukes in about 12 seconds. That's best case scenario and often you get full or partial resists. On some mobs the nuke combo of torrent and ice strike (or blast of poison) is very effective.

I often use the torrent/blast of poison combo in Kael arena where a mob will last 15-20 sec.

In XP groups I'll sometimes have a nuke memmed if the groups damage output is a little gimped -- the mobs die too fast to make Bane efficient, but slow enough that I have excess mana... I'll nuke from time to time.
