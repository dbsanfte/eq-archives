Jinheim
Ogres start with pretty low agility. You want to make sure it is always over 75 in order to avoid any AC penalties. I'm not sure I would put points in to it, though. We can agi buff from a very low level and it would only take one or two pieces of gear with agi on them to get you over 75.

After that, you'll probably find stamina more useful to you as you are leveling up. Once you are higher level with decent gear, though, you'll find that you cap stamina (remember we can buff it by 60 at level 62, 80 if you have PE) long before you cap your wisdom.

All in all I say it matters very very little where you put your starting stats. An even 15/15 split between stamina and wisdom will serve you just fine.
Kahn
Agi > 75 is nice, but will easily be sorted with minor twinkage and buffs.

It will be a long while before you cap wisdom, so I would put as much as possible into that. We cannot buff wisdom ourselves, and enchanters can only buff it 25-ish (?) points.

Stamina is probably the most important stat, but ogres have quite a lot of stamina anyway, plus you will be able to buff it quite much yourself pretty soon.

Max WIS, rest into STA.

Wodon
Apart from raising your agility to 75, I'd put it all in wisdom. Ogres have plenty of stamina already - I've never wanted more as a barbarian, & mine can easily reach the cap at level 59 with gear & buffs, but wisdom increases your chance to learn spell casting & trade skills, & decreases your fizzle rate significantly (fizzle rate has been parsed). Mind you, my wisdom is capped with buffs & gear at level 59 too (you can raise the cap for both wisdom & stamina after level 61, but the returns diminish greatly). The thing with starting stats is that they all can be fixed, whatever you choose, later in the game, but wisdom is the one which will get you there faster.
