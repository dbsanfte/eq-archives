Shaman (possible) useable ones from that thread:

Improved Innate Run Speed
We can all likely guess what this is; a Passive AA that increases run speed beyond Innate Run Speed 3 (which is already approaching SoW). Innate Run Speed, at a single point per level, is typically the very first AA most people obtain. Expect this one to cost more than 1 AA per level.

Improved Innate Regen
Most likely just what it says, providing 1 or more levels of higher Regeneration skills, probably at a higher cost per level than original Innate Regen, which only cost 1 AA per level.

Improved Lung Capacity
Innate Lung Capacity made players able to stay longer underwater. If the cost for the Improved version is much higher than 1 AA per level, odds are alot of people will skip this line as they did the original. It's simply too easy to get Enduring Breath (Worn) these days.

Divine Avatar
A 3-tier ability, seemingly for Shamans but listed here among likely Cleric abilities, it appears to be Activated and is, so far, slated to be a 140 point boost to STR, DEX, AGI, and ATK. Higher tiers currently show the same stats, so they will either be raised even higher, or higher skill levels will translate to shorter recast delay.

Critical Affliction
Sony has been working toward increasing the benefit of Damage Over Time spells. Now that they've added visible messages of DoT damage, as if each tick were a nuke, this ability may represent a Passive ability to give a few percentage chance to get a Critical Tick of DoT damage. It may also go into effect as the DoT lands, providing Critical Ticks for the duration of the DoT, but this is less likely.

Doppelganger
Another Activated "swarm of pets" ability, this will send several copies of the Player himself into a battle for 10 seconds. There are currently 3 levels of this ability, each adding a higher number of clones to the party.

Fury of Magic Mastery
This would seem to be an extension of Fury of Magic, a very disappointing Planar ability that increased the chances of casting critical DDs (which included a high AA price for very small percentage increases). If so, expect to see even more Critical Spells out there, at a time when Sony claims to be trying to bring melee up to already inflated caster damage quantities.

Spirit Shield
Unknown. Shield effects are typically an AC boost, Damage Shield, or Damage Absorb.

Advanced Channeling Focus
Channeling Focus allows spells to be interrupted less, this is likely an extension of that, providing even fewer interrupts.

Spirit Speed
Unknown, very likely a special form of Haste. (comment: Possibly a shaman upgrade to SoW finally?)

Enhanced Rabidity
Most likey part of the Rabid Bear/Hastened Rabidity set of Shaman skills, providing further "Rabid" benefits.

Sturdiness
Possibly an advanced form of Passive hit point increase skills.

Virulent Plague
Unknown. Most likely related to DoT damage. It would certainly be interesting to see a DoT that transfers from mob to mob, using AE dynamics. Certainly Sony hasn't been that creative here.

Suspend Minion
Already in use, this ability allows a pet to be held in suspension and brought out at need, drastically reducing pet-chaining difficulties. What change was made is unknown.

Wolf Pack
With so many Wolf Form, Spirit of Wolf, and other related spells, it's very curious that this was added. Effect unknown, and very likely Activated.
