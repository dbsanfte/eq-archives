Bruzza Bashalot
I am a Troll, so I had similar factions decisions to make.

VS: Screw it. OT is not the be-all end-all for banking now (I am bound in the Nexus, bank in SH) for us evil-types. I have been max KoS for a long time now, and I still use my hammer daily, if not more often (I love to solo in Howling Stones). When you zone in, one WEAK Skellie will aggro on you. Run toward the corner of the wall (away from the foreman and bank). Root him, invis up and run between the foreman and the workers (it can be done with no aggro, trust me), past Cliff and you are home-free. Out of 200+ hammer poofs, I have never been killed, and have only created five (spectacular!!) trains to Skyfire. You do not need VS faction IMO, and it means less and less as you work up through the 50's toward 60.

Giants: I have been Giant, then Dorf/Dragon, and now I am back on my way to Giant faction. RP is a big reason, but the Giants have better quests than the other two factions IMO, and you can still do the entire Coldain Ring quests max-KoS. However, most of Velious requires a guild effort to get any benefit from, so if you plan to stay with your guild for a while, go with whatever faction they are on.

Luclin factions: Still deciding, but leaning toward Katta for the quests so far....

Hope this helps you out! Good luck :)
