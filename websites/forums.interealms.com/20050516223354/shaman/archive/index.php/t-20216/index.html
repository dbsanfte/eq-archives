Live others have said clickies, AA and useful solo tools like a ceramic gavel of justice are what make and break root/rotting.

I normally root/rot the ogres in tactics. I usually have C5 to lessen the downtime because its boring enough with a lot of downtime. Before zoning in I refresh sow, invis and head to my place of set up. I equip my epic for the mana free dot, a ceramic gavel of justice for mana press on dots and load up the following spells.

Turgurs Insects
Canni IV
Malos
Malosinia
Malicious Decay
Bane
Q
Tnargs

Since the ogres are fairly magic resistant I pull with malicious decay and VP to start. Then I malos, malosinia, slow. Demem malosinia and malicious decay and replace them with BoS and BoU. Epic dot, Bane, BoS, then BoU. Normally VP will last pretty close to the duration but I have ornate pants so at this point I refresh root and canni 5. Then basically refresh the dots as they fade until about 15-20% and then unload spirit call 3 if its up and usually is about every third mob.

So with VP, clicky root pants, Ceramic Gavel of Justice, and Spirit Call you are saving an incredible amount of mana. BA4 and crit dots are very nice but not as critical as the first four I mentioned.
