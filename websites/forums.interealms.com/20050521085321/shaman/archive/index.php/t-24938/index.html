[QUOTE='shezam']
Oh just a little note, i have been told the closer i stand to my pet the more frequent the hits?

No. If you stand closer, you have a greater chance to see the hit messages. The farther away you stand the greater chance you will not see the hit messages.

For some reason, it appears the range to view "miss" messages it greater, ie, you can be standing in a position where you can view miss messages without seeing hit messages.
