I don't know the exact location, but it's really simple to get to. Move into NC from DS, and when you come to the first bridge on the left, take it. Just after the bridge, set up shop. Now, you are looking for a roaming Kyv. Pext Trident can spawn anywhere along that ledge you will be on between the bridge you crossed, and another bridge just a little ways past your camp. Be sure to do a /time check after you down the PH so you know when to expect 1) another PH, or 2) Pext Trident.

Him having the element of surprise is not good. After clearing the 2 static spawns just past the first bridge, and 2 just on the other side of the camp, I sit and wait for the PH to path by. Down the Kyv, and move yourself up on the hill a bit. Med/rebuff/read a book, whatever. Wait for static's to respawn. Clear them again, and get set for a possible namer encounter. If the zones not full of people, you don't have to worry about pulling him to you before someone ganks him. Just sit and wait a minute or two, and he'll path right to you.

I believe I landed slow right away, without debuff. What I did, was this. I Q'd dogdog, sent him in. Then I cast epic, Breath of Ultor, Pox of Bertox...then I got summoned. Keep Q on yourself at all times. Depending on your gear/AA's, you may need to spam an occasional Tnarg's Mending. If you have Bane, cast it too. Hold off on your Spirit Call ability until the mob is around 20% life or so. This is to help ensure when he thinks about running, and for some reason your root isn't on em,you can close out the fight in a timely manner. Any DPS is good DPS.

At this point in the fight, you've got great aggro goin on, and your DoT's are doin the work. All you need to worry about is keeping the Q's rollin, and the Bane refreshed. Good luck, and post how it goes for ya!
