izmalis
Another expansion has been announced while previous expansions still have bugs or room for improvement. While I don't normally subsidize poor design by paying for bug fixes that should be free, in this case I'll make an exception. I'd cheerfully pay for the following improvements to LDoN instead of another expansion:

- Improved Healing, Improved Damage and Mana Preservation foci purchasable as augs (both type 3 AND type 7)
- Ability to choose your adventure type (alternatively, retune them so that collects are not >>> slaughter >>>>>>> the rest)
- More type 7 augs. Not all of us want to throw away our hard earned gear for an upgrade
- A difficulty setting between normal and hard to provide a progression that's more staggered than the jump from fully slowable 300 hitters to mitigating 990 hitters.
- Refunds (at least partial) for points spent on gear that you have purchased and replaced
- Much higher points rewarded for the successful completion of a hard adventure. 104 at L65 compared to 51 for a normal is woefully inadequate due to the difficulty being much higher than 2x a normal. I'd suggest 150 - 200 at least.
- Better rewards for taking the time to search/disarm/open chests.
- Better exp in general, but especially for LDoN hards.
- Scrap the whole idea of faction and having to "unlock" themes to spend points.

Yes, I realize SOE never goes back and fixes previous expansions. This is just a wishlist that I'd pay for as it would greatly enhance my enjoyment of the game far more than a new expansion that I will likely not buy and never see if it follows the PoP -> Gates "raid or quit" concept.
