Henora
bind by Debbis the Fish.

Alice in Wonderland 4 / Stomples and Archers area is the farthest away from anything, but in the Stomples room is a just click back to the Treasure Chest room. DO NOT bind outside in the forest. You will have to run through the dispell traps and rebuff everytime you want to fight. The only zone out is to Cobalt Scar.

Exp is okay with 60 monk / 60 shaman. The mobs are really not exp mobs though, I mean - the puppets for example have over 30,000hps which takes me, monk and dog ~5-6 mins to kill usually. So it's definately not exp like killing frogs in seb every 30 seconds.

Quests - It took me a month to get the cards to make the boots. I bought one card and a friend gave me another. So in a month of fighting 3-4 hours 4+ days a week I got half the cards I needed for the boots. I was fortunate to also buy probably the last shaman doll on my server. In that same time I got enough squires for 4 squire flowers and also 3 sets for the knight flower.

Make sure you have exp in 60. I took about 10 rezless deaths in a month there. Other people are uncommon in PoM these days.

Don't fight in the garden area, it sucks. The plants all assist each other. My monk friend and I primarily fought the archers, AiW (alice in wonderland) 4 and 3. The white stallion (only slowable with Plague of Insects and resisty to that) and puppets. To keep puppets from despawning, kill the halfling Geb before Bristlebane spawns between 8 and 9am gametime.

When using your pet do not stand by a wall, do not summon him near a wall. Be prepared for him to poof/suicide on you 5-6 times per session for no apparent reason.

Good luck. I am quite happy to be done with my boots, PoM was entertaining for like 3 days.
