Ok, time for all I konw about PoJ so far.

You can meet the prisoners on Trial before seeing the Tribunal, and recieve a flag when you say you will plead their case. I reccomend doing this in case you win and need the flag.

Prisoner that I met was Mauvin. He was at the end of a long corridor of prosoners and KOS guards (the other prisoners I did not attempt to talk to, was with guild groups at the time and trying to hurry).

Once flagged, just gate/succor out to the entrance. We tried fighting out and got what we think was social aggro from the guard outside, and din't want to kill him for fear of faction hits/Judgement.

Go find the appropriate Tribunal. The prisoner hints at his quest (Mauvin was Fire) and the Tribunal will tell you the quest they guard when you ask "What Ordeal" Or similar type of statement. Offer to pleade the case of the prisoner, and when ready.....

The statement "I AM READY FOR THE TRIAL OF (fire, torture, hanging.....)" will send the GROUP of whoever said it into the trial. It is a SINGLE GROUP affair. While one group is doing a trial, or even has bodies IN a trial, nobody else can activate it.



Trial of Fire:
Keep the guardians away from the pillar in the center:
Fire guardians spawned, at least 2 of them, and hit in the 300s. I was unable to slow as the first 2 I saw aggro'd me before I saw them. They were in adjascent corners, so I assume there were 4 of them. Lacking any tanks (thought both our groups could get in, and only mine did... the backup group), we all died but I presume there are a couple waves of these fire elementals.


Trial of Hanging:
The second group went here while waiting for our bodies to get Grave-dumped. They had no flags from the appropriate prisoner, and got into the trial anway. They said there were 2 spirits up, that they could not hit, and that the prisoner died within about 10 second according to logs. Probably some trick to the trial, but no personal experience.


Trial of Torture:
While coming back to the graveyward I found out some warrior had been following me. He asked in tells how to trigger a trial, I told him, and he activated Torture. He said 2 mobs spawned, each an SK with a LOW HT value. They hit in the 360s and he survived long enough to see 2 more SKs spawn and hit him with higher HTs, and harder hits (said he lasted 45 seconds, and this was only a 52 warrior. So DPS of the mobs isn't amazing)

Sevenkilts
Mavuins flag is for all trials and it is sufficient to get flagged once with him.
Regarding Execution, it is 4 mobs per wave and not 6. Each time you kill one mob the Executioner despawns, respawns back at wall and then walks again towards the victim. kill 4 waves and the High Executioner spawns. Kill him and you are flagged to enter Plane of Valor and get a Mark of Execution. We assume once you collected the marks of all trials you can meet the 7th Hammer, but thats speculation so far.
