Well honestly i guess im more of a prizick about this, but in the long run it ends this madness... for the most part.

#1 You need to have a talk with your guild leader and have them enforce a requirment for carrying a minimizing device. I just picked one up for my SK and it was 850pp, so if you are in a raiding guild and you cant dish out that kinda low end cash for a tool, then you need think again about how much raiding means to you i think.

#2, Shrink potions. Always carry atleast 2-4 potions. The amount you really need to figure out cause its relitive to how well #1 is currently enforced. Also you should talk to the GL about being funded for this task.

#3 Talk to the guild leader about having a dedicated Gem carrier. I cant for the life of me remember what recharges the minimizing device, but whatever it is, have someone who raids often carry a stack of them and a toolbox.

#4 There has to be a cut off on shrinking and buffs for that matter. Obviously this isnt a iron clad situation where you should be a jerk to anyone asking you for buffs. But there is a point in the raid where you spam /rs Last call for buffs and shrink. Let me be clear this is the LAST CALL. After that they are on their own or you can hand them potions for shrinks. Which are a pain to get back sometimes, but hey, it beats the headache of leaving a group and trying to get someone to re-invite you. I think thats a big problem, people just dont understand how much of a pain in the arse they cause.

Anyway, thats how i handle things. YOu might think im being a jerk, but really thats not it. Im trying to help people learn how to work as a team and to think about the problems they cause when they dont do the simple things asked of them by the guild. Like a said, 850 isnt alot of a shrinker and if you have a guildie that can make one, its even cheaper.
