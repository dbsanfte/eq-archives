Bigguss
For the next 10-15 levels, what will help you the most is a good-ratio weapon. Incremental increases in AC, HP, or WIS will help a little, too... but more and faster damage are your friends right now.

You'll want to keep both piercing and your blunt skills trained, and make sure to keep your defense skill maxed as you level. Here are a few recommendations for cheap weapons (ideally you'll want one piercer, one one hander blunt, and one two hand blunt... of course that's ideally...)

Piercing:

Bloodstained fang (Dmg 9, Dly 32) - sells for next to nothing (10 pp?)

Rukgus's sticker (Dmg 6, Dly 25, +3 Str, +2 Wis, +2 Int) - probably under 100 pp

Ebon war spear (Dmg 8, Dly 31, Rangeable, +4 Str, +3 Wis, +5 vsMg) - probably 100-200 pp, this one is nice because when you upgrade you could move it to your range slot to continue to benefit from the stats.

Bettle stinger (Dmg 8, Dly 23, +4 Agi, +4 Dex, +4 Wis) - 100-200 pp)

Spine chill spear (Dmg 10, Dly 28, +3 Sta, +6 Wis, +10 vsCold) - 200-400 pp

Slime coated harpoon (Dmg 10, Dly 25, Rangeable, +5 Wis, +10 Mana) - 200-500 pp

One hand blunt:

Screaming mace (8 dmg, 35 dly, Effect of Screaming Mace), Cold iron morning star (9 dmg, 35 dly), Enamelled black mace (Dmg 8, Dly 28) - These are old-world weapons, now make nice lower level weapons, and are probably around 50-100 pp

Wraith bone hammer (Dmg 8, Dly 26, +5 Str, +2 Wis, +2 Int), Sharkbone Warhammer (Dmg 8, Dly 26, +5 Wis), Velium Etched Stone Mace (Dmg 7, Dly 22, +3 Str, +3 Wis), or Centi warhammer (Dmg 15, Dly 32- great ratio!, +5 Str, +5 Sta, +2 Wis) - 100-200 pp

Smoldering Brand (Dmg 9, Dly 22, +3 Str, Effect of Burn from level 1 - NICE weapon), Ykeshan War Club (Dmg 9, Dly 24, Effect of Ykesha from level 37) - 200-500pp

Two hand blunt:

Drakkel Forged Sledgehammer (Dmg 22, Dly 50) or Gatorsmash Maul (Dmg 30, Dly 70) - 50-100 pp

Drakkel Icegrinder (Dmg 27, Dly 45, Effect of Ice Rend) - 200-400pp

Shield: (throwing this in there since they can provide nice AC/HP very cheaply)

Bark shield (+15 AC, +5 Agi), Chitin shell shield (+15 AC, +10 vsPos), Crystal webshield (+15 AC, +10 vsCold, +20 HP, +20),
Crustacean shell shield (+17 AC, +4 Str, +10 Sta, -7 Cha), Velium round shield (+15 AC, +10 vsCold) - 50-150 pp

Clay guardian shield (+25 AC, +50 HP) - 200-500 pp

Other:
4 AC/45 HP, 5 AC/55 HP, or Adamantite bands (75 HP, -10 vs magic) can generally be found cheap (offer 50 pp...)

Basically, don't sweat it. Banded and a nice weapon are all you really need until well into your 30s. :)
fudorf
Things to look for in the inexpensive range, (some of which, if like me, you will be wearing well into your 40's, and maybe 50's)

Gatorscale Leggings - Decent AC, and a decent Wis boost for low/mid levels (leggings are the shamans worst nightmare) - generally go in the 500pp area. Have seen for less.

(Still wearing this, at 53) Giant Woven Vest - 18ac, +STR/HP/svCold - AC is weak at my level, but the price on this is dropping through the floor. Have seen it on vendors for less that 400pp... and HP=Mana after a while.

Imbued Ivory Collar - again, nice balance of AC and wis, although a little more costly - about 1k, but have seen it auctioned at less than 300, so keep your eyes open.

Chipped Bone Bracers - Again, AC and Wis. And it makes your dot's a little more effective... anywhere from 300 ea to 4-500 a pair.

Imbued Ivory Earing (presuming you are a follower of the Tribunal) - once again, AC and Wis, with a little sv cold... about 1-300 ea, depending on seller.

There are loads of other things you can look at. If you plan on being primarily a tradeskiller, you will want all the wis gear you can get. With smithing, you will also want all the STR gear you can get, but that is unimportant at higher levels (when you can self buff to 255 with no problem). If you plan to be a raid shaman, at some point, with a high end guild, you will want to start building to AC/HP gear. No hurry there, as you have 40 or so levels to worry about it. If you are just going to be the 'average' shaman, in a nice small/mid sized guild, and not concerned about ToV, Planes, etc, go for a nice mix of AC/HP and wis gear.
