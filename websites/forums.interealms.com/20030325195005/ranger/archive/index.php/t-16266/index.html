At 50 gain a level. You cannot work on AA until you hit 51.

AA is very much a matter of playstyle and preference. The general consensus is that in general, level > AA. Depending on where you do your xp'ing, however, it might be worthwhile to hit 51 and then work on AA until you max out runspeed.

I'm catching up to a formula I'm gonna be following until I hit 60; get 1 AA in 51 get 2 in 52 get 3 in 53 and so on. Whether this is optimal, I doubt many here would agree but I'm having fun doing it and thats what counts.
RedChief
Swiftwind or not, you will have a problem getting a group in PoP until your mid 50's. Lower level rangers are considered mana sinks. I know that I have problems getting groups by myself (54 ranger). it is only when I duo a 60 shamen I can find a group.

As to AA, it is much easier to get AA at 60, or even 55 then it is at 51. The benefits of getting to 55 out way the AA you could do in the same time period.
