Hmm..if I remember correctly the barbarian vendors in FV are on Halas merchant faction. Halas faction is tough to get. There are quests you can do that gan generate good faction with Halas, but if you cant go quests you are stuck killing things.

You can kill gnolls in Everfrost for faction. BB also gives positive hits if I remember corectly.

If you go to East Freeport, between about 9pm and 5am game time you'll see Giz Dinree, a dark elf who stands where Bait Masterson stands in the daytime. She's about level 16. She'll spawn about 9pm, if she's been killed, she'll respawn about 2am. Killing her gives good faction with Wolves of the North (Halas warrior faction) and some other assorted faction hits (Bad DE rogue faction too). Once you kill her enough so that the Halas guards and guild masters wont make you into a snowcone, you can turn in gnoll teeth to warrior GM for good faction with most of Halas.

I say most because the rogue faction in Halas is really screwey, and many factions that raise other factions in Halas will lower Rogue factions. Not usually a problem except that the Halas banker isnt on Normal Banker faction, but on Halas Rogue faction. So good factions with all of Halas except rogue= shopping/training but no banking.

Halas factions are almost harder than Neriak factions.....
