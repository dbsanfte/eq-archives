At 60 soloing Nurga is decent AA and the cash is good, so should be fine at 55. Might even be decent regualr xp at 55. If it's not switch to AA xp while farming for cash. A named rat drops some Plate Boots of Light that I sold for 1k on Vazaelle. Droga is definately harder and probably not soloable at 55. Nothing really seems to see invis much. You can invis into droga and setup camp in this building that has a side room with a named or PH for him. He drops a nice rogue dagger as a common drop and a belt for a rare drop. Then you can pull from outside the room inside. Get a group and get some xp and maybe some drops from the named.
paladiin
I took my 59 monk/55 shammy combo down to Nurga to check it out. Really I wanted to see if I could get the flame dancer beads for the EEIII quest item.

Could invis as stated with no problems. Got back to where the beads drop. Mobs still dk blue to 55 (supposed to be like 49-50 there). They were significantly harder than spiders in Velks of similar level. HP seems high (at least 2x velks spider hp), hits were fairly hard though I could keep up, and the casters were a pain. Nearly everything is a caster! Oh, and mobs run - this is very bad, as root tends to break way too much. Agro range is small, but they will assist if you get a running mob too close.

I personally just gave up. XP per kill wasn't bad, but with the mobs being significantly harder deep in, it just wasn't worth it in terms of downtime and potential death.

Never went back and tried the entrance mobs (who were also dark blue to 55 iirc). Though I expect the outcome would have been the same. I've reached the conclusion that both these zones are group goodie bags - just go for the loot.
