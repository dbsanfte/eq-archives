Chutoi
Mystical arbiter of earth in PoEa gives you a passkey of the 12 (PoEb key). You can check it by typing /key to see if it shows up.

You must hail the planar projection from killing each of:

Fennin Ro
Xegony
Coirnav
Avatar of Earth

The Avatar of Earth is spawned by killing the Rathe Council in a set period of time. (7 minutes, I think--it's been like a year since I did a rathe raid).

Hailing each of the four PPs gets you an item. The item is equipable as a shield (with a cool graphic!), though it has no stats (sorry, don't remember names--been too long).

When you get all four shields, you combine them in a special jar. The jar is a real bitch to make, mostly because it requires uncommon/rare items from each of the various zones pre-time. The good news is that you only need 1 per guild--it's not destroyed in the combine, and it's tradeable.

When you have combined your key, then you have to zone into time via the PoInnovation. Find someone who is flagged to open the factory door. In the first room, go LEFT (not right as you would to the MB). Go talk to the NPC robot and say "yes" when he asks if you're ready for transport.

It transports you to a zone with basically 1 path. Go to the big, cool-looking machine in the middle and click it. Welcome to PoTime.

You can now click the sundial in PoTranq for access to PoTime at will. You don't need to keep your key with you--you can bank it.
