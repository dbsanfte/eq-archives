Einnod
When I choose to solo here, I will normally pull from the earth wing. Mostly because it's easiest to pull solos here. All the melee giants(krigers, militis, and tyv) will summon. The profets and the elementals will not summon. Profets are shaman, and they will malo you and attempt to dot you or nuke ya. They'll also gate when low on health, so be careful.

On the elementals, I'll just root/dot em. On the giants I'll tank em in teh face and have my pet beat on em.

Each wing is resistant to different types of spells.

The wind wing is highly PR, but dots will land. Also they are somewhat resistant to other spells.

Water wing is completely cold and poison resistant.

Earth wing is completely disease resistant(no VP)

Fire wing is resistant to fire(don't effect us unless we nuke em down with burst of flame!)

Hope this'll help ya.

There are realy only 3 places where a shaman can solo in BoT with any kind of efficiency:

Courtyard
Easy kills, all our spells can land
Easy singles

South wing
Harder kills, forget about trying to land disease based spells
Easy singles

West wing
Easier kill then in South, but harder then in CY. Everything lands except fire based spells (Like we care about that).
Harder to get singles unless you are used to pulling.

I don't remember if the stuff in CY summons, but the giants do for sure. So you gotta be able to tank these if you wanna solo here.

I don't recommend doing North or East cause most of our spells are resisted there.
