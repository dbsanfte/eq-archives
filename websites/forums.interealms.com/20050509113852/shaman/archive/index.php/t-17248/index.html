Each time you are seeing this message, "sorry master this is not a legal target", there has been a /pet attack command issued without a valid target. You should get a, "Attacking a rat master", after your pet has been told to attack a valid target. Its just the invalid Target message tends to be more noticeable. Now the question is why is this command being issued when your not attually wanting it to be.

Questions to ask yourself.

1. Does it happen to any selected target.
2. Does it only happen with one group member or all of them.
3. Does it happen as soon as you target a NPC/Group member.
4. Does it happen at some point after target a NPC/Group member.
(Example: Target mob [nothing] then start to cast/move pet attacts.)
5. What keys have you pressed up to 6 sec prior to you recieving the pet attack/unable to attack messages.
6. Are you using any macros for target selection / announcements.
7. Have you linked your left mouse button in windows to any keyboard keys? or have any windows accessibility options enabled?

Macros have a max run time of like a min or so I forget the exact time. Would be best to test this in a zone full green mobs with no GUARDS/HIGH Level NPCs near so you can detirmin exactly what is going on. Then do some tests and see if you can answer those questions. If its just happening with group members you might want to see if you can get a friend or two to join you for 10 mins or so.

One thing to note pets can be given commands from normal conversaion if you happen to speak the key words in range of them. So if you say, "Joe, have you ever had your pet get lost in this zone before?". Your wonderfull little pet is going for a nice dirt nap (You just told it to get lost).

Most likely though you have a couple of comands sharing a common key. You'll mainly just need to watch and see exactly when your pet is acting up and figure out what key is causing him to attack. I personally have a Hotbutton with assit and then pet attack on it. I put a 3-4 second delay from the time I do the assist and the time my pet attacks so that I have time to then spam a pet back off hotbutton if I dont wish for my pet to attually attack.
