Hitpoints always will be king.

Most mobs you run into are going to be geared towards whooping on tanks who have far more AC than we will ever get. Hopefully it will be the tanks on the sharp end and not you. PoS giants don't care if you have 800AC or 1800AC because they are still going to kill you solo. At least more hp gives you a buffer and time to recieve a heal, land a slow, or gate.

Also keep in mind that hitpoints don't get debuffed like AC/resists.
