Sarita Icestryke
You can kill HGs starting at 34. I know, that is when I started. Now, I'm not going to tell you that it is worth it till around 40, but at 34 with dogdog we have the capacity to solo the giant wonders. At 34 I didn't have great gear or anything, just tactics. Never be afraid to zone if the fight starts off bad (a couple resists, etc).

Here is what I did to solo.
1. Buff and park pet with taunt turned off. You want him setting off out of the way as you don't want him to come running if you do get hit right away.
2. Go find a HG and I always started with root.
3. debuff the HG. Malise spell (can't remember which one was available at 34.) you may want to cast your smallest one as they aren't THAT overly resistant and you can't afford the lost mana.
4. poison, disease if root is still sticking.
5. basically go into kite mode until you have enough aggro that you have the aggro.
6. run by your dog and sick him on the giant.
7. canabalize/med all you can as you stay away from the giant and refresh dot's as necessary. if you need to, root the giant to cast those spells.
8. I don't care just how well you've done up towards the end, but at some point you will be out of mana and won't have a really good chance to make up enough mana to cast more spells. This is the point that you have to judge the situation. If you are still holding aggro, you can continue to let the dog nip away at the HGs heals, otherwise, you will need to go into melee mode. Having decent melee abilities is crucial as when you finish, odds are you will be out of mana and down to a bubble of health.

At 34, it took me 10 times before I ever got the hang of this. Then I was only about 50% until I hit 35. At 35 the ease went up as did my success rate. Went to about 75%. By 40% I might have to zone 1 in 20.

I started HGs in Rathe off and on at 39 when I got VoS and a decent pet. Its expensive to do solo so I really try to group or partner with another class if possible.

My tactic:

- Summon high conning pet (max hit 35)
- Buff myself and pet
- load 125mana slow, scourge, VoS, EB, root, greater heal
- quickness on pet
- pull with slow
- let pet tank for 10-15 seconds while i stack DoTs
- Reapply VoS when it fades
- finish with EB
