Inner Acrylia is good. Just outside the Jails you can set up there and be in a spot where you have a chance of 3 different named spawning. High Pirest is a roamer, Archanist is in the room with the pot (his PH is on the Pot actually) and then you have the Arch Sage (or Mage... something like that) that spawns in the jail room between both cells.

Their loot still sells for 3-10k or so each. Lots of Acrylia ore drops and the runes sell for a nice bundle.

High priest can also drop a Regen mask that I believe a SK can wear. No level requirment or recommendation either.
