Giac
Delay Death is a great AA for a tank.

Without Delay Death a character dies at HP -11 iirc. From 0 to -10 he's unconscious(might be wrong on the numbers).

Delay Death adds 50 HPs for each rank of it that you can drop below the -10.
So in theory you wil still be alive at -260, in practice, however, you will still be unconscious as soon as you drop to 0 or below. When you are unconscious, you can't do anything, which for a Shaman is rather crippling.
A tank's job, however, is to tank and being unconscious doesn't affect this ability. That's why it's great for a tank and not so great for a Shaman.
On raids Dealy Death can come in handy, however, if you have people who actually assist heal on your raids, you might stay alive thanks to outside heals when you'd already be dead thanks to the 250 HP buffer.

Being a fan of extra abilities instead of improvements to existing ones this would be my list:

1. Mnemonic Retention - Because the only thing that is better then extra abilities is your choice of an extra ability.

2. Call of the Wild - This will save you about 984358349 hours of CR eventually.

3. Spirit Channeling - Because I love being able to keep up my group contribution while regaining mana. Because I love chaincasting heals for ages. Because I love scaring clerics with channeling -> MGB Focus -> canna 5.

4. Ancestrail Aid 1 - Any group heal is good, any instant cast no mana group heal is better.

5. Spell Casting Subtlety - Look, a GoD AA skill on the list too!

6. Mystical Attuning - Raiders only pretty much, but very nice for them.

In no particular order:
- Reflexive Mastery
- Defensive Instincts
- Healing Adept Mastery
- Healing Gift Mastery
- Healing Boon
- Resplendent Cure
... because becoming better at good things can't be all bad.

- avoidance AAs
