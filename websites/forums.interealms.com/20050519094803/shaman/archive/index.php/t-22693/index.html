Goldark
Played for about 2-3 months, in that timer I completely mastered Artisan and Weaponsmith, raked up more cash than I knew what to do with, and killed the most powerful mobs in the game with some friends.

Eng-game content is really lacking; character progression stops very early on as there's nothing to do once you've spent all your skill points, and 99% of your gear will be purchased from crafters so there's little demand to raid mobs for anything other than modifier components to give to your local friendly crafter.

On the plus side, its probably great for more casual gamers. The graphics are lovely, character customisation is amazing (facial features, clothing etc), and SWG is after all Star Wars. Some of the planets are very nicely made with sunsets and sandstorms that are really engaging. But its all fluff to me, the core gameplay is what I look for.

SOE have been planning a massive reworking of the entire combat system, a first-hand admission that its not working at the moment - yet somewhat mind-bogglingly, this has been postponed until after the expansion is release. Money grabbing at its finest IMO.
