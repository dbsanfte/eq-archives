Nerun Starblade
Shrink twice, stand behind him in the corner to avoid him pushing you with melee, and break invis by casting slow.

Then, unleash hell with your dots - I usually just use BoS and Ancient Bane, but I also have Bane now, so it will be even easier with that. I usually end fight at about 50 mana. You'd be amazed how far he can move without agroing the others in the room. Agro range is tiny in droga - I love it.

Loot hammer if it wasn't a PHer, reinvis, and run to next PHer to clear it too.
