Kriel Darkbane
Shamans have the same heals spell as a cleric from 19-23, missing out (now) only on the cleric's 5% bonus. Before 19 a shaman's heals are weak, but that's generally still your role in a group. It's frustrating being a shaman healer before 19, but that's usually the role you're stuck in. From 19-23 it's something you're good at. Enjoy being a healer while you can do it well, that won't last forever. ;)
