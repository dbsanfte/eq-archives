stormrazor
You can actually do some these quests quite early, I did some pieces at level 22. These quests require gems and no drop items combined with banded armor. I went and asked if I could loot the no drop items I needed since they are useless to everyone but a Iksar Shaman and before I knew it I had Scaled Mystic BP, Boots, Cloak, and Leggings. All before level 25.
I wore some banded and Rhino Hide until I got Scaled Mystic.
You can wear Scaled Mystic for quite a while some have even into their 50's. Weapons were the only problem I had with my Shaman (Iksar). I was lucky enough to be hunting in the Sarnak fort when a 56 mage did a shout for anyone who wanted to loot the Jade Prod for the Chancellor. I was set until I could make my way to EC to buy a Drakkel Sledgehammer (bad weapon) delay sucks on it, even with haste its horrible.

The cudgel quests are crappy, but the 8th cudgel is a nice range weapon with a right click effect (I think) called Carnage. So do those if you like questing.

here is a link to the quests: Scaled Mystic quests: (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/adv_search.html?stn=Scaled+Mystic)
