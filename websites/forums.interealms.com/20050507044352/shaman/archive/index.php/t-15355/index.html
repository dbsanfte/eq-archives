Krakster, they drop in NW and NE corners of HoH. Normal MOBs are the undead black armors but sometimes a white-armored one will pop in their place. These rarely drop the Windblade or Girdle of Mithaniel. I'd recommend the NW top camp if it's open because it has the most black armor MOBs so better chance of a white armor popping. Basements suck unless you're stuck soloing/duoing.

If you want the War Bladed Marshall's Staff, it drops fairly commonly from the Stampeding Piglet in Tactics. If you can't make a Tactics group, the piglet is soloable if you don't mind sitting on your butt waiting for the stampede.
