qhor
Another vote for the Dreadlands. It's not great experience per mob
but the mobs abound. Being an "anti-uber" you may not like depending
on KEI; I however dislike grinding and if I need to drop 50p every three
hours to double my kill rate, I'll do it without hesitating.

That being said, I'd advise buffing yourself up, getting a KEI and racing
(or getting a TL) to the DL. Do levels 40, 41 and 42 in one or all of the
three Yeti caves. The mobs don't hit particularly hard, should give 2%
apiece at level 40 and drop halfway decent platinum and flowers that
sell OK. See eqatlas' map of DL for locations.

43 to 49 chill around KC, I prefer the southeast ruin myself. DL's been
pretty empty of late on Lanys with the Planes and LoY and whatnot.
Pull, in this order: Cockatrices, Drovlargs, Spiders. Avoid ravishing
drovlargs. If your defense is lagging, take the time to fight stuff toe to
toe and let your pet nibble on it from behind. It'll be a bit slower
leveling but the melee skillups are worth it. If your skills are
satisfactory, pull with slow, sic pet with taunt on (sidenote: Keep your
pet regen/hasted. When you get HoS give it to him as well. If you have
the clickable Jaundiced Bone pieces with strength and dexterity buffs,
use them on pet. If not, no big deal. Also make sure pet is dual
wielding). Layer DoT's and let pet tank, rooting the mob if you pull too
much aggro.

This method netted me around 60% of a level per KEI I think. Avoid
groups unless they have a cleric, enchanter, high-damage melees and
someone who knows how to chain pull. Elsewise solo experience rates
are better.

When you hit 49 you'll have more options on places to hunt. BW, HK,
PoJ or PoI groups perhaps. Envenomed Bolt is a great DoT. HoS is a
great buff. And so on.

Best of luck to you. If you have other questions post 'em.

39-46 I hung out in Crystal Caverns. I spent 39-40 killing orcs until I had enough faction to sell to the dwarfs there (everyone starts dubious), then switched to terrors/geos until 46. There are 4 terrors around a broken bridge that are single pulls, cake if you slow, send pet and tank/dot. There are also 2 more a little farther inside in the great hall. Single pullable, same routine to kill. Also if you can get the geo ramp, they are awesome till 46. Single pulls, plenty to kill, super loot (named geos drop gems that sell for 15-190pp). They do assist and run downhill when low on health, but if you start from the bottom of the ramp and work your way up you'll never have an issue with runner adds.

It was a nice relaxing time, vendors and bank in zone so you never had to run far for a bank run. Also, the dwarfs act as guards so if you get in trouble, just hoof it to their town. 9 times out of 10 they'll kill whatever is beating on you before it kills you. :)

Once I hit 46, I went to City of Mist and did the 9 entrance mobs and 6 courtyard mobs (once I got a couple levels). Stayed there till I hit 51, then did the gnomes in Echo Caverns till 52 (my current level).

Go fight the gnome guards in echo caverns. The xp in there rocks. there are 4 guards and 1 single ( harold the herald ) you can pull. 1% xp each mob. You'll need to pull with root because you can't single pull the guards till 49.

Pull by rooting a guard.
The other guard will come.
Sick scooby on the guard that comes (give him a couple weapons)
slow
dot
melee
take on other guard same way.

Move up to the left and rinse/repeat with the next 2 guards.

Move up and pull harold the herald with a slow (stay up against left wall and get him just in your view, that way you don't get adds ) run back down to where you fought the second set of guards and follow the above.

Move back down to first pull spot and med

Any hp buffs you can get prior to going in will make this easier at lower levels, but you can easily get through the 40's in here.

Also, close to zone if things go bad. close to bank, vendors, kei, etc. CR is very simple if necessary.

You take a faction hit to the kingdom of above / below, but I've yet to find a place or read a post where this is a bad thing

No loot, only pp so you won't fill your bags with junk.

It's a side path that is a dead end so you should never get trained

Also, just beyond the path to the gnomes (going straight ahead as you enter the zone ) you'll find a camp with 4 bugs. They are far enough apart that you can single pull them with slow and are also decent xp. it's on the main path though so there is a slight potential for adds.

The room outside the TM is also a pretty good spot to single pull but the named bug can spawn in there occassionally and he'll ruin your day

Hope this info helps
