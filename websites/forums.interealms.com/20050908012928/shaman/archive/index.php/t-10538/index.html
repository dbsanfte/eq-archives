Juujuu
The zone is interesting to go there once for an adventure in hunting in a new place.
It's really bad for xp though.
At level 60 you have green mobs hitting you for 120s and rampaging/flurrying from time to time (though very infrequently if they do actually do either).
You also have random mobs that are immune to slows.
The xp is bad since all the mobs in zone are overpowered for their level.

As you travel around the zone, you have set spawns of mobs, but they only account for about 2/3 of all the mobs in zone.
The zone is full of "traps" that aren't traps rogues can deal with, but instead just traps that spawn mobs for you to kill for more xp.
Occasionally you encounter some really rough mobs, like the ones that hit for 230+ and are immune to slows. They suck when you get 2+ in camp.
The mobs in zone don't have set character models for their names.
I think that the mobs look different on each persons screen.
Many of the mobs in zone will proc a random character type illusion onto the target.
The illusion that other group members see is not the same as what you see, so the whole illusion thing adds a new twist to combat.

There's a few named in zone worth tracking down and killing, plus theirs grieg himself.
If you do alchemy, you want the +% alchemy stone from the gnome chick (she doesn't always drop it. HUGE regen and large HPs).
Beer goggles is a common drop off a named mob.
There's an ok mask with 15% intimidation on it if you have a rogue/monk that solos a bunch.
There's a 13/20 1h slasher scythe that necros can equip with good hp and int on it on another named. It procs summon meat (can't eat the meat, but necros can convert it to bone chips with AA skill).
That named can also drop a paly/cleric weapon that procs divine aura, and another weapon with remove greater curse on it.
There's also some fairly bad weapon off Servitor? that has FT1 or FT2 on it, but I don't know what else he drops.

Grieg himself drops some nice stuff.
He's got a good range item with teleport to Dreadlands on it.
He drops ornate horse bridles.
He drops FT3 gloves with 35hp/mana.
He drops a belt with stats similar to Dain belt with haste.
He drops a plate BP with ok stats, but has 30wis on it (really good for palys low on wis).
He drops 50hp/mana boots with +% channelling skill.
He has a few other drops too I think, but I can't remember what they are.
If your guild is capable of killing stachoo/vindy without difficulties, you should be able to do Grieg.
Grieg fight is a long fight.
It's also freakishly annoying considering he procs blind, so the main tank is blinded for about 95% of the whole fight.
