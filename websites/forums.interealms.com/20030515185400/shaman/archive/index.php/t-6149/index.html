Zatriz
I don't have a map I can link, but I can try to explain it.

Go to the south with and work your way inwards. Go down the little incline and turn left. You'll come to a room with a rug on a dias (trap). From there go left again. At the T intersection, turn right and there is an illusionary wall just as you turn right. Turn right and go through the illusionary wall. As you go down the tunnel you'll see a hole in the wall. Just past the hole on the left is the Iksar Apparation. Behind it is another hole in the wall that is actually a 1 way wall.

Hope that helps. Oh, and beware all the HT mobs in there. :)
