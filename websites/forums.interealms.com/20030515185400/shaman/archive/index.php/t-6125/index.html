Gloriana
I know you say you're a soloing wis shaman, but I've found the game really changes with 51 and Turgur's. Turgur's has a couple of results:

1) The power of Turgur's makes you very valuable to groups.
2) Increased taunt factor of turgur's. The spell has a tendancy to really make mobs mad.
3) Related to 2, mobs you encounter as you hunt in the 50+ range start hitting really really hard.

With level 52 and regrowth and level 53 and Can III, hitpoints will become increasingly important to you as a method of mana regen. Your max mana pool may be less important to you than your speed in regenning mana.

I made a transition from wisdom shaman to more AC-oriented shaman in my early 50s (I became a hitpoint junkie after hitting 60) so I do hear what you're coming from, I just have the experience of playing both a wis shaman and an AC shaman to base my advice.

Some basic gear advice:

1) You said you have about 60K to spend (10K for EDST, 50K for other stuff). I would encourage you to spend the whole chunk on a fungi tunic. I know that sounds kinda wacky, but it's very very powerful. Of all the gear I've looted, it's the only piece of gear that I put on and experienced immediate greater ability. (JBB would be the other contender).

2) Mask - crystal spider eyes and enshrouded veil both offer a bit of wisdom while adding a chunk of AC AND mana and hitpoints. Enshrouded veil should be VERY cheap on your server, it runs less than 1K on mine.

3) Replace the rings with 2 velium fire opal or 1 velium fire opal and 1 shadel bandit ring. I know this is a big loss of wisdom, but the addition of 130 hitpoints is a VERY good thing.

4) Shield slot - a Charred Guardian Shield would give you 10 AC at the loss of one wisdom. There are some higher priced shields would be very nice for you (with wisdom) Crystal chitin or bone braced buckler (pricey).

5) Legs - we just don't have a lot of good leg choices. Since you're a barb, I would suggest no drop jarsath from KC. You can try to loot a pair (since they are no drop they can rot) or get a basement group. They are the common drop from the undead jailer. Brown chiton is also a choice as well as the GD quest legs (can never remember the name)

6) Belt - chitinous belt or spider fur belt, both should be very cheap.

7) Shoulders - death watcher shroud (very nice, but might be pricey)

8) Wrists - dragon hide from WW

If it's any consolation to you, your gear in many places is MUCH better than what I was wearing at 51, never say never about gear/loot, you just don't know what's going to happen next in this game!
