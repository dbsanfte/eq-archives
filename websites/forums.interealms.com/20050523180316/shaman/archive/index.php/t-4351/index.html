Aktunka
The troll smithed shields are going to cost you more than 5k hehe. It takes 2 blue diamonds per combine attempt, and there are 3 different combines where each of those can be lost.

1st combine is pottery to make them into blue diamond powder.
2nd combine is brewing making celestial essence
3rd combine is combining them with a sheet of folded acrylia.

After you are past that, then the actual shield combine attempt is made, and they are trivial above 250. The success rate is not great and is ruled by the RNG. Also, another component of this is made with ground spawns from Innothule swamp and in the swamp its a pain in the butt to find them.

Your best bet at getting them at a reasonable price? Gather all of the items required, have someone do the pottery and brewing parts for you so thats all done, and find a troll and just ask him to do the smithing parts. If you have done all the leg work and are putting up all the items, then he shouldn't charge you too much. maybe up to 2k or so, not sure. One nice thing is that you will be fairly unique on your server. For example, only ONE vengeful shield is in existence, and I am the one that made it hehe. Dorfeatr has a shield that makes him look different from all the other level 60 shaman out there (it has MM shield graphic hehe). Anyhow, good luck.
