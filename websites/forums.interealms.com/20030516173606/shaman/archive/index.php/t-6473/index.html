Huksh
I am a vah shir and was in the same boat as you around that level..not really a very high wis but I really didn't find it to be a problem. Once you get a few more levels and can hunt in the arena, POG or even hate you should start getting some decent upgrades.

My suggestions are:
-Can't beat an iksar regen bp if you can swing the cost.
-See if maybe a caster that can wear the grey flesh gloves might have a pair of shissar guants that they would trade with you.
-Earrings - the 2ac, 25hps, 25 mana ones ( black spahire?)
-Face - enshrouded veil if you can find it cheap
-Legs - not sure of the value on those but they seem to fetch a fair price maybe you can sell them and use the plat to finance some of the other things mentioned here. Kylong Greaves have decent ac and are cheap, should hold you for a while.
-Shield - definatly pick up a SBS, much better ac, saves and hps. Or if you can find one cheap a skull marked shield is a great value.
-Back slot - tough call, not a lot of really good cheap cloaks.

Hope this helps. Good Luck.
