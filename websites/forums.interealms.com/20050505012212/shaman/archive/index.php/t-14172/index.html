Drajjen
I have been PL'ing a necro also so I figured I would share what I did.

Go to Guk top. I know according to everyone there is no ZEM there, but I have to think there is and i'll tell you why.

At lvl 20 my necro was getting 6 to 7 percent per kill or better down near the "Old Froglok". I moved on about lvl 26 to the ancient croc area and got 5 percent and better off the crocs and pulling the frogs outside his room. This took me all the way to 34.

My plan was to move to Lower Guk at 34 since I would have the new pet and a little extra dmg. I moved down and what do you know, the frogs in Lower Guk, that were yellow actually gave less xp than the blue frogs in upper guk. I zoned out and killed some more blue frogs and would get around 3% xp, then zoned back into Lguk and kill a yellow frog and get 2% xp. At any rate, I have to believe there is a ZEM in Upper Guk.

Once you leave upper guk head to Gunthak and camp the beach or go to the first small town (not the one leading to Dulak). On the beach there is a lot of undead and your necro will be able to nuke to his/her hearts content, just use the shaman to pull with slow, sic pet, and nuke, if mob aggros you FD and pop right back up and nuke some more.

The xp here may not be as good as some other places but the loot is not bad at all and you will have the opportunity to get your evok skill up quite a bit on the beach.

The town area is much harder (because you will get over pulls and ST gets resisted a bunch) but the xp at that spot is fantastic.

mgbaron2
Upper Guk does in fact have a 12% ZEM. It was left out of the original Producers Letter that listed the other ZEM's but was mentioned in the following patch. Refer to the Patch History on Castersrealm.com if anyone wants to see for themselves.

From the January 17, 2001 Patch:

"Changes/Enhancements

While not mentioned in the Producer's Letter, the Zone Experience Modifier has also been increased in Guk (Upper Guk), by roughly 12%."

My experience per kill has been roughly: dark blue = 3-4%, white/black = 6-7%, yellow = 8-9%, red = 10-11%. This was observed as a Level 10-20 Dwarf paladin and a Level 10-20 & 32-37 Troll shaman.

Hope this helps.
