Gloriana
It starts in SS, you get a toolkit from Leuz.

Then, you head on out to WW, Scout Charisa spawns every 12 hours or so, a bit up and behind the SG zone (she's a little human looking enchanter).

Turn in the toolkit to Charisa and a bunch of giants and a Giant Captain spawn. Use harmony or good pulling to get the giant captain solo. He is super MR, but can be slowed with Plague of Insects (he loves that!) One very strong group can do this (cleric and a tanky type is a MUST).

When the Giant Captain dies, he drops a mirror, take the mirror to Charisa to cycle through her rewards. First she gives you a robe, hand it back and she gives you a bracer, hand it back and she gives you the lantern.

Once the Charisa quest has been done, she doesn't despawn automatically but she has a bag in her hand, that lets you know the quest has been done and can't be done again until she respawns bagless (Charisa up NO BAG is a pretty common shout in my guild).

You also get some kind of plans, which I think you can turn back to Leuz for more faction.

The key to Charisa is MOBILIZATION. She spawns pretty much every 12 hours and lots of guilds have folks looking out for Charisa. I don't know the camping etiquette on your server, but one poor shaman sitting by Charisa is not enough to make other guilds back down on Emarr. Whoever gets there first with a fighting force to do the quest, turns in the piece and starts the quest.

A good idea is to get a really strong group exping in SG (gem drops are nice) with someone popping out to take a peek for Charisa occasionally.

Good luck, Scout Charisa was a bit of an obsession with me! Check Allakhazam for a more coherent quest walkthrough.

Henora
The Talisman of Benevolence is nice, but I don't think it should be the deciding factor in your faction choice. Luclin has introduced good range items up the wazoo.

For example, the Divine Intervention, which I use.

Divine Inspiration +15 AC, +15 Wis, +15 Int, +35 Mana

Talisman of Benevolence +7ac +15wis +12 Magic/Cold Resist +38HP +55Mana

You got a 20 extra mana, 38 hps, some good saves, but also 8 less ac from Talisman... I'd say they are reasonably comparable, considering the differences in level of difficulty to obtain.

The Divine Inspiration drops in Maidens Eye, the guy who drops it is even soloable (I did it at 58, without my epic) but hard enough. He is a rareish spawn you can camp while doing exp... which is nothing like Benevolence, which involves racing to WW, fighting with other people about triggering, requiring a defensive warrior and cleric, etc.

My advice is if your guild is doing giant faction, do giant faction. Everyone and their brother is in HoT these days, West is a lot more open anyways on my server.
