Grafsha McDaughts
If you have a mule or open character slot (to make a mule) use it in the bazaar. Drop everything there you don't want that someone else might and spend a couple hours a day in trader mode.

Watch the bazaar for spells and prices. There is great fluctuation. A guild shaman used this advice and picked up both Q and Malos for a total of 20k. It took him a couple of weeks watching the bazaar when he could while he spent his playing time collecting cash and sellable drops to build up his bank. His goal was to get 40K to buy just Q as that is what he saw it generally selling for.

Take your time, work your levels and keep your eyes open.
