We know about the Nightmarewood Compound bows, but the book that comes from Ellis Cloudchaser in PoK talks about some other bows that havent been made yet. Here is a listing of the components he talks about. Maybe while youre in the higher tier zones you can look for these drops.

Bow of Raltor Yalvon:
- Obsidianwood
- String crafted from:
--- Guts of fiends
- "the bolts and gears of the cams smithed and filed from molten ore and assembled and greased by a tinker."

(Not sure how the cams are made exactly from that wording.)

Bow of Ulmoth, the Slarghilug hero
- primordial driftwood
- string crafted from:
--- strands of slarghilug kelp
- "the bolts and gears of the cams smithed and filed from e'cian ice and assembled and greased by a tinker."

Bow of Ier'Shon, the legendary mephit archer
- featherwood
- string crafted from:
--- silk from spiders in plane of air
- "bolts and gears of the cams smithed and filed from wind metal and assembled and greased by a tinker"

Bow of Golgar, the ogre survivor in the Plane of Earth
- wood of the petrified arboreans
- string crated from:
--- vegerog vines
- "the bolts and gears smithed and filed from pristine planar steel and assembled and greased by a tinker."

So if you see where any of this stuff drops and would like to share the information that would be great.
