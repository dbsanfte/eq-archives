BlargX
Careful on Rizlona and maybe one more mob i forget to get the heck out of the hall that leads to her. Deathtouch mobs spawn there once you engage the named.

Xuzl has a very nice detrimental haste shield -- perfect shaman item! Speeds up slows and malos remarkably. Did it one time with 14 very average people and one or two ubers; only me and cleric as healer. (I wasn't even full ornate back then.) Nice thing about sol ro zone raids is anything can drop ornate molds. This often serves as a great motivator, and it means you can sometimes get a great haul even if you don't get just the right drops on the nameds that you want. These guys are worth farming a lot for guilds working on the VT and even early elemental stages; chances are there's always someone with a slot that could really use something of at least ornate quality.

Protector of Dresolik also drops a very nice primary that shamans can equip.

I think Riz, forget, drops shoulders commonly with a nice shaman focus.

Sol Ro zone is basically the reward zone for all of the elemental flagging you had to do while watching endless melee drops happen and almost nothing drop for the healers. It's loot heaven for shamans; I'd suggest you go do it as much as you can.
