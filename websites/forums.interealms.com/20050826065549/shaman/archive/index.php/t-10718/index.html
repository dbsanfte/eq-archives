Slaorm
If you keep your UI windows away from the edge of the viewable area they won't get moved when you zone. From what I've heard if any of your windows touch the edge of the screen the game thinks they are offscreen and moves them for you. I moved all my windows closer the the center by 5 pixels or so and they haven't moved since.

Has anyone tried hand-editing the file?

There should be a line XPos1024x768=? in there, or whatever your resolution is. If it's negative, change it.
