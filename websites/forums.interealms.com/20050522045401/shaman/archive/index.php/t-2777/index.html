Nektar
Torpor owns, just gotta know how to use it. This is what I do:

1) In fights - with no aggro (groups/raids): Always canni down to around 60 then torpor up in case of adds.

2) In fights - with aggro:
a) High damage: I generally try to cast torpor at 80 in case of interrupts (torpor is 6 second cast*).
b) Moderate aggro, with damage less than 150 double: Torpor at 60-70.
c) If slow is resisted, /e slap cleric so he can wake up :D

3) Downtime: I canni down to about 20-40 health, then torpor.

Of course, during each torpor cast, I keep canny'ing.

* Torpor takes 6 seconds to cast, but you can ask your local mage if he can summon a focus ring which cuts down casting time to about 5 seconds, which can come in handy sometimes.
