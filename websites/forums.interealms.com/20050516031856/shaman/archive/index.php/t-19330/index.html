Groxb
I have a question for other Shaman out there. Do ya cast regeneration on other group members who obviously do not need it (basically, anyone except Shaman and Necromancers)? This is looking at things before you get your group version, which then it becomes just as easy to cast the group version as it is for the single.

Personally, I don't and I don't understand why others do. I can't stand it when tanks ask for it. What is my reasoning? Shaman and Necromancers use regeneration as a means to replenish their health without requiring a direct heal - therefore, a method to generate mana without large amounts of mana in return (just the mana cost of regeneration). I specifically tell the Clerics/healers in my group not to heal me unless I am being attacked and under 50% life. Tanks on the other hand are being healed every few seconds, so what's the point of the regeneration? It's not going to save the Cleric any mana. When the tank's health reaches a certain point, the Cleric is going to heal regardless of whether the tank has regeneration or not. Regeneration isn't going to slow down the process of reaching this critical healing spot when mobs are hitting in the 200's per attack and he is only regenerating 5-25 HP every six seconds. I am not talking about when the Cleric is meditating and the group is waiting, and even if I was, I would just toss a direct heal to those in need oppose to regeneration.

Am I the only one like this?
