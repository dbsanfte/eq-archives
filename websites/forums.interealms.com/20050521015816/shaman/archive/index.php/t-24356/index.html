cattywumpus
Vah Shir Shaman here too. Love it myself but that's just me.

The Vah Shir race isn't popular with the power-gaming crowd since none of their racial abilities are that great. They are very handy skills to have but really don't give you any advantages in battle where most players want the edge.

If you want to be the best there are other races with more useful skills. Paticularly with shaman, Ogre unstunability and Troll or Iksar regen are hard to replace but you will pay a penalty in experience points for them.

Whatever class you choose learning to play it well will serve you far better than any choice you make now. A well played Vah Shir Shaman will be far more desireable to a group than a poorly played Ogre Shaman but a well played Ogre will have a slight advantage over a Vah Shir of equal talent.

Pick a class and race that makes you interested in the game, not one that you think will make you powerful. The choice you make should be one that will last you a while (some have been playing over 5 years) so make it something you choose for fun, not something that you hack away at in the false hope that you will be better than the next guy.
