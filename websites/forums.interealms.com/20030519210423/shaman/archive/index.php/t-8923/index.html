Solariis
I find most pickup groups don't pay attention to mobs that I root even when I announce it with my hotkey. So I usually get some distance, slow the add then sit to get aggro then root park out of the way of the others. 90% of the time a slow followed by sitting down will get aggro so it's very effective. I'd just tell your sk and pally friends to not aggro the adds unless you are having problems with resists and are getting pounded on too much while trying to root/slow the adds. This allows you to get aggro as you want and park them out of the way.

Then again I don't claim to know it all so there might be better replies with other useful hints :)
