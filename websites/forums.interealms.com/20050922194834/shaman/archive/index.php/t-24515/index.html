Detton
I worked with Alchemy. Heres another thing about alchemy you may fight interesting:

Of all the magic damage types (Frost, Fire, Arcane, Shadow, and Holy) only ARCANE has a VERY limited number of spells that grant that resistance.

I think the only class that has a resistance to Arcane magic spell is the Paladin, and it's an Aura (only one aura up at a time, it's really limited).

Only ALCHEMY has potions that raise arcane resistance (Potions that grant to ALL resistances for a short period of time)

This also makes it invaluable for PVP (the duration is pretty short on it)

I prefered skinning/leatherworking with my hunter, more. Skinning was MUCH easier to level up. No hunting for herbs or mines, beasts that you can skin are all over.

With herbalism, i found myself running into TONS of herbs that i couldn't gather because of skill limitations. They are not all over, and unless you set aside quite a bit of time to skill up (or constantly go out of your way every time you can) it's a little tedious.

Worthwhile, but tedious all the same. :) Alchemy is INCREDIBLY valuable also because a LOT of potions you make in alchemy are used by leatherworkers, and lets just say I would PAY ANY AMOUNT FOR SHADOW OIL! I can't find ANYONE selling that stuff. There needs to be more alchemists. :D
