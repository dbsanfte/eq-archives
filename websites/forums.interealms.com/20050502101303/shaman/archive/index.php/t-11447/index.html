To join a raid, you have to have PoP, period.
To join a raid, the raid leader must invite you, and you must accept the invite in the Raid window (alt R). This will make you a raid group leader.

OR

To join a raid, a raid group leader must invite you to his group and you must accept the invite in the group window. You will then join the group and automatically join the raid.

To speak in raid, type /rs and your text

To leave a group, but stay in the raid, disband in the group window.

To leave the raid entirely, you MUST disband in the raid window.
