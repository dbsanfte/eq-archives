The new spells are No Drop so it requires you to have the expansion and go there to get them yourself. The exception being any spell for a hybrid that first belonged to a parent class before the expansion, like Circle of Summer.


A lot of the new quests that I've seen in Plane of Knowledge involve getting 4 items, one from each of the previous expansions Original, Kunark, Velious, and Luclin. These quests seem to be designed for mid-level advendurers. (example quest item: a minotaur battle axe)

Many of the new quests involve getting items from the new planes though. These quests, of course, are designed for higher level adventurers(L 46+)
