how about these?

/whot for a who target

/tt target tell

/rtarget target the last person that sent you a tell

/target (and a PC name) to target them

/guildsta target and get a guild stadus of an anon person

/ho to make a hotkey

example
/ho loc /loc
that would a loc button and it would be on your cursor to place somewhere(the name would be loc)

didja know you can use shift and a number to go to that hotkey bank?

or F1 to F6 to target members in your party?

and you can use Alt and 1 to 8 to cast from your spell gems!

/who all guild#
just start pluging in numbers in the place of the #(remember no space between guild and the number)
it will do a who all for different guilds and if you spend enough time you can find the number for each and every guild on your server :)

this is really usefull for those guilds that you can't do a /who all and the name
