1) Fix Guk targetting. There is nothing more frustrating than the inability to click on MOBs to target. This is semi-componded on multi-pulls and semi-solved with the cycle ability. But there are times you want to click on a distant MOB to root it and simply can't.

2) Implement two new 6-man levels of play. One is in between the current "normal" (MOBs hit for 388 to 392) and "hard" (MOBs hit for 988 to 992).

The second is harder than hard where MOBs hit for 1288 or so.(*)

3) Change the points. It's fine to keep 51 points for a baseline reward. Heck, I've been in groups where it took 89 minutes to win. But

105/51 is 2.1

However, 988 / 388 is 2.5

The reward for a now "hard" should be closer to 129 points, not 105.

This would mean that the new "medium" (688 hitters) would be worth 78 points for a succesful run, and the new "hards" would be worth 170 points or so.

4) Implement a new line of armor and augs. Where is the MP 4 aug? (for example) Implement a line of armor costing 2100 or so per piece. Scale it so the base armor is at EP levels.

5) Change the final reward (Wayfarers Brotherhood Emblem) so that it can be equipped in ANY slot and not just the Charm. Currently the WBE replaces the Adventurers charm for a whopping net gain of ZERO.

100 hit points isn't going to break any "down the road" encounters and is a nice "atta boy" for those who complete 366 (MINIMUM) adventures.

6) Fix Raids. No I haven't been on one, but neither have I gotten married to a needle-sharing, cross-dressing Haitian prostitute with AIDS. There are some things that go without saying.

(*) Note you will have to change pathing and other things or the LDON model. Trial 6 doesn't require you to face up to 4 MOBs at one time, why should an enclosed LDON.
