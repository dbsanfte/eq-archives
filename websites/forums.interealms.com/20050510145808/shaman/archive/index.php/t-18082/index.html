Your buffing life is too simple. Allow me to complicate things a bit. :D

Pick up IOS if you haven't. Notice that IOS stacks with Kragg's and pretty much every other class buff. (There are exceptions, but it's pretty compatible.) It also stacks with your single stat buffs. Stack IOS + Kraggs + Brute + Cat + Dex + STR. Now, you've maxed out your melee's stats and you can give a ton more HP to your casters so they don't get eaten so fast. The stamina boost from this, plus the HP boost from Kragg's is a significant difference in HP. Also, don't forget your AC buff when in a group without a CLR.

The caveat is that this takes a ton more mana, buff slots, and time. I usually reserve it for LDON, close friends, or if we are hunting in an area where we are in significant danger and the extra HP will be needed.

Now, when my group has a death or a wipe and I chose NOT to use IOS, I'm always wondering if the situation could have been avoided if I had just went to the extra trouble of using IOS. I am a Troll afer all and so I'm pretty lazy. :p

This is also good for aggravating BST's since they don't get IOS until 61, lol.
