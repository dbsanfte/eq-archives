I had posted part of this on the gucomics forums a few weeks ago, but wanted to get some ideas here too. I'm talking about general game issues mostely, not shaman specific. Theres three main areas I think need to be address, but how do you feel?

Here are my thoughts on what OoW will need to make it worth it personally:

1) Leveling to 70 should be no more difficult than leveling from 60 to 65 was. What worries me here is the AA costs in GoD. If they balance leveling exp off the same exp they balanced GoD aa's (elemental experience) off of then they are going to be screwing a large chunk of the player base. Levels should not require the same exp as 30AA's. I remember their being a quote from one of the dev's right before PoP came out assuring everyone that leveling was going to be fairly quick and painless because people didn't have fun leveling from 51-60. I hope that holds true for this level increase.

2) Spell availability should be the same as PoP. GoD spells are a serious PITA to get. Personally I loved the PoP system because everyone can get their spells eventually. At first they were rather high priced, but now most of the 61-64 spells are really cheap. Any group that is appropriate level for the spells has access to places to get them (BoT being the easiest for specs and runes) and the drops are fairly common. GoD drops are rare and the results are non tradeable thus making them inaccessible to most people. Bringing back ancient spells was an even worse idea. Worse yet I have a feeling that they will be balancing a lot of content in OoW via access to GoD spells. Since in the previous major expansions the non quested spells were always tradeable it was fairly safe to assume a good percentage had them. Based on the poll on the website a little while ago I think its not the case with GoD spells. They should make a version of the GoD slow one of our 66 or 67 spells just like the new style canni.

3) Make tradeskills available to everyone again. In Luclin and earlier anyone with enough time/paitence/plat could make pretty much all the tradeskill items in the game. The high end cultural stuff was on par with quested armor from the previous expansion. In PoP they made a serious shift in tradeskill dynamics. All of the best gear required elemental access to obtain parts for and even then they could be insanely rare. There needs to be a whole new set of tradeskill items that are as much of an improvement today as the pottery, smithing, and other items introduced in the famous blue diamond tradeskill patch introduced. The components for these have to be available to everyone just like the cultural components were. There also hasn't been a worthwhile tradeskill quest for non raiders since the 8th shawl. This should be addressed as well.

The first two are of the most importance to me in considering buying the expansion, but the last one is something that needs just as much attention.

Sadly, I really don't expect any serious changes before OoW goes live. Their track record is almost 100% to push the same or weaker crap live that initialy shows up on lucy for the spells.
