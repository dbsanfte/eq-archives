Spear Dot, Elemental Legging Dot, LDON Dots, Short Tick duration Dots, Long Tick duration Dots. 3) Since we all know Bards are the King of slows, true ae, neg Magic resist modifiers, high slow percent slows, true slow versatility. We need something to put us back on top. Maybe we can copy the level 62 bard song and make a level 65 shaman spell. Increase its duration an extra 2 ticks (So its better then bards and shamans will be the king of slows again) Then we can toss in some disease counters to the effect so the shaman draws aggro and we can increase the mana cost to say around 175 and up the effectiveness like 15% which is really 3% with slow mitigation. Since this spell main intent is to give shamans something bards have already we can call it Balance of the NihIl which would help equal out shamans and bard slowing.

4) I was also thinking we can use and upgrade to "Q", since it still heals the same amount it healed when it was called "torpor". Shamans never got a "healing Upgrade" with when mobs started hitting for 600-800 on average and the introduction of slow mitigation. We really need a new "Q" style upgrade. Lets see Maybe we can make the "HOT" heal an additional 10 dps a sec to compensate for the mobs 400 dps a second increase they got in the POP expansion.. We can give it a really powerful name like Ancient Chaos Breath for its powerful addition of 10 dps healing a tick it grants us.


Now that were done with spells, I'm thinking of some aa's that would be useful for us.


1) Since melee's get a revamp, new skills that do just about everything. We can have shamans restore there endurance at a faster rate. This will be a cool effect at first, and then later we can add the effect to melee weapons and armor in the new expansion like all shaman spells. Lets call it Call of the Ancients for now.

2) Now we can really use an upgrade Rabid bear and Hastened Rabidity, since these are the most sought after "AA's" in the shaman arsenal. Shamans become amazing with this skill, for instance We can get a rare drop Sharkspine Club after about 280 hours of camping it in the plane of water, which isn't a big deal since we all know how easy it is to convince guild members to grind in water. Then we can use its wicked ratio and amazing proc and attack raid mobs with it, after begging for beastlord and ranger attack buffs and pissing off the melee in your guild by looting a 41% haste belt to do around 56dps for five minutes. All that for a mere 17 AA. I know it might not seem like much, we can't all be rangers have a discipline that does 1000dps. But its 56 DPS with the best shaman club in game, it really is amazing!!! What's our alternative, chain nuke with our kunark bracer and do around the same DPS? We can enhance this ability, so we can use it more often like our kunark bracer.. Perhaps we can call it enhanced rapidity

3) Now we need a "bug fix" I mean an AA that will allow our pets to be buffed by group buffs. We can make it cost, umm lets say 20 aa's. Since shamans are truly a pet class we should get this ability, We get all the other pet "AA's" .. We really are the best pet class. Just think about those poor mages, necro, and beastlord. They have to get rare drop items and do ldon adventures to focus their pets. All we have to do is cast "Focus and Q" continually on our pets to have the same effect as these items do for mages,necros, and beastlords. Maybe we can call this AA Pet Affinity
