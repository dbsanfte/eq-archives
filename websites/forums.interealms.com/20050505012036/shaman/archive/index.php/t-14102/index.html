Jinheim
Looking at his magelo, he already has CA3 and LR5. In fact, he has a lot more than the "key AA skills". He is being pretty humble about his 120aa.

That being said, I would recommend first getting 1 rank of spirit call. The extra damage they do doesn't really impress me, but I find them quite useful for pulling. Whenever I am pulling a 2-spawn in PoP, I can send my pets in before I aggro the mobs myself and trust them to stay alive long enough for me to root one and slow the other. Also, I hunt a lot in tactics where the Daiku Death Knights HT for 1091. A HT + triple 583 that stuns me and allows him to triple me again can quite likely kill me. I use my pets to eat the harm touch. Also, if you duel a lot, they are really great mainly because no one can channel sh1t through 4 pets and it causes people to panic that haven't faced it before. Most SC advocates say it is a good AA because it is free damage every 12 minutes. I disagree because the damage is rather unimpressive, but I think it is still a good AA for the utility. I wouldn't go past the first rank for a while, though.

I have also heard that CS and ID are not that great for us in comparison to CA and LR. I have CA3 but have no started on LR or CS yet, so I can't really say. The way I understand it, with max CA and LR you have a decent chance of having a lucky streak of misses, thus saving your life if you get aggro from some big baddie. With CS and ID you will be taking consistantly less damage from every mob, but that is probably not going to save you. If you get quadded for 2000 instead of 2500, you're still gonna die. If the mob misses half of those, though, you will be alive :)

I know nothing about channeling focus =\

As for Virulent Paralysis... A lot of people swear by it, but I never really saw it as being that useful. It doesn't refresh fast enough for me to feel safe unmemming root, and I doubt that a disease-based root gets resisted less than a magic-based root does on a malos'd mob. I do not have this AA, though, so I cannot really testify. I was considering training it, but then I got ornate chain legs which have a right click greater immobilze effect, and that ensured that I will never ever train VP.

The healing AA's, though, I do know about. I have HG3 and AHG3, and I am very happy with them. HA and HG function sort of like CS and CA, respectively. HA will make you heal for more. A 1500pt tnargs vs. a 1300pt tnargs is a decent upgrade, but the 200hp difference is most likely not going to save someone's life. HG, on the other hand, gives you the chance to double your heal. A 2600pt heal WILL save peoples lives. I have only had HA for about 3 weeks, and I have already saved more people's lives with crits than I can begin to count. The ideal answer is, of course, to get both :) However, I feel that HG is hands-down the more useful of the two, and you should max it before you even start on HA. Also, I went in to Advance Healing Gift thinking it was going to cost 2 - 4 - 6, but it actually only costs 2 - 3 - 4 which was a pleasant surprise.

I also notice that you have quickbuff2, but no haste of mithaniel or other spell haste item. That leaves you with a 4.5 second Q. I have nice items in my shoulders and range slot (only place a haste of mithaniel item can go) that I am unwilling to get rid of. Thus, I went ahead and trained QB3 and I am very happy that I did. A 3 second Q is really awesome. Between QB3 and SCRM I can keep Q up on my entire group, ensuring no one dies to medium-strength AEs. QB3 is also very nice to have for.... you guessed it... BUFFING. When 20 people die and get rezzed and all ask for rebuffs at once, it is nice to be able to do it in half the time. A lot of people feel that QB3 is a waste of AA, but I do not feel that way. Eventually I will have a spell haste item that will make QB2 + that haste the same as QB3 and thus render than 9AA obsolete, but I feel that it was worth the 9AA to have a 3 second Q in the meantime.

So, my advice would be to spend 4 on spirit call, 9 on QB3, and then 20 on Healing Gift. That should keep you busy for a while :)
