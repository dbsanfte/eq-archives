Yeah, my one and only fear raid, it wasn't how hard they hit that did us in on the break, but the fact they hit fairly well (up to mid-100's?), rather fast (They follow the old world formula of mobs having lower delay the higher level they are, and these are the highest-level old world mobs), and the fact that there's almost always going to be three or four, and they cast.

Your best bet is to buff yourself the hell up, run to camp invis, drop invis and just let whatever's closest proximity aggro on you. Slow it, DoT it, and back off. I think an epic and two Banes could kill most PoF yard trash, but I'm not sure. Just do this on whatever aggros you from your spot at camp.

Oh, and the scarelings are easy to handle with a pet. Summon him, tell him to guard right where he's summoned, and pull to him. When phased / shadowstepped / whatever, pets go back to their "bind point", if it's closer than where the spell would put them. So, pull to where you summoned the pet, and that proc will have no effect

Ok, Fear solo break in... I have done this dozens of times, and this method ALWAYS works... I will reference the map linked above.


Zone in invis and lev up (and be bound just outside by the big rock). If you EVER see a mob heading toward you, just hit gate right away (as in running toward you... aggro'd and all that).


Move north (straight) to the edge of the hill and look out in front of you to survey mobs. Check each one you see to know if it will see invis on you. If any do, then wait for them to path away.

Angle toward the Spinning Hourglass area. But DO NOT GO ALL THE WAY TO IT. There are tentacle terrors there that will not only see invis, but also dispell and knockback.


At about 750 e/w loc, move to going DUE NORTH. You should be facing right at the west most edge of the firewall.

You will have 2 times when you are cresting hills here. Just get to the top of them quick, or hope that your lev still is holding you up high enough to see past them. Check any mob you see to verify it won't see invis.


When you reach the firewall, just hug it and move clockwise along the wall till you are due north of the firewall. At about 400 e/w loc you will have the NORTH CAMP safe spot. There is occasionally 1 eye type mob that paths through here, but that is just barely enough to make it not a safe spot to bind if you are any class otehr than shaman (or if you are ANY class and died).



This is the ONLY path that will get you safely to north camp without killing anything first. And stopping to kill things will often get you dead. There is a house along west wall with see invis, and there are the TT's at the hourglass that see invis, so you have to do this little windy route.


IF you are spotted and start getting hit, do not run, just channel Gate. If you get shadowstepped be HAPPY cause you can cancel out of your gate casting (duck) and start a new cast... normally you get stepped far enough that you can get off a gate easily.
