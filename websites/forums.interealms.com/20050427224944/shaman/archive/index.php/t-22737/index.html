Singles will be pretty much impossible up there - 3 or 4 can happen. Most do not summon that i have run into and vp3 has held the duration without md. Turgs sticks without malos most of the time.

The bottom line is that there is really not a lot of room to work with up there. The other side of the coin is the exp sucks as$ (at 65...can't remember for 61). Unless you are looking for something specific, I would avoid it. It is usually empty for a reason.

It would be pretty tough (not impossible) to handle I think. Just make sure you get fully buffed up before you head in.

And finally...there is only one way to really find out! Go do it!

Barm McLir
One you invis past the birds you have two mobs that will come at once plus possibly a wanderer (or two) that are hard to see. Binding sight on one of the static mobs then switching into third person helps you look for a clear moment to pull. Beyond this you'll run into spider traps and groups of mobs.

Like a lot of lower end PoP, it could use some more exciting drops to make it worth the hassle.
