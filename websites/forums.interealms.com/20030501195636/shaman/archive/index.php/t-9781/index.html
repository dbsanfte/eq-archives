Fangface
After reading UgZug's Alchemy Guide, I decided to recruit the help of a few friends and waltz into Grieg's End to claim a stone for my sort of new shaman character, which is quickly becoming my main. We had three members in the group 52 monk (me), 65 pally, 65 druid. It wasn't hard to reach Alchemist Valrona. She on the other hand is a freaking pain to kill. Alchemist Valrona has approximately 20K HPs and Massive Regen. HPs are hard to estimate due to the regeneration. Doubles in the mid-100s. Ultimately the monk and pally were taking her down and she'd regen back most of what we'd do to her, so in the end the druid just started to hit her with AE's and nukes and the pally acted as a healer. Once we did that method she dropped with ease, most waves of the AE took her down 15% and the melee attacks were basically just neutralizing her regen.
