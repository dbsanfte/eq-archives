It seems they made both the AH and the IH parts of the heal double from the crit - where they used not to.

Before June 14th patch:
1550 * (2 (crit) + 0.19 (aa) + 0.45 (item)) = 4092 max

After June 14th patch:
1550 * (1+0.19 (aa) + 0.45 (item))*2 (crit) = 5084 max

They also made AH not affect CH type spells - which has been posted as being an error (EQLive, druid class board - too lazy to dig up link)

So, I am guessing it was all a mistake.
