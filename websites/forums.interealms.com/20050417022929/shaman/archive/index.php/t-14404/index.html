Go to Dulak and camp at the edge of the Dandolak Dock. it's a nice safe spot no mobs wander there that will agro you. Pull Oceancrashers on the pier and Shadys from the Warehouse and the Gnome Arcitiect. You should be able to make 1% normal exp every 2-3 kills which are pretty fast kills. faster if you got a jbb or epic. Oceancrashers drop BD's, Diamonds, Rust encrusted bracers, and other gems. The Gnome drops BD's, Diamonds and other gems. Shadys drops the Medals for brotherhood charm and assorted gems. I usually seem to make about 600-800pp an hour to hour and a half at this spot.
