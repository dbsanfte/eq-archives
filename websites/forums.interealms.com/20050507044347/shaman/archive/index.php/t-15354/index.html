afreeman
The penalty is 10%.

Barbs get a 5% penalty, trolls and iksars a 20% penalty, and halflings a 5% bonus. There are also class bonuses of 10% for warriors and 5% for rogues (class penalties were taken out a long time ago).

Remember, however, that experience progression uses an exponential curve. Because of this, for example, it takes about 10 TIMES as much exp to get thru level 62 as it does level 52.

This means that an iksar/troll with the same number of experience points will, in the high-end game, be only 1 or at most 2 levels behind a halfling warrior with the same number of experience points.

Thus: go with whatever race you want to play (and ogres are an excellent choice, since they don't get stunned from the front).

In the long run, your choice of race will make no difference, and in the short run, all it will mean is that you will be maybe a level (at most) behind your equal-exp contemporaries.
