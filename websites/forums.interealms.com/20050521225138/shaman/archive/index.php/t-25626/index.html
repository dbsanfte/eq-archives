Balkamp
Here's how we do it with excellent success:

Upon spawning, MT group snags Tarn & drags him to one of the cubbies where there is NOT a portal & tanks him. His aoe isn't bad, BUT will signifigantly lower melee dps on the rest of the raid. Get him away. This MT group will consist of well, your MT, a RT, & usually a shaman, cleric, druid & necro. Shaman debuffs & spot heals, cleric chain CH's MT, druid looks after RT & spot heals, & necro feels neclected. Tarn ramps & quads for 900, ergo the need for an RT.

2 feran groups, well balanced for the adds.

Everybody else on the portals.

Here's a few tips:

Our feran groups drop their targets fast enough so the dps'ers in them can help take the portals down between spawns.

When Tarn spawns he'll fire an aoe in the middle almost immediately. Pet discipline is required for this. If a pet gets in the RT spot & goes down at any time during the event, the cleric or the shaman in the MT group ends up RT. Not good. Pets must be suspended or held before this event starts, no exeptions.

After the portals are down, those groups assist the MT & take him out.. Feran groups stay on the ferans.

Tarn is an excellent target for any elemental-ish guild. Always drops 2 69+ runes, 1 piece of decent loot & usually the oow tier 1 armor legs quest piece.

Happy hunting.
