Jing
I think you're misreading then. Only Execution and Suffocation are DPS sprint fights I'd say. Most of the others involve keeping your group alive, killing monsters and spawning the next wave til you get to the boss. Execution and Suffocation are DPS because you have a time limit, and if you are too slow the prisoners die. Not all trials are like that. Lashing for example has no time limit at all and you are in complete control of when you want to spawn the next wave. However, the mobs in Lashing hit harder than mobs in Execution, making it harder to just stay alive, not to mention an untargetable npc attacks you from time to time.

Up until Saturday night, I had only done Execution trial and never bothered with others. We got bored and decided to try other trials. To our surprise they were remarkably easy. I'd say at least 2 and probably 3 are easier than Execution trial and we beat Stoning, Suffocation and Torture on our first try without knowing what to expect from each one. Our first try on Lashing we spawned the boss when the 4th wave of mobs was still up and we lost our cleric and paladin pretty soon after. On our second attempt we beat the trial with ease. We tried Flame 2 times and got owned, didn't even beat the first wave and it was late so we logged.

One thing you can do in most trials to stay alive is kite. Most trials mobs run slow and you can use SoW, horses, bard speed, etc., in Justice. However, even with just Run 3 you can easily outrun the mobs. Just run a mob or 2 in a circle while your group kills other mobs if you need to. This even works in Lashing, where you have a pretty small room to kite in. However, I prefer to run mobs to a corner and root them (Petrifying Earth is a killer root for this, because of the increased duration and it has a -30 resist adjust, so it lands easier). Kiting can buy your group much needed time to med or heal. Obviously kiting won't work well in Execution/Suffocation because you are on a time limit and need to kill the mobs very fast. However, I'd still rank Suffocation easier than Execution because you only need to kill 2 mobs per wave fast. You can be slow on the other 2 because they do not kill the prisoners. Just kill the "Spirit of Suffocation" when he spawns (2 of them per wave, one is delayed and spawns maybe 20 seconds into the wave).
