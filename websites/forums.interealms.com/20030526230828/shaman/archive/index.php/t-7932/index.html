I was sitting here bored (away from home til tomorrow so I can't actually play) when I started comparing IOS and Kragg's to Focus of Soul.

For a Shaman, it appears that Focus of Soul gives about the same hit points as the other two buffs stacked at about 65. The ability to add an extra 50 or so STR and DEX probably wouldn't be worth it to many Shaman (unless they are soloing as melees, not something I would want to try at 65!)

Then it occured to me that the extra STA you can get out of this combo (even before you factor in any extra STR and DEX) would be more useful to a tank.

When I get home I'll see if I can find a tank that would be interested in comparing the effectiveness of stacking the buffs in this manner. Obviously this would not work well in some circumstances (such as when the tank is getting so many buffs that they can't afford the extra 3 spell effect slots or if this would put the tank's stats above the stat caps), but I'm thinking that it may just be worth it at times.
