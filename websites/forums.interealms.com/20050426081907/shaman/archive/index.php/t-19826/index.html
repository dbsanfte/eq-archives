Zarniwoop
The protectors/sentries are immune to root and hit hard. They have a huge agro radius, so if two are close together you will have to deal with them both at once. I imagine a really well-geared shaman could probably solo one, but it wouldn't be fun. People used to solo the ones near the zone in by exploiting a pathing bug. I'm not sure if this is still possible.

I think at one point you could solo in some of the towers (e.g., Xuxl's tower on the far left), but the last time I was through there, the mephits summoned. This was a change, and would make soloing there uncomfortable.

I have had success duoing here with a monk, and I'm sure it would be doable with any melee partner.
