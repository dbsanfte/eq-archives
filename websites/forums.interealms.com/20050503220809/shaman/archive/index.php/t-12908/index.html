Seriously though, I don't have a whole lot useful to contribute to this discussion except for a quick tip on how to recharge magic items. Bear in mind that its pretty expensive plat wise, and works best on 10 charge items (I think 10 is the most an item can hold). BTW, this is sort of a secret technique, so keep this info on "Beta" level secrecy (hey, something had to come after Alpha pet).

Get your charisma maxxed, go quest for faction items, cast whatever illusions you can, and have an enchanter pet cast alliance on you (if that works on other people, if not do all this to him and have him cast alliance). The point is, get the merchant to his/her rock-bottom price. Then, make sure the merchant has plenty of empty space for new items in his inventory. Finally, sell the item to the merchant, and when it appears in his inventory buy it back. In the interim, the game forgets how many charges the item had, and you get your item recharged.

However, most charged items have insane costs associated with them (try selling and buying back a rez stick from a merchant sometime if you don't believe me; that's an expensive rez!), so you can just think of it as the Merchant's Union of Norath using their secret allegiance (the same way Bankers use their's to instantly transport your stuff from bank to bank) to swap around charges from all the charged items they get. Sure you buy back your rez stick, but somewhere out there are nine other poor saps who sold merchants their rez sticks when they had only one charge left on them, and although you thought these sticks all dissapeared everytime the server got reset, they actually went to a secret merchant vault somewhere.
