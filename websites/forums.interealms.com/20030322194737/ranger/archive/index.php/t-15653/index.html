Farm spider silks.

Find a buyer and act as their supplier, that way there is no time spent in the bazaar.

It is boring but you will make good pp doing this.

To get a little more money combine 2 silks into 1 silk swatch which you can sell for 10pp each.

Try the Titans in Stonebrunt. The Astral Leggings and Cloak sell for quite a bit. On Zeb the average price is like between 4k - 5k each. Take on greater wolf form, nothing aggros you but the pandas. 1. Titan leggings and cloak;

2. Footpads of the Tiger (from Snowbeast); Sells for about 1kpp on Zeb;

3. Heretics Quest for the Swiftclaw Sash; Sells for about 1kpp too.

I accidentally spent a whole RL month there (was playing a necro and left my ranger there). Now I have 8 Titan Leggings and 4 cloaks to get rid of, not to mention 6 Footpads. I almost had a heart attack when I saw all the pelts in my bank lolol.
