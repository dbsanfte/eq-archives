Scurg
Spirit of Wolf (sow) - Everyone gets this buff first thing.

Stamina - Everyone is a must, at higher levels you can add some nice health power and it helps the intellect casters more then the tanks, especially the chanter as they dont have much HP.

Agility - Everyone should get this buff as well as it adds extra AC. Mainly Melees but all if you have the mana to spare, every little bit helps.

Strength - Only Melees get this buff, it helps their damage output, not by much but it makes them feel better, however I think helps the Rogues and Monks more then any with damage output. Also you might get request on this buff from casters now and again because of ecumberance.

Dexterity - Only Melees get this buff, it helps their weapons proc and helps raise their melee skills. Even though some dont have weapons that proc I still buff them with it as it makes them feel good about them selves.

Charisma - Only per request.

AC Line - Melees, and others if you have mana to spare and if no cleric is in your group.

Inner Fire - Buff all for cheap mana, I stopped using it at my level however, their is no upgrade to this line of spells.

Talisman line - You get this later in level, Buff all melees and only Int Casters if requested or if you notice they are very low in level compare to you ask if they want it, it doesnt stack with their self shields.

Harness of Spirits (HoS) - You get this later, buff only Melees

Haste - Only Melees (self explanatory for you I hope ;) )

Regeneration Line - Only Main Tank (MA) unless you have mana to spare then do the other melees.

Tips: Always buff yourself first to give yourself a timer (except haste) with the upgraded spell first. For example, I just go my newest Stamina buff so thats going to last longer then the rest of the buffs, so just buff yourself with Stamina first and then use the rest of your buffs in the order I stated above.

Now when your Stamina buff fades you know its time to replenish the rest. Try to put an order in the way you buff, for example I always Stamina, Agility, Strength, Dex, etc..., this will help with timing buffs.

With Haste, designate one person in your group to call out when haste fades, buff him first and wait 30secs before hasting the others.

I think thats about it, time to go back to work. :( Good Luck :)
