Harvyst
Ditch the Mushroom veil for either crystal spider eyes or something better. Basically, there are a lot of things with about 35 hp and 3 wis... veil fo the executioner, for example.

Total gain: 35 hp and 3 wis.

Get the Earring of the Solstice. It adds hp and wis, and ft1. It will cost about 7-10k depending on the prices of your server to buy the parts, but it is one of the best earrings out there. Replace your forest loop. 55 hp, 5 more wis.

Total Gain: 90 hp, 8 wis

I'd replace the Froglok BP with a Tin Mail Shirt, those run 3.9-5k on my server, and have 10wis 25 hp 7 sta, which works out to 46 hp. Also slightly better ac. This gives you 5 more wis, 46 more hp.

Total Gain: 136 hp, 13 wis

Replace your Nathsar bracer with a Coldain Military Wristguard - easy to MQ. Gain: 2 wis, 3 ac, and a nice focus effect.

Total Gain: 136 hp, 15 wis

For yoru range, You can get a griffon's eye for 10 wis (at the cost of -20 cha), those go for 2-3k, drop off of Griffons in jaggedpine. Gain: 5 wis.

Total gain: 136hp, 20 wis

It's a start. To go better you need more money (for instance, the girdle of mithandrael has 10 wis and 55 hp and 10 sta), or quest, or get velious armour, or get no-drop from the plane of storms. (Rain travelers overcloak, etc).
