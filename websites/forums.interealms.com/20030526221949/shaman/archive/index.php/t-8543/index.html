Thurdar
To the person asking where it dropped, if you look at the map of 2nd floor Ssra there's a 0,0 marked on it. Mystics spawn in the big room with a tube leading up just underneath that.

On the first floor, take the right side door when you get to the first big room from the zone line. That corridor will take you up to the 2nd floor. We usually set up camp right outside that room on the second floor, at the T intersection. No roamers or close spawns so it's a safe place to pull to. You need a key from a commander on the 2nd floor to get in to the mystic room though.

The reason you don't see PE or group acumen for sale all that often is because it's a pretty rough camp. One type of mob there (elite guards, not all that common) isn't slowable and hit's for about 450 so you need a real tank and a cleric to keep him alive. You also tend to get multiples per pull so an enchanter (dunno if a bard can mez there) is pretty much required. My last trip there we did it with a 65sk, 61 shaman druid cleric enchanter, 59 paladin and it was pretty easy. Don't think we could have done it with the 59 pally tanking though.

PE seems to be the common drop, in 4 trips there I've probably seen a dozen PE and 3 acumen drop.
