Seveca,

This link leads to a thread with a discussion about mob slowability and a link to a good chart: http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=12590&mode=linear&perpage=40&pagenumber=1

Some mobs are theoretically magic slowable, but with high resists, so it makes sense to Malicious Decay, then Grummus to get him slowed, then try to land Turgur's. For example, the three times I have done Mithaniel Marr, Grummus landed on the second try, but with four shaman's spamming Turgur's, we couldn't get it to land.
