In order to keep progression at a specific speed, you have to ramp up mob dps vs. raid dps. Effects bumping up mob dps and lowering raid dps are used, but here is where the raid hp spike occurs....

Raids are usually based on an MT controlling the target... and staying alive. You ramp up a mob's dps too much and the MT dies as well as the rest of the raid. If you do not ramp it up enough, you risk the chance that the raid will skip the content. Players would not be happy if the MT was the only one to recieve upgrades, and the raid encounters would become dry if the only change was mob melee dps.... so items of near quality drop for all classes.

Group content only requires, at most, six players. It is also easier to tweak due to less players/variables. Adding an additional 30hps to each player in a group will have a stronger effect than adding an additional 30hps to each player in a raid.
