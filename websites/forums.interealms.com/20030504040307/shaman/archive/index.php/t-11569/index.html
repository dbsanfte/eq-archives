eximius
This is advice to get Stoicism Spell, and a walk through. It is not too hard to figure out without a walk through--Hint: The mobs you have to kill are very close to the underwater quest giver, but are in a different zone. After two weeks of being broken, this spell was fixed on Drinal. Thanks SoE for fixing it before the full patch.

(1) Get it. Wow. Awesome. Game-changing, primarily in allowing Shaman to work hard to develop more mana without KEI. (This is a gimped version of celestial heal, slows attack (33 off unbuffed) and movement (100 off unbuffed), but with Cannibalization is really excellent for Shaman--paladins and clerics have much better heal over time spells.)

(2) How to get it:

(a) Gunthak, point south of lighthouse, impassive sea spirit gives quest. (It is on the way, and worth starting right).

(b) Along the right of the Gunthak Beach is a Cavern. Beach has nasty mix of live and undead, and many level 50s die from trains of greens here. Go slow, use invis, dont train and expect to swim away. Inside Cavern, all are live mobs-invis works well. Take right turns through cavern system and then through open indoor seaside of Gunthak to Dulak Harbor, find and kill drunken pirates for an empty flask. This may take a while. Drunken pirates are to the right. Watch aggro and trains here. There are a couple of corners to fight from here, and invis is your friend, so a pet is difficult. It helps to have a nice group that can plow through a lot of pirates fast, since it often takes 30-50 pirates.

(c) Gunthak: Turn in empty flask to impassive sea spirit to get stone (giant and did not fit in any container).

(d) Gunthak: Med, whistle for pet, buff and get ready to fight. Turn in stone to impassive sea spirit to have him call Flotsam and Jetsam spirits who drop no-drop Piece of Flotsam and Piece of Jetsam. Tuen these into Impassive Sea Spirit for Stoicism Spell.

Good luck and enjoy, fellow shaman.

I am an avid quester.
Hints for questing:
1. Con the quest npc, you may have done something to lower your faction making it impossible to do the quest without raising your faction first
2. If you have some way to raise your faction (i.e. enchanters alliance spell line, druid wolf form in the old world, etc.) use it for every quest, no matter how good your faction is
3. Always hail the quest npc.
4. Humor them and go through the conversation with them anyway.
5. When handing in quest items, be sure to check the name at the top of the trade box (it could be the familiar, pet or npc behind/beside you that you've targeted).
6. Right click each item in the trade box to be sure you're handing in the correct items.
