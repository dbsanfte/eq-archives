Just a couple of common items with good tribute value:

- Tactician's Bulwork (sp?) Shield - can buy in bazaar for 6-8k, worth 27k tribute pts.

- Delightful Orb of Forgotten Magic - can buy for 5-10k, worth 24k tribute pts.

Also, there is a type of pick that can be made with around 46 skill in Blacksmithing that gives very good value, 100 or so will get you almost 200k pts (max).

Seems that most LDoN items have high tribute value.
