Melkiah
copy this to the bottom of your dulak_1 map, and you'll have the spawn spots of all the good cave rares marked. All of these are confirmed by moi. good drops for alts or farming, and all can be fought without adds, if you're careful. I run between camps with invis and just kill ph's/nameds. start with the luggald assassin (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=11658) . pull him/ph to the NE corner after the pathers go away. after he's down, re-invis and head to the enraged soulstealer (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=11201) (there is a pather that comes through here that you need to watch for). kill him and then move to the defiled dedicant (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=13656) downstairs. invis up to the spot, stand a little bit past the statue north of the defiled you can get a root off before he runs to you, and then either root rot him or fight him at his spawn point. There is a mob in the water that will add if defiled gets too far into the pool, and you also have to be careful of the same pather that goes by the enraged camp. after dropping him, invis and run to the fleshchild of innoruuk's (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=11282) room. SE corner is safe to pull to. Then it's off to Incantator Cawrolis (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=12977) , who is rumored to have one of the better drops in the zone, the bowl of bonedust (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=23089) , though I have yet to see it.

All of these guys are dark blue to 65 and not that hard to handle. Have fun.
