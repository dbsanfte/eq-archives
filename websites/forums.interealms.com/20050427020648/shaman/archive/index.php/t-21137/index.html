The named hit for around 1024 I believe. They are partial slowable, they summon, they rampage and flurry. I'm not sure on the hitpoints, since I hardly do any damage when fighting these guys (just debuff and heal). They proc that soul claw proc or whatever it's called. The good news is they are mezzable, so you can mezz on incoming and debuff the lights out of em. Make sure you use cripple. Have your nukers/dotters continue to cast toward end of fight, even when mob is low, cause the warrior mobs proc a rune/mitigation-thingy at the end.
Oh and no on the duo, bring a good group.

In 4 nights we had 3 of the melee earrings drop, but no caster rings. In general, the mobs are tougher than Hard LDoN's, but pulling is not an issue in Barindu, 100% singles.
