When a full group kills a mob, you end up with 216% worth of xp split between you, so if you're all the same level you get 36% of the kill ...

When a lower level player (e.g. me at 52) groups with higher level players (e.g. 5 level 65s) the higher level players each get a bigger proportion of the xp than the lower level player.

If it were linear (which I'm sure it's not - I would imagine it would be exponential, but for a worst case starting point ...)

XP level ratio would be:
individuals level / (total levels of players in group added together)
so in this case it'd be 52 / ((65 * 5) + 52) = 0.138
and 65 / ((65 *5) + 52) = 0.172

Soooo, Total XP * XP level ration = 37.2% for the level 65 players
and 29.8% for the level 52 player

Leading to the logical conclusion (remembering that is a worst case senario - the XP level ratio will be much more in the favour of the level 65s) that all you level 65 players should form a 5 person group and group with me as opposed to some useless level 65 dr00d (ahem) as you'll get more experience per kill :D
