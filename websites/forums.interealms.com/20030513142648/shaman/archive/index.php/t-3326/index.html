Shiuna
OK, finished this quest tonight using a level 1 mule. I did this:

1. Made level 1 troll warrior worshiping Rallos Zek. Left Neriak and went immediately to Plane of Knowledge and he had a lot of fun goofing around there, talking to the pretty ladies and petting the kitties. Then he went to the Great Divide and on to Thurgadin.

2. Some nice soul shrunk him as he entered Thurgadin so he didn't have to walk around stooped over. Couldn't find a soulbinder so asked in ooc for a nice person to bind him there.

3. Headed back out to Great Divide and on to Eastern Wastes. Two people stopped him to ask if he was lost and explained that this zone was probably too dangerous for him. Troll explained, in his most elegant troll vocabulary, that a pretty shaman was paying him in rabbit meat to die for her. Both of them were nice enough to buff him and wish him luck.

4. Made it to the RyGorr fort in the center of EW without incident. Someone was pulling orcs out of the fort so he was able to run around the wall to the Crystal Caverns entrance and zone in. At that point he camped and the shaman logged in.

5. Took shaman to Crystal Caverns and bopped orcs on the top level until one had the bottle of karsin acid. Made sure path was clear back to troll, and camped. Logged troll in and looted acid, then troll found an orc, punched him, and found himself back in Thurgadin.

6. Took bottle of acid to Erdarf in The Holy Harbinger, a bar marked as location 18 on the eqatlas map. Gave Erdarf the acid, he took sick and left, and dropped a gem of persuasion.

7. Troll left Thurgadin and headed to Kael Drakkel via Eastern Wastes. He was dubious to the giants so it was an easy trip thru Kael to the wolf trainer. The trainer is located under the bridge leading to King Tormax's Throne Room. Location is approximately neg 1100, pos 1700 on the eqatlas map. You can see a spot of water directly east of the bridge. It looks like there's a down slope on the left side just before the bridge, but there's ice everywhere and he slid into the water. He got out ok, slid over to the wolf trainer, turned in the gem of persuasion, and received a giant gem of persuasion. He turned in the giant gem and received the giant control harness.

8. Troll jumped in the water, drowned himself, and returned to Thurgadin. He then went back to Great Divide and followed the river to the shardwurm caves. A group was pulling the shardwurms so it was an easy trip to the broodmother. He entered the caves from the north end and stayed up on the left-hand wall. He took the first passage on the left, and then the first left into a double chamber. The broodmother is in the back chamber. The troll was able to tiptoe between the two guardian shardwurms and camp behind the broodmother, as he was indifferent to her.

9. Had a wizard friend position himself on a hill southwest of the Coldain Velium Mines entrance, at approximately neg 6300, neg 3200. Moved shaman to position directly north of the shardwurm cave entrance, camped her, logged on troll, and turned in harness to broodmother. Broodmother immediately started walking out of the chamber and headed toward the north end of the caves. Camped troll, logged on shaman, and had plenty of time to see the broodmother shamble out of the cave, turn east, and head southeast toward the coldain mine. Shortly after the harness turnin, two coldain dwarves spawned outside the cave entrance, and the wizard targeted Korf. It took another 2-3 minutes for the broodmother to wander over to the cave. She attacked one dwarf and Korf took off southwest across the hill. He resisted roots and snares from the wizard, and kept running. The shaman was finally able to aggro him with some spell (can't remember which one, sorry) and rooted him, nuked him down, and looted the wristguard. The wizard was supposed to camp to avoid the faction hits, but stayed to make sure Korf didn't get away. Took negative hits for Dain and Coldain, and positive hits for Kromrif and Kromzek.

All in all, a fun quest with a satisfying ending. Korf conned dark blue to a 50 wizard and 50 shaman, definitely lower that when he is inside the caves (conned red there). You should be able to solo this at level 50, and maybe lower. If you are going to solo, I would recommend camping the looter at the mine entrance. You will have 2-3 minutes to summon and buff a pet, if you want to. I lost mine while following the broodmother, so we just nuked. We did ooc some notices to the zone while the quest was in progress, asking people to leave the broodmother alone until after the quest was over.
