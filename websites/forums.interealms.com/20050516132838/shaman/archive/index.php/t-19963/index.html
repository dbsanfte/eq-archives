There is a perfect spot in HoH for root rotting solo. On the way to Rydda trial you pass 4 small temples (2 on each side of the path), 3 of which are guarded by a pair of wrulons and the 4th by a pair of black armor suits. Once the first cat is dead you are golden and can proceed killing the 8 spawns in a circular manner.

I've had lots of success soloing tactics recently. You'll want the following at a minimum:

BoS
BoU
VP3
Malo/Malos
Torpor/Quissence

Added bonus:
Epic, clicky root (e.g. ornate pants), BA IV, EA IV, AE IV, Bane

I don't have the foci effects or bane, but typically will get BA IV from the tribute system. Epic just adds a bit more mana-free dps, and clicky root is great if you get an add so can't use VP, or for the wraiths & oracles that like to dispell VP, but if you don't have it then just mem your favourite root spell instead.

Litgruv mentioned tactics. I used to pretty much live in the pigpen area. Diaku oracles will own you.

I've never had major problems with oracles. Sure they cast, debuff themselves and so forth, but its not a massive deal. Root and slow, reapply as necessary. Chances of em debuffing both at once is fairly low it seems (never happened to me), at worst I get hit a couple of rounds or so.

You can also get the wraiths as roamers or room pops, and they can snare you and debuff you and resist slow too. Pretty hard to fight when you're snared and dotted and debuffed.

These guys are a bit of a pain, but so long as I keep the good buffs protected its not that bad. Worst case is it roots you and starts beating at ya. For that reason, I tend to use normal root on these and save VP just in case I get rooted and don't have radiant cure up. On the plus side they like casting spells at you, which is generally better than getting thumped for 500/hit, and they're not all that common.

They also changed the aggro there. Now, the mob near the pigpen corner will usually add in to your arena pulls. Never did that for the huge amount of time I used to spend there; does it like crazy now.

If you pull to the near-right corner as you enter the room before the pig pens, you won't aggro anything else there except the roamer mob, which is easily timed. I've found I can just leave the 2 corner spawns and the roamer that comes right to the pig-pen entrance, although generally I prefer to kill them anyway.

I'm not uber by any means - not flagged for anything beyond Tactics, about 2k6 unbuffed hp, no focus items - but I do have clicky pants which helps. I generally died a few times each evening the first few couple times but I think I got it sussed now and its normally ok to run to zoneout (unless you get snared/rooted or zone is busy so you can't train - in which case chances are a cleric is nearby anyway)

Give it a try, its good fun! XP is ok (not great, they take a while to kill and net about 5% AA so group xp is better) but the loot makes up for it. I've yet to get some ornate drops, but living in hope of some soon, and piglet has given me a couple drops that sell quite nicely, plus you're guaranteed to see some SPs if you stay there long.
