Shadrak
Shadrak was "born" in early 2001, to be a partner for my shadowknight. I always intended for him to partner with a melee character, because from my first days in EQ, shamans always made excellent partners for melee characters. Their combination of regen (back when it mattered), slows, hastes, stat buffs, AC buffs, hp buffs, resist buffs, and heals really made them excellent partners for melee types.

As I understand it, one of the AAs for GoD was originally intended to be, or at least represented to be, an endurance-regen spell. If you read the warrior or monk boards (both of which I frequent), you'll see them fairly indifferent to the adding of +endurance to old-world items. The major problem with endurance is not the total pool, it's the regen speed. Adding a shaman-only spell or ability to increase endurance regen would be a benefit to shamans, as it would give us another value to add to groups.

We've all been kicking around ideas for how to improve FA. The stat buff portion of FA is useless, as most everyone acknowledges. I really like the idea of adding some advanced combat effects (like strikethrough) to FA, but I'm not sure SOE would ever consider it. As an alternative, why not add an endurance-regen component to FA? That would inject some usefulness back into a spell that's been completely replaced in value by the beastlord spell Ferocity.

To scale down with lower-level shamans, you could do the same with the level 60 spell Avatar, and maybe an early-50s version, each with appropriately-lower levels of endurance regen.

It wouldn't make shamans indispensible. But it would add some value to the class, without stepping on any other class's toes, and with the timers already in place over disciplines to govern their frequency of use, I honestly don't see this as overpowering.

In fact, given the timers for monk disciplines, I never use up all my endurance anyway, as the timers prevent me from doing so, even at level 58. Nonetheless, warriors seem to be more endurance-bound, with their incite abilities and other disciplines. Sadly, I know almost nothing about rogues, but I assume they have endurance-regen issues as well. Making this change might help shamans reclaim some value to melee-centric groups.
