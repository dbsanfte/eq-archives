MekuDeku
I kill this guy everytime I pass by him to get my guild Remove Curse. You can do it lower than 50, but I wouldnt be less than 46 without epic or JBB.

I clear his little guys and they usually carry weapons. I give these to pet. Then I sic hasted and regen'd pet on him. I keep my MR up because he likes to Thunder Strike. I go into his room and put my back into one of the corners there. I just keep myself and Jabober healthy. Let the pet do all the work. Having regen on helps much because of the unresistable knockback. If your back is in the corner though you wont get interrupted. YInterruptions happen when you move from your initial casting point. Since you are not moving (only up and down) then you can still cast. You can time your casts between his knockback. You can cast your Ice DD if you like to hurry up the fight.

He is a lot of fun to fight because it is a different sort of battle than I am used to. It takes me about 4 kills to get a spell. I give all eyes I get to my vendor and sell them cheap because he seems to drop it every time. The gloves are nice. I still use them on AE raids. +5 to mr/fr/cr. Not bad for easy junk.

Hope this helps.
