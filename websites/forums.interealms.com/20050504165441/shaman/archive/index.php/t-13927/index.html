PLing a mage essentially consists of healing the pet while it shreds as many low blue mobs as possible.

Keep a DS on the pet - either the mages or the druids (depending on if you are using the Druid or the Shaman to PL). 1 Burst of Flame from the mage per mob.

Give the pet a Muzzle of Mardu, a Girdle of Magi'Kot, and 2 Fists of Ixiblat (beg from a high level mage, or purchase in bazaar).

There really isn't a better way to do it.

Scalia
PLing a mage essentially consists of healing the pet while it shreds as many low blue mobs as possible.

Well, if you have a high level DS available to you, then yes, healing the pet may be the fastest way since mobs will be dying quickly. However, absent a DS, it is probably more efficient to keep the agro on the Shaman and let the pet do its thing.

Using this approach, the Shaman pulls with slow and then just stands there, taking a beating, healing himself and/or rooting as needed, and helping finish the mob once it gets low on health. As long as the Shaman is in melee range and the Mage isn't, the mob will always be attacking the Shaman and not the pet regardless of how much damage the pet does. The Mage can nuke, mana regeneration permitting, to speed things up.
