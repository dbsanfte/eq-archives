Chutoi
Most of the suggestions I see for improvements tend to be pretty overpowering but . . .

What about a 20pt AA skill called slowing mastery for shaman only? With the EXCEPTION of mobs over level 69 (ie, raid level mobs), it would reduce mitigation by one level. Thus, the effect might be:

Fully slowable mobs: no effect
Mostly slowable mobs: fully slowed
Partially slowable mobs: mostly slowed
Slightly slowable mobs: partially slowed
Unslowable mobs: no effect
Raid level mobs: no effect

In terms of implementation, I don't think it'd be that hard to program--each mob is flagged for a % slowable anyway, and all the %'s slowable are already programmed in . . . Just have it check if the slower is a shaman class (or using a shaman spell, whatever) and then send it to the different subroutine.

Thoughts?
