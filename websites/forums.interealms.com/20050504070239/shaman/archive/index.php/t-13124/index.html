I play both an enchanter and a shaman, so here is my perspective:

Shaman: I could care less about KEI. I don't ask for it, don't need it, and while I will not click it off if someone casts it on me if it came to a choice between KEI and Virtue, Virtue would win.

Enchanter: KEI is da bomb. With no other spell or class can you simply login, sit down, say nothing, and have people give you money en masse.

Regarding duration, you guys sure are a suspicious bunch. There are two types of extension:

1) focus item

With the focus item extension, assume 15 percent longer duration. A reputable enchanter will always list the focus item in use (or simply identify it as a focus item extended c3 in which case you should assume its an amulet of insight and therefore a 15 percent extension). If you have doubts, you should either ask the enchanter to link the focus item (which they will do) or find an enchanter you trust more. It isn't rocket science.

2) aa

This is harder to pin down. It is possible to have the AA titles without having the duration affecting AA. I think in this case you just have to assume that you will be getting a c3 without extension and be pleasantly surprised if it lasts longer.

Regarding the amount of the donation, VERY few people donate 100p. Most donate 20-30, with a fairly sizeable chunk donating 50 and ALSO a fairly sizeable chunk donating <5 or (more often) 0. There are sadly alot of people that will quite happily walk away without giving any donation at all (which is fine -- it all sort of balances out).
