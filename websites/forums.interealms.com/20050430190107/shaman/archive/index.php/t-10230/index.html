Well it depends on the zone. Almost all zone require you to kill a boss type mob in a certain zone to advance to another zone.

For example to get to PoV and PoS you need to do 1 trial and pass it in PoJ. Then to get to HoH you need to kill a dragon in PoV (Aerin'dar) and u get flagged for HoH. There is also a key quest available to do but I hear its a waste of time and it gives u problems later I think.

To get to CoD you need to kill grummus, or do key quest etc.

But to get to BoT I dont think you can kill any mob that gives u access but rather you have to do a key quest which is pretty easy if you have a descent guild.

So to sum it up, for certain zones you need flags (not all) and they are gotten by killing a mob, hailing the projection thing that spawns after u kill the mob and zoning into the zone. Or do a key quest and then get flagged.

Gloriana
Everyone starts off with access to Plane of Justice, Plane of Nightmare, Plane of Disease and Plane of Innovation.

Tier 1 --> Tier 2

1) Plane of Justice - complete a 6 person trial and receive flags for Plane of Valor and Plane of Storm.

2) Plane of Nighmare - complete 18 person Hedge quest and receive flag to the Lair of Terris Thule.

3) Plane of Disease - Kill Grummus (raid force) or do exp group type quest to get flag for Crypt of Decay.

4) Plane of Innovation - Kill Behemoth (large raid force) to receive flag for Plane of Tactic.

Tier 2 --> Tier 3

1) Plane of Valor - kill Aerin Dar with large raid force or do quest to receive flag for Halls of Honor.

2) Plane of Storm - kill giants to complete quests for Bastion of Thunder (boss giants take several groups)

3) Lair of Terris Thule - Kill Terris Thule (large raid force) to get flag for Plane of Torment

4) Crypt of Decay - Kill Bertox (large raid force), killing Terris Thule and Bertox get you flagged to kill Saryn in Plane of Torment (I think)

5) Plane of Tactics - not here yet, so not sure what to you have to do to progress to Rallos Zek.
