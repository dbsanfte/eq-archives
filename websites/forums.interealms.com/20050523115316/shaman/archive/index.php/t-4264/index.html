Yeah, you're definitely headed in the right direction. The gear you have is a nice combination of AC, HP, and Wisdom. :)

If you're looking for cheap upgrades, here's what I'd recommend.

Ears: Imbued Golden Ivory Earring (cheap- under 100 pp, maybe much less if you provide the ivories- and a great combination of AC and WIS.)

Neck: Ry'gorr is great (what I wore, well... not at your level, but when I got a little higher) - an alternative is the Black Alloy Medallion - stats are pretty good (ac 6, +5 wis), it's cheap (couple hundred PP- maybe an even swap for the Ry'gorr), and it will make every spell you cast under level 44 cost 10% less mana (it has the focus effect "Mana preservation II"). That's a good thing!

Face: Arctic Wyvern Mask. You get a nice balance of AC and WIS with the mask, for just a few hundred pp (or less- I see these in the bazaar for 50 pp sometimes.)

Head: Ry'gorr is fine, JB is your next upgrade.

Wrist: If you DoT much, you might want to swap out one of the totemic bracers for a Chipped Bone Bracelet (CBB) (with the CBB you get "Affliction Efficiency II" - which will cause all DoTs under level 45 to cost 25% less mana to cast. Again, this should just cost a few hundred PP- probably an even swap for the totemic)

Arms: JB is your next upgrade, but you've got time to look for a bargain, or just wait... totemic is fine. :)

Hands: See Arms

Shoulder: Tattered mantle (100 pp or so, maybe less.)

Chest: I'd stick with the totemic for awhile. Decent AC and a nice focus effect (I remember the short duration of buffs at low levels drove me NUTS!)

Back: You're fine here. Until recently there werent many reasonable upgrade options at all. Even now there isn't anything much better available for around the same plat.)

Waist: Spider fur belt (50-100 pp) is a nice alternative belt- lose a little wisdom, gain some AC/HP (it's AC 10, +40 hp, +5 agi and dex), and probably make a little money on the deal.

Legs: Your legs are fine- this is a tough spot to upgrade, especially on a budget.

Boots: See Arms

I wouldn't stress over upgrades that are going to cost you much- you're in good shape right now. I'd definitely upgrade the earrings (just because those earrings are such a great deal for a barbarian) and any slots you can upgrade for little or no cost.

You want to start saving your money for the true upgrades- those few game-changing items that will truly help you down the line. Those items (iksar BP, fungi staff, horse, JBB, fungi tunic) aren't cheap... so start saving now! :)

Happy hunting!

P.S. Don't stress out over your wisdom. At 51 I had around 120 wisdom and it didn't hurt me a bit.
