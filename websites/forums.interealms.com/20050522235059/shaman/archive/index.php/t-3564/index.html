Rathelon
Velious armor graphics wont show up properly on Luclin models. Basically, you can do one of two things;

1) Go back to the old model and see the old Velious armor graphics

or

2) Use the new models and set LoadVeliousArmorWithLuclin=FALSE and your Velious armor will look the same as generic chain, plate, leather, etc..though you will see color (i.e. blue chain for Wolf Caller's).
