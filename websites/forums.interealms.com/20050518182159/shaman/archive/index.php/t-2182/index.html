First off, I would really ... I mean REALLY encourage you to reconsider the choice of race. Faction work for NFP doesnt take that long, but you are stuck with your race for ever. Nothing 'bad' with barbs, but other shaman races have much more going for them. I play a barb, and if i could change to ogre or troll, well thats a no brainer.

Gear wise... fungi definitely. Jaundiced bone bracer for using from lvl46 and up into your late 60's. Apart from that, just aim for ac/hp and get the wis as a bonus. Ive always found you can function perfectly well at 175wis even at higher levels. Its very seldom your manapool that is important, but rather your rate of manaregen.

Dont be afraid to wade into the fray and play semitank, it's good for you as it lets you know how much you can handle and you also get your defense up.

G'luck.

CGS for your shield and a Jade Prod, Howling or Slimy Poon, and a Rune Etched Icewurm fang are all good choices for your range and primary weapon.
