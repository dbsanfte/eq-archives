Archyr
Your warrior friend is right...in the low to middle level of the game, the warriors role is to help kill the mob....after 50+ his basic job is to keep aggro and be a meat shield, this is achieved by their taunt (which caps at much higher than ours) and high aggro low delay weapons, preferably those with debuff/damage procs such as Wavecrasher, Willsapper, Lammy, Fleshgrinder, Bloodpoint etc...
Our job/role in the 50+ game? kill stuff dead and make sure that they don't notice us :-D
How we do this is variable, can be archery with the right AA or by our amazing damage output in melee due to high ATK.
