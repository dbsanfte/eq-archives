deria
That sick bay is irritating. When you think you have everything, I would suggest this:

1) hail everyone in the infirmary
2) hail everyone in the infirmary again
3) do it one more time just to be sure
4) go says "portal's destination" to the fire drake at the fire entrance
5) go hail the guy standing at the disease entrance, and do his dialogue
6) go unlock memories a few times
7) go hail the librarian at the top of pok elevator, then say "what information", "what new information", "new lore".
8) go back and unlock memories some more

That usually jars something loose. For my enchanter, it turned out I had forgotten to do a post-flag hail.
