If i were upgrading your gear to snatch a few extra AC points out- i'd keep the iksar BP and switch out the following:

Back: Get an Ancient Seahorse Hide Cloak (ac 20, +25 hp) - 4-5k (all prices based on Karana, don't know about FV)

Shoulders: Get some Imbued Granite Spaulders (ac 20, +10 to all resists) - around 10k

Neck: Get a Shrouded Medallion (no drop from 4-arms camp in Maiden's Eye, ac 12, +15 vs magic, etc.) or a Crystalline Torque (ac 12, Mana preservation III, from Velks, or 4-5k.)

Bane warding shield would be a nice upgrade, too. Or a Bone Braced Buckler.

Replacing your rune etched bracer could help- best choice is a JBB, then (for pure AC) you could grab a bracelet of quickness, poison etched wristband (from DN, ac 13, 1-2k), or a bracelet of fortitude.)

Bottom line: I'd avoid replacing regen as long as possible.
