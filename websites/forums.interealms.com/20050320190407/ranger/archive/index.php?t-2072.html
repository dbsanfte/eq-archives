Yeah, we've had a favorite everything-else thread already, it's time for a greatest movies thread. Here are the one's that top my list.

American Beauty
Absolute masterpeice. I know a lot of people out there are going to strongly disagree with some of the sexual taboos in this movie, but if you can look past that, it's got a lot of very cool philosophies on life hidden inside. The motto of the movie (which you can see on Lester Burnum's desk in his workplace) is "Look Closer." You can probably watch this movie then times before you begin to put the whole thing together. Absolutely amazing in every way. Soundtrack also rocks.

Forrest Gump
This one probably tops my list of favorite movies. If you've never seen this movie, make it a priority to do so. Forrest rules!

Gladiator
We all know why this movie rocks. I'm sure everyone here has seen it already;-) It's one of my few DVDs, but I'm very proud to own it.

Pulp Fiction
So... weird. But I love it. Samuel L. Jackson and John Travolta rock as hitmen. This movie is 2/3 weird dialogue and 1/3 swearing.

Magnolia
This movie was sooooo long. But if you can spare a few hours to watch the whole thing, it's really good. The whole thing is about random happenings that occur every day that turn out to be completely insane coincidences. So many characters to fall in love with in this movie. And when the frogs fall from the sky... so weird.
