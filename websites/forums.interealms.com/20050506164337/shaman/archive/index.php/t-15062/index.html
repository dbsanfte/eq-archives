Kozul
I was always told just simply casting a spell of the right skill-type, no matter what spell you use, will work towards a skill up. For example, I recently had to get my divination up, so I set up a hot key for True North and chain cast it in a quiet zone. Do the same thing for any skill you want to get up.
weissblau
Yes, repeated casting works fine. Set up a macro hotkey. I used to put Burst of Flame in slot 6 (Evocation), True North in slot 7 (Divination), and Sicken (Conjuration) in slot 8 and use something like

/stand
/pause xx, /cast 6
/pause xx, /cast 7
/pause xx, /cast 8
/sit

Where 'xx' has to be tuned to make sure you pause enough for that spell to be cast. Note that although the /pause is at the BEGINNING of the line it doesn't take effect until the END of the line.

Just get in a safe place, target yourself, turn on the TV or grab a book, and whack that button over and over and over again. If you get low on health or mana, just cool it for a while.

Note that you can use Summon Food or Summon Drink to work on Conjuration and it's cheaper manawise, but then you have to do something with the food/drink on your cursor before you can hit the button again. Depends on how involved you want to be with the process.
