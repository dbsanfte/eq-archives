Haleice
I fixed my problem.

For anyone else that is having weird problems like me, where the settings are all correct but the effects aren't working, check your username_server.ini file. Mine had a load of garbage at the bottom of the file.

It seemed that even though it could read the settings it just ignored them until I deleted the garbage. (loads of square boxes and the like!)
