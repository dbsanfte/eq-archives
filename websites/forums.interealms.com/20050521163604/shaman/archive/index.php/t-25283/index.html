I just finished this one tonight.

Name of the NPC is Vassi - he is the iksar merchant on the second floor of the Jade Inn in North Freeport - behind the dancing courtesans.

For Armor Quest 1 you need to kill 18 Fallen Crusaders at the Ruins of Valmarr and Jereth Blackshield.

The Fallen Crusaders always come in a pair with a Fallen Disciple, which is a healer type. Kill the Fallen Disciple first than the Crusader. At level 20 they conned green with 1 up arrow or blue with 1 up arrow. I duoed them with a 21 Shadowknight and we didn't have any problems - took about 45 minutes to do. I don't think I could have soloed them because I would have lacked the mana pool to keep the wards and the nukes going.

Jereth is the boss of the smugglers down on the beach just off of Wailing Caverns - he conned even with 2 up arrows to me at 20. You definitely need a group for this one - we had 21 sk, 21 brawler, 22 inquisitor, 18 druid and myself 20 defiler - was still a fairly long battle but we won comfortably.

Reward is some very nice gloves that were a huge upgrade for me. You can see the details in my profile as soon as EQ2 players updates the info.
