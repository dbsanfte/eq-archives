Don't think it's a /bug or /feedback issue. If it were actually "Extend Torment V" then it might be, but it isn't. There are already numerous focus effects in the game that behave similarly to "standard" ones, but not entirely. My personal pet peeve is the 8th shawl, which is great in terms of making buffs last longer (20% over the normal 10 or 15% from Extend Enhancement), but unfortunately it doesn't affect Q. So I either have to wear a seperate EE item for Q, or get a shoulder item with EE and swap the shawl in when I buff, or buy the tribute effect, or something.

This way of doing focus effects makes "magic items" more unique, which I think is really cool, and it also makes equipment progression less of an obvious path and more of a personal choice. ET affects Bane, but AotD affects whatever our 66+ DoT is, so you have a choice of which appeals to you more. Or if you want to put in the effort to get both, at least this way you gain some benefit that neither one can provide on its own.
