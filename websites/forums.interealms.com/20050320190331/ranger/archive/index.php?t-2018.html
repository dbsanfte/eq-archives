All I can say is ICEFANG ICEFANG ICEFANG.

Started doing him at 39, and he is very doable. Don't pull him near ghost and you will be fine. He is a roughly 4.5 min timer for respawn, and alternate cons white / blue at 39, and straight blue at 40. And there is no faction hit for killing him.

As a woodelf the ghost was indifferent to me as was Icefang. Target Icefang, walk around back, back off to max range of snare and pull with it. Being at max range should give you a second attempt to snare if first one is resisted. Once snared kite in an arc around front of the ghost, give him a good distance. If you try to kite Icefang behind the hut, you will get an occassional walrus add, I have never had an add since I started pulling him to the front. This gives you a chance to plunk a few arrows in him, get flamelick going, and here is the key: ( I don't know why Rangers don't do this more often.) Do not engage until fear has landed. I always see rangers engaging and trying to fear at the same time. This is why they get hurt, and end up having to root, back off and heal, and then go at it again. This just eats mana, and life people, and in the long run, cuts down your exp time, due to heal / med breaks. Just get fear to land, and engage, count to 15 and recast fear, wash/rinse/repeat. If he gets fear resistant, and it doesn't land, just back off. Do not go toe to toe. Turn off auto attack, ake a small run back (using your bow as you go) , recast fear and reengage. I have done this numerous times with out getting hit but a few times at lvl 39, and usually end up with over 80% life, and 80% mana. With no break, run over and do the same thing to a Walrus. After the walrus, sit down med, and by the time your full mana, Icefang will be back up.

I did 39 in a few hours here..and am half way through 40 after just an hour. I was getting over half a blue each kill at 39, and a steady half blue per kill at 40. If you can get here with one other person, it is a really good camp. Ice fang and walrus's. HQ Walrus pelt is needed for Ring quests anyway. The occassional walrus tooth, which is an ok piercer.

Gear at 39 is (2) SSoY, Trueshot, some Ivy Etched and Rygor armor.

Just some food for thought.
