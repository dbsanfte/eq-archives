How do you calculate a weapons ratio?
Bigguss
Ratio is just damage / delay.

Estimated damage per second is: ((Damage*2)+1)/(Delay/10)

So a 9 dmg, 22 dly weapon would do an estimated 8.64 dps.

Of course, that's before haste, strength bonuses, double attack, yadda yadda yadda.
