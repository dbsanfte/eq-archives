Zhorn
Here is one reasonable sure-fire way to do xfer droppable gear (albeit an expensive way).

Hand gear to some mules on a SPARE account.
Legends enable spare account.
Move mules to Legends.
Create NEW mules on Legends and give them the gear.
Level the new mules slightly so they dont look *quite* so fake.
Xfer your main char naked to new server.
Cancel Legends on spare account then get the mules sent to the SAME server as your now naked main.
Hand the gear back.

Note the extra step of making NEW Legends based mules (which are allowed to PICK a destination server when you cancel Legends).

Yes its expensive, yes its a pain but when you are xfering gear for yourself + some friends characters at once its not so bad.

This is how I xfered myself and a few friends gear. Some people just sell their old plat and then buy new plat on their new server (if they cant find someone willing to trade). Thats kind of risky though.
