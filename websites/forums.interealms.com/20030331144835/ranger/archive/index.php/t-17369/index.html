"Who can see a RPing ranger porting about - Anyway who needs to with Nexus and PoK."

I can see an RPing Ranger porting about and you can too when you watch me or many others use a Tolan's BP, Thurgadin gate potion, Vial of Swirling Smoke, or hitching a ride with a druid or wizard.

Nexus and PoK won't help you if you need to get out of Hate, ToV, or the remote depths of any other dungeon. A self port to LFay zone will drop you off right next to a convenient PoK portal and give you fast access and mobility to reach zones quicker and cut down on more useless down time.

I'm not advocating that Rangers be given any special considerations regarding ports. However, self port items already exist as an earned luxury and don't seem to have caused any balance issues so far. The 30 second cast time on the TBP ensures that it cannot be abused for solo exploitation and I see no reason why a few more such items couldn't be introduced, even for other classes.

VI/SoE has a history of being overly cautious about rewarding players and adding game features that could increase enjoyment in the game. I say enough of the stingy paranoia about game balance and fess up the goods. Take some of the tedium and frustration out of the game so we can concentrate on having fun.
