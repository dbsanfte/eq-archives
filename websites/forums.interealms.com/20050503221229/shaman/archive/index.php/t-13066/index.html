Kianor
Fletching to 200 is easy. Fletching to 240 is either a pain (though not on the tailoring or smithing scale of things), or expensive.

You *can* fletch to 250 on storebought items, but it gets very expensive. From 200 to 240, I'd say you would be looking at around 40kpp.

If you get a gnomish tinker to make bow cams, you can offset much of the cost up to 235, but I believe it's nerfed now, you can't make a profit on any combine, so you will still lose money to get to 235. (I got to 235 on my ranger pre nerf, but without knowing about the tinkering trick).

Fletching on acrylia arrows is much the same as any other tradeskill based on acrylia. You have to farm the acrylia, or buy it at ridiculous prices in the bazaar, and smith it into arrowshafts. You have to farm or buy the condensed substance and smith it into arrow heads. At that point, it's much the same as fletching any other arrows. This is the cheapest way to get to 250.
