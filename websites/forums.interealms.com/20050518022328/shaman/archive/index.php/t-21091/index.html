Jinheim
There are three levels of slow mitigation. When slow is mitigated, you will see "Your spell was slightly/partially/mostly successful". These are in order from worst to best effectiveness.

To my knowledge, the exact percentages have never been parsed, but it comes out to around:

slightly - 25% effectiveness (~18.75% slowed with turgur's)
partially - 50% effectiveness (~ 37.5%)
mostly - 75% effectiveness (~ 56.25%)

Three tiers are correct but those are definitely not the numbers. Our best guess at this point is that slow mitigation is really two numbers, a base amount that does not get mitigated and an amount above that which does get mitigated.

So you might have a 30/50% mitigation which means a 75% slow has it's first 30% not mitigated at all and the rest (45%) mitigated by 50% for an overall 52.5% slow.

a 60% slow would be mitigated down to 45% and a 30% slow would not be mitigated at all.

As far as I know noone has done the extensive parsing necessary to determine if any numbers are fixed for a given tier of slow mitigation. My guess is they are not, but I am not sure at all of this. The bit about a base percentage that does not get mitigated is pretty much given though - Henora was it I think who did some pretty conclusive parses and posted the results.
