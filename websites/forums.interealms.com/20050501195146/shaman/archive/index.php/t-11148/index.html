I have played all 3 and I am playing an ogre now. They are all good, and I would order them ogre, troll, iksar. I baught my JBB for 15k and it is well worth it.

You will find strong advocates for each race but post 60 - torpor the differances between the races becomes more a mater of flavor then substance. Iksar will not compare favorable between about lvl 45 and 60 if you have the resourses for a JBB, an Ogre only starts to top the list after level 60 and torpor marginalizes the regen advantages of the iksar and troll.

Any of the 3 will be fine. I would just take each out for a test drive and then play whichever you personaly find to be the coolest.
