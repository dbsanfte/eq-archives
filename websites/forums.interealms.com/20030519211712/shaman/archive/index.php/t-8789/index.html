MQing the Coldain Prayer Shawl quests? I think that the only ones that can be multi-quested are the 3rd (the recipient must already have the 2 shawl) and the 8th (the recipient must already have the 7th shawl).

MQing the 3rd shawl is pretty easy. Get someone that has already done the 3rd shawl to give the Seal (recieved earlier in the quest) to the Loremaster, then you give the Loremaster your 2nd shawl.

MQing the 8th is a PITA! The person doing the MQ must make all the armor for you, and then start the quest by giving the issue kit to the dwarf in Iceclad. When the quest is over, you give your 7th shawl to the Avatar of Below.

I'm writing this from memory. Check EQTraders.com for more accurate info on the quests.
