In the never ending quest to find a good leveling spot, I have come across this gem. Tenebrious Mountain on the Moon. I set up camp at the Grimling Forrest zone, and Sonic Dire Wolfes, and Darkwing bats are dark blue and very easy with pet and Jbb. I did not have a lot of time last night, but a couple quick kills and voila, I had filled an entire blue bub (at 46). I have been at the same spot at 36, and the same mobs (yellow and even back then) were a real challenge, and not worth the effort. Now, at 46, I slice through them with ease and xp is very nice. With 850+ Ac I barely loose more then a bub of HP (1474 buffed) and the only spell I cast is Togors to pull. Mobs are PLENTIFULL, there were two other people hunting in the same area and there was absolutely no shortage of stuff to kill.

Good Luck,
