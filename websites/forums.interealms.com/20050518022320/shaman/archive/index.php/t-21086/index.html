Jinheim
If you want a wicked exp duo that includes a bard, consider bard/wizard. Quad kiting with selos, mana song, and rizlona's is awesome. This duo is fairly dependant on having lots of space, though, so you won't be very effective indoors.

Cleric and bard wouldn't work very well, in my opinion. The bard doesn't tank or hold aggro very well, so the cleric would be spending most of his time and mana healing the bard. This leads to possible downtime (even with mana song), and poor damage.

With a shaman, though, you have a perfectly viable duo in almost any situation. A shaman's slow is usually effective enough to allow their HoT's to keep them healed while tanking. Mana song + cannibalize ensures minimal downtime. A shaman with mana song going has some amazing mana regeneration, which means they can throw on some DoT's. Shaman dot's combined with bard dot's and/or melee will put out an acceptable level of damage, especially with Call of the Muse (is there a lower level version of that song?).

So, between cleric/bard and shaman/bard, the shaman partner is definately better.
