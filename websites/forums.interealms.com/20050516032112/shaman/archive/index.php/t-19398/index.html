With the impending release of GoD, do you think prices in the bazaar are likely to increase, or decrease, for existing items? I'm thinking of pulling the trigger and buying some upgrades. But I'm not sure if now is the right time.

On the one hand, a new expansion always means new gear, and some of it at least will be droppable. That brand-new gear will probably be really nice and REALLY expensive. The older stuff that it replaces will probably plummet in price.

On the other hand, there won't be nearly as many people playing in those old zones once the new expansion comes out. So the flow of these new items into the economy will be drastically cut.

Specifically, I'm wondering about ornate armor. There are a few pieces I'd like to get for my mage, but I'm wondering if it makes sense to wait until after GoD, or snag them up now. Ornate gets farmed a lot right now. But when GoD comes out, a lot of those higher-end folks won't be farming ornate any more. They'll be in brand-new zones.

So what do you think: buy it now or wait till GoD comes out and hope it gets cheaper yet?

Ruatonim
You should only buy things when they are cheap enough that you are willing to buy them. Ask yourself what the price you want to pay is, and then search for that price. The bazaar always works in cycles between having too many sellers for a given item or too few. Of course, there are a few exceptions to this with items that drop very frequently.

The chances of ornate molds "plummeting" in price are pretty slim in any amount of time less than 6 months. This is my prediction anyway.

My advice, then, is wait until you see a bunch of people selling the items you want and then wait for someone to lower their price drastically (or negotiate the price you are willing to pay from all the sellers until someone bites). Of course, always keep a look out for some lone seller selling items at way under the normal going rate :)

On a related note, whatever armor type berserkers end up being, these types of armors will be available in mass quantities a month or two after the expansion goes live when everyone quits their twink :)
