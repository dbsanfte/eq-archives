Got my first XP group at 30, and did Tribals and Zelniaks at the Grieg's End zoneline in Dawnshroud Peaks. Did that till about 34ish. At 34-37, I did a level a night (roughly 3 hours play time, sometimes more, sometimes less) by getting a KEI in Nexus and camping the ledge thing between the GE zoneline and the Shroom house in Dawnshroud. Park DogDog on the hill, go out and pull a tribal or a Zel back to the dog, slow it, tank it in teh face, DoT here and there and build up your DEF. Loot=crap, but the XP was incredible.
