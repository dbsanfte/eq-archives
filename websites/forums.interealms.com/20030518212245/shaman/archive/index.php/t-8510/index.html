A lot of my stuff is buyable and it's mostly HP gear, and some of it ought to be in your price range. A few items like the Hiero cloak or BBB might break your bank though :)

Slargg
actually, I disagree. With the plat that you have available, get yourself a nice weapon(rune etched icewurm fang is about 4k) and a cheap haste item. For the rest of your gear, go for ac, then for hp. At the lower levels, this type of a setup allows you to melee quite effectively. The only way that root rotting would be better is if you get a horse and can have constant kei. As you level up, hitpoints become more important, but before 30 or so, ac is the way to go, you can always switch gear around as you level up.

The horse is definately the way to go once you stop meleeing(typically lvl 45 when you start using jbb) But you do loose a bit of plat when you sell it back so its nice to be kinda sure before you buy it.

Eriadoc
I agree with Slargg on this one. If you're going to focus on AC and HP, put it to use and melee. You need Defense anyway, so best to melee. I personally didn't have a haste item, but it would have been nice. I did OK with quickness.

With the introduction of PoP items to the market, a lot of the older gear that has good AC, HP, and WIS/mana are going much cheaper. Look for low-plat values that give the best bang for the buck. Remember, you'll have to upgrade some of it in your 50's anyway, so best not to break the bank on things you won't keep for a long time.
