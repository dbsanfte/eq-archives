From what I remember of my time in the Halas area as a young shaman, from the patchwork armor you could look into the folowing items that are fairly cheap and good (ok not a jbb good but not everone is a million$ twinck when they start).

1st thing if you dont have a neck item do this quest:
Give Jinkus (inside shaman guild in Halas) a piece of Mammoth Hide Parchment (from Cindl - Ask her 'what mammoth hide parchment?'), and a bottle of datura ink (from Greta outside Smokes and Spirits - Ask her 'what ink?').

He will give you a wanted poster. Take this to one of the Bank guards in Qeynos. They will give you a List of Qeynos' Most Wanted.

Return this to Jinkus for Initiate Symbol of Justice, exp, and faction (+ShamenofJustice, +MerchantsOfHalas, -CircleOfUnseenHands, -CoalitionOfTradefolkIII, -EbonMask).

2 Hunt some bears cubs in everfrost for the low, med and high quality skins and do the following quest:
Chanda Miller (near fire at Shurefall Glade Tunnel in Quenoshills) makes bear hide armor. The better the piece, the more faction you need to get it. (you must give gold not any other currency)

Bear Hide Belt - 5 GP and Low quality Bear pelt

Bear Hide Boots - 15 GP and Medium quality
bear pelt;

Bear Hide Cape - 21 GP and High quality bear pelt.

Buying one of the items also gives you the
following faction adjustments: +KaranaResidents, +GuardsofQeynos, +PriestsofLife, +KnightsofThunder

3 Now for rings and earrings, go to Queynos sewers and hunt the following mobs:

An Injured Rat and a undead knight. Both are static spawn and have placeholders. go to eq atlas and find them on the map... go there and kill both locations until they spawn and you get 2 rings and 2 earrings... carefull for the necro who spaws near by I have never seen him come to the camp but better safe than sorry

4 After that I advise you hunt gnolls whenever you see them and do the gnoll fang quest in Quenos, it's lucrative and will allow you to buy some ringmail just shout for a smith who is selling it goes for about 1pp per ac, this is good armor and is also used in the Totemic armor quest that you might do someday.

Gnoll Fang Quest: Captain Tillin located on the top floor of the arena in Qeynos. give unstacked fangs for some experience and a moonstone. Use your Moonstones for the Moonstone quest. (give to Mcneal Jocub in the Fish's Ale) Giving a gnoll tooth to Captain Tillin raises your faction with GuardsofQeynos and Antonius Bayle and Merchants of Qeynos, and lowers it with the
Corrupt Qeynos Guards, Circle of Unseen Hands

Finally, you can also look into the Bandit sash quest it is harder to do (cause they aggro like mad) but lucrative:
Chesgard Sydwen, in the house of thunder, is willing to reward those who help eliminate the bandits in the Karanas. Bring him a bandit sash and he will give you xp, faction, some decent gold and an item (often bronze). You get the following faction changes: Knights of Thunder, Priests of Life and Guards of Qeynos go up and Bloodsabres goes down, The sashes are found on the bandits in W Karana. look at the map for eq atlast for loc of 3 camps...

Hope this helps, I have played my shaman from 1 to 20 something this way and enjoyed every minute of it! Good Luck!

I agree with Shabby, you can make a reasonable amount of cash on spider silks. Buy a large sewing kit (it can serve as a 8-slot container in a pinch), and do the combines on your spiderling silk and silk to make silk threads and silk swatches.

2 spiderling silk = silk thread
2 spider silks = silk swatch

Stating the obvious, but don't undercut your prices too much in the bazaar, get a good feel for costs on your server and price your wares appropriately.

You can also go into Permafrost and hunt there. There is a fantastic ZEM on the dungeon. In here, you can loot to sell in the bazaar permafrost crystals, unrefined ore, and the high quality ores.

My experience is that Permafrost is usually empty. You can also pick up some good upgrades to your gear with the ringmail (or even rawhide) that drops there. You will also get runes, pages, and words which are used for spell research. You can sell these items, or create celestial essence to sell (using celestial solvent & a mortar) found in Shadowhaven.

Check out www.eqtraders.com for recipies. Learn about what components are used in what trades. You will get experience for killing the mobs that drop tradeskill components. Use that to your advantage.

You can make the money you need to buy your equipment. Take advantage of the bazaar and all the cheap gear that is available to you.

Good Luck!
