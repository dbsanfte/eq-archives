OK, set up in the dining room and pull out DogDog. Give him your best regen, and buffs. He will be your tank, unless your defense is lacking and you need to work on it. Go to the room and target a mob. FACE IN THE DIRECTION YOU ARE GOING TO RUN, and Turgur.

If resisted just run to the room and cast it from there. You'll get the timing down soon. Let DogDog tank, with his regen spell he'll be over 80% most of the time. Take out the Isabella first, he faction drops fast and she wanders.

I'm KoS to her, so I kill her ASAP (I'm a barb, been hunting there awhile). DO NOT let the nobles run when at low health, and beware, they and Isabella backstab for high 350's. The bards will help them and you'll have to let ol' DogDog bite the bullet while you run.

After both nobles go splat, pull the bards..but watch out...they hit harder. Once slowed, they are all cake. I had a horse and a JBB, they help alot. Turn off models and you'll never know the horse is there.

I let DogDog tank because my defense was high, and could take two at a time if things got ugly. Keep DogDog at more than 15% health at all times.

When things get sticky with 2 mobs, slow both, and root one away from you, DogDog, and the other mob. Once rooted, JBB the mob DogDog is on, JBB like there's no tomorrow.

Make sure to keep DogDog alive, he's significant damage for you, and saving you mana by taking hits.

Been awhile...but one Ebolt, plus DogDog damage, and alot of JBB clicking would finish them off.

If I think of more later I'll edit it in. Spent 51 - 55 here, it's cake once you get the pattern down. I could do it in my sleep, it's really easy, but the only downside is it's mindnumbingly boring. (No, I'm not exaggerating)
