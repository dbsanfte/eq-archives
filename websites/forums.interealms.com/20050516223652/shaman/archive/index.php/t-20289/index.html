The irony of the cleric thread against shaman receiving a bigger patch heal is very evident in the druid thread where druids are arguing for improved healing.

Clerics are very very very territorial about thier healing monopoly.
Yet its painfully evident that Shaman, Druids, and Clerics are NOT well balanced.

Clerics on Raids are a HUGE problem. You want 2-3 minimum clerics per druid/shaman. I bet on most raids you have 5/6 clerics per shaman even. We had 14/72 Clerics on our first Quarm Kill. 2 of which were brought in to sit other people in a full raid. Why is it clerics are so so much important to the raid then any other priest. Why is it if enough clerics don't log in that day, guilds call XP. Why is it almost every upper end guild has cleric recruitment listed as "permantly open"?

Another problem is that against upper level content. Clerics are far far too required. You want to do a Vexx/Tipt/Kod+ run you MUST have a cleric. 95% of all LDON Hard Groups won't go without a cleric either.
Even in elemental Groups with high hps tanks where a druid (Maybe Maybe Shaman) can get by.... even here the healing is so so less efficient that the groups XP would go up if you replaced the healer with a cleric.

Clerics usually combat this arguement and say well in LDON normal, and other lower content, Shaman/Druid heals are sufficient. And since sufficient +other abilities, they are vastly better than Clerics here.
While this might be true of bottom feeders (Time Groups doing LDON Normal) it is not true for the average bazaar joe in BOT.

Clerics also tend to combat this arguement by saying that the other priests (Shaman/Druid) have way more utility then them. Druids have Evacs/Ports/Snare/DPS. Shaman have Malos/Slow/CC

What clerics refuse to recognize is that the other priests have "lost ground" in thier other core areas.

Druid's have lost a lot of utility over the expansions. Sow/Soe/Porting is not the advantage it once was with books/camps/aas/horses. Evac is almost useless in the GOD Crawls. You think a group wants to crawl through tipt for 2 hours and evac at the end to do it again? snare is just too common really. And Druid are NOT DPS classes like they once were. Druids would be among the lowest sustained DPS classes of the 15 classes. Druids simply put are NOT getting slots when its "Group looking for DPS."

Shaman's likewise have lost a lot of ground in their area speciality CC.
The addition of another slowing class (Bst), Slow mitigation have made having a Shaman to slow not as necessary as it once was. Almost all groups due to slow immunity/mitigation + charm + mez will prefer a chanter to handle CC by a huge margin. As such many groups are looking for a Slower, are starting to be looking for Chanter, or on lower end content a BST (since they slow enough + dps)

Clerics have also refused to acknowledge that while Shaman/Druids have lost utility/advantage in other areas. They have consistantly picked up advantages. Ranging from the difficulty of CR making Rez required in GOD. to the fix of Pacify which can make them a good puller.

I am honestly not against Clerics getting some more dps/tricks for trivial content. So they can contribute more at lower ends of the game.
However, they so dominate raids and upper end content in a way that is far from healthy to the game. Most guilds are even looking at preferred combo for Ikky Trial as Clr/Clr. not even Dru or Shm for backup, but a 2nd cleric.

All in all, all the priest classes are out of whack.
Druids need thier 64 ICH fixed in Mana Cost as 600 is insane. They need to be closer to Clerics in spots so that they are roughly 80%ish.

Shamans need thier 64 ICH back. They too need a better direct heal.

Clerics on the other hand need something else to spend thier mana on vs trivial content. Some contribution that a group will want them roughly equallly to the shaman or druid when healing is sufficient.
