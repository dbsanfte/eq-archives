Irksome
The boots are the mid-way reward for the epic quest. You also pick up a decent shield pretty early on (and easily attainable at your level).

Do you have a guild or a few friends? At your level, you could probably pull this off with one good group, perhaps a bit more if your levels or equipment are on the lean side. The final battle for this is in Mistmoore, where you must kill Black Dire.

You can solo pull Black Dire to the zone and fight there. Makes recovery easy, and allows you a relatively easy escape if it goes bad. Just don't use the words to trigger Black Dire, simply tag him and run like hell for the exit. Took a few tries to get it done when I did it way back when, but it was just a matter of patience and persistence.

Be sure you have a good tank and a good cleric, and Black Dire is kinda mean.

The boots are always in my inventory, and I still use them daily. Excellent reward to work towards.
