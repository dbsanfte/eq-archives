I was in the same boat you were in. I xferred to Saryrn (and eventually back to fennin, don't ask me why) and arrived buck nekkid. I went first to HPH and killed gnolls naked to get some ringmail / leather. I then ran to WK and found the wandering spirit to get my quest shiedl back (only need to say the phrase again if you are past it). Then I bot a few odds and ends from friends and camped the rygorr fort for a few hours till I had a few pieces of rygorr and whacked a few ulthorks and got arms and tunic. Was not great but it gave me enough to go around and solo some cash dropping mobs I just had to endure all the laughs of people seeing an oracle with a spinechill spear, ulthork tunic and rygorr.

One more thing I did shortly after arriving was the Kromrif leggings quest which are very nice leggings for a 50+ shaman and not terribly hard with one group, just need to find out when broodmother up and make sure other people don't mess up quest.

One more thing I forgot, I hung out at KC to loot a few pieces of jasarth, stuff rotted pretty regularly and you can get helm, gloves, bracer , chest easy. Arms drop in basement and legs are semi common too just politely ask people in the zone and most are happy to let you loot.
