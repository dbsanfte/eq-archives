Daeroth
Sol B LDC's and Lava Beetles are always good. Best place is to set up shop in the room with three spawns. You will net 1% exp per kill until about level 51 if solo. With a shaman buffing you, healing you and slowing them it shouldn't be difficult. To speed up kills have the shaman send in their pet once the mobs is below 2/3's. That should help decrease kill time but ensure you get the exp. Only hard part is splitting out the spawn a bit and breaking the room, as at level 48 your aggro radius will be larger and you have a good chance of having to deal with a couple lava beetles from outside the room in addition to the 3 LDC's inside. Once you break it and setup against the wall though it should go smooth. You can pull the LDC's and Stone spider from near the ramps up to the FG's as well. Just note that the Stone Spider is 100% MR and stuns with a spell effect like crazy, so make sure you have your dmg shields up. Killing the stone spider will be a long fight, but it's a rare spawn. The rest should be easy.
