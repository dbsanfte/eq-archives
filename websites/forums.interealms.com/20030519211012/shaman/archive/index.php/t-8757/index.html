Aiming for items that are not TOO pricey or difficult to acquire ...

Face: Crystal Spider Eyes OR Death Mask of the Elysians
Ear2: Silver Hammer Earring
Arms: Hardened Grey Flesh Sleeves
Hand: Coldain Skin Gloves
Shdr: Pauldrons of Witness
Feet: Barbed Dragonscale Boots

Also ... consider saving up the Pages of Dark Power and seeing if you can catch 'em all for the Cloak of the Wise.
Besual
Waist: Spider Fur Belt (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=3891) or if you can get it cheap Barbed Rubicite Belt (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=10320)

Head: If you are max ally to CoV you can go for the Circlet of the Falinkan (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6452). Most of the steps are doable with one good group. May be Vielfang will be a tough fight with only one group.

Back: Hierophant's Cloak (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=2192) is easy to get with one group (if you have enough time to camp it)

Neck: If you like quests you could go for Qeynos Badge of Nobility (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=13796). Small Clockwork Talisman (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16558) isn't hard to get too and can be upgraded later.
