Think about it. 10 stamina in gives you just 30hp.

Let's look at the whole picture and compare a level 60 ogre and a level 60 troll with a level 60 iksar, all with regrowth on.

An ogre will have 1301 base hp*
A troll will have 1247 base hp*
An iksar will have 1145 base hp*

Both iksar and troll will regen 33 hp/tick to ogre's 22/tick

In an hour's time, the iksar will have 20945 hp to canni from.

A troll will have 21047hp to canni from.

An ogre will have 14463 hp to canni from.

For all the ogre's stamina, an iksar's regen gives them 6,482 more hp(roughly 45% more) to canni from in an hour.

The troll will have 102 more hp to canni from in an hour(a little less than half a percent - .48% - more).

Stamina is not nearly as important as some of you make it out to be - as far as choosing a race. Other things are far, FAR more important to a shammy than starting stamina. Like charisma. Or tails.
