Well, the OoW manual (PDF file, downloadable from SoE website for free) has a list of AA/Tradeskill titles that are currently available, there are more to get from completing Epic quests, like Heyokah for 1.0, and Fateseer for 1.5/2.0.

the tradeskill titles are pretty simplistic, no title for skill under 100, Apprentice for skill 100-199, Journeyman from 200-249, and expert for 250.

AAs follow the same rules as before (@ 6 AA, 18 AA, & 24 AA), though instead of Baron/Venerable/Elder, you get a choice of 4 different titles per AA "goal", and you keep titles you've earned, meaning that you can be "Baron" if you have 900 AA's and switch to whichever you want at whim.

and thats the gist of it as I understand it.
