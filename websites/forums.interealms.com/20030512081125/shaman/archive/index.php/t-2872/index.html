I dunno... I think for the money, there are other spots that would give better improvement. +12 wis, +35 hp/mana, +3 all saves isn't bad at all !!!

Things I'd think about upgrading:

Earrings... definite upgrade potential here. Get an Ivandyr's Hoop (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1933) - nice ugprade for just a few k. For the other earring, I'd think about buying components for the Earring of the Solstice (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=12456) (component prices vary, but I know a guildmate of mine got one after spending around 20k... and it's a great earring for the price.)

Upgrade your range item- some nice no drop range items drop in ME (Divine Inspiration (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7338) , Stone Idol (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7177))

Waist slot could use an upgrade- lots of candidates (Girdle of Reflection (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=4657), Chitinous Belt (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7391), lots more choices... (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/listitem.html?sort=Armour+Class&invslot=Waist&cfilter=9&statfilter=None) )

Shadel bandit ring.

I'd think about upgrading all of those before the bracer. :) Then again, I used to be a total AC junkie, but am lately being more and more convinced that I'm going to get tossed around like a rag doll fighting anything 50+ no matter what my AC is.
