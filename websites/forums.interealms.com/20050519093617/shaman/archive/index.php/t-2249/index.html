stormrazor
Banded is a good place to start, I wore banded until 24-25. It cost me about 2pp per AC, roughly 87pp for a full suit give or take from a blacksmith. At 15...I'm sure 87pp is quite a bit. Upgrade pieces gradually, start by seeking out some banded, then move up to Totemic, RyGorr, Jarsath, Seb scale, JB whatever else comes next.

Use your money for spells early on the money will eventually come, I didn't have 1000pp until well into my 30's. The money will come and I got my Forest Loops for free as I killed the Giants that dropped them. I also got a Strawspun belt for free.
Money will come, build your character gradually if you don't have access to a higher level main character or rich friends/guild.
