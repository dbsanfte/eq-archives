1. It will be MUCH easier to buy and sell 'minor' loot that in the past has gone to vendors. Bronze armor is a good example - every once in a while somebody is looking for it in EC, but I'm not going to carry 50 stone of armor around in case somebody wants it. Ulthork Hide armor, Tizmak armor, etc.

2. It will not make much of a difference for the high-end items. High end items are always a seller's market anyway. You might find a JBB on a bazaar drone, but far more likely it'll be a live auctioneer like before.

3. Prices will come DOWN on "commodity" items, including, unfortunately, SOW potions. The first week, somebody will put SOW potions out at 100 plat per 10-dose; somebody else will put them out at 90 plat, and so forth, until they are selling for 70-80 plat per 10-dose.

4. There will be a market for tradeskill and foraged items! I can make Halas 10-lb meat pies, for example, which are great stat items, and sell them on a vendor. Fine steel armor need not be sold back to the vendor, sell it to n00bs and twinks and make a few more plat from it.
