Rakhal
I personally worked on 2hs 'cause it was a good way not to attract aggro and I also wanted to use Swarmcaller effectively. Around level 46 or so I got a Swarmcaller and was generally unsatisfied with the proc so I ditched it along with my intention to max 2hs skill at each level . I was unaware of the fact that the slowing rate of the proc increases with levels.

Within a few weeks after the release of PoP I got a Weighty Polearm for next to nothing and as a result my 2hs started going up again. At level 56 while soloing mobs with panic animal in PoP zones, I found myself badly in need of something that could slow the mob so that I didn't get hit as much between when panic animal wore off and my recasting it - bear in mind that there is still (at level 56) a significant chance of panic animal being resisted by mobs such as the festering rats in PoJ. This is where I took out my Swarmcaller again and found a great use for it. At level 56 it slows for much more (and lasts longer) than it did at level 46. It's cut my downtime by a huge amount. So unless you have an Earthcaller in your main hand when you're level 55+, Swarmcaller is a very viable option for slowing mobs while soloing. Granted, my dex isn't low.

Even if you have no intention of ever using a Swarmcaller for slowing at higher levels, surely it's worth keeping 2hs maxed for those times during hunts/raids that one of your guildie knights lets you borrow his/her Windblade! Major eye candy.
