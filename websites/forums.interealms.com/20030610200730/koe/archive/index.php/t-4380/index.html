I know its been a while since you all have heard from me or even seen me. I also know that my parting took many by suprise and perhaps was not understood by all, myself included.

I realize that I was allowing my real life issues and frusterations to bleed into the game (subconcously) and that was very unfair to everyone, including myself. I was not consous of it or I would have never let that happen. I think perhaps I was overlaying my real life problems onto events which transpired in the game, thus giving the game events more effects than they should.

Since that time I have played in EQ very little but have turned to facing my demons of RL that I had been running from. With my new found time I have been able to spend much more time taking care of family and have looked up old friends to laugh about the past and to be there for each other in the future. I have found a caring woman and have vowed to just enjoy the moments I have with her and see where life will bring us instead of rushing into anything.

I guess what I'm saying is I think I have found my center again and no longer feel as a boat listing in the water. I'm not sure that I will return to EQ as I like my new life now. I may come in from time to time just so I don't loose my skill and to stay in touch with you my friends. If you would like feel free to drop me a line at freeman_de@hotmail.com
I found that even after I left many of my friends in the keepers stayed true and where there for me even when I could not be for them, or even myself. All I can say from my heart is THANK YOU. My friends you will always have a special place in my heart even if we do not see each other on a daily bases as before. Stay in touch as I care about each of you.

On my search in the past few weeks I have found that some of my friends from the past (high school and college) have been called up and are standing in harms way for each of us. For them I post this message below. Merry Christmas and a bright new year to each if I don't see you before.

With Love,

Mish
