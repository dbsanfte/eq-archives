Begging is for:
1) getting true n00bs killed: "Go beg from the guards - they always hand out good stuff to beggars"

2) Swapping tall tales. Rumors abounded near release of EQ that you could beg decent items from mobs. One example was the cutthroat rings from the dervish.

3) Something to do with your pet while waiting for inc. You won't ever get anything, but your skill goes up.

4) Maxxing yet another completely useless skill.
