Kraaga
I have the key, although all my quest pieces, but one were obtained by a swarm kiting bard friend of mine. At 61 you should be able to kill all of the larvae. They were all blue to me and had 30 min timers on the corpse. It will take quite a while I would imagine and they are fairly dangerous. I've only killed them in groups.

In CoD itself, on Druzzil at least, it's BYoG and I cannot tank the puslings even when they are slowed. High damage double attacks still sneak through. Also they eat your pet alive and obviously disease DOTs are out. I've never landed one.

Lately, I've been grouping with a necro and the bard from above and after slowing, the necro gets aggro by DOTing with everything he's got then runs in tight circles while Bard and I DOT. Sometimes we add a Beastlord for faster kills. We stay away from Greater Puslings and it usually goes well.
