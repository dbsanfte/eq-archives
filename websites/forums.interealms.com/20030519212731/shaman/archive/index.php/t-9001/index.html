here's a trick. Activate levitate, dont worry about the breath. hand the gem to ooglyn, head to -1600, +4200 over the water.

Then just sit there and wait. the NPC's dont notice the dept change, and only care about vertical/horizontal alignment. Therefore you can sit on the surface of the water for the 30 minutes it takes for the important things to happen. None of the dialogue before Abe is important.

The other great thing about this is as some may know if you place yourself at just a few inches below the surface of water, you can see through the murkiness all the way through the water like it was air. And as you may know, the sharks spend all of their time near the bottom of the water. Using this method you can simply wait until Abes glowing white avataar shows up under the water, and then make sure no sharks are anywhere near him.

I did this at 45 with invis vs animals when I could have just as easily done it as soon as I got levitation.
