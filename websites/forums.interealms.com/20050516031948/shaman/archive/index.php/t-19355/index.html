I soloed the bad boy this evening for my friend's epic. In addition to my self-buffs, I had virtue. I did this because there was only myself, the 65 cleric and a 65 monk in attendance, and if my attempt went poorly I wanted there to be time for the cleric to keep me alive to help with the kill.

My pet was up with two weapons and lots of crap buffs. I had lots of crap buffs, focus, regen, bramblecoat, kilva's and greater potion of cold up. A 53 mage wandered by the fight in the middle and buffed me with his damage shield, but I clicked it off.

I slowed Rage first try after malos, then landed malicious decay and eventually malosinia after a few tries. I then used epic, Ultor and Saryrn throughout the fight, with one spirit call in the middle.

Reslowing was never difficult, and the time between hits was so long that channeling a heal, switching spells etc. was easy.

I logged the fight, and the parsing results are as follows:

Damage from Epic: 5652
Damage from Ultor: 8515
Damage from Saryrn: 12,800
Damage from Kilva's and Bramblecoat: 1617
Total damage from me: 28584

Damage from Gibantik: 12737
Damage from Spirit Call: 2992

Total damage inflicted on Ragefire: 44313

Melee damage inflicted by Ragefire on me: 8828

I don't have a total for his AE damage, although it was definitely the bulk of his damage output. His melee is really weak, with the max hit being 315. From looking through my chat window after the fight, I was partially resisting a lot of them. The only annoying thing is that it would often skip my SoW clicky and take out one of my other buffs.

At no point did my health drop below 50%, so this guy is definitely soloable without virtue.
