Philby
I always keep snare and root memed.

Tangling weeds is also good as it is a quick taunt and can be used if your snare gets resisted. You can use Tangling weeds if you get a quick add and want to run away a bit.

Flame lick is a nice taunt as well and can be used if a mob is snared by a higher level druid or if SK/necro is using their snare.

Always nice to keep heal memed although your present heal is starting to show its age. You can cast it and sit down to get the benefit of ncreased mana regeneration from sitting if you time it right.

The only buff spell I ever memed was SoW as if it dropped on somebody and you got a bad pull, death could occur. Plus if you go LD while in wolfform, having SoW available to cast quickly is handy.
Gavving
Well I can't remember my lvl 30 spell line up, and besides the spell list has changed a bit since I was level 30... But here's some basic suggestions:

Always keep memed:
Snare
Root
Heal

I then normally allocate 2 slots for nukes/dots. Depending on the level, target, and what I needed practice in (i.e. conjuration).

2 more slots I allocated for buff slots. Swap in and out depending on what buff you need to recast. Keeping SoW memed in this slot when possible is a good idea. You never know when you need to reSoW really fast with a mob on your tail and you don't have time to sit down and mem SoW.

The last slot I swap out for Fear if I'm fear kiting, or I used to keep the short duration DS spell in it. Now I also use that slot for Jolt/Cinder Jolt.

Time your buffs so you can rebuff stuff at the same time. I.e. Feet like Cat and SoW have the same duration. Buff with both of those at the same time, buffing with FLC first. Then when FLC starts to flash you know your SoW is going to go soon as well.

Oh yeah about the Heal spell. From mid 30s to 39, casting Skin like Rock to heal yourself is actually more mana efficient than the healing spell. Oh and don't discount using bind wound. Once you skill it up, it's actually as fast or faster than meding for mana to heal.
