First off, congratulations on maxing defense. I don't know how many times I've chatted with various shaman on my server only to find out they still don't have their defense maxed even at 50th or 55th level. Great work.

Now on to your question.

The reasion your piercing skill is lower than Offense would be because Shaman use different math to figure out max Piercing, One Hand Blunt or Two Hand Blunt than other classes.

Most skills for most classes use the math:

(level x 5) + 5

So at 41 your max cap for most of your skills will be 210.

However, Piercing, One Hand Blund and Two Hand Blunt, for a Shaman, use the following formula to figure out their cap:

(level x 4) + 4

Thus creating a max cap at level 41 of 168 in all three skills.
