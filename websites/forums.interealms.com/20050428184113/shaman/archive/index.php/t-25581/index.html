I am playing a Night Elf druid on Earthen Ring. Named Srathor. I play him when my main group isn't on for me to play Feather.

It is a extremly versitle class, but it is a major love or hate thing. So far I am really liking the ability to chose what to effect. Be it healing Tanking or some damage. Sure I do not do nearly the damage of the thief. Not even when in cat form. But I do enough to get by. It helps alot on the xp front killing 2-3 at a time and makes it aweful exciting fight wise.
