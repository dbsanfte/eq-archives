To each their own.

A couple of points that I noted to help make the adventure a success.

1). When you get to the entrance of the adventure buff up/ fm/ fh before entering (if you have time). We all ran separately and the first one there entered. This started the clock. By the time we were all in we had lost 6 minutes.

2) Figure out all the dross before hand. Roles/ML/etc. no time to do that and be efficient when in.

3) MAKE SURE ML DOES NOT HAVE LOOT NO DROP CLICKED TO AUTOMATICALLY. Our one augmentation drop was looted by the ML before anyone knew about it. Hope it was an honest mistake...........

4) Stay together and move quickly. Not rocket science but pulls of 3 mobs at once were common.

5) Don't wait around for the perfect group. We had shaman, necro, beastlord, chanter, cleric, rogue. No problems at all without a pure tank.

6) Get used to the lagfest in the wayfarers camps.

Finally, I was peeved to log on, get invited to a group, then realize I had to do a quest before I could begin. Wasn't 21 bucks enough? lol. Once I stopped moaning it took 10 minutes.

Anyway, that is my take, I think I am going to like this expansion.
