Best I could suggest is to find out what message board most of the Quellious players use. Most servers have at least 1 board, though I think generally there is only 1 "main" board. If you go on and introduce yourself, I'm sure that you'll find some guilds at about the level in the game who would like to talk to you.

My server (Lanys) is being merged with the 7th Hammer tonight. Already there's been a fair amount of talking back and forth as the higher guilds size each other up a bit and try to decide what this will mean for all of them. At least you have another month yet to start the process of making contacts and getting a feel for things.

Also, if you don't find a guild on Quellious, you have the option of going to any of the other servers due to the 1-time free transfer. It might not hurt to check out a few of the others to find out if there is a guild that meets your needs on one of them.
