Bukbuk
1. All shaman dot's stack with each other. As mentioned we have two lines, disease based and poison based. Generally the diseased based will have a skull graphic and the poison based will be the knife and bloody finger. Perhaps those are the old pics but I don't think so. It is worth picking up Sicken at you level, it's a great spell to pull with.

2. Any of the shaman races are viable, they all have their ups and downs. Pick the one you like best and you've made the right choice. Barbs are popular because they were the only good race shaman until cats and frogs came out. It's all personal preference on looks really.

3. Well seeing as you're a frog you'll be in the swamp area. I don't know how it is with the froggie revamp in Innothule though. I can tell you that if the mobs are getting to easy you can head to south ro. With planes of power you have the benefit of easy travel all over the world. You can head to crushbone, blackburrow, shar vahl, paludal caverns. Probably just about anywhere you went with your enchanter. Grouping is entirely up to how you feel that night. Although it will be easier in groups once you hit lvl 9 with sow and light healing.

Good Luck!

1.) All Shaman DoTS stack on each other. No matter if they are all poison all Disease it doesn't matter so get them all.

2.) Race is really irrelevant, in the end it just doesn't matter. Froggies are viable and they do a cool backflip when casting. (Truth is Iksar are the best, but since you had to ask you're not worthy of being one.) JK
You see alot of high level barbs because when most older players started Barbarian was a wise choice because of faction purposes. Them not being KOS, with PoP and the Nexus that's a moot point now.
Experience penalty is the one thing I hate about Leezards and Trolls its way harsh at upper levels.

3.) What I did was when I got a new level I would solo until I raised my weapon, casting, and defense skills. I would then group or duoing as grouping and duoing may leave some of your skills behind and they are a beast to raise at higher levels. Max them first then group. If you can travel to Kunark you can level up to 18 in Kurn's Tower. I stayed in the Field of Bone pit until 10 then went to Kurn's until 18. Crazy exp there and I made a killing selling bone chips.

Hope this helps

Later on, summon companion is only available from the EC tunnel, and I got my pet spells from the Barbarian fishing village in West Karana, by the river. I think its west anyway. Not sure if they might be in the library now or not :) There's also the spell vendor in FV, who will trade a few of the higher level spells with you for a random different one (of four) in return.
