Caelum
Hope its not too late to post some advice after facing off against the beast myself.

First you got to be right underneath Aary to get slow off. This likely means you're going to get hit by the mana drain. So make sure you have someone in your group to heal the rest of them, because you will be OOM after slowing.

Second, after slowing Aary, you will want to canni back your drained mana for when you have to reslow. However do not do this until the mana drain wears off, or else you'll just be wasting HPs.

Anyways, hope this little bit helps, good luck tonight.

RedChief
If you can do Aary, the only dragins you will have a problem with is Vyemm and Lady Nev. Vyemm has a nasty knockback and is a pain to slow (disease only). Lady Nev is more of a endurance fight. I ended up making a macro key to sit, stand, and attack because of her AE.

Every other dragon fight on the front side of NToV is about positioning, be it the dragon or raid.
