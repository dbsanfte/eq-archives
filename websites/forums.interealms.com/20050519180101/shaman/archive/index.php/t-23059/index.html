Icebreaker
If you enjoy solo'ing I would suggest that you go for Natural Durability 3 and Lightning Reflexes 5, ND3 is a nice chunk of hit points which is never a bad thing, and with LR5 you take a noticeable ammount less damage. Spirit Call and VP are also a nice addition as they give you ways to better deal with certain situations. SCRM is great as well, if you get SCRM, ND3, and LR5 you will find that you have a much easier time staying alive. Getting hit less, taking more dmg, and Quiesence lasting longer can't be beat.

Hail,

You can't go wrong with getting spell casting mastery. :) It will save you mana on EVERY spell cast. This will make a big difference with SCM3 on your spell-casting endurance in any situation. I'd recommend this next for the most bang-for-your-AA-buck.

If you are really hp challenged, ND1-2-3 might be better first so you can safely hit Canni5, but I'd still recommend SCM early. If you do alot of adventures, Radiant Cure 1 and Virulent Paralysis 1 are pretty cheap investments for some extra tools in your arsenal.
