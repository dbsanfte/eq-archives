You two would ROCK in SolA until about lvl40.

See all the SolA posts in this forum.

Things to love about SolA:

1) It gives an extra 18% exp over other zones in it's level range.
2) Everything higher then Young Goblin drops Fine Steel and Plat.
3) No one is ever there.
4) Tons of Goblins to kill from lvl25-34.
5) Plenty of gnomes and clockworks to kill from lvl34-40.
6) Some "rare" drops that can still be sold for some decent plat (still a little amazed that after all these years I sold a pair of Impskin Gloves in the Bazaar for 900pp just a few months ago).
7) Mobs have little to no hitpoints (compared to Kunark, Velious and Luclin mobs of the smae lvl range).
8) Mobs hit like sissies (compared to Kunark, Velious and Luclin mobs of the smae lvl range).
