Its not so much of mapping things as just understanding how BoT is arranged. I'll try to help.

When you zone into BoT, you are in the graveyard. Exit this area up the stairs into another room and directly across from you is a small room that is referred to as SS or Safe Spot. You can sit in this room afk and be reasonably assured that you won't get trained.

Leaving SS you go to your right and up some stairs into a large "courtyard" type area. This is the point of reference for the rest of the standard BoT camps.

There are four "halls" you can kill in. North, South, East and West. Some of the halls are fairly small . . . North and West for instance. Some are much larger and can support two (East) or even three (South) groups killing in them.

UE for instance stands for Upper East. I've also seen it called Deep East. Most of the times people who do this camp will set up in the room that is near the little strip of water on the map. There are a lot of places they could be, but usually it's this room.

LE stands for Lower East. Most times people doing this camp are actually in the courtyard of BoT pulling the mobs down the ramp.

US stands for upper south. I've also heard this called Deep South. This usually means the group is in the huge room far into the South hall. You can't miss this room, it's freakin' gigantic.

MS stands for Mid South. It is, strangely enough, about halfway between LS and US. There is a room with a bunch of giant sized tables in it that is typically used as the "camp spot".

LS stands for Lower South. Most of the time LS is pulled to the courtyard area.


It doesn't take but a few visits to start to understand the camps in BoT. They aren't really much more than descriptions of where the party is killing at.

The camp names vary wildly depending on server. If Sudedor is on the same server as you (I didn't look) then use his terminology.

US=Upper Sand on E. Marr, not Upper South. I don't remember if Sand wing is to the south or not.
UB=Upper Blizzard, UL=Upper Lightning, UW=Upper Wind. MSL=middle sand left. LS=Lower Sand. The lower camps typically pull nearby the stairs right to the area on the side nearest their wing. Lower camps on E. Marr frequently get trained repeatedly by middle and upper camps (and even by another lower camp's mobs) camp that have an incompetent group.

The named spawnpoints are also sometimes called by their mob's first name. So the UB camp might also be called out as "Oreen" when someone asks for a camp check.

The 'courtyard' area on E. Marr is the spot in BoT where the sparky dildo is (to enter the Agnarr lair), not the area nearby the 'safe room'. Courtyard is typically the gather point used for BoT Tower & Agnarr raids on E. Marr.

I personally avoid BoT except for raids most of the time. I recommend always zoning in to BoT invisible. ;)
