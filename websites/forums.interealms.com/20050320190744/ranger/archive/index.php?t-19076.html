LOCATION
Odus. Take the Nexus to Toxx forest and follow the wall, keeping it on your left until you find a paved area, go through the tunnel and you will enter paineel. Walk straight through making sure not to aggro the guards, stick to the tight wall until you find an opening, there will be a fountain and to your left the warrens.

LEVEL RANGE
Approximately 6-20 for best exp,

6-8 stick to the tunnel entrance, there are many trains and this is only place you have a good chance of escaping from. Blue mobs and aan occasional bat which can range from blue to yellow, good exp.

8-10 Wonder down the right tunnel a bit, try not to aggro the light blues and try to pick up a few of the higher mobs, sentinels etc.

10-12 Go down the right tunnel into the the door in the left wall, there will be a square room full of kobalds which should be a challenge for your leve;l, hug the left wall and go down the narrow passage, there will be a slightly higher mob here and in the room to your right another which will probably aggro, if its a shaman i would attack it 1st, once it is clear go to the jail cell on the right and this will be your pulling area for levels to come. Mkae sure the 2 slightly tougher mobs you just killed spawn greens to ensure no adds when pulling here. Pull from the surrounding area being careful of Packmaster Dlesh.

12-16 From the entrance tunnel go down the left and find an area on the wall where you can pull easy blues, alternatively you can go to the bats near the bat lord, these wont aggro you but other kobalds passing through might, tougher ones.

16-20 Follow the right tunnel all the way to the end until you get to the princes throne room. pull to the opposite wall or into the little room just right of you before you get to the bridge. If you manage to get a groupyou can clear the place and maybe kill the prince.

NAMED / LOOT

The Prince - Drops forlorn armor and cold iron minor pieces, also drops small pouch of crushed bone dice for quest in Stone Brunt, yields a right click snare item, charged. Might have to clear surrounding healers before engaging him. He will be found at the bottom of the right tunnel.

King - Drops crown of Forlorn (nice), Cold iron BP and other pieces of cold iron, also drops a idol very rarely, 10 charges of level 1 necromancer snare spell clinging darkness. In the King room through a labarynth of tunnels. Shaman will attack you aswell as kings pet.

Warlord - Drops occasional Cold Iron and drops a below par 2hs 10 40 or something similar, PH is a brawler, lorekeeper may aggro on you, located in a small room off the king room.

Lorekeeper - Drops 4 scrolls which are needed in a erudin quest, yields about 50pp reward, located in a small room off of the king room, warlord may aggro.

High Shaman - Drops a runed bokken staff, not worth alot but a few nice resists, also drops occasional cold iron and a necklece for purposes i dont know, located next to king in king room and the king will aggro.

Bat Lord - Drops a hat which azzar habib wants, is located in the long coridoor near Stone brunt zone point and the quest yields a 3int belt plus stats.

Huntmaster - Located off to the right of the long coridoor from stone brunt zone, drops forlorn bow and arrow. lower level gear. lots of greater shamans in there to heal him up.

Mighty Bear Paw - drops a hide that can be used in tailoring, not sure the result but is classed as an animal and hits hard for his level, located in a small coridoor off of the long coridoor near stone brunt zone.

The Muglewump - Is actually a dragon, level 25ish but you would need to be 32ish to solo, casts slow and drops lots of invisibility potes and alot of no drop caster items, plus a shield with 3 charges of rune on, no drop, located through a large network of tunnels.

The Diviner - Drops a mantle and a bowl, bowl is for a quest and the mantle is for wis casters, located in bat caves which can be accessed from king room and muglwump room.

Smithy - Drops a hammer that fits in range slot and has 2 str on, located at the end of right tunnel from warrens entrance.

Foodmaster - Drops a large box, weighs 20.00 and i dont know what to do with it, also drops BBQ erudites, banquet sized, located at the end of the right tunnel from the warrens entrance.

Diviner - Located in batling tunnels accessable from small room off of king and muglewump, drops a 2 wis shoulder itemand a 3wis int quest 1hander. Not for the faint hearted.
