Kriel Darkbane
Primarily, go to the bazaar and buy the highest AC stuff for him that you can afford. You can get some pretty good stuff for low prices on most servers nowadays. HP is secondary to AC for the pre-50 levels, and wisdom well below those two. You want efficiency, which means taking less damage.

Something else that'll really help is a regeneration bp. Even an iksar ceremonial bp (5 hp / tick regen) will help a ton. If you have or can beg/borrow/steal a fungi, it'll help HUGELY, assuming your shaman isn't vah'shir and thus can wear it.

A cheap horse or lizard would also help a lot, as your shaman can regain mana while getting hit (as long as you don't have autoattack on). You'll want to melee enough to raise one combat skill at least, but you'll have the ranger DS's to help with damage. Once you reach 34 and the shaman has a pet, you can likely do very well with the shaman sitting on a horse for mana regen, casting occasionally, and doing most of the damage with DS and pet.

At L 45 the best thing you can have is a JBB. A JBB and a horse combined will you you level very fast, even solo, and even faster with the ranger to heal to let you canni faster. Come to think of it, if you could beg/borrow/buy/steal a JBB for a little while when he hits 45, just with the ranger snaring stuff for him, he could level superfast into the low 50's.

Oh, and for the lower levels, a good weapon will really help. The Rune Etched Icewurm Fang is just about ideal (12 dam 19 delay, I think?) for beating mobs to death through those first 20 or 30 levels. Or you can look for a good 2h'r of some kind, that may be cheaper.

Kriel / Untar
