Anyway, ah yes, the good ol' race discussion. Before the lurking idiots like Ogrym crawl out of their holes to throw in their biased 2cp worth, let me offer you an objective response.

Barbs -- pros: low EXP penalty, good starting faction, can wear all types of shaman armor (save for race/deity-specific stuff of course).
--cons: night blind, most common shaman race (this bugs some people, but I could care less), no innate racial advanages (such as unstunnability, natural regen, natural nightvision, etc.)

Ogres -- pros: Awesome melee stats, cannot be stunned from the front, innate night vision.
--cons: Horrible starting faction (although, old world faction really isn't a big issue anymore), high EXP penalty (10%), more limited in what they can wear (compared to Barb), low starting WIS.

That's just a very basic guide to the two races. What it ultimately boils down to is what suits you the most. Not having fun with your Barb? Then remake an ogre. Same goes for the opposite.
