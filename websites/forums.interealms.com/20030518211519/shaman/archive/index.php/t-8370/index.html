ShakoBoy
Heres a couple ideas...

Earrings: Do the Earring of Solstice quest and buy and Silver Hammer Earring.

Face: Crystal Spider Eyes

Neck: Tracking Wolf Collar (This might be a little expensive depending on your server) or a Amulet of Insight.

Fingers: Shadel Bandit Ring/Circled Toe Bones

Bracers: Barbed Rubicite Bracer/Coldain Military Bracer (Or a JBB if you have that kind of cash)

Belt: Spider Fur Belt is a nice cheap uprade.

Back: Ancient Seahorse Cloak thingy.

Chest: At your lvl the Iksar Regen was ok but it was getting to un-noticable to make a huge difference so I dropped it and bought a Polished Tin Mail ChestPlate thing. It has fairly nice stats and is pretty cheap.

Arms: Hardened Grey Flesh Sleeves/Crystal Chitin Armplates.

Legs: Brown Chittin Leggings/Barbed Rubicite Leggings (Again, the second is more expensive so server prices come into play again =\ )

Boots: Tribal War Boots maybe?

Shoulder: If you like do trade skills I would start the Shawl quests. They are a fun break from the exp grind and the 7th shawl isn't to bad, and the 8th is great. If you don't like tradeskills maybe you should like for a Peerless/Matchless Dragon Wing Mantle or an ODS.

Range: Goranga Idol/Begginers Magic Manual

There ya go, that's about all the updates I can think of heh. I hope these opinions help you a little bit in deciding what new gear to buy :)

Btw, about how much wis you should sacrafice. Just find a number of wis you feel comfortable with and try to stick at that lvl well improving your gear more towards HP/AC. My lvl of wis comfort was 175-185. but it all depends on the player =p
