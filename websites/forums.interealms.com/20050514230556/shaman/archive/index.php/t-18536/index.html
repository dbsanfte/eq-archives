Piglet
Well I sorta agree and disagree with you both. :p Your wis should be whatever your usual group dictates. If you usually solo or play in groups with healers, then ignore the wis stat and let it rise as you get better gear - go hp/ac all the way. If you are often the primary healer, the extra wis will come in handy as long as you don't go wearing jasper rings or plat ruby veils etc (ie don't sacrifice everything else for a few wis). Experience will tell you whether you need a bigger mana pool or can focus on your hp gear, although the golden rule is hp>all with canni (if you have time to regen the mana in-between using it, then it doesn't matter how big your mana pool is).

The Fungi will do you until the late 50s if you solo with melee. I can't remember how long I melee'd until, but I do know that when I only had an hour or so to play I would mess around in BW in my early 50s and a Fungi then would have made a LOT of difference.

As for the rest, you can solo as well as anyone else tbh. And melee in a group isn't out of the question either if you're not healing and the mob is debuffed. Horses for courses, you can do a pretty good job of most things as a shaman, the important thing is to do what you enjoy, that's what keeps the class fun.
