I know it used to work as the Druids you spoke to described it, but from what I can tell it is currently possible to run around, jump, turn, etc., catch the med ticks, and see that mana actually put to use when casting spells. What used to happen, I recall, was that you'd see your mana bar go up, but as soon as you cast a spell the bar would drop way down (the mana from medding wasn't really there). I just went in and tested it, though perhaps I wasn't doing it properly, and it appears that you can now move around and med all at once.

Even before, however, I know that cannidancing did work as long as you just stood straight up, cast Cannibalize, and sat down. If you moved or turned between ticks you lost the med mana.

Hope that made sense.
