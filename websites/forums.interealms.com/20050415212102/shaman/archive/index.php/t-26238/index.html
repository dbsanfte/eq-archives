Normal single-target rampage does not hit the first person on hate list. AE rampage will, and mobs that flurry with a "Goes on a RAMPAGE! message" will hit the first person on the hate list - Mith Marr is a good example of a mob that gives a ramp message, but doesn't actually ramp - but plain old rampage will never hit you if you're top on the list.
