bonestomper
sweet! i know this one.

as you go out to EFrost from the guard Bandl, take a right and hug the wall.

around the corner is two huts for goobs, that is where casters spawn. if you decide to do this, i would recommend you med and heal at the standard (flag on a stick) away from the huts, mobs cycle thru there often that will kill you.

it will be the longest camp of your entire life.

you can start there at lvl 4 and get exp till 7, you will still get exp for turning in the shattered caster necks later.

the NON shattered beads are one of the rarest things in the game, i would suggest you look at the initiate symbols of the tribunal and just run to Qeynos and back
bonestomper
more hot info, if you do this.

loot is not poor for the lvl and they gobos drop wrist pouchs, boxes and the occassional pack back.

save the regular (non caster) ice necks for the quest in the Warrior guild.

you will get random 5th lvl spells for the shattered caster beads.

they also drop an inordinate amount of booze. beer and stuffs.

as you face the huts, there is 3 spawns. the one on the right, outside the hut, and 1 in each hut. casters spawn at all three spots.

depending on lvl, you might want to tag them to Bandl the first time to break up the spawn.

the weapon stinks, but it was my first magic weap over 2 years ago.

now the tribunal symbol quest gets you a neck slot item that summons a hammer, and is far easier to do.

it is that rare
