Goldark
Totall agree on the rings.

Here are a few more ideas, many of which you can get yourself or with a group of friends.

Elder Wolf Hide for shoulders - soloable I should think, certainly easy with a friend. Can take a little while, depends on your luck - I got mine in about an hour. Drops off the named wolf in Hollowshade, spawns in the back of the cave.

Coldain Military Wristguard - search the board for info. Would
probably need a few friends at your level, not 100% sure though. Extended Affliction 3 and reasonable stats makes this a must-have item considering how easy it is to obtain.

Some people hate it, but if you don't loath the place try going to Karnor's Castle. Jarsath armour (no drop) drops there, and most people will pass on it. It'd make a good upgrade for your legs, although the legs aren't such a common drop.

You could consider trying to get groups in Old Sebilis - if you get in the crypt you are in for some nice goodies - JBB, ODS, Heiro cloak - all worth a lot of pp, and all nice shammy kit. And if you don't get a group there, it'll still be nice xp and decent loot from the gems and stuff at other camps.

Ivory Imbued Collar or Twisted Metal Torque would be good neck upgrades. The collar is around 200pp on my server.

Go to Velketor's - Crystal Spider Eyes for face are a significant upgrade. Frenzy camp is good loot in general. Cystal Chitin arms are another Velks drop that'd be a good upgrad. If you can swing an invite to the UD camp, spider fur belt is a nice drop there too.

Finally, I'd suggest saving up for an Iksar Ceremonial Breastplate (about 5k) - more AC and 5hp/tick regen, which will be nice until 58 at least, or even higher.

Good luck.
