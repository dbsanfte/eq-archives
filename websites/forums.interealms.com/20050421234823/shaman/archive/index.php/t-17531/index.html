If you think you should be able to zone into Tower of Sol Ro, the Elemenal Planes of Air/Water/Earth, or the Plane of Fire, but you can't, try this:

First, make sure you have all of the following completed to enter Tower of Solusek Ro:
Grummus kill, Carpin Cycle (/key will show key to lower depths of lxanthom or something like that), Bertox kill
Hedge Trial, Terris Thule kill, Keeper of Sorrows kill, Saryrn kill
Manaetic Behemonth kill, Tallon Zek kill, Vallon Zek kill
Plane of Justice Trial (any), Aerin'Dar kill*, Dragon Trial, Princess Trial, Villagers Trial, Mithanial Marr kill
* you can skip AD kill and still get ele plane flagged, but you need to be raided into HohoB under 85% rule to do so. Just get AD flag anyway because being raided into HohoB is a pain.

To be able to enter the Planes of Earth/Air/Water you need all of the above flags, Askr the Lost's key quest into BoT, and Agnarr kill, as well as Rallos Zek kill.
To be able to enter the Plane of Fire, you need to have the 3 Ele Planes flag, and get Sol Ro kill.
To be able to enter Sol Ro's chamber, you need the 3 Ele Planes flag, plus all 5 minis in Sol Ro, though it's possible to skip the minis by being raided into Sol Ro's chamber under the 85% rule (get all 5 minis anyway because raiding people into Sol Ro's chamber is a pain)

So you have all the kills, and you still can't get in!
This is what you do to fix up your flags (get a Sow because you are going to be running around like a madman)

To fix up the Bertox series of flags:
1. Go to Adler Fuirstel outside of the PoD
2. Hail him
3. Say "what ward" to get a character flag
4. Go to the Seer in Pok and unlock memories
5. Go to Adler Fuirstel again
6. Hail him
7. Go to the sick room in potranq
8. Find Elder Fuirstel lying on the ground all alone in there
9. Hail him and get a character flag
10. Hit the Seer with an unlock memories
11. Find Elder Fuirstel again and hail him and get a character flag
12. If he says "welcome back friend.....blah blah blah" you have your full Bertox Flag

To fix up your Saryrn flag:
1. Find Adroha in the sick room in potranq sitting next to Elder Poxbourne
2. Hail her
3. Say "tortured by nightmares" and get a character flag
4. Hit the Seer and unlock memories
5. Find Elder Poxbourne and hail him to get a character flag
6. Find Fahlia in the sick room sitting next to another sick dude.
7. Hail her
8. Say "what plane of torment" and "i will go" to get a character flag
9. Hit the Seer and unlock memories
10. You should have full Saryrn flag now

To fix up your PoTactics flag:
1. You need someone to let you into the factory in PoI if you don't have the flag to open the door. (the flag comes from the dragon in zone. It's unnecessary for anything except opening the door for the entire raid in PoI)
2. Hail the gnome you see there and say "i will test the machine" to get a character flag
3. Hit the Seer and unlock memories to get access to Plane of Tactics

To properly get your PoJ trial flag:
1. Find Mavuin in PoJ
2. hail him
3. uh....read what he says and say the [bracketed] words to get a character flag
4. Head to the Tribunal in the middle east side of the Trial area
5. Hail him
6. say "i will plead mavuin's case" (SPELLING COUNTS. NO PARTIAL CREDIT FOR TYPING PART OF IT WRONG)
7. Head back down to Mavuin
8. Hail him and get a character flag

To get your preflag for the Plane of Fire:
1. go to the red drake outside of PoF
2. hail him
3. Follow his [bracketed] text until he gives you a character flag

To properly get your Tower of Sol Ro flag:
1. get all your above flags fixed up first
2. Get a levitate. If you don't, get a cleric to stand next to the bottom of the elevator in PoK because there's a good chance you will fall for 20000 points of damage.
3. Hit the seer and unlock memories
4. Go up the elevator in the library
5. Hail the Grand Librarian Maelin. say "what lore". say "i have new information"
6. Hit the seer and unlock memories
7. Go up the elevator in the library
8. Hail the Grand Librarian Maelin. say "what lore". say "i have new information"
9. Hit the seer and unlock memories
10. Go up the elevator in the library
11. Hail the Grand Librarian Maelin. say "what lore". say "i have new information"
12. I'm not exactly sure how many times you have to do this, but it's at least about 2 or 3 or 4 times.
13. You can stop this cycle of visiting the grand librarian when he says something about gathering some stuff from the elemental planes to power a machine to take you to the plane of time to rescue Zebuxoruk
