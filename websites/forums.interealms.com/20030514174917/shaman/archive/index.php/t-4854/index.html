gummox
Pottery is BY FAR the easiest tradeskill to skill up in. You can get 122 skill in pottery with 10 plat or less making medium bowls.

Buy 8 stacks each of:
meduim bowl sketch
block of clay
flask of water

Combine one each in the pottery wheel, you'll fail more often than you will suceed at first, but that's ok because you only spent 10p so far anyway. Once you do start succeding regularly, feed your destroy button. You can't sell them without firing them, and even then they barely worth more than the firing sheet.

If you're skilling up in Cabilis, there's only one water vendor in West Cabilis. I don't remember his name, but he's in the cluster of Kloks east of the Necro guild.

If you want more info on pottery, or tradeskills in general, www.eqtraders.com is the tradeskiller's bible.
