Because they are a tribal people, but also the chosen of Mithaniel Marr.

Frogloks were created from half of Mithaniel Marr's lifeforce, and barbarians were created from the other half. Froglok's fought and fought to try to free Mithaniel Marr from Cazic Thule's minions, but couldnt do it until the barbarian horde showed up to help. Barbarians were the first 'men' (humans are our country cousins), yet we stayed 'primitive' and cant be clerics or worship Mithaniel Marr (unless you play on the diety server) and humans can do both.

So the frogs worship Mithaniel Marr (truth and valor), so it only makes sense they can be paladins. But they are 'primitive' so they gave them shamans. The only reason I see that they got cleric also is because they didnt think it was right to have a hybrid class and not the parent class also.

My personal opinion has always been why not just let any race be any class? You can still have racial hostilities, because that really doesnt have to do with good and evil - that's just a matter of perspective. Dwarves think ogres and trolls are evil because those races like to eat them. I doubt ogres and trolls think dwarves are good, except in respect to how they taste.

You will still wind up with many of the same common combinations, because certain races are just more suited to particular classes. Yes, some people might make troll wizards - but they (the trolls, not the person) would be pretty stupid wizards. It's still going to be more advantageous to be a high elf, erudite, dark elf, etc. because they are smarter. It would however, bring a little bit more diversity to the population.
