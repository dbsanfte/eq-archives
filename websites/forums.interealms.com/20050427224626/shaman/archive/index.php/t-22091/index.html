Kromnik
Droga will be your godsend. You will emerge pale, grizzled, and full of plat from selling all the loot from the named. Start out on the easier stuff to learn the place, and then move up to the harder mobs. At 63, you should be able to handle Flame Master camp no problem. You really only have to kill the ones around him, and that should leave you plenty of time to canni up before respawns. Boots of Kingly splendor are common, and fetch 4-5k on TM. He can spawn 1x, or multiple times in a row.

Bind yourself outside droga, use spirit stone to gate out, bank, gate back, run down before next spawn.
