darkpaw
SD Exit is where you can kill with no adds easy if you're a semi decent puller. Theres 6 static mobs and 2 wanderers. Just pull to the tunnel near the exit and you'll be fine. You will occasionally get two on your first pull once you're KoS if you don't pull well.

Natyn's is good if you don't want to ruin your faction. Its harder to pull and doesn't drop as much cash stuff though.

Either would be painfully slow before you get bane of nife though, but the exp is good if you can do it.
