It's been a while since I hunted the guy, but here is what I remember....

The named overseers ar ein the cave commplex heading to Acrylia Caverns. Harmony will do you no good, the grimlings in Grimling Forest are immune.

The Overseer himself is an interesting mob, he hits pretty hard for a level 40, but has level 40 appropriate hitpoints. He also likes to summon. The other grimlings that inhabit his immediate vicinity seem to be on a pretty fast timer as well, so care will be needed to keep them from respawning on top of you.

The main thing you need is the capability to kill a level 40 mob quickly. We went in with a level 50ish group and were fine, but I was surprised how quickly his cave respawned.
