Date: Nov 30

Time: Start time for binding will be 8:00 p.m. est

Duration of the War: 60 min.

Purpose: Have great fun - see story line posted in Tales

There will be a great /guildwar that will rage for one hour or until one side is vanquished.

The server will be made know in two days hence when each team has a leader and proposed guild name.

Sign up under the flag you wish to represent. Friends, recruits and members join in the fun.
