Dagor
I had convinced my wife(46 rogue) that I needed an elder wolf hide From Ghowlik, even if the MOBs in that cave were seriously green to us. Well after buffing a couple of newbies, I ran over to meet where wife who had been casing the sonic wolf cave. I proceeded to summon up a pet, talisman us both, alacrity her and the pet...and we started to pull. I am low on mana so I start to cann away. well about 2 minutes into our pulling, My wife says "Uh Oh" we are getting pounded. She is right her health bar is suddenly at half. I stood up, and she is fighting half a dozen or more wolves right at the cave entrance. I tell her "what are you doing there? pullem back to here" I have about 60% life, and not quite 50% mana..so we're fine. Well she comments that she "can't come back there...something is wrong". I assume that the trouble is video card related(Something wierd happened to me once while pulling), so I trot on over and dropped a heal on her. That pulled my dog into the mix, defending my honor. I pulled dog dog off, and siced him onto the wifes target. Then I saw it..."Ganeker....You shall not evade me!!" What the heck!?! "OK hon, we are in some kinda trouble here". I figured we had happened upon a rare spawn Uber Mob in the cave. Then I notice that first my pet isn't dead yet, and that the thing beating on him is Green. So my wife decides to go seriously on the offensive. OK lets kill'em all. There were three of the sonic wolf defenders, that were summoning us. They were about 10 strides apart from each other, and since we were on two different agro lists, we couldn't double team them without being summoned away every 8 or 10 seconds. We had two defenders dead, but wolves had continued to join the fray from inside the cave. In the end we had the defenders dead, but we were both at about 5% life, and no mana. My wife was the first to fall, and I fell to the last of the wolves. It was nip and tuck at the end. My Chloro was keeping me in it, and I just couldn't coax one last proc from my Barbarian spritist hammer.


Anyway...just a warning for those who want to drop in and pay Ghowlik a visit. If we had been prepared we would've come out fine, but the element of surprise got us. It was actually a very fun fight, and turned out all right as we were able to find a cleric willing to trek 3 zones to rez us. Level 20 something MOBs that summon. What'll they think of next?
