Given you don't raid atm and already have Malo........

If you two box a melee and don't have primal/feral avatar or a primal weap on your melee then get Ferine Avatar.
Also if you are grouping you can happily keep 4 to 5 melee Ferine Avatar'd the whole time. God know how much DPS that adds!

Blood of Saryn is pretty awesome when soloing OR duoing (if you tank).

When soloing it wont break root (particularly handy when combined with 4-minute root + Ulthor).

I have to admit I cast Ferine Avatar ALOT more than I cast Blood of Saryn...... but then I dont solo that much and duo a lot less than I used too.
