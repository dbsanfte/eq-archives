In order for corpse summon in ToV, the necro/SK must be in the room you port into ToV at, only some certain spot in there works (probably the evac point).

Sorta confusing calling the Sham Shennanigan SS with the armor from HoT being called Skyshrine armor ;) Forgot that they had the same abbreviation till I saw your post.

As for how hard, if you can't solo stomples (hits about 350 triple) then your chances at thrones drops dramatically. Doing him requires torpor and some nice gear, preferable also Tnarg's.

3 minute root also helps a ton for AiW4 twins. If you want to practice at what kind of pull that is go solo the entrance guys in CT, then imagine that with no zoneout nearby just in case things go wrong :( It isn't hard when you get used to it, but not everyone likes doing 2 summoning mobs at once.

If you got those 2, probably take you a month of solid hunting to get the drops you need, but your luck may vary considerably (white crown and blue throne will take forever). if you can't do stomples you may as well plan on nearly 4 months, and if you can't even do the twins then I don't see you getting them before you get sick of the zone unless you are VERY lucky or buy your cards in the bazaar.


Getting the doll for the armor requires a ton of work as well, or random luck. Need to farm the plane for parts of the words of wealth. You'll need a ton of them to make a gift box, then just pray either you get shaman on first try or that someone with a shaman doll is interested in trading with you. Depending on your luck again, this could take you a couple months in addition to getting your thrones.
