Jahvah
Easiest way is to go up the far right of the beach, hugging the wall. Theres a couple of open spots on that wall and the back wall of the beach you can set up camp.

When you port down from PoK you need to do two things.

1- Cast Levitate
2- Cast invis

Then follow the path on the right side of the beach described above. You'll reach a large cave. Follow that cave all the way to Dulak. Check your map tool to see exactly how to get there. If you don't have a map, check www.eqtool-box.com. You should be able to download a detailed map of every zone in Broken skull.

At level 44 pretty much everything on the beach will be too low to gain much exp from. You'll be far better off in Dulak at the zone line.

Remember, the only mobs that see invis in Gunthak are the undead, and a few luggalds so just stear clear of them and make the run all the way through the cave to the Dulak zone. Fight the scouts at the entrance until they go light blue, then move inward. When you hit fifty you can take the stormwave and probably the Lady Dirulia.
