Popu
Just looking for some opinions here and wondering about usefulness.

Pet discipline (6 AA) - makes your pet stay no matter what. Will take ae damage while sitting there, will die when owner goes invis.

Suspended Minion 2 (8AA) - Make your pet go away while you are in your zone fully buffed and armed. Resummon him at will. You can invis w/o pet poofing. Pet does not take AE damage. You can keep him suspended during an AE fight until MT calls assist. Can you suspend pet while it's in combat? You can have a pet standing by to summoned at will.

If I am understanding this right, why would someone spend 6AA on pet discipline instead of going for SM2? Having your pet suspended on pulls and the unsuspending it seems to give it very close to the same functionality of /pet hold with some added benefits.

Am I missing something here?

taraddar
Don't have pet discipline yet but do have Suspend minion 2. Couple things with suspend minion.

1) it has a cast time. It's not hugely long but it is still noticable. I'd guess proably 3-4 seconds or so.

2) You can't suspend a pet if it's on any mobs aggro list.

Yes it is possible to buff and equipe a pet and hold it in reserve. I've used it for RZ to keep pet out of the way till engage is called.

SM wouldn't do anything in a situation where you already had your pet out and where trying to control him in an ae. It also doesn't seem to be useful when your pulling because of the cast time.

Either way I can't really see getting either aa till your 200+ aa's really. If you've got that many you really should be able to just get both.
