One of the best places to hunt in a group is the Paludal Caverns just south of Shadow Haven. Get to Shadow Haven from the Bazaar. Paludal is impossible to solo, so you need a group of 3-6 people. The recondite bandits give a lot of experience and drop bronze brigandine armor along with Recondite Insignia Rings which give 2 to Agility. I suggest that you are level 15 or stronger before attempting this. Good luck to you.....
