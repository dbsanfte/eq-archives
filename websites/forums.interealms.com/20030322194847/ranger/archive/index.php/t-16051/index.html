Samanthie
Well hon, I understand your plight. At level 20 you may not have saved up very much pp yet, and you might have the funds to buy only 1 nice weapon. Try buying a centi short sword first. If you have the money left over at level 20 then buy a centi hammer. Both are cheap on our server, and you can use them for skill gains. Carry around a piercer, a 2HS, and a 2HB as well, even if it is nothing more than fine steel at first.

During those times when you are LFG, waiting for friends to come, or are generally just bored, look for a group that is killing mobs and ask them if you can hit on thier Blue conned mobs for skill level. Most groups will not mind the extra DPS, and you can work on your skills while waiting for what ever reason. When you hit your skill cap for that level, work on 1HB then 2HS then 1HP and finally 2HB (or what ever route you want to take)

If money is not an object, well then take the time to select really nice weapons that you can swap around for the different situations that you will be in.
