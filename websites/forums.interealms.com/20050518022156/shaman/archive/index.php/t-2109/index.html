Sebazy
Yeldema is by far the easiest, no aoe, comparitively low hps and weak melee, occasionally she rampages, but very rarely enough to hurt dogdog significantly. The only thing that makes her a challenge is the roamers nearby.

If you want to step up and try the aoers, I'd recommend Gafala as a practice mob, she is close to my prefered fighting area and hence can be engaged fairly safely.

The aoers have a load more hps and damage output than Yeld though, so be prepared to change your strategy dramatically. Get the hang of her and you'll pretty much be able to solo the entire NW nest area with a resonable success rate.

After that theres the Tov valley dragons... A couple of them are dooable, but they are extremely challenging and risky.
