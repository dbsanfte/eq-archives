Azhdarkhos
As far as I can tell, there isn't any timer on the Undead Crusader spawns - it's a straight-up matter of one (the rare one) of a set of skellies on a particular spawn table. I had a couple of instances where I managed to catch Crusaders within a spawn or two after the last one went down (less than a half hour apart.) From what I can tell, the wandering skellies in that part of the tower (second floor from the top) can all spawn as a Crusader, but none of the static skellies will.

When I first started camping the Crusader, we only knew of one spawn point for it - on the curving stairs up to that floor on one side. There was a skellie that would spawn there and wander up and down the stairs. After getting frustrated and going on a mass slaughter to relieve the tedium, I found a Crusader in one of the other wanderer's paths elsewhere on that same floor. About that time, other people had started reported finding the Crusader in other wandering skellie spots.

Best bet is to get on that floor and kill anything that moves and ignore anything that doesn't ;)
