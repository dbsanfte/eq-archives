Kriel Darkbane
Has anyone noticed how nice our pets are, after all the changes lately? They're higher level now (at least, you don't have to summon repeatedly to get the high level version), and they had their attack ratings buffed also. They no longer draw aggro when they wander off, and they no longer have a chance to take major chunks of exp when you're solo'ing.

My shaman is L 47 now, and I've found the L 44 pet to be great. If I find a not-exceptionally tough low blue mob (eg a greater kobold in Sol B, my recent exp spot), all I have to do is slow it and my hasted pet can beat it to death. If I poke it with the spear enough to get one hit in I get full exp. This isn't how I actually go about it (thanks JBB!), but I could if I wanted to. I notice that sometimes if I have a string of interrupts on the JBB casts, my pet is tearing that kobold apart. If I end up with 3 kobolds, my pet basically solo's one (after slow), while I kill the other two.

I realize I'm at a fairly high point for the effectiveness of the shaman pet, and that at very high levels the then-old L 55 pet isn't terribly good. But for now, at least, that little pup is a god-send.

Finally, a question: I didn't expect the L 44 pet to hit as hard as it does - up to 43/hit. How hard do the L 49 and 55 pets hit? I have a feeling that it doesn't go up much at all from here.
