Though I haven’t tested it, the general consensus I’ve read on numerous boards is that you do not get sitting health regen on a mount. It makes sense to me that we would if we get sitting manna regen, but whoever said Sony was perfect?

And unfortunately, Ggrowler, your canni dancing days are not over unless you only hunt in outdoor zones. Once you are in an indoor zone, such as ALL the LDoN adventures, you won’t be able to summon your mount. So you’ll be back to dancing for your manna.

However, there’s good news on the horizon. Once you get Canni 5 and Torpor you’ll never have to canni dance again. You still can if you like, but you won’t really need to. I’ve been going on LDoN adventures for over 100 adventures now and I’ve not danced in any of them. Manna has not been a problem. I always get KEI to make life easier, but Canni 4, 5 and Torpor are what really make it possible. I still need manna between clicks of the magic Canni 5 button, but I’ve found that simply chain casting Canni 4 and using Torpor are more than enough to keep up with a fast paced adventure.

Because of the lack of a sit graphic, I never used to use Form of the Great Bear. But now I can happily take bear form since I never have to worry about whether or not I’m sitting. And fizzling on casting Canni 4 is no longer such a pain since I’m not stuck in my Canni macro and I can simply cast the spell again. When I’m outside I always summon Pokey, my little green lizard, but since gaining Canni 5 and Torpor adventuring indoors is no longer the annoying ordeal it used to be.
