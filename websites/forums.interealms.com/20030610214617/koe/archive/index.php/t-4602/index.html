The people that have been rendered unable to play by the upgrade I can certainly understand having a gripe. Ultimately I think it is something that will end up happening in long running MMORPGs but certainly Verant did not give much warning in this case.

As far as the frequently brought up argument of a new software release being brought out too soon. It is something that is done a lot in the computer industry, and overall I don't have a problem with it (within reason). Programs unlike products allow updates (fixes) to be made after the fact at very little cost. So the question often comes down to is the product "good enough" for release. Personally as far as Luclin goes the answer for me would be a huge yes. I would far rather have the extra content we got in Luclin now to mess with, even if there are messed up loot tables, bugged bard songs, broken quests rather than wait for months before I get anything. I realize that others may have a different opinion but as long as the company thinks they have a product completed well enough that people would rather have it now in its current state then wait, they will release it and clear up the remaining bugs as they arise.
