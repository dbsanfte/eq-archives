This is a bit of a trick question, since the answer is situational. There are times when WIS is most important (during a big fight when you need to do a mana dump), times when AC is most important (when you're getting a lot of attention and no cleric/torpor is handy), and times when HP are most important (primarily end-game stuff- when clerics/torpor, AC soft caps, Cann5, and high WIS on everything conspire to make HP the next target.)

By the way, I think you left a few things off the poll that are sometimes more important than any of the three:

Resists
Regeneration
Mana free damage

What I have noticed is that it depends on your style of play and job on a raid. If your the buffer at a raid then you need Wis +mana and HP. If your out there slowing and debuffing then you need the AC and HP over wis and mana.

At the same time if your soloing and tanking the mobs you need all of the AC HP you can get. If your in a group and can controll your aggro then your better off with Wis mana gear.

What I do is I carry 1 pack that just holds opptional gear. If I am getting beat on I put on the ac/hp gear. If I am not then I use the wis/mana hear. My hp's stay pretty stable reguardless of what I change out to. I have 1 hot button page set up for this for quick change. I have the pack on it and the slot that goes to each pieace of gear in the pack. All I have to do is turn to the page open the pack and change out what I need. This also works well for resist gear. Makeing it possable to change out to resists rings, neck, face for a caster. Then back to ac or wis for the next mele mob.
