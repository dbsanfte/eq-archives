What I've noticed is that everything can agro through walls in that zone, but you also can cast through walls in that zone so use that toward your advantage. ALSO, the whatever SCOUT's have a normal to large sized agro range, unlike the small to non-existent agro range of the other mobs in the zone. What I did was I inis up prior to entering the zone, then run up to the first row of houses. Stand AGAINST the building across from the houses and unlick invis, this will agro 1-2 lightblue/green mobs from inside that building (that are on almost ontop of the wall, aka 1-2 feet away from you through the wall). Kill them with root dot or whatever. Then invis up and go into any of the 6 buildings target, come out, get against the building wall, and cast a Slot or DoT to pull. Make sure when you pull that one of the wandering guards isn't gonna cross in front of your pull path. then make sure to move along the wall to line the mob up with the wall as well and k33l. Invis, rinse, repeat.

If you do it right, you'll never EVER get an add except for the 1-2 mobs that will agro through the wall from inside the larger brown building.

As far as agro near the boats, don't go there. Not necissary, the drunken pirates aren't near boats, they are in the houses just as you enter the zone, past the scouts and to the right.
