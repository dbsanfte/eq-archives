Yes, as a raid shaman, I value:

MGB - which you have
SCR/SCRM - if you're a small guild and you're the only shaman online, you're going to want your buffs to last. Also, if you're helping to heal, SCRM will extend Heals over Time.
RC 1-3 - can be a lifesaver
Quickbuff 2 - If you're the only shaman online, you're going to want fast buffs. It also makes your HOTs cast faster which can help
Ancestral Aid - If you're in a small raid force, every MGB-able HOT can save the day. Also, extremely useful in exp groups for "oh **** moments"
COTW - Shaman rez, I love anything that adds to our abilities.
Healing Adept/Healing Gift - I love the heal AAs, shaman heals are better than they used to be, but adding these AAs really makes them shine. Nothing like landing a 5K crit on a tank at the exact right moment

There are tons and tons of useful AA you're going to want to get, it's just a matter of priority.

veeshanMurtak
One thing you may want to consider before starting to buy AAs is to check your bottlenecks, i.e. focus on your limits (not weaknesses).

You say on raids you mainly heal. When you do this do people die because you can not heal fast enough? - Get Ancestral Aid (ha! I remembered), Healing Adept and/or Healing Gift. Do people die because you run out of mana? - Get Spiritual Channeling or Spellcasting Mastery. Or do you die yourself because you get aggro from healing? - Get Spellcasting Subtlety or one of the tanking AAs.

Likewise for buffs, is the limiting factor your mana, your cast time or your patience? Back when Canna 5 was new many members of this board used to tell freshly-raiding shamans to get canna 5 before MGB - because it helps you buff a raid better then MGB does. Similarly Spiritual Channeling and Spellcasting Mastery will probably help your buffing more then SCR will and at the same time they will help your healing more then the Healing Adept and Healing Gift AAs (albeit in terms of stamina, not speed).

Or you could take the advice I once got in a different game: "Just use all the broken crap". For us shamans these gamebreakers are cannibalize 5, spiritual channeling and those AAs that give us abilities we can not get from our spells (group heal, group cure, etc). Only after you got the gamebreakers focus on what you were going to.
