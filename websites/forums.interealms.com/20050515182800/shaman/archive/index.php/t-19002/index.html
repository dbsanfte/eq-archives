Finish SCR up to SCRM.

This will make you regain about as much HP after Quiescence (1800) as Canni5 takes off (1924).

It will also make all your buffs last longer, which means less rebuffing tedium.

I never did the first level of everything and I don't recommend it unless it's a new hotkey ability like RC1 or VP1, the passive ones are generally too small to notice, though many people recommend HG1 for the (very) occasional crit heal.
Tarthug
All suggestions assume that you get cann5 first.

I highly recomment SCR3 and then SCRM. That's 21 AAs but the payoff is the 6 tick torpor/Q and best of all, even your stat buffs will last an entire LDoN advent, maybe even two if you're really fast so you don't have to rebuff mid-advent. If you're stuck healing, this will make Q much more effective.

HG3/AHG3 is a good route if you're playing healer most of the time. I find my heals crit often and the difference a crit makes can save you a second cast. Again, if you're slowing and healing, you'll want to find all the mana savings you can.

If you're not playing healer as much, go for ND3. The extra HPs are very helpfull.

I'm working through CA3/LR5 (I currently have LR1), and I've already seen the difference this skill makes, especially outside of PoP. If you're slowing, healing, doting and rooting, you'll be building up all sorts of aggro and LR5 will help you survive it while spending minimal mana to save your own bacon.

I don't know how effective SCM3, HA3/AHA3 and CS3/ID5 are yet, but I've flagged these as secondary goals.
