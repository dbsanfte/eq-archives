Not sure about the list...

Our crew did Ragefire, Pyrewood (not an instance, but all elite mobs), Wailing Caverns, Shadowfang (seems about the same as WC), and then Razorfen Kraul was the next one we did. So the progression seems about right to me.

Razorfen is at the south edge of the Barrens, near the Great Lift to Thousand Needles. Was very very fun. Mobs range from upper 20's to mid-30's. Be sure to stop in Ratchet and pick up the Blue Tubers quest. One of the more unique quests I've seen in a MMORPG to date.

It does suck not knowing if you should be hitting an instance. Doing them when green or grey is just no fun :).
