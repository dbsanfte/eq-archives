Check your keyboard options under the UI section, there should be something like "Switch between targets" or something like that at the bottom of the section, and assign it to an unused key.

Basically, if you had an_orc_pawn00 targeted, then you went to target an_orc_pawn01. Now you want to go back to targeting an_orc_pawn00, hit that hotkey and it retargets that one.

It was introduced in a patch in May or June I believe.

oyah.. and to the question that was made about the hotkey in question..

Options menu(alt+o), then to keyboard. on the dropdown menu, switch it to all. Towards the bottom somewhere you'll see a programable option that says last 2 targets. Program that to a key of your choice, and play around with it. You can switch between 2 mobs that you are fighting at once. Also very helpful when you are charm fighting. you can attempt to keep the pet on the second target(takes practice) and when you see charm break switch target and slow so the chanter doesn't get his arse whooped
