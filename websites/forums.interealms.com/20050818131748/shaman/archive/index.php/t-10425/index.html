You have to follow a certain progression:

General --> arch --> class

At level 51, you can start putting points into General.

At level 55, you can start putting points into arch, but you must put at least 6 points into general as a prerequisite.

At level 59, you can put points into class, but you must meet the requirements of 12 points into arch first.

Shaman generally go:

6 General (run/regen) (Baron/Baroness title)
12 Arch (Mental Clarity) (Venerable title)
because it's the fastest way to Can 5.

Note: 24 points in class is required to achieve the Elder title.

Also...each ability in general costs 1 point. In arch, skills increase in cost, so for example:

Mental clarity 1 - 2 points
Mental clarity 2 - 4 points
Mental clarity 3 - 6 points

While AAs can be very valuable, most people think it's a good idea to hit 60 before attempting to seriously gain AA. The level 60 spell circle is a huge power boost for shaman, the difference between a 59 shaman and a 60 shaman with torpor/malo is noticeable.
