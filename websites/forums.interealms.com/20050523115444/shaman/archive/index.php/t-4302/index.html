Gathu
Well, depends where you exp. In Cazic Thule maze (which is where I do my duo'ing), I often take an absolute crapload of damage, easily as much as lodizal puts out. 2 spiders at once is just pain, especially if one is a large spider (level 58 or so, hits for like 250s, rather than the normal 200ish).

General practice = monk tanks with stone stance while malo/slow go in (though those mobs mentioned (excepting chottal) are outdoors, so you can kite for slow), then shaman tanks rest of fight. As long as you're not in danger of dying (IE: Mob outdamaging torpor consistantly) it's better to have the additional dps.

The danger of dying thing is completely conditional though, varying based on mob and personal gear...so posts here probably wouldn't help any individual poster.

Haste number is using 100% as a hasteless baseline, to show how torpor doesn't just slow you down, but it takes away whatever haste you have. 181% is 100% + 40% monk epic (I'm too lazy to celerity) + 41% haste item, though that varies per monk...but then, some other shamans probably actually cast celerity for an extra 10%, so it balances out.
