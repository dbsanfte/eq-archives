Early levels are easy - buff up shaman with conviction/virtue etc.

Get best DS potion you can make/buy - any extra clickies like 10th ring or Kunark BP useful also.

Go to an undead area (hint Kurns tower up to about 20) and get shaman to run around and aggro every undead on first floor. Stand and heal with torpor - use mage PBAE to tag every mob aggro'd on shaman. DS damage is not counted so your mage will be doing the only damage to all mobs and collect all xp.

Make sure you pick zones with a high ZEM - at later levels you will want to buff mage pet, heal it and send in doggy at around 50%. Worked well with a melee, my SK (sadly account cancelled now) could outdamage lvl 61 doggie around mid to high 30's. :(
