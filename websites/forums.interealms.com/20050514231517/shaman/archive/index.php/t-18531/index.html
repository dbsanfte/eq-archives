Tediousness. Things in EQ have gotten better from the "old days, when I was camping for the bear-pelt in the Lich caves. Slowly becoming a FPS. This may be really subjective, but many things about the current game play bug me, because they are too easy. I guess my choice of quitting the game for a long time, (after Kunark) then coming back to it shades my perspective, but man things are too easy. This game is beginning to feel like Dungeon Siege in some ways (great graphics, game play that does not require skillful play).

Things that EQ is great at:

Improvements. This is EQ's strength. Comparing game play of EQ back when it first released to today, EQ has obviously been constantly changing and improving. My friends were always trying to get me to play EQ again, talking up how cool the LDON were, or how neat the new planes were. I have to say I can see where EQ took the good ideas of other games and added them to EQ, and occasionally (LDON) came up with some really neat ideas on their own. None of the competition that I've tried is at the level of refinement that EQ is.

Large player base=your friends are on EQ. This is the reason I usually end up playing again after quitting EQ. Many of my friends are playing EQ, so if I want to socialize with them, here I am.
