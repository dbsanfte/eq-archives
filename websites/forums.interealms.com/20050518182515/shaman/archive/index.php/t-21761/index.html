Urdegish
I dont believe you can make right-click hotkeys auto-cast. You can have combat stuff happen by using the numerical system of '/doability #' where # is 1-12, 1-6 been your social hotkey bank and 7-12 your combat hotkey bank (I think that is correct). But because you cannot put a JBB hotkey into any of those banks, you can't type any command to make it cast.
Urdegish
For canny hotkey, enter this if you are sitting:

/sit off
/cast # (Spellgem for canna)
/pause 14
/sit

For Chain canna cast, on horse perhaps:

/cast #
/pause 14
/cast #
/pause 14
/cast #

I believe the pause 14 is the correct length, it gives a little amount of time for any small lag issues. Neither of those combinations covers fizzles.
