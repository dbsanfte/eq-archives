Kalrog
Well, it depends how much plat you have to twink with. The best things you can get for your shaman are a Fungi Tunic and a JBB. Then go AC/HP gear and then Wis/Mana gear. JBB won't help too much until 45, but you did say twink. Fungi regen is always nice, and especially so after you get Canibalize. The most important thing is to get XP as fast as possible to get him up to the level of your monk, right? So go someplace that you can solo and group. I usually get XP faster in a group, if it is pulling well. If not, you can always just drop the group and go solo and not waste any time if you are killing in a place that has both options for you. Good luck! And welcome to being a shaman :)
