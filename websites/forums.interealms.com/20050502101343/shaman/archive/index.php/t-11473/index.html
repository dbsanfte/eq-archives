Kianor
eq_newb: Of the 5 spell casting types, Abjuration, Alteration, Conjuration, Divination, Evocation, you can train in specialize in all of them (for int casters at level 20, priests at level 30), but only one of these can go beyond 50. The first skill that hits 51 in specialize becomes your specialization and can go up to 200, from then on all the others are capped at 50.

Note, this is for Specialize Alteration, Specialize Abjuration, etc - not for the basic Alteration, Abjuration, all of which can can max out at 235.

As for making sure you hit 51 in the right specialty, most shamans will max out Specialize Alteration way before any other. I suggest keeping an eye on it, but do your normal stuff - but you shouldn't need to do anything special to make sure that maxes out first, especially if you cannibalize a fair bit. Hell, I hit 200 in Specialize Alteration before Specialize Evocation even got to 50. :)

Of course, if you want to chose a different specialty, or if you're doing say, nothing but dotting, you may find it helps to do the level 1 spell again and again thing. :)
