Kinladar
Best puller for this is a Ranger Hands down.

Sow in the nexus, then zone into Echo. Levitate, then invis yourself. The general can see invis but has a fairly small aggro range.

There is a sweet spot on the stairs that lead up to him that you snare the first assassin, then boogie outside, and make sure the second assasin and the general are following. Get enough room to snare assasin number 2. Then again make sure the general is close enough that you won;t loose aggro,and pull him past the raid force. Keep running for the Fungus grove zone. WHen you pass your raid force, have someone tag the general, and pull him into the tunnel leading to SH.

This is where your main force should be. They can then dispose of the general, while you zone and loose the aggro of the casting masters. That's the reason you have to zone out. The evil casting guild masters will eat you otherwise.

it;s a risky, adventurous pull, and not for the light of heart, but what a rush.
