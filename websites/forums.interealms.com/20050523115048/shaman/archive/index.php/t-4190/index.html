What what I've read (and this could be misinformation) the Mac version will have it's own server(s) so that the code can be maintained separately. If that will be the case, you will at least get to experience a fresh economy with no twinking, and will probably be surrounded by almost all true newbies.

Grobb is the starting city for Trolls; there is no choice. The starting hunting zone is Innothule Swamp, which is a good newbie zone. Plenty of Frogloks to kill, who drop Rusty weapons and Netted Armor. Verant recently accelerated the leveling rate between Levels 1-10, which I can see certainly has some benefits, but the downside is that you'll never have enough money to buy your spells. Do the quest given out by the Shaman guild leader (the one you give your note to) and you're pretty much set with all the decent Level 1 spells.

Once you get Spirit of Wolf at Level 9, you can visit Oggok (Ogre city) and Neriak (Dark Elf city). As a Troll, you won't be welcome in a lot of the pinky (Human, Wood Elf, Dwarf, etc.) cities, so be careful when traveling around.

And as for guilds, since I believe it will be a new server, any guilds will be new ones created by the players there. Just make a note of everyone you've enjoyed grouping with, and try to keep in touch with them.
