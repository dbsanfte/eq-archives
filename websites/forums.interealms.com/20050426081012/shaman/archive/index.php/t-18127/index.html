Ruatonim
Use the ranger to harmony pull (if you weren't); this alone will get you single pulls. Beyond that, I would suggest NOT camping the boats at your level. There are many, many MOBs throughout the zone that you can kill without messing with the shoddy pathing, and potential adds, of the boats.

I've spent a lot of time in dulak, and the boats are the worst possible place to get experience in the whole zone. However, thats just my opinion of the zone.

Jorlaan
On stormwave if you go up the ramp on the boat and not to far onto the boat, that's almost always a safe pull spot.

If you get a runner though, it'll go right down the ramp and aggro the stuff on the docks.

On the dandolak the safe spot is approximately the same place but there might be a spawn on the crates to your left as you come up the ramp. You need to time that one so he doesn't pop when you are fighting and surprise ya.

There's also a safe spot on the lady di boat. I dont' recall where it is offhand though.

On oceancrasher I haven't found a safespot yet. We took a group to that boat with a babysitter to get someone to planar level and my enchanter was kept extremely busy mezzing adds :-) Of course, she was only about level 50 at the time, so level definitely affected aggro radius.

What I did on my mage was simply keep moving. I would pull the one nearest the boarding ramp and kill him. than go up to the back of the boat.

Method was pull mob x and kill. Move to mob x's spawn point, pull mob y. Move to mob y's spawn point and just start a cycle. On the first boat I could keep the entire boat cleared at lvl 51 on my mage. Not sure how well a Shaman could do the same but, I suspect they would easily reproduce the results. bigbadbowlindude
I was not too much of a fan of the boats, they were almost always camped and the drop rates have been hit by the nerf bat pretty hard. Here were some of my favorite spots.
My first favorite spot was in the armory, I am unsure wether or not that is the "official" name, but that's what I called it is at about -500,500 I start at the very end then pull in a circle from left to right or right to left. There is only one, maybe two gaurds that follow a path back and forth, most of them are far enough apart they can be singled easily togorian mines zone in is close by too.. good safe place to med without worrying too much about trains.
My 2nd favorite place was at about -1500,0 behind the little hut. Two static spawns in front of the hut, a few mobs inside of the hut I believe I would get adds on ocation when I would open the door then I would pull mobs from various other locations. As long as you would snare/root in time no worries really of getting adds. I would get the rust encrusted wrist item from the ocean crasher mobs by the doc there.
To break the monotany I would camp at about -500,-500 right in front of the hungry shark there is a spot outside to the side by the porch thing I would pull mobs there and as long as they stayed there the patrolling gaurds never cared.
At all three locations there is a small chance of getting a named( I emphasize small), if by some chance you get the named good chance it's not going to be worth much of anything... random gem drops are decent though even with the nerf bat.
If you like them good.. if not sorry. I never saw what the big deal about the boats were.. but oh well I guess.
