BlargX
It's Trooper Mozo. He has four slots; put a bones in each of the four, or put up to four in each of the four slots, and you get faction raises for every set of four. Monks trying to do whistling fists quest like to buy bones, but plenty of other people do, so they can all get non-KOS in Cabilis.

4 or 5 plat per stack of 20 is pretty standard. Get plenty of backpacks full, and even drop the bonestacks somewhere very out of the way and safe and pick them up on an alt in cabilis to store up extras, if you get full up. People are thrilled to be able to buy 5 or 10 or 20 stacks at a time; their alternative is to spend sometimes a solid week in Kurns killing green skeletons to get bone chips.

Take a tour of the vendors inside Cabilis and outside, too. They often have bone chips on them, too.

Heck, I even give Trooper Mozo some bones sometimes with my iksar monk. Iksars don't even have too good faction in their own city!
