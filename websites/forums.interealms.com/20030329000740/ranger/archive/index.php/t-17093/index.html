The best place to hunt at 24 is Luclin.
It will be very easy for you at Paludal Cavern, you can solo there till you reach 26 then most will be light blue.
from 26 to 30 move to Echo Cavern (secret entrance) and solo needlites. They are great for ranger because your damage shield will almost kill them alone :D If they are to easy you can pull some boglings at the same time.
after 30 move to dawshroud peaks, there are many things to kill there. I particulary like shrooms in the ruined castle. But there are also zelniaks you can fear kite.

Good luck fella
