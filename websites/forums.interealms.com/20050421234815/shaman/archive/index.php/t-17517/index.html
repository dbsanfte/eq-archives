I second the post by Eisgeist. Go to Nadox. Take the zone in that is half way down the tunnel between Gunthark and Dulak. Invis past the elementals and run down the lava river. Head up to the right wall when it opens up and you will be standing up alongside the bridge. Go past the bridge entrance until you reach the rocks. This is a safe spot to pull to. You should be getting 1 percent a kill here and they are not too hard. As you become more comfortable with the zone you should be able to venture down the tunnels and pick up the pieces for your blood of nadox spell which is pretty useful.

Don't forget that you can whack the elementals before you run down the lava river, they are good for xp and you can sometimes get some boots to drop. There are 3 that spawn near the entrance and one below the river that you can use F8 to target when close and then slow it and swim back to the zoneline to fight it.

EDIT: If you are a barbarian and choose to use this method to get into the open area save yourself some annoyance by casting bear form before you head down the river. It is an awful climb up the wall in barb form but a direct run up in bear form.

I'm sure many will find fault in my post, because the zones I choose may not be the best exp or most popular zones. I can only say that the exp bar is steadily creeping up and I'm not dying that much.

Here are a couple of places I've been visiting lately. Some folks would say that hunting light blues is a waste of time. I say, sitting on your backside waiting for an offer to group is even more of a waste.

Velks labyrinth - There are some tough mobs in this zone, and yeah its an exp grind, but you also get to collect velium (nice cash from bazaar sales) and crystalline silks for tailoring, (you are going to do the shawl quest right?). Your surely not going to be soloing the upper level, but there are places to get some solo exp at.

Dulak's harbor - yeah it can get crowded here and it's a pain in the butt to get too as well, but in recent weeks I have been the only soul in this zone. In any case, who cares if the ships are all camped, you can still find a spot to set up shop and pull DB and LB mobs damn near anywhere in this zone safely. The PP's were coming in fairly regular and you can gather the medalions and finally fill the charm slot.

Dawnshroud peaks - At 57 the rockhopper cave still had DB and LB mobs to pull, and you can stay very busy soloing there. As I recall DSP is still an exp bonus zone, and flawless hides drop fairly regular for those of you intent on doing some higher level tailoring. HQ rockhopper hides were also falling frequently and you can knock out some of your lower level tailoring with these and get exp instead of hunting green conning lions in E Karana.

City of Mist - I had never been here until I started doing part 6 of the epic, and I entered the zone with more than a little trepidation. Sure this zone has greens, but there are also LB and DB and if your still working on your epic then you can get the 6 reports collected. Some decent drops come of the DB mobs in the stable area as well as a fair amount of PP too. I'm embarrased to admit it, but at 57 I've had to high tail it out of that zone on more than 1 occasion.

Fungus grove - the first mob I conned in this zone was a DB lotus and it killed me. Yep, call me a wuss, that sucker can summon/nuke and it did so until I died. Want some excitement while soloing, go try that place out.
