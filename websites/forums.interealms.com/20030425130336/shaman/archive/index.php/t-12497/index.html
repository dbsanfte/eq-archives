No, focus items don't stack. In this case, the focus item which allows the most mana savings will take precedence.

AA abilities can stack with focus items, however. An example of this would be the quickbuff AA stacking with the enhancement haste focus of cog encrusted epaulets. Quickbuff AA and enhancement haste will also stack with the cleric spell haste line.
