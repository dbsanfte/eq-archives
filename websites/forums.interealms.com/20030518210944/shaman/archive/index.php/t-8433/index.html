Harvyst
With replenishment and regen 3, I don't miss the sit regen rate that much.

Soloing, I pull, then jump on the horse.

Raiding, I'm on the horse from the moment we set up camp, I click off when we move. In Hate, it's nice to not be the the one on the roamer's hit list... ditto for Fear.

Grouping, if I am not pulling, I'm on the horse... the mana regen is incredible, and especially when doing constant pulls, I can keep my mana up without much work, often throwing in nukes and BoN to keep my mana occupied.
