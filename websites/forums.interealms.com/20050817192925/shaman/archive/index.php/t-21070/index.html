Okay, just a quickie--do the following and then come back and post results. Let's make sure you have all your pre-flags.

Go to the grand librarian. "what information?" "what lore?"

Go to the guy (adler?) outside the PoD ****pipe and ask, "what ward?"

Go to thelin's healer in PoTranq and follow conversation all the way to end. Not sure, but think it's "what jeweled dagger?" to get you within 2-3 prompts of the pre-flag.

Go to Fahlia. "I will go to torment" Hail Tylin, too. If he thanks you, you've got that whole line done (bertoxx, keeper of sorrows, saryrn, grummus, and I think, TT).

Hail Adler's brother, too--just to make sure.

If fact, you may want to do all of the above 3-4 times, with a trip to the seer to unlock memories each time. Yes, it's a pain in the butt, but sometimes all you are missing is one pre-flag and they can be a real pain to straighten out.

Once you do that 3-4 times, then come post what's missing. Once we eliminate the pre-flag questions, we can see what you're missing!
