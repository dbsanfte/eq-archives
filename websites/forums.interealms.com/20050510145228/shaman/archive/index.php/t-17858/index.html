Zaviax McCleo
I have a lot of friends between 58-63 so I do lower level average groups a lot. It becomes very necessary to do the math when your friends are spread out in level so much. Under 63 average we can be pretty easy going on who we have in our group. 63+ we need to be more picky on who we have.

That being said I always do the math before setting the group just so we know what to expect. So I can confirm 100% it's based off group level average.
BluntDoctor
Mob level is based on the average of your group. I believe points at the end of the adventure is a combination of both average group member level AND your level. The average level determines the total points awarded. Your level, in comparison to the rest of the group, determines your share of that total.
Ruatonim
The dungeon difficulty is assigned when you get the mission and accept it, and is based on the average level of your group.

Point award value is either based on average level at the END of the mission (I got level 65 in a dungeon and got the 51 points instead of 49 you get for being 64), or it assigns points based on the level YOU are and the difficulty level of the dungeon.
