weissblau
It's not just Legos of the Hand that drop the shard, that is a widely spread myth. Any named Lego upstairs or at the entrance can drop it, and in fact Legos of the Hand have a worse drop rate upstairs than other named.

Drop rate at the entrance is pretty bad, though. You're better off upstairs for sure. We had the best luck turning the corner from the teleporter and going on the second hallways, but that might just be coincidence. We spent a long time farming by the porter on three different occasions, then decided to just move along the hallway, and got a shard quickly all three times after turning the corner.
