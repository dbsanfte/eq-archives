Icklymuk
It actually needs to have the pause first, then what ever your doing...
/pause X, /cast 3
Judlace
Yes, for some reason, the /pause command needs to be first (with a comma after it and NO space before the /cast or /doability command), but it goes off AFTER the /cast command on the same line.

So just enter the time to pause after the cast before the cast and it will work fine. Five casts on one hotkey. :)
