Then buy Morrowind, if you EVER played Daggerfall and wished for less crashes and a little less randomness Morrowind is it. It's a smaller world but it's not Daggerfall light, it's got the stuff to keep you playing one more hour and one more...

BE WARNED: EQ players and even partly hardcore gamers will find it easy to level, you can "exploit" tons of stuff, it's up to you to keep the game honest. Anyway, it's very immersive and the easy combat doesn't get in the way of actually LIVING in Morrowind.
