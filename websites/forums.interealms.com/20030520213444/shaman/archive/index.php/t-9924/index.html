After I saw the reply that the faction hits for Nathyn's isn't too bad, I went and killed these buggers. I'm level 55 (with fungi, horse, and jbb, but no epic). I also had my trusty side-kick (2nd box 42 druid with horse, manapres2, healing2 foci) with me. No outside buffs though (no kei, no aego, etc).

If I was careful, I could sometimes single pull the outside 4, but I usually didn't bother and got 2 at a time. They aren't too bad. I pull with slow and if I noticed an add coming I quickly switched targets and rooted the other one. Then slowed the add also. Then killed them one at a time, aggressively rerooting the add.

I could pretty much NEVER single pull the ones inside the first door. Generally, I got three. Once I had the spawn broken, I was able to single or double the inside ones. Again, root and slow are your friends. They really are not very difficult mobs. Whatever you do, DO NOT try pulling the inside mobs with root. I tried this and ended up with 5.

Once I got the hang of it, I was able to keep the outside 4, the 2 in the first room plus the one that always adds from there (no clue where she comes from). And the 2 from the next room in that guard the staircase. After that, I'd have to work on respawns.

I'd guess at 52, solo, you could probably take the front 4 as long as you can manage to pull no more than 2 at a time. If you're very good and have very good gear, you might be able to work in the next 3. Maybe not though. Personally, at 52, I'd kill the giants at the burning woods giant fort. Good XP and much better loot.
