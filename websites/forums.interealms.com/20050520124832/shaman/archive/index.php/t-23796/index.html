Gloriana
A focus effect will stack with an AA, but not with another focus effect.

Therefore, the 15% focus of mediocrity would stack with SCRM but would not stack with the LDON beneficial extender. Since the LDON focus is only 10%, the VT focus would fire - since the best focus takes precedence.

I've never had the Blessed Prayer shawl (which doesn't work on Q) but I imagine it would look like this:

You cast Q and your VT crown flickers.
You cast focus and your coldain shawl flickers.

The best focus effect always takes precedence!
