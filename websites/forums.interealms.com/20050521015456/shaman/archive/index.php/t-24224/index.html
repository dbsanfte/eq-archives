Grammaw
Conventional wisdom holds that you should go run3 regen 3, then mc3 then Can5.

However, if you have OoW, I would strongly look at mnemonic retention. Level 55 is needed, but then it is just 3 aas for an extra spell slot. An extra spell slot for the rest of your life is pretty uber.

Run3 is sweet. Sure, we can buff our run speed with SoW/Billy outdoors, and Shrew (if you have LDoN) inside, but run3 is just plain handy.

Regen3 I would say is questionable anymore. We can buff our regen to pretty significant levels. And with the recent regen changes, 3 extra points is IMHO, not all that needed for us.

Instead, you might want to look at your playstyle. If you use a mount and stat food, Innate metabolism can be very worthwhile. Lasting breath isn't great in and of itself, but it is a prereq for eternal breath, which can be durn handy down the road. If you are heavy into tradeskills, Crafting mastery might be worthwhile. OOW seems to have shaken up the tried and true aa paths.

My suggestion would be:
run3
Mnemonic retention
regen3 or metabolism3
then follow the canni5 path of MC3>>>Can5
After that, there are so many viable routes to take that you will have to see what best suits you.
