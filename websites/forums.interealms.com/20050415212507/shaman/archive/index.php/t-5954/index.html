Just a comment here from my experiance. Unless your company is requiring the plus exam, don't waste your time with it. If you really want to step into IT, go for the big certifications.

I can't help you with most of those but look here for info about VMS: http://www.openvms.compaq.com/wizard/faq/vmsfaq_001.html#faq_general

Basically its an OS that was designed by DEC (bought by Compaq, bought by HP) that was used mostly on their VAX mainframes. VMS stands for Virtual Memory System. Interestingly, Windows NT has its roots in VMS, I believe they were designed by the same person, and the acronyms VMS and WNT are related.
