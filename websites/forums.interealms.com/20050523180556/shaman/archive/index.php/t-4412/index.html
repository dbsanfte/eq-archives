Twoflower,

Here is what I would suggest.

1.) PoTC earing (network with your guild crafters, farm stuff crafters need) My earing cost me like 3k total.

2.) Swapp your Barbed Rubi Bracer (sell it) for a Coldain Military Wristguard (yes you loose a little ac, but you get extended affliction 3 and trust me that is huge) (I'll gladly help you with that quest, got my faction mule parked in the right spot).

3.) Buy or camp a IGS (Imbued Granite Spaulders) You will LOVE the resists and the Ac.

4.) Get a better range item. Heck, if you have wis to spare you can't go wrong with an Idol of the Thorned (camp yourself and have your alts get xp, or buy for like 3k).

5.) Shadel Bandit Ring is your friend.

6.) Crystal Chitin Guantlets are sweet.

7.) Try to get the Deathmask of the Elysians, chrystal spider eye is ok too.

That should boost your ac quite nicely. To really break 1k ac you will need some Thurg/ SS / Kael / PoG armor.
