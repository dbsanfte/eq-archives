Porgy Beastmaster
I don't know if anyone else has come up with this idea yet or not.

I thought of an interesting way for us to get some better DPS, without crimping on anyone elses style, and without letting us use our "superior mana regen" to out DPS and out last everyone

An explosion DoT

Basically, it would work kind-of like a buff, that added no hps, but when it wore off it would deal X damage. (The way your max hp goes down when Virtue wears off, except this time it would subtract from current not max)

An example might be:

Pus infestation:
resist: disease -40
cast time: 6sec, recast 2.5 sec
450mana
duration: 3 ticks
When wears off Pus Infestation deals 1600 damage

I dunno how best to balance it, but you get the idea

this way we couldn't really nuke something to death, because restacking the "DoT" would actually be WORSE since it would lengthen the damage time. however, the "buff" or "DoT" wouldn't generate ago until the damage was dealt, instead of every tick, and the damage would occur in 3 ticks instead of 5 - 11. *and* we could do other stuff in the mean time, like heal or canni ect. We could still drop this on multiples, however, as I said, you couldn't use this is burn out a monster.

Let me know what you guys think?
