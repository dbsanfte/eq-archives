Don't delete your beastlord. I play a beastlord as my second character, and although he is not quite high enough for me to testify on their 60+ tanking ability, I can say that beastlords appear to be much like shamans in their DUO ability. They go good with just about anyone (except pure melees). I've talked to some beastlords in my guild and they say that some of their best duos have been with other pet classes. I'm not sure what tactics they use, but beastlords and necros are both extremely versatile classes, so I am sure you can find something that works :)

edit: I had an idea. If you are duoed with a necro, then you will have both snare and sow at your disposal. This makes aggro-kiting an easy possibility (i.e. you piss off the mob and run it in circles while your pets chew it). Pull the mob with your big poison dot, hit it with drowsy, and run it in circles while the necro applies dots and whatever. If you lose aggro, hit it with your quick ice nuke, a poison dot, or drowsy. You will always have enough mana for drowsy, so its a great aggro-getter.
