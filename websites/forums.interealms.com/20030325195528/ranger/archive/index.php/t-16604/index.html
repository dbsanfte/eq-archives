Filson
Just did this last week. We did the drop-in method, vs. the fight-in method.

Drop-in.
1) Make an initial group that includes three critical people:
- A mage with CoH
- Someone with some sort of invulnerability and ressurection (a cleric comes to mind)
- A group leader who is not one of the two above.

1b) Everyone else going down goes with the group leader and enters the Hole through the front door and sits tight.

2) Drop in advanced party. If you don't have the firepower to clear the landing area, you can scout using a Brell-worshipping halfling rogue. When all's clear, jump in from above. You'll zone in during the fall. The mage will go splat, the cleric will go bump, and you may have a fight on your hands. DO NOT JUMP IN THE GROUP LEADER!!!

3) Rez the mage.

4) Disband everyone but the mage and the group leader and invite the first four going in. Cast CoH, repeat.

Alternate method. Group up and bash your way in. We had 52 (yes 52) 47-65 level guildies and probably could have just maimed our way to the bottom, but we spent the time more wisely. The result was a 4-hour raid that netted 12 epic pieces (6 ranger, 6 from the castle)

Have fun, and bring someone who can punt or port you out. There are teleport pads, but that's another battle to get to.
