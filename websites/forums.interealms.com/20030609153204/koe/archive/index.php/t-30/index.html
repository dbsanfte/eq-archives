Cyrssi
Hungry earth,
Swallows my pride.
Empty mirth
follows, I lied.
Spoken in vain,
My words of love cried.
Broken in pain,
Silence replied.
Shackles of hate,
Locked to the stone.
Tortured by fate,
I can not atone.
Darkness ensues,
Clouding my heart.
What did I lose?
How do I restart?
When does it end?
How to Begin?
My heart I defend,
But it knows my sin.
"You used me to gain",
It scowls with rage.
"Now I bear this stain,
And sit in this cage."
Why is it me,
That owns you this blame?
I question of she,
This self-wounding claim.
Have ye not thought,
Of the lies toldeth me?
"Those lies that you bought,
were easy to see."
Blinded by you!
I point and acuse.
"That may be true,
but you, not I, lose."
With that you are wrong,
My lost empty soul.
By my side all along,
You've been in this hole.
So you see not I,
But you own this life.
As hard as I try,
I can't end your strife.
Be known it was true,
What you spoke every day.
And lies told to you,
That drove us this way.

Fallen

Fallen from grace,
Fallen to waste,
Washed up, used up,
Dead to the world.

Blazing with passion it burned,
Ingniting everything around it,
None questioned its strength.

Raging with love it yearned,
Illuminating the darkest reaches of self,
None doubted its commitment.

Aching with loss it turned,
Folding upon itself,
None considered the depths of its truth.

Fallen from grace,
Fallen to waste,
Fate's strings sear my soul,
Her shears rend my heart.

Cleaved in twain,
Trust stains the ground,
Bleach white in purity.

Wracked with pain,
Fear entombs my thoughts,
Endless hallways of nightmare.

Drained of sane,
"Love", "Promise", "Forever",
Meaningless words of deception.

----------

Untapped love burns my veins,
Ravaged heart darkens soul.
Unknown fate holds the reigns,
Driving onward frightened foal.

Charging forward with reckless speed,
Wind whipped sand takes my sight.
Rock torn hooves shake and bleed,
Sapped by pain, the will to fight.

Masters crop cracks my skin,
Forcing on with no concern.
Failing muscles just give in,
Wasted lungs heave and burn.

Pain subsides one moment brief,
Then is gone to my relief.
Broken beast a lifeless heap,
Tortured heart can finally sleep.
