Don't worry about messing up. When it comes to the point to choose cleric, druid, or shaman the guy will present you with three choices at once which clearly state I wish to do so and so I want to be a cleric, I want to be this so I want to be a druid, and I want to be really cool so I choose Shaman. Just pick the right button and you will be fine. As for myself I had a hard time choosing cleric or shaman and sat there for almost 5 hours with the three choices while reading everything I could find on both such as spell lists, skills, etc. In the end after playing over 65 levels in EQ1 as a Shaman I decided to stick with a good thing even though the role and spell set seems to have changed so much.
