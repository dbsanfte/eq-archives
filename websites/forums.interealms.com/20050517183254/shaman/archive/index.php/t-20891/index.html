Well I start Junaper in August of 2000. I must say that she was the best character I've ever created in any game.

As of late, there seems to be a mass exit from the game, and I can't say I blame the people for leaving. I left the game for the Luclin & PoP expansions to venture out and find a new better game. I wasn't very successful and came back to EQ to hopefully rekindle the friendships/social environment that I had when I first started playing EQ.

I canceled my payments 2 months ago, and haven't played or thought about playing since. I've tried multiple ways to change my play style to get more excitement out of the game (Trade Skills, Quests) pretty much anything other than XP grinding. I've explored the old level that I was excited to see and explore when I was younger, and it brings back a lot of memories, but get very discouraged that I can't see the higher end game without turning EQ into my full time job. I have put almost 3 years into playing Junaper, and feel that I should be able to see the elementals planes/Time without have to dedicate 40 hours a week to some guild to raid. A game should NEVER require that much time to see what could be considered the end game.

I'll admit on my way out that Shaman are really broken compared to the character that I started in 2000 (Kunark Era). But that is the way the game has evolved, and it is no longer the game that I enjoy. I remember when I start playing the game lore that was involved in the game was great, the game no longer has the sence of RPG, but more of a stamped out set of level/zones. I personally don't think that their will ever be a game (for me at least) like the Original EQ.

I've always loved my Shaman and will greatly miss all the friends that I have met in EQ. It looks like it is time for me to find a new hobby or game. Have fun all, and thanks for the memories.
