Hey guys.
I was poking around Akheva Ruins yesterday, and saw The Va'Dyn up.
I got a cleric friend to come over to help me kill him so I could get the Toeslicer that he was wielding for my bard.
As a side effect of this, I managed to get a reasonable parse of how well persistant casting AA from GoD works.

Before the data parse:
-I spent the 18AA to get persistant casting
-I have 7% stun resist on my Breastplate from the plane of time as my only stun resist gear.
-The Va'Dyn will stun you through normal melee, but also procs Storm Tremor (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2927&source=Live) for even more stuns than a flurrying mob would do.

I went through and counted some totals:
I was successfully stunned 87 times.
I successfully shrugged off 14 stuns.
Of those 14 successful no-stuns, 4 of them were through casting a spell.
Of those 87 stuns, 22 of them were stuns that interupted a spell.


Analysis:
14 stun resists + 87 stuns = 101 stun attempts total.
14 successful stun shrugs out of 101 seems a bit high for 7% stun resist.
If you consider that 4 of those were persistant casting stuns, it might be that the remaining 10 stun resists were solely as a result of my 7% resist from the breastplate.
The remaining 10 resists out of 101 stuns from the BP would probably be well within standard deviation, showing stun resist to apparently be working properly.

22 spell interupt stuns + 4 stun resists = 26 persistant cast potentials
4/26 = 15.3% chance to shrug off a stun when casting a spell.
Note: the sample size is too small to say for sure that 15% is the hard amount how often it works, but this is the only data sample I've ever seen to work from.

Something to consider:
I wonder if +7% stun resist affects persistant casting percentages?
Like, if I didn't have my BP, would I only have channelled 8% instead?
I don't expect to kill Va'Dyn again like I did, so I can take off my breastplate to test.



Conclussion:
18AAs is not worth a ~15% chance to channel a spell through a stun, IMO.
DO NOT BUY THIS AA IF YOU HAVE ANYTHING ELSE MARGINALLY USEFUL TO BUY INSTEAD.
