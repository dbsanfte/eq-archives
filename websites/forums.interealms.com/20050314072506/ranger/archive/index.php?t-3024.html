GMs are not able to see what is on a mob or corpse until actually inspecting the corpse. And the logs only contain information about item transfers (loot, trade, buy, sell, destroy), so they will not have anything about loot items unless a character actually loots them. So, the GM can see if someone is taking the items, but if the item is poofing directly from the corpse, then the logs will not have it, and the GMs will not know what items dropped.
