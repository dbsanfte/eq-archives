Harvyst
I don't think he is saying the crown stacks with extended enhancement III (there is no extended duration III).

Zephyr of Brell, the 8th shawl effect, is similar to Extended Enhancement III (such as the Amulet of Insight), except I believe Zephyr of Brell is 20% extention, and EE III is 15%.

However, Zephyr of Brell and EE III do not stack - Zephyr wins, since it is a IV effect and works on level 65 spells. (Such as Blessing of Replenishment).

Neither Zephyr of Brell or EE III work on Torpor as torpor is too short - the 48 second minimum limit.

However, this crown of ambivalence does work on things as short as 24 seconds, and according to the earlier poster, on both good and bad effects. So it would override a EE III item or a Extended Affliction III item.

However, since Zyphr of Brell is a IV item, and I do not know if this focus effect is a IV focus effect, I do not know who would win - the 8th shawl or this crown - in being used. Generally the game goes for the higher effect (IV over III).

Both would not be used. But I don't know who would win.

By the way, you can get 70% extention to buffs.... SCRM (50%) and 8th shawl (20%) .... I am 4 aa points from getting SCRM, but I've heard from other 8th shawlers on the Eqtraders board that can and do get 70% extention to buffs.
