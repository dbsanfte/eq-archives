To get to the stables:

Go into the zone, and over the bridge to the gate. Open the gate, go through and left. You'll arrive in a large(ish) courtyard. There will be a passage on your left, this leads back towards the entrance (but isn't a way out, it leads to the balconies that you can see from the bridge). There will be another passage aheaad of you (leading south). This leads to the stables.

Alternatively look at http://www.eqatlas.com/kunark/cityofmistmap.html
Nytmare
The Student's Log (report #6) only drops from Spectral Courier in the stables area -- he's not always up, so you have to keep clearing the stables, and he doesn't always drop it.

The other 5 reports drop randomly from every mob in the zone.

If you don't have any reports yet, concentrate on getting Student's Log first, and you'll end up acquiring some or all of the other reports while you're at it.
