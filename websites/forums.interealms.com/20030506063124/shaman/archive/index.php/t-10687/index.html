Aridor1
to get the scale temper you combine:

1 un-stacked iksar blood - uber rare drop off all kunark iksars, this was hardest to get for me, the best choices is to kill iksar necromancers in seb jail or go to bazaar and buy them, sell for around 100pp on my server.

1 un-stacked froglok blood - got it in 10min of killing frogs at upper guk, any frog there will do, doesnt need to be named or higher lvl.

1 un-stacked sarnak blood - off sarnaks in kunark.. best place to camp for those is at the sarnak fort in LOIO, got them fairly fast, around 20min, theres lots of sarnak at the fort.

1 un-stacked bloodwater - bought at various merchants in cabilis.. if you are a goodie race like i am, pay some noob around field of bone 5pp to pick a couple up for you, he will be more then happy is my guess :)

combine that and you get scale temper, trivial at 135 brewing.

then take the scale temper and combine it with tae ew blood vial and you get lizard-blood temper trivial at around 150 brewing i think.

hope this helps and good luck all on your farming.
