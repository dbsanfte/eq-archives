Kahn
You seem to go for a lot of wisdom on your gear, where most shaman go for hp. Most important upgrades IMO would be:

Change Ivory imbued earring for a Solstice earring for ~8k

Leaf scale gloves is a VERY cheap (I'm talking 10-50pp) upgrade. Sell your JB gloves to vendor and you actually make money from upgrading.

Change wis ring for shadel ring (~2k) if possible, otherwise 6/65 ring (~400pp).

You can get a Metal linked crown for ~300pp which is nice upgrade from JB helm.

Range slot - goranga idol is a cheap alternative.

Face, Boots, forest loop, arms and neck piece could also use an upgrade. Gwain had some good suggestions.

You could probably upgrade all of slots mentioned easily for 15k total.

Those Artificers ringmail stuff is nice! too bad there's no resists on them.
