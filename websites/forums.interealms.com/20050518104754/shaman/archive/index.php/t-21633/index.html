What I know about PoI factory that I don't know how many other people know about or care:
-When you go into the factory on the left side there's an NPC you talk to asking if you have the power source or something like that. He's the guy you talk to to get flagged for the plane of time. You say "yes" and if you have a quintessense of elements he ports you to another part of zone where you walk over and can talk to a gnome (I think it's the guy in the library, Maelin Starphyr? or something). You click the machine there, and get flagged for Time. Anyone that's Time flagged knows this.
-There's another NPC you can talk to there for a ring event. He ports you into a room in the zone that has lots and lots of spiders that start spawning. Apparently you kill as many as you can, and named start to spawn but that's just hearsay. I've only tried it once, and our group had to evac out in like 2 mins cuz of about 10 mobs in camp.
