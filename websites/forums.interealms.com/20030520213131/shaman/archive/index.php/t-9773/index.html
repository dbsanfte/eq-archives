Gloriana
Hiya Toein.

Grummus is all kinds of nasty. Hits hard, has a nasty AOE dot and has a nasty AOE curse too.

Suggestions:

* Get as many people as possible with Radiant Cure 1 - it cures Grummus' curse (a couple of clerics with MGB elixir or druids with MGB spirit of wood are also really good)
* Mem abolish disease NOT disenfecting aura. Shaman are the only folks that can cure Grummus' dot with one cast.
* Keep Grummus far enough way from casters/clerics that they aren't hit by the dot OR the curse. We always pull him to the long, rectangular room past the spider. Melee pin him in one corner of the room away from everyone else. We failed once, because Grummus ran over the casters and cursed everyone, they just started dropping like flies, that curse is MEAN.
