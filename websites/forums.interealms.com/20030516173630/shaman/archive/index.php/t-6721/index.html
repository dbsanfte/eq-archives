Harvyst
I think at 46, you would have to be a very very good soloer to do this, and then only do the bridge area. 5 of my pages dropped from the fog golem right at the gates, so you've got a good chance of getting those.

The spectral courier can spawn in either the north or south part of the stables. It spawned once in each for me. (Second time it dropped my item). Be aware that the stables are rough, as there is very little wiggle room, fast repops, and lots of large aggro ranges.

Your best bet is to team up with a ranger/druid/bard who has track, get your other pages while watching for a pop in the stables.
