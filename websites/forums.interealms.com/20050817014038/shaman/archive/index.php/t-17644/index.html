- shaman : lvl61 nuke <- fastest nuke, 2nd big nuke, it is our best nuke ?

Actually, Spear of Torment is our 3rd or 4th best nuke, and it isnt our fastest cast either.

Spear of Torment does 675 (level 61 - 275 mana - 4.2 sec)

Blast of Venom does 680 (level 54 - 340 mana, 3.9 sec)
Velium Strike does 925 (level 64 - 300 mana - 7.8 sec)
Ice Strike does 645 (level 54 - 250 mana, 7.0 sec)

Blast of Venom, which is the lower level version of Spear, casts faster and does more damage for 65 more mana. Ice Strike does 30 less damage for 25 less mana. And Velium Strike leaves Torment in the dust, doing 250 more damage for 25 more mana.

Or you could do Tears of Saryrn for 1950 (or 2600 with 4 waves) for 450 mana, providing no waves are resisted.

I have the ornate gloves, but I dont use Spear of Torment because it just doesnt compare to any of our other nukes at that level. IMO, I'd like to see it changed to a focus effect that helped Tears of Saryrn (either reduced mana or improved damage).
