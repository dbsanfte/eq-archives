sz_howlur
first off, you need at least 160 mr. that should allow you to resist the majority of his roots, assuming youre around the same level as him. since the pally is based entirely off mr, it will also mean that he can't blind, stun, etc.
secondly, unless you have incredible dual wielding weapons, break out your 2 hander. i'd stick with a 2 hander that has around 40 delay and great damage. you're using it to joust.
third, snare is your best friend. when he's snared, the joust and the bow make it super easy. he can't turn around quickly, he can't run away, he can't joust you effectively.
land your snare, bow him down until he figures out that he can't get to you, then start jousting. if you get low on life, heal when you need, but always keep the pressure on him. when he uses loh, you know he's done. it means he's pretty low on mana and running out of options.
as far as the people telling you to kite him, that's kind of laughable. he's not going to follow you around like some mindless mob while you make him a pincushion (unless he doesn't have much of a clue). you might be able to get away with it early on, but he'll catch on and run away or regroup. just keep your mr high and snare on him and you should be fine.

This is a no brainer - bow kite. Thats all you do against a paly. Like it's been stated, keep MR as high as possible, weapon/weapons with greatest MR - all MR gear etc. The pally should hardly ever even touch you.

If he does happen to get you rooted, take your biggest, baddest 2hndr (delay really doesn't matter) and melee him till root drops, then continue bow kite. Against a Pally you basically have to run them oom or drop some serious bow crits on them to win it.
