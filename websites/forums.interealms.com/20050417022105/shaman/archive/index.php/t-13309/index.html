Loreddar Of Karana
The boat is no longer needed to get to Timorous Deep. You may not have seen them, but there are these nice NPCs on some of the docks called Translocators. If you hail them, they'll offer to send you across the sea to a complimentary site in another zone. If I remember correctly, the translocator for TD is on the docks in Butcherblock (if not there, then it's the one on the docks in Firiona Vie). The correct Translocator ports you to the docks at the elf village in TD, which is on the island next door to the iceberg where the golras are. Lev/Sow gets you to the iceberg from the island in about 30 seconds. You can also bind on the docks on the island in TD.

Happy hunting!
