Yeah, do the first few parts. While the test of patience takes about an half an hour, it's not terribly difficult (except on your patience ;) ). You don't need to kill black dire to get the faction hit as the only faction hit for that part is from turning in the pelt. The two fools in RM aren't difficult, depending on your level. They're level 32, so if you have a pet, you can take them. They seem magic resistant, so I'd just sic the pet on 'em and use the cold nukes. Try to slow if you can, but don't blow all your mana. I killed them at 44 and it took only two blasts of blizzard blast to send them running. Anyways, good luck to you. :)
