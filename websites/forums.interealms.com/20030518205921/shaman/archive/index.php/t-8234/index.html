I duo pretty regularly with a necro and poor dogdog gets eaten virtually all the time while the massive damage lvl 60 spectre pet (compared to dogdog) gets ignored. The necro tells me it is because pet aggro is 90% distance related and dogdog attacks at short range.

It is possible to use them but it means you have to place the mob directly under the necro pet leaving dogdog at long range, this is a pita to do on every mob.

I ended up not bothering with pet when duoing with necro unless we were fear kiting.

If you are going to tank then it will be np as mob will always focus 90%+ on a PC rather than a pet.
