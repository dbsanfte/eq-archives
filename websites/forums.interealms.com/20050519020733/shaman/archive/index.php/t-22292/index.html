Karshak_rn
from the zone in run along the east wall, or the right wall, until you see the castle. Get as high up here as you can then run with lev on looking up. You should make it to the rampart. Sometimes you will hit the roof and have to sink down to get onto the rampart.

Once on the rampart you run down the stairs and out across the courtyard before heading up the stairs and out of the castle.
