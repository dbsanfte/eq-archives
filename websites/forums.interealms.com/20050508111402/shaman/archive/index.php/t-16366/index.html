Perelandra
Timers on buffs do not count while the pet is suspended. Shrink and Run Speed Changes won't still effect the pet after a suspend (it will still list SoW, but the pet will run slow).

Pet Discipline is still very beneficial. As a Mage it is considered the most important AA, but as a shaman I'm not sure where it would fall. If you plan on using your pet while raiding however, you should most definitely have this.

While you can use Suspend Minion as a poor man's discipline, you will end up having to kill the pet a lot as a pet on any NPC's aggro list cannot be suspended. I wouldn't even think of using a pet with only suspend minion in ToV - but use it all the time with Pet Discipline.
House
You can't suspend the pet if it's on a MOB's hate list, even if you tell the pet to back off, so it really can't be used in the same way as /pet hold. Plus it takes several seconds to work whereas /pet hold is instant. Since I wouldn't want to deal with a pet at a raid, the main use I find for Pet Discipline is when my group is pulling casters.

Suspend Minion 2 is a pretty decent ability. Zone in and instead of sitting around waiting for the rest of the group to assemble, you summon your pet, give him weapons and buffs, and then suspend him. Now you can invis to your camp and, once there, bring out the pet at no mana cost. Also very nice if you need to invis to go pick up new group members.
