I’m still new at playing a shaman, as I’ve been soloing all this time and doing little else, and I’m terrible at recommending gear, but I’ll suggest what I can. First off you should spend some time in the bazaar. Sort by troll and shaman and look at the different options per slot. The bazaar is a true godsend. I remember all the items being shouted out in the tunnel in EC and not having ANY idea what they were. With the bazaar you can see the stats on every item for sell simple by right clicking on the item in the search window. Thank you Sony.

If you need some cash you can sell spiderling silks, spider silks and lion, wolf and bear skins in the bazaar. They don’t sell for as much as they did the first few months after the bazaar opened, but you can still make some good money that way. Just check out the prices on your server and price your wares accordingly.

And if you’re looking for a place to level and like to group, you might try hunting in Paludal Caverns, which is reached off of Shadowhaven. The zone tends to be crowded and often populated with not so nice players, but the XP bonus in very good and most people level very quickly. The atmosphere rather reminds me of what Oasis used to be like. I’m told you can reach level 25 in Paludal if you want to stick around that long.

Best of luck.
