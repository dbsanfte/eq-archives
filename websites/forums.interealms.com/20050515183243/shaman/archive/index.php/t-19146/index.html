Gardeltron
STAT BUFFS

Shamans cast 2 kinds of buffs that are relevant:

1. Offensive buffs that increase ATK, STR, DEX, HASTE

2. Defensive buffs that increase AC, HP, STA, AGI, RESISTS

Right now, our standard buff is Focus that is part offense buff and part defense. At high levels, most players only want ATK, HASTE, and HP, since all other stats are close to being maxed.

So my suggestion would be to develop a couple of new spells one for offense and one for defense. Each spell should have individual and group versions.

1. New offense spell - Have it buff ATK, STR, and DEX. It should have a long duration, and stack with other class buffs, but not the Focus, STR, DEX, or Avatar lines.

2. New defensive spell - Have it buff HP, STA, and AGI. It should have a long duration. It should stack with other class HP type buffs, but not with our STA, AGI, and HP buffs.

I would also advise against any upgrades to the Avatar line, especially at the group level, unless the duration was long (over an hour)

HASTE buffs

Currently, our HASTE buff line is very weak. Since enchanters get a slow that is only 5% less than ours, I suggest that we get a new HASTE buff in single and group forms that is 5% less than the analogous enchanter HASTE buff.

IMPROVING NUKES

We need more diversity in the kinds of nukes we get. Currently we have only poison and cold based nukes. Here are some suggestions:

1. A new higher damage POISON nuke is needed, something along the lines of 1000-1200 damage for 300-400 mana.

2. A DISEASE based nuke line would really be distinctive, and add something unique to the shaman's ability to do damage. Disease is interesting because it has its own debuff (Malicious Decay line) which only shamans have. Make the top of the line spell do damage around 1000 with a mana cost of aroiund 400 or so.

3. An upgrade to our FIRE based nuke line, introduced at level 1 but then abandoned, would be nice. This could be as simple as adding shamans to the fire based nuke line of druids, or instituting a similar line of nukes that did slightly less damage for more mana, capped with a spell that does around 1100 damage for around 450 mana.
