Horomen
You raise a good question.

How do you raise "Skill" in things like Chain armor?

Stuff like Chain Armor you acquire the skill at a certain level and then it goes up every level. Its how they put the level limit on items so you cant supertwink a level 1, etc. Some weapon skills you also get at certain levels.

Other skills you have from the beginning and you work up as you use them (swimming, crushing, slashing, etc)
