Things I would go with:
6/65 rings on both fingers. (~500pp)
Tattered mantle (12 ac 20 hp) shoulder (~200pp)
Slime covered rubicite coif from CT (16 ac 65 hp, 6 agi, 4 fr/cr) (4-5k)
Earrings, either get jewelry made black sapphire ones (25hp/mana, 2 ac i think), or any of the 3 from chardok, generally sell for 500-1k, stats 2 ac 35hp/mana, 1 wis, and have fun effects, scale of wolf, cure disease, cure poison, very handy)

If you don't have much money i'd work on rings/earrings first. You'd lose 14 wis, but gain up to 200 hp (180 w/ bs ears), and 10 raw ac (about 15 noticeable).

Other than those, work on getting some PoG or Thurgadin quest armors, they'll upgrade most of your stuff with a pretty nice ac/wis/hp balance.
Bigguss
Wow. This one is almost too easy. :)

Ears: Imbued Golden Ivory Earring (cheap- under 100 pp, maybe much less if you provide the ivories- and a great combination of AC and WIS.) You could probably break even on this one.

Face: Arctic Wyvern Mask. You get a nice balance of AC and WIS with the mask, for just a few hundred pp (or less- I see these in the bazaar for 50 pp sometimes.) You should make money on this.

Neck: Fine. Keep your eyes open for a Crystalline Torque. 10% less mana on all spells = good.

Head: Get a JB helm. You'll gain 15 raw AC in exchange for 6 Wisdom. It's a good trade. The JB helm should set you back a couple hundred pp.

Wrist: If you DoT much, you might want to swap out the supple scale bracer for a Chipped Bone Bracelet (CBB) (with the CBB you get "Affliction Efficiency II" - which will cause all DoTs under level 45 to cost 25% less mana to cast.) Bracers are a tough slot to upgrade economically... supple scale isn't great but it's okay.

Arms: Fine

Hands: Fine. Upgrades are Coldain gloves or Crystal chitin gloves, but these will each set you back a few k, and what you've got now is good enough.

Fingers: 6/65 rings

Shoulder: Tattered mantle (100 pp or so, maybe less.)

Chest: Fine

Back: Fine. You AC/HP upgrade is the Ancient Seahorse cloak (ac 20, +25 hp) but it's at least a few k... and it looks like you want to maintain a wis/AC balance... so what you've got is okay.

Waist: Look for a cheap Chitinous belt (ac 13, +5 wis, lots of other stats). Flayed turmoil belts are really cheap now, too... same with smiter's girdles.

Legs: Tough spot to upgrade, especially on a budget. Kylong legs (ac 16) are more available then they used to be, and make a nice choice. Jarsath legs are the freebie choice, but not so easy to come by (drop in Karnors basement and NO DROP.)

Boots: JB boots will only set you back a few hunderd pp, will provide a nice raw AC bump, and will give you right-click Nimble. Worth having.

Range: There are some nice AC range items worth looking into. I use a Stone Idol (no drop from the 4-arms camp in Maiden's Eye, ac 5, +8 wisdom, +15 magic.) Even a goranga idol (couple hundred pp) gives nice AC.

Weapon / Shield: Fine

I think the most bang for the buck looks like this- in order: Head, Face, Feet, Range, Right ear, Rings, Legs

Hope this helps.
