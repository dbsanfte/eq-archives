Ciele
I'll assume you got Greater Immobilize.

In PoP, alot of crowd control can be accomplished by "root parking" mobs. If you are ever going to use this technique the new roots are a must since they are less resistable than the lower level roots such as Immobilize and Paralyzing Root.

In fact the new roots have a built in -30 resistant adjustment making them harder to resist on the initial cast, and harder to break while they are on, i.e. they'll last longer, even up to their maximum duration.
