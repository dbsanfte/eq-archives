Okay, so that is probably an odd question.

But if you needed to get 3 or 4 mobs pounding on you and only you right now what would you do?

The problem is, it always seems to be Duhikae that the mobs beeline for...if I see it coming, I can DB to get them off of him, but I need to make sure Shazzog has their full attention when it drops (all this assumes we don't decide to duke it out, of course. Half the battle is getting the Warrior and SK that are usually with us to back away and let me sort it out)

The cleric is getting the aggro from all the adds by healing. You pull it off him by healing him. Or, even better, throw that first quick heal on the puller or tank with the shaman, not the cleric. Then the mobs all beeline for the shaman in the first place, and not the cleric.
