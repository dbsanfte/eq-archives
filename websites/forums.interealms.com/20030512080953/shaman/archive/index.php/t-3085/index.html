get your MR near 100 atleast.
throw up 3 crap buffs first.
throw on talisman stamina and resists last.


if he's using a pet (most good duelin necros don't) root it and forget it till root breaks.

keep your mana for heals and roots. Use 1 YES ONE dot. Disease dot. DR usually sucks. When it fades apply again.

The only time i got beat by a shaman in a duel was when he kept all his mana for heals and beat me down in melee.

Your DD's won't eat through his damage absorption shield but dots sneak under it and eat from the inside.

Keep him near, beat on him as much as possible and slam him when he's casting if you're the race to do it.

a smart necro will have his damage absorption sheild memmed to negate your nukes and it's a pain in the butt.

shaman/necro duels are long. Be patient.
