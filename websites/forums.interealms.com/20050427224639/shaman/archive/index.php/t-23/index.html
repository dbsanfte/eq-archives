You have to have as many points in that camp as the item that you want to buy. For instance, if you wanted to buy Anathema, you would have to have done enough missions in BB to have 410 Mistmore points.

Now, say that you had 500 total points, of which 410 were gotten in Butcherblock. You went to South ro camp and spend 300 points in trinkets and junk there. That takes your total points down to 200. THen you can a few Everfrost missions and got your point total back up to 500. Then you could go to Butcherblock and buy Anathema for 410 points. Even though the bulk of the points that you used to buy Anathema in BB were not from running a BB adventure, you already had done enough adventures there to unlock the items in that price range.
