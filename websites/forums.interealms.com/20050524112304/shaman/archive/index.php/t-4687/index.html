Hardened Grey Sleeves from grey 16 AC +6 Str +6 Sta +6 Dex +5 vsCold +25 HP

-or-
player made hp/mana earring if you want to go that way

face - personally I had a cougarskin mask for a long time ac 6 2 dex/agil 10 hp. I have that one from rokyl atm ac 5 +7 int/wis more damage 2, but I am trying to get the gangrenous beetle mask from OS. 7 AC +5 Str +5 Dex +10 vsDis +20 Mana More ac and DR to raise that up) if you have the cash try to get crystal spider eyes 8 AC +3 Wis +3 Int +35 HP +35 Mana. i would love this item, but no drop > manaburn

head - that othmir is doing you no good. get a Jscale helm from KC +10 AC +2 Wis +2 Int +5 vsMg That stuff just rots. Heck get a full suit of jscale and keep what you like and toss what you dont.

wrist - drop the supple scale and use jscale bracer 8 AC +4 Wis +4 Cha until you find something better. only thing supple is good for is casting heals away from Gore's AE and pvp. If you are 3 football fields away from the mob you are killing then go play a druid. shamans get up close and personal. you should be bathing in your enemies blood, not being prissy like a caster.
-or-
coldain military wristguard ac 10 5 str 6 wis 5 mr 5 cold extended affiliction 3 (makes your dots last longer). This is my favorite piece of gear atm until I get bracer of deep sea from ntov. anything that makes me do more damage, slows last longer, malx stick longer, you gotta love.

neck - shrouded medallion 12 ac 5 str 25 mana 15 mr 5 fr/cr no drop from robed guys in ME

Shield - if you want to hunt chardok (before you get your ring is more faction work later) or you can afford it, the tried and true sarnak battle shield is good. i love the hp's and resists on this one. or the skull marked shield from the grey.

You should go to allakazams and research heavily. I have used my moneys worth and more on that site. get yourself an account there and research. did i say research?
