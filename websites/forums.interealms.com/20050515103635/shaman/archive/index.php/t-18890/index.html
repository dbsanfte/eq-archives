csivils
This may hurt more than it helps, but I think you should reconsider the double necro thing.

The second necro adds so much less than the first one does.

I only have 2 accounts, but if I had 3... here are the things I would look for.

1. You want classes that add well to each other
2. You want classes that are easy to play, max of one meele
3. You want to have as many options open as possible

Using a necro as a starting point, here are some possible group arrangements. I'd recommend you look at each one and see what sounds interesting to you.

Since your torn between Shaman/BL, its obvious you want a slower. The 4 possibilities for slower are bard, BL, shaman and enchanter.

Bards are very difficult to 2box, I can't imagine trying to 3box. Great class, but probabbly not suited for this.


Enchanters are a strong class, but it would mean that the third slot would need to be some type of healing. There is a large overlap with shaman, so your necro/chanter/? choices would be down to cleric, paladin and druid. The necro/chanter/cleric combo is strong, but more so in the later stages. I'm not sure its a strong startup combo. The other two combo's don't grab my attention, but they might be something you want to play.


With a shaman slower, you have a slower and healer but chances are that the last choice should help with the shamans lack of dps. Wiz is a possibility, but I'm not crazy about that since it doesn't add much to the other classes. Warrior/Monk goes well with the shaman, but since you have a necro to FD pull its again not a lot. IMO the best 3rd choice with a shaman would be a mage. They have a strong pet and all kinds of summoned utility items.


To be honest, even though I'm a shaman player I think the two beastlord combos are actually the funnest (to me) looking. With beastlord as the slower, there are 3 factors to consider.
* You don't have a "player" healer (you have plenty of pet heals)
* You don't have magic debuff capabilities
* You would have your single meele class and need a caster for the third slot

Necro/BL/Mage
This can be a pet centric group, with the BL slowing. The mage can debuff magic resistance which helps with landing slows and for necro undead charming. The main drawback is the lack of a big heal to take on heavy hitters.

Necro/BL/Druid
Until the druid gets direcharm, this is less pet intensive than the other groups. The BL/Druid is a great combo and the necro adds a lot of dps on top of that. This combo isn't has high a dps as the necro/mage/bl, but it has ITU, fd, track, ports, big heals, good mana regen, all kinds of utility along with dps.

If I was going to 3box from scratch, the last combo (necro/bl/druid) is the one I think I'd go with.
