soupypoopy
Well Weiss, that hotkey isn't actually casting canni multiple times. It's only casting it once. There's a difference between a multi-cast canni hotkey and a failure-safe hotkey.

A multi-cast hotkey does indeed suck for the reasons you mention. The failure-safe hotkey I listed above really only adds 0.4 seconds to the hotkey's overall execution time.

Here it is again:

/sit off
/pause 2, /cast 3
/pause 2, /cast 3
/pause 14, /cast 3
/sit on

This hotkey casts canni ONE time per press, not three times. If you fizzle canni, the hotkey will attempt to cast it two more times. The maximum time taken for this hotkey is about 1.8 seconds.

Compare and contrast this to a multi-cast hotkey:

/sit off
/pause 30, /cast 3
/pause 30, /cast 3
/pause 30, /cast 3
/sit on

That will take about nine seconds (assuming three seconds is enough time to cast canni and ungrey the spell gem -- which I'm not certain of). First of all, you miss a med tick, defeating the whole purpose of canni-dancing. Second of all, it takes too damned long. Finally, it fails to deal gracefully with fizzles, and that's an issue given Canni III's tendency to fizzle.
