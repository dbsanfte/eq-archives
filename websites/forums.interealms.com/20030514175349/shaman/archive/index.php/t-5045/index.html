clock thing is taken from another site somewhere... it measures in game "ticks" ... so a poison dot lasts 7 ticks from when it lands, etc., and timer under it can be used to keep track of longer duration spells like slows, etc. on raids.

I wish I could take credit for the timer, because it's a nice piece of work IMO, but I don't remember who's responsible for it. Certainly someone with more freetime than me.

It takes the place of the pet window, but I figure I've used hotkey commands so long for my pets I can keep on doing it and get more use from that window as a timer. Plus my ISP kicks me off every 5 hours, give or take, so this timer is also perfect for knowing around when I need to camp and reset my connection.
