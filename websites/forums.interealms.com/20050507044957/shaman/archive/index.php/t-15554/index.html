Plane of Hate. That places changes more often than Britney Spears doing a concert. Anyway.

Zone-in. Here you have your first potential for WIPE. Aggro is stupid in Fear and you need a little luck. (Especially since you are (admirably) trying it at your levels). There are roamers which pass by the zone in building. Level aggro (as in your 46's) are gonna bring many from a lot of places. So you need to do a couple of things.

1) Identify a MA and make sure EVERYBODY assists.
1a) Identify a SA and make sure he has a healer.
1c) Identify a TA and also make sure he has a healer.
2) No sitting if you even THINK there's a MOB incoming.
3) MOB priority.

a) Golems (look like the ones in NeK forest) SOW themselves. And they like to run. (This often leads to CR) Now while you cannot snare or slow them (100% or near MR), you can DISPEL them. This normally falls to Druids. Because they cannot be snared, they cannot also be mezzed. (That should be self-evident, but hey, I've seen dumber things happen).

b) Clerics (Look like the guards in NeK and Neriak). " Highly " MR, but they ARE mezzable. You need to lock these bastards down because if not they heal other MOBs.

c) Small MOBs. IE Rats, Gargoyles, and Ghouls. These *******s get in and amongst your clump of people and it is VERY hard to target them. Standard MR though. (iirc). Rats the worst.

Ok, so on zone-in you've identified your target priority: Golems, Clerics, Other, you've got your healers set, and all is gtg.

EXPECT zone-in Aggro. EXPECT to have people zone in at different times and see bodies on the floor. Do NOT lose it. CR naked is a hell of a lot tougher than 3 adds.

* IF * you get wiped, make sure : a) A Cleric gets out, b) A Monk gets down, and c) A Rogue clears his aggro and also gets out or hides.

Zone-IN successful.

Great. Sit there and get your breath back. Now start pulling. I recommend if you are new to zone to minimize movement. Realize the clock is ticking against you. Unlike Fear, the roamers DO respawn, and the rate (AFAIK) is 22 minutes.

There is a map at the Safehouse (sorry don't have URL) that outlines the buildings and MOBs in them. Your puller will NOT have to move far to start bringing in bad guys.

Let's talk aggro. Specifically puller aggro.

If I had a platinum piece for every time I thought I feigned off a MOB only to have IT AND FRIENDS come tear-assing for my Monk throat - I'd be buying beers for all of Norrath.

Roamers pass aggro to every, single, stinking MOB they come near. And oh by the way, just because you don't see them in the first 10 seconds does NOT mean they are not coming for you.

** YOUR PULLER SHOULD NEVER ENTER CAMP UNLESS THEY HAVE JUST LOGGED IN. **

Heal and buff your puller from a distance. He or she should be prepared to die - often. They have to to save the raid.

Vorpatril
As has been previously mentioned, the aggro ranges in PoF is legendary, but if you hit the zone in at the right time, you can be safe for a bit. How do you know when to zone in? One handy technique is to send a SK/Monk/Necro in invis and have them FD at the zone in point. Then have then use the different camera keys to pan around the area to figure out where the wanderers are. Let them get a feel for the pathing and have them call out when the area is clear. This isn't a guaranteed thing - it's just a way to not be attacked the moment you zone in.

After that you are on your own ... ;)

5) Root Class/Healer

Once they are in then everyone else needs to zone in as close to the same time as possible. (Practice if need be on another zone line to get everyone on about the same time zoning.) As soon as the Party is in zone the Crowd control starts rooting mezzing every add that comes in. Summon the corpse to where you zoned into and have looter loot in express mode. (RIGHT CLICK to pick up everything. Is far quicker and some dont know this.) When looter is done he calls EVAC. If at any time it looks like there are too many incoming then EVAC out and try again. If possible provide a gate potion to the FDer so he can drop group safely and FD on a EVAC call if too many adds come so you can cut down the rezoning time. If done correctly you could do it with less like 1 chanters, 1 wiz/Druid, 1 Monk/Necro/SK, Body Looter.
