Torgiran mines are fun. From about 53 you can make excellent xp here. I solo'd from the dulak entrance all the way through to the huge room (no idea if it has a name) and all around the tunnels to the boss mob (didn't agro for obvious reasons). The mobs get harder as you progress, just note the different miners names and when they change expect a little harder battle. I had no problem slowing them but they often come in pairs. This is a fun zone, and usually empty. You can also get xp from handing in the keys that drop to the resistance miners who then poof.
Enjoy.
