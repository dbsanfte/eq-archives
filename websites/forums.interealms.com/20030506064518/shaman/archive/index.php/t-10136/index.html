taraddar
Did you have your pet up? You pet can add a descent amount of dmg to the fight and doesn't need much mana input once the fight has started. Just park him someplace and drag the fight to him once you've got him debuffed.

You don't include your lvl or a link to your gear so not sure where you stand on that. If you have an affliction efficency or extend afflction item definitely use them. If you don't have either the coldain military bracer is an easy to get extend afflction item.

For these types of fights there are 2 ways you can go. You can lay heavy on the dots and hopefully kill it before you run out of mana. The other option is the slow and steady method. For slow and steady you just use pet/ JBB/Epic etc for damage and spend your mana healing yourself. It won't die quickly but should die eventually. If you are FM and have mana to burn then you can use a dot.

As far as healing goes I would probably torpor when ever you get down to 75-80% or so and cann if you aren't full mana. Keep yourself healed and keep the fight going and you will win eventually. Epic when you can, dot when you have mana to burn and let those and pet take him down.

With more aa skill/better gear/higher end spells it is possible to just dot the heck out of them and have them die before you do.

I have done Gw's with a 2 boxed paladin(63), then with a necro (62) and then a cleric (62) ... when i dinged level 60 I decided to "sow my ogre oracle oats" and solo 55-60 lv mobs a bit just to get the hang of mana managment, hp losses and topor. Here is how i pull and kill GW's without Malo or Pox ( whole reason i go kill them besides the fact that it is FUN!! )


get to island to the east of VP ent get pet up buffed regened hasted ect I have always been cleric and chanter buffed prior to going to SF to do this ( so not a real solo guys ). Park the pet there, go pull a GW's from either VP ent (static) or a roaming one ( path from VP to zone wall and back rather slow so take your time picking the right one ). I use lev and sowboots to make it easier. Pull target with Malo ( i use malonise due to not having malo i have NEVER had it resisted ) start walking the GW back to the island keeping an eye out for adds, if you do get a add ROOT it fast and keep moving towards island, if you are far enough away when root breaks or wears off the mob will reset till you go near it again. On the way back to the island slow the mob, and when you get back to the island ROOT it ... cripple, canni canni canni canni Topor back up ... then when fm reslow and use epic ( the dot starts off slow and will not get you summoned right away for a few ticks ) cast plauge ( long cast time and lasts longer than Bane and E-Bolt ... this will get you summoned. Then cast your favorite line of dots dd or what ever you like best, rinse and repeat dots until he is dead, I have found that without PoX i have to reslow at about 20% to be safe for the last few seconds of the fight, I do not have the real nice hp chewing dots yet. The pet is for nothing more than extra dmg output and he uses war disc "shield" on you when you get low on hp if it is going bad. Best I have done on this is ending the fight with 80% -100% hp and 25% -45% mana range ... I had a Jbb but sold it due to long recast interupts and just plain borring to right click over and over. I do have a horse and will sometime sumon it while fighting depending on how the GW is taking the spells ( i like to shrink 2 times so i do not have a targeting problem )..
