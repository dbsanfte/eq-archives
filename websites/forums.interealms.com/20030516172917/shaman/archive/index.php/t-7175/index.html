700mhz AMD duran

The Duron may be holding you back (fair cpu's, but basically a Celeron). You could probably get a 900 Athlon fairly cheaply if your board will support it (check the website and manual, BIOS update may be required). Might also want to make sure your fan is decent with the uppage in temperature.

32meg Nvidia 2 video card

PCI or AGP? The AGP Radeon 8500's and Nvidia G3 Ti200's have gone cost effective and make for a nice upgrade as well.

Or you can do what I did for my wife: Buy a Intel 1.8 (or higher) Celeron (boxed) and an 845G board for around $150 together. It was a cheap solution and I was able to use all her existing hardware. Make sure you get a P4 based Celeron though, the performance is much better than any of the P3 based Celery's.
