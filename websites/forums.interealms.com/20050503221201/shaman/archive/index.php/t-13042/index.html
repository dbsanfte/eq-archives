As far as I am currently aware...
1. Root can't be extended by any focus effects.
2. Extending beneficial buffs uses the Extend(ed?) Enhancement focus items (eg Totemic BP has lvl II on it).
3. Spell Haste focus items affect all your spells except those cast off items/potions etc.

Pick a corner, and when the signal comes, slow every clockwork bug you see, as fast as you can. With torpor, and a bit of luck, you can end up with a constant steam of them on you for the whole raid, anything upto 6 at a time.

I should mention that the objective is that the spiders never get past you, or they mess up the raid, getting aggro and keeping them on you, whilst the others kill them, seems to be the best way to do this, and is great fun (mezzing and rooting is a BAD idea - but charm is fine).
