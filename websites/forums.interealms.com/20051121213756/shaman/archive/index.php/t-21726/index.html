Kahn
A friend of mine is playing CoH on his PC with:
733 mhz processor
512 mb ram
Geforce4 (the cheapest one in stores for ~$35)

He can run the game in 1024x768 pretty well with most settings at the lowest, which is not really that bad, it still looks quite nice!

There will be a fps drop in areas with lots of effects though, so you should stay away from using dark armor and other powers with loads of particles. I would also recommend playing a ranged kind of AT (defender/blaster/controller) to get away from the close-up effects.

Doing missions etc in smaller groups you will be fine even with some AE effects. Solo should be no problems at all.
