and

EQShaman Skills (http://www.eqshaman.com/)

As for an initial strategy, just about everyone seems to agree that the first 3AA points should go to RUN3. This will increase your base run speed to just about jboots speed, allowing you to outrun most of the nasties you'll encounter.

It's really important for indoor environments where SoW won't work. Outdoors it's not as important if you have the insta-cast SoW boots that are part of our epic.

When you get RUN3 depends on how much you plan on fighting indoors. Personally, I've had all my chars stop XP'ing at 51 to get RUN3. RUN3 has saved my collective lives more times than I can imagine.

Hope this helps!

The advice I was given by my shammy guildmates was this (Incidentally I feel Levels >> AA so I went to 65th 1st but thats purely personal preference)

Run 3 (it's a must simply for getting round in no sow zones)
Regen 3 (apart from Run 3 the other general AA's are bog useless)
Mental Clarity 1, 2, 3 (12 AA's to get and it hurts but its a pre requisite for.... )
Canni 5 (definately a ground breaker in AA terms and has a noticable performance effect on me in groups, Raids (when not on my Rogue main) and soloing)

After that SCR needs to be maxed and then SCRM, the Spell Casting Reinforcement AA line directly effects how long your buffs last and in that how long you Stoicism/Torpor/Quiescence lasts so you get more heals for the same mana, believe me its a winner.
After that simply go for whatever takes your fancy, Mass Group Buff is great for a raid Shaman, Spirit Call is a lot of fun and handy DPS, Healing AA's I personally like a lot too .
