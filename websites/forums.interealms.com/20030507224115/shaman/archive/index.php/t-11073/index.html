I don't know about 5% per kill, but I get 1.5% per kill in PoN just doing GY trash, and kills only take around 2 minutes with 30 seconds or so cann/torpor afterwards. So in the 10-15 min you spend killing one Razorfiend for 8% I've probably killed 4-6 gnarled treants for 6-9% AA, and probably at less risk.

I like to go just north of the graveyard, past the tree that looks like a treant and directly across from the mob that triggers the Daffiyd event. There are two trees to the left, one tree diagonally left, and a random spawn tree-bird-bat to the right just out of sight.
