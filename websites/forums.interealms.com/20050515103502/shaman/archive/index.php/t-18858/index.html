Ok I bought the aug yesterday and today I did some parsing. What I discovered is that like most focus effects, the actual benefit is random. Instead of being a set 10% extension, it is actually a random 1-10% extension. This is what happens when used with short duration dots:

Bane is extended from 6 ticks to 7 ticks every time.
Blood of Saryrn is extended from 8 ticsk to 9 ticks every time.

I only had the patience to run through two breaths of ultor:

The first lasted 166 seconds, a 10 second (6.4%) increase.
The second lasted 169 seconds, a 13 second (8.3%) increase.

This means you can expect 1-3 extra ticks of breath of ultor.

I had similar results for turgur's. All my of my tests were extended randomly for up to 30 seconds (small parse size, so you might hit the full 39 second extension with more trials). This means you can expect an extra 1-6 ticks off of turgur's.

All-in-all a good aug, but mainly for the extra tick on bane and blood. If anyone has some results for the higher tier detrimental extenders (timeburn or chronoburn), it would be much appreciated if you did some similar testing for comparison - it only takes an hour or two.
