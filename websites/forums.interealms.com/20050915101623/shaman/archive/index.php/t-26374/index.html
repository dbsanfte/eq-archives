I have cancelled my EQ account. My current subscription expires on May 30th, kind of ironic that's the day they Tunare and Eci server mergers scheduled.

EQ is imploding. They've lost the faith of their customers. They've been top dog for too long and have become complacent. They can't compete with the new games and are scrambling and playing catchup.
