At 30th level, what is the wisdom to mana ration up to 200 wisdom and up to 250 wisdom?

I'm looking to make the largest possible mana pool I can at 30th level for repeat casting of the imbue spells (200 mana single, 600 mana mass), but am unsure if +mana or +wisdom is the way to go and at what point should I go one over the other. Any data or info would be greatly appreciated. Thank you.
Nytmare
Under 200 wisdom: mana per wis = level/5.
Over 200 wisdom: approx level/13.

At level 30 this means 1 wis = 6 mana under 200 wisdom; and 1 wis = 2.3 mana over 200 wisdom (up to 255).
