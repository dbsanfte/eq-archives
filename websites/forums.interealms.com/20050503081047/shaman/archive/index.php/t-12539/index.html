agrad
Yes, dogdog can proc both primary and off hand weapons.

There are plenty of nice weapons to give our pets, usually I just do a search in bazaar for primary slot items, all effects, max price 50pp.

This will generally return a list of stuff, some of which may be suitable pet weapons, as people try to sell off things.

There was a list in the equipment forums some time ago, of proccing weapons for dogdog, but I just mostly buy what is cheap. Some examples:

Wakizashi of Frozen Skies (81pt dd) - 30-150pp
Gnoll Hide Lariats (stun) 5-50pp
Serrated Bone Dirk (Engulfing Darkness) 25-50pp
Dragon Horn Ykesha (Ykesha 75pt dd) 30-100pp

Plenty of other choices out there, but again I usually go for what I can find cheap. What I really need is an unlimited supply of journeyman's walking sticks for the puppy, the tash proc would come in handy a lot of the time :)
