Chutoi
Yes, they should DEFINITELY overwrite each other. Think of it as a raid feature.

Let's look at an old encounter most people have probably done--Aary. Heavily MR, and hard to land slow. Back then, you needed to land slow quickly, cause back then, when Velious was newish, he was a beat-ass mob.

Malos. Slow with Balance for the -40 resist assist. Reslow with turgurs for longer duration slow at your leisure.

5.5 minutes later start reslows. Bounce, bounce, bounce . . . suddenly he's about to be unslowed again. You can use Balance again if needed.

If turgurs blocked Balance, you're at the mercy of the random number generator on reslows, with no resist adjustment.

Sure, it's a rare situation, but it does come up even in PoP. Let's not shoot ourselves in the foot on raids for a minor convenience issue.
