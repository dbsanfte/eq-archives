JUst some FYI that worked for me. At 17, I went to HighPass Hold and grouped to fight Orcs right next to zone to Kithcor Forest, action was fast and furious, make sure you have some good Healers, loot was good compared to what I was use to in Oasis. I quickly leveled to 21 ( took less than 3 days I think, playing about three hours a day), the main problem you will have here is getting into a group, the area is small and allows for only one group to pull and camp the area, so once you have gotten into a group, milk it for all that it is worth.

From 21 to 31, I went to HighHold Keep, made 10 lvls in 5 or 6 days ( playing about 3 to 4 hours a day on average). Be careful inside this small outpost (It has a Bank and merchants) you will see alot of corpses around the bank due to the fact that Gnolls and Prisoners are trained through there quite frequently. Here again, the main problem you will face is getting into a group, be patient, it is worth it once you do. The loot was the best I had ever made up to that level and the action can quickly become over-whelmming, sending your party fleeing out of the basement, do not depend on the guards to save you, they are under-powered and are often killed by the same train you pulled, and then you are next on their list if you are still standing around watching the fight.
At this time I am still working on level 32, and will let you know if I find any quick leveling spots. If anyone has any suggestions on some hunting grounds please let me hear about them. Laters and good hunting!

Regarding faction in HHK, one way to do it relatively quickly is to hunt in Crushbone for orc scalps. They drop FAR more frequently there than they do off of the orcs in Highpass, and they can be handed to Captain Ashlan for faction. You may need to hunt some orcs in Highpass to get enough faction for the turn-in, though wolf form should do the trick.

This also gets you some faction in Qeynos and Freeport (Corrupt Qeynos Guards and Freeport Militia), for what it's worth. :)
