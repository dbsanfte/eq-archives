Vladimirr
DEX, DEX, DEX. It's all about the DEX. Seriously.

A Geerlok tool (which is tinkered by gnome players) increases your effective skill. All this does is give you better success rates. It DOES NOT change the rate of skillups.

DEX increases the rate of skillups. Especially 255+.

Click on my name in my sig for my Magelo profile, then go to the "BIO" section and look for "Mianu's Profile". That's my wife's setup, and she has a separate profile for Fletching Gear. That gear may give you some ideas for upgrades.

Lastly, if you get a DEX buff, get one from a Shaman (group Focus is nice). DO NOT get Boon of the Garou from an enchanter unless you are FAR AWAY from all NPC's that will kill you in werewolf form.
