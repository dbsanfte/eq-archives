Juujuu
A long long time ago, almost the only item that could go in the range slot were throwing items or bows. There were a few weapons that could go in the range slot for stats, but not many (runed bone fork for example for 10mr fr cr). When Kunark came out many many more items were able to be put in the range slot. The problem that happened, though, was that most of those weapons also had a range: X which indicated how far away you can throw it at a mob. Probably after many people petitioning about losing items from range slot due to accidently throwing items, or from being charmed and throwing them away, Verant removed the range: X value so you cannot throw the weapons at all. They realized that no player in their right mind would ever throw an item from range slot that is there solely for the stats.

FYI some items have stats that will go in the ammo slot too, but you don't receive any stat bonuses from items there. The shissar fangs from Ssra temple are a good example of this. Best ammo slot items I can think of in the game are summoned coldstones from mages, or the fungal fluid thing from Fungus Grove with 10 charges of snare.
