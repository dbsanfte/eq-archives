Hail,

I admire your desire to quest for this item. :)
I hope it isn't for the 5 charges of minor healing. ;)
But I would highly recommend you NOT trash your faction (since that is a concern of yours) and instead BUY TWO Opal Encrusted Steins.

Each Opal Encrusted stein has 25charisma and 10mana on it, and they aren't LORE (like the Crude Stein) so you can wear two of them at once for a 50 point charisma boost. If you have any friends who are in the upper 180's (or higher) skill level, you can ask them to make them for you. They are typically used in the skill progression ladder for Potters, and the pricing on them is extremely low.

If you use the /bazaar feature, I'd expect you could probably get each stein for ~40-50pp, possibly cheaper if there is a glut (common) on the market from a few recent skill-up sessions.

That said, I have heard that they nerfed the Crude Stein quest, but I have no solid facts if this is the case or not. Not worth trashing your faction to prove/disprove this.
