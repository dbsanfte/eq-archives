Juujuu
It sounds like you don't have enough Truespirit faction yet, for some reason. Here's what you do to fix that.
1. Go to Rathe Mountains and kill the bandit and then get the gem from the spirit.
2. If the paladin or shadowknight are up, then nab them and take their woe and envy and shield for turn in again for more faction.
3. Go to Freeport and turn in the gem to the barbarian. Bind yourself here.
4. Go to step 1 until you have a better faction level.
5. Go back to the wandering spirit and try talking to him now. If you say "shield of falsehood" he should give you a shield if you have enough faction. If he doesn't give it up, go back to step 1.
