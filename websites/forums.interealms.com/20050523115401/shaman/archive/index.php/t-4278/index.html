Jaradis
You guys are all doing it the hard way or giving inaccurate info. I just PL'd a wizard to 24 in 7 hours with a my druid. Its not hard at all.

Simply do this. Buff both characters as well as you can. Do NOT group them. Go to an area where mobs are KoS and auto agro without having to do anything to them, or unequip your weapon and punch them once to agro them but you want to do as little damage as possible. Mobs should be low red to your druid. For a 30 druid you want mobs that are level 35 or less, any higher and you'll get too many resists. Undead preferred, runners make it a pain.

Have the druid give YOU a damage shield, then go pull 3 mobs (most you can pull is 3, any more won't stay agro on you). Let them beat on you and don't cast any damaging spells on them. Do NOT slow them. Have your druid cast ONE AE from a safe distance after they are half dead, all you need to do is 1 point of damage (assuming shaman did no damage, otherwise you need to do 1 point more than shaman did), then sit and wait for damage shield to kill them off.

Damage shield does not count at all towards kill, so if the druid's 1 AE is the only damage done besides the DS then the druid gets full xp for the kill.

You can have the mobs attack the druid and heal him once in a while to get his defense up but other than that let them beat on you. Most mobs will hit your shaman for much less than they hit the druid so you don't have to use as much mana healing.
