Well, he has 145 mana in mana items, which is worth well over 15 wisdom, so he's not THAT lacking in the ability to kick out the spells, guys.

I had 120 wisdom until about level 52, when I started getting upgrades here and there that, like someone noted occurs, eventually raised my wisdom whether I wanted it or not. The higher you go, the more wisdom seems to just kind of accumulate. The one thing I would think of doing where possible is getting some wisdom gear so that when grouped you can switch in some more wisdom items. You will be tanking a lot less in groups so need AC less, and needing wis/mana more, so should switch some in. A great compromise item is the jaded velium rings, AC 5 mana 30 HP 30. Mana 30 is worth more than wis 3 at your level, so you are actually getting the equivalent of almost wis 4 by equipping just one of those rings, and still getting almost the same AC and still keeping some nice hitpoints. Try to keep lots of situational gear around -- which gets pretty expensive, by the way. It's all very worth it though, you'll make constant use of good switchable equipment.

I notice a lot of gear suggestions for him, too, which as someone also noted he may not agree he needs as much as some would think he does. But also, it assumes he has money to blow at this point or anytime soon, which may or may not be the case.

THAT being said....besides the epic, the three shaman items that completely kick ass to the extent that you really MUST try to get them are the JBB, the fungi tunic, and the iksar regen BP. You are iksar so can't get a JBB, and if you could afford a fungi you would probably be wearing it, so there's no point dwelling on that.

Which leaves the ceremonial iksar chestplate. Regens 5 standing/6 sitting, AC 19 weight 6.0. Until you get this item, buy NOTHING but any spells you are missing. Level and live like you're broke! This item is far better than any chest piece you could be wearing(and probably anything you could put in any slot besides a fungi tunic). Goes for 5-7k on my server.

At your level, fighting in The Overthere, for instance(which is exactly what I did at your level), you can sometimes GAIN hitpoints WHILE FIGHTING. It's not possible to exaggerate the importance of that.

Get that BP and then save up for fungi and/or rune etched icewurm fang, which is the best weapon you'll hold until you get your epic, at 12/19, wis 5 mana 35 and spiritstrike DD that procs a fair amount.

I guess for an iksar, I'd say ikky BP, fungi, and rune etched icewurm fang are the three items that will change the game the most for you until you get an epic and torpor.

By the way, I banked many fine items while wearing ikky BP and fungi BP--including even my HoT CoV BP. Don't even think about underestimating regen -- which I'm sure you're already loving as an iksar.

By the way grats on having some guts and not playing your iksar like a root and rot druid. Most do.
