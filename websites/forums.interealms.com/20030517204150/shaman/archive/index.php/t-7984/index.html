Okay, hoping that I can clear up some confusion here. Yes, there is an activated war in Jaggedpine Forest. It is done the a few minutes after the head gnoll, Barducks Darkpaw, is killed. The gnolls come and attempt to take over the small viliage. If the main guard is saved, then you get a few items from him, but not anything big from what I have heard. I have seen everyone in the town killed except for the banker, and I have seen no one killed before. Anyways, the main gnoll is up every 24 hours, and if after he's been killed and then gnolls have taken care of the city, it takes about 2 hours for the zone to reset itself.

Okay, as far as exp goes, I did 54 and 55 in HHK. I realize that you may not see the exp move that much there per kill, but I'm telling you that the mobs are soo easy to kill, that you're bar will move fast. Because the area became perma camped for a few weeks when I made the mistake of telling my roommates brother about the area, I went adventuring to find another spot. I can tell you that solo wise, it was easily the best place to go to.

When I hit 56, i was still able to get 1/2 yellow per C3 there, but I thought that it was slower than what I started to get in JPF (jaggedpine forest). I did most of 56 - 58 in JPF, and then 58 - 60 in Katta. All of this was done solo, so I know that it can be done. I was not able to click on your link, so I don't know what stuff that you have, but I have found that with my limited gear, i was easily able to handle that area. Oh, and I didn't get the horse and ODS until almost lvl 60, but that did make a huge difference
