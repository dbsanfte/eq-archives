Zolina
If you can pull it off, enchanter charm + ranger snare/bow = really nice exp. Have the ranger snare a mob then the enchanter charm it as a pet. Pull another of the same mob, snare it, sic the pet on it, but don't slow it. Have ranger bow attack the mob. Mob should die very quickly, and its enrage should do sizable damage to the pet. Have enchanter break charm, root former pet, and finish it off with bow. Two mobs dead faster than most six-person exp groups can kill one mob. Just make sure you have plenty of free room to run and choose mobs can't summon, otherwise a premature break of charm could kill both ranger and enchanter. Another tactic is to slow the mob and keep the pet for 2-3 battles before finishing it off, either way works fine.
