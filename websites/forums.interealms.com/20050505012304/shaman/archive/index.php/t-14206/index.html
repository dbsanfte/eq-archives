Skeeter
If you dismiss LOY as 'not worth it' you're really missing out. I loved hunting in Dulak and Crypt of Nadox. And the Mines are well done IMO, I love the 'mine' feel -- and the feeling you get when you 'free' a resistance miner! Start the '20' countdown! heh. :)

Plus Blood of Nadox *rocks* if you ever hunt in zones where you are fighting NPC shaman mobs (like Cazic) -- can get rid of slows and poison on the whole group with one casting. And I love stoicism -- it's great until you hit 60.

The best part is the money. While it's not quite as profitable as Seb bugs, you do get lots of gems, planar gems, and a lot of toys. I went from 40 to 44 there and banked a few thousand pp in the process, got several equipment upgrades and a ton of twink items, and 5-6 backpacks filled with racial meats and blood.

While doing the Blood of Nadox quest in the Crypt, you have chances at a nice int caster belt (with Brilliance, nice twink item especially once KEI no longer works on under 46's tomorrow) and a rare drop with FT at one camp; 8 wis shoulders, a 5 wis off-hand item, and an axe that proc's a rune-type spell (think upgraded PGT) at another camp; and the luggald flesh that you need for the nice ring quest(s) at the third camp (and a chance at the 7 wis ring if you grab the trolls for some variety).

Granted, if you're in the Elemental planes, LOY has little to attract you. But for the rest of us -- particularly those between 40 and 60 -- there are a lot of fun camps and a fair number of nice items. Plus lots of charm quests, odds are you'll grab everything required for at least one or two of the quests just regular hunting. You can start soloing in Gunthak at 40, easily -- though it's more fun with a group. And even the 'beach trash' are worth a bit of coin. ;)

The walkthrough on this site is awesome if you decide to do the Blood of Nadox quest (also called Pureblood). Good luck!
