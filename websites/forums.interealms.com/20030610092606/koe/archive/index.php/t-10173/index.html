Well, it depends on how badly (how deep) the scratches are. If they are very slight, you can get some nice CD polish and a soft cotton cloth and buff it repeatedly.

If the scratches are more severe, then you can try an EXTREMELY fine sandpaper and just sand the disk down a bit to get past the scracth. After you do that, get the CD Polish and buff the entire disk multiple times.
