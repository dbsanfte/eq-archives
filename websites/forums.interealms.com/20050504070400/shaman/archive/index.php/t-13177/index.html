weissblau
Before the first board wipe, someone had posted an excellent thread on the various efficiencies of various levels of cannibalization versus medding.

Their conclusion at the time (pre-horse, pre-KEI, pre-Torpor) was that

*) Chain Cann 1 + Regen is barely better than medding. The health and mana regen from sitting is worth about the same as the chain Cannibalizing.

*) Chain Cannidance + Regen is significantly better than medding and is pretty important at low levels.

*) As you level, the importance of the sitting health/mana regen decreases and the importance of the cannibalize regen increases.

Basically once you get Torpor/Cann IV, whether you are sitting or not is more or less irrelevant. Just one more Cann IV makes up for a lot of time on your rear end.

Oh, and a "hybrid" method - cannisitting - is halfway between dancing and chain cann. Basically you chain cann but sit down after the cann takes effect. This puts you sitting about 1/4 of the time, and when the tick occurs in this 1/4 you get the sitting benefit - but you don't have to watch the health bar for the tick update.

These days with horses, stoicism and buyable KEI, the rules are different. If you have a KEI, you don't really NEED to cannidance... you're still better off than a cannidancing shaman with no KEI. If you have a horse, the health benefit of sitting is gone but you keep the mana benefit, so you get half the benefits and don't have to dance. If you have Stoicism, you can probably regen the health so fast it doesn't matter (dunno, I never had that).

But even if you don't need it to be better than a cannidancing shaman... a cannidancing shaman with KEI is going to have better mana regen than a KEI'd shaman sitting on his or her fundament. So if mana is a problem you still need to use the most efficient way, and all this is still relevent.
