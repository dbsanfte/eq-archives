L51 shaman slow is desease based not magic based.

Just for a bit more clarification, 51 slow checks against magic but is cured via cure disease. This is a relatively recent change to the slow line, enabling players to cure slow off tanks without needing to annul all their beneficial buffs. Previously, turgurs required an annul to remove so you can see the benefit of being able to cure it with cure disease!

Don't feel badly, it's confusing to have a spell check against magic to land and then be curable via cure disease.

Turgur's is DEFINITELY magic based to land though, that's why malo/tash/bard song is so helpful in sticking it. Note that neither malo or tash have a component which lowers disease.
