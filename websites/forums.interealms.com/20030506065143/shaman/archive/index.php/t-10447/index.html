No, im not talking about bank area, or nathyns house.. im not sure if anybody else knows about this spot, never seen it posted, so i guess not many ppl know.

a couple days ago, me(57 shaman) and my 52 warrior friend were in Katta looking for some exp... all camps were taken, nathyns, bank area, gy.. so we started doing gargoyoles from the graveyard house for a while, they were easy but had high ac, so i started looking around for a place to hunt when i found this *aweome* exp spot!!

this is how you get there.. first.. go to the graveyard.. then walk out, and outside of the graveyard to your right there will be some stairs leading to this big house. this is the place.. at the stairs there is usually 2 leggionares that are too low lvl to give u good xp at 57.. but just outside the door there are 2 centurions.. those are all lvl54 mobs.. they hit for 157 max, have no resists (never got a resist on any spell exept for the odd JBB partial resist)..some summon, some dont ive noticed.. at the time i was just pl'ing my warrior friend but he was getting 3% xp per kill and we were doing it non stop.. at 57 sometimes i pulled 2 but im pretty sure at 58 you can single pull all of it.

so once you are done with the 2 entrance mobs, open the door and there will be a cent to your left and to your right.. further into the house by some stairs are 2 other cents.. when you go up the stairs there is one cent to each side..
i doubt you will be able to clear the entire house though, the repop time on them is extremely fast.. with me and my 52 war friend we were pulling and killing non-stop and we could keep about 5 clear before the first repoped.. also, you dont have to kill the lesser guards outside of the house becuase they dont help the cents.

there are also corporals in the house that are just as hard as cents and drop a 210 range throwing items which is nice to give to one of your monk friends :)
btw.. we pulled all cents to the graveyard.. if a undead popped and added i would just have my pet destroy them.

this is about all i have to say, if you guys have any questions let me know. im going back there tonight to see how much exp i get solo :)
