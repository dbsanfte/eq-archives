Hrm, I'll try and answer a little bit. Although you should note, I solo'd a good bit up to around 55, but havent solo'd one bit since then. You mileage may vary.

As far as mobs you should still be able to tank, there are some you should still be able to tank. Golems in the Grey, just about anything on the plateau in Scarlet Desert, toilers in maidens eye, certain mobs in skyfire. I can't remember which of the skyfire mobs really. You didn't ask for gear advice. And there is alot of people willing to answer gear questions if you actually wanted it. But I thought I'd throw a few out there to mebbe get you thinking about what you could do.

There are things you can do to upgrade your gear short of temple of veeshan. Some are expensive. Some are very expensive. Some you probably won't see solo, but if you plugged away at them in groups, you'd get them eventually. You might want to think about raising your hp's as well as ac. It's hard to raise both at the same time. But if you manage it, it's well worth it.

If you were floating in money, here are some things that you could buy for upgrades on ac and hp's

Imbued granite spaulders
The Seahorse cloak out of Siren's Grotto
Heirophants cloak
Buying the stuff to quest the Potc earring
Crystalline Spider Eyes
Coldain skin gloves
Cobalt Drake Belt
Spider Fur Belt
6ac/65 ring or shadel bandit ring

And of course, for spending some real cash, there is the new cultural player made chain armor. Very nice hp's on all peices. Decent ac on most of it.

That's a good chunk of money. I don't think most people have the cash for wholesale upgrades like that. But, mostly start small, and work your way up. Try to get what you can in xp groups, and pay for as little as possible :D
