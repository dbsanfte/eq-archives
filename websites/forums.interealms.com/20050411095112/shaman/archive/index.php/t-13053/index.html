/serverfilter works again.

When in huge raids, this is what I do
/shownames 1 -- this in itself seem to make a huge difference.

Then I open the options window and
Spell effects Low
Particle effects Low
I turn off other melee hits/misses
I turn off damage shields

If I'm still having a bit of a slow-down, then spell/particle effects go off, and I switch spells/songs/etc to group only

Outdoors in Luclin and -- I believe -- PoP and Ykesha, you can /showgrass off, and turn off some small graphical doodads on the ground. Like I said, I believe PoP and Ykesha have some of these "grass" objects in the outdoor areas. Haven't looked yet.

Sometimes the new UI (even the default UI) can load incorrectly upon zoning. This can cause your system to start slowing down further and further. If you find yourself with an abysmal framerate, try /loadskin YourSkinName 1
