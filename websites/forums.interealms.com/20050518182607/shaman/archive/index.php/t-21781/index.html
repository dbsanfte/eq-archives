Jaidin
I suggest a mage or necro. A necro would be more intensive since you would be casting a bit more than the mage but you would also have FD to pull with if need be.

On the other hand, mages and shaman compliment each other very well. The mages summoned items greatly help out the shaman and his pet while the shamans buffs greatly help the mage pet (and the necro pet though too).

I 3box a war, mage, and shaman and really appreciate what the mage brings to the combination. Also, a shaman and plate tank won't be doing a ton of damage so the fights may last longer, depending on what you are killing of course. In that case, the mage would get great use out of his damage shield. Of course, in a longer fight and a pally tanking, then a shaman and necro could really put their dots to good use also :)
