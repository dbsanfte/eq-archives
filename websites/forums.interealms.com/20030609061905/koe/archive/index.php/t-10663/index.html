Dearest Guild members and friends of the game,


Today Tannin Steelblade, born May 9th 1999, has been laid to rest in his home town of quenos. The Lord Protector Lewz Hopeblade granted his long time friend his final request to be laid to rest in the home where he came from.


Ok so i stink as a writer but what i have to say here comes from all honesty and love. As many of you really smart folk may have seen i have not been around in quite sometime. Game politics? nope,, the drama,, nay Human warriors not being voted the best looking warriors.. well maybe.. gah naa.. I have come to the point that everquest has offered me all that i wanted it to. Since the release of the planes of power i got to thinking .. ok when will it be enough. I sit in a chair and play something i love meanwhile i never see my family, smoke way to much, and get bigger and bigger .. i digress. Each of those issues can not be blamed on everquest but i feel there is more to life now that i am getting older. Hopefully marriage and children... ok scrap the kids but traveling back to the places i have been before would be great and spending more time with family is always good. Ok i am mumbling.

To Rizz I just want to thank you for giving me the pleasure of being a part of the most fun loving guild on the server. Being able to tackle somewhat big critters while still maintaining a democratic leadership is no easy feat. I have always respected the way to lead and was damn proud to follow you. ( oh and btw.. yes those girs up there were doing lap dances)

To Lewz, whom some of ya might have known as Gandahar before the evil euro maffia scared him into hideing and made him change his name. Thank you for always giving me hope that an everyday joe can get the really Phat lewtz if ya hang out in the market long enough :) But mostly for the great conversations we had about life, guitars, women, and smashing your head through a coffee table .. i still dont know how ya lived through that one. Salute my friend keep the tradition alive for me BUD.

And to Tari Without a doubt the kindest and most loving of anyone i have ever met in this game. The only thing greater than your playing ability is the the size of her heart. This girl has gave me a ear to talk to i cant count the times about all my woes and conserns and problems. I have gave it serious thought tari and this is the best move for me in my life. Please watch out over the others like you did with me.. hell you may end up accheving world peace the way your going.. i am gunna miss you hon no matter what illusion ya take.


There are so many good memories of my years with KoE and before that i can bring up, people i met and spent time with and cybered with,, i wont tell anyone wulf. Please dont think any less of me if i left you out of my closing remarks.. shoot a name at me and i will come up with something good i swear. Well .. then there is Hammerstalker.. ya still owe me for the superbowl game buddy :) I sent you that hooked on phonics number by number edition so ya learn what 3 points means hehe.. no hard feelings man. I know i might not share all peoples opinions but you and Toke and Aranuil were always good in my book.. you cats take care ok.

Klydon, Zelf, Jlove, Blumgan, Knarlz,, and the old Elite crew Bem, Mawa , Coatie, X box, the list goes on and on.. thank you for making the adventure the most pleasureable it could be. I have set out to do the things i wanted to do. And being part of Keepers was the most fun.

I have never been very good at this sorta thing.. but know now that this time is here .. it is final Tannins body was laid to rest at around 9:30 CST at the gates of quenos. In short i am leaving the body to rot. I have given Lewz my RBB to give back to the guild so you can use it wisely. the rest of my gear is on him for all to see. So if you have time and would like to maybe pay a final respect or 2 he is there .. deep down I really hope he leaves the world being thought of fondly. I tried to be a good player. Damn it Lewz i am starting to cry again. This is so friggin hard. Ya know what my favorite part of everquest was? It was that moment ya logged in and the full screen came up. It was my love opening up for me to an almost endless amount of possibilities. That moment .. that second you feel imortal to a certan extent. I am gunna miss that too.


I will leave ya some info incase any of you want to ever contact me and fill me in on what cool new things are happening..

Tannin@spacestar.net


I am going to miss you guys.. more so than ya think. Tannin will never be played by another rest asured of that .. well my tannin anyway.. well at least that name opened up for someone heh.. Which reminds me If anyone wants Blisto .. Email me i am sure a 56 druid in pretty damn good gear is usefull to someone. Let me know

Join swords forever guys, it was truely an honor serving together. Keep shooting for the stars and tearing them a new black hole. Good bye my family

Love always

Tannin Steelblade
