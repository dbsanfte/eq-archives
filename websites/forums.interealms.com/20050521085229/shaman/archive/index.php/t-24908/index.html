First to dispell some confusion that might be present: one man's hard is another man's normal. Literally. Basically there are tiers of LDoNs: one the game gives to groups with average levels X to Y, another for groups, average level Y to Z, etc. If you are in the X to Y range, and you pick hard, you instead get the Y to Z range adventure.

Back when level 65 was the max, SOE made a level 66-70 adventure, to be the level 65 hard adventure. Officially, they never made any new adventures, even after they raised the level cap, because they don't make any money adding content to an expansion you've already bought (well that last part isn't official, but you can read between the lines). However, a lot of people wanted higher LDoNs and >1450 pt equipment, so maybe they decided to compromise and give higher level LDoNs but no new equipment.

If you get a "normal" mission with a level 68+ average group, you might get an adventure that seems "hard" (because its the same one 65's got when they picked hard) but it should still be a "normal" in game terms. However, you guys are reporting that you got 100 or so points from these "normals." What I want to know is, is this because these higher level adventures give a lot more points than lower, or is it because the game is actually giving you hards and calling them normals?

Has anyone tried doing a hard LDoN now with a 68+ group. Do you get 200 points? If so, full set of incorpreal, focus IV effects, and 40 hp augs here I come :)
