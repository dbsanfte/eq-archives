I bought Bane, Anathema, Odium and Curse - the latter two just for the sake of it. I do sometimes use both Bane and Anathema, but mostly just use Bane.

It depends on the situation. If mobs last just long enough for Bane and/or Anathema to last 5 ticks, then I tend to keep Bane and Anathema up. If Bane is resisted, I can still do decent damage with Anathema, but the mobs die too quickly to make Blood of Saryrn worth casting.

If mobs are taking a little longer to die, then I use BoS over Anathema.

Note: If Blood of Saryrn only runs for 5 ticks (same duration as Anathema), or less, then Anathema is more efficient in terms of damage per mana. It's still only marginally more efficient than Velium Strike, but I prefer dots to nukes because I can be doing other things.

For PoFire kiting groups, I pile on the damage as many dots as I can (and have fun seeing if the druid can hold agro from me :)). Similarly for soloing, but without using dots with the DD component that can break root.
