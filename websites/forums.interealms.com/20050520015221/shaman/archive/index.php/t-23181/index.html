I haven't really pvped with my shaman since the patch, but I can tell you that with 250ish PR, I can land blood of saryn in about 1/7 tries. With 300+ MR I can land bane in 1/7 tries. I can get rooted quite often. I can get stunned some. In that aspect, I don't like it, but I can live with it.

On the other hand, there is no more insta-gibbing. 40% is max damage a caster can do to you, so I haven't died in any pvp against a wiz(haven't tried SK/necro)

I still can't kill bards. I doubt I could kill necros or sks. any other class I'm fine against, I think.

On the other hand, I saw a 65 rogue do a crit BS for 2400 in pvp the other day, I don't think I like that.

I like the changes. If you check Lucy you can see the resist mods on our spells. Seems like our dots were made less resistable, and with the 40% spell dmg cap its pretty hard to finish us off (for those who use the stoic line of regen spells).

My pvp strategy has changed from dispel and malise first so i can land SOMETHING, to just throwing all my dots at my victim then keeping myself alive while they rot.

Plus the AC reduction has benefitted us battle shamans. Sure melees can hit us harder, but pesky druids and mages can be seriously pummeled by a shaman with a good weapon and slam ability.
