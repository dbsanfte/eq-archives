Another small problem with the bazaar, is that a merchant can only have 30 items for sale. Just like NPC merchants.

BUT you can put up to 80 items in traders satchels, and set prices for them all. And all of these 80 items will be listed in people's searches. So it's quite likely that items you find by searching, but then can't find on the trader themself, are hidden by the fact that the trader is selling more than 30 items.

Presumably, if one of these traders sell an item, another one becomes available for sale, just like an NPC merchant.

Brog
One little trick I noticed and did. I was selling SoW potions (They sell very well). If you put a bunch in any slot, if they are in the top 30, they take up one slot for each potion. If they are below 30, people can not get to them to buy it.

What I would do was put one potion in slot number 30. It would still show, and it could be bought. Then, I would put all the rest in slots 31 on. As one was bought, a new one would move into its slot, and so on. That way, only 1 was shown, but they were all available to be bought.
