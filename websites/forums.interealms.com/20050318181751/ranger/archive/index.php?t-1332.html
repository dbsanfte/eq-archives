Well met Darriel! As others have indicated, 40 minutes per yellow is not that bad at all. The key to leveling in OT is your group. There are going to be LOTS of blue mobs out there, so you want to get a good fast group - prefereably one which can dual pull (have two pullers bringing in mobs at once). This is easy on the Ledge or the SW corner, or over by the Sarnak tower. If you do the Sarnak tower you can bring extra value by Harmony pulling tower.

Soloing can be nice if you can't find a group (try sabretooths in the SE corner), but your best experience will certainly come in groups. Try and replace any raw hide armour you have - at 36th level this should be pretty easy to do really. Maybe a Blood Stained Mantle? AC6 and +10 vs magic, which is always nice. If your really trying to get fast experience, find a static group and maybe hit Mistmoore or Kedge Keep - both zones have much better experience modifiers than OT, but you need to bring a group. Lower Guk also will have much better experience, and you may find a group easier there to be honest. If you try it, look for a Bedroom group (BR). Good luck my friend!
