eqphreak
I think Dire is pretty easy actually - I did it with myself (3 characters on two accounts). 52 druid, 54 enchanter, 44 shaman.

Basically just pull to zoneline with tash - tash him, run and zone, go back find him, tash him, run to zone etc until he is solo.

Log in shaman and cast malaise, slow (both landed first time) - log out shaman log in druid

Keep rooted with enchanter and cast dot with enchanter (really easy to keep him rooted)

Dot until dead with druid - log in shaman and loot.

So basically I did it with just two characters but shaman debuffs help alot.

He does have a lot of hit points so it was a long fight (15 mins approx), but fairly easy as long as he doesn't touch you.
