House
Between your current level and Level 24, things will be rough. The Level 19 spells are a joke. Even the upswing at 24 is short-lived, and you'll seem sort of weak until Level 34. At Level 39, you become much more powerful, and at Level 49 you ascend. Unfortunately, Shamans take a while to come into their own.

The best advice I can think of is to get into a dungeon group and level as quickly as possible, so that you can put these dark years behind you. Try to tank/melee as much as possible, to keep up your Defense skill (very important) and your weapon skills (somewhat important). Also, unless you have some great weapon, Piercing is generally pretty weak for melee output. I'd recommend 1H or 2H Blunt instead as your primary weapon, and that you devote some time each level to keeping Piercing at a reasonable skill level.
