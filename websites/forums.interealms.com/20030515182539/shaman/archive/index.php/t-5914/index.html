Aeshere
Yes, they have always stacked. They still do post patch. The patch did nothing for shaman except now 2 (or more) shaman can put the same dot (dot with the same name) on a mob, where as before only one of the same dot could hold on a mob at a time, but dots of the same line (diff name) would stick provided a dot with the same name wasnt already on the mob. Here are some examples of how it works now:

Example 1:
Shaman 1 can cast BoN on a mob
SHaman 2 can also cast BoN on a mob and both stick
Total Dots on mob: 2

Example 2:
Shaman 1 can cast BoN and Ebolt on a mob and both stick
Total Dots on mob: 2

Example 3:
Shaman 1 can cast BoN and Ebolt on a mob and both stick
Shaman 2 can also cast BoN and Ebolt on a mob and both stick
Total dots on mob: 4



In short, all shaman dots stack with each other now, regardless of name and line. The only exception is tring to cast multiples of the same dot on the same mob by one person (before the first one wears off). In this case, the 2nd one overwrites (refreshes) the first one you just cast:

Example 4:
Shaman 1 casts BoN twice in a row on a mob
Total Dots: 1 - the 2nd one overwrites the first one he cast

Example 5:
Shaman 1 casts BoN Twice in a row on a mob
Shaman 2 also casts BoN twice in a row on a mob
Total Dots: 2 - the 2nd one overwrites the first one each of them cast, respectively


Hope this helps
