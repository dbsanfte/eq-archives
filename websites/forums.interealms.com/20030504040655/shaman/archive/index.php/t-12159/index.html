Fayclopz
In the very beginning, the fury/rage line of spells would drain mana to provide the enhanced stats, but they haven't for a long time (Ever since I had them, and I've been playing for over 3 years..of course, I was a noob for a LONG time). Castersrealm kept the old info on their site for a long time so that's probably where you saw it.

So to answer your question, they are worth using, and they don't drain any mana other than the spell's mana cost. They don't last very long (maybe 10min), so I don't always have it on me. But if I'm pulling something tough I'll use it. They give nice AC having both an AGI component and direct AC. The only thing they don't stack with is avatar, at least of the spells I own. I'm not sure whether they stack with primal essence because I don't have the spell yet, but I'm thinking it doesn't stack. For a shaman with primal essence, I would say keep PE on over the rage buff, just to avoid the annoyance of having to cast it so often.
