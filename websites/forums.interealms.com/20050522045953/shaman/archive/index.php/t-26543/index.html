Tsuyoi
A few of the places I liked to hunt:

Droga: Easy place to hunt and decnet xp, plat and loot. Most of the items from here sell in the few kpp range, but also make for excellent tribute. Faction is nice also.

ChardokB: Once you get enough faction you can easily start hunting in ChardokB (Halls of Betrayal). This will be a hotspot starting next patch so will likely be very decent XP. Some good drops in here, with the FT2 neck going for 30kpp in the bazaar.

Bloodfields: I setup in the 2nd field (the one closer to RCoD) and kept pretty much all the wanderers and some of the static spawns down for hours at a time. Very good for Loyalist faction and named drops in the 10kpp area. CC is necessary since you will get more than one in camp sometimes.

Harbingers Spire: I was here forever getting the belt quest done for 2 characters. You can do very well in the 1st floor up to about level 68. Easy pulls, lots of easy mobs, and some decent drops. Just watchout for any of the named dogs. You'll need RGC or the RC aas to reliably take them down. For a bit more fun go upstairs to where the girplans are. Hit for 600 but once slowed your monk may be able to tank. Wouldn't recommend this until he is at least 68 though.

Plane of Mischief: This place is good for all your characters. Decent XP up to 70, easy mobs, decent plat, and great spell research drops. Fight the haflings on the castle wall and into the castle itself. Get a good map since this place has many traps and pitfalls. Your only zone out is a tree that will take you to CS, so have some way of gating on your monk. One major benefit is the page for RGC drops here which will get you at least 35k itself, or 45ish if you use it to create RGC.

Noble's Causeway: There are a few spots where you can do well here. Things can get ugly fast so need to be on top of your game. One place I liked at lower levels was the Pixt Trent camp. From the DS ZI head up the path to the 1st bridge on the left. Follow the trail to the 1st arch. There will be 2 mobs there, and a wanderer just beyond. The wanderer is the PH for the named. The path between the bridge and the 1st arch is safe as long as you aren't too near the arch.

The safest place for that trio is probably Grieg's End or the Deep, where mobs hit for close to nothing. ZI mobs in GE will be light blue to your level 70 Shaman, but they will dark blue deeper in, where they hit for 210ish (the horror!). The XP is so-so, but you can kill them fast. Poor-to-mediocre cash and gem loot and occasional minor named drops.

Droga should be decent XP around the King's castle where mobs are level 60ish and you shouldnt' have too much trouble from adds what with the Monk's lull and the Chanter's mez. Decent cash and good named drops.

But all that aside, I would really really advise getting your Monk better gear, a 2HB weapon and some defensive AAs before venturing to higher level zones. That gear is vintage 2002 or so.
