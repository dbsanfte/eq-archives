PDPAUOA - Please Don’t Post Answers Using Only Abbreviations.

I don’t mean to sound bitchy about this, honest, but it really has been a problem for me and I’m guessing for others as well. Sites like this are an invaluable resource. I can’t count the number of times I’ve had questions about things in game that could only be answered by more experienced players. And I am truly grateful that people have taken the time to answer those questions. But even the best advice in the world is useless if the person reading it has no idea what’s being said.

This is particularly true for people new to the game. One popular question is where to go: for the best loot, experience or whatever. It doesn’t help to tell someone to go to BB or EC if they have no idea what those letters stand for. After two years of playing I’ve gotten better at deciphering most location abbreviations but there are still a few zones I’ve never heard of and I still find myself replying to a post asking what zone was being referred to.

And zone names are nothing compared to weapon and armor names. At least there are a limited number of zones. To this day I’ll read posts about gear upgrades and have no idea what gear is being referred to. It might have been exactly the type of weapon or armor I was looking for, but with only the abbreviation to go by even a search of Allakhazam often proves fruitless.

As I said, I’m very grateful that people are taking the time answer questions. But it would be extremely helpful if whomever is making a post would refer to the place or item in question by its full name first and then use abbreviations.

Thanks.
