Seen a few questions on soloing at 56, so since that's where i'm at, i thought i'd post the places i've tried. Please feel free to add.

Jaggedpine Forest
What: Griffons (only the occasional dark blue at 56), giant anacondas and panthers (panthers will lower faction with jaggedpine folk, and animals i think)
Pros: Easy to get to, bank, supplies and druid port very close by. In wolf form, nothing except poachers, gnolls and pontamids will agro on you, all of which are static locations.
Cons: Trees are a PITA, can be hard to find a mob in peak time. Also i found judging positioning when fear kiting the anacondas somewhat difficult at times and would get the "you cannot see yout target.." message.

Plane of Justice
What: Festering Rats
Pros: Great XP, nice spot you can kite to and the mobs get stuck due to verants great pathing code.
Cons: Mobs here hit hard and have a ton of HPs, also get adds sometimes. is this dependent on lvl?

Timorous Deep
What: Raptors
Pros: Less crowded that other spots, raptors have low HPs and pathetic magic resistant.
Cons: Raptors have a HUGE agro range, means fairly likely to get adds. Fairly out of the way, hard to get to, and no easy zone if you get into trouble.

Plane of Disease
What: Diseased Rats (?)
Pros: Great XP
Cons: Even more HPs than the rats in PoJ, hit harder too.

Western Wastes
What: Velium hounds, Brontos and Mammoths
Pros: Relatively racant, decent loot, decent xp, can check for scout
Cons: Pain to get to, Pain to get out, can be painful to run into Ice Burrowers.
Other: Is a great place to get some exp while wasting time waiting for ToV raid to begin. Check the hunting guide out under the Guide thread.

Plane of Nightmares
What: Small bats, ravens and night stalkers are all soloable
Pros: Great exp, ok loot, easy to bum a rez if you mess up
Cons: somewhat crowded, mobs can tickle if you loose control, large agro radius
