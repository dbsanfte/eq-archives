Defense is a friggin bitch to raise. I started out lvl 46 with 165 in defense, with the intent to max out before I hit 49, and get piercing at least to 160. JBB/REIF in hand I went to Bloodgills. I split my exp time 50/50. 50% of the time I JBB blased while the bg's beat on me unslowed. 50% of the time I hasted myself, and melee'd the mobs down, unslowed. End result? 1 yellow from 49, 200 defense, 175 piercing. Took quite a while to do this since exp'n is much slower when you're meleeing or jbb'n unslowed mobs, but it was well worth it. Now back to real levelling.

The moral of the story, it might take a while, but beat on mobs unslowed til you max your defense. Then level normally. Everytime you ding go back to meleeing to keep piercing/defense maxed. You'll thank me later. I wish I had started doing this when I was lvl 25, instead of not meleeing much after 25, root/dotting, and grouping til 40'ish. Have fun. Shamans ROCK =)

Chutoi
As for raising defense, AGI buffs are key. You can cast one on yourself, or perhaps talk a high level shaman into helping you out with a talisman if the cat (you'll have to be grouped and he'll have to cast it on himself) or an Agility spell. I'm not sure what your current AGI is, but it factors into defense increases. If you can get it over 125, it'll help a lot.

As stated, the best method is via a pet of a friend, so they can back it off and not kill you. However, it can be done reasonably safely with mobs too. Check the beastiary at allakhazam.com and find some mobs that are just barely dark blue to you, and go play with them. Drag it to a zone, and keep root handy. Root is very fast casting, so you can root, back off, heal yourself, and then step back in. Have SoW up for quick escapes. Burn a couple of mana bars that way, and you'll get your defense up quickly.

When soloing, always keep AGI up for defense increases. It'll help a lot.
