KEI mixed with Bear Form (has regen)/Chloro (or whatever)/SLN (has regen component) = almost zero downtime = EQ that doesn't suck (which DAoC was supposed to be but I digress).

PoP made travel trivial (I always thought the Firepots did too but alas I was one of those that didn't get the chance to bind in TD before they made it unbindable - which, if you don't know, is bindable again).

They have made many improvements to the game that are clearly geared towards making the game more fun. I hardly ever see crowds anymore (cept maybe the bazaar, Ssraeshza Temple, Nexus or PoP bank) as the world is so big now that we are no longer cramed into LGuk or SolB (how's that for old school). They have added so many new and improved equipment items that you can't possibly keep up with it thereby flooding the game. That makes it almost meaningless to farm (cept for a small percentage of super uber items) which means loot whores, camp holders, lists for camps, fighting for camps, camps in general, fighting for drops, are mostly a thing of the past.

In fact, the area of the world per capita is so big, you pretty much have to bring your own group to many of the places that used to be overly camped. Finally, with Paludal Caverns and its insane xp bonus, you can get from 1 to 25+ in days, so starting a new toon is also trivial.

They have addressed all the issues I had with the game. There just isn't any reason left to NOT play.
