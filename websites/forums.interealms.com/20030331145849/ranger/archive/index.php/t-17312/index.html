Braslo
The final combines to make the actual weapons (Dark Courage and Black Insanity) are both no-fail.

The combines to make the Drunder's Steel, Blue Vial, and Black Vial have trivials associated with them. These 3 items are tradable, but some of the pieces to make them are no drop and/or lore (some both). So you have to make sure the people with the right skills loot the right pieces, then do the incremental combines and hand out the final items to the melees for the actual combine to weapon form.
