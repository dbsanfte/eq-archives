Sarania
The damn ghosts are annoying as hell. i usually find it best to kill them all at the T before you hit the domesticated pens. Usually if you kill them there you should make it through the castle to the other zout before the ghosts respawn.Otherwise you end up getting chain adds from these guys. I reccomend you do the ring and range quests both. The turn in guy for the range is not far into the castle (straight to the T then left). the staffs usually drop between zin and castle. the gems mainly drop in the castle and the area just past the castle if you dont enter. The pages all seem to drop off Chokidai. I found the domesticated were the best for drops. The turn in guy is in the room to the right after you exit the back of the castle. BTW the Rabid Chokidai can drop torpor and can spawn anywhere but inside the castle. I got my copy when it spawned as the first mob from zin lol.
