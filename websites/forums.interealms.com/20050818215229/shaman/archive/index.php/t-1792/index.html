The Avatar of War showed up in FV to launch an attack on the outpost.

From what I could tell, the event was very confusing to the players. First of all, AoW did shouts asking the players who they were going to side with. Many evils and Zek worshippers chose to side with the giants and were going to help slaughter the Tunarian citizens.

Well, the way it worked out was that the Avatar of War (after going LD) summoned some forest giants that just stood around and were killed by the players. I don't think they dropped anything. There was a GM controlled elf commander who was defending the outpost also.

Oh, there were over 350 people in FV. You couldn't move. And for some reason, all of the following seemed to be disabled somehow:

- spellcasting
- sitting/meditating/looking in your spellbook
- zoning
- gating
- clicking on any buttons in the user interface

And no, it wasn't due to lag, although that would have explained it. It was very bizarre to say the least and really a very poor event. After the giants were killed, the commander thanked us for defending the outpost (even though half of the players in the zone were trying to side with AoW) and that was about it.
