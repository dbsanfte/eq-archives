5kpp isn't a lot of money to outfit a shaman. I've probably spent around 185kpp just on my basic gear and I'm not even close to considering myself a good twink but then again, I play on a server where prices are higher then other servers. Prexus has really cheap prices right now because of the 2 rollbacks. Characters that were once deleted, appeared on the account again with all their equipment so the market is flooded with cheap gear. Anyway, keep in mind, the shaman is very gear dependant because they melee. You can get by with 5k but it'll be a tough road.

As far as to which race to play, I chose a frog. I like how they do a flip when they cast a spell and they can use a jbb. I think barbs are ugly but that's just me. The bottom line is play what you like. It's a long game and you might as well enjoy your character. All stats can be overcome eventually.
