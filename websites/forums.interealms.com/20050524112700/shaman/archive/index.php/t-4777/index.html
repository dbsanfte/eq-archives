Kriel Darkbane
Runspeed Runspeed Runspeed.

Those should be the first 3 aa points anyone gets, in any class. My main is a bard, with even better self-runspeed enhancement than a shaman. And his first 3 points were runspeed, and they're still the best 3 points I've spent, even after starting to buy class-only skills.

Runspeed works indoors. Sure, SoW works outdoors, but if you've been in a dungeon for 30 minutes it's gone. Runspeed isn't. Outrunning the train you're pulling to the zoneline is a wonderful thing. Being able to keep up with your friends who all have runspeed 3 is a wonderful thing. The inta-SoW boots do reduce its value outdoors, but let's face it. An awful lot of high level people already had Jboots too, and they all still bought runspeed for indoor use as well.

Most importantly, the first 6 aa points can only be spent on basic skills. Far and away the best choices are runspeed 3 and regen 3. The other basic AA skills are fairly pointless, with very little effect. What would plus wisdom (or any other stat) do? Virtually nothing. Each point of wisdom is what, maybe 12 extra on his maximum mana at L 60... assuming he's not capped out on wisdom, which he someday will. Runspeed is good forever, the extra wisdom is almost meaningless.
