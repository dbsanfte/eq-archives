M. B'fang
To make this a might bit easier, you can invis past most myconid mobs you'll encounter enroute from the illis jail to the jugg/reet area. IIRC, only myconid adepts saw through invis, shaving much off your travel time.

In my experience only the wandering juggs see invis, however it may be completely random. The first two juggs you encounter (static spawns, guarding the archway) you should try invising past. Careful if the roamer is in the area behind them, it will see through and aggro the other two.

This leads you to the area marked "3" on the map provided by Mojo. Inside are a total of six juggernauts - four static and two roaming, along with a total of four reets (two in each alcove). Of the roaming juggs, one moves between alcoves (left to right), while the other wanders out toward where you enter. Best bet is to ambush the latter jugg as it wanders outside room "3". Invis up and head into "3".

Directly across from where you entered "3", you will notice a wall. This is a false wall, and beyond it paths several juggs that see invis. What you want to do is position yourself in "3" in such a way that when you toy with camera angles you can see just beyond the false wall. When no juggs are present behind it, and the remaining roaming jugg in "3" has pathed into one of the two alcoves, run yourself through the false wall into the either left or right corridor. Remain there and kill the wandering juggs from that area (will be approx 3-4).

Once the juggs are downed, you're free to break the reet spawns which are housed in the three alcoves beyond the area marked with a "3". Please note: when running past the false wall from "3" bewteen reet spawn locations, hug the bend (you'll see when you get there) otherwise, you'll have the two static juggs guarding the false wall in "3" aggro.

Froglok reets are comprised of three classes: warrior, shaman, and wizard. The shaman and wizard reets will gate when low on health and start summoning. Just be prepared.

Good luck!
