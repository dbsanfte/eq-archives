Since magelo seems to be down, I can't examine your gear/AAs to see what you can or can't solo. I would say, if you've got about 2200 hp or more self buffed, and canni iv, then you could solo the emperor in sebilis(who drops lvl 60 spells) Getting down there would be the trick, if you look at my post in this same forum(60-61 solo challenges) you can see how I get down there solo. Emperor is on a 28 minute spawn, iirc, as are all the mobs in the area. All you typically have to clear is the 2 mobs outside the BoC room, the BoC(or PH), and the emperor. You can spread them out to make soloing them easy, and have time to afk and grab a bite or read a couple pages of a book in between. Torpor is nice for emperor, but won't be necessary. If you have stoic, the better for you. Kragg's mending will be your friend here without those 2 spells.

You could also try skyfire(boring and SUX exp) for your 56+ spells. Named mobs drop 56-58 spells(rarely) and guardian wurms drop lvl 59/60 spells(but bring along a tank class, as you will probably die without torpor.)
Another good source of 56-58 spells would be juggs in sebillis. They commonly drop those spells, but I would bring along a couple people at 61 to help you.. possibly even a full group.

As silly as it sounds, farm spiderlings, spiders, and lions, cats, and bears for silks and pelts. That's probably your fastest solo moneymaker, on the order of several kpp per hour if you do it right.

The EP capable guilds have players powerskilling their tradeskills up in order to finish quests and be included on rolls for specific tradeskill related drops (to make things like Bristlebane Party Platters). Most of these players can farm cash or items very rapidly. They don't care how much it costs (well, much :p ) to skill up, and they are paying insane prices for these mundane items that a level 10 player could farm in safety.

Summon dogdog, find an empty zone w/ these mobs and commence slaughtering. /pet attack 2 or 3 mobs in a row (he'll automatically go to the next mob you ordered him to when the first one is dead) and then kill some others with Blast of Venom, Shock of Venom, or group a few together and use Gale of Poison.
