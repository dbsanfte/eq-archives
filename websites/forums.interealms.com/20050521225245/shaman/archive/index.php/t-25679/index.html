Binter
Soloing: The main issue with Sol Ro that makes soloing difficult, Psi, is the fact that Sol Ro mobs had the biggest agro radius I've ever seen. In most cases, you can't even get into casting range before they will agro on you. And mob placement means that from the zone in, it's virtually impossible to get single pulls outside of the roamer. To get anywhere in Sol ro, you have to be able to handle 2 mobs at a time, which are slowable but unrootable.

Soloing in Sol Ro is hard, but not impossible...but it takes gear.

Duoing: see above.

Grouping: A group of 65's would best be served on the upper levels, where the ornate drops more frequently. As far as I know, there are no named spawns anywhere in Sol Ro outside of the 5 minis, the nameds along the paths to those minis put there as part of the effort to reach the mini, and Solusek Ro himself. Another option is to click up to one of the mini areas and work your way towards a mini. This requires a very good puller, because in many instances you can bring a huge train. Ornate drop rates are even higher, and some nameds on the way to a mini can drop loot (but not all), but be mindful that all nameds in the mini areas would be quite a handful for a group of 65s. They're pretty tough.

Above all, just have fun exploring. Sol Ro Tower is one of my favorite zones, probably because it IS quite challenging. The unique aspects of the mobs make this one of the more interesting places to xp, and you will certainly be kept on your toes. ;

And as a reminder, remember to tell your groupmates, THERE IS NO ZONE OUT IN SOL RO. Gate or port are your only options, unless you kill Sol Ro himself and use his zone-out to zone into PoFire. ;)

Making sure all your melees have a gate potion as an emergency backup would be wise.
