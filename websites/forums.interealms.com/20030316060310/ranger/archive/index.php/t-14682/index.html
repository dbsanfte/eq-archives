Nulian
To get through siren's is actually pretty trivial.

Once you zone in, invis up and and cross the pool to the right-side ledge. From there, swim across to the left-side ledge.

Put on sneak, and drop down directly above the two seahorses guarding the underwater tunnel. If you do it right, they won't agro you.

Once you're a safe distance past them, get rid of sneak and swim past the seahorse in the middle of the tunnel - it's possible to sneak past him, but I generally don't bother. It tends to be hit or miss, anyway.

From there, swim up the shaft and down the waterfall. Re-invis in the little alcove on the left and run to the WW zone.

Getting back is even easier, just swim over the waterfall back to the CS zone.

There was a post on how to do this some time ago, do a search and you might find it - had detailed screenshots of the route.
