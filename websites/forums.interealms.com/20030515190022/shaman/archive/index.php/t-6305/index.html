Basically, the point I am trying to make is that the JBB / horse combo makes the REIF unnecessary. So my recommendation of things to want in order are:

1. JBB
2. Horse
3. ODS

In your situation where you group indoors a lot this may not work, but keep in mind that if you buy a horse now and then next week you see a deal on a JBB, you can quickly sell the horse and get 7K back immediately. Then save up and replace the horse again. I'd rather have a horse for the interim than have that 8k sitting in the bank doing nothing while I waited to find a JBB.

So the order of things to buy might be more like:
1. Horse
2. JBB
3. ODS

But keep in mind, I am a soloer almost exclusively, since my small family guild just disbanded. I am a casual player, so I will take a while to get to 60 and need to buy expensive spells.

Your mileage may vary.
