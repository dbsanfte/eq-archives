From the sticky right above you:


3. Reputation
This system is a fun feature of the board, but has little practical use. Since the question "What are the green dots for" seems to come up quite a bit, we will address it here.

A poster's reputation is denoted by the number of small green squares next to your post count. Reputation is earned and lost based on votes given by other users.

If you see a person has posted a particularly helpful or insightful reply, you can click the talking head by the REPLY button on that post and give the poster a positive reputation boost. If they have posted something inappropriate you can give them negative reputation. Just because you disagree is not a good reason to give a negative reputation bump.

People with high reputations give bigger positive/negative boosts when giving reputation to others.
