Bakkal,
Your comment, "What reason exactly would there be to make up the information?", sounded a point ppl should understand regarding information (msg boards are the next generation of national enquirer). Some ppl take a perverse pleasure in outright posting BS information they know isn't true, don't ask me why some ppl are just that way. The next category are those of us who share "our" experiences regarding the situation at hand, from these experiences a lot of confusion may result as the information changes as it gets passed along and some eventually take it as a truth. Finally you have those who spend a LOT of time testing theories to see what actually works. The number of ppl who actually to the last and do it correctly are pretty small. That said, trying to decipher if the person really has a clue or just gets off on posting crap to confuse ppl. Most the ppl here try to be truly helpful but all we can share is our experiences and how we did things. The timing and conditions for each of our encounters will probably vary and thus skew each of our perspectives. Don't accept most of the information as absolute fact (the slow chart is pretty damn good though). Take the information presented and see how if fits to you and try out some of the stuff to see what works for you.
