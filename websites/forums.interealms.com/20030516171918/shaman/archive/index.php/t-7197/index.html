yes, catching the golems up (and if possible CT not up to reduce chain aggro) is the big trick. My suggestion - keep checking who's in fear (/ all cleric 60 65 works well), then ask them if they killed the golems. Three days later the golems will be back up, and you can lead your friends there.
Fright does not have an AoE, but does have a DT on a 45 sec reclyle. This, and his screwy pathing, makes him tough to pull. We usually send in a sacrifice (the shaman that wants the tear) and then pull him to a group along the south wall. Just have at least 3 MT to take him down, they will most likely die due to the deathtouch (so I usually get the 3rd for slowing him).
Terror can be treated like a dragon with an AoE, its cold based and I haven't resisted it yet.
