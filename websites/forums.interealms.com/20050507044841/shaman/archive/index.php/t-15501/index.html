weissblau
This is a fun fight for a single group. Best pull spot - invis up and run straight down the hall from ZI, through the room with the fallen column and into the next hall, straight into the next room (avoid the rubble on the floor, traps) and once past the rubble break hard LEFT and go into the little alcove there. There is a Shrouded Anisher in there; kill him. His respawn is like 20 min. There are no wanderers in that area, you can pull all the rocks you want. Rocks drop A Lucid Shard btw, keep one if you ever want to get into Vex Thal.

Va'Dyn has a lot of HP, like the Rubble Rousers, but he hits a LOT harder than the RR's do and rampages. Get slow in quickly and follow up with a cripple. I didn't have any trouble landing slow after Malo at level 64.
