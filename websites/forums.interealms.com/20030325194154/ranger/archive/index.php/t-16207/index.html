magimix
The only problem I found with the Mammoths in EF (to echo a previous comment) is that they are mobs that become fully exploited very quickly. Basically, a single L30 Ranger (for example) could very easily hog all the Mammoths in the zone - they don't spawn very quickly. Therefore, if you go into a zone and there is already a tracking class hunting them, it may be hard to get a look in.

They spawn all over the class, and trackers 'own' them :-)

However, the loot is great. I levelled from 30-32 in EF, and made about 1000PP in the process - all from Tusks. Also, even at L30 odd, the XP was *very* good indeed for the degree of risk involved (basically zero risk, assuming you know how to manage any adds to stumble across you when fighting).
