Goldark
Levels >> AA. Easier to find groups, faster xp, access to higher level zones, less resists on slows, more resists on spells cast at you, better spell line-up. Its all good apart from some easier mobs giving less xp as they turn light blue, but that's more than balanced out by the xp from higher tiers.

Canni V is a bucketfull of AA points to get from scratch. If you're like Haleice and have MC3 already then sure, go canni V first, but in your position I would run for 65. Quiessence is amazingly good, and you get new DoTs which are a big improvement for soloing.

@Haleice - Root-rot might sound druidish, but to give you some idea, in tactics I get about 5-6% aa per kill, takes maybe 7 minutes (I don't have bane, which would really knock that right down) per kill. Add in a minute between each mob for pulling, rebuffing etc and you're talking 5-6% every 8 minutes. The bonus is that you're getting SPs, piglet drops and the odd ornate pattern too. If you don't have tactics access, there are a number of thread on PoV golems which suggest you can get decent xp soloing at 65 there too.

Mind you, get a decent group and you're talking an AA in 45 mins to an hour with a group of 65s in BoT or better yet Tactics. Soloing is no longer the way to go, but if you can't get a group you can still solo for xp at 65.
