Grafsha McDaughts
To Dulak Harbor with you!! Duo'd with a druid go for Dandolak or Lady Dru boats. Dandolak will be good for a few levels, Lady D will start light blueing out at 55, if not 54. Suggest Lady D first though to give you a feel for the pulls, etc ... both boats are pretty much the same layout and pull strategies.

Did both with a druid and we found it nice to clear a couple lower deck mobs then let druid kite 4 mobs. While druid medded, I killed upper deck pulling singles. Was easy xp once routine was established, and not bad loot either.

Other option would be Nadox at the Turg Mine zone (zone into Nadox and lessor succor across.) Pull mobs from bridge and work into temple/room. Initiates are casters so they can be a pain, but all on bridge are melee types (casters in temple.)

These are easy, steady xp spots up to 57 when they drop off lots ... but by then you are ready to duo plane zones.
