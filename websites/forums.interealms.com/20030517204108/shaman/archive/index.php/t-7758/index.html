mool
Whelp I finally got to finish this tonight myself (12/15).

It was a real nail biter :p

My observations:

1) DISPELL THAT DAMAGE SHIELD - we didn't notice this until well into the fight but he had a 30pt damage shield up that was ripping the pets apart (I certainly could have missed it but I don't recall reading anything about a damage shield on him in any of the other posts on the subject).

2) definitely pull him to the left side arches... we tried fighting right in front of his door but got adds because the fight took too long. Unless you have a number of good tanks/pets, expect a good long fight.

3) He gates so start stunning him when he gets low (although if you do just go ahead and fight him in his room this isn't that big of a deal but then you have all the adds to play with during the fight).

4) Mana sieving/theft of thought does appear to help... near the end of the fight he just completely quit casting apparently out of mana. If we had started that earlier it probably would have made the whole thing easier.

5) I had about 150 FR/CR and was able to survive a couple of AE hits.... ice comet polished me off while pulling him on first attempt though.

6) He's pretty damn resistant. Tash landed but not much else (tried to mark of karn him and some other goodies). I do believe mana sieve and theft of thought were landing though but forgot to ask to confirm.

7) Just go ahead and plan on lots of adds including the possibility of reavers (especially if he gates). Fortunately the adds are mostly pretty easy.

Lastly, just a goober tip but if you can do the raid thing, do it so if you get the kill but get overwhelmed with reaver adds you have plenty of time to clear them or escape and get back to the dead rak for your scale (since corpses last longer when killed by a raided group than a normal group).
