galelor
you are going to want to check his AAs and see what he has. pallies get a few activatable AAs such as a new stun, 2 hand bash, RC, and a few others.

when i box my pally and shaman i drop the FoA/FoA stuns with cease or a QWoS(if i know i well need to postion a mob) and while the mob is standing there stupid and getting beaten on i malos/slow. (my pally doesn't have AAs.)

don't use push stuns in XP groups unless you need to get the mob out of the wall. remember, the mob goes the way the pally is looking when the knockback hits.

i have found the only way to run a pally out of mana is to group heal a lot...group heals tend to be nice agro increasers too.

good pally holds agro and tanks while keeping the mob in position for the other melee classes (imo). if you are playing a raiding pally you will have a few more things to think about =p

fyi- virtue+AoTC+focus+stam+bss are prolly the raid buffs you want, pallies don't use druid buffs because they lose HP/AC and don't really need the extra mana regen from Nine.

that was my quick crash course in pallies =p
Beafly
From this shaman's point of view, (and my first main was a Paladin now effectivly retired) you need to go ask the paladins. Paladins of Norrath (http://p202.ezboard.com/bpaladinsofnorrath) (PoN) should be your new home for quite awhile. Consider that any time you join a group as a level 65 Paladin there are skills and reactions that everyone will just plain expect. You need to cram 65 levels of skills into your brain and muscle memory. PoN is an awesome resource. Pay attention to the stickies, use your ignore rant skill (which if you read this board should be maxed anyways), look for posts by Romidar they are usually very well thought out even if I don't always agree.

My tips? DA always memed. Heal your group not yourself. Master stun agro, learn when to use DD component stuns and knockback stuns and when not to. Become friends with your chanter. Practice holding agro on multiple mobs at the same time. Don't expect to do damage and don't get excited if you do, it won't last long.

There is tons of other stuff to know... think about how long it took you to learn to play your shaman effectively.

But hey, It should be fun! Enjoy.
