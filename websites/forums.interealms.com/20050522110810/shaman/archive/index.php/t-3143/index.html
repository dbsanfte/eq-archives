ssizzor
Well, I just got my totemic helm yesterday, after about 2 hours of work...

Make a human ranger, get him to level 5 (takes an hour or so), dump all his points in tracking, get him to the cave in Everfrost (drop an enduring breath potion for him if you need), park him in the middle of the cave (in an alcove away from the wanderers), and camp.

Then just come back occasionally to check the spawns...

I got lucky and got him five hours later or so, but even so... shouldn't waste much of your time..
