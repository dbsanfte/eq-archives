Hiho,

well, Hole raids stand and fall with your knowledge of the zone. Especially if you go down there with such a force.


1. Be sure to have a good MA. Very important. Try to get the highest Warrior you can, it helps a lot.
2. Get a crazy mezzer. Maybe 2 that work in a team together. Trust me, you will get adds in the city, cause the pathing is unpredictable.
3. Get a puller that knows the zone inside out. Respawn is the enemy out in the city, and you must pull quick, kill, and move to the next spot to go on.
4. Make a halfling rogue (worshipping Brell), move him there, bind, sow and explore the zone. Most mobs will not be KOS, if you run into one rinse repeat. Know the place.
5. Keep your raid force on its toes. No slacking while this move through the city. No afk for 5 mins. This may kill the whole raid. Stay focused for 30 mins and you will make it.
6. Do all the other epic parts till this one, otherwise you might not have enough true spirit faction for the turn in of the icon. It is not too bad to lose the icon because you can get a new one quite easily, but its hard to get another raid into the Hole organized, so spare yourself the trouble and do the rest of the quest beforehand.
7. Search for things like "the hole", "kirn", "epic" on this board and you will find many more useful information about it.

So long
