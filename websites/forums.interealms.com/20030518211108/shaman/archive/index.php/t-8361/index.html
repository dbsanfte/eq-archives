Hail,

Sorry if this disappoints you, but I think of your little Mootogo Focus Staff as nothing but a Happy Meal toy for a shaman. As in, a boobie prize that would make you smile if you looted it, but not something you are going to actively seek in the /bazaar.

The ratio isn't anything to shout about. The 5% channel I really don't see as being something to spend money over, since shaman should already have a high Channeling percentage. The svp/svd resists are good, and that coupled with the 8wisdom are where most of the item value is derived. If it had 8 STA +25hp as well, it would be worth more.

You didn't post all the details about the item, such as which classes/races can use it, so I'm basing my PC purely from a shaman perspective.

Speaking generously , I would say that staff is worth 500pp-1kpp. Probably less.

In comparison, a Tash stick regularly sells for about that much on my server last time I checked. That's a USEFUL situational toy for soloing shaman. The Togor's stick is also a USEFUL blunt that I'd possibly purchase for that amount (maybe more).

This is a midrange blunt weapon for pre-epic shaman in my opinion. I'm sorry if that's bad news for you. YMMV.
