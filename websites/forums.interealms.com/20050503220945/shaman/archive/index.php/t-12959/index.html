Very polite request, bonus points as far as I'm concerned. Ask any questions you have, but first I'll give you some basic info I have from my experiences. I have one RL friend who plays a 58 bard, and I have two good in-game bard friends, 60 and 61. I've found bard's work wonderfully in concert (pun intended) with a shaman to maintain crowd control in situations where even good enchanter's get overwhelmed (ahh, the memories of our warrior pulling trains to us in Sebilis ...). Between mez, slow, fear, and charm, Bard's have a lot to offer in terms of saving me mana on healing, which is nice because usually when I'm playing with bards I wind up beig the only healer. Not that this is typically a problem, because of everything I already mentioned, plus mana/health regen songs.

In PoP groups and such I often have the luxury of having a cleric and a bard, and once in a blue moon an enchanter to boot. In these cases I go for DPS mostly, though having a full party keeps me using a lot of mana for buffs. Of course I usually remain the primary slower/maloer, because shaman slows are UBER! I imagine (I'm usually to busy to pay attention to songs in these groups, so I don't remember for sure) that the bard's in these situations twist more towards DPS, although I'm almost certain that they keep haste up also, which in its own way adds a large amount of DPS, in a full group with several melees.

So there's what I know off the top of my head on shaman/bard combos. Its not as good as the venerable shaman/monk or warrior/cleric (or /sigh enchanter/cleric) combos, but still a really good one.

One last thing: at one point, back before Verant nerfed the bug that made it so only 4 mobs could attack a person at once, some innovative shaman here on the Crucible pioneered a brilliant method for exping with just a bard/shaman duo (60/60) in Fungus Grove (and elsewhere). The shaman would pull a patch (4) mobs with AE slow (50% attack reduction, only affects 4 targets max, we get it, if we have access to it that is, at 58). Then the bard would steal agro with AE damage songs. The shaman would run off to pull more mobs, and the bard would tank the first 4. The shaman would keep coming back with more patches, and each patch would get taken by the bard with his AE songs. However, since only four could attack him at once (even though all took damage) a single shaman with Torpor could keep the Bard alive by casting it between pulls. Using this method the enterprising duo could get at their best upwards of an AAXP every 20 minutes, if I remember correctly.

So, good luck in your quest for knowledge, let me know if I can help in any way :)
