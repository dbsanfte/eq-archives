Giac
Don't ask for static melee debuffs. If Shamans had a debuff to lower the target's dps by a set percentage, you'd have the Slow fiasco all over again and the class and it's future abilities woulod be balanced around it.

If they want to give Shamans a useful debuff it would make sense to lower the hit rate of the target by X % of the target's hit percentage.

This would result in a lower damage output of the target, but it wouldn't be as concrete as a slow since the RNG is streaky. Sometimes the mob would hit 30% of the time and others just 5%.

This would add some uniqueness to the class and together with mitigated slows it would reinstate us as the kings of melee debuffers.

However, healing would still need to be revised for all 3 priest classes. That's if they intend to keep EQ a popular and interesting game.

I think one of the problems with the shaman is not that we don't have good spells/abilities or that we don't have clear roles to fill in groups etc. The problem is more that some of our abilities and roles have been trivialized.

1) For elemental geared folks, are buffs aren't critical:

Fo7

Most people will want this because any extra HP is a good thing. It helps that it stack with most other HP buffs, but the Str and Dex are unimportant

FA

Its usefulness is restricted by a short duration and long recast time. But for the most part this is solid

Talis of Diaku
Talis of Boar
Talis of Wrulan
Talisman of Tribunal
Ancestral Guard

With the exception of Talisman of Tribunal, these are considered useless buff slots wasters by most folks and Talisman of Tribunal isn't regarded too highly by those who's resists are where they should be ;)

I'd make some simple changes to solve these problems:

a) Add a new HP buff along the lines of Talisman of Kragg that buffs approx 900 hp and stacks the same as Kragg.
b) Add a group FA
c) Add an Endurance regen buff

2) Our debuffs need an upgrade:

Slow is fine as is, mitigation and all.
Cripple needs an upgrade
Malo line is fine as is.

3) Heals - in theory I like the changes they've made

4) DPS - no idea....
