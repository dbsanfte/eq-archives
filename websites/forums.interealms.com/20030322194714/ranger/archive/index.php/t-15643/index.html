Aethien
I've heard it said, and I tend to agree that MR or other resists > all. Always sacrifice AC for better resists, if possible. Snares and Roots, Flash of Light, etc. are the things that will get you killed more quickly than not having enough AC. Can't move or see, you won't be lasting long as a melee. That's why the DRT is a nice little item for lowbie Elves like us.

And don't ignore DIS and POI resists, too.

Basically, you should have all of your resists as high as possible. 100 is a good minimum, pre-buff number to shoot for, but that's a long way off.
