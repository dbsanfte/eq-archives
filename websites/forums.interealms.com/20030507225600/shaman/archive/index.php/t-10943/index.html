Most shaman clicky items don't take effect till your 40+. I know the JB stuff is like that and thurg armor you will not be able to get till your 45 or so. You have to be warmly or above to coldian for those quests. Personally the legs, boots, and chest are the only pieces worth getting, not sure on the bracers and helm though. CC arms work better for me, along with leaf scale gloves. I'm 54 now and have yet to wear any JB armor. If your looking for good stat items i recommend Arctic wyvern wristbands and gorgot or a large smoothmetal torqe. Metal Linked crown is good too but i think it has a level requirement. Just look around, good place to ask whats in for shaman fashion is here. So keep posting. =)
