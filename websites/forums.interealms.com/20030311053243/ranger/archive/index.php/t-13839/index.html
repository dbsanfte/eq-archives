No one can really answer that question exactly, since no one really knows how much actual experience it takes to gain a point. (I’m sure EQhackers may have something to say about it)

But two things already stated.

One, the amount of experience needed to gain 1 AA point is a constant. It doesn’t ever change. Gaining your first AA point takes as much exp to get as does gaining your 100th AA point. Also, gaining a single AA point at lvl 51 takes as much exp as gaining a single AA point at lvl 60.

And Two, the amount of experience needed to gain 1 AA point is actually a little bit less then the amount of experience needed to complete level 51. I believe if you used a 50/50 split, starting from the beginning of level 51 you would gain your first AA point and still need something on the order of half a yellow bubble to finish out lvl 51. Maybe not exactly, but it’s close to that.
