I'm personally not to stratholme yet but from my beta experience and my guildies I know a fair amount about it.

It's true that you need a very competent group to succeed in stratholme, usually a raid is formed of abut half a dozen to 8 people. Yes you need priests and mages, shaman are nice and so is a well equipped warrior, but that doesn't mean other classes are included. You also need damage, and rogues are great at providing it.

That all said I would not set my hopes on completing the smith quest. Like said before it takes a competent group, which means pickup groups are a bad, bad idea. Loot disputes make them even worse of an idea. You'll need to wait until your guildmates get to high levels to really make any progress in there.

Until then you will just be punished for being an efficient leveller. Blizzard didn't add much in the area of high end content for soloers, and pvp battlegrounds are a great idea but far off in the future. Lots of my guildmates already in the 60's just spend time in the auction house buying and selling for profit while waiting for the rest to catch up.
