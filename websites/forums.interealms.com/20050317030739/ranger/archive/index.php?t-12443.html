I threw together a quick list of where things drop and what combines are needed for this bow from trolling EQtraders and the Glade. Just thought it would be nice to have all the recipies for this bow in one place.

Pottery
Block of Clay + Small Jar Sketch + Flask of Water = Small Unfired Clay Jar
Highquality Firing Sheet + Small Unfired Clay Jar = Small Clay Jar (Trivial at 31 you need 2 of them)

Tinkering
Clockwork grease (foraged in PoI) + Clockwork grease + Small Clay Jar = Jar of Clockwork grease (May be a no fail combine)
Jar of Clockwork grease +1 Innovative Clockwork Bolts (PoI Drop), 1 Innovative Clockwork Gears (PoI Drop) = Innovative Cams (Trivial> 201 and < 237 You also need 2 of these)

Tailoring
Nightmare Arachnid Silk (dropped in Plane of Nightmare off spiders with "creeper" in their name) + Nightmare Arachnid Silk + Wax = Nightmare Bow String (Trivial >160 <=209)

Fletching
Nightmare Bow string + Nightmare bow Staff (dropped in Plane of Nightmare off various treants) + Planer Tool + Innovative Cams + Innovative Cams = Nightmarewood Compound Bow (Trivial > 250)
