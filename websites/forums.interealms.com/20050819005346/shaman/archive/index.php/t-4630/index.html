I did this with my monk and it worked, so i thought, why not try with shaman? I got my shaman to 29 and decided to head over to ds. The only things you want to solo now are zelniaks and shrooms, but i talk about zelniaks. First, they are not that great at magical resistance, i usually land a malise anyways for a backbone. The regular zelniaks can con from dark blue to yellow to a 29, and the large ones are always red. Now that we know what to hunt, we gotta knwo the best place to find them, and there is one spot that i have found. When you leave netherbian lair, you take a right and follow that wall allll the way to the shroom temple place, pass the temple, but stop in a small area beside the temple against the wall. Pull the zelniaks onto the rock wall, and just run back and forth along the wall using root/affliction/envenomed breathe. I don't bother with slow, cause i never melee, psh :P. With the yellow zelniaks, i would get a full blue when i killed it, evens gave me a little less, and blues would give me half a blue. You can stay in this small little area until 40, but at 40 the regular zelniaks will be almost all light blue, but you can stick with the large ones. Hope this helps out you fellow shaman, I konw i'm going to stay here till 40, tiz great.
