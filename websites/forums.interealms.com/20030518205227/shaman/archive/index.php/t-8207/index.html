I can confirm ... he is NOT a rare with the normal snakie as a PH. Vilefang is on a 24 hr (+/- 2 variance ... but that may be off somewhat) timer. If he is up, there are 2 snakies, but they are single pullable by ... well, anyone. If he's not up, sitting around killing Entropy Serpents isn't gonna make him pop unless you get very very lucky about when those 24ish hours are up.

It is possible to get a lowbie ranger down there sneaky-like, or escort him down in a raid. Keep him in the water, on the right-hand side of the pool, between the 2 tunnels - no aggro there, and surface when you need air. =) Makes tracking him (oh - and Zlandicar) easier, at least until you mess up - which you eventually will - and either let the poor ranger drown or watch as something eats him.
