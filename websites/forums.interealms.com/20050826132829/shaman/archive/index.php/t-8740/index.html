I personally did Crystal Caverns from 39-46. Here's how it went:

39-40: Orcs - by the time I hit 41 I had enough faction to sell to the Dwarfs there. Really nice to hunt, sell and bank in the same zone. Plus the named orcs drop nice items. Cyrstal covered shroud, herbalists pack, crystalline belt, frozen 1hs & 2hs (nice vendor loot), plenty of velium bricks/blocks.

41-46: Equally split between Terrors/Geos. When geos were open I'd camp them. Non-aggro until you kill tons of them (still dubious at 47 after 3 levels of killing). They drop normal gems and the named geos drop chipped gems that sell for 15-180pp. Normally drop 1-4pp per mob as well, super awesome loot.

When the geos were taken I'd camp the 6 terrors in the great hall/broken bridge area. 4 spawns around the broken bridge, single pull, dark blue until 46 (then some dk blue, lt blue and green). I'd pull with slow, melee and send pet. Once those 4 were dead, I'd take the 2 in the great hall. Again single pull with slow, melee, send pet. The terrors drop decent loot. pp, some gems, crystal fibers (which are lore so pet would always get one).

For buffs I'd just use haste, regen, bearform, fury, ac. Pet would only get haste.

Was a great zone for those levels, especially if you play at off times and like to solo. For variety, throw in some crystal spiders and get plenty of crystalline silk. It sells very well in the bazaar.

Echo is great for your level. My advise would be to do Underbulks and Fungal Fiends until about lvl43-44. The Stonegrabbers are great exp, but you wont be able to slow/dot/dot/sick dogdog on them like you will the Underbulks and Fungals (don't think I've had an Underbulk or Fungal resist a slow or dot).

So, after you hit lvl43-44 and have max'ed your defense, then I'd say pull the Stonegrabbers with Slow and tank it "in teh face". It'll take you and dogdog a while to wear it down, but you'll get it.

Also, watch out for Trillicor (named Fungal Fiend - has a stun/whirl dot - kinda like Clockwork Poison if you've ever tried to kill the Fungus Mutant in Upper Guk) and Fireclaw (named Underbulk - hits hard and fast and if it's got the Fireclaw Talons he'll be procing out the wazou!!). Never been able to stick a dot on Trillicor either.
