I thought I'd start a thread talking about some of the less-obvious solo/duo spots for shamans 60+. I actually enjoy grouping in PoP, but sometimes I just don't have the time, or I just feel like some peaceful, mindless, solo slaughtering. What I'm looking for are the non-contested, decent, fairly easy solo spots, mostly in the old(er) world.

I took some time off around the beginning of Luclin, so I really don't know that expansion too well. Here's what I've found so far:

Swamp of No Hope - Black Ravengers. These things are dark blue at 62, and they are soloable..but very tough. They summon, and are at least somewhat MR. I duoed a couple with my monk, and the exp wasn't great for the risk (took all my mana, but that was mostly keeping the monk healed - should have root/rot more). I hoped this spot would work out, since they are so plentiful and non-contested.

Fungus Grove - bugs. I found these accidentally, and there were alot dark blue at 63. The problem is, they're social, and its pretty close quarters in there..though I'm sure there are pull spots. How hard are these things to pull, and once pulled, how hard are they to solo? I think I was seeing mostly nobles and such, a good ways back in the zone.

These are the only "less obvious" ones I've come up with so far. Here are some more obvious ones:

Sirens, DN, WW - mostly light blues. Both easily soloable. No exp to speak of. I have done constructs and spiders, but I'd like to try the rats (spiders are LB). Again, plentiful and fairly safe, just not sure on the exp.

Chardok - haven't been in since the revamp past the tunnel, and everything there was green or LB.

Sebilis - haven't been back in a while. The problem here is that the only stuff that gives exp may be too much of a pain to get to.

Plane of Growth - went up, everything was dead...might be a decent spot for my goals.

Kael - haven't tried soloing yet.

Cazic Thule - haven't been since revamp. Might be ok, but I've heard the exp was nerfed.

Old world City Guards: Last time I tried them, they were too MR. I doubt thats changed.

Anybody have any ideas? I'm not looking for the next great HHK, just curious as to some fun little quiet spots to make some exp, even if it isn't great.

~Akhasha

Here's some of my favorite spots from 60-61

60: Chardok upper tunnel near gatehouse, first few mobs can be light blue but as you make your way down everything is dark blue and very good xp. Don't waste time at ZO/ZI, mobs there are crap xp. Mobs are nicely spaced out in pairs and all single pullable at 60 in the tunnel. Tunnel goes on forever so no shortage of mobs :)

61: PooooooooooooooGGG! For money that is, xp is very bad. The problem though is that A LOT of people know about it. If there's not a raid then there's a handful of shaman and necros soloing there and mobs can take several minutes to find due to the 8 hour respawn.

61: Sebilis, gang or necro camp. Jins and zols are light blue but boks, krups, varying bugs are still dark blue and of course all illis are dark blue. The gangerous camp is not bad, the 4 iksar necromancers and leperous scarab are dark blue and decent xp and occasional gems.

60/61: Felwithe. If PoG is empty then for easy money do Felwithe guards. The drawback here is the high MR even on green guards so getting malosini/slow to stick can be very streaky. But overall it's pretty easy and all guards drop a sword that sells for 20pp.

61: PoD. Rats and goos for XP. They hit for 250 but are easily slowed. Takes 4 BoNs if you don't have epic and 1-3 KMs on pet, so kind of long fights but very nice XP especially AA.
