Boinshar
IMO Malos isn't as effective compared to BoS.

On any mob on which you can land Malo, but not Malosinia, you don't need an extra -10 vs all but desease to win. Also, off the top of my head I can't think of a single mob of consequence that fits into the above catagory.

BoS on the other hand, is a fire and forget DoT. Don't let all the horror stories about agro affect you--it's not nearly as bad as some people try to make it out. You can safely stack 3 DoTs and rain on any raid mob without fear of agro as long as your MT doesn't have autoattack off. Of course, it's easier if you just stick with BoS (which is ~52 dps by itself) and use your two rains or nuke for dps. Reaching 200 dps as a shaman is not unheard of, and maintaining 150 dps isn't that difficult.

For exp groups, BoS have above average agro so wars with sub par equipment will have issues keeping agro. That is what root is for. If any hybrid has issues gaining agro over BoS, then they need to learn how to use their spells. Again, root solves most agro issues you are likely to encounter in an exp group. If not, you always have LR5 to fall back on.

The following is the way I would rate our 65 spells in the order from most useful to lead useful for the shaman, then another order for a raid:

Q > BoS > Fo7 > Malos > FA

Fo7 > Q > Malos > BoS > FA
