The weakest link is the graphics card.. That is generally an on-board graphics chipset (on the motherboard, meaning there is no separate graphics card) and it is not very good.

To install a graphics card you need to know what slots you have available in the machine (ISA, PCI or AGP) and insert an appropriate card. Your geforce2 is either PCI or AGP (you can tell by looking at the metal lines on the plug, 2 rows vertically means AGP, else its probably PCI)

PCI slots on the machine are usually short white slots compared with short brown slots for AGP. (generally anyway)

My second system is very near those specs with the exception of my graphics card and it runs fine.
