Goldark
Did this with my shaman (gear and AAs was a little worse than it is now, but similar - see magelo in sig) and whilst a little challenging its not all that hard.

The main problem was the dispell trap. This seems to move around, some times it was in the room above kelkar's (where I setup camp) and this made it nearly impossible. Other times it was elsewhere and I could handle it.

Take a kei + virtue before you go the first time, but be prepared to loose them both. Its entirely doable without so don't worry too much, but its a nice safety buffer whilst you learn it.

Mobs are moderately resistant, after malos I got some slow resists but only the odd one here and there. Everything is rootable, having VP is a big big plus, spirit call also helps a lot to interrupt casters.

Take a few sets of mage toys for dogdog, ups dps and casting interrupts a lot. Get tribute points to fill in gaps in focus effects if you have GoD.

For the 5 mobs in bottom room, pull the mobs to the sides and you won't get all 5, just 2-3. Root one on inc, set pet on one whilst you slow the other. With pet enrage, you can really buy some time to get things under control. Quiessence both dogdog and yourself before the pull just in case.

Kelkar himself is quite MR resistant, I strongly suggest having disease debuff and slow memmed when you pull him. If you find him there when you arrive, with full spawn in place, I'd get some backup. Yourself and the mage would probably suffice for that, a tank would be a bonus.

Watch out for the priests casting CH, it can be a real bummer sometimes.
