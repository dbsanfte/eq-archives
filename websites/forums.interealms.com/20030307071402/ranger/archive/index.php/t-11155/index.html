What does it mean when a spell is "lure?"
Gavving
The term "Lure" comes from the Wizard Lvl 50+ nuke spells that are called "Lure of _something_". These spells were orginally given to wizards so that they could actually nuke stuff that had high resistances. Thus Lure nukes are coded to be very hard to resist.

So anything that's called a Lure effect is very hard for a mob to resist.

The call of Fire proc is coded the same way Lure nukes are coded, and thus are very hard for mobs to resist.
