Since you're a half-elf, the armor received from doing the Crushbone belt and bone chip quests in Kaladim will probably be useless - I believe it's for small races only. If you do decide to head to Faydwer from Antonica, be sure and kill a few fire beetles first - you'll need that fire beetle eye, which is not available on Faydwer.

I'd consider buying a velium weapon for about 10pp, and hunt willowisps in the Commonlands or North Karana. Sell the regular lightstones, and turn in the greater lightstones to that Gypsy in North Karana for an item that you can then sell for around 8pp.

Karana bandits are a bit more dangerous unless you team up with a druid (for Harmony). It's too easy to get several on you at once.
