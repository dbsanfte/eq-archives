frynque
many good points. and i have to agree with most of them. it all depends on what you're after.

some points that others might have overlooked:

loot: yes, being higher level means getting better loot, but not if you dont have the gear to get the new gear. being level 70 in gear that is embarassing for a level 60 because you power grinded has its drawbacks.

spells: again, there is the argument, higher level == less resists, but also being higher level casting spells that are 1-10 levels below you because you leveled faster than you acquired spells also has its drawbacks.

AA's: in some cases the boost from certain AA's outweighs the advantages of "specific levels". getting 60 is a huge level for a shaman. arguably, 61-63 offer only modest bumps in power, but most of the 59+ AA's offer noticible bumps (canni5, spirit calling, MBG, SCR, etc.).

gear: alot of good gear is level augmented (required/recommened level), so levels win this one over AA's, unless you're wearing alot of gear that has flat stats. depends on how you're equipped.

XP: as you level, the "sources" for XP dwindle. having say.. 15 zones to grind AA/lvl's in offers more possibilities and variety than having say.. 7 zones that offer "acceptable" xp return for time spent. so maybe spending some time in a "favorite zone" getting AA's isnt so bad. or maybe you dont care about scenery, then level right on thru. again, personal taste.

i'm sure you can come up with even more concepts, but these were always my major points. i level'ed 5 toons between 51 and 65, and only 1 did i drive to 60 [my enchanter, because before the KEI nerf, C3 was a huge cash cow]. all the others, i did various ratios of AA/Lvl xp from 51-65, depending on what i wanted at that time. if i wanted 57 for a spell or /disc, i switched to more lvl-xp. if i was gunning for a particular AA that i wanted, i switched to more aa-xp. if i was missing alot of drop spells for the next level, i spent more time on AA until i acquired the next level spells, so that i didnt feel hokey casting old spells at a higher level. but thats just me. enjoy the game, and play how you feel best reflects you.
