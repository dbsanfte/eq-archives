Colbe
You might try Lake of Ill Omen, it should last til level 30. Also, aviaks in Lake Rathe (if you can take the hits, start with the aviak at the diving board and work your way up) last til level 20/21, then move to South Karana aviaks til around 24/25, then group at the spires. Being evil, you could also kill treants in South Karana (they are good coin at that level) around 27 with a partner. Highhold Keep is good, also - kill orcs in a group to get faction up for evil races (also good xp). I wouldn't try EK, myself, til around level 20 or so.
