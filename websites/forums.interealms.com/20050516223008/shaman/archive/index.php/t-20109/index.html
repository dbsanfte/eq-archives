Gart Egilsson
The key to Bertox is having smart pullers driving the mob pulling.

You got to keep chaining them in.

Once the nameds are all dead (I think there's 12), then the big cheese spawns.

By this time, we've holed ourselves into one of the hallways. Pull Bertox with the guy you want to be rampage tank, and engage. His rampage is a bear, so keep the clerics back and really be careful about managing the rampage list. Multiple tanks should be shooting arrows in him before the heals start landing, ideally.

Do lots of DPS and kill him fast.

This isn't a very precise strategy, and lots better exist out there. I don't lead the raid, I just run where someone tells me to go. But this is what I've seen.

Bear in mind there's a timer. So you can't dawdle on rezzes and buffing when folks die.

Flufy
You've got 2 hours (well, 2 hours and 5 minutes) to kill everything including bert. I usually mem Tigirs, both rains, and whatever buff I'm responsible for.

Your monks will die, likely numerous times. Buff them as soon as they're rezzed, don't wait for them to ask. Time is critical, especially on your first few attempts.

That's about it. Monks bring stuff in, tanks pin it, everyone except the monks kill it, repeat as needed.

On Bert, you need to be careful. If people are too close to him (which they will be) they're going to get wasted by the ae rampage. Unfortunately, it seems like you need to be within rampage range for spells to land, so get in, debuff him and get out. Hopefully you won't die before he's debuffed.
