Eriadoc
for a shield, you might want to look at a Clay Guardian Shield (you said CGS, but I thought that to be Charred Guardian). Clay can be bought on the Rathe for under 200pp usually, not sure of yours. What I'd really recommend though, is to save for a Sarnak Battle Shield. Under 1k, and will last longer, IMO. Earring, do the Jasper Kundan Earring quest in Echo Caverns, if you have a few friends to help. It's free, and like a forest loop with STR. For a mask, the Fine Cut Diamond Inlaid mask from PoK is nice. The spell haste comes in rather handy, and the sarnak blood is easy to get. For the boots, try and get some friends to help you get your Black Fur boots. It's a step in your epic anyway, decent stats on them, and right-click instant-SoW. Kromrif Military Leggings are easy to obtain with a friend or two, and they have very nice stats for shaman. The fine antique ring from PoK is easy and very worthwhile. Jaded Velium Rings are cheap enough, if you know a jeweler. For a cheap tunic, while you save for iksar regen, a Shardtooth's Flayed Tunic is AC 14, 35 hp, 35 mana, I believe. Usually available for about 50pp on my server. A Smiter's Girdle is a nice, cheap belt, with decent AC, as well as a few STA and WIS.

Just a few ideas, hope they help =)
