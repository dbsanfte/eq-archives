When I completed the 8th cudgel quest in Howling Stones, I saw the following text:

"…The spirit of Rile begins to shudder and shake, the bones fly from your hands to meet their rightful soul. The spirit and bones being to glow and meld into one another, forming a swirling mass of ethereal energy. Abani begins to mouth incantations in an unfamiliar tongue. Their voice rises ever higher as mystic energy surges through the room…"

Can anyone else confirm this? I wonder if someone is pulling my… tail. ;-)

Also, there’s one more thing I’m curious about. When I turned in the box to the Iksar apparition, I saw this:

Vauris Sathir throws the relics…. Vauris Sathir’s insane cackling turns to unholing waiting as his remains are ripped apart and joined with the swirling mass of bone and spirit that swirls over the platform…

So, Vauris’ remains were joined to turn the swirling-mass into Venril Sathir (which makes for a pretty weird twist on the whole story). Yet some other accounts have suggested that the apparition immediately turns into Venril Sathir after the hand-in. Can anyone who’s recently finished the quest clarify whether they also saw the apparition turn into Vauris?
