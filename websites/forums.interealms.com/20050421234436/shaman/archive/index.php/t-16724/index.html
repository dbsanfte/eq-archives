Dual Wield and the Shaman pet
Our pet will dual wield if you give him weapons, pretty much any non-nodrop 1hand weapon will work, rusty ones included!

Our pet is classed as warrior but he will use other class specific weapons, such as the Serrated Bone Dirk (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=7502). Damage and Delay of the weapons given seem to generally not effect his damage. He can use Hand to Hand weapons such as the Hand of Ixblat (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=28596) which is summoned by high level Magicians. The shaman pet will proc weapon effects if he meets the minimum level required for that weapon proc.

I've been using the /tar command a lot of late for leopard'ing the wolves - it's easier than carefully spreading the swarm around the mob, then having the mob move a few inches and the wolves all congregating in one spot.

In my case, the command is:

/tar Retron`s_pet00
/tar Retron`s_pet01
/tar Retron`s_pet02

Note that the ` is a backquote - it's the key left of 1 on UK keyboards, not sure where it's located on the US layout.
