BlargX
In all the months my guild spent getting geared in HoT armor, I can't tell you how many times it was extremely critical that I get a slow in, and how many times things came down to a matter of mere seconds. Whether or not I got a slow off, and when, was commonly vital to how many people died, or whether any died at all.

You can be stunned for a long time.

And then you can get stunned AGAIN.

What with melee damage disrupting you and shoving you, and bashes, the last thing you need is to be stunned on top of it. If you're a mage and get stunned, it probably won't matter too much to the guild. But if people are counting on you for slows, not getting them in is BIG.

I would recommend nobody ever take up playing an ogre except people who REALLY want to hit 60 and keep on playing a long time after that. The 15% experience penalty is huge, and effectively far larger than the troll and ogre 20% ones, because regen is critical to shamans pre-torpor, and is absolutely worth any price pre-torpor. It's merely neato afterwards, no big deal. My 60 shaman never wears a fungi, though he's had one(have another on my monk now).

The iksar/troll regen easily makes up for their experience penalty in helping them keep up a good kill rate and leveling speed; ogre non-stunnability does not. It's beautiful in raids and not bad in regular groups, but for pure experience pre-torpor? ...it's not much.

However, if you can just tweak up an ogre with a fungi to start with, then who cares. Never take the fungi off until you get torpor.

After playing a druid and an ogre shaman, I know exactly how valuable not being stunned is, and after playing a shaman and an iksar monk, both of whom I've played with and without regen pieces, I'm well aware of the virtues of regen.

They're both great in their place. Regen is king pre-torpor and trivial after, though I would never say it has NO value. Just minimal. Non-stunnability from the front is king after torpor. It matters.

But for the casual player or genuine newbie who can't buy an iksar regen BP or a fungi tunic -- play inny troll.
