Porgy Beastmaster
The two more game balancing spells, Complete Heal and Slow, were so game altering that for a while, neither class got new and interesting things to play with that helped out groups.

Recently, after some rightful complaining, Clerics got a huge melee upgrade to make up for the fact that Complete Heal was being "balanced" by making it nearly the only purpose of the cleric. And that Verant was going to hand out some similar healing to Druids. DPS, and Hitpoints of MoB were increased to try and balance it, which was partly sucessful, and in the end, clerics felt shafted.

I think the same thing is happening with shaman. Enchanters, and Beast Lords *can* slow nearly as well as we can, just like shaman's and druids *can* heal nearly as well as a cleric can. Perhaps (wishing) verant is planning something to fill this gap, the way they did with clerics. A melee upgrade would be fun, curses would be better, but IMO to make it a lot more balanced, a faster (4 - 6 tick) Dot that deals some reasonable damage, has a fast cast time and reasonably low resist rate. I'm not talking about 4K in 30 secs here, I'm thinking 500 damage a tick, for 4 - 6 ticks. for like 275 mana with a 2 - 3 min recast. something you can use every other mob, or something. Make it a curse or lure. or heck, how come all these people get neat Debuff DoTs those are cool (not really class envy, just trying to come up with something). Sure... fix DoTs, that needs to happen anyway, but gimmie something to do other than slow, send dogdog, and buff in a fight. Mobs don't live long enough to warrent spending 250 mana on a slow when I good MT can take a Cheal for 200 mana and mitigate the same damage.

yes a beastlord gets his 65% slow post 60, a shaman and druid get their Complete Heal at 58. vs. Clerics much earlier. I'm talking about end game here.

And, I love my shaman, I'd never play anything else. If they don't change a thing, I will still love my shaman. I honestly believe in the pre 60 game they are almost overpowered. Tanking, DoTing, Healing, Slowing, Soloing, Buffing, Sowing machines. But anytime I get outta old world, in a good group, its Slow, Pet Attack, can can can can, Heal (maybe) sit. And slow doesn't feel all that usefull for its cost sometime.
