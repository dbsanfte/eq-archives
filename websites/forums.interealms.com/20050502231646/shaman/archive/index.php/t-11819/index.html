Ximix the Troll
I evaluate gear like this - Convert all other stats/mana/saves/ac into an equivalent number of HP.

My formula is:

1 AC = 3 HP
1 Wis = 2 HP
1 Sta = 2 HP
1 Agi = 1 HP
1 Mana = 0.05 HP (probably too low - but I don't care for +mana gear)
1 Save Magic = 0.5 HP
1 Save Fire = 0.5 HP
1 Save Cold = 0.5 HP
1 Save Poison = 0.25 HP
1 Save Disease = 0.25 HP

Strength/Dex/Int/Charisma have no value.

I use this formula to determine upgrades, etc. Focus effects, and similar abilities obviously need to have seperate consideration.
