EQ was getting boring for me, alot of my friedns had quit, but I loved EQ the sub-culture...

Sometimes I look back and wish I had my Regen BP, my SCHW, all my other goodies, but I wasn't having fun, they were just 1's and 0's.

Now, when I get upgrades, or skills or levels, it's more fun. Before if I had a Ranger, I would have solod to 30 in a week or two, never stopping to smell the roses.
