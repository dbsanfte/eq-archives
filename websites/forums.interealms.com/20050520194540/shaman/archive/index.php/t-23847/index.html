Kanamori Hitatomi
I've done this guy with 2 groups several times and he was a push over. His guards are the problem, you need someone to get agro and kite them (get strong agro, so the shaman can debuff and slow them). The main guy is weak, he'll I could almost tank him at 7khp. If your tanks are BoT/LDoN/Bazaar gear, you can take the boss, the problem is with the guards. You would need a kiter or two to handle them (snare works wonders). Kill the guards 1 on a time in main camp while kiters continue to kite. BTW, Zxul is permarooted to the stage he's on. No mater what you do, if agroed, he'll run to edge of stage and warp back. Use this to advantage to preslow or whatever you want to do. Once on him, it's all out dps and he goes down in 20 seconds. Good luck with him. And I will recommend doing it with 3 groups first time just to be safe. When comfortable, take out the extra healers (good idea to keep 2 clerics - yah, they are your competition for the sheild, but just do him 3 times and everyones happy).

He has 4 guards called "A Conjured Automaton", which have a flaming sword graphic. They hit for ~1500, and hit fast. The usual tactic is to single pull, mez them on inc, then debuff and slow before the tank taunts them off and engages. If you dont do that, and the tank engages before they are slowed, there is a good chance that he will die in seconds. Xuzl himself is a pansy compared to them, he just takes longer to kill.
