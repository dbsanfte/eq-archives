Two suggestions, the same I sent them in feedback:
1. Add a recourse to the spell which decreases HP of a shaman, and the healing effectiveness of direct heals on them (not heals over time). Somewhere around 15% per cast would get you 3 casts and maybe 6 during a raid per shaman.

or

Change the bloody code!
Give each character a new attribute called: Burnout. A value between 0 and 255 (i.e. 1 byte). This number goes down each tick by 10 (to 0). If someone tries to cast a spell with a burnout and are > 100, the spell is prevented (fizzle, message, dont matter).

Each spell you want to 'limit' how quickly it can be reused you associate a burnout with it, Between 0 and 99.

Example: Lets say panther has a burnout of 50.
Tick 0, 0 burnout.
Cast panther, 50 burnout.
Cast panther, 100 burnout.
Tick 1, 90 burnout.
Cast panther, 140 burnout.
< you now have to wait 5 ticks (30 seconds) before casting another panther. 30 seconds because thats how long it takes to remove 50 burnout.

There you go, you've rate limited Panther to 3 casts + 1 per 30 seconds there after. Effecting groups very little (since 3 is about the norm for exping, i.e. not losing mana) and you've allowed shaman to cast ~ 5 in a raid situation (i.e. losing mana).

AND you could give classes other burnout spells. And as long as you don't give spells a burnout of > 99 they could be combined and you will no longer need a recast delay (note if you have a spell which adds >= 100 burnout, it is effectively a 1 minute recast, and you could simply use the recast delay on a spell to serve the same point). Spells could add burnout to their target (i.e. mana feeding a necro may make the recipient unable to temporarily cast by giving them 50 burnout).

You could change all of the Hybrid nukes (the ones with the 18 second casting time) to a 30 burnout. So they could cast 4+1 per 3 ticks.

The point is they need to be creative, it is their bloody game they are killing off.

At the very least the developers and pr people should talk to eachother. Because its a JOKE what you are doing and does nothing except reaffirm the posts of the more jaded EQers on the various boards.
