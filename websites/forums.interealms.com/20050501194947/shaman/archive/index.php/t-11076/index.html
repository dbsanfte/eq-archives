Kriel Darkbane
Snare and fear are a good combination - if it's feared it's not casting at you.

But so far everyone has missed the obvious solution - nuke! If your dot's aren't fast enough, nuke the little caster buggers down. At lower levels the caster mobs still have few enough HP that it's possible to nuke them down relatively quickly with a couple of nukers. At L 24 both shamand and necros get a good new nuke spell. Sure, you're not wizards, but frost strike and shock of poison are NOT bad nukes. If you're in a spot where you have to fight an occasional caster while exp'ing, just nuke them down.

If you're in a spot where you have to fight LOTS of casters while exp'ing, go exp someplace else. You are correct that shamans are not ideally equipped for fighting caster mobs, and that will only get worse as you level. Shamans excel against melee mobs, so why fight that when you're trying to exp?
