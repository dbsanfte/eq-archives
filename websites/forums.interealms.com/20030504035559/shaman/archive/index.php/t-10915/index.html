DoT spells are not sold in PoK, or we'd be going there to buy them. There are three places to buy those spells:

Everfrost - tundra northwest platform - Starn Bearjumper (2875, -3435)
Cabilis - Shaman guild - Vessel Kirtokk (1040, -215)
Cabilis - Shaman guild basement at bottom of east tower - Grazik Plaguebringer (1125, -260, -24)
Shar Vahl - alchemy bldg beside bank upstairs - Dar Khura Ahmih (-215, -345, -165)

The Shar Vahl vendor is the only one who is friendly to Ogres and Trolls, so just go there until your missing vendor is replaced. Well, if he's ever replaced. Iksar Shamans still don't have a 51+ Kunark spell vendor :rolleyes:
