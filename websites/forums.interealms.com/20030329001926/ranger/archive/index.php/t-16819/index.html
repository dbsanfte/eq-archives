Though being 46 would allow you access to PoP combat zones, you will be completely vulnerable and unprepared for the monsters there. Once you reach level 46, I reccomend you participate in some old-world planar raids, i.e. Hate and Fear. With strong backing, you could begin to do Tier 1 PoP zones in the low 50's, but it's really hard to be effective there until say 54ish. For pickup groups, I think it would be difficult to get a group (as a ranger) being less than 56 or so.

I don't mean to discourage you, it's just that PoP is geared towards higher end stuffs. Keep levelin, you'll get there soon!
