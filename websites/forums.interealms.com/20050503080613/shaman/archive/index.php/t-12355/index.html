You're gonna cap at 200, all Shaman Melee skills do.

But yeah your max damage should be going up as you gain skill and level, and also as you gain skill you will get more ATK, which will allow more sucessful hits, which is far better than a few more points on max damage (though not as cool looking).

Though once you max skill and cap your str your attack will only give you more hits closer to max.... your max hit doesnt increase with attack.

I havent seen a max hit increase since I capped way back in the day... and str doesnt seem to effect max hit after 200 STR either.

Oh and I did some parsing recently with my mithril battle hammer.(max hit 88)
Self buffed 305 all stats except int and cha using 41% haste (Gauntlets of dragon slaying) and SLtW 30 - 31 DPS
Same buffs no haste item (still SLtW) 21-22 DPS
Same buffs SLtW. 41% haste and Ferine 32-33 DPS.

140 attack makes next to no diff to non melee..... each parse was run for exactly 5 min on the same mob (yes the mob was never killed), and the wep procs a 302 DD (I dont have any AAs or focus that mod DDs) which did figure into the parse.
