Weyalan
Hmmm, my advice is to get Velious quested armor.

Seriously, even the easiest / worst Velious quest armor is very good. Runecrafter's (the Thurgadin faction quests) would look real nice in Chest, Arms, hands, legs & boots. And frankly, it is not that hard to get. All you need is a level 59+ tank, a competent cleric, a slower (that would be you) and an evac (drood or wizzy). You can camp the Arena pretty much untroubled these days - not many people go there any more. And the thing about doing the Arena with a small tight group is that there is almost no competition for the drops. I actually have cleared the arena with 60 warrior, 58 cleric and me (56 shaman at the time) - the warrior and cleric split the plate, I got all the chain (see my Magelo), and we split the leather and silk for multi-questing.

By getting your Runecrafter's armor, you will (a) improve your gear a lot, and (b) be able to sell the existing armor in those slots to buy upgrades for other slots.
