Bigguss
If you have Luclin, hit the bazaar. There are some amazing low/mid-level weapons of all types. You'd be amazed what you can find there for just a few plat.

If not... cheap piercing weapons...

Bloodstained fang (Dmg 9, Dly 32) - sells for next to nothing (10 pp?)

Rukgus's sticker (Dmg 6, Dly 25, +3 Str, +2 Wis, +2 Int) - probably around 50 pp

Ebon war spear (Dmg 8, Dly 31, Rangeable, +4 Str, +3 Wis, +5 vsMg) - probably 100-200 pp, this one is nice because when you upgrade you could move it to your range slot to continue to benefit from the stats.

Bettle stinger (Dmg 8, Dly 23, +4 Agi, +4 Dex, +4 Wis) - 50-100 pp) , Rangeable

Spine chill spear (Dmg 10, Dly 28, +3 Sta, +6 Wis, +10 vsCold) - 100-300 pp

Slime coated harpoon (Dmg 10, Dly 25, Rangeable, +5 Wis, +10 Mana) - 100-400 pp
