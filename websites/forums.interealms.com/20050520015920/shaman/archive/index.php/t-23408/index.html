I start running across the bridge, noticed our tank was there too. Thought someone called run, but then the tank turns around. So, so do I. Should have kept running. Got back to the fight and it was going badly so we started running again.

I got to the NPC, and tried to hail and click the stone as quick as I could. Got the message that I had died followed right by the message that I hailed and then zoning. No yellow text and no clicking the stone.

Aargh! Probably my 1 chance at the Tipt flag and it's blown by a druid who missed a snare (remember, she's been trouble all night) and my indecision about running.
