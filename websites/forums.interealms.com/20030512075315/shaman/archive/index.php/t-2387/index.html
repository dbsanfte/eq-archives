Chazic
Well curently my shammy is level 40. With all buffs up and in AC/HP gear my Wis is 140, AC is about 730 and HP is about 1150. Currently though my defense skill is not maxed out though I'm working on it. I've noticed with higher AC (those times I forget to take off Sale gear CHA or WIS gear for tradeskills) I take considerable more damage from the creatures I regularly hunt. My current operating procedure is to fully buff myself and buff dog-dog up Haste & Str. Then run out and smack a nice blue mob with a slow and run him back to my puppy parked in nice safe place. I will then root the mob as it closes with me. At this point I will hit him with a slow and Venom Bolt only. The Disease ussually takes longer then the mob will live so I dont bother with it. I'll let the mob merinate with venom while I'll anni down to 3-4 Bubbles of health and med/regen. Once Venom runs its coarse I reapply venom and reroot one last time. I now send in Dog-Dog and watch his health till he is about 3/4 to 1/2 health which at that point I join him on tendorizing the mob. Once the front side of the mob has been tenderized enough the mob will turn and allow the back side to be tenderized which at that time I sit and allow dog-dog to finish unaided. With the new pet changes we dont have worry so much about dog-dog doing too good a job on the tenderizing part so that helps alot. Technicly you could replace the Venom Dot with the Scorge Dot but Scorge just takes so long to work. And Venom and Root are the exact same duration in time so if you reroot the mob a few seconds after dotting him you'll always have a very good idea when root should be dropping with out haveing to time it.

P.S. Have noticed that the RAGE/FURY spells dont attually have any regen components or mana loss components. Though the loss of the regen kinda bits the non lose of mana more then makes up for it. With the Added stats with that spell line and low mana cost it makes for nice buff if you cast it before the fight to avoid the long recast delay it adds. You just have to not cast the Dex buff as it will not stack with Dex for some silly reason.
