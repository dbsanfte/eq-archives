lotholeaffall2
The ONLY dangers of the Kael run are 1) getting lost/not knowing the way and loose camo/or bump into a see invis giant or 2) Knowing the way but loosing camo and training someone else.

Buy 1 or 2 cloudy potions from a merchant at the thurgadin bank, then drink them if you loose invis inside Kael.

Bind at SS zone-in, CRs will be fine then. Forage the drake eggs there. Pull raptors from the ledge that is located to the left after you zone out of SS. You should get good xp from raptors and should get the guts in no time.

If you decide to try the holgresh solo, pick the wanderers outside the caves. I can't say enough about the importance of resist gear when you fight those. They suck at melee so you don't need AC, but you need to be able to resist some 500dmg nukes.

I had a lot of fun when I did it, I am sure you will have fun too.
