Now I am not 100% sure about the rest of these, but this is what I have found out in my understanding of archery:


Attack = If you are able to land a shot. Average dammage.

Archery Skill Level = The maximum dammage you CAN do.

Level = Yours vs. the mob. The higher level you are the more bonus you recive, the lower level you are the more penalty you recive.

Skill level and attack are kind of intertwined, cause as you increase in skill level, the more attack you recive. However if there were such thing as a 500 attk buff, I dont think that you would hit harder, but be more accurate, and have your average dammage higer. Dex also assists with both the maximum dammage you WILL do, as well as the average dammage you do.

All 4 of the stats are connected together, but independantly, this is what I see.
