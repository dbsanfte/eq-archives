Poguk
Well after getting the non-glamorous AAs that improve my performance but are kinda boring (ie. run3, regen3, mc3, canni5, scr3, scrm, qb1)... I decided I wanted to take a break from them and get a few "fun" AAs, even if they don't really do much hehe.

So I maxed out Spirit Call. Pure fun factor. So that every 12 mins I could "Unleash the dogs of war" and get a wow factor. So yeah, I'm easily amused.

I love this AA! Moreso than I thought. I mean, it really spices up a long grind of buffing, slowing, DoTing... /yawn. Every few mins I can watch as a small army of dogs swarms a baddie and pummels him into the ground, and know that I brought them into the world, and it amuses me.

I know, it doesn't add an insane amount of DPS, and those 5 dogs are not hasted or alpha'd... so individually, they don't do much DPS. But 5 of them together... and I'm surprised at how much "spam" they make. When I cut them loose, between those dogs and my regular pet, and a DoT.... I'm one of the top DPS people for that fight. I mean, they can really spam!

But they actually do serve a real purpose here and there. Against big-time casting mobs... those dogs are great at interrupting via melee push and via bashing. 6 dogs, all bashing, is bound to interrupt a caster. This applies to duels too.... the casters in my guild hate those dogs.

They also prove to be valuable for "mob push".... when we need to position a mob for a raid. I make sure I am positioned right... unleash the dogs... and that mob straightens right up. :)

So all-in-all... I'm pleased with AA, if only for amusement factor. I hear a lot of folks say how it really is a worthless AA... and I say "how can you say that!?!? it's fun!"

Although... there was a noticeable difference going from SC1 (3 dogs) to SC3 (5 dogs). Much more spam and some serious interrupting/bashing/push power.

Anyway, for 9 AA, I enjoy it.

Also got VP1 and so far I've been disappointed in how often it gets resisted... and when it lands... doesn't seem to last long. 5-10 secs tops. Perhaps I need VP2 for it to improve.

I'm gonna get 1 point in Radiant Cure, and then go back to keep working on the boring-yet-necessary Archetype AAs.
