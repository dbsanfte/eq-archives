Lots of bazaar time.

You just need to watch what items go for and hope you find some good deals. I personally have been very fortunate finding strange deals. Watch what people auction. I have seen people selling noctivangant blades for 250pp (they easily go for 3kpp). That is an extreme example of course. Mostly I might buy something for 200pp and resell it for 250pp, just a small percentage increase, but the really good deals can make some nice money. People don't like to run a trader all the time, so they just take what money they can get. I will invest the time to let my trader sit there overnight while I sleep, and all day while at work.

Don't think badly of me, I am not one of the people that completely overprice things just trying to make a buck (Like selling Torpor for 100kpp). If I have something and I see people that just have it way overpriced, I will totally undercut them, sometimes by thousands of pp. Over time you build up your bank account and buy more expensive items. Buy for 10kpp and sell for 12kpp. Still a small percent, but it is 2kpp profit.

I do other things also. Like, checking most vendors when I pass them. Buy hides, silks, and other items cheap from them and resale them for profit. I also spend a very little time on tradeskills making leather padding and silk items, which other tradeskillers need. I farm certain items some times also.

I am only level 25, with a 32 Warrior alt. I have never been on a raid, never got any items or money from my guild, and have been playing total for less than 7 months (usually just on weekends). But, I have a lizard, solstice earring, and other nice items with a healthy bank account left.

Just be patient.
