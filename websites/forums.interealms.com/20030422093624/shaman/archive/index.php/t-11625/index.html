I have one quick question about hotkey. I am thinking about getting JBB and I want to know if I can program hotkey to right click on JBB. If there is a way, can you explain to me? Thank you.
Mamutoi
Equip your JBB, left click and hold your cursor over the JBB in your inventory. This will create a hotkey with your JBB in it. Drag and drop it into your hotkey area. Then you can just right click that or hit the appropriate number key to activate that hotkey. Example.. if you put the hotkey in the #4 hotkey slot just press 4 on your keyboard to use your JBB.
