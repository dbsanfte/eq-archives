Iranicus Spellbinder
Its very easy to solo in HS, although I prefer LCY/RCY PoD

Important to get Kei and try to get Aego for break in cause it can be nasty sometimes. There are always two mobs that walk around the cube when you zone in. The good thing is that if you dont take a step in any direction you are exactly out of agro range, so bring up dogdog, buff up and dont forget Project alpha. Now the two that walk around can be any types of mob: sperm: bottomless feaster, sepulcher skeletons or greater war boned skeleton. The sepulcher's harm touch for about 550 damage so if two are up root one and send dogdog against the other. Then slow both, Dot, heal if need be and keep rinsing dots. Epic is nice to have as well as JBB but I had neither when i soloed here. Once you have killed both, choose a wing to start at, stand on one of the higher posts so that you dont have to climb stairs to pull. There are always 2 up so make sure to root one and send dogdog in while you slow the other. Once again dot and rinse. Just use this method for clearing all the wings though without bane of nife its a little difficult as ebolt isnt as good. An important thing to watch out for is respawns so its best if you time them. I believe the respawns at entrance are 24 mins. As for the post about someone getting skulls from a mob that came outside a door on east wing I find it a little hard to believe cause skulls only drop of mobs in the south wing. So dont rely on that to get ur skulls. Also at lv 58 you can solo pull most of the mobs in the zone, except the east wing as harder stuff pops there. Your best bet if your collecting skulls is hope that a rogue wants to help you or that a necro with a south wing key drops by. This happens a lot since most necros love this zone and farm it for loot (and south wing has really nice loot).

Well good luck
