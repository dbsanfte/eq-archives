Kazim
Since I have a horse and JBB, the only time I even consider meleeing is in an indoor dungeon. And there I think it's a no-brainer to use a 2hb unless you're tanking, in which case a case can be made for a 1-hander + shield.

I use a barbarian spiritist hammer for my 2hb, swinging it in between jbb blasts. Prior to getting a JBB, granite face grinder worked well.

I've only got a slime-coated harpoon for a piercer, since I don't want to spend money on a slot where I'd obviously prefer to have my epic.

And I use a centi warhammer for 1hb occasionally.

In my opinion, the decision tree looks like:

If Tanking the mobs and you have a good shield, then use a piercer and a shield until your piercing is maxed. After that, use whichever of your piercer or 1hb has the better damage ratio.

If not tanking the mobs, use whatever weapon you have that does the most damage to the mob. Generally, that means whichever one has the highest ratio. But a few weapons have procs which put them slightly ahead of other weapons.

If you're using a JBB, use the weapon with the highest damage you can find. Go ahead and totally ignore delay, since unless it's 80+ it won't matter.

Goldark
I don't worry about weapons anymore. Once you hit 46 and can get a JBB, that will do more damage than 99% of the weapons out there. What's more, you can sit on a horse (outdoors - and PoP is all outdoors) and chain cast it, all the while getting full meditating benefit. You can't do that if you melee.

Later still, mainly post-canni V, you will find mana regen isn't much of an issue and will probably dump the JBB in favour of casting rain spells or even straight nukes. When you aren't doing that you are probably hitting canni IV, canni V and torpor. You won't have much time for swinging a weapon, but if you do go for max dmg as delay won't be a big issue. You'll just be swinging between casts.

Weapons are nice for farming greenies, but to be honest my 61 pet does that very well without much help from me. I carry my epic plus a shield - combined I believe the stats are far superior to any 2hb.

Until you reach 46 and can use the JBB though, I think the REIF is a great choice for a piercer. I never went down the 2hb route but agree that the driveshaft looks decent. Whatever you do, bear in mind that you might not be using that weapon after another 10 levels.
