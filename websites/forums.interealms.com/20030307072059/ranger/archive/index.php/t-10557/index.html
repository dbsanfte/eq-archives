Stridure, actualy the geonids are one of the worst mobs to camp for cash in CC at all, simply because there are only eight spawn points, and the ones that drop the worthwhile gems are rare. The only mobs worse than the geos for CC cash are the spiders.

The miners, with the tons of velium they can drop, are the best cash in CC, especially with their numbers. You can run through and kill, over and over, and they're easier than the geonids by far as well.

When getting cash, the secret isn't really finding some huge plat dropping mob, or some mob that drops something worth a ton of cash; those work but aren't very efficient overall (because the mob might go very stingy with cash, such as the HG's, or might not drop the item/the item might stop being worth the camping). This is why places such as SolA, and the Paineel skellies are popular; they drop consistant amounts of coin or other vendorized loot.

I went to North Karana for the purpose of getting materials for a fleeting quiver. I didn't expect it to be so lucrative. Here's what I'm getting at level 45...

- Hill Giants drop up to 30+ plat, FS weapons, toes and lambent stones, and exp.
- Griffons drop up to 5 plat, griffon feather (for Fleeting Quiver) and blood, and exp.
- Highland Lions drop all manner of cat and lion pelts, including the HQ Lion skin (for FQ).
- Wandering Lions and Bears drop pelts to sell in the bazaar. I'm getting 1pp for ruined, 2pp for LQ, 5pp for MQ, and 10pp for HQ (but not the hq lion skin, that goes for 100s)
- EK is close and you can harvest silk fast.
- Bandits drop a plat and sometimes a sash that can be turned in for a couple more pp and exp.
- Foraging gets you plains pebbles which sometimes sell.

With tracking, you can expand your search for griffons and giants to East and West Karana by just zoning over and hitting track.

Unfortunately, as wonderful as this all sounds, the whole Karana hunting area can only support one, maybe two rangers/druids.
