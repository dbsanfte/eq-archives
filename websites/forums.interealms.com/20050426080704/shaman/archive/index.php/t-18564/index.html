At 62-65, I'd do one of the following:

1) Put a group together yourself. Paladin/sk, cleric, bard, beastlord, and one other high dps class. I prefer necros because their group heals and mana regen kick so much ass. With this makeup, it's basically impossible to run out of mana. With a knight tanking, you can nuke and dot with impunity. Go to inner caverns (or any camp) in POV and chain pull. You can probably get about 15% xp an hour doing this. That's roughly a yellow and a half in 2 hours of xp grinding.

2) Put the same group together and go to BoT and kill the bees (stormriders) up top by the towers. It's more fun than regular xp grinding because you have to keep moving. The mobs also heal, so you need to keep pushing them like crazy or have the bard chain mez to interrupt.

If you're confident of your group's ability and your gear/spells/skill is sufficient, forgo the cleric and get a knight tank, a bard, and 3 more high dps classes. A beastlord is ideal because they can often preslow, saving you a lot of time and mana, not to mention beast crack and buffs.

You can get about 15% an hour xp killing these things at a brisk pace, and the loot is great. You get tons of EP and SP drops, as well as about 500pp each per hour in splits.
