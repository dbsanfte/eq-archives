Barm McLir
I think most shaman spend their first six points on Runspeed 3 and Regen 3. Runspeed may seem redundant for a class that can SoW, but since it works in dugeons it can and will save your bacon. After that, I'd recommend Mental Clarity 1-3 since it's a pre-requisite to mass cannibalization (our best aaxp ability IMO).

Having said all that, let me say this -- levelling does far more for you than aaxp. I waited until 60 before even thinking about it.

Good luck,
