Ungkor
Ok, tonight was my guilds second Quarm attempt. But a first for me, as I was afk during the first attempt.

Well, I thought to myself, tonight I shouldn't be lazy so I went about making sure everyone is FA'd, cured, happy. Basically steeling myself to be all that I can be:)

So, I dilligently setup hotkey set 5, with 7 /tar meleeX keys, and a 8th key with /cast FA /3 FA on meleeX so I can keep track of who's next. As I start going through the cycles, I get 4 tells saying don't bother, and 2 tells saying "I don't have the room for FA with the dots and HoTs"

My point:

I guess its been said before, but it really sinks into a player hard, when you're doing something big with your guild, something you've worked on for a long time, to have what used to be one of your bigger assets tossed aside by the raid.

I guess with whatever comes out with Oow, in whatever final form it takes, I hope that at least one of our buffs is considered "invaluable". That, when space is an issue, at least one shaman buff is the one players do not ever want to click off.

That being said, I certainly didn't feel useless. Continual cures and keeping the real healers healed kept me quite busy and feeling useful. Quarm being at least partially slowable feels good, and having to re-debuff him every 25% gives shaman a fairly big role.

However, I couldn't help but contrast Time to GoD. We raid often in GoD now, and the differences are glaring. Nothing slowable, fast battles, insane dps. I've said it before, most of you have also, but I still just can't imagine how SOE can't see how badly GoD hurt the shaman as a class. Its in every way fundementally opposed to our strength as a class.

Perhaps in Oow they'll release the "Land of No Nukes" or the "Zone of 'FD don't fool me boy!'" or "HollowShade of No CHeal". It would be interesting to see an entire expansion seeminly designed to take away each's classes biggest strengths.

Oh well. Still having fun, with Time being rotated between GoD runs, and most things still being an upgrade for me, I have some to look forward to. And I'm really looking forward to our Oow group heal/edur regen (although its an expansion too late).

Other than that, I'm in a "wait and see" mode concerning our buffs and dmg spells. But from what I see so far, there isn't much thats going to redefine us so far. I really hope that SOE does something big with our class soon, because I've been reading tons on EQ2 and it looks amazing:)
