If you're primarily soloing/duoing, then I suspect you are looking for to do a few basic things:

1. Kill quickly
2. Emergency crowd control.
3. Tank "in the face" when necessary
4. Eliminate as much downtime as possible

Perhaps thinking of your next few AA's in this way will help you:

ND3 - More hitpoints = tank longer without healing = larger mana regen "buffer" (Note it's like 200-300 hps you get TOTAL with ND3, so the jump from ND2-ND3 isn't that impressive.)

VP2/3 - Longer, more reliable lasting root *that is manaless* Also good emergency crowd control if mobs aren't too disease resistant.

SCM3 - 10% saving on mana. Less downtime

SCR3/SCRM = Buffs last longer and Torpor/Quie heals more, less mana spent over time rebuffing, less downtime

SC1/3 = Basically a DOT with a knockback component *that is manaless*, also good for emergency crowd control (until the pets get killed/depop)

What you pick really depends on what you feel you're suffering most from.

Most shaman I know seem to gravitate towards SCM3 and SCRM before moving on to VP/SC and the Healing AA's. I know personally I needed a larger mana pool to keep downtime to a minimum so I went for SCM3 then SCRM. I think the mana savings is VERY noticable with both. Also a nice convience factor of not having to rebuff every few min (FA and haste).
