Chulain
Best way to raise the skill is just to use it. Find a blue mob that doesn't hit very hard, slow it, tank it, and beat on it. Make sure you are buffed, hasted, even borrow a haste item if possible.

I can't remember what mobs are best for this at 55, maybe someone else can offer advice on that.

Also, try to get a weapon with the lowest delay possible. Even if the damage is low. Lower damage is actually better, so you can just keep hitting the same mob for awhile without killing him.

Training points are pretty cheap now, since you get 5 each time you ding an AA. It might be wise to dump a load of points into 1hb to get it started. That's pretty much a personal preference.

Don't forget to work on 2hb as well. Use the same technique.

Once the skill gets over 100 or so, you can just use a 1hb or 2hb weapon while soloing normally. Pull with slow, dot, tank mob while autoattacking. 2hb weapons are pretty nice actually...my Hammer of Spiritcalling will swing once between each JBB blast. Not quite as effective anymore at 65, but if I remember 55 correctly, that was my standard soloing tactic.
