thRover
You get an upgrade for every flag your character receives. It doesn't matter that some of the zones were opened up to certain levels. If you get the flag for that zone, you get an increase in your charm stats. This includes access to the Plane of Time.
Barliman
You get upgrades for passing the justice trials (storms/valor access), killing Behometh (tactics access), killing Grummus (Decay access), Terris Thule (Torment Access), AD (Halls of Honor access), and the Bastion of Thunder key quest done in storms. Basically the rule is if you get access to a new zone you get a charm upgrade with the exceptions of b zones like ponb, hohb, etc. There are also upgrades for sol ro access, elemental zones and time.
Luclin's Arriana
You get an upgrade for every flag your character receives.

Not exactly. There are a lot of flags that don't give AA or Charm increases.

You get upgrades when you are flagged for access to the following zones:

1)Valor/Storms (Any justice trial)
2)Torment (Killing Terris Thule, or the alternate quest)
3)Access to BoT (Doing the storms collection quest for Askr)
4)Access to Tactics (Killing behemoth, or doing storms diaku quest)
5)Access to Halls of Honor (Killing Aerin`Dar or the box of souls quest)
6)Access to SolRo Tower (Need lots of stuff for this, and they need to be done the "real" way, alternate access won't do it)
7)Elemental Access (Killing Rallos Zek)
8)Time Access (Killing Fennin Ro, Rathe Council, Xegony, Coirnav)

The rules for getting the bonus AA point are, IIRC, slightly different, but basically follow the same pattern.

Baz4
Can confirm of personal (and recent) knowledge that PoNB access, via Hedge trial, does NOT affect your charm.

The Hedge trial in PoN and the factory access trial in PoI (Xanamech) give you character flags and access to another area, but do not count as access to a new Plane. In effect they are sub-quests en route to the next Plane.
