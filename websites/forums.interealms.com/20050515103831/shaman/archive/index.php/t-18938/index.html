Jiva was the hardest for my guild until we figured out how to manage the crowd control, just gets the adds nicely mezzed and kill Jiva as fast as you can, then kill the mezzed adds one by one. What worked for us is to assign mezzers to a set of two adds, first two adds the first mezzer gets, next two for the next mezzer in line etc. Like others here have said, if you did Dreso with 18, you should be fine on all the others.

As a shaman note, all Minis are magic slowable and not that hard to get slowed.
