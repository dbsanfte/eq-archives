Moregar
If I am not mistaken, your STR buff > HOS STR component, that is why it stays...but if HOS is cast first, no STR buff can be cast. Your DEX buff is <= HOS DEX component, so it gets overwritten. The amount of allowed STR/DEX buffs change with each upgrade, until the single buff is no longer high enough to not get overwritten.

Hope this makes sense.
