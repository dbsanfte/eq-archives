Balerius
Doomshade is along the lines of Vindi but with more HPs.

Doesn't hit quite as hard as Vindi but flurries instead of rampages. So a good set of rolls by Doomshade can produce a string of flurries that can eat your MT's HPs in a hurry. Doomshade has no special resists that I recall.

Pretty much a straightforward melee fight.
