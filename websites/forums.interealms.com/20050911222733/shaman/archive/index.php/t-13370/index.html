Dulaks harbor is where I plvl my 49 monk to 54 fairly quick. Go into the refinery area (fort looking thing) straight back against the wall. I did NOT group them. I made a pet and buffed him.Sent monk out to pull swapped back and hit a hot key.

ASSIST
/assist smaxx (my monk)
/pause 2
/pet attack
/attack off
/cast 4 (slow)

This was an easy way to slow mob and use pet DPS to speed kills. Gotta watch the mobs they will warp to the above walkway so pull to the side w/ the 2 burdoned ore cart guys or root them.(clear them early in your cycle). I left with a lot of gems and parts/meat from troll,ogre and iksars. At lvl 54 stuff started going light blue so I moved to mines above.
