Hi Everyone, although i havent posted much over the years, i have read the forum almost daily. I offer forth this tip i use to get more usage out of your spell slots.

Ok, i dont know about you, but i usually have a set 6 spells that are always memmed, and i have 2 slots in which i swap out spells. Also, while i like the 10 spell sets you can mem, i only use a few of these regularly (mostly after death, auto mem your standard set).

I have set up a bay of hotkeys for the spells i swap out on a regular basis.

so lets say you have your spell sets as follows..

1. your regular lineup
2.-10 favorite swap out spell 1-9

or how ever many spells you want to set up to hot swap.

Now in spell sets that you can memorize put fave swapout spell in your normal swapout spellgem slot and nothing else. (ill use shrink as example)

Now, in hotkey do:
line 1 /memspellset 1 (assuming that is where u just did shrink)

do this with the other frequent spells you use and all to go into that same spellgem slot and you have a very quick and easy way to ease up the tedium of swapping out.

ya can almost switch on the run since the process is automated and will be done much faster than when you do manually.

Hope this is clear, i am afterall just Ogre 8)
