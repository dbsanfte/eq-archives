75% slow would slow the mob's attack speed by 75%, i.e. they would attack 4 times as slow.

Your equation is incorrect. 1 - (1/1.75) = .43 is how you actually arrived at that number. The actual equation should be;

1 - (1 * .75) = .25

Let's use x as the value of the mob's normal attack speed. If I am slowing the mob with a 75% slow, I first have to find out what is 75% of x, then subtract that value from x. So there are 2 operations here;

x * y = z

x - z = s (the new attack speed)

If x = 1 , as in your example, then 1 * .75 = .75 and 1 - .75 = .25 so the new attack speed is .25 (25% of x). Or, 1 - (1 * .75) = .25
