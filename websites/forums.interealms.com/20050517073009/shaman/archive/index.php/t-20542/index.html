Finally, I know there will be people that say "Damn, how do you get all that plat?" and "I'm always broke."

Here are some logical tips on making plat:

1) When new expansions come out, farm new items that are high in demand.

2) When in groups, roll for any droppable item that [I]nobody wants. You worked for that item just as much as everyone else in the group. The people that 'pass' to be nice in my guild are always the poor ones.

3) Clear out your bank or strip any twinks of items that you do not need. Historically, prices almost always go down in Everquest. Sell them high while you can. New expansions bring better items.

4) Create an AFK Bazaar trader if you can. Whether you use another account or sell while you sleep or work, it all helps. When I'm at my keyboard, the last thing I want to do is stand around in the Bazaar.

5) Farm quest or tradeskill items.[/INDENT]

Good luck!
