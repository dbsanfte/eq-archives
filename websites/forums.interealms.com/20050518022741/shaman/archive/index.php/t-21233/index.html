As for acumen, stamina has been removed from the game entirely but it does grant a great vision effect (ultravision) and also see invis. Unfortunately the spell adds no other effects other than vision enhancements now so the "quickening of the mind" is all fluff.

Ungkor
I use tigirs all the time, but I'd trade it for AE malos any day:)

Like other posters have noted, its situational. I hardly ever use it on tough mobs, its mostly useful for farming, or content you can be sure to survive multiple mobs on you.

For instance, farming shards in Ssra. 3-4 skele's is the typical pull when not doing AE groups, so I wait for them to all swarm the MT, then cast tigirs, and root the adds.

When in AE groups, its nice to use Tigirs to drag 4 mobs at a time to the center of the AE action.

Sometimes, on highly magic resistant mobs, where even after malos, slow won't stick for a couple attempts, I use tigirs as a backup slow, even on single pulls. With the refresh time on Turgurs, having a second slow memmed can really help out.

Basically, expect to get just as much aggro overall as you do with Turgurs. Expect to get 3 mobs on you when you cast it, plan for that, and you'll be fine.
