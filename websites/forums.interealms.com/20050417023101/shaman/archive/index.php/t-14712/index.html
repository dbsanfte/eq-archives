Where? Dulak's Harbor in LoY. I started there last friday EQWing my shaman monk combo both 43rd level averaging 4 hours a night weekdays and about 8 on weekends. I parked Meow at zonein from the Gulf (one you zone in, look to your right, there is a angled corner, park shaman there) There are 5 static spawns near zonein with at least 2 roamers that walk close to the zoneline. Then there is a yard full of trash beyond. Also close to zoneline in some houses are the drunken pirates needed for the Stoticism quest.

Ive made about 10k in plat drops, gem/jewelry drops. Plus occationally there is a shout of something rotting which you could use. Monky has a new pair of boots because of this.

The boats are normally camped, once I get to 48th on entrance mobs i'll try my luck there. I hear regular and blue diamonds drop on the boats.

You will see (alot) mantle of stealth's drop off yard trash. way too common, 2 charge invis with decient stats. Lore, you wont make any cash of these.

I wont go into tactics here, if you solo or 2box(eqw) this zone rocks.

Normally what I do is pull 2, dog dog attacks one, monky attacks other, slow monky's mob (who btw is at 50% life already) then slow dog dog's. Stoticism dog dog if needed. The finish up with monky. I use a mount so im always medding and ive never used KEI.

Occationally you'll get free heals and buffs from new peeps coming in zone... at least on povar you do.

Thats all i can think of atm.
