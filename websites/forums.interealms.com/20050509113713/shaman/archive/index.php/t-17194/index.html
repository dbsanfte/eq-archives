Jeones
That's what I thought. I was surprised to find that it increased my charm (Intricate Wooden Figurine) stats even though it was not an actual flag.
Juventus
Yeah, the way the charms work is by zone access. Basically you get a free AA point and a charm upgrade every time you earn access to a new zone (not from level based access). If you do the alt entry quest and then go back and get the regular flag from MB, you don't get another charm upgrade from MB.

After tier 3, the charm upgrades are few and far between, as it takes lots and lots of flag earning kills to get to the next zone.
