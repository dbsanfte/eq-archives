I bought my 1st for 15k, sold it for 21k, and rebought another for 15k. The sell and re-buy were within a week, I can't do without it. DO NOT pay more than 2k over the "normal" price, hold out. I had someone wanting 25k, and they got nothing from me. Someone else 25 minutes later sent me a tell and asked for 18k. I told him I'd pay 15, or sit on my ass in Gfay till someone did sell me one for 15. The convo went like this:

Seller: I'll sell for 18.
Me: Sorry, paying 15, max.
Seller: 17.
Me: 15 max, sorry :(.
*Pause of 30 or so seconds*
Seller: 16k, final offer.
Me: 15.
*Pause of 30 or so again*
Seller: 15, come to Gfay bank.

Moral: People win these, and then think "Ooh, I can buy this and this with the cash when I sell it!" So they run over to the server trading zone and /auc. They don't want to spend upwards of 30 minutes there because they want to go back to leveling and they want to buy their new items. They WILL come down eventually. Be a stubborn jackarse, don't give them slack. If they come down to within 2k of what you were looking to spend, but won't budge for anything, then buy it. You DID make a profit, since you paid less than they wanted.
