Rathelon Steelbear
There are no advantages to fighting in bearform, and one minor disadvantage (cannot slam in bearform, just like wolf form). Damage is determined by what weapon you are using, just like wolf form (except wolf form gives extra ATK, bearform doesnt).

The advantages to bearform are infravision, extra wisdom, and a little extra hp regen.
