Emmhim
It seems to be consistent through EQ that Sony sees 1 FT = 2 regen.
E.g. when looking at tribute, you can get 2, 4, 6, 8 and 10 regen, but only 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5 FT.
The same with our canni spells - the conversion is approximately 2 HP for 1 mana.

For worn regen/FT, this means that either the worn regen should cap at 30, or the worn FT should cap at 17.5 FT.

Whether this is fair or not is another thing, but I find it is OK. Considering how much damage a caster can do for 1 mana, going from FT15 to FT35 would heavily increase the dps for some classes, while other classes would be stuck unchanged - too unbalancing.

vincenzovs
i think it's a game design decision.

It seems sensible to me too, given that mana regen effects (point for point) have a bigger effect on you compared to HP regen effects (point for point).

If you check out items of the same "level" (e.g. ldon items at the same price etc) you will often see that mana regen 1 equates to regen 5, and mana regen 2 to regen 10 etc..

So it makes sense to me that the regen cap is higher.
