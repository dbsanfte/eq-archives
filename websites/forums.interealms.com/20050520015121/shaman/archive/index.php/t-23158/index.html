Vigdis
The game registers your postion every six seconds and updates your hp/mana accordingly.

What you want to achieve is to fool the server into thinking you are sitting constantly by timing your standing and casting between each tick.

So if you wait for a tick, then stand, cast and sit rapidly, the server will believe you have not stood up and thus you get meditation plus cannibalize bonus to your mana.

Should ofcouse have Regen up unless you have someone with more mana around to heal you.

The first step is to make a macro for the canni-dance (shown by a few of your fellow shaman) Second is to time the canibalization correctly. If you are "sitting" when the tick occurs, EQ will assume that you have been sitting on your butt medding away... If on the other hand you are standing when the tick occurs then you just wasted a tick. How do you time it... ?
place your cursor on an existing buff and you should see the number of seconds it still has... when the number changes (a tick) use your macro and canibalize and sit down before the next tick. I usually leave the mouse cursor and keep hitting the hotkey until I'm needed to do other things. Of course you should have regen and any regen hp item on to help you get a better mana regen rate.
