That tells you how poisoned the target is. He stays poisoned until either the default time is elapsed (doing the maximum damage) or he removes those counters, using a cure effect. Cure effects remove X counters with a bit of randomness to them. If you cast a cure that happens to remove 6 counters, 4 are still left. The second casting will probably remove those 4 and cure your target.

Note: Our top slow line (Turgur's) is magic-resisted. However, it places disease counters. Therefore, to cure it, DO NOT DISPELL, cure :)
