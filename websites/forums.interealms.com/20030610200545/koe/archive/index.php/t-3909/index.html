Drolgin/Anders

From our conversations this weekend, I had a feeling this might be coming, and took some time to think on it.

First off, No matter what comes of this, I want for you what I want for everyone... you own personal happiness. Life has enough hardships, enough misery, enough garbage that we need to find all the enjoyment we can, where it is available... especially in a game thats sole purpose is entertainment. I, like the rest of the Keepers, want that happiness for you.

I understand the feelings you are having. Going from a big fish in a little pond to a small fish in a big pond is the best metaphor I can summon at the moment, but it is a disconcerting feeling.

No matter how this phenomenon makes you feel, remember and understand you ARE loved by every member of this family. You soul and spirit are part of the energy that forms KoE. And its soul is part of you.

You will always have a place in my heart, and if I may be so bold, in the Keepers. I only hope we can help you find the magic you so miss in the world of Norrath. I only ask that before you depart us, for now, forever, whatever it may be; that you look back on some of the amazing times we have shared in the past. For once put any annoyances, any misunderstandings or squabblings out of your heart, and truly remember the love we have for you.

With great regret, but hallowed thanks, I say Goodluck my friend, but not goodbye; that word dare not fly this melancholy eve.

Aliera/John
