Henora
I've killed Yeldema half a dozen times in the past, she really is a creampuff. I was so scared the first time I did it, but it was surprisingly easy. I have fairly low end gear as well, about ~2200 hps, ~1000ac selfbuffed...had about 2000 hps the first time I tried.

Basically, I fight her near her spawn, just at the cliff edge, on the coast. Only had trouble with an add once. Velious Drakes don't assist, but cragwurms and other dragons do. (they don't assist if you are on COV faction and aren't naturally KOS to them, that is.)

The first time I attempted, I took way too long, lost my pet to riposte right away and had an add, and failed. After that, no problems.

The last time, it took me like 8 minutes or so(I am pretty conservative with my mana, so I am not stacking dots like mad or anything). Basically, buff, summon pet. Park pet inland a bit so he attacks from the rear. I pull with Malo, then channel Turgurs. Apply epic dot and keep it up constantly. Canni and Torpor as needed, apply other dots as you feel comfortable. Dogdog chews at her from behind. A little while later, dead Yeldema. JBB if you feel like it and are able to use that, I usually don't.

Keep a timer on your slow, so you have time to reapply before it wears off, leave room in case she resists, which sometimes happens.

I don't kill her anymore as I am on CoV faction and 2 kills will drop you from max ally to warmly. And, thanks to a certain friend shaman and monk pair(*wave* :) ), the dragon boots aren't worth much on my server.

-Henora
