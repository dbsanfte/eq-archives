Brief rundown of Plane of Justice:
-Lot of mobs in cells
-Some of them talk some of them glare
-If you go one way you enter imprisoned mortal type beings, which are indifferent to apprehensive. The glares npc's will attack you once you hail them, so I stopped after one or two.
