Originally every 5th level from like 34 up was a mathmatical hell level because of the way they figured experience. It was literally double the exp of 53 for 54 then went back down at 55. Originally they smoothed out all the hell levels through 49 (a day or so after I dinged 50 btw, wasn't a happy kitty). A few months later they straightened out the 50's (right after I finished 54) by taking the total exp needed to go from 51 to max 60 and spreading it out between all the 51+ levels in a more linear progression. About a day or so later they patched again taking lvl 60 out of the equation because it became a full level and not the half level it always was, leaving all of 51-59 mostly smoothed. 54 and 59 still take a bit longer then the other levels in my observation, but isn't even close to what it originally was.
