Well, we are talking about your next 9 points for investment purposes.
IF you don't raid enough to make MGB your top priority, THEN I wouldn't waste the next 9 on Quick Buff3 SINCE you have a haste item that works with QB2.

My recommendation:
Radiant Cure1
Virulent Paralysis1
Spirit Call1

If you raid at all, Radiant Cure is a must have in my opinion. Much more so than QB3.

Virulent Paralysis is an underrated AA in my opinion as well. It has saved me many times. It isn't perfect, it can be resisted, but an AA that you can use every 2 minutes is worth investing in sooner rather than later.

Spirit Call1 is FUN, it works as a great off-tanking tool & for strategic mob placement (which VP serves to do as well w/ the knockback portion). Twelve minute cycle time means it is adding roughly a free ~3k aggro-free DoT on a frequent basis.

If you raid often enough, then MGB would be top priority. But you said you don't, so bump this down a ways in your progression list imo.

QB3 would be nice to have, but QB2 gets things done for you at the moment. Especially with the bone in range, this shouldn't be a priority for you. Remember, even if you find that nice range item (I use a CT brain), it doesn't mean you can't swap out range items if speed in casting is going to be an issue for a specific situation.

Good luck no matter your decision!
