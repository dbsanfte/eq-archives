What you have to look at is the BIG PICTURE, as it were.

Yeah, charm got nerfed, and enchanters aren't happy. I'm not, because my favorite alt is a 65 chanter, and we got hosed.

Charm itself WAS overpowered, and it DID need to be adjusted. But what you have to look at is the aforementioned BIG PICTURE.

See, charm was about all chanters had LEFT.

Before you go off on me, consider this:

1: Enchanters are the kings of crowd control, yes. But today's camping strategy places that crowd control at the PULL, not the CAMP. 90% of the groups out there won't so much as THINK of doing a camp without a ranger, bard, or monk doing the pulling. Just as the short fight goes against the very core of being a shaman, a long succession of single pulls goes against the very core of being a chanter. This leaves most enchanters with very little to do but tash, slow, maybe toss a nuke (which is generally what we do if we're not down there meleeing the mob, like I do. ;) )

Auli, my chanter, commonly pulls when she's in a group. If they're gonna put the CC in the pull, then dammit, I'm gonna be the one doing it.

2: Being that chanters are CC kings, SoE has MASSIVELY ripped the class of those very tools. There is no upgrade to Word of Morrel, the PBAE short duration mez, usually an enchanter's last line of defense against a runaway mob. They've linked the recast timers of the Omens PBAE stun to other stun-line spells so they can't be chained, eliminating the ability to chain stun to control crowds. And mobs now have such high MR that attempting a mez before tashing is literally flipping a coin to see wether you live or die. If mez lands, you live. If it's resisted, you die.

What was worse, they farmed out more dependable CC to another class, the Bard class. Bard mezzes are much more reliable, harder to resist, and bards can stand up to a mob longer than an enchanter while they're trying to mez it. And if they have the room, they can attempt a mez while running at bard speed, something an enchanter can't do.

3: The extreme duration of buffs like KEI and Vallon's virtually eliminates the need for the enchanter in a group. KEI buffbot vendors in PoK are helping to kill the desirability of the class as a grouping option.

This is why Auli NEVER sells KEI in PoK.

4: Enchanters suffered just as much as we did because of slow mitigation.

5: SoE literally "stealth nerfed" charm TWO DAYS before the expansion, and they DID NOT tell anyone. They instead left it to the Enchanter player liaison to break the news to the Enchanter community that charm had been nerfed, and that was ONLY after an enchanter noticed the changes in charm on Lucy and started making noise on the EQ Live forums and the Runes, the Enchanter message board.

Even now, SoE refuses to communicate with the enchanter community over the charm change. The one thing they got from Brenlo was, and I quote: "This is not a nerf."

6: Charm was literally the last bastion of Enchanter abilities that SoE hadn't torn into little pieces and rendered either useless, mitigated, or nerfed. And even that was itself nerfed over a series of expansions...first the xp penalty nerf, then the pet agro nerf, then the 4-man hate list nerf, and so on and so on.

And now charm is nerfed again to the point where the risk versus reward makes it generally unwanted.

Understand, not many groups are going to want a pet that takes 33% of the xp, hits only slightly harder than a mage pet, but then turns around and does its FULL DAMAGE when charm breaks and it goes after the enchanter.

And the way things have evolved, charming a strong dps pet was one of the few things left that enchanters could do and do well, given that changing game philosophy and SoE's weakening or farming out of other Enchanter core abilities had reduced Enchanter effectiveness in what's always been an Enchanter's core abiilties.

Bards are now more effective CC than enchanters.

Shamans are better slowers, and can heal, which is a VERY big deal in OoW (thus why we're desirable once again).

Beastlords now come very close to matching the dps a charming enchanter, but without the risk of a charm break.

Enchanters can slow, and buff, and well, slow and buff. All their other abilities are now greatly reduced.

Most enchanters could live with this nerf IF SoE restored their ability to do CC. Put reasonable resist modifiers on the OoW mezzes that would make the land reliably, introduce an upgrade to Word of Morrel, and stop the ridiculous recast linking of PBAE stuns, and many enchanters would be quite content.

So yes, Enchanters are ticked. And as a part-time Enchanter, I completely understand why and fully agree that what SoE did was raw.
