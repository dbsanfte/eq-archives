I solo the entry room at Hatesfury when I know I will have several short streches (30-40min) to play. You can camp aginst the wall at interance with little danger.

I pull the single rat to the right as you enter, then the two pirates on the right ( they come together but you can root the closer one in place while slowing). On the left, root the pirate near the ladder down and the nearby snake will come. The last two mobs in the room come single.

Will occasionally pop a named in this room.

Beware as there is one roamer that enters the room and wanders close to entry hall. You need to be prepared to root-debuff it as well.

That said, I believe you would find it much more rewarding to clear the docks. Quite safe to pull to the Hatesfury zone line and I don't see much xp difference if any.
