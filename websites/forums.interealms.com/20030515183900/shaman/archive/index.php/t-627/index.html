1. Increasing faction (usually to 'max ally') requires redoing already completed parts of the epic quest over and over again and obtaining the faction hit.

2. Anything up to but not including the tear turn in to Rak is skippable, in theory. It's just all a matter of obtaining the 'max ally' faction. If you get that, you could theoretically skip the entire quest if you felt so inclined, and turned in about 5,000 gems to the drunk in FP.
