Chutoi
Vad, there are a number of things you can do to reduce the amount of stress on your computer and video card. Some of the common ones I use are

/shownames 1
/showspelleffects off
/serverfilter on
/dopropertinting off

Cleraly, more RAM would help, but that may not be an option. The above commands will reduce the amount of work your computer does. However, that being said, your issue may not be due to your computer at all. You didn't mention what kind of connection you were using--dial-up, cable, DSL, etc. The issue may be with your internet provider. I'd recommend you go to dslreports.com and play with the tweaker. I think they have a speed test.

weissblau
Odds are that you wouldn't notice lag spikes in other applications like downloads. Downloads are all about THROUGHPUT - how much data you get through in a given time period. EQ and related games are all about LATENCY - the time it takes for information to get from you to the server and back. Odds are really good that you don't have any latency-sensitive applications except for games.
