Detton
Theres no real difference in the stats each races. With only a difference of a few points, it is VERY easily made up with equipment.

Racial traits, on the other hand, are different. All three of those races' traits are AMAZING for shaman. I'll key on the big ones:

Orc: 25% resistance to stun and knockdown (WOO!), Axe specialization (You can train 1h axe as a shaman, and with enhancement talants, you can learn 2h axe. A 5 point difference isn't too bad.

Trolls: Berserking, when you're weak on health, gives you a solid melee AND spellcasting haste. +5% damage against beasts is decent, regeneration isn't too shabby. Throwing weapon mastery is complete crap. :)

Taurens: Very good selection here, too. +5% bonus to your health, +10 (i think it's 10) nature resist, Cultivation (+10 to your herbalism skill. A lot of shaman choose to be alchemists so it's helpful), and War Stop is a PBAOE quasi-uninturruptable stun (.5 casting time.) that can affect 3 targets.

All three make great shaman. Tauren lands will be slightly less populated, because they aren't quite as popular (No idea why) and it's isolated. So, leveling will be a little easier there, IMO.

But, orcish 25% resistance to stun/knockdown and troll Berserking are EXTREMELY nice traits.
