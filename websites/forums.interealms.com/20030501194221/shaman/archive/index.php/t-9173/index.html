It's pretty hard sometimes to make people understand a simple "If you appear, STOP AND DIE!"....

Once you fight down to the tower, there's a safe spot over near the cliff edge... to your right, facing the tower. If you crowd over there, the wanderers shouldn't come near enough to aggro, and you'll be far enough away from the tower mobs. From there, just use Invis to Undead on everyone, and run up to the top, left room (Kindle's). Expect someone to lose ITU and train you.
