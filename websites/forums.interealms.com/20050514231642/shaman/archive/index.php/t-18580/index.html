what I said:

As for your original question, I would advise just doing run3 anyways. As far as I am concerned, there are only 3 general abilities that are not completely useless:

Run3
Regen3
Metab3

Some people choose to do stamina or wisdom but that is a poor choice. You'll reach the soft cap fairly easily and eventually the hard cap. The bonuses you get from 6 extra points of wis and stamina are pretty trivial. Regen3, as well, is mediocre now imho. Before PoP came out, regen was pretty good, but now mobs hit so hard and fights are so fast that it just doesn't matter. It is nice to have, of course, but it isn't vital at all.

On the other hand, run3 is something which I still find useful today. Sure, you can get the same effects by casting pack shrew, but I WILL NEVER HAVE TO CAST THAT SPELL EVER. To me, that is worth 3aa. I hate casting bih'li, and I'm pretty sure I would hate casting shrew as well. Run3 saves a buffslot and saves yourself some hassle. To get an idea of how nice it is having run3, try this: Put your epic boots in the bank for a week, and go back to casting sow whenever it fades. If you say that you don't mind and it doesnt bother you, then by all means skip run3. I, personally, couldn't stand it. Also, it DOES wear off, and can wear off at the worst times. I have had to train out of ldon dungeons before. I have had to train out of tactics as well. I've had to kite SRT mobs for several minutes before I got enough room to mem and cast gate. All these things have happened many times. In all these situations, if my spirit of shrew had faded, I could have easily died. With run3, though, I never have to worry about my shrew fading.

Your last option for general skills is metab3. I use stat food and I have metab3 trained, but I also have over 300aa. I wouldn't really suggest something this trivial to someone just starting out on AA.

Out of all these, run3 is really the only AA I feel strongly about training out of the general abilities. Once you have that, it is really a tossup for me between regen3 and metab3. Nothing else is really useful.
