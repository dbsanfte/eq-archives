At 65, for exp grinding i make sure i'm within the 5 level bonus. Can't speak much about PoS, but it seems only Razorfiends + Wasteland mobs (Golems, crawlers and spiders) are 60+.

Exp grinding without the within-5-level bonus just plain sucks.

At 65, you are pretty much stuck to Tier 2+ zones for good exp. I know certain people kill the High Guardians in PoJ but I"m not sure about faction hits and splitting them. (Usually it is a cleric/chanter combo so i'm assuming pacification or a druid with Harmony of Nature).

You didn't state what flags you have. My advice would be to PL the other character you 2-box so they can pass the trials as well (if need be) and just exp in PoS or PoV.

Make sure you kill mobs which don't mitigate slow (unless the other char is also a caster).

In terms of light blue cons, once a mob turns light blue i believe you only get 1/2 of the exp. That means you have to kill twice as many as before when they were dark blue. The only times I kill lt blue stuff is when I'm farming stuff... like in PoJ caves for the ring.
