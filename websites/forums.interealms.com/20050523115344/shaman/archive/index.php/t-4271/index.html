Delirios
I did a nice amount of plat in KB with my lvl 30 necro by farming Upper Guk. I killed Froglok Squire for Woven Grass Bracelets (150pp each on Kb), Ancient Crock for arms (400pp on Kb) and Leggings (450pp on Kb), and if I had time before repops I went to the froggie in the ramp room that drops Collar of Undead Protection and Runed Bone Fork (400-600pp on Kb).
The Ancient Crocodile room gives great exp to up to lvl 34 so you'd get some exp too. All of the camps I mentioned are very easy to handle at 30'ish. You can single pull froggies at your level, and the crocodiles wont assist each others unless you fight too close.
