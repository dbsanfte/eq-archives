Just added info, as I read some conflicting stuff about this earring. I was thinking based on what I read, that the Skull drops only from A Frightling Horror, so I kept clearing the Frightlings in the hopes that this would help spawn the Horrors. Much to my surprise, I actually got the skull off of a regular Frightling, and not from the Horrors, so just thought I'd share the info here.

This is a nice peice of resist gear, and way too easy to get, so I humbly recommend spending a bit of time in Lfay before VI gets out the Nerf stick and beats this thing down. It just seems way to easy to get, kill level 15'ish mini-skele, and turn in head to NPC in same zone. Had mine in about 30 minutes or so after entering LFay = ).

Using the EQAtlas map of Lfay as reference, the Frightlings spawn at the Pixie Tower (#2 on map) and the NPC is located to the Druid Ring Ruins at the MM entrance, hope this helps.
