I assume you're talking about the piglet in PoTa.

Here's the tid: You know how mobs have insane regen if you're out of their agro range? Well, same thing is true for piglet. The only difference is that the piglet has an agro range of about 2 feet. To know if you're in agro range of him, root him. Then slow him, dot him, do whatever. If he is not facing you, you are out of agro range of him and you need to move forward. If he's facing you, you'rein agro range of him, and just stay where you are.
