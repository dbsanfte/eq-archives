Gloriana
On the final level, one of the portals can not be killed and continues to pop out named mobs when Agnarr reaches certain levels of hitpoints.

On the last level, we do something like this:

Zone in. One ranger aggroes Agnarr (without damaging him), snares him and runs him around and around the room.

The rest of the guild takes out the portals which can be destroyed, puts a MA on the named mob and gets rid of the trash.

Named mob is killed, trash is killed.

Everyone but the kiting ranger runs back to the stairs, everyone gets rezzed, everyone re-buffs.

Ranger uses WS to bring Agnarr to the waiting MA. Agnarr is shoulder pinned to the wall. When mobs pop, everyone but the MA (and clerics healing MA) runs over and fights the other named, returning to Agnarr when the mob is dead.

Rinse/repeat until just Agnarr is left, kill Agnarr.

Run up the stairs, find Karana standing in his pool, tell him "I will follow the path of the fallen" or something like that, get flag.

Voila.
