Jaruden
About 100 threads around on this if you search =P

That said, I generally only use the first 6 pages:
1/2 buffs.
3/4 heals, cures, minor buffs (sow/billy/group acumen), etc.
5/6 damage / debuffs. Slow, malos, dots, dd's, pet.
On 7/8 I have resist buffs and some other stuff that's very rarely used (blood of nadox, levitation, everlasting breath).

Seems to work well for me, I can find any spell pretty quick, and if it's an older spell I need for some reason, there's always the menus.

Thorzion
Organized by how often I use the spells with the most used ones in front.

1/2 Things like root, sow/bih'li, kraggs, stoicism acumen, Turgurs, cripple

3/4 buffs, pet

5/6 Dots, nukes

7/8 other utility: shrink, grow etc.

Spells that I no longer use are in the back from pages 36ish to 50. I have those organized by type. All STR spells grouped together, all HP together. I also have those organized by level starting with low at the top of the page and later ones at the bottom. Makes buffing younger characters much easier.
