Jinheim
Whenever you solo anything in PoP, you want to pull with malos (or malo in your case, doesn't make much difference). If you pull with slow and it gets resisted, then you try to root it and get resisted, then get resisted on root again, you're either going to have to kite around (not alot of room to kite in most pop zones) or just die (which especially sucks in lowly populated zones like hoh).

Pull with malo, run away a bit, and root it with the root of your choice. I have ornate legs (greater immobolize clicky) so I just use those, but any root will work, really. When I solo in pofire, I keep true greater immo memmed because it casts a lot faster (1.8sec vs. 3sec) and I am more likely to get it off between their attacks (only partially slowable, and hit for 800's). Basically, though, any root will work.

Once it is rooted, apply slow, malosinia, malicious decay, whatever else you want.

Start dotting, and remember to refresh root and slow, if applicable.
