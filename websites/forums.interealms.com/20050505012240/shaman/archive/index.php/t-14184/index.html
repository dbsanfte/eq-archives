Hitpoint, Armor class, Dexterity and direct attack buffs all DEFINANTELY affect pets. When you pop PA/FA on a pet you're seeing improvements from the +atk and +dex (with proc weapons). Some people swear agility does as well but I've never looked into it.

Stamina has no effect on pets at all. There was a recent bug on test where using a pet heal would cause the pet to report its HPs plus the value of the heal combined. Buffing stamina never changed the HPs on any pet. Apparently, even SOE isn't aware of this since summoned mage belts actually have a +sta stat on them.
