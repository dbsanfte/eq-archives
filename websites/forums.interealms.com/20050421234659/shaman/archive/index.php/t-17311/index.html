Coccyx Oracle of Thule
The spiders are rediculously easy. They have about 5500 hp, but they don't hit any harder than mobs in Dreadlands (despite being an average of 8 levels higher). They hit for around 90. Even the Gargoyles only hit for ~109.

Only the translucent spiders drop the silks you are looking for. A_Crystaline_whatever. The Blizzards and Shards don't.

Crystaline Watchers are rogues. They backstab for around 280.

Crystaline Devourers have a nasty dot called "velium Poison", which does about 1k damage over 2 minutes. Buffing poison resist should protect you lvl 57-. After lvl 58 I wouldn't worry about it.

The Gargoyles are slightly higher level than the spiders, and have higher magic resistance.

Since this is an unkeyed, easy dungeon, you can expect a higher ratio of retards, KSing and training (both intentional and unintentional). Fortunately, it's also a popular place for people to PL toons on secondary accounts, so it's relatively easy to get a rez, should you need it.

The maze area where the spiders spawn can support approximately 2 groups. Any more than that and the mobs get extremely hard to find. There are also 2 Icepaw Kobold camps, both of which can be reached by one of 2 fake walls. The castle is rarely camped (At least on Xev).
