Karshak_rn
Ragack,

If I understand you correctly then the answer is no, you do not miss out by waiting. For example, Virulent Paralysis has a pre-requisite of level 61 meaning you cannot train this until you get to level 61. It then states you can train it every two levels. Therefore you can train it at level 61, 63, and 65.

Alternatively you could wait until 65 and train all three levels there.

So, the only restrictions on AA are when you are <65.
