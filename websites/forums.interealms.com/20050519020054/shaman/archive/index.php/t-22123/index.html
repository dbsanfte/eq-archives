Weyalan
Haste is the most obviously beneficial buff. Swift like the wind is a must for pet.

More hp are of inarguable benefit to the pet - Focus is good for the +hp (and the marginal benefit of +str and +dex). Virtue, Brells, Pot9, etc are all good for pet (stack them if you can).

Improved Atk is of noticeable benefit, so both +atk and +str buffs should, theoretically, be worthwhile. Ferine avatar has good +atk and +str (and using it on pet can be a timer for refreshing on your group members).

If the pet is wielding proccing weapons, +dex will increase proc rate.

Regen is good too.

Both +AC and +agi should, in theory, help, but in practice, parsing seems to suggest that either there is no benefit, or that the benefit is very very small.

As a general rule I always buff dogdog with
SLTW + Focus.
If I am not pushed for mana, I give FA and regen

If I am pet pulling (using Spirit of the Howler), pet gets Spirit of the Shrew and, when about to pull, Quiesence.
