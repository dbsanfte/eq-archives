Juujuu
I've found it easier to cannidance since the new UI.
Find a player window xml file that has a percentage listed of your mana, and same for your health.
Watching to see your health change percentage is much much much easier than watching for a change in your mana bar.

The other thing you might want to do is set up your hotkey for your cannibalize spell like this:
Hotkey name: Wince
Line 1: /sit off
Line 2: /cast X
(X is the number of the cannibalize spell).

That way, regardless if you are standing or sitting, you can hit your wince key to cannibalize. It will work if you have your spellbook open too. That combined with a simple sit hotkey is all you need to cannidance. Don't rely on macros to get all the sits and stands done properly, especially with lag happening. The only macro I have is my multi wince semi-afk hotkey. It looks like this:
Hotkey name: Winces
Line 1: /pause 65, /cast X
Line 2: /pause 65, /cast X
Line 3: /pause 65, /cast X
Line 4: /pause 65, /cast X
Line 5: /cast X
It chain casts 5 cannibalizes for me when I'm trying to regain mana, but have to do a quick afk.
