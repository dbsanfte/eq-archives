From the June 6th Patch:

** Focus Items **

- Spell casting time foci should report their new casting time properly.

- Spell duration foci designed to affect DoTs (the Affliction line) should now work reliably.

- Spell duration foci affecting beneficial spells are no longer random. They now always extend the affected buffs for their maximum. (We never wanted to make it harder to time your buffs.)

- The Affliction focus line should now only be affecting spells that do damage.

- Bards should no longer get focus item messages that weren't actually affecting them.

- We've re-assigned quite a few of the focus items to have their originally intended effect.

All of the same items have focus effects. However, as many people noticed, quite a few effects were mis-assigned due to a tools error. (Common, mid level items were frequently being assigned high level effects, and vice versa.)

This situation has been fixed, and the items now have the effects that we originally planned for them.

Jonaman
The info on Lucy, the best spell parsing site atm:

Affliction Efficiency III

1: Decrease Spell Mana Cost by 25%
2: Limit: Max Level(60)
3: Limit: Spell Type(Detrimental only)
4: Limit: Min Duration(36.0 sec)
5: Limit: Effect(Hitpoints allowed)

Extended Affliction III

1: Increase Spell Duration by 20%
2: Limit: Max Level(60)
3: Limit: Spell Type(Detrimental only)
4: Limit: Min Duration(36.0 sec)
5: Limit: Effect(Hitpoints allowed)


So it seems only DoT's should be affected but as Viscerator said the only spell line that wouldn't be affected by any focus would be debuffs, and that's silly, even for VI (one has to hope). In fact, all spells you cast on a mob which last 36 sec at least are affected by Affliction foci, even root and the likes.

I have an Extended Affliction III and Affliction Efficiency focus item, increasing Bane of Nife from 150 dd, 214 dot over 7 ticks for 425 mana to 150dd, 214 dot over 9 ticks (parsed a few times) for about 320 mana, increasing the efficiency of Bane of Nife from roughly 3.88 damage/mana to about 6.49 damage/mana.
