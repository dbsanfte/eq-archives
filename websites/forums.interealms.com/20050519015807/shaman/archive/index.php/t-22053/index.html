Purdypog
Hi all. I find it odd that so many posts on the crucible deal with issues that are really overall content balancing. Now, I am one of the first to agree that Everquest could really be a lot better, especially with the advent of the instanced zone and the huge potential that opens up.

But, be that as if may I still thinking we (shaman) are barking up the wrong tree on a lot of our issues or just presenting it poorly.

It seems to me that, although I don't always agree with the somewhat negative and attacking posts on this board, the developers have a pretty good idea of Shaman dissatisfaction. I suppose even the ranting and raving has served its purpose. Brenlo is (finally) doing a fairly good job of posting comments on the various class boards and has promised to bring some of the points to the attention of people who have the power to affect changes. Let's hope that is the case.

I do though, have a feeling based on my 5 years playing this game and watching Verant/Sony tune the game, that they will not re-tool any one class nor will they make radical changes. Their methodology so far is to make tweeking changes here and there when one class was unable to get an experience group because of the dual reasons of not having the tools to contribute in a meaningful way and player perception of the value of the class in an experience group.

Content balancing is not easy nor is class balancing. Someone on these boards mentioned the monocromatic vision of many posters and I agree 100%. Shaman balancing is not as easy as saying that because our slows are mitigated 40% that our healing should be increased 40%. That ignores Sony's issue of content balancing.

Healing: It is my strong opinion that Sony does not want to provide shaman with the healing tools to be a primary healer in newer more difficult content. Shaman healing spells speak for themselves on this point. But instead of just throwing out random #'s (like in the polls), what we really are asking for is to be able to have a cleric or DRUID be a main healer and for us to be able to spot heal to a degree that the content is still balanced and we are able to provide enough utility and damage outside of that healing to be balanced as a class. There is no magic number until Sony decides what to do with Druids, IMHO.

Damage: I think that it is easier to balance healing from a overall content standpoint prior to balancing shaman direct damage (if appropriate) - and I guess I need to expand on that. If shaman healing is pumped up significantly then we may be class balanced without any changes to DPS. My only comment here is that the casting duration of our direct damage spells is too long for a utility class who is casting malos and slow on every mob and doing all the other shaman things as well.

I respectfully submit that our wish list should start with items that are not related very much to content balance nor class balance to a large degree. These items which unless I am sadly mistaken would be simple to fix, not overpowering and are the right thing to do. We need to totally separate the below items from content and class balance to get a possible quick fix, then push Sony to re-tune healing (which it appears they are doing) and lastly to take a last look at shaman class balance which includes utility and direct and indirect DPS.

1) Make ancient canny non-ancient since it is a class defining spell unlike the simple upgrades of other classes. It's like wizard's having a very low level harvest and then making the next upgrade ancient. It just makes very little sense. A far more reasonable ancient spell for Shaman would be a better slow - maybe 5% better than the non-ancient GoD slow or with a long duration or both.

2) Increase the duration of FA (and champion) and provide a wear's off message.

3) Make puppy able to duel wield without weapons or give us a spell to summon the weapons.

4) Figure out how to provide out of group shrink with confirmation.

5) Adjust Rabid bear (and upgrades) to be worth purchasing by tuning it against the damage output that we can put out via spells. That's all most of us Shaman want.

Please feel free to comment and if I left out stuff I apologize.

Respectfully yours,

Purdypog
