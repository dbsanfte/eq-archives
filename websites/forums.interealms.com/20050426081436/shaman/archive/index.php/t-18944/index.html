Korelen
When I was that level, I hunted in DSP. I averaged a level per session ( usually 3 hours or so ) when I was in a group. We usually killed anything near ME or GE zone-lines. XP started to really slow down in the 40s around this area, and I moved onto TM to kill bats and sonic wolves. GF zone-line is an awesome camp spot in your 40s. I averaged a level per kei, and I usually killed the Runner a few times for Rallic Pack. At the time, I would sell them for about 1k - 1k5. With the buff nerf, I do not know if this is a viable spot to camp.

Also, you might want to try CoM. It is a good zone, and you can live there for a while as well as work on your epic drops.
