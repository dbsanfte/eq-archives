Grippa
You don't say whether you are soloing or grouped ....

Chardok is indeed nice, and with a group you can do the gate area, or possibly beyond, depends how good the group is.

The Hole entrance, I was soloing at about level 49, and I could do the first 5 or 6 elementals. That was before I got my epic, at 55 you might even be able to solo up to the first golem. With a decent group, including a chanter, you can go beyond the drop and get super fat xp :)

Akheva Ruins, you can solo the entrance, but watch out for adds that path almost to the zone line, and also watch our for the transient patrollers, they are necro casters and very nasty. Again, with a reasonable group you can go in a bit, the first room with the fallen pillar in it is a nice spot to camp and rake in the kills.

You could possibly try and get into a Cazic Thule group - in fact I was there last night but I'll be the first to admit 55 is still a little low. One resisted slow can get you pounded down very fast. Soloing is not an option in this zone.
