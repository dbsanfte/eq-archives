Michael J. Kaspar
Two groups, with some individuals under 55, would be tricky. The main problem you will have is agro in the city. Lower level folks will auto agro mobs, and in the city with its funky pathing this can be death to a raid.

I would recommend you do this:

1. Find other characters that need to go there as well. For example, the Enchanter mob (that needs to be killed) is on the same floor as Kirn, so don't just go there for yourself. Go there for a few different folks and you might get better participation.

2. If you choose to go with two folks, make sure you have at leave a couple higher level tanks, as well as a 60 enchanter. Highest level pulls. Monk or SK would be perfect, but as long as someone pulls that understands the pathing, as well as the route the raid needs to take through the city, you should be ok. I pulled city as a shaman (at 60) and it worked fine.

3. Prior to entering the city, and after departing the city on the way down to undead tower, if people go AFK / LD its not a huge problem because the route is easy. From the point when you start pulls into the city to the point you conclude pulls on the back end of the city, AFK's / LD's will kill you. You need to be able to kill approximately 15 mobs to make it to the first safe spot, then another dozen or so to get to the docks. From there, its about 20 mobs on the way out the back side, and you can do that in two phases with a move in between. Respawn is fast, so if you can't kill enough to make it to the next safespot, you going to be in trouble.

4. CoTH is a valid option, however I don't know if sub 60's will agro at the CoTH point. If they do, you could get a horde of undead coming to play with you.

One final point... on EMarr there were several raids in a row that did either of the following:
- left Kirn's pet up, thus preventing Kirn from spawning
- screwed up Kirn, thus leaving him agro

Make sure after you do Kirn you do his pet. Furthermore, if Kirn is agro prior to you turning in the scale, you will need another way to get faction to go on to the next step.

Good luck and good hunting :)

Dagor
If you come across a Kirn and he is already agro. I would kill him without handing in the icon, bite the bullet chill out for two hours, and do him in the normal fashion. You get your faction from turning in the icon, so YOU NEED to turn it in. I believe when you turn in the icon, that Kirn pops, and a new Agro Kirn spawns immediately. So if Kirn is initially agro, you likely are dealing with the Agro version, and not the one who will give you faction for turning in the icon.

One thing to note is that the wizzard epic is also based on the Truescale faction...except they get negative faction hits from their epic quest. So If you have any epic or near epic wizzards with you, Kirn may very well agro on them from even a pretty good distance(through the wall in the hallway). If you experience this kind of agro from Kirn you can proceed as normal once he has been mezzed/charmed/walked back to his room/blurred. This happened on my trip to the hole, and once he was back in position and turned in the scale...and all went well.
