Kishin
05-14-2002, 02:09 AM
you are right, hajime...
primary and offhand are checked by two entirely different delay timers. if your two weapons have equal delays, you will see a quad as four immediately consecutive swings, but there will be a discrepancy if your weapons have different delays.
can't remember the exact ratio of weapon delay to real time, but if 10dly = 1.0sec, and i'm wielding a lammy and centi LS with no haste, my swings will look like this:
0 sec - i hit autoattack
1.9 - swing with lammy, check for dbl atk
2.7 - check for LS, if successful check for dbl atk
2.8 - swing with lammy, check for dbl atk
4.7 - lammy
5.4 - LS

you get the idea. swings will seem close most of the time, but the checks will only actually land together every 51.3 sec.

we put the faster dmg weapon in the primary because of the primary hand dmg bonus, and we put the better ratio in our offhand due to the lack thereof.
