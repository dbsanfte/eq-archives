So there I was, having trouble not only dialing in to my ISP (Earthsuck - DON'T ASK) but staying on the Internet for more than about 2 minutes. Called said crappy ISP and both numbers were "good."

Finally got ahold of Sprint and they advised me to unplug the phone jack, the computer, anything attached to computer (like my answering machine which I think was the culprit) and let it all sit for 10 minutes.

So with much trepidation and derision ("Unplug everything? C'mon" /snort) I did.

And lo and behold it worked. Their diagnosis was "static buildup in the line" and apparently this cleared it up.

Just thought I'd share.
