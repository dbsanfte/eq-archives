Was 65 so I don't know for sure what it's like for level 50 type folks in GoD zones, but I don't think we can forget how important resists are. I've soloed a number of nameds in GoD, but being 65 has a lot to do with that.

If you are under 60, you don't have malos or malo. That's BIG. Malosinia is resistable, and if it gets resisted much, you can get in real trouble real fast. Take your mid-50's guy and have him try to get malosinia, root, and slow on mobs, or make his DOTs or DD's land and land for more or less full, and you may be asking for stuff that just isn't going to happen. Heck even ONE level can make all the difference when it comes to resists -- and even stuff quite a few levels BELOW you can resist a lot.

So, if a level 55 or even a 59 says he's having trouble, I believe it.

And that's just the spells HE casts being resisted. What about the spells the monsters cast?

For instance, some mobs in GoD try to snare you. At 65, with pretty good disease resists(spell/augs/special DR armor), I resisted most but not all of it. I would be surprised if a level 55, by contrast, didn't get hit by almost all of it. Last thing you need is getting snared and trying to run away from a mob that hits for 600's and seems to resist all your roots or only have them hold for a few seconds.

I've beat these mobs, even some nameds, most of the time at 65, but I'm not 55. I tend to believe what the lower level guys are saying about the difficulty.
