Zyphun
My secret to leveling is that I do nothing but level. I kind of split up my time, figure out what I want to accomplish and work at that till it is done. I didn't take time to do quests except the epic that took all of a night, I am still without it because I haven't taken the time to go pick up a tear from Fear, and more importantly I didn't join a guild. Well that’s not true I did waste some time I mean, I do like to spend some time just chatting and I spent most of a week doing that and playing with a new friend I'd made and didn't worry about XP.

As to exactly how I leveled so fast, well I spent a lot of time in my early 50's in City of Mist at the gazebos and in Dulak on the boats. I moved to PoDisease and into mostly left courtyard groups when I out grew Dulak at about 55. At 58 I got flagged for PoValor, which by the way, was the weekend before it opened up. LOL I spent 14 straight hours in a group that Saturday afternoon/night in caves just off of zone-in in PoValor (it's called the Inner Camp on the Povar server). Spending time like that is not something I usually do, but I used to have fun pulling all nighters so I decided I've do it again for old times sake. During those 14 hours I made 59, 60, and 61. I spent a lot of time after hunting in PoValor (random camps) and in BoThunder. I joined a guild at 63 because they were doing PoPower back flagging the week after I joined and I didn't want to miss it. I had planned on waiting to join a guild until I was 65, but my hand was forced. ^_^ That slowed my leveling down a lot and it took me two weeks to get from 62 to 64, but I think it was well worth it. When I was 90% into 64 I got my last flag (the Village People trial in HoHonor no less) for the first three elementals and dinged 65 in the PoEarth. Oh another thing is I didn't take any time to work on AAs at all. All 7 of my current AAs are from the flagging and talking to the dwarf in PoK. I didn't actually move my AAXP slider bar over to getting AAXP until I got 20% into 65 last night.

LOL And no I am not in Triton. ^_^ The whole reason for me playing again is because my roommate wanted someone to play with again and he's in Forces Unknown. I actually picked being a shaman as a class to play because that was what it looked like FU needed at the time. I made him a frog because I wanted to be unique.

As to who I played before, At the time I left just after PoPower, I had a level 61 cleric, 54 bard, 52 paladin and a 51 druid, my druid being my character with the most days played at 200+. I left EQ the first time because at the time was frustrated with the direction EverQuest was taking and I knew I'd go postal if I had to do the PoJustice trial again to get people through into PoStorms/PoValor. Things have changed, I’ve changed and EQ is fun again.

Krakster is right about it being easy. Anyone that really wanted to could do what I did I think. Though honestly I did just about the same thing with my Paladin back about two years ago. A little over a month and I think like 6 days played to get them to 52. Now though with all the gear that's so easily available and XP being handed to you on a silver tray the game is almost unpleasantly easy. Luckily I find other things in the game to enjoy. ^_^

BTW I am by no means the fastest leveling person I know. Personally I think I to long. I have a friend who took a warrior from 1 to 65 in about 14 days played during the same time I was leveling. He's now got 25 AAs and is decently equipped as well as being elemental flagged (it's his main and one and only character). Of course he was PL'd and botted a lot so I guess I can rub that in when he brings it up. ;-)
