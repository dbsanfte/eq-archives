Oh, Veksar, good idea. Shaman can bind the pally there, too. I was going to say Halls of Betrayal for the same reason (undead) but Veksar will work just as well, and it's a LOT easier to get to. Just remember, if you crawl all the way to the courtyard (which is very likely) if it's just going to be the two of you - keep an eye to the wall that adjoins the zonein area. Avoided a handful of corpse summons by dying next to that wall, and simply dragging through it.

Exp bonus in both zones is pretty extreme. Loot in HoB is a bit better, though. If you're a faction freak, HoB will help with Chardok, Veksar (weapon turnin quest) will get you into Cabilis.

I can't think of any other places that'll make as much use of the pally's benefits toward undead.
