Kensei
Scarlett Desert isn't bad. I killed a LOT of Bloodtribe there in my low to mid-50s, with a druid healer. The named have decent drops, and as long as your gear isn't hopeless you should do OK (can't check Magelo, I use Netscape). I assume you have a way to slow them, if not get a Swarmcaller and head there.

Velk's Spiders aren't a good option for you yet. While you can probably take one, you are too low level to single pull them so if there is another one in the area you're screwed. They are also simply not available very often.

The entrance to the Hole is a good idea, IF it is open. You can certainly do the front 6 with that combo, and should be able to single pull them. Might be able to take the roamer too, but the double spawn (warrior/crusader) will probably be a bit much, and those things don't root worth a damn.

Have you considered Old World dungeons? SolB bats would be great for you, as would LGuk dead side. Stuff there is wimpy compared with most of the stuff we fight these days, but still gives full dungeon exp.
