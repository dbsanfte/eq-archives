Time for me to make my plug for dungeons again

the xp modifier in th dungeons make them the best place for xp hands down. At 22 with good gear I would guess that runneyeye will give some great xp, as will najena, warrens will be greening out, but plenty of blues and yellows to keep you busy for a while.

If you are really set on fear-kiting, EF is the way to go, hunt mammoths and if its too crowded get a group together to kill redwind and Co. that is good xp for your level and a harmony on redwind allows you to single pull.

Most of these mobs have lower hp than mammoths or most animals and hit for a fraction of the damage. Grouping is not so easy and fear-kiting is impossible, but the xp is unparalelled.
