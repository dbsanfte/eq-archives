Well you have to choose your buffs according to what fits the situation best. If you're tanking and need the AC, then use CoE. If your fear kiting or not tanking, then you want the ATK, so use FoN.

Use Nature's Precision until you get level 51 and Strength of Nature, they both will stack with CoE, FoN, and wolf form. Then use that all the time instead of NP until you get 60 and Warders Protection. At that point you have to redo your buff stacking again.

Here's how I'd do it:

NP/SoN = always

FoN = when fear kiting, or not tanking.
or
CoE = When tanking or getting hit, or tired of buffing with FoN all the time.

SoW = always
or
Wolf form or Greater Wolf form = when faction issues allow and its outdoors.

See, it's easy. :)
