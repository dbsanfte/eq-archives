Panteroo
pre-PoP Chardok was IMHO, the PREMIER experience zone in the game. Ranging in ability areas from a low 50's group to a full 60's group to a full raid force. I have spent hundreds of hours in chardok and everything a single group could kill (including a single group kill of the Chardok King recently). I went there for AA points prior to pop and with a solid group of 60's we could get 1 aa point in just a hair over 2 hours.

The dogs are animals and will sometimes see thru invis. Best bet would be to grab a healer type for duo action. With a like similiar leveled healer you could likely duo zone in, zone out, entrance fort (possible exception of the named in the top left *turret* ), and the lev tunnel all the way down to the Captain spawn door. Any one of those areas will keep you and a duo partner in constant supply of mobs.

If you have a full group of mid 50's you should look into the Bridge Keeper/Captain Area and the Observer/Bridge area.

A full group of highish 50's should head directly to the mines to either the bank or the Foreman/Collaborator area. Be forewarned, mobs are higher level and respawn faster here. Crowd control and slow will be necessary. Can also do the Graveyard and initial Royal Arch areas.

If you ahve a group of 60's the deep mines are the place to be. The bank/waterfall area is fun, Overseer/Underboss is fast paced with some good loots, the Herbalist house is a pathing nightmare and should only be considered if you have serious quality people. My favorite tho is the Kennels. 2 named mobs there with fast respawns, tons of mobs and the likelyhood that you can and will die at any second. Very nice loots too.

It was not uncommon for my group to leave after a 4-5 hour night there (at the kennels) with about 1.5k in coin for everybody AND 5-10 loots that could occassionally top 80-100k in value. This is a constant barrage of mobs with a high dps group tho... things like not paying attention (1 runner = wipe) and afk will kill you np.

good luck
