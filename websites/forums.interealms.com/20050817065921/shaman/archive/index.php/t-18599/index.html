Our level 29 invis is completely random meaning that it can drop at any time from the moment it is cast. It is NOT inherently inferior to any other targetable invis spell including camo and sup camo (even the group version). It's also the same spell wizards, enchanters and magicians recieve at level 8 prior to the 10 minute set duration invis they get in the 50s. The necromancer level 8 Gather Shadows is also random duration with Skin like Shadow being a 20+ minute set invis (with an annoying zoom feature) at level 56. It used to be nightime cast only until Luclin was released with it's lack of day/night cycles which warranted the change to the spell. They also have a set duration ITU upgrade as well as a 7 second refresh insta-click ITU trainable in the Class AA set while wizzies and enchanters have random duration ITU.

The only other classes that never recieve a set duration invis (that have invis as a castable spell) are druid, ranger and beastlord (afaik) though both druids and rangers can dump AA points into an instant click invis hotkey with a 7 second refresh timer.

With that said, I do agree there are more important issues to be focused on then our invis spell. Carrying a couple instant invis potions will remedy just about any emergency invis situation, even if it is a pain in the rear to maintain stock and space for them.
