Gavving
Doing epics is all about doing favors. You have to do favors for (usually their epic stuff), to expect them to do favors back. This works good in a guild where you're all at about the same level in the game. But if you join a guild where everyone already has their epic, it can be difficult to get anyone to help you.

I always viewed epics as a bank account. You have to make deposits if your going to make withdrawls. Thus you need to have people that you can build sweat equity with, and that requires you to get to know and get chummy with people. Its alot easier to do that if your in a guild, and much more difficult to do it if your a loner.

Your getting to the stage in the game where you have to get more than 1 group's help to advance in the game significantly. I wish you good luck. :)
