MMshaman
Loy will probably be your best option for pling. find one with around the level of your shaman, there are lists all over the net of the new ZEMs and level ranges. I would try and choose one that is barely dark blue to the shaman, ~level 35-40 mobs i would assume, rather than level 40-40 mobs. I have found in PLing my shaman that it is much faster with lower mobs.

As for what to use, i would have the cleric and shaman grouped, and the rogue doing the work. Although it is a little bit less exp per kill, overall you are leveling two chars at once, and its always nice to have a cleric that is higher than the group (56 they get 96% rez too), so you are doing the same amount of work but getting more overall exp. Once the cleric is 56, i would drop him from the group and focus on the sham.

Nurga and Droga were really quick exp for my shaman as i PLed him from the 40's to 60's, using any mix of 65 warrior/rogue/mage/cleric/bard/wiz/ranger.

Good luck, and happy hunting.
