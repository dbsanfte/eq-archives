Spiders gone. Burnyai gone. Sleeping Ogre is actually sleeping.

Straight Gobbos.

Stalagknights are SK's. They HT for 321-341.
Flashdrowsers are Enchanters.
Slavedrivers (with whip) are fighter types.

There are others, can't remember atm.

They will assist each other if close enough.

The ore you find (10 pounds) is for Death Steel Weapons (130-150 smithing range iirc)

The Mt Death Brew can be consumed for AT increases OR used with Mt Death Salts for making tempers or the forged sheets of metal.

Mt Death Salts (Blue, stackable, triangular) and Gobbo skins are used in Chardok Faction quest.

Gobbo Warder drops the Shroud of Nature (Ac 13, Wis + 6, Sv Fire / Cold + 8).

RARELY you get a drop of some armor. "Goblin Forged" or something like that. Not sure what it is used for.
