So... you're in ssra right now. If you're not, and you don't know how to get there, this guide isn't for you. The snakes down here aren't seb frogs; theyhit for 270 and cast sunstrike and stuff. You're probably wondering why the hell I'd want to exp on things like that. Well, here's why. They're weak and the experience is amazing. Two kills = blue and then some. But how do you get to them? Follow the instructions below.

1) from ssra zone line, go through the center door on the lower level.

2) straight until a T

3) left

4) go right, through a room with 8 snakes

5) go right again

6) you'll be at a door that has a tube after it. Go through it, to the basement.

7) Now, run to the temple at the back of the room (east), and up that ramp

8) you'll see a bunch of caster snakes are probably taskmasters too. But they're not named taskmasters.

9) slay

They cast dispell, sunstrike, pyrocuror, and all that other stuff... but like I said, the experience is really amazing. Have fun or something.

Kylt
Xp here is very very good. Couple other things to note. I call the area the temple so...

Pathing on the temple is buggy as hell. Your pet will *instantly* die if it moves on the 2nd floor of the temple so if you choose to use one, it's better to pull the mobs down the middle ramp from the 2nd floor to the main basement floor. These mobs do gate, so the downside of not fighting them in place is that you'll be petting up to replace your summoned and thus insta-poofed pet occasionally.

Now, if you want to pull down the ramp, it's a *real* good idea to kill the roaming Taskmaster. He's worth 6 percent alt xp so if you can kill him, kill him :P He's a caster so not overly difficult, the beginning of the fight he'll chain sunstrike/root/whatever else he feels like so even if your slow gets resisted you're ok for a reslow. That said it's a good idea to pull him to his spawn spot, as he gates too - his spawn spot is the archway on the... um non-mines side of the basement (west? east?).

Disciples cheal and gate. Acolytes gate. Defilers gate. The nameds Prime Educator and... Arch Tormentor? are immune to poison and disease so it's epic/melee/cold for those.
