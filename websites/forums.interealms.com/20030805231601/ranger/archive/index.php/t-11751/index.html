TheRiov
As far as I'm concerned this is >THE< best spell to pull with in a raid situation. (except situationally snare) In a raid you're not going to be main tank usually. Snare holds a lot of aggro with it and even a first bowshot tends to get you hit a few times. Jolt places you on the mobs Hate list, but at negative 500. Anything else anyone does to aggro the mob will automatically add them to the hate list above you. It allows your MT to immeidatly get aggro from you and lets you swing right around and be ready to engage.

Any other spell you use-- from Cancel magic, to snare, or a DD spell or even a bowshot grants you positive hate values. It may take the MT a few moments to get that off of you. Jolt on the other hand means that ANY agressive action taken against the mob means the mob will turn on the tank.
