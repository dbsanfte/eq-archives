Cragael
Without even going far from the guidelines of what SOE has already implemented as AA, Focus effects and so on...

1- Quick Debuff - 3/6/9 AA costs, 10%/25%/50% spell haste on all detrimental spells with a duration (i.e. Mal*, Cripple, Slows and DoTs)

2- Cursing Adept - 2/4/6 AA costs, 10%/20%/30% damage increase on DoT per tick.

3- Cursing Gift - 2/4/6 AA costs, 2%/5%/10% of a 33%/66%/100% crit on any given DoT tick.

4- Study of Decay - 3/3/3/3/3 AA cost, Cursing Gift 3 pre-requisite, increase chances of crit to 12%/14/%/16%/18%/20%.

5- Insight - 3/3/3/3/3 AA cost, each of the first 4 levels bought allow you to raise one more specialization above 50. 5th level allows you to raise all specializations to 250.

6- Will of Druzzil - 2/2/2/2/2 AA costs. Each level gives an additional -5 resist adjustment to each Magic Based spell cast.

7- Breath of Bertoxxolous - 2/2/2/2/2 AA costs. Same as Will of Druzzil, but for Disease based spells.

8- Saryrn's Touch - 2/2/2/2/2 AA costs. Same as above but for Poison based spells.

9- Voice of Ec'i- 2/2/2/2/2 AA cost. Same as above but for Cold based spells.

(You could probably make a fire based one too, though it wouldn't be of interest to shamans)

10- Rabid Pet - 3/3/3 AA costs. Applies to True Spirit pet. First level is innate DW without weapons. Second level is a 50% HP increase. Third level is a Rabid Bite proc (Disease based, 25 damage proc, stun(0.0/65), -50 Disease Resist adjustment)

Now, that's the kind of AA they should give us (and druids, and necros). Not that derivative nuker/healer crap.
