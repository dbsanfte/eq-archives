But, there are a million different ways that SOE could have provided the same effect without the annoyance value of panther. There is just ZERO reason to make a 1 minute duration buff. Give us 1 meaningfull, VISIBLE, long duration melee buff (something like the visible +crit enchanter haste), and a faster casting nuke or shorter duration higher damage dot (with negative resist checks), and you accomplish the exact same thing without changing the role of the shaman this dramatically.

Shaman Role 1
The role of the shaman used to be:
1. Debuffing
2. Healing
3. Damage dealing
4. Buffing

Shaman Role 2
The role of the shaman now is:
1. Debuffing
2. Healing
3. Buffing

Panther is so much better at dealing damage in a groups there is ZERO reason to cast our dots or nukes really. While I've never argued about the value of the spell, I still hold firm to my believe that SOE could have let us retain our 4 original roles (as shown in "Role 1" above), while at the same time changing our existing spells to accomplish the same thing that panther does.

1. We are the only (pure) casting cast without negative resist checks. Why is that?
2. We have the shortest duration and most complicated buff sets (and least visible effects.. enc crits, etc) of any buffing class. Why is that?

Instead of addressing the two above items, SOE gave us panther.
