Neh is pretty much like Ghiosk. A souped up reaver. Procs blind, can't land spells, etc etc.

Of course, you need a rogue, for the locked door. Hrm, can't think of much else that you have to have.

You could do Neh with one group. I prefer two though. Enough people to make it easier, not to many to get cramped in the confined spaces.

You probably want to lev people before you start climbing the ramp up to the upper level. Some how, some way, people always find a way to fall off.

Really, the trip to Neh is harder than the Neh fight. You can get a lot of adds if you arent careful.

Let me get this straight in order to get to Neh you have to go through the same locked door that Rak is behind where the Reavers are correct? If so which way past the the Door past Rak do I need to go. Left as in away from the lift or right?
Grippa
Go through the front door of the 'castle' (into the room where Rak's reaver spawns). Then go though either one of the doors at the back of the room and turn right. Go along that corridor, turning right in the small room, then up a slope. There's a room directly ahead of you but on your right is a secret door that leads to a transparent ramp. This ramp leads to the 'sky houses', and if you follow it all the way you'll get to Neh Ashir.
