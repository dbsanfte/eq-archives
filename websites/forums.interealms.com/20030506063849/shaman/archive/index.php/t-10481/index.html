I've never owned a JBB so I can't relate on that level. Typical solo fight though is:

1. Pull with slow or malosini first then slow on incoming, depends on mob's resists.
2. Sick alpha pet on mob and root so I can sit down to med if inside.
3. Let pet build up some aggro and BoN. If root breaks and you get aggro then reapply root. Malosini if not already on high PR mobs.
4. Med while pet and dot kill mob, reapply dot if needed.
5. /yawn and pull another :)

I also turn off horse and luclin graphics, can't stand them anyway. With no horse graphic you can also duck to interrupt spellcasting easily.

What sort of "high end" are we talking here? I can't efficiently tank the mobs in Tactics when I solo, so I've gotta fall back on Root/Dotting methods.

I usually pull with Malo, run it back to my camp, then cast: Petrifying Earth, Malosinia, Turgur's, Insid Decay, Breath of Ultor, Epic, Blood of Saryn, re-apply Root. Rinse and repeat the dots and roots until it's dead, Cann5/Torpor/KM as you need to.

Works well until you encounter one of the mitigating Death Knights that resists one too many roots.

Pre-60 I'd usually just pull with Turgur's and use the epic + dog to do the damage. Most mobs I could out-regen, or end up taking very little damage in the end. Usually hasted myself and the dog and worked up some melee skills while I was at it, for something to do.
