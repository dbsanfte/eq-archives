I'm sure most you probably already know this, but I thought I would post it anyway since I saw someone claim Extended Affliction was useless to a high level shaman.

Not only DoTs are affected by Extended Affliction. My Coldain Military Wristguard 'glows' when I cast slow, root, Malo, etc. Pretty much any debuff with a duration is considered Affliction. Slows, roots, Malos are all very important. Who wouldn't want them to last 20% longer? Anyone ever wipe on a raid because Slow wore off at the wrong time and you couldn't get another to stick in time?

All in all, EA3 is a boon to some of the Shaman's best skills. I would suggest that all shaman actively seek both Extended Affliction and Extended Enhancement items.

Affliction Efficiency III

1: Decrease Spell Mana Cost by 25%
2: Limit: Max Level(60)
3: Limit: Spell Type(Detrimental only)
4: Limit: Min Duration(36.0 sec)
5: Limit: Effect(Hitpoints allowed)

Extended Affliction III

1: Increase Spell Duration by 20%
2: Limit: Max Level(60)
3: Limit: Spell Type(Detrimental only)
4: Limit: Min Duration(36.0 sec)
5: Limit: Effect(Hitpoints allowed)

So: Yes, it increases the duration by 20% and yes, it's supposed to only work on DoT's.
