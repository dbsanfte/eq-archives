Improved Nukes = Something to do when I am on a raid where the mobs die quickly. They may not be a great help, but if I can drop 3k of damage on a mob in 30 seconds, thats not bad. I doubt anyone will complain, anyway.

Improved DoTs = Great. Extremely great. I want them. I would much rather stack 3 dots instead of two dots.

I'm a little annoyed about the focus 3 items being outmoded by focus 4 items, but i heard that pottery skill can be used to make focus 4 items and thats not too bad at all. We'll have to wait and see on that one.

In general, I'd say that our spells are ok. We didn't get rewarded with uber-spells like some classes, but we didn't get the shaft either.
