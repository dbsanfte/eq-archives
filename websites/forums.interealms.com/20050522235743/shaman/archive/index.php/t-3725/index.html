Jinheim
Make sure that you have torpor cast at all times. If you are starting to overflow with hp, cannibalize. If torpor can't keep up, throw yourself a chloroblast. When you are a shaman, the fight is not about quickly killing your opponent. It is about outlasting them. If you are going out of mana from chloroblasting yourself and trying to keep dots up on yeldema, forget about the dots. Just use epic and JBB, he'll die eventually. When Nixx first soloed him, I think it took him 26 minutes or something to finally kill the big wussy.

That being said, get your AC and HP up as high as possible before the fight. With 1060ac and 2400ish HP self-buffed, I can just laugh at him. Voice of the Berserker and its 40ac is great. I've been in love with that spell ever since I got it.

Also, after many many WW raids without malo, I have learned that the best way to get slow to stick is to stand directly under the dragon and chain cast malosi until it sticks, and then cast turgur's. However, this is done with someone else tanking the mob. Your best bet for yeldema (i assume you do not have malo from your post) is to try and pull with slow. Just kite him around and keep trying until it sticks. He won't summon until you damage him.

Rooting him won't work. If he is damaged at all, he will summon.

Pet-tanking won't work, especially if you dotted him. When I am exping, my pet cannot hold aggro over slow + jbb + epic. Even if he could hold aggro, there is no way he could tank a dragon. Our pets are big sissy's. Have him out for the fight, though. The extra damage he does will help a bit.
