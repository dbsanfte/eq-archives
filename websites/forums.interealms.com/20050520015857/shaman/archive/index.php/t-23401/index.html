Einnod
lately, dispells seem to be a little buggy, but I'll tell you my experiences with them.

Cancel Magic: This spell will(sometimes) take off 1 buff, and only 1. The buff it takes is random, and most of the time it takes nothing off at high levels. I believe that this dispell takes off counters and not actually buffs, but I can't in any way confirm this.

Nullify Magic: In my experience, Nullify will always take the top buff and 1 random buff. It will also take off negative effects. Use this one in pvp on other people, it will take off buffs they have buried occasionally.

Annul Magic: This is the best of our dispells for PvE and (defensive) PvP. This spell will always take off the first 2 buffs/debuffs that you have on. Use this when fighting necros/druids, or any other class that will debuff/dot you. I've noticed this spell being somewhat buggy as of late, with it skipping over the first buff/debuff that I have on and going to the second one. I think this has to do with the fact that EQ is not recognizing that I clicked my buff back on because I did it too fast. Again, I can't confirm it, but I believe that's where my problem is at.
