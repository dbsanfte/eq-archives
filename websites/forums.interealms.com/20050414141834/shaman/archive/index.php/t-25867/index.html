Missions are about 5000 times better than LDON. They use instanced versions of regular zones of course, and they have fun things to do. One of the missions is to plant a type of poison bombs in strategic locations around the thundercrest isles to kill off the inhabitants. Once you get to the last spot, you have 20 minutes to set the bomb and get out of the zone or you get killed by the poison AE. I did a lavaspinners instance last night where you had to kill 15 each of 3 types of mobs, and loot 7 each of 3 different items to complete the mission. You get about 4% AA exp per kill at 70, so it's much better than ldon and much easier. Mobs hit for around 680 or so in the lavaspinners instance since it's one of the easier ones, the harder ones I assume go up by around 200dmg max hits. I'm going to be spending all my time doing these because they are more fun than regular exping and just as good of exp.
