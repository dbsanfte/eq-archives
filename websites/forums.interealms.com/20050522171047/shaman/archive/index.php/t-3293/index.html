we don't have any PBAE(point blank) spells. We have targetted AE spells. Targetted AE spells hit 4 targets, plus yourself(if you are in range).

infectious cloud, torrent of poison, tirgirs insects, and poison storm are our AE spells. They are all targetted AE spells.

I tried them all when I was doing my own chardok faction.

I wish I was wrong, if so, please tell me the name of the shaman spell that is PBAE.

The best method I found for factioning in an AE grp was to have a bard puller. When they come back with many goblins, use the spell "Fleeting Fury" on them, click it over and over while the mobs get incinerated. If fleeting fury is flashing on the bard while his/her song pulses then you get faction. Healing doesn't give you faction in droga.

Also pulling for an AE grp is a decent way to get faction.

A less efficient way is to get say a ranger/monk/rogue. Have then pull 4 or so at a time and use one of our targetted ae spells, like infectious cloud, to tag all mobs at once for faction.
