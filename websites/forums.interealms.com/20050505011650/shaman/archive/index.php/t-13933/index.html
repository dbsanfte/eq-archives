Litchebane
I don't know where you are getting the extra 20%, SCRM is 50% total because it's really SCRM (20%) + SCR3 (30%) but yes the shawl effect will stack. So you get 70% total extension with that combination, subject to the restrictions of that specific focus effect. (spells have to be minimum 42 seconds or something like that) Focus effects do not stack so picking up another buff extension focus item won't extend the duration.
Jaruden
70% is the top you can get with shawl + scrm (and I love it, FA lasts forever). 50% of it (the scrm part) will work on torpor/Q, because it works on all buffs, but your shawl will only work on buffs over a certain duration.
