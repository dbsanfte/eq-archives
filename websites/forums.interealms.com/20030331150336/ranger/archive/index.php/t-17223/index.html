Does anyone know if the spells tangling weeds and snare stacks? i doubt it. So what is the point in having both if snare reduces the mobs speed more?
Elladan
The spells do not stack.

Tangling Weeds has two uses. Quick agro, and farming greens. Quick agro is kind of self-explanitory. When farming greens, having a quick cast snare is wonderful. It slows down the fleeing mob, doesn't take much mana, and of course, casts quickly.

Snare is for when you want something to stay slow for longer. Kiting, stopping fleeling mobs in a group, etc.
