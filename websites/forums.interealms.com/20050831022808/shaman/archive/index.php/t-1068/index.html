Isazo
For me, EQ is entertainment. It is about fun. Sure some grinds are boring, some raids tedious. In general though, it is fun.

Krytog's argument for the statis quo is an interesting counterpoint. for me that point of view is "anti-fun". I sincerely believe that when the Warrior who looted the earring clipped it to his virtual ear the real person felt a rush -- like "Hey, I got something cool, I'm better at XXX than I was before".

My point is this: FOR SHAMAN there is no such item that is realistically capable of making us significantly better at XXX than we were before.

Define XXX however you want:

1.) No category of items (JBB, epic excepted) that improve out DPS.
2.) No category of items that improve our slow spells percentage
3.) No items that increase the amount of STR given on a buff from 55 to 62 or whatever
4.) No category of items that 'automatically tash' the mob so our spells stick faster/more frequently
5.) Etc

YES we are a support class. DPS is an easy way to make a comparison, but the fact that our primary role is not DPS DOES NOT invalidate the fact that in terms of equipment it boils down things that are not 'core' strengths of shaman -- ie, I can tank a little better (certainly necessary, but not our primary or even secondary role) or Mana pool depth -- I'll grant that mana pool depth is nice, but any Cann V shaman will not be the mana-limiting factor for any group or raid.

My point is this: There are no items in the game for shaman that can make us 41% better at our role, no matter what you define that role to be (DPS, Buffs, debuffs, etc). I think in the name of FUN having equipment upgrades that actually DO SOMETHING for shaman in particular and casters in general is long over due and would be widely welcomed.

I recognize that making casters materially better at their roles (however you define them) may result in an untuned game. For that VI has nobody to blame but themselves.

Another way to state the same argument: For non-Aoe encounters, the 'third shaman' on a raid (responsible for buffs not debuffs) can do the job materially as well NUDE as a pimped out, equipped shaman. The third warrior on a raid that shows up Nude gets sent home -- his equipment DOES something for him.

Just my thoughts.

-Isazo
