For all your spell information needs. All the data is taken straight from the game files, so it will tell you exactly what a spell does. As for the turn ins....

SoT and CotR are the only level 62 spells we need to do turn ins for with Etheriel parchment turn in.

Level 63 and 64 we have Spirit of the Predator, Brushfire, Frozen Winds, Nature's Rebuke, and Earthen roots which we get from spectral parchment turn ins. Note that if you turn in an Etheriel parchment to the druid spell quest guy you have a chance at Earthen roots as well.

And lastly we have two level 65 spells, Cry of Thunder and PotW. PotW does exist, at least two rangers on the glade have it. But how many level 65 spells have you seen cast yet from ANY class? Anyone had Voice of Quellious cast on them yet (C5)? Very few people indeed.
