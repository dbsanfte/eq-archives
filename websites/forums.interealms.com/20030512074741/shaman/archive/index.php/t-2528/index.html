Like has been stated time and again. In the 40's forget about DL. It is like lambs to the slaughter in that zone. The lag is terrible and the crowds are ungodly. However there is a nice quiet spot to level from 40-50 or so if you really push it. I am talking about Echo Caverns underbulks and other assorted pests. They are super easy to fight if you are duoing and only slightly more challenging when soloing. As a duo I can clear most of the bugs and bulks before the next repop starts. The loot is pretty decent as well. The underbulks drop a nice throwing item that you can either sell to rogues or sell to a vendor for 3pp. And the stonegrabbers drop anywhere from 4pp-8pp and change. Not a bad place to spend your 40's if you don't like the idiots that inhabit DL.
