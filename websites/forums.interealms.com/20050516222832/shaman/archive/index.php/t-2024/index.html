Bigguss
Ideally you should try to keep all of your weapon skills (1hb, 2hb, piercing) maxed, but if you have to choose, the generally accepted order of preference is first Piercing, then 2hb, then 1hb. The logic behind this is that there are many generally available piercers up to and including our epic weapon, which is a spear. Two hand blunt is nice for maximizing damage output, and 1hb has taken a back seat because there historically haven't been as many good 1hb weapons (with Luclin this changed a bit.)

I started a long time ago (before we could even use spears) and trained 1hb pretty much exclusively- I finally got my piercing skill up when I hit level 30 or so (probably right after I got my pet), and my 2hb skill is still in the cellar.

Honestly, I don't think it matters all that much. Once you get above the 30s, it becomes increasingly difficult to do significant damage with a weapon- spell damage becomes more effective compared to melee the higher you go. If I would have stuck with 1hb (never would have trained piercing) I would have been fine.

Given that you're on FV (and good weapons are going to be harder to come by) your best bet might be to just train with what you can get. :)

Well, if you're gonna train in 1hb, then you should definitely get a shield. Also, if you use spears (most are 1h piercers), equip a shield. Clay guardian shields are nice for ac/hp. Charred guardian shields are good for wis. Both shields are cheap. Try to max out all of your weapons skills, as the others noted. Take enough hits to keep defense maxxed and you'll be good to go. Spells do make up most of your damage output at higher levels, but melee damage will play a significant role throughout your career, or at least into your mid-high 40s. Try to keep your eyes open for a good deal on a nice weapon. You never know when they'll happen and good luck on your journeys.
