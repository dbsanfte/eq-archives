Oh, wait, those items aren't NEW features. Sheesh. Well here's some ideas for new features:

1. Give me some decent spells. I want a sta/agi/ac buff to go along with Focus, both individual and group versions. I want a new poison dd. Some other creative spells like curses would be nice.
2. Give me some decent shaman equipment. How many monk-only items drop in NToV? Lots, but mah Shield o' Wurms is the only shaman-only thing that drops. Heck, monks can even wear Cloak of the Sky.
3. Give me some new, fun, well-balanced abilities that I don't have now.

I know the content will be there. It always is. Nice new monsters, quests, and encounters. But I need fresh air. I need to see my character in a new light. I need to get out of the old, stuffy, slow mastah, buff-o-matic in groups/raids, jbb-epic solo routine. Give me some choices of different things I can do or roles I can play. I can still do the old stuff if I need to.

Show me something new and exciting for my shaman, and I'll show you da money.
