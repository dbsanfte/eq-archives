Asta Khaan
Team up with a necro inside second castle, at the courtyard (Fennin trigger spot i guess). I call it the picnic table spot. :)

Without pushing it too hard I can score about an AA every 45mins. Necro has Bane of Thule. Using necro to agro kite and our pets beating from behind. Im sitting in the middle tossing on bane/ancientbane/ultor and just spamming canna like a madman. With darkness this is easier than pie and totally safe. Gets a bit mindnumbing tho.

As for soloing, I agree its better to kill the even con stuff. While its nice to see the huge jump from downing the redcon doomguards or the yellowcon magmas, its not worth it due to their resists and hp. One unlucky rootbreak/resist coupled with a stun and you're dead vs red/yellowcons (triples for 1100ish). If the two phoenix birdies up past first castle are dead,you have lots of excellent space to move about right there before the magma bridge. Two evencon spawns just at the foot of the stairs, and you can pull from inside the castle as well. Using levitate and the terrain elevation coupled with the fact that these mobs arent too fast runners, you should have no trouble root/rotting them.
