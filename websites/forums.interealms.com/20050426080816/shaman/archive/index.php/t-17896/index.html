Glimugs
Well, last night I got invited to duo with a buddy of mine in Grieg's end. At 54, I was running into yellow and even cons mostly, some were dark blue, some were fairly resistant, but once malosi landed, not too much of a problem. Mobs would double, maxxing in low 200s, so not too tough at all. Slow, epic dot, Ebolt, JBB from my leezard while non-alpha dogdog chewed on him. No weapons drop for dogdog here, so bring your own.

I believe I could solo here with caution, near 1k AC and 2200 hp self buffed. Be ready on the root and careful of traps. Mostly necro-type mobs, never saw any fear attempts though, just hittin dogdog with dots like infectious cloud and heat blood.

Loot was fair, windstones, black sapphires, up to 5pp, down to 5gp. Occassionally a named mob will spawn, but no significant danger from them. Dropped a blade of insanity 11/21 1hs, rather nice weapon with a debuff proc and a near worthless neckslot from another named. As for xp, was getting half a blue AA per kill at level 54. Regular xp was not far behind that, and this was teamed with a 65. Pops were reasonably timed and fairly spaced out, only had one add where we were pulling to and I think that was just a proximity aggro as I never saw any wandering mobs.

The appearance is indoor, but its an outdoor zone, SoW works as do mounts, lev does not work in this zone btw. Did not run into any see invis mobs. Do not step on the carpets in the rooms, they are triggertraps that spawn mobs. Sorry for the longer post, but I think this zone has been an under-used and worthy of further explorations. Once I get back from this warzone and get on a decent connection, I'll be back in there.
