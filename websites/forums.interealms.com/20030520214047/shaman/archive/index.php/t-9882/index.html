Kiamak
I found no info anywhere on this PoP ability. So said what the hell and spent 3 aa on it. I'm very glad I did. For those of you curious here is what it does.

It's a knockback root that is very low resist.
It has a very fast cast time.
It last 1 min 14 seconds.
It has no stun or damage effect.

The knockback is great if your soloing and get a slow resist. It moves the mob away out of melee range. It's also great for moving an add off of you. DD will break the root however.

If your considering getting this ability, it is definatly worth it. Has been a huge help to me in groups and solo.

Togh
This is well worth the 3AA that it costs. The thing to keep in mind is that this is a skill; therefore, it does not follow gem refresh rules. This is a semi-emergency skill.

- Slow resisted? Must wait 2 seconds to cast root or just click VP.
- Root resisted? Click VP instead of being beat on for the next 2 seconds
- Add joined the group and being on cleric? click VP and it's rooted AND not beating cleric in less time than pulling the MOB away from group through use of aggro and parking.

Remember, as you level to 65 things are beating you for a ton. I'm only 62, and I'm regularly soloing things that hit for 638! 2 seconds of waiting for spell gems to clear means alot!

It does not replace having root in the spell lineup, but it compliments us SO nicely! I love spirit call, but VP is quickly becoming a more valuable skill for me to have.
