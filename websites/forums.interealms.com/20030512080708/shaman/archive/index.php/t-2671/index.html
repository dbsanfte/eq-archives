if you choose to do the test of patience at your level, realize that there is a 100 percent chance you will have to deal with the killer shark.

Some people have overcome this by levitating OVER the water and listening for the spirit to spawn (skipping Abe & company). I actually used the ITA method.

When the killer shark pathed by he immediately aggro'd (he doesn't seem to be immune to ITA..it's like he has an AoE dispell or something). The first time he did I had to gate out. After having to do the 'drunk' part a second time I wised up and memmed good old Flash of Light. When the shark aggro'd me this time I flashed him..and re-invised while he ran away. He never came back.

If you do choose to do this the 'old fashioned' way..bring help. A big tank or a chanter could make this very simple.
