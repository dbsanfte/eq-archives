If it were me..

You know about your boots. I found an interesting droppable set from mines of ssra. Dusty Soriz boots. 40 hps nothing special but better than Gebs. Would be very cheap.

The Turtlebone ring? Keep it, but put ac6/65 ring it its place and use it only for EB gear.

Ranged slot. Your sta is pretty low. Go with a Idol of the Thorned or Goranga. That charm isn't doing much for ya imo

Helm. Been an incredibly long time. There has to be something out there better than that. Anything with hitpoints on it. Jaundiced Bone as a worst case scenario.

RMB. Try a sentry Bracer/ +15 sta bracer. See if you can find a deal. If you've got 7k, this is a place to look to upgrade.

I always like the Earring of Solstice. I'd start picking up this items (or just do it all now). Great earring. Hard to get 2 earrings better than it to replace it.

HP -> Sta -> AC at your level. As you approach 60 you'll want to make resists on the items you upgrade with a little more important.

Your question about saving for Malo/Torpor. Unless you're being pl'd you're a long way from 60. If you're a powergamer type then you're likely to join a guild once you approach 60 (lots of guilds have these spells in the guild vault) If you're not the powergamer type, you're a good bit from 60. Get some upgrades. (Since you're buying mostly tradable ones, you'll make at least some of your money back in resale) You have a fungi tunic. With the steady value of Fungi and the lowering price of torpor/malo, you'll have a nice bartering item for torpor once you hit 60.
