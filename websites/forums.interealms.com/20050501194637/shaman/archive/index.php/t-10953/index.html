Sounds almost like its too good to be true with an AA ding every 20 mins ,ect?

That's because it is too good to be true. There are only a very few enchanters on every server that can charm solo for great experience. It is difficult to learn and even harder to master. Even the enchanters that have absolutely mastered it do not get an AA in 20 minutes. Not even close.

Normal, run of the mill enchanters are fairly bitter at the moment. Their crowd control has been removed from the post-60 game, for the most part. They have become the buff whores that we hate to be. They can still slow, to some degree, but charming is their only really good skill that they have left.

Of course, I don't play an enchanter, this is second hand information. Take it with a grain of salt.

Faern
Being an Enchanter is sort of like being a Free Safety in football. You, and only you are often responsible to clean up and correct the mistakes of your teammates.

Overpull? Lock it down.
Broken Mez? Re-mez.
Non-assisting type? Explain the concept of mez.
Running MOB? Root.

Charm soloing is A LOT of fun. It can also lead you to pull your hair out. If you're going to go the route of rolling up an Enchanter I recommend: www.castersrealm.com and go to the Enchanter section.

Realize that even with Rune, Bedlam, arcane Rune,etc you ARE in robes.I have yet to cast through 3 MOBS beating on me. Stun, does nothing but buy you 4-8 seconds. (And place you even higher on their aggro list)

You WILL die a lot. You think Shaman slow has aggro? Now imagine Tashing a MOB and then dealing with it. You WILL die due to the mistakes of your teammates.

Enchanter is a fun class. Only you can decide if it's for you.

Can you get an AA in 20 minutes? Yes

Can you sustain an AA every 20 minutes ... I cannot but then I tend to be a grouping enchanter.

With the change to Affliction items it is even more difficult now.

I generally duo a 65 enchanter and a 65 shaman, both with 90+ AA. A little low by todays standards but still enough to cover the core AA abilities on both classes.

NOTE: I am not a shaman, I can play one in a supporting sense only. I use my shaman to complement my enchanter and I can handle a shaman in a group/raid scenario but my enchanter is my primary focus ... at least until he dies :)

When I solo, I use my shaman exclusively as a buff/heal BOT who is generally parked outside the zone I am soloing in.

Abusing the shaman for heals/buffs/invis/SOW/Levitation ... pretty much everything to spare my mana possible.

I have a cleric I can log on as required to buff me and ress me and some higher end gear and sustainably I estimate an AA every 45 mins to 60 minutes. Charm is VERY random and thus on a given day I may approach an AA every 20 minutes on the next day I might hit an AA every 60 minutes thus the wide estimate range.

Some enchanters claim sustainable AA every 20 minutes and perhaps they can but I cannot ... even with a shaman and a cleric at my beck and call I cannot sustain an AA every 20 minutes.

The funny thing is ... 2 nights ago I was grouped with:

65 warrior (roomie)
65 cleric (roomie)
65 enchanter (me)
65 shaman (me)

Using charm to kill Ogres in Tactics for J/C components and we were averaging about 1 AA every hour with each of us duoing 2 characters and my pet not hasted. The warrior pulled and help a little on CC when my charmie broke ... the cleric healed my pet and the shaman helped keep him debuffed. Both healers healed me. Had our regular cleric been present, ie not a BOTTED cleric, I would have hasted my pet but he was not. Given a hasted pet, we likely would have made about 1 AA every 45 minutes.

So, can I guarentee that there are no enchanters out there that can Charm Solo for an AA every 20 minutes sustainable? No ... I can merely say I have better than average equipment with all the useful AA, spells and I have buff/ress BOTS and I cannot do it sustainably.

I have a wand of tranquility for C3, I have my Epic for Haste, I only have a mana preservation 3 item (mitigates slow but not charm). My affliction extension and affliction haste items no longer work so I will be swiching to Mana Preservation IV in my ranged slot. I can even break out a mana robe and use my shaman for healing while I robe on a horse to regain mana.

With a good cleric, in the right spot I might be able to approach this rate duoing using heal for aggro transfer allowing a safe recharm.

Shrug, I am not a soloing enchanter by nature, I much prefer grouping so I cannot say my opinion is the be all and end all of charm soloing :)
