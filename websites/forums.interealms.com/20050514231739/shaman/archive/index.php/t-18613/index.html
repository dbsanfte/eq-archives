Scalia
One nice place to get Defense up for mid 20s - low 30s characters is Maru Seru at the Seru zone. Start with Rockhoppers, which should be blue to you, and work your way up through Stonegrabbers and Beetles, which max out around 28. They are social, but easy to single pull and you never run out of mobs. The loot is not that great, but Iron Oxide, a rare drop of Stonegrabbers, still sells for 40ish pp on E'ci and Rockhopper hides, which you will get plenty of, sell well to budding tailors.

The only critters that will agro are Lightcrawlers, who run at fast SOW speed, and Zelniaks, who have a HUGE agro range. If you are a good race, you should be able to zone into Seru. Otherwise stay away from them or Root/Flash of Light and either DD/DOT them to death or run to Netherbian Lair.

Raising Defense to 180 shouldn't be too hard. After that it slows down and 190+ is a pain. But at that point you will have a decent slow + an AGI buff and will be able to stand toe to toe with many dark blues.

Crystal Caverns are very good for raising Defense in the 30s since the mobs are weak by Velios standards and are VERY good XP and loot. You WILL need a group, though, unless you know exactly what you are doing, which takes a long time in CC given its tortuos design. And not many groups will let you tank for them :)

Graal the Dorf
Something to remember: Defense will only go up when a mob is missing you. So lower the mob's atk as much as you can, and bring your agi/ac up.

Dodge, and other defensive skills, will go up any time a mob swings at you.

Wrong. A swing doesn't have to miss to give you a skillup in defense.

Each attack has a slight chance of giving a skillup in defense. The higher level the mob you are tanking, the higher the chance of a skillup. The higher your AGI, the better the chance of a skillup.

You want your AGI as high as possible, the mob you are tanking to be as high level as you can comfortably tank, and preferably lower the mobs atk by as much as possible.

[Thu Jan 01 00:54:00 2004] You try to crush a hollow crystal, but miss!
[Thu Jan 01 00:54:02 2004] You have become better at 1H Blunt! (141)
[Thu Jan 01 00:54:02 2004] You crush a hollow crystal for 7 points of damage.

Notice the time stamps?

Here is another one, this time for Bash:

[Thu Jan 01 00:58:36 2004] Auto attack is on.

[Thu Jan 01 00:58:37 2004] You have become better at Bash! (56)
