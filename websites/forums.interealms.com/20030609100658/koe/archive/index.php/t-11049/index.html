Soriathus Serpentine
2 factions: Frogloks of Guk (Old live frogloks) and Frogloks of Gukta (New live "chosen ones")

It really depends on your race/deity though. If you are a non-evil race and deity you start out at least non-kos to the "Frogloks of Gukta". I also assume if you are non-evil race and a follow of Mithanial Marr you will start Amiable with them. Also if you killed any last night you could have screwed up your faction because I killed a few an lost Gukta faction.

If you ARE an evil race and/or deity, even if you have Ally faction with the ghoul lord scalp, you are KoS by default to Gukta. Likewise you could work on your Gukta faction and not worry about your Guk faction at all (unless you want the wood elf crown like I do). Next time I log in, I will go back to lower guk and see if I get positive Guk and Gukta faction. I assume if you find/kill trolls you should probably get good Gukta faction. If anyone finds out ways to raise gukta faction, please let me know, I'd like to be on a friendly basis with the frogs.
