/agree Lagger

Definitely find safe places (away from wandering mobs) to park your pet. Then you go find mobs and pull to the pet. The pet is considerably lower-level than you and will aggro things that may not necessarily attack you. Mobs that are green to you may not be green to your pet. This can cause problems, park the pet (/pet guard here) and pull to him. Also, try to position things as you pull to where the pet is parked so that you are attacking the mob from the front and the pet is attacking the mob from behind. This isn't always possible, but it is ideal, as the pet will take less damage (from ripostes, etc) and will do more damage (damage bonus when hitting a mob from behind). Tank for the pet. Doggie can dish it out, but can't take much damage. Also, just for future reference, when you hit level 44, go to EC and buy Summon Companion from Rushka in the EC tunnel, back by the shady swashbuckler guys. It will allow you to summon your pet from one part of a zone to some other location (your location) within that zone. I love this spell. It allows me to zone into where I am gonna hunt, summon pet, med/canni the mana back to full and park pet. Then I run to my desired hunting spot, summon companion (tell him to /pet guard me or he'll run back to where you parked him) and get to some serious hunting.
