howjan
Just curiously how crowded is the high end on test? i.e. how many other guilds do you have at the ssra/VT, working on elemental access, and already in elementals.

There is a raging 3 raiding organizations on test. TSF so far has the furthest progression. We're made up of a pile of guilds and freelance people. The organization operates basically as a /gu but some of us simply will not share guild tag. We have about 100 people on the dkp list and field around 70 charactures on a good nite.

Behind us is Primal Brood. They are working on flagging to the elementals and are currently around Bertoxx/Mith Marr or so. They have cleared VT a few times.

Slightly behind them is The Alliance, another raiding organization from several guilds. From what I gather they have done some flagging runs, but not very far. They have run through VT a few times.

That's it, there are some small groups of folks that run through some stuff here and there but as far as pop progression of note we have 3. I think the spacing of the various organizations is an overall bad thing tbh. We simply do not feel the push and drive to go as far as hard as we can. There's no rush because the stuff is going to be there when we want it. Of course...it's nice to know you'll be the only ones in a zone as well, it has good and bad points I guess.
