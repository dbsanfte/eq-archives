weissblau
The drop spells 51 to 55 are not really all that critical. Some are nice to have, sure, but they are not make or break.

Level 55 is where the rubber really starts to meet the road, with Form of the Great Bear, Spirit of the Howler, and Torrent of Poison as dropped spells. From 55 on count on struggling to get all your spells if you don't have a strong guild support network to help you get them.

However, I STILL would not let a lack of spells pre-60 stand in my way of levelling. The simple truth is you get resisted less, you resist more of the mobs' attacks, and farming plat to pay for spells gets easier. Pre-60, get Run 3 but go right back to levelling.
