1) It forces people to EXPERIENCE the content - not just kill, kill, kill, exp, exp, exp. You progress through the bosses and the encounters if you want to see new zones. It's called a content progression.

It's not because WE are uber and you SUCK. It's because you don't have the levels (read: spells, hp, gear, resists, etc) to be effective. By forcing you through trials or encounters you prove your capability before ever stepping into the zone.
