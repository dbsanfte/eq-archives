Not sure it helps but here after 20 levels or so of just grouping with Guildees(Im in a very small close knit guild) I had many of the same problems you did although at 56 not 58. I finally broke down and did a few pick-up groups in the planes. Tier 2 obviously has much better xp 1% every 2 kills or so and 2% AAXP every kill. Have done the pick-up group about 5-6 times to get through level 58 and half way through 59 now. Some were excellent and I tried to stay in them as long as possible, had a few that were not so good. My advice is to look for people who are lfg in larger more respectable guilds or people that you have played with before. Know it is probablly not the "sweet spot" you were looking for but it took me forever to motivate myself to do pick-up groups again. Hope it helps, know its not much, but it took prodding from friends who outl leveled me to get back into the swing of pick-up groups. I really enjoyed CoD and Plane of Torment, although both are bring youre own group.
