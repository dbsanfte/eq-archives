Kensei
Crystal Caverns for exp in upper 40s? You could do Geonids or Terrors, but most will be Lt Blue by then. Of course, you can kill a lot which will compensate for that, and the spawn rate is fast.

You can safely cross Kael invis, so either camo up outside and run, get a Tolan's Helm for the superior Camo, or buy Invis potions from the potion vendor in Thurg (10-11pp each). Just don't stray from the direct path or you'll find giants which see invis. If you do the quest to improve your Kael factions it will lower your Coldain, though it might be possible to keep both Kael and Thurg non-KOS by hunting in Skyshrine, buying dwarf heads, and doing the Kobald paws quest (Kael quest, paws drop in VL).

Good luck! I just use invis myself, and enjoy killing giants.
Valencia
The respose I got was the back door of Crystal Caverns. Which backdoor is this?? Is it the right you take before getting to the queens room?? I think what they mean by the backdoor is the geonid camp. The tunnel on the right as you head to the queens room from the city is the geonid tunnel. At the end of that tunnel is a zone-out to EW, just outside the Ry'gorr Fort. Thats the back door. You can kill the geonids starting from the top one near zone-out and work your way down and just zone-out if it gets ugly. Easy to single pull and great loot. Many will be light blue to you. You will need Sup Camo to get to the geonid camp also because the only entrance to CC is through the little crystal behind the Ry'Gorr fort -- unless of course you want to fight your way down past the orcs. BTW, the geonids are NOT KOS, unlike the spiders/orcs/terrors, until you've killed like a thousand of them....;)...and nothing in the zone sees thru inviz.

Also, I recommend Sup Camo for a Kael run. If you don't have a way to re-camo yourself in Kael if your inviz breaks, you are pretty much dead. However, if your inviz sticks and you stay on the path to WL, you won't run into any see inviz giants. If inviz breaks, just find a spot not too close to any giant and re-apply.

In either case, if you don't have a Tolan's helm already...get one..the Sup Camo effect is invaluable.

Good luck!
