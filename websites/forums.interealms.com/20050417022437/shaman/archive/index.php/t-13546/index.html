All you need is a JBB.
Zone into Dulak and follow the wall to the right until you come to the huts with drunken / broken skulls in em.
on the opposite side of the huts is a huge hall and when you follow the hall you come to a spot with some crates.
That is your camp for 2 reasons.
You can pull most of the huts when at camp. Invis up, open door to a hut and target a mob. Head bak to that crates and cast slow.
Somehow the roaming guards will NOT aggro while you kill AND the mobs will not run.
Just slow em and JBB em to death. very fast exp, at 45 i got 2 % per kill.
Not to mention the really nice loot *Cash gems, mithril amus etc*

sharookan

PS: You also get the empty flask there which is needed for the Stoicism spell quest.
