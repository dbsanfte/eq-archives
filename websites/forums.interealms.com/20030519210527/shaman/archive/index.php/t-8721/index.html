Hehe I started out twinking my new kitty shaman because I was bored with my paladin. One thing to get is a pair of Opal Encrusted Steins, they are crucial with +50 cha between 2 they really make a huge difference and you can get them for as low as 30pp each if you try hard enough.

As far as actual gear, I would recommend a Spine Chill Spear you can get one for only about 100pp probibly. Arctic Wyvern Mask for sure (its a cheapie), if you can get a charred guardian shield for 200pp or less grab it with 9 WIS its great. (later on upgrade to crystal chitin shield with 20ac, 9 wis and 40 mana they rock). If you can find Sabertooth Forged Armor especially legs and chest grab it its great stuff and it commonly sold for not too bad as it has no stats, but AWESOME ac. My kitty has Sabertooth Forged Leggings (17 ac) and Chest (24 ac) great items. Sometimes you can get Jaundiced bone vambraces for under 300pp if so grab them good ac and looks decent.

And save money for spells!
