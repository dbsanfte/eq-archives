Hail,
I have 202 aa and have no intention of training planar power in the next 50+ aa or so.
Your choice of course. :) But with your Magelo as shown (234 stamina), every time you are casting Boar right now you are missing out on 14 points worth of Stamina towards hitpoints. If you add PE to that (not likely when raiding I'd admit), that would cap your stamina at 305 w/ PP5. The difference between 280 stamina and 305 may not be huge, but an extra free 40 hp or so seems a worthwhile investment to me at least, especially when you already have almost all of the better AA's done.

If you were to invest 3x in Innate Enlightenment at a cost of 9 AA's, your wisdom (& int :p ) would be capped at 310 & your indoor buffed wis (+28 for VoQ, +10 for Bearform) would be sitting at 308 according to your Magelo.

If you were to knock out all of Planar Power instead at a cost of 10 AA's, your wisdom PLUS all your other stats would be capped at 305, which would put your indoor buffed wis and stamina both at 305. You lose only 2 points worth of wisdom derived mana, but you GAIN all the hitpoints derived from the extra 25 points of stamina. Of course you can always get IE1 later and raise your wisdom cap to 315.

In addition to the hitpoints & mana benefits from Planar Power from raised stats, there are less directly noticable benefits as well. Your melee stats (while not a major consideration) also go up, so you have the chance to proc more if you have a weapon that does so, and with a higher strength cap you will have a higher Attack number (& be able to carry more loot unencumbered) Are these extras important? Not really, but they are free fringe benefits from Planar Power that you just don't get with IE.

It just seems to me to make more sense to get Planar Power before Innate Enlightenment for the above reasons, but invest however you think is best so that you have the most fun w/ Grogbat. :cool:

Take care,
