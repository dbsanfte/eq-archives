#1 Priority - the JBB.

Auction for it, check the bazaar, it's worth it.

#2 Priority - the horse.

The horse lets you med while you cast spells, lets you change out spells without sitting, lets you avoid sit aggro in Hate and Fear when you go there. With a JBB, it's killer, each JBB brings in 1% more mana from sitting and does damage as well. Without the JBB it is still nice, but its true advantage comes in to play when you have a manaless form of damage (besides attacking) while medding.
