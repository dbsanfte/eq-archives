Since Verant went to the effort to warn us of additional downtime, I think it's pretty safe to say EQ will be out of commision for a while. I love a good web comic. Reading a few years worth of a good comic's backlog is a great timekiller. I remember an old thread where everyone had good suggestions for books, so I hope I can get a few good suggestions for comics too. Here's one of my favorites to start things off.

Irritability (http://icomix.com/maze/comicpage/index1.html)
This is hands down my favorite comic, print or web. The comic follows Chappy Chappy and friends as they cause general mayhem. This used to be published in the University of Texas' student newspaper, and I can remember countless times I got odd looks as I laughed out loud reading it. A few links to some of my favorite strips: Character Intro (http://icomix.com/maze/comicpage/browse.php?index=137) , Extreme Snowboarding (http://icomix.com/maze/comicpage/browse.php?index=210) , Two Caesars (http://icomix.com/maze/comicpage/browse.php?index=232) ,Fuzzy Dice (http://icomix.com/maze/comicpage/browse.php?index=511), and Beholder (http://icomix.com/maze/comicpage/browse.php?index=484)
