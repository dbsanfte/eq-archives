Henora
have you hunted much in Sebilis?

If you have, your legion of cabilis faction should be maxed out.
That makes you indifferent to the troopers. If you haven't maxed your legion of cabilis, then you will probably be KOS.

You hand in bone chips to the guard on the right, at the entrance to Cabilis, in the Field of Bone. Just hail him, he will tell you something about bone chips.

You gotta be really careful on the hand ins though, as there are wandering crusaders in the area that are on a different faction and are KOS to you. They are pretty mean...they don't see invis. So when you are doing hand-ins position yourself so you can see if they are coming and run away.

-Henora

Dandi
But if you try the right zone and do a bit of merchant mining after your server has been up at least a couple weeks, you'll get more bone chips than you can carry. Any popular newbie zone featuring skellies will be good, just find the nearest vendor that a newbie would be likely to use. The tailoring vendor in Everfrost at the Halas ramp is a very good one, and I'm sure the vendors just inside Neriak would be good too. I'm sure there are more, too.... Just be prepared to invest a bit in getting to the bone chips in the first place. There's a good guide to Merchant Mining HERE (http://www.eqtraders.com/prices/mining.htm).
Ragnarita
You can still use the OT hammer when KoS, the skelly on the boat isn't on VS faction (on beta neutral I think) You may sometimes agro another skeletal Worker, but they are green and rather easy to kill.
The bank is a bit trickier, but you can use it at night when the general goes out of the outpost. The bankers are not on VS faction.

btw if you wonder why the bankers and the skelly on the boat aren't on VS faction, they are used for the Cabilis necro skullcap quests
