Sorry I didn't have a chance yet to read all the previous posts, and I'm by no means an expert at this, but here are a few ideas:

1. Shrink to the smallest size, w/o bearform, which makes it much harder for people to see you.
2. Keep up SoW and levitate.
3. Go /anon, partially for the obvious guildtag reasons but also to minimize the amount of text that someone might manage to read over your head.
4. Con the corpse from a distance to be sure of when it's opened up for general looting.
5. Use skeleton form if possible, as it's difficult to see and target.
6. Go invisible. A lot of people have see invisible items/spells on, but some don't.
7. Create a diversion, such as a train.
8. Leave the zone ASAP so that a /who won't show your name. Don't gate near the raid as this will show up in the chat window and logs.
9. If the item is rare and droppable, wait a week or so before putting it up for sale in the bazaar.
10. Deny everything. Don't say "Oh yeah I was watching you guys kill AoW and yeah I checked the corpse to see what he'd dropped but I didn't take anything"; instead say "Huh? *** are you talking about? I've been farming shards for the past two hours."
