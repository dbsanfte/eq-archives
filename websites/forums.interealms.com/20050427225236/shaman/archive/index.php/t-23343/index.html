Hail,

At 52 you should be grouped in PoI in my opinion.
Especially without a mount. You really need to get one of those! ;)

At 54 if you have the points you can get Anathema. That plus epic and a disease DoT should let you root/rot singles if you are very determined. Tanking in the face isn't a good option for you right now, though it will be viable later on in the lower Tiers of PoP if you want to do that (I did). At 56 with a new powerful poison DoT, you'll have more DPS at your disposal as well.

52....wouldn't recommend soloing in PoI unless you are extremely well twinked, and even then it isn't efficient. If you are determined to solo I'd recommend old world or Kunark zones for you, though the exp rate will be lousy compared to PoP. SolB, Lower Guk, Burning Woods, Skyfire, etc.

Two or 3 player groups in PoI will be much more effective as Sudedor said. Grab a knight tank and any DPS class and you should be okay, though for most efficiency you should be using Stoicism out of combat to help heal. If you are primary healer AND slower, don't worry about doing any dps (just use dogdog & click your JBB) as your contribution with slows and heals will be plenty.

Take care,
