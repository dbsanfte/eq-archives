Luclin's Arriana
Power pool attacks will never do as much damage as a secondary attack (like the Tanker secondaries), nevermind a scrapper primary.

They can add nice bonus effects, like the knockdown/end fly in Air Superiority.

I've heard many good things about Tough and Weave in the Fighting pool, and Boxing would be a good way to ladder up into those. Disorient attacks are always nice...and since the damage isn't going to rock your world, you can slot it up with enhanced disorient to make that side of the attack more effective.

My scrapper can't take power pool attacks because I use Broadsword, and any power pool attack puts the weapon away, forcing me to waste time re-drawing it when I use a primary attack again. I have Air Superiority on my Controller, and it works quite well.

If you were a controller or something and for some reason wanted a melee attack, it wouldn't be a bad choice though.
