Henora
I posted something about this, a long time ago.

I was trying to catch up Abjuration. Heh, it's still only 212.
My conclusion was to use the low level fast casting spells.

You can get far more casts total, both due to shorter casting time and lower mana cost, in the same time period using lower level fast casting spells of the class you wish to raise.

In my test, which was limited by a small sample size, I recall that I noticed no significant difference in speed of increases by using a higher level spell.

I just find it easy to chain cast fleeting fury on yourself, or nuke yourself with burst of flame. You can do this in downtime in groups and stay at FM.

My trouble spots were, and still are, Abjuration and Evocation.
Divination, Conjuration and of course, our staple, Alteration I never had any problem with... well Divination until after level 29! :)

-Henora
