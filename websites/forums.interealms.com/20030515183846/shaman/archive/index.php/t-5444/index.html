Check out the thread in this forum on the Darkpaw cave in Jaggedpine Forest. It's right next to the Surefall Glade Druid portal (you can invis if necessary until you get your faction up). The cave is usually deserted, or has someone pulling to the entrance, and could support 30+ people. It's all an outdoor zone, and there are merchants and a banker. Just keep moving; it wasn't built for you to sit in one spot and pull. You shouldn't really need an Enchanter but it wouldn't hurt.
