I'm not sure if this is appropriate to your circumstances, but here's a repost of my guide "How to kill Kirn if you are not very uber" :)

Hope this is useful to someone out there:

----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Here's how I killed High Scale Kirn, I hope this will be useful to
someone else, as The Hole really seems to be a sticking point for a lot
of shamans trying to get their epic.

There are several things you need to carry out this plan, but you don't
need to be in an uber guild, you just need a few friends.


Preparation

Make a halfling rogue, and scrounge a teleport to Toxx. Bind in Paineel,
or ideally, at the entrance to The Hole. Learn how Hide and Sneak work,
and then go exploring! Never drop Hide/Sneak unless you want to die -
and if you do, be careful not to train other people who may be in the
zone.

Find the undead tower where Kirn lives. Find the 'safe ledge' outside
the tower. If you are facing the entrance to the tower, the ledge is on
your right. No mobs will agro you if you are on this ledge.

Basically, spend a few hours learning the zone, especially the tower
area. See where Kirn spawns - and while you are there, if his pet is up
but he isn't, petition a GM to fix it. Preparation is the key, you can't
just run into a zone like the Hole unprepared.

Make a load of Invis to Undead potions.


Your party

Most importantly, you need a mage, with the Call of the Hero spell, who
doesn't mind giving up two evenings of their time for you. I found a
mage via my server message boards, and offered help hunting wurms in
Burning Woods in exchange for their help in The Hole.

You will need a few tanks, and definately a cleric. You can probably
persuade a couple of enchanters to come along, as they can get their
epic drop from the Ghost of Kindle, who lives right next to Kirn.

You can probably persuade another shaman or two to come along, as they
will want to see how it's done, for when they need Kirn themselves.

Lastly, you should try and get a porter so you can evac to safety when
you are done.

For the record, my party had: 3 shamans, 1 warrior, 1 pally, 1 cleric, 1
mage, 1 enchanter, 1 ranger. All were between level 51 and 54 (except
the mage who I think was 57).


Getting in

There are two ways you can accomplish this:

1- find a guild who are planning a raid on the Hole to kill Kirn. Ask
nicely if you and your mage may join their raid. Assuming they say yes,
get your mage to the safe ledge, and either bind or camp them there.

2- find a dwarven Cleric of Brell, who has never hunted in The Hole, and
is high enough level to Revive. I personally didn't use this method but
I know someone who did, so my details may not be totally accurate. Go to
Paineel, and have a high level cleric cast Divine Aura on the dwarf.
Drop the dwarf into the Hole. They will land in front of Master Yael,
but won't be KOS to any of the mobs there.

The dwarf should then use Invis vs Undead, and make their way to the
safe ledge.

Next, take the mage to the normal entrace to the Hole, and kill them (in
a duel). Use your rogue to drag the corpse to the safe ledge, and the
dwarf cleric then revives them. BEWARE - corpses from duels are only
rezzable for about 10 minutes! So be fast!

The dwarf can now gate to safety, with a large chunk of your platinum in
their pocket :)

Either way - you now have a mage at the safe ledge.

If you are coming back the next day (or next spawn) to get Kirn, camp
at the safe ledge. If not, just proceed to the next step!



The Raid

Assemble the raid force in The Hole, at the zone. Log on the rogue, and
have them invite the mage into a group. Then (assuming the rogue is
bound at the zoneline) plummet to your death!

The rogue is now back at the zone, and invites each person in turn, they
get COTH'd to the safe ledge, and then disband. The last person to get
COTH should be the rogue himself. Leave the rogue at the ledge, you may
need him for corpse recovery if anything goes wrong!

Form groups, buff up, and hand out ITU potions to anyone who can't cast
the spell. Everyone ITU, and lead them into the tower. Go as fast as you
can - anyone who loses ITU should simply stay in one spot and die. As
long as the cleric and some tanks get to the top, you should be ok.

When I did this, we actually fought a battle on the stairs leading up
the tower. This was EXTREMELY nerve wracking! :) But we only had 2 mobs
to kill, we finished them, recast ITU and made it to the top.

When you get to the top, turn left and assemble in the room where the
Ghost of Kindle lives. Only ONE mob in this room will agro you on sight.
Stay away from the door to avoid agro from mobs outside. No one except
the puller should leave this room.

Drop ITU and kill whatever agros. As long as half your people survived
the run up the stairs (and no one trained you) you shouldn't have a
problem here. After killing this mob, use the rogue to drag dead people
up to the top for a rez. Buff up anyone who needs it.

When everyone is ready, kill the rest of the mobs in the room. Gratz
Chanter_00 on their epic drop from Kindle. Then pull any KOS mobs from
the hallway, and adjacent rooms (carefully!) and kill them. Kindle
respawns in 20 minutes, so you may have to kill it again.


Kirn!

When the revenants are cleared, you are ready to pull Kirn. Make sure
you are not in bear form, there are reports that this messes up your
faction. Get a symbol buff for those extra few HP. Everyone except the
pulling shaman should wait in Kindle's room, away from the door. The
shaman should hand in the icon to Kirn, and leg it back to the party.

You will probably take Kirn's Harm Touch on the way back. As soon as you
get back, the MT will taunt Kirn off you, and just kill him as you would
any other mob. He was white to me at level 53, and I had no problem
landing Malosi and Tugur's (after Tash).

I think you should have all your tanks in one group with the cleric,
and the second group shouldn't attack Kirn until he is down to 50-60%.
What you don't want is him ping-ponging all over the room and possibly
attracting agro from outside.

And that's it! Kill the scaly, loot his ring, and leave! If you haven't
got a porter, you can hold the room until you can convince one to come
get you! Use the rogue to invite them for a COTH from the zoneline.



I hope this helps some of you who despair of ever getting your epics,
due to the Hole stage. I used to think the same way :) But with a
totally non-uber party, made up of friends, guildmates, and just people
I asked nicely, we did this raid, and took down Kirn with no deaths to
our party! Which I am pretty pleased about, I hope you have as much
success as I did!
