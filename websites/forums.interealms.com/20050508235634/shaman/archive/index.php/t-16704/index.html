Henora
At level 56 your options are a bit different than they are at 65.

First, use your pet. If you can afford it, pick up some proccing weapons for him in the bazaar or from your local mage. If not, give him some of the "fine steel" weapons from the dungeon you are in. Giving him weapons helps him out quite a bit.

In terms of spells, you have a few choices.
Bane of Nife - 1300ish dmg, 42 seconds, 425 mana, breaks root.
Anathema - 1400ish dmg, 30 seconds, 440 mana, recast time.
Torrent of Poison - 1620 dmg total on 3 waves, 380 mana, resist check each wave.
Blast of Venom - 680 dmg, 340 mana, high mana cost, quick cast.
Ice Strike - 650 dmg, 250 mana, long cast time.

This is a very similar spell line up that we have at 65 as well. The spell you want to use is going to depend on the agro management of your group, how long the mobs are living and personal preference.

If mobs are living a long time and agro is not an issue, use one of your dots at your level. Get the coldain military wristguard thing for Burning Affliction and use Bane of Nife! If mobs are living a very short time and mana is not an issue consider using Blast of Venom, it's very quick casting. If mobs have low poison resists and your evocation skill is good, using Torrent of Poison is an attractive option as well. 3 waves landing fully will give you more damage for less mana than any of your other options.

At level 65, I choose to use nukes. I use them for agro control and because my groups kill fast and chain pull. I also use them because I have nuke AA, mana pres, improved damage and a bard thats playing rizlonas all the time. In my groups I have 2 spell slots for damage. Generally, I use those 2 slots for Velium Strike/Tears of Saryrn and Ferine Avatar.

I almost exclusively do hard adventures. Usually a monk tanking, otherwise it is a non-enraging blow equipped warrior. The monk and warriors do pretty well, but it is no where near the "agro lock" of knights. We do not have the uber gear that can compensate for poor play so I have to be careful with my agro. The mobs in hard adventures can and have taken half my life routinely in a single round (6000hp, 1275ac, LR5). I cannot afford to waste my clerics mana healing me and a fast heal on me will likely have the mob turn on them...and deaths are the quickest way to lose an adventure.

The most mana efficient DPS I have is my pet. Don't underestimate his contributions, especially the 61 pet. Just give him a couple weapons and some haste and he will work hard for you. :)

and most importantly - NEVER DO COLLECTION MISSIONS!
Poor drop rates can screw you out of a win, through no fault of your own. I have not actually failed an adventure, but guild groups of mine who have lost adventures have lost almost all of them on collection. One group estimated they would have to kill 140 mobs to collect the requested number of drops at the drop rate they were experiencing in their dungeon. Consider the toughest slaughter mission we have received is 60 mobs.

1) Is torrent of Poison a rain that can hit multiple targets, and if yes, will it hit my tank who is in melee?
Yes, it is a rain and can hit multiple targets. General rule of thumb is that it gives up to 3 waves on a single target and up to 4 total waves on multiple targets. At least that is my experience. If you have 4 mobs, it will hit each of them once. If you have 2 mobs, it will hit each of them twice. If you have one mob, it will hit it 3 times. I am sure someone else has an experience that is different but thats how it usually happens for me.

On non-pvp servers rain spells do not hit other player characters except yourself, so stand back! :)


2) What is Amathema? Where do we get it?
Anathema (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=4094&source=Live) is one of the new dots in our new line from LDON. Rather than a disease or poison dot, it is a magic based dot that adds curse counters, making it require remove curse to cure. It is slightly shorter in duration than our poison dots and has a 24 second recast time. You can buy it at LDON adventure merchants with adventure points. See this thread for details: LDON Spell List (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=16362).
