Just wanted to share a little something I've been doing quite a bit lately, it may or may not work on your server and it's not a sure thing, but I've made about 400k off of it over the last couple months.

If you've spent any time in the new Droga or have done any research on the Droga quests you know that there are a few pretty nice items you can get. Each quest has 4 components and all of the pieces are droppable.

I've been a bit bored with actually "playing", although I do find some enjoyment hanging out in the bazaar, buying and selling items.

Once I knew the components for a few of the quests I started searching the bazaar for the pieces, and I've had luck finding a lot of them for sale individually for a very low cost. Over the course of a week or two or I've been able to gather some components and then sell them all as a set.

I do go and see if the turn-in mob is up before I gather the pieces, and on my server they have been.

Here are the things you can look for and the results of each. You could of course go to Droga and farm the pieces yourself too, I've just been having luck finding them in the Bazaar for very little money, I guess some people don't know of the quests.

If you're in the Bazaar with some time to kill do a search for some of these components and see what comes up.
