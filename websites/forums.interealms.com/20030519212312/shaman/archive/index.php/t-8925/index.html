You can only wear the patch fungal vest from FG as a vah shir, and only at 51.

But, Iskar Ceremonial BP is wearable (I wore one for 20 levels) and much cheaper. It is only 5 hp per tick, but it is only about 5k on Rodcet Nife.

Shoes.

Shoes are hard. They are generally very expensive to get good ones. I wore totemic for awhile, then I was lucky to get a mq of thurg from a friend. If you can at all get a thurg mq, do it, they are nice shoes. Otherwise, I hear barbed dragonskin shoes are nice, but they run about 12k on my server. Ouch.
