As long as they still have ally faction with True Spirit then yes you can still do this as the faction reset doesn't actually happen until you turn in the scale. Ask them to do a shield turn in or two to make sure they're maxxed for security's sake if you're worried about it (never know when ya might take a hit to True Spirit Faction after all).

And yes you can still skip the Neh part as long as your faction is high enough.

Whether it's "easier" or not is relative... if you can get a group together to do Rak you can probably do Neh well enough on the same trip as long as you can handle pulling the upstairs reavers (and generally you can find a few druids and rangers around that still need their reavers anyway to make it worth getting a bunch of folks together for the whole thing).

Just do the trip up but don't mess with Rak when he spawns and once Neh is down, get your groups to move and start buffing up for Rak while you run and do your turn in. They should be all ready to go when you zone back in.
