Yathandra
Easiest way to max out alteration is to use a lvl 1 spell and keep casting it until you get to lvl 51 specialize. Then throw points in the other specializes.

Using lvl 1 spells to help raise skill is good, because as you get higher up you will need to practice some of your not so used skills like abjuration to prevent massive spell failure.
