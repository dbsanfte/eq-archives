Hi,

I thought about a new class based on processes for the next Everquest expansion and would have like to have your opinions about it.

It would be a melee-type :
- damage table of the warrior
- mitigation/avoidance of the monk
- leather armor
- hps of the ranger

The class could choose between shapes, and each shape would grant it a process with its melee attacks.

Type of processes :
- Stun (0 sec / up to the level of the player)
- DD (2 damage / level)
- Lifetap (1hp / level)
- Snare (30 sec)
- Root (30 sec)
- Heal over time (3 ticks / total of 2hps per level)
- Mana tap
- Slow (1 percent / level)
etc...


For example, choosing to turn into a Werewolf at level 36 would grant a lifetap process of 36hps.

AAXPs would be a great way to specialize or improve those processes to make it useful to a group/raid :
- an AAXP that makes the lifetap heal the whole group
- an AAXP that makes the stun lasts 1, 2 or 3 sec
- an AAXP that makes the mana tap add mana to the group
- an AAXP that makes the DD less resisted
etc...

Why I think it would be fun :
- Variety of shapes and abilities
- Nothing really similar in the current classes (except elemental pets & warders )
- Not another Hybrid based on current classes

Why I think it would not be overpowered : You have to rely on processes
- so if you are the slower, the slow could land too late
- If you have 2 mobs to slow, you have to process on both
- the power of your process scales with your level

Why I think it would be a useful class :
- You can more or less solo, provided you turn into the right shape at the right time (slower, then healer etc...)
- You can be useful in a group, provided you choose your right function (In a caster group, turn into mana-tap shape etc...)
- Two of the same class can play together in a group an still have different functions.

So here it is, let me know what you think of the concept, the AAXPs, the different shapes etc..

Cheers !
