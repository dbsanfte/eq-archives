I suppose if you really want to PL.... um... you will REALLY miss out on learning a shaman. Much like enchanters, we have MANY spells, and really, much to learn as far as tactics. PL'ing a shaman is not recommended, but if you MUST, just use your monk to beat down a red creature, FD, and finish.

Be warned, you need to max that defense, as well as offense and any weapon skills you feel you need to use (most would just say *all of them*)

Leveling a shaman without PL'ing is fairly quick *anyway*, I have around 120 levels worth myself.
