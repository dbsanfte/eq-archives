Einnod
I'd recommend the pit in potactics. Good exp, and chance for war wraith blood and bricks of bloodmetal(both used to make bloodmetal earrings of engagement). Mobs here are mostly warrior(occasionally SK, necro, shaman). Hit in the 400s range, but not many hp. Solo pulls are easy to get. Also, about every 45 mins or so, you can hear a stampede coming, it's an emote to the zone. When that happens, get to a corner so you don't get run over by it, and watch it go by. There's a stampeding piglet that you need to try to grab, if you can. He drops a few nice items that you can sell(or use, who knows?).

Syrellian
Lately I have been soloing the Golems in PoV at the NW corner. There is plenty of room there to run around so that you can root, snare (If you have the equipment/items) and dot. I have not been using the dog dog because it seems to die too quickly, and to me, is a waste of mana healing it and what not. I usually just pull with slow, Malos, and use the following DoT spells: Epic, Breath of Ultor, Blood of Saryrn, Pox, Bane of Nife. After I land the Dots, I usually just back off, hit Cann5, Q myself, and refresh dots as needed. It takes a bit longer this way, but I have found that this is a very safe way to xp. Inbetween the times I am waiting on Cann 5 to refresh, I just Q myself and hit Cann4 repeatedly for safe mana regen. I usually get 5 or 6% per kill AA-wise. Most peeps in PoV (at least on Fennin RO) seem to like the usual camp spots: OC, IC, EC, WC.........Northwest corner is almost always open in my experiences and as I said, there is more room to run around if need be. I realize that my method of soloing is not exactly the fastest way, but for me is definitely the safest as far as PoV is concerned.
