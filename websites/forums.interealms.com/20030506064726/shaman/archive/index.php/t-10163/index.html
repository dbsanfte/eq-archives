weissblau
Idunno, I wouldn't go so far as to call our melee totally irrelevant.

If you have a cheap haste item (FBSS, Silver Chitin Handwraps, Sash of the Dragonborn) and keep haste spell on yourself, REIF damage can rival JBB damage, but it has the advantage of no cast time and less downtime from stuns. If you're tanking for the pet you might as well go that route.

That said, JBB is much more important than REIF. You can always pick up another REIF when you get the cash. If you get a line on a JBB, you need to be in a position to grab it.
