Zyphyr
1,2,3 - All generate an XP loss.

4 - is not supposed to generate an XP loss, though I have seen it happen. If you fight in the Arena (the zone, as opposed to the arenas within towns) you are guaranteed to not lose XP.

As for getting s CS rez for an XP loss in a duel - unless it is caused by a BUG (you died within an arena area), you are not entitled to one. The time you saw someone get one was an example of a GM/Guide going beyond what he was supposed to do - he (or she) was just being nice.
