Well friends, it's that time again, time to pay the coffers of my provider and keep this site going for another 6 months. This is our 5th donation drive and with a little time, we've always managed to raise the money needed to keep the servers going. I always hate making these posts

because I feel like I'm begging, but bills are bills and they are due. You always make it easy on me though in the end, so let's keep that tradition alive :)

I've been playing EQ for over four years now (hell, I just hit 65 yesterday!) and I'm still as happy with this game as the first day I logged on so long ago. I've been running this site for about three and a half years now... the last two years fully funded by contributions from you all. We all have a lot to look forward to, another expansion seems to be in the works, a whole continuance in Everquest 2 and even Everquest in its current state shows no signs of letting up.

This community has long been a wonderful resource, where people from all classes, SOE representatives, other gaming companies and people that don't even play EQ anymore come to share stories, experiences and help each other figure out this huge universe. We've consistently maintained a great environment in which to do all of this... and it's really just me behind the scenes. I have the maturity of you all to thank for keeping this place so awesome throughout the years.

Lately, I've been beta testing the newest version of vBulletin and everyone seems to like it a lot. I'm trying to work on some new technologies that will interface the boards with the static site for guides, comments on guides and spell discussions. Hopefully, when vBulletin goes final, I'll have more details on that.

So, I ask again of you, please help keep this place going. As many of you may know, I've never earned a dime from this site (well, a few of you helped me on my ring purchase ;)) during my donation drives. It all goes to keep the servers paid for and the massive bandwidth we all
consume (close to half a terabyte!) each month. It helped when I moved servers as I only have to pay $1800 per six months as opposed to $1950. However, both sites have yearly leased licenses that run $70 bucks that are both due. So, the total for this next 6 month period will be $1940.

As usual, I'll keep everyone informed as to how much money we have received so far, and provide updates when I can to how the drive is going. I still have most donor information from the previous 4 drives and will add anyone from this drive to the list as well. I still have a sort of "Hall of Fame" list I keep that I can smile over and hopefully in the future provide a permanent home and some added perks to the site. I even have this GREAT group of Japanese rangers that help a lot each time! (*waves*)

Every little bit helps.. out of about 11,000 Rangers and 10,000 shaman last time.. yes, 21,000 people, I received a grand total of just over 200 donations... yes, if you do the math, you'll see that only ONE to TWO percent of everyone on this site donates :( Each drive, I see many of the same names, all doing their best to help the site. If even 10% of the people on the site donated, I'd probably never have to ask for donations again!

Just some stats for you. Last donation drive, the numbers were as follows:

Total raised: $2024.78
Ranger donors: 172 ($1640)
Shaman donors: 30 ($250)
Other donors: 18 ($130)

I haven't been feeling the love from the shaman lately.. not sure why they aren't helping. Maybe you shaman don't want a site anymore? :) I had to drop the SK's awhile back because they were eating up too much bandwidth and not helping defray the costs. Hopefully, shaman will balance it out more this time :)

As usual, you can donate via PayPal, or you can send me a PM, or email at sunmoon@interealms.com and I'll send you my address if you want to donate via check or money order. Thank you everyone that can help keep this site going. I can't thank you all enough for the years of enjoyment I have had running these sites!!

Please include your board name and class when donating so I can give you credit!
