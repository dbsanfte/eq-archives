Gildana,
you need to open your eqclient.ini file and edit the line that reads "LoadVeliousArmorsWithLuclin=" Im assuming your is set to FALSE...change it to TRUE. Know, however, that if you load Velious textures and have any Luclin models turned on...the models that are Luclin enabled will appear without chest armor. Its kind of nice running through places like PoK...allot of the npc's there are dressed in various Velious armors...more interesting to my eyes than Luclin models...imo anyway :)
