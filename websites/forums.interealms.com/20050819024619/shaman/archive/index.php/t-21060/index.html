Haleice
I also 2 box on one PC using EQW. I have spell effects and new models on both , but sound turned off one. My spec is AMD 2100 1GB ram ATI 9700 Pro.

I've altered the EQW.ini file for EQW to limit the amount of frames per second in the window in the backround. This always helps as there's no point in drawing stuff real fast if it's not going to be seen. I also tell it not to update and lessen the priority.

[Foreground]
Vsync=FALSE
MaxFPS=60
[Background]
Vsync=FALSE
MaxFPS=30
Update=FALSE
PriorityBoost=FALSE

You may want to tone them down even further for your PC.

The Vsync, while it's nice, will also have a hit on performance if you are getting graphical lag. Setting it to false means that you get jagged images when you turn but it's slightly faster than waiting for vsync.

I also use EQ Play Nice (http://teqim.sourceforge.net/?p=eqplaynice) because it stops EQ hogging all the resources, and makes anything else run more smoothly, including a second copy of EQ. You need to update this after each patch so you may have to wait a day or so for it to become available after each patch.

Another tip for you. Don't try and zone both chars at the same time even if one has already started to zone, wait for it to finish before you start the second, or you will end up waiting a whole lot longer and risk going LD with one of them.

I hope this helps.
