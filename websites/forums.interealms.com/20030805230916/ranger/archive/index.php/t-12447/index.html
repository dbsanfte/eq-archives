The elemental warriors at the entrance to the Hole were a good place for me to solo at 52. Didn't try before that. Was doing them before getting Swiftwind and before a fairly major equipment upgrade. With self buffs, it took some mana to stay alive (ie - to heal myself) but I did not need to break out Swarmcaller or anything like that to slow them either. The crusaders hit me too hard now, at 54, to try to solo them, with better gear than I'd had doing the warriors, and with half the epic. Self buffed entirely, ie - no aego, no clarity I could keep the first 3 or 4 spawns down on my own. With KEI I could keep all 6 of them down, though there wasn't enough med time between the last and the first respawn.

They are definately doable to a solo ranger, and well before 57. Heck, at 54 most of them are light blue. None are green yet, but very few are dark blue . . .
