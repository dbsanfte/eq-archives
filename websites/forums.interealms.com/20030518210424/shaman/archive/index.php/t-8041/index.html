Lately, however, I'll duo with a ranger or rogue friend and slow, root, and DoT heavily to gain agro and do the old 'run in a circle like a foo' routine while psuedo dogdog beats 'em down. Exp moves slower per kill, but adds up faster in the long run due to the decrease in down time.
