Xatrei
ahhh - that's a bit different :)

Still the same basic principal though - unless the shaman waits until relatively late in the fight, he will more than likely draw aggro when he lands a slow. Root can help with this as long as all the members of the group recognize & understand the affect that root has on aggro. I'm constanly suprised by the number of people that I encounter that don't seem to understand (or maybe they just don't care) that aggro is proximity based with rooted mobs. I personally don't like to mess with rooting for aggro control except when in 2 or 3 person groups, or in groups of experienced people that I know and trust. I usually just take my lumps and wait for the MA to regain aggro.
