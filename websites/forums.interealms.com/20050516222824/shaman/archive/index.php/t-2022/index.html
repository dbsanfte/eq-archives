ok, my evaluation on the ice giant camp:
Great experience from 49 - 51 (wouldn't want to solo them without ebolt), exp slows down to less then a blue an hour at 52, not worth it really at 53. I would suggest taking the inner hall) spawn, less folks running around mistakenly aggroing the second front spawn (usually known as north ans south).
Root, turgur, ebolt, plague, and when root breaks just mellee him with dog. Make sure you have them slowed, they can be dual wielding and take of 250 dmg in a single round. The spawn cycle is 6 min, and I usually had the mana back up in time to take every spawn as it pops; once you time it right you get 2 kills out of every alac.
Cash is about 300 pp per hour, more if you have a bot to cash in al the loot.
