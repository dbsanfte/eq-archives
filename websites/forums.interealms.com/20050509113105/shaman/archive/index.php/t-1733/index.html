Irksome
I 2-box a 60 shaman and a 54 warrior. This is a GREAT combo, but a ton of work. To play either one well, you need one hand on each keyboard. This gets fairly stressful.

Hotkeys don't help much, either. The basic stuff you can hotkey (assist, targeting, healing, following, etc.), but during combat you need to be doing stuff on the warrior (taunt, kick/bash, positioning, disarming, etc.), and you need to be doing stuff on the shaman (debuffing, epic dotting, canni'ing, JBB'ing, etc.).

If you can handle all that work, you can do very well with this combo. Even at 54, my warrior tanks a ton better than my shaman, even tho my shaman has much better AC (1140 vs. 1000). Since hitting 54, my warrior can easily pull agro, even after slowing and crippling. DPS is decent, especially if you can get good weapons and a haste item. Basically, the warrior becomes a very efficient DoT (mana cost being buffs).

If I were to pick a 2-boxing combo to start from scratch, I'd probably go monk/cleric.

Pingpang
To dual box, you need a snare of some sort on one of the duo. Since you dont want to use a shaman, the best combo may be a druid/monk. You've got snare, heals, ports, excellent melee damage, the ability to fear kite if needed (monk has ID, druid can scare animal types) and harmony when outdoors.

There are many possible combos for a dual boxer, but from my own experiences with it you HAVE to have a snare of some type (darkness spells will do also); the ability to fear a mob makes many fights easier. A puller who can FD will also make things much easier.

You've ruled out a combo with a shaman, but you may wish to reconsider this. A shaman with a ranger or SK is a great combo.

If youre looking for the quickest combo to lvl, then a halfling druid with a halfling warrior/paladin/ranger would work (or a halfling cleric with a halfling ranger).
Refob Proudftoot
I would highly suggest NOT two boxing a monk and druid if you want to level them past 35. Druid and monk sounds really really nice in theory, until you realize that it's not possible to kill nonstop with this combo. Druid heals simply are not mana effecient enough in the 50s range to heal a monk and keep them fighting nonstop.

Warrior and cleric is something else to avoid, if you want to level to 60 at a reasonable pace. Yes, it is nice to be able to be self suffecient on heals and keep yourself alive when you're exping. The flip side of this is that warriors once you get to 50+ kill blue mobs alone incredibly slow without haste. You can generally fight non stop with cleric + warrior combo, but even at 60 fighting level 45 mobs, the exp is terrible because you kill so incredibly slow without haste and other melee.

The whole purpose of two box duoing is to be able to get exp faster than having to rely on other people, IMO at least. Downtime or incredibly slow killing = not good exp.

Shaman + warrior, on the other hand, is a great combo because you fight non stop, keep haste on the warrior AND contribute alot of damage on the shaman at the same time (thx clickies ^^).

Honestly, nothing I've seen or tried comes close to shaman + monk.
