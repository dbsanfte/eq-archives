Bigguss
My first inclination would be to duo with the Pally - why level up a monk when you've got a nice character ready to go? :)

The paladin should be great for keeping aggro, stuns, LoH, even limited self-heals. In other words, defense is covered. Great now, and better in a few levels when you won't have to worry about your pet getting beat up. At 45, he can start using a deepwater helm to self-heal or heal you as you chain-cann- nice!

I'd concentrate on bringing the offense up to snuff- meaning concentrate any money you're going to spend on gearing him up to high-ratio weapons and haste... mediocre armor is fine.

Just perusing the pally spell list- wow, they get some nice stuff!

Your team is also great against undead, you have limited single-pulling ability (lull/soothe line), and as you get a little higher, you've also got invis vs undead and emergency ressurections covered. And you're not conforming. :)
