Seredith
Were you trying to slow from range or up close once engaged? Slowing from range isnt going to work.

In my experience sometimes you're just battling with the random number generator on Kland and everything will bounce, another time it'll slide on first time. Bard with a tash stick and orb used to be your friend when he was being troublesome but those tashes no longer stack.

Don't rely on him being slowed in order to kill him is the best tactic, have good DPS and don't hesitate.
