Tsuyoi
For soloing look up the posts in these forums for Droga, Veksar, Harbinger's Spire (1st floor), Bloodfields and PoM. These will provide decent xp, plat and drops. Nothing exceptional though.

You can also do the pigpen in PoTactics. Get skilled at dropping the piglet to finish out any 65 spells that you need. The bulwark is also decent tribute. XP will be good here.

For just straight XP look up the posts for rotting golems in the PoValor.

In your shoes I'd focus on getting xp and plat at the same time. If you have someone you can trust to make spells for you go for the hobbits on the wall in PoM to get the Remove Greater Curse spell component. I just sold an RGC spell for 44k. Use the plat to get bazaar upgrades. You can get to 4k unbuffed hp and mana in the bazaar.

Grouping will get you xp much faster and the potential for better drops. If you group do DoN or OoW. The tier 1 OoW armor is nice at your level and you'll get important focuses from them. You may also want to jump on the fabled mobs since they are here. If you can find a group there are many upgrades available from the Fabled mobs. And they pop so frequently that you can expect a few upgrades a night.

Hi folks , long time lurker random rare poster....Hangurin , your topic "speaks " to me ..Especially as i am a soloist by choice and nature .

I've done my leveling , both regular xp and aa xp 95% solo . Several nice suggestions in this thread tho I as a "control" freak am not a fan of the panther / leapord spells as they bust root. But thats just me, they ARE great dps additions to our spell suite.

You stated that you have all the expansions , most 65 and earlier spells, but didnt mention wether or not you have Qiesence (sp error i know) if you have that , you are all set .

Personally I have never tried solo xp in PoV,PoI,PoJ, BoT ,or PoT - I tend to avoid zones that actually have other players in them, and those most certainly will, I went instead to Ykesha zones, Droga and PoM for my xp, with a dash of Cazic Thule thrown in for good measure. The choices i made might not be the " best" for xp per mob, but i was able to do several at once for nice consistant xp gain . Check out Torgiran Mines , Hates Fury and Nadox. With the spells we have available to us at 65 and 66 you can do some nifty lill xp jaunts there.

My suggestion would be hit torgiran mines first, the center area where the large bridge is , all mobs are pure melee type, ( until you go past, then you get some int caster type luggalds, and some sk type trolls ) nice thing 'bout pure melee mobs- we are completely set up to make them rue the day we decided they need to die for our xp pleasure.

Dogdot is more then capable to tank these whilst you canna dance. This camp spot gives you alot of room to work, all mobs being solo pulls, and a few nice safepoints to log in and out and not have to worry about getting bumrushed while you are getting set up, can be done totally self buffed, so no need to rely on kei or virtue ( note im not saying they arent great things) but you can live and level nicely here logging in and out with no need to go elsewhere for buffluvin until you want to / have to sell off loots to vendors.

The platinum skull ring vendors for 33pp, the effigy of innoruuk 70ish pp ( ithink) , worn belt buckle 33 pp and drop frequently. I'd pull a troll, get him melting with dots, slowed, then sic dogdot on him with a Q for safety, and start the next troll in my rotation..after a few shifts getting used to the spawn / area i began pulling 2/3 at once , locking them down with a ultor /saryn /root combo melting - then a dogdot for good measure ( note- i dont give dogdot proccing weops as they bust root, and i am a total control freak- i want them mobs WHERE i put 'em until *I* bust that root to reposition them . Sic the dogdot on em in turn and go thru them like sh*it thru a goose. It might not be the fastest xp gain per mob, and will slow down once you get higher..but its a good way to get used to multiple mobs at once ( no they do not summon here - also a bonus) and make some decent plat.

my magelo is in my sig, nothing on me was gained with more then 6 ppl, most solo or with a cash stash earned from my xp grinds, nothing phenomenal on me, but it works, and when i do group ( WoS / NC / RcoD ) I feel that i am able to fill almost any role required of me fairly well { slower , dps, CC, back up healer }( exception being main healer- i just dont have the mind set nor spell tools to do it- go ahead flame me i know someone out there is chomping at the bit to do so ) .
