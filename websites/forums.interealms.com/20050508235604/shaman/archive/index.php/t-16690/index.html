Sherah
Given the npc's you are currently killing, ssra isn't a good replacement or next step...doesn't sound like anyway. People tend to underestimate Ssra. You need grc (bard cursed song helps a lot) and good dps not to forget becoming highly competitive. So probably out of ssra, you can start nailing the rhags/AL cycle and see how you stand up. If you can't kill the AL decently, then you need to take a step back. Basically mobs don't stay up long on most servers in Ssra...so going for ST/Ntov route gets you in the groove for competing and nets you some real nice weapons/gear you need to make ssra happen.

Camping zlandi/klandi/lendi (good to have a cov coh mage) wasn't too bad..we didn't get into kael all that much and so that is how we keyed up..and on some servers, sonty is up a lot. Once you get into ST, you can primal up and your dps will skyrocket and ssra would be more feasible. Camping a mage in the ST tunnel prior to the FA/dragons helps gear you up much faster (the first two or 3 named in ST drop very few primals compared to the ones further in) and for the most part can take the place of NTOV loot if you go this route. By the time my guild was done with ST, most of NTOV loot was rotting after our 4th raid. Having this gear enables you to do more with less ppl and therefore will increase your competitive edge in Ssra. Your guild has to want this though and be very focused.
