Here's the tricks to winning:

The dragon AoE dispels multiple buffs, and it does a gravity flux type effect - that does a LOT of damage. Heck the fall can be from so far on high it can 20K you.

1) Everyone (no exceptions) on the raid get 15 buffs (or more). make sure that your [B]last[B] buff is levitate. Make srue your first 3-4 buffs are junk.

2) Have people cast group buffs on their own group regularly. to preserve your decent buffs. Make sure you have 1-2 group buffs memmed - quick casting, low mana cost.

3) Evereyone should have instant cast clickies of one form or another and should be spamming them like mad (most raid guilds require an instant cast clickie)

4) The dragon is immune to spells cast from outside melee range, but is easily slowable from inside melee range with just Malos on.

5) Once you engage and slow go maximum DPS (except for the restoring group buffs).
