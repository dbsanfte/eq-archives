I think you can take Lodi! Or at at least be close enough to make it worth the effort to try! Your biggest problem is YOU, unfortunatley

A level 56 warrior with 3k hp and 1100Ac unbuffed will be looking pretty tidy with cleric and shammy buffs - Im guessing what you oughta be able to get the warrior to around 4k hp and 1150 - 1200Ac - this is plenty for Lodi (if you are worried about it, buy / beg an aegolism for your tank from a high level cleric).

The biggest problem with Lodi is going to be slowing - once you slow Lodi, your tank will be absolutely fine and you are home free. So, slowing her is the key. at level 51 it is possible for you to slow, but very very hard. I would seriously recommend having a chanter along for tashani (or whatever MR resist chanter has at given level). also, any other class that can MR debuff, should do so (don't wizards get malosini?). You should have Turgurs insects, Torgors insects, and Malosi all memmed, and you should chain cast these in rotation until Lodi is slowed. The problem with chain casting these 3 is that after about 4 casts, chances are you will havre aggro, and the more you cast, the more aggro you generate. Basically it is gonna be a race to see which happens first: (1) you stick Slow on Lodi = you win (2) Lodi kills you because your tanks cant get aggro off you = you lose, Lodi then kills everyone else (3) You go oom - Lodi wins.

Right now, I would say the single biggest factor in the sucess / failure is your level. the warrior is good enough, the cleric is good enough, it is the slower that is the weak point - go level!
