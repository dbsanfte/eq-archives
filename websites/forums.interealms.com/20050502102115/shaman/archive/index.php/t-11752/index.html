Uggok
Necros beat chanters for soloing for xp with charm, though they have more limited zones. I agree that making a combo based on charm is, uh, risky at this juncture =)

The idea that any plate melee has a necro's DPS is just wrong. The idea that a necro/shaman (higher DPS, no healing required) has the same kill rate as plate/shaman (much lower DPS, significant healing required) it nuts.

Pre-PoP, solo shamans have two weaknesses: can't pull tricky spots, need more DPS.
PoP, solo shamans have three weaknesses: can't pull tricky spots, can't tank, need more DPS.

Necro brings more than enough DPS and some of the best pulling in the game all the way through, and in PoP, you just don't need to tank because xp mobs don't generally summon. The only time a necro isn't overwhelmingly the best solo class is that period when they deal with non-fearable summoners all the time, and that is the shaman's most powerful soloing period. A tank in PoP is nice, but not at the cost of gimping your DPS and wasting mana on heals.

I have never seen a "you have healed your target for X you have gained experience." Have never seen a "you tank evaded a swing you have gained experience" either. I only seen "mob is dead, here's some experience." DPS > all.

Mage is an interesting choice, with chain summoning and COTH pulling. I just don't see the overall utility or sustained DPS I get from a necro, but I have very very little experience duoing with mages. I'll have to try it soon.
