Landragoran Heronwing
I took my monk to keasora for solo work at the library area. If you're unfamiliar with the dungeon just do it like this: enter the zone, go up to the first little room. If you look to your left, there is a small drop down. Go down there and then straight thru a door to another drop down. Or you can just take a walk to the left and around and down for a while. The place has fast respawns of skellies who dont run. There are cleric class skellies further in the actual library areas and those can be annoying with the heals they like to do all the time. My monk at 45 was able to solo all those skellies and most of the library. But he was pretty twinked, fungi, good haste, high AC, etc.
