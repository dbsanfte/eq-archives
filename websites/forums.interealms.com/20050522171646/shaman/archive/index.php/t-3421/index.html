Corrupted Black Panther in WL

I was hunting black panthers for skins for our guilds GM Tailor and saw this panther. I had no clue he was for a Tunare following Paladins Quest. He conned blue and I decided to pull him.

I pulled him with a Malo as even the Haze panthers are fairly MR. I ran back to the wall where my pet was parked and cast slow on him and sicked pet. Typically I will cast a few dots on a mob while it is rooted and the pet is tanking. Then I will check pet HP's. Well, by the time I got a Pox, Bane and EPic on him my pet was dead! So the fun begins.

Pet dies, Panther turns on me and I root him to back off and Canni/Torpor. I then get the message 'You have been summoned.'
So here I am standing toe to toe with a level 55 undercon that is hitting me for 150-200 per hit.

I just kept Torporing, canning and reapplying Pox, Bane and Epic Dots between his attacks. Interrupts definitely didnt help things, but about 8 Poxx, 10 or so Banes. 8 or more epics and a countless number of JBB blasts he finally dropped. Had to reslow him 4 times total, may have had a bit of overlap on the reslow, but better safe than sorry.

Warning: Do not try this without Torpor :p

He dropped a NO DROP quest item for Tunare Paladins and that was it.

Conas
Try the coterie vampires in Maidens Eye some time. The higher level ones double for 150, flurry, and have a 200hp lifetap proc that goes off quite alot. Its not uncommon to take 400 damage or more in a single round with the harder ones. They have a shedload of hp, are fairly magic resistant, dont run, and are immune to poison and disease:) Beware trying this without malo/torpor/epic/jbb.

Druids kill them by kiting and using magic/fire.

Shamans are the only class I know of that can solo tank them. Unslowed/Uncrippled these things are pure evil.

Self buffed I have 1010ac, 2203hp.

They are a nice hard mob to work on your solo skills.
mormegil
Shimmering Rockfiends are (grey) fun since you can't JBB (immune cold it would seem) 'em and they move fast and hit faster. If slow is resisted you have a tall glassof beatdown to drink.

Sun Revenants (grey) have a lot of HP and hit pretty hard, but pretty much everything spellwise hits. You have plenty of time to malo and then slow them as they have this Fred Sanford pimp-limp.

Deep Cavern Lotus (fg). Pray they do anything but chain that lure-like DD they have. Their attack selection determines win or lose.

Warden Bruke (ew) actually was my favorite solos of late. Unslowable plus I couldn't land Bane, cold DDs or the epic dot. It was melee, send in dogdog and chain torpor yourself. The only DS I had was the 3 points from JBBP. I was sure I was dead 3-4 times, but just pulled it out. Kiva's is highly recomended, but probably would make it less fun. :)

I'm like 1100 AC, 2500 hp self buffed fyi, so if you're end-game equipped your milage may vary.
