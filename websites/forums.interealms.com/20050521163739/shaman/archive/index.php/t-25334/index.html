Toktuk Wartalon
All servers are located physically in San Diego, SOE doesn't use localized servers the way Blizzard does. I would assume that you're likely to find a wide variety of east coast guilds on whatever server you choose, just like in EQ1.

As for "newbie" servers, the game is just over a month old, so there's no need to create "newbie" servers as of yet. If you're looking for low population servers, just pick one that has a Light-Medium load at character creation.
