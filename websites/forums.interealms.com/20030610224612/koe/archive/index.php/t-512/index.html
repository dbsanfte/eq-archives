Background:
KoE was wandreing about its Kunark home - Howling Stones - doing some spring cleaning. The groups were in the process of clearing out the South wing when they came upon a new set of Mobs. Rizz noted that there was one of the dreaded carpet traps, and stops to warn the group . . .


Rizz: Ok everyone. Listen up. You see this rug here. This is a trap.

Hammer, daydreaming: I am so wonderous. I know everyone is looking at me in awe. It is so nice to hear Rizz stop the raid and tell everyone of my grandness.

Grinric, also daydreaming: Hammer, how I love the so. Where you go, I shall follow.

Rizz: Everyone got that. This is a trap. If you step on the carpet you will fall and die. So don't go near it.

Hammer, still daydreaming: Yes, Rizz, go on about my godly grapeness. Tell them all so they can hope to have a glimpse of understnading my magisty.

Grinric, still daydreaming: Hammer's purple sheen really exites me. I can't wait until we are alone.

Rizz: OK, now that that is very clear, Valfaroth, pull us some nastys.

Valfaroth: 4 Inc!

Hammer, finally awake, thinking to himself: A fight! A chance for all to see my splendor. Hmm, I wonder what's under that rug.

Hammer goes to the rug and falls to his death.

Grinric: /shout NO!!!!!! Hammer my love, I shall rescue you.

Grinric dives onto the rug and falls to his death.
