Mawafu Steelfist
Multiply the lower lvl character by 1.5 or just add half your lvl to it.
So, if you're lvl 11 you would add 6 meaning you can get XP with lvls up to 17. If your wife's warrior is 24 this means your cleric needs to be at least lvl 16 before the 2 of you can group for XP.
