Your stuff isnt that bad. Get out of the gnome skin as soon as you can. The jarsinth from KC would be an upgrade, but not for very long. Leaf scale gloves are getting very cheap in the Bazaar; Id recommend a pair of those.

Id aslo say get some Jaundiced bone Arms. Those usually run about 400-500 plat for the arms, helm, and boots. Legs and BP are much much more expensive (last time I saw the legs they still running 11K), but also pretty nice items.

As far as tactics you are in an interesting situation. I know that your rogue and shaman can group and both get exp, but Im pretty sure the exp your shaman is getting is small. My general recomendation is to head to Tier 1 planes, but I really dont think your shaman could tank there, and I dont know if you could either.

You could try some of the new LOY zones. Exp there can be good. Getting a boat in Dulak should get your shaman some good exp (and yourself some good AA) as well as some ok cash and the oocasional drop. Your shaman should have no real problem here after you hit mobs with slow...the pet could do all the work.

Hope some of that is usefull to you. GL in your endeavors.

Well, honestly the most effective way to combo a rogue and a shaman relies on the shaman having some outstanding gear to tank with.

Shaman lays on some DoTs, runs Quiessence/torpor on himself and keeps the mob rooted, rogue can then sit at max melee and still backstab.



In your case you can modify this slighty by keeping the mob rooted and rogue at max melee still I suppose, then after the rogue gets hit, move the shaman in. Mob turns to face him, you can backstab, then move the shaman out of the way all before another hit lands if you are lucky/no lag.
