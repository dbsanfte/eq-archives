At level 54 you will spend most of the fight against Woushi stunned and unable to move or cast. You need people high enough level to resist enough of her spells to actualy fight. A couple real good groups of 60's or more. I have heard rumours of one group doing her...but that is exceptional if true.

Greetings,

Killing Woushi was a guild grouping for us on Innourrk server, we killed her with about 5 groups with a full spread from 50 to 60. She proc's an AE 360 degree stun about every 3 seconds so if you take longer than that to cast your not getting anything off. We didn't wait for any MT agro situation, we just zerg'd her and she went down with no casualties. We can do it with less but I'm not sure what any minimum number of people is for any given level.

The Emerald Dragonscale Tunic (BP) is (from memory)
Ac22 Wis20 Cha10 Save disease10 and another save +10.

It still runs 15k to 20k and can be bought.
