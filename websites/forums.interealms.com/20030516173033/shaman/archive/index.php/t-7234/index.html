Canabull
Lift weights, and STRETCH!!! I never pulled a muscle in HS Football (closest thing we had to wrestling), but my Senior year, at the behest of my coach, I stopped eating anything with fat content (to lose weight), including milk. Separated my shoulder and almost hyperextended my knee.

Stretch and eat right, and if you want to lose some extra weight, just do extra NORMAL cardio work. Even a 1 hour jog once a week is better than cutting out important foods from your diet.

If nothing else, try to get in touch with a local college coach and see if he/she will give you some advice (most will if they have the time, potential recruit 8 )

BTW, good joint workout is heavy weights, short pulls. REALLY strengthens those ligaments and keeps your bones together.

Pain today, LOTS of pain tomorrow.
