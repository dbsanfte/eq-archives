Once you're in good with the Legion, gather up all of the Marauder Snout Ring 'MM's that you can find and turn them into Drill Master Dal in groups of 4. He is just inside the Warrior guild (look up and behind you when you get to their area. I'm not sure if you get bigger hits than the bone chip quest, but it sure seemed like it. It also gives some sweet xp if you want to PL a young iksar. You can almost always find the rings on vendors at the Windmill in LOIO, in FV and in Cab. They drop off outcast iksar marauders and manslayers. Note: there are two versions of the rings, only the MM ones work.
