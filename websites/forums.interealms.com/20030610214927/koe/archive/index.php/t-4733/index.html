Every Monday I get off work early (is my easy day), I first check to see if there is anything going on or if I can find a group, then drag my wifes Druid with me too OOT. If and I mean IF you can get that AC camp, good luck I have tryed to make him spawn in OOT and south ro I don't know how many time using as many tricks and secrets as I could to no advail. In fact I once got him to spawn in South ro, but it broke my heart and miffed me off. When he spawned he poped right next to the Inky merchants there in South ro, he walked over hit one and she cleaned his clock before I got there and could drag her way from him.

If you do deside to camp for the AC OOT is your best chance, I have seen whole groups set up in South Ro and KS like you have never seen in your life. Just try to keep 4 wizards and a druid from KSing you.

The clops there you have to kill in OOT is still blue to me at 54, although he doesn't hit to awful hard, he does have alot of HP. To get to him the easiest way is to catch the boat in freeport (a few invis and sow potions will make that much easier and not so deadly for a KOS orge) ride it to sister island. After the boat leaves the docks there it is the next island you come to (same side of the boat, and very short trip) with a tower on the top of the island.

The only down side to going for Jboots is you should check your faction with the gnome in Rath mountains first, a friend of mine (a Orge sk with Cazic religion) said Hasten would not give him the quest although he was dubious to him. Not sure if killing Minos in Steam front would raise that faction or not.

That is about the only tips I can give ya bro and good luck with em, I just have DC make me sow potions for around 60 pp a 10 stack now, until I can get enough alt experaince to run with near jboot speed :)
