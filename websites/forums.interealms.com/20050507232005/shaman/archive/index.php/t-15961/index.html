Chifari
The best way to do the most damage fast is Tears of Saryrn. Yes, you get one resist on average. Yes, from a pure damage:mana standpoint, it winds up a little worse than velium strike with an imp damage item (only cause a wave gets resisted). But I find it is easier to do one cast of this before the mob dies than to do two velium strikes. From dmg:mana perspective, Tears on average lands a little over 2 waves per cast, so it's (gut feeling) 1400ish base (ignoring crits) dmg on average per 450 mana, or about the same base ratio as velium strike. (Imp dmg cold item tilts the balance in favor of velium strike, although it's slower). Tears of saryrn is good burst damage, and has a chance to land all 3 waves (when it can be great).

As for dots, they're still the best damage:mana ratio BY FAR. The poster who says 2x velium strike = a BoS is only correct if the dot doesn't run to its full duration (often typical in xp groups). A fair comparison would be vengeance of eternity (30% to dot) vs. wrath of eci (35% to cold)+ crit aa. Since the foci dmg is random, it's 15.5 on avg to the dot, 17.5avg+whatever from aa (not sure of the number granted by scm3+fom, let's say 20%) or so to the nuke. Even assuming you crit 20% of the time on the vel strike (prolly a way high number, but picked it to emphasize how big a gap there is), that is 925*1.375 = 1271 dmg for 300 mana, or dmg:mana of 4.24. BoS is 2240*1.155 = 2587 dmg, divided by 535 = 4.83 dmg:mana. If you don't talk in the world of uber time gear and instead compare burning affliction iv vs. anger of e'ci (20% vs. 23%), the dot still comes out with the edge.

I did not even consider the fact that there are higher mana preservation items specific to dots (conservation of bertoxxulus, affliction efficiency iv) that further increase this gap.

(Although there is the problem of the rarity of the affliction foci versus damage foci (an itemization issue that needs addressing)).

So, assuming the dot runs full course, it's a way more efficient way to do damage than any of our nukes.

I'm just too lazy to deal with aggro and remembering to cast the dot at beginning of fight every time, so I fall into the 2x vel strike or 1x tears of saryrn (depending on resists of what I'm fighting) camp of shammies when xp'ing.
