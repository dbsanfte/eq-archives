Jinheim
There aren't many affliction haste IV items, only two that I know of. One is torch of lvaxormaomomom from bertox cycle, which I have, and the other is the pottery item.

However, there are lots of detrimental haste and general spell haste items that give a sufficient boost to the casting time of dots (in addition to slow, malos, and nukes). I personally do not feel that the slight extra reduction in dot cast time is anything to worry about. You generally aren't chain casting dots, and the speed at which a dot lands does not save your life like a faster slow or heal does. An extra half second of casting time once a minute doesn't make much of a difference.

Ditto for affliction efficiency IV items. I have a grummus shield as well, but I don't use it because it isn't worth wearing just to save me a little bit of mana when I do cast a dot. Standard mana pres (pres of xegony in my case, but mana pres IV isn't hard to come by) is good enough.

Now, the only time I bring out my dot focus items is when I am in pofire kiting at tables. I bring them out here because I am constantly stacking three dots, and the extra cast time is needed because I have a mob that triples for 880 chasing me.
