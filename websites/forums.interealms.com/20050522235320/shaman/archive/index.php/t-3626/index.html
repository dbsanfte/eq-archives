Uggok
As a troll shaman, you already have snare (if you follow Inny). SHort duration, but stops runners dead in dungeons.

Lots of classes work well with the shaman. But when looking for the best combo, keep in mind that our strength is our absurdly powerful melee defense (slow, heals, buffs) and our weakness is comparably weak offense.

Now think what EQ gives you xp for. Hint: offense

Your idea partner will have a lot of O. Rogues have the most O, but that means you are the tank, which is ideal after torpor, but you lose a lot of canni mana before torpor. Next offensive choice is the monk. Ding ding. In addition to being the #2 damage dealer, Monks are the #1 xp tank in the game, hands-down AND cheap to equip AND they have the #1 utility skill that you lack, FD.

1-60, over a broad array of circumstances, monk just cannot be beat because they tank so well and kill so fast.

My next choice would be a well-played necro, with your health regen spells they have insane mana regen and they do very very good damage and have a huge array of utility spells (mez! fd! heals! and much much more! not available in any store! order now!)

After monk and necro, rangers are like monk-lite with spells (though at the very high end they do comparable damage), then you get pallies and sks, where you give up significant damage and a little bit of tanking but gain a wide array of nice spells. Mages are also not to be overlooked.
