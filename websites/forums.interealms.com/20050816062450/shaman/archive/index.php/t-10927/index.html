Kriel Darkbane
Blind does not work like root. Root makes a mob attack the nearest player. Blind doesn't.

Blind makes a monster run away, unless the person who is at the top of the aggro list is in melee range. Then it attacks that person. If the person at the top of the list isn't in melee range, the mob runs away.

You can (rarely) see this with a paladin who's using flash of light to hold aggro. Usually they'll hold aggro, and the mob will stick to them. But if a caster in the group manages to gain aggro (by overnuking/dot'ing and then sitting down), the mob will run off and do its very best to find a big train.
