Filoranae
There are actually 3 Comm1 camps and 2 Comm 2 camps.

Comm1 :
Assassins room - Seems to be the quickest spawn rate. Need a rogue to pickpocket the door in.

North C1 - from the temple front door, veer right (past the see invis snake), thru the short L corridor, the next room he can spawn at any of the lower level spawns. Been a few months, I think 9 possible spawn points here. All mobs are PH's, clear them all.

South C2 - from the temple front door, go up the ramp in front of you, thru the door, thru the L corridor, first door on your right. There are 8 spawns in this room, he can pop in any of these spots so while some people pacify thru it, it is better to clear it. Then thru the short corridor, there is the mirror to North Comm1, another 9 spawns where he can spawn.


Comm2
North C2 - up the tube from North C1 - 9 spawns in this room, again I think he can spawn in any of these spots. Then out the door of this room, there are 4 spawns do the left and 4 to the right, and he can spawn in any of these as well.

South C2 - up the tube from South C1. The only camp I never actually did, I think it is the mirror of North C2 but I'm not sure about the 8 spawns outside the main room.

From months of camping these on various toons, I'd say :

NC1 is the easiest of the camps for a shammy, you can solo it.
C2 is the hardest to get for most classes because they can't get to it alone, and depending on the skill / abilities of your group it can take 5 minutes to an hour (ugh) to get everyone up to C2. Then after you are up there it is a monotonous grind to kill the PH's over and over and over waiting for the Commander to pop.

South camps seemed to have better drop rates, but maybe it just seemed that way because a guild we were competing against was always there and we were always in North ;)

I have killed so many damn snakes it is beyond reason....

It is true that EVERY snake on F1 and F2 are potential placeholders. Even the once in the entry room - killed one commander there before.

No camp is any "better" than another camp in regard to the pop rate... it just appears that way because there is a high concentration of MOBs in certain areas (e.g., assasin's room).

People usually kill at the tubes for two reasons:

1) They are pretty clearly identified so as to easily establish your "camp" to avoid cross-pulling.

2) You can pull from areas where BOTH commanders may spawn. Most people need both commanders so this gives folks a shot for them.

Me personally? I liked the Rhag2 tube (South tube as described before) because once I'm there, I can be pretty choosy over who I allow to join my "farming" session and also because it's the area with the most least contested spawns (I have no worries about someone quickly moving in to kill my commander pop if I'm engaged with something else).
