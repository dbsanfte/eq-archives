Numrum
The spectral courier spawns in the stables area in one of three locations. One is in one of the houses to the east. Two mobs spawn in each house, and the spectral courier can spawn in the southern one.

The other two are in the houses to the west. The southern one has a main room and a back room. Two mobs spawn in the main room and two in the back room. The courier could spawn in the farthest spawn point in the back room.

To be honest, I'm not sure about the other spawn point because those are the only two places I've seen him. My monk friend who was pulling said he spawned one other place.

Just keep clearing out the houses and eventually he'll spawn. Usually in a 4 hour night I see him spawn twice or so. About 1 in 5 he has the book we need, the other times he has the scrolls that monks and sks need.

Good luck to you.
