Xev_Grammar
The PoV/PoS flag is one group doable.
The CoD alternate key is solo-able.
The HoH key is duo-able (but you have to be creative-- like getting a vexed banshee to attack the nightmare up top and getting summoned, then fading agro).
The Torment key is duo-able, except for the Terror Matriarch part.
The Tactics key is solo-able (duo or group preference)
The BoT flag is one group doable-- the minis may need more than one group unless you're uber-- can only get 3 people flagged at a time (minis drop 3 medallions at a time, and you can only single-character quest the BoT flag).

By solo-able I mean 60+ bard or less. Any other class can probably solo but it would be a pain in the arse.
By duo-able I mean 64+ bard and 65 shaman.
By group doable I mean a decently outfitted group that isn't botted-- you really need to be on your toes, especially for PoV/PoS.

Nytmare
Just Plane of Tactics. Quest (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2292) in Plane of Storms gives your group a key/flag for alternate entry.

Plane of Valor, Plane of Storms, Bastion of Thunder, Crypt of Decay, Halls of Honor, Plane of Torment have all recently been opened so a key is no longer required. Entry quests for those zones are still doable if you're inclined - good for increasing the power of your Intricate Wooden Figurine charm.
