I'd give him back the twinkage you don't care for and fill in those items yourself with ones you would like. That's the honorable way to go about it.
Give those GEBS back to him, as well as any other items you don't care for. Work for the items you want, and buy them with your own pp. I guess you don't have to strip down and go naked, but once you have an item in hand to replace what he gave you, it's only fair to give the item back to him. After all it was a gift, requiring time and effort on his part to obtain. He can sell them for pp then and tho he may not "approve" of your particular gear choices, he has no reason to be upset with you.

I've twinked several of my RL friends who now play. One of them had a similar situation. He decided to be a rogue and I twinked him out pretty decent. I gave him banded, with a Iksar regen bp, a slimy poon for stabbin and another dagger for offhand when he was ready for it. I gave him a couple swords to use as well to train 1hs. Oh and I gave him dragon arms and dragon boots (from WW). So suffice to say, he was a well equipped noob. He got himself to lvl 20 fairly fast and made a twink necro.
He then decided he liked necro better and switched mains. I was a little peeved cause I had spent a bunch of decent items twinking a char he won't play again. But he grew into his powers and got most of his equipment himself. The bp and boots and such he gave to his RL friend who started playing shortly after he did. I've met the guy too and that was fine that the twinkage was going to a worthy cause. I later gave him more items to twink the necro with, but most of the stuff he wears is stuff he gained himself, and gained pride in doing so.

Sooo. While nobody has the right to tell you what equipment to wear, it would be horribly rude to sell off the gear he gave you. Offer it back to him as you replace it, with thanks for the use. If he wonders why, just tell him you've learned a lot about the shaman class and these items just won't work properly for you.
