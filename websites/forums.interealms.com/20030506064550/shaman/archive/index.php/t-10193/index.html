When I duo with a melee that might get eaten by planes mobs I usualy just agro kite in a circle while they beat the piss out of said mob. Velk's and Chardock come to mind too.

Now that Velk's is pretty much deserted, you should be able to pull plenty of spiders. Shaman slows and stacks a few dots, rogue then just beats it down.

Chardock has great exp all the way up to lvl60 in the Levi tunnel. It would be a little slow as the shaman gets close to lvl60, but it's very steady.

Once the shaman hits lvl60, then you'll pretty much have to group in the Planes.
