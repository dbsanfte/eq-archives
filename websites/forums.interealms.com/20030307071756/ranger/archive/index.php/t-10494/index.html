Endless Quiver has NO innate haste included. Do not get rid of your fleeting quiver. =)
RangerKeaianea
To further expand...

You will still gain the haste from the fleeting quiver even if you do not have an arrow in the quiver (say, the single arrow is in your ammo slot). However, if you remove the fleeting quiver from your inventory, you will no longer have the haste associated with the fleeting quiver.

Besides, the quiver is good for storing the other arrows that may be of use to you (acrylia, fire, magic ice damage / wood elf cultural arrows / shardwing arrow (is that the right name?))
