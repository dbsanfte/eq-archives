Padraic
Basically, I think your method of solo'ing will change alot over the course of your shaman's life. Biguss posted a link to a solo'ing guide that is pretty handy.

Iirc, you are level 15. Pretty much meleeing is the way to go. As the guide mentioned, 19-24 are the worst levels for a shaman. Your spells aren't so hot, the mobs are getting tougher, etc. Hang in there until you hit 24, it all gets better then.

I did a combination of solo'ing and grouping throughout Pad's life. Solo'ing occassionaly is important, to keep your skills up. The single most important one is defense. Try to keep it maxed as much as possible. Defense raises = free defense.

At some levels, you might feel more comfortable root dotting, at others, tanking. Some people like Kiting stuff. I never could get into that myself tho.

As far as race is concerned, I wouldnt worry about that to much. Yes, there are upsides and downsides to every race. And a consensus has mostly been met on which one is the "best", for most people. But, the difference isn't gamebreaking, and you can be happy whatever race you chose.
