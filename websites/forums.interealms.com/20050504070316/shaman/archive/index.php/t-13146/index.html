I'm one of those lucky people who got whupped doing Rak after they changed the resists on mage pets.

First time we had 2 mage fire pets and a group on standby ready to jump in if things went bad. The fire pets were both losing hp to the area effect so the mages couldn't effectively chain cast by alternating and they were losing their hp fast enough that rak aggroed the clerics. Things went downhill from there.

Second time, we went in with a solid 60th tank, a cleric a rogue a wizard a necro and myself. The outcome was never even in question. Also it was remarked that with the resist changes the necro specter pet was much more efficient and needed no healing at all due to it's drain life proc.


*****Things you want to keep in mind.******

-Have everyone bind at the com entrance and clear the way before you trigger rak. That way if things go bad people can run back and keep fighting, healing etc .... (this would have saved us a lot of trouble first time)

-Pets resists are now based on their caster's resists.

- Rak's ae has a very short range, casters can stay well out of range and do their thing.
