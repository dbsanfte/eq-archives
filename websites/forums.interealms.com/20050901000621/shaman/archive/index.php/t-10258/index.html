agoz_rz
You've got a couple options (in my long time since playing EQ some info might be off, but heres what I recall).

At that level, you can either...

Use your dr debuff (I think you get one at 39) until it sticks, quick recast time quick cast and it'll stick sooner or later, even if his resists are good. Once thats on, the safe way to do it is to stick scourge then kite him till it wears off, and repeat. This is a very safe tactic (very un mana costly) and it'll take a while, but it should work.

Another option is make it quick and take advantage of your very pvp-oriented 39 spells (however, this method can be VERY mana expensive). Malxxx, Blind, Dot, Melee (if you dont have a decent weapon/skill bag this part), Root if he tries to run and you wanna melee, other wise keep him blinded and refresh dot. use VoS, its very good damage and fast for your level. The only problem is that, even if he has crap resists, it can be resisted alot.

edit: for second method, you can swap blind for slow and tank him if you want. The nice thing is that slow will last a whole lot longer than blind, but its harder to land and you will be taking damage so will prolly have to heal.

If he has decent resists, I'd go for the first method. You can alway so do some combination of the two.

zathrys
Here is some advice from a monk....while malxxxmay be decent debuff, it can be canceled with cancel magic, an 11pp crystallized pumice clicks this effect, it will also remove blind...if you have pet sic it on him, with a 3 sec cast time and no channeling 1 hit will interrupt the monks cast....slow is your friend...monks HATE being slowed and since it uses a disease cure most monks cannot remove it. Most monks also concentrate on mr/cr/fr..if he is iksar his cr will prolly be low unless he has some nice resist gear...fast casting cold nuke can help..stacking your poison dots will chew hp fast. And the idea that ALL monks can be defeated with nukes is a fallacy, I dueled a 51 wiz when I was 50, 1 IC landed for partial dmg (my cr was about 40), no snare, no root, no stun landed (mr 75), he had jboots and I had run3, while jboots were faster by the time he would turn to cast I was there to interrupt. Any monk worth his salt will have 2 fast 1handers for fighting casters and a good 2hb if there is a ds. An IFS (38/40) hitting for 200+ pretty much negates the dmg of any ds. Basically cast dots and kite him if you have pet, if slow lands he is done.
