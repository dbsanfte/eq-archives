WALKTHROUGH TO GET TO ARMOR VENDORS IN SKYSHRINE:

Go through the door on the opposite side of the door that leads to WL by the CS entrance. Go right, then left, then left. This takes you downstairs. Just keep going straight and you will reach the teleporter. You will then follow a small path and enter the maze.

Navigating the maze:

I found this map helpful for the maze: http://www.eqmaps.com/m2/skyshrine_maze3.gif

The quickest way through is going from the entrance to F, then G, then P, then the exit.

After this it's pretty hard to get lost, just keep following the outside.
