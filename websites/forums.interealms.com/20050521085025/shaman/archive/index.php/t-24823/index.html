Giac
Shaman survivability has nothing to do with DoT aggro(DoT aggro is broken and a is a problem on its own). Shamans can't tank slowed mobs anymore due to Slow mitigation. A plate tank will survive longer against an unslowed mob than a Shaman of similar gear will against a slowed mob nowadays. This might be intended so I won't ask for a design change.
Fact is you can't realiably self heal anymore as your are constantly forced into a channeling roll and the damage you take is still much higher than it used to be. On top of this, healing will create additional aggro, thus making it a questionable tool even when you can use it.

Because of the points above Shamans need a short term ability that enables them to endure damage. Vindictive Spirit does that to a degree, but it is still

1. Resistable(seems to be less of a problem in Omens, but very frequent in GoD high end zones)
2. Not instant casting(imagine getting summoned, yeah it happens!)
3. Very range restricted(if the mob isn't in range, i.e. 50", the spell will still go off and just not affect it, thus not placing the recourse on you, but it will grey out for 30 seconds)
4. Not working like other PB AEs(you have to target an NPC to cast)

Nonetheless, it's definitely the best spell a designer has come up with in years. I'd like to see an upgrade for this in the future as it really adressed a huge Shaman deficiency. (Nice work, Rytan).

I'd like to see Vindictive Spirit to be a 0.5 second cast. Having a 100" range and be able to be used targeting oneself. Other than that, the spell is great. Future upgrades should improve the recourse and the slow %.
I know that this is wishful thinking, but I don't think it would alter the spell too much.
