Go to Nedaria and do a /corpse. If the message is "the corpse is too far away to summon", use a chipped bone rod (or necro, SK, or bard) to find it. If the message is "you have no corpses in this zone to summon" then try again inside the instance or simply petition. If nothing else, this will let you know real quick what zone your corpse is in.
