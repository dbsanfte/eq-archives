Gloriana
If you want to give reputation, click the small talking head button next to the Edit button at the bottom of someone's post. A pop up box will appear and you can either select "I Approve" or "I Disapprove." There is a space for comments as well.

I'm pretty sure the feedback is anonymous, you can of course leave your name in the comments section.

If you've already given that person feedback and haven't fulfilled the requirement of giving 20 other people feedback before returning to them, you'll receive a message. If you've already given your 10 feedbacks allowed in a 24 hour period, you'll also receive a message.

You can see which of your posts have received reputation hits by clicking on the User CP button.
