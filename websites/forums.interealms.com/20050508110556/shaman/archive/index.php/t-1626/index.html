The best place that I've found for IGS so far is the room just south of the red "4". Pulled around 10 sets out of there so far. The city area should be good as well, but I think I've just had bad luck. Mainly, you want the areas with the highest concentration of golems and revenants.

Places that are fun xp for one group are 4, 6-8, and 11. Lots of mobs to keep you busy in these areas. Lots of golems too, which is always a bonus. There's always somewhere to move that has mobs spawned.

For lower level groups, the area around A, D, black 13-14 is pretty fun. We called this the castle. It's a fun place to crawl. It's too easy for a high 50's group though.

This is one of my favorite zones, although I haven't been there since it was changed.
