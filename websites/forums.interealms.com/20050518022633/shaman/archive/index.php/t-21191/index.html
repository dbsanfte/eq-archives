I am on a Zek Server (with Vigdis and Astaa, incidentally), and I am too ghetto to be any great shakes at PvP, even leaving aside the fact that Shaman are horribly underpowered in PvP - my gear and AA suck too much to be much of a threat, but I do my best...

I find the added risk inherent in the PvP server ethos keeps the game more interesting for me...I don't claim to be better - I am sure if I played on a non-PvP server i would be in Plane of Time now, but PvP server is what works for me.

If there was a PvP section it would, alas, be filled with posts complaining about how screwed shaman are in PvP.
