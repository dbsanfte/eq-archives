Rotun
I've been playing for somewhere around 4 years and have almost always played my main, a ranger. I have a few alts that I've started over the years, a 30-ish warrior, a mid-20s rogue, a 30-ish mage, and a 62 shaman. Every now and then I jump on one or the other just for a little variety.

However, for a long while (say the past year or more), grouping with these characters is just terrible. First, there aren't many low or mid level characters running around these days, which is understandable given the age of the game. Second, when I do get a group most of them are awful - constant afks, greedy looters, and very stupid mistakes caused either by being ignorant of their class, or just not plain not caring. This is why my only alt that has made some progress leveling is the shaman because he can solo. With that shaman, I've noticed that the overall quality and fun of groups starts to go up in the mid 40s and 50s.

So my question is this: In today's game, can you still have fun starting a brand new group-dependant alt? Or am I being too negative and maybe have just had a really, really unlucky run of bad group experiences?

The true newbie doesn't know all the abilities of the different classes at the different levels. He doesn't know that the ranger is not supposed to taunt over the warrior. (I mean, they get Taunt, right? Aren't they supposed to use it?)
