Sudedor
The Hole has a key required for entry to the zone. This key can be purchased from a vendor in Paineel itself.

Due to a glitch in the engine it was previously possible, and easy, to bypass the need for this key. However with the advent of DX9 collision detection has been revamped and made this previous exploit more difficult to utilize.

Long story short, easiest way to be certain of getting your entire group into the Hole is to spend some time using one of the various methods of improving Paineel faction and buy the darn key.

Once inside The Hole there is another key you might want to get if you have no rogue. It drops off various mobs, I got one off an Old Contruct. I think this key is called "A Sacred Key" but I really don't remember for sure.

The entrance to the zone of The Hole is an underwater rock door which is locked. You can either:
shrink and wiggle/duck through a crack in the rocks, which is now more difficult, or
have a rogue pick the door lock, or
buy the key, sold in Paineel for 106pp on the vendor Rallia Hapera (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=4893)

To get into the city of Paineel to buy the Hole Key you need a Paineel key offered by the two skeleton guards. You need amiable Heretics faction to get them to give you a Paineel key. You can get Heretics faction either by killing Erudin guards or slaughtering kobolds.

Within the zone of The Hole (now called Ruins of Old Paineel), mobs drop keys to access deeper areas of the zone including High Scale Kirn's lair.
