Heya,

Thought I would give you all some tips on Glowing runes since they can be a pita to get...This is in noway a sure way or an easy way to get the runes but some help. Will say some camps that can be 2 boxed or camped with a friend. If you equipment aint so good bring another friend or two :).

In ruined city of dranik you have the Kyv Bowhound and Stoneworker Stonetooth, can just run between these two camps.

In MPG you can run between two camps that are fairly close to eachother, Blind Hunter Rekamu and Odiumate Isienne, OI can also dropp a 55hp aug which is nice.

In RS you have the Prefect camp, Dogs and Pyrliens aint social so easy to pull. This mob dont only dropp glowing runes but also a sweet 65hp aug. (Just watch out for the roamers, I had no problem pulling this camp with my SK)

Also in RS you have the Researcher/Summoner camp, these two nameds can spawn as charmers so its not allways possible to 2box this camp, when I have done it I have had a bard standing there singing mana and resist for me, very easy to box now with melody :). Also in this camp you can get 70hp or 70mana aug which is sweet.


GL on getting your 70 runes :)
