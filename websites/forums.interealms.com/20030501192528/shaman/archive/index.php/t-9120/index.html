Thurdar
I always go down over the waterfall. Just make sure people stay close to the wall and you'll be OK, the pool at the bottom isn't all THAT small :). I usually ask the cleric to DA before jumping, that way if anyone else screws up and dies they can be rezzed back in.

Expect about 3 mobs when you hit the bottom, nothing too dangerous, I don't think you'll get any golems on the initial jump, just some elementals and an imp. After that you can pretty much pull singles to the undead tower.

If by warrior epic guy, you mean the scalekeeper, he's non agro. Not sure if he's social, I've always made it a point to fight well away from him.

Once you get to the undead tower remind everyone no AoE, Invis to Undead and walk up to the last switch-back in the ramp before you get to Kirn's level. That's a safe spot to pull to.
