SoW potions are trivial to me now, so what potions should I make now? I want to beable to make some money off of the potions to help cover costs. Please post any suggestions.
uglytobone
Make shrink (Ant's) potions. They do not sell as fast as sow, but if you are able to sell what you make it will help lessen the costs. Also, the additional profit on sows due to skill ups will help offset your expenditures. Pre-Bazaar I was able to skill up to trivial on shrinks at a small profit.

The increased profit on sows will eventually pay for skillups anyway as cost per stack decreases to about 55pp. I do not make shrinks for profit, but I still do well on just sows.

Aquatic haunt also sells, but extreemmmmeellly slowly. Same with Antiweight. Don't overstock unless you have lots of bank room and patience.
