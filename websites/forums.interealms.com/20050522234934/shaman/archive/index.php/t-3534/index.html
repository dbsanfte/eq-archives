Landragoran Heronwing
Iksar get some very nice armor from the kunark expansion that other shamans do not. Sebilite Scale armor. The legs are like 16 ac 6 wis and other stats. The Tunic is like 24 ac 5 wis or something. It drops in sebilis, often deep sebilis. But it's twink armor that people sell pretty cheap, so you can definitely buy it. Kinda like JB armor . . which sells for VERY cheap these days if it's not a rare piece like bracer, legs, or bp.

Honestly if I had it to do again I might go lizard. The natural AC boost means that when you can acquire velious armor you will be AHEAD of the other shamanic races in the stats/AC department. Up till then you won't be much behind.

One suggestion tho, and pay this careful heed. WORK ON DEFENSE! Spend the time each level to melee stuff and raise defense five points every single level until lvl 40 rolls around and you can laugh at all the lvl 50s with like 150 defense and absolute CRAP ac in their Jaundiced Bone armor. Meanwhile you are wearing chain and have like 200 more ac than them. Work defense . . . every level you need 5 points of defense. Don't forget it or you WILL regret it.

Finally, iksar has the best overall package for a shaman. Trolls fall behind with the AC thing. The hp totals won't be much different . . . one nice piece of gear different most likely. But iksars get 40 additional AC naturally, which is so nice. Plus the regen makes for a smoother ride.

The only real downside IMHO is the exp penalty which is rather harsh. You will be consoled however with the smoother playing offered by a race with so much regen. Basically you will exp longer, but easier.

My monk is iksar instead of human. The choice was easy and even the big exp penalty wouldn't make me change anything.
