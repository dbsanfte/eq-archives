Padraic
I don't have to get in pickup groups much anymore. Have a pretty solid group I run around with now. So take this with a grain of salt.

About the only place I actually see people lfg is probably Maidens Eye. I'm not sure how successful those people are at finding them however. They seem to be looking for awhile.

Akheva is pretty good xp, although not much in the way of loot. It's pretty much a bring your own group kind of place, unless you are a wizard kiting at the entrance.

Acrylia isn't bad xp either, but it's DEFINATELY a bring your own group kind of place as well. And I have yet to see a drop of any sort, except an occasional gem, acrylia, or the black acrylia (used for making the smithed weapons)

Those are my favorite xp spots in Luclin. I can take Maidens Eye in small doses. The last time or two I've been there, it looked like the Karnor's Castle crowds might have gone back to Karnors. You weren't trained 2 seconds after zoning in.

I try to avoid Fungus Grove when I can. The caller quest there is fun, but I haven't seen any large rewards for doing it. We farm the lotus things occasionally for the potc quest. But shrooms and bandits have to be some of the most boring xp on the planet. I'm not sure how many people actually look for and actually find groups here though.

The grey is an outdoor airless zone, and alot of the stuff is easily soloable at 55. I dont see any pickup groups there though. The xp is beaten quite handily (for me anyway, your mileage may vary) by Akheava and Acrylia. I'd stay away from the Shissar unless you have a jbb or epic (I don't have a jbb, so I don't know if they hit for full damage. I just know they were a rough solo until I got my epic, being almost, but not totally, immine to poison and disease)

All that is on xegony btw. Your server might be different.
