XeroOmega
the video card is nice, you should be able to run EQ perfectly fine on it, just be sure to read up on SOE's community forums about problems with specific model video cards.

The HDD is pretty average, should suit your needs just fine (a 7200 HDD will reduce loading/zoning times), personally I prefer alot of storage space, because I do alot of graphics and media work in the times I'm not at work and not on EQ. 200+ GB would be my preference, but they are obviously more expensive.

CDRW/DVD combo drive, just like the HDD, pretty standard stuff, though your CD drive will have no bearing on the performance of your system for EQ. I'd prefer a DVD+R/CDRW drive, but again they are more expensive.
