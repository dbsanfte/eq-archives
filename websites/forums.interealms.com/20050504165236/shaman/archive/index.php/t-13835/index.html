Caelum
Put as much as you can into WIS, the rest into STA.

Charisma is nice to have, but not vital. At lower levels, the price differences are negligable. At higher levels just buy some charisma gear, put it on, and buff your charisma those times when you buy and sell.

Charisma only helps when you are buying or selling from vendors. Stamina means extra HPs, which will come in a lot more handy a lot more often.

I'm pretty sure Sciek's kidding here, but the +20 (or is it 25?) cha steins are so readily available, even this ugly iksar can get decent prices.

Sta is (at least mid game) one of the hardest areas to raise. As you move up in levels most of the gear you'll get will already have some wisdom on it and as you progress further, you'll actually be trading a lot of it out for sta/hp gear. All that to say, focus on sta after you bump your wisdom up enough to be comfortable. You might want to decide before you create your shaman what your starting gear will be. If you have enough wisdom items available, sta might be your better bet...
