Lately, more and more druids are hunting solo using animal pets to almost effectively double our DPS output with a noticeble decrease in mana consumption. The downside has always been the huge increase in risk. This has been somewhat offset with the inception of the AAXP Ability, Dire Charm. Dire Charm is losing popularity in PoP now though, because the level cap on possible pets is rendering them relatively ineffective against the new, tougher mobs.

My Druidy perspective on whether Rangers should be better nukers or healers is this - To each their own. This is the mantra we teach all the widdle puppy druids. Don't let anyone tell you how best to play your class, since being able to find what works best for you is what will help you emerge as a creative player that thinks on his/her paws.

IMHO opinion though, the ability to Heal is a hell of a lot more useful than nuking simply because while you will only see marginal upgrades in your DPs in combat. On the other hand, your viability as a single lone player will shoot through the roof. ESPECIALLY in a PvP scenario.

Just my thoughts.
