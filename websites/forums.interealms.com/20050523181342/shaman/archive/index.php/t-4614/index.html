Eb spell is from the deep - I copied this long long ago from the old boards. I am sorry I don't have credit to anyone for it.

Anyhow, enter The Deep from Echo Caverns. The first tunnel after you enter goes straight back and comes to a T intersection. Go right at this T. Follow this tunnel back...now I forget if it curves to the left or it comes to a T again...either way you go straght back and go left... At the next T intersection there take another left.

VERY shortly after this left you come to a deadend. There will be two mutant guards at the front of the alcove and in the back will be either the deranged or a placeholder. We have had good luck getting higher concentrations of the deranged by leaving his guards up in the front and only pulling his spot (level 60 caster with a non damage spell should get him solo every time). Anyhow, it really isnt that far in, and with the group you have it should be a cakewalk and pretty good alt exp to boot Good luck
