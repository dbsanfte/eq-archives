Jinheim
When I fought rak, his melee damn near killed me before his AE finally went off and finished me. I was completely unbuffed, though. Fully raid buffed, with some fire resist gear and buffs, you should be able to handle him fine. Just turn in the tear and start healing yourself. Wait until pets have aggroed for a few seconds, and then back off. His AE is not nearly as bad as some people make it out to be. It is also a good idea to bind nearby, just in case you do die. I would definately not want to miss the boss mob of my epic going down.

As for the amount of healing capacity you need to take him down, we did it with a 52 cleric and myself (57 shaman at the time) as the only healers. We did have KEI, though.
