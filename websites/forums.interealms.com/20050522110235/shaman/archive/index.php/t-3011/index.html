"Shaman (pronounced SHAH-maan) is a word from the language of the Tungus people of Siberia. It is a proper noun and does not denote sex. Both male and female would be called Shaman. Plural is Shamans, not Shamen."

Don't take my word for it, do your own research. I just wanted to help put a stop to the misconceptions, especially since some people actually correct others saying that "shamen" is the correct plural form...
