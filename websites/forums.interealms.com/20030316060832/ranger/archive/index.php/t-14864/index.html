Benaveriel
Well, here's my take on it. I hope this isn't too confusing, this is actually the first time I've examined the exact numbers. :)

Warder's Protection (WP) doesn't stack with the following:
Call of Earth (CoE), Strength of Nature (SoN), Call of the Predator (CoP), Shield of Spikes (SoS). It also doesn't stack with druid/mage damage shields.

Force of Nature (FoN) doesn't stack with CoE (but does stack with WP).

FoN doesn't stack with Greater Wolf Form (GWF)

Nature's Precision (NP) doesn't stack with SoN (but does stack with WP).

Thorncoat stacks with everything. As does Storm Strength, Skin Like Nature, Resist Cold/Fire, Eagle Eye and probably others I'm forgetting - so I'll leave these all out of it for now.

My usual choice for level 60 is:
Indoors: WP, FoN, NP
Outdoors: WP, GWF, NP

Section with the actual numbers, skip if you're not interested
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
WP gives 21 DS, 70 ATK, 13 AC, 75 HP
CoE = 25 AC, 4 DS
SoN = 75 HP, 25 ATK
FoN = 3 DS, 15 ATK, 3 regen
SoS = 17 DS
CoP = 40 ATK
GWF gives 40 ATK
NP = 15 ATK

So, available combinations:
Outdoors only:
CoE+SoN+CoP+SoS+GWF = 25 AC, 75 HP, 105 ATK, 19 DS.
WP+GWF+NP = 13 AC, 75 HP, 125 ATK, 21DS
Anywhere:
CoE+SoN+CoP+SoS = 25 AC, 75 HP, 65 ATK, 19 DS
FoN+SoN+CoP+SoS = 0 AC, 75 HP, 80 ATK, 18 DS, 3 regen
WP+FoN+NP = 13 AC, 75 HP, 100 ATK, 24 DS, 3 regen
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Remember, on top of these you can have Skin Like Nature, Thorncoat, Chloroplast, Storm Strength, etc.

So, unless you really need the AC from CoE, or really want a druid/mage damage shield, I'd recommend using WP - it's much easier, lasts ages, takes fewer buff slots, and has superior ATK. Use it with NP, and FoN indoors, GWF outdoors.

Edit: Forgot that NP didn't stack with SoN.

MoP is a lower level version of CoP, and as such I ignored it. It doesn't stack with CoP or WP.

CoE (on Velious BPs) is a Velious expansion spell, WP is a Luclin spell, and a very nice one at that. WP came after CoE was already on our BPs, and I for one don't have a problem with that, especially since I still cast CoE on other people a lot - it still saves mana if you have a Velious BP.
