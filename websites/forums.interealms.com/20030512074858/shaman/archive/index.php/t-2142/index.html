I was actually moderately displeased with my MX440 until about a week ago when I got fed up and cranked the Core clock from 270 to 300 and the Ram from 400 to 460. Oddly enough, this dual proc board has had some quirks to it and overclocking my video seems to have smoothed them out :P

How much are you looking at spending? To go from an 800 to a 1900 is likely going to mean a new motherboard, and possibly new ram as well.
