Well, its not gonna be easy to just get a pick up group or even 5 good friends/guild members to get these. BP gems only drop off the toughest mobs in those zone. In SG its the underwater city, in Velks is probably up near Velk himself, and DN must be deep in the depths (I know Vaniki can drop 3 at a time).

If you do go to those places it will take time. Keep in mind BP gems that drop could also be the caster or priest ones. And even a group of high 50's could have troubles in any of these places.

I recommend exp'ing in places where items drop that you can win and sell. Velks, Seb, maybe even Karnors or Chardok. With patience and luck you may be able to scrape the money together in a couple/few weeks. Trust me, it will be worth it.

The best place for BP gems, bar none, is the siren temple in SG. Invis, swim down the well from up top and go up a couple ramps and to the back corner. Pull to there, with a L60 pulling you should get very few multi pulls. Make sure to load up on MR gear, an ench/shaman helps here for slows and MR. The sirens are enchanters and will charm/gflux/dispell so MR is very important. i have done this area with 3 L60's with minimal problems. Good exp and about a dozen BP gems in 2 hrs.
