BlargX
Hand the three shissar organs in to Drenic Garrison in Katta, first floor of the huge cathedral next to the zoneline to Tenebrous.

Faction is incredibly horrible, and all faction hits are miniscule. Frankly, I wouldn't bother if I were iksar unless you are enjoying the experience of killing the mobs. Coterie vampyres give a vanishingly small faction positive faction hit, killing in seru gives tiny hit, and turning in pyrrohitite stones to Berk Thurstone(dwarf) one at a time or vampyre ashes(unstacked) and blood 4 at a time and handing a guy in the warrior guild the shrieking substances 8 at a time, combined into the little box he gives you, are all quests that raise faction. Shrieking substances and pyrrohitite drop off sonic wolves. Berk Thurstone is the only PC you can faction with on these quests till your faction makes dramatic improvements. The pyrrohitite is unstackable and weighs 10 each.

Iksar can still do the EB necklace quest. My iksar friend ran into the quest room(either invis or FD flopped his way in, I forget) had a chanter he was grouped with first charm Drenic Garrison. Then the iksar handed in the 3 organs but DID NOT click trade. Chanter invised him, THEN he clicked trade. Boom, got necklace.

My Rallos Zek ogre shaman did more than a solid month of insane hours camping faction items, in addition to having killed MANY vampires and killing a fair amount in Seru, too, but even though he didn't start out KOS in the first place, he couldn't get the quest. So he had a bard friend do the charm/invis trick, boom got the necklace.

By the way, you can do this quest over and over again, for more faction hits. Might as well load up on organs while you're getting them; they start to rot in any prolonged killing session.
