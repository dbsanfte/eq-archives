So anyway, I got the first 3 things on that list. Runspeed 5 is only 5% slower than SoW--and since it works indoors, I recommend it highly. It won't get you away from some Kyvs or those superfast pet swarms that mobs in GoD cast, but it sure helps. (While I'm at it, I do have to rant about 1 thing--how come the fricking mobs can cast 2 pets that last for 30 seconds and hit for 300s and have tons of hitpoints, and shaman get pets that have 250 hps?!? What's up with that?!? They're blue con mobs and cast better pets than us!!!!)

FoMM and Crit DoTs are both pretty decent. They do add a good bit of dps. I haven't run parses, but I'm going to go out on a limb and guess that crit DoTs are firing at around 20% of the time on level 3. Agro can be a concern with a string of crits (especially if you're nuking and get another crit), but overall both viable skills.

So . . . now what? I'm kind of at a loss as to what to get. I finished all my other AAs, so I only have GoD left. I think I've got like 10 AA saved, and they're not exactly burning a hole in my pocket.

Packrat and enduring breath . . . well, maybe when the other stuff is done.

Second Forte is supposed to max out at 100 skill (don't know if this was fixed or if people can still get it past 100 or not). For 15 AAs, I just have a hard time justifying what's probably about a 2.5% savings in mana, if I remember the parses previously posted somewhere here in the forum.

Casting persistance sounds highly craptacular. Also from parses on this board, it is effectively a chance to channel through a stun. Thus, (a) you must already be casting, (b) must get stunned, and (c) pass a skill check against this AA for it to work. That's a pretty low percentage of usefullness. It does NOT add anything to channelling is what I'm told.

Pet Affinity? Been discussed ad nauseum. Frankly, I don't use my pet much in Time or GoD. 20 AA seems a lot to pay for a pet I don't use all that often. If it offered innate dual wield . . . maybe! Let's just say this AA is not too exciting for me. Our pets barely make a speedbump for GoD mobs.

Pet swarm--I'm a bit reluctant to get this because (a) it's still broken, and (b) I don't use it for DPS, I use it for push. Level 3 is nice with all the doggies. Drop it on a cleric mob if you're soloing and let them interrupt his low health heals and gates. Use it in raids to wall a mob (Strafe to get an even distribution). Dunno, maybe when they get pet hp fixed . . .

So what's left? Casting subtlety and the healing ward.

How good are these two? I'm concerned about getting subtlety because sometimes I need to tank. If our group MT goes down, I WANT agro. If a mob is chasing our clerics, I want to be able to pull agro and park it. Does CS3 affect our ability to hold agro? I've heard mixed reviews as to how it works with DoTs or just slows. Would I need to keep an agro spell up if I get CS3?

The healing ward is, of course, situational. How much are healing levels, refresh, etc. Do minor AEs still eat the ward? How many AAs does each level cost?

I dunno, guys. What would you guys recommend? Any particular skills you really like?
