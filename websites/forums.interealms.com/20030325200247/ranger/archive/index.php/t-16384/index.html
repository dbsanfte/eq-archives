Western Wastes for Making Money....

Get KEI and head to the wastes - get your regen up and lev. Head to DN gorge and pull animals into gorge. This makes it easier to kite without being jumped on by a KoS non CoV mob.

1. Kill Velium hounds @ level 60 - not bad AA exp (not as good as PoP - however you are getting exp AND loot!). They drop velium hound fur that sells from 400-500pp depending on market demand and supply. I sold a stack the other day for 400pp each. They are not common but drop at a decent rate.

2. Kill Glacier Mastodons @ level 60 AA exp is fair to good ( not as good as velium hounds). They drop velium mastondon fur. Need 4 of these to make a cloak (trivial >250 tailoring) used in the wood-elf illusion mask quest. These sell for around 750-1000pp again depending on market demand and supply.

When I want to get away from it all and dont feel social, need money or just have only an hour or so to play I head to WW.
