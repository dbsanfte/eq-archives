oak bark (foraged) + water flask (0.010) = oak tannin
oak tannin + sm. brick of mithril (~6.5pp in felwithe shop of holos) + silk string (~0.4?) = lt. mithril string
sylvan oak (foraged) + knife (smithed, returned on failure/success) + 2 standard bow cams (~75pp for both) + string = bow

Total cost:
2 forages & ~81.91pp
trivial is below 175, I hear
bow is 20/60/200 5str/wis (tunare)
IMO, stick with your trueshot LB, though you probably won't have any problem making this bow, as the trueshot is better.
