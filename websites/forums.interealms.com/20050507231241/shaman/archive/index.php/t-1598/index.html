I was the same way. I quit at like level 17 and thought it was too difficult to level. Then the second time around - I guess I knew what I was doing - I blew through the 20s like it was nothing.

Anyway, to address your question:

You didn't mention race but if you can wear it, JB armor is nice to fill in on some slots (e.g. arms, feet, etc.).

If you want a really good weapon I'd recommend the Rune Etched Icewurm Fang. It goes for probably around 5k or so now and is a really great weapon especially for a lower level shaman (IMO). But you're best off using a piercer because the epic, if you ever get it, is piercing.

Get a good shield ... CC, SBS, whichever. Some of the Arctic Wyvern armor is nice also.

For jewelry it depends on what kind of shaman you want to be (WIS v. AC/HP) but the Velium Fire Wedding Rings (6ac, 65hp) are great.
