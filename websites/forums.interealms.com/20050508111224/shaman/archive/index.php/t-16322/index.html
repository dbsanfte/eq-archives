You get 4 mana per wis at 255+, as far as I can tell from magelo.

For stamina, the formula is:

Level/20 hp per sta, then modified by your ND if you have any. So at 65, you get 3.575 HP per point with ND3. Past 255, this number is halved to 1.78.

I trained IE5 and PP5 somewhere in my mid 100's. It isn't a necessity, but it is worth it for the extra mana, imho.
