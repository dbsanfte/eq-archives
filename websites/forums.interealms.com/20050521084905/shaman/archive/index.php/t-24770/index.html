drib
There are bugs with Spiritual Channeling now. However, the general idea of this AA is a little vague given how it was implemented. You use health as mana while under its effect. However, the current functionality doesn't allow you to go below 50% health (or what would be 50% health at full mana cost of the spell you're casting) and it defaults back to using mana.

Here's my main assumptions about this AA:

1. Using this AA implies you want to use health instead of mana, because you either are low on mana or want to conserve your mana.

2. Health can be restored much faster than mana, making a HoT or instant heal equivalent to massive mana regeneration.

Activating this AA should make your health bar function exactly as your mana bar, but with one exception: it should not allow you to kill yourself casting a spell or allow your health to fall into low-health aggro range. You should get "insufficient mana" messages if the raw mana cost multiplied by 1.8 is greater than your current health. As it was stated in another post, Cannibalization is a fixed amount of health loss and only careless usage of it will end up killing the user. The health use under Spiritual Channeling is random unless the user has no AA or focus effects that affect mana consumption. If the developers wish to have it default back to mana if you have insufficient health to cast a spell, then that would work as long as they lowered the current threshhold to just above low-health aggro range.
