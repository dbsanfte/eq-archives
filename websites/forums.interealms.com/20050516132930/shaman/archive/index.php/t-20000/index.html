Just off the top of my head pulling out, I'd say something like the following is not an unreasonable aim...I wouldn't expect all shaman to have this at the moment if you are raiding HoT still.

Equipment: 2500+ unbuffed hp, 100 in all resists wearing normal gear (not BD stuff)

Level: 65, it makes such a difference.

Essential AA: Canni V & MGB

Nice AA: SCRM, RC

More Nice AA: ND3, LR5, SCM3, QB2

Essential spells: 2xFo7, 2xTalisman of boar, Tnarg's mending for all, later on at least 2 should have RGC too

Useful spells: Quissence, FA

No hard and fast rules for what's "needed", but I think these are all good targets to aim for. Plenty posts on AA progression but I've listed those I think most important for all shaman on raids.
