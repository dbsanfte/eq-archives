Zaviax McCleo
Yes, you need the GoD expansion to be able to have and use them.

In each starting town there is a tribute master. Handing in Items to these tribute masters, or coin will result in Tribute points. Each item has it's own set value. You have to go to your own starting city to access the tribute master.

With those points you can purchase certain abilites. Like Flowing Thought or Focus effects like Spell Haste, Extended Range, Burning Affliction etc.... There's also HP and Mana boosts and many others.

You can select what abilities you want, up to 5 maxmimum. Once you leave the tribute master you need to hit Alt-U to bring up your tribute window. Once you activate them you use up points and a timer begins with 10 mins counting down. Every 10 mins your points get spent again.

To give you an idea, I have in my Tribute Window, Burning Affliction, Extended Range and Spell Haste 4, along with +150 HP. That eats up 100 pts for me. So when I activate my tributes I eat up 100 points right away then every 10 mins another 100pts get eaten up. I can deactivate it at anytime though. (new with the last patch, making it much more worthwhile.)
