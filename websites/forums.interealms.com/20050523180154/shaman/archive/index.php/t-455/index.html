Jaradis
ToV = Temple of Veeshan, located in Western Wastes

NToV = North (wing of) Temple of Veeshan. Same zone as ToV but ToV is split in 3 wings. West Wing, East Wing, and North Wing.

West Wing is where armor drops for the Kael armor quests. Mobs are on Claws of Veeshan faction and 3 named dragons give quests to the dragon allied guilds.

East Wing is known as Halls of Testing or HoT. Mobs here are KoS to everyone and they drop the armor for the Skyshrine quest armors as well as parts for the West wing quests given by the 3 dragons.

North Wing is the very difficult high end area. Mobs are KoS to all and much harder to kill compared to East and West wings. Very high end armor (some of the best in game) drop from the numerous named dragons there.

Jaradis
No key for Temple of Veeshan. Just a level 46 limit like the planes. Oh and one thing, if you are KoS to Claws of Veeshan, there is no way out of Temple of Veeshan other than gating or porting out. The zone out is on the far side of the zone from the zone in. If you aren't KoS to dragons you can walk to the west wing and just zone back out though.

Veeshan's Peak (the zone in Skyfire) is the one needing a long key quest.

Seriously, it's a tough zone. NToV has mobs that are kill on sight (KoS) even if you are ally to the Claws of Veeshan (CoV) faction. The EASIEST mobs in ToV are level 60 and hit in the 300s with the exception of some hatchlings in NToV.
