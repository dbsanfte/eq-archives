Fudapuda
I don't have the loc handy, but if you run straight out from where you zone in to TT from EJ, you will run in to the lake. When you hit the lake shore, turn right, and follow the shore. When you reach the right side of the lake, down the hill to your right are a set of ruins with anywhere from 8-10 mobs inside and around the edges, all either ghostly looking or skeletal. That's the spot.

There are at least 3 named that appear in there, as well as a host of others. Any of them can drop the ring--it is not restricted to just the named.

Important notes: though these guys appear to be undead, they are NOT--cleric and paladin vs undead spells and abilities do NOT work on them. And they attempt to flee when they get low in HP, which undead ordinarily don't do. They aggro from a long range, and you will often get multiples, but once the camp is broken, it is easy to manage it with a good group.

A couple of other nice items drop, btw, at least nice for twinks, though not for shaman. Shield of the Risen Cultist, some sort of Sword of the Risen Cultist, Staff of the Risen Cultist (you get the idea).

Hope that all helps.
