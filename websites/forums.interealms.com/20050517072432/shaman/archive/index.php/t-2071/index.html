I'm in CotG. We don't recruit per se. We guild folks we know, or people get to know us and we decide if they fit or not. We have 100+ active members, we have very few rules, no obligitory raiding, most of our membership is North American with a few Europeans, we do 2-5 raids a week, are dragonkin (90% of us), and we do not recruit through zone wide shouts. People are sponsored, if a new guildmember is disciplined so is their sponsor. We do not mandate that guild members group with each other either and most of our raids are open to all. We have RL get togethers (people come in from all over), a good number of ex/current militart types (mostly navy), average age is early 30's.

I've been in the guild since july 99 and I call it home. I'm not big on raiding, but a few of my guildmates are. I enjoy the lack of dudespeak and although the average level of the primary characters is 55+, we are not uber, most don't want to be. Basically I like the comraderie, only thing that irks me is the PG-13 guildchat restriction, although it really doesn't impact the language I want to use, it does cut back on the bluntness of some of my bad jokes.

I'm not trying to recruit you by any stretch, just inform you and give you a personal perspective. I recommend looking over our website and if you're interested look us up online and get to know us. Its a long process to get in, up to you if you think its worth it.
