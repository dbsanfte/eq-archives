Shaman "Trade-In Reward" Spells
In both Firiona Vie (Barbarian hut w/ Alchemy vendors) and The Overthere, there is a person you can turn in these 4 spells to:

Talisman of Jasinth (level 51)
Spirit of Scale (level 52)
Cripple (level 53)
Cannabalize III (level 54)

When you turn them in (one at a time, all together, or any mixture in between, including multiple copies of the same spell) you will get one of the following four spells randomly. There is no surefire way to predict which you will receive.
Insidious Decay (level 52)
Talisman of Shadoo (level 53)
Shroud of the Spirits (level 54)
Torrent of Poison (level 55)
