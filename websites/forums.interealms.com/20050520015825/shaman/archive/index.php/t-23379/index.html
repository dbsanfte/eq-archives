Is it possible to make a hotkey for insta-cast SoW off of the Black Fur Boots. If this is possble, can someone please explain how to do this?
Suddail
If they are equipped leftclick on foot slot hold it for a few seconds and the icon will pop on your cursor. Put this in your hotkey bank.

If it is an inventory slot do the same just by clicking inventory slot.
