Hail,

Yes, the 8th shawl is worth it.
70% buff extension (with 50% coming from SCR3 & SCRM) means:
SLTW lasts 27.2 minutes.
Blessing of Replenishment lasts 34 minutes and change.
Focus of Sould lasts 2 hours and 14 minutes!
Endurance of Boar lasts over 2 hours!
Torpor extended by 70%!!!

That's worth it to me irrespective of the inherent stats (which excepting AC, are decent) on the shawl.

You know what you are in store for. HOURS & hours of mindless clicking.
But you can do baking, brewing, pottery, and jewelcraft in a single night for each. Smithing & Tailoring are the only ones that are difficult, and even those can be powerskilled w/ funds. There are ways to MQ certain aspects of the quest as well, though I don't advise that personally.

There are many alternative shoulder slot items that have better stats, but few with the outstanding benefits of 20% buff extension on all your spells *plus* the bonus of FT3.

Good luck whatever you choose,
