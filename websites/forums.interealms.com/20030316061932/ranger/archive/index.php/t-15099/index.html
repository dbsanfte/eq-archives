Bloodgill Goblins in LoIO give good exp up to 53 or 54. They're in the sunken building in the middle of the lake. There are two platforms on the North and South side of the main building that have two static spawns each. Regular harmony works on them and there are no wanderers. Just bring lots of fish scales. Fighting underwater is always exciting.
