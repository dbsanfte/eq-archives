The only real time I let dogdog tank--provided it's not an OH %#$%!!! moment--is on incoming, when he tanks the unslowed mob as I slow it. It works fairly well.

He also pulls the job of off tanking adds til I get them slowed and root parked.

So, if you want a tank, look more into doing the tanking yourself. You'll be better at it, and it's much more fun. ;)
