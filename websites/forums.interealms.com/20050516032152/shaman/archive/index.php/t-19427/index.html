Jalelin
Ignoring natural mana regen, hit point regen and spells that increase either, cannibalizing hit points to mana and then casting a heal spell on yourself will leave you with more mana than you started with (at least, it's the case if the healing spell is Torpor, Quiessance or the mini-CH).

When meditating, you get your mana increase with each six second "tick". The server only checks to see if you are sitting at the point of the tick. It does not check to make sure that you have been sitting for the entire six seconds. This means that as long as you are sitting at the instant of the tick, you can have been standing and doing something else for the majority of the time.
Weyalan
1. you regain mana faster sitting than standing. Cant remember the exact ficures, but it is like 1 mana per 6 seconds standing and 10 mana per 6 seconds sitting

2. The mana regained while sitting is not continuous. You gain zero mana for 5.9999 seconds and then get the whole 10 mana in the 0.0001 seconds of the "tick"

3. You can tell when the "tick" occurs by watching your hp in your inventory, because like mana regain, hp regain also happens at the tick.

4. As a result of the fact that mana (and hp) being given back at 6 second intervals, rather than continuously, in order to get the full benefits from sitting down, you only actually have to be sitting down when the tick takes place. The other 5.999 seconds you can do whatever you want.

5. Provided you can time things right, you can move, run, cast, eat, drink, play the piano, and provided you sit down for a brief period over every tick, you can regain hp and mana as if you were sitting the whole time. In particular, because canni doesn't take 6 seconds to cast, you can stand up, cast cannibalise and sit doen again in time for the next pulse of mana and hp - thus getting the sitting benefits, while cannibalising. Repeat this process every 6 seconds and you are "cannidancing"

6. You can automate the cannidance with a hotkey. A typical cannidance hotkey looks like this:
/sit off
/cast 7 (or whatever spell slot you use for canni)
/pause 18 (this is the length of pause while spell is casting - typically 15 - 25, depending on your computer and connection, lower is better, but whatever works)
/sit on

So, by watching you hp on your inventory bar, you just have to sit until you see your hp go up (you have regen on all the time, right?) and then hit your Cannidance hotkey, and you will automatically stand, canni and sit down in time for the next tick
Valsuvious
yeah, cannidance in it's state has always seemed to be a much better way of increasing ones mana quicker and faster than just sitting would, or by just chain casting canni while standing. Explaining it different, it's called cannidancing cause your character looks like he's dancing while doing it. Take the role of someone else looking at you while doing it. You see the person stand up, cast a spell, then sit back down, repeating this stuff every six secs. Now, as the person doing it, what you are doing is what is explained above. You watch for the second that you gain mana/hps (it's referred to as the tick, every six seconds you gain so much mana/hp while either standing or sitting, sitting down you regen more of both, so that's why you are sitting when this tick occurs). It's characterized by seeing your hps/mana go up at that tick. At that time you immediately stand up, cast canniXX then immediately sit back down until the next tick comes. If you time this right, you regain the mana from the canni and from sitting at the tick, thus increasing your mana back at an advanced rapid pace.

Now, like you said, when you canni you lose hps. The heal over time spells are great to get those hps back, cause they do not cost barely any mana to cast, and you get alot of hps back from it when it's duration is finished.

This step will take a little bit to get used to, but it's very effective in getting your mana back quickly after a fight. You can also check these threads out if any of us have confused you at all.

http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=11981
http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=16828

When you get used to doing the sit down stand up timing technique, you can do others things with it. Sometimes after fights, if I had to run somewhere, I would run for 5 secs, sit down for the tick, and then get up and run again, repeating that process.

Oh, and note that if you get a mount, you get mana back as if you were sitting down the whole time even while casting a spell (oh, but not if you are meleeing), so if you are up on a mount you can just chain cast canni and it would be as effective as if you were cannidancing.

As described above, Canni-Dancing becomes second nature to you after a while and you'll catch yourself doing it at FM. My method varies a bit in that I like to keep my hotkeys free for other things. I have mapped Sit/Stand to the END key on my keyboard and use the bard style casting arrangement (all spell gems on the numpad 1 = spell 1, 2 = spell 2, etc.) Also, I mapped Hotkey #2 (Heal) to numpad 0. This seems most efficient to me as I don't enjoy reaching for two keys to do one thing. So, Canni-dancing for me is a couple easy keypresses: END, 2, END.

The best thing you can do for yourself would be to decide how you like your keys set up and how you'll be able to hit them fast. Move all the complex keypresses to something you find easily accessible (open spell book = M, Target mob = ], Attack = \, etc.) With this setup, I can control every one of my alts and not scramble around the keyboard looking for buttons.
