A Couple notes:

As far as cultural armor goes, I believe with the recent patch, pretty much all races can make some sort of non-diety specific cultural, tho diety specific usually has superior stats.

Humans can make all Human Cultural armor regardless of which city they started in, Freeport or Qeynos.

I personally might consider Human Enchanter or Human Druid if I wanted a Tradeskill only type.

Enchanter could Enchant all the various ores and make the purified/crystallized/etc vials of mana.

And Human Cultural is wearable by a larger outlay of races than most, and I believe at least Rallos Zek Heraldic is widely hailed as some of the best cultural and wearable by all races that can have Rallos Zek Worshipping Warriors.

A Druid could be useful for sowing, porting, and tracking to most efficiently gather large quantities of the drop items needed, although you could also hire newbies to collect stacks of em in many cases. More and More Drops for trade skills are off pretty tough things anyway (i.e. Thought Leech Hide Armor)
