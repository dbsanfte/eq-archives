Guess my point is, from everything that I've done parsing wise, to me it seems that the best combo of spells combined with my focus' to get the best ideal dps in a raid, is use Sisslak and bane (all of my parsings have had bane come out ahead dps wise, but then again, i do not have a poison dot focus that I use regularly), use both rains (yoppa and tears of saryrn), and then hit Yoppa Spear because the first rain won't be refreshed when I can cast again. This will deplete your mana alot and quickly of course. During most fights, if I know that I really won't have to cure/heal anyone and I can do all out on dps, I demem ancient canni and put something else in that slot for dps. Usually I do what I described above and put 2nd rain in canni slot, and only use a rod and canni5 to get some mana back. Normally I end the fights pretty low on mana of course.

During groups, ideally for me, I like to use one rain, one poison nuke and sisslak. I will hit ancient canni whenever I can, but this set up usually gives me pretty nice dps numbers, especially since I have maxed nuke crits and most of my dot crits done.
