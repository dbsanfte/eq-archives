***SPOILER***

In PoK track Holly Longtail, she is downstairs of the large building you track to. There is a caster in the very middle of the floor that will provide you with a collectors box. In the box gather:
1) ****atrice beak - can be purchased off of vendor in PoK or Bazaar.
2) HQ cougar skin - we all know where these come from, and on Rodcet Nife they sell for 15-50pp in bazaar.
3) Undead froglok tounge - These can also be purchased in Po
K
4) a tiny rockhopper eye - goto Shar Vahl from the outdoor newbie area. When you get to the first bridge drop off the left side to the lvl1-4 area and track needle claw hoppers, and whatever is tagged "hopper" drop at a pretty fast rate.

Combine all 4 items and it locks the box. Go back and track Holly Longtail, a Vahl Shir Beastlord with pet and turn in box.

You receive an antique ring: not 100% on stats as I didnt write them down but close to:

AC2 Mana+25 HPS+35 Mana preservation II - NO DROP
Not a real ubber ring, but it takes about 20 minutes tops to do and not bad for fear kiting.

If anyone attempts the quest for the melee warrior guy in the same room, please post the reward you get as I assume it will be a sword of sorts. TIA
