Balkamp
From DS, head towards WoS. When you get to the "T" intersection where the ikkav is, hang a right. Theres a little valley there, very narrow. Continue down valley till it opens up to a gorge, go up the left wall to the path/road. You should park halfway down the incline on this road. Uphill, behind you there is a bazu spawn that will wander towards you. Remains static for a minute before it starts to path. Downhill, in front of you, are 3 single pullable ferans along the road.

Nice spot.
snapdragn
Just a bit north of Balkamp's camp are a few (6 - 8) murkglider stingers. At 64 the are some of the highest trash mobs in the zone, so exp is a bit better than the other murkglider camps (near the zonelines).

well, there is a very nice camping spot, and seems a little crazy, but its nice, and no one is ever there.....the valley where the murkglider schoolleader spawns...take the path to WoS, and its a off-shoot from the trench,

obviously this is only viable, however, if the schoolleader is not up (which he usually isn't) just stay up on the walls if he happens to spawn, and skirt out of the valley, MSL and his nurses won't aggro....there is one murk that wanders in and out of the area, but his path is fixed to a point where he won't add if you know where he moves...

all of the murks are single pullable from the mouth of the valley, and its very easy to clear them and pull from the ledge where the dragorn mobs are, or if you prefer to clear the valley, there is a bazu, and a small (named?) feran up on the ledge, he's called "tamed bazu herder" but he never seems to drop anything for me. its pretty good solo EXP, and the EXP is still pretty nice if another shammy or necro want to do a root-rot camp...
