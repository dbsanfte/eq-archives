Viscerator
If your level 60, and want to make decent experience while collecting heads, then yes head to the dwarf mines is the place to be.
The expediton guards are all blue to 60, and can drop heads at same rate as the guards around the river. You just can't slay them as quickly.

The mines are located in Great Divide at around location (-6000, -3500). It's south of the Giant Fort near the wizard spires by EW zone.
There's a cave you can go down in, and miners and guards to kill.
If you pull from the correct spot you can get the guards singly at level 60. There is 6 guards you can kill plus a named one I usually avoid. Respawn here is very quick, and they are static spawns.

For factions, you need:
+ZEK Ally
+Tormax Dubious (tormax faction in our quest giver room)
+Rif doesn't matter, you don't run into these guys near the quest rooms, and can invis into the bank area.
