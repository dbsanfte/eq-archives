Hail,

You can camp the stampeding piglet in Tactics.
Drops range from a War Marshall's Bladed Staff (sells 7k easily), a Tactician's Bulwark (9-12k), Blood earring of rage (20-25k), or Adjutant's Saber (2-3k). Spectral parchments are also a common drop, which MQ for about 3k on E. Marr right now. Only downside is all but the specs are Lore, so you either need a friend to come pickup stuff or make a quick bank run to toss stuff in the shared bank slots after a kill. I've seen 3 WMBS drop in a row before. Stampedes are a random event, but you will generally see one every hour or so. You can easily solo the 2 static spawns in the room outside the stables using root/rot techniques. VP3 makes this trivial to do. You can pull pit or back hall mobs as time/mana allows, just keep a reserve of mana ready in case of a stampede so you don't miss any opportunities.

You can park yourself pretty safely in a corner (wanderers won't aggro) and AFK as necessary, but you do have to pay attention, as there isn't alot of time if you miss a stampede message. Competition for the spot is somewhat stiff, as it seems a favorite spot for druids or necros who afk there w/ some macro or something that let's them know when the stampede call comes.

I personally solo & group there still for exp, and the drops from piglet are a nice bonus. I've made alot of cash at this camp both soloed and grouped, and it is very convenient if you need to afk a few minutes.

Take care,

I'd say droga. The safest camps would be, as someone else mentioned, Runri, and Earth Seer Gamolk as well. Both have spots nearby where you're entirely safe & could afk indefinitely (if not for other people and the silly/stupid things they do). Flame Master Jepal and King Dronan are slightly riskier camps but with better rewards, Jepal's boots go for up to 15k on my server and the CHoS up to 18ish, with a great demand for the hammer to boot. All mobs are singleable, no need to kill their friends as well. Respawn is 20 minutes. For the King you need to stand directly behind him or his PH, up against the wall in the corner. Can't move from this spot, and I've heard shrink helps. Also can't melee at all, as this will push him just far enough out to aggro his friend on the other side of the oven. For Jepal, just stand on the table slightly east of him, slow from there & fight him from there - neither will run, both have mobs nearby enough, as does Gamolk, but you'll need root and/or snare for Runri. Pet is an absolute no-no for King, and I wouldn't recommend it for Jepal either.

Gulbane -One other option for you to consider would be The Crypt of Nadox.

Zone in from the caverns so you are at the 3 elemental spawn . Shrink 2x invis, jump in the lava and swim untill you see the bridge. Climb the right bank of the lava river about 15 feet prior to the bridge. Cross bridge going to the left side of lava river.

After crossing the bridge you will see 3 tunnels to choose from. Take the center one, it'll open up into a large cavern/ room of Luggalds - this room has 4 side rooms ( one in each corner) and a tunnel on each side.

The tunnel you want is the continuation of the one you originally used to get there. Once you have exited the largish cavern of luggalds you are in a rather short hallway, this hallway is the safe spot for your upcomming loot farm. Has no wanderer's, no spawns and can go indefinetly afk.

The room just after this hall is your target area, has 1 wandering mob who will path up to the tunnel entrance, but no further also has 7 static mobs, any one of whom can pop as a named ( read loot droppping) mob " An Undead Weoponsmith" his loot table is a weak charm * Rusted Sword hilt * or * Cracked Darkwood Bow of Suffering * and rarely a * Sand-Eroded Shell *. The shell is the big money item here- easily 8-10k on my server ( Tarew Marr ).

In addition there is a chance for another named to spawn in this camp " 1st Mate Bresanev". He spawns off the death of a previous mob- i.e.- when the corpse hits the ground the 1st Mate shows up and says *hi*. Bresanev's loot table is * waterlogged britches* and rarely * Gem-Inlaid Longsword*. Britches fetch anywhere from 1.5k to 3k, sword 10k-15k. All mobs in this room drop 7pp-13pp + 2/3 zombie skins, some gems as well ( ruby / fire emerald / sapphire / diamond / blue diamond ) sometimes you'll get a decayed coin-purse, pouch of bone dust or a sand eroded trinket, vendors pay 9pp, 95pp and 23pp respectively for these. If you have the room to stockpile and save them the zombie skins can be sold to tradeskillers in vendor mode for 2-5pp per skin, and they DO add up. Room is simple, cash is decent and can make about 1 orange aa bubble per hour no muss, no fuss and no stress.
