Wizards are truly gods in the endgame. Druids are...not.

However, druid snare is MUCH better, and as a TEAM, I think shaman/druid is better. (Actually I think you should make a monk heheh but that's another story.)

On PL'ing in general, reds are usually less efficient than just killing more blues and sometimes yellows. The main reason is reds have too many hitpoints(which makes killing them slower and getting any notable effect from damage shields less likely), and both melee and spells land on them far less often than when you're just fighting blues. This is what I've found when using my own shaman 60 and druid 54 to PL people.

Fighting blues will let you put off slowing and healing as long as possible, too, letting the druid's damage shield get more aggro on him, too. That means more mobs dying simultaneously, increasing experience rate. You will have to heal fast against yellows and reds, and then the mobs will be wasting their time aggroing you. You'll get interrupts then when trying to heal, etc, and it is just not as efficient.

Don't forget to target yourself when doing group buffs! ;)
