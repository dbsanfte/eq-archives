Jaruden
The whole pop expansion is divided into what is called "tiers". Mostly it's just a progression, each tier is a little tougher than the previous. Character flags are a new feature instead of keys to get around in PoP. For example, you have to do the trials in PoJ to get a flag that says you can enter PoV or PoS. Think of it as looting a key to the zones that you don't have to bother using. There are dozens of different flags to get, some are simple little things, but most are for progressing further into the more difficult planes.
