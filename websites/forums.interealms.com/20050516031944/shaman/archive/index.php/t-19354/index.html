As far as aggro goes, I've noticed in the groups I've been in that they fixed it so a Knight is "snap" aggro, while a warrior is "aggro over time." When working together, this is a very good combination.

The Knights can draw instant aggro with their aggro spells, but a warrior using the proper provoke disc can take it from the Knight after aggro has been generated. That's the way it should be, imo. The Knight's there to draw the snap aggro to keep the mob off the casters, then the warrior takes aggro and does what he's supposed to do...tank the mob.

The only problem is that a Knight can recapture aggro with another round of aggro spells, while the warrior has to wait for his provoke disc to refresh, or get a good taunt or two off.
