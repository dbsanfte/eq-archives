Ralence
If you're using archery primarily, finishing off CF3 and getting FoA3 might be a good choice.

I'm also a big fan of the first level of GotF, just for the fact that it bugs wolf form, and I despise the illusion part.

ND and PE are also good skills, since you've already done CS1 and CA1, if you grab ND1, and then PE, you'll get a cheap bonus to HP and defensive skills.

I took a lot of the melee skills myself, as quite often mobs are shoved in the wall. It's not as big of a deal now with PoP, but depending on what you're killing, you may want to consider ambi and/or ferocity as well.

Now that you've got the archery stuff done, you can start to have more fun with them, pick the ones you think will help you personally, and go with it. We all play differently, and there's a /lot/ of good skills for rangers.

Zolina
For increasing damage per second:
Combat Fury / Fury of the Ages are always good
Ferocity if you melee a lot
Ambidexterity if you dual-wield with a good offhand weapon
Punishing Blade if you use a 2H weapon a lot

Useful abilities:
Entrap - manaless snare
Innate Camouflage - almost-instant cast invisibility
Guardian of the Forest 1 - brief ATK boost and lets you use wolf form in zones where it's KOS
