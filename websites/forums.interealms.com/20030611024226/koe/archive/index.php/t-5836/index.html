Well folks, I'm still in shock here. But I will try to put that aside and let my feelings show :)

I logged into the Bazaar and ran into Grizz, Cshae, Zelf, Aliera and one more (Was it Than? Sorry I missed whoever our 6th was) and Cshae wanted to go to East Karana to see the Warrior Epic NPC. Supposedly she wanted to see where it was so we headed out and got there.

Then she said if she were to summon a Green Scale would I be ready to complete my Epic. I said of course, starting to think this was some kinda joke, but then she /emote Summons a green scale and dropped it on me.

My jaw about hit the floor and I started shaking when I saw "Green Dragon Scales" appear in my trade window with Cshae. After lots of "Waran says, !!!!!!!!!!!" I completed my last few turnins before heading out to East Freeport to pickup my Red Scabbard.

In front of all my family and friends in KOE, I started to have a speech but were summoned away to kill Lodi out in Iceclad. Voting for "Let's go!" in our polls, we jumped at the chance and took the big turtle down. Grats to all the recipients of great loot. Plus we didn't need any coffins ;)

Anyway, after the kill, I combined my 2 hilts and 2 blades and created the Jagged Blade of War, which separates into the Blade of Tactics and Blade of Strategy for 1HS.

I'd like to thank ALL KOE and friends who completed this AMAZING accomplishment. This isn't an accomplishment for just little ole me, it's just as big for KOE!!!
