snapdragn
Once you break a certain threshold (2 bubbles of health is a good estimate), your ATK and AC drop off dramatically because your AGI is dropping.

So the more you get hit, the easier it is for you to get hit. As Mordaen pointed out, it's known as the 'death march' or 'death spiral'. Its a slippery slope.

Once you get into that 'zone' it's hard to get out. You can't kill the mob that's on you, your HPs drop quickly, and you move so slowly you can't run away.

The best thing you can do is get your /loc. Makes it easy to get your corpse. :)



EDIT: There is a chart *somewhere* that shows the approx relationship between AGI and AC...basically, there is a HUGE jump at 75. Most of the other time it's a trivial increase. So keep your AGI up high enough so that even if you start losing points because of the 'death spiral' it will remain above 75.
