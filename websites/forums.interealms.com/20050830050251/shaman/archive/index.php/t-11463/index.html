Here's all you really need to know:

In a brew barrel combine A Blackened Vial (drop in Nadex) + the troll skull blood (drop in Nadex off the Blackhands) + Gunthak Gulf Water (Foraged and dropped in Gunthak). You get A Black Potion.

Then combine A Black Potion + Pure Blood (dropped off a Luggold Initiate in Nadox) to recieve A Glowing Black Potion.

You may be able to hand in the glowing black potion all by itself for the spell, but I handed in the "A Torn Page" (no-drop, all/all, Nadox drop) along with it to the npc and I got my Pure Blood scroll!
