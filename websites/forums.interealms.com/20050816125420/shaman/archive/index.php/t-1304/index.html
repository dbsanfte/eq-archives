Four days, eh? :)

Of course, check the hunting grounds (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/forumdisplay.php?s=&forumid=19) forums, but I can let you in on a spot that has been great for me.

In the Northeast corner of FV there is a giant camp... three giants, on a nice short spawn. There are tons of wanderers a few levels lower than the giants- just about all warrior types. I started there around 40 on the wanderers, and started in on the giants when I hit 43 or so. At 45 (when the bracer starts working) you'll easily be able to hold the entire camp.

There is a zoneline 30 seconds from the camp, there is a bank/city in the zone, and the giants drop very nice loot (forest loops, lumberjack caps, straw belts, minor gems, tunics that sell to vendors for 60pp, etc.) Wanderers (frogs and dogs) drop regular stuff, couple plat and the occasional bronze/fine steel weapon.

Experience is outstanding here- I'd go hunt in Guk, Karnors, Maidens, whatever... but I always kept coming back. Went from 42 to 49 in under four days played (and much of that was not grinding- I went in a couple guild events, did some trading, made a trek to Halas to buy spells and give stuff to newbies...) - most of it here. Also made a few thousand plat along the way- nice! Heck, even though the giants are mostly light blue now (at 49), I'm tempted to stay a little longer... I'll bet I'd still get a yellow bar every hour and a half or so.

Hope this helps.
