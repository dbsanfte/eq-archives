Fury of Magic
(FoM) Level 1: 3
Level 2: 6
Level 3: 9 3 Increases the chance of landing a critical hit with a direct damage spell. The amount it increases your chance to land a critical nuke is unknown. This spell works on regular nukes, such as Velium Strike and Spear of Torment as well as our rains, Tears of Saryrn and Torrent of Poison. Critical nukes at this level will double the base damage of the hit and then add any increase from damage enhancing focus effects on top.

Requires: Level 3 of Spell Casting Fury (Archetype Ability). Level 61 for training rank 1, level 63 for rank 2 and level 65 for rank 3.

What Rombus over at Graffe's (http://www.graffe.com/) thinks about Fury of Magic Mastery...

"Once all critical abilities are trained, the Wizard has an 18-19% chance at delivering a blast worth double it's normal value (provided it is not partially or fully resisted). A very worthwhile AA."
Obviously less worthwhile for us since our nukes are lousy in comparison to a Wizard's, but I have FoMM & enjoy critting for over 2k with Velium Strike and 1350x2 fairly regularly withTears of Saryrn.
