[QUOTE]Does Charisma effect the chances of being resisted when casting the shaman slow line of spells?
[QUOTE]
No.

Charisma has very little effect on a shaman in battle. There are a very few specific encounters where charisma will come into play, and by that time you will be ready for them.

DO NOT WORRY about your base charisma stat.

How does charisma affect your life as a shaman?
With low charisma, you will pay more for vendor bought items, and receive less when you sell loot to vendors. Instead of 1sp for a flask of water, w/ a low charisma you'll pay 1sp3cp. Over time and with more expensive cash loots, this can add up. But your base stat means nothing. We get charisma buffs, and you can buy 2 opal encrusted steins for about 50pp (total) in the bazaar. Equip both and even your ugly mug will get better prices (that's +50 to charisma with the steins alone).

I have over the long time I've played Frekkels been equipped with various gear that has had my UNbuffed charisma in the 140's, and buffed (w/ steins equipped) at well over 200.

In my current gear, my unbuffed charisma is 52 as a barbarian! I have noticed NO difference in my combat effectiveness with a charisma in the 140's OR at 52. When I go to buy/sell, I unequip my -25 cha Brain of Cazic Thule and self buff Unfailing Revarence to get my charisma over 105, which is what you want to generally get best merchant prices.

Another effect of charisma:
We get Befriend Animal (a severely limited animal only charm spell) at level 29. This spell is at best a novelty for our class from levels 29-34, and a higher charisma will effect your ability to stick this spell on whatever light blue or green con animal you are charming. This particular use for charisma for us is so minimal that it should have NO EFFECT on your decisions for gear/stats.

In conclusion, do NOT worry about your low charisma except for its affect on your bank balance. Buy the steins and keep those for when you are dealing with NPC merchants. Buff your own charisma as well when doing that. But DON'T worry about buffing your charisma when you are fighting. Charisma is NOT a stat that you should be concerned about in an operational sense while playing.

Take care,

Just an additional clarification -- for every faction level (apprehensive, indifferent, amiable, etc.) there's a charisma at which you get "best price" from vendors and further CHA becomes useless.
