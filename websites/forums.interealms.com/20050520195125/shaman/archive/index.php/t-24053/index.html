Ruatonim
Unless this has been "fixed" at some point after I quit playing, there is always an option to change your helm (or any other armor graphic for that matter). All that is required is that you can change your form to a NPC form (skeleton, bear form, wolf form, goblin are the ones I can think of).

What you do is equip the item that you want to see, then change your form to one of the above listed. Then you take off the item and place what you actually want to wear on in its place. When you click off the illusion you will be wearing what you want, but you will have the armor graphic you desire!

This is easy to do as a shaman since you can easily do bear form!
