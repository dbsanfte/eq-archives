Ooooh. Good catch, Glor. Raketh, there are 3 sets of velious armor.

Thurg quest armor takes the armor that you get off giants in Kael. One decent group can kill giants in the arena in Kael (best place for chain drops I've seen--though it's still pretty uncommon)

Skyshrine quest armor takes the armor that drops off mobs in the halls of testing in ToV (east ToV, usually called HoT). HoT can be done with 1-2 groups if they are GOOD (read all 60s and very well equipped). Level 55-60 guilds should probably get at least 4 groups and be prepared for a wipe or 2.

Kael quest armor requires the armor that drops off mobs in north and west ToV. The armor is an uncommon drop off mobs in north, and the mbos in north are MUCH harder than the mobs in west. West is where you should go to farm armor for the kael quests. I would say that WToV might take 1 more group than the HoT force.

Hope that helps. Short answer, Kael and skyshrine quest armor will probably need raid forces for the most part. Thurg quest armor is much more easily obtainable.
