Goldark
My guild is reaching the stage now where HoT is becoming trivial, Vindi is killed with 18-24 people flawlessly, and we found on our first attempt that we can kill Dozekar with only one cleric. Now I know we're not uber and these aren't huge accomplishments, but to us they are a big step :)

Whilst we would like to move into NToV soon, we are still equipping people with SS gear and aren't quite ready to trash our CoV faction. One thing we are looking at though is moving into Sleepers Tomb, via keys from Vindi/Statue and Velketors. They seem to be very rare drops so far, unless we've just been unlucky, so we may move on to killing Zlandicar/Klandicar for keys too, and use Vindi to reset faction with CoV.

Anyways... I'm wondering how worthwhile it is to get access to ST nowadays. The main attraction for our melees is the prismatic weapons that proc avatar... but we now have 3 shaman in the guild, 2 with avatar, and myself looking to ding 65 which I'm sure I will do before we get enough people keyed to do ST.

Given that Ferine Avatar is a 40% upgrade to avatar, and has a 30 second (?? - Lucy says 60 seconds) recast time with no reagent cost, how worthwhile is Sleepers Tomb? I ask this in terms of:

1. Prismatic Weapons, bearing in mind the ratios are worse than many PoP items so they're really just for the proc.
2. Time taken to get keys
3. Other loot
4. Raid practice in a different zone.

Any thoughts from people who have been there? Or from guilds who skipped ST, did you think you missed the items you could have got there, or was your time better spent elsewhere?

Thanks in advance for any info, opinions or whatever :)
