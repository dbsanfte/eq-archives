Unsure about CoV faction items yet but from what I've been told by my friends, you need to be Ally to the quest giver and non-KoS to Yelinak. I'll find out soon enough. :)

For Kael quest armor, which I have direct experience with, you need the following faction:

Kromzek: Ally (since this is the Questgivers faction) If you're hunting in ToV (non-HoT), then you should get to Ally very fast. I was ally after 2 hunting trips into Western Wastes, actually.

Kromrif: no bearing -- only issue is getting past a see-invis mob on the way to the quest hall

Tormax: Only for shaman, you need non-KoS since the area the quest giver is in has Tormax faction mobs. Best way to be non-KoS is to turn in Icepaw paws and Coldain heads to Bevellos (sp?) -- or kill Dain. Another way to do this is to have someone train out the Tormax faction mobs as you're getting CoH'd right in front of the questgiver. A shaman in my guild did that. Was a sight to behold.

Since I'm KoS to rif, I've always been CoH'd into the quest hall. /shrug
