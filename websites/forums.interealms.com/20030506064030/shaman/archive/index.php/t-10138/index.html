Dread
I am level 60 and still use the JBB.

Even when grouping there are times it is still very good. Most of the time I prefer to use rains ( like most shaman ), but there are times when a rain isn't appropriate. Here is how I use the JBB

1. When the mob is about half dead and Bane is a waste
2. The MT is weak and can't hold agro that well. ToP will peel the mob off MTs of lesser quality
3. The mob is moving. Sometimes the MT is trying to get the mob into a better position, or maybe the mob is ping ponging, or maybe maybe the mob is running or feared.
4. The mob is very poison resistant.

Now, I am not saying that the JBB is better than using rains, because rains are good dps / mana, but our rains are very situational. Our DOTs are great too, but unless you get them in early, they are a waste. Having a DD that is mana free and doesn't require a spell slot is still extremely useful. If VI would get their head out of their a$$ and give us more spell slots, then the JBB wouldn't be as good.
