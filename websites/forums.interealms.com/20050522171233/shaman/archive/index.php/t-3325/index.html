Gloobonious
Sontalak is pretty much kill-as-fast-as-you-possibly-can.

It is also based on luck... sometimes he'll AE you to death early, other times he'll be nice and give you a huge expanse of time to wear him down. Either way, you need to go in full damage instantly. The fights I have participated in took probably around two minutes.

Also, because he dispells himself, try to keep Malo/OOS on him at all times. I noticed that it made a HUGE difference in how well Ice Strike would land.

Sontalak is really a test of your guild's DPS. If you are having trouble killing him, work on raising that -- with primal, more manaburn-equipped Wizards, etc...
