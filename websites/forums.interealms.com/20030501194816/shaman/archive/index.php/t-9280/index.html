Just a little note, from experience with my rogue, the Storm Giants (only found in Kael) give better faction with CoV then the normal Frost Giants (found in EW, GD, WL and Kael).

When I was grinding my faction with my rogue, it took forever for it to move when I was hunting Frost Giants in GD, but an hour or so in the Kael Arena it jumped from apprehensive to indifferent.

The faction increase is about 6-8 Frost Giants to everyone Storm Giant. Likewise doing the Giant Helm and Toe quests are great for faction too.

Naagruz
At 40 you will probably be limited to giants in GD/EW/IC and any Kromriff are going to give you more +coldain than +CoV which is fine because it will set you up to work on Thurgadin armor. You will want to be in the low to mid 50's to be effective in an arena group where you can pick up Thurg armor and good +CoV hits from the zek giants there. Getting in on any opportunistic hits on Vindi/AoW/Tormax will greatly reduce the time needed to hit ally with CoV.

Generally, you want to be L55 or higher before hitting ToV for armor. The more levels the better because you will resist more and get resisted less.
