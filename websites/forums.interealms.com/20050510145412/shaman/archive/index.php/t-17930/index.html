Tarthug
Shaman Dots are just AS good as necro dots..
No way, we are outclassed.

Look at Lucy and make a comparrison. Sure, in any standard EXP grp the differences are pretty mild as generally we only use Bane for 420 a tick while Necros have horror at 430 a tick. Their spell costs 20 less mana, does 10 more dam a tick, but most importantly, necros have a -30 resist adjustment on horror. While your bane gets resisted unless you malXXX for 300+ mana first, chances are theirs is racking up damage.

Soloing the difference becomes far more noticable. They have 6 60+ DoTs that can stack, one has a 75% snare on it. We have 3, no snare and our mana costs per point of damage (per tick) is 1.81 while theirs is 1.76 (ignoring snare dot). Looking at the total damage/vs mana over time the difference gets wider. So, they have more stackable damage, it's more mana efficient, but the real kicker here is they have resist adjustments on all their dots while to get that we need to cast an EXTRA spell that even then doesn't have the total adjustment when averaged out (Saryn's Kiss has a -200 check on it). Malos is a 55 adjustment that doesn't include DR while the BUILT IN average for necros is -86 (BoT, Horror, SK, DP, FP). Even more amazing is that their dots work 4 of the 5 resists (FP checks on fire), while we can work only DR, PR, and MR. Overall, there is no comparrison. We stack up pretty well though as a distant 2nd, ahead of druids (not by much).

On raids (a topic I'm less familiar on, please correct me if you see an error) with FD and their top 2 damage DoTs, it's safer to have a Necro doing damage than a shaman (although it's likely they'll be twitching, or using a pet to rack up absolutely insane damage, depending on the zone).

While I wouldn't want to play a necro, please don't mislabel us by saying we're as good at damage over time. We're not in the same class.
