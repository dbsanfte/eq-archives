Start off in High hold keep doing nobles and bards on the second floor. not only do you make xp, but you can kill the guards and make about 300pp an hour in fine steel and 50-55 pretty fast
55-60ish you can do Nathyns guards in Katta Castellum. they are up by the tenebrous mtns zone in. You can do other guards, lots of people do the bank, lover levels (55 ish) do the guards at the twilight sea entrance, but all of those guards drop faction with katta castellum. eventually you end up KoS with the whole zone, no fun. I'm max KoS to nathyns guards, and that is bad enough, because compared to the nobles in HHK, they have a HUGE (monstrously so) aggro range.

At 57-58ish it's really time to start grouping.. POD and PON are going to be nice spots. the xp is about as fast as katta solo.

50-52 COM. Start with the U turn, pull the Goo house too. Remember, max casting range is your friend. if its not camped, then summon pet to the U turn on the opposite side, and kill those. You'll lose your pet if you just try to run through w/out summoning.

53-54 HHK. Nobles/bards etc. Doing all the mobs(issa/bards/nobles/3rd floor noble) i racked in a blue every 12/15 minutes. At 54 it was about a blue every 25 or 30 minutes.

55-60 POP. Doesnt make sense to exp anywhere else really. Probably make 2 blues an hour or so, depending on wether your DPS is any good. A strong guild will help you out in finding those groups of course.
