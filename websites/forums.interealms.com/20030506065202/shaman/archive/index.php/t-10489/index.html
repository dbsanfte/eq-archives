Goldark
Spider fur belt, Elder Wolf Hide, Skull Marked Shield, Goranga Idol, quest the coldain military wristguard. Those are all fairly cheap options.

If you are looking bigger, JBB is certainly one option - I use mine at 61 still so I'm not sure I'd worry about it not being of use.

An alternative is ODS, about 10k on AB server, or a horse (9k or so). Both are great for helping keep your mana bar topped up, and would probably give the most bang for your buck. Which of the two you prefer probably depends on how much you hunt indoors vs outdoors.

Beyond that your gear is pretty good - certainly better than mine was at that level. You'll be looking for Velious quest armour next I expect, plus the gladiator chain in the arena is nice too.
