Macnair
I'd suggest Plane of Innovation, if the focus is on gaining experience. Zone in, move past the group that's there for exp at the zone in, and move either left or fight. Your ranger can tank these np, your shaman will have little trouble slowing them and healing the ranger. You'll figure out the mana management for your duo pretty quickly, and you will have about no down time. You can do this for 20 minutes or multiple hours. You will often have two mobs or occasionally three: just pull the add over to the side, root it, kill the first one, turn and kill the second one.

A slightly riskier suggestion would be the caves in the bottom of Plane of Justice. The mobs here seem to be a little more resistant, they are hidden around corners, and they are closer together: it can sometimes get a little messy. But you have the offchance of looting an alkaline etched stone or two, to sell in bazaar for decent cash.
