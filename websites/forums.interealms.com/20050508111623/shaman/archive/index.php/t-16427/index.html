Hail,

Some personal rules I have for LDoN that haven't been mentioned yet.

1) Assign a Masterlooter. Nobody else loots. Roll ONLY on no drop loot in the dungeon. Everyone can meet back at the adventurer merchants (or wherever) to roll on Lore tradable stuff and to sell/split the cash after you win or lose. (CASH on these adventures is decent, if you are FFA'ing loot someone is getting shafted.)

2.) Assign the Puller, MA, Primary Healer, MasterLooter, etc before you zone in. Though this sounds like common sense, I've seen 3 out of 6 people act as pullers before this was straightened out. Miscommunications like that can be deadly.

3.) If you have an Evaccer, USE it in a nasty overpull situation. Succoring to the zoneline only takes a minute, and you can be right back where you were before in another minute. It is like an automatic get out of jail free card if used right. The loss of pets is nothing compared to the time loss in a dungeon when a member dies, EVEN if they were bound nearby (which is another rule).

4) Take care of all buffing prior to entry into the dungeon, & there is no reason not to go in with everyone at full mana. Pet classes should have their summon pet spell memmed & ready to cast right away.

5.) Everyone goes in at the SAME TIME. As soon as the first person enters, the clock starts, so don't waste a few minutes IN the dungeon waiting for the last person in your group to arrive. Go inside as a group.

6.) IF you are waiting to do chests and such at the end (which I agree with), STAY TOGETHER as a group when you tackle them. Blood of Nadox or Disinfectin Aura might be able to cure the nasty dots, but if you are looking at one chest 2 passages away when the rest of the group gets hit on a different one, they still might die.

7.) When attempting to disarm a chest trap, only the person making the attempt should be within AE radius of the chest. If the chest has no obvious traps (or you have none who can detect them), use dogdog to attack the chest with the rest of your group (including you) standing back out of range. If the trap triggers the 3-spawn of mobs, your pet will not aggro them and you can leave them alone (if you want) w/o wasting any time on them.

8.) Keep the group apprised of your progress. Many still don't know that hitting Alt-V will bring up the adventure window and give them a rundown, and some are too busy to check often. Don't let your apparent good progress on a collection quest lull your group into slowing down, because I've personally gone almost a half hour w/ no drops before when beforehand they had been dropping almost every 2 minutes.

9.) Clerics & rezzers, mezzers, etc, aren't required for a LDoN (normal setting). Abnormal groups CAN succeed. Don't waste an hour waiting for one key class.

10.) HAVE FUN!

Take care,
