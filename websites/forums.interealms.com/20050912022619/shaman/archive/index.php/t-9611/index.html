"Satallite Internet"

This is the problem.

As far as I know (which, admittingly, isn't that far), ports probably aren't your problem. EQ works by sending and recieving packets of information concerning the game. Your download speed (as you said, 40 kb/sec) is fine.

The upload is the problem. Satallite Connections save up packets and send them in one chuck every few minutes, or so I've been told. So if you are on long enough, there's a good chance the server might think you've disconnected, since you haven't sent information packets to them in a short while (a few minutes).

This is all based on hearsay, can anyone confirm what I think I know?
