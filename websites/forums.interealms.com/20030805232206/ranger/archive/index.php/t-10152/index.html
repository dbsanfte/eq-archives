Kedawen
The hardest part is the first break in when you drop down. There are several spiders, some of which see invis. With Epics and Fungi, you should be OK.

Either IU or crawling will get you to Xalgoz.

My technique for pulling him was to stand in the entrance to his general area (maybe one wanderer) then bind sight hop until I could see Xalgoz. Target him or his placeholder, then cast from where I was going to fight. While he is not visible, you can cast through the wall, (at least I could before the recent LoS patch). He won't aggro any of his buddies on his way to you at 55.

You could use IU to simply walk up to him to target, then walk back to your fighting area, but I'm cheap : )

Eldron
With lev on, go in and go straight ahead. Drop down the shaft and make sure to catch the first ledge. (you'll be facing it, use F9 to spy the ledge before you jump off) If you miss it, will will float down to the pit of spiders and things will be tougher on you.

Once on the ledge, head straight forward to the room with the broken up floor. There will be one mob between you and that room. Drop down through the floor, do a 180 and look for the tunnel.

Head down the tunnel, go straight at the T. Clear mobs around the towers. Head in to the right. There will be a tower that is open on the top lvl and leads down to the bottom. You can re-lev here, walk up the highest point of the wall, step into the tower and cast a lvl 9 dd on a mob below, quickly step back to the ground. The mob will run up to you from the bottom lvl. Kill all 4 on the ground floor of this tower. Then float down to the bottom of it once clear.

I like to come in this tower as now you have a nice place to pull to. I have not had anything aggro through the walls of the tower. So at 51, I could single pull mobs back to that tower. Whats more, the temple is right in front of that towers doorway. Clear the mobs one by one. Make sure to keep an eye on the tower spawn timer. Its nice to fight in there, as you wont get any healer adds. Which is probably a must to keep up with the spawn if you are solo with no healer for yourself.

Everything in the church can be single pulled. Bring a ItU caster or a few ten dose shaman potions..

Using ItU go to the church entrance, target Xalgoz (or Ph, he will be in the middle furthest back). Move to the back of the church (outside behind where Xalgoz is), there is a little niche there. Use cancel magic to pull Xalgoz through the wall. He will take a long path, but even my lvl 45 ranger didn't get adds from this. He is a Necro, so Magic resist gear is very useful.
