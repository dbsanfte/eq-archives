I just did some quick calculations and the overall spec suit looks to beat the mire suit. One thing to note is that the Mire suit has no dex. The Spec suit has less sta but more than makes up for it in every other area.

Note: This counts 2 bracers.

[FONT=Courier New]
--------Mire----Spec
AC------227-----251
Str------27------25
Sta------35------22
Wis------21------28
Agi------30------26
Dex-------0------28
Cha------17------29
HP------495-----625
Mana----490-----620
SF-------23------36
SC-------26------29
SD-------27------31
SP-------26------36
SM-------25------37
[/FONT]

The Mire is much more cost effective, but it is probably something you would want to make up your mind about now rather than later as it will be more costly in the end if you do change your mind later on after collecting a few pieces.

The Spec suit has two Type 2 slots and one Type 3 Slot compared to Black Mire which has a Type 1, Type 2 and Type 3 Slot. There are a few nice Type 2 augments available, although expensive. I think I recall one as having 5 sta, 5 dex and 20 hp.

If we take an example cost for a piece with augmentations. Lets say Venegance 3 and The Type 2 Aug I mentioned above would be used in both (a second time for the Spec piece).

Mire
----
Armor - 760
Type 1 Aug - Free (One of the dungeon drops)
Type 2 Aug - 1150
Type 3 Aug - 1150
Total - 3060 adventure points

Spec
----
Armor - 1492
Type 2 Aug - 1150
Type 2 Aug - 1150
Type 3 Aug - 1150
Total - 4942 adventure points

Both have their advantages. the Mire has better value for time invested while the Spec has superior stats.

Breastplate:
Incorporeal Chain of the Specter: AC: 61 Str: +13, Dex: +11, Wis: +10, HP +100, Mana +100 Sv Fire: +12, Sv Cold: +12, Sv Poison: +12 Rec. level: 65 type2, type2, type 3 RNG ROG SHM

Both BPs are excellent. Only reason I would choose Black Mire over this is because of the overall HPs you would get and the cost of the two items. Given that the Black Mire has 10sta I will gain about 125-130hps vs. the 100 (110-115 visible) hps from the Specter. All depends on if you are getting points quickly and if you want to spend twice as many points. Advantage Black Mire (imho)

Arms:
Incorporeal Arms of the Specter: AC: 29, DEX: 6, STA: 6, CHA: 6, HP: 80, Mana: 70, Sv Fire: 7, Sv Cold: 7, Sv Magic: 7, Rec. level: 65 type2, type2, type 3 RNG ROG SHM

These arms are very similiar. The specter will net about 20hp more. Only reason I would take the Black Mire over them is because it has poison/disease resists and half the points needed. Drawback 20 less hp and 5-6ac. Advantage Black Mire (imho)

Bracers:
Incorporeal Wristband of the Specter: AC: 23, CHA: 4, WIS: 4, AGI: 6, Hp: 70, Mana: 70, Sv Fire: 3, Sv Magic: 3, Sv Cold: 3, Sv Poison: 3, Rec. level: 65 type2, type2, type 3 RNG ROG SHM

Armor class is virtually the same. Hps are the same due to getting 5hps per STA. Specter doesn't have any STA so the HPs are the same. Advantage Black Mire (imho)

Overall, I think both sets of armor are very nice. The boots, gloves and helm I don't need so I won't comment on those. The advantage to the Specter is that you get 2 type 2 slots vs. one type 1 and one type 2 and some of the pieces are clearly better.

The arms and the bracer I am going to go with Black Mire since they are so similiar. I am still up in the air a little about the BP. It will all depend on how close I get to the STA cap.

In the end though its all about time invested vs. reward. The Black Mire is half the points of the Specter yet some of the items are similar or actually better in my opinion. The armor sets both have type 3 slots which is the most important slot. The other slots can be filled up with augments you get on adventures or by spending even more points to fill slot 2s (I haven't see any slot 2s drop on adventures). Since I want to maximize the amount of points I earn I will probably lean towards the Black Mire and save up for Type 3 slot augments... as well as that nice little weapon in the Guk camp:
