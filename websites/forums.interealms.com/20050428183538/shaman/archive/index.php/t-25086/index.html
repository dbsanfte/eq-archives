Tolgar
There is a bridge that crosses a river between Barrens and Durotor, at that bridge travel south along the eastern side of the river until you get to the mountains. Right where the mountains and river meet there is a small 'spirit' stone that marks where the hidden path leads up the mountain. The path is narrow and winds back and forth up the mountain and Telf is near the top along the path.
Makelike
From Razor Hill head out like your going to Otown but veer to the right instead of heading down the ravine. You will come out above the ravine and pass a little house with an orc farmer inside. Continue along the edge and you will come to the hidden cave to your right, it will have 3 burning blade thugs outside. In relation to the ravine its right above the fairy section.
