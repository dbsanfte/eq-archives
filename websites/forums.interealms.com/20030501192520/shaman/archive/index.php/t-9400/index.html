Thurdar
I thought kindly is good enough for Kirn, I didn't think I hit ally until after that turnin. Been a while though so I'm not positive. Might be worthwhile to do the intial stages again a few times to get her up to ally before you try again. With PoP, killing the dorf in Butcherblock and repeating the freeport turnin a few times might be the easiest if you're a good race. Evil race would proably be quicker to do the Rathe Mountain part again. I just hated the wait for the wandering spirit on that leg.

As for how to get there, I always preferred to do it with a single group. I'm biased though, we used to do a lot of exp camps in our mid 50s though 60 in the Hole. Our camp spot was always beside the docks and if you can make it that far, jumping down the waterfall and fighting to the skelly tower is trivial (just don't fight near the scalekeeper). Plus, I love that jump off the waterfall. :D It's one of the coolest sights in EQ (imo) and everyone should do it at least once. Just make sure people slowly walk off the edge and don't touch their movement keys on the way down and you'll always hit the water at the bottom. Be sure to make fun of anyone that can't follow directions and splats when they miss the pool. :D

All you really need to hunt the hole is a good 55+ tank, enchanter, cleric and shaman (and someone to evac out if you jump off the waterfall). Good crowd control is key here since you can't really pull singles, pulls tend to be between 5 and 10 mobs which used to be really fun when you had a couple of unmezzable rock golems. Now granted, this was back in the way when mobs that hit for 150 where scary.
