Tarthug
A few tips.

1) make sure your pet is doing the most damage it can. I give my pet 2 proccing weapons, usually mage fists, muzzle, belt (why not), Str of the Daiku, Focus, HASTE (SLTW), and PE (ok ... it's overkill). Because you lack your epic and you have not mentioned a JBB then you'll need to make up mana free damage. The pet is your best dot.

2) don't load it up with dots. Our dots are not all that efficient. I only used Pox (only 1 cast) back when I did it at 61 because I'm impatient. Let dogdog do the damage and try to help and tank for it.

3) this won't help much, but load up on FR, but do not sacrifice HP or AC for it. Use potions and whatever else you can get your hands on. Every little bit helps. Shoot for 100 FR at 65.

4) cast Q at 90% health, keep it up all the time, keep Tnarg's loaded as you'll likely need 1-3 casts of it towards the end.

5) DO NOT LET SLOW RUN OUT!!! Put it on a timer and keep it up. You cannot win against an unslowed GW.

6) Make a higher end DS potion ... why not, every little bit helps.

Hope it helps.

GandarrTheletch
Gwurms aren't really MR, you can use that to your advantage.

Pull with Malo or Malos, reinvis yourself and run him to a safe spot, then root him. While he is rooted, debuff the snot outta him, his low MR will keep him rooted for a while. Hit him with a DR debuff, sinia, Cripple, slow him ... then get your mana back! Before you start doing damage, leave him rooted and debuffed to the gills and just canna5 or canna-dance until you are full mana again. Also, swap out the spells you don't need! I've never had a sinia run out while killing one of these, ditch that spell and put in Kragg's. Load up your best combo of dots you are going to use, reslow him (start a timer) and start casting. I usually lead out with a disease dot and swap it out for a poison dot while he's still rooted since he won't summon you till he hits 97 health, hit yourself with Q and bonzai!

Usually the extra debuffs, the extra mana you regained by having him rooted after you debuffed him, and having the quick heal spell to hit yourself with if there is a damage spike will be enough to guarantee your victory.

Good luck!
