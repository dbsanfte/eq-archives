If you have the help of a guild, or even just one group of friends over 50, quest the Shield of Bane warding. Nice AC and even wis, easy to get. and the quest itself gives nice exp.

Attend Naggy raids and hope to win the Polished Mithril Mask from the named FG in the lair.

Get a JB helm. Dirt cheap, and very hard to beat that AC on helms.

Do the Coldain Military Wristguard quest.

Buy a pair of also dirt cheap JB arms.

Go sit in KC for a while if you want to be REALLY lazy and loot bodies you find for the no drop armor, pretty sure most every one of those beats your AC, the Bp sure does, and it is quite common

Go hunt sun Revenants in Scarlet Desert. The camp on the south border near the Grey has an occasional named spawn which drops sun leggings, those beat yours.

While hunting in SD, listen to /ooc because eventually someone hunting at the Rev temple will get a LORE smiter's Girdle which is rotting away. Go loot it.

IMMEDIATELY upon reading this, get to the bazaar, buy a Charred Guardian Shield and destroy that ToV, cost on CGS is lower than anything else I listed, and if you think 1 wis is more important than 10ac then you aren't even reading this post anymore.

Easiest thing to get for leggs so far is Jaristh Scale Leggings. Just shout and beg and normally people will be cool and let you loot the leggings off of the jailer. Trash your Bloodstained mantle and loot the Jarsith BP too. I used it for about 6 levels just due to being poor.

What you need to realize is that AC is much more important that wisdom. See, we shaman, can cannibalize, which bring our mana back quick. And if your AC is 600, you're going to take a few hits, and fall like an enchanter.

So screw all that wisdom gear, that's a mistake. Go buy a Totemic Helm, if having a JB Helm gets you down, it's still a very reasonable helm for the price, and many shaman at high levels still use it.

Check out my profile, most of the stuff I'm wearing costs under 1000k a piece. Accept a few things. OH! and if you don't know how to Canni Dance, read up on it. It's a life saver.
