Orakul Fatmystic
==================================================

The small sarnak just inside the zoneline in Chardok, Herald Telcha gives out a quest for two chardok rings. He won't attack you even if you are kos and won't fight back. He has low hp and may have been killed. Quick respawn time (same as the rest of the dungeon).

To do his quests you need faction. Kill goblins/spiders in Nurga/Droga to gain faction, and save the 'Mt. Death Mineral Salts' and 'Green Goblin Skin' they occasionally drop. Turn in two salts at a time or one skin at a time to the Herald for decent faction hits.

Max Ally Faction from Max KOS: 3700 faction hits
One Droga/Nurga goblin/spider kill: 1 faction hit
One turn-in of either skins or salts: 2 or 3 faction hits (unconfirmed)

All rewards are ALL/ALL and MAGIC LORE NO DROP
All rewards require that the quester is level 50 or higher

==================================================

1st Ring:

Requirements:
High warmly faction (100 points below ally, 1000 points in absolute terms).

1st ring steps:

Talk to Herald Telcha in Chardok and say "I serve the sarnak". You will be rewarded with:

Di`Zok Signet of Service
AC 2 CHA/WIS/INT+1 HP+65 Mana+45 - Weight 0.1

==================================================

2nd Ring:

Requirements:
Ally faction

2nd ring steps:

1. Talk to 'a goblin traitor' (2-3 hour spawn) in Nurga and say "what evidence". He will give you a note. (-50,-75)

2. Take this note to 'a Sarnak spy' in Frontier Mountains. Follow the dialogue and you will eventually say "I will get the report". He will give you a forged note. (+1880,+3635)

3. Take this forged note to "a shady goblin" in Warsliks Woods near Cabilis along with a 'Drogan Warlord's Insignia' (random drop in Nurga/Droga off goblins). He will give you a report to Skargus. 24 Hour spawn approximately. (-1645,-1135 on the 2nd floor of the building outside the city entrance)

4. Take this report to 'an angry goblin' (5 day spawn) in Droga (+180,+1940). This will cause him to walk through the tunnel and into the dining room, then into a small cubby at approximate loc (+250,+1600). Then he despawns and Warlord Skargus spawns. He's a level 56 SK, has 10-15k hitpoints, hits for 165. Beware of his ~850 point harmtouch and possible fear spells. Kill Skargus and loot his Head of Skargus.

5. Bring the Head and the 1st Ring back to Herald Telcha and you'll be rewarded with:

Regal Band of Bathezid
AC5 CHA/WIS/INT+2 HP+85 Mana+65 - 5 Charges of 'Aegis of Bathezid' (25pt dmg shield, 25 min duration) - Weight 0.1

You may hand back the ring to Telcha and get it back with 5 full charges. Make sure you're still ally.

==================================================

Cords (3rd) Quest:

Requirements:
Ally faction
Undead Dragon Sinew (Trakanon, Sebilite Juggernauts)
Urn of Spirits (Drusella Sathir)
2nd ring

Trakanon is a 3 day spawn in Old Sebilis, Juggernauts respawn in about 27 minutes in the deepest area of Sebilis. Can also drop off the Sebilite Protector. Trakanon requires several groups to kill, while one (strong) group can hold juggernaut area.

Drusella is supposedly a 24 hour spawn deep in the east wing of Howling Stones. Easy with one group.

Cords Steps:

Hand Undead Dragon Sinew, Urn of Spirits and 2nd Chardok Ring to Queen Velazul Di'Zok in Chardock. You get the 2nd ring back along with the Cord:

Spirit Wracked Cord
Shoulder, Arms and Wrist slot
AC 12 STA+5 HP+100 CR+10 MR+5 - Effect: Aura of Battle (innate, +10 atk and 2 regen/tick) - MAGIC LORE NO DROP - Weight 0.0
