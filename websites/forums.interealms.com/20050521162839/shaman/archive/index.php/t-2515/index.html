Level: 19
Mana: 75

Max Duration: 2 minutes 6 seconds

Actual Effects: Target: Damage over Time (126)
Target: Disease Direct Damage (30)
Description: This is the second in the line of disease spells.
They are slow working, mana efficient, somewhat overlooked DoTs. Over the course of two minutes, this spell will do 120 damage. For only 75 mana at level 19, that is excellent.
Some say the spell isn't even worth casting since it takes so long to work. Try timing how long a monster takes to die while solo and while grouped. I think you'll see that the spell can actually do its full damage.
Not all our DOT's stack, disease will not stack with disease and poison will not stack with poison. BUT poison will stack with disease, so if you cast Tainted Breath THEN cast Affliction it will appear as if they stacked when in fact the last spell you cast overwrites the first (not sure but since it has a immediate damage factor may work the other way around too). This works for all DOT's.
On the other hand, hey can be a taunt and recasting them may save your casters life.

This spell will only remove Magic, Fire or Ice based spells and is the weakest in this series.
