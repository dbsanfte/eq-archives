Its a basic skill check like most others in EQ; your skill checks to see if it fires everytime you cast a spell, assuming your skill works, you use a certain % less mana. It also uses that same check to see if you prevent a fizzle as well.
The AA Spell casting Mastery makes your specialization "fire" more often, increasing the chance by 30% at the end of the AA. I am currently working this one and even iwth just 2 ranks in it I already see a significant savings.

The mana savings isn't random or otherwise. Specialization has been parsed to be a flat savings % off casting skills in a certain casting skill type. While the game does indeed claim that specialization is a random check, this is not what actually happens.

50 specialization is roughly ~2.5% savings
200 specialization is roughly ~10% savings

SCM1 is ~2.5% savings
SCM3 is ~10% savings

I'm not sure what SCM2 is..guessing ~5% but i dont recall.
