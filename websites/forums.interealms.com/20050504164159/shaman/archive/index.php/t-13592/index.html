3 of us went to hate yesterday, myself, 61 necro and 61 wizard. Layout is completely different. Instead of zoning into a small room you're now in a large 'cubby' for lack of a better word. One roamer (ashenbone drake) was visible, but didn't agro. We spent a couple of hours exploring near the entrance. Agro (even at 57) was very small, but still happens through walls/doorways. Very few roamers (I believe 2 near the zone in spot) and the spawn time seemed to be roughly 20 minutes. Mobs (near the entrance) consisted of the same type but drops have been reduced slightly (especially on regular sellables) however equipment drops seemed to have been increased (we got one bp, one robe and several other pieces) in the couple of hours we were there. Hard to tell if pathing is really fixed since the layout is completely different. I did end up agro'ing 2 chests (which use to have almost zero agro range) through a wall maybe 20 feet away. After hearing the emote, we waited about 20 seconds before they finally showed up, from another direction no less.

We'll probably go back after the novelty wears off. We started as the only one's in the zone but soon had 4 to 6 other full groups near us. Without knowing the zone very well, we didn't want to stray to far from the entrance.
