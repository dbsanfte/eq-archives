If you have an EB item you can solo the Skeletons in The Grey. they are decent exp, no loot. Plane of Disease if you zone in and head left along the wall untill just before the gap in the wall, set up and reverse Kite the goos might work. But for the best EXP try to get into groups in Plane of Valor... can't remember the level requirement, and then get flagged for Bastion of Thunder Bees.

Also level to 65 before you bother with AAs as you will get in better, faster groups and in certain zones get an AA in 45 mins or so... thats with Elemental flags, you need to be guilded and 65 for this. But even BoT Beez or PoV groups are pretty good exp.

Go for run3, regen 3, mental clarity 3 (enables Canni5) Canni 5, then if you like to solo go for Virulent paralisis, the new DoT crits and Pet sumoning (starts with 3 dog pets that last 1 min every 12 mins that are like a free dot). I also like extended buffs and am working on Quickbuff for quick casts of Quiesence. I have mass group buff for raids as well but if you are not in a guild that raids it is totaly useless.

Hope that helps a little. Best advice is level up to 65 then get cash for spells while you work AAs.
