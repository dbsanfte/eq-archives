Naturally I raced to do both and didn't have a whole lot of fun while doing it, because you're always looking at that next piece of armor you want or what spells you get at the next level. Thanks to my friends I made when I was very low level, pre-10. I got my epic at lvl 56. I made 60 and got a copy of torpor (for FREE) from a friend of a friend.

Now that I'm post 60, I'm enjoying the game and RL alot more. I don't play near as much as I used to, and I'm quite happy with my non-uber armor (I have 950ish AC and barely over 2k hp unbuffed) and casual-raid guild (we pick a target and raid once a week).

Experience life, make RL friends, I've even made a few in game that I've met in RL. Just remember, if it ceases to be fun, it's no longer a game, and you should not play it.
