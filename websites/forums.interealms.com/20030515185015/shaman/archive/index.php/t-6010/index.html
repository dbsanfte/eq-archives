Falora
Okay, I know I've been the whiny voice of "the little shaman" who would never see a JBB or Fungi or any of those other neat items/spells you big folks get. Well, I must apologize to you. It's possible.

Last night I went to the Bazaar mainly to get some food since I didn't feel like making a bunch of Fish Rolls. When I'm there I usually check the price of a Barbarian Spiritist's Hammer, look for spells and, for the fun of it, see if anyone happens to be selling a JBB.

I couldn't believe it when I actually saw one for sale. And it was only 15k! I just knew it had been sold but checked out the merchant anyway. It was still there! Unfortunately I only had 4k in the bank so I started making desperate, pleading begs to my guild for anyone to loan me the 11k I needed. As I was begging and getting my cash from the bank I got a /tell from the merchant. It seems he was on while I checked him out. He asked if I was interested in the bracer and said that for a fellow shaman he'd lower the cost to 12k. More desperate tells to my guild then followed. I just KNEW someone was going to poach it from me. So I begged the guy not to sell it for at least 10 minutes.

As we were talking I asked him if he'd trade the bracer for 4k, my ODS and my Sarnak Battle Shield. He hadn't played in a while and was pretty much selling off his shaman stuff. He asked what an ODS and SBS were. When I showed him he, get this, agreed to trade the JBB straight over for the ODS! He was mainly playing a cleric and felt the ODS would be a great benefit to the character.

To be fair, I told him the JBB usually went from 15k - 25k and the ODS usually sold for around 6k. He didn't care. He wanted to help out a fellow shaman and felt the deal was square.

I nearly cried.

So I'm now the very proud owner of my own Space Ghost power blaster.

For all of us little guys out there, it's possible for miracles to happen.
