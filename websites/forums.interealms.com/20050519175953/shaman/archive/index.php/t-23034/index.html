Korelen
If you are looking for spells, then your best bet is to get KT flagged. To do this, you need to complete the following:

1. Sewers -OR Smith Rondo
There are four sewer trials that you need to complete.
If you are in a guild, you may want to suggest for them to kill Rondo. He will flag you for all the sewer trials so you do not need to complete it.

2. Vxed
You need to kill approx. 50 mobs, and a nihil ghost will spawn near the end of the zone. There is a zonewide emote that is triggered when he spawns. You can then crawl to the end of the zone to hail this npc. Once you do that, there is a stone you can click to exit. Named drop spells here: GoD Slow and GoD heal.

3. Tipt
Your goal here is to kill a boss kvy, and he is quite the nasty. There are basically what my guildmates refer to rooms. First room is the crag trap. This trap will spawn 4 crags. Once all the crags are dead, a named crag will spawn. He is always dark blue from what I have seen. He drops a piece for BiC as well as a few decent augs - a hp/end or a hp/mana. The second room are undead. The ghosts can drop a piece for the BiC quest. Once you clear that, you go to the riddler. ( I think there is something before this - I forget ) If you complete the riddle correctly, you will get a BiC quest piece if you did the pre-flag in AS for Tipt. After this is the boss. WAR cannot go defensive on it, or it will turn on your healers. SoE thought this was a good way to balance this encounter so that knights could tank him. /shrugs. The boss drops the last piece, and you need to cross the bridge and hail a npc. Once you do that, you can click on a stone to exit.

Sewers, Vxed, and Tipt will grant you access to KT.

KT is an awesome zone for xp. Generally, groups will xp off the crags and hynids in the field that is the right of the summoner's ring. To the right of your camp is a temple. There is a kvy that spawns in the doorway that is a PH for a named. Named can drop spells. Also, trash can drop a decent range item and a mask. Named can also drop augs. Your best bet to get yours spells are in KT since the frequency of named are greater than they are in the sewers, vxed, and tipt. Also, if I recall correctly, Jeikli (sp) [ BoT / Q upgrade ] only drops in KT+.

Fudapuda
Tipt:

First trap spawns 6 cragbeast babies, not 4. Most are easy to single pull, and a skilled puller can get all 6 separately.
Then Enraged Cragbeast Mother or something like that: easy to take down with ranger WS, or just heavy DPS, as she doesn't have many HP. Two healers spamming fast heals is usually plenty. Mama drops, IIRC, 4 different augments: 30hp/30mana, a +10atk/+2HP regen one, one with HP and endurance on it, and I think a 4th one. Temporary Power Cell A drops (I think 100%) on mama.

Point about ghosts: total of 8 ghosts, and some are VERY hard to split, so you'll get 2 at a time sometimes. And when each (first) ghost dies, a second one spawns on death spot, so you really end up killing 16 ghosts. Temporary Power Cell B is random off of ghosts, and frustrating cause the drop rate is still too low IMO.

Third trial is a room pretty tightly packed with mobs, one of whom drops a key to a gate later up the canyon, so you have to clear the room until the key drops. Mobs can be single pulled in most cases, and if you clear one side, you can ignore the other side and move on. Temporary Power Cell C drops from any of the mobs in this trial, and often drops more than one per run.

Riddler is easy. They've added some new riddles lately, but all are pretty easy to figure out, especially if you go ahead and collect the drops and see what he's offering as the solutions. Temporary Power Cell D you get from Riddler--just hail him (after completing the riddle) and if you have the Tipt "preflag" from Vaifen in Abysmal Sea, he gives you some text like "I sense that you are here for another reason than your companions, I think you could use this" and hands you the Power Cell.

Final trial: WS works well, tank with Furious works well (don't use Defensive disc, as that now drops you from the mobs aggro list and he kills somebody else--usually your healer). He always drops a bracer, but class is random. All are in the 130Hp range, plus ACs appropriate to the class, and some effect appropriate to the class. Temporary Power Cell E is (again, I think 100%) dropped by the named Kyv.

Tipt is fun, and its good exp too, just slightly less than what you would get in KT for the same period of time. Its just hard to motivate people to go there, because you are committing yourself to probably 3 hours or more of grouping, depending on whether you use pooka pet and how good the group is.
