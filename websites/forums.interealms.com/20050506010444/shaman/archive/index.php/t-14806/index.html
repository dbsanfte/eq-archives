Luclin's Arriana
As a shaman, my duty was always on one of the 4 crowd control teams. AFAIK, we have very little to contribute to the behemoth itself. A malo(s) to make nukes get off better, and then spam nuking with our cruddy nukes.

Best advice, 4 crowd control teams of 2 people, an enchanter and either a shaman or a druid. Once the Behemoth becomes targetable, everyone besides CC gathers at one of the doors (don't rush in from both sides). MT gets supernal elixir, /discs riposte or evasive, runs in to engage behemoth to turn it away from rest of raid, and get it to waste its first AE on a single person. Cleric MGB's supernal elixir, rest of raid charges. Max DPS...melees /disc at 90% health, manaburns/lifeburns, clerics nuke and heal after the fight is over. Get behemoth dead, clear as many corpses out of the Behemoth's room as possible, then run everyone else out...CC teams will be on the verge of being overwhelmed by bugs at this time, so they all die, get dragged/rezzed. Living people hail the gnome for the tactics flagged. Specific intelligent people (and those with divine aura/harmshield) run into behemoth room between the 4k AEs to drag out the remainder of corpses.
