Usually a hard core raiding guild won't take too kindly to ppl switching to other toons and not playing their main. In our guild, when a player receives a nice piece of phat lute, it's considered an investment by the guild as a whole to better the guild, not just the one toon. If you disappear for a while to play an alt, and the loot has not been utilized for the betterment of the guild during that time, then the investment has been wasted, and you are not likely to receive much in the way of loot any time soon.

Just one perspective.
