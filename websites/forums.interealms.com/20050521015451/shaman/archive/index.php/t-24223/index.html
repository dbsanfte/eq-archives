I'm currently 2-boxing oow runes using a 56-cleric on my 2nd box in the Murkgliders hive instance. Strategy is fairly simple. Root-rot PH mobs with the shaman:

- pull with malicious decay, then root them with VP
- stick breath of wunshi, breath of ultor, bane, blood, and epic
- reapply dots and VP as they wear off
- cleric sits around ITU and does nothing (can rez me in if i screw up)

Then when nameds spawns:

- unsuspend my pet
- pull with petrifying earth, hoping it sticks
- clicky malosini from VP neck + turgurs + cripple
- epic
- ancient nife, hoping for root break before summon
- channel spirit call
- keep Q up on myself
- bane + blood as healing permits
- cleric casts cheal spells like crazy
- keep it rooted once it hits about 20% (no snare)

Haven't lost yet, but pretty sure at my current gear/AA levels there's no way I could kill these solo without a drastic change of tactics. Up until recently, the cleric as my boxed partner was a no brainer, since I wanted to level him to 56 for the 96% rez + he has invis2undead. But now that he's 56, I have some other options, a 55 ogre SK in mediocre (blue diamond cultural) bazaar gear and a 65 druid in fairly decent bazaar gear. I'm thinking about switching my 2nd box toon to one of those to get snare. On the surface, the 65 druid seems obvious (despite having to use ITU potions), especially if I'm going to continue root-rotting, but I figured a melee partner might make this a bit easier if I could manage to keep a gimpy SK healed up versus the murkglider hive mobs (named and regular).

Any thoughts? Would you prefer the (55 sk + 67 shm), (56 cle + 67 shm), or (65 dru + 67 shm) for this? Why? What strats would you use for various combinations? Should I be farming runes somewhere other than murkgliders with these duos?

Did the instances with my 67 Mage (67 Shaman here). My mage has his GoD pet and all of the mitigation aa.

A mage GoD pet tanks better than a 200 aa warrior - no doubts. In addition the pet stuns out agro moderate paladins and can hold agro through any dots and mage crit DDs.

I can kill the named in around 2 minutes or less with hardly any mana loss just casting tnarg twice and 2 Qs on the pet. The mage does 3-4 bolt nukes.

I use potions to IVU and move to the other spawn on the mage. He CoHs the shaman. We kill the next named/ph and repeat back to first spawn.

I can honestly say in my opinion that a shaman/mage duo can take so much content - even content that doesn't slow. The mage also has his epic but although the epic is better for taunts the GoD pet is the best mitigator. In fact I managed to kill a BoT wing named with the pet as MT and just myself and a druid patching. Its a mean combo - and the maintenance is low - setup some hot keys for /assist <shm> and /pet attack - sit back and Q the pet and watch the mobs die.
