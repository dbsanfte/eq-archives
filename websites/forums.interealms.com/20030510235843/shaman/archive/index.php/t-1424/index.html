Samuqan
My method (and it seemed to work well for me):

Go into tank mode. This includes going for AC/HP gear more so than WIS gear, but a good balance is fine. But most importantly is that you will need to buff yourself to your best abilities so that you can take the hits.

The JBB is very easy to channel through, almost surprisingly easy. It's not rare for me to take 3-4 very hard hits and still channel through a cast of it.

Basically, you'll use the same strategy you are now, only that instead of meleeing, you'll just be constantly clicking your JBB. The pet is still a breathing DOT, and you still tank for him, only difference being that your primary damage output is now the JBB instead of your melee.

One thing, though: the REIF is still a great weapon to use for this, just because it likes to proc quite often when your DEX is high. Keep auto attack on and you'll get in a hit after every JBB cast. Sometimes, the hit will also be a proc of the REIF.

Donar
If your ac is decently high and you keep regen on yourself, it doesn't really matter that the jbb breaks root. You can still channel through quite a few hits, especially if you slow the mob, and if your ac is good, you won't take that much damage. A high damage weapon, regardless of delay, is better in this situation as the cast time for the jbb is so long. You can still root mobs, of course, and heal yourself if you're taking too much damage or getting too many interrupts, but for the most part, stand your ground and cast between hits. A pet is still nice to have, in my opinion, but not always necessary.
