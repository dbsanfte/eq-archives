Well, I have been made a class representative for the priest forum on the druid boards. So I am going to try not only say what I think shaman need but rather what you all think we need. I am going to be posting a few polls later on, but to get the options for these polls I need a frame to work with. So I am going to outline what I think shaman would be happy with.

Please tell me if you would be happy with this, or if not, what is missing. Do not post specific ideas please - that is partly reserved for the polls and partly for further discussion.

Proposed shaman improvements:

* Increase single target heals to a level where shaman are, through a combination of slow and heals, clearly preferably for a group to a second cleric as far as damage prevention / healing is concerned.

* Add some sort of emergency heal for shaman, most likely as an AA.

* Add some sort of group or AE heal (mainly for raids).

* Increase OoW DoTs by the same percentage that necromancers receive.

* Make shaman DoTs more useable by decreasing DoT aggro and / or adding resist modifiers to them.

* Increase shaman nuking power to roughly the level clerics are at now.

* Increase the HPs of the focus line to be above the HPs rangers and beastlords can provide.

* Replace the stat modifiers of the OoW buffs with effects like strikethrough. The overall effect of such a buff should be of a magnitude a little below that of Bulwark of Vie (seeing as vie wears off with damage).

* Slighty increase the resist modifier on Nihil. Slightly increase duration on Nihil.

Would that make you happy?
