TOAST OF THE IRISH:

A man stumbles up to the only other patron in a bar and asks is he can buy him a drink.

"Why, of course," comes the reply.

The first man then asks, "Where are you from?"

"I'm from Ireland," replies the second man.

The first man then responds, "You don't say. I'm from Ireland too!!

Let's have another round to Ireland."

"Of course," says the second man.

Curious, the first man asks, "Where in Ireland are you from?"

"Dublin."

"I can't believe it," says the first man. "I'm from Dublin too! Let's
have another drink to Dublin!"

"Of course," replies the second man.

Curiosity again strikes the first man as he asks, "What school did you go to?"

"Saint Mary's," says the second man. "I graduated in '62."

"This is unbelievable!!" the first man said. "I went to Saint Mary's and graduated in '62 too!"

About that time one of the regulars sauntered in and sat down at the bar.

"What's been going on?", he queries the bartender.

"Nothing much," the bartender replied. "The O'Mally twins are drunk again."
