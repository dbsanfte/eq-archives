Dandi
Anyone have any advise?
First, decide what spell skill you want to specialize. They all can go to 50 but only one can go over that to 200, and that one will only go over 50 when you have at least one point in all of them. Because of that, people generally only say you've "specialized" in the one that you take above 50. I would strongly recommend Alteration, but feel free to research and decide for yourself. Do not go on to step two until you are ready to immediately do step three.

Second, go to a shaman trainer. Put 1 point into each of the specializations that doesn't already have one.

(Optional step: Get KEI.)

Third, go to a quiet corner in a small, quiet zone. Whatever you do, be certain that you are far enough away from popular hang-outs that people with spell casting messages on will not get spammed. (My favorite spot is behind the waterfall in Rivervale.) Mem the lowest mana and fastest cast time spell in the skill that you wish to be your primary specialization. For alteration, Minor Healing has the fastest cast time + lowest mana cost. Cast it repeatedly on yourself until your chosen specialization hits 51.

Fourth, either in the same session or at another time finish getting your chosen specialization and it's spell skill to 200. (Meaning get both "Alteration" and "Specialize: Alteration" to 200, or if not alteration whichever skill you choose.)

Fifth through eighth, do the same for the remaining spell skills. Find low mana, fast casting spell and chain cast till maxxed. Because those specializations can only go to 50, and skill improves much more quickly at lower abilities, the specializations for those will max much more quickly than the main skills.

Voilà, dramatically reduced fizzle rates. :)
