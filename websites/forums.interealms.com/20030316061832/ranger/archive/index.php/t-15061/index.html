Ladas Sindarin
Pbmax, attack gains from buffs and items (AoB and Vengeance) affect melee damage and archery damage. Just because you can't see the true attack rating for archery doesn't mean that casting Spirit of the Predator on yourself won't increase your archery damage.

In short...

1) The listed Attack on your character screen is a composite number based on your Skill with the weapon in the mainhand, your Offense, your Strength, and any attack buffs you have.

2) Attack buffs apply equally to all your forms of attack (1HS, 2HB, Archery, Throwing, Kick, etc)

3) Boosting your attack through items or buffs will enhance your archery damage.
