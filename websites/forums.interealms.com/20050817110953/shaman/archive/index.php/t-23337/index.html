Kror
Play what attracts your interest, there are no bad shamanic race/class combos.

Barb - well rounded very common
Ogre: - unstunnable from melle from the front is as close to overpowering as a racial ability gets, rare, can't fit through some doors w/o shrink, exp penalty
Troll: Regen rocks at early levels, nice quests (snare necklace) big exp penalty, rare
Iksar: Regen and AC bonus same exp penalty, forage, uncommon
Vah Shir: Well rounded, safe fall, great quests, semi common
Froglok: not great, not bad, only small shamanic race, good set of quests, will date the age of the toon

What else do you need to know to find a race that might be fun?

Ungkor
I'm a 65 troll shaman now.

If I had to do it again, I would go Ogre due to the frontal stun immunity. Stun immunity is much, much more useful at higher levels than regen, which is of small benefit due to the high regen spells and items you get.

JBB and troll regen (which is much better than Iksar regen) was amzaing at lower levels though. I practically leveled with JBB alone from 46-50. Its a very good amount of free dps at that level range.

One problem you'll face with Iksar, is you'll be KoS everywhere. Nobody likes a lizard hehe. Trolls are much the same, especially since you'll want to pick Innoruk as your deity to get the snare necklace quest.

Troll: Highest regen + jbb + night vision + 20% exp penalty
Iksar: good regen + innate AC + cudgel + night vision + 20% xp penalty
Ogre: front stun immunity + night vision +15% xp penalty
Barb: fast leveling, no night vision

Overall, I'm happy with troll. Even at 65, I still use the snare necklace. But, there are many times when stun immunity would have saved my life when tanking.
