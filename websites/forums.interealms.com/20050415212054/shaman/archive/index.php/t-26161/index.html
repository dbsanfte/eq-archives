+% skill mods like Dodge and Ripo and Specialization and Tradeskills are all "only the best works"

+% mods are capped at 35. That's Spellshield, DoT Shielding, Shielding, Strikethrough, Stun Resist.

Improved Dodge, Ferocity, Cleave, Sharpshooting are best described as melee focus effects, not mods. They work just the same way, with only the best working.
