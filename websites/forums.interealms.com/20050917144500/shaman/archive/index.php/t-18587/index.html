If you are a good race, just kill Nathyn's guards in Katta instead of the regular. To find them, zone in from TM, follow left wall (two left turns). You will see a dead end with two guards at it. On your left through an entranceway will be a mansion looking thing with 2 wolves and 2 guards. On your right is a large courtyard. You pull to the courtyard. The wolves can be single pulled easily. The guards usually can (at your level, later they come solo easy) by pulling from max range with a debuff (seems random, sometimes both come). Once those four are dead, open the door they are guarding, there are two more in sight to pull. If you get through those, there is a hidden room on the right of the first room, just inch in and autotarget, there are 1 or 2 back there. Lastly, the two doors at the back of the room offer a couple more guards to play with.

All of these give good Katta faction, negative vampire faction. Be careful, keep root and sow up and you should easily be able to make the zone if you run into any trouble.
