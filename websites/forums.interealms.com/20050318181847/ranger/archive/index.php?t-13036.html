I imagine once we get into the elemental Planes there will be lots of summoned mobs. Hopefully they will ALL be summoned, since if you're fighting half of one and half of the other, Nature's Rebuke really isn't that big an upgrade over Cry of Thunder...

One way you can check this out is by asking your friendly local wiz/mage/cleric to zone in and cast their low-level spell Sense Summoned. It has a zonewide range (handy for spotting Severillous' pet!) so if there are no summoned mobs at all, you'll find that out. For example it returns nothing in the Plane of Nightmares, and in the Plane of Disease it returns a mob's pet very far from zonein, so there seem to be zero summoned creatures there as well.
