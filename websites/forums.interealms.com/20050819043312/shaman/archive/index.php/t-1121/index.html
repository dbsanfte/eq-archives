Originally posted by Wally6500
learn SOW potions then stop. Any higher and you pissing money away.

I'd say learn Shrink and then stop. Shrink gets your skill to 70, which cuts down a LOT on failures. Going from SOW-trivial to Shrink-trivial cut my average cost from about 75-80 for a 10-dose of SOW to about 68 per 10-dose. I recovered about 600p of the 1k I spent getting it up there by selling ITU and Shrink potions (although it took a while), and the hundreds of SOW that I've made and sold then have been 10p cheaper to make each.

The secret to making money at it - no secret, really, just preparedness and persistance. Always have 3-5 on you; make an auction button and hit it every 15 minutes or every time you zone if not grouped or otherwise busy.
