deria
I suppose it depends where you are and what level you are. I never melee, unless I am really, really, really bored.

The time is better spent doing the canni-dance (indoors) or sitting on the hourse (outdoors) and hammering away at the nukes.

That being said, however, there are two thing to keep in mind:

1) At your level, you might do more damage meleeing than using your nukes/dots.

2) Meleeing will allow you to raise your defense. I strongly recommend that, at the VERY LEAST, you melee enough each level to get your 5 points of defense, offense, and piercing. It is *incredibly* hard to raise your defense once you enter 55+ range. You'll save yourself a world of trouble by taking care of it as you level. It is especially important, regarding defense, because you will be taking a *pounding* alot once you reach higher levels.
