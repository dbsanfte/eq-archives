Ungkor
Its about identical to duo'ing with a rogue or rng. Snare/Stun axes, however, can provide some very good aggro. ranger/rogue/zerker all seem to have about the same dmg mitigation as far as I can tell.

Honestly, if you don't want to duo with a traditional tank class, I'd probably recommend ranger, due to the added utility of their spells, the ability to track, etc..

Zerker's seem to be a fun diversion right now, nothing more hehe. They cannot do 1 thing that defines them over another class.

But...I kinda like my zerker. He's a nice change of pace from playing a shaman. I can pretty much take aggro at will, which is fun, and the style of the class is appealing.

I had good experiences with duoing with a berzerker. They have the ability to stun and snare, and they can put out decent DPS. They hold aggro well and seem to be relatively tough, but it may just be that the one that I duo'd with is really well equipped too :)
