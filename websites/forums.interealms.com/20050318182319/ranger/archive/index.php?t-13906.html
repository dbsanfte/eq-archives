Glade:

Just a little information that I have observed in PoD with the rats at Level 61.

Diseased Rats
Snare and Fearable quite easily. At least 4-5 spawns that roam towards the beach near the Graveyard. More elsewhere, but much harder to pull and fear without adds.

Swamp Rats
Snareable with some attempts, but NEVER have landed one fear (no immune message). I usually just root it, and zone, not worth the time it takes to kill it with arrows. They seem to hide in the water around the fly islands.

Pestilent Rats
Snareable most of the time on 1st or 2nd try. Fear is resisted quite often, but you can get it to land. They are usually found near the oozes in the middle river of slime areas. Again most of the time, just not worth killing.

Feeble Rats
Pretty much the same as Diseased Rats. Snare easy, fear easy. Can find some singles near the river if PoD is quite busy and pull to one of the walls.

My preference is to stay near the zone in, pull rats till they are all gone, maybe pull a few tsetian flies (some light blue some dark blue at 61), root-arrow kill till I can get a group. XP moves, but nothing like Castle/Caves XP.

Hope it helps,

Karali
