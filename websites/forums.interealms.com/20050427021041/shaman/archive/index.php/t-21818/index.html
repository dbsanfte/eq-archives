LOTS of GoD is root/rot-able, and if you and the ranger are both pretty skilled at moving and positioning mobs, a lot is open to you.

Riwwi arena is an insanely good loot camp, you'll do that with no problem.

Farming Barindu or Ferubi for random drops is harder, but I think you can work your way up to being able to do that as soon as you get a feel for how GoD mobs behave. And the drops, although, rare, are absolutely worth the time. =)
