Halybuton, the Bone Barbs (for the Totemic Greaves) also drop in Rathe Mountains - this is where I got mine as I didn't want to deal with Unrest. Coming out of the Feerrot, turn left and go to the second camp on the left, which is undead standing around a rock. Just keep killing stuff until a Barbed Skeleton spawns and drops the quest item. It's possible they spawn elsewhere in Rathe Mountains as well.

Sslytherr
There is a quest I did with my main ( a cleric ) for a paineel quest that required a basalt drake scale.

These scales drop off of the variety of drakes in Rathe Mountains. You can find "basalt drakes" there, but the scales can drop off of the plain ones. In fact the first drake my crew killed during a HG killing spree dropped my basalt scale. They are no drop though, and most folks just leave them be, so ya dont see any for sale.

If yer needing this scale, just go kill drakes in the HG area, they not that uncommon, just gotta not waste time looking for the basalt drakes.
