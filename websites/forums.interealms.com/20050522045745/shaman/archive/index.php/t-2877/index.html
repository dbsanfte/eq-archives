The glacier bear is a rare spawn, but is predictable and not too badly camped. The trick is to kill the 2 wandering ice bone skeles which are its PHs. Spawn time is just under 8 hours. I checked the cave, found the PHs were up, killed them, went away to do other stuff for 8 hours, came back and repeated. It took about 4 cycles to get the bear, but most people seem to get it in less. With my pet, envemoned breath and the poison DD (lvl 34 spell) he went down in no time. Conned light blue. I completed most of the Totemic quests (except BP and helm), as a shaman "rites of passage" thing. However, with the benefits of 20/20 hindsight, and with the influx of alternative armors these days, I think that you have to be pragmatic about Totemic. Some pieces are ridiculously easy to get (bracers, gauntlets, boots), some are ridiculously difficult (Breasplate, Helm). At level 29 either Rygoor or Totemic are a major upgrade to Banded, so I suggest that you do the easier pieces of Totemic and fill the gaps with Rygorr. It is definately worth seeking out a high level Shaman or Enchanter to get an uber charisma buff before you buy your dufrenite.

I will also suggest that you might wish to set up a "free heals" station at the entrance to Karnors Castle, and ask politely for looting rights on Jarsarth Bracers - they are really quite good, drop like candy and usually rot on the corpse. There are, of course, other Jarsarth drops but they are either crappy (helm) or very rare, or drop so far into KC that you are unlikely to get to them alive!

I would also mention the really quite nice stats on Blackened Alloy armor from Runnyeye. Ok, so its not uber, but some of it is pretty damned nice - the Medallion is something like 6AC 5wis 5int, which is pretty sweet around the neck. The belt has +5stam, etc. Runnyeye has a hefty dungeon exp bonus IIRC as well.
