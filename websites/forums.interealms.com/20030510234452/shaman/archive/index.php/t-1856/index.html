Togor
If you are looking for a quite place where you can solo dark blue singles all day long, East Karana may be the place for you. I was tired of looking all over for spawns in Oasis when lots of other people were doing the same thing, so I decided to move on. In East Karana there is a camp of gnoll reavers that wander all over a hill and so are easy to single pull. Just run to the right of the bridge from Nk to EK as you enter the zone. At 17 all of the gnolls were dark blue. At night undead reavers spawn. These were dark blue as well but were a little tougher but still doable. If you are careful, you won't get adds. If you do just run to the zone.
