Akelan
The rules on mobs in the wall are simple. If the mob touches a wall, you touch the same wall. If the mob is pushed deep in the wall, get as hard against the wall as you can and turn slightly into the wall. If the mob is pressed into a corner ( touching 2 walls) you need to get as deep into a wall as you can and hope that is the one the mob's center is inside.

Walls are used to control push and rampage/enrage damage; or directional procs, but aside from that, if your guild spends all its time with the mobs in the wall, find a new guild; they will never learn what a ranger, or even a rogue, is good for.
