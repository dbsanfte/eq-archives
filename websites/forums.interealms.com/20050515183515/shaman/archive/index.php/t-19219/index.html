Yedak
A lot of the confusion comes from the history of the lure spells.

At one point, the resist system was less comprehensive, and resists, particularly in velious for npcs were just insane.

So, they gave some special, unresistable nukes to wizards called lures to keep from marginalizing their contribution.

When they re-worked the resist system to reward heavier buffing and debuffing of resists, they made the lures very hard to resist, but no longer irresistable.

This evolution is probably the source of confusion.
