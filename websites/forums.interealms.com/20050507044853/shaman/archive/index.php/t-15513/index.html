Bazza
Pet hold works like this...

When you hit pet hold when your pet is not attacking your pet will follow it last command but not hit any thing it would normally do such as a mob hitting it or you.

When your pet is fighting some thing and you hit pet hold the pet will come to you or your last guard spot. So as long as you have the pet following you when you hit pet hold the pet will return to you.

If your are going to use your pet in any raids or camps with AoE mobs pet hold is a must for keeping the pet under control. It a key AA for mages beasties and necros and still very useful to the shaman community I would have thought.
