taraddar
As people have said it is possible to Turgur's RD but he's so damm resistant it isn't really worth trying. He also majorly mitigates it, you get a slightly msg if I recall. Last time we did him I don't even think we bothered trying to slow him.

Couple tips.

1) make sure your rangers don't get feared. Can't confirm it personally but I read that thier archery bugs and recycles slower after being feared. Supposedly majorly kills thier dps. Best way to do this is have them one on side of the room and the fight on the other or in the next room with the dragon in the center of the room.

2) He seems to be somewhat range sensitive for nuking from what I've heard from our casters. So hug the beasts belly if your trying to blow him away.

3) he procs a single target dot. I don't recall the name of it off hand but it was several hundred dmg a tick range and was disease based to cure so you want someone chain casting abolish disease on the MT.

4) What ever you do don't push him up on one of the coffins. If you kill him up there the mobs spawns up there and you can't get up to him to hail and get your flag =(

Other than that, kill him really fast, keep him off the walls. We've had Rydda Dar warp up to the roof of the building on us once because we pushed him into a wall. Pets then killed Rydda up on top of the roof and no one got the flags. We did get the loot though, because Rydda was nice enough to summon a mage up to the roof and kill him. We then res'd the mage up there, but the flag mob had despawned before we could CoH anyone up.
