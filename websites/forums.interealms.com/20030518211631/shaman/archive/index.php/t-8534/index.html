Shabura Naarteban
If you can kill much of anything you'd think of as a "raid-level" mob, you can whack Servitor. She's really not qualified to be a Luclin boss ... she clearly cheated on the application exams.

Her resists are minimal. In ~ 10 kills, I don't think I've ever had a slow resisted, and I rarely bother to wait for debuffs on her. Her melee damage is fast, but not impressive like Vindi or anything of that caliber; any tank worth their salt should shake it off well. The proc IS brutal, but its NOT AE ... so don't ping pong her and you are fine.

There is one possible catch. When you get to her room, peek at her before you pull her. Occasionally, she dual-wields (we've had 2 Lurid Daggers once ....). If that is the case, she will quad and do remarkably fast amounts of damage. Its just unpleasant. But ... its also rare, and you probably won't see a dual-wielding Servitor unless you get into hunting her professionally. =) Even still ... I pulled (with slow) the dual-wielding Servitor, and tanked her till she was around 85-80% without heals (was buffed to 4.2k or so, Runed, Bedlamed, Spellshielded for the proc, Torpor up on pull ... and had instructed the clerics NOT to heal me because i expected to die instantly ... lol, I probably could have tanked her the whole fight with healing).

Good luck - she's a fun fight, with nice loot for the RvR.
