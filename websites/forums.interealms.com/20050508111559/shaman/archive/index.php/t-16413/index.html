My understanding of Radiant Cure is just as you have said:
RC 1 removes 9 each curse, poison, disease counters, and has a *chance* to remove one detrimental effect.

RC2 removes 12 of each counter and has 3 chances to remove detrimental effects.

RC3 removes 16 of each counter and has a 6 chances to remove detrimental effects.

I'm not sure what the chance of removing detrimentals is, but for me with RC1, it *seems* around about 50%.
