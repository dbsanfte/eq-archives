Nothenon
Krodah's numbers are right on, as far as barbs are concerned. I've maxed all 6 Cabilis factions, and am indifferent to all. You can turn in bone chips to Trooper Mozo once you're dubious.

I killed 2000 iksar bandit types to raise faction with Legions of Cabilis(troopers) and Brood of Kotiz(necros), then the 8000 bone chips took care of the rest.

If you just want to bank, you can skip the necro faction, and just turn in enough bone chips to get to dubious with the crusaders. FYI, con BOTH of the crusaders in FoB. One of them is on scaled mystic(shaman) faction, and you'll be dubious with them sooner than the other factions in the city. As a shaman, you get a small bonus with the scaled mystics.

If you skip the necro faction, watch out for Grype in east Cabilis. He's the only NPC in E. Cab. that is on Brood of Kotiz faction. He spawns upstairs in the Tink & Babble at night in game.

I did this all before Luclin came out because i wanted another place to train, and buy spells/alchemy ingredients. Not sure if I'd do it now. :/
