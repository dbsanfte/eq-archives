I call that building the cathedral, cuz it has big stained glass windows.

Drenic Garrison is the EB quest guy. My iksar friend handed in the organs WHILE FIGHTING Drenic, having chanter charm him and then invis iksar on turn-in. My ogre was not KOS but could not do quest, so bard friend invised me after i opened up trade window -- success. I think they may have nerfed that.

The quest to gain faction you can do with the WORST katta faction is handing in pyrrohitite stones to a dorf in his own little room...Berk Thurstone, in dorf section. They weight 10 each and do not stack! They are somewhat but not really really uncommon drops off sonic wolves in tenebrous mountains. Good luck. ;)

Do that and enough coterie/seru killing and you can hand in at the mage tower coterie blood or ashes, four at a time, to Avir Sternbla or something on the 2nd or 3rd floor of the mage tower in Katta to get faction and a bit of coin and very cheap gems. A barbarian in the warrior guild will take either the blood or the ashes, not sure which, so experiment.

Also don't remember if you get able to do the following before or after the barbarian.

Corporal Kent in warrior guild gives the shrieking substances quest. He gives you a heavy box to fill with 8 of them(fall off sonic wolves in tenebrous just like the pyrrhotite, but stack and are not so heavy); combine it and it fits into a bag when combined. You get faction and throwing items -- bane damage to vampyre waters--like holy water.

Nathyn Illuminous is the guy in his house by the zone to tenebrous who drops the 100 cha robe. His guards are on his own faction, as are the sonic wolves in his yard. Killing him and his boys won't hurt your faction around the rest of Katta, and in fact gives some positive faction. However, you do your killing outside probably, so if you're KOS, you're right by a bunch of guards including a roamer. These guys are high level guards than the ones down below by the bank that most people kill. Nathyn will completely kick your ass even with a full group, so don't get carried away.

I've done all this stuff over a period of months, handing in literally hundreds of quest items and killing many seru and coterie for days on end, and my ogre shaman still cannot buy or sell or get many quests in Katta, though he could bank and walk through freely from day one absolutely everywhere.
