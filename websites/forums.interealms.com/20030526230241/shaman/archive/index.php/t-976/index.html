This is a very interesting idea to me. After hearing it thrown around several times, I wanted to ask once and for all who actually does this? Lvl 33, I pretty much have been soloing Tizmaks for the past few weeks now. I open w/ Malosi, run a bit and hit em w/ T's Insects just as they approach me and drop an EB and a nuke or two in while I melee them to death. Seems to work just fine. If what I'm hearing is correct though, I should be able to drop an EB and TB on them at the same time? I had no idea you could stack 2 of the same resistance based spells. Not that TB will give me any more of a marginal edge during the fight that the Nuke will, but it's just interesting to hear that one can actually do this.

Right now, I believe it would be a waste to drop a TB which should only do about 65 or so dmg, vs the Nuke that'll do about 145 or so (which hasn't been resisted yet since I generally open w/ Malosi). But I can imagine the benefit something like this will produce once I get scourge.

Perhaps some of you can give me some insight as to which dots you were stacking at lvl 34 to overwhelm your enemy?
Scurg
All Shaman DOT's Stack but not the same spell.

For example, EB and TB both stack as well as Affliction and Scourge but you can not cast EB twice and have it stacked, it will just overwrite it.

At your level I was using EB and TB, I would nuke only for emergencies.
taraddar
Yes all shaman dots stack. I frequently use both bane of nife and ebolt on mobs when soloing. Sure drops the mobs pretty fast that way =)

You can't have multiple people cast the same dot or cast the same one multiple times but as long as it's a different spell it will stack.
