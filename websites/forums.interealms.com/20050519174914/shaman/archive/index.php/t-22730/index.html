Hurrk
MGB Shrink is absolutely useless. Sounds good on the surface perhaps, but would not be used in gameplay. Having that on the list provides SOE a bone to throw us that, like so many other bones, is meaningless but makes it look like they did something for us. It's almost criminal having that on the list.

RATIONALE:

The only people that would possibly need MGB Shrink are raiding guilds. They won't use it for the following reasons:

1. There are not enough shaman to support fluff MBGs like this one. We MGB Tribunal and Focus, we conserve any other MGBs we have for the eventual wipe....and you betcha, raiding guilds do lots of wiping. :) You wipe before the MGB clocks have reset, and now you are doing buff by groups after a wipe. Nope, wont do it.

2. Raiding guilds advocate self-sustainment. Meaning, be able to shrink yourself, invis yourself, breathe underwater on your own, see invisible on your own. Start something like this, and you will start some folks to thinking they no longer have to shrink themselves. Next thing you know, people will be asking you to play musical group in a raid. Not gonna happen. Typically your group role is too important for you to be constantly ducking in and out of groups. Most especially in those raids where we have to group clerics together for bard resists. In those events, shamans and druids become sole group healers for their groups.

3. People are busy on fast paced raids. Mr Warrior the Ogre might want to be shrunk, but the cleric group leader (since the advent of leader xp, guess who is usually group leader in raids) is busy as heck, and really doesn't want your tell asking him to kick someone from group, invite you to group, let you shrink, then after you leave group, get the other person back in group. Heaven forbid anyone be afk for a bio break or something while they (and you, who might just find yourself out of the raid waiting for all this) is finally done.

4. You MGB shrink, then your raid starts pulling mobs.....and people start dieing. They die all night long. A lot, if not most of the people you shrank with your MGB will have died once and lost the shrink, before your MGB resets.

If this isn't removed from the list, since its useless, you can expect to get it. Yay. Incoming bone from the devs. Aren't you happy the devs listened to you?
