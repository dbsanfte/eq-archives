Bozarkia
61-65 decent duo spot, almost never camped on TP at least, is the gamite frogs in POV.

From zone in, turn right and follow the zone wall until you see em perched on a big rock, there are 3 there, once those are dead, follow the water down until the next batch of 3. Always by the time I've finished killing the second set of spawns the first set is repoped.

They are single pullable from the correct angle, its off to the left of the spawn in both camps. Might want to try with a fd class pulling first till you get a feel for it.

3% aa a kill in 65 and can drop parchments. I've duoed this camp alot from 61-65 and several aas, got most of my etherals here. There is named who spawns, a tragilmite frog or something like that, he's like 62, is douable and has so far dropped nothing but a few copper peices for us.

So far I've only duoed it with monks and sks, but I saw a 62 shammy there duoing em with a 63 warrior (neither was uber)

They hit about 400, cannot be rooted, sometimes hard to slow, I usually lead with a malo after getting creamed a few times on early resists. They do enrage, and some run. The ones that run can be snared, the ones who don't cant.

If duoing with a monk, i've found I can stack 2 dots and chain jbb without aggro, with an sk I've loaded up 4 dots and chain jbb. They have a lot of hitpoints, so I lead with ultor and pox and epic. With an sk I add in a bane as well. With horse and canni 5, I'm usually near full mana at the end of fight and leads to constant pulls.

Pov is definately douable. I've done wastelands with a warrior, and outercave with several different classes. Frogs have been my favorite so far, no kos mobs path near, out of the way of trains, decent xp, quiet spot and etheral parchments.
MontyPhank
I generally 2-box with a 64 Shaman and 63 Mage. My favorite spot is the one that Bozarkia mentioned in PoV (frogs and larva).

I park the pets and "slow pull" the larva (see Bozarkia's post on pulling the frogs). Sick the pets on it, malo, and cast rains, dots and finish with bolts.

With tranquility and horses, I am generally net out manawise.

If I get into trouble, it is usually during the "slow pull." So, I run the shaman down the river bank while casting CotH with the Mage. That gives me an instant reset and conveniently breaks the camp for me.

If things get really hairy (like the rare add), then I run the Shaman away and head to the zoneline with the Mage. CotH the Shaman and then CotH the Mage.

It works nicely. I've only had the rare death (mostly when my mind is numbed with the boredom of killing frogs and worms).

BTW, I have also done this camp with a rogue. Not very mana efficient (I am heavily T-Rod dependent without Can5). But it is doable.
