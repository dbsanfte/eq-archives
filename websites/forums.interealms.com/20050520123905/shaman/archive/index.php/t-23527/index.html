Focus effects have been updated - it looks like Focus IV ones will stop at 65, whereas some of the "named" ones have been updated, eg:

Fury of Druzzil
09-07 01:24 Changed Slot 2 from "Limit: Max Level(65)" to "Limit: Max Level(67)"

Marr's Gift
09-07 01:24 Changed Slot 2 from "Limit: Max Level(65)" to "Limit: Max Level(67)"

Discordant Disease
09-07 01:24 Changed Slot 2 from "Limit: Max Level(65)" to "Limit: Max Level(68)"

There have been new focuses added to level 70, including some generic Focus V ones.

Shaman spell changes:

* Vindictive spirit has been mentioned on another thread (recourse added)
* Spirit Veil now has the "invis" icon and is now self only and beneficial. No mention of invisibility under the description, but they've been tinkering with other invis spells of late as well. The duration of 10 minutes, plus the self-only bit makes it look very much like a fixed duration invis.
* Our root PH spell has been changed to "Shroud of Erana" - it's a 15 minute self buff with a recourse sounding spell next to it, Shroud of Erana Parry. This was our Malos PH, which now does nothing at all. I'd suggest we'll be getting a similar thing to that druid defensive type spell.

Then after some small wording changes to other spells and some bits that look like the "Origin" AA spells, there are some new bits.

Some of them have "spirit", "prophet" or "heyokah" in them, but I'm not sure what they're for - could be AAs, I guess, as they don't currently have classes or levels associated with them. Some of them are listed as "focus effects", which is pretty straightforward, but others are mysterious.

For example,

Krekk's Presence
Slot Description
12: Mitigate Melee Damage by 10%, 750 total
Mana: 0 Casting Time: 0
Recast Time: 0 Fizzle Time: 0
Location: Any Time of Day: Any
AE Range 60 Interruptable: Yes
Short Buff Box: No Target Type: Group v2
Spell Type: Beneficial Category: Unknown(-99)
Source: Test 09/07

Classes: None
Duration: 1 min
Autocast: Krekk's Presence Recourse

Cast on you: The spirit of Krekk guards you.
Cast on other: Someone is guarded by the spirit of Krekk.
Wears off: The spirit of Krekk fades.

The recourse is a self only buff that increases your chance of resisting a spell by 15% for a minute - and there's a similar set of spells with upgraded stats (Kreljnok's Fury).

And this one:

Prophet's Gift of the Ruchu
Slot Description
10: Critical Damage Mob(70)
11: Chance to Critical Hit(50)
12: Increase Hitpoints v2 by 400 per tick

Mana: 0 Casting Time: 0
Recast Time: 0 Fizzle Time: 0
Location: Any Time of Day: Any
AE Range 60 Interruptable: Yes
Short Buff Box: No Target Type: Group v1
Spell Type: Category: Unknown()
Source: Test 09/07

Classes: None
Duration: 1 min

Cast on you: The spirit of the Ruchu strengthens you.
Cast on other: Someone is strengthend by the spirit of the Ruchu.
Wears off: The spirit of the Ruchu fades.

Note that both of these are group spells - and the duration is reminiscent of melee class disciplines.

There's also a somewhat amusing spell been added:

Grace of the Order Strike
Slot Description
1: Decrease HP when cast by 250
2: Decrease Chance to Avoid Melee by 7%
(duration, 3 ticks)

Grace of the Order Parry
Slot Description
6: Increase Movement by 125%

Debuff the mob then run far, far away! :p

I doubt it's a shaman spell (name suggests a proc of some sort), but what melee class would want to hit and then leg it?

After that there are some new cleave type effects, then bits that look like item clickies (mana pool enhancers etc).

To sum up - even at this stage, there are still some quite big changes being made. The addition of a defensive type spell for us looks very promising IMO.... :)
