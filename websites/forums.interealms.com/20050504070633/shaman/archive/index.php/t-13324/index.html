Grixx
I 3-box a 63 SK (my main), 65 Shaman, and 60 Druid and it's proven to be very effective thus far in Tier 1 and 2 planes. My best spots thus far have been (not surprisingly) the Golems when open, or the larvae and undead in PoV. I've also done BoT stormriders and the High Guardians in PoJ. I've done 10-12 of the WW dragons just for kicks (the dispells and AE get to be interesting hehe). As far as tactics overall, I set up hotkey macros so that I'd only have to press one key to Malo and Slow the mob, about 5 seconds after I have SK engage, to make sure he has agro. I then send in dog dog for the attack, with the SK pet alongside. Thus far, I've primarily used the druid as primarly healer and for regen, DS, and free Epic/ES Vamb DoTs. I'll occasionally have the shaman toss on a DoT, but only if mana is plentiful. Before going out on a hunt, I grab KEI from someone for all 3 toons, because I've found myself being a tad lazy on the Cann4 - mainly due to the fact that I have no Torpor or Quiescence.

The combo has proven pretty effective - plenty of healing, evac, snare, slow, etc. The only downside I've noticed thusfar is DPS. The only way I've thought of maybe improving is to have the druid dire charm something, but I really don't want to put the energy or time into the Druid - he's pretty much where I want him to stay. Throwing more DoTs on does help a little - but then I find that my chain pulling suffers - due to the fact that mana is too low. I try to keep all chars at 80% mana or better at all times - in case of emergency.

I would like to find some challenging hunts (exploration, fun, not necessarily uber exp) to take my trio on - maybe deep DN or something - and would welcome any suggestions. :)

Thanks!
