Detton
At level 34, and the 39 pet, a decently buffed dogdog will be able to hit a mob often enough to crank out a vaguely respectable amount of damage. Make sure hes facing the back of a mob.

Buy mage toys. At least a belt, and the muzzle. The strength from the belt, and the haste the muzzle gives, makes a big difference. Not to mention the 250-500 extra hp (depending on which if you get the belt, or girdle) actually makes him somewhat beefy.

With toys (The fists of ix mage weapon procs for our level 34 pet, but other weapons dont) the pet is actually pretty good at level 34/39/44.

It gets harder and harder for dogdog to hit anything reliably enough at the higher levels. If it COULD hit, it would put out something resembling DPS.

You'll like the pet at 34, even without toys. Toys make it alot better, though. ALWAYS buff str (for atk), tailsman, health (for the few hits you'll want it to tank to hold agro while you dot/slow), and quickness. If you have the mage-summoned fists, buff dex too. If you have the full gear setup, you might as well throw in agility and the AC buff. Trust me - He can tank pretty well in that situation at that level.

Quad attacks by default for 55 a hit, alternates kick and bash, you get the idea. This pet is also a serious undercon and with just Augmentation of Death it averages 35+ DPS on golems in PoV. Add in mask, fists/swords and belt + focus/diaku/FA and that average goes up signifigantly.

Our pet, on the other hand, is a companion summoned from the spirit world to assist us in our travels and adventures. From an RP standpoint it was never meant to be a killing machine and simply will not be superior to an int caster's undead pet that used to destroy level 50 warriors pre-kunark and regularly went on rampages through Sol B (prior to the change to pet aggro). At least it won't be "out-of-the-box".

Now if you're asking if our pets can match the DPS of the 49 nec/64 sk pet, well yes. A dual weilding dogdog with haste mask, belt, 2 fists/swords and SLTW/Focus/Diaku/FA can indeed reach 35+ DPS on golems in PoV as well. It'll even exceed the SK pet's HPs at this point, though still inferior in dmg mitigation and avoidance. It just comes with the costly price of all that gear and buffage.
