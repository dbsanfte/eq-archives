That's all I can think of off the top of my head. And this is just Arch, not class.

If you tend to be a tanker, I would say CA and CS are actually better than ND. In a place like the planes, the complete dodging of an extra 300+ attack, and the decrease of 30 damage out of 300 every swing is invaluable. If you are a raid style, ND is king to work against those AE spells and such. CF is eye candy, and does increase DPS a bit since crits can do more than your normal max damage, not to mention you'll increase the number of bow crits you get. *can't remember ever breaking 90 with my 10 damage weapon, but was doing up to 130+ with CF1 last night* In all, the Healing Gift might occasionally mean something, but I think the others will help more in the long run. If a 400+ heal would save someone's life just long enough for that CH to go off, who cares if you healed 800? CH would have doen that anyway. And rangers will never be designated healers no matter what. Might be more helpful as a solo'er, but in all honestly you can get just as good exp in the planes in groups than you can solo.

I don't use spells anywhere NEAR enough to warrent spending AA's on them, nor do I really have the wisdom to use a lot of nukes anyway. Haven't had the need to tank very much, so I'm gonna work on my HP and damage output mostly.
