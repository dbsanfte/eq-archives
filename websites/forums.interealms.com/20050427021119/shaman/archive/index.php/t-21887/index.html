It sounds like you may have picked the wrong place to setup. My shaman soloed on the Oceancrasher fine when he was around that level, although I'd probably pick Lady Di or Dandulak if they are available.

Run down the dock onto the ship and hang a right. Setup on the wall near the barrels/ballistae (I can't remember which it is on that ship) where nothing should agro on you. Just make sure the captain is not spawned, since I think he spawns near there. Summon pet and give it HoS (or hp buff and Str buff), Alacrity (or similar haste) and two weapons (Shivs are good for this). Make sure to keep HoS (or other hp buff), Sta buff, SoW (if you don't have Run3 AA) and Regrowth (or similar long term HoT) on yourself.

Pull the mobs closest to you first. I usually start at the bow and work my way to the stern, using slow to pull. Use Turgurs if you don't have a spell haste item, otherwise Torgors is fine. If it is resisted, just sick dogdog on him, wait for him to establish agro and step back a bit. You should have more than enough hit points to handle this once or twice. When slow lands move back in to melee range and maneouvre yourself so you are opposite dogdog to maximise his DPS. Unless you have an uber weapon, do not turn on autoattack as you will lose the mana regen benefits of having a mount. After it is slowed, you should have few problems timing both an E-Both and an Envenomed Breath between swings. You'll need to reapply the DoTs once. If hit points are getting low, use Stoicism to heal yourself. Keep a root memmed because these guys run on low health. After it dies, Canni and Stoicism yourself back up and you are GTG again. If things do get out of hand, let dogdog be the speedbump and run for the Gunthak/Dulak zone line. It is a bit of a run but, with SoW or Run3 and 30%+ health, you should make it.

I've soloed levels 51-52 and 6 AAs while I was in Dulak, mostly at the fence but often on the boats. Once I got the hang of the zone, the only time I ever got adds was when either I was trained or I screwed up. My gear was mostly bazaar level, although I did have a Fine Antique Ring (Mana Preservation II), the PoK Spell Haste II mask, Canni II and Stoicism. I also had 200 Defense from getting beaten up by Erandi Nataasu (the guy outside the entrance to the warrens in Paineel).

This is a great zone for Shaman at that level. The loot is excellent, the XP even better now it is a hotspot and, with the recent addition of the PoK portal to Gunthak, there is no excuse for a shaman to not go there. If you have a mount, you can do fine with just self buffs. In your case, Sudancia, with the addition a monk you should be tearing this boat apart! Good luck although I don't think you'll need it!

I went onto Oceancrasher with some lower level guildies over the weekend. I was level 58 and they were 52 and 48, and found they drew a huge agro radius. Rather than setting up quietly and single pulling, I found it was an exercise in crowd control, root parking and off tanking. Nothing we couldn't handle with Virtue and Turgurs but a surprise nevertheless.

If you are going there with lower level friends, I'd recommend clearing off the dock first and getting higher level characters to pull to there with low agro spells or, if high enough, proximity agro. Turgurs and/or Torgors work fine for this. When an area on the ship is cleared, move the party up to there. When mobs run, root them or tell the lower level characters NOT to follow it. Otherwise, you'll get more adds then a math test :-).
