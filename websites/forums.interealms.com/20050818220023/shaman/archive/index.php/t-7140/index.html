Giladari
I've been levelling thru the 40s in Crystal Caverns. The geonid spawn (if you can find it uncamped) is ideal for soloing since they don't aggro you unless you're KoS and it takes a great many of them (as in killing them for 6+ levels) before you ever hit KoS. I've gone afk medding up sitting in front of one many times. Unless some twit from spiders or geonids can't handle his pull and trains you running to zone out, its perfectly safe.

7 (or 8?) mobs going up a tunnel to zone out spaced so that all but 1 mob will get you a single mob if you pull with a spell. (I usually pull with slow or my best DoT if I'm buffed from a trip to the Nexus)

The other option is Terror Tunnel in Crystal Caverns. Tunnel is on an approximately 13.5 min spawn. Starts with 2 mobs at the entrance (root one so you can break the spawn up) then a mob mid tunnel and one at the end of the tunnel just before the room of about 8 that I think you can single pull relatively safely. Even without any form of clarity, this should be a nice little spawn for you and it also has the benefit of being trainfree, often open and totally safe to med at the entrance of the tunnel (just sit far enough in that the spiders from the next tunnel don't aggro you)

CC also has the benefit of a wonderful XP bonus too.

Hope it helps!
