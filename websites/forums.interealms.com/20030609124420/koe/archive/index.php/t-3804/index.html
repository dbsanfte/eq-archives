Don't EVER say you don't need an item with INT on it. The reason being is sometimes you can increase your INT on part of your body and strip it off another for a better +mana/resists/stats/etc piece.

As far as leggings it would also depend on your budget and what you are interested in. Some suggestions:

Netted Kelp Leggings: nice stats including +6int and svF, drops in SG
Hand Woven Leggings: AC8 with +10svF, random of sarnaks in LoIO
Loam Encrusted Leggings: +2int with other nice stats and i think svF, drops in The Hole
Silversilk Leggings: AC4 +4agi +15hps if I remember correctly, been a long time since i camped Magi =P
