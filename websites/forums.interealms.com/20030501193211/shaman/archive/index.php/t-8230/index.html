Csin Csity
Resist potions do NOT stack with with resist spells. I believe they do stack with CoS/CoW, but most people cast other spells that DO stack with CoW/CoS and overwrite.

I used Greater Potions of Cold to help out with a monky lavastorm epic fight once, since we were short a few key classes they helped out.

DS potions DO stack with mage DS's..and a few others. While it's not mega-uber, it can make a difference and the tanks like being able to DS themselves.

Of course calimony is very useful for keeping my favorite people (me) alive.

If you are going to make/bring potions to a raid, make sure that you clear it with the RL before you do. Get your reimbursement paid up from the guildbank/raid-dues or don't bring em. Clerics get paid for 'dots..so should you. If your RL doesn't seem responsive, ask the MT. One tank I knew had cash coming out the wazoo from good drops. He would buy DS potions and calimony's before the raid..use only a few of em, and use the rest for soloing..god I miss him.

Good luck!

Jeradin
DS is pretty damn sweet potion for raiding.

It stacks with Mage DS, and will stack with Druids DS, also stacks with Chardok ring effect, Bramblecoat and Bard DS.

It does not stack with rangers Call of rathe/earth line, Coldain ring and Fiery might.


Mages 61 DS = 45 points.
Druids 63 DS = 40 points.
Shaman potion = 33points on a level 65 toon.

Solo I can get 61 DS going on myself, Potion + chardok ring + Bramblecoat.

Also, our posion risist potion stacks with group PR and DMF, great for trak raids, and we used FR resists potions back in the day for Taledor raids.
