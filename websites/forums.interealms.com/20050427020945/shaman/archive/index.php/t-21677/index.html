I got the spell and did a bit of charming in Nedaria, near the JPF entrance. It was interesting experience as I have never had a pet class before. However, the XP was very slow, especially as charm usually broke at just the wrong moment.

In the end I went back to Mistmoore and ground out the XP to 34 and now have a pet.

Incidently, I got the pet spell when my conjuration skill was about 75 - near impossible to cast it! Had to spend a morning hitting summon food/drink to get the skill up to 130 before it became reliable enough to be of any use.

Psycotika
I used charm to solo the basilisks is JPF for a little while. just malise one (not the Genitors) charm and have it /pet guard far enough away from you so you can charm again when it breaks. pull another one, slow it and root it next to your pet. always have it rooted so if charm breaks you dont have to deal with two mobs. cast a dot on it so your pet wont take a big chunk of your exp. your pet should be able to take about 2 mobs before you its almsot dead. just wait for charm to break and root/rot it.

rinse and repeat. hope that helps
