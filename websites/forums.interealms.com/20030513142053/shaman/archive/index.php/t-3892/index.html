Grippa
I'm sure you can find more detailed info if you use Search, but here's a quick rundown:

Lord Ghiosk: Spawns from a Black Reaver, is roughly as hard as a BR. With mid range gear and a good puller, a single group around level 55 can kill Black Reavers.

Neh Ashiir - Same toughness as Ghoisk, maybe a bit harder. Again spawns from a BR, but because of location, you might need a couple of groups.

High Scale Kirn - The hardest to get to - A single uber-group can get to him the conventional way, it would probably take 3 groups of 50-55 people in mid range gear. Kirn himself is about as easy as Neh. There are alternative strategies for getting to Kirn, search and ye shall find :)

The Iksar Child - Spawns from golems in Fear - these take a full Fear raid to kill, I honestly can't remember how hard they are as it was many levels ago when I got my tear.

Lord Rak - Since the caster AI changes I think he's a bit less extreme. We killed him very recently (since the patch) using 3 mage pets, one of the pets went linkdead during the fight (well its master did), and we still had no problems.
