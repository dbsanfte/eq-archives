The cap is still 15, and it isn't just for "flowing thought." It also takes in to account ANY worn mana regen effect. The effect from the aten ha ra turn in neck is called Blessing of Vah Kerrath, and gives you 8 mana and health regen, as well as a DS. That 8 mana regen counts towards the cap of 15, even though the effect is not "flowing though VIII". Buffs from items, like the effect "Lunar Whispers" from acrylia burrower talisman, do NOT count towards the cap, because it is a buff and not innate worn regen. Mental Clarity, also, does NOT count towards this cap.
