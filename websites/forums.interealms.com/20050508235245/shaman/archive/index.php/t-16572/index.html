Yodelroy
DPS isn't needed in LDoN it's wanted. Simply because a good DPS group allows you to complete a mission in 20-40 minutes, maximizing the AP farming potential. Most people want the maximum amount of APs per day, they don't care about 'comfortable' playing styles. So we pull 4-5 mobs at once.

An ideal group of uber 65s IMO for doing undead LDoN zone in 20 minutes is something like: bard, sk, monk, nec, nec, pal. A few secondary and tertiary healers can easily keep up with pulls of 3-5 and increase the DPS by far over a primary healer. Wave heals + nec zevfeer + nec shadowbond x2 + bard regen song + self-healing melees (monk mend, sk lifetap, pal elixir) can keep everyone going nonstop easily.

The mobs are so easy to tank once AE slowed, and die so quickly (30-40seconds) that single target slow is a total waste of mana (UNLESS you pull with it). By the time slow lands, the mob is at 80% and dies 25 seconds later. So instead of target slowing, just AE slow and toss quiescence all over and burn.

This is all just one way of playing, the type suited to max/min players who are AP farming. You can enjoy LDoN equally using standard tactics but you'll get less APs. Or you can try hard mission, whch nets more xp+fun, and you cannot blitz through it.
