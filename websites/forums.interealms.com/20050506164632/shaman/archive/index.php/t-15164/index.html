If you look threw the list you can see that there are a variety of Mana pres type items ranging up to 40% but with a variety of differing limitations.

Sanguine Preservation- is 40% mana pres but it's for self buffs only which makes it fairly useless for most.

Pernicious Focus is 30% mana pres on dots which is probably the best actually useful focus.

At 25% you see a variety of standard affliction and pet summoning mana pres items, the single nuke focus effects for elemental gloves.

At 20% you finnally start seeing some more general mana pres items.

Conservation of Xegony is the general 20% mana pres focus effect but it hasn't been found on any item in game that I know of.
Preservation of Solusek is detrimental and
Preservation of Mithaniel is benificial 20% mana pres
Both focus effects have been found on items in PoTime.
There are also a couple VT focus effects and all the ornate single spell versions also at 20%.

Past that I'll leave it up to you to look at the other ones.

PS your necklace is 15% mana pres. Standard MP4 items are just 10%.
