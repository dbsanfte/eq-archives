Thordan
I did Mental clarity 3 first and then went to Cann 5 right away. I think Cann 5 is the best skill we get overall so that was a no brainer for me. After that I did MGB because I spend 90% of my time raiding and it was pretty nice to have. Then I did Quick Buff 1 and 2, and after that I did Rabid Bear.

It just depends on what you do most of the time. If you spend a ton of time raiding then get MGB. I also like Spell Casting Mastery but it really isn't needed since I almost NEVER run out of mana with Cann 5. Rabid Bear was cool just to have as a toy but the points could have been spent better in other places, I got it just because I wanted my class title.

If you solo alot the quick buff for torpor, channeling focus for the interrupts when slowing and torporing yourself, and spell casting reinforcement for extending torpor would be the way to go. For raiding MGB and spell casting reinforcement are prolly your best bets. Personally I am going for spell casting reinforcement right now.
