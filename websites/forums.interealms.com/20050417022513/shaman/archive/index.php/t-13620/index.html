Dulak is pretty nice mid 40's. Right after you zone in, invis up and get by the mobs in the "passage". Those sometimes come in pairs. Once out in the open area, you might have to fight one without your pet, but you can pretty much single pull in the big open area all you want.

If there aren't enough of those, the passage between the buildings on the right has one single pullable outside one of the buildings and if you stay in themiddle of the passage you won't aggro anything in the buildings. At 48 my enchanter was able to single pull from the buildings using slow. I hear shaman can too, but I don't know at what level. Further down that passage hang a right and go up against the stone wall. No mobs wander there(though they do close by) and if you dont allow runners you shouldn't get adds.
