Landragoran Heronwing
Affliction Efficiency is really nice to have. The item I wear is the shield from Grummus. It's roughly 25 AC, 85 hps/mana +7 wis int stam, +8 resists. The AC is a bit low for a shield, but the hps are very decent and the effect of AEIV simply can't be exaggerated. Especially on raids where I do a LOT of Malo/Malosini/Turgurs all the freaking time. Saving 33% mana per spell helps more than you would expect. Stacking that with my SCM3 ability and specialization in alteration means I'm saving around 58% mana per spell if it's a debuff, 44% mana per spell if it's a DoT.

I'll deal with the lowish AC, always another slot I can make up for that in. One 50 AC ranged item will do the trick nicely. I want the Torch of Judgement in that slot. But in the mean time, there are still lots of items to upgrade, and I'm sure someone with truly uber gear would make the same choice as I have. Btw, check my magelo if you wanna know what shield I am referring to.

Moral of the story: AC/hps are nice, but saving mana is even nicer.
