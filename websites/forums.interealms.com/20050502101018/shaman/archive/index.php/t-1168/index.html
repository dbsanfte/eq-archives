Straif
This quest is a bit harder to do for the good races so here is what you need to do:

1. Buy some Red wine, I used 40 bottles - could take less, could take more for you. To do this quest hand him 4 bottles then have a friend invis you...hit trade, boom: faction.

2. Buy 5 bags of Explorer knives from the troll in Rathe Mtn.

3. Get a druid to help you with the next part. Have the druid cast Harmony on the trooper, stand as far away as you can from the trooper, yet still be able to trade with him. Hand him 4 stacks of 4 daggers. Druid friend of yours casts invis on you, then hit trade. (have him re-harmony every now and then)

4. When you have faction enough with him just start handing in the daggers 16 at a time.

5. Enjoy your new-found scally friends.

6. Next step is to do the proper quests to get necro faction.

A little note about the major place of the first three expansions that iksar need faction with, here. I have a 45 iksar monk twink who has never killed anything that would give or take away any freeport faction.

Two days ago I asked in our /market channel on my server if anyone was doing a raid on Sir Lucan, the guy for the pally soulfire quests who gives a huge boost to North Freeport faction and gigantic negative hit to West/East Freeport faction and guards.

A few people said, heck, let's kill Lucan, if he's up. (8-hour timer) So with like 4 people, we killed him -- my 45 monk, a 60 rogue, and two paladins. Nice faction boost, but still KOS in NFP to guards, though not to banker. I did assist on someone who was not KOS to find out about banker's con.

Yesterday I bumped into a bunch of strangers who were going to kill Sir Lucan again, and I asked to tag along just to land a faction hit. So now after only two Lucan killings and about a half hour of total work, I can walk through North Freeport just fine.

Further, with market channels, you don't even really need Freeport faction, or Faydark faction, wherever your server's market is, nearly so much at all.
