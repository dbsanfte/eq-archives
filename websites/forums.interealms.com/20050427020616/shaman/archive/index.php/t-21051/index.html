Goldark
Not a gear thread, but I'd suggest replacing bracers (e.g. windriders trinket) and gloves (e.g. kylong) for some more hp. Haste isn't doing much for you at your level :)

You should do fine though with what you have. If you've got GoD, I'd suggest spending tribute on BA 4 and spell haste. BA4 for saryrn and ultor, and spell haste so you can get that malos/slow combo in quick, and also faster heals if it goes a bit bad.

Lastly, if you take some ivu potions and move very carefully, you can get to the courtyard area past the castle entrance. Mobs there are a bit tougher, and the rabids in that spot drop the rather tasty warbeads (FT 2, sell very nicely, bit short on hp for my liking though - but I do want some for my bard!). I did it once when I got bored fighting near the zi, haven't tried since so maybe it was a fluke but I didn't aggro anything (note that a few things DO see invis to undead though)

Probably goes without saying, but if you're not friendly with chardok CRs here are really really nasty. Always carry some EEs and pearls in case someone else needs a hand, one day they may repay the favour.
