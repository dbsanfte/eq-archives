Shabby
At 35, you will want to be grouped ( unless you try soloing some cactus).

Groups (lv. 30-40) tend to form up in three places, at least on Torv. :

1) "the ledge", which is the side of the zone near the FM zone ( I forget if it is north wall or south wall),

2) the "NW/SW corner" (again forget which direction is north, hehe, but it is the corner away from outpost and away from WW zone side), and

3) "Skyfire Ramp area".

You will not have to worry about the DE guards, being a evil type, but your group mates may.

I always prefered grouping in the corner group or by Skyfire. Pretty easy to get a group there, very popular zone. Loot is so-so.

Save any braids/tails you get from sarnak Bezerkers because evil types turn them in 4 at a time to the Skelly felkow at the outpost drilling the undead marines. Once you get your faction to amiable by doing this quest ( turn in about 75 braids?), then you give a jade (storebought) to the foreman fellow and get a hammer that procs a port to OT at lv. 50.

When in OT groups, try not to be put into the position of being the main healer in a chain pulling group, will only frustrate you :)


Have fun!

there are different 'sets' of mobs that you should know:

around lvl 30, 1200ish hp:

stoneleer cocks
sarnak berzerkers
thorny succulents
sabretooth tigresses

around lvl 35, 1500ish hp:

stonegazer cocks
sarnak knights
spiked succulents

and then there are the lvl 40ish tigers, much too hard.

pull with slow and put scourge+ebreath on them and melee them down....it's a little hard, but just keep it up until you're lvl 39 with a great new slow and dot (togor's and vos). then you can try the lvl 35ish mobs (solo at least)
