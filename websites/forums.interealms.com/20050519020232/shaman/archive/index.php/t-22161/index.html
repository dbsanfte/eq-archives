We are all aware of this horrific template that has been hung on the priest classes of Norrath. In an earlier post, I took a shot at explaining why, in my opinion, The Holy Percentages (tm) were accurate, fair and balanced at one point in time.

Now, I'm gonna take a shot at defining where they should be now. I'll try to keep the math as easy to follow as I can, shouldn't be too bad really.

Let's start by setting a baseline encounter from which to draw conclusions about the way The Holy Percentages (tm) should be set for the new encounters.

Our encounter will take 35 seconds from first swing to "You gain party experience!". In this time, our mob will do 35k damage. This mob is "partially" Slowable, and we'll assume that Turgur's will have a value of 40% Slow given the "partial" mitigation level.

Given this, our Cleric friend will need to heal all 35k damage. The mob is not Slowed at all, but as the Healing gods of Norrath the Clerics don't care. They Heal the damage and move on.

But now our Shaman bretheren attempt to play Healer. We all know about the delay in getting a mob Slowed. It takes 10 seconds. Our tank has taken 10k damage so far. He may or may not live thru the experience. *shrug* Not important to the math. (Yea, it's pretty important to the tank, but math doesn't care ;) ).

Now, mob is Slowed (Yay!) and over the remaining 25 seconds of the fight will only do 60% of the damage he normally would have done. Which is to say, the mob is still gonna do 15k points of damage. Once again, the tank may or may not survive, but the math doesn't care.

So for the course of the entire fight, a Shaman needs the ability to Heal 25k points of damage. I grant you, 10k points of damage less than a Cleric needs to Heal is a HUGE amount. But the significance lies in the ratio.

Seventy one percent. For that encounter, the Slowed mob still did 71% of the damage it did unSlowed. So, for a Shaman to heal, we need to have Heal spells that represent 71% of the power a Cleric would bring.

Look at that number one more time.

Seventy one percent.

That seems a bit different than 47%.

The Holy Percentages (tm) are wrong. Dead wrong. They need to be tossed on the midden heap and revamped.

Try not to fall out of your chairs when you see this.

Assuming Slow doesn't mitigate at all, during the time we are casting Slow our tank still takes his 10k points of damage. But, thereafter, the mob only does an additional 6250 points of damage, for a total fight damage of 16250.

Anyone want to know what that works out to compared to a Cleric?

46.43%
