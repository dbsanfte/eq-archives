Slurm
The trick to it is to use mor hps and less mana... even without the fungi you regain hps faster than mana -thats what makes canni useful.

Try pulling with slow, meleeing, and tossing in a dot.

When you have downtime and need to med up, watch for the tick (when you see yor man/hp go up) and time your cannibalization to happen between the pulses. With a little practice you can be sitting when the tick happens to get the sitting med/heal rate, stand, cast canni, and sit down in time to get the sitting med/heal rate again. Its very effective, but keeps you busy -having played a bard it should be easy for you.

Soon you will have a pet. Shaman pets are not capable of tanking for you, but are more of a longterm dot.
