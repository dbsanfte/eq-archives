Go raid the plane of hate. Shaman bracers drop from both the golems, as well as the treasure chests. 9ac 5wis is a good upgrade to totemic.
If you want to buy something, or hunt for it yourself, go to Skyshrine and kill the Sentry mobs near the zone (Don't kill the 2 sentries AT the zone. They never drop anything). Sentry bracers are 9ac 2wis 2int 15hp 15hp 5dr 5pr 2mr non lore tradable. I think those numbers are right. If not, they are damn close.
Also, dragonhide bracers from WW have stats like 11ac 8wis 6cha or something similar in stats. Better quality bracers from WW have bigger numbers on them. You can probably buy one for about 3-4k.
