What can I do with it?

Viewport lets you set what part of the screen the game view itself is shown in. This way you can put all of your ui boxes around the outside and not block any of your view. I can't stand playing without it.

Example

/viewport 0 0 800 600

Would show all of your view in the area of the screen from (0,0) to (800,600). Everything else your free to setup all the windows you need.
shamuofxev
viewport gives you the "picture" in a black frame. Basically you can arrange all your other windows around it, so nothing overlaps your game info. I like it in situations where I have to manual target using the mouse.
The format is /viewport (x-offset) (y-offset) (x-pixels) (y-pixels)
For example, if you're running 1280x1024 resolution on your monitor, /viewport 120 0 1024 768 gives you a centered 1024x768 game window, with enough space to put your action, buff, song, hotbutton windows left, your target, group, self , spells windows right, and two large chat windows underneath.
Similar, if you're running 1024x768 base resolution, try /viewport 100 0 800 600
