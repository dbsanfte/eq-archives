Got bored today and decided to parse the new 67 pet and see if it is in anyway different from the 61 pet.

I went to Wakening and found my old friend the Mist Panther (level 60 mob that hits for very little) and decided to use him for a little test.

I summoned each dog and buffed him up with Swift like the Wind, Ferine Avatar, Focus of Soul and Spirit of Might (great buff by the way). I also gave the dogs each 1 piercing weapon and 1 club.

Well here are the results.

61 weap
35.71 DPS
51.8% hit
Average 44

67 weap
40.14 DPS
52.8% hit
Average 46


Well as you can see there is a little bit of an increase in damage but it is not exactly spectacular and could easily be the result of test error as this is just one mob.


For kicks I also did a 61 pet with the same buffs no weapons.

61 no Weap
23.87 DPS
52% hit
Average 45

You don't apply the 5% to the max hit. You only apply the 5% damage modifier to the pet's Damage Interval and that's why 5% Damage Modifier doesn't equal 5% more damage(DPS).

The buffs that increase Damage Modifiers don't modify the Damage Bonus.
