I put this quest off a bit myself, but when all was said and done it turned out to be very worthwhile. Looking back now i wish that i had done it sooner.

My advice is do the quest, or buy the spell (The final spell is not no drop) as soon as you can.

The quest was really pretty easy. Its even MQable if you have a hard time with any of the pieces. The only no drop components were the bones from KC, and the ground spawn diamonds in TD are ALOT easier than doing it the long way around.

You will be very happy when you can eat your health down at a much faster rate.

Do you really want to keep pressing that Can1 button again and again more often than you do now? It just gets worse as you get bigger and have more HP. Can2 is a great spell for its level range.

My advice is don't wait for Can3, get Can2 as soon as you can use it. The whole Cannibalize line just keeps getting better as you get bigger!
