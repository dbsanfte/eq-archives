Pick up groups in PoV is the way to go, especially if you want to get out and meet new people.

For variety:

Shrooms in FG are ok if you can get them broken because you'll have a hard time getting singles at 59. They spend a lot of their time casting disease based spells so they hardly do damage if your DR is 140ish buffed. A small percentage of them run, so keep root up.

Katta used to be good exp, but I thought that "city" exp was nerfed a while back. It's about as mind numbing as PoJ though.

You can do Sol B LDC's, the exp is ok but still slow.

If I were you though, I'd just grind it out in PoV pickup groups. It's the least painful way to go.

One method to get through the evil that is 58 and 59? LDoN. I've been in Everfrost, NRo, and BB so far. Took 2 adventures to finish 58, and 2 more to get me a full yellow into 59 this weekend.

Ok, I realize not everyone will be able to do this, but it is nonetheless a viable alternative to the Planes. At 58 you can (obviously) group with 65's and be actually effective.

The comment I heard more than once from some 65 Clerics in groups was "OMG this is sooo much easier on me with you slowing..." It really isn't that much different than any other LDoN. Yes, there are more resists but not too bad...DogDog is kinda worthless other than as a DoT with legs (what else is new)...Some may have a harder time finding a group on different servers, etc, etc.

My point is, for xp and some verra nice loot, give it a shot. If nothing else, try it on weekday evenings when less people are playing...It might get you a foot in the door, so to speak with some of the 65 crowd.
