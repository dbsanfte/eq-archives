I know there are a number of people of various classes that want to go to Fear for a variety of reasons, ranging from obtaining class armor to Epic Quest Mobs to just wanting to go there for the first time.

Assembly point: Outside Fear Portal in Feerott (please be there 20 min in advance to facilitate final group placements).

Open to: Keepers of the Elements, Petitioners, and Friends level 46+

Size: 7 groups + reserve list

Objective: Kill them all

Min. requirements: Adequate number of healers. 2 enchanters.

Additional requirements: Please think of your clerics and chanters and come with 10 peridots. Melee who may have to leave early SHOULD think about purchasing a gate potion in advance.

General: this is intended to be a nice run into Fear to clear the Plane. I would like to see some of our people get some armor upgrades (seems we've been hitting Hate a lot more) and maybe even kill some epic mobs. I am putting an initial cap of 7 groups in place and reserving (initially) 5 for KOE members. I know we haven't been here in a while, and I have a feeling we could have a very strong turnout. We will be making a reserve list, and changing people as needed (nice thing about Fear: it doesn't require a long walk or port to get there).

Depending on force composition, we may try some AOEing on the mobs to see how that works (MAYBE!!!). If you wish to attend, please sign below. Additional info will be posted as avail.
