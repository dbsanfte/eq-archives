The reason ENC slow seems to stick more is the religious use of Tash. It actually does not stick more then the SHM. Slow is still based on MR checks. CHA effecting slow spells is just an old sarcastic joke that went around the SHM community in response for the serious CHA buffs we get. In all seriousness, the reason we initially got such good CHA buffs was because we used to be a charm class. Verant being who they were just kept rehashing old ideas without any background checking when it came to spell upgrades after the level cap was set higher.

Turgur's used to have a built in resistance modifier which allowed it to land more often. I do not think that value exists anymore, but was there when the spell first went live.

Having played both SHM and ENC extensively, I can say that my ugly ass Troll sees resists about the same as my vain chanter. What mobs you will see more resists on are Warrior class mobs, as they have a higher MR from class bonus. Having magic based spells bounce a little too much is a good sign that you are dealing with a warrior.
