This thread reminded me that I've been "planning" on going and getting the HS master key for ages, and never got around to it. So last night, I took my ranger twink and myself back to Charasis and started carving away.

I was surprised to find how many mobs in there are still light blue at lvl 70. Crawl time was about 5 hours, from initial ZI to final key combine. Sathir wasn't up, so I didn't get a chance to see if I could solo/duo her, but otherwise, it was a fun evening. Nice way to blow off some steam.

And btw, there's a nice walkthrough for the key quest on EQExpert (though I'm not a fan of that site).
