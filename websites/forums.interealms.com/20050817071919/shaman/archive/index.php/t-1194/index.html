In general non defensive warriors don't tank those mobs when they are unslowed. The other problem is that under level 60 warriors don't taunt HoT mobs worth a damn.

We have two warriors, level 58 and 59. Our primary tank tends to be a level 60 SK unless we know the mob is unslowable. An SK can outtaunt tash easily, of course he gets the piss healed out of him until the slow lands.

So our strategy with or without Malo goes like this:

1. Cast Malo (you skip this step, no Malo).
2. Enchanter casts tash.
2. Chain cast Malosini until it sticks.
3. Then cast slow.

Between the enchanter and I casting slow the mob gets slowed pretty quickly.

I think the real key is having a class that can taunt, my regular group has never had a level 60 warrior to work with, so I can't comment on their ability to taunt mobs off of debuffers.

With that strategy it has been quite easy to do HoT with two groups. I'm not talking about gimp camping the entryway either.

Landragoran Heronwing
Yes, I didn't realize the white wurms were buffing up the others with resist magic. That's an excellent argument for dispelling first. Dispelling won't remove slows anymore btw, but it will remove Malo/Mala/Malosini.
So you need someone who's really fast on the dispell, using Annul Magic. Then you need a shaman with Malo or Mage with Mala, either works fine. Then you need everyone who can to SINI till it lands, and then they call it in the channel you're using for calling debuffs. Then everyone tries to slow. If you have a bard along with ooS it makes it much much smoother, but you can do it without that bard if you have to. Bard oos only debuffs around 16 MR, which isn't a ton, but helps. On certain mobs, it makes a big difference.

Another angle nobody has mentioned yet. Get the bard using a Journeyman's Walking stick. Have the bard melee the target until tash procs. Due to coding, spells casted (or procced) by bards stack with other spells. So you get bard tash plus chanter tash. If you use an Orb of Tashani on the bard, you can really make up for some serious lackage in the Malo department.
That's about the only way it makes sense to Turgur HoT mobs if you don't have Malo/a working for you.
