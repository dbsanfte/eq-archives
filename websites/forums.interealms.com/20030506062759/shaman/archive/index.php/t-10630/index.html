I recall doing alot of Burning Woods around this level. No, there is no Exp bonus but the fact that you have a JBB and Horse means endless mana. In BW there is an endless supply of blue mobs at 53. I did some of 53, alot of 54 and 55 here.

Set up camp around the giant fort. If no one is there pull the giants non stop. Ancients and Verdants will be blue, Arbors Light Blue. You will be making good exp and fantastic loot.

If Giant Fort has alot of activity just wander around outside and pick off blues at random. At 53 you should be able to kill, loot, do a 360deg circle while standing in place and pick from 3 mobs at least to get next.

I imagine the exp would only be so so with no JBB and Horse, maybe even poor, but with both it is great. Just slow, sic pet and JBB. Heck, IF I ever wanted to go below 4 bubs of mana I would drop and EB on the mob, unless I had a mass rebuff to do on myself and pet at same time, I was altmost always just below FM.
