Gardeltron
I've been playing Lineage 2 now for several months. Here's my 1 minute analysis:

1. Graphics are lovely. Landscapes look real, shadows are done right, there are many pretty scenes.

2. Animations are very good. You get the feeling of hitting mobs and being hit by them that is convincing. I don't know how many times I sat and shook my head at the silly swinging at monsters one does in EQ.

3. The game is based on the Unreal graphics engine, which is probably its strongest point.

4. There is a strong gender bias in the game. Female characters wear short skirts that show their panties and in some cases expose an abundance of cleavage.

5. Classes and races are severely limited, resulting in low character diversity.

6. Character customization is almost non-existant.

7. Availability of items and weapons is severely limited.

8. Because of 5, 6, and 7, everyone looks the same.

9. Spells and abilities are limited. Most upgrades occur by slight increases of existing spells/abilities.

10. Quests are generally useless, although you must at least twice over your career engage in boring quests to upgrade to the next tier of character development.

11. The UI is abysmal. There is a single chat window that cannot be adjusted in any way.

12. PKing, scamming, and griefing of players by unscrupulous individuals is spiralling out of control.

I've stayed with the game as long as I have because I've met some fun people, many of them EQ and DoAC refugees. I guess I'm just biding my time since I lost interest in CoH, don't have any interest in WoW, and am skeptical about EQ2.
