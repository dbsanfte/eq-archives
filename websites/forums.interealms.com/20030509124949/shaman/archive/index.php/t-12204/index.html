At 52 these are the buffs I use most of the time:
SoW from Black Fur Boots; Harnessing of Spirit
and Stamina. These or their replacements are your most effective "buffs."

In a group I need enough mana to slow. It is more important than almost anything else. I almost always have sufficient mana to give everyone stamina, haste melee if no enchanter, and to HoS tanks. I will HoS casters if mana permits, or Talisman of Altuna. I do not pass out agility usually.

The group spell I sometimes use is Spirit of Bih'Li which enhances attack as well as provides group SoW.

I also may Guardian and Rage and Bear Form myself.

Dog-dog gets HoS, Alacrity and Spirit Quickening.

I use Stoicism all the time now. Great for casters and for myself. By the way, have mana, and be prepared to cast Superior Healing if Stoicism doesnt keep up with DPS upon that caster. The tank should be able to pull the mob of the caster if the mob is not running.

My spell line up is as follows: 1. Sup Heal 2. (Buff) 3. Immobilize 4. Malosini 5. Turgurs 6. Envenomed Bolt 7. Stoicism 8. Cannibalize II.

Good luck. It is a great solo and group class. Also remember to hand dogdog two weapons. It adds a slight bit of damage (15%). DogDOg is nothing like any of the other pets, but this helps a little.

In grouping situations, I cast Talisman of Kragg on all members, except the melees who complain and want HoS because they think that stats do something. Talisman of Brute is also one I always cast. I occasionally Billy unless there is someone to cast Pack Spirit in the group. 15 atk is nice but the duration on billy is sooo short. I also do talisman of the Cat and Rhino when I'm feeling particularly buff happy. They dont really do anything but I cast them anyways to shut the melees up. If there is no Enchanter in the group, I haste as well.

Solo, the buffs stay darn near the same. very rarely Ill cast Guardian on myself for additional AC, but otherwise it stays Kragg, Sta, and Sow.

As for the pet, I think lots of people overbuff their pets. 90% of the time I give my dogdog. The other 10% I put haste and sow, when I know we will be running around together. I basically never HP, AC, or stat buff my pet. I have never noticed a signifigant difference, at least one worth the mana.

Of course I do dual-wield buff him :)
