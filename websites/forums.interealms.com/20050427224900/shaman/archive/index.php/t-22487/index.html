I don't know about "definitely soloable", but I would say it's soloable with a little care and luck. There are a few spots I can think of that could cause trouble.

The first would be making it through the shroom area. The shrooms aren't dangerous individually, but if you get three or four (easily possible with the roamers if you try to move too fast) with some healers mixed in, you could be in for a challenging fight.

The second challenging area would be (going from memory) pulling the Juggs from the area with the secret wall - see number 3 on the eqatlas map. (http://www.eqatlas.com/kunark/sebilis2map.html) I seem to recall always getting a number of mobs on this pull. I'm not sure if this is the spot I'm thinking of, or if the area just before. If you're good with rooting, it should be doable.

I don't have a lot of experience soloing deeper than this; the only times I've been down further has been on Trakanon raids.

Next, there's a wanderer just past the entryway into juggs, you can dispatch him easily, he runs perpendicular to the entry path. Kahn
If you get by the shrooms, be aware that pulling/breaking the room with Juggs/Reets (the area with the invisible wall) can bring a lot of adds, I've seen pulls of 7 there.

Juggs are rootable and summons, so don't start dotting many mobs (they only start summoning when they get below ~95% hp), take them down one at a time. Even using Spirit Call on the wrong mob can get you summoned into a heap of Juggs. Keep gate memmed if things turn brown and pray to the channeling-goddess.

That is NOT an easy camp to solo IMO, the rooms past the invisible wall have fewer mobs and are a lot more doable, although the bends in the halls make it hard to keep an eye on wandering Juggs.

If you can, you should really bring a friend (or two!) for this trip. If you fail there is pretty much no way to get to your corpse but summoning.

Yup, definately don't camp the 4 in the jug room with the secret wall. Go past there and hang a left and you get a room with 3 and a wanderer in the hallway leading to it. If you come back out from there you run a decent risk of aggro'in the wandering Jugg close enough to the main room to bring 2 of his friends, but honestly if you are ready for it 3 Juggs isn't too hard to pull off for a sham.

I'd suggest you get either pet hold or suspend minion however so that you can keep the dog from getting any damage on the jugs you are root parking during initial crowd control.


To break the main jug room, get in the hallway and pull one of the jugs on the far side with root, then target the other one IMEDIATELY (I'd suggest you have it set so that the toggle targets key, "~" by default, will do that for you) and hit it with Virulent P. This should net you 2 jugs aggro'd, so run back to the doorway and root one, then move up to the room entry, slow the only non rooted Jugg and refresh roots on all the others once you move to a semi-safe spot. From there, just be paranoid about keeping roots fresh and apply dog and DoTs liberally to 1 target at a time. If you keep your mana at a nice level then when you get down to 2 juggs, pop a DS potion and just tank both to speed things up.
