Aktunka
If you don't solo, the necklace would be of not a lot of use for you. If you DO like to solo, it is very handy. The snare necklace makes shamans ability to solo in dungeons tons better. Throw it on mobs with SoW early in the fight to get rid of SoW. Now the mob wont be so fast. Start casting it near 35% health. You should be able to land that, get in two bracer blasts, then reapply snare (this is the safe way and should give you time to redo it if this one is resisted...if you wanna risk it you can get in three bracer blasts usually). The snare necklace makes soloing in some spots viable and much easier. I solo in the Illis jail in Sebilis sometimes. Without the snare necklace, I don't know if this would be that viable. It isnt that great of an idea for a shaman to go chasing his mob out of a cell and run into another two illis and a krup or something like that hehe.

Anyhow, if I were your level, and level 25 is quite easy to get back to, I would reroll the character yes. If you ever like to solo, and you don't want to be confined to root-rotting or kiting like you may have done lots of with your druid, then the snare necklace (even though it is not even as good as the lowest level druid snare) is a godsend.
