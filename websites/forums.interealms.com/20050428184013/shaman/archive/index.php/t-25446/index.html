I have 3 pallies in my crew and this is how we handle big pulls. I main tank which also means I pull because I want initial aggro. Also, I'm spec'd pure DPS because we're light on damage and it seems to hold aggro better than aggro seals/spells (sadly).

- I pull a crowd to the crew which is hanging back
- I do damage to mob #1 on incoming
- I drop AE on crowd when at rest of crew
- pally #2 picks a mob, gets aggro, fires off his AE then assists me
- pally #3 picks a different mob, gets aggro, fires off his AE then assists me

At this point dmg is spread among 3 tanks, all mobs not being offtanked are on me due to my AE going off first. Offtanks are responsible for healing themselves, priest concentrates on me and only flash heals offtanks in emergencies.

We've had no problems with this so far, in fact it's worked great even on huge pulls (6-8) of elites. Any time the priest manages to get heal aggro one of us hits priest w/ invul shield and he just heals to his heart's content in complete safety.

We also keep blessing of salvation (35% aggro reduction) on the priest/rogue at all times, but I never use the aggro seal unless I'm worried about dropping aggro on a boss mob. However, we solved that pretty handily by letting me tank the mob for awhile solo with a heal chain going so I have solid aggro lock and heal aggro is spread among 2 other pallies and a priest. This has worked really, really well so far. We'll see how far we can milk this crew.

Ok, since after a month or so away from the boards, I already broke the seal on another thread, I guess can respond to some of this.

First off, if you've never played a warrior, please refrain from stating how easy it is for warriors to get aggro. Aggro control and rage generation have been and still are the greatest issues plaguing the warrior since the nerf in OB.

Single mob, sure I can get aggro, IF, I do the following:
+ battle stance
+ charge (generates about 12 rage)
+ demoralizing shout (people say generates threat, I have my doubts about how much)
+ berserker stance (down to about 5 rage now, not enough for an immediate special move unless I connect hard with a 2h weapon on my first swing)
+ bloodrage (generates more rage per hit/getting hit supposedly)
+ sunder armor (AC debuff, supposedly generates the best threat for warriors)
+ sunder armor again
+ sunder armor again
+ etc...

And all this relies on the shamans not using their most threat generating nuke (I think this is their highest damage one so they ALWAYS use it and get aggro) and that no one needs healing because the threat per heal ratio is incredibly high. Oh yeah, and no nukes.

Against more than one mob, I can demoralizing shout (since it's an AE) and then if I get enough rage, do some AE attacks to try and keep attention on me. If someone whacks another mob, he is now the offtank. I have no way of getting aggro back on that mob.

Obviously I'm not a defense spec'd warrior. Even if I was, taunt gets aggro for oh, about 1 second before the mob goes back to what it was doing until after the 10 second cool down I can taunt again.

If the mob is 2 levels above me? Forget it. Miss rate is so high and if I can't hit it, I'm not generating rage enough to do any special moves to piss it off.

Solo? Yeah, warrior is pretty good. I have no problems on mobs 1-2 levels higher usually. 2 or 3 mobs my level or lower, yeah, I can probably do that too.

But aggro generation and rage in groups, fighting tough mobs, warriors have a hard time. Yeah, I might be the last one standing, only because the rest of the party already got aggro and died.

Anyway, on the Horde side with a warrior tank, you will have a hard time trying a classic EQ MT type group. Most people are too idiotic to control their aggro and the WoW warrior has no current way to counter this.
