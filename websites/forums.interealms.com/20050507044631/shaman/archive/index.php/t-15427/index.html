Actually, Slace, I think you're about done. I believe the cipher of the gods is the zeks and saryn, etc. The problem is that you are missing 3 pre-flags.

Go to the library and take the elevator to the top (where grimel is, if you've worked on the aid grimel quest). There is a Grand Librarian Maelin or something up there. He's a gnome in the back.

Hail the librarian for 1 flag. Then ask him, "What lore?" and "What information?" He pre-flags you for both RZ and Sol Ro (the god, not the tower). You may need to unlock memories and come back and ask again.

I think that's all you need to complete your elemental flagging.

You don't have the MB flag yet from PoI in order to be able to enter Tactics.
You don't have the VZ flag from Tactics.
You don't have AD flag from PoV (though you don't actually need it to get ele flagged, it's good to get for completenes sake, and the free AA and increase to charm stats)
You don't have the other 2 mini's in Sol Ro Temple (Protector of Drexsomething and Rizlona)
You don't have Agnarr killed thus giving you Karana's flag.

To get access to Sol Ro Temple you need:
kill MB
kill VZ

To get all of your elemental plane access you need:
kill MB
kill VZ
kill Agnarr

Killing AD is NOT a requirement for Sol Ro Temple access or Ele Plane access. You can get the 3 trials done, and get Mith Marr's flag without AD having been killed. I know this for a fact since one of the clerics in our guild is such a situation.

Doing any of the 5 minis in Sol Ro Temple is NOT required to get flagged for PoF. My 55 monk has never seen a mini, but can free walk into PoF because he has all the other flags.

Doing all of the 5 minis in Sol Ro Temple, having Sol Ro Temple access, and having killed RZ are all the requirements to enter Sol Ro's chamber. If you have a raid of 32+ flagged people for Sol Ro's Chamber, you can get ANY number of other non-flagged people up there using the 85% rule. It's a huge pain to do so and can take a fair while, but I'll leave that up to the reader to figure out how to do that.
