Play time is prolly anywhere form 2-7 hours this week each night. So really not that bad in 59. Just gotta be diligent and attentive when you do the exp grind.

Good luck grinding through this hell we call 59.

59 is not a difficult level if you go at it hardcore. Just find a few days where you have nothing else to do and can take off work or whatever, and sit down and play.

I was at about 4 blues in to 59, then I just said "im sick of not being 60, im gonna go for it." I sat down in FG with just me and my friend's 60 sk (two boxed) and didn't move for about 45 hours or so. When it was over, I was so sick of mushrooms that I couldn't even eat them in real life, but I was also level 60 :)

However, I would not recommend FG solo. I was unable to single pull at level 59, I would always get half the patch. I had to use the SK to pull. Also, the shrooms take forever to kill with just JBB and epic, so you need to lay a bunch of dots on them. However, this takes a lot of mana and you end up with downtime. I would stay in katta at the bank, it is still great exp.
