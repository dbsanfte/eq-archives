To determine how often a weapon procs, comparitively, you can look it up on Lucy. Simply find the weapon, go to "raw" tab (top right) and look for the field "procratemod".

Note also, proc rate is independant of how often you swing, it's a fixed number of procs per minute. You do need to swing to proc, of course.

Staff of Eternal Flames (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/itemraw.html?id=24789), Hammer of Hours (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/itemraw.html?id=21886) and Sharkspine Club (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/itemraw.html?id=21885) all have mods of 0, so they should all proc about the same number of times per minute. I'm not sure how 1h and 2hers vary, though.

The cleric summoned hammer, Hammer of Damnation (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/itemraw.html?id=29365) has a mod of 530 - that procs a LOT.
