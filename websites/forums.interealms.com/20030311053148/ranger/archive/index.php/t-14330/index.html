Habermas
Been there, done that. In fact, headed back there tomorrow. (I hope you aren't on Veeshan!)

Tigeraptors and Tigersauruses are in WL on hillside to the left as you exit the SS zone. You can bind in SS (even if you are KOS to guards, find a nice caster to bind you just inside zone; you will be glad you did). Biggest danger is Woushi in zone over by Kael entrance and the other named dragon that once came over and killed me (I think he was trained) at raptor hillside. Also there are wandering mercenaries, including one Tier'Dal Shadowknight that gave me fits for a while. SS is a good zone to run to. On the raptors, use harmony and snare, use panic animal. About one in five drops a skin. First time I sold these skins I got 300pp each; since then prices have dropped dramatically. Supply and demand...keep the supply down and the demand (price) will go up. If there is competition in the marketplace, tuck them away for a few weeks and bring them out when no one else is selling.

WL is a great zone for rangers, but BE CAREFUL!
