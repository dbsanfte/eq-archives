BlargX
Tactician's bulwark for 27k points? Hmm, now that's good info!

I'm maxed in all tradeskills, and found a use for the unsellable cultural plate armor that stopped moving on my bazaar trader mules months ago. They can bring from around 9k to like 21k in tribute points, and if you get lucky with your success rate(about 30-40% is a good success rate on those), you can make quite a lot of tribute off of not a whole lot of plat.

I've also found the same thing applies to other tradeskilled armor. Tae Ew shields bring somewhere over 4k in tribute value, and sometimes you can buy all the bloods needed for 1k, if you're lucky. Worth looking out for. Same with nightmarewood compound bows. Both are more or less unsellable these days, but still bring good tribute.

I tried looking for some of the items that others said they turned in, but found that what many recommended using goes for literally 10x as much on my server, or was never found in the bazaar, or was lore, and the transferring back and forth was just not worth the effort for the small return.

Tacticians bulwarks are really easy to get, though. I might try to scoop some of them up sometime from the bazaar. I probably sold 50 of them myself.

The tributes can get used up very quickly, I find. I'm not comfortable going through many hundreds of plat per hour, even though I'm doing fine for money and don't really have anything else to spend it on. But I already have detrimental haste and BA4 and crit AA. If I didn't and I was going to do a lot of dotting to camp loot or get exp, I'd probably be a LOT more excited about tribute.

Or if I had a lowbie I wanted to level fast. Those tributes can be fantastic for a lowbie without the benefit of good gear or AA yet. I'd say that was probably the best use of the tribute system -- to power up lowbies.
