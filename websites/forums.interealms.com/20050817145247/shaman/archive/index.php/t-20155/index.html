Thorzion
Cleric
Pros
-Always 30 seconds from a group
-guilds will kiss your ass to keep you
-always usefull, always stack, always wanted
Cons
-Dependant on a group post 20
-if the group dies you are the first target for blame
-can be mind numbingly boring for some

Enchanter
Pros
-some of the most sought after buffs in the game
-incredible utility
-insane solo ability
Cons
-can be frustrating
-soloing is dangerous (charm soloing)
-mid 50's and up can be very dull, as most exp is done in DL2.0 (aka PoP)

Beastlord
Pros
-good solo ability
-good buff lines/slow
-good damage output
Cons
-Umm...did SoE make any bad sides to the BST class?

Paladin
Pros
-Tanks that can heal and root are great
-good buff lines
-GREAT heal lines
Cons
-Agro maintenance can be troublesome sometimes

Warrior
Pros
-Beefiest tank
-with new disciplines, agro management is much easier
-get into a guild MT posistion and you call the shots
Cons
-no solo ability (other than highly twinked, and then of dubious ability)
-general lack of ulitity makes them one sided
-completely gear dependant

Rogue
Pros
-Highest damage class in the game
-good ulility through hide/sneak for scouting, CR, etc
-did I mention wicked nasty damage ability?
Cons
-Agro management can be an issue with an undereqquiped tank
-one sided....you poke things from behind
-gear dependant

Ranger
Pros
-great melee output, especially with AM3/EQ
-good utility through buffs/tracking
-moderate solo ability through upper 50's (fear kite animals)
Cons
-Until Jolt (level 51) you will tread the agro line very closely
-Fewer HP than other melee classes/hybrids (in many cases) leadsto problems when agro is directed at you

Monk
I've no experience with a monk post 18, so I offer no advice

Shadowknight
Pros
-fastest agro by far
-good damage output (5 sk's with HT aa's=50K instant damage)
-make great SA's on raids
Cons
-Dependant on mana for agro generation
-gear dependant

Wizard
Pros
-Second highest damage output, first among non melee classes
-port/evac utility
-some of the best solo ability (quad kiting)
Cons
-Completely mana dependant for damage
-Little other ulitlity besides ports
-Groups may be hard to come by late 30's to early 50's

Magician
Pros
-Nasty pets make a big mess of mobs
-Good nuking ability
-able to summon a wide range of utility items that every raid wants/needs (rods, pet weapons, focus items)
Cons
-No ROOT..can be very annoying
-keeping pets alive against PoP style mobs can be taxing/mana intensive
-LoS problems with Bolts (which are a main form of mage damage) can be annoying

Necromancer
Pros
-Insane solo ability (fear kiting red cons from level 12 and up)
-Best self mana regen in game (best lich is 75HP for 60 mana every tic)
-wide range of group utility (mez, snare, root(vundead) mana feeds, rez)
cons
-Grouping can be difficult, as many do not understand the power of a good necro in groups
-Raiding content is alot of twitching....dull

Druid
Pros
-Great utility through ports, buffs, mana regen, tracking
-second best heals in game, can sub for clerics in PoP/LdoN
-nukage on par with Mages
cons
-they are druids....enough said
