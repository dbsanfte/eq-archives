Nektar
Lodi shield by a long shot. Better ac, has resists (the 3 most crucial ones imo), and eb effect. Permanent eb can come in very useful sometimes, and most of the eb items out there has crap stats like fb earring, amulet of the grey wastes, and ancient fishing pole (I'd like to see a pallie with one of those). So not only does lodi shield have great stats, but it's an extra plus in places where you need eb because you won't need to sacrifice any stats by otherwise using a pretty awful item stat-wise just for the eb effect.

I'm sure there are a few other eb items with nice stats (like lodi ring for one), but I'd keep lodi shield at all costs. Incredible stats for a back item with a combination of AC/wis/resists, all of which are vital for a high level shaman.
Henora
Like most other equipment choices, it will likely completely depend on your gear situation and your goals.

I made this same choice when we randomly killed Inny a few weeks ago. I could have had the cloak of scales if I wanted it, he dropped 2. Ended up giving these cloaks to a SK and a Necro (nice nice cloak for an iksar necro :) ).

I kept the Lodi because the AC loss would have dropped me under 1000ac. I already sacrifice a lot for HPs, but decided that 1000ac would be the lowest I would go. That's my reason.

Also, since I dedicate most of my other slots to HP/sta items, I don't have a ton of resist gear on... 20 FR/CR and 10MR on an item is awfully nice. EB is just convenient. One less piece of alternate gear I have to carry.

..and, if I was going for pure HP, I guess I would probably look at the Hiero cloak. It has 75hp/mana 4+ all stats and drops in the seb crypt, not off a God, as weakass as he might be.

Hiero 10ac, ~87hp, ~99mana.
Scales 15ac, ~50hp, ~110mana.

Good luck
-Henora
