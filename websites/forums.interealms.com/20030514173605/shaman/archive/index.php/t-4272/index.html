There are 2 sorts of faction hits.

1)Faction hits for killing mobs. Every person who acted on the mob to get on the Hate List (with a few minor exceptions) or who has acted on some on the hate list gets the faction hit assuming they are still alive/in-game when the mob dies. Unlike the XP, it isn't split up - everyone gets the full hit.

2)Faction hits for quest turn-ins. Only the person ding the turn-in gets the hit. In the case of MQ-ing, it goes to the same person who gets the quest reward itself.
