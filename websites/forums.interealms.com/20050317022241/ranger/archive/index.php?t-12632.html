so far what i have seen...

Plane of Knowledge:
non-combat zone (like Bazaar/Nexus)
lots of merchants and quest givers
everything is non-kos, in fact i think most of it was indifferent, so probably always be non-kos.

Plane of Tranquility:
non-combat zone (like Bazaar/Nexus)
quest givers and some merchants (fairly low NPCs population)
everything is non-kos, don't remember if they were indif or not

Plane of Disease:
combat zone
everything I saw and conned was kos, from multiple characters (some people might be non-kos based on diety, but i highly doubt it). most things near the first part of the zone do not see invis, I don't know about further in.

Plane of Inovation:
combat zone
everything I saw and conned was kos, from multiple characters (some people might be non-kos based on diety, but i highly doubt it). most things near the first part of the zone see thru invis, I don't know about further in.

Plane of Justice:
combat and quest zone
everything near the zone in appears to be indifferent, and you can talk to the prisinors for stories and quests. Further in things start to become KoS. I don't know if they see invis or not, didn't check far enough.

Plane of Nightmares:
combat zone
everything I saw and conned was kos, from multiple characters (some people might be non-kos based on diety, but i highly doubt it). most things near the first part of the zone see thru invis, I don't know about further in.
