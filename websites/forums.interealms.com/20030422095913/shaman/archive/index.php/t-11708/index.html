Wow...been awhile since I saw a shaman with Wis in every slot. Most shaman, as stated above, value HP, STA, and mana items to Wis items.

However, since you say that you are going to be playing this shaman with your necro this MIGHT not be a bad setup. I say this because cannibalizing takes attention, and that might not be something that you are going to be able to do while trying to play your necro. I do it, but the shaman is my main and my SK is there just to tank while I do real damage with the shaman. Not knowing your play style and motivations its hard to say. If that is the case then having a large mana pool might be preferable to having a fast mana regen. But...With a wis of 213 Magelo says you have 2566 mana. I am 55 as well, and have 179 wis, with 2357 mana. So you have 34 more Wis than I do, but only 209 more mana. Thats less than one cast of Turgurs.

But if you are wanting to make this shaman into something that will be able to stand up solo, and do well in groups (withouth the necro) I would follow the advice that has been given above. Check some of the Magelos that are floating around the boards, lots of times we tend to wear alot of the same stuff at the same levels.
