Jorlaan
You can't resize the general chat window or move it, but you can drag the other windows and shrink/expand them.

One thing to remember is that when you create a new chat, it seems to inherit the settings of the general tab. You'll have two windows spewing the same stuff to you, so you'll need to click off what you don't want in each general and the new window.

I wish they had at least one smaller font point size than they do. I'd like the shrink the windows even further.

I also want to be able to move the spell casting bar somewhere else. I assume I can do this with a custom ui mod but I haven't looked into those yet.
