In my case, I am not anywhere near either cap, and probably will not be for the foreseeable future.

I understand that 1 mana regen is better than 1 hp regen, but would you, for example (and assuming neither is capped) take 1 mana regen over 3 hp regen?

The point is that mana and hp are completely interchangeable, via healing and canni. 1 mana heals about 2.5hp and 1hp gives about 0.5 mana (approximatlely). But shaman tend to concentrate on maximising their hp pool, and then use whatever mana pool this gives them (and please understand, I agree with the principle). So, it would seem to me to be a logical extension of the hp > mana philosophy to try to maximisze ones hp regen and then use whatever mana regen that gives you.

Specifically, in my case, I am thinking that I will take the regenII over the FT1 aug that I am considering (or I will possibly even be patient and save for the regenIII aug instead of either).
