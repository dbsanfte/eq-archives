Landragoran Heronwing
Ok how many of you shamans cast Malo?
How many cast slows?
Dots?
Root?

Affliction haste affects all of these with a hefty savings in time. For example, with the Affliction Haste III bracer I got tonight from Eashen (bless his scaley heart) my cast time on Malo has dropped from 5 seconds to 3.4 seconds. Turgurs has dropped from 3.0 seconds to 2.0 seconds. This represents about a 33% savings in time.

Faster slows = less interrupts on slows. A 2 second slow is pretty darn quick. A 3.4 second Malo certainly has a better shot of channeling thru while that MR as hell mob beats on you.

Anyway, I am a bit ignorant of the items out there with effects like this. Is there a lower end item with AF 3? I think this might be one of the most useful effects for shamans out there.
