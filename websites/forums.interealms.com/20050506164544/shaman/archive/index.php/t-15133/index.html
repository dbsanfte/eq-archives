Sorry to hear that Jharassik, but you should know you're just making your life harder than it needs to be. AAxp is way easier at 65 than at 60-64 for several reasons.
1) groups want 65 shamans >>>> 60-64 shamans.
2) raids want 65 shamans >>>>> 60-64 shamans.
3) More powerful spells mean mobs die smoother, faster, and present less of a challenge
4) with the revamp of the exp system, shamans can solo lvl 51+ mobs for blue-con exp while easily farming stuff.
5) Quiescence makes SO much possible, where Torpor did not. For instance you can Q your dog and he tanks very well in places like CT. This gives you additional options. Malos also really helps vs. uber resistant mobs, we're talking -15 additional debuff resists here! Not trivial! That's like having a personal OOS bard along!

That's about it . . . there's no reason anymore not to level to 65. None whatsoever.
I recommend you just do it and start saving to buy Q and malos. Then get BoS and the rest of your spells. That will keep you occupied for quite some time. Once finished, you'll have acquired so many AA you won't believe it!

Finally, people don't recruit for groups based on the AA levels of a player. It's /who all 65 shaman
