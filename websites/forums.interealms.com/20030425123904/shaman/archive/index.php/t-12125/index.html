The Hole is easy

I did the Hole with a single group, without an enchanter. We had a 61 SK puller, a 61 Warrior tank, a 59 cleric, a 57 druid and me 56 (at the time) shaman. Getting to the base of Kirn's tower is a crawl, but it is not a particularly difficult crawl. You can download a fairly good map. You need either a competent FD puller or a competent crowd controller.

You don't even need to fight thru Kirn's tower if you use IVU potions to get to his room. Not that clearing the tower is too hard.

Kirn himself is easy.

The best way, in my opinion, to get this part of the epic is to organise a "multi-epic" raid. Shaman are not the only class that needs epic pieces from the Hole: Rangers need whatshisname the wretched which is near the spiral tunnel down to Kirn's Tower. Paladins need Mummy of Gholour (or however ist spelled) at the bottom of Kirn's tower, and I think SKs need something too. So, get Rangers, Pallys and SKs to tag along for their epic too - combining raids = stronger raid force
