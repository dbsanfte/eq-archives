Mangler Moreau
Grab yourself a kei and run over to Howling Stones. I absolutely love this zone. Couple of tips before you zone in.

1. Make sure you have root, superior heal, your pet spell, and gate up in your spell slots.
2. Before you enter HS make sure you are invis.
3. Upon zone in you will be underneath an overhang. There will be 2 entrance mobs in the area. Quickly root the mobs, back away, heal yourself if you have to, and summon pet. Zone in will always be tricky solo but once you get familiar with zoning in you will get yourself a routine.
4. The entrance mobs are on a 20 minute timer. So keep this in mind as you pull Pyres.
5. Get a map of the zone

As a level 58 Iksar Shaman I get 1 percent regular exp every 4 or 5 mobs. I get 1 percent AA exp per kill at 58. Nice zone for AA.
