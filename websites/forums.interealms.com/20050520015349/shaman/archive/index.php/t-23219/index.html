Ungkor
Well there are two options for pop spells:

1. Farm loot, then buy the spells. While the runes are no drop, the spells are not. So, go to veksar with a bud or two and farm 36% haste belts, or something like that. Should be able to get 1-2 per night, and they sell well.

2. Farm runes in bot. If you have one group, pick a wing and do the named in each wing. They occasionally drop runes and ornate armor. If your guild can muster 2-3 groups, you can also do the bot tower named, which drop better ornate pieces, and a better chance at runes. 1 person in each group needs their tower key to admit the group though.

If you are talking GoD spells... well good luck there hehe.

The sewer missions, experience version that you get from Gamesh in Barindu are easy, but you have a low chance of spell drops. Probably the best one is the "lair" mission, where you kill 30 mobs or so, move down a hallway to a room, and hope that the sludge eating fly has popped. He will occasionally drop a GoD rune.

A better drop rate, but significantly harder mission set, is the vxed/tipt instanced missions. If you can get a solid lvl 65 group with an enchanter, you can farm the beginning of the missions for named mobs. You'll want a tracker also, and probably a monk or bard to pull the named to the zone line. I got Daluda's Mending this way.
