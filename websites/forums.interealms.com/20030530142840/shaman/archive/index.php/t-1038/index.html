When you zone into Shadow Haven from the Nexus, there is a Barbarian alchemy vendor in one of the nearby buildings. He has a really, really easy FedEx quest that you can do over and over again to raise faction with the merchants (though you should be apprehensive to start with, and be able to buy the spells, just at high prices). I went from apprehensive to amiable in about 15 minutes.
