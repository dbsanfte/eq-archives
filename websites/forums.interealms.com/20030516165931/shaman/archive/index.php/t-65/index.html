I managed to get allied with the alchemist and trainer - do the Trade Commisioner about 5 or 6 times. There are also 2 delivery quests from the Bazaar to Shadowhaven - one is given by the alchemist to deliver something to one of the 2 gnomes in the back-corner behind the bank. He will give you something to take back to the alchemist.

The other is a delivery from the dwarf ore vendor to the other gnome in the bazaar - back-corner behind the other bank. That gnome has a continuation of the quest - but I didn't bother with it.

If you do the deliveries in the right order you can do the cycle really fast - it took me 2 hours to get to allied - and I bound at the bazaar so I could just gate back after Fungus grove.
