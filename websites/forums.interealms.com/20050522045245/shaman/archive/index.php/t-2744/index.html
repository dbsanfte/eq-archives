One thing I wanted to point out regarding malosi and turgur's. The turgur's line and the Malo line both resist against magic without modification. If you check the spdat they will have:

Resist: Magic

If you check one of the paladin stuns with damage, you will see:

Resist: Magic +20

That means they have a 20% chance to be resisted. EDIT: means they have an ADDITIONAL 20% chance to be resisted.

If you look at the resists with an spdat parser, Malosi has the exact same chance to be resisted as turgur's does. If the only thing you are casting on the mob is slow, don't bother with any of the malo(x) line unless you have Malo.
