Okie..just FYI..this quest is full of time wasters, so here's what I learned last night and today.

The coerced channeler is on Cabilis faction, so he's KoS to just about everything in Dalnir. Hence he's only up briefly, and doesn't always respawn each cycle.

Opposite the area where the indifferent Iksar Prisoner is (greenmist quest?), is another hallway. At the far back of that hallway is a room with chains on the wall like the prisoner's, and a broken table. This is where the channeler spawns.

I went LD over lunch in this room..when I logged back on the room was full of mobs. As I was gating out I saw the channeler standing in the corner near the door. Mind you, I had spent all last night clearing the whole floor hall by hall and never saw him.

Kicking myself for gating when I could have finished the quest right there..I ran back across WW to Dalnir, and made my way back down to that room...and he wasn't THERE!

In a rage I nuked and dotted everything in the room, and then worked my way back out to the 'main ' room to camp the lumpy goo a while so I could figure this out. After a few minutes of no lumpy I decided that I was going to camp the broken table room and see what happens...perhaps he had simply despawned like the prisoner in Droga?

I got back to the room, and to my HORROR I found the channeler in a fight for his life against a smouldering goo. I dotted it, and it turned on me..which is great since channeler had perhaps 1 bub of life left. Once the threat was dead, I finished the hand-in and gated..as Vagnar was kind enough to cause a small train again as he ran off into the hallway.

Hope this helps!
