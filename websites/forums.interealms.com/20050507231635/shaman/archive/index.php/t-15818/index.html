Chazic
Putting your back to a nice solid wall helps a lot as well. A lot of times the reason we get interrupted is not due to being stunned but due to being knocked back by a hit. If we were able to perfectly move you could move back to where you started casting before you completed casting and you would complete you casting with out interruption. This is the number one reason why it is so difficult to cast spells underwater as your pushed back very easy by hit in the water. So if your having difficulty getting spells off due to interrupts plant that little butt next to the wall and face out. It'll be 90% easier to cast that spell then. Trees and rocks work as well when no wall is available.

Kahn
Pull with malo/s towards a safe spot (VP entrance is pretty safe, although not 100%). Then root the wurm before it reaches you, it will not summon if you have not damaged it. Slow and cripple, you can change spells and canni a bit before engaging him.

Quies on yourself right before you start fighting. Keep the pet (dualwielding, focused and hasted) in the wurms back, also get Spirit Call pets in its back if you have it. I have canni5 but have never used it while fighting guardian wurms, too risky for me with low hp without clr/dru hp buffs. Keep Quies on yourself all the time, kill with pet, epic, jbb and possibly more dots if you have the mana. Keep Tnargs up for emergency heals when the wurms gets a few lucky shots (which he will ;)) It is really not that hard.

Good luck!
