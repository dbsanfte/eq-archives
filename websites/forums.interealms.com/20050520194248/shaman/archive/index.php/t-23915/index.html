Chutoi
The elite sun revenant (wizzie 1.5 epic START mob) is kind of a badass. Hits reasonably hard-ish (flurries 1k or so), has a ton of hp, but has some badass AEs. One is an unresistable mana drain (detrimental) at -1000 mana/tic. He's got some DoTs and DDs. Bottom line, he's not good to get near.

The kill is tedious, but easy. All his AE ranges are 150 range. Put a single tank out front, let him get agro, and go to town with spells and ranged attacks. Pious remedy (supernal remedy), sylvan infusion, and, of course, daluda's all have 200 range for heals. Once he's slowed, he's cake. It just takes a while with ranged attacks.
