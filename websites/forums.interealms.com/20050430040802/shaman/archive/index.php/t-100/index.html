Kith, by the way are different types of Changeling in White Wolf's roleplaying game by the same name (characters are all half-mortal, half-mystical-critters from Celtic and similar folklore - Phooka, Sidhe, Troll, Satyr, etc. - in a modern world setting, fighting against the retreat of magic from the world.)

Nockers, by the way, are a bit like gnomes - they tinker up strange devices that never quite work as intended... and swear a lot. Anyone who fiddles with computers, hardware or software, for any time at all knows the feeling ;)
