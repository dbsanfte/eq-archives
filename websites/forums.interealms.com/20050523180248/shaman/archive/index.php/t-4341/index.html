the tactic to use is pull one, root him, charm him, then pull another, root it on top and tell charmed pet to attack it. KEEP INVIS MEMMED. Charm is not very reliable at all, I use to charm Grimalkins in LOIO at 29, it would be resisted or fade after 12 seconds. I found it to be useful if I got an add, I would charm one tigger and sic him on the other, most of the time I would end up with 2 angry kitties on me. When I was successful with it, I would charm one kitty and assist him tanking. I would root both the mob and the charmed pet, when charm broke I would back off and recharm and re-root. When mob died I would wait until my charm broke. When it broke I would then kill it....twice the exp, but not very mana efficient exp though, also not very reliable.
Pet does not steal exp anymore unless you do zero damage to the mob. If I'm not mistaken. I found charm animal to be a waste, I've used it maybe 3 times after I got it. I haven't used it since.


If mad exp is what you're craving, go to the Golra Iceberg in Timorous Deep. There is a 20% exp modifier in that zone and the Iceberg is basically abandoned. No roamers, so adds will not be a problem. Just pull with Slow, DoT, and beat em down. I went from 29-34 there in a very short time and I'm an Iksar.
29 is the first level you get a "valuable" slow spell...tagar's insects. It is quite an upgrade to Walking Sleep, use it !!Always Slow your enemy, makes them much easier to deal with. For solo exp, what I did was....Haste myself, cast Slow, DoT, and melee. I rarely ran into trouble that way. If I did get in trouble I would root, back off and heal myself. Very easy exp until you get a pet, and life get even easier from there. Pet is a unlimited DoT as long as he is alive. Debuffs at 29 and below, weren't very useful for me. I never used them, unless I had trouble slowing a mob.
For me charm was a waste, after I went to the Golra iceberg, I didn't need charm anymore, because I hit 34 and got Cujo, who is loyal to the death.

Had to do a bit of research on this one before posting. In the way of slows at 29 you are better off sticking to good ol' Walking Sleep. Walking Sleep slows from 23-35% (it does lvl/4 + 20%); Tagars slows from 35-50% (lvl/2 + 20%). At 29 you only slow for 34.5% with Tagars, 27.25% for Walking Sleep for a gain of +7.25% for twice the mana. Not meaning to step on yer toes Stormrazor, but the mana saved at 29 is huge, especialy with even a single resist for us low wis Shaman out there. :)
