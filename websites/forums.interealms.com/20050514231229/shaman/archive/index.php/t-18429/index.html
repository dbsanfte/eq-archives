Here's the skinny.

TRASH
Two names = 4 arm. ie "Eom Centien" (spawns robe on death)
Va'dyn = rock
Xakra = robe

Eom = level 66 -- ONLY Divine Stun AA works
Pli = level 64
Zun = level 61
Zov = level 58, mezzable
Qua = level 55, mezzable

Centien = Warrior. Ramp/Flurry
Senshali = Rogue. Flurry. Procs Greater Shadow Poison (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2160&source=Live)
Va Liako = Shadow Knight... Ramp/Flurry?
Liako = Paladin... Ramp/Flurry?
Thall = Cleric. Higher levels AE Word of Command (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2157&source=Live) . Procs Mark of Darkness (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2165&source=Live) -- GATES
Zethon = Wizard. Higher levels AE Blackwind (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2162&source=Live) -- GATES

NAMED
(If I don't have a max hit it's coz I never been hit by it)

Va Dyn Khar (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=13443)
The First named you kill. Drops nothing but key to get into VT.

Thall Va Xakra (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=8778) -- TVX, slowable, silence, ramps, max hit 900
These are your first VT named. 3 day respawn. Very easy. AE Silence. After you enter VT there's a path north and south. It leads to these. Clear the wing before you pull because all the mobs there (28?) will assist whenever you aggro the named. The guards drop keys you need for further VT access.

Kaas Thox Xi Ans Dyek (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=8776) -- Blob1, UNSLOWABLE, rooted, grav-flux, flurry, WARDERS, max hit 650
From enterance go straight (west). You really can't miss him. Best spot to leave a CoH bot is in the "nook" behind. You will really want a CoH bot there.

Diabo Xi Xin (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=8788) -- DXX, ramps, slowable, max hit 1200
From the door to Blob1 go North. Follow it around til you get to a big room. Stairs there take you to DXX. He's all alone. Guards got some keys you need.

Diabo Xi Va (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=8789) -- DVX, UNSLOWABLE, max hit 654
Opposite of DXX. Go South from Blob1. These guards have to same keys as DXX.

Diabo Xi Xin Thall (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=8787) -- DXXT, UNSLOWABLE, WARDERS, rampage, silence
From DXX or DXV just keep going around. Next big room has stairs to DXXT.

Note: There are rooms on the north and south sides. There's one 4arm in each that patrols back and forth. This is the best spot to place a CoH mage (allowing you to skip from Blob1 to DXXT. This is a HUGE time saver). So far none of these 4arms have seen invis.

Thall Va Kelun (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=8817) -- TVK, ramp, quad 1000, slowable, WARDERS
Port up from clicker behind DXXT (2 mobs there). Clear mobs toward the West. Always has two 'Thall' guards, but they suck so it's easier to just pull them all at once rather than splitting. Time is precious if you wanna be in bed before 4am.

DO NOT CLICK THE THING NEXT TO TVK OMG!

You only have to kill ONE of either TXD or DXVT. They share the same Warders.

Thall Xundraux Diabo (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=8816) -- TXD, UNSLOWABLE, WARDERS
Go North from the TVK area. Some pulls here are pretty big. This is my personal choice of hardest mob in VT.

Diabo Xi Va Temariel (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10400) -- DXVT, slowable, WARDERS, Blackwind (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2162&source=Live), super gimp
South from TVK area. Some of the pulls can get a little large but even with bad luck you shouldn't get anything worse than 3-4 Eom.

The Porters behing DXVT/TXD click up to the room before Mini-Aten. There are generally 4 very angry high levels mobs here (Eom/Pli). So be ready.

None of the mobs need splits and you don't need to hurry before respawn. Nice relaxing bit at the end of the day. This is Candy Land.

Va Xi Aten Ha Ra (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=8786) -- Mini-Aten, slowable, WARDERS, grav-flux

DON'T CLICK THE THINGY! There's a secret wall behind it.

Kaas Thox Xi Aten Ha Ra (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=8818) -- Blob2, slowable, grav-flux, max hit 1650
There's two of these. One North and one South.

Aten Ha Ra (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=8815) -- AHR or Aten, slowable, AEs Word of Command (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2157&source=Live) + Silence + grav-flux, ramps, max hit 1054

Grats you completing VT

"Diabo Xi Xin Thall -- DXXT, UNSLOWABLE, WARDERS, rampage, silence"

What does WARDERS mean?

Warders are untargetable mobs that are placed throughout the zone that punt people to vey bad spots if they come near them. They were added after the zone was released because people would just CoH to AHR w/o killing anything else. When you kill a named (say blob1) all the Warders between that named and the next named despawn. As to AHR, you can safetly camp a CoH mage in her prep room BUT if there are any warders left up, she likes to AE Death Touch. Before a patch about 2 months ago, warders spawning were not linked to the named so many times a named would be killed before the warders spawned, in essence screwing up the zone requireing a GM to fix.
