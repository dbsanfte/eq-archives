Zaviax McCleo
Nath guards are the ones in the mansion with sonic wolves guarding out front. From the TM zone just hug the wall on your left and you'll get there.

Takes a lot to get KoS there. I've soloed there a handful of times and killed many guards. Still dubious to them but it did bring me amiable with the other guards.

I can't remember if I started at 55 or 56. But when I started I was able to single pull the two sonic wolves in front and the two guards but there were times when both guards would come. It seemed to help if I kept them out of line of sight and pulled.

Once you start pulling from in the house you'll normally get 2 or 3 at a time till you hit 57-58. But handling adds is very easy anyway. Just park the add with root and rot it while Scooby takes on the other.

Very easy, good XP, sucky loot. It's also very safe, if for any reason you get in trouble the zone line isn't far and no adds to worry about.

As for Dulak there's a few spots off ships to solo in. The boathouse near the Oceancrasher is one. Park yourself along the wall in between dock and the static gnome. That's a safe spot. At 56 I was able to single pull all the mobs to me. There's is one mob in the boathouse that will aggro through the door, but only that one. (He's always a Erudite) Also, one of the mobs guarding the dock to the Oceancrasher will aggro if you go up to the door so take him our first.

If that spot is taken you can move across to the other side of the boathouse in front of a dock that doesn't lead to any ship. This spot is very safe. No pops, no roamers. You have a few buildings to choose to pull from. It's heavily camped but it's safe and big enough to hold two people soloing. I've shared it many times with other soloers and duos.

Another spot is the warehouse area near the Torg mine zone. There's a safe spot in back and you have an abundance of mobs around you. I still solo here at 58 even though they're low blue. May be little XP but I can rack in tons of coin, BD's, Ikky Blood and occasionally kill a named that drops a SK weapon which sells for a nice chunk in bazaar.
Turmoiltoad
The castle entrance to nadox from gunthak might be what you are looking for. When you zone in, you can see the mobs in the room, but can't "click" target them because you are behind a false wall. But, you can f8 target the closest. The first one if the tricky one because you can't pull them (at least not at my level) into the zone in passage without them pathing really wierd and bringing friends. So, F8 target, send pet, slow, heal pet, DoT, Heal pet, DoT, etc. You don't want to get agro because of the pathing reasons. But, once the first one is killed, move into the room hugging the right wall and you are golden. You can single pull all of the room excpet the "door" mobs. There are two wanderers that path through as well that can be singled. These mobs don't run on low health and you have a zone very close. Remember, from inside the room you can't see the tunnel entrance, so don't be alarmed.
Lemyoo
The Dandolak boat in Dulaks has been a sweet spot for me at 56, and now through most of 57....think they should be very doable at 55.

Now if that boat is camped, I've been heading to the area where the Soulstripper guy pops. Easy to single pull here, and once you get the roamer out of the way and know when he is going to pop, the only add you have is if one gets crazy legs when it's about to die...even then, they have to make it to the bridge before they bring friends, which is plenty of time to lay down a root or hit with a life-ending DD.

What I do is make the right at the tentacle bridge, and run almost all the way down to the steps invis'd. Right where the cave declines before reaching the steps is where I make pet and buff up. Nothing (except roamer) will aggro here. I stay ivis'd until the roamer gets far enough away for me to make dog, toss on speed, and get on the lizard.

The way I found works best is to kill roamer 1st...then one of the emissary's thats flanks either side of steps...THEN the servant of hate who is enraged soulstealer PH....then other flank...then guy at bottom of steps. Rinse and repeat. I kill the flank guy before the servant of hate in case the soulstripper dude pops while I'm fighting the roamer, since his aggro range is bigger than the others. Avoid the mobs up by the bridge -- one is a CH'ing cleric thats light blue to 56 and not worth the effort.

I did Twilight at 54 and 55, and while it got me through, it was just mind-numbing. I wish I had gone to Dulak's at that level -- xp is faster and the loot/cash makes it well worth the while. I've done 15k just in gems/weps over the past week and a half, playing maybe 3 hours a night.
