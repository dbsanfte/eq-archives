Trying to explain with a minimum of faulty grammar... ;)

The correct steps are this:

1. Give soulbound hammer to Mairee Silentone, receive Hammer of the Ancients
2. Retrieve swirling sphere of color from plane of sky as told by Mairee
3. Give hammer of the ancients and swirling sphere of color to Mairee Silentone, receive Dwarven Smith's hammer
4. Give dwarven smith's hammer to Kinlo Strongarm, spawns Usbak the Old, receive mithril ore (given to you immediately by Usbak the Old as he spawns)
5. Give ancient longsword to Usbak, receive refined ancient sword (Usbak despawns)
6. Give mithril ore to Kinlo, receive refined mithril blade
7. Give refined ancient sword to Telin Darkforest, spawns Faelin Bloodbriar, get refined ancient sword back
8. Give refined ancient sword to Faelin Bloodbriar, receive Swiftwind
9. Retrieve shattered emerald of corruption from the plane of hate
10. Give refined mithril blade and shattered emerald of corruption to Xanuusus, receive Earthcaller

I understand that your friend ended up with the Dwarven Smith's Hammer, the item that needs to be given to Kinlo, and that he already has a refined mithril blade.

I suggest the following to avoid any trouble: wait for your friend to complete his epic so he no longer has his own refined mithril blade. Get your ancient longsword from Telin. Then, let your friend hand the dwarven smith's hammer to Kinlo. He will receive the mithril ore. Hand your ancient longsword to Usbak and receive the refined ancient sword. Your friend hands the mithril ore to Kinlo and receives a refined mithril blade. After you get your shattered emerald of corruption, head over to Xanuusus. Have your friend hand in the refined mithril blade, then turn in the emerald, and get Earthcaller.

I hope I got that right.... ;)
