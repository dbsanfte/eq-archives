Well I hate to say it but as my main is a level 58 Wiz I would have to say Mage. I love my Wiz but unless you have a group of friends to group with or can raid with him your going to spend most of your time running in circles when you want XP.

Now don't get me wrong I play 3 peeps over 50 at this time (Wiz 58, Monk 55, Shammy 51) so I have a clue as to more than one class and they ALL can be great fun to play but your ability to get groups with a wiz, frankly, sucks ass. Wizards have the worst mana regen off all casters (they get the least class specific buffs to help) and most dependent on pure mana. I just got a JBB for my shamman and she can now nuke more often and longer than my wizard ever could. I'm sure my wiz has a higher DPS but if i ever tried to use mana like my shammy i would be kicked out of groups before they said "Hi".

Mana usage/thought is tougher on a wiz, aggro can be just as bad as a shammy and you HAVE to get it once in awhile with no AC and less HP it can be fun :) Your jobs are going to be snaring, nuking, CC, EVACing, Porting and Inviser (about the only "buff" you have).

The one thing thats going to take getting used too on the Mage is no inate root. That is probably the spell i cast most often behind slow on the shammy. The pets are awsome and the stuff you can conjure is great. I see more mages getting groups than wizards.

So while I wouldn't trade mine for the world, being a Wiz is not for the faint of heart nor those that want groups
