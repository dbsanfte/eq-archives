Luclin's Arriana
All of the weapons with type 2 are LDoN weapons.

There are pre-LDoN weapons with type 1 slots, and there are a few pre-LDoN items with no augment slot at all. Fungi tunic, talisman of vah kerrath are 2 of those.

Type 6 slots most definately exist. Higher end LDoN weapons have them. Type 6 is an increase-damage for weapons, but better than type 5. Type 5 is low power elemental increases, type 6 augments increase actual base damage, and sometimes have elemental damage too. +1 disease damage is type 5. +2 damage, +1 fire damage is type 6. These are LDoN exclusives.

Type 8 is only on Time armor, and there have been (AFAIK) no Type 8 augments found, so the only thing you can put in Time armor are Any-Slot items. There's a fairly decent selection of those kinds of items. The +40 hp solid coal of prowess, or the +20 hp/mana pebble from Guk are both in this category.

People with nice old armor don't benefit as much from augments as people who upgrade into LDoN armor, but they are hardly left out in the cold.
