Boinshar
VP 2 and 3 adds pushback range, height, and root duration.

It's highly useful when you pull more than 1 mob--it's a free CC tool that can leave a mob parked out of camp, or push a mob who's already in camp out of it's melee range.

On raids, when you have melees not watching where they position adds, VP's a great tool that you can use to "help" them.

VP is an AA skill, so you do not have to wait for the 2.5 sec for spells to come back. Normally, it takes a shaman 3 sec to cast Turgur, wait 2.5 secs, then 2-3 sec to cast root (depending on which root you use). With VP, it takes you 3 sec to cast Turgur, and 2 secs to cast VP--the mob will be taken care of in 5 sec flat, instead of taking up to 7.5-8 secs.

IMO, it's best use is for helping clerics/enchanters when they need to stand there and channel. Our normal roots would not work--mobs are already in melee range, so we'd normally have to agro, then park. With VP, just face the direction you want the mob to go, and push the button.
