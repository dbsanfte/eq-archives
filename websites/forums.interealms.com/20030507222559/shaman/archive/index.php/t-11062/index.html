Way more than a blue per hour in PoP. 58-60 in 50 hours is no problem.

At 58 I was seeing 2.5 blues per hour at the hobgoblin camp in Plane of Nightmare. Get in hobs, docks or hedge and you will fly. You'll need some higher level friends to do really well at those camps, and stay away from the Scourge hobgoblin 8^)

Gloom nightmares are easy to slow, easy to tank after they are slowed, and amazingly good XP.
