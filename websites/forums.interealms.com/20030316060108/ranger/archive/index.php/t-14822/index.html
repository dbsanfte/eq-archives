Benaveriel
I thought I'd share some of my experiences with HoN, and see if anyone else has found the same.


When I first got the new spell, I was level 60. I headed to East Wastes to try it on the bodyguards. I'd never done them before, thought I'd kill two birds with one stone (see what BGs were like, try out HoN). All the dwarves were dubious to me. I harmonied 2 bodyguards (dark blue), and the other guy (light blue), no resists or immune message, then pulled the other BG. Single pull, no problem. Fought it, killed it, headed back to the camp to try the next one.

However, when I got back to the camp, the other two bodyguards came out of their tent and tried to gang up on me. Interestingly, the other light blue guy I'd harmonied didn't. It was probably about the time HoN would have worn off too.

So, HoN in this case seemed to delay agro for its duration but they still agroed. I've not seen this particular behaviour at any other time.


The other problem was in PoN. Got a full group together, headed to the consort camp (first and only time there). There were two spiders by a tree, so I harmonied one, pulled the other - no problem there. Then pulled the first one. Single pulling, just as it should be.

Then came the Consorts (one Fiendish, one Infernal, I believe), both yellow at 61. So, I harmonied the near one, no resist message, no immune message. To be safe, I harmonied the far one, rather than pulling it since the pathing was new to me, and it might have pathed through the near one. I got the same result - no resist message, no immune message. Then I pulled the nearest one. There was a little distance between them, certainly enough that harmony should have worked, and it didn't path over the other, but both came.


Both these cases show behaviour I wouldn't expect from harmony/harmony of nature, and I haven't seen at any other time. Usually it works just fine. I don't have a problem with it being resisted and agroing, but in neither case did this happen. I haven't used it a great deal, but I have used it to pull in PoI, and have used it in PoN at other places with nothing like these problems.

Any clue what happened in these situations? Is it just a bug? Anyone else found the same problems? Anyone know how to get around this?
