I'm going to have to disagree on the levels thing for HoT. It's not just levels. To do a 7 person HoT run, you need very high end equipment, and a hand-picked set of classes. If you need the drops for HoT (and I would strongly suspect that group of 7 didn't--they were probably either goofing off, or farming armor for their twinks/guild), then you probably will need 2-3 groups. I'd say 2 full groups of 60 minimum. Most guilds needing HoT armor drops won't have the uber weapons for the necessary DPS, or the AC/resists to handle extended engagements with fewer characters.

Comparing a single piece isn't necessarily a good way to go. Compare all of them. Some may have a difference of a few AC. Keep in mind that small changes in AC can have a sizeable difference when worn, as a defense bonus is included. 5 raw AC may be an actual 9 AC difference. Stats and resists are very important, too.

On the boots, notice that the stamina of +5 will be a +15 hp at level 60.

Personally, I have my wisdom at 255 for a larger mana pool (not that I wouldn't be willing to drop it in a heartbeat for more AC/HP if I see a decent upgrade). I would consider 15 more HP and 3 more wis a decent upgrade.

If you choose to go the route of the velious armor, I'd try for SS or Kael armor, not thurg if you can swing it. Either of those are substantially better than even thurg. However, I wouldn't turn my nose up at thurg either.

Some other thoughts . . . if you want to do the shawl or ring quests in thurg someday, killing in Kael will help get you faction. Also, I don't know what your guild is like, but my guild would happily boot someone that was out exp'ing during a raid. Even if you'd rather be doing exp, it might behoove you to take one for the team and go anyway. You might get a decent armor upgrade, but you are definitely building more goodwill. You may need help later (epic!).

Just my 2 cents.

I disagree with one of the first posts about a 1 group deal not being able to do HoT unless goofing around. Me and some friends (once all level 60), in a mix of hate/kunark armors, started farming HoT 1 group. It's a tough place to start, but once you've got it down we never died. Took about 2-3 weeks, outfitted all 6 of us in full SS (except robe, f silk), and made 200-300k in sold mq's from extra drops before we finished our sets =)

As for the original post, some thurg stuff is better than JB, some not, but don't forget about getting HP gear. I don't remember pieces off hand, but giving up 1-2 ac for 20-30 hp is well worth it.
Get 3-4 groups in the Arena and you'll get enough armor drops to start equipping people. The rates aren't that great if you're killing slowly with 1-2 groups, but a good sized raid can get some nice stuff in a few raids. Plus named mobs and sceptres of mastery for your monks =) Gladiator chain sleeves/legs are both nice pieces at that level too.
