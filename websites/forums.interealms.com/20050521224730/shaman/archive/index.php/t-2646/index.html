Low level SKs are easy to kill.

Buff your own MR. This is very important. If he lands darkless on you, you are dead.

Make a pet, haste it.

Mem your best heal, your best nuke, and Scourge. I forgot if you get a desease debuff at that level or not, but use sow to get out of range, then cast scourge at maximum spell range.

He would most likely try to cast spells on you, which is to your advantage. All of his spells are rather sucky compared to yours. If you can land 1 Scourge and do ~200 damage with nukes, your pet should be able to finish him off easy.

Watch out for his HT, which should do ~ 240 damage in pvp at that level.
