The posted information for the ST key on the Glade "Norrath Key Quests" is incorrect. At one time way back in the day it was but now is not.

KIll any of the 1st brood Dragons: Lendi, Sont, Klan, Zland or Yelinak. Take their talisman to Jale'Dar Shade in DN. A rogue can pick the lock with max lock picks and a mechanical lock pick set, or you can use the item from Zland (i forget what it is)

Give any (one) of the talisman's to Jale'Dar Shade and get ST key
