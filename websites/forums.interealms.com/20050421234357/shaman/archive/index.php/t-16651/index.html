High Hold Keep and Coldain are totally seperate entities...and are seperated by two expansions...so no, you wont hurt yourself by killing them, nor will it hurt to kill freeport guards for the same reason. If you mean will other cities in antonica attack you for killing guards in HHK? Yes they will..you lose quite a few human factions by killing guards in HHK.

Is it good to work on coldain faction? Sure. Then you hunt in kael and collect chain pieces for the thurgadin quest armor (need three gems also).

There is a way for magelo to automatically generate a profile of your gear, but i dont know how it works. Of course you have to sign up for an account to make this aspect work in the first place.

Finally, for skyshrine armor you need to raise CoV (claws of veeshan) faction, which you will usually get with +Coldain faction, but its the CoV faction not the coldain for skyshrine quests.
