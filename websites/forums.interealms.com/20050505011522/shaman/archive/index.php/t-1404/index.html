Well, I broke down and bought a horse tonight. Just like that write up had said, it does require a little start up to get going and to make a complete stop it takes some slowing down. You only regen with standing regen, even if you sit down with the models turned off you don't get the sitting regen if you have the horse buff on.

I tested the horse out, and I am pretty happy with it. I turned the horse/new pet models off and everything worked like the guide had said. I was getting meditation skill raises while standing =). It's pretty damn neat to see your mana bar go up while you click a mana free nuke - Thanks JBB :D

To answer a few questions for House:
1) No, you can't use the horse indoors

2)You can't duck while on a horse, unless you turn the horse models off. I turned the models off so I can duck and interrupt my spells when needed.

3)Yes, it's true, it does take a few seconds before coming to a complete stop, unless the models are off.

One other thing, the link that was provided on Caster's Realm stated you get a 150 default Safe fall skill while on the horse. I didn't seem to notice a difference at all in going down some of the hills in Dreadlands. I admit I didn't spend a whole lot of time testing it out, but this would of been a neat feature :( Oh, well.
