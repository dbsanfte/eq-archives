Tyric
Against a melee, particularly a paladin (a tough class for a shaman to beat), dont fight them in an Arena. Fight them somewhere where you have room to maneuver.

Use levitate, and get yourself up a slope then run straight out towards the downslope to get a bit of extra time to cast. Against someone you know has high DR, mem ID and IM, and cast both. Slow with Plague of Insects once IM has landed, then drop Pox and/or Plague.

With paladins, once you've gotten the spells on him that you want, dispel once with Nullify to clear out the first two slots. If he doesnt have more than one insta-clicky besides JBoots, his DOT heal (one of the main things that make a paladin so hard to kill) will now drop into that second slot, where you can dispel it as soon as he casts it. If he has a Goblin Skull Earring *and* JBoots, then you're pretty much screwed. :p

Errr...just realized the paly you were fighting was only 56th, so he didnt have Celestial Cleansing yet, but the tactic still stands for 59+ paladins. Fortunately, paladins only get Counteract Disease, so it's not easy for them to remove the debuff *and* the DOT/Slow, as long as you reapply ID everytime he cures it.
