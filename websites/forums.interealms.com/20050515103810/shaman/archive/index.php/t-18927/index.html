A couple years back, I played a half-elf warrior as my main. I transfered to Stormhammer about a month after it opened, because the guild I was in on Saryrn dissolved and I was kinda' curious as to what SH had to offer.

About a week after my transfer, I was running through East Karana on my way to Freeport for a GM event. The event wasn't scheduled to start for about half an hour, so I was giving myself time to get there by leaving my smithing in Qeynos a little early. As I'm running along, I spot an NPC that I'd never seen before... a human, running at almost SoW speed, carrying a large golden-colored staff.

I targeted the human NPC, and he con'd indifferent green. Of course, being the curious warrior that I was, I ran up and intercepted him. After a few spams of the "hail" key, he finally replied with something along the lines of "grumbles a few words to his staff", then started running again.

So, I did what any curious warrior would do in that situation... I plugged an arrow in his backside. As the NPC started to attack, I realized that I still had my +str weps equipped and started fumbling through my bags for a better blade. Pulling out my old Blackened Alloy Bastard Sword, I started to attack....

I realized that he was only hitting me for maybe 10-15 damage a pop with his staff. Pretty weak for an NPC with what I estimated at the time to have about 2K hp. I beat him up fairly quickly, and as he died, he shouted something along the lines of:

"NO! They cannot keep us apart Micah! I must have you, I must be with you forever!"

So I loot up the body, and low and behold... the staff is named "Micah". It's a 5 dmg - 40 dly 2hb... ranger only. No other stats.


I've never been able to find any information on this mob, or the staff. I used to have screenshots of it, but they were lost in the great Western Digital 40gb failure of Aught-Three. Now, my old warrior's account is finally history, and the item is gone forever.

Has anyone ever heard of this mob? or this staff? I realize it *could* be a Stormhammer specific type thing, but I'm not too sure considering that no GM event was taking place at the time of the kill.

Oh yeah, also... this was right around the release of the Jaggedpine Forest, shortly before they closed Maurader's Mire.
