Jalelin
Haste is capped.
Haste is capped in a way that works well. Ignoring V3 haste, the haste cap for the combined total of item haste and spell haste is 100%. The maximum item haste is 45%, with 36-41% being the common level of item haste. The other 55-64% of the haste cap is given to your local enchanter or shaman to make up. Haste also does not get to the point of diminishing returns - 10% extra haste is as valuable if you are starting from no haste or 90% haste.

If stats were capped in a similar way, there might actually be a point to casting a lot of these spells. If item bonuses could only get to 45% of the total stat cap, and spells had to make up the other 55% of the cap, there would be incentive to bring a Shaman along. The incentive would be bigger if the bonus for stats was consistent throughout it's range, rather than diminishing at higher levels (e.g. STA giving less HP per point).
