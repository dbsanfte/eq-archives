Ohaern Halveric
I've messed around some in Hates. Root does work , so does VP. Mobs are a mix and match of live /undead , some live mobs see invis so make sure to con 'em all when moving thru.

Pulls depend on where you set up your camp, usually areas are a mix of single pullable, and forced multiple pulls due to mob placement, just root one and split spawn like any other camp.

Ralo the black and Uumaven the Soulless are the only two nameds I've seen thus far. Ralo took me 30 mins, lot of fun, but damn the loot was craptacular " Zrathxil Splinted Cloak " forget the stats. Uumaven the Soulless was a cakewalk, had the " Ebony Mail of Hatred " in retunr for my time.

All in all it's a fun little zone, never have an company decent amount of aa for my time and some misc vendor trash / coin loot, mobs average 9 pp per kill on the troll types, luggalds have no pockets - thus no coin.

Gulu
The entrance possibly one of the worst places to solo, and might very well leave one with the impression that Hatesfury is a death trap. Instead, try heading to the upper deck. Easy single pulls, and not altogether horrible AA exp. Most of my time in the zone has been spent collecting pieces to spawn Captain Jrank. The nameds will drop some better-than-mediocre loots for your trader, although there are some laughable drops as well. This zone is entirely deserted at any given time on my server, and I get the impression that the situation may be similar elsewhere... definitely my favorite place to explore!
