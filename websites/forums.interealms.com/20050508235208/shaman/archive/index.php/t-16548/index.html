Luclin's Arriana
The NPCs in the camps are easy to find. They are in the wayfarer camps, they are not the magi, and they do not have other functions.

That eliminates the raid recruiters, adventure recruiters, adventure merchants, soulbinder, and magus. Should only leave a handful of NPCs at the camp. Hail them all. You know the right one when you get a message that says either you gain new information, or you need to adventure for more information.
Bigmeeno
Just go back to the camps you didnt get all your hails done at, find the NPC that does that, and hail them until they tell you that you need to adventure more.

Sometimes, they will also tell you to go talk to other NPCs that are NOT located in the camps. Normally, those NPCs are located in an adjacent zone, and you pretty much need a tracker to find them the first time. One of these is in WFP, near the PoK book.
