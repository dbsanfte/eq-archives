Zyphyr
A Shaman should almost never be using his mana to Haste the pet of a Mage he is grouped with.

Burnout (level 12) = AC 8, Str 12, Haste 15%
Burnout 2 (level 29) = AC 9, Str (39-45), Haste (29-35)%
Burnout 3 (level 49) = AC 14, Str 50, Haste 60%
Burnout 4 (level 55) = AC 17, Str 60, Haste 65%

Quickness (level 29) = 30%
Alacrity (level 49) = 40%
Celerity (level 56) = 50%

It doesn't hurt anything to throw the haste spell on a Mage pet (but it will cause problems on Necro pets - their buffs have stacking problems with Haste spells), but it is rarely beneficial.
