Duo + excellent, about the best duo for xp per hour.
Group + very good, snare and pet add great dps and security.

Raid + very, pet adds good dps, timing slows and malos is a great bonus

Enchanter: - Bonus mind buffs, slows, fantastic pet dps when charmin, very desired in raids.

Duo + Excellent, Crowd control rocks. Charming is just insane. tash compliments malo, stunns means pet can tank just dandy.
GRoup + Excellent, same as above except mana regen significantly increases group dps.
Raid + Excellent, massive dps with charmed pets (if somewhat tricky to control) and crowd management (chanter mezzes, you slow and malos, and point the tank at the right mob)
