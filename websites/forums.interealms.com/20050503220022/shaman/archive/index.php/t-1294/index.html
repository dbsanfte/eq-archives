Colal
Head to the north end of the zone, there is a plataue that you can park your party on, and pull to. Free of wanderers ect.

Almost directly south there is pool of lava. Wait about 5 mins and 2 GW's will eventually path to this area, just be ready to con everything.

The 3rd generally wanders between this pool and the VP zone, so it is basically a little off to the west.

1 will generally be around the VP zone, often times stuck there.

By no means is this a perfect way to find them, but even without a ranger I can pull all 4 within 30 mins using this method.
