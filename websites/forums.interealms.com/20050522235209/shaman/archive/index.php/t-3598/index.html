You can certainly change your faction to giant and then switch it back again. Is it going to be easy or quick at your level? Nope. You're not going to be able to preserve dragon while raising giant but you can preserve coldain while raising giant if you kill dragons.

You need to be killing mobs with large faction hits to swop factions quickly and that means getting the hits off bigger mobs like Dain, Yelinak, Tormax, Statue of RZ etc.
