Stormaye
On the lower end of the game...

Chanter charm doesn't really become effective until at least mid 20's. The reason is that, before that time, the mobs hit for less in relation to the number of hit points that they have. As levels go up, they do more damage, but have relatively fewer hit points. PoP is the upper extreme of this.

It's possible to solo without charm, especially at lower levels, using pet tanking, stun lock, even ghetto wizard tactics.

Enchanters are great charm soloers after the mid 20's, but there are some caveats: charm will break, you will die, you will be frustrated until you learn how to do it. Use of SoW potions or Jboots or a fast horse will reduce your deaths.

The biggest weakness that I've noticed is the lack of direct DPS from chanters. It means if you need something dead *right now*, it's probably not going to happen.

Check out Xornn's (http://members.tripod.com/xornn/) for a good primer on using chanters.

Kariina
Yes chanters can solo even at low levels and even with ghetto gear.

I took a break from my shm (on TP server) recently to join up with some friends on KB where i made a chanter and leveled up to 54 so far. I can tell you for a fact that at every level i was able to solo effectively for xp. Of course i made the chanter for grouping so i can also tell you that you should develop your group skills as well when/if you decide to make a chanter.

Animations - they make decent pets, personally i do not recommend using charm without Jboots or sow. I got my jboots solo at 51. started the AC camp at 49. The animations can tank well, just remember to give them a weapon. Chaters get the best haste in game so agro transfers well to the pet when they engage. All u need to do is root the mob.

"Healing" the pet - i just Berserker str on my pet. It has a componentless 200 hp rune and works well in keeping my pet in the fight for a little bit longer.

Charm - yes it is dangerous. My style of solo i used the animation a lot because i need to go afk. However i find that the higher level charms are quite good. If you r nervous about charm kiting get a dr00d partner and they can snare the charmed mob for u. Makes it so easy to recharm if needed.

Conclusion - yes chanters are great both as soloers/group/raid situations. they are one of the "needed" classes. very easy to equip even on a low pop server as KB. Finding spells however is another story. Try out chanter and see if you like. I know i did.

As for dire charm: Before PoP, DC was good because you could charm mobs (max level is 46 i believe) that were close to the level of the mobs you were fighting, and your DC pet could solo all on its own. It's all about command of druzzil now. What the previous poster said is most likely correct. By the time you have a chanter ready to scribe CoD, it will have been nerfed to impotence.
