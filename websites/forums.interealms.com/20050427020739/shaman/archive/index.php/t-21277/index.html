Hail,

Affliction Efficiency IV (through tribute or an item) will help alot with your mana costs. Keep wearing the BA4 bracer. VP3 will help alot - I recommend getting that soon if you want to solo alot. VP3 lasts 2 minutes and a few seconds, letting you keep a mob perma-parked without using any mana.

I recommend this for your lineup:
Pull with Malicious Decay or Turgur's, depending on your style.
VP the mob where you want him.
Breath of Ultor.
Malos.
(Slow w/ Turgur's for safety).
Blood of Saryn.
Bane.
Anathema.
Epic.
Petrifying Earth.
Then refresh Blood, Bane, Anathema, and Epic as they drop they way you have been doing. Work out the last 10% of the mobs health as exactly as you can, so you aren't wasting mana on 2-3 dots that are only running to half duration when the mob drops. When I'm paying attention, the mob dies to the last few ticks of my epic DoT w/ all other DoT's worn off. This also lets you use dogdog to help finish a mob off if he's running away at low health. Pet /hold helps dogdog from getting whacked if you get clipped once or twice during root/rotting.

Time your CanniV & do as much of your active regen during battle as you can.

Remember that downtime is to be expected when soloing in Tactics until your gear and manapool grow some.

Take care,
