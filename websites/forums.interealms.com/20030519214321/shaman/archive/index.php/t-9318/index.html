Gloriana
I have a healing IV item and it only affects direct heals such as Tnargs. It does not affect Kragg's Mending which is flagged (although I think this is idiotic) as a "complete heal" and is under the same restrictions as a cleric's 7500 point heal. I am happy with my improved healing IV item, tnarg's now heals for over 1400 (sorry, can't remember the exact amount).

Torpor is considered a buff and is affected by focus items such as spell haste (as well as haste of mithaniel) and mana preservation. It is also affected by AA skills such as spell casting mastery (10% reduction in mana cost), Quickbuff (up to 3.0 second cast with QB3) and Spell Casting Reinforcement (up to 1800 hitpoints with SCRM).

For torpor, focus effects can stack with AA skills. My Quickbuff 1 AA stacks with Spell Haste III for a 4.5 sec cast torpor. Add in that newfangled cleric spell haste spell and my torpor cast time goes down to 3.9 seconds.
