yes as a Human Ranger of Tunare or of Karana you can train in Felwithe, no problems; however your faction will not allow you to start the Trueshot Bow quest; you will have to prove yourself by wacking a few hundred orcs in Crushbone or by turning muffins in in Freeport for faction.

Doing the quest once will get you to indifferent (if I remember right) 2 times to high amiable, the third gets you to kindly.
