The rock guys are all around the area labeled Wastelands. Follow the route as depicted on the map, while avoiding the undead camp.

There are two typical group camp spots on the Wasteland side: East Wall and West Wall. Sometimes the area by the "locked" door is camped also.

Be extra careful with spiders. They are very MR, hit extremely fast, and procs stun.
