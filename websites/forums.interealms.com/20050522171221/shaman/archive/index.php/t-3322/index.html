snapdragn
F1 toggles between yourself and your pet. If you are on a mob, F1 targets you. hit it again, and it targets your pet. hit it yet again, and it's back to you. Just keep mashing F1 until you get who you want.

Iksar BP works exactly the same as the spell. It gives you 5pts of regen per tick. This stacks with everything, more or less.

So if you are standing up, you get 1 (standing) + 5 (Breastplate) = 6 plus 5 more if you cast Regen on yourself total = 11.
If you are sitting down its 2 (sitting) + 5 (Breastplate) = 7 plus 5 more if you cast Regen on yourself total = 12.

It's a huge increase at your level. When soloing, wear your Totemic BP, cast all your buffs, then wear the Iksar BP all the time (and also cast regen on yourself at all times).

If you're gonna wear just one, wear the Iksar BP.

Ikky BP: Works much like bard mana songs do for mana in that every tick you get the +hp regenned, standing or sitting you'll get the same bonus. You get (amount of regen for your race) + (modifier for sitting if any) + (amount from regen item).
