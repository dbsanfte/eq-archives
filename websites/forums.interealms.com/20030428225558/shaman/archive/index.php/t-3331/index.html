Some changes that have happend to VS in the past months (cant remember whan these went in)

His Tap goes through Rune now. So you cant use rune to keep him from healing himself with the tap.

He is Immune to slow, not a change just wanted to say it to be sure some one did. I believe hes also immune to stun.

Cripple Will help to cut his damage "some" its not encounter altering change but its something you can do to help.

This encounter is all about Damage Per Second, no matter how you get the damage thats what its about, use what ever strat gets you the most damage fastest.
