This is why I get tired of hearing about Cleric Epics... because they are no longer a "must have" item.

The Cleric Epic was implemented to help curtail the Huge amounts of Mana needed to get a raid going after a wipeout. A good Wipeout used to mean at least an hour or more before you were ready for another attempt at X-mob.

Now, myself and one Cleric with MGB can have 65 people salted and ready to raid in under 5 minutes.

The Cleric Epic was an item MONTHS ahead of it's time in terms of Effectiveness.. but the Rest of EQ is catching up to the Cleric Epic now.
