Start out at the mushroom temple. You should be able to pull single shrooms. I usually slow, debuff, tainted breath, and melee. Wasn't the fastest route, but I managed to make some plats and get to 34 here. Once you hit 34 you will shred through the shrooms (dogdog rocks!).

Once I hit 35 I started trying the tribals. They are just past the temple, near Griegs end zoneline. They were difficult, but the money is just unbelieveable!! They drop acrylia, which can be sold to players for 100-300 pp each. 4 hours of hunting should net you enough acrylia to make 1k.

I just dinged lvl 40 and will be here till they turn lt blue around 44-45.

ThatDumbGuy
I did zelniaks at about 37/38, and moved onto large zelniaks around 39, just dinged 40 and they still work okay. Work from the Sanctus Seru zone, I just buffed myself to the hilt, summoned pet, kept myself and dogdog hasted. Just run up to the zelniaks and start beating, back off at about 25-30 percent and let dogdog have it, canni, med whatever. On Large's I pulled with Tagars (34 slow), and did the same thing. The wonderful thing about zelniaks is there are a buttload of them, and they ARE NOT social at all. You can kill one and have another walk by with no problems...
My favorite spot for this was straight out east, and a hair north of the SS zone, when the path divides up. There is a semi-valley, more like the foot of a ridgeline there. Its between the forest and the hills. If you kill all those, head over the hill into a deeper, full of zelniaks. The only downsides to either are was an occasional wolf or lightcrawler add (and occasional named zelniak or wolf, which drop items for Twighlight Armor). The wolves are annoying at this level, the lightcrawlers only a minor nuisance. Names can be pretty tough if they take you unaware, I rotted them abit sometimes. Also, note that there is a huge concentration of lightcrawlers aproximately ESE as you head out of SS. I got over a yellow an hour (as a barb at 39 on larges), solo, with very little trouble. I have nice gear for my level (925 ac 1250 hp buffed)and a REIF and an Ikky, so your mileage might vary, but I think its a good spot, nonetheless. The most important thing for me that was downtime was almost nil for me (a med break about every 20-30 minutes, for about 3-4 minutes, I really just blew more mana cause I wanted a cigarette), because I get sick of medding with a quickness.
Good luck.

My experience with DSP is that at 29-high 30's Zelniaks to Large Zelniaks are about a 1/3-1/2 a blue each. It is a fast grind, especially in the area next to the Shroom temple. I never bothered with the shrooms, there was no point. Just slaughter Zelniaks.

At 43 I went back there just to see what things were like since I'd seen a number of other there in the low to mid 40's. But the exp just wasn't worth it anymore. most of the Large Zelniaks were dark blue still and the wolves and blood thirsty trap wolves were all dark blue. But you spent too much time tracking them down or waiting for the traps to reset.

Anyhow, if you're interested in leveling like made from lvl 29-late 30's, DSP is a fantastic spot.
