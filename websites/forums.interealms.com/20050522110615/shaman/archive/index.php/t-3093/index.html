Jing
He does have a PH, but killing it is a bad idea. He is a timed spawn. When you kill him, his PH spawns and will stay up for a set time (something like 8 hours or more), and then despawn and Captain Bvellos will spawn. The PH is a kromzek with a generic name like "A Storm Giant Commander" or something like that.

This is the same way Dlammaz Stormslayer spawn works. His PH is named "A Storm Giant Warlord", and I believe the kromrif in the noble house in Kael work the same, too. Verant did this to encourage a one time clearing of camps, instead of camping the same spot for hours.
