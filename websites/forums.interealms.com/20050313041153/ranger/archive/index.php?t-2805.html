This is a general alert that there are bodies, at least on Innoruuk that were rezzable before the server patch that are no longer rezzable after the patch.

Here's how it worked.

If you had a rezzable body that you didn't get a chance to rez, prior to the patch, and you logged, you may not be able to rez that body after the patch even though you may not have been logged in.

I played my necro last night and near 9pm PDT he died. I then used my 60 ranger to pull the body to the zone in to await a more opportune time for a rez.

Two hours later I logged in and /con the body and it indicated the body had 2 hours and 34 minutes left on the rez timer. It was around 11pm so I decided to camp out.

The next morning I entered the game and noted that my body was no longer in the same place I had left it the night before.

I then /petitioned and subsequently was disconnected. A couple hours later I logged back on and /petitioned again. Shortly after this I was disconnected again.

I then waited till about 7:10pm this evening and /petitioned again. This time the guide came on and told me the body was in such and such a place. That place turned out to be the spot where the body had died the night before. Very strange as I had the body pulled to zone in, had logged, had come back 2 hours later, and /con'd it again after not finding a cleric to rez it.

This miffed me so I ran down invis with my ranger and pulled the body to zone in again. When I first grabbed it pull it to the zone in this evening I noted the rez timer had expired. I also noted the amount of time the body had before it decayed.

That timer said 6 days 6 hours and 41 minutes, approximately.

I then decided to con one body near this body and noted it had the exact same corpse decay timer. At this time I logged and switched to my 38 monk because I knew he'd be where there were bodies that were there before the patch. I ran and /con'd the other bodies in the area and found 2 more with approximately the same corpse decay timer.

After this I asked aldrin (someone in the zone near me) go and /con the same bodies and he noted they had the same exact corpse decay timer. I then went into Karnors and con'd several other bodies and they had the same corpse decay timer.

I then /petitioned again asking that the gm come on and address this. I gave the guide that answered several names he could check.

Basically what I think happened is that when the brought the servers down any body that was rezzable became unrezzable due to them doing something to the corpse rez and corpse decay timers. The corpse decay timer was also about 18 hours into the decay.

With the servers down and the time the char died it would be impossible for the body to have aged 18 hours at 7pm tonight. It has to mean that they had the corpse rez timers running while they were doing the patch. If the corpse rez timer had expired then the corpse decay timer had to be going too.

The first guide I spoke with was condescending and unhelpful. He didn't investigate, didn't ask questions and made me feel like I was trying to take advantage of the system when I reported my body missing.

I have played EQ for 3 years and I have 8 chars that are 52+ and 3 of those are level 60. I would hardly care about a rez but I don't like being treated in the manner he did and I dislike seriously his unwillingness to actually investigate.

If anyone thinks they have a body that should have been rezzable prior to the patch and found out after the patch that it was not rezzable /petition in game and get your GMs to address the issue. If we all get them to take note of this then they'll have to at least admit something is wrong and fix it or in the least grant you your full exp back.

FYI, during the times I logged I /camp desk. This takes me all the way to the desktop.
