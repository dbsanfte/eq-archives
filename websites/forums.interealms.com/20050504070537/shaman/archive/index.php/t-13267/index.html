Bilei
I play a shammy(level 54) and a necro(level 60) on the same account..wait that's not what you were asking =) I only have one account so 2boxing is not an option for me. But, when playing my necro I very often duo with a shammy. Regen on a necro is like keiX2, if the shammy is running low on mana and can't canni, I can twitch as I usually have so much mana I'm looking for something to throw it at and being able to stack on the dots while fear kiting is really nice. If we have the room to fear kite, I am usually in shock over how fast we kill. If fear kiting isn't an option, the necro pet is a nice expendable tank. With the shammy slowing the mobs and the pets buffed up all nice and shiny, there isn't much need to heal. I usually do patch healing with the reverse tap line necros get if my pet needs healing and tap my health back as necessary. Splitting mobs is a breeze. FD, mini mez, root, harmshield, off tanking are all options. I prefer to duo with shammys over everything except a bard on my necro.
In other words, it's a great duo =)
