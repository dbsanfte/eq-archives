If you want to start Rain Caller, first you have to do is Trueshot Longbow quest. You don't have to fletch Trueshot, you only need to make Treant Bow staff (which is reward of Trueshot Longbow quest)
When you hand in a combined bag of materials, he gives you a treant bow staff and say something like incarnation. which means enchanted form of Trueshot Longbow, the Rain Caller.

So you go to Felwithe to meet Kinool, say to him like "what about Rain Caller". He'll begin to talk about lost bow, "She no longer exist" or so. In the conversation, you'll see that you need gold faerie dust from Princess Joleena. So you go to L.Faydark and wait for her, only to find out that she won't give you the gold dust unless you retrieve her wing back.
