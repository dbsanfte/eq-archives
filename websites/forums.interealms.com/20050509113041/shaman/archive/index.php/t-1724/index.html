Anyhow... back to your questions...

There are alot of guilds that have a pretty diverse makeup and then there are the "uber" guilds.... Guilds are typical to life, like a club.... Most are pretty easy going, but its those that are in "power" that set a tone, a philosophy, and the goals of the guild.. Just like a club... If the "owners" appear to suck(loosely used term), then the guild will suck... All guilds have some sort of problem... Its natural.... You try and find a guild that matches your goals and personality.. ie if your a skater, dont go and join the cheerleader squad.... As for "uber" guilds, well they are more like an exclusive club. Hard to get in, expect a high level of dedication, and don't have time for whiners....

There are many ways to get in... but some guilds, usually the higher level ones, will place restrictions, ie certain level, equipment, resist gear, keys obtain.. This is done to make sure new recruits have at least attained a minimum level... ie . if its a 50+ guild, dont expect to be 20 and all the members to PL you...

As for raid schedules... Most guilds post a minimal raid schedule, and the rest are ad-hoc on a daily basis.... As for pick-ups, my best success is to sit around NFP and watch for one.... Some pickups are by invite, so if you made alot of friends, they will remember you. Then again, I've had invites to PoG while I was sitting in Troll town working on potions... They simply did a /who all shaman <level> and I was the first to respond to a tell...

As a tip, not only this game, but for life.... Attitude is everything..
