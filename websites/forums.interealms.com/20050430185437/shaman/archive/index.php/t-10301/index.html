You can quite easily play 2 character for actual xp on one computer. You have to have a good connection to the internet though, like a fast steady cable modem connection. You also need to have a computer with enough power not to lag down hard with 2 EQ sessions running. Turning down all graphic options and going back to old models helps for that. The performance suffers badly when you have too many people on your screen at once. If you are hunting in small groups of people, it's no problem. I have been helping plevel a friend's mage in the hole at times, applying Quiescence then swapping to the mage to manastone down.

If you have 512 MB of ram, and a 1800 Gig processor or so, you should have no problems duelcomping with EQW.
