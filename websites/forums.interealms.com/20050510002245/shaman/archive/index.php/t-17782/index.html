Long and rambling as usual:

Well, it was a great weekend, but I'm depressed. I'm lower level than many of the vets here so many of you have been there and done that...but maybe you can smile at some of the things that happened to me this weekend thinking back to when you were in the low 50's, get a laugh at my expense, feel nostalgia, or otherwise be entertained.

The Woohoo:

First off, I dinged 54 and immediately inscribed canni3. I never got canni2 since the quest is ridiculous and I was leveling fast. Things did slow down at 51+ but I still hit 54 pretty quickly. I was VERY happy to escape the carpal from cann1, even though I still canni3 a lot. I've been looking forward to canni3 for a very long time :)

Secondly, I acquired a Soulstripper, something I'd been coveting for a while. I kept peeking at it in the bazaar and realizing I could never afford to buy one until I wouldn't need it. I have a RL friend on Xev and duoed with his 55th Beastlord at the camp til we both got one (he for his 60th druid who never got lucky on previous camps). We spent about 8 hours there...it was traumatic...enraged soul only spawned twice but with weapon in hand both times. We actually did well for xp and pp too since we pulled everything around us to keep busy between PHs....and every time that PH spawned we groaned. Anyways, Soulstripper is going for 5-7k on Xev, which I could never afford right now due to saving for and hoping for spells, so it was a major coup for me and a considerable upgrade for my weapon.

Thirdly, I did the stoicism quest. I'd put it off forever after a bad experience trying to get it at 48 (I'd just leveled, had no idea how to work the pirates, and got killed nowhere near them and lost my level). At the time I said screw it and just put it off to focus on leveling and canni3. This time at 54th things were easier (of course). I got lucky in two ways and majorly unlucky in another.

First time there a bored BL (thanks Sbasis) offered to help me slaughter the area (side note - there's nothing like having invis drop in the tunnel and building up a train of 20-30 critters to take to Dulak zone with you). After about 2 hours (and this was after the Soulstripper camp - I was a glutton for punishment that day) and many plats the flask dropped and Sbasis retired for the eve. I was delirious and went to do the turn in and finish the quest. I totally screwed up by summoning my pet first...because in my haste in delirium, after totally buffing myself and my dog, I handed the flask to my pet instead of the spirit. I sat there stunned for a while. I was thinking, oh well, torpor is selling for 22k in the bazaar and I can probably hit 60 in a month if I'm diligent. :-/

So, the next day it's late and I've got nothing better to do, and I'm pissed, so I decide now that I know how to fight the pirates (everything is easy there at 54th, solo pulls, and the plats are not bad) I'll go try for another flask. I happen to arrive just as Quintanas (51st shaman I'd grouped with before) is gating out. I fight for a while with no flask and Quintanas returns and asks if I'm after flask for just dinking around (he wanted to solo the spot for xp/plat). I tell him I need a flask. He replies that he has a flask offers to pop the critters so I can finish the quest. I readily agree so we both win - he gets to hunt and I get to finish the quest. I easily dispatch the killables and get my Stoicism spell...finally! I guess kharma of sort of struck...I messed up the quest but was able to somewhat cheese it to finish it without having to fully camp a second flask. Since I'd died once and legitimately camped my first flask I didn't feel too bad about the cheese. (and another thanks to Quintanas for his time)

Fourthly and finally, I was checking my trader this morning before heading out and doing my regular spell searches, and I found someone selling Infusion of Spirit for 1k, which is about 2k less than it's been going for forever (and I refused to pay ~3k for it). I immediately scooped it up and now finally have that spell. I've been watching this one since the 40's.

All together it was an eventful and awesome weekend for me...I really should be happy...but I have to whine and be depressed...

The boohoo:

I'm half way to 55th now and have all my spells bought and ready...except one...and that one is the 55th pet. I'm crushed and strongly feel a need to have this spell ready for 55th. I've whined about this enough in two other threads here so I won't elaborate...but as of now it's looking really bleak. My only hope is luck.

Cheers,
