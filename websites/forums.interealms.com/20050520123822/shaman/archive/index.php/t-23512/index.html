A chanter has the rune line (Arcane is a 1500 rune, no peridot cost, not a great choice but certainly better than nothing), Doppleganger, an AA which makes an aggro clone for the chanter, and Color Cloud, a 2 second cast time stun up to level 65 mobs. Now certainly chanters still dont have it as nice as beastlords when it comes to unwanted aggro (push button melee immunity every 3 minutes is pretty phat) they certainly have it better than us.
The problems that SK experience are indeed different than us at the high level, but the basic problem is the same. Their spell line up and AA line up is inadequate for the tasks that they are supposed to be able to do in the high end (certainly in the lower end game, as I have said before, all classes are more or less just fine, this really does include even up to tier 2 PoP, maybe tier 3). Lower level SK can chain lifetap to help provide a nice buffer of hit points, and give them a decent way to fake mitigation. This is mana intensive however, and will run even a nicely geared SK OOM pretty fast.
Paladins, by their own spell make up (various stun lines), mitigate better than an SK, if for no other reason than taking 2+ seconds every so often from a mobs attack cycle helps them simply get hit less. Whats even more rude is that these stuns are cheap to cast, for example Force of Akilae, level 62 Paladin spell....65 mana for a 4 second stun with a -45 resist check..which do you think mitigates more damage, that or Touch of Volatis 62 SK 280 mana for a 350 point lifetap? A shaman slowed mob with a paladin stunning it might simply as well have taken his ball and glove and gone home when he saw the two coming. VERY often if I main heal a paladin he requires just a Q or 2 every 2-3 mobs, an SK needs Q and blast heals, even given similar gear and AA.
SK have Aura of Pain, an interesting Ac drain that grants a ton of Ac that depletes over time....this is certainly NOT equal in any way to the paladin stun line, and high mana cost scaling lifetaps dont either....what good is a 300 point life tap when your getting smacked for 1500? Your better off having a paladin outright delay that 1500 hit with a stun, which by the way will also give him a total lock on aggro.
SKs and paladins in high end encounters are supposed to be crowd control tanks, using non-damage forms of aggro (stuns, terrors, Fear) to mez tank mobs while the main targets get waxed. Without some sort of increased mitigation ability, either by Disc, AA, or spell, Sk will not be able to adequately do that role without seriously being overgeared, and even over geared Knights have troubles. Paladins have LESS of a problem with this, due to having access to the stuns, but given stun immune mobs, the disparity is notable.
SK and paladins should be viable tanks for ALL encounters other than high end bosses, just as we should be viable healers OR viable slowers with the same limitations. To ignore this need or to poo-poo it as SOE seems to be doing, is not going to impress the players, its simply going to cause people to quit in frustration, or switch to an alt and level up something that is actually useful in the high end..IE anything else other than a knight or shaman (heh and monks too, but thats another rant).
