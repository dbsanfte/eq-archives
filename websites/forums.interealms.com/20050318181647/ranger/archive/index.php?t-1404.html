I hunted there actively. It's a pretty nice place to group, but often gets boring. As people have stated, the only people there are usually quad kiters. The routine i seem to use is fear kiting underwater. I guess mana conservation is the best way to go. The only two spells i have up are SoW and chloro. I have a fish bone earring for EB, Tolans helm for invis. Invis up, go scout the area, harmony, pull with snare (or wait until they're in the water and snare if you're impatient like i am), fear and keel ;). I believe chloro is a msut for this area... the only down time you'll have is medding for mana. Other than that you should always be at max hp. I usually claer 6 to 7 raptors before respawn...as for the amount of exp is usually a blue an hour or so.

If something does go wrong, you have 3 options. Die...not a very fun option, but if you are going to die..die on land. It's a pain to get looking for your corpse underwater. Option 2, run..or outswim the raptors. They are very fast, keep them snared and using tracking to determine how far away they are from you.. Generally if you're tracking them and it days " %T is behind you" they're still chasing you. Once you feel like you're OOR of them, camp out. Option 3, fire pots=) Just make sure you know which one goes where..it's interesting to pop up in a KoS city.

Chessboard is quite a ways away from the raptor isle... I leveled from 57-60 doing raptors, and here is the way I suggest doing it.

#1. Get an enduring breath item. Helps alot.
#2. Pull raptors off the smaller of the two isles. Its alot easier to get single pulls off the one.
#3. Always keep levitate / chloro / gwf up
#4. Pull them underwater, but not too far from the isle. When you fear them they will run east.

...and if anything goes bad, snare, root, and camp.

I never used harmony here, and I very rarely ever got more than 1 raptor on any pull. If you do it right, a lvl 58 or 59 ranger can keep this whole island clear of raptors.
