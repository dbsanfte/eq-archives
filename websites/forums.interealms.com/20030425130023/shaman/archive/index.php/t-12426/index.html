agrad
I have nailed this guy 3 times so far, all in the same place, between the bridge and the stairs down that dead end room. I generally invis up to the turn in the bridge, root/dot the mob there, then buff up quick and get a pet rdy. Using ER3 bracer I can always pull him single between the 2 mobs at the end of that fork on the bridge. Be careful fighting him to close to those 2 also, because if one of them is an archon, they will heal him if they are close enough.

The Enraged is a necro mob and likes to cast a lot, and depending on how lucky you are with resists, it can be a very easy fight at 58+ or annoyingly difficult.

I've parked a ranger in dulak specifically to track this guy, as he is typically one of the highest cons in the zone, so tracksort-consider generally shows him at the top of the list when he is up.

Hope this helps you get him.
