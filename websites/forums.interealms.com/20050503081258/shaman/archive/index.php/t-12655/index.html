of course, I am on two machines, and the monitors, keyboards and mice are right next to each other, so switching over is not that hard, but gets confusing at times :P

I think that since you are simply going to be using your shaman as a bot for your tank and will probably not be grouping with him outside of your mini-group it would be a good idea to have a /target *name* and /assist *name*.

you should have plenty of time between pulling the mob (with SK) and assisting, standing and casting slow with the shaman. I do it just fine with the warrior, and since you get a snare spell with the sk, you got loads of time.

also note that my canni is set to hit 3 times (still using canni 1) and it is a lot less distracting if I can just hit the button once, and forget about and focus on the mob for about 4 seconds then look for sit on the shaman.

I found that if I do this order:

tank: pull
shaman: assist tank
tank: stop inbetween shaman and mob
shaman: cast slow
warrior: engage mob
shaman: pet attack
shaman: canni
warrior: make sure all is looking OK
shaman: sit

end of fight..

shaman: root, be ready to re-root if warrior procs a weapon

depending on mobs HP, or the zone, don't worry about the root.

all I worry about as far as buffs, is quick and cloro (for me and warrior) and that is about it. rarely if ever do I have to heal.

with this method I can consistantly just pull with no downtime, this was the case in gunthrak, and this is as well the case in solb.

probably more info than you wanted, but for most fights where you will gain good XP this is all you need to do!

I would setup hotkeys that efect the other character, and rely on using shortcuts for things with the other, minus a few odds and ends.

Way I usually do it with my beastlord and when i had my shaman :

(wont post code, just functionality)
Shaman's keys
1: ASSIST AND PET ATTACK (benefit is if you dont have a pet up you still get the target) - you can also build in your /cast slow into this one.
2: /sit
3: TARGET SARJENT and /CAST 3 (heal)
4: Canni5 and Torpor/Quint Macro
5: Spell gem for torpor/quint
6: Spell gem for Slow
7: Epic
8: JBB or free slot
9: free
0: TARGET SARJENT and /FOLLOW (use this when there is a problem so I can escape easily)

One other thing to note, is ya really dont need to back off your pets (keep mobs back to it), your spells are able to be mapped to keys ( ie, hotkeys map to the top number keys, while spells can be mapped on the numberpad, gives you 20 choices)
