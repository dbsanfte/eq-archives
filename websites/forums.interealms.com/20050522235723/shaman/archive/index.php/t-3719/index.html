Chutoi
For some reason, I thought that bug had been fixed also, Jing. Have you done Aary recently? Perhaps I was mistaken.

At any rate, you don't technically need an insta-click--the insta-click are just much easier to do as they have no cast time and you can rush out and re-slow, etc. As mentioned, canna will work, or any right click item, including a JBB (if you can use them). Just duck out of anything with a cast time. As a matter of fact, even though they can't be used in ToV, right clicking Jboots, or Black fur boots (or whatever the vendor jboots are called) also refreshes the mana bar even though they won't give you SoW in ToV.

Think of Aary as doing 2 different AoE. The direct line of sight is a true mana sieve--you won't have any mana at all shortly after being hit, and it's not a refresh problem. You really are out of mana, and basically cannot get more mana until the DoT is gone. If you still have the DoT and burn a mod-rod from a mage, it will give you a little mana, but most of it will disappear on the next tic of the DoT anyway.

The other non-line-of-sight AoE is also a manasieve, but it's a wierd one. Your mana will decrease exactly the same as with the direct line of sight one, but when you use a right click, or successfully get a spell off, it will refresh your mana bar to where it would be if you hadn't been sieved. The reason everyone should have a right click, is that if you are waiting between casts, the false AoE may get your mana too low to cast something. If that is the case, you have to have some mana free spell or clickable to refresh your mana bar. If you don't, you might as well really be out of mana.

Summary: have a right click item to refresh your mana bar (Black fur boots), and don't get hit with the direct AoE!
