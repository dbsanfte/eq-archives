Okay, guys. I have seen a few posts where people are confused on flags. Here's a roadmap to get into the elemental planes. I wrote this up for my guild a few months back.

Caveat--keep in mind that flags done out of order may not show up with the seer until you have killed the predecessor mobs and then unlocked memories.

Hope this is of some use.

Chutoi



Plane of Nightmares line:
Hail elder poxbourne in Plane of Tranquility and follow script for pre-FLAG
Complete hedge script for FLAG for TT's lair
Kill Terris Thule for FLAG to PoTorment

Plane of Disease line:
Hail the guy outside the ****pipe to CoD. Ask What ward? for pre-FLAG
Kill Grummus for Crypt of Decay FLAG
Complete high priest script for KEY to bertoxx (all need)
Kill Bertoxx for FLAG (note: killing bertoxx will also get you access to PoTorment if you do not already have TT flag above)

Plane of Innovation line:
Do save the gnome scipt for factory FLAG (ONE per guild needed, so most guilds need only do this ONCE)
Kill Manaetic Bahemoth for PoTactics FLAG
Kill Tallon and Vallon Zek (no order) in Tactics--text, no flag
Kill Rallos Zek in Tactics for FLAG (RZ needed for elemental planes entrance, but NOT for Sol Ro tower entrance)

Plane of Justice Line:
Hail Mavuin. "I will plead your case" for FLAG
Complete a trial for MARK OF [trial name] (note, keep mark--get all 6 for access to the 7th Hammer, though not needed for elemental access)
Tell a justice, "I wish to plead the case of Mavuin?" for FLAG
Hail Mavuin again for FLAG. You are now PoV/PoS enabled.

Plane of Valor Line:
Kill Aerin Dar for FLAG to Halls of Honor
Complete Save the Lady Trial (this and next 2 trials--no order needed, and no flag--you just get text)
Complete Dragon Trial
Complete Save the Townspeople Trial (can zone into HoHb with 3 trials)
Kill Mithanial Marr in temple of Marr for FLAG

Plane of Storms line:
Collect 1 medallion from each of two of the three races of giants. Also collect one giant head and a beard, a sash, a bone. Give the head to Askr and follow text to get Bastion of Thunder KEY
Complete a courtyard key (one no-drop gem from each wing combined in a box off a 65 named from any wing boss)
Complete an Agnarr key (combine one no-drop sphere from each wing in a box off a tower boss)
Kill Agnarr and say to Karana, "I wish to follow the path of the fallen" (I think) for FLAG. (needed for elemental planes, NOT sol ro tower)

Plane of Torment line:
Once TT flagged and Bertoxx flagged, say to Fahlia in PoTranquility, "I will go to Torment" for pre-FLAG
Hail Tylis in PoTorment and follow script to kill Keeper of Sorrows for FLAG
Kill Saryn for FLAG

Elemental Planes:
Once you have RZ and Karana flags, ask the Grand Librarian "What information?" and it will open elemental planes of water, earth and air

Tower of Solusek Ro line:
Once you have Saryn, Tallon Zek, Vallon Zek, and Mithanial Marr flags, Hail the Grand Librarian in PoKnowledge and say What information? for FLAG
Kill 5 tower mini-bosses for FLAGS
Kill Solusek Ro for FLAG to plane of fire.

One last thing. I don't remember which line it goes to, but there are 3 flags from the Grand librarian. One you get from hailing him after completing all the above. One you get from, "What information?" and one you get from "what lore?". If you are having problems, just make sure you did all three.

Good luck guys!
