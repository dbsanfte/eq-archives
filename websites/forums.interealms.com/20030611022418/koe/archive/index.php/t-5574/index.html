All I can say after downloading and trying the Demo out (Pre-patched version)...WOW

For those who don't have a clue what this game is <a href="http://www.westwood.com">click here</a> and go to Command and Conquer: Renegade.

Basically, Renegade is based on the Command and Conquer world, but instead of you being the "Overseer" or "Commander" of the game, they throw you right down to the unit level. Much like missions in Counterstrike similar games. C&C fans that like first person shooters will drool all over this game.

In the C&C mode (Which is the only mode you can play in this demo) your mission is to take out the enemy base by any means necessary, much like an original C&C game, but this time it's in the First person :)

You have the option to buy vehicle units, drive them around and fire them, as well as being an infantry units. They even preserved the major weapons from the game as well. Ion Cannons and Nuclear beacons are in game, when they go off, you should see the show :)
