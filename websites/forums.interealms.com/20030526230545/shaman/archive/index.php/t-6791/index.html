deria
Well,

1) How Many People

That depends on the poeple. I've done it with one shaman, one cleric, and one warrior. Generally it is nice to have a ranger or monk to pull, and an extra melee class can be helpful. If your group is all <55, get more people. If your group is all 60s, you need less people to accomplish the same goal.

2) Spawning

They usually spawn at VP. If you kill the 4 elder wyverns there, the next spawn may be an elder wyvern or a guardian worm. Sometimes they seem to spawn out in the zone, but I'm not sure what triggers that. Usually the best approach is to zone in with a tracker, find them, kill them, and zone out. You can try forcing the spawn, but its awfully boring.
