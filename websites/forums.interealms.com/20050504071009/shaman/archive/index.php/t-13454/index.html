You also had bear form going. Thats another 1hp/tic. Add the 75hp from that to the other total and we get 900 hp.

If 900 hp is only 15% of your total hp pool that means that you have roughly 6000 hp. At level 29. wow. That puts all shaman everywhere to shame.

Im thinking that you were not gone nearly as long as you think you were.
