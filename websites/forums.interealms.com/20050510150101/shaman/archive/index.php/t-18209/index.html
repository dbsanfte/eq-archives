On one of them someone thought critters were pacificed and they weren't and we happened to be very near the zone and decided to flee rather than take on 5-6 critters with no enchanter (root and pet mez works for 3-4 but beyond that it's sketchy - with 3 pets and many root-immune critters). It always feels good when you face a seemingly impossible battle but win with no deaths due to remaining calm and executing well.
