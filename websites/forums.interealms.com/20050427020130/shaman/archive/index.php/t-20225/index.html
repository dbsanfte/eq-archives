Pjrell
I've been grouping in PoV since 56 and at the time, I had trouble getting slow to stick. I dont have any parses to back me up but I would bet that I was getting resisted about 50-60% of the time. I would first pull with malosi and if it stuck, could usually get slow to stick afterwards. If it didnt stick, I would follow up with a slow and hope it did :). I would alternate the two until slow did stick and there were a couple times where I pulled agro and died miserably.

Im now 59, however, and the mobs are still red to me but I have much greater success getting them to stick. I cast malosini first which usually sticks and then cast a followup slow which sticks even easier now that the mob is malod. At 59, I would say my resist rate is closer to 20%-30%.

I would say you can do it at 56 (maybe lower?) but expect to get agro and die alot from resists. It definately gets easier as you gain levels.

rookz
pinning up a malosi or malosini up on the mob on incoming gave me better percentages of slows landed with less risk over using togors and turgurs.

Well friend, I'm not sure how your getting better percentages. I've parsed this several times, and the bounce ratio always comes out the same... malXXX and torgors (and turgurs for that matter) are resisted an equal amount... two things to be said for maloXXX is that the lower versions will cast a bit faster than torgors, and you'll get less agro... Bad things to be saide for malXXX, even if the faster casting nets in malXXX landing sooner than torpor would have, it usually results in landing slow later. In PoV at level 56 I RARELY (maybe once an hour) have both turgurs and torgors bounce to where I'm up for cast number 3. If your casting turgurs, then following with malXXX, even if it is faster casting than torgors, you still have to follow up again with turgurs... this nets you alot more unslowed mob damage on the MT. Also, if turgurs and torgors bounced, then turgurs and malXXX would have bounced too... this is the only time the faster cast time of MalXXX works for ya, but honestly happens to rarely to warrent being a primary option.

Turgurs/torgors rotation will simply get it slowed faster.

Now, if you have no rezzer, or are MH, then malXXX is better simply to try and reduce agro, as dying has alot more consequences... But as MH, you might quickly get stuck in a heal loop that simply can't cancel out the damage Your MT is taking... Chain slowing will possibly get you agro, but you can survive a slowed mob till the MT gets it. If it doesn't get slowed before you die, I GUARANTEE you that it wouldn't have gotten slowed before you got stuck on the loosing end of a heal cycle, and ended up with a full whipe anyway.

Also, if your Hps are very sub-par you might want to take the malXXX route, as you might not be able to take a few rounds... But when I was lightly buffed to around 3.5k hps, I managed to slow through an angry unslowed mob on more than one occasion, and often lived to tell the tale... If death isn't going to butcher the group, then getting it slowed is more important imho. Turgurs/Torgors just does it faster.
