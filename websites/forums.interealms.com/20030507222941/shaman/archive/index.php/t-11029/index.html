Weyalan
Ok, 150 plat is not a whole lot, and you will not be able to buy any major upgrades with that, but do not despair.

First, lets get some perspective here:

At your level, wisdom is almost irrelevant. Every point of +wisdom will, at level 8, give you a bit less than 2 points of additional mana! So, getting +wisdom items is not really a high priority. Certainly, +wis does not take priority over +mana or +hp or even, in my opinion, AC.

Ok, as for upgrades...first, it is important to get something in every slot - and even if that "something" is just AC, thats fine. If necessary, make yourself some patchwork, or do the boots / belt / quests from the miller twins outside Surefall. Do the Initiate Symbol Quest from Halas / Qeynos. Even putting patchwork armor in a spare slot will make a difference to your PvE ability

Ok, once you have something in every slot, then you should head to the bazaar. There are often great bargains to be picked up in the bazaar - gear that used to go for literally hundreds of plat can sell nowadays for very little - basically, spend some time in the /bazaar window with the shaman filter going through each slot. you should be able to find a few pieces that have AC and some +stats or + resists that are only in the 20 - 50 plat range

if you want to make some good money while you are in the bazaar, farm silks and pelts and sell them in trader mode while you are looking for gear.

Personally, I would buy blackened alloy armor in preference over banded armor - similar AC, less weight and often a few +stats too. People used to buy banded for the Totemic Armor quests when they got to level 20+, but these days, Totemic armor has been superceded by so many other armors that are easier and/or cheaper to obtain, so most people don't even bother with the Totemic Quests, which is, IMO, a pity.
