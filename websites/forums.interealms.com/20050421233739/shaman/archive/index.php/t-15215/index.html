Burning Woods is under-appreciated at these levels. I moved in there with a 44 shaman and 44 monk and duo'ed the hornet's next until 47.

Check out the Hornets' nest in the south-east end of the zone. They're all warriors so no casters, nothing else wanders in there and the pp drops are pretty normal (keep the wings, they go for 1pp each, you may also get a rare spell of a scoriae hornet - keep to sell in bazaar).
