Grippa
As far as stats go, I'd say make sure your AGI is above 75 (otherwise big AC penalty), and then split the rest evenly between STA and WIS.
snapdragn
I would suggest 5 in AGI - you need to get your AGI up to 75...that's the CRITICAL level for agility. The drop in AC is huge when you go below 75.

I don't think you can put 25 in STA - I believe the maximum is 150, and you already start at 127.

I would push Wis up to 100. Yes, Wis isn't very signifigant in the *long* run, but it is free mana, higher chances of skillups, etc.

Since you are on FV, then gear is going to be a lot less available than normal, so I would carefully consider your base stats.

Final Suggestion from me:
5 in AGI = 75 (critical!!!)
23 or 25 in WIS = 100 or 102
0 or 2 in STA = 127 or 129

Deian
AGI determines your armor class. Changes in this attribute won't show significant effects on ac, however, unless you cross a threshold - like 75 and 100(? not sure about the 100).

If your naked ac with 70 AGI is 100, it will be 130 with 75 AGI.
BlargX
Standard advice for ogres is to do 5 agi and the rest wisdom.

Lot of people are thinking along the lines of uberness now and saying wisdom doesn't matter, so put every point in almost any character into STA so you can get more hitpoints. That's pretty silly for a class that relies on wisdom, and we don't get wisdom overnight...just eventually. Like, maybe in your high 50's you'll start to feel like you just don't care about more wisdom at all, because almost everything you pick up that is an upgrade will have wisdom on it anyway.

I did the 5 agi 25 wisdom thing and found it did really well for me. I can take off some regular gear to put on resist gear for special situations, but not feel like I'm going to lose too much effective wisdom(I worry more about losing the AC and HP).
