One solution is to stagger your buffs a bit.
I.e. Don't cast them all at once. Cast the most important ones first and then cast the rest a few mins later.

Also dont caste crap like +AGI buffs or +STR buffs.
Just buff the HP's/STA of whoever is tanking and anyone else who might get snotted.

Maybe even do a few cana between buff casts.

BTW It all gets REAL easy later on when you get group buffs.

But there are a number of things you can get to help out.

Focus is your FRIEND!

Get a Mana Preservation II item - I use a Black Alloy Medallion, saves 10% of your mana cost of ANY spell.

Extended Enhancement - I went with a Drakescale Belt with has Extended Enhancement II on it. It'll help make the buffs last longer (can't remember how much longer).

There are a bunch of quest in the PoK library where you bring the NPC an item from each continent/expansion to get a Focus item.

Take a look at my magelo if you want a few more hints. Personally, now that I have a few focus items and decent AC, I'm in the process of converting some of my equipment for Hp equipment to help with the Canni'ing.
