I'd stick with what you've got. The 5200 from everything i've ready would be a down grade in every way for you. It's majorly castrated as far as performace goes so it isn't going to perform any better in current games than what you currently have. In fact it will probably be notably worse considering most tests I've seen but the 5600's on par with the 4200 or 4400 for performance and they weren't too far ahead of the geforce 3 ti500.

As far as the new dx 9 stuff goes.
1) There aren't really any games that use the new features yet.
2) the fx 5200 is so underpowered that by the time there are actually dx9 games around you won't be able to play them very effectively.


As far as your situation goes I would recommend sticking with what you have unless your willing to drop down at least 200-300 dollars for at least an ati 9500pro or a geforce 5600 I would stick with what you've got.
