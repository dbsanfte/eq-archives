Hail,

I don't know if anything is actually changed, but I have heard of more Fear wipeouts than normal lately. I believe inattentive raiders who aren't familiar with the symptoms of chain aggro to be the blame.

If in doubt, it is always worth the confusion of a regrouping of the raid party after everyone camps out. ESPECIALLY when the DT golems are up. If someone calls to camp for aggro reasons, don't argue, don't ignore it, just DO IT. Anyone who remains behind w/o camping is endangering the raid force. Camping to clear aggro is easy to do.

I've seen groups of 65's to whom Fear is completely trivial wipeout because they were a little arrogant and chain aggro ate them up just like your average raid force of people in their 50's.

My spouse leads a small guild, and an alliance of that one and 2 others went up to Fear a few weeks ago with miserable results. They were doing a West camp break-in, only 2 or 3 people turned a little TOO far left and ended up heading SE instead of West. Chain aggro and one of them making it back to camp (instead of dying on purpose) wiped the whole raid. It roused practically the whole zone, which hadn't settled down when they went to CR and they wiped twice more trying to recover bodies from the first one.

Eventually they called on some uber help. Two groups of 65's all with over 100 AA's each went in. One of the groups wiped and the other evacced. Then they called in for some serious help and cleared the plane as revenge in under an hour after the CR's were done.

(Two weeks later they went back to Fear and had no problems on the break-in or during the raid. The idiots from before were taught how to turn left properly I guess. :p )

If you wipe from chain aggro, I'd send in ONE person w/ FD to check the zone. If it appears riled up, I'd get everyone out of the plane for a half hour. Send everyone on a snack break or have a nekkid player orgy/language lesson in a corner of PoK or something. After a half hour, everything should be settled down and a re-break for CR should be able to occur smoothly. It is worth your sanity and your exp to take a break.

Take care,
