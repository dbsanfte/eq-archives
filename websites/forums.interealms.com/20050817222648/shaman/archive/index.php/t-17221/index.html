Spell Haste Focus Effects for 60 - 65
click the links and see the "Items with Spell" section for information about what items have these effects.

-General
Minor Spell Haste IV (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=4234&source=Live) - 8%
Spell Haste IV (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3525&source=Live) - 15%
Naki's Superior Pernicity (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3203&source=Live) - 15%
Speeding Thought (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3124&source=Live) - 18%
Haste of Druzzil (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3526&source=Live) - 18%
Quickening of Druzzil (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3527&source=Live) - 23%

-Beneficial-only
Haste of Mithaniel (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3531&source=Live) - 20%
Speed of Mithaniel (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3532&source=Live) - 25%
Quickening of Mithaniel (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3533&source=Live) - 30%
Wind of the Nightcrawler (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3125&source=Live) - 30%
Enhancement Haste IV(buffs) (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3535&source=Live) - 30%

-Detrimental-only
Haste of Solusek (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3528&source=Live) - 15%
Speed of Solusek (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3529&source=Live) - 18%
Blaze of the Lightbringer (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3128) - 20%
Quickening of Solusek (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3530&source=Live) - 23%


I think that covers the focus effects that we can use. The other types of spell haste would be our AA ability Quick buff and the Cleric spell line.

The easiest items to get would be the Wicked Bone Atlatl mentioned above, the Ceramic Totem of Xegony (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=29037), the magician-summoned Naki's Spiked Ring (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=29799) and the Cleric buff Blessing of Reverence (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3472&source=Live). The cleric buff stacks on top of item-based spell haste.
