Originally posted by Dublizard
Ok, this may seem like a stupid question to many of you, but I have to ask: Why do so many shamans dismiss wis nowadays?

Because Kunark changed everything.

The primary function of shamans 51+ is slowing and debuffing. Slow, etc. gets you aggroed and beat on, for which you need AC to take less damage. Not only does the protected HP keep you alive longer, it is also a reserve of mana using Cann. Thus, a shaman with WIS that a cleric or druid would laugh at can function very effectively, provided they are playing like shamans and not trying to be a cleric or druid.

The change to the INT/WIS cap is merely icing on the cake for high-level shamans whose gear gives them a lot of AC and WIS.

But, as an iksar, this does not concern you. You get get "AC and WIS" as a racial advantage.
