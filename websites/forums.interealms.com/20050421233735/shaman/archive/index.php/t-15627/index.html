Wraith_DoN
You won't be able to cast sow indoors, so what you come in with is what you get. The dog distraction sounds like a good idea. Maybe try binding at a safe area near zone-in so in case you gate out you don't have to run far or zone. Full Buff your pet and place him near the corner of the turn before the 2 guards. Try and get both guards to agro on the dog, if one doesn't try rooting it. Get and pull body closer to you, decide to gate or make a run for it. The key thing is don't do any damage to either giants yourself as they will summon. Be careful of wandering arena guards on the otherside.

It would be a great idea to get an aego or virtue before you head out!

I have a hotkey called corpse. It's commands are /corpse and /loc, that way if you lose contact with your corpse you know where it is, as in some hilly terrain can make dragging difficult.

Good luck and let us know how it turns out!
