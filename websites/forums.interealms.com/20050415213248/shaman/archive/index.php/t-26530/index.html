I have MANY classes over 60; 66 chanter, 65 shaman, 70 paladin, 63 rogue, 69 necro and a few classes over 40.

Necros went from way overpowered, to unwanted in groups, to just right and continue to go up and down.

Paladins were ok pre rok, sucked in rok and really shot up in PoP when they found that holding aggro was so important.

Enchanters were GODS when PoP came out, then went back in the crapper/bot bitch once charmed got nerfed to hell and people realized that chanters were good for buffs only if CC wasn't NEEDED.

Shamans were the solo kings at one point and their slow/buffs really made them awesome. I remember NEEDING a shaman for many camps. Torpor was just so powerful too back then. After beastlords came on the set, shamans started to shine a bit less. Then slow mitigation etc. and shamans started to go down hill a bit.

Warriors/clerics will always be needed. You can't raid(anything good) without them, PERIOD. Kinda stupid, but soe just can't figure out how to change that.

All classes seem to be THE class at one point or another. The next expansion comes out and they sink back down and another takes the lead. Thats why I play SEVERAL classes.
