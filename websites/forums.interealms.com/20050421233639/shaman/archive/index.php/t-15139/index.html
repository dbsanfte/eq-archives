Noxin
1. Nathyn guards in Katta (first building on your left as you enter Katta from TM). Positive hits to various Katta factions and Coterie. Subpar loot (weapons, shields, coin), good xp. Merchant in large building in courtyard that you can sell back weapons and shields. Outdoor zone so horse can be summoned.

2. Archers and scounds behind secret rock wall in Echo. Good loot, good xp. Archers summon, but are cake once slowed, and with pet. First take out the two scounds in front of entrance. Then, from ground level, cast slow on one of the archers atop the wall. They will summon you up top to the castle, and your pet will follow up the stairs without aggroing all the nasty red mobs behind the castle facade. After polishing off one archer, walk along beam and rinse/repeat with other archer. Each archer drops 2 bows that sell to merchants for about 45pp each with good faction/CHA.

3. Chardok zone in. Chance of random drops (SBS, chokidai hide shoulders, etc.)

4. Sebilis. Shaman and wizards dispell.

A good tip is to buy or make an Everfrost spirit stone. This allows you to bind close to zone where you are hunting (for CR or in case you have to gate out). You can use spirit stone (30 second cast) to port to EF. Just remember to re-bind if you go hunt in another zone. Nothing like dying in Akheva or PoP, and being bound just outside of Seb in TT.
