The above poster is right if you are german or french (still can't get over SoE naming french server Sebilis i.e. frog dungeon :) ).

Anotonius Bayle is an SoE server and there is Venril Sathir which is another UbiSoft (English) server.

If you start on VS you can't transfer to any server but another UbiSoft i.e. french or german. If you start on AB then you can make a one time transfer to one of the UbiSoft servers (no way back) or transfer to another SoE server (USA one). I'm not 100% sure but you may have to import Everquest from USA to sign-on to AB, I think if you buy EQ in Europe it may be the UbiSoft only version - like I said, not 100% sure.

Ping time for me is good, best is around 70 over aDSL but normally around the 120 mark although lately seems to be closer to 200 average. Edit: ooops, missed one of the original questions after all that - AB is very active during European prime time as it is also physically located in London.

AB seems to have a very high proportion of 65's but I guess its the same as all other servers nowadays. A lot of twinks around but that helps if they actually group - nothing like a lvl 20 warrior in full royal velium, hiero cloak etc joining your group for fast xp :)

Bazaar is very cheap now after pp dupe settled down and still tradeskillers willing to pay for newbie area loot (silks, hides etc) although this seems to be dieing out a bit.

AB seems to have a lot maturer population than when I played on american servers, maybe a generalisation but I think european players are on average older than most USA players (for example in my guild, approx 100 full roster casual I think we have about 2 people below 16 with the vast majority between 18 and 26 and then there is old farts like me :p).

At high levels most guilds in elementals etc seem to work together on rotations with nearly all guilds at that level having a flag sharing agreement on backflagging other guilds members and I have never heard of "cock-blocking" being a problem on AB.

I'm not saying its all sweetness and light, and the odd race does still happen, but on the whole most guilds work together to eliminate the time sink of training other guilds, getting in a gm etc.

So, I can't speak for KB but AB is generally ok, the only real problem must be server-wide and thats finding groups at lower levels - I would definitely recommend you and your friend form a self sufficient duo that you can add to if needed so you don't have to be LFG if its just the two of you.
