Ok, my rough guide for AC and content (I used to parse a lot of data, these days I just aim for max HP/AC/resists) ...

Original EQ content, AC ~ 800 for sweet spot
Kunuk / Velious content, AC ~ 1000-1200 sweet spot
PoP content, AC ~ 1400 - 1600 sweet spot
GoD content, AC ~ 1800 sweet spot (since the knight tanking patch, pre patch I couldn't find a sweet spot for AC).

I currently sit on AC 1978 buffed, and I can tell you, it makes a difference on GoD mobs now (whereas before the knight tanking patch, I was still getting mowed down fast) ...
