The question of the rechargable FD ring comes up in shaman chat about once a day so I figure I'll post this info here.

Plane of Sky keys are no longer no rent. You can clear to Isle 9 once, and keep all your keys forevermore.

On your next trip to Sky you do not have to kill a single mob. You can simply click from isle to isle using invis as necessary.

This makes the Sister of Spires for the rare piece of the FD ring, or any other mob in Sky, farmable. Clear sky once to get keys, then return every 4? hours and kill whatever mob you want just by heading straight to that isle.

Also you don't need a large force - our typical group is 5 people for DT mobs - ranger ranger shaman cleric warrior. Ranger eats 1st DT on pull and 2nd ranger trueshots, I lay on thick dot aggro and eat 2nd DT. Mob always dies before the 3rd one.

By far the most informative PoSky information I've found is this site:
http://www.aotg.net/eq/plane_of_sky_guide_by.htm

Hope this information helps.
