his seems more about EQ 1 than 2. But other than that fact....

The value of items is completely based on supply and demand. And hence the perception of value in a virtual economy is largely based on availablity. Low end (read: common) items suffer from rampant deflation, and middle and upper range items hold their value so long as the perception of value is there.

There are numerous other factors that go into this of course. But those are the larger scale ones.

Common items are cheap. Rare items are expensive. And really rare items are just exorbitant.
