Crypt can be done by a 58. With no epic or JBB it comes down to a wire depending on ur mana pool and hps. The best way to get there is to skip all the mobs from the locked door to the ICGs. Just get an undead potion and run like mad after you get through the locked door. All mobs should be undead from there to the crypt. Then once you are there, you'll have to use regular invis to get past the ICGs.

When I did it I didnt make a pet for the first kill. I was afraid the ICGs would agro me if i werent invis. After that, I did though and the fight is way easier. So basicly, if you kill the duke the first round, you are good to go.

One other thing - the pesky caretaker (cube that wanders down the center of the crypt end-to-end) can see invis. So blast down there with IVU but don't go down the stairs until you check for him.
