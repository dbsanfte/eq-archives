Valsuvious
I once thought that it was a waste... but it comes down to this. Most people that play alot, find that they tend to run out of food and water alot. Alot of us use the stat food, cause while it's more expensive, provides us stats. Normal daily consuption of food and water is not that bad, however, while in exp groups and such where you will need to putting out the damage and keeping up with the mana regen, alot of us switch to using horses/lizards. Now, while this in itself is not a problem, the problem comes with the horse/lizard comsuming YOUR FOOD/WATER. I don't know exact figures, but you will run out of food/water alot quicker than if you were not on a horse. I personally keep at least a stack of Halas 10lb meat pies and some type of water on me, but it's gotten to the point where I go through food/water so fast. I have to literally restock food supplies once a week. So, using only 3AAs for metabolism will decrease the food/water that is consumed by you and your horse/lizard by 50%. In the long run, it saves alot of money that you would normally use on food.

Of course, this isn't for those that don't use horses/lizards, but I think that once you get into certain aspects of the game, you will like the extra regen you get on the horse. I know that during the warlord fight in PoEb, on my horse standing near a bard, I can chain cast both of our AE rains for just about the whole fight, using a rod and hitting canni5 as soon as I can at the beginning and everytime that I see it refresh.
