I would invest some serious time in the chanter , grouped with you slaughtering hordes of mobs in various outdoor zones, dreadlands, emerald jungle, wakening lands, eastern wastes, Trakanon's teeth, or continue your assault in burning woods. City of Mist might also be a good spot to hit near the zone. Dawnshroud isn't bad either :)

Mobs hitpoints go up a LOT as you get out of the old world and into kunark and the rest of the expansions. Honestly from my standpoint, slaughter hordes of dogs in solb using slows and the berserker series to cushion the loss of hitpoints. The xp is good and their hitpoints aren't anywhere near what kunark+ mobs have. Once the enchanter has swift like the wind at 49 your dps might be high enough to go back to burning woods and be a lot more efficcient. Also shiftless deeds is a huge boon for the enc/ranger duo. Try a few at the zone and see if you can keep up.

If you insist on fearkiting though, the above list has fearable mobs in your level range if you want some xp too :) Be warned tho, until your chanter gets a bit closer to you in level you aren't going to make great xp in your duo, her power needs to be almost as much as yours to be efficcient :)

Once she's in her 50s, I'd mosey the 2 of you over to scarlet desert and fearkite little aliens and bandit types/bugs etc, anything blue to you guys you can probalby fear out there for decent xps, and at the top of the mesa you can make some darn good xp in your 50s :) However, they hit somewhat hard, so be prepared to get owned a bit if your gear hasn't improved by then much :)
