Thorzion
You should be able to handle the first boat ok (Lady Druilia I think?). Just work clockwise from the enterance to the boat and root them before they run. You will be able to single all but one if memory serves. There is one near the mast that will bring 2; just root one and back up. Always clear toward the stern (front) of the boat first, as it makes a pretty good staging area for a bit.

Make sure to work around the boat as you clear it. That way you avoid adds and repops on you. The agro range is pretty small, so you can also med if you need to. With KEI and a temp or ageo you should be able to do fine, although a horse and a JBB would be really helpfull to you here.
uglytobone
I suppose all those other threads mentioned these things, but just in case:

1) Don't attack named or corrupted, except Gornit. You can beat the named but he will drain all your mana. Corrupteds summon and then you may also have to fight the thing that spawns upon their death.

2) Always sow yourself and have gate memmed. If you get trained, let the pet stall them while you run to a safe distance and cast gate. Yeah, getting back can be a pain, but getting a rez and getting back can be worse.

Good luck.:)
Bukbuk
As was recommend previously park your dog someplace safe and pull with slow. I recommend using your disease dot as it won't pull aggro off your dog like poison will. The seafury's don't hit very hard - max around 78 I think - but they do have a ton of hps and most likely your disease dot will finish it's cycle. If you don't do any tanking you could probably drop a poison on when it's at half and be safe. Keep root up and keep your mana up. You may get adds and will want to root rot one while dog takes out the other. If you feel like tanking go ahead, like I said they don't hit hard and when slowed they are borderline pathetic. The aren't difficult and once you take a couple down it will get much easier. Nothing to really panic about but at 49 you may want to think about grabbing a dd partner - tank or caster would work.
