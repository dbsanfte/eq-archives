I just dinged 57 in JPF huning black panthers and anacondas the entire level 56, and part of 55.

At 55 and 56, black panthers don't attract any adds, even if you path right over something else. I didn't kill them earlier, so I don't know if they get adds at 54 and below.

If I knew then, what I know now, I would have hunted the panthers much earlier. They are only slightly tougher than the Griffons, and fear kite along the same pathing.

The faction hits are Critters of Jaggedpine Forest, and Residents of Jaggedpin Forest. Your critter faction bottoms out at amiable pretty quickly. I started them with warmly Residents faction, and after killing at least 300 panthers, I'm still warmly. I've killed a few dozen gnolls and every named poacher that pops up, so I suspect thet the good faction from named poachers is much higher than the bad panther faction.

The absolute best thing about the panters is that no one else was ever killing them. I managed to find things to kill while other 56 Rangers in zone were complaining about no anacondas. Another nice thing about them was they went down about twice as fast as anacondas, for what seemed like more exp.
