cattywumpus
I did 24 to 32 in Marus Seru then moved to Dawnshroud Peaks till about 38.

The Greyhoppers are pretty easy kills since they are straight melee types and drop lots of Low Quality Rockhopper Hides which can be made into leather padding and sold for fair plat in the bazaar. Once you get your charm spell you will zip from 29 to 32 pretty fast with a charmed Greyhopper. Just be prepared to hoof it when charm breaks and your facing 2 (or 5) rather unhappy hoppers.

Once you get to 32 you should be able to single pull the patch of shrooms in the northeast corner of DSP. Nice XP solo and pretty easy to get a group in DSP (maybe not since LDoN). Not much loot from the shrooms but a couple of merchants right beside if you need them. Keep an eye on those merchants, lots of players farm the rockhopper caves for shards and flawless and superb rockhopper hides and they will dump plenty of lower quality hides that can be sold for nice plat. Other shroom patches are harder to single pull, not sure why though. Only real problem there is the jerks trying to follow the wall around the zone aggroing your shrooms.

Kriel Darkbane
Just do a balance between solo'ing and grouping. If anything, I'd tend to solo a little more now, and make sure your defensive/weapon skills are maxed when you hit 29. AT L 29 you get greater heal and are a really good group healer, so it's easy to group from 29-34 without solo'ing much at all.

Any relative amount of solo'ing/grouping is ok, as long as you do'nt let your skills lag too much. Personally, I think I'd find a decent spot to solo, and put up LFG while you're solo'ing, until you get an invite to a group.

If you want to level the fastest possible, the place is Highhold Keep basement. It's the only place with pickup groups that approaches the leveling speed of Paludal.
