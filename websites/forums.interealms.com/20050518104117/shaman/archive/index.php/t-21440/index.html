the one that spawned was a roamer that you prob killed before, which is why it spawned near the stonemite room and then ran out. If the stonemites are up, you will notice them. They are 9 in the big room, in rows of three.

What I think that you did was get your expedition from Gamesh, who is the npc that can give you any of the four different sewers events. However, if you do it this way, you cannot do the stonemite event because that is part of the progression. If you want to be able to get to the stonemites and have them up, you must do the trial by getting it from the High Priest in Barindu.
