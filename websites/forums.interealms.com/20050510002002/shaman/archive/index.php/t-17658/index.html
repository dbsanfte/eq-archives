Ramma2
Found Nedaria's Landing. You can get to it through Jagged Pine forrest, the zone in is in the north eastern part of the zone, across the river near a bunch of water ladies.

Inside, its a forest type zone, with a few rivers and waterfalls. Its sparsly populated with various animals, all green to 65. I killed a bear that agrod on me when invis dropped, no faction hit, died in 2 ticks of Blood + melee.

Theres a large cliff that divides the zone, only way up it is the small grassy hill in the northern part of zone.

There's a wayfarer camp, with Morden Rasp and one other guy, they elude of things to come, but offer no tasks or quests.

Large ocean, will be for the boat that will most likely sail to the new expansion.

Might want to check it out with a lowbie, see how good the exp is, otherwise no reason to come here at this time. Next post will contain map.
