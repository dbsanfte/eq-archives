The Spectral Courier spawns in 2 spots.

1) Looking West into the West Stable: He will spawn in the single room on the left side that has 4 spawns inside it. He will be the last one (at the back of the room).

2) Looking East into the East Stable: He will spawn on your left side, in the room that has 2 spawns. I think he is the back spawn in that room as well.
