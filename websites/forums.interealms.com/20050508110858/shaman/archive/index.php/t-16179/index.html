Jtun Nahseer
From my guildmate:

This encounter was introduced with the last patch. Basically all evil races in LoY worship Innoruuk and the Luggalds, a race of fishlike men (very similar to Kua Toa from AD&D for those of you who played) are his creations. In the Crypt of Nadox there is a new mob that spawns in the underground incubator/temple area called The Luggald High Priest. In this room are 5 Luggald Apprentices and some other warrior mob luggalds. All you have to do to trigger the event is start killing the Luggald Apprentices and eventually the High Priest (whom you do not kill as he despawns with the summoning) will call out to Innoruuk for aid and Innoruuk himself from the old Plane of Hate will spawn and try to kill you for defiling his temple. The first time we did the event we left 1 apprentice up to account for med time before the fight and Inny spawned anyways. Last night we cleared the room rather quickly and waited about 5 minutes before Innoruuk actually spawned to kill us. I'm assuming they're giving you some time to med up after clearing the room. We were so leet and stuff we didn't need it :P. Innoruuk himself is exactly the same spawn from before the Plane of Hate revamp. He's L70, slowable, is a Wizard and uses AE dispel/100pt dd Grav Flux. His loot table has been upgraded and changed to make the event more appealling. He drops Veng2, FT2 and 41 haste items as well as some Ext Range III and Mana Preserv III stuff. Not VT drops but many of us can still upgrade from them and similar to what would drop from the Rhag Cycle in Ssra. We killed him with 12 people the first time and 14 people last night with a couple bots. Each time between 3-5 people died but he goes down pretty easy with groups of L65s in VT gear. His respawn time is 3 days.

Enjoy while no-one knows he exists.
