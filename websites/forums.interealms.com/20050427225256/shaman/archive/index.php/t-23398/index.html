Hail,

These are the useful AA's for soloing:

VP3 - Allows you to keep most mobs perma-rooted for zero mana.

Critical Affliction - Increases DoT damage through crit ticks. More damage for same mana is good. ;)

Spirit Call - Speed bumps the dogs may be, but they can save your life. :p Advanced Spirit Call dogpack puts out some decent DPS.

Quick Buff 2 - Let's you cast Quiscence significantly faster, which can make a big difference on how often you are interrupted while being hit.

Pet Discipline - Allows you to /pet hold, letting you single pull mobs easier using pet pull strats.

Radiant Cure - zero mana to cure many detrimental spells w/ rapid refresh. Great for getting rid of stuff in combat without having to bring up Disinfecting Aura or Remove Greater Curse.
