What I do is - for a raid, I bring up the window when I need it and close it down for the most part.

For buffing people, I mostly use /rt (reply tell) for targetting. They send me a tell asking, I use /rt to target, easy peasy.

The other way to target is using /target Theirname (/tar for short, and you can shorten their name too - first 3 letters usually does it). I use this also, particularly when I've got a more recent tell than the one asking for the particular buff.

I believe there's a way to toggle /rt back through recent tells, and not just go to the most recent one, but I haven't been bothered enough to work it out yet, I'm quick enough at typing "/tar name".
