So, I was playing this weekend (at my lowly level of 9), and I got a mission to look into the Lost. Most of you have probably seen them before; I hadn't. I run around in King's Row for a while, trying to find some, and eventually make my way over to King's Garment Row or whatever, where I find myself some Lost. As I'm fighting a group of three, 2 scroungers and a Head Rifleman, I found myself thinking, "Dude, I *want* that guy's plasma rifle! Why doesn't the assault rifle blaster power look more like *that*?" Then I ran into a Head Swordsman with plasma buster sword in hand and stop sign breastplate, accessorized with tires for shoulder plates. These guys are awesome! I wish I had a screenshot.. Maybe I'm just making a big deal over nothing, but this is easily the coolest "mob" set I've run into in a game.

So, anyway.. I guess i'm just curious what your favorite villian types are, and what things you might like to see in CoV (just wish-lists, obviously we don't really know what they're doing with CoV yet). I for one would like to see some of the Lost textures made available - I really want a Stop-Sign for a chestplate.. I mean, really really. *grin* And I've not even gotten to the later stuff; I can only hope that higher up villians continue to be as cool.
