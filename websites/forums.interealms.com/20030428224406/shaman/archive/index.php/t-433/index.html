Some backround info:

A week ago my little brother and his friend took part in an Finnish Television show called Far Out. The format of the show might be familiar to you by some other name, at least I know that it's shown in other countries too.

Every week 4 pairs take part in this show and based on the studio audience votes one of these pairs wins a trip to some european country. On their travels they need to perform some silly tasks like playing rugby in Poland, selling ice to eskimoes, guiding japanese tourists in an unknown city and so on. These tasks are videoed and shown in next week's program.
All the time there's 2 competing pairs out there travelling, and based on these video's, their travel reports and audience votes people can vote which of these 2 pairs will continue travelling to some other city and do something different, and which one returns back to Finland with a comfortation price.
In best case people have spent 4 months travelling around europe for free and had some fun on the way too, and that's what I would like for my brother too.

Now my brother and his friend won last week and at the moment they are in Bristol where they need to do a Robin Hood'ish deed, and take part in Rugby game. The other pair is Paris at the Asterix park doing something different.
I have mobilzed everyone at my work, everyone I know, and everyone at my friend's jobs, and my whole guild, to vote for my brother and his friend so they could continue travelling and having fun. I was thinking that it wouldnt be THAT lame to ask help from this board too. Afterall what are communities for?

Of course this site is written in Finnish, so I'll add a picture so you know were to click. The idea is that the audience can decide which pair will be dropped out from the competition and sent back to home, and who gets to go on travelling for free.
