Ospri
If you're describing the spot I think you're talking about, then you were close to the Broodling...

From that point (with the slippery ramp up to your left, and the 3-spawn ahead & to the right), take the walkway back towards the Crystal Eyes spawn. It will branch off to the right before it gets to the slippery downwards part. There's a couple spiders along that right-hand branch that you can't see from the ledge; one of them is the Broodling or its PHer.

From the ledge and up the ramp on the top floor are the Crystal Fang and Frenzied Velium Stalker. The Fang is one of the spiders in the first cluster as you hit the top of the ramp. Just kill all of them, because you'll want to clear to the Stalker which is one of the spawns on the walkway over the Crystal Eyes area. I used to just go up to the other side; there's a safe point over there you can pull to and you don't have to keep running up that slippery ramp, although they've recently added a hunter along the path to get to that one.

Hope that makes sense; a map would be a lot easier :P
