Dandi
Kinring - if I understand your question correctly, yes. Any recipe has an equal possibility of improving your skill (in alchemy as well as other tradeskills), provided it is not already trivial of course. Titan potions will get you there as quickly as any others that are trivial above your current skill. However, at 10.5pp per attempt, Titans are still more expensive than quite a few other of the potions that trivial up to 96. Aura of Heat and Aura of Cold both cost 8.4pp per attempt, with several others of the same cost clustered at nearby trivials. See the EQ Traders alchemy prices (http://www.eqtraders.com/prices/alchemy.htm) for more details. :)
