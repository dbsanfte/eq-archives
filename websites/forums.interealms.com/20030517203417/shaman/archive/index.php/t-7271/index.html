Since Kirn attacked, you're doing fine. They say that a good way to check your faction after Neh, is to ask "what spear of fate", and see if the spirit gives you the answer. So I've heard. At least, he answered for me.

The Reaver raid itself can be done very fast with a high powered group of 60's. There's no need for a "break-in", if you have a high level puller who can pull them out of the castle to the gazebo area.

Assuming you already have the tear, the whole Neh/Rak thing took for me, about an hour. Part of that was getting everyone there and ready.

I had a wonderful 60 necro who pulled, she somehow was able to get past the door without a rogue, so we didn't need one. We had 1 60 cleric, myself (60 shaman at the time), 1 60 necro, 1 60 mage without epic, for ghiosk and all the other reavers, up to popping neh and rak.

For neh we had the addition of a 56 cleric and a 56 mage.

For rak we had another 56 mage join us.

So in total, we had 7 people. Now with the modrod change, you'll probably need more clerics. But there's no need to break-in to the castle... I've found reaver bits for the shammy epic go so much faster if it's a group of people doing it, as opposed to a guild raid. Once it's a guild raid, you end up with 10 druids and rangers getting in the way, making everything more complicated than it needs to be.
