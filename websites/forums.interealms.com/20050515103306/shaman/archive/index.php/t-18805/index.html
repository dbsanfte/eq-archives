You do need to watch out for those wandering guys too... they just hit too hard!

Heres a tip; If you are facing the water in Dulak, on the extreme right where the shore meets the pavement there is a small nook. It appears to be out of the regular pathway of wandering mobs and while there were 2 (I think, its been while) that pathed close, they did not agro while in this spot. Park the dog near there and just pull the mobs back to that corner.

EDIT: Maybe I should be more specific as to what water you face, as I jsut remembered that there is water on 2 sides of the zone.

When you zone in, follow the wall sharply to the right all the way to the waters edge. Its right where the pirates spawn; most of the shaman I know that did this quest pulled to there as well.
