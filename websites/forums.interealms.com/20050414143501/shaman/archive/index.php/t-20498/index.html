It Works!

It took me a few hours last night, but I finally hunted down an updated driver for the Mobility Radeon GPU in my laptop. Turns out that ATI doesn't support these chipsets very well since they are put into laptops made and configured by various different manufacturers. Thankfully I have a Dell and they keep their drivers up to date. So I checked Dell's site again, and this time there was an updated driver with DirectX 9 support (wasn't available last time I tried to upgrade to DX9). EQ is up and running and pretty well I might say. I'm experiencing far less video lag in low/med population zones. Large tight groups of people are hell for it (MGB's in PoK) but they have always been that way. I can even turn on the target ring and some of the environmental effects without dragging.

All in all, I'm pretty pleased.
