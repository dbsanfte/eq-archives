Alandrel Mesmer
You can make the curved blade portion of the mistletoe sickle using only 2 emeralds, 1 mistletoe temper, 1 curved blade mold, and 1 celetial essence. In addition you need to turn one of the emeralds into essence of tunare which requires a tunare druid or cleric to imbue the emerald, a jar of acid, and an additional celetial essence.

After all is said and done this will take you to about 188 trivial level but it will cost you about 37pp per try.

And keep in mind the curved blade itself is useless without the hilt and pommel. The hilt requires a ruby in place of one of the emeralds and a hilt mold. The pommel requires a sapphire and a pommel mold.

Combining all 3 of these to make a sickle is not trivial at 250 skill and the success rate is about 1 in 3 tries at 250 skill.

eqtraders.com has all the recipes and sub-recipes.

Not a way to skillup for the faint of heart. A good estimate on using sickles from 188 to 250 is about 900000pp.
