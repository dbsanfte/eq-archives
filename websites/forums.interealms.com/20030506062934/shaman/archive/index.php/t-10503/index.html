Solariis
Maybe some of you know about this already but I had to get this info from an SK board. This is the easiest cash in the game. IGs are always camped so this is a nice alternative when you need funds for KEI, alchemy, etc.

The place: The city of Felwithe
Target: guards

Mostly light blue and green to me at 59. Every one of them drops a two-handed sword which sells for 20pp with max CHA and also 1-5pp of cash plus gems. They can sometimes be surprisingly resistant, might take 2-3 malosinis sometimes before I can slow but most just take 1 malosini. Even after a malosini root either gets resisited or doesn't last long so wait for pet to build up enough aggro before dotting.

They heal themselves a lot so dot heavily and it helps to enable alpha pet. With merchants nearby I had no problems finding an endless supply of rusties for puppy. Pull guards right to the zone edge so you are not in range of another, real pain to have two paladins healing each other.

Downside: You lose faction with Emerald Warriors, Merchants of Felwithe and Merchants of Kelethin. Emerald Warrior faction can be fixed by killing orcs in gfay. The other two factions I won't lose any sleep over.

Cheers and enjoy the loot off those rich high-elves.
