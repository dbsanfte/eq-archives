Hey everyone,

I just wanted to get your thoughts on the Proposal thread and what you thought went well and what could have gone better. Right now, we are at 350 unique responses, 348 in support.

* So many people, overcoming the huge obstacle of shaman versatility and coming together to try to find consensus
* Changes to the GoD cannabalize spell suggest that they heard us
* The poll was useful to narrow down our areas of focus

What didn't go so well:
* Posting to TNZ, I'll just leave it at that
* Brenlo did not respond to the emailed proposal
* No response from the other two SOE feedback email addresses
* No SOE representives posted here, even if not to agree/disagree with us, just to let us know that they are listening

After years of saying "Why even bother to try to come together, we're so different, we all play so differently, there's no way we would ever agree on anything," I thought this attempt was extremely valuable and positive.

I'm interested in your feedback. If there is a future effort, how can we be even more effective?
