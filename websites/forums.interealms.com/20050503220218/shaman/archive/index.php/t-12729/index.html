To the best of my knowledge you need everything short of sol ro to get in to the elemental planes other than fire. And then Sol Ro to get into plane of fire.

As far as your specific issue it sounds like you didn't compleate the flag after your grummus kill. You need to go back and hail the guy outside PoD. He should then recognize you killed grummus and tell you to go visit his brother. Go visit the brother in the sick house and follow the prompts and you should get another flag. You'll then probably need to tell one of the other people in the sick house that you will go to torment and unlock a bunch more flags.
