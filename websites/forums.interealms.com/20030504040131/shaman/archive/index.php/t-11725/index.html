Bartmeista
counters are actually fairly straight-forward as far as how they work, which is as follows.

1) mob_047 casts Togor's Insects on your grp's tank
2) your grp's tank tells the grp, "gdi i'm slowed $*^@"
3) at this point i still use SS greaves which has Abolish Disease as a clickable effect so i target 'em, click, and wait for a 'cure' msg.

this works because if you look at lucys info on Togor's Insects (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=507&source=Live), note the "Increase Disease Counter by 12" portion. this means the debuff will remain on the person until a) the debuff wears off as its duration expires, or b) we remove 12 Disease counters.

note that Abolish Disease (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=98&source=Live) removes *36* Disease counters, so we definitely get a happy tank once cast.

the same applies for other debuffs, curses, etc. you get the counters on ya, you gotta get the same amount off to 'cure' yourself. :)
