Landragoran Heronwing
HoT is now an accepted stop off in a player's route to uberness. It's a time sink for a guild, whereby you must pharm armor for everyone. Once that's done however, you're well enuf equipped to start harder challenges like the ST key dropping dragons and vindy/tormax/statue that can also drop ST keys. ST weapons make your DPS rise as a guild to the point where you might be able to gank Aaryonar and gain access to NToV. After you manage this, Lord Vyemm is your next challenge, to prove that you deserve to be there you need to kill him. Once you've managed all of this, pharm NTOV as much as you can to get excellent upgrades for the whole guild. Head to Luclin on off days and research the AoW fight. Kill CT whenever you can.

That's pretty much the path my guild has taken and I've been with them the entire way starting from HoT. Oh while yer doing HoT you should also be doing Dozekar as much as possible, and HoT named drakes for symbols. The Doze quests are excellent, and many items are better than NTOV quality. It's a slow slow way to upgrade a guild, but at least some of you will get *nice* upgrades during the long boring HoT pharm.
