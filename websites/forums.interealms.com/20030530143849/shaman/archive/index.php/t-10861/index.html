Rate of Weapon Random Process
Myrrh Imbris and Ruca Bearjumper, Lumiere Divine; Zebuxoruk


Objective

Answer: Is there is a common proc rate, among weapons of the same Type / Skill or Delay? Does proc rate scale to delay? Would hastes of slows affect the delay that proc rates may use?


Summary

There appears to be a common¹ proc rate by weapons of the same delay; type does not seem to matter. Proc rate does scale to delay. Hastes and slows have a great impact on proc rate.

¹not absolute. As from the Cleric high level summoned Hammers, it is strongly evident that weapons with the same delay can have different proc rates, though I have not tested this to confirm yet.

Delay * 0.0022 gives a good estimate of proc rate.


Contributers

The Shaman of Lumiere Divine; Agile, Delve, & Kolisari of Lodestar on Zebuxoruk; and Seyyla Snackwalker.


Notes

Verant has said that Dexterity affects proc rate. For these tests I am assuming that it is the only stat that does, so Dex is kept constant (at 255, it is also assumed that lower Dex would result in lower proc rates; this is not covered at the momment as I'm just comparing the rates to one another).
