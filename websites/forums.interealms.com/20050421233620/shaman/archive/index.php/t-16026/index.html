POWERLEVELED: (Written as level 40+ characters getting EXP with a 60-65.)

This is *not* a guide for people who rely on their horses/lizards. I have always loved dungeons, so this is where I EXP or PL my alts. This is also not a place to PL if you're not willing to enjoy a challenge or a good crawl.

Tactics:
1.) Using a 65 Paladin, who has no problem healing himself here. Pick up KEI on the Paladin. This can also work if you have a Cleric or Druid to PL you, although it may be a little slower. Shaman/Cleric/Necro/Etc...

2a.) With Knight: Shaman slows, sometimes Malaisements first, on incoming, while Paladin tanks; can also nuke for slightly faster kills.
2b.) In case of Druid, Cleric, or Necro doing the PLing: root, slow/malaisement, DoT, tank, get healed.

3.) Remember to always root MoBs at 24%ish unless you have a snarer.

4.) These are not generally loot camps, but you can still walk out with enough for some spells.


The Hole:
Why? Because it has a 25% exp modifier, and can get you exp just as fast as any PoP zone if you want to risk going deeper in.

Zin: 40 to 45/48.
Pulling from Zin to the drop off, including the 1 or 2 wanderers and the Golem and Crusader at the base of the stairs, will net you a whole yellow at 40, 3/4s a yellow at 43, 1/5 yellow at 45. Consistently, every 16 minutes, barring race penalties. At 45 you can either start going in deeper (I recommend the jail area, which can be loads of fun) or move to the next PL spot. Remember to Maliasement the Golems. Enjoy your IGS if/when they drop, as well as various other Withered Leather/Loam Encrusted gear.

The Jail Area: 45 (with a good Knight/Necro PLing you) to 55.
I love this spot because not only do I get good EXP on whoever I'm PLing, but because there's the off-chance you'll spawn Slizik the Mighty in the jail cell--leave the named rat alone and count it as a strike against you--that drops Idol of the Underking, which can still net you 40k on my server.

The Crawl: (For those who want a challenge.) 50 to 55.
Unless you have a gater doing the PLing, or you're PLing someone who can't gate, learn to love Thurg Gate Potions or 10-dosers if you plan on getting out. Start at Zin and begin clearing down to the Jail Area. If you kill fast enough you can clear the Jail and walk back to the drop off unmolested. Turn the other way and go deeper in. I wouldn't recommend going *too* deep, as CRs can be a pain. But if you want a good challenge to keep you on your feet, this is it. There will be 2s and 3s because of pathing and wanderers. I do not recommend going further than the second room (draw a line between E and F on this (http://www.eqatlas.com/hole2map.html) map).


Sol B/Nagafen's Lair:

Bats: 45 to 50.
Sonic Bats are your best bet until 50. The EXP goes fast, the bats die easy, and if you buff MR you never have to worry about their stun. I tend to use my Paladin for PLing, although I do sometimes bring out the Cleric, depending on what's needed. Bats will start slowing down at 50, at which point you can start taking "The Walk."

The Walk: 50 to 58/60.
The Walk is the trek from LBugs (Lower Bugs, right beside the Sonic Bats) to Effy (Efreeti Lord Djarn). Clearing LBugs is easy if you can single pull them. I always pulled from a distance using a low-level stun. When I didn't have a Chanter helping me with slows I'd use stuns to mitigate. If you're PLing as a Shaman, this should be cake for you. If you're PLing the Shaman, and you're not a Knight/Necro, you might want to consider bringing a friend along for slows. The EXP is hard to beat outside PoP. You'll be receiving AA, albeit slowly, and they'll be making fast progress on the trek to 60. if I remember correctly from when I did this with my Cleric I was getting a full yellow from LBugs to Effy's room (sometimes including Effy and a GEB or Ring drop) at 55 with a Paladin and Enchanter grouped. When you've cleared Effy's room begin heading back to LBugs for the repops, or to clear them. Rinse and repeat.

58+, you can sart doing the Planes for exp, but I do not dual-box/PL there, so you'll have to get advice on that elsewhere. ;)
