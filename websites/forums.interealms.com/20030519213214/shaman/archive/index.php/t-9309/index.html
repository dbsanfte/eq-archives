Shabura Naarteban
I love the item. The stats are good, and on Druzzil Ro its rare enough to still attract attention when people note you have it.

I do use the effect from time to time. The duration sucks, but its free, so all it takes is time. Its not a raid buff, imo. But I do use it in xp groups. Mage pets (heck, any pet) with a proccing weapon love Carnage. I always keep it up factioning in Droga, and often use it on my pet in normal groups so that I can clock Carnage to the wear-off message from my pet. As for players, I'll usually tag the group's MT with it, or some other damage dealer like a rogue if the MT has Primal/Prismatic. Its not something that I am necessarily dedicated to keeping up ALL the time, but if I've gotten the mob slowed and have enough mana ... I'll click the cudgel.

Besides, the quest is an achievement and a matter of pride. Even without the effect, I'd be glad that I have it.
