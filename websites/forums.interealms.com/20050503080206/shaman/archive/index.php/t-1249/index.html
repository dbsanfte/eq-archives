Actually, the best way to do it is to do the main quest from Trade Commissioner Henry to Fungus Grove a few times, then do the 3 lesser quests (Coglixar Xorlopodil for House Stout, Daloran Glimmerblade for House Fordel, and Beriol Talkorakoir for House Midst) at the same time. ALL of them take you up to Katta Castellum eventually (though the House Stout one involves a few detours), so why not combine the 3 trips up there into one?
