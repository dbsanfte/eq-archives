Jinheim
Just a few general brief things, since my other post is extremely long.

First off, how are your resists? If you are resisting their spells as much as they resist yours, then you're fine. If not you will probably lose no matter what you do.

Second of all, your main strategy against casters and priests is going to be to just outlast them. I don't know how my gear compares to yours, but with about 4k unbuffed HP, I don't feel there is any caster or priest class that is really a threat to me. It will never be like "surprise you're owned!" because they burned me down. Just heal yourself and eventuall they'll go oom, then its only a matter of time, because you get mana back quicker than they do. Necros are an exception to this, but I don't feel like writing any more detailed guides unless you specifically ask.

For melees, your main strategy is going to be to use a DS potion and nukes. Against melees, as long as you have good MR (to avoid snare/root), you can just kite them. If you are starting to go low on health, quiescence yourself and run away. It takes forever but eventually you'll wear them down. Paladins take the longest because of their self elixer and the fact that most of them have a weapon that procs heal, but shadowknights are the most dangerous, because they have near unresistable lifetaps and of course, a harmtouch that takes off 75% of your life. To beat an SK you have to keep yourself quiescenced constantly and kite constantly to make sure you don't drop low enough they can kill you with a harmtouch. Pretend 75% is really 0%, and you've only got 1000hp. I've pulled off a few wins against SK's with full AA HT's, but it isn't easy or fun.

Just heal yourself in the beginning when he's trying to burn you down, and he'll go lom before you do. After that, just try to build your mana back up until you've got enough to burn him. Spirit call really saves the day against casters, because they've got two choices: Stand and get their asses beat (our pets will really hurt a caster, whereas they don't do much to a melee) while trying to cast on you (which in a necro's case means heal themselves), or run around and kite your pets while getting nuked (which means they can't heal themselves).

Being on a PvP server for 3 years, I have seen everything. Everyone that posted has the right idea. We cannot PvP like we used to be able to.
Spells...

DOTS - TOTALLY useless now. Since the average person resist (on a pvp server) is at about 200 now, forget about hitting someone with a dot, even with Malos.

HEALS -If you have spent all of your AAs on healing spells, you can keep up with blasts from most classes, unless they critical. That is until you are oom. Quiescence can and will be dispelled.

PET - The little bit of dps it does on pvp isnt worth the mana it takes to keep your pet alive when it is attacked by someone.

MELEE - A joke, unless you are after a pure spell caster. But they can usually regain their casting and recast dmg absorbing shield on themselves.
(I use the Sharkspine Club with a 125dd augment proc, and Dark Mace of Thought when I am lom) = not too bad against a spell caster.

POTIONS - flame shield potions are very usefull against many melee characters until it gets dispelled.


Only things we can do against classes =

Melee class = let our flame shield potions do most of the work and hope we dont get interupted when you heal yourself. (rangers = you need to stay very close to them so they cannot use their bow)

Caster class = melee them, bash them when they cast. And try to keep up with your heal when you get hit with DD.

TOTAL = shaman are not a very effective tool when it comes to PVP anymore.
