Bustyn - Been a bit since your question but to answer it, there is no "standard" level or debuff layers on whether a mob is slowable or not for this list. The shaman reporting could be a level 60 shaman with Malo -> Malosini, Chanter Tash, Bard stacked Tash, and OOS, or a level 1 shaman with no debuffs, or anywhere in between.

I would venture a guess that most of the mobs reported slowable that are 60+ take Malo, then Malosini, plus tash and probably OOS to slow reliably, and even then may take mutiple tries for a level 60 shaman though.

That doesn't mean you can't slow the same mobs, but without the levels and debuffs you may well die before you can get it to stick ;)
