Bigguss
I think you're worrying over nothing. :) 138 wisdom is just fine (it's about what I have at level 51, and I consider myself fairly well-off gear-wise.)

If I were to change anything for under 1k...

Swap out one of your RMBs for a CBB (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=809) (chipped bone bracelet). Same stats (minus the 4 str) and you get "Affliction Efficiency II" (25% less mana to cast any level 44 or lower DoT - I still use it for stacking VoS on top of EBolt.) You should even make a bit of cash on the swap.

You might want to think about a BA Medallion (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6020) (I say "might" because that Innoruuk piece is nice!) Stats aren't bad (ac 6, +5 wis, +5 int.) You'll also get "Mana preservation II", which will cause all spells you cast under level 45 to cost 10% less. That's a great effect... especially since you've got 14 levels to go before any spells you cast won't work with it! This should also be pretty reasonably priced (few hundred PP.)

That's about it- your gear looks pretty good... not much to upgrade without blowing big plat. Well, I might swap out the charred guardian shield for a clay guardian shield (ac 25, +50 hp, few hundred pp) - but that's taking you in the direction of less WIS- which is not what you say you're looking for. :)

Make sure your defense is maxed- it's free AC!

You're getting close to the stage where incremental gear improvements are going to give you a lot less bang for the buck then just putting money away for the big stuff (JBB, Horse, Fungi staff/tunic, etc.)

Good hunting!

P.S. You might want to check out this thread (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?s=&threadid=2439) for a few ideas on shaman legs (most of them are too expensive, but there are probably a couple nice reasonable suggestions buried in there.)

BlargX
The AC 2 hp 35 mana 25 ears are much better than the forest loops. Forest loops at your level give you in the high 20's of mana, and the others give you 25 mana plus 35 HP at what, the loss of 1 AC and dex 3? Easy choice.

I'd go with rygorr legs.

I'd also get used to having to carry backpacks full of alternate gear. You might have to do a lot of earning to do that.

At 52, I soloed with 120 wisdom and did fantastic. But grouped, you want to get more wisdom. People will need your heals, and you'll have to make some sacrifices. Other times, you'll need some resists -- there's always a sacrifice waiting to be made somewhere.

My shaman at 60 carries 3 bags PLUS full of stuff -- one bag is full of blue diamond resist gear alone. Then there's the froggy crown for fire/cold resist, etc, etc. Pre-epic, he carried a dark ember for fire resist and a spine chill for cold resist.

You already have the ikky BP, which is incredible at your level. My guess is you are seriously kicking ass. I'd recommend you solo constantly until your defense and all your weapons are maxed -- get a 1h blunt too. At your level, with an ikky BP, you can kick the hell out of mobs in The Overthere and make much more exp solo than you would in most groups. When you get your 39 pet, you'll be real happy. Use it smartly and it will NEVER die.

Basically, with the ikky BP alone you're very well set. Time to start saving up for the JBB. It isn't sold very often at all, so you need to save as early as possible so you have the max possible window of opportunity to buy it. It is truly god-like at 45, and while 15k (JBB price on my server) is well beyond the ability of most non-twink shamans at 45, the sooner you get it, the faster you will rocket through the late 40's and low 50's.

Fill out your stuff with cheap stuff like rygorr(which is actually good stuff) and save, save, save.

And when you get done saving for the JBB -- start saving for a fungi.

Shamans can go a long way without buying too many upgrades. And they pretty much have to if they want the good armor and equipment.

P.S. -- another thing worth saving for -- rune etched icewurm fang, 12/19 piercer that proc's a very nice cold DD. You can actually do some serious damage with this thing. As a comparison -- a mini-epic monks have called the Whistling Fists is only 12/20, and has NO DD component.
