I know this is an old thread, but:

Naming Policy (http://eqlive.station.sony.com/support/customer_service/cs_naming_policy.jsp)

The naming policy was changed a while back. Now names that form phrases are allowed where they weren't before. That's the only change.

Names with titles, that are borrowed from popular fantasy or RL (which in itself is open to interpretation as to how recognisable the name is) are still not officially allowed. There have always been people with bad names making it to level 60 (65 now) before getting noticed.

A lot of names go around that are against the naming policy, I'm sure many of them would be changed if they were petitioned.

A few years ago, they did a sweep where they looked at and changed names offline. I know a couple of people who's name was changed as a result. One was offensive in spanish, but innocuous in English, and the other's surname was removed where it formed an offensive phrase in combination with the first name.
