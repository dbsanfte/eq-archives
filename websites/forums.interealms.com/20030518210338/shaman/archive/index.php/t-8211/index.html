Low lvl gear, totemic. Easy to find. The Breast plate has extended buff focus on it, but no wisdom, so it's kind of a toss up.

your duties will pretty much be as you have said. Healing, slowing, haste when you get it. As soon as a druid or cleric comes along, you should become back-up healer. You'll also be primay stat buffer. Sta Str Dex Agi. Get ready to be bugged ;) heh

You can get some decent cheap Wis gear to get you started. Lower lvls are all about the Wisdom. Then as you progress towards 200+ WIS, you'll want to start looking into gear with more AC and MANA.

Take a look at some of the Magelo profiles floating around, they should give you a good idea of what to shoot for in the long run.
