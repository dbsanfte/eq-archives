Jebeksis Stormcaster
Basic rule about PoM: if you go there expecting to be able to get items in a reasonable amount of time, you're going to be disappointed. Guaranteed. It takes more patience than just about any other solo activity/quest in the game. Between the very rare drops of most cards, to the endless quest for WoW top left, to the mob/pet depop bugs, it's easy to be frustrated, especially 61+ when the exp is pretty weak.

Just enjoy the zone for what it is. Yes, it's buggy. Yes, there are plenty of unfinished quests and bizarre items for which there is no apparent use. However, it's quirkiness and very unique nature keep bringing some people back (myself included). If you can handle just being there to pick up some exp or whatnot, then you'll do fine, and the boots will be a bonus (unless you plan on buying all the items, of course... many of which are readily for sale, at least on Stormhammer).
