The following formulas seem to be working for me all the time:

EDIT: Level 60 - Skill 240 - Str 255 - Dex 255 - Eagle Eye

==============================
For Bows:

Bow Max Hit = (Bow Dmg + Arrow Damage ) * 5.7

Example (Primal Bow with 11dmg arrows)

Max Hit = (33+11) *5.7 = 250 dmg

Add AM3 its 2x = 500 dmg

Add Trueshot its 2x = 1,000 dmg

Add Crit 1.72x = 1,719 dmg

Arrow damage doesnt seem to include the +magic/fire/etc damage unfortunately. 5.7 is rounded up to simplify.
==============================
For main hand weapons:

Wep Max Hit = (WepDmg+MagicDmg) * 5.7 + DmgBonus

Example for KEC:

KEC Max Hit = (15+1) * 5.7 + 11 = 102.2 => rounded up to 103 dmg

When magic is resisted, its 15 * 5.7 + 11 = 97 dmg

==============================

Steel Warrior tested the wep formula a while back, and it seems to apply to bows as well, just without the damage bonus and magic dmg.

Just a note, the TS multiplier scales with DAMAGE, not delay. Otherwise a lesser quality DN bow would be perform better with TS than the better DN bows. But since they're all 30 damage, they perform equally (on a hit per hit basis), just hit slightly faster with better bows.

Since higher damage usually goes hand in hand with higher delay, this subtlety often slips by a lot of people.
