Reandn
I always keep in mind the mob's resistance properties and try to select my arrows appropriately. I use a fire-based mithril arrow standard (the non-Tunare one, since I worship Karana), and since my other spells tend to be fire-based (Calefaction (now Brushfire) and Cinder Jolt), it usually becomes obvious when a mob is fire resistant. In that case I will usually swap in the cold-based acrylia arrow. I carry around the magic-based acrylia arrow also, but don't use it much.

And I can definitely notice a big difference in the damage when I've selected the optimal arrow for the fight at hand. Since the bow is really my primary weapon these days, I figure it is worth the extra effort to try to select the right arrow.

Besides, I've got a Fleeting Quiver just sitting there with empty slots, may as well carry around a full selection of arrows in it!

- Reandn
