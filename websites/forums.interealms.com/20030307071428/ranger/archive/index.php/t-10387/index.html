Both have positives. Druid can get you around fast to many places to hunt. Cleric can allow you to hunt a higher variety of mobs. With new Druid heals, they are not as limited in supporting a Tanking Ranger as they once were. Dots & Nukes are nice to help reduce the need for healing, as the mob dies faster! Still, Cleric buffs, heals, fears, stuns, and of course REZ make them very versatile.Other than ports, Ranger covers all the same traveling limitations that a Druid does, as Clerics don't travel well.

I'd move one so they would be on the same account, and can play your Ranger with either depending on how you feel! :D
