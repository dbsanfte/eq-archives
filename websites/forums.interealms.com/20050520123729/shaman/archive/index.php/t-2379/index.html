Juujuu
The place Yeldema spawns is fairly good for not getting adds, but then this was about 2 months ago that I last farmed him. If you are standing at Yeldema, face the white dragon on the nest that's nearby so that the ToV rift is to your right front side. If you run out about 10 seconds towards the white dragon, you'll have virtually no roamers come through camp. There's mobs that path nearby, but for the most part, they veer off away from you. The only issues I've had are with a buggy Von that warped on top of me no matter where I pulled Yeldema one day, having Rak Sappara path through there once in about 20 kills, and the very occasional velium hound run through. Keep on the constant lookout for potential adds, and kite your dragon away from the add if you see one coming.

The place Jaradis mentioned is good too. If you are facing that white dragon from Yeldema's spawn location, aim left at about a 45% angle and you'll find the spot. Not very many wanderers used to go through there.
