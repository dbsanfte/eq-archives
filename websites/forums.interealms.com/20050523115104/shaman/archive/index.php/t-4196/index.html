To gain access to inner acrylia, you must complete this quest (http://pub35.ezboard.com/fthesafehousefrm13.showMessage?topicID=3430.topic) for each person who wants to get in. This quest is harder than emp key/emp bane/10 shards combined. If it wasn't for having to kill Emp himself, it would be harder than the entire VT quest, and much more time consuming.

Once inside, there are a total of 4 scripts. 3 of them are independant events, much like the Ring of Fire that is elsewhere in the zone. All 3 of these are either broken, untuned, have horrible rewards, or a combination of any of those 3.

The 4th event is a long and detailed script that progresses you deeper and deeper into the zone, much like Sirran in sky except with a lot more depth. Eventually this script spawns a tagetable version of Tashakhi (the boss of the zone, aka 'The Master') and grants access to his lair.

During that event, you kill 4-5 mini bosses (it's been a few months now, the memory is hazy), who either drop nothing at all or a no rent key for some of the locked doors you encounter along the way.

After 3-4 months of key work, we went in and took Tashakhi out a couple of times before VI removed his trigger. His loot table seemed to be about 5 items deep, with 3 of them being garbage and 2 of them actually being nice (but lacking).

Of the other 3 events in the zone. We have only defeated 1 of them, and got loot that redefines the word "craptastic". The remaining 2 involve bugged and untuned fights which we decided were not even worth the effort anymore due to what loot we had already seen. One of them, in fact, was designed to be a "difficult encounter for 36" and yet repeatedly spanked us with a force of 60+.

Once turning our backs to IA, we moved out west to Ssrasaly and in 1/3 of the time it took to get a raid force keyed for IA we managed to get about 98% of our active guild members keyed for Emp and a large amount of those stupid bane weapons where they need to be. Plus, we got to kill a lot of BGH snakes along the way and get loot we would actually use!

It's a disgrace that VI has the mindset that it's ok to have a "full plate" working on the next expansion when their most recent one is not yet finished to the point that it can deliver what was promised at release. But hey, that's just par for the course for this *******s.

Too bad we are the only guild to put a serious effort into IA keys. Be a lot harder for VI to backburner that zone if there were more people who actually cared what happened in there.
