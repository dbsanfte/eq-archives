6/551 Dec - means 6 men out and 551 runs scored. Dec means 'Declared' - The Aussies decided to end their innings before all their players were out, as they had scored enough runs.

England 270 runs scored and all players out and 387 runs/all out.

Cricket has 10 outs to an innings, and 2 innings for each team in a game. Each batsman (read batter in baseball for that) stays in until they are out.. and scores runs by running up and down a 22 yard pitch.

It's a bit more complicated than that, but that's the general idea!
