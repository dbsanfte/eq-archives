Nazgruul
Well you've picked the right zone to hunt in at your lvl. I tried solo'ing there at lvl 15, but most everything I conned was red or yellow.

The Patog fiends are a bit lower lvl than the bandits so try to get a partner for those until lvl 16 or 17. Once you get those lvls, solo'ing will be much easier. The fiends have a nasty dot they hit you with, but since we have cure disease, it's easily curable. Takes 3 casts of the lower lvl one.

Once you hit around 21 to 22, start solo'ing the bandit camps. Just make sure another group is there pulling. If no one else is there to help thin the camp out, when you go to pull, you could possibly get up to 4 bandits along with the one you pulled :)

Good luck.

Lorace
Bandits and Patog fiends are pretty much the hardest things in there. If you want to solo, you can find some areas with single pull bugs, mushrooms, and maybe phlerg fiends, but expect adds in most of them from time to time.

One problem is that coming in from SH end, you'd need to get an invis to get to some of these areas. Paludal is built with the easiest areas nearest Shar Vahl, and the hardest areas near Shadow Haven.

The shik'nar area is even doable within a few levels, but again, you'd need invis to get there. Best bet may be to find a partner. The xp here is the best in the game. The respawn rate is very very quick, so even if you can find 2 or 3 single pulls in an area, they respawn so fast that it might be plenty.

Azhdarkhos
Spawn rate in Paludal is roughly 8 minutes, so you can happily hunt a small area without running out of prey.

Recently, some RL friends of mine and I started a couple of groups worth of gnomes, halflings and dwarfs to play one night a week, and have been hunting in Paludal 3 weeks running now, for about 2 hours per night. We've been averaging about 2 levels per night (missed getting 18th last session by one blue bubble.)

Your best bet is to get to the Shar Vahl side and work your way back - the mobs escalate very smoothly in level moving from either Shadeweaver's Thicket or Hollowshade Moor entrances towards the Shadowhaven entrance. Move in until you find the right size critters for your level and lay waste ;)
