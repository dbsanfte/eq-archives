My GL currently has a petition in from not getting a corpse back after LDing in a LDoN zone, so it isn't completely fixed.

As for how much you should get back: I twobox, so I've actually had plenty of chances to watch that in action.

On either of my level 54's, a clicky rez will restore all but (around) 1% of the XP lost.

In other words, if my Shaman has 32% in, dies, then my Cleric clickies him, the Shaman will be back at 31%. I've had one instance when a rez brought him back to (for example) 32%, and one where I am pretty sure it took just over 1% (sending him back to 30%, for instance).

In the first case, there had been a few XP gains just prior to him dying, so he may have been up a percent to begin with and I didn't see it.

54 is my highest, so I don't know if that carries through in higher levels...
