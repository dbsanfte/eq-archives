Actually, getting to EK is not very hard at all. My ranger runs it all the time. Take the port book from PoK to Misty Thicket. Run past the wall and across the zone to the entrance to Runnyeye. Zone in and follow the right hand wall to zone into the Gorge of King Xorbb. It’s an extremely short run through Runnyeye, past very low-level goblins. Once you are in the gorge, just follow the left hand wall and you’ll eventually zone into East Karana. This is a longer run and there tend to be a lot of wondering mobs, but at 21 most of them may leave you alone. But if not you should be able to outrun them with SoW. To be honest I think the run is shorter then running from the elven tree city to the dwarven docks and I used to run that all the time back in the olden days.
