I'm looking for somewhere where the exp can at least compare to the exp modifier in hhk.

First off, unfortunately that is not gonna happen. HHK type experience is now gone, if you solo. That said, there are several spots mentioned in the first couple of pages in this forum. The ones I personally used are as follows:

Dulak - Dandolak's Run. Nice and blue, single pulls and you can buff up and summon dog dog just to the right of the entrance once you get on the boat. Good cash and gem loot. Can usually net 600-1000 per hunting session here, depending on the length. Plan on dinging 59 here if I do it solo. The Lady Druila may be DB here at 56 as well, I never hunted that boat so I can't say for sure.

Dulak - Soulstripper camp. Everything down here DB and the shot at the Soulstripper, which still sells for 4-5k. Single pulls with only one wanderer to be concerned with. I have probably gotten 5 or 6 soulstrippers, sold em all for 4-7k. He is a nasty mob though at 56 you should have no problem. Was here last night still 80% dark blue and 100% dark blue if you pull from beyond the SS and leave the stuff near the bridge alone. Though if you do this camp I recommend taking out the cleric at the bridge. He CH's and that can be a bitch if the Enraged Soulstealer manages to get up there.

Chardok - Zone in. Single pulls at 56, EXP modifier in zone. Did most of 56 and a couple AA here.

Nadox - I solo'd at the back end of the temple, right near Torg zone line. Single pulls, good repop times some decent loot.

Katta Guards - I did Nathyn's mansion. This is about the fastest exp of all listed above, but it is mind numbingly boring, no gems, crap loot and did I mention boring?

Katta Guards - Bank. I always hunted the Nathyn's with my Shaman but my Bard did the bank guards as well. See above for the fun factor.

Tier one yard trash - Never liked this, lost too much mana and health for it to be worthwhile.

That was where I spent 56-58, nearly 59 where I am now when I solo'd. Grouping was always better experience, but like you that is not always an option.

Good Luck!
