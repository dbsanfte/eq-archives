Talsondar
Few decent solo choices actually:

Chardok Zone IN: Make your pulls at no less than 1/2 mana, I got adds here until 56, at which, everything has come single. Great Experience and pretty good loot.


Karnors: I always solo'd T1 here, which I believe is defined as the first set of stairs to your right as you enter the castle. This is the area where the captain(?) ** the one who drops the jade mace ** spawns. Did most of 55 here with little problem. Occasionally a train will divert itself and a CH will get cast on your mob, which causes some trouble, but for the most part, your standard slow and root park adds (sometimes up to 2 adds) and sic pet and JBB.

BW: If you move away from the fort there is still a ton of blues, just have to be more picky than before. Experience is much slower here than Karnors or Chardok but those spots may be camped.

Good Luck!
