Do not confuse 'bard haste' with v3 haste.

EQ has 3 forms of haste.
v1 is item haste, FBSS or Swiftwind for example
v2 is spell haste, VoG or DotB
v3 is the weapon haste, this 'raises' haste cap, or rather directly effects the delay of your weapons thus increasing your total haste.

Warsong of Vahshir or its lesser Battlecry have conflicting v3 hastes as well as the new Rizola's Call of Fire. This is why some people are saying its not worth it. Up to you, quite often on raids a bard is put in the ranger group for increased DPS, thats my guild. Yours you might never see a bard.
