Filson
Here's a quick roadmap for going epic by level.

Levels <39. Forage for the five foraged items as you happen to be in the appropriate zones during your normal adventuring.

Levels 39-45. Actively forage. Do the Ancient Pattern and Platinum Speckled Powder subquests.

Level 46. Formally initiate quest with Telin. He won't speak to you earlier than 46. Do the Coin subquest up to the point where you come back to Telin.

Levels 46-49. Do the DE Corruptor, jade reaver camp, the Hole, and VSR as available to you. It has been recommended to me to MQ the SSoC.

Level 50. Obtain Swiftwind. You cannot do turn in before level 50.

Level 50+. Raid Hate as available to you in hopes of obtaining SEoC and Earthcaller.
