Here is an example on how raid experience hurts the grind:

Lets suppose you kill a MOB with 100 exp points.

NORMAL GROUP EXP
20 % grouping bonus
group gets 100*1.20 = 120 exp
exp/person = 120/6 = 20

RAID EXP with a SEVEN PERSON GROUP
*assume a 30% exp penalty* (I don't know the real number)
group gets 100 *(1-.30) = 70 exp
exp/person = 70/7 = 10

Normal / Raid exp per person = 20 / 10 = 2

So normal exp is TWICE as good as raid exp (assuming a 30% penalty).
