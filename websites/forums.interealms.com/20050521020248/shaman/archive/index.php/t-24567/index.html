Chutoi
It depends on the people. All things considered, not that many people use spreadsheets. Any time I do any kind of modelling on spreadsheets, I make it as idiot simple as possible. Input the fewest numbers in easily identifiable places (usually via a different fill color), and then let it run the calcs. If you know you have a sophisticated audience, sometimes it's okay to leave it more unfinished. However, even an experienced excel user is going to have trouble coming to a spreadsheet and checking things out. The builder does most of the work. Any review or user level should be relegated to a spot check of calculation results, not formulas.

Just my 2 cents. Maybe make an input page, and then a results page or something. :)
