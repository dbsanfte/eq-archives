Faern-

Are you running your DSL through a router? I use one on mine, and I had terrible problems with the Blizzard downloader until I configured port forwarding on my router. If memory serves, there's a link you can click on in the downloader to get to the Blizzard help page, which provides instructions on which ports to open and how to open them. Alternatively, you can just disconnect your router temporarily and plug your DSL modem directly into one computer for the duration of the download.

Once I made the changes they suggested, my download rate went up to about 60-70 KB/sec. It's not great, but it beats 10 KB/sec. Hope this helps!
