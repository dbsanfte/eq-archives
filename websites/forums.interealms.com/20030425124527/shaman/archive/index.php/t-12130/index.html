Improved Healing - increase amount on direct heals

Mana Preservation - reduce mana on beneficial spells (with exclusions)
<a href="#range">Range Foci</a>
Extended Range - increase casting range

Spell Haste - reduce casting time

Great list both of you. One quick suggestion: all of the PoP crafted ranged slot focus effect items say crafted for zone and mob. While this is technically accurate, the items you need to make the crafted items do drop in specific planes off of specific mobs. In fact, to make all of these items you need some store-bought stuff, some random blood, a random imbue spell, and two hard to get items from the planes. Instead of just saying crafted, you could instead list the mobs/zones where these two hard items drop, since that would give people an idea of what and where they need to kill to get the item.

To figure out the zone, just look at the name of the item: Sword of war stuff drops in Drunder, Fortress of Zek, rod of storms stuff in Bastion of Thunder, totem of the rathe stuff in plane of earth, toem of xeogny stuff in Eryslai, the Kingdom of Wind. The rest should be pretty self explanatory (incense burner -poFire, etc.) As for which mobs drop which item, you can do this search:
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/adv_search.html?classes=9&race=0&type=Any+Slot&effect=9999&fzone=0&foodtype=0&smod=0&stat1=None&statlim1=0&stat2=None&statlim2=0&stat3=None&statlim3=0&pristat=ac&priwt=2&secstat=hp&secwt=1&tristat=none&triwt=0&level=&price=&qt=b&ocrafted=n&qitem=y&st=glaze

at allakhazam, which will give you the list of glazes (you combine the two hard items, along with two store-bought items, to make the glaze, then you combine the other stuff with the glaze to make the item) which have names similiar to their item (Valourous glaze for Ceramic Shield of Valor for instance).

Alternatively, you can just look up which mob the hard items drop off individually at http://www.eqtraders.com/secrets/info/PoP_drops.htm

Just use the following list to find out the names of the two hard items needed for each ceramic item.

ceramic gavel of justice enforcer stone, grallok quartz
ceramic shackles of torment tormentor's hoof, putrescent blood
ceramic hammer of innovation moonstone crystal, sillicorrosive grease
ceramic skull of decay foul smelling liquid, putrid ooze
ceramic sword of war living coal, war wraith blood
ceramic rod of storms stormwatchers stone, sandstorm pearl
ceramic shield of valor large shard of stone, metallic liquid
ceramic incense burner of ro fierygranite, solidified magma
ceramic water sprinkler of marr shattered crystal, liquified earth
ceramic totem of the rathe emerald colored mephit scales, oil of a frog
ceramic totem of xegony alabaster beak, ivory stormrider canine

If you did all this, it'd quickly become apparent that certain crafted items, like the Gavel of Justice, are WAYYYY easier to obtain than other crafted items, like the Water Sprinkler of Marr (/drool).
