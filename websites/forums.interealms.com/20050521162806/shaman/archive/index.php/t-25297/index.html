(Regarding the Malos line)Surely you're not including Malicious Decay, are you?
The Malos line, that is all spells that share the same characteristics except for numerals and resist checks are Malo, Malosi, Malosinia, Malos, Malosini, Mala and so on.

Malicious Decay is from an entirely different spell line. It debuffs only disease, which is the one resist that the Malos line does not debuff.

So Malos will indeed not help to land VP at all. Malicious Decay (and other disease debuffs) will. That is also pretty much the only reason I have seen exping shaman memorize the spell.
