BlargX
Spiroc Isle in Timorous Deep(between Oasis and Overthere, the isle with the ogre on it) is fantastic experience at 51. Drops off very quickly at 52 though; the most sudden drop-off of experience I can remember.

Cyndreela at the portal to Fear is good experience, and she drops a fair amount of bronze and gems.

The wizard brothers(and their necro friend) deep in the Third Gate section of Neriak are very very good experience at that level, but you'll need to bring some resistance jewelry.

Cheetas and lightcrawlers and sunflowers in Scarlet Desert are good experience and it's almost impossible to run out of them. If you move along the zonewalls, you'll see the mobs get higher and lower in certain sections. Moving right from the entrance from Twilight, mobs get higher level. There are also aliens and tribals, both walking around and in the many caves. You can also go up on the plateau and kill aliens and revenants. A piece for the shield of bane warding drops off a named here, and you can also get a shard for the Vex Thal key.

I can't say I've ever been impressed by COM as far as experience goes, though it's a great zone just as a zone.

The Hole entrance is fantastic at 51 but I doubt you could solo it there. Mobs often come as doubles, and sometimes it's hard to find space to root them. A full group is great there.
