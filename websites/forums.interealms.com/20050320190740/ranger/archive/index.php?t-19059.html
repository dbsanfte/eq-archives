I think it's an excellent idea!

New adventures. New items. New quests.Making friends all over again.
Of course, we'll probably spend the first few months constantly telling each other who we were in EQ1 and trying to match up with old guilds, etc.
But who cares?

I used to think being a shaman was cool. Because I was the shaman and no one else in the group had my capabilities. Nowadays, any group I'm in has someone with an alt shaman that's higher than me and is constantly trying to teach me how to use my class. It will be refreshing to start out at the begining again, if not for any other reason than to add humility back to the game.

Besides, everyone knows where everything is and how to complete quests. It's no longer a question of "can it be done?", instead it's "how long will it take?".

However, I foresee a TON of beggars. Everyone is soooo used to twinking out their alts that they wont be able to stand hunting without the huge advantage they're used to. :rolleyes:

But it should be fun either way.
