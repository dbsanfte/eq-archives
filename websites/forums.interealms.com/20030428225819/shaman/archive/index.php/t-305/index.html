Think the herb that is doubled up in original alchemy and the revamped alchemy is Figwort. They have the same name but they are different herbs and cannot be used interchangably.
Could be wrong, but i think Feverfew is only available in Shar Vahl.

Hint for herb shopping in Shar Vahl: there are TWO vendors selling herbs in the shop next to the bank. The trick is the one standing behind the counter on the left is indifferent and so prices are high. The other vendor (Marissa? or soemthing like that) will be standing behind. But when she is on the left side, she won't trade with you as she is busy. You have to wait till she crosses the room to the back wall to deal with her. This vendor was amiable to me, so i got much cheaper prices. Sorry if that's confusing, just have a look. :)
