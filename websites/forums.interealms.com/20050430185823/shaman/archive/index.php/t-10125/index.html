Goldark
VERY cheap? Banded armour and a fine steel spear. Around 5pp each.

Still pretty cheap - splitpaw gloves and a few other splitpaw items. Spore covered xxx (e.g. sleeves, boots etc). All around 20pp or so each.

Got a little bit more? Consider a velium fire wedding ring or two (450pp each), spine chill spear (300pp?)

Finally - spells cost a lot at lower levels. No need to buy them all, concentrate on heals, dots, nukes, haste, ac. Buffs don't do a hell of a lot at the low levels, so don't fret it if you don't have every one.
