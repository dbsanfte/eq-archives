weissblau
Icegrinder drops in Great Divide for sure. (What's GW?) Icegrinder is a very rare drop, however - much more common is a Wraithbone Hammer from City of Mist. Might tide you over until the Icegrinder drops.

Rune-etched Icewurm Fang drops in Velks, I think, but it would be a tough solo camp. That's probably the best shaman piercer for melee damage, and melee will carry the first 20-25 levels easy.

Ry'Gorr mail from the Ry'Gorr fort in EW is always good. I see you have some.

Charred Guardian Shield in Sol A is an obvious choice. If you want to go all out, Bone Braced Buckler drops in the Grey and is an AWESOME shaman shield - AC25 STR10 WIS10 HP55 Mana55.

For the wrists and neck, Ry'Gorr is easy to get and better than banded.

For the neck, Ivory Imbued Collar drops in Chardok and is very nice.

For face - Frost giant beard if nothing else. Arctic Wyvern Mask would be great, but it's player-made and not a camp. Crystal Spider Eyes from Velk's are very nice.
