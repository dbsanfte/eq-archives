Don't sweat about duelling another shaman higher than you. I duelled a shaman 2 levels higher than me, and he has his epic while I do not. It was a close fight, but I managed to win. Barely. Soon as I saw he was dead I had to mem Disinfecting Aura and cast ASAP asn I had 2 disease DoTs on me.

Basically I tried to keep him blinded, get behind him and smack him around while casting Blast of Poison. I used Blast of Poison cause it is quick casting. I did not even bother with DoTs in the duel. Too slow casting. I did get Malosi on him after the first Blinding Luminance.

We duelled with no pets.

So it is not impossible for a lower level shaman to beat a higher level one, but it is tough. BTW, this was a level 52 vs level 54.
