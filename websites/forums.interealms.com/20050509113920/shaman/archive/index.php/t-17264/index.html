Jinheim
Suspended Minion should be towards the very back of your list. I didn't train it until AA #294 and I definately do not regret waiting that long. It's pretty cool for duels but otherwise, all it really is is a small timesaver.

RC3 is alright, but RC1 and RC2 are nearly worthless. Even RC3 doesn't cure most LDoN effects in one cast.

VP3 is a good AA and I'd get it early. It is great for crowd control in LDoN and you can use VP as your primary root against mobs with really low disease resist, but VP is not a substitute for other roots. If you don't have clicky root or a castable root memmed, then if your VP gets resisted or wears off, you have to wait 2 minutes for the skill to repop.

Spirit Call isn't that great. It was "a blast" when pop first came out and almost no one had ever even seen it before. The novelty wore off pretty fast, though, and now it is just another ability (an ability that I do not find all that useful).

If you spend almost all your time raiding, I would advise this path:

SCM3 -> QB2 -> HG3 -> HA3 -> AHG3 -> AHA3 -> Defensive AA -> whatever you want.
