Currently in level 54, player X has Y amount of XP. This is let's say 40% of the total XP needed for level 54. Now, when this change goes live, player X is going to have Z amount of XP, which is equal to 40% of current level 54. Jaradis
Your XP bar won't move. Those that are currently at the beginning of 59 (or end of 58) will benefit because they won't have to do nearly as much xp in 59 as they would have before. No one will end up being penalized. If you aren't 50 yet it won't matter 51-60 still will be the same amount of XP. If you are in the 50's already (say at 55), it won't matter because the levels you already did (51-54) will end up absorbing some of your future level's xp so you'll end up having to do less xp overall.

No one loses out, except the person sitting at the beginning of 60 and they haven't filled their xp bar. Even then, the benefit of not losing as much xp on death will make up for it.
