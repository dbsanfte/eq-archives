Kriel Darkbane
I really don't like the 36 person limit on raids in LDoN. Or, rather, I think it's inconsistent with the apparent difficulty of those raids, and the other game content. Raid guilds have expanded to the point where they can regulardly field 60+ people for their raids. What do those guilds do, when just over half their force can go on a raid? It's going to get ugly.

And then there are the small guilds, who don't have the gear that is required to do even the normal difficulty raids in LDoN. If the raids scaled in difficulty based on raid size, and had an easier setting, it would be nirvana for casual guilds. Now you could actually PLAN when you're going to have a raid - which is what those guilds need to do to get the people online to have a raid force, even a 20-30 person raid force. But those guilds can't do the raids, because the lowest setting is too hard. Maybe in 6 months or a year they can gear up via LDoN adventures and then have a chance. Maybe.

I really don't see how the LDoN raids are good for ANYONE. Well, perhaps for people in a PoT-capable guild that is losing membership. After a bunch of people get bored and quit, and their raid numbers shrink, they can turn into a LDoN-raiding guild, if they don't keep recruiting except for required classes to maintain 36 person raids.

It really seems like something has to change here.
