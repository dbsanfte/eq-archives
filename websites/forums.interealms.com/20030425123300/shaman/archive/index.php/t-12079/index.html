What I did to get a Tae Ew Tunic and shield was buy the blood needed to make the items, at about 2k per blood. So thats 6k then for the tunic I bought the distilled mana for about 400pp, I then found a smith who said they would do the combine for me for a donation of 1 blood per combine. He also provided the metal, the molds, and the other item needed in the shield combine he charged me 100pp for.

So after all was said and done I paid 6.4k for the tunic and 2.1k for the shield. I got really lucky in finding a smith as well as a brewer all in one who was not hung up on only doing cultural combines, like a few smiths I have come across. Is a pretty cheap way to get some nice upgrades, if you can get as lucky as I did then you could prolly just save up about 9k and hang onto your fungi. Just an idea not sure about what the smiths are like on your server. Good luck.
