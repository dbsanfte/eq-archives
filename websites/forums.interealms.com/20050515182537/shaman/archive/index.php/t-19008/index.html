You said that you had played previously, so you probably already know this, but try your best to keep your melee skills up! Especially your Defense skill! Next time you try to solo, head to PoK and check the OOC chat to see if any shaman are offering buffs. Anyone that asks me **nicely** for buffs gets them! :-) HoS or IoS will go a long way to hleping your melee skills raise quicker.

Please don't let your time grouping in PC fool you! XP will never come that easy again! The rule of thumb that I use is "one yellow bubble per hour." If I'm not getting that much XP, I'm doing something wrong! Yes, lvl 59 drove me nuts because I couldn't get that kind of XP anywhere.
