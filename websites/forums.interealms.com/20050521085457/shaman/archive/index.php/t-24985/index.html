Weyalan
I suspect you have a somewhat skewed perspective of PvP servers Drib...

On VZ at the upper end, PvP is actually relatively honorable. If you are the sort of person who deliberately trains mobs, you very very quickly become the sort of person who gets killed on sight by everyone. And since most groups for somewhere like WoS are not pickup groups, but guild groups or alliance groups or freinds groups it is fairly easy to encorce that particular "Play nice policy". In fact, what tends to happen is that guilds, or alliances of guilds, will fight for the rights to a zone - i.e. have a big PvP fest to establish whos zone it is. The winners then pick their spots and any non-KoS guild groups are welcome to come and pick any spots not taken...and thus it stays until the vanquished guild or guilds get their act together to reclaim the zone.

I cannot speak for the other Zeks, but on VZ, there is far less deliberate training and general Fefery than you might think...particularly at the upper levels....so in the situation that you list, PvPing for a camp is actually a perfectly good way to sort it out.

And while I understand your comment about PvP being (largely) determined by who has the better gear, the point in this case is that most people who want to camp WoS are probably going to have similarish gear...I mean, If you are fully time flagged, you wont be in WoS anyway, and if your gear is seriously sucky (like, say, worse than mine) you probably aren't going to survive PvE in WoS...so again, given that it is probably going to be at least 6 v 6 PvP (and usually more like 12+ v 12+), on average, the gear over the opposing forces is likely to balance out fairly well. In this case, it becomes about which guild (or alliance) has the resources and the motivation and the will to get some reinfircements there fastest, because essentially, in PvP with more than a few people involved, numerical superiority > all ather factors.
