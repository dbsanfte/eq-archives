Landragoran Heronwing
Ouch. Honestly you took the long route.
There are much easier ways to get Spirit Callers armor. Let me explain.

Firstly, the faction that is easiest to get is Kromzek. Oddly enuf, this is also the faction that you have yet to get to a reasonable level. Even more strangely, this is the faction you need to talk to the quest giving giants for Spirit Callers armor.

Let's put it like this, if you know a mage with CoH who has decent faction with all three giant factions, you only need to get kromzek faction to do the quests. The quest giving NPCs are in a hall that is set apart from other giants. You can talk to them and get the quest armor without ever coming into contact with Tormax or Frost giants. Well, if there is a mage in your guild . . . which I think is quite possible.

Go to WW, WToV, and Skyshrine. Get on a decidedly unfriendly term with dragons. Kromzek will love you in no time at all. Then get your mage friend to CoH you into the room, do your quests, and be done with it.

Kromzek is one of the easiest factions in the game to get. Specially if you have a guild that can do WToV and WW.

Leave the dorfs alone less yer on a dain raid or something. It's just not worth the effort to obtain a faction you might never use. Frost giants seem to be ally to you anyway, and that's not an easy faction to raise. Tormax is even harder, and is really not worth it unless you win a Dain head and want to turn in for a belt. Otherwise, there is no point to having it.
