Zuksharn
The only thing you can do to make getting the boots a bit easier would be to buy the cards and doll from someone.

Assuming you do this you'll only need to get a deck of spontaneous generation, book of mischief and pot of gold all of which are quested containers in PoM. All this information is on the PoM website but admittedly it's a bit tricky to understand at first. I'll skip some of the logistics and locations of needed NPC's... you'll find them after some time exploring the place.

First off you'll need to get the Deck of Spontaneous Generation from Ferjeneror. Give him a King Cod Card for it, or alternatively you can fish in the moat outside for the 4 different colored cod cards... giving him those 4 will get you the deck also. Ferjeneror is a sphinx in the center of the hedge maze. Combine the blue throne, white throne, white crown and white knight in the deck to get the boots of distraction.

Next you'll need to get a Book of Mischief. Kill any of the gorilla mobs for a chance at getting a library card and give the card to Posie the Librarian. She's a flower looking enchanter mob. In the book you'll need to combine the top right, top left, bottom right and bottom left pieces of the Words of Wealth. The page parts can drop off any mob in the zone really but the left sides seem to be more rare.

Combining the corners will get you a completed Words of Wealth. Give that to Bob the Painter and he'll give you an Empty Pot of Gold. Combine your shaman doll and the boots of distraction in the pot of gold and you'll have the new boots.

It's only this "easy" if you buy the cards and doll. If you are unable to do this you'll need to farm the cards plus a ton more words of wealth page parts which will take a lot of time and dedication. To get a chance at getting the shaman gift box (which produces the doll) you'll need to make a total of 4 additional completed words of wealth and 4 additional top left pieces. Combining a completed words of weath with a top left piece will result in a Funny Money. Give 4 funny moneys to Peaches at the entrance to the castle and she will give you back a random gift box... you have a 1 in 8 shot of it being a shaman box. The box has 5 charges. Each charge one will give you a doll.

Even if you don't end up with a shaman box you can sell off the dolls you do get for the cards you need for the boots... it's a good way to do it.

Best of luck!
