Hail,

As a victim of the faction problems, I seek out these type of threads to try to help. I finally finished my epic last Sunday. I've become fairly well versed in the Truespirit faction problems along the way. You can read about them here: http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=1515

My strongest recommendation is to NOT DEVIATE from the quest.
All the ramifications of the May patch stealth faction mod to our epic quest are still not known. I would NOT repeat earlier steps to try to get around handing in the Ghiosk books.

Grabbing the icon and trying the turn-in as is would be folly in my opinion. Without the Ghiosk turn-in hit, he'll likely eat it. But trying THAT way would be BETTER than trying to cheat your faction hits by repeating earlier steps and then trying Kirn. Almost ALL the GM's know about the faction range now, and I seriously doubt they'll help you on later steps if you screw things up by knowingly repeating earlier steps to bypass Ghiosk.

Camping the Spectral courier can be a pain, but it isn't very hard, just time consuming. Get one or two friends for company (and a safety margin) to help you, and you will get it eventually.

Kirn isn't going to disappear, and with the mobs being mezzable now in the Hole, it really isn't that hard to crawl through to him. A ten person party (myself included) went on my Kirn raid, and that was overkill. One solid group group could probably do it, as long as you keep moving steadily forward. Took us four hours, and that was with forming up, a half hour to get one person through the crack (/laugh), and an hour spent on repops before we changed tactics to keep making progress. (We finally created and then defeated our own train to move forward.)

Go with your friends on their Hole raid for the experience if you can't nab the Student's Log beforehand, and you'll know more for when you go for Kirn on your own. I thought Kirn was a 4 hour respawn not 2, but I could be mistaken. We didn't stick around after we finished Kindle and Kirn on our raid. ;)
