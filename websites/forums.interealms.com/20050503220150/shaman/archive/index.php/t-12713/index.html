Kianor
I have a drogmor, but when I was considering whether to buy it or a horse, I looked into the differences.

Pros:
- Increased speed (depending on model).
- Medding mana regen without needing to sit (no sit agro).
- Inherent safe fall of 150.
- "Cool" factor (*shrug*)

Cons:
Both:
- Acceleration/deceleration - it takes a little more time to manoever, which takes some getting used to.
- Harder to target things, particularly things on the ground. You have to kinda sidle up sideways to a corpse so that it's in the corner of your screen, not right in front of you.
- You need to have Luclin models on, at least for your character
- You can't duck to interrupt spells. To interrupt a long cast spell, you have to click the buff icon off then duck.

Drogmor cons:
- The "bucking bronco" feel while riding it. This is slightly better when shrunk.
Horse cons:
- The head is in front of you at all times - alleviated slightly if shrunk, but makes looting in particular harder.
