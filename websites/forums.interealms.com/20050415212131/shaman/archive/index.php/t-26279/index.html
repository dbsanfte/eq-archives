# Key to the Emperor's Chamber

Crafted: This item is crafted by players.

* Ring of the Shissar (Non-Tradeskill, 0 trivial)
+ Ssraeshzian Insignia (1)
+ Zazuzh's Idol (1)
+ Zeruzsh's Ring (1)
+ Taskmaster's Pouch (1)

Found in:

* Ssraeshza Temple

In the basement of the temple, kill named Taskmasters or Warden Mekuzh until you obtain a Taskmaster's Pouch. Then kill Commander Zazuzh, on the first floor, and obtain the Zazuzh's Idol. Then kill Commander Zeruzsh, on the second floor, to get the ring. Then go to the imperial guard chamber and kill the guards until one of the named Mobs spawns. They drop the insignia.

Combine the idol, ring and insignia in the pouch and you will receive the Ring of Shissar, which gets you into the Emperor's Chamber.

Short story: all is soloable except Insignia from pre-Emp room (and maybe that is now, too). Is that what you wanted?

Regarding the shards you need. I suggest getting a big tank to go with you. It will make things go alot faster. You can solo it, but I got my shards from the Deep, Akheva, and Ssra pretty deep in. Having a friend makes life alot easier.

Regarding the Emp key: same thing. Typically what we did is everyone was expected to be at the point where all you needed was the part from pre emp room. You then farm the named until everyone is keyed. If you bring 6 people on your raid that need the part, expect it to take anywhere from one to two hours.

Regarding VT: I participated in a couple raids with the last two months. 18 to 25 folks, most from higher end guilds than myself, but they were playing twinks or two boxing to get gear for alts. Pretty much the only mobs we killed were the named. We would CotH around the zone with specific instructions to not do anything once called. No sit, no spin, no kneel, no anything. MA would then jump out of the pile, position the named and after that pretty conventional.

If you go the traditional way in VT, killing to get to AHR, it takes roughly 4 hours if you are really pushing it. This assumes everyone is on the ball. VT can still produce some hellacious trains for the unwary. Using the CoTH method, it takes roughly the same amount of time, but more of it is dead time just standing around. In most cases, this allows you to do laundry, grab a beer, hit the head, etc. without things going to pot. In at least a couple spots, you do need to be awake because roamers do come through there, and their agro range is just big enough to be a nuissance.

GoD and OOW geared folks will tell you I am hedging the time requirement, but since I am not GoD nor OOW geared, this is the experience I have :).

One last thing: the CoTH spots we were getting called to were entirely legal, but they were creatively exotic. Exotic to the point of "How did they get here?"

Enjoy your trip. VT is still one of my most favorite zones in the game. Very little mitigates, and as a shaman you really can feel nostalgic :).

1st CoH spot - Kaas Thox Xi Ans Dyek (aka Blob1) room. Blob1 is perma rooted. CoH to him, kill to despawn warders.
