Well, the trouble with the Shop camp solo is, as you have said, breaking the spawn. The mobs there summon, so it makes root parking for CC a limited choice, and a pacify puller or mezzer or the ability to handle 2-3 mobs solo is darn near a requirement.
I leveled from 58-61 and around 8ish AA in Shop and other veksar camps; there are very few that a shaman can do solo, if only for a lack of DPS. At best, your looking at your Epic, Anathema, Bane and Dog-Dog doing all your DPS and that gets mana hungrey. Personally I can and have done the bottom floor of shop; this was with KEI and a virtue and my gear was a bit lower level than what I have now (not that I am uber by any stretch of the imagination) and it tough. I would suggest a few other camps instead for you:
Behemoth- the iksar behemoth golem drops a caster/priest bracer with ID 4, and it fetches a similar price to the Soulscream, its his really really rare dop though.
Heirophant Vradik, pops in the area just past the fountain from shop, pops in the second alcove (3rd?) as your facing the stage area in that room, he drops the Dented Kunzar Warhelm, a nice plate class helm that fetches 4kish on Karana. All easy pulls.
Trooper Muruk-drops the Darkguard Chestplate (used to be a really decent BP till LDON's Architect one started to flood the markey), hes near the grandmaster area just past the Highborn camp and the area is easily single pullable.
There are a few other camps that are soloable, but to be perfectly honest your looking at long camps with very few (plat wise) rewards.
You really are better off snagging a few friends and forming a group and farming shop together; drops there are streaky, you can get 2 in a hour or not see one in 3-4 days.

65 shaman in my guild regularly solos the shop without much trouble. The way he breaks the spawns is he will set up in the corner by the stairs up to blacksmith. From there you can inch around the corner til you see the PH for the named, root him and the mob in the middle will come solo. Take care of him then kill the PH. Not very difficult as long as you are able to keep the one rooted and out of the way because they won't summon until you do some damage to them. The ldon spell Odium will stick on veksar mobs, I think its in the neighborhood of 500 MM points or so. Not a amazingly damaging dot but its better than nothing.
Other camps to check into in veksar are the chef, which drops Gomdurig and the kylong waist item which both sell pretty well, and the mob that drops the darkmail pauldrons, his name escapes me at the moment. Both of these PHs are easy single pulls and you can fight them with no fear of adds if you pay attention to your positioning.

I solo in Veksar all the time. I am only level 64 with very ordinary gear and a patchy spell book. Soloing is easy in Veksar. Money is good. I have been able to buy torpor, and 2 or 3 other spells recently (yes, I soloed without torpor too) and a few small gear upgrades

I do not solo the shops - far too tough for a ghetto shaman such as I. Nevertheless, although the soulscream belt is the cash-cow item of the zone, you can make plenty of steady plat in easier safer places...

The Theatre is the easiest (yawn) spot. Bascially, when you hit the golem area, instead of turning right to get to the shops, go straight over to the next area and then diagonally right to get to the theate.

The good thing about the theatre is that there are 3 named all pop within a very small area. Each named or PH can be single pulled easily, and there are no wandering mobs.

The loot from the 3 named is not spectacular:
1. Dented Kunzar Helm 2.5k & Moss covered leggings 2.5k
2. Narathius (piercer) 1k & Mantle of Ill Omen 3k
3. Ribbon of Empathy 4k and Bow of Gloaming 1k
sure - not big coin, but steady safe money.

If the theatre is camped, it is also possible (although harder) to camp the 3 named on the way down from the start of the undead to the golem area. The first 2 (PH's Phantom and Duke) are very easy, can be single pulled easily, and are cake - the guy after the swim is harder - I am not able to single pull him (but I'm not very good), but it is still possible to keep all 3 clear. from memory, loot includes:
Earring of Darkfaith
Glowing Bath Token & Shiny Pantaloons
Ring (forget name)...bleurgh cant remember rest.

Anyway, the point is, that you don't need to go for the high profile high resale loot - by camping multiple easy named, you can make good safe steady plat
