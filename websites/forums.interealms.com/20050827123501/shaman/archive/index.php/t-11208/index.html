shamuofxev
I spend a fair amount of time in CT, mainly doing maze mobs with a warrior with druid bot and my own warrior bot as meat shield. I would strongly advise against moving into the maze without a meatshield, the mobs hit too fast to rely on channeling.
Some peculiarities: Forget splitting of mobs - even at 65 you will mostly get 2, even if they are light blue. The casters are nasty, the healers have an aoe heal, the others have an aoe slow or aoe rain, both of which are unresistable. Some of the raptors and spiders are highly magic resistant, have malo and grumus up just in case. There is also a crazy bug that ports you to safe spot should you summon your horse too close to a wall.
Loot in CT can be pretty rewarding, we rarely got out of an evenings session under 10 k in bazaar sellable loot. Blood is about 2 per hour on average. We have gotten two pristine scales, compared to about 50 blood. Since you need 6 scales for the combines you will need to spend a hell of a time there to get a full set for an attempt.
