Has anyone else played around in here?

I've gone here the last few nights to try to kill some named but they seem pretty rare. I've saw 2 named in about 8 hours total. All the loot is droppable and some of it is pretty nice. Everything is solo pullable and easily root rotted.

How the arena works:

There are 2 tunnels on each side that are guarded by 2 mobs, either A Converted Laborer (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=14631) or A Twisted Stoneservant (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=14664) where random mobs wander in and kill these greenie slaves. One mob wanders out of one side, kills a slave or 2, then goes back in the tunnel and despawns. Then about 15 minutes later the same thing happens on other other side. I haven't timed it, that's a rough guess. There is also arena seats above the ground level w/ nocs, mastruqs, and aneuk glyphcallers in them which are easily single pullable. Simply cast on them and they warp down to you.

As for named:

There is a PH for a named up top, An Officiator (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=14218), for Officiator Keviazh (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=14219). I've yet to get the named to spawn but to get this one solo you need to invis and lev up, run thru the tunnels, go back up on the entrance side of the arena, cast, let her warp across then run float/run back into the arena. If you don't let her warp and you run down early she goes thru the tunnels and brings one nasty train. Trust me! ;) I get an add doing this occasionally.

The other named come out of the tunnels. The 2 unkillable Pixxt guys do zone wide shouts commentating on what is going on in the arena. They have a variety of shouts and I'm not sure if they are tied into the spawning of named. When I killed Pixxt Hihkx (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=14598) (dropped boots) they shouted about this beast that they had saw before and wanted it in the next fight. I've saw this msg again but the mob didn't come out. Also, when I saw Warlord Vokken (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=14635) they shouted about showing the slaves a true warrior. I didn't get to kill Vokken cause I didn't see him in time due to killing a different mob so he made it to the tunnel and despawned. :mad:

Hihkx hits for 800 and has a spirit call style pet. The pet hits for 400+ but all you need to do is root it and it poofs a few seconds later. I didn't have BoS or Bane when I killed this so I did it the long way w/ Ultor, Pox, and the occasional Anathema so my roots weren't constantly being broken and the pet came out again later in the fight. I'd imagine if you had Bane (100 pts away!), BoS, and Ultor running you'd only get 1 pet at the beginning of the fight.

I rarely kill the mobs that wander out of the tunnels b/c the commentators shouts seem to coincide with when a mob is due to come out so I don't get caught off guard. They also seem to be related to when a named is due to come out but it could just be a timed script that has nothing to do with the spawns at all. Any ideas? Do you think killing the mobs that do wander out would increase the chance of a named? Or do you think it is totally random?
