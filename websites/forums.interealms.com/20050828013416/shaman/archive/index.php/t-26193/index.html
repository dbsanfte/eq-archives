Vazzkorr
Hate to disagree, but windfury, while nice...is not the highest DPS on average...

Best weapon buff is rockbiter, hands down for an average increase in DPS

Second Best is Windfury. It has slightly lower average DPS but has a higher peak DPS capability.


Flametongue is situational, but can end up doing more damage against high AC mobs in some cases, since AC has nothing to do with resists.

I have parsed fairly extensively on this, but to give a relatively simplified example, consider the following

Lets take a weapon, where unbuffed you are averaging a hit of 90 damage

Buffed with Rockbiter, you may be averaging about 160 damage per hit


Now, the same weapon buffed with Windfury, will hit as hard, maybe slightly harder than rockbiter for two hits on proc (20 percent), and the same as unbuffed the rest of the time, so taking the raw numbers...
90 + (2*160)/1.2 = 154...

As you can see, DPS is close, but a bit lower...

My parse logs bear this out extensively...

I think too many people get attracted to Windfury from the occassional burst damage, and just assume that it's the most dps

Rockbiter downside is really aggro...if your using rockbiter, you generally will get/keep aggro...windfury causes aggro too, but not as much

Flametongue does have one other use. It allows you to use a weapon buff in group, that doesnt generate as much aggro, thus making it easier for the tank to hold the mobs.
