Okay -- so you regen 1 mana per tick standing and 22 mana per tick sitting.

<h2>Best Use of Canni III</h2>

Let's look at the formulas now:

x = mana per tick sitting
y = mana per tick standing
c = mana from canni III

<b>(OPTION 1) FULL CANNI DANCE</b>
(You stand, cast, and sit in time to catch the med tick all the time. So in 60 seconds you'll catch 10 out of 10 med ticks and cast canni 10 times.)

Mana Regen Rate Per Minute = 10x + 10c = 10*22 + 10*40 = <b><font color="red">622 mana in one minute</font></b>.


<b>(OPTION 2) PARTIAL CANNI DANCE</b>
(Whenever you're not casting you're sitting, but you don't pay atttention to med ticks. You just dance when your icons have ungreyed. In 60 seconds, you'll cast canni 15 times and catch about 6 out of 10 med ticks.)

Since canni III casts in 1.25 seconds, we'll add a little bit of buffer for lag and whatnot so that the total time that the server thinks you are standing is 1.5 seconds. The recovery time is 2.5 seconds. So every four seconds you will be up and casting for about 1.5 seconds and sitting for 2.5 seconds. There would be 15 such periods in a 60-second block, so you'll be casting canni 15 times during this period.

Mana Regen Rate Per Minute = 15c + 6x + 4y = 15*40 + 6*22 + 4*1 = <font color="red"><b>736 mana in one minute</b></font>


<b>(OPTION 3) CHAIN CASTING CANNI</b>
(Standing the whole time and casting constantly. In 60 seconds you'll catch no med ticks and cast canni 15 times.)

Mana Regen Rate Per Minute = 10y +15c = 10*1 + 15*40 = <b><font color="red">610 mana in one minute</font></b>.



<b>CONCLUSION</b>

<B>Fast Mana</b>: If your goal is to get mana back as quickly as possible (for example, you are in an intense group situation in which mana is the limiting factor or you have very high hp regen), then you should go for option two for pure speed. 126 extra mana per minute is a pretty huge difference between partial-canni dance and chain-casting. That's 12.6 mana per tick extra.

Compare that to Clarity I which gives 7 mana per tick, or clarity III which gives 14 mana per tick. (Admittedly, those are passive mana regeneration mechanisms, so they're in a league of their own.)


<b>Max Mana per HP</b>: If your goal is the maximize the amount of mana you get for each hitpoint you cannnibalize, then you should choose option one.

<b>Slower Mana, High HP cost</b>: Option three will neither net you the most mana overall nor the most mana per HP spent. It is a poorer choice in all situations.

For either method one or method two, all you really need is a HOTKEY and you are golden. (It's not very convenient without a hotkey.) Here's a hotkey that would work well for it:


/sit off
/pause 1, /cast 3
/pause 1, /cast 3
/pause 13, /cast 3
/sit on


This hotkey will attempt to cast canni III three times for each time you hit it, thereby eliminating the issue with Canni III's notorious fizzle rate.
