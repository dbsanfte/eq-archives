Litchebane
Summoning is the least of your problems. His mana drain which overwrites KEI is kind of a bigger problem. Also his knockback kinda sucks too. Basically put the MT with his back to the gate, keep the gate closed, and kill him with normal tactics if you have the DPS. If your DPS is low, then try and have two cleric rotations and leave the 2nd rotation outside until the first rotation has no mana left from the manadrain. If your DPS is high you will kill him before the clerics run out of mana. Oh yeah, make sure everyone has levitation, the floor is ice.
Giac
If the fight is still the same it used to be during the Velious era, you should be able to keep KEI if you keep up a charisma buff above it.
The AE mana drain DoT will then overwrite charisma leaving KEI.

You will just have to refresh charisma when the DoT drops, before he AE's again so it will go into the charisma slot again. Remember, whichever buff(charisma or KEI) will be higher(i.e. be above the other)will be canceled out by the DoT.

Horse and new mana buffs should provide enough mana regen to keep up with his damage easily. Good luck.

AE goes off every 2 minutes. Run out and avoid it. You'll only have to do that 2-3 times until Yelinak is dead, or you are.

To deal with knockback, have 2 main tanks, and be ready to use either depending on the proc.

Clerics just have to suck up the AE though.
