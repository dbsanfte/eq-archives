Taladaen
Load True North in spell slot 7
Load Sense Animal in spell slot 8

make a hotkey
/pause 20,/cast 7
/pause 20,/cast 8

Adjust the pause command for the amount of time for those spells to refresh, 20 = 2 seconds.

You can just mash on that button & cast those 2 spells over & over when you want to increase divination. My wizard is currently doing something similar to keep Abjuration (fizzled too many times trying to cast greater shielding) and Conjuration (familier) on a similar scale.
