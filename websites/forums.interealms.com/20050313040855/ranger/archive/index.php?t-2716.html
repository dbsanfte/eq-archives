MQ = multi questing. When one player helps another by turning in a no drop quest item to the NPC, then the player who is doing the quest turns in their own quest items to finish in the "turn in" to the NPC to get their item/reward.

Multi Quest

If an Quest NPC requires 2 bat wings (say they are NO DROP) and 2 rat whiskers, and you only have the whiskers, but a friend has the wings, you can complete as follows.
Your friend turns in the 2 NO DROP bat wings, you both confirm the items are turned in. You then hand in your 2 rat whiskers, and you will receive the quest Reward.

In fact, if you wish to be on the receiving end of the Reward, you only need 1 of the 4 components.
