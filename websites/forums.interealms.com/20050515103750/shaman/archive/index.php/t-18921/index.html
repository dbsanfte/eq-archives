Jinheim
I don't think anyone knows the exact ZEM number, but I get about .5% of an AA per kill from a full group in normal mode. This is well below even pov. I know lots of people that have done their 60's in LDoN, but it is only really worthwhile because you are getting adventure points at the same time as you're getting exp. It is definately viable, but it isn't nearly as fast as pov/bot. One of the biggest limiting factors is that between adventures, you've typically got at least a 10 minute downtime of rebuffing, getting new people, people taking afks, etc... If you're in a great group and you tear through an adventure in 25 minutes, you have to wait 20 minutes to get another one (can only do one ever 45 minutes). That really kills any possible exp rate. Typically, I ding every other adventure, sometimes it takes three if I started with practically no exp in to the point.
