Karshak_rn
Gonthorn,

The boats go up in difficulty from right to left as you enter the courtyard. Stormwave is easiest, then dirulia, dandolak, then the next two.

Start on Stormwave if you can. Invis onto the boat, as you enter it take a left at the top of the ramp you just ran up to get onto the boat. You are fine here as long as you stay close to the foot of the next ramp up to your left and close to the edge of the boat. Pull dog dog up and buff yourself and the dog.

Then run up the ramp hugging the left side of the boat. Stop when you hit the cannon. Turn and face the middle of the boat and target the mob standing there. Send in the dog and slow at 97 percent. This should leave agro on the dog. Dot around 85-90 then chain cast jbb if you have one.


Once that is dead target the mob to your left and send the dog in. Kill that one. Then the one in the opposite corner. Ok, now you have the top cleared. Head to the other ramp, and you will see 2 mobs standing fairly close. The first one is the PH for the captain which drops the best LoY charm. He also drops legs and a staff so the charm is not consistently dropped. Pull the closest mob with slow to bring it onto the top deck. You can back off here as it is coming up and engage pet. You might need to take a round or two of punches before the dog gets agro, or you can root as you back up.

Once that is cleared pull the other one with slow as well and repeat. Then head down the ramp and target the one near the mast. Send the dog in and treat as the first mobs. I think there is then two up front. Pull with slow to separate and waste them. If you have a horse and use mana conservatively you should be close to clearing the spawn just in time to med before the re-pops. Once you get the hang of it you can add the two engineers on the dock in by pulling with slow.

Dirulia has the same kind of approach. Safe spot at the top of the ramp, on the right. Once buffed do the same kind of cycle as stormwave but start off by heading right up the ramp. The captain PH is the right hand one of the triangle of mobs as you face the mobs from the mast. He drops a nice ring.

Good luck, sorry for the long winded breakdown, let me know if it was helpful, or if you need info on Dandolak or the other boats. They all have a safe spot, it is just finding it that is the problem. :)

Hey, first thing you want to do is get a spiritstone... they cost around 700-800pp to make and are player made with a 200+ pottery skill... Don't bother making one if pottery isn't your bag, just buy one in the bazaar, they run around 1Kish. They are race dependant:

http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/adv_search.html?stn=spiritstone

They have a 30 second cast but drop you fairly close to a pok book. I am a barb and when I was hanging out in Dulak I bound at the Gunthark/Dulak zoneline and just used the spiritstone to get back to pok (takes less than 2 mins). When I wanted to return to the fray I just gated and zoned back into Dulak.

Looks like you are close to achieving your objective...so pre-congratulations on that. At 57 you might find some good xp grouping in Valor at the walls, or IC/OC if you can get into a group. I found Nadox to be a fun spot at that level though if soloing is still your preferred method. Take the lava zone in, whack the three elementals you can see. Then pull the one under the lava back to the zone and kill it. From there you can head down the river to the central cavern. Head up the right slope and run along the bank to the wall. That is a safe spot you can pull to. Alternatively I found the Torgiran mines to be good fun too.
