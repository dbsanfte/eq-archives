Soe provide the goods, which do enough for people to maintain their "addiction" but I don't think they really inject fun into the game anymore and the emphasis seems to be about gimmicks and using tired old formulas in terms of new equipment, spells and new mobs. Admittedly I am generalizing I know there are some attempts of difference, it just seems incredibly minor to the game a whole.

I also think they don't engage in communication enough with certain communities or if they do take a overly long time. Admittedly, certain communities are adept at making issues out of nonissues, and no doubt it is hard from their end to try explain this without the background atmosphere of contemptment but efforts all count. Brett's communications to the different communities is a start though.

So some of the flack SoE do get is undeserved, however, in other ways they are the direct perpetruators of the way the game has gone to be cheapened and weakened. Although, it is also equally viable that a lot of people have finally begun to tire of the game and so want to attribute blame. Its kinda hard to recognize the most true reflection of the state of the game.
