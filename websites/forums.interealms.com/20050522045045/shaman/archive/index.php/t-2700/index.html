Bigguss
Hmmmm... my recommendation depends on the price of Ceremonial Iksar Regeneration BPs on your server. If they're 4k or less, then sell the Forest Loops (300+ each), scrounge up a little more cash, and get an iksar BP. This is without a doubt the item that will give you the biggest power increase.

If you can't afford an Iksar BP for awhile, JB pieces shouldn't cost that much more than Ry'gorr (at least on mature servers) - a few hundred plat each for the minor pieces (boots, gloves, arms) - each is a great value for the money.

Beetle stinger is a fine weapon. Ideally you'd want 1hb and 2hb weapons- but I wouldn't consider it critical. As you level, your melee will become less and less effective. Around 40 it becomes laughable. I never trained my 2hb skill as much as I should, but even if I had, the pitiful damage output (compared to the JBB or spells) makes it not worthwhile. I've even considered going to dolly route (Paw in main hand, 9 AC + 9 WIS... nice!) but I just can't make myself do it. Matter of principle.
