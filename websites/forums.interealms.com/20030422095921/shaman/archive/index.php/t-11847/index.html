Slissia
Because the lower cannis are short casting time, you can actually get them off in between ticks. The server only checks for whether you are sitting and medding at 6 second intervals, so if you time it right you can stand, cast canni, sit before the next tick, and get the benefits of both canni and medding.
Mamutoi
I made a macro for Cann. Don't remember exactly how it went but basically.
/sit off
/cast 2 (or whatever spell slot your Cann is in)
/pause 25 (this changes depending on which Cann you are using and what its cast time is)
/sit on

Hitting this macro will make you stand up, cast cann wait until spell has been cast then sit down again. Play with the pause time to get it down to as close to the cast time as you can.

If you are on a horse you don't have to worry about the Cann dance since you are always "sitting" so always get the med tick.
