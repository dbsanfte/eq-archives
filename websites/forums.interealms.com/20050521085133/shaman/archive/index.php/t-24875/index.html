1. No, you are fine. You need to choose your first area of specialization before level 10. You do this by talking to an npc in Q harbour. If you want to be an alchemist pick scholar first, then before lvl 20 you can pick alchemy.

2. You need to join a society. First choice will be a wholesaler society until you ding 10 and have picked your spcialization area (scholar). After that you need to join a scholar society. Look around in the Qeynos city zones for npcs standing next to doors. Talk to them and one will ask you if you want to join. NPCs in these tradeskill zones will give you tasks.
