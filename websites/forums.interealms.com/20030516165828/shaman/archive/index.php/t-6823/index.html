Cebilu
easily - I see shamans soloing there all the time
At 60 you can single pull the Revenants around the Ssra temple and can easily hold a couple of the camps there.

Some of the named snakes that spawn (and the named golems) drop grey flesh gear - good monk gear that sells very well. Also a chance at getting a Golems Jawbone (cant remember stats offhand - go look on Allakhazams)

Only things you really have to avoid are the many trains by Idiots running to Ssra to raid, Boulderlings (pain in the ass for my wizard - may not be too bad for a shammy), and the many roaming traps. However if you stay close to the temple you should not get aggro from any of the roamers and the traps do not come too close.

My main piece of advice is to get a second EB item as soon as you can and keep it in the bank - if you do manage to die (difficult as the zone to the temple is only a few yards away) you will need it to get back to your corpse, as i have yet to find a decent supply of fishscales on the moon. That said if you are non-KoS to katta, by hunting in the grey you can collect the 3 snake organs needed for a neck EB item (no drop)
