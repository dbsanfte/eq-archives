Drops of the netherbian swarmlord in the cave with Tawro. There is a large room in the cave that spawns a mini-ring type event. Swarmlord spawns after half a dozen cycles. It can be done by two or three groups (maybe one crack group of 60's with uber equipment).

I got the spear, and stat-wise its almost as good as the epic. Gives me a few more ac and hp over the epic, but fewer resists and of course, no DoT. For meleee, its like a 12/20 spear that only works part of the time (it checks vs magic on each hit). So you'll be hitting for 1, 2, 5, then it will go up to 20, 50, 75, then back down to 10, 2, 7, etc.
