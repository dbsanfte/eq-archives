I'm not sure if you understand about the dispell. Its not a one time cast dispell. Dragons dispell EVERY cast of their AE, with a single group he will get off more than enough AE's to wipe out all your buffs if you don't have at least one form of instant click buff. You used to be able to use bard songs to counter the dispells by having the bard song in the first buff slot, it would get dispelled then take hold again next pulse, but you can't do that anymore with the new songs buff window. I would recommend people get Gobbie Earrings, Telescopes, or something of that nature.
