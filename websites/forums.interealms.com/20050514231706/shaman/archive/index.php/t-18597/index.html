Okay, I'm just going to assume you're completely uneducated about what you're referring to. No point in assuming I misread, since, well, you came out and said it.

First off, "voodoo" doesn't exist, except in mostly-fabricated accounts by white protestant slaveholders trying to explain the beleifs of their three-dollar savages. The correct term is "vodoun", and isn't a "healing" religion - It's a supplication religion, where you give of yourself to higher powers - in this case, God. The spirits that practitioners pray to (not worship) are the Ioa, the messengers of god. NONE of them are evil, though some can have negative connotations, such as trickery, battle, that sort of thing. Santeria is a "sect" of vodoun developed to hide the religion under the noses of slaveholders. Instead of calling the Ioa by the names of Legba, Ogun, Shango, or Eshu, they used the names of Christian saints, and quite a bit of Catholic ceremony.

If you were wondering, animals are rarely sacrificed as such - Usually it's more common stuff like yams, cabbage, rum, or whatever. It's not a "blood sacrifice" thing, it's a "feed the spirits that carry our prayer to God" thing. Don't even get me started on that "zombie" crap.

Ahem. Sorry folks, I got some relatives, see...

As for "evil" shaman... Read up on the mythology of Shamanistic culture. Hell, for something closer to home, read news stories of modern religious figures who use their positions for personal gain or social detriment. Mix in fantasy details like evil races and gods and monsters galore, and you have yourself the "defiler" type of Shaman. Instead of helping his people, he blackmails them... Rather than supplicating the spirits, he abuses and enslaves them. Instead of healing the bodies and souls of those wounded and tainted, he CAUSES the hurt and taint. The defiler is essentially the anti-shaman, and in my eyes, has the potential to be the most thematicly frightening class in EQ.

Of course in game terms it means that they'll have the DoTs and the Mystics will have the heals... :cool:
