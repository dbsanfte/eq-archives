Unknown_shaman
Make some blistering flesh potions and get a 2 handed practice sword.

I'm PLing a tank atm. He's level 37, and I do do in my spare time.

I did newbie grounds Steamfont until level 14. Then took off for LOIO. Gave him some burning flesh potions. (1+lvl/2). Pull a whole bunch of skeletons and kitties and just have him whack away. The Skels and cats dont run when low, and as long as he gets a hit in you are good. The bonus is that all of his Defense, Dodge, Parry and Riposte skills will skyrocket. Just make sure to keep track of his skills in weapons and swap out when one maxxes. I have within 5 pts of Max skill in all weapons/bash atm.

I believe the practice sword starts proccing at lvl 20ish, which give a 6pt stackable DS as well. And I went to the Overthere at like 25. Was fun at lvl 25 to have my tank taking out lvl 38 tigers. Rhinos and tigers dont run when they are low and are great to pull in.

I don't know if Kats are KoS in the outpost (I think they are), but if they aren't the bonus is the bezerker whip quest which is significant exp til high 30s. Will also get you an OT hammer, which when trapped in a bad place is pretty good to whip out and get a proc to get yourself outta dodge.

Process was, when working on weapon and defense skill ups to NOT slow the critters and let them beat themselves against the tank until Defense and related skills were maxxed. Then you slow the critters until offense skills are maxxed. Then you start using DS potions and the Practice sword (Swap it out as soon as it procs for higher damage), just to mow things down to get to the next level. At less than 50% health I would JBB the critter.

Also get a horse. Nothing ever aggros a 60 shammy in most locations as long as they are standing up. And I never found mana regen a problem because of the horse sitting rate.

I plan on hitting CoM soon...Maybe tonight if I get the chance. There is a huge modifier there, but things tend to run when low there.

Try underwater, there is a very nice bonus for fighting with the risk of drowning (get an EB item, of course). Kedge Keep is great exp at the entrance until 35 or so, and with you there to keep him alive, you can probably do quite well there now.

Once you are done with Kedge, move to Lake of Ill Omens, and do the bloodgill goblins. They are good til near 50. I solo'd them with a Troll warrior with fungi starting at about 45, made mad exp.

Liberal use of potions helped me a ton. Try using cripple but not slow, or a lower level slow. Put up a damage shield and watch them melt. Buff the crap out of the twink, remember to use the group versions to get stuff onto him that you cannot get on him directly (group him, buff yourself).

My 2-box warrior is now 59th, soon to be 60th. He is invaluable for me, allowing me to do legitimate exping even for short periods of time, and providing me an instant group. Getting him flagged for second tier planes has been a pain, though. =)
