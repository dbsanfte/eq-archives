I promise you, my post is about Macroing cannidancing. Let me give you a brief history first. I got used to hitting '3' on my hotbutton to sit and stand, I was able to put things like forage, sense heading, or /loc and do them each time I sat/stood. I was frustrated that I could only cannidance 2 or 3 times in a macro even after the autostand command upon casting:

example 1:
/pause #, /cast 7
/pause #, /sit
/pause #, /cast 7
/pause #, /sit
/cast 7

I had tried to cast 5 times and manually sit while casting using a macro like this:

example 2:
/pause #, /cast 7
/pause #, /cast 7
/pause #, /cast 7
/pause #, /cast 7
/cast 7

But, I couldn't sit inbetween casts, because my hotbutton macro/sit thing wouldn't work. Turns out you can run two macros at the same time, but you can do the keystroke to sit. I never used Cntrl-S, so I mapped that in my options menu to the '.' key on my keypad. Now I use the example 2 macro, and hit '.' to sit inbetween casts.

Three cavets with hotbuttons that tooke me a while to figure out and others may find useful:

1) If you put a /pause and a /command in the same line, the pause ALWAYS occurs AFTER the line, never before. (aka, EQ responds to them like a stack instead of a queue)

Example 3:
/say Hello World,
/pause 100, /say this is a...
/say test!

If those three lines were in a macro, the following would happen:

Uber_Shaman_01 says, 'Hello World,'
Uber_Shaman_01 says, 'this is a...'
<<< 10 second pause >>>
Uber_Shaman_01 says, 'test!'

2) If a hotbutton macro was started, and its long and a group member decided to pull 3-4 mobs to camp... you don't have to duck out of the spells that are macroed and 'fight' with yourself to cast what you really want to cast. Just have a hotbutton that is short or does almost nothing, and click on it from the macro tab (the place you create macros to drag to your hotbutton bar). A macro started from here will immediatly stop any macro currently started. If you overwrite a long one with a short one, it effectivly stops the long one in progress.

3) The pet window that has the 'Pet attack' button is not useless. If you are in the middle of a macro (aka cannidance) you can still sic your pet on things using this, if you don't want to type /pet attack. As stated before, you can't use a hotbutton macro if one is already in progress.

Hope this made sense and is useful to someone. :)
