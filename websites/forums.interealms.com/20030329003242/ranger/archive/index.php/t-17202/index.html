If they made an awesome arrow, they get faced with the fact that most rangers who could get it would need only one. If the mob dropped more than one set, several rangers would be thus equipped with this arrow, and never lose it, ever.
This is catering to one class, and making an item that would never leave the game.
What they would like to do I am sure is make a mob that drops 20 arrows (timesink) that will be horded and used for the boss fight, but that isn't really possible since the rangers would have those arrows from that point onward.
The idea was to make some arrows expendable, but then many would cry that they bought EQ, why is it now being taken away with expendable arrows, they spent 45 aa points to get it, blah blah blah. On the flip side, they could make them summoned no rent arrows on an item with a limited charge. Then you get people who make corpses just to hold the arrows. With a 5 time charge on an item, and the ability to unstack arrows, you could have 100 instances of that one type of arrow. I am sure they wouldn't want that either. People are resourceful about finding ways around limitations. Any idea that limits usage generally has someone find a way around it quite easily.
So the big question is, what do you do?
The simple answer is, avoid that situation like the plague. Don't step in it, and you won't get any on you.
