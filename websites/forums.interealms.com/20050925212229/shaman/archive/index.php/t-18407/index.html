I think at 47/48 I was killing the Spirocs in TD, and I did this up to 49/50 if I recall correctly. Most of the time they were not camped on my server, and there was only a few times that I saw they were. Usually it was a wizzie or druid that did not know how to kite. Was entertaining.

The spirocs do tend to hit fast, and there is one that is a rogue. Be careful that you do not turn your back on him.

Loot... well, it blew, but I was looking for xp and not loot.

To get to TD, I hopped on the boat from Oasis, and jumped off at the first stop. Once I was on shore, I would turn left, and run until I saw the bird tower ( a meaner version than in South Karana ). I would call Rover, buff him, and I would pull birds to the shoreline. There were a few times in which I got adds, but they were easily rootable. Heck, you could even bind on the island, use your Spiritstone to port to Halas - run to PoK for kei and back again. At the time, I did not have a Spiritstone, but the possibilities with that little piece of goodness.
