Couple of notes on the Tower of Money, er Shadow.

Your bank account will not suffer for coming here at all. Lots of coin and FS type weapons.

A note on the keys.. Making possible arrangements to have a necro with a coffin might be a prudent thing to do if you get spanked. The key holder can be summoned to the zone and off you go again.

While many of the mobs are undead, many are not. Just depends on what floor you are on. They are packed in pretty good, so if you can get the chanter or a FD puller, you can control it. If not, well, they call it green death for a reason.

Key holders do pop pretty often, so without too much trouble, you can get a duplicate set of keys.

I would not hold out too much hope you will get something out of the wedding party.. their drops seem to be very rare. The enraged relative is a load compared to the rest of the mobs on that floor and will be blue to you. Be prepared.

The upper floors will present some tougher mobs that you will get xp off of them. Tserrina is just a plan nasty mob and you never know when you will get her. She is not stunnable or snare/rootable. (She will head down the hallway and out into a big chamber where there are a lot of mobs on a very fast respawn). She does drop some awesome stuff, not only for chanters but her neckpiece (5 wiz, 45 mana, 5 char 2ac) rocks for wizzy casters.

All in all, I like doing the Tower.. Goodluck and good hunting.. :)
