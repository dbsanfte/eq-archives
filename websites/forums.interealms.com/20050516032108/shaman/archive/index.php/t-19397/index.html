Yeah, there seems to be a big lull in buying now. I have stuff that used to sell overnight, everytime I put it up sitting in my bags for days at a time now. I figure it has to do with a couple of factors: 1) the big SOE casino that drained literally millions of plat out of the economy during it's short run without really putting anything of value back in the market and 2) The great exploit crackdown. Now that the massive influx of platinum has come and gone and the economy has had a lot of that plat taken out of the system, we've hit a recession in the EQ economy. We all are farming gear left and right to sell, but very few people have the plat to buy it with.

Will the economy recover? Eventually, and when it does prices will be much more reasonable than they've become in the past weeks/months. In the meantime, be patient and remember that there are other avenues to acquiring the hard plat to buy what you need with, like farming stuff that is vendor sellable and farming coin drops. It's slower and it's much harder work..but in the end it gets you to the same goal.
