Aye I have to agree with Juventus and Krytog, the giant fort and for that matter BW in general. I spent most of my time soloing 54 in BW (no JBB or epic). Actually wasn't to bad. First I meleed a lot to max out my skills (worth it imo) and then I did the root/rot method to speed things up.

The giant fort and the entrance to Chardok are camped a lot, so a few other options exist for that zone:

1) Pull to the zone edge west of the giant fort. Lots of mobs over there. So many sometimes that some folk have nick-named it "The Zoo" and/or "The Highway."

2) Pull hornets to the road where it starts up the hill towards DL. *Nothing* wanders overthere so no adds. I did this and had a few gem drop and up to 6pp on a lot of horents. Not a bad spot. Just watch out for the hornets are social and they don't run.

3) There is a small undead house just south of the giant fort. You could pull to the cracter that the giant fort sits over. Not bad. An occasional mob does wander up to the rim and others do pull up there.

4) Sarnak are not that bad on pulls either. Just that fighting a shaman can be a pain if he slows you and you are meleeing him. They also run and with their sow can be a pain.

Little fyi: You can camp out down in the crater under the giant fort. Nothing wanders down there.

My favorite pull spot in BW - from the giant fort's ramp, head right and down the hill to the burned area that faces the meteor crater. (Looks like a big hill from this side). Mobs wander into the edge of the burned area and just stop. If you stay deeper in the burned area you are completely safe from wanderers.

I can solo everything but wurms and Gullerback there easily and safely at 53. I leave the wurms alone unless I have a melee partner - I can usually kill 'em but I have an awful lot of downtime afterwards.

Epic - no, JBB - yes, Fungi - no.
