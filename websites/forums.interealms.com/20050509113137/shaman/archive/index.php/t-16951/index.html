I was wandering what would be my bet bet as far as gear goes.

ANYTHING with +'s to hit points. If it doesnt have that..dont use it. If you're on sullon zek like it seems..you also want as much for resists as you can get (magic being the most important i think--snare, root, mez, druid DOTs, chanter spells in general, cleric spells in general, paladin spells in general).

Also, DONT use purely wisdom gear...EVER. If you start to think to yourself "wow i have a lot of wisdom, i'd make a good druid"...yeah, that means you need to ditch the druman gear.

Finally, up to the lower 50s you'll want a decent amount of AC as things you're fighting will hit you much less, and you're more likely to be tanking what you fight (49+ pets make nice tanks really).
