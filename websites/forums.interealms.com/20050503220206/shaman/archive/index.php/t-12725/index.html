Shandy Orcbane
Huge turning point -- you begin to get a taste of what real power is like.

Your companion's fragile, but if you keep him alive he can be a nice little 30 point/tick dot.

Bites for 30, kick/bash for 10. Does a good job interrupting casters that'd be dark blue to you at that level. He can dual wield (but doesn't do so on his own) so try to convince him to do so by giving him 2 rusty weaps. This will "force" him into dual wielding. The off-hand attack is pretty rare, tho... but it exists.

Buff his dex, then give him a 1h velium weapon first (then trade before giving him a 2nd weap). Think you'll be pleasantly surprised.

You get a good DoT at 34: Scourge (hits for 40, does 504 in 2:06) Talisman of Tnarg will go a long way towards promoting your (and your companion's) survivability.

I solo'd a LOT doing the root/rot and finish with melee methods.

At this level, you can take the first couple of steps towards the Spear of Fate without any outside help. By the end of 34, you can be holding the Shield of Falsehood.

34 was fun =)
