No, resists don't recyle.

Resists cap out at 500, and they never "roll over" or recycle or anything. I'm not entirely sure if they EVER did that, but if they did, it was fixed about 3 years ago, or more. It is amazing to me how long these rumors can live.

With Psalm of Veeshan I can easily get all resists over 300, or at or extremely close to 500 with other resist buffs and maximum song modifiers active. It makes you practically immune to all magic - except for uber raid targets who have humonguous negative resist modifiers on their effects. And you can resist even a lot of those. Against "normal" spells a high enough resist stat makes you practically immune.
