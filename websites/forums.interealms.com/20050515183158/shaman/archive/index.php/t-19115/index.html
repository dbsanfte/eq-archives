I will probably go with conjuration. I spend my time raiding, grouping, and soloing/duoing all fairly evenly. For damage raiding and grouping I almost always go with velium strike and/or tears of saryrn. For soloing/duoing I pretty much always dot. In experience groups or raids if I'm running low on mana I can just stop nuking for 2-3 mobs and it really doesn't have much of an impact. On the other hand if I'm running low on mana while soloing/duoing there is a huge difference if I stop dotting for 2-3 mobs since I'm usually doing at least 50% of the damage. So for me the mana savings would make a much larger impact on conjuration. Like Dredak said though, it all depends on your play style.
