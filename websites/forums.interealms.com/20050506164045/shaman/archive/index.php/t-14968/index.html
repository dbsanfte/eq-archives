Kianor
Ranger tracking skill maxes at 200, which can be obtained at level 39.

What level you need for this very much depends on the mobs you're trying to track and how close you can get a level 1 char to them. In some cases, a rogue with sneak/hide may well be better than a newbie ranger, because it may be easier to level a rogue to 3 and move him up to the spawn point, rather than level a ranger to the point where he can get the required tracking skill.

I'd suggest the ranger boards might be the best place to ask, because even if you don't intend to play a ranger, they still understand tracking, and spawn tracking in particular better than anyone else.
