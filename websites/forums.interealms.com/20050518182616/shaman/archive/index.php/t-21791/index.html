Kianor
Hi, welcome to the Crucible.

It really doesn't matter. Pick whichever you like the look of. Each race has slightly different attributes and there's not a lot to distinguish between them.

A quick summary though:

Trolls have innate regen which really can help at low levels, but they have an experience penalty also. They're also a large race and hence have slam.

Ogres are immune to stuns from the front, which can be very useful, so I'm told. They are also a large race with slam and an exp penalty.

Barbarians have no night vision, but are the most "human" looking, so the most popular. They used to be the only non-evil shaman race, but that's no longer true. They're large with slam, and a slight exp penalty.

Iksars have an exp penalty and innate regen to compensate. They have an AC bonus, but can't wear jaundiced bone armour which means no nuke bracer, which can help a lot at around 45-55, but probably isn't as big a deal as it used to be.

Vah Shir are cats, and have safe fall. Pretty neutral choice, no major drawbacks, no major advantages.

Similar with frogloks. They have a cute backflip when they cast a spell, and some people think they look good. Again no major drawbacks or advantages.

Hope this helps, and feel free to send me a tell on Xev if you see me on.

Everyone overlooks the Vah Shir's hidden ability...sneak.

Sneak lets Vah Shir buy and sell anywhere, so long as they aren't KoS. This is an unbelievably handy little ability, especially at lower levels. It's also quite useful for getting past KoS mobs without having to fight. Of all racial abilities, I think Sneak is the most overlooked, and probably one of the most useful.

Vah Shir eat and drink twice as fast, yes, but that's in a trade off for their ability to burn out poison/disease DoTs twice as fast. At lower levels, again, this is very handy. Think they call it enhanced metabolism or something.
