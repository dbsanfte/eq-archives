1: Increase Str Cap by 30
2: Increase Agi Cap by 30
3: Increase Dex Cap by 30
4: Increase Hitpoints v2 by 278 per tick
5: Increase STR by 30
6: Increase DEX by 30
7: Increase AGI by 30

for 5 ticks

Increase Str Cap by 60
2: Increase Agi Cap by 60
