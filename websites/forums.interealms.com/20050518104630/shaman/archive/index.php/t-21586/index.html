Yes, they have fixed it, but you need everyone to know what they are doing.

What happens now is that and Enraged Imp non-agro spawns randomly when you kill a scareling, and killing this will spawn the Ireblind Imp.

You just need to not attack the Enraged Imp, it is non-roaming, and non-agro, but will assist any scarelings you attack close to it.

The only difficult bit, is making sure everyone knows not to attack the Enraged imp, and to move your break-in point if you spawn it.


The only other difficuly is, if the Ireblind Imp is already spawned when you zone-in, but then you just need to make sure you don't agro him.

Bandicoot
Ireblind imp is really not a problem to avoid, even if he is at the zone in.

He will aggro on you but if you don't fight him he eventually loses interest and wanders back to his spawn point.

So all you have to do is set up camp from some distance away from where you see him and leave him alone. If he adds just fight the other mobs and within about 30 secs he is gone. His behaviour is unlike any other mob I have seen (who will fight on once aggroed).
