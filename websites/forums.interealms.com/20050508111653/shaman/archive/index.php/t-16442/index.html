Sariss
The points you get, are based on YOUR level, versus the level of the Adventure you get.

Your level is pretty self explanatory... higher your level, the more points you get.

The dungeon level... is based on the average level of your group.

If the average level of your group, is 63.00 or higher... you will get a "Level 65 adventure". In a 65-A Level 65s will get 51 pts.

If your average group level is 60.00 to 62.99 you will get a level 60 Adventure. This adventure will be easy for level 65s in the group... you'll notice less hp mobs, less powerful spells/traps.... you'll basically kill a lot faster.

In a 60-A level 65s will get 34 points.

I've tested this several times over the last couple of days... I was sick of getting 51 points this time, and 34 next time... I don't always group with a full group of 65s... many of my friends are 59-65.

Selecting the HARD setting... will bump your Adventure level by 5.

If your adventure is level 60... and you select hard... it will be the Normal adventure for a group of level 65s (65-A). If you're in the level 65-A and you select hard, you get bumped to level 70-A which is the nasty 900 hitters.

Yes , I've tested it.
