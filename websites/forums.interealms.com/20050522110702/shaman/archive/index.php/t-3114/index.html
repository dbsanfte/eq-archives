I dont know if everyone here has an enduring breath item and i am the last one to get on the bandwagon, but there is a really easy quest for a eb item in luclin.

You need to get 3 shissar organs (no drop) and give them to a guy in Katta (the same building as rallic aristos).

The organs can be found on the Taskmaster something in echo caverns (with the 8 skele spawn) and on various shissar in the grey.

The result is this:


Amulet of the Grey Wastes
MAGIC LORE NO DROP
NECK
sta 10 sv cold 4 sv magic 4
Effect: Enduring Breath
wt 0.1
ALL/ALL

All in all it is a good little item for about 2 hours work, and most of that was running from the grey to katta :)
