Benjdragon
Check out what they are going for in the Bazaar.

This is on Bristlebane.
Normal prices for SoW are about 90 plat for a 10 charge.
Ant's potion, Antiweight, and Unlife Awareness goes for 175-180 plat for a 10 charge.
Kilva's Blistering Flesh is 200 plat a 10 charge.
Kilva's Skin of Flame is 400-500 plat a 10 charge.

If you are carrying them around and not selling in the Bazaar you might be able to ask 10% more for them.

I have found that the stat potions sell very rarely. Even if priced at cost.

Hope this helps.
