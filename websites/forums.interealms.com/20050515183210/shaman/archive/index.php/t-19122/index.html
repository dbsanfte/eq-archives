Actually I was thinking more about this AA and how my guild handles healing during raids, both bosses and yard trash. And considering how things are right now, and how things might possibly go in terms of encounter design and difficulty I think this AA would be an extremely bad idea.

The only times our clerics run out of mana during a boss fight are fights which have an AE mana drain/dot. Think Saryn, and Terris Thule in Plane of Time. With this sort of AA, clerics get to keep functioning, without the support of other characters, that is a bad design direction in my opinion. If future encounters include mana drains, clerics get to keep going and casters without an instant mana surge button are **** out of luck.

Encounter designers have to design with this AA in mind so make fights last a bit longer than they would have had to before which in turn makes clerics EVEN more necessary, something that most agree is problematic for raid and guild composition.

There are probably other unintended consequences with this AA in terms of encounter design, and if I was a designer for this game something like this would've probably been nixed from my list a long time ago.
