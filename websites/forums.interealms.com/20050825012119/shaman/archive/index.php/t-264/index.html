Chutoi
Short answer, any buffs under level 50 can be cast on any character. I think any buffs over level 50 have to be cast on level 45 plus. Not sure about the 41-45 range Morbad mentioned. I haven't tried it.

This does lead to some fubar'd results. For instance:

Chanters can cast celerity at 39--ie on anyone. Shaman cannot cast celerity on anyone under 45 because it's a lvl 56 spell for us.

Componentless levitate and enduring breath can only be cast on characters 45 and up because they are spells over level 50--if you want to help a newbie, you still have to carry components and dig out the old spells.

However, compare these with group buffs. I can group with 5 level 1 characters, and cast group focus . . . on myself. It hits all of them. Now that group spells carry the same stats as single target spells, you can still get a few high level spells on low level people if you want.
