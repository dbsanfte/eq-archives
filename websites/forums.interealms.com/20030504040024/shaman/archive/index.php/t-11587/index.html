Kialan Lecariuz
I know there is a stoicism thread in the spells section - yet there is confusion whether or not the quest is broken/fixed. I can honestly tell you, IT IS FIXED. I just got Stoicism for myself and a level 49 shaman. Here is how you do it.

IMPORTANT NOTE: Always hail the npc before doing a turn in! ALWAYS! Or else there is a possibility they eat the item.

1) Go to Dulak's Harbor and kill drunken pirates till "Empty Flask" drops. It is no drop, and can either be a fast or slow camp. Took me 40 kills, took another 3.
2) Go underwater to the "an impassive sea spirit" in Gulf of Gunthak at /loc 700, -300, -150 (underwater in a sunken boat). Have the flask in your inventory, target and hail him. Give him the empty flask. You should now have "A Large Rock" in your inventory. It is no drop, 26 pounds, and giant (doesn't fit in rallic pack even!). Hold on at this point.
3) Make pet, buff up, make sure you have waterbreathing, etc. Mem a DOT or two, and make sure pet is hasted.
4) Target and hail the sea spirit again. Give it the large rock. This should spawn two elemental NPCs near the opening of the sunken boat named Flotsum and Jetsam. They are Green to a level 60, Light Blue to 54, and I hear Dark Blue to 42.
5) Pull with slow first, and kill both. Nothing a good DOT or two shouldn't fix! They only hit around 60 damage.
6) Loot the "Part of (elemental's name)" from each of them. You should have two items.
7) Target and HAIL the impassive sea spirit. Give him both items at the SAME TIME. Congrats, you have recieved Spell: Stoicism!!

Enjoy!

Finished this quest over the weekend and thought I'd post my summary:

My information during the quest:
Level 47 Troll Shaman on Terris Thule
900 AC, 915 ATK, 1750 hp
Buffs: Tali of Altuna, Guardian, Fury, Alaricity, Chloroplast, Spirit of Bihli

Dulak's Harbor:
Took me about 20 drunks before I got my flask, plus about 400 pp in vendor loot (gems, bracelets, etc...) I zoned it, invised and ran along the right wall to the harbor. I set up along the barn wall opposite the drunk houses, right next to the pile of boxes & barrels in the corner. I didn't have any aggro in this spot from wanderers, even while fighting. If I stayed along the barn wall and moved farther inland, I would aggro an Engineer's assistant through the barn wall. Dark blue, not too hard a fight but wastes time.

Fight strategy was: slow (Togors), Venom of Snake, & melee (I have a Rune Etched Icewurm Fang plus Silver Chitin Hand Wraps). They conned dark blue/light blue at about a 50/50 ratio. By the time VoS wore off, they were at 20% so I just melee'd them down. The never ran when low on hp and dropped easily. Also, I have very, very few resists on the drunks, either to slow, root, VoS, Scourge or Blast of Poison.

I pulled the following drunks:
Hut closest to water: Drunk inside door on the left. Invis, open door, target drunk, run to fight spot, pull with slow. Single pull with a full spawn in the hut, no adds.

2nd hut: I think I pulled from this hut. Same strategy as above.

3rd hut: Sleeping drunk on cot to the left of door. Same strategy with pull, single pull with no adds with a full spawn in the room (even though they were ~10 feet away and staring right at the sleeping drunk).

4th hut: Drunk standing outside, single pull with slow, no adds.

5th hut (last in row away from water): I never pulled from or checked to see if there are any drunk spawns inside.

All the fights were single pulls with no adds if I stayed next to boxes/barrels along the barn wall. If I moved inland along the barn wall, I would aggro passing guards and some through the barn wall. A couple of times I screwed up (pulled while guards were walking by) and got 3-4 adds. I hoofed it to the zone line easily with SoW, bringing a fairly large train but zoned with no problems.

Empty flask graphic is purple, not broken, no-drop, SHM only.
The rest of my loot was Shiv's, broken bottles, some words/runes, and gems.

Gulf of Gunthak:
Once I got my empty flask, I gated back to the lighthouse and buffed up for the final fight. I summoned a pet and dropped everything I had on me and dogdog. Note: Dogdog was not Alphatized.

Buffs:
Tali of Altuna, Guardian, Fury, Alaricity, Chloroplast, STR, DEX, STA, AGI, CHA, Spirit of Bihli, Enduring Breath (IMPORTANT since you go underwater). NO BEARFORM!!! Dogdog had everything but Enduring Breath.

Swam underwater to Impassive Sea Spirit (+700, -300), hailed him and gave him the empty flask. I had 1 open inventory spot, but the Large Rock ended up on my cursor. I moved behind him (facing the open end of the boat), hailed him again and gave him the Large Rock. Flotsam and Jetsam immediately spawned at the open end of the boat. Both were Dark blue and scowling, but did not aggro. Pulled left one with slow, single pull. Dropped VoS, sent dogdog and melee'd. They do run when low on health so be sure to root. The fight was amazingly simple and lasted less than 30 sec (VoS didn't have a chance to wear off). Looted the piece and pulled the second one with the same strategy. Same fight, looted the second piece. No resists on either of them.

Went back to the impassive spirit, hailed again and turned in the 2 pieces. I had an open inventory slot again, but the spell scroll ended up on my cursor. Memmed it right there and it rocks! :) And yes, a few times I've gotten an extra healing tick if you cast it at the right time.

I got good experience for the quest, don't remember exactly but I think about a bub and a half (blue) xp.

Happy hunting to all you Stoicism questers!

Elnrik
If you ever notice a mob not respoinding to you when you say the [text in the bracket] it's because you don't have that mob targeted. Same with mobs gobbling up turn ins. (Pretty sure of this, anyway.)

Btw... spent two 5 hour sessions in Dulak camping those pirates before the darn thing dropped. (Lucky me, huh?) During that time I discovered some stuff I thought I'd share.

I camped the houses as per the guide above, and out of sheer boredom, discovered that the houses along the docks have drunken pirates as well. (So do the houses behind the warehouse (opposite side of where the guide says to pull to), but those are almost impossible to pull as there's nowhere to pull them to without adds.) Pulling the ones along the dock became easy once I found out that if you stand inbetween the door frame, while the door is open, you can click the door to close it and find a few hidden drunks behind the door(s). This worked on the houses described in the guide above as well. This makes it easy to pull, as you target, reopen the door, get to a safe distance, and cast slow to pull without aggro'ing a single add. Then walk them back to the area with the barrels and boxes, as per the guide, and have your pet kill the sorry thing.

Anyway... once I got the drop, I headed into Gunthak to finish the quest. To keep you guys from looking around for that elusive Impassive Sea Spirit, it's in the wreckage of a boat that is half way between the lighthouse and the shore. (Sorry, didn't get a loc.) It's all the way in the back of the wreck, and it's a really small mob. If you are running around with your clip plane turned down, you might miss it like I did.

Turn in, get rock, buff pet, turn in rock... Flotsam and Jetsam spawned behind me at the entrance to the boat that was surrounding me. They didn't aggro. Really easy kills. It took one Envenomed bolt plus the pet kicking it's ass and they dropped quickly. EB didn't even finish. Single pulled them w/ slow. Loot, turn in, scribe and happy cann'ing shammies.
