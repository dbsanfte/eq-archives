stygiana
Looking at a few recent discussion I would like to add my 2 cents to the discussion about a can VI spell.

Like many I do not feel the need for carpal tunnel syndrome and so I would like a Can that is less clicks, but still gives a decent amount of mana back.
So I think we need some form of mana over time (MoT) spell

My idea would be something along the lines of:

Slot Description
1: Decrease Hitpoints by 600 per tick
2: Increase Mana by 200 per tick


Mana: 0
Skill: Alteration
Casting Time: 3.0
Recast Time: 30
Fizzle Time: 2.5
Range: 0
Location: Any
Time of Day: Any
Fizzle Adj 0
Deletable: No
Interruptable: Yes T
arget Type: Self
Spell Type: Beneficial

Classes: SHM/65
Duration: 4 ticks


This spell would also have to block can, can II, can III and can IV casting else it would be way to powerfull.
The recast time is 1 tick higher then the duration so it would not possible to keep it up at all times but maybe this should be longer then 1 tick.

The general idea is to give us a short duration “buff” that converts hp to mana at a less then optimal ratio but that in return frees you from the chain clicking.
Maybe the hp and/or mana ratios need some tweaking, maybe the duration should be longer or shorter and maybe the recast time need some work but it is the idea that I like.

I hope some input will follow and I really hope this is something SoE would consider as for me it would be a nice addition to our spells

Sithrax
This spell would be great - though I'm not sure SOE envisions us getting back 200 mana a tick in addition to Cann5. However, the idea is fundamentally sound. Let's face it, the pacing of EQ has changed tremendously with PoP and LDoN. Passive mana regen is now FAR FAR better than active regen. Plus, I'm sick of mashing my Cann4 hotkey 1000 times a night.

There are several ways to address this:

1) Mana over time, as described. I don't know that I'd stay with the suggested efficiency ratio or mana generation rate, but something along these lines would make a big difference.

2) A high damage Canni with a long recast time (30 sec or so). This could also leave a recourse icon to prevent Cann1-4 from stacking with it.

3) An AA version of either of the above.


Any of these options would go a long way towards reducing tedium. I don't know why everyone thinks SOE can't implement this... it's more like they won't. Modrods don't leave an icon, yet they have a reuse time attached to them - does the recast time go away if you get a brand new modrod? I haven't tested this, but I think the reuse timer is persistent with your character, not with the rod itself. Same thing with the Donal's BP... leaves an icon, won't allow another Donal's CH to land until it wears off. This suggests to me that it is at least possible to create a circumstance where spells that lack an icon can prevented from stacking. Maybe this is an item-dependent phenomenon, but at least it holds out some hope.

This is a cool idea. Although I don't think they'll ever give us what is essentially a lich spell, it could be done like this:

They could call the spell Injured Bear. Conversion would be about the same ratio as other canni spells but more hp/mana than CanniIV, probably something like 225hp/125mana.
