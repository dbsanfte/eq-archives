A guide to mobs that can be slowed, with thanks to Nixx for the inspiration. Last Update 22 April 2003.

When posting updates please state:

- The FULL name of the mob
- The zone
- If you managed to slow the mob with Turgurs
- If you managed to slow the mob with disease slow and which (Turgurs bounces)
- If the mob gives the immune to changes in attack speed message (client message)
- Mob does not give immune message but to all intents and purposes appears to be unslowable

Please do not post 'my mate slowed soandso' unless you have it on very good authority,
first hand experience is the most useful! Screenshots of contested (in terms of being slowable, not popular)
mobs yawning are appreciated!

It is assumed that magic, disease debuffs are cast - MaloX / Tash, Insidious Decay etc.

<a name="menu"><strong>Key:</strong></a>
Magic - Mob is slowable using magic based spells, Turgurs etc
Disease - Mob is slowable using disease based spells, Grummus etc
Immune - Mob is immune to slow spells and is reported as such by client
Unslowable - Mob gives no immune message but is to all intents and purposes immune to slow
Mitigates - Mob can only be partially slowed

** Possible to slow with turgurs but difficult
*** Possible to slow with turgurs but very, very difficult
% First floor only, other Elites are immune
