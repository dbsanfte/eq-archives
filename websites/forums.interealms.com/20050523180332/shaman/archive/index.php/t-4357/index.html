Fenira
Pets supposedly don't break mez anymore, or aggro monsters if they are not engaged in a fight.

10% healing penality was removed from non-clerics.

DoT nerf on moving mobs was removed.

Tons and Tons of very nice trade skill items were added.

Alt Abilities were added, some of which are VERY nice. Some of course are crap.

Caster Focus Items were added. They do various things; make spells last longer, cast faster, use less mana etc.

Newbies now return to their bind point fully dressed with no xp loss until after level 10.

The Bazaar is in the game and functioning. Very easy to make a nicely equiped twink in mid level gear now. :)

Shared chat channels are in the game. Can also talk CROSS Server now.

IMO the game is more interesting.
