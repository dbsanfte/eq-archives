Molnarr
One of my RL friends is the MA for our guild. For big raids, he wants me to bring some damage shield potions, especially the more expensive kind. They stack with druid/mage damage shields, and he ends up with a combined damage shield that does more than 50 damage per hit. I only consider this viable if the raid is for you or if the guild will at least partly reimburse you the cost of the potions, but it can make a real difference in damage and can help the tank maintain aggro.

I also recommend bringing some shrink potions as mentioned by others. This is not so much to make it easier to fit through doorways. Instead, it makes it so more melee characters can get within reach of the mob. This is especially true for Hate since the mobs are small and shrink can not be cast.
