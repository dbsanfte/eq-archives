Started a Kitty Shammy and love her. My main is a 56 SK so I was able to accumulate a fair amount of cash to twink with. I think I had 9k to twink with and feel like I made the most with the cash. To be honest most of this gear was purchased by buying low in the Bazaar, and I really didn't have to spend tons of time there to find good deals. Just wait til an item you want is being sold by many peeps and you can usually get an excellent deal.

Also, I'm a battle shammy and tried to strike a balance between AC/HP and WIS. Another great thing to increase WIS is to buy a Misty Thicket Picnic and one of those tea drinks (Oakleaf Tea?) and place them in your top inventory slot. They add awesome stats (+10 wis). Just remember to eat a ration and water every 30 minutes or so and you wont consume these.

I just dinged 40 with a defense of 195, and I think buffed (Nimble, Shield,Fury, Talisman) my AC is 940ish and HP are 1450.
