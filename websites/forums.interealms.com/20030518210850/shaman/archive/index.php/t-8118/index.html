All turn-ins must be done in the order outlined in the walkthrough. Assuming you have not factioned at all, the next mob will not like you if you do not do the previous bit first. For instance, when I gave my woe/envy/marr's promise to the wandering spirit, without doing the test of patience, he ate it.

The following items can be killed and looted at any time:
Tear from Iskar's Child in Fear
Ghiosk's books from CoM
5 reports and Student's Log from CoM
Black Dire's pelt from Mistmore
Woe/Envy/Marr's Promise from Rathe Mountains

Everything else - and all the turn-ins - must be done in order. (Kirn, Neh, Rak, the tests, etc).
