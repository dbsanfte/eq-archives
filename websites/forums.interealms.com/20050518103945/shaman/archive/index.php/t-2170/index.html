Cush
The shaman is a different class, because how you play changes as you level.

At lower levels, you're more a melee with some buffs - you can also play sole healer for your group. Don't forget about using Inner Flame at low levels, instead of the heal spells.

Buffs help, but their aid can be limited... a lot of the help is in people's minds. So go as appropriate. Not sure when we get our first root, but I pretty much always keep one memmed. Actually, I keep my best heal, root, gate, and select debuffs/offensive spells up, switching in for buffs as needed. Of course, at lower levels, they don't last long, and you don't have as many to choose from.

As for what you cast now, the really big thing is to keep your skills up - especially if you're levelling fast.

As for spells, dot and melee is probably the easisest way at that level. Pull with the disease dot, melee till dead, heal yourself with Inner Fire (twice as mana efficient as your 1st lvl heal) and repeat. At 9th lvl, you can add your first poison DoT to the mix.
