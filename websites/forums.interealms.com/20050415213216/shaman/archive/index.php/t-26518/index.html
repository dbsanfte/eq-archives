Wondering if anyone can find a flaw in this idea? Which would in one swoop fix a number gripes across classes... I truly believe the panther issue has nothing to do with the spell itself as is, but rather it's a reflax to wizard complaints. Lets stop thinking every situation has to be solved with a nerf.

Here is my proposal for the changes, based on the rational given that a wizards can't out DPS us when we cast it on OTHER players.

Add a new spell line for Enchanters which adds an increase chance to critical your spells (or add additional modifier to your nukes) that will be equivilant to how panther ups the dps of melees. (short duration buff)

With that you would no longer need to nerf the spell and make everyone unhappy, wizards would have nothing to complain about. Enchanters would feel useful agian on raids where there is nothing to mez and you would promote another layer of interdepence and promote a fun enviroment. Shaman have fun casting it, melee's have fun recieving it. Same would go for enchanters and nukers. Yes I know fun is a dangerous thing, but we are playing a game here.
