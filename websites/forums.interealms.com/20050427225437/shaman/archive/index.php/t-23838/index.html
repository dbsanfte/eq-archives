/assist Rogue
/pause 5
/pet attack

That would have my pet assist target the rogue and kill the dude as he popped.

Using this method, the guy was never up more than 30 seconds before being killed.

At that rate of kills, it took about 36 hours. He spawns every ~6 minutes. That's 10 kills per hour for 36 hours = 360 kills. Hell of a lot better than killing off Mistmoore over and over - but it's still a lengthy process. If it weren't for Autofire, I would have shot myself in the head.
