Thyrm Jarlson
I had this happen on my second PC, but not on my main. Since having 2 computers, I could easily compare the eqclient.ini files. There was a line on my secondary that looked like this:

SpellParticleDensity=0.000000

And my main:

SpellParticleDensity=0.500000

So I made the other one the same and it fixed the problem. This setting will put spell particle density at medium.
