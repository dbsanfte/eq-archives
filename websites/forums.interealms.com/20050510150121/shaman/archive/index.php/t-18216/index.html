The only hastes that are stackable, are the hastes in lucy listed as "v2 or v3".

Note how Lucy lists the haste as:
Target Type: Group v2

It will stack with your v1 haste. If you want to exceed the cap, you need v3.

One v1 + one v2 + one v3 = maximum haste possible.

There are some bard songs that are v1.

Since you were either doing Swift Like the Wind (60 percent), or Talisman of Alacrity (50 percent), and not everyone has a 40 percent haste item, it is a decent song to twist in.

As to what song are "best" to have twisted, what composition was your group? To a point, it can be subjective, and also can depend upon what sort of tasks you're having the bard do (just buffing, meleeing, pulling, crowd control). Sometimes it's best to just let people do their thing, and not get too irritated by it, esp in pickup groups.
