Your gear is pretty good, but as you did have alot more wis then you need :)

For rings: Shadel Bandit and a 6/65 ring will be good

For Back: Ancient Seahorse hide Cloak, or Hiero/Lodi if you have cash

For Chest: Fungi/Iksar BP. Sell the current one to help finance if you need too. Regen >>>>>>>>> Wis, and losing 20 wis hardly hurts your mana pool anyway.

For Wrist: Jaundiced Bone Bracer, or Coldain Military wristgaurd. Bracelet of Beauty is crap for shamans, get rid of it. JBB is a huuuuuge asset for us shamans, and if thats out of your price range(14-20k on Saryrn), then do the Coldain Military Wristgaurd quest. Takes an hour, and has extended Affliction 3, which is really nice.

For Arms: Barbed Ringmail are good.

Your head piece is ok i guess, but I'd suggest getting a thurg piece if you can. Its much better.

For Face: Crystal Spider Eyes

For Feet: Tribal War Boots

Legs: Get something with more AC. Shaman legs are hard to upgrade, as theres a huge gap from Jarsath quality, to 20k legs. SS gear is great if it ever drops.
