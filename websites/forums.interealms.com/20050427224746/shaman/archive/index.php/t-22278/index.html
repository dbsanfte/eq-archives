Kellenial
Don't let the "hotspot" designation solely guide where you hunt. Most of the hotspots seem geared towards groups of that level, not soloing, although towards the higher end of the spectrum, soloing there may be possible. At 45, there are many places you can choose to hunt solo that are NOT hotspots. You won't get the same rate of experience as you did in a hotspot zone, but there may be other perks to it. It depends on what you seek.

Another option is to be proactive with a group. Start your own. Use the LFG tool to find similar level people and ask them if they want a group. At 45, with average equipment, I'm sure getting two people between 45-50 with you and Torgiran Mines will be MUCH easier, especially if one is a tank. Many people are hesitant to try LoY zones, but if you demonstrate you know what you are doing, you'll make them feel comfortable with it as well.

In any case, don't get discouraged. Grouping is there although it might be a little hard to find sometimes. Don't forget that at your current level, with average equipment, you can still solo fairly well...especially if you get slow to stick. Just try not to sell yourself short on what you can and can't solo.

EDIT: As far as 50s soloing Dulak, at 56, I still solo the armory area, etc. The reason being is that, while I still get experience there, I'm also getting a fair amount of pp, gems, etc. Have you seen the price of shaman spells in the bazaar?!
