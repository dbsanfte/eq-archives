You could duo PoG but the armor drops aren't that great there except for the BP and legs which fall off the much more difficult named. However with the money you make there you can easily outfit the cleric with enough buyable armor to at least get him on his feet again.

You could also duo the bank in Kael for corroded drops. The drop rate isn't that great but I've seen a lot more plate drop there than any chain. Seen plate BP, arms and bracer drop in the time I soloed there.

I don't know enough about cleric armor to help much with where to hunt the nice cleric no drop stuff but I know that from hunting in PoG you will make sizeable amount of money to pay for a respectable suit of dropable cleric armor.

Hope that helps :)
