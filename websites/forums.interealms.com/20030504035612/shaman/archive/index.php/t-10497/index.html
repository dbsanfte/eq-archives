Got Disinfecting Aura and was wondering if I can move Abolish Disease and Counteract Poison to the back of my spellbook? I can't find any information on how many counters it removes of each so am not sure if its just a convience spell or replaces them completely. Thanks for any information.
Stelmach
Ok, looked around on Lucy and came up with this.

Disinfecting Aura removes 10 counters of Disease and Poison.
Abolish Disease removes 36 counters of Disease.
Counteract Poison removes 8 counters of Poison.

Didn't see any higher level poison cures in Casters Realms spell chart. Abolish Poison was listed cleric only. So unless the mob was doing both Disease and Poison I would probably stick with Abolish Disease.
Zyphyr
Disinfecting Aura is 10 counters twice for a total of 20, not just 10 counters.

There is currently no poison or disease in the entire game with more than 18 counters.

In other words, if it is poison/disease curable 1 cast of DA will remove it. Period.

As it stands, DA totally replaces both Abolish Disease and Counteract Poison. Only if they add a Disease or Poison with more than 20 counters will there be any reason to use anything else.
