Henora
The Turgurs Line of slows is Magic based (tirgirs, togors, turgurs, tagars, etc). It checks the magic resistance of targets to see if it will land. It can be cured by casting cure disease. To resist these spells, increase your Magic Resistance.

The DR based slow is Cloud of Grummus or Plague of Insects.
This spell Checks the disease resistance of targets to see if it will land. It is curable by casting cure disease. To resist these spells, increase your Disease Resistance.

There are lots of mob cast DR based slows out there, including Bane of Thule (CT), Slime Mist (TOV), Shrieker Slow (FG) more. Mob-only slow spells can check against pretty much any resist. Go to lucy and type in "slow" and you can browse some of them. I notice Cold based slows, disease base, magic based, poison based.
