This has been changed back and forth several times, but has been working this way for a couple months.

Note that Maniacal Strength stacks, but the 58 Dex does not stack, with FoS. This means if you have a melee with proc weapon and low dex, you may be better off to use 58 Dex + HoS than to use FoS. Or to do so on your pet if you give it some of those nifty mage summoned proc weapons.
