Here's my guide:

Look for someone who's constantly in bazaar (the millionaires that wont share their secrets...they are very obvious because you can see them in the same spots over and over), look as what they are selling, buy what they are selling when you see it cheap (from other people), then resell it for the same price as the millionaire. This is probably the quickest way to start making money in bazaar.

If you see someone sitting in bazaar 24/7, chances are they are doing it for a reason and for a very good reason. If they are there 24/7 they are probably making killer money. Just look at their items for sale and start buying/selling those items a few gold cheaper than them. It'll make em very mad too!
