Gloriana
Wow, a real new person to the game!

Enjoy this time, explore! They did some great stuff in game...I still remember the first time I saw the sun setting over the North Karana bridge or the first looming spires of a Wizard ring, seeing the wonderful city of Kelethin, falling out of Kelethin... Your first lev, the first time you meet a person in wolf form, the first time you GET wolf form, finding out that the bear head hat isn't a really special high level hat but just plain old leather...Finding an ancient tarnished coin in a fountain and thinking you've found something worth a lot of money!

First giant. First specter. First time you approach the tunnel in EC and being amazed by the number of people (and the lag!) Riding the boat! Wondering what "crack" is and why people want it. People being chased by little floating shields.

Autoattacking a guard. Autoattacking a guildmaster. Autoattacking a vendor. Some friendly person showing you how to remap your auto attack key, going overboard and remapping nearly all the keys (anyone else have R as the jump button? heh, didn't think so!)

Hotkeys. Chat colors. Assist. Using F keys to target your group mates. Not knowing you have to put a point in med, forgetting to put a point into dodge. The wonder of infravision. The joy of ultravision. Watching monks do this weird thing where they punch the air for no reason. Watching monks fall on the ground. Seeing a drum equipped bard zooooom by you, throwing pink sparks in every direction.

Marveling at the names of the monsters. Quillmane. Crookstinger. Grizzleknot. Lockjaw. Running from Lockjaw. Seeing a gelatinous cube for the first time. Following a faerie through Gfay just because you like the sound of the bells.

I would tell you more, but it will be better when you find it for yourself. I've been playing for 3 years, and there are still mysteries to be discovered...
