this is the info I have found so far

anyone able to add anything or confirm/deny stuff

Burrower Beast is an event similiar to Ring of Fire. Once someone steps
into his lair, the event will start. A Massive Burrower which is not targetable
spawns at entrance , and Burrower Beast (untargetable) spawns at left side of dead-end
tunnel
There are 11 waves: first 3 waves of 11 worms doubling 230~, 4th, 5th, 6th waves contain 6
medium size of worm in each hitting 400~, and final wave of 2 giant worms flurries 400. In
first 3 waves, 3 - 4 rocky worms are mezzable while others are not, so splitting tanks on rest
of unmezzable worms. 6 medium worm will be easier since some tanks from first wave would
start assisting to kill 6 unmezzable worms in next 3 waves. Final wave before Burrower
Parasite has 2 giant worms which flurries 470~. Main tanking each till they both dead. During
each wave, there is 2 minutes break to rez, heal-up, and buff. Burrower Parasite will now
spawn in the back of lair, so you dont want to sit there or get chewed up first. He only double
and flurries 1170, but he is not slowable or crippleable. Even you failed attempting Avatar of
War, you will not lose to Burrower Beast once you get cleric healing chain setup right.
