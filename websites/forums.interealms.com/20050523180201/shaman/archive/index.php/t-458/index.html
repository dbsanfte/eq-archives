Weyalan
Dufrenite drops rarely in unrest, and is a much more common drop from SolA.

However, in terms of return for time invested, neither is a particularly effective strategy. In the time you spend waiting for dufrenite to drop, you would probably be able to have earned enough money to buy it anyway.

My tip, get a couple of friends and spend some time in Runnyeye - The Blackeded ally armor is actually quite good, and some pieces are quite valuable. A BA Coif (8AC +8 all resists) or a BA Medallion (6AC 5wis 5 int) should sell for 200 - 300 plat, and even the BA belt (8AC 5sta) can go for 100+ plat. Even the normal BA armor sells quite well - it is a bit better than banded and a lot lighter too.
