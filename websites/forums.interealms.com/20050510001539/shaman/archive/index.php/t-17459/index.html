Two losses are from the very first week of LDoN while people were learning how it all worked - so they're acceptable. Some people didn't do LDoN for a while and got to learn all about it from others. One of those was just a poorly and hastily assembled group - bad class mixes and bad personality mixes. I've grouped with most of the people from that group seperately on other occasions and kicked butt. The other of those losses was a low collection on high difficulty with a killer group that'd just finished two other high diffs.

Two losses are from doing low quantity collections more recently before it was firmly established that they suck hard and are nearly impossible to do. Both failures were with very strong groups that were simply crushing the dungeon and just couldn't get the drops. You can't get too upset about a loss when your group kicked arse and tried like heck to finish.

All of my groups are pickup groups with the occasional familiar face. None of my adventure losses have really had anything to do with the people involed.

I have encountered some real jerks, some greedy bastages (and poor MLers), some flakeballs from hell:

- I've had pallies and clerics that don't know how to sooth/pac - not so bad - you just tell them how it's done and by the end of the adventure they'll be decent at it. Once or twice more and they'll be as good as anyone at it.
- I've grouped with a battle cleric a few times. When we have a good pac/puller and I'm slowing the critters deal so little damage that the cleric can get away with meleeing and stunning. He was fm almost all the time. I hate to be fm - I'll friggin start rebuffing people if I'm fm - so I was doing touch up healing so he could have fun doing something different (than sit and med and cheal).
- once had another shammy turn 34 and decide he was gonna solo in the other direction from the group with his new pet and self-proclaimed uberness - this is after he held up everything to scribe his spells. He ended up gating out to go solo (and took a loss, the moron) after we all indicated just how much we didn't like the idea.
- if someone other than the desired tank is taking aggro and damage it's the group's responsibility to clearly indicate WHY you only want one person taking damage (ideally). you can't expect everybody to be as perfect as you or your guild and most people are willing to learn if you can enable them with knowledge without insulting them.
- beastlords are a highly diverse class. I don't think I've ever grouped with a beastlord that didn't nuke.
- once in that Xev crash LDoN last night our group leader and self-proclaimed MLer logged back in (as did me and some others from the group) but she never offered to split what we did get and kept the one decent drop we'd got that one of the warriors in the group would've used (he was a non-twinked main like me).
- once had another self-proclaimed MLer say she sold all the many goblin bones to the magus...and she was a seasoned LDoNer and a forage-enabled class - so I highly doubt didn't know about the smiithing drops.
- once had a bard that was a phenominal puller just insta bail as soon as the adventure was finished. We were pretty sure he had a named on track and went to solo it (we had no other tracker).

...but it's never ruined the overall fun of any given adventure for me. I do keep a little mental list of all the people that've dissed me in LDoN but it is a short list.

It's up to everyone in a group to make sure a group succeeds. It's about communicating, establishing a plan, and executing that plan.

There are idiots at all levels...and just because someone has a 65 or something somewhere or is in a great guild doesn't make them in any way know everything. Ie, the idiots aren't confined to the levels you're doing LDoN at! :P

<shrug>

Ultimately, it should all come down to having fun...it just bums me out to think that people might skip on the fun of LDoN because they think the only way to have fun is with their guild. I have been in many guilds in many games...and I have yet to be in a situation where the only people I ever did anything with was my guild.

Right now my guild is Xev Server - and it makes things a lot easier! :P
