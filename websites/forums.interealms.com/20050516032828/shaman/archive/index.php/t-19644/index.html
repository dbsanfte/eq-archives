I find LDoN is good in the low 50s but really dries up around 54-56+ unless you are way below the group.

For example - did an adv the other day with my 56 bard, everyone else was 58 except one other 56 char. We killed fast, finished adv in about 45 mins and I got 6% xp. Factor in the time spent running there, buffing, getting adv (but not any lfg time) its about an hour for 6% xp. I don't think that's anything special.

I'd suggest doing your LoY spell quests if you haven't already, and get some xp there whilst you are at it. Then try to get xp groups, either in PoI (if you can get a factory group, that's superb xp!), PoN across the river either at docks or zo2, or PoD at the castle. Its been too long to remember what rate of xp to expect there but those were teh best places when I did it.

If you're talking solo, I think you might struggle in PoP (depending on gear), and have more luck in dulak.
