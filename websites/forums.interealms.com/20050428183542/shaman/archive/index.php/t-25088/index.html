Quests from 15-20 range start handing out 6-9 sp, this is nice. Places like Pyrewood have mobs that drop at least 1 sp plus a lot of good items. Selling your old soulbound gear to vendors is good money. As a warrior, even the packrat that I am has found that there is no reason to keep more than one of each type of weapon. Lack of inv and bank space sort of forced me to this, but I sell some of my soulbound weapons to vendors for 10sp or more.

Most tradeskills can be pure profit, costing only the time to collect the materials. Or if you are able, buy cheap materials and make more items to sell in volume. I can sell easily crafted armor that costs 4 or 6 copper bars to make for 2 sp from vendors. Free cash money there.
