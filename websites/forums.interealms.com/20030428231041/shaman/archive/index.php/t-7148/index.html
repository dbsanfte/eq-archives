This thread is about the spell effect Calimony, which is the effect of drinking a Stinging Wort Potion.

When you drink the potion you get an instantaneous 300hp increase in both current hp and total hp (i.e a 300hp heal and a 300hp buff)

It is well known that DoTing yourself prior to PvP will prevent you from being mezzed. I was interested to discover whether or not the DoT-like hp decrease from Calimony would have a similar effect. Cripple has nothing to do with it
