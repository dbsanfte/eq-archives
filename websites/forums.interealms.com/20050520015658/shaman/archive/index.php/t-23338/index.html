I think the point you have missed in all this is that the Shaman class has not been kept "up to speed" with TODAYS EQ content. No one is denying the power we have in relatively trivial content. And yes, BoT and other pre-EP PoP zones are "trivial". As the game has changed and evolved, the Shaman class has not changed with it. We are still designed to be strong in longer fights, grouping with people who are not maxxed out, against mobs that do not hit for much more than 800ish a swing. None of these things describe EQ today. Yard trash regularly hits for 1200+. Almost everyone, even straight-up Bazaar geared players, is so close to maxxed stats that our buffs are insignificant. Many of "todays" fights last LESSS than 45 seconds, which makes our strongest abilities, slow, DOTs, etc, obsolete. Slow mitigation, gear mudflation, and low HP high DPS mobs have created an enviroment that makes Shaman a relatively useless class.

We are not alone. A lot of classes are suffering from this shift in the game. SOE is trying to make adjustments, but they just cannot seem to get past the whole "But shaman are SO POWERFUL in trivial content" thing. It seems like they dont DARE increase our healing or DPS, for fear of re-creating the goddly class we were in the days of old.

I dont think the Shaman class is dead, but we are certainly near the bottom of the list in this new EQ world. It honestly wouldnt take much to bring us back up to a "respectable" level. A few more hundred points on our fast heal and our fast nuke would make us at LEAST mildly effective. And honeslty, I do not see how either would make any older content any less trivial than it already is.

So far, SOE has upped our HoTs, and upped one buff to a "useful" level, regen. The big problem is that NEITHER of these changes deal with the immidiate problem, how to make Shaman more effective in todays "Short Fight, Low Hp, High DPS Mob" world. Slow is being mitigated, and even if it wasnt, there are other classes with more utility available for that task. Our heals, while handy, cannot keep up with the DPS of many of todays mobs, even the trash (lets not even bring up the Nameds, eh). Our buffs have been reduced to one single thing, the 544 hps from Focus, and of the other Priest classes, this is the lowest, least versitile of them all. 9 adds mana regen. Virt adds AC. Focus adds . . . more useless stats. Other than that, our buffs are almsot totally irrelevant. FA is almost useless in its current, low-duration format, and even if it was longer lasting, once again, it would be a single-directional buff, +attack. The stat portion is worthless. New regen is helpful, but its certainly not going to keep anyone alive when yard trash quads for 1K a whack.

The bottom line is we just dont bring enough to the table to be attractive in groups anymore. Our raid stackability is horrible. Our healing AND dps is the lowest of any of the priest classes, we cannot tank in higher content (and we actually WERE fair off-tanks in much of the older content, until now).

I am not one of those "Shaman are doomed and suck out the wang" nay-sayer people, but I have to be realistic about my class. I love playing a shaman. I am useful in many aspects of the game. I am an excellent addition to many groups. I can solo or group effectively in a large portion of the EQ universe. But in the end, we are a "6th wheel" in todays, cutting edge portion of the game.
