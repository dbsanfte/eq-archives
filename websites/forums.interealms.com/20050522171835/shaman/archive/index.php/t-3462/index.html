Gloriana
Sell the Tobrin's eyepatch in your quest to save for a fungi. Replace it with Crystal Spider Eyes or Death Mask of the Elysians.

If your guild is on dragon faction, get to ally (or the minimum required level) and get a Talisman of Benevolance for range.

Ditch the RE bracer and go with one of the nice droppable bracers that are now available (there's a thread on this page talking about it)

Start working on the earring of the solstice quest. Shop around and buy the gifts as cheaply as possible, or offer to help master tradeskillers out with the grunt work (ie finding a cleric to imbue emeralds or making stacks of celestial essences) for free combines.

Shadel Bandit Ring.

If your guild is on dragon faction, put together some raids on that giant that drops the Orb of Infinite Void.

Ditch the RE boots. Barbed dragonscale or nice, or the dragon hide boots from WW.

Ditch the RE helm. Is the 5 int that important? JB is very cheap.

IGS or Strength of the Elements for shoulders.

Legs are tough to upgrade, cross your fingers for SS legs, they're wonderful.

taraddar
Since your on CoV faction i'd look into the circlet of the farlkin or what ever it's called. Should be pretty easy to get if your guild still does arena raids for faction or armor still.

Also look into the talisman of benevolance for your range slot.

For visible armor your goal is obviously going to be Skyshrine, or dorf. Fungi is a great bp tho before you hit 60 and get torpor.

For an earring, the earing of the solstice can be bought in parts for about 10k on my server now and is a great earring that will last you a long time. Can cut a fair bit of cost off that too if you gather some components and pay for combines where possible.

Another earring option is the sanctum guardians earring if you can stand getting the smithing skill.

For face i'd sell the eye patch and get a mask of war or a crystal eyes mask or a death mask of the elysians.

For a ring grab a shadel bandit ring.
