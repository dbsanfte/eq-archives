Goldark
Do the coldain military wristguard quest (search the forums, there's loads of info here, and note you don't need the wolf harness, just the gem of pursuasion)

Its an extended affliction 3 item, great for soloing. You WILL need your friends for the final part of the quest, its soloable at level 49 or so. The other parts are best done with a Rallos Zek worshipping mule, Troll warriors are the most commonly used. Mine has dinged level 9 after doing the quest for lots of friends :)

Mana preservation 3, or affliction efficiency 3, are probably your next best bets. MP3 = Obulus Death Shroud, 10-15k. Not sure about AE 3 items, again, search the forum there is a thread on focus items.

Note that afflcition items work on dots, slows and roots, not just dots.

Hope that helps.
