If you post your gear along with your resistances (use the Equipment forum) - I can probably give you some suggestions as to where to go.


Just as a starter though, here are what I consider the basic necessities to work for if you are just starting out:

Jewlrey:
- 2 Blue Diamond Electrum Earrings
- 2 Velium Blue Diamond Bracelets
- 2 Velium Diamond Rings

If you are L55+ and have Tunare as your diety: Helm of the Tracker
Otherwise: Crown of the Froglok Kings

Miscellaneous:
- Shrouded Medalion (drops in ME) or DTC (Skyshrine quest)
- Runed Bone Fork

Also, if you don't have your epics already - start working on them. Each sword will give you +5 to all. (That's +10 everything when you get both.)


When your resistances get higher and you get higher in level, swap the Runed Bone Fork for a bow that either gives +HP or a resistance increase thus giving you archery options as well. (Wind Saber for example is +10SVC)
