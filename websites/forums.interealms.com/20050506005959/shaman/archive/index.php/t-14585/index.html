Loreddar Of Karana
I killed clockworks and gnomes in SolA for quite a bit around your level. The trick is knowing how to do it and making sure you don't pull too many at a time. If you go left at the entrance to the goblin area you'll go into some small "rooms" where the clockworks start, and then go over a lava bridge. Just past that bridge there will be a hallway to your left, then an enlargement of the cave where two clockworks/gnomes are, and then a room to your left. That room is a safe spot, and you can pull into it. If you're careful, the most you will get is a double pull. I would sick dogdog on one to get aggro, and then slow either the single or double while they were on dogdog. Then I'd double dot both. You need to be able to melee and tank, but it can be done. I'd pull everything around that area to include the single spawn just past the swinging axe thing. If you go deeper into the tunnel past the swinging axe, you'll pull 3-4.

I did that spot solo for a couple of levels. The loot is good and the experience is great. However, there are a couple of caveats. The first is that I was able to use KEI, and I made sure I was FM before a pull. That was the only way I could handle the double pulls. The second is that occasionally a gnome conjurer (sp?) will spawn. They have pets, and are tough to kill. I could only handle them singly. If I knew I would get one with a double, then I didn't even try (because I died trying once).

With a small group like you have, you shouldn't have any trouble camping this particular area. You'll learn which pulls will be double and which single, and you have a safe area to sit with no wanderers. Have fun!
