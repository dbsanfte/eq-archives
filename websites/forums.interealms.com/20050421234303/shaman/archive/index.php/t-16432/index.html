xorekill
I would recommend, if you want to solo, the castle near the Dulak zone in Gunthak. From the Dulak zone, just hug the right wall around. You'll find the castle with a pit and valley to either side. From here, you can go up and pull down either mushrooms, drogmors, or initaites (various races) and solo with some difficulty. On a bad pull, you'll only have to deal with 2, unlike in Dulak where a bad pull can sometimes net you 3 or more depending on pops. But, be aware of the Ogre initiates, as they seem to have higher resists and more hps than the other mobs. Be sure to land a mala-x before you slow.

There are days when you can be LFG and giving tells to everyone you know and people you don't know for 2 or more hours.
