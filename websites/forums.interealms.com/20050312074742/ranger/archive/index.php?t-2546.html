Where do you usually xp?

If you want faster points grab ambidex first, then get yourself finishing blow one if you solo for xp in Maidens at all. Quite the speed increase on lower dark blues, you will find. Then ya can save 6 for ND3.

Since you reside on a Zek, finishing might not be the best expenditure of points since it's a purely PVE skill...however it will increase the grind rate solo at say..the chief tree gorangas in Maidens or umbrous toilers.

Really, it's important to note that you are on the Zek..you fight people from time to time..not just mobs hehe...HP are pretty important, and ambidex sorta is a PVE skill too hehe.

You use a 2hander a lot? HP might be the best choice for survivability..you have a "few" hp for a zek ranger with no drop gear LOL (for instance..im sure he could be wearing mithril boots/gloves/velium encrusted and/or mosquito earrings etc...) Over the 3k hump will make ya grin quite a bit, makes smackin the casters around a little easier....thinking about the manaburn guerilla warfare kinda freaks me out over there.

Anyway in your situation, and wishing I would have started on a Zek, I would build from there...

Ambidexterity
Finishing Blow 1
Natural Durability 3
Channeling Focus 1
Casting Fury 1,2,3
Mental Clarity 3
Healing Gift 2,3
Natural Healing 1,2,3
Body and Mind Rejuvenation
Combat Agility 2
Combat Stability 2,3
Combat Agility 3

Ambidex will speed up kiling casters, and pretty much every melee mob you will ever face by enough of a margin to want this first. With your weaponry I estimate around 6-7 dps average on luclin content increase.

Finishing Blow makes the grinding more fun, and saves "kill that snared mob that's running" time, 10 seconds of attacking per mob adds up after FBlow 32 in an xp grind day, all at level 1 (2 points). And, just for kicks..it works with your bow...so why not :) Cheap 2 points for speed of kills. Heh, wonder how broken this would be if it worked on pcs...

Durability because hitpoints are god on the Zeks, and frankly pretty much anywhere for survivability.

Channel focus because its cheap, and it helps your spells under pressure. I have level 2 of this skill....I didn't see much improvement from level 2, but level 1 sure was noticable in duels especially. (double joust run of 300+ total on me during a finishing calefaction channeled through).

Calefaction and firestrike with casting fury 3 just plain tear up hilly terrain ....if you ever get jumped in Maidens terrain kills our archery in combat there a lot of the time....Oasis memories.
When you crit for 900 you can usually get an interrupt on someone else who is casting, say, manaburn for instance. It also makes the always on Call of Fire processes a bit more potent. This skill isn't so hot at the first 2 levels..but if you have the time to max it, do it. If for no reason than increasing your total killing power. Luck is good right?

Clarity 3 because you want the gas to fuel the developed spell skills, and to prep for body and mind.

Gift 2 and 3 so that gheal will be a heated-combat-useful heal 1 out of 10 casts. The one that crits will save your life eventually, or your friend.

Regen for the "attrition kills" rule....We tear things up quickly, but we don't have much of a way to fix our hits other than to kite and regen a lot of the time..and besides you need body and mind :)

Body and Mind so your blue comes back a lot faster, and the extra regen is pretty nice :)

Then finish up tanking in no particular order :)


If you raided, I'd do tanking immediately...if you PVE primarily, or just want to get the xp-survivability stuff out of the way, get tanking before developing your spells.

My $1.25 hehe, sorry novel :) AA is a lot of fun :)
