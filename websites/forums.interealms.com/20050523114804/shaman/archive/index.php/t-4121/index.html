So, in one of the other threads I noticed someone say that shaman could duo well with almost any other class. What I wanted to ask the Crucible was: how about clerics? The reason I'm asking is because my RL friend plays a cleric (I'm level 52, she's 54), and while we work great as a trio with a tank (cleric tank works awesome, then I throw in haste, pet, JBB, and slows and things speed up) I have yet to figure out how to effectively duo. So far, the best we can do is just to have me tank, and her heal me, but fights seem to take longer this way because my JBB gets interrupted more. So then we try making my pet tank, like I do when I solo, and she heals the pet. But with the pets low HP the cleric's heals are way inefficient, and since my pet's life isn't usually that much of a factor when I solo, her healing of him doesn't exactly speed the exp gaining up at all. So, anyone have any ideas on different tactics we could try? If so, I'd love to hear them.

P.S. I'm toying with the idea of going to CoM and basically just soloing, with her using undead DoTs and DDs to add supplemental damage. Has anyone ever tried this?

Yeah, this is a tough one.

I think your plan (acting as if you're solo'ing but having her throw in damage through DoTs and DDs- and concentrating on undead mobs) is a good one, but probably still not at efficient as having a melee around.

Definitely have a dog up and hasted. I'd seriously consider throwing a Cripple-series debuff on the mobs (so dogdog can hit better), Slow (duh), Cann like crazy (she can heal you), and alternate between JBB, nukes, DoTs, and plain old barshing (get a big two hander and swing between casts.)

I'm thinking you'll need to play a little dangerously in order to get good xp from this combo. Think of it this way, you need to be killing mobs twice as fast as when you solo... and you basically have a very similar "safe" damage output.

Might not be efficient, but is might just be a messy good time! :)

Thurdar
A cleric is probably the toughest class to duo with efficiently. But since I have a few good friends that play clerics, I've done that duo often. It actually gets better at higher lvls, once you get cann4, BoN and Pox.

Pull with Mal-x, since it will help the cleric land spells, slow if need be, then stack your 2 best poison dots and maybe a disease dot. JBB and re-apply poison dots when required. After the fight, canni down and get a complete heal. At 60, I can come close to doubling my kill rate with a cleric partner, although I'm sure that if I had torpor that wouldn't be the case.
