walk through time!

ok so you wanna own SFK ?
do you have 4 friends ? do you think you can get 4 people ? moderate skill around lvl 24+ ? then go for it!

ok most of the mobs in SFK are 20+ elite.

most are just straight melee, but others such as the undead spirits can silence, curse, stun and cast spells.
one curse makes spirits come from the ether and attack you, one hit kills them (they have 1 hp), i wish i had a map i could spot all locations to you with it, but i do not. the undead horses, do not **** with them, they can chain stun your whole group and wipe you very fast, leave them alone!

the sons of arugal curse with a curse that lasts 5 mins and ever minute does 200+ damage to you, pretty nasty.

the gate to the courtyard is locked, you must free the death stalker adamant in the dungeon to get in the courtyard, you must kill rethilgore to get him free.
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=12975

once hes dead, and you open the cell, talk to him, and lead him to the door, leave the human mage alone, hes not worth the effort.

the courtyard is huge, holds around 25+ mobs, all tight packed, pull the left ones first!.

Deathstalker Vincent is on the left, hes for a quest. http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?wquest=1098

ok so you've cleared the courtyard ? cool, no pass those horses on the right stable, leave them alone !

see that door going into the halls below ? don't go up the ramp to the right, you want to kill the sub-bosses for thier loot. so down we go !

you will encounter curse using undead here, haunting servitors be careful.

Razorclaw the butcher drops http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=1292
and is near by make sure you have some form of crowd controll for when you get adds.

once hes dead the dungeon opens up into a great eating hall, lots of cursing undead as befor.

Baron Silverlaine is in here, he drops
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=6321
he casts spells like lightning, and fireball. use earth shocks or stun spells on him.

go up the left stair case. hit a landing, go up the left, kill 1-3 mobs wandering the stairs, and befor you hit the next room, hold there, don't go in, or your gunna wipe. you will see wailing guardsmen and other undead spirits, these guys silence, pull them 1-2 at a time, keep casters back, pull towards the right side of the room, so as to have a place to downtime near.
