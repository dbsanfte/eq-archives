Is there any way to change the resolution and still use the 3rd party EQW? When I change from 1024X768 to 1280X1024 the box goes way up to the top left of the screen and I can't see everything in the window. Anyone know how to fix this where I can use a larger resolution with EQW?
Schpleef
Well first off, your desktop resolution needs to be higher than your EQ resolution, of course. To change the position of your window, edit the Eqw.ini file in your eqw directory. Under the [Positions] header, try something like this:
1280by1024X=10
1280by1024Y=10

That should get it to where the window's title bar is on your screen - from there, you can release the mouse (ctrl-alt-R) and right-click on its taskbar icon, select "Move", and put it wherever you want it using the mouse.
