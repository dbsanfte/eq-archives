Zyphyr
So what I'm wondering is, if I get my tear and just do Neh and Rak ashiirs together at the same time (turn in ring, then give diary to spirit, then return and kill rak) is there any reason not to do this?

This is a very common way of doing it.

When I do that turn in, if all has been done correctly, will I recieve the "your faction cannot possibly get any better with True Spirit" message?

You won't get that message until you do the Rak turnin. The message means "If your faction wasn't already maxed, you would have gotten a hit". For you to get it on the Diary turnin, the message would have to mean "The faction hit you just took put you at the max". So, unless you did something multiple times you will just have to assume that you have sufficient faction.

Since you are doing everything in order, the only way your faction could possibly be to low is if you had somehow killed something that gave you a negative TS hit and if you had done that then a)you would presumably know about it and b)somewhere along the line previous to Rak you would get a turnin eaten.

Worrying about your exact faction level is something that only people doing the faction-bypass method really need to do.
