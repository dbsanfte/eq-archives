Tarelan -
54 is probably too low to hunt in Katta solo - but the tunnels may allow you to single pull - by level 56 they wil aggro on line of sight - if they cannot see you, they will not come. So - learn how to creep up to tunnel to where you can just see one guard - try to hit it with slow before it reaches you (they usually react slowly, and slow lands before they get to you)

There are 2 roamers though - you want to get them killed and timed (just under 16 minutes to repop), or you will be practicing a sowed run down the walkway to the zone line - safe but frustrating, as the guards are slowpokes.
