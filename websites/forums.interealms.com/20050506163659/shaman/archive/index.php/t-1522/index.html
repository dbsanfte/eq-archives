Jinheim
Sit in karnor's for a few hours and collect jarsath (affectionately known as jarcrap) pieces. The only piece that anyone ever wants really is the legs.

Rune-etched icewurm fang is a 12/19 piercer. That's a very good ratio for a shaman weapon. Should run you 2-3k. If you can't find one, howling harpoon, slime-coated harpoon, and spine chill spears are acceptable substitutes. I used a howling harpoon all the way up into my 50's.

Once you've got these pieces, start filling in the holes with jaundiced. For your jewelry, get ac/hp/mana pieces. For a shield, try a simple charred guardian shield. It has good wis and decent AC for your level. Whatever you do, do not ever ever ever equip anything that looks like a book or a dolly (paw of opolla, testament of vanear).

Snatched from another thread (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?s=&threadid=1323)

Piercing:

Bloodstained fang (Dmg 9, Dly 32) - sells for next to nothing (10 pp?)

Rukgus's sticker (Dmg 6, Dly 25, +3 Str, +2 Wis, +2 Int) - probably under 100 pp

Ebon war spear (Dmg 8, Dly 31, Rangeable, +4 Str, +3 Wis, +5 vsMg) - probably 100-200 pp, this one is nice because when you upgrade you could move it to your range slot to continue to benefit from the stats.

Bettle stinger (Dmg 8, Dly 23, +4 Agi, +4 Dex, +4 Wis) - 100-200 pp)

Spine chill spear (Dmg 10, Dly 28, +3 Sta, +6 Wis, +10 vsCold) - 200-400 pp

Slime coated harpoon (Dmg 10, Dly 25, Rangeable, +5 Wis, +10 Mana) - 200-500 pp

One hand blunt:

Screaming mace (8 dmg, 35 dly, Effect of Screaming Mace), Cold iron morning star (9 dmg, 35 dly), Enamelled black mace (Dmg 8, Dly 28) - These are old-world weapons, now make nice lower level weapons, and are probably around 50-100 pp

Wraith bone hammer (Dmg 8, Dly 26, +5 Str, +2 Wis, +2 Int), Sharkbone Warhammer (Dmg 8, Dly 26, +5 Wis), Velium Etched Stone Mace (Dmg 7, Dly 22, +3 Str, +3 Wis), or Centi warhammer (Dmg 15, Dly 32- great ratio!, +5 Str, +5 Sta, +2 Wis) - 100-200 pp

Smoldering Brand (Dmg 9, Dly 22, +3 Str, Effect of Burn from level 1 - NICE weapon), Ykeshan War Club (Dmg 9, Dly 24, Effect of Ykesha from level 37) - 200-500pp

Two hand blunt:

Drakkel Forged Sledgehammer (Dmg 22, Dly 50) or Gatorsmash Maul (Dmg 30, Dly 70) - 50-100 pp

Drakkel Icegrinder (Dmg 27, Dly 45, Effect of Ice Rend) - 200-400pp

Shield:

Bark shield (+15 AC, +5 Agi), Chitin shell shield (+15 AC, +10 vsPos), Crystal webshield (+15 AC, +10 vsCold, +20 HP, +20),
Crustacean shell shield (+17 AC, +4 Str, +10 Sta, -7 Cha), Velium round shield (+15 AC, +10 vsCold) - 50-150 pp

Clay guardian shield (+25 AC, +50 HP) - 200-500 pp

Range:

Goranga idol (ac 8, +25 hp, +3 sta, +4 dex) - few hundred pp

Many of the above spears can be put in your range slot when you upgrade or switch out a different weapon

Since you have a little more money to spend, you might want to consider a Rune Etched Icewurm Fang (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=3969) (12 dmg, 19 dly, +5 wis, +35 mana) as a piercer.

As far as armor goes, the most common progression is Imbued Northman (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/adv_search.html?stn=imbued+northman) (player made- get some Imbued Golden Ivory Earrings and maybe the necklace, too!), Totemic (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/adv_search.html?stn=totemic) (couple hundred pp each, except for the helm and BP which will be a little more), Ry'gorr (bit more expensive than totemic), minor jaundiced bone pieces (gloves, helm, boots, arms), then planar/quest stuff.

Just like a monk, a shaman loves regen (at low levels you're doing a lot of bashing, at higher levels you can cannibalize hit points to more mana). An iksar BP would serve him well for a long time if you can find one reasonably priced.

This thread (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?s=&threadid=1379) might also help- a large list of magelo profiles to peruse to see what others have chosen.

If I was starting over with 7k, I'd grab an iksar BP, slime/howling/spine chill (two of 'em- one for the range spot) spear, the imbued ivory jewelry, some Ry'gorr and the minor JB pieces, and go to town! :)

Good hunting!
