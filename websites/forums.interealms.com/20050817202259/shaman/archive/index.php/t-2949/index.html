Chutoi
Well, Bimaz, you are still at a young stage. Your need for HP/AC isn't as high yet, since you won't get cannabalize until level 24. You may actually get more use out of the larger mana pool. I'd advise you to keep the +wis items until you hit 24. THEN start thinking about moving more towards a HP/AC configuration.

As for shields, probably the best one you can get would be the SBS, or sarnak battle shield. Prices are VERY server dependent, but I'd suspect to see them sell for 3-5k pp. Upgrades over the SBS typically don't come unless you are in a very good raiding guild, or perhaps if you can get lucky in the beer script quest in Fungus Grove (level 55+).

Take care, chief.
