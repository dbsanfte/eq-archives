Calabar
I know how you feel. My combat skills were doing okay all the way up to level 38 when I started grouping. Then I became almost a pure caster till about level 50. As a shaman you have several options, at least for weapon skills. As someone mentioned, group with friends who don’t mind you stepping into melee. Slow and dot the mob and then step up behind it and swing away. If you solo some, tanking with your spirit puppy biting at the rear, your weapon skills will increase along with your defense / offence skills. Another option, which I’ve done with my ranger who has far too many weapons skills to keep maxed, is to fight outside a group. Find a group fighting blues or better and ask if you can beat on their mobs. Shaman do so little damage that they will get all the xp and loot so they shouldn’t mind. You can also offer to buff them in exchange. You won’t get any xp this way, but your weapon skills will go up without much risk to your health.

Again as others have said, if all you’re after is skill gain then get the fastest weapon possible. For example, if you want to work on your 2H blunt skill get an abandoned orc shovel (Delay: 27, Damage: 4). It’s about the fastest 2H blunt in the game and its completely free. Just don’t expect to do any significant damage with it. You can pick this shovel up off the ground in an orc camp in Lake Rathetear. If you zone in from South Karana turn left like your heading to Rathe Mountains. The orc camp you want is between these two zone lines. It either the second or third patch of land you come to. At your level the ors will just stare evilly at you and let you walk away with the shovel they left lying on the ground.

Best of luck.
