Talsondar
Haste is a good thing. Once you hit 30 look for a deal on a Barbarian Spiritists Hammer. It is a 2hb 22/47 hammer that procs the same DD as the JBB bracer, you will mow thru levels 30-45 or so with it. Prices in the bazaar vary from 500-3500 or so, just hang out until you see someone looking to unload one for less than 1k.

For pure speed of leveling forsake wis and mana for HP/AC without question. Once you hit 30 and especially 34 when you get your dog dog, you will feel like you are being powerleveled with that hammer.

Just buff up HP/AC/Dex (& use the self only fury line as well) and you Dex should be over 150. With Dex over 150 I consistently proc'd a couple times a fight (usually more if the mob lasted long enough) and with Self Haste & SCHW I was swinging a heck of alot.

If you can swing it, use the SCHW over the FBSS, no AC on the FBSS and using SCHW opens up the Waist slot for a Spider Fur belt which gives good AC and 40hp.

Good Luck and I melee'd with great success to where I am now, which is 47, so dont look to give up your haste anytime soon...
