Level 65 Troll Shaman on Quellious here.

AC 10, Wis 20, Hp 50 is just too hard to beat. I recently swapped out a Valorium Ring for a Glittering Ring.

At level 51, FT3 > AC/Hp.

Once you are 60 with Torpor and Canni5, you won't care about FT3 - since it is insignifficant compared to the mana regen you get from Canni5/Torpor.

Then you would prefer boots with more AC/Hp/Stats and be willing to give up FT3.

However, the road from 51 to 60 with Canni5 and Torpor is very long, and there will always be a market for FT3 boots.

I would go for Glittering Ring and Slime Boots at 51.
