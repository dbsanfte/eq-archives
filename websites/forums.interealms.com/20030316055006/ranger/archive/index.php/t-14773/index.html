Did it with 2 druids and 2 rangers, all in the low to mid 50s. We were able to invis all the way into Barducks (the casters mobs do see invis, but we managed to avoid the few we saw).

The druids could have probably done it alone, we just pulled him solo to the wall of the cave, rooted him, dotted him,and shot arrows til he was dead. Then succored out to go defend the town.

The only reason to bring more people is to defend the town from the waves of gnolls that attack after Barducks dies. That was fun :) . We didn't have enough people to save everyone, but it was a fun fight and good expeience.
