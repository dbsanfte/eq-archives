Kazim
If you plan to hunt mostly indoors, buy an iksar ceremonial chestplate (regen). If you plan to hunt mostly outdoors, save your money for the lizard/horse.

Iksar breastplate is comparable to flowing thought 3 or so anywhere you hunt. Horse is comparable to flowing thought 15+ when you're outdoors. So I guess if you're outdoors at least 20% of the time or more, you're best buy on average will be the horse.

If you like to melee a lot, another way to go might be to get a REIF or a big 2HB (tae ewe war maul or granite face grinder) and a haste item like SCHW/FBSS. Those would up your melee damage significantly, possibly increasing your kill rates as much as the extra mana would. But eventually your melee will be such a joke that those would be kind of "dead end" buys. A mount will last you forever. An ikky bp will last you until you can upgrade it to a fungi or get torpor/canni5.
