Grizlor
Seriously, changing the cast and recast time on this spell would be not too much of a class balancing issue, other than reducing the hassle of debuffing multiple monsters and freeing up time to do other things. One might argue that since it's no agro and debuffs many resists, it should have severe limitations. I'm of the opinion that those benefits are already countered by the mana cost of 400, which needs to be cast on every mob in a raid OR exp group setting. when doing any enounter with multiple mobs, the 10 sec recast on malos seriously leaves me banging my head against the wall.

I'm suggesting malos be dropped to 3.0 cast time and 2.25 recast. Yeah, I know that would make it close to tash only with no agro, but the damn spell costs 400 mana. That's the same as a complete heal, only it doesn't do any damage, nor does it heal anyone. All it would do is speed up the debuffing process overall and let us focus on other things (spot healing, cannibalizing, applying damage) for which we need as much time as possible due to the spells being ineffective (moderate casting time for low output. See Spot heals, spear of torment) or ridiculously long for moderate output (velium strike, any DoT).

If anything is killing us, it's lack of time to be able to work with the situation.

Comments?

Edit: That post on the eqlive forums is asking us to address issues that are hassles or otherwise simply inconvenient, this would be a great one to push.
