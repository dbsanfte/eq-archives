If you have Luclin, try fear-kiting the blue-con zelniaks or large Zelniaks in Dawnshroud Peaks. I personally say do the zelniaks until they go light blue simply cause they have about 2/3 at best the HP of the large and were giving half a blue exp still at 39. At 40 the blue Zelniaks are giving a little less, but they are still worth doing just cause they are so easy to kill. Just snare, fear and go to it, no Zelniaks/large zelniaks add and as a ranger I've never had to worry about 99% of the wolves either (there's one or two that will attack and to me the wolves seem to be a bit underconned). The loot however is just non-existent, so if you are looking for that too, you'll have to go elsewhere like tenebrous mountains and get in a group, supposed to be some good loot there. Best of Luck.
