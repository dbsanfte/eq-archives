SKs won't want to fight him because killing him loses faction with their guildmaster in Echo caverns.

SKs might be able to help with the pull though and then zone to SH before he dies.

My primary concern would be that you might not have the tanks to handle him since only the Paladin can really be expected to work that role. You'd be better off finding a 55+ warrior willing to tank, I think.

As for the pull, use multiple pullers and keep at it til you get him separated from all the adds. Find a spot against a zone wall to pull to and pull him there once he has been separated. Have the MA keep his back to the wall since he procs a nice knockback. If the rest of the raid avoids getting aggro then you can kill him if he stays on the MA.
