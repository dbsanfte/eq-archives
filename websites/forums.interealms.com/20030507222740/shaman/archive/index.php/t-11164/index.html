In your price range, no item will make as big a difference as a Ceremonial Iksar Chestplate. I can see how you might think you can't get one, but I'd be very surprised if that were true... they are quite easy to get, and druids farm them for sale in the bazaar. Unless there's something different about your server that I'm not aware of, there should be plenty of these flowing through the economy. I got two, each took about 8 hours of farming the TT cycle. Not hard, my ranger grabbed the mob and pulled him back to my necro... the fight is cake for two people or any kiting class. Worth every minute of the camp.

It's not vital, but I still wear mine at 54, and probably won't replace it for a while yet. I figure if I'm fighting and am going to need AC, I'm better off with the regen. With canni, it's like FT3.
