Yah, kill him. If you don't, he tends to add at inconvenient times. I start by killing him (easy for a shaman to accomplish). Then I'll casually kill the daiku boarbeast on the side of the door to the pit. Then I'll start pulling from the arena till piggy pops, nail piggy with Paralyzing Earth from out of aggro range of the herd (it's rather astonishingly small), then follow up with Malos, Turgurs, and drop all my DoTs on piggy to kill him fast before anything happens. Usually this tactic works well, and I can get one stampeding piglet per hour. That adds up to a bunch of loot usually. I've already outfitted some lower level friends with those shields and sold some earrings. It's "one" way of getting loot in the game. Not a terribly bad way at that either.

If luck's on my side, I try to get some named from the pit. That adds spec's and the occasional mold.
