Nebmai
On Prexus, I just picked up Gem Encrusted Ring for 16K, with four other people offering it to me for between 17K and 20K. I also did the Coldain Bracer quest in GD--and I can tell you that these two focus items have helped immensly! My slows never wear off, even in long fights, my roots hold longer, my dots are more efficient. I'd say go for Afflicition Efficiency instead of Mana Preservation, because here are the spells that will save mana:

Affliction Efficiency (25% off the following)
Roots
Dots
Slows

Other spells you'd be casting (10% off)
Buffs
Regen
Healing
Pet
Haste

The cost of Obulus Death Shroud and Gem Encrusted Ring are about the same on most servers, if the servers were around when GER was still dropping. If you're soloing, you're probably casting a Dot (or two) and a Slow every fight, making Afflicition Efficiency a huge bonus (extra 15% mana savings) over the Death Shroud.

In addition, I'd recommend also picking up an Amulet of Insight. They run 4-5K on my server, and have Extended Enhancement III. This allows not only for your long duration buffs to last 15% longer, but also to extend the time your short duration buffs (Regen/Haste/Pet Haste) last.

Let's assume you get a KEI which lasts 3 hours even.

Base:
Alacrity (to Pet): 115 Mana, 11 Minutes.
Spirit Quickening (to Pet): 50 Mana, 6 Minutes.
Regrowth (Self): 300 Mana, 15 Minutes.
Regrowth (Pet): 300 Mana, 15 Minutes.

Over a three hour period (no modifiers):
Alacrity: Cast 16 Times (1840 Mana Total).
Spirit Quickening: Cast 30 times (1500 Mana Total)
Regrowth: Cast 24 Times (7200 Mana Total)
Total Mana Used (No modifiers): 10,540

Over a three hour period (Mana Preservation):
Alacrity: Cast 16 Times (1656 Mana Total)
Spirit Quickening: Cast 30 Times (1350 Mana Total)
Regrowth: Cast 24 Times (6480 Mana Total)
Total Mana Used (Mana Preservation): 9486 Mana

Over a three hour period (Extended Enhancement)
Alacrity: Cast 14 Times (1610 Mana Total)
Spirit Quickening: Cast 26 Times (1300 Mana Total)
Regrowth: Cast 20 Times (6000 Mana Total)
Total Mana Used (Extended Enhancement): 8910 Mana

Over a three hour period (EE + MP)
Alacrity: Cast 14 Times (1449 Mana Total)
Spirit Quick: Cast 26 times (1170 Mana Total)
Regrowth: Cast 20 Times (5400 Mana Total)
Total Mana Used (EE + MP): 8019 Mana

As you can see from the above, Focus items make a huge difference! However, to a shaman, the most negigable of these is MC3--I'd rank the order of importance to be Afflicition Efficiency (quarter mana cost Dots and Slows), Afflicition Extension (longer dots and slows), Extended Enhancement, and then Mana Conversation.

Hope this post helps!
