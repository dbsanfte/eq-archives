Crypt (http://www.trollworld.freeserve.co.uk/Grogbat/crypt.jpg)

Ok a quick, hunt ya own JBB guide:

I have spent a fair bit of time in the crypt, mainly when I was looking for my 60 spells. I have seen 2 JBB drop in all that time (both in same night ironically).

Take a balanced group of 6 down there, and unless you are all low/mid 50 it will be slow, so take friends and make it a social event. A 60 puller makes things go smoothly.

Your generic 'full group in a box' tank, cleric chanter, rogue, shammy, evac etc all mid 50's can clear the crypt. I prefer to take as few people as possible.

Minimal Group: 60 shaman with spells, 60 rogue both epic, just invis past Crypt guardians

Minimal 'kill everything' group: Good Shaman, Good Monk/SK, Good Rogue. Doable without Malo and Torpor if you are all switched on, just grab another healer type if worried, some of the nameds and chottal could be a bit rough if slow is resisted more than once etc. Need to FD split Guardians as they CH across the room even unaggroed ;)

Minimal 'comfortable' group: Mezzer(52 up ench does fine, think necro/Bard have to be 54ish to mez ICG), shaman, melee, rogue -see shot above (60 necro, 60 shaman, 60 rogue, 57 ranger all epic), only had one moment when hierophant resisted 3 slows after Malo haha

Unsubtle 'heavy melee' group of damage dealers plus shaman can just ride out the C-heals and run the Guardians out of mana -did this with 60 pally, warrior, rogue x2 and shaman - pally just stun tanked one ICG while the rest tore the other down..

Mob wise it's nothing too tough apart from Chottal/nameds. Not too many MObs, but they are some of the tougher ones in upper seb.

SK/Warrior/Nec frogloks, some with pets, some do ~601 HT ish. MR nothing special but they run low on health.

Most 'skeleton mobs' are Melee's, easily slowed, some have HT (skeletal Barons I think, maybe mistaken).

Froglok Mage: new-ish spawn, roams about before ICG's. Easy mob but vicious damage sheild, easy once it's annuled. Watch for DS on other mobs if he's up.

Fallen Monk: for Monk WF quest, dies easily.

Crypt Guardians: Bit harder than the Seb Golem on pyramid outside crypt. Cleric mobs but don't cast too much, but will try to CH. MR nothing special. Need splitting/mezzing as they assist CH if they have line of sight (it seems..). Can be pulled into one of the small rooms an killed to avoid CH if you have already cleared them once (ie: not respawned yet)

Blood of chottal: just kill it already.

Emporer 'wot no torpor?' chottal: 60 necro mob. Can be singled pulled by a level 60 using some none damaging means (annul is good if he is up when you get down there). Casts a lot, nothing talisman of epurtation won't fix apart from the magic dot (boil?). likes to dispell. Fight by doorway to his room or he may gate an summon you to nasty death. Leave chottal's skeleton guardians alone unless you are bored/clever/fancy a nasty CR.

I like the Crypt overall, xp nothing special unless small group, but good chance of loot thats still reasonable for difficulty level. Reminds me, must head back for a heiro cloak soon.....
