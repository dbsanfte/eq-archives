You may have to pay a visit to elf land and start killing prisoners in Crushbone. Been ages since I worked on Neriak faction. Also there is the Wine Quest you could do. Just go to the 3rd gate, get a pal to by you a bag of red wine, Turn it in to a guy in the library (even KOS i dont think he attacks) till he gives you good faction so you can buy wine yourself. This might take like 5 bags of red wine.
