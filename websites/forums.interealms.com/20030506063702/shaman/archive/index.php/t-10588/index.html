Gormadoc
There are both recommended and required level tags placed on items. If a particular item has a recommended level of say 51 and a player lower than 51 equips it the stats that are in red are what the character will get. Once the person becomes the high enough to use it 'properly', they will get the full strength of that item.

If there is a required tag on it.. no one below that level can equip the item.

*edit* The earring of the solstice has a recommended level of 51.. the easiest way to see if your character can use an item is to have someone link it to you in game. If you see red stats, those are what you'll be getting when it is equipped.

I do not know if the flowing thought on the earring will be affected though =/
