61 and 62 go at about the same rate if you kill in PoN/PoD. 63 takes longer in PoN/PoD because the mobs don't fall within the "mobs within 5 levels of you give better exp" category. 62/63 in PoV goes pretty fast because the mobs are level 62 or 63. 64 slows down on the desert mobs in PoV, but the inner and outer caves are even -> dark blue cons.

Sciek Spellbottler
61+ each level takes more xp than the previous. Where you hunt and how good your group is will determine how fast 61-65 goes. Stay in Tier 1 zones and you can make it all the way to 65, but it'll be a longer crawl than if you moved up to harder zones/mobs to keep your xp targets within the 5 level sweet spot. No brainer that a better group will get you better xp heh. The old mentality of killing as many of the easiest dark blue cons doesn't work post 60. You need to take on the harder mobs that are near your level. The difference is quite noticeable.

This is an article that explains how the old hell levels work. Essentially, there is a modifier that changes periodically. As of late, EQ has made that modifier "less abrupt" than it used to be to smooth out the levels.

http://www.archofchaos.com/article.asp?item=7

(Please note, there's a lot of stuff in this article that is no longer true, but many of the principles remain the same).

As an FYI, when they first attempted to smooth out the hell levels in the 50's, they actually hid some of the xp that used to be required for level 60, IN level 60. This caused people's xp bars to move slower than what they were used to (this was just before PoP came out). There was lots of complaining about this, so they stuck that xp back into the 50's, and level 60 remains 25% of a "standard" level.
