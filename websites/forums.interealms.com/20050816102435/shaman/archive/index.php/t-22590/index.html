From their point of view, its bad PR and bad business to 'force' someone to buy a pre-existing expansion (ie: Kunark) set in order to play in a new expansion set. Every expansion module to date has only required access to the original EQ game. Basic business sense dictates they want as many people to be able to buy the new expansion as possible. Not limit it only to those who bought Kunark.

Yes, they could absolutetly give Kunark as a free download to all customers that don't currently have it. In addition, I agree, there probably aren't many people out there that do not already have Kunark. Anyways, I'm not arguing for SoE not to do something, just explaining the immediate rationale of why they wouldn't.
