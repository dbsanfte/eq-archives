Bigguss
Keep up:

SoW (escape, pulling)

Spirit Strength- will increase max damage a bit

Spirit of Cat (will not stack with Feet like cat) - will increase AC a smidgen

Spirit of Bear - will increase HP a smidgen

Turtle skin (increase AC)

Inner fire (increase AC)
-------------------------------------

Keep root up for runners or adds (you can root-park them, then maybe drop an Affliction if they are going to be parked awhile.)

I'd also keep a nuke up in case things go bad.

Mostly, though, you'll be slowing and healing (and disempowering if you have the mana.) Don't cast Affliction on any mob he's on (as it is not likely to live log enough for it to do much damage), ubt feel free to toss in a tainted breath if you have the mana (only takes 42 seconds to work.)

Good luck!

House
Your case is special, though, as for right now (with the level discrepancy between the two of you) it's unlikely you'll be able to land many offensive spells. With this in mind, I would drop just about every buff you have on the Monk: SoW, Spirit Strength, Spirit of the Cat (does not stack with Feet Like Cat), Turtle Skin, Spirit of the Bear, Inner Fire, and Dexterous Aura. You can also cast resist buffs on the Monk if it's relevant to the MOBs you're fighting, plus Serpent Sight if the Monk is a Human. During the fight, keep your Healing spell memmed in case things get ugly, but as much as possible try to heal the Monk by casting Inner Fire between med ticks, as Inner Fire has a better mana/HP ratio.
