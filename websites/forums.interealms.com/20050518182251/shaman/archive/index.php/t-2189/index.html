Taken from Castersrealm:


A number of new spell effects for Caster Focus items have been added to the spell data file on Test.

The effects of these are only guessed at by our members on the Casters Realm team but the new effects do show some interesting potential for new caster items.

Note that these are only on Test Server and may change before going live.

Reagent Conservation - Percent chance to not use a component on spells.
Extended Enhancement - Percent increase on duration of buffs.
Improved Damage - Percent increase on DD spells.
Spell Haste - Percent reduction on casting time of dd's, heal's).
Mana Preservation - Percent reduction in mana cost of dd's, heal's).
Improved Healing - Percent increase in amount healed.
Extended Range - Percent increase in spell range to target.
Summoning Haste - Percent reduction in time of summoned objects (non-undead pets only).
Summoning Efficiency - Percent reduction in mana cost of summoned items (non-undead pets only).
Enhancement Haste - Percent reduction in casting of buff spells.
Affliction Efficiency - Percent reduction in mana cost of dots.
Extended Affliction - Percent increase in duration of dot's, more ticks, same damage per tick as prior, more total damage.
Reanimation Haste - Percent reduction time in casting undead pets (undead pets only).
Reanimation Efficiency - Percent reduction in mana cost of undead pets (undead pets only
