And what I gave him is actually help.

When you are hunting in an area, you get what I think of a "linker quests" that link you to a new area that you might want to go hunt in.
For example, if you hunt in Stranglethorn Vale, Krazek has a quest for you to get something from the Dragon Whelps in the Swamp of Sorrows.
Until you get that quest, you don't really know that you might want to hunt there.
But in getting the quest, you look on your map to see where it is, you go on an adventure getting there, and in looking for the mobs you need to whack for the quest, you find more NPCs in the area that have quests for you to do, and you find more NPCs to hunt that are higher level than what you were hunting.
Pretty much every quest area has 2-3 linker quests for other areas.
Shimmering Flats has quests for you to go to STV and Tanaris.
Duskwood has quests for you to go to Ashenvale and Menethil Harbor.
Thunder Bluff (Mulgore) has quests for you to go to the Barrens and Sepulchar.
Gadgetzan has quests for you to go to Feralas, Felwood, and Ungoro Crater.
etc etc etc.

If you don't know where to go, you haven't been doing your quests.
