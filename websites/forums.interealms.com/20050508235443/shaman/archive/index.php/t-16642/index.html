Visiting this board isn't a social experience for me, it's an information-gathering one. There are quite a few people who come here to post new things they've seen, figured out, or have to ask. I like to keep pace with those because I find them very interesting.

When I see a question that I've seen previously answered... multiple times... I'll suggest the search function. Call it a compulsion toward trying to make everything neat and tidy. No, I don't feel the need to brutalize someone while I do it. But I don't feel the need to pander to these people who are too lazy to USE the features that the Crucible webadmin has gone to SUCH LENGTHS to implement. It's laziness. Nothing more. The search function works. It works well. I have never failed to find what I was looking for with it, though I may have had to try more than one string of words.

By suggesting that someone use search, I am giving them credit. I am assuming that they are merely lazy (in that they didn't bother to search) instead of stupid (unable to use a simple search tool).

I'm a lot less offended by people talking trash to these search-averse lazy folk than I am by the mentality of "Screw you for telling me to use search, you pompous prick. I came here to post my question and receive the instant gratification that I've come to expect."
