Granted I use WinMe, but when I reformatted/reinstalled a month ago (as well as the few times before that this year), EQ music was always horrible. I have a SB Live! X-gamer 5.1. The fix that worked for me (but didn't work for syndaen, who has the same music probs I had...he has sb live plat I believe) is to go to the Device Manager and to disable SB16 Emulation. Once I did that, I was good to go and all the crappy music problems went away.

Hope that helps, and if it doesn't I hope you find a solution :(
