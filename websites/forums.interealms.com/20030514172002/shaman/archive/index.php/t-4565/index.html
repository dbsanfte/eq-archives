Hey Yo:

I am not sure how many people have had a chance to visit Jaggedpine Forest, but it's a decent place to XP. Now I was 58 when the zone opened so I didn't get to fully experience the hunting in this zone, but the experience is still pretty decent at 58 even with all the LBs.

I hunt the Darkpaw gnolls in the cave north of the little town where the zone to Surefall is.

The setup I use is as follows:
1. Summon pet in town
2. Buff self and pet in town (I get: sta, kragg, agility, and regen; dog gets: haste, kragg)
3. Mem summon companion
4. Run into the Gnoll caves until I see a spawn that's blocking my path.
5. Summon Bitey and finish off the gnoll
6. set Bitey to guard here and move on

The place I eventually setup at is the mouth to the cave where Barducks Darkpaw spawns. It can be reached by heading into the first room and turning left, then following these directions from each room: Straight (or left), Right, then left.

This will bring you to a large cavern with lots of gnolls in it. At 58 only some of the warriors are still blue to me. There are also Oracles, Curates (who CH), and Revenants. These guys hit fast for around 100ish, but have very little hitpoints. Once slowed they are pretty much trivial. The respawn time is fast so you will have plenty of mobs to pick from. There are two wanderers that path into the tunnel, but I was able to handle up to 4 at a time so I didn't worry about a couple adds.

Each Gnoll will drop ~5plat and change. They also drop semi precious gems (peridots and stuff). They also drop some low to midrange loot. All of the weapons they drop have a visible graphic when the mob has an item so it's easy to know when you have found something.

I was able to get 1.5 blues per hour here at 58. I think that rate should be higher at 56 or 55. This is about the same experience I get in Katta doing guards. For any Barbarians that have the Spiritstone of EF - this zone is really conveniently located. Just run through Black Burrow and zone into JP.

~Bax

Another location you might want to try, guaranteed to have no wanderers, is accessed by keeping to the right in the caves until you fall through a pit. Runners path into the wall, so even if you don't have snare you're safe here, and nothing wanders down here. MOBs are all the same level and variety as the ones in the big room (where Barducks spawns). Another spot that's good is at the bottom of the slope where Barducks spawns, provided he's been killed recently (respawn time seems to be about 24 hours). No wanderers here either and lots of room to operate.

Also, at Level 57, all Warriors are blue, which helps with experience. Things slow down a bit at 58, as you said. At 59, my guess is it will be time to move on.

It's also fun to group here: just keep moving and choose a different, loopy path each night, and it will take a while to get boring. If you want to kill Barducks the best bet is to take him outside, as he hits really hard for his level, and the MOBs around him will repop fairly quickly. But he doesn't summon and isn't particularly resistant.

Also, if you're evil it doesn't take long to max faction with the pinky town in Jaggedpine, giving you a bank/merchants within the same zone. And before that there is another cave with gnolls who start out apprehensive to everyone, and they have a bank and merchants as well.

I tried this place yesterday at 55. I found the little pit as the guy described, thanks, it is all you said it was - no wanderers, only one close pop, and the runners path right into that wall. That's the good bits.

The bad bits is I found the xp slow here compared to HHK. At HHK I got a blue every 25 mins. Here, it was 40 mins to a blue. Not horrible, but not great. (that's 1 1/2 blues an hour). The warriors are easy, but there's some really funky pathing problems in the cave. For instance, sometimes I had to let warriors wack on me as I ran because if I outran them to the pull spot (where dog is waiting), they'd get confused and path back to their spawn point and loose aggro. Grr. The hill into the cave where the pit is is particularly bad for pathing and loosing mobs.

Then there's the defilers. Annoying little critters. They are necros. They cast blood boil, plague, and venom of the snake, and yes, they like to cast all 3 while you're pathing them down the buggy path to dogdog. (If you run away, they still manage to land spells on you even though you are OOR, and then they do that go back to spawn point and loose aggro trick). Seeing your hp drop way down even with an iskar bp and regrowth, really is a bit unnerving.

The oracles are another pain, they do cast venom, but not blood boil. Getting enstill stuck on you also is a big pain.

The warriors are pushovers.

But any spot can spawn one of the three. At 55, some where lb, some were db, I think it was a 50% mix. Warriors are more common than oracles or defilers. They are all social, but with a low range, you have to be pretty close to get an add.

So I went back to HHK and ground through the most boring level ever there, got 56. I am definatley going to find another place now, HHK is driving me crazy :). But I don't think Jaggedpine is "uber" xp, if half the mobs were turning blue at 55, I guess 75% must be blue at 56, and the xp wasn't that stellar to begin with. For starters, no "dungeon" bonus while in the caves.

Loot was ok, I made about 150 plat, plus I got a gnoll ring with +5 int and +5 to two saves, fodder for my bazaar mule.

But if you're looking for pure bang for your buck, do HHK. This place was a bit more of a hassle than it was worth.
