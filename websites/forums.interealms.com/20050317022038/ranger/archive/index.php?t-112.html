One of the interesting things about dieting is that low-fat diets can sometimes make it harder to lose weight.

Fat content is one of the things that your body uses to regulate your metabolism. By going on a very low fat content diet, you body thinks that it's starving, and so radically lowers your metabolism, which reduces the rate at which you burn calories and fat. So, in effect to lose weight on such a diet, you do have to starve yourself (unhealthy) AND when you stop the diet, you metabolism is so slow that a normal healthy diet immediately throws the weight back on.

The best bet is a good balanced diet, regular exercise and lots of water. The only adjustment in what you eat, should be in the overall amount that you eat, not the proportions of what you eat.

Most diets that work with any degree of speed are very, VERY bad for you. You have to decide what's more important, your health or your weight. The drug based diets aren't much better. There is basically 2 categories, appetite suppressants and drugs that block fat absorption. Some are addictive, and almost all of them will only work for as long as you keep taking the pills, then all the weight comes back. It's excellent marketing.

The only healthy diet that actually works is a balanced diet with lots of exercise. This is painfully slow, some people with very slow metabolisms have to exercise 4-6 hours a day to see any results, and even people with faster ones will see MAYBE 5 pounds come off in a month. There are some unlucky people out there who will NEVER lose much weight without seriously hurting themselves. It's genetic, and nothing can be done.

I get there, have to take a test (scored 96th percentile on the ASVAAB, they had high hopes for me), then find out that I'm 5 lbs overweight. 5 lbs out of 235. Like I said, I weigh 320 now. Even when you aren't very fat, being told you weigh too much is devastating, even to a man. Just keep working out though, get that weight off, and in a few months when you see them out again and they are staring at you, just tell them what they did 4 months ago, and to go to hell. Don't fall out of it like I did, I really wish I hadn't. Not that I'm unhappy, I'm ALWAYS happy. But I'd be happier if I was 230 instead of 320.
