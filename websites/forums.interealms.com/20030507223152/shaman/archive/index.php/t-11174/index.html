Calabar
I’ve been using a spine chill spear and a charred guardian shield since I started playing my shaman. But for the last few levels it hasn’t been doing very much damage, relatively speaking. I’ve been stepping into melee with the mobs to work on my combat skills, but it’s my spells that are really killing them. So I decided to look for another weapon.

I also want to work on my 2H blunt skill so I thought I’d try to find a decent weapon for that skill. And since I miss a lot anyway, I thought it would be nice to do some decent damage with the blows that do land. After reading another post, I started looking for a barbarian spiritists hammer in the bazaar. I have yet to find one, but I did find a poison wind censer for 1200pp.

The censer has a delay of 28, the same as my 1H spear, and does base 20 damage, which is twice what my spear did. I looked it up on the net real quick, out of fear of someone else buying it, and found that its proc of Gale of Poison did over a hundred points of damage. So I bought it. When I tried to use it I found that I wasn’t high enough level to make it proc. That’s when I did more research on it.

Whoever created this weapon had to be laughing his or her fool head off. It drops your poison resist by 30 points and then procs an area effect poison spell that also effects the wielder. That’s rather sad. Thankfully it doesn’t proc until level 50. Still, its very fast for a 2H blunt weapon and it does twice the damage my other weapon did. I’m thinking of staying with this weapon to train up my 2H blunt skill until I can get the barbarian spiritists hammer. Does that sound like a good idea, or do I really need to catch up on my sleep? :p
Jaruden
It procs a rain spell, so not just 1 hit for 100, but 3 hits for 100.

It was put into the game at one point a while ago when the devs felt like "spicing things up" so they put in some items that had nice dmg/delays/procs, but hurt the wielder as well. I think 3-4 similar items for various classes got put in at that point, then, as most things, they gave up on the theme and went back to making good items with no drawbacks.

You may want to look into a Hammer of Spiritcalling. 39/43 ratio, it's a little slow, but packs a wallop. Generally pretty cheap, 2-4k on almost all servers from what I've heard. There's also an 18/20 2hb that drops in CT as well, very nice for skilling up.
