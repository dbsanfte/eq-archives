Blazz
I guess the main question here is basically: "Is it crashing for JUST you, for you AND a bunch of other people you play with, or is the zone crashing as a whole in EQ".

The initial poster said the zone itself is crashing, which indicates an issue with SOE rather than the individual systems of the people raiding there. This is the first I have heard of this, myself. I raided quite a bit in Ssra a couple years back and it was always a fairly reliable zone.

My best advice is to continue to /petition, /feedback, /bug the situation, and I also recommend you encourage your guild to do a "Mass E-Mail" about the condition of the zone to SOE. Nothing abrasive or flaming, just have a bunch of people describe the situation. You could also post on the SOE boards, and see if the problem is limited to your server or is also happening on other servers. Sometimes, a particular server gets a definite problem with certain situations that other servers do not experience. A bunch of e-mails from ONE server on ONE zone would probably get a lot of attention. Just remember to keep the e-mails civil. You can also mass /feedback and /bug, but I DO NOT recomment sending a ton of /petitions. This can be detrimental, as it fills the petition queue with a ton of spam, and that can piss of the people you are trying to get to pay attention to your problem.
