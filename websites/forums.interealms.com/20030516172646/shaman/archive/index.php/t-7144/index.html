GroutRavenPaw
Mana pool is largely irrelevent for a shaman because of our Cann series of spells. You want HP over Mana every time. Trust me, makes a nice difference. If I could replace every armor slot I have with armor of the same ac but with 100hp on it I'd take it hands down. HP > Wisdom > Mana pool.

Think of it this way, do you ever go out of mana that often when using your Cann spells? Do you ever go out of HP that often when using your Cann spells? I'd imagine you run out of HP more often than you run out of mana just like I do. HP items and regen items help a ton until you hit 60 and get the spell Torpor.

Chose HP, Chose life.

Ears - sell forest loops to vendors. Rplace with either Imbued Ivory Golden Earrings, Earring of Cleansing, Earring of Purity, Earring of Twisted Leaves or Earring of the Solstice.


Fingers - keep +7wis rings for alchemy skill raising, or sell. Replace with 6AC 65hp rings (cheap) or Shadel Bandit Rings (expensive, but better)

Bracers - do the Kromriff Military Bracer quest. Get in UP groups and hope to win a roll on a Brecer of Sense. Go to Seb and try to win a roll on a JBB.

Arms - Barbed Ringmail

Shoulders - Elder wolfhide (from Hollwshade Moor) or camp the Hole forever to get Imbued Granite Spaulders. Obulus Death Shroud if you ae rich (but the price might come down soon)

Chest - Get Barbed Ringmail, Froggy BP, Iskar BP, etc

Belt - Chitinous belt

Legs - ColdainMilitary Legging Quest

Range - Ceremonial Smudging Stick (Hollowshade)

Primary - Rune Etched Icewurm Fang

Secondary - Crystal Chitin Shield

Just some options - there are a million others
