The biggest problem you will face, over the long term, is that if you start farming Tier 1 gear now, without also doing the subsequent Tiers, you will quickly find that a lot of the Tier 1 stuff goes to "rot".

That's fine, nobody cares....except that, once you DO reach the point where you can progress to Tier 2 and beyond, you are going to find that many of your less ethical guild members suddenly start doing the math, and showing up for the raid late. Amazingly, they'll manage to log in JUST as Tier 1 is being finished.

Every guild I've talked to about Time has experienced the same thing. People who have nothing to gain in Tier 1 just quit showing up for Tier 1. And unfortunately, that makes finishing the Tier considerably tougher, especially if that's the peak of your current gear.
