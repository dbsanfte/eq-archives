Kriel Darkbane
People don't hate LoY. In most cases, they just don't care about it. Apathy is different from hate.

Look at the expansion trail before LoY:

Kunark - 10 new levels, huge new adventure areas, outdoor and dungeon both. Two new cities. Tons of new loots, spells, etc... And it saved EQ from overcrowding hell.

Velious - A new entire continent. Introduced competitive factions that PC's could ally with. Tons of new raid content. Uber loot such as had never been seen before. Tons of quests. Most people on many servers were not L 60 yet either, so still had lots of exp'ing to do.

Luclin - Effectively, an entire new continent. A new player race and class. AA exp. Quite a few new spells. A lot of new raid content, with unbelievable, better-than ever loot (note the theme here). A lot of the zones weren't so hot, especially after they were "tuned" but some were quite good.

PoP - 5 new levels! New AA skills. All high end content. Vast amounts of raid content for the uber crowd. Loot such as has never been dreamed of.

LoY - a new player race. A few new zones for L 35-60 people. Nothing at all for high end players except expanded bank boxes, armor dyes, and the mapping feature. Even the two new spells are lower level, and often nearly useless for someone who is past that level (although for some classes they are quite useful). LoY didn't expand the gameplay in any meaningful way. It made some nice tweaks that could have easily been free patches - except that they were saved for LoY so that all players would have a reason to buy it.

All previous expansions provided new raid content with improved loot, and all of them except velious provided expanded levels or AA skills or both. LoY was aimed at the lower level people who didn't get much out of PoP, at least in the short term. So all the people who WEREN'T in that category didn't really care about it. And for the people who are in that category - it provides a few new exp zones with nothing in particular to recommend them over previous zones, but which are just hard enough to get to that it's hard to find a group there. Then you have all those tunnels in Gunthak that you have to run through invis to get anywhere, which is dangerous and discouraging for many classes, until they are of a level where they don't really want anything from LoY anyway.

LDoN continues the trend of not expanding the high end of the game, and catering more to the exp group crowd than the raid crowd. There's nothing wrong with that, as the exp group/casual crowd really needed what LDoN provides, and there's a lot of those players. There is some raid content, and maybe it will someday be tweaked so that someone actually uses it.

But it will be interesting if there's ANOTHER expansion that doesn't substantially outclass Planes of Power loot. Has mudflation finally hit the limit with Planes of Power? Or will they come out with even more ridiculous loot for the ubers to chase? And will you have to have farmed plane of time to be able to do that new content? I sure have gotten off on a tangent, haven't I? ;)
