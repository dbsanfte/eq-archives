bammboozle
I'm going to throw in my 2 cents for this zone. First off, I came here as a twinked troll shammy at 15. After a couple rough starts I found a pattern that got me to 21 in 8 hours. I am twinked, which bumps my AC and mana pool, but it can be used the same for just about anyone, just at a slower pace.
Anyway, I am zoned in the Nexus so the first thing I do is buff myself with everything I got. If you can get KEI, great, if not, last buff is SoW.(you cant SoW in SH or Paladul). Go to Paladul from the SH side and take the right hand trail past the bandit camps that heads upwards. On this trail up to the peak will spawn a mushroom, a wandering fiend, and a stationary fiend at the bend/peak itself. I usually concentrated on the fiends.
Root, Tainted Breath, then Spirit Strike to death. Re-root and med as needed. The trick is that your SoW'd, and you have a kite path consisting of the top of the peak(where the stationary spawn is) all the way down to the bandit camps if needed. The path literally follows the lake. If you get in trouble, zone out through SH side. the bandit camps are primarily camped 24/7, so you should rarely aggro them, and if you do, your SoW'd. Make sure to bind in the Nexus, so when SoW is going, just gate back, re-SoW, then run back to Paludal. The only times I died is when I was doing something stuiped cause I was in a hurry. At 15, I would get between 1/4 to 1/3 a yellow per yellow con. Easy experience, and pretty fun too
