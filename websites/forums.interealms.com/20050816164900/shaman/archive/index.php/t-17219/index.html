Skeeter
It's worth the hassle to bug a mage for the mage summoned items - especially if they're hunting in the same zone as you, you can buff their pet. Throw focus of soul and strength of daiku on their pet, they'll love you!

Things to get --
-the bag of plate armor (they summon a bag and it's filled with armor, it's just one casting for the full set of 8 plate pieces).
-a couple of weapons. they'll know what to get you.
-the haste mask.
Every mage gives their pets those items at a minimum -- many add the elemental defender (a cloak) and possibly other items.

If you're soloing, duoing with a mage works really well too. Their pet can tank better than dogdog, and your quiessence works nicely on slowed mobs so they can nuke instead of healing. Mage has great nukes and pet damage -- shaman adds slows, heals, crowd control, and dots that make mobs melt. Just stick to mobs that don't run or use root (mages don't have root, so you'll need to root -- those ornate pants are handy for that, heh).
