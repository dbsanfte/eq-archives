BlargX
You can root/rot in Umbral plains -- shak dathors are your best bet. When root breaks, you get summoned. The gantru are slow kills, so they're not as good, and the elysians don't give good experience.

You can also do trees and such in Plane of Nightmare. Skeeters in Plane of Disease, close to the zone(the closest ones, not the others like malarians, cuz they're much harder). Rats there are considerably tougher, so try to avoid them. You can also do the larvae.

If possible, I'd really try to get a duo going, though. Even splitting the experience two ways, you'll still have an excellent chance to get more experience, because shamans kill so slow. A druid who can charm rats in POD or bats in PON is a great partner, as is a melee.

And I'd think about doing some hard-core money camping. Torpor will really help your experience gain out a lot. Not to mention save you from a fair amount of deaths.

Even if you hate ME, I loved to solo the Cheif tree. Easy to single pull, and can mostly tank/JBB/epic/dogdog mobs. And if someone trains you, then they are complete idiots since that isn't anywhere close to zone. I loved to set up on top of the wall, just down from the wall of the ruins. Nice thing about it, when they run, you just sit then and let your pet eat them as they run off, and you continue to JBB them. Very few (if you get the way they run off) will actually keep going, at which point you'll need to root them. Otherwise they'll stop about half way down the hill and try to decide if they are gonna run, or attack you.

Goldark
If you play at quiet times then it might be worth trying the Plane of Innovation too.

Positives:
Left side is single-pullable for miles
Right side is single/double pullable and one can be rooted
No roamers = no adds at zonein (if you keep back)
Mobs are rootable and tankable, your choice
No casters
They don't run off so you don't need snare (good for us barbarians)
Easy to get to and no break-in time

Negatives:
Useless if its busy, too many trains
Root sometimes breaks quick (use malo/malosini, holds better then)
Archaics are not slowable, if you get one on the left and one on the right you are stuck.

I normally pull with malosini, run back to camp, slow as they get back in range, stick pet on it, cripple, pox, bane, jbb, renew bane.
