Zyphyr
Get a friendly druid to go with you.

You go in invis.
Druid casts harmony on him. His agro range in now 1 foot.
You open trade window from maximum trade range.
Druid casts invis on you. You now have indifferent faction.
You hit trade.
You get the necklace and the faction hits.
