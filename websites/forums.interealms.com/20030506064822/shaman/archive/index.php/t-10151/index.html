Best advice I can offer is to start a level 1 shaman (same race as your brothers) and level him up (untwinked) to the bare minimum of level 24. That might not even be high enough to fully understand how to play the class as our role changes significantly at several places throughout our career.
eqphreak
Pretty simple really, debuff and slow the thing first (root helps), then use your epic + jbb + dots to kill said creature.

Keep your buffs up at all times, mainly your HP buff (Focus) + stamina, and sow always. Probably just use the boots for sow as I do.

Torpor + canni = unlimited mana, and you can tank stuff for a bit if you have good AC/HP. Or just root and dot it.

good luck don't make us look bad ;)
