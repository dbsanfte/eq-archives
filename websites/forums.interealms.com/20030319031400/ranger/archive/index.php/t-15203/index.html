Beohram
At 62 about the only thing that is light blue in PoN are they young bats, and the Banshees that spawn on the zone-in side of the stream.

At 63, werewolves and about 2/3 of the ravens turn light blue. Coincidentally enough, the ravens that remain dark blue (the lvl 53 ones) are the ones you were never able to fear.

At 64 pretty much everything that spawns on the zone-in side of the stream, birds, bats, trees, and spiders will be light blue to you.
