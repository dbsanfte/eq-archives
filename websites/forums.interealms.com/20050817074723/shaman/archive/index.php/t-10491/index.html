I Pled a mag to 55 and didnt even use his pet till 44. Heres what I did and it can work for most any Int class or mele.

Give him what ever Hp/Ac gear you can lay your hands on, A good 2hb wepon (mele stats), Fbss, By 44 get the mag the staff that drops in chardox that has the click nuke and change into Int mana gear.

1-5 painell newbi area, Buff him and turn him lose.
5-10 The warens, Once agine buff and turn him lose.
11-24 Katta ridge, Buff him up and send him on a killing frenzy.
24-44 The guards in Highhold keep. They have crap for HP, slow, debuff and mag can mele away for killer xp. Xp gets slow around lv40. (note only do this if faction is not a major issue for you, be it evil race or that you simply dont care)
44-49 Tower of frozen shadows, Buff mag and pet, pull mob, send in pet and have the mag stand there clicking his little nuke staff. (You did rember to get the staff from chardox right:P )
49-55 PoJ, you should know what to do by now :)

I also grouped with the mag in places like seb while I worked on my live froggy faction (damn that helm) PoI. FG ect.. Stright Pling is most always faster XP. But sometimes its just safer / easire to group with the char being Pl'ed.

Like any good 2 boxing shammy I have a lot of Pled chars. The above is what I have used for all of them but for. PoJ I just started Pling there.
