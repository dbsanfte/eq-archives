People need to realize that the "new" Cripple isn't anything special. It's not debuffing anything the old Cripple didn't.

Check out the numerical differences between the 2 spells:

old Cripple:

Slot Description
1: Decrease DEX by 58 (L53) to 75 (L70)
2: Decrease AGI by 68 (L53) to 85 (L70)
3: Decrease STR by 68 (L53) to 85 (L70)
4: Decrease AC by 30 (L53) to 36 (L70)

new Cripple:

Slot Description
1: Decrease DEX by 100
2: Decrease AGI by 100
3: Decrease STR by 100
4: Decrease AC by 39

The new Cripple debuffs:

+25 DEX
+15 AGI
+15 STR
+3AC

In other words, nothing to expect wonders from in there.
