Delirios
Remember also that if you cant find your corpse for some reason or another (sometimes corpses get stuck in the wall and are only partially visible thus making them hard to spot in unknown area) you can ask help from Bards, Necromancers and Shadowknights. They have a spell that locates your corpse. Chipped bone rods from merchats do the same thing, just can be a bit costy for an untwinked new player at lower levels.
I guess you already knew this from playing a necromancer tho...

EDIT: You know how to set up a /corpse hotkey right? Targetting your corpse and using /corpse -hotkey/command allows you to "drag" your corpse to you from 50ft away. This is handy if you dont know exactly where your corpse is. Just spam the key and run around, or if you happen to die at a hostile place, ie. in the middle of an orc camp for example. Sometimes it's also good to drag your corpse to a safe spot to loot it safely.

dirtybax
Here is how I addressed the need to get a loc at early levels when you die.

1. Bind Sense Heading to ability 1
2. make a button that says:
/doability 1
/loc
3. Put this button right next to slam and mash it during battle. This serves to raise your sense heading skill and get a loc every now and then. Should you die, just scroll up and look at the last Loc you got
4. If you die edit that button and put in a line that says /corpse at the bottom. Now you have an all in one corpse recovery button.
