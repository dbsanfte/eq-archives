deria
While KM is certainly not a replacement for torpor, it IS an improvemnt over what was available before. If you do not have Torpor, use KM until you get Torpor -- but remember that Torpor is where the action is.

Torpor can be affected by AA (duration) and some focus items (like mana preservation 3 items) -- which change the ratio signficiantly.

For example (bear with me if my numbers are slightly off),

Torpor with no modfifiers is 1200 hp for 200 mana.

With mana preservation 3, its 1200hp for 170 mana.

With spell casting reinforcement 3, its 1500hp for 170 mana.

With spell casting reinforcement mastery, its 1800hp for 170mana.

With spell casting mastery 3, it *might* be 1800hp for 140 mana. I'm not actually sure if spell casting mastery and mana preservation 3 stack -- maybe someone can fill me in.

The bottom line is that torpor grows with you; KM does not.
