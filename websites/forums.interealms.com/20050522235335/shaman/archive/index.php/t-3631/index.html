Most of the "good gear" doesnt drop in outdoor zones but in dungeons. That is why you wont see much of it, if you hunt in LoIO.

You dont say what you are wearing, but at your level you really dont have to worry about keeping different sets of equipment.

Raid gear and resist gear are very situational and are something that first begins to matter, when you get near to the socalled "Endgame" where you go up against the different bossmobs that are spread around the world.

At you level my advise to you would be to focus on getting xp and only buy gear if it is a direct upgrade of something you are wearing.

In your mid to late 30's you should begin to consider doing the totemic armor (unless you are an Iksar that gets their own armor quests in Cabilis)
