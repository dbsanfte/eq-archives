OK Shammy, to solo from 48-54 easy,,,

Grab an occational KEI in the Nexus and run your Shammy over to Dawnshroud Peaks.

Start off killing wolves, they will be dark blue to you.

At about level 50-51, switch to Stonegrabbers.
The Stonegrabbers are 1 blue in 3 kills and you can pull 1-2 at a time when you get the it down.

I did it and I didn't even have to use my pet!

Enjoy...
