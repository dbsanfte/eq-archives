shamuofxev
ok, the thing in WToV you want is LtK for the ST keys. With 32 people and clerics who know how to do a decent CH chain you should have no problem with Vindi either, if you have a good MT that can hold aggro Vindi is easy. Vindi gives a pretty decent faction hit, so in case you want to switch to do WToV and go on giant faction you might want to skip that one, the Tormax hit is hard to make up.
Other nice targets at that stage would be Rhag 1 and 2 in Ssra, IV/Va'dynn in AR, and the named in UP (RC, doom and the camel).
