Your gear is nice. You are still growing into some of your gear, but you should be able to ride that gear without any major upgrades into your 50's. When you get Regrowth you can swap the Ikky BP for something that has more AC (and hopefully some nice stats to go with it)

The horse is a great purchase if you do most of your exping outside. And with the large humber of outside zones and the relative scarcity of really good dungeon zones, who doesnt nowadays? You will only wanna use it for standing regen though; dont rely on the horse for speed...with sow you are about twice as fast as the horse. Lizard works the same, but is a little more expensive. The lizards head does not obscure as much of the screen, but as a shaman you have shrink, which can elimate that problem entirely.

The Antique Ring is only up to level 44 by the way. You will still use sub-44 spells after 44, but thats when I would replace the ring with the Velium fire.

Have you thought about a JBB? Expensive, I know, but like the horse, its one of those items that completly changes how you play the game. You will FLY though the mid to late 40's with one. FYI, a GREAT place to make money at your level is n the new LOY zones. You should be able to make good exp there and the money is the best Ive seen in a really long time.

Your gear is fine for your level and will last you easily into 50.

Some upgrades to work towards are the Solstice earring, a Shadel Bandit ring, an Enshrouded Veil/Veil of the Executioner, Iksar Hide Cape, and a new BP post-50.

Just keep your eye out for items that balance your gear and contribute to your mana/hp pool and resists. Most gear now has considerable AC on it anyhoo, so I would not worry with that aspect as much.
