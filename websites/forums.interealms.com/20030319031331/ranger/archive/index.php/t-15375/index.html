Medain Valacirca
You might give Maidens Eye a try.Since PoP came out its been a lot less crowded and lfg people usually get invites to whatever groups might be there.Also some no drop gear that might be upgrades for you drop in there.

Get PoP if you can, the transport system alone is well worth it.You will be able to cover a lot of ground looking for groups or get to your groups a lot faster no matter where they may be.

Lastly dont be afraid to start your own groups. The command /who all 47 53 will tell you where people your lvl are hanging out, add a lfg on to that and you can usually find people to start a group with.
