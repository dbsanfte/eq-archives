Dandi
If rotations interest you, Karana might be good too. There are still abundant FFA targets for those who enjoy the race. The main purpose, in my understanding, is to give smaller guilds a chance at the most contested raid targets, especially those needed for epics. As far as I know, the Karana rotation targets are Vox, Naggy, Venril, Trakanon, Severilous, Talendor, Gorenaire, Keepers, Zlandicar, Yelinak, Cazic, NToV. Oh and Fear is also on rotation. Hate used to be, until the revamp. PoA is also on a slightly different system that allows people to select a few consecutive days.

Every now and then there's a movement for going completely FFA and a guild or two runs around KSing for a while, but so far none have lasted more than a few months. You can visit the homepage of the KGC (Karana Guild Central) (http://kgc.igz.net/cgi-bin/Ultimate.cgi?action=intro) but much of it is available only to representatives of member guilds, to prevent FFA types from having any more information than necessary. It's not a perfect system but is definitely a huge bonus to those who prefer smaller, less competitive guilds, like mine. :) There is often a wait of a month or two between chances at a target, but you're guaranteed a 24 hour window in which to make your attempt(s) undisturbed.
