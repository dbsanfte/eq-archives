Well I resolved my search problem, and found out what I need to know.

Basically Ghiosk spawns from a reaver. That reaver is located on the ground floor of the Castle, and can be easily single pulled to a single reaver capable group(melee heavy with Cleric and healers). That single pull is made by a rogue picking the lock, and someone pulling him with a ranged weapon or spell. Ghiosk is pretty much entirely MR even to Disease and Poison.

Alternatively one could hang out in CoM, look for a Reaver raid and seek to join in or at least ask if you can loot the books if no raid shaman are in need of them. Apparently Ghiosk is usually killed several times during the average Reaver raid.
