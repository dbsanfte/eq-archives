CALI - This board does not have an "anti-cleric" tilt. (side note: it is a running joke for shaman to ask for CHeal - no one takes it seriously here. often tied to wanting a 1200 pt dd, lol )

Shaman are, and imo should be, very versatile. One of the advertised versatilities has always been healing skills. I never understood why a shaman casting SupHeal would heal only 85.7% as many hp as a cleric casting the same spell. Cleric also gets much better heals, which is as it should be, imo.

no raid will be saying they don't need another cleric, because they have a shaman with improved heals from this patch. a group pushing its limits will still need a cleric. a group grinding exp at a camp regarded as very safe for their level will continue to be able to do so without a cleric. or a shaman, or a druid. in that case, any of the priest classes should be able to keep up with the damage from the mobs.

Clerics should remain the best healers, and they will remain essential for raiding. The two other priest classes should be able to do reasonable healing, as well.
