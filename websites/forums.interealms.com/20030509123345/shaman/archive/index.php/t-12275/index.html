Shandy Orcbane
There was a driver problem for the 7500. Updated drivers took care of that a while ago. RAM makes a godlike difference in how the game plays; even going from 768 to 1024 makes a difference.

Turning on the texture cache will dramatically reduce load times for zones, on your second and subsequent visits to the zone.

The files can get corrupted, and new graphics added to the game can mess up how the caches work, so if you see things like all your inventory icons looking like bead necklaces, or our feared "clown dog," delete the *.txc files from your EQ directory.
