Colal
I soloed my first king at 54. My success was 100% dependant on the setup of the room. However with enough time you can get the room setup anyway you like. Here is what you do:

Bind in the hall way, and pull something, if it comes single then you are good to go. If more than one comes, then enstill (long lasting root) one of them and zone out. Then gate back and kill the single mob.

If you want to farm crowns. Keep doing cycles of this until all 4 extra mobs in the room will not come when you pull the king/king PH.
