Of the choices you listed, I would say CoM hand-down for XP. The problem is, at least on my server, CoM is usually a ghost town unless someone is raiding there - so you'd have to BYOG. You could probably solo there at 46 with good equipment, but I don't know if the downtime would make it worthwhile.

KC (or anything else in the LOIO-OT-DL-KC cycle) is unpalatable to me unless I have a group ready-made, just because of the huge numbers of idiots in the zone. You might be able to find a group in KC if you're really set on going, but 50+ players seem to be preferred for groups.

As far as farming FS, you could do that in Mistmoore. I'd recommend Sol A though: there aren't many people there these days, and it has better FS drops IIRC.

Good luck!
