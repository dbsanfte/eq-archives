From my guild boards:

Keyboard commands:

/taskaddplayer <name>
/taskremoveplayer <name>
/taskquit
/taskplayerlist

Alt-Q for the task window

There are basically 5 functions for each zone: They are an exp group static zone, they are also instanced mission zones, they also have small events (for 18-24 people), large events (for 36-42 people), and raid events (for 54 people).

For the static exp zones, the level spread begins at level 45 for Broodlands and works it's way up to level 70 (MPG static) difficulty in the Nest.

The mission system begins at level 50 and scales up to level 70 hards. The missions are requested in Lavastorm, and send you to one of the instanced version of the four static zones past the Broodlands in DoN.

The small events begin at level 45 (in Lavastorm) and scale all the way to level 70 Qvic+ difficulty. The large events begin at level 65 Vex Thal difficulty and scale up to level 70 Txevu difficulty. The raid events begin at level level 70 time, and work their way up to Anguish+ difficulty.

Instances:

Tirranun's Delve - Average difficulty. For level 70s, similar to Causeway. Instances available for 53-70
Stillmoon Temple - Above average difficulty. For level 70s, similar to deep Dranik. Instances available for 58-70
Thundercrest Isles - Hard difficulty. For level 70s, similar to Muramite Proving Grounds. Instances available for 58-70
The Nest - Hardest difficulty. For level 70s, similar to Riftseekers. Instances available for 63-70

So there are effectively four levels of difficulty to choose from for missions.

The static zones are generally one level easier than the instances. Note that the Broodlands has no instances

Broodlands - Average difficulty, intended for players ~45-55.
Tirranun's Delve - Average difficulty, intended for players ~55-62.
Stillmoon Temple - Average difficulty, intended for players ~58-65.
Thundercrest Isles - Above average difficulty, intended for players ~63-70.
The Nest - Hard difficulty, intended for players ~65-70.
