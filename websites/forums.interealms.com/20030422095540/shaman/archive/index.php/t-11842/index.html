The epic DoT runs for 84 seconds, or 14 ticks. It starts by doing 12 damage on the first tick and each tick after that does an extra 12 (ie 12,24,36 ...)

The effect is magic based, it checks against magic resist to land, and can be removed with dispell magic.

And it does stack with every other DoT a shaman can cast. I think it also stacks with Splurt.
