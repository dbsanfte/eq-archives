What do you think of Ldon so far?

Good points:

Many of the dungeons are very enjoyable.
A little late for me maybe, but ever noticed how you stop watching the XP bar in Ldon? =)
Many of the mob models are very impressive indeed.
Some of the dungeons are fantastic too, guk is really absorbing, and doing Mira/Spiders den for the first time ranks as one of the most memorable EQ dungeon runs I've ever done.
The merchant rewards are fantastic, I'm retired from hardcore raiding and having full Luclin endgame gear but with weak arms/bracers is annoying, finally I can do something about it =)
Once you get beyond the overcomplicated nature of augments, they are fantastic, the weapon procs are brilliant (I urge anyone to go stick the 100 pointer Miragul 75 cold dd on there main weapon) and finally giving non mid/high end warriors access to UNRESISTABLE enraging blow style pure hate procs is possibly one of the best moves SoE has ever made on the class.

The bad:

A couple of dungeons are glaringly bad, the Dreary Grotto should have never made it past beta.
Augments are overcomplicated, but admittedly it needs to be to allow good augments without throwing the balance off completey in certain cases.
The regional points system sucks, its nothing more than a timesink enforcement and doesnt really allow people to focus more on the dungeon they enjoy, and if people all have different item region wishlists in your regular group, your in for a long haul. However this will blow over in a month or so imho once people eventually get that tally built up.
Zero new AA is a bit of a wasted opportunity imho, just makes me glad that I'm enjoying myself enough to not be bothered, still hastened rabidity came in useful in todays arena clearing so /shrug.

My biggest gripe however is that for me and my regular group, Normal is far too easy, but hard is basically unwinnable, we have robust enough to not really be in danger of wiping, but we lack the dps to get the job done consistently. Imho there should be 3 tiers of difficulty with medium mobs having pretty much the same damage output as hard mode, but with less hps. Hard's point reward should be upped a bit to make it more worthwhile, medium should be about where hard is now points wise, lootwise I'd say normal level loot with occasional hard level drops would be fair.
