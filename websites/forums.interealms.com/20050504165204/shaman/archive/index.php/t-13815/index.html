Hullk
I leveled up my cleric faster than I thought was possible. Here's how I did it. It involved using a descent damage/delay weapon (Got a sarnak devastator for 3k in the bazaar - 12/20 5 str 5 sta 25 mana, effect: holy might, procs at level 1), and Clarity 3 or above.

Level 30-40ish - Undead in Kasorea. Find a nice pull spot (I usually worked my way to the Xalgoz area). Have the cleric start meleeing, and chain nuke with your undead DD. At the same time have your shaman chain cast with your JBB (if you have one). If not, throw on an ice nuke.

The mobs there don't have that much hitpoints (1kish), so your 2 cleric nukes, and 2 JBB blasts should kill it, with the cleric doing slightly more damage.

Level 40-50ish - Undead in City of Mist. The zone in area should be dark blue until 45 or 46, so use the same strategy as above. After 46 you'll want to head over to the castle area before the locked doors and the black reavers. The undead there are higher level, and should be dark blue up to 53ish, but the exp slows down.

After level 50, I just started grouping him with my shaman and my brother's warrior and druid. We usually hunt for exp in Crypt of Decay, and the exp flies.

Only took me a week or so to get him up to level 50. May not be the fastest method, but it does work. Hope that helps.
