Mullzy
60 and Topor are a big part of how I solo (well, everyone would say that).

1) Fully buffed with best regen (any type is better than nothing) and doggie (only have speed on dog but HP buff is handy sometimes)
2) Pull with slow
3) Land epic
4) JBB, Cann, JBB, Cann, JBB
5) Topor
6) Repeat 4 and 5 until dead (usually no point to land epic again as you can do more damage in 3 clicks in JBB than the epic dot does in a minute.

Without Topor I would do variants of the following:

1) Fully buffed with doggie (speed/hps/regen on doggie)
2) Pull with slow
3) Land Epic
4) JBB until mob around 30%-50%, then root and let my doggie tank and epic do it's thing
5) If doggie gets to under 50% I'd step in and tank some more
6) Cann a bit and let regen work up to where you're comfortable pulling another mob.

I rarely used any Mana DoTs because even though you kill them faster... you have more downtime. The mobs you can XP off of in the mid 50's don't have many HP's and your JBB does more dps than any of your dots anyway, so epic+jbb+doggie usually does a really decent job.

The only way I'd Epic DoT/Mana DoT/Root/Med is if I had 2 mobs on the go. Make it more exciting and you get more xp/hour. I've tried 3 or 4 mobs at once using epic/root but it's easy to lose track of which mob *really* needs a dot refresh. If you refresh even 2 or 3 ticks too early you waste most of the damage.

Good luck regardless!
