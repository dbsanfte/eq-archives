to start, you recieve the WC (wailing caves) quests, inside the left eye socket of the giant skull entrance to WC. WC is in the oasis south west of cross roads, watch out for the koltar :P

you should get one in ratchet too, 99 year old port, i only saw the goblin once out of 6 clears of WC.

you will have the extermination quest, killing of the evil druid leaders, serpent blossom (from Thunder Bluff in the caves below the elder rise) if there is anymore im too tired to think.

in WC its a small dungeon, you will get multiples, at least 2 at a time, blobs duplicate themselves befor they die if you let them do it. the winged serpents poison, the raptors can sneak and hide and backstab,
stay to the right of the water deeper in the cave, you will fall in a hole if you don't watch your step, head a lil further into the instance this is where the fun begins ....

the instance.

once inside, its a whole new ball game, mobs hit harder, faster, more frequent, and there are spell casters (druids) they cast sleep, this is a good time to have a shaman to use tremor totems.
you will see a druid upon entering, hes a good guy, talk to him he will buff you for free, and give you quests.

-killing the serpent leaders-

1. lady anacondra, the first leader to kill is near the sleeping druid, she casts sleep alot, and turns into a serpent to dot you and do more damage, she heals when low on life. and average group of lvl 19 can take her.
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=12052
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=3039

2. lord cobrahn, go east in the water withthe crocs, you can kill the vipers on the way for the eradication quest. he does almost the same as anacondra, except he does more melee damage. and has large ammounts of adds near by.
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=6460
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=10410

3. lord pythas, hes in the winding caverns, he has lots of these little root mobs around him, they hit fast but have no hps, use AE spells on them, like shaman fire blast totem or mage nukes. hes not as hard as cobrahn or anacondra once his adds are killed.
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=6472

4. lord serpentis, hes a son of a b*tch, hes tough, hes green (and he killed duke in the G.I. joe movie :P) he is tough, have potions on hand for everyone, he has a bad way of chain casting nukes on your healers. his biggest buddy is just a step away, don't let him run to him.
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=12989
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=6469

5. Verdan the Everliving, the giant elemental, serpentis's buddy, hes lvl 22 he HITS like rosanne bar at a buffet, chain heal your tank after debuffs, totems and HoT's.
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=6631
http://wow.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?witem=6630

after you clear this room, drop down the far side of the room into that tunnel, it takes you to the entrance (WoW quest desingers and dungeon designers thought of it all !)

all of these mobs drop amazing loot.
