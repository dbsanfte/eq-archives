Thorzion
Exp mods are really nice. 2 months ago I would have recommended Gunthak harbor without hesitation; since the exp mod was bumped up a bit and a PoK stone was added the population has exploded (at least on my server) and its a bit harder to find a quiet spot to work in. While before it was rare to see more than 3 people in the zone at any one time it now has more than 20 people at prime time. Find a quiet spot there and go to town. That should take you from about 28ish to about 38ish, when the exp goes from 2 or 3% per kill to 1% every 2 kills or so.

After that, run over to Dulak..takes you to 45ish really fast. If not there, then try Nurga. Good exp mod, cash, and exp.
