I would choose a monk over a warrior for a few reasons...

-Feign Death (great for CR recovery and splitting mobs)
-Stronger DPS (faster kills)

I also find monks easier to twink out (no-drop and purchased items)
-Iceburrower Skin of worms in Western Wastes
-MQ Shackle of Tynnium
-Fungi (great for either)
-etc.

Pretty much anything you will be duoing a monk can tank as well as a warrior (if you need a warrior- its probably too intense of a fight to be duoing...), and the monk's utility will come in handy more for when your shaman gets himself in trouble.
