afreeman
I think its more related to 2 other facts.

First, Droga (and Nurga) only has a +12% zone experience multiplier. While that is a good multiplier, several other dungeons have better, such as the hole with a +20% ZEM.

Second, Droga mobs, like most any mob from LoY zones or other "retuned" zones, have a truckload of hitpoints. At 62, with can4/torpor and mage-equipped alpha dog, I can only keep 3 droga spawns cleared during the 20 minute spawn time. In Sebilis, I can usually rotate 5-6 mobs of only slightly lower level in the same time.

Combine these facts with a player that has recently been exp'ing in the planes (which mostly have +35% or +42% ZEM mods and reletively low-HP mobs), and it all starts to make sense.

Finally, remember that there was also a patch that dramatically improved exp for killing mobs that are close to your level (I think the formula is within 5 levels of your level at 60, or within 7 at 65). Below that, even blue mobs will give comparetively little exp. Remember that patch that made many mobs that used to be light blue into blue mobs? Well, it didn't really change the exp they gave very much; they are basically still light blue for exp purposes.

Since mob exp is determined mostly by the level of the mob, you should power-level in the old world: the mobs are far easier for the amount of experience you will gain. In fact, the Hole is probably one of the best places for higher-level PL'ing, unless you have access to a CoD chanter.
