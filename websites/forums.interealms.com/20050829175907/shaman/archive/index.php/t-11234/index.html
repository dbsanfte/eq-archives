BattleAxe
Too many variables. To get a good picture - you need to rank at each level from about 30-65 in a bunch of categories:
Max theoretical DPS, Max useful DPS, useful group utility (evac, slow, CC, res), healing ability, tanking ability, solo ability, etc. You might need to break it out even further and rate the class in each category at each level by how well an imbecile would do compared to a skilled, knowledgable player.

You also have to define what you mean by each category. Take solo ability - do you mean max mobs killed per hour, max experience per hour, max level killed per your level, or the lowest level your class can solo Yeldema?

The one thing that EQ has over every other current game is the complexity of its classes. A single list that tries to combine all this information is meaningless.
