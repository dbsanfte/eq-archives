Celt
First of all, get 65 , or bring 65s with you to help, fire was hard as heck before then, the rest get easier but still :) Second, dual comping is very difficult in these.

I'd rate them easiest to hardest

Execution: Bring dps...lots of it.

Torture: Bring dps, and a slower. Kill the spectre when your team is low on health for a big surprise :)

Hanging: bring dps, and a slower, and watch the friggan fungusaur clones cause they make casting...difficult when they hit you.

Stoning: bring dps, and rangers with eq/am3 , and a slower and its not so bad. Show the npcs what the line of sight bug is all about.

Lashing: bring dps, and when that dark elf shows up you need to do some exploring fast with one member who can deal some damage.

Flame: bring dps, 4 people who can tank (doesnt have to be a "tank", shamen work), and a paladin, and get ready for a long night :)

Please don't ask for any more spoilers than that :) I defeated all of the trials when the last member of my 6 man hit 65. My makeup was myself (65 rng, 250+ aa), Kraiven (65 rng, 300 ish aa) Dantos (65 mnk, 150+ aa), Ughmooze (65 shm, 150+ aa) Bizdor (65 cleric, 150+ aa) and Wyldkeel (65 pal, 200+ aa). We waltzed through all the trials but flame, and stalled there overnight while we devised a strategy that would win. All in all, the other trials take lots of tries, a few deaths, and most importantly, FULL RAID BUFFS :) DO them as a guild, and you will have much more successes. All of our trials were completed with enchanter buffs on top of our existing setup.

I also know flame was completed by Wiz Brd Pal War Mnk Clr with ranger atk buffs, and enchanter mojo. It can be done, keep at it and don't stop trying :)
