Enchanter: Delegates one party member to recieve credit for all aggro party generates (other party members remain at the bottom of the aggro list permanently)

Necro: Each party member is able to select and raise a corpse (ala Wake the Dead) for 1 minute of every 15 (meaning Necros in party, including leader, with WTD can do it twice)

Paladin: Automatically levels the health of the entire group (all damage/heals are split on a weighted average, making it so that all members will always be at the same % health)

Ranger: Triples chance of all party members acheiving melee/ranged crits

Rogue: Evade and SoS affect entire party when activated. 50% bonus to all party member damage when behind mob.

Shadowknight: Health and mana tap procs provide life/mana to entire party (SK himself gets double the normal, others get same as SK normal would have).

Shaman: All spells group casts are granted an additional -50 resist mod to appropriate resist

Warrior: all melee damge done to group members is reduced by 25%

Wizard: triples chance of party achieving spell crits (heal, nuke, DoT and HoT)


My only goal in writing each of these was to make it so that it WAS overpowered, and that it would provide an effect that would greatly benefit the class itself, but also provide something to anyone in the group. I only listed what I envisioned at the final tier of the power, most of them would be appropriate to split into a couple levels (or as in the beastlord, a couple seperate abilities).
