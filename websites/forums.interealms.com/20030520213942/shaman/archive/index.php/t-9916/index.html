Xanden
I think the mana/tic is more along the lines of 22 mana at your level. Let's call it 20 for easy math, knowing that it's low.

Mana regenerated per minute: 20 * 10 = 200

So let's call 200 mana your per-minute benefit of using a horse.


ODS: saves 15% mana per spell cast. So, using the number above, let's look at how much mana you would have to expend to have the ODS out-benefit a horse... 200 mana is 15% of 1333 mana. So, if you are regularly expending more than 1333 mana per minute, then the ODS will be a better choice.
Xanden
There is also more to consider:

--If you are constantly cannibalizing, the horse means you won't have to worry about timing your canni-dance to keep from missing med-ticks.

---If you are currently canni-dancing and have it down pretty well pat, you aren't missing too many med-ticks and the horse will not provide much more mana regen, just make medding easier. In this case, the ODS would give more benefit but you would still have to time your canni-dance (which I hate).
