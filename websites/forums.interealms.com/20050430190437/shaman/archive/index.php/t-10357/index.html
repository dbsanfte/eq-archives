Goldark
There are a few sides to this....
1) Announcing a spell so someone else doesn't waste mana casting it to (e.g. heals)
2) Announcing a spell has landed so others can stop trying, or healers know they can relax (e.g. slow)
3) Announcing something as a warning (e.g. Canni V)
4) Hotkeying for fast access


With that in mind, I have a hotkey for torpor and kragg's mending that announces the heal and casts it. In case of fizzle or interrupt I hit it again, or alt-# or mouse click. Can lead to some spam but I find that far better than landing Kragg's just for a cleric CH to hit
1 second later.

For point 2 - I have a seperate hotkey to announce slow and malosini landing. I hit them after the spell lands successfully. On trash mobs I normally don't bother.

For point 3 - I have a hotkey for canni V for conveniance and to warn the group why my health is dropping

For point 4 - I have a hotkey for slow (to cast it) so its in easy reach.

There's a need to balance spamming the group with inane comments (I'm assisting xx, I'm nuking yy - who cares?) and informing them of important things and preventing wasted heals etc.
