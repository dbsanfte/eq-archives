I'm already doing this exact same thing, but with only Kunark on the enchanter's account. I started a gnome enchanter and after about an hour and a half of playing time, I've got her almost to lvl 8. This includes running around and getting spells for lvl 1, lvl 4, and lvl 8. I figure I've spent about 45 minutes total so far actually hunting.

What I've done so far is just put whatever buffs I could on her, including a damage shield, had her summon a pet and then pull with a DoT. DoT will do the damage required and then the mob will beat itself to death on the damage shield, while I healed her holding a Book of Obulous (Improved Healing II).

Lvl 1 - Killed newbie area stuff until she dinged Lvl 2

Lvl 2 -6 - Went to the druid circle in Butcher Block and had her kill the 8 skeletons in the area. You could do this until lvl 9 or so, but XP really slows down at lvl 6.

Lvl 6-10 - Dwarf bandit camp near the docks in ButcherBlock. There are 5 or 6 bandits. Be sure to harmony the camp and pull them to your enchanter a good distance away from the camp. They seem to have a huge aggro radius. You'll see anywhere from 5-10% XP per bandit at these levels.

Lvl 10-12 - I'd recommend the skeletons and various other things wandering around the mino caves in Steamfont.

Lvl 12 and on - This is where your enchanter will get mem-blur. This is the magic lvl when your enchanter's XP will start to fly. As said before, just find something that would be red to your enchanter, but not too red, snare the mob, beat it until it starts to run then back off. Have your enchanter cast mem blur 2-3 times, just to make sure it sticks and have her finish off the mob. Easy XP.

I'd recommend staying away from the typical XP grind cycle and focus on dungeons with an XP bonus. XP will be faster and you'll get chances to practice your chanting skills on overpulls. :)

When your enchanter becomes light blue to you, you should be able to group together and do the grouping and killing green to you, red to your enchanter type thing.

Not all of this is from experience, but it's what I plan on doing for my enchanter's route of leveling up.
