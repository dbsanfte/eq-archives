Nytmare
The Great Oowomp is neutral, any race can do the quest with him. It's only the surrounding Ogres who will be KoS, they are on Venril Santhir faction. Barbarians and Vah Shir have the option of doing the quest in FV or with Oowomp.

Army Behemoths can be found inside the temple downstairs (3 spawns), on the roof of the temple up the back ramp (3 spawns), and inside Lhranc's house (2 spawns). They are an uncommon spawn, so you just have to keep killing the placeholders until one does. You will not find an army behemoth in the stables area -- it's too low level for them. They can also sometimes be found up on the wall or in the reaver castle, but those places are hard to reach unless you're raiding black reavers.
