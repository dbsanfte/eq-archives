MUDs. Rules were quite different. You could only complete a quest once (they were flagged). If you told someone how to do a quest you were banned - you were only allowed to tell who started a quest. I think a fun addition would be arena events...

Similar to the "Ring" events but a single ring per try.

Go to any of the arenas and hail an NPC "Event Master". He will ask for a "ticket". Tickets are purchesed from a nearby ticket vendor.

You would have single PC, single group and multi-group tickets. Say 6 tickets of each type. (iron, steel, copper, silver, electrum, gold, platinum).

Each "win" flags you so you are no longer able to compete at that ticket level (no farming).

Once you buy your ticket and hand it to the event master, you will have 15 seconds before the mob pops. For single events, it's a single mob that is based on your class and level. For a group event, either a single mob or multiple mobs, for a multi-group, a swarm of mobs with a "boss" or a single "big nasty". Summoned monsters would have all of their normal abilities/weaknesses.

The event ends when the mob or all players are killed. Mobs would give XP and skill ups. Deaths would be real. Once the ticket is turned in, all participents (mobs and players) can not be targeted by anyone not in the fight. If you want/need buffs from those not involved in the fight, you must get them prior to the ticket turn in.

The mob (or mobs) will drop a total number of no-drop, lore, no rent tokens equal to the number of people in the group. i.e. a single group kills a single mob, 6 tokens drop.

You hand your token in to either the event master or a prize master and recive a class specific, no drop item. Items would be equivelent to mid to upper range loot for the PC level range.

Having seating around the arena for spectators would be a fun place to hang out and watch people do their thing.

Imagine going to the Arena in Lake Rathe and seeing an 18 man raid take down a dragon. Or a single group take down a swarm of what ever.

During patches, fill the arena with water and fight mermaids, fish, sea dragons, what ever... Add lava pits, platforms, bridges, traps whatever... People would come to try just to see whats new. Change the mosters summoned each patch. One time it's mobs from Hate, next time it's Plane of Growth or Kael, Western Wastes... Anywhere. Hell, even make the event win flags reset on a patch.

Since merchants can have 80 slots visible now, that's 80 different ticket types you could have.

Even allowing people to do it multiple times, it would give people something to do if they only have a few hours to play or they're tired of wandering around doing whatever.
