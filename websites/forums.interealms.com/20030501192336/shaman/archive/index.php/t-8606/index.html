Not new - just revamped:

Here is a post i made to my guild:

"Great news on test:

Shaman potions had their negative effects removed. This means that we now can make potions that might actually be useful at raids and even in groups.

Listing a few combos:

1) 25 sta, 25 CR
2) 25 str, 25 PR
3) 25 cha, 25 DR
4) 25 dex, 25 MR
5) 25 agi, 25 FR
6) 25 sta, 15 agi
7) 25 dex, 15 sta

Exept for the 25 to all resists, 25 sta will give our MTs approx 150 - 163 more max hp if they arent already maxed (maybe a reason to increase max stats to 305? )

All potions lasts for 30 mins, approx cost per combine is 18pp.

Many more (with str, cha agi etc) but i only listed the ones i found most useful.

Note: Shaman potions are stackable with everything (or should be) but not with eachother, so you cant stack potion nr 1 and potion nr 6 (both have sta), but you CAN stack potion nr 1 and potion nr 2 (no component the same).


Note that this hasnt gone live yet, but it was just changed on test - among with lots of other spells - so expect a patch soon. "
