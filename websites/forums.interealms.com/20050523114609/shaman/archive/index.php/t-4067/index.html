For Intelligence from 201 to 255:

(Level * 2) + ((Intel - 200) * Level * 0.1)

Then add the two numbers together (if over 200 INT).

The result for a level 60 caster with 255 intelligence is 2850. This is an approximate because I'm not 100% sure on the accuracy of the 0.1 modifier. It could very well be 0.11 or something dumb like that.

If you put on more than that it should still read 66%. Test with lower level characters, since the margin of error is so much smaller with the smaller mana pool.

This also leads me to believe that if they do a stat revisitation in PoP and allow players to go over 255 in a stat, it will increase your base mana pool and therefore increase your +mana item space.
