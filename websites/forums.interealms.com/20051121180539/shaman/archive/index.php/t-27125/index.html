for as long as i have played EQ, i have always tried to do my best to stay on top of various miscellaneous effects items that may be useful for me as a shaman. maybe i have always just been missing something, but this information has never been readily available in any sort of organized manner. about the closest you could get would be scrolling through enormous lists of items that included every kind of effect, useful or non-useful in existance.

for clarity's sake, an example for shadow knights would be holgresh elder beads. the effect is useful for the class, because they have a lot of beneficial spells that can only be cast via an npc. things like focus items, +effects, and class specific gear are somewhat obvious and easy enough to find.

i am more than willing to put together and host a page for this, but there is no way i can gather all the information on my own. however, if people posted items they are aware of or use themselves that are useful to the class, i'm sure it would end up being a pretty useful reference, and probably lead to most of us seeing a couple of ideas we never thought of before.
