Planes of Power seen from the eyes of an unguilded ranger.

When I first got to PoP I was amazed, I liked it. I got it a bit late so a lot of the initial info such as how to get access to Plane of Valor and Plane of Storms was already spoiled and I got my flags pretty quickly.

With access to PoV experience came quickly and it wasnt too long before I was level 65 and had all my 61+62+earthen root spells. Since I had been slacking in the AA department I figured I'd grind exp at bit more to get AM3 and such. That didnt take too long either, but made me sick of PoV.

I then fooled around a bit in pick up groups in PoI, PoD and PoN, but quickly found that the experience I got there was so laughable compared to PoV, so that fun didnt last very long.

I decided that to get my level 63 and 64 spells I would have to gain access to Halls of Honor, so I started working on the access quest, I knew it would take a while to complete so when opportunity presented itself I leeched a flag off my former guild's Aerin'Dar kill (with permission of course).
This was right after the Windblade nerf, but that didnt bother me too much, I just wanted my spells. I then found it insanely hard to find pickup groups there, due to the fact that so few(3 guilds at the time) people had access, and of course they mostly made their HoH groups in advance which left little room for me. I went there a few times with friends and once managed to get into a pickup group. I got two spectrals before the final nerf and havent seen one since. Add to that I wasnt exactly lucky and got Earthen root(again!!!!) and Nature's Rebuke. I rarely come there
anymore, it just doesnt seem worth it.

Next zone up was Plane of Storms, and I must say I have really enjoyed this zone too. The variety of mobs is nice and the loot aswell. I found out about the drops from the flying turkey thingies and finally found somewhere I could get upgrades to my equipment with just one group. I then proceeded to lose every random on several cloaks and one belt. Then they nerfed it, so bugger, too late...again.

I still like the zone and is currently working on my Bastion of Thunder key, though I doubt I'll get it, and if I do, I probably wont really have a use for it, since from what I hear you need more than one group to do anyhting in there.

At the same time I'm also working on my Crypt of Decay key and Plane of Tactics key using the quest methods, but from what I can tell it will be the same as Halls of Honor. Going there once in a while with friends but unable to get any sort of pickup group there.

That's enough babbling about my journey in Planes of Power with the conclusion being that if you arent guilded then PoP have little to offer in terms of long time enjoyment and I suspect that it wont be long before I get tired of the game. Possibly also has something to do with that fact that being a powergamer and unguilded doesnt mix very well.

Kind regards,

Jander
Unguilded Forest Stalker on Antonius Bayle
