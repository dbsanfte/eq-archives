Slowster
If you are willing to spend a little more like around $500.

Purchase a NVIDIA Quadro 900 XGL. It's has 128 meg of high speed DDR memory.

It does make a difference. But the biggest difference i received was when i upgrade from 1.2 ghz 500Meg 100mhz memory to 3.4Ghz 1gig 1ghz RDRAM. With that above card and maxed eq settings i had virtually zero lag in the bazaar. I think the biggest performance hits is how often the computer writes a swap file.

One thing to note about video cards is scalability. i.e performance gains with higher speed processors. With this card and an upgrade in proc speed, it's bench marks went up expotentially. I didnt perform the test myself but i saw a few test online and this was the best professional card on the market in 2002. There is a 980 model that is marginally faster but more expensive.

*note: Don't pay more for 8x agp card. There is a 1% or less increase in speed than a 4x and you'll pay alot for that 1%.

um hate to say it but your on crack. The quadro cards are virtually the exact same as normal FX cards but the drivers have been tweaked for highest quality 3d rendering at the cost of performance. When your doin CAD work or animation you need a perfectly accurate display more than pure performance - a missing line on a cad drawing can mean the difference between a correct part and catistrophic failure.. If you bios flash and install normal detonator drivers you will have a FX not a quadro, and see better performance. So basically you are paying more for less GAMING performance. But I bet it LOOKS great!
