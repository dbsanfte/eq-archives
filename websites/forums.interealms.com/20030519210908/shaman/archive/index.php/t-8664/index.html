Slargg
well, if you have read any of the posts, you should know that we are going to tell you to ditch your wis gear and get some ac/hp. So, here goes:

Some cheap upgrades are bs electrum ears, velium fire rings, go for barbed rubi bracers on both wrists or possibly a bracer of fortitude. Velium bs neck gives nice hp as well as some mana, crystal spider eyes, slime covered rubicite coif, quest or hardened grey flesh arms, shimmering terrorhide cloak, brown chitin or quest legs, barbed dragon booties.

You will find that most of these upgrades are actually pretty cheap. You will also keep a decent wis level, and get some raw mana while gaining some ac and a ton of much needed hp.

You dont need to switch our your bp, unless you can get a fungi. You need to switch your mindset when looking at gear.

I really dont see why people come here with the same wis profiles over and over and post that they aren't performing as well as they thing they should. There is a reason that we say go with hp/ac over wis and it is because it makes you a much better shaman.
