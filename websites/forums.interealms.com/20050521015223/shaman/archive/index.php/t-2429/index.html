I'll try to answer you questions.

The ones I am sure of:

2. Does extended enhancement affect heals over time like torpor or is there a min duration for buff spells?

Extended enchancement DOES NOT affect torpor but does affect other buff spells.

3. Can more than one focus item be activated at a time? Eg: can mana preservation combine with improved healing?

Yes. Different focus effects will stack but same focus effects won't. For example, mana preservation will combine with quick cast. But mana preservation 3 on one item will not combine afflication efficiency 3 on another item.

4. Do Spell Casting Reinforcement and SCRMastery affect heals over time like torpor.

Spell Casting Reinforcement DOES affect torpor, but there is some argument as too how much skill you need to see a difference in amount of torpor ticks. I for one saw an extra tick with Spell Cast Reinforcement 1.

5. Do SCR and SCRM stack with Extended Enhancement focus items.

Yes it does. But since extended enchancement does not work for torpor, it does not help amount of torpor time

6. Does Quick Buff reduce the casting time of heals over time like torpor.

Yes it does. If you get Quick Buff 3, torpor will be 3 second cast and other "normal" buffs will be half time.

As for question 1:

1. Does improved healing affect heals over time like torpor or just instant cast heals?

I am not sure on this but I do not think heals over time are effected.
Sciek Spellbottler
1. Does improved healing affect heals over time like torpor or just instant cast heals?
--- Just instant cast heals.

2. Does extended enhancement affect heals over time like torpor or is there a min duration for buff spells?
--- Does not affect Torpor. Min. duration 48.0 seconds.
source: http://lucy.fnord.net

3. Can more than one focus item be activated at a time? Eg: can mana preservation combine with improved healing?
--- Yes, different focus effects do stack. However two different effects that do the same thing will not. i.e. spell haste is 10% haste, affliction haste is 33% haste to DoTs. Affliction haste will take precedence and spell haste will do nothing. Think of it just like item haste for a melee character when multiple items can affect a spell. ;)

On AA and focus:

4. Do Spell Casting Reinforcement and SCRMastery affect heals over time like torpor.
--- Yes, see above post.

5. Do SCR and SCRM stack with Extended Enhancement focus items.
--- Yes... Spell haste and quick buff also stack to a point. Apparently, spell haste is capped at 50% however and once you have quick buff3, spell haste will do nothing additional for you when buffing. Not sure if the same applies for duration increase though.

6. Does Quick Buff reduce the casting time of heals over time like torpor.
--- Yes.
