Developer's Corner: Focus Effects after level 65 (http://eqlive.station.sony.com/community/dev_view.jsp?id=63311) -- "All existing foci are having their effects changed to scale down at 10% effectiveness per level past their previously explicit level cap."

That means focus III items will work on spells up to level 69 now, but at reduced effectiveness per spell level above 60. It's not a secret, to the developers anyway.
