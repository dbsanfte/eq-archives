Zorlac Sabboth
There are two places where Courier can spawn, the real stables or in the area opposite to stables. The area opposite to stables has much lower level mobs so you can pull em either solo or 2. In every of the 4 rooms there, only 2 spawn points exist, no roamers. You should be able to handle 2 of those spawns, once slowed they are very easy.

I wouldn't suggest trying the real stables, they are a PITA for your level. Hell at 60 when PLing toons there, even I pulled more than one. You don't have lot of space to move so you will get aggroed.

In the area opposite to stables, pathing is very very very very bad, never fight inside. Always pull to alley. If you fight inside and you move one inch, mob will path all around the area, aggroing 2-3 rooms.

Barm McLir
There is a second, and I feel easier, spawn point for the spectral courier. If you are facing into the stables, do a 180 and you'll be looking into a small courtyard with a door in the far left corner. Inside are four spawns. Move slowly and stay away from the right side of the courtyard or you'll get adds. Once you get inside, the spawn deepest in from the door is the placeholder for the courier (marked with a red star if this map works). This is where I got him solo at 51.
