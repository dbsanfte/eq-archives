Azmodai
After 3 initial attemps, I finally defeated Yeldema solo! No outside buffs or help was used other than rezzing my sorry ass. Big thank you to Zydane, Maraya, Isha/Ayisha, and Bushiin/Celetss on TM...thank you soooo much!!

Heres some shots of the fight. Unfortunately the final pic of me standing over her didn't take as I was out of space on my HD. :P

For those wishing to try thier hand at this feat, here's what I know. The main problem that I encountered was dealing with the adds. On the third fight, Cargalia came by and pasted me with a 1300 pt grav flux. :P Amcilla and Cargalia are the only two dragons that path by there and they only do so once an hour. The way I finally dealt with it was to pull Yeldema straight west off the cliff, where dogdog was waiting in the water and fight her there.

Pull with Malo. Then Slow, Cripple, Insideous Decay, Pox, Epic, JBB (ad infinitum). Rinse, repeat. After the first set of spells landing, I had about 60% mana left, and then reapplied Slow 1:30 in to the fight and then Pox once more. The rest of the mana was saved for Torpor. The big lifesavers were the mana free epic and JBB and dogdog (w/ Focus).

Yeldema quads for 219 max, but her average hit was about 177, and frequently hit me for 56-100. She rampages, but only rampaged twice in that fight, and each time the hits were all low (50s) or missed. She has 24k hps, and is a warrior class. Doesn't gate, or cast any AE, but she does summon, which was fun when pulling. :P

I only had 1056 AC fully buffed and 2249 hps. Thats not alot. :/ I will need to work on that. I had to Torpor myself at about 1k hps in order to get it off in time. If I waited any longer, then I ran the risk of any interruptions killing me before Torpor took effect.

Anway, she dropped her Talisman, 5 AC, 4 to all stats. Dragon meat (gave it to a guildie (cuz I always eat the damn food) for staying up so late with me and porting Maraya in from Seb to rez me), Shroud of Death (55 SHD spell), Call of Fire (55 RNG spell).

This was SO much fun! The challenge itself was worth the deaths. Now to try a different dragon =D
