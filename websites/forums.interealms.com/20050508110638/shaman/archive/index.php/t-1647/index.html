Gardeltron
Potion: Absor's Revenge
Effect: 1000hp, 200ac, 50sta, 50agi, 15wis, regen 15hp/tick, regen 7mana/tick
Duration: 2.75 hours

Components: Hoodad stems, Hoodad leaves, Essence of hoodad

Hoodad stems are a rare, no drop, no rent item foraged in Stonebrunt Mountains

Hoodad leaves are a stackable lore item bought from Ancient Hoodad for 513pp (with cha of about 150). Ancient Hoodad is a triggered spawn in the basement of Ssra temple. He is triggered by telling the Avatar of War: "Gimmah j00 phat hoodad lewtz." He despawns 30 minutes after he is spawned.

Essence of hoodad is a lore, no drop, ultra-rare drop off of the Enraged Hoodad in Vex Thal.

Enraged Hoodad
Level: 80
Spawn interval: Random, but a minimum of 7 days
HP (approx): 1,000K
Magic Attack: AoE cast every tick that interupts all spells not cast from a clickable item.
Melee Attack: Death touch each tick on the highest pc on the hate list. Iksars are immune to this attack.
Immunities: Immune to melee damage and all spell damage except for cold.
Damage cap: Maximum damage per PC attack of 263 hp. If an attack exceeds this cap it is resisted.

So, Enraged Hoodad can only be damaged by clickable items that hit for 263 DD cold damage or less. These items must be in posession of Iksars if the fight is to last very long.

Enjoy!
