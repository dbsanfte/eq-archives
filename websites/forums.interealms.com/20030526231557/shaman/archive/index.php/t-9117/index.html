Today my guild got our first glyphed rune word and we decided to turn in for shaman spell.

I got Ferine Avatar. I swear I almost cried in RL, the last of my choices for a level 65 spell. I felt wretched that we could have tried to turn in for hand of virtue or something else, but we blew it on avatar. I almost never use regular avatar, I just don't have the slots to keep it memmed often and it is far too costly to use in exp grps. Though, as a guild with 0 primal weapons perhaps I should have been using it more.

Anyways, I came here with a bit of good news, despite the fact I was unhappy to get the spell.

Ferine Avatar only has a 1 minute recast timer!!
It also requires no emerald.

This really makes it leaps and bounds more useable than all the previous versions, in my opinion.

That is a great spell, you can keep it on 8 or 9 melee at all times with good timing and casting reinforcement aa and item.

In a raid situation you would probably have to assign 1 shaman to this as his main duty.

Someone in FoH discovered that there is an attack cap from items and the primal procing avatar is limited also by this cap. I believe some of the ubers out there are already limited by this cap, so casting the avatar line is useful to them as castable ava isn’t limited.
Also they don’t have to swap out primal to get the effect.


PS Casting avatar every min on 9 pre assigned melee is difficult, as you need to keep a list. What I did was create a small channel window, called it avatar list, and created a hotkey that tells me who I cast Ava on last

/cast 5 (Slot 5 is where I keep my Ava spell)
/3 %t has been Ava'd ( 3 is Ava channel name)
