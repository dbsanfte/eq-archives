- As a shaman there is little you need VS faction for apart from banking on Kunark or buying 50+ vendor spells if you are Ogre or Troll which you could get a friend / alt / mule to do for you in OT or FV. The only other mildly useful item you can get with VS faction is the hammer that procs OT gate. Once you have the hammer you can go kos and dodge/outrun the mobs on your way out.

- Some of the lower level luclin spells are on vendors, rest are drops. You don't need Luclin to scribe them and can send someone to buy the vendor bought spells for you. You'd be lucky to buy Lev and especially the EB - they are none too common drops.

- Goodness, lots has changed. Targetgroupbuff is probably the most useful thing for us. Log on and type /tgb on, just once, and you'll be able to cast group buffs on groups other than yours.
