Nytmare
Dispells, like cures, work with counters. Cast a 6-counter dispell against a mob's 9-counter buff, and you'll need 2 casts to get rid of the mob's buff. Same when mobs cast on you. That is why a single dispell may not remove any buffs. Higher dispells have more sets of counters, giving them not only a chance to rid a buff on the first cast, but rid 2 or 3 buffs.

I do not know why oftentimes a middle buff gets dispelled instead of the first -- I hate that.

Dispell does not remove slow! You can cast slow on a mob, then do dispelling afterwards, and slow will remain in effect. Dispell does remove debuffs like Malosi and Root though.
