This had been building for a while, but after reading a post someone addressed to me with the word "wut" in it four times (/wince) enough is enough. It wasn't on this board though, thank Tunare. Folks here seem to care a lot more about how they form their words.

For everyone else...

LEARN HOW TO SPELL!

It is not wut, it's what.

It is not ur, it's your (or you're).

It is not u, it's you.

It is not wru, it's where are you.

It is not thx (still trying to figure out how to pronounce this...the closest I've gotten is "thex"), it's thanks.

It's not r, it's are (or our).

It's not y, it's why.

It's not ne1, it's anyone.

It's not nething, it's anything.

Roleplaying is one thing (Me gunna barsh you gud!!) but plain ignorance is another :(

Thank you fellow rangers (and other Glade visitors) for being so easy to read...it truly makes this a better place to visit. To everyone else...quit being so lazy and type it out. It may seem a chore, but so many more people will take you seriously after you begin writing in a way folks can understand. You will also no longer come across as a child.

I know folks whose primary language is not english, yet they type better than some of my fellow english-speakers. When they don't, their errors are grammatical or structural, not these kinds of typos. At least they try.
