Gloobonious
A couple of cheap suggestions...

Arms: Upgrade them to JB. RE Arms aren't that great, except for the MR. Else, you're better off with the AC of JB armor.

Leggings: The lesser Dragon Leggings from WW are cheap, and offer good AC.

Bracer: If you're wearing that for the effect, I suggest doing the Coldain Military Wristguard quest. You get an EA III item, with much better statistics overall.

Cloak: For a few KPP, you could probably pick up an Ancient Seahorse Cloak. AC20, +25 HP... a very nice upgrade.

Boots: Get some Tribal War Boots. Better AC and statistics. You can click your Black Fur Boots from the inventory, like JBoots, so no worries here.
