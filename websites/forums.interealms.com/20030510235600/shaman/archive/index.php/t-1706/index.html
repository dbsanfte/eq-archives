Kardyss
Paludal is the definite spot for your early 20s. I got from 19 - 24 there. If you solo, you can fight the sensi mushroom that is near the exit to shadow haven. It gives you like a blue and a half for quite a bit. You can also get into a group and fight the bandits on that level.

For your mid and upper 20s, take the trip to South Karana and go to the Aviak village (known as KFC). start with the egrets, then darters (note, darters can outrun a SOW). Next fight the rooks and harriers. I wouldnt fight an avocet unless you are in the high 20s and you are buffed.

Good hunting.

Andrewe
Marus Seru is extremely fast experience for the mid-high 20s. It's an outdoor zone, yes, but it seems to have a quality ZEM. Start with rockhoppers and progress to Stonegrabbers.

Netherbian Lair is even faster exp, and you can start in a group as soon as you leave Paludal. I found the mobs regen made them out of my reach to kill solo until I reached 24, though. (I could finish one off and end with low HP and OOM, whereas duoing with a melee they were almost trivial.) Pre-24 if you put two DOTs on a fungoid or trog, their HP actually still rises a bit per tick.
