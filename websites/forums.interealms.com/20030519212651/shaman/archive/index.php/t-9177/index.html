taraddar
For haste the best you can do is a 50% reduction. Past that it doesn't do any good. So if you have QB3 and an enhancement haste 4 item you still end up with a 3 second torpor. Standard enhancement haste items won't work with Torpor tho. The speed of mithaniel and some of the VP enhcancement haste items do worth with it tho. The standard spell haste 4 items do work also.

On the buff reinforcement side of things there doesn't seem to be a limit. scrm + 8th shawl = 70% longer buffs. Once again note that most focus items don't work with torpor. There is one crown from VT that does tho.
