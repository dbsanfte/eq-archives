Biggest original change was the exp penalty reduction on death. But that's been around so long the majority of current players would never know what it was like.

I do think the biggest change came with Kunark, and equipment. Kunark brought new equipment that was so vastly superior to anything in the old world that it changed the game entirely. When you had meleers who went from the best weapons being 1:3 ratios of 7/21 or 8/24 to ratios of 1:2 or better on weapons that were trivial to obtain, it did two things. One it gave a huge burst of melee power, and two it raised expectations far too high. People expected, no demanded, that they get similiar increases in power every step of the way. It raised the bar too high in one fell swoop, and VI had no choice but to keep raising it. Now people simply toss aside weapons that are not better than a 1:2 ratio, they are deemed unworthy of lvl 1 twinks.

For me anyway it was a sign that VI didn't care about the game, they were going to simply run with MUDflation since EQ only had a few years worth of real viability, might as well entice people as much as possible for the short term.
