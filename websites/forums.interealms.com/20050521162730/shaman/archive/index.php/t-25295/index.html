The happy fun extra xp day is quite nice, I've gained 1.75 levels so far, plan to grind till the sun comes up tonight and see if I can get one more level.

So far I've been fairly happy with this. In EQ1 we were mostly told "tough". Well, this time, every chat room was moderated and moderators were trying to let people know what was going on. And now to get 3 free days, and 2 days of happy fun extra xp - well, it's very nice way to make up. Because free days mean little to me- the time I didn't get to play is worth more than the cost of my sub. But extra xp, means I can accomplish over these 2 days what I didn't get done during that... well not questwise, but xp and levelwise and tradeskill wise.

I think they did a good job this time. I'm usually all for Sony bashing but I've been fairly happy with the way they handled this crisis.
