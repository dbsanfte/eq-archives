I think this observation maybe key in the discussion. I don't necessarily think the player base as a whole has decreased in maturity. It is more that veteran players have come (or perhaps had come maybe more appropriate) to expect a certain level of civility and levelheadedness for the higher-level player base that simply isn't extant. Prior to "the great experience balancing of 2002" trudging your way to 60 to a very very long time. Players who made it there displayed true determination and dedication to their characters and their behavior tended to reflect that. Now the old exp barriers in the 30s and 40s no longer exist and thus no longer deter the ph4t l33t twitch game playing 14 year olds from breaking into the 50s and 60s. With access to higher level zones these folks have more money and more loot to throw and begin acting in a more and more repulsive fashion. I guess this just isn't our old EQ anymore...
