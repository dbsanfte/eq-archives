I'd go beastlord, druid, paladin or beastlord, druid, necro

I'm assuming you need a healer, and druids can both cut the mustard healing and add great dps, plus have utility of snare. Shaman/Cleric are other options, neither have the same dps potential plus I prefer Bo9 to focus or HoV.

Beastlord replaces shaman as slower+buffer, with their paragon, great pet, SP and some tanking ability its a winner. Chanter would be close second but too reliant on suitable charmable pets. Pre charm nerfs would have gone chanter for sure.

Paladin for tanking (or SK but I like the pally for rez, stun, hp buff and emergency healing). DPS sucks but for hard LDoNs or high level PoP their (knight/warrior) tanking ability would be essential.

Necro for more dps, great utility and fun. This assumes you don't need a traditional tank (either the two pets and maybe bst tank or some form of kiting (snare and either aggro or fear kite). In areas like easy LDoNs the Bst + pets can tank just fine. Hard LDoN/high level PoP zones would be a problem.
