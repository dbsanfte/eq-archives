Jaruden
Calculations look about right (assuming those numbers), but as mentioned, the real bonus is in the kill rate.

Before this patch, it was more like: 10 pts for soloing, 2 for a full group. So that full group had to kill 5 times as many mobs to beat a soloer. As for PoP mobs, I find it often takes me 5-6 minutes to kill a mob, and most groups can kill a mob a minute. So it was about even. But then you factor in deaths, afk's, LD's from the whole party, and their rate over an hour is lower than yours going steady (you have full control over yourself, except maybe LD).

Now groups only need to kill 3x as fast. They can probably still kill 5-6x faster than you, so they're going to be getting around 16 pts for your 10. Take into account ld's, afk's, deaths, etc, and they're probably down to 12-13, which still beats your rate.

That's the aim of this patch, to make the amount of exp gained by a group out-do that of a single person, due to the rate at which they can kill mobs.
