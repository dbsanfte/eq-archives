Jinheim
Play an ogre, without doubt. I have a beastlord alt, who is an ogre, and he's really awesome. Your most important stats are stamina, str, and wis in no particular order. Ogre has the highest strength and stamina by far of any beastlord race, and decent wis as well (compared to other bst races). Your easiest stats to raise will be strength and stamina. I capped both of them on my beastlord with only medium twink gear. Even if you aren't twinking at all, you will have absolutely no trouble capping str and stamina later on in your life, especially if you are always with a shaman. However, you will have a much harder time capping your wis as a beastlord. I would advise putting all of your points in to that.

Some people will say to put 5 in to agi, and here's why: If you have under 75 agi, there is a pretty severe AC penalty. Ogres start with 70 agi, so they will have a bad AC vs. other races. However, you should have no trouble at all finding 5 agi somewhere from gear bonuses. If you are starting completely from scratch, it might be worth it to put +5 agi and +15wis, but if you are doing any twinking at all, the +agi wont be necessary.

Good luck, beastlords are a really fun class.
