gummox
If you're getting to the white bone things, then you're starting in the right place. I only found one spot on the zone wall to climb high enough to even try. I tried using SoCheeta several times and almost got inside every time too. If you're not 52 yet (I was 50 at the time) you can use Scale of Wolf. It worked for me twice.

The walkthroughs say you've got enough time to summon and buff Doggy after you give Atheling the skull, but be warned: Doval WILL despawn if you don't engage fast enough. If you feel the need to kill Atheling after Doval, Just sic Doggy on him and keep Doggy healed unless you want to be teleported, which leads me to my last tip:

The 4 Sarnak around the table in the area you get teleported into are dark blue but single pullable at lvl 50 with Nullify Magic (most are shaman type mobs and all are heavily buffed before you get there) unless there's a named one (Somethingorother Klyseer).
Naagruz
To the right of the castle wall, back about 100 meters or so, is a good hill to launch from. It does take quite a bit of practice to get in there so keep trying. You can do it with SoS on or SoC pretty easy once you figure it out.

The best way to fight Doval is to fight on the edge farthest from Atheling. That way you avoid getting TP'd into Chardok. Nuke big too because Doval is a wuss.
Shjekai
If you can tone the graphics down a bit, for example drop your clip plan down to nothing. Graphics lag can hinder your movement.
Gormadoc
Also, make sure you are looking straight up the majority of the time!!! If you are looking straight on you'll always drop. With looking up I managed to get into the tower with standard SoW. Hit the home key after a little bit just to make sure you aren't walking through the tower (like 10 or so seconds) but then immediately look back all the way up. Only took me two tries to get it.. also you need to go back a little further until you can go up the mountain quite a bit.. that's your 'fly off' point.. ;)

Nytmare
Video lag causes gravity while levitated, so you can reduce your rate of descent by futzing with your video settings (or buying a faster PC). Lower the clipping plane, lower the screen resolution, use old models, turn off 3D sky and other video effects, etc.
