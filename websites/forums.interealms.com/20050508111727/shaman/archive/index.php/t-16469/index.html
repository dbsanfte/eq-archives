Jahvah
Got the expansion, absolutely love the adventures, but I do have one gripe. This is more of a lowbie issue, so many of you probably will never have to deal with it.

At level 32 I've completed 3 of four adventures, and had a blast doing so. Loot is okay, being mostly sellable swords and such on the level with fine steel.

My gripe is this...for completing a successful adventure at level 32 I get 2 points. I understand that balancing rewards for your level is important, but when you look at the cost of certain items, the reward stinks.

Let's take a look at the lowest level of spells: Lesser Augury, cleansing, and exorcism. These are level 14 spells, they cost 25 points each. So, if the average mission takes about an hour (not counting the other hour of trying to gather a group) and you get two points for successful completion at level 32, you're talking about spending 13 hours of straight LDoN adventures to gather the points for a level 14 spell.

I love the dungeons, the whole concept is awesome, but they need to rethink the cost of these lower level spells. I'm not saying at level 32 I should be getting more points. That would be unbalancing. My point is the low level spell point prices need to come down. At this rate I should be able to get spirit of the shrew sometime next year.

Let's say a person is level 34 and just bought LDON so they are doing adventures for their first time. They see our curse spell that is 36 points and decide that is going to be their first goal. The next adventure they succeed at will also give them 3 points and they will most likely be 35 at this time. So now they have 6 points, 30 points remaining and they've already lost one level of usefulness to the curse spell. Now they continue adventuring gaining 3 points and half a level for 5 levels. Now they are most likely level 40 with around 36-40 points. That is excluding any failures they may have had which would still possibly yield as much experience without as many points collected.

So, do they spend all of the points they have earned so far on one spell they should have gotten 6 levels ago? Or....do they keep saving for a spell that they will be able to use in 4 levels....but wait, they have been saving up points at Northern Ro camp and they now have to start all over again at Southern Ro to unlock the 72 point level 44 spell.

I guess you can see that I agree with Jahvah and that the points should not be on an exponential curve as they are.
