Aye Tweadle nailed the basic mystery of the JBB, in that when you hit 51 you get the best slow and then the mob will really only have one chance to hit you during that 8 sec cast time.

At 60, most xp mobs are slowed enough so that they *barely* get that chance to hit in. I have my ac as high as it can get and many times they dont even hit me so channeling isnt a problem.

However, even if I do get hit or bashed I still channel the JBB well over 90% of the time Id say. On the other hand, the epic is much harder to channel for some reason, even though its also a clicky.

Keep in mind though that channeling clickies does not increase your channeling skill.
