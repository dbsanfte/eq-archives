Solariis
If you are like me and prefer dungeons to outdoors you'll love this spot. Your hair will be tingling at all times cause if you die it's not going to be a fun CR :) I made approximately 2 blue bubs an hour there at 60. In fact I finished out my last yellow bub there and wish I had done this earlier. Don't waste time at ZI/ZO the XP is crap there. This spot is the upper tunnel near ZO.

Some facts about this spot:

1. Pretty sure you need to be 60 to single pull mobs here.

2. Two BoNs + alpha pet is all you need to kill any mob in the area. Mobs are totally gimp on resists so slows and dots land without any need of a malo/malosini.

3. Have your resist gear on, forget about AC since 50% of the mobs are wizzy/necro types. Slap on talisman of epuration for PR and DR and use your resist magic/fire/cold spells. With those and resist gear I'm 120+ on all resists and most spells bounced right off me even from the dark blues. When they did land I barely got scratched.

4. Mobs are on 26 minute timer giving you plenty of time to make your way down tunnel as far as you like to go. Hell I went so far down I don't even know where I was at.

5. Loot is about 3pp per mob with possibility of 1-2 gems per trip to help offset the cost of a KEI. Tribal war mask and ivory imubed collar dropped also.

The spot is the upper tunnel near the ZO. You need to have levitate on to reach the tunnel. In order to get up there you head a little ways up to ZO and then turn around. You should be facing the gatehouse. Now hug the left wall and run real fast and you should reach the upper tunnel. There is one spawn there to greet you so have pet memmed. Root and slow it and summon pet.
At this point in the tunnel mobs are about 50% light blue and dark blue but as you make your way down they all become dark blue and very nice XP.

Spiritstone is really handy here. I just bind outside and when KEI runs out I port to EF then take book to Pok/nexus and get KEI and gate back.

Have fun here and don't die :)

I dont suggest going anywhere much past that though as at 60 you wont be able to single anything, actually pulling 3-4 in some spots, so be careful.

Whats great about Chardok though is that you can have a great time solo/duo/trio, get some loot, hone skills...then you gotta fight your way back OUT :)

weissblau
My favorite spot in Chardok - but it's a bit risky getting there:

Run through the fort to the first bridge, ignoring any aggro.

Jump off the bridge.

CAMP immediately to clear aggro.

Any mobs aggroed from the fort area will take about a minute to get down to you under the bridge.

Under the bridge is a safe room with some slaves in it. You can easily pull the captain from there. I was doing this at 58 with a partner and making great XP and good drops.

At level 60 getting down to the skeletons in the mines is the trickiest part. Once you get down there you'll get mostly double pulls which are easily root parked, or if you're thinking ahead and know which pairs are where, you'll root one to pull the other. ;)

The tricky part is when you get down past the BK turnoff in the "lev tunnel" The first big room you come to as you clear down that tunnel will have 2-3 mobs in sight and I've always seen 3 aggro together on the first pull. At 60 it IS possible, but make sure you're FM when you get to that first room and be ready to root park stuff quickly. Recommend pulling by rooting one right off the bat, back up and root another asap, then slow and sick pet on the last one.

Just a heads up... I've recommended the lower tunnel for xp as well as the skellies to a few people and several complained that they got stomped by the 3 pull halfway down heh.
