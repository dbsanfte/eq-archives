Jinheim
Avatar is 140atk, and melee classes get I think a 1.35x multiplier on atk (feel free to correct me on this, I'm not sure). If it is 1.35x, that brings FA to about 190atk. I remember reading (on the ranger boards I think) that 10atk yields approximately a 1% increase in melee damage. Thus, FA comes out to about a 19% increase in melee output (I'm pretty sure this guess is significantly more than what it really is). Keep in mind that all classes do a significant chunk of their damage via spells and procs. Even monks and rogues do damage via procs, which FA doesn't help (assume 305 dex without FA, which is easy to achieve). Considering that, FA is a significant damage increaser, but it isn't monumental.

Now, compare it to haste. Lets assume a rogue with no haste item and no capped haste does 100 dps. Add in a 41% haste item and they now do 141 dps. Give them enchanter haste (not considering the 41 atk from VQ) and they do 205dps. Haste, in this situation, gives a 45% increase in damage. Haste is slightly less significant for hybrids because it doesn't increase the rate at which they can nuke/dot, but it does increase proc rate, since they are swinging more.

What about ranger buffs? Strength of tunare is 60atk, and Sprit of the Predator is 70k atk. That's 130 base atk, only slightly less than FA. It will yield about an 18% increase in melee damage.

SCRM and EE4 aren't hard to obtain, so we'll assume that everyone has it. Ferine Avatar lasts 6.5 minutes base, but with SCRM and EE4 is will last 11 minutes. VQ lasts 42 minutes base, but is easily extended to 70 minutes - that is over an hour for the best melee damage increasing spell in the game. SOTP is 80 minutes base and SOT is 60 minutes base, but rangers don't get SCRM so the longest they can extend it is 116 and 87 minutes

Lets recap:

Enchanters: 45% increase to raw melee AND proc damage for everyone in the group/raid, buff lasts 70 minutes. The buff is MGB'd before the fight starts and no maintainence is required.

Rangers: 18% increase to raw melee for everyone in the group/raid, buff lasts 87 minutes. The buff is MGB'd before the fight starts and no maintainence is required.

Shamans: 19% increase to raw melee, buff lasts 11 minutes. Theoretically you could do 11 people per shaman, but realistically its only 3 or 4. AND it is a huge PITA as well as a noticeable drain on our mana.

edit: Beastlords get ferocity, a 150atk buff with the same duration as FA, but a 2 minute recast. They don't get SCRM so It is about a 9 minute duration at most. This is slightly more atk than FA, but a bigger PITA to sustain. On top of that, most beastlords fero themselves which sort of wastes it since they get much less return from it than a rogue or monk would.

This is one of the few things that out-right pisses me off about our class. When I made and leveled Jinheim, I viewed shaman as a battle-priest. Our role was to get in the thick of things and support the melees. We keep them alive and enhance their damage. Imho we do a decent job of keeping them alive, but we certainly aren't the best at it. What about enhancing their damage? We're #3, behind chanters, and rangers*. It is my firm belief that we should be #1, or at least offer them something that no other class does.

* - For this rant I pretend that bards don't exist. For the record, though, they give their group 30% overhaste, 60% normal haste, and can generate god only knows how much atk. Warsong of Zek gives 18 base atk, but I have no idea how to calculate their real bonus with all their instrument and AA modifiers. I know it is a lot. That's just for the melees. Casters get 21 mana per tick and 15% improved damage (nukes AND dots) if they are in the bard's group.

I see two fixes to this, one is reasonable and the other is just a pipe dream of mine (bonus points if you can guess which is which!).

1) Switch haste lines with chanters. Chanters are getting the best persistant (i.e. not bard song) caster buffs and the best persistant melee buffs. We get no persistant melee damage buffs (except bih'li!), just FA which is inferior to ranger buffs and a much bigger pain than bard song. We have nothing to offer casters.

2) Improve Ferine Avatar. Two easy ways to do this:

a) Make it a group spell with a duration on par with that of VQ. This will be a significant increase to a raid/group's DPS, but I don't feel it would be overpowering. Basically, it would be just a little more than ranger buffs, since not everyone gets SOT, but everyone gets FA (and its 10 more atk).

and/or

b) Remove strength, dex, and agility from avatar. Replace with combat effects, accuracy, avoidance, and strikethrough. I will admit that although I know what these effects are supposed to do, I have no idea how much of an effect they actually have, and I have no idea what sort of numbers would be appropriate. All I know is that it would give us something to offer that no other class has, which would make me a happy shaman. It would also make us a lot more desireable in groups because I can promise you most non-time geared melees will get a hard-on the first time they see "You strike through your opponents defenses!" and insist on having a shaman in all groups after that.
