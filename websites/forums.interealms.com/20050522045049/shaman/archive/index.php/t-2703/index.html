Looking for a consistent stream of low dark blues to get your Defense skill up at 51? I know it's not considered cool, but Dreadlands is a great place for you. Slow a dark blue gorilla, dvorlag, or chicken... they don't hit that hard, they have low HP, and there are zones to run to if you get in trouble. Of course, make sure you have your AGI and DEX buffed up (think AGI is the one used for defensive skill gains, but why not be safe and have 'em both buffed.. especially since HoS includes the DEX buff.)

By the BW zone is a nice pullspot and normally not too crowded. If DL is too crowded, just zone over and try the same thing with hornets in BW !! :)

Good hunting.

Nektar
I only really started slowing till around 45 or so, and till then my defense was poopie. It goes up quick though, and you can raise it several ways.

a) Just tank/solo by having the mob slowed, yourself buffed with hps, ac and regen.

b) Pick anything that's light blue, doesn't hit hard and use him as a sparring partner. I've done this in kc at level 56 to raise my piercing with a drolvarg sentry :) Even had a bard at one point help me out and play cantana for me

b) Group up in a fast pace dungeon. With lots of adds, you'll have to slow, and slow = big aggro. Provided you don't die, all the aggro generated from slow should raise your defense pretty quickly. Anyways, at 51, I'd recommend grouping in kc for that. I started in Sebilis at level 53, and I honestly think you'd have a hard time trying to find a group there at 51.
