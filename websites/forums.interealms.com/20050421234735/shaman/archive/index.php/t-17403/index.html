I've done that boat a few times. I've never found a safe spot, but if you go up the ramp and then to the other side of boat, theres some ropes and boxes that I set up camp at. Eisgeist
When you go up the ramp go straight to the other side of the ship. Then go about half way up to the front of the ship. Stay tight against the low wall and face boat number 3. On your left should be a mob standing next to the ships mast and 2 mobs on you right where the front of the ship slopes up. Staying against the wall pick the middle distance between the mob by the mast and the closest of the two by the front of the ship.

I may have just been lucky but I was able to bring up doggy and buff there. I took about two steps towards the front of the ship and that mob came after me so you don't have much room for error. After that mob I killed the other one at the front of the ship to give me some room and then continued with the one by the mast.

Good luck.
