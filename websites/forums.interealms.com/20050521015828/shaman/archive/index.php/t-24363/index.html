Sithrax
(Disclaimer: I know we have balance issues remaining. However, given SOE's general reluctance to address them, I'd like to ask for some changes to make the game less tedious in the meantime.)

Has anyone else felt like a @#%$% buff machine lately? I swear, I'm already longing for the days of PoP, when I could just MGB Focus, throw out a few STA/CHA buffs here and there, and start raiding. Sure, we had to do Trib once in a while, but it's not like every mob had a PR/DR/prismatic/chromatic AE. Oh my, how times have changed. Now I'm piling on the buffs like a madman. My chat windows are lit up with tell spam asking for this buff or that buff. I'm alternating between buffs and shots from a bottle of JD I've been keeping handy for raid times. Raids pass a lot quicker when you're unconscious after doing 20 shots in the first 30 minutes.

Don't get me wrong. I applaud SOE's efforts to make our buffs more relevant (even though the actual effect hasn't been proven by parse yet, I'm going to go and assume that they actually work). I was kind of hoping they would improve our existing lines, rather than unleash the buffing nightmare that we have right now. Look at what we have to keep running on 54-72 people:

1) Focus for everyone.
2) Spirit/Talisman of Fortitude for everyone.
3) Spirit/Talisman of Sense for everyone.
4) Tribunal for everyone (happens a lot with newer prismatic/chromatic AEs.)
5) Spirit/Talisman of Might for all melees.
6) CHA for warriors, bards, clerics.
7) Ferine Avatar/Champion (let's just not even go there).

That's a whole damn lot of buffs to keep active on a raid. Does any other class have to deal with this kind of horror in terms of keeping buffs active? I mean, when I die I just send a tell to a druid for 9/Steeloak and a cleric for Symbol, and I'm good to go. When someone dies and sends me a buff request, they usually say "Buffs please", because it would take 15 minutes to type out all the stuff they need.

Please - for the sanity of the 6 remaining shaman who still play EQ and raid, show us some mercy. Here's what I would wish for:

1) Combine Focus and Spirit of Might.
2) Combine Spirit of Sense and Spirit of Fortitude.
3) Give FA/Champion a base duration of 30 minutes.

This way, we have one offense buff, one defense buff, and one special buff that only a few get. I'd be happy if SOE would just do this and give us nothing in return - but if they feel compelled to fill up those spell slots, here are a few more things to make life less tedious:

-Replace Spirit of Sense with a group 60% haste with the same base duration as Talisman of Alacrity

-Replace Talisman of Sense with our new bearform! Now with a sexy new illusion!

-Replace Spirit of Might with something or other. New group SoW, perhaps.

-Replace Talisman of Might with our new Malx upgrade, Malolololo (or whatever). Give it a -70 to ALL resists, 3 sec cast time, 2.25 sec recast time, 425 mana.

-Spirit/Talisman of Might would be our new combined STA/aviodance buff. Call it Spirit/Talisman of the Defender.

-Wunshi/Talisman of Wunshi would be the same, just add the 5% damage modifier in there.

These changes would make a lot of shaman happy, I think. Yet, they wouldn't address the fundamental balance issues that seem to make SOE so nervous (healing, DPS, slow mitigation). Nothing original or scary here! What they would do is help preserve shaman sanity. Everyone wins - after all, people in mental hospitals don't pay monthly account fees.


P.S. If SOE is unwilling to implement these changes, I hope they will at least put in a new /buffbot command, kind of like the new /melody for bards. This would allow us to put one shaman in this mode and have him autobuff everyone who sends in a tell requesting one of our 47 buffs.
