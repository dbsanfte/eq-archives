Hail,

Well, I did it. I was extremely careful, and found some things out I think are important. It took me several run throughs of the text because:
* I left my stuff in the bank & didn't realize until turn-in time. (/smack self)
* I aggroed an undead vassal taking a shortcut and had to zone.
* I died because someone left a crystalline golem ON the bridge between the bridgehouses & I wasn't ready or expecting that. (/smack self again)
* You can't zone in between any of the steps as far as I'm aware.
* Several of the publicly available spoilers are missing small pieces of information. Pay attention to all the text. The Allakhazam's spoiler I printed awhile back (which wasn't on their site today) and the Safehouse spoiler I printed again beforehand today were missing small parts of the text.

I'm not promising this is correct, but this is what I did:
The tents are located at loc neg 950 neg 500 roughly. Sometimes hard to see w/ clipping lowered until you get close.

Hail Johon Popoah twice, until he gives you a line of text with "compound" in brackets.
Say, "what compound".
Hail Johon Popoah AGAIN, at which time he'll tell you to "...leave before there is bloodshed".
Move over to Paralin Notion.
Hail him.
/say I am brave
/say I will help your company
/say what squad
/say what about that razorfiend
/say I will provide you with assistance
/say what trials
/say what things

Now, go STRAIGHT (Not literally, but don't fight or talk to any other NPC's. Definitely don't gate or zone.) to Master Sergeant Aramis Daryln in the bridgehouse (far side). Be careful to avoid aggro from anything.
TARGET the Master Sergeant but do NOT hail him.
/say I am here to aid the cause.
(IF YOU DON'T GET the text from the Master Sergeant containing the words "Aerin`Dar" in brackets, go back to Johon and start over! There is an invisible temporary flag that occurs at this step.)
/say what is Aerin`Dar.
Run immediately back to Paralin.
Hail Paralin Notion. He'll give you text with the word "prove" in brackets.
/say How shall I prove myself.
Turn the 3 hearts (spider, razer, larvae all in same trade window) in to Paralin.
(Get exp message).
Run back to Master Sergeant and this time Hail him.
/say what glass lair
/say what about Aerin`Dar
/say what company
/say what mission
Run back to the tents where Paralin was, but enter the tent w/ Captain Ryglot Cupperhide.
Hail Capt. Ryglot. (make sure you are close enough to him when hailing)
/say why are you so busy
/say I will help
/say what mission
/say what war
/say I am willing
Turn in the 3 orb pieces.

The completed orb that he returns to you is your key to Aerin`Dar's lair.
