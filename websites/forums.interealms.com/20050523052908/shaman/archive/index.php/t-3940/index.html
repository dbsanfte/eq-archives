Jestice
I recently dinged 49 and proceeded to rush right off to Halas for my new spells highly anticipating my new dot. Having purchased all available except for HOS I figured I would try my hand at an ice giant - make that my first kill with my new spells. So out I ran and finding the entrace to Perma empty (due to a patch in about 15 minutes) I brought up my new dog - hit him with JB buffs and alarcity (no need to regen him - only using him for one kill), buffed myself and happily replace Scourge with Plague and Envenomed Breath with E-Bolt. Switching my gear around to give myself my solo wisdom vs AC level that I feel comfortable with and checking my mana (80%) I faced my new foe and rooted him.

Ok have one rooted ice giant - through a slow on him and send in the dog. Canning a couple of times I cast Plauge - hmm there goes one bub of mana. Root breaks - no problem melee a round while I cast root. Back off and cast E-bolt - hmm resisted and I check another bub of mana gone. Cast again and it sticks breaking root (now down to maybe 1.5 bubs with canni going) - np I'll melee/canni him til E-bolt wears and then reroot and recast.
E-Bolt wears off - np root back up cast a quick heal on myself (guy hits a little harder than I am used to) and cast another E-Bolt and rush in to finish him off. Ok he's dead but I think to myself gosh it seems like I used up a lot of mana - hmm new spells - didn't research it so well - zone going down so no time to look into this further.

Next afternoon - I find myself back in the Dreadlands (I love this zone for soloing - just turn off ooc and shout) and I figure I will see how my mana seems to be this time. So I do all the preparations - dog/buffs/gear and run out to find my favorite foe - the drovlog. Pulling with E-bolt (love the dot doing full damage as I run him back to my dog) and bring him back go Goner or who ever my pet of the day is. Dog runs to give him a hug and I root him and apply Plaugue and another E-Bolt - dead drov and I am down bubs of mana. OK he will die fast enough without Plague so I will switch that one over to Envenomed Breath. Ah down time is much less.

So I found myself evalutating the efficiently of my new spells. Using the dot damage calculator for EQshaman and spell info from CastersRealm I find this

E-Bolt 110/146/7 for 320 mana for a total of 1125 damage with MOBS regen built in 1125/320 = 3.51 dam/mana
Breath 40/59/7 for 160 mana for a total of 446 damage with MOBS regen built in 446/160 = 2.78 dam/mana

OK definitly seems to be more efficient to use E-Bolt but this just doesnt' seem so in practice. Hmm I log back in and decide on combining both E-bolt and Venom and this seems to work well. During a minute of Down time I look over my gear thinking how nice a JBB would be and I clicked on my wrist piece - Chipped Bone Bracer and there is the answer right in front of me Affliction Efficincy II. On my other arm is a Serpintine Bracer with Extended Affliction II. This changed the above equation to read like this.

E-Bolt 110/146/7 for 320 mana for a total of 1125 damage with MOBS regen built in 1125/320 = 3.51 dam/mana
Breath 40/59/8for 120 mana for a total of 504 damage with MOBS regen built in 504/120 = 4.20 dam/mana

Now I know that time is not factored in here and speed is a very important factor in xp killing but I had so totally underestimated the power of focus items. To me a focus item is now something to be actively sought out instead of a nice addition to an item. Depending on the situation it may even factor in a bit higher on the HP/AC/Mana/Wis scale I look at when seeking out new gear.
