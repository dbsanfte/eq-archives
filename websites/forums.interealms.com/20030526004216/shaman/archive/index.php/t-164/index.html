SisterLi
3k seems like quite a deal to me. I think I payed 6k for mine. I think it's very useful and cleanly worth it.

This spell is the bomb. It is perfect for when you are fighting blues that are dying too fast to warrant individual slows. It is also great for stomping trains. I use it extensively, and thus keep it memmed.

The aggro it produces is actually a bonus, it works exceptionally well for the patented Shaman mez(TM). Invison a small train of 6 or so mobs. Cast Tigir's twice, or once and follow up with Turgor's on the remaining mobs.

The aggro draws all the stragglers to you. Leaving the rest of the group to deal with the mobs one at a time. Drop epic dots on the stragglers as possible. Keep them aggro on you with judicious application of the JBB. Rest of the team picks them off one by one. You may need a few heals, but no one else will, so what's the healer doing with their mana any ways?

Rogue_01 "Train"
Shaman_001 "All slowed"
Rogue_01 "Make that "involuntary pull""

Only downside is keeping track of which mobs have been slowed and which have resisted. One way to do this is to hit the escape key, clearing your target. The unslowed mob is likely to hit you before the slowed mob, thus becoming your new target.

It is a great tool for gaining control over multiple mobs. It would be nice if it did not have a 4 mob limit, but even with that limitation it is handy. Of course if they lifted the 4 limit we might actually have a role in AoE groups, but you can't have it all. =P

I've used it extensively in Howling Stones, City of Mist, Chardok, KC, and The Grey.
