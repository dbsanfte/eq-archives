Entharion
Dark Elf/Wood Elf illusions do not grant you Hide.
Iksar/Wood Elf illusions do not grant you Forage.
Iksar illusion does not give you regen.
Ogre illusion does not give frontal stun immunity.
Gnome illusion does not let you tinker.
Racial illusions have no effect on experience gained.
Non-Player Race/object illusions do not affect faction (one exception being wolf form cast by druid or ranger, this still affects faction. Wolf form cast by enchanter does not).

Large race illusions DO allow you to Slam.
Troll illusion DOES give regen.
Racial (playable) illusions affect faction.

I think that covers it ;)
