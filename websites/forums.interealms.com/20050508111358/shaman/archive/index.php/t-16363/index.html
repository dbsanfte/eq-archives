From my experience in Beta at the lower levels, 4 people is doable, but very very challenging. You will certainly need to be well rounded, and good crowd control will be a must (more so than usual).
Slurm
4 person groups are harder. The adventure seems to be populated based on your average level, not taking into account how many are in the group... so expect to face challenges designed for 6 people.

Gardner
If you did a 4 person adventure - I would recommend the rescue / boss kill but even then you will have to kill plenty of mobs.

I ended up in a 4 person yesterday (2 couldn't zone in due to a bug) and we didn't really come close to completing the collection mission even with Time enabled dps.

Even though it might be a pain, most of these adventures are balanced for 5-6 rather than 4.
