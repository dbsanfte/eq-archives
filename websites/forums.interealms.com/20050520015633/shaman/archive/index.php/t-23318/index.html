Heh, I finally decided to get LDoN (downloaded it online), and went to get my first adventure a couple of days ago.

After a bit of running back and forth to POK (for Intricate Wooden Figurine) and Halas, I ended up in the Everfrost camp. There, a group was formed with me (52 shaman) and three beastlords (51,52,52). One of them had done one adventure before, and the 51 beastlord was to be our group leader, since he was the most experienced.

Still, it was a long time since any of them had adventured, and it took some time before we were able to even get an adventure (we decided to do a normal one, since we were only four and a good mix of rusty/inexperienced). And then a 30 minute timer started ticking....51 BST went LD, and I had no idea where this "portal" was (my compass was gone, and I had no idea how to put it back up). Someone said we'd better hurry, since we were on a timer. I checked..oh, my, we only had 27 minutes left to finish the adventure!

Anyhow, we found the portal at last and I zoned immediately. We were, after all, in quite a hurry.
"wait...don't click it"
Hm? Oh my, I had started the adventure, and the 51 BST was still not back. It slowly dawned on me what kinda timer the 30 minute timer was.....

In short, the 51 BST went LD a couple of more times, one of the BSTs died 2 times...BUT we completed the adventure! We collected 22 artifacts, and had maybe 15 minutes left. It was a blast!

What can I say...I wish I had done this sooner :D

Whooo! I'm now the proud owner of 15 LDoN points..only thousands to go :D
