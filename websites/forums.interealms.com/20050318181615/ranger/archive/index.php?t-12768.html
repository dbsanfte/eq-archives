Okay here are my PoD ethereal parchment drop secrets:

I have gotten 5 parchments out of PoD, 4 of them when I was soloing:

Rankle (named ooze): drop rate 2 out of 4 for kills I was involved in. Spawns anywhere you can find ooze-mobs (middle of the zone). Data from friends I have talked to place the drop rate lower than 50% though (i.e. they claim he always drops nothing), so maybe I have been lucky.

Carrion Larva (might have last part of name wrong, it is carrion-something, and is a wurm thing): drop rate 2 for 2. The first one of these I killed enraged (making him 57+?), second one did not enrage.

Scaevian larva: drop rate 1 for 1. This is one of the mobs for the Grimror quest, PH is Tsetsian Pupa (note odd spelling: other similar mobs are called Tsetsian Pupae).

All of these were easily soloable with EQ/AM3 using root and shoot. The only tricky part is safely pulling them single to a good fighting spot. None are casters.

I also was involved in a failed Grummus raid, and we saw perhaps 4 ethereal and 1 spectral parchment drop off the mobs on the way to Grummus (spectral was off of Aramin I think). Ethereals were off named and the hockey stick and mage guys.

I have also seen and heard of ethereal parchment dropping off of Diseased Underbulks and Diseased Malarians. I think the drop rate on these is quite low, and these are among the highest level "normal" mobs in the zone, which is why they do drop, just rarely.

My advice on hunting in POD: Kill the things with the weird names. I have been doing a lot of soloing here, both looking for quest items and because I can solo and get XP while LFG. I love this zone because very few things see invis, and I have Innate Camo (Innate Camo rocks!).

- Reandn
