I had an awesome awesome thread with all my hunting experience post patch, but when I went to post it it told me that I had too many images in my signature (i have none?) and when I clicked back my awesome awesome thread (THAT I TOOK ALMOST AN HOUR TO WRITE) was erased. So you get the condensed version, maybe i'll write it again tomorrow:

1) moktors in UP are decent exp, but they vary in level. some give 4%, about 1/2 give 1-2% (in my experience). all the lower level ones make it not worth it to fight here, imho. If all I got was the highest level variety it would be great.

2) xin thall centiens in ME. in the NW corner of the compound, there are a few high level ones you can pull. I get 3% or better every time from these. They also drop cool loot. here's what I wrote on eqmaps about centiens:

These come in one of two varieties: cleric or gimp-shadowknight.

The SK version casts dooming darkness, despair, invoke fear, a 220ish lifetap, and boil blood. I have killed about half a dozen of them and have yet to be harm-touched, although once I think I did see the HT spell particles, though i never got hit by it.

The cleric version comes in three flavors, which I distinguish by which nuke they use to own me.

Retribution Version - Level 51-53. They nuke for 500ish max, but I am level 60 with 110MR and their nukes barely touch me. They probably have about 10k HP. Since these are lower level, though, they only give 1% AA per solo kill :(

Reckoning Version - 54-55 mob. These hit with you with reckoning for about 650. Probably have about 12kish HP, and give 3% of an AA, usually. These are pretty dangerous if you are not on your toes.

Judgement Version - 56+ mob. Very, very, very nasty if unprepared. The will nuke you for 925, and have about 15k HP. They give me 4-5% of an AA per solo kill. The first centien I ever killed was of this variety, and dropped a vah shir claw earring and gave me 5% of an AA. This motivated me to kill more! The second time I found one of these, he dropped the pally spell Healing Wave of Prexus. The third one dropped a stone idol (thanks!)

I find these to be a very fun and rewarding mob to fight against, as well as giving great experience. I might be able to get better exp by killing goranga gatherers 10,000 times, but centiens are MUCH more fun and exciting, because there is danger! You never know if your next pull will be a judgement mob that gives you the fight of your life :)

3) Gorangas in ME - These are exactly what you expect. Slightly under 1% of an AA per kill. Boring, but the goranga chief drops divine inspiritation which would be a big upgrade for me. They're worthwhile exp because you can chew a bunch of them at once by root rotting. There are a few (hunters for example) that are higher level and give 2 or 3% per kill sometimes!.

4) Our favorite mob: FG shrooms. I timed myself, and I got 41% of an AA in 2 hours time exactly. I had KEI, but not canni5. I had no down time at all. This was using a conservative technique of killing one at a time. Tomorrow I will try killing entire patches at once. The zone is not as crowded as it used to be. since the resist changes, the slow is much harder to resist and it really slows down exp for melees by destroying their DPS. However, I laugh at their slow (100+DR) and even if it does land, I don't care :).
