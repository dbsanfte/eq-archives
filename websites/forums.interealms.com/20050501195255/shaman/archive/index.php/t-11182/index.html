Jaradis
Bazaar merchanting, buy low, sell high. Watch for good deals and buy them up and turn around and sell them for a profit. Its really not hard once you get started and it takes little time away from your xp'ing. Just check out the baz when you log in, then head out to xp, check it again when you get ready to log. Buy what you find at really good prices and then put them on your trader while you sleep.

I find spells are usually a good way to make quick cash, just be sure to do a bit of research first on what is in demand and what going prices are.

Check NPC merchants too. As I'm typing this I have a mule in bazaar now. Today I've sold 4 Chitinous Belts for 750pp (everyone else is at 800+) each that I picked up off an NPC merchant in Fungus Grove for about 10 pp each. You never know what you'll find on NPC's at times.
