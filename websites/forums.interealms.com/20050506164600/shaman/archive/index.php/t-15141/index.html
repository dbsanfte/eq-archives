I made a realization the other day that may be somewhat obvious to others, but since I didn't realize it until recently, then I figured others may not have realized it either. So I'm sharing.

Mages will soon be able to summon a new item with burning affliction.

They have 5 other summoned focus items right now...
- Ring : Spells casting haste
- Bracer : DD damage increaser
- Mantle : Buff extension
- Neck : Reagent usage reducer
- Earring : Mana Preservation on DDs (15%)

The realization I had was on the earring. It's not just on DD's, but DOTs as well. This is important to me, since I only have MP3. I don't have MP4 or Affliction Efficiency. So 15% MP on PoP DOTs is pretty nice when soloing. And even better once the new summoned item comes out.

That is all
