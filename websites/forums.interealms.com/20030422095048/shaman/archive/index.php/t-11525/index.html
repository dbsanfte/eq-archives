Weyalan
The late 40's and early 50's can be frustrating levels for a non-twinky shaman. You get lots of nice new spells, spells that are really quite powerful and that really make a difference to what you can do. However, shaman are rather dependent on their armor, and like I said, unless you are very rich, your armor is probably pretty shabby (I know mine was). There are lots of nice items for sale in the bazaar, but my god, where do you find the thousands of plat that you require.

I started farming the Ice Giants in Everfrost. If you are reasonably competent, you can solo them from about level 45 onwards, although it is slow and risky. From about level 49 onwards, it becomes a fairly safe easy proposition. By the time you get to level 53 you can probably handle 2 and by 56 you can take all 3 (with KEI)...anyway, they drop between 5 and 40 plat along with assorted gems, fine steel weapons and other stuff - I figure on average 30 to 40 plat worth of plat and loot per kill. At level 50, the exp will be very good too.
