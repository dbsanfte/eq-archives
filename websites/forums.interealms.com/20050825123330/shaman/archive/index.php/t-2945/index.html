My experience with resists and areas of the dungeon. Without an enchanter, I usually stick to the maze, the pools/sewers, and the lower throne room area. In these areas pulls tend to be a bit easier (level 53-55 mobs) that can be slowed and tank mezzed with Malo (no tash needed). Once you want to crawl into 4c, Avatar Pyramid, or any place with the Brown lizards (57/58+ mobs), then you'll like to have an enchanter with you.

The Brown clerics have a *HUGE* mana pool. They don't seem to be bound by the 2 Complete Heal limit, and even with mana tapping they usually get off a CH or 2.

Many of the shaman cast AOE slow, so have your clicky pants or your best cure disease memmed to get tanks back in it.

The necros cast 3x350 or 3x550 poison rain depending on level. The range is small so back casters away and let tanks engage Nuke these down.

I do know that one group of 60's can go anywhere in the dungeon with a little skill short of the Ring of Slime event.

I think a great introduction is to crawl the maze. Most spawns are singles or pairs (although some rooms have 3 mobs). There are many nameds that pop in there and the loot is great.
