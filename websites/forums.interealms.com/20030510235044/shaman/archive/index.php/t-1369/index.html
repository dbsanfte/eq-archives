I looked over some of the ph/spawn information available and decided to check it out.

I camped out on the big stairs leading up to the dining room. I summoned up doggie and parked him on the stairs.

I did this last night and after wiping up the whole upper floors including the barracks the crusader eluded me.

Being the stubborn type I logged on again today during lunch. Dogdog and I wiped out the upper floors again, taking extra time to make sure all the easy-to-kill skellies were removed, including the dining room itself but NOT inside the barracks. After 20 minutes I heard footsteps while running the halls...ran up the stairs to the barracks and BOOM--one crusader down.

20 minutes later I was re-clearing the room next to the dining room and out of the crowd came yet another Crusader..I'm assuming he spawned off the stairwell and came up behind me.

So nothing for 2 hours last night..then 2 skulls over lunch. If I had to place bets I'd say the key is to keep the upper levels clear all the way down to the balcony and keep cycling around keeping the spawns cleared. Keep dogdog parked and have him come to you, since there are a few odd pathing bugs that I've noticed on my last trips to Kurns which make doggie pet despawn if hes on follow.

Of course..your results may vary. Even though this is supposed to be a newbie quest..having a 49 pet whacking his way through trains of skellies sure makes it a lot less tiresome...:)
