If you are worried about things taking awhile to die then Veksar isnt for you. Mobs there seem to have an inordinate number of HP's. They dont run, which is good. If you are grouping your shaman with the warrior this wouldnt be a bad place as you can help with DoT's.

Planes solo arent as good as they once were. They arent bad, but mobs here also have lots of HP's for the exp they give.

When I'm Pling an alt, I always try and use the oldest expansions that I can. Why? Because mobs on older expansions dont have the HP's that newer expansions do. Chardok has about the same exp mod that CoM had, and mobs that dont run. One way, so make or buy some gate potions before you go. Veksar has a better exp mod, but the kills will take longer. Seb is ok cash/sellable loot (some camps) but lots of casters can make it sort of annoying.

Or, just take him to PoI and have him solo there. Ive done that too.
