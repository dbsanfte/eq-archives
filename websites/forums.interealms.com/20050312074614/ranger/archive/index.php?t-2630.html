It's mostly about whitespace on the board and loading time/bandwidth usage, I gather.

Even if your sig is all text, if it goes higher than 100 pixels, it takes up too much scrolling space. I mean, I'd bet the average post is less than 100 pixels in height, since many or them are simply "/agree" or something like that.

If the file size is too large, it takes up too much bandwidth and it's too slow to load on a dial-up connection.

Technically, my signature is out of specs, since the text makes it too wide, but Sunmoon's never complained, so I keep using it.
