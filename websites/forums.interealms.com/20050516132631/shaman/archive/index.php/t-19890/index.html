Chutoi
Don't misunderstand. It's not impossible, not would it be impossible to drop many raid level mobs--just not any biggies.

Shaman do have decent DoTs, however, there is the problem with only 30 debuffs/DoTs on a mob. Consider--Malos, Slow, and probably cripple (to reduce rampages). That leaves 27 spots for DoTs. Let's assume you can land 27 Banes (probably needing about 50 shaman here). Assuming everyone had some kind of DoT focus, that's probably about 2100/tic in DoTs. On a raid level mob, figure you'll need at least 2 shaman healing every second, so tie up probably 20 for a reasonable heal chain (2 shaman healing at the same time, 10 shaman 'groups' in chain).

That still leaves about 30 shaman to nuke. Figure an average of 1100/nuke with focus x 30 with a recast of (7.8+2.2=10) for an additional 3300 dps.

There aren't that many mobs big mobs that you can reasonably take down with all of 5400dps with a 6400hp (self-buffed) shaman as a main tank.

Not to mention that as I think about it, the healing will probably require 3 shaman healing at once. That will drop the dps to probably 4300 or so and prolong fights. You might be able to clear a chunk of NToV.

Pets are possible, of course, but they'd get eaten by any AEs.
