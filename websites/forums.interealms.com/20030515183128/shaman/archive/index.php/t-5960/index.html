Uthgar
Scounds are easy. I can single pull them at level 59.

These is normally my pattern.

1. Get on the rock and slow the left archer. Send in 2 dots of your choice. This will get you summoned to the top. Kill and then start on the second archer.

2. levitate down or jump and kill the 2 scounds in the front of the gate.

3. Pull and kill the 2 scounds across the cavern in front of the other building.

4. Go down the middle hallway and swim into the room with the 3 skelletons. I root the middle one and pull the left skeleton. I generally only get one skeleton at a time. kill all 3 skeletons.

5. Head back to the archers and med while they respawn.

6. goto step 1

The scounds and skeletons drop 3-6pp each.
the archers drop 2 Bows worth 23pp each.

I generally start and end each night by killing the archers. thats nearly 200pp for about 15 minutes of work. This is where i get my KEI money each night.

The exp is not great imho. Exp in the grey, chardok etc is better.
