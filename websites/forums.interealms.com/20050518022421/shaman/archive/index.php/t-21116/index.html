The thread in the Developers section of the board on shaman issues has become very long, but I think a short, to-the-point response to Rytan's 3 points on shaman balancing is in order. I will list his 3 points with a brief response that I hope summarizes much of the discussion on these boards and at the Shaman's Crucible:

Rytan: issue #1 -The ability to function as a secondary healer

Recent changes to shaman healing spells were insufficient. More work needs to be done here, especially with regard to burst healing. An increase of Daluda's Mending to 1800hp or more has been suggested.

This, of course, assumes that shamans are no longer to be considered primary healers at all (at least in Gates). Is this the intent of the development team?

Rytan issue #2 -The impact of shorter fights on DoTs spells and Slow

The problem of slow impact in short fights has been addressed with the proposed change to Balance of Nihil now on the test server. Most shamans consider this sufficient, but caution that this change by itself cannot solve all the problems experienced by high level shamans.

DoT's were not created for the short fight, plain and simple. The real issue is DPS contribution, which is nil for shamans in short fights under any realistic scenario. Two changes have been suggested by the shaman community: (1) increased effectiveness of DD spells, for example, a new disease based DD that is fast and does around 1200 damage. (2) a useful melee enhancement. Shamans have been asking for years that Rabid Bear be made more useful. This could be done very simply by making it an on-demand skill, rather than one that can be used for 6 min every hour. A trainable bash skill, like what was given to clerics has also been suggested.

Rytan issue #3 -The increased number of slow mitigating NPCs in some areas of Gates.

Slow mitigation, including immunity, are important limitations on what a shaman brings to a fight. The changes to Balance of Nihil will be relatively ineffective if slow mitigation and immunity, especially of boss mobs, is not addressed. This would also have the favorable effect of allowing knights the ability to tank in many more Gates encounters.

Rytan's summary - Personally I feel that shaman are relatively well balanced in most other areas of the game outside of Gates content, which is in agreement with most of the feedback I’ve seen on the shaman boards. Therefore, I expect any change to the shaman class to be specifically aimed and alleviating their concerns in Gates of Discord.

The sentiment of the shaman community is expressed at the Shaman's Crucible. Please take a moment to review the post entitled "Proposal for Shaman Improvements (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=19207) " on the general section of that message board. Many of these concerns apply to Gates, but there has been a general erosion of shaman utitliy that has affected their performance in other high end encounters, including elemental plane encounters, hard LDoN adventures, the Plane of Time, and elsewhere. Two major issues are not addressed in your 3 points:

1: Buffing - by the time a shaman gets to the elemental planes, the usefulness of shaman buffs is questionable. Focus remains the only viable buff, and that solely for the HP it adds. Ferine Avatar, along with its predecessors, is severely limited by its short duration and long cast time. Solutions include a group version to Ferine Avatar and other buffs that more directly affect melee performance.

2. Mana regeneration - The shaman model of active mana regeneration works for the long, slow fight, but not for short, quick, active content. Cannibalization helps, but can only infrequently be used. Chaotic pain would have been a viable solution, but making it an Ancient spell, effectively removing it from 99% of the shaman community, negates the potential it has to solve this problem.

Thank you for all that you are trying to do to solve shaman issues. We only ask that you consider the larger scope of the class, both in content other than Gates, and in other areas not identified by your 3 points
