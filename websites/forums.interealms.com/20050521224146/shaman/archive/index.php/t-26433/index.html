DoN is the new LDoN, you can get groups there pretty consistently there as long as you make yourself available, and make yourself known. this doesn't mean spamming constantly in OOC "blah blah LFG" but its ok to say something every 5 - 10 minutes.

WoS is a good zone to get groups, but the numbers are a little low at that hour, make friends with the few that stay there and you'll get a regular group there.

typically, if I want to group, I'll put up LFG and go solo in nearby areas, if you want to group in WoS, try your hand at NC murks, if that doesn't work for you, farm faction in DS. if you want a group in DoN, start GM ancestral armor quest and work on that in Stillmoon. there are mobs that you can kill for a little EXP/loot while LFG, that way you aren't idly getting bored for nothing.
