Ok. I have been reading post after post of things that we do NOT like about the Shaman class we so all love dearly. I have yet to peruse over a post about what people LIKE about their Shaman.

NO flames please, no negative comments. I just want to see what people Like/Love about the class that we think is the best Class on the servers!

I will go first.

1. I like the fact that when I can't find a group, or just when I want to be alone with a friend or two, and we do not have a healer class.. I can take over a few heals.

2. Canni is my friend! I have tried playing another class that doesn't have a mana regen (Cleric, Enchanter) at a low level and I found myself sitting on my hands trying not to chew my nails off waiting :D <3 to Canni!

3. Shrinking my group to get in small places that nobody else can go is a great thing!

4. It makes me feel good to know that I can make SoW potions and help the races who can't cast it on themselves.

5. I can make people drink funny looking drinks and turn them into crazy looking races. I'm the one laughing, because I know that the smell of Froglok blood and Wormwood smells awful.. and it probably tastes like Ogre Socks! ROTFL!

Alright everyone! Lets show everyone how much Shammy love really means to us!
