The Bandits of WK really should not be overlooked in the guide.

There are four camps in the series.
The first starts at the easternmost bandit farm, which is fairly much due east as you come out of the tunnel from Qeynos Hills.

From there proceed westward, and slightly north, there are two more bandits stationed by some Birch trees at the base of the hill,

occasionaly you will get 4, if you happen to hit a change of guard.

At the top of the hill is a Bandit encampment. Numbers are fairly random as they will occasionaly break off and Run to man certain spots in the wilderness or Run to the other bandit groups.

The third band is to the north, and westward, at the northern base of the hill, amidst some rather large trees.

There is no permanent encampment here, and, again, numbers can vary. It is on the Westward, downsloping side of the hill (gentle slope).

From there follow the bases of the hills south and west.
Where the bases of the east hill meets the base of the west hill follow that west ridge up,
over the crest and south westward will be a spot overlooking the Ogre shrine with a number of bandits upon it.


AT lvl 30 I no longer pick up aggro from the entire camp when attacking, except for the Brigands.
