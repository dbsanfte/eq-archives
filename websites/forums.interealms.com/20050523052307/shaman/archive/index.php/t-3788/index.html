Spine Chill Spear
10 dmg, 28 dly, +6 Wis, +3 Sta, +10 vs cold, Proc Ice Shock (38 cold DD starts at level 40) - can't be put in the Range spot

Bettle Stinger
8 dmg, 23 dly, +4 Wis, +4 Agi, +4 Dex, Proc Steal Strength (drain 15-27 strength from mob and give it to you, starts at level 30) - can be put in the range spot

The spine chill spear is a little better weapon (better ratio, better stats, better proc) - but it generally costs a little more, doesn't proc for ten levels later, and can't be put in your range spot when you're ready to upgrade.

(Just checked, on Karana the cheapest Spine Chill is 125 pp, the cheapest Beetle Stinger 100 pp in the bazaar.) Given that small price differential (and the general cheapness of the weapons) I'd go with the Spine Chill.
