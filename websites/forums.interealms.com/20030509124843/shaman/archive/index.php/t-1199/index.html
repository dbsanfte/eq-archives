Most of my gear is not very good, but I do have all easy pieces of JB armor, and 5ac/55hp rings.

I have solo'd the Yeti's from level 34-40 and it was the fastest leveling I have seen since my teens. How to kill them for the people who dont want to spend the time getting a rythme:

1. Get a pet out, dont bother buffing him at all.

2. I buffed regen, fury, talisman and protect on myself.

- Protect (instead of shifting shield) was used to bump me over 800ac. Fury is a great buff. I dont ever let regen or talisman drop.

3. Pull the yeti back to your dog with Scourge.

4. Channel a Tagar's. Immediately follow up with Envenomed Breath. If either spell is resisted cast it until it lands.

- I had both Tagars and Walking Sleep up, incase I got a resist, the mob was atleast slowed a little bit. This tactic was very useful untill about level 38-39. At 38-39 only the higher level yeti's hurt me much.

5. Melee with the dog, healing yourself if you need it. When the yeti starts to run, sit and med while the dog chews it up.


Out of all the levels I got here, I only had 3-4 adds, and 3-4 close calls. The exp flows in and the loot isnt all that bad, lots of those frosty flowers which sell for a plat to the vendor...and plenty of gems/words.

These are also great for maxing that defense skill out if you have not already. Down with the druid/shaman! Up with AC!
