No. The way all the epic turn-ins like this work, is the NPC is non-aggro. You hand in the quest piece and the mob despawns, then respawns immediately, this time with the drop you need.

For example, if you noticed, when you turned in the icon to High Scale Kirn, his pet disappeared...

Common procedure is that if you can see Neh with the torch, and there's a mage that needs her, the raid is extended two hours. Neh is killed, then everyone waits for her respawn so the questing shaman can do his/her turn-in.
