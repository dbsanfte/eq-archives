Jaidin
You get all spells awarded to you, they're just at the App1 level. You purchase upgrades from there.

Also, some spells you get at certain bubble dings. For example, you may see some lists state something like:

10 - spell x
10.4 - ability y
10.6 - spell z

This means that when you ding level 10 you get spell x, when you hit 40% into level 10 you get ability y, and then at 60% into level 10 you get spell z. All abilities and spells just appear in your spellbook or your hotkey bars if you have room. :)
