Zixor
for one canni dance its something like this:

/sit off
/pause 15, /cast (#of can spell)
/sit on

You'll probably need to adjust the pause time based on your lag... I think 15 worked for me with can3... or was it 25...

I'm not sure how you could get 3 actual dances into one hotkey though, there's not enough room. Maybe you mean something like this:

/sit off
/pause 40, /cast #
/pause 40, /cast #
/pause 15, /cast #
/sit on

not really 3 dances and you'll have to adjust the pause again.
