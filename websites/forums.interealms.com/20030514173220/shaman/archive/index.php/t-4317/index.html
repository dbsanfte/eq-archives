The spot we camped is the open area near Tenebrous zone. Chanter pulled w/ tash, got hit once to send his pet in, and rooted the mob while i slowed it and sent puppy in to help. Togors lands easy and the guards are WEAK, hitting for 60 max and i don't think they even double hit. 2 hasted pets took out a guard fast enough that VoS didn't have time to do full damage, so i stopped using it. Got up to two adds at some times because we were sitting right at a spawn for one guard and in the pathing for another, but wasn't a big deal with chanter there; if you don't have one it should be easy enough to find a spot without adds.

Each guard was worth about 1/5 of a blue to me at 44, and as i mentioned kills took under 48 seconds with almost zero downtime.
