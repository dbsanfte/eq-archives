Grafsha McDaughts
At 55/52 combo best advice is start at zone in and start working thru the content. Seb is one of the great zones, IMO.

Casters are shaman/necro/wizard/enchanter ... shaman and wizard are the most common.

Mobs are old world dmg and hitpoints.

Mobs do add and at your levels you will generally get a minimum of 2 per pull - given the probability of a caster being one mob, FD splitting will get interrupted often (FD ... casting mob spell hits after.)

One of the nice factors - the best, IMO - is the zone itself defines where to go and what to do. As you progress deeper the difficulty increases.

Seb is still my favorite zone in the entire game. Took some Guildies down to Juggs last week and did some spell farming. Forgot how much I missed that place /wipes tear from his eye. (A couple of them didn't even know there was a second level....I know I know....I just shook my head too).

Anyway, avoid this zone if you don't have the Cleric with you. At level 55, the risk vs. reward is not worth it unless you are going there simply to get away from idiots or to have a little fun. Exploration past NGE at your level is dangerous to say the least. But if must go there, keep your Magic resist up at all times. Having an Enchanter turn your Monk against you may discourage you from ever returning to this magnificent dungeon.

I would love to tell you all about the zone, but it would take many hours. When the Cleric is on, you could probably make it all the way thru the first floor....minus the Crypt of course. Take advantage of those times to learn the zone, then duoing there will become quite simple in later levels. Then you can plunge down to the 2nd level and start all over again.
