House
Easiest faction for you to increase in Neriak is the Cleric guild, giving you an 'ally' merchant. Just do the note quest (talk to the Dark Elf in the Grobb Shaman guild) multiple times, or collect a bunch of Halfling Necklaces and Raider Caps. In the Dark Elf Cleric guild, the merchant in the back left, standing behind the bar, is on the guild faction.
Grippa
The most fun way to raise faction in North Freeport (where the bank is) is to BARSH the guards in the other freeport zones!

Trouble is you can't really do this at your level, but it's worth remembering :)
