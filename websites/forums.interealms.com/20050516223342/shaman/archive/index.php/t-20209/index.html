Wisey, i would say go for crit dot, even if it is expensive.

1- DoT is versatile, you can use it for solo and groups / raids. Yes in GoD mobs drop fast usually, but if you can get a focused bane, you can nearly always get 3+ticks damage, that like 1,5k 2+k dmg around, wich is not bad. Even more when your dps have to afk, or are slacking you can see it easely ;) And look, it is more mana / dps efficient than 2 spear of torment 1,3k dmg without focus for 550 mana in 10sec around(anyway anger/fury of solusek is hard to find or ****ty slots for shamans). It is also better than that crappy Velium Strike wich need 10hours to cast, even with haste focus.

And no, with SCS 3, you can still hold aggro whenever you want to (if you like beeing the rabbit in C2...)

3- DoTing allow you to dmg the mob AND do something else like spoting the tank / cleric, looking / debuffing adds, scratching your head, etc... :rolleyes: In GoD, mobs need to be burned out quickly because of the super high dps they have, most of the time you have to chain spot someone. This way, you can bring both dps and healing power at the same time, wich isnt crap to my eyes.

Now like Ceno said, it is up to you, everyone has his own style of play, i just showed you how DoTs can be efficient for me. Remember as well, crit DoT maxed is 9%, Crit nuke maxed is 16% (7% spellcasting fury, 4,5 fury of magic, 4,5% fury of magic mastery), and allow you to land critical blast if you melee, but require you to chain cast / melee, focusing your attention to only 1 spot, that might be deadly if you see an add too late because of that.
