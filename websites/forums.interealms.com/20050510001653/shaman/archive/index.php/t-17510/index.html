Devnull
A monk without Run3 is in a world of hurt. There ae still places in the game where monks are solid pullers, and Run3 is a godsend for any puller. Being smacked from behind and stunned for even a second or two can be fatal when mobs are swinging away for 900+ a pop.

Shrew will replace run3... right up until it fades midpull, or is dispelled midpull (I've seen pullers lose 12 buffs on one pull in Guk).

As far as patching people up goes, First Aid isn't going to help you much. You can already bandage them up to 70%, if you feel like having your friends carry around enough bandages to heal five or six thousand hit points: as a monk, you won't be able to carry enough. I honestly don't see getting First Aid at all, unless you're shooting for Critical Mend early on. If that's the route you're taking, then yeah, you need First Aid, but I'd heartily reccomend taking a different path at that point. Thirty-three AAs is a hefty price to pay for an ability that amount to a 1500 hit point heal every six minutes... one that may not even work.

As a comparison, you could get Combat Agility 3, Lightning Reflexes 5, and Natural Durability 2 instead. I'd submit that you'll save your healers more mana that way in the long run than you ever will be bandaging them after a fight goes poorly.

Just my thoughts on the issue...
