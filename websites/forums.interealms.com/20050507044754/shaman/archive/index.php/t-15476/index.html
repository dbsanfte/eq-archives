Jebeksis Stormcaster
Why is this a new thing? Prices for almost all items (with exceptions for ones that don't drop in game any more, etc) tend to drop over time as more of them are in circulation on the server. Or, in the case of spells, as a larger percentage of the people who need that spell (for instance, all the 65 shamans on your server) have it, the potential market to which someone could sell a particular 65 shaman spell becomes smaller. Yet 65 spells keep coming into existence at the same, if not an even greater rate (most likely at a greater rate than the creation of new 65 shamans).

It's been like this since the game started. Remember when it cost 1k (or more) to buy canni3? 61-62 spells went for 10k+ soon after PoP was released, but now people can't even be bothered to loot ethereals.

The absolute "best" items have always seemed to go for a couple hundred k, regardless of era. It's just what those particular best items are depends on what the most recent expansion is (ie, the best items come from the most recent expansion, replace the previous best items at the top of the price list, and driving down the price of all old gear).
