For the Azarack isle, just make sure that your chanter is grouped with a very good cleric and a good secondary healer. IMO keeping the chanter alive when you first enter the isle is the most important thing here.
Tefon
If you're unsure about all the folks on your raid not following directions clearly, have a break in team of 6 - 12 go over with 2 chanters, clerics and tanks dmg dealers and clear the spot. A well organized team breaks the Azs easy, but if you get a bunch of peeps not assisting, etc, you will get a ton of adds and lots of deaths if you're not careful.

Once break in spot is clear have everyone else come over to clear the rest of em.

This has worked well for my guild when we have a bunch of people on a raid that we don't normally raid with us.
