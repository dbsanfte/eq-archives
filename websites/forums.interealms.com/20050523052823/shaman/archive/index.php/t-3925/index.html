Bigguss
I can answer a few of the questions.

First, check out the castersrealm horse FAQ: http://eq.crgaming.com/editorials/horses.asp

You used to be able to stack movement increasing spells (SoW, etc.) with a horse as long as you weren't seeing the horse models. This was changed in the recent patch- now in order to stack SoW, etc you have to have all Luclin models turned off.

You get just about 90% of your money back when you sell your horse (I'm thinking of the cheapest one.)

Not sure about combat abilities.

I believe a slow horse would be of less value to an SK than a shaman- the biggest benefit to a shaman being that we can med while standing. This doesn't work if autoattacking, however... which I assume a solo'ing SK would be doing more or less all the time during a battle.

Hope this helps.
