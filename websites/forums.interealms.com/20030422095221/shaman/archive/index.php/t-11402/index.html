I am not 100% certain of these answers but will do my best to answer them.

You med on a horse as long as you do not have auto attack on. If a mob is attacking you and you don't have auto attack on, you get the med. So yes you can just stand there and get hit and get the med effect.

I am not to certain about the med through casting. I "think" you do. I sit on my horse and chain cast JBB but I never really thought about that before. Anyone have an answer for this?

Besides the med effect I really like that I don't get sit agro while on a horse. Makes it easier to flip through that spell book to quickly mem a new dot or nuke. Before horses I would have to sit down to do that, which usually drew agro.
