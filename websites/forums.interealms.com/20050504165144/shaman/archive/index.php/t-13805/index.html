If you spend most of your time hunting outdoors, buy a horse. It will give you permanent mana regen better than KEI.

EDIT: I clicked your profile. You are way to wisdom heavy. You need to drop some wisdom for hps and ac. Get in the combat a bit, and max your defense skills. Swap out your +wis rings for some 6ac/65hp rings (vellium/fire), swap out the +wis earring, the boots are next on the hit list... The returns for a wisdom over 200 are VERY low, so dont worry about the mana loss. Especialy if you can get the horse you will regain mana quickly enough. Learn to cannibalize if you havent already, cannibalize and regen will do a lot more for your mana than a high wisdom.
