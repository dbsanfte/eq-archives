The augments will be tagged class 1 to 18 (so far anyway). Class 1, the lowest end augs, will cost 1 pp. Class 18, the highest end will cost the 4k. The prices are affected by charisma, so mine were a bit lower than the ones in your logs.

The highest type of aug I had to test was a 70 hp LDoN raid aug which was class 15 (1500pp for me at 250 cha), The solid and radiant coals of prowess were class 13, and 1k (at 250 cha) to re-use.
