I am a big fan of this zone but I have to admit - its usefulness to a mostly level 65, elemental guild is close to none. 60 and below spells drop all over the place in this zone. Rare ones. Torpor and Malo galore, with Sunstrike up the wazoo. But that's one of the main reasons its useless to an "uberguild". Runes give upgrades to basically everything that drops in VP, and the equipment is useful for a few classes (shaman being one of them with the PE clicky hammer and snare weapon) but stat-wise is pretty pathetic. The best thing I've seen out of there is a 35hp/35mana +5 all resists +5 agi dex and str all/all belt. Hardly worth the effort to elemental guilds.


If any people in your guild need 60 or under spells, this is THE place to be. For anything else... there are much better places.
