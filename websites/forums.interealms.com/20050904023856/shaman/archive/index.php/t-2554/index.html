Khayla
Definitely one of my favorite zones in the game from 30-40. I highly recommend trekking out there.

The only annoying thing is sometimes all the orcs are dead from high level farmers...but this is rare.

There are several types of camps...orcs, tentacles, spiders, and geos. Geos are ALWAYS camped my server, and I wouldn't recommend trying them solo anyways until 40's. Spiders are lots of fun, crowd control is helpful here though as they can easily gang up on you. Terrors and orcs are the most soloable mobs for xp, and they all drop cash, velium weapons, and velium pieces/bricks for tradeskills.

And on top of it all, there is a bank and merchants to sell to :)

[QUOTE]Do you think myself a 29 shammy along with a 28 beastlord, 31 warrior and a 28 shadow knight would have any trouble in there

Only problem you may have is keeping the war healed, however, your DPS should be ok for what your going up against. CC does have some downsides, oracles will cast tash/some type of slow/root/poison dot on you and will heal other orcs. Herbalists also cast and trains. Pathing in CC can be a little tricky and can get you a mess in a hurry. Don't push the orcs off the walkways, if they fall down, they will be coming back with friends. They will also run at low health.

Spiders are pretty easy, their velium poison is pretty bad, uncurable AFAIK too. Wanderers in their tunnel will always assist. Loot there is ok, crystalline silks for tailoring, crystalline silk net (root effect), crystal grinder, words, runes, etc.

Tentacles I never really hunted much, they have some ok drops, chipped velium amulet, some piercer weapon, don't know alot about em though. There are some "named" spawns down there to be wary of though.

CC is a great cash/xp zone, but I always advise getting down to dorf town and work from there. As long as your not killing geos, you won't be KOS there, so you can exit the zone through their tunnel.

I soloed my monk from 31 to 42 in CC in about 2 weeks tops and probably made 3k doing it. I could have made more if I hadn't just vendored all the velium.
