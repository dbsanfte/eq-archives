Shabby
I would not attempt to solo Seafuries until you are level 49 (or have JBB). You get 2 new DoT's at level 49 that make all the difference. Seafuries have somewhere around 3500-4000 hitpoints.

The good new is, they do not hit too hard (max. 72). Give your self and doggie haste, pull with slow, when you get back to doggie, lay on your 2 DoT's, melee a tad if you want, or root and step back and med while doggie does his job. Have root and/or nuke ready because the clops do flee when low hitpoints, and you do not want them going for help.

Levels 49-50, the exp. here is ok, and the semi-rare gems sell for very nice cash.

i have been soloing seafuries for since 46 here and im 48 now. it isnt too hard soloing these, i dont even have JBB or any real twinkage i didnt buy for myself with money ive made through my lvls.

buff yourself up and get pet. regen and haste are the most important.

here's a tip, pull near the "druid ring" type thing with that barbarian. theres a strip of land that juts far out into the ocean. pull the seafury out there and sick pet. i use VoS dot and tank with my pet. with the slow and high defense skill, the thing wont hit you too much. problem of course is they have so much hp. i have to VoS like 4 times and recast slow when they are at half life.

this strategy only works if you are tank-style shammy and have your defense/offense/weapon skills up high, or else they rip you up.

and exp is until 54 here, i used my necro here til then. of course, you need to kill pretty fast to make it worth your while by then

the gems that drop are worth up to 75pp. and if you get the gornit camp you can bank. ive made a good 4k here in the past lvl.
