I usually think of there being three "tiers" of pet using classes:

* MAG/NEC/BST
The top tier, with powerful pets - and those classes gain a substantial portion of their DPS from them.

* SHM/SHD/ENC
These classes all get regular upgrades, and the PoP enchanter animation is similar to True Spirit in that it's also L58, has around 2K HPs and enrages. However, the pet isn't a main source of DPS.

* DRU/WIZ/CLR
Either one-shot pets or very weak ones - and these classes don't get pet upgrades, either. They're more of a party trick than anything else...

As such, it makes sense that the top tier should get pet focus items. However, I'd argue that if shamans were to get access to them (much though I'd like it), then it would only be fair to make them affect SHD and ENC pets too... the former being a problem due to their low level.

Here's the DPoC focus, from Lucy:

Summoner's Boon
Unknown #167 (100/15/0)
2: Limit: Max Level(65)
3: Limit: Min Level(59)

As far as I'm aware, the levels refer to the level of the pet itself, and as that's 58 for SHM/ENC and low 40s for SHD... well, a cynic would say they set it at 59 to avoid any shamans moaning about how we could use the focus if only our class were on it! :p

Havng said all that, we do now get L60 pets, but they're in the form of swarm pets that can't be controlled - and it's highly likely IMO that our new Omens wolf will be base level 60.
