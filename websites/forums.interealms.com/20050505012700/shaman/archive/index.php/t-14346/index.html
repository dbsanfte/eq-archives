Juujuu
I have a bit of experience farming emp.

I think you need 201+ skill in order to pick the lock.
I might be wrong though.
It's been a LONG time since I've bothered going down there.

As for killing Emp and not having him run and not gating...
I'm not sure of the directions for his room, but this is the layout:
There is a locked door into Blood room.
There is a coffin in the center of the room where blood PH spawns.
There is a hallway (I think there's a door there, but not 100% sure) leading into Emp's room.
The room has 6 guards plus the Emp ph in the center of your vision.
At level 60 you've already seen you can solo pull Emp and ph.

My setup was having pet parked near the coffin.
I'd stand between the hallway and the coffin and pull Emp.
I had malo and torpor already when I was farming him to try and make some cash, FYI, so I'd pull with malo.
If you don't have malo, I'd try pulling with malosini.
Slow asap of course.
You have lots of time to kill him. I think every 20 minutes you need Emp killed before the blood PH repops. No rush.
Get emp down to about 30% health or so.
Keep him between the coffin and the hallway. You can even let him get pushed into the hallway, but not too far. He won't aggro the nearby adds if he's in the hallway near the blood room.
At 30% or so, lay on the poison dots that you can, and start nuking him down.
Keep him rooted as much as possible.
He'll start to run of course.
If you let him get past the coffin, you will most likely get adds if you don't root him, or he'll gate if you do root him.
You MIGHT be able to let him get a bit past the coffin before you root him, but it's unlikely
For when I was down there, I had along with me a Fungal Fiend Gland thingy that has 10 charges of snare.
I tried not to use it, but for the 1 time I let him get too far, I popped a snare onto him and it saved me a lot of CR or gating out.

In short, if you let him run past the coffin, you either need to root him and kill him before he gates, or kill him while he continues to run before he runs too far and warps out through the locked door and gives you adds.
