from lvl 41 to 50.. in 1 week!

BLOODGILL GOBLINS in Lake Omen (Lazy Typer)

Levitate over the water, look down. If you do this "just" right, you can see to the very bottom of the Lake. the BG's spawn 2 at each corner of the submerged temple. Root one BG and make a dash to the coast... aggro dies on the rooted one if ya back off real quick. Root/Slow/DoT x2.
always keep SoW/Levi/Chloro on... make sure to have Canni II also.

As I posted about what kind of gear a Shaman should have, I recommend HP/Mana, Focus Effects, Recovery (Iksar Bp-Fungi/Solstice earring) gear. This helps out ALOT! I was a Wis phreek until I was shown the error of my ways...

Once you hit 46 it goes alot faster! You can get that nice Chanter/Shaman/Cleric/Pally/Druid buffs! I was Root/DoT'ing 2 at a time with ease! took me about 4 minutes to kill 2 BG's at a time... a lil advice, use you Pet to kill off those pesky "Greenies" that might bother you when you meditate. Also, you can use your pet to finish off the BG's when they're almost dead and are "Slo-Mo'ing" away from ya!

Of course I did this back in 2000, hopefully they wern't nerfed in anyway!
