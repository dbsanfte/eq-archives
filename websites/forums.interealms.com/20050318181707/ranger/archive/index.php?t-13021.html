I also do not think there will be a nerf. Some people may be shocked to see dozens of level 65s already, but the fact is, there are a certain number of hardcore players who would get to 65 amazingly fast no matter how hard you made it. Also, because levels are so important to raiding, major guilds that normally would raid every night have used a lot of that time to grind xp, and with their high-end Luclin gear it's only natural they would make xp faster.

Also, level 65 isn't the top of the ladder. AA skills make a huge difference in how effective your character is and PoP has a LOT of them. And if you spend your time xping 24/7 to get those skills, you're going to be missing out on what looks like a ton of fun content in PoP, so a balance has to be struck, and even the hardcore people will slow down.
