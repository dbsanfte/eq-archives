BelegurthValar
Best place I found to start it was West Freeport. The dude is in a tent accross the newbie area from the Gnome who sells the Fuzzlecutter drinks. You get a bag and a hintbook, but, here they are (Out of order).

Scythe off Specter in Ferrott
Tooth off Shark in OOT
Eye off Gnolls in Paw
Doll off Cats in Kerra
Gland off Skunks in Tox
Ring off Goblins in Dagnors
Granite off Gargs in Mistmoore
Wisp Stone off Wisps in West Commolands
Skin off Goblins in Sola
Tounge off Undead Frogs in Lguk.

All the items are No Drop, and have "Collectors" in front of the name. Seems fairly common drops, I did it all in about two hours. COmbine all the stuff in the bag, and hand it to back any of the people in the various town, for your wonderful spammy stick.
