My advice:

1) If you have low 50s on your raid, don't depend on them for ANYTHING. They will spend most of the fight feared.

2) I usually have a Bard kite her before engaging and if the Bard has the above mentioned Lost Composition, takes about 1.5-2mins to drain her dry (if that). Otherwise, the Bard eats her weak cleric nukes and when she starts casting low level spells (like Strike and Furor and Smite), she's ready to be owned.

3) If you're 65 with less than perfect resists, you'll still resist her AEs more often than not due to level.

4) She dies FAST usually. People xp on PoP mobs who have more hp than she does.

I usually engage her near BW zone in that flat, clear area. A lot of times, the clerics can melee and nuke since her dps is so abysmal. Defensive is NOT NEEDED by any stretch of the imagination. Gore is 1 or 2 grouped a lot on Torv from what I've seen, but these are usually level 65 characters with Luclin+ gear.

I honestly don't know if cures will work for her slow. She usually died so fast (I'm talking like 15-30 seconds, depending on how many wizards/mages were present) no one even thought to try :)
