Are there really people out there who didnt post because of the rep system? Are there people who changed their opinion on an issue based on it? My gosh, I hope not, if so then we are truly doomed as a species.

Heres why I liked the rep system. It was FUN. Yeah, it was broken, and lame, and stupid, but it was FUN. I used it all the time. I commented on peoples posting in rep bumps I made to them (always signed). I didnt give out ANY bad reps, only good. If a post was bad, I just left it be. If a post was really good, I pos bumped it, EVEN IF I DISAGREED! There are a bunch out there who know this is true, because I signed em all.

See, I compare this to the nerfing of the DE mask in 1999. There was no real reason to nerf that item. It was a fun part of the game that people loved. Its actual benefits were actually fair, shrinking for large races, etc. But they took it out, and it ruined the fun. Now we have potions of EVERYTHING. They took out the on-screen co-ords too. Now we have a compass and maps. They FORCED you to hit Sense heading, now you start at 200.

Rep was fun. It didnt hurt anyone, and in fact, it gave people a private avenue to express their opinion without posting to the thread. I got a lot of bumps, both pos and neg, with legit points in them. Since I was never a guy to back down from a MB Fight, I ended up getting a LOT of rep bumps. It just so happened the pos outweighed the negs and I moved up.

Trolls came. They negged a lot of people for stupid reasons. Then they left, and went to Druids Grove or whatever. And truth be told, SOME negative bumps actually MADE ME THINK. I remember one inparticular, guy said "I agree with what your saying, but do you have to be so snotty and abrasive about it? If your right, your right, move on, no reason to bury someone in your self-rightousness". It was unsigned, and it was a big neg hit. I re-read the post in question, and damned if he wasnt spot-on. I was rude, I was abrasive, and I didnt need to be. Probably affects how I post to this day.

Anyway, its just a thing. If anyone stopped posting or coming here because of the system, I am saddened by that. If anyone changed their opinion becuse of neg bumps, that bothers me. But I dont really think these things are true. I think MOST of the people had fun with it, but the squeaky-wheel minority was, once again, the loudest, therefore, its gone.
