How to Grief Another Guild's Instance in the Plane of Time

Are you sick of only getting time every 5 days? Is there another guild on your server you'd like to "get"?
Here is your chance!

A few words, by way of explanation: the plane of time is controlled by instancing. That means in theory that
each guild in time should be able to share equally. Each killing their own individually spawned mobs, without the
blocking and griefing so common to other expansions.

In practice, instances are attuned to the first person who zones into the tier one trials in Plane of Time B. So if you
clear half of the plane of time one night, and come back a second night, as long as someone who was there on the
first night zones in first, you get to restart time at roughly the same spot after defeating tier 1.

"But wait!" you say. "How do I grief if the zone is instanced?"

Here's how: camp a player in tier one, and log that player on while your rivals are doing that tier. You won't be
in a raid, and will get booted, but you will get the toon you logged in attuned to your rival's instance. The next day,
zone the toon you zoned in on your rivals in first with your own guild. Voila, you get their instance! Grats you on
extra ph4t l00tz!
