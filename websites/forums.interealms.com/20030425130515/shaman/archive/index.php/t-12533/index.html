Goldark
Extremely nice gear for your level, so I guess you have some cash. As Schpleef says, you need to work on your hp and ac, and don't worry about wisdom (assuming you are gonna level up towards 60 soon)

1. Horse/Lizard thingy. This really really should be your next purchase, no doubt in my mind. ~8k
2. Tae Ew chain tunic. ~10k or get the bits and find a smith to do combine to save pp
3. Dusty Sorriz boots. <1k
4. Swap out the Lodi shield for Heiro cloak unless you are raiding HoT or similar all the time. Better yet have both and swap in Lodi when you need resists :)
4. Swap in a Barbed Rubicite Bracer for the spiritcharmer one.

Not sure where you stand on spells, but unless your guild will help get them then you will want to reserve about 80k to get your level 55-60 spells. (Prices will vary hugely between servers though).

After that, you're mostly looking at no-drop bits.
