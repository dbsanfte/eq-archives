Uthgar
Malo(s) on Inc (almost zero aggro spell).

Be carefull with this bit of advice. Its good advice on mobs that dont rampage.

It is true that malo is a low aggro spell but it will still get you on a rampage list if you cast it on inc. Being on a Boss Mobs rampage list can be a quick death for a shaman. Wait about 2 seconds after incoming to malo then start slowing. Generally you will have rampage tanks assigned that are to get on a mobs rampage list. Firing a arrow on inc usually works. the first few people to hit the mob are generally on the rampage list (this includes debuffs).

Goldark
Its already been hinted at, but the answer as always is - it depends. Practice makes perfect. I died the first few times raiding kael arena in my low 50s, from slowing too early. The tank died once, because I didn't get slow to land soon enough (darned resists).

In general I'll open with malo as soon as I see the mob (heavy rampagers excluded). I will then alternate malosini and slow until I get aggro or one or other sticks. Note that the 3 second malosini cast time fits in almost perfectly with the 3 seconds recycle time on slow.

If I get aggro but think I will live I try to channel a quick torpor in, then just malosini for a couple of casts while tank gets aggro back and I heal up. If you get aggro and think you will die anyway, try to channel a final desperate slow attempt. At least you may save your friends, even if you die yourself.

Tailor the above in certain cases:

1) Mob rampages
Discussed by others above. Let the rampage tanks engage first. Then maloxxx. Then alternate slow/malosini(a) until they land.

2) Tank dies / clerics struggle with mana / CH chain starts late
Sometimes a problem on very hard fast hitting mobs. Stand ahead of the force, malo on inc. Run back to tank. Slow before mob gets there. Pray it lands. Stand and if you get a resist, expect to die :) Mainly used on raids with multiple shaman against mobs like rumblecrush, where an unslowed mob engaged on anything less than a top-end tank is gonna lead to a quick death for all involved. Never had to do it myself but I've heard of it being done.

3) No rampage, tank struggles with aggro
Maloxxx after the tank engages. Attempt two or three times, or until it lands, then try alternating slowing with malosini(a). Add in more maloxxx if you get aggro.

Another key with standing next to MT is to not stand past main tank. Malo is very low agro but shooting arrows at Mob is even lower. So the mob will engage whoever is closer. If the main tank moves back behind you, then you are dead.

I do agree that it is very situational. Some mobs need to be slowed as quickly as possible no matter what. In general tanks can usually survive a few secs with an unslowed mob.
Wulf
Standing in melee range when slowing bosses is pretty stupid because of proximity aggro. Get some kind of range focus (Supple Scale Armband is easy to aquire) and stand as far away as possible to get slow on as fast as possible without aggro.

Standing in melee range works at the low-end when mobs hits for less than 1k but when mobs start to hit for 3450 it's better to back away :)
