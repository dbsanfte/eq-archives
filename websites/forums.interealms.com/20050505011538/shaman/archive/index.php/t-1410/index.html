> I assume I should try killing greens melee style with a lot of hp??

Nope. By and large, you'll only get still raises from dark blues. Really, the best thing you can do to raise skill is to melee with a faster weapon, so you get more shots in. However, right now you shouldn't be worried about maxing them so much as keeping them all in the general ballpark.

This was the one thing you could say about hell levels: they took so long that by the time you got through them, your skills would be maxed again :-). Now that the experience curve is smoothed, you won't notice that so much but even so, the increase in required XP will mean that as time goes on, you'll have more time to increase skill. A few points here and there doesn't matter as long as you're making an effort on all your skills.
Bigguss
One thing that might be contributing- low DEX. I seemed to notice an increase in skill gain frequency when my DEX was over 100 rather than it's normal cruddy 75ish level. Of course, haste and a low delay weapon helps (more swings = more chances.)

Also, it's definitely true that the tougher the monster, the better your chance to gain skill. Light blues = almost never... dark blues = rare... white and above = well, less rare. :)

If you're desperate to gain skills, you might want to try the old pet dueling trick (not sure if it still works.) Basically, have a duel with a pet-class friend, and just beat on his pet. You can't get skill gains from PCs, but you can from pets... so just have your friend back off the pet when you or the pet get too low on health... heal... and go back to beating on it.

Good luck!

Congrats :)

I've been trying to max out my weapon skills as well. Hand to hand was easy because it stops at 75, but the other 3 are 200. Piercing was easy (my main weapon), but 1hb and 2hb were low.

First order of business is make sure your dex is as high as you can make it. If you know a shaman with a higher dex buff than you, have them cast it on you. Your weapon skillup rate is thought to be dex related.

Second order of business is to get the best available haste on yourself. You hit faster and therefore get more chances to skill up in a shorter amount of time. I'm presently using Celerity (my own buff) which is a 50% haste, and that works fairly well, but if I can find an enchanter with Speed of the Shissar or VoG, I'm in heaven :)
