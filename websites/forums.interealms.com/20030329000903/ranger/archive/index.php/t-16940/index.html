Viken
If you want to know what the damage increase is just do the math. Its simple.

Example: 30 / 36 Nightmare Bow
Before EQ: Summoned 3 damage arrows
After EQ: Fletched 11 + 4 fire damage arrows

Before EQ dmg ratio = (30+ 3) / 36 = 0.91667
After EQ dmg ratio = (30 + 11) / 36 = 1.13888
After EQ dmg ratio (inc. elemental) = (30 + 15) / 36 = 1.2500

So with a Nightmare bow EQ will give you 24.2 to 36.4% improvement depending on elemental component. With a 33/35 bow the damage increase is 22.2% to 33.3% depending on elemental component. To compare, AM1 is +30%.
