Although at this point, my spells are getting a bit hard to afford, and I would like some suggestions on ways to raise money to buy them.

Bazaar:
Spiderling silks (or shadeling silks) - buy from vendors for a few silver or kill spiderlings yourself and sell for 1-2p each
Low/Medium/High Quality Cat/Wolf/Bear/Rockhopper Pelts/Hides - farm yourself or buy from vendors for a couple gold and sell for 5-10p each (I believe you can still get xp from the mobs that drop these although you may be a little high now)
Spider Silks - buy for gold sell for 2-3p each

Those are the cheapest/easiest items I can think of that you can do at your level but there are MANY more.

I'm not sure if I'm doing you a favor or a disservice by sharing this link with you but EQ Traders Corner (http://www.eqtraders.com/) has all the recipes for things you can make with baking/brewing/tailoring/ect. A lot of the things you sell to vendors for almost nothing are sought after by tradeskillers who will pay you quite a bit to farm for them - as you did with the swirling shadows.

Fishing is also decent way to make money although slow. You can buy bait for 3-4 copper and sell the fish you catch back for a couple silver. It is a really slow way to go but I earned my first few spell circles (any my first pair of shoes!) that way as a newbie before I realized EQ message boards or tradeskills existed. I had a fishing button so I found a rod and used it. :)
