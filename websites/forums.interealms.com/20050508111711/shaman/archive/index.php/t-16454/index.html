Jinheim
Shaman melee is about the same as a druids: That is, nothing impressive. If you want to stand in a mob's face and melee as a shaman, a druid is probably as good a partner as any. They can heal you and give you a nice DS to increase your overall DPS.

However, if you really want to melee stuff, why not go with a melee class? Any melee combined with a druid will be a perfectly viable duo, but once you hit pop, you're going to want to be able to slow. Thus, I'd suggest a beastlord.

Another good partner with a druid would be necros. You do awesome DPS as a necro, and the druid's own dots (which aren't shabby at all) and nukes (also not shabby) can do some pretty impressive DPS.

Also, possibly the best partner for a druid, is a chanter. Even at lower levels, a charm pet controlled by a druid's snare, along with heals when your chanter takes a few hits, is an awesome combo. When you hit 64 and get Command Of Druzzil, enchanter + druid is easily the most powerful duo in the game.
