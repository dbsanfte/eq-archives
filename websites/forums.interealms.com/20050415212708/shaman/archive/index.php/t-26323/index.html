general consensus in the community typically is:

lvl to 62-65, stop for canni5, then push to 70.

as you get into higher levels (post-65) money will start to become less of an object, as you'll start getting large upgrades from normal group mobs, if you just push for groups in the right zones.

when I entered OoW, I was a 65 bazaar geared shaman. by the time I hit 70, almost all of my slots were no-drop gear that were much better than what you could buy in the bazaar. shortly after, I joined a raiding guild, and my gear improvements since have been phenomenal.

getting to 70 is worth the effort by itself. there are several examples here on the boards. lvl is still greater than all.
