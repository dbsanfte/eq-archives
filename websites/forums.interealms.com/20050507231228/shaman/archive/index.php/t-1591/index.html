greetings!

I spent nearly 3 years on RN, with over 20 characters over 30+ durring my stay. I found it to be the best server out there. forever, it was the "unknown" server. never had a big influx of people or deflux of people.. always easy to make friends and not worry about them takin off, etc. I was in several great guilds.. one of wich "Brew Masters", was my longest/favorite.

some of the bigger guilds (from memory):

Avatars of Discord - was at one point the uberest guild on RN.. but deminished a bit as time wore on. unknown to me if they are still together. (59+)

Eastern Keep - known (when i was active) as the most populated guild on server.. started as an eastern/europian guild, then bloomed into 1/3 the server population :P. They had a ton of ubers, and tons of 30+. they were a controversial guild and had quite a few bad apples that squabbled their rep. but they had many good people in it too. (51+)

Axiom Shift - GREAT guild, tied for first with Brew Masters in my favorites. I have lots of old friends in it, and they treat you right. Look Burdusk up, hes a RL buddy of mine and 60 rogue. tell'em Peirce Braznin sent ya :) (57+)

Dol Amroth - second favorite guild.. very good guild with lots and lots of good people. very into raiding and highend encounters. good bunch of people! (57+)

Fin De Cycle - again.. tied for first with BM/AS. before i left EQ (not by choice) i was transfering into FDC from BM. great guys, lots of fun and are the sister guild of Dol Amroth. both are great and do lots of fun stuff. give Chantisis and Fluffanutter a call.. good guys and should give ya any advice ya need. tell'em Peirce sent ya :) (54+)

Echos in Eternity - not too familiar with this guild, but they are pretty high up there.. and my oldest friend in EQ is/was in it. talk to Drogoe Bagginz and tell him Peirce Braznin sent ya :)(59+)

and then a couple good middle ground guilds for you to maybe get into are:

Brew Masters - my favorite guild, TONS AND TONS AND TONS of good people in it, they are very well put together. They all strive to help eachother and others out and dont bicker and fight all the time. They pride themselves on not being of the UBER mind set, yet like to do alot of higher end stuff. They are a very casual guild and support those that cant spend all their free time GRINDING!!! hehe. call Maxo, Faelin, Blackrose or Perdus and they'll help you out. tell them Peirce Braznin or Prestto Chainjo sent ya. (tell'em i said hi too :P). they have lots of good allies (Troubles Band my fav of the allies) and they all help eachother, very non greedy. they balance out their raiding, to support the younger half of the guild.. they make life fun for all levels. (30+)

RN is a great server, but im biased. so i hope you enjoy your stay. and it couldnt help to call up some of the people i told you about. ill be back playing in a month or two when i get a new comp. youll probably be 60 by then :P
