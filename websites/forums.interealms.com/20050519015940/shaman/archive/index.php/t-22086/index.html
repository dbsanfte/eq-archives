Grafsha McDaughts
necro/druid combo = aggro kiting or root rotting. Necro grows without need for KEI; druid provides regen. Both can snare, though recommend use necro snare (key to nec maintaining aggro.)

Currently I duo with a necro/druid combo aggro kiting PoP zones with quick and efficient kills. Without KEI or virtue on either toon (self buffs and PotG/Pot9/regen/buff pet with necro haste/druid buffs) they can constant pull with no downtime.

Druid brings portability also (no real biggie anymore) while necro brings corpse summoning / rezzing in a pinch (those far off, hard to convince a cleric to come rez you kind of places.) Toss in necro FD when/if it goes bad and you don't even need to travel back to the bodies ... just clear aggro and rez.

Combo of druid dots (and nukes) and necro dots is massive dps. At higher levels both can charm (undead and animals) and both can DC pets.
