Jinheim
Click on "update this character".

Where it says this:

"Bonus applied to your charm item: % Value between 0 and 200"
Put in the amount of mana on your charm MINUS any bonuses you get from your adventure stone. i.e. if you have 6 mana from your adventure stone and your charm shows 46 mana, put in 40. It should be a multiple of 10.


Bonus applied to your augment charm item: % Value between 0 and 200
Put in the amount of mana (or hp, they are the same) on your adventure's stone.

Check and make sure you entered the correct starting stats and fill in any applicable AA (natural durability, innate stats) that you have. If you looted any +6 (or whatever) augs during an adventure, be sure to apply those.
