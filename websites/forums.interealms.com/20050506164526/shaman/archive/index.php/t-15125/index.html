Weyalan
Unfortunatley, you will pretty much need to do this solo. In order to get skill raises in defence you need to be taking hits (well, strictly speaking, you need to be getting missed!).

The idea is to try and find dark blue con mobs that hit fast, but not hard. Ideally, you dont want to slow them, because slow mobs = less hits = less skill raises, but realistically, if your AC is not high, the chances of finding mobs that you can tank unslowed are low.

Using the "cripple" line of debuff skills is of marginal benefit in this exercise.

Maximising your AC is sensible even at the expense of other stats (such as wisdom)

Do it at a zone line, so that you can zone at low health / mana

Toggle you pet taunting on and off so that you can give aggro to / get aggro from your pet as needed for healing yourself
