The way combat is -- very fast, lots of button pressing -- i think 2-boxing will be very tough.

Combat is very fast, but with that said, I do have a friend that twoboxes a mystic and a swashbuckler right now in EQ2. He does pretty well with it and it seems like a good pairing. I've done a lot of duoing with some monk friends, and it's been a pretty formidable combo. The monk can dodge/shrug off a lot of hits; while it's not a replacement for a plate class, they can tank and take a considerable amount of hits.

As far as the mastery stuff goes, yeah, those are separate from the sub-class spells. Anyone can obtain skeleton master's smite, zombie master's smite, etc. For melee classes, it actually gives the player a kick; ie, "skeleton master's kick" that you can use against any skeleton-type mob with a 1 min refresh. Currently, mastery spells are in for quite a few mobs... bixies, centaurs, giants, treants, elementals, skeletons, zombies, shadowed men.. etc. Some of the drops are quite rare, but these mastery skills are meant to be completed over the life of your character. You're not gonna run off and buy the mastery tome and farm skeletons until you're done. You could, but it would take a while. The mastery spells scale infinitely over level (increase in power as you gain levels) - so no matter what level you get it at, it will do damage based on where yer at.

It's a cool, fun little extra bonus attack.
Hope that helps.
