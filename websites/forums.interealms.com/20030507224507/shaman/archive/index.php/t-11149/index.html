Mozeley
I found a great place to solo last night.

The zone out bit Plane of Justice gives you 1 percent per kill, which for me is something that has been unheard of since soloing at 45 or so in Dreadlands.

Just make sure that the only mobs you pull are the Diseased rats, as anything else is likely to pull a Sentry of Justice. Sentrys are doable, but they path strangely and are very likely to bring one of their Sentry mates with them.

When you pull the rat, nullify Magic on it (they sow themselves),and just make sure they don't go anywhere near the corridor, as you're very likely to get Adds that way.

And at the end of the day, even if you get a situation that you can't handle the zone out is only two seconds away at the most.

Happy Hunting.
