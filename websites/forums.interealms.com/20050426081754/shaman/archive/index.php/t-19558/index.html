Wodon
Loam encrusted gear from the Hole is nice for int casters - robe has some godly mana on it. When I was twinking out my wizard just recently, went through slaughtering elementals for 2 or 3 hours with a 65 chanter friend, got about 6 pieces. In theory they could drop from the entrance mobs, but the drops we got were further in. stay away from golems, gargoyles, & constructs. bad loot, mitigate slow, & very MR.

There are some nice int caster drops in COM that I soloed at 57/8 Mysterios glowing sleeves & glowing something necklace (sold both for 2 or 3k each). The named mobs that drop stuff can spawn more or less anywhere, got a couple of named while camping spectral courier for epic. only mob that gave me real probs was Mighty golem - resisted slow 3x & I had to run for it.

Luggald Assassin in Dulak drops a nice int caster piercer, captain of the stormwave drops a nice int caster 1HB, but he's a rare spawn, & his common is mellee legs.

Do the Fine Antique Ring & Fine Cut Diamond Inlaid Mask quests in PoK with him too, the focus effects are nice, & will serve you till lvl 50.

Do the havester Quest in the Temple of Soulseek Ro - Droppable, stats aren't that good but it looks way cool.

Park him at the entrance of Velks, kill the Fierce blizzard hunter, & let him loot the ear (after you get one for yourself) that room is the best solo XP I've had around that level anyway. I posted about this somewhere in this forum.
