I would actually recommend a slightly higher starting level. I was there as my necro at 38 and it was rough even with two people in my group that was higher level than me. It is definitely a nice dungeon, but make sure you have an evac or know the way out before you set up shop. Ludaian
Droga can be tough, but once you learn it's secrets you're set. Moving through Droga can be difficult. Everything there will agro you and there are lots of roaming patrols. The good news is nothing there sees through invis. What we did was invis until someone dropped, killed the 5 (or 10) gobs that they picked up, rinse, repeat until we got to where we wanted to be. My advice would be to get a map, and know what you're after and head straight there. It's a very cool dungeon, but it can be deadly.

There are a lot of nice drops there, but in my experience they can be stingy depending on what you're after.
