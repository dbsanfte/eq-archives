Rafeeky
I dont know if this will help, but I was having freeze up problems due to my AGP controller driver in the device manager. I use a Radeon 9700 and was getting constant lockups in EQ when playing or camping. I fixed this situation by changing the device driver for the AGP controller to PCI-PCI Bridge which is its "generic" setting in Windows. I lost some performance but not much, and it never locks up anymore. I think I lost about 10 fps in EQ.

Note: Other games work fine with either the AGP controller or the PCI-PCI Bridge. Just EQ is the black sheep.
