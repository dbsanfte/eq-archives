Replace a fungi tunic? Not until 60 and torpor.

JB boots and arms instead of those, I guess; possibly compensate for the loss of WIS by swapping in a +WIS ring if you are having mana pool size issues. There are some nice shields with at least that much AC but also some other stats, like a Bone Braced Buckler. Look into Imbued Granite Spaulders for the shoulders, or an Obulus Death Shroud for the mana conservation focus effect. Barbed Rubicite anything is good, has AC and WIS. And the Range item could be as simple as a Howling Harpoon to get some extra WIS or a Goranga Idol for extra AC, that one doesn't have much going for it.
