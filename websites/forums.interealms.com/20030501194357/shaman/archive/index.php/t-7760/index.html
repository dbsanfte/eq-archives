The viability of alchemy (which would be the subject of your post rather than "quick question") is discussed about every other thread in this alchemy forum.

There are many complaints because it's expensive to skill up alchemy to the high end, but there is little profit to be made there. If all you want is profit, skill up to 40 or 50 and make Blood of the Wolf (Sow) potions. If you want practicallity, skill up to 103 to be able to make Shrink (Ant's) and Invis to Undead (Unlife) potions. If you want to make gate potions for guildies, skill up to 180.
