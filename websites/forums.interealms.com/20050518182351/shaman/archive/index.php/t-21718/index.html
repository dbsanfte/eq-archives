Valsuvious
well, back when it was first introduced, the crit % was closer to 9%. I think that it's currently at (or at least was supposed to be) 14% or around that. That is each tick. So the more dots you have on the mob, the more times you could see the crits.

Personally, I've had a few bane casts of over 5000 total dmg before. It doesn't happen often, but it can happen.

As to overall dmg, i don't know how much it will add. So much determines on how long the mob lasts and if bane runs the full length. Part of the problem is that crit dots do bring extra agro, even though they aren't supposed to. So, although you will be doing more dmg, you will also be getting alot more agro.
