Bigguss
I played around with magelo a bit (since it seems to track hit points very accurately) and it looks like the formula (for shamans) for computing additional hp from stamina is pretty simple:

Each point of additional stamina will give you (level/10)*0.5 hit points.

At level 10, you get 0.5 hp per additional stamina point.
At level 20, you get 1.0 hp per additional stamina point.
At level 30, you get 1.5 hp per additional stamina point.
At level 40, you get 2.0 hp per additional stamina point.
At level 50, you get 2.5 hp per additional stamina point.
At level 60, you get 3.0 hp per additional stamina point.

Hope this helps

So for a level 60 character, RH (assuming it does the full +50 Sta) gives:

Warriors: +300 HP
Priest: +150 HP
Caster: +100 HP
