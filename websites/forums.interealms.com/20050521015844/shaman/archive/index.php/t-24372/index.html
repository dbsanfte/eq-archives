The subject is the question. How does your guild distribute the new OoW runes? My guild just hit the tip of the iceberg on theses runes and can't seem to come up with a way to hand them out in a fair manner. Some want to roll, some want them to be charged like loot, some want them to be assigned to top raiders. So I'm here for some anwers. Help me out please. :D
Raccoo
So far only gotten the 69 runes to drop with the guild I'm in. At first we let everyone roll that attended the raid. Since several people (mainly the ones that were 69+) were angered that level 66 people were rolling on runes they wouldn't be able to use for months, we had a poll and now only the people who can use the runes can roll. It makes more sense that those who can use them immediately get them first. At least to me it does. Although, I've not been lucky enough to get one yet. hehe...
Ryam
In my guild spell drops are randomed according to raid attendance. I kinda like the system, as it rewards those who raid more, and yet doesn't force you to spend DKP on spells. :)
Clawmarks
We do alpha order no matter if its 69 or 70. So Bards first and Wizards being last on that list till it comes back around. Then with in the class themselves its left up to us to decide how to handle the runes. We just decided that those on the most and been in the guild the longest get the first runes and on top of it in the case of the shaman it takes 3 runes to get our first group Wushi spell..so we each passed till one shaman had the group version.

Its working nicely. Couple things to keep in mind though. You can single group lvl 70 runes if you know where to hunt and same can be said with 69's. It greatly helps you if you dont solely count on raids for your runes and also that your guild set aside some time and actually go after the rune droppers for a couple nights here and there to get the ball rolling.
