Banuddar
This is not intended to be one of those "walking 7 miles through the snow" posts, so hopefully it will not come off that way.

To me, the most fun part of the game was levelling my first character to 60. Sure, there is still the raiding stage of the game which does keep me playing, but, in my opinion, nothing can compare to the fun of the lower stages of the game.

Lately, however, Sony has dumbed down EQ's leveling system so much that people can now blow through this stage of the game entirely. The introduction of KEI was a major factor in this process, as was the introduction of new zones like Paludal Caverns and basically anywhere in PoP, where people can get experience much, much faster than ever before, with less risk than ever before as well.

Previously, stuff in everquest was actually scary and challenging; corpse runs are were long and difficult, and getting your experience back was often impossible. As time progressed, however, EQ's challenge magically disappeared- in PoP zones, corpse runs are a thing of the past, and even in older zones getting a corpse summon is usually a breeze. Even finding a rezzer is much easier, now that practically every cleric past 55 has a rez stick.

Getting from 51-60 took me months of experiencing, and now, with these new zones, you can feasibly do it in 1 month or less, totally skipping over some of the most fun stages of the game.

Almost nobody bothers with quests anymore until higher levels. I haven't heard of a person trying to create a totemic piece in over a year. These sorts of things were what made the game fun for me, and now they are totally ignored because they are not necessary.

Now, don't get me wrong, I do realize that this is the nature of this sort of game. As new equipment, skills, and zones are introduced, previous content will obviously become easier to complete. However, with LoY, this will happen like never before.

First of all, let's look at the new "mini-torpor" that shaman get. This, combined with 4 hour KEI, will make it so the shaman from levels 45-59 literally has unlimited mana, and will make the game SO much easier at those levels. I do believe that they should make the level 60 torpor easier to acquire, but Stoicism is totally unecessary at those levels, and will only make EQ less challenging than it already is.

New zones for the 45-60 level range- why??? Isn't getting sickeningly accelerated experience at those levels in Planes of Power enough? People are already blowing through these levels extremely rapidly due to the previously mentioned reasons.

So what is my point in writing all this? To be honest I'm not sure, I know it is too late to change anything, so it is mostly just to vent my frustration with how they are ruining the progression in everquest. Soon, 90% of the people playing this game will be level 60+, and all of the raid content will be even more overly contested than it is currently. And tons of older content will be ignored entirely, simply because these expansions have made it so that there is no reason to bother with it.

EQ should focus on establishing new ways of progression at the higher end, rather than simply ruining the fun and challenge at the lower end.
