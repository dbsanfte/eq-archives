Shaman is a necessity for malo and slows and the hp buffs.

A main tank is now needed, being paladin, SK, or Warrior. I'm not sure if snare is actually needed or not in HOT. Keep that in mind.

When i have done 1 group HoT our group consisted of...

1 warrior
1 bard (tash sticks are good to have here)
1 shaman
1 enchanter
2 clerics

The main problem that we ran into was that there wasnt alot of dmg output so it would take some time to kill... espically the wurms.

It is doable with everyone in thurg lvl equipment but you will struggle at first but you will see it get easier as you get SS armor.. (espically on your tanks). Also be sure that your casters have adequate resist gear.... if not they will be eatting alot of dmg from ae's and have to be healed too much.

Like it has been said before DRAIN all the casters.... but one thing to remember before you slow/malo/ooc ect is that the cleric mobs... espically the white wurms have casted aego on themselves. We usually cast dispell bout 2 times to make sure to strip the buffs from it... makes it easier to kill.

We have our clerics bound at ToV zone in so if it gets hairy they just gate and come back and rez us after everything has left the camp.
taraddar
I've done Hot with 1 group before with out much difficulty several times.

The group has varried a bit but the basics are
Lvl 60 enchanter
lvl 56+ cleric
lvl 60 shaman
lvl 60 warrior
+ 2 others.

The 2 others have varried, been a 57 ranger, a 60 druid, a 60 rogue, lvl 60 bst, and a couple others.

For the most part the group isn't a really uber group since most are peoples twink not thier mains. The notable exception are me as the 60 shaman and the 60 rogue we had on a couple trips.

You need a good tank, warrior probably strongly recommended if going non uber. Our's is in mainly dorf armor with a pair of good buyable weapons (sod and horn of ssraga now but lesser when we started).

Need to have a cleric, gotta have ch, no question. Have done it with a lower than 60 cleric with out too much problem.

Need to have 2 of the 3 following for buffs/debuffs.

Shaman with malo, enchanter, bard or mage with mala. We've always had me as a 60 shaman with malo and an enchanter but you could still do it with a 60 mage with mala and a bard if the bard had an epic or better to haste the group and thier new lvl 60 mana drain song.

Throw on 2 more, dmg dealers, evacers and a feign puller all aren't bad ideas.

Things will take a while to kill but as long as you slow everything you should be able to keep mana up for long enough even with just one cleric.

2 groups with
2 clerics
1 bard
1 shaman
1 enchanter
1 warrior
1 monk
and what ever other misc people you want to add on should be more than pleanty altho you do want people to be as high a lvl as possible with descent resists if possible. Some things have some pretty mean ae's.

It may take some wipe outs or bad pulls at first but once you have the pulling down pretty well singles aren't bad to handle with that type of group.
