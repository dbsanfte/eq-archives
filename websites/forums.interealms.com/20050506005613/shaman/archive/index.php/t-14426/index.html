Thorzion
level 24 you get cannibalize. With it you can turn HP in to mana. After that, your life as a shaman will never be the same. After a hard pull other castes are at 30 ro 40 mana, you are at 60 health but FM. heh...

At 51 you get turgurs insects, which is the spell that everyone thinks of when they think SLOW. At 60 it slows mobs by 75%.

At 60 Torpor makes its appearance. You can heal yourself after canning very rapidly with this spell.

Those are the spells I think of when I think really good spells. True, we get some great buffs and regens. Heals are substandard with what you had as a cleric.

Cragael
Also...

At 24, you get Regeneration. Combined with Canni, it will really change the way you play.

At 29, you get Quickness (your first haste), Targar (not your first slow, but the first real one with a whorthwile effect for the level) and your first Bearform

At 34, you get your first pet and your first direct HP buffs.

At 39, you get Togor, the first slow that will make the tanks and the healers go "Damn!".

At 44, you get a mini-torpor called Stoicism.

Shamans get one of the most satisfying spell progression in the pre-50 game, really :)

HarpuaKB is close but not quite right.

Focus Items > Regen > AC > HP > ..... before PoP and / or torpor

When you get to PoP and the mobs have high attack ratings then AC no longer mitigates damage and HP becomes more important.

When you get torpor, or at a minimum stoicism, then the importance of regen items is significantly reduced.

Another milestone is lvl 45 when Jaundiced bone armor start to work. The Jaundiced Bone Bracer with a clicky DD spell is a god leveling tool from 45 to late 50s.

Don't join the HP is God crowd in your early levels. Having some HP is nice but if you concentrate on AC instead of HP until about level 50 or 55 you will be much more efficient on the way up.
