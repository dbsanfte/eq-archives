Hi all,

I remember we used to have a shaman wish list sticky for ages, but it eventually vanished. I thought it was a well thought out, collective compilation, and was updated a lot.

Perhaps it's time for another one. Off the top of my head, I've done a small list. I'm sure there's many more that can be added. Perhaps someone better informed then me should try and get a stickey put up. :)



- Still waiting for post 60 STR/DEX spells to be removed.

- Alchemy, improved but still not rewarding enough. Definately not on par with other trade skills, not even the other class specific ones.

- Forage Herb, this has been a good idea for years. Would make alchemy profitable and if done correctly not overpowerd. My personal suggestion is that the more expencive the herb, the harder it is to find. So say a 100pp herb could only be found in the bottom of Lower Guk, or deep in HS? Each zone could have a specific herb allocated to it, otherwise people wil just get bogged down with random herbs they don't need. This could maybe be an AA skill. This would be totally seperate from normal forage. I think even from a Roleplaying PoV, this is a good idea. Only drawback I can see is coding.

- Critical DoTs, we're not nukers we're Dotters. Crit AA's are of limited use to us, how about we get the DoT equivilant? Even at the expence of losing our nuke AA's?

- Rabid bear, this is a gimic AA and serves no real practical use. Something radical should be done to it maybe?

- Shaman melee, this is more a personal request. But I've always felt that the melee penalty on shamans is far too great. It doesn't need to be made much better, just less gimped.

- Spell drops, at least before SoE had the excuse that our top of the range high level spells (topor, malo, VoB, etc.) are soooo powerful that they have to be limited greatly in their drop rate. But let's face it, with some of the great stuff out there now for other classes, and the fact that we get even better versions which are easier to get at 65. We have a stronger case now then ever before to get thier drop rate massively improved.

- Healing? Controversial one this one, personally I'm happy with our healing the way it is, I don't even like healing. But many others seem to be unhappy with it.

- Affliction focus for more then just DoT's? OK, most of us grudgingly admit now that it shouldn't have been there in the first place. But the amount of time it was left in, only to be removed unannounced left many people bitter. Perhaps some compensation is in order or maybe just reversing the decision? (again).


Some of these are personal ideas, some other seem to be the general concensus. I'm not trying to speak on behalf of all shamans here, just trying to get the ball rolling on putting our collective minds to positive use in this way again.
