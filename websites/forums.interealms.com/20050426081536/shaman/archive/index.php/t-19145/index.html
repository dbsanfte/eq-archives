Kamidari
I 2-boxed him a few weeks back. Was a little scary until I got him slowed, since his unslowed melee + 1k ae do some pretty fast damage.

I think I was maybe fighing him in the wrong place though... I've seen pics of him up on the beach, but I tried to pull to the rocks (since not much in CS likes me, and I really didn't want an add), but he wouldn't come out of the water, so I was constantly trying to keep him right at the edge of the water so I could hit him, but didn't have to keep my shaman in the water for heals to land. He summoned my warrior in fairly often though, so it was rather annoying.

Anyway, it was a long fight, but not really too mana intensive once he was slowed, and I was only self-buffed, so I'd think with raid buffs and maybe slightly better gear and/or the hp/defensive AAs, he'd be soloable.
