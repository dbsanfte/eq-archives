I LIKE EverQuest

I LIKE the friends

I LIKE the teamwork

I LIKE spending 12.95 a month for something that provides more enjoyment than one single dinner and a movie

I LIKE planning things, following through, and tasting victory

I LIKE reading up about all the lore and storyline in the game

I LIKE that moment when I THINK I'm gonan die, but get that awesome rush when I win in the end

I LIKE the feeling of progress when I've wasted 100+ hours but finally got the last piece of my quest

I LIKE clicking combine and have an item appear on the end of the cursor or a text message show up saying, "You have gotten better at Tailoring (250)"

I LIKE seeing: "You have gained experience! Welcome to level 60"

I LIKE going back to places he's been and feeling incredibly powerful, like Sauron blowing through the enemy hordes

I LIKE to see my armor get better, my skills go up, and being able to kill bigger things

I LIKE to travel and see all the wonders of Norrath, Luclin, and the planes that I've never seen before

I LIKE experiencing end game content that most people in the game never see

I LIKE the family

I LIKE my guild

I LIKE EverQuest
