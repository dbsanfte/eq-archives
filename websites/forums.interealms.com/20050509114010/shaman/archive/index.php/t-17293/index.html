Juujuu
Assuming you have Grummus' PP hailed for flag, and Bertox's PP hailed for flag, this is how you get that flag fixed up:

Go to PoD entrance.
Hail Adler outside of it (last name is somethign like Fehrstel)
say "what ward"
Hail him again.
Go to the seer and unlock memories.
Go to his brother in the sick room in potranq.
Hail his brother (named is soemthing like Elder Fehrstel) to get a character flag.
Go to the seer and unlock memories.
Go back to his brother again and hail him.
If everything goes ok, you should get a character flag and he will say "welcome back friend...".

I used to think that Saryrn's flags were somehow intermingled Bertox, but I'm not sure. I know you can get Bertox flag without having any of Saryrn's flags. I think you need Bertox to get Saryrn though.

If you are having problems with Saryrn's flag, first make sure you have the hedge trial completed, you've killed Terris Thule, and you've gotten the flag from the Keeper of Sorrows.
Go to Adroha Something in the sick room in potranq.
Hail her.
Say "tortured by nightmares" or something like that for character flag. (if you hail her and talk to her, you can find out the trigger text for yourself for the flag).
Go to the seer and unlock memories.
Go to the sick room again and hail the guy lying on the floor next to Adroha.
You should get a character flag.
(At this point you *may* need full Bertox flag to progress. I'm not sure though)
Go to Fahlia Something and hail her.
Say "what plane of torment".
Say "I will go".
You should get a character flag.
Go to the Seer and unlock memories.
That should complete your Saryrn flag.
