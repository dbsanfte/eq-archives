Bigguss
AC, HP, and WIS are all nice, but they're not your most important stat.

Your most important stats are Regeneration and Mana-free damage. Think about it, the more mana you have, the more time it takes you to med it back... you are looking for a way to get your mana back faster- and that's what regen is all about.

Here are my recommendations (admittedly a bit for the future, since they're a bit too pricey.. . of course this might just act as support for a recommendation not to spend plat on minor gear upgrades right now.) :)

1. Get an iksar regen BP. 5 hp/tick = 3000 hp/hour = 1500 mana/hour (if you're a cannibalizing fiend.)

2. Alternatively (if you can find a reasonably priced one) get a fungi staff. They definitely have limitations (they snare you, the effect needs to be reapplied every 5 minutes, and it doesn't stack with regen spells) - but it's mana-free Regrowth... 9000 hp/hour = 4500 mana/hour. Stacks with the iksar regen BP or fungi tunic, too.

3. If you spend a lot of time outdoors, get a horse. Get the cheapest horse, and turn the models OFF. Turning the models off allows you to cast SoW on yourself without it being overwritten by the horse speed effect (and you don't have to look at a horse-head all the time.) While on a horse, if you are standing still and not auto-attacking, you are medding. Even while standing. Even while casting. The ability to med while standing/casting is huge.

4. Your longer-term items are (of course) the JBB and fungi tunic.

A few other recommendations that won't break the bank:

Ditch the fingerbone necklace and get a Black Alloy Medallion - stats are pretty good, it's cheap (couple hundred PP, you might even break even on this one), and it will make every spell you cast under level 44 cost 10% less mana. That's a good thing!

Ditch the plat ruby veil and get an Arctic Wyvern Mask - you should make a few plat on this deal. You get a nice balance of AC and WIS with the mask.

Wrist: Sell the RMB and replace it with a Chipped Bone Bracelet (CBB) (with the CBB you get "Affliction Efficiency II" - which will cause all DoTs under level 45 to cost 25% less mana to cast. You should make some plat on this deal.)

Feet: JB boots are a nice and very cheap upgrade. After selling the totemic, JB should only set you back another couple hundred.

Rings: Sell your +6 WIS rings and buy some 6/65 rings. Should set you back a few hundred pp total if you get good prices.

Primary: Ditch the spirit tome. Put either a similar-mana weapon there (slime harpoon, jade prod, spine chill, etc.) or a Paw of Opolla (9 ac, +9 wis)

Hopefully the sum of all of those deals won't set you back much.

Your gear goal is to get your AC to 900. :)

Hope this helps.
