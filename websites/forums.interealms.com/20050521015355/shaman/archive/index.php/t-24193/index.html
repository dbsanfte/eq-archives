This was brought up on another thread, and I thought it might be a good topic of discussion, to see how others orginize their hot-key banks, etc, maybe we can get some tips from how others use theirs.

Here is mine:
Bank 1
1. VP, no message, just the hot-key
2. Trushar cast macro w/message
3. Canni 5 cast macro w/message (soon to be gone, Canni 5 just doesnt make a big dent in my hps anymore, so I dont need to warn people too much. Moving this to bank 2)
4. Fast heal cast macro w/message (mostly for raids only)
5. Ancestral Aid (used to be slam, but I find I dont slam much anymore)
6. Stand/sit
7. Working spell set-macro, /mem 1 (I have a spell set named "a". Each event or group, I decide what my "base" set will be, mem it, and save it as "a". Keeps me from having to swap back to the spell-set bank all the time, and "a" insures it will always be spellset 1)
8. /assist. Just plain assist, so I can assist-heal, although thats not as necessary with HoTT anymore. With the addition of HoTT, I usually edit this now to /assist XXXX (whoever I want to assist, MA, or off-tank if I am his slower). I have also found this invaluable when grouping. It seems a TON of the OoW models are a total PITA to loot. So I hit F1, F1 (targets pet), 8 (assist pet, targets the corpse), and type /loot. I may macro this soon. It saves a lot of lame click-click-move-click crap.
9. Canni 5 hotkey. So I can see it pop, and I can just use without the macro if needed.
10. Slow message. Once I got Nihil, I stuck the cast right into it, since it almost ALWAYS lands first cast now. If I have any trouble at all, I malos inc, then hit this key.
Bank 2
1. SOW boots
2. Shrink orb
3. mem FoS spell-set (changes one spell to FoS)
4-10. Utility pet, Summon Drogmar, AA and Leader hot keys, like HoTT, Spirit call, pet Attack, Slam (can get there easy if a healer is a problem) etc.
Bank 3
Spell set mem hotkeys, /corpsedrag,
Bank 4
/assist keys pre-set for most of the guilds tanks and off-tanks
Bank 5
/tar keys pre-set for most of the guilds tanks and off-tanks, couple chanters.

I use the Keypad for 90% of my spell casting. Its VERY annoying that the new spell slot is not assigned to 9 on the keypad, but I will admit I have not checked my settings, I should do that. I used to use more macros for re-peating functions like Canni 4, /stand - /cast slow- /sit, etc, but I stopped and most of these now ocupy banks 6-10 somewhere.

Thats about it. How do you set yours up? It should be noted that I do not use a UI that has my inventory in a small window for clickies. If I need a clicky, I drag the slot over and move stuff some. Only 2 real clickies I use are SOW and Shrink anyway.

2. As requested in PM, here are instructions for making a clicky hotkey:

A. Open inventory to show the slot containing the item to be hotkeyed, and have a hotkey with a blank slot (or one to be replaced).
<img src="http://samanna.net/1pix/clicky1.jpg"></img>

B. Left-click-and-hold until a copy of the item (not the original item) attaches to your cursor.
<img src="http://samanna.net/1pix/clicky2.jpg"></img>

C. Left-click to drop the copy onto your hotkey.
