Here is my advice to you, as someone who hasnt been playing for years, like some others. Stay where you are, but build your guild, slowly, a couple people at a time, a couple new raids every other week, etc. Enjoy your current station in the game. There is a TON of fun content between where you are now and Plane of Time, and you would be doing yourself a disservice if you just skipped it.

I took about 8 months off the game, the end of Luclin and the beginning of PoP. When I came back, I joined up with some old friends in a small guild. However, this guild quickly fell apart, because so many bailed out to higher guilds. I held out a while, but eventually I had to make a move. Being one of the more well-known people on the server, with an impeccable reputation and a history of raid leadership and strong ability, I litterally had my choice of guild. There was the guild that was 1 God from Time, the guild that was 2 Gods from time, and then there was this other guild. It had some of my old friends. It was led by a former member of a guild I started during Kunark but that sorta drifted apart. They were in Late Luclin. Just starting Ssra, doing Luclin bosses like Burrower, IV, Shie, etc. Shards were being camped, Emp keys were being gotten. This is the choice I made. The other 2 guilds had done VT while I was gone. They werent going back. They would never do the Emperor again. I didnt want to skip that content.

Eventually, I got my VT key, got a bunch of VT gear, and we progressed nicely. We are now an EP guild, 5 or 6 on the server, 2 gods from Time, but still a very close bunch. We are large, but we recruit very selectively, and have a "One No Vote Your Out" rule on recruits. The 2 other choices I had are well into Gates, one farms Quarm, the other has just recently started farming him.

My point is I would have missed a TON of fun stuff if I had skipped Luclin. Looking back, even camping shards wasnt so bad. It showed everyone what people are made of. The amount of helpfulness and generosity was astonishing. I am so proud to call myself a member of Omani. Guys fully keyed for VT would spend hours helping people with the PITA shards. One bard in particular was known as the "ME Shard Guy". He musta gotten 25 for various people, and that is no exageration. Hours and hours of selfless dedication. The guild is FULL of people like that, too.

Pick your guild wisely. The game is HUGE. I HIGHLY recommend you don't skip from ST to PoTime. That would just shorten the life of the game. A lot of the whining and moaning you read here is people who are just kinda BURNED out after 5 years of this. Take those whines with a grain of salt. The people like you are the reason EQ will probably never die. Don't push it and spoil the fun for yourself.

Good luck, and feel free to send me a PM anytime and ask about anything. Your post made me smile and remember some very very good things that I truly love about EQ.
