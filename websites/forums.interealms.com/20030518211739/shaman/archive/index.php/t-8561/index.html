Hail,

That's a hard call to make. Obtaining my JBB (via auction) was one of my happiest moments when I was in my 40's. I still use it regularly when soloing at 60, but almost not at all when grouped or raiding.

The JBB pricing has been relatively stable on my server, with prices stable at around 15-20kpp (I paid 18kpp awhile ago). Torpor prices vary from about 35kpp (seen ONCE) to well over 60kpp (crazy people) in the bazaar.

I bought Torpor for 40kpp, after getting 20kpp gifted by my guildleader. If I was flat broke when I found the seller, I would have done the swap, but I wouldn't have been happy about it.

The JBB is a practical tool, but not an essential one at 60. Torpor in my opinion is an essential tool at 60, even with Kragg's Mending available from 58+.

I don't envy your decision. I would recommend patience, and group more until you get Torpor. As good as a JBB at 45 is, I'd say Torpor at 60 is an equally (if not greater) profound change in your gameplay.

Good luck,

Naagruz
Torpor changes your effectiveness in a group and solo far more than a JBB will at the same level or higher. If the level cap never broke 60 you might have had some justification for keeping the JBB as a AAxp tool, but it is FAR more effective to get a group in PoV/PoS for exp or to root off and DoT multiple mobs for exp. Sure, it is fun to bust out and go solo something that summons where the JBB is more of an asset, but this is usually not getting you any fast gains in exp.

If you have to choose between the two, ditch the JBB and pick up Torpor for sure. This is not an option on Xeg and the 20-30K from your bracer would leave you about 70K short of a spell. :/ Definitely put out the effort to get your hands on Torpor through the usual routes before just buying the spell in bazaar because free is still the best price.

Some replacements for your wrist slot might be a Barbed Rubicite Bracer, one of the WW dragon bracers, or something questy like the Spirit Wracked cord/ToV armor.
