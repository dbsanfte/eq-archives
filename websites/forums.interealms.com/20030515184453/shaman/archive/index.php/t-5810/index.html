Here's what my guild used to do, before moving on:

-Kill the two static wurms, and *3* wandering drakes/wyverns/racnars.
-Put all non-MT melees up on the stairs, behind the wall.
-Put all casters inside the cubby between the stairs.
-Pull Dozekar into a corner (left corner when facing out of cubby seems to work well), the monk FD's or dies, and the MT gets aggro. CH rotation starts.
-After aggro is secure (10-15 seconds), all other melees come down to engage. A Shaman should Malo the second he is engaged, however, due to low aggro and the need to get him slowed FAST. (Quads for 900 or so.)
-Simply hold him in that corner, and you can heal your MT till the world ends. Don't let him get pushed too far away. Keep him slowed, hold him there, don't let people over-aggro, and you'll take him down easily. And yes, an instant-click in your first slot is immensely helpful. Don't leave home without it.
