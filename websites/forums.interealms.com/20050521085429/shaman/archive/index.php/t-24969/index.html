I have to agree that GOD would be a good place to level overall, given its wide variety of mob types and places to exp either solo or grouped, but I have to disagree (you know me Tordail:) ) on OoW as a good choice for a solo player in their 50s. DS mobs are nothing to play around with, even at light blue/green the Kyv can occasionally proc Chaos Claws on me with resists in the 150s....its a 400ish point nuke, nothing to sneeze at when your level 53, Virtue or no.
I would have to vote LoY as a most excellent solo expansion...MULTIPLE spots for single pull loot camps, exp bonuses in most if not all the zones, decent cash loots, if somewhat mediocre items (at least in comparison), and to top it all off the mobs arent that hard at all.
You can go from 53 to 56 or so in Dulak (more if you arent in any real hurry), then move to the Mines and go to near 60, get your LoY level 44 heal Stoicism which would help your solo power immensly till you get to 60 and maybe acquire Torpor (thank God its cheap these days).
LoY is a oft overlooked expansion, but it shouldnt be :)
