Hail,

Here is my advice. Take it or leave it.
1. Go back to Kubax w/ 2 ivories and ask him to imbue them for you. Take them to a 250 Jewelcrafter and have him make you the Tribunal Imbued Ivory earrings.

2. Sell your old earrings.

3. BUY a slime coated harpoon at auction for 300-500pp. Put it in primary. Do not remove it until you have lots more money to spend & your other areas are all upgraded. This isn't the best piercer, but it is steady & readily available. If you can't find one for sale, a spine chill spear is another good weapon, but I don't know what it sells for anymore. (The proc on the SCS won't work for you for a long time yet, and the slimey is a faster weapon)

4. Sell that horrendous (if culturally pleasing) weapon you have in primary. Repeat for shield. Or give them back to Kubax, which would be more honorable.

4. Buy a clay guardian or charred guardian shield with the money you have left if you can. If not, look for a lesser shield w/ more ac than 10.

5. Go kill stuff & make more money/levels. Come back to post again for more help at level 29.

EDIT: Just saw that ring you have is a actually silver jasper. Thought it was platinum or velium jasper. REPLACE it with a platinum fire wedding ring (5ac/55hp) for now. Velium is too expensive for you right now.

Take care,
