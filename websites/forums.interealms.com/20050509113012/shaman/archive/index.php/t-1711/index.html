You are misinterpreting what they said.

In game terms scribing a spell scroll is transfering it into your spell book, not creating a scroll.

For quite a while it was possible for a Cleric to scribe the Imbue spells for deities other than their own. Now they can't.

My cleric (Tunare worshiper) has the imbues for Mith Marr,Erolisi Marr, Tunare, Brell, and Bertoxx in his spell book. And he can cast them all.... I'd have the rest but I never got around to getting them.

With the change (bug fix really), he can't add the other imbues to his spell book (but can still use the ones that are already scribed).

The quills, parchment, etc are for use in Practice Research for Int Casters.
