What faction is needed to start the quest? IF the first lesser spirit is charmable, and IF you can start the quest with indifferent faction, you can bring along an enchanter buddy to charm the early spirits to let you do the first steps. Most likely you'll have to repeat the early stages several times to make up for your faction losses, but it's worth a shot. If you can just find one step that gives a positive TrueSpirit faction hit that you can do this way, then it's not unrecoverable.

Nytmare
True Spirit faction starts at indifferently. A lesser spirit, Bondl Felligan, a greater spirit, a wandering spirit -- they should all con indifferently initially. You first reach amiably at the step where you hand in the gem to the wandering spirit.

a wandering spirit regards you indifferently
You say, 'Hail, a wandering spirit'
a wandering spirit stops and flashes brightly...
(you give him the gem)
a wandering spirit says 'Oh, it is you, shaman!...
Your faction standing with Truespirit got better.
You gain experience!!
a wandering spirit judges you amiably
