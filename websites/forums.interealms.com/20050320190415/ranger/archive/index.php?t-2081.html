Get out of Karnor Castle at all costs. You are losing at least 50 percent of the potential EXP you can make almost anyplace else. KC is probably the worst Zone Experience Modifier (ZEM) in the game. After level 51, no one should use KC as a EXP zone unless you just like to prolong leveling. (yeah, yeah basement is decent till 59ish , but not likely hes gonna be soloing or doing much duo down there)

Chardok is great place to Duo (but only if you dont want rings or cords, cause it will tank your faction and it takes weeks to repair it).

Since you have a 59 cleric to pull/break with, Zin/Zout for Sebillis. Howling Stones Zin is really good, once you break with cleric pulling its cake and usually unoccupied zone on all servers.

If you have Velious, hit Kael Frostie House. Good till mid 57-58. Otters in Cobalt Scars are good, most quaders leave the shaman otters alone, Cleric can single pull them easy.

If you have Luclin, Akheva Ruins, Acrylia Caverns, plataue in Scarlet Desert, The Grey is really good EXP.
