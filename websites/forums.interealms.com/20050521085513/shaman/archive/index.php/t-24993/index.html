i am not completely up to date atm, but here is a list:

GoD:
Sewers 1-4: instanced zones starting in Barindu
Vxed, Tipt: instanced expeditions, starting in barindu as well
KT: Kod'Taz, the zone you can enter once you won sewers 1-4, Vxed and Tipt
Yxxta: zone behind KT
Ik1-4: Ikkinz Trials, instanced trialzones, starting in KT
Uqua: Instanced trialzone, starting in Yxxta (some call it the RZTW of GoD)

if you have all those flags, believe me, you will know what follows, so i wont make the whole list here.

Omens of War (OoW):
Draniks: Draniks Scar, the lowlevel zone you get ported to by a PoD (Priest of Discord :))
RCoD: Ruined City of Dranik
NC, Causeway: Noble's Causeway, from Dranik
WoS: Wall of Slaughter, you have to run through Draniks, causeway to reach it, good zone for named farming
mpg: Muramite Proving Grounds: you can reach it through WoS, higher level mobs, good exp, some nameds
RS: Riftseekers Sanctum, highlevel zone, you cannot enter before lvl 69, you can reach it through MPG
CoA: Citadel of Anquish: endzone of OoW, only zone that needs flags for access.
