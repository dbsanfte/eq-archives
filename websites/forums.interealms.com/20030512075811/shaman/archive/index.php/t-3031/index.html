Wow, he's right. It's quite a bit of traveling.

I've never found a good map of the entire place. It's huge.

The maze itself is easy. Right, left, forwards back. All you have to remember.

I usually make the trip more from instinct than memory. I'll try typing out some directions, but a good bit could be wrong.

If you zone in from Cobalt Scar, you end up with the room with the fountain. One exit will take you back towards the Wakening Lands. Take the other one. Then, it's something like first right, first left. Or Second left, or something. Eventually you end up in a room with a small pool at the end of it. There is a small stairway on the left side of the room (it blends in with the wall, and hard to see). Take it up. Gonna take a left at the top of the stairs, then the next left. Go through a few doors. I think eventually you'll take a right, and end up on a balcony with a named wyvern. There is a small raised surface that is a teleporter. Either step on it, or jump off the balcony. Takes you to the same place.

Then, start running up the stairs, and follow the passage. Eventually you'll come to an opaque wall. Go through it, puts you in a small natural cavern. Follow it till the end, till you hit another opaque wall. When you go through it, you'll be in the maze.

That is where the right, left, forwards, backwards comes in. Take all rights till you get ported. Then left till you get ported. forwards and backwards the same way. Then you are at the exit of the maze.

Ok, then comes the long part......


There aren't a whole lotta turns after that. Just a long, long stretch of passagewas and stairs. Start running. Lots of doors on the left, you wont need to go through any of them. One thing of note - There are at least 2 invisible pits you can fall through on this run. Probably lots more. I usually use lev and dont worry about them.

Eventually you'll run out of passageway, and have a door on your left. Take that door. Start running again. Follow that passage till it ends. I think that's when you end up in a library. Run all the way through the library and exit the other side. follow that along till you get to a run with a spinning orb. click on the orb to be teleported. Then follow that along till you get to a room with another huge orb. That one doesnt port you. Go around it and out the door on the other side. You'll get to a room with another orb. click on it and you are now in the skyshrine quest givers.
