In summation, the JBB itself rocks, I just wish it had been the result of some epic quest and not just a rare drop of a random mob.

Hail,

I am NOT in an "uber" guild. Or even an uber wanna-be guild.

I bought my JBB at auction after months of saving and a week's worth of solid hardcore selling jewelry w/ a 250 skill jewelcraft enchanter. Took about 2 weeks of INTENSE searching to find a seller once I had cash in hand. The JBB changed my play completely from 47-52. As I level, it has less and less importance, but it is still nice to have. A valuable tool that I don't want to ever give up.

The Fungi I wear is guild equipment on permanent loan to me, received at level 52 I think. It also has made life MUCH easier for me. I don't want to ever part with it either.

My epic was attained at level 53, and could have been completed much earlier except for some faction issues related to patch-changes and raid scheduling for Kirn. It wasn't a high priority for me to finish after I picked up the Fungi, as the REIF (another guild loan since returned) I was poking with was doing just fine. Now that I have had it for awhile, I'm still very happy with my epic. It isn't as valuable as either the Fungi or JBB imo, but it is still one of the items that changes the way you can solo.

I obtained my earring of Solstice this week. I haven't changed the way I played one whit since I equipped it. The extra AC & wisdom means the hollowed pixie rib will rot in the bank forever & I'll likely be replacing some other equipment slot for more ac/hp when I see something nice. But in no way is it game-altering for me.

I don't see myself banking the earring of Solstice any time soon, but to me it is nothing more than a nice equipment upgrade. Very similar to when I replaced my JB gaunts with Coldain Skin gloves awhile ago (thanks for the tip Gloriana!).

I am my guild's pottery expert. I decided when I'd finally get to wear one of these earrings, as the Golden Idols of Tunare were our main obstacle in getting these earrings en masse. After finally taking a break from raiding, I whipped up over a dozen idols, and handed them over to a guild officer. The earrings are much more valuable to other classes, but are still a nice piece of jewelry for shaman.

I saw someone auctioning a few days ago to buy all 9 pieces of POTC for a JBB. I almost asked our guild leaders to make the trade for one of our other shaman. The only reason I didn't was because the other shaman are on probation. If the deal is still available afterwards, I will make the recommendation. The JBB isn't impossible to acquire, but it is a major time-sink. My guild has already invested the time into tradeskills, so POTC components aren't anywhere near as hard to acquire as a rare drop deep in OS for us. YMMV. I think we have a stack of everything but idols in our bank, and our supply of idols is limited by our supply of iron oxides and the spare time of a certain shaman. ;)

JBB>FT1 earring w/ stats.
