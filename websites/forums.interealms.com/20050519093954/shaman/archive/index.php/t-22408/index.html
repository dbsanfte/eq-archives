Hey, about last month, after having the spell since the day it was released, I finally got the sense to make a macro for my Canni IV that was /cast 6 one line, then /pause # the next line, and staggered so forth, to cast it three times.

Now if I'm grouping I never use it, but if I ever happen to be grinding out a few AA's solo or duo, it helps ease the soreness in my finger... BUT...

The pause seems to vary a LOT. At first I set it to 52, which caused the macro to hit the spell about half a second after it ungreyed. Later that day, all of the sudden the macro was not hitting the spell until a good 2 seconds after it ungreyed. So, I adjust it down to 48.5, and it works as it had before.

Then, about two hours later, it's casting it too quick, and I have to put it up to 54 to make it cast regularly.

Since then I've given up trying to keep it so it casts it as fast as possible... I just leave a second and a half buffer there for variance.

Any idea on why the heck it is so variable? Is it a lag thing? Because it doesn't seem to vary but once every few hours and sometimes it won't change at all. Anybody else havin' this problem? What number do you use for your delay, and does it screw up much?
