Forget the trolls, go iksar. Yes, yes, do what looks cool, that's good advice. After all, when you've gone 50 levels, if you're sick of looking at your plain jane Barb, there's some enjoyment taken away from your experience. Iksars are the slickness, looks wise. The ladies love us, too. (Think forked tongue and a tail.) That being said, this thread wouldn't have been started (for the nth time, btw. Search is your friend.) if you didn't want some stat boil downs. XP penalties are laughable, especially when grouping. People have two boxed a penalty and non-penalty char side by side, and when the non penalty hits 60, the other is 25% into 58. Sure, a lot of that is because 59 to 60 is a bitch. And it will be more of a bitch for an iksar. But you've had 59 levels of ass-kicking lizard greatness, and you're playing when most of the hell levels are no longer an issue. Considering what the regen rate will grow to before its usefulness curve begins to head down, which also goes hand in hand with the best base wis of all, it greatly reduces downtime. This will save some groups you get into. Besides, you have to take downtime into account. Depending on your play style, you might actually level faster. If you have to stomp a few more mobs to get to a level (other than 59, see above), isn't that better than sitting down a few minutes longer anyways? The answer is yes, even if the regen doesn't end up saving you time. Killing is fun, sitting is less so. Secondly, as for the jbb, it's really only a nifty tool for about ten levels, and then it becomes a toy. Fun, yes, but not really something to worry about. Especially when you have the AC bonus AND the iksar-only armor. Plus a high agility, although agi's usefulness is.. iffy at best. We also have forage, which wouldn't be too shabby for trade skilling. Then think about newbie zones. Lots of other race's newbies come to FOB and Kurn's before heading to Paludal because it simply rocks. And as a lizard, you can head right over to our well supplied Sham guild whenever you hit the big levels. No books, no being afraid of guards, nothing. You like questing? Here's your cudgel. As for faction, let's be honest. If you have Luclin and PoP, it isn't something that will make you lose sleep. Besides, you can hunt guards in other cities without thought. Can't lose what faction you didn't have in the first place.

So let me summarize the lizard's selling points. Regen, base wis, Cabilis (w/ FOB and Kurn's, plus everything else Kunark has to offer), AC bonus, Iksar-only armor, forage, cudgel quest line. And, as this post opened up with, an Iksar just looks cool. Sure, we don't have the flipping, dancing, prancing, swishing, baton twirling emotes of frogs, but we are bad ass.
