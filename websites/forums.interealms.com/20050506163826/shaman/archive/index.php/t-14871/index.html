I personally 2 box a necro and shammy on stromm(lvl 56 atm) It is still new, not much in baz to buy. It is pretty populated, but the population is very evenly spread out through the levels. Many older servers are more top heavy thus empty as you level up, then you suddenly run into hundreds of people in the upper end zones. I highly reccomend my combo as a change of pace from the melee/healer combo. It is also pretty laid back as I dont have to worry about charm breaking, can get groups easily most of the time if I choose to, and can exp at my own pace when I want to.

Another combo to look into is chanter/druid if you like charming. this combo will easily take you to 65 if you wish, will be a great addition to groups, and can help you pl your friends if you get ahead of them. The one thing that really helps benefit a charming class is snare. You are thinking that the snare neck from a shammy fills this role. It does not!! Snare neck is useful only to stop runners, and not even very good at doing that. Do not count on snare neck to add a usable snare for everyday situations.
