Well it SEEMS like a random occurence but it's not :P

See it's te way ticks are counted.

So... u begin to cast a spell and u MUST make sure the spell lands within the first 1.5 to 2 seconds of the tick.

Here is an example

1...2...3...4...5...6 (Here u get the heal from the spell / damage from any dot currently on you)

HOWEVER

If you get q to LAND ON YOU AFTER second 6 but before second 8 it won't count that tick but u will get healed at the end of it. So by the time second 12 comes around u receive the healing but this PARTIAL tick didn't get counted as part of the 4 standard ticks. It gets ignored but u get the benefit from it.

WARNING: Failure to land the spell withing the first 2 seconds causes screwy things like hp going up but then goign down at the end of tick 5 :( . time it right and u will get it every time. Ideally landit on the 7th second or less as long as it's after second 6 :P

I realize this is harder and harder to do as ppl get funkier aa and items that shorten cast times but if u get your math right u can always extend your torp / q/ stoic by a tick on top of items /aa.
