Here's a few places I've solo'd in 54, not in love with any of them and I would be interested in other suggestions myself:

1) BURNING WOODS: A bunch of stuff here is dark blue to you. I've found 4 hunting spots: (a) the giant fort; (b) The hornets nest; (c) the Sarnaks in NE corner outside Chardock (d) multiple mobs near undead ruins--giants, wurms, gorilla's, skelly's and hornets. Leave Gullerback alone if you soloing, he is HIGHLY magic resistant.

2) SCARLET DESERT: Again, many dark blue mobs. There are several tribes here, you start out non-KOS to all of them. I'd leave most of them alone because it makes the zone a tad safer if you only focus on killing one tribe. I kill the Bloodtribes (drop Acrylia!) and the Sun Reaver fellows. Easy zone to exp. grind in if you can get a port to Twilight Sea.

3) OCEAN OF TEARS: Not great exp, the seafuries are mixed, dark blue and light blue, but this is a no brainer EASY camp where you can go AFK. I find this place just plain relaxing. Some gems drop here that sell well. If you want more cash, claim to Gornit (named HG) camp if it is open and kill clops between Gornit spawns. Save you seafoam for your guildies doing cultural smithing, they will love you.
