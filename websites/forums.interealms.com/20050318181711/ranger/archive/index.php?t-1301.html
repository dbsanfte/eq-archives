Kunark - Just outside CoM in EJ there is a lot to hunt, Trak's Teeth nearby has the hunter/forager cycle. Both give decent exp if you are constantly pulling and killing. Both are underutilized on my server. Burning Woods has plenty of blues, your party could clean up at the giant fort there for several levels. Skyfire has some good points to it as well.

Velious - Kael has plenty to keep you busy, your group might be able to make the arena home for awhile. Soloing is a lot tougher there than animal ridden zones. Lots of competition for that camp usually. EW is going to Light-Blue out on your party real soon. You can solo there for another level with a few dark blues around. The giant fort will keep you happy for a level or two, Max. You might want to explore the Wakening Lands and further on.

Luclin? Scarlet Desert is good for your group for several more levels, you can solo to your hearts content there, scarlet cheetahs especially, search until you find a good camp spot, lots of roamers. Your party can do the tribals to at least the mid 50s Bind in Katta.

Old world? Sol B, Bats and Bugs. Permafrost, the bear pit and Ice giants. Neriak guards.
