What you will probably find is that you are missing 2 new Ui2DAnimation sections from your UI Animations xml file, they are "A_CheckBoxNormal" and "A_CheckBoxPressed". They came in as part of the new Alarm feature. Go into you default UI Animations file and copy the 2 complete sections into your custom one. This worked for me.

Failing that look in the UIErrors.txt file in your EQ directory.
