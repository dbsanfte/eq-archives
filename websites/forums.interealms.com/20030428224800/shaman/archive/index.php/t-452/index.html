weissblau
The only real long-term money maker you will have is sow, unless you have a "patron" requesting specific potions. If you work hard at it, you can sell sow potions for good money. Oh, you can sell shrink and ITU as well, but it takes a long time.

You will fail about 40% of the time if sow is exactly trivial to you, meaning your cost will be as high as 90pp per 10-dose. To do really well, you need to make ITU and shrink so sow is supertrivial. Trivializing shrink (skill 70) drops the failure rate down to 15% or less, so your cost is almost always below 70pp. I would definately advise training up to 70 skill at a minimum, even if sow is all you want to do.

On Rodcet the "standard" price is 110p if you don't want to seem like a gouger, and I'll sell for 100 each if they are buying more than two. I do see them advertised for 120, and you can sell them for that, but you sell more than 10% more at 110, so it's worth taking the price reduction.

I don't work very hard at selling them, but a shaman I know made 4000 plat in 10 days hawking SOW potions. 10 hours a day in FV and EC auctioning. No thanks, I'll be poor 8^)
