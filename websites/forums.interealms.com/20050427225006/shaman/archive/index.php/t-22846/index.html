For a different change of pace, I've been hitting Howling Stones lately. Yes, the north and west wings will have a lot of greens, but there is some decent bazaar loot that drops there and the beastlord will get some decent xp.

Once you get your south key, you'll find the zone more challenging as it will be mainly dark blue with a little light blue mobs. The mobs don't hit especially hard and have relatively few hps, but you will get multiple mobs on several pulls. Cash drops pretty well throughout the zone.

A good goal here is to obtain your Master Key. It is a fairly easy quest in that the drops are all ground spawns. Your only challenge will be getting to them.

Finally, in the east wing you may find Drusella Sathir. She drops the urn for the Spirit Wracked Cords which may be a decent quest for you if you have hunted Droga enough to get your chardok faction up.
