BlargX
I'm an ogre and I love it. No frontal stuns is a great ability.

However, a lot of people talk down the power of regen. Regen is a huge help at least until you get torpor, and really doesn't hurt after, either. I've played a couple of ogres to 65 and one into the 50's, a troll warrior for a while, and an iksar monk that I've kept at 60 for a long time as well as a human monk, and a 54 druid. Regen was a huge help to the monk in leveling compared to the human, and to the troll vs. the ogre warrior. Seriously, it was like night and day how fast I leveled with regen toons. Free health is a really great thing. It far more than makes up for the extra experience penalty you get when you choose a regen toon.

Eventually, regen starts to count for less and less -- when you're VERY high up and have VERY good spells and are starting to get some really good gear. But it never sucks -- my ogre wouldn't be caught dead without his regen spell on, and it makes leveling much easier and faster. It's the fashion to talk down regen these days, but regen and the mana and hitpoints you can get from it will undoubtedly do many good things for you, even save your life here and there along the way to 65.

I'm quite happy with my ogre -- I made him when just about nobody made ogres and troll shamans were almost the only things you saw besides barbarians, and even if only in my mind's eye, he still feels unique and uniquely cool to me. But I also remember how great it was to have regen on the troll warrior and iksar monk I made(who I still have), especially compared to the ogre warrior and human monk I made.
