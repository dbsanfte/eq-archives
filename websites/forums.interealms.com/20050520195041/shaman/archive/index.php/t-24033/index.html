Weyalan
Go to any Priest of Discord in any city, hail him and then say "I wish to go to discord" - he will then port you to Dranik's Scar.

There are wandering mobs that path close to the port in point. also, some people will train mobs to get to the port out point, so don't assume that you will be in a safe, aggro free point when you zone in. The mobs are green cons to a 65 but even so....

To get back, go to a priest of discord in Dranik's Scar and say "I wish to go home"
