The ring is actually the key to get to the emporers area in ssra and so is likely to be fairly camped if you have any uber guilds looking to get him and into VT.

As for the actual quest it's self.

You need a bag off the taskmaster in the basment. Uncommon drop off any of them.

You need an idol off the commander from the first floor.

You need a ring from a different commander on the second floor.

You need an insignia off the general, arbitor or adviser on the third floor.

All of the items other than the bag always drop off thier mob. The third floor mobs are a full guild raid in difficulty. They are all unslowable. The general hits hard and has alot of hps. Need a good number of clerics in a CH chain for him. The Arbitor as a mean ae gflux with a 1100 point nuke component. Not too bad when your prepared for him but still hurts and great potential for adds.

You take the 3 other peices and combine them in the bag to get your ring (key). The ring is interesting because it is a nice enough peice to be worth wearing once you've made it but it isn't worth the effort by it's self imo. If your looking for a nice ring to quest for check out the chardok ring. Simular stats and alot easier to compleate altho still time consuming.
