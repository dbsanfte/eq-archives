From my understanding, the light velium bow will be the better choice for trueshot damage. I recall hearing that trueshot damage takes into account the delay of the bow in its damage calculation. A very low delay bow wont have as much damage bonus.

The windsabre is an awesome bow for getting your archery skill up though, and will probably be more damaging when your not using trueshot.
