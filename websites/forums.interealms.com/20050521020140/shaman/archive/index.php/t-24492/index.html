You don't use it to cure poisons.

You don't use it to cure anything with counters.
You use it to cure things that can't be cured.
Examples of this are maloXXX, snare, boil blood line, cripple, any pretty much any type of spell effect that you might dispel to get rid of it.

Some spell effects don't get removed with RC.
Some seem more resistant than others.
The "remove detrimental (X)" indicates how many attempts the spell effect has to remove the debuff.
RC1 will get rid of malo, but not all the time.
RC3 almost always gets rid of malo due to the 6 chances it has.
