Brohg
Gonna go with "no way in heck" since this is a family forum and all. Advise hitting 51 in Lower Guk, then coming back with some savage Turgurs beatings, and Superior Heals from the druid.

You'll level quickly with a steady group like that so well balanced, so getting used to the way things will eventually work I think will help you out. At 65 (ding, 66+, but you get the idea), in groups with druid healers, I usually split things with them. They watch the tank, since they have a good big main heal from 58 on. I do the other healing, and back them up when I can on the MT, since my mana is insanely better than theirs. You can't be primarily responsible for healing in challenging situations because your job is debuffing the monsters with slow asap, and the tank might take some heavy damage before it lands. Once that's out of the way, though, you can flip with the druid if you like, and take over healing so he can cast damage.

Happy hunting :)

LoY is a great handful of zones for levels, basically empty these days, even the pharmers avoid it. I leveled my beastlord, rogue, sk and ranger there some solo, some PL and the risk vs reward is excellent.
