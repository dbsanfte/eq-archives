For those of us who fletch and blacksmith both, the tradeskill patch added something uselful which I saw this today on the EQtrader's site...

1 Large Block of Acrylia + Chisel = 1 Large Brick of Acrylia
1 Large Brick of Acrylia + Chisel = 1 Small Brick of Acrylia
3 Small Pieces of Acrylia + water (non-foraged) = 1 Small Brick of Acrylia


The Chisel is returned regardless of if you are successful or fail. (This also works with other types of ores not just acrylia.)
