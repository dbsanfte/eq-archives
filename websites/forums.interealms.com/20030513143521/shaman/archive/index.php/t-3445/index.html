Grippa
Here's a few ideas:

First idea - Find another Hole raid. Ask nicely if you can join them. When they kill High Scale Kirn (assuming they do), your group stays in that room and rakes in Phat XP for 2-4 hours while waiting for Kirn to respawn. A lot of people say it's a 4 hour respawn but I've seen him repop in less time.

Second idea - Find a Mage with Call of the Hero. Get the mage into somebody elses Hole raid, and bind him on the safe ledge outside the undead tower. When you are ready to head in, the mage gates to the safe ledge, and summons your raiders.

Third idea - Find a Mage with COTH and a low level Brell worshipping Dwarf Cleric. Make a level 1 Brell Halfling Rogue. Drop the cleric into the Hole using DA, and then using ITU the cleric runs to the safe ledge. Kill the mage, and drag their bod to the safe ledge using the rogue (warning - bodies from duels can't be rezzed after 10 minutes). Rez the mage, pay off the dorf. Rest of your group gets a summon to the safe ledge, use ITU to get up the tower. Kill Kirn.
LaughingWoman
Fourth idea - find a Ball of Golem Clay (effect: illusion earth elemental - one charge) or two. Find a mage who has never hunted in the hole, and - with the illusion on - they'll be non-KOS to everything but the rats, I believe.

Big problem with this method is that the balls used to drop in CT before the revamp. Rumor is that they drop in Feerrott now, but I haven't talked to anybody who's gotten one this way. Anybody know for certain?

The other epic pieces that I know are in here are ranger and chanter. Pretty sure the chanter epic mob is right next door to Kirn. So if you have any friends going after these bits, just keep tabs on em and tag along. ;)

If you're going to fight there, from everything I hear, you need 2 groups of 55+, there are GOBS of mobs on the way there.

The tear from hate is easy - tag along on open Hate raids till you get one. One dropped to a friend shaman on my frist one, I got mine the second trip - didn't seem hard. Or ask a couple of guilds if you can tag along just for the tear next time they go. We would never turn down a shaman for such a thing, provided a guid shaman didn't need.
