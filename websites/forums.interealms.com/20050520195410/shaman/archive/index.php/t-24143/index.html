Diary of a Shaman

1st october 2004


12PM

zoned into Wall of Slaughter and went to work forming a group. Loving the expansion! Slow mitigation in some zones removed which is such a breath of fresh air. Nice to have the abilities I worked hard for to actually work - if fizzles and resists were not bad enough now we got partial slows - Good Lord, who thinks up such things?

12:30PM

Group formed. Not many people in the zone, someone suggests hunting Girplans so off we go. Groups seems chatty and everyone is having fun. We discuss the expansion and we all agree that it is very good.

One group member exclaims that there must be something wrong because we are having so much fun, we all chuckle and agree.

Helping Druid to heal the tank.

2:30PM

GM spams serverwide message saying that some zones are being updated and reset, including WoS, no information on when this will occur or for how long.

3:30PM

Disconnected.

4:00PM

I log back in and go to work forming a new group, some of my old group is here so we hook up. We head up ramp, hard left along the wall. Feran Swiftclaw is up! Cleric and I full mana, everyone buffed - we pull.

Hmmm, what is this? Partially successful?! Whaaaa?

Druid cannot keep up on heals I try to heal tank but his hitpoints are dropping so fast my heals are like a drop in the ocean.

Mobs seems to be zapping all our mana, no DoT icon though.

Oom. Feran at 54%.

4:05PM

Sitting in PoK with the rest of my group, guess I better head back and get everyone rezzed and buffed.

6:30PM

Getting pissed off now, group has fallen apart several times and had 4 seperate corpse recoveries.

Mobs seems to have had their attack rating/damage increased, I got a resist on a normal mob and was quaded extremely fast - my 5.5K HP disappeared in 2 swipes.

Corpses everywhere in WoS now.

Going to log, not having fun anymore.

10:30PM

Browse EQshaman.com - I notice some Shamans on the messageboard that think partial slows are acceptable. Makes me sad to see how far we have fallen and how brainwashed some of us have become.

I ponder the response if a Cleric could only partially heal a tank or Necromancer partially Dotting a mob.

Epilogue for the Day

When I see my slow is mitigated it is like a slap in the face. I am not sure how to describe it but it makes me angry and sad at the same time. Fizzles and resists are bad enough now a 3rd restriction.

Maybe Sony is, at some point, going to add an AA ability that increases slow ability? Such as first rank removing mostly, 2nd rank removing partial and 3rd rank removing all slow restrictions.

They seem to add AA's to fix stuff nowadays. Doesn't make me feel any better, in 30 minutes they destroyed the fun me and 5 people were having.

Forming a group in WoS is hard enough but now we have to be even more picky. To tank a named in WoS your going to need a big tank and most big tanks group with fellow guild members or leave a group at the drop of a hat just because they can.

When slow mitigation was taken out I was able to group with my friend, SK, who could tank. Now his HP drops so fast it is not possible to group with him in WoS.

Your also going to need a good cleric which means more time LFG or forming a group.

This game sickes me sometimes.

Ungkor
I don't really care if they mitigate slow, or remove it entirely from the game.

What I do care about, however, is that we have not received any boost in any spell area to compensate.

We haven't received any significant boosts in either buffs/dmg/healing/debuffs that aren't normal power increases expansion to expansion.

Healing catching up to druid or cleric? Nope. Falling behind in terms of pure hp healed.

Dmg increased relative to similar classes? Nope, our closes dot class, the necro, got a large boost in power over us with their new curse.

Buffs better in any way? Well, now this is a debate I suppose. They did change a few things to increase stat caps, and a couple % increase buffs, rather than only stats. So, overall, our buff line did improve in a new way. Is that improvement significant? Enough to counter the continuing nerf to slow? No way.

Chanters got a new haste with: chance to critical hit(20) on it. Our only means to increase crits is our AA hot (which btw won't stack with our own buffs atm...sweet).

Ignoring stat increasers until someone proves in a parse that they mean something. So we are left with:

1. 7% avoidance mod (agi buff)
2. 5% dmg mod (diaku buff)
3. 5% dmg mod haste buff (anyone know what lvl haste?)
4. 10% dmg mod FA

Thats it. That is the new spell line changes that are different from what we had before. Everything else is a % increase of the exact same spells.

So, our slow gets mitigated from 75% to about 30%. We lose 45% slow in other words.
We get in return 27% (7,5,5,10) worth of stuff. But its worse than that, because the above items aren't % increase to damage, its a modifier of some sort. It will not take a 100 hp hit and make it a 110 hp hit in other words.

I guess I'll have to wait and see after I get all the new spells, and then I can do a group dmg parse before and after. Like (made up):

Mob X 500dps

If mob X slowed 75% does 7500 dmg to a tank over 1 min fight before slow nerf.

now with mitigation
Mob X slowed 25% does 22,500 dmg to a tank over 1 min fight

IF, and only IF, after getting all the new buffs, a group is able to kill that mob in 30 seconds, thereby preventing 15,000 in damage, will I say that SOE has done their job and rightly compensated us.
