I think a whole lot depends on your server.

I'm on a server combined from two original old servers, yet it's still strangely a pretty gimp server, and I've never seen malos or quiescence for sail. What else is, I've never seen any spell for sale over level 62 except for the very occasional str/dex singles. No Blood of Saryn, Velium Strike, whatever you'd care to name.

Look in your market place and ask around guilds if you like, to see what's going on. It's not above many guilds to hold extras for their up and coming members, or even to keep them out of the market, either to increase their rarity and thus price when they do sell, or simply to keep others from getting ahead.

And...if you're planning on getting ANY of these spells, you'd better be sure that 10k stays more or less trivial to you from now on -- do whatever farming you need to. The worst thing is to see an opportunity vanish because you were 2 or 3k short. It happens!

Eventually these spells will probably be sold, but torpor is still 100k on my server IF it is ever for sale at all -- and it virtually never, ever is. You might not want to pass up a good chance at a spell being sold if you think it will be a long time till you get another shot.
