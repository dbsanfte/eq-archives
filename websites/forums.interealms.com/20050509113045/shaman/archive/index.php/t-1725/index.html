Are you a group Shaman primarily ? If so your gear seems ok. If you'd like to do both (group and solo) then my suggestion is get that AC up more. I have more unbuffed AC at 39 (I'm an Iksar if that makes a difference)

Drop some Wis gear and go for some HP/AC gear. If you can't quite do the Kael/Thurg quest stuff get ahold of some JB armor.
Gloves are nice - Crystal Chitin gloves are good
Shield - Crystal Chitin Shield, Sarnak Battle Shield, or Clay Guardian might serve you better than Falsehood.

Sleeves - Spirit Callers or JB might be a good upgrade.
Nice rings
I'd ditch the helm get a JB helm or Totemic might even serve better.
Get into a Hate raid and pick up some Rune Etched stuff.

Of course these are just suggestions maybe a Shaman of higher levels is more qualified to answer. Hope I could help a bit.

A couple alternative suggestions, though:

1. Get an ancient seahorse cloak to complement your arctic wyvern cloak for when you want to do ac/hp

3. JB or CC arms - Nathsar is for tinies.

4. Stick your boots in an inventory slot and get some JB - super cheap and lots more AC.
