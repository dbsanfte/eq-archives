Focus items were created to help address weaknesses in caster classes, as compared to melee classes. Now shaman (and enchanters) are at the same position they were in pre"balancing."

Shaman were able to survive without affliction foci, and will continue to be able to do so. However, they now have a much more difficult way to go, as other classes are getting an easier way to go. Even before, leveling a shaman was a challenge to people, more so than many other classes. Making it just that more difficult is going to ensure that shaman are rare indeed in the future.

Another thing to consider would be that the PoP playtesters no doubt used these focus items, meaning that PoP mobs were designed around the use of affliction foci. These focus effects could be the make or break difference in many PoP raids, for many guilds.
