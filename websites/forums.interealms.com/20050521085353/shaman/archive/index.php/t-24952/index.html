I should have written this a couple months ago, but it has only recently hit me that I think I am really done with EQ. I will probably always log on once in a blue moon to poke around and say hello, and will certainly troll the forums (it's funny, this place means almost as much as the game itself), but I don’t see myself becoming a real EQ player again. I will be playing WoW, and if you are interested in hooking up -- or just want to say hi -- send me a PM or e-mail.

Anyway, I’ll remember a lot of things from the game; over the years Uggok has taken on a life of his own. I like to think of him with his Spear of Fate hanging on the wall in a nice comfortable house in, well, not Grobb but perhaps Shar Vahl or Paineel, sleeping on his rocking chair and giving unwanted advice to newer, younger adventurers. Of course, I am sure he still goes down to Innothule with Fifi to smash some frogloks to keep in shape. Someday, he will go to sleep in the town where he was born.


To the posters at the Shaman’s Crucible, I think I spent as many hours here as I did actually playing, thanks (and thanks for the 5 green dots, considering all my jackassery over the years!). Especially to Henora, who was always a few levels ahead and willing to help me out with a tip back on Terris. To Sunmoon and all his mods, Thantu, Glorianna, Faern etc, thanks for this board. To the old school shamans who showed me the ropes when I was new – Tudamorf, Ooga, Talamay, Juuju, Tugg, Kazlaaz, Eduin, Boinshar, Nixx, and more – and to the just plain great posters, Ysgwyrthyr, Callir, Eoin, Ceno, Barm, Chutoi, Gart, Jinhelm, Rodahan, Arriana, and all the rest. Thanks. This is the best board I have ever seen, and it has been a complete blast.
