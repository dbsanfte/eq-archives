Take off all your gear, or die because you need no buffs for this also, and equip a weapon that does not modify your atk rating. But make it a weapon that uses the same skill as your normal weapon. This will show you your base atk for that type of combat (slash, pierce, or blunt). Then enter that number into Magelo.
