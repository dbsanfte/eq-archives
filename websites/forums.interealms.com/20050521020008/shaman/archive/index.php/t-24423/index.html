I'm a male barbarian shaman. The pics were taken while I was playing in Grieg's End with some friends. Some of the nameds will change your graphic to just about anything (opposite sex included). The entertaining part (at least to me) is that each player / computer can see a different model when the effect is on. For example, the female high-elf pic was what the bard in the group saw, however from my perspective, I looked like an pre-luclin graphic earth elemental and a female dark-elf to the mage in the group. It's only a short duration (30 seconds I believe) but can have lots of entertainment value when hanging out with friends or when it's near halloween. :)
