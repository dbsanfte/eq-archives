When I was searching for my copy of Q, I spoke to *alot* of shaman and 99 percent of them did not themselves have the spell (and alot of them were in guilds that I would have expected to have a never-ending supply of runes).

That being said, it was the probable price of Quiessence that prompted my question regarding horses. I eventually decided that as much as I wanted to run like a bard, I needed a torpor-without-ill-effects more. I eventually found the spell through Kongming (who has been very helpful in the past as well when it comes to spells -- nice person). It was more money than I've ever spent on anything, but its also one of the few spells that I *knew* would be absolutely worth it (and it does rock, beyond question).

So in the end, no bard-speed for me but I'm very happy with Q. Since I also have Ferine Avatar & Malos (both from turn-ins of runes) I'm actually in a pretty good situation spell-wise. I could care less about Blood Of Saryn (which sounds odd, I realize, but the agro on our DoTs is just too much to bother with when I can do more damage in less time with our nukes) and while I would certainly like Fo7th, I can live without it until I get it as a turn in.
