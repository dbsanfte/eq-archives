Zorlac Sabboth
Paeblas are not that hard to camp. Don't pull them to entropy serpent area, past the pool. Go to their spawnpoint directly. AFAIK, the first platform outside water has only 2 spawnpoints, with 3 roamers. The 3 roamers are in water so they shouldn't aggro. Only one jumps out at the end of his route. There is room next to the 2 platforms that has a crapload of paebla in it, have a monk pull em, or SK.

Spirit talkers are not to worry about, cast Talisman of Epuration and when they end up casting shaman dots on you, too bad you resist all of them. If they slow tanks, cure tank, yadda yadda yadda. You can stun a good majority of the Spirit Talkers, some might be unstunnable due to level. Just have all melee/pets on one side and it shouldn't gate.

Dual wielding rats are not pushover, especially for puller. They are hasted and have very low delay. Once slowed they're easy money.
