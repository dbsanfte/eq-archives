Also if your magi are banishing they're wasting mana :p We found that if we just all nuked it was easier, cause banish got resisted like half the time anyways... Ideal group would be Paladin, Cleric, You, Mage, Rogue, Bard, since the bard can mez any mobs that get out of control, contribute some damage, and keep everyone's mana up.
Your job is to Cure Tash and Slows, and throw around some direct damage. The 61 poison nuke works pretty well here because it's quick casting. just make SURE you get a mage summoned earring, Bracer, and Ring. if they don't have the ones that affect 61+ spells, just use Ice Strike.

My spell lineup during Trials was always:

Ice Strike (Efficient Nuke)
Spear of Torment (Quicker, "oh ****" nuke)
Torpor (or Stoicism if you don't have it, you want a heal you can drop on the cleric if they get agro)
Malo (For when the prime exe pops)
Slow (Also for the prime exe.. he mitigates 75% of slows but every bit helps)
Tnargs (This will probably get you serious agro, but if someone is GOING TO DIE it's what you need)
Canni
Disinfecting Aura (For the curing)

The pally can grab agro doing Healing Wave of Prexus if things start to get out of control. For the most part, your cleric should be using Supernal Elixir, which is a great low agro heal over time spell. If a mob gets on the cleric, I'd just slow it to get agro off him, and try to keep it occupied. The damage dealers will switch to it next if you communicate it necessary for them to do so ;)

Lastly, When the prime exe pops, have the paladin throw a couple stuns at him then proceed to kite him around the platform if your mana levels aren't so hot. I'd use this time to mem Kragg's mending instead of a nuke in case the cleric needs a bit more help with the healing. The prime exe doubles for 720, and since you're using a paladin he can't disc defensive on him :-/ Kiting may be a little bit gimp, but it's a surefire way to have a chance to get mana if you weren't able to manage it very well through the 4 waves.

Grizlor
don't let anyone tell you that banish sucks in exe trial. banish makes the exe trial a joke Banish has a 2 minute refresh time, costs 500 mana (same as a firebolt of tallon), and like 80 plat to cast, and it it resists the mage/druid is probably going to have agro. Using banish was really more trouble than it was worth. Your best bets are to get a mage bracer, ring, and earring (earring is 15% mana pres on nukes/dots/slows etc), then just assist the tank and nuke. also make sure your pet is equipped with mage loving, and that all the pets are ASSISTING THE TANK... often pets will get hit by spells or get mobs on them which make em switch targets. Know what your pet is attacking!

When the prime exe pops, if you are pretty much out of mana, have your tank tag him and proceed to kite him around the platform. you no longer have a time limit, and as long as he doesnt go below 97% health, he won't summon the tank.


Oh yeah, and DS potions really ARE a big help :D
