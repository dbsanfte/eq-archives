Weyalan
Wisdom is great, but have a look around at all the +wisdom items, and compare it to the +stamina items. If you are truly concerned with top end performance, put all into stamina. I go +sta/hp more than +wisdom, but my wisdom is still well over 200, while my stamina is only about 195. It is almost ridiculously easy to get wisdom up to 200. And remember after 200 you get a limited return on wisdom. At level 60, each additional point of wisdom up to 200 gives 12 mana. Every point above 200 only gives about half of that (can't remember whether its 5 or 6).

At the top end of the game, wisdom is largely irrelevant because wisdom only determines the size of your total mana pool, whereas your performance is defined by your rate of mana regeneration - its no good having a huge mana pool if you empty it and then can only refill it very slowly - If you have a large hp pool it will improve the rate at which you can regen mana via cannibalising. You have to accept the concept of the interchangability of hp and mana: HP = mana via cannibalisation. Mana = HP via healing (torpor). HP are what keep you alive. Go with Stamina.

Put it this way, regardless of where you spend your points, when you are level 50+ you will be scratching your head trying to work out how to increase your hp, but your wisdom will be just fine!
