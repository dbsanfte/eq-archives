I solo'd level 50 killing the Gnome guards in Echo Caverns. If you don't mind becomin KOS to them, they are pretty easy and always came single with slow pull.. Dog dog with HoS can even tank them. I used Venom of Snake (Ebolt was overkill). There were 2 in front and 2 back a bit further by a curtain. I didn't pull from inside the curtain as i'd sometimes get adds, sometimes not. I would get about 1 blue per cycle of the 4 guards, 20min respawn approx and they would drop up to 6pp.

Only warning is that while u only become KOS with those gnomes, the 2 Kings are tough (one can't be rooted) and sometimes they are pulled to the zoneline. When that happens they literally sit right by the zoneline until a server reset, so you'll have to enter Echo invis every time just in case.

I also had luck soloing in Tenebrous near Katta to get to 50. Things might start being blue at 50 there but there might still be plenty of aliens and such that are dark blue.
