Everquest 2...

Despite what everyone says or feels about the new game. One has to see this game in action to see what an amazing game it has the potential to be. The graphics and model detail/animation is amazing. The water effects has to be seen to believe. They had a playable demo version of it yesterday and many of the webmasters for the big EQ sites were playing the game.

One big thing I noticed is that clerics will not be the end all be all class for raids anymore. There are 3 priest classes....all the priests can heal for pretty much the same. They will have different abilities that seperates them each other such as better hp buffs, better regen, etc etc.. but they will all be able to heal equally. No more needing X amount of clerics to go do some raid..

Iksar models look pimpin. Its cool to see your melee class draw out his sword before battle and put it back on his waist after fight is done or to hoist his shield over his back.

Well Im sure the Baelish or some other website out there will have more info on thier sites soon. All I can say is I was one of the EQ naysayers but after seeing this demo, Im kinda looking forward to it.
