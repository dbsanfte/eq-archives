Back to Sol A! I'm soloing there now, I went all the way from 0% into 34 to 20% into 35 with kei's of course. Main problem is downtime for me, but monk will DEFINATELY cut that way down. Just use pet (pet needs tnargs, regen, haste), Envenomed Breath (mobs don't last long enough for scourge, EB has better dps anyways), instill, and walking sleep. Pull with walking sleep, dot, walking sleep the adds, dot the adds, and remember to keep the last one instilled so it doesn't flee. That would bring me down to about 60% hp, 40% mana solo, meaning i had to med a LONG time. if you and your pet take most the hits for the monkey, yall will be livin the good life.

Clockwork castle is doable if u do it safely. I prefer the bridge and the spawns on the far side of it. You can even kill EXG and kindle who are nearby.
