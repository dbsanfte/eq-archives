Jalim
The flying monkeys are a pain, but with one other person along to help you should do fine. Resist gear for them is big though, it helps a TON if they can't land spells on you, or at least not for full damage. Again, resist gear, resist gear, resist gear. If you can bring a healer or even another ranger about your level you should be able to gang tackle them. Just go for the wandering ones though and stay out of the cave. The raptors you need are easily fear kited, so no problem there. I think the shackles quest is a good idea, it is fun and a decent item. Another ranger and I did it for both of us in just a few hours, and we had a blast.

As far as helm of the tracker, you need to collect a bunch of heads, and one of those (I forget the name) is in the same zone (WL) as the other mobs for the shackle quest. You can't do the turn-in until 55, but if the giant you need is up in WL I would shout in the zone for some more help and grab that head if you can. The other heads are all from Kael in the arena, which you will need some help (usually a Kael raid) getting.
