The Warrens is a great place to level 5-20ish. You do have to be careful with runners, but if you are a snarer it's easily solved, and even rooting can handle most of the problems. A more practical solution may be just to go tag a 2d or 3d mob; they don't run when there's a group of them. There's a saferoom that's good to pull to across the bridge from the Prince's room. Loot, xp, faction, and fun. It's a great zone for that level. Interesting drops from the Muglwump too.
