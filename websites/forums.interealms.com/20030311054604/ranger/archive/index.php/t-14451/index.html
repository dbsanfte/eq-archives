DSP until 47?

Can you say stop hitting me in the head with that dull arrow and kill me already?!

DSP does not offer enough EXP after level 43 or 44 to hang there. You can get great EXP in Jagged Pines killing the basiliks in the caves like Sandary said. You can FEAR-KITE them and gain great EXP, but most importantly, YOU WILL NOT HAVE TO WORRY ABOUT WONDERING MOBs, as you will in the forest of Jagged Pines.

Also, good EXP for low 40's to 45 or so, is Dreadlands. Easy to get a group and there are constant pulls.

When you hit 45, get some crack (KEI) and head to Burningwoods. There is hardly ever more than 10 peeps in the zone and you can solo the skellies and Sarnaks (except the champions). Root, Snare, Flaming Arrow, bow, bow, bow, bow, then REPEAT. Good EXP, and open arrow, run to zone instead of dying.

Also around the mid 40's, you can head to Karnor Castle from DL. Easy to get a group, but sometimes need to be patient. Zone in on the RIGHT side and take the trains to the RIGHT side as you exit, it's just being polite there. Good EXP there, I earned 2 levels in 2 nights and am at 47, almost 48 now.

Change it up, as soloing in DSP until 47 would be enough to make me QUIT EQ!
