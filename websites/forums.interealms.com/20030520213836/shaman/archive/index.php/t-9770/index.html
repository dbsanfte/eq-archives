darkfox
Is there a way to make a macro to have your pet attack? There has to be a faster way than just clicking attack.
mool
if you click the attack button on the pet hud (or any of the other buttons) and hold the button down you can drag it to your hotkey pad just like the dragging buttons off your ability button hud.

Alternatively, make a hotkey button that just has "/pet attack" or "/pet back off" or whatever.
