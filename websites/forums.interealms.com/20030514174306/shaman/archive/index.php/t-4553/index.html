Rewts
The charcoal is indeed MQ'able as are all other parts. The only exception is that one person must get all of the tormented va'shir skulls. This person also must have enough faction with the quest giver in Katta to turn in.

The fungus hide shield is indeed very nice. However, you will need a significantly stronger group to do the caller quest (even at the slowest speed) AND that shield is rare. It may take roughly the same amount of time to collect the quest pieces for the Shield of Bane Warding as doing the FG shield, but most of the quest mobs for SoBW can be solo'd.
