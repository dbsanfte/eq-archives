I've done this exact thing (FD PL) from 1 -> (nearly) 60.

Using a druid to damage shield PL works best 1 -> 25, so I did that.

Then:
25 -> 45 CoM (now your JBB works so finishing the mobs off is sooooo much easier)
45 -> 51 KC (now you have the good slow and sheal so you can take on tougher stuff)
51 -> 58 Torgiran Mines at bridge (trolls are great xp and easy to single pull)
58 -> 60 PoN

You could technically skip the Mines and do PoN starting at 51 and level much faster since you have a 61 sk who tanks and holds agro faaaaaaaaaaar better than the 58 monk I'm using. I'd just suggest getting a JBB (free finish off damage) and a horse so you can regen mana automatically while outdoors.

Also, it doesn't matter if the mobs flees- just root it before it dies and you'll get full xp if your sk is FD'd. I do this all the time- pet tanks while the JBB does the last couple % of damage. Ez pz.

You can take the mob down to flee-mode have the SK FD and have the Shaman hotkey:

/assist SK
/cast Root

As long as you gain aggro again, you will get the exp. When you cast Root, the mob will turn and reaggro.

As far as good zones with exp modifers:

10-25 Paludual - Move from Phlargs to Bandits (heavily camped now)

In order to get exp:

Your shaman needs to have AGGRO after a successful FD. If the mob is in flee mode, this is NOT aggro.

If you want to regain aggro after a mob is fleeing, have your Shaman cast Root. The mob will automatically reaggro on your Shaman. Then kill mob with your Shaman. If you Root, regain aggro, and root breaks, you can still kill in flee mode and get the exp.

Important, stay FD until after mob is dead.
