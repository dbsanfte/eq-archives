Kinedorf
I'm not sure whether be is LB to a level 55 but Erandi Nataasu, the Erudite SK who stands outside the entrance to the Warrens in Paineel, was DB to a 53 Shm. He is definitely LB to a level 56.

He doubles for 20 points each hit (yes, only 20!), gives no faction hit for killing him and repops every 15 mins. Just buff yourself to the max (HoS, (or IoS + hp buff if you have it), Dex, Agi, Alacrity, Fury or Rage, Regrowth) and wade in. He has a 401 hp harm touch that he'll use after the first few hits but it is nothing you can't handle. Don't slow him! Using stoicism to heal yourself when required. If things go pear shaped, step backward into the Warrens.

I got my offense and defense to 200 this way. I also got my piercing up from around 40 to 170 ish. I'd recommend any shaman around level 50 who does not have their melee skills maxed to spend some time here. I only wish I'd spent more time there to get my 1H Blunt and 2H Blunt up.
