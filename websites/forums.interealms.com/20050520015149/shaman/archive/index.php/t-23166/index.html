Aurigabone
For a single class duo partner, the Shaman IS still the melee's best friend. No other class has the combination of haste/slow/heals (and buffs to some degree) that melee's need to xp effectively, IMO. Why do you think there are so many shammy bots out there? ;)

Shaman aren't being replaced by a single class, but by a combination of classes. Enchanter+cleric can provide all key shaman abilities plus CC and DPS with charm. Cleric+Beastlord cover the required haste/slow/heals bases and the beastlord has sick DPS to boot. Probably even BL/Druid works in the vast majority of xp situations.

Add to that the fact that full groups are by far the most efficient way to xp and having a "jack of all trades, master of none" shaman in the group seems less desirable. Just pick a combo of classes that covers haste/slow/heals and get as much dps as you can in the process.
