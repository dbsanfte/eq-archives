One of the big flaws in FFXI that I've seen is that most of the melee characters are just variants of one another. The exceptions are Pal/Wars and Thief/Whatevers. Pal/War are by *far* the best tanks, so they are always prefered, Thief/Whatever (Usually warrior, but often ninja) are great for renkei chains and for controlling aggro. All the other melee classes in all their combinations just exist to do damage to stuff. Since there are so many of those, and since any of them can fit into the slot pretty much, the individual demand for those classes is extremely small.

If you want group desirability, go red/white/blackmage, or bard. Chronic shortages, and little to no overlap in function.

Not quite, 2 limitations.

You can only combine the abilities of 2 classes.

And your sub job is temporarily lowered to half the level of your main job.

For example.

You level a warrior to 20, do the subjob unlocking quest then decide to try playing a thief.

So you effectively restart as a level 1 thf, but this time you'll also have the abilities of a lvl1 warrior as well as bonus stats and hps to boo.

Now as you level, the cap on your subjobbed warrior will raise too, forexample when you hit 10 as the thf, your subjob cap will be 5, so you'll provoke. When you hit 30, your subjob cap will be 15 and you'll get Berzerk. For the main part the only limitation is that you won't be able to use your subjobs 2 hour special.

Its also worth remembering that your subjob effectively gives you roughly half of what a character of that levels hp/mana is as a bonus on top. So for example if you go warrior, then sub white mage, unless your a taru you'll find your mana pool is utterly tiny.

So its generally wise to pick a subclass that compliments your main well.

And I wouldn't say it blurs the class lines at all really. Subjob abilities are capped enough to simply not make that viable. I'll certainly never replace a main bard or turn one down even though its my subjob. However it does add a nice bit of flexibility to your class and allow for you tweak a job down the route you'd prefer.

Just take what the forum trolls are ranting about with a pinch of salt. So far the only class that really does seem pointless as a sub with no redeeming features = Dragoon, simply because the Wyvern is summoned via a 2hour ability.
