weissblau
I took several minor tweaks and I'm very, very happy.

The one called "3tabpet" is one of my faves. It makes your Pet window have 3 tabs at the top - Pet, Inventory, Statistics. The Pet window has your weapons and range item at the bottom, the Inventory has all your worn gear, so for swapping stuff out quickly it can't be beat.

The second one I forget the name of, but it turns your hotkey bank into a smaller hotkey bank but with all your inventory bags lined up on the right hand side. This is a godsend, let me tell you, especially for gear-swapping.

The last change I took was a group window that lists me as well as my group, shows the health % of every member (the normal one does this now I believe) and expands the pet health bar out into a seperate bar that's more easily clickable.

OH! I almost forgot the other tweak because it's not a custom UI window.

I cram as much UI as I can at the bottom of the screen and then use the /viewport command to make sure there's black behind the text.

Right now I run in 1280x1024 mode and use

/viewport 0 0 1280 720

bigboyg
I can't remember the name of it, but there's a pet window that includes you, target, group and group pets. If you're like me, it's nice to have all the high priority stats in one area. There's also a very nice hot button mod that includes inventory slots and experience bars. I prefer the experience bars away from the group window as it means I'm not constantly reminded high slow my XP is going up.

Other mods that have helped me a lot:

Mafoo's spell mod. It takes up more screen space but it has the name of the spell gem and the NUMBER. Very handy for quick casting.

Mini buffs - just makes the buff effect icons a lot smaller.

Mini Selector - reduces the size of the main window selector drastically. Very handy.

Right clickies - an inventory mod that allows you to place all your right click items on the screen. I haven't actually used it yet, but it seems like a great idea if you don't want to see your whole inventory.

Also (you never know) you may not have used the extra chat windows yet. Just in case you haven't, right click on the chat window and start a new window. You can then filter chat and combat reports to different windows. I have a main social, guild & tell, and a combat window. The best 'mod' in the game, I think.
