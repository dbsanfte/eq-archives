Khayla
I usually don't solo for safety reasons (yes I'm a chicken). But I decided to try it in PoD after reading this thread, and knowing guild cleric was only a zone away ;)

I highly recommend the flies, they are quite easy and I had almost no downtime. Go straight out from zone in to the first patch of land (yes you have to swim or lev). I set up camp near the big rock. There are about 5 static spawns of flies if you walk forward from there and to the right. I pulled them single every time.

Things to watch for:
1. RATS - these suckers hit hard, have a lot of hit points, and really aren't worth it. I was able to take one but it took most of my mana, and killed my pet. They wander, but aren't hard to avoid and do not seem to get near home base. Stick with the flies unless you are feeling adventurous.
2. Traps - there is a risk of springing larvae traps if you wander around too far from "home". I tried once to go farther and pull an extra fly...sprung two larvae and had to gate out.

The flies don't seem to be resistant. Slow, pox, epic, and jbb is what I used mostly and only had a couple resists. There is enough room in this area to let the flies run when low health. They are pretty slow and never got too far before dying.

This is a popular spot for quad kiters...and if they are taking those static flies there is not much else nearby that camp.

Be careful, and good luck
