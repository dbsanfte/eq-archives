Baz3
If it wasn't for the PoP expansion, this would be considered good experience at that level. and it is still decenent experience for 60 and below. At 61 the regular exp seems to vanish, and the AA exp (set at 100% AA) crawls (3 to 5 kills for one % to show on the bar).

Go there for the loot, but don't expect a lot of loot. It is decent cash and gems, but the lesser loot there is no longer very valuable in game (cane of the tranquil, club of ykesha, box of nil space). The cloak and the bracer are still great loot.

As long as you have your rogue, you need to swing over and kill the Blood/Emp room. The Emp drops spells, with the potential for a rare spell (Torpor/Malo/Pox/Sunstrike/EoT are all possibilities - but all very rare).

chanter is not required. slowing and healing are needed, and enough dps to break the spawn on the two Crypt Guardians, who like to CH each other. The rogue to open the doors guarantees you some decent dps, though.
