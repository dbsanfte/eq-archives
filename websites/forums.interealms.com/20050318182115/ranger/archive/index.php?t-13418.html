The giants on the coast seem to be weaker than the ones in the forest, but their "village" area has some just stupid pathing issues. We came back with 12 and demolished him.

Also, fighting on the coast, we did have the trees assist the giants against some of the group members. Pulling the giant past the tree would switch the tree from Dubious to Threat.

It hit for 358, double attack, and thought it wasn't super high in HP, it was a bit more than I could handle with pure melee and no slow (EC wouldnt proc). We went in the other day for some experience and I had well over 90 of them on track. They were in groups of 10+ running around killing anyone that got into aggro range (which was huge). We killed a few, but it wasn't worth the risk of the huge trains and social aggro from everythign else in there. It basically cleared the zone for a few hours.

I noticed some odd things in Storms in the last week or so.

First trip, we were killing the giants, and I had ridiculous amounts of resists. Of course, with new harmony, resist=aggro, so it caused quite a few problems in our monk-less group, not to mention large amounts of ranger deaths.

The giants we did kill weren't that hard, but the real problem is, the exp wasn't that good. Toss in the /real/ long respawn time, and it really wasn't worth the effort.

The also seem to corpse camp, it's possible the ones we were killing were just bugged, but after our last wipe, we waited at GY for corpses (20 minutes), and then ran past where we were to get to another area, and the giant that killed us was /still/ in the same spot, it was at least 45 minutes, and he hadn't reset.

I went back a few nights ago and did the frogs near the tornado, they weren't that bad, but again, exp wasn't as good as PoV, and respawn was 1hour +, so we had to resort to going back to the cactii, scorpions, and armadillos in the desert.

So far, I agree with what's been said, PoV seems to be the exp area, and PoS is geared solely for raids.
