Haste of Mithaniel
Raw Data
Slot Description
1: Increase Spell Haste by 20%
2: Limit: Max Level(65)
3: Limit: Spell(Complete Healing excluded)
4: Limit: Min Casting Time(3.00 sec)
5: Limit: Spell Type(Beneficial only)
6: Limit: Effect(Percentage Heal excluded

Line 6 is the important part, % heal is the shaman/druid incomplete heal, so the WBA does not affect casting times of the ICH; on the other hand, its a nifty range slot and I highly recommend one to anyone that doesnt have a Spell Haste IV item.
