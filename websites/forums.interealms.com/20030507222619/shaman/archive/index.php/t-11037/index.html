For soloing Emp this post has about everything you need to know:

http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?s=&threadid=10978

As far as the crypt goes, it is actually very easy to do solo. All you need is a rogue to open the door for you. Its easy to get down to the crypt after that, if you are level 65 you can just run all the way down without agroing anything, if you do have to fight down its very easy, at level 60+ everything will be single pulls, nothing is a hard fight, just kill stuff one by one all the way down to the crypt.

Now when you get to the crypt entrace (the steps leading down), wait for that froggie (or cube if your lucky) to path to you and kill him before going into the crypt. then just invis yourself and walk into one of the rooms, solo the named/PH, invis up and go into the next room (on the same side) and do the same. Now when you go across the room it is very important that the frog that wonders into the crypt does not see you, you may have to wait until he moves out of the crypt before you cross, otherwise he will see your invis and you will have to gate or die. Its actually not hard at all I was doing it at level 60 pre-POP. One thing to remember is that the names do run so keep em perma rooted at like 20 percent. Also don't under-estimate the names, after killing a bunch of PH's the names are a lot harder, just make sure you play it safe and don't do anything stupid. uUm, not much else I can think of, kind of a fun camp to solo and you could walk away with a JBB or herio cloak, it will get boring though and you will go through a lot of dry spells with no names. exp sucks, even at 60.

Solariis
The upper tunnel in chardok is a blast at 60. It goes on forever, almost everything is dark blue and you can single pull everything at that level. Two BoNs + pet kill is all you need to kill most mobs and it's very good xp.

The upper tunnel is located at the gatehouse. If you look up and to your left you'll see it. You need to levitate yourself and run up from either ZO or the inclined area near the gatehouse to reach the tunnel. There's one mob right there to greet you then everything else after that is in pairs but single pullable at 60.

I went back there at 61 to show some guildmates. Was mostly light blues until we got to the first Y section where from that point about 75% of the mobs were dark blue to me and excellent XP. Not the greatest loot in the world but a lot fun if you prefer dungeons over the boring outdoors like me.

If you are looking for pure loot then look no further than PoG. Read Urdegish's excellent guide a few threads down about it.
