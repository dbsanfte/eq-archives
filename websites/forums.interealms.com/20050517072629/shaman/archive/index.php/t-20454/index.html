At level 25 to 30 there is a wonderful mob for you to fight that dramatically increases the speed at which you get defense skillups - the needlites in Echo Caverns. They hit extremely quickly, but not very hard. Have your mage friend cast a damage shield on you & pull them with something from our cripple line, Disenpower or whatever the highest for your level is.
