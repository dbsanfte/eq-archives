You could try changing your magelo to give yourself ND3 and see the difference in HP it provides.

But anyway, ND3 works just like it describes itself. +10% hp, based on base HP, HP gotten through stamina (including buffs and items), and HP obtained by wearing +HP items (IE: If you upgrade a 0 hp item to a +100 hp item with ND3, you'll actually get 110 HP).
