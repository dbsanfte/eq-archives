Orgak
I don't have 2 characters that I play simultaneously, however my mule account and future Rez Bot are on a second machine and I use a KVM. I would think it would be very difficult to "2 box" in the traditional sense where you actively play two characters at once. Obviously you could park a CLR for buffs and rez's but to have 2 toons engaged in a battle would be beyond me without 2 monitors.

I have a Belkin 4-port SOHO. I'm very pleased with it. It has keystroke switching as well as buttons which are conveniently located. It does sound and USB. Tip: Get the expensive cables from Belkin. Huge difference.

The only problem I'm having is that on my 2nd (ghetto) box, I have a Radeon 9000 vid card. When I switch away from that box I have to first go into windowed mode and then shrink EQ or it will lock up the game. My primary is a GeForce 4, 128MB card and it works wonderfully. Always did hate ATI anyway.

Oh, and on a side note, this KVM works with the Logitech cordless desktop (Optical mouse version) which is rare. I tried using the little cheapy Linksys (blue, flat, button on the front) and ended up bashing it in with a hammer.

Edit: I should clarify that I don't have the deskspace for 2 monitors so I just use one. You can plug a monitor in directly to each of the boxes and when you switch your KVM, only the keyboard and mouse will be effected.

I 2 box quite a bit, and I would never consider doing a KVM style.

It (the keyboard and mouse) does take up more room, but you need 2 monitors anyway so just put your keyboard on top of a monitor if you need it out of the way or behind a flatscreen.

Canni, and adds are why I would never use a LVM. I like to go into dungeons and do semi dangerous spots, and being able to use both hands at once to cast 2 spells or otherwise.

But if your low risk, there is no issue in it. Buff, solo, come back. switch for heals. etc. but that gets a bit old, and doesn't use the talents of both characters IMHO.

I can canni macro, or nuke-a-duke w JBB, while backing up my chain stun pally, etc. It would suck having to switch every 3 seconds.
