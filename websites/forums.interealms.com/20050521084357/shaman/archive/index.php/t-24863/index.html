Sk is better than monk cause he can lifetap, and once he has his lifetap aa's he counters the sham slow heals. Monks suck cause they get massive spike damage, and thats tough on dual boxers.

Pallies are good but stuff all dps, and you have to put a lot of dots up to make up, but they can out agro anything, and with thier symbol you dont need to go hunting virtu all the time, so they rock well.

Bards are fun, cause you can mitigate thier tanking lacks with slow.

However, once you get WA5 and a couple of agro weapons on a warr, warrs are greater than all.

Warrs with Wa5 and some good agro weaps tank best, and defensive rules. Who cares about sustained damage, cause a sham can put out massive healing over time with canni.

Warrs take 50% of the dmg till you get around to slowing the beech and crippling, then they take sweet fa.

In summary. Three things matter pullability (bards > all) tankability (warrs greater than all) DPS (Monks do better dps but SK's come close and can actually tank post pop tier 2).
