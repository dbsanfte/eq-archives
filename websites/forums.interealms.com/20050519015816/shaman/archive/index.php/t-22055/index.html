the macro is as follows.

Chutoi
Standing mana regen is extremely slow--something like 1 mana per tic (six seconds) absent some kind of mana regeneration buff or flowing thought items (which you probably don't have at your level).

Previously, until level 34, players had to 'meditate' with their spell book open and blocking all view to recover mana faster. Now, sitting does exactly the same thing as 'medding' or meditating. I forget the mana regeneration formula, but it's a bit under half of your meditate skill.

Anyway, the game reads stuff ever six seconds, or a tic. I think that's due to the packet size of information sent between your computer and SoE--but I'm digressing already! If you are sitting at the time the current tic ends, the game gives you your seated mana regen amount. Then you have six seconds to do whatever you like--stand up, run around, cast canna, etc. As long as you are sitting before that six seconds is actually up, the game thinks you are still sitting and gives you another full tic of seated mana regen.

As someone above noted, the net effect is to be able to gain the mana regen from your meditation, and the mana regen for your cannibalization all at the same time. This increases your overall rate of mana regeneration and reduces downtime.
Vigdis
Yeah, watch for the 6-second update on you mana bar when you sit and med. Right when you see the update, you stand, canni and sit quickly. Then after 6 secons you repeat the process.

The pay-off is that you cannibalize once every 6-seconds and add that mana to what you get for medding, since you sit during each tick, the game believes you have been sitting all the time and thus you gain a full med-bouns on top of canni bonus.
