http://eqtoolbox.not.net, click on downloads, click on "everquest skins other" and then download UImap 1.0b

as with the other UI's, you download it and put it in it's own folder, or whatever folder you're using. (see elsewhere for details)

It's based on your Actions menu UI

So, hit CTRL+A or your actions button. Stretch the little box out....

and you get these:
pic 1 (http://pages.sbcglobal.net/geek/trans/map1.jpg)

pic 2 (http://pages.sbcglobal.net/geek/trans/map2.jpg)

pic 3 (http://pages.sbcglobal.net/geek/trans/map3.jpg)

pic 4 (http://pages.sbcglobal.net/geek/trans/map4.jpg)

Each page is for a continent. It takes about 10 seconds for me to switch between pages (cable connection). Once you have a page up, you use the scroll bars on the bottom and right to pan around and find the zone you want on that continent!
