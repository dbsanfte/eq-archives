Bigguss
Three points:

1. If you're at 985 unbuffed, you're easily getting to 1000 AC even with the lamest of buffs. Aside from being able to say you got their unbuffed, there isn't much benefit unless you're always getting your buffs dispelled. :)

2. Given the gear you have, your next upgrade is a fungi tunic. I'd be willing to sell of a few of your pricier pieces to raise money for it, too (barbed booties, bracers, of course the iksar BP, the sleeves, probably even the horse- you can always buy it back.) The difference between a fungi and an iksar BP is going to change your game more than getting your AC to 1200. Not sure if this is possible, though (availability and price of the fungi vary by server.) Is this is an option, go for it.

3. In case you ignore #2... barbed ringmail shoulders (ac 19) or leggings (ac 25) are both nice choices, and campable (pricey to buy, though.) Dragonlegs as buyable, but pricey (start around 5k on Karana, if you're lucky.) Sebilite scale and Kylong (each ac 16) represent more minor upgrades, but are available for around 1k. Crystal chitin and oiled leggings are generally too expensive for their stats for a shaman. One other nifty choice- replace your spear with a Di'zok sceptre of authority (10/20, +5 wis, +25 mana, and 10 AC.) Or if you get really lucky, you might stumble on a reasonably priced Stretched Fungushide Shield (ac 32, +15 sta, +10 wis, +15 vs disease.) Free choices include a shrouded medallion (ac 12, +25 mana, +15 magic) from the 4-arms camp in ME, or a Divine Inspiration (range, ac 15, +15 wis/int, +35 mana) from the chieftain camp in ME.

But ignore #3 if at all possible and go with #2.

Happy hunting!
