Also adjust your mouse settings in Control Panel. IIRC that's what I did to improve my mouse speed, in addition to the mouse speed setting in EQ. You might also consider upgrading to DirectX 9.0b if your video/audio drivers are compatible.

The jerkiness may be caused by your mouse pad. I was using an "X Files" mouse pad that had a silvery textured feel to it. My optical mouse was jumping all over the place. Then I switched to a plain jane pad and all was well in the world.

Might be worth a try.
