Bigguss
Spinashe:

1. You continue to med while casting and while right-clicking. As long as you're not moving and not medding, you're medding.

2. You can use SoW while you are on the horse, but SoW won't make the horse go away (won't override it.) The horse shows up as a buff (and takes a buff slot.)

3. Yup.

One interesting thing- while the horse shows up as a buff spot you need to re-summon it every time you log in (unlike your other buffs). No biggee, but there have been a few times I've logged in and started killing stuff, then thought "Geez, my mana regen sucks!" before I realize I forgot to summon Mr. Ed.
