Agree. I would add the following

Regular Zones:

Draniks Scar
Trash mobs: lvl 45 to 55 mobs. Little to no exp at level 65
Named mobs: Solo for most 65 plus - Easy Single groupable for 60's
Boss mobs: Reported Boss mobs but no information to date

Harbringers Spire
Trash mobs: lvl 50 to 60 mobs soloable. Poor Exp at lvl 65 Plus Single groupable - slow exp for 60plus
Named mobs: Soloable for geared higher level Shamans 65 plus - Single groupable for 60's lvl folks
Boss mobs: single strong 65 group

Blood Fields
Trash mobs: lvl 55 to 63 mobs Soloable for Ok exp. Single groupable - slow exp for 65 plus
Named mobs: Some solo for geared higher level Shamans 65 plus - Single groupable for 60's lvl folks
Boss mobs: 2-3 groups+

Ruined City of Dranik
Trash mobs: lvl 65 to 72 plus mobs Dual team possible otherwise requires single group - Very Good exp
Named mobs: Single groupable - Good Exp
Boss mobs: 3+ groups/Raid

Nobles Causeway
Trash mobs: lvl 60 to 67 mobs soloable. Ok exp (1% AA/8 mobs per 1% normal Exp at lvl 68), Single groupable - slow exp for 60's lvl
Named mobs: Some solo for geared higher level Shamans 65 plus - Single groupable for 60's lvl folks
Boss mobs: 2 groups+

Walls of Slaughter:
Trash mobs: lvl 63 to 65 mobs soloable. Ok exp (3% AA/4 mobs per 1% normal Exp at lvl 68), Single groupable, good to fair exp
Named mobs: Some single groupoable, some 2 groupable, good exp
Boss mobs: 3 group - raid force
