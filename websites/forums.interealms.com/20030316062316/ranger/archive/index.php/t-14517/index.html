I've had the best of times duoing with an enchanter friend of mine in PoV. He dire charms one of those rusted suits of mail, and I harmony pull the non-kos frogs, malarian larvae, or the undead near the zone. The pet makes a great tank when the mob is slowed, and I just sit back and shoot, healing the pet between fights and tossing any necessary chloroblasts on my friend while he runs circles around me if he gets agro from slowing. Mobs usually yield 3% - 5% aa exp per kill. Archery damage is very low agro, so really the only risk is the enchanter getting agro from slow, which can be remedied to an extent with boggle.

Anyone else have any other usefull duoing tactics from level 61+?
