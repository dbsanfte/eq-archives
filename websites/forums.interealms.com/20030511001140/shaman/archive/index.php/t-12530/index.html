Landragoran Heronwing
wis, then agility.
Trolls have low natural agility and you need a bit extra to be sure you won't go into penalty.
Trolls also have very low starting wis, so you need to max that first.
Stamina isn't worth considering for three reasons:
1) At low levels, a few more points of stamina might give an extra 5 hps . . . hardly worth it.
2) At high levels, your gear will likely have stamina on it in large amounts, plus you have large stamina buffs you can put on yourself. Using myself for an example, unbuffed stamina is at 254. That only leaves 26 points of stamina left for me to buff before it's hard capped anyway, which just isn't worth it.
3) You have a rather high base stamina on a troll in the first place.

Your first instincts were correct. Go wis then agil for starting stats.

For armor, I would just go to the bazaar and see what is available for sale. Filter by slot and stats or hps or something you want on armor. I think you will be amazed at how cheaply you can buy adequate armor.
