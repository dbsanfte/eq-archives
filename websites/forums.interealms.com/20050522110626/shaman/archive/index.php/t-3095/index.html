taraddar
It's probably possible but I do see some potential difficulty with it.

1) Getting the mobs to aggro.
With the change making pets non-aggro when not engaged you can't just park the pet in a room and let them aggro on him automatically and kill. You can work around this by placing yourself in the room but that risks death for yourself and since your talking about being afk possibly an unresable death from an expired corpse. With all the nukers and sks with ht i'd consider that a very real possibility.

2) runners-
Even tho the pets are non aggro when not engaged they will still aggro when they are chasing a mob so you need to make sure you don't get runners. A SBD or 2 or a stun whip can help out here but a proc is hardly a sure thing and still might get a runner which depending on the area could potentially kill your pet and then you (see 1).

3) kill rate-
With just the pet killing and needing to be a limited spawn so to speak the headway you'd make would be fairly minimal I'd think since you need thousands of gobblins killed and you miss out on potential extra faction from skins/salts. Might still be worth while in the long run but just doing afk killing would take a long long time.

4) location-
Need to find the right location. idealy it's going to be someplace out of the way with the proper number of spawns in the imidiate area but with nothing else near by so runner risk is minimal. Idealy also there should be no wandering spawns in the area since they make spliting and controlling the timing on the spawn difficult.

Possible, probably. Easy, definitely not.
