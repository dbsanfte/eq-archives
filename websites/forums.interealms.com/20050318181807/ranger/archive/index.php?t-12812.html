You talk to the Tribunal members for each. When you get to their room, there are six of them standing around on a platform. Each one is named identically, The Tribunal. Target one, and say "I am prepared". It will give you a bit of text that tells you which trial that particular member is controlling. When ready, say "I am ready to begin the trial of XXXX" (at least I think that is the phrase) and it will port your party into the room to begin the trial.
