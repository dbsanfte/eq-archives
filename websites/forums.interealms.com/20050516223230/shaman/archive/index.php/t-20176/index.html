Class comparison is a basic and major factor of the game design - you could almost say the major factor.

And when you look at the class structures, you have priests, melees, robes, and hybrids. The priests are the healers, and we cant heal anymore. Dont tell me I'm suffering from 'class envy' because I can see that a paladin - a hybrid - is nearly equal to me as a healer. That's not envy, it's balance (or lack thereof, more specifically).

Unlike the majority of voices here, I am not satisfied to be '75% as good' as a druid, as some have suggested. I dont need to be as good as a cleric, obviously, but there's no logical reason anymore for druids to have such an advantage over shamans when we are both priests. So what I have slow - that helps everybody in the group/raid. And why are only shamans being penalized for slow, when at least 4 other classes have it? So what I have cannibalize - druids and clerics have passive mana regen buffs, and any like-equipped and AA'ed druid or cleric can nearly equal my mana regen these days and not have to spend time casting a spell to do it. People who play other classes, throwing out these same old excuses (which did in fact have merit long ago during Kunark and Velious) need to catch up and listen to the people who actually play this class instead of speaking from ignorance.

Some of us have played our characters since the beginning or nearly so, and are quite attached to them. Maybe you would reroll on a whim when you find something you dont like about a class, but that doesnt mean we are like you. We also enjoy our characters, in spite of the things we may see wrong so we will speak up if we think something could or should be improved.
