I used to power level my lowbie guildies being the cool Guild Leader that I was. ;-)

Nagafen's Lair: Zone in through Sol A though. Pull Dogs to the zone line with a slow. I had lvl 16s to 35s down here and they were able to kill the dogs as they hit so slow with Turgur's on them. They get easy to hit with Cripple on them as well. Even the caster dogs died quick and the Xp was awesome. This is also a good spot to solo if you are 45-50.

Dawshroud Peaks: Lvls 16-35. The Tribals Camp outside the Maiden's Eye zone Entrance. This Camp is a great place to power Level. These guys are little higher then the dogs above in level, but they also drop Acrylia and Polished Steel Ulaks. The spawn rate keeps the pulls coming. Slow/Cripple is a must for these boys.

Infected Paw: Lvls 16-35. This is a hole in the ground in the center of South Karana. These Gnolls are big and hit pretty hard. Usually people dou them around lvls 30-35. But bring your teenager down here and Soak up the 20% XP bonus in this zone. Cripple/Slow is a must here as well. Magic Resistance is a good Idea too because you might pull an Enchanter that could enchanter your teenager and get him to smack on you!.

Paldula Caverns: To level 25... no place better..

BUFFING:
If I'm only Power Leveling 1 person I will Buffing them with every buff I have that will stick on them. IF it's a group I buff the MT with everything I have and HoS/Haste the rest of the Group. I also buff Myself to the Max because I take most of the hits.

PULLING: Slow to pull. Then Cripple. If the Plved can't take hits then you Simply root them to hold the argo and stab them till they are down to a little more then 1/2 alive. I don't use a pet when Plvling because it tends to do way to much damage and controlling can be an issue. If you insist on using a pet it's better to let the Pet be the one that kills the creature and let the plvled take it past 1/2 dead.

That's about all I can think of! Good Luck!
