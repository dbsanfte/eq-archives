Just as an added spot for farming cash drops, at lvl 60 you can single pull most mobs, and with a gate potion for the beastlord, you could consider doing pits in Velks. The named mobs themselves don't drop much good loot (except lute of the howler, but given your situation, I'd suggest steering clear of Khelkar unless you have a cleric and possibly tank with you - he's hard to slow, and you need to be able to tank a mob that will be hitting you for around 200-250 a pop fairly fast - with the right AA and equipment he's a lot easier, of course) but 2 or 3 hours you can come out with around 1k each in cash (after selling paws etc)

Another option with little competition is Dragon Necropolis. You should be able to easily duo the entrance mobs, and there's a semi-rare chance for a phase spider carapace to drop - still sells around 10k on Tunare, I'd assume similar in other places, as it's a decent BP still. Also decent pp drops with occasional Velious Quest gems. As you get more confident, you can move deeper and work on the rats for Velium weapons - the Priceless, Worn, etc still sell ok in bazaar, and sometimes you can get leather armour pieces that may (or may not) upgrade the BL.
