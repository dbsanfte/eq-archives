Landragoran Heronwing
Ikky bp is still a very good choice for your level of a shaman, even with Stoicism, which isn't quite as efficient as Torpor . . . actually nowhere near as efficient. But it's still lightyears better than nothing.
Zone into Stonebrunt from The Warrens which you get to thru Paineel. Then run East until you see the water, follow the shore to the boat, click on the door next to the dock and you zone to Gulf of Gunthak. Levitate and run across the water to the shore (need invis after you get there), hug right side and there's a passage thru the cliffs . . . make all right turns and you get to some docks, go along docks to the wall, then take a left and take immediate right to a zoneline to Dulak Harbor. Take your first left and you'll start seeing drunken pirates here and there. You gotta kill those until you get an Empty Flask. Then go back to the Gulf, bout halfway between the shore and the lighthouse, there's a spirit in this overturned sunken boat you hail and do a dialogue and just follow the instructions he gives you, which include giving him the flask at some point, then you get a log or something, give that back when you're ready to fight some stuff and two elementals spawn Flotsam and Jetsam and you kill them (easy) and then loot a piece from each and hail the ghost again and give him the two pieces for the spell as reward.

whew.
