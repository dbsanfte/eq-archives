Versus a mob you will drain anything from (close to) zero to 400 mana, depending on the mobs level and resistance.

Versus another player the effect is never resisted, ie it always hits for the full 400 mana drain. The effect is not scaled down by the 66% pvp dmg nerf since that only applies to damage spells.

The procrate on the mace was extremely impressive, so impressive that it was better to melee with it than medding, which lead to the procrate being severely nerfed. Right now I'd rate the procrate as 'Low'. For reference a primal 1hander is high and tash stick about medium. That's just my own very unscientific opinion of it.
