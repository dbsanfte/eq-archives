Jing
I have all healing AA's maxed and elemental arms (Marr's Gift on those, up to 25% bonus on heals).

Here's how I've been told it works. Healing Adept % is a flat bonus and is doubled by crits. So if you have Advanced Healing Adept maxed, you get 19% flat bonus to all your heals. 19% of 1300 is 247. So with this maxed, you get 1547 on all your Tnarg's. Healing bonus focus items are different though. They work like DD bonus focus items, meaning the bonus is variable, up to the max. Improved Healing 4 focus is 20%, while Marr's Gift (Solusek Ro cloak and Elemental arms have this, and probably other items) is 25%. However, healing bonus focus items are not doubled by crits. This is why you can crit for odd numbers instead of evens.

So with AHA maxed and Marr's gift you get 19% to 44% healing bonus, or 1547 to 1872 on Tnarg's. If you crit it, you get 3094 to 3419. However, I could swear I've seen some crits for over 3.5k, so I think this isn't exactly right. If the healing focus items were doubled, you'd see crits for over 3.7k, and I've never seen that.

Adept does affect Kragg's, I just did a heal over 2200 with it, Kragg's will not crit though, ever. The reason no one mentioned it is because once you have a few AAs casting tnarg's is way more efficient. I'm only at adept4, but with my IH4 hat I can crit for nearly 3200 with Tnarg's, and do 15-1700ish normally. if Tnarg's crits, which it does fairly often it heals more than Kragg's does, and I can cast tnarg's twice for way more than kraggs can heal in the 10 secs kragg takes to cast.

also, for the guy above this post, marr's is just a better version of improved healing4. The way heals work, it takes the basic heal amount, adds whatever adept% it should, if then checks your healing gift level, and sees if it will crit, if it passes this check, it will double the heal up to this point . If you have improved healing4 (adds up to 20% of base value to a heal) or marr's gift (adds up to 25%), it calculates what percentage it will add and adds it on after the crit is calculted. Marr's or IH4 do not affect critting or not critting, they just add more on to the back end of the heal.

IH4 adds 1-20% of the base value randomly, between 13 and 260 for tnarg's. with adept3 (10%) and IH4, you will do 1300+130(HA3)+(random13-260) or 1443-1690. a crit with adept3 and IH4 would do ((1300+130)*2)+(random13-260) or 2873-3120.

I hope that helps more than it confuses.
