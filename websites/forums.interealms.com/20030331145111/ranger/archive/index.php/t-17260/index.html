They supposedly smoothed the Hell Levels out, so that there are no more Hell Levels. Whether this is true or not I do not know.

I went through 45 the slow way; xp greens (what are now light blues) in Crystal Caverns. Then again, I was after the silk for my chanter; the xp gain was just a bonus.

Got a question for ya. Have you got your Tolan's Bracer yet? Few rangers have it before they enter the 50's, but it does open up some options for you if you do have it.

You can solo the geonids (assuming they're open) in CC. However, unless you've got Tolan' helmet, stop before they become KoS; they guard the easy way out from the dwarf town.

You can fear kite in a number of places. Jaggedpine (might be troublesome weithout wolf form, so might be worthwhile getting a lupine), EW, EJ from what I've heard.

If you get the bracer, you can use a slower form of soloing that opens up a whole new slew of mobs. Root, dot, and shoot is going to be slow, but will be the only way you are going to be able to solo some mobs.

One last thing : don't ever expect to see your xp bar move on a per kill basis again, until you either a) make an alt, or b) get a group in the Planes (not gonna happen untill the 50's, based on what I've seen).
