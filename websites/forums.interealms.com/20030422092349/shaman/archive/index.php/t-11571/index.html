Can I evade the guardians and the fight the named skeles in their rooms? Will the guardians heal the skeles from outside? Should I attempt to kill the guardians first? I have epic with pretty good gear (1000 ac 2400 hp's, no torpor). Thanks.

At 60 you should leave the guardians alone. It's possible, but it is a hassle.

Here's how you do it.
Invis up and move in in one of the rooms. Don't stop at the door. Move in without hesitating. Kill the PH(or named). After you killed him invis up and move to the next room(that's if you want to clear all 4 spawns). And here's the important point. As long as you stay in the room, the guardians won't assist(won't heal either).
But the named skellies start running, when they get low(PHs don't, just the named ones). If they run and the door opens, you are screwed. If you have a snare proc weapon, keep meleeing till it procs.

Last advice: Watch snare. Some of the named skeletons are really really mean and sometimes dispel their root/snare and run off, when you thought you had him already.

Good luck!
