Hi All - Thanks for the past information you have provided to me in previous posts.

I was lucky to receive a Rune Etched Icewurm Fang(REIF) from a guildie who won it on a roll in Kael(for free). SOoo - I went in search of a new place to hunt. Just did a few /who all 43-49 searches, and sent a couple friendly tells, and this is what happened.

A 44 paladin was in Katta, pulling from the graveyard and fear-undead'n the mob's. Most times it was just myself and a paladin, sometimes there was a ranger there with us.

Needless to say, I was 43 - halfway to 44 on saturday, and by monday at Midnight, i just dinged 45. The exp was great, and with me hasted and my REIF, I was able to make GREAT exp at a wonderful pace.

I also was able to solo it without significant down time, as I had camped there and my friends from sat night weren't back on yet.

I was told that there is one thing to beware - Coterie Assassin - he is invisible, and a baddie who wanders around. He never caused any trouble, but I would keep see invis upu if you are alone.

Good Luck all - hopefully that helps someone else.
