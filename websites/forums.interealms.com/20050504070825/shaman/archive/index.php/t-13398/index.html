While there are many opportunities for roleplaying in EverQuest, they are rarely realized. At no time has the focus of EverQuest ever been strongly directed toward roleplaying; it is instead directed toward character development and advancement. This is not necessarily a bad thing, but if you are looking for a "roleplaying experience" you will likely have to look elsewhere.

That being said, there ARE some things you can do to eliminate some unwanted noise.

1) turn off shout -- you don't need it.

2) turn off ooc -- you don't need it.

3) turn off auction -- you REALLY don't need it.

4) /ignore Nizerx (if you happen to be on The Rathe).

Those 4 things will eliminate everything except buff-beggars. For those, uh... roleplay with them. :) "Aye, child, I would be happy to grant you increased strength in battle, but first you must prove your worth to me by obtaining fo me the meat from a mammoth. I am too old to hunt."
