The thing about Lord Ghiosk is that you need to kill Black Reavers to make him spawn.

Black Reavers are about level 50, immune to almost all magic, and when they die, another mob spawns. This new mob can be another reaver, or a special quest mob (Ghiosk, Neh or Rak), or a regular phantom.

We did a reaver raid at the weekend, with 3 groups ranging in level from 45 to 55. We never really had any problems except when someone agrod Lord Rak! If we had had more time, I think we could have go to and killed Neh'Ashir, but our puller had to leave :(

Anyway, Ghiosk is about as hard as a Black Reaver, you could kill him with one goup of 50+, but getting him to spawn probably needs more people.

OK...here it is...

You can do the Test Of Patience at a lower level but beware because apparently the Killer Sharks can see through Invis to Animals. If no Killer Sharks go by, then you are golden. Other creatures in the water include Pirahna, basic Sharks, etc. Those will ignore you if you have Invis to Animals up.

As such, you can try to do the Test of Patience as soon as you get the spell Invis to Animals (level 14?). BUT, you have been warned. Luck is when opportunity meets preparation. So make sure that you have everything in order (your bind, gate spell memmed, a /loc hotkey, and all necc. spells up and running) and that you have a decent evac plan if something goes wrong. that way you can pick up where you left off without much backtracking.

All that being said, I fully encourage y'all to take the risk:). This is a game after all. If everything were fully predictable then the 'adventure' aspect would poof. Take the chance knowing you might have to do a corpse run anyway. When you succeed it should make it pretty sweet.
