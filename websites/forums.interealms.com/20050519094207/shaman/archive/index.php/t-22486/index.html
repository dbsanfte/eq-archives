Karshak_rn
There is a drake surrounded by protectors just outside the VP entrance. the protectors are all tethered. kill them. then hail the one in the middle that you didnt kill (He is a named NPC) and go through the text. he gives you a container.

Once you have the container you have to kill a dragon. There is a huge dragon sitting outside the entrance to VP. Hail it, say "I am ready". He tells you to go and activate the four portals.

Send 4 people in your raid to the 4 portals around the zone and have them click at the same time.

A named dragon appears somewhere in the zone. For us it was in the middle of the lava lake. Kill that, he drops 48 bloods.

Combine the blood and the veeshans peak key that you didn't destroy in the container to get the new key.

If you destroyed the key you have to get 3 essences instead.

We didn't win because it was a pickup raid and we underestimated him. We had a little over 40 in it. He's really not hard, there are a couple of little things to know but its not tough. We will get him later.

Thats all I know.

HS
In HS, you have to get an essence of howling stones which drops from hexbone skeletons (what I have been hearing, and where I found mine ). These mainly spawn in the east wing of HS. It is a really rare drop and can take some hours of camping.

If you are not familiar with HS, you have to either have a rogue or a master key to get into the east wing. If you don't ( like me ) you have to kill a sperm type mob and get a candle, then go camp the North wing until you get to the named and get a norent key from the named. The named who drops key is behind a locked door, and the key is a ground spawn somewhere in the wing.

In the west wing, its the same thing-the name is behind a locked door. Find the key, go through the door and kill it and loot the key. Note, if you are at the portal and there is no named up, you have to kill the named PH and wait for a repop until the named spawns.

Once your done with the west wing, you can go to the south wing which connects to the east wing. The mobs are gimp and die with my pet + a bane. I had a warrior along but he was doing more harm than good ( stepping on rugs, bringing 30 mob trains... even died once when he fell off rug :mad: )

Dont step on the rugs, if you do you are dropped out of the wing ( with a possible 20k fall ) and you have to follow the path to get back to the entrance.

I got mine after about 15 hexbones, but people have reported killing 100 or more before getting one. So this is rather rare.

Thanks to lanys.luvly for the extensive help he gave me in tells about HS ;)

Chardok


In chardok the echo of chardok spawned for me right inside the castle past the bridge and elite guards. It was a 2k AE and a stun, and also hits 700s. The AE is very nasty because while your healer is stunned, it can kill your tank. Warrior went down at 10% ( I was 2boxing tank and cleric was with us ) but I didnt have problems tanking it from 10% (making me think that a melee with good gear could). I also heard a monk can pull this to zone.

Clearing to the mob took around half an hour, but would not pose a problem to anyone who is ally to chardok. The warrior solod all the mobs on the way there. If you are not that well equipped, be careful of trains as in some areas the mobs are relatively well packed together.

Edit : Echo of Chardok - DPS on warrior with 2250 AC is 301 when slightly slowed. It had approximately 50khp, and low AC since warrior did 251 DPS on it (used fellstrike somewhere in there). Warrior took around 40k damage in the fight which lasted 3 minutes. He died at 9% or so and I took around 4k damage before it went down. Echo of Chardok is also indifferent until attacked, so its easy to clear the gimpy trash right up to it and then pull it.

Sebilis
In sebilis the echo of sebilis spawned in lower jail, and is really easy to get to. This one is significantly easier. It has a 1500 point AE, but no stun. It may hit a little harder but without the stun its not hard to kill.


Edit : Around 50khp on the sebilis essence, and again low AC as warrior did 271 DPS. Warrior took approximately 48k damage this fight, even though he was stonewall the whole fight ( I didn't use stonewall in chardok ). Be careful on this one. Echo of sebilis is also indifferent on spawn, so it is easy to kill the gimpy trash right up to it (I only had to kill 4 frogs I think) and then pull it.
