Grizlor
...DPS is 99% of the time is a defined ratio between the ammount of damage you do and the time it took you to do it.

There's certain asshats (Bracy comes to mind) who have zero concept of DPS, and think it has to do with efficiency, or think that since "a wizard can do 3000 damage 1 second, then no damage for the next 10 seconds" that DPS can't be applied.

Since damage is never continual in this game, the application of "DPS" like you'd talk about "MPH" isn't exactly the same. No matter how small a slice of time you take, a car travelling at 90mph will be going 90mph.

In everquest, such a division of time can't be made, even by the slowing down of time as many of us shamans supposedly have mastered.

All damage comes in bursts, even what is normally referred to as "Sustained" damage. Melee comes in hits. DoTs do damage in ticks. Nukes... do a big blast, then nothing for a while. A car travelling at 60mph, or 5,280 feet per minute, or 1056 inches per second... That's something continuous. A car doesn't move, stop, move, stop, etc... Though one could say that driving through new york city stopping at lights, you averaged 20 miles per hour. Again it's a whole ratio thing.

hope this clears it up for you.
