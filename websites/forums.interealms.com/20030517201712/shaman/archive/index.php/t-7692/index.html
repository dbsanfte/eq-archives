Krenk

Wrong. Stopping runners in dungeons is the most important point for snare tactics. Dying is not an option, zoning is. And rebuffing, breaking spawns again, setting up camp is all annoying enough to log.

I've had this necklace since before there were shrink potions, long before shrink was allowed in Neriak. I was the second shaman on my server to gain this item. They did not have anything like this when I created my Troll. I had just wanted good faction with the home base on old server, Tallon.

I've seen people get this necklace and remain clueless about how to use it. Thinking it's cast time too useless, or snare time way too short. That's an inability to adapt.

The key point to learning this device: snares don't overwrite, they refresh. Spam it within 30 secs. If it's snared before it runs, that makes resnaring it easier. In some cases, if you don't keep the mob snared, you won't get a chance to snare it on low health.
