Goldark
Crystal Caverns in Eastern Wastes (Velious) is a good source. The geonids drop gems, you can make about 1kpp an hour selling them to vendors. Alternatively there is a good supply of spiders right next to the geonids, which drop crystalline spider silks. They sell for quite a few pp each.

Hill giants are still an option, but farmed quite heavily.

Alternatives are to go for loot camps, e.g Frenzy camp in Velketors, Crpyt in Old Sebilis. You'll need to group up for those, and so are dependant on winning a throw of the dice, but at least your cleric friend would be getting some xp at the same time.

Your best bet is probably trade skill supplies. One of the advantages of starting on an old server like Bristlebane is that there's lots of rich people that are working on tailoring and smithing.

Also, ask your friend if he'd help you with the start of your epic. The shield is pretty good and the boots are priceless. They'll save your life often by the time you hit 50th lvl. Not sure if they work at any level but the clicky works at 20th, I did it with a friend not too long ago. Only place that you and a cleric would need backup is for Black Dire. You'd probably need a 50th lvl melee around for him.
