Spoiler.

In the Plane of Knowledge, in the Library are spell vendors for all the classes. If you face west to enter the Library from a bridge, the Shaman vendors are inside on your right on the main floor. There are 5 vendors with the name Mystic, and they sell standard level 1-44 Shaman spells, plus 4 new PoP spells.

Mystic Abomin (70, 980, 0), otter shaman, sells:
19 Tiny Companion (no drop) -- seems identical to Shrink, except that it's pet only and shrinks 50% vs 34%.
49 Shock of Venom (no drop) -- 460 Poison DD, 230 mana, 3.9 cast time, conj
Compare to 44 Blast of Poison -- 400 Poison DD, 200 mana, 4.5 cast time, conj
Compare to 44 Blizzard Blast -- 430 Cold DD, 200 mana, 5.3 cast time, evoc
54 Blast of Venom (no drop) -- 680 Poison DD, 340 mana, 3.9 cast time, conj
Compare to 54 Ice Strike -- 675 Cold DD, 250 mana, 7.0 cast time, evoc
61 Replenishment -- 25 HP/tick regen, 275 mana, 20 min duration
Compare to 52 Regrowth -- 15 HP/tick regen, 300 mana, 20 min duration
