Kineada
It's possible to do. Had a druid in my guild solo PoD by DS tanking him. Time level druids are raid buffed in the order of 7khps.

Brackencoat = 13 ptDS
Legacy of Bracken = 40 ptDS
Spirit of the Wood = 55 ptDS
Wrath of the Wild = 350 ptDS for 1 hit
Dain Ring = 10 ptDS

Wrath of the Wild can be pre-cast then recast after the first hit for a total of 700 damage on initial engage. Spirit of the Wood lasts 5 ticks (30 secs) but can be extended to 7 ticks (42 secs) via AA and equipment.

Druids also have ATK debuffs which can shift the per hit average closer to min hit.

Hand of Ro debuffs 100 ATK
Ro's Illumination debuffs 80 ATK

If the druids has reasonably good AC (in the order of 1000 and defensive AA's LR5 and ID5) then he will be taking lots of hits but those hits will be minimum damage rather than max (80's instead of 200's).

With a nice corner to wedge ourselves into, we can channel all our nukes/heals (channeling focus 3 helps). Fully AA'd/hasted nukes take ~4secs to cast and do between 1550 and ~2100 damage. Fully AA'd/hasted heals do between 2030 and 2500 heals in 2.8 seconds. On a non-bashing mob, we can channel 5500 heals in 10 sec.

Druids do well tanking against fast hitting (hasted) mobs with low ATK. 63 ptDS (plus 55 for 7 ticks) will tear fast hitting mobs up. We prefer fast hitting/low max damage mobs to get the most efficiency out of our damage shields.

If the druid you watched was tanking mobs, he prolly grew up porcupping for exp. It isn't the standard quad-kite way that most druids use to level but still a valid way to exp at certain levels.
