I was thinking about this today after getting my EBolts resisted 3 times in a row on 4 different mobs(I ended up OOCing for a partner).

I believe the theory held currently is that the DD on our DoTs is there to force root to break(maybe as an answer to the DD on druid roots as well). Well if that were true why couldn't they seperate the resist check for the DD and the DoT? That would still force the root to break, but wouldn't make soloing as inefficient as it can be, and lead to alot of shamans dying or zoning. (This is mainly for the pre-50 shamans, as mana isn't that hard to acquire afterwards) Yes, I'm aware shamans are next to gods at 60, but frankly it took alot more pain and suffering than it should of to solo pre-51. (<3 my new regen)

I think this would be a relatively easy fix, and would help spread our DoTs away from druids a bit, who seem to have much more effective DoTs than us. (Yes, I'm aware our damage is better, but that doesn't help when it's so easily resisted)

I know druids might get a little angry at this request, but the fact remains that our only means of soloing (reliably) is to Root/rott, kiting does not work very well for us, you have superior DDs for that(Ie, even if you can't get root to stick due to it's DD requirement you can still kite it around snared). Even with our DoTs fixed, it would not make us anywhere near as effective as quadkiting, it would just make for alot less pain and suffering for shamans. ;)
