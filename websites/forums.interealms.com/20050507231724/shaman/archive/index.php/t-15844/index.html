Most of those questions have been asked and answered before on these boards, I ll try to answer a couple of them anyways.

>1) The Canni dance - Its like the great, white whale; I have heard
>a lot of stuff about it, I have the tools to hunt it, but just can't ...
It s not much else but -Stand- -Cast- -Sit- /repeat
Mana and health regeneration happens once every 6 secs, called a tick because it does not happen continously but all at once. If you sit at the time the tick happens you gain sitting mana/hp regeneration. When the tick happens your class symbol will spin and your mana and hp grow a bit. Right after you see your bars move, stand up, cast Canni, sit down. If your connection is not too laggy you will get the sitting regenaration then.

If you want to make a hotkey for it it looks about like this

/sit off
/cast 6 (number of spellslot you have Canni in)
/pause 12 (depends on lag)
/sit on

I seem to remember a change to sitting regeneration that you don t gain it if you stood up between ticks, someone may clarify. The hotkey is still usefull to save ticks.

Abividad
Your need for a haste item will decrease as you level, and definately will be almost nil when grouping in PoP. At 29 though, it is a very nice bonus, as any worn haste effect will stack with your quickness buff. The "entry level" haste item is the Flowing Black Silk Sash from Lower Guk. If you can though, hold out for Silver Chitin Hand Wraps. They are only 1% more haste, but also have very decent stats on them, and that will free up your waist slot for a belt with some nice AC/HP/stats as well, since the FBSS only has 5 AC and no stats. On Povar server, the Hand Wraps are about 50% more (2000pp-FBSS vs. 3000pp for SCHW)

As someone else said above, once your wis is 150ish, you're good. When you gain more seasoning and reap the fruits of your labors as far as new equipment goes, you will be amazed at how wisdom will take care of itself. I went from 852AC and 160ish wis at level 55, to 972 AC and 191 wis at level 58, while concentrating on increasing AC and HP unbuffed. The wisdom just sort of came without even looking at it.
