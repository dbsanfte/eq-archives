Gear recommendations...You didn't give a budget, so I'll just give some off the top of my head based on what I have and is reasonably attainable/purchasable.

1. Barbed Dragonscale Boots
2. Coldain Skin Gloves.
3. 65hp6ac ring to replace the jasper drood ring. (Keep it for tradeskills if you want.)
4. Chitinous Belt to replace Fear froggy belt.
5. Crystal Chitin arms to replace your RE arms. (Turn them in and hope for a nice gem, or save for CR gear.
6. Quest the Coldain Military Wristguard for one bracer slot.
7. Buy one of these: Artificer's Ringmail Bracer, Bracelet of Sense, Bracelet of Fortitude.
8. Gantru Charm or Goranga Idol for Range slot. As an alternative, you may want to review the new PoP quests for the Intermediate Magic Manual, and see if that's within your capabilities.
9. Tattered Mantle, Obulus Death Shroud, or Imbued Granite Spaulders for shoulder slot. First you could duo easily, third as well (if you get lucky), and second is usually available in bazaar, but that's budget dependent. Or group grind for it.
10. Gladiator Chain Leggings from Kael Arena area. Not too uncommon a drop. Not a major upgrade, but they are okay. If you are looking to buy legs, look at some Peerless or Heavy Dragon legs or the new Velium Field Plate legs made with tradeskills.

That's all with HP/AC in mind, but not completely dumping wisdom.
