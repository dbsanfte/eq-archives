KEI'd wizzy = lethal, just keep him alive.

However, thats not the strategy I've been using. I realize you didn't list a druid as one of your toons, but I've been having so much fun doing this, I'm going to post it anyways. Yes, I am a druid, deal. :p

First, get your wizzy to 18 or so, grab a kei and run over to paludral w/ either your cleric or shammy. Now find a high level druid who likes to ae or ask a friend if you can use their druid. I'm level 60 with mostly HoT gear. Head to sol A, put on whatever hp gear you have, buff up (THORNS) and get ready to hit mid 30's. Have the druid pull trains, and let the wizzy toss a couple pbae's (if the wizzy getes too much aggro, a heal should get most things back on the druid). Thorns will make short work of the goblins and the druid shouldn't have much problem tanking. In about 4 hours, I took a friends wizzy from 18 to 29 and the goblins are good until 34-35. Next stage, we're going to try kodiaks in GD. :)

I realize this might not be the right solution for you, but perhaps you can think of other ways to incorporate ae'ing into your plevelling? Wizards have 2 major strengths for exp, pbae, dd. DD+kei is good and pbae owns.

Trell
Wizard is the easiest character I've ever PL'd. Made it to 35 the first weekend and 50 by the next, with EVO, ALT, and DEF maxed. KEI is the key. With KEI he was level 30 before I was ever able to use a full bar of mana.

I use a 52 Druid and 53 Shaman to PL, but never at the same time. Up to 30 I just use the standard DS/Healing from the Druid, with the Wiz chain casting his 2 lowest PBAoEs. That way I always had 4 on the Druid and 4 on the Wiz with the AoEs hitting all 8. With the right kind of positioning, I can even hit the ones standing nearby waiting to hit on us.

After 30 it's more efficient to use the Shaman. Shaman pulls as many as he can handle tanking with Tugur's, so he gets a good aggro going. And Wiz stands next to him and PBAoE's everything down, while the Shaman is spot nuking with JBB.

The only thing you need for this tactic is KEI and go heavy on the AC and HP and you can play Tank Mage for quite a while.
