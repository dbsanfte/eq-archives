Mainly you will be hurting on the levels when it comes to resists. With dragons AEs someonve over 55 takes far less damage than someone under, even if the lower person has higher resists. Problems you will run into are gating dragons which will proceed to wreak havoc on your raid as they summon you over one by one. I'd say your best tactic around that is either fighting near the nests, or layering on so many DoTs near the end of the fight your MA can finish them off and loot after his summon.

with your 4 person group you should be able to take on an AE or 2, just add in another healer and some serious DPS (like a rogue) for the other 2 spots.
