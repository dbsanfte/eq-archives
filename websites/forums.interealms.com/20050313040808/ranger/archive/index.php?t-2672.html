Basically Multiquesting works because Quest NPC's keep track of what's given to them, but not WHO gives it to them. Thus in a situation where there are 2 items to be handed in, one person can hand in the first one, and the NPC will register that hand in, and wait for the 2nd one. The Person who wants to recieve the final reward then hands in the 2nd item, and the NPC registers the item as completing the quest, and gives the reward to the last giver.

Here are the facts:

1. all halflings get sneak, it's a race enabled ability, like all wood elves get forage.

2. The questing part with Mairee Silentone in Eurdin is intended to be done with players that are only at dubious faction, she just hate people. The givaway is that she talks to people about the quest with dibious faction, so the quest can be done with that faction also. Most people just prefer to sneak so they KNOW they are safe doing the quest.

3. When turning in items for a quest, the NPC only pays attention to the order that the items are actually GIVEN to him. Who actually walks up to the NPC or opens the trade windows first have absolutely NO effect on the outcome of the questing. It is strictly based on the order of hitting the GIVE botton. However, when multi-questing with another player it is MUCH MUCH safer to have each person open and fully complete their trade before the next person. That way you don't get into problems with lag or miscommunication causing people to hit the GIVE button in the wrong order.

4. The patch change to the Invis thing, according to the patch message, only relates to INITIATING a trade while invis. You are forced to be visible to START the trade. Once the trade window is open, you should still be able to have another player invis you before actually hitting the GIVE botton.

So the answer for you Merrikat is this... both of you can sneak if you want to to make sure you have an acceptable faction level. The sneaking Is't really required. HOWEVER, if you decide to do it without sneak, make sure you BOTH talk to Mairee to make sure she responds correctly to the quest dialog Crydee mentioned (you should do this with every quest you ever do, to make SURE your faction is still correct, and nothing has changed to cause a problem). Next have the druid go up and turn in the Sphere from Sky, completing that trade in it's entirety. Then after the druid tells you he has completed the trade, THEN you turn in the hammer.

Also, make sure you have the correct hammer. This part of the quest is to take the hammer you got from Hole and give it to Mairee so that she can look at it. Then she gives it back as a new name and tells you to get the Sphere from Sky and then give her the Sphere and the hammer again. Which results in you getting another hammer back with a third name. Check Crydee's walkthru to be sure of the exact names of each hammer.
