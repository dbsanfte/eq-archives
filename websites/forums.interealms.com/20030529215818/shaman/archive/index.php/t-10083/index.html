Xanden
There are two haste types, spell and combat. They are not related in any way.

Spell haste will make your spells cast faster. It is a focus item effect, and also an effect on some cleric spells I believe.

Combat haste will make you swing your weapon faster. This is the type of haste listed simply as "haste" on items. You can wear multiple haste items but only the highest percent one will take effect. Likewise, Celerity, Quickness, Alacrity, SLTW, etc are combat haste, and only the spell with the highest percent will have effect. Some bard haste can be added to combat haste, so you can have three types working together: item, spell, and bard song.
