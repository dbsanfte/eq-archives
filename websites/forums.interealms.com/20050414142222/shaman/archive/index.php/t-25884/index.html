Zyam Spiritmonger
DoN:Sony does something right.

Bazaar- A bit funky at first but easy to get used to. I think the size of the zone and ability to spread traders out reduced lag a lot.

Barter Zone- This zone should probably be combined with the bazaar. Many times buyers are also sellers and it would make sense to have them both in the same zone. VS buying in one zone (with 7 people using it) and selling in another (with 300+)

DoN Missions- The first roadblock is ran into was the inability to get a mission right off. You must complete a miniquest to get an initial faction hit and gain access to the missions. (you may still be in a party to recieve them but can't GET them if you havent done the mini task)
I first chose to gather 7 bloods of some sort from lavaspinners layer. This dungeon had what it seemed was mid 60's mobs and were hitting for about a 400's I fell through an invisible floor and was forced to CoH myself out. I gave up and decided to get another task. I chose a task that sent me to stillmoon temple. This zone is very well designed and with the placement of caster mobs implements a new form of difficulty. Cleric mobs that CH and Many casters that gate. in this case it can quickly make this zone (where mobs hit for about BoT Calibur) as difficult as MPG. My task was to kill 20 (stillmoon) creatures. I was in a raid with about 3 groups of guildmates who all banded to just pull trains to get as many as possible credit for kills. All who were in raid got credit for kills if that was their assigned task.

EXP- WOW. For the difficulty of the mobs the exp is insane. (stillmoon temple instanced mission) I was getting 2-3% or more aa per kill. The mobs were hitting my warrior alt. (11.2k hp and about 1950 ac) for mid 400's. It was a total joke.

Time spent vs reward- There are clearly some missions you will encounte that will take a LONG time. Then there are some you will lable (farm status) I found one that I consistantly complete in about 30 minutes or less and recieve 38 crystals. I find myself often completeing it before the lockout timer is up to get another.

Guild friends did some of the Norrathians keepers tasks and upon completion Were awarded 3 aa and + 10 to their Stat cap. It appears these tasks progress to a point of mini raids etc. Im hoping to get them done soon and anxious to see what will be of the mini raid porgression.

Merchant Items.- well I have only seen a few. The merchants were removed due to exploits (which I can understand but im anxious to see more) I saw 55 hp augs and Honestly.. Thats about what id expect for non raidable aquirable gear. +15 or so better than the previous hp augs. an individual in a consistant raiding guild will usualy only be upgrading 35-40 hp per set of content anyway. ex. VT-100 hp items Elementals-135/145 hp items Time-165-195 Hp items Gates-200-235 hp items. It makes sense.

All in all I think so far this is a good expansion. I like the zones. Its fresh. Instanced always = a + to me. The guild hall portal is awsome. Corpse altar is ingenious and the healing pool is nice too (needs an upgrade Id say another 25%) This expansions looks wellplanned and by the hasty reaction to the dupe bug etc It appears They intend to keep it true.
