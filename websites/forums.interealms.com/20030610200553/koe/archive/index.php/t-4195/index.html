Let's not forget what is going on in the world. Klydon for example, can be called up at any moment. I would like to share a letter, written a long time ago, that speaks of how our country does what is needed.

Dear Mrs. Bixby,

I have been shown files from the War Department, a statement, that shows that you are the mother of 5 sons that have died gloriously on the field of battle. I feel how weak and fruitless any words of mine must be, that would attempt to beguile you from the grief, of a loss to overwhelming. But I cannot refrain from tendering to you the consolation that may be found, in the thanks of the Republic they died to save. I pray that our Heavenly Father may assuage the anguish of you bereavement, and leave you only the cherished memory of the loved, and lost. And the solemn pride that must be yours, to have laid so costly a sacrifice upon the altar of Freedom.

Yours very sincerely and respectfully,

Abraham Lincoln
