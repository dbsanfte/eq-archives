You're interpreting those ratios incorrectly. That ratio is the number of hit points it takes to provide one point of mana, meaning that a lower ratio is superior.

For example, Canni I provides 1 mana for each 1.72 hp burned; 29 mana at 1.72 hp each = 50hp (approximately, due to rounding off of the ratio to two decimal places)
