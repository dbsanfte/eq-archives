Trebaar
Hoppers in DSP hopper caves should be nice for you now. Pull to the outside, and park doggy on the grass waiting for you. Take some time to learn the spawn and so on. This mob shares cave (time share) with tribals, so they may not be up when you visit. Of course the attraction of flawless and superb hopper hides to sell is a bonus.

Jagged Pines is not to everyones liking.. but hunting the poachers at the camp can work ok.

You can solo hill giants in Rathe Mountains, and Ice Giants in Everfrost.

Some of your solo ability is going to depend on equipment (eg focus gear), and some on creative problem solving for particular areas. If in doubt, practise an area IN A DUO... with another character that snares. You have the "saftey" that the mob will chase you more slowly, and you can check what dots land and what root works. With snare kiting, tell doggy NOT to taunt, and he will run behind and snip the mobs heels. If you are doing this, of course, you should not root the mob.
