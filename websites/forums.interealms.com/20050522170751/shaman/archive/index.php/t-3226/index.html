I posted on this subject months ago in response to all the JBB Fanboys and Can't-Have's whining.

I don't have the logs here on my laptop, but the end result is that you can do around 42-45 constant DPS using just icestrike on any large encounter that you care too. That is, with Canni5 you can maintain a constant non-stop firing rate for 12-15minutes depending on gear. If you spend AA on critical nukes, this just goes up.

Cold works on pretty much every luclin encounter, and lots of Ntov and Kael bosses.
If your looking to KS single smaller targets, use both Icestrike and Torrent at the same time.

Of course this all assumes raid buffs and Kei. Don't forget to get your mage focus ring and bracer before the fight! You won't be nuking for 675's then.
