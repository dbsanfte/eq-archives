This was posted quite a bit ago, but I'll post for future readers. Recently I've been leveling up my Shaman twink, but not wanting to go looking for group on offtimes when I'm not sure how long I really want to play I would 2 box my friends 65 paladin.

I'm not sure how your gear compares to his, he has close to 1500 ac, and about 5.9k hit points, I found doing fly's in PoD were really easy. Paladin required almost no heals, so with average bazaar gear against slowed mobs I would say it would easily be doable. They hit for max of about 170ish, are lightblue so can be single pulled by a level 65 character, and gave 1% every 2 - 3 kills (Iksar penalty). The fly's are near the zone, named something like "A Tsetsia Fly" don't mistake them for "A Malarian something or other" which spawn near the water, they are much more difficult, (hit for 400ish, lifetap for 700).

I would set up camp in between the fly area and where the diseased rats spawn. I pulled the rats too, but I'm not too sure on the situation with different characters, considering the paladin can heal himself.

Snare would be a big plus here, I was able to keep them stunned as they were running away, and root if they got too far.

I tried the hole route as well. Same two characters. I fought down near the undead tower, where most everything was yellow to my shaman at 52, the mobs were harder than the fly's and the exp wasn't much different. It was pretty risky since a few times I would get several wandering add's, was up to 5 mobs at one point and had to use lay hands. They're really magic resistant deep, I wouldn't suggest doing it with just 2 characters.
