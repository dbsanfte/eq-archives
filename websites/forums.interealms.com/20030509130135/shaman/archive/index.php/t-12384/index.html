Barliman
I'm not really sure about the guardians since I've never tried them but the castle/crypt with the gargs in PoN is one of the best exp places in tier 1. I've really only been once with a full group including a cleric. I got an AA in about 3 hours 15 minutes. Normally in Valor I get an AA in roughly 2.5 hours.

The biggest problem with crypt/decay in PoN is the pulling. When you pull those gargs on the outside you almost always get 1-2 banshees from the other side of the wall. Worst part is you don't see the adds until they warp on your head. My advice is to bring a good heads up group that doesn't have a problem dealing with the warp adds. I'd grouped with everyone for so long we work well together and we didn't have a mezzer but it certainly wouldn't hurt to bring a one. The other slightly annoying thing is that exp drops off quite a bit at 65 because the banshees turn light blue and make up at least half of the pulls but at 63 I was getting an AA every 3 hours 15 minutes.

It sounded to me like you are looking for good experience places to 2 box your shaman and paladin in tier one though. So I would suggest hobgoblins for PoN or maybe courtyard in PoD. You could certainly do courtyard in PoD I just don't know how the experience is 63+.
