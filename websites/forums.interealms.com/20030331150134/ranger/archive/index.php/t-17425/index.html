Negil Wintersfury
From zone in, invis up, follow the path right till you reach the first "T" intersection, turn right. Follow the path till it ends and turn left. Enter that building, you will see a collection of armors inside.

Black armors are non-aggro mobs, immune to snare and do NOT summon.

White armors CAN SOMETIMES see invis and are aggro. They are snareable and DO summon.

In the back of the first floor, you will find stairs down, the first room you come to has six armor spawns. Pass through that and the next room has 6 human spawns.

Valorian Guards and Guardians do NOT summon ARE snareable.

Valorian Sentries are NOT snareable.

Use harmony to split them, kite them along the wall in the long open area. About 9mins per kill with no KEI, 7mins or so with KEI. 6% aa exp per kill solo. Use greater wolf form and swiftwind (if no primal bow) and go to town!

Rarr!
