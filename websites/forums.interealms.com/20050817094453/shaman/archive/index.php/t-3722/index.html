Hrendra An'ei
Actually, large bowls only get you as far as 148.

Beyond that, you have to work on poison vials (save pelts and zombie skin), *OR* with the new patch, make Casserole Dishes. The trivial's somewhere between 188 and 208 on the CDs. The only problem is that it's still annoying, as the components don't all stack (unlike the poison vials method).

So you have a couple of options...waste time farming, or waste time making multiple vendor trips :)
