Quest Series 2
I’m not sure that this is essential for progression, it seems like a really long collection quest for a nice item.

Fezben (main quest giver) gives you 2 items:
Vaifan's Experimental Sealer (4 slot container)
A Socketed White Crystal (no stats).

Completing different "tasks" for the 3 sub-npcs will presumably upgrade the crystal into a more powerful, useable item (charm?).

Summary
L'diava Rexxen
Yxtta - Find and kill Xounii Resnen. Bring "proof of what happened" to L`diava Rexxen.
Kod'Taz - Go to Kod'Taz and speak to Kevren Nalavat. He should be able to help you access the three trials. Enter each trial and bring back anything you find of Kitren's to L'diava Rexxen.

Taminoa Bialu
Qinimi - I believe that the scout for this area was caught sneaking around the housing district, but I have no proof. If you were to search this area, maybe you would find some sort of sign or marker he may have left.
Riwwi - Find something of Reyna's and return to Taminoa Bialu. (slaves in the area would not interact with her until she proved herself).
Barindu - Find two of Talwin's journal entries and bring them back to Opury Foop.
Ferubi - Find Smith Rondo and give him the farstone (supplied by Taminoa Bialu).

Vaifan Cogswin
Tipt - Five different types of Vaifan's Power Cell. The cells are named from A to E. When you have collected all of them, place them in this power pack (supplied by Vaifan), activate it, and return it to Vaifan Cogswin. Cragbeasts, a pack of ghosts, numerous Muramites, and a native riddlemaster are all mentioned as possible droppers.
Vexd - Collect flickering finkenheimer, a tarnished sprocket, some uncoiled springs, a greased bolt, and some connection rods. Combine 4 parts in Finkenheiemer and return to Vaifan Cogswin.
Sewers - Go to the four sewer areas and find a timing gear, a rusty spring, an oiled belt, and a processing gizmo. Combine in gearbox (supplied by Vaifan) and return report (product) to Vaifan Cogswin.
