Bigguss
Just so you're clear on the downsides:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
It must be equipped to use the effect.

The effect won't work until level 35.

The effect last around five minutes (so you'll be swapping it in and out a lot to reapply the effect.)

It snares you (I think a 60% snare)

The effect overwrites SoW / Spirit of Scale / etc.

The effect generally does not stack with regen spells (unless SoW is also up, in which case it will overwrite SoW instead of your regen spell.

All of this said, the staff is great great great- it just takes a little getting used to !!
