Well, you have little choice of mobs unless you are following someone in. The biggest problem is the resists, and getting around those. At 65, you can single pull the ledge (just inch up the side and he'll agro), but I was never able to land a slow. Get him agro'd, get malos in, then try to sini/turgurs from there. I didn't have a tash stick, with one of those you might get lucky enough to get slow in. Once that happens, it's an easy fight.

I tried probably 5-6 times at one point when I was really bored (had a cleric logged outside). I did, however, successfully slow and live against a wyvern once after a doze raid (pre-PoP). I was kos and waiting for coh to ntov areas, and someone agro'd it. It killed an sk and monk, but I landed slow and got him to about 70% before someone came back and helped finish it off.

The racnars would be really easy, if you had your pick of mobs, followed by drakes, then the weak wyverns, then the normal wurms, then the tough wyverns (the ones that quad), IMO.
