Tranzar - depends on your play style of course, but most of the people here would probably suggest that you focus on either AC or HP first, then wis/mana second. Personally, I plan to focus on AC until i routinely hit 1050 AC self-buffed, then I'll gradually switch out AC for more HP, which will hopefully get me in good position to make proper use of canni V when I eventually get it.

My personal recommendations, in priority order highest to lowest, through level 57 at least:

1. horse
2. jbb
3. iksar regen bp or fungi tunic
4. epic spear
5. extended enhancement focus item
6. mana preservation focus item
7. extended affliction focus item
8. affliction efficiency focus item
9. ac gear until you hit 1050 ac
10. hp gear
12. wisdom/mana
13. resists (against certain types of mob, this goes to #9)

In other words, to equip a horse, jbb, regen bp, or a number of useful focus items, I'm willing to trade out my AC/HP/whatever. But assuming I've got whatever of #1-#8 that I can get, I'd work on AC until I hit the chain-class soft cap, then get HP, then worry about my mana pool size. And you'll want a set of resist gear bagged for when you start raiding.

Some advice:

Do the Coldain Military Bracer quest (you will need a level 50 friend to help with the last bit)
Do the Kromriff Military Legging quest (level 50 needed to complete last bit)
Details of these quests can be found elsewhere on this board
You could get a runed smudging stick for your range slot by camping Grimling chief (you should be able to solo him at level 40), although it is a long camp
You could try to get an Elder Wolfhide also solo, although this might be too hard at 40.

At 40 you can probably handle the forest giant camp in FV - this is good loot and plat, excellent exp and you might get forest loops which you can wear or will sell to vendors for like 250plat or something in that range

Giant scalemail sleeves are common drop in KD and sell for almost nothing. You can often get howling harpoon for 300 plat or less, which can be your primary melee or range slot if you need the wis/mana

I would not bother buying a JB helm, it is cr@p. You should be able to pick up a Metal Linked Crown for real cheap. Alternatively, go solo yourself a blackened alloy crown
