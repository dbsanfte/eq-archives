Rodahn
Ah yes, the good ol' stat debate.
That's actually another philosophy I have changed.

I used to be pure WIS. If it didn't have WIS, it didn't get equipped by me.
Post 50, however, that began to change. So much of the better shaman gear was more AC/HP-related, and so many of the shamans I encountered/talked to were shedding their WIS gear for more melee-like stuff.
So I thought about it like this: if I drop my WIS and go full AC/HPs, it'll help with me taking punishment, but I cast A LOT, and I like to have as much mana as possible to deal with buffs, debuffs, etc. And since I mostly solo/duo/small group, I will need more mana to keep up with everything.
On the other hand, if I stick with the WIS route, I'll crumple under any serious attack directed at me.
I finally decided to pursue a balance between ALL stats. Not just AC and WIS and HPs -- everything: DEX, STR, WIS, HPs, Resists, CHA, MANA, etc.
If I had to choose the stats most important to me, however, they would be (in no particular order) WIS, HPs, and AC. Resists are up there, also, but really any uber mob is gonna zap you with their spells for a heckuva lot of damage regardless of what your resists are.
