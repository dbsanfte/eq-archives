2. Mana Regen

In the past, cannibalize and our ability to regen mana was blamed for shortcomings in other areas, such as mana cost and duration of our buffs and manacost of heals. With the new and widespread abilities in passive manaregen for other classes, an upgrade to cannibalize 4 needs to be implemented. We are the only class forced into a position where we have to sacrifice so much to regain mana. Most other classes simply use a buff slot.

* Other classes get mana regeneration methods that are nearly equivalent to shamans
* FT and other enhancements increase mana regen rate across all mana using classes
* Mana regeneration spells are widely available to other classes
* Cannibalize is active mana-regen and has a large number of penalties when compared to passive mana-regen (can't cast any other spells while regaining mana, can't move while regaining mana, cannot engage in any social interaction while regenning mana due to constant clicking)
* Permanent Spell Slot required
* The current Canni upgrade is unsatisfactory and does not address any of our concerns.


Mana Regen Suggestions:

* New AA ability for Cannabalize.
* An upgrade or addition to Cannibalization, allowing (at the same ratio), greater or more frequent conversions of HP to Mana
Alternately, some across the board adjustment in mana cost for our spells, considering the cost of similar spells to other classes
* Upgrade Can 4 - bigger bites, less clicking
* New Canni AA - hastened cannibalize
* Can 4 as an AA, to eliminate "greyed-out" spell gems
* CAnnabilize over time spell/AA
