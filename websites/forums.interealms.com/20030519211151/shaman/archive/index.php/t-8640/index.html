There are 3 factions: Grimlings, Sonic Wolves and Owlbears.
They attack each other about every fifteen minutes. You see a message like:
"the sound of gnashing teeth is heard. the sonic wolves look nervously to the south."
A player has to help the attackers defeat the defenders.
