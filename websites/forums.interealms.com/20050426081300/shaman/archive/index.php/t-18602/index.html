Kozul
like he said, get to 62, much better xp spots open up then.

For now, grab a mage or sk/pally and duo trash in PoN, I did that before around 60 and it was great xp, more than 1% a kill at least. In two hours with a friend, I'm sure you could get at least 35% of 61. With mage you got your pet toys, and their bad ass pets, just grab a virtue cause you will get hit a lot, even if you want the mage pet to tank.

If you got a sk/pally, cast away of course without much worries of getting hit....never actually tried it with a tank (did mages a few times), but I'd guess the mobs don't die as fast, but you're most likely healing less too, so it's a tradeoff (there was some minor downtime between pulls the mage way).

You can try soloing there, but they hit really hard, your dead if you get an add (or you have to zone-root never lasts long on them), and it's sometimes hard to single pull the trash. What I found to work the best, regarding downtime after a kill, was to use root to split up the tanking between you and your pet, with you tanking most of the time, but stepping back to heal, re dot, etc. I cast rage a lot when soloing (no voice of berserker), which lasts 20 minutes (I think?), if I could find single pulls quick enough, I could take down 3 before I needed to recast it. At about 2% a kill, you're looking at a full yellow, but that's high end, I'd guess you could average 12-15% an hour there.

Also, the trees took a bit longer to die than the spiders and ravens, and hit harder (even though there all around the same level) and I didn't notice much difference in xp, so if you can choose a different target, I'd do that.

One disclaimer, I had NO pox while soloing or grouping here, you do, I'm sure that's a HUGE help here.

Thyramon
Getting 62 is probably the closest thing to a hell level you'll have to deal with these days. The amount of xp required to go from 61 to 62 is a huge step up from the amount between 60 and 61.

Your best bet here is patience and perserverance. Put up your lfg flag and go from plane to plane /ooc'ing that you're looking for a group. A good tier 2 group is the fastest way to push through this level. However, any xp is good xp at this point, so don't be too picky, take any group you can get that nets you xp.

Some of the places I ground out level 62 were droga (bring a decent group and you can do well here), LDoN, any plane I could get a group in, Chardok B, and Veksar. Some were better than others, some were easier than other, and some were just fun for a change of pace.

The trick is to just push through it, once you get 62 and can get a group in BoT, you'll be able to grind out to 65 in a few days time.
