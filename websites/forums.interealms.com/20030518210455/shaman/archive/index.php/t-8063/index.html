Keg of Beer- Neriak Foreign Quarter: Darien Felton loc 140, -80 right across from Soulbinder

Bundle of Weapons- Grobb: Ootok Loc -240, 40, right to the left as you come in the zone.

Ball of twine- Feerot: Fugla loc 965, 1255 Stone huts to the north east of the bridge near Oggok.

Purification Tablet-West Freeport: Toxdil Loc 620, 200 Right outside the gate on the north wall is a secret passage, run through the wall to find him

The armor assortment still comes from Thurgadin like in the good version, as does the case of meat from Bazaar.

All of the items are lore and no drop.

Iksars as far as I am aware can not do this quest unless they have worked on faction.
