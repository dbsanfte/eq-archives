First of all, the lower your level, the higher your resists need to be.

For most Vox/Naggy raids, the lower level limits is about level 44 (although Clerics as low as level 39 are generally welcome). At level 44 you would be aiming for probably 150+Fire/Cold and 125Magic resist (with buffs), but at level 52 you would probably get by with 125 Fire/Cold and 100Magic.

My advice is to aim to get magic resist and cold(Vox) or fire(Naggy) as close as possible to 100 using only gear - i.e no buffs. If you can do that, then with buffs, you should be in good shape. Remember that you can use level 53+ players for pre attack buffing, so you can get all them uber level 60 buffs before the fight commences.

Bards - Bards are the kings of Vox/Naggy raids - they can sing songs that raised fire/cold/magic by 71 - and that will seriously cut the damage that your main tanks will be taking. It is probably best that bards do not engage in melee - the ripostes alone can kill em
