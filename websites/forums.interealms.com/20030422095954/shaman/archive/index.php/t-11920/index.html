Nytmare
There are two Iksar slaves in the cell. The one for the cudgel quest is on Scaled Mystics faction and gives a negative faction hit. (The other one is neutral and gives no faction hit.) With max faction, I killed the iksar slave and then turned in bone chips to Trooper Mozo until max again. These are his faction hits:

-20 with Scaled Mystics (80 bone chips worth)
-5 with Legion of Cabilis (20 bone chips worth)

Not that big a deal, but if you insist on avoiding the faction hits, the only way is to have someone else kill him for you.

For the previous cudgel quest, casting identify on the skull canopic says "Item Lore: crafted by Vok Nir". This gives the clue of who to talk to and what to say. If you go to Galdon Vok Nir and ask him "did you craft this Dai Nozok skull canopic" he'll start giving you answers.
