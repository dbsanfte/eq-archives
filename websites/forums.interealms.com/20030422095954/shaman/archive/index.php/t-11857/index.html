Duurg
I solo'd one for fun not too long ago, first attempt fail'd due to him being so MR and not landing a slow.

Second attempt was like this:
If you pull any CG before roughly 8pm EQ time, you will get an add of a goon or two. I usually waited until 9-10pm to pull the CG close to the PoK port up. Had lvl 61 pet with 2 lvl 62 mage summon weapons, FoSoul and haste on pet. Pull CG with malo, sicked pet, then either cast the lvl 58 malosini for -65 to all resists due to his high MR, then I'd slow. He is also fairly resistant to Pox/BoN, or I was unlucky to get them to land. So I just used JBB, epic and pet. I spot heal'd with Tnarg's (no torpor) or Kragg's. If you get aggro, let him beat on you for a second, then slowly back off, and pet will get aggro. I'd also canni 4 (no canni 5 yet) when I needed more mana, it was easy to do, since pet had aggro and CG's attention.
I fought it before VI "fixed" the Coldain Military Wristguard focus effect, which made it so I only had to slow about 3 times or less. A normal fight would require about 5 times slowing, roughly about every 13-16% damage done to the CG (very rough guestimate). Same with applying malo/malosini. Watch him as he'll enrage at about 10% HP or so, and then he'll run a little bit.

I've only seen lvl 58 spells, so if he does drop lvl 60 spells, it is uber rare and not worth the fight.

Also, if you fight close to the PoK portal, you can get out that way if worse comes to worse. You'll be summon'd a couple of times, just keep running to it and you'll get there. Just pray you don't get stunn'd as he'll make a mess out of you.

Max damage was below 160 or so I believe, at an AC level of about 1180ish. Buff's were KEI, FoSoul, lvl 62 sta, agi, group acumen, sow, lvl 61 regen, and Virtue.

Good luck
