Scarus
I tried Barbarian and Ogre before settling on a race (back before Luclin so no cats or frogs, and Trolls have always looked like arse). While I like the unstunability of Ogres, the chain look of Barbarians, and the slam they both share, in the end I went with Iksar. Why? Because despite faction issues (the old world thinks we smell of wee) and a deep fear of that 20% XP penalty they... spoke to me.

I love the lore, atmosphere and look of Kunark, I like the idea of a strong, noble civilisation that was brought low, but is now poised to rise from the ruins of its cities and reclaim a fallen empire.
And let's face it, reptilian argonath are just plain cool.

I put 25 points into STA, 5 into WIS (more hit points to cannibalise into mana, and we get strength buffs from the go so 70 STR doesn't hurt as much as you might think) and haven't regretted it.
As for the XP penalty, while I can't say I have never thought "I'd have levelled already if I were a Barb" it certainly hasn't hurt me. I'd say the regen, especially for a shaman without any real twinking, balances it out once you get canni.
