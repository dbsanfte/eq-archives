Hiya Nruter,

Let me see if I can answer your question about shaman dot stacking. The first thing to know is that shaman can stack multiple dots of the same line. So, for example, I could stack level 56 bane of nife with the level 49 ebolt. One shaman can not stack the SAME dot on a mob, so I couldn't stack bane of nife and bane of nife.

Now multiple shaman can stack the same dot on a mob. I could land bane of nife and a guildie could land bane of nife and another guildie could land bane of nife. (each mob only has 15 slots though). In a group, you can both stack pox, or both stack bane, or both stack epic.

Multiple shaman can land the same dot, single shaman can not land multiple casts of the same dot.

In reading the threads here I see most people recommend a combination which includes EBolt or Plaque instead of Pox/BoN, is this only for mana reasons?

Perhaps they aren't the correct level to scribe PoX/Bane? Or maybe due to rarity, they don't have them yet. I usually stack pox/bane.

I can't answer your questions about the manafree clickable items, I don't have an poison/disease clicky dots.
