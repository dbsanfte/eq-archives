The whole point of the faction work-around is to skip Kirn in The Hole. Since you're already at max, you didn't need to kill Kirn, and you don't need to do Neh either.

You can kill Neh if you want to feel good about completing that step. She drops a no-rent Key of Torsis that works on the elevator in the castle, but your rogue works just as well for that job. She can drop other loot too.

No you can't add to max faction, because, um, it's maxed?

The negative faction hit you're talking about happens AFTER you receive your Spear of Fate, and doesn't affect anything except preventing you from hearing the Spirit Sentinel's final speech when you complete your epic.

Later in the game if you acquire another Child's Tear for MQ, you can easily go and rebuild your Truespirit faction back to max just like before, no problem there.
