Aaellyn
well in short, a spell set is a snapshot of whatever spells you have memmed when you create the set

for example i have a damage spell set .. includes ice nuke and bane .. and nothing else .. while my MGB set has fo7 and group sta .. and nothing else .. it just lets me unmem 2 spells and get 2 others up without all the clicking and though the chat box.

if you leave spell slots blank in a spell set, the spells you have in those slots aren't affected when you mem a set.

I have 10 spell sets i use and just for convenience sake. I've found them to cover just about everything.

1 - group - this has my normal group setup .. includes a root, a nuke, FA, and the regular staple of malos, slow, quiessence, heal, canni
2 - raid - this one only has focus of soul, single sta and FA on it
3 - shrink - just shrink
4 - mgb - i have fo7, group sta and tribunal in this one, replacing root, nuke and FA .. i use it for mgbing .. and random single group buffing
5 - debuff - i keep malosinia, and cripple on this one
6 - disease - this one replaces malos and slow with incidious decay and grummus .. for those times where disease will do
7 - haste - loads up single and group haste
8 - ranger - i keep this one for the odd requests (usually by rangers) .. bih-li, diaku, and probably PE if/when i get it finally
9 - damage - replace root with bane and FA with BoS
10 - solo - i keep this one up for the very few solo times i have, includes bane, BoS, Ulthor, a root, malos and no slow (more for fire than anything else)

and that's my spell list layout .. the max you can have is 10 .. if there wre more, i'd probably use more :)

I find them extremely useful.

I have one set for when I die and/or am buffing only myself, another for when I'm buffing a full group. Saves a LOT of clicking and paging through the book. I also have one for "rescues" after I die, that includes gate, levitate(the componentless version) and SOW(since when I'm dead I don't have my SOW boots) and invis, so with just one click I'm ready to SOW, lev, and invis myself to get back to my death spot pronto.

I also have two that are lifesavers for dot soloing/duo'ing, as these are often borderline panicky situations where I don't need to be messing up or taking an instant longer than I have to -- because if root breaks while I'm memming spells, I might be paste.

One has malos/malosinia/slow/malicious decay/root. I cast all those or sometimes skip slow, then mem up a set that keeps the root, dumps teh debuffs, and adds in only my DOTS that don't break root -- ulthor, saryrn, bane.

I actually have a third one for when I'm with either my SK or someone with a snare, that includes pox, but takes out any heal but Q, so I can just go pretty full bore on my dotting. I still like to add in pox when the situation lets me.

Anyway, this really saves time and eliminates some of the fudge factor and danger factor.
