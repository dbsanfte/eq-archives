Kriel Darkbane
If you do try this with such a small crew, use extreme caution. Try to do some research on the Taskmasters first. There's probably one or two you can succeed on.. almost surely if you get 60 and Torpor first. They have quite a lot of HP so will be hard with a small low-damage group.

BUT a couple are unslowable. One is unslowable and flurries (Kavamesh, I think?). If you pull him you have no shot at all, not even with a charmed pet. If you get any of the unslowable ones you won't win, unless they're rootable. I don't remember if they are or not, but think that at least some of them aren't, or are difficult. I certainly remember some runners when killing them... not sure if that's because they were unsnarable or we just didn't snare them.

A couple of them rampage, also. I think one rampages and is unslowable, not sure any more. One rampages and IS slowable, but he's still pretty badass and will wipe you guys out with the rampage if you're not extremely careful.

I'd say there's probably at least a couple you can kill - a couple are primarily casters and not very scary. But if you don't pick on those and get the wrong one you'll be kibble fast.
