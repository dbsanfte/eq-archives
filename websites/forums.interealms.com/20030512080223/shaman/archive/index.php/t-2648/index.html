AC & HP > Wisdom gear...

Yes, but I don't see it as an end-all be-all law. More of a general principle to help guide your gear decisions. Things also change as you hit certain benchmarks.

One benchmark is when you get Cannibalize at level 24.
Your wisdom and mana that used to be the sole determiner of your mana pool now aren't as important as your total HP. AC is very important. It becomes less so once you start hitting soft-caps, but that won't be a problem for you for a long time.

Hitting 45 and obtaining a JBB is another benchmark in my opinion. This completely changed my game. If you are an Iksar (I didn't notice) then hitting 45 won't mean much to you. The racial restrictions of this one item have created a large # of threads on these boards, but I would say most are in agreement that it is an extremely powerful item. Definitely get one if you can use it.

Hitting 50 is another, though lesser benchmark, as now more equipment choices that were level restricted become available to you. Finishing our comparatively easy epic starts to gain priority in your choice of weapons.

Once you have Torpor (I do not at 53) at 60 things will change again. I can't comment on these changes as I'm not there yet.

HP regen is also extremely important. You have that taken care of, courtesy of the monk's Fungi loaner. Flowing Thought items would also factor in, although not as much as HP regen items.

Focus Items... Almost all of the powerful shaman here did not start a shaman with the availability of focus items, so you are actually in newer territory here. The importance of these items (with a few exceptions) is much less at the end of the game then maybe for you currently in the Focus Item I level range. I would test things out and see how each item benefits you. Gloriana's advice is sound, so don't switch out your Fungi for a Lockjaw Tunic just because it has a focus effect. :p (Not to be insulting to think you would, but just to point out a glaring case.)

I personally set aside my Thurgadin #7-8 Prayer Shawl Quest as a high priority since I've obtained an Obulus Death Shroud. The mana savings to me is worth much more than a little AC/HP/Wisdom. My #4 shawl will probably sit in the bank for a long time since I can't stand the population that dwells in Velk's.

When I put on the ODS, I took off the Chokidai Hide Spaulders I had been wearing. Now I only where the Spaulders only for tradeskills...

Definitely dump the wisdom rings for HP/AC. At your level, courtesy of your monk, hunt or buy the easist pieces of JB armour for the AC alone at your current level.

Complete a search for equipment threads here, and in an hour or so, you will have a good idea of other gear choices to strive for.

Good luck, and welcome to the ranks of Shaman.
