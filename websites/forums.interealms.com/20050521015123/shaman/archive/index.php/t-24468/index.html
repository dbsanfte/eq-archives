I can't recall the exact names, but in regards to which are raid mobs:

Cipheron is for the Enchanter 2.0. Usually pops a chest if you have an enchanter on this step of the quest to loot the piece. Otherwise nada.
The red con Girplan on the east wall is for Wizard 1.5. Don't think we've ever seen loot off him.

Velkitoran, or whatever is a medium raid mob.
The Murkglider Hivequeen is a medium raid.
The Redwing lady is a medium raid.
Hulcor is probably the hardest of the actual raid-loot mobs.
All of the above generally drop 1 loot item and 2 runes of the 69/70 variety.

And there's a named Dragorn in the NW corner that's a red con, but we've never caught him up to see if he's a raid mob or not.

The rest are one group mobs, depending on how good your group is.

Sorry if I butchered any names, don't go there much.

Shadowhunter usually goes down to 2 groups when I've seen him. Champion is a hard single-group or fairly easy 2-group mob if you have a solid tank and a cleric plus backup healing (we killed him with 8 last weekend) but he's not worth killing cause he doesn't drop loot--he's an epic mob.

Red wing lady mentioned above is Pyrique, IIRC. She's a really interesting fight, but definitely raid material--she spawns 5 or 6 adds you have to tank or at least off-tank.

The raid-level Girplan is Guardian. Taskmaster and the other XXXmasters or whatever they are called are loot mobs.

Couple of other raid-level mobs there too, but I'm forgetting names at the moment.
