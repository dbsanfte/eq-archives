Gaknab
By far the BEST XP rate I have ever discovered.

Duoing with my Roomate a 60 Warrior in Maiden's Eye.

Zone in from DP, go straight out past the fortress and hang a left. Sit on top of the hill there right next to the lake. You run out and chain pull Umbrous Toilers, or anything else close.

The Warrior sits on hill and kills everything you bring in.

IMPORTANT!!! Keep the Warrior in Berzerker frenzy, his DPS goes WAY WAY up. Crippling hits Rule.

Buffs used:

Khura's Focusing (or FoS)
Riotous Health
Acumen
Primal Avatar
Celerity
Regrowth of Dar Khura

We've done this many times. The xp rate is almost 6 blue of ALT xp per hour for a Troll Shaman. I haven't come across anything to beat it yet.

I do the same with my warrior and shaman. But I take a right at zone in from Dawnspeak and head over to Akheva Ruins. It is the large domed building with what looks like a giants mill wheel over turned at the entrance.

Once in AR, invis up and head straight inside to a fallen pillar. Set up camp on the far side of the pillar to the left, near what was the base of the pillar. Nothing paths through there and nothing spawns there.

A level 60 can single pull all day long with lots of mobs to pull.
