Ximix the Troll
Here is how I solo in tactics:

Zone into the GY and head upstairs. Pass the intersection with the Tallon / Vallon raid content and head toward the pit area. Before you get to the pit, you will find an empty little room off to the right.

I pull Diakus with Malo and run into this little room. They are easy to solo pull so long as you are slightly careful. Slow them as they arrive, and then root with VP / Petrifying Earth. I then root rot them with Ultor/Pox/Blood/Bane/Epic dot, throwing in Spirit Call whenever it pops up.

Soloing Diaku definately takes more mana and greater risk than Golems in Valor. I have no clue which is better exp / hour. Some of the Diaku mitigate slow, and these can be nasty on root breaks. Diaku take a while to root to death too, since they have pretty high hit points. No horse in Tactics either.

It is slow going, but I have gotten a Spectral doing this (off a regular Diaku). I usually get bored after 2 or 3 kills though and go look for a group.
