After 2 failures and a bunch of scrounging up the best 24 we could find online, we finally beat Relv's quest. We expected something grand out of this, perhaps entrance to bastion of thunder. When Falto finally dropped we went nuts, loot was linked -

Spectral parchment, some 40 hp/mana boots, and a thunder crystal, oh my.

I was (happily) awarded the crystal and we all gathered around Relv. Chatting got no unique responses, so we turned the crystal in.

You have gained a character flag! .. well no not really, one could only hope.

instead we got this (relatively speaking) piece of **** - Ruby Pendant with a Gold Chain

Magic Lore
Slot: Neck
Wis: +8 Int: +8 HP: +75 Mana: +75
SV Poison: +15
Recommended level 53
all/all

at first glance this is not bad, probably the highest hp/mana droppable neck item in the game. But then you look a bit closer.. this is the 1 big reward from Relv's quest? Perhaps you don't know relv's quest.

24 people max limit. 3 hour time limit. Kill 120 mobs, 30 level 62 mobs that split into 3 level 58'ish mobs when they die. The boss is Falto, and among other things quads for over 1600 and has the classic turgurs immunity that most warrior-class giants in storms have. Let me refer you back to that 24 person max limit.

The quest takes a solid four groups, the reward is totally out of balance, and through our best efforts we've found zero link between Askr and relv, or any other plots in storms. What that means is Relv's quest is purely a loot quest, theres no side benefits, it's a one shot one-necklace deal, with a horrible risk/reward ratio.
