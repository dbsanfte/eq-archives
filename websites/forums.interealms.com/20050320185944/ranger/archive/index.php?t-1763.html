For sure HKH is a great place for exp. If are lucky enough to play on off hours there is a chance that you can get the basement to yourself. Start at the top floor. Harmony then pull using your arrow. Pay attention to the pathing of the mobs and you will know which ones to harmony. The great thing about HKH is that the guards are very close. At lvl 36 or so I was able to solo the warrior room and was getting a full blue per kill. At 39 you can hold the raider room and still get a full blue per kill all the way up to 42. It is possible to still get decent exp up to 43 but you will only see blue raiders about 1 in 10 so I'd say at 42 it time to hit EW.
