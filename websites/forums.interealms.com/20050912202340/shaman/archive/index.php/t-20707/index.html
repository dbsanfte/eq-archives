Used to practically live in this zone when it first came out, now just a few trips for twinks/PL'ing.

Hard LDoN does give more xp-wise, but this zone I find truely FUN and enjoyable. (nice self paced dungeon crawl)

Names usually spawn at a decent repop rate. Occasionally you won't have a named in a clearing or 2, but that is rare. From SolB side (in upper bugs) you zone into the kobold side, from SolA you zone into gobbies. Note: When zoning from SolB, if you stay above the lava you will zone into SolA. Swim under to hit solc. Also, succor takes you to the sola/gobbie side zoneline.

As mentioned the most notable pice is the Ring of Firewarding
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55233
This drops from Spiritmaster Heili (kobold). He's in the 1st room left of zone in (all the way back). Room usually has 2 other kobolds in there with him. He is a set 4 hour spawn. Can also drop 2hs http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55234
and 1hs http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55239 The ring is is rare drop, 2hs seems to be most common.

Most of the names have certain areas that they spawn more often in, however, ANY model of that type can spawn the names. Goo, Mephit, Imp, Fire Elem, Kobold, Goblin, Beetle. Not sure about the fire giant (Typhoeus, or something like that - he may be a set PHer)

Goo (Pulsating Pool of Magma) drops necklace of the mirage seeker (rare) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55225 The good hide under the lava pools. Can usually just barely see their 'eyes?' peeking out. Otherwise, you don't know until they charge you. The 1st pool just past the kobolds is a 3 spawn. All 3 at once can be fun, and if one is the named, it's very interesting fight. Usually, the spots under bridges are where the goos hide out.


Mephit (Kurshara Perecaran) drops all/all sleeves http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55218 and dagger http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55228

Kobold (Tybog Aladmond) drops BST/MNK 2hb (rare) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55262 and caster/priest shawl (common) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55216 Tybog is by far the most common pop in SolC. Rarely a spawn set goes by w/o him. Seen him pop 3+ times in one set (continuously clearing).

Imp (Erpar Flamegar) drops all/all ring (common) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55214 and all/all mask (rare) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55232

Fire Elem (servent of flames) drops CLR/PAL/SHD slield http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55227

Goblin (Xaon Bulzekle) drops all/all shoulders (rare) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55229 and caster belt (common) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55223

Beetle (Gigantic Ashwalker) drops plate boots (common) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55219 mask (all minus war, int caster) (rare) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55231

Fire Giant (Typhoeus) drops cleric hammer (rare) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55230 and plate BP(common) http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=55217

The Ring of Fire even is found just past (left) of the tenticle terror mobs. It's 8 (?) fire elems standing in a circle around a large lava pool. If ring is active, when last one dies, you get a message (if standing close enough, small range) about "somethig stirring under the lava"... Then there's a 4 pop (lvl 65), then a 2 pop (yellow con, 66-67) then the named, Protector of Fire (red con). He mitigates slow, and can really hurt your tank if not really on the ball before it gets slowed. One groupable. The pulls aren't a problem, pets can single pulls just make sure to stand far enough away so that the mob doesn't get you on the aggro list too soon. For some reason they don't assist when it's just the pet attcking them. Call the pet back with the /pet hold AA and you're golden, BAM one mob! I would snare the mob when it got far enough away from his buddies to avoid their assist. Been using this trick quite frequently. When I spammed my brand new ring in guild, everyone else wanted one so I took a few more peeps down and got 2 more over the course of a couple days. The ring dropped for me 33.3 percent of the time, as did each of the weapons that the Spiritmaster drops. Since I was boxing, I bound at the zone in area, yes you can bind here, which made escaping easier. Spiritmaster down, get loot, gate to zone, either camp or spiritstone out, then run SK through by flopping out or training. I would kill fairly fast on the 22 minute respawn and only get 2 respawn at the beginning. But there's the gate thing so you can avoid the fighting once it's all over :D .
