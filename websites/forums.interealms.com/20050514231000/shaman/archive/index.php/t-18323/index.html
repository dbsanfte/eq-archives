Kriel Darkbane
What tactics are you using to solo? My 58 shaman does pretty well solo'ing in PoN on yard trash. I pull with Malosini, then Immobilize, then Turgur's, then summon my drogmor and start root-rotting. If root breaks due to the DD on the dot's, I finish refreshing any dot's that are down and reroot, and back away. This works pretty well, netting me something around 8% of a level an hour, maybe more, with a KEI buff. It's not great, but I can do it while LFG is up. I don't even bother using dogdog here, as having to heal him constantly isn't worth the damage he can do. Keep the mob rooted, and slowed in case root breaks, and you don't have to heal hardly at all, except to help with canni'ing. Tanking in PoP is a very bad idea pre-Torpor, I think. A drogmor/horse and KEI help a ton, of course.

VL is a good spot to solo, I think, if you can find the floor level mostly uncamped. I did that some at L 57 and found it quite easy. The mobs don't hit hard, so you can use dogdog to tank, while you meditate, if you moderate your dot'ing. I found I could typically slow (maybe malise of some sort first), dot, root, and med, while dog tanked. Eventually root would break, but the dog would tank the majority of every fight and not need much healing.
