With the addition of focus items it seems like there will be a large shift in effective healing strategies. For example, it looks to be fairly easy to get level 2 versions of healing enhance, spell haste and mana preservation. Getting level 3 versions of these items will be much more difficult. With that, our old level 29 Greater Heal will be significantly more mana efficient in healing over a level 51 Superior Heal. For example:

Spell Mana Heals Cast HP/mana HP/sec
GH (no focus) 150 270 3.75 1.8 72
GH (max focus) 135 324 3.18 2.4 102
SH (no focus) 250 525 4.5 2.1 116
SH (max focus) 225 630 3.82 2.8 164

As you see, a max focused GH is more mana efficient than a no focused SH... though the superior heal is faster. Of course max focused SH is WOW! The max focused GH is more mana efficient then unfocused Cleric heals except Remedy, Divine Light and their heal over times.

With these changes we are significantly better healers :-)

PS, does anyone know how focus items effect heal over time spells?
