You will use malo all the time. In non-pop exp groups its nice, in PoP groups its necessary. Tier 2 mobs can whack you for 700 a shot, and a bounced slow will get you agro. Malo then slow will land slow 99% of the time with no resist on exp mobs.

Malosini has the same chance to be resisted as slow. You can bounce it several times before it sticks. True, the agro is low on a bounced malosini... but you arent doing your group any good by delaying a slow that long. Once you get the hang of casting malo as soon as the mob is in range and slowing as soon as the gem refreshes, you can have a mob slowed (with minimal agro) the instant it reaches your group.

As for malosini after slow lands.. mages can cast malosini too, let them contribute something :p

As for the original question... Pox vs Malo:

Get both, but hold off on buying them and let the prices drop. You can scribe Pox at 59, Malo at 60.

Pox is very useful. If you are soloing, Pox + Epic, + puppy while you tank will kill nicely. In PoP where tanking is risky, its not as useful. Thats where you want the new dots -to root and rot.
