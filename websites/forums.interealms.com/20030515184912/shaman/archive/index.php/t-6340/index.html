Depends on a few things, but my advice:
Go with what Glori said, OR a Sarnak Battle Shield to replace your current shield. Pay less than 1kpp for this or don't buy it. The AC + hp improvement would be significant for you.

Look for the most bang for the buck with what you are already wearing when it comes to upgrades when money is tight.

The new rings would add 12 raw ac and130hp at a loss of 12wisdom (no big loss really). Heck, even PLATINUM fire opal rings would be much better (5ac 55hp, much cheaper than velium to buy).

You really should have a better ac headpiece. Keep the othmir fur one for tradeskills if you need wis gear (and your wis rings), but otherwise sell it asap & replace it with something with more hp/ac. I'm not up on lizard wearable headgear. Use the search feature in the bazaar & check often on prices for at least a week before making a purchase. Use your guild and higher level friends for trustworthy pc's.

Goranga Idol would be a better range item, & you should be able to pick that up relatively cheap or hunt one on your own with some friends.

I'd see about trying to get an even trade on the acrylia studded cloak for an arctic wyvern one. You get more ac at a minor loss of wisdom, but you gain decent stamina (more hp) too.

I'm not up on lizard equipment, so sorry I couldn't be of more help.
