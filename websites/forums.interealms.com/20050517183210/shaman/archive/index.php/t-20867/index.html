Disclaimer
The following is my personal analysis of the current state of the high end shaman. It does not necessarily represent the views of other shaman who post here or of the Crucible as a whole.

It is written from the point of view of a post-time shaman. As such it deals mainly with high end issues. Please do not attempt to apply my conclusions to such places as BoT, Cazic Thule etc.

It is applicable for parts of PoP, hard LDoN adventures, LDoN raids and GoD, especially the single groups trials.

Yes, shaman do fine in Plane of Valor. Or rather, shaman for whom Valor is appropriate content contribute adequately compared to other classes with similar equipment.

Now imagine that the shaman grouping with you had their heals halved, their heal over time spells cut by a factor of 5, weren't casting slow because it is mitigated to worthlessness, could not buff stats and could not cast DoTs.

Would you still think they do fine?
