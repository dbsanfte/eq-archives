run the "traders of Shadowheaven" quest several times. I took me 6 runs to be at least apprehensive with all SH factions. If you bind at Henry and gate back you can do that in less then 30 min, don't forget to refresh sow in the bazaar, only zone it works.
Nytmare
I don't know the minimum you need, but running Trade Commissioner Henry's quest 20 times put me at max Traders faction and apprehensive for the three House factions. Another 20 times brought House factions up to amiable. So it seems this quest increases Traders by 100 points and Houses by 10 points each time.

You can also do Steano/Gearo quest that raises Traders and Stout faction (not sure of the others, might raise them all) and Mistala raises Traders, Guards and Fordel (Pally side) faction.

Gearo only involves SH and Bazaar, it's much easier to do than the Henry quest. BTW, Gearo seemed to be broken this weekend though. He wouldn't give me the gems to take back but still accepted the tailoring items with faction. About 20 trips got me to apprenhensive.

Did the Mistala quest 3 times (run out to Dawnshroud and back) and that got me indifferent to Fordel.

I've only done the Henry quest twice.
