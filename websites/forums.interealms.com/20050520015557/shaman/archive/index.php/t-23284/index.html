Greetings!

My guild Clan Ta Veren on Terris Thule (http://www.clantaveren.com/) is looking for a few good shamans. Currently we have 2 “full time” and 2 “casual” shaman but the two full time players are getting busy in RL so there have been raids without shaman support. While I am not an officer I would like to see my guild get the proper shaman support even if I can’t be online.

We are a raiding guild that is working in the Tacvi zone at this point. Our usual raid times are 4-11PM PST on weekdays and longer on weekends. We love good challenges and progression but yet we take of our own people (we did A LOT of backflagging raids) Loots are distributed on a DKP system and raids are mandatory, as in you can’t log off and play a toon just because the mob doesn’t drop any loots that interest you.

Requirements:
First of all we are looking for people with HIGH playtime. You HAVE to be level 65 with at least about 100+AA’s. More requirement details can be seen in http://www.clantaveren.com/join.htm

Ideally you should be at least Kod Tav flagged, since you should be motivated enough to have the 6 one-group trials done. Cross-server applications are accepted but only the exceptional ones will be considered.

Attitude:
From our page: The most important thing we expect from our applicants is a positive and proactive attitude. We are looking for players that will make an impact in the guild -- highly motivated self starters who realize their enjoyment of the game is ultimately in their hands. We are looking for players who love their class and enjoy playing it in a raid environment. Also, at the level that we play, you will die. A lot. If you are not willing to beat the learning curve of new encounters, and stick to it until the challenge is defeated, this is not the guild for you.

While we are proud of our accomplishments, we prefer to keep things to ourselves. If you like to shout things like “look at me I am super l33t!” or have the impressions that guild chat must be filled with obscenities, we are probably not compatible with each other.

That’s all I can think of now. If you are interested please feel free to apply via our site. Or if you have any questions, please /tell me in game or email me at abcxyz@gmail.com.

Thank you for your time.
