Slot: FINGER
AC: 5
STR: +8 DEX: +7 WIS: +6 INT: +6 AGI: +7 HP: +90 MANA: +80 ENDUR: +80
SV FIRE: +9 SV DISEASE: +9 SV COLD: +9
Required level of 50.
WT: 0.0 Size: TINY

Race: ALL
Slot 1, Type 7

The mob is The Fabled Eldak Howlingbear in Frontier Mountains - to get to him, head to the giant fort near the centre of the zone, then go straight through and into the tunnels. At the end are two giants, Eldak and Mentrax, both on a 20-minute timer.

Regular Eldak is L50 and drops nothing, Fabled Eldak doubles for 552, is mostly slowable, enrages and (presumably) summons. I used the same tactics as with the Fabled Duke in Seb, but it was overkill - Fabled Eldak was dead before the wolfpack went home.

As Eldak or the Fabled Eldak spawns every 20 minutes and the ring isn't lore, I can imagine this being a popular camp for non-raiding players!
