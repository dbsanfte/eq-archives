After emailing back and forth numerous times, I received word that a fix has been put in for those of us who did the Swarmfiend and never received the chest. The bad news is - you have to kill the swarmfiend again . . .

Greetings xxxx,

After conferring with the Development team, the answer that I received
is that the fix was implemented so that you can go back and do that
event again and the chest will drop. This was implemented because we
are unable to verify who did and didn't do the event, therefore we can
not reimburse for it.

The option of redoing events is only open to NPCs that are not triggered
at certain stages of the quest. The Swarmfiend, a regular spawn, will
work for redoing the event.

I hope that information helps and I'm sorry that we are unable to help
you further.

Thank you for your patience and safe journeys!
