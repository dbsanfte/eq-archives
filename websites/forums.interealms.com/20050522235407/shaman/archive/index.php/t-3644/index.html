ZEM - Zone Experience Modifier

Every zone has a ZEM. The ZEM is a multiplier that adjust the amount of XP that a mob gives for that zone.

In general, dungeon zone have a higher ZEM than outdoor zones. Underwater zones have a higher ZEM. The ZEM is supposed to account for the difficulty of the zone so that more XP is given in more difficult zones. I have no doubt that CT has probably one of the top ZEMs in the game. That's why a group of high-levels can kill a relatively low number of MOBs and still rack in a ton of XP. Without a ZEM, all mobs of a given level would return the exact same amount of XP.

When they announced that certain zones were being given a bonus of such and such percent, this meant that they increased the ZEM by that percentage. Example:

Permafrost old ZEM : 0.60 (I'm making up this value)
Permafrost bonus : 25% (I'm making up this value)
Permafrost new ZEM : 0.60 * 1.25 = 0.75

So the percent bonuses that were listed are generally useless in knowing the ZEM for those zones, because we don't know the old ZEMs for those zones.

The only way to find out the ZEM for a zone is to either be a developer who has access to the ZEM tables or to use ShowEQ to kill MOBs of varying levels and noting the XP given while creating a huge table, then corrolating the data to calculate ZEMs for all zones, using a single zone as the baseline (1.0 ZEM) zone :)
