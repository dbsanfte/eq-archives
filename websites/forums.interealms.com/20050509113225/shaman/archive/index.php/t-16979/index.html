When a mob mitigates slow, that slow is reduced by a certain percentage. I'm not sure how much the exact mitigation is, but it is something like 75%, 50%, or 25% mitigated (for slightly, partial, and mostly landed slows, respectively). Thus, a turgur's (75%) slow on a mob that partially mitigates will come out to 37.5% slowed. Grummus (40%) on that mob will come out to 20% slowed. So, basically, turgur's is always the best slow as long as magic lands. If not, grummus is the best.
