Top of the page (well, below this one now).

Trials are on Plane of Justice. Talk to Mavuin (track to him, he is at least 59, I think 60), and go through dialogue with him to get the character flag. Then go to the Tribunal, and say "I am prepared" I believe to start the trial. Your group will be teleported to the trial area and face something like 4 waves of 4 mobs each on a very strict time limit (this may be different for different trials).

I have heard that Lashing and Execution are the easiest, but I do not know for sure.

One last thing. You can only bring in one group, and they all have to be CLOSE to the person saying the line to the Tribunal. These are limited, non-raid level encounters, and they are rough. They seem designed for the 61-65 group levels, and the more below it the rougher it is.
