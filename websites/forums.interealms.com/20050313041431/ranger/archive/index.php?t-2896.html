After looking at a few options, it looks like you would need to setup the wheel's action in windows. (Default is scrolling)

Select Fxx (ex. F11) in window's mouse option.

Bind Fxx to the number that corresponds to the slot Range attack button is in under options in EQ.

This looks like one viable option.

Just dont touch that wheel when in town and have an NPC targetted :)
