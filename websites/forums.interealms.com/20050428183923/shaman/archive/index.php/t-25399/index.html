PennyAntics
First of all, there is mathematical and anecdotal proof that Windfury produces higher DPS than Rockbiter. Look on the WoW shaman boards for the exact numbers. As soon as you get windfury you should be using that instead of rockbiter in all situations. Flametongue and Frostbrand are situationally useful when mobs are particularly high-armor and low-resists. But Rockbiter is obsolete.

Also, I've found I rarely need to toss down three totems for every fight. Usually just one or two will give a helpful boost. When there is a boss, I will throw down everything in my arsenal, but if it's just isntance or group fighting, one or two totems do the trick.


As for getting and holding aggro, prep by always having lightning sheild on yourself. When a pull is incoming, hit it with Earth Shock. Earth Shock has a much higher aggro than the other shocks. The mob should come to you. Once it starts hitting you and recieving lightning shield damage, it should stay on you until it dies, unless you have absolutely terrible melee damage. Toss in another earth shock when mana appropriates.
