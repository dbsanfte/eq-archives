Regen is very nice and it is not like you are going to be able to twink on SZ where you can only have one character.

For much of your shaman life, regen will be the best racial ability you can have but both troll and iksar have it so it does little to help with your choice.

Trolls have nice mele stats and can use JB armor.
Iksars have high wisdome and an bonus to AC.

Iksar is a much easear shaman to play untwinked then a troll but in the end I would much rather play a troll. JBB is just a powerfull tool.

I personaly could care less about the snare neck.... I never found it to be all that horibly usefull because the snare effect is very week and of short duration and the cast time is long. I would rather just root runners and hammer them down.

If you are interested in the Iksar Cudgel quests and play a troll... pick Cazic Thule as your god and you will have less trouble with faction in Cabalis.

I played a iksar to 52 a troll to 36 (hated the new models) and now play an Ogre.

Out of the 3, the iksar was very easy to boot strap in comparison to either the troll or the Ogre.

Iksars have good natural AC, a high starting wisdom, and regen. All will help a lot at lower levels and HP are rather meaningless untill the end game.

Trolls will be harder to get up in level but stronger in the long run. Another thing to think about on SZ is that trolls have very low starting Fire Resist.

Hope this helps.
