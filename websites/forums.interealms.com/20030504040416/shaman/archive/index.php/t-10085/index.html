Well, group replenishment would be a lovely thing to have. I cast group regrowth all the time in raids, and clerics in particular ask for it. On the down side, replenishment has what appears to be an extremely short duration. I won't cast this one as a single unless I'm soloing.

Group boar is also a great thing to have. If you have 5 mins to get your raid buffed and your puller thinks, as in my guild, that 5 minutes = 45 sec, you will want this spell.

Group and single dex and str buffs are a waste of time. Verant was looking for padding for our spell list, and that's what they came up with.

The offensive spells are all good. I hear from people who have it that Tears of Saryn generates a fair amount of aggro.

High end debuffs are must haves.

Shamans get 3 Imbue Spells and they are not turn-in spells. You actually make these by making the following combines in a planar jeweller's kit (from PoK):

Imbue Justice: Alkaline Etched Stone, Raw Diamond, Spell: Imbue Diamond (from FP pally guild)

Imbue Disease: Bubonian Blood, Raw Diamond, Spell: Imbue Diamond

Imbue Disease: War Wraith Blood, Raw Diamond, Spell: Imbue Diamond

These are tradeskill related spells. Check EQTrader's corner under PoP Pottery if you're interested in what you can help make.


Just in case you didn't know, you can use ethereal parchments to get 64 Petrifying Earth if you do the handin to the necro parchment guy instead of the shaman. You can also hand in an ethereal parchment to the BL parchment guy to get the very handy level 49 spell Infusion of Spirit which stacks with the cleric spell Blessed Armor of the Arisen (focus line does not).
