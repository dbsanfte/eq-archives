don't bring pet up until you set up your camp. get your buffs lined up correctly so that you can invis yourself, then go to your camp spot and start to prepare. Always shrink yourself! Also, having full manna to start out with always helps. now the place I am talking about is very easy to find. get into sol a, and pass the initial pendulumn thingy. when you come into the opening where you can turn left or right (right is a dead end) or go down and striaght ahead (to where the vendor or bartender is). well we want to go left. keep going left and don't turn off the main path. if you followed this right (and assuming no one has killed any of the clockworks) you'll see one sitting in a little cove all by itself. there is a small room before that little cove. if you hide yourself from the gobies outside, then you will not aggro them. anyway, bring out y our pet and immediately tell it to sit! i usually only worry about hp/ac buff and haste for my pet. i sually give it a fine steel dagger as well (dunno if it helps or not). anyway, i start off with a root and send pet in to attack. if root doesn't hold (these guys are highly mr) just keep plugging away until you get one in. if you get one of those clockworks that proc knockback, you are just out of luck for now. just try your best to use the infamous malaise, slow, dot, dd, melee method. anyway, once you have cleared that clockwork, tell your pet to sit (i can't tell you how much you really need to pay attention to this). the key to pulling is to not get hit. if you got a caster on you (the gnome conjurers and curators) then your dog is going to attack. just try your best to keep dog in the little cove. anyway, just go out to the crossing over the lava and you'll see another one. rinse repeat him. out across the lava youll see another one. now, this is kinda tricky. at lower levels you generate sooo much aggro that sometimes you bring 2 more with that one. so what I did was malaisement, if others came running just get the heck out of there. if not, rinse repeat. the next pull is the hardest of them all. you almost always get 2. they are right across from one another. just slow one, and try to slow the other back at camp. never let yourself get hit out away from camp (because dog will come running).

word to the wise, if your dog gets stuck somewhere, just let him go. somehow it aggros all the gnomes. dunno why.
