Mixelplux
If it's a vital part of your role in a situation to draw aggro, what tools that you currently have in your repetoire would suit that purpose? Turgurs? Slow casting time and unless you have a click stick has a recast time of 6 seconds. I would say that is rather a useless tool for on the demand aggro ;)

I can't agree with the argument that not getting SCS is beneficial to us on the basis of needing to draw aggro. You need to draw aggro because you are tanking, you will load a spell best suited for that. Cripple probably would suit that purpose best if you don't want to use a dot. With a cast of 2.25 and recast of 2.25 (which is instant in regards to spell gem refresh), it's far more reasonable for quick aggro than ANY other spell you would have memmed usually.

On the note of if SCS is usefull for shamans? Hell yes. The times you want to draw aggro are far far less than when you don't. As often as you could save a raid by drawing aggro of a cleric, you'd wipe a raid from doing your full potential on a mob without SCS. Also, clerics have far better tools to help themselves not to die due to aggro than using us as a speed bump.

Also, all this depends on where you hunt. While I'd say that there really is no raid that not having is better than having it, there are quite a few raids that having it makes a huge difference.
Hunt in Uqua or above where getting a slow on a mob without drawing aggro is imperative, where resists can run high and chain casting slow is required, then you understand the power SCS has for us. Because, in these raids drawing aggro will mean a raid wipe more often than not. Mobs that one round you more often than not then spawn adds when you die are no joke.

Get SCS, while not as beneficial to us for all of our spells (dots), it's definately a huge boon.
