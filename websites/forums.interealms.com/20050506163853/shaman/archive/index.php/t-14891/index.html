Goldark
I've spent a good few days there now. The trash mobs are easy kills solo. I've not tried any mini-named solo but think it would be possible. However, the mini-named do mitigate slow down to "mostly effective". I was puller, healer and slower in a group with no tank - I tended to tank at first, and then mage epic pet would take over once it got some aggro going.

The mini-named mobs I've killed the most are the rabid chokidai. They drop 2 different earrings, chokidai hide boots, and a nice FT2 set of war beads (neck item). There's also the sarnak weaponsmith (drops a nice dagger and some other things) and armoursmith, a named ghost I've seen a few times, and a few others. The mini-named drop spells, including the level 60 kunark era ones. I've personally witnessed sunstrike drop. Over 4 days that was the only really significant spell I saw but there were plenty of other less notable ones.

The really crazy thing though is the rate at which these rabid chokidai pop. They are pretty damn common, in one evening I must have killed 10 of them and they always drop one of 4 items, all of which are reasonable and some like the necklace all the casters were drooling over.

Mobs are all pure melee only (except Koruscant), standards hit for about 220 max (normally less) and miss a fair bit (AC ~1050-1100). Quiessence/torpor is enough to keep up with one non-slowed mob, and once slowed they are very easy. Not sure how many hp they have, similar to somthing like PoI trash mobs I guess, perhaps a bit less.

There are a number of quests but I've not done any of these yet. They involve collecting 4 items - 4 staffs of suffering, 4 different pages, or 4 glittering gems. Although the gems and staffs are lore, if you identify them you'll find there are 4 different numbers, all with the same name.

Downsides are mainly getting to the zone, doing a CR if you wipe, and the size of the zone - its pretty small and once you get more than 4-5 groups there, mobs can get a bit sparse. Whilst the zone in area is almost all single pullable, further in you will get adds and crowd control is very useful. Root is sufficient but mezz much better.

If you don't have a non-kos cleric, mage, or necro with an EE, then the CR is really, really hard if you have a total wipe. You'll probably need to get outside help, as just getting to the zone would be hard enough.

Always take some pearls, an essence emerald and some coffins with you. Even if you don't need them, someone else is likely to, and its well worth making friends in that place. Sooner or later you'll probably need their help.
