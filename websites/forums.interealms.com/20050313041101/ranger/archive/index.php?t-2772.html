What and Why
Well yesterday I decided to parse a few different weapons in hopes of finding out a few things such as:

-- How much extra damage does Avatar give you?
-- How do 2-Handers compare to Dual Wield?
-- How much are we hurt by CoP not stacking with WP?

Results
So I tested differnet weapon componations and came up with the following results which do not include procs, kicks, or damage shield. It may be important to note that I have Combat Fury 3. The number in parentheses is what the DPS would have been with 40% haste.

-- Earthcaller + Swiftwind --> 44.9 DPS
-- Priceless Hammer + Swiftwind --> 48.0 DPS
-- Primal Warsword + Swiftwind --> 53.3 DPS
-- Primal Battleaxe --> 51.9 DPS (54.2)
-- Primal Warsword + Priceless Hammer --> 50.7 (53.0)

With each test I hit between 63.9% and 65.4% of the time.

How I tested
The test subjects were Mushrooms in Fungus Grove and I was selff buffed with Warders Protection, Natures Precision, Feet like Cat, Call of Flame (or is it Fire...). When I did not have Avatar I had Storm Strength up. I also had cleric buffs and resist disease (to hopefully minimize stuns, slows, etc.). I also used the cleric to heal and to make sure my Stamina bar did not drop too low.

I fought about 20 mobs per test and I put everything into individual logs. The parsing started at "Auto attack is on" and ended at "You have slain...". In order to make sure the parsing was as clean as possible and again to minimize the effects of the Shroom spells I dropped the lowest 6 results and the highest 3 results.

For haste with the 2-Hander I was stuck with a 34% haste cloak.

I also had some previous tests of Earthcaller + Swiftwind and Primal Warsword + Swiftwind.

Conclusions
2-Handers are very viable options for those of us without some of the great 1-Handers coming out of Luclin. With 40% haste my 2-Hander result would have been on par with my best Dual Wield result. If it were not so un-Rangerly I would probably use the 2-Hander for the good stats, eye candy crits, and low aggro. However, I am a Ranger and have no desire to use a big, bulky 2-Hander. In fact, I <i>may</i> go for Ambidexterity now, just to increase the gap between the 2.

Avatar added 11% to my damage output. That is how much more damage the Primal did over the priceless. Locz, I stand corrected on my initial thoughts on Avatar.

My prior results from when our buffs did stack were <b>exactly</b> the same for Epics and for Primal + Swiftwind. I did not have up anything more than WP and CoP on the older logs. And apparently using Natures Precision this time around compensated.

<b>Misc.</b>
Also in my testing I found some miscellaneous things interesting:

Double Attack percent ranged from ~35% with the Priceless/SW combo and the Priceless/Primal combo to 44% with the 2-Hander. Assuming it is 40% that would put Dual Wield at ~80%. I was sure DA would be higer :(

With Avatar up 3.5% of my hits were crits. With no avatar the crit rate was 2.5% . My Dex without Avatar is around 160 or so. Agility is 146ish. This is another item that I thought, and was hoping, would be higher.

Call of Fire procs were <i>very</i> interesting. With nearly every combonation I was getting 1.7 to 1.8 proc's per minute. That worked out to a measly 1.5% with the slowest combonation to an amazing 6.5% witht he slow 2-Hander. What this means is that proc rate <b>is</b> tied to the delay of the weapon and that Verant spoke the truth when they stated that weapons would proc about the same amount of times per minute regardless of the difference in delay. I noticed no real difference in procs with or without Avatar, leading me to believe that Dexterity does not impact spell procs. Tests I did ages and ages ago did show proc rate on weapon effects to be tied to Dexterity, but that information is long gone.

Thats it, sorry for the lenght of this post.
