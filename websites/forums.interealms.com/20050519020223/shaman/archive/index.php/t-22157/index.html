Chutoi
For those really bored, you can teach yourself languages with an alt. Put both you and your alt into the appropriate langage, make a hotkey, and spam away near a zone line. If I recall correctly, someone can train you to one higher than their own skill. Thus, bob (0) teaches joe to (1). Then they have to zone. Joe teaches bob to (2). Then they have to zone. Bob teaches joe to (3), etc.

Tedious, but that's how people had to learn the languages originally for all those obscure ones!
