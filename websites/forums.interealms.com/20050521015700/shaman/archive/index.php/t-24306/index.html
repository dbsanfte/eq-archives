A decent tank, burst healing, enchanter buffs, and dps were what these groups used to plow through content--I could help a little but made little difference. The best groups told me not to waste mana on slows. (Malos, Slow, Qs, Bane, Companion with weapons, casting all the time and cannibalizing as much as possible, I love to mix it up shoulder to shoulder with the main tank.) I do know that our spell set allows us to cope with a lot of different situations, and shaman can bring quite a bit to the table with any group if things get out of control. I like the character and refuse to roll another for that reason. That is why SONY no longer receives my cash each month.

In fact, the mitigated slow has been found to be a bit of a waste compared to good burst healing efficiency or good burst dps in fights lasting less than about 10 ticks. (Show me a stats thread if I am wrong in this--this is my recollection from recent threads).

I just don't want to be a charity case, waiting too long to get groups unless there are folks on with whom I have often grouped.
