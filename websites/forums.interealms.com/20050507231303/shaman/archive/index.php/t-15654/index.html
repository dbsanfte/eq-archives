Rorgg
My opinion is that it's worthwhile to attack until you get a horse. When you have the horse, you get much more mana efficiency by having attack off to get the med bonus than you do from meleeing. Whether or not you're TANKING is a completely different story. That depends on a lot of factors, including if you have any of the Heals over Time, what level of Dogdog you have, and which DoTs you have, not to mention what you're fighting. Even on PoP mobs, some I'll root-dot, some I'll let Fido tank, and some I'll tank (though not melee) depending on circumstances.

I'd say go ahead and get the mount. KEI I can do with or without, but when I don't have my lizard, I FEEL it.

Disclaimer: unless you're fighting inside all the time. But I find that unlikely.

Keep in mind too that when you turn 34 and get your pet, you will kill things much quicker if you tank it, buff and alpha your pet, and position your pet from behind so there are no parries, dodges, etc. Find a safe spot, single pull, if you ever get into trouble, just root it step back and heal, maybe throw a dot on or two.

before I returned to the game I played a rogue (started him a week or 2 after beta), thus I've got quite a hunger for melee - all the time (my magelo reflects this).

Having said that I'm always in the hunt for the _most_ efficient way to kill a mob and at level 42 its still to tank it in the face. Although now I am contemplating the trade-offs between dropping the mob faster by using the dots and saving some time versus saving the mana and spending a little more time tanking it.

I would recommend getting defense maxed out first before retiring to 24/7 rot 'n dot.

You've to to work at getting STR up to 150+ (only really a challenge at earlier levels for iksar/va shir/frog shamans) though to be semi efficient (aka land the majority of your hits). At level 49 you can do away with that though (much better str buffs).
