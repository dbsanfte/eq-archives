Frekkels
Hail,

In addition to the visual change, the extra wisdom, minor hp regen, and innate ultravision, bearform also offers these advantages:

1. Superior Climbing ability. If the surface has a slope other than vertical, you can generally climb it in bearform even if you couldn't EVER do it in your natural form. Climbing the wizard spires, the chasm walls of Western Wastes, or the hills and valleys of Mons Letalis (en route to Ssra on a CR w/ no batwings I found this very handy most recently) for example. Shrinking while in bearform makes it easier to wiggle through the Hole entrance (w/o a key) than in a normal large race bodytype (even shrunk in my experience).

2. ICE skating! Yes, it isn't as cool as the "ass-boarding" of days gone by, but if you are on certain flat surfaces you can ice skate while in bearform. My normal ice rink is at the ToV entrance room. I do this while waiting for the raid force to get grouped/buffed when bored, or when waiting for a rez (since I generally bind outside & zone-in after death). I usually bearform right after a death when there are peeping toms staring at my barbie booby beauties.

You can ice skate by ducking while in bearform and then moving around. Your paws will be locked in position, so there will be no "leg-moving" graphic as you run around. It looks kind of neat, and can be good for some comic-relief as you race an ass-boarding Ogre around the room. :D Great fun! (It doesn't work everywhere though.)

Take care,
Cragael
The climbing trick can also be achieved with shrink... it has to do to the relative distance between the 'bottom' of your model (i.e. your feets) and the 'center' of your model (usually the center of your upper torso, i.e. the point used to calculate line-of-sights for AE, spells and aggro).

The large the distance between the 2 points, the farther away the slope is from your 'center' point, the sharper the physic engine thinks the slope is and the slower you climb.

I always wondered if it was what they had in mind or a lucky artifact from a piece of bad code.
