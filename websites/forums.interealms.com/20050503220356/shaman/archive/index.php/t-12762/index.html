Nytmare
255 is the stat cap for level 60 and under. From 61 to 65, the cap automatically goes up by 5 per level. Also starting at 61, you can train AA planar advancement to raise your caps an additional 5. And you can train AA innate enlightenment to raise your WIS/INT caps an additional 10 points per level. So at 65 with AA points, your WIS cap can be as high as 355.

The fact that the caps can go up probably means that the displayed cap is indeed the cap and any extra WIS over the cap is lost.

Pre-61, the cap is 250. Your gear,buffs, etc may actually add up to more than 250, but anything above 250 has no bearing on game mechanics.

61+ (or maybe at level 60), the cap increases 5 points per level... so at level 61, a Shaman max Wisdom is 255, at 65 the cap is 275... additionally, there is an AA that will allow for even higher maxxes up to a in-game maximum of 305 Wisdom.
