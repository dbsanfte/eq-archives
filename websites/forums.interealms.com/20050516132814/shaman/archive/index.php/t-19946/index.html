Zatso
The answer was alluded to by Ruatonim. This is the same reason that DOTs like Festering Darkness which is only 50/tic but lasts a long timehave HUGE aggro. The amount of aggro and the amount of damage that is credited to you is figured when you cast the spell. It is the potential damage, not the real damage. If they did it any other way it would cause huge programming problems. This means if the DOT was one that would last 30 minutes at 5 hp/tic it would add up to a LOT of damage - maybe even more than the mob has. Doesn't matter - you are credited with that amount of damage for purposes of aggro and KSing. The answer is to not DOT in a case like this or use a short duration DOT.
