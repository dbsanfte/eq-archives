First I'll say that your gear is great, not much that is too terribly bad.
First I'd dump the veil...good wisdom but no AC. Get a Arctic Wyvern Mask, not too terribly expensive. Or Crystal Spider Eyes.

Leggings gotta go ! Get a pair of Rygorr or if you have money to burn get as much JB armor as possible.
Your shield is okay, but I'd use it for grouping only. I'd get a Sarnak Battle Shield or a Clay Guardian shield. Much better AC +HP's. Since you're a Barb get some Ivory Imbued Earrings.
Say goodbye to your belt, get a Strawspun belt (very cheap) or a Spider Fur Belt.

It all really depends on how much money you have to spend. Check the Magelo profiles of some Higher level Shammys to get a good idea as what to get next.
