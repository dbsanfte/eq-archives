I'm not playing now but the old fix for this was to delete all your texture cache files in the EQ folder, these have the extension .txc. They will be rebuilt the next time you zone. If you don't feel safe doing this (it is) just move all of them to another folder on your HD until you see if it works or just delete the affected zone .txc files.

Remember remove only dot txc files no others.
