I'm far from experienced but I can help you with a few tips from what I've learned so far.

-PoJ was a decent grind spot for grouping, but the 0 loot wasnt an option for me with spell costs coming up. Only grouped there once.

-PoD/PoI/PoN where you will probably be hitting alot to grind and solo in PoP from level 53ish to level 56ish.

-PoI/PoS try to get in here from level 56 plus. great exp compared to tier 1. Places that stick out as gems is the dillos in PoS and a good golems group in PoV.
