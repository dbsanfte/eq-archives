First of all, thank you, Henora, for allowing me and others with stories to tell this opportunity.

Basically, my only rules are these:

1: As a creative writer, censorship is a touchy subject for me (as it may be for you), but please respect the language filters set in place by Sunmoon (as this is his house). On a related note, please also try to keep the subject of your stories appropriate for a public forum (i.e. keep your work "work safe").

2: Please respect the authenticity of others' work. In other words, do not plagiarize! If caught, the theft of intellectual property can result in major legal issues.

That's really all I can think of at the moment, so enjoy your stay here!

Also, if you have some original work of shaman or EQ-related fiction, please share your work with the rest of us -- don't be shy! :)
