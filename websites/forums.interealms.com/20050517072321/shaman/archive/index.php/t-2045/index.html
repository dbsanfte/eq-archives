one of the benefits on a horse is if you are not in combat you are considering "sitting". In this case you get the benefit of mana regeneration without the agro aspect. You do not regen health any faster however.

There are several flaws about horses. One, they drown in water and poof. secondly, invis is still bugged on a horse, and thirdly, Levitate is whacked where you lose the floating.
Wargbearjumper
Horses...

Benefits -

When on Horseback, you are sitting, Hence, you are medding.
But only when NOT casting and NOT Meleeing.. when you are Idle, you are medding (Quadding Wizards LOVE this)

Endless Weight Allowance (Need to carry 80K PP somewhere... no problem)

Can be fast as all get out if you buy an expensive one.

Limited Safefall capacity (like 50 or so)

Downsides -

The can drown and they do bug lev and invis.
