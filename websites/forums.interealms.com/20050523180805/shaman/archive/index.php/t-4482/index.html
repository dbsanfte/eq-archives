Roxxanne
I have gotten great exp soloing the gazebos in CoM at 52 with a JBB and horse. Breaking the 3 spawn in each gazebo is a tough fight, but doable solo if you use pet and immobilize carefully. Pull with Immobilize, hitting the nearest mob facing your direction. Sometimes all three dont agro. If they do, get pet on one, root park one, and tank the third one. If it goes bad, gate to entrance and try again. Same thing with other gazebo.

Sometimes, another group have broken up some of the spawns for you, making it that much easier to get going. Once the spawns are broken, I could hold both gazebos and have time for a few other pulls from the approaches. Awesome experience.
Chutoi
I'd recommend the goo house in CoM. I did that up though 54 or so. Most of the goos will be blue until then. Be careful of the goo in the center of the central goo house. It is a higher level goo (the ubergoo!) than the other 2 in the house. You should be able to single pull all of them.

There are 2 houses just past the central goo house (towards the castle). There are also 3 goos in each house. However, it is very easy to accidentally get all 3 of them at the same time when pulling, so breaking them can be hard. Creep near them and use proximity agro to try to get singles. I don't believe any of the 2 houses have an ubergoo.

All told, with the central goo house and the 2 other houses, there are 9 goos. They are great exp. The ubergoo can also drop some spells and a club (wintry club?).

Breaking them may take some patience, but it can be done. Good luck.
