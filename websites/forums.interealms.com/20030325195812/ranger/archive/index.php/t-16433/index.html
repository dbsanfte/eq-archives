Amise
If your guild is largely unexperienced with PoS, you could find it takes longer than 8 hours to get to pegasus isle, but it's probably doable with some organisation. The first time I went there we made it to that island on the first day, but didn't finish clearing, simply because most of us were inexperienced with the place. I really enjoy PoS, I'd recommend you go there if you can, but do make sure you do your research well. For example, make sure you already have a plan of action mapped out should someone fall out of the plane (ie are you going to try to get them back, or forget about them). There is no CotH in PoS, so moving people around when they get left behind/fall off etc is a big time-wasting pain if you haven't worked out beforehand what you're going to do when it happens.

It is probably the easiest epic MQ to get, though, because each full clearing of the island yields 6 spheres.
