LaughingWoman
My man and I duo quite happily with all the combinations we've tried, and if a friend logs on, ANY class adds a nice new dimention to the group.

We usually grab some people to do elite quests, just to have them done earlier than we can duo them, but we've found - as was said above - that if we wait 2-4 levels, we can duo elite stuff that less experienced players will tend to only take with a full group. Bosses present a problem (level 20, deadmines for alliance, as an example), but luck, planning, potions, and special items (e.g. bag of marbles) can bring you the win. Which is why we usually just grab a group for a couple hours - more warm bodies will also tend to win it for you.

WoW is much more forgiving of error, you don't have so many 'noob disaster' errors as EQ, like broken mezzes and training. They're possible, mind you, but it's a lot easier for someone to remember to not hit the SHEEP, than it is a mezzed EQ mob.
