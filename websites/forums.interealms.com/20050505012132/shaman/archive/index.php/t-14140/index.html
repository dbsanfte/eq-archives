UgZug
You have picked three excellent solo classes... and have posed a very difficult question.

Shaman - can kill damn near anything solo (within reason) - but not necessarily quickly. We can keep ourselves healed, MOBs debuffed, and deal enough damage to eventually kill it.

Beastlords - short in the healing department, but tough and resilient and great DPS with their pets. They can kill a steady stream of medium dark blues - but I wouldn't take on a white or higher.

Bards - if you like INTENSE soloing... pulling 10+ MOBs and swarm kiting has been confirmed to be the FASTEST possible exp in the game (there was one particular situation in HoH where bards were bringing in 6-7 AA points per pull in about 45minutes time). This is the most labor intensive type of solo and probably only good for an adrenaline junky. You will fail often and die or cause others to die while doing it - but your net exp gain is still good.

Which is better? Depends on your play style.
