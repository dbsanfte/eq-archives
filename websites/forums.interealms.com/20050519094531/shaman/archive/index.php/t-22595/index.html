Tenjhai
Well I have a few time items, and I have tried this guy. It was for my friends Zerker epic, and well we didn't read up real well before trying this guy. The first time we tried with a 65 ranger (my boxed alt), 65 shaman (me), 65 chanter, 65 cleric and 46 Zerker. I pulled with ranger and didn't know anything about the ae's or anything.

First ae went of and he mezzed/stunned me. After I finally got him pulled he ripped through my ranger then killed the cleric immediately. Soon after the enchanter died and I was left alone, so I thought I would give it a shot.

Just using Bane, pet, meleeing and healing myself every once in a while, I took him down about 60% of his life. I tried to load Blood of Saryrn a few times for extra dps, but he can't be slowed so he kept closing my book while trying to load.

I would say the hardest part was trying to find him after he ran. Since my ranger was dead, I couldn't track. I would just swim around spam trying to cast a spell until he was in range.

His ae's are kinda nasty if not taken care of. One of them is based on your alcohol tolerance level (luckily I was around 190). The ae stun / mez can be countered by using a Crimson Potion, cost around 12 pp and can be bought at the potion vendors all over. This does I think 20 regen but also does 2 damage, so wakes you from mez right away. I didn't know about these potions the first time but they helped us own him easy the second time. Also, his melee proc is cold based, and since I'm a barb shammy the extra resists helped a little. I also used Rabid Bear while solo and that helped. I wasn't getting hit anywhere near max on proc, where as my ranger got hit for max everytime.

So to sum it up, I'm positive a more prepared shaman that had the right spells up with EP+ gear could solo this guy and just sharing my experience with the ugly leezurd worm thing =p
