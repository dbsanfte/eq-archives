ladikennoch
My quickest solo cash spot to this day is the PoJ basement....my suggestion is to go down there during off times and clear the small hall with 4 spawns, run into the big room and set up camp in the corner that is to the right of the door. There is 1 wraith spawn and then row after row of rocks n alien quarterbacks. This is a ridiculously easy camp to hold down solo and all the rock and alien dudes drop tons and tons of diamonds and other junk that vendor sells for 50-100pp each. If I am paying attention and actually killing fast, even pre-65, I could consistantly net 2-3k per hour. If they are all still dblue to you, you will need to use a root-pull strategy...they are aligned in rows of 2....root one and then immediately put fluffy on the "free" one and get it slowed. While fluffy bites your slowed one, turn back and slow and dot your rooted one, then return your attention to finishing the 1st one that is beating on you. They do not hit hard.

Only downside is that if you gate to go sell stuff, you will need to clear hall to get back down again.

If you find yourself comfortable straight up tanking them, then buy yourself a set of mage weaps for fluffy, they will die a LOT faster and save you oodles of mana.

/cheer
