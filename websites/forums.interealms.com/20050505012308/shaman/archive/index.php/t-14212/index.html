herkybevo
Bind sight is a spell which enables you to see from a PC's, NPC's, or mobs point of view. Your "spirit" will drift to their body. It is actually a pretty useful scouting tool. You can cast it while from your "new" perspective. It makes scouting a whole zone safe and fairly quick. It is also fun to watch you attacking the mob from its point of view. If I have missed something please post it.

Nytmare
39 Assiduous Vision has a longer duration than 19 Vision, otherwise they work the same. The range is zone-wide, so you can cast it on any mob in the zone -- allowing you to jump your vision from mob to mob or stay with a moving mob. It can be cast on anything targettable including PCs, NPCs, corpses.

It's useful for scouting around corners in dungeons. One time from the entrance of Chardok, I cast it on a corpse in my group who was dead deep in, then was able to target someone nearby who was trapped alive down there, to get them invited for Call of the Hero.
