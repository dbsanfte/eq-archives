I once bought a JBB for 15k. I loved it-- I couldn't see how one could live without it. But then I traded it and 10k for a Dathor Great Hammer. I kinda regretted it but Dathor Great Hammer was awesome. I scrounged up for another one and bought it for 25k, and the Dathor Great Hammer + JBB combo was amazing! Eventually I got epic and sold my hammer for 60k and bought other stuff with it so that great synergy was gone (thinking back though, it really wasn't that great-- I was just trying to convince myself that it was cool).

Then I hit 61 and gained about 30 AAs or so, without having Torpor. I got so desperate that eventually I traded 35k, an Obulus Deathshroud, and my beloved Jaundiced Bone Bracer for it. I figured I would prolly not need my Jaundiced Bone Bracer as much if I had torpor.

I was right! JBB really is outdated once you get JBB and especially if you have canni 5. In an xp group the best damage you can do is rain-- if you have a somewhat decent tank you can rain without fear of agro and still do great damage. If you need to overburn you can cast two spear of torments between each rain (or alternate rains)-- of course, you'll agro. But in normal situations you just don't have time to JBB. Malosini, Turgur, Cripple, back off.. canni maybe.. rain, canni canni, rain, canni canni.. there is just no use in using a JBB and it just decreases your potential damage output since you can regenerate mana quickly with torpor and canni, and thus cast much faster more damaging spells. I eventually replaced my empty bracer slot with a Barbed Rubicite Bracer to up my hitpoints for Canni 5.

Soloing, there really isn't any point in JBBing either, especially in Tier 2 when you *want* root to stay there. In tier 1 your dots and your pet tear up the mob quickly enough that really a JBB is pretty negligible.

JBB is amazing 45-61 or so. Past that it is pretty sucky and if you keep depending on it it will limit your potential for damage imo.
