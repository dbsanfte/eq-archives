I see alot of talk on the boards about shaman wanting upgrades. We are falling a bit behind but where does out power come from. I have always seen the shaman as the melee buff king. Acumen, for the stamina regen which is now sadly obsolete. FOS, for the strength, dexterity and a side order of hitpoints. Strength raising attack rating and dex adding more procs from the melees weapons. What I really want is for FOS to become the melee equivalent of an enchanters VOQ/KEI. Every pick up group I am in I hear the wizard or the cleric say "I'll be right back, running to pok for KEI." With melees basically maxed str and dex, our meleeing enchancing buff has become nothing more than a hitpoint buff. What i would love to see is them to revamp the FOS line of spells. Not a new buff that would take up yet another buff slot, but a soild FOS update that makes melees cry without it. Adding time level melee focus effects would make this happen.

I would like to see the following added to FOS
+30 accuracy (lowers hit/miss ratio)
+30 combat effects (increases proc rate)
+2 shielding (lowers damage taken)
+20 Strikethrough (You swing, they dodge, you strikethrough and hit them anyway)

This would make melees stronger and give us more of a role. Maybe these number are too low, or too high. I just want to see melee get more from FOS than 544hps.
