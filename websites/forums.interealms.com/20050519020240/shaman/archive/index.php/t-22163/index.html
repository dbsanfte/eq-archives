The shield's won't be blocking anytime soon. That's what Brenlo and the other mod's said over on the eqlive forums.

Also basically how shields work: Every class has a 'soft cap' on their AC. Untill this cap is reached AC gives the full benefit to what ever it does (mitigation,aviodance). After this cap you get diminishing returns (like stats). What a shield does, is it raises the soft cap, and not only gives you more AC, it makes the AC you have more effective (basically). I don't have a link right now, but all this information came from posts from the developers over on the steel warriors and the eqlive forums.

Another useful tidbit of information: The defensive AA's (LR,ID ect) function by raising their respective AC softcap (this is what the same dev said). This means you actually need to have a decent AC to get the full effect of these, however again this same dev said that a 65 war's w mediocar gear was above the soft cap, and i would assume the same is true for sham, becuase although we have a lower AC, our soft cap is also lower.

Hope this helps explain stuff.
