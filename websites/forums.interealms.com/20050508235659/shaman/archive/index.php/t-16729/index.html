Kriel Darkbane
The first bard charm song, at L 27ish (maybe 28?) has a 3 tick duration, and I don't know if I'd bother announcing that all the time. Starting with the second charm song, in the high 30's, the songs start costing mana, and have a 1 minute duration. Once a bard gets up to using that song, I think they should definitely announce they are charming.

With my bard, I have made hotkeys to cast my songs. They look like:

/stopsong (ends the previous song)
/cast X (casts the song in spellgem #X)

In the case of charm, I have just added

/g Charming %T

to the hotkey. And I swap that hotkey in whenever I mem charm. That way everyone knows when I'm going to be charming something. I think it's the only thing I announce, other than when I sing the group invulnerability song. I also message that one, as it confuses the heck out of the group otherwise.

If a bard still twists using the actual spellgems, then they'd have to have a separate hotkey to announce charm, and that'd be a pain. Bards really OUGHT to use a hotkey setup to cut the twisting buttonpressing in half, but many do not.

Low level bards may not have an instant invis item, but with the low level 3-tick charm that doesn't really matter. As they start using the 1 minute charms more, they need to get an invis item of some kind, or use Hide. Hide can slow you down, but in the 40's maybe you couldn't afford an invis item. At high level, they should have an item to break charm with, since the invis song affects the group. Any type of invis will work. My bard uses Boots of the Mosquito - a right click insta cast from inventory Invisibility to Animals. Breaks charm instantly whenever you want.

In any case, I wouldn't suggest that a bard NOT charm just so the shaman can slow. Mez'ing as a bard is a pain in the ass, with the 12 second mez's. Once you have the 1 minute charm it's MUCH more easier to use, and jumps up the group DPS substantially. The best solution is that the bard announce the charms, or at LEAST tell you he'll be charming when there are two mobs in camp.
