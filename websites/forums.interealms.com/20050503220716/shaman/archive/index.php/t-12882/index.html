Steelbear
My advice, if you do pick a melee class...dont twink him until he's about 10th level at least. If you give a level 1 super nice gear (particularly weapon), it will retard his skill development. He'll kill mobs so fast with a good weapon that'll he wont have the opportunity to get ranks of weapon or defense/dodge, etc. Just use buffs at low level and let him use his newbie weapon until about 5th level. Once he has a decent start on all his combat skills, then you can twink him and let him work. The skills eventually catch up.
