My favorite zone for this level range is jagged pine forest. If you pull to the river (staying a good ways away from it--nasty potemids), you shouldn't have any trouble. Can EASILY make it to 51 doing this; the downside is you are a troll, and druids/rangers tend to like this zone too...

If you do go here, I would avoid the griffons, panthers and anacondas until level 49+, and even then I don;t advise pulling them unless nothing else is nearby (shouldn't happen). THe other advantage of not pulling these--these are the mobs the high level rangers/druids are here for. I suppose you could set up an ambush for an unsuspecting druid or ranger this way :)

On my server, this zone is usually pretty much deserted at all times thanks to LDON i assume.
