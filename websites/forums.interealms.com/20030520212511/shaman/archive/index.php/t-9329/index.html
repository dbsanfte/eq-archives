Jaruden
The staff does not stack with our regrowth spell (or higher versions). also you have to click it to get it on, and the snare rather sucks.

The tunic, on the other hand, is always on, stacks with all our regen spells, and has no negatives.

I'd keep going for the tunic, it's well worth it.

Free clickys are always good. I use staff to get rolling after a res in and sometimes while exping. The only real downside to it is that you can't click it from inventory and don't really want to keep it in primary because of the stats you sacrifice.
Zalaton
I absolutely loved my fungi staff before 60.
In outdoor zones if you get good at it (just takes practice) you can fire off staff while the mob is inc, kill the mob with benefits of free regen, then re-apply SoW boots immediately after. It gets to the point where you don't even have to think about it.
That being said, Fungi Tunic is still even better. The regen stacking with everything and being always on is just fantastic.
So to answer your question: Don't give up on the tunic, but don't get rid of the staff :)
