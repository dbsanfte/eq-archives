Gloriana
I think you're getting two AAs confused:

Call of the Ancients - stationary ward with a regen

Lasts 5.5 minutes, 30 minute refresh, wards do not stack (clerics, druids and shaman all receive this ability)

Call of the Ancients I - 30 hitpoints
Call of the Ancients II
Call of the Ancients III - 50 hitpoints

Ancestral Aid - group heal over time, can be MGbed

Lasts 5 ticks, stacks with all other heal over time (except other shaman Ancestral aid), recast (15 minutes? not sure).

I would highly highly recommend Ancestral Aid. You can start training this ability at level 67.

Ancestral Aid I

1: Increase Str Cap by 30
2: Increase Agi Cap by 30
3: Increase Dex Cap by 30
7: Increase AGI by 30
8: Increase STR by 30
9: Increase Hitpoints v2 by 278 per tick
10: Increase DEX by 30

Ancestral Aid II

1: Increase Str Cap by 60
2: Increase Agi Cap by 60
3: Increase Dex Cap by 60
7: Increase AGI by 60
8: Increase STR by 60
9: Increase Hitpoints v2 by 305 per tick
10: Increase DEX by 60

Ancestral Aid III

1: Increase Str Cap by 90
2: Increase Agi Cap by 90
3: Increase Dex Cap by 90
7: Increase AGI by 90
8: Increase STR by 90
9: Increase Hitpoints v2 by 333 per tick
10: Increase DEX by 90
