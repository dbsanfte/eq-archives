Working on my next 'step-by-step' guide..this time to katta.

Katta guards are much like the HHK nobles, with relatively low HP's and nice plat per kill if you include the armor they drop.

The reason that Soupypoopy pointed out in favor of HHK was the elimination of the exp penalties for race/class in a city zone. This means that trolls/iksar and other races that have a racial exp penalty can level at the same rate as their barbarian cousins here and still enjoy our racial advantages.

So the answer is: if you have no exp penalty because you chose to be a smelly barbarian, then HHK has no real attraction to you.

I started Katta at 56, and have had zero problems. 55 should be fine - but you need to be careful to get singles. guards in tunnels aggro on line of sight, so it is a short learning curve to get one at a time there. the guards at the zone in from TS will always come in pairs on the very first pull, as you cannot get in sight of only one. not a hard break, and the zone makes it safe.

I have not had any problem pulling singles from a pair by pulling with turgur, once you are pulling from outside of proximity aggro range. This works great in bank courtyard.

WARNING: I still don't know why it happened, but I had one courtyard guard path by bank and then either run into bank or despawn (despawn is not unusual here, the guards apparently work shifts!) - then three centurions ran out of the bank and killed me before I could get off a root. since I don't know why it happened, I am not sure how to prevent it. I have made it a habit to get cloe enough so the guard I am pulling does not go around the courtyard to get to me.
