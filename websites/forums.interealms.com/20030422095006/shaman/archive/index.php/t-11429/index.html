Weyalan
The nail has been hit onthe head - MAX YOUR DEFENSE! It is AC for free. At your level, tanking for your pet in FV will get you raises on Drolvargs, and probaly later on Forest Giants and Arbor Giants.

I am not sure what your budget is but here are some relatively cheap upgrades to look out for:

Shoulders: Elder Wolf Hide (you could camp it yourself with 2 or 3 friends)
Waist: Chitinous belt
Hands: Leaf scale gloves - these rot all the time in Plane of Nitemare, and sell for peanuts in the Bazaar
Range: Ceremonial Smudging Stick (you could probably solo that, but its a long camp). Seal of Thoridain (from 3rd Coldain Shawl quest - v easy), Goranga Idol
Primary: Thunder runed war spear
