The CC Geonids are down past the Dwarf Outpost. They are all melee, no casters what so ever. Not to many hps, don't hit really hard.

Hints:

1) Start at the bottom, work your way up. No pulling needed, just kill one and move up to the next (I think there are 8-9 or them) Plus, you have a Terror below the first Geo at the bottom. After you kill the last one, just go back to the bottom and wait for the respawns.

2) Keep a watch for trains. There is a 'Zone-out only' shortly after the last Geo. Just look at the floor, if you see what looks like a 'step', you are at the top. Spider Tunnel and Terror Tunnel are not far from the Goenid Tunnel, trains are usually taken this way.

3) Don't be surprised if they do NOT agro you. Geonids are the only thing, other than Dwarfs, that won't agro you right off. But, if you keep killing them long enough (this will take awhile) you will become KOS.
