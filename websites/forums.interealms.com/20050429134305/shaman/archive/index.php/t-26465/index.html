Gart Egilsson
If I had to do it over again, I would not do enchanting/tailoring.

It's just not worth it. Maybe if I had a guild funneling me supplies, but I don't.

There's essentially nothing from tailoring that's really usable over what you can just pick up as you go or in AH. The market is swimming with bags, and I've found that wasting time trying to sell is, well, a waste of time. I can't even sell enchantments over what I could sell components costs for. Lord knows buying enchantments is easy enough, given the expectations of people who want to buy them off me.

The only reason to be an enchanter is to simply disenchant items to sell to suckers who want to be enchanters.

Unless you just like the idea of it, which I did.

That said, they are a synergistic combo, and I do disenchant a lot of tailored items for components for enchanting skillups. I skill up tailoring, disenchant it, and use those items to try to skill up enchanting.

Storage space is a real bear though. I got components all over the place and am constantly running out of room for my loots.
