Here's my attempt at a little more complete (and newbie friendly) JBB guide. I'm stealing liberally from Durnitol (love the name, btw!)...

The JBB (Jaundiced Bone Bracer) (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=893) is considered one of the most powerful pieces of equipment a shaman can have- it is one of the "big three"- along with the epic (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=748) and the fungi tunic (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=11737). These three are so powerful because they provide mana-free damage output or mana-free healing.

The JBB has a right-click (manaless) effect ("Winters Roar"- as our level 34 spell) which does 263 damage to the target. This is a cold DD, so is obviously less effective against targets with high cold resistance. The effect takes eight seconds to cast, and is relatively easy to channel through. 263 damage every 8 seconds provides a maximum sustained damage output of 32.875 damage per second. This level of consistent damage output is not otherwise attainable until well into the end-game. Chances are, the bracer will continue to be used up to and including level 60. Between level 45 and the low 50's, the bracer makes solo'ing much easier.

Like other Jaundiced Bone armor pieces, the effect will not start working until level 45. Also, like other JB armor, Iksars cannot use it. This is an item of contention among many iksars- as they believe they have been deprived of a game-altering piece of shaman equipment. There are no other equally available shaman alternative which provide anywhere near this level of mana-free damage in the game (the epic would be an alternative, but because its affect is a DoT, it can be used in conjunction with the JBB, not as an alternative.)

The JBB is a rare drop from Arch Duke Iatol (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=4957) in the crypt in Old Sebilis. It is also tradeable; and while prices tend to vary by server, expect to pay a sizable amount (most servers average around 10-15k, other go as high as 30k.)
