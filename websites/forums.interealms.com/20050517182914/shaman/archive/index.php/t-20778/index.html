God's AEs in Phase 4?

To answer the original question of phase 4 gods, you stand there and take the AE. Spread the bards around where people will be standing.

Another trick is:
Have VoQ on the top buff slot. Throw another 2 junk buffs below it. I use the clicky sow boots & sta. Then toss on the rest of your buffs. The mana drain AE will overwrite VoQ and take over that slot. Use annul magic to dispell it (annul removes 2 buffs). Just keep two buff slots open up top and dispell & clicky as needed. This works well for Saryn & Theris. Watch out for MGBs cause it can easily take over a top buff slot, forcing the next mana drain AE onto the bottom buff slot.
