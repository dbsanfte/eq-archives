Hail,

I would estimate 3 weeks of intensive grinding. That means no stops, no running around vendors, etc. Stock up on a backpack full of stacks of food and drink, head to PoI at 52, and get into a Guardian room group if you can't catch a group killing Clockwork Golems inside the factory. I personally recommend the guardians first because unless you are extremely well equipped the Golems will probably kill you several times per night at 52 unless you have a great group. Grind PoI until 55. At 55, move to Valor when possible & do your best to get in the group at the Inner Cave camp. Inner Cave in Valor is some of the Best Tier2 exp possible. Clockwork Golems in the PoI factory is about the best Tier1 exp I've found. Have several exp areas in mind each night in case the great spots are camped so you spend more time grinding and less time searching.

Remember that your focus is to level fast, and not about loot. Offer to give up all rolls for loot with a more powerful group (you will still find level 65 people grinding in Tier1 & 2) to keep your spot. Find a group that is also on the experience fast track and get with them EVERY night.

Having a reliable group of high playtime good players, even if not with the 'perfect' makeup of clases, is more important, probably THE most important thing to get you levelled quickly than any other factor. You can grind OLD world areas if PoP is camped and with a good group you will still make good progress, but almost nothing beats full group PoP exp. from what I've seen.

Best of luck to you!
