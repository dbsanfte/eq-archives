Your duo could handle PoJ, just know that a puller under level 56 will get adds. The zone in area in Chardok is real good for your levels. I did it at 53 solo so you should have no problem. Lots of good stuff out there at 54, wait till you get closer to 60, its like hitting a wall. :P
