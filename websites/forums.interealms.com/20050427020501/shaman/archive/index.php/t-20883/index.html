Goldark
Well the answer is impossible to say without knowing the expected time to land turgurs and the expected duration of the fight.

In more general terms though, any moderate-long duration fight with a high dps, highly MR mob is generally best opened up with MD+Grummus, then malos, then sinia/turgurs alternated until one or both stick. If mob is very very MR then forget turgurs and help heal or deal dps if healing is covered.

Its not always a choice of one or the other, using grummus or turgurs is not mutually exclusive :)
