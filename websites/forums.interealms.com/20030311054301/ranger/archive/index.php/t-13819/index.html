Skill ups in Ftetching go off of WIS INT or DEX, whichever is higher. For a Ranger, it is easier to raise DEX to max (255) than either of the other two.

Go to the bazaar and select for DEX items. I found that I could spend a reletively small amount of plat and raise my dex considerably. The down side is that I have to dedicate a bag for my DEX gear now. Also you can bum a shammy buff or buy DEX potions to top it off. I noticed a BIG increase in skill ups when I fletched with 255 DEX.
