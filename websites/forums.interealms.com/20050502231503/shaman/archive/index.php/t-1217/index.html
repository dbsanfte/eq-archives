Jinheim
It doesn't make much of a difference, really :) Totemic will serve you well until your 30's. At low levels, the only gear that really matters are your weapons and your +HP and +mana gear. If you were to get a pair of 6ac/65hp rings. you'd notice a huge difference. On the other hand, if you found a +30stamina piece, you'd barely notice any. That being said, go for any hp/mana gear you can scrounge up, and don't worry too much about your wisdom :)

As for weapons, a friend gave me a howling harpoon when I was level 8. It served me very well up until two days ago (at level 54) when I bought a rune-etched icewurm fang. Slime coated harpoons and spine chill spears will also work well. As other people have said, don't neglect your blunt skills, though. I did, and I am regretting it now /sigh. Unfortunately, I can't recommend a good low-end 1hb or 2hb for you.

I recommend you spend a little time perusing AllaKhazam's equipment listings. Probably the best way to do it is by equipment slot.

Say you're looking for a new arm piece. Go here (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/dyn/items/), select Arm (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/dyn/items/Arm.html) then select Shaman (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/listitem.html?sort=Armour+Class&invslot=Arm&cfilter=9&statfilter=None) from the "Narrow to Class" dropdown- then press the "Reload" button on the right.

You'll get a list of every arm piece wearable by a shaman, with basic stats. Click on the one you're interested in, and it will generally tell you what drops it, what quest you need to do to get it, or how much it costs (although you should treat the pricing data with suspicion.)

This is a good way to begin to get familiar with the levels of equipment out there.

Once you have some more specific questions, feel free to open threads. The problem is that the "What should I wear" question is too big to answer... we need to know what your race, class, level, available money, current gear, etc. are before we can answer.

Open a thread with "I'm a level (whatever level) (whatever race)- here is my current gear (list current gear) and I have about (whatever money) to spend - what should I get?" - then we can help you. :)
