Kensei
At your level, you may do slightly more damage with the weapons switched. It depends on things like your Dual Wield %age and the MR of the mobs, but that will tend to be low. This isn't necessarily a big difference, though.

If you want to keep the LSoEE in your off-hand, I'd get a Jagged Blade of Mourning for primary. They go really cheap, and you'll do more damage with it than you will with the Lammy until you are well into your 50s and have more haste.
Tanglewood
"What I have been lead to believe is that in your primary you should have a fast hitting / low delay weapon. In your offhand you should have a heavy hitter."

Damage Ratio is what you want in the second hand - not high DMG.


"I mostly solo so I dont know if it makes a difference if grouping or soloing."

The difference when soloing is that you don't have to worry about aggro management - only damage output. This means that procs are actually good, and you shouldn't fear using fast weapons and high haste.
