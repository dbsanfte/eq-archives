Been a while since I had to take attendance on a raid, but I recall doing something like this:

1. Turn log on by typing /log on, or /log. Do this right before engagement of big mob.
2. Do a who command that gets you what you want. /who guild, /who (if you folks are the only one in the zone), or as a last resort do a /list 1 command to list out folks in the designated raid channel.
3. Type something like /say above is the attendance for <mobname> on <date> at <time>.
4. Turn log off.

You can do this multiple times if you kill multiple mobs.

When your finished, you can open the log file in notepad. Copy and paste the sections into Excel, then use Excel to get rid of what you don't want. Only reason I would open it in notepad first was so I could block select exactly what I wanted to manipulate. In the end I could do the whole process in about 5 minutes.

Hope this help :).
