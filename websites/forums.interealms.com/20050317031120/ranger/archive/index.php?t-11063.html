Little late, but:

EQAtlas (http://www.eqatlas.com) updated it's map of CT after the revamp. Most of the important spawns are noted, such as Toxiferious, Blob, Mosquitos, etc.

As for forces. A solid 55+ group can do the entrance or maze (chanter 58+ is a must, or a VERY good puller). If most of your force is 60ish, it makes things pretty routine compared to any other dungeon-crawl type place (think SolB where things hit for 400).

The lizards here have quite a few HP; it's a "Luclin" zone, high AC, high HP, annoying AEs. Bane of Thule, or something, is an AE DoT/Slow; it can be cured with a single Counteract Disease or Poison, I think, or maybe two casts.

Blood is pretty common. You can do the entry courtyard and get quite a few blood vials. It's very common in 4C (the pyarmid to the north of the maze, with the Thul Tae Ew that can drop the fleshgrinder).
