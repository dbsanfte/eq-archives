Each PoA avatar is its own mini event (dust being the hardest imo). The key ports up only 1 group and there is a named waiting on the otherside of the portal. Many guilds camp out a necrobot up top for ghettocohs (like posky, sorta).

Arbitor of Earth requires killing all 4 poearth rings. It is best to off the big croc first since arb is on a timer (something like 45minutes-1hr). Killing all 12 members of rathe council within 7min of the first's death spawn Avatar of Earth (very easy).

Coirnav is a timed 15minute event with lots of adds and some named.

Fennin is a 6hr timed event with Doomfire guardian the trigger. Lot of trash to clear, but at least the mini named all *can* drop loot, but don't hold your breath.
Elira
1. There are four ring events in Air. If i recall correctly it goes Mist, Wind, Smoke, and Dust. To spawn these avatars you have to go through and clear their respective islands, then a mini named, which drops one piece of loot spawns. After you kill the mini names the Avatar will spawn off in one of the side buildings. It helps to have a ranger track these. Each avatar will drop 1-2 key pieces for xegony. You really only need 1 key, but more can't hurt too much, especially with how contested the other elemental plane zones seem to be.. at least on my server.

2. To get to the Mystical Arbitor of Earth, you have to complete all 4 Earth A rings within 24 hours. They can be with or without loot. After you have Earth B access you will need to clear down to the rathe, which involves two steps really. The first is you must clear the front area of mobs within ~30 minutes to unlock the front door. After that there's 3 mini Warchiefs, and one very Angry Warlord Gintolaken who you have to go through. After that you get to clear down to the Rathe prep area. This would be a good place to camp a coth mage. Fast forward a few days of beating your head against the rathe. After you get them all tank mezzed/mezzed, you have 7 minutes in which to kill them all. If you do this sucessfully the Avatar of Earth will spawn, and you'll be sincearly dissapointed with how lame he is.

3. The trigger is Guardian of Coirnav. If it's not up, no Coirnav for you.

4. See 3, just replace Coirnav with Doomfire.
