Haleice
If you play in a outdoors a lot then get a horse it's an excellent investment for a shaman.

Just remember to turn off all the models if you want to still be able to sow yourself. Also you only get mana while you are not moving or meleeing. You get it when you cast when you sit stand etc.

The one point to be aware of is that the mana bar doesn't refresh until you cast a spell or click an item. if you use all your mana and stand for a while and then click your boots you'll see your mana bar shoot up.

I've had my horse for about 6 levels now and she was worth every pp I paid for her.
