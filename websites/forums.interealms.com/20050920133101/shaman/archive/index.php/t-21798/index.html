Stragak
Here's where I have hunted (race not important).

1-3 Newbie zone. It wont take long to hit 3, so don't bother wandering so much.

3-8: Steamfont mountains. Kobolds are decent exp, they dont add or help (with exception of Shamans healing), and they just sit there waiting to die. Also you will make enough to pay for all your level 9 spells and probably all your level 14 spells by level 8.

9-14: Either go to field of bone (the pit area until 10 or 11, then on to Kurns) or.. go north of the kobold camps that got you to 8, kill wandering mobs till 9 and then just a bit farther north is the minotaur slaver camp. After a rough battle on the first pull, you can break up the minotaurs and fight one at a time. Decent exp, loot and faction with Gnome guards (Ogres are dubious by level 12, Troll/Iksars by 14 if you start at 9). TIP: Flash of light will often "mez" the minotaur you pull after he runs a while as long as you DON'T MOVE after you get the "worn off" message. Course you might HAVE to move when the other mino starts to run away near death.. but sometimes you're lucky, then med up and take a couple steps to pull the "mezzed" one back to you.

14-18: Kurns tower is nice. Most skellys on the upper floors are single pulls or just run up and bash them, just stay on the far side of the next closest skelly and he won't help in all but a couple cases.

** Now is the time to do your LDON startup quest, if you have that expansion so you are ready in case you get an invite **

18-24: High Jerk Tolerance: Paludal Caverns, no contest. Low Jerk Tolerance: The Overthere between the Pok book and the "mud pit". Up to you, I usually avoid PC, but sometimes I go there. If you run into nice people, it CAN be a fun time, though your weapons skills might fall behind due to the rapid leveling.

24-30: Hmmm... LOIO is nice. Kill goblins (faction up for Firionia Vie and Cabalis guards). Exiled Iksar (faction up for Cabalis Necro/SK guild). Outpost explorers (faction up for all Cabalis guilds EXCEPT Necro/SK, and faction down for Firiona Vie). If you don't mind losing FV faction, this is a FAST way to get faction with Iksars, instead of the bone chip quest. Oh, when the Iksar guards faction is up enough, turn in those explorer knives to the Iksar guard standing way, WAY out from the city by the ruins for LOTS of nice iksarian faction. Feerott is also nice, lizards near the fallen pillar across the river are decent exp, and drop FS weapons, though really the goblins in LOIO drop PP sometimes as well, and you get the faction boosts.

30-34: The harder lizards in Feerott, or move from LOIO to Frontier Mountains or even back to Overthere (grouping is best, but if you have good Iksar guard faction, the lovely arcut sisters (near the ramp into the cavern in middle of zone) will protect you, from anything except chickens.. even the Dark Elf Dragoons have fallen to them.. although things may have changed since last I was there.

Hope this helps or at least gives you some more choices...
