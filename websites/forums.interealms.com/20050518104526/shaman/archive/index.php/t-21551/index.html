You can have 5 tribute's running at the same time. As Gmork stated, you can only upgrade and activate them at the Tribute Master in your home town.

ALT-U brings up the Tribute window, and shows what Tributes you currently have running. Downgrading you can do anywhere. You get Tribute points by donating items and coin to the Tribute master. THere is currently a cap of 150,000 (I think) tribute points. When you have Tribute effects turned on, they run on a 10 minute timer. Every 10 minutes a certain number of points will be deducted from your pool of points for every tribute effect that you have active.

Shiuna
Here's something I wrote for my guild board:

Those of you who bought the Gates of Discord expansion have a neat feature available called Tribute. Tribute provides benefits in the form of stat increases, buffs, and focus effects on a temporary basis. You activate the benefits by returning to your home city and finding the Tribute Master. You can give him plat and items in exchange for favor points. You can then select up to 5 different benefits to be active. Each active benefit uses a set number of favor points every 10 minutes. You can turn off the benefits at any time but you must visit the Tribute Master to turn them back on or to add to your favor points.

Tribute Master

The Tribute Master is a new npc in each home city. He is listed in the find window. To interact with him simply right-click him like you would a guild master or vendor. This opens the Tribute Master window. In the left panel is a list of all the available benefits and the favor cost/period. In the right panel is a list of all the benefits that are currently active. Under these panels is a scrollable window containing a description of the currently selected benefit. At the bottom right is a timer, cost per period of the currently active benefits, and the amount of favor in your pool. At the bottom left is a donate button.

Favor points

You buy favor points by donating plat and items to the Tribute Master. 1 plat buys 1 favor point. Common items generally have a favor value equal to what a vendor would buy them for. For example, a peridot is worth 10 favor points. Nicer items are usually worth a lot more than vendor value. For example, a Soulstripper is worth 5280 and a Tacticians Bulwark is worth 27,786 favor points. You're probably not going to donate these, though. He won't accept nodrop items or junk like rusty weapons.

To add points to your favor pool, open your inventory and select an item. It will show up in the little window to the left of the Donate button and the value will be displayed below it. If you wish to donate it, press Donate. To donate plat click the plat icon above the Donate button and a quantity window will open. Enter the amount of plat you wish to donate, and press Donate. You are restricted to 200,000 favor in your current favor pool.

Benefits

In the left panel of the Tribute Master window is a list of available benefits. Each benefit has an associated level that you must be to activate it. Higher tiers of a benefit have higher associated levels. To activate a benefit simply select it in the left panel and press Upgrade until it is at the tier you want. Once you close the Tribute Master window the timer starts counting down. Each 10 minutes the favor cost of all active benefits is deducted from your favor pool. To turn off a benefit select it in the right panel and press Downgrade until it disappears. You can also do this at any time by pressing Alt-U, which gives you a Tribute Benefits window with only the active benefits panel and the Downgrade button.

Some of the benefits, like Mana Preservation or Burning Affliction, are triggered by an event. Just like with focus effects, when the benefit is triggered you will see a message. For example, if you have the Mana Preservation benefit activated and cast a spell, you will see a message like "Your Mana Preservation IV Benefit flickers with a pale light". Others simply increase the associated stat until you downgrade or deactivate them. A benefit cannot increase a stat beyond your current cap. For example, if you're level 65 and your strength is capped at 280, the Arm of Strength will not increase it beyond that.

Some of the benefits are:

Aura of Clarity - Flowing Thought I-V, cost for fifth tier is 37 favor/period. This is identical to the Flowing Thought focus effect on items and is capped at a total of 15 (I think).

Power of Conservation - Mana Preservation I-IV, cost for fourth tier is 29 favor/period. This is identical to the the Mana Preservation focus effect and does not stack. Only one of the MP effects will activate.

Strength of Body 1-6, adds 10-150 HPs. cost for sixth tier is 21 favor/period.

Strength of Mind 1-6, adds 10-150 to mana pool, cost for sixth tier is 21 favor/period.

Visions of Suffering I-IV, identical to Affliction Efficiency I-IV focus effect, cost at fourth tier is 29 favor/period.

Power of Suffering I-IV, identical to Burning Affliction I-IV focus effect, cost at fourth tier is 29 favor/period.

Swift arms 1-5, 21-41% Haste, cost at fifth tier is 37 favor/period.

Sage's Advise 1-6, +2 Wisdom per tier, cost at sixth tier is 21 favor/period.

Geez, this could be expensive!

When I'm out adventurin' I usually have Aura of Clarity V and Power of Suffering IV active. This gives me an additional 5 mana per tic and 10% damage on my DoTs at a cost of 66 favor/period, or 396 favor per hour. At a cost pf 1p/favor, this would be prohibitively expensive. There are a few items, however, that have an unusually high favor value for their cost to make (you knew tradeskills were going to be involved sooner or later, didn't you? ). The Forged Pick cost a little over 2p to make (smithing trivial 49) but is worth 320 favor, and the Forged Warhammer costs a little over 6p to make (smithing trivial 62) but is worth 507 favor. SOE has been changing these when they find them, so I'm guessing that this bargain won't last forever. A ratio of 1p for 160 favor makes this feature a lot more desirable.

So, all of the items to make the forged pick except sheet metal are in my home city, Halas. I buy 100 sheet metal in PoK, then go to Halas to make the picks. I can usually make and turn in 100 picks (for 32,000 favor) in 20 minutes for a cost of about 210p. Not bad!

Good Luck.
