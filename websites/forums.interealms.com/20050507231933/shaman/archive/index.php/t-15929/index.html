Rorgg
It's pretty simple:

Solo Group XP = 1.000x per kill
2-group = 1.2x/2 = 0.600x
3-group = 1.4x/3 = 0.467x
4-group = 1.6x/4 = 0.400x
5-group = 1.8x/5 = 0.360x
6-group = 1.8x/5 = 0.360x (not a typo, still divided by 5)

So, obviously your cut per kill is going to drop as you gain more people. The question is -- how much faster will you kill? If your group of 5 or 6 is killing at least 3 more times the number of mobs you'd be killing solo (which is usually the case) you're better off grouping.

Note: I said USUALLY. It depends on the group, the environment, and your ability.
