Jaradis
We all know the main reason that GoD spells are No Drop is to get people to buy the expansion. So here is a solution:

In Abysmal, create an NPC, preferrably next to the turn in guy for spells, that will remove the No Drop status on the Geo stones. You hand in the No Drop stone, and receive a similar stone but its droppable. BUT, its also No Rent, No rent in the way Sky keys used to be as well as the AE blockers in Uqua: If you zone, die, camp, etc it vanishes. You can then hand the now droppable stone to someone, they turn that stone back into the NPC to change it to the No Drop version, and they can then turn it in for a spell.

It allows spells to be tradeable, but you have to have the expansion they are from to be able to trade them since you have to be able to zone to Abysmal.
