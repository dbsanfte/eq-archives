Here's how to do it. Once you get lvl 29, you're golden.

Go to Marus Seru.

Get good hp/ac gear.

Put on these buffs:
Hp/ac (and any other hp/ac buffs from nearby clerics or druids, etc)
agility - so they miss more...you gain from them missing too
dexterity - for skill gain
frenzy (adds ac and dexterity)
regen
sow

Go out and collect 4-6 rockhoppers, stonegrabbers, and zelniaks.
Do not pull lightcrawlers and if they add bring your train past guards and guards will kill only the crawlers.

Find a long stretch to run along. Go to one end. Get yourself beat down to 40 percent or so(in case you get chain bashed real badly) and in the meanwhile attack one of them just for chance weapon skill gain. Run to the other end and heal (Greater healing does 300). Run to the other end and heal( another 300 - should be near full life if not full). Stand and get beat up to 40. Rinse Repeat. When you go oom run to NL zone line, get beat up till around 20, and zone into NL.

Med up and repeat.


At level 29, I'm averaging at least 4 to 6 skillups in defense everytime i did this. Much quicker than solo tanking. And then you can just rot root to level if you wish without worrying about defense skill.
