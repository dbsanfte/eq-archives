Gonna need a rogue. You have to travel through HS south wing, through several locked doors, up around some hidden back tunnels, then through all of the east wing. Trying to do it solo would take hours (assuming you even could). 1 group is probably enough to do the whole thing, the more you add the faster you go. I'd recomend yourself, rogue (necessary), enchanter (really helps, can pull faster), and a cleric (just in case someone dies to massive HT, there's a lot), you won't be dragging anyone back to IZ. Add anything else from there to speed things up.

Here's a good map, http://www.eqmaps.com/m3/howling5.gif, the entrance to east wing is blocked by the fake stairs (can't climb up). The green dot in east is zone out, backtrack from there 2 red dots and that's drusella's room. She wanders around, I think there are 4 or 5 other mobs inside as well.

I believe her spawn is 3 or 4 days, don't remember off hand. Easiest way to track her is plant a mage with sense summmoned at the safe spot below IZ, DS's pet is the only summoned mob in the zone.
