Another useful trick - If your guild is short on mages like mine when I do find a mage to summon a pet kit for me I ask for 4 or 5 sets(weapons, belt, muzzle). I then put the extras in a summoned bag or my foldable bearskin potion bag. Go to the arena, bind nearby and have someone kill ya. Now loot your corpse of all but the bag of pet stuff. Now you have a ready standby when you can't find a mage. Just be sure to check the corpse periodically and repeat the process if it is due to poof soon.

Of course this is all theoretical since everyon knows that pets can't use items. :D
