Goldark
As recognised above, aggro is key to this and there are a number of solutions.

a) Use the level 14 version of cripple for low mana, high aggro

b) Flash of Light. Just make damn sure you both stay in melee range at all times :)

c) Root and use proximity aggro.

All depends on where and what you are fighting:

Mobs hitting too hard/fast for you to tank? Use option a) combined with DoTs and kite the mob whilst rogue + dogdog runs behind hitting the mob's back. Snare item really helps here, and mainly outdoors tactic only.

Mobs hitting like jesses, reasonable open spaces? Use option b), stick within melee range at all times, and heal as required.

Fighting indoors and can't afford to mess up with FoL due to massive trains? Getting adds so you need to move around to do CC? Use root, mob will attack whoever stands closer, so get rogue to stay at max melee range whilst you tank IN TEH FACE (tm). If root is breaking too easily, use option a) instead - bit more mana intensive probably, but works fine and you can throw in dots and nukes without worrying about breaking root.

Point is that you CAN duo with anyone....the fact is rogues arent very good partners for shaman. Rogues are best in a 3+ person group. Some points to remember:

1- Rogues suck at tanking. They'll have a bit more hp and ac than shaman but the second you have them tanking instead of another class they lose BS and that means they just lost ~30% of their DPS. You now have yourself a fabulous paper tank.

2- You want the Rogue to BS so you want the Shaman to tank now... can Shaman tank? Sure I guess but once again you are much better off with any other class tanking and let us do our job. We aren't built to be tanks.

There are a dozen different ways that you CAN group up with a rogue. It just isn't the best idea. Look through the boards on all the "best duo member" you won't (or shouldnt) find rogue in there. It's all about min maxing. If you want to have a pure DPS char have either a Necro, Wiz or Mage... but dont waste your mana you could be dotting with on healing a paper tank. It's sort of like someone saying "We want our chanter to tank Quarm can we do it?" Sure you could I guess with enough clerics... it just doesnt make sense that you would.

The way Rogues are getting hosed right now, dont bother anyway, have them roll up a Paly or BL. Rogues used to be big dps now they are blown away by bows that deal more damage while attacking from a distance, and casters that never run out of mana. If I didnt have 3 years into my rogue I'd roll up another class in a heart beat. As it is I'm giving it lots of thought.
