This post was made before Velious came out, I haven't time to edit it for changes since then. So some info is wrong basically it's good info though.

Important changes to keep in mind:

We now have Ally faction, but max faction is still the same number.

Secondary faction hits now occur even if first one is maxxed (means goodies can get into cabilis)

Velious and Luclin faction doesn't conform to old world+Kunark general rules ie you get a bigger faction hit for kiling the bigger stuff.


Faction ranges

-800 and below: Ready to attack
-800 to -500: Threateningly
-500 to -100: Dubiously
-100 to 0: Apprehensivly
0 to 100: Indifferently
100 to 500: Amiably
500 to 800: Kindly
800 and higher: Warmly

The game adds up your constant initial faction and the earned factions, and the number from that is what is compared to that list.

Your inital faction is made out of 3 parts. Your Race, Class, and Religion. So at character creation it goes down the list of every faction in the game assigning an initial faction for you. Stuff like if you are a Shaman, then you get a +50 with every other Shaman guild in the game, everyone that is not an Iksar gets -1000 with the Iksar city factions, etc. Illusion spells change the race factor temporarily.

And there is the earned faction. Do a quest, kill something, this is what changes.
Not all hits are equal, killing a goblin whelp gets you a -1 with their goblin clan faction. Kill a high power named goblin and you end up actually getting -10 or something with that clan. Same with some quests, the first faction gets you a +10, the rest are +1. Usually only the first faction listed is the one that may be a large amount, I think almost every time the 2-4th factions will only be +1 or -1

There is another oddity with the earned factions, this number is capped at +2001 and similerly capped at -2001. So no matter how many kills or quests you do, if the change would push you past those bounds, the faction does not change at all. Also, if the first faction in a list hits that cap, all other factions from that action will not happen, the game will just ignore them. So if you are doing a quest to raise a faction, it will only work so long as the first faction is not capped. After that, the secondary faction changes that you were trying for will just be ignored.

Just as a starter do NOT even attempt to faction Cabilis if you have a godd aligned god, it's not possible.

IF you want to faction it as an agnostic or evil character or are just plain interested then follow the instructions bvelow.

Cabilis Faction is made up of 6 Factions:

East Cabilis and Outside

Legion of Cabilis (Warrior guild and Troopr guards)
Crusaders of Greenmist (SK guild and Crusader guards)

East Cabilis

Swifttails (Monk Guild)
Scaled Mystics (Shaman Guild)
Cabalis residents (Merchants, Bankers and some NPC's)

West Cabilis

Brood of Kotiz (Necro guild and all West Cabilis Merchants)

There are 2 factions you can raise by killing things:

Legion of Cabilis and Brood of Kotiz. The important faction here is Legion of Cabilis (Troopers). If you have read the other thread on faction you will know that positive changes have a maximum of +2001. This is important as the only way to raise faction with the other factions is to do trooper quests. If you do a trooper quest the primary faction you receive back is for Legion of Cabilis. Thus if you Max out Legion of Cabilis you cannot raise any of the other factions by questing.

You need to get +500 with Legion of Cabilis before they go Dubious (you start at -1000) This means goblins Sarnaks etc. As soon as they go dubious STOP.

DO NOT kill ANYTHING else that gives trooper faction. Why? I hear you cry, the reason is this: You now have 1500 pt's left worth of trooper increases, the more your trooper faction goes up, the less you have to work with raising other factions.

There are several Faction quests troopers give out. Most give a +2-+10 trooper faction hits and +1 to the rest, all of these are bad. The Trooper Mozo bonechip quest gives +1 to all factions, this means you can get +1500 with the other factions before troopers max out.

The trooper Mozo quest gives the following faction hit per 4 Bone Chips.

+1 Legion of Cabilis
+1 Crusaders of Greenmist
+1 Swifttails
+1 Scaled Mystics
+1 Cabilis residents

That means to get your 1500 increases in the other factions you need SIX THOUSAND bone chips.

You need 1500 increases as here are you starting factions with Cabilis:

Cazic/agnostic

-1000 to everything

All other evil gods:

-1000 Legion
-1750 Crusaders
-1500 Scaled Mystics
-1500 Swifttails
-2000 Cabilis residents
-2000 Brood of Kotiz

The Bankers, merchants and NPC's are on Cabilis residents faction. Remember that ALL merchants have a false cap of dubious, only the bankers and NPC's will show your true faction with Cabilis residents.

So if you did the Bone chips quest 1500 times you would be dubious with Cab Res, this is enough that you have reached REAL dubious, you can the do merchant quests till you reach apprehensive and then you will be able to bank and buy/sell.

The reason you can do merchant quests when Trooper faction is maxxed, is that the primary faction change will be Cab Res so that will go up.

Then all you have to do is Kill 2000 forsaken Iksar to faction West Cabilis and you can do anything in Cabilis that an iksar could do.

BTW it is easier than I have described if you are Cazic or Agnostic as you start out -1000 with everyone.

I hope I have laid that out so that it makes sense.
