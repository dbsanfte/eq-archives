Zhahz
I love LDoN but at 55/56 the xp is only really remarkable for me if my group is doing high difficulty adventures. On normal diff I might get half a yellow...on high diff I'll get more like a full bub. To me that's decent xp for the time spent. I happen to belong to a chat channel of LDoN lovers and if we have a group that's all or mostly from the channel we usually do a high diff. Last night our group beat a normal diff slaughter of 52 in 45 or so minutes...then we barely beat a high diff slaughter because we got cocky and had a slight mishap near finishing. So, I mostly do normal diff LDoN for fun, adventure points, and minimal xp on the side and prefer high diff if I'm wanting xps.

I'm still a planes noob and should try for more planes groups. I've only been to PoN and that was many levels ago. I did some PoJ with my BL duo friend but it sucked with tons of adds due to our pull spots.

I duo with a BL in Acrylia Caverns and the xp seems to be pretty good...and the loot is decent too...and you get boatloads of acrylia which can bring in extra cash depending on your server's acrylia economy. We did a 5 person group there once and xp was flowing nicely. There are rarely any people in AC so you can just pull away. I don't watch my xp bar that closely so I can't say with precision, but I seem to get about 1-1.5 yellows and 1-1.5k in plats per 3-4 hour run we do there (and I could be getting more plats for my acrylia but I sell it at a slight discount to my partner who has a smith - more acrylia for him to skill on and fast selling for me without cluttering up my trade mule). Groups there should see similar returns as you can just pull more and faster. AC is fun for us to as a duo (and BL/shaman duo seems to rock in general). We often deal with 2-3 pulls and roamer adds on top of that - it keeps you on your toes.

So, my advice would be to find a lesser visited dungeon appropriate for your group's level and go to town. Most xp is gonna be fairly similar except maybe the planes where the difficulty is also gonna be hihger, so I think the key for good group xp is more about being able to pull constantly with little downtime.
