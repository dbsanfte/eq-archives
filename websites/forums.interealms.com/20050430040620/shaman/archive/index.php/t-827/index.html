Philoso
As everyone knows, Paludal is awesome xp. Solo you can easily get to 23, but that's where it gets tougher I find. Once you hit 23, almost everything minus the Recondite and the odd Patog Fiend and Sensate Elder turns light blue. That being said, you can get in a bandit group (or even pull some for yourself if there are some unoccupied ones hehe) and easily get to 24. In a group, you can easily get to 25 as well. There are some places you can go from about 24 though.

If you go to Marus Seru, I would suggest bringing a tank along. I found the Greyhoppers to be something of a hassle. Definitely wouldn't recommend straight-up melee on them. Had to slow it and dot it twice. Root-rotting it down a bit can be done, but I just found it wasn't really worth it.

Dawnshroud is doable 24+ solo. Head over to the Sanctus Seru zoneline and find yourself some small zelniaks. At 24 they are yellow/red, but not much of a problem (least I didn't think so compared to the hoppers). HPs aren't too high, they are absolutely not social (as opposed to the hoppers), and give about 3% per kill. Lightcrawlers here are dark blue at 24 (from the ones I've seen so far), but haven't tussled with them yet.

Haven't bothered with Netherbian yet. I have noticed that the Fungoid Sporelings are dark blue, and someone else mentioned that the Netherbians are also dark blue. Just watch out for adds is all I can say about this zone. Very easy to get adds sometimes.

Then there's the brute cave in Warsliks Woods, near the Dalnir zone. Perfect for soloing. 5 spawns total, 3 in one room, 2 in the other, and each spaced apart enough so they won't add or aggro on you. These range from dark blue to yellow at 24, and the yellows can be a pain if they resist root a lot (which they do), but otherwise are cake. Just have to be careful that they don't fall into the walls when they run or follow you. You could also melee these as well. I think they've got about a 14 min respawn time too. There's also the giant fort, but I haven't hunted there as a shaman yet.

LOIO is also another possibility. Lots of mobs wandering around, but at 24 and above you'll be looking to move nearer to the Sarnak fort. XP in WW and LOIO can seem slow though, but at least in WW you can make a few pp from loot.
