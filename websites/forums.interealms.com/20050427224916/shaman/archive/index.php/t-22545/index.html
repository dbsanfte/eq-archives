Barm McLir
He's way in the back of a cave with small red aliens who all have a ton of hitpoints. The callers see invis and the cave is pretty twisty. They are not bad to slow and don't hit very hard but take a long time to kill. Use bind sight to scout out each room.

There are some wanderers and the repop time is kinda short.

Radir/Ghost of Radir has annoying MR and slowing can be a nusiance. Hits in the 300 range if I remember, flurries, and has a BOATLOAD of hitpoints. You can proximately aggro pull him into the hall and skip killing the mobs in his room. Cripple should reduce the number of flurries. The spirit spawns in Radir's original spot - not from where you kill him.

You're probably going to end up fighting that same roamer(s?) a couple times during the fight.
