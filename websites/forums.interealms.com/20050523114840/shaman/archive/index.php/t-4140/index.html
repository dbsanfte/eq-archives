Basic BattleShaman techniques:


*****************************************

Remember, Cripple is your friend--Cripple every mob you intend on meleeing. If you don't have Cripple, Incapacitate works just fine.

Get a good Haste item (Sash of the Dragonborn, Silvery Belt of Contention, etc.). Keep your Haste spell up at all times.

Maximize your ATK rating (try to get it over 1000). This usually means keeping Rage up at all times.

Use a good dmg/dly weapon, or a good proccing weapon, or a combination of both. Grab an REIF or a Club of the Ice Ocean in the bazaar for 3-6K, or a GFG for 3K.

If you're going to use a proccing weapon, get your DEX over 200.

When possible, always attack from the REAR.

******************************************

I've never parsed my results, but a lot of my guildies are amazed at the amount of damage I can output using a GFG or a proccing weapon. Can I come anywhere close to their damage output? Hell, no, but I don't *whiff* that much anymore, either.

I just wish we got the same damage bonus for using 2Handers as melee types did. THEN this would be some serious fun.

(On a side note, when duoing with a lvl 60 monk in my guild, I don't even bother to do this stuff....Monk damage is sooooooo insane. I just rack and stack mobs, and let him kill them.)


Hope this helps some of you younger shaman discouraged by your eroding melee skills. Remember, we can melee, it just takes the right equipment and using every spell in our inventory.
