Im a glutton for punishment. It has become increasingly obvious that shaman are not healers in current content, and actually stopped being healers sometime between Luclin and PoP.

But anyway, I was comparing the heals that the priests (and I include shaman here from habit, and not from practice) get. Here are my conclusions:

Shaman (honorary mention)

Daluda's Mending
1550 healed
490 mana
Efficiency: 3.16

Obkin's Mending
1690 healed
562 mana
Efficiency: 3.00

Net gain
Obkin's heals ~9% more than Daluda's, with an efficiency loss of about 5%

Druid

Sylvan Infusion
2100 healed
550 mana
Efficiency: 3.81

Chlorotrope
2275 healed
625 mana
Efficiency: 3.64

Net gain
Chlorotrope heals ~ 8% more than Sylvan Infusion, with an efficiency loss of about 4.5%

Cleric

Holy Light
3275 healed
650 mana
Efficiency: 5.03

Pious Light
3770 healed
750 mana
Efficiency: 5.02

Net Gain
Pious Light heals ~ 12% more than Holy Light, with an efficiency loss of about .2% (yes, thats two tenths of a percent)

Now, let us look at the healing %'s that Rytan and the guru's up at SOE said the priests will follow (100/64/47)

Pious Light = 100%

Chlorotrope = 60%

Obkin's Mending = 44%

Obviously they need to increase the healing power of Obkins to bring it into line with their proposed healing percentages. Druids also need their heal upgraded (as much as it pains me to say it).

If the Holy Percentages are going to stay 100/64/47, then:

Clerics : Need nothing. They are baseline here

Druids : Amount healed needs to increase 137 HP, to 2412. That takes it to 63.9% of cleric's Pious. That changes the efficiency a bit, to 3.85, which is slightly more efficient than Sylvan Infusion.

Shaman : Amount healed needs to increase 82 HP, to 1772. That makes Obkins 47.002% as effective as Pious. Efficiency changes to 3.15, which is almost as efficient as Daluda's Mending.

I think I'm going to cross post this to Sonys boards as well, but this is what Ive found so far. Interesting to note that according to Lucy, Druids have another heal that has not yet been revealed. Will be interesting to see how it stacks up, becuase if it increases healing power at all it encroches on the clerics holy and blessed 100%.
