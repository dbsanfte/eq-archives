Kror
I just meleed when I had to and leveled up whatever weapon I had.
When I got my Corpse grinder my 2HB was still at 70 skill or so. I just would haste myself and melee whenever I had a chance. I got 200 skill about 2 weeks after getting it. Would have been quicker if I soloed a bit more during that time, and just meleed with Dog-Dog helping.

Bottom line: don't stress too much about the skills. when you get a good haste, haste yourself to melee as chances for skill-ups are determined per swing and haste gives you more swings per minute. As a shaman you are not about melee damage and will eventually not have to melee much at all.

The way you are doing it will work fine, but worry more about your casting skills and making sure they are up to par at this point and don't worry too terribly much about the melee skills.
