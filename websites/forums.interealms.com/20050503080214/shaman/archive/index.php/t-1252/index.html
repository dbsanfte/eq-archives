We went from a plat class to a chain class, and VI seems to forget this.

Shaman were always a Chain class. There were just a few pieces of Chain type armor that they put plate graphics on (Totemic, Rune Etched, Jaundice Bone).

Sorta hard to 'forget' something that never happened.

Go check out the REAL plate type armors that existed pre-kunark and you will see that none of them are Shaman usable.

Bronze? Nope. Clerics (an actual Plate wearer) can use.
Steel? Nope. Clerics can use.

The other (provably) plate type armors are further restricted by issues beyond plate-vs-chain-vs-leather-vs-cloth (single class, or melee only). And none of them have Shaman on them.

Everything usable by multiple classes that had Shaman on it pre-kunark also had Ranger and Rogue - the other 2 Chain wearing classes.

Plate = Warrior, Paladin, Shadowknight, Bard, Cleric. That is IT. Shaman aren't on the list. Never were.
