Qhan
This is sort of a rehash on an old discussion, but I'm looking for some more creative ideas than the old shaman/monk or shaman/SK duos.

I have a level 65 shaman, and have a 2nd account that I am constantly rerolling, looking for the perfect partner. Some things to consider: complementary skills, methods of escape, ability usefulness solo. Here's my list:

- Shm + Monk: Fun, great combo. FD is great, good dmg output, but the def nerf hurt alot. Did that on my old server though, wanted to change up.

- Shm + BST: This combo is a good one, but the skills overlapped a little for my taste, plus the bst has no gate and no FD, and that caused some problems. Plus on FV there are a ton of beastlords, and I wanted something a little different.

- Shm + War: This one failed for obvious reasons; the war is a great tank, but he was very gear dependant and thats no good for a poor shammy.

- Shm + SK: Popular combo, and a great complement. I don't have a good excuse for giving this one up really.

- Shm + Pal: This is an obvious combo that complements well. Pally has stacking buffs, heals, and can rez. However, back to the BST, no FD or gate.

- Shm + Bard: Only problem with this is that I'm barely ambidextrous enough to play shaman, much less toss a bard in there..I'd need 3 new arms. I love the bard class, but I can't imagine 2 boxing one and not doing a terrible injustice to the class.

- Shm + Caster: I haven't done a shm + pure caster, but I've thought of it...maybe a mage. There's some nice utility in those combos.

- Shm + Cleric: Flavor of the week. I found that I like to spend my time on the shaman, but have the backup assistance for whatever I'm fighting. The cleric will offer great buffs/heals, a little bit of dmg (very little), and can gate. I'm actually finding this to be a great combo; I can actually just tank myself with Q on, and keep the cleric behind in case things get nasty.

What are your suggestions? Have you found any unexpectedly fun combos? They don't necessarily have to include a shaman, its just that mine all do :).

i've done shaman+chanter ..pacify pulls are a huge plus. KEI 60+ is great and i suppose any clarity is good :). Shaman+ranger is pretty decent. Shaman can tank mobs and ranger (AM3/EQ) can pluck away with arrows and not lose any DPS. Harmony pulls outdoors. Snare. Ranger can tank and shaman can play cleric, and ranger is capable of taking aggro from a shaman relatively easily...

Shaman+BST isnt half bad once the BST hits 44 and gets the mana regen buff. The pet makes a good tank as does the BST. Buff overlap isnt really a concern especially 60+. Paragon of spirit is nice when things go bad. Shaman can root adds. BST provides huge DPS to the duo. Shaman more capable healer. Despite the overlaps is a very nice duo.

Those are the duos i've done anyway..
