-Fix the cleric thing. There is a thread here now about it. Its SERIOULSY dragging the game down at this point. Cleric is a mega-burnout class. As a shaman, I can go ninja-AFK for a couple minutes if I need to, and about all that will likely happen is the tank will take a bit more damage and need an extra heal. On raids, no one even notices if I am AFK these days. But if a Cleric looks away from the screen in a fight or a raid, even just for a minute to answer the phone, someone is probably gonna die. They NEED to spread out the healing a LOT LOT more. I am beginning to think that I should change my position on the healing situation for shamans (currently, I feel like we only need a minor bump, to become effective back-up healers, but I am slowly changing that)
