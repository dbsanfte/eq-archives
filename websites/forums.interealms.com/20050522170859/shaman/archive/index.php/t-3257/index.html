Not sure if this still works....nor if it works for Offense / Weapon skills....BUT it used to work for raising Defense skills.
If you know a friendly chanter, /duel them and attack their pet. The pet is considered a npc by Everquest and you should get skill increases from it as opposed to attacking the chanter where you would see no increases as he is a PC.

Failing this, I've found most groups are happy to let Shaman bash away at a mob if he can still fulfil his role in the group.

Only other way is to grit your teeth, solo for a bit and prepare to slow up on the experience grind as you hammer away for 10mins per mob :rolleyes:
