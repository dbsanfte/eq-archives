Naagruz
Peeps used to obsess about the Totemic BP and I spent much time competing with 80+ other peeps in SK for most everything. I saw Grizz twice and never was fast enough to outkill those nasty druids, so I went on my way and came back 3 expansions later to help a guildy with Quillmane.

There are wandering treants that mirror the pathing of Grizz along with quite a few named mobs. Just keep in mind that the original Old World mobs always have some sort of PH that will set the timer going for the next pop. So pretty much kill anything that stands out and add in a few critters for good measure and eventually you will get your pop.

The undead area is a major point of respawn for most common critters in the northern part of the zone and it would not surprise me if it is the only one. Named mobs prolly have their own unique spawn point.

Gud luck, and just keep in mind that I picked up my Vindi BP quite a bit before I actually completed the Totemic BP quest :/
