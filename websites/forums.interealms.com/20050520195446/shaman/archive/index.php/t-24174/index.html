Ungkor
The only way to truly balance us is to remove slow from the game entirely. Give us more of everything else we have. (Of course, that would require retuning every single mob, and all heal lines, so SOE won't do it. They'd rather nerf three classes than do that much work).

Its precisely because slow couldn't be tuned to work with game mechanics at the higher levels, that they mitigated it to rebalance the game (tank dead unslowed, tank easily kept alive fully slowed).

And we have been balanced around our ability to slow. Adding more slow is exactly the WRONG way to go if you want us balanced.

Anyone that doesn't understand that is blind. Run the numbers at extreme dps: its easily proven that slow can't be tuned at upper end encounters. Thats precisely the reason that most boss mobs are immune to it now. If they wanted the encounter to be challenging after slow, the pre-slow dps would eat any tank instantly before slow. Can't be tuned. So, immunity and low enough dps that its challenging, but possible, is SOE's answer.
