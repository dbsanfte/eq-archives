Do the Totemic quests! Especially the Helm!
By lvl 30 I had a full set of totemic. I quested all the pieces myself. For the harder mobs (GRizzleknot, Grifenne, Lancer swordfish, Centaur Charger), I often had help from other people in the zone.

The key for me was hunting in the zone where the drops were. Every now and then I would OOC that I was lokking for no-drop item X from mob Y. That way I never stopped XPing but still got the drops i needed.

For the mobs that I didn't get help on, I learned some good EQ survival skills (how to pull, dungeon pathing, solo techniques). I'm not saying that I'm better than any other player, but I am better than I used to be! :D I still pick the brain of most higher level shaman I encounter

Takk
Tometic is the way to go for the first nifty looking armor. The BP and the helm are usually the bottle neck since the bear for the helm is usually on a 7 hour or so timer and the mob for the bp is on an 2 or 3 times a week timer. However, since most tometic was "old world" armor, many people don't seem to do it any more so you may find it alot easier than I did.

Another alternative is go to karnor and ask to loot the "jarcrap" armor. It makes you look like a newbie but its stats are pretty decent. Plus most people at karnor just hate the stuff (no drop). The only downside is that most of the mobs are extemely red to you and you have to be very careful when you pick up the stuff.
