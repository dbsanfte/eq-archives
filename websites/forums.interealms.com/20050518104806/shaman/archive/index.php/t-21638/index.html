OK back to basic LDoN 101 - Points are points. It does not matter where you earn them, you may spend them anywhere. Points are points.

Buying power at the various camps is based on the amount of points EARNED at that specific camp. However, the points themselves may be earned anywhere.

Example: You do 30 runs in EF and have 1500 points and Buying Power. You spend it all in EF on a BP. Then you go to N Ro and do 30 runs, now you hav 1500 points and 1500 buying power in EF and N Ro. You may now use those points to go back to EF and buy something, or buy something in N Ro. You do not need to spend the points you earn at the camp you earned them. Points are points.

Now that we have this explained, what this new stuff is basically doing is making armor ~= to the "top" (for shaman, EF, plate, N Ro, etc) in other camps. Therefore, I dont have to get 1500 "buying power" in EF to buy top armor, I can buy it in EC since I have more than enough "buying power" there already.
