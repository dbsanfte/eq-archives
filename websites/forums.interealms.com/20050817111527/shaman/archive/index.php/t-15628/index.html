weissblau
Use the /viewport command and you'll never have trouble reading text again.

If you just do /viewport it'll tell you your current screen dimensions (like 0 0 1280 1024 for me). Then do /viewport 0 0 1280 750 or something appropriate for your resolution, and you end up with a nice black band at the bottom of the screen. Cram your chat windows into this area and you'll never have problems reading text in there.
