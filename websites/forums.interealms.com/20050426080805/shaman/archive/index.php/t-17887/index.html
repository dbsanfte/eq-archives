Grixx
I've been root, rotting in PoN and it's pretty good experience. Yard trash (trees, ravens, spiders) yield about 1% regular exp in lvl 60. Malo em from afar, root, DoT, reapply root as needed. It's pretty easy.

For a real challenge, try the gargolyes at the far side of PoN. Get a levitate up, and hover across the river over near the hobgoblin area (careful - they see invis). Hug the zone wall past the hedge and be careful of the Foreboding Guardians. You'll come to a big stone fort which has gargoyles on the walls. Set up camp on the zone wall beside the fort. There's a big space there where you can have plenty of room to root, run, etc. Gargoyles hit a bit harder than yard trash but yield 1.5% to 2% regular exp in lvl 60 (about 5% AA each). They aren't much harder to kill. Don't bother with the pet. Just root/rot em and keep your distance. Malo really helps here. It's safe from any possibilty of trains and you can even camp there - it's 100% safe.
