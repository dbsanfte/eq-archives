Getan
Not really a penalty, you just don't get bonus xp for a raid group.

So if you had 5 people in a normal group and a mob worth 500 xp, each person would get 180 xp since the group has an xp bonus of 1.8.

On the otherhand, take the same 5 people in a raid group and they only get 100 xp each.

Now normaly you have a lot of people in raids, lets say 50. If the mob gave 500 xp then each person only gets 10 xp.
