Bamana DeMoro
That hammer is in the ammo slot, not the range.

This is commonly done to give you an extra item you can leave on your corpse. In this way you can loot all of your important items and still leave an item on your body so it might be rezed at some future date.

This is much preferable to leaving an important piece of armor or a bag.

Henora
I have a swollen fungus beast gland in my ammo slot. :)

but to answer your question... range slot gives stats. ammo slot does not give stats.

shaman CAN actually throw shattering hammers.
put a shattering hammer in the range slot. Then put a stack of shattering hammers in the ammo slot.

Then go to your combat hotkeys box and click range attack and you will throw hammers... not with any skill of course, but you can do it.

Beware hitting that range attack button though, if you have an item in your range slot with a range modifier (like a spear with Range: 100) those items can be thrown as well.

like the other folks said, people leave items in the ammo slot, so you can loot your corpse and just leave one thing to hold it for a rez while still wearing all your gear.
