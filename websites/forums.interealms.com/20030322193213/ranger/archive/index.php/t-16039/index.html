Astroshak
If JP is too crowded for your tastes, try soloing in the Hole, at the entrance. There's a bit of downtime, but you should be able to do it. With KEI at 53 I was able to keep the first 5 elementals down; without it only the first three.

You're 51 but you've got better gear than I did when I started that. Just keep your buffs on (especially FoN; the regen on it is more valuable than hte AC and 1 extra DS point from CoE) and use your mana for healing and snaring instead of nuking.

Not as efficient as fear kiting, but it does give a break from staring at animal butts and there's always a slight chance you could get an IGS (I never did, but I understand that those elemental warriors can drop it).
