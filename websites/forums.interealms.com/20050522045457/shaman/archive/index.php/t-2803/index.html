Jinheim
To answer your question:

The JBB is a very powerful tool, and makes soloing very easy. However, there is more to the game than exp grinding. I believe that in the high end game, iksars are the best shamans. They have the AC bonus and the regen, two very important things for a shaman. However, trolls have an easier time getting there, thanks to having a more gentle exp penalty, and being able to use a JBB. The lack of JBB isn't that much of a disadvantage. You can still group and duo just as well without a JBB. You cannot solo as easily, but an iksar with fungi + iksar regen + regrowth is a cannibalizing beast, and has more than enough mana to cast lots of dots with little downtime, which a barbarian shaman with no fungi (like me) cannot.

Either race is a fine choice, and if you already have a shaman made, stick with it.
