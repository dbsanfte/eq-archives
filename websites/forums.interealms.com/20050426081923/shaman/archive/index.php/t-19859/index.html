Lanadena
The misty thicket walled area (goblins, orcs, spiders) should get you to 13 or so. You can also hit Northern Karanas bandits, and the wildlife there (lions and beetles) from 12 to 20. In the 12 to 25 range you can also go to Eastern Karanas for the gorge hounds, spiders and gnoll reavers. The plus side to Eastern Karanas is that it's close to High Keep where you can do some banking.

None of these places are hunted regularly on Xegony so if it's the same on your server, then you don't have to fight for your pulls.

You can effectively level in PC at about lvl 4 and up. Go in from the shadeweever thicket side and you will enter in on mobs the same lvl as you.
