Gloriana
You can easily start it at level 46, there are a few good reasons to start it pre-50. The two additional rewards (shield of falsehood, black fur boots) are very nice. Black fur boots with insta cast SoW usuable from inventory will be a vital tool for the rest of your character's existance!

Another good reason is the pages you have to collect from CoM. It would be much better to collect the pages while the area still gives you exp, rather than wait and have to camp it for days when it's green and pretty much a waste of your time.

With the help of a 50s group to kill Black Dire, you can do the whole quest up to killing Kirn pre 50.

Harvyst
I agree with Glorianna. The sooner you get the boots, the better. I always thought the shield stank, but that's my personal opinion.

The first bit can be done as low as your 20's, if you can do Invis Vs Animals correctly. I couldn't, so I came back at 49 with friends.

The shield bit can be bought in the bazaar, but at 46, should be a breeze.

Dire requires a few friends, a solid group of high 40's low 50's can take him.

At 46 you can solo the entrance to CoM and get the pages, only the spectral courier requires a friend with track to get.

Ghiosk books rot frequently from reaver raids, just shout that you want them and you'll get :)

Kirn, Neh, the tear, and Rak all require raid forces. So you could get it as early as 46 (assuming you wanted to get your own tear), but it is only clickable at 51, IIRC.
