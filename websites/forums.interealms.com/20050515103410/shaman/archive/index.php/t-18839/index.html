Zorlac Sabboth
Coirnav encounter:
Location: Plane of Water, aka Reef of Coirnav.
Event description:
Coirnav is sitting on his throne in the Worldstone Keep. He then summons wave after wave of mobs with each having a "leader" or mini boss.
First wave: Corenzo (sp?) the Annihilator and his cronies. They are fire immune.
Second wave: Ahmed the Cursed and his cronies. Adds are cold immune, Ahmed and other generals are magic and poison immune.
Third wave: Bartuc the something and his cronies. All lightning immune.
Fourth wave: Some named Demon and his cronies, poison and fire immune.
Fifth wave: Lister the Tormentor and his cronies. They all have stun and pushback attack, immune to fire.
Last wave: Coirnav himself. He spawns adds and can clone himself. Coirnav is fairly easy, has a nasty cold AE that pushes back.

Rathe Encounter
Location: Plane of Earth B aka Ragrax Stronghold of the Twelwe.
Event description: You need to push five seals to spawn boss (from east to west).
First seal: no effect.
Second seal: Spawns Infector of Souls and his cronies. Kill them all. Infector os mostly poison immune and can be fire and/or lightning immune, on rare occasions is phisically immune. Cronies are poison immune.
Third seal: Spawns Lord DeSeis and his cronies. His cronies can curse so melee be ware. DeSeis is immune to two random spell types.
Fourth seal: Spawns some named Gloom with cronies, they are immune to lightning and drain mana.
Fifth seal: spawns The Rathe himself.

Rathe is pretty badass, lightning based targeted AE attack, root prison and cold AE from melee. His melee is very tough.
