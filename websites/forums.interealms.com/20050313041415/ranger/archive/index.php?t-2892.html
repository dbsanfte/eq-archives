1. Use Aoenla Darkwood's Fletching Guilde (using Cheapest Method) to get skill 202.

2. After that I was using a combination of Trueshot Longbows and the elemental arrows to try to get higher to no avail.


With the tradeskill patch, all types of acylia are now usefull to fletchers as you can break down the large bricks and blocks or combine the small pieces to get the Small Bricks that are actually needed. I went heavy acrylia farming, converted it all to acrylia shafts then made a combination of the Condensed Flame and Condensed Shadow based arrows.


Being able to do my own blacksmithing helps a ton as I do not need to go looking for a blacksmith nor wait for a blacksmith I know to get on.
