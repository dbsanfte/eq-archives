Vladimirr
The Aerin projection only stays up for a few minutes.

For the hand-in quests that flag a single person (like the HoH or Tactics ones), supposedly you can't progress any further in the plotline. So if you got to the trials in HoH, they wouldn't flag you to get to Marr, or if you killed the ZekJr's, you wouldn't get the flags there either.

Those quests are simply for the purpose of opening up a new hunt spot for you.
