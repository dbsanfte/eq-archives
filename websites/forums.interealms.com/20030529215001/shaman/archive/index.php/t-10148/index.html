Slurm
Some changes were made to exp allocation over the past few months (some even older than that) that explain what you are seeing.

1. experience from a kill is divided equaly among all party members in range. It is no-longer weighted based on level.

2. a bonus is given to the exp based on the number of party members (this was done to encourage full groups over soloing/small groups).

3. bonus exp is given to classes with exp penalties (this is how they took out the class based exp penalties -not by changing the exp table, but by addind a multiplier)

4. if the mob's level is close to (or equal/higher) your level you get an additional bonus multiplier (10%). This level range is LESS than the dk blue range -so, mobs that are on the high end of dk blue give you more exp than ones on the low end of dk blue.

5. post 60 the dk blue level range was reduced. At 60 the dk blue range was about 15 levels. At 65 it is 10 levels, with the exp bonus (change 4) ending at about 8 levels.
