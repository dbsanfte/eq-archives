If you're *only* worried about AC, there's a 30ac ALL/ALL earring in Umbral that's not too hard to get. My group, 60 epic Monk, 60 Enchanter, 58 Cleric, 56 Epic Druid, 60 Epic Ranger with Endless Quiver and Archery Mastery3 and myself, 57 Post-Epic Paladin took him down with minimal problems. The MoBs name is Festersore, level 55 Necromancer in the second junction room of the Netherbian Caves (left wall facing out from zin). Be sure to manasieve him though, he managed to get a gate off (/rude!) and summoned our GAR to his spawn point. The GAR pretty much ended up tearing him to shreds though. Festersore's PH is unknown to me, but he *does* have one.

On a side note, these caves are fun and can be challenging with one group, up to and including Tawro Icequake and his Spirit. One of my favoite spots in EQ. I recommend exploring them all with a single 55+ group you trust.

There aren't too many very high ac earrings. If you have descent smithing or are willing to work it up the sanctum guardians earring from the spirit of garzicor quest is a really nice earring. Only 5ac so not a major upgrade for ya but very nice stats.

For the belt you might be able to find the artificers ringmail belt, 15ac 5 wis 40hps mana. Very nice belt as far as tradable stuff goes.

The bracer definitely has to go. Artificer ringmail bracer is another nice peice, 12ac 5 wis 30hps/mana and 5 sv all. Or a wis or sta bracer from Umbral, one of the dragon bracers or the new rubi bracer from CT.

The divine inspiration is a good range slot item for ac and wis.

The death mask or crystal eye's are both nice masks with good ac.
