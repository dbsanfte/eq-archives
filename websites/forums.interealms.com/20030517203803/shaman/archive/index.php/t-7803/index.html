Head to the moon! I've solo'd and grouped in Maiden's Eye and The Grey, and grouped in Umbral Plains. The mobs tend to have a lot more hps here so I've found it more effecient to group. Scarlet Desert is another place on the moon worth looking up. It is not nearly as dangerous as Maiden's Eye and I've spent a lot of time there before PoP came out. City of Mist is said to be easy to solo but a lot of stuff may be lt blue at 54. If you want raw exp though head to the planes. The generic group make up in the new planes requires a Chealer, a slower and a mezzer. So finding a group should not be a problem. On a good day you can walk out with several yellow in xp maybe even a whole lvl depending on how long you stay. On a bad day the planes can be endless frustration.
