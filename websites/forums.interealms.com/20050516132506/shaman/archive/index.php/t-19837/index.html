Dekurgen Heyokah
Full access, then its PoF tables all the way. root/rot, dont get hit.
If tables is occupied: you can either ask to pull through tables from courtyard to fro bridge(assuming trigger isnt up)if they are just doing mobs at tables OR you can do magma guardians back at castle#1(they take longer and there arent as many of them though). Root/rot, dont get hit a lot.
14% aa exp per kill, thats about as good as it gets for shaman anyways afaik. I swap out Rosey pants for ornate and keep them perma rooted while I dot them with BoU,(Pox from rosey pants),Bane,BoS,Scourge of Nife,epic, refresh Bane and BoS until they fall down(They usually die just as or just before BoU ends). You may want to skip epic except when they are close enough from a broken root becuase its a pita to move into range on horseback and cast the sloooow weak arse dot while doing a perma-root rotation.
I keep tables area cleared when Im there alone, it's not as fast aa's as a good group-but you can still do fairly well.
<shrug> hope that helps. Good Hunting :)
