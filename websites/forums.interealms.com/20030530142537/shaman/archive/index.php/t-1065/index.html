It's an interesting dance. The slows are eventually very comparable:

Turgur's
Slow for between 66% (L51) to 75% (L60)
Mana Required: 250
Spell Duration: 6 seconds (L1) to 360 seconds (L60)
Duration Formula: 7
Casting Time: 3.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds
Recast Delay: 6.0 seconds

Forlorn Deeds
Slow for between 67% (L57) to 70% (L60)
Mana Required: 225
Spell Duration: 6 minutes
Duration Formula: 6
Casting Time: 6.0 seconds
Spell Recovery: 2.25 seconds

A shaman slowing can do so while the chanter handles mezzes. Also, we can have much higher AC than an enchanter, allowing us to handle the slow aggro better.
