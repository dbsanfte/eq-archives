Well before you go out and go ape crazy buying stuff couple things to note. Your Mobo is gonna be a 440bx at best which is agp 1x which restricts you from installing the 8x vid cards avaiable now (not that 8x makes a lick of difference now its just 8x cards wont work in 1x slots). Your system is a bit to slow to really take advantage of 150 bucks of vid card right now. Thats ok if you were using a riva going to a g3 even a g2 will be a sizable improvement. Something you can find probably easily still is a g4mx440 just make sure its a 4x one.

If all you have is a pci slot and no agp just look for the g4mx or g2 line cards and you'll be fine. Normally I'd rec the 150 dollar range myself for most modern gaming but that would send your particular system into a chain reaction of upgrades.

PS also the ati suggestions are good however i've found at times going from an nvidia to an ati or vice versa card can make older installs of windows break particularly the 9x series and involves some tinkering. In your case the least hassle would def be going with an nvidia in the sub 100 range. However your gonna want to think about upgrading that computer in the near (year or so just due to age and upgrades of games we play like now) future, even the cheapest computer nowadays with a decent vidcard tossed in will floor your old machine.
