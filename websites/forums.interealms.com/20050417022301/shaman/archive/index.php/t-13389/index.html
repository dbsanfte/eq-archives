Zyphun
I know exactly how you feel, I spent what seemed like forever trying to find a place to solo at 52. What I did find was City of Mist. You know all those people that push it as a great XP spot? Well believe it or not they are right. ^_^ Baring a hard break this camp is as easy as it can be challenging and it's always rewarding. Some pointers and superstition from my time leveling from 52 to 54 in two days though...

The best place for XP without getting XP without help is the back area would the gazebos. The 6 mobs that spawn there are the same mobs that spawn up along the top edges of the city. This is both a blessing in the xp they give and a curse as the mobs that you'll find to break can be some what tough to do deal with in close quarters. I would greatly suggest coming to CoM with C3 and Virtue.

The attachment is a map of CoM, marked X is my pull spot. When I first get there I kill the spawns at place marked 'E' then cast levitate on myself at point 'Y' and run to point 'Z'. I'll prep here and bind, when I go AFK for any reason I always go to the opposite side of 'Z', but so long as you don't get trained, I've never seen a mob path even close enough to 'Z' to agro on you. When I am ready, I pull to point 'X'.

I've marked out the mob spawn points in red. The purple spot (marked 'D') is a place where you'll probably find two mobs swimming against the corner. I pull in order A to E. When you pull and to avoid adds I suggest the following... Pull the highest level on first, though the mobs seem to have a mingling level range they go as follows from lowest to highest:

Spectre/Ghosts - A Shadow, An Apparition, A Phantom, A Wraith (<-- nasty)

Skeletons - Spurbone Skeleton, Plaguebone Skeleton, A Greater Spurbone, A Great Plague Skeleton, Greater Plaguebone, Greater War Boned Skeleton (<-- nasty)

Golems - A Vapor Golem, A Fog Golem, A Haze Golem, A Mist Golem, An Army Behemoth

At level 52 with using slow to pull I seemed to be able to pull most everything but the top two of each category by themselves. By level 53 I could usually pull all but the top one by themselves. With the Specters you have to be careful as they cast a lifetap. This will agro your pet and you'll end up with 3 or more mobs to play with. Nothing you can't handle most often, but we're trying to keep this sane. ^_^ Just keep Poochie on a short leash and you'll do fine. ^_^ Also the pathing can be a bit off when you're pulling to X. The mobs will sometimes fall into the water, the best advice is to just let them path back to you, so long as there aren't some high level pops in the corners (and there usually aren't) you won't get any adds, though if there is a mob at point 'D' it can agro and you should be ready for it with root. If the pet falls into the water though, you have two choices, kill it or let it path back to you. If something is agro on him (i.e. it's in the middle of a fight) he will train everything from southern side (spot 'C') and sometimes the ones in water on northern side (spot 'D') as well. When you get to spot 'D' (usually a goo or golem) I suggest pulling it back to spot 'C' unless you like fighting underwater.

With C3, my lizard, my JBB and my mage equipped super pooch (Haste Mask, HP Belt and two fists) I was able to easily clear all the spawn spots. As a warning I had one of the mobs from point 'D' spawning on the roof of the gazebo at spot 'B'. I pulled him first and dealt with adds. Also when you're fighting and cleared point 'D' you'll sometimes get mobs that will path from the water to point 'X'. Just keep an eye out and never go AFK at point 'X' and you should be fine. ^_^

Well there's my off the cuff guide to hunting my little piece of heaven in CoM. I got lucky as I was leveling last night someone killed Lord G and didn't need the books. ^_^ I am now one step closer to my epic ^_^
