How do you activate innate camo or do you?
Viken
Open up your Alt Advacment window (by pressing V) then click on Class then click Innate Camo then click on the Hotkey button (towards the lower right) then drop the Hotkey onto your Hot Buttons window. The Hotkey button will be greyed out until you purchase the skill.

Alternatively, you can write your own macro/hotkey using the following command:

/alternateadv activate xxx

Where xxx corresponds to some AA ability. Innate camo is 80. GotF is 184, and Entrap is 219.
