Kanamori, I don't know how populated the databases are yet for equipment. However, you might want to look over at EQ traders and check out the new augment systems. You can take an attunable augment (almost any kind) and, via a process of alchemy, poison making, and smithing, create a new augment that can be a burning affliction 5 (and go all the way to 70).

BA5 is only 20% however. Depending on where you are in the expansions, if you have discordant focus items available (qvic+), even the level 70 spells are downgraded only 10% off of a 45% focus (ie, 40.5% focus still).

The new augment system looks pretty sweet so far. I'd like to make one for faerune. :)
