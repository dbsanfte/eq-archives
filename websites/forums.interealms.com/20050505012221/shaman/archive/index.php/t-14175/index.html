Since the original poster mentioned the ranger epic, I'll have to say this:

A lot of the time, rangers only use Swiftwind because you can get better main handers than Earthcaller, but it's hard to find a better offhander than Swiftwind. With 40% haste and +30atk, even with a better haste item, many high end rangers still equip it for the atk when using archery.

Also, if you don't count Earthcaller, Swiftwind itself is about as difficult as the Shaman epic, so it's one of the easiest epics. Much of it is easy to MQ or go to "tag along" raids with another guild, so many rangers will have Swiftwind whose guild isn't capable of Hate minis.

My ranger alt (was my main) has both epics, but Earthcaller is bagged, only used when solo or when we have no slower in the group, because the Toxiferous Axe I have is better. Tox Axe currently sells at around 9-10kpp, has a better ratio and is faster, and doesn't have an agro proc.

In other words, many of the half-epic rangers you see running around either have the second one and have bagged it, or never bothered to get it in the first place, because you can buy better for under 10kpp. Part of that is because of the difficulty of getting the Hate drop (or getting people to go to Hate at all), of course, but much of it is because it's not worth getting except for completeness/accomplishment, or if you solo a lot.

On epics in general: for a guild like mine, a small family guild that raids a little, epics are around the best weapons we'd be able to obtain for some of our classes, so although they're nearly 3 years old and there are much better gear out there, it's still worth persuing for us, and still gives a sense of accomplishment for our guild when we do them.
