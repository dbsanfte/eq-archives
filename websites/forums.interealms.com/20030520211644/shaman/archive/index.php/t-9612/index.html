Duurg
Correct me if I'm wrong here.... Most weapons that doggy can use appear to be melee (rog/war/rng) based. I usually go with a 1hs that is war, pal, sk, bard, ranger/all, or even use the mage summoned war/none (none = pet only). Also, only give items that are either primary/secondary, and non lore items.

Try any FS weapon, or even velium weapon for starters. If possible, upgrade to <a href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=2665">Gnoll Hide Lariat</a>, or <a href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=625">Dagger of Dropping</a>. Those work well, and are kinda fun to watch your doggy use / proc. Another one that the 55+ pet can use is the <a href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=246">Whip of Strangulation</a>, just adds a little debuff to str and agi, along with a small dot. I once even tried a Sword of Skyfire when farming, but sadly the 55 pet was too young to proc the AE on it (lvl 45 pet, and need lvl 47 or so to proc).

What ever you try, make sure you turn on "Others Hits", and watch your pet go from biting, to 1hs, 1hb, or 1hp as per the weapon you give him. Give him dex if he has an item that can proc, and haste him. If you question his abilities, give him a FS dagger, a velium spear and watch him work. It's very nice to see the dual wield, along with bash and kicks.
