Bethiana_MM
None of us are happy about what is happening to the Panther line of spells. We're all in agreement that Sony's lack of communication has always been a problem. After all, they promised to balance us with this spell and have instead nerfed it to near uselessness. Why? We haven't gotten an answer, and if we do it will be a dismissive one-line response from someone who couldn't care less. Therein lies the problem. We still care, because we log in and spend our free time playing this game as Shaman. It's not a job to us, as it is to the developers, and we don't want it to be. This change makes absolutely no sense to us, and the only other time I've seen such a unified uproar from a class was when Sony attempted to make Slay Undead an activated ability that reduced the Paladins tanking ability significantly while active. They roared, things changed.

I think we can do the same here, but not with Panther.

A lot has been taken from us over the years, and very little has been given back as compensation. There was never a problem with the slowing classes' slowing abilities. Shaman slowed faster and better because it was our job, so why did the other classes need to have their slows brought up, thus making the already near insignificant disparity between slows altogether more miniscule? Did they do this because with all slowing classes so nearly equal in their ability they can finally up our healing power? If so, great . . . but we should be made aware. As it is, I see this as nothing more than a knowing big brother taking the younger's toys away and dangling them over his head. Maybe there's a new, better toy you can open up and play with after dinner, maybe not. We don't know. We don't know.

Communication has always been Sony's weakest point with its customers, and it could alleviate a lot of grief and annoyance if they would actually tell us what they're thinking.

Trust me, we are interested.

While requesting a fix for the nerf on the Panther/Leopard line will make us happy in the short-term, we should know better by now. Short-term is just that. We need to think about the long-term, and the only long-term fix I see that needs to be addressed is communication. What was the point of the guild summit? The top ten lists? The class representatives? We, as a community, see so little, if anything, come from that. (That is not to say I do not appreciate all of Grimbear's work. I do, very much so, but Sony tells him very little and does even less.)

My plea isn't for a fix to Panther. You know what? Leave it the way it is. Bend this spell over and do horribly unmentionable things to its horribly unmentionable areas, but in return give us customer service, become a part of our communities, communicate with us openly, ignore the trolls, flamebait tossers and ninnies and be our friends, not our enemies as so many of you appear to be. And you know why we harbor this animosity toward you and don't trust you? We don't know what you're thinking, and we can only see the end result of your work.

Until then,

I can't give you my money if you can't tell me what you're doing and why, at the bare minimum. I won't accept that from any other company, and I certainly won't accept it from Sony.
