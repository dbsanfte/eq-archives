Hail,

Make sure you keep your DEFENSE skill max'd for your level. This is a huge AC hit for you if the gap gets too big, and gets harder to keep up over time. Also keep your 1hb,2hb, and piercing max'd for your level. Weapon skills aren't crucial, but you will want to try hitting (you'll miss alot) stuff every now and then. Don't forget to spend a little time each level to keep your divination & evocation up to par.

Dogdog weapons always poof, whether the dog lives or dies. Think of them like arrows or throwing knives for shaman. :D

I don't think shaman is a good class for someone who wants extra time to chat. In good groups, we're normally pretty busy (though bards are busier). I would think wizards would be a better class for non-keyboard intensive playing.

Regardless, welcome to the community. :cool: We have a wealth of information on these boards, though some of it is buried quite deep. ;) Keep using the search feature to try to find the answers, as most questions are common and can easily be found if you look. :cool:

Happy leveling,
