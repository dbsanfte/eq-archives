Jarren Icewulf
Definitely works with horse models off. You just don't see your mana bar update (increase) like normal. It will only update when you cast a spell. So you could be standing there for an hour and your mana bar will look as if it never increased or filled up. Once you cast a spell, any spell (including JBB). Your mana bar will update and reflect how much mana you have. So while chain casting your JBB, you should see your mana increase with every cast.

Also, if you are a Shaman, and you bought the cheapest horse (which is smart choice), then its best to have Horse models turned off anyways. That way your SoW will take effect and overwrite the horse speed.

While standing still and chaincasting JBB. you will be gainig back mana as if you were sitting and meditating. Anytime you move or attack (melee) then you willo not get the meditate effect.
What I do, is turn attack on just as the JBB spell lands, then turn attack off while casting JBB again (8 secs). That way you get that extra melee attack dmg in there, while medding 95% of the time.

Hope this helps clear up any confusion. :)
