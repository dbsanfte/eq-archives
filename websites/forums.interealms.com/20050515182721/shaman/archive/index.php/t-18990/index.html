afreeman
I'm looking to my Troll brethren and hoping I get a "hell yeah" we level so slowly we lose exp with each kill.

I'm not one of your troll brethren (I play a barb), but I would be if I was rerolling today. Trolls and ogres are the best 2 races for shammies, IMO, with iksar 3rd.

Remember that while it will take you 20% more exp per level as a troll, the level formula is exponential, and thus you won't ever be more than a level or 2 behind a frog shammie (the fastest-leveling shaman race). Since any given level can now be had in, at most, about 10 hours of play time, I think its fair to say that the exp penalty is not that significant in the overall scheme of things.

In fact, I did a spreadsheet a while back that compared various races/classes on this issue, and found that a halfling warrior (the fastest race/class combo) would hit level 60 (the cap at the time) just before a non-warrior troll/iskar would hit level 57. Because of the race/class penalties, the halfling had something like only about 65% of the exp that the troll had. However, because of the exponential nature of the calculations, this didn't scale directly into levels.

This calculation would be off today, because the exp gained per kill has been adjusted for mob level (it was just a flat deduction for high greens then), but the overall idea remains the same.

Put more simply: no, its not that big of a deal. Leveling for any class really starts to suck past about 55, regardless of race. Don't reroll because trolls are one of (if not the) best races for a shammie, and you're only about 1 day of solid play behind where you could have been as a frog anyway.
