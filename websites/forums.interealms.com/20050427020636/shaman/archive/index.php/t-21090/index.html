Howdy Howdy,

I'm level 55, and have been hitting CS pretty steadily for a while now. My mage did it when he was 51, so a shaman shouldn't have much trouble.

What you want to do is hit the "a wyvern" spawns. They uncommonly drop "a wyvern hides" which sell for about 175-250p in the bazaar. they rarely drop ToV quest gems, and they normally drop between 0p and 6p.

The xp is increadible at 51-54, but it cuts down at 54 and 55... however, if no one else is camping them (quad kiters go there alot), I can pull in about 1000p an hour, after all is said and done.

Try it out guys, it's worth trying. :)
