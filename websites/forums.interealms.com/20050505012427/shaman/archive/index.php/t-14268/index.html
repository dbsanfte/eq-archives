Went through some of Veksar with a group, after servers came up. It has a very good dungeon feel to it. It has a purplish tint, which gives a relief from the rusty brown decor so many other dungeons have. Drops are appropriate for the level range of the zone.

Ive been to several camps here. One was the big room after the first set of golems. Has about 10-15 spawns and 2 named that pop in room. All spawns were drk blue to me at 65. Mobs have low aggro radius so you can single pull the whole room.

Another camp was pretty deep past the hand area. We saw a lvl 65 named Skeleton "remains of Sythrax" which dropped a nice 1hs with an aggro proc. I sent the SS into Allakhazams.

The great thing about this zone is even though it takes a little time to get to the deeper camps, most a re very close the the actual zone out/in. You can leave the camps via ramps that drop you down into the main courtyard. You can target friends for CoH and even drag corpses through. So if you want to insert more replacements, have them die near the wall then target and drag them in and rez. Guildies did this over the weekend.

Most mobs can be slowed with Turgurs the first shot with no debuff. The deeper camps require debuffing.

The closer camp was easily head by 3 of us (65 War, Clr, Shm).

Some decent loot drops here including the Kylong Gauntlets which I am know using since the were better then Wolf Callers and Hurricane.
