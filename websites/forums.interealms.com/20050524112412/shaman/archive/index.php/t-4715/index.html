**Disclaimer: reading this MAY cause you to laugh and having a second pair of pants nearby is recommended. And strange looks by people around you and the comment "WHAT are you laughing at?" are in no way the fault of the author**

OK to properly understand this story you sorta need to have a druid of level 24 or greater, or a ranger level 46 or higher..otherwise it MIGHT not be as funny...

*keep reading it gets better, I promise*

So...yesterday I logged on my shaman (level 34 at the time) and half heartedly solo some cougars while waiting for my friend to log on his necro. I note a level 37 druid (darn those quad kiters) and see a TON of corpses around the gnome pirates. And, yes, the druid did train friend and I with 6-8 cougars on purpose (and no we didnt steal any of his kite, he was just jealous that us killing one at a time was faster than his kite :) )..but that isnt the point of this thread.

*Don't give up; winners never quit, and quitters never reach the funny part*

Just as I'm getting bored, friend logs in and we start massively slaying cougars (shaman+necro is VERY VERY nice).

*almost to the good part*

Fast forward about two hours..
I log on after going LD and friend going afk to see the two that had left corpses on the ground by the gnomes (level 34 sk and warrior) getting torn apart by a very very bad pull of about 4-5 cougars almost right by me *GASP*...yes I could have tried to save them but they were at about 200hp each so no point in me dying too.

*DONT stop! you're almost to what you came for!*

Fast forward about one hour...
Friend and I are still killing cougars and I see the SK zone in. We keep pulling and pulling; trying to get level 35 was all that was on my mind :).

Note: I ALWAYS have pet up (hope I havent ruined it yet ;) )

*OK! the wait is over*

So friend is pulling while i'm medding and pet is sitting by me regening...and lo~ SK has finally retrieved corpse and is headed in my direction (ACK....MUST......HIDE............!!). he stops several feet away (near pet) and.. (ACK DONT!) "Hail, Garbatik"

I quickly check myself, and yes..still named Rautonim hrm...

I change to third person and yap...just me and dog sitting there...

At this point i'm almost sure he accidentally clicked pet; I know pet always seems to be in the way when i try to loot ( "Garbatik judges you amiably blah blah") or else he is a roleplayer.

He waits the (must be mandated by the EULA right?) 3 seconds before asking the dreaded question "Could I bug you for a SOW". At this point, I'm about to fall out of my chair laughing because Clueless_SK_01 has mistaken dog dog for a druid.

I was totally shocked that someone could make it up to level 34 and NOT figure out that wolf (transparent at that) was NOT a druid. I didnt know what to say, and typing was hard while trying to maintain my composure. I must have waited at least one minute trying to figure out how to break it to him that Gabartik wouldn't be performing any tricks for him today. I maybe could have kept silent to see how long before he begged a port *snicker*.

Instead, I said "have you ever seen a shaman before?" and /pet who the leader. He did realize his mistake (luckily...) and claimed he was tired, haha. I decided that after all that he was worthy of 40 of my mana, and a quickness, and a regeneration..hadn't had a good laugh like that for ages.

Although i will add /rude for walking up to someone who is OBVIOUSLY killing stuff, and that sort of behavior NEVER lands you a "sow plz" or "buffs plz" whatever that is :D

At least my day that started with being trained by a rude druid turned out to be so good. ding 35! And FINALLY my REIF is proc'ing...no more seeing "your will is not sufficient blah blah" after whiffing :D

Moral of the story Rocky and Bullwinkle style:
Not all wolves can port you
OR
The transparant wolf don't do no tricks

I'm sure other's have escaped LOIO/OT/DL and ventured far enough out to mingle and make marvels of intellectual comments/begs such as this..hope i meet more, could use a good laugh every now and then
(apologies to those who do the kunark circuit and are intelligent, hats off for dealing with these people 24/7)

fin.
