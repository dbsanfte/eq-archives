Landragoran Heronwing
I'll give u a description you can't really mess up.
Go from entrance to the lev tunnel. Then go down there, take your second left turn, where to make a right turn would put you at bridgekeeper spawn. Go down till it forks and make a right, then an immediate left. Then make your next right, and next immediate left. You will see a house with mobs around and in it. Once you kill the chokidai dogs, you can simply invis everyone around back and left of the house, along the water to the last tunnel on the right, then click the stone.

I think it's easier to jump down from the BK spawn, there's a big pit on the left after you go past him. It'll drop you in front of the bank, in sight of the waterfall (the entrance is behind the waterfall).

Alternatively, go up to the BK spawn and go right toward the graveyard area. If you jump in the water, you can swim right down through the waterfall, and avoid all the mobs at the bank.

Zabi Bbuzzin
easiest way i go is head threw threw castle ent between zone in and zone out tunnels, when u get to first bridge jump off to the left on rocks til you reach bottom. you will be aggro as there are undead and living mobs on the way but 99 percent of the time they cant run down to the bottom of pit. once down in pit either get invis real quik and get behind the little rock there(may need shrink) or kill the slaves as they aggro when you sit sometimes. camp to clear aggro.

come back in and with invis run out of the cubby your in, go right down the tunnel to a T intersection, take left and immediate right(dizok cpt room door on your right) run up the ramp to the bridge keeper spawn point take a right turn and fall into the area down below which is bank area, look left for the waterfall and go threw that and hit the switch.

depending on luck and mobs up, at 65 i did this with no aggro besides the aggro at castle, but camped out for that.
