Calabar
I have a great respect for necromancers. Two of my best friends in game are a married couple, the husband plays a female gnome necromancer and the wife plays a female barbarian warrior. They make a very effective, if somewhat odd looking, team. With the ability to snare, fear, DOT, life tap and use a powerful pet, I feel necros are the most powerful solo class. But for some reason they aren’t overly popular in groups.

I soloed my shaman up to about 38th level without a lot of difficulty. Getting SoW at level nine helps a lot. So I feel shaman can be very effective at soloing, but I feel necromancers are more effective at it. That having been said, shaman have the advantage of being very desirable in groups as well. After playing a ranger and druid to 51, I was surprised at how popular I became all the sudden when my shaman started gaining levels.

Like any other class in the game, it really comes down to your likes and dislikes. Both necromancers and shaman can be effective at soloing, but each class has its own feel. I liked the potential of my necromancer, but I found I enjoyed playing my shaman more. If your looking for a change of pace that isn’t too drastic, give a shaman a try and see how you like the play style.
