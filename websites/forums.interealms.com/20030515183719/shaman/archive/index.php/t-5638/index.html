UP is an awesome solo spot for level 60 shamans with torpor. I also have horse, JBB, Epic, and decent gear (1850hp 1025ac 202wis unbuffed). No special AAs, just run3, regen3, and MC3 so far. Each mob is about level 55-58, the 58 ones enrage, and have about 20-25khp and double for 191-199 depending on level. They are easy to slow, and each fight need 1 reslow for me. I have gotten about 1y in 45 minutes if I try hard. Usually no downtime and 2-4% a kill. Mobs here all summon as well.

I summon a pet, focus, and regen myself, and haste pet. I use my horse when tanking the mob too. I pull with slow, and keep pox and epic on the mob the whole fight, while pet chews away and I JBB. I torpor when at about 60% hp left, and have never died due to it not landing in time.

I usually pull Moktors, or the rare DA or netherbian when I see them. Moktors are easy mobs, easy to slow, no need to malosini, and easily baned/poxed. DAs and Netherbians are easy, but are immune to Poison and Disease to make it a bit longer of a fight. DAs also BS, and proc darkness which is a snare/dot. Pulls are very easy and I barely get 2. Pathing is a bit bad in the tunnel near entrance, which is where I pull to. You need to walk the mob back to your pet, otherwise it will turn around and run away. This helps when you pull 2.

There are a few named here, I have soloed A Gantru Raldukan. He is easier than normal moktors IMO, as he has about 32khp or so and doubles for only 142. He has dropped the Gantru Charm twice (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=8964) and the Helm of Raldukan once (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=8985) Some decent cash gems also drop here, which sell for 10-15pp.

This is some of the easiest, most fun, and best exp U have ever gotten. Please add any info I missed, or ask questions about here.
