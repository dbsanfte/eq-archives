I leveled my SK to 60 in Nadox. Start at the entrance from the temple in Gunthak (invis all the way in). Start off in the Citadel. Named will pop there and drops a decent ring and arms.

Next, invis up and go to the Furious/Acolyte area. 2+ named there. The Broken Skull Prophet drops decent chain sleeves, and a caster mask. The Veteran also will spawn in the place of one of the furious. He drops a so so dagger, and some decent plate legs.

I like to set up camp in the hallway just past where the Furious path down to the hallway leading to the Citadel. Safe area, and one roamer to worry about every hour or so. Aggro is small in this zone.

Now, you're getting comfortable with the zone. Invis up and go down the tunnel to the undead area (where the SK can work his Envoke skills). There's a little ledge before you get to the main undead room. This is your camp spot. There are two/three undead there, and the Captain can spawn in the spot of the second one. He drops a nice plate cloak and decent caster shoulder item.

You then can take the other 4-6 in the room past the 2 entrance ones. You have one roamer to deal with. Sometimes he comes from below, sometimes from above. Just med with one facing up and one below. Keep an eye on your pets, because they'll see him coming.

Captain can also spawn in place of one of the undead next to the pillar just inside the room. You will also occasionally get the First Mate to spawn from one of the undead kills. He drops decent monk/bl/druid legs, and a rare Bard sword that sells very well 10k+. Stay on the ledge. You will get an occasional train (sometimes unannounced) from either the Luggald area, or someone going to or coming back from Hates Fury. It sucks, but SK can FD and if you can get running, you might make it back to the zone out.

The thing about Nadox is you can get a number of named that spawn from a corpse. The first time it happens, it will scare the **** outta you, because you're not ready for it. Your SK if he is cultural and has a WMBS can handle these mobs easily, even though they are even, to red to him. They all slow easily.

You will get a pretty good supply of gems (rubies and sapphires) for your trouble. A ****load of zombie skins for pottery. The undead also drop a Coral Ring, which is decent AC/Wis/HP/Mana, and sells quick at 1-1500pp.

The reason I liked this area was it's undead, which allow you to raise evocation on the SK, which is harder than hell to raise, since you only can do it on undead.

Not the loot droga provides, but the undead are dk blue to you and one level below your sk. Gems, and the drops make it reasonably profitable. XP modifier is okay for the zone. It's also a reasonably quiet zone, with not a lot of people.

Don't go into the undead room and try to clear it. You'll get adds.

You could also try a dash to Hates Fury, and do the entrance mobs there. You should do the pulling. You will get an occasional double pull. BD drops are fairly common.

It's just another alternative.

Your duo could handle droga pretty well on most of the spots, as well. I just like Nadox, because it was a change of pace, not crowded (which I like) and reasonable loot/xp. It doesn't get mentioned much, but it's not a bad place. It's tough to find higher level undead, and this is one of the places that have them.

Luggald area is tougher, but better xp. They have a ton of hp's and hit hardest in the zone.

Nadox is another two box spot that works well, in my opinion. You won't get rich there, but you can usually play worry free.

Spent a lot of time in Veksar with my 65 shaman and 62 warrior, it is a good place to duo. The shopkeeper spawn is quite easy to break actually. Kill M1 and M2 in shops area, both single pulls. Rebuff / pet up etc, med to ful in room M2 was in. Have shaman target M3 the far western mob in the Shop camp(the closest). Have SK target M4 (the middle mob). Creep up shaman up to point X, so he is in LoS of his mob and cast root. Back up to the NW corner of middle room, where the SK should be waiting. Root almost certain to stick, and only the M4 should come. Have SK get aggro, slow mob, sick pet. Once that mob is locked down by SK, go back to rooted mob and debuff. Keep root parked until first mob dead, then move into room and kill. Then you can pull the PH for rotting shopkeeper, and room is nicely broken. Set up shop at bottom of stairs and pull shopkeeper and blacksmith rooms.
