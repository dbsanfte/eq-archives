I'm the author of EQ Companion.

The way EQ Companion calculates DPS is by dividing total damage done with fight time.

Fight time is calculated separately for each fighter and within each fighter, for each attack type (slash, kick, etc.). It starts from the first time you do damage (or try to do damage but miss) and ends with the last time you do or try to do damage. This works out really well with melee fighters, and really, is the only way to calculate melee DPS accurately. For non-melee it does not work at all, though.

If you only cast one DD, the fight time will be 1 second and hence your DPS will be same as what the DD did.

I have received a lot of comments about this and in the next release I will change it so that if you only do non-melee damage, your fight time will actually be the time the whole group was fighting.
