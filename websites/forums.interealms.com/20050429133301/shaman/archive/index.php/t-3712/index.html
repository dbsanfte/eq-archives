Seredith
Oh for Birus with the mouse being too slow, have you tried speeding it up? Theres probably something in options but I used this:

/mouse 1 (slowest) to /mouse 5 (I think its 5, fastest)

If like me you're anal about getting your windows all lined up then pop the mouse down to /mouse 1 while youre arranging them, then whack the speed back up, much more control!

Another top tip, have a play with /viewport - its great! If you wanted to have your stage stop in the top area of your screen and a black area at the bottom of your screen (where you probably have chat boxes etc) then try something like this:

/viewport 0 0 1152 620

(this is for 1152x864 res, adjust depending on your resolution)

First two numbers are the anchor in the top left of the screen so 0 0 means your 'stage' is anchored firmly in the top left. Second number is how wide, last is how tall.

To put everything back to a fullscreen effect use:

/viewport reset
