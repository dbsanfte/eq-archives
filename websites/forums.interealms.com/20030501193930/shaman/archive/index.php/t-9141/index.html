A couple things to keep in mind about it all - epic quests are just that, epic. They're not something you should be able to finish easily & quickly. I think I started mine around 45ish (did the first step while grouping in OoT, killing seafurbies), and finished it up by about 50 - solo'd Tabian & Glaron at 49 for that part. The other parts are difficult, but could be good goals for your guild to work on - 46-50 is a good range for a raid for about every part of our epic. So use it! Encourage your guild to help you, and get some raids rolling in new lands - City of Mist, Fear, the Hole... all places you need to go, and all places that your guild can use to get some great raiding experience (not to mention, epic parts for other classes). Especially the Hole - taking a mass of people down to Kirn is definately going to be a learning experience, if you guys haven't raided much; expect to die some if so.

I don't think I consider the faction route to be cheating on the quest... the only thing you're doing is cheating yourself & friends out of some good fun.
