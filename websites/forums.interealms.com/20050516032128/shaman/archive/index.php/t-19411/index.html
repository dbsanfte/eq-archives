Jinheim
Rizlona - I forget what she does as a bard, but she's gimp. Once she turns in to a dragon she's basically like a ragefire that has more HP and is easier to slow.

Xuxl - Use FD pullers / bards to pull the swords one or two at a time. They can be rooted, slowed, and mezzed easily. However, they hit extremely hard and flurry. As long as you only have one loose at a time, you are fine. Once they are dead just zerg Xuxl, he's a gimp.

Arylixir - Basically a PoV golem with a lot of HP and a DS. I successfully tanked him once with no trouble.

Dreso - 4 slowable mobs, I think they are mezzable. If not, they are easy to tank mezz. Once they are dead, a slightly less-gimp mob spawns and you kill it. The hardest part of dreso is the clear up there. It isn't really that hard, just extremely boring. There are some mininamed fiends that have an AE that requires RGC to cure, but it isn't a very bad AE.

Jiva - Probably the hardest one, but still easy. A lot of adds will spawn while you are fighting Jiva. Just mezz them or they'll start AEing. If you have several unmezzed mobs running around, the AE's might add up enough to where they are actually a threat. Sort of like a mini-rathe.

These are extremely easy, and any guild that is capable of killing mith marr and all the other prerequisite flags should hvae no trouble with them. GL.
