weissblau
Had an EQ LAN party at my house last weekend and did some normal LDONs with three VP-enabled shaman. Wow, that was a blast!

Typically we didn't worry too much about pulling singles (this was only a normal, after all) and we VP-parked all the adds. Usually we played "mob hockey" and tried to knock them onto each other (or the enchanter). Once every few minutes we'd call DOGPILE! and send 13 AA pets on a mob. Drove the chanter crazy, though, because we'd dot all the parked adds while she tried to mez them.

The warrior would turn from tanking a mob down to grab one of the parked adds, and discover that it was at 10% health due to dots and dogs. All in all if we kept 2-3 parked at all times our kill rate was pretty good - DOT damage stacks great when spread out over multiple simultaneous mobs.

It was a very fun set of runs, and we didn't have any problems finishing on time. If I recall correctly, we did it with

SHM SHM SHM WAR WIZ CLR - ~45 minutes
SHM SHM SHM WAR WAR CLR - ~75 minutes
SHM SHM SHM WAR WAR ENC - ~80 minutes

And we weren't particularly rushed on any of them.
