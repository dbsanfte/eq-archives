Geal Icehammer
You can make a hotkey to stand cast Canni wait for the spell to cast then sit back down. It should look something like this.
/sit off
/cast 3 (what ever slot canni is in)
/pause 27 (2.7 sec pause varies with canni spell and lag)
/sit on

Canni Dancing is timing your casting so that you are sitting during the med tick every 6 seconds. So you wait for the tick then hit your hotkey.
finaljustice
The hotkey is simply a /cast button that you can use tp speed your Canabalizing. You can also put /stand /cast /sit in there and use it to do the mana dance and use the spell gem only if you're already standing.

The mana dance is when you stand, canabalize and sit down before the next tick starts, so you get both the mana from the Cana and the regen from sitting. Watch yer HP's, and when they move, quickly stand, cast and sit back down. Personally, I never did this myself, as I found it too much of a pain to do it quickly and effeciently.
