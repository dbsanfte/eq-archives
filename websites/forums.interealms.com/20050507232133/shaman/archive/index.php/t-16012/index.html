HoT has been 1 groupable for a long time now (2 years+ probably - we've been doing it since later 2001). If you can get lucky around mob resists, it's shaman soloable (I've tried, only once been successful, a few too many deaths :) )

If you're kos, it's a bit tougher, but still easily doable. Best to test the waters while nonkos, makes it easier. If you're kos, the mobs are really weak, you can easily just train everything to the HoT door and die inside, then have the nonkos rez you.

Avoid the door if you're kos, Zemm is very picky about who he lets live. Sometimes you can walk around freely and he'll not bother you, but sometimes he'll come dashing right in when you think you are safe. Pick a side of the ledge and stay there, don't try crossing back and forth.
