To get to fellspine go to western wastes zoneline and follow tunnels till you get to the lake with the well that leads down to the siren area... go down the well and go through the cross shaped tunnel into a larger water area with 2 water type servants above... keep swimming under them into the next tunnel and follow that until you see a coldspine seahorse. When you find that, kill it. That is fellspine's placeholder.

He can and will spawn anywhere from the bottom of the well (3 spawns here... 2 normal mobs and a servant mammal) all the way to the end of that tunnel below the 2 watery/icy servants. The end of that tunnel has 4 exits and is very obvious when you reach the end.

Spawn time is approx 28 mins.

Recommend only camping this if you can solo a coldspine seahorse till fellspine spawns... after that call in some guildies for help.

Fellspine hits for 210s, highly resistant, but has same hitpoints as the other seahorses... about 12k is my guess.

ultra rare drop: fellspine's tail somethin like 7dmg 17dly 9wis 9int rng pal and sk usable and a 300dmg dot in 3 ticks with blind effect... recommended lvl 55 is on that.

So, to reiterate... from the center of the well to the end of the long tunnel opposite the well in the water is fellspines location... when he spawns he moves slightly then stops and never moves again due to broken pathing. So you have to kill him where he spawns even if he spawns next to 2 molkors or sirens. Same with his placeholder... pathing is very annoying here but its still very possible to kill this guy without exploiting.

Seen him twice in SG, bugged in different ways each time i ran across him. First time he just sat there at his spawn point and didn't attack me, but I don't recall landing turgurs at all after malo, but I did get epic on him almost every time IIRC. Second time he was between the well bottom and his spawn point, but he did attack me. That was a fun fight, had to gate a few times cuz of bad tactics(tanking while trying to slow). Finally just did /pet attack and after a 3 minute dance he found Fellspine, and I landed grummus, and swam to a corner and tanked him for a few seconds till dogdog had enough aggro to hold him. Kept casting Kragg's mending on pet when he got to 50%. Very close fight w/ just self buffs. Don't recall first try, but I relanded Grummus on him as it wore off when he was at 50%, and just used epic and dogdog for dmg. Did this at lvl 63 both times and got that crappy mask both times.

In your case, if you have a 60 monk, 48 SK and yourself 62 shm, pretty sure you can win. Keep in mind he was bugged both times i fought him. Grummus be a big help if you have it, otherwise you're stuck w/ plague of insects but I think you can still do it. Tricky part is getting past all the bugged seahorses and other crap to get to him.

As for your location, I have to assume the first time i saw him he was at his spawn point, so he was about 6 npc's deep, and if you don't aggro any see invis I THINK you can find a good spot to set up camp to solo him at.

Remember to have EB items or fish scales before you go, and back yourself into a corner if you are planning to cast, cuz if you start tanking and try to cast you're gonna be pushed all over under water.
