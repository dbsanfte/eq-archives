I was wondering if anyone has ever compiled a list of what is expected of a given class.
This is what I consider the minimum I should expect out of any other class on a standard "grab-some-buddies-and-kill-stuff" group.


Bard - Be able to twist 3 songs. Know when resists are needed. Over-cap haste. Regen between pulls. Immediate crowd control. Slow if no chanter/shaman/beastlord.

Beastlord - Pets and DPS. Keep his own warder under control, healed and buffed as needed. Spiritual Radiance for anyone with a mana bar. Melee when he can but if he has to med a fight or two that's fine. Slow if no shaman/chanter.

Bezerker - we shall see. I'm guessing DPS similar to a ranger; either mele or ranged. Not a tank but can take a hit or two.

Cleric - HP buffs. Heals, res. Ocassional paci for pulls. Stuns for mobs that gate or cheal.

Druid - Damage shields. Snares. Dots and nukes. Cabbage. Be able (and willing) to hold primary healer role. Evacs and ports.

Enchanter - Mez, haste, slow if no shaman, and crack.

Mage - DPS. Damage shields. Pet toys. Arrows.

Monk - DPS. Know how to FD pull. Be able to dump aggro.

Necro - Subversion. Dots. Pets for damage. Mez.

Paladin - Brell's. Stun casters, especially healers and gaters. Hold aggro and tank. Know how to paci-pull. Emergency heals.

Ranger - Predator. DPS either ranged, melee or spells. Be able to identify named mobs in his tracking window. Snare running mobs. Aggro control.

Rogue - DPS. Pick locks. Scout. Drag corpses.

Shadow Knight - Hold aggro. Damage. Snare. At least know how to FD pull.

Shaman - Slow. Focus. Haste if no chanter. Be able to hold primary healer role for short periods.

Warrior - Hold aggro. Know and use disciplines and new melee skills.

Wizard - Nukes including mana management so they have the mana to burn something down when needed. (Nothing like waiting for the big guns to reload because they pissed it all away on yard trash). Evac. Ports.
==============================================

Note that the only debuffing listed is slows. Mages will usually malise (unless they ask the shaman to malise) because it helps thier spells to land. Enchanters will usually tash a hard target if you ask them. Asking them to tash every mob is a little overboard. You might as well ask them to find a higher level shaman.
