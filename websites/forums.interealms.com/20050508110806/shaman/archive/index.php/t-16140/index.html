vivala
You can easily pull the first reaver inside the locked door of the castle without a rogue. Shrink and go bear form, or get on your mount, and shrink, can't remember which. But you will be able to <F8> target the black reaver inside. If your high enough level, only he will come when you cast on him, I did it at 61. Since they are pretty much magic resistant, I put the pet on him and healed pet. Took quite a while to kill. Have pet push it right back against the door. Stay back out of range, once it dies, you or pet won't agro the next reaver or Lord Ghiosk. This will give you time to rebuff and get mana for next pull. Lord Ghiosk is much tuffer than a reaver. I actually made a level 1 ranger and got him outside the locked door and bound him there. He can track all the reavers from there, if he dies, he's bound there, so no matter.

Good Luck

Of course, now you have to deal with a very mad reaver and no pet, but that should be easier for a shaman then a mage.

You can also put your nose on the door and then cast eye of zomm (clickable off stalking probes). It will appear on the other side of the door, and then you can use that to target the reaver. Then send pet, let him aggro, and spam /pet back off. Eventually the reaver will come through the door for you.

Of course, this works even better with summoning mobs, because they will just pull you through the door without having to bother with /pet back off, although fido has to live long enough to get the mob to 98% or whatever it is for them to start summoning.

Be aware that this could also be considered an exploit, although I have never found any hard data one way or the other. <Shrug> If it is, I am not sure what else eye of zomm would be useful for, though, so I am guessing not.
