Give the dusty skull to Granix in east cabilis on the bridge. He'll give it back and tell you to give it to qulin. Once you give to him, he goes through a lil spiel and walks to the water. Spawns an sk skeleton that ht's for 730 and hits for 240's I think. Kill that, give head back to Qulin. He'll give you a note, take it back to Granix. Granix will give you a container, fill that with the 4 glowing skulls, combine, give it and your 6th cudgel back to Granix for the 7th cudgel.
Think that's right, the text points you along what to do fine once you give Granix the dusty skull.
