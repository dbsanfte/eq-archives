Cragael
/sit off
/pause 3, /cast <# of your canni spell slot>
/pause 3, /cast <#>
/pause 14, /cast <#>
/sit on

The first 2 'pause are to deal with fizzles, the last one is to let the Canni cast before trying to sit. Set the pause durations (in 1/10th of seconds) to work with whatever level of lag you get on your client and with whichever of the Canni you're using.
