Update: Leveled to 52 last night. I did it all at the gnome camp in Echo Caverns. It wasn't the best XP but it was steady and headache free. I made 52 in about 9 hours total. Not a record, but not bad I guess.

For Zamrak: The gnomes I speak of are in Echo Caverns, the one half of it that leads to The Deep. The gnomes are located at the first right hand turn you come to. You'll go up the incline and see two guard, farther up is a open area with a camp of casters of to the right. There's also a tinker merchant. When pulling I leave these guys alone, they were mostly light blue to me and not worth the trouble. After the open area is another ramp with two guards. After then you find a room with the kings and their subjects. You can explore all this without worry as they are non KOS to begin with. With SoW up, I pulled the kings to zone and zoned before I started anything. They will stay at zone in so long as no one kills then and they respawn. Once this is done you can pull all of the mobs single, I usually just faught them in the big open area away from the casters (they got the right side, I took the left side). Just as a heads up, if the mobs have been up a while, they will buff each other. That means that mobs around the female caster (Pomela(sp?) is a cleric and will complete heal) are buffed with cleric hp buffs and the ones around the Court Sage (he's an enchanter and will charm/mez you and your pet if your MR isn't high enough) have a damage shield that gave my Poochie fits til I dispelled it. All and all it's a cake walk of a camp. I'd just slow and sick Poochie on them most of the time, I'd melee/dot too if I felt the desire. Before the camp I got Poochie some mage summoned weapons, mask and belt so that might have helped make it easier too. Hope this helps, if you have any questions, please let me know.
