itchyban
I can't comment on how well a Bard would do against the Needlites, but as a mage, they were my happy hunting ground from my mid-20s through, as I recall 30. They are on a predictable clock, can be single pulled (except for splitting the pair in the back, right corner) by working the room clockwise, and give great XP. I duoed with a melee class in there for a bit one day, and we had *lots* of time to trade languages waiting for the next cycle of 6 or 7 Needlites.

Added bonus is that they hit very quickly, but for not much damage. I maxed my Dodge and Defense skills while in there with very little risk to my body.

Highly recommended!

After the Needlites, I hit Marus Seru and then quickly discovered the delights of the DSP Zelniak family (Small, regular, and Large) which was great XP up through 40.
