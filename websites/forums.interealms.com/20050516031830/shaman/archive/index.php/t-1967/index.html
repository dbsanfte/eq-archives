Sorry for the harsh words on the other post.


Some info on sneak.

The skill level is only how reliable it is to turn on and off. What sneak does is when you are not in the 180 degree arc of a monsters view you are always indifferent to it.

Once it is on even with a 50 skill it is always on. (Moving slowly is a good indication.

So you can bank with Kill on site bankers, Use invis to get behind them then sneak and drop invis.

You can be indifferant with almost any Quest giver. (Sometimes doesnt work though on faction based quests)

You can sneak past many See invis critters if you can approach from the rear quarters.

You can perform something called a sneak pull. Basicly you can sometimes split a multiple mob pull by having sneak up and Pulling with something that doesnt break sneak. If the mobs are facing away from you then your aggro radius is much much smaller. So a single can come much more often. (I also used to play Feather Silverthorn, a level 60 thief on E marr that is where I learned to sneakpull.)

Is it a gamechanger? No. But it is something that you can use alot once you figure out how to leverage it to your advantage.
