weissblau
Hats off to one of our guild shaman for naming this technique "Butt-pulling".

Get 2-3 shaman with VP lined up facing away from a badly placed mob, but toward where you WANT the mob placed.

Shaman 1 VP's... then shaman 2... then shaman 3.... voila, well-placed mob.

Helps to warn the tanks.
