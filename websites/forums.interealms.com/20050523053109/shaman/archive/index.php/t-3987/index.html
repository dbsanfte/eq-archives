Wow... 2years ago! You may want to invest in a piercer (Harpoon of the depths comes to mind, should be easy to get, and low cost) If not, then some other low-cost piercer to keep your skill up. Banded is not too bad actually, keeping your ac up is always a good thing.

Soloing is doable, if not tough until 24. As far as spells, the only notable that comes to mind is *Grow* which you will find in Luclin. If you don't have the expansions (particularly Luclin) I suggest you get them if at all possible. The best thing you can do is browse the boards and look for advice, there is a wealth of knowledge here (I am at work, so I can only say so much) Good luck to you, welcome to the Crucible ;-)
