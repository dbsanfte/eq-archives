A couple options...

Echo caverns can still be good experiance at 50 in the Taskmaster Room (both the outer room and where the TM actually spawns). The TM has insane poison and disease resists so no dots, but with dogdog you can still probably solo it. If not, any damage partner should work.

I solo'd through 50 on the gnomes in Echo. After zoning in from SH, the first tunnel on the right leads to the Gnomes of Above and Below. At 50 you can single pull the guards in the front with slow. Buff dogdgo with HoS, chloro and alacrity and he can tank them if you don't feel like it. With dogdog damage all I needed to use was Venom of the Snake, i didn't even need the 49 dots. After the first two, go up and to the left after the first room are 2 more guards near a blanket that hangs from the ceiling. Pull them the same way. Don't pull from inside the backmost room with the kings because you don't always get singles. Polyanna is a cleric and very annoying, the Kings are pretty darn tough and one is unrootable. In the first room around the fire you can single pull the "Guildmaster" (don't remember the rest of the name) from inside the little tent with slow. He is often buffed with a damage shield from the others around the fire tho (I never bothered with them).

I got about 1 blue per cycle of those 5 mobs, they have a 20min spawn iirc. After splitting them up it's not a long wait between spawns. They don't drop items but up to 7pp in change.

One warning if you wanted to do this you only lose faction with those gnomes in Echom but sometimes zoning into Echo the kings are by the zonein. So if you kill these to the point where you become KOS to them, make a habit of zoning into Echo invis. The kings when someone pulls them and then runs to zone, will sit at zone (literally they sit down at the zoneline) for hours. And they hit very hard. I tried to take them together, rooting one (luckily rooted the one who CAN be rooted) but the other was still pretty tough with the 51 slow and dogdog. With canni1 i wasn't able to get mana back fast enough to keep pilling on the dots after they wore off and keep the other rooted.

Some more info on gnomes, this is pretty much what I did at 51.
First off if you have jbb, pretty much single pull the first 2 groupings of guards, you can solo pull these. Slow, dogdog, jbb if you have, if not ebolt, vos, whatever floats your boat, Rinse repeat, then when you get done with the first 2 groupings of guards go under the curtain to where the king's spawns. First pull inside the door is Harold the Herold, conned light blue every time at 51. Then the next pull is Court Jester, cannot be dotted, rogue mob. Then you will see 4 mobs, 2 Royal Guardians, 2 Kings. Above cannot be rooted, Below can. So what I would do is Instill Below, run out into main hallway where I pulled all the rest. Usually Below doesnt come, slow above who hits in upper 120's, dot jbb if you have, and go back and below should aggro, finish him off. By this time you should have respawn on the front guards kill them off and when you go thru the cycle again you can single pull the royal guardians without the kings there. Another solution to getting the kings out of the way is train the to the deep, they sit down when you zone, and they will be out of your way for a day or two. Good aaxp/exp at 51, but most turn light blue at 52 so enjoy while you can.
