Isazo
Binding in or Near the nexus provides a mobilization advantage for contested luclin mobs (I guess RC, Doomshade, Itrear Vius, Rhags).

If one of these mobs pop and your raid force isn't bound in the Nexus, you are at a 8-10 min mobilization disadvantage.

Further, having most of your raid force bound in the nexus allows convienent collection of people by porters. Melee grab a (shardless) TL to bind for 25 Plat, and can be picked up and transported anywhere on Norrath or the Moon.

If they bind in say Thurgadin, they need to run to the spire and then wait for port up. Or get collected at Great divide portal for non-Luclin raids.

For a character in an XP oriented phase the pot room may make sense, for a raid-oriented character its just tough to beat binding on Luclin (and therefore having a spiritstone is extremely handy).
