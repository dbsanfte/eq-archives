At 51, Turgur's is our last slow that saves vs magic. It slows at a max of 75%.

Since there are some mobs which can't be slowed with regular magic based slow (Seru, Vyemm), at level 54, we get Plague of Insects which is a disease based slow. It slows up to 30%. Finally, at level 62, we get Cloud of Grumnus, which slows 40%.

Note, although Tugur's saves vs. magic to land, all the slows are curable via abolish disease.
