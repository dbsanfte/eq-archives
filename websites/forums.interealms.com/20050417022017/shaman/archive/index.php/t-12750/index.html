Might be hard (or at least tideous) to solo in most of the planes before you get Bane of Nife, though I heard of soloing PoI zone-in without much is possible. I personally hate PoI zone-in though.

If you're going to solo or duo in any of the planes, I think most people will agree that you (or the tank) will want to focus on hit points. I think it's wise to focus on HP for any character even before your primary stat, because it's what keeps you alive. Don't sacrifice wisdom completely, but if you have to choose between two pieces of equipment, one with high WIS and no HP and one with 2-4 less WIS but with 25 HP, you'd want to take the latter piece. I have very few pieces of armor with no WIS at all and usually it was to balance another piece that had more than enough (e.g. JBB and Bracelet of Sense). If you come in at around 200 WIS in your 50s, you're probably doing okay.

AC shouldn't be a problem either. Whether you have 900AC or 1100AC at this level, it probably won't make a difference in PoP. Shaman aren't PoP tanks, even with a slowed mob. You'll be looking to avoid damage rather than absorbing it. Though I have tanked a (slowed) nettling wraith or banshee add a few times while the rest of my group killed the first mob. The mobs are going to hit you hard no matter what; Shaman don't have the luxury of high AC items compared to the mobs at the appropriate, respective levels except at the very high end of the game. Though I would say you're probably making some wrong choices if you don't have at least 800AC unbuffed in your mid 50s (and having both Offense and Defense maxed at 200).

My best advice for PoP is to get into a small group (you, 2 tanks and a cleric) and try to arrange so you're the highest level in the group (hard to do in mid 50s, but possible). Avoid groups with casters other than Enchanters, because they'll just sap xp (those freeloading wizards and mages).

I would recommend getting to 56 though, since you do get a very nice DOT upgrade with BoN. Makes getting AA much faster (Darkpaw gnolls in JP last about 40 seconds with BoN, JBB and alpha pet). You also get a haste upgrade with Celerity, which your tanks will love if no enchanter is available.

If you duo, make sure to have both Chloroblast and Superior Healing up and if your tank doesn't mind, Stoicism (get this spell if you don't have it).
