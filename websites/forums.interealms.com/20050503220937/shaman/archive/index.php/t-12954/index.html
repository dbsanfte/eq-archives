Quick question about focus effect regarding DoTs.

Currently, I have ODS, for Mana Preservation and a Extended Afflication item. As I was shopping the bazaar, I noticed a Focus item for Affliction Efficiency. Checking what Affliction Efficiency does, I noticed it reduces the amount of mana for Dots. My question (and Im sure the answer is No) but do Mana Preservation and Affliction Efficiency items, stack?
Landragoran Heronwing
no.
Also spell haste focuses do not stack. For example: You have a Spell Haste IV item and an Affliction Haste III item. You cast Bane of Nife. Which will be used? Answer: the focus with the greater percentage, the Affliction Haste III item will glow or sparkle and the spell casting time will be reduced by 33%.
Loramin
You can still wear and use both items though. The affliction efficiency one (which saves you more mana) will activate on DoTs, and the ODS will activate on everything else.
