Um, your wisdom is already 173...

* Replace neck with one of those new blackened iron medallions from Runnyeye, similar but also has AC, save up for an ivory collar from Chardok

* Upgrade to plat or velium ruby veil, make some long term goals for a bone mask of the jarsath or enshrouded veil

* Head - totemic helm, better ac AND wis

* Bracers - runed mith bracers have more AC and wis, totemic have more AC, sentry bracers are nice (not sure of the stats on rygorr but probably definitely more AC

* Gloves - any gloves but those, please

* Shoulders - lizard scale mantle, tattered mantle (great AC)

* Waist - smited girdle 10 AC 3 to all stats, maybe a strawspun until you can get a smited
