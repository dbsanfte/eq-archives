Gloriana
Just a note: pet resists were changed in the last patch. I am not sure exactly what they did to mage fire pets.

Pets use either their innate resists or the resists of their owners, whichever is better. So, a mage fire pet would still be immune to fire AOE and a shaman pet would benefit from the buffed MR of its owner.
