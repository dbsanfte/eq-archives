kiztent
Don't use FD PLing. You'll just have to spend a boatload of time getting your offense, defense and casting skills up. I have a badly PLed cleric with no AC as an indirect result of this.

Soloing to 37 won't be that bad, and at 37, start using the shaman to buff the SK and have the SK solo while grouped. The shaman will still slow as well. It's good practice for when you start 2 boxing later against real mobs.

Yes, you won't be in the "I made 65 in 3 days" PLing class, but you will have a shaman that is useful and won't fizzle. Just make sure you keep defense maxed.

My shaman, even doing all that, had to spend a few hours working on abjuration. And a little while dueling the SKs pet to get defense maxed.

It's really not worth the time in the long run to PL so fast your skills don't stay up to snuff.
