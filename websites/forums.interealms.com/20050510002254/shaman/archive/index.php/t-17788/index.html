Samanna
Maybe it will help if I repost what I put on on my guild's boards:

1. You earn points in each theme.
2. You have a total current points pool.
3. The game records your 'high water mark', or total ever earned, in each theme.
4. You can spend points anywhere, but you can't spend more on any one item than your total earned in that theme.

Example.

Theme One, you have won 1, earned 51 points.
Theme Two, you have won 2, earned 102 points.
Theme Three, you have won 3, earned 153 points.
Theme Four, you have won 4, earned 204 points.
Them Five, you guessed it, 5 for 255 points.
You never spent any, so you have 765 points.

You want an item that costs 150 points: you could get up to five of them, actually, but not from camp 1 or 2, only from 3, 4 or 5, the camps where you have earned 150+ already.

You want an item that costs 200 points: you could get up to three of them, actually, but only from camp 4 or 5, the camps where you have earned 200+ already.

You want an itme that costs 75 points, but only comes from camp 1: go adventure there some more.

How it works:

Each camp has its own points. You can only buy items at a camp if you've earned points there, UNTIL you get 30 wins in a single camp.

Once you accumulate 30 wins in a single camp, you can use all the points you've earned in any camp to buy items from THAT camp.

So, for example, since our uber armor is in Everfrost, you go and win 30 times in Miragul, and get 1492 points, enough for the sweeeeeeet armor.

Now, since you have 30 wins at Everfrost, you can take the points you've earned getting Bane from Ruj and Spirit of Shrew from Mistmoore, and points gotten from helping your caster friends in Tak and Guk, and use them to buy the other uber armor pieces.

sarelorm
There are items that are Specific to each camps THEME. For those items, you must have earned sufficient points IN THAT CAMP to spend. There are also items which are not specific to a theme (i.e. the shaman REEBO line of spells, etc...) which you can purchase with points earned from any camp. When you click on the merchant and see what he has, notice the SECOND column which denotes the theme (GUK, RUJ, etc..). If there is nothing in that column you can spend any points you have available on that item.
