Rumar
Find a map of PoF, there's some around I don't remember the link anymore, but I've seen it here in Crusible too.

Basically I suggest clear the zonein hill first, then clear to east and to south (all the way to wall) don't advance more to east when puller starts to eat golem DTs. Then move camp to yellow tower in south wall and clear wall and it's vicinity, pull first of the golems (I believe you can pull now eather Dread or Terror) mezzing adds and all focusing on golem kill, pull the other kill, then clear more towards north and pull Fright.

Terror & Fright give tear, Dread & Terror DTs while Fright casts AoE cold if I recall correctly. When ready for golem pull, have someone (like tear receiving shamans :P ) go with puller to agro golem first for DT so puller is alive to make the actual pull. They ain't bad, just need to not waste time in downing them due to DT.
I believe DT's have been report to be about 1min recycle, but they don't DT usually that much.
