Lalaas
Ok, I have to admit I'm still in a bit of shock here. Last night my guild went to CoM, with a few friends from GWM and the Tarew Marr Alliance.

Friday night we participated in a raid to PoA, and it ended nearly before we started. We got wiped on port to isle 2.

So, I wasn't too optimistic about the CoM raid, but I was slightly hopeful. Once we got inside the locked door, there were 6 reavers on track. We took out the first one, close to the door, and the raid leader shouts out the link: "Jade Reaver." I thought, "great! at least one's dropped. Mebbe there's hope, with some of these longer chains I've read about at the Glade."
The group just before us had apparently had a chain of 68 (IIRC) reavers spawn, with 6 JRs.

We killed the other 4-5 that were attainable, not the one that spawns the uber-mob. I died once. None of the reavers we killed chain-spawned.
So, about 3 hours after we ported over there, and about 35 minutes after the last reaver, to my amazement, I was awarded my Jade Reaver!!!!!

"Holy Cr#$%!!!! " I thought. This isn't really happening, is it? Sure enough, I was given the reaver. The group decided not to wait the 4-5 hours for another black reaver spawn, so we left around midnite est. With a druid friend of mine (Thanks, Kiyraah!) I ran around Norrath working on the next sections of my epic.

Now, finally, I'm ready to face VSR. All of a sudden, Swifty, which seemed beyond my reach, is light-years closer!

Thanks to all in BRR, GWM, and the others in TMA that made this truly an amazing night for me.

-Lal.

<edit> By the way, I have to also thank Crydee for his excellent guide, and Sunmoon for the Glade. Also, all of you who post here. I've been in EQ for 3.5 years, and found the Glade 2 years ago. Took until Sept to register & post, though. :)
