Paldaan
With regards to bows, I THINK that ratio is king. There is no 'Main hand damage bonus' which encourages the use of low delay bows similar to low delay weapons in your primary hand. If you are using trueshot the old belief was that you wanted to use the highest damage bow you could while still considering ratio to a certain extent, but I think that pure ratio would be more important than that.

So, what I'd look for in a bow is ratio first, then look at stats. The Bow of Doom is very nice, and there are probably only a few Rangers who have better with new boss-mob level PoP bows that are dropping. That Ssra longbow would be pretty nice also. All are probably better than the Thunderbolt, though that's still a nice bow, and probably better than any other buy-able bow depending on how you play your Ranger. I know a lot of Rangers who equip the Thunderbolt as their regular range item, and one of the higher ratio bows for when they're actually bowing a mob.
