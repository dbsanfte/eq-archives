With such a plethora of incredible talent that has gone through that show, the original characters are still my favorites. Many many funny bits, great characters, but the one that always had me in stitches because I knew a kid so much like her, so much so that her parents dreaded SNL,

Lily Tomlin as Elizabeth Ann

One comedienne, on stage by herself, one large rocking chair, kid style clothes, speaking softly, and getting people to first crane their necks to hear her, then fall on the floor laughing.

Incredible talent.

The entire first cast made such a huge impact on television, almost all of them going on to movies and continued fame. So many top-notch comedians have come from through that show.
