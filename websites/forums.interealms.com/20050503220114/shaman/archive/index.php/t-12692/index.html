Unless you have a procing weapon, skip the Monkey - it isn't doing you any good.

Droping the Cat is probably also a good idea - the couple points of AC you get from it are almost certainly blocking so little damage that the mana would be better spent on heals.

Spirit Strength - only has a minimal effect on your damage output. You can probably skip this one unless you are having weight problems.

I don't see Regeneration in your list. I am hoping that you just neglected to list it. If you really aren't using it - start now. It is is the most mana efficient healing you have available (though it does work slowly of course).


Both fighting styles are perfectly valid. Some situations call for Tank soloing (your a), and some for Root-n-Rot (your b).

Which is best depends on your equipment and what you are fighting.

You should do some tanking each level to insure that your Defense and Dodge skills are maxed out. Beyond that, do whichever you prefer.

Also, never forget that with Cannibalize your HP bar is really just a 2nd mana-bar.


As for your stat points, as long as you didn't dump them into Int or Charisma you are going to get some benefit out of them wherever you put them so don't worry about it.


Equipment : without knowing what you currently have, it is rather hard to make upgrade suggestions.
