Bear form has no faction modifiers at all.

Iksar Pros:
1) Innate Regen, at 65 I have 21 standing regen without equipement or AAs. Self buffed I regen at over 50hp pet tic standing.
2) Best newbie zone in the game
3) Cudgel Quest faction is automatic
4) Infravision
5) Swim at 100 skill at birth
6) Small AC bonus
7) Forage (skill locked at 50 from birth)
8) Best looking shaman of them all

Iksar Cons:
1) 20% experience penalty (same as Troll)
2) Faction (but very minor, only old world cities)
3) Cannot use JBB (nice 20000pp damage toy to help level from 45 to 55)
4) Have to get a friend to buy the odd level 50+ spell from OT or FV for you unless you build a little faction.

If you are going hard-core then Ogre for no frontal stun and a few extra HP. If you are a casual player who plays for fun, go with the race you think will be most fun to play.

Choose the race that you won't get tired of playing after three years.
