Fleury Moistfeet
It's my understanding that the lines of focus effects do not stack. What I did was try to aim for the focus effect which gives the highest %. For example, the Ring of Flamewarding from VT has Focus of Flame as its focus effect, which is a 26% damage increase in fire DD spells. Now, the Intricately Etched Seashell has Fury of Ro as its focus effect, which is a 30% damage increase in fire DD spells. Think of Fury of Ro as an upgrade to Focus of Flame. So, if you were to take a focus effect to increase your fire DD damage, you would opt for the Seashell.
I hope that makes sense, I'm not terribly good at explaining things sometimes ;)

As to cleric/mage focus effects stacking, I'm not certain of the mage focus items but the cleric spell Blessing of Reverence (BOR) certainly does stack with the spell haste line of focus effects.
