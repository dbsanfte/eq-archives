If it's not heavily camped, do the nobles and bards in HHK, at 51 you should get 1% per kill. I did 52 and 53 there in the same day (about 12 hours of playing) at about 0.75% per kill. On my server, they're usually pretty well camped Fri-Sun, but during the week, they seem to be open.

They're also an easy source of AAXP up until level 53 (might be green at 54, haven't been back to check).

I got through 54 on the nobles in HK; about half were blue but it still was excellent exp. You can ride them out till 57, but at 55+ they're mostly light blue and arent' the phenomenal exp they were in the 40's and lower 50's. In the upper 50's you can solo in velks for pretty good exp and loot. I got a few pairs of schw and some silver chitin wristbands over the course of a few levels there. If you don't mind a DL/KC-like atmosphere, with constant bickering and idiocy in ooc and shout, you could try grouping (or soloing :p) in tier 1 planes, although a friend of mine who plays a now 52 mage said that HK is much better exp.
