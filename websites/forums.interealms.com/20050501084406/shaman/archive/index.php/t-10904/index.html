talas213
The amount of experience that one AAXP point requires doesn't vary with level nor does it vary with # of AAXP points acquired or spent.

My guess is that the rumor was started by someone noticing the killing mob X at level 51 gave A% experience per kill at level 51 and killing mob X at level 55 gave something less than A% experience per kill.

The reason isn't that AAXP points got more expensive it's that you receive less experience from killing mob X at level 55 than you did at level 51. That is the close the mob your killing is to your level the more experience you receive per kill. The farther the mob is from your level the less experience you recieve per kill. This is intended to encourage people to kill mobs closer to their level.

Short answer..

1) No, it's false.
2) No, the number of AA's spent doesn't affect the cost of the AAs
3) Yes.
