Korelen
Your best bet is to accumulate some regular experience - at least 20% saved. This should let you take four unrezzed deaths or about that much. Once you have at least 20%, you can split your xp or go 100% AA.

If you tend to raid a lot, I suggest you double or triple that. Currently, I have 60% regular xp saved, and that should last me a bit. I did this because I have been xping less and less because of raids, and I wanted to have a large enough buffer that I would not de-level.

In the end, it depends on your play-style. Experiment, and find an area that is comfortable for you. If you tend to xp a lot without a rezzer or tend to die with a frequency, it would be advantagous for you to have a larger regular pool of xp.
