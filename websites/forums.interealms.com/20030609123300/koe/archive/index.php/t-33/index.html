Armour and Albatross


Laid upon my tender breast,______________ Laid upon my rotting chest,
Guardian of my heart.___________________ Source of my disease.
Free of weakness; love and rest,__________ Free of joy, love, and jest,
No courage to depart.___________________ Decaying heart doth sieze.

Coddled by the shield of gold,_____________Corrupted by the shield of old,
Natures purest ore._____________________ Lost loves last cruel deed.
Deepest love for which I hold,_____________ Deepest love that hath fold,
Can cause us pain no more.______________ While silently I bleed.

Open heart, behind the shield,_____________Wretched heart, cased in stone,
Beats alive and free._____________________Closed off from the world.
Old, cruel wounds that have healed, ________Bitter heart will not atone,
Lessons learned for me.__________________Spear of blame is hurled.
