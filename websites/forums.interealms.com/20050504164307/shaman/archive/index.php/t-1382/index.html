Definitely go with Alteration. There really is no other option, though I suppose if there were a second place it would be Conjuration. Don't worry about sitting there and casting spells over and over to be sure that Alteration is your specialization: simply playing normally should take care of it, as Cannibalize is an Alteration spell and is probably cast at least 10 times as much as your other spells.
