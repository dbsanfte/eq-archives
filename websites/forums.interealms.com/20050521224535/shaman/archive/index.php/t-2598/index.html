Crashem
Shamans used to own pre kunark in pvp. These days we have a heck of a time against uber opponents. Hard to kill a shaman, hard to kill as a shaman.

I am assuming you are 60th:

1) Against non uber opponents, shamans will own them from malo. After malo, you can have your way with your opponents. Keep them perma blinded/rooted/etc.

2) Against uber opponents, you got problems. My main suggestion is to use disease line. Use ID to debuff disease and use the resistable one to see how their DR is. If resistable disease debuff lands, then go to town on them. Also, get some melee going. Works pretty well. Finally, uber cast blind until it lands to give you some time to cast other stuff.

Against casters, torpor > DD. Just play the mana game, and usually they will run out of mana before you :).
