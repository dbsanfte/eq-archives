Tried this again last night. Wasn't able to make it from the north fire pots over to the East platform. Always fell just a bit short. Did a bit of research and found that this is highly dependant on your lag and video card. In order to make the run you need to do the following:


Go into options and turn off all video affects that might add lag
Make sure you have a SOW effect on (best to do outside of zone)
Make sure you have a lev effect on (SoE preferable for both SOW and Lev)
Kill mobs at either north or south wing entrance
Backup to get on top of a firepot so that you can turn and face the east platform
Face 100% up
Time your run to coincide with the "up" part of your lev bounce
If you have a 2nd person, have them stand on the platform with you but between you and the east platform. They will give you a little boost.
Run to the east platform and kill the mobs there
Reset your video preferences to what you prefer


In some cases you will never be able to do this run it seems. Reviewers of the strategy have noted that on one machine that they can always make the run and on a lesser machine they never can. I may fit into that second category unfortunately. :( Guess I'll find out tonight.

For the 2nd night in a row I got overwhelmed when I did a bad pull and ended up leaving my monk's corpse laying there. Definitely helps to have a few necro friends to help with the CR/Rez here.

From my recent experiences I'd say that doing the key quest solo before 65 would be extremely difficult. Even at 65 it would be very touchy.
