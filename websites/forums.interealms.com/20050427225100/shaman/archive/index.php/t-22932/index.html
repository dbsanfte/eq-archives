Butcherblock is decent area to level up - there is a skele-bone quest (NPC is paladin guild end of hall to left NPC.) 4bones per gift gives you decent XP an a trash clothing or something you can use or sell right there. Bird-claw quest (same idea - 4per) is a bit more of a hassle but still good to keep in back of mind - NPC is paladin hall somewhere inside.

newbie runaround area is good till 5 or 6. Then switch to gobby camp outside Kaladim ( go left from Kaladim all way back along wall to gobby camp - they drop heads and blood (3 - 4plat each). Then switch to first gobby camp at about 8th or so - found by following road left outa Kaladim to second guard house then right to gobby camp in valley.

PS - the first camp outside Kaladim spawns some Wizards and Shamans that will own you at 5th. Pull them to the guardhouse and let the guards own them - run back and continue with DB, white an Yellow mobs.

By the time you have reached 7 - 10 almost everything in BB is accessible - EXCEPT Crazed Gobby Camp - behind first guardshack that has a dwarf standing beside a Blacksmith anvil. These gobbys are not for anyone under 20th soloing. They are same class as the Crazed Gobbys deep in High Hold Keep . . . stay clear till your 20+
