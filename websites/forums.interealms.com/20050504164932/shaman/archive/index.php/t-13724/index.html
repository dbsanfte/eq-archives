quick guide
26-29 marus seru, pretty easy kills. all I did with mine is banshee aura (DS) and tank for my pet while leeching. he does have some HP gear but with leech and life taps I could easily finish off a yellow con with above 80%hp

29-34 DSP find a nice quite spot and pull anything.

34-44 = cake. nurga is hands down the best XP for these levels. plus it gets your chardok faction underway.

also you could try keasora at those levels. by the church is loaded with undead so you could solo shaman style. necros get a 70% slow VS undead at 39 plus they get nice ratio nukes to undead.

44-49 plagebone theaves in DLs (hard camp to get)

this is what I plan to do/did with my necro
