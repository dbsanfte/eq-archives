Levels help quite a bit when it comes to resisting regular spells. At 57, you will prolly want to get your resists into the 150's range at a minimum if you are looking to avoid some of abuse those Krup casters are shovelling your way. Some spells that mobs cast have negative modifiers to your resist values so you may need to go pretty far above what seems reasonable to expect any resists.
