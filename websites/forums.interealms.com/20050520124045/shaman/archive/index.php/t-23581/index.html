Tugela
Timer groups are used in discs. Basically it uses a reuse timer to lock out a specific family, but other families can be cast immediately. So, if defensive was in group 1 (I dont recall off hand which it is in), the reuse timer is 10 minutes, in other words you cant use any other discs in group 1 until the timer is reset. You can use the discs in group 2, 3 or 4 though, provided you have endurance. I expect that they are moving spells over to the same system. For example, if you have a spell with a recast time of 30 seconds in group 5, any other spell in group 5 will be locked out until the recast timer is up. You could still cast spells on other timers though. Most spells have a recast time of 2.5 seconds, so it wont affect us too much.

I imagine the reason for doing that is to allow them more flexibility in making diverse spell sets with powerfull effects that might otherwise be unbalancing if they could be chain cast.

One implication if they are actually doing that, is that if you cast a spell from one timer group, you might be immediately able to cast a spell from another timer group without waiting for the recast timer to reset. That could be useful.
