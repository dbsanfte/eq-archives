The bug in archery was actually fourfold.

1) Archery has unlimited range. This allowed some to kill mobs out of aggro range. However, upon experimentation, this was not really true for summoning mobs.

2) You don't have to be facing in the correct direction of the mob. You could be shooting a mob and connecting hits south of you even though you are facing north. It would correct your direction to face the mob only if the angle between the direction you faced and the mob was very small.

3) Player Characters were valid archery targets. You could shoot arrows at PCs, but not hit them of course. This allowed you to hit NPCs at point blank, as you could simply target a PC directly behind it. Alignment wasn't that easy, but it worked nevertheless.

4) When receiving the target is above/below message, you would fire arrows. This made it possible to fire arrows as fast as your client refreshed this message. Many of those arrows would succeed the check in connecting for a hit/miss roll on the NPCs. The higher damage bows and trueshot disc were the best to use for this "bug".

Seems all four of these are fixed with the clean up patch though, and I agree that it helped "blow off some steam" regarding wp/cop and the fact they didn't address stacking issues with WP and BMB and damage shields.
