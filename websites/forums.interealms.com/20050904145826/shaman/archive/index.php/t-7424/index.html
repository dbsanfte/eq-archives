Just as follow up for anyone that is having the same problem.

I think it's related to zone alarm/pest patrol not fully disabling some ad crap.

I got a copy of ad-aware (freeware spyware checker) and that found a few items that pest patrol didn't. Once they were deleted explorer went back to normal. :)
