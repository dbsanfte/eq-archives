Kianor
Here's my thoughts on Nihil from another thread:

I will say this may differ for a shaman without a Time's Antithesis, but this is my (all be it limited) experience of it, from playing with it in the zone where I got it, Vxed, to try to get a feel for it.

We were farming nameds at the zone in for spells. Before I got Balance, I was Malosing the mobs before they got to the group, and slowing slightly before or as the warrior engaged. With Balance, I tried to cast it about the same time as I previously cast Malos. It seemed to be resisted there as often as not, and much more often than Malos/Turgur's.

My first impression is that I don't really like it. It's fast cast, but that wasn't an advantage where I was - where I could Malos a mob before it got to us. If we were in a situation where I couldn't cast on a mob until it was on top of us, the speed would be a bigger advantage, but my feeling was that it was still resisted too much for that to really make a difference.

It's got the -40 resist mod, but even so was resisted a lot where we were, but Malos/Turgur's was resisted hardly at all. I'm sure it depends on the zone, but at that particular spot, Balance was resisted too much for it to be useful over Malos/Turgur's, even though it took longer to cast. I tried Malos/Balance for a while too, but after a Malos, it wasn't really noticeably less resisted than Turgur's. In that zone, the sweet spot for resists seems to be around Malos-strength, so the -40 on Balance wasn't quite enough, but -55 was fine most of the time.

Since it's only 50%, I was also never that happy with leaving it at that either, even with as quickly as mobs were dying there. I know this is a little shaman slow-snobbery, but slowing mobs by 50% seems to me to be a pre-slow, not a real slow, and kinda a waste of having the class with the biggest slow in the game.

With BoR and detrimental spell haste, my Turgur's casts at 2.2, and the difference between this and the 1.5 of Balance is not that much. With a Time's Antithesis and 0 recast, the speed benefit is even smaller.

Duration-wise, the fights were over quickly, so Balance never really had a chance to wear off naturally. I don't see the 1.5 minutes as being a big drawback.

I got my Time's Antithesis recently, so I had Balance in the spell slot Turgur's was in previously. If I hadn't got my TA yet, I would feel uncomfortable about Balance taking up an extra spell slot, or even replacing Turgur's.

This experience is only in one zone, but that zone is fairly typical of the Discord zones I've been in so far, in terms of resists and fight lengths. All in all, it seems to me the resist modifier and the faster cast time isn't enough to make the spell worth using over Turgur's.

I can see me using it on raids for the resist modifier, to get any slow on a hard-to-slow boss mob as soon as possible.

I could also see me using it on mobs that resist a little bit less than the Vxed ones I was up against - there are bound to be places where Balance slows reliably by itself. With the 0 recast of the TA, though, I doubt that Balance will provide a significant overall speed advantage in most situations.

For the types of fights it seems to be aimed at - the hard hitting, short duration Gates of Discord fights, it's disappointing. If it was a slightly bigger slow (65-70%) or had a bigger resist modifier, it might be more useable, but as it stands, its main purpose seems to be that shamans can no longer complain that beastlords have the fastest slows.
