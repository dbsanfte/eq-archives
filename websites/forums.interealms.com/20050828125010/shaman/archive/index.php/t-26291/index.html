Okay, so the crew finally gave BRD a shot last night. I said I'd post my experiences either way so here goes. The night started off inauspiciously as we dragged my rogue through UM and then after ganking Archadeus I forgot to click the @#$!! discs, so yep, I get to do that tedious dungeon yet again. After that fiasco, we headed to BRD with our standard crew:
- 60 rogue
- 53 paladin MT
- 53 paladin healer (me)
- 50 paladin offtank
- 51 shadow priest (pure dps)

Per Mill's advice we decided to try the left side first through the locked shadow door, which we had the rogue pick. I decided to see what kind of damage the tank was going to tank on the first 5 pull of 54 elites, so I held off on healing immediately which I normally do. 5 secs later the tank is raped and we wipe.

After CR, we tried again and on the next pull we were okay but it required perfect coordination as well as pulling our 50 paladin out of the front lines and putting him on heal duty. With him spamming flash heals and me landing big heals we could keep our MT up, but it was iffy and in subsequent pulls a mistarget on a heal wiped us again. It was very reminescent of our first time doing hard LDONs in bazaar gear (not pretty).

After that CR, we decided to head into the main part of the instance where the mobs are much lower, 51-52 elites with smatterings of 49 nonelites. That was only slightly more difficult than ST and we managed to roam around quite a bit before running into trouble. One of our biggest problems was level aggro as we were getting adds from all over the place and 8 mobs in camp was pretty common. Even with 4k AC I couldn't ignore the damage while healtanking the way I could in ST.

We managed to do the ring event in the arena and take out some random named, but we came up pretty empty on loot. It doesn't help that we're stumbling around completely clueless about where to go- any suggestions would be a godsend. We also experimented with having the priest heal but that resulted in a dead priest near instantaneously due to paladin aggro issues.

For the future, our 50 paladin is quitting WoW and we'll be replacing him with a 50 druid from a friend's crew. Once my rogue gets up we'll replace my paladin as well when circumstances permit. It was a rough start, but that's pretty common whenever we hit a new dungeon for the first time. The difficulty level was definitely ratcheted up but I don't think it's anything we won't overcome with practice.

If you have any additional advice I'm all ears. I'd really like to know where all the stinking loot is- we've been salivating over this place for months and were supremely disappointed so far. Would a random green have been too much to ask? Sheesh.

Get everyone to at least 55 and hit it again. Then hit it some more. BRD is great xp and coin, but it's also an instance that takes several trips to complete. I suggest 55 because most of the mobs beyond the Arena are of a level closer to the patrols you encountered on The Dark Iron Highway (that area you found after picking the locked door). Felwood, Burning Steps, Winterspring, and Western Plaguelands all have quests that can get you through the 50-55 stretch.

Kolmirg
If you know exactly where you're going you can do all of BRD in one 5-6 hour marathon session. When I go in with guildies we try to keep it to one to two hour chunks with specific targets & quests in mind. You can complete the Key quest, the Heart of the Mountain quest, and Lord Incendius in about 90 minutes (all close to each other). You can do General, Golem Lord, and Barroom in just under two hours (assuming you have the key). Emperor takes about three hours. Maybe you could do it in 2 if the entire group is 60 and you haul ass skipping any unnessary encounters. Killing the named giant adds 30-45 minutes to any trip because you almost have to clear through 30 elite dwarves and elementals to get back to him. Clearing down to the quest prisoners adds 15-20 minutes. More if you need to kill the lady for the prison key.
