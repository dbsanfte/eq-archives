weissblau
I was soloing in PoN near the graveyard quite easily at 60 with Torpor. My favorite spot - go north from the GY, there's a "fake treant" and then the zone edge veers right, there's a static spawn at the tip (often a bird, sometimes a tree, rarely a spider). Keep going past this point to the next "valley" in the zone wall and set up camp here. There are four static spawns in easy pulling distance, three trees to your north and the one you just passed. Trains run around the outcropping so they pretty much miss you.

Strategy: root the mob, apply Maloxxx, apply Turgur's, apply epic, back off and apply your best disease DoT. Root will break, so run back to camp, take the hits while applying more DoTs, reroot and back off. Either heal yer dog or just back him off when he gets low. I keep Regrowth on him so if I do back him off he recovers super fast.

PoD/PoI are just about as easy.

I have attempted to solo HoH, and while I have made individual kills there I never got into a viable rhythm, mostly due to the fact that it's very hard to pull singles there.

I have soloed successfully in PoS/PoV but it's too much work. Honestly, if I'm soloing I prefer a steady supply of low blues but NO DOWNTIME, even if the XP isn't quite as good. I get bored easily and don't want a 10-minute recovery period after a kill. Someone better at soloing, or someone who likes a 10-15 minute intense hunt followed by 5-10 minutes of downtime, would probably find some spots in PoS/PoV to be quite good.
