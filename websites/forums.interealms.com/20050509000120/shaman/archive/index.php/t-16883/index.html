Unfortunately, I can't answer your question precisely. I think you're on the right track, in wanting to take a minimalist force, if you keep wiping due to foolish errors. Whether two groups of 53-62 can do it, however, is going to depend HEAVILY on whether you're weighted toward the L 62 end of the scale, or L 53. That's a huge difference in capability. If you have a couple tanks, a couple clerics, a chanter or shaman (preferably both), and a couple DPS classes at the top end, you're likely ok.

Hmm... the pulls to break the king room, and just to get into that area, can be tough. The king isn't all that tough, but he's not a slouch. I think two groups of 62's could do it, with your level of gear, with the right classes. If you could pick the classes and take 12 competent L 62's, I think it'd be easy. You could do it with less, but I really am not sure how much less. A good bard/monk duo for pulling could greatly reduce the difficulty. If you don't have good pullers, it'll be much harder. I'd feel much more comfortable with 3 groups, but you may not be improving your chances if the 3rd group is going to be all low level people who have no raid experience and tend to get bored and screw around at bad times.

Prince is easier than King. I think any force that can get the king can do the prince.
