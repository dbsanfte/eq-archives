Waste of $5 as it is right now. At the moment, you access the first zone, which is in roughly 25-27. You can only enter with one or two people, so if you were hoping to explore with a group of friends, you will be disappointed. Click on the tomb, get a quest update. You then proceed to click on tombstones to spawn named D'Morte mobs. Do this a dozen times and then click on the tomb again - recieve a neck.

Travel to Timber falls, get quest update, then run all the way back. And when I say this was an uncreative way to update the zone and the quest, let me stress this, because all it was was a waste of 15minutes of my time.

Zone back into D'Morte, mobs are exactly the same, except they're low level 30s now. Head up to the room with the named (I think it was SW). Kill named, get quest update. Break down wall, kill named inside the tomb that spawns upon examining it, and boom, your neck updates.

That's it. Nothing more to the zone. No other "creative" content.

Loot drops are tagged low-mid level 30, but have low-mid 20's stats on them. Experience wasn't anything to write home about, so that didn't make up for it. Overall, the preorder on the adventure pack was an enormous disappointment. Absolutely no creativity was shown with this zone. So don't expect Nek Castle, which I would say has been Sony's best work up to this level range.

Do yourself a favor and just wait until late March, and even then, I'd wait to see what other people say before wasting $5 on this lackluster adventure pack.
