Grippa
At your level, stat buffs will indeed have a minimal effect on your performance. As you go up levels, the stat buffs get more useful but they are never really make or break.

If you are meleeing a lot, you can't really go wrong with more AC. I'd buff AC and HP and leave it at that. When you get a haste buff, that becomes the most important one for meleeing characters.

Taladaen
You mention applying all your buffs to _your character_ so I'm going to assume you mean while you're soloing. Melee types will swing more often & have about the same or better base stats as you so they will increase their skills faster. If you're short on mana, just HP and AC buff yourself, if you've got mana to burn go for the other stats.

Read our faq about what the stats do & determine if you need them.
