Gloriana
Just curious Frekkels but what do the green dots mean next to your post count

The green dots represent the reputation system. If you hover your mouse over the green dots, a pop up message will appear stating the status of the individual.

If you would like to feedback for a particular post, click the small "talking head" button on the bottom, right hand side of a post. A pop up box will appear and you can either select "approve" or "disapprove." You can also leave comments if you want. The reputation bump is anonymous.

If you would like to check the last 5 reputation bumps you have received, click the User CP button on the top, right hand side of your screen.

You are limited in the amount of reputation you can give in a 24 hour period, I believe it's 10. If you give feedback to a person, you have to give feedback to other people before you can return to the first individual (I think it's 20 people).

The more positive reputation bumps you have, the more you can affect others by bumping. If you have 0 reputation points (or heaven forbid, negative) your bump doesn't affect the other person's status, although they can still see the bump and the comment (if any) in the User CP.

You can also click the talking head for one of your own posts and it will let you know if you have received reputation for that post. Salas, I bumped your post in this thread if you want to check it out.
