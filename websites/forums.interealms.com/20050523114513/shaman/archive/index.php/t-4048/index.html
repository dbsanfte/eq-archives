Yes, I would actually recommend a JBB over HP/MANA gear.

JBB is a consistent, mana-free 25-30dps on most mobs, including resists. That's 150-180 mana-free damage per tick, and it stacks with all your DOTs. By comparison, his best poison DOT at lvl 46 does 10dps (Venom of the Snake).

Even at 49, Envenomed Bolt only 25 dps, but costs 320 mana. That means that even at 49 the JBB is infinity percent more mana efficient. Or to look at it another way, it doubles the damage output of EBolt alone.

You won't see any change this dramatic EVER in your playing life as a shaman. When I solo now, frequently the only spell I cast is Turgur's Insects. I use epic, JBB, and dogdog for the rest. I only EBolt if the creature is particularly difficult or has a huge number of HPs.
