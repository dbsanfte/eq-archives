Greetings Everyone!

The news with regards to these 30 hour (plus) downloads for some of you is found here: Everquest Tech Forum Thread (http://boards.station.sony.com/ubb/everquest/Forum3/HTML/048298.html)

Apparently by checking all the expansions (owned or not) under Advanced Options in your patcher, you will download all the files without the "cap" that existed in the past.

Cancel your patcher and uncheck the boxes if you Do Not wish to download all these files.

On an interesting side note, you can now copy the EverQuest.exe by itself into an empty folder, run the patcher and download ALL the files for all expansions... a nice emergency tool if your disks are ruined or lost.
