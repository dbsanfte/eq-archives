House
You can actually solo pretty easily at the Ry'Gorr Orc Fort in Eastern Wastes. Just go invisible to get inside the compound and park your pet in one of the front corners. If you're careful, you can steadily pull single Centurions and Legionnaires. They drop Orc Fang Necklaces and Earrings. Look out for Elite Guards, as you're too low level to solo them. If some other folks show up, you can group up and pull things to outside the fort, getting loot like Ry'Gorr armor, Spine Chill Spears, and (if you're lucky), a Dark Ember.

House
I wouldn't suggest soloing in Burning Woods, as almost all the MOBs in the zone are wanderers and you will get adds. The best fixed camp in the zone is the Forest Giant Camp, but you need to be in a group to hunt there at your level. Level 51+ Kunark spells, Forest Loops, gems, and assorted stuff drop from the normal giants, Gorgul Paclock drops Treeweave, and Carpenter Grundo drops a staff for a Skyshrine quest (reportedly also Treeweave, though I've never seen that). Gullerback, who drops the Tattered Mantle, also wanders around the fort, and with a good group you can take him down. Basically, the loot in this zone is pretty good, but I've never though the experience was that great (outdoor zone, of course).
