These are based solely on my experiences. Talisman of the Boar being the best and Talisman of the Diaku being the worst. As far as which would I buy or not buy that is really dependent on how much platinum you have. Personally I would not buy anything below Talisman of Alacrity unless it was very, very cheap.

Talisman of the Boar – Very useful on raids and in groups. Single target works for groups though it’s a pain but group is a must for raids.
Tears of Saryrn – Best DPS to use in exp groups. Since pop came on a normal pull I cast malox, slow, then rain 2-3 times.
Blessing of Replenishment – Maybe not as needed in pop but still extremely useful at 25 regen a tick for the group. If a caster grabs agro for a round or two there isn’t much of a need to heal them with replenishment.
Velium Strike-Pretty great nuke (for shaman) even if the cast time is fairly long and is great to use if rains aren’t an option.
Talisman of Alacrity-I usually go SLTW in exp groups just because I tend to feel guilty giving 10% less haste but if we end up with 4 melee in a group I use it. Also fairly effective on smaller raids where there is a chance an enchanter won’t be there.
Breath of Ultor-Very nice for soloing but I tend to group quite a bit more since pop so it falls in the middle to low end of the list for me as its useless in groups.
Malosinia-Pretty nice but not a huge upgrade from malosini in my opinion.
Malicious Decay-Alright but again, not a huge upgrade from insidious fever.
Strength of the Diaku-Cast it on the pet once in a while if I’m partnering or soloing for some more procs. Haven’t done any real tests but it seems to give dogdog a few more procs anyway.
Tiny Terror-This would probably move to number 2 or 3 if it was tgb’able but until then it will be fairly useless.
Talisman of the Diaku-With focus of soul/seventh and primal essence no one really needs any more str/dex. Only time I’ve used it is power leveling or buffing newberts.

Slurm
1. Talisman of Boar - stamina buffs are good, and being able to buff your group (or mgb your raid) in one click is better.

2. Petrifying Earth - really a good root. It lands easily, and holds for the full 3 mins more often than not. Crowd control, or soloing this is a good spell.

3. Breath of Ultor - this plus the 65 dot Blood of Saryn means you can solo again.

4. Vellium Strike - finaly a nuke that isnt a joke. Something to do on raids when they call NUKE!

5. Blessing of Replenishment - 25 per pulse regen really does a lot for a group. It reduces your need to heal by a lot.

6. Talisman of Alacrity - group haste that lasts half an hour. If there is no chanter in your exp group having this spell will make your life much better.

7. Strength of the Daiku - cast on mage pets and watch them proc... a lot.

8. Malicious Decay - If you are trying to land cloud of grummus this is as important as malo before a regular slow..

9. Tiny Terror - its faster to shrink a group at a time than one person at a time, but without tgb its still one of the biggest frustrations I face on a raid.

10. Tears of Saryn - good spell, resisted too often to be useful. Good for adding damage in groups on mobs that die too fast for dots -if it isnt resisted.

11. Talisman of the Daiku - you cant cast the group version on pets, and players dont need this buff.

Imbues are for completing your spellbook. Without the JC aa you arent going to be making your own jewelry and risking a 10-40k+ failure -let the chanter do it.

Swift Like the wind is a good spell, but you can buy it from a vendor... and I'd rather cast talisman of alacrity for double the duration and group effect.
