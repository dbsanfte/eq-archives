Spell Casting Mastery
(SCM)
This ability reduces the amount of mana used when casting spells by 2.5% at rank 1, 5% at rank 2 and 10% at rank 3. Spell Casting Mastery stacks with mana reducing focus effects.

Perelandra
SCM actually has nothing to do with specialization (ignore the description). It provides a flat savings on all spells based the level (2.5%, 5%, and 10%). This makes it a highly desirable AA since it stacks with all other forms of mana preservation - including specialization savings.
