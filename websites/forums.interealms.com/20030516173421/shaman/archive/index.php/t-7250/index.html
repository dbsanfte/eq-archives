But deep inside me I like to play online RPG, and I love being a shaman as my alter ego.

My questions to you: Are there any significant enhancements and / or changes in the shaman class? What do you enjoy and hate the most about the game in general?

Hyacinth
EQ still entertains me. I keep playing because I want to see more of the game, am still finishing up a handful of different quests (and am seeking more), I enjoy the people in my guild & friends I've met in game. When I find myself getting bored or burned out, I take a small break & come right back with a fresh attitude.

This game is so versatile, it satisfies a wide number of folks' interests. Whether you like to roleplay, quest, level, or be uber, this game will make you happy (to an extent).

As far as why I like to play a shaman, we're very important to groups. We're definately sought after on the Planes & any raid force. I love the subtle power of the shaman. We can make or break a fight. We can partner extremely well with just about any class, just in case you have a hard time looking for a group. A number of shaman do very well solo, as well.

As far as significant changes? I've been playing my shaman since 4/01. I haven't seen any significant changes to the class itself.

The last expansion, PoP, was a wonderful addition to the game. Great graphics, new fights, et al. I'm sure you can read a thread or 10 on PoP alone.

My 2cp.
