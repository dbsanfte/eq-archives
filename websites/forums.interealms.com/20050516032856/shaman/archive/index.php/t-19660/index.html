To get some perspective here, notice the post where on the same mobs, a necro parsed his DoT damage. The DoT damage from the necro placed him consistently in the top 10 damage dealers (often number 2).

A shaman chain casting Bane + Blood of Saryrn would rank about 20th in damage. However, the hate generated by that combination would be huge and the shaman would die pretty quickly.
