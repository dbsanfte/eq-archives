Yeah, Mages have some *incredible* things to give your dog.


Things I've learned as a Beastlord (Afaik, all these apply to Shaman pets):

In general, there are two reasons to give weapons to a pet:

1) to activate dual wielding

2) to get whatever proc is on the weapon

Other than that, weapons have very little effect - as stated previously, pets ignore weapon delay these days. Weapons can increase the maximum damage of a pet, but only if the listed damage on the weapon is greater than half the pet's normal maximum damage. (You need some pretty expensive weapons to have that happen, unless you want to use two handed weapons and give up dual wield on your pet.)

Oh, and if you do get proccing Mage weapons - give the dog a dex buff! He'll be spamming that 150DD proc like mad.
