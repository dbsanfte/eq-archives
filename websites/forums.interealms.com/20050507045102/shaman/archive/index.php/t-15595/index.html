weissblau
You have to pause long enough for the spell to finish casting. You are probably getting a message like "cannot perform this action at this time" or something like that.

The /pause increments are approximately 1/10 of a second, so check the cast time of the spell and multiply by 10.

Why are you casting slow in a canni macro?

BTW I use a slightly different macro

/stand
/pause 8, /cast 7
/pause xx, /cast 7
/sit

where xx is long enough to deal with the casting of your cannibalize (varies from Cann I - Cann IV). This means that if I fizzle on the first attempt, or if I just hit the button a bit early, I still get the canni off.

The gotcha here is that the /pause command takes place at the END of the line it's on, even though it appears at the BEGINNING of the line.
