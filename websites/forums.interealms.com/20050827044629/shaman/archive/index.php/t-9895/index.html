greyfish
Gnome guards in echo are great exp solo. 1% a pop and very safe. 1st right as you enter echo on the deep side. 2 guards there. You have to root 1, target the other and sick scooby on him. slow/dot/engage. 2nd guard may unroot before first is down but should be no problem to tank both for a few rounds. Back off 2nd one so it turns on scooby, slow/dot/engage. Moving up brings you to 2 more guards, then past them you can single pull 'harold the herald' with slow. I run back down to where the 1st set spawns and med. I dinged 49 in here the other day and at 49 I can finally single pull the guards with slow. Stay away from the kings and the other npc's, they are casters and will only cause you problems.

Shaman/monk - I did COM with a necro and that's pretty much non stop action. I think this would be a great place for you and your brother to explore. Very quiet and there are 3 or 4 places you can set up, so you should never have problems finding a camp. The stables on the left is good but can be tricky because of adds (also shaman epic piece drops in here ). Good place for monk to work on his FD. Main courtyard area is another spot, then temple area to the right. 4th spot I poked around was to the left and right of the castle entrance. Slime and undead , be careful of adds.

Back to Echo for the shaman/monk; may be the Taskmaster room. If no one is camping outside that room, you'll have extra mobs to pull if you clear the TM room ( note - always leave 1 undead skelly up so that the thought horror doesn't spawn ).

Hope this helps.
