What spell is better at lvl 34 winters roar or shock of the tainted?
Gloriana
Really depends on the resistances of the mobs you're fighting.

Shock of the Tainted:
130 mana
230 damage
3.9 cast time

Winter's Roar
150 mana
263 damage
4 second cast time

The poison nuke casts a tiny bit faster, for a bit less damage for a little less mana. The differences are really negligible. But, if you had a mob that was immune to cold, the poison nuke would be your best bet.
