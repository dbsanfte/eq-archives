Anyhoots, I agree with Wey, go to Fironia Vie. There is a fantastic soloing spot in the NE corner, just past the mini giant fort. It is in the "canyon" that leads to the Swamp of No Hope.
Watch for where the ground changes color as you enter the canyon area.....NO WANDERING MOBS GO PAST THAT POINT. This is critical because you can single pull Drolvargs and Frogluks back there all day, no adds. And there is a zone line to run too if trouble occurs.

Set up your doggie to guard at a spot about 100-200 yards back from where the ground changes color. Give pet and yourself haste, pull with slow and bring Mob back to doggie, when you get there, lay on a DoT and melee the Mob to Death. Have a nuke ready for the runners (or root them). I suggest sticking to the melee types, the shamans are a pain, and I found nuking them the best way to kill them, this will not help with your defense (on the other hand, sometimes you have to kill them to get more warrior types to spawn).


I spent several levels at this spot in my mid-high 30's increasing my defense and offense skills after many levels of grouping (and healing)!


Good luck to you!

Legs: Kromriff Military Leggings would be cake to solo at 53. The leggings are 14AC and 6wis along with some strength and some resists

Neck: A Blackened Alloy Medallion from lowbie dungeon Runnyeye would be better than this - its like 5AC 6wis 6int

Shoulders Personally I would pick Tattered Mantle over the shawl (12AC +disease resist) but you might prefer to camp in Hollowshade at the Sonic Wolf cave to get the Sonic Wolf Cape (or whatever it is called) with 10AC good wis, other stats and resists

Range: Hollowshade again, but this time camp the Grimling Chieftain for his Runed Smudging Stick - AC, Wisdom, and plenty other stuff too

Chest: Blooodstained Tunic blows. Ry'gorr battle mail will be solo-able for you now I think. Personally I would wear the Totemic BP instead of the Bloodstained.

Fingers: Personally, I would splash out on some 6AC 65hp player made Velium Fire-Opal rings. Sell them 7wis rings to a druid. If you want to camp upgrades, try 5th floor of ToFS for the wedding rings that drop in the wedding area

Bracers: If you hang out in Karnors (and lets face it you can solo in Karnors if you want, but its easier to be in a group), you will pick up Jarsarth Bracers which generally rot on the corpse 8AC and 4wis, but I cant remember what else. Hell, I would wear the totemic bracers instead of RMBs

Ears: Forest loops and pixie ribs arent bad, but personally I would get some imbued Ivory Earrings

Head: Pegasus Headress is definately druid material. Either the Totemic or the JB helm are way better IMO.

Your Hammer, Belt and Cloak are good items. The JB gloves and arms are not bad, but you would be considering upgrading them about now if you didn't have so many other slots to upgrade.

Resist Gear. With all the resist gear and jewellery you already have, I would not worry about getting any more resist gear for the time being. To be honest, the 20k that you spent on resist gear would have been better spent on a good BP. I dont know what server you play on, but on some servers you can get a Fungi for that. Alternatively Iskar Ceremonial, Froggy, etc are all relatively cheap. I would also suggest that you get the Shield of Falsehood, which you should be able to solo in a couple of hours
