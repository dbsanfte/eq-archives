Gloriana
SCM - appears to be a flat 10% mana savings and stacks with mana preservation focus items.

SCR/SCRM - one of my favorite AAs, extra ticks to torpor/Q as well as longer lasting buffs make this a good one

ND - need a few more hitpoints to can 5 comfortably?

HG/HA - maxxing out these AAs (especially in addition to an IH item) makes us better healers then I ever thought we could be. Healing crits are just plain fun.

Virulent Paraylsis - disease based root and knockback, up again in 2 minutes. I've found it very useful to de-wall mobs and quickly get mobs off casters (useless in places where mobs have a high DR, like CoD)

RC - get the first level as soon as you can. At two points, it's a cheap buy to quickly cure your entire group of poisons, disease, curses and magical effects

Spirit call - helpful with controlling an add or adding a little extra damage. High fun factor.

MGB - incredibly helpful for a raid shaman.
