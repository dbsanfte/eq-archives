The Obulus Death Shroud has become somewhat synonymous with the focus effect (Mana Preservation III - makes all cast spells cost 10% less mana.) The price of the ODS has skyrocketed since the focus effect was put in, etc. etc. The ODS is recommended as standard high-end gear, etc.

I've been wary about getting one because it would represent a big decline in stats over my IGS. I figured someday I'd take the plunge, but it was really bugging me than even un-uber me was going to give up 10 raw AC and 9 to each resist for this effect.

Well, last night I saw a Crystalline Torque (ac 12, mana preservation III, all/all, drops in Velks) go up in the trade channel. I picked it up for 4k, and I love it. It's still a bit of a stat downgrade (over the shrouded medallion I had before) but not nearly as bad as the IGS swap-out.

Just wondering- why is the ODS the MP3 item most people go for by default? Seems like the Crystalline Torque is going to cost you less stats, and is cheaper to boot.

Thoughts?
