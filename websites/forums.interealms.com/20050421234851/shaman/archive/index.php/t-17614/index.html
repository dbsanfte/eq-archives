mcast12
Zone: Old sebilis

Mob: Myconid Spore King (lvl 58)

Mob Rarity: uncommon (around 1 in 4)

Tunic drop rate: rare (around 1 in 10)

Recommended: you are 65 with at least cani5 and Q. Also, VP3 / SC3 / LR5 makes this very easy, I grab a virtue as it helps with the split.


Getting there...

I assume you have a map and know how to get to lower sebilis. Fight/walk down into tube, either fight or avoid the single wandering krup then swim to area underneath the jails. Get dog buffed etc and park him in the underwater tunnel, then have a look up through the water to see if any wanderers are close. There are 3 mobs roaming the jail room I usually get agro from 2, swim back to dog, slow both, root one and fight other. If you're feeling brave (or have the AAs/HPs) just run into the room and agro any or all of them, I can usually fight all 3 with ease. Once all 3 are dead wait for the 2 wanderers that pass LZO to path near the jails, pull and kill them both. If you are struggling to handle the mobs at this point you are unlikely to be able to split the king camp solo, so just zone out. Okay, so now you're past LZO and are standing just past the door to the fungus caves. You have 3

options:

1. wait for mobs to path by you, swim across the little water area and head to safe area
2. fight you're way via the right fork to king spawn area then head to safe area
3. invis and just run to safe area

The safe/fight/camp out area is in the king room (see attached map).


The pre-split preparation...
Its important to know exactly what mobs path where, there are 3 mobs that pass the king near the water and 2 that pass over the "safe area", although one mob that passes over the safe area has a long pathing route and doesn't appear often. This is why I call it the safe area, you should really only ever get agro from one mob here. This camp is all about patience, wait for the 4/5 pathing mobs to wander by and see if any are adepts or priests. Adepts and priests are your worst enemy and the only thing that makes this camp slightly tough. If you have a healer in the king 3 spawn and one or more wandering healers its going to take longer. If there are no healers in the king 3 it should be easy, simply wait for any wandering healers and kill them one at a time, you must do this before you split the king camp, note: the 3 at king will not agro as long as you don't get too close. If you have healers in king camp move to alternate fight area marked on map and kill all the wanderers leading into king area.


The split...
Okay so now you have killed any healer wanderers and are ready to split. The only thing that makes it hard is the healers, otherwise its just a straight 3 mob fight. To split simply root a reaver or warrior but not a healer in the camp, the other 2 will agro you. Slow all 3, have your pet on the healer and use VP3 (if you have it) on any non damaged and non healer mob. So you are fighting 2/3 mobs now, pet on one you on other, epic and BoU both, if they are healers just wait till they OOM, this can take a while, when they are OOM start using your poison dots to finish them. It is actually very easy to fight all 3 as Q will out heal any damage you are taking. When split just spread the spawn time out a bit, grab a beer and kill any healers that path through king room, leave the warriors and reavers up they are your non healing friends. I always save SC3 for healers as they cannot cast through the interupts from the 6 dogs, dot them up get them to 50% then SC3 and they will be dead.


The king...
Pull with malos, slow, send dog and root in the safe area corner. Remember when a summoning mob is rooted it will not summon you if dog is on him. Load up all your dots on him, then SC3, Q your dog then just wait till it dies. He hits for around 250 max but casts heals a lot, he is utterly trivial at 65 and I have never had any resists after malos.


The loot...
Tunic is rare, I have gotten 2 from maybe 20 kings, never seen the staff, the robe is common (grats destroy). The fungus priests can drop the war only shrink bracer which sells for aroung 15k on my server.


Other notes...
If you want a reet crown wait for a group to pass you, follow them and camp out in the reet caves there are 2 caves with 5 reets next to each other, camp in the 2 spawn room, I got my crown after 3 hours soloing. I did try getting there solo but the juggs have too many HPs to keep up and the 5 jugg split is almost impossible past the one way door.

king/ph spawn time is 27 mins

reavers hit for 157 max, warriors/priests 120ish

any fungus can resist slow up to 3 times without malos, be aware of this when splitting, hence why virtue helps with split

priests will DD you for up to 550 a pop so try to get resists up if you don't have many HPs

I did this camp at 65, with 3700hps self buffed, all spells and 140AA, it is doable with less but won't be as easy

Good luck, I'm off to try something more challenging now, but this was certainly very fun and profitable.
