Only safe spot I know of in barindu, aside of the Qinimi zone, is right behind the pipe for the instanced zones.
Yes, mobs mitigate slow. Yes, they will thrash you if they touch you. Yes, the ones with pets suck. Yes, gaters suck. Yes, the nocs are not snarable (or rootable afaik). No, they do not summon (from what I have seen). Yes, we can solo there.

Been soloing the Qinimi side of Barindu inhopes of getting the ring to drop..no luck thus far. I go, and prefer to have Virtue, Focus, Boar, Wrulan, KEI and a PR buff up always, along with my furry speedbump. I do everything I can to keep a mob from touching me and do a fine job of keeping things permarooted. same as any other non-summoning mob. Can also use Bane if you have the mana regen for it as well.
I'm average geared imo...and handle it fairly easily.

You have to cross the zone, Qinimi to get to Ferubi, Barindu and some other zone that escapes me atm. Qinimi is at the northern end of Natimbi. Lev and invis up and head up the hill in a northerly direction. You will cross a ravine (actually to cliffs) and enter a ruined building. Follow the wall to your right (kind of a zigzag pattern) and you will zone into Qinimi. You can download maps of the GoD zones from eqtoolbox, which will probably be much better than my directions, also. I don't have much information on the mobs though as my machine was having issues there until recently so I haven't been there much yet. Good luck to you. :)

If you have graphics problems or trouble in forest zones, move your clipping way back (to 20-30%) before zoning in to Natimbi. I kept crashing multiple times there, though I was fine in Qinimi, Ferubi, and Barindu.

I would not recommend soloing in Ferubi. Soloing in the zone NEXT to Natimbi would be doable, though not very practical in my opinion. That darn chaos claws proc is a pain the bearbutt.

Corpse Retrieval in many of these zones can be very difficult. I'd recommend a group until you get used to things. The graphics on the new monsters and NPCs are kind of neat, and the building architecture is also quite different than most of the rest of Norrath.
