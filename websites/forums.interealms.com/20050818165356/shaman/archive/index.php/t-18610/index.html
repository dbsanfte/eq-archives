Ronax
Commonly I am the master looter for LDON adventures. Sometimes I am too busy to loot each one the second they die so I will save a few and loot em at once. This is when I noticed the bug. This bug happened to me 2 times before I made the connection.

Bug: If you are stunned from a mob hit, and try to loot a corpse (while stunned) your loot attempt will fail, you will be unable to cast any spells, your hotkeys will be useless as will your clickies. Bug can be fixed by relogging or zoning.

Just a little tid bit, hope someone will find it useful.
