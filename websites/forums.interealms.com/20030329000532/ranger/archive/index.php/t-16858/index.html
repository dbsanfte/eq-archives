I've never seen any other castable DS stack with Warder's protection, whether druid or mage, single target or grouped.

There are many threads on this subject, I'd do a search. I suspect most of us use WP, Nature's Precision, Force of Nature, Thorncoat, Skin Like Nature, and Storm Strength when self buffing. If you use GWF, you lose Force of Nature, so outdoors you do have a choice to make.
Elured
Shield of the Eighth (the rings are very easy to get, except the eighth, which takes a bit of camping)
Thorncoat
Warder's Protection
Nature's Precision
Greater Wolf Form unless faction/zonetype dictates otherwise.

Force of Nature is too freaking short and expensive for me to bother with; I hate Nature's Precision's duration bad enough, and it lasts half an hour.
