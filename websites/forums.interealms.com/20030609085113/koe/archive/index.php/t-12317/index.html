For the last 2 days (got it a little early thanks to some website) I've been listening to Weird Al's Poodle Hat album just released in stores today. I must say it's friggin awesome, there's about 2 tracks or so that IMO kinda suck but the rest are either instant hits or will grow on you (50-60% instant hits and 20-30% will be so so at first and then grow on you). There's a parody on Eminem's song that was the theme for 8 Mile. Also there's Ode to the Superhero to the tune of Ode to the Piano Man which is about Spider Man the Movie. Overall amazingly funny and good listening as a whole. The E-Bay song I think is my fav, well that and the Eminem parody.

P.S. thanks for all the good messages, PM's on Aim/Yahoo/ICQ ets, and emails. I'm feeling FREE, even though it's only been a short time since I logged back on I feel like a new born man, free from the machine, free from the NEED to logon...
