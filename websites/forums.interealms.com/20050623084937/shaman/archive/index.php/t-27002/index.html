Giac
I voted for an improved Canni AA. The reason is simple. Whenever spells are upgraded, the increase in mana is proportional to the increase in its effect.
The ratio for heal spells for the different priest classes for instance, will be constant. We will always pay X mana for Y point of healing and be a set percentage less efficient at doing so than Druids and Clerics, but at the same time, new mana regen buffs, higher FT cap, mana regen click buffs enter the game and reduce the relative mana regen advantage classes like Necromancers and Shamans have over othr classes.

Before FT was introduced, the standing mana regen of a caster was 21(25 with self buffs) and the Shaman was regening about 110-131(canni dance). A Shaman was basically regening mana at a rate of ~524% of a caster. With the introduction of FT items, a FT 15 capped Shaman would "only" regen mana 365% faster than a caster.

These are theoretical figures btw and only valid during downtime, as Shaman mana regen has always been of active nature and susceptible to different circumstances.

Every expansion, Shamans and Necromancers lose some of their advantage and a hastened Canni AA would be a welcome upgrade for the next expansion.
