Yhakfiend
It all depends on what you want to do. I kept my fungi, JBB, and horse through my 50's because I still solo'd some here and there, and they were invaluable tools.

Then, I hit 60, and after getting Malo and Torpor, I decided to dedicate myself to being more of a "defensive specialist" shaman for a while. So I got rid of the JBB, Fungi, and Horse, and focused on grouping for experience.

I don't regret it one bit now, but that's because I've put the solo game on hold for a while. If you plan on soloing through your 50's, keep them around until you upgrade your heals, debuffs, and DoT's.
