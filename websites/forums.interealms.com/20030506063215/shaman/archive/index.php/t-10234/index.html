I hunted Ulthorks quite a bit in my low 40's and with my druid from 31 to 44 (tried to be a considerate quadder, hehe).

Some tips -

The cliff by the Coldain camp to the east is free of roamers, as is the cliff/beach to the west. Pull the ulthork out of its habitat into these areas to avoid any chance of adds. Quadders who know what they are doing will pull into these areas, but they are pretty big and there should be room for 3-5 people.

Ulthorks drop tusks, rarely. Keep them. When you get four sets of tusks, go to Cobalt Scar and find the otter chief. Give him all four sets and get back a gem or sellable item worth, on average, 125-175 plat. You will often see people offering you 10-15-20 plat per set of tusks, don't take them up on it unless you really need the cash right now.

Make room in your bags and reduce your weight. These guys drop lots of Ulthork hide armor and coin loot, as well as Walrus Tooth piercers. Just selling the armor to vendors will net you 3-5pp per piece. You WILL be overburdened quickly and will have to run sell. Thurg is pretty close if you have good Coldain faction, Kael if you have good Giant faction. You'll be making that run every hour or so, so I'd advise binding in Thurg/Kael so you only have to run it one way. Just selling the dropped loot to vendors and pocketing coins netted me 100-150 plat per hour, IN ADDITION TO the loot from the Ulthork tusk quest.
