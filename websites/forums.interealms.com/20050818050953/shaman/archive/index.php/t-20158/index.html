Kelgrael Snowstar
To transfer back to the home server from legends what you need to do is cancel your legends subscription and then send a message to: legendsmoveback@soe.sony.com from the email address the account is currently registered to, with the station name (aka - the account name), the home server, and the names of the characterss to be returned to their home server.

It is not an automatic process, and it can take anywhere from 1 day to two weeks.

Good luck!
