A Couple notes on Truespirit faction (From the research I've done as an SK preparing to try the Epic):

-SKs are on Truespirit Faction, but in the course of the SK Epic, we pretty much go from low apprehensive to high apprehensive, and I believe none of the Shaman Epic mobs will interact with us at any point cuz our faction is so low, so SK doing Truespirit MQ'ing for other classes is just as hard as other classes, to the best of my knowledge.
-At the end of the SK Epic, our Truespirit gets a major hit, usually dropping the SK to Scowl unless they've done some extra faction work.
-One SK I know accidentally killed a Wizard Epic Mob and got scowling to Truespirit. He actually ended up having an Enchanter charm the lesser spirit, got the gem, had the Enchanter charm the Drunk in NFP, handed it in, rinsed and repeated a LOT of times, and eventually got his faction back to Apprehensive.
