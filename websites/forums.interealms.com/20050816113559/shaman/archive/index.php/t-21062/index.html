I notice that potions now have a level, and items with a level restriction on a proc now state the level at which they proc. Have you noticed any other changes not documented in patch?

The damage shield potions have a minimum level above 30, and SOW potions are at 25. Gate potions are at 12.

That level added to the description of potions is the effect level. For some potions like Kilva's, the effect level helps define the amount of damage on the damage shield. For other potions like gate and SoW, it doesn't seem to mean anything. It's NOT a required level - that's just a wording problem.

For some items it makes sense, like the Spear of Fate now shows an effect level of 50 which is when you can start using the effect. For potions, it does not really make sense and they should take the level out of the description.
