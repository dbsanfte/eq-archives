How much benefit does the 3% make, if it isnt that great then it maybe better to just go for a straight out damaging ratio bow like this new one.

e.g. shadows = 38/35 raw ratio but 41/35 if you take the archery mod into account.

I think you are getting skill mods and bane damage mixed up.

If a bow has a +3% archery mod on it, that effects your archery skill value. So if you are 200 in archery, you are effectively 206. This number is used in damage calculation / chance-to-hit rolls.

If a bow has +3 Cold Damage, that value is actually computed in the damage calculations. So if something is a 15/20 weapon with +3 cold, the weapon is (optimally) 18/20.

Elemental Bane damage (i.e. cold, fire) is subject to resist checks. Target bane damage (i.e. Extrapalar, Greater Ahkevan) is not.
