I grabbed this out of an old post by Arasai/miser(sp). Good info and the "room" to pull to is the far end of the tunnel!! :)

I cleared out all the mobs in the room and the hallway leading to the crack in the wall. Then everyone went into the room in the hall and I triggered. Takes FOREVER for the script to play out. After triggering this is what happens exactly:

1) Iksar Apparition turns into Vauris Sathir and sits there for a few mins. (Untargetable)

2) Vauris Sathir runs into the room with the sparklies and despawns, inturn spawning 4 "arisen" and a swirling mass of bones.

3) At this point I just waited and let it play out, I didn't pull the arisen. The swirling mass will despawn(it was untargetable) and Rile Sathir and Venril Sathir will spawn(both untargetable. note that VS looks like a mass of bones in this script) For about ten minutes they goof off and do nothing(perhaps some text?)

4) Venril will kill Rile and become targetable. Have a level 60 pull him to the room, Enc mezzes any Arisen that come with, and you get him slowed ASAP. (If you are 60, pull with malo and slow on inc....it landed first try for me then I landed cripple first try as well)

5) Remember TO KEEP THE ARISEN MEZZED. They respawn immediately after dying as long as the script is in progress. After the fight is under control, get any arisen that didn't come with VS. Get them mezzed.

6) After VS dies, a spirit of Rile will spawn on the stage. Take the drop from VS and your cudgel and give it to him. Room should be safe if you have all the Arisen mezzed. (I personally got respawn in the room, if this happens just do the hand in then hurry back to room.) At this point you can kill all the arisen mobs.

For me, VS dropped the turn in item and the amulet. I kept both just incase it was for a further quest. After VS died, I saw spirit of Rile, but I wasn't in exp group so I couldn't get the trigger item. When the room repopped with normal mobs(the script room) Rile seemed to poof and it freaked me out. After clearing the room again Rile was back and I did the turn in with all the arisen beating on me. (We thought the script was done and I had ****ed it up so we killed them! haha) It's an awesome fight, we had 9-10 level 60's there for it and it wasn't really overkill. Better safe than sorry.
