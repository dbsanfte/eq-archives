I agree about the SK and paladin spells. The problem to me is that they can use very low mana cost low level spells, which they can cast with significant frequency, and not have to med to keep it up, particularly with KEI or better. Paladins can maintain agro using low level stuns that are too low to actually stun the mob - that doesn't seem right to me.

I think the ideal solution would have been to make it so that lower level low mana cost spells have less agro associated with them. Especially that stuns that don't affect a mob shouldn't generate agro. Knights *should* be able to create agro lock, but it should be at a cost of mana - so they can't keep it up 100%. But I also believe it's too late for that change - to change it now would be a nerf to the knights, this is how I feel things should have been in the first place.

So, this is not a call to nerf knights, though I admit it might sound that way. I don't like seeing the nerf of any class, but this is what I see the root cause of the problem.

Giving warriors better agro would also help balance things between knights and warriors, and would not involve nerfing knights. However, I can't see a way to improve warriors without making raids easier too.

Ungerargh
Warriors need some lovin'.

Easy enough to do.

First, increase the melee damage of all three pure melee. How to do such a thing? Either through innate ability, or through AA, allow pure melee to have a higher base damage on their weapons than other classes that can use those weapons. On the monk board, where my post is falling into oblivion (1 reply...200 reads...eh?), I suggested:

1H: pure melee get floor(delay/15) damage added to the weapon.
2H: pure melee get floor(delay/10) damage added to the weapon.

This would only happen for certain types of weapons for each pure melee:

Monks: 1HB, 2HB, HTH (only class that hard caps all three skills, deserves to have all three get improved)

Rogues: Pierce, 1HS (hardly see them use anything else anyway)

Warriors: 2HS, 2HB, 2HP, 1HS (goes in line with the most common weapon type used by warriors - 1HS - and keeps 2H weapons viable for them).

Now, more warrior specific:

Warriors are already on a better mitigation table than knights. Improve this further.

Taunt needs an upgrade. Ideally, this would be something only of use for an xp group, so it would not unbalance raid encounters. Have an additional taunt, or have the upgrade to original taunt, only function when mobs are <= the warrior's level.

So, more damage, better mitigation, and improved taunt. Sounds like a winner to me, and allows warriors to reclaim their rightful position as MA without reducing the power of knights (except relatively). Right now, a warrior tank in an xp group is usually a liability - this should not be the case.
