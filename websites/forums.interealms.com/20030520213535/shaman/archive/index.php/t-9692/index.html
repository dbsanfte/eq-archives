You can argue which is better till the end of time.
With a little Magelo/EQmaps research, this is what I found.

You may be suprised, what I did was equipted a cloth shirt (4ac) and calculated my base HP/Mana AC and resists.

Here are the results (of course this may vary with your total STA/WIS/AGI)

Chestplate of Vindication:
HP's (boosts) +114
Mana (boosts) +0
AC (boosts) +64
Resists (boosts) +15 to all
Effect: +2 hp a tick for a long duration and 10attack

Wolf Caller's Breastplate:
HP's (boosts) +64
Mana (boosts) +265
AC (boosts) +62
Resists (boosts) +4 to all
Effect: +50 to stamina (mana free)
(I didn't factor in the hp bonus for this, because a shaman can cast it while wearing Vindi BP)

Purdy interesting stuff.

I personally wouldn't mind having the Vindi BP for it's looks, and Aura of Battle is nice... but I'm pretty content with the Wolf Caller's after my research.
