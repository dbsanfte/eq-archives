Faern,

If no one that you don't trust has physical access to your PC another thing you can do is to add a firewall. What some people dont realise a firewall will also alert you to software that is trying to contact the internet from your PC. So if you have got a keylogger installed that your virus checker has missed, then the firewall will alert you.

I use ZonealarmPro for my home and work PC. You can get a free cut down version from Zone Labs (http://www.zonelabs.com/store/content/company/products/znalm/freeDownload.jsp?lid=zadb_zadown)
