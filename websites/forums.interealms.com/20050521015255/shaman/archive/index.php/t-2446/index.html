Aye, for skills with level progressions, the number of AA points is as follows:

Achetype Abilites: Level 1 is 1 points
Level 2 is 2 points
Level 3 is 3 points

Priest Abilites: Level 1 is 2 points
Level 2 is 4 points
Level 3 is 6 points

Class Abilites: Level 1 is 3 points
Level 2 is 6 points
Level 3 is 9 points

You need 6 points in Archetype to put any in Priest. You need 12 in priest to put any in Class abilities. The minimum to get the Cannabalization Skill is 23 AA points. 6 in any combination of Archetype skills, 12 in Mental Clarity under the Priest skills (mental clarity 3 is a prerequisite to canna), and then 5 to actually get canna 5.

It's a long trip! :)
