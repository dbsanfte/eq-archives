Zorshaaka
Kurns (8-30) is one of my best memories in the early stages of the game. Helped by the fact I had made my first EQ buddy, sadly long gone. The fun of pushing further into the zone was excellent. As previously suggested try to find a Warr of slightly higher lvl than you, well twinked, and explore. As you say fun is the key here so enjoy.
With that in mind if you can find a good monk to group with there is always Dalnir (25-35). Great xp, and some loot for a change. Monks love Dalnir as there are some very nice drops for them there. Beware though as it is a tough zone if you are not of the appropriate lvl, and you really need a good group in there to do the depths, depending on lvl of course.
Then there is Kaesora (30-45), the bane of my young life. I was desperate to get in as there are some good Shaman drops for a change. I lost count of the number of times I popped in to see if there were any groups inside. Nada, zilch, zero, you get the picture. Very annoying, till I got a guild group together to explore inside. Well worth it, but you will need a group so join a guild if possible.
Then of course there is City of Mist (35-53). A very good place for all Shaman to hunt as we can gather some of our epic drops along the way. A little easier to find groups here too.
Now I have to admit to never having been in Nurga (30-45) or Droga (35-45). Something I am going to put right next time I log thinking about it. You will not be able to spend a lot of time in these places if your experiences are anything like mine. Very hard to find groups as most people prefer the usual treadmill. Occaisionaly you can gather a group from OT to go to Dalnir, but this is often not worth the effort as people drop out and leave you short handed, once again find a good low lvl guild.
Ahh, that reminds me, guilds. My first guild was a low lvl Iksar only guild that worked quite well for this kind of thing. Unfortunately I joined way too late to enjoy the benefits early on but I would recomend not looking for a powerful guild as it can be tough to get a group to do the stuff you want. Look for a low lvl, well managed, fun group and enjoy.
Well that is my 2cp worth, hope it helps. I love going into dungeons in EQ, I only wish my sentiments were shared by more. Unfortunately most people just want to lvl, rather than exploring the game environment. Somehing of a shame I reckon cause Kaesora and Dalnir are really very enjoyable, and testing environments. How many people have been down to fight the Kly on a regular basis? not many I reckon.
