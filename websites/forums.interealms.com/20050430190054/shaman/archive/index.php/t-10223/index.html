Grizlor
Mage/Shaman is one of the most powerful duos in the game simply because the classes compliment each other to remove weaknesses.

The shaman covers the defensive side of the fight, being able to slow, buff up the pet, keep everyone alive, and otherwise control the situation better (plus pets don't bitch when you torpor them :D)

The mage gets the killing done, with efficient nukes, and a decent tank that goes between the 2 casters and the mob, which coincidentally puts out some decent damage on its own. Summoned Focus items add a little versatility.

Mage/Shaman is one of my favorite duos, but I'm also partial to Necro/Shaman or Bard/Shaman, which give a lot more flexibility at the expense of sheer destructive power, or tanking ability.

Thurdar
Mage/Shaman lacks a lot of utility. You don't have FD, lull or harmony to split camps and you also miss out on Snare and Fear. Earth Pets are also annoying, once a mob is rooted he always seems to beat on the shaman pet.

On the other hand, you're combining the best defensive with the best offensive class in the game. It can't help but work well. As long as you don't mind sticking to places where you can pull mainly singles the sheer killing power of a mage combined with shaman slow and crowd control seems to work well all the way into the 60s.
Stubble
