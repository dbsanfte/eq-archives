shamuofxev
I'd try the gnolls in the large cave in JPF. Much lower HP then PoP or Luclin mobs, easy to slow, single pulls. Large zone too, so if you have you can group him with another level appropriate toon at the zone in and get the exp bonus. Just slow, and have him chop away.
