MadWitchdoctor
Oddly enough i was once in th same position you are in, having a difficult time finding a spot to solo for exp much less cash as it seems all of the usual shaman soloing spots for lvl 55+ are perma camped not just by shaman anymore...

So i did a little exploration as often i am prone to do when my lizard brain flatlines from boredom.. heres what i did, though the method may vary depending on your race if your KOS or not the result is the same..

As an iksar i am KOS but i had heard much talk about Jagged pines.. so i went to see it for myself while helping a guild cleric with their final badge of courage..

Get kei first then go to qeynos hills. Invis yourself in qeynos hills, and run through sure fall glade and zone into jagged pines.. once inside jagged pines head north east from town until you see a cave opening with gnolls.. keep an eye out for defiler and oracle gnolls.. they can see through invis but most are warriors and can't..

Follow the the tunneling leading into the caverns until you hit the first intersection.. go right and follow that until you come to a second intersection.. go right once again and keep following that tunel until you fall down a pit with an illusionary floor... don't worry at your lvl and guessing you have at least decent armor for your lvl you will survive the fall with minimal damage..

at the bottom of the pit you will see that your inside of a tunnel.. and should see at least 1 gnoll in the tunnel.. as long as you don't move towards him you will be out of agro range giving you time to drop invis, buff up, including regen and summon Dyno Mutt.. buff dyno mutt up and regen /haste him as well and med back to full or canni up depending on your masochistic you are.. once your full mana and hp's again your ready to begin your long hunt..

Pull the gnoll with slow, root him and back away while letting dyno mutt eat his lunch, stack a Dot like envenomed bolt on top of him and just keep the gnoll rooted, slowed and doted until he dies.. this is just your break into to the main cavern.. once your past this gnoll you can move in to an area that slopes up.. here there are 3 gnolls evenly spaced apart..
these will be your night long victims..

All of the gnolls have short agro range, so you don't need to worry about anything as long as you keep them rooted during combat and especially if they try to run.. at lvl 55 most are Dark blue cons until you hit 57... Most of them should be warriors and they hit very hard in hth melee.. defiliers (necro's) use standard necro based spells and often fear your pet so having summon companion up during a fight helps.. oracles being gnoll shaman mostly use scourge or malaise so it's best to take down defilers and oracles first.. killing the gnolls also gives you positive faction with with the jagged tree folk so eventually after hundreds of kills you'll be able to bank in town if your initally KOS..

repeat the killing cycle of these 3 gnolls (very bottom near tunnel entrance, side of hill, very top of hill) using the same method as the first gnoll and you will be lvl 57 in no time.. the gnolls are on about a 4 or 5 min spawn timer so you won't be bored very often and gives you plenty of time to recover mana..

they typically drop about 4 - 6 plat each as well as crude weapons that sell for about 7 plat a pop and occasionally drop magic items and sabretooth forged armor.. the armor drops sell for around 70 plat a shot..

You don't need an epic, Jbb or horse for any of this.. i didn't have any when did it.. Exp is fast and very good not to mention the loot is excellent..

once you hit 56 and get bane of nife it becomes even easier as the mobs die faster..
