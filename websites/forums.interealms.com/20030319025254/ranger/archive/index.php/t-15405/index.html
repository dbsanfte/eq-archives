Domandred
There is a quest and it is easier then getting the thing to drop. Don't let these guys fool you.

Here is the quest. Round up 4 to 8K (varies depending on server), go to the bazaar and search for Tolan's Bracer. Now the hard part. Once you find the name of the Player-NPC selling it you have to find your way to him or her in the maze of people in the bazaar. Don't worry this is only about as difficult as trying to find help during the christmas rush at your local department store. Once you find him browze his wares till you see Tolan's Bracer. Click on it and hit buy. You now have Tolan's Bracer much easier then camping for it and time frame wise it is a much better deal this way.
