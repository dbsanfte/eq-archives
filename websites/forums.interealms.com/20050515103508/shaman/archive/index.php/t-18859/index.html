Helfdane
Lodizal spawns on a small island/chunk of iceburg out in front of ToFS in Iceclad; there are several minature walrus running on the chunk that get wacked by the gnolls there from time to time, so its not hard to miss it.
As to spawn times, me and a paladin friend camped him this time last year for approx 1 week, netting him 7/15ish spawns (rest were KS fiascos).
Lodi spawns every 11hrs +/1 1 hr, my friend and I were there at the 10 hr mark every time and it happened exactly on that 10-12 hr time period, usually closer to 12 hrs; he spawns directly on the island and will aggro the closest sitting PC. Once Lodi spawns and isnt aggroed (or on a failed kill) he wanders towards the gnome pirate island, and despawns approx 5 min later, and respawns around 5-10 min later and heads back up the coast, he then despawns if not aggroed and waits till the next scheduled spawn time.
Sure a 65 shaman can solo him, hes slowable, if a tad bit resistant to magic. He(she?) is a level 60 spawn and summons, turns to run at 22ish % and enrages (iirc on the enrage, I seem to remember him rampaging too).
Again, yeah a 65 shaman with Q and alpha pet can solo him easy, stuff in Tier 2+ hit harder, faster and have more hit points and the typical LDoN mob is as tough; the main problem with Lodi is KSing. On most servers KSing him is a way of life, and some guilds perma camp him for EB items, shields for alts, Belts for melees, etc.
If you really want the shield, bring a rogue and a wizard/mage and let the shaman tank w/ Incapacitate for aggro purposes; just have him keep Q up on himself (or torpor, both work) and spot heal with Tnarg; add a KEI and a Virtue and its trivial.
He always drops one shell piece (sometimes up to 2), and the map piece for the Eyepatch of Plunder quest; I have seen 2 Belts and 2 Shields from the same spawn, along with varied level 60 Velious era spells (PoTG, Aegolisim and Focus to name a few).
Hope this helps.
