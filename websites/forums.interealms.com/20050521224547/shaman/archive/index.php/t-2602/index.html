Grippa
One of the more useful hotkeys you can make is one that just does "/assist".

When I'm about to pull a mob, and I've seen other players in the area, I target the mob and hit my assist button - if the mob has a target, it's already been pulled so I leave it alone.

That hot key is also great for finding out who needs healing in raids etc, if they are not in your group.
