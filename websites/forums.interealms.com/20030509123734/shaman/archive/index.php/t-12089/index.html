Sorry that i dont have any pretty maps to show you or anything but if you are a little familiar with the zone you should be able to follow my directions.

After you zone in you head to the wall and start to go towards the hobgoblins. If you dont know the hobgoblin camp maybe your familiar with how to go to the docks camp?
Anyway head towards the hobgoblins and cross the river after crossing the river stay with the wall on your left. Head towards the hedge, pass the hedge (keep wall on the left) be careful of some guards at a door a little ways passed the hedge. Just keep the wall on your left and you will eventually come to the city. Sitting on top of the fences at the city are the gargoyles.
