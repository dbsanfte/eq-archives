I'm not sure how far they're willing to go with this melee revamp, at this stage in the game.

I'm not sure if any of you played "EverQuest Online Adventures" (EQOA) which is the PlayStation 2 version of EQ. It's got some simliar stuff, and some vastly different stuff.

Every class has "power" which is basically like mana. Casters get spells... melee's get skills. All use power.

So for a warrior, they have a skill called "Taunt" which is just like a spell... uses power... hopefully taunts. Later they get a more expensive (in power cost) upgrade called Taunt 2. Etc...

So you actually has some tactics in choosing which skills to use. Just like casters have to decide what spells.

Do you use that new, powerful nuke (or taunt, kick, etc...) which burns the power (mana), or do you use a combination of cheaper, but less effective abilities?

I'm not saying SoE is going to go that far. It would be a radicle change for melees.

For example... if a warrior wants to unload that nice crit disc... they can... several times... or whatever.

Anyone who's ever told you that being out of yellow with 100+ sta affects their DPS is full of ****. They never have a parse to prove it; it's always observation. I used to know a pally who swore that being out of yellow made him miss more; there are all kinds of takes on this.

The fact, proven by many parses, is this: if you're under 100 sta, being out of yellow means your melee stats (str included) go down. If you're at 100 sta or above, your stats remain unchanged. The only adverse effect to being out of yellow at this point is not being able to jump.
