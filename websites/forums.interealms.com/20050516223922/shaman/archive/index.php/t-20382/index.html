Thunderwolfjr
Hi All,

I'm a very casual player, taken me over a year to get to level 57 with my shaman main (although I also have several alts I play for fun). Can testify that the shaman is still a great class for the slow and steady player. I can get groups when I want, can start my own if I need to, I solo just fine, and with the new LDoN high dps dots, I can kill stuff just fine (and not break root). I love having max stats self-buffed, being able to heal myself (got to do the stoicism quest), having a pet, JBB for free dd, canni, haste, slow, regen, sow, invis, etc., etc.

True, a necro or druid can solo even better than a shaman, but being a necro is a lonely life and best suited if you plan to solo 99% of the time. But you can't deny that a necro has a lot of great tools, and rarely dies (FD, pet, etc.). A druid is not as bad, but I can get a group in 1/10th the time it takes my druid buddy. Many times I'll get an invite when not even LFG. Just make some friends and play well, and folks will seek you out. Also, I can do my job and not worry about folding like a house of cards as soon as I get hit, I like being able to take a punch.

Bottom line, lots of fun. Some of the 300+ aa time-enabled shaman on this board have issues they would like addressed. Fortunately, that will never happen to me, I will never see the Plane of Time. I'll just have to settle for having fun.
