Viscerator
Chuk has it right.
You just move the "tick" mark to the point in time where you cast or did your particular action. The timer loops around, and when it approaches your tick, its time to redo.
I set one up for:
6mins - turgurs.
13mins - malo/cripple (figured they both cast at around the same time)
1.8mins - disease dots
3mins - canni5 and I let it double as a PoI timer
1hr - general use timer - it breaks down to 10mins per major mark.
