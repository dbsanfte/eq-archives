Valsuvious
Really, it's not just your fault. Not sure by reading your text, but for people raiding with the guild for the first time, it should be told to everyone to make sure you hail the projection to get your flag. I'm not talking about going through each person to make sure they hailed, I'm talking about the raid leader or officer or whomever says in raid chat to "make sure you have hailed the projection". Especially if you have new recruits on their first raids.

Oh, since you haven't really ever raided before, and didn't know much about the projection, here is some more advice. It prob doesn't matter much depending on how far your guild has actually progressed, however, ONLY HAIL THE PROJECTION ONCE. Make sure before you do it, you have it targeted, and you are close and hail it once. I say this because every time that it's hailed, it has a set number of flags it gives out, not based on players, but based on hails. So, if it was supposed to give out 36 flags and you hailed it 10 times, then it would only give out a total of 26 other flags for everyone else. This might not seem like a big deal right now, but whe you get into places where it only gives out a small number of flags compared to how many people you have, then it will be a big deal. Also, sometimes you get yellow text with actual character flags when you hail. Sometimes you get white text and what might not seem like a flag, but you did get one. I suggest that you get close enough to hail, hail the person, then back off a sec and see if they said anything to you. Every flagging mob will say something to you once you hail it if you are close enough. If you get some form of text, then you got the flag, no matter the color. This is pretty evident when you do things like Hall of Honor trials where the text they give you is white, not the normal yellow color.
