Fletching is going to suck, no matter which route you choose for skilling up. The rate of skill increases was recently decreased on fletching (and pottery) and experiences like yours seem to be the rule rather than the exception these days. Just try and keep the stat you use as high as possible when you fletch (one of DEX/INT/WIS), there isn't anything else you can do to influence the rate of skill increases apart from that.
