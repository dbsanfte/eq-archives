warmag
Quick cash. Two ways I did that at that level. The XP way and the non-XP way. XP way is solo seafuries in the ocean of tears. Non-XP way is kind of silly, but it works (if you're a barb shaman). Bind at Halas bank. Go into Permafrost with pet and kill everything between all the camps excluding King's room. (Preacher, Archaeologist, Alchemist, Jailer, and one other I forget the name of that drops the Wolf Fur Slippers). Kill a path as you go because the greenies will probably aggro you in spots. Strength & rage buff yourself for the strength bonus and loot up only the "good" stuff (chain armor, high quality ore, etc.) especially permafrost crystals because those seem to sell really good in the bazaar to smiths and potters (10pp-50pp each, depends on market). Clean the place out if someone's not camping it, and gate back to Halas, sell, bank, run back and repeat. Kind of slow and boring and no XP at all but I made about 1K per day doing this at level 51 and in 4 days was able to purchase a fishbone earring and start soloing in Kedge Keep.
