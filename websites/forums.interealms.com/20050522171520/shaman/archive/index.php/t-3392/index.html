Rathelon
I got a runed totem staff from the gnoll camp in Lake Rathetear at level 12. Took me 3 days of playing and dying repeatedly because I was too low level to be there, but finally a couple level 18 or so folks helped me kill a totem staff carrying gnoll.

I used that weapon until 35th level when I killed what's-his-face (cant even remember it's name now), the evil eye in RunnyEye for the lens and got a Staff of the Observers. That's about the same time I finally finished the 'new' totemic armor quests and got to retire the last of my banded armor. Though, I was still wearing a runed circlet because I didnt like the barbarian horned helmets, and the totemic helm quest was a pain in the ass.

I was still using the Staff of the Observers months after I hit 50th, when Kunark came out. I had upgraded most of my totemic to Rune Etched by then, however. Shortly after Kunark came out, I received a spitestone shield on a Hate raid, and a friend gave me a howling harpoon so I retired the 2h blunt and learned piercing. Then came epic quests, and I was glad I had switched to piercing because I was almost maxxed by then.

By the time Velious came out, I was weilding epic and wearing full rune etched + spitestone shield. That's pretty much how I was equipped up until level 58 or 59 when I got my first piece of Wolf Callers.

When I hit level 60, I was using pretty much what is on my magelo profile now, except I only had Wolf Caller arms and the rest was RE. I had an AC 6/65 hp ring instead of the shadel bandit ring, and I think I was still using the howling harpoon in range slot instead of the talisman of benevolence.

EDIT: Oh, I just looked at my profile and remembered...I wore a totemic cloak until well after Velious came out. I finally upgraded it one day when we found an ancient seahorse hide cloak in Siren's Grotto. Then I did the cloak of truth quest when Luclin came out.
