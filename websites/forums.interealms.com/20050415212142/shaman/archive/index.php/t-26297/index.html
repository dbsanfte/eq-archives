Maybe we should forget feeding SOE class balancing information, and make up a new top 10 list that can be accomplished with relative ease.

Take our current Top 10 list:

1. Fix Shaman healing! We need larger, more efficient single target blast heals to deal with GoD mobs as well as group heals (perhaps group HoT is more in line with our class) to help in raid situations (MGB Option would be very helpful). [currently being evaluated]
2. Improve shaman DPS and burst DPS. [currently being evaluated]
3. AE Malos or lose the insane recast delay on Malos. [new item]
4. Make FA less of a hassle, 25 minute duration, 5 second recast. [new item]
5. An upgrade or addition to Cannibalization, allowing (at the same ratio), greater or more frequent conversions of HP to Mana. [currently being evaluated]
6. Add direct combat effects to all of our level 60+ stat buffs. [new item]
7. Reduce DoT aggro! [new item]
8. Endurance regen buff. [currently being evaluated]
9. Reduce the cost of alchemy components. [currently being evaluated]
10. MGB Shrink. [currently being evaluated]


As a coder for SOE, I"d look at that and just say.. "Hey boss, I'm taking next week off".

Thats why we need a new top 10 list. A list thats easy to do, and will have much greater rewards than silly things like healing or dps.

My proposal:

1. New chain graphics.
I'm tired of seeing the same chain graphic from level 1 - 70. I've gone thru 10 tunics I bet, and not one has looked different. I get way less cyborz than other players, and its crushing my ego.
2. Barking Dogs.
I for one, am fed up with howling. And why wolfs all the time while we're at it? Can't my spirit call summon forth a random assortment of barking dogs? Couple collies, a few chihuahua, and maybe a pug or two?
3. Upgrade to Slow.
Ok, not your typical upgrade. But if they intend to keep mitigating (pun intended) our primary ability, at least make it entertaining. I want bigger flies damnit. Real nasty horsefllies. And the mob had better swat and swing his fists around between blows. I mean. are those flies doing something or not?
4. FA and Panther Cast Animations.
Ok, these just are the lamest things ever. Have you seen the troll boogie? Waving my arms in the air and squatting like I'm dropping a big... you know what. How about some chanting. Maybe bounce around for a bit like a rain dance. Anything remotely 'native' looking.
5. Chance to crit buff.
We are THE buffing class right? Might as well crit them. 3% chance for Panther to do 1100hp per proc sound good? You betcha. Chance for Acumen to have xray vision, seeing thru a wall or two, uber I tell ya. Chance for levitate to actually let you FLY. Oh yeah.
6. The Troll Run (personal one due to class).
Have you seen my run? I'm the slightly retarded looking fellow wiggling around like a worm when I run. I'm just an oversized human. No need for me to look like I have a broomstick up my you know what.
7. Bring on the Rain.
Druids stop the rain. Shaman make it rain. Seems fair to me. A little dancing. A little chanting. And booom. Lighting and Thunder. Of course, shaman rain brings an increased chance to forage due to the healthy nature of the spirit water (oh, and give us all forage grrr).
8. Head Shrink.
Ok.. so obvious. We absolutely have to have the ability to shrink people's heads. Maybe it grants a slight decrease to being stunned as a bonus:)
9. War Paint.
Ok, between alchemy, and our brothers in the north's knowledge of tatooing, we should be able to make a fairly durable paint. Its just an insult to the Native Shaman everywhere that we can't wear any ceremonial paints.
10. Talismans
Talisman of fate. Talisman of.. talisman talisman shalisman. If this is such a big part of shaman heritage, why can't we make them? Like a headress of griffon feathers:). Or, Puma skin war braids tied to our spears, or a necklace of teeth for the love of Innoruk.


I'm sure there are plenty more ideas out there, for items that would make that SOE programmer want to come to work early rather than call in sick. Lets get to work compiling a list of workable ideas, and forget this silly crusade to keep tanks alive, or be wanted in groups. Thats just wrong folks. Lets get real:)
