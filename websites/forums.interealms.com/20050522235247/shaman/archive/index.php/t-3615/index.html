Alandrel Mesmer
You can single pull past most mobs in COM at 54. Before then you will get assists most places.

Exceptions to this are mist golems, most phantoms, wraiths, and greater war boned skeletons. These can all be level 45-48 and will assist if you are 54. Of course you can pull them past other lower-level mobs with no problems.

Goo's are kind of a big unknown as they can vary in range with no name change.

Harmony pulling works very well in COM and can be done by any ranger or druid who has the spell. Ranger's make pretty good partners in COM.
