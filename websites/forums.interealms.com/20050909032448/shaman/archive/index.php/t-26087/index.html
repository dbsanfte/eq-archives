Gaknab
Ok here's the rundown on what happened.

Basically the Cheiftain did not eat your first stone, he accpeted it like he was supposed to. However because you had not yet done the teir 2 group mission, you had not completed all the steps to obtain the second faction award, so you did not get it.

After you completed the teir 2 group mission and you went back to camp and hailed the Cheiftain you recived the 2nd faction reward, as you had then compelted all the teir two tasks.

The stone the GM reimbursed you can be destroyed.

I am not sure why you were able to get the teir 3 quests before completing all the teir 3 tasks. You should not have been able to.

I would hazard a guess that when you petitioned to have your stone replaced because it was "eaten" the GM might have done something else on top of giving you a new stone, possible adjusting your faction level.

It's also possible that something got thrown out of wack because you did the turn in out of order. From what I understand you are supposed to complete the 3 quests, then the group mission, then the raid mission. In reality you can do them in any order, however DO NOT turn in the raid item until you have completed the rest.

You are not the only person that has had this problem.

Anyways because the Cheiftan will not send you to one of his underlings to get the teir 3 tasks, it appears that everything is working as it is supposed to now.

Now I bet you're saying ***? I can't progress, how can everything be working right?

The reason is becuase unless you also did a ton of missions aside from the assigned tasks in order to get faction, you will not have a high enough faction level to get the tier 3 tasks.

You need to have a high amiable faction level before the NPC's will give you the teir 3 tasks. If you have just done the assigned tasks in order and perhaps a few missions for crystals, your faction will not be high enough.

Keep working those missions, and turning in the sphere for the faction boosts. I'll bet after 5 or so more missions, 10 at the outside your faction will be high enough to get the teir 3 tasks.
