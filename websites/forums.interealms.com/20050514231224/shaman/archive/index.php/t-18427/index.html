for Trakanon himself, everyone needs to have 150PR minimum. Well over 200 is recommended. If you can get a mercenary bard or 2 for resists, its well worth it. If Trak can land his DoT, you're pretty much dead. End of story. 1200hp DD + 150ish/tick DoT on everyone (probably 3+ times too, since it will take you all a bit to kill him) is murderous for a small crew. You have a lot of cleric support, which will help mitigate some of it, but it's pretty bad news if it lands on someone repeatedly. And with only 9 of you, losing 1 or two DPS folks (or a cleric even more so) will be a serious problem.

The rest of the stuff should be pretty easy for you with that crew though. The protector and jumpy aren't too bad. It may take you a while to get through the reets, shrooms and juggs though since you are a little light on DPS.

good luck, he's a fun fight!

Dalarian
For Trak, more DPS >>> more healing. I'm not sure how often he Trak Touches, but that is going to be a problem with that small of a crew especially since you're DPS-lite. I suggest having one of the lower level clerics DA pull him to the back of his lair when you're ready to engage him since you have no pally, that way you avoid one Trak Touch, and if something goes wrong while the rest of you run in its not a terrible loss.

You say you have a mage for CoH but he isn't in the class list you gave, that would be another way to beat the trak touch.

All in all:

Good:
Trak only has 32k hit points.

Bad:
He teleports people
He has what is still a semi-mean AE by todays standards, especially since you have no bards for resists
He procs Venril Sathirs 'soul tug' lifetap
He will be buffed by a level 60 chanter.

Ugly:
Even when you know exactly what you're doing he is easy to wipe on with a small crew.

I really hope your monk knows how to pull the lair, it can be a real PITA although CoH at least lets you fix screw-ups easier. Poke around the archives at Monkly Business, pretty sure I read some good descriptions of pulling his lair there.
