Level 49 introduces some great spells, especially if you solo: Envenomed Bolt, Plague, Rage, new pet. I wish I had been playing my Shaman pre-Kunark because I have to assume that we were seriously overpowered at Level 50 at that time.

Grouped, you probably won't get as much use out of these spells. I use Envenomed Bolt sometimes in a small party that is low on melee output and healing (i.e., when duoing). Malosi is a very worthwhile spell if you have a bunch of casters in the party. New Strength and Dexterity buffs make the melees feel good.
