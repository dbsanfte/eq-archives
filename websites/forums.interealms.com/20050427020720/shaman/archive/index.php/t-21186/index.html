Mojarda
As others have said, IA is a good spot at your level. Made quite a bit of cash there.

An alternate camp, that is easy and safe, is the Top Shelf in Velks. It isn't sexy, but at 61 most spiders are still DB; all are single pulls; they give average AAxp; velium still sells decent; and camp spot is safe to go AFK (although someone will usually take the camp if you do).

At 61, with basically the same spell set as you, I could take 4 named/ph spawns (Crystal Eyes, Crystal Fang, Frenzied Broodling, & Frenzied Stalker) plus the other spiders between these spawn points and camp. Most named mobs drop items worth 500-2k in the bazaar, but the Frenzy drops SCHW (3k on MT) and SCWB (7k on MT).

In a four hour hunt, I could get 4-5 named to pop and get 3k-4k in velium and silks. If I was lucky with drops, that might be 10k total plus an AA to boot.

Couldn't handle more than 2 of sessions in a row, however, as it is mind-numbingly boring, but a decent change of pace from IA, Droga, etc.

Hail,

of all zones mentioned above (HS, OS, Droga, Nurga, Acrylia, Inner Acrylia, Velks, PoN) what would be the best balance of exp and cash? im pretty much in need of both atm. again, any help is greatly appreciated.
Now that you have Torpor, you can effectively solo in all of the above areas. I would say the PoN hedge would be the best exp if you can handle the mobs, and Droga or Inner Acrylia would be the best for cash drops if you don't have competition w/ other farmers. One of the rooms in Innovation (down the left path, first room on your right past the factory door) would also be soloable if you could get there safely. Good exp, fairly safe from trains once you make it there, and if things go bad GY rezzes are easy to get. Tinkering tradeskill drops can be sold in the bazaar, but you won't get rich.

OS gem drops have been nerfed, though you can still do alright on item drops if you know the area well and the zone isn't busy. But CR's are a major pita if something goes wrong, and asking a cleric to go all the way out there AND have to then get to the body is a pretty big favor.

HS can have similar difficulties for CR's, but I think the exp is better.

Velks is still the bastion of rude people & farmers last time I was there. I avoid that zone & at 62 the exp isn't that great anyways.

Temple of Droga and Inner Acrylia would have okay exp for you and offer the best chance of cash rewards in my opinion. I feel Droga is easier (and less boring) than IA, though IA probably has the edge in terms of cash value (Droga gets farmed more imo - so items are devalued more) per solo session. Droga is easier to get to if things go badly, and easier to CR as well in my experience (nothing sees invis).

Best of luck to you!
