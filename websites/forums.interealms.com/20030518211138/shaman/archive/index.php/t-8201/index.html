Well, on the advice found here, I changed the area that I was hunting in.

I moved north from Kaesora to the area about even with Kurn's Tower. I got there at about 10pm or so (game time) and started killing all of the iksar roaming in the area.

At what had to be the stroke of midnight, a rogue shaman popped on the grassy area near the eastern zone wall and started walking ~SE. I swear I saw him fall outta the sky. He wasn't even SoW'd when I slowed him.

I resisted his attempt to slow, then he closed to melee... Half a dozen shots to the head with my trusty club and a nuke later, the mandible is mine.

After I was finished gloating over his corpse, I turned to see where I was at in relation to the Pit ramps. I was (roughly) east of the northern most ramp on the Kaesora side. (First ramp south of Kurn's Tower) When I first seen him, he was at the military crest of the high ground in the grassy area on the sand side. I didn't get a /loc due to the fact that I was giddy that I actually found him. I would say his spawn point is within a few paces of 0,0 if not exactly on it. He's much tougher to track down then the one in WW.

I then made a bee line to LoIO to look for the 'cudda... Did the "Sense Animal" thing and started moving counter-clockwise around the lake from the Cabilis zone area. At about 12 o'clock (if the lake were a clock) he popped up. I lev'd and headded out, still sensing... When I passed over him, I did a 180, went under the water and nuked... A second later, he attacked me... Again, a few shots to the head and another nuke = dead fishy. Got my Skull with an II, a fresh fish and some fish scales.

Now I have everything but the clay. So, I ate the fish for lunch, and headed to the nexus to catch the spire down to the Dreadlands. Got Sow, Lev and Invis up, and ran the top of the zone wall to Burning Woods. I stood pretty much at the zone line and foraged. I got my Mendglow Clay on my second attempt. Then I got nothing but a Hornet egg, (which I ate) and some meteor things... /shrugs

Anyway, I had skilled my pottery up to 50 a while back in prep for fixing the skull... I hustled back to Cabilis and found the pottery wheel. The combine was trivial at 50.

Hierophent Oxyn was quite pleased with my return of the skulls. He awarded me the Iron Cudgel of the Seer and sent me on to speak with Crusader Quarg...
