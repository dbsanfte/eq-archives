Roydal
West Wall break is really the only way.

I helped in a CR once of an alliance that did a Fear break running north.. when we went in, there were over 100 corpses scattered.. everywhere.. with monks running around screwing up aggro even more.

The key to a clean break is control. Pick the strongest 18, evacer in each group, zone in, turn left 90, run straight down to west camp and start fighting - if anyone aggros on the way - bring it. Keep fighting until clerics or encs either run out of mana or just start drawing too much aggro - when the adds arrive, and they will heh, they will be no use to you. At this point, evac out, and port back to Feerrott to try again. This can be achieved with 1 group, but I will guess your level range is 46-54ish.. so 18 is a good figure to start with.

Keep going at it until your group can run down to west wall, not be aggroed, and finally sit safely - at this point, bring the whole raid in a group at a time, and proceed as normal.

A little brief, but there are my ideas for you hehe.

Must say, a Plane break is always a lot of fun no matter how many times you do it, especially when your swarmed in mobs ;).

Tylon
hehe, I think its only called VU tree on Fennin Ro.

If you have 3 groups and a chanter that is 55+ then you can try a West Wall break. Basically you zone your groups in about 15 seconds apart after an FD class inside gives the all clear. Make sure everyone is invis before zoning in. Then have them make a 90 degree left turn and head to the wall. Everyone stay in a tight group.

If you got lucky and noone aggroed on zonein then eventually your gonna get a Scarling, Spider, or Ape in camp....most likely several once one aggros. Assign one ranger/sk/pally to keep your enchanter alive by keeping aggro off em. Make sure everything that enters camp is ensnared. Once fighting start people will not be able to sit for a good 20 minutes or more. You need a couple good shamans too. When a mob enters camp it needs to be slowed immediately cause if you lose your enchanter you are gonna be in for a long CR.

The best setup for mid 50s is 3 groups with at least 2 enchanters 54+, two mid 50s shaman and a bunch of melee. The trick to a WW break is fast kills and keeping your enchanters alive.
