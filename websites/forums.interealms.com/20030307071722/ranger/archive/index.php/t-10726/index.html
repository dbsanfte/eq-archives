For what reason does Enrage happen? How many times will it happen during a fight? When/ why does it end?
Telloran Treehugger
Enrage will happen when a mob gets low on health (usually at about 3/4 of a yellow bubble). I'm not sure if there's a level limit where this starts happening. It only happens once during a fight and lasts about 10 seconds. If your anywhere in the front arc of the mob he/she will riposte every one of your attacks. If you know the mob is one that enrages beforehand just take the necessary precautions (i.e.; don't be in the frontal arc, or turn attack off).
