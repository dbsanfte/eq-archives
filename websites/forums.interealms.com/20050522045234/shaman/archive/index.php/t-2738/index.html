I agree with most of the above answers. I would just like to add a bit more about how flexible Torpor really is..just in case you were having any doubts ;)

Uses for Torpor:

- Soloing (shines when combined Quickbuff and Channelling AAXP). During Combat, after combat, before slow cast..etc etc.

- Efficient healing spell for casters and clerics DURING combat. Snare/Slow component is mostly Irrelevant during combat when cast on casters.

- Efficient healing spell for melees AFTER combat (during if really needed in emergencies but usually ticks them off)

- AE Group. Think I am crazy? Well I am but that is beside the point. Shamans with Quickbuff 3 have the MOST EFFECTIVE AE heal. Get a chanter and wizzy...slap Torpor's on them at beginning of AE and go to town. Rinse repeat as needed.

- Pet Group. Most economical pet heal spell.

- MAGE/SHAMAN. Can you say unlimited mana? Hehe I knew you could. Combine a Torpor casting Shaman with a Mod Rod 2 pooping Mage and you have perhaps the most efficent mana sqaud to grace Norath.
