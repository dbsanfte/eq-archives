Fascaser
In Addition to what's been posted:

Range: Goranga Idol
Earring: Any of the Chardok drops ... like Earring of Twisted Leaves.
Feet: JB boots first - they are cheap. But better to pick up Hate boots ... plus any other Hate drops ... for the turn in via PoK for exp at the very least.
Bracer: Any of the dragonhorn bracers - may be a bit pricey for you
Face: Enshrouded Veil - pretty cheap


But as a Troll myself ... I started working on natural resists ... mostly on Fire Resists. With a starting fire resist of 5 as a Troll ... there is only so much you can do with the standard resist gear. Things like SBS shield will help a bunch for you. Lodi shield for your back is helpful as well (AC, HP, Mana, stat's, EB, and resists). There are also things like the following which you can quest for or loot at your lvl.

Range: Stone of Morid
Hands: Ogre Butcher gloves or Saprophyte spore gloves
Head: Crown of the Froglok Kings
Feet: Tribal War Boots
