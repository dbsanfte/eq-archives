Drolgin Steingrinder
From the day I first got lost in Butcherblock (something I still do) to exploring ToV, it's been fun. But at some point, it's got to end. Like many others have said before me, EQ is more of a chore than a rollercoaster ride these days, with the main content being in farming ubers or camping ultrararespawn Y for quest Z.

I don't regret the good hours I have had in EQ, and I will miss the friends I've made. However, I have too many important commitments and way too many things in the RealWorld(tm) that I want to explore for me to waste my time doing something that doesn't really give me joy anymore.

So Drolgin steps out of Norrath and into Copenhagen. Who knows, if things change for me, and for EQ, I may be back - I will probably stop by and check out Luclin. But the 3AM raids I can't handle anymore - and those were the last bastion of fun in the game part of EQ.

I will take a lot of good memories with me, mostly of my friends and the things we did.

Rizz, for caring about Keepers of the Elements and the members of that guild to the extent that it sometimes ruined your own fun.

The KoE officers for putting up with me and for letting me know I had their trust - Zelfani, Hammerstalker, Canoe, Aaeamdar, Aranuil, Ryutono, Chmee, Cyrssi, Klydon and Vhela. You guys have done a great job, and I hope you will continue to work hard to make KOE the friendliest guild on the server.

KoE members old and new - We did good, all things considered. My one regret is that I couldn't help enough when it was needed. Thanks for every laugh you brought to me and for every thought shared.

The LateNightCrew - Larain, Droogimp (F DROO!), Burien, Cshae (*HUGS*), Grugr (where the hell did you go you big lug?), Sparck, Slashir. You made me have fun in the game again, brought me back to EQ hardcore, and for that I thank you and curse you ;)

Anyone who ever made me laugh, picked me up or put me down when I needed it - I thank you.

There are just too many memories for me to put them all in writing - but trust me when I say that I haven't forgotten you.

I'll probably keep trolling the boards and the IRC channels, and if I start going totally nuts over the absence of friends, I may log on to say a few hellos - but for now, I'm out. If you want to get in touch with me, drolgin@hotmail.com works wonders ;)

Take care all, have fun - danish dorf out.
