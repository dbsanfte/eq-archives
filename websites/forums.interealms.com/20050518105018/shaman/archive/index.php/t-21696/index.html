Most of the changes I do like, but there are a couple that I don't care for.

1. Bring back the spell book for medding at low levels. As much as I hated staring at it, there were 2 very good points to it. the first was I leveled my butt of to get rid of it, and second was the sense of accomplishment at finally having gotten rid of it.

2. PoK stones - These do make travel very easy, but the rob alot of the thrill from the game as well. Remember the thrill of running from Qeynos to freeport for the first time, running Kith at night, the challenge of getting from Paineel to Qeynos while KoS in Erudin? At higher levels they are fine, but for those starting out the thrill of discovering the world, and the challenges of moving through areas where it feels like you are being hunted have a rush all thier own.
