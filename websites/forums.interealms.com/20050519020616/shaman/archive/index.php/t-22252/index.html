beriah
There have been spell groups in the game for a year + now.

Right click on the book select SAVE SPELL SET. (the book under your casting bar)

Then you can /mem SPELLSETNAME to quick mem that set.

Make a bunch of diff ones with what you want, and goto town.

I think if your not using this your the only one not using it hehe.
