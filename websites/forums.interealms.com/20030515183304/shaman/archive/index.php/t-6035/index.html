Baz3
don't worry about the WIS. (mainly because it won't help ya much for a long time. WIS*level/10 = mana therefore at level 10 one point of WIS only equals one point of mana. At level 50 it equals 10 pts of mana.)

for a long time in your career, ac will be most valuable, as you do not have spells to avoid damage (slows are not very useful until Tagar's, imo), deal damage (Dot's and DD's are expensive and inefficient), or heal (Inner Fire is you most mana efficient heal - it is just too little after a while). You will be a melee until at least 24 and probably longer. and you will always want to max out def at each level, which means meleeing) that haste sash is a great item for you.

hp is always good, and becomes even more important as you get regen and cannibalize lines of spells. Your rings will work for you "forever" - many oracles are wearing those same rings! as you approach level 60, hp> all is a common opinion.

The only spot I thought was weak, was the shoulders. But I would have been dang glad to have those shoulders at level 10, so what do I know?
