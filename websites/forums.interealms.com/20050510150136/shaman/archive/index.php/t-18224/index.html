Sciek Spellbottler
Proc rate is pretty much constant over time. Delay doesn't affect proc rate in that sense. However, if you slow yourself then you swing less over time, and thus you have more procs per swing.

It's a point of view kinda thing. If you're talking about proc rate over time. Delay has no effect. If you're talking about proc rate compared to number of swings, then yes delay does have an effect.

20 delay weapon, unhasted with no double attack/dual wield... You'll swing every 2 secs or 30 times per minute. Say the proc rate of said weapon is 2/minute. Then you'd proc roughly once every 15 swings.

Now you slow yourself 75% effectively changing the delay to 80, swinging once every 8 seconds, you only swing 7.5 times per minute. Proc rate is still 2/minute so you'll proc on roughly every 3rd or 4th swing.

Make sense?
