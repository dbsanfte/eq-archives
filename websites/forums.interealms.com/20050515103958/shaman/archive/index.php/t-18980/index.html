I did the following zones either solo or duo: Here's what I remember:

The Deep - Duo, I did it before you could invis in there. Used my pet cleric to run with DA on, then jump off the cliff. My shaman ran like hell, jumped, then /q out more than half way down, lived... The mobs are easy to single pull once over the bridge. Be careful of the enchanter type mobs, they charm.

Acrylilia Caverns - Solo, when you zone into AC from grimling, there will be a mob standing right there, kill him. He drops the shard alot. Just keep him down and you'll get it. I tried going deeper in, killing around the gate, none of that worked. This is the easiest way solo to get it.

Akheva - solo - invis and run to the big room where they suppossedly spawn. Go into either the left or right corner. Sort of easy to get singles, but you can get over run. Don't try a ruble rouser, tons of hp's. Just kill the mobs close to the corner, you'll eventually get it.

Fungus grove - solo - Got lucky here, was killing everything in the area with a group of people for nights on end. One day logged in and was going to gate out, but decided to kill one mob, got the shard. Lots of hit points, long fights, don't let them run, root, root, root. Kill the priest first, then the other big bugs.

If you need more specific info, let me know.
Quiggle
8 out of 10 shards are soloable to a 65 shaman with all or most of his spells. The adds really stack up in Ssra and Akheva and I don't recommend trying to solo those.

The Deep-- Invis and follow along the right wall and you'll see an area with several "a thought leech _____"s. You don't have to cross the invisible bridge to get to this camp and nothing should see invis on the way. Invis past the leechs and set up on the ramp on the other side of them. Then just kick back and kill until you get a shard. For all of the shards, you should use bane and blood of saryn if you have them, they'll go down pretty quickly. There are about 10 leechs here and you should be able to clear them all before repops. Anything can drop a shard except young thought leechs.

Acryilia Caverns-- I don't really know how to describe how to get to the camp, but it's right after you click over to inner Acryilia. The mobs are dark blue to 65 and are pretty easy to kill, you should never get more than 1 or 2 adds.

Fungus Grove-- Probably the most annoying solo camp once you're KoS. Assuming you know how to get to the room with the royals/warriors, invis there and set up camp right next to the cave entrance. Here you can cast a pet and get set up to pull. All pulls can be singled. Just root/rot and send your pet in if you feel like healing him evey once in a while, otherwise don't bother. Each kill takes about 5 minutes, so use BoU as well as the other ones. Remember to keep them rooted because they run when the get below 10% and the adds will stack up fast.

Get a group for Ssra, a few enchanters/wizards and you can AE 20 at a time. For Akheva, grab a group or 1-2 people and pull from right outside the Va'Dyn's cave.

-Quiggle
