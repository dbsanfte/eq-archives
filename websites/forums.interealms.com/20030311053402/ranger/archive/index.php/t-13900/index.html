Brannuer
There is a lot of really cheap MR/CR gear. For Vox, I don't think you need to worry about Fire Resist ... I managed to get to 118 CR and ~100 MR for around 900 plat. Most expensive item was velium ring (500pp), everything else was around 100pp or less. RyGorr legs, Bloodstained mantle, Gold Diamond mask, Chill Dagger, etc (Wurmy and JBoM both have SvM on them)... I had about an hour to get all the stuff my first raid and ended up not too bad. If there's a bard on the raid you're laughing, but I will definitely agree on the levels. Get to 52 before trying .. you'll last much longer.

Oh, and pickup Vox raids seem to be pretty chaotic. You stand a fair chance of dying, since you'll (in all probability) have a large, unorganized mob with no clear definitions of who's doing what and when, lots of whining/people not listening, etc. along with unexpected developments. But it's fun to fight a dragon in her lair, so take the chance and get some screenshots. Just be sure to know where/how corpse recoveries are being done :D
