Mingh
Drop the howling harpoon in range, and get Sarnak Prayer Beads 35 hp's or Goranga Idol. (sta + hp's)

Brown chitin legs are quite cheap and good HP upgrade. (25 or 35 Hp's)

Drop the Wis rings and get the 6ac 65 hp's to replace. 130hps

Plat ruby veil can be replaced with death mask of the elysians. (cant remember the Hp's - around 35 or so).

These alone will give you around an extra 250hp's and keep your wis at around 200.

I run at 158 wis, and havent had a problem with running oom.

Possibly a coterie cloak - 40 hp's or a hiero if you can stretch that far (75 hp's)

Take a look at my profile for more ideas, id try to balance the wis/hp's to get better all round feel.

Good luck.
Helfdane
Sadly your at the same point a lot of shaman are at; leg slots that are both cost effective and worth wearing are far in between...pick up some Brown Chitin Legs, maybe Dragon Hide legs....with one you get a decent hit point/mana/wis jump, the other grants you a nice ac hit /shrug just depends on what you consider most important at this point in the game. Oh yeah forget Seb Scale legs, barbs cant wear em :(
Ranged slot, I cant say a lot about, they are a PITA, I got a cheap Sandwich of Foul Smelling Herbs and decided to use that till I can find something better (really freaks people out on inspections)...Goranga Idol, Sarnak Prayer Beads, Beginners Magic Tome (simple PoP quest) are all solid choices.
And I have to agree with the other posters, snag a few hit point/ac rings. I use a Djarns Amethyst Ring and a Shadel Bandit ring, but a pair of Velium Fire Opal rings do just fine as well.
I try to balance a wis that I can live with (I try to aim for 195+ with bear form, I used a few AA to let me get that easier) with a ac that is easily buffable to the 1k mark (a huge world of difference) and a nice chunk of hit points.
Good luck and have fun.
