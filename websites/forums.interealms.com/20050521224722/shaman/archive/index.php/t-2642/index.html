Tailen
Extended Affliction extends the duration of all dots of 4 ticks (24 seconds for you laymen) or longer, by 15% I believe.

Type I Focus Items effect spells of up to level 20
Type II Focus Items effect spells of up to level 44
Type III Focus Items effect spells of up to level 60

Take your best guess based on the name of the focus effect, odds are you'll be right about it. Verant did a fairly good job at naming the effects on the retrofitted items. If your still not sure check out Caster's Realm they have a good list there, though it might not be fully updated yet.
