Nytmare
Depends on class, level, current STA value, and AA abilities.

For CLR/DRU/SHM priests with 255 STA or less, it's (level/20) HP per point of STA.
1 Shaman gets 0.05 HP per STA
20 Shaman gets 1 HP per STA
40 Shaman gets 2 HP per STA
60 Shaman gets 3 HP per STA
65 Shaman gets 3.25 HP per STA

Total HP value is 5 + level*15 + STA*level/20 + straight HP from gear. Every time you ding a level you automatically gain 19-28 HP, depending on your STA.

Casters get fewer HP per STA, melee/tanks get more, warriors get the most.
