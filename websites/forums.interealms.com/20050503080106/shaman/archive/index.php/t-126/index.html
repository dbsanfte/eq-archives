stormrazor
It really depends on the type of player you are. If you are a casual player then Iksar or Troll may not be a good choice because of the exp penalty.

Trolls are awesome at the end game by 60 they have a built in Fungi Tunic (so do Iksars by the way). Innate regen is awesome regardless of what some might say. After playing an Iksar I don't think I could play a Shaman that didn't have innate regen.
Trolls have access to snare necklace and JB armor...but choosing a race because of gear choices to me takes some of the fun away from playing a Shaman in the first place.

Iksars get AC bonus (awesome), innate regen, +sv fire, infravision, high wis. They do get limited armor choices, no JBB (but to be honest, many Shaman will never see the JBB anyway, regardless of race) and the exp penalty really sucks !
Iksars Hate armor is superior to Rune Etched hands down and they can wear most of the high end armors anyway.
One thing to remember Iksars are hated by every living thing on Norrath except other Iksar, and sometimes that statement isn't even true. I can't bank anywhere except Velious, Cabills, Runnyeye, and Shadow Haven. (haven't seen much of the moon).

Ogres are cool, massive str, HP's and unstunnablity from the front.
They are huge though and doorways are a problem. They have low agility, but overall pretty good stats, they have 3 less starting wis than Barbs.

Barbs are a safe choice...but are common, I like being different.

Vah'Shir are also a safe choice, but can't wear a Fungi till 51 (fungus Patch Tunic)

I think you should base your choice on the type of character you want. Base it on which race you will have the most fun with.
In the endgame I'm not sure that one race has an advantage over the other.
Whatever race suits you best is the race you should choose. It's not the race that makes a Shaman good , its the player using the Shaman that makes the Shaman good !
Just my 2cp

There doesn't seem to be much loot in the swamp.

Hmm, I'm not sure what you would consider good loot, but I've always thought of Innothule Swamp as one of the best newbie zones due to all the dropped loot. Frogloks and Froglok Fisherman drop lots of Netted Armor, Rusty Weapons, and Splintering Clubs, plus various things like Blood and Tongues that can be stacked and sold for good money. Fisherman drop Froglok Meat that can be turned in to the Warrior Guild for experience and a Rusty Axe that sells for a couple gold. They also drop containers (i.e., Backpacks, Large Bags) so you don't have to buy them.
