Shabura Naarteban
I hate Rydda. Yes, Rydda is slowable. It *is* possible to land Turgur's on him, but you won't do it before one side of the battle is dead. Its also possible to land Grummus - but here's a secret. Its not worth it. Rydda mitigates slow pretty harshly. Combined with the lower effectiveness (and longer cast time) of Grummus (plus the fact he resists THAT a lot, too), the net result is benefit from slow that comes too little and too late in the fight for it to be worth the time. We often have a lot of shaman, and at least 1 or 2 will continue to try to land some kind of slow while the rest move on to effecting the combat.

Drop extra heals on the tanks. Patch heal other folks who take damage. Run Quiescence on the key raid members if your clerics are not using HoTs for healing support. Or ... even consider adding dps yourself. If I recall, both cold and poison land well (yes, be cautious with dot aggro).

This isn't an encounter that we shine in. There are others that will make up for it elsewhere in PoP (Babnoxious + Blood of Nadox = win, just off the top of my head), but this is one that just has to be beaten. Do what you can, and help wherever (heals vs. dps) your guild needs you most. Good luck with him.

Stor
Bards and enchanters make or break this fight.

As you've probably noticed, once RD spawns, all the npcs including pets despawn. Have your enchanters set up in the next room and charm their pets. (To give you an idea of how much dps pets are, we once wiped to RD at about 80% and we eeked out a victory because we had 6 chanter pets.)

As soon as RD spawns, have your rampage tank engage, and then have your main tank pop his AE taunt. This should set rampage quickly.

Make sure you have as many bards as possible playing MR resist. If you only have a few bards, here is your grouping priority 1) MT & clerics 2) enchanters and wizards 3) rogues and other dps.

If you have multiple shamans, arrange before the fight to cast malos and malicious decay right off the bat. Immediately after, all spam grummus till it sticks. One designated shaman should be chain casting abolish disease on the MT to deal with RD's proc.

RD is still a pain in the ass even after you've killed mobs like Bertox a couple times.
