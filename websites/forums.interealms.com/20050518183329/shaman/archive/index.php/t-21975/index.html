CrustyOldShaman
Positive changes, in no particular order:

1. Dragons can now fly

2. Dryads are in the game. They are actually small fairy-like creatures rather than the dryads of Greek/Roman myth. They hover rather than walking. Their appearance is very customisable.

3. Combat lag appears to be fixed.

4. Mob AI appears to be slowly changing. Mobs are increasingly forming groups which cannot be split. This is leading to more varied and interesting fights.

5. Formulae for high level gear (level 81+) is now in game. Its very expensive so should provide the necessary money-sink needed for high level players.

Problems that remain:

1. No dungeons. Adventuring is essentially in outdoor zones. I miss dungeons.

2. Little high level content. Once above level 60 the choice of content available to you starts to dwindle.
