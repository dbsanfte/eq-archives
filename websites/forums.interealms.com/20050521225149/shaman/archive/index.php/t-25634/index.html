Nytmare
My 3 biggest peeves:

* Zone-in time for Emerald Jungle and Trakanon's Teeth is exceptionally long for many players. This has been broken since DX9 was added 10 months ago. The time it takes to zone in to Plane of Knowledge and Bazaar is also a lot longer than it used to be. Zone time into these zones may be reasonable when first starting your EQ session, but it doesn't stay reasonable.

* Intermittent video glitch, where all the textures disappear so all you see is sky when facing certain directions, especially west. Must restart EQ or reboot to fix it. It can happen to multiple people in your party or raid all at the same time, but apparently not to everyone. Plane of Torment is especially problematic.

* Message queueing doesn't work properly anymore while zoning, you often miss messages (group chat, guild chat, etc). Messages that are queued sometimes get duplicated or out of order.


Bunch of other bugs, some new some very old:

* Certain focus effects still aren't filtered properly -- duration extension effects such as Extended Enhancement and Extended Torment always show up even when your Focus Effects filter is set to not display them, but only when you cast them on another player or NPC. They filter ok when you cast on yourself.

* Trying to give a quest item to an NPC while you're invisible causes you to become visible to NPCs, but you do NOT get a message that your invis faded, and your invis icon does NOT drop from your buff box. Very old bug.

* Necro pet names have those old numerals after their name when they fail FD. "Goner00 has fallen to the ground"

* The target window and Target of Target window in the default UI are way too small to show the full name of many mobs. Make them wider.

* Iksar Shamans don't have a usable vendor for their 51-59 store-bought Kunark spells. Every other race/class combo has one. Necros have a vendor right in Cabilis for these spells, why don't Iksar Shamans? Our workaround is to mule those spells.

* Some spell sounds have long been messed up. For example, Acumen buff landing makes the glug-glug drinking sound. Enchanter spells make a very obnoxious "nyaah nyaah nyaaaaaaaaaah" sound while the chanter is in scaled wolf illusion form.

* Certain buffs AND debuffs cast by higher level players block the exact same buff/debuff cast by a lower level player, even when the buff/debuff effect is exactly the same from level to level. When the effect is identical, the buff/debuff should refresh. Focus of the Seventh, KEI, Malosinia, for examples. I'm noticing this after OoW release and buffs+debuffs from 66+ players.

* Dranik's Hollow "Firepits" expedition has blank black textures unless you modify a weird video setting in options.

* You can't run downwards when floating and mounted. I don't know if this is intentional or not, but it really ought to work. Workaround is to dismount whenever you need to push yourself to a lower elevation.

* For certain race/gender combos, cycling through the F9 views while sitting causes the view to appear at standing height when you return to first-person view. Workaround is to stand up and sit back down again. Female Iksar (Luclin model), for example.

* Recent problem with your view (first-person) switching from standing height to ducking height when you back up against a wall, even though you are not ducking. (Auto-duck is not turned on.)

* In Options, General, the Accept button for the current font partially extends off the right side of the dialog box.

* Bugs with the new group-shifting options in the raid window -- group leaders shift out of position 1 when you add a member to their group; multiple people in one group can incorrectly display as a group leader in the window; group leader status for a raid member can be turned off, but not on -- we need to be able to set a raid member as a "group leader" so he can start inviting.

* Planar Projection lasts 10 min for all encounters that I've timed EXCEPT Aerin`Dar. PP duration for that event is screwily unpredictable.

* Chat window text gets blurred while zoning due to the screen freezing right in the middle of scrolling in the "loading please wait" line of text (if the chat window is full, which it usually is). The screen should wait until the chat window fully updates before freezing.

* Handing multiple stackable items to an NPC one at a time causes them all to stack in the first slot of the Give window. NPCs don't accept stacked items anymore, so the items ought to be fitted into the next slot in the window instead of stacking by default.

* The tabbed Abilities dialog box does not stay on the last tab you were using if you restart EQ. On the Hotkeys tab, the hotkey bank does stay on the last bank you were using if you switch characters, however the bank number display gets reset to 1, which is incorrect if you were on banks 2-10. Pressing the left or right arrow to switch banks fixes the erroneous bank number display.

* Various mount problems: Group buffs and MGBs sometimes don't land on other players when you're on a mount; /random messages are sometimes not seen when you or the roller is on a mount; mobs sometimes summon you repeatly when you're mounted even when you're positioned right on top of them.

* Mobs have started casting Fear inside dungeons a lot.

* Lev sometimes causes Hide to fail (rogues).

* Advanced Pet Displine AA (/pet focus) doesn't stay set after you zone or suspend your pet.

* Mirao Frostpouch in Plane of Knowledge is incorrectly identified as a Poison Supplies vendor. He's actually a potion vendor.
