Mamutoi
Whenever I try to figure out which weapon is better damage wise than the other one I first look at the damage/delay over the proc. You can't depend on the proc, its always a maybe when it comes into contributing to the damage. Even when it does proc it may get resisted, or only land for partial. Unless of course the proc is some ungodly damage or effect in which case I would choose proc over damage/delay.

So looking at the damage/delay of your two weapons:

Rune Etched Icewurm Fang: 12/19 1.583 ratio
Barbarian Spiritist Hammer 22/43 1.954 ratio

So looking at the damage/delay the Rune Etched Icewurm Fang would be better. I would probably use that instead if I was in your shoes. Besides the damage delay, it is also a one hander, so you don't have to give up your shield. If you look at the proc for damage the REIF is less damage, but weapon is a lot faster so you will be hitting more often and have more chances for it to proc.

This is just my opinion though.

PS. Once you get your epic and a jbb you will likely put both weapons away.

Feradach
Actually, from what I've read procs are a function of time, not number of swings. You'll get the same chance to proc over say a 60 second period no matter how many times you swing.

I've got both a REIF and a BSH and it does seem like overall they'll proc at the same rate (proc chance per second) whether I'm slowed, hasted, or swinging normal. I have no doubt that DEX does impact the proc rate, but the delay on the weapon or a haste buff doesn't seem to have any impact on how many times you'll actually get the weapon to proc.

I love my BSH and pull it out every once in awhile just to keep my 2HB skill up, but the REIF is my weapon of choice due to the superior dmg/delay ratio (better than our epic, btw).
