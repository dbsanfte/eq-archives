Giac
The upgrade in power from the 1.5 to the 2.0 is fine, imo. I do think that the idea to make the effect an HoT that heals a set amount is kind of weak as it has little to no scale-ability(is this even a word?).

See, right now, the Shaman epic effect is fine and handy due to it stacking with AA and spell(the +crit mod is junk, imo, but it's "icing on the cake" or something), but 6 months from now, it will be less powerful than it is today in relative terms.
A perfect example of how an epic effect should be designed are the Cleric epic 1, 1.5 and 2.0, as they scale with the power of the character(s) to 99%.
If they really wanted to go with a HoT effect for the Shaman epic, they could have made it heal 5%(6-7% for the 2.0)of the target's total HPs/tick and preserved its power through the future expansions.
