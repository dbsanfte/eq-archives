I don't know what your earning potential is, nor your current budget. Some of the following suggestions are expensive, some are cheap. They are only suggestions - different strokes suit different folks

Ears - dump the +wisdom earrings and get any of
(a) Earring of purity
(b) Earring of cleansing
(c) Earring of twisted leaves

Neck - ivory imbued collar

Face - crystal spider eyes, deathmask of the elysians, golem jawbone

Helm - Metal linked crown, JB helm

Fingers - dump the +wis fingers and get either Velium Fire (6AC 65hp) or if you are rich get Shadel Bandit and Circles toe bones

Wrists - Do the Coldain Military Bracer quest. Get a Jarsarth bracer from KC - they rot all the time. If you are rich get a bracer of Sense or Fortitude. Do the Bracer of Hammerfall Quest

Arms - JB arms are v cheap. Barbed ringmail sleeves are nice

Hands - those gloves are ok

Back - Do totemic quest. Get a wet thorn cloak. Buy a cloak of truth if you are rich.

Chest - Barbed ringmail is the cheapest of the OK BPs.

Shoulders - Elder Wolf Hide

Waist - Chitinous Belt, Girdle of Mithaniel

Legs - legs are fine

Feet - feet are fine

Primary - fine for now - get a REIF, or even a tarnished driveshaft

Secondary - SBS is fine

Range - get a gantru charm if you can, or even a Ceremonial Smudging stick, Goranga idol etc. If necesssary put your howling harpoon in range and equip a different weapon
