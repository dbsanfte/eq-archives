vivala
I believe the way it works is this.

In fungus grove the small mushrooms don't hit very hard. You have a bard playing the 2 or 3 ae damage songs, and if they can play a regen song, then so be it.

You have the shaman cast replenishment, disease resist, and other hit point buffs on the bard. At this point the bard should be regening hit points at a very high rate, a fungi would make it even easier. Shaman goes out and pulls a shroom with turgurs. Bring it back to the bard, shaman epic dot's the shroom, and casts torpor on the bard. Bard should be able to take agro and keep the mobs standing around him. 80% of the shrooms in fungus don't run. If the bards disease resist is high enough, probably around 150, the shrooms spells won't land. Shaman goes and grabs another shroom. The number of mobs you keep in the fight, depends on the level and gear of your bard.

Of course the guy that created this method is going to say this isn't exactly correct, but i'm sure it's pretty close.
