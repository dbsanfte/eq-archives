Rathelon
At 60-61, with Malo, you should be able to slow most mobs. You will need to hit it with Malo first, then Malosini, and then slow it. For the dragonflies, as Guntar said, use Insidious Decay and disease slow because they are very magic immune.

Dont worry about the tank dying if you cant get your spells in instantly, because the tank is there to take a beating. That's his job, and he has the hps/AC for that. You dont. Dont slow as they come in and pull the mobs on yourself..that could be why you're dying so much. Let the tank engage it and position it while you are casting Malo/Malosini. Then after those are in, and the tank has called for assist get right behind him and try to slow (hint, use shrink so the tank doesnt have to fight to move around you). The mob will most times turn on you once slow lands (or gets resisted), but if you are standing right behind the main tank he will be able to pull aggro back very quickly. Just torpor yourself and wait for the mob to stop hitting you and back off (dont sit down, of course).
