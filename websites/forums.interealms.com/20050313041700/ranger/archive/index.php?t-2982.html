In general the ranger duo in the late 50's is impressive. The combo relies on a couple of concepts, mainly damage sharing and passive regeneration. The combination of two 60 rangers normally works as follows for simple exp grinds:

Both use Earthcaller or another weapon with moderate damage and a slow effect. One ranger acts as primary for the fight and absorbs the damage with an agro switch to the other ranger if needed. Normally a slow is landed rather quickly between the two and total damage taken during the fight is low.

In the second fight the rolls are reversed with the former primary tank acting as damage dealer in regen mode using fungi + chrolo + AA, etc... With standard standing regen of approx 35hp/tick and a random heal here and there the ranger is back to full by the end of this fight. Swap rolls, rinse, repeat....

Down time tends to be confined to medding when blue runs out but KEI + PoTG normally eliminates this and can ussually be bumbed from someone after a raid.

In terms of exp -- Duoing beats soloing hands down if spawns are available owing to the virtually non-existent down time.
