The key element isn't just who would be the "best" third person to add to your group - it also matters the "ease of use" if you are going to be two/three boxing. em2johns
The thing you would be looking for with that combo, is a healing type class that doesn't sacrifice DPS. Two gimp healers, make for one good healing squad. From best to worst for your setup imo.
Druid: Buffs... more hit points for you Monk! Pot9 manna/hp regen for later. Two secondary healers is a good combo.
Mage>Necro: DPS... and heals for their own pet. A mage/necro can effectively keep his own pet healed, leaving you to save your manna healing the monk. A mage can cast malosini, as the shaman casts slow making for faster slows on pulls, and less aggro. This would probably be better than the druid, if not for one thing. Buffs.
Paladin: Similar to the mage option in that the Paladin can heal himself to a lesser degree. You lose DPS, gain some mitigation, some buffs. On the downside, you lose your monk tank possibilities. If you are three of you pooling your funds together, you can probably get the monks gear to a point where he can tank similar to a gimp Paladin.

The downside is your monk will have a hard time staying up in Tier 2+ zones. Hopefully, you can convince a tank class to join when you get to that point. Tank, 2 adequate healers (shaman included), and a monk for dps makes for a good team.
