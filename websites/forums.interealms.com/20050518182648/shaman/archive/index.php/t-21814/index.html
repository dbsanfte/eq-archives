eximius
1. Go to tribute master in your home town. Give him platinum or loot. Some loot does well as tribute--search these.

2. You can set the tributes you want at the Tribute master.

3. Once a tribute is started, it can only be downgraded once you leave tribute master, that is you can turn them off.

4. This is especially great for deciding whether or not a focus effect makes a great difference in an encounter.
Kryscha
To 'downgrade' your tributes, use ALT+U to bring up the tribute window (once you've left the tribute master) and simply select the tribute effect and click downgrade. If you've changed the default key mapping, then obviously you won't be pressing ALT+U. You can keep downgrading until the effect is removed.
