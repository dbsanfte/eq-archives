EQOA has a variety of differences to EQ today. These differences are based mostly because it is on a console system. I for one do not have a USB keyboard and I cant really afford one atm so communication is rather cumbersome... even using the button pusing responses they give you. Here is what I have found different so far.

*No Iksar, Vah Shir or Ogres races.
*Yes there are 2 different human races. Eastern and Western. with each belonging to Freeport and Qeynos respectively.
*No Beastlord class.
*No skill raising system. You dont have rais that 1hblunt or sense heading. As you level you get ability points to plug into your stats.
*Heavy anti-twink system in the game. Level caps on things such as banded and leather. You have to be 30 to wear banded if I remember correctly and to use steel weapons.
*Cookie cutter newbie quests meant to introduce you to the game. These are pretty well done I suppose but they are the same for each class/race combo. The level 5 quest can be difficult if you cant find someone to group with and/or you dont have a keyboard for quick responses.
*Mana bar changed to Power bar and is used by each class. As a caster its used as you mana bar as a melee it is used for your various abilities such as the double attack skill for my paladin (Spiritual Strike).
*Only have 5 slots to have things "memed." I already ran into problems with spell swapping with my shaman at low level.
*More action based game. Power and hp regens faster than it would in pcEQ and things refresh faster. I havent timed it yet but Lay Hands 1 refreshed every 5-10 min.
* It is on Tunaria (ancient Antiocia) and it doesnt look familiar at all. I realize the time difference between the two but the only thing I find familiar is some little things in qeynos (the clock) and free port.
*There is no zoning. The world is loaded as you wander around.

um that is all I think at the momment. I will post more in the future.

Edit: oh a big one
*Items have durability and HP. As you use items in combat thier HP decrease and you have to get them fixed by the local blacksmith. I havent had items degrade in durability yet but as they do they are supposed to degrade in performance as well.
