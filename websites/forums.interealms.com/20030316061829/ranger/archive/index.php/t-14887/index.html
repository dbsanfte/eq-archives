I think some of you have the wrong notion of how hate works. Everything you do to a mob, be it slash, kick, bash, beg, nuke, slow, tash, snare, etc... increases your hate by a fixed amount. Ok, I hope everyone reads the above thread on taunt, and then mabye grabs a warrior and does some tests for yourself.

Taunt only matches aggro of the top person on the list. It does not add any aggro at all.

Try this.

Find a zone with humanoid mobs that say something when a taunt lands successfully.

Pull the mob with snare, and snare, tangling weeds 10 times.

Then bring the mob to a warrior and stand in front of the warrior without attacking. Have the warrior taunt without attacking until you get the msg that taunt was succesfful. The NPC will tell you.
But wait.. the NPC didn't turn to face the warrior. It is still attacking you. Have the warrior hit attack, and do the minimal damage they can before the mob turns. Have them attack with their hands. It isn't much. Now snare again, and you get aggy back.

Now have the warrior taunt 10 times without attacking. They won't pull aggy off you I bet. Now have them attack with hands, boom aggy transfers.

The point is... Taunt adds no hate.

Now continue the test. Find a wizard to add to your party, and mabye a shaman to slow/heal. Find a mob and pull it with snare. Snare it 10 times before you get to camp. Have the shaman slow on incoming. The shaman won't pull aggy because you have built so much hate with snare. Tank for awhile and have the warrrior taunt without attacking again. When a taunt is successful back off. The mob will stay with the warrior because of proximity aggro.

Taunt is all or nothing. It either works or it doesn't.

Now put your wizard to work. With the warrior just standing there, doing nothing. No taunting, no attacking, no begging.
Have your wizard unleash hell... no concussion please.
Depending on the level and the "magic hate" of the mob the wizard can nuke, and nuke, and nuke. Even if the warrior doesn't attack at all they can hold aggy from your snares probably until the mob is dead.

If someone taunts off you they get all your hate, and no one can take it away until they build more hate than you built.

You can use this same strategy for boss level mobs (especially luclin ubers) When you move into a room have each tank in your raid fire an arrow at the mob to set rampage then you start snaring, tangling weeds, and flame licking. Just keep this up while the clerics work out their complete heal order. Mob AE hits you or you get nuked? Just heal yourself, and you are adding aggy. When everyone is ready just have the main tank walk up to the mob, fire up the heal rotation, and they start taunting. The second they get attacked this means they landed a successful taunt. Now the entire raid can go full burn and melees can assist with NO FEAR of anyone pulling aggro.

For unrooted mobs you can do a variation of this strategy (AoW for instance) IF you can kite while building aggy do so, if the mob has special effects or other nastiness just pull it into position, hit weaponshield, and keep snaring and tangling weeds for 10 seconds. After this send a tell to the main tank and have them AE taunt off you. Make sure they don't AE taunt before you are done casting, and give them a few seconds to get some attacks in. Until you and the MT coordinate timing you will probably die (if you want to get really fancy you can have a cleric use the nifty Divine Aura others AA ability to save your ass after AE taunt), but 3-4 snares give the main tank a HUGE start on aggro for a fight. You won't see many wizards getting summoned unless they really get out of hand.

Good luck in your tests, and have fun. You are an aggro management scientist now.
