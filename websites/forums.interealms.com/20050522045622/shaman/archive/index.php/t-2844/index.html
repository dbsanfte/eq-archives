Welcome to the Crucible.

Don't think you need any factions for the epic except truespirit, which is gained by doing the quest in the right order.
Just check the guide at EQShaman: http://www.eqshaman.com/ for the full quest details.

One thing you can do, is start hunting in CoM to get your pages and also the ghiosk books.

Apart from that, with the SoL expansion, faction is more of a choice than a necessity. I have an iksar monk, and the only place i have tried to faction is Katta.

You may also want to work on one or two of the Velious factions. Large guilds seem to focus on giant faction (kill dorfs and dragons), smaller guilds seem to focus on dorf and dragon faction (kill giants).

Apart from that, the rest is up to you.
