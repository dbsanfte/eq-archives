Rashere and I are keeping a very close eye on players progressing through the end game content. We get excellent feedback from watching the events and talking to the players and make changes as necessary. Mostly these are small tweaks. I'm very satisified with the quality of events that players are currently tackling in Qvic, Inktu`ta, and Txevu.
