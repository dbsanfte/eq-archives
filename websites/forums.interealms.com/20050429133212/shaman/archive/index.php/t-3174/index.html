Jedafix
When one dings 40 you gain an amazing ability. You can group with level 60s and earn exp. However if you try this with say, someone quad kiting Othmirs (blue to 60) you notice the exp is horrendous. However, if the mobs that the 60 kills are green you will get a fair chunk of the exp. Now fast forward -

Being a troll, I always look for places with either a) city status or b) nice exp bonus. Dungeons or cities are really the only places I like to fight to help make up for the racial penalties.

I have been able to race from 40-42 (working on 43 quickly) and perhaps even 44 in droga. Why droga, that goblin infested harm touch laden one way death trap? Simple - chardok faction. Sure we all will end up in here some day but you know what - there are already a lot of level 60s who come in here and mow down the mobs. Rogues and monks are really good for this but any class can handle themselves in droga. Droga has nice faction (40-42 and im dubious to dok) and nice nice nice nice exp. Faster than powerleveling in my opinion. When you zone in ask someone in zone if you could group with them - i have never had someone say no. Just follow them around dropping the random heal, buff whatever they need and let them kill. The exp goes to waste otherwise so no one seems to mind. Plus a greater heal after a harmtouch keeps people going more consistently :) Also, I had a nice monk who turned on autosplit and let me have the cash loots that dropped and I have made a pretty penny in here. Nothing to buy a JBB but enough to pay for my level 44 spells. The faction is nice, the exp is nice, and meeting some of the upper level crowd is nice. Good luck to everyone and please dont flame me - im sensitive :)

p.s. If you happen to be a troll you get around 2 yellows per hour in here so barbs/ogres/kittys will probably get a level per ~2hours. Snare/root is very nice way to help the 60s as well :)
