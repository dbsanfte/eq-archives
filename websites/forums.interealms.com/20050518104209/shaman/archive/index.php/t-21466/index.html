This factors in a lot when clearing to raid mobs since 9 times out of 10 I will not cast Malos on a trash mob until slow has bounced 3 or 4 times. :) Does this pull the mob off the tank....sometimes, but remember if I have enough time to cast 4 slows on a mob it means that said mob is mezed and i always try to be beside the mob when mez is broken so the war does not have to chase it. I would be kinda shocked if most end game shaman did not use this style of slowing when it comes to trash mobs.
