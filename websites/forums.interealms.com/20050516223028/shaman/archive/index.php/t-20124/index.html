Jinheim
There are some abilities which give you completely new functionality (Spirit Call, Radient Cure, Virulent Paralysis), but the majority of your abilities are merely percentage improvements to your character (all archetype abilities, etc...).

For the former, it is usually enough to just train the first rank of an ability and wait until later to finish it out. I got Spirit Call 1 at around 50aa, but I didn't train ranks two and three until I had over 250aa, for example. I did the same thing with Radient Cure, because although the initial rank for these abilities are great buys, the successive ranks are not as worthwhile.

For other abilities (namely archetype abilities and the PoP improvements on them) you are going to want to finish out an entire line before you move on to the next one. I feel you get more returns and sooner if you do it this way.
