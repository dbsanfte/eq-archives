Henora
Dispells work differently.

There are many dispell AE's out there that take your first (top) buff. One example of that is Avatar Power, one of the oldest AE's out there.

Terris Thule has an AE that take your first 12 buffs.

Regular old mob and trap dispells, I have never seen any convincing evidence about the order that they take buffs in. My ANECDOTAL evidence suggests that they often take the most recent buff cast on you. So using instaclicks and junks keeps those buffs as your most recent ones.

Other reasons for using junks:
Slot 1: Virtue
Slot 2: Focus
Slot 3: KEI

If you get dispelled, you're gonna lose one of those for sure, cause you only have 3 buffs.


Now here is another buff setup.
Slot 1: Acumen
Slot 2: Bramblecoat
Slot 3: Shield of the 8th
Slot 4: Lunar Whispers
Slot 5: Spirit of Wolf
Slot 6: Virtue
Slot 7: Focus
Slot 8: Kei

Now you have 8 buffs for the dispell to choose from, since you have more junk buffs than you have good buffs, the odds are a random dispell will take a junk buff.

I think that's the best simple summary I can give about junk buffs in my experience.
