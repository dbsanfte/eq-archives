I don't use a router, I run ICS (Internet Connection Sharing) on one of my PCs. This works fine for me, but a friend had problems with it, so YMMV.

To use ICS like this, you need 2 ethernet cards in the PC, one connected to your cable modem (assuming you have one) and one connected to the hub for your local network. Hubs are much cheaper than routers, and ICS comes bundled with Windows from version 98SE onwards. The performance hit on the machine is negligible.
