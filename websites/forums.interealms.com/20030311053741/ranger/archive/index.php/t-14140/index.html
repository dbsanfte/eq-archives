Ring 2's mostly pretty easy if you're familiar with the dorf's route. Just before he gets to the giant fort, the prison area, and the R'ygorr fort, snare and drag off the mobs that will aggro on him, and root them off somewhere with your longest-lasting root. You can do the same with any roamers that are liable to be problems too. In an emergency, you can also aggro-kite the mobs while the dorf plays pet for you.

It's easily possible to do the whole thing without killing a single mob, up until the bear at the end. Just gets a bit tedious...

Nightork
In EW near the Ry'gor fort theres a dwarf camp at neg 2700 neg 450, in which is Garadain Glacierbane.

Ring 1: Garadains Blanket.

hail Garadain Glacierbane (GG from now on) and he starts off some dialogue, about you not being a friend of the mighty Coldain (no matter what faction you are, unless your kos obviously)

Anyway, he says to proove your a friend you should go out and make him a blanket.

Blanket is made by combining 2 HQ Cougar Pelts and 2 HQ Tundra Kodiak pelts in a sewing kit.

The cougar pelts are dead easy to come by, kill snow cougars in either iceclad or in EW to get them, their pretty common.

cougars are around levels 30-40 at a guess, a rare 1 or 2 are still light blue to me at 57.

The Tundra Kodiak pelts is a major major bottle neck to anyone starting the rings, they really are rarer than rocking horse ****, Tundra kodiaks only spawn in EW, and any roaming mob can be a PH for one, as best I can tell (tip: if you plan on farming for them, slaughter stuff like snow bunnies, they die in a couple of hits and gives you a chance of getting a kodiak spawn in place)

Kodiaks are low 30's at best, all green to me, and have been for a long time, mainly found between GG's camp and the IC bridge, but also a few around the GD entrance.

Track, obviously is very handy for this, they can be bought however, and retail from players between 500 and 1k.

The combine should be trivial around 50ish tailoring skill.

Ring 2: Garadains Nephew.

Hand in your Coldain blanket to GG, recieve a copper coldain insignia ring Ac1 +1str +1vmagic, GG says something about being able to get some sleep now, if his nephew was ok.

Skip this dialogue, and just say 'what assistance'

GG will hand you an axe, and say something about your willingness to help is earning friends etc etc.

What this one is about, is GG's nephew has run away to hunt some beast thats been terrorising Thurgadin.

Boridain Glacierbane can be found at roughly neg 1900 pos 1600, on a hill north east of the Ry'gor fort.

Hand Boridain the axe GG gave you, and he respawns and starts to wander off towards the giant fort in search of this beast, the rabid tundra kodiak, he will agro giants, and orcs on his way, and dire wovles.

He will reach the fort and turn around, having walked between the 2 guards there, if the frost giant captain is standing there he will get agroed by that to, even if hes harmonied.

He then heads off towards the ruins in the north of the zone and stops between a giant and a rygor guard, before turning round and heading towards the Ry'gor fort, between these 2 steps he takes a break, which is useful for medding etc, he then returns to his spawn point, ish and sits down, at witch point the rabid tundra kodiak spawns and moves towards him, I assume it would attack him, never let it get that far myself.

Kill the kodiak, loot its pelt, hand its pelt to Boridain and he gives you the axe back, now broken, which was 'on the killing blow' as he puts it, lol.

The way to do this is either by brute force, or by harmony, harmony has and allways will work best IMO, harmonise Boridain, and all mobs that will agro, moving towards him, and all mobs at the stations he stops by.

Its worth waiting before handing in the axe, if you can check if the frost giant captain is at the front of the giant fort boridain first visits, because harmony or not, he will agro, if you cant wait and want to get rid of the captain, who would path away eventually, then harmony the 2 guards and agro him, then drag him to the coldain camp directly north of the fort.

*note* even if Boridain gets agroed and killed once hes returned to his spawn, you can save the pelt and hand it in some other time, make sure if this happens, you go to GG and get the axe, and start the quest off again, or Boridain will eat the pelt, you can hand in multiple pelts once hes spawned and get the axe.

Boridains on a 2-3 hour spawn timer.

The rabid kodiak is about 45.

Ring 3: Garadains hunting knife.

Hand in the broken axe and copper ring to GG, to get a silver coldain insignia ring Ac2 +2 str +2 v magic.

Garaidan then moans about his hunting knife and asks you to make a new one for him since hes busy at his camp etc etc.

What this involves is gathering a HQ Walrus pelt from a Walrus in EW these range in level from about 35-40 ish and are very very plentiful in the south western corner of EW.

A fairly common drop, and easy light blue exp if your 50-55 ish.

Aswell as the pelt you will need a Rhino horn from a wooly Rhine in EW, these spawn all over the place, but are fairly common in the north of the zone, and in the area west of sleepers tomb at around neg 7000 pos 2000.

Rhinos are roughly the same level as walrus's.

Other than the rhino horn, you need a skinning rock, which spawns on the ground in EW, in the same sort of area as the rhino's and walrus's, they apear as red rocks on the ground, and are often seen.

Combine these 3 items in a smithing forge, (strength greatly affects your smithing success rate aparently, prehaps get a buff before attempting it)

A skill of around 50 would almost certainly make it trivial.

Ring 4: Garadains smithing hammer.

Hand in the silver ring and the hunting knife to GG, to get the 3rd ring Ac3 +3st +3vs magic (seeing a pattern here? )

Garidain then proceeds to moan about how badly crafted it is, and asks you to find Tain Hammerfrost, who left a little while ago with a smithing hammer which he can use to refine the blade.

Tain spawns a little ways west of the Iceclad bridge, at neg 4900 neg 2400 ish.

You hail him, and he says something about 'help'.

Tain is injured and needs an elexir from a giant whos been chasing him.

Be very careful when doing this ring, its highly dangerous, as soon as you say 'blah blah help' to Tain, he respawns, and 4-5 giants will spawn to the south, one named and some normal giants, they hit hard, and fast, and theres no way you could take them down solo unless your very patient or very bored.

Or a druid who does that sort of thing lots.

Now depending on your level, theres 2 ways you can do this, both involve a similar principle.

At 56 and above, you would be able to solo pull Ghrek, below that level, you would agro all the giants with an arrow pull.

The way I do this, is as the giants are running towards Tain (they spawn with instant agro on him) target Ghrek, and fire an arrow off at him, depending on your level this will or wont agro everything, either way Tain will die, unless you do this in a group.

Drag Ghrek, with the giants if your <56 north, to Dobbin Crossxes camp at neg 500 neg 3000, when you get near to this camp (within sight of) snare Ghrek, and run the other giants through the camp, they will all agro the dwarves, and instead of chasing you will fight them (as long as you havent hit them!)

With the giants occupied, run back to Ghrek and Cancel/anul the snare, and drag Ghrek west towards the north west camp which you may have seen earlier with ring 1.

Resnare Ghrek, the reason you cancel the snare is so, when the giants kill the dwarves at Dobbins camp, your far enough away so as not to re-agro the giants, and its just you and Ghrek.

Personally I like to let the dwarves at the northern camp do the dirty work of inflicting dmage to Ghrek here, as regen/arrow/dot kiting is long, tedious and boring.

With Ghrek snared, and prehaps a few dots on him, he will be rather angry with you, so you run him through the dwarf camp, and get the dwarves to beat on him, now be careful as to how much you let them beat him, you can control how much agro you have on Ghrek..

A: by how far away you are from him

B: by casting on him, snare, flame lick etc.

Eventually Ghrek will get annoyed with the dwarves beating him, and will turn on them, its up to you when you let this happen, I let them get him to about 2/3 health before either jolting or backing off, Ghrek will after this be nice and weak, and you would have saved yourself some time.

Kill Ghrek, loot the elexir and wait on Tains respawn, roughly 4-5 hours.

*note* I recomend doing this first, since hes on such a long respawn, and you wont be able to hand the elexir in, unless you have a group taking the giants and Ghrek down.

While your waiting for him to respawn you can do the other rings....

Also the Elexirs arent lore, so you can repeat this process when he respawns, again, like Boridain in ring 2 you MUST say 'what help' and trigger the spawn to be able to hand in the elexir, and you can hand in multiple elexirs and recieve multiple hammers.

Hail Tain, give Elexir, get Coldain smithing hammer, which also isnt lore.

Ring 5: The Ry'gor messanger and the invassion plans.

Hand in ring 3 and the smithing hammer to get ring 4 Ac4 +4str +4vmagic.

GG then says something about the invassion, and plans and a messanger.

The Ry'gor messanger is a strange fellow, Ive yet to find any patern, or logic to his spawning times, he does however spawn at the Ry'gor interogator in the ruins to the north, and runs straight towards the Ry'gor fort entrance.

Hes low level 20-30 maybe, so will be very low on your track list.

When killed he drops the Ry'gor invassion plans.

Hand these plans into the coldain lookout in GG's camp and a batch of orcs will spawn to the south west of the camp, 3 oracles or so, and some Ry'gor invaders, and a named SK, Scarbrow T'huk or something.

Kill scarbrow and loot his head, again the 56+ single pull comes well into effect here, as soon as the orcs spawn, the oracles cast a few buffs, and scarbrow leads the charge towards GG's camp.

Arrow him, hopefully single pull him, if not snare and drag any adds through dwarf camps, at 57 I can mellee scarbrow, but he has a nasty HT.

Kill him, loot his head, and wait 45 mins or so for GG's camp to respawn.

Ring 6: Garadains mission.

Hand in Scarbrows head and ring 4 to GG,get ring 5 Ac5 +5str +5mr he will then tell you about a failed assault on the Ry'gor, and how no one knows what happened to the dwarves in the assault.

In the southwest corner of the zone, theres a camp with a named wolf, icefang there, also between the game hours of Korigain spawns, he is a ghost and was one of the lot in the failed asault, hand in your 5th ring to him and he explains what happened, how the dwarves had their wolves charmed and got munched bassically.

At this point icefang respawns and wanders off, I suggest as soon as you hand in your 5th ring head to the Iceclad bridge, to the north directly of this is an orc camp.

Icefang wanders around, then heads towards this camp, as he gets near 3 oracles spawn at the camp and a named, poxbreath yellowfang.

Best way to solo pull Pox is to invis up, walk into the hut just before icefang gets there and they all run off after him, target Poxbreath, run out of the hut and go behind it, load snare or calefecation or any agroing spell and cast from max distance, Poxbreath will come on his own, and yes you can pull through the hut walls.

I can solo this at 57.

Loot the note to kromrif.

At the Ry'gor fort, theres an exit portal from CC to the west of the entrance.

Ocassionaly (like the Ry'gor messanger) a Ry'gor emisary spawns, when he does he heads to this exit portal, paces up and down a bit, then Rodrick Tardok spawns, agro him, kill him, loot his head and his dagger, he is immune to snare and root, but suceptable to fire spells.

*note* If no one attacks the Ry'gor Emissary, he paths into the Ry'gor fort, speaks to the oracle (oracle wasnt up when I did this, important?)

And wanders back out to the portal again spawning ANOTHER Rodrick, this was repeated by myself and 5 others till we all had his head, and where apropriate the dagger.

Very handy to know as there is often a cue for him.

Ring 7: Garadains rescue mission.

Hand in Rodricks head, the kromrif note and ring 6 to get ring 7 Ac7 +7str +7mr.

Garadain then sends you on a mission to rescue an important dwarf, namely Corbin blackwell.

Corbin is guarded by a Ry'gor interogator, 2 Ry'gor prison guards and a couple of Giants.

Loose them in a dwarf camp somehwere and hand him your 6th ring.

He also requires a shackle key from Warden Bruke.

Warden Bruke has a PH, and resides in the same sort of area as Corbin, not sure of the precise loc, but he has a kromrif priosn gaurd as PH, and sapwns, alone in a ruined building thats in a circular shape.

When you agro Warden Bruke, a random number of giants sapwn, if you pull from a distance the giants wont agro, Warden Bruke is unsnarable and un slowable and unstaunnable, the agro on the dwarves trick used in ring 4 doesnt seem to work aswell with Bruke, he seems to pop back to his spawn point for some reason when agroed by dwarves, and he kills them, he will pop back to his 'home' like a mob gating.

Kill him anyway, loot his key, hand that and the 6th ring to Corbin and he will start to wander off towards Dobbin Crossaxes camp.

When he gets near to Dobbins camp, around neg 500 neg 1200 a truck load of giants and orcs spawn.

These all spawn with instant agro on Corbins spot and they charge towards him, to avoid him getting agro, arrow him from a distance, and root him, camp out and back in to clear agro on him.

The giants and orcs wont have agroed him, and wander past the ulthork fields, and path towards the fort, if you want a bit of exp, pick them off individually and kill them.

when you log back in, corbin stands still for ages and ages, before eventually carrying on towards the camp, when he gets there, Dobbin and he have a few words to say to eachother, about the dain will be so pleased etc etc.
Corbin will despawn, then after about 10-15 seconds, Dobbin says 'show me your ring and I'll give you some proof for Garadain' or some such, hand him your ring get some letter, take letter to Garadain, get 7th ring, easy.

The other way is to agro all the giants and Ry'gor before Corbin gets attacked, you could theoretically agro one onto him and kite away the rest to a dwarf camp or 2.

Ive yet to manage this solo, but have got all but 2 Ry'gor off Corbin.

Level is a problem with this, and could be a deciding factor in soloing it, due to the agro factor of dragging mobs off before they hit Corbin.

Bassically escort Corbin to Dobbins camp, Dobbin will despawn and say something about 'take this to garadain'

At which point you hand him your 6th ring

Dobbin takes your ring and gives you a note.

Ring 8: Garadains war.

Hand in the note to recieve ring 7 Ac7 +7str +7mr.

Garadain says something about the war with the ry'gor, and tells you to tell him when he is 'ready' you say I am ready, he says hand him your 7th ring a,d take the plans, and give them to Gloradin coldheart.

She spawns on a 24 hour timer in the hut next to GG's.

Once you hand her the plans she disapears 'to tell the Dain about the war'

At this point the Ry'gor fort depops and 2 Kromrif commaners, 2 Ry'gor oracles, the chief Ry'gor and Firebrabd the black pop inside the Ry'gor fort, a bunch of orcs and giants pop outside to.

What you need is Chief Ry'gors head, none of the giants and orcs see invis, invis yourself inside, harmony pull the chief and kill him.

At level 60 solo pull the chief, root, arrow, dot, kill etc :)

Kill the chief, loot his head, hand this in to GG, get your 8th ring Ac 8 +8str +8mr
