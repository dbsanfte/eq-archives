Samuqan
Best place for chain drops in Kael is in the Arena. As far as I know, the icehouse is basically only plate drops. I was there this weekend - nothing dropped! - and a guildmate said chain and leather can also drop there, but I've only ever seen plate, and many people I know refer to it as the "plate camp", so that seems to make sense.

I've seen chain drop the most in the arena, though. That's where I got my one piece that I have. It can also drop off lieutenants near the "bank" area (the ramp you go down coming from the Eastern Wastes side on the way to the Arena).

The problem is that the Arena mobs can be of much higher difficulty than other mobs. When I was there this weekend, briefly, I dumped all my mana trying to debuff one of 'em, and went basically the entire fight without getting slow to stick (with the lovely new agro, that made for some fun).

If you can get the forces, I'd suggest doing the left side of Arena. It's the best I've seen for drops.

Now in terms of the original question: which armor to shoot for and when to start hunting for it. Well, first of all, you have to decide which faction you want to be on. Basically it will come down to being on either dragon/dwarf faction or giant faction. Most common is dragon/dwarf. If you go for this faction, you'll have access to Skyshrine armor (necessitates strong CoV faction) and Thurgadin armor (necessitates strong Coldain faction). Coldain faction is the easiest to raise by far. If you want Kael armor, you'll have to be on giant faction, which is difficult to raise also.

When it comes down to it, you'll probably go with dragon/dwarf faction. And since you're likely KOS to giants already anyway, you have a good enough start. Even at your level you can start working on your faction. Go to Eastern Wastes or Great Divide and kill the easier giants and drakkel wolves. The faction hits aren't going to be that big, but, you'll probably get decent experience at your level and when you see how hard it is to raise CoV faction later in life you'll be glad you started early.

Soon enough you'll have very good faction with the dwarves in Thurgadin. I'd recommend doing the coldain prayer shawl quest(s), too, as it helps your faction and you get to do an interesting quest. Save the heads and toes from giants that you kill.

I'd also recommend against hunting in Skyshrine as that can make your dragon faction seriously fubar'd (my brother hunted there for a few levels and it took him a long, long time to fix his faction).

PoG and dorf quest armor are the easiest to get and will be your best shot. The SS requires hunting the halls of testing in ToV and Kael quest armor requires hunting anywhere else in ToV.
