Yesferatu
The reason a shaman should win this fight is time.
I have a druid, and a shammie, I love dueling with both of them.
The thing that pisses most people off is how long druids and rangers, and shammies for that matter, can last in a duel.
You're not in a hurry, so concerve mana. I would save as much mana as I could for heals and canceling their buffs.

With regrowth, plus our line of heals, we can regen mana much faster than a druid. With Canni 4 there is no reason for us to go out of mana. Basically, wait them out. Play it smart, keep a few slots open for their dots...if they get you...dispell them. Use as little mana as possible...hopefully, they will burn themselves out.

Once you get them low on mojo, cancel any damage shield they have ang charge in for melee. Yeah, it may sound a little odd, but if they are resisting most of your dots and they only do 60% anyway, there is no shame in using your advantages. Druids can't melee half as well as a shaman. So, burn them out of mana, just keeping yourself alive, and once they are oom, move in for the kill.

I have finished duels at near FM just from playing it safe and using canni. Once a druid is oom, they're not getting it back. Once we're out of mana, we're just a few HP's away from full.

Think long term.
