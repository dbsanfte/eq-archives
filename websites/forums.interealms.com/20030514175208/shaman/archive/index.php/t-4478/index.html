jraven1
I'm PLing my 35ish Enchanter with my 49 cleric! I'm doing Bloodgills, at the moment. I have the Cleric root, then land two of the 500+ nukes. The BG must stay rooted the whole time. As soon as the second hits, I have the cleric cast Atone and the Ench cast Mem Blur. Then I have the Ench cast Tash. If the BG spins and looks at the Ench, I know he has aggro. If he's still looking at the cleric, I just keep mem blurring and atoning and tashing (while it's still rooted) until the Ench has aggro. Then the Ench hits it with the level 34 nuke and the level 34 dot. If root breaks at that point, I just let him swim around until it dies.

I know it wouldn't work quite the same with a shammy, but it might actually be easier. If I was using a shammy, I'd just throw on one ebolt, which gets a BG down to flee mode by the time it wears off. I'd still root it and have the ench keep Mem blurring and tashing until it had aggro, though. That technique should work for any mob. BGs just happen to be weak, so I like them.

ludgurorf
I powered my chanter to from 1 to 35 in a few days. Key is the new rules on pets.

I pull with malosini line, my pet agros, chanter tashes and resumes medding. Wait until mob is about to death walk, then chanter stands and casts mez. This breaks agro and mob stands there. Chanter casts mind wipe. Starting around L3O I started casting mind wipe twice for safety. Chanter then goes and stands close to mob and nukes one, mob wakes up, gets hit and my pet agros. I back up and pet finishes off. Full exp. Less about bub of mana used. Usually can recover that during next pull.

Previously mezz would not help and the pet would finish the mob and there were lots of timing issues. Now it is so easy. Pets dont take exp now so the key is to have manaless ways to reduce the mobs hp's. Hence shaman pet. Pulling with malosi allows for mind wipe to stick easier. Probably will have to change tactics about in the 40's but not much. Toss in Jbb shots probably.
