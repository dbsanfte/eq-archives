Astroshak
Easiest way to raise forage is to simply forage, over and over. Don't worry about hitting some key like that in combat, just keep hitting it over and over while between fights. Be sure, especially if you have some stat food, to eat and drink what you forage, unless you're too full.

I eventually placed each of the three on a separate hotkey and mapped them all to the same movement key.

I anticipate that this will serve me well until I start running out of hot keys.
