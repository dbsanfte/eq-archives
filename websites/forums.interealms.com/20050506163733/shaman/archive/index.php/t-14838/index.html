ND3 is better. As a shaman, most of the damage you take should be self inflicted, LR doesn't help with canni damage or AE. Plus ND3 is 12 pts for improvement, LR costs 27 with CA3 thrown in.

When do you get the first tick increase with Q and scr?

SCR3 is 25% increase, Q is 4 ticks. 4 * 1.25 = 5 ticks, meaning you're guaranteed an extra tick at SCR 3. SCRM is a 50% increase, 4 * 1.5 = 6 ticks, so SCRM is another guaranteed tick. The way EQ works though, it's possible to see extra ticks before this depending on when the spell lands.

Hope that's not too confusing :)
