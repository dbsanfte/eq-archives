I really think using a JBB or not is a matter of choice. Using the epic as well.

The choice is :
- with JBB and a horse, casting epic the chain casting JBB, at level 60 you do about 50dps and gain 20mana/tick.
- without JBB and without a horse, you can do the same amount of damage, and canni the mana back.

(facts for dps calcul : epic is 80sec, add 9sec casting time, add 10 casts of jbb, thats : 1250 (epic) + 2 630 (jbb) = 3 880 damage in 89 sec, that's 43.5 dps)

Now another point :
- if you're fighting mobs one after another, the calcul above is true. And you'd rather been using the JBB
- But if you have, say, an add. You root/pet/slow/whatever him. If you keep solely chain casting JBB, you'll do damage to one mob only. Id rather been slowing/doting/rooting both of them in this case, and not use JBB, not to break root.

I'd say, until you have big dps dots (Pox and Bane and epic), jbb is cool for both soloing and grouping. When you have those big nasty dots and torpor, I'd say JBB is cool if you want to relax. After all, chain clicking a bracer is not that hard. JBB is 35dps, so it's not like you did nothing. You actually help your group chain-casting it.
