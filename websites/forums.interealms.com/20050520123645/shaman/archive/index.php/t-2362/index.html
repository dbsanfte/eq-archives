Gresgoth, great job for your first attempt. Since you are looking for feedback, I'll provide some. I don't know all that much about this stuff, but I have been making movies for a bit, so take the advice that I give you with that in mind.

It appears that you could be exporting at low quality from your initial capture device. So if you are porting to VCR from your Game PC, you might want to bring it into your capture card at like 720X480. Then use this to edit, that way when you are ready to clip down to 320X240 you will have less degradation.

Also, I noticed a black boarder around your film, you might want to edit this out. You can use Adobe Premiere and export the 720X480 movie that you have already captured and cut/trim the black border out. This is another reason to do your initial export in 720X480, so when you start tuning your film, you won't loose as much quality.

Also, I prefer full screen mode when shooting film. I think it looks so much cleaner /shrugs. To each his own I guess.
