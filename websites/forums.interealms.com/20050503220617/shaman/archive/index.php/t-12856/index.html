Zyphyr
Passive abilities (things like Mental Clarity, Spell Casting Mastery, extra stats) work when you delevel.

Abilities that have a button to activate (Mass Group Buff, Sprit Call, etc) don't work if you go below the level to activate. BUT, for the multi-level PoP abilities (like Spirit Call) they still work at full capabilities if you drop below the level required to purchase the higher rank (such as droping back to 62 after buying SCall 2) so long as you remain at least the level required for the 1st rank.
