"how u know wich giant is on wich faction?"

The giants that have long beards are on Kromrif faction.
The giants that have no beards and are in the green plate armor are Kromzek.
King Tormax, his 2 guards, Dlammaz Stormslayer, Frozen Jesus, Keldor'del Torek, and one or two other giants in Tormax's throne room are on Tormax Faction.

"Atmo am dubious to some and indiff to other giants in kael. how many dorf heads to get to amiable?"

Each dwarf head given to Capt Bvellos in the bank gives +50 Kromzek +5 Kromriff +10 Tormax faction (Riff and Tormax might be off slightly but the Kromzek is right). Takes 27 heads to go from Dubious with Zek to ally.

"am aprrehensive to dorfs atmo will one kil make me kos?"

No, a few kills will make you dubiuos, then threatening. Just buy the heads if you want to keep Dwarf faction.

"its amiable kaction for the kael armour turn in right?"

No, you need Ally to Kromzek. And at least dubious to King Tormax since our quest mob stands right next to a very mean Tormax faction giant.

I'll give an update on my progress since that post and share some thoughts on the process.

Since that post, I've spent about 5 hours killing in WToV with my guild, 6 hours killing mobs in Skyshrine (mix of solo & grouping). Also, I've spent several hours of "/auc WTB Coldain heads - 100pp / each" and slaughtering every dwarf I see to farm heads. I reached Dubious to Kromriff sometime during my last session in Skyshrine and since then I have turned in 36 heads to Captain Bvellos. I am now ally to Kromriff and Kromzek factions but Tormax factioned giants still scowl at me. At this point it's just a matter of acquiring enough heads to reach the dubious mark with Tormax - from what I've read I should be getting relatively close.

Here's what I've learned during this process:

To raise Kromriff faction, you have to kill LOTS of dwarves. Once you're dubious to the 'riff, you can give 15 to 20 heads to the recruiter near EW to reach Ally faction with them.

Raising Kromzek faction requires killing Skyshrine, WW or WToV mobs. When raising faction, the more mobs you can kill / hour, the faster your faction will rise. For this reason, I've come to prefer sacrificing exp & loot in favor of fast kills by slaughtering the greenies near the WL side of Skyshrine (Don't bother with Enots and Kcor at the entry gate because they have huge amounts of HP and aren't worth the effort to kill). I went from scowling to dubious in roughly 6 hours of faction hunting here.

Tormax faction seems to be best raised by turning in heads. You could try getting into a Dain raid (missed out on one last night by a few minutes :(), but unless you're trying to get your 'max faction beyond just non-KoS, it doesn't seem to be worth the effort.

Anyway, good luck with your efforts - as has already been said, raising Kael faction is a bitch.

Easy head farming:

Go down into the dorf mines in GD. Invis past the guards. In the first room there are three dorf miners, probably green to just about anyone 50+. Use paralyzing earth to root each of them in place, then sic your epic on them. PE usually won't break before epic is done (sometimes it will, just stick it on again, these guys don't hit hard) and then lay on the JBones when the mobs run low on HP. Multiple dorf kills. Very easy.

After the first room, then go hit the third room. Ignore the second room, as unless you want XP, the guards are /not/ worth your while. The greenies drop heads just as much as the blues. In the third room, there are three greenies on one side and on the other side are two greenies and one light blue who is as easy as any greenie. By the time you're done with these six little pricks, run back to the first room.

Easy peasy Japanesy.
