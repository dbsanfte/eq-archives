But I then went back to EF and did a few more Mammoths, and I swear the exp was just as good, with easier kills and better loot than Zelniaks.

Hollowshade Moor sounds interesting. Marus Seru isn't bad, but again, very little loot except for various Greyhopper hides that you can sometimes sell. And they're social, unlike the ones in DSP.
