Hail,

Add the Corrupted Elysian Elders in Umbral Plains for:
Death Mask of the Elysians & Death Watcher's Shroud.

Both generally solid upgrades for shaman in a guild of your capabilities.
Singe group doable, just use a solid pull team.

Tawro and Radir (along w/ the Swarmcaller you will take out enroute) in Umbral Plains should also be regular small raid targets as well. Single or two group this encounter to get the rather nice bracers that drop. It is a roll of the dice whether you'll get the bracer of sense or fortitude (the 2 most shaman would want), but these are good target mobs for your guild even if they don't drop. The other drops from these mobs are likely to encourage other classes to want to come back to hit these again and again. :) (I have the Pebblecrusher from one of these, which makes a nice weapon to use when soloing against casters or when trying to prevent mobs in ToV from gating.)

These are easy targets of opportunity for your guild, nice for backup plans when your planned spot is already taken, and relatively easy to check on w/ a ranger tracker. Nice quickies (or even just a full group w/ some time on their hands when favorite exp spots are taken) for upgrades. :)

If you have at least one member flagged for gaining entrance to the PoI factory, there are a few decent (though not spectacular) drops for shaman who don't have great gear that can be had with a single group or less. Polished Tin Mail Boots and Shirts drop off of the Tin Soldiers & Clockwork Golems, respectively. Golems can also drop the Tarnished Driveshaft, a nice 2hb toy that shaman who haven't found a decent big stick yet might like. Tin Soldiers can be SOLOED by a capable Oracle, and the Golems can be taken down repeatedly (& even for very good exp for those not flagged Tier2) & without pause by a single group in the 55+ range with one good puller.

Also in Plane of Innovation are the Manaetic Prototypes that would make good small raid targets using 2-3 groups. Cog Encrusted Spaulders (if memory serves) & some other neat things drop off of these.

That's all I can think of off the top of my head. Hope that helps. :)
