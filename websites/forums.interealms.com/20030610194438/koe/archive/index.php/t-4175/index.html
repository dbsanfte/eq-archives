Bemidian
You must start this quest with Indifferent faction. If you are less than indiff, there is only one way to raise faction but you can't be KoS (scowls or threatening). I was apprehensive. I had to do the very first turn-in to Pheno twice with the piece from the Coldain in the Thurgadin mine. I went up to Pheno, opened a trade window, had someone invis me (to make me indiff) and it worked. I was then raised to Indiff with geonids. I did it again without invis since I was now indiff. If you are dubious or apprehensive, then you are going to have to do the first turn-in as many times as it takes to become indiff, then one more time after that, then the rest of the quest.
