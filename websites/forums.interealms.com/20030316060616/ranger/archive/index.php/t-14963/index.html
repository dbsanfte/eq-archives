Okay I got flagged for POV a while ago, but it was after they had done some of the nerfs against quad-kiters, soloers, etc. Last night I decided to try soloing in there while LFG.

I was told by a few people I asked that "some" of the mobs were flagged un-rootable/un-snarable, and some were not (random for mobs even of a specific type, like the mushrooms in FG). So I tried. I tried:

- Planarian Larvae
- tree frogs
- undead vassal
- another type of frog
(all non-aggro, non-see-invis except the undead vassal)

Tried multiples of each target. I went 0 for 10. Every single mob I tried to snare was unsnarable, which meant I had to run for the zone each time I tried and failed (I was careful not to train the zone-in when I was doing this, though many of these mobs are non-aggro anyway).

At that point I got fed up (had also been LFG the whole time with no offers) and camped and went to a movie.

Note that this was on Wednesday, which was a patch day. It was at least 12 hours after the servers came up, though. If some mobs are snarable and some are not, then if there were a lot of people soloing only the snarable ones, eventually all the ones left would be unsnarable. But considering the servers hadn't been up that long, I'm not sure that's what was really going on this time.

So, was I just really unlucky? Is it worth pursuing this at all, or are they all going to be unsnarable? Are there any mobs in the zone that are always snarable? Being forced to zone out 10 times for every mob I successfully engage just isn't worth it. ;-(

My intent is to solo using root and shoot, so the requirements are non-casters, snareable/rootable, and non-summoning.

Eolair Strongbo
Were they unsnarable, or just resistent? I haven't ever fought the larvae or the tree frogs, though I do know that the majority of the vassals are all unsnarable.

Many rangers have claimed to be able to solo in PoV/PoS once you have endless quiver and AM 2 or 3. Probably AM 1 as well, will just take a while.

The razorgills in the caves are all snareable though, and there is a pretty decent kiting area just outside the caves. The Crystalline Golems are all snareable, though they do occasionally resist. The WL is a TERRIBLE place to kite though, so you'll have to drag each one down to the lower area.

In PoS, I'm pretty sure the majority are snareable. The Hurricane frogss, the bear-looking things *forgot name* and the tree's should all be snareable unless I'm mistaken.

Just realize that everything in those zones is pretty resistent, so 2 or 3 resists is not at all unusual.

Aegerenth
.. its all about size in Plane of Valor when you talk about snareable vs. unsnareable larvae.

Prior to the stealth patch, all the larvae where snareable. After the 4am patch, only the small larvae are snareable.

It's pretty hard to tell what is small and what is large at first, but there is definately a size difference between the two types.

As far as spiders and crawlers are concerned, I find more of them than less that are snareable, so I just stay away unless I am pulling for a group.

As far as EXP is concerned.
Golems are the best.
Fiends in the cave are next best, but
Larvae after that, because I can usually find the ones that are snareable.
Frogs are the same exp as the larvae.

I stay away from Vassels, Spiders and Crawlers, just because if I am soloing, its not worth my time breaking the skel houses with harmony (mana as well if i don't have KEI) so it's just easier to find something solo and pull and plink.
