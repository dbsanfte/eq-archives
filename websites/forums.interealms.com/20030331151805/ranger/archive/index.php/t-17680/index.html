odd combo spells and what lines they use:
Warder's Protection (rng/60)
Atk - CotP line
AC - CoE line
HP - SoN line (which is also brell's and BL's spiritual vigor)
DS - druid single target DS line

Force of Nature (rng/49)
Atk - Wolf Form line
DS - CoE line
Regen - unique line

Frostreaver's Blessing (ring 10 effect)
Atk - almost unique line
DS - CoE line
Regen - shm/dru regen line.

Chromatic Chaos (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=30387 effect)
exactly the same as frostreaver's blessing, but 5 atk/ds/regen instead of 10.

so basically you can have NP up with WP, and anything that gives the ds but not ac of the CoE line(ring 10/FoN). fortunately, WP's buffs are all on lines that rangers use, so it's a buff you almost never have to forgo: we don't usually MT for a group, so druid or mage DSes are unneeded; WP will always be better than a brell's or SV; and we've grown accustomed to Augment not stacking with CoE, so when it fails to stack with WP, it's no surprise. no big loss, either - it stacks with pure haste and VoG/SoV.
