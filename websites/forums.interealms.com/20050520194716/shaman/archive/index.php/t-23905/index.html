Loramin
Setanta, as I understand it, LDoNs have levels, each of which covers a certain range of average party levels. So which level LDoN you get is controlled by your average group level, but it's not like the game gives you an LDoN of level 37.25 if that's your average group level, it just gives you the 35-40 level LDoN (unless you pick hard in which case you get the level 40-45 LDoN, or something like that). It's been reported that LDoNs post-65 are actually the same level LDoNs given to 65s. Therefore, you are fighting the same LDoN you did at 65, but since your level is higher they're easier. SOE probably figures that if you do a LDoN of a lower level than you are supposed to, you should get less points for it. By the same principle, I'd imagine if you were exactly 6 (or 5, whatever the max is) levels away from every member of your party, and you got an LDoN of their level, you'd get less points than if you got the next level up LDoN with a group of people your level, although I've never tested this or anything.

However, the real question as I see it is why aren't you getting the 66-70 level LDoN when doing normals? SOE said they wouldn't add any new LDoNs, but the 65 hard level LDoN (which already exists) should have been the same as a 66-70 normal LDoN. Were you in a group with a majority of 65s or something?
