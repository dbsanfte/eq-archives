Gojirax
Run through almost any town inside Qeynos and look at those people that are talking your ear off as you walk by.

If you spend 30 minutes running through Nettleville Hovel, Castleview Hamlet, Oakmyst Forest, Baubleshire, Graystone Yard and even South/North Qeynos you will have more quests than you know what to do with. (You can either plough your way through them or delete the greens and move on to the white/orange/reds)

Another thing is, a lot of those green quests flag you to get a higher level quest from the same person. (So when you do your turn ins for quests, be sure and hail the person again to see if there's another quest available.)

Hailing the guards and NPC's roaming around Antonica is a great way to get quests from 13-20 too.

Kromnik
Prime Healer quest starts at zone-in to Vermin's. There's a "Brother" gnome who gives the quest. You kill 20 scorpions and 10 snakes (crypt lech, and something burrowers). Nice neck piece. Mobs are basically the first ones you come to when you go down the halls. Careful not to aggro the skellies in the alcoves. Shillers (bats) will also sometimes aggro.

BTW, don't try to solo it. These mobs are "group" mobs. I thought since they were blue I might be able to pull it off @ lvl 15...nope. You will need a tank, and possibly one other person to do it successfully/quickly.

There's also another quest killing the cubes down the hall from the mobs in this quest. It's just coin (silver/cp), but the xp is pretty good killing them. Inspect the box on the table as you go down the hall. This will give you the quest. I also received another drop for a quest while doing this, killing bloodsabres, that I didn't do because of time. You also will get a shot at some adept spells and advanced tradeskill volumes as drops, so this area is worth going to, and usually doesn't have the crowd that BB does.

I find the number of quests I can do that are orange or white or even blue decreases as I gain in levels. I'm now level 21.

I've done 186 quests so far. Trying to catch up to my friend who has 198. I've even done the courier ones.... anyhoo. Once you get higher in level, the quests that give real rewards tend to come from chests off of mobs (for example, a foul wind, freedom), or from saving distressed merchants and buying books... and the level 20 armour quests, which are a REAL pain in the rear, but will probably take all of my time until level 30.

I miss the days when you could gather 50 quests just by running through a zone. These days, they are further and further between and some of them involve either a) gathering 100 ores and lumber (These boots are made for walking) or b) finding a rare double up mob and killing it (the windstalker rumbler) or c) go farm these mobs until a rare version pops and then kill that (armour quest #1, outside qeynos creature quest).

I miss the "go kill lots of even cons and run around" style quests, those are quicker to do and less of a pain and frustration.
