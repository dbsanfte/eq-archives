Okay, since I have the day off and all the time in the world, let's revise!

To keep LDoN adventures from being double-rewarded, let's make the flag a purchased object. Sort of like, the "sneaky key" to planar access.

Now, we'd have to split the earned points into 2 pools - regular and hard. Shouldn't be a problem... it stays the way it is with normal adventure merchant purchases, and we'll just throw in one more merchant in each camp who's selling PoP access. You'll have to have sufficient points pooled from either regular or hard adventures to "buy" the flag. Considering they already have a system in place to "score" regular and hard wins and winning percentage, we're already half-way there.

So now we stand at:

Storms / Valor / Decay = 2550 Normal adventure points OR 1040 Hard adventure points

HoH / BoT / Torment = 2550 Normal adventure points OR 1040 Hard adventure points, with the pre-requisite Storms / Valor / Decay flag

Tactics = 2550 Normal adventure points OR 1040 Hard adventure points, with the pre-requisite HoH / BoT / Torment flag

Sol Ro's Tower = 2550 Normal adventure points OR 1040 Hard adventure points, with the pre-requisite Tactics flag

And finally...

Elemental Planes = 3640 Hard Adventure points, with the winning percentage kept in place, and the pre-requisite Sol Ro's Tower flag

I say this because if you start at level 50 in LDoN, by the time you earn 5100 normal adventure points you should've leveled quite a bit.
