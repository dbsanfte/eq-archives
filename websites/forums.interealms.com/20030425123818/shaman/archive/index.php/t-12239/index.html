Vikor
The CoD key can be done solo with enough patience. The various named larvas all have known placeholders and you can run around invis to find those. Search the Safe House for all the exact names. Personally, I invited a bunch of high 50 people, I set them up smack in the middle of the north section and I chain pulled all the larvas to them. We usually managed to gather 10+ larva skins of various type per 2-3 hours.

I don't see how you are going to solo the HoH key though. The Luminii Crawler isn't rootable which makes it pretty hard although possible. The prellium is rare as hell and only seems to drop from the 'broken prototype' mobs (not sure of the exact name but they are the thin humanoid ones with the circular saw). The difficulty is going to be the Archaic mobs you are bound to encounter; unslowable and unrootable.

Finally, the agony mephit might be the worse to solo. For one, you can get on the plateau by (1) going through the whole hobgoblin cave, (2)doing the die/pull corpse trick with a SoS rogue or (3) by using the cheesy camp trick. Even assuming you can get up, my understanding is that one and only one agony can be up at the same time and therefore if it's already up deep in, you need to fight all the way to it. The respawn is too fast for you to go deep solo. If the agony is not up then you can simply kill the few mobs near the plateau and hope it shows up with the repops. Also, the agony mephit is an enchanter mob and that makes it pretty painful.

Once you have all 3 items, the PoJ part is trivial at 65. I got all three souls in about 5 hours.

All and all, I wouldn't do the HoH quest solo but you can most likely pull it off with a partner or two.
