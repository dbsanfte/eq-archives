Zatriz
XP is pretty bad. Levels needed would be at least 45+ in my opinion. There are a lot of green mobs here as well, unless you go real deep.

Mobs will spawn randomly and out of thin air. Sometimes opening doors or walking in a certain area will spawn a mobs. Mobs hit hard and are very social. Slow works about half the time, as does Snare or Root.

Loot sucks.

Some of the mobs proc an illusion spell, turning the player into a random creature. Fun, but annoying.

45ish might be ok with the first few sets of mobs, but I would not try to go deep.
