Well those cultist bands are pretty nice.

5ac, 65hp (assuming 4hp per sta), 85mana (assuming 6mana per wis) and nice saves to the harder to raise ones.

regal band of the bathezid (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=12316) is nice :)
5ac, 105hp, 62 mana on this.

but so is that valor ring.
5ac, 97hp, 40 mana

Circled toe bones (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16637) are pretty good too, thats what I wear, in combination with bathezid.
toe bones = 6ac, 63hp, 85mana.

forgive me if my math is wrong, its late. I'd probably stick with the cultist band, unless your wis and sta are maxed, or you could get regal band. Or else I would do toe bones, or valor, depending on whcih you want more a little more mana or a little more hp. Really all pretty close.
