Well, if you want to just get back into the exp grindstone I would go to OOT & seafurbies (BTW, it's REAL EASY to get there now so they're camped more often. See note at bottom). Personally I would look for someone you used to group with & try to hook up. Otherwise I would go hang out in some dungeons & try to find people exping there on a regular basis, try to get in their group a couple times & you'll build up some hunting buddies. From there you can hope for a guild invite.

-- Easy way to get to Seafurbies: TD Firepots are now bindable. Bind there, take the firepot to E FP, you'll end up on the docks. At the end of the dock is an NPC who will teleport you to Sister Island. Short swim from Sisters to the seafurbies.

Good solo spots (if I remember correctly):

Burning Woods, to about 57. Stay away from Ancients and Wurms, but everything else should be fair game at 51. Plus, you're near Chardok.

Hole entrance, but I can't remember what level you need to be to solo pull the elementals. They start greening out around 55, I think. (20% exp bonus here-- level in 4/5ths of the time)

Skyfire, but the chromo's are a royal pain if you don't have a tracker that can pull around them.

Maiden's Eye, for when you get a little older, or get really well equipped. This is where I spent most of 56 and 57, mainly because it is so close to the Bazaar.

Wakening Lands, if you have your epic or a JBB. Sentient suits are decent xp, without too many HPs, and can be pulled solo if you know how. But they are totally PR and DR.

Groups:

Karnors--If you are forced to go to Karnor's, get in the Hands or Basement group. You can solo at the zone line while waiting for an opening, or just stick your head out into DL and kill some ragers. (Hands/Warlord camp is the best (non-AE) exp I've found from 51-55. Once you get it broken, and if you have a good puller, it's non-stop carnage.)

Velketors-- Gimp city, but you can almost always get a group.

Hole-- take a group there, still the best dungeon in the game. Stay in the pit till you get to 55.



Hope that helps. Welcome back.
