Weyalan
Ok, so you have all the quests from Allakhazams, but here are some quest tips dredged from way deep in the memory banks:

1. Do the initiate symbol of the tribunal quest - it involves heading to the bank in S Qeynos and back, but it is quite an adventure running thru Blackburrow at level 6. Assuming you are untwinked, the necklace is a good newbie piece, and the hammer it summons will allow you to start killing willowisps in Qeynos Hills soon.

2. Once you hit about level 8 or 9, the 2 of you should be able to kill the rabid bears and wolves in Qeynos Hills. Take the rabid pelts to the cleric guild in N Qeynos (the thing that looks like a flying saucer) and hand em to the lady in leather gear (cant remember her name) for great exp, gold and faction, and a spell if i recall.

3. Blackburrow will become a good hunting ground for you. Keep the gnoll teetha nd hand them in to the guard on the balcony in the arena in S Qeynos -he will give good exp, faction and moonstones. Take the moonstones to the pub near the docks in S Qeynos and give them to the barman in the back room - he will give you beer and exp. you can sell the beer or drink it! N.B. You can also hand the gnoll teeth to the lady in the Warrior guild (Margyn?), but the rewards, IMO are not as good as in Qeynos

4. In Blackburrow keep the scraps of gnoll fur that drop like confetti and combine them 4 at a time in a sewing kit. Once you combine 4 you will get a gnoll fur patch - combine 4 of them and you will get a gnoll fur bundle, combine 4 of them and you get a nice newbie cloak. In fact, the cloak stats are only good if you are a true newbie, but it is a good way of getting your tailoring skill up - from memory, I was at skill 30 or so by the time i completed the cloak, and it did not cost me a cent

5. Have a chat with the brother and sister who sit next to the entrance to surefall glade (names Miller I think), they can make you newbie armor such as boot, belt, cloak.

6. When going to Qeynos, always deliver mail to the bard guild. The guy in the bank in Halas will give you mail to take to qeynos - it gets you gold and exp and faction. Also, always kill a few snakes in the area out front of qeynos - keep the snake skins in the bank cos you use them as a component in your AC buff spell that you get at 9 or 14, cant recall exactly

7. Dont bother camping for the caster beads - I camped the goblin camp for hours and hours and never got the beads heh heh, only the broken ones

Weyalan
No need to hurry with the levelling - smell tehm roses bro.

You should now be able to kill will-o-wisps. They give good exp at your level, and also drop either a "burnt out lightstone" (destroy it), a "lightstone" (sell it to vendor) or a greater lightstone (keep it). When you have a few greater lightstones, head to the gypsy camp at the crossroads in North Karana. One of the gypst women, if hailed, talks about a "concordance of research". Give her a Greater lightstone and she will give you the concordance, and some exp. The concordance sells to vendors for about 9 plat, which is good loot for a lowbie.

Also, kill Bandits in the Karanas, and collect their sashes. The sashes can be handed in to a NPC in S Qeynos for exp and armor/weapons. The NPC is, from memory, located round the back of the arena, and is outside, not in a building, and has a barrow, but thats all I can recall.

Once you both reach about level 19, you will be able to commence the easier Totemic Armor quests. Totemic armor has been superceded by better / cheaper / easier to get armor these days, and you can buy Totemic in the Bazaar for lest than the cost to get, but if you are on a RP type server, you may wish to undertake the Totemic quests as a "rites of passage" thing. The quest givers for the quests are in Lake Rathe - 2 barbarians in the village half way down the West side will tell you about the quests. The ogres near the Rathe mountain zone will adjust your dufrenite, as required. From memory, bracer and gloves are the easiest pieces.
