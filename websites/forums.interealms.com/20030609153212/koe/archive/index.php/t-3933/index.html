Something I came up with ... i've suffered writer's and composition block for the last year or so, finally was able to spill something out...

_________________________________________________

The quarrel began like any other,
Although now I cannot fathom what it was about.
It turned into a reluctant challenge, in which I became the horrid victor.

I stood and watched the lifeless spirit fall to the ground,
and as I saw the light vanish from his eyes
I realized what I had done.

The heavens now cry for me and for what I had done...
which now purges this blood from my tattered rags
into the vast gutters below.

And although I stand erect,
and visibily unmarked...
i doubt the darkness of my scarred soul will ever be absolved.
_________________________________________________

So it ends...
and every detail hauntingly remains...
the sounds echo through my mind.
souls burning... passions seething... voices rising...

We drift on,
the sweet musky flavor of this ancient city
saturates my skin.

I gaze ahead blindly, lost in my thoughts.
The silver moonlight dances and flashes across the canal.

I lift the hazy veil from my face, raising my eyes to the purple dawn.
the stars wink at me and we share our secrets...

and as the memories slowly fade....
hot tears tingle my flesh
and a new day begins.
