I did the beach in gunthak until 44. Very good xp and constant pulls. I prefer the Mokon camp on the far left shore because he may spawn and you can get a charm or possibly the Bow (which I did not get). At 44, I searched around a bit and found a few nice, very uncamped area in Gunthak again. If you follow the tunnel all way to the town, but before you get to the Dulak zone, you can take a left and find yourself in an "open" area with mushrooms and drogmoors. This is heading towards the luggald castle. On the right side (pretty much anywhere) you can set up camp and pull. Sometimes you will get two, but these guys are not hard at all. I rarely get resisted, that don't have many HPs for stuff that is still DB, and they are all still DB at 46. No coin loot at all here, BUT, you can get some kinda rare, interesting, drops in the form of 'edible mushrooms' (potions basically). So far I've found there types, too bad they are Lore:
Penecillan spores http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=3652
Clarifying spores (will cast clarity on target)
Inigorating spores (will cast Fortitude on target)

Mages get the ability to summon haste belts and wpns that our pets can use. The girdles give haste and 250/500 hp's depending on which one you buy, and they stack with our haste spells. The sword and fists are wpns with procs. The sword proc doesn't work on lower lvl pets (think you need to get the 55 pet, but not 100% certain), but the fists proc for all of our pets. I'm only 52 so I use the fists - think it's about a 75 pt fire proc.

Lots of times people leave these on vendors in the bazaar. Just do a search on "summoned" and look for "girdle of" and "sword of" and "fist of". I may be a bit off on the actual name, but they all have "summoned" in the title. They are also no rent and can only be used by pets - forget exactly how they say pet only...think it's something in the 'who can use' part of an item popup. They usually sell for 10 to 20 plat each.

If you can afford it, it's well worth the plat.
