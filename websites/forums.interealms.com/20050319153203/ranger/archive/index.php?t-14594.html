Groups tend to be better generally, but if you want, I suggest getting Kei and haste, and am3/EQ is needed.

in HoH ~ Hunt the humans in plate (guardians), but avoid the 63+ Sentries, since they are immune to root/snare. Invis and run to a safe spot (some spirits see invis btw), then harmony if needed, and snare. Resists occur here and there on snare, almost never on root (our 63 one). Shoot arrows and use dots, as a nuke will break the root. Some of the rooms are large enough to operate in, as are the halls where they widen out.

I have however encountered some bugs here: pathing mostly, and sometimes, somehow, you can agro basement mobs who train the bleep out of you from nowhere. Have noted that if they are camped and being cleared, this doesn't happen. I also think agro might be bugged there - I have seen some of the spirits act very strangely (could invis and remove agro, but as soon as uninvis'd, they come after you, then invis and they unagro again). I had a guide test some things, and he bug reported it.

Parchments are now super rare drops off certain mobs only.

CoD is also doable if the zone in area is clear, as you have room to snare and root there, but otherwise, pretty hard since this is a dungeon (no harmony, etc.).

I highly suggest duoing over soloing. Many mobs which cannot be snared/rooted are mezzable.

At level 65 with AM3 and EQ with only KEI (purchased in nearby nexus) you can get about 1 AA every 3 hours in HoH. I generally harmony pull singles and kite them around the graveyard. They give 5-6% AA each and take about 10 minutes to kill with no haste. I cast drifting death as I bow kite them in a circle and refresh it as soon as it wears off. It adds nicely to damage but my low conjuration skill hurt at first.

This is a boring but effective way to solo for excellent experience. Can you get better exp. in a group? Definitely. I've got 1AA in 2 hour in PERFECT groups (warrior, cleric enchanter/shaman all present) in HoH where we chain pulled and had NO wipeouts. However, in a typical group in PoP you are going to DIE and have group wipes fairly regularly. It's a way of life in PoP. You get NO experience while you spent 30 minutes to an hour doing CR. And therefore, on average, "typical" groups in PoP yield much less exp than you can get soloing in this manner.
