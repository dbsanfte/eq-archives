Kusai:

a) Welcome to the crucible. :)

b) I think it's great that the monks have a nice upgrade list. Monk equipment is very one-dimensional. Ours is not.

c) Individual shamans have very different playstyles, and what may be 'uber' to one shaman may not be to another. Personally, I think Clattering Bone Necklace(AC: 8 STR+6 STA+6 HP+30) is better than the Ivory Imbued Collar (AC: 8 WIS+5 MANA+10 SV POISON+10 SV DISEASE+10 SV COLD+2 SV FIRE+2 SV MAGIC+2), for example. Many other shamans do not.

d) There are many pieces of equipment that cannot be worn by all shaman races. Jarsanth and Jaundiced Bone come to mind.

Combine b, c, and d and its tough to get a common 'upgrade path'.

e) Most shamans here at the crucible believe that mana replenishment is much, much, MUCH more important than a large mana pool. The easiest way to replenish your pool is to have HPs - a LOT of them. And have a high AC to protect them. Wisdom isn't a top priority around here. But that's for another thread.

PS - Barbed Ringmail is very nice - you traded 8 AC and 50 hps for 20 Wis. I'd be happy with either tunic.
Naagruz
Wud have to agree with Snap that there really is not a simple upgrade path for shammies. If you want to see a fine example of this just go to Bazaar and do a search of shammy chest items. You will find some that are very close, but have a tweak or two that set them apart.

You really just want to keep your overall profile in mind when planning upgrades. Ask yourself fabulous questions like these when considering a new bit of gear:

1. Will this item give me more casting powah?
2. Will this item give me sexy AC?
3. Will this item turn my resists into poo so I die lots?
4. Will this item give me more hp so I live longer than girly casters?
5. Will this item just make me look "special"?
