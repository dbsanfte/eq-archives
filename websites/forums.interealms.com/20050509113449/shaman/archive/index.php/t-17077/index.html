I have played on Vallon Zek since before Kunark. There is no longer any item loot, only coin loot, so everyone banks often.

You can PK people that do not belong to your team and they have to be within 8 levels. The teams are as follows:

Blue team: Human, Erudite, Barbarian, Frog, Cat

Purple team: Gnome, Dwarf, Halfling

Yellow team: High Elf, Half Elf and Wood Elf

Orange team: Dark elf, Ogre, Troll and Iksar.

Vallon Zek was all four teams against eachother for the first months. Then the "Light" teams banded together vs the "Dark" team (orange) because there were so many Darks back then.

Now that every high end guild is more or less 4-team ie mixing players from all teams the light vs dark thing is of the past.

Vallon Zek today is dominated by guild vs guild conflicts and this is troubled by the fact that /guildwar is disabled on VZ/TZ so even if your guild is at war with other guilds, you cannot still hit people of the same team... For instance, if an Iksar is fighting a Woodelf 1 on 1 and suddenly comes a high elf cleric who starts healing the Iksar, then the Woodelf cannot hit that cleric since he is on the same team. This is called immortal-healing and is a large problem.

In mass pvp 4 team guilds are benefitted by the fact that they can employ shamans of both Blue and Orange teams wich means we can cast malos on all kinds of players. 3-team non orange guild can never cast Malos on a player of the blue team since barbarians are the only "lighty" shamans.

High end pvp is dominated by 2 groups of guilds that fight eachother. Every guild is usually alone but there are occasions when members from guilds in the same group join eachother for raids. The strongest guild on server at current is Defiant (time flagged) and their #1 competitor Ancient Dawn (2/4 elemental gods dead).

With the current imbalance between classes most 1 on 1 pvp can be tipped to one persons favor depending on what class you play, but when pvp really shines is when large guilds face eachother ie over 100 ppl fighting for zone control. Lately the competition has been harsh and there have been occasions where people train eachoter on purpose and refuse to abide to Loot and Scoot. When you die in pvp you will leave zone for 1 hour but sometimes people Bindrush and get back in fight after they have died. It's kinda sad but the constant fighting have left very little love between some guilds and officers are hard strained to maintain the fragile balance.

I dont have a good view of lower guild pvp since i only play my lvl 65 main, but there are still large alliances of guilds who either run pure-team ie only orange or 3-team ligthy vs darkies.

I dont have a great deal of info on Tallon Zek but it should be similar.
