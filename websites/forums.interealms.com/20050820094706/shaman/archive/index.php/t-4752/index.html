Hello Everyone, I got a quick question. Is there a command to turn attack off. Basically, the reason why I am asking is that I recently got a horse for my shammy. I duobox the shammy with my monk and alot of my macros go like this

/target Ryuodin (My Monk)
/assist
/pet attack

or

/target Ryuodin
/assist
/cast 1 (slow)

My goal would be to be standing 100% of the time and not be autoattacking. The reason for this is right now, as long as I am not moving and I don't have autoattack on, I med while standing with my horse (in outdoor zones only of course)

So, I have tried /attack off and it didn't seem to work. Is there anyone out there that knows how to either target my Monk's mob without turning on autoattack or to turn attack off immediately after assisting my monk.

Thanks for any info, I appreciate your time.
House
I'm not certain if this fixes your problem:

In your eqclient.ini file, setting AttackOnAssist=FALSE will stop attack from being turned on when you /assist. Or at least I know it did a while ago (these things have a way of breaking over time).

However, /attack off should work in your macro; perhaps try a short /pause after turning off attack.
Jaruden
if you do /assist off, it will turn off the autoattack when assisting feature. This is pretty much a permanent arrangement, and saved per character I think. Anyways, that should do what you need.
