Savvik
I did them with my Bard for a few levels using the damage shield. Since Shaman and SK's don't have that, I suggest buffing to the max, slow and just tanking them.

When you enter the room, go to the left. This will be your pull spot. work clockwise around the room to get singles. In the corner farthest from you there will be 2 and unless you root one, they both will come. If the gueen is up, might be a tough fight, she is lvl 27 or 29.

If you get rooted and dotted entering the room, camp out to clear aggro. If you don't, anytime you are in line of sight of the plant things, they will root and dot you. You can avoid the plants by hugging whichever wall looks farthest away from the plants. For the last one (on the roof in center of tunnel) duck and hug the wall. Shrinking might help...

Good luck and let me know how it works out. I also partner with an SK and we were thinking of coming here also.

If you group with others, you could do Dawnshroud, but if you had trouble with Marus Seru, you can't do Dawnshroud. Zelniaks hit alot harder here than in Marus, the lightcrawlers blind you, and don't even get me started on the wolves...

Did you try Netherbian Lair? If you can pull singles, you can do very well there. If you get an overpull, root one then run to the guards. When the guard has aggro on the add, loop back and finish off the first target.

At 24 (my current level) I can solo the drones and warriors either straight up melee ( pull with a slow ) or root rot. Melee ends with about 20 - 30 % hp left and root rot ends with about 20 - 30 % mana left. With the 25 SK, I slow, lvl 19 dot and we melee them down. Adds are either rooted or trained to the guards.
