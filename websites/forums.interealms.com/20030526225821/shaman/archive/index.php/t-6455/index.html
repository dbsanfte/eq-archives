Tarthug
Poisen is faster and usually has a higher DPS depending on level of the spell. Poisen resists can be debuffed with out MalXXX line.

Disease DoTs are usually more efficient, longer lasting (almost 2 minutes) and DR resists can be debuffed with the decay line. The irresistable decay debuf we get at 53 or 54, a great pull spell (very fast cast) and helps us land our DR dots.
