Dretch
Do you have Luclin, do you have PoP?

If you have PoP, get to PoK and then hit Feerott, via the portal. Kill the Lizards and Froggy Tads for Netted and Mesh armors. (yeah they suck, but are free, and fill up those slots) You can get a full set in about 30 mins of exp'ing.

Now, if you have Luclin, and PoP, get to Shar Vahl (sp). Hunt in Shadeweavers thicket (after soulbinding in city), and then at 9th (sow) head to Paludal Caverns, and hunt at the Shadeweavers zone area. I soloed a Mage from 7 to 12 here in about 2 hours. Oh btw look for the armor quests in Shadeweavers; some of it is pretty nice for lower levels. Then with all the plat you will make ;) goto the Bazaar and get some upgrades. Saw a Rygorr Mail for 25pp the other night...there are many deals to be found.

Orgak
Don't give up! I play a troll shaman on Zeb and I love it.

The posts from others here are solid information. The only thing I might add is that SoW potions are not as profitable as they used to be due to the bazaar. What used to sell for 100pp is now selling for 70pp and even lower. Not to mention, there are hundreds of them for sale at any given time. Tradeskill loot is your best bet early in the game.

One of the reasons I think that Trolls make the best Shaman is their innate regen. When you get Cannibalize, your main source of mana will come from wincing. With the built-in regen, you'll be able to generate more mana. If you stack your bear form, with your regen spell, and your innate regen, you're getting a lot of hp per tick. Because of this I am hardly ever ever out of mana. My hp bar climbs very rapidly.

Moving on, you're probably a follower of Inny. (I think you have to be, forgive me if I'm mistaken on this.) If you are, then you need need need to do those Symbol of Innoruuk quests which take place mostly in Neriak. This is a neck piece that casts darkness as a clicky effect when worn. It is a soloers dream! Look up the quests and do them all, the last one is called a Regent Symbol of Innoruuk. I'm level 51 and I still use mine.

On Zeb, Troll Shaman are rare. I've met a lot of folks who didn't even know that Trolls could be Shaman. It's a pretty cool feeling to know that you're not like everyone else. Not to mention, the Troll Shaman is the most despicable, hate filled, evil, monstrous, vindictive, and downright nasty character in the game (according to Verant). To me, that's pretty cool.

In short, imho, shaman are arguably the most versatile, independent, and capable characters in the game. I've played a Pally, a Wiz, a Cleric, and some others, and I've never been as satisfied with my play experience as I am with Big Green. After learning from the veterans here on this board, I was able to equip my shaman in such a way that makes him as hard as a cockroach to kill. And if I don't want to group, I can solo just as easily and do quite well. Personally, I think that the Shaman is way undderrated. They are much more powerful and versitile than they get credit for.

So, play this guy out for a while. I think you'll be quite satisfied once you get to know him. If you're on the Zeb server, give me a tell. :)
