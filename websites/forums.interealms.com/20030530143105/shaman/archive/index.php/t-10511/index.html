Hail,

I try to tactfully mention how to correct the misbehavior that is causing the problems. (Druid snaring for example).

In extreme cases, I give them my 30 minutes notice. I have a "thing" to take care of, and would like to leave as soon as possible. I can usually stick it out with any group for 30 minutes.

In very extreme cases, I'd tell them, "I don't feel soandso is safe to group with", and explain that's why I'm leaving. That gives the rest of the group the opportunity to boot the idiot and replace him. As a shaman, this can work to fix the problem sometimes, as usually we're a key ingredient to making the group a success.

Guild business is usually an acceptable excuse to leave a group, but I don't use that one unless it is true, because it happens alot to me when rare mobs spawn and we are racing for them. Your morals may vary, and you could invoke that to get out of a bad situation if you wanted.

In any case, limiting your groupmates with those you know or are from respectable guilds may reduce the frequency of your bad experiences. If they get you repeatedly killed due to their stupidity, a respectable guild will usually bail your dead group out w/ a CR & rez.

I never group with someone unguilded unless I already know them or am really in dire need of their services. If I can't find a group of people I trust to group with, I'll just solo or duo somewhere. :)

Take care,
