Drienne
The Essence Tamer that drops the stone is on the 5th island. The easiest way to get it is just to buy a multi-quest for the stone. It tends to drop so frequently when you go to PoA that most guilds that have been there more than once or twice have at least one sitting around. Barring that, find some friends or a good guild, and you can take care of this in a few hours. 3 groups max can get to the 5th island and take care of the tamers for you (though now, with added levels and spells you could probably get by with 2 solid groups). As for VSR, I did it with 15 level 50-60's, including a 60 warrior tank. With the proper people, it's a 3-5 person fight, but 2 groups of moderately equipped 57+ should be able to take him without incident.
