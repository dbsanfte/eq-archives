Chutoi
Actually, take a bard, not a chanter. Much like the execution trial, this one ends up being a massive dps fest. Go with a warrior/sk, a bard, a shaman/druid, a cleric, a rogue/wizzie/ranger, and a rogue/wizzie/ranger.

The little ass mobs seem to memblur themselves occasionally and just rush right to the middle. A good bard can mez a mob, do melee, and also sing fire resist (which cuts down on your damage). The bard can also gate mobs (highsun?) back to their spawn point.

At any rate, the secret on this one is that I think you have 1 minute to kill any mob that gets into the middle. Don't be afraid to change targets. That's the whole secret. :p

It took us a while to figure it out too, sadly enough. Massive dps, have the shaman offtank one of the four spawns and nuke, one or two bard mezzed, and then everyone go massive dps on the first couple.

Good luck

Chutoi

P.S. Even bard gate won't reset the 1 minute timer. It just gives you more room to move around (ie, doesn't drag offtanked mobs into the middle when everyone rushes in).
