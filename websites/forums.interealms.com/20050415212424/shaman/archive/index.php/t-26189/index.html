XeroOmega
is it normal to mash the canni 4 button constantly?

yes, yes it is.

this is one reason why I keep my HP regen up, it helps counter the effects of canni.

the need to canni can be offset by a number of things.

how active you are.
how much FT you have.
how much Regen you have.
how big your manapool is.

a shaman is a busy class, we do something ALL the time. learn the canni dance if you don't have a mount. /snicker

if you find yourself running a bit low on mana, lighten up on DPS and stick more to healing for the next fight, or until you get the hang of how much DPS you should be doing. for healing, stick to HoTs, they are very mana efficient and you can negate damage done to melees by casting at the right time. and it really doesn't matter if a melee is at full health when you cast it.

FT isn't overly important to me. I went from FT1 to FT15 over-night when I started raiding. I didn't notice too much of a difference because my mana comes primarily from canni.

Regen will off-set the damage you take from anything. the more you regen, the less you have to heal, so I keep a regen spell going on myself at ALL times.

and having a larger manapool...that really speaks for itself.

its not uncommon for me to blow ALL of my mana on a single fight. most fights I sit around 20-40 mana (pained memory has spoiled me and made me lazy!) but I can regather 20% of my mana by popping off canni5 and pained memory over 3-4 seconds. if I start to find myself in a mana crisis, I stop actively DPSing and let Dog/Dots do damage. this gives me a "calm time" to regain mana. I still cast spells, but its more on the healing side, as it still provides group benefits and its much easier on my mana, allowing me to recover for a kill. one fight is usually more than enough for me to get back to a managable position with mana.

As a shaman you should be casting nonstop if you want to play the class to it's fullest. How much of that time is spent canning depends on what you are casting. Just malos and slow can be taxing when you are killing and pulling fast and you have low mana regeneration (and no clickies to help out). What will never change though is the nonstop casting.

Some things that may help you out:
- only cast disease debuffs if someone is asks you to/complains about resists on his disease spells
- don't buff pets
- get a horse
- get torpor
- cast your second-highest malos line spell - usually it will be enough (used to work for me back in Velious anyways)
- get canni V (duh :) )
- get FT items (duh)
- get mana preservation (duh)
- get spell haste (more time to canna in) - this includes the cleric buff
- get clickie items for anything you cast regularly - root, slow, malos, damage spells, haste, anything really

I hope some of that helps you
