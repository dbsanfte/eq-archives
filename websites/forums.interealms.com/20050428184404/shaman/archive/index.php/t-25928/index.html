Now that my crew and I are PLing up dps melees (rogue/hunter) to replace our pallies we've discovered to our delight that paladins are the ultimate melee PL class if you 2 box. Blessing of might on a rogue is insanely good. Heals + rez are also fun. Pull too much? Bah, just shield the class being PL'd and use that time to bandage up.

The best part about the healing is that if the rogue pulls 5 (which I do intentionally, saves pull time), the paladin will get heal aggro nearly instantly. But who cares if the paladin is getting beat on by 4 lowbie mobs? After I finish my first mob in 6-8 seconds I simply turn and backstab one of them off the paladin. Rinse and repeat and watch the exp/loot roll in.

So far I've managed to burn through an entire fresh application of poison in under 10 minutes, a personal record. I'm going to Stockades next to see how big of a train I can pull. I start to lose track of who I'm fighting once I break 6 mobs or so, but since they don't last very long I guess it doesn't really matter. I also give up any finesse required by my backstab build and just spam sinister strike and slice and dice. That lets me clock over 40 dps at L23- not bad eh?

Paladins rule: they are PL gods! That was why I leveled one up- so everyone else could benefit. I knew there was a reason.
