It sounds like you want to go somewhere where you can set up without roamer concerns, and have a series of mobs to take out that are drk blue, and if a loot drops, that's okay, but not necessary.

I'd say Veksar...

Coming right out of the water tunnel, set up in the corner. No aggro/roamer concerns at all. One mob in that room with you, and 4-5 in the next room. Animist/Feral Lord spawn for possible loot, if you can kill quick enough.

Another spot might be the Undead blacksmith, upstairs from the clerks. 3 spawn, with a safe med camp spot upstairs around the corner. You should be able to pull all these single, and the repop will be about right for root rotting, if you don't want to worry about healing doggie. Grab some pet weapons from the bazaar if you're going to use dogdog. It will really up their dps, and are better than the weapons you'll take off the mobs.

You'll need IVU potions to get there.

A camp where safely AFK tanking dark blues at 65 isn't easy to come by. You might find a 1-3 mob spawn somewhere, but I can't think of one.

PoNightmare INSIDE the Hedge is absolutely free of trains, and you can take it as fast or as slow as you want. All the static spawns are easily single pulled, and you can generally stop wherever in the hedge (outside of spawn points) for a break as long as you need. The experience inside the Hedge on trash mobs is much better in my experience than fighting mobs out in the rest of Nightmare.

Drops from memory are blood raven parts, ribbon chokers, and maybe a few other unimportant bird/nightstalker parts that you can either vendor or bazaar vend, depending on whether they are tradeskill components or not.

With EP gear you might be able to afk a blood raven once slowed, but I wouldn't try it. They are trivial fights for me on my mount though tanking in the face - I can easily read a story to my kids, feed the baby, or watch a movie while killing there, though I can't completely ignore what I'm doing or I will die. My gear is fairly good for being outside of the EP's, so you should have an even easier time of it.
