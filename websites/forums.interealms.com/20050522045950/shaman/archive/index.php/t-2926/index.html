Alminah
It took us 3 rubies for one hoop and something like 3 stacks of rubies for the next...

She is on the same spawntable as the nobles. Bring a tracker and kill them as they pop, it has never taken us more than 15-20 mins to make her spawn. There are 3 nobles that spawn pretty close around Guk entrance, kill one and next pop in 7 mins or something the like - just concentrate on killing them and all the froggies on the island in the meantime.
The stupid red bard is not on same spawn table, but you should kill him anyway just because!

We used a level 1 troll mule to do the actual hand ins to Lynuga since we didn't want to loose our precious faction with the trolls. A shaman can buff him well enough not to have to worry about the froggies, and then sit there letting the pet kill the froggies all around.

You can minimize the cost buy running back and forth selling all those stuff you get but since it's lore it's just annoying. We found that the time it took was not worth it, compared to killing some HGs after instead for the time you 'gained' by just destryoing the stuff.

If it's true that your faction with her gives you better rate for hoop to drop I believe troll mule is better than wolfform. Just, you can't do it solo then.

After a while she despawns, so don't count on sitting doing hand-ins for hours and hours once you made her pop.

Good luck :)
