Ragnorokk
I have come to somewhat of a delemia with the current loot in luclin regarding items with flowing thought.

It is conventional wisdom that shaman are in no need of flowing thought items. With todays mana regeneration buff spells combined with our own cannibalize powers, FT is somewhat of a waste for us. Not that it isnt useful, but compared to other classes such as clerics and druids that have no natural mana regeneration other than sitting, we have little need for it.

Ofcourse I totaly agree with this. It is even arguable that, even if we had no special means of mana regeneration, clerics would benefit more from flowing thought items because it is far more important for a cleric to be able to regain large ammounts of mana on a raid than for shaman to do so.


In the beginning of flowing thought, there were two issues. To begin with, most flowing thought items had a low FT number (1 or 2). And all items with flowing thought had reduced stat bonuses compared with other loot from similarily difficult encounters. In other words, to get flowing thought you had to trade off stats.

This was quite perfect for shaman, because we were more after gear with pure stats and were thus content to leave all the ft gear for clerics, druids, and the like. But luclin was to change this...

Today, the best gear in the game has flowing thought. To clarify, the best shield in the game that a shaman can get is the shield off the high priest. If this shield had absolutly no flowing thought at all, it would still be the best shield we can get. This will remain true thought many different examples, and espically ture in vex thal.

So, to get to the issue at hand. For purposes of argument assume the only loot we are considering is luclin loot. If you are a shaman, and are looking to upgrade your gear, your going to have to make a choice.

Take items with flowing thought over clerics and druids, or never upgrade certain slots.

To get to the examples. If you want to upgrade your chestplate in luclin, there are only two bp's that even drop that a shaman can wear. One has FT5, and the other FT7 (burrower bp, mail of judgment). You may want these items >Even if they didnt have flowing thought<. But they do, so because they do you have to deal with the above issue.

Now me personaly, I wouldnt take an item with FT over a class that needs it more than me

But it is a source of frustration to know that certain slots just wont get upgraded because the best items in the game (stats wise) have large ft also.

So my question is, is FT a gift for us? or a bane.
