The hide is used for the following:

-------------------------------------------------
Fairy-Hide Mantle

Properties MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM NO DROP QUEST RELATED
Armor: Shoulders
Armor Class 9
Skill Mod None
Boosts STR: +5 WIS: +12 SV FIRE: +10 SV COLD: +10 SV MAGIC: +10
Effect Guardian Spirit with 2 charge(s).
WT: 2.5 Size: ?
Class SHM
Race ALL
Obtained From Quest (L50)

Description:
Talk to Gina McStargan, who will ask you if you are ready to take the test of Sight. She will ask you to retrieve three items for her to pass this test.

- Phosphoric Globe
- Sphinx Hide Light
- Damask Mantle

These items can be found in the Plane of Air as they drop off all the different monsters which inhabit this place. Hand all three items back to Gina and you will be rewarded with the Fairy-Hide Mantle.
