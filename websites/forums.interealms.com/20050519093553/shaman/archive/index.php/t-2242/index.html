I've since restarted on a new server, but for levels 45 through 57 on my old one I generally kept the dog hasted, pulled with slow, and tanked the thing while chain clicking the JBB. it's pretty easy to channel, despite the long cast time. Occasionally on tougher things it would help to throw in a Dot or two. A weapon with bigger damage helps, as you get in in a swing betwen JBB blasts, but it's not a huge concern.

I would be buffed up with the usual hp and ac buffs. Occasionally could cannibilize between JBB blasts too if the mob wasn't doing much damage. I did have a fungi tunic. Would have been faster with the epic Dot, but I didn't have one. Damage shield potions would probably work well with this method as well. If I ever got low on hps, was easy to root and step out to heal (or just channel a heal) - dogdog does not take hits well but can certainly fill in long enough for this every now and then.

Did not use any other debuffs except on real tough stuff with high resists, in which case I might have used a Mal*.

For a level or two, before I tried the above, I simply rooted and nuked with the JBB. Not nearly as effective, but if your ac or defense skill is not good, might be safer until you can raise them.
