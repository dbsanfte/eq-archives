Megumi
SCR1: 5%
SCR2: 15%
SCR3: 30%
SCRM: 50%

Base time for Torpor is 4 ticks. Notice that to get an extra tick, you will need at least 25% extra time on the buff. (4 * .25 = 1.0) So, you'll see the first extra tick at SCR3. SCRM will give a second tick (4 * .50 = 2.0).

SCR1 and SCR2 do nothing to help Torpor.
