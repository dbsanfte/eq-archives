My spellbook is a bloody confusing mess to anyone who looks at it until they figure out that I am the queen of hotkeys

I have my book broken up into 10 sections and I have a hotkey for each section. All my frequently used spells are on the first page of the section.

Section 1: Heals
Section 2: Buffs
Section 3: Vision (invis etc)
Section 4: Roots
Section 5: Cannabalizes
Section 6: DD's
Section 7: DoT's
Section 8: Utility spells (gate etc)
Section 9: Summons (pets etc)
Section 10: Slows/Debuffs

Mind you I have a hell of a lot of spare time and I figured out exactly how many spell gems I would need in each section if I had all my spells and was level 60, lol.

Shandy Orcbane
I'm not as ultra-organized as Our Lady of the Hotkeys, but I have mine similarly broken up, and can switch spells out in barely more than the memming time.

"Main" -- pages 1/2 My "general purpose" spells, such as Root, SoW, Insects, Chloro, Cann, Main DoT & 2ndary, 1 DD, and Alacrity.

pages 3/4 -- Bih'Li, Bear, current Pet.

Buffs 11/12 -- Current stat buffs, Talisman/Shield, next page holds resists/rage, further pages hold progressively weaker stuff.

Heals 21/22 -- Yep. Heals & Cures.

Combat 31/32 -- 2nd best DD, DoT, Debuffs, Slows.

Misc 41/42 -- Gate/Bind, Shrink, Sight, Invis, etc.

It makes for a few minutes of swapping and thinking when a new spell rank's achieved, but it's worth it.

/hotkey Main /book 1 is the quick 'n dirty way of making a hotkey.
