Cragael
Let's see...

- Earring (no one is sure if it's either mana cost or aggro reduction effects, but only for DDs anyway). Those will work with all our casted DDs, whether poison or cold, but not with the JBB. Doesn't work with our Rain spells or DOTs in any way.

- Necklace (chance of not using a spell component). Basically only worth it if you're casting Avatar, unless you're expecting to run tight on scales for EB in an underwater raid, for some reason. Doesn't work with the Imbue spells.

- Mantle (chance of extending the duration of a buff cast). Plays merry hell with buff timing. Used to be bugged to and applied only of buffs cast on self. And the best one only buff the duration by 10%, which mean you would have to be extremely lucky to get an extra tick out of torpor with it.

- Bracer (chance of DD crits). Works with all our DDs but not the JBB. Doesn't work at all with our Rain spells or our DOTs

- Ring (fast cast). Works with all our spells with a casting between 3 and 9.5 seconds. Works great with Malo, Torpor, slows, DD, etc.

Usually, in raid, I only bother with the ring. The proc rate on the mantle is too flaky to make it worth dropping the stats on whatever else I am using. I don't DD enough (myself, I like using torrent of poison or JBB if I need to do damage) for either earring or the bracer to do me much good. I sometime get a necklace if i am going to be casting Avatar a lot, but that's about it.
