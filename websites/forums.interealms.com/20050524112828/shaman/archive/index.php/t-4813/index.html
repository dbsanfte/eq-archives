Chutoi
While it's not completely true, consider the counters to be a mark of the 'severity' of the spell. There are poison counters, disease counters, and also curse counters. The various cure spells remove a specified number of these counters when cast.

Each poison, disease, or curse has a set number of counters when it hits a player or pet. For instance, Bane of Nife has 10 poison counters when it hits. Cure Poison (level 20) cures 1 poison counter when it hits. Thus, you'd have to cast Cure Poison 10 times to 'cure' the spell. Each cast would remove 1 counter. However, Disinfecting Aura removes 20 poison counters and 20 disease counters, so it would cure Bane of Nife in one cast.

Just an FYI, Remove Curse removes 8 curse counters.
