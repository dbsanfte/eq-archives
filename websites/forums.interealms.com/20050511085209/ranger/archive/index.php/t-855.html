"Yous have a sticker on chest dat say, 'Hello! My name is Smellybreath' with words 'A.A. Meeting' at bottom."

"Ok den. We will not forget. Us is gonna train now for few years and we'll bash yous good!"

"Good luck with that." Smellybreath chuckled to himself. "I'd like to see that happen. Yeah right! Take care, you little kids! I am sure I'll never be seeing you ever again!"
