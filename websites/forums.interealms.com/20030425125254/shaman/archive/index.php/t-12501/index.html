Schpleef
At 42 you could get a head-start on coldain/dragon faction by hunting the zone lines in Kael. I'm not sure if zone line mobs drop the giant scalemail armor, but I think I remember them dropping it. Most pieces have decent ac and some hitpoints, some even have a few wis. If you want to quest armor, you could look into the twilight sea class armor quests; they're generally suited for roughly level 45ish soloers/groups. I'd also look in the bazaar; you can modify your search by looking for (for example) only troll shaman items with +hitpoints, and search for each slot. This will show you the best "bang for the buck" items you can get, assuming you like gear with hitpoints. If you don't, go make a druid :D Some suggestions off the top of my head are... coterie general cloak, dusty soriz boots, sarnak battle shield - all under 500pp and have 40hp each.

Shand: I've bought and sold SBSes for 700-900 multiple times, seen them for even less sometimes. The trick to bazaar is, go there during prime time, check prices, then hit it during off peak hours, when more afk mules are there...then check during prime time again. Only buy if it's a really great deal (unless it's a really rare item that you hardly ever see in bazaar). Just don't be hasty and buy something the first time you search for it.

Certainly at 42, you need to upgrade from totemic. Assuming you are wearing totemic in the slots that you can, here are some basic / cheap suggestions

Head: Metal linked crown
Chest: Tin mail shirt
Arms: Jaundiced bone (bazaar 300 plat)
Wrist1: Jarsarth (free, loot in KC)
Wrist2: Coldain Military (quest)
Hands: Leaf scale (often free loot in PoN, but level restricted, so buy in bazaar 100 - 150plat), crystal chitin gauntlets (600 plat, bazaar)
Legs: Jarsarth (free, loot in KC) or Kromriff military (quest)
Feet: Jaundiced bone (bazaar 300 plat), wolf fur bootie-slippers (epic quest)

Here are a few other cheapish upgrade "guesses"
Ears: Earring of twisted bark (250plat, bazaar)
Face: Crystal spider Eyes (300 plat, bazaar)
Neck: Imbued Ivory Collar (250plat, bazaar)
Fingers: 6Ac65hp
Shoulders: Elder wolf hide (hard camp in Hollowshade), tattered mantle (200plat bazaar)
Back: Wet Thorn Cloak (500 plat, bazaar) Totemic cloak (quest), Acrylia studded cloak
Waist: Chitinous belt (500 plat, bazaar)
Shield: Sarnak Battle Shield (600 plat, bazaar)
Range: Beginners magic manual (quest, buy the required baked item in bazaar, 150 - 200 plat)
