Gloriana
The better focus effect takes precedence.

For example, before I got my haste of mithaniel item, spell haste III (10% faster) affected torpor. Now that I have haste of mithaniel (20%) it affects the casting time. They do not stack, the same with affliction haste and spell haste, the better effect (affliction haste) takes precedence.

This is true for mana preservation (10%) and affliction efficiency (20%) items as well.

Focus effects CAN stack with AA skills. My quickbuff 1 skill stacks with haste of mithaniel. These also stack with the cleric spell casting haste spell. In addition, my extended enhancement IV pottery shield thing stacks with SCRM (although EH IV does not work on torpor due to the duration of the spell).

I have also heard (although I don't have it myself) that SCM will stack with mana preservation/efficiency items.
