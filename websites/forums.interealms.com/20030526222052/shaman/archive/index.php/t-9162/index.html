Ancere
Simple rules for slowing in HoT:
Note- It helps if Bards OOS, Enchanters Tash, and other mages/shaman try to debuff along with you, but this is the patten to follow even when you are the only debuffer on the raid.

1. Malo.
2. Malosini. Cast until it lands. Use your entire mana bar if needed.
3. Turgurs. Cast until it lands. Use your entire mana bar if needed. (Pray that you have a good tank that can outtaunt you, most of the time not a problem.)

For the MR mobs (Shimmer/Glimmer Drakes) just insidious decay and Disease Slow.

In any case, watch how you agro on the raid. Different tanks and mobs will handle your debuffing differently in terms of you getting summoned. Pray that you have high hps and a good healer :)

Mala (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=1772&source=Live) Magician 60, unresistable Magic/Cold/Fire/Poison -35
OR Malo (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=1578&source=Live) Shaman 60, unresistable Magic/Cold/Fire/Poison -45, low agro
OR Malos (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3395&source=Live) Shaman 65, unresistable Magic/Cold/Fire/Poison -55, low agro

Tashania (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=678&source=Live) Enchanter 44, unresistable Magic -33, high agro
OR Tashanian (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=678&source=Live) Enchanter 57, unresistable Magic -37 (L57) to -41 (L65), high agro
OR Howl of Tashan (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=3342&source=Live) Enchanter 61, unresistable Magic -48 (L61) to -50 (L64), high agro

Let's say you have a 60 Bard, 60 Enchanter, and 60 Shaman with Malo. 10 OOS + 45 Malo + 38 Tashanian = 93 unresistable Magic debuff :)
Add Malosini which is resistable but overwrites Mala/Malo/Malos because it is better, and/or slow with Turgur's.
