So there I was, in the Plane of Nightmare when a 65 enchanter walked up and asked to join our group. Group was then a 61 pally, and a 61 shm (me). She was invited, and then proceded to charm the next Hobgoblin that we pulled. Impressive I thought. She hasted it, and next thing I know she's adding a good 300 dps to our group. Sheesh!

Later on, she ditches the Hobgoblin and goes in pursuit of a more suitable pet. She tracks down a Horror Nightmare! He cons yellow, a level 62 mob. She goes on about how hard, and dangerous it is to keep a beast of such power tamed. The risk is soo high (yeah right).

As we are pulling more Hobgoblins, the poor suckers found themselves unable to mitigate the damage dished out by this horse. He was hitting for max damage pretty much everytime, dealing a good 560 each hit, slicing and dicing away once every second. As most shamans could tell you, our pets are not known for having a great deal of hit points, so when the mob enranges (hobgoblins enrange), one usually spams "Pet back off" a couple times. Didn't have to bother. Seconds after it enraged it was dead.

Pulls were pretty easy. We would pull a hobgoblin. I would cast three spells sequentially. Malo/slow the mob, then CH on the paladin. By the time the CH landed, the goblins was dead. 15-20 seconds tops. Eventually the paladin learned to just back off, and let the Nightmare do the tanking. Then I would just slow. Eventually I stopped slowing too. Basically we were just a couple of leeches.

To sum up.. enchanters make the best duo partner hands down after 64 and a few aa's into domonance. Keep up the good work Sony.
