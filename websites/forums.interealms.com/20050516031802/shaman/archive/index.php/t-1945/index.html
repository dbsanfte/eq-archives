Go to Katta after the patch. Best way to find them up I think. There was a long thread about the Cloak of Truth -quest at the Safehouse, you might want to do some searches there.

Word of warning about the quest, just in case you didnt already know this:
Do not help anyone completing his Cloak quest if you still want to work on yours.
Aborgast and Miller are on "the truth" -faction, and completing the quest involves killing them both. When you kill Aborgast you take a negative "the truth" faction hit, and another as soon as Miller bites it. (Of course you can go around this with mem blur or /q) Those 2 hits will lower your faction enough to get your turn in items eaten, or enough so that the quest givers wont talk to you anymore, at least without sneak.
I helped a guildmate to complete his quest while I had the whole cloak done all the way to final turn in, which I never was able to do. My items got eaten and the GM's told this is done on purpose to prevent farming the quest...
