veeshanMurtak
As it seems the cross-linking has started - let me summarize the gist of what shaman want and why we want it.

In GoD and to a lesser degree in LDoN and PoP shaman have been severaly nerfed in several areas.
- We have received fewer and lesser upgrades then every other class to every single one of our spell lines.
- Slow mitigation was implemented - just the very least form of slow mitigation halves the effectiveness of slow, our main ability.
- Fight durations are a lot shorter then what we have been balanced for. This matters a lot when you spend 650 mana per MOB before even casting the first heal.
- Mana regeneration for everyone has gone through the roof. Yet shaman mana regeneration has been improved to a much lesser degree. In addition the nature of our mana regeneration forces us to spend time doing nothing else. This time (aka downtime) used to be plenty and has decreased steadily as mana regeneration has gone up.

This is why we want adjustments. The biggest part of these adjustments should be for those who actually experience the problems - meaning high end PoP players, possibly the people who do LDoN hard adventures and especially those in GoD.
- We do not want adjustments for lower level shaman, or at least not above minor issues, such as our stats buffs being obsolete because of stat caps.
- We do not want CH, druid CH or anything close to it.
- We do not want to heal as well as clerics.
- We do not want to heal as well as druids.
- We want to outheal paladins.
- We want to be a better choice over a cleric for a group that already has a cleric and that does not already have a slower.
- We want to be able to heal as well(factoring in our slow) as a druid who does not have a slower in his group.
- We want to be able to patch heal well enough to get off another slow after the first one was resisted.
- We want to be able to outdamage a druid in a 30 minute nonsto fight.

In short, we want to have some situations where a shaman would be wanted. Currently we do not pull our weight in the GoD/LDoN enviroment.
We heal less then paladins, buff less then beastlords, have our slow mitigated and get outdamaged by every other class. That is not how things should be.
