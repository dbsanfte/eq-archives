Also, historically, Harmony was changed from:
10-21 11:05 Changed Slot 2 from "Reaction Radius(15)" to "Reaction Radius(15/40)"
10-21 11:05 Changed Slot 1 from "Frenzy Radius(15)" to "Frenzy Radius(15/40)"

As noted above (Harmony effect that was changed) there is a reaction radius and frenzy radius.

Level puller/splitter to lvl of mob has an effect too.

Take pulling the Chief camp in ME.

A lvl 60 can single pull if at full spawn (without use of HoN), but take a lvl 55 and he will get adds.

Thus the splitter should be the lvl 63sk instead of the lvl 55 warrior (still dependent on lvl of mob and type of mob status). Some zones can be worse than others.
