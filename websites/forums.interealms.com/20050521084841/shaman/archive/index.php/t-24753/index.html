Bloodfields isn't that great of xp. I get 3% regular per hour in 67 duoing my monk and shaman. Easy, though, and very little chance of death. The named in the 2nd field wander so the ranger would be good there to grab them. Items don't sell well but could make for good tribute.

You could try the ZI in the Ruined City of Dranik. I can't quite duo there yet. But the XP should be better and you possibly can take on some of the easy named. Dragorn Laborer could provide some good loot and runes if you can take him.

Other option if NC is full is to grab someone to get you a DS expedition. They can leave right after spawning it. I haven't done any of these yet, but from what I've read the 66+ ones can spawn named that drop runes.

Good Luck,

Lately (at least since I read the RCoD thread) I've been farming RCoD. The XP is fairly nice (3% aa per kill) and I can tank most mobs reasonable well there. But one thing that makes it duo'able is my shaman has Breath of Trushar + Yoppa's + Qvic arms (45% heal focus) + most healing AA. Landing a 5.5k crit heal can make the difference between having to pop WS or saving it.

You may want try easier mobs in WoS as they dont do too much DPS. But remember to Cripple asap as it does help reduce the procs/triples.
