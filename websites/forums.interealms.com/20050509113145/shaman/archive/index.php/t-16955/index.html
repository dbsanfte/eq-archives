Jaruden
RC (radiant cure) is an AA ability on a timer (3 min?). It does a group "cure", and has a chance of affecting things other than poisons/disease. With level 1, you'll occasionally get other things like Malox. With level 3, you'll get it pretty often, but it's never guaranteed.

If you're fighting something that ae's more than once per 3 minutes (which almost every one does), then the AA is only going to benefit you once, and Nadox will come in handy for those other times.
Steelbear
Radiant Cure 1 removes 9 poison counters, 9 disease counters, 9 curse counters, and gives 1 chance to remove a detrimental effect. The last part is what removes Malo/Tash spells, but it's random so it doesnt always work. Can only be used once every 3 minutes.

Blood of Nadox removes 18 poison counters, and 18 disease counters. Wont remove curses or detrimental effects, but it can be used as often as you have mana to cast it. It's worth getting.

No, you can't cast Blood of Nadox on other groups. I have tried, you can target someone else, but it will cast on your group.

It is WELL worth getting though, whether or not you have Radiant Cure. If you get hit by AE, it can cure the whole group in one cast. I didn't use it much before I joined an elemental guild, it's useful for some elemental encounters. But it's also useful in LDoN now.

A mob AE tashes, Radiant Cure will take it, the mob does it again, well, you've used Radiant Cure already, if you don't have Nadox, you'll either have to cure each group member individually or wait for RC to pop up again. Initially, I also used it for curing after an AE poison trap goes off, but I haven't come across those recently (either it's a Mistmoore Catacombs thing or they've removed it).

As for elemental encounters - there are some fights where people are getting dotted and are dying left and right, but I can keep my group alive, thanks to Blood of Nadox. If you're in an elemental guild or if you aspire to be, the spell is incredibly useful in those encounters.
