I am really liking Freedom Force. If you liked any old Stan Lee or Jack Kirby comics, you will like this. Very campy and over the top, though.

Nice things include being able to pick up or destroy anything in the game. Building in your way? Crush it, but you lose prestige points, which you need to recruit new teammates.

The missions are short and straightforward.

I don't like the AI, which won't use your team AS a team....so you have to make each one do it's own thing. I usually just run a mission with a single character, and let the others stand around and spectate.

You purchase power and skill upgrades with points you get after leveling (each mission is worth a set amount - heroes that stay behind get a portion, too - since you can have a max of 4 heroes per mission).

It is a nice change from swords etc. There IS a half naked woman with a bow, though!!!!
