Chain drop rates always sucked in there.

Both Thurg Quest MQ's and Tae Ew Legs are good.

Balanced gear is always good (HP/Sta/Wis/Mana/AC) but so is gear with an assload of HP :)

At level 60 the Thurg Legs will give you +45 hp total (+15 hp from the +5 sta) vs +72 for the Tae Ew.
However the Thurg Legs would give you about 200 mana vs 48 mana for the Tae Ew.

Guess you have to ask yourself if 152 more mana is worth 27 hps less...

Overshadowing all this of course, is the fact that when you get Cana 5 you have to deal with the sudden loss of 1924 HP's every 3 minutes.

But then by the time you are 60+ and have the AA's for Cana 5 you may well be in a raiding guild and get phat lewts anyway!
