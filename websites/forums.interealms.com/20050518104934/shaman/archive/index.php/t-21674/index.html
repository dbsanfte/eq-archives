Luclin's Arriana
I took Air Superiority with my Controller, just to give some extra punch. My Controller is Earth/Forcefield. When I do bosses, it is a combination of Fossilize, Force Bolt, and Air Superiority.

Fossilize locks them down (albeit, briefly), Force Bolt knocks them off their feet so they aren't hurting me, and Air Superiority applies the hurt back to them (along the with occasional knockdown which is nice).

Slot up accuracy. Any offensive power can miss. Accuracy is king. With debilitating effects, it usually takes 2-3 applications before they will start landing on Bosses...that's one of the perqs of being a Boss. Avoid melee range (especially Skull bosses...Shadow Maul is just EVIL) while you soften up their resistance so you can keep them under wraps. Some Insight inspirations wouldn't be too out of line either...Skull bosses like to throw out debuffs from the Defender Dark line, which really screw with your accuracy.
