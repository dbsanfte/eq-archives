started building up fund to buy at L51, got it up to 13K but could not find it for sale. I think people in nfp learned i was getting desperate and tagged me for a sucker. The prices kept going up.

3 months go by with no success getting it even though I have money to buy it. One work day I did not go into work right away due to a morning doctor appointment, and on a lark I logged on for 30 minutes to check. Put out my normal hotkeyed /WTB JBB message and got a tell from a player from Japan. He sold it to me for 10K with no haggling. A real gentleman. Had worked up to 27K at that point. I suddenly had an extra 15 K over what I had expected to pay, and instead of blowing it on other gear, i went back to leveling. I was extremely grateful and figured the extra money would help my spell fund someday.

A week later got my first crypt group, and won a jbb. that day I traded it and my extra money for a fungi.

I am 59 now, and that 2nd Jbb is the only 10K + item I have ever won a roll on, and i got it after I really did not need it. Game is ironic sometimes.

What I learned was to check the market at different times.
1> Servers can have different economies at different times of the day. This knowledge has served me well ever since.

2> Use a mule on key purchases.

3>I also came to realize that never, never, never tell someone what you are willing to pay for anything. This continues to irritate me to no end. People /auc saying they are selling something and then they say, what are you willing to pay, or make an offer. Screw that. Asking someone to Make an offer is saying to them, "maybe you am really stupid/desperate and will wildly overpay".

It is a totally different matter however to say, I looted something and I have no idea what this thing is worth, and let them make an offer. I usually offer to get a a price check.

If I walked into a store and they did not have prices marked I would walk out. Can you imagine going to buy a car, and having the salesman say, make an offer, and I will tell you if I will sell or not. This is absolutely nuts but Eq players have allowed sellers to get away with this forever. After 3 months of getting jerked around on my Jbb, I now steadfastly refuse to play those mind games anymore.

Today when someone says make an offer, I have a hot key, that says, "/R Give me a tell when you figure out what you want for it. or get a price check and then we can talk.". 80% of people will be forthcoming and tell you. Others I refuse to do business with.

I challenge people on this practice all the time. (now i use mules to buy and sell for me) I have actually had people say things like, "you are a shaman, you guys overpay all the time for key stuff" and " maybe you will overpay, it cant hurt to let you if you want to." So, apparently the shaman community has a reputation of being desperate and it is a sellers market for jbb, fungi, malo, torpor, pox, etc. As long as we are willing to state our level of desperation by naming our own price, we deserve to get slaughtered in the marketplace.
