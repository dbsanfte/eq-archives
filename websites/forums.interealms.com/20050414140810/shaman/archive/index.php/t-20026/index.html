You have to edit the table into a single line of HTML code. For some reason the board adds line feeds after the [/tr] tag if you don't.

Build your table in your WYSIWYG HTML editor, then go to "code" view. Place your cursor at the begining of the table (just before the [table] tag) and hit the [END] key. Then hit the [DELETE] key one time to bring the next line up to the current. Hit [END] again, then [DELETE] again.

Wash/rinse/repeat until the entire table is one single and continuous line. Copy that and paste it into your post.

Works for me.
