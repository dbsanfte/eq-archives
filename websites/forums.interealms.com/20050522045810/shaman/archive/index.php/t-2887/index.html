Bigguss
Well, I'm glad you said "I'm inclined to spend it on a small number of quality items that will last me into my 50s, rather than spending a few hundred here and there." because that's where you'll get the most bang for your buck.

Get yourself an Iksar regen BP. 5 hp/tick = 3000 cann'able hp/hour. No other item in this price range will help you anywhere near as much.

Other items which would help you similarly (alternatives)

Fungi staff - prices vary widely... you might find one for 5k. This is another regen item, you need to be level 35 to use it, so that's perfect. It has right-click regrowth (15 hp/tick = 9000 hp/hour) but with disadvantages. It overwrites SoW and Regen/Chloro/Regrowth spells, it snares you (60%, I think) and you need to reapply it every five minutes. The effect shows up as a spell buff (so you can click it off if you need to get rid of the snare), and it stacks with regen items (iksar bp, fungi tunic, etc.) Definitely a nice item.

Save up a little more and get a horse. They go for 8400 for the cheapest one- and allow you to meditate while standing up. If you hunt outside a lot, this is a huge savings. There are tons of horse threads... I'd dig up the details but I'm going to be late for work! :)

Just remember, HP or Mana regen, and mana-free damage > All !!
