Juff
At your level, I doubt you'll be able to clear the cave of mobs - you'll need to play this one without your pet. Invis up, head to the back of the cave where he is (PH is a sonic packleader), and position yourself so you are right next to the rock that sticks out and the back of the cave. With the right positioning, the only mob that aggros will be Ghowlik. I'd estimate his HPs to be in the region of 1600, one Bane kills him.

He will run when low on health, and he is highly resistant to root, so you'll want to finish him with a nuke. Otherwise, looting the body will aggro several wolves, if he goes too far.

Will be difficult solo at 34, and more than one person may cause issues with adds. The Elder wolf hide is either an uncommon or rare drop, and the PH seems to be up 75% of the time. The mob is on a 12 minute cycle.

cattywumpus
With the group you mentioned you should be able to pull out of the cave with no problems at all. If you could find a cleric it would make it almost too easy. I can solo the wolves at level 24, just not quickly enough to empty the cave.

There are three wolves in the entrance tunnel and two more inside that you need to take out to be able to single pull Ghowlick/Packleader so you need to be able to kill 6 wolves every 12 minutes. Should be cake with the DPS of the group you mentioned.

Sonic Wolves hit fast and run at near SoW speed, as the groups shaman you're main duty will be slowing. The respawn rate in the cave is pretty fast so you must be in a mana conservation mode and work efficiently if you want to pull from outside.

Most of the wanderers in the area come out of the cave so they should not present too much of a problem. If they do just move up on the hill and pull back to there.
