cattywumpus
At low level a Ceremonial Iksar Breastplate would be a huge advantage and it will last you well into your 40s and beyond. I would start with that and then just go to the bazaar and look for deals. If you're patient you can find some great equipment for bargain prices.

A couple of Velium Fire Wedding Rings would be very nice, or you could get one and do the PoP Collector quest for the Fine Antique Ring with Mana Preservation 2. Very easy and you can buy all the needed items for a song in the bazaar.

Jaundiced Bone items are a bargain for straight AC gear but you may want more stats at the begining of your career. JB will come into it's own as you level up though so it's not such a bad idea for the medium to long term.

What you should actually get depends a lot on what is available on your server and for how much. There are a lot of minor variables between server economies that are hard to predict untill you actually see them in action.

And yes you can solo all the way to the 50's with purchaseable equipment. My first Shaman went to 51 with 90% solo and small groups. My guild was more social than raid capable.
