Kazim
Somewhere easy to get to makes sense. Start with echo caverns on the easy side, as a previous poster suggested. Mosquitoes hit super fast so you optimize the use of your DS and get lots of defense skillups.

Once your defense is maxed for each level, I'd approach it like this:

- paladin pulls with something fairly high agro like a stun

- shaman casts a couple low level spells on it to keep the casting skills up. dots/nukes/slow/whatever.

- shaman starts beating on the back side of the mob. hopefully it turns to the shaman for those nice defense skillups. Use low level heal over time spell from the paladin coupled with regeneration spell to keep shaman health topped off. If defense is maxed, a couple quick low level stuns chain cast from the paladin will keep the agro on him where it'll do minimal damage.

- at some point, the mob will be below 40% or so health, at which point if the shaman's defense skill is already maxed out, the paladin can turn on auto attack. If you find that the paladin KS's the shaman, then you can do one of two things, either wait longer (til 30% or less health) or equip a lower damage weapon on the paladin.
