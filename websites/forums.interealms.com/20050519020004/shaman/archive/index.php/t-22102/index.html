Landragoran Heronwing
The cool thing about the Master Key Quest is that all parts except for one are droppable and can be gotten by a SoS rogue. So have the rogue go to each wing and grab the piece, then you have to go and clear Drusella's room or something to get the last piece. Alternatively if you don't think you can solo that room or duo it or whatever, you could just pop on a Quiescence, run thru stopping in the far left hand corner and picking up the bag which is the last piece, and then running thru the far door, then next door, then across this other room infested with golems and such and thru a last door and then zone out a portal to the outside of HS. Of course that's assuming you have the HPs to make such a dash and you don't get stunned a lot on yer way there. A well-equipped shaman can solo the room without too much difficulty if Drus isn't up. If she's up, you'd better be really a good player to have a shot at pulling that off.

The zone itself is home to a few higher-selling drops. Like Sash of the Dragonborne (usually i get two to drop in 4 hours), Steel Hilted Flint Dagger (procs 650 DD, ranger/bst), and Adamantite Club (procs 60dd/6sec stun (worth bout 8k)). There are some other items that sell for less.

I love HS, it's a great place to duo and solo. I love to solo here, it's always quiet on my server. Only problem I have is splitting some of the rooms where the mobs are dark blue. I'll 2 box with a rogue sometimes, but I think I'll switch to 2 boxxing with a necro, so the necro can split.

Once you get the master key it's real easy. I usually zone in, kill ZI mobs and then invis past the golems, usually S wing. In the room behind the golems I summon, equip and buff pet, then just start pulling and crawling. It's actually quite fun :)
