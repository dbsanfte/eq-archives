BlargX
Monk quest gear is not so great.

You need to get some levels. No-drop gear is higher end stuff, in general.

You shaman is BARELY old enough for TOV; any younger and the AOE spells would wipe her out constantly. If you play so sporadically and are low level, your chances of getting no-drop loot aren't good. A few levels might help a whole lot. Without them, you might put in a hundred raids without getting a drop.

Monk quest gear is wanted almost entirely for the spells they cast -- very low level shaman spells. Your shaman can already cast them and better, so there's not much point to even the main virtues of no-drop monk quest gear. My monk would feel guilty looting stuff like that when it is such a huge upgrade to the other leather-wearing classes.

Your standard store-bought monk will be wearing things like hardened grey flesh sleeves and pants, and barbed dragonscale boots, and perhaps a shimmering terrorhide cloak, spider fur belt, or suchlike, depending where he keeps his haste item. Go for the lightweight hitpoint items and you'll be wearing mostly the best stuff you can get until you get up to Ssra level and VT loot on him. It's nice that you can get good stuff that way for your monk, but he's also very strictly limited; you won't get much above 2500 hitpoints on your monk without GOOD no-drop gear -- stuff above the level of HOT/kael. And that will make him a very poor tank in PoP. His damage will remain nice, but not his tanking.

You might want to try to get into raids in Plane of Growth, too. The people who go there these days are usually raiding Tunare, so they are fairly high end people, many of whom only use the PoG armor drops for their twinks or let them rot. Your chances of getting an item or two aren't bad there because of that.

However, PoG raids can last for hours, even all day. So if you go, be sure to let people know right up front that your time is strictly limited, or else they'll see you come in and maybe loot something and seem to disappear WAY too quick, making you look pretty bad. PoG is not a place for 30-minute raiders.
