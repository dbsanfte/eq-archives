Its roughly 1.5 bubs of HP left. Runja
Low health aggro is a percentage of your total health. As soon as you drop below 20% health (one bubble), you automatically become the most hated target for all mobs that have you on their aggro list.

Even dogdog obeys this rule. Try it. Go to a place with lvl 35-ish mobs (so dogdog doesn't kill them too quickly). Pull two. Tell him to attack one while you attack the other. Epic yours and nuke it down. As soon as your mob drops below 20% health, dogdog will run over to finish it off. Its fun to watch.
