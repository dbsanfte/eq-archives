Was thinking that with new expansions in the works it would be nice to see what people consider a good expansion, and why they think that way. Heres my take on it from the ones we have now:

#1 Kunark in a landslide. Great content for newbs starting out here and also lowbies who want to come and xp all the way up to level 65. The best dungeons in the game imho, really good storyline, lots of zones, great quests for the time and even today in some cases. Lots of good loot that still stands up today.

#2 PoP, probably contreversial placing this high but I like it better than velious. No dungeons but plenty of good xp spots. Some good quests, makes race a non factor ( a good thing Imho) and makes travel trivial for antonica, and kunark, and to a lesser extent the other expansions. I like the progression here as well. Good mix of droppable and no drop loot. Spell acquisition I like compared to kunark and velious also.

#3 close behind PoP. Has 2 good dungeons (velks and cc) and a few that are a bit more high end like sg and dn. Lots of raid content, some good quests and storyline. Most of the best gear is outdated now or no drop, with a few extremely rare and pricey high end items as the exceptions.

#4 Ldon nice concept but not being able to use your points anywhere is kinda a pain. 5 new zones for $30 kinda sucks also. Items are rapidly dropping in price due to so many dropping its disgusting.

#5 Luclin sucks imho, mobs are to hard for the crappy xp, few droppable items of note, rare spells, time sinks, crappy quests and not enough decent dungeons besides paludal. Was a real pain in the ass moving around when it first came out also. And lastly kitty's just suck.

#6 Loy was fun for a while but its not a great expansion. Some decent quests, a few decent xp spots but not alot there really. To few zones, a pain in the ass moving around between zones if you don't have invis and lack of a decent theme/purpose for the entire expansion made it almost obsolete within a month or two of release.

1) Velious

An expansion with lots of interesting raid content. No need for keys (except for ST) or flags. Very well tuned, the toughest mob of the expansion (AoW) wasn't killed until just before Luclin was released. There were some serious problems with mob resists which have been addressed since.

2) Kunark

Sebilis. Chardok (even before the revamp, it was interesting). Howling Stones. City of Mist. Nurga. Even Karnor's Castle holds good memories for me. So many wonderful dungeons.

3) Luclin

Okay, so it brought on the dreaded Emperor and VT key quests. Other than that, I thought this was an okay expansion. Really needed more dungeons, though. A coherent storyline would have helped - the whole Katta vs. Seru thing was never really explored. Beastlords came in with this expansion, though, which is a definite mark against it.

4) Planes of Power

This is the first expansion I really disliked. Bland, unoriginal zones with insane xp modifiers. Getting xp is completely trivial, increasing the number of high-level imbeciles significantly. Flagging and backflagging are a total nightmare and a massive timesink. Tons of artificial content blocks that have nothing to do with skill. Wretched itemization before elementals. Slow mitigation - a basically good idea that is implemented poorly and used far too frequently - is introduced.

Some of the encounter design is decent, at least. PoP's content has been thoroughly explored, though, and there's a long wait until the next raid expansion. Poor design overall.

5) LDoN

Some people really love this expansion. I personally don't. The dungeons have no personality. The normal difficulty missions are too easy. The high-risk missions are almost worth doing, but are still pretty dull. Whenever I do an adventure, I feel like I'm doing the executioner trial in PoJ - OVER and OVER and OVER again. Ugh. Don't even get me started about how stupid it is to have a leaderboard in a game like EQ.
