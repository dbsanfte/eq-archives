Today you can enter time a few different ways.
*Kill all tier 1-3 planar progression mobs and get their flags, then go kill the 4 elementals.
*Kill the 4 elemental bosses (killing each one gives you a key to their zone), combine their 4 items into your time key, enter through poi.
*Get gimped in with people who can already access time.
*Obtain the alternate key via joining a time raid and looting the key from phase 2 or 3 (I forget which phases it actually drops).

***the way you use the alternate key (looted from various Time mobs) is to have it on your cursor and click on the Time dial.

I still want to kill Fennin Ro to finish my original PoP progression, but I don't need to because I looted the alternate key.

You no longer NEED to kill the planar progression tier 1-3 mobs. You no longer need to kill the 4 elemental bosses either. But you still can. Conceivably you can get level 45 toons into Time by going with a Time raid and looting the alternate key.

As for your problem with P1 where some people can't zone into the instanced zones, everyone must be off of their mounts. No one can be on a mount inside the instanced zone. As soon as one person summons a mount, it will block anyone else from entering that one instanced zone (but will not affect the other instanced areas). So have everyone zone in without a mount and do not summon the mount until everyone is inside. Try that and see if it fixes your issue.

We do, however, have some difficulty zoning into the p1-p2 parts, but we solved that. Since the instance is limited to 3 groups, we discovered that if people only click once each, they have a better chance at getting in. If all 18 click twice, only 9 or so got in, and you had to wait for the instance to realize that those 18 clicks did NOT = 18 people.

There is also a separate known issue with zones freezing/crashing. It appears to do with buff slots. In Qvic, Natimbi, etc.. if you have full buff slots, and an npc AE's something, it has been known to cause the zone to crash. There is an eqlive post from the devs about it.



So for us, the /ooc /shout when raiding is: "Make sure you have at least FOUR buff slots free", "make sure you click any instance or zi stone only ONCE". "If you don't get in, wait 30 seconds, and then click ONCE again". Thats kept the majority of our raids crash/bug free.
