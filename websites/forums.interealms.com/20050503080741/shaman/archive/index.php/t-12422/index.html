Jorlaan
keep all spiderling silks you find. sell them in bazaar. You'll afford your spells in no time at all. (assuming FV works like most servers).

They sell for even more if you save a little, buy a sewing kit and combine them into threads yourself.

They sell enormously better if you work tailoring skill a little bit and combine the right number of threads with low quality cat/bear/wolf pelts to get leather paddings.

I'd just stick with selling the silks or silk threads though as you need more skill in tailoring for the padding than you can train at the trainer.

I've seen many of the quest armor pieces for sale on vendor on my server for only a few silver each. I don't have any of hte armor for my baby warrior yet though as I really haven't been playing him much.
