Qhan
I am currently creeping out of another EQ downcycle, and during my downtime I've played some other games and done some thinking on some fun new ideas for EQ. I'm looking past fixing quests and balancing classes, and really just thinking of the "fluff" that they like to add with every expansion. I'm interested in hearing other people's ideas as well. Try to keep ideas at least mostly within the constraints of the EQ engine (ie, no flying, no removing zone lines, etc).

Player Housing
- DAOC's implementation of housing is nice, imo. You can purchase tradeskill items (kiln, etc), you can sell your wares, have a teleporter, bind point, etc. It could also serve as storage, and the way they use trophy items is also pretty cool (be nice to have a Vulak head gracing your mantle). Not only would it be another way to spend time, it would also be a way to spend plat and get some out of the economy.

Guild Vault/Guild Housing
- Similar to player housing, but for guild members. It could have private meeting areas that only guildmembers could access, guild vaults, etc.

Visually Magnificent Armor (Epic Armor)
- Before everyone pukes at the thought of another round of epic quests, I'm thinking more along the EQOA line of epic armor quests. Quests that could be completed by a single group. The armor would have a very distinct look, and while the stats should be nice, they wouldn't replace the normal battle gear of a time-equipped character. As an alternative, maybe have the "rare" part of the quest be something like the emperor's power sources, something that drops in large number on a kill.

Item Insurance
- I'd love to pay an NPC 500 plat and have him insure all my items. Essentially, he'd have a copy of my items in case I died and chose not to retrieve my corpse. I set the price high, so that people wouldn't use it as a way to not get corpse summoned etc. Obviously there would be some ugly logistics (how to ensure the armor didn't dupe, etc) but I dig this idea the more I think about it.

New Coin type above platinum
- Let us trade in 100 or 1000 plat for a mithril, or something similar. More than anything, it would allow us to travel with a little more money (my first char was a monk!).

Offline Messaging (Mail)
- EQOA has a great in game email system, that even works while offline. I'd love to see this, and even expanding on it to allow Guild leaders to email all members, and possibly even offline mail reading.

War Zones
- Dynamic zones to add to the storyline. Give us a swampy zone where the trolls and frogs are in a fierce battle, and we can join in and choose a side. Possibly make these instanced type zones so that your guild could go in and help the trolls win a key city (obviously, not grobb because we wouldn't want it to affect the normal game), and in return they reward you.

Thats all I have for now. As you can probably tell, I'm sort of weary of the treadmill of slaying mobs, and looking for another level of depth. I am curious to hear other ideas. And please don't flame, I'm certainly not suggesting Sony drop class balancing to give me a house!
