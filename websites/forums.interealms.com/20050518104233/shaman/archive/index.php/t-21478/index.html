SM1 will allow you to zone with your pet. SM2 (I think ... where's my caffiene) will allow you to zone with his buffs intact.

SM1 lets you put your pet away (suspend) but it loses all buffs and weapons when called back. e.g. Need to go invis but dont want to lose your pet. If you zone you lose the pet.

SM2 lets you put your pet away (suspend) but it KEEPS all buffs and weapons when you call it back. If you zone you lose the pet.

I got it because it is nice for farming. Create your pet buff it give it some weapons and then suspend it. Invis up and go to your camp spot.

Also you can use it to have 2 pets ready to go. Create a pet buff it give it some weapons and suspend it. Create another pet. Now you have one pet active and one in your back pocket just incase.
