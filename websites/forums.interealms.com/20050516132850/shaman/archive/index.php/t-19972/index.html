If you're levelling a melee, remember there's a long gap between when weapon stats can be maxed (39) & when you can rase them again (50 or 51).

Just level your alt with his best weapon choice. You'll probably be able to keep 2 maxed per level, 3 as things slow down a bit. When the time comes that you can max the skill, go find a group in DSP or OT & ask if you can beat on their mobs with your low skill weapons (have ants potions handy if you're a large race).

Not sure about the Aviak idea. IIRC they hit fairly hard, but the only time I tried them was on my first toon, who would have been in fairly poor armor at the time.
