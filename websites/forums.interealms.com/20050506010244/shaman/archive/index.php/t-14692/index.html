Hannduvdeth
If you don't have the key, bring somebody who can casts Eye of Zomm. Stand next to the unpickable door, cast the eye, and it will be placed on the other side of the door. Now move it and use it to targer the Shissar (or the ph). Now the shaman (or any pet class) assists and tells their pet to attack. The pet will hit the door then eventually warp thru. As soon as it starts attacking, /pet get lost. Your target and probably the snake next to it will now make their way to you, warping thru the door. Be sure not to use a pet that will do alot of damage and be sure to kill off the pet fast (or I suppose you could use /pet hold and /back off) to avoid putting the snake into summoning mode. Had that happen once, and we had to play a fun game of aggroing thru the door trying to get summoned back.

To get to the shissar, from the zone in hug the right going thru 2 doors to get to a tube room. Take the tube up. As you leave the tube (only one exit in the room), take a left, go past the four snake alcove, and take a right. This leads to a door with 3 snakes in it and the locked door. The shissar/ph is one of the two snakes on the other side of the locked door (actually there is another door past the locked door I believe, but it's not locked).
