frynque
aye, as you can see, you have a TON of options 51+, especially with the addition of luclin/PoP and newer expansions. some of my faves:

* sebilis (cant stress how valuable a seb key is, no matter how badly nerfed kunark loot has become)
* kael bank area (faction and good warm up for arena runs)
* lower guk (solo the frenzy, fbss is still worth about 1k on many servers)
* PoJustice zoneout (slow xp in the 50's, but its pretty easy)
* PoJustice tunnels (if you can put together a group, one of the few camps in PoP that actually drops "real" loot [plats and gems] )
* PoNightmare (if you feel comfortable casting malo* before slow and eating some aggro, PoN in a group can be a blast at this lvl)
* CoM (the revamp pushed up the lvl of xp'ing here a little bit. you can xp "modestly well" back by the gazebos)

additionally, ME, UP, the Grey, the Deep, and SSra if have some hunting buddies.

i personally feel that 51+ is the sweet spot in EQ, ever since PoP. you have plenty of options for leveling. i know you asked about soloing, but at 50+, you should also be spending at least some time grouping, to build alliances and hunting buddies since you're gonna find going into tier2+ in pop and beyond, alot of group invites are gonna be from people who know you.

== frynque

1. Do not sit around waiting for a group.
2. Good group experience is double or triple good solo experience.
3. Look for a POP group while soloing. I think a wall or cave group in Justice may be best-- you may be just a little low for that though--outside cave not too bad--Inside cave can be a bit of a death trap. For soloing, as suggested try everfrost IGs if open, Sea Furies are too far IMO, and The Hole is not that bad of a run but getting a res can be a pain if you overpull there and there is a water escape which can be a little tricky. Frankly if your careful death should be rare there. Also LDoNs are OK but dont sit around for 30 mins waiting for one to start--if that is your plan bind at one of the camps and then solo, letting group know you are bound at camp and no more than 1 minute away. You can even jump to camp, join LDoN group, and run back to solo some spots.

cattywumpus
If you would post a Magelo it would help with suggestions. Certain pieces of equipment can make a huge difference in soloability of some areas at level 51.

I did 49-55 and 17 AAs in Dulak on the Stormwave. The boats are easy to single pull at that level and the cash loot used to be nice. The Pirates on the Stormwave don't have too many HP and only hit for about 100 max. All pure melee MoBs so it's pretty sweet for a shaman. Should be dark blue until 54 or 55.

If you can't get the Stormwave take the back of the Armory. Again all melee and easy singles but the loot sucks.

Once the Stormwave goes light blue move to the Lady D or the Dandolak's Run if you're not sick of the zone yet.

Using a Jaundiced Bone Bracer I was able to clear the boat. Without one you should be able to keep the lower deck clear without too much problem.

I would pull with slow and then slap on a DoT. Then I would tank for my puppy and alternate nuking with the JBB and canniing from lizard-back while Scooby would chew them a new one. I could be at full mana before the next one popped.

redribbit
I agree that you should avoid The Grey, and even some of the higher end content in Luclin for soloing at least. In the Grey mobs are almost completely resistant to Poison and Disease (your dots at this lvl). Once you get a hold of Anathema and Bane you can fight in the Grey successfully (though if they did nerf the exp I suppose it's just not worth it).

Another great place to solo at lvl 50 to say 54 is the giant fort in Burning Woods. The loot there isn't half bad either.

You should look for spots with a bit more room so you can pull and root rot without too much agro from other mobs.

I also agree that soloing isn't a great idea any more unless you just don't want to deal with people (I get those days a lot lately). Just think though, Once you get to 60+ you can head to Vxed with a 65+ Druid, Ranger, or Necro for some really nice Root Rot Exp. Personally I love duoing. It's a bit simpler for those days you just want to relax (though don't get hit while in Vxed LOL)... there will be nothing left of you.
