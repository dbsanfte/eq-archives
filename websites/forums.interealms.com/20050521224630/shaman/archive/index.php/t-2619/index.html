Gloriana
When fighting Roy in NTOV! (and I'm sure other mobs which I can't remember right this second).

Roy has a very nasty dot that sucks all your mana and keeps you from regenning mana. If you get the dot, it overwrites your c3 (to add insult to injury!).

However, if you have cha above c3, it will still take your mana but it will overwrite cha NOT c3 so when the dot wears off, at least you can regen mana quicker.

Most casters stay behind a wall to avoid Roy AOE, but shaman have to get near him to attempt to slow him, so we easily get dotted.

I would wait til a melee called AOE, run around malo, run back (hopefully not dotted), wait til a melee called AOE, run around, attempt to slow, repeat until slowed. I rarely escape this experience without the dot, but by careful use of cha buffing, I don't lose c3.

So, for this fight, I use cha buffs on all groups, first ensuring that they have stacked their buffs correctly to get cha above C3. This will give all the casters at least one AOE where they won't lose c3.
