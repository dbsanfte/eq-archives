Sciek Spellbottler
Until you get Greater Immobilize and/or Petrifying Earth, good ol' "Root" is the only one you need. I've yet to see any of the others reliably hold any better to merit the extra mana costs and in some cases longer casting times.

Greater Immoblize casts FAST and has a resist mod which means it is less likely to be resisted and does seem more reliable than any previous root.

Petrifying Earth is basically the same as Greater Immoblize with a slightly longer cast time and mana cost to pay for a potentially longer duration, and it does indeed seem to last quite a while if you leave the mob alone.

You're doing the right thing using the first "Root" spell for crowd control at 59. When you get Greater Immbolize you might wanna switch to that though. It is more reliable IMO. PE I use for root/rotting, GI for quick crowd control functions.
