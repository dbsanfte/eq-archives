Re: Alchemy revision ideas
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Hail,

1. Lower component costs and/or raise vendor sell-back costs to make skilling-up less cost prohibitive. This has to be the WORST of all tradeskills in terms of cost to raise skill versus utility of the tradeskill. (Yes, smithing & tailoring cost a fortune to raise. So does Tinkering. But they ALL have viable profit making recipes once you've attained high skill. Alchemists get vendor sell Titan's potions at high skill level. )
Make Gate potions less expensive to create so they aren't a toy of the uber rich, but can be sold at a modest markup to be made available to the common mid40's/50's player.

2. Allow the level 65 shaman to raise their skill above 200. Heck, change the level based skill restriction formula entirely. My recommendation is to add 50 points to the skill restriction formula, so that you could obtain fifty MORE points based on level than is currently possible. (This would solve the currently impossible goal of reaching 250 in Alchemy.)

3. Give us some more NEW potions. Make some utility potions for all classes if worried about shaman's already considerable power increasing w/ potion usage. Examples:

a. An SOE potion (self-explanatory).
b. Create a NEW line of potions. One possible choice:
Transmutation line

Soandso's Minor Transmutation- Changes all copper coin into Gold coin in a 100:1 ratio. (IE: 1000 copper turns into 10gp.) This should be an INEXPENSIVE recipe to make and help make alchemy less cost intensive to raise skill.

Soandso's Lesser Transmutation- Changes all silver coin to Platinum pieces in a 100:1 ratio. (IE: 1000 silver pieces turns into 10pp.) This should also be an inexpensive recipe to make and be 20-30 points higher on the skill ladder over the minor version.

Soandso's Transmutation- Changes all gold coin to platinum in a 10:1 ratio. (IE: 1000gp turns into 100pp). This should be inexpensive to make and be 20-30 points higher than the Lesser version.

Soandso's Greater Transmutation - Changes 10,000 Platinum pieces (held on cursor) into a 10lb "lead ingot". This lead ingot would vendor sell for 10,000pp, and would take care of player-player monetary transfers outside of the bazaar zone. The lead ingot would also vendor BUY at 10,000pp, allowing players an easy way to carry bulk cash.
Figure this would be 20-30 points higher on the trivial ladder and still be reasonably priced to create (figure 5-6pp per combine attempt, so a 10 shot potions would sell for ~100pp)
Soandso's Reverse Transmutation - Changes a lead ingot back into platinum pieces. Same trivial & similar cost as Greater Transmutation.

5. Let tinkers make a "collapsible alchemy bag" for shaman to use instead of carrying that lousy bag around. (The bearskin potion bag is fine as is at 10 slots, & doesn't require collapsibility in my opinion.)

6. Make MORE potions that are necessary for quest completion in the same vein as the recipe for the PoP all/all gate item quest.

7. Make a Grandmaster's Alchemist Trophy once the skill-cap problem is fixed.

Very Respectfully,

A stroke of genius above. List the effects of the potion when you open the box describing it.
