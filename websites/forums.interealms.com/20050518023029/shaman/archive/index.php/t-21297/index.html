By the time she got to the point where SoW potions were trivial, she was too much fun to stop playing, so Flummox sat and moldered. Shiuna leveled to 65, got all her spells (except expansion ones), farmed bazaar heavily for equipment, and tradeskilled her hiney off. It was a lonely path, however, so Flummox was reincarnated on a second account so they could duo (wheee! someone to talk to! :D ).

We LOVED the LoY expansion...perfect for solo/duo and plenty of undead to bop. LDoN was a disappointment.... have to group and she's been soloing for so long......Then GoD was a bigger disappointment, except her alchemy is 200 now. Nothing since LoY for my style of play.

I'm stuck at the Hole for my epic, stuck at the escort mission for my 8th shawl....my roadblocks are a lot different than the people playing in GoD, but at least I have a way to break through them.

But, y'know, I'm still having a lot of fun. A few days ago I cleared the Tower of Frozen Shadow.....never been there before. Even though almost all the mobs were green, The final fight was no pushover...Tess has a lot of hps for a caster. I think I'll visit other zones I never got a chance to see.

I'm hoping that the new expansion will have more stuff for solo/duo teams, like LoY did, but I'm not holding my breath. At any rate, I don't see me quitting this game for quite a while.
