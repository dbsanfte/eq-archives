Some raw numbers, assuming the rains get 1 resisted wave per a cast (things I fight so far tend to do better on average than just 2 waves, but I am not high end yet)

ToP
(540 * 2) / (6 + 12) = 60dps

ToS
(650 * 2) / (6 + 12) = 72dps

So if you just chained cast both rains (you even have time for a canni 4, once after you have cast both rains) you would do 132dps. If you have spell casting fury, the dps looks even better and you still have the option of throwing in a dot to add some more dps. With horse, mana preservation, SD, C5, Canni 4 & 5 and FT items you could keep it up for quite a while.
