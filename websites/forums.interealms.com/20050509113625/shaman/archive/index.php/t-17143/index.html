Calabar
One thing I would suggest doing if you want to do some sight seeing is to explore all the different cities. I tend to get into rut and only visit certain places on a regular basis. And with the introduction of the Plane of Knowledge people have even less reasons to visit different cities. I’d suggest starting with player cities. The creators of this game did a very good job of giving each city a different feel. I was amazed the first time I entered the ogre city. It reminded me of watching the Flintstones when I was a kid. Ak'Anon itself sums up everything that’s gnome. And the neon look of the dark elf city is quite unique. A quick way to visit all the player cities would be to create a newbie character and explore the city you start in. When you’ve seen all you care to of that city delete the character and create another. If all your character slots are full you can do this on a different server. And once you’ve finished with player starting cities head over to the moon and some of the Luclin cities. Katta Castellum, for example, is a wondrous city to explore.

If your feeling really adventurous head over to EQ Atlas and pick a not too dangerous zone, for your level, that you’ve never been to and go explore it. Put on levitate, a fresh SoW and invisibility and go exploring. And of course keep gate memorized just in case. After over two years I still find zones, even low level ones, which I never knew existed. Perhaps I’ll take this suggestions to heart and head over to EQ Atlas myself. :)
