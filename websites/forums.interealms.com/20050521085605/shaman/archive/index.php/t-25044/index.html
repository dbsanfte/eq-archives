NereasAequitas
I have two boxed all four bot wing bosses with my cleric bot out of bordom, and solo'd Hreidar (had to have cleric pull mind you). I think you may find it rough trying to duo them with a bazaar level SK, as I've seen more than one bazaar level geared group wipe to a wing boss. WIth only quiesence and and his low AC you will likely not be able to keep up with the DPS. But the only way to find out is to try it, worst case you get a rez, so for the tips you wanted:

Hreidar Lynhilig (west wing): Disease slow, lands easily after MD. Magic is technically possible but very resistant. Poison dots land well. You have two options for fighting him, you can either clear his room and fight him in there, or you can clear the room adjacent his and pull him out, then tank him in the SW corner of the room, if you keep him up against the wall he won't gate. I would say Hreidar is the easiest of the four.

Laef Windfall (north): Immune to slow. Get your SK's DS up as much as possible for this one, you won't be contributing much of any DPS at all. The wing is highly resistant to poison disease and cold, magic lands on trash but Laef himself has high MR. Epic dot is about all you can reliably land. Despite being immune to slow his DPS is not that bad, as long as you can sustain your mana healing the DS will wear him down eventually. All wing bosses are clerics so be sure to tank him in his room near his spawn to avoid gating.

Oreen Wavecrasher (east): Disease slow, easy after MD. Again magic possible but unlikely. No poison or cold here either, and no magic on Oreen himself. Disease dots will land though, as will epic. Similar to Laef it will be a long fight, as long as you can sustain your mana just let the SK kill it and refresh the disease dots now and then.

Gaukr Sandstorm (north): far and away the hardest of the four, very high MR and the wing is naturally immune to disease based spells. I would not even bother attempting this guy if you don't have a tash stick, and hope it procs early, Balance of the Nihil is a big plus as well. He has a 1000 DD proc, and will drop a bazaar SK very quickly if you don't get lucky with the slow. If you do get slow on load on the poison dots and rains if you have the mana for it, you'll want to try and get him dead before you need to try and reslow.

Anyway good luck with it and have fun, always enjoyed BoT despite it being low xp and train central, was kinda sad to see it die with OoW. Now if I could just solo a tower boss!
