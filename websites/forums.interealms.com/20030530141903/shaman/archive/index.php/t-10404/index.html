Gulug
If the drop rates are anything to go by, your looking at maybe anything up to 90 hours of camping to get the spell in SF.

90 hours in the crypt will probably make you enough to buy malo and torpor a couple of times over. Trick would be to do it with as few people as possible. I have done it with rogue (without which you cant get in), shaman, monk and pally. You could probably do it with just the rogue, shaman and monk if you are good. Better to get a tank though. Pallys are good cos they can stun the guardians and prevent their CH.

The hardest part is the guardians in the main hall. As you wont have a mezzer, keep one rooted in the main hall and pull the other one outside and kill him out of line of sight of the one in the hall so they dont heal each other. Once you have them broken its pretty much a cakewalk. You will need a 60 though to ensure a single pull on the emperor.

Im not a sham, but i won a copy of malo in the crypt. I didnt realise how important it was till i asked a few people about it, as I thought it was just another spell.
