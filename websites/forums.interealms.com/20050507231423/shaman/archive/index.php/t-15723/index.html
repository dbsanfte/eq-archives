Matey
The 100k Drogmor is very fast. It's even faster if you strafe run with it. I think it's worth the money just to cut down on travel time in the old world(though it sucks if you die and have to run back with sow :). You can move sideways extremely quickly with it by strafing and then tapping the up arrow. This makes maneuvering in a group to get out of a mobs way extremely simple. Typically I'll turn sideways and just strafe back from the mob. Lizards are affected by graphics lag IMMENSELY. Start up and slow down time is entirely dependent on your framerate. When I'm just running around, I typically turn the clip plane all the way down so that I can start quickly and stop quickly as opposed to running for quite some time with it all the way up in PoK, for instance. Be very sure that you want the expensive lizard because if you decide to resell it back later, you're going to lose around 40k on your purchase and get back only 66k or so. That's a lot worse than buying a 9k lizard and selling it back for 6.6k.
Bigmeeno
The 100k drogmor was my first ever mount. And I would never live without it again. Its fast enough that in most cases, if I need to zone, none of the mobs can even begin to keep up with it.

The start/stop delay on it is one you learn to live with. If Im pulling, I go back on foot or stay as far from the mob as I possibly can. But its rarely caused any problems for me, and when it did, it was because I had not anticipated something or didnt think about what I was doing in advance.

Oh, dont forget to buy Innate Metabolism 3. hehe.. you will need that. Or you will go bankrupt buying food and drink. :)
