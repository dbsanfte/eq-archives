Runningwolf
There are a couple possible reasons for this.

The 49 fire pet is actually pretty easy to get. It is focusable by both the fire staff from Hate and the torch focus item, making it relatively impressive. It also has a very nice damage shield and hits in the 50's for damage

The 52/58 fire pets are wizards, and although the 58 pet got a recent upgrade to it's melee damage, it's damage shield (O'Kiels) is still weak compared to the 49 pet.

The Decoy pet mentioned (56th) is basically a high hp, high damage shield pet, but it's melee damge is in the 40's. This pet also has a taunt proc/cast that it uses to help hold agro more than other pets.

The usual choice for Rak is the 49 pet, partially because people don't perceive much use for the 56 decoy pet and may not have it.

The recent changes to pet resists have messed with the fire pets quite a bit and people are experiencing problems with their immunity to fire damage which all 4 of these had previously. Some people are saying the resists are fine, others are having their pets killed by the second AE. Just something to be aware of for now.
