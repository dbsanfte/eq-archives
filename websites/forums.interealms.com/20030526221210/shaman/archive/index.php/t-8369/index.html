Zyphyr
Ant Legs is actually the effect on the shrink Potion. It was created as a seperate effect because Shrink was limited to indoor only (said restriction was removed Sept 26th of this year) and they wanted the potion to work anywhere. It isn't actually a castable spell.

Diminution, I haven't seen anything that actually has said effect - it is essentially a wasted slot. It isn't a castable spell.

Shrink is the one that you really can get, scribe, and cast.

Tiny Companion was added in PoP for all classes that have pets (and all get it at level 19). It differs from Shrink in the following ways:
1)50% reduction instead of 34%
2)It only works on your own pet

For a shaman, it is a pretty much pointless spell. Shaman only got it because they have a pet.
