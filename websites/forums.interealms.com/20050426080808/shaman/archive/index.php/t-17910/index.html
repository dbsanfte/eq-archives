Good suggestions, I'll be trying them. I especially like that neither is in PoP; I really hate that expansion with a passion. My own contribution:

Howling Stones - A solo AA takes about 2.5 hours in the south wing, about as long as a solo AA in Valor. Mobs in this wing hit for about 200 with nameds hitting for 230s. The north and west wings are fun to blast through now & then, but are mostly green/light blue so not very good exp. Throughout the zone, the nameds are fairly common, random spawns and therefore the loot is common, though most of it's lore, so you'll be banking often. Since nobody ever comes here, the loot is fairly rare in bazaar despite dropping so much, and you can make a killing. The east wing is a bit higher level and tougher, and the loot isn't as good as south, but on the other hand you don't need the master key for it.

Speaking of which, the quest for the master key is outlined here: http://pub146.ezboard.com/fthesafehousefrm13.showMessage?topicID=6811.topic . Overall a great walkthrough, a few points I didn't think it covered well:
-To get in the north wing, kill the 'flying mouth' creatures near the zone-in, each one drops a tallow-marked candle, which is the nodrop key to north. To get in the east wing, simply levitate and invis up, go to where the golems are at either the north or south wing entrance, get on the firepot, and lev across. Going straight up the steps to east will drop you in the pit (false floor :)). The key to west is dropped by the north wing 'boss', to south is dropped by the west wing 'boss'. Unlike all other nameds, bosses have only one set spawn point - toward the end of the wing. www.EQAtlas.com has a pair of excellent zone maps.
-There are a few locked doors opened by 'intermediate' keys; these are ground spawn items you'll find somewhat near locked doors. Explore a bit ;)
-Aside from the master key, all other keys in the zone (aside from the one you need to enter in the first place) are norent. If you want to quest the master key, be sure to have at least a couple of hours free.
-The piece of the key in Drusella's room is past the four golems. If you're lucky the one roamer will be a live mob, and you can simply invis (be sure to con the mobs anyway, it's possible one or more may see invis!). You don't have to kill the room to get the piece, indeed this would make it pretty tough. If you have an instant gate potion (1049pp with good cha at select vendors) you can snag it and instantly gate out. If you have an item with a DA clicky, you can click it, *quickly* grab the piece from her room, which is a container, combine the other three pieces in it, use it to open the door leading to the zoneout (which will put it on your keyring) and not die.

As an added bonus, I've been able to work my lacking weapon skills on these 200 hitters, and much prefer in-the-face tanking to root-dotting anyway. The revamp seems to have lowered most mobs' hitpoints, which is why exp here is relatively fast. To compensate they do hit a bit harder, but nothing like PoP.

I guess I could add to this...

Inner Acrylia Caverns
-All mobs dark blue
-No gaters,
-Weak casters,
-Practically no adds (other than a few spots that you fight 2 at the same time)
-Four named mobs that drop very decent stuff (worth roughly 5-15k an item on Rathe)
-Very good plat
-Large supply of Acrylia Ore (for tradeskillers)

Here's how to do it:

1) Fight your way in -- random mobs see invis on the way in so if you get spotted you will have a rougher time
1-a) DO NOT Train yourself into Inner -- You click on a statue to port to Inner Acrylia, since its not a zone switch the mobs on you will follow you leaving you with a nasty CR and the mobs you trained will stay in place (they are to dumb to go back through the portal themselves)
1-b) While you are fighting your way in you can check on A Grimling Primalist (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=11697) who drops very nice stuff.

2) Once you’re in Inner start your killing; There are 1-2 mobs right when you pop into Inner. Warning: One of them may be a Named that HT's (A Grimling High Priest (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=7381)) -- You should dispose of them with ease. (btw, if there is no named there is another roaming regular mob that is his PH)

3) if you had a clear way in (or cleared it yourself hehe) -- move ahead down the stairs -- Kill 2 grimlings by the yellow gate, and a 3rd mob in the corner. Procede to "Jail" -- this place is 100% safe for you to be medding in or buffing, there are mobs inside the jail but you don’t need to worry about them... there is ONE mob though you can kill there, and that is either a PH or A Grimling Arch Sage (I couldn’t find a link for him but found he drops Black Acrylia Shield (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=20448))

4) Go back to the room where you killed 3 last mobs, and go left to another room (don’t pull anything from there yet) -- the mob on the "altar" looking thing is a PH or a A Grimling Arcanist (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=12709) -- pull him to the room you fought the 3 mobs earlier. You may or may not get an add. Again, simple fight.

5) Now turn to gold gate from earlier and turn left and up the stairs, fight your way through 2 mobs (singles) and now you’re in a big room. Facing the room to your right there are 2 mobs by the entrance, dispose of them and move down stairs.

6) now you pick left or right (don’t matter) and kill 2 mobs on your way (need to fight those 2 at the same time) ... keep going around the corner until you see a door; open it, and right on front of you is a room with a "throne" and a mob on it, its a PH or A Grimling Warlord (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=13313)

Warlord drops the least worth stuff, so if you have troubles keeping up with killing all of the PH/named, this one would be the one you could "skip."
Also, High Priest (or his PH) pops by Warlord and wanders to the spot where you pop into when coming to inner and back to Warlord (he has a simple path) ... so if you didn’t kill him on earlier, the best time is to do it when you’re on #5 on this walkthrough, just wait for him to pass by the middle and pull him.

ALL Mobs repop on a 30 minute timer so if you’re fast or duoing you can keep ALL of the named/PHes killed at all times (usually by the time I kill last PH I run back in and the 1st one should be popping soon.)

Other drops are the Runes, all worthless junk, but they are stackable. Usually after 2-3 hour session I get about 1-2k of merchant junk alone (the weapons they drop sell to merchants for about 150pp)

Have a good hunt =)
