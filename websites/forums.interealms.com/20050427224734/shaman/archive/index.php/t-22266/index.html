haruuq
the loot part would depend on your definition of "decent", but as far as leveling the sk goes, that's an easy one.

i helped an sk friend level pretty fast at that stage by taking him to Echo Caverns. at the time, the xp mod there was awesome, and i kept him buffed and healed w/o problems, and slowed mobs there for him to fight them. pull with slow, cripple mobs, switch to sk and have him peel...torpor on sham to make up for dmg taken, and sk has an easy fight.

no real loot involved unless you decide to pull Taskmaster Torzakh (spelling?) and kill him, which is really easy with your sham (you can solo him with sham w/o problems), but the xp was awesome at the time we were down there. also puts you really close to the Deep, and once the sk is a few levels higher, you can use your sham to "fish" and cc mobs with root, and have the sk wail on them. there's a named you can camp even if you're pulling to entry area that's the first mob to the right at the first intersection...have only seen him up a couple times, but he's an easy kill and has 2 decent droppables. necklace for int casters, and a mask with decent stats.


enjoy :)
