Zoulen
This place blows Dreadlands away once you reach lvl 44 for soloing and duoing (grouping too I would imagine, but you'd better bring your own - I've never found enough people already there).

Tackle the hornets in the SW corner of the zone. It takes a while to find a good pulling spot with all the trees and wandering mobs but it's very worth it.

If you're soloing, avoid the ember and Solarae (sp) hornets. They have a LOT of HPs. Duoing they aren't bad at all if your partner is a damage dealer like a monk or a ranger.

To compare the exp in this zone, last night I duoed in both DL and BW with a 50 monk, me at 47. The only visual bump I received in DL was when we killed a Ravishing or a Steep. In BW, every blue kill was like killing a ravishing and a steep or better.

It takes longer between pulls, but since you get so much more exp, it's worth the wait.

Enjoy.

[edit] forgot to mention loot. The loot is exactly like DL - minus the crap that drops that sells for only silver.

I checked out this spot just about an hour ago. The bees are fairly good exp (1% per kill at 44) and are pretty weak, hitpoints wise. However, like the original poster said, the Solariea or however you spell them bugs are mean bastards. They have crazy amounts of HP and hit for about 100 on average (Usually between 90-110) If one of these resists your slow or outlives your slow duration (what it did to me) it starts pumping out damage like nothing else.

Avoid them.

However even though the exp was good I'm not sure I'm going to return. It's kind of tedious running there and back for KEI's, or selling loot. If I'm going to run that distance I'll probably just head to a place which has faster spawns and weaker mobs, Bloodgill gobbys.
