Zone in and fight two that are there. At 58 i believe you still get good exp from the sets of golems around the outside, but i haven't fought there for over a year now. Anyway, yes, drop down the north to the right of the north set of stairs. There are lots of mobs downstairs, but you drop north as there is a ledge a little ways down and you can sit on the ledge (which slopes down to the rest of the area) without aggroing mobs, make pet, buff etc. From this ledge, you can single pull the mobs you see to you (and you shouldn't get adds). Again, been a long ass time now for me, but there are a few wanderers, and a few statics you can see from this ledge. Once they are spread out you can also pull mobs from the entrances down below (either east or west doors which once through there also split again for more mobs. HS is a great zone, i kinda wish i didnt get ot faction oh so long ago.
