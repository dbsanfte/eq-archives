I assume they're talking about Slow Mitigation. A number of (is it PoP only?) mobs will now be only partially effected by slow. This is either:

1. A huge nerf to shamans as it decreases the number of mobs we're fully effective against;
2. A benefit to shamans, since otherwise they would have just flagged these mobs unslowable; or
