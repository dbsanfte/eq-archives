They DO stack. But it breaks down to simple haste stacking.

You can stack ONE item, ONE spell, and ONE song that have haste components. This has been known forever.

What you are doing when you stack Celerity and Spirit Quick is trying to stack 2 HASTE spells. The higher one will cancel the lower one out, so celerity (50%) will cancel SQ (30?%). However, because SQ aslo has an ATK bonus and an AC bonus only the haste portion of the spell is overwritten. Your pet will still get the AC and ATK bonus that SQ gives, but will use the haste from Celerity.
