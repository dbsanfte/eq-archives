Daareon
I wouldnt recommend velious for fear kiting - simply because the mobs have lots of HPs and hit hard. Unless your timing is absolutely perfect 100% of the time there is an appreciable downtime to soloing in most velious locations.

DL is good if you dont mind pickup groups. You can get a group almost any time of the day and it's usually non-stop fighting. I found the XP wasnt really good though. Passable, but not great.

DSP is better for soloing (mobs have low HPs, dont hit hard - unless you do hoppers). CRs are easy too which may be a plus. No cash to speak of unless you do hoppers - but again hoppers hit hard and have a ton of HPs for that level.

SolB is sadly hard to get a group in. It's a shame because you can level all the way to 55 here and the cash is good. I solo'd off and on from 40-50 here.

Lower guk hasnt been mentioned. XP/loot is as good as SolB but sadly these days the zone is empty. Last time I was there we had our necro pulling Frenzy... from the Lord room. For three hours.
