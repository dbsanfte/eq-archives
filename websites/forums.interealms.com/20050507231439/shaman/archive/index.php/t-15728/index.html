Skadi
I never found soloing to be all that effective of a leveling method. I am not a big solo player anyway, basically its only something I do when I am really bored or just want to try something crazy.

Anyway.. at level 47 I just dont see you being able to solo anything effectively in PoP. You cant kill fast enough because you dont have the spells yet. The mobs are going to be red cons or yellows in most cases at least and youll have problem sticking spells because of that. I would suggest, if your set on soloing going to other zones. Scarlet Desert might not be bad lots of mobs for that level range.

Solo Tactics are pretty much the same, i stick a resist debuff, slow, root, send in doggy (buffed and hasted), dot, re-root as necessary, heal pet. You could tank too depending on how hard the mob hits and how much hps you have and keep stoicsim up to heal yourself, but being snared and slowed might not make that worth it.

I still recommend just finding a group though :)
Jinheim
Pull with malos, then root it. Slow it, then apply any other debuffs you need (malosinia, malicious decay). Apply dots. Sit there and wait.

If you are soloing somewhere easier that pofire, pull a second mob and do to at once.

That's pretty much all there is to shaman soloing. We might not solo as fast as other classes, but we never have downtime, and we are very hard to kill in comparison to a wizard, druid, or chanter.

Solariis
Without the 61 doggy soloing PoP zones is pretty painful and even then it's pretty slow. If you want to know what it's like to feel good about your soloing capabilities then hit CoM(City of Mist) from 47 to 55. The zone is perfect for shaman. Mobs have low resists and don't run and die fairly fast. The 49 dog helps a lot and your 51 slow makes it total cake there. Plus you get decent loot and the rare drop(which aren't worth much nowadays but still fun to see a wu stick drop).
From 56 to 60 you can easily solo Chardok which is also a fun place. Seb is a blast also but it's hard to solo the entry mobs till 58, at 60/61 you can own the necro and gang camp by yourself.

Ah those were the days of fun, easy and fast soloing. PoP zones are just a total pain. I could solo rats/goos in PoD at 61 but it was just too slow. I can solo ravens/treants/spiders in PoN but it's just plain boring as hell.
