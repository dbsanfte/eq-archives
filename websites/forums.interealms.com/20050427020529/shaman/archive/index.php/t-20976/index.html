Cliff Golems are highly resistant. Pull only at night or you will get guards as well. Park our pet over on the shoreline. Pull the golem with Malo; kite him till Malosinia sticks; kite him till turgurs sticks; run him to pet; torpor/quiesence yourself; summon mount; epic....

Re-epic as needed and don't forget to reslow. Will probably take 2 or 3 re-casts of turgrus to finish him off. Overall, they are pushovers once you get the process down.

Personally, I am 10-1 against them. For a while, I'd kill the fist golem befroe logging for the night. The one loss was my first attempt and I didn't keep a timer for turgurs. It wore off and I had to gate.
