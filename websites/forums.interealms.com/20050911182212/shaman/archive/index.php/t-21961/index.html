Kaloog
My advice is simple. Don't stay in one spot for too long just because it's great exp. I know that goes against the grain, but I honestly suggest going around and exploring places you haven't been to. Check out old world spots like Befallen, Najena, Blackburrow, Crushbone, Unrest...all those places are very cool, and have themes to them that a lot of folks never get to see because of places like "PC".

So it'll take a little longer to get from level N to N+1....you will be far richer for moving around and living up to the name "adventurer". Don't wait to top out at PC...go now!
