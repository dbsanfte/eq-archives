Stareff2
I wanted to Share what I have found to be a tremendous success, mybe others arent doing it and would like to try it.

Whenever I slow a Mob I like to get in the thick of it and Stand literally next to the MT. By doing this, the tank can resume aggro and I take no damage whatsoever. Its rare that the mob even pays attention to me when I do this.

This also helps the tanks mitigate dmg faster by not having to regain aggro and chase the mob around.

Also we are great CC whenever a Chanter/bard isnt around. I will go toe to toe in order to keep a mob off the healer until I can get them rooted and slowed. I have enough HP now to just stand there and tyake a few shots while I get my slow off first and then root.

We are also great splitters if done with the same technique as above.

Play hard, play fair and play like youre the best shaman in the lands....
