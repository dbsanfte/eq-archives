My character's name comes from works by two different authors.

Crydee comes from Raymond E Feist's Riftwar Series.
This was a human frontier city near the border to elven territory. The main ranger of the serries (Martin Longbow) is from this city and is one of the few humans who is allowed to come and go from the elven area as he pleases. Other rangers in these series all seem to have Crydee as their main home point, even if they were not born there.

Ossiriand comes from Tolkein's Middle Earth, specifically from The Similliron.
I don't remember a ton about it, though it was home to the green elves (which would be the EQ equivalent to wood elves) who used the colour green to disguise or camoflage themselves from outsiders.


Knowing all of this, you would city that:

- First name is of literay human culture. Surname is of literay elven culture, specifically wood elves. Only a half elf would have a name of mixed cultures.

- Now using the ideal that a half elf first name was often that of the opposite culture from which he was raised in, you then that my home city is Kelethin. You would also know that his diety would be Tunare as wood elves don't worship Karana.

- Now considering that the only classes that are really associated with the colour green and to any strong degree are rangers and druids. While Ossiriand could have had both (I don't really remember) - Crydee only had rangers. Thus narrows down my character class to that of ranger.



Yes, I spend days figuring out my character's name.
