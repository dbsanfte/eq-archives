What results has that petition garnered? None. How exactly do you think the problems you reference are going to get fixed, then? Another petition? What precedent would lead you to believe version 2.0 would be met with any more action than 1.0?

SOE has shown, through their own actions, that they only respond to histrionics. Well-mannered, thoughtful posts are met with silence. SOE established the precedent of only responding to the loudest, most vocal of protests. Furor throwing a fit about monk mitigation, the warrior agro protests, etc. These were responded to with changes to game mechanics. The shaman petition accomplished nothing at all.

We would all love to play a game where the developers responded to reasonable, logical posts. Sadly, they don't. They only respond to significant threats to their profit margins.

Witness the recent summit, which was precipitated by an unprecedented number of player defections, massive player discontent of a scale I've never seen in 5 years, and numerous high-profile guild disintegrations, two at least of which went directly to the primary competitor, Worlds of Warcraft. SOE only responded with the placebo-summit when it became obvious that a HUGE number of high-end, multiple-account players were leaving out of frustration and taking their subscription dollars with them. So they invite 60 high-profile site moderators and guild leaders to a wine-and-dine session in an attempt to dazzle them back into complacence.

You can attach to the developers all the nobility and integrity you want. It won't change the reality of the past 5 years.
