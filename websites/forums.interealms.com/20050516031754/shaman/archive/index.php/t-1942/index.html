I think your tactics are fine but againt guards that can LoH themselves, it is basically a lose situation. Ya have to basically kill two mobs for the experience of one.... The only other thing I would try might be pet kiting...
Tactic:
1) slow him (also draws agro and protects pet)
2) dot him with your best disease and poision spell (retains agro)
3) flash of light him. and step back
At this point if you have more agro than your pet it will ignore him and "run away" (wish I had snare but if you have enough room you don't need it)
4) pet nips him as you canni dance, prepare for reapplying another poision or disease spell
5) when the flash of light fades, one angry mob goes for you... you refresh or stack another DOT and go back to step 3.
6) when you get low on mana then you duke it out... Hopefully, a mob can cast LoH if it is running away but don't depend on it.

Juujuu
I've been in your position for killing the guards.
Here's what you do.
Go to the place you teleport up as you walk into Erudin from Tox forest.
Summon a pet and park it there and buff up.
Head into Erudin a bit and watch for the wandering guards to come nearby.
When you see one, pull it back to your pet and slow him.
As a paladin, he's going to be healing himself a lot, and use lay hands. Expect this. You don't have the spells available to kill them fast enough before they heal themselves. (I don't think)
The guards are only level 30 or so, so you shouldn't have that much trouble tanking them.
The biggest problem is if you get 2 guards on you at once. That's a lot of healing power, and much more damage output. If you do, take the teleport down to Tox entrance, and camp out. The guards can't path down to you through the teleport.
