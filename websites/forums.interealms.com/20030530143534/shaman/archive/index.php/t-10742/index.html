Donar
hmmm... 40 is perhaps a touch low to solo on the taskmaster side, but maybe not. I could solo there from 43ish on, but I remember that the mobs (underbulks, stonegrabbers, lizard-looking things) were a bit tough. After 44, they got much easier, due to the upgraded ice nuke and pet. I was purposely tanking each one, trying to finish maxxing out my defense skill, so maybe the fights weren't that hard, but I can't remember. I do know that the new spells at 44 made the fights much easier. At 45, I was able to use my JBB and well, that made the fights trivial. The 44 pet CAN tank them, if fully buffed, so you don't have to, but depending on your playstyle, defense skill, you may want to. They hit pretty quickly but not too hard. In comparison, a HG hits harder but a seafury cyclops hits more weakly. They will stay dark blue to you until high 40s. By then, though, you may be ready to single pull the skellies from the taskmaster room. You can stay in echo until 51 or so, if you like, but hehe, that may get a bit boring to you. The exp is good, especially considering the difficulty of the mobs. The only mob to really be careful of is Xil something or other. He's a high 50s chanty mob. If he's up, kill somewhere else. ;) He despawns after 15-20 minutes. Also, be careful of a named underbulk, Fireclaw. He hits hard and fast and, while he can be slowed, he can NOT be rooted. I like echo. When I was hunting there, it was usually empty, so you could hunt as many mobs as you could handle. More people have discovered it on my server now it seems, so not sure how good it will be for you. Good luck. :)

Now my cleric is back in echo killing archers for aaxp. Sometimes he will group with a druid or a shaman and kill scounds while waiting for the archers to respawn.
