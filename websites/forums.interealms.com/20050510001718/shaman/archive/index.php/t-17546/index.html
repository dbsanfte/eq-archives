There is a time limit.

What I do is this: Have the raid split up in to two groups (but still in one raid window). One group goes to the southwest corner, the other goes to the northeast corner. Knights and rangers kite mobs in the second room while pullers bring them in one at a time to the two waiting groups.

You don't have to split your raid up, but I've found that it works better that way. You ARE on the clock, and for some reason, two smaller groups work much more efficiently than one big zerg.
