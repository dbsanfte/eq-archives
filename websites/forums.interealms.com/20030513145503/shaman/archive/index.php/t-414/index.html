Gloriana
Don't forget my helpful Rak fighting tips (lost to message board wipe)

* Rak's spawn point is where he spawns when his reaver dies, since he has a tendancy to gate back to his spawn point when nearly dead, it's a really really good idea to pull his REAVER where you want to fight the battle, gating/summoning Rak = bad
* Don't fight in the temple. The risk of adds is too great and can lead to a mess.
* Don't fight near water, if Rak falls in the water, it could be very bad.
* Only the mage pets should engage Rak, this includes melee that like to "helpfully" shoot arrows. I personally witnessed a Rak fight that failed for this reason. (note: I'm sure a well equipped couple of groups could probably fight Rak without mage pets, but why risk it on your epic? go for what's safe).
* My guild (which has never lost on Rak) always pulls Rak's reaver to the area we call the "staging area." This is a small courtyard right before you go through the arch which leads to the pathway to the moat and temple. There are no roamers/spawns and there is a nice corner that the mage pets can push Rak into.
* After you hand in the tear and Rak aggros, wait until the mage pets are engaged and Rak is on them, before you slowly step out of range.

Good luck with the fight!

taraddar
Reason is simple. The lvl 52 and 58 fire pets are wizards. They don't have the huge dmg shield that the previous pets have and they will spend all thier time casting nukes that rak will resists. Mages generally consider the post 50 fire pets useless because they cast so much instead of meleeing.

As far as the reavers go. There is a specific reaver that will always spawn the specific mob. The first reaver is just inside the locked door and can be single pulled easily by anyone lvl 60. This one will always spawn ghiosk. In the hall directly behind that throne there is a wooden pannel on the floor. This is an elevator up to a room with 5 or so reavers in it. The elevator can either be picked or the key nek drops works on it. One of these 5 reavers spawns rak. Not 100% sure which one it is but I beleive it's the middle one in the back. All the way up top there is a single reaver in the last room (fake floor in the center of the room so be careful) and that one will always spawn nek.
