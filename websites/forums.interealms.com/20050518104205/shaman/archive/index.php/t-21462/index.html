Got this from the Enchanter boards. Idea of a scaleable Epic which was also touched on in the Epic 2.0 Graphic thread.

Step 1: You get an ok item, bazaar-loot level. Stats simular to old epic.
Step 2: You get a very nice low-end raiding item, 100 hp/mana level.
Step 3: You activate the unique power of the item.
*** Everything up to step 3 should be doable with a 3 groups of bazaar-geared L 65 characters with 50 to 100 AA.

Step 4: Upgrade to post-VT quality.
Step 5: Upgrade to post-Elemental quality. Unique power improved. Requires
Step 6: Upgrade to post-Time quality
Step 7: Upgrade to end of path. Unique power improved.

Obviously there's not only some waggle room in that - BUT it is something that 'fits' both the casual and Time-Dude/Dudette's style of play.

Thoughts?

One item is not going to make a bazaar level character ready to farm Time. There is no reason to make is excessively complex to attain that item.

It should be *hard* for 1 group at the most difficult points, but not impossible. There should be instanced parts that are ONLY solo-able. Its a simple thing really.

Even if the new epic pieces are Time quality, there is no reason that they should become less personal.
