Solariis
IMHO, PoG is much less dangerous than Seb. If you zone into a full respawn there is a safe spot in the SW corner to setup and pull. Can't remember exact loc but it's something like -3500,+3500. I've gone AFK there for 30 minutes once and never had anything aggro on me. It's very high up giving you a nice view of that whole SW corner. Once that area is clear you can either continue pulling to the SW corner making for long pulls or slowly make your way up north but not too close to the wall else you risk the treant pathing your way. At this point it does get a bit more dangerous because some mobs path near the wall so getting an add is possible. With KEI and horse I can handle one add as long a it's not a named, treant, white horse or spirit stalker. If it's a wolf, black horse, or sanguine kodiak I can easily keep it root parked. 2-3 adds though and it's gate time :)

You don't have to worry about any ice comet nuking froggies like in seb. The caster types in PoG are very weak and are easily resisted. The only danger in PoG on the west side is the tranquil treant and the 3 named(2 horses 1 wolfy). It's one of the few zones I have gate memmed so if you do get a tranquil or named add it's easy to escape. The other danger is getting rooted in the middle of a pull if your MR isn't high enough, easily prevented with 120+MR buffed. Just stay away from the east side which is full of nasty cullers and black panthers.
