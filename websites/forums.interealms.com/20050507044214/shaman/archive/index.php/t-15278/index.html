Sebazy
Imho.

If your a powergamer that wants to race to 65 and join the resident uberguild, go ogre, you'll have a hard time with mana for the first 43 levels, but after that its clean sailing. Front stun immunity really is fantastic in pop. Also has slam.

If you see yourself as a casual gamer, without access to good gear and are not in a hurry to get to 65, go troll, natural regen is a big deal pre stoticism and even after that, its still a nice helping of free mana. Again trolls have slam, + the snare necklace quest line as well as Jaundice bone gear which is nice cheap and easily availible stuff thats quite nice to wear at the lower levels when AC really matters.

As far as the other races go....

Vahshir's noteable bonus = Sneak, a very useful ability, especially if you like questing, they also get safefall but I think its quite a low level, not really useful except it cuts down on the falling for 1 damage spam when you run down hill =)

Frogs get nothing beyond good base stats and no xp modifier.

Iksars get regen and also of note, a moderate ac bonus, forage and of course our tail =) However we pay for this with awful base stats and the inability to wear a suprising amount of gear which can be quite a nuisiance at the low/mid end. You'll be cursing your lack of strength for the first 30 levels, you'll be cursing for lack of stamina for the last 5.

Barbs pretty much fall under the same category as frogloks, however, for that 5% xp penalty you gain: A) Slam, B) The ability to be taken seriously, but you loose decent eye sight which is a nuisiance till you get vision.

Unless you are a direhard quester or only want to play in the old world. Faction really isnt that big a deal these days.
