Rustyspear
There was a good post on this on the necro boards but i'll try to lay down all the info to do this.

In a nutshell, most people are kos to giants so, u'll need to make a warrior that worships rallo zek. I personally did this with a troll warrior and had no problems running around in kael to get the MQ done. To make this even more effecient, it helps to have a friend that can cast lev, invis, and sow. The invis part is a requirement that will be discussed later

First requirement, mule that worships rallo zek and gets ported to velious.

Go to crystal cavern with both your main and your mule. Park the mule somewhere safe and lay waste to orcs until the bottle of acid drops. I believe its called korsic acid or something like that. Log in the mule and loot the bottle. An alternative to this is to kill the queen and loot the sceptre she drops. Either way, u'll probably want to invis and lev down to the coldain city and pull orcs to there until a bottle drops.

Once u have the bottle or spectre, run to thurg, and find the npc that is mentioned in other walk throughs. To find here, u go to thurg and left at the "T" and go past the bank, contiue straight pass the next "intersection" and open the first door on your left. You'll see her and can turn in the sceptre or bottle even if u cant fit through the door. You'll recieve an orb thingy. Helps to bind in thurg.

Time to hunt down a blood wolf harness. Good news, its droppable and comes off the named dire wolf. Something like Blood Dire Wolf. Hand the harness to the mule

Time to go Kael. In kael, u want to go the tormax side of kael. Under the bridge to the tormax area is a giant wolf trainer. Not completely sure of the again, but the word "trainer" is in his title.
Hand in the orb thingy from the chick from thurg, and then hand in the blood wolf harness next. You'll get the blood wolf harness and a harness of control. Once u have that, u can pick a fight with a giant and get a free TL back to your bind (thurg) with everything on u.

Back to GD, invis and park the mule behind where the broodmother spawns. Becareful and make sure that none of the wurms break invis. This can be a pain if one does.

Seeing how everyone and there mother wants to do this quest. U can log in the mule and check to see when the broodmother is up. When she is, hand in the harness of control. U have 4mins to your main in and get to the coldain mines which are in the south east part of the zone. Korf Battlehammer-something, i dont recall the last name, will be outside where the entrance to the mines are. Keep him targeted, when the broodmother arrives snare/root him. He has around 5k hps and the broodmother will become his pet. He tries to run away and make sure kill him. When he is dead, he drops the wristguard and broodmother pops.

Tada, quest done. Look at other guides to fill in the holes, like npc names and names of exact items. Anywho this is the way i did.

GL
