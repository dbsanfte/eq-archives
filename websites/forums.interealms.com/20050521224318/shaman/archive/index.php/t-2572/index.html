Jaundiced Bone Bracers are clickable for the level 34 Shaman spell Winter's Roar.
The effect is an 8 second Cold Based Single Target DD (direct damage) that does 263.
DPS (damage per second) = 32.875
DPM (damage per minute) = 1972.5

Being a level 34 DD spell, this effect can be enhanced with the use of the following level II or III focus items effects:
Extended Range ("ER") + 15% Spell Range
Improved Damage ("ID") + 20% Spell Damage
Spell Haste ("SH") + 15% Spell Haste

Using all these effects would provide the following:
A 6.8 second Cold Based Single Target DD that does 315 damage with a maximum range of + 15%*.
DPS = 46.32
DPM = 2779.2

Being a DD spell, the damage from this spell can be increased through the use of AA specialization Spell Casting Fury 1/2/3.
At SCF3, each use has a 7% chance of doubling the damage done.

For the focus item enhanced Winter's Roar spell, SCF3 would give a 7% chance to hit for 630.
This would effectively raise the average damage done by the use of a Jaundiced Bone Bracer + ID2/3 focus item (like Wurm Scale Coat/Imperial Wardog Collar) + SH2/3 focus item (like Djarn's Amethyst Ring) + AA SCF3 to 337.05.

Using a Jaundiced Bone Bracer + ID2/3 focus item + SH2/3 focus item + IR2/3 + AA SCF3 would provide the following:
A 6.8 second Cold Based Single Target DD that does 337.05 damage with a maximum range of + 15%.
DPS = 49.57
DPM = 2974.2

That's a 51% increase in damage over the standard effect on the Jaundiced Bone Bracer.
