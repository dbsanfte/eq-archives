Taladaen
Let me throw my opinion in with most everyone else here. Shaman and Druids play very differently, they don't have to be equipped the same. Your slow spell draws a lot of agro, you *will* have to tank a mob for a few rounds, might as well be prepared for it.

Get your defense to 200, go solo, slow a mob and tank it while your pet beats on it from behind. The exp will be slower that way but as you get into your 50s and 60s you'll appreciate the extra AC (which is free). Get used to seeing how much damage you can take and how long before you have to root & back off. This will get you used to tanking a mob for a round or two until your MT gets agro off of you.

Once you get into your high 50s and 60s your gear will have all the stats and your wis will naturally increase, worry about keeping yourself alive and that means AC & HPs.

Well, the #1 question you should ask yourself is....

"How often do I run out of mana?"

The #2 question you should ask yourself is...

"How often do I run out of hit points?"

These are what you should base your gear choices on...

If you run OOM all the time, you probably want a bit more wis... where as if you're dying with 75% mana every single time, you want more HP/AC gear.

More HP = Less deaths = more Canni = more mana.

More wis = More mana.
