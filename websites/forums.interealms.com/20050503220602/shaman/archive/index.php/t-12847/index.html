Gathu
If this is the trial I'm thinking of, bloody forget about it. Shamans might be more useful in it than they are in execution, but it's easier to do exe with 5 people, than this with 6 (including the shaman).

When several friends and I got bored off our asses and did all the trials, stoning was the second hardest (flame being the hardest by a huge margin).

But if you insist...

1) How often do the 2 bow men shoot

Fairly standard delay, maybe every 2-3 seconds. The damage was negligable, but if they get on your casters it can interrupt a lot.

2) How hard do the mobs hit and can they be slowed rooted

Like 390 or some ****. Half can be mez'd, I honestly forget if they can be rooted, and they can be slowed but mitigation is bad enough that it doesn't matter much. If memory serves.

3) Are the bow menn up when fighting the mob boss Don't really recall, probably. The event itself was way harder than boss for us, but then, we had a really well equipped 65 warrior, so most of our trouble was adds getting on healers, not their damage while hitting tank.

4) what is critical in passing this test? High lvl war/tank? More healer types. Are chanters a must? Certain spells?

An enchanter helps a lot. You should have at least 2 healers, so that they can heal eachother. You have to be ready to kite mobs, as well, they hit hard enough that casters/priests ain't tanking them for very long waiting for a melee to get them off. And yes, a decent tank.

5) Any specific tatic that helped and if you were successful or not Not dying. That might seem lame, but it was simply a question of not losing people. Mezzing, healing, kiting, etc. whatever makes people not become corpses.

For reference, order of tests from easiest to hardest for us was Torture, Execution, Suffocation, Lashing, Stoning, Flame.
