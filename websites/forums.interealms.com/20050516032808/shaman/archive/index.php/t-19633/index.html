Chifari
I've also tested this spell, and, let me tell you, it is damn good.

It is one of those things you look at on paper and go "hmmm ho hum whatever". But once you start using it, you just incorporate it into your playstyle and never look back.

Believe me, you *will* be converted once you use it a few times.

First, it's almost never resisted (maybe once in 100 casts for me). It's difficult to quantify just how good it is to be able to slow a mob RIGHT NOW. It also seems to be fairly low aggro compared to Turgur's... and even if you grab aggro, the mob is probably slowed. I didn't have any SCS or anything, and I hardly ever stole aggro with this spell (and sometimes did with Turgur's).

When adds come, you can alternate Turgur's and Nihil (or just nihil) and get them slowed extremely quickly... much more quickly than with a Time's Antithesis, even, due to resists. It's like you malos/slow the mob in 1.5 seconds.. what isn't good about that? This spell singlehandedly makes a shaman MUCH more effective a healer by being able to handle MULTIPLE mob pulls (shaman could always slow/heal effectively on 1-mob pulls).

Also, when mobs mitigate partially or more, 60% becomes 30% and isn't all that big a gap between 30 and 37.5. There are many situations where the slow can simply be your primary slow... you just let the cleric CH a little sooner and you crank out more dps.

Finally, this addresses the situation shaman have talked about with "tnargs loops" and needing a faster heal. Surprise!!! You actually don't. What this slow allows you to do is totally annihilate the mob's dps with one quick 1.5 second cast. You can basically think of it as a new shaman fast heal... for those situations in, say, Earth, when 2 mobs come at you, a shaman with this spell can nihil/nihil fast, thus negating the need for a larger direct heal.

For raids, the spell is pretty darn useless... but it is going to be a nice addition for addressing the PRIMARY problem shamans had as main healers, and that is dealing with when 2 mobs hit camp and you need to be slowing/healing quickly at the same time. Even if I had Time's Antithesis (I don't), I think nihil would still see lots of use.

My assessment of this spell has nothing to do with being Time or Elemental geared. It's just good, no matter who you are.
