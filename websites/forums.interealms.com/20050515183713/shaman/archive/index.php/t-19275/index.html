kowalsky
usually shaman want to get Canni 5 asap, not wasting any AA before getting it.

to get Canni 5 (class AA), you need MC3 (archetype AA)
to get MC3, you need Regen3 (general AA).

but there is also some requirement to be able to get archetype and class AA:

General: These may be purchased starting at level 51.

Archetype: These may be purchased starting at level 55 AND after you have spent 6 points in the General catagory.

Class: These may be purchased starting at level 59 AND after you have spent 12 points in the Archetype catagory.

( http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=14626 )

so you need 6 general AA before starting MC3.. hence the common choice for the first 3 AA = run3, and the next 3 AA = regen 3 if you choose the fastest path.

but you are also facing the "level" limit, most will get Run3 at 51, then level to 59/60/61 (or even 65) and get the next AAs untill Canni 5, then done.

but some might want to spend more time doing others AA before ding 59. all in all, it really depends on the amount of time you need to get 1 AA. :)
