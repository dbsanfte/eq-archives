Kaalisti_Painstorm
Use at own risk: (sorry but you gotta say that these days)

First, get your drivers from: http://www.guru3d.com, they're the biggest & baddest download site out there. Unless you're using the newest stuff you may not see the things listed below. If you have issues with beta drivers grab certified ones instead.

Second, get Destroyer from there if you're not running XP, and follow the directions. You could possibly be having old resident drivers conflicting.
(I think xp users don't need to use it)

Third, tweak your settings thus:

Display Settings > Settings > Advanced
Geforce/NVidia > to the pop up screen on the side

1. nView Display mode > Device Settings > Color Correction > Digital Vibrance = medium (makes the colors go wow)
2. Performance & Quality >
Image Settings = Performance (tweak as needed for speed)
AntiAliasing = 2X (tweak as needed)
Anisotropic = off
3. Direct 3D settings = Enable fog
Mipmap Detail = High Performance (tweak as needed)

Now then, all that said...
Unless you're running a 3 gigahertz, with a gig of dual channel factory matched RAM, you ARE going to experience lag in PoK and Bazaar. If you don't have the system behind the video card, it won't do you much good.

To increase your speed in game:
Turn off Luclin models you don't care about. Leave horses on if you have one.
Turn Clip plane (in display settings) to something other than farthest, and bring it in when in laggy zones.
Turn Opacity down to at least 20%, and off for huge raids.
Turn Particles to medium, and off for huge raids.

Hope this helps.
