When slow mitigation first went in (PoP went live), it was a hard cap. So we all slowed the same, no matter what. Plus there were negative slows, which means you actually sped up the mob (Fennin Ro comes to mind). After a few weeks and a lot of uproar about the both, they fixed it to a percentage, so in your example, it would be 5/4/3.
