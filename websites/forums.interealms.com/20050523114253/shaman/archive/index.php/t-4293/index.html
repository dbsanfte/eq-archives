Kriel Darkbane
Where you work on their defense isn't as important as when. L 24 is coming up fast on the time when it gets to be really hard to fix. In just a few more levels, all the dark blue cons will be double attacking, and hard. Then it will be very hard to find something they can tank for any time at all (especially the enchanter). So find the lowest blue con mobs you can find, and have them work defense on those. Which particular mobs you should use depends heavily on where you are (use something close by). I can't think of anything especially puny offensively you can use, though...

Often, dungeon mobs are weaker than same-level outdoor mobs, so if you can find a dungeon with mobs the right level that may speed things up. Paludal would have been great a couple levels before 24, and might still work. The shrooms there hit softly for their level, and there might be a few dark blue ones left. If not, you could use the highest-level shiknars, but they hit harder than the shrooms (sensate reishi's).
