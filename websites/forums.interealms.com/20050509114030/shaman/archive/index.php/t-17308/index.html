shamuofxev
Being in a very similar situation (130s AA, same raid type, doing LR right now) I've been thinking about this too. I would start the healing AAs after this. With some of the elemental boss/time encounters you will spend a lot of time spamming tnargs on the main tank, and HA/HG will help a lot. Then I'd go for SCM, it's around 10% mana savings, so the equivalent of 400 - 500 mana. Better value then planar power.
RC3 is situational, but I know at least one PoFire fight where RC3 is the only way to cure your group fast (GRC is a pain, all you do is cast RGC to get everyone cured before the next AoE hits).
VP was a bit of a disappointment for me, I don't use it nearly as much as I thought.
QB2 is very nice if you have a haste of mithaniel item, otherwise go for qb3 to max Q.

Njorls
Planar power can wait a little bit, and I sometimes wonder if I'll ever have enough aa to train innate enlightenmet.

SCF 1 is pretty much useless, I think you only crit for 25% more at that level, you need to train it up to level 3 to do the double damage crits. Don't train SCF unless you're willing to put 12 pts in it.

SCM is nice, I got it right after Canni5, this should be high on your list. It's a better mana pres than planar boots, and it stacks with everything.

HA/AHA is nice. 14 pts (HA3, AHA1) will make Tnarg's 13% better.

HG3 and AHG1 for 14 pts will make Tnarg's crit for 12% of the time for 14aa, again, a nice way to spend pts.

RC3 is ok, I only got it because my guild was going to Plane of Time and I thought it'd be useful there. I did fine with spell RGC, Rad Cure 1 and blood of nadox in elemental planes. RC1 will cure the Rathe ae, the single most annoying ae in the game.
