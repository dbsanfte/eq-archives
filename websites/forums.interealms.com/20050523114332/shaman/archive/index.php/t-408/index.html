Chutoi
Honestly, it doesn't matter, Hamesh. Both require guilds to get, and it depends what faction your guild is on. Presumably you are in a guild, but if not, I would never recommend choosing a guild just based on faction. Choose one where you know and like the people, and then get whichever armor you like.

I think (and this is from memory) that AC is a bit higher on Spirit Callers, and stats and resists are a bit better on Wolf Callers.

Both sets of armors are from ToV mobs. The Wolf is from the mobs in the Halls of Testing (east ToV), and the Spirit are from the mobs in West ToV (though technically all the mobs in north, west, or the entry area can drop the armor for Spirit). I believe HoT is a little easier than the other areas. Easier is a relative term. The armor can be done with 2 good groups, but getting that kind of caliber firepower in a pick-up group isn't likely (hence the guild requirement). All the mobs are a minimum of level 60 in ToV. The non-named range between 60-63 I believe.

Given the level of difficulty (and probably the competition for the armor within your groups or guilds) don't worry about which is better. Go with what you can get! :)

If your guild is trying to decide on a faction, go with giant faction. Being on CoV faction limits your raid choices too much, imo.

Chutoi

*Edited -- let me emphasize two GOOD groups--mostly level 60s with very good equipment and very good players. If you are in a slightly younger guild, I'd go with 4 groups minimum.
