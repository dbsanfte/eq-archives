Sthaz
Carnage = +40 Str, Dex, Agi and Attk. It will stack with HoS (tho HoS will overwrite, it can be recast). It also stacks with any other Talisman line spell (Kragg at least, only one I tried). It stacks with other Agi, Dex, and Stam buffs as well. It will not stack with Str buffs or Rage (not sure on VoB) or Focus/Khura's. I'm guessing it won't stack with Avatar either. However, HoS + Carnage + Deliriously Nimble = (approx) 100 str, 90 dex, 90 Agi, 40 Attack, 250 HPs. Not bad.

It WILL save lots of mana for buffs, but it does NOT save much time at 15 seconds per cast and only lasting about 6 minutes.
