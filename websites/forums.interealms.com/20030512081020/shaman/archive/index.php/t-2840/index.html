Gallaena
I know plenty about being poor!

But, given what I know now about the game, if I were in your shoes, I'd gather trade skill supplies and sell them. Take a look at www.eqtraders.com to check out what you might be needed.

Off the top of my head, the following easily obtained items:

-Spider silks (created into swatches)
-Spiderling silks (created into threads)
-HQ pelts
-MQ pelts
-LQ pelts
-Wolf meat
-Bear meat
-Mammoth meat

Again, this is just off the top of my head. Besides trade skill items, you could save and sell stacks of bone chips, stacks of bat wings, stacks of snake skins.

After you have saved up enough to buy a set of banded, then hunt the South Karana for even more animal pelts. They are quite common. Another place to go after you get some decent armor is Permafrost. Save all the permafrost crystals you can get your hands on and sell them. The last offer I heard on Cazic-Thule was 1k for a stack of 20 - sounds like a really good deal to me. Say, long term, you spend a week in Perma and have 1 or 2 stacks... 1-2k for your coffers.

When you have a reasonable amount of skins, pelts, and silks gathered, trot over to EC (or use your auction chat channel), and sell your items. I'd gather information about the worth of the items before I sold them though... again, take a look at eqtraders. In fact, you might be able to sell the trade supplies through their message boards.

Go back to Halas and surrounding areas. You'll make more money there.

Good luck!
