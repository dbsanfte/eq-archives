I tried some Kodiaks in the Great Divide at level 36. They are good exp. The caves are just below the enterance to velks. There are a few kinds of kodiaks with feral cave kodiaks being the lowest and ferocious cave kodiaks being the strongest. I would stick with the ferals if posible at my level. I set up camp near the wall and pull out of the caves. There are some wanderers so you need to be cautious about those, Nice thing is you can zone into velks if you get into trouble. I was getting about a half blue per kill. The loot here is pretty dismal. Occasional kodiak claws and hardened kodiak armor. The named, Icetooth can be tough at low levels so be careful if you see him. This is another viable option for levels 34-39 or so. I would recomend checking it out if you are looking for a new spot. Bring a group and go into the caves for real good exp.
