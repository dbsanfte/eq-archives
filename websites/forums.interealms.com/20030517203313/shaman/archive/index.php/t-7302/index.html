note: iksars can use the cazic thule symbols as well

Regent Symbol of Innoruuk (neriak) (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1254)

magic based 30% snare that lasts over 6 ticks (36 seconds)(could be extended by focus items)

Initiate Symbol of Cazic Thule (paineel) (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1207)

undead-only fear that lasts 3 ticks (18 seconds)
cast time: ?

Disciple Symbol of Cazic Thule (paineel) (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=1238)
casts Panic Animal (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=241&source=Live)
animal-only fear that lasts 3 ticks (18 seconds)
cast time: ?

I personly prefer following innoruuk. The snare has been considerably more valuable to me than fear would, plus the regent necklase has some decet stats (7 AC, 3 Wis, 15 Mana). As well, as Smell mentioned, you can get an illusion halfling mask for the ocassional trip into the good cities.

Regarding getting the faction needed to start the quest, you can get a note from a human, behind and above the fire pit near the trainer in grobb, that you can turn in to I think the quest giver in Nek/3rd gate that greatly increase faction. Doing this twice allowed me to start the actual quest.
