Brohg
Spell Casting Fury (Archetype AA) does two things with each rank. 1) increases chance to crit on dd spells 2) increases the damage done when you crit dd spells.

Spell Casting Fury Mastery (shamans don't get this one), Fury of Magic (PoP Ability), and Fury of Magic Mastery (GoD) all only increase the chance to crit.

From what I remember, you have a total of 22% chance to crit a dd after all those are complete. Mage Compendium had that number for me, when I looked months ago.

Destructive Fury (OoW) only increases the damage done when you crit, does not increase chance to crit.

Critical Affliction (GoD) and Advanced Critical Affliction (OoW) both only increase the chance of crit on dot spells. The damage done by a crit is not variable.
