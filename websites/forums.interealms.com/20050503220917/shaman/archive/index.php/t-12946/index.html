EQ checks for SITTING every 6 seconds. if you sitting.. you get your sitting meditation, It doesn't matter if you stand for 5 of those seconds.. as long as you are sitting on every 6 ( tick )

so canni dance.

/sit off
/cast 2 ( or whatever gem slot canni is in )
/pause 17 ( pauses 1.7 seconds - just longer than canni i think )
/sit on

so this makes you
1) Stand
2) cast canni
3) waits till the spell is cast
4) makes you sit.

you wait for your mana to go up one percent ( to get the timing down )
then do macro.. stand canni sit. then you get sitting med. then canni again.. wait for sitting med. repeat..

it works alot better than just canni a bunch of times then letting yourself regen back up.
