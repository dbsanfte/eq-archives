I think anyone who isn't part of the trinity plus 3 DPS is going to be carried - not useless, but any DPS class is better.

Don't confuse the Executioner trial trinity for the standard trinity. The standard trinity is Warrior, Cleric, Enchanter, while the trial trinity is Healing, CC and DPS. The two are similar but different.

Tanking is ONLY necessary for the Prime Executioner and then the quality of tank only needs to be proportionate to the the quality of the healer backing him/her up combined with the overall DPS of the group (more DPS = faster the mobs drop = less healing needed).

-- CC really ought to be an enchanter to be most effective, unless the DPS is through AE casters.
-- Healing can definitely be Cleric or Shaman and probably a druid.
-- DPS is anyone who has high DPS with a fast impulse response. (i.e. auto-attack or nukes, not DoTs)

That said, shamans do NOT have to be carried through a trial group. Plunk us into the healer spot, give us a tank who can stand up to Vathoch on our heals, a CCer and sprinkle liberally with DPS.
