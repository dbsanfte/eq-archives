Jaruden
I have a radeon 7200, it runs pretty well, but in high polygon areas it drags a good bit (bazaar is really bad). SOE has done a bit of optimizing for nvidia cards and drivers, if you primarily play EQ and not other games, you'd probably be better off going nvidia. The GeForce 4400ti series is pretty good, and runs anywhere from $150 to $250 depending on ram and who made the board. I think some of the earlier gf4's might be a little cheaper, though obviously won't perform as well.
