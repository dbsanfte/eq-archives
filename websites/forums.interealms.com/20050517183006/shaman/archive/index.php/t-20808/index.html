Taladorm
Just for refrence you can cancel your Eq billing from there web site.
This is from the SOE knowledge base.

You can also cancel your EverQuest account through our website: www.station.sony.com/en/. Once at the website, select My Account in the upper right corner. After signing in, Billing Info will be in the upper left, directly under View Registration. On the Billing Information page you will find Subscriptions on the left, click it to display Your Subscriptions. Under My Subscriptions choose EverQuest (# Month) Active. On the following page, click Edit Subscription and then Cancel Subscription.
