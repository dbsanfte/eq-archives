Note: NONE of the dragons in WW are soloable unless you're well used to the pathing and patrolling in the zone. There's no perfectly safe spot to pull a dragon, but there are several spots that are very safe as long as you know when the wanderers are coming through. Go there witha group to learn the patterns before trying it yourself, or you'll be having a long day of being naked. And BTW, every solo you do is going to be a close fight, get ready for some adrenaline-pumping action! :)

Takes me roughly 25 minutes to take out a typical ww aoer which has a ~50% chance of dropping a set piece of armor (with a fairly random grade). Add that to the fact that only 3 pieces of armor that are obtainable solo are in any demand (mantles/cloaks/bracers), and all 3 are a pig to get compared to say, boots or legs (mantle/cloak = 1 easy static source, bracer = 1 easy rare wandering source, there are other droppers, but they are not imho soloable).

There are far better (and safer/easier) cash farming spots than ww dragons.
