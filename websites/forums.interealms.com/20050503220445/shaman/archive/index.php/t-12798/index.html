So.. I'm in a group, and we start to break up leaving me (55 shammy), 58 necro, 56 rogue and a 52 Ranger... We had been making fun of the warrior we had before he left because when I would slow and get aggro I'd take less damage then him.. So we figure let's try me as the main tank.

It worked well, in fact the exp was better then the full group with cleric and a warrior. I only kept everyone with HoS/Sta/Haste, and even after my Hero Bond faded (leaving me with self buffs), the group worked well.. The exp was better then when we had a cleric and a warrior.. Kills were almost as fast. All I did was keep Stoicism on and Sup. Heal every once and a while. I'd E bolt for aggro. Was always FM or close to it, and the only death we had was because the necro couldnt land snare. One time we got 2 ravens and a treant and we still survived.

Was a very good time and good bragging rights for the guild. Got alittle awe inspired unto those who dared to listen to my story.
