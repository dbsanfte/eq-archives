weissblau
I agree, fantastic feature. So many things done RIGHT that could have been done WRONG:

1. Doesn't rememorize spells that are the same between the two lists

2. Forgets the changed-out spells before attempting to load the list so you can have the same spell in different slots in the different lists (if they didn't forget them all first, you'd end up attempting to mem a spell you already had memmed).

3. Instant lookup of the spells, all you pay is the mem time. You're standing until you hit the button. In many cases this means you can mem a good bit of a new spell list in the time it takes the mob to charge you due to sit aggro.

The other area that nobody is talking much about is the right-click menus for the spell gems. Empty a spell gem and right-click on it and you get a list of spells BY CATEGORY, with their level listed, and you get the insta-lookup insta-mem behavior of the spell lists but for a SINGLE spell. IMO this is gonna be very useful for swapping out debuffs and/or swapping in DOTs during combat.
