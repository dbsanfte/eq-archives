I am assuming you mean that you were filling in a form on a web site, and maybe you hit the escape key, and all of your text poofed. I have done this so many times it is unbelieveable (thanks to heavy use of the vi text editor in unix) and someone told me I could do CTRL-Z to undo my escape key. So this might be too late for you, J, but for anyone who has lost their entire post by hitting the wrong key, try hitting CTRL-Z and it should all come back.
