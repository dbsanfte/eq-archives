Aktunka
I have been thinking about this for a while, and with some of the changes they are making in game lately, it seemed like a good time to bring this up. I am proposing the following AA skill tweak for class AA for shaman.

Make the Quick Buff skill be a Quick Buff/Debuff skill.

This would adress what the primary function of what a real shaman is on raids. Also, adding a quickness function to a classes main function is not without precedent. Wizards and druids both have the class AA ability to train for quick direct damage spells to reduce the casting time on their DD's and both of those classes typically have that as one of their major roles. I don't believe that it would be asking anything out of line for the possibility of our class AA skill for quick buff to also affect debuffs as well (since the truth is that there is only one spell that it is worth having it for now as it is).

Comments?
