1) getting to Akheva: run through Dawnshroud to Maidens Eye, in ME take a hard right and follow wall until you see castle entrance to AR. invis should be up while running athough there isn't much chance of aggro. When you get to AR entrance, run exactly halfway between the 2 Xius Vi (they see invis) guys on the steps, if you get too close to either you will get aggro and get smacked... even if you do, it won't be too bad, you can make it to the AR zone. Once you get into the ruin, keep going around the big round thing, (watch for the Life Tappers) and you will see the zone.

2) inside Akheva: cast bearform to see, set up right at the entrance facing the long tunnel. Get your alpha dogdog set up and buffed, and and set to guard. There are 3 centi dators in the first long tunnel, 1 in the middle and 1 on either side at the end. You can single each of them. Pull with slow ALL THE WAY BACK to your dog. If you don't, you may aggro one of the wandering transient patrollers. These guys are necros and can ruin your whole day... after pulling with slow, I rooted, plague, BoN, and E-bolt (reroot as necessary), and let dogdog go to work. At about 45%, I recast BoN. That usually is enough. The centi dators don't hit very hard, so dog is usually good with a regen. With KEI, HP buff, and canni III, I was pulling and killing one every 6 minutes or so. There are 5 centis total that can be pulled, and they respawn almostly perfectly.

3) dealing with the transient patrollers: my method is to kill one (just be prepared to get nasty spells thrown at you) and avoid the other. They appear on the same cycle as the centis so once you kill one (or both) you are good for 1/2 an hour or so.

4) XP and loot: I make about a blue regular xp an hour here... which is about average for me soloing. Loot is up to 4 pp per mob, along with centi meat, toes, twilight worms (fishing bait I think), essence of darkness, and a very occasional weapon (have seen spear and short sword so far, no long sword).

hope this helps.

the centis and transients are not undead.
