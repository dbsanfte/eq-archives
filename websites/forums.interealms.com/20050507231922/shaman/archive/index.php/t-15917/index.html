Sariss
Warriors' also have a class bonus, though I thought it was 10 not 5%, but the point is that he was counting both bonuses.

Awhile back, SoE took out the CLASS based xp modifiers. If you remember way back, Shadow Knights and Paladins (and maybe Rangers?) paid a 40% CLASS xp penalty. At the time, a Troll Shadowknight was the slowest leveling class in the game.

Warriors and Rogues actually got bonuses... I can't recall exactly, but I think Warriors got 10%, and Rogues 5%.

RACE bonuses / penalties were also there. But people knew less about these until one of those stat based spoiler sites broke it all down. For the longest time, the popular belief was that because Humans had no racial bonus (other than having no racial weakness, which to some, is a bonus) that Humans had a RACE bonus to xp.

SoE came out and said that Humans have no bonus, or penalty, aside from being "average" in everything.

Halflings, as a RACE, actually get a 5% xp bonus. No one knew why really, Halflings had good stats and starting location, but that's the way it is... probably because they have to put up with being called Hobbits.

So, back in the day... a Halfling Warrior was the fastest leveling race/class combo that existed, and a Troll SK was the worst.

I can't remember when, but it's been a long time now.. SoE took out all CLASS based xp bonus/penalties. Once players realized the xp hits, they started to think that having a Shadowknight in your group, would take an unfair split of the xp to compensate for thier lower leveling rate (SoE had said that xp in a group, is split, such that each member of the group would level at approx the same rate). Paladins and SKs were actively being ignored for groups, simply because people feared they would suck xp, even if they were an amazing player.

Anyhow... as far as we know right now...

Trolls / Iksar : -20% to xp
Ogres : -15% to xp
Halflings : +5% to xp

So a Halfling anything, is the fastest leveling class in terms of xp per kill. But 5% is not enough to pick a halfling unless that's the race you enjoy... personally I like them... sneak and hide on my old cleric was awesome.
