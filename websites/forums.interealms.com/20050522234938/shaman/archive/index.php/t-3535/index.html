but if you really want to learn go google for it. There are several good pages on the internet about it.

Really good for melees - no more battlespam flying by and obscuring important messages.

Here is what I have in my eqcleint.ini for the hitsmode section.

[HitsMode]
MyHitsMode=3
MyHitsAlwaysDraw=1
MyHitsX=222
MyHitsY=850
MyHitsCount=4
MyHitsFontSize=2
MyHitsShowHeader=1
MyHitsShowBG=1
MyHitsBGWidth=300
OtherHitsMeMode=3
OtherHitsMeAlwaysDraw=1
OtherHitsMeX=222
OtherHitsMeY=925
OtherHitsMeCount=4
OtherHitsMeFontSize=2
OtherHitsMeShowHeader=1
OtherHitsMeShowBG=1
OtherHitsMeBGWidth=300
OtherHitsOtherMode=3
OtherHitsOtherAlwaysDraw=1
OtherHitsOtherX=222
OtherHitsOtherY=1000
OtherHitsOtherCount=4
OtherHitsOtherFontSize=2
OtherHitsOtherShowHeader=1
OtherHitsOtherShowBG=1
OtherHitsOtherBGWidth=300


Here are my 2 buttons macros to turn it off and on, taken from my character .ini.

Page2Button9Name=HitOff
Page2Button9Color=5
Page2Button9Line1=/hitsmode my on
Page2Button9Line2=/hitsmode me on
Page2Button9Line3=/hitsmode other on

Page2Button8Name=HitsON
Page2Button8Color=16
Page2Button8Line1=/hitsmode me m3 -1 -1 -1 -1 -1 1 300 1
Page2Button8Line2=/hitsmode my m3 -1 -1 -1 -1 -1 1 300 1
Page2Button8Line3=/hitsmode other m3 -1 -1 -1 -1 -1 1 300 1
