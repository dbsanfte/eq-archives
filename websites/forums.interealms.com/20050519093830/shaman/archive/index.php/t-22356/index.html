cattywumpus
You will still need to do some faction work before you can turn in those bone chips (just less than a non-Cazic worshiper). An Inny troll is still able to do the quest with just a little more work.

The best way I found was to kill the iksar bandit types in FoB. They will give you Legion of Cabilis faction that you need for the troopers and Brood of Kotiz faction hits which will make you safer in Cabilis once you get there without giving any important negative hits. Once you are non-KoS to the troopers you can start handing in those chips for faster faction hits with everyone else.

You can hand bone chips to Trooper Mozo while still KoS to the Crusaders if you stand on his left side as far away from him as you can get and still open the trade window.

There is a very informative thread (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=8900) in the Quests forum already. It's worth the read if you want your cudgel.

I would say that the snare necklace is much better than a few hundred bone chips though. At level 20 I would consider re-rolling myself but then I don't mind the low-level game and can level a toon back to 20 in a couple of days.
