Kukgulano
With the PoP expansion I have a far greater use of the Worker's Sledgemallet I got during one of my OT quests.

The mallet is a Giant 2 handed blunt weapon that procs a gate spell to the shipyards in the OT. It procs after level 50, so don't bother trying it before then. For those who don't know about it: You must raise your faction in the Outpost by handing in the Sarnak braids of the berzerkers in the zone to the Skelly Captain, then when you are "Amiable" you visit the Skelly Foreman and give him 2 pieces of jade.. he will give you the mallet and some exp.

I carry it everywhere I go now... and if i need some transport all I do is whip it out, slow a target, start wailing on it and Whammo I am in the OT.

A quick dash over to the PoK port site there at the outpost and I am within moments away from almost everywhere. Several times I have bound right where my group is hunting and made this trip to the nexus to catch a KEI, and I gate back within a couple minutes.

In my humble opinion, this hammer should be in the inventory of every Troll and Ogre shaman who seeks easy transport.
