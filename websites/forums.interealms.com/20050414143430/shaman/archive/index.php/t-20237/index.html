Why did you reformat the HDD? Was it because of a virus? If so, you could have a MBR virus, which can only be cleaned by booting from a special cleaning floppy made on a non-infected machine. Check for viruses from a clean bootable floppy with a command-line scanner first to eliminate them (some are very sneaky and can hide themselves from virus scanners once in memory) If that comes back negative, check that the EQ directory and ini files are not checked read only, that would explain why window positions aren't being saved. Other than that, maybe one (or more) of your drivers are out of date, or an incompatible version. Check the versions first, as it is the simplest explanation and usual problem.
