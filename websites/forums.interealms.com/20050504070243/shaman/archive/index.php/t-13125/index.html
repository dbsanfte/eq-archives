Shadow Knights have some of the best mana regen of all the classes. Shaman and Necromancers are the only two I would say that have greater. Pact of Hate has the same mana regeration as KEI. The recent changes to mental corruption when combined with the Soul Abrasion AA can net vast amounts of mana. SKs get the Mental Clairity AA abilities as well and have access to a great number of Flowing Thought items.

Higher end Shadow Knights can put out some very respectable DPS as well all though it does drop off considerably when tanking (due to riposte/parry/dodge etc of the mobs.) The latest SK pet is a pure monster and with Augmentation of Death he is a buzzsaw. The new spears coupled with fast running high DPS dots and great offensive new AA (Speed of the Knight, Knights Advantage) really allow them to push the upper ends of DPS.

Having said that a Necromancer would be a great partner for exping. They can snare split, FD have great DPS and in a pinch ressurect as well :)

Do not sell any class short, there are many people out there who will suprise you.
