Depending on the rest of the raid, I can chain rain or nuke from about 95% mob health and I don't get summoned or attacked. If I lay on a third or fourth dot, I get summoned soon after.

SCS reduces the initial aggro generated by a spell. If your spell continuously generates aggro (like DoTs), SCS really does nothing. When a DoT lands, it places counters on a mob and that generates aggro (for poison it's a lot of aggro) and that will be reduced. The 1000+ damage per tick for a few stacked DoTs is still pushing you up the aggro list pretty quickly. You get an extra boost because the people who do the most recent damage on a tick get a little extra aggro. DoTs are the last damage to be calculated on a tick. This is why tanks call off other attacks if they lose aggro, they want to be the only one attacking the mob and generating aggro. Even if one other person is still attacking, it makes regaining the mob's attention a little more difficult.
