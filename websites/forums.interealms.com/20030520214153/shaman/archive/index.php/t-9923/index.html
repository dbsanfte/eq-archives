Gear tips you have gotten so far are good.

In general, you can fill slots with Ry'gorr, Totemic, Jaundice Bone (JB). 6/65 rings great. Get a nice piercer, and 1HB (I never use 2HB because like extra AC of having a shield, but some folks love two-handers). SBS nice shield basic shield.

Best play tip can share is to MAX YOUR DEFENSE evert 2-3 levels. A lot of shaman who either (1) level too fast or (2) group a lot do not max defense because they do not melee enough. Defense skills=ac points that you will need when you start slowing mobs :)

Also, as many others will say, Shaman really come into their own when they get their pets, in their 30's. Be patient, tis worth it.
