Vulash
I did the exact same thing you did. Came back a few months back after over a year off.

Here are a few things I've noticed since I returned -

First, in PoP mobs hit much harder, so be careful to let your tank have decent agro before you slow. In some groups you'll be able to slow on inc like old times, but in some (with wars especially) you can't or you will get eaten on resists. Leading with Malo* is now a great idea as it gives your war a little time, and lessens your chance of a slow resist.

Second, you won't see as many pulls of 5-8 mobs and the group living, stuff it to hard. Even if you have a chanter be very wary on CC and help any way you can. Learn quickly what mobs can and can't be rooted in your xp spots. (for example in PoV - Golems, fiends, and small larvae can be rooted. Larger Larvae (most of them), skeletons, spiders, crawlers all have a chance to be immune to root)

Third - most xp mobs will now enrage, be ready to back your pet off if your tank isn't positioning the mob properly.

Fourth - Pets no longer break mez, and when mez is broken everyone in the area receives a message as to who broke it. There is an exeption though, and somehow every once in a blue moon a pet can break a mez, i believe it happens on a riposte.

Fifth - DoTs from different people of teh same type/spell now stack. So 2 shaman could stack 2 BoNs

Finally - vindi is doable by one group now /boggle

I'm sure there are tons more, but thats all i can think of this early in the day ;P
