Kianor
Post patch experience:

Zoned into Valor, 20ish people there, I did a "/ooc 61 shaman lfg", and got a tell from an SK asking if I could come buff him. He was 62, but his group had a 56 cleric and he'd just died at OC.

I went there, and their enchanter was just leaving, so I was invited into the group. The SK did warn me in a tell that if he died again, he would leave. It was the lowest group I've had in PoV, in fact, the only time where I haven't been the lowest person in the group. 62 SK, 62 necro, 62 rogue, 61 shaman, 57 ranger, 56 cleric.

The ranger was a touch irritating, "wow, this is sweet exp", "I wonder if I can make 59 today", etc. He was also doing archery some of the time, which at 57 is pointless, except to skill up.

I wasn't in that group very long - a friend needed help so I left. But while I was there, the exp seemed about the same as before per kill, though our pulls were slower.

Second group I got was in PoN. After a couple of false starts crossing the river (*sigh*, it's not *that* hard), we killed hobgoblins. The experience there seemed to be a bit better than before, but I wasn't there long enough to tell for sure.

A lot of guildies said they really noticed the improvement in experience in PoD, and I've heard a few people say that PoV exp rates are much the same as before.

Seems to me that maybe the PoV exp modifier was nerfed more than the tier 1 planes, so that it's not that much better to exp in any more. Anyone else have any experiences to back this up or contradict it?
