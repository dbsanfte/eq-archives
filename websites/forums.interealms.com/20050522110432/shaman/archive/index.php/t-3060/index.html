Samuqan
There's one in Greater Faydark.

Being a troll, you might be KOS to the North Karana guards. One of them usually paths through the spires if I remember correctly.

But at level 15, the other spots are likely either very dangerous to get to, or a long ways away (unless you have a port).

The Bazaar connects from the Nexus. When you are in the Nexus, you port into the center of the zone. There are four pathways that lead to four miniature spires where you can port back down to Norrath. Three of these four tunnels lead to zones (one is a blocked passageway).

The best method I have for knowing which one is the Bazaar is that the opening of the tunnel to it is guarded by one guard and a female that is not dressed like the other guards. She seems out of place. Look for her. Or, you can hail each of the guards and they tell you which zone the passageway leads to.
