This post is just to consolidate alot of the information at www.eqtraders.com on the "how-to's" of making Nightmare Terror Chain armor as a reference tool for myself and those gathering components.

brick of nightmare iron - drops off mephits, hobgoblins in Plane of Nightmare

nightmare mephit blood - mephits in Plane of Nightmare

fetid slime - slarghilugs in Plane of Nightmare

midnight stone - various mobs in Plane of Nightmare (Stalkers are what we usually hunt.)

Powdered midnight stone is made from combining 3 midnight stones in a mortar & pestle. (NO-FAIL combine)Yield 4, wt 0.1, tiny, all/all, (stackable) Midnight stones drop in PoN.

Nightmare terror temper is made by combining nightmare mephit blood, fetid slime, powdered midnight stone in a tanaan brew barrel trivial <=169; wt 0.1, tiny, all/all, (stackable); used to temper nightmare iron. Mephit blood and fetid slime drop in PoN.

Nightmare iron rings are made by combining nightmare iron, nightmare terror temper, & file in a tanaan forge, which yields 2 nightmare iron rings,(stackable) wt 4.0, small, trivial <=149

Distilled Mana Vials are made by casting the 400 mana cost level 39 Enchanter spell Distill mana. Components required are a poison vial and 2 sapphires.

Enchanted Bar of Nightmare Iron is made by combining a distilled mana vial and nightmare iron ore in a tanaan forge, trivial 135, wt 5.0, (stackable), small, all/all

The SPELL: Imbue Nightmare is made by combining a raw diamond, the spell: Imbue Diamond, PLUS a nightmare mephit blood in a planar jeweller's kit. This is a NO FAIL combine, and results in the paper spell scroll for scribing. (The imbue diamond spell is found in Temple of Marr in North Freeport from Locarme Zennix (92, 287)

Black Diamonds of Nightmare are made by casting the spell Imbue Nightmare (by a 62ENC/NEC). Components required are a raw diamond and a nightmare mephit blood.

Nightmare emblems are made by combining a blank emblem with an enchanted bar of nightmare iron, and an imbued black diamond of nightmares in a planar jeweller's kit, trivial >250, magic, wt 1.0, small, all/all. (Blank emblems are vendor bought in PoK.)

Nightmare Terror Chainmail is made by combining a tanaan smithing hammer, MEDIUM chainmail pattern, an emblem of nightmares, 1-3 of the Nightmare iron rings(1 ring bracer; 2 rings coif, sleeves, gloves, boots; 3 rings tunic, leggings), and a nightmare terror temper in a tanaan forge, trivial >250.

(Medium chainmail patterns can be found in PoK from Halena Paerk at loc 72, 1374. A Tanaan smithing hammer purchased from Tallis Paerk at loc 67, 1388.)

The final results are:
Nightmare Terror Chainmail, recommended level 61, required level 46; RNG, ROG, SHM; BAR, DEF, DWF, ELF, GNM, HEF, HFL, HUM, IKS, OGR, TRL, VAH
This armor is a dark purple in color.

bracer AC 14, DEX 4, WIS 3, INT 3, AGI 3, HP 20, mana 15, sv magic 3, wt 1.0, medium
gloves AC 16, STR 2, DEX 5, WIS 2, INT 2, AGI 4, HP 25, mana 15, sv magic 5, wt 3.0, medium
coif AC 18, DEX 8, WIS 2, INT 2, AGI 2, HP 25, mana 20, sv magic 5, wt 3.5, medium
sleeves AC 18, STR 2, DEX 5, WIS 4, INT 4, AGI 5, HP 25, mana 20, sv magic 5, wt 2.5, medium
boots AC 19, DEX 5, WIS 3, INT 3, AGI 5, HP 25, mana 15, sv magic 5, wt 4.0, medium
leggings AC 24, STR 2, DEX 12, WIS 3, INT 3, AGI 7, HP 35, mana 30, sv magic 7, wt 4.5, medium
tunic AC 40, DEX 15, WIS 4, INT 4, AGI 12, HP 40, mana 35, sv magic 10, wt 6.5, medium

Any corrections or additions to this quick-reference guide would be appreciated.
