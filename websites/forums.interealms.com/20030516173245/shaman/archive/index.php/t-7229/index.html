I figure I will give you a summary of what is going on, to make it a bit easier to understand.

The effect is called the Biefeld-Brown effect (discovered in the 1920's-30's by Townsend Brown). What it is is basically when HIGH voltage goes through a capasitor, and if the capasitor is light, IT WILL FLOAT. As in fly.

The object you see is made of balsa wood, aluminum foil, and a small amount of copper wire. The copper wire around the top is connected to an outside powersource (for now), and when the powersource comes on, it will make the object fly. (So it is flying without any moving parts)

The amount of force that the capasitor is able to generate so far is small, just able to float about 90grams max so far from amature experimenters. However, the amount of charge on these capasitors are recieving is the same charge that is from a computer monitor about, so this is a small charge.

Drop a nuclear reactor on this technology (think nuclear sub) and stick it in space without any atmosphere (zero resistance), and you basically have a precursor to the star ship Enterprise.

This really is cool. BTW, check out the experiment where they stick the capasitor in mineral oil, to see if it worked outside of air - it does work.
