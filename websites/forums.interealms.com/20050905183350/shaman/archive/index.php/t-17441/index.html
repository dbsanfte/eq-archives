Zoning times on a fast connection seem to have a lot to do with hard drive speed. If you can afford it, get a sata card and a 10,000 rpm drive with nothing but operating system and EQ on it.
Otherwise you're stuck with regular defrags and the usual optimizations like fixing virtual memory size.
