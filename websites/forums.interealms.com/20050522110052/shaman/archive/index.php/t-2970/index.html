I think most people would say your going a bit heavy on the wisdom gear. There are 2 things to consider.

1) at your lvl wisdom provides very little mana. At 20 you get 4 mana per wis so your earrings are 12 mana your rings are 24. Mana items do have some scaling in them but it's still easy to find a 55mana ring or 25 mana earring.

2) You haven't quiet gotten there yet but at 24 you get cann 1. With it you can turn hps into mana which allows you to recover mana faster than most anyone else but you need to have hps yo use it and AC protects those hps when mobs do aggro on you and take a swing at ya.

Some quick and fairly easy sugguestions for a twink. Ditch the loops and get electrum BS earrings. Ditch at least one of the rings and get a shadel bandit ring. Get a tatterd mantle for your shoulders. A clay guardian shield or SBS sheild makes a nice shield. Regen tunics are about the best tunic a shaman can wear till 60. Coldain skin gloves may be viable on your server too. You could easily get another 200 hps from those items and get 50% more hps and most of them still provide more in raw mana than you are getting from the wis you have in those slots currently.
