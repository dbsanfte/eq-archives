Sometimes it works and sometimes it doesnt. Clerics have to make the same choices other classes do. What mob gets slowed first? What groupmate gets healed first? The only difference is that clerics deal with the life and possible death of the rest of the group.

If a bounced slow makes you splatter then smack a Malx on the mob first. If a cleric tries to CH you then ask politely for fast heals next time but be aware that they may not have any large enough quick heals to save you. Also be aware that clerics may steal your agro to themselves with those fast spammy heals so they get to die.

Just because you die doesnt mean the cleric is always at fault, horrible, a noob or whatever. Take responsibility for your actions as well. Have a quick heal ready for yourself, malx the mobs that you have trouble on, make sure you have all the hp buffs you need. Talk to your group and try to make sure that everyone knows what to do in case of an emergency.

Should I be able to tank unslowed PoP mobs that well?
NO, no one can tank unslowed PoP mobs well at 57 unless they have very substantial hp.

Well, there are a few things you can do.
Make a Turgur's resist hotkey. Explain to the cleric that if you hit your resist hotkey (I just type /g resist myself) or type something approximating the word "resists", to drop a fast heal on you, no questions asked. If the cleric doesn't seem to listen, you can just start casting a Stoicisim on yourself before slowing. Another thing you can try is rooting the mob before slowing, but melee heavy groups (esp. w/ AM3 rangers) won't like that much. If you have a druid in your group, you could mabye sent him/her a tell and ask for backup healing anytime you say resists.

I personally HATE being healed by others except when I'm on rampage (from slowing or debuffing too quickly) or when slow resists. Any other times I expect to be able to take care of myself just fine.

NO, you shouldn't expect to be able to tank unslowed mobs in Tier1 for very long at all. I max out at in the low 5700's with raid buffs, and usually exp at about 5k-5400hp. I don't expect to tank Tier3 mobs unslowed w/o help at 65, and you shouldn't expect to tank unslowed mobs in Tier1 at 57. If the cleric doesn't understand this, find another cleric, or grab a 58 druid instead.

Best of luck,
