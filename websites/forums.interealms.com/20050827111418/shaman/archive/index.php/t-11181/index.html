Spiritblood
AA is just a way of spending experience points gained. So if you kill a mob that gives you 1000 exp and you have set you AA slider at 50 percent 500 points of exp will go to AA and 500 towards normal levelling.

1 point of AA will cost the same amount of exp it takes to go from 51 to 52 so it is a set amount no matter what level you are. So as levels begin to take more and more exp to get through 1 blue of AA will start to get "faster/easier" to get than 1 blue of normal exp.

There are many AA skill but none is more essential than Cannibalization. I am 61 and I have that AA skill but I dont have Runspeed 3 and I dont really miss it. Its all up to you and how you play. Good luck.
