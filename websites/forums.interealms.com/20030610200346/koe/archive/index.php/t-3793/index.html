I've spent quite a lot of time in Droga after various drops such as earring of essence (+10 svm), bracer of vajeen (+10 fire, +10 cold, dru/rang only), and book of rings (forget all stats but it has nice resists). Soothsayer Dreznek (sp?) drops the earring and bracer. He pops in a room with 4 mobs. The initial break can be pretty challenging depending on how many legates are up that will heal. It is also possible to get adds from outside and above if you aren't positioned correctly. I've been wiped out from these adds several times. :) Most of the time it has just been me and a necro doing this room. I have also been able to solo break the room recently but again if there are too many healers mana gets burned down quick. The goblin fanatic drops the stone and the book of rings. He is a random spawn both in the Sooth room and the surrounding room. I think the stone is a common drop. He also drops an iksar necro only book. If you sit in the Sooth room and do the 4 spawns you definitely have a chance of popping a fanatic.
