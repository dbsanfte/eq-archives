Braslo
the real VS is a 3 day spawn, but will never despawn once up (but server/zone reset will despawn him).

VSR will spawn anywhere from 0 to 24 hours after real VS is killed.

Once VSR is killed/triggered/use(whatever you want to call it), the next VSR will spawn 23 to 25 hours later.

At the 3 day time mark (give or take a few hours), real VS will spawn, which will cause VSR to despawn if it is up. (real VS takes priority over VSR and his triggered counterparts)

I have never heard of VSR despawning on his own after just a few hours, but it might have been changed.

VSR is non-agro...
give firefly globe to VSR, this will despawn VSR and Spirit of VS will spawn where you did the turn in.

Spirit of VS is non-agro...
give resurections spell scroll to Spirit of VS, this will despawn Spirit and VS-lite will spawn where you did the turn in.

VS-lite is agro and will probably agro on the person doing the turn in unless someone agroes him immediately.

After VSR is eliminated (i.e. despawned), the next VSR will spawn 22 - 25 hours later as long as the real VS has not respawned.
