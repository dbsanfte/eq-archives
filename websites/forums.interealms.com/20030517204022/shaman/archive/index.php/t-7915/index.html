Thanks to all my friends at the Crucible for your help and advice.

Fight went flawlessly. I actually brought 4 groups 50-60 including 3 chanters and 3 clerics...can you say overkill?

I had everyone stay inside the tunnel closest to the illusion wall out to the hallway. Lacking a 55+ puller with good iksar faction I pulled it myself (58 shaman). It went like this:

I pulled the room trash out of the room in a big train into the tunnel..groups mez'd/stunned and ate em up.

Did hand in to the iksar apparittion..who BTW hangs out RIGHT AT THE OTHER END OF THE TUNNEL. This surprised me as I thought he would be closer to the stage. He's VERY easy to aggro into the tunnel..so keep the softskins back.

Once I did the hand in..the iksar turns into rile and moseys over to the stage. The arisen pop in front of the stage and after a while VS shows up.

I hung back at the tunnel behind the broken bricks and watched the show. 12 minutes later VS was targetable. I pulled with slow..landed it and got 3 arisen as adds. One arisen (the priest?) never aggro'd me..I might have been lucky. Not that any of this mattered, I pulled the whole lot of em into the hall and groups made quick work of VS while keeping the other arisen mezzed.

Once VS was dead, I looted his skull, and made my way back to the stage (giving the still-unaggro'd arisen wide berth)..did my hand in, and the last arisen despawned along with Rile, who was kind enough to hand me his cudgel for my troubles.

Anyway..the hardest part was getting all those people into HS..:)

Please let me know if anyone needs tips on how to finish their cudgels..the effect is very nice, and it's still a pretty uber range item even in light of the new PoP items. (btw CARNAGE does currently stack with HoS..and bunches of other things. The cast time doesn't seem that long. It does NOT require you to have an emerald in your posession as someone said)

Thanks again Crucible!
