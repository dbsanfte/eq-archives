Looking to add some more to my toy collection, I keep at least 2 bags of clickables and proc weapons around, just for occasional use. Some are pretty handy, some I don't ever bring out. Droppables work best, but no drop stuff is fine to list, as long as it's not impossible to find (elemental planes, etc).
None of this is actually worn, just hanging around for swapping in as needed.

Shrunken Goblin Skull Earring - Insta click.
Earring of Purity - 5 sec counteract disease. Real nice for curing slows and dots.
Earring of Cleansing - 5 sec counteract poison. Mobs seem to use poison dots a lot less, but still useful when they do.

Tae Ew Ritualists Mask - Free invis. I haven't had to memorize invis since I got it (other than CR). So handy.
Sabertooth Amulet - See invis. A friend was letting them rot so I grabbed one, handy to pop it on sometimes instead of bothering to acumen myself. Although I finally got group acumen, so this is less useful.
Runebranded Girdle - Haste. Fun to have, doesn't provide much real use outside the farming of greens.
Boots of Shadow Walking - Shadowstep! Great fun for duels, causes problems trying to use it elsewhere (especially in dungeons).
Silvery Mask - Never know when you'll want to identify something.
10 Dose Potion of Unlife Awareness - Haven't found an item with ITU, so I keep a stack of potions.
Black Fur Boots - Who goes anywhere without these?

I would often carry the mage Torch of Alna on my Shaman. I had gotten into the habit of right clicking it from inventory to reclaim energy when ever i had to go invis, or zone, or do something that would kill my pet.

It does NOT work anymore after the patch :(

Was a fun quest to do a long time ago though. As far as i knew all 4 of those mage items: Broom of Trilon, Torch of Alna, Stein of Ulissa, and the Shovel of Ponz all used to work from inventory for a Shaman. I can vouch for the Torch and the Broom working anyways till the latest patch. Since there is no reclaim energy spell available for the Shaman, i decided to do the mage Torch quest back when i was a newb for the right click effect on it.

I guess someone decided that being able to summon a pet for no mana via the PoP armor as well as be able to reclaim energy for no mana via the old mage focus items was just too unbalancing...... as if any shaman would bother doing this instead of cannibalizing to regain mana anyways.

Now the mage items got nerfed so that they no longer work from inventory, and they changed the right click PoP shaman armor so that it no longer summons a pet.

I like to think about changes like this as "Things that you don't get to read about in the patch message"

It's not always fun discovering some of these yourself a few days later but at least this isnt a big kick in the nuts to the Shaman class, just a little thing that probably hasn't been working as intended for so long that we just kinda assumed that no one really cared about it.

Oh well one more clicky gone. Im sad to say that i finnaly broke my torch today, it had been with me a long time now. I also destroyed the old Broom of Trilon that i have kept in the bank for so long, it was just taking up space anyways, but i couldn't bring myself to get rid of untill this patch - more bank and inventory space for me i guess.
