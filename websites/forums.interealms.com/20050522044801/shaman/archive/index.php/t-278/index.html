Some thoughts about clicky items:

I still have yet to ding 60 (grinding through 59 faster than I thought I would though) and get my hands on a copy of Torpor, but I have hunted WW with groups quite a bit. 2 clicky items and 3 crap buffs should be fine for most of the weaker (soloable) dragons. Personally I use my black furries and a tinkered spyglass. Spyglass is easy to get, but the effect tends to be short-lived. I pretty much click it between every cast/JBB blast to keep it up. In my experience, it is fairly rare to get a buff further down the list dispelled. It is usually the first one(s) that get dispelled.

What do the rest of the WW hunters out there like for clicky-items?
