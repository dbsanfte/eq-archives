theknightmax
You can do the 4 guards and the jester and 1 other guy on the wall opposite of the kings. Dont try and pull any of the others or you will end up with both kings and their guards. Also the kings dont return to their spawn point so you can spread them out then zone. wait a bit and all the guards and other mobs are back in their normal spots but the kings are still where you left them. They drop some crappy rings but it is a fun fight.

It is a good camp especially if you can do the 4spawn underulks. The jester can be a bit trickey because sometimes he brings polly and that wizzy nukes hard.

The faction hit only effects the gnomes in echo.
Hemmar
The echo gnome guards can be single pulled at 49, including the jester and the herald up above, this just requires inching into spell range then running back to a slightly safe spot to fight. The Jester seems to be a bit more resistant than the others and unless you have already broken the area, he will probably be damage shielded by one of the mages by the Kings.

The Kings are not so hard to kill but are immune to snare. If you want to kill them, train them to the zone, the other mobs will path back while the kings will sit where they are when you zone.
