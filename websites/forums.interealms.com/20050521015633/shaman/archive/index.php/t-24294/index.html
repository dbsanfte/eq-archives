Khubla Khaan
This works to stop that annoying nag screen to buy their magazine when you log into EQ.

Open up the eqlsPlayerData.ini file with notepad and changing the two lines containing weborder to this:

WebOrder1FirstOffer=1100000000
WebOrder1LastOffer=1100000000
