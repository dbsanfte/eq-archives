Braxton
Virulent Paralysis rocks.

I have been using it in NToV and lands 95% of the time.

There are some situations VP is great for.

1. Puller brings mob into camp and the MT calls for everyone to PUSH THE MOB in a particular direction, I get on opposite side and VP, it knocks the mob back a good ways and saves a whole lot of pushing.

2. Mob is pulled that is a gater, when the mobs HP's are down to nothing and I see the mob start to cast gate, I use VP and it interrupts the cast 100% of the time, thereby preventing a mob from gating.

3. Mob starts thumping on me due to a resisted SLOW, I VP it and it knocks the mob back and roots it long enough to get it slowed.

VP rocks in a lot of situations.
