taraddar
Best place to ask this is on the magelo board but I'll answer anyway.

Charms have a "base" stats which is what shows up for the item. That base stat is either multiplied or divided depending on the charm. In the case of the flag charm the base stats are the max stats and then it's devided to what ever's appropriate for the level. Magelo captures the base stats at this point and that all it does. Jelan is working on putting a multiple in magelo profiles for charms so you can set it to the right level but it isn't there yet and he's on vaction right now.
