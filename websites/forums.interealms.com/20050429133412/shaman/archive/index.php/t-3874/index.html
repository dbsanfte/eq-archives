Tarnil MacDuff
Here is what I have scrounged together so far, I think I like it the best of what I have seen.
If you want to use it go ahead. I have yet to find a better spell gem arrangment so until I do I am keeping it as the default.
The pet window has been replaced with the stat window to make a smaller inventory.
Chat text goes into the window on the right, and everything else on the left.

One of the major things I did was flatten most of the buttons, I think it looks nicer this way.

http://members.shaw.ca/Silverhand/EQSkin.jpg


Download here (http://members.shaw.ca/Silverhand/EQSkin.zip)

Edited to a smaller file:

To install, just make a new directory under uifiles, copy all the files from the default directory, and overwrite with the files from the zip. Use /loadskin to load it in-game.

Hope you'll enjoy it.
