a note on dex and agi and raising melee skills:

My understanding is a higher dex increases the chance you have for an attempt at a skillup, and agi increases the chance of actually getting a skillup...so they are both important for melee skillups.

I haven't done parsing to substantiate this, but I've read numerous posts to this affect on the SteelWarrior site, and this seems to be the case for my 54 warrior. (ex: without dex/agi buffs I got about 3 skillups in 5 hours...with about 50 or 60 added to dex and agi I got about 15 skillups in 5 hours)
