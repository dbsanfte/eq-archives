Juventus
PoJ is pretty awesome at that level, go down to the second floor of prisoners and fight them inside their rooms. After level 57 the guards and rats will leave you alone and you can hop from room to room slaughtering prisoners in semi-peace. Whack the guards when you see them too, as they can drop decent cash loot. You can play with the two opposing faction, creatures and prisoners, to reduce the adds you might get from the room occupants.
