Name: Healing Adept
Level Requirement: 55
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 2 points
Level 2: 4 points
Level 3: 6 points
Description: This spell increases the amount of healing done by instant heal spells (not Celestial heals spells or regrowth etc).

The first point improves healing done by 2%, the second point by 5% and the third by 10%.

Advanced Healing Adept

This ability increases the maximum effectiveness of your heal spells by 3% per rank. Trainable once each on levels 62,63 and 64. (2 aaxp per pop)
