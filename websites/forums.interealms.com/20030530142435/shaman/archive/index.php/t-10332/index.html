eqphreak
Enchanters are alot of work .. much like the shaman. You gotta be on top of your game to control an enchanter AND a shaman at the same time. IMO its not worth it if all you're going to do is use the enchanter pet and nukes. The best way to play enchanter is to use charm, and good luck controlling two characters at once when your doing this. You don't want to be worrying about cannibalizing or casting buffs when your hasted pet is doubling for 400+.

This isnt to say that at the lower level game it isn't viable, but at the high end I would say get a melee partner for your shaman.
Humbert
I've got a 48 enchanter on another account. I had thought about pairing my shaman and enchanter before, but now I just don't have the time to play 2 chars.

From my experience it's a really fun combo. For kicks I took the enchanter to dreadlands and sat Humbert near him, buffed him up and let the enchanter charm a mob, then go out and pull mobs to the pet. It's a real blast. I don't know what changes are coming to charm, but for now it seems fun.

There were a few other fun things I liked: project illusion and dire charm, extra mana regen, mez, tash and rune.

The only problem with 2 boxing I didn't like was the enchanter doesn't bring alot of damage to the group unless you charm, and in that case you'll be controlling him most of the time.

Overall I like it, especially the thought taking risks like charming dragons or getting fast exp from charming. Just wish I had the time to play him :)
