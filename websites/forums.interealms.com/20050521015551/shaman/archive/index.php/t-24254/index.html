Ronax
Doesn't a rooted mob just look at the nearest pc?

No, this is only the case if both pc are within melee range. If a mob is rooted and all pc are out of melee range, the mob will "face" the one that has the highest aggro. Proximity and sitting will affect the level of aggro, but after only a few ticks poison aggro will outweigh proximity and sitting by far.
