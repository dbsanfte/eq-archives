Rolyen
Another nice little exp spot at lvl 50 - 53 is the bat3 camp in SolB. Invis up, head through SolA (look at a map from eqatlas or something to find the bat3 camp - its right next to the solA zone-line. When you zone in, systematically take out the bats using swarmcaller to slow, and root/heal yourself when necessary. This part is a little slow and dangerous, but fun. Once everything is cleared comes the easy exp part. You see, the bats are fear-kitable. Start with the bat nearrest to the solA zone-line. Snare him, fear, and whack away. He'll path all the way to the tunnel, which is where you turn back and wait for 'em at the zone line again. As soon as fear breaks, he'll come back for more. Rince and repeat, pulling each bat to the zone-line before fearing 'em. Our 18 second fears are perfect for this, as they drop early enough where the bat doesn't keep running away and get you into bad train-situations. Just remember to not follow it too far down, or you get into some nasty areas of the dungeon.

Also, this is particularly nice because you get the dungeon exp bonus (20% I believe). One can do this well into 53 IIRC. Anyways, good luck!
