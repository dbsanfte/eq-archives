eqphreak
There's an excellent ring that drops in SolC, the pocket zone off of naggy's lair. The ring has Extended Enhancement 4 and the spawn name is Spiritmaster Heli. I have duo'd him successfully with a cleric may times.

Basically, once you're in the zone from solb side (kobold side) he spawns at the end of the first hallway/tunnel to the left. You can scout to see if he's up with Vision, and also check for see invis mobs while you're scouting. Usually there is only one or two mobs that will see invis but I've been lucky and had none.

Heli spawns at the end of the tunnel with 2 adds right next to him, and 2 more shortly before his spawn point. You want your cleric to pacify all 4 of these mobs and fight him in the room just before you get to these. Just kill the two mobs in the 'camp area' and pull him to there, won't take you more than 5 minutes to kill him if you concentrate on dotting and the cleric concentrates on keeping you alive. I've found its best to just let the cleric keep SE on you and dont use Q, just dot the crap out of the guy and keep your mana for dots.
