Jinheim
Don't reroll. The only reason that ogres have more HP than vah shir is because they have a higher starting stamina. Your stamina is very easy to max out once you start getting decent gear.

If you would rather play an ogre than a vah shir, then by all means reroll, but I don't think its worth redoing 55 levels.

If you do make an ogre, you're going to want to put most of your points in to wis, since your stamina is already so high.

edit: there is a severe AC penalty for having under 75 agi, so put 5 there.
