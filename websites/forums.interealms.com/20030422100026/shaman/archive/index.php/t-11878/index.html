DON'T JOIN YOUR OLD GUILD. DON'T EVEN LET ALL YOUR IN-GAME FRIENDS KNOW IT'S YOU. Go back to the old world and play like you just learned about EQ. Remember how it feels to die fighting a bat, spiderling, or beetle. Look at level 20 content from the view of an untwinked level 15. It looks a LOT different than from a level 60+ perspective. Lose a full bulb of health when that mob whacks you for 45 damage. You say you've already been there and done that? I bet there's some combination of race and profession that will take you where you haven't been before.

Too crowded at the top? Most of the low level zones, old world or new, are scarcely populated these days. Getting a group is a challenge in itself. Do you know the "old" content so well that all of it represents nothing but boredom to you? Have you ever tried some out-of-the-way spots for leveling and avoided the well-known high exp per kill areas? Remember when guards were your best friends?

I know some of you have done just what I suggested. But I don't think enough of you have. Give it a try. And do it with a single PC. Multi-boxing is not allowed. Play the game, don't game the game.
