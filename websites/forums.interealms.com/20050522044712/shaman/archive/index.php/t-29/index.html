We wont get snare or ensnare as a spell as it makes soloing stuff alot easier. Luckily the VP ensnare spear I have procs very reliably and the effect lasts for 11 minutes.

One of the great uses I found for it was snare parking one of the two giant halfings in POM in the AiW area. Root one and run up the other end of the zone and snare the other, and camp/gate out. This would leave the snared one parked safely out of the way for me to nail his friend. Dragon Constructs are another good use for snare as it stops them running off and warping all over the place.

It has saved me many times because if things get hairy and you get interupted, its easy to run out of range between being summoned and cast a quick heal.

Snare gives you lots more control when soloing which is why I highly doubt we will ever see it in our spell book.
