In my experience without two of:

Malo
Tash
Oos

You are in a world of hurt trying to land slow.

If you had Oos and Tash you could likely jump to Malosini without Malo.

The named golems (at least post-sleeper waking) are not substantially more MR than the regular golems.

Even with Malo + Tash I'll frequently not stick Malosini (or Turgur) for 5 or 6 casts ... you'll want as much MR debuffing as you can get. Especially if you are there with a dozen (or fewer) people this resists will (literally) kill you.

One thing to remember: They first two named are within an easy jog of the zone. Keep SoW up. The golems are slow -- I don't think anything in sleeper summons.

I'm not suggesting you zone, but kiting until the tank regains aggro is definately an option.

$0.02.

Zorlac Sabboth
Current non Legend$ ST:
Regular golems need at least Tash and/or OOS plus Malo to be slowed.
Gargoyles can be slowed ok with Malo only.
Master of the Guard needs standard golem debuffs.
The Progenitor needs Tash, OOS and Malosini to be slowed. Very resistant.
Final Arbiter is a PITA to slow. Extremely resistant, even with all debuffs on it can take 4-5 tries to slow it.

Every named golem requires also Insidious Decay for SK taps to land.

EDIT: The small golems can be slowed without any debuffs, not worth it though, they die too fast.
