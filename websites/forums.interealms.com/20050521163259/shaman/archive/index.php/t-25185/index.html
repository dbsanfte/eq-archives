Zaviax McCleo
You know where the black market is in Qeynos Harbour?

From the dock area, as you leave it you'll see a building on your right you cannot get into. Behind that building you'll see a Erudite Broker. Jump into the water next to that guy and hug the right wall. After you pass under the bridge you'll see a hole in the wall underwater. Go in it and it will bring you into the building where the Black market broker is.

Black Market brokers sell stuff from Players in the opposing town. Seeing as the Freeport area has an influx of Orcs you should easily find a shattered bone there easily. (I got mine for around 4sp or so I think)

Harvyst
The necklaces get better with each tier.

In order they go

shattered (level 10)
cracked (level 20)
weathered (level 30)
unscathed (level 40)
enchanted (level 50)

Now unscathed cannot be completed. Unscathed orc and gnoll are not dropping for anyone on any server.

But weathered is very nice, I'm wearing it (click on my sig to see my profile). It's also a nice bit of xp. Also be aware that the level is the level it cons white at. So you can wear the weathered at level 20 - but it will be orange. At level 30 it's white, at level 35 it's green, etc. You can't wear it earlier, but you can bank it and get a nice xp boost when you're little. I have the glowing and shiny shards bracelet in my bank account waiting for me to ding 34 and 44 respectively.

I'm currently doing the enchanted ones slowly, as I pick them up easily in the enchanted lands. I've got unscathed done to the 2 that don't drop. That should get fixed in The Big Patch. In the meantime, weathered isn't that bad, and it will be awhile until I have Enchanted done.
