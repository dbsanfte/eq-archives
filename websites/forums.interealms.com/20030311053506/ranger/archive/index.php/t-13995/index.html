Blessing of Vah Kerrath is an innate effect, not a spell, so I really dont think it can be considered here if you're just talking about spells.

If you're taking about getting the most atk out of items, you just need to get as many Aura of Battle and Vengance Items as you can, up to 250 atk. (I believe that is the cap Sean helped discover).

As for spells...

Night's Dark Terror doesnt stack with Avatar, so remove that from the list.

For the best ATK spell layout, to my knowledge, it would go like this:

Ferine Avatar (lvl 65 shaman) 140 atk
Ferocity (lvl 65 beastlord) 150 atk
Protection of the Wild (lvl 65 ranger) 130 atk
Nature's Precision (lvl 39 ranger) 15 atk
Guardian of the Forest 3 (lvl 65 ranger) 140 atk
Frostreaver's Blessing (10th coldain ring) 10 atk
Warsong of Zek 18 atk (62 bard) (this varies according to bards instruments)
Dance of the Blade (bard epic proc) 30 atk
Speed of Vallon (62 enchanter) 41 atk

+ whatever strength spells you need to max it.

From my calculations, thats 674 raw atk added with spells.

674 * 1.33 = about 896 atk added to your atk rating in game. So breaking 2000 atk shouldnt be all that hard in theory.
