Okay, I think I figured it out. Here's what I did...

Fully buffed with everything (I still had buffs running from Sunday night), I zoned in to Echo from Shadowhaven and called for DogDog. Medded up and headed for the plateu near The Deep zone line. 6 mobs were at the top of the plateu near the zone, two in front and 4 in back. In front there was an underbulk (dk blue) on the left and a stone grabber (lt blue) on the right. In back were a grouping of 4 underbulks in a rough diamond shape.

I jumped into the entry to the Deep at the top of the plateau and pulled the stonegrabber with a slow. Nothing agro'd. DogDog jumped in and I stepped back, landed Curse, and jumped back in to melee. Stonegrabber died quickly.

I moved a little further in to the room and pulled the lone unerbulk with root, slow, then curse. Then I canni'd. I sent DogDog in as an extra dot. When root broke, I rooted again, as it started running to the fiends parked over into the plateau edge. Underbulk died within a hit or two.

I canni'd some more, then set DogDog to guard at the top of the plateau again. This time I headed for the 4 underbulks in back. I hugged the cave wall all the way in back in an attempt to get *proximity* agro, rather than pull with a spell. And lucky me I got the underbulk on the far right of the diamond shape to follow me all by himself.

DogDog jumped in a little earlier than I wanted because the underbulk got in a shot on me as I was running back to the puppy for battle. I rooted him, backed the dog off, slowed, Cursed, and tried an Incapacitate cast this time. Worked very nicely. Root broke, so we meleed again, after which I canni'd.

One of the two front mobs popped again while I was dancin' so I took that out again using the same technique. It made me realize that I was probably being conservative with time and a bit wasteful with mana. Rebuffed, took out the pop, canni'd again to full and then headed back for the rear underbulks.

Parked the puppy directly in front of the rear underbulks in a straight line, but against the far wall. Then I inched forward to see if I could proximity pull the first underbulk and it worked again. Yay me! Ouch, root, back off puppy, slow, curse. Dance dance dance, dead underbulk.

With two left and a pop on the way, I thought I'd try for two to see how I handled it. I rooted the rear underbulk, then slowed it, and the left underbulk didn't seem to react at all. So I rooted that one to get its attention. Cursed the first one, danced some more, and then was lucky enough to have root break.

I had moved up to the entrance to the Deep zone tunnel in case the front mob spawn popped in the middle of the fight. The slowed bulk followed me there where we duked it out. Root broke on the second one as the first one hit about 25 percent health. I took a few hits as I rooted the second one again and dotted it. As the first of these last two were ready to keel over, a well-meaning, but otherwise kill-stealing, camp stealing boob of a shaman sent in his dog to dispatch the last of the bulks I was trying to kill.

So I didn't quite do it myself, but at 85% health on me, a dead underbulk, and a cursed and rooted final underbulk, I would have had it if he hadn't screwed up my kills.

Edit: It occurs to me that I should mention I dinged 47 over the weekend. Killing all 6 of these mobs netted me a bit under 10% xp (maybe a litttle less as I wasn't strictly watching).
