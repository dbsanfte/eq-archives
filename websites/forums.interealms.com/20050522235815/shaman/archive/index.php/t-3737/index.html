Baz3
not sure about there levels - were all green to me by the time I passed by there.

The mines have plenty of Grimlings to kill, and they are far enough apart that you can get singles easily, once you are past the two outside guards.

The foreman is quite a bit higher level than the miners, etc. and may be a very tough fight at level 41. There are a lot of casters that spawn there, so I would recommend getting a melee partner - more interrupts would be very helpful.

I suspect that a pet would draw aggro, and pathing is awful, so I would not summon one until you are familiar with the place.
