Go to the middle of the beach next to the water, park your pet, and pull mobs from all sides. With a little trial and error, you will find that you can pull mobs from a good distance without aggroing others. Mobs start conning light blue at 42, but even at that level there are more than enough to pull. I expect to solo here until 44. Right next to you on the beach is where Mokon Brokenskull spawns, and if you are lucky enough for him to spawn AND you can take him, he drops some good loot. (a decent charm and a bow worth 2k)

Every once in a while they resist the DOTs or Slow, but not enough to warrant debuffing them before casting other spells.

Overall this is an easy place to solo if you are tired of soloing in Dawnshroud Peaks...
