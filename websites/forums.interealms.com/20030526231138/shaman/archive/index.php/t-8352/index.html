The spells are rare, but attainable. In fact, I would say NOW is the time to work on it. With so many people focused on PoP, there are targets like Gorenaire, Talendor, Venril Sathir, and others available for you to fight. Also, the SkyFire Mountains zone is empty 99% of the time (literally empty of players) so you can easily hunt Guardian Wurms. They're very likely just standing there waiting for you to kill them.

I spent some time hunting Guardian Wurms (with guildees) and managed to get Torpor. My guild actually gave me Malo, then we saw it drop off Gorenaire a few days later (which is great, because there is another guild shaman waiting for it). My guild has been tremendously helpful to me at every level of my character -- we certainly aren't the biggest, but we stick together and we look out for eachother (which seems like the way to be to me).

Regarding the spells themselves... Torpor makes you into an incredible mana machine. Malo turns you into a debuffing powerhouse. I went a very long time without Malo thinking that Malosini would get me by. Malosini will not get you by. Not even close. Don't let anyone tell you different.
