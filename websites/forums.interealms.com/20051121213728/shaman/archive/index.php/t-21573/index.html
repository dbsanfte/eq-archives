Bufr
Leadership - Maneuvers.

This gives a 5% bump to defense to all team mates in a 60 foot radius. It stacks with other maneuvers. So a 8 man force with this skill gets a +40% defense bonus. I and 2 other friends have it. As the Tank I can pop a Luck inspiration + 3 maneuvers and tank 3 or 4 orange bosses without putting much pressure on the healer. Minions are meaningless in thhe same situation.

Fitness would be a good pick too. Stamina is a wonderful addition to any template.

Luclin's Arriana
Bear in mind that you get 20 powers (not including the base 3). Your primary and secondary sets have 9 powers each, for 18 total. That works out to all of your prim/sec powers, a pre-req for a travel power, and a travel power.

If you want more, then you need to make sacrifices in your primary or secondary sets. Sometimes it's easy (like Blasters ignoring most of the melee attacks in their secondary set), and sometimes it ain't.
