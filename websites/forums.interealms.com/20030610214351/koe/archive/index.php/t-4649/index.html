Jice Virago
For assisting in killing Nag last night. After getting screwed by Fartstorm at Sev I felt compelled to kill something big and challenging. Nag was up. We had some interim difficulties and two failed attempts (squarely my fault due to not counting Giants and not having groups set up correctly), but everyone stuck it out because the goal was in sight.

In the end, Nag died to 13 people, the bulk of whom were not decked out in Velious gear and very few were even level 50. We also had one cleric and two shamans for healing (49, 52, 44 respectively) so this was not a well oiled killing machine of a force. People who don't know me don't understand why I do things like this, but killing a tough target with minimal forces is one of the most enjoyable experiences for me in EQ. It really brings out the best in people and instills a feeling of accomplishment that is unmatched. For those who fought, I promise you the typical Zerg Nag with 30-50 will never be entertaining again and I thank you for comming.

Next time we do it with 12 =p
