Each step of the shaman epic requires a faction check. By the point you get to black dire, you've had several +True Spirit faction hits. What type of a group would be able to take him out? My guild is raiding mistmore in a week, and I would love to be able to kill this guy. What would be a minimum group of people, or even more than 1 group, to take this guy out.
Chutoi
Frankly, you could do it with 3 people. 60 tank, cleric, and yourself. I have a friend that did it solo (60 ogre shaman with JBB and good equipment).

For lower level groups, I'd recommend 2 groups of low 50's or probably 3 or 4 of 40's. If you don't have a 60 puller, you get all four of his friends, too. Before BD spawns, his friends were green to me. After he spawns, they also respawn a lot stronger (you can't kill them first--they will respawn with BD).

I'm sure you already know that if he's not up, you have to kill the Advisor to spawn BD.
