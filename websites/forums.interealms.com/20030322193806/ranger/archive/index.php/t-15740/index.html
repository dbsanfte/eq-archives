At 43 DSP Zels should still be excellent exp. The thing about this zone is only the Lightcrawlers can be a pain, but still go down quick and isn't that hard to deal with...

You can also try Bloodgills. I didn't have exceptional gear when i started these at 45, I only suggest you do it with a KEI if u don't have nice regen. They have little hp, and don't hit too hard, plus you will get swimming up as well.

Another spot I came across recently, was in Twilight Sea. Get a port or if you feel your invis will hold, run through Fungus Grove. Once u take Portal into TS, hit track and try a sea turtle.

There is a island with elementals, pull them to there if u don't like kiting in water.... this works pretty good, cuz you don't get agro from the elementals. These also have little hp and don't hit too hard. =)
