Current Organization:

Page 1 - 4 most common spells. page 2 is all my buffs.

Pages 5 - 8 are less common spells. All my resist spells are on one page. Bear Form, Pet, Summon Pet all on one, then things like Cha Buff, Summon food/water/bags , levitate, etc.

All my old spells start on page 25. I only have the highest lvl buffs, Dots, etc in the first part of the book, everything else that is 'outdated' goes back here in some sort of pseudo order...I try to keep all the DDs on a page or 2, all my heals together, etc.



Two things I do to help speed things up:

1) my Meditation Hotkey is \book 4. This puts me in the 'middle' of my most common spells. One page left or right and I have most of my spells that I would ever swap out during combat.

2) a hotkey for \book 25 takes me to the spot where my old spells are. If I ever need to whip out burst of fire or some other spell I don't have to page, page, page, page all the way to the back...I know they are all past 25.
