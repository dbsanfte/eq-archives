For Gloriana and others interested, the shield and the other weapon things able to be made is what the Troll Cultural Smithing is. The good thing is that they are not limited to trolls or dieties for the most part for who can use them. That is unless a troll were to imbue them for you. The smithing for the trolls is really rough right now since it is the very first time trolls get any cultural smithing (May 8 patch) and all the trivials are the same as the new high end smithed armor is so far. Example, the smith upgraded Ykesha changes a yak from a 8/24 weapon with effect Ykesha to a 12/23 weapon with effect: ykesha. This is pretty pathetic since the delay is too high and there are no stats of any sort on it. The cost of it makes it unsellable since 12/23 weapons drop and rot all the time for free in places like ME. Anyhow, off topic hehe. See your local troll master smith. He should be able to help you with the items that have been successful so far. Or, if you want to read about them for yourself, check out

EQ Tradeskill Website (http://www.eqtraders.com)

and read the smithing message boards about troll stuff. Good luck.
