AtommWarbird
I think that the plight of warriors is a somewhat different one than ours. Warriors can't really solo in any real sense (well for VERY short stints using all their disc's) while we are all aware that except for the hightest content we can solo quite effectively. I am not an elemental equipped Shaman and I can solo quite effectively at "the tables" in the Plane of Fire for instance.

Our normal guild and RAID functions are to slow, buff and heal (let's not argue about whether or not it's patch healing or primary healing for this discussion). The warrior's primary function is to keep aggro on the group/raid's target so the rest of the crew can concetrate on killing the monster and keep only the warrior's healed. If they can't simply keep aggro they cannot perform this function and on many kinds of encounters his group/raid will simply fail.

We argue that we're marginalized in high end content by other classes abilities and the design (high dps/low hps) of the encounters at that level. That still leaves us with most of the rest of the game (including LDoN's, groups, Tier1-3 planes, etc..) where we are still a valuable addition to groups and RAID's. If Warriors couldn't taunt effectively and survive under difficult circumstances than they were simply USELESS. They can't solo so if they weren't group/raid worthy they were simply relegagted to tradeskilling ;)

I'm not saying that as healers we are not able to stand alone in the highest end content. I personally believe that heal + slow for us should about equal DS/DoT/Heal on Druids and Heal/Buff on Clerics. I disagree with SoE's insistance that "We can Slow" is somehow a vaild argument not to suppliment our healing when they make all the mobs mitigate it.

Perhaps a simple solution to ask for is to for one of our slows to simply "break through mitigation" so that we can heal more effectively. I might see an argument that if we were given this ability AND we used it when there are Druids/Healers were around it would make the encounters trivial. I haven't thought of a good counter to that but I'm sure this good community will.
