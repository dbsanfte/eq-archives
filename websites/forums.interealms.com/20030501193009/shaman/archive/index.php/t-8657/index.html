Here's a trick for all those doing Shar Vahl alchemy.

There are two vendors selling herbs in the building near the bank. The one that stands at the counter is indifferent and prices are expensive. The other vendor is usually on the left of the counter and will not sell in this position. When she moves to the back of the room, you can buy her stuff and the prices are much better. I believe the feverfew from her is in the 20-30pp range.

Griffon feathers are pretty easy to get either farming or paying 8pp to vendors in North Karana or Jagged Pine.
