Hope you got an extended warranty, as power surges are usually not covered by manufacturer's warranty (though homeowner's insurance might take care of it)
Run chkdsk (scandisk on 98/ME) and make sure there's no filesystem errors. If that comes up clean, you've probably got a hardware failure of some sort. vid card, motherboard, ram, and cpu, or any combination of the 4, could be causing the problem. Most likely, it's the motherboard. :(
