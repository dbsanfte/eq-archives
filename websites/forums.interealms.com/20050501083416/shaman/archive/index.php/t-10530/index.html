Goldark
I've only played a barbarian shaman, but I think the XP difference is around 10%. If you are soloing untwinked though, you may find the racial benefits offset that xp penalty anyway.

Ogres are still unstunnable from the front.

You may also want to consider a troll, for
1) Innate regen
2) Snare necklace

If you don't care about having a JBB (you should) Iksar are another consideration with their regen and innate AC. But the JBB is a great crutch from level 46+, not something I'd like to rule out.

Plenty of other threads about the races if you do a quick search I'm sure you'll find some info.
