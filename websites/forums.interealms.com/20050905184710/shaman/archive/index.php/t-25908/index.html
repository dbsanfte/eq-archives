I loved the rep system. I never looked at the dots under people's names unless bored, and they didn't affect how I read people's posts. I took everything as it was stated and evaluated it from that.

If you posted something you were unsure of, or worked hard on, you could go back and check the rep bumps for it to see how much the post was approved of.
