Steelbear
Well if all you are looking for is variety try some of these places:

Crypt of Nadox - all the undead trolls and luggalds are dark blue at 65, and you'll find a lot of gems and some pretty cool items on the named mobs when they spawn.

Hate's Fury - whole zone is dark blue at 65, still good gem drops but itemization wasnt as impressive to me.

Veksar - havent hunted there a great deal, but most of it was dark blue once you got into the golems and beyond.

Pocket zones - Halls of Betrayal and Mines of Solusek (or whatever it's called) are both good exp and I know HoB has good loot and spells. SolC probably pretty much the same way.

Droga/Nurga - more good exp and mobs here probably have less hps than Nadox or Hate's Fury, and there is a cool sword there that I plan to get for my warrior (sword of the bloodsworn).
