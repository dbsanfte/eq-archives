Amarin Crystalthorn
As many of you know that were on last night, KoE did a General V'Ghera raid in Kithicor Woods for Blaydde, Harryfooty and Grizz. This was the final epic battle for the Rogue epic weapon Ragebringer. Failure to win this battle means that the quest must be restarted.

By about 6:30 pm EST the necessary forces were assembled at the crossroads just west of the dark elf cabin in which the trigger mobs reside. The pull group began to split the cabin so that there were only a couple of dark elves left. The rogue then turns in a trigger item to one of the DEs while in hide/sneak mode, thereby spawning the General. The pull group then pulls the General (again splitting him from the cabin) to the assembled forces. The General is a level 63 SK and can be very tough to bring down.

Once the cabin was split, Grizz called for Blaydde to turn in his trigger item. I sneaked to the cabin and clicked on one of the DEs. Sadly, Blaydde was immediately aggroed (this occasionally happens), and in his haste to escape, he traded the item to the DE rather than cancelling the trade. Blaydde died fast. But more significantly, the General did not spawn. This is because if the turn-in happens while the rogue is aggroed, the trigger item is lost and the General does not spawn. Blaydde knew this but, in his excitement, he hit the Trade button instead of the Cancel button. This also happens a fair amount, as visitors to the Safehouse Boards can attest. People make this mistake ALL the time. The result is that the quest must be restarted.

As you all might expect, Blaydde was crushed. The guild was incredibly supportive, making clear that they would help re-do the quest, but, although I tried not to show it, my disappointment was extreme. But we proceeded to trigger the next General, this time for Grizz. The turn-in went well, the General was spawned and soon he died at the hands of the guild. We rezzed Grizz so that he could loot the General's pouch, but soon there appeared in guildsay instructions from Grizz for me to loot the pouch. I could not believe my eyes. I protested in say, in tells to Grizz and in guildsay that I could not, WOULD not, loot the pouch - that it belonged to Grizz, who would only have to re-do the quest himself. Grizz would not budge, however, and Waran and Zelfani assured me that if I did not loot it Grizz would let it rot. After arguing some more, I eventually looted the pouch. Blaydde is now one Kedge Robe away from completing Ragebringer.

Grizz's actions last night were, of course, extraordinary. I am relatively new to KoE (though I have known many of you since release), but I have always known of Rizz's reputation. (Eariana spoke glowingly of him when she originally joined LoA in '99) Last night was the first time I benefitted directly from his renowned generosity, and it occurred to me that his generosity is not only extraordinary, it is uncommon. I have many good friends in EQ, but I can say with confidence that few if any of them would have done what Grizz did last night, and this for a relatively new member of the guild that he barely knows. I am humbled.

There is one final piece to this story, however. What no one in the guild was aware of last night (except Drolgin), was that Adam did the trade to the DE, not me (though I was with him). Adam is my 14 year old son who plays Blaydde with me, and he so much wanted to handle Blaydde at the final moment. The resulting failure to spawn the General terribly upset him (though, as I said, this is a common problem with spawning the General). Grizz's act of generosity made Adam's day. As the riad continued for Harry's General, Adam turned to me and said, "People always say they will follow Rizz anywhere. Now I understand why."

Add this to the legend that is Rizz
