Feradach
The BD wolf spawn is not based on your gem turnin to the spirit. The two are completely independent. Go turn in the gem, kill the wolf whenever you can, then come back and give the BD pelt to the spirit.

If the wolf is not up when you get to Mistmoore, go kill the Advisor. This will cause him (and his buddies) to spawn.
Urdegish
1) No. The gem does not spawn the wolf, the wolf is spawned via killing the Advisor inside the castle Mistmoore, or otherwise on a timed spawn.

2) Group of 50's basically. Level 60 wizzy and cleric just nuked him to death for me :)

3) No. If you fail, you just try him again, and again and again if need be until you kill him. You then hand in his fur for the boots
