Raytheon
Factions are:

Prisoners of Justice (generally humanoid prisoners)
Creatures of Justice (generalyy non-humanoid prisoners)

If I recall correctly, you start KoS with Creatures and non-KoS to Prisoners.

Guards have no faction. Rats have no faction.

Not sure about the denizens of the Grallok caves.
Eynel Stormsifter
High Guardians near the portal to the Tribunal have the following negative Faction hits:

Guardians of Justice (All the black caped guys)
also a minus to a factioned named after the efreeti there, don't recall name.

Other High Guardians don't have the faction hit
