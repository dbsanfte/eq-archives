Chazic
With Full Health and Virtue you should be able to back off of one fight ever 3 mins and either Torpor Self (I tend to cast before Canni 5) or cast Tnag'S Healing. I realize Tnags is not near a mana effencent. But hey if your OOM any mana is better then no mana. And heck Canni 5 will be back up online in only 3 mins which in LDoN enviroments goes buy fast as all get out. I just tap "V" (After I select Canni 5 from the Alt Advance menu) and am able to get an exact time when it should be refreshed. You can use the "V" key at any time even while casting. A second tap will cause it to dissappear once you get your refresh time. Oh and for those not awear of this timer being there its in the top right corner of the info portion of the Canniblization ablitity.
