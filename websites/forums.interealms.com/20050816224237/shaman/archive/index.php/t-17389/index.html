I liked how they did pretitles in the game I played before EQ. At specific levels, each class had 1 or more unique titles. At lower levels, it'd be just one title, as they got higher in level there would be 2 or 3 titles at a given level. You picked which title you wanted to display, or you could chose not to display a title at all. You just toggled them on or off with a simple text command.

For example; I played a ranger in that game and they got (to my best recollection) these titles:

Level 10: Scout
Level 20: Tracker
Level 30: Ranger, Trailmaster, Pathfinder
Level 50: Hunter/Huntress
Level 70: Warden

There was another title at 20th and 50th, but I just cant recall what they were right now. But you get the idea. Each class had it's own set of unique titles like this, and every class at level 30 had their class name as one of the titles (i.e. Ranger).
