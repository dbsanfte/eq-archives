More Skill-Activated spells?
PlanetEverQuest | Infernal | 9:53

In case you missed my previous conjecture, I believe Verant is working on an entirely new concept for Caster-classes in Velious. Specifically, I think they are implemting SKILL-Based Spells, much the same as Harm Touch or Lay on Hands currently works. They are technically Skills, but they actually activate a SPELL. They are all new to the end of the SPDAT file and in sequence to each other, also they are level 1 spells and often 0-mana cost.
New (Possibly) Skill-Activated Spells
These spells are listed in sequence with the spells I've theorized are skill-activated, but they are not attributed to a class for level 1, but I don't believe that would mean they *cannot* be skill-activated. You'll have to guess whether they are going to be Skill-activated or Item-based.

Manaburn (Wiz 1) No parameters are defined, but I'd guess this is an unresistable uber-nuke, burning ALL mana at a fixed ratio for a do-or-die nuke.
Restore Companion (Nec 1, Mag 1, BL 1) 1981 HP Pet Heal spell
Gather Mana (Enc 1) +5000 Mana, Self Only
Frenzied Burnout (Mag 1) Pet Buff, +100 ATK, +17 AC, +100 STR, 195% Haste. Only lasts 1.5 mins though :(
Lifeburn (Nec 1) Probably the same as Manaburn, but it's Vampiric. I would guess the Mana:Damage ratio is not as good as the Wiz one, due to it being a Lifetap. This is also unresistable.
