Juujuu
The super big giant lady isn't hard. You kill her. You get loot. No biggie.

(One of) the end boss of Uldaman is pretty damn cool. You go into this room and everyone clicks on this idol or something in the center. Then, one at a time, stone statues animate and attack you. When one dies, the next starts attacking. They seem to have immense AC.

After they are all dead, you go into the next room, and have to activate the center stone again. When you do so, the giant statue in the room activates and attacks you. It's something like a level 50++ elite. You have to tank that for a LONG time. Reason for this is, as the health level goes down of the statue (or maybe it's time based) you get small dwarven statues coming alive as adds and start attacking you. I think each of these adds is an elite as well. So you have a healer keeping MT up, and 3 people jumping on the adds each time one comes alive, then going back to dps on the boss until next spawn. If you have a full group of level 45s here with like, Warrior, Priest, Shaman, DPS, DPS, you'll probably have the fight go for about 5-10 minutes. Very fun though. We wiped at about 33%, but we didn't know what to expect either as the fight went on.
