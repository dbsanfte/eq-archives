I did my adventuring in Paludal as a bard...my Shaman was already too high level when I discovered this place. My bard was untwinked, so no special equipment needed.

I don't have any tactics, but I know that from lvl 9 a single blue mob was giving 2 blues of xp...by lvl 22 the blue mobs were still giving more than 1 blue each.

The fungal fiends have a really nasty disease proc...lasts for hours and is 2 dmg per tick...so basically if you get hit with disease you have to stop soloing until it's healed. Because of this a lot of soloing classes have a hard time.

You should be able to find a hunting partner no problems...just /ooc that you can cure disease and heal and they should love you.
