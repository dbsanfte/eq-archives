Sudedor
Currently:

App I - Automatically receive this at the appropriate time with a very small number of largely irrelevant exceptions. (Udor's Spirit of the Hawk for instance)

App II - Can typically be purchased from a scribe in a city.

App III - Made by tradesman.

Adept I - Relatively common drop from mobs. Note that certain spells drop more often, some rarely. Adept I books are by and large fairly common however.

Adept II - Not currently implemented. Originally they were to be dropped off of very tough (perhaps raid level) mobs.

Adept III - Made by tradesmen using very rare harvested components.


Now, all that goes out the window when the upcoming tradeskill patch goes live.

Basically, the "quality" of the scroll determines its level. So if I, as a tradesman start to scribe "Weakness" if I get a "Crude" scroll, it's App I, if I get "shaped" scrolls it's App II, if I get a normal scroll it's App III, and if I get a "Pristine" scroll it will now be App IV. Same will apply to Adepts.
