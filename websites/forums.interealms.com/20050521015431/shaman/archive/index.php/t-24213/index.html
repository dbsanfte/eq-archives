Tohlagos
This message is for all those shaman, and for that matter any class/player that is struggling with a quest...

After two years and ten months since I started, this last Saturday I completed my 1.0 shaman epic.

Everything up to Mistmore went smoothly. (As it should).

I can remember my second fear raid (remember our first fear raid Poguk? hehe), and how I had died just as Terror went down (I believe it was Terror).

I was looking at my inventory when my rez box popped up. I didn't think about about it and out of habit closed my inventory. At the time there was a bug and my rez box poofed. After repeated attempts of other rezzers, another rez box would not pop up.

So I camped and came back. However, by the time I did the Iksar child corpse has rotted with the tear. (The child had been killed by some tank who was not not familiar with the shaman epic -thats what I was told anyway.)

I didn't think much about it and though it would be easy to get another.

Over the course of the next 18 months I did another 8 fear raids for the tear. None of the those raids produced a tear or even the mobs to spawn the child.

...I remember one of those raids where I was the only shaman. I was buffing and slowing six groups by myself. We had wiped trying to break into Fear. Trying to slow mobs, while Scarlings were teleporing me around and I was completely naked because I didn't have time to loot my corpse was "interesting.". That raid took over seven hours and after many deaths we started loosing people. That was a long day and one I hope never to repeat. :)

Then PoP came out and Fear started to loose the interest of the higher level guilds. The raids into fear by some of the higher guilds to take out the epic mobs and just let them rot to keep the lower guilds from gaining items stopped.

Months passed and one day a friend asked me if I needed my Tear. I explained how I felt "jinxed," but he persuaded me to go. The raid went flawless and an Iksar Child spawned. I was told it was all mine. So I ran up to it and slayed it with my REIF. I stood there until I could loot it.... no one played any games with me that time... no tells from people saying that they wanted it for their alts...no tells that time about how I was not high enough at 58 to "deserve" the tear and how it should go to a friend... no rubish transpired.

The entire raid waited for me to loot it and I got no garbage from anyone. It was a very cool raid.

More time passes.... all I needed was Neh and Rak. (Kurn and the Hole had gone flawless). But, I could not get people to commit or interested.

Then a few days ago I had my computer over a friends house doing a LAN party. He looks at me and says, "Lets finish your epic."

Neh was over in a minute. Smooth as silk.

Rak took a little bit longer, but no hitches. I looted my icon.

I ran alone into the lake where the spirt was. I was the only one in the zone. I blink and breathed in deeply for a few minutes thinking to myself about how long this had gone on and that it was finaly ended. I thought a lot of things... then I did my turn in.

I am happy to be an owner of a Spear of Fate, thank you.

So, to all those out there that are struggling with a quest, please keep going. I am glad I did not give up. Even at 65, it was worth completing.

Cheers,

Toh
