bigbadbowlindude
Yea these guys suck.. They litter SF everyone avoids them like a plague.I found this out while in desperation to kill something while there was a shortage of other mobs in my area. They do however drop essence of sunlight quite frequently, which I just found the other day can sell from 100-400pp each(I never knew this I've vendored them /bonk!).
Tools I think you need to kills theses efficiently. Your pet, black fur boots, stoicism, and spirit quickening is nice if you have it but not necessary, and a muzzle or any other summoned goodie.
Ok first finding these bad boys will never be a problem. Summon pet, really don't bother with buffs.. they will dispell(yea I know duh). Maybe give yourself a hp buff if not kragg's then tuna.. less mana. If you can get one to come near your pull spot that is the best then sick pet and back off to your spot, wait till your pet bites into him for bout 10 percent then slow, if not pull with slow run and sick pet on asap so your pet takes agro.I like to keep pet up with spirit quickening 20 haste and few other stats for 50 mana is pretty good. Maybe toss in a dot or so after your pet has good agro. When pet gets low on hp I just toss a stoicism on him, I know it slows him down.. but I got patience. :)
By doing this I can normally kill them as much as I want without being dispelled. Although I wouldn't go out and get kei. Or you can always team up with a Necro/SK and fear kite. Or you can just not kill them lol. Good luck. :)
