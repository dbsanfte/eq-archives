Some suggestions although keep in mind I'm a primary cleric not a shaman so not all of these may be good ideas. I'm sure someone will tell me I'm wrong if I am and since I'm the first to admit I'm not an expert I won't take offense. :)

For short term, you might want to bank one of your rings for later and switch to Fine Antique Ring. Until you get your level 49 spells it will save you mana on all your spells and you are only losing a little AC and HPs. In a few levels you could switch back and resell the Fine Antique - on FV it is droppable and sells for 250-300p.

Fine Antique Ring
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM NO DROP
Slot: FINGERS
AC: 3
HP: +30 MANA: +20
Focus Effect: Mana Preservation II
WT: 0.1 Size: SMALL
Class: ALL
Race: ALL
