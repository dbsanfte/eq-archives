Guild Emeritus is currently recruiting on Lanys T'vyl. We are a guild that is playing using an era-progression methodology. For instance we are starting out only utilizing only old-world and Kunark zones, and limiting equipment to old-world only as well as capping lvls at 50.

Our goal is to revitalize the older content as well as have fun experiencing the progression all over again. We will be progressing through the end-game for each era in order starting with "vanilla" or Classic Everquest and finally ending up with the Velious expansion. As we progress new items, zones, and levels will be unlocked.

We are looking for dedicated and talented players who are tired of playing in the never-ending ratrace that plagues the current game.

If you have any questions or if you think you would like the chance to go back and have a lot fun doing it all over again, come to the board and submit an application.
