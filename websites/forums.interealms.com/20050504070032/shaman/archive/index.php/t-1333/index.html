Azhdarkhos
I'm currently up to the last fight with Rak to finish epic. Once upon a time I had looked into what it would entail to faction-short-cut the quest, since at the time we had another shaman with the Tear for multi-quest. After reviewing various reports of how many turn-ins of various earlier parts of the quest are required to max Ally, and after completing each of the steps of the quest in the intended order, by comparison it seems that it would take more time to try to "short-cut" it.

Basically, the only steps you might need to skip are Kirn and Neh'Ashir since these take a sizable force to take on. The Ghiosk books can be had simply by hanging out in the zone when you know another guild is farming Jade Reavers.

Assuming you have the guild power to do the last few steps, it is simpler and faster just to do them. Since there are several epic quest npc's in the same general vicinity as Kirn, guilds will organize raids to take them on all in one go. Similar with Neh'Ashir since the Black Reavers rarely drop the Jade Reaver for the druid/ranger epic, requiring several clearings sometimes to get one, and fighting your way up to Neh's reaver is a productive way to kill time between reaver spawns. When I did Kirn and Neh with my guild, the raids took about 6 hours total for both, during which we also got several epic quest items for other people in the guild.

By comparison, the paladin and shadowknight spawn once every two hours, and from reports it takes about a dozen repetitions of this step to get to Ally. Along with the long respawn time, you have to deal with other shamans (and other people farming the items) hunting them at the same time you do. Add in the time spent tracking down the Wandering Spirit to turn in the items for the faction hit.

Bottom line: whether or not this method is worth doing depends on what resources you have guild-wise. Also, keep in mind that if there are any glitches in the last step that cause you to lose the Tear or Scale, you probably won't have much luck getting help from a GM if you've short cut the quest.
