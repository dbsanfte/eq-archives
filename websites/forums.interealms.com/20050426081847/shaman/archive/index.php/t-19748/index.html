Jinheim
There are four problems:

1) Opening with spirit call would be good, it would give you time to get malos in without worrying about interrupts. The problem is that one bounced slow outaggros spirit call, so then you've got zlandicar in your face and you have to try to channel slow and heals at the same time.

2) He's a resistant bastard. I have always had trouble slowing him with tash, malos, and hos all in. You would have to have extreme luck to land turgur's. Odds you'd have an unlucky string of tnarg interrupts and die before you succeeded in slowing him.

3) Bane is going to land just as often as turgur's, severely crippling your ability to do damage to him. I am also unsure how well poison lands on him, but I don't think it is good. This means the fight is going to take a very long time. I remember it taking a whole raid of sub-ST geared people a few minutes to kill him, so I shudder to think how long it is going to take a dogdog to kill him. This brings me to my fourth point.

4) Your pet isn't going to resist the AE. I don't remember exactly how bad the dot is, but if you have to be constantly healing yourself and your dogdog, you are most likely going to be going out of mana before zlandi goes out of health.

Basically, though, I think that if you can land slow you might be able to do it. It will be a hard fight, but I think you would come out on top. However, your odds of landing slow are pretty bleak. My advice? Get one of your guild paladin's log in info, duo zland, and don't share the loot =P
