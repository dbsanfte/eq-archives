The primary role of a ranger is to kill stuff if that's what you are asking. Your damage potential should be at least as good or better than a comparably equipped warrior. With the addition of Trueshot, a good bow, decent arrows, and some AA skills, archery allows you to out-damage rogues. This is what you should be doing on raids.

Depending on your situation, you may be called upon to act as a backup tank. With good equipment, you can go toe-to-toe with some nasty stuff if you really have to. You are no warrior, so don't think you are a tank, but in a pinch you'll do. In some situations, you'll be called upon to Weapon Shield, buying the group 20 seconds of breathing room.

Finally, you provide some buffing and utility spells, and I find that downtime healing helps the real healers med up faster.
