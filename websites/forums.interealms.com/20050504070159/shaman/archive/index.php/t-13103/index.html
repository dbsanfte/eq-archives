weissblau
Yeah, you can do the multi-cast cann thing, but it is a Bad Idea (tm). You can't interrupt a hotkey once it is going, and you can't issue any other hotkeys until it is finished. With the /pause, /cast hotkey you're locked in for a good long while, unable to respond to events.

Yes, you can do something while the hotkey is running - but you can't do it via a hotkey. i.e. you can cast a heal with ALT-1 (or whatever it is on your setup) or by clicking the gem, but you can't use your HEAL hotkey that announces the heal and does a /cast.
