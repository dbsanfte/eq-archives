Forza
I'm about a lvl and 1/2 from getting duel wield. If I put a 1HS in one hand and a 1HB in the other would my skills in both stats increase? If not how does it work? :confused:
entilzah
yes, every successful swing by either hand gives you a chance to skillup. though when you just get it, dual wield doesn't trigger as frequently so whatever's in your offhand will add skillups at a much slower rate.
Kensei
Don't rely on offhand swings to increase weapon skills, it takes WAY too long at your level. Rotate weapons through your primary slot to get the skillups, and keep something fast in off-hand until you get to max skill for each level in DW.
Aethien
I'd second what Kensei says. Even with a fast Piercer in my off-hand, I don't get a lot of skillups.
