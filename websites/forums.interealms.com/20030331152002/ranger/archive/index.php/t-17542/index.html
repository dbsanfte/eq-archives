With any pet class, you either focus on the caster while the pet beats on you, or you get rid of the pet and then focus on the caster. Do not actually stand there and melee the pet though, that's about the worst thing you could do.

Agro the pet, run him off into the middle of timbuktoo, snare and root. Go find the caster, and let the beat down commence. If he's intelligent, he'll be using a 2 hander to avoid your damage shield. I'd recommend you using one too, although I don't know how much more damaging your swarmcaller would be over your two lammies.

Basically, once his pet is gone, he should be cake no matter what you do.

Saltydawg
Well I can shed a little light here.

I have a 60 Beastlord and a 53 Ranger....Beastlord on blue server and Ranger on zek server so I have pretty intimate knowledge of what both classes are capable of.

Beastlord vs Ranger can become a long drawn out fight....it really depends on player skill in pvp environment.

Things the Beastlord has in his favor......

Flash of Light.....blind people often get confused and are extremely limited in their ability to attack.
It is also easy to break los on someone when they're blinded so that ranged attacks will not go off even if they're spamming hot keys.

The Beastlord and Pet will outdamage a ranger in straight melee fighting. A Ranger will not win if he simply runs up and starts to melee a beastlord and pet.

The Beastlord has slow spells...up to 65 percent slow depending on level.

Things the Ranger has in his favor.......

Snare......this is the single most valuable tool a ranger has in pvp style fighting. If you land a snare the fight is yours 90 percent of the time....provided you dont do stupid stuff :).

The bow.....ranger bows have longer range than most spells and with a snared opponent its easy to bow kite them.

Things that even out..............

Both classes have nukes and get them at about the same level and they hit almost exactly as hard...its a draw here.

Both classes have dots....again they are very similar in damage power...another draw.

Both classes have about the same ammount of hp's/tanking ability........another draw.


These fights mostly come down to equipment and pvp exp.

Magic resist will be a key factor...the beastlord needs to avoid being snared and the ranger needs to avoid being blinded/slowed.........this is probably the biggest factor....who can land their "good" stuff.
The dots and nukes dont really have much bearing on these fights....they just dont do enough damage to significantly hurt the enemy.

If your trying to win as a ranger then pump your magic resist up as high as you can get it........snare the beastlord and use your bow....avoid direct melee combat because your bow damage will be greater than a beastlord's ranged damage. (save your mana for snares and healing yourself when needed from his dots and nukes)
If you dont get the pet snared you will lose(beastlord pets are nasty)....snare the pet and then kite him around.

When both the beastlord and the ranger have extremely high magic resist this becomes a long drawn out fight(neither will land their "good" stuff)....an endurance contest (fungi tunics are really nice for these type fights).
