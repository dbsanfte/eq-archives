I vote for the fungi staff, or (if you want to bump up your budget a bit) a horse.

Unlike the REIF or a little bit better AC, the staff should make a big contribution in both group and solo situations. More regen = more mana and less downtime- and you don't really need the epic boots to be effective (let you in on a little secret- I still haven't bothered getting around to getting mine! The snare speed isn't that bad, and you can always just root and step back.)

My other suggestion is to think about a horse. As far as I'm concerned (and maybe I'm just a newb being only 49) the big three (jbb, epic, fungi) should be expanded to "the big four" to include a horse. Constantly medding while standing and casting is huge. When grouped, you don't need to sit down (and get aggro) to med. While solo'ing, the additional mana drastically reduced downtime. Note, though, that medding while standing only seems to work if you're not moving and not swinging a weapon (great for JBB'ing, or root-rotting for handbags.) The cheapest horse costs 8399 pp to buy, sells back for 7619 pp, and works mostly in outdoor zones.
