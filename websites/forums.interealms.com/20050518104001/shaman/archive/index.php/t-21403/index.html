EverQuest Price Drops and 1 year Bonus Plan
As reported by MMORPG.com.

SOE announced today from the E3 show that they will soon be introducing price reductions on some of their expansions as well as a new one year plan that will include all 7 expansions for one price. Here is the official press release:

LOS ANGELES, CA - May 12 2004 - Sony Online Entertainment Inc. (SOE), a worldwide leader in massively multiplayer online gaming, today announced new pricing for its three most recent EverQuest� expansions. Additionally, EverQuest fans will soon be able to purchase a one year subscription to the game and download all seven current expansions for the low price of $119.95.

Beginning today, players will be able to purchase the three most recent EverQuest expansions, EverQuest: Legacy of Ykesha&, EverQuest: Lost Dungeons of Norrath and EverQuest: Gates of Discord at newly reduced prices. Ykesha suggested retail price will be $14.99, while Lost Dungeons of Norrath and Gates of Discord will each carry a suggested retail price and digital download price of $19.99. All three products can be purchased either via digital download from Sony Online Entertainment or at retail.

Sony Online Entertainment has also announced plans to add a new incentive program for all new one year subscriptions to EverQuest. Starting soon, EverQuest players who purchase a full year subscription to the game will be able to download all seven expansions for the game (from the first expansion EverQuest: Ruins of Kunark�, through the most recently released, EverQuest: Gates of Discord) directly from SOE for no additional cost. Soon, one year subscribers will need only the original EverQuest game to access the thousands of hours of gameplay found within the enormous 3D fantasy world of EverQuest.
