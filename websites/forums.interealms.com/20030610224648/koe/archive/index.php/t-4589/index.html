Well from what I understand EQ has been tweaked for the Nvidia Geforce brand of cards like it used to be tweaked for 3DFX Voodoo.

Best for the money is probably a Geforce 2MX. $75 US

Best on the market is the Geforce 3 TI500. It runs around $300 US.

There are a few models in between and it really matters on budget. The different manufacturers aren't all that different, most use the reference design on the boards but usually have a little difference in heat sinks and cooling fan. Asus has a good product but is one of the more expensive. PNY is cheap and decent. Also matters whether you want TV/in out or other features or software.

Check out www.pricewatch.com

However, memory and processor speed to have a larger impact then they used to in EQ. If not at 512MB already, I'd spend the money on doing that and maybe a processor upgrade (both so cheap now).

Well best on the Market....GeForce3 Ti500 (this is what I run) cost about $300 US. You can get a GeForce3 Ti200 for about $200. Then you drop down to GeForce2 Mx 400 about $100 but most places have rebaits on these from 10 to 25 dollars.

Now bear in mind the GeForce is a chip just like the P4 and alot of different companies use the chip set in their cards. They will all cost about the same amount but quality can be extreme. So make sure you look for one made by Hecules(Prophet) or VisionTek(Xtasy) both of these are great and have never heard of problems for them. Side by side both with Ti500's only difference I could find is Prophet has a faster refreash rate(but just bearly) the Xtasy I feel is the better of the two only .05 slower on refresh but has over a 1/3 more triangels per inch.
