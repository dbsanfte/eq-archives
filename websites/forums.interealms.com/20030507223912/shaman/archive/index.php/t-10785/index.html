Juujuu
Silver Hammer Earring (I think that's the name. comes from PoJ Execution trial) has 25hp 55mana.
Skull Charm from CoD has 35hp 65mana on it.
I'd personally get the 25hp 35mana earring that right clicks that crappy sow "upgrade" over the ihoop.

For neck slot, when you hit level 58, you can try taking a tank friend and maybe another healer, and go farm yeldema every few hours until you all get his talisman. 5ac 4stats 4resists 10hp/mana.
Also, go on 8th shawl quests. The coldain tracking collar is like 40hp/mana/10ac with other minor stats.

Lose the mask for Crystal Eyes, Enshrouded Veil, Death Mask of the Elysians, or some other mask like that with 35hp/mana and about 8ac on it.

Drop the helmet for the Circlet of Falinken from the Dragons in Velious. It's a relatively easy quest that a single group of people can do from start to end. The hardest part is catching certain mobs up, like the snake in DN. 50hp 75mana and stats and affliction haste 3 is pretty nice for a single group doable quest.

For your rings, they are quite fine. Next upgrade you want is the Ring of the Shissar (it's the key to get into the emperor's throneroom. doubles as a kick ass ring).

Drop the worked bracer, and go get a Supple Scale Armband. You lose like 3wis and 9ac (no hp on either I don't think), but you get the extended range 3 focus in exchange. Well worth the payoff

Drop the arms for Grey Flesh Sleeves from the Grey zone. They have about 25hp on them.

Check the stats on Grey Flesh Gloves, and Coldain Military Gloves to see if they are better for you.

Stick with Fungal Tunic until level 60 when you can hopefully scribe Torpor and cast it off to one of your twinks. Vindy BP or one of the Velious quested BPs is what you want to upgrade to.

Get a Heiro Cloak on the back slot. 75hp 75mana is hard to argue with. Other option, though it lacks hps, is Lodi shield.

For the belt, look into a Girdle of Mithanial? (spelling?). Not sure the cost of them though. Maybe too expensive. There's a belt that drops often off the bugs in FG. It's got some hp on it I think Very common drop if you are camping the Lucid Shard for VT key quest.

Those legs are actually rather good. Only upgrades I know of to get would be Velious quest legs, or the legs that drop in the Hedge Trial (they have like 40hp 40mana)

Get a Talisman of Benevolence for range slot.

Orb of Infinite Void is the shield you want.

Get epic for primary hand.

You may also want to look into the Strength of the Elements should quest for 100hp if you happen to ever catch Master Yael up in the Hole.

Also, check out the Regal Ring of the Bathzid (spelling?) and the Spirit Wracked Cords quests in Chardok. It takes a lot of work to get the faction for them, but the stats are worth it. There's 100hp on the arm/shoulder/bracer slot item, and like 85hp on the ring.
