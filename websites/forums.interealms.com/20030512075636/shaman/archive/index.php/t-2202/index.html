Gloriana
A shaman's mana pool is not soley determined by wisdom and mana items, but also the amount of hitpoints that can be quickly converted to mana. Hitpoint to mana conversion is one of shaman's most powerful abilities and if you neglect this aspect of being a shaman, you might as well start a druid, get to 255 wisdom and sit on your butt when you run out of mana.

Higher AC = less damage taken per hit, meaning more hitpoints available to cannabalize

Higher hitpoints = more potential mana

Starting at level 24, the combination of cannabalize and regen adds greatly to a shaman's arsenal. This ability gets stronger with levels, and finally, a level 60 shaman with Torpor and Can IV and the AA ability mass cann is a mana regen God.

You know how to make a cleric cry? Both of you get to 0 mana, cleric sits to med, shaman starts cannabalizing, start calling out the mana as you regen, 20, 40, 60...

to add a bit to Gloriana's info - at level 38 a WIS point is giving you about 8 points of mana capacity - it is raising how much you have when full mana by 8 points (formula is level/5, not sure how it rounds)
it is common for shaman to wear their WIS gear while waiting around for something to start up, say a port or a raid, and then start switching out to hp/ac gear as they start cannidancing. This allows a max mana pool at the start of the festivities (euphemism for buffing) and max hp for cannidance.

hp is important source of mana, especially with regen items and regen spell line. ac is important to protect those hp when ya get hit, and ya need to get hit a lot to max out your defense.
