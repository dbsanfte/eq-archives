It's easy to PL to 30+ in 3-4 days /played. I have PL'd a druid to 30 with 1 day /played, so 24 hours, and a cleric to 24 with 16 hours /played. Compare that to the glacier bear, grizzlenot, or spectral courier camps and it's not that bad 8^)

I buff with HoS/Guardian/AGI/STA, haste and chloro, slow the mobs for him, and sic the pet when the mobs get to half health. Find mobs that are just barely red so the melee damage adds up pretty fast and treat the n00b like a tank, regardless of class.

This technique really starts to lose steam around level 25-28 with caster types. I could PL to level 20 in about 6-8 hours played, it takes 16 more hours to get to 30.
