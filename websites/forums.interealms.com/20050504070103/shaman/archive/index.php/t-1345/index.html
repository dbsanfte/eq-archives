Keep the Iksar. I like the trolls too, but i love being Iksar.
Second, put as much into Sta as ya can and go from there. Never assume your gonna be lvl 50 at most and have crap gear. Make a goal to get 60, get awesome gear and be a god and then go make it happen. I never thought i would kill in ToV, AoW, Burrower and so on, but i have and now i have 255 wisdom and some to spare (259 if there was no cap) So i ended up taking the sta as AAXP.
Third, see if you can get lucky in Karnors with some jarsath items. I got the tunic my first trip at lvl 14 with a few other items.

Iksar race is a blast and i actually enjoyed the difficulties from the start with the faction and uniqueness of the class. When i made my shaman I was one of like 6 on my server. A lot more now but was still fun to be unique for a while.
