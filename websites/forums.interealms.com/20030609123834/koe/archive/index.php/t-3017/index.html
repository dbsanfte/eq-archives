What I did to tailor past Cured Silk, to get to where I am (121) and what I plan to do myself:

1) Get a friend or 2, at least someone to talk to, mabey PL, and go to CC and camp every spider there is. You can get some decent loot int he Queen room there and mabey even make a profit in all. The most important thing is the silk though.. make swatches like no tommorow. Turn the swatches into caps or mantles when you're in the city and sell them back to a merchant (they're worth about 5g each). I went right up to 118 with mantles.

2) used the rest of the silks I had that day I hit 118 to try and make some tunics.. only got 1 skill up I think (didn't have any other use for the silk by then though).

3) Go back to Upper Guk/wherever you get Spider Silks and start camping them again. Going to need lots to make Wu's Fighting Caps and Shoulders. Make them till they trivial out, then switch to the more silk-intensive tunics. They trivial at 158 I think.

4) past then, pretty much anything's game. Everything else is super-expensive, super-risky, and >250 trivial. I myself am going to do Artic Wyvren Hide armor (when I solo, the wyvrens in CS are what I kill, so getting some free hides.. plus I think the Wyvren Hide armor only needs Cod Oil or some easy-to-get brewing component). I wouldn't go out of my way to do any of the Velious Fur/Ice Silk tailroing myself just for skill.. mabey if I got aggro'd and got a lucky drop <shrug> (Plus, the Orthmir have a bunch of good quest for pple friendly to 'em). Either way you go at this point, past 158 you generally seem to be screwed :(

Hope this helps give you an idea of what its like!
