Youichi
Go directly to City of Mist ! Do not pass go, do not collect 200pp.

I had seriously good fun, duoing with my wifes monk in CoM at these levels, do the stables for the Spectral Courier, do the Temple, Clear Temple basemant to pick up your Icon. Clear the Goo house, and the two houses pass it, that seem to nave no name.

If your doing well/leveling, try the U-turns/Gazebos.

And don't forget to pick up the 6 notes, for your epic.

You might get lucky and happen across a raid who doesn'y need Lord Ghiosk's books.

And the best thing about CoM, apart from the odd raid, THERES NO-ONE THERE ! No trains, no whiners, no camp/kill stealers, just you, your partner and lots of static mobs, Bliss :)

CoM can take you to 54 pretty easy. I think the front end of zone (templ/stables) area started to go light blue around 52. The goo house was still DB at 54 and most of the back bowling alleys are about the same.

Dulaks Harbor caves should be good till about 54 if IIRC and the loot was great.

Of course you have the Nobles in HHK. When those were camped I would head to Kaladim and zone in and start pulling the little dorf Fort at zone in. 8 mobs, same exp as HHK, more HP and a little tougher to break but never, ever camped (I think I'm the only one on my server that ever camped them). Should take you to 54 ish.

LoY is great at those levels. Dulak's Harbor, you could be doing boat 2 or 3 together, and maybe get yourself some Rust Encrusted Leggings. It has a good balance of exp and cash drops in my mind, including the chance of diamonds and blue diamonds.
Gambum
Hornets in Burning woods. From DL, hug the left wall till you see the Hive, there's a gully behind it, set up camp there. Mobs have low hp and very low MR/CR, if you have a jbb you'll rip thru them. 3 static spawns, easy to split and a ton of roamers that are easy to single.

The zone is very quiet and a little more accessable than CoM.

If you have an Ikky monk, forage for radiant metorite for the shackle quests.
