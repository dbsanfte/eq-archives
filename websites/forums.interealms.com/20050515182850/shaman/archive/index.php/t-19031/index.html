Balance... so many people have a different meaning of the word... would such a spell be unbalancing? I don't really see a problem with shamans having a great line of heal over time spells, while druids have a better line of direct heals (both CH and non-CH)... and clerics get the best of all worlds.

Maybe I just have MGB Heal envy... but I really think this would be an awesome addition... and one where I'd sit back and be happy about it. (and no, not some ancient spell for 3 shaman per server).

Of course there are AA's to reduce the casting time, and to increase the duration.

Best case is 6 x 3s , 18 seconds... and then normal recast delay... thinking it's 2.5 seconds on spell gems, something like that... so another 5 x 2.5 = 12.5 seconds... so about 30 seconds.... extended Q would still be running on the first person.

The main thing being you'd be locked into doing these Q's.

Of course we don't *NEED* a group heal, we've lasted without it this long. But I do see the value in untility to raid and group. Maybe not something you'd use all the time... but with than single Q, Malo/Malos and Turgurs... what spells do you use "all" the time?

Q and Turgurs are my 2 single most used spells... and Cann 5 my most used AA. I consider those to be 3 things very core to the class. Upgrades to any of them... and I mean upgrades, not gimped special use spells... are good for the class.

Group Q is not a direct upgrade to Q, it's an upgrade in the "Q family of spells" that would follow our buff lines... get Single target STA, get group STA. Group STA isn't better than single STA really... it just lets you hit everyone with a spell to save time and mana. This is Group Q... for those situations that might need a group heal.

Someone mentioned a single target Q upgrade. That would rock as well... but I see them as different issues. If somehow I magically had the choice to choose between... Q2 +500/tick (same type of mana ratio or better) and Q1-Group +300/tick (approx same mana ratio or slightly higher cost)... given how much I use Q-1... I'd have to say the upgrade to Q.

But if SoE really wanted to let us have some fun, they'd give us both... for whatever situation we wanted to use them in... just like giving us a true slow upgrade would be good. Perhaps updating the slow percentage would be a bit too much for them... at 75% don't have much room to go up.

So you've got... mana cost, casting time, recast time, resist modifier, duration, and mitigation to play with to upgrade it.

The resist modifer seems to be what most people want... but SoE doesn't seem to want to just let us have that without sacrificing recast time, or duration, or cost... etc...

But that's away from the topic of my original post... I just wanted to express that I would love Group Q. I'd love them to take our most used spells and make them better. It's true that new spells, levels, abilities, and gear trivializes old content... but you can bet that GoD will be designed with that in mind... an easy level 65 xp camp will probably be equal to BoT with medium and harder ones going up from there.
