Chutoi
You have Tallon Zek. If you had the Vallon Zek flag, it should show up on your list.

You also have the Agnarr flag (that's the one that says something about Karana).

Keep in mind that from this point on, even if you kill a boss mob or two, it MAY not show up on your flag list until you have gotten OTHER boss mobs. That doesn't mean you don't have then. For instance, if you killed the Keeper of Sorrows and Saryn in Torment, I don't think you'd receive her flag until AFTER you had killed Bertoxxulous. The flag would still be on your character, but it wouldn't show up until you had killed Bert and gotten your flags straightened out with the Seer in PoK.

Good luck.

Juujuu
The complete list of flags you have to do (excluding hailing all the appropriate mobs) is this:

-Kill Grummus for flag for CoD
-Complete the Carpin cycle in CoD to become /key'ed for Bertox fight.
-Kill Bertox

-Do the hedge trial for flag for PoNB
-Kill Terris Thule for flag for PoTorment
-Kill Keeper of Sorrows and Saryn

-Complete any PoJ trial
-Complete Askr's quest for flag for BoT
-Kill Agnarr

-Kill Aerin'Dar for flag for HoH
-Complete the 3 trials in HoH for flag for HoHB
-Kill Mith Marr

-Kill Manaetic Behemoth for flag for PoTactics
-Kill Tallon Zek
-Kill Vallon Zek

Aside from access to BoT and Agnarr, you need every single other flag in order to be able to enter Temple of Sol Ro Tower zone.

-Kill Rallos Zek for flag for being able to fight Sol Ro, and gaining flags for PoAir, PoWater, PoEarth
-Kill all 5 minibosses in Sol Ro
-Kill Sol Ro for PoFire

This is as far as my guild is so far so I don't know what more is required.
There's also an option flag you can get off the dragon in PoI for access to the factory, but you don't need it to progress.
You only need one person to open the door to let you into the factory.

Your checklist flags (the ones you got without getting the previous ones first) will not show up until you get all the previous ones.

For example, MM won't show up until you get all the trials, RZTW won't show up until you get VZ and TZ, and VZ/TZ won't show up until you get RZTW either for that matter I believe.

Take a look Gemdiver's guide at The Mystical Order that someone gave up above if you're confused. Its all purdy with pictures and everything! He's also pretty consistent in naming the NPC's that you need to talk to for the preflags too.

I wouldn't worry about things not showing up. I got all my flags out of order and worried too when the seer didn't tell me them, but when I got the last one it all came into place.
