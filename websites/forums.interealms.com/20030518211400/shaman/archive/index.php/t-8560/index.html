Naagruz
Just going on posted info and some chats, I would say you are out of luck if you are hoping to finish any of the cudgel quests past the first. Barbarians max out their Scaled Mystics faction at a point that is too low to continue questing past cudgel #1 by normal means. It may be possible to continue the quests by using faction boosting items or by multi-quest. You will most likely have to get a MQ of cudgel #7 in order to ever possess the 8th.

Factioning Cabilis is somewhat longer for barbarians if you are looking to preserve FV faction because you have to do the bonechip/scorpion pincher quests in order to max faction. If you won't be sad about becoming KOS in FV then definitely kill explorers for knives because it truly is the fast route.

After you max your faction with the bulk of Cab West you will need to kill Iksar Treasure Hunters/picaroons etc. to gain favor with the Brood of Kotiz that dominates Cab East. Once you get these necros to dubious you can forget about them unless you want to use the vendors.

Carnage is prolly the handiest pet buff ever. Lasts around 6 minutes and is +40str/dex/agi/attk. It will not stack with PE, Avatar, VoB, and primal procs.
