Wulf
Open up with malos, then melee with tash stick and cast nihil as often as you can. Once nihil lands equip TA or mem turgur. Once turgur lands dispell until you dispell your own malos (you'll know you got all his buffs then). Un-suspend your pet after slow lands so he doesn't get plowed by rampage.

Re-cast malos then get sinia on, and after that it's really easy, just nuke with velium strike and keep turgurs up. Bane works semi-decent and epic dot works really well. Don't bother with disease or poison based spells.

I've killed Zlandi solo about 25-30 times and never died so it's a pretty good and safe pp source, although the CoM is rarer than you might think :/
