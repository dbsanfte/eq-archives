"whats am3 and eq?"

AM3 and EQ are both Alternate Advancement (AA) skills that rangers can get. AM3 is Archery Mastery 3, and EQ is Endless Quiver which means you can use one arrow and it will never run out. AM3 increases your dmg with your bow (I think...correct me if Im wrong)
