As to the poster wondering where some of us are getting our money...
Eventually if you are fortunate enough you will reach a stage in the game where there are little to no tradable items that you can purchase for upgrades. The 'fun' toys are either rare as hell or obscenely expensive (even for the wealthy), your spellbook and that of your friends is pretty much full (excepting ancients possibly), and you find yourself in the role of a shopper with nothing worth buying when you browse the bazaar. If you don't succomb to twinking, you can end up having a very full bank account w/o even trying too hard.

Of course many of us in this situation don't stop playing, so we end up continuing to accumulate platinum. Even without smart business practices you can end up gathering hundreds of kpp just by playing the game and selling your loot below market value.

I personally have gone from being one of those players who rarely had 2kpp to scratch together to now having over 400kpp with very little I'm interested in buying. When I win loot, I typically play 'Santa' and give it to friends or allies. My solo playground (Tactics pit) can generate between 10-50kpp in a single session. If I really farmed it hardcore (instead of casually while LFG for an Adventure) and sold everything I acquired I think I could have a million platinum within a week's time.

I'm not in a big guild, not EP flagged, and I don't have any 'leet' secrets for gathering pp. Read the boards, do the research, and most of all, spend your time IG on pursuits that are enjoyable to you. If a good percentage of your time IG that you enjoy is also productive in terms of item/wealth accumulation, you'll soon be able to blow thousands of pp in a casino as well. :p
