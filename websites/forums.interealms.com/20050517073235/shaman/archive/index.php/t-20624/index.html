I have a Gladiac 64 and kept wondering why since the patch my performance is worse. Talked with a computer friend and he told me this which might explain some problems with folks with older video cards.

Theres a diffrence between dx9 compatable and dx 9 compliant.
dx9 compatable alot of the video perforamance is actually being handled on the computer side thus using more memory.

dx9 compliant means the majority if not all video performance is being handled by the card thus freeing up resources.
