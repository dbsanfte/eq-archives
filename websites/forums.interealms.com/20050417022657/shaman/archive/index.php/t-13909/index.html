Oddloots
I used to spend a lot of time in the grey, so I'll share what I can remember about it.

Are these traps static, as in always in the same spot?
Yes, I remember these traps being static. I remember them always being by a pile of rocks.

Are they triggered only by moving over or through the trap?
They seem to be triggered when your character crosses their location coordinate.

In The Grey, the spots where I have always been jumped are when I am moving over a rock or between 2 rocks. Is this the only case or are there traps in the middle of the open areas as well?
I remember them all being by rocks, can't recall any that are in just an open space.

If I levitate high over a spot where there is a trap will it still trigger?
Your Z axis does not effect the trap. You will trigger it when you cross it's X, Y coordinate.

If I move over or through a trap will it ALWAYS trigger the attack mobs or is there only merely a CHANCE of the trap going off?
Yeah, pretty sure it will always trigger the trap.

If a rogue were to Disarm a trap does it stay disarmed for a specified period of time? How does this work? (I know, this isn’t the Rogue board but maybe someone here knows :p )
I asked my friend who plays a rogue and he does not recall ever being able to sense or disarm the traps in the grey.


Some extra notes about these traps is that I remember most of the spawned mobs cannot see invis. So if you're invis, you can trigger the trap, you'll see the message spam from the trap and the mob will spawn, but it will not have agro on you. I believe that after the mob is non-agro for a couple minutes, it will despawn.

Best tip I can give you about the trap is to not run over them ;) ... run around rocks instead of over them if you don't want to trigger any of them.

Good Luck
