Elured
Find a group doing blue mobs and ask if you can help beat up their stuff. No experience for you, so you're not actually "taking" anything from the group while doing crappy damage.
Drunia
Go to bazaar, buy a Silver Swiftblade. 16/24 2hs RNG only, no stats. Goes for about 50-150pp. Next go kill blues. Ask a group if you can hit their stuff, fear-kite some animals, whatever. Get a dex buff or put on some cheap dex gear, you'll get skill ups faster. Huge difference between 135 Dex and Focused Dex in terms of skill ups.
Kensei
Gear kite with the SSB will raise it faster than anything else. Plenty of blues in JPF for you to play with.
Rezz

3) Swing away.
Erryn_On_Prexus
Do like they said above. Get a silver swiftblade in bazaar. Go find a group killing stuff and ask to help them out.

I would suggest swinging at reds, this will get you more skillups, and preferrably find a group with a chanter and shaman. Get a dex buff from the shammy and a haste and the skillups will be great.

For locations ... At 51 I'd go to UP, but crossing ME is kinda dangerous at your level. I'd suggest UP cause everything will be red and there may be a group without a ranger that will let ranger loot rot. On second thought, UP may be deserted now that PoP is out so that may not be the best choice.

If you skip UP you could take a look for zone line groups in PoN, PoI, or PoD. Or you can skip PoP and go check out ME since it's easy to get to.

To summarize, the keys I've found are red mobs and dex buffs.
