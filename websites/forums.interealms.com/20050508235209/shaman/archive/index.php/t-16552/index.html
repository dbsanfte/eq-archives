The adventure points you get are serving 2 purposes.
1. They serve as the "currency" you use to buy new items.
2. They server as "faction" with the camps.

If you acquire 500 points worth of adventures in Mistmoore, and 1000 in Takish, then you have 1500 total points to spend as you wish.
But, you have effectively 1000 points of faction with Takish and 500 in Mistmoore to buy things.
You can spend up to 500 on 3 items in Mistmoore.
You can spend up to 1000 on items from Takish and have 500 points left over.
You can spend 750 and 750 on items from Takish.
You can spend 15 points one hundred times at any combination of Mistmoore and Takish.
Because you still haven't done any adventures in other places, you can't really buy anything from them yet.
Whatever the number of points you've gotten at a camp over your entire adventuring career, that's the most you can ever spend at that camp.

In the short term, it means you don't have faction to buy anything.
Once you've done 1492 points worth of adventuring in Mistmoore, you've effectively hit max "faction" with them.
You'll be able to see every item they have for sale at that time.
You can then go off and accrue points at ANY camp you want, and spend those points in Mistmoore because you've done hunting there already.

It's really not something to get worked up over.

Luclin's Arriana
When you complete an adventure in a given theme, you are awarded 2 different kinds of points in equal value. The first kind is faction points, the second kind is buying points.

Faction points are PERMENANT. They never go away. They are good for that theme only. They affect what the adventure merchants are willing to sell you.

Buying points are temporary. They are used as you buy items. They are good in any theme. They have no effect on what the adventure merchants are willing to sell to you.

If you earn 500 points in Takish, doing nothing else, then you have 500 faction with Takish, 0 faction anywhere else, and 500 buying points. You could buy up to 500 points worth of items from Takish, nothing from another other camp. If you bought a 500 point item, you'd still have 500 faction with Takish, but zero buying power. If you then went and earned 300 more points in Takish, and 500 points in Miragul, you could go to Takish and buy a 800 point item from Takish, because you've raised your faction there to 800, and by points earned in Miragul, you have 800 to spend. If you then went to Mistmoore, and earned 2000 points there, you could spend all of them in Mistmoore with a 2000 limit, or in Takish with a limit of 800, or Miragul with a limit of 500.
