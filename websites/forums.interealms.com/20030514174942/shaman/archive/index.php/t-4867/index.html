Sorry I didn't take any screenshots, but since I just did the quest last week it's all still pretty fresh in my head.

All 4 items are Lore, the Shaman Powder and the Strange Ochre Clay are both tradable, the other two are No Drop of course.

Diamonds: Too easy, no further explanation nesecarry.

Clay: I've seen it for sale in the bazaar for 500p (LMAO!), but I bought mine off a vendor in Cabilis for 5 gold and some change. YMMV

Bone Chips: You don't have to go very far into KC to loot these, but if you've never been there before go to www.eqatlas.com/atlas and get a map before you head in, it's not someplace you want to get lost in at lvl 39.

Powder: Hang out in Traks Teeth and kill stuff at the zone entrance (almost everything is dark blue and decent exp) untill a group shows up to farm Sebilis keys. 99.9% of the time they will have a ranger, druid, or both to track the Hunter/Forager. Offer your services as a buffer/slower in exchange for the shaman powder. My group only killed one Yun Shaman, and i also got a free Sebilis key too. :)
