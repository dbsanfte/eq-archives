Warrior health - 5%
Enchanter health 20%, mana is low
Shaman (me) health at 25%, mana at 5%
Mage health 30%, 5% mana.

Mage, Warrior, and myself bind wound the warrior to 50% while the enchanter keeps the mobs locked down w/ mezzes. Mage uses mana to summon more bandages & making rods. I now have enough mana finish Turgur's slowing the last of the mobs and have enough for healing. We break the mezzes one by one, with the enchanter going OOM right as the last mez wears off, but we finish the last mob off together. What a rush! We continue binding wound as we call for people to log back in.
Still one of the most memorable half hours I've ever spent playing.

Is Bind wound worth maxing out? Sure. Bandages are cheap, and you can work the skill while waiting for a rez or whenever you have some extra time. Skill-ups are rapid. Is it a practical skill to use? Almost never, but you never know! Is it worth spending any AA on? I don't think so. Binding up to 70% is good enough. :)
