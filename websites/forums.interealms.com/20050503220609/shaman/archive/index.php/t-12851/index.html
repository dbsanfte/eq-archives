Jinheim
City of Mist - Easy mobs, easy pulls, huge exp bonus, and usually empty. I PLed my beastlord here from low 40's to low 50's.

Also: Your prime target for powerleveling should be high blues. You might get more exp per kill for a higher con mob, but the extra time it takes you to kill it makes it not worth your time. A lot of people think that soloing reds is the best exp, but it isn't. It takes you forever to kill them. Reds are only good if you're very low level with a high level DS.
