Greetings shaman,

I'm a long time lurker of the Crucible (posted about four, maybe five times in my entire shaman "career" which lasted 2 1/2 years, but lost my pw =/), and a tremoendous proponent of what this board has to offer for all shaman, and even beyond.

With that premise in mind, I ask you all to hear me out and determine whether what I am about to request is worthwhile. I wholly believe you will.

I've since departed the ways of the shaman and found myself reborn into another EQ profession. This new endeavor has me, once again, levelling through some old haunts, along with doing what many would never wish on their worst enemies...playing the exp game over again. In my travels, however, I've noticed a huge trend in early 30 to 54 level shaman. That being, they do not Cannibalize. Or, should they, they do not Cannibalize properly.

To be more specific, those with whom I've inquired about why they are simply medding respond that it [Cannibalize] is not efficient. When they grew frustrated with me prodding them to cast it, I notice why they feel its not efficient...they do not know how to "dance". Simply, the shaman would stand, chain cast Cannibalize, drop to 30% health and sit down. We all know that pre-Torpor (and maybe pre-Fungi) that is is NOT the way to utilize said spell.

My request is simply thus, being "the" site for shaman, could there not be a sticky under "Combat and Tactics" or "Spells" for shaman new to this site to have at their ready? I believe there is a guide accessible from the main page, however, I'm certain that many simply click to forums. Granted, their loss, however, oftimes we must make amends for our constituents.

I forsee a not so bright future for many shaman, and, sadly, a poor reputation for this class due to most people's ignorance. I know there is a nice thread on how to "canni-dance" somewhere within these archives. Mayhaps a simple locked sticky would prove beneficial to many.

Just a thought.

-Stanos
