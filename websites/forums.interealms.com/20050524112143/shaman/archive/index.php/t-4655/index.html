There was a great thread on this, which I thought was in the combat and tactics forum, but I wasn't able to find it so now I'm wonderin where it went. At any rate, for your level there are a lot less tactics then there are for higher level shaman. In fact, I can think of only three tactics:

1) Root/Rot (Root a mob, DoT it with every DoT you have, re-root it, rinse repeat)
2) Kiting (more viable since that patch the other day; throw a few DoTs on a mob then let it chase you around until it dies)
3) DoT the mob and melee it (only do this if you have good AC).

And then of course there is grouping. At later levels, you can add in:

4) Pet Kiting (like regular kiting, only you let your pet nip at its heels while you kite)
5) Pet Tank (you slow the mob, pet tanks, you cast spells)

Getting back to your original question, I found root-rotting to be the best form of experience at your level, though a good group generally was better exp. Of course, this was before they removed the kiting penalty *shrug*. I'd say just experiment and find what works for you.
