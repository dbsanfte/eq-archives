The Morning


Soft and supple morning dew,
Serves a simple thought of you.
Cross my cheeks they have run,
But now portend the rising sun.

Streaked upon gentle bloom,
Tiny drops of springs perfume.
As silky scented auburn mane
Stringing heart without restrain.

Bowing stalk like bended knee,
Thoughts of you humble me.
Thoughts of days; even one,
Beneath your love, the rising sun.
