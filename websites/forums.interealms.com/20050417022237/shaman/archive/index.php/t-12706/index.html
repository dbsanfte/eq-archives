I duoed poachers in jaggedpine with a warrior or a beastlord at 43-46 quite well. Here's how i did it:

1. Camp the middle tent on the lake side. There are 6 total in the tent. Pull the one in the door first with slow. you shouldnt get any adds.
2. Target one of the two inside the tent and use root on them. The other one will come running. Have warrior/beastlord get agro and sick doggie on it and slow it. When root wears off, root again then slow unless the other one is dead.
3. do the same with the next two inside the tent.
4. Pull the last one with slow.
5. If you do it enough you should be just finishing the last one when the first one spawns again. Make sure to pull far enough back so you dont get agro from the first one. The rest of them should be spread out enough on respawning making it easy to just pull with slow. I seemed to make about a yellow every 45 minutes or less.
