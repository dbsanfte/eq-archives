Under the display options, try turning Screen Same Resolution on. (at least, I think that's the name for it)

The "old" or "classic" screen displays at 640x480... always :(

Turning this setting on will keep your screen at whatever resolution it normally is, and the spellbook will be a small 640x480 bracketed by a lot of black space, but will switch fast.
