EQ Forums (http://boards.station.sony.com/ubb/everquest/cgi-bin/forumdisplay.cgi?action=topics&forum=Technical+Help&number=3&DaysPrune=20&LastLogin=)

Most often, when all things are the same, and you cannot login only one toon, your files for that zone are corrupt.
You can test it by zoning in another toon into that zone and see if the same thing happens..... OR you can just go into your eq folder, sort files by name, and delete every single file with that zone name on it.

EQ will automatically re-download those files, if it doesn't, do a thorough filecheck.
