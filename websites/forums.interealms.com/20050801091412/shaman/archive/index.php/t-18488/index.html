Larenn
At 65, I can single pull the north east corner, as well as west wall and east wall. Literally, chain pulled singles today while "level encouraging" my cleric. I mention my level only because the aggro range *might* be smaller than it could be for your level.

For east wall, take the two golems in the pool first (I pulled the south golem first), then the golem thats a bit further back. There are a spawn of three along the cliff edge that you can't break without FD or lull, so write those ones off and dont even try. Beyond that, you're looking at mostly wanderers left at EW.

WW is much easier. There are three in a row that spawn on the northern half of the field (the southern half is where the cliff is). Pull those first, with the closest to the wall, then the next, then the farthest. With WW, I've found its a lot safer to pull if you always pull to the north... so go a bit north of the mob, and pull it almost straight to you, then run back to the castle. If you try to pull east west or at an angle, the pathing causes the odds of adds to go up a lot. Just careful not to go TOO far north when pulling or you might aggro a #$%^#$^% spider.

After the three north most golems are pulled, keep working your way south as you pull, again always pulling them from the north.

Hope that helps,
Larenn

Droga mobs vary greatly in level. Most of the east side is light blue at 65, which means the mobs have to be maybe around level 45. And nameds in droga are not any harder than regular mobs, as a general rule (maybe a few more HPs is all). I was solo'ing Runri at 61, but I am sure I could have done it at 60 with torpor and malo, and I was doing Japel at 63, although it was hit or miss; if I didn't get off a heal in time to deal with a chained nuke, I was toast. I had about 2200 HPs unbuffed at the time.

For the west side (e.g. king, et. al.), being 65 is all but mandatory; those mobs are up to level 61 and have around 20k HPs. Wearing them down with nife and pox would take forever.
