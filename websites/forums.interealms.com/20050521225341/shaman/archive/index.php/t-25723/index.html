Without a magelo it's hard to tell where you could solo. But, from 65-70 there are a few easy spots:

Droga - do the named all across the zone for ok XP and profit. Plenty of guides to this in the forums.

Nobles Causeway - There are a couple of spots where you can solo. Avoid the named unless you have good gear and AAs. They summon.

Bloodfields - Lots of spots to root rot here. Inside the tunnels you can pet pull the Dragorn. In the fields find a safe spot and tag the wanderers. They don't hit that hard so not bad even if you grab one that summons.

One spot I used is to go through the tunnel and out into the 1st field (be sure and go through the courtyard). Hang a right and follow the wall to an aclove. This area is safe from pathers and has a decent number of mobs to pull to it.

Harbinger's Spire - You can camp the mobs in the first floor. Stick with the dragorns. There are many camps here and a variety of named. There are several threads on camping / farming this zone.

Plane of Mischief - Camp the castle wall and 1st couple rooms of th castle. Goal is to get the research components for RGC. That spell still sells quick at 60k.

You can also still get good experience soloing in PoValor, PoTactics, PoFire, etc. There are threads on all of these zones in the forums.
