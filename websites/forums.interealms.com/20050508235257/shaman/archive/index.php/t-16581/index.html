Zorash
Actually house, even if they are unslowable the spell being resisted creates aggro.

Bottom line is, if SKs just taunt and melee, they aren't going to hold aggro any better (actually worse most times) than a warrior. They DO however get a few great spell lines to help with aggro management. They get a direct aggro increaser, similar to the encie line (+x% hate), they get a "dd hate" type line, (quick cast +x00hate to target), and they get their snare line. They have other tools, but these are the ones I've seen work the most effectively. A good sk holds aggro very, very well...just like a good pally. Heck, they hold it almost as well as a shaman determined to get aggro ;)
