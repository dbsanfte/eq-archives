A level 65 shaman can level a mage faster than any melee to 60, unless you severely twink the melee, but that would require alot of planning.

Unslowed mobs on a buffed/hasted/FA mage fire pet works wonders into the low 50's. The reasons why are...Pets don't follow PC level buff rules, so Focus, FA, 60% haste, Q, etc. all land on a level 4 mage pet. Fire pets DS = level of pet + 1. Bingo...big damage.

Mage pulls with Burst of Flame for full XP, and works on casting skills while pet and DS kill the mob, rinse and repeat. Nuke at will. Move on. This scenario is also equipment independent. KEI optional too.
