Sciek Spellbottler
Recruiting / levelling up a Necro or two and some rogues with SoS would help immensely with those CRs and raiding in general.

Add Statue into that list of stuff to kill. If you can do Vindy, statue IMO. You just have to have heavy healing up front until you get him slowed, but from there it's cake. Take a shot at Tormax if you can find him up too, good stuff to be had there.

For a taste of harder Luclin stuff give IV a shot. It's not a hard mob once you get the strat down, but it does require your raid to all be paying attention. One stray melee or caster can bring a ton of hurt onto your raid there, so it's a learning experience.

You might also wanna start taking some shots at NToV mobs when you have a decent raid force online. Ikatiar and Eashen then Aaryonar once you have little problem with the first 2 and on to the rest of NToV once you down Roy.

Rhag 1 & 2 probably would be easy enough for you and could net some copies of RGC which will be needed as you get into Luclin.

You're at a point right now where there are probably a TON of targets that would be a decent challenge and net you upgrades for your guildmates. Recruit some rogues/necros or level some up or whatever, but do it either way. They'll make those CRs a WHOLE LOT more bearable and faster.
