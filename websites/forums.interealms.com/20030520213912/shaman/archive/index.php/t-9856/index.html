Nruter
I have had the best luck with a melee heavy group, (warrior, ranger, rogue, monk in your case) add cleric and you.

Damage Shields help a ton, use potion for anyone that will get hit, and get a mage/druid to group DS right before you start.

Best tactic I found was to cast AE slow once and you can torpor tank the three not being killed by melees.

If your group is working well together I would suggest casting BoN on one of the last mobs, of each wave, that is going to be killed. It will go full duration so best use of your mana for DPS. Judge what other nukes/ damage is needed based on mana and speed of the melees in killing.

Chanters and shaman in same group tend to reduce DPS too much unless the enchanter has either a lot of AAXP or sufficient levels and is nuking heavily.

Damage Shields will allow you to get a head start on the mobs after the first and at 61 it should be just over 70 points per hit with just potion and mage.

Only time the cleric ever needs to heal me is if the boss aggros prior to slow landing (700 a hit adds up fast) and very very rarely if a torpor is interupted (keep it up on you all the time).
