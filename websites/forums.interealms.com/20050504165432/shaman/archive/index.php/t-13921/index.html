ciccero
How many of you guys think content in "The lost dungeons of Norrath", will be doable with just 1 group 61+?

If so, how many of you think the reward will be nice with 1 group?

EQ has been content chasing since day 1 ... back in the day, everything was more-or-less balanced risk vs. reward.

Then as more stuff was added, pushing the envelope higher, in an effort to match their risk vs. reward model, they made harder encounters to get better gear.

Simple enough.

But it's getting to a point with harder and harder content, to get better and better rewards, (to keep that carrot out in front of player's faces), that to fit in, the content is to a point that no single group, or "practical" guild can achieve it. So where does the cycle end?

Will gear and drops in the new expansion be doable with 1 group and equivalent to stuff in PoP that takes raid lvl forces to obtain? I doubt it, so where's that leave us?

I liked EQ a lot better as well when things were realistically achievable for most any character and not just higher end raid guilds.
