Uthgar
I was bored so i figured I would try soloing a gaurdian wurm. I headed over to Skyfire to scout the zone to see if any where up. I found 2 up which amazed me as i never see them. These 2 where wanderers and not at the VBP zone.

I ran to the dragon statue and setup camp. Summoned doggy and buffed him up and handed him 2 gnoll hide lariats. I headed back to the spot where i saw them near the Overthere zone in. There was a group there and i noticed a druid pulling in one of the GW's. I knew i had to find the other quickly before the druids group could grab it. I found it quickly and pulled it.

Landing slow on the pull i knew i had time to land my other debuffs which i followed up with malos and malicious decay. It was a tough pull weaving through mobs to finally reach my camp. I started with a Breath of Ultor and followed that with a epic dot. I kept myself torpored and canni 4 to maintain mana. I was self buffed only and running 1200ac and 3k hp and i was worried about using mass canni so i just stuck to canni 4.

I was slowly loosing mana but i figured i had plenty to kill him off. around 50% i noticed a fast flurry of hits.. crap, i forgot to reslow him... channeling a slow, torpor and tnargs. i was backup on life and it was slowed. I was now at 20% mana. Bad News. I torpored/canni 4 just to maintain life and mana and just used epic dot and doggy for damage.

Slowly its life droped 15% to 10% to 5%. I forgot to back off the dog and he dies to enrage. I finally get it to 3% which it starts to run. I am less than 5% mana and 25% life. I recast epic and start chain casting JBB. I cant kill it fast enough as it starts wandering into the center of the zone toward a Chomadrac. I panic and root it. I totally forgot they summon and it kills me. arrrgh..

The druids group finished it off and rezzed me. They said it dropped mala and ask if i wanted it. I declined and gated out. Anouther day ill have revenge hehe.
