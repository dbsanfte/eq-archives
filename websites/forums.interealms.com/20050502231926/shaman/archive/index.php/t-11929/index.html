Gilador
EQ Companion home page is http://home.inreach.com/kai/EQCompanion

I am the author of it and I also play 61 Shaman (I dual-box Gilador and Silgurdar, 60 warrior). I have to admit, though, that if you are after a parser which would be able to parse your DPS including DoT's, EQ Companion will not do a very good job. It does an excellent job parsing melee and DD's, though.

Another problem with parsing Shaman fights is that sometimes you are just too far away from the fight and your log file does not have all the damage messages your pet for example does.

This same limitation applies to all parsers, though.

You should give it a try, though. I'm sure you will like it :)
