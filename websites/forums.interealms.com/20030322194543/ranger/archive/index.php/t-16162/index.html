Celestial Tempers are made with 1 purified water (made from 2 water flasks plus a celestial essence) and 1 blue diamond powder (1 blue diamond + a jar of acid).

The water and the temper are made in a brew barrel, the diamond powder is made in a kiln.

This (http://www.eqtraders.com/index.htm) is a link to a trade-skilling site with info about all the tradeskills and recipes and such, a great place for this kind of information. :)
