Gorgos Gookrusher
All mobs have insanely high regen when they have zero agro on them.

You zoned out so you dropped agro and then your dots kept chewing on him... if you had a lvl 1 attack him and run he'd have died from the dots. Because the agro makes his huge regen stop.
Jaradis
If he would have died unagro'd due to a dot, he would have poofed and not left a corpse (unless level 55 or over). So you wouldn't have gotten the sword anyway. Only mobs level 55+ will leave a corpse if they die unagro to a dot, this was done because big raid target mobs would poof at times and leave no corpse.
