This is easy to do at almost any level. At 44 I would find a group and ask if you can buff their whole group for the trade of letting you attack their mobs MELEE (you wont do enough damage to them to steal the exp) and they will get free shammy buffs. I did this for a couple of days and built up my attack and defense skills. Especially if they dont mind you beating on the fresh mobs (from multiple pulling groups) when the main mob is at about 40 health. That way you get beat on pretty good and your attack will stink bad enough that it is easy to have the tank pull the mob off you.

Also Giants in Rathe are great for building up defense. I do the following without my pet:

Slow
Root
Dot - I prefer disease for this
Tank untill dead
(rinse and repeat)

Note keep all AC buffs and HP buffs up. Also Chloro...Lastly dont forget to have trained in channeling...too many young shammy forget to train in this for some reason and well....how do you plan to re-root or dot them when they are beating on you.

Also, Keep a good DD memmed in case you get hammered on and I also always keep my gate up for this event.

A nice place I've been hunting to up my defense is Echo Caverns on Luclin. I hunt the underbulks in the hallways since it is easy to single pull them. I slow them and incapacitate and then just go to it. Fully buff yourself of course, but other than that, things should go smoothly. My defense went from 160ish to 190 (currently) in roughly a level of experience. The underbulks do hit hard and decently fast, so I recommend slowing them. Incapacitate helps out tremendously. Here's what I tried:
1) Ping Fuzzlecutter in WFP.
After multiple attempts before I gave up on him, I received a total of 2 points. Each time I'd buff myself to the hilt, and incapacitate him after I was hasted. PS: Don't kill him, and warn the newbies not to try to help you, because I usually had an audience each time I did this. I also announced in /ooc what I was doing, in case any traveling gnomes wanted to use him for vending w/ good faction before I started whaling on him. Be kind and /petition for him to be repopped once you are done with a practicing session.

2) Forest Giants in FV.
A GREAT camp while levelling in the mid 40's. Without a JBB, you'll need a partner though. I received the remainder of my defense points (hit 200 and missed the message I was having so much fun) while soloing these. I also picked up 3 levels and 3 forest loops there... :D
