Mushroom Temple in Dawnshroud Peaks is *excellent*. Set up on the South East side of the temple - vic -1700, 300.

10 or more static spawns, all are single pulls. plus NO mobs that path nearby that are aggressive. Tribals and Zelniaks wander around outside once the mushrooms green out.

Just be careful getting there...parts of DPeaks are dangerous at your level.

Nice loot, you can get backpacks full of mushrooms that sell for 2 - 5 plat each.
