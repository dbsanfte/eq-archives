Kanad
20k for a Hammer of Spiritcalling? where are you from? On Mith Marr this hammer goes for 2k and less (mine was 1.4k).

This is a good situational piece... not good for working 2HB, but if you have a good 2HB it hits hard. I get 120-110 hits but the average is alot closer to 55. Your mileage may vary.

The snare proc is situationally useful too, but I've found it to be unreliable. One fight it might go off early and then not again, and the next it might hit just before the mob runs. In either case the short duration makes it not so useful, but I like it.

Enjoy it if you have it, but once I have my epic I doubt I'll ever use it again. Just my 2cp :)
