cattywumpus
Only change I would make is dump the froglok breastplate for an Ikky. Regen is a far greater benefit than a couple of points of AC. I recently tried a Barbed ringmail and found that I took far more damage in a battle than I did with my Ikky. The regen would replace HP faster than the AC would stop it.

If you can get 2k for the froglok you should be within striking distance of the ikky within the price you quoted.

At 52, 2 to 5k is not a lot to work with for equipment, if you want to upgrade much now raiding is your best option. Most upgrades I'm looking at at 51 are at a minimum of 8k (earring of the solstis gifts or GM brewers corker(artisan's seals go 8k on zebux)).

Naagruz
Sorry bout the mask, but sell it! No resists, no hitpoints, not even AC can save that from being the weakest link. Enshrouded Veil is a cheap, logical upgrade.

Not sure where your loyalties lie, but a VERY easy Kael quest would give you a Velium Studded Cloak AC12 +5FR/CR +25hp. The Girdle of Reflection and Coldain Military Wristguard quests are also good time vs reward items to shoot for.

Another neck option, besides those mentioned by Cosik, would be a Sabertooth Amulet.

Ditching the SBS all depends on how much you value resists and if you are in few situations where they are important, then definitely go with the Bladestopper. DO NOT EVEN think about blowing your money on another weapon at this point because you 1) don't have a SoF yet and 2) need to get cracking on the cudgel.

It looks like your gear is pretty balanced though and some raiding/levelling will definitely help your situation out. Levels open up areas like DN, ToV, WW, and CT where you can find some nicer stuff to fill those slots. :)
