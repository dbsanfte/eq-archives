Levels will give you fewer resists in general, and make you resist better. Malo/Torpor/Etc. aren't required spells when you hit 60, they just make life (much) easier. They will come to you when it is time.
As for AA, while the amount of exp to ding is the same you should take into consideration that the amount of exp you get from killing a mob changes as you level. (Ie, a level 50 mob at level 51 might be worth 500k, but the same mob at 60 might be worth 300k. Not real stats, just an example.) My personal suggestion would be runspeed 3 if you spend much time in dungeons, then level.
