CrustyOldShaman
You can enjoy playing level 60 shaman without malo or torpor: its a great class even without these spells.

Torpor is no longer vital: Kragg's Mending will allow you to canni/heal after a fight to reduce downtime to minimal levels. Stoicism will ease the pain of a torporless shaman (ie. make it easier to self heal during a fight) even further.

Malo is nice as you don't get a chain of resists just trying to lower resist rate, but for a lot of (most?) non-raid mobs resists aren't usually an issue.
