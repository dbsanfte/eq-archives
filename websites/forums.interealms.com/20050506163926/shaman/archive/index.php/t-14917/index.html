taraddar
They don't. You only get the best of any type effect.

Take my gear for example.

I've got Pernicious Focus, Preservation of Xegony and Mana Preservation IV

Pernicious Focus is 30% mana pres but only on dots.

Preservation of xegony is 15% mana pres on all spells and go old MP4 is 10% on all spells.

So what happens is when I cast a dot I get Pernicious Focus and get the 30% mana pres.

When I cast anything else I get the Preservation of Xegony and get the 15% mana pres.

You can have multiple types of effect on a spell tho but not of the same type. IE I can decrease cast time, decrease mana cost and extend duration of a spell all at once but I can't save mana twice.
