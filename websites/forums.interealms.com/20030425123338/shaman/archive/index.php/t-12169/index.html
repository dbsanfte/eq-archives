cattywumpus
I might recommend the chipped bone bracer for the wrist slot for the time being. It's pretty cheap and the focus effect will last you at least till you can get decent stats out of the ones you're wearing now.

The CC arms are good to the 50s, not many great arm items out there for less than 5kpp. If money isn't a problem look at Hardened Grey Flesh Sleeves.

The Fine Antique ring from PoK is very nice for the time/cost of the quest and you will be wearing it for quite some time as well.

You might consider getting the spell haste 2 item or a chrysoberyl talisman for the neck slot until your Channeling gets to the point where you don't get interrupted 75% of the time.

Horse effectiveness is determined by your Meditation skill, It would really be a luxury prior to around level 30-40.

As far as weapons go, look for bargains and try to keep all your skills up. I paid 200pp for a Blight, Hammer of the Scourge and have been quite happy with it but melee DPS is still not really that great. At 45 I'm more efficient Horse tanking and letting DoTs and Scooby do the work and not turning on auto-attack so I regen mana at sitting rates during the fight. If you see me swinging it's because I hate to neglect skills.
