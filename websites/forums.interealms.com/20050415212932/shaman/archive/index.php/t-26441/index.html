Grizlor
It will be made with alchemy. the process involves grinding components together to make the ink, then scribing it with that ink.

The recipe is as follows:

1 Avatar of War toes
1 Torpor Scroll
3 Inktu'ta grave mold (Foraged)
1 Discordant Scoriae
15 Obsidianwood Sap
1 Veeshan's Peak Ash
1 Camel Toe of Aten Ha Ra
1 Vander's Bane (To crush the stuff together, consumed on the combine)

The paper is currently obtained from a quest requiring a nest instance.

Good luck!
