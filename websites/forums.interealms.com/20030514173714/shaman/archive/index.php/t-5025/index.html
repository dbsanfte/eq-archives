I have a 53 warrior and I can tell you that it's all about delay.

If you want to skill up faster, get a low delay weapon, a haste item (Silvery Belt of Contention is a good +WIS haste item) and keep yourself quicked.

More swings per battle = more chances to skill up.

Also, if I remember correctly, DEX increases your chance of gaining offensive skills (AGI is defensive). So keep yourself DEX buffed as well.
