Well let me be the first to welcome you here to the boards. Not all of us are grumpy shamans and even the grumpy ones are happy to share their knowledge. Feel free to ask any question you have.

Now just a few things that will help you out in the near future. HP is the best thing you can get, then AC then lasty Wisdom. At lvl 24 IIRC we get Canniblize it takes 50 HP for 20 mana, not great but its a start, with more HP you will be able to munch on yourself more often and thus regen more mana.

I know your on the newest server so the "economy" balance isn't there yet.

(Some stuff here are general tips, you might know them or not I try to let everyone know.)

One of the best places for any shaman is the shaman channel. So in case you can't remember or didn't know how to join other channels its /join serverwide.crucible:crucible then you use /1 or /2 depending on how many channels you have. Sometimes odd fights break out, but they are very rare.

Next thing as I am sure you have already figured out after having an enchanter is to make two separate windows, this is very useful overall for listening to people talking to you, or hearing the people chatting without your text sending the whole thing up.

Other then that I can't really give any more advice, most of it is learned from the wisdom of the others who have already passed through these boards and onto other things. However it is up to us young ones to pass it on to the others.

I am usally found in the shaman channel mostly listening but I ask a question or two everyonce in a while.
