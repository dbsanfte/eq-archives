Good call on the Earring of the solstice, it should be the next item. If your guild has enough tradeskillers, it can be very cheap.

Another decent earring choice is Silver hammer earring (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16449).

A bigger step up is raptor bauble (drops in CT) (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=10447)

I chose the Tae Ew shield as well. Assuming you know a GM smith, the hardest parts are the Tae Ew blood (which you could solo at CT entrance) and Iksar blood (just boring farming in Dalnir or guard/necro in Seb).

To maximize hitpoints, i wear a slime covered rubicite coif (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=12186).

Finally for the neck, you could look at the ribbon choker (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16451) or tracking wolf collar (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=12307).

One of the key thing for me is to avoid low health aggro when duo'ing using Cann V, so maxing hitpoints have been pretty key.
