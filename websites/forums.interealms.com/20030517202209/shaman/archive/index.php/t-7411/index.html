Legs:
Jarsarth leggings are (from memory) 15AC +5wis +5int. These legs drop extrememly frequently in Karnors Castle, and just rot on the corpse (because they are no drop) - go to KC and ask nicely if you can loot a pair. Alternatively, do the Kromriff Miitary Leggings quest (but you will need a higher level friend (50ish) to help you with the final battle)

Back:
Wet thorn Cloak goes for around 150plat
Totemic Cloak is possible for you to quest at your level, although it is one of the tougher of the totemic quests.

Wrists:
Totemic Bracer is very very easy to quest
Jarsarth Bracer is common drop in KC
Coldain Military Bracer is awesome (but need level 50ish friend again)

Earring:
The player made imbued ivory earrings will give you more AC

Range slot:
Ceremonial Smudging stick might be worth a look?

Weapon:
since you like 2hb, keep eyes open for Granite Face Grinder, its faster and does more damage (but no proc)
