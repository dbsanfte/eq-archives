Landragoran Heronwing
All the pots are useful, ESPECIALLY the evil city pots!

Neriak potion puts you in a rather bad place unless you have invis, tho not directly in aggro range of anything . . . but you do have invis so that's not an issue. However, it's a short run from there to lavastorm and solb. Also a short run to EC and WC.

Freeport pot puts you on the docks, fairly safe place even if you are kos. From there you can hit Ro, WFP, EC, or even take the boat to OOT if you have business there.

Erudin pot puts you at the docks in between guards, they dont see invis tho, not much does in that city. Good access to the Hole and luclin spires.

Cabilis port puts you next to the zone to LOIO, it's a safe zone in spot, and you can stand there without invis and not be aggroed. Great access to kunark really. LOIO is pretty central to everything. And if you're going to Seb, just invis up and hike to Swamp of No Hope on the other side of East Cab. SoNH --> TT --> OS.

Kaladim puts you very near a well traveled druid ring, from which you can often get a port.

Felwithe is nice cause it's a short zone and short run away from a spires. Klethelin port is actually further from the spires.

Grobb is great! It takes you to Inny swamp fast as hell and you can hit Guk if you have business there. Always nice to hit Guk, grab an EE bag and go hit EC and sell it eh? They sell so fast . . . prolly have it sold just in time to go check the spawn again.

Ogguk port is also really handy for when your guild goes to PHEAR after demis or Cazic or whatever. Also ideal for getting to CT zone hunting groups in a hurry.

Riverdale is great for a quick bank and fastest access to a druid ring. You get set down by the zone to Misty, so once you zone in, just take a few steps and zone again to Misty. Instant drood access.

Akanon . . . erm . . . well you get to harrass the gnomes! Haha, stock up the larder as it may go heehee. They taste like papaya you know! Um . . . access to MM for dire wolf killin or brownie pharmin for baking recipes. Come to think of it too . . the brownie guards are kinda good exp. They don't pop often enuf tho.

Halas . . . well it's home for many of us, and you can buy any herbs you need at the drop of a hat in a city with vendors that like you. For the evils . . . it's good access to Permafrost for exping in the basement. That can easily be done solo btw, and is quite good exp. I've soloed there on many an occasion. I'm not sure what level is the cut off for single pulling stuff here, but around 57+ will be fine for solo here i think. Lower if you're more skilled. The guards you port in by are green cons to a lvl 60, but the zone is across a small lake if you need to use it to escape them. I doubt if they see invis, but I don't know if they do for sure. If yer swimmin skill is ok, i'd give it a shot.

The only places I don't have fast access to now are velious and luclin. But those are still fairly fast, at least as fast as they were before actually if you count time spent waiting for a pad to activate.
