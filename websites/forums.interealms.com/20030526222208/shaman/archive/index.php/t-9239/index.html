According to Lucy, Bane of Thule puts on 4 disease counters.

Counteract Disease cures 8 disease counters with a cast time of 4 seconds for 50 mana.

Disinfecting Aura cures 20 disease counters with a cast time of 6 seconds for 150 mana.

The Earring of Purity has roughly the same cast time as the spell if I recall correctly.

Ximix the Troll
Bane of Thule really sucks since they made it unresistable.

Our Radiant Cure AA will cure it once in a fight. Casting single target cures does not seem worthwhile unless your group is able to prevent the shaman lizard from just re-casting it 20 seconds later. Velious legs are too slow to be effective.

The best answer to Bane of Thule is to have an enchanter chainstun all Cazic Thule shaman mobs (except the 55+ unstunnable ones). Clerics also get a group heal/disease cure spell that will remove it from the whole group at once (Word of Restoration, I believe). This spell does eat up a lot of cleric mana, though.

When we fight a level 55+ unstunnable Shaman lizard, we sometimes just have to endure Bane of Thule for much of the fight.

With a combination of chain stun, Radiant Cure, and Word of Restoration, you can mostly neutralize Bane of Thule, though.
