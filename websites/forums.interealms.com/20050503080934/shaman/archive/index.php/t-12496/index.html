Ok I did it yesterday.

Through observation and feedback here from others, this is what I have deduced.

When you hit the island and see that Ooglyn is in a warping rotation from her Spawn point then walking to the shipwreck and repopping to repeat her course, just tag her and swim her past the ship wreck. if you are worrried about not getting full True Spirit faction because you didn't pass her the gem, just pass it to her after you complete the test to get your faction hit.

I just rooted her and logged out, relogged in and followed her to the designated location and the first of the spirits popped 10 mins after she left. Whole exercise took me a total of 2 EB casts.

If ooglyn isn't walking her route but is standing at her spawnn point, then give her your gem and tag her past the shipwreck.

When you see an ooglyn or many ooglyns walking the route, that means someone else had passed her the gem but didn't know about tagging her past the bugged point of the shipwreck and there is no need to hand in the gem to her to get the spirits to spawn.

Why do this? because just incase the spirits do not spawn, you can then pass her your gem and tag her past.

If you have been waiting more than 30 mins (20 should be more like it) then the spirits will not pop. I noticed the time between each spirit is about 12 to 15 mins and the first is about 10 mins after ooglyn leaves.

Hope this helps.
