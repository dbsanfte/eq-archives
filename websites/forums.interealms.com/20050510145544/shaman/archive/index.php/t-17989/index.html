Among my various toons, one is a 55 necro. My shaman is only 36 at this point so I can only speak on the first 36 levels with real authority.

First off I voted Shaman on the poll since the question specifically asked who can solo uber mobs best. If you talk about undead uber mobs specifically, necro wins without debate.

Secondly, necros kick the crap out of everyone but bards for general soloing. Druids come in a respectable third. Soloing well is not about doing any one thing best, it's about doing lots of things well and thats why those three classes are the best soloers. Shaman absolutley have better than average solo ability, mainly because they can do 3 things: slow, heal and Cannabalize. Lots of classes can solo, but few as well as a necro.

Next, and I say this with the kindest and most forthright intentions: if you believe necro DPS is poor in a regular group situation, you are mistaken. It is absolutley not maximized in a group environment, but assuming they know what they are doing a necro still kicks out some very good DPS.

Finally, it is painfully obvious that shaman are more wanted for groups but saying that shaman are much better at what they are wanted for than necros is specious at best. Such a statement assumes the only thing you want a necro for is DPS. In general, groups look for a shaman when they need slows and heals. Every group needs slows and heals. Groups looking for DPS start out looking for rogues and work their way down through whatever perceived ranking they happen to believe in. Groups simply don't look for a necro, because what a necro is best at is soloing, however a necro absolutely can fill the role of DPS in a group while providing a number of other utilities, such as CC, mana regen and patch healing. What mentioning any of this has to do with the logic behind discussing who solos what best escapes me however.
