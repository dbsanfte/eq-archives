Maybe try the halfling guards along the wall in Misty Thicket. They always drop a spear worth 10pp, a shield worth 1pp and 0-3pp in coin.

There are 7 guards there. 2 right in the gate and 5 along the wall. Here's a quick summary:

Standing outside the wall (Runnyeye side) and facing the gate.

2 inside gate: ~6 minute spawn
Left side: Deputy Asler
Right side: Deputy Keld

5 along wall: ~20 minute spawn
In front of gate on right: Deputy Eisler/Poohl (they switch spawns)
Along left wall: Deputy Felp
In tower by gate: Deputy Drebo & Deputy Budo (Budo has no spear)
Along right wall: Deputy Looh

I don't know how they will compare at level 39, but at 53 I can chew through them as fast as they spawn and get 250-300pp per hour. Be careful at your level though as Budo might be too tough for you. He's still dark blue at 53 (all the rest are super green). Also, be careful of Ella Foodcrafter - she paths to the gate every ~5 minutes. Don't fight near her or let her see you if KOS. You'll be dead in no time flat.
