Owwiefixer
It is a pretty boring camp and the spawn time for Empie Chotty should be reduced big time. I've got 100+ hours in this camp, between solo and often times with a partner. Conversation seems to be the best boredom ward. :)

We've pulled the whole guard spawn for grins before. It wasn't too difficult. I'd say a good duo or solid trio should have no problem. They dont hit very hard, slow easy and root park nicely. 60+ Shammy + enchanter or 60+ shammy + necro shouldnt have any problem splitting this, and once split the shammy should be able to handle the rounds.

The best idea is to try and aggro the guards outside the door. If you space these out, they can give you something to do. Just sit right up against the door and they will aggro through the wall and come in.

If you can get a group of say 4 people, the bounce between empie and 4R is also a good cycle. The timing to kill the 4 rooms, then back to empie room is almost perfect. There are also all sorts of neat toys and trinkets to be had in 4R- from hiero cloak, nullspace WR box, snare whip, JBB, ykesha club, tash stick, etc.etc. Nothing to write home about, but fun toys.
