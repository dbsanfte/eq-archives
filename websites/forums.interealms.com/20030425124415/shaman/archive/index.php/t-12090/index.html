Well, here you have a question of what is more important for your character. If you are a HP freak (like some), then the Girdle of Mithaniel is the better choice.

But, in my opinion, the Girdle of Magnificence is a better balance. High AC, high wisdom, and solid resists. The only area where it is inferior is hit points, and even that isn't a huge difference.

If your guild kills Dain or has any intentions on killing him, you'd probably find his belt to be of interest.

Other than that, I'm not really sure what else would be an option. The belt I use is from Lendi the Keeper in WToV. Check my magelo link if you want to see the stats but it's not as good as the Magnificence.
