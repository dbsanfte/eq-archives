Jaradis
All it does is add disease counters, it still checks versus magic.


Changes that this will cause:

- Those slowed in zones like Ssra where mobs love to cast Tigir's and other slows can now be cured without having to dispell half their buffs.

- Mobs that AE dispell will not dispell their slow anymore.

- Players can no longer dispell the slow on a mob in a means to cause grief to a raid because you can't cast Abolish Disease on an NPC. (ie. No more dispelling Statue of Rallos Zek to get raids wiped).

- Shaman that manage to slow themselves after torporing themselves and forget to change targets can easily remove the slow without having to remove all your buffs. :p
