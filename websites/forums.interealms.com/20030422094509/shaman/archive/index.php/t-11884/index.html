Summarizing the quest, you need 6 pieces of luggald flesh, 3 from luggalds in the caverns in Dulak, and 3 from the luggalds in Nadox(the same ones that drop pure blood). In Neriak commons, right by the waterfall, there is a new NPC, Fleshweaver T'soma. Hailing him will get you a bag, in which you combine the 3 luggald flesh from Dulak. Turn that in, and ask him "what task?", and you get another bag for the Nadox flesh. Combine and hand back. This gives you a hidden character flag. Now head to Tox. In the NE corner of the mainland there is an NPC named Assistant T'so. Hailing him will spawn a level 59 giant skele SK(provided you have the hidden character flag). He will most likely HT you(about 1000 points). After malo, I had no trouble landing slow. Hit pretty hard, someone got hit for 490, but he only hit me for about 300. No problem with a few friends though. Loot the heart and return to T'soma for the ring.

Not a bad ring for the difficulty involved. Extra regen is always nice, even with torpor. We had about 6 or so of the fecund luggald flesh drop while camping for my pure blood. I got the flesh in Dulak from greens, fairly quickly.

There is also a "good" version of this quest in Freeport, with very similar rewards. Both versions have a somewhat crappy 1hs weapon as the reward for classes not listed on the ring(including poor beastlords :) ) I am planning to check to see if I could do the FP version, even as a troll.
