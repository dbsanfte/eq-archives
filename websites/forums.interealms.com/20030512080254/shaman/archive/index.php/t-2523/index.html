Donar
The Ice Giants in Everfrost/Permafrost are immune to cold-based spells. If you want to fight them, you need to rely on the poison and disease dots and or the poison based DD spells. I usually slow them, cast plague, ebolt and sic the dog on 'em. I refresh ebolt if necessary and by then the IG is pretty much a goner. Just keep an eye on the pet's health. The IGs hit hard, so be careful. Good luck to you.
Helfdane
I am an experienced Ice Giant slayer, I soloed them from 49-54, with occasional excursions to City of Mist and Chardok. I have found that the JBB will indeed land on a Ice Giant >IF< you Malosi it, simple Malaisement will not debuff the save enough to allow it to land. I only found this out by accident, having Malosi'd a IG after it resisted 2 Turgor's in a row and I never fight one unless its slowed :-)
When the beastie turned to run, I jumped in and started swinging my Rune Etched Icewurm Fang at it, and to my surprise the proc landed...after a little experimentation I found that the JBB would land as well, but is more often than not near fully resisted, doing in the low teens damage, sometimes 1/2, and I have yet to see a full damage blast.
As others have stated before, the best way to solo the Ice Giants in Everfrost is to root/rot and/or use Dog-Dog, always include a nice slow in there, whatever flavor your prefer, it will save your life if you go LD or if you get a resistant one and need to head for Bandl :-)
