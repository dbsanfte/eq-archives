Gwain Buffmonkey
There's a sekrit to getting kael armor!

You know the area behind the ice wall? The temple, with vindi and such? Go back there. Jump down and kill the armor or whatever. On the right wall where the armors are, there's an invisible wall. Go through it. Down the tunnels are more GoZs and such. You'll pop out on a little ledge overlooking the inside of the arena. Hop down, kill your way out of the arena, then cycle through the temple again.

Much bigger faction hits, much more frequent armor drops. Mobs are tougher, but not that much tougher.

I went the CT route after getting ticked off at chain drop rates.
Here's a few tips:
1) take a cleric to pacify! With my GF's 57 cleric pacifying, single pulls are no prob. BUT once the 2 lizards at the door are pacified and pulled, if she gets close enough to open the door BAMMO. 3-4 mobs ...gotta zone. So we had monk sneak/hide up to door, open and she pacified at max range. rinse repeat

2) another spot if you have a wiz friend is the maze. Have the wiz TL your group into CT. There are no roamers near the spires. Of course pulling is trickier due to getting lost, and more casters. We tried this method a few times but our monk kept getting lost and having to try to FD thru 3 lizards nuking him.

Ultimately I took a group of me (@ level 58-59), cleric (57), Pally(57), Monk(52), and 1-2 various guildies rotating in and out over the course of 5 nights ( 3-4 hours a night). We stayed at the door for simplicity sake. The drop rate varied widely. One night we got only 1 blood, the next night ( same camp same team) we got 5.
We found a smith that would accept blood for payment ( i.e. give her enough to make a tunic to keep in addition to the one I'm wearing). In that period we farmed enough blood for : One tunic( mine) 2 shields ( cleric and pally). I'm going back for more to upgrade my shield. :D

Hope this helps
