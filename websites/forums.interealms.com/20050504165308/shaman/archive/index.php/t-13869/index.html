Hi-

I'm was in a very similar position to yours about six months ago. Played through Kunark, started Velious and quit for 18 months or so. If you feel that your are going to continue to play for a while, I highly recommend buying the expansions, at least through PoP for the Plane of Knowledge teleport stones. They COMPLETELY change the way the game is played. No more 40 minute trips around the world.

The old world is pretty empty now, which can be a mixed blessing.

To upgrade your equipment you will need Luclin and the bazaar, EC is dead and gone. You will also be amazed (at least I was) to see what 500 plat will buy today vis-a-vis the good old days.

Head to the bazaar and forget about totemic. Spend some time getting a feel for the prevailing level of prices and buy Ry`Gorr and cheap Jaundiced stuff. Look at metal linked crowns and leaf scale gloves to have something to grow into. They go for ~100pp on Torvon.

Sounds like you have the right mind-set to enjoy the game, so get some new toys and have fun!

40-something Shammy after 2+ years

taggletoe
Runnyeye comes to mind. At 37 you should be able to solo the third level if you're careful. Battlewizard Unak should be your main target because he drops the Black Alloy Medallion 6 AC 5 wis and mana conservation II.

Warsiliks Woods would be another good place to hit. The Iksar Bandit Lord drops the Bracer of Scavenging(9 AC 5 Sta and Dex) and the Grachnist drops 10 AC 10HP gloves. You can also try to get lucky with a Forest Loop off the giants in the fort or you can hike it over to the Frontier Mountains and solo the blue giants there hoping for a loop.

With your 700pp I'd look to save 100pp more and buy a pair of 6AC/65HP rings. They will save you much more often than a pair of +7 wisdom rings.

There really are some good items to be had for cheap if you ever make it to the bazaar. It's easy to pick up a 10/25 spear for 200pp and assorted JBB/Ry'Gorr pieces for 400pp. Getting Ry'Gorr yourself would require a fairly strong group at 39 because only the Elites drop it, they're 40-42, hit in the 100's, and difficult to single pull at that level.

weissblau
By the time I get Velious, I'm hoping I'll have hit 38 (which I assume is a nightmare level)

No more nightmare levels/hell levels. They did away with those a while back.

No more class penalty to XP, you'll be happy to hear. The race penalty stands but it's only 5% for barbarians. There's a small XP bonus for grouping (they are tweaking this on test and it may get bigger).

No spellbook needed to med. Just sit. Works at any level.

DOTs do full damage even when the mob is moving. This had been nerfed for a while but was unnerfed.

There are some new spells, even for your level, but you have to have some of the later expansions to get them (Velious/Luclin/POP).

There are "focus items" now that affect spellcasting. Better healing, better DD, longer DOTs, faster-casting spells, etc. Check on Allakhazam for more info.

If you get Luclin, you can use the Luclin spires for transportation and it helps things a lot. If you get POP you can use the books and they are AMAZING, SO much easier.

BTW there is an all-in-one expansion called Everquest Gold that has basic, Kunark, Velious, Luclin, and POP (but not Ykesha) and sells for not much more than a Velious upgrade. But I guess it's too late now if you already bought Velious.
