Some of the classic Kunark dungeons are good solo spots.

I enjoy solo'ing in Sebilis. It's not easy, but you are probably high enough at 59 to single pull most of the early stuff, simplifying things a great deal.

Chardok is also good, as long as the stuff near the entrance is still dark blue, and I think most of it is at 59. If you can get into the tunnel, it's probably mostly two pulls at most, which you should be able to handle, also.

VL is another quality spot. It's virtually empty now, and the mobs are quite easy. Surprisingly, the spiders are not poison resistant, so they're easy kills. Now that I think of it, I think VL is the best solo dungeon for a 59 shaman. I was doing well solo'ing there until I start root-rot'ing in PoN.
