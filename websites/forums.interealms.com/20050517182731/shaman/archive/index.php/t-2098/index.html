*sigh* You all are fishing rookies.

My friend taught me all about fishing and it's way more than a "relaxing spot in a zone" You have to find a place where you can fish while you're getting exp. You have to bait your group towards a body of water telling them how much better the pull spot is over here by the water. Then when they finally get there, you sit back and let them rake in exp for you while you fish/forage all day long. If they ask why you aren't helping in the group just make up something about "I'm mana pumping" or something else useless. This works best as a necro as most people don't expect you to be able to do much anyways (even though we all know differently)

I'd have to go with LOIO for the low levels. Easy to bait your group down to the water. Plane of Growth is nice for the high levels. Can sit in the creek all day and fish and just wait for your class drops.
