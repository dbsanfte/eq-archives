I love the shaman/ranger combo.

Just so you're not misled: I wouldn't recommend a ranger as a "fire and forget" type character to 3-box with.

Archery doesn't have autoattack yet, unless you have a gamepad or similar device. So an archery is sitting there hitting range attack constantly - it's on a par with cannibalizing all the time. :)

Plus, an archer is continually moving around adjusting position trying to avoid arrows vanishing.

You also need to be level 59+ with a significant amount of AA for archery to be worthwhile as a main form of combat for a ranger, except for Trueshot discipline once every 72 minutes. AM2/3 is where archery does enough damage to consider using it over melee, it's 27+ AA for AM2, another 9 for Endless Quiver unless you want to be summoning arrows all the time.

Apart from that, a ranger would add DPS, snare and utility to the 3 person group, but I understand your wish to avoid playing 2 melees. :)

-mage, like the shaman, has summonables that help such as levi rings and so forth, pet dps is nice, thier nukes are great.

-wizard i would choose wizard last, why you ask ? wich primary healer are you goign to add ? if its cleric, you need some more punch, so add a wiz. if druid, go with mage or sham.

-beastlord add these guys if your going cleric and would like the added dps and utility, slightly better than the shaman, the beastlord can also offtank, but can not heal as well as the shaman, slows are so close you won't notice it.

Healers - Druid and cleric.

-Druid, more dps than the cleric, but less ehaling, less overall healing utility, BUT he does pack evac's Soe, and other goodies, if you go druid pick up a shaman or beast, you may come up light in the hps area but you can make that up with some of the gear you can farm.

-Cleric, big daddy bomb healer, has more dps than the shaman, and more healing than the druid, can save you in almost any situation if played right, has the best HP buffs in the game, can turn a bad situation into one you can walk away from. if your going Warr, beastlord, cleric
there is almost nothing you can't do.
