The answer to your question is.....it depends.

XP fight I'd say SK. They are the shammy of melees, lots of tools in the old toolbox. Boss fight (assuming it's something that's actually difficult, and not just something that used to be difficult), it's pallys, hands down. If it's hard enough that you're seeking whatever advantage you can get, it's hard enough that a tank that can heal himself is to be preferred. And I say this having a 65, 100+ AA SK myself.
