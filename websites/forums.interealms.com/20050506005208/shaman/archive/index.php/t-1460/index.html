taraddar
Hole could definitely be done with one group but it would take you a while to get down there. Recommend 2 for speed.

As far as CoM goes one group could easily kill lord ghiosk for the 3 books. After that when you go back to do lord rak and nek after killing Kirn in the hole you'd need a bit more than 1 group.

For nek and rak you need

1 cleric to keep people alive

1 mage for a fire pet to kill rak

1 rogue to pick locks

1 tank to tank the reavers and bosses

1 feign puller to split the 5 reavers in raks room.

1 mana regen crowd control source (bard or enchanter)

1 shaman to do the epic =)

You could theoretically get it down to 1 group of 6 if you used the feign puller as the tank but that would complicate things a bit as far as pulling goes. Also you probably want to add a druid/wizzy so that the tanks can get out after your done. A second mage for a second fire pet wouldn't hurt either.

I'd say 2 groups to make it simple and safe.
