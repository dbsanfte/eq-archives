stormrazor
You may want to balance a bit more. Look for wis items that won't sacrifice too much AC/HP's.
At 34 and upper levels HP's will more important as your regen spell will improve. Chloroplast makes a difference. I hover around 134 without bearform and have 815 unbuffed AC. My HP's leave a little to be desired but I'm an Iksar and I fall short on the HP totem anyway. I'm rarely LoM and never OOM. I could proabably use more Wis as a surplus of mana never hurt anyone. But I won't get more wis if I have to sacrifice too much AC.
If you find that you are running out of mana very quickly, you may want to invest in some wis +mana items that give AC as well. That way you aren't sacrificing too much AC.

If you got a Magelo profile better upgrade ideas can be given.

Grizlor
Head, Arms, Boots. JB. Almost given away... might be able to get as low as 300pp apiece

Fingers.... AC/HP rings... 4/45 will run you 80pp apiece tops.

Shoulders - Tattered Mantle. 100pp probably. 12ac and 20hps = good. DR is nice in Sebilis when you get a bit higher ;)

Wrist - Not many choices... Sentry is nice because AC is decent, and theres 3wis... don't entirely neglect wis, just put it on the back burner :)

Legs - Try to quest your totemic. Otherwise, I'd go with Ry'Gorr legs.

Face - Crystal Spider eyes. These Rot often in velks because they are so common. ask a higher level friend to pick you up one.

Weapon - Try to get a Jade Chokai Prod or something like it. Spine chill spear / Slime coated harpoon are good too.

Shield... Definitely Clay Guardian... sort of a poor man's SBS, but it gets the job done. 25 ad and a hefty 50 hps... with the added plus that it's maybe 500pp tops :)

I know getting the cash for this stuff is going to suck, but if you can replace a piece every now and then, it all adds up.
