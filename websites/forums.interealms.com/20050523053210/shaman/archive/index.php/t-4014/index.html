Hail,

Putting this in General forum since I'm covering multiple topics, but most of it is alchemy-related.

I sat in the front row for the first session Tradeskills discussion with Dan Enright. My notes were horrible, but I'm writing this as soon as I got home so hopefully the hangover isn't affecting my memory too badly. :)

The session was taped by someone (Caster's Realm or Allakhazam's, can't remember which), so a more detailed list of what was said should be posted with greater accuracy soon.

Shaman-related highlights:

Discussed removing negative attributes to Alchemy potions.
Dan understood that this detractor isn't really necessary with the EQ of today. He said that instead of re-writing the current potion stats, it is more likely to write code for NEW stat potions w/o negative affects. No ETA or priority given for Alchemy re-vamp.

Problems with alchemy are well known, and Dan said the re-vamp would likely lower both the component purchase price AND increase the vendor sell-back price to reduce the expense associated with alchemy. Once again, no ETA on this.

Invisiblility potions for Shaman is not going to happen according to Dan. He tried, but the idea was shot down in a meeting. Furthermore, he said he tried to let the store-bought potions be made combineable in a potion bag, and that was also shot down. :( Verant doesn't want a 10 charge invis potion IG.

Making a Geerlok for Alchemy (& poison-making) was discussed, so this may happen eventually.

Many other classes/cultures discussed Cultural armour deficiencies and problems. Dan took notes on most everyone's concerns. I didn't get a chance to address the new Cultural armour stats, but I did tear out Sister Li's post on page 2 of the Fain Faire thread and set it on his notebook before slipping out.

Strength was confirmed as a tradeskill attribute for smithing, but it only affects chance of skill-up (NOT success) if it is higher than int/wis. The recipe being attempted (if not trivial) has no bearing on skill-up rate according to Dan. The trivial check is different than the skill-up check.

I spoke with Ryan Parker (on the dev team) for awhile, and he mentioned reading our Fan Faire thread, so most everything posted before Fan Faire started was being read by at least some people over at Verant.

Oh, Dan Enright did mention (slip?) that the artwork for the new epics(!) for PoP has been done, but that the work behind them isn't.

The tradeskill cap (for all) has been lifted in the code already, but hasn't been changed yet IG, though it may upon PoP release.

I spent a half-hour at our class discussion table & met some nice Shaman from other servers. Thanks for the advice. :)

Tanuden, you deserted us before things really got going, but it was nice drinking and chatting with you.

There was a very nice female Shaman dressed up with a cool home-made epic (nicely done), the blue diamond facial tattoo, a kilt, studded leather hauberk, gloves, boots, leather bags, and a stuffed dogdog. Pretty neat. (Sorry I forgot name/server, but was in "Illuminati" or something like that guild. :p)

LOTS more was discussed, but my brain's drained right now & I need sleep. If anyone has questions, I'd be happy to try and answer them. :D

Take care,
