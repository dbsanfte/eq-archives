Scalia
Anytime a melee charactor looks at any weapon will show a damage bonus. Wont when we have on in our hands though when we look at it. But will to a warrior , monk etc.

That's right. The displayed damage bonus depends on three things and three things only:

(a) whether the class of the character looking at the link gets a primary hand damage bonus at level 28 (all pure melee clases and hybrids get it, priests and INT casters don't);

(b) the character's level;

(c) for two handers only, the weapon's delay - see the damage bonus table at Lucy.

The displayed damage bonus does not depend on the type of weapon (slashing, piercing, etc) or whether the character can use it.
