My guild is spending a lot of time farming pieces for the VT key. I've now run to the Frame camp in Akheva about 4-5 times for various people, and there's one trap on the way that we just can't seem to avoid.

If you run straight in to the second big hall, the shard camp is on your left, and IV's room is over to the right. Exiting the big hall toward IV's room we always trigger a trap with robs, centi, and mind worms all at once.

So my question is, if you don't have a bard/rogue who can disable the trap, is there any alternative to just fighting the mobs? Like a spot you can run where it doesn't trigger?

One strategy that occurs to me is to have everyone wait at the shard camp, then have one person go trigger the trap and run to zone. Everyone else scoot by the trap site. Then either COH the trainer to the group (and have him camp to clear aggro), let the trainer use FD to come back, or just wave bye-bye to the trainer if he's not coming with you to the frame camp.

Of course, if there's a secret way past the trap, that would be nice, too.

There are a couple of other traps but we have found ways by them by just hugging the right walls at the right times.
