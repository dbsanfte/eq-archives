CelEQ
I never really cared about wisdom. I remember at one time in my career (in my mid 40's) I must have hit the (then) 200 wis cap. That was with stuff like gator legs and 6 wisdom rings.

But then i noticed I NEVER got full mana when grouped. So why would i need a larger mana pool if I only use the bottom 40-60% of it.

Since then I went for regen-HP/AC-Resists-wisdom.

When I got Torpor I dropped my fungi tunic, those extra 17 points of regen dont really matter with 300+ regen :) and put on the Breastplate of Vindication.

Right now my Stats are something like 1100AC, 2KHP, over 100 on all resists (130 on MR/CR) and.... 180 wisdom or so.

With Koadic's i hit 200+ wisdom so I guess I would have a nice amount of total mana with bear form. I dont have any 100 mana items tho, and my total +mana isn't really exciting (+300-400 max). And I dont care about it either.

The only times a large manapool would be needed is at the start of an uberfight (raid or WW dragon / CT solo). But even then my defense matters way more. (Right now slowing the Flurry drakes in NTOV get me killed alot so more max mana is useless.)

Also recently i have been wondering if resists really matter that much. I can buff up most of my resists up to 255, and I get hit as (un)frequently with 255 as I would be with 150-180. So lately I have been keeping my HP/AC gear on on raids, and my blue diamond stuff.. well its banked.
