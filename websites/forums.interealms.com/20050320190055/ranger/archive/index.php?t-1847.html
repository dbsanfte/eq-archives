The problem appears to be purely a server load issue. I have numerous friends from Bristlebane who moved to Stormhammer. Stormhammer is far less populated than other servers and my friends tell me that they almost never see lag issues or link deaths even on full up TOV raids and such.

One way to improve performance for customers is to lower the population caps on servers. However, servers and bandwidth cost money so spreading that cost across a smaller player base = higher costs.

So there is your answer. They can offer "premium" servers or tiered service servers with lower population caps so that performance is better.

As for client side graphics, what did you expect? As we get closer and closer to photorealism in online gaming graphics, it will take larger and larger mathematical resources to do the required computations. Thus client side hardware requirements will go up. And as polygon counts rise, so will memory resource requirements.
