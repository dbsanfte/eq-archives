This is why I love DKP systems so much, it sort of forces people to actually think what goals they have for their gear and to not make on the spot impulse buys that later may end up rotting in the bank. If someone foolishly buys stuff that they soon will want to replace, they most likely won't have the points to buy it because of their wasteful spending.
