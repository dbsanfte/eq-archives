Jaradis
That isn't exactly true Fudapuda. Any WORN items that add to mana regen do count towards the cap, FT or otherwise. Only items that add a Buff that increases mana regen will not count against the 15 cap. Example: Aten Necklace has a worn effect that is 8 mana regen and 8 hp regen. That 8 mana regen counts towards the 15 FT cap (and the 8 hp regen counts towards the 30 hp regen cap).

Items like Shadowed Storm Sphere that have a buff that increases mana regen, does not count against the cap.
