Thorzion
Jaundiced bone armor is usually pretty cheap across all servers. The Legs and chest being the only really expensive pieces (at least on Rodcet Nife). You can pick up arms, helm, and boots for less than 500 plat apiece usually, and they have solid AC, even though the lack on stats. The clicky effects dont kick in till about 45 though.

Ry'Gorr armour is another good choice for young shaman. Good AC, nice stats, and usually dirt cheap.

Does anyone even do the Totemic quests anymore? They were great quests when I was a growing shaman, and I really enjoyed meself when doign them. Lower AC than the other armors I mentioned, but a great way to learn what your shaman can do. I still have my full totemic set (minues helm) in the bank. I wear it to weddings and such)

See if you can find an arctic wyvren mask. should be pretty cheap and has nice wis bonus to it. Crystal spider eyes is also a good choice, but usually 5x more expensive than the wyvren.

Studded acrylia cape for your back. 10 wis on it. Cant really beat that. usually between 500 and 1K, depending on how many tailors on your server.

As far as starting points go, toss a couple into wis, but the majority into STA. STA=hp. and hp=mana after level 24 and canni. Wis items are really easy to get and incredibly cheap, as are str, dex, and agi items. STA is usually harder to come by, although there are a lot more choices than when I started my shaman.

Enjoy your shaman! Great class with lots of abilities. Dig through the boards using <search>. Lots of great info here..some of it hidden behind years of chatter....
