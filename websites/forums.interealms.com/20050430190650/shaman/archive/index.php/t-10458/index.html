Shabby
once you toggle /Targetgroupbuff on, then you can cast a GROUP spell on another group (one you are not in) by targeting a player in that group and casting...will land on everyone in that group.

Does not convert a single target spell into a group spell.

The ability to cast a group buff on another group saves valuable time/mana on raids.
