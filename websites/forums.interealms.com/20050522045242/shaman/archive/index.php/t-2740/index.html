I hear the 'non-cleric healing penalty' thing bantered about, what is that exactly?
Jinheim
Greater Heal (level 29 heal for us) heals 300 points of damage.

Druids and Shamans take a 10% penalty to healing spells. Thus, when we cast it, it does a 270 point heal. (270 is 90% of 300). Clerics get a 5% bonus. Thus, their superior heal does 315 (315 is 105% of 300).
