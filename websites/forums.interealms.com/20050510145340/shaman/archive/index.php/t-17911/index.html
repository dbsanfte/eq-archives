Nothing has happened to the spawn rate that I've heard. They've always been extremely streaky. Sometimes you can get 2-3 an hour and sometimes you won't see any for 5. I would say you probably just had some bad luck. At 61 you should really only need a 60+ melee partner to kill them so if some of the group get tired and leave don't give up if you still have a melee around.

On the other hand I think you may be better off farming some plat, leveling to 65 and forgetting your 60 spells if that is an option for you. The 65 spells are cheaper on my server anyway and your 60 spells won't really ever be used again once you get 65. Also, with the spawn rate and rarity of the 60 spells dropping from a GW you'd be lucky to get malo or torpor after 30-40 GW kills which would take quite a long while. Of course you could get extremely lucky and I certainly hope you do but don't count on it.
