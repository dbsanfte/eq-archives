Hail,

I'd agree the REIF is probably one of the best non-epic piercers that you are likely to find in the bazaar for purchase.

It also isn't that hard to camp yourself with some friends.

There are some other awesome piercers out there, but none that readily come to mind as being attainable with 2-3 groups. But my experience is limited and I stopped weapon shopping once I obtained the Spear of Fate.

You might want to look at some 1hb weapons. There are some pretty nice ones out there that you can get with your 3 group force or even buy in the bazaar.

I picked up a Pebblecrusher (10dmg/20dly procs stun, some bonus stats) from either Radir or Tawro. Your little raid force should be able to take them out easily, and your friends will want to help in order to roll on those nice stat bracers.

I'm still working up my 1hb skills. Hopefully you aren't in the same boat as me (200 piercing, embarassed to mention the others) and can look at 1hb as a viable option for you.

Good luck,
