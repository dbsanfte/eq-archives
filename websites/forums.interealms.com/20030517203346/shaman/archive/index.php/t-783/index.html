You really need an experienced hate puller for these raids due to the buggy pathing, especially if you're going to do the 2nd floor. Monks and SKs make for great PoH pullers.

You'll need a rogue scout to tell the puller where the mobs are and a CoH mage is helpful to CoH the rogue back when he gets in trouble and clear aggro. An SK and or Necro can be usefull to recover corpses lost in scouting missions, although likely a monk can FD pull the corpse back for a res, it just takes longer.
