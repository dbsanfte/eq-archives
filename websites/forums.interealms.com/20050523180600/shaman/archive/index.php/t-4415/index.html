I love those bears. :)

If you're hunting what we call Kodiak3 (the one right below the Velks entrance... and yes I know only 2 of the caves really have kodiaks in em, but it's still Kodiak3 :p) you can pull the mob to an area a little over to the right of the cave entrance. That will minimize, but not completely eliminate, adds. Very few bears path over there. You might get an add every, oh 20 minutes or so.

I duo'd those with my warrior friend up to around level 40. Great exp. :) I've gone back with all my alts so far. Have fun.
