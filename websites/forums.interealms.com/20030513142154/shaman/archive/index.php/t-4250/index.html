I'm doing this right now. Mine is almost 39. I am 52. Here is what I do:

Gorlas and the higher Gorlas (can't rememebr the name) in TD until 35 then she was high enough to group with me. This is a good out of the way place with uncontested spawns. We are bound on top of the firepot spires, so easy access too.

Grouped with me in OOT on Seafuries.
I go out and pull with Tugur's. Hasted puppy attacks, I drop an E-Bolt and she DOTs. She DS's me as I get aggro after the E-Bolt. I simply tank, canni and JBB it to death. She snares it when low and throws me a heal every now and then to keep from needing any downtime. With this strategy, I have zero downtime, even when I have to stop and rebuff myself, I go out and pull as soon as I am buffed.

I turned my exp to all AAXP so that she could catch up to me. I've gotten almost 2 points so far.
She gets 1% exp per kill, so 100 kills per level. It takes about 1 hour to get 20%, so 1 hour per yellow bubble of exp, 5 hours per level.
With gem drops and plat, I get 1.5-2k per level on her. Plus the Sea Foam sells for 20pp per in the Bazaar and I get about 40 per level.

All of this is with JBB and horse, no epic or fungi. I am thinking of getting an Iksar chestplate with all of the plat that I am getting. I have already used some of it to outfit her pretty well.

This may not be the best or most efficient plan, but it is working for me. She gets exp and I get plat.
