Well I have Staff of Eternal Flames, the beauty staff from pofire which procs 400 dd(-300 resist). I am going to say this, there is a MASSIVE difference between rabid bear mode and being hasted and have a haste item. This was my observation today, I thought I would go KC just for fun. I slaughtered mobs with rabid bear and staff in no time but restriction is that lasts only 10 mins(extended duration aa and items). Also I did much more dps than my pet, double hitting for 210 etc.

With no Rabid bear and hasted with swift like wind, buffed with str of diaku, focus, spirit of bihli, Grim aura(shruiken earring adds like 13 atk or something) and I was wearing a cocw, killing a mob then was very slow and!! I had more procs in non rabid bear mode than rabid bear.

As another note, I got parsed on a VT mob one on the first floor that silences. The person parsing only had melee dps on(so no account for procs) and I was parsed at 48dps which apparently was just just around the same lvl as lvl 65 pallies dps. If it had accounted for procs too, I have fury of magic 1(which is 800 dd crits), the dps would have been much higher(although was a mob that silenced).

In short, if you want to have a fun time meleeing use rabid bear, any mode you try to obtain that mode is nowhere near similar. From this I would like Rabid bear to be a innate ability, that would totally rock or have a much much reduced reuse time.

As to weapons that are obtainable by means of buying or so there are choices of Dathor Great Hammer(very slow but can hit for 300s sometimes), Hammer of Spirit calling I think. If you are VT able Mace of Dark thought is ok also, or Solro able there is a mob that drops a weapon with effect firestrike that is nice also.
