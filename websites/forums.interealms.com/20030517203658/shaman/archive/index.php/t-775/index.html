I could be misunderstanding you, but from the way you are describing your current armor and weapons, it sounds as though you are buying equipment from NPC vendors. If so, you are spending much, much more plat than you need to. What you want to do is buy hand-me-down equipment from higher-level players who have outgrown their items, or who have looted equipment from MOBs and are looking to sell it. You should be able to buy a full set of banded armor, which has better AC than ringmail, for anywhere from 40pp (someone nice making it for you 'at cost') to 90pp (the standard 1pp per AC cost for banded).

There was a thread here recently about low-level 1H Blunt items, so you might want to look that up for weapon suggestions.

If you don't want to buy from other players, really your only options at your level are to (a) make tailored studded/reinforced armor, which requires bear/cat pelts and some smithing ability (I actually made myself a full set of Studded Leather around Level 8), or (b) save up a couple hundred plat and become a master smith so you can construct your own banded.

I don't know of any doable quests at that level for armor, but it's possible there's something new on Luclin.

As the others mentioned, banded armor is cheap (at most, 1pp per ac -- standard price, so don't pay more than that). Also, it will last you well through your twenties if you don't come across anything better that you can afford. Concentrate primarily on getting your spells first, armor later. Once you're at a point where you are buying spells in advance, start concentrating on armor/weapon upgrades. It's hard getting started, if this is your first char, but it does get easier to get money as you get higher in level. In other words, concentrate on getting levels and your spells. The rest will take care of itself. :)
