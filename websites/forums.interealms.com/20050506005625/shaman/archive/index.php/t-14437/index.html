Can anyone tell me how t find these secret Lugald caves in Dulak Harbour? I'm puzzled and starting to wonder if I am looking in the right zone.
Justanote
Follow the left wall til you slip thru the fake section. It's on the right side of what looks like a wooden wall.
stormrazor
Once you zone into Dulak follow the wall on your left all the way around until you hit a wooden wall. There is a hidden entrance in the wood planks, walk right through and you'll come to the mouth of the caves.
STAY INVISED, there are scouts on the way there as well as scouts right at the mouth of the caves.
Shandrillar
When you zone in, follow the tunnel, hug the left wall (kill things as needed). You will soon come to a wooden wall, the secret entrence is along this wall (it is invis).
