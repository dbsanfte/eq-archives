Tsuyoi
I duoed a lot here. My favorite camp was the Deranged Underbulk camp. Go to the first T and make a right. Follow the tunnel around the bend and at the 2nd T make a left. The DU spawns at the end of the tunnel.

Once you get it down there are lots of spawns and they aren't that hard so XP is pretty constant. Not that far of a run from PoK either. Drops are pretty bad, though. There are 3 named that can spawn near this camp: Deranged Underbulk, a named mushroom who's name I forgot, and Oldencamp the Lost. Oldencamp drops 500pp so is a good kill.

Until you can handle 3-4 at a time don't go south from the first T. From my experience there are a few spots down that tunnel where you will get multiples.

Tsuyoi
There are several other places I camped there back in the day. No names for them so I'll just post by camp #:

Camp 1: At the first T make a left. Be prepared to fight 3-4 mobs at a time on the way down. At the 2nd T make a left. There will be an aclove there with a quest mob (unsolved quest). There is 1 wanderer that can come down this way, otherwise a safe place to setup camp. If you are fast enough the group of mushrooms that are to the right at the 2nd T has a named that drops a shammy no-drop neck if I remember right.

Camp 2: At the first T make a left. Be prepared to fight 3-4 mobs at a time on the way down. At the 2nd T make a right. Go to the next junction, and make a left. Go to the end of the corridor. A named spawns there. Don't remember the named or what he drops, but I did camp there a bit so loots were probably OK.

Camp 3: At the first T make a right. Follow the corridor around to the 2nd T (near the DU camp). Make a right. At the next T make a left. Follow the left wall until you end up in a dead end, I believe you will pass 1 tunnel that branches to your right and 1 that goes to the invis bridge. There is a named in this corner and this area drops the shard for VT access very often. The named drops a BL spell that sold pretty well at 10k a year ago.

Camp 4: If you are finding the areas before the bridge to be easy to handle you can cross the invis bridge and fight on the other side. Mobs there are higher level and have better loots. I don't have locations for any of the named, but what I did was after I crossed the bridge I made a right and headed to the end of the chasm. There I pulled the shrooms in the 1st tunnel. They often dropped gems and rarely a fluid that sold for 4k easy. Fluid was used for Seru bane weapons I believe.

At your level you should also consider Nurga, Droga, the Hole (have to gate out of the castle BTW), Chardok (if you are OK ruining this faction), Howling Stones (horrid for CRs), PoMischief (farm spell components from castle wall and castle), etc. Probably your best bet for cash is to go to PoM and farm the components for Remove Geater Curse. This spell will still get you 40-60kpp easy.
