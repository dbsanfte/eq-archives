Yeah, you can turn in your Rune Etched armor to one of the shaman guildmasters in Plane of Knowledge. In return, you get .25% of lvl 61 exp, or 1% of AAexp plus a random gem. So far I've heard the gem being a peridot, diamond, raw diamond, or blue diamond. Not worth it if you might use your RE sometime again, but much better than just destroying it.
