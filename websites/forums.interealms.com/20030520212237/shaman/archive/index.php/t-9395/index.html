Hail,

Piercers:
Spine Chill Spear is fairly cheap.
Slimey that you are using works just fine though.

If you can spend a little more (or get a deal), one of the best pre-epic piercers that is widely available is a Rune Etched Icewurm Fang.

1hb:
Journeyman's Walking stick is a nice situational weapon, that procs Tashani. Price should be around 500-700pp, varying by server. (Note, this weapon stinks for damage/delay. You want it for the Tash proc.)

For practicing your skills, seek out as low a delay weapon as possible. I don't think there are any really cheap high damage/low delay 1hb you could buy right now, but I've never been much of a blunt shopper.

Take care,
