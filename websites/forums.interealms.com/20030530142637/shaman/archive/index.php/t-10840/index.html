Barm McLir
A few years ago, there were only a couple sub forums on this board and Thantu was very resistant to adding more. I agreed with him. I look at this board as a pub where we all kick off our stinky boots to brag, debate, and share our medicine bag of tricks. I really miss all those all Nixx Darkpaw's adventures from before the purge.

Today, we've got too many forums and new moderators that, in my humble opinion, are too eager to click their extra buttons. I'd assume The Grey = Off Topic but I'm seeing shaman related threads tossed in there to rot.

A few weeks back I started a thread asking the shaman community to voice their support of monks, our famous duo partner. I found my thread trashcanned into The Grey -- some nameless mod decided that it was on par with the cancelling of Farscape.

Landragoran Heronwing started "Inflammatory Post" which I enjoyed very much. It was curative and fun -- relieving some of the tension over the nerfs and the silly drama. Was it a clever bit of social commentary on the shaman community? No, apparently it's off topic noise that belongs in the junk drawer.

I would like to suggest the Thantu and the mods take a hard look at the structure of the boards. You might prune/collaspe some of the underused areas, make the topics more defined, and clarify amongst yourselves what kinds of threads belong where, especially The Grey.

Sorry, just something that has been nagging at me for a while. This is still the best EQ class boards and I appreciate the work that goes into them immensely.

Regards,
Barm
