Grippa
This was patched a while back. to answer your question, yes you have to have new models enabled for whatever race you are, and horse models turned on.

I suspect it's a bug that it even lets you summon the drogmor - if you try with a horse you get an error message saying you need to turn the models on.

IMHO this is one of the few bad changes made to EQ recently - gameplay shouldn't be dictated by PC specs - they should have made it so that horses with no models behaved the same as with models on.

I find I dont use my horse much these days anyway - it really encourages the "PoP style" of play - ie sit in one spot for 4 hours while the puller brings constant mobs one at a time. Which is very efficient for xp but not as much fun as a good dungeon :)
