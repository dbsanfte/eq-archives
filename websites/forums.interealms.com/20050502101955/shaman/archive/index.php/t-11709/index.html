Go 50/50 on your XP till you get run3. If you dont you WILL wish you had latter. Consider it an indoor sow, helps with pulling and out running umm.... mispulls we shal call them :P. And liveing to make it to the zone SAVES you XP in the long run. Even a 96% res loses you 4% XP each time not to mention the time you spend Meding and buffing agine that could have been spent getting XP.

Now has for doing your AA's after you get can5. The first thing I would get would be ND3 more HP's helps you no matter what. Next the one thing all shm have in commen is that we buff and debuff. SCM will save you 30% mana on all your alteration spell's. QB3 will take buff casting time down by 50% but there are items that will do that to. Now from here you need to look at how YOU personaly play no 2 people are the same. Look at what skill's will best fit the way you play and that will give you the best advantage. Exsample someone who solos and never raids, will have no use for MGB. Now with that said...

At 61 you will want to get SC, RC, VP. All 3 are usefull and SC is just plain fun at least to me :P Pet hold and Suspended Minion are also usefull in thire own rights. Now for the arch skills. I think you can break the arch skills down into 2 catagories. the first and most usefull being...
MC, ND, CA, SCM, CF. The second being. CS, SCF, HA, HG, SCR. What order you would want to them in would agine depend on how you personaly play. Your gear can also effect what AA's you want to get. MP3 is about that same has SCM2. But what happens when your gear changes and it will. Once you have the AA skill you have it 24/7 no matter what. Say you have a ODS you maynot want to get SCM right off. But it still wont hurt to get SCM2 simply becuse what happens if you get some better stats sholders that dont have MP3 on them.

The reason I bring up tailoring your AA's spent to the way you play. Is that with 136 AA's spent atm I have a few put in places I wish they were not. I tried to do what others had done with thire AA's. Thats why I say plan ahead, look at the skills and say "ok which will do ME the most good next?" Max it then move on to the next :) Almost all of the skills are good and usefull, I just wish we had a few more fun ones besides VP and SC. One of my favrot things to do is to face the oppist direction that the tank is standing, VP the mob, then watch the tank look around for it:P The kock back from VP is like a stun spell it will throw the mob in the direction that you are faceing. So if you are face to face with the tank and VP, it will knok the mob behind the tank and root it there:P.
