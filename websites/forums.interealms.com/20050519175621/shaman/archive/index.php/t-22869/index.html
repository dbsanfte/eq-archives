I think, in essence that your statement of "get in a guild that can help you get the gear you need" is rather simplistic, and not entirely thought out. Getting into a guild is not that easy these days. On your server, guilds may actively open their arms to undergeared peeps and take them on whirlwind shopping sprees in the Elementals and Tier 2 GoD zones, but on Karana, you have to have a damn near full set of flags, have good gear and good AA before most raid guilds will touch you with a 10 meter cattle prod.
There are people that have those things that will and do apply, they go and earn DKP, raid points, etc for thier drops and earn upgrades as they can, but they dont get tons of upgrades very fast.
Then you have people that play at odd hours for what would be optimal for their time zone, such as me. I work 8pm EST till 8am EST as a ER RN, I work 4 days a week. On my days off I spend my time with my lovely wife and child. When do I play? 9am-1ishpm EST, sometimes later. On Karana I essentially have 3 choices guild wise, any number of French/Euro guilds, while they are nice and I do know of 1-2 that would likely take me, I really dont fit in. My French is about as weak as it can be and that would make it unfair for the guilds to basically give me an interpreter to lead me around. My other options are the Asian guilds (again Language barrier). There are 1-2 other options that are raid capable, but I dont have the flag requirements. So I stay in a family guild that casually raids, have as much fun as I can when I can, but I do want more, but LDON is my "end game" for now, and there are a lot more like me than most high end raiders want to admit.
Then there are the people that simply dont want to raid, but do want to go do new things. They want to bring groups of friends and give Vxed. Sewers, etc a try, but they cant as they simply dont have either the firepower or the beefy tank or the crazy charmin chanter.
Saying "go get in a guild" is sorta like saying go get a job. The statement is simple, the underlying meaning and process is very complex.
