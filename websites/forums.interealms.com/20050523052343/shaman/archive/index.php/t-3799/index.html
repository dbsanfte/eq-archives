Hail,

If you do a search in this and the quest forum, you'll see many many threads discussing this encounter.

If you are the highest person in your guild at 51, you are going to need outside help to get past Kirn in my opinion. Zerging through the Hole with a bunch of 40's people isn't something I'd recommend. :\

At 51, you could hang out at the Hole entrance, and possibly beg an invite into a 2-3group force of 50's that are intending to go in deep. Another guild seeking the enchanter epic mob "The Ghost of Kindle" would be your best bet, as he is right next door to Kirn. A more powerful guild looking for Jaeill (sp?) the Wretched for a ranger epic turn-in and kill is another good prospect.

My guild went to the Hole with about 10 people if memory serves for my Kirn encounter, and we could have cut that by a bit and still be safe. It took us about 3 hours, with an hour wasted about halfway through figuring out how to advance fast enough w/o respawns coming up our tail. The respawn rate is what is going to get you at a few points, not the individual mobs themselves. We had a 60 paladin as the Main Tank though, w/ a 60 Enchanter for mezzes & KEI too.

Good Luck,
