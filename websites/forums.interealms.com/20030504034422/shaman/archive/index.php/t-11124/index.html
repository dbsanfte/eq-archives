Actually, our damage output is an issue with DoT's. I mentioned it in my other post, but will restate it here. Our dot's have damage that scales depending on the others in our group. What I mean by this is that if you group with people that are using epics or worse (and that class of gear) then your DoT's will generally be just fine, and you will likely get full duration off of at least the first DoT. If you are grouping with people using NToV type gear (speaking of melee type stuff) then mobs die faster, which will mean less damage that your DoTs do because mob is dying faster already. Put yourself in groups with the best equipped people on the server, and you will see even less efficiency.
