cynwydd
i duo with a mage (my husband) a lot. as far as mage pet tanking goes, if you're not using your puppy, the earth pet is probaby the way to go. if you _are_ going to use your puppy, air is the best choice, as fire pets are _stupid_ and always seem to cast the wrong spell at the wrong time (dispell, anyone?), and water pets, though the dps can be nice, are rogues, and really are made to backstab and not to tank, and can die _very_ quickly.

admittedly, most of my experience with these comes from the higher level pets, since i didnt start playing until my husband was 57. mostly we used earth pets, and i didn't use my puppy, since his dps was just not worth the hassle. now i use my puppy most of the time, since the 61 airpet holds aggro _very_ well. (it can also take quite a beating compared to the 62 water pet)

my advice is this: if you need a good tank, stick with the earth pet, and hold your puppy back for adds and to keep the beastie occupied while the mage resumons if/when his dies. if you _really_ want to use your puppy, have the mage use an air pet. water pets are for use with a people-type tank, and fire pets are just scary.
