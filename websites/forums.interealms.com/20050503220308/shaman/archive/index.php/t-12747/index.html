Gardeltron
Blood of Saryrn: dps of about 53 for 7 ticks (42 seconds)
Breath of Ultor: dps of about 22 for 26 ticks (2.6 min)
Pet (depends on mob): dps of approx 35

So for short periods of time while you have mana, you could theoretically do 110 dps. This does not factor in mana regen, resists, and the variation among mobs in their ac, damage mitigation, etc.

Problems with this scenario are that the dog cannot tank effectively mobs that are much more than light blue or low dark blue at level 65. If the shaman tanks, then lots of mana will go into healing, and even with good regen, mana regen (FT stuff), mana recovery will be a problem.

Another source of dps for one minute (6 ticks) is spirit call. With SC 3 (5 pets), each doing approximately 1500 damage (will vary with mob), you could theoretically get an additional 125 dps.

The overall dps of a level 65 shaman is not sufficient to effectively solo in PoP Tier 2 and 3 zones as some classes can, so shaman soloing at 65 is probably not an effective way to gain experience.

Boinshar
As far as DPS goes, popping on BoS and then rain is probably the best way to go. However, given the poor resist rate on rains when dealing with certain mobs, BoS and nuke is a good alternative.

As far as soloing area goes, when I want some AA and only want to spend minimal effort--a state known as Braindead exp grind--I go to PoV to kill some golems.

Pull with malo, run to camp, root with ring (or PE for long duration). Turgur, BoU, BoS, Epic. Reapply DoTs until mob dies. Canni a little and burn Can5 once is all it takes for ~5 min per kill from pull to mob dropping dead, with FM at the end. This is with self buffs only. This usually nets 1 AA per 90 min given sufficient pulls, which is not bad for minimal effort spent.

If you want to be a little more proactive, there's little reason why you cannot kill a golem every 4 min and have no downtime.

PoV, HoH, and PoTactics are good for Braindead exp grinds. With VP and PE, breaking spawns take slightly more effort than killing.

If you feel like tanking a bit, PoS and BoT are viable soloing areas with just self buffs. Or, you can always bum a buff or two to make it effortless.
