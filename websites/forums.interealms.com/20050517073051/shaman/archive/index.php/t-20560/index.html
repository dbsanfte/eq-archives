Tyck
Wow. The /rewind command never seems to have gone live, so with this patch they make it happen automatically.

This means that if you hit an object you 'bounce' back along your path to the last waypoint the game saved for you.

What does this do?

1. If your last waypoint saved was before an evac, upon zoning back into the zone and hitting somebody in your way at the zone line, you will port to the point you evac'd from.

2. If you zone in and do not have a waypoint saved and hit somebody, you can end up under the world.

3. If you're fighting a mob and he pushes you into a wall, you'll bounce out of the wall (mostlikely with him behind you). Can you say "instant free backshot".

4. If you've pulled a mob into a corner so his Directional AoE or Rampage only hits you (cornering the mob) then if you move back just a hair too far and Touch the corner behind you... BAM! you are out of the corner, the mob turns around and WIPEOUT!

5. If you're running across a zone and hit a tree, you will Ping*Pong back and forth along your path.

6. If you're trying to 'scoot' along a narrow ledge you'll be bounced back and likely fall because your facing stays the same and your rate of movement stays the same. Try scooting along the ledge that leads into Freeport Sewers over the pool of drownded citizens.

7. If you run into another player or MoB, you will bounce. Now, think of the places in the game where you're walking along narrow bridges where there is a lot of crowding (hello DEEP!) or paths along lava (hello OS!).

Man... I get motion sickness now just running thru town every time I hit a door or corner of the building! :eek:
