Kaalisti_Painstorm
Go back to the old world. Solb & the Hole are a most excellent place for a shammie/monk duo. Plus as a bonus, they're not as camped any more these days, at least on my server.

Solb
LDC's & Imps, (especially with epic) are a nice steady xp flow. Without epic just take a little down-time =) You can even pick up an extra healer if you find the need. Keep acumen up, nab the guano harvester if you see him. If you have your JBB, the mobs here have very little resistance to cold.

The Hole
Get your monk some gate potions first for easy exit.
You can shrink yourself with potions to squeeze through the door if you need to. There's a couple of good places to sit and grind around the drop off. Most are blue/light blue to 55+.

The xp mod in these two zones is quite nice, but the Hole has a better modifier. Solb's comes from being a dungeon, with casters.
Snowfoot
Howling Stones is awesome to 60 and beyond for exp. Mobs have moderate hps (Harm touches though but no biggie). I did there from 58 to 60 with a wizard and usually 1 melee. Never crowded either.
