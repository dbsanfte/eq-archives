If you check the Epic FAQ sticky thread (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=7737) at the top of this forum, you'll see a link to What faction is needed at what points? (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=1523)

My post at the bottom of that thread lists the steps that give you faction. At the point before killing High Scale Kirn, you should have gotten 8 faction increases. Your log parser says 9 -- maybe you repeated one step?

Your log parser is assuming 1 point of faction per increase. However, the Shaman epic gives different amounts of faction for different steps: some steps give a little faction, some steps give a lot. So there is no easy way to tell exactly how much faction you have. All you can do is con a Truespirit mob to determine his general faction level, and make sure you've done all the steps in the right order.
