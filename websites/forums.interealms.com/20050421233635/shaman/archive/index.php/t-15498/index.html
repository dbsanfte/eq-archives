If anyone knows a "sweet spot" for a solo shammy to grind some good xp... I'd love to hear it. As per pp/hour.. I personally don't care about pp right now, I am ONLY concerned with getting to 65.

It's hard to get good xp these days solo, you are better off grouping in the planes. From about 51-54 I did PoN, then stayed and switched to AA. The xp flows there at those levels if you get in a good group with non stop pulling (just the spiders, trees and ravens). If I remember correctly, I was getting well over a gold an hour, think I even made a whole level in about 3 hours.

If you insist on soloing, check out Katta guards. You might be a little low, but worth a shot. There's a great thread a few posts down you can check out.

You said you are not interested in pp, but a pretty good solo spot where you can make xp and pp are the archers/scounds in echo caverns. There are 2 archers on a 25 min timer, but each drop 2 bows worth 23pp each, plus plat drops. The archers are a joke and hit like sissies, plus when they get under 20%, they stop attacking (static spawn). The scounds would prob be a bit tough, but doable, mem your disease dots, buff up you and pet to the max. Also, scounds aren't single pullable until 56 there, so just root one and kill the other, then when they respawn next time, you only have one at a time. I still go there myself once in a while, xp is not all that great now, but its easy, and it's a nice quiet spot.

You can also try Maiden's Eye, but stick to the non casters, such as the goranga lookouts and wanderers. Lot of trains here though (I always camped near the zone in case of adds), and they do roam close together, so it can be a bit tough to single pull. They are from around 52-55 in level or so, with a good amount of hp, so fights will take a while, but should be really nice xp.


EDIT: Just wanted to say, that if you indeed are only concerned about 65, and not about pp, then soloing is the wrong way to go. Group in the planes, no loot there, but amazing xp.
