Hmmm...

Unfortunatly I dont see any melee on you list of posibilities. Thats a shame because the meatheads are the ones that make the shaman a truly great class.

Of the options you have I really dont like any of them. My gut reaction is the druid, because you and pet could tank and do the shaman thing while the druid nukes, dots, snares, and heals. That wont work in the planes though because even with slow and cripple you are still not gonna be able to take that abuse for more than a few rounds.

Shaman/wizzy might be fun. Would suffer the same problem as the druid, but without any of the neat buffs, abilities and heals. Faster killing power at the price of utility.

Necro has posibilities. Again, no planes, but fear kiting with both pets and both sets of dots might be interesting. I dont fight with necros very much, so Im not sure how the dot stacking works with them. Although at 63 necros are using a lot different dots than the ones that shaman and necros share.

Cleric? if you are gonna have a cleric and a shaman you might as well get a warrior and make it a trio, cause theres not mucht that a shaman and cleric cahn do for each other except trade a few buffs. The cleric would really become dead weight after you got the HP/AC buffs from them.

2 shammies? Over kill, and the level 54 one wont be able to do much. why bother?

My advise? Find a SK or Pally and duo with them. Both need less healing from you as they both have ways to heal themselves. And your bufs will make them much much better, enabeling much better experience.
