Jaruden
Charmed (or maybe just 60+ charm) pets will take exp according to how much damage they do to a mob (not sure the %'s). Regular pets used to take half exp if they outdamaged the rest of the group (in this case, the shaman), but now I believe they only take 1% of total exp if the pet outdamages the owner. If the pet does 100% of the damage, I believe he takes a bit more. You might want to sic the pet then nuke once, just to be sure you're getting exp.
eci_shaman
Actually, unless they changed our pet... as long as you do 1 point of damage to the mob, you get full exp (not matter how much damage it does vs. how much you do).
Morel
Yes, just one point as far as summoned pets go. I've heard that you need to work a bit harder with the charmed pets, specifically dire charmed.
