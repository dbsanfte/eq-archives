Karshak_rn
Nadox is a good place to grind out aa if you are still learning the solo grind as most mobs are dark blue. Inner Acrylia is also good for the same reason. I like both because dog dog can pretty much tank here if you get into trouble and the zones tend to be kind of quiet. If you didn't by chance do Blood of Nadox you can solo it while getting some good experience. You can learn how to tackle adds, and in Inner Acrylia there are some nice named to play with. Neither have top quality xp but it depends if you are looking for the best xp, or the best fun.

I think I found these two places the most relaxing to solo, although I think PoValor has the best solo xp for root rotting that you will find at your level of aa. Check out a great post on soloing Crystalline Golems by Twoflower.
http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=19513

If you can handle the BoT crowd (Which I assume is the same on every server) try and hang in there to get canni V. it will greatly reduce your downtime, or uptime (if you are a glass is half full type of shaman).

Tsuyoi
Definitely recommend finishing up Bane and Anathema ASAP. Then depending on what you want:


Droga - Good for loot and decent XP. Built for shaman

Nurga - Same as Droga, but lower level and lower loot. Faster kills if you are interested in Chardok faction

ChardokB - Great for XP and OK loot. Best if you have non-KoS faction with Chardok, but I run down all the time without training. Secret is to jump into the slave pits and camp there to lose aggro, then invis and run in the rest of the way.

Charsis - Need the key and a terrible place to CR. But, rarely camped, has good loot and is easy once you know it. Get a group down here first and finish up your master key then come back to solo.

Veksar - Need Bane since higly disease/poison resistant. Seems to be better xp and loot than droga, but harder. Also good for Cabalis faction.

Siren's Grotto - Camp the haste belt off of fellspine. Or the sirens. Or the Ulthorks near WW. All good camps. EB needed here. OK XP and decent loot.

The Deep - Easy kills, bad loot, XP comes from mass numbers that you can kill if you are quick. Was easy and no-risk xp here for me at 65.

PoN - Can root rot a lot here. Consider the gargs past the PoNB entrance or doing the maze. To get into a maze start event but never hail the quest mob when inside.

PoV, PoI, HoH, IA, UP, etc. - Already mentioned or straightforward

Tsuyoi
Can't really comment on the % of an AA per kill since I duo everything. Even started bringing my GF's druid along 3-boxing so I get the leadership AAs. But in all the spots I mentioned I get from .7 to 1.5% of an AA per kill.

The thing you want to focus on is % of an AA per hour. Include the time it takes to get to the site and your camp spots. The planes are easy since they are so accessible. Other places I mentioned may take you awhile to get there. ChardokB, for instance, takes me over 30 minutes for the OTM since I run through FM every time to grab some more faction and to check on the angry goblin.

For just straight XP, PoV golems or PoTactics around the pigpen would be your best bet. Can solo 60% an hour there at 65 with Bane I believe. PoF looks to be about 1 AA an hour, although I'm not flagged for that yet.

The other places I mentioned I can get from 30 - 40% of an AA an hour for my duo. Personally I like the change of pace and the better loot that these places offer.
