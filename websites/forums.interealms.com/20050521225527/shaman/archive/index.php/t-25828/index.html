Brohg
Basics are more important, I think all agree. But once you have your hp, casting mastery, reinforcement, canni all done, this is a good one to get. I click it anytime they'll get to fight for most of the minute, either on one boss mob or on several smaller mobs. With a 12min reset, it's never really down when you can use it.

If (IF) (big if) you managed to get full damage out of it each time it popped, and clicked it whenever it did pop, you'd get like 18dps from it. 18dps ain't bad for us :) As a bonus, it's not actually 18 constant dps, but a burst of high damage when you ask for it. Being a minute duration damage, it stacks well with our main dot damage plan
Veril
Spirit Call is great.

It lets us do something we cannot do naturally - any AA that does that is a boost to our power.

It is mana free damage every 12 mins.

The pets interrupt spellcasters (gate/ch) like mad.

One of the most useful way you can use it is on summoning mobs. Root the mob, bring the pack in. While the mob is rooted it will fight the pack and not summon you. This provides a time for you to recover, mem spells etc. Very very useful indeed.
