Gloriana
Probably, your guild isn't going to kill a lot of the trash stuff if they are serious about clearing it.

Why? Because some spawn that see invis and some spawn that don't see invis.

Your raid leaders will probably kill just enough so that the mobs you have to pass don't see invis. They probably won't kill any that don't see invis due to the risk of a see invis mob spawning.

Bring your patience, VP is a game of waiting. Hosh and Silverwing cross each other's roaming paths so the initial pull is a waiting game for Hosh to be deep in his lair before the pull.

Be prepared for odd mob warping, mobs that gate away (Hosh is bad for this, kill him in his lair if at all possible).

The drops in VP aren't too bad for shaman. Hammer of the Dragonborn, Spear of Constriction. Phara Dar loot is especially nice. You can see the drops here: http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?zone=93

Some of the fights are really fun and require tactics that we don't use anywhere else "wurm herding" heh! Make sure you get all the melees to bind in one place for easy CR!
