Yes, reports are that rare, although they can be streaky.

2) Are the reports all equally rare, or is one rarer than another?

From my experience, reports from each house are equally rare.

3) Can i use one charge of the key and drag corpses through the locked door assuming i ever get all 4 reports - and if so, if this necro summons or using group member to /as /corpse?

Arx key has unlimited charges. You cannot drag corpses to the arx since the effect is a teleport of a single group into the arx.

4) Do reports drop more commonly inside the arx itself (someone told me this, just trying to find out if it's true)?

Mobs in the arx drop only shoulder reports, but drop them fairly often.

5) Assuming both a minimally baned and largely baned melee force, how long will the fight most likely take?

Never been with a minimally baned force. Encounter with Seru lasts about 15 mins or so for our guild.

Seru's loot is comparable to stuff that drops from Emperor and VT. He also drops ancient spells. However, clearing the houses to get reports is tedious, as is making bane weapons. Doing minibosses to get shards can also be tedious, although they drop some decent stuff, including a silk bridle. Our guild focussed on prepping for Seru for several months, and the payoff was worth it. It tested our commitment to each other and our will to succeed. Both the loot and experiences we had prepared us for greater things. Personally, completing the seru earring quest was one of the highllights of my long eq career, second only to getting my epic. It taught me what a great guild I was privledged to be a member of.
