I did a couple of LDoN groups yesterday at level 20. I know it's hard to find LDoN groups at that level, but the recent change to a minimum group size of 3 (down from 4) makes that a bit easier. My group (20 pally, 20 rogue, 20 shammy) beat 2 collect missions in MMC handily. I think we had at least 30 minutes left each time. The loot was good too - 28 to 56pp per adventure and a couple of augs dropped (+2 strength, +1 dex). We got 1 AP per adventure.

I got about a half a level of xp per adventure. Paludal would be WAY faster xp wise, but not nearly as much fun.
