First thing you need to do is disconnect your computer from the internet. Now run an antivirus scan(using an up to date signature) on your computer. Odds are you have a keystroke logger installed on your system.

It sounds like someone ganked your login and password and used it to change your secret question. You should eventually be able to recover the account and hopefully with all or most of your gear.

Edit: One thing I forgot to add. Whether or not you find anything I would change any passwords for any accounts/games/etc. that you have used on that computer.

Good luck and remember to keep your antivirus signatures up to date and run a scan periodically. Passwords just don't change on their own. :)
