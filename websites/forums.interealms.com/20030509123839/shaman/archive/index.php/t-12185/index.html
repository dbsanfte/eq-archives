Radag
I had 63 aa at 59 and it was very nice. When I got to 60 with torpor I was perhaps only 30 or 40% stronger not 100% LoL.

Core shaman AA stratagies mostly revolve around our level 60 torpor spell.

Torpos is a heal over time spell that givies you 1200 hp for 200 mana. A shaman can convert this back into mana using the various canabilzation spells for about at about 2 hp for 1 mana so you end up net 500 mana and this is the core of a high level shaman.

run 3 (3 points)
regen 3 (3 points, 6 total, Baron)

mental clarity 3 (12 points, 18 points total, Veneralbe title)

Canibalization (Can V) ( 5 points, 23 total)
This ability takes a huge chunk of HP and convets it to mana all at once every 3 mins. With torpor and the Can IV spell and Can V you can make great chunks of mana.

Other great skills to pick up...

Spell Casting Reinforement 3 (SCR3) and SCR mastery (SCRM). This extends the length of buffs and when compleated torpor will last 50% longer giving you a self heal spell that will heal 1800 hp for only 200 mana.

Quick Buff (QB1, QB2, QB3) This allows you to cast buff spells faster including torpor. Torpor is normaly a 6 sec spell but with QB2 and a spell hast item or QB3 you can get torpor down to about 3 sedonds wich makes it a lot harder to interupt.

Naturela Durabilty (ND3) This gives you more HP and makes it safer to use Can V and thus makes torpor more effective.

These are all pre PoP skills but post pop has some very nice skills also. I would just say that most shaman choose the core aa skills to optimize for the use of torpor and mana recovery. That takes about 70 or 80 aaxp depending on which kind of focus items you might have. Lots of shaman also pick up some PoP skills because the are cheep / fun / effective, but I would think that most shaman spend the bulk of thier AA points for torpor.

Hope this helps and sorry for the garbage spelling.

For shaman its all about canni v. Its power is unreal. You must have mental clarity 3 to get canni v.

Run 3 is the single best aa you will ever get (even better than canni v) as it will allow you to outrun mobs indoors. If you are in seb, you understand this already :) With a couple points in run I could make it from the chef to the exit alive and alone... there will be times when you have to run and cant just gate.

I disagree with regen 3. If you have torpor, you wont need it. Until you do regen is very important. As I had torpor before I finished run 3 (yah, I had 1 aa at 60) I spent my other 3 general aa points on metabolism 3 (less food/water for my horse to eat).

Once you have canni v, its all about your playstyle. I am a raiding shaman, so i went for Mass Group Buff. If you dont raid, skip Mass Group Buff.

I strongly recommend Radiant Cure, as it saves a spell gem, and works on curses and roots and snares as well as disease/poison.

Virulent Paralysis is highly recomended as having a good root with interrupt that doesnt take a spell gem can be handy.

Spirit Call is fun, and an easy way to add damage on demand.

I am working on Spell Casting Reinforcement Mastery now for longer buff duration (again, not as useful to a non-raid shaman).

Spell Casting Mastery will save mana on every spell you cast, and is a good choice for any caster.

Beyond this.. I just dont know yet.
