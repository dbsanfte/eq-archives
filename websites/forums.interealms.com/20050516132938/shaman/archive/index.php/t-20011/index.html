Here's a bit of info on the lightstone quest.

There is a version of it for good guys and bad guys. I guess agnostics can do both?

But anyway, you hand in either regular or greater lightstones to the gypsies in North Karana if you are a goodie. Watch out for griffawns and griffons! Faster than SOW, some of 'em. This gives you faction with some of the good guys, like the citizens of freeport or something like that -- various freeport and karanas citizen and vendor type faction.

Bad guys like my iksar and my ogre hand in lightstones to the DE merchants in the desert in South Ro. For this you get factions with such people as Dark Bargainers(they're in Neriak, the DE town, for instance). It's a nice way to repair (some) DE faction if you screw it up killing DE's.

As I recall, you can get druids to take you to south ro and wizzies can do south ro and maybe north ro too. So it can be done pretty fast, which is nice, because the lightstones don't stack!

Now, talk to the NPC's in the two camps and you'll see they take either regular or greater lightstones. Hand in your lightstone and I forget what you get for the lesser, but for the greater you get a container that sells for the merchant right next to you for 9pp each. They are similarly priced at vendors, so unless you're really dirt poor, you can do this for quite a while without suffering even if you buy all your lightstones.

So you get experience and faction and cash for turning in the lightstones. Lightstones are very common on vendors in West Commonlands and similar areas wherever willowisps are.

If you want to kill them yourself, though, you'll need a magic weapon if you're using melee; regular ones don't work.
