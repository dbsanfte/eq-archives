Nytmare
) If a 40+ player comes into the zone, tell him "my corpse is stuck at the ogre camp, do you have time to help me drag it out?"

) Make a level 1 Ogre -- they might be non-KoS. Log in the shaman and /consent the name of your new Ogre. Log in your Ogre and move to the Ogre camp and target the corpse and drag it away.

) Run really fast through the Ogre camp, spamming your /corpse hotkey. If it's your own corpse, you don't even have to target it first.

) Cast a spell on the central Ogre, and train them away from the camp. Double back with them all trailing you, grab your corpse and run away like mad.
