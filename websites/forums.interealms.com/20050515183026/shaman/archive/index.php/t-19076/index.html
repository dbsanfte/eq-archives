Sharok
I didn't want to clutter the poll and general suggestion thread more, so i ask a question here.

At the moment it seems, that a relative large chunk of shamans (the polls are still running of course) is in favor of heal updates - OK.

What i see often tossed around is 'we want an update to Tnarg's Mending'?

Why that?

Yes, we maybe loosing distance to paladins regarding fast heals, but does it really is a big problem? With Q and Tnarg's i can cover most emergency situations quite well, that call for massive patch healing. But in my view the gap between druids and shaman in the 'big heal' department is much more of an issue.

Who cares if paladins even get a few 100 HP more with their fast heal, when druids big heal power exceeds shamans for over 2.000 HP per 10 secs? (Renewal 4.680 HP versus Kragg's 1.950 HP + 300 - 600 HP for an active Q)

So my question is do we ask for a 'fast heal or big heal'? An update for Tnarg's OR for Kragg's Mending?

If we indeed ask for a look into fast heals, we can certainly forget about any main healer role comparable to druids in the future. There would be (for any content more challenging than LDoN normal adventures) TWO main healers left, clerics and druids.

I am not necessarily opposed to ditching main healer role out of our class description, if we have enough other valid roles. We should just be aware, what consequences such developments could have (it's unlikely, that more expansions will come up with trivial LDoN mob types, that even a shaman can heal).

Thoughts?
