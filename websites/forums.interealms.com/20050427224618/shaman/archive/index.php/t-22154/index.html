natimbi has a soloable camp with +20 hp type 7 non-lore augs
virtually all god zones have nice items dropping rarely from trash mobs, a'la sebilis
a group of non raid-geared players could make it as far as kod'taz with a bit of skill; the tank would probably need VT or the best LDoN / questable stuff though.
the sewers are easy and even soloable if you are careful; spells and nice augments and 90-ish hp/mana loot can drop there.
vxed/tipt are fairly easy with nice risk vs. reward for augments, spells, 110-ish hp/mana armor.
qinimi, barindu and riwwi are soloable as well, with nice trash drops.
ferubi might be soloable now, but i much prefer single group dungeon grinds here; rewards are nice and include three or four different lore augs , either 25 hp/mana type 7 or 10-15 ac type 7.
kod'taz is mostly single group content, with nameds dropping really nice augs and spells. off of kod'taz are the ikkinz trials, which are fairly difficult and would not be feasible for a group without elemental+ gear.

keep an eye out for any no-drop stuff you see in gates content, look up the breakdown in communication quest and roll for any BiC drops you see. buy a silvery mask and identify stuff, most gates items have some interesting if not entirely pertinent item lore.

hm, VP3 is an incredibly handy and generally underrated AA under any circumstances, but it can be a lifesaver as well as a good solo tool in many gates zones. get it. otherwise have fun, die a lot, make friends with some rogues and necros, etc. hope this helps or whatever

I've been playing in Qinimi and Riwwi lately killing nocs for the BiC quest. I actually had 5 chalks drop for me last night - too bad no one could come and get the un-used ones

I would make my initial camp near the Barindu zone of Qinimi. I would pull the nocs with a DR debuffer and VP them. I never had adds when I pulled them, and I was able to rot them in under two minutes - do not have BA IV or Crit Aff AAs. Just Bane, BoS, Breath, and Epic. For giggles, I would spirit call them - they rampage, so be careful. Kinda fun tho :P

There are two nocs at the bottom of the stairs. In the next room, there are two more. Then, you have the large room in which 4 nocs spawn. Down the stairs you have 2 nocs. Also, there is a roamer noc. You can move around safely with invis. I would be careful tho because you could have people training to Barindu if you camp here.

In two hours camping there, I got 5 chalks and 1 key for the BiC quest. Also, I got a few tongues and some vendor loot. One of the nocs droped a Blue Diamond.

Some Notes:

1. I had VP 3
2. Nocs did not summon
3. They did not run on low health
4. They will break VP or resist without the DR debuff
5. They proc a poison dot I think that can be cured with rc3 ( do not know if lower ranks can ).

Next is Riwwi.

I went here for the dust, and on my second kill, the dust dropped for me off of a noc near the Qinimi zone in/out.

A guildie friend told me the best place to hunt in this zone is near Ferubi zi/zo. I tried it last night, and there were a lot of nocs. You can use the same tactics as I did in Qinimi.

Some Notes:

1. Again, VP 3
2. They did not summon
3. They did run, so you want to make sure you root them. I have ornate legs for the clicky root, and that seemed to hold on them log enough for my dots to kill it. Also, it seems to me that the aggro range on these nocs are small. I rooted a runner close to a mob, and it did not aggro. I do not know the exact distance, but I thought it was close :P
