You could try Neriak Commons guards near Foreign Quarter zone.

There is a spawn of 11 (near priest of discord) that is suitable for your levels and if you are really careful you can single pull most of them (they only aid each other if they can see you). I used a monk for this but you should be able to handle 2 fairly easily. Note: they are highly immune to magic, root wont work and Turgurs is largely a waste of time. They dont have that many HP though so just bash them. EB works reasonably well as does Ice Strike.

Just watch out for Draggon N'Retta...he is much harder. The zone is close if you run into problems. The guards are rich and drop about 15pp of gear on average.
