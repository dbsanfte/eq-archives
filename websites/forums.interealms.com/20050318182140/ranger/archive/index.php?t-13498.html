Here is what you have to realize about the castle in PoD.

It is an outdoor zone. Therefore LoS restrictions do not apply. Things will aggro right through the walls(mistmoore ring a bell?).

The guards right before Grummus(the unmezzable ones) will usually attack you if you even go near the stairwell. If Grummus happens to be in the room just below stairwell at that moment he will add. Best to have someone scout with an eye and make sure Grummus is not in the room. May as well just pull whatever comes too, as splitting is a pain for monks due to the casters. It's easier to offtank the guards than to have your monks die over and over.
