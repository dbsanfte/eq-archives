Hamenu
What they're looking to avoid is people who try to bypass the transfer rules, and smuggle the gear to the new server.

Here's an example of something people have historically done:

1) Give your droppable gear to a character on another account.

2) Create a character on your second account, on the new server.

3) Sign up for Legends, move both characters to Stormhammer.

4) Transfer gear between the characters, cancel legends and ask to send them to their "home" servers.

Meanwhile, back on your main account, your main character has just transferred to the new server, and gets to meet up with your alt, who just brought over some gear and platinum.

A "suddenly nekkid" high level character wanting a transfer is a pretty big clue to the CS folks that something like this might be occuring. This is why they won't transfer characters that look like they've been stripped just before the transfer.

PS. The return home from SH is manually reviewed by the GM's, and they don't allow this sort of thing anymore.
