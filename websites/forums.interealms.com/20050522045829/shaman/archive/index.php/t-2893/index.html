Gloriana
You might not have enough money for all this right away.

face - enshrouded veil - 10 FR
shoulders - tattered mantle - 10 DR
neck - ivory collar from Chardok - 10 PR/DR
feet - black fur boots, tribal war boots, JB
hands - JB, CC gloves, coldain (Nice hitpoints on these)
chest - iksar regen bp
wrists - dragon hide armor from WW
legs - jarsath, no drop from KC, those quest legs from GD

Specifically resist gear:
head - froglock crown +20 FR/CR
shield - crested MM shield +10 PR/DR/MR, SBS +10 FR/CR/MR
2 velium diamond rings +8 all
2 velium jacinth rings +14 MR
sabre tooth amulet +15 MR
2 blue diamond earrings
2 blue diamond bracers

Don't forget when planning resist gear, MR > all other resists. Even if you're resisting a dragon's fire spell, you're not going to be doing any healing or debuffing if you spend the whole fight running away.
Bigguss
First and foremost: Get an iksar regen BP. Regen > All.

Easy upgrades to get your AC up: JB arms, boots, gloves, tattered mantle (each 100-400 pp)

Sentry bracers are a nice mid-range wrist item with resists.

Glori's resist advice is good.

Ditch the cloak. :) Lots of good cloak choices if you're willing to spend a little (+10 wis acrylia cloak, +8 wis arctic wyvern, 20 ac / 25hp seahorse cloak, etc.)
