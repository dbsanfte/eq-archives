eqphreak
It works, but you have to MAX all weapon skills except H2H. Max = 200 skill for us so you have to get pierce, 1hblunt and 2hblunt all to 200 before it will have all the stats listed.

If you only have one skill to 200 it will have some stats, 2 weapon skills to 200 it will have slightly better stats, and finally when you get all 3 to 200 the focus effect will appear on the charm and work. You must re-equip the charm after you max your stats or it won't show the updated ones.

Shiba
Hmm for some reason that map isn't matching what I remember the lay out to be...Here are more specific directions (special thanks to Veremar on Bertox for these directions, following them is how I found it myself!).

From Nadox Zone-in:

1) Head down corridor and go through fake wall.
2) Go straight and open door. (static guard behind door)
3) Follow passage left and then follow it until you hit a "T" intersection. (wandering guard along this hall)
4) Take the "T" to the right, be careful, if you continue ahead straight to the wall you will fall down a trap into a water area below. So you need to take the left just before you get to the next "T" at the end of the hall, and that will wrap around and lead you to the other side.
5) I think if you follow this 2nd "T" to the right it will hit an elbow that turns to the left. As it does so, you will see an open doorway straight ahead, and one to the right.
6) Follow the doorway to the right and it will take you down and to the left.
7) you will enter a big room with exits on all sides and 4 static mobs in it. (also 1-2 wanderers pass through here) Take the left corridor. It will then turn to the right and empty into the room that the Seer spawns in.

Hope this helps, it definately helped me!
