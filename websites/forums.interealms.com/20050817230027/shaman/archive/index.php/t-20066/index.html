Landragoran Heronwing
VP3 needs maxing. That's one of the most useful activated AA's we get, I'd put it right behind Canni5. It has uses:
1) Root-parking CC, pushes the mob way back and out of melee range of the group.
2) combined with Malicious Decay, it can be great for having a reliable root on very MR mobs. I use it in PoF tables for a reliable root on those mobs, since you really don't wanna be taking melee dmg from them. VP3 lasts 2 min 6 sec, which is just enuf time for it to pop back up so u can reapply it.

If you want to be useful and an attractive person to have along on some raids and LDON's, get radiant cure 3 asap. It can be very helpful to your group to get rid of DOTs, slows, etc. Radiant cure 1 seems almost pointless, but once you get it up to radiant cure 3, it can really make a difference.

If you don't raid much, different story. But it's one of the things I would want in a shaman if I wanted to have a shaman handy, for many raids. Not spectacular, kind of like MGB -- you may hate getting it and spending all the points on it, but having it sets you apart from the average joe and makes you more useful than he is.
