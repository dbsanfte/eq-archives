The broodmother's regular spawn point is to the rear of the guardian wurm cave in GD. Basically, upon entering the caves from the Thurg side take the first left and then go left again. If you are above ground she can be conned thru the ground by the empty tent with fire to the left of wurm mountain if coming from Thurg.

The broodmother cons indifferent and is far enough out of the way to do the hand in and then gate to the mines/giant camp.

If you are doing the Coldain Military Wristguard quest you MUST wait until the Korf starts to run to kill him or he will NOT drop the bracer. I killed him early once and had to the frikkin quest over again. :/
