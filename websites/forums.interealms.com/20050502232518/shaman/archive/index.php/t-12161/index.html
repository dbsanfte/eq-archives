Get Run 2 and 3 then do nothing but 100% regular xp till at least 60. The 60 and stop for aa or 65 first debate has been done in other threads so I'll just comment on AA. First, imo Wis 1 and 2 is a waste, you don't need it.

After Run 3:

Meta 2(or 3) (get some nice stat food/drink, it will last ages)
Mental Clarity 1-3 (we don't need this but it is a requirement for Canni V)
Canni V (massive AA, better than anything)
Spell Casting Mastery 1-3 (This will greatly increase your abilities in casting, huge mana savings)
Natural Durability 1-3 (Good HP upgrade, about 250 hp based on your current gear)

After that its dependant on your play style. MGB is good if you are in a raid guild.
