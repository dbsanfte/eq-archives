Frekkels
Hail,

Also beware of runners, especially when fighting on raised areas or nearby other mobs just out of aggro range. Bind outside and I'd recommend just gating if you get 3 or more in camp at once if you are in the Inner Sanctum. Yes you CAN manage 3, but it isn't mana efficient and generally leaves you vulnerable afterwards. 3 mobs in the NE section on the other hand are pretty trivial to deal with. ;)

Hoodlums and Depredators are scattered over much of the Inner Sanctum section, as well as several other areas (which I'm sorry I can't recall off the top of my head). Get a clicky invis item if you can (Ring of Stealthy Travel, Tae Ew Ritualist Mask, etc) and you can easily search for them.

Just remember the non-named mobs can respawn as any of several types (kill mystic, hoodlum respawns) so you might be better off just mowing down mobs in a large arc from say King all the way down to Ice Master Cialin with maybe an occasional foray to nail Heaprit's PH & any of the other Rogue/SK named/PH's, because they will always be guaranteed to be the same class as their named.

The Quest drops from the trash mobs are as already mentioned EXCEEDINGLY rare. I camped the sashes for my spouse's enchanter. We were lucky in about 5 hours the first night to get ONE sash. Second day we spent about 8 hours and got none, but I bartered some of the named drops we had picked up w/ another Droga farmer (a necro) for one that he had picked up. Be sure to network with any other Droga farmers while you are hunting - some are only aware of the named drops and can be unfamiliar (or uncaring) about quest pieces. This can really help you out sometimes. :cool: My third night in Droga camping sashes for hours finally netted us our third one, and we were VERY lucky.

Since that time I have soloed in Droga many many times and I have only rarely obtained any of the other non-named quest drops. Friends who farm in the zone have similar tales to tell.

I highly recommend that you bring at least a full group with you, the more DPS the better. Just pull w/ Malos & Slow and you can also double as main healer if your friends are equipped alright. Soloing there for quest drops would be torture, though I have a Mage friend who did it for a few weeks before he got quest pieces for himself, his cleric friend, and his bard alt. He's a bit crazy I might add...
