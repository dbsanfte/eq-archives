Thunderwolfjr
I beg to differ, but as far as I have researched and experienced with multiple characters, there are only 2 caps, at levels 10 and 20. At levels 1-9 your weapon damage is capped at 10 hp, regardless of the actual damage it does. At levels 10-19 it is capped at 14 hp. Starting at level 20 there is no damage cap on the weapon. Yes, there are other caps, such as haste caps based on your level, but I do not believe there are any other caps on weapon damage.

Now, in case you are not aware, the weapon damage is bugged on weapons that have recommended levels. Right now you will hit for max damage regardless of your level, the recommended level is not working with weapon damage. The weapon damage caps at 10 and 20 still apply, but starting at 20 you will hit for full damage regardless of your level and the recommended level of the weapon.

Enjoy until they fix the bug.
