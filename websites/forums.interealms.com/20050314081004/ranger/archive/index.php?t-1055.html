I camped here at 27 and have ridden this camp until 31. At 27 I could take the whole camp one at a time working around clockwise. Each clear of the camp gave me a full yellow bubble of exp at 27 with a 5 min rest/med period between respawn of the first one I killed.

At 30 I was still getting 3 blue bubbles of exp for a clear of the camp. I went from 27 to 31 in 5 hours of play time. It's INSANE exp for a ranger. Make sure your snare lands because you will have a hard time taking two of these buggers before 30.

Also, the queen spawns every so often and is 28th level. She drops a nice int caster only cloak and can be soloed at 30 with the damage shield up.

Have fun !!
