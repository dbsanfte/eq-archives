Chutoi
They're rare all right. I have all three. Of the three, Breath of Trushar was the easiest to get. Consider:

Many nameds, especially in Kodtaz, have a chance to drop a geomancy stone. If one drops, anyone the group can roll on them. Each person only needs 1, since there is only 1 turn in spell. That means that, assuming 1 in 3 drop a geomancy stone, you just roll for it. Your chances of getting one go up as you spend more time in the zones since more and more people will have them and not need to roll. Kodtaz named, in particular, are pretty easy to get to. So, figure about a 33% chance of getting a spell drop if you get a named.

Now, consider the other 2 drops. They're off nameds that either have PHs, or require farming, etc. The nameds are often in trials, where you now have to kill 20 mobs to see if one even spawns. If you get one, they seem to have a spell no more often than 1 in 3, maybe less. If you get a spell, the spells are also off a loot list that includes a lot of other class spells. Figure 1 in 11. 1/3 * 1/11 = 1/33 = about a 3% chance of getting your spell IF you can get a named. Those aren't good odds.

The spells aren't bad. Daluda's helps some, but it's not going to solve all your healing needs by any stretch of the imagination. Breath of Trushar is good for topping off tanks inbetween pulls. Nihil . . . is nihil. not bad, but could be much better.

Good luck
