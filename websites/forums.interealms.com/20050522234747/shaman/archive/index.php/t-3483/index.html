Start saving every piece of bear, wolf, mammoth (from mammoth calves), and lion meat that you get...PUT THEM IN THE BANK.

When you start getting enough, start working on baking. Fish rolls til 122, then work on 10lb Halas pies! I am now a 154 in baking, am a level 20...but I made my first Halas pies when I was 17. I sell them for 200pp a stack on the Tunare Server.

The hard part is collecting the meats! While hunting, you'll acquire enough HQ pelts to finance your endeavors...and it's WORTH it for yourself as well!
