Kianor
For chance of skill ups, a tradeskill uses the highest of your int, wis, and in some cases another stat.

For smithing, strength does come into it. The highest of strength, wisdom and intelligence is used to base your chance of getting a skill up in.

For fletching and jewelcraft, it's dexterity.

I'd say if you mostly want to do smithing for now, work based on strength, since you can nearly max it with existing gear. If you want to do other tradeskills, plan to build up some wisdom gear, since most tradeskills have no third stat.

Remember also, KEI and bear form give wisdom bonuses, so you don't really need to get 255 from items. If you're paying 10pp or so for KEI each time you're doing a skilling session, that's probably more cost effective than buying items to bump your wisdom up by that same 25.

Side note, success/fail rate only works off your current skill, and has nothing to do with int, wis or anything else.

Kianor
There are lots of pet theories about tradeskills, but if you head to http://eqtraders.com they have the best advice I've seen.

Here's the FAQ on it: Stats and Tradeskills (http://mboards.eqtraders.com/phpBB2/viewtopic.php?t=31)

It's commonly accepted there, after plenty of testing that the following holds true:

- The highest of wis/int and in some cases a third stat is used to determine your likelihood of a skill up. The third stat is strength for smithing, dex for jewelcraft and fletching, and no other skill has a third stat. (Tailoring has been mentioned as a dex possible, but tests show it's not implemented that way yet.)

- *ONLY* your current skill determines your success probability, for all tradeskills.

So, brewing is dependant on wis or int only and dex doesn't help tailoring at all, though some people are superstitious and max wis and dex both. :)

If you can provide proof that dex effects tailoring success rates, head over to EQ trader's corner and post it there - I'm sure they'd love to see it. If you want speculation about it, and people debunking that particular theory, I suggest you go to their tailoring forum and do a search on "dex". :)
BuzWeaver
Q: What stats are used in what skills?
A: For ALL tradeskills, the greater of your Wisdom or Intelligence will determine your skill-up rate. In smithing, Strength also affects this rate, while Dexterity affects Fletching. NO stat changes your rate of success, as far as we can tell.
