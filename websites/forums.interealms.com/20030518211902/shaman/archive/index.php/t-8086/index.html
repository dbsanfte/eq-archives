Shabura Naarteban
Look for some good stuff to be coming out of the tier 2-ish zones in PoP. Circled Toe Bones are nice out of CoD - their price will depend a lot on how much people are in there on your server (I paid 5k, but my server norm is around 15k atm). Silver Hammer Earring and the superior Skull Charm are both excellent earrings, but are both imo inferior to the Lizard Bone Earring out of CT (although THAT runs upwards of 100k on most servers). Death Mask of the Elysians is an excellent choice (at less than your mask sells for), and Dreadfang's Mask (~15k) is even better - if you can actually FIND one. I prefer Barbed Dragonscale Boots (5-8k, depending on server) to the ones you use for the extra HP, but YMMV.

If you REALLY are wealthy ... White Dragonscale Boots are droppable, and I have seen sell (rarely) in the 250k-400k+ range. Mithril is excellent (especially Helm at 200k ish and Gauntlets around 100k) and wearable, since you are not an Iksar.

You might also consider some non-droppable gear well within the reach of a non-HoT guild. The Talisman of Benevelonce is nice, but you will likely have competition with guildmates for the item. If you are masochistic, the Chardok quest offers a Ring that is much superior to your DWB and is mostly un-arguably better than even Circled Toe Bones (much more HP), as well as Spirit Wracked Cords for the very patient. Less masochistic individuals might want to look into the Circlet of the Falinkan (4 kills, none of which are challenging, requires CoV Ally).

In any case, you are certainly not hurting for equipment... good luck as you continue to improve.
