Kiisekai
Here's the deal with the chant rocks. I spent so much time on those quests (and finally got my 8th /cheer) that I know them like the back of my hand... errr claw. A while back they revamped Nurga a bit. That's where the piece actually drops, Nurga (not Droga or FM). They took out the rockchanter spawns so no chant rocks were dropping for a while (they had to do this right when I needed the drop). Eventually they replaced them with stonechanters who now drop the chant rocks. On a side note, the drop will be called A Chant Rock and will be slate in color. They can also drop items with the same name but a different color (I.E. blue, etc.) The correct rock is tan and will look like a sharpening stone if you have ever done a little smithing. Happy hunting.
