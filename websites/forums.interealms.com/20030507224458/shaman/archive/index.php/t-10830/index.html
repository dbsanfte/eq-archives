not sure what the economy is like on your server, but the things Im about to list are dirt cheap now on my server, so most should be easily attainable once you get a little cash:

range: goranga idol, as low as 50pp
earrings: black sapphire electrum earrings, 250ish pp
rings: velium fire wedding rings, 500ish pp
head: metal linked crown, couple of hundred Im guessing
waist: spider fur, 100ish pp
shoulders: tattered mantle, 50ish pp
face: crystal spider eyes 700ish or enshrouded veil, even cheaper
shield: sarnak battle shield, couple of hundred maybe
neck: ivory imbued collar, couple hundred

all of the mentioned above should raise your hp by a little less than 400, increase your ac by about 52raw (~78 total), but will reduce your mana pool by about 250ish, all for around 3k, many of the items listed are groupable at your level as well.

as for what/where to farm at your level, not sure, but save and sell everything you get. 3k is pretty easily attainable, especially if you get lucky on a drop in a group that sells well so try to group in camps where nice things drop.

Cystal caverns - camp the geos and sell the gems to vendors. Make sure you get best prices. You can make up to about 1k an hour if you are lucky. Solo or take a partner.

If geos are camped, take the crystal spiders instead, and farm the silks. They sell to players for a reasonable amount.

As a bonus, you'll still be getting xp there at 49 I think.
