oh, Cali, matter of fact, those UO "pure black" dyes were actually a hack. UO never intended to use pure black, just mostly a dark grey, but by the time they decided to do anything about it, mostly everyone had a pure black dye tub, and it would have caused too much a headache to patch it all gone, so all they did was make it so you couldn't create any pure black dye tubs. So then the smart people never modified the color of the pure black dye tub, and buy another for any other color they wanted to color items with. Also, don't forget, even though UO was easy to dye, you could only dye generic cloth items freely, to dye armor required GM smithing, at the time of making the armor to get the desired color, even then you had about 3 colors for your full plate (dark grey, rose and some other one like gold). Also note that full magic plate was looted off mobs, and even then you couldn't modify the color, so full magic plate was always silver, and not colored.
