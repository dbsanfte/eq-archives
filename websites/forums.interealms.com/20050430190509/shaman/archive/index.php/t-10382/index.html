Ximix the Troll
You will start out KoS in both Cabillis and Paineel. Faction work can fix that though. You will probably have an easier time getting faction fixed in these two cities if you are a Cazic Thule worshipper.

Hunt in the Warrens to fix faction with Paineel. Kill most Kunark Goblins and Sarnaks to fix faction with Cabillis Troopers. Turn bone chips in to the guy in Field of Bone (lots of them) to fix faction with the rest of Cabillis.

Innoruuk worshippers will be more welcome in Neriak, and can do some nice uests there - primarily the snare necklace.

You can increase faction in SV by killing the Loda Kai in Shadeweavers. They're great XP up to ~15 on the front side and maybe 30 on the back side.

Paineel is super easy to faction and the quickest way to faction Cabilis is to do the explorer knives quest in LoIO. You are probably not KOS to bloodsabers below Queynos and can get around the entirety of Oggok, Neriak, OT, Thurg, SH, and SV.

The places you will get killed that might surprise you as a new CT troll are: Kael, Katta, Seru, FP, and HHP.

Just keep your distance from every mob and con it first. As a rule, everything wants you dead and you should have SoW/invis up always. Outside of quest completion, faction is meaningless for a gating class with the PoP expansion.
