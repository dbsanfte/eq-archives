Everyone probably has their own way of doing this; this is how I have my own arranged:

As a spell becomes obsolete I move it to the back pages of my spell book.

Pg 1-2: Heals and Regens (Cannibalize kept here also next to regen as these 2 spells share the same slot)

Pg5-6: Shields and Resists

Pg 9-10: Buffs (STR, DEX, AGI, HEALTH, FURY, etc)

Pg 13-14: Damage spells (Nukes and Dots)

Pg 17-18: Divinination spells and other utility spells

Pg 21-21: Divinination spells and other utility spells

Pg 24-25: Misc

To create a hotkey to these you would do the following:

R/click on one of the SOCIALS buttons and name it say HEALS; in line 1 put /book 1

R/click on one of the SOCIALS buttons and name it say SHIELDS; in line 1 put /book 5

Hope this helps.

I organize my spellbook based primarily on what I swap in and out the most often, and secondarily based on having lesser used spells more page flips away from where my book always opens.

So, my SPELLS hotkey is /book 3; that way I have 4 pages within a single flip, rather than the 2 I'd have if my book always opened on the first page. That "home base" page has the typical buffs I'll be using, and the spells that usually occupy my swap slot (my poison spell, and a lesser slow spell).

One flip backwards gets me to most of the spells I want to memorize after I die, or other spells I want to have in a hurry when needed, like gate, invisibility, and cure poison/disease.

One flip forwards from the home base gets me to my bread and butter combat spells--my most often used damage spells and debuffs.

Another flip forward is for more specialized combat spells--the extra poison dots I'll sometimes use, or voice of the berserker which I reserve for soloing.

Beyond that, it becomes a bit more haphazard; I try to keep "travel" spells in the same area, f'rinstance, but the lesser-used spells have organically found homes that are further away from home base the less often I find use for them.

I don't have extra hotkeys made, because it's just as easy for me to type /book 10 to get to resistance buffs as to hunt down a hotkey in an out-of-the-way place.

I hope that's of some use.
