Ribak
I spent a lot of time researching gear recently as I needed to sort out my own shamans' things. There are many good posts on this forum but nothing worked better than actually sitting down in the bazaar and comparing gear/prices/what people recommended to see what was actually worth buying. There are a couple of great pieces out there that I don't see mentioned very often and hence I managed to pick them up pretty cheaply. All in all I would suggest :

Belt : Chitinous belt (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=28728) - I managed to pick one of these up for 700pp which considering the stat increase over the turmoil skin belt was pretty good.

Legs : Brown chitin leggings (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=28729) - You lose 1 point of AC versus the Rygorr legs but you gain +25HP, +25 mana and +4 wisdom which is worthy trade off to my mind.

Hands : Shissar scaled gauntlets (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=6428) - Losing 2 points of AC this time for +40HP, +40 mana and +5 wisdom. Again a worthy tradeoff imo. Prior to these I was wearing crystal chitin gauntlets (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=25290) so in my own swap I took an 8 point hit on AC for the HP, mana and wisdom. I considered that in my mid 20s the stats would be more useful than the raw AC though as 40HP was 2-3 hits :)

Fingers : Shadel bandit ring (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=28708) - This is basically an upgraded version of the staple velium fire wedding ring. Same AC, 10 less HP for the sake of 55 more mana and Summoning haste III. I've seen them for sale on AB at around 1.5k-2k so they are somewhat more pricey but well worth it imo.

Face : Crystal spider eyes (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=25282) - As suggested above these are pretty much standard for this slot now going by other peoples magelos that I saw. You can't argue with +hp and +mana along with some wisdom.

Neck : Ivory imbued collar (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=3099) - These are insanely cheap at the moment and very good for the money. I've regularly seen multiple versions of them for sale at the same time and so the competition has been pushing the price down drastically as everyone tried to undercut each other. Got mine for 200pp I think.

Ear : Earring of twisted leaves (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=5813) or Earring of the solstice (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=28771) - The twisted leaves is a minor upgrade to the ones you have at the moment but it does have a right click scale of the wolf that works at level 50 so should be quite useful for you. Got mine for something like 750pp, not sure if that was too much although the price has gone back up to ~1500pp since then it seems. Earring of the solstice full multi-quest kits go for 10k (eek) so this is a bit pricey for one slot but extremely nice :)

Weapon : REIF (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=25059) - This would let you put the howling harpoon in the ranged slot as well as being a damn good weapon in its own right. Procs at level 35 I understand so it would be great for you prior to the epic.

I've yet to find decent upgrades for the head and arms that don't cost the earth unfortunately. Jaundiced bone seems to do the trick for helping the raw AC that you lose in other slots when looking for other stats so I'm content with that in mine for the time being. Perhaps other people will be able to suggest something there :)
