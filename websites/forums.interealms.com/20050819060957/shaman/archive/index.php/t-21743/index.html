Shiuna
I've been using this technique for single-pulling crowded rooms in Droga. I first cast lvl 61 pet, buff, equip, and suspend him. Then I cast lowest level pet and just buff with spirit of shrew. Then I Q him and send him in to attack. As soon as he engages I back him off. With casters sometimes I have to back him off 3-4 times until the mob follows. As soon as I have the mob safely away from his friends I re-engage the low-level pet and start recalling the suspended pet. If low-level pet is still alive with 1 second to go on recall, I poof him.

I just started doing this so I only have experience doing it in Droga. The key to success seems to be that the pulling pet must be green to the mob and the pet doesn't die during the pull.

I tried this in the rat room in Forgotten Halls; the pet died immediately and the whole room came after me. That's when I found out where your corpse is dumped :D . So it seems that if pet dies before mob moves to safe spot, aggro is transferred to you and it's just like a regular pull.
