For the Bp You need a banded tunic, star ruby, Watchman's Spectacles( Tim deep), Nectar of Isolation(Tim deep).

For the greaves. exile head(Loio), recruitment letter( Loio ), fire emerald, and banded leggins

For the arms youll need a frenzied gnawer tail (Kaesora)Froglok Treated Planks( Swamp of No hope ) banded sleeves, and a sapphire

For boots youll need a rhino hoof (Overthere), Spirit caller beads ( Warsliks or Loio), a fire emerald, and banded boots.

For the bracer you'll need banded bracer, ruby, Coercion Implant( Dalnir),Gooey Adhesive(Dalnir).

For the helm you'll need, banded helm, sapphire, Bargynn's Digger( Kurns basement) and Sythrax Building Plans( Feild of bone in an underwater cave filled with skeletons which are non KOS)

For the cloak youll need, banded cloak, star ruby, Giant Hammer( Warsliks) Worn Charbone ( Loio off a charbone skeleton not the lesser charbone)

And finnaly for the guantlets you will need, banded gauntlets, ruby, Venomous Parchment( Firona Vie or Swamp of no hope), Warlord Drawing Pen ( Loio )



Helm, gaunts, greaves, and BP start from shezlie furscale when you say Oxyn sent me

Bracer, boots, cloak, and vams are from Zazlan furscale when you say Oxyn sent me.

Well hope this info helped a lot :) GL on your SM armor quest fellow iksar shaman :)
