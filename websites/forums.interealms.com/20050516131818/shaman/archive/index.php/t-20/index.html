What is your AC in all that stuff 100-200 ? Druid gear on a Shaman equals = DEAD SHAMMY !

First off ditch the Paw and the Testament (Yikes!)......and the BS Tunic. Replace Tunic with Ikky BP (if you can swing it), or some JB Armor.
Get a good poker..Spine Chill Spear, Slimey Harpoon, Jade Prod, you may not have enough for Rune Etched Icewurm Fang (best poker till epic) Or use Howling Harpoon as main weapon and get Slimey or Medicine Totem for Range slot.
200 Wisdom at 25 is doing you no good, mana regeneration is much more important to a Shaman than a extremely large mana pool. We can regen our own mana with Cannibalize. Swap out some of that Wisdom gear for AC/HP gear. You should go more for AC/HP's than Wisdom.
Replace Testament with Charred Guardian Shield, Sarnak Battle Shield, or Clay Guardian Shield. Good Wis on Charred plus AC, killer AC on Clay, killer AC and resists on Sarnak Battle Shield.
Get some Platium Veluim Rings 6AC 65Hp ones.
Forest Loops would be even better for you than the earrings you have...Forest give +3 wis and AC.
From the looks of it you're a Barb so go with JB armor where you can (minus the bracer) can't use that till 45.

Either way ditch some of that Wis gear for AC/HP gear. Wear it when you need a larger mana pool..like when you're grouping.

But we're not druids, let's leave the tree hugging to them.
