Strats for Shaman PvP:

Key spells: Nullify Magic, Cancel Magic, Feral Avatar, Haste, Tnargs, Trushar, Spear of Torment, Torrent of Poison, Malos.

Key equipment: High dmg proc weapon (ex. Hammer of Hours, Sharkspine Club), 40%+ haste item, high resists and HP. Lots of instant clickies (Dain ring, AoN, Sow boots); alternately Symbol of the Planemasters.

Key AA: Rabid Bear, Spell Crit line, pets aa.

Most of your DPS will be in the form of melee + procs. If dmg shield, then dispell with both dispells asap. Use clickies as soon as dispelled. Keep haste, FA on self. Cast heals on self as needed. Determine when the druid tends to heal self, typically they try an heal themselves around 50%. Malos before they reach 50%. The second they hit 50% and try to cast heal, sic pets on them and chain rains or spear. Maybe try a VP to interrupt casting if ur lucky.

If they get heals thru all the pets and nuking, then return to melee + heal self. keep up melee + heal self for 12 minutes then repeat.

Alternatively you can get a Symbol of the Planemasters and rely on proccing stun, which is disease based. In addition to HoH or Sharkspine Club this is pretty tough to beat. Especially since it takes over 200dps to beat Breath of Trushar + occassional tnargs.

+Combat Effects helps a lot as well.
