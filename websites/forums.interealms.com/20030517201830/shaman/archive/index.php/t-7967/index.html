gandalfh
If you go through the waterfall and hug the left wall, there is a small incline in the large room before Zlandicar's room, nothing paths there. You need to make sure Zlandicar isn't up, because he will assist the rats and he has a pretty big aggro radius. He drops a ST key, so people tend to keep him pretty dead.

When I camped Vilefang though, I actually just hung out in the water, since I was alone and only wanted to kill one mob. If you hug the left wall halfway across the lake you can setup camp in the water and go AFK, the serpent won't aggro on you there.

If you hang out in the safe spot before Zlandicar you will have 3 to 4 rats to kill per spawn cycle, and there is a couple of named that pop there too. Not sure what exciting stuff they drop.
