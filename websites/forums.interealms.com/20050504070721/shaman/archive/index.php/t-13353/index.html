Paladin/Shaman duo in Tactics is really fun. No need to go begging for a rez if things go bad, and no need to go begging for buffs or what not that most other partners always want to get before they are ready to exp.

I've always preferred pallies as partner though. A rangers track ability CAN be a wonderful thing, and their DPS can be amazing, but I want somebody wearing plate armor as my partner. An SK's aggro abilities are really nice, but his special abilities (FD, HT, etc) aren't going to save me when things go bad - a pally's LoH, superfast heal, and stuns have saved me many many times and taken a bad situation into one that was easy to win.

ANY partner can be fun (I like duoing with a non-uber enchanter), but I feel a pally brings more to the table than the other normal partners.
