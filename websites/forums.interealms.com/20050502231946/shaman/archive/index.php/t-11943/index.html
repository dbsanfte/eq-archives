Binter
If you're looking for races with abilities that will last through the entire life of the character, then just about the only choice you have are Ogres and Vah Shir. ;)

Ogre immunity to stun does have its uses, though it is severely overhyped. Being bashed doesn't instantly interrupt your spell when you've got a channeling skill of 200+ unless it stuns, and at level 65, I rarely get stunned when I'm bashed.

Vah Shir, on the other hand, have 2 racial abilities which are used for your entire career: Safe Fall and Sneak.

Safe Fall may not seem like much, but when you're running with SoW, at least a Vah Shir doesn't take chintz damage every time he/she steps over a rock. It saves the aggravation of seeing the damage spam the window all the time.

But Sneak...ah, Sneak. Sneak ensures that a Vah Shir can buy and sell absolutely anywhere he goes. Even at 65, there are merchants/races that won't sell to me, but Sneak-o-Sell fixes that. And at lower level, Sneak actually helps when you're trying to get past mobs without aggro.

Think the ability to buy and sell anywhere isn't worth much? Then go talk to a Troll or Iksar.

Both of these racial abilities will be used your entire life. The usefulness of just about every other race's racial abilities fade over time, even regen.
