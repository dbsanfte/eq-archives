Now the only machine connected to the Internet is my spouse's, and our router is lying unused on a shelf for now. We've recently moved & the long 8' table that used to hold 3 "primarily for EQ" machines side by side is now in our garage holding up tools & some boxes.

I don't foresee myself returning for EQ2, WoW, or any other online MMORPG. They just eat up way too much time, even if it is a very economical form of entertainment. It was a good run, but I'm done. I'll be playing Morrowind, Halo2, & any # of other free online games I can quit (or save game) from at the drop of a hat without feelings of guilt.

I'll browse the boards here for another month or so, then I'll probably be done posting as well. I'm transferring IRL for some training for the first half of '05 at the beginning of the year (which won't let me cruise the Intenet from work anymore), and then I'll be off to Hawaii for another sea tour on one of the submarines based there in the summer. Submarine duty and MMORPG's don't blend well. :D

Thanks everyone here for putting up with me (too many to thank individually to name here). I'd like to hope I've made some tiny contributions to our community in my time here that maybe helped a few over the years. I definitely learned a TON of information and received loads of great advice because of these boards. :cool:
