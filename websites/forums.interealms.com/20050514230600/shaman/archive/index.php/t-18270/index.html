Jinheim
They are soloable, but they are in fact more dangerous than doomfires. The field mobs can hit you five times in one round (not including bash) versus only three from a doomfire, and are much harder to single pull. Also, there is no really large area where you can stand to solo them without adds. There is one spot on the zone wall where the cliff meets the castle, but there isn't much room. They give about 11% solo per kill, vs. 14% for a doomfire.

Also, in my experience, they are generally more resistant than doomfires are. However, with virulent paralysis, either type of mob is easily controlled and soloed. Doomfires are generally more rewarding though. The spider mobs can drop valuable obsidianwood sap, but they are also very resistant to poison.
