darkpaw
I'm pretty much an expert on hollowshade.

Prince does spawn outside, but if grimlings hold the cave there is nothing you can do about it until the zone is reset because the war script really doesn't work. If things are working properly, eventually one of the races (with help) will take over the zone and spawn a named in the Vah Shir fort to be killed.

As for the summoning mobs these are part of the war event. When a race is currently being attacked a group of rooted, summoning mobs will spawn outside the cave. If you kill them, then the attacking race will win. They all have names like grimling defender or something like that. If the group of defenders is up you don't want to mess with them by yourself.

As for farming the prince, wait until owl bears are up and kill as many as you can outside the cave. Make sure you loot perfects too, don't know about other servers but can still get 200pp for them in baz on Torv.

I found best way to kill owl bears at that level is to charm one of the higher level ones rather then using dog dog.

darkpaw
The war starts automatically sometime after server reset and continues until it eventually breaks.

The defenders only spawn after one of the attack messages like you mentioned. I believe the attacking race is listed first and the defending race is listed second.

If the script is still working properly you'll see an attack message every half hour or so.

If no assistance is lent, the attackers automatcially lose and the zone continues the way it was.

If someone kills the defenders the attackers walk right in ignoring all other mobs and take control.

The way the script is supposed to work is that when one race controls the zone, the vah shir for despawns and pops a named and guards of the controling race. The names drop some very nice loot for their level. They stay up and in control until someone defeats the named or the server resets.

Unfortuantly, most of the time when a race takes over the named never spawns and the zone stays stuck like that until the zone is reset.

As for owlbears hardly ever being up a lot of people help the grimlings take over on purpose because when the grimlings are in control of the cave it is the absolute best spot in the game to farm acrylia. With a lot of 1 minute spawn locations you can get a truck load of acrylia in an hour or so.
