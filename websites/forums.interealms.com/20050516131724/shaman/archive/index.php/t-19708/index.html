Ginslunger
Spent about 4 hours in a couple of GoD zones last night, primarily Qinimi and Riwwi. We had BRD, SHD, CLR, BST, NEC, MNK. The BRD, SK and CLR are all elemental equipped, I am partly VT Equipped and the NEC and MNK had no elemental or VT gear.

Here are some observations:

The mobs are FAST. Many of them moved at Bard speed.

The bard made it look easy, but I'm sure it would be difficult to get singles without a good puller. A good puller or good crowd control is important

You need a snarer, mobs run and run fast. If you don't have them snared or rooted, you going to get a lot of trains.

Trash mobs occasionally rampaged, flurried and enraged

Most were hitting in the 400 - 600 range max, but it was very inconsistent. I only had 5500 HP buffed and no defensive AAs and yet was able to tank when we got trained by 5 mobs and the SK went down. Wasn't easy and used a bunch of the cleric's mana, but we made it.

Exp was on a par with Valor or Storms or less. In 4 hours of hard killing, I got about 1.2 AA with 100% to AA

We got a couple of drops: A low end VT comparable 20AC Spell Haste 4 chest piece for casters/priest off a trash mob in Riwwi and a BoT towers comparable priest shoulders off a named in the arena in Riwwi.

I thought it was comparable to BoT main floor mobs, but tougher to pull single and more problems with running.

I didn't think the exp or rewards were great, but they weren't terrible either. I had a blast exploring new places and seeing new mobs.

Just my opinion, but it was worth the purchase to me.
