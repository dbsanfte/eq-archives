High Priest someone-or-other gives you the expedition on the east wall of Dranik's Scar. You enter through a cave that is right there.

Details:

- 2 named, one bat and one rat.
- The bat and rat have standard place holders, "a hideous rat" and a "tattered bat". They appear to always spawn in the same place.
- They spawn in rooms close to each other at the end of a rather long dungeon.
- The hideous rat is in a bricked room not yet marked on the map, but is on the way to the Bat Cave noted on the map anyway and since the PH is a different name you can't miss him.
- The tattered bat is in the Bat Cave shown on map.
- The expedition itself seemed to just be a standard exp zone (no "mission" style thing you can win) with the named as the sweetener.
- The experience was decent enough, I had leadership on and got about 11% of 66 in a little bit over 2 hours, other group members reported 12-13%. Our group was not particularly dps heavy though.
- Better still, the mobs were challenging for us but not overwhelming. Pulls were 2-3 at most generally, mobs hitting for to about 6-700? This placed it just under high diff LDoN in terms of mobs' power, but the easier pulls made it significantly easier.
- We discovered late in the expedition that the bats are indifferent! This makes skipping that content easy, and you can also invis past lots of stuff - Some murkies can see invi, not sure about rats, didn't see any who cuold.
- Tracking seems very limited in here. Could be the open nature of the dungeon or intentinoal.
- Not sure if the named is always in the same place, but we got a hint about the rat from a friend outside the expedition who had got it before and the room was described to us perfectly.

As there is no eqtoobox map yet for these expeditions I mapped out a route to the static named spawn with a simple line map and you can download it here:

http://www.comcen.com.au/~teebeez/maps.zip

Now the loot is NOT WoS quality, more like Nobles' level from what we saw, but it was stil reasonable (ranged item with 50ish (bit more?) hp / mana and a buncho stats dropped with a Lesser Muramite Rune from the named we got).

There are also a fair number of sellable / gem drops and mobs drop coin too. Overrall I thought the exp and loot (especially given the existence of rune drops!) was good reward for the difficulty of the dungeon. The named was dark blue and easily doable by our group, our tank had a bit under 8k hp buffed.

/end quote

For our group, this was completely enjoyable, we ended up with a couple of armor drops that were decent, The two named always dropped the same armor for us. And either one or the other would have a rune.

Runes were only level 66/67, but terrific way to farm.

Now the new rumor is that you have to kill 100 mobs before the nameds spawn. Cuold be wrong. I will tell you that once we found the ph's and started a rotation between the two, we had plenty of nameds to keep us entertained.

Good luck guys. When WoS and Causeway get you down, grab a group and head over.

Blazz
OK, heres my report on this instanced dungeon.

Got a Paladin(68) who 2-boxes a Cleric(66), his RL Wife who plays a Warrior(67), and me(67 shaman), and off we go. Got the Murk instance first try, so I am not sure if there are other choices you have to "pass" on, and in we went.

First of all, rats and bats are non-aggro. Only the murkgliders are aggro, but the rats and bats assist them. The murks are undead, so IVU works jsut fine. We fought from the ZI, but in retrospect, this was a mistake. The dungeon is MASSIVE. After about 6 rooms of no hideous or tatterwing, the Pally invised and went off to look. He found the hideous rat a few more rooms along, and we all invised and went.

After killing the ph's each once (they are close, only about 10 mobs total between them) the warrior had to log out. We got an Enchanter friend to come join us, and he found us no problem. The warrior tried to run out, but said there was nothing to click to get out of the dungeon, so it would appear the only way out is to gate, port, or leave exd (which will boot you).

The enchanter was able to mez all the mobs easily and charm a Murk easily. He indicated it was hitting for 250+ charmed (half its normal DPS output) but he had brought pet toys for me and him and I kept it FAed and it was a nice chunk of DPS on top of the Pallys Slay Undead hits.

Respawn timer here appears to be between 15 and 18 minutes. There are a number of short hallways with JUST a rat or a bat in em, so they are safe to stand in, but pulling into there will aggro the bat/rat. Also, ALL mobs run, so get a snare. Some of these rooms are PACKED with mobs.

After killing the ph's 5 times each, we were about to give up when the named Bat popped. We killed it, and it dropped a 50 hp/mana mask (not great, LDoN quality tops, if that) and . . . . ready . . . . a 68 Spell rune! Thats right, a Murmite Rune (not lesser, or minor). We killed a couple more ph's and left. The enchanter was going to hijack the expedition with some of his guildies (all of us were once in the same guild together, but we went our seperate ways. We still hang out a LOT daytimes, and did dozens of LDoNs together).

So in conclusion: Get some decent Undead DPS, a snarer, and other regular stuff and your good to go. IVU down til you get to the hiderous rat. Kill it, kill the next room, and get to the next hall, where you will see the room with the tatterwing bat. Pull it. We staged in that hall by the Bat Cave, so the boxed cleric didnt have to run around too much, but you could easily just work back to the rat spawn and start again. We were fustrated, thinking we had spent 2+ hours for nothing, a minor rune tops, but when the 68 rune dropped, we all decided it was a worthwhile camp. Nice and easy, fair XP (not WoS or KT quality, but fair), some cash loots, oh and a tradeskill aug dropped in there, random, +1 spell shielding (not seen this one before). But all in all, I am going back.
