Ungkor
Having the mob snared is very nice for landing all your debuffs on him before he reaches camp. It really does maximize a shaman's dps if he can start landing dots when the mobs is 99%. While SK's can do that, so can a good ranger or druid puller.

In terms of aggro, in a higher end guild, I see very little difference between our warriors, sk's, and pallies. But its true, that among under geared players, SK's and Pallies hold the edge with their aggro spells. It isn't so much that incite+taunt, etc. from a warrior is overall less aggro (in some situations), but the ability of the pally/sk to tag the mob at a distance with a high aggro spell certainly is a good thing.

Prior to late elemental/Time, I really preferred the SK/Pally tank. Post Time / into GoD, I don't really care, but often prefer a warrior for their multitude of defensive discs.
