Well, you seem to want a whole lot of information :) BTW, you'll love the shaman.

There's tons of threads on this board about whether Shamans should focus on AC/Hitpoints/or Wisdom. I think that a majority are big believers in focusing on hitpoints and ac, and fill in with wisdom where you can. So, when you are looking for equipment, keep that in mind.

I'd suggest that you buy a Ceremonial Iksar Breastplate (Ikky BP) (for the regen--very nice for shammies who canni-dance for mana starting in 20's) and a decent shield. Buy a nice piercer, a 1HB, and 2HB weapon. The Ikky BP runs 4-8 K depending on server.

Maybe kill in fill in misc. slots with totemic/rygorr for now.

I'd visit this board at least once a week and read the threads. You'll learn what you need to about playing a shaman.\\


Good luck to you!

Since I came to this board, My shammy play has improved dramatically. Things that I found really usefull are as follow:

1- check the zone hunting guides on this board

2- when shopping follow this simple rule assing a point value to the items your considering as follow
1ac=1sta= 1.2 point
1wis=10hp=10mana= 1 point
this helped me a lot when I had limited fund and made chosing between 2 close items easyly.

3- Solo a lot and swich between 1hb, peirce, 2hb this will ensure you max out those skills and especially the all important defence for the later game. (I went from 20 to 35 woiedling a Drakkel Icegrinder and I really hated myself at 40 when I got a decent sheild and peirce but my peirce skill was only 120)

4- learn to live buffed. all buffs, str-sta-agi-sheild-talismam-sow- and fury/frenzy (or dex before you get that line) It does make a difference. I can solo blues at 40 with those buffs on and my pet erasyly... if one of those goes down I most likely will have to consider running.

5- dont waste plats on alchemy. More than any other trade this one is a plat sick hole and unless you have a guaranteed market like trough your guild, its not worth the effort.

Well thats my 2 coppers... Enjoy the game, despite all the bitching in moaning you'll read, shamam remain the best class to play!
