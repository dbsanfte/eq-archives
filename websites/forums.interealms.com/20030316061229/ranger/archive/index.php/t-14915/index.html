Neriak guards are great XP up through 54. The dual wielding ones suck, but with the healer it shouldn't be so bad. At 50 the ranger may require quite a bit of additional healing.

The Hole does suck. AE group or something, wonderful. Solo or duo, hell no. It's wonderful till you get adds you didn't know were there, and die somewhere in the castle, making for a wonderful halfling brell CR.

Although it might be a little tough, if you have a swarmcaller you could do Velks. Most the time it's pretty empty, especially if you play at odd hours. The XP rocks solo up through 56, although you could stay longer (I started to go nuts).

Whatever you do, just make sure the ranger is ungrouped. Playing the druid, just make a hotkey to target her again if you need to deal with adds or whatever. It will make the XP go much much MUCH faster.
