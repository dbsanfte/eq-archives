Seeing as I posted a question I thought I'd also offer up some info.
I hunted Golras on the ice island at neg 5000,neg6000 (I think) south of the elf base anyway.
With the pet I could solo them easy enough at 34 and very easily at 37. They are all reasonably fast spawning statics. Nicely spaced out as well and i never had any problems with adds.
I avoided the Hulkings at the lower levels.
With my melee partner we could mash them all with very little downtime and take out Hulkings too.
In the time I hunted there I hardly met anyone else. So be prepared for a lonely time.
THe loot was pretty mediocre sometimes a few plat sometimes less.
At 37 and almost insanely bored of the place I duo'd with the warr on Chief Owoompa (sp?) . Hes a nice fast spawn still blue at 37. He dropped mostly dross but we did get one Batskull earring in around 3hours of camping.
To sum up:
If you have limited time to play and want somewhere thats not camped and not too taxing the golras may be for you. And you won't have to listen to constant inane spam . Only the despairing OOCs of people who have fallen off/cant find/waiting for the boat.
Cheers
