OK , well your NOT a stupid newbie to start with , at 52 my gear was pretty weak and i was pretty damn poor, you just need to commit yourself to making cash if you really wanna buy things.

A few places i can think of to make cash off the top of my head for your level.


#1 High Hold Keep - The guards in the zone drops fine steel and plat each. The fine Steel you sell to a mechant on the first floor who just happens to not be affected by anything you kill in the zone. The bottom is easy and gets a lil tougher to kill each floor you go up, beware of the nobles and the roaming female which i cant remember her name right now.

#2 Old Seb - A really good option for you though maybe tough, would be to get a group and go to seb and farm these items. Seb is a gold mine , even if your not camped where you wanna be there is a good chance that something there will drop that you can sell for good play or atleast gems and PP will make it usualy worth hanging out there for a few hours , plus itll be good XP for you.

#3 Old world planes - Hate, Fear, Growth are all good places that drop ALOT of gems worth selling , i can usualy clear around 1.5k solo when im there.

#4 Rathe Mountains - Well i have mixed feelings about this place, while killing the Hill Giants here are great to make cash, its very over populated and farmed. You will no doubt find your spawns being ganked and people yelling alot, its a very volitile zone and personaly i hated it, but its really good cash. It is streaky though, sometimes the giants wont drop for quite a long stretch, then suddenly they do.

#5 LoY - The new Expansion is pretty good for cash , some good drops if you farm areas to force the named spawns and all those swords and shield that drop sell for a few plat too, probly not my first choice to farm for cash, but as said above, you will getnice XP still as you go.

#6 Everfrost Peaks - There is 3 Static spawns of ice giants on the way to permafrost dungeon ( vox lair ) and they drop a nice amount of cash also. The trick to alot of these places im naming is to talk to the people that are currently camping it and get on the waiting list so that when they leave you can move in after them, i got NG camp so many times that way im Seb.

#7 Tradeskills - This i cant directly point you to, but if you go to www.eqtrader.com you can find out what trades are used alot or even just ask around in game. Farming up those supplies and selling them in the bazaar can be very lucrative.

#8 Crystal Caverns - This is a tricky area, while its a excellant source of cash and velium bricks ( sell in bazaar ) i would suggest you becareful to not over run people leveling there and drive them out. For a 30-40ish player this place is godly on the XP and cash, one of the best dungeons for its level in the game IMHO and it would be unethical if you ask me to come in and deprive a XPer a good area so you can farm cash for toys. oddly enough there is usualy places to kill things in here which isnt camped.

#9 Mob hunting - If you complete you stoisicm quest you will be a very good soloer for your level at which this point you might wanna do what i do. Track mobs and kill them. At this point in my career i dont enjoy hours of farming cash, i tend to check on key mobs that i know i can solo and sell the loot i get form them in the bazaar. Very very fun for me and very rewarding.

#10 Vamp Castle in TM - At your level i dont see why you cant get a group and go farm the vampires in the castle in TM. The named have a chance to drop some really good loot and ive always been happy when i walked away from there after a hour or 2 of soloing.


there is other places , but i dont know if they fit your level and i cant give away all my secrets ;)

Anyway , good luck and i hope i was helpful. Peace..
