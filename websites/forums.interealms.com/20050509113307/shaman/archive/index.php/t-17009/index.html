Jinheim
3400's isnt a normal crit for me, but it is possible and not uncommon. Most of my heals land in the 3200-3300ish range, with a few in the 3100's and 3400's.

My noncrit heals are almost always about 1600, quite often for up to 1800 and change.

When crit heals are calculated, it takes the base heal (1300) and multiplies by your healing adept modifier (19% with maxed AA), then it doubles that value. That gives you a base value of 1547, so a crit of 3094 without any healing focus items. Then, after the crit is figured, it looks at the base heal of 1547 and applies the focus effect to that. That brings you to 3480 (or 3481 depending on rounding) as a max crit heal with EP arms.

With EP arms, your minimum noncrit heal (with max AA) will be 1562, and your maximum will be a whopping 1933.

edit: These calculations were done assuming you have EP arms (1-25% improved healing). Ornate arms or the rathe totem (or any other IH4 item) will be 1-20% improved healing, so slightly less.
