House
I have Natural Durability 3, Lightning Reflexes 5, and Innate Defense 5 so can speak from a bit of experience.

ND3 is great and easily worth 12 AA's. Just think of how much time you'd spend working on a quest for an item with comparable hit points.

LR5 is good but I wouldn't go for it right away. SCRM, QB, RC1, and VP1 are definitely better. After you've spent the 27 points to reach LR5, though, you'll notice a difference so it's not an ability to completely neglect.

ID5 is not of much benefit for its cost (27 points, same as LR5) unless perhaps you do a lot of solo tanking. It's something to purchase after you've exhausted most of the good options, but before you're down to Alchemy Mastery and Rabid Bear.
