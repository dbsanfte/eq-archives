No offense, but ignore Seredith's comments regardin getting through SG. Go to the corner when you first zone in, force yourself into the wall/corner, sit and camp. As a barbarian, I usually have to shrink or bear form the first time. Get as close to the wall as you can, sit, and camp. Log back in and you *should* be in the wall if this tactic worked. Sit and camp again, log back in ... after the third time, you'll be next to the zone to WW (this is called the poor man's succor). Not sure if this is an exploit or not, but the trick works just as well in LK in Velk's. Trust me on this ... I've done it a few times.
