What level are you? Not sure what level people consider newbie anymore :cool:

As far as brand new newbie when you are still hunting in Everfrost I don't think there is anything specifically for shaman. You can do some quests for some patchwork armor or some other basic gear but it is all/all stuff. Check out this page on Allakhazam's for quests that start in Halas: http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/qsearch.html?zone=11

The only newbie quests just for shaman are for the Gavel of Justice and the Tribunal Initiate.

Once you are a little higher level (in your twenties) you can do the Totemic Armor quests.

I am assuming you are talking about the kind of quest that most race/class combos have. If so, then no there isn't. The following races don't have those quests:

Barbarians (except Beastlords)
Erudites (From Erudin, Paineel has them)
Iksars (except Beastlords)
Ogres (except Beastlords)
Trolls (except Beastlords)
