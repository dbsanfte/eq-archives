As a follow up to the guild Summit, an idea was struck to invite all EverQuest healing classes to come together, discuss our healing and balance issues and hopefully be able to present a more unified list of issues, ideas and solutions to SOE dev's.

While difficult to host on any of the four healing class class sites, The Druid's Grove has stepped forward and is now inviting all healers to join together and talk, civilly and constructively, in our new forum The Priest's Covenant (http://thedruidsgrove.org/forums/forumdisplay.php?f=54).

The staff and community of the Druid's Grove would cordially like to invite any of you with thoughts and concerns on this matter to join us.

If you are a moderator or administrator on this forum, please PM Stormhaven or any of the other Mods/Admins of TDG and we will give you a unique title to make you easily identifiable as a "class representative". In addition, we will grant some of the users moderator rights to "maintain the impartiality". Please supply a URL to your “home” forum and an example post so that we can verify your identity.

Note: This particular forum will be heavily moderated, regardless of class (we WILL make no exception for anyone!), against flaming players and SOE alike, "classism" and other negative statements. We are here to be constructive, not destructive.

Thank you and we look forward to seeing you there!
