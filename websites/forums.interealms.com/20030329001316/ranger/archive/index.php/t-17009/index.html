Khone
have a SK or Pally tank the dragon make sure he keeps the gnome behind the dragon. If the gnome is in front it is a guaranteed death for him.

No AE spells, use /assist SK and you should be fine.

Mob positioning is the hardest part of the fight.


If you mez/charm/agro the gnome on the final fight - he will get bugged and not hand out any flags. - he needs to be in the last fight for it to work.
Muvandog
Have a shammy/ench slow the gnome before the fight, then blur him. He's probably dieing from enrage ripostes. If he's slowed, he won't die so fast.
