Kriel Darkbane
I'm on Kane Bayle too, with a new character. My 60 bard on Torv is only seeing part-time action now. It seems hard to do the "farm bone chips and sell them to a necro" thing on this server, as it's young and not many people are high level and throwing around money yet.

The spot we've found for cash that worked well around that level was the Warrens. Take the NK spire up to Nexxus, then down to Toxx. We started in the Warrens at L 10 and did well. The kobolds run, but we were generally able to knock them down before they got real far. I even did ok with that solo'ing there, if I made sure I pulled them far enough to have a good "runway".

Anyway... the kobolds can drop bronze weapons worth 2+ plat, and occasionally a bamboo something or other weapon worth 6+ plat. The exp is good, and the kobolds are wussy, so you can do well with very little gear. One issue is buying and selling - I don't know what faction you'll start with in Paineel, although killing the kobolds will work it up. At the worst you can pop up to the Nexxus to sell everyone now and then - and stay bound at the Shadowhaven soulbinder until you get L 14 and bind. Read up on the kobold molar quest if you go there, it's good money and faction when you can do it. You can't use the medium-size armor that drops there, but I guess you can't have everything. It still sells for pretty good money. Just make sure you have spirit pouch, so you can really load up without getting overloaded.

We made a lot of cash for our level at the Warrens. In fact, we're now in the low 20's and again suffering for cash because we can't find a spot to exp and make near the money we made in the Warrens (which financed spells, banded armor for all, and the purchase of fairly good magic weapons for all, by L 17).

Well the worst thing I think about VI putting in all those armor quests for some races is not the armor itself, ITS THE BAG!!!

To do those quests you can start at L1, you just go to the quest guy and boom you now have a 10 slot pack you can. If you are not in a city with those quests usually the first thing you have to do is get enough cash to go buy a bag to put things in to sell. You end up spending a good portion of your time running back and forth to vendor to sell **** to get enough cash to buy your first bag. /shrug

Well anyway you said you are a troll so I will give ya some tips since I started a troll sk recently to mess around on.

L1-5...

Hang out in front of the town zone bythe guards to that first island. Here you should find tads, lesser kobolds and decaying skele's. The guards are close and you should have no probem running if you get in trouble. However now that you don't loose exp or items till L10 I go the bonzi route myself. Just keep killing and killing and don't worry about death since you are only a few steps from where you are hunting anyway.

Froglocks, L2-4, will carry netted armor and boxes/4 slot containers you can use.

Another option is at L3-4 you can head over to feerrott and as soon as you zone in hang a right. Go all the way up till the stone hinge. Here is where all the spiderlings are. Spider silk sells for 1 silver and change each, or you can use to make some armor like the other guy said.

L5-7 the lizard camps on the town side of the river are doable. The ones by the river are the best since they only have 3 ea, the trick is breaking the camp. I usually do this by waiting till furb or hurd wonders close then agro'ing and pulling to him. Then you can wait 6 min or whatever it is and as soon as the 1st spawns pull and now you have 1 spawn and then a 2 spawn which is pretty easy. This camp drops weapons etc.

I can keep going tell me what level ya want me to stop hehe.

Rule of thumb...

At low level anything that has a weapon in its hand kill. These sell for usually 1Gold and change each. Well worth it to build up quick cash at low levels.

Things to keep fire beatle eyes, silks, weapons, bone chips (stackable). The meats and stuff just trash they are only like 8 copper per and take up to much room.
