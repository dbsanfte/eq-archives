Rathelon
My personal opinion (and somebody will not agree) is hit points above all else.

That being said, it is possible to find a compromise. If you cant get a high hp item in a certain slot, you can try to fill it with a high AC item that has some hp and/or wis on it. Or a high wisdom item with some hp/mana etc.

I do some tradeskills, so I usually go for a wisdom boost after hp, but it really comes down to what is available to you.
