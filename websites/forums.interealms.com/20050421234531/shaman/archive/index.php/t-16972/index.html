Update again, and thank you all for all the advice. I headed down to Chardock, and left after a short time. What wasn't lb was multiple pulls and just wasn't worth it solo. If there is a spot, I didn't find it.

However... I've found me new home! Charisis. First attempt sent me gating, as I was totally unprepared for the amount of mobs at zone in. Luckily, first impressions were wrong. They had been trained there on accident. I've dinged 59, and am now 59.4. The xp here is awesome compared to what I was making in LDoN. The north is 100% soloable (ala self buffs only), west wing takes kei to keep up, and south add a virtue. I can't yet touch the east wing. I've caught enough pick up groups that I have my hand of glory, so can now open all doors and travel unrestricted. I've also picked up a 24% haste belt for kicks. =) Sold a few already, and a few other drops I've collected. I'm on my 8th cudgel, and took a group to try the final fight only to have it turn into a bloodbath followed by nasty nasty CR's... All in all, I rate this zone A+ for shaman. Recommend HP buffs, resist magic, and epuration if you have them. Keep resists up, as there are ALOT of casters. As per XP, north is 2kills=1% (which is simple to do without kei, with kei and virtue I am a lawnmower =) as per west, I guess it works about out to 1.5=1 (or 3=2) and south I'm actually getting very very close to 1=1. Quick summary of the key to enter charisis... go to kaesora and kill xalgoz in the library. Loot a fang and if no other shamans need it, the necklace (for 8th cudgel =). Go to LOIO and kill Chancellor of Di'Zok for a jade chokdai prod (or about 300p in the bazaar nowadays). Turn in is a frog in SONH, D-something. Indiff to all, kinda near oowump (within sight of him, so be careful getting there, if you are KOS). Key is soulbound on entrance, and well well worth the minimal effort to get. A few other notes, I find necros are your best friends in Charisis, as it's primarily undead. About the only thing you can do for a necro is slap regen on them to help out there duration-canni-type-spell. Not a whole lot they can do for you, however... I've duo'd quite a bit there with a few necros that go down there, and let me tell you, your slow + jbb/alpha pet + tank abilities with their undead toys will add up to some fun. You can slow and heal, and SHOULD have some decent HP's (if you dont, and just concentrate on wis... please delete yourself and make a druid...) Once things here are slowed, regen + hp + armor=tank. Stoic helps that much more. When I get around to writing my own guide to leveling, this is going in there.. and I'd recommend north wing at 50 in a group, or about 56 solo. If you are a kei and virtue junkie, probably sooner. I tend to avoid them if I can. With the new canni4, I only take 4 mins to go from 0m to full mana/health. Sitting with kei takes me about 6.

After all my pessimisim (sp?) about other zones, I'm glad I found one that's fun, entertaining, and "worth it". Since it is quite possible to overpull and need to gate, it makes it a HELLUVA lot more fun than boring static spawn camps, but the xp-to-risk factor is high enough I'm gonna keep coming back time after time foir a while now.

Edit... forgot to mention. As per how long this is good for, a few 65 buddies now live here too. They do the east wing, and get 1% aa per kill and routinely perform genocide. It's a shaman/ranger duo, I've hung out with them a bit, and we move fast.. I basically am just another DoT for them, and play healer on large pulls, but it's quite fun in this zone.

Btw, I did make a trip to the hole, and managed to kill me a good named that was trained to zoneline. DO NOT BOTHER with this zone. Not sure how it is farther in, but omg the entrance was rough. I hung around for a bit, then helped out a few people with CR's... I hate it and wont go back... not worth it for me.
