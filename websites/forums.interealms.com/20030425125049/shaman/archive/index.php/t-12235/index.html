First, I would and did work on all of our weapon skils at each level untill you get them close to 200.

That said, I would not spend much at all on a piercer, I would spend the cash on a nice 2hb with a good ratio and a hast item. At level 20 your damage caps will go up quite a bit and the 2hb will make a nice diferance in your damage output.

When I was a little Ogre shaman I would solo after each ding untill each weapons skill was maxed. At first you level so fast that skills will get left behind and I did not get them all level caped untill about level 27 or so. I would start each level with pierce, then 1hb and then finaly I would just equip the big 2hb hammer for the rest of the level anytime I was solo. My kill rate with the big hammer was significantly faster so I would just save it for last.

I got all my weapons skills into the 170 - 180 range so that If I got somthing very nice as a drop I would be able to use it.

Now at 61 I have 200 in 2hb 200 in 1hb and about 180 in pierce but I only swing the hammer while indoors between casting CanV, CanIV and torpor.

level 1 to 51 almost all my damage came from 2hb.
level 51 to 59 almost all my damage came from JBB.
now my damage comes from spells and I spend 90% of my time generating more mana.

The above does not include pet damage.

For solo grinding 2hb > pierce.
In group selected a shield weapon combo to up your ac and mana / hp.
