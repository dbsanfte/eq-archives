I did almost all of level 59 at the firefall zone in Shadeweavers. The area marked as Unknown Zone in the eqatlas map http://www.eqatlas.com/luclin/shadeweaversthicketmap.html

The mobs are all dark blue. They do not see invis, they are non-agro. Some are casters, some are just melee's. If the named is up, sinlge pulling him can be tough... Unless you have some extended range item you will be in agro range of the other mobs when you cast on him, and they will assist. The named is KOS.

Loot is non-existant, except for a rare robe off of the named (I sold em for 1-2k aftr giving em away to my friends twinks). Exp is pretty good solo.

The casters will try to gate when they get low on health. The named will also. The named can summon. If a caster gates, expect an add. If the named gates.. gate out yourself, or you will be summoned and gang raped by all of his friends.

The only hard part is getting the named with a full pop. Once you have the spawn broken up you can kill nonstop with ease. Spawn times are fast. I was not able to kill all 4 mobs before they respawned. I typicaly started at one end, and by the time I got to the 4th mob, the first was respawned.
