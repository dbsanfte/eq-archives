The Zek hit from actually killing Coldain is very small compared to the Rif hit. The best ways to raise Zek faction are: ToV raids, Western Wastes raids, soloing Sentries in Skyshrine, and turning in Coldain heads to Captain Bvellos. While killing Coldain is certainly a good way to acquire the heads, it's not the only way, the heads can be acquiring from bodyguards with no Coldain hit, and can also be bought.

I went from KoS to Zek back to ally, despite our Statue/Vindi raid in the middle (I /q out for the statue hit) in two days by fighting in west ToV for an evening, then soloing Skyshrine for a few hours, then turning in about 40 coldain heads, most of which I bought from people for 50-100pp each, acquiring the rest killing bodyguards for exp in EW. Overall, I spent about 2k, but did it very quickly so I could make my last armor turn-in and go back to killing giants again.
