Astroshak
If you want to save money while making bows, then get a friend's gnome to tinker cams for you; around 100 or so skill.

This no longer makes money, but it does save a bit of money (because the cams cost so much from vendors).

Alternatively, if you'd rather save money but spend more time, hunt acrylia, and the condensed substances (fire, ice, shadow). The arrows made from these will still get you to 250, and get you there a lot cheaper than the compound darkwoods will; but at the cost of a lot more time spent.

The third option is only available to wood elves (are you a wood elf?). Mithril arrows. Supposedly they changed the rate of skill gain since I did any skilling (when I did it, I made money on the darkwoods, but I did it to get a BFT made, if that helps any with the timeframe); but when I did mithril arrows 40 combines got me from 202 to 204 (got a lucky one at the very end of that batch) and then it took me about 280 combines to go from 235 to 243, which is where I stopped as I've got a geerlock. My fletcher doesn't have great stats; 124 wis, 98 or so dex, except when I could get buffs for them.

250 is a nice goal; I'd worry more about 243. 243 and a geerlock (or the fletching trophy, I forget the name but its a knife) will put your effective skill at the max, 255.
