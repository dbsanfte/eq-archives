Hail,

Your 4 person group can level extremely fast by adding one more hotboxer who uses 2 dps classes who plays when you do.

Smash through adventures and grind in Tier1 PoP. I recommend Innovation until you are 60, then move to Valor until 62 and then move to HoH if you can handle it. If your group can get together regularly at the same time for 4-6 hours per day, you can all be level 65 in under a month's time. At that point you can jump on the pickup raiding circuit and begin knocking out flags.

Eventually if you are personable and even only moderately skilled you will get some guild invites. Some of the higher end guilds even have 'feeder' guilds for lower level people to work their way up inside. These aren't all that common, but can be a great opportunity for you to learn & grow. Much better than staying in a smaller guild when you have no intention of staying there once you become powerful. Smaller guilds don't like playing stepping stone most of the time. ;)

Find your server boards and lurk there. Soak up information about different guilds. Look up their rosters & try to group with uberguilders playing their alts in their spare time. This can get you better known & may strongly impact your application process months down the road.

Take care,
