Hail,

PoI factory Tin Soldiers.
There are 7 spawns, counting the 2 door guards.

You will likely need help breaking the door spawns, as 2 at once would probably kill you at 61. Maybe use the young pet pull technique to get the first door guard single (I haven't tried it on them, but it should work). The rest of the pulls should be easy singles as long as you are careful.

You need the Xanamech flag to open the door on your own, but if there is someone else flagged nearby they can open the door for you. If there is a group already inside they likely won't mind you taking the Tins because they will be camping the Golems.

The 7 tin soldiers would be a steady good exp camp at your level.
Other than that, I would recommend learning how to cross Plane of Nightmare to the Hedge, and solo inside there without activating Thelin.

There are good threads buried here that I wrote with more details about both of these camps.

Take care,
