That group should be just fine in JP doing the entrance and just inside the caves with no problems.....good steady pulls with as many or as few as you want to handle; the gnolls do run and pathing sucks sour swamp a$$ so look for roamers and runaways to suddenly pop into being next to you.
Killing the gnolls gives great faction hits to the JP peeps and to the Qeynos guards, however it does nothing for your animal faction, so invis is recomended if you plan on running to the gnoll caves...also your ogre will be better off zoning in from Black Burrow to the Gnolls of Brell Serillis caves, no one is KOS there (not even leezards)...the reason behind this is that he is mucho KOS to the druid/ranger factions in JP, but a few hours of grinding on the gnolls will fix that....have him save all the gnoll teeth and turn them in...hehe I was at a higher faction there than my druidess friend :D
As far as the crowd control goes, just root them and watch for the horrid pathing....once you get used to it, you can deal with it easier....heck there is a good chance that your shaman can solo them heh....just as a warning, the Gnoll Warriors are all DB at 57 and only 1 in 5 LB so that may mean that they are pretty much con even or close to it to your 3 box set up, so expect resists and a huge aggro radius for your squad...have fun, they are a blast to beat on, so-so loot, with some nice odd rare drops (the Gnollish ring of Sorcery and the HtH weapon being the best items) and the exp flies, at 58 I can make a blue an hour on them deeper in, killing warriors, necros, clerics and shaman mobs....oh yeah watch for the clerics, they do Complete Heal (arf) so make sure your warrior has slam hot keyed and your cleric tosses a stun when they are your target.
Have fun and take care.

I took the advice posted here and tried out JP Gnolls last night. I'm a 46 shaman and the only notable equipment I have is the Iksar BP and Spine chill Spear.

I was getting GREAT xp soloing the entrance to the gnoll cave to the tune of 3 blue ticks per kill, but it was very risky and very hard work. If you have JBB's this would be MUCH easier, and probably alot safer.

The warriors were better for me because they slow well and I had fewer resists. The casters were a pain because it took some time for dogdog to taunt the agro while the mob was nailing me with DD's that took a full bub of HP per shot. I went through 2 blue bubs in 15 minutes, and could have been much faster if I didn't have to med up so much between fights.

It was much safer & faster when a 53 paladin grouped up, which dropps the Xp to a little more than a single tick per kill.

Recomendation is: Twinks/JBB's will be great here at 46 solo and all the rest of us poor people should bring a melee or recognize the risk.
