Jinheim
If you go straight towards the coast from yeldema's nest and go down the cliff, you'll find a pretty sizeable ledge down there where the cliff meets the water. Nothing will aggro there, and there's enough space for yeldema + you + dog without anyone having to go in the water. Just make sure that the dog is pushing him up the cliff rather than in to the water.
Jaruden
If you're non-kos it's a lot easier. Drakes won't agro you at that point, and most of the things that wander over Yeldema's nest are drakes. There are a few safe'ish spots north of Yeldema, there's a sort of indent in the ground near one of those little spire things, only a very rare velium hound walks by. There's also a few spots near the water you can find where mobs very rarely path through. From there it's just luck or waiting till the pathing mob goes by.

Mostly, you're going to need to explore around and see what you can find. Pathing has been changed in this zone numerous times (to make it a little harder, especially to solo), so there are precious few "safe" spots. Key is to kill as fast as you can, and hope most of the time.

The ToV entrance is safe though, if sont isn't up and you can drag the dragon down there.
