Xanden
Just did this recently, so tossing in some tips:

1) The ground spawn of the diamonds seems to be "permanent" in that I picked them up, and they were still there on the ground. Was this to combat the long respawn, where people would time it, farm it, and sell in the bazaar? Easy and fast, just get the /loc and run to it.

2) Shaman powder: grab a tracker and head to the NE area of the zone. I spent a lot of time there as a ranger, and Yun shaman were on track near the EJ zone almost full-time. They do aggro other mobs, so you need to get to them fast when they pop.

3) Ochre clay: bought in the bazaar for 50 plat. I would go this route unless you know CoM or plan on going anyway. It can either be the easiest thing you get, or the second hardest depending on which route you take.

4) Warlord bone chips: I hunted these myself because they are no-drop and I didn't want to hang out in the zone waiting for someone to announce it. Get one melee 55ish and camp the LCY/Well area and pull everything. With KEI you can keep 15-20 spawns cleared and make some good exp. Warlords can spawn in many points, starting from the two guards in the hallway outside of the courtyard and going to the back of the rooms around the well. Warlords always drop them, and I have killed probably 6-8 warlords over a span of ~50 hours in my hunting there. Usual luck-driven spawn, I had ten hours with no warlords and then I could get a couple in one hour.


Most of this is in the walkthroughs, but these were my experiences on the individual drops. If anyone on The Nameless needs a chip MQ or shaman powder, I have extras in my ranger's and shaman's banks.

I got Cann2 at 52 because I heard Cann3 had a horrible fizzle rate. It's worse of course, since it's 15 levels higher, but recasting takes no time and you lose no mana because the spell has no cost... so I don't see why anyone would care about even a doubled fizzle rate. Cann3 has been working out great for me. Cann2 would have been nice at 39, but the dang quest is not set up to be feasible at that level.

Get it anyway, it's a decently fun quest that isn't very straining and can be done with no more than two people.


EDIT: Remember, my main is a ranger. What I consider an acceptable fizzle rate is skewed, and any rangers who use Ensnare know what I'm talking about. Your mileage may vary.

Durdend
You probably looted Canni III.

Canni II is a quest, and worth it.

My experience.

1) Ochre Clay isn't too uncommon, bought mine in the Bazaar on Rodcet for 45pp. There were about 5 clays for sale at the time, ranging from 45-75pp.

2) Bone chips were a little frightening. A kind Mage and Rogue were happy to help me out. They did all the killing, I did what I could to help. Warlord spawned in a room by the well in Left Courtyard for us.

3) Diamond Dust was easy. The hard part was waiting for the darn boat at the FV docks - I didn't want to miss it and have to wait another 10-15 minutes. With SoW and Levi, got to the Goranga Island np. Picked up the bag, got out of aggro range and gated.

4) The Shaman Powder was the worst part of this whole quest. I hired the tracker thing, didn't work, asked trackers in zone, asked guildies, nothing but a lot of frustration. Finally found the "Shaman Powder" for sale in the Bazaar on Rodcet for 50pp. Note, it's not Yun Shaman Powder, it's just Shaman Powder (the reason I point this out is for weeks I'd been checking the bazaar by typing in "Yun" - thinking the proper name was Yun Shaman Powder).

Make sure it's the BLACK Shaman Powder, and then you're all set!

Just do the turn in and enjoy.
