KumoeSpearbear
Question is what are you coming back to?

Are you coming back to a guild that is progressing into the Elemental Planes, or are you coming back to a guild that is stepping into GoD?

If you are coming back to the former, you will have a much easier run of things, because of the backflagging system that exists on many servers. It is easier to get into the planes than it was a year ago, and LDoN is a great balm to use when the raiding get to you.
GoD however, is a pothole. You need stellar gear and a great group of players to advance into it. After taking a year off, you might find the distance between your gear level and what is needed to be a problem.

If you come back: Best bet is to either raid a lot in the planes, or spend a fair chunk of your time in LDoN gearing up. You will progress pretty quickly that way.

Ok, to answer the question: its a good time to come back, with the caveats I mentioned above. But, with WoW and EQ2 around the corner, the time you would need to invest might be too much.
In the end though, the choice is up to you :)

Happy hunting,
