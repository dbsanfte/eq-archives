Matten, first off i aplaud you for playing on the newbie server, you sir are experiencing EQ how it is best. If i could give you any advice, it would be to stay there and enjoy the game while it lasts cause soon that server will change and fall inline with the rest and over run with ubers and uber twinks rushing through the game think there is level 1 then ding level 65.

Stay there, group up, explore Blackburrow, work hard at your character and dont expect anything but advice to be given to you. In alot of ways i envy you so dont throw it away.

As far as real advice. Start building yourself as a HP and AC monster. Dont forget to sprinkle in a lil wisdom, but you are not a druid, you are not a weak lil wizard, you are a tough as nails living in nature, drawing from nature kind of class. You should be off tanking adds at all levels, or atleast be able to when needed. Once you good slows start coming you will be taking the hits alot so get used to it and prepare, when you solo you will also be taking the hits. unless you root and rot, which i never liked.

Spend your cash wisely, when you reach 34ish realize that Crystal Caverns in Velious is a gold mine for tradeskill supplies to sell and straight up velium to sell to vendors.

Work hard, play hard and have as much FUN as you can and the pay off will be so big when you look back.

Good luck

When I start a new character (I have 4 newbies on Stromm - all in starting cities I've never been in before or have little experience with) I go to www.eqatlas.com and click on the starting city, then the quests link. Doing the quests in your own starting city will get you basic armor and weapons. Will introduce you to solo and group hunts and move you through your levels in a steady fashion. Steady leveling means your skills are right up there and generally your cash and gear stays even as well.

I then go to the class boards like this one and read the writeups on quests and hunting grounds. The possibilities are limitless.

My level 12 stromm beastlord is working hard at tailoring and various quests and somehow ended up at leavel 12 and with almost 20 plat in the bank without doing any /auction at all.

There are tons of quest and camped items at every level. Yes there is a level 3 hopper that drops a magical weapon, a level 3 spider that drops a magical weapon, etc. Nothing beats the satisfaction of doing your own research and outfitting and playing your character your own way. IMHO.
