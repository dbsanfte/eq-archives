I played muds for many years before EQ ever came out. The muds were more dynamic and had far better quests than I have ever seen on EQ. Generally at least once a week, 2 to 3 gods would get together and there would be dynamic content. Totally new and unique creatures and events would take place. Sometimes there would be a new area added ( ranging from as small as adding a few rooms to an already existing area to as large as a 100 room new zone ), but most of the quests took place in already existing zones. I remember lots of quests where the god would take over a new creature and would require us to talk/kill/interact with other npcs, but would only give obscure clues. The older players who really new the world shined, and it wasn't always about who was the highest level or had the best equipment.

This stuff isn't that hard to do. I took a plane jane Diku mud and turned it into a massive multiclass world, and I am not even a coder by profession. Special Procedures for mobs and rooms can be hard coded, but any advanced mud had a scripting language for adding these things, and no hard C coding was required. If scripting languages were invented for free open source muds, I would have a hard time believing that EQ doesn't have something similar.

I understand that in a graphical world, it takes more time to come up with completely new graphical models. But, they wouldn't have to use a new model every single time. And, by god, they are paid to do this. How is it that a couple of unpaid college kids could come up with more compelling and dynamic content than a full blown gaming company that has millions of dollars of profit every year?

I play on stormhammer, and even there I am extremely disappointed with the dynamic content. Almost all of the quests are offered during the day when most people are at work. There is only soo many times that I can do a royal rumble in the arena without it getting old.

Since PoP has been out, I have been totally unable to get anyone to go to CT. Not only that, but I understand that the exp has been nerfed there as well ( I heard the zone multiplier was lowered ). Do I level up and make this content even less rewarding? I told myself I would give myself until I got can 5 to visit these old world zones, then move on.
