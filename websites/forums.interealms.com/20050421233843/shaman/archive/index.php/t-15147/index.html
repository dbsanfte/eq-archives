junkilo
You can play however you desire, but if you are going to stick to this character for a while then I'd suggest learning to tank stuff in the face (you are going to need every defense skill you can find).

some strategies-
1. tank it in the face. pull with your best slow, tank a bub, nail it with your best poison dot (optional), root, back up med and canni until root breaks, cast best str/ac debuffer or blind it (lowers atk) (optional) and tank it down. Sometimes all you will need on a mob is a good slow however.

2. root n rot/rot 'n dot. pull with slow (optional) and land every dot you have on the mob, root it and med/canni. re-root/re-dot until dead.

3. fast nuke. pull with slow and nuke it to death while the mobs whackin on you.

4. blind kiting. load up a mob with dots and root it. move far enough away that you have plenty of time to cast on it with it running at you full speed. blind it, move away and watch the mob run away, re blind/re dot as necessary. (this tatic is tough to pull off successfully).

good luck.
