Einnod
Out of all the kunark dragons, Talendor would be the easiest solo for a shaman. He's almost straight-up melee and his dot is very easy to resist at 65. The only trouble comes from his fear, which seems to land on me with over 300mr.

Trakanon would be the hardest solo kill as a shaman, for the simple banishment factor.

Severillous would be somewhat difficult because he is resistant to pretty much all our spells.

Gorenaire would be the second-easiest, even counting the fact that she's a cleric.

If you are looking to solo a dragon with your current gear, you really need to get lucky with landing a slow. You can only cast on the dragons in melee range, which makes it harder, but it's doable.. just malo the dragon of your choice, kite it till you're at max casting range, quiesc yourself, and then tank while channelling slow. I, myself, have never been able to kill a kunark dragon solo, the best I've done was with a 65 war and 58 druid trioing Talendor.

Note: With my current gear, I am at 5900hp raid buffed(pot9, symbol, fo7, bss, and max sta)

There are two problem that I see with soloing the VP named. The first is moving around in there ... you generally have to clear from place to place and I don't think you could realistically make progress against the repop rate.

The second is the order the dragons come in. Could a shaman solo Xygoz, for example? Maybe, although like all Kunark dragons, the real trick is landing slow. Unfortunately, Xygoz is far from at the front of the zone, and the first dragon you have to face is quite possibly the most annoying. Hoshkar's AE is just flat obnoxious, and few of us are going to have the DR to resist it. For the Elemental+ people not familiar with VP, its the same AE on Balatador the Cursed (PoAir) or the big croc in Earth. I've also never gotten a slow to land on him, and, even with 7k buffed hp, I just can't stand up to his DPS solo. =\

Of course, there are folks out there with over 1k hp on me, plus the new slow, so YMMV.
