Krux
I've been using a new tactic (new to me anyways) in SD.

Basically I,

Ensnare the mob
Cast stinging swarm
sit down and wait
I keep an eye on my mana percent.. when it hits 100
I ensnare again.

I know this is sort of a well duh.. tatic but I never really used it before as a Ranger.

It is a slow death granted.. but it's very safe. I sit with full health points. should something happen I can always melee or run.

I picked up Tolans Vambracer , which cast Stinging Swarm and it's even better. With the bracer I can cast Ensnare about every 30 secs..

What I like about this method is
A: The mob is parked, not going anywhere. Ensnare last 1min 36 secs. Lots of time to med.
B: Using low mana costing spells, instead of say (Immolate), I can keep my mana near 100%..
C: If I'm low on health, I can still kill stuff.. and heal while medding.
D. If I'm out fear kiting and I run out of fearable mobs, I can switch to this.

FYI
Ensnare last 1 min 36 secs.
Sting swarm last about 54 secs.


I've been practicing this on two mobs at once, and works good. Just go to watch the mana.

Why is mana watching or keeping it at 100% important? IT's sort of a safety net.. plus it allows you to constantly cast stuff. IF I'm using a spell combo that I can't gain back while sitting between Ensnares.. then after awhile I'll run out.

Also keep in mind, the more casting that you have to do.. the less time you have to med.

Oh and if you can get any items or spells cast on you that help you regenerate mana faster then all the better. You might even be able to switch in Immolate then.


Anyone else have some suggestions that might spice this up?
