besides that info in the other threads, I have felt gear is a personal taste and most of those ideas either compliment or go full force against the grain of what I do or like to wear.

My personal goal is to have matching armor actually, but I always look for hp then ac then wisdom then resistances in that order now.

you will also have a full bag or 5 or gear to swap out to do certain things like bagging a few pieces of JB armor for the effect when mana is tight or the RH BP or VP hammer etc...

so equipment is always and will always be a personal taste and also include what you can afford or be lucky enough to win.

What I did long ago was never listen to these kinds of threads but rather go to web sites and do searches for certain gear based on their attributes.

IE: shaman usable, tribunal usable, neck slot, modifies HP, hp mod between 25-125, non-legends, magic-lore but NOT no-drop

that is a pretty good search to find shaman twink gear imo, add the no drop stuff if your in a raiding guild and make a wish list and stick to it, but keep in mind one great item might make you dump some resistance stuff in your bag or change the "need" for another item on your wish list.

I hope this helped a little bit, but check out alakazams or eqmaps since they have a great search function.
