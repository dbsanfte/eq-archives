Blast Of Venom will fizzle more than other spells. It is designed that way (you may wish to check lucy.fnord.net to see why). There is an attribute for each spell called a "fizzle adjustment". The higher the number, the more likely you are to fizzle. The spell Blast Of Venom has a fizzle adjustment of 25, and the spell Spear Of Torment has a fizzle adjustment of 10. The spell Torrent Of Poison (our AE) has a fizzle adjust more in the middle -- 15.

What does all that mean? It means max your skill in the class affected, and then keep on leveling. Your level has alot to do with fizzle rates.

Why is it designed that way? I have no idea. Perhaps to put shaman in their place -- we are not really supposed to be wizards, after all. Sunstrike (a wizard spell) has no fizzle adjustment at all. I guess that makes sense. I would expect a wizard to fizzle less on nukes.
