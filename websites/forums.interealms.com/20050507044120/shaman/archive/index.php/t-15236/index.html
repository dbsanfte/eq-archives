Hamenu
From what I've heard, Fire Pet resists are broken, and no longer work like they used to. Pets are now supposed to use either the resists/level of their master, or their own innate resists, whichever is higher. When this change was made, the fire pet was no longer completely immune to Fire AE's. However, water pets do seem to be immune to poison, and earth pets do seem to be immune to disease.

For Fire Pets, about the only place they are useful post-50 are in this epic encounter, and SOE probably felt that this wasn't the way they wanted the encounter to go. Since SOE has been really quiet on the issue, most people seem to feel that they want to keep it this way, but didn't have the courage to announce it as a nerf.
