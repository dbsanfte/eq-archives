Jestice
Pay the plat for a port, Velious is imho the best expanision to the game.

Get to the Great Divide and you can either hunt the wolves as the above post or start on the Tizmarks. There is a bank, and almost full services only a short distance away, and the possiblility of getting a group exists - though they are few and far between. My suggestion is to get a friend - I perfer rangers or paladins - and kill Tizmaks 'til about 33 or 34. Then move to the Eastern Wastes (with the same friend) and there you can fight Ulthorks and Wulrus's. Or maybe even venture into the Crystal Caverns. On the whole the Tizmarks and the Ulthorks drop pretty decent loot (Velium Wepons) and a good group in CC is awesome. Wolves are about the same xp - but only reward you with faction - which a few good nites in the Giant City of Kael can easily be attained.
