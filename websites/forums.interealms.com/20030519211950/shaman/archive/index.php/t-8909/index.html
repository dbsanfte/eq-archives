Hmm, based on your equip and description, it sounds like your guild is extremely similar to my own, though a few months behind (a casual guild that also raids).
As such, I'm probably in a decent position to recommend gear.

Note that all prices are based on Fennin's economy, so please don't hold it against me if they're radically different from AB's.

Tracking Wolf Collar (from 8th shawl war) is 10ac 5wis/int 40hp/mana with a slight dodge mod, nice upgrade. Runs 7-10k.

Either Glassy Frog Eye (2ac 5dex/wis/int 40hp/mana +saves)
or Silver Hammer Earring (3ac 6wis 25hp 55mana +saves) would be good ear upgrades. They run 9-10k and 4-6k, respectively.

If you have PoV access, I would suggest you work on collecting metallic liquids and razorfiend talons to get yourself a pair of Valorium Rings of Gallantry (5ac 8str/sta/agi/dex 65hp 40mana +saves). They sell for ludicrous amounts, 30-40k, but as I said you can self-attain the components.

Myself, I prefer a Heirophant's Cloak to Lodizal. It is 10ac 4-to-all 75hp/mana. Runs 17-19k.

Orb of the Infinite Void is a great shield, but if you'd rather not shell out all your money on a single item (I opted to buy mine, and don't regret it), look into a Skull Marked Shield (22ac 7wis 50hp/mana) for 3-4k, or a Bone Braced Buckler (25ac 10str 10wis 55hp/mana) for 10-12k.

Depending on how much you mind item swapping, a Barbed Rubicite Bracer (13ac 5wis 35hp) at around 2-4k would be a good JBB replacement. I don't know the stats on Ogre Cultural Chain offhand, so I can't say how it compares.

For belts, a Barbed Rubicite Belt (12ac 3dex/sta/wis 40hp 25mana) like I wear is probably your best 'value' at around 7k. But if you wish to splurge, a Girdle of Mithaniel (8ac 12str 10sta/wis/int 55hp 45mana) is definately a superior item, at 24-28k.

For shoulders, I personally have switched to a Peerless Dragonwing Mantle (14ac 55hp/mana) for 7k from my old Obulus Death Shroud. My reasoning for doing so was getting the level 62 heal: Tnarg's Mending. It's a patch unaffected by ODS anyway, which was always the biggest use I had for it. I do still swap the ODS in for buffing on raids, so you may or may not wish to rid yourself of it completely.
Other valid shoulder choices include Death Watcher's Shroud (15ac 5sta 7wis 15hp 30mana +saves) for 8-10k, or Pauldrons of Witness (7ac 5dex 7wis/int 35hp/mana) for 1-2k.

Anyway, I hope that at least a few things here are helpful, feel free to click my sig to see what I use.
