Shandy Orcbane
Well, True Spirit pet will /shield you from time to time. This is a warrior ability, and he uses it. He decides when; we have no control over it. Horses, being pacifists by nature, don't do this.

Normally, targeting a horse will redirect the targeting to the horse's rider. They do have HP, allegedly. There are riderless horses popping up frequently in Plane o' Knowledge, but since you can't hurt anything there, it's hard to check. I have yet to see one unmounted outside that zone (not counting stables in Bazaar.)

I've sat on my horse through a barrage of annuls and dispells, it was the only "buff" I had when all was done. Fighting the consorts in PoN, the paralyzing poison they have will knock you off your horse.
