oOga[FoH]
It was a crazy crazy fight.. I have been a member of FoH since the first month of final release and quite honestly, nothing to date compares to the fight we had last night.. Everyone performed their duties to perfection and we finally got him after over 30 long minutes.. When we started gathering the items necessary to take Seru down it felt like it would never end.. It was not fun one bit, and as an officer, it was very challenging at times to keep people motivated to press on.. Let alone myself.. We went after him for our first run on Monday night.. We had taken in folks for initial testing as we gathered the necessary items, but Monday night was our first full force attempt.. And well, he spanked us hard.. I was furious.. I was asking myself over and over why on earth would Verant require a nearly 60 day GUILD effort just to get to him, then to find out that we all completely wasted our time.. The developers already have so much on their plates that there was no way in hell they were going to set aside the needs of the majority of the 400k+ player base to retune Seru for a measly 50 people.. Dont get me wrong, we have been in this position in nearly every expansion.. This is definitely not new.. However, nothing had required the incredible time sinks as the end game content in Luclin does.. We had put in our time, and god damnit, we wanted our paychecks..

Well there was no way in hell we were going to wait around for Seru to get tuned.. So we did what we do best.. We put our heads together, crunched numbers and killed the sob.. Everyone had to give 110% effort or the win would be out of reach.. I can honestly say that each and every person put in 150% and we accomplished our goal..

Now that Seru is dead and we have the attention of the developers, we can get the finer points of the encounter tuned properly.. Many things about the fight such as guard repops, pathing and features of Seru himself are damn right absurd.. Kendrick, the original designer of Sanctus Seru and Lord Inquisitor before he departed from Verant last year, will even tell you the same on page 3 of this post: http://www.fohguild.org/forums/showthread.php?s=&threadid=677

The loot was great and I can't wait to go after him again.. And yes, he can be yawned.. Although I will warn you, without a Trollbane turgur, its a pain in the ass..

Cheers!
