Sepisis
Getting your faction in SH from apprehensive to ally takes about 30 minutes.

Doing the registration quest (same one as trade commissioner quest) will hit you up to amiable. From there on you can get it up by running sewing kits from Stealon near the zone in from Nexus to Gearo in the bazaar. Gearo gives you a bag of gems which you give to Stealon.

I would suggest that on an empty hot key page you make a macro that says "I will make a trip to the bazaar" (For stealon) and "I will take the gems" (for gearo) As well as placing an empty inventory slot in the hotkey page.

This quest grants a good amount of faction with SH merchants. The hits for the houses of midst, stout, and fordel are considerably less, howerver.
