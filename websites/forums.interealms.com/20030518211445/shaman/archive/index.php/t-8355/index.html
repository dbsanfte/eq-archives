A month ago I paid 27Kpp and a Barbed Ringmail tunic for mine. I was really sweating it because that plat took me about 6 months to scrounge up (my playtime is quite limited). I was hoping it was worth it, after reading all the testimonials here on the Crucible.

It is worth it. I was skeptical for the first couple of weeks that I had it, because I had never been challenged by anything yet. I'm a casual player, and a death in a tough place ruins my playtime for a week trying to do a CR. Consequently, I have been quite cautious.

This past weekend, I headed over to the Ocean of Tears to kill some Seafury Cyclopses. I knew to avoid the Tainted/Corrupted Cyclops, but no one told me about Quag Maelstrom! :D

I pulled him with a slow, and ran back to my pet. As soon as i got there, I tried to cast EBolt, and was stunned to realize that my mana bar was empty! Mana Drained! Got real nervous for a bit, but calmed down when I realized that he was already slowed, my pet was already chewing on him, and I had my JBB! :D He rolled over like a two dolla' ho!

Without my JBB, I'd have been on another annoying CR. Togors would have worn off long before I could have meleed him down, even with my barb spiritists hammer. Man, I sound like a bad infomercial testimonial! :p

If you have the opportunity, get one! I followed the advice that I read here last year, which was to quit buying little upgrades and save my money for the JBB. Now with the JBB, I can farm plat much more quickly than before. Got 500pp from the seafurbies in 30 minutes last night!
