What is the best way to set up spell message so that groups know that things are getting slowed, they are being healed, etc?

Been playing EQ since launch..but never had a caster class who needed to get that info out before.
Immish
It's been a while but I'm pretty sure the spell gems are numbers frrom 1-8 top to bottom. So make a hot key as follows

line 1 /cast 1 (or whatever number coresponds to the spell)
line 2 /gsay Healing %T or /gsay Slowing %T

Then use those hotkeys whenever you need to cast that spell.

Might be an easier way but if there is I don't know it, of couse I only know a little in regards to pure casters and this shaman is my first serious attempt at one. Hope my info is correct and that it helps.
Faern
I have actual hotkeys made and I hit them AFTER the spell lands.

This prevents messages such as

/Shaman_01: /g: a Dazzling Occulus has been <ROOTED>

Me thinking to myself: 'Erm no hit hasn't.' (Charmable MOBs in LDON aren't rootable, snareable)

Or this:

/Shaman_01: /g A {MOB} Has been SLOWED !
(Insert cute spammy message here)

5 Seconds later (

/Shaman_01: /g A {MOB} has been SLOWED !
(Insert cute spammy message here)

Note, this isn't a slam on Shaman who link their spells and a message but to point out that you might want to relay critical information AFTER said information has actually come to pass.
