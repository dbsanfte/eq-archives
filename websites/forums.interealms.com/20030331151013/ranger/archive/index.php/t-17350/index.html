Well, he's not completely wrong. Yes he's wrong that the amount of exp required per AA goes up - I think we can agree this stays the same.

However, as you gain levels, the amount of experience you gain for killing the same mobs at the previous level will be less. So depending on your situation, you may gain AAexp slower than you did before.

Case in point: I heard some that hurricane loroks in PoS give 8% AA exp per kill to a 65 ranger. Me, a 62 ranger, receives 10% AA exp per kill. No, I can't kill them quite as fast, but I get close.

The real test is your personal situation - grouping, soloing, the zone you're in- just time and see how long it takes to gain a single AA point.
