My experience with "normal mode" GROUP adventures has been exclusively in MIR, and I would not call them easy by any stretch of the imagination. There is quite a variance between the types of missions, so I would rank them in terms of difficulty (easiest to hardest):

1. exterminate X mobs
2. collect X items
3. assassinate Mr. X
4. rescue Mr. X

The assassinate and rescue ones would be easier if the the guy to assassinate/rescue was always in the same spot, but that would be pretty boring (only done one of each, so I have no idea if that is the case or not).

My standard group (which is equiped in gear similar to my own, if you look at my magelo) almost always wins our encounters, but they are often VERY close and we work VERY hard to do it. Its funny how an expansion can change how you play; our cleric has never nuked so much in his life, and I've actually been using quiessence all the time on the puller and MT (literally all the time -- we never stop moving). I was also somewhat surprised at the amount of DPS me and dogdog can put out during an adventure if we really work at it. In a normal group, I hover around 70 percent mana with some lulls and some spikes. In a LDoN raid, I hover at 0 -- and as soon as I get mana, its used either slowing or nuking. I don't even bother with malos at all -- I just cast and hope.

EDIT:

For those finding these group adventures too difficult, try these suggestions:

1. ignore all chests and/or "injured mob" traps
2. move in small jumps with your puller
3. pull multipes -- its why you have an enchanter
4. dps > all -- the mobs dont hit that hard, so dont kill yourself trying to slow them; if they resist twice, just nuke them down
5. dont stop -- if the cleric goes afk, you heal. if the tank goes afk, you tank (or someone else tanks).
6. never let them see you swea... oh, wrong advice
7. use dogdog -- he sucks alot less than you might think
8. use mage gear -- the cast time ring, damage bracer, and that earrring they summon are all good focus items
9. get your bane spell (i haven't yet; you know you want it though) -- that spell seems custom geared to doing alot of damage in a s hort amount of time during which you can canni for mana instead of just sit there waiting for your nukes to finish casting
10. canni-dance on the move -- my group thinks im insane, im sure, but I sit down for every tick that i can, even if we're moving from one location to another

The most important thing is to move like lightning. If you have someone pulling mobs to you over ANY distance, thats wasted time. If they pull singles, thats MULTIPLIED wasted time. If they try to counteract that by leaving early to pull the next mob, thats wasted DPS which translates to wasted time.
