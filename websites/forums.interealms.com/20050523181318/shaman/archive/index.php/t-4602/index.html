Athame
Sure FT3 works standing. Does a shaman ever sit anyway? :) Anyways, I'm not sure what math you expect because it depends on how much time you have to cast stuff and if you're being interrupted and such. But a tick is 6 seconds, casting time of canni 4 is 2.5 seconds, 3 second recast time, each cast gives 82 mana, so basically when you chain it it's 82 mana a tick versus 3 mana a tick from the boots. Let's say you don't have aa points and torpor heals 4 ticks of 300 hp for 200 mana. Considering regen you can probably cast about 9 canni 4's, giving 9*82=738, minus 200 from torpor is 538 mana in about 10 ticks, 1 minute. That's 53 mana a tick, on top of buffs songs and other FT gear. To gain 538 extra mana with the boots you need 179 ticks, in other words 18 minutes. Granted, you have to keep casting for it, but the ability to gain mana this fast even if it's not constantly, outweighs it by far. I hope this (on the fly, not sure if its totally correct) math shows that an oracle with torpor simply does not have mana problems as long as they keep casting. You simply do not have that option without torpor, unless you have a personal heal slave. I bet it is often said on this forum, but for mana regen, torpor > all.

Edit: let me add that without any aa points or other gear according to the above math torpor+canni4 has the potential to give you mana 18 times faster than those boots. But with aa points in buff duration, quick buff, canni 5 or gear like mana preservation (not really high priority).. this will only get better. AA points will have no effect on the effectiveness of those boots, in fact, FT points are capped. And, eventually, since mana won't be a problem, you want boots with ac/hp or some more useful effect anyway.
