3 vox
3.1 intro to vox

the dragon that raids fear ... well in a duo she is the easiest of the 2 ... now you might say
that i am forgetting that she can CH her self ... well i am not forgetting it but vox is unable
to cast anything when she does not have any mana left ... this fight normaly takes around 10min
and in the end vox does not have any mana left for CHing ... however unlike naggy vox does not
run at low hps ( well it happened to me once so i will give it about 3% chance )
vox have a lower dps than naggy but allot more hp or ac since it takes abit longer to kill her with
the same set up and i never used logges to check which of the 2 made a diff

3.2 moving and clearing to vox

map
http://www.jestersinnorrath.co.uk/GalleryImageFullSize.asp?lUserID=389&lImageID=28

looking at the map you come in at point A from everfrost. normally i use this spot to buff up / rebuff
now make a pet if you dont have one buff it ( fa, haste, regen and focus ) and give it 2 1hand weps
suspend the pet if you have suspend minion2. invis up and move both chars to point B. just run for
it and ignor any goblins that might aggro. kill them when you get to point B
if you do not have suspend minion just run and kill the few extra goblins at point B

Point B is where the giant grounds start. the thing to remember with the giants is that
you have to be in melee range before you can cast anything that will stick (unresistable spell will stick)
moving from point B to C you have to clear about 4 giants and 1 giant spider if you do it right.
move might add if your are not carefull. on the first try it might be an idea to clear the giants
in the room south west of point C to make sure they will not add later on when pulling

the best thing to do about the giants is just to slow them. use epic and bane
( they have high dr / pr resists ) and your pet to kill them. and just canni your mana up
while fighting them
have the shaman do the tanking and the monk just hitting them from behind along with the pet

when you get to point C bind the shaman ( atleast until you have tried it a few times )
the reason for the bind is to make sure you dont have to run back from the ent if you make a bad
pull and get banished out of the zone

the main pull spot is at point D. it is here the shaman will wait while the monk pulls
the shaman needs to be located so it can see around the corner but still be behind the it
meaning you can move to the corner and have half the screen filled with the wall and the other
seeing down the corridor

the main pull tactic is the monk tags and then runs like hell to point C and wait for the FD
call from the shaman

the mobs will be charging down the corridor from point E to point D. the FD needs to happen
when one or a few of the mobs have turned the corner so the shaman can see them the monk FDs
and the shaman then tags them. then just slow them and kill them and then repeat
until the lair is cleared

there is about 3 icecy terror mobs located before the entrance to the lair but dont be foolled
by the fact that they are outside ... the giants including vox will chain aggro

if a FD fails have the monk run strait up to the lair again and keep trying to FD the mobs off
if the monk it dying you can allways just jump down one of the trap doors in the floor this will
give you a few more secs to live in and get a FD off if you use the ones west and north of point E

when the lair have been cleared of everything but vox ( about 5 to 6 giants and 1 icy terror )
move up to point E. rebind the shaman near the wall. mem gate
rebuff every shaman buff on the monk and then med / canni to full mana.
when ready drink the DS potion. click off the 4th junk buff
and replace it with a Q. then click the stone door and run into the lair with the monk
start the resist diciplin. aggro vox so she will follow you. then run back near the door
and try and fight close to that spot since you will be in casting range of the shaman

while fighting only use mend and stonestance if you get into a bad run and potions
if you have used them allready. though the hole fight chain hit a hotkey with
the ornate glove buff so it is allways activ and filling the 1st buff slot
personally i keyed the gloves to the numpad number 0 and used my left hand to hit
it all the time while shifting my right between the 2 mouses

about 30 secs after the monk have entered the lair recast the Q on the monk
get banished out of the zone. as soon as you are able gate back to point E.
then as soon as you are back at point recast the Q and repeast this action until vox
have been slain

4 final goal
the main goal of this should be to get in and out again in under 45min
and in total to kill both dragon in under 1.5 hours from where you enter either PF or solA
while not using a single heal potion and only doing this as a single person controlling 2 chars
