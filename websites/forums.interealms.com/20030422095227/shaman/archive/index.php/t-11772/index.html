Best advice I can give you is to window shop for a week without buying anything at all. Why? because you get the best picture of what is out there, as well as an idea of prices.

This has helped me hugely in the past. Two of my first great bargain buys were Chokidai Hide Spaulders (250pp) and a Rust Encrusted Bracer (150pp). Should have been 3k for the pair on our server.

So that would be my advice - don't buy a specific item, buy a bargain. You can even sell it again, rinse repeat and end up with a hell of a lot more than 4k to upgrade with :)

Chitinous belts are cheap on my server. Something you might want to look at. So are Goranga Idols. You could also camp a Gantru Charm for range slot with a duo partner or small group in UP.

Consider replacing the cloak with an Arctic Wyvern. HP>wisdom. You should be able to sell or trade at about an even cost.

Consider replacing the platinum fire wedding ring with a shadel bandit ring. Another easily camped item, so I wouldn't recommend buying it.

Your gear doesn't look that bad for your level. I also recommend browsing for a week (or longer) to get a feel for market prices and snag any true bargains you can. Be careful though, as LoY items come out, some item prices will crash with very little notice. If you aren't comfortable playing professional trader though, just browse and don't buy.

Good luck shopping!
