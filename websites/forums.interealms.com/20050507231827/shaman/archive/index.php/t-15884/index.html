The level 61 spell True Spirit summons a level 58 pet. The level 55 spell Spirit of the Howler summons a level 40 pet.

A level 58 mob is much more likely to hit another level 58 mob for full damage whereas a level 40 mob is unlikely to hit a level 58 mob for much damage at all. That is the difference you can expect to see. Of course, if you are fighting a level 62 mob such as the Crystalline Golems in PoV, neither is likely to be all that effective but the level 58 pet will live longer.
