Being on a server like that sounds like so much fun.

Since you don't have Malo or Malos yet, you are probably just as well off slowing first. Malosini and Slow have the same resist checks, so if you can land malosini you could have landed slow as well. The golems need disease slow, if you have it yet (can't remember what level it is).

You'll probably figure out pretty quick how resistant everything is, and you may decide to start trying malosini first because of lower aggro if slow bounces alot.

For soloing, you might try poking around the dreadlands or Burning wood, those should be pretty decent in the low 50s.

Goldark
You might find the Amygdalen warriors quite hard to slow at 52, I'd suggest you open with your malosini line (not sure which you have then) just to be sure you don't get aggro, and then alternate malosini with slow until one or both land. Other mobs, except the named/mini-named, are easy to slow. Take some MR gear if you can to resist the toad's fear, which can be fatal for the raid sometimes.

Hardest part in fear is the break-in, try to let the tanks get aggro as you're not much use if you die from slow aggro on the second mob. I'm sure the more experianced of you will give clear instructions on the break-in strategy.

For hunting, Old Sebilis is good at 52 and you can farm some good cash there from the gems, plus a few good items. I'd get in the crypt at every oportunity, if you can take out Emp then you'll be getting some nice spells that your guild will be needing soon.

Other nice xp places might be PoHate (if you have a wizzy friend), The Hole, Karnor's Castle if its not a train fest (getting a bit low for you now though) and of course if you can get access (not sure if that's the flagging problem you refer to?) then tier 1 PoP is just coming into your level range too, and is great xp if you have a good enough group to keep going there.

Kael arena is a nice raid target with the quest armour, but you seem to need to stay a while to get past the streaky droprate. Or for single groups you can probably solo the easier mobs around the arena for faction and xp and the odd chance of a drop.
