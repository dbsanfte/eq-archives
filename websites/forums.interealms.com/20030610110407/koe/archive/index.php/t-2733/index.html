Jice Virago
This is the same thing I posted on our board...Bored, scattered, and with little to do, saturday was starting to look like another wasted day. Then Rizz sends me a tell and we talk. He wants to kill Nag. Nag isn't up, but I say "How about Vox?" to which he says sure. KotE hasn't had the best track record against Vox and its been eons since we have done her, but I am salivating at the chance to take out an old school dragon with alts so I log on Jezebelle and start gathering the troops. KotE people arrive and Rizz shows up eventually and we get started.

Attempt #1- I totally blew this one. I didn't check with my trackers to ensure the lair was clear and I mistimed the seiver charge. End result was Vox CHed like 4 times before the spank finally finished. I knew, however, looking at how hard we were chewing her down, that we had her at this point.

Attempt#2- Textbook perfection. We even had a main tank for most of the fight (Fenna) holding agro. She never got off a single heal. I knew she was oom and that was it, she was hitting the canvass.

Loots were pretty solid on the average. Fortunately we are running with a crowd that is not out to screw us here, but I feel like we made out like bandits thanks to Rizz's overwhelming generosity. The loots were-

White Dragon Scale- Greyeyes for Bard Epic
Warhammer of Divine Grace- Serephima
Torbin's Mystical Eyepatch- Melrin
Some gay Treasure Pouch- Modari/Morn
Icecommet- Orren

I would like to thank KotE for the privilage of leading this run. Running big fights like this and doing with 26 what takes others 50+ is why I play this game. Thank you again, Rizz and everyone.
