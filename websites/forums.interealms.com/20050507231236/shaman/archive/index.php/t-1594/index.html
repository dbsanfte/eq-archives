Henora
well troll and iksar both have innate regen.
and both can wear a fungi tunic.

So yes, they are "better" than a vah shir in an iksar CP.
In terms that they can regen faster. This is important to a shaman because at level 24 we get the spell cannibalize. Then our HP = our Mana.

Actual regen numbers are
Fungus Tunic / Fungus Patch Vest = 15hp per tick.
Iksar CP = 5hp per tick

Iksar/Troll innate regen....well I am a barb, I don't remember what it is exactly... something like...at level 60 it is:
12hp per tick standing
18hp per tick sitting

All this being said, play whatever you like. If your goal is have fun with an interesting race, try iksar or vah shir. If your goal is to level as fast as possible then I would say troll. If I was starting over untwinked I would choose iksar. Keep in mind that Vah Shir CAN use the fungus patch vest, it is just not as common or accessible. IF you aren't going to level your shaman really quickly, it might be more common on the market by the time they can wear it (level 51). If you are going for maximum speed, use a race that can wear the fungi from level 1.

I can't tell you a troll is 3x better because they can wear a tunic that regens 3x more. They also have an hefty exp penalty. Vah Shir have no exp penalty... so, that should be taken into account as well.
