Greetings fellow Shamans,

Those who are sick of tabbing screens while playing Everquest just to see what they need for their epic/alchemy or handwriting it down (Then losing the sheet or making a typo), or doing towns of print outs (And we know how expensive and time consuming that is), there is a new way to view the information while playing the game just by a click of a button.

All you need to do is download these files by right clicking on the Blue link below and press 'Save Target As...' into your storyline folder in Everquest (E.g. C:\Everquest\Storyline). Then after downloading this go to Stories window while playing EQ to view them.

Alchemy (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Alchemy.txt) - Gives a list of recipes, trivial, effects when used, duration when used, and costs per dose

Epic Notes (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_0.txt) - This is information that you need to be read BEFORE you start your epic

Epic Part 1 (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_1.txt) - Beginning the Quest.

Epic Part 2 (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_2.txt) - The Drunken Shaman

Epic Part 3 (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_3.txt) - The Test of Patience

Epic Part 4 (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_4.txt) - The Test of Wisdom.

Epic Part 5 (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_5.txt) - The Test of Might.

Epic Part 6 (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_6.txt) - The City of Mist Reports.

Epic part 7 (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_7.txt) - The Three Books of Lord Ghiosk.

Epic Part 8 (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_8.txt) - Obtaining the High Scale Icon.

Epic Part 9 (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_9.txt) - High Scale Kirn.

Final Part (http://www.angelfire.com/magic/everfan/Shaman/story_Shaman_Epic_-_10.txt) - The Final Steps To Complete Your Epic.

Please note that all information was taken from http://www.eqshaman.com/ and all credit to the original makers who spent their time writing down the guides just to help us lil people! I just converted the text to be viewed in story mode. Also if you want to view all the Epic parts in order while playing EQ then download them in order. Reason is that EQ Storyline goes by the dates and times when it has been downloaded/Saved and not by Alphabetical order.
