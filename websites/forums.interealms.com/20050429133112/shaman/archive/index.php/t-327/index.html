Nurga is great at your level, awesome experience and its very easy to get to the spiders, you can level here to 48.

Cast Sow and Levitate before entering the mines. Now Cast Shrink twice and Cast invisable then run up the waterfall.

From their pretty much follow the hall, you will come to an open area with railroad tracks, just keep going straight, the opening is slightly left.

From their follow the pathway again and then you will come to another large open area, if you look straight and slightly left again you will see another opening, at this opening you MIGHT see a spider their, becareful passing it as some spiders can see invisable, once you get thru the opening you will see 2 spawns on your left then go a few steps further you will see 2 spawns on your right.

If you go alittle further you will see a sleeping ogre which is a monk quest npc, if he agros kill it, not your fault, just blame on the imaginary guy who was their before you. ;)

Pass the sleeping ogre to your right lies 8 more spawns (2 spawn, 3 spawn and 3 spawn), this one can be alittle tricky as you might pull 3 so you have to becareful.

Best way to break the spawns is to enstill one, use it to pull the other(s), keep it enstill to the first is killed. Once you kill one then enstill the it again the camp out to kill agro and break the spawn.

If you have to many and cant handle it just enstill them all and camp out to kill agro or bind outside and gate.

Try to get the 4 spawn at your level, when you get alittle higher you can single pull some, then you can go to the other spawns. With no JBB and at your level I was doing the 4 spawns steady and the other spawns at 47 I can do the 5 out of the 8 spawn easy till 48, you can probaly stay to 49 but the exp starts to slow down a bit, this is when I went to Spiroc island in TD but thats another threade. :)

Good Luck

House
Spiroc Procax = Spiroc Proven (rogue)
Spiroc Lighthealer = Spiroc Lightbringer (paladin)

It's true that the Warriors (who are actually shamans) and, even moreso, Lightbringers are tougher than the other Spirocs, but with a Shadowknight or Necromancer in my group we had no trouble with these guys, even at Level 45. The Warriors are somewhere around Level 40-45, and the Lightbringers are always Level 50. Just fear the Lightbringers and you'll be okay. They drop Level 51+ Kunark spells and Level 49 spell research components.

Solo it's another matter: I didn't start soloing the Warriors until around Level 48, and, though I was able to solo the Lightbringers at Level 50 with pet kiting, it would take about 10 minutes and really wasn't worth it.
