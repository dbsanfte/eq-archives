Niall Graycloak
Swap the serpent bracer for a Chipped Bone Bracer - cheap and usually available in the bazaar

Swap the boots for Kobold Hide - same as above

Unless you are nuking a lot, replace the Cloak with an imbued northman ringmail or acrylia chain cloak - pretty cheap from any decent barbarian smith

If you want more AC, you can probably get a Jaundiced Bone helm cheap as well

Also watch for a Dustscryers crystal ball - mana conservation II and usually cheap in the bazaar

Most of the crystal chitin armor is good, but its rather expensive as well. As for places to go, you might be able to start hunting in Netherbian, South Karana is an old favorite, Mistmoore or Sol A if you prefer dungeons.
Carrieanne
You may want to find a Mana Conservation 2/3 item for the reason that you can swap it in/out for buffing or casting pet later. This will not lose you ac/hp this way but you'll gain a mana saving on all your spells cast - slows, dots, heals, buffs you name it.

I would swap the Howling into the primary slot as it will proc stun.. (I used a howling in prim, SCH in range till I got my epic).

Hmm.. I still think that a little more wis wouldn't hurt but thinking about it further you'll have obscene regen for a level 24/25 shm.
