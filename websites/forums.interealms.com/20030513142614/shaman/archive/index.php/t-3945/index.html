Bytorg
To answer your first question: Depending on how far away you are from the critter that you're killing, the damage might not update on your screen correctly.

For example, if you rooted a monster and cast a DoT or two on him it might look like he hasn't taken any damage at all. Then when you cast a nuke or get close enough to him for your client to update with his current hit points, you'll see his life drop quite quickly.

Regarding the second question: I don't recall what the exact damage of the proc is, but I believe the Velium weapons have a small proc that will work at level 20.

Another option would be to do the Screaming Mace quest (or buy it if you're an evil race). The proc on that weapon is Yaulp which adds AC and STR to you. It's a fairly decent item and having a little extra AC from the proc will help you as build up your melee skills.

Something to keep in mind: Soloing between levels 16-24 (or so) is fairly painful. You'll probably find that most critters will take most of your mana (and a good chunk of your health) to kill at those levels.
