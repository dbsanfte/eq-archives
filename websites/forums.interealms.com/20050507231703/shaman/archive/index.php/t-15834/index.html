Jinheim
Get your MR up as high as you possibly can, or else you are going to spend the fight stunned and blinded.

You said that the pally has high MR, FR, and PR. You left off DR, so try landing disease slow on him. Paladins have the ability to cure disease, but I don't think any paladin in the history of everquest has remembered this and actually cured a slow during a duel.

If you can get disease slow to land, you can probably get disease dots on as well. Do that. Have a pet and a DS potion up, and just stand there and heal yourself. Hopefully he will burn all his mana trying to stun and blind you unsuccessfully and not have enough left over to heal himself. Also dispell all his buffs (he gets a self-only haste spell and decent HP buffs), and watch for him to use his heal over time on himself and try to dispell it.

Paladins with great gear and a lot of AA are probably one of the hardest classes to kill. At high end, they use almost exclusively 2hander weapons, which means a DS potion isn't going to do much to them. With EP gear, they usually have enough worn regen to almost completely negate the DS damage they are taking. On top of that, if they geared themselves to have a lot of FT (some knights do, some don't), they will have enough mana regen to heal themselves almost indefinately. On top of that, a lot of their weapons proc a heal or rune effect, which causes problems for obvious reasons. The biggest problem I have ever had with knights, though, is the freaking twohanded bash AA. I have serious difficulties channel even a 3 second quiescence through a bashing paladin.

Basically, paladins are not as dangerous as shadowknights, since pallys don't get any uber low-resist nukes dots or snares. With 210 unbuffed MR, I have never had a paladin land a spell on me. However, although a paladin cannot kill you quickly, they can last for a very very long time in a duel and eventually wear you down. Remember: if you go oom, you're screwed, but if a paladin goes oom, he can still melee.

That is how I would beat a well-equipped paladin anyway.
