It's good for shamans too, because the wisdom is so high you can drop a lot of items that you're keeping on you mostly for wisdom. There are not many alternatives out there for a shaman that come close to this shield in terms of AC and wisdom unless you are looking at stuff from NTOV or off a few highly-contested mobs.

AC 25 and 20 wisdom is a very nice combination, and it even has 12 sv magic on it. Unless you're going against fire and cold casting mobs, it's often superior to the SBS even though it has no hitpoints on it, and the crystal chitin shield isn't even close -- that shield has an AC only of what, 19? And a wisdom of 8 or 9, which can't compare to a wisdom score of 20 at all.

Not to mention it looks about 1000 times better than either one. ;) My ogre shaman looks ridiculous with most shields on; they look like overgrown cufflinks.

I sold one shield for 35k and the other traded for a value of about 30k.

It may be hard to do so consistently, though. There are a lot of people who think that's way too much money, but they wouldn't want to have to camp the thing, either. So you have a market which can't make up its mind on what the shield is really worth.
