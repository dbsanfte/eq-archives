This was mentioned a long time ago.

Many of the PoP zones are keyed. So if your die in a keyed area and can't find enough people with keys to help you get your corpse back, you're kind of screwed.

Enter the graveyard. At some point before the res timer expires, your corpse will move to that zone's graveyard, which is outside the keyed area. That way you can get it back and maybe get a res.
