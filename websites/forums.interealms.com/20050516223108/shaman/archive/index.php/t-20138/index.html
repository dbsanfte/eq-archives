Jinheim
You can't beat a time geared bard unless he sucks. The best effort you can do is just to chain cast spear of torment or your poison rains until he drains you of mana, and then run away.

Wizard - It sucks really bad to fight wizards because of their clicky rune. It is hard to do enough damage to kill the rune and then do any significant damage to the wizard before he recasts the rune. On the bright side, he has no way to heal himself. On the downside, wizards mana regen is pretty impressive and you'll never really truly run him oom. He'll be able to hit you with a lure every now and then. Use poison nuke/rain.

Druid - A very long and boring fight. All they need is a little mana and they can pretty much CH themselves. It's like a cleric that can nuke hard and has unresistable dots. You're going to want to keep your first few buff slots open so that you can dispell their MR dots, and you're going to want to try to stay high mana. Once they start to slow down and you think they're near oom, burn them as hard as you can. Spirit Call pets are pretty effective against them and can keep them from channeling their CH, but of course there is still nature's infusion. The druid should never be able to kill you, but it is going to take you a very long time to kill them.

Necro - I duel a necro in my guild regularly and it usually ends with one of us getting bored after a 30 minute stalemate. I think in the end I would win, but it takes forever. They heal themselves with nearly unresistable lifetaps (although with enough MR you can start partially resisting them and even a few full resists), and their mana regen is better than yours. Their pet is also annoying, but pretty easy to kill. If they have that time wand that casts the rune, then it is going to be really hard to kill them. Like a druid, try to conserve mana and do a spirit call + velium strike burn once you think they're low enough of mana that they cant chain LT. You're going to want to keep your first few buff slots open so that you can dispell pyrocur (their EP pants clicky that almost all time necros will have) and whatever else he manages to land on you. Also, you can try to dispell him and get rid of his lich. It costs 5 mana and is fast to recast, but if you just constantly dispell it it will hurt his mana regen and really piss him off.
