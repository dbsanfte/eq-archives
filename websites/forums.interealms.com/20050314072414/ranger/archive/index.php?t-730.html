I'm normally a lurker, but as i've had plenty of experience here, i'll share my thoughts. I play both a ranger (53) wizard (59). I"ve been on raptor isle doing my thing for a LONG time. Once raptors were made fearable i've even brought my ranger there.

This is on Zeb, so i've no clue on how raptors are on other servers. You have generally 2 camps.

Large - 8 static spawns 2 roamers. 1 static is on far side of island and generally left alone.

Small - 1 roamer, 5 static

1 person CAN hold all of Large (8 spawn) leaving the single. Done this many times. If you can hold them, it is considered a camp.

2. Same goes for small, if you want all 6 they are yours.

So two people can monpolize this area, if its taken, you do something else.

Exceptions.

Ask to split the large - sometimes someone is nice and says sure, i don't need to do them all. I'll share. But its always been at their discretion. Spliting large is usually only done with other quaders as the spawn cycle gives just enough time for one to finish before the other goes back out for the second. If split with a ranger or any single class that doesnt quad you'd probably not finish it in time before 2nd set repop and be in a good bit of trouble. (seen it happen)

Ask small if he's taking the 2 leftover static, if not, they could be yours. Ask Large if he's taken the farside static. Normally even if somone is on large taking 8 and someone on small. Those three are usually left alone. These are the ones that my ranger usually pounces on, as they are out of the pull path of the others. And if you do happen to mess up, they won't path over the safe spot on their way back home.

Being as they are camp so fiercely, its hard to get a spot unless you're there at the right time. Buy my ranger has never had a problem geting those last three. However IF a ranger is there, it is possible to hold small island and keep it clean. Doesnt' matter if you're not a druid or wizzy, bard or whatever. YOu're there, they are yours, end of story.

Honestly i've never had any bad dealings with anyone there. If you know the area, how to keep mobs away from that ever so sacred safe spot, you'll never step on any toes.

As to "how can somone can claim such a large area". Well, groups claim large number camp spots in many zones, for xp , loot etc. It's recognized as acceptable camp because well, that's what it is. Raptors are the same way as any other zone, there are rules to the camp. If you follow them, regardless of class or group (yess i've even seen a cleric/monk/rogue do large) your camp will be respected as will you be.
