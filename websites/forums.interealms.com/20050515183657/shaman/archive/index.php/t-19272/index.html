Chutoi
It's only 32 people flagged in raid for you to be able to get unflagged in zone. At that point, you can get 4 unflagged in, I believe. From there on out, I believe you can get in 15% of your raid as unflagged, up to maximum of 10 unflagged in a raid of 70 or 72.

Raid exceptions to flags do not apply to keyed areas. As noted by previous posters, lower CoD (bertoxx area) and PoEb are keyed and cannot be entered with the raid feature if you are missing the keys.

There are some problems with the raid feature. First, if you form up the raid in a different zone, it has a tendency to get bugged. Thus, if you form the raid in Potranq, then zone into PoFire, unflagged may or may not be able to get in, even if you wait 5 minutes for the raid to register in PoFire. As a general rule, form the raid up in the zone you wish to raid.

Another problem with the raid feature is linked zones. For instance, H0Hb and PoEb are good examples. To get unflagged characters in there (assuming they aren't high enough to zone into HoH by themselves since restrictions were removed), you have to form a raid in HoH or PoE, get the unflagged inside, then run to the portal of HoHb or PoEb. If it's HoHb, the best method I've noticed is to reform the raid in HoHb, then send someone out to invite the unflagged back in HoHa. If the unflagged are in danger of getting booted (by the three minute rule), have them camp out at 1 minute remaining, then camp back in. With the majority of your people in HoHb, you can invite them into the raid and they can zone across with no problems.

PoEb is a little different. They can zone across if they have a key, but you have to have the raid in PoEa for an unflagged person to get into PoEa or PoEb. Additionally, a keyed person (if unflagged for PoEa) used to be able to stay in PoEb without a raid. However, this was changed so that even if keyed, an elementally unflagged person requires a raid to stay in PoEb. Form the raid in PoEa, get unflagged in, have them zone into PoEb first and then camp out at 1 minute left. When all unflagged are in PoEb, then zone the rest of the (flagged) raid over and invite the keyed but unflagged back to raid.

Never be afraid to reform raids, but one important note: A PLANAR PROJECTION for flags IS TIED TO THE RAID. Don't reform to try to hail a pp!

Mixelplux
Actually, there are a few nuances most don't know about, and Chutoi addressed one of those.

Flagshare spots as noted above are limited to 15% of your total raid force, with a minimum of 32 in the raid. If you are doing a calculation (# in raid * 15%) always round down.

Flagshare spots will NOT be regained if a flagshare person leaves the raid or zone. For the instance of the raid, when a flagshare spot is taken once, it's gone. Nothing but reforming the raid will give you back flagshare spots. That is why, as Chutoi mentioned above, forming a raid in the zone you will want to raid is a must. Use a group leader to zone out and grab people so they can zone in.

One person can use up multiple flagshare spots. Most notable is HoH and HoHb and Sol Ro and Sol Ro's chamber. A person lacking both flags, will use up 2 flagshare spots. There are ways around that by reforming the raids as you move through the zones and resetting your flagshare count, but it's time consuming.

Raids and Flagshares can be a nightmare at times. There are still bugs with it, but if you keep in mind to always reform a raid fresh when you change zones, it keeps them to a minimum.
