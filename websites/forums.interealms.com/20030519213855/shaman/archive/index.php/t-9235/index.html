Csin Csity
Script took 12 minutes exactly

From the doorway to the tunnel, all I saw was the cloud of bones standing on the stage. Then after a while VS showed up. They sat there looking at each other for a while, then finally started fighting. Fight took forever and a day, and since I wasn't close enough to 'hear' the action, I still to this day don't know what the exchange was between the two of them.

Finally VS won, became targetable, and I ran in and pulled VS and a few of the arisen followed.

It was a long wait. However, this same wait gave my team hiding out at the other end of the tunnel plenty of time to heal, buff, and med to full. When I finally pulled VS and the arisen they didn't stand a chance.

Be patient, the quest takes forever to finish, a few more minutes can't hurt.

Good luck. Even though folks may argue that there are some better range items available in PoP/Sleepers/whatever, this is an incredibly uber item that a dedicated individual can do almost 100% on her own. By the time you are doing the final fights for 6,7&8 you've had plenty of time to network up the teams needed, and in a 'family-style' guild, even folks in their 40's can make a real contribution. IMHO one of the best thought-out quests in the whole game.
