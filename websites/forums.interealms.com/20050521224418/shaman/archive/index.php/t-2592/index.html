With the new high end smithing changes, there are actually some nice player-made leggings available.

For instance, the stats on Artificer's Ringmail Leggings (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=12476) are pretty comparable:

JB legs: AC 24, +8 Wis, +75 Mana, +8 Str, +8 Dex, right-click Health, Wt 7.5
AR legs: AC 22, +5 Wis, +40 Mana, +40 HP, +10 Str, Wt 3

AR legs are Barbarian/Tribunal only, but there equivalents for other races, too.

Since the smithed stuff is still new, prices vary widely. On a thread here I saw someone got a set for just under 15k. I asked around last night and was given a quote of 30k. At 15k, I think they're worth it (I could sell my existing legs to pay for most of it.)

On Karana, JB legs go for between 20-30k, but they don't come up very often... and there are a few people always flooding the trade channel with "WTB JB legs!!"

Good luck.
