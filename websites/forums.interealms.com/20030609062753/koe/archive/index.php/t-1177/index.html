Looks good so far. I have been back through here the other night and some memories came back. Respawn rate is fast in here and it promises to be a fast paced night.

There are 4 basic mobs in Icewell Keep for those of you who have never been. All will summon, so it can be nasty on healers/casters.

Ice Spikes are tentacles as your standard caster type mob that likes to hurl you away and then summon you back. These are not messable, but can be charmed. They will be the first target of choice when confronted with more than one mob. If we have two, will probably go with an add tank, given our force mix.

Frosty Cubes are a melee mob that can be mezzed.

Skeletons are also down there and are standard melee undead. Also mezzable

Golems are standard golems found elsewhere. Resistant to magic (but you can get it to stick) hard to mez. These will be the second mob of choice behind the tentacles.

All have typical velious type hps.. (a lot).

Gem drops have been good down here and also there are some good spells that drop here as well (including improved camo/invis).

To Confirm: This is on Tuesday, 12 June and starts at 7 CST. Please be on time.. :)
