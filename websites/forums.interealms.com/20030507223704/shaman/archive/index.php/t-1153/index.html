Shabby
I just spend the last 2 levels in Nurga, but I went at lv. 44.

The Spiders in Nurga remain an EXCELLENT shaman soloing spot for lv. 44-47. Head into Nurga, shrink yourself, and run up waterfall to the right. Cast invis on yourself and run to 1st spider cave, right by the Sleeping Orge (Spiders caves marked with a #1 on EqAtals.com map). Pull up a pet, buff up. I kill the ogre to get him out of the way (he is for monk epic, 2-3 hour respawn, lv. 30).

Now, take out the spiders. Cast resist poison on yourself, maybe resist magic (Strathmore spiders occassionally spawn here and they are necros) , haste, and usual other buffs. There is a 2-spawn right past the sleeping ogre. Sometimes you are lucky and casting on one will not pull both. If you pull both, root park one and kill the other fast. Once you have broken this 2-spawn, go around the corner and break the 2-spawn there. I found it fairly easy to keep the 4 spawns broken, no JBB or uber equipment. I FLEW thru levels 44 and 45 here.
