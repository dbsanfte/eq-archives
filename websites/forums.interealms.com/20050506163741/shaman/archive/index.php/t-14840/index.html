Since you're in VT, look out for:

Book of Contemplation (15% haste to all spells)
Nightcaller Leggings (30% haste to beneficial spells, think it's the fastest beneficial haste available... REALLY nice item)
Lightbringer's Earring (20% haste to detrimental spells)
Mask of Dark Thoughts (18% haste to all spells)
