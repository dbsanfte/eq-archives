Hehe... cat's hav no exp penalty and Barbs hav a 5% penalty.

Yu shud definately melee IN TEH FACE so ta speak. Da important ting is ta keep yer combat skills, particularly Defence, maxed. Uddawise, wen a mob breaks root, it gonna cum wailing down on ya wif yu as tuff as a Barbie doll. Latah on in life, it gets harder ta keep skills maxed so best ta start early and keep da habit.

In terms of weapons, der be a few 1hb options until ya get ta 40+ or so. Den dem seem ta dry up. Methinx da last time me used a 1hb woz wen me woz about 30th in (da old) Cazic Thule. Yer best bet be ta try and keep piercing and 2hb maxed each level. Der be sum fun 2hb weaps out der (nuttin like hittin fer 100+ regularly :)). Melee ain't one of our strong suits. Our main reason fer bein IN TEH FACE is ta tank fer puppy. At higher levels, startin at about 29-34, we get haste and slow and our melee is just like anudda DoT. Even latah, wen yu get yer beloved JBB, yu'll hav one swing in atween each blast so might as well make it a gud'un wif a meaty 2hb.

As fer yer question about AC or Wis, da answer be most likely HP, den Mana, den AC, den Wis. It all depends on why yer dieing however. If yu be dying wif mana left den HP and AC be da way ta go. If yu constantly runnin OOM, den Mana and Wis be da answer. It'll change wen ya hit 24 and get Cannibalize but until den, yu probly best ta go HP and AC. Wis drops outta da sky latah on so dinna worry 'bout it.
