Roxxanne
Breath of Ultor, Blood of Saryrn, Epic Dot, Bane, and Anathema do not break root because there is no initial DD component. That makes them superior for root rotting.
Valsuvious
yeah, it's the DD component in the spells that break root. Meaning, the second that the dot lands, it has a chance to break root, but will not break it once the dot is on (mobs get a chance to break root every so many seconds, so it's not the dot running on him that breaks it).
Litgruv
Well technicaly every tick has a chance of breaking root, but yea, those listed above have no DD component.
weissblau
If I get bored waiting and decide to use dots that have an initial DD component, I generally try using the disease dots because since they last longer, their DD component occurs less often :-)

If I'm VP-rooting I wait until VP is going to break anyway, then cast a dot right before it refreshes. It's great that you can get a countdown timer by clicking on the VP button when VP is not available yet.
