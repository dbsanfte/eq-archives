Huth
If you can get yourself to Hollowshade when the owlbears are up you can probably pick up the head and range items i have, they drop of the named owlbear prince and people are usually killing them to get perfect owlbear pelts for the rallic pack quest.

If you have PoP the quest for the Antique Ring is easy and most things (if not all) can be found on traders in PoK and the Bazaar.

There are some nice items that can be found for cheap in the bazaar, my giant scalemail arms were only 180pp.

Depending on how much time you are willing to stay in the bazaar checking stuff every 10-20 mins or so you can pick up some real bargins..

From my profile

Reinforced Drafgonhorn boots : 200pp (on AB usually 700-1k)
Chipped Bone Bracelet : 60pp (on AB usually 300-500)
Elder Wolf Hide : 500pp (on AB usually 1k-2k)
Shadel Bandit Ring : 500pp (on AB usually 2k-3.5k) very lucky with this one tho and i spent 7hrs in the bazaar whilst doing other **** in the house and checking evertime i walked passed the pc..
Earring of Twsited Leaves : 500pp (on AB usually 1k-2k)

A forest loop earring is always usually cheap around 300pp if not a little less. Serpentine Bracers are usually dirt cheap (50pp) and has Extended Affliction II and has nicer stats than your ivory bracelet.

Anyway i hope some of that was of help.
