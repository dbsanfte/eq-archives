AtommWarbird
I routinely 4box on a P4 2500 Mhz with 1GB of RAM. Each active EQ instance takes around 400 MB of RAM if you have the Luclin Models turned on, 300ish if you turn them off. You can probably get away with 3 instances as long as you turn off all the Luclin models (except the min each char may need to ride his/her horse) and social animations.

You'll have to look down on the 2nd and 3rd toons in places with wide open spaces (PoTran) or places with lots of people (PoK, Bazaar) but other than that you should be OK. I experience occasional pauses on one of the chars. Zoning takes about 50% longer for each char.

The good news is that if, like me, you're multi-boxing LDoN's you shouldn't have any real problems. Tight indoor zones with no one else other than who you bring with you makes it a breeze.

Good luck!

AtommWarbird
Grimreaper made a very good point about turning the sound off. From what I've been able to tell, having sound GREATLY increases the resources needed by EQ. Since I can't live without sound at all I only turn it off my 2nd, 3rd, and fourth instances. (I do VERY occasinally 5box but to be honest it's a bit more taxing on my nerves than I can usaully stand.) If I have the sound on even a 2nd instance I get a lot of lag, sputtering and freezing.

To REALLY turn the sound off (i.e. EQ doesn't even think about when it should be playing them) put a file called nosound.txt in the root of our EQ instance.

Hope this helps!
