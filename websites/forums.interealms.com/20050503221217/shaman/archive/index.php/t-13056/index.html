Gardeltron
I theorize, and ...That Verant or SOE as they are now known,... may have thought that shamans were too powerful.

Very good point Gag. Since before SoL, I and a number of other shamans, kept insisting that shamans were not as powerful as many posters on these boards were apt to claim. The combination of Turgur, Malo, Torpor, JBB, and Fungi Tunic, all Kunark additions to the shaman's tool box, produced a powerful class. Add in Bane of Nife, Pox of Bertoxxulous, and a pretty decent pet and shamans were very powerful.

After Velious, it became obvious that EQ developers sought to level the playing field. This perception of shamans as the most powerful class was evident in SoL as well. It's not that shamans didn't increase in power in these expansions. Rather, other classes increased in power faster than shamans.

Finally, with PoP, shamans have been put in their place. At level 65, we are in many ways much weaker than most other classes. PoP is about keeping high DPS players alive for short periods of time. The shaman contribution to this formula boils down to buffs. We make a decent contribution to hp of players, top off melee relevant stats (primarily str and dex) and then hang around to watch the mobs die. Our heals are underpowered relative to other priests, our DoTs too slow, and our DD's irrelevant. In this thread we've shown what has happened to shaman soloing ability.

So shamans have been solidly nerfed at level 65. Enjoy the lower levels while you can guys.
