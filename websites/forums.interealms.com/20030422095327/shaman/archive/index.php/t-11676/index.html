Get the horse.

It will increase your ability to hunt in outdoor zones by a lot. Permanent medding is good. With the extra mana regen you will more easily be able to farm the plat for a JBB (assuming you arent sick of farming plat by then).

Roxxanne
The horse and the JBB are both great, but if I had to pick which first I would say JBB if you can swing it plat-wise at lvl 45.

At 45 when it starts to proc, a JBB is just godly. I got mine at lvl 49, and didnt start regularly using dots again for damage until the high 50's, when the mobs started to require more damage. But I still use the JBB solo and in groups at 61. It works indoors and out and at 45 you wont need the mana regen boost of the horse as much when all of your damage is mana free. Having to use dots for damage eats a lot of mana, more than you will gain back by horse medding during combat. Plus you give up melee damage to horse med during combat.

That said, you definately want a horse too, because nothing beats the JBB+horse combo. And if you get too bored farming when you only have enough for a horse, you wont be sorry about that purchase either. Horses rock for shammies for the rest of your career.
