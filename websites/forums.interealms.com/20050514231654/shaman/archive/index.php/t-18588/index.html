There are only three times that invis will drop.
1. after the third tick, aka 18 seconds
2. after the 2 minute mark, or 20 ticks.
3. as the spell timer exprisers.

The only thing that will drop invis barring those 3 things is pc casting/attacking, or a mob that sees through invis.

Once you know this Invis becomes really reliable...
