Yes quite a few of us have :)

And to be honest I think the chance of getting a lvl 65 spell in PoP is easier than getting Torpor/Malo in Kunark.

Even a small family guild can do the following to get lvl 65 spells:

1) Get each other flagged for PoV/PoS by helping each other
2) Kill mini giants in PoS for Medallions and regular tribal giants for BoT pieces
3) Enter BoT and kill hallways named for spectrals/runes (ie lvl 65 spells)
4) Farm ring off of hallways named and gems to enter Towers. Two of the tower named can be done by small guilds for another shot at lvl 65 spells.

Skadi
Execution Trial is the easiest and I would concentrate your efforts on that:

when I did it we had similar group, AE Stun/Tash from chanter, I would AE Slow. Moving quickly to each mob is critical. I was 62 at the time and did not have any new spells yet besides those that are vendor purchaseable. The new poison nuke they added (level 55ish?) does about 640 damage with about 3 sec cast time was invaulable. Pacing yourself is also key. Besides the initial AE slow each round I didnt do it agian. It appears the faster you kill a round the more time youll have before next, atleast it "seemed" that way. Try to get a rod before going in from a mage as they can be helpful. Also I found a horse was extremely helpful and all casters should get on one. It was fairly easy to get to wave 4 without mana problems but seemed that wave burned my mana most, probably because other nukers were running low. I did spot healing in the last wave so cleric could get mana for the last fight. Make sure you have enough to Malo/slow the boss at the end, he hits hard, no time limit to kill him, so if you can get to him with your cleric in decent mana shape and you get him slowed quickly you should have no problem :)
