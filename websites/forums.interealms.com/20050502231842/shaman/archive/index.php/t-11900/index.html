Prepared to be shocked :) Sounds like you quit around the same time I did, only I came back last november. Changes off the top of my head:

- No more hell levels
- No more spamming shout/ooc in EC for goods, the bazaar is your friend
- Easy travel with PoP books
- Pets are not KOS unless already engaged in battle
- Pets immediately back off from mezzed mobs
- Pets always summon at maximum level instead of a range of levels
- Pets can dual wield when given two weapons
- A 2+ hour c3 spell called KEI
- Slows casted on players by mobs can be removed with our cure/abolish disease spells instead of dispelling the crap out of them.
- Alternate Advancement (AA)

Lots lots more..

Oh and just about everybody uses magelo now(www.magelo.com) to list the eqiupment on their toons.
