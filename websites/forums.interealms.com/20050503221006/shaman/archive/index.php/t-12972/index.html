cattywumpus
I do the battle shaman thing with my Vah Shir. I'm now 51 and other than having trouble finding a good hunting spot that's not permacamped am still doing fine.

Ogres and trolls would be better for the fighting shaman than any other race. Higher starting STA means more HP at low levels and the regen/unstunnabilty are useful.

You won't be an MT for a group but you can solo dark blues pretty easily.

As for equipment it would depend on your wallet. There are a lot of topics here on "How do I equip my new shaman?." If you can afford a Rune Etched Icewurm Fang that wil take you to the planes, otherwise a good 2HB is the way to go. Better ratios for far less money than you will get from piercers or 1HB. I personally recommend keeping all your skill up though. Much better to do it now than later.

Steelbear
Battle shamans are still an option..maybe more so nowadays since everyone has adopted the hps before wisdom philosophy. I was a battle shaman all the way through 50th level, and still some after Kunark came out (had to learn my piercing). I dont tank nowadays (got 4 level 58 wolves to do that for me), but I dont run when a mob comes a knockin' either.

Piercing with haste (if you have a decent spear) would probably net you better damage than 2h blunt, but it all depends on the ratio really. Some 2h blunts would probably outdamage a piercer. I maxed both skills. The main drawback to 2h blunt is you'd be hard-pressed to find any weapon that could make up for the stats you'd lose from a nice shield in your other hand. There are some nice 1h blunts out there also, so that's of course an option too.
