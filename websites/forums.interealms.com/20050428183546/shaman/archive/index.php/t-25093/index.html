Soul - there's a trick to Ragefire. The "Slaying the Beast" guy is linked to all the troggs along the dead end path that goes to the left way down near the bottom of the initial trail. If you kill that trail first, no mob zerg later when you attack the quest mob.
Macheras
We did all 5 quests in Rage yesterday, was a blast.

2 were from Org (Thrall and Neeru), 2 were from TB, and one was from UC. It's good to get them all if you can before you go in, nice to wipe them out in one shot.

What have you guys found is the optimal level to go in to Rage? We tried when our highest was a 16 shaman and the tank (me) was 13 and the priest was 12, and had a 15 rogue with us. So this 4 man group found it difficult to say the least. We went back with our average level about 17 and it was pretty much too easy. We could rush and 3 spawns and take them down no problem. We still didn't have cc though, so it was brute force, but interesting still.

I think the best time to go is right around lvl 15 or 16 at most to be challenged, but still have it survivable.

We had some issues finding the best loot settings too, between NBG and group loot. Wasn't too much of a problem since only 2 items were bind on pick up, the rest were bind on equip, so could still sort it out. Except our shaman did loot a bind on pickup sword grrrrr.

And also, agree that sometimes some recon can help :). If you try to clear around a boss mob from different angles, it's easier to fight him solo. We ended up doing that on a couple of them. Also, be patient on pulls to minimize adds. Pathing allows you to pull a lot of singles if you watch carefully, although some 3 pulls are unavoidable.
