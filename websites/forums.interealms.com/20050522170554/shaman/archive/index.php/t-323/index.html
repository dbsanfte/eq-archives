My usage was to use two blues of mana on buffs, spells, or whatever, then hit the canni key, torpor myself and canni 4 a few times, cast some spells, canni 4 more, cast more spells, canni 4, torpor myself, and by the time torpor was done, I'd be ready to canni 5 again.

Basically canni 5 fills up about two blues of my mana bar. Which to me is incredible.

Anyway I'm recommending this skill highly, even saying that all that grinding might have actually been worth it! Lookin for oracles out there with the skill and new ways to use it to great advantage. So far, feeding mana to clerics via mod rod 2 mages is my newest idea.
