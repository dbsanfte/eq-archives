mormegil
Cripple seems to be most effective mitigating a level desparity.

First, as many have noted, using cripple on lower level mobs seems to have no effect.

But, in the low 50s, my 49 pet + spear dot could kill a crippled IG when if I did not cripple, dogdog would die. When I duo'd, tho, cripple didn't seem to help the melee. To me and the melee, the IG was blue, but to my pet, the IG was red.

I have assumed, based on those observations, that that phenomenon had to do with the fact that your level verses a mob's level is a huge factor in how hard you hit and how hard a mob hits you. In lowering AC etc, cripple seemed to help mitigate a level desparity - in this case, between the pet and the mob - while doing nothing for me or the melee. Somewhere I have an old parse that shows one specific Sea Fury hitting a 38 ranger (even con) much harder than me at 51 (very blue) even tho we had the same AC, to bolster this.

So, based on what I know about cripple and level difference, I would guess that the mobs you are fighting are around even con and up, and that cripple might actually have a noticable effect.

People also surmise cripple lessens rampages - tho I don't know this to be true (tho I cast the spell /shrug)
