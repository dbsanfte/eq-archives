At 30-40 the pvp game is as balanced at it will ever get. As a melee you'll live and die by your mr. Casters have very few hit points and die quick if cornered. They dont have craploads of mana, so it happens their manapool goes empty before they score the kill, especially vs melees/healers. This is all untwinked of course, which hardly exists anymore.

For you I'd think necro and druid are the most stable choices. Necro has good dmg output and selfheals as well as good utility/survivability. Lacking sow/jboots is going to be the weakness. Druid a bit more defensive with more buffs and of course sow.

If you have a lot of patience and like a challenge i'd go with cleric or chanter, but gotta get to lvl34 on chanter to get manadrain.
