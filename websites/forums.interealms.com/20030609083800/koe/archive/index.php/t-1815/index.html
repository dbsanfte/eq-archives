Bearan et. al,
Don't worry about never being able to go. The big reason for us doing this is to offer the guild a raid opportunity - one large one small - every week. Next week we will likely switch the days they are being run on, and so forth. I know that different people have different schedules. The point of making sure these are run every week is so that people don't have to get all stressed about not being able to attend any one raid. Days will get moved around, times will get pushed earlier and later, target locations and raid objectives will vary, but in the end, we want to be able to offer the guild a major raid and a minor raid each week. Or maybe the experiment will show this is too frequent, and we will move to every other week. The point is, we will get a raid up here on a pace that the guild is comfortable with and in doing so hope to take some of the pressure for people to attend every raid away. Hopefully, with in a month or so, a pattern will reveal itself that allows for consistancy and enjoyment for all.
