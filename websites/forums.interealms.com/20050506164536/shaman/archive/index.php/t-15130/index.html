You might want to think twice about killing in runneye. It's the only place in the old world zones that an Iksar can bank without having to do faction work. The upper level will be agro to you, but if you invis past them, the lower lvls are indifferent or amiable (can't remember which) and there are merchants and a banker you can use.

I would suggest using runnyeye as a staging area, and killing halfling guards. Duo'd with the SK, you could start killing them at mid 20's, collect their weapons and stuff and sell them in runnyeye. You can make 3-5pp per guard if you do this. Plus, you'll be making a start on raising your faction with the DE's (dreadguard outer and inner I think, or is it just outer.. hmm) and if you're not in a huge levelling hurry, it's fun to be one of the very few iksar that can walk around inside Neriak. Of course, there's a lot more factioning involved than just killing the halflings, there's dwarves, gnomes, elves, humans... Certain mobs (not all) will raise your faction with the various factions inside Neriak.
