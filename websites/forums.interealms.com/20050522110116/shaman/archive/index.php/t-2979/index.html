Nektar
The boots could use an upgrade. You can proc the boots from a general inventory slot, so if you're looking for straight up ac, I'd recommend matchless dragonhorn boots. I got these first-hand soloing Yeldema, and they're ac 19 agi +11 magic + 7, all 3 very nice stats. The agi adds even more to the AC and the extra mr can always come in handy for raids/groups/duels etc.

Imo, you could use a little more wisdom if it's currently at 155 (don't expect everyone to agree with me), so I'd change the belt. Flayed Turmoilskin Belt from Fear I suppose is the very basics: ac 9 dex 6 wis 5. You'd lose 40 hps and 1 ac, but you'd gain 5 wisdom. These belts have been around a while, so I expect you can find them cheap. Cobalt Drake Belt is also nice (ac 8 sta 5 wis 8 mana 5 cold 4), but it's a little more expensive, and the ac is even lower, but you do get 3 extra wisdom and 5 more sta = more hp.
But what I would recommend the most if you can afford it is silvery belt of contention: ac 10 sta 4 int/wis 5 and 21% haste. Sells for around 10k on my server, but you'd have no ac loss, you'd have the extra wisdom, and at least you have the 4 stamina which gives you x hp's (anyone know the formula for sta > hp conversion?). Oh, and if you're a battle shammy, the haste is nice too :D

For the face slot, I use mask of war, which would definatly be an upgrade to what you have. Mask of War is 9 ac str +2 sta +5 wis +9 right click yaulp II. Higher ac than arctic wyvern, more wis, extra stamina and a free right click buff (takes 1.5 seconds to cast).

I'd also drop the supple scale armband, unless you need the focus effect at all costs. You're taking a big dive in ac for that slot, and I don't think it's worthed. Actually, I have a pretty viable alternative that you can work on. When I was soloing in ss the other day, a square helm dropped. It has the same exact focus effect and it's ac 12 agi 3 wis 6. You'd be losing 6 ac from the helm, but gaining 6 extra wisdom and preserving the extended range III effect. Plus, that will free your bracer slot up, and what I used to wear was reinforced dragonhide bracer: ac 12 cha +5 wis +9. So you'd gain 9 ac and 4 wisdom over the SSA.
