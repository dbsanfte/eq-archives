If you've got your flask, the hardest bit is done - drops off the drunks in dulak's harbour for those who don't know - in the huts to the right as you enter the zone is where i got mine, took a few hours

go to the spirit at the back of the inside of the upturned ship hull (pos 690 neg 150 if memory serves - i marked it on the map so i could find it again easily) and talk to it, give it the flask, it gives you a rock - talk a bit more, hand it the rock back

at the opening to the ship hill spawns 2 tiny elementals "flotsam" and "jetsam" - they agro immediately - kill them, loot their no drop items and hand them into the spirit, spirit returns spell: stoicism

the battle here was utterly trivial to a 47 shaman and 55 beastlord

the spell itself is extremely cool and the effect draws lots of oohs and aaahs from people who havent seen it before
