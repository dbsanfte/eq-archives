Ok if you're trying to slow ubermob, you've got a 1/5 chance (hypothetical, i made that up). The first time you cast, you have a 1/5 chance of slowing it. The second time you cast, you still have a 1/5 chance. Keep trying... by the 10th try, the odds that you still have not slowed it are 1,048,576/48,828,125, which comes out to roughly 2.1%

So... each individual time you try to slow you have the same chance of it landing, but trying over and over again increases the cumulative chance of getting a successful slow (and also getting you killed :)).

And in response to the malosi vs turgurs resistance check, i've found that the malo line gets resisted more regularly than the turgur's line. That's just how it is /shrug
