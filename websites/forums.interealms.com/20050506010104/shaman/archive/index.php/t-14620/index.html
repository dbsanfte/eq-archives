Skeeter
Brewing in three (somewhat easy) steps:

1. Go to POK. Purchase a bunch of fishing grubs off Caden, the gnome near the halfling bank. Then purchase an equal amount of water -- can be purchased right by the brew barrels. Combine 1 fishing grub plus 1 flask water in the brew barrel, makes fetid essence. Trivial level: 122.

2. Go to EFP, there is a building near the forge that sells elven wine, gnomish spirits, dwarven ale, and mead. Purchase a stack of each of these, plus a stack of casks, and use the brew barrel right nearby to combine one of each of these to make Faydwer shakers. Trivial level: 188. (I prefer this spot if you're not KOS because everything is sooo convenient!)

3. Pick any town that has standard brewing supplies. You're making minotaur hero brews now -- each attempt takes 3 malt, 1 yeast, 2 flasks water, 2 short beers and a cask. Trivial level: 248. Since casks stack now, these aren't so bad -- people whine a lot about making these but they obviously haven't done high end smithing or tailoring!

If you have decent wisdom, you should be able to knock out 248 brewing in an afternoon, for a fraction of the cost you'd spend on *any* other trade skill (except for maybe baking, if you foraged all your ingredients). And you don't have to farm/forage/kill a darn thing, this is by far the easiest skill to GM.

I would say Baking is the easiest and cheapest, and most useful if you aren't looking to make a lot of money out of this.

Easiest route (imo)
Bear sandwich - Bear meat, bread (Trivial 41)
Beer braised bear - bear meat, short beer, spices (Trivial 68)
Fillet of Bear/Lion - Bear/Lion meat, filleting knife (Trivial 143) Make equal portions of each, keep for later.
Bear/Lion fillet in cream - bear/lion fillet, creamy fennel sauce (Trivial 152) Keep for later.

From here I'd recommend buying Centi Meat/Toes or Gnoll Parts from NPC vendors or players in Bazaar. These will take you to 188 (191 on Gnoll).

Now go back and get all those Fillets in Cream you made skilling up on, go get a few Mammoth/Wolf meats and make Fillets in Cream (each meat makes 10) and make some Halas 10 lb Meat Pies (226 Trivial)

Misty thicket picnic baskets till 250.
