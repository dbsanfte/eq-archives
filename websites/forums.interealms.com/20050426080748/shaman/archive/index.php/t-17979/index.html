I'm not sure you need to drop wis but whenever you can you should do some upgrades to gear that have +sta/hp while keeping your wis up there. I have 214 wisdom (14 from augs) and also have a high stamina and nearly 2k hp.

You already have quite a list of places to duo - and many of those places should be pretty good. Personally, I would prefer to duo with someone closer to my level. From what I understand, xp is distributed according to level so a high level partner might take most of the xp - unless you're fighting greens to him.

I duo Acrylia Caverns with a beastlord friend of mine. It's pretty good xp and the loot isn't bad either - you get tons of lightweight sellables, the occasional crap drop that sells for nice plats, and boatloads of acrylia that probably sells well in the bazaar (depends on your server economy). The only catch here is that the acrylia is heavy - so you need both carrying capacity and WR containers.

You could check out eqatlas and look for zones with the level ranges you desire.
