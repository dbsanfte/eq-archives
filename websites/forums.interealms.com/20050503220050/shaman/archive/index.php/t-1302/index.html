Welcome to the Crucible.

Iksar is a good choice for a Shaman (hey I'm bias) anyway, one thing to know about Iksars..HUGE exp penalty and we can't use the most sought after Shaman toy available the JBB (jaundiced bone bracer). Regen and AC bonus is killer though, most Iksar shaman have more AC than some shaman higher level with better gear.
Don't let the no-plate thing bug you....granted iksars do not get alot of choices as far as mid-level armor goes but we can wear most of the high end armors ex:Thurg, Kael, PoG armor.
Rygorr armor is a good choice you can wear that until 50's. Shoudn't cost you that much. Good piercer and a shield should get you started without costing alot.
Spine chill spear - not sure how much those run
Clay Guardian or Charred Guardian (I prefer Clay myself)
Slime Coated Harpoon - will run you a bit less than Spine Chill Spear.
