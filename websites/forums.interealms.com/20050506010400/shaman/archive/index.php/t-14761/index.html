The amount of hp healed per bandaid doesn't increase.
What changes is how far up you can heal.
With my monk, because he has 210 bind wound (anything 201 or over) he can bind himself up to 70% health.
With his first 6 aa, I went run3 bind wound3 instead of the tradional run3 regen3.
As a result, my monk can bind wound anyone up to full health, instead of stopping and having to regen the last big up.
