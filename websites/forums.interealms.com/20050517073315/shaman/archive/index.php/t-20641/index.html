They do indeed have around 2000 HPs, the same as the normal pets.

Originally they were badly broken, but they've applied several fixes:

* HPs have been fixed
* They now bash/kick like normal pets (max of 37)
* They attack faster (originally they'd just stand there for a second after each attack round, now they're continually in their attack animation)
* The proc rate's been upped (used to be from 2 to 5 procs per use, now it's from 4 to 7 - this is over a few dozen uses both pre and post fix).
* They enrage now, just as with normal pets.

The only bug with them is that they'll sometimes cast "Unsummon self" and poof before the minute's up, if you don't keep them busy that is ;)
