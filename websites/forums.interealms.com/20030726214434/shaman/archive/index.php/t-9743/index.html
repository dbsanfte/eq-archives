Jaruden
Exp'd (solo, duo, group) in CoM from 39 to 51. The key to CoM is using the fact that mobs give up agro after a certain distance to your advantage. There are a LOT of places you can root 1 mob, get his friend to follow you, then run just far enough back where the rooted mob won't come. Finish one off, med/canni back up, and grab the other one you agro'd. Pulling the courtyard mobs back past the goo house is the best spot for that, though there are others.

At 49 you should be able to single pull the right side of the stable pretty easy. Sometimes the mobs get linked together when they've been up for a while, nothing you can do about it, you're going to get 2-3. The left side of the stable is a little higher level, I wouldn't recommend trying to pull it solo, it can be tough.

Great practice for root crowd controlling mobs though, get 2-3 and solo them all, you've got to keep them separated enough while dots + dog work at it.

Best bet is to get a monk or 2. At 47 or 48 (pre-ebolt) I had a 49 and 44 monk with me and we were able to clear out both sides of the stables. Courier spawns a lot more often that way.
