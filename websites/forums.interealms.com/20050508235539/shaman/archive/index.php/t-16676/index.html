Skeeter
Got news for you... they have moved mains over.

Entire guilds of them, in fact. High-end guilds. To "help with the Testing process."

Granted, you and I may not be able to do that. A few weeks after Kunark came out I retired my 51 cleric and my wife retired her 49 bard (both in mostly full sets of Hate gear) and we started over at Level 1 on Test. But those 'in the know' have routinely been moved with gear/etc to Test.

Heck, we recently had a 65 necro with Elemental (including POFire) access get 'moved' to Test -- then when he didn't have any friends to play with and couldn't raid (since only one raiding force has Elemental access) he first farmed a bunch of stuff and tried to sell it, then quit.

I believe most folks have a distorted view of the 'testing' process, however. Normal Test server is NOT like the beta servers. On regular Test server, GM support is minimal at best. The server drops without warning, we get a patch (that's our sign something changed), we log in and see what happens. Sometimes you end up naked. Or level 60 with 0 AA's. Or you find your shared bank account wiped. Whatever. And you start /bugging and /petitioning. And if you're lucky, someone responds (in all fairness, when the major bugs happen, the dev's do go out of their way to make folks whole again). If you're really lucky, they actually fix the /bugged NPC/item/whatever. I once /bugged something every day for a full week before a prod patch, they pushed it to y'all with the prod patch, and the next day it was fixed. My /bugs obviously hadn't been read, or it'd have been fixed before the prod patch. (My point is, we normally have no clue what changed -- we find out the hard way.)

So before you start trying to pull strings to get your main moved to Test, make sure you understand how Test works. And realize that the population is low enough that a) your rep matters a lot and b) without some friends you won't be grouping. Don't expect customer support or even a guaranteed server to play on. Make sure you enjoy server drops without warning (you did force a save recently, right? if not, there goes a night's worth of exp/loot). And above all, have a sense of humor -- otherwise spending a good chunk of your play time in the chat room waiting for the server to come back up gets pretty boring. ;)

And if you're going to play with the /testbuffies, please don't come to Test and expect everyone to hand you stuff. You already got a free level 25, with decent gear and a full spellbook. Take an hour to earn some pp in a newbie zone if you need to for spell components or food/drink. Don't expect the long-time players to give you dragon lewts and such just because you have decided to play on Test... earn some levels and try to fit in first! (You can easily make 100pp plus an hour at 25... go to any of the zones that have LOY dye droppers and farm them. My favorite is Steamfont, kill the newbie kobolds, sell the hides and teetch, then sell the steamfont springs for 5-10 pp each in the bazaar).
