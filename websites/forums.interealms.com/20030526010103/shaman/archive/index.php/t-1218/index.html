weissblau
Guardian is exceptionally difficult to cast. We don't get many Abjuration spells, so your Abjure is probably lower than it needs to be.

I fixed my fizzle rate by doing the following:

1. Self-buff with your best DEX buffs.

2. Get Inner Fire up. I put it in slot 8.

3. Define a hotkey

/stand, /cast 8, /pause <n>
/cast 8, /pause <n>
/cast 8, /pause <n>
/cast 8, /pause <n>
/sit

You'll have to figure out what number to put in place of <n> so that you can successfully cast the spell before the next cast attempt. When you don't get the "can't do that command right now" message you have it right.

4. Push the hotkey and watch TV or read a book for 30-45 seconds until you sit down.

4. Push it again.

5. Push it again.

6. Push it again.

7. Push it again.

.... you get the idea. My Abjure is now at about 189 and I can cast Guardian successfully about 3 times out of 5. The cool thing about this is that Inner Fire heals a bit of damage, so you can cannibalize every once in a while and do this forever. Do it in EC or another good sales zone and you can auction SOW potions every two or three cycles and make some cash, too.

Substitute True North for Divination and Burst of Flame for Evocation if you need practice there. Target self or pet for BoF.
