Check out this: http://pub10.ezboard.com/fmaximus94788frm10.showMessage?topicID=74.topic

The artist, Thomas King, has created an AWESOME set of spellbook and spell icons/gems art. There's a spellbook that's nice for shaman (the "spirit" book), and several of the others are really really nice as well. The spell gems take a little getting used to and might be a little difficult to see on high resolutions, but they're beautiful if you can handle them. I would also recommend checking out Remelio's mods, there's a link on T.King's page. He has a really nice graphical mod of the spell-gem bar, as well as a few other nifty goodies. I'm using several things from this page, and I would recommend that anyone check out his stuff, if only to see some beautiful art.
