the new fletching kit is fier`dal only, which means it is only usable by wood elves.

This new kit can not be used to make any of the regular fletching items that have been in the game up till now.. you have to use a regular fletching kit to make those items.

However, the patch added many new fletching items that use mithril components. Mithril is a historical component that elves have used for ages. It is considered a cultural or ancestral skill for elves to make mithril items, or items with mithril components. So all of these new items are intended to be made by elves only, so the elves have to use this new kit. This is the same as smithing and tailoring have forges and kits with race restrictions for making the specific items based on the cultural or ancestral skills.

Now, for most of these new culturally created items, they still allow a wide variety of classes to used them, but they usually have a restriction based on your race or the diety you worship. For example... these new bow and arrows can only be MADE by elves because of the mithril ancestral restriction, but they are USABLE by any warrior, ranger, rogue that worships Tunare. Which means that half elves and humans can use the items as long as they worship Tunare, but they can not make the items themselves.
