Ronax
I screwed up. OOW came in the mail yesterday and I eagerly opened the box, clicked the account link which popped up a window with a field to put in the CD key. I put the key in, clicked submit, and another screen popped up congratulating me on upgrading OOW onto my brothers account. (This wasn't my intent, I was trying to put OOW onto my own account)

So I did the live chat thing. The agent asked me a few questions about who I was and the stations names and such and told me a new key would be emailed to me in one hour.

I have to admit I was amazed, because I really only hear about how terrible the CS is, and really haven't had to deal with it before this.

Anyways, I am posting this for 2 reasons.

First reason is to warn you to CHECK which account you are logged into ON THE WEB PAGE. This does not depend on which account you have most recently logged into Everquest with. Nor is there any indication WHICH account you are logged into when they give you a field to put in the CD key. If you or anyone else has ever logged into the web-based account maintenance page, and there are existing cookies from the last time someone logged in, it is entirely possible that you could be logged into someone elses account maintenance page without realizing it.

Second reason is to give this particular CS episode a thumbs up, since its a rare thing to read about anymore.

Well since mostly negative people peruse class forums (all the happy people I know don't even touch class forums), I assume all we'll hear in this thread is bad ones.

Last week I bought an aug for my monk's Velium Inlaid Claws, which is a 17/20 weapon and worth 10-20k. I put the aug and weapon in the aug pool to combine, and got a message that they were incompatible. I looked at the aug,a nd sure enough, it was for the wrong slot. *My* fault.

So I grumbled, closed the aug pool, and sold my aug back to the vendor for half price or whatever, and logged off for the night as it was late.

Logged on the next day, and --- where is my Velium Inlaid Claws???

In the aug pool. Someone got a nice little gift after I closed it...

I put a petition in using the petition tool, saying I had left my claws in the aug pool and that I was wondering if there was any way I could have that returned to me, and logged off.

I logged on 2 hours later, with a note in my petition tool from a GM saying that my claws were returned to me in my inventory.

So there ya go. A positive CS experience in-game. It was prompt, polite, no-fuss. In fact I've had very few if any experiences that were otherwise.
