3) LDoN points system - They are finally working out some of the issues here with the buyback and augment removal, now if they'd just eliminate the need for 30 wins in each theme and just go off the total wins I think it would be ok for lvl 65's at least. The other major problem with the points system is the cost of low level items/spells vs the ammount of points they get per adventure.

What else could have been great, but ended up being poorly implemented?

Wasn't really like that, and definately isn't anymore. The main thing was we had no DSL or Cable stuff anywhere so the only other option was ISDN which would cost a fortune and was really only available to one company (Telstra. THE company). Could get unlimited 56k accounts. Then after a while everyone realised that 56k sucked ass and started demanding DSL/Cable. Optus came in and started providing cable with help from Foxtel (pay TV), and a few other companies started working on DSL. That screwed Telstra because they lost their monopoly so they started charging these other companies HEAPS to use Telstra's equipment. Hard to provide competitive prices when you yourself are being gouged. The other companies got pissed and tried to get Telstra busted for non-competitive practices while starting to install their own equipment. I think Telstra panicked because their own equipment went up WAAAY faster than it was supposed to be done (where I live I wasn't supposed to get Cable til 2012... but I could get it today) to try and compete. Eventually they got busted and had to start providing good prices to their competitors... and there we get to today where I can sign up for 512/128 ADSL with ~30GB cap (after which I get charged 10c/MB) for $80 a month. Which, at least here, probably the best deal I can get.

So yeah. Internet is still pricey, but my long distance is wikid cheap~
