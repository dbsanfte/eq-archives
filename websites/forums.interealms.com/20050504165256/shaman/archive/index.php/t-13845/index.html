Calabar
The issue with pet weapons has never been weight for me, but simply bulk. Unless I have procing weapons, my favorite puppy fangs are rusty daggers. They are very lightweight and easily obtainable. If you can travel to Freeport, which with the port books is now just a mater of faction, there are two vendors that have endless supplies of rusty weapons. I believe they are both in the same section of town as the docks. The one I use is in the slum pits. Rusty daggers are only a couple of silvers each, so they won’t kill your belt pouch. Another way to get rusty daggers is to fish. Every time I fish I keep six of the daggers and drop or sell the rest. Another possible source of rusty daggers are NPC vendors near fishing spots. There is a pool of water in PoK and two vendors standing next to it. I can often find rusty daggers on one of them before I rush off to join a group.
