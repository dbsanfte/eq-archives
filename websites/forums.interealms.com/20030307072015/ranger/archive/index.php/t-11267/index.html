Tirwen
If you take the Nexus to Antonica, you'll run west through North Karana and West Karana before coming through Qeynos Hills. Wizzies can port to WK, but the closest is probably a druid port to Surefall. From Surefall, you'll run through QH to Blackburrow to Everfrost.
Magrath
Go west in the Nexus, click on the book on the pedestal. Find the stone that says "Halas" and click on it. Poof, you're in Everfrost. :-)
