Greetings,

Good wisdom gear for the hands and feet can be tricky. The Twilight Sea armor quests have decent gear, but by the time you get all the pieces your usually planar already or in Thurg armor.

Many shaman go the druid/cleric route on the feet and get Eboots (Ac5 Wis9/Int9) and hands can either be splitpaw (low ac) or just skip wisdom adn get totemic or splurge and get CC gaunts from Velks if you have a bankroll.

If you know exactly what pieces of quest drops you need for a certain piece of shaman TS armor you might be able to pick them up fairly cheap as no one really does those in any force (your milage may vary).

If you know a very good tailor you can try getting some artic wyvren armor pieces (very difficult even with 200 skill) or studded acrylia.

Equiping the hands can be difficult for any shaman and you may just want to go heavy ac on the hands until you find something that suits you.

Something you might think about is your own cultural armour.
If you have cash, you can buy much better, but for the price, each piece has some wis and fair ac for the money. Expect to pay 200-300pp for each piece. For a little more, you can skip the wis gear and buy the JB pieces for nice ac (even if you can't use the click effects until 45). If you wanted to, you could skill up to the banded level and MAKE your own Northern Imbued Ringmail.

I use splitpaw gloves for the wis when working tradeskills, but I wouldn't be caught dead in them fighting.
