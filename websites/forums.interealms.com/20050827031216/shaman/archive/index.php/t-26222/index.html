Kolmirg
Sword and shield:
Take less damage, cause less damage per hit, lowest rage generation v singles. Longer fights, but less downtime in between. Shield bash for interrupts. Can use Shield Block skill to force a block and activate Revenge to hold aggro. Hint - you don't have you use Revenge on the mob you just block/parry/dodged.

Two Hand:
High damage per hit. Better rage gain. Take more damage from lack of shield. Biggest possible Overpower and Mortal Strike hits. Faster fights, but more damage taken means more downtime.

Dual Wield:
Fastest rage generation. Offhand weapon hits for half damage. You miss alot (24%), but you hit much more. Take more damage than 1h and shield. Faster fights, longer downtime. More rage means more opportunities to use specials.

In my experience there's very little difference in damage output between using two handers and dual wielding. When I'm in damage mode I use whichever setup nets the highest dps. Unless you can break 55% mitigation without a shield I'd recommend 1h & shield for tanking. Shields at the high end are 1900-2000 AC, so that's a nice mitigation boost. Plus you have a chance to block attacks (not much, but every little bit helps).

Barm McLir
The WoW warrior boards are a mess of stupidity and the Steelwarrior WoW section is sadly anemic -- which means finding useful information is difficult.

I'll just give you my (non-parsed) impressions from playing a warrior to 55.

The Good:

- You can't "gimp" characters in WoW because you always have the option to re-spec.
- We can use every weapon except wands, i.e. versatility and variety. So use any nice weapon that falls into your lap.
- We're extemely gear dependant. Why is this good? Well, the inverse would be that gear doesn't matter and that's DULL in an MMORPG! Tangible upgrades are candy. Plus we have less competition for drops, especially horde side. More candy.

The Bad:

I just cannot find a decent radio station in North East Ohio. They ALL suck.

Groups:

If you are tanking for a group, use a 1hander and shield. You're not there to kill stuff; you're there to keep it on the phone while your buddies repo it's car. A good shield is an easy 2K armor which means less healing needed. Plus, you get shield bash to stun casters and shield block which sets you up for a revenge and that's tight aggro. Between sunder and revenge, I can lock a mob down pretty solid.

If the content you're fighting is silly easy, ignore the above and go Conan.

Solo:

Many of our special abilities such are based on weapon damage and that favors the big two handers. I've read a lot of opinions that say dual wield is gimp and it did seem that way at lower levels. Post 50, I don't see a big difference in killing when using dual or a two hander. Dual IS faster rage. I like that both are viable (I still prefer a two hander though).

If you want to save some gold, get a good two hander, a good one hander, and a good shield. The Thrash Blade is a solid one-hand sword from a fun quest in Maraudon. Talk about a pretty princess.

Oh and price polearms everytime you go to the AH. Only 3 alliance and 2 horde classes can use them so you can find bargains. I have a 51dps polearm that cost me next to nothing.

Cheers
