BlargX
I went from a guild in which I was the only non-bot main 60 shaman to one where we have like seven 60 shamans, and although I miss being central a bit, it's also kind of cool in its way.

I'm under much less pressure while I'm at a raid, and I don't have to go to every raid now, either. Very nice!

However, there's a huge line of shamans for any shaman loot that drops, which there isn't all that much of anyway. That's okay though; the guild I'm in now kills more stuff faster, so it evens out, or is even better.

Generally, I like having plenty of shamans. In a guild with way too few shamans, I get to be central and always doing stuff, and am appreciated. In a guild with plenty of shamans, I know I won't get killed as much slowing, and that on a raid we have a LOT of healing power. We frequently have four or more shamans on raids, and that's a lotta torpor going 'round. So I like it either way, really.

Strangely enough, though, sometimes everybody takes the same day off, and then I'm the only shaman online. It can never hurt to have extras of vital characters. They may seem extra one day, but the next day you'll be wishing you had them.
