Granted, a lot of raiders make plans before raids are over - I know my group does. It could be as simple as - we are going to do ldon or something. On the weekends, we try to help people get flagged. I know my RNG friend has flagged more people for KT than I can count.

I suggested to people that they should post on their respected guild MBs or try to set up groups before raids are over. In the end, you just have to get lucky.

If they are doing BiC, this might be your best bet to find people to help you. Make deals with them in which they can have any BiC drop and if a spell drops, it is yours. I did this for a Tipt group a few weeks back. One of our guild ENC needed Cell E, and I was able to loot A, B, C, and D.
