Juujuu
Agility is the statistic that determines how fast you gain skillups for defensive skills (like Defense).
The fastest way to gain Defense skill is to get hit by unslowed mobs that will give you xp if you kill them (blue, white, yellow, red cons).
I've seen speculation that red conning mobs give you the most chance to increase skill, but I haven't ever seen any proof of this.
When you start levelling more, the xp will slow down more, and you'll have more time to work on defensive skills.
Just keep slowing mobs and tanking them to kill them and you'll get your defense up soon enough.
