Buffa
If I remember correctly, the first bear form spell is a level 29 spell. It can be bought in Shadow Haven. The form gives you +5WIS and +1HP per tick regen. If you are a big race, you also lose the ability to bash while in bear form.
Donar
Form of the Bear is a level 29 spell. It turns you into a bear and adds 1hp regen per tick as well as raises your wisdom by 5. A nifty side-effect if you're a large-race shaman is that, while in bear form, you can fit in buildings such as the huts in FV and Kelethin even though you can't fit in them in normal form.
