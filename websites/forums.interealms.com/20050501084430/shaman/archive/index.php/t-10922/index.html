I'd had one in my bank for some time w/ no idea what it was for, so I asked. He told me it was for part of a key quest to get flagged for a Tier3 zone. I said thanks and mentioned casually that mine won't be seeing use any time soon.

He became very friendly all of a sudden (not that he wasn't before). He wanted to buy the block MQ outright. I at the time was rather flush with pp (I'm about broke now, w/ less than 2kpp), so cash wasn't an incentive for me. I asked instead for some spells I was missing (VoTB, PE, & Acumen of Dar Khura), but he didn't have anything I was looking for.

Eventually I mentioned that while he's anxious to get his girlfriend (or maybe it was sister) into Tier3 with him, I was still trying to get to Tier2. At the time, only one or two of my guild family were flagged.

We talked some more, and he was willing to get me through the Exec. Trial come hell or high water if I would chain the Block to him afterwards. All I had to bring was any high level DPS friends I had (he gave me level/classes he wanted) and DS potions.

Turns out I was able to bring in my good guild friend (62Mage) into our group, and he scrounged the rest pick-up from friends and contacts. He borrowed a BoC for the trial. :D

WE SHREDDED the Trials. I think I could have sat down in a corner and played Gems the group was so good. I was casting Torrent and Ice striking, and throwing in the occasional support heal. Dogdog (55, NOT 61 PET) did some damage too. So I felt I at least contributed a bit, but realistically they would have been fine without me. (I even won a parchment too btw. :) )

After I was flagged, I chained the Block to his girlfriend, who happened to be a 65 (seemed to be hotboxed alot) shaman! :rolleyes:

A week or so later, mainly buoyed by the story of my mage friend and I, our guild finally started taking Trial attempts seriously and putting our people though. We still don't have everyone flagged, but we're aiming for everyone 60+ to get theirs soon. We don't need any help to do it either. I was just impatient, and from reading here it didn't look like I'd have a chance in hell of getting flagged pick-up.

A week or so later, I chained another Block for Acumen of Dar Khura. I have MY block (not chaining anymore) now and am shooting to get that flag in the next month or two. (I'm in no hurry for that one, as none of us are flagged Tier3 yet.)

- Druid use Spirit of wood during 4th wave...adds more DS and gheal.
- Ranger disc for 4 wave if needed...normally no need
- Cleric uses only healing dots, if has and druid uses fast heal
- when Exe becomes targettable ranger or paladin kites it, if mana low...normally no need.

With this combination all casters have 80%+ mana after 4th wave and we can proceed to kill Exe immediately. Killing also goes so fast that have plenty of time to med between spawns.

After this we found this combinastion 5/5 flawless victories in a row.
