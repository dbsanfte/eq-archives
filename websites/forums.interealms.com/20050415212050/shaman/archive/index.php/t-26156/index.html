Welcome to the dark side, er, I mean the wonderful world of two-boxing :)

There are a lot of topics dedicated to it, lots of useful information buried there, make sure to use the search button or at least look up the threads linked in The Medicine Cabient in this post: http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=16123

To answer your specific question, SHM/WAR is perfectly viable at 60+, probably one of the more effective two-box setups. Just keep in mind that Shamans spend a lot of their time hitting the Canni button, especially prior to Canni5, which slightly limits your two-boxing choices and/or efficiency compared to some other classes.
