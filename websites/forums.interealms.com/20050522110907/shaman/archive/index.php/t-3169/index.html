Whether or not to melee depends in large part on whether you're grouped or solo'ing. If you're solo'ing, I'd definitely include melee in your strategy for awhile (at least to level 40 when you can max your Defense skill.)

If you're solo'ing, here's a great guide (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?s=&threadid=1348). You can make good money selling tradeskill/reagent newbie items to players (High quality bear pelts, Spider/spiderling silks, Bone chips, etc.) so make sure you don't just automatically dump everything on the vendors.

Try to keep your weapon skills all close to maxed. I kept 1hb and piercing maxed, and regret neglecting 2hb. Just remember, skills only get harder to raise the higher you go. If you don't do it now, it's going to be a lot harder when you get higher.

So you ideally should have one weapon of each type (one hand blunt, two hand blunt, piercing) and switch between them as your skill maxes at each level.

Some weapons you might look for (snatched from another thread- many of these might be too pricey now, but keep an eye out for them. Also prices also vary by server, and this is from an old thread... so who knows!)

Piercing:

Bloodstained fang (Dmg 9, Dly 32) - sells for next to nothing (10 pp?) and a fabulous ratio for the price.

Rukgus's sticker (Dmg 6, Dly 25, +3 Str, +2 Wis, +2 Int) - probably under 100 pp

Ebon war spear (Dmg 8, Dly 31, Rangeable, +4 Str, +3 Wis, +5 vsMg) - probably 100-200 pp, this one is nice because when you upgrade you could move it to your range slot to continue to benefit from the stats.

Bettle stinger (Dmg 8, Dly 23, +4 Agi, +4 Dex, +4 Wis) - 100-200 pp)

Spine chill spear (Dmg 10, Dly 28, +3 Sta, +6 Wis, +10 vsCold) - 200-400 pp

Slime coated harpoon (Dmg 10, Dly 25, Rangeable, +5 Wis, +10 Mana) - 200-500 pp

One hand blunt:

Screaming mace (8 dmg, 35 dly, Effect of Screaming Mace), Cold iron morning star (9 dmg, 35 dly), Enamelled black mace (Dmg 8, Dly 28) - These are old-world weapons, now make nice lower level weapons, and are probably around 50-100 pp or even less.

Wraith bone hammer (Dmg 8, Dly 26, +5 Str, +2 Wis, +2 Int), Sharkbone Warhammer (Dmg 8, Dly 26, +5 Wis), Velium Etched Stone Mace (Dmg 7, Dly 22, +3 Str, +3 Wis), or Centi warhammer (Dmg 15, Dly 32- great ratio!, +5 Str, +5 Sta, +2 Wis) - 100-200 pp

Smoldering Brand (Dmg 9, Dly 22, +3 Str, Effect of Burn from level 1 - NICE weapon), Ykeshan War Club (Dmg 9, Dly 24, Effect of Ykesha from level 37) - 200-500pp

Two hand blunt:

Drakkel Forged Sledgehammer (Dmg 22, Dly 50) or Gatorsmash Maul (Dmg 30, Dly 70) - 50-100 pp

Drakkel Icegrinder (Dmg 27, Dly 45, Effect of Ice Rend) - 200-400pp

Shield:

Bark shield (+15 AC, +5 Agi), Chitin shell shield (+15 AC, +10 vsPos), Crystal webshield (+15 AC, +10 vsCold, +20 HP, +20),
Crustacean shell shield (+17 AC, +4 Str, +10 Sta, -7 Cha), Velium round shield (+15 AC, +10 vsCold) - 50-150 pp

Clay guardian shield (+25 AC, +50 HP) - 200-500 pp

Hope this helps.
