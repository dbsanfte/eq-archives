You are in the same boat I am (except I ONLY solo). Iksar shaman here and unguilded (by choice).

The only glaring hole in your profile I see is the left bracer. You can cast root faster with that thing, but it's not worth it. You've got great AC and HP considering your level.

Check my profile in my sig for some ideas. If I were you, I'd get to Dalnir and you should have no problem getting a Gooey Adhesive and a Coercion Implant so you can quest yourself a Scaled Mystic Bracer. You should be able to find a Seb Scale Cap for a couple hundred PP in the bazaar and that's a slight upgrade to a Scaled Mystic Helm.

Considering you have a couple low AC items because of their Focus effects, I would think that you should be able to hit 900 ac easily with a few upgrades, which is more than I have at 55 when I use my 2hb! The boots (and the CBB) won't be any good to you once you get your 49 spells, so you may want to keep a lookout for replacements when you're in the bazaar. A second Scaled Mystic bracer would be an easy upgrade at 49.
