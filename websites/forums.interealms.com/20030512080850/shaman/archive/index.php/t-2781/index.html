Ran a little experiment yesterday with regards to additional damage output from EA3 with BoN and found some interesting stuffs. Was able to get my PR down to 5 and cast BoN on my self with and without the CMW several times. I found that:

1. I never took more than 94 points of damage from initial hit. Bane usually hits for 150 on opening strike.

2. Damage without CMW was pretty consistently near 1270.

3. Damage difference with CMW was varied with a low of 136 and a high of 322.

4. Lower value differences in damage were more common than higher ones.

Not sure if these inconsistencies are due to innate regen, my dial-up, or something in programming, but I was pretty surprised. I also had a bear of a time landing BoN even with a PR of only 5.
