Baz3
My advice is not to focus on just those two factors.

The advice is basically good. but if you will look around these forums, you will see anumber of equipment threads, and AC vs. WIS debates. Check those out for the basic argument.

As to just the WIS vs. Mana issue at your level .... the key is that WIS does not provide the same mana at all levels. One point of Wis provides level/5 points of mana. at level 15, a 7 WIS ring gives you (7*15/5)= 21 points of mana.

If you have a choice of an item with 3 WIS (lizardscale mantle) or one with 10 mana and some ac, the mana and ac is better - for a few levels.

At 24 you will get Cannibalize and regen ... and you begin to learn that a shaman is about mana-regeneration through both regular mana regen AND hp regen converted to mana.

AC is very popular with many shaman because it saves your life when you get aggro, and because it conserves hp to use for converting to mana.

WIS vs. mana? situational - and changing.
Bigguss
The ratio of WIS to mana changes as you level. I believe it goes something like this:

Wisdom per mana = Level / 5

So...
Level 60 -- 1 WIS = 12 MANA
Level 55 -- 1 WIS = 11 MANA
Level 50 -- 1 WIS = 10 MANA
Level 45 -- 1 WIS = 9 MANA
Level 40 -- 1 WIS = 8 MANA
Level 35 -- 1 WIS = 7 MANA
Level 30 -- 1 WIS = 6 MANA
Level 25 -- 1 WIS = 5 MANA
Level 20 -- 1 WIS = 4 MANA
Level 15 -- 1 WIS = 3 MANA
Level 10 -- 1 WIS = 2 MANA

This means that a +30 mana item is the same as a +10 wis item at level 15, but only a +3 wis item at level 50.
