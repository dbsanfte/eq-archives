Brothur
I have been looking at what is to come for me in terms of endgame and my role in groups for varying opinions and ideas for increase my over all usefulness. What I have found is that there are a few items that need to be addressed in order for our survival in the future. I would to be implemented in one form or another in hopes of keeping the noble Shaman, a viable class to play.

1 – Cannibalize over Time. A short duration spell, similar in duration to our ‘heal over time’ line. I know that this would take a buff slot, however I believe that this could be beneficial for movement and keeping up in a fast passed environment, while at the same time staying true to our class defining health to mana regeneration

2 – Reverse Splurt DoT. This spell would be fast to cast, 2-3 second range and would deal a majority of its damage in the first few ticks, gradually reducing until it’s effects are gone. Since this spell would be cast soon after a slow, the agro would have to be relatively small, sort of like the disease Dot’s are. No name suggestion here.

3 – Thalger’s Spiritual Gift. This spell would fall in line with the Enchanters Clarity line of spells, however, it would add Endurance, an improved Endurance regeneration rate, and a moderate boost to Attack. To remain useful, this would have to stack with the Avatar line. The spells should have similar duration and level restrictions as the Clarity line does. This name I came up with, referring to the creator of the Spear of Fate, and the gift that he might grant through other Heyokah’s.

4 – Rage Upgrade. The Clerics can melee and efficiently regenerate mana with Yalup V. I don’t think hath it would be to much to ask for an upgrade to the rage line of spells to slightly increase the Shaman’s mana regeneration to that of sitting for a short time during combat. After all, we would definitely not out damage our melee hybrid cousins, Beastlords, nor would we mitigate damage as good as they can due to the limitations of our skills. But the minor addition of DPS could help keep us in groups

5 – Buff Restructure. Now what I am hitting at here is to get some of the new abilities, like accuracy or strikethrough incorporated into our existing higher end buffs, as these are level restricted to the younger players, and make our buffs a little more meaningful when most players have their Stats already boosted to the 305 cap. Granted, this one is vague to say the least, I would still like to see this in the near future.

6 – Improved Healing. I don’t want to be as good as the Druid as a healer, but I want to be at least somewhere in the ballpark on this one…. This can be done in a few ways, a group heal over time would be one. Another could be just simple upgrades to the heals we do have, and a slight boost to the cap on Mending of Craig to name a few.

In closing, I do not believe that the Shaman wants to be the healer in a group, so I do not place high importance on the healing aspect. However, I do firmly believe that what Shaman need is a reasonable increase to our contribution of DPS. Be it in the form of fairly efficient DD spells with a comparably long recast time, or in splurt type as I suggested above, or better buffs that make the overall group deal better damage than their stat caps restrict them to, or just better melee capabilities to get more involved while contributing more.

This is my first post in these forums, so please add any constructive comments or suggestions to help me get a better picture in my dreams of what a Shaman in this era should be.
