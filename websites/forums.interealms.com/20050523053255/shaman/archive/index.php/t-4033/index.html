I LOVE my focus items.
The ones I have are:
Obulous death shroud.
Enshrouded Veil
Coldain Military Wristguard.
Supple Scale Armband.
Some others at level 2 that barely ever come into effect.

I wear those shoulders full time.
It's hard to think about taking those off for right now.
If I got an afflicition efficiency 3 item, I'd no longer use the ob mantle.
As it is, whether I'm soloing, or in groups, it's constantly flickering.
Every torpor, chloroblast, malo, cripple, turgur, pox, bane, haste, buff, blah blah blah is less mana.
I think someone did an estimate, and basicly with the average casting they did, and the amount of mana saved, they would have to have shoulders with Flowing Thought 5 to be roughly equal in value in terms of mana conservation/regeneration.
The shoulders have decent stats to them as well.

The only situation I'd use the Imbued Granit Spaulders is in places like NTOV, where mana efficiency isn't quite as necessary all the time for shaman (backup healers and debuffers), and where resists are very important.
Even then, if your resists are high enough, you might want to use the ob mantle instead.
You can probably get a blue diamond helm, neck, 2 bracers, and mask for about 10k PP total, which will provide most of the resists you'll need.
A lodizal shield will round out your resists rather well, and you can then use the ob mantle and not worry about the IGS.

Basicly, IMO, the focus effect of the ob mantle far outweighs the nice AC and resists of the IGS.
