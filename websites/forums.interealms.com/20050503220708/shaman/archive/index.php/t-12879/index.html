If you're just a handful of good friends, I'd suggest doing BoT mini's like mormegil suggested - their (rare) loot is exceedingly good for the effort required to kill them, especially if you have a CoD ench / CotAM mage.

Some other things that require a small force which drop decent loot off the top of my head: Ring of Ooze in Cazic Thule and the Restless Burrower in Akheva...both are easily done with 12 people, perhaps 18 if you've got some people who aren't 65 yet.

As mentioned above, if you've got a solid core of key classes; ie, good warrior, good cleric or 3, good DPS and some of the critical support classes, you'll be able to take down quite a lot in Planes of Power, but you'll still be frustrated by Velious and Luclin uber mobs which still stick by the classic Verant formula of making an uber mob uber simply because he pumps out suchandsuch DPS and has suchandsuch HP.

Boinshar
With that group of people you should be able to kill anything in Velius and earlier. Vyemm, Zlex, Tunare, Sont, and AoW might give you some trouble, but nothing else should.

If you have enough time, farm NToV for gear. If not, race other guilds to kill Vindi/statue/tormax. They are easy kills for some pretty good items. Dain's also a good choice, but he's more annoying and drops lesser quality items compared to the others. If you want to do the 10th ring war, leave Dain alone.

In Ssra, you can kill XTC (2 groupable) and glyphed--although you may want to inform a cursed capable guild so the cycle don't get fubarred for a few hours.

Other Luclin mobs may have too many hps for your raid force. As far as pop goes, aside from BoT nameds you should be able to kill the Junk Beast (killable with 5 people) and maybe the dragon in PoI (doable with 18).

If I were you I'd work on some ST keys since you have very few people to worry about, and that would net you decent gear in a zone without much competition. Most FB dragons are killable with 3 groups or less.
