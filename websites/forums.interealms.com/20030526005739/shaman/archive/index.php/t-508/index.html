In Shadowhaven. When you are in the Nexus, go thru the SH portal, look for for the large building with Barbarian's inside. (it's door is facin AWAY from the Spires) Once you reach the building, just go straigh back, and cross of the little pad. That takes you to the hidden area where you can buy spells. Just look for the Barbarian's again to find the spells. To leave that room, go to the tree in the middle of the courtyard and click on it. :)
