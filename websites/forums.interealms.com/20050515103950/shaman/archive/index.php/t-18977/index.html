Just wondering what some other people's thoughts are on fighting with pets around in this next expansion. OK, mainly just wanting to ramble, but maybe some good can come of it ;)


From what I have seen, we can get 2 more levels of spiritcall making for a total of 7 SC pets out. We also have AA for pets getting critical hits and proc'ing a 75 point DD as well as our group spells hitting pets.


Should all of these enhancements also work on the 7 SC pets, what do you feel is the best line up of spells for utilizing the pets? If you never tried hasting the SC pets before, I can assure you that even doing them with SLTW singly makes for a huge change in DPS. So obviously the first spell I would aim for casting after SC activation is Tali of Alac. Beyond that though I wonder what spell gems are best spent on. You could try tossing in a group DEX boost for more crits and procs, or throw out some DoTs.

It should be interesting to see what new strategies arise with all of this pet modification, and I hope that people not only keep an open mind about what to try, but also an open dialogue about their experiences.
