Varyss
The first decent money making spot I ever came across in EQ was the treant camp in south karana. Not only do the treants drop a lot of plat and gold, but they drop staves that can be sold for about 10pp apiece, plus a lot of random junk like spell research components.

The camp is extremely easy if you have 3-4 casters and you just chain nuke every time a treant appears, but I doubt you could solo it at your level. So if you can get a group together, I'd recommend that spot.

cattywumpus
The raging highland kobolds in Stonebrunt are a good source of cash (about 150-200pp an hour if you clear 1 of the two camps). I doubt at 24 you could do that but you should be able to pull from the camp easily. They drop FS oriental weapons (nice for a change) and a couple of types of armor. Easy to net 5pp or more per kill.

Stonebrunt is on the butt end of the world and you should have no trouble getting the camp.

Only real trouble you should have here is the wandering gorillas and pandas, both are aggressive and dangerous at 24. Easy to avoid if you see them coming but sometimes they will sneak up on you anyway.

There are merchants in kitty city that should be indifferent to sell the heavier loot to, and usually have some gems which will lighten the load of coin so you can hunt longer before you have to find a bank.
