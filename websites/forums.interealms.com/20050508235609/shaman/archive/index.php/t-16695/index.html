Ruatonim
search would net about all the info you need on this, but anyway

creation points:
-short term: points in wis helps you out most
-long term: stamina is best
-LOOONG term: no difference, all your stats will be 300+

RACE:

regen:
-short term: all the same to about 20
-long term: (50-60) troll/iksar--advantage
-LOOONG term: (60+) all nearly the same...25 replenishment(61) 300 torpor(60)/quesience(65)

quirks/pros:
troll: snare necklace, regen, high base sta, JBB
iksar: ac bonus (40), regen
ogre: stun from front impunity, high base sta, JBB
barb: high base sta, JBB
vah shir: high base sta, JBB, sneak (huge for quests and other things)
frog: JBB, innate swimming highest (YAY!..wanted 2 pro's for each :P )

negatives (cons):
troll: low wis, low base agility (not super huge, but under 75 agility you lose quite a bit of ac), 20% exp penalty
iksar: no JBB, not able to wear plate (mithril is about the only gear you lose out on here), 20% exp penalty
ogre: nothing really...lack of regen hurts until 60 though, 15% exp penalty is pretty steep for the stun immunity IMO, low base agility
barb: same as ogre, only 5% exp penalty though so not really a con, night blind (gamma correction mostly eliminates this)
vah shir: i think they are part horse, they sure know how to eat like one at any rate. not able to wear a fungi
frog: no cons at all...lack of any special quirk is the only true con.

and of course...the races are all pretty much the same in the end..play the one you like to look at (and remember you need luclin models on to use a horse so if you think barbs or trolls or ogres or iksar are ugly as luclin models keep that in mind)
