We used a SK to HT for poisitioning and aggro.

Several suggestions.

1) Make sure your SK stays up and keeps aggro, do not ping pong him even for a second or you will wipe.

2) Keep casters outside his room, if positioned right they can stay out of AE range and cast on him. Improved Range Item's help.

3) Staggered MGB HoT's work well, If you have enough MGB'able clerics and druids you can keep Elixir and that wood thing on people the entire time.

4) Put compotent people on CC. Your lower levels. We are great as CC. Us + chanter + lower level war = corner held long after the MB is dead or you have wiped.

Maivia
Not sure if it was 4k, may only be 2. I could most definatly be wrong with this so, but 1 spider in his room will mean your dead so just try and keep them dealt with.

Basically how the spiders work is, they spawn somewhere in the factory and carry energy to the behemoth, to make him targetable you start killing spiders, and he wakes up to see what the problem is yadda yadda yadda. They are still spawning however. During the fight your CC teams MUST stop all spiders period, they spawn rather quickly. If you look at a map of the factory there are 4 corners surrounding the Behemoth's room, each corner needs its own CC, but there is no way into his chamber not through a corner.

Your only gonna actually kill 1-2 spiders with just enc/war/shm. But MB needs to die in 1 Min, 2 Min tops. After that time healing gets tough and most of your raid starts dieing. If you have a LOT of DPS, I would suggest making bigger CC groups, it all depends on your composition.

After the fight everyone needs to return to a corner, as your spider CC now has lots of spiders to kill, finish these off and Keep killing them. Do not make the mistake of calling of Spider CC untill AFTER flags and loot has been done, otherwise stuff gets nasty.

Hope I answered your questions. If not let me know, can always try again =)
