well, I have a 2 box war/shammy currently 52/51. I started these guys together at level 1, and they have been heavily twinked since. My gear for the shammy is standard ac/hp with horse/fungi/jbb. Obviously plat can play a roll in what you can get, but a horse and ikky bp aren't very expensive.

Currently I rarely Can at all as all I have to do fighting seafurbies is keep hos/bin'li and haste up for the war and haste on dogdog. Then I slow and jbb, dont need to heal or even cast regen as the seafurbies are such pansies.

This said, I caned a lot earlier and I will begin to more as jbb damage gets outclassed with dots and I need to regen mana faster. The horse is by far the best piece of gear for your shammy as it takes away sit agro and the need to sit unless swapping spells. This makes your 2 boxing much easier.

When I fight, I am not worried about adds because I know that the shammy can tank until the warrior gets around to taunting the mobs off. I can easily deal with 3 blue con mobs due to the fact that the shammy can tank when need be, and the war can tank 2 or 3 slowed mobs no problem. Knowing that she has the hp to tank a mob lets me explore places where I know that I cant get singles. Getting singles will be easier for you, as you can fd split when need be, but you wont always have to, and roamer adds need to be dealt with some how, shammy tanking is often the best way.

To sum up, go for the standard hp/ac route and get your defense up. Definately buy a horse and ikky bp, and really work towards getting a jbb if money is tight. Warrior/sk dps isn't super high so the shammy will need to add damage in addition to dogdog. The easiest way is jbb, and for the late 40s/early 50s, this is also the fastest way.
