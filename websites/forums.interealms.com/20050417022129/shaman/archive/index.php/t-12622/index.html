At 33 to 36 I have been fighting in GD at either the Tizmak caves in or the cave bear caves.

Bears are good exp but no loot. The tizmaks are slightly less exp per kill but frequently drop 5 plat vellium weapons. With Kei and pet I can constantly kill Tizmaks and get around 60 plat an hour in weapon drops.

I found awesome exp at the orcs in CC, but solo is a little sketchy as even with a pet you still get a lot of potential for multiple adds. That said I have not found better exp or loot yet for my level.

I also found I was having a fun time kiting and rooting giants in GD for decent exp and gaining toes and heads for the first 2 shawls.

Hope it helps.

I don't think these areas are soloable easily, but here is what I've found so far.

Beach - low 30's.
Tunnel - mid 30's.
Before the castle steps - mid-high 30's.
Castle steps & drogmars pre-steps - mid-high 30's.

The area pre-castle and on the steps has very few ppl hunting there, so it's nice and quiet.

Not areas you probably can go LFG in, but byog and you'll be fine. Or if you're plevelling yourself too.

The area of Gunthak, near the Dulak zone, is probably around 40 or so, but you'll get frustrated by all the uncalled trains being dumped on you.
