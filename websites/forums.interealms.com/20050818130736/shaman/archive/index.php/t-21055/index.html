My advice is to not stick around online waiting for GM's to show up or to care or do anything. In-game service doesn't happen all that much. E-mails will often get you very polite and timely and competent service, however. There's a weird divergence in the quality of service in-game and out, so just stick with what works and forget petitioning online. Not that they wouldn't want your money once you restarted your account -- but it's a weird problem and you want them on your side.
