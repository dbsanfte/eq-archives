I fat fingered some keys last night and ran across this little oddity.

Hide.
Have puller pull a mob (velks upper dog) and tank it in front of you, turned so that the mobs back is to you. (typical grp xp grinding)
Sneak. (that was my fat finger)
Auto Attack mob (hide breaks, sneak doesn't)
The mob cons indiff while you continue to slice away at it.

Some of the time (i'm guessing the mob was pushed or sneak roll failed) it would scowel after half a fight. But for the most part I was able to repeat this several times.

Anyway, not sure if this is just a stupid ranger trick, a bug, or might actually be useful in an agro battle as a ghetto-evade.

Enjoy,
