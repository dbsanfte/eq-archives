qhor
yeah, the experience per hour at tables is definitely superior to hraquis, due to the fact that it's so mindlessly easy. i find the hraquis camp more entertaining though.

feel free to pull any of the hraquis mob types. the mystics (shaman mobs) will likely malo you and attempt to land a few dots. the magi (mage mobs) will try to malosinia you and toss a few fire and magic nukes. the faith wardens (cleric mobs) will try to land a few magic based nukes and run themselves out of mana before they're low enough to ch (usually). any shaman with a mix of vt / elemental or better gear and self resist buffs up should basically resist all spells cast on them by the hraquis. the only thing you should worry about with the casters is them dispelling themselves and removing your root; however with the number of debuffs and dots you'll have on them, it's unlikely they'll hit the root on the odd occasion they even cast dispel.

you basically nailed the pull strategy i use. keep a clicky invis item out (chardok ring (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=5733) or ct mask (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=27830)), invis up after a kill, snoop around until you see a lonely hraquis, malicious decay it, swim back to the fishlord, and spam your vp key until the mob's in range/line of sight.

oh, i'd advise against pulling the crabs since they're highly resistant and innately quad... vp and magic-based roots alike will break long before they run their full duration. they're nice exp in groups though, and drop stuff for the crab food (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=29775).

Step 1. Get to camp :p

Step 4. Immediately use Virulent Paralysis 3 while facing the Yellow Star

--Or--

Step 5. Cast bane, epic, whatever .... then with about 30 secs left on VP move back to Black Star.

papiraul
I do 1 AA every 30-35 mins at Tables..

This is how I do it:

If you dont have Dot focus items get a Ceramic Gavel of Justice (Will save mana when dotting).
You will also need Affliction Haste IV and Burning Affliction 4. You can get this at your local tribute master.
Easiest way to get trib points is by making Forged Picks. Material for combine costs 2pp, it's trivial smithing at 49 and will give you 320trib a piece.
8 backpacks of Picks will yield about 38000trib points.

Be sure you buy VP3. Do this as early as possible cause AA's at tables is easy to get. You will be able to buy all the other stuff you need pretty quick afterwards.

I got 2 spellsets I use.
1: Solo Debuff
-Bane
-Anathema
-Odium
-Malosinia
-Malicious Decay
-Quiencense
-Pox of Bertoxxulous (this breaks root.. I'll explain further down)
-Canni IV

2: Solo Burn
-Bane
-Anathema
-Odium
-Breath of Ultor
-Blood of Saryrn
-Quiencense
-Pox of Bertoxxulous (this breaks root.. I'll explain further down)
-Canni IV

Make 2 hotbuttons for them:
/loadspellset solo debuff
/loadspellset solo burn

K, this is how you optimize the kill.

Load "Solo Debuff" spellset.

Keep max distance to target.
Cast Malicious Decay (This will lower dis resists to be able to land VP3, and it will guarantee VP to hold its full duration. It will not get resisted)
Cast Malosinia (This is rarely resisted. Max amount of times I've got resists is about 5 times in a row. There is no need to malos before, you will only waste mana that way, and precious dot time.)
Cast Bane
Cast Anathema
Cast Odium
Press hotkey to load spellset "Solo Burn"
Cast Breath of Ultor
Cast Blood of Saryrn
At this stage Bane will run out.
Cast Bane
Cast Anathema
Cast Odium
Now press Canni V
Target yourself and smack on a Quicense.
Now be sure to click VP3 now and check how long time is left until it refresh. You got one tick to reapply it after it refreshes.
At this stage Bane will run out again.
Cast Bane
Cast Blood of Saryrn
Now reapply VP3.
Cast Anathema
Cast Odium
Etc. etc..

When you feel safe and got all this timed in you can add in the Epic from start of the dot chain. It will wear of just before you VP3 the mob the second time. So reapply it after that if you want added dps.
Also if you feel like risking it some.. If you time it right and keep an eye out for how long VP3 got left before it breaks, you can add and extra dot. Pox of Bertoxxulous. This will break VP3, so be sure to cast it when VP3 only got 5 sec left before refreshing. And be sure to be at max distance before you do it. Pox will break VP3 but since VP3 refreshed during the time you cast it you will be able to reapply it directly after.
With BA4 and Crit Aff 3 AA I usally dot the tables mobs down in 3min 30sec.
Canni up as fast ass possible after kill and you should be able to DING an AA every 50 mins or so. (Or even faster if you got good manaregen and stuff.)

/Cheers

I assume that the fort you are talking about is the first castle that you crossed and were fighting by the bridge? If so, it's easy. Ignore the casters as they are a pain and single pull at your leisure. The two guards at the foot of the stairs are easy to pull, once they are gone you should stand at the top of the stairs on the landing and pull the wanderers. From there it's easier than it appears. The agro range is smaller than a gnats testicles. Just stand at the top of the stairs that lead down to the courtyard at either the left or right side and target the nearest guard there. You can single pull those although it wouldn't seem possible. Pull them out of the castle to along side the wall. You shouldn't get any trains this way and you shouldn't run out of spawns.

Best thing you can do is make the run to castle 2. If you are having no issues soloing at castle 1 you will be fine at castle 2. It's the same method. There are a few threads talking about castle 2 on this board.
