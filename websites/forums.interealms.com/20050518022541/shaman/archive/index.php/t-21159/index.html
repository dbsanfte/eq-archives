weissblau
I really believe groups of mobs is the future. They seem to be headed that way in POP, I haven't done anything in GoD so I can't speak to that.

Some interesting features of POP encounters -

*) Unavoidable spawns DURING the battle that force you to switch targets and switch back (Terris Thule is the earliest version of this I can think of)

*) Multiple strategic objectives that have to be held - like Behemoth, where the four corner spiders must be held off during the fight

*) Gradually building waves of enemies that effectively put a "soft" time limit on the battle - like Rallos Zek the Warlord and his multiple adds - basically if you don't have him down by the second add wave spawn you are touch and go, if he's still up when the third wave spawns you're very unlikely to succeed.

Thinking of these as whole encounters, they are pretty well designed, IMO. The trick is making RANDOM encounters work this way... which is a lot tougher. A very interesting challenge, and I hope the SOE team can pull it off for EQ2.
