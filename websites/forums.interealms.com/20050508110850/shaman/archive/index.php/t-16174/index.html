I was never very much into the raiding bit, but some stuff right off the top of my mind that could probably suit you is ToV and NToV once you get the experience of it. Work on clearing PoGrowth. While this is an extremely long and boring thing to do, it's good raid technique practice. Plow on into Ssra. Do Rhag1/2. The loot kind of sucks but its good raiding experience.

The point at which you will start running into lack of bard problems is Cursed cycle in Ssra. I'm pretty sure this was the final straw that made AD (on VZ) turn 4team.

Tracking is really nothing more than a luxury that saves you a little bit of time when you want to know what raid targets are up in a zone. Level a ranger up to lvl10 and park him in Ssra for example to check on nameds.

Alright, correct me if I am wrong here. Your guild does not have access to: Druids, Paladins, Rangers, and Bards?

No druids and paladins isn't too bad of a loss. No rangers can be a serious hit on your dps, obviously. No bards truly sucks and is what will really limit you.

Here's some things you guys could probably do:

Itraer Vius in Akheva.
Vindicator in Kael.
Tormax in Kael.
Dain in Thurg.
Yelinak in Skyshrine (no bards will make it harder, but velious content is really trivial with level 60+)
NToV & WToV nameds (AEs are trivial even without bards..
XTC in Ssra (Need RGCs though, which you get from: )
Rhags 1 and 2 in Ssra (total wuss mobs).
POI dragon.
Various minis in PoN.
Tower Bosses in BoT. (If you can handle it, these are an awesome way for small guilds to upgrade and get runes. They are always up and fairly easy clears/kills, except for the wing where mobs have the 1.5k AE stun.)

The only mob I can think of that would be damn near impossible without bards is cursed. You NEED to have a bard singing resist song in the MT group in order to keep him from getting charmed. Maybe the massive amounts of MR gear than PvP people generally wear can offset this? I am not sure.
