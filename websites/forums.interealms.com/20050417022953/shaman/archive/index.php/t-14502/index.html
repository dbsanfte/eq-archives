Karshak_rn
Dulak is a fun zone for the high 40's, low 50's. I am not sure why you don't want the boats. At 52 you can easily solo the lowest boat (Stormwave) and the second one (Dirulia). In both boats invis up at the zone line and run to the ramp for the boat. There is a safe spot once you are on the boat to summon pet and buff up. You run up the ramp onto the boat and then up the ramp to the raised platform at the back of the boat. At the point where the slope up meets the level part stop and stay as close to the edge of the boat as you can. You can sit here all day without agro.

Summon pet, chuck some weaps at it and start killing. I usually send pet in and let him get some agro, then slow, then ebolt. jbb, jbb, jbb, jbb, loot. If I get agro I just take the hits and melee. You will have plenty of time to heal as pet crunches the next mob. You should be able to single agro/pull every mob on these boats. I have never had an add in many sessions. I am also a 52 shaman and I can sweep Durilia in about the same time it takes for them to start re-popping. This is a constant xp stream, add in the gem drops and pp and I take in around 500-800pp per kei (lol).

I might be getting confused about the mines, but are they the tunnels that are accessed through the hidden entrance? If they are then they are not too tough to hunt in either. If you plan on soloing them I would start from outside. As you enter the courtyard from the zone line stop halfway along the wall to the left and summon pet, buff, etc. You might get a wanderer but at 52 it will be light blue and just annoying. Clear the way into the mines and you can just progress along. When you get to the bridge if you take a right and clear the 2 mobs at the entrance you can camp the mob that spawns half way down that tunnel on the left. It is the placeholder for the soulstealer that drops the soulstripper. He can be tough when he pops but I have been able to solo him at level 52 with no kei and self buffed only. Just be careful.

This is a great zone to hunt in, I have spoken to folks that are still getting great xp on the harder boats in the high 50's.

If this post is inaccurate be gentle.... this is my first post on these boards.

As Karshak said, the first two boats will be cake for you at 52. I would start on Stormwave (first boat you find when you go right from zone in) and camp it until the Captain spawns and you get the charm he drops. It is the perfect caster charm as it's stats go up as your casting skills increase. In three KEI sessions on Stormwave, I made just under 3k pp and then go the captain to spawn as well.

Dirulia would be your next boat and it isn't very hard either. I checked out the third boat, and though the mobs may not be a lot tougher, they are packed in a bit more so adds might become a problem. Can't say for sure cuz I only scoped it out while invis.

Another good spot for just straight xp is the Armory. I'm not sure if any named spawn here but the xp flows steadily at 52 (about 2% for every three kills) and the usual gems, jewelry, and weaps drop. The Zraxthil Forged weaps are great for selling and for pets. They actually have some stats on them. The best spot I found for the armory is at the very back. If you go straight out from zone in you will see large wooden walls on your left. The armory is the first or second section you come to. You will see two guards at the entrance and they are named Armory Guards, so it isn't hard to find. This si the area where you go to Torgiran's Mines as well. When you enter the armory, you will see a wooden ladder on your right. The mines are are up that ladder and along the platform to the left.

Anyway, once in the armory, go straight back to the far wall, slightly right of center. Do not camp all the way in the right corner because when you drop invis, the architects on the other side of the right wall will aggro you through the wall. Stay just right of center near (if I remember correctly) what looks like mining carts. You should see what looks like a machine to your left as you face the back wall with a troll and iksar mob on either side of it. This is yor camp and at 52 you should be able to single pull about 8 or 9 mobs before respawns.

I usually tag with slow and immediately send out pet. He almost always gets aggro before the mob has reached me at camp, then I just cripple and JBB them to death. They do run below 20% health, so have a root spell up in case dogdog can't take them down fast.

There are a couple other good spots, but at 52, I found the xp dropped off a bit and it took 3 kills to get 1% at times, so I won't even mention them. At 52 though, you should definitely be on at least the second boat (Dirulia), but get the charm from Stormwave first. It is too easy to pass up. The xp won't be as good on Stormwave at 52, but the charm and the loot will more than make up for the time invested. You should still see about 2% per three kills though, maybe a bit less. And you should certainly make 500-800pp per KEI session in any of the spots mentioned here.

Shirkamon
I began hunting in Dulak at 39 with my shaman/beastlord Duo. I am now at 55 and have been on every boat except Windscorn. The boats are fun but very predictable. The drop items are good from the captains. Stormwave, The Lady Dirulia, and Dandolak can all be single pulled without adds and have a safe spot on each boat. The safe spot is is against the rail to the front (fore) side of the dock ramp.

At 55, experience is a little slow; however, I can make between 750p and 1500p in about 4 hours playing time on plat loot and vendor loot. Weopons bring in between 4 and 5 plat and there are gem drops and jewelry drops (amulets, rings, and bracelets). Gems and jewelry sell between 12 and 238 plat (blue diamonds). I have the Rust Encrusted pants from the Dandolak Captain. A Rust Encrusted Bracer from Oceancrasher mobs. I also have a Seaworn ring from the Dirulia captain. I have never had one drop, but the Rust Encrusted BP from the Dandolak captain has regen. Hope to get one eventually.

An alternate to the boats and armory are the Sharkbar located on the docs between Stormwave and Dirulia. You can set up outside the bar and single pull to clear the bar. Once the bar is clear, you can set up near the shark pit trap in the rear. The named chef drops a couple of nice pieces.

Another good spot is across from the armory in the courtyard of the interior buildings. There is another bar where you will see dark-elf assasin's, spawn. I find this area just as good exp as the Dandolak and just as profitable. It is rarely camped and with the exception of a guard roamer every now and then, it is relatively safe. I set up camp directly across from the bar not quite agains the back of the building (just in cast there is aggro through the wall). There is an ogre on the right side of the building that you should take out first.

There are no casters in Dulak, expcept on Windscorn, so slow, root, dot, and dogdog are perfect for the solo player. Together with a melee tank, either a monk, pally, warrior, or beastie, you can rule Dulak. Also, invis is your friend.

As for Torgiran's Mines, I have made it as far as the Bloodguards that drop the Grobb Hide Mask and Cowl. For the most part, plat loot is lower in TM that in Dulak. Gem drops are about the same, but the equipment drops are much better. Torgiran's is a straight dungeon crawl. Everything can be slowed and up to the Bloodguards, there are no casters. Trains are rare on the prexus server; as my 2 characters are generally the only ones in the zone. If you want to camp the bloodguard drops, get a melee or pet class companion. They hit hard and have about 7k in hit points.

Good luck in hunting LoY. I'm off to Nadox.
