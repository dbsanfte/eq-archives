I've done this trial a few times and found that trying to CC is more of a hindrance than a benefit. Get a solid knight in place of the chanter/bard and have him offtank the 2 soldiers. The elite guard roots easily with VP or regular root, and can be parked until the other 3 are dead. With a decent tank and offtank (I've succeeded with a ranger offtank,) your options are wide open as to what other classes you bring along. It helps if the group has respectable dps though. With a war, clr, pal, rog, bard (was only meleeing), and shm, all in time + gear, we were finishing each wave with over a minute to spare. This tactic might not work for everyone, but I have found it to be much easier, and fun, than CCing.
