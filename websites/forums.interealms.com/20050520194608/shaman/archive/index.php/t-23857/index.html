Grab 5 melee and head over to PoF. There is an excellent camp in the 2nd yard that is almost never taken. On the left wall there is a cubby area populated mostly by spiders with a gazeebo*sp filled with the Firehead doods. Move your group deep into the cubby and you have mob paradise. I can keep a group of 5 melee going without breaking a sweat. I prefer to have a Pali or SK for the tank, with a possible 2nd tank there for adds and intercepting chain pulls.

The camp is always open and the mobs are easy enough for a single shammy to keep up np. Though some will have an easier time than others depending on gear, spells, etc. Have fun.

esma
I am not ep flagged, working on tier 2, got equipment mostly matching ornate. The best Exp i have found for 66-68 so far is doing expeditions in Dranik's. Since the zone is instanced you don;t have to compete for mobs, the mobs are reletively well spaced so it is hard to get more than 3 on a pull, usually 1 or 2 which i can easily park add here.

At 66 the exp is 10% per 60 so 1% per 6 kills
At 67 it is double so 5% per 60.

I found that a reletively moderate group can kill 60 mobs in here in about 75 minutes so that is 750 mins (12.5 hours) for 66 and 25 hours for 67.

The good thing is the level of the mobs are based on the group/person who requests the expedition, so the exp here can keep getting better.

Bad thing is named mob spawns (there are ph's) here are scarce, about 1 per hour in my exp, so getting rune drops here take alot longer than WoS.
The ok side is you can make about 200pp per hour (not alot) from the drops off the mobs.
