A few of those actually are found in Shar Vahl. The vendor just isn't in the same spot as the rest. There is a guy near the Alchemy vendors who sells all the poison/disease spells.

Tiny Companion is a PoP spell and is only available from vendors in Plane of Knowledge

Vision spells are sold by the Gypsies in Oasis.

Scale of Wolf, I am not 100% positive on this but I think it is only sold in Firiona Vie and The Overthere.
