Drienne
Starting stats aren't important in the long run. However, it can really change your gear early on. Like entilzah having to concentrate on STA (although you need 100, not 101 to negate the effects of fatigue in combat). I'd say it depends on what class you choose. Human/Wood Elf, go straight STA. Half Elf, put some points into WIS/STA. Getting your Wisdom up, even 5 or 10 points, to begin, will help you in the early and mid levels a bit. However, my very best advice for general use is: all melee/hybrid, put points in STA. Get to 100 from bonus points if you can, then look elsewhere (STR or WIS).

on the short run, it doesnt count that much

however...
since items are rare, you might be unable to reach the caps unbuffed.

just imagine, you have the choice between a 20 sta 50 hp item (10kpp), and a 40 sta 50 hp one (50kpp).
if your sta is 265/305 you'll pick the 40/50 one.
if your sta is 285/305 thanks to your 20 starting points into sta, you'll pick the 20/50 one.

having better starting stats will make you have lower needs to reach the caps. not so big issue, but it can counts on the very long run.
