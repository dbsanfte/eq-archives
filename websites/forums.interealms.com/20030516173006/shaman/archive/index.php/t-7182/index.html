Kaneras
I would just forget the Arena, too many troopers and guards and low-level junk. Head to the Tormax bridge and kill Dlammaz Stormslayer, you'll need 2 strong groups for this with a good chanter for tash and a good slower. At least one monk is mandatory, 2 is better or use a SK if you can get one. Dlammaz hits for 500 and has 100k hit points, he's a bad ass and if you don't slow him it's over.

Behind the spot where Dlammaz spawns, you will find "Sentinels", which con red or yellow to level 60 and aren't as hard as their con indicates. They drop armor in droves, sometimes 2 peices at a time, just keep pulling these and you will be amazed at how much armor drops, including tunics and leggings. The trick is to get the spawns broken and pull giants constantly. The less giants your monks have to split the faster you will kill. This spot is a riot to hunt by the way, one of my favorite places in EQ.
