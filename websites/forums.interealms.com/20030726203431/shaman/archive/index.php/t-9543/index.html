BlargX
Any 60 can single pull there. Why he would do so when he can get much better experience in POP, I dunno. Shrooms give only about 1/5 of a bubble per kill at 60.

Be aware that you should really get your disease resists up. They cast lots of disease-based spells, including DD's for 200. Being under 60, you will be especially vulnerable and need resists even more.

If you don't have a mushroom veil or a bronze statute of bathezid, you might want to think about getting them -- or whatever gets your DR high. You'll find that what level of disease resist you have makes more difference than anything else here; try to get over 130 at the very minimum, or your kill rate will go way down due to using up so much mana healing. For someone under 60, the sky's the limit on getting enough DR.

Realize that you cannot SOW in here(though the zoneline is only about 10-15 seconds away, if you fight in the back), and since the mushrooms can root you and stun you, escape can be rough if you get in trouble.
