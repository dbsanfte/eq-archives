SO, I have read post after post about how alchemy could change to become a more useful tradeskill. Here is yet another such dream.

:eek:Potion on a rope!:eek:

Now imagine a potion that was first made via alchemy, and then perhaps undergone some kind of JC combine (or what have you). Now the potion would become a "charm." This charm/potion would have a few minor stats of some variety, but could also be activated from that slot and consumed as usual potions are.

When your ten dose "charm" is used up, you can leave the empty husk in the charm slot for the few minor stats, or go on ahead and buy yourself a fresh one.

Here's an example:

http://www.ne.jp/asahi/matts/page/SOWCharm.jpg

Something like this might help kickstart alchemy a bit.
