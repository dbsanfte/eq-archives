Caill
There's been alot of talk about new abilities that may be useful in future expansions. There's also alot of complaining about the lack of usefulness of shamans beyond the first one or two on a raid. I have an idea that might solve the second problem and be cool addition to the game.

What I'm thinking of is a line of spell like abilities called chants. These abilities would be more powerful the more shaman there were in the in the raid/group, ie the more shaman there were chanting. You could have slows that would be more effective based on the number of shaman--either by making the mob more slower than the normal percentages, or by overcoming mitigation. We have mobs that mitigate a single shaman's slow, but what about the combined strength of many shaman? Perhaps a shaman slow chant would have an AE effect in that any mob that came within the chants effective radius would be slowed.

You could apply this to more than slow. Dots that were more effective based on the number of shaman chanting. How about buffs that were more powerful based on number of shaman? The ideas are pretty unlimited.

You'd probably want to limit the ability in some way- say by allowing only one chant at a time. Perhaps different chants interrupt each other by causing disharmony or something. But a single focused chant by a number of shaman would be an aweinspiring thing.
