For what it's worth, even though I've cancelled, along with a great many other folks here, I still think I'm entitled to have an opinion and speak about a class I put over 5 years into.

I'm not going to ask about the shaman class now though. I've already said and tried enough there, and said my good-byes to those I wanted to send 'em to.

What I want to ask about is MMORPG loyalty. Those shaman still playing EQ who haven't cancelled yet... why do you still play? Why do you log in?

Do you enjoy EQ and still have things there that you truly like and want to do, like camping quest drops, or raiding, or whatever? Is it the actual game that you log in for?

Or is it the people that you've met and gotten to know? And how strong are the bonds that cause you to log in and play with those folks?

EQ can last years based on folks logging in because of that "well, I know lots of folks" comfort factor alone. But how many old-timers out there, as this game has matured, feel it's the same game and the same closeness of players as we remember?

I'm not trying to make any point or champion any sides or justify anything to anyone here. I only want to ask that question. Why do you log in to EQ vs. any other MMORPG?
