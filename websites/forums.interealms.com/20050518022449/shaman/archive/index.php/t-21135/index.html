As a 65 druid who boxes a 60 shaman I inherited, I have a few thoughts... :-) The quickest way to PL the shaman would be to charm the forest frogs in PoS. Park the shaman on a hill, and hunt with the druid. Use the shaman for extra buffs and healing, while you learn the druid a bit. Alternative sites include the forest in the plane of mischief and the plane of nightmares. You could also try quadding with the druid, perhaps the otters in CS, but it won't compare to the XP from charming.

I've also found Droga to be decent experience for my shaman - granted, my primary focus is loot, but the XP is a nice bonus. In this case it's root, slow, dot, dot, dot, and tank with whoever has more aggro.

2-boxing and charming isn't too bad, particularly since you can essentially ignore the shaman. Once you get it down, though, you are pretty powerful, in areas with lvl 55-60 animals. If you have any more questions about charming druids, we probably ought to take this to the grove or eqdruids. :)
