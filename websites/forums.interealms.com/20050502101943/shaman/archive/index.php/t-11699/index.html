Kazim
It seems like the SoE developers are listening to feedback and addressing our concerns recently. Perhaps we should keep a regular thread going to track any remaining or new issues we'd like addressed next time they're looking at shaman issues? That way when SoE surveys the various class boards for feedback/issues, there's something there for them at their fingertips.

Here's a crack at such a list:

************ Class Balance Issues *************

Healing and Slow Mitigation

Having such an exceptionally good slow spell may have justified giving shamans a "complete" heal which heals less damage for the same mana and casting time than the druid version. Yet with slow mitigation, the advantage in slowing has been drastically reduced, with several other classes (including melees using weapons proccing slows) able to provide a slow spell which when mitigated yields a very similar result to that of shamans. Since the slows are less of an advantage than they were, perhaps it's time to take a look at increasing the cap on Kragg's mending to be something closer to 3000hp?

Personally, I don't think that slow mitigation is causing shamans to lose out on xp groups, so I don't really view this as a huge class balance issue. But if other shamans do, then we could try to express their concerns here.

Shaman Melee Skills

Some of us thought we were rolling up a "battle priest" when we created our shamans. Yet shaman melee is a total joke. It's nearly unusable. Clerics are clearly the superior of the shaman in melee and are the real battle priests now. Not that clerics are going to be meleeing all that much either. It would be really nice to be able to get a good weapon and not feel silly actually using it once in a while.

Why a goofy little frog cleric can shield bash for more than a giant ogre shaman is a question which is in sore need of an answer.

Having rabid bear in its current incarnation as an AA skill just rubs the dismal state of shaman melee skills in our faces. And wasting another AA skill on an enhancement to this silly skill defies all reason.

King of Stat Buffs?

Being the king of stat buffing is a dubious honor at best given how small an effect most stats have. Yet sadly, a large portion of the shaman spell lineup has been dedicated to stat buffs. Some nice progress recognizing the low value of these buffs has come in recently in the form of very nice replacement spells for some relatively useless 63-64 stat buffs. A continuation of this sort of progress would be most appreciated.

It has been suggested several times that minor "direct" improvements be added to some of the stat buff lines that shamans get, making them have an effect more in line with what may have been intended when the lines were first developed. Add a minor +ATK to STR buffs. Add some minor +AC to AGI buffs. Perhaps add a -FIZZLE or +ARCHERY to DEX. And perhaps a -AGRO to CHA.

************ Quest Related Issues *************

Symbols of the Tribunal

There are a lot of barbarian shamans still wondering where the disciple and regent symbols of the tribunal are. The other shaman gods reward their loyal shamans with a nice out-of-class ability for their loyalty. Tribunal worshipers would love to be able to prove their dedication similarly and be rewarded in kind.

Broken Arrow from Epic

I for one would love to have confirmation from SoE that this piece from our epic quest is either part of an unsolved quest, so we can get ourselves motivated to solve it. Or is just junk of some kind. At the moment, most every shaman believes it's either total junk or that that part of the quest is broken. If it's part of an unsolved quest, a tiny bit of encouragement from SoE may get the shaman community re-energized to take another look.

************ Spell Related Issues *************

Rarity of Kunark Spells

Hopefully, the absurd rarity of Torpor and Malo has been adequately addressed by the new priest spell research given to INT casters. This remains to be seen.

Spirit Quickening

This spell appears to be entirely useless. If the shaman community is somehow mistaken about this spell, some hint from SoE would be most appreciated. If in fact this spell is as useless as log parses have seemed to indicate, then an upgrade to fix it or a replacement spell would be most appreciated.

************ Alchemy Related Issues *************

Cost of Ingredients

The last expansion had some nice improvements for alchemy. However most potions still remain too expensive to be viable. Most of us feel that a cross the board reduction in herb costs would open up a number of potions as reasonable things for shamans to actually bother making. Currently, many of our potions are simply to expensive to bother with.

Resale of Potions

Skilling up Alchemy is extraordinarily expensive, since venders don't value potions any more than most other players do. An improvement to vender buyback prices for potions would open up alchemy for the less wealthy shamans.

PoP Alchemy

PoP brought a lot of very nice recipes for most trade skills. Yet Alchemy received only one recipe. And it's for something that only magicians need. It would be nice if Alchemy felt like it was being treated with even a fraction of the care that the other tradeskills are receiving.

************ Brainstorming *************

This section is in case SoE needs some ideas from the shaman community of things which we feel would enhance our experience without grossly changing game balance. If we never get any of this stuff, no big deal, but this section is where we put our requests for things that would make common activities easier for us, taking some of the grind out of the game, leaving more time for fun.

Minor PBAoE spell

Many of us would like some minor form of PBAoE spell. Nothing spectacular enough to alter game balance. Perhaps something like a 20% slow spell? Or a pbaoe version of one of our weaker DoTs (scourge?)

Maybe some NODROP or NORENT potions with more powerful effects?

Curses

In at least a few of the past threads similar to this one, there were suggestions that shamans should transition from being the masters of stat buffs to the masters of curses. Curses to increase casting time, fizzle rates, reduce melee damage, reduce ATK, etc. would be most welcome.
