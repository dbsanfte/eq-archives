I put together a shaman spell details list for the Story Window in the UI and wanted to share it with anyone who might find it useful.

I did my best to get everything as accurate as possible. Being only level 58 though, I have no first hand experience with any spells above my level. With that being said, please feel free to check it over and make sure I am not missing any important details about those spells. I gathered most of the spell data from Lucy.

Giving credit where credit is due, I shamelessly modified the files that Ramsus had put together for his Research Components window for this endeavor. I hope this further inspires other folks to make some cool story windows.

At this time, I have no plans to make lists like this for other classes. This was shammy spell list was put together out of my passion for playing the best class in EQ, the Shaman.

Having never posted a file to a message board before, I hope this works, otherwise I'll look like a big dork :)

Just unzip the files and read the Readme file for their proper placement within the EQ directory.

Enjoy!
