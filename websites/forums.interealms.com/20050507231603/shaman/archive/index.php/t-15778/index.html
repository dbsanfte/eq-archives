I've actually ran two copies of EQ from a single directory on my lowly 1.4Ghz machine and it seemed to run fine. What I did was crank my resolution to 1600x1200 and then ran each copy of EQ at 800x600 so I could keep both windows of EQ side-by-side so I could see everything.

Do turn off your sound though in at least one of your copies. I've not actually ran two copies in an outdoor zone which I imagine is going to be a little choppy if you're under a 2Ghz. But in the dungeon-type zones it was fine.

Hope this helps.
