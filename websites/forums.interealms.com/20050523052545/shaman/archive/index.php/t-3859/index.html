Shaman smiths have a couple of issues

First, we are not able to make a non-imbued version of our cultural armour.

Other races, such as dwarfs, are able to make a non-imbued version, and are able to make cultural armour for more than one deity.

The end result for shaman smiths is that we have a significantly smaller customer base.

Second, the stats on our cultural armour are not as good as the stats on the plate classes cultural armour. A quick way to see this is to compare the plate class cultural armour to their velious armour -- most of the cultural pieces are hands down upgrades. Now do the same comparison for the shaman. A few of the cultural pieces have better hitpoints, but not a ton more. This can come at the cost of AC or other stats -- and there is no piece that is a hands down upgrade. The shaman's cultural tunic is not an upgrade from the Skyshrine BP, or even from the Thurg BP (disregarding the effect).

Compare a Tribunal Shaman's Cultural BP -- AC 30, Sta 5, Wis 10, Hp 40, mana 40
to the Thurgadin BP AC 37, str 3, sta 6, Agi 3, Dex 3, Wis 14, HP 30, Mana 80 -- effect Riotous Health.

Compare a Tribunal worshipping warriors cultural BP -- AC 51, Str 10, Sta 10, HP 80
to the Warrior's Thurgadin BP -- AC 47, Str 14, Sta 10, Agi 5, Dex 7, MR 4, PR 10, HP 90 (effect invigorate).

Now compare a Tribunal worshipping Bard's cultural BP -- AC 51, str 10, Sta 10, HP 80
to the Bard's Thurgadin BP -- AC 42, str 8, Sta 10, Dex 10, Cha 10, MR 8, FR 2, CR 2, PR 2, DR 2, HP 50 -- effect Selo's song of travel.

Note that the culutural armour is comporable or even an upgrade for some circumstances (again, disregarding the effect) for the plate classes. It's a clear downgrade for the shaman.

Third issue I should let others speak to -- but trolls don't really have cultural smithing, although they can wear armour made by ogres. From my quick glance, it looks like the ogres could make similiar claims about the stats on the chain armour they can make.

Why is all of this a "shaman" issue rather than a race or diety issue? It's just something that you should not have to give up when you decide to be a shaman.
