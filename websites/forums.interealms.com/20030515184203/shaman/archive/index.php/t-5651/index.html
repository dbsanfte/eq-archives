Iynna
Having Epic does not guarrantee correct faction to aggro Rak on turn in. This was the case for an MQ I had arranged. Rak conned ally, but ate the tear.

The epic shaman needs to be at "can not get any better" true spirit faction to be sure. This can be checked by doing a Ghiosk book turn in or some other lower level faction turn in.

After you kill Rak and loot the scale, then you need to be at max ally to turn in the scale for your spear. Again, it's not just getting the scale, you have to be at the proper faction for the turn in. Conning at ally doesn't mean faction is high enough. You have to be at max ally which can only be tested by doing some other turn in and getting the "your true spirit faction can not get any better message".
