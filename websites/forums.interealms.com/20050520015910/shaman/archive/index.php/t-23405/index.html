Juujuu
Hey Barb, you guys down Xegony with any regularity yet? We got a recruit who's a close RL friend of mine that needs that flag to finish up her Time flag.

Anyway, about time.
There's 6 phases of Time.
Phase 1 has 5 trials that all need to be completed within 1 hour; earth air fire water undead. Named drop loot if no one (your guild or any other guild) has done phase 1 within the last 12 hours. It's kind of annoying that if someone else does time before you, you can't get your phase 1 loots, but once you've farmed time for 3+ months, you don't much care about phase 1 loot anyway. It's all recruit and applicant and alt gear by that point really.
You cannot bring any more than 18 people into a single trial area.
Phase 1 is special in that if you ever run out of time in your instance, you have to re-clear phase 1 before you can resume clearing whatever phase you have left.
Phase 2-5 has a ~5.5 day respawn time
Phase 2 has 5 sections of mobs to be cleared (earth fire fire water undead), but there 2 sets of 2 sections can all be pulled to the same area (like, earth and fire can both be pulled to the earth OR fire trial), though I don't know which of the sections are together (undead is separate). Named drop loot always. 1 hour to complete this phase
Phase 3 has a lot of mobs to clear. When you clear the mob type for a particular god, you get a new wave of mobs to clear for the next god. Named randomly drop loot (about 33% of the time I'd guess). 1 hour 15 mins to complete this phase.
Phase 4 has 4 gods to clear. Terris, Saryrn, VZ, TZ. 4 hours to complete this phase (-1 hour for each god that you've already killed)
Phase 5 has 4 sections of trash mobs to clear, then 4 gods to clear. Rallos, CT, Inny, Bertox. 4 hours to complete this phase (-1 hour for each god that you've already killed)
Phase 6 has Quarm to kill. 2? hours to complete this phase.

You form your raid up in Time A.
When you are ready, you zone into Time B and begin your raiding.
There's a graveyard in Time B that you can enter if you are not in the raid to get your corpse/res etc.

The zone is semi-instanced. If say, Watchers is raiding Time, then no one can zone into Time B until our raid has left the zone for whatever time period needed for the instance to expire. While our instance is up, only people in our own raid can zone in. While our instance is up, the zone remembers what mobs we've killed (the gods or the phase we've gotten to). When we return at a later time, the instance will remember our progress, and let us continue where we were before, sorta. When we leave the zone, then say, you guys are raiding afterwards, you get a completely new instance to raid.

As an example, Watchers had Thursday and Saturdays as our 2 days in the week to raid time (we do it all on Saturday now, but pretend we still need 2 days for this example). I think Alarius has Friday as one of their days to raid time:
Watchers raids Time on Thursday. We finish clearing phases 1 2 3 and Saryrn and Terris thule in phase 4. We leave the zone.
Alarius goes into Time on Friday. They have a separate instance. All of phase 1-6 is up for them. They clear the zone in 1 day.
Saturday comes around. We zone into Time, and have to reclear phase 1 again. After that, we can run straight up to phase 4, and kill VZ and TZ, then carry on with phase 5 until we clear Quarm.
Carry on through the week until Thursday comes around again. We zone into Time, and clear phase 1 - 3 and clear terris and saryrn as they are the only ones up. VZ, TZ, phase 5 and 6 haven't respawned yet.
Alarius does their clearing on Friday with their own instance.
Watchers goes back to time on Saturday. We clear phase 1 as always, then clear the VZ, TZ in phase 4, and clear phase 5, but suppose we screw up and can't do quarm.
Carry on to Thursday. We zone into Time, clear p1-3, and Terris and Saryrn, then run through phase 5 because it hasn't respawned and do Quarm.
When it gets to saturday, we will have to do p1, VZ, TZ, then phase 5, then we'll be done for the day since the other stuff hasn't respawned.

As it is, I think Sojo on our server raids before Alarius does on their 2 days. Unless maybe Sojo has taken over our Thursday. I'm not sure. Sojo could zone into Time at say 6:00pm on whatever day they raid it, clear phase 1 2 3 before 10:00pm, then zone out and let their instance expire for the day. When it expires, Alarius could then zone in after them, do their own instance as far as they can get, and there isn't competition for the mobs. The only competition that happens is for what guild gets time on which day.

You'd have to talk to some of the officers of Enshadowed/Watchers/Alarius/Sojourners to find out what the Time raiding schedule currently is.
I know Watchers does Time 1 day on Saturdays now, thus giving up our Thursday quite soon, if we haven't already done that (only 2 1-day clearings so far)
Enshadowed as Time on Monday/Tuesday I think. Alarius I think had Time on Wednesday/Friday.
I think Enshadowed took over Sunday when SR left the zone so they could do Time in 1 day like SR used to do.
Sojo has probably taken our Thursday spot, or taken Sunday if no one else took it, or raids before Alarius since Sojo starts very early, and Alarius starts very late.
I don't know how you guys will fit into the zone, but Zenith is Australian or Asian or Japanese time, is it not?
You guys should be able to prety much raid whenever you want to.
You'll most likely want to raid about 12 hours after Sojo, since they start raiding so early that when they nab the phase 1 loots on their day, the named will have respawned so you can get their loot again when you go in about 12 hours later.
