Response from the Guild Summit Boards! Happy happy :)

Hi Gloriana,

Yes, Nadox is on a 3-day timer. However, if you do the quest in the correct order and have the appropriate bits, Spiritseeker Nadox will spawn when you zone in. If someone has recently killed Spiritseeker Nadox before you arrived, you will have to wait two hours before you can zone in again to trigger the spawn (that also counts for retries if you fail). Once you have completed this portion of the quest, you will not be able to trigger the spawn anymore.

If you are doing this event out of order in the epic, you will have to wait the general 3-day timer because it is outside of the progression of the quest.

So, be sure you are at the correct spot in the quest before you do this event unless you're willing to camp it for the 3 days.

Vahlar

UPDATE OCTOBER 5!!!

After Vahlar described what was supposed to happen, many shaman were still stuck at that step. Vahlar immediately investigated the issue, found a fix and is implementing it across live servers.

Howdy folks,

I've made a few changes that should make this a little easier the next time we get the zones patched. I'm doing my best to have this change in asap (within a day or so).

If you are at the correct portion of the quest (have the correct flag), you will be "notified" when you zone into Nadox if you have awakened a nasty spirit thief. This still functions the same way in that you have to be at the right "spot" in the quest or he will ignore your presence.

It seemed this didn't work for several of you and I apologize for that. All should be well when we get this change on the live servers. I honestly did everything I could to remove bottlenecks from this quest and am very sorry several of you had to endure long camps.

That said, if you are not at the right spot in the quest, you will have to endure the natural spawn time of Nadox. That won't change. Awakening Nadox is a gift to those who are at that point in the story.

I'm still reading these boards with regularity, so I'll be keeping an eye on the progress.

Again, apologies for any hard times,

Vahlar/Holly

Some additional clarification:

1. If a shaman spawns Nadox successfully and another player kills/loots the mob, the shaman who spawned Nadox will not be bugged. In 2 hours, the shaman can spawn him again. My recommendation - have some friends on the spawn point to ensure your group gets the kill of your spawned mob!

2. Swarmfiend, chests and you (per Vahlar)- As far as the Swarmfiend, you have to be at the right part in the quest and only have killed it once in order to get the chest loot. We added that into the quests to provide rewards for encounters where you need several people to help so you don't go through the agony of begging a ton of friends to come along to help with your event with no reward for them, but it's a one-time deal. So, if you've gotten the chest once, you shouldn't get it twice from the same encounter. And, if you're not at the right point in the quest, you won't get it either, so it pays to do some things in order in this case
