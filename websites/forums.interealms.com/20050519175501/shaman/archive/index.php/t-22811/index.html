Fudapuda
IMO that duo would have a very difficult time against any "named" mob in any of the Sewers.

The Sewers vary in difficulty quite a bit--IIRC, Purifying Plant is the hardest, while Crematory is easiest?? (Purifying Plant has golems that are non-slowable, so you won't ever even get close to where any named might spawn). That duo might be able to handle the yard trash in Crematory (since you have to kill 20 mobs before any named spawn) but after that, since you have no tracker in your duo, you are going to have to just crawl from end to end of the dungeon to see if any named are up, and getting a pull of 2 or more mobs will destroy you. Yard trash hits at max of anywhere from 400-600, and they tend to have large aggro ranges.

I'd really suggest recruiting a full group to do the crawling. Some of the gear that drops is nice enough for any pre-Elemental PC to use, plus spells drop and BiC pieces drop. I'd also strongly urge you to make sure you have a tracker with you--spawn the instance, kill 20 mobs near ZI, then track to see if any named popped. If not, kill the instance and start over. It will save you a TON of time.
