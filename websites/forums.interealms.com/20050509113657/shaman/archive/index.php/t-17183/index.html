Calabar
If you do decide to hunt on the beach in Gunthak, I’d recommend keeping Lev up at all times. If you get into trouble the run to the zone is not a long one, but you have to cross a lot of water to get back to the lighthouse. For some reason the powers that be don’t seem to penalize mob movement speed in water like they do ours. Even with 200 swimming I can’t keep ahead of them. But with levitate you can quickly outrun them and zone out.

I cast levitate on myself any time I’m heading back to the lighthouse from Dulak. Half the time my invisibility will drop half way through the tunnel and I have the rest of the tunnel following me to the water. Trust me, lev can be a life saver. And if you are in need of batwings, they are sold in the lighthouse.
