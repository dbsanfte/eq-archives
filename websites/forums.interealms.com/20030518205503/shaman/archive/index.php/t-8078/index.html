Azhdarkhos
So close and yet so far...

Official word, given at many a Fan Faire, and backed up by an *enormous* amount of in-game testing by players (info found at www.eqtraders.com):

Skill-up rate is determined by the highest of Int, Wis, or a Secondary Stat if the tradeskill in question has one (Str for smithing, Dex for Fletching, maybe others in the future). In this instance, an ogre smith would use the highest of Int (not likely), Wis or Str to determine rate of Skill-up. For an ogre shaman, Str will likely be the determining stat since it's easy to buff Str to ungodly levels as a high level shaman.

Success rate is determined *solely* by skill level - no other stat affects success rate. Geerlok and similar devices that have a bonus to a tradeskill add to your effective skill for purposes of determining chance of success on a combine. Such items do not affect whether or not you can get skill-ups on items that are near trivial point, nor do they give any bonus to chance for skill-up. At present, these type of items do not stack with each other (i.e. if you have one item with +5% and another with +15%, you would only get the +15% to your skill level.)

Both of these have been extensively tested by players on live servers and have been found to be true. For more information, checkout the various FAQs and such found at www.eqtraders.com, on the main site and in the various forums.
