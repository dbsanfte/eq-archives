alt u brings up the tribute window. there is a personal and a guild tab. The guild tab can only be accessed by a guild leader. For personal tribute, you have to go to your tribute master and pick which skills you want. Then by clicking alt u you can activate/de-activate it anytime you wish.
There are some nice focus effects if you are missing them for spells up to 65 like mana pres 4 etc.. (dont know if they extended it for oow spells) and you can get hp/mana/stats/resists. Personally i just have 25 to all resists that i use for quarm and GoD prismatic/chromatric AE's.

psionic11
Just a head's up --

I was looking for a way to get Q to cast quicker and longer during battle, so I figured I'd get the 2 tributes for quicker and longer lasting buffs, since supposedly quick buff shortens Q cast time. (I have QB1 AA and it does shorten Q cast time).

So I singled out each tribute and activated it to test its effectiveness. The buff extension tribute at Tier 4 had no real effect on how long Q lasted -- the buff icon showed 30secs remaining for an instant but then changed to the usual 24 secs left and proceeded to countdown from there.

Then I tried the quick buff tribute. It did shorten all the normal buffs like Fot7 and such. However since it didn't work with our heals, I won't be using that tribute for quickening heal time in battle.

I did find that Power of Alacrity shortened cast time on our heals, and that is the one to use for more effective healing in battle.
