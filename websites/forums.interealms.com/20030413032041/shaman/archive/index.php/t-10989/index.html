Grickk!!

<a href="http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=7560">A Thought Stealer</a>, and it's PH (the wandering worm 'a mind burrower' that isn't a trap) are on about a 5 hour, 40 minute timer +/- 20 minutes. According to info, the 60 thought stealer spawns about 1 in 4 PH, and if you are after the <a href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=7158">Ethereal Earring</a>, seems like a less than 5% chance for it to drop.

On the chief, it seems that VI's modified the PH for that. Formely I've seen that if you are in the corner (between the hill and ruins) then the closest mob to the little valley along the hill and corner of the ruins, if spawned a goranga Tribeseer, that would be the main PH for the Chief. Rumor had it that any mob around the tree could be a potential PH. Lately, I've seen first hand that the PH for the Chief is still a goranga tribeseer, but if the tribeseer pops next to the Chief spawn location (the mob location closest to the ruins, same back row as chief, just one mob to the left if you are on the hill with the ruins to your left.) then Chief has a good chance to spawn on the next repop.

Good luck Grickk!!
