My advice would be to place your first 5 points into Improved Healing Wave. This will take .5 off of the cast time of your Healing Wave spell. At first all you get is healing wave and each new LV of it adds .5 to cast time. At about lv22 your get Lesser Healing Wave which is your blast heal. You will then have LHW 1.5 cast and HW 3.0 (2.5).

For PvE I have found that a hibred build of Restore/Enhancment works well for me. What I have head for the PvP is to go more into the Elemental and to get Natures Swift.

Natures Swift is a really usefull one to have. It allows you to once every 3 minits turn a spell into an insta cast. It can be a real butt saver when used with a heal. Or you can use it with a Lighting Bolt combo to turn out some quick damage. How I use it is lets say I am about to kill a caster mob. My cast order would be Lighting bolt, Earth Shock, Natures Swift, Lighting Bolt, Mele. What would happen is that the first lighting bolt hits the mob would start to cast. Then Earth shock hits and interupts and stuns the casting so the mob will come after me. Right befor it gets to me the second lighting bolt will hit. At this point most caster mobs will be down to half HP's or lower. Very usefull when you have a pair or more of mobs together and need to drop one quick.
