Firstly check the other post in this section. Also, I think that a partner maybe beneficial for you. I think there are 3 wanderers. I killed these as they could be ph for priest. Secondly for info, names hit for 300-400, which should be easy enough once slowed but something to take into consideration.
My personal style was invising past a lot of spawns once I found which after I had previously killed the see invis ones. But I have suspend minion which is pretty nice for use in here.
Respawn is around 30 mins, at 65 grimlings seemed to have pretty low aggro range. For the Anarchist spawn, I basically killed 2 wanderers, then killed one just in on the left, then pulled the ph/anarchist.
The mobs have pretty high hp which I have guess you have found, so to prepare for this get some mage weapons for pet, and maybe a ba4 item if you don't have. I could stack Bane+blood +ancient. But this uses a fair bit of mana. I have a pretty good mana pool though and had Kei. Watch out also as they will dispell and can run, so either root or give a snare weapon to pet possibly.
I think it would be most beneficial though you had a partner so you can learn your way around and discover the key areas here and where you fight etc.
A consideration also is there are 2 names which pop in outer Acrylia, primalist and ritualist if you want an alternative.
