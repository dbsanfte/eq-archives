Well I’m home from a night out at my favorite brewpub. Am I buzzed, yeah. Am I drunk, maybe. Have I thought about EQ the last half of the night? Hell yeah!

First, all you people that just rant on anyone to feel good about your pathetic little self please stop reading this and go back to your sad little existence. (Even though most Shaman seem to be cool.)

I love life, I love the world and everything in it. That being said I think I hate more people than I like. You know what I mean.

Sitting at a bar with lots of good friends having fun and drinking good beer, what do I eventually think of? EQ. We all have put a bunch of time into our characters. A lot of us more than one. Right now I am without a guild but I remember everyone that I have been with in a guild and would do anything for them. I sit drinking my last Oatmeal Stout waiting to get home to check out my trader. I want to see if one of my friends from Canada, Austria or Slovenia are on. I miss them and want to see if they are on so I can help them for helping me get my sad little bear corpse rezz’d. If no one is on I want to lvl like a madman. If I’m too lazy to grind I want to find a newbie (even though there are few these days) to give them some gear and buffs. I remember when a guild member’s house went up in flames and we all helped out with donations. We have no idea who someone really is but we care about them because we really do know them.

Anyway, most people in EQ rule. Shaman’s and this board rules. I hope enough people stay on EQ after EQ2 starts to keep it fun for a while until we all go to EQ2 which I think they will. I don’t expect to get any responses to this but just maybe a bump up so a few people can read it even though it’s just my going on and on.

Thanks much,
Eisgiest, 57 Shaman
