Personally, I duo-box a wizard. (65 shm and 52 wiz) He isn't the best companion for hunting, but the wiz is certainly not bad and fits my play style perfectly. The mana regen is sometimes a problem, but that has been minimized with a horse for him and KEI whenever I can get it. The main reason I like the wiz are for the ports (mainly evac) and translocate. He acts as my emergency backup when I solo. Things get too hairy... evac. If you get creative, translocate boxes can be used in interesting ways to provide for some, um, fun experiments. :)

For DPS and high xp gain, the wiz isn't the top choice, but he isn't bad by any means. For utility, he's top notch and a lot of fun to have.
