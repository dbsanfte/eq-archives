Well, lets see.

First you have to understand FV's special rules. Mainly TLC and no No Drop items.

TLC means that if a mob is green to you or anyone in your party it will not drop any lore or magic items. So that means no farming of stuff green to you.

The no No Drop means that you can buy any gear except epic related items. (you still can buy jade reavers, pegasus cloak, and such.)

FV is a younger server. So we dont have people in Vex Thal or doing the top planes yet. Which means that other stuff is hit often by the top guilds and they can sell all the drops if they wanted. But that doesnt happen often.

It takes time but if you are creative you can earn lots of pp.
