Sword fish are not near the sirens luckily..

When you fist zone in from Cobalt Scar you'll be on an area. You can only go so far in the area without getting wet. There is one mob in your way to get to the first pool. That mob can drop a molkor Hide, providing of course if it's a molkor. If you look acroos the first pool you'll see more land mobs, they can drop the hide as well, if they are a molkor. You should be able to cast on them standing at the waters edge.

The first pool contains 3 fish/horse. They can only be fish/horse but are random spawns. The tooth only drops from the fish, but the tooth is pretty common. I'd say 1-3 will drop a tooth.

You should be able to solo for the hide or tooth provided you are decently equiped and I'd say level 60 (torpor) but I'm sure someone could prove me wrong and do it at a lower level.

If your hunting the tooth and get in trouble, just get out of the water. Fish cant follow you, also when you get out of water it seems to clear agro after they reset back to their spawn points.... much like a feign death. So if you accidently pull 3, get out of water and watch them move back, get back in water they shouldn't instantly agro you, unless your too close to them to begin with. Fish/Horse have a pretty big agro radius.

The named seahorse can spawn from any fish/horse, so you may get lucky and get a 20-40k haste belt out of it.
If you hunt here for long you'll also get a nice fishing pole.
