Haagen
I decided to take a long break about a month ago, 60% of which was determined by not having enough time to play, 40% was being dissatisfied with the game. On my server (7th Hammer) Kod'Taz is just now beginning to get popular as an exp zone. So I dusted off the shamizzle and put a group together of Time+ geared folks.

I honestly had a lot of fun. Fights lasted about 45-60 seconds (wizzy/mage/sk/paladin/shaman/cleric, so our DPS wasn't bad), long enough where slow made a huge difference and both Bane and BoS had time to run the full duration.

On top of that, I actually had fun. It reminded me of really good cappy groups in KC, or frenzy groups in velks. That kind of like "it's fun killing things" fun. I don't know how to describe it. It just didn't feel like I was running myself into the dirt playing a game I didn't like. Maybe it was killing stuff in a new zone, I don't know. We got a couple named, dropped the FT3 aug (sigh), and that was a hoot as well.

Just one thing though, cash loot sucks! They need to make some of the hynid horns and other wierd drops sell for a few plat instead of a couple gold.

But anyway I had a really good time. I'm looking forward to playing again when I have time.

I have been a big fan of GoD (except our aa's) since the get go, so many may not like my opinion :p

GoD is challenging - even after the retune. A lot of people do not like that nor are they willing to do pita cr's in case something goes bad.

As it stands now, (trials 1-6 and a quick hit in KT as my reference point) GoD seems to be fine. For decently equipped people the trials may seem easy, but a lapse in concentration could still result in a cr.
