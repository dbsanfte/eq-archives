Kriel Darkbane
If you want to PL a bard, you're going about it the wrong way.

All you need to do is:

1) Get him an earring of the solstice (ft 1).
2) Get him boots of flowing slime (ft3) or some other ft2+ item (not easy)
3) Learn to swarm kite using charm.
4) Buff him up and get the shaman the heck out of the way.

Bard solo'ing is stupidly fast and broken due to swarm kiting - IF the bard has some mana regeneration. Basically nothing increases a bard's mana regen except mental clarity AA or flowing thought items. And charm takes 20 (?) mana a pop at that level. Normally you're getting back 1 mana /tick, or 10 mana per minute. This isn't nearly enough for continuous charm kiting. The L 39 charm takes 60 mana, and lasts at most one minute. Due to mobs dying or charm breaking, you need more than 60 mana/minute for continuous charming.

With a few points of FT, you can nearly continuously swarm kite and make phenomenal experience solo. I have little doubt that you can level much faster this way than with a shaman PL'ing you in the 40's and 50's. The drawback is that you'll need to work melee skills separately.

A normal untwinked bard can't do this all that fast, as the price of boots of flowing slime or other tradable high-FT item (there are very few a bard can use) is beyond them. But if you can afford to twink the bard, or loan him something off your shaman, he can solo for exp very fast without further aid. Even with only FT4, you can go almost continuously, if you swarm kite a pack down and then kill the last one with good old-fashioned fear/snare/melee or chant kiting.

Bard swarm kiting exp is one of the broken things in the game, and is the reason that the bard PoP charm spell sucks so badly (very low level of mobs charmable). At L 65 a bard can charm nothing in zones worth good exp, but up until L 60ish a bard can make obscene solo exp if he knows how. I personally wish they'd find a way to break swarm kiting and up the level a high level bard can charm, but that's not likely. Until they do... enjoy. ;)
