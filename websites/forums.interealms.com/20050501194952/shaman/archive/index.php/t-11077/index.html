Wally6500
Been to Fear many times so I'll just list some observations and suggestions.

1. Terror Tentacles are not you friend. The have a large range debuff. If these guys are pulled to the camp you can quickly debuff the whole raid.

2. For AE MoBs I would wear all the MR gear you have. I like to get my resists as high as possible.

3. Do not sit down unless you are told you can.

4. Maloxx is your friend. There will probably be lots of adds so you will need to help the chanters as much as you can. Maloing adds will make it easier for them to do thier thing and easier for you to root.

5. Do not wander. The fastest way to wipe a raid is wondering around and agroing a bunch of MoBs.

You didnt say anything about who is going with you. If it is a group that has been to Fear a bunch you should be ok.

Even the easy Golem is nasty. The best is when someone agroes Fright or Terror and he does an accros the zone DT.
