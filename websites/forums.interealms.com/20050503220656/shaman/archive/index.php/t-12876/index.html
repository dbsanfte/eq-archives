Shaman have two types of slows, magic based and disease based.

* Magic based - Turgurs (level 51) slows up to 75% by level 60. Resists vs. magic but "cured" via cure disease. This is a fairly recent but positive change making it possible to cure a slow off a melee character without stripping all their beneficial buffs. Turgur's has a duration of 6.5 minutes at level 65.

* Disease based - Cloud of Grummus (level 61) - slows up to 40%. Resists vs. disease and cured via disease. We get a lower level version of this spell Plague of Insects at level 54 (25% slow). There are some mobs which are only susceptible to disease based slows (Turgurs won't land). Cloud of Grummus has a duration of 3.3 minutes at level 65.

We also get Tigir's Insects (level 58) which is a spell that can slow up to 4 targets (including the caster, if you're not careful) by 50% at level 60. Tigirs has a duration of 3.3 minutes at level 65.
