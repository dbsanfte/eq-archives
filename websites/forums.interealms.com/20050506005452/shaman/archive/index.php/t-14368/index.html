IIRC adv healing gift is 2,3,4. Adv fury of magic is 3 6 (9)? (not at level 3 yet).

Yeah you have a lot that can be trained. Combat stab, healing crits, healing adept, crit nukes, planar power, RC3, SCRM, chanelling focus,- it's all good stuff and I couldn't live without any of it at this point. So as to what to do first.......

Well um combat stab definitely helps with mitigation. Healing crits/adept - with elemental arms and crit/adept 6 I *consistently* crit for 3300+ with tnargs, so that's handy :P Crit nukes obviously up your dps, planar power your hps, RC3 is useful against the smorgasboard (sp) of aes you'll come across, SCRM *will* increase the length of your buffs (items stack on top of the 50 percent), chanelling focus I think I would slit my wrists in plane of water without it.

You could just do them all at once. Make a list of the stuff you'd like to train and then just start throwing points at them cheapest first. It's probably the most fun, at this point you'll be training something every time you earn 2 AA. And since its all useful you'll won't find yourself lacking in one particular area as you progress.
