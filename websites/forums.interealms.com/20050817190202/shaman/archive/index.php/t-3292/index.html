Depends, Seafuries drop about 4 to 6pp each and gems and rings going from about 17pp up to 90pp or so. Luck is a big part of this for the gem drops. You can also pop Gornit there for HG type loot. Just stay away from Quag Maelstrom, a named Seafury because he has mana drain and you will die unless you have toys. The corrupted is no walk in the park either.

Toyless, Seafuries take 2 EBolts, slow and dogdog and melee, you can melee them without DOTS or 1 DOT but that takes longer I think. Slowed they are puss. Nice exp on Seafuries at 51 till 53.

OOT map at http://www.eqatlas.com/oot.html but go East and a little North from first dock from Freeport, it's on Sisters Island, Seafury Island is the second island over, can't miss them they are just larger versions of the Rathe clops. Note that zone is backwards and when you zone in on the boat from Freeport you are put at the eastern end of the zone and from Faydwer the western.
