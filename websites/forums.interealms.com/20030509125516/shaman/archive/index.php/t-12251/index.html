Schpleef
I've been considering setting up a wireless network myself, but haven't yet - so although I don't have personal experience, I can tell you what I've read & heard - the 2.4 Ghz spectrum is pretty crowded, with phones, wireless networks, microwave ovens, and even some fluorescent light fixtures. Getting a 5.2Ghz phone would help, however setting your network to channel 1 or 11 has been reported to help with phone interference.
