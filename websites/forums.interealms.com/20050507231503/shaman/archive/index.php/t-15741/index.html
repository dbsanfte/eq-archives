This is a repost from my guild's boards. I wondered who has input/education for me as we don't have too many enchanters at the moment, and are in the process of recruiting some. This seems the best place for advice on this matter:


Here's the scenario: The group consists of one shaman, one enchanter, and at least two tanks. For this scenario we're going to assume both tanks are capable of being the MA, if needs be.

We're fighting tougher mobs - hitting in the 200 - 400 range. On 'incoming' the puller calls an add.

The shaman starts casting malxxx, the enchanter is ready to Tash (both low enough on the hate list to not pull aggro - usually). Slow begins to cast right about as the first mob is as camp. Three seconds later, the mob is usually slowed, and the add is unslowed and wailing on the tank. Complete heal has already started.

What do you do about the second add? There seem to be three main options here, and I'd like to know what is the general concensus?

1) The second tank pulls the add away, and we fight it as normal - malxxx, tash and slow.

2) The enchanter mezzes it, then the shaman slows it.

3) The shaman slows it, then the enchanter mezzes it.

Personally, I vote for three. The reason being with mobs as hard as the ones we are fighting, a resisted mezz means almost certain death for the Enchanter as the cleric would be mid CH as the enchanter pulls aggro, and honestly the shaman heal is unlikely to get off before the enchanter dies, even with Chloroblast, our quick heal.

If the slow gets resisted, the shaman will pull insane aggro, and the enchanter will get at least 2 mezz attempts (in my experience) before they go to the top of the hate list and pull aggro from the shaman. The shaman should be able to survive that long.

So it seems to be a simple choice - both the enchanter and the shaman are crowd control, but the SAFEST way to do it seems to be for the highest AC/HP character to take the aggro, then let the enchanter do their work.

Am I missing something here? Do the enchanters in the guild have some advice on why it's better to let them mez first? This is an important issue for us as we are now mostly fighting the higher end mobs that kill the casters in one round - we need a very tight strategy to survive. I'd like us to know these things before we group together, so we can get straight down to business.


I'd appreciate any input - no flames if I'm making some dumb assumptions please :)
