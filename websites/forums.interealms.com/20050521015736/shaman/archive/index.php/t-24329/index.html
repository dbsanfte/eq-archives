Valsuvious
Here are the ranks and what they don't stack with:

To read this. Each spell has a specific stacking ability with other spells/items. The spell itself has different slots. Therefore, ancestral aid 1 has seven slots. Each slot has specific ability (for instance, slot 3 has the ability of increasing the dex cap) If anything is in the same slot as it, it will either not stack or override it. Now, it will override if the features of AA are better or the same than what each person already has on. This is why it will not override Rabid Bear or Diaku (cause of them having a higher dex and str value in the same slot) and also why it won't override Spirit of the Grove AA until you get AA2/3. However if the druid has spirit of the grove 3rd rank than you could only override it if you have the 3rd rank of AA. If you don't notice, there is nothing on here stacking issue wise with using it in combination with our HoT spells and epic click on at the same time $$

To cap this off.. if you ever have a question about what will and will not stack on any spells, I suggest you use this website: Lucy (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/) and imput the name of the spell. After that look to the top right and click on the stacking button. There it will tell you exactly what it will and will not stack with.
