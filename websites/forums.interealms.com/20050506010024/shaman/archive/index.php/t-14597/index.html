Lesiter
He's on a spawn timer just like VaDyn's. I can't remember off the top of my head if it's 3 or 7 days though.

Once the IC is up, you have 1 hour to engage before it despawns. It is slowable, doubles for 200ish on a defensive warrior, has an aoe mana drain, and spawns a bunch of little crawlers if anybody dies.

If you pin him against the wall in the hallway directly north of his spawn point, you can hide your mana-users against the wall at the base of the steps. Just make sure your tank is in range before the fight starts.

Easily doable by 1 group of 65s or 2 groups of 55+
