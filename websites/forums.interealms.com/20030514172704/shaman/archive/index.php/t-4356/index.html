I hunted the same place you are, at the same levels...great spot. :)

For casters in general - send in your pet first, let him get it's attn. After a bit of aggro has built up, land a DOT or a slow (usually a DOT is better). When pet is down to less than 50% health, I will
wade into melee - find a FAST weapon, something other than a 2hb and try to interrupt as many spells as possible...with dogdog on the back of the mob and you in the front you should be ok.

Another bit - mobs can't cast while running, so have pet engage at a distance, then DoT, nuke, sit until the mob is mad enough to come running to you...if you have room to 'kite' a bit you can keep them from casting much.

Specific info on the Geomancers - they are just a pain in the butt. Crappy loot, more trouble than they are worth. I would tag one with a DOT and run back to my camp spot, he would usually cast once on me and then come running. park dogdog far enough behind you so that he doesn't engage mob before he gets close to camp (to prevent adds). Then wade into melee and try to do as much damage as possible as quickly as possible. I would still use a Poison DOT over the nukes, the efficiency is much higher.
