Ridik
As a former, Jr. Ubah shammy I can relate to your situation. I had Fungi, RBG, and a brawl stick in the 40s and I was a monster. That was before I'd gotten a horse, jbb, or epic. To answer your question, yes you are quite the Jr. Uber shaman, and you will stay that way quite happily for another 10 to 15lvls or so. JBB will be god for your next 5lvls, and still an amazing item thru your career(I still use mine a TON at 61 soloing). At 49 you get some nice spells and it is worth changing your play style a bit to make the best use of them. You'll kill faster, but probably notice a bit more downtime. Then a few lvls later you get Regrowth and Turgurs, and everything is back up to Jr. Uber status again. Dont take as much damage due to slow and hp regen goes up so you can Canatank well. I think the "Jr. Uber" phase faded for me around 56 or 57, when I was still a much better soloer than most classes, but just didnt have quite the upper hand on my mobs that I had in lvls prior. However, at 59 and 60 I got Horse, Torpor and Epic and am feeling like quite the bomb again. With horse and Stoicism I think you'll maintain your current enjoyment quite well. But work on your epic as early as you can too, also an esential soloing tool.
