Puller rushes right pulls golem to stairs out of ch range.
Shaman runs left occupies other golem up where he pops untill the group has finished.

I've split them many times, as a 50's monk... here's how I do it...

1) Clear out the 3 spawn room at the top of the stairs when you enter the crypt (where the new fallen monk spawns)

Clear the wanderer (PH for caretaker)

Monk pulls... trick here, is having a good tagger...

Monk runs down, waits for ONE of the ICG's to start casting, and runs up the stairs... as soon as that spell hits, monk fd's, tagger tags the ICG that followed the monk... badda bing, they are split.

:)

Hope this is something that might help ya out a bit...
