Your friend is wrong. Its 10% increase total, it does not stack. The way Magelo shows it is exactly how it works.

Don't forget, that while unbuffed it will add an additional 221 HP, buffing STA will also earn you the extra 10%. So just from an additional 70 Sta (Boar + PE) you'll get another 23 hp extra from ND3, and that keeps adding up all the way till 305 STA. So for you, you'll be looking at an increase of 244 HP currently, with the potential for a lot more as you gain more gear and Sta.
