So, what new aa skill would you like to see for shaman in OoW?

Post your idea, then we can assemble them in the form of a poll to see what people in general would like to see.

The rules:

1) You get to post one idea only. No page long listings please.
2) Do not attach costs or magnitude to the proposed skill.
3) Be realistic. No DT 2.0 abilities or anything like that. Remember that most aa skills grant small incremental improvements, stuff that does not isnt going to happen.
4) Dont post ideas other people have allready posted. That is not constructive.
5) Don't forget the small convience sort of abilitities.
6) Keep it simple. One sentence will do. There is no need for defence or sales pitches. If the effect isn't obvious from the title, add a brief one sentence explanation of the concept in brackets.

Examples (these are not suggestions btw)

Spear of torment enhanced damage
Spear of torment reduced mana
Breath of Trushar enhanced healing
Daluda's mending enhanced healing
Enhanced innate debuff (applies a neg resist modifier to all spells)

Ideas proposed to date:

Class A: Offensive abilities

1) Dire spell casting speed (works for detrimental spells the way Quickbuff works for buffs)
2) Advanced spirit call III
3) Extended Torment AA
4) Pet avatar (invul, on a timer, insta-cast)
5) Enhanced dot crits (every round, on a timer) [Duration timed or applied to the next cast dot?]
6) Innate Rabidity
7) Dire charm animal
8) Pet dual wield


Class B: Defensive abilities

1) Enhanced virulent paralysis (something to reduce reuse time and/or resists)
2) Some sort of personal defensive "disc" type AA.
3) Reduced slow mitigation
4) Slow affinity (resist modifier?)
5) Group damage mitigation

Class C: Healing/curing abilities

1) MGB Radiant Cure
2) MGB HoT
3) HoT adept

Class D: Buffing abilities

1) Enhanced buff magnitude (more hp on focus for example)
2) Enhanced max hit buff (on a timer, short duration)

Class E: Personal modifiers

1) Hastened Canni 5 (reduces the re-use timer on Canni 5)
2) Canni 5 crits
3) Sacrificial Canni (die, and give mana conversion to another, similar to pally sacrificial heal)
4) enhanced spell casting subtlety
5) Group canni
6) Improved invis
7) Passive canni

Class F: Misc. abilities

1) Unstopable spell casting (insta cast next spell, allways lands, long timer)
2) Advanced suspended minion (allow repeated zoning with a suspended pet)
