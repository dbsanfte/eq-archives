Viken
You can single pull at 60 or use Harmony if less then 60. He has 100,000 hitpoints and double hits for 270. He only stays up for about 10 or 15 minutes at most. If you fail to kill him within that time you have to wait 10 hours to try again. If the scout is holding a bag that means someone has already triggered the giants and attempted the quest. The note that you get when you complete the quest only results in more faction hits and maybe a few plat.
