Shugamayatu
I don't think folks are interpreting your question correctly. If I understand your question correctly, namely:

1. you as part of a group of 6 start the trial.
2. you die sometime between porting in, and the final kill.
3. can you be rezed and loot your mark, than leave?

Answer -
Yes!
you will have to be rezed 2 times though.

First time will bounce you to the trial staging area, second time will bring you to your corpse.

Note: chain clicking won't do much as you're "still zoning" from rez one, while recieving rez 2. So rez (bounce to trial stage), then ask for rez 2. Loot, leave, life is good.
