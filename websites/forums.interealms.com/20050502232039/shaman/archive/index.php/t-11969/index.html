Thyrm Jarlson
When you registered the 2nd account, it automatically got a 30 day free period as all new accounts. You will need to wait until that is over before you can transfer your character. I moved my shaman from my main account to a new one 8 or so months ago and had to wait for the trial period to end.
As the above poster mentioned, you DO need to have the exact same expansions and billing info on both accounts (and said billing needs to be the original billing info on the account).
