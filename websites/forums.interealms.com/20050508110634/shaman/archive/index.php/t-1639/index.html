Epyx
The best xp spot is the walkway around the city. You can enter it as you walk into the city proper...to each side you will see a small raised platform with a floating square glass section. Clicking on this glass will teleport you to the walkway around the city.

All gaurds here are blue to 60 and while tougher than Katta gaurds are about par with Gorangas/Toilers imo. All are slowable/rootable and can drop up to 24pp each. All in all a great spot to duo as you can make good xp and money. Shaman/Melee (of course) and Shaman/Necro work great here.
