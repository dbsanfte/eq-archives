Jukax
Every little bit helps out yourself and your guild. Don't feel guilty if you won / were awarded something on the raid. Just imagine, if you had 10 slots of items with just a small upgrade of 2-5 ac and some stats, that adds up pretty quickly. The only time I would pass on an item that would benefit you is if it would benefit someone in your guild much more, or you are holding out for something even better. eg, having Jaundiced Bone BP and passing over on Skyshrine BP while you're waiting for your chance at a Vindi BP.

People forget that EQ, at the high 50's + game, is all about small leaps and bounds. Until you hit the "Uber" end game, you won't find items that are a vast improvement over what you currently wear.

Frekkels

Circlet of Vallon / Tallon go to monks because they are one of the highest AC head items monks can get at Kael raid levels and they are light.

You could have taken the Circlet, but you have lots of other upgrades that over what they do. They have other choices from Tormax/Dain/Vulak/Ssra/TOV or Cowl of Mortality etc... or else they are wearing something like a frog crown or a shissar scale headband. I've seen lots of monks wearing Circlets of Vallon/Tallon at 60. Sucks for them, cause it looks like a paper bag! :D

upgrades for you: Thurg/Pog Chain Helm, Skyshrine/Kael chain helm, Circlet of the Falinkan, Slime Covered Rubicite Coif, Tae Ew Spirit Callers Coif, Mud Smuged Cap, Artificers Ringmail (or other racial smithed) -hrm, you're a kitty I think so this doesn't apply, Spirit Talkers Headdress, any of the hats from the dragons in West Wastes (dragonskull helms - they have good sta) ... etc - none of these are uber mob drops.

Point is, lots of upgrades for you - not as many for them. Circlet of vallon/Tallon is barely an upgrade from JB. You can do better, don't worry about the ugly bag hat.
