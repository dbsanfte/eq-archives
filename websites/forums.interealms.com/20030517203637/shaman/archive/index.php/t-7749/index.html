Jorlaan
Been hunting in DSP lately. Just dinged 31 in there. (hrmmm that probably means my sig is WAAAY out of date :-) ). Tried the shroom temple for the first time last night.

There seem to be a few "traps" that spawna shroom on you when you walk through doors (or the shrooms are there invis). I get a message when it happens though, so I expect a trap.

If you expect it, then you can just yank that shroom outside to fight.

The shrooms don't path straight from their "stones" to you, so you are trying to yank them outside, expect at least one add. It seems better to stay inside the building and let proximity aggro pull them to you.

There are a couple of smaller shroom patches around the zone that have 3 or so shrooms. There's one just to the left as you leave netherbian lair. There's one over the hill to the left near some vendors who don't seem to help or aggro :-).

My wizzie partner and I can pull those three shrooms, and then yank a few zel's or even 'red' wolves before repop with KEI. The effort on the wolves isn't worth it, but its nice to say you can take down a red or two :-)

Experience/downtime is better for a duo in the shroom building though, even without KEI.
