Factions are:

Prisoners of Justice (generally humanoid prisoners)
Creatures of Justice (generalyy non-humanoid prisoners)

If I recall correctly, you start KoS with Creatures and non-KoS to Prisoners.

Guards have no faction. Rats have no faction.

Not sure about the denizens of the Grallok caves.
