In Kael, on the ones you can slow, Proximity is not a factor on the giants (i.e. usually talking about w/in melee range).

"Or is it only applicable to dragon mobs, etc? "

No it applies to others as well you just have to learn which ones they are. For example the Fire giants in Sol B you have to be in Melee range to slow them.
