IshyDrackos
Spear in hand here I sit.
warming my hands before a fire lit.

My companion lays by my feet, transparent in the glow.
As I mourn an age of not that long ago.

Ghosts and memories float across my eyes as I stare at the fire
flooding my soul with despair and ire.

Many and varied be those I knew of not that so long ago
where, oh where, did they all go

faded to dust,
faded away,
where they went,
who am I to say

bitter tears I cry, for those who are now dust
I continue on doing what I must,

There is no rest for me, don't you see,
long have I labored here among the stones, grass and trees
doomed to linger on
While all those I knew are now long gone.

Shaken from my reverie
I stand to see what is there
no rest for me I see

Grabbing my spear and calling spirits to my side, I go into the night
doomed to stay, doomed to tarry, doomed to fight
I charge into the darkness, to continue this never ending fight.
