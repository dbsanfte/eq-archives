Slargg
your gear isn't all that great, dont worry about peeps being upset that you have better gear than they did.
You didn't give a budget but I will try to guess. If you have a lot more plat than your gear shows say so for some higher end advise.

Some tips:
earing of the solstice(should be about the same as bs electrum for you now and will just keep getting better, replace the fingerbone hoop.
Amulet of insight(a very nice focus on this and neck is quite a tough slot to upgrade pre 51)
shissar scale hat
hardened grey flesh sleeves do give a bit more hp but not a lot so replace as budget allows
ods(focus till 60 rather than 44 will help a lot, alternatively get a sharp claw whatever from pop with affliction efficiency 4 for a better focus, except when buffing)
Fungi tunic(this should be a big priority for you as you should be able to at the very least downgrade some other slots to afford one. If you just cant manage it get an ikky bp)
Heiro cloak(I assume that you can afford this as it will last you a long time, otherwise get an ancient seahorse hide cloak)
brown chitin legs(loose a bit of ac for more hp and mana, or go for mq thurg or ss legs)
barbed dragon boots
sarnak battle shield, or skull marked. if you have the cash go for ooiv
your range is nice but if you have to sell to buy fungi do it and grab a sandwich of foul smelling herbs or even goranga.

Dont worry about dropping a huge chunk of wis to get more hp and regen. What you should be most concerned with at this point is getting a fungi and a horse. Your manapool will only be a concern for a very short time in your late 40s and early 50s, and only if duoing with a poorly geared tank. As you level up and get can 3 and 4 your hp pool becomes much more important. Also without having good hp/ac you cant be as effective in duo/group/solo or even raid type situations.

Personally, I had a similar(if not better) quality of gear as you do and I have been quite happy with having 150 wis. I am able to tank pretty effectively and I am never afraid to slow on inc. Now, at level 50 I am only looking for ways to get more hp and higher ac, wis is a very distant concern for me, except when I tradeskill :)
