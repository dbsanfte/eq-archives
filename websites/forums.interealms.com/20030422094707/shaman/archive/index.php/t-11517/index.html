em2johns
I find that farming items that you can sell to high level folks who really don't care is the way to go. On that note, I say hit up your local Splitpaw Lair just off of SK, and farm Gnoll Hide Lariotts and Dagger of Droppings. Sell the daggers for 10pp each in the bazaar, and the GHL for 20-50pp each. I personally pay upto 40pp per Lariott I find in the bazaar. I'm a high level shaman, who drops pp on silly pet weapons.
Good luck man. Way to plan ahead!
Splitpaw is good exp at 34 too, and you can occasionally find high level shamans in there farming Lariotts too. I met a couple really cool newbies in there myself buffing them.
