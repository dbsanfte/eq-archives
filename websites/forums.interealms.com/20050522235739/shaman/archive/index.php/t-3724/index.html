Litchebane
A few pointers:

- in order to sell something it has to be in a traders satchel which you can buy at a vendor near a bank. Weight is irrelevent in Bazaar so load up as much as you want.

- You have to price the item. Same items will all have the same price so no bait and switc, doh! Prices are saved in a .ini file tied to the character. It's marked with chars name and has bazaar in it. It's computer specific so if you switch comps you better copy over that .ini.

- While you can stuff your trader full of 8 satchels holding 10 items each, the buyer can only browse 30 items. So just keep 30 items in the trader's satchels. Fill the rest up with regular bags and switch items in and out as needed.

- When someone buys something from you, the searchable list (/bazaar) is NOT updated until the trader goes out of trader mode and back into it. Hopefully they fix this somehow.

- You can put your trader into afk mode if you want so if someone foolishly sends you a tell you can respond with "Buy my stuff, I'm sleeping!" or whatever.

- you can shrink/grow or otherwise transform your trader if you want to be unique. Of course equipment worn also shows up as normal so play dress up if you want. You can't sell equipped equipment.

That's it for now, happy trading!

Multiple stacks of stackable items take up 1 of your 30 slots total. For instance, I was selling 400 Truffles. When someone comes to buy from me they see 1 Truffle that they can buy 400 times.

When you price things in a stack, you are pricing a single item, not the whole stack.
