Eriadoc
AC is a great thing to build in the lower levels, as you'll want to melee to get that defense up. HP helps a lot with this obviously, but if you are OOM a lot, you'll have to med more. More mana will allow you to fight more critters in a set amount of time. Cannibalize converts 50hp into 20mana, so keep that ratio in mind when you gear up. If an item will give you 50hp, consider it 20mana, and a small buffer to get hit with. It's a good idea, IMO, to get WIS to 200 once you get into your 30s. The reason for this is each WIS point will start to yield 10 mana per. After 200 WIS, it gives nowhere near tha same bang for the buck. So keep that ratio in mind as well. Regen items will help immensely.

And remember, C3 is only 50pp in the Nexus, hehe.
