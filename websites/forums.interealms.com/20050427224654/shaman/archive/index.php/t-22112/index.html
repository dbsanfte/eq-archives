Exin Krup Gutkan
You could easily hold the enterance for a while. This should help you get a bearing on how quickly you can clear. If you can clear quickly excellent. You could also head down to the pit area (directly below zone in) and take out the mobs there.

There are some harm touching mobs, but the enterance isn't that bad. Last I checked and remember there is usually a skele, a spermie, and a golem at enterance, maybe only 2. Then there are 2 mobs on each of the wing enterances.

As for traps, yes there are pits. The fall itself isn't too deadly, but the skeles at the bottom enjoy having at a weakened player. Tip for this is to stay away from rugs. Some rugs are fine, but as a basic tip unless the rug is mostely impossible to avoid or it is huge, it is a trap. Stay away from rugs and you will avoid 99% of the traps.

If you want to head straight to the south wing you can levitate over to the enterance since it is a false floor before the door. Since there is actually no floor there the door is unlocked. Take note though that there are large wall sized picture frames that are one way walls. Once you are past them there is little way out other than falling down a hole and running to the port up in the pit or gating.

As for camp spots I haven't done HS solely as a caster so I have never camped out. There are a decent number of wandering mobs, but I am sure there are some corners where at most if you log in you will be hit on by only 1 mob.

HS is definately a great place to explore and I enjoyed leveling here before the 65 cap. Good luck to you.
