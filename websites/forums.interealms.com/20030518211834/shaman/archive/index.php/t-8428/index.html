Gloriana
No, they don't stack, the best focus effect takes precedence.

For example, I have a mana preservation III item as well as an affliction efficiency III item. When casting an affliction type spell (slow, root, malo etc) the affliction efficiency item triggers.

Now, you can stack focus items with AA skills. For example, my QB1 AA skill stacks with my spell haste III item for a faster casting torpor.

Heimdall Azureguardian
If the focus effects are the same effect: Efficiency/Haste etc, they don't stack. But Efficiency stacks with extension stacks with haste etc. You can save mana, and cast faster and have it last longer, but you can't cast it for less, and less, and less.

Having ODS, and Coldain Wrist and AoI is not redundant. ODS makes the spell cheaper. Coldain makes your Dots/Debuffs last longer. AoI makes your buffs last longer. Circlet makes your dots and debuffs cast faster.
