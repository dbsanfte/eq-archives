Widda
I have spent a good amout in Paludal myself (15 - 25) and I absolutely LOVED the zone. At 25 I moved to echo caverns. Echo caverns have two entrances, one through a secret door near the soulbinder (in a room behind him) and the other across the pond near the dwarf, gnome and halfling areas. The camp I am going to tell you about is in the area behind the secret door.

Once you enter, turn left, and follow the main path until you come to a massive sliding door. There are some mobs in the hall behind the door, but if you stay in the middle you should be able to cross without aggroing them. keep going straight until there is a chance to turn right. Btw, the big tentacles that grow on the wall will randomly root you and suck some live out from you (about 1 bub), but you usually can just run by them. once you turn right, go straight, following the right wall. This should get you into a large room filled with Needelites (big mosquitos). They are blue well into 29, and are really amazing xp. Loot is decent, and there is a named spawn. If you got really good gear, you can clear the room easily, and do one or two of the alien looking guys that are nearby. Be aware that the Needelites hit INCREDIBLE fast, so pull with slow, and then dot. Luckily, they do not hit very hard ( wis shaman do not need to apply at this camp). With decent ac, you should see hits going like 2,2,2,5, 13, 4, etc. As an added benefit, you can be sure that your defense will be maxed within the first yellow in the level (sometimes within the first blue)

Have fun,
