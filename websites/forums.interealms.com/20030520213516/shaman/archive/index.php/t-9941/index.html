Kazim
Be careful where you set up camp. The sun revs will run away when low on life. If you camp somewhere that the running sun rev will path quickly toward friends, you may find yourself fighting several adds. I usually rooted them and let the pet finish them off, rerooting as needed when they ran. A big nuke may work also, but jbb blasts aren't big enough.
