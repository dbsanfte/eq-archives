Step 1: Cast your regen spell
Step 2: Cast SoW
Step 3: Click staff - it will overwrite SoW instead of your regen spell.

Of course, this means the stacking it will only work outdoors. You can stack them, then run indoors. :)

I believe the order of buffs matter.

In order for fungal regrowth to properly overwrite sow, and not try to overwrite (and not take hold) over regen, SoW must be before regen in your bufflist.

So,

1. Cast SoW.
2. Cast Regen spell
3. Click fungi staff.

fungal regrowth will overwrite sow.
