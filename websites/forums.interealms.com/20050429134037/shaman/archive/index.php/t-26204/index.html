Tronc
There's nothing wrong with the exp in LoY... try torgiran mines if you find the boats slow - don't know if the exp bonus still applies though?? The idea with the LoY zones is that you can make about 500p per hour (likely better) all the while getting decent exp.

It's not the planes....nothing is. But it is a very safe spot to solo in as most everything is a single pull. More importantly if you haven't done the Stoicism quest then it is well worth it and very easy. See the quest section for details on that.

At 52 you could do the caves in Dulak's Harbour as well - the Enraged Soulstealer might be a tough (close) battle but if you're prepared I suspect you could do it. Keep resists up for his dots though and make sure the wanderer is killed before he's due to pop.
Keep in mind he's got a larger agro than the others that spawn there.

Just so you know - I spent 8 months at lvl 55 (now lvl 56!) so for the most part for the soloing shaman I would give LoY zones as the best zones to be in.

Nurga and such can get into multiple pulls, as can Sebilis and most other places of that nature. Doable but most certainly a much higher risk especially CR in Seb ;)

With your gear I'd give it another few levels before soloing in PoI too - feel free to try it if you wish - can't hurt. But you will need a virtue and I suggest one for dogdog too.

Another spot you could try but is a pain to get to would be Acrylia caverns. You'd do well in there but again multiples may be an issue and mobs do run @ 20% health. Plenty of wanderers in there too. If you die there - you're eating the exp loss too unless you have a pet cleric you can call upon.

Good Luck!
