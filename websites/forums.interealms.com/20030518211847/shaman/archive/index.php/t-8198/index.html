howler
Well, since I am forced to level here, figured I might as well toss some information out there for you all.

Non-factory groups:
Best group of any here is the all pet group. Mages, beastlords, shamans. Slow is necessary in this zone, as well as a high sustainable dps. Hence, the all pet group.

Archaic models - immune to slow, snare, root, etc. They can be stunned.

Best route is to head left from the zone in, not right. Mobs on the left do not add when pulled, ones on right do add when pulled. Harmony will work on certain models, but not others, so that is a valid option most of the time. The room that was mentioned above on the left side is very good experience, but the best experience grouping is in the factory.

Factory groups:
Must have a slower! Mobs hit hard and furious. Have a class that can single pull these, whether that be a monk or an adept harmonizer. After slow cheal and a competent tank, it's all about dps. Concentrate your group in the left hand corner, furthest from the entrance. Have your puller pull to that empty hallways. Either stay away from the doors once you're inside, or clear the tins. Tin adds destroy more groups than anything else. More than two models pulled in the factory will destroy your group most everytime.

To get flagged for the factory, spawn the gnome to do his thing and take down xanamech - info can be found elsewhere.

For chanters, this is a fantastic tier one zone. Charm kiting at the entrance with these hard hitting mobs is just absolutely astounding - dangerous too.

Zone is easily trainable...that's an understatement.
