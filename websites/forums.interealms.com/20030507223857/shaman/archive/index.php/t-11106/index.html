Only 2 horses are worth considering at all :

1)The cheap gluepot. Purchased soley for the standing mana-regen and no-sit-agro aspects. Click it off and use SoW for actual travel.

2)The 100K+ horse. Purchased by the rich to gain faster travel in addition to the benefits of the gluepot.

The other 2 models don't really provide any substantial benefits over the gluepot. Especially for a class that can cast SoW.
