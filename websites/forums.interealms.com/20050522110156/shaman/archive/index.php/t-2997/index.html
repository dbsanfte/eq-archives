Megumi
I pulled this from another thread that was going off-topic, because I thought it was interesting. People without it think it's overrated, go figure. People with it like it. I have it and I like it a lot. It's especially useful for recovering from a wipeout.

Some random tips, which may or may not be common knowledge.
<ul><li>It takes up to double the mana for the spell. The most efficient time to cast it is when you have just over the amount normally needed without MGB. This is what I mean about recovering from a wipeout. You only have to canni back one cast of Khura's Focus and you can buff the entire raid.
<li>The reuse timer starts counting when you activate the ability, not when you cast the spell. So, if you know that you will not be casting a group buff for a while you can activate it and just wait. Then, when you use MGB your reuse time will be less than the normal 72 minutes.
<li>It makes clerics cry, because of Focus + BAotR stacking issues. They get over it. :P
</ul>
