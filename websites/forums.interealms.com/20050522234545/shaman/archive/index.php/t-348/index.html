I've got to agree with ShadoWynd - Marus Seru is the place. I moved there last night and gained nearly 2 and half gold bubbles of XP at level 25 in about three hours. The mobs are plentiful and most of them were conning yellow to red at 25 - so you should find plenty to hunt at 30 (most of the people there were high twenties, low thirties). There were anywhere from 5 to 10 people in the zone so if you find a mob, it is most likely yours.

Be careful, the mobs will gang-up but only similiar types (at least in my experience). The Zelniaks are highly agresssive with a huge aggro radius, but everything else is pretty tame. I was easily soloing with minimal downtime. If I got an add, the zone to Netherbian Lair is close by. The guards will help with the Zelniaks, but not with the rockhoppers.
