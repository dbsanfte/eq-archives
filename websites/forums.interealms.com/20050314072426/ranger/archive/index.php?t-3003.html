You are going to get as many answers to this question as there are posters. Don't worry about it too much. Most people agree that stats aren't all that important in the end.

Your optimal stat boosts depend on your choice of race and your personal play style. I rarely ever cast much, and I always have KEI on anyway so for me WIS is a total waste. Others like WIS. STR has some influence on melee damage. DEX will help you with archery. Finally STA will help you with those precious hit points.

The important thing to remember is that EQ is all about boosting your stats through equipment. By the time you hit level 50 your stats will come from your equipment not those first 20 points you spent at creation. So these first 20 points should be spent for the time you play before you get decent equipment. Basically from level 1-30.

From level 1-55 archery will add up to about 1% of your total damage output. I cannot justify putting points into DEX for that.

If you solo a lot, you'll need to cast a bunch. That's buffs, heals, dots, roots, fears, etc. For this WIS is your first choice.

If you group a lot, you will be called on to be a support melee or even a main tank especially early in your career. As a melee, you want your points mostly in STR to max damage. For tanking, you want STA for those hit points. Nobody is expecting you to be the healer of the group so WIS should not be your first choice.

I chose wood-elf and put all my points into STR. If I had it to do over, I'd have been a halfling but still put points into STR.

Casters Realm says to put all your points in STA. Like I said, there are many answers to this question. Really don't sweat it cause 1) no matter what you'll get it wrong and 2) it doesn't matter anyway.
