Sucks. Shadowbane is Ultima Online first expansion pack about 5 years late. Was in beta, played on 3 servers, on each got chars to R3 - R4.

Game is buggy as hell, not even good enough for the beta, and they are already releasing it. Lag is crazy, even with the really powerful machines you feel like playing current EQ with Voodoo1 video card. Basically:

PROS:
* Good selection of classes and disciplines, really plenty to choose from;
* Group formations - making travel as a group really easy and fun;
* Levelling up - pretty fun and not hard at all;
* Ability to build player cities, from small to huge - very neat;

CONS:
* LAG, horrendous lag!
* BUGS, horrendous amount of bugs!
* Disciplines - most of them are not working at all;
* Balance - there is no balance. Wizard kills everyone, melee get killed by everyone;
* Content - there is no content in the game whatsoever;
* No quests, no dungeons, just random patches of mobs to level up;
* Solo ability is "done" to every other class really - other then pet class, i.e. wizard - at lvl 20 already;
* Graphics suck too. Would have been cool for a game like 4-5 years ago, now it sucks;
* Point and click way of moving - some on, good for Diablo, but NOT MMORPG;
* Only 3rd person view sucks;

Frankly the beta was a nice change for EQ and I played it. Too bad I was gimped from the start, since I liked to play melee classes. But heck, it was FREE, so I played it. But definately I am not going to pay even a dime for such outdated, without content, laggy, buggy, barely quarter working product. I didn't mind the PvP, in a way it's pretty useful - morons just get thwacked down there and no fussing - but heck, it was completely unbalanced. If you chose a melee class, i.e. tank type, then you already made a choice to be a free loot bag for others. Classes who can hide could do to you big damage even before they become fully visible. Wizards have pets, which even when being lower level then you, outperform you in any way. You have no ranged means as a tank class, and your styles are useless, because everyone sees when you execute it, and then everyone has enough time to take 1-2 steps away from you and your style will fail, and takes too long time to refresh to be of any use again in PvP. When you die, ALL of your gear will take damage from it, and damage is at times so big that casters can go naked in a blink of an eye, and melee people have their gear half damaged, amounting in repair costs for well over 10-20K (and higher) per death. And this is with the usual gear, imagine when you get high end magic gear - you will pay the blood out of your nose to fix it and still wont cover it. Imagine lagging out and dieing, then getting ganked by someone 2-3 ranks (i.e. 20-30 levels) above you, and then dieing again to stray mobs or a train - and your melee person is also without a gear, because it was destroyed. Casters are competely naked already after the second death.

Imagine the situation, where a higher level wizard, about 10 levels higher then you, comes to where your group is hunting (newbie lands still) and starts to KS you. You can't do really much, because the wizard pet outperforms your whole group. Also, the XP in SB goes to the person, who delivers the killing blow, not who does most damage. Also everyone can loot the corpse as soon as it dies. Basically, in this example, the wizard keeps following your group, taking all your kills, looting your kills, laughing about it and you cannot do anything - you petition, and in best case the answer you get from CS is: Dear customer, please remember the name of the offending person and when on mainland (off of newbie island, where is free PvP), kill him". Yep, that's it, that's the whole customer support. And natually you cannot kill the wizard, unless you're another wizard and equal level or higher, or this one is really stupid in PvP (i.e. stands in front of you and let's you just kill him). Heck, there's even NO point to PvP, because all it gives to you is killed target and the chance that he had some loot you can take and sell (if you make it back). In DAoC at least there were special RR points given for PvP/RvR, with what you could enhance your char (AA points in EQ), nothing like this in SB.
