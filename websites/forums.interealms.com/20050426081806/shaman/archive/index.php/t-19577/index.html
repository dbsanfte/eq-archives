Hail,enjoy playing in tactics when i've got limited down time.
/agree. 45 minutes is plenty of time to zone-in, do a good check of the pit to look for any enraged or frenzied mobs & take any you see out. Sometimes successfully snagging & killing one or two of these will take 20-30 minutes if pit is in full pop. I do this to farm spectrals w/ the frenzied (many of my guildmates are poor & can't solo Tactics on their own) and hope for ornate. Once my sweep of the pit is done, if the room outside stables is open I kill the 2 statics there and hope for a stampede to nab piglet.

Casually checking the pit out in this manner an hour before a raid has netted me many spectrals, a piece of ornate or two, and several piglet drops over time.

If Tactics is populated, another quick option is to farm Velium weapons for dogdog in the Great Divide. Run through the Giant fort and slaughter anything nearby wielding a weapon for 20-30 minutes. Do this once a week or so keeps dogdog in Velium toys. Not as good as mage toys, but better than rusties. ;)

If there are others in your guild w/ 45 minutes to kill, that's enough time to QUICKLY run in and grab a normal adventure if everyone moves fast. Pure guild group that can mow through a normal in 30-35 minutes & can take a loss on the chin if an officer is demanding your presence NOW! in the case of a protracted adventure might cause you to be a few minutes late.

Other ideas: Park your raiding character at Larren and then spend a half hour organizing your bank w/ your mules. I almost always could at any time spend an hour (at least) virtually any day doing this, but I rarely do. Eventually the disorganization backlogs & I have to kill 2-3 hours to straighten stuff out again somewhat. :p A half hour here or there helps keep this at bay.

Duel someone else with time to kill, and parse some pet damage w/ different buffs/weapons. Parse other classes' pets...

Tradeskills can also be done quickly in a half hour if your inventory isn't too cluttered & you already have decent wisdom so you aren't changing out gear in order to click away your platinum. Restocking alchemy potions (DS/IVU/gate/etc) is another productive way to spend a half hour, and something I'm usually forgetful about doing.

Take care,
