Q will go from 24 secs to 31.2 seconds, which may or may not go a full 6 ticks; it depends when the buff landed and when the tick happened, from what I know. So, for example, if the tick starts at time index 0, and you land Q at time index 5, and the first tick happens at time index 6, then Q will drop at time index 36.2, which means you will juuust manage to get 6 ticks (times 6, 12, 18, 24, 30, 36), but if you landed it at time index 4.5 instead, then you will have it drop at 35.7, and you will only get 5 ticks. Long story short, get scrm.

OK Here's the deal with SCR and SCRM and extending Torpor/Quiescence.
SCR will extend by 30%, which will put Q at 1500 hps every time, and depending on lag, sometimes 1800. SCRM will add 20%, making Q last for 1800 hps healed. From there, you'll need focus items to increase Q duration. Currently there are only two items in game that will do this. Not sure what earring name is (elementals) but the hat is the one i have on my shaman, and it does two things: extends all spells with a duration for 15% (which bumps Q to 2100 hps healed) and then adds one tick to the duration of any spell, which makes Q last for 2400 hps.
