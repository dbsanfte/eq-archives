Peatnallie
3 crystalline silks (can be found off off spiders in velkator's labyrinth, or crystal caverns off the spiders), 2 glass shards (bought in thurgadin) and 1 small BRICK of velium (found in crystal caverns or velkator's also) Combine all of these ingredients in a pottery wheel (122~ skill in pottery is trivial i believe)

Then take the product and combine it with a HIGH quality firing sheet, purchased off a merchant (one farthest from banker in the bank area sells em).

Head to Frundle Frekler (sp),a female dwarf, straight across from Paladin Guild in Thurgadin. Text message is "what velium vapors" and you will receive an item (forgot what's it called. Combine the vial with 2 small PIECES of velium and you will have the gate potion as your finished product. These are lore, but the vial fired with the high quality sheets are not, so you can bank a few of them.

EDIT: Firing of the vial is failable, thanks to Elladan for the information.
