Targar
Well there are a few things you need to understand. First Solo'ing in new world places RoK, Velious, Luclin is much different from solo'ing in the old world. Mobs hit harder, have more HP etc etc. At your currently level you would have a much easier time solo'ing back in the old world than you would in the newer lands.

Next you are in the shaman "hell stages". The stages of a shaman life when they don't get any heal upgrades, don't have a pet yet, don't get any new DD's or real dot upgrades. This is usually from around L20-33 or so.

When you get to L34 and get VoS your first puppy etc things really start to turn around solo-wise. At 34 when you get your first puppy your effectiveness almost doubles.

However you do have it much better now that they have removed the DOT penatly for moving targets. Before the Root nuke/dot thing had to be used for ohh the last year and a half or so. With that now gone you can go back to what us very old time shaman did which was dot/kite. This is even more effective combined when you get your puppy at L34. You can turn off pet taunts and your dots will keep the mob agroed you while your pup chews on his ankles from behind.

Cheer up you don't have that much more to go, but the 20's are really hell for shaman. After that its gravy.

I know LoIO is the pits but you can do to 30 there. Zoning in from Frontier go left and sit on those hills there and pull from the road and near-bye, lots of stuff roams there. When you have what you need to do down and feel comfortable move ahead from there between the crack in the hills across the road and go lakeside.
