After an unbeleivable night in CoM (more **** ups than you can imagine) I have my epic :)

Did every step and each one only once. Was bricking it as well as some of our methods were rather un-orthadox, and just before handing my tear to Lord Rak I was REALLY wishing I had handed in an extra set of Ghiosk books or something just to make sure, but there wasnt a problem thankfully (well - not with my faction anyways).

Needed 3 rez's afterwards, although the xp from the final hand in is very sweet.

The raid kind of went as follows:

All arrive in CoM, decided to try and pull Neh rather than have to fight up to the top (in hindsight fighting to his spawn point may have been quicker). 1st attempt, ranger pulls, I hail, nothing happens except ranger dies and someone didnt see the DONT ATTACK message and died as well. 2nd attempt - didnt even see where ranger had pulled to, ranger dies, Neh returns to spawn point. 3rd attempt, monk pulls, FD's, Neh warps away. 4th attempt, monk pulls, I had in ring as he FD's (really ****ting myself as I was worried about Neh warping back and me losing the ring), Neh aggros, we kill Neh, that part of epic sorted.

We then kill reavers till Lord Rak spawns. Someone aggros Lord Rak. Lord Rak goes on killing spree. Lots of dead people about. After our cleric manages to get back to CoM, rez everyone, we all buff and get ready for proper attempt. Have 3 mages so shouldnt be a problem. I hand in tear, Rak aggros, pets attack. All going well until we get an add, enchanter charms add and sends him in against add. For some reason Rak aggros and and goes legging it off across the bridge and then leaps into the water (bastard). From there it all goes a bit pear shaped. Finally get Rak out of the water (2 mages and numerous fire pets later). He gets to about 20% health and then gates back to spawn point, summons me, kicks my unbuffed ass (death number 3). Monk mate gets fed up at this point, runs up to Rak, hits attack and starts laying some serious smack down on his ass. Couple of heals and about 30-45 seconds later Rak is lying in a dead heap on the ground :D

Was certainly one of the more interesting Rak fights. Have done that part of the quest a couple of times now and has always gone smoothly, just typical it was for mine that it all went a bit wrong. Got my epic tho so I am happy :D

Just want to say thanks to my guild for being awesome and coming to CoM for the fight. Had a huge turnout for it which was absolutely great (even had to turn a few people away as we just had too many for me to try and cope with).

It is days like today that remind me of why I play this game, seeing loads of people all coming to help a friend in need. Was an awesome night :D
