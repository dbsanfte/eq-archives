One way you can pull is to use the old SK pet pull trick.

Grab a pet that will con green to the MOBS. Toss on a Shrew & a Q and /pet attack then spam a macro that does /pet back off and /pet follow as soon as he taps the mob.

Green con to the mob means that only the mob he hits will aggro. Using a combination of /pet back off and /pet follow will get your pet out of the way more quickly. /pet back off has the pet walk to you . . . /pet follow and the pet runs to you.

There are reports that some changes were made to pet pulling . . . you might want to test this in a more forgiving environment before taking it to LDoN.

To quick add to this- ideally you want to buff your pet to the gills w/ as many hp buffs as the party has. Tough splits you want to rune the pet as well. In LDoN hards w/ a tough room to break w/ hasted mobs, the key if you're pulling as an sk is to pull while feigned if you're afraid the pet will die. If the pet dies on the pull, all aggro will immediately chain to all mobs in range and transfer to the pet's owner- learned this the hard way after wiping the group several times.

Pull while feigned on the tough splits and just have the cleric nail the pet with supernal remedy on the way out and supernal light on inc to save on the bone chips. This is the poor man's way of successfully breaking rooms in LDoN hards if you can't handle 1+.
