afreeman
Just a quick question. I know shaman can do well solo, but I doubt they are the fastest exp or cash earners. Though they can take down big targets here and there...

Neither one is true anymore.

Shamans were the kings of solo'ing in the velious era, and we still hold our own, but with the release of PoP, charm classes became much better at solo'ing the big mobs. For sheer kills per hour, and thus cash per hour, necros outrank us, and probably druids and chanters do as well, if there is a charmable mob nearby.

To give you an idea, 2 druids in my guild took down a mini in PoStorms recently with a couple of charmed frogs. These hit for about 1000, can't be slowed, rampage, flurry, and enrage. I don't speak from the perspective of a time-geared player, but I doubt any 2 shammies in similar gear (mostly ornate, maybe FT8) anywhere could do the same. Similarly, CoD chanters can take down most any named in a Teir 2 PoP zone (and probably any Teir 3 zone as well by adding a cleric). Meanwhile, most shammies would still consider low-end mobs like Lodizal or WW dragons to be a substantive kill.

To give you another idea, consider that a necro in my guild can kite-kill the diakus in PoTactics at about twice the rate per hour that I can. He has more AAs, but we're both 65, both have all spells, and I have better gear.

Yes, we can solo well, but we're long past the days of being the best at it.
