If your guild has a chanter between 57 and 61 or so, ask him/her if they want to duo with you in PoN. You assist them with thier charm kiting by shooting (you do have a Tolan's Bracer, I hope; not feasable otherwise) and by ensnaring both thier pet (before charm) and the pulls. GREAT xp.

Despite what people will tell you (or me) I was doing this last night with 90 percent of my xp going to AA. In an hour and a half of that, I'd gotten 2 yellow and 4 blue AAxp bubbles. Honestly, I've not seen that kinda xp in a full group in PoP yet (though being only 55 myself makes it a tad hard to get into the good camps, unless friends are there). We broke up a couple trains last night, as well. I was partnered with a 61 chanter; he told me that 57 is when he started charm kiting PoN because at 55 his spells wouldn't last long enough to make it feasable. He also told me that rangers and druids are the best partners for such an endeavor.
