Zarniwoop
For pet buffs, stick to haste and possibly sow and/or regen depending on what you're doing. Some people cast focus on their pets for the added hit points, but my pet never tanked for me that often, so I never bothered. Str, sta, agi etc., won't do anything for your pet.

Our higher level pets can dual wield (only the level 55 and 61 pets, I believe), but they need to be given two weapons to activate this ability. The type of weapon doesn't matter (a rusty dagger will do the same as a fine steel longsword).

At 34, the only advantage to giving your pet a weapon would be if the weapon had a proc. A velium weapon, for example, would add the proc damage when it went off. A gnoll hide lariat (procs stun) is another popular choice. But, at 34, you'll probably see more benefit from selling the weapons you come across and saving the cash for equipment upgrades.

As a longtime ranger, I've always paid close attention to dual wield. Well, here's my understanding:

1) Every NPC in the game of the appropriate level will dual wield when given appropriate weapons. Class doesn't matter. Obviously, if they are holding something they like more, the weapon will just go into their inventory.

2) At one point, certain class pets after a certain level were given innate dual-wield and didn't need to equip their pets with weapons. This seems to be mage/necro/enchanter/shadowknight pets only. Haven't checked druid pets.

3) Our pets are 'standard mobs,' in that they cannot quad without weapons. Relatively few mobs in the game are flagged as "quadders" and are able to dual-wield without weapons. The other pets were flagged as "quadders" but not ours, obviously because we are not a "pet class." Enchanter pets always start with a shield in their secondary, so we'll never know if they could dual wield without a second weapon.


Possibility: Our pets are animals, and as such their innate attack is "bite" and not "hit" like other pets. It is possible that "animals" can never be "quadders," at least I have never seen a biter/clawer also quad.
