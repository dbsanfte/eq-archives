The upside of LDoN equipment it that it allows many people to buy high quality equipment with adventure points that they would never otherwise own. The down side is that it’s all no drop. So yes, you should be very careful about what equipment you buy. That’s why I feel LDoN is not as good an expansion for lower level characters as it is for 60 plus characters. If you’re a lower level character saving points for mid level LDoN armor, those points are almost wasted because all you can do is trash that armor if you get better. And if you later save points for the high-end armor for your class you have to start saving points from scratch. Still, the adventures are fun and you get good experience, decent cash and often upgrade equipment that drops from named mobs. So it’s not such a bad deal.

Also keep in mind that if you add an augmentation to a tradable piece of equipment that items becomes no drop. You can buy something to remove the augmentation, but the augmentation will be destroyed in the process. So if you win a roll on a nice augmentation that drops during an adventure, such as +6 to stamina, be sure to place it in an item you plan to keep for a long time.
