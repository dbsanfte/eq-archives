PoS quest IS required for elemental access. I can confirm that, since we had to go back and do it for a guildie with all other flags.

Since it is required for elemental access (otherwise granted by killing RZ as the final element), it stands to reason that it's required for sol ro, too.

All you need is a giant head, beard, sash, bone, and 2 amulets off the named (1 amulet off of ocean, desert, or forest giant mini-named--can't be 2 from ocean mini-nameds, etc. Has to be 2 different venues). If you've gotten all those flags, you can probably get 1.5-2 groups needed for the PoS mini-named easily enough.
