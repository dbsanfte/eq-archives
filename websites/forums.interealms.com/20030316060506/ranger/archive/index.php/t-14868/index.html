Zolina
I heard someone quote 6% damage increase from Ferocity 3. Unlike Ambidexterity, Ferocity affects both 2H and 1H weapon damage, so it's more versatile. Further, Ambidexterity has diminishing returns if your mainhand weapon is far better than your offhand weapon (i.e. for Swiftwind and Acrylia Handled Broadsword it may only give a 2% damage increase), while Ferocity will always give you the same damage increase. Lastly, you'll be increasing your damage as your progress toward Ferocity 3, while Ambidexterity won't take effect until you have 9 points saved up. I'd recommend at least getting Ferocity 1 before starting Ambi.
