Sengal
As far as armor progression (assuming you have no or little starting cash) I would suggest:

Patchwork/Leather while you get started.

Banded (sells for about 1 platinum piece per point of AC) Banded is respectable up to the 20's (or higher) for a non-twink.

Ry'Gorr or Totemic around the mid twenties. Totemic can be quested, ry'gorr is very cheap to buy and equally good.

Jaundiced Bone around mid-30's to 40's. Drops in various Kunark dungeons. Certain pieces are ultra-rare and difficult to get (bracer, chestplate, and greaves) the rest are fairly common.

Late 40's to 50's you can start to think about Velious quest armor (Thurgadin is probably the easiest to accquire).

Of course if you have the cash, upgrade to the next teir of armor as soon as you are able. HP's and AC are very good for a shaman.

Note: there are hundreds of comparable armor pieces at each level that I listed above. This is just one possible upgrade path. You mileage will vary :)
