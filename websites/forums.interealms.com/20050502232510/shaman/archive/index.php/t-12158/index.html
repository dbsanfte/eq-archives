Faction in Sanctus Seru is very complex. There are 4 separate houses (Eye, Shoulder, Heart, and Hand) each of which has its own faction. There are also factions for the Arx (where Lord Inquisitor Seru resides), Seru himself, and Legionnaires. Killing a mob in Seru can increase some of these factions and decrease others at the same time. Faction is also increased for certain Katta factions. Supposedly Katta and Sanctus are on opposite factions, but it's pretty convoluted.

About the only way to increase factions across the board in Sanctus is like you said, hunt named mobs in Grieg's End (especially Grieg himself).

Actually, you should ask yourself why you want good faction in Sanctus in the first place. There's nothing there on merchants that you can't get elsewhere (with the exception of one quest item I can think of). There aren't many quests there other than the earring quest (multi-step quest like Coldain ring and shawl, but no trade skills involved). Doing the earring quest requires being a member of a large raiding guild. So, basically, if all you want to do is shop and bank there, go somewhere else. Shadow Haven isn't that far away.
