I am currently at the Jaggedpine camp, but I didn't like the pit camp as well as I like the other place that I'm fighting the gnolls at. An easy way to find this place is to fall into the pit that is mentioned before and then run around the only direction possible and take the first right that you can. This will eventually lead you into the big open area where I think that the head gnoll is in (i haven't seen him yet so I am not sure, but I'm assuming he's around there somewhere). Anyways, if you turn left inside this cave and set up camp in between the first and second gnolls, they are not close enough to agro on you and also there are no roamers that I have seen in there. I have only found this place camped once by a 58 druid (who I might add was very rude in letting me know that it was camped and that there was not enough room for the both of us to fight there, even with the 30 mobs or so that are in close proximity). From that point you can pull mobs from all over the room and not worry about any adds except for that first gnoll. I usually end up rooting every mob just before they are ready to flee and leave pet on him and just med until the mob is dead. Very easy exp here for you at 57 and also mobs are all dark blue if Warriors, but light blue for the casters

Last, when I pull any casters, i just start with Bane, and then sick the dog on them. I keep MR buffed and let the pet attack and keep them rooted away from me. If you are facing the Curates, then at just about 2bubbles of life left, I use Ice Strike and they do not end up with enough time to CH.

Oh, yeah, i have only had one problem with this method, but what I do is invis before the cave, and just charge in, the casters have a chance to see you and start casting, but you are usually far enough away from them by the time that it lands that they don't have time to recast anything on you, andyou can quickly reinvis yourself.
