Barelini
Finally, to be useful, higher level players would have to read and respond to posts from lower level players,

While, in general, this is probably true, for the specific purposes of the request in this topic having higher level (end-game oriented) players answer the questions almost defeats the purpose.

In browsing many boards over the years, it is amazing how many times a level 65 player will answer a question about level 35ish play and be totally off-base. This is due to 1) they haven't played in that level for a long time and have no clue how any changes have affected gameplay; 2) even if they have an alt it is usually being PL'd or twinked to the point that their advice is close to worthless to an untwinked character; 3) they assume that "the majority of players are 65" and the player asking the question is therefore an idiot for not having a high level character and knowing the answer.

That last one really bugs me personally. I've played EQ since August 1999 and my highest level character is a level 55 warrior. I have several characters in the 50s but none playing in the end-game. I also have a slew of characters in the 30-40s, including my shaman (lvl 42). I know a LOT about the game from lvl 1-55 and almost nothing of the end-game. If you are in an end-game type guild, it is natural to assume that "the majority" are also there, since all your friends and guildmates are there too. It's possible that it is a truism but I honestly do not believe so nor have I seen any statistics to validate this statement. My belief is that "most" players do not play in the end-game and do not read message boards. Again, no statistics to prove it, just a feeling based on my own personal experience in my guild and the alliance it belongs to.

I do agree that an additional forum probably wouldn't get used too much and that most questions are answered in the existing forums, more than adequately. Sometimes a lowbie just has to wade through a lot of useless drivel from high-level players to get to the answers that are actually useful at their level though.

All of that said, there are some truly knowledgeable players at the highest level who give really good advice and actually target the information to the question asked. These folks are priceless to a class board and are generally recognized and appreciated, especially by the folks asking the questions.
