I'd continue PLing the necro until at least 55 when you can take him to tier 2 in PoP and then start grouping up. Xp will be bad for both otherwise because you're fighting stuff that's bad xp for a L65 and won't give much for a L45 because the L65 takes so much of the xp. As long as the necro does 51%+ of the damage he will get full xp, so by using your pets, nukes, dots, etc... you can rapidly speed up the kill rate of the necro.

I basically did exactly this when PLing my sk up to the point that he'd be useful tanking for my shaman in places where they'd both get good xp.
