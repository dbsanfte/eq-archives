It depends on your role in a raid. if all you do is MGB and debuffs, mana isnt your big concern..

If your job is debuffs, MGB, keeping 5 rodding cler/mages constantly torpored, doing 40k in torrent blasts and rebuffing all fallen dead in a 30+ minute fight you will need as much mana as possible. I do all that junk in a single fight and even with 4400 mana i get low on mana.

Yep buy a heiro cloak, that lodi shield is just trash really, sell it before the bottom drops out on its value. Buy a dragonwing mantle or start working on your 8th coldain shawl quest. Buy a BCG if you see one even for sale. You got a few other weaker spots but those are basically raid upgrades.

Just make a list of your weaknesses and when an item drops that is a considerable upgrade over that weak spot go for it. When all your weakest spots are covered make a list of your second most weakest sposts and do the same.
