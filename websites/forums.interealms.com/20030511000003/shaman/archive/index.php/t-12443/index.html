Dear Csin....


Here's what I do for EXE Trials.


As was said early... Shamans are slow, steady DPS, given enough time.. we can kill almost anything. The problem with an EXE Trial is that the key to that is fast, burst-type DPS.. that means, Melees, or Wizards, and the all-time King of Fast DPS, Mages.

Build yourself a Group like this. Tank(Warrior,Pally,SK), Cleric, Rogue/Monkx2,Wizzy/Mage. Then, there's you. The best way a Shaman can cotribute to an EXE Trial I have found is this.....

Each Wave is 4 Mobs, and as your party chews through each mob, the Executioner Resets, and generally, by the 4th Mob, you party is going full guns to kill the last mob in time. Here's where you Turn the Shaman's weakness into an Advantage.

1. Load your Slow, and all the Posion DOT's you have.

2. Make your group aware that when the 4 mobs spawn, they will Assist the tank and engage them in the order the tank chooses... EXCEPT.. the 4th Mob, is yours. You will split from the group and Engage a 4th Mob... the LAST mob. You slow it, and DOT the living heck out of it and Torpor Tank it while the Party kills the other 3. By the time they get to you, the will be falling behind on keeping the Executioner at bay... when the Warrior assists you, he will be happy to find the last mob will be sitting at about 50% HP and be a very fast final mob in the group.

3. Do this through all 4 Waves and you will find it works quite well. When the Executioner pops.. he mitigates the slows so much, it's almost not worth doing, just Assist your Cleric and Spam heal the tank as the rest of your group chews him to pieces.
