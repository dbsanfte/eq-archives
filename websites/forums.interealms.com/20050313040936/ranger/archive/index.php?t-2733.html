Tried the new arrows tonight after I made my Fier'Dal fletching kit.

Just to rehash, here are the components:


in the Fier'Dal forge
---------------------------
arrow head: small brick of mithril, file, flask of water
arrow shaft: large brick of mithril, arrow shaft mold, file, water

In the Fier'Dal fletching kit
------------------------------------
fletchings: small brick of mithril, mithril working knife

Combining the three mithril components with a large or medium nock was trivial at 202 fletching. Combining with a small nock was *not* trivial at 202, and resulted in one of the mithril knives used to make the fletchings. I was 0 for 22 attempts on it, burned a few hundred plat. It looks to be bugged. On success it apparently makes an equippable by none/none throwing item. Anyway, Maena at eqtraders said <a href="http://pub126.ezboard.com/feqtraderscornerfletching.showMessageRange?topicID=351.topic&start=41&stop=55" target="new">in a thread there</a> that she was getting 33% success at a skill of 228. Considering every component used is bought off a vendor, once/if they get the kinks worked out, I'll be ecstatic.
