LaughingWoman
The ToFS keys are ring-able. A lot of the tower is undead, which makes it ideal for pallies, not so great for a solo shaman. That being said, I LOVE this place. Corpse runs are absolute hell (especially once you get past the 4th floor, which is all mixed live and dead) - but completely worth it in my opinion.

The wedding is the 5th floor, used to be a pretty busy place for the velium that drops off the golems pretty regularly.

Also, each floor has an exit (great for emergencies) that plops you back in iceclad - in the middle of a couple whirling dervish paths. Of course, there's absolutely nothing better than clicky sow boots in that case.

I'll say that it's extremely easy to get keys if you bring a group and just clear the floor until the keyholder pops. It's possible to get solo, but you have to be pretty patient for some floors...

LaughingWoman
Yep. One of those insanely handy patches... The command to check your keyring is /key.

It's not actually in your inventory. When you use a ring that's capable of fitting on your ring, it'll display on this /key list. E.g. if you zone into ToFS, get your first floor key, run to the mirror, click your key on the mirror, and say /key - it'll say ToFS floor 1. Now you can destroy that key.

This works for most every key - with a few notable exceptions, none of which I can list because I don't have them. *grin*
