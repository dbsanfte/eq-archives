these are soloable, as I know a druid that does them. Snare doesn't work on them, but he uses dots that cast in 5 seconds or less and kills them in about 45 minutes. At level 60 he was getting a blue+ in AAxp per kill.

We have several high damage dots that cast in 5 seconds, but one thing he said was, if you get hit you might as well bend over and die.

The one time I tried, I got one down past half and had to zone due to wolf adds, so make sure that you are watching for the wolves if you try.
