farmatyr
Don't remember where I read this, but it seems like a wonderful strategy. First, get him on tracking before you start the duel. Don't touch the pet at first. Instead, snare the magician. This will agro the pet, who will run after you. There is a fairly short range on how far away the magician can issue commands to the pet, pull the pet way far away from him, cancel magic if you have it (if not, use a 2 hander for the DS) and kill the pet. It takes 6 or 8 seconds to resummon a pet at level 16, so he doesn't have much chance to get a new one up before you interrupt him. Don't dot, nuke between swings, and beat him down while he tries to channel through with a couple nukes.
