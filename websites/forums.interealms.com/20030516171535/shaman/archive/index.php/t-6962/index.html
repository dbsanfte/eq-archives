Duurg
PoD... well I've done a lot of group hunting there at LC (good exp IMO). Sadly, if you are there for parchments, they are pretty rare. I've seen more drop off the crawlers once LC beetles were completely pulled. Come to think of it, I've only seen 1 parchment drop off a beetle in my days of camping there.

PoN docks is an exellent camp. Kinda tricky to get to, but once there, no one around, and root/rot works great. Nettling Wraiths are warrior based mobs, and drop parchments more often than most mobs in PoN. Easiest way to get there, lev up, run to close to stalkers, invis up, and jump over the wall before stalkers (watch out, they see invis, so don't get too close, so do spiders and trees, so be careful). Once over ledge, run over water until you see the dock. Don't go in water or you'll aggro some nasty fish, and don't run along the shore as crocs see invis. I camp out at the dock to make sure I didn't have something following me, then come back in and get to work.
