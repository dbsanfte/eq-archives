Regarding the question of what "100%" means, it means that your chance of criting is increased 100%, so, if your chance of criting normally was 10%, it would increase to 20%, ie you would crit one out of every 5 hits as opposed to 1 out of every 10. Likewise, if your chance of criting was 1%, it would become 2%. From that perspective warriors benefit the most from that part of the spell due to their inherent ability to crit/crip.
