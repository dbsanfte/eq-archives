Zaviax McCleo
PoD is my new solo spot, though I usually rely on having Aego first before doing it. I'll also get KEI though it's not really needed. Though with it I'll usually finish the battle with 60% mana which is really good when you hear how much is needed to take a rat down there, lol.

When you zone into PoD hug the right wall, pass the lake and when you come to the beach you'll see a rock. I always stay behind that rock. No roamers there and there are rats who walk up and down the beach to the left and there are roaming mobs to the right. Watch out for quad kiters there though. You'll usually see them there and if not, a Necro fear kiting. (which is more comforting than quaders imo)

Get scooby up, give him a HP buff. You can cast celerity too if you want but you'll be using him mainly as a root spell.

Go out and pull your mob with Malosini. When you get back to the rock sick your pet on it at the same time you're casting slow. No slow = quick dead pooch. As soon as slow sticks root, back off, summon horse/lizzy, back pet off. Epic dot, Plague, BoN. Keep re-applying DoTs and root as needed. Don't let Scooby tank too long. I usually pull him off when he gets to 2 bubs of health and then cast Stoicism on him.

To take down a rat I usually cast two epic dots, one plague, (sometimes two though the second is casted with 25-30% health to go on the mob) and about 4 BoN.

With Lizzy and the time that it takes to take down the mob it's no wonder I'm at 60% mana at the end of the battle. I wouldn't want to do this without a mount.

XP wise, I haven't tested 100% XP yet. With full 100% in AA I was making 2-3% per kill. With 50/50 I was making 1% in each or 1% in XP and 2% in AA, depending on the mobs.

Goos don't give as much as Rats but are noticeably easier.

Good luck! :)
