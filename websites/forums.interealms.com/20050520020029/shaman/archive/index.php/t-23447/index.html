Goldark
The science of war site is superb and their tactics are mostly very solid.

Grummus:
Fairly easy tactically, but you need a decent enough tank, enough DPS to kill him before the dots kill you, and some mgb heals over time can really make or break the raid. Probably want about 40+ people levels 60-65 including a core of 65 Warrior MT and a few 65 clerics for the first goes, but is doable with around 30 at your level once you know what you're doing.

Terris Thule:
Very easy mob for even a small, moderately equipped guild. Its all about the tactics and timing, so be prepared to wipe a few times at first learning the script, but once learnt its a doddle. Key tip: Illusion enchanters as ogres and have everybody standing by enchanters for the butterfly phase as they AE stun.

Probably want 30+ for first attempt, 24+ subsequently.

Xanamech:
Easiest of the lot and really good fun clearing up to him. I'd suggest doing this as your first raid. 24+ for first attempt, 18+ subsequently.

Manaetic Behemoth:
He's a toughy and with your levels/AA/gear you will likely want 45-50 people at least. I wouldn't even attempt it until you've done all of the above at least twice. Once you've got the hang of it 30-35 is probably enough, any less than that and handling the spiders plus getting him killed fast becomes very hard without some EP+ gear.

Don't forget you need the PoN maze before you can do TT. Also, try raiding some of the other mini-named such as Vahksiz the Shade (PoN) and the manaetic Prototypes (POI factory).
