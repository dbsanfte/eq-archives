The drunken pirates are found by following the RH wall when you zone in from Gunthak.

Zone in invis because the scouts at the zone in area have a big aggro range and are tough for their level.

While invis, follow the RH wall until you come to an alleyway behind a row of small buildings - the pirates are in the buildings. There is a good little "safe spot" next to a barrel type still in the alleyway.

You can go into the buildings invis and target a drunken pirate, then go back to your pull spot and cast on them from there - it worked for me, anyway. I would pull with slow, and their pathing was long enough to land another spell before they arrived. It took me about 12 or so kills to get the bottle
