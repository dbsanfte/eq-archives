LDoN adventures should be perfect for you 2 if you can find groups. They are timed instanced dungeons for a single party (or a raid but hardly anyone ever raided 'em). The experience will be good at your levels, and accumulating adventure points for wins there will let you upgrade your gear, as well as getting some great spells (the curse line rocks). In addition, they drop augments that you can use to upgrade your existing armor. The adventures have a 90 minute timer on them (but you can leave earlier when you have met the objective of the mission), and give you 1/2 an hour to get to the dungeon and get inside.

Unfortunatly, LDoNs no longer provide significant exp after level 65. Since most are over 65, it is harder to find groups now - everyone is in teh new zones grinding exp and looking for spell runes.

Get maps - you will learn to love them. http://www.mapfiend.net/

Have fun and good luck.

I'll take a stab at this one since I have taken off two 6 month stretches. Burnout is the #1 issue for me, so I'm careful about overplaying. #2 is to take a longer term approach to spells,aa's armour etc so I don't get pissed if I lose on a roll, etc. #3 is to download maps, woot - nice not to get lost. #4 is to leave ASAP any crappy group you get into, but do it with some style so you don't hurt peeps feelings. #5 is to figure out what you like to do in EQ, don't grind if you don't like it, don't be in static camps if you don't like it, etc.

Myself, I came back and really liked LDON (as above peeps have said). Have fun with it, change up the groups (have all pet groups, have all shaman groups, be the puller, be the only healer, etc. etc.). LDON is only boring if you are chicken and don't experiment. LDON will fine tune your skills immensely if you are puller, only healer and only slower and no CC - try it and you will see.

I would also recommend thinking about a guild - although raiding isnt my favorite thing it allows you to see parts of EQ you would not ever see and hopefully get a steady experience group partnership going - help on epic, etc.

Thats about it. Have fun.

As someone from a small guild, I thought I'd add a few points from what people have said.

If you want a tactics flag the easiest way is to go kill Diaka in the Plane of Storms as you can get the alternate access flag by collecting the four corners of a Diaka symbol, which when handed in will flag a group for tactics (You still get the bonus AA and PoP charm boost doing it this way).

GoD isn't too useful to me, but I did find the first zone was ok Xp and has a few nice drops that randomly drop from anything there. Also some of the quest items there are quite nice and you get access to the Abysmal Sea which is a god send for tradeskill and had most buyable spells for sale.
