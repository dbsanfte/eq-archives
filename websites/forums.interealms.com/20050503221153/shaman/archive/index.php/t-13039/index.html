Well, I haven't posted to much on here, but I've read the boards for a few years now, and posted occasionally when I had firsthand info on someone's question. I've been 65 for a little over a month now, and one thing kept evading me - Torpor. I'm not in a huge guild, and I'm also the raid leader, and do lots of things with my guildies, so I don't make alot of cash. No time for farming. So no Q ;) , and no buying Torpor. I've killed countless Kunark dragons, and even more GWs for hours on end trying to get Torpor/Malo.

This morning I woke up to a post on my guild's board. Apparently a few guildies (one in particular) really wanted me to finally get this spell, so they went down and farmed emp themselves. Low and behold it dropped :) They had a pickup or two in their group (who, ironically, I knew) and when he found out it was for me, he didn't roll either. So just like that I get Torpor in my sleep :)

I consider myself a pretty decent shaman, I do far more then just slow mobs for groups ;P But I always felt like I wasn't all I could be without this spell (you know, tanking rampage when you need, easier to offtank adds, not to mention the mana regen). I still need malo, but being 65 I don't have to many problems, so Torpor was the huge one.

Anyway I'm stoked right now, and I figured many on here could appreciate just how hard/worth it this spell is, and also how awesome it was for them to spend 3 hours camping a spell for me while I was asleep.
