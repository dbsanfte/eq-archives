The following is how I leveled up my skill with the least plat(after practicing and having required level). Keep in mind i was mid 50's before i starting taking alchemy serious. My wisdom was also 225+. Starting with Lesser vigor(4pp) max skill at 17. Next is Lesser adroitness, cohesion, power, or accuracy(4pp) maxed at 22. Then minor cold or antibody(4pp) maxed at 27. Then minor heat, purity, or null(4pp) maxed at 32. Next, is sow(5pp) maxed at 37. Then IU(11pp) maxed at 42. EB(12p) maxed at 47. Dmg shield(10pp) maxed at 52. Disease or Poison Tx(7pp) maxed at 57. Ultravision(7pp) maxed at 63. Shrink(9pp) maxed at 70. Stability, Vigor, or cohesion(9pp) maxed at 76. Accuracy, Power, or adroitness(9pp) maxed at 83. Null or antibody(9pp) maxed at 90. Aura of heat or cold(9pp) maxed at 96. Aura of purity(9pp) maxed at 103. Spirit shield(27pp) maxed at 110. I then did a few greater negation(35pp) to about 117, and then switched to Kilva skin flame(30pp) up to skill 136. Greater Stability , Vigor, or Cohesion(18pp) to skill 143. Then Greater Power, Adroitness, or Accuracy(18pp) to skill 150. Now, you can either do Greater Aura Cold, Antibody, or Null(18pp) to 156 and then Greater Aura Heat or Greater Purity(18pp) to 163 OR start with Grow potions(11pp) until skill 175
