Two big things need to be fixed in PvP (I'm a bluebie so when I say PvP I mean dueling or just arena brawls).

1) Interrupts. I have gotten to the point where I just decline duels from any melee. Knights are the worst because they have bash - the absolute worst is a knight with that stupid runic proc axe. Not only does it take me five tries to channel a spear of torment, it only hits him for like 100 damage which is absorbed by the rune (which procs again immediately). Not being able to channel spells due to some knight just hitting autoattack and bash is probably the most frustrating thing I have ever encountered in EQ. Any time I duel a knight with EP plus gear, it takes me at least half an hour to kill him or just give up. I get bashed and stunned over and over and over again and there is nothing I can do to counter it. Dueling and losing is ok. Dueling and losing because you can't even cast a spell is not ok - its annoying. Oh yeah - beastlord pets. These are even more annoying because you can't joust them. I've been playing FPS games for years so I can literally run circles around most players and buy myself a second or two to get a headstart on a cast, but against a beastlord's warder, I can't do this. It autotracks and you can't get it off you. It just stands there quadding and bashing you, while the beastlord quads and kicks you. Fortunately they don't do too much damage or there's no way a bst would ever lose a duel.

2) Resists. If they just made our disease dots unresistable I'd probably be happy. I don't need to be able to do a lot of damage, I just need to be able to do something. As it stands now, no dot will ever land on an VT+ geared person. Without our dots all we have are nukes. Velium strike is pretty crappy, but with malos in I can usually kill a caster with it off of an endless chain of small partial resists. However, against a melee, there is no way you are ever going to channel a VS (see point 1). Spear of torment is a joke. A lot of classes have access to some form of rune, whether it be a clicky, a proc, or an actual spell. A wizard with epic can pretty much stalemate me just by recasting the rune every time it finally breaks. A necro or wizard with their time wand, you can just forget about this duel being over in under an hour. If I had some way of doing consistant damage, though, it would be ok.

Basically, I am just annoyed that shamans go from being one of the best pvp classes to one of the worst (although still one of the hardest to kill) due to gear inflation.
