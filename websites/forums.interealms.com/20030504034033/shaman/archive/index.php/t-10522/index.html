Ok, not having decent heal spells for MANY levels does bite, but you can still heal in some situations.

I OFTEN play the group healer in guild exp. groups. The key is to know your limitations--many places require a cleric as main healer. In my 50's I've been main healer in Burining Woods, Chardok, OS, Tene. Mtns (hunting named vamps) and many more reg. exp. group situations.

Long as you are not chain pulling (I mean literally NO breaks), you not fighting red mobs, and not fighting +50 summoning mobs with 30K or more hitpoints who rampage, etc..., you can often (with care) be group healer. Not in the PoP zones though......
