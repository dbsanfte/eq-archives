Crystal Caverns is perfect for velium farming. On average, you'll get a brick, block, or small piece off of every 3rd or 4th miner. Plus, killing the orcs now gives Coldstone dwarf faction. In the dorf city down there, there is a banker and two forges. You can just fill up on bricks, run back to dorf central, break em down into pieces, throw the pieces into the bank and run back out to get more without ever leaving the zone. To top it off, the orcs can drop some decent cash and cash loot.

I, myself, usually get about 4-6 stacks of velium pieces in about an hour of casual pet grinding there. But like people have mentioned earlier, the real downside of CC is that it tends to get crowded during prime hours and weekends... it's pretty easy for a mage or necro (even us with our dogdog) to wipe the place out in a sitting.

Solemneyes
I am currently ally with CoV and Yelniak, and although the factions are not too extreme to level back up, my question is- is it worth it to farm velium there? Any information you have would be much appreciated.

As far as is it worth it to you to farm there - for the fastest farming with a forge in zone - it cannot be beat. I have tried all tofs, velks, CC and SS and most of what you have heard here I have found to be true.

tofs: quiet and mostly peaceful camp allowing afks between spawns. However - no forge and takes a long time for spawns if you don;t have something to do during the lulls between spawns.

velks: pretty slow in my experience for farming but might be nice if you are two boxing and PLing an alt.

CC: if you play LATE at night when less people are there its not so bad - there is a forge in zone but you have to be an active farmer (IE running around searching for spawns). Notice I said less people - not none. This is one of the few older zones that seems to almost never be empty.

SS: The only bad thing here is the faction. There are more spawns than I could kill before repops (that drop velium), theres and easy to hit forge you can incorporate on your farming loop, and often your the only one killing here. If the faction is nothing to you (IE your in a guild way past velious content or you just don care) than by all means this is a great spot. If faction is more important than sheer farming speed/volume then I would recomend CC or tofs. Velks IMO is only worth doing if you have a twink in tow for xp or something.
