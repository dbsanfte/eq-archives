This one got beat into the dirt a couple years ago. Folks posted pages and pages of logs, statistical evidence, etc.

Other folks rebutted it saying CHA didn't matter, etc.

Quite frankly, in my own experience, 2 factors help to land slows -- proximity and charisma.

This isn't a guarantee that it's gonna land every time, but in my own experience, I've found that charisma over 100 and being right under the mob really help to land that slow on the first cast.

Your mileage may vary, and some folks will probably say it doesn't help...

Whether or not it helps, that's anyone's guess, but I don't see how it hurts either.

As for REALLY high CHA, say 150+, I haven't found that to make much of a difference. Certainly not enough to warrant giving up AC / mana / HP / resist gear for the extra CHA, it's easy for us to get CHA to 100+ via buffs, and if you can get it to 125 or so without giving up much other gear, I'd say go for it. In ToV, etc. I try to run with around 110 - 115 CHA to help land those slows.
