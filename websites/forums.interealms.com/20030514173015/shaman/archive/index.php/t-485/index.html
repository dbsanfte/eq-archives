House
My Troll did a lot of the Grobb quests and Lynuga conned dubious to him, yet she still gave him the quest (I think it took about 20 rubies to get the hoop). You should be okay as an Ogre unless you've done tons and tons of the Grobb quests, as I believe you're correct in that she's on Broken Skull Clan faction.

Best way I found to do this is to find her in the swamp, hail her, bind there, and then run to Grobb to buy a Ruby. Gate back and turn it in, and then run back to Grobb to sell whatever you get and buy another Ruby, until you get the hoop. I calculated that I spent a little over 1kpp for the hoop, which is pretty good (though I know I might just have gotten lucky). Also, if you're adventurous, I'm sure you could attack her and lead her to the Grobb zone line.
