Okay, this may sound strange coming from me--especially since I've been a pretty obvious proponent of the "shaman are fricking broken! fix us already!" group--but damn, I've just been having fun lately (yes, in EQ!).

Maybe we're all suffering a bit of burn out. The tired old "been there, done that, got the T-shirt" syndrome. I know we're still broken. I know we desperately still need rebalancing at the GoD+ level.

Still, I'm suddenly having fun again. Hopefully you guys will, too!

In a fit of macabre masochism, I started the breakdown in communications quest. It's a traditional SoE epic quest--tons of rare drops, mindless camping, repetive camps, but damn if I'm not enjoying it. I'm having to do sewers, vexd, and tipt again, in addition to most of the lower level zones in GoD, but I can help guildmates camp spells while doing it. It's something that is going to require a guild to complete, of course, but still.

Raid wise, we're playing around in uqua. We still have some back-flagging to do for Ikkinz Raid Trial 4 to get the whole guild flagged, but we're aiming to stay mostly in uqua with the occasional 1 day Time raid to keep fleshing out the equipment of our newer folks. Uqua is annoyingly hard as others have described--one or two accidental deaths can basically wipe a raid (each person that dies spawns another mob).

So what is up? In the words of Arnold, "it's not a tumah! It's not a tumah at all." I'm just having fun again for a change.

So tonight, if you log in, go do something fun! Take a cleric buddy and see if you can train every mob in Umbral to the zone in! Go find that annoying buff beggar and give him the Inner Fire he so richly deserves. (Hit your guild leader with it too. That's always entertaining!). If you're unguilded, go find one! If you find the right one, it can completely change the character of the game for you--in a good way. It's just nice to log into a bunch of friends and go hang out. Plus, you can't imagine how much better you will get as you learn each other's strengths and weaknesses. You'll pull off stunts you wouldn't believe! Go solo something nasty for the heck of it!

Tonight's mission, should you choose to accept it, is to go have fun!

Today's upbeat message coming at ya from the leezard!

Best wishes,

Chutoi
