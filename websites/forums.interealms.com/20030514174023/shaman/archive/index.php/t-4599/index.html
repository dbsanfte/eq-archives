Jaruden
I'm getting to be an expert at these, so here's some more tips:

Sit the party in the hallway, 1 good group of 60's will do it, more is better just in case. Clear the room of all mobs. Handin to the apparition, he becomes Vauris and walks to the dias. Sit far back in the tunnel and watch. Do not move for the 12 minutes the script goes on.
Once he pops, pull with malo (if you've got it). You will get 2 arisen adds (and possibly a little bit of room repop, named mob sometimes). Get slow on him ASAP. Even worth having the enc tash before mezzing.
Once he's dead loot the bones, and kill the mobs you have mezzed. Then kill the last 2 arisen in the room (you have time). Once those are dead, you can pull any more room repops to clear the way in, or just charge through and do the handin. I dont know the full timer on Rile, but there's really no reason you can't kill VS and his arisen long before it runs out.
Extra loot from VS is the mask (stats above), and a necklace which kinda sucks. I looted the necklace as a momento of the fight, it's like 3 ac 3 to a few stats or something.

If anyone is doing the fight, feel free to send me a cross server tell for any questions you've got.

Venomblood Draconis
Pretty much everyone has covered the strategies you need to use. When I did mine, we set up in the tunnel just outside the room (on the dirt, not on the "floor" area. ).

We had an Iksar SK doing the pulling, so he wouldn't aggro the Apparition. Once all the mobs in the room were dead, I turned in the relics to the Apparition.

It seemed to take quite some time before he moved into the room. As stated by others, the thing takes time to go through the different parts, so be patient.

The Apparition (now Varius) eventually moves into the room and the swirling mass of bones and 4 Arisen spawn. The mass is untargetable.

We pulled the 4 Arisen up to the tunnel and locked them down. We did not kill them. The Iksar SK pulled them, that may be why VS didn't come with them. Just slow them and keep them mezzed.

Eventually the swirling mass will "warp" up on the stage with the remains of Rile and the fight will occur. VS wins, of course.

Now wait for VS to become targetable. Once that happened, my group killed VS in the room, but back a bit from the stage. Once VS was dead, Rile popped. VS did run on low health, and he finally dropped near the wall on the east side of the room.

I did the turn in and got the cudgel. There were many guild mates in the room with me, but not in close proximity to the stage, so I don't know what Rile's aggro radius is. It is not room wide if he does aggro. I suggest you tell all your non-Iksar buddies to stay well back.

Following the turn in, we killed the 4 Arisen. We had one instant repop when we did this.

We were able to accomplish the whole encounter before the room repopped, so we didn't have to worry about those mobs.

This worked for me. I don't know how it will work for others. I had 2 groups with me. 2 enchanters holding 2 Arisen each. I slowed all of them.

I hope this helps.
