I am assuming there is some margin of error, how much is debatable, but for my theories here I assume up to a margin of 1 DPS.

a. Error exists between the DPS difference on the parses with MS, and FoS.

b. The SLTW+DW+FoS is more accurate than the one with MS.

c. The extra strength in focus added no DPS, therefore TrueSpirit is at max str with MS.

d. The +140 ATK from FA adds 5.6 DPS, the eqivalency of a buff of 140 STR without a stat cap.

e. The parse with MS+SLTW+DW is in error.

f. The parse with FA's 140+ ATK at 5.6DPS will be deemed accurate at thie point in time of this theory.


Conclusions:

True Spirit's base strength is close to 180, this I believe to be fairly accurate. When 140ATK adds 5.6 DPS and 75 STR add 3.8 DPS we have a definate discrepency. Logic only points that 75 str bring dogdog close to max STR and that the STR parse without FA is either too high, or the FA parse was low.
