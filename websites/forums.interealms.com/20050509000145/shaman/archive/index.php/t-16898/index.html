Mana efficiency comes late for shaman. Our spells are far from mana efficient. Canni does make up for this.

I wouldnt worry too much about focus items yet, as your level 3 focuses will not work with spells above level 60. If you can get a level 4 focus item, by all means get it.... but you will gain more by leveling than by spending your time and effort/money on the focus items.

Unless you have torpor sitting in the bank, go get stoicism. Even if you have torpor banked, I'd get stoicism. Its half of a torpor, which is still very nice. This method (stoicism/torpor + canni 4) is awesome for stretching your mana pool during action. You regain slightly more than 1 canni 4 worth of hps per pulse with stoicism. Two canni 4 will recover the mana cost of a stoicism. In 30 seconds, thats a net gain of ~160 mana. It will keep your fingers busy, but if you make it a habit of stiocism/canni whenever you arent doing something else, you will keep your mana flowing well.

If you get a break, such as a long pull or the like you can canni down then cast your CH. This is actualy going to give you a bigger boost in mana than the chain casting stoicism/canni routine... but you need uninterrupted time to do it in. If you arent afraid of low hp agro, you can even do this during a fight after you have slowed/dotted/sent in your pet. Canni down ~2k hps = ~1k mana -400 mana for a ch = 600 mana gain.

With torpor/Q, specialization increases, scr/scrm, scm, focus effects, and of course canni 5 your mana recovery rate will increase by huge amounts as you continue to level. It only gets better from here. Soon it becomes more of a problem to find USEFULL ways to spend all the mana you are generating.

EDIT: oh yeah.. if you are outdoors use your horse/drogmoor!! The best investment you can make by far! Also you will be amazed how much help it can be just keeping a regen going on yourself/your group -it all adds up.
