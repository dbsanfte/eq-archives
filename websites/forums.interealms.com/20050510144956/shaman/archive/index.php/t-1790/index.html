Bigguss
Check out this thread (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?s=&threadid=1813) - most of the same pieces will work for you.

The main ones are an iksar BP (regen!!!), 6/65 rings, JB helm/arms/gloves/boots (cheap, nice AC, right-click buffs later), ry'gorr for the rest, clay guardian shield, nice ratio piercer and basher (to keep both skills up, maybe a slime harpoon and a smoldering brand.) Since you're starting from scratch I'd also pick up a Rallic pack (85% weight reduction, 10 slots, giant capacity, around 1k) and an EE bag (8 slots, 70% weight reduction, large capacity, few hundred pp) - don't know what I'd do without these.

Make sure to save some of that money for spells, food, ports, etc. If you're going to be hunting outside, you might want to think of saving for a horse. Reference details are here (http://eq.castersrealm.com/editorials/horses.asp) - basically they allow you to meditate without sitting down (while just standing around, casting other spells, etc.) If you get one, I recommend turning off the horse/elemental models (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?s=&threadid=1324).

Good hunting!
