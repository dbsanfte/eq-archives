Zarniwoop
Given that RD mitigates slow to only be slightly effective (approx. 25% of 40% slow, or 10% slowed), the shamans in my guild didn't spend too much effort trying to slow him. If you have a lot of shamans in attendance, it may be worth having one or two try to land Grummus (after MD). It's also a good idea to ask the Beast Lords in attendance to try to land slow as well. Swallow your pride :)

A more important duty for the shamans is making sure you have someone chain-casting Abolish Disease on the MT and Rampage Tank, to cure the slow-debuff that RD procs and to help ensure your tanks are keeping agro.

Any remaining shamans should be spam healing the MT and RT.

That's how my guild was successful.
