You should be able to do it...Here's what I did solo at level 34:

First, I fully buffed myself and pet, including disease and magic resistance, and making sure pet had haste and regen. I parked my pet a ways away, medded to full health and mana, and pulled with slow, eating the harm touch (make sure you have regen on). I then pulled back to pet, sicced doggie on him, and rooted him. If slow didn't stick, I would try until it stuck. Slowing him is key! After rooting, I dotted with both poison and disease (forget the names), and cannied up some more mana. If root broke when dotting, I would just root again. I'm pretty sure I had no problem landing spells, except he tended to resist root. When the poison dot wore off, I dotted again. I also threw in some nukes to bring him down quickly. When root finally broke, he hated me so much that I just kited him around, letting my pet nip at his heels until he started running fleeing.

It was a tough fight, but I think I ended up with ~30h and 20m. I think the three most important things I did was to haste the pet, slow Glarion, and to eat the HT. I could take it (the HT), but it would take too big of a chunk of health from my pet for him to be effective.

Hope you get him!
