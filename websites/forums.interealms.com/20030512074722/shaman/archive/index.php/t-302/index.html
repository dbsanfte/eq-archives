Greetings,

I've assisted a few people on the Shield of Bane Warding Quest out of Katta (norms, Iskar and Ogre's) and have noted some places to get faction hits that do seem to help.

Iskar start off unable to do the Quests in Katta and will get a real WWF smackdown from some less that happy people in there.

Sancrus Seru - Hunting in this zone will give positive hits to Katta factions but will obviously tank you with Seru.

Mons Letalis - Hunting the Thought Leeches in the caves (n1000 p35) give all positive hits with Seru including Seru himself - a good place to counter hits in Seru and you'll need a brain anyway for the SoBW Quest).

Tene Mnts - kill Coterie vampires - alot of them. The hits are not big but they do add up over time.

If you can get beyond KoS, start doing the Ralic Pack Quest with the Grimling Runner (you'll need invis the first few times you do the hand in) and then you should be good to go.
