in case you weren't joking, MQ means Multi-Quest. the deal is person A has some no-drop items for a quest that needs multiple items turned in, and person B wants to get the quest, and has everything EXCEPT what person A has. So person a gives the quest NPC his items first, then person B finishes up. The way quests, work, the person who turns in the LAST item to the npc gets the quest reward.

for example: as soon as my thurg faction is high enough (at amiably now =\), i'm going to get my Thurg gloves, because my housemate has the necessary piece of chain from Kael and i have the gems.
