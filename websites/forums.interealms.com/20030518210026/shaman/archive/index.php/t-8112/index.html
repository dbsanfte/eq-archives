To get your tear, you need to break and do the mini-bosses of Fear.

To do this, you need experience. You will have to practice breaking in and holding a corner of fear before you can even think about pulling a demi-boss. Fear isn't that hard (compared to Hate, etc), but it can easily lead to a wipe and a painful painful painful cr.

Anyway, to answer your question - if this is your guild's first trip to fear, bring everyone. No less than 3 groups. A monk is a /must/, if only to retrieve everyone's corpses and warn when the door is clear.

Good luck.

Experience is the key to breaking fear... if you have never done fear, before you lead your guild there, tag along on another guild's fear raid. You'll get an idea of what camp 1 looks like, of how a break-in goes, etc.
