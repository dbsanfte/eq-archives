Tugela
You can't parse stuff like this on one mob and expect to get a clear answer. You need to do it with a variety of mob types/levels in different scenarios (group/raid etc) and with different toons (level/gear/class), since all of these things might affect the numbers you produce. In this particular case it is "increase chance to avoid melee by 7%", which means that it is a relative increase to that value, not an absolute one. If your chance to avoid is 20% you shouldnt see a noticable effect, whereas if your chance to avoid is 80% you will see a large effect. That implies that the results from whatever parses you do may vary greatly depending on the specific mob involved and the quality of your toon.
