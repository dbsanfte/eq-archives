Hiya Tify.
Don't know how experianced you are in Sebilis, so you might know this already, but here goes.
I've been 2boxing my 53 shaman and 61 beastlord at NG (Necrosis scarab/guardian, the 3-bug spawn across from building to zoneout) with good results. I can see that you have both epic and JBB, so you should be well able to hold the camp. Its no spell drops, but its good loot as the bugs drop gems often, and the Necrosis scarab(the named rarespawn of the camp) drops decent loot to sell in bazaar or to a vendor(Cane of the tranquill sells for 150ish pp to vendors, rest of loot should easily sell for more in Bazaar).
Its a fairly straight forward camp, 3 bugs and a Krup-roamer. When you pull the nearest scarab, #2 will add, but as they are easily rootable this shouldn't be much of a problem. Slow, dot dot pettank and you're fine. Rinse and repeat. You want to kill the krup-roamer first. They are high level frogs, and can come as shamans, warriors or wizards. They DO hit rather hard, but they dont have unreasonably many hp, and nothing the above cicle wouldn't be able to handle.
Now for a final note, the rare-spawn on the camp Necrosis scarab DOES summon, but with it rooted, slowed and hard dotted you should be able to let pet tank it while you heal up whatever damage you get when you get summoned. I've seen this done by lesser shamans, so you should be able to do it aswell.
Can't tell you what kind of xp you would get, but the xp i got on my duo was good enough. Solo should be significantly better.
