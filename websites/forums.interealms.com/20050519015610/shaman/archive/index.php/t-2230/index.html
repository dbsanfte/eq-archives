Major changes... hmmm....

We get Summon Companion now to summon our pets.

Minor bearform at 29. Several other new Luclin spells were added at all levels.

No spellbook required to med, ever. Just sit down. (Once you get med).

No XP loss until level 11. Level 10 and below, no corpse when you die - you get respawned with all your gear at your bind point.

Best low-level armor quest AFAIK is still the one that results in leather of some kind, is it the spider fur quest? I forget. You can look it up.
