Tobyus
I'm not sure how high they are, but the shin ghoul knights and such in lower guk may be doable for you. They are pretty wimpy compared to stuff in newer expansion packs at that level. If you can find someone to group with, then the bedroom might be a good choice too, then you can pull bats, ghoul frogs, and spiders from the hall way past that area.

Find you a melee person to team up with and you can have a ball in guk. Just don't kill things in Upper or Lower Guk that aren't aggro to you, and you will save yourself some headache while learning the zones, and possibly for the future for some quests :)

Edit:
If you happen to pick up Lost Dungeons of Norrath you can open up some more grouping options and some really nice options for upgrading your gear.
Lileann
With both my SK and Shm I soloed and/or duoed pretty close to that level at the giant fort in FM. You may want to go to Lake of Ill Omen first till 30 (killing on the plains btwn the mountain and the water on the fort side is pretty good) or hack on Sarnak recruits near he fort and FM ZL. Then head to FM and after a couple of more levels the fort will be easy but the killing should be fast enough that its still worthwhile. Been awhile since I did it with my shm (he's 65 with 70 some AA's ATM) but wasn't too long ago with my SK. There are usually a couple of peeps around the fort who may team up with you to make things faster as well. Where you are at right now (Marus Seru) is actually a really good spot, just a little slow at times. Good luck!
