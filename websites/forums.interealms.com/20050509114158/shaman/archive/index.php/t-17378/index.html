Grumbash
With the release of LDON, things have changed somewhat for a Shaman. LDON has actually been a good training ground for playing the shaman and allows you to learn new tactics. Being non-stop, there isn't any time for a Shaman to sit and med. However, if you play it right, you will have plenty of mana and be able to be an active melee fighter also.

First is to get yourself the Soulstripper, a must for many Shamans. Lifetaps 75 hps. Then you need to Augument this with 340 LDON points for Vampire Talon 50 hp Lifetap weapons process. (Potential 125pt Lifetap based on number of processes). With low delay and haste, you can do some DPS and heal up as you use Canni 4 between swings. I tend to get lots of processes due to high dex, so it works for me.

With using Regen, you will have plenty of mana and Hitpoints to canni and never need to sit. Also, you will get much praise from your group as being an awesome Shamy, both as a melee master and spell caster.

Be aware there are times you will have to step away and do re-buffs, slows, etc.. but most often you will do well (Make sure ya have KEI also). Keep in mind though, if there are multiple mobs due to a bad pull, your going to be real busy doing slows, etc.. thus expending a heck of alot of mana. This may require you to canni up a bit before jumping into the fight again.

Never, Ever, Canni past 60% when using this tactic as you may find yourself dead quick if agroed due to failed slow, etc..

Now to the point of the issue. If you can handle LDON's fast pace and continue to keep mana, then doing regular grouping in the planes, etc.. will be childs play. Suggest practice the fast pace of LDON to learn the skill for making mana.

Now this is only my suggestion and it works for me. I quite often get complements for being a pretty good Shaman and many comment that they rarely grouped with a Shaman that never had to sit and med.
Different strategies work for different people based on how the play their Shaman. I just had to develop one that worked for me.

I suggest getting the Soulstripper and augumenting it with a Lifetap process. In addition to the standard process that comes with the weapon and the Augmentation, you can't lose. It sells for roughly 4500 pp and has been the best PP I never had a problem giving away.

Anyway, hehe, Can't wait till I get Canni 5.
