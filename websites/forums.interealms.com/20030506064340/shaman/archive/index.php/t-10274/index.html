Heigelyac
3areas of interest on your map.
1. area 3 is where Nathyn Illuminous' house is. 2 sonic wolves and 2 guards out front, more guards inside.

Nice spot because killing these guards RAISES your faction with other guards, merchants etc. bad spot because once you have bad faction here, the guard's aggro range is HUGE.

2. The twilight sea zone in. you have 6 static guards and 2 roamers there. Lower level than nathyns, but easy. also, when you zone into Katta from twilight, if you take the bridge out of this room to your right, jump off and swim to the wall, there is an icon there (a big white picture) that you can click to zap right to the bank (area 11)

Drops faction with Katta


3. Area 11. The bank. Lots of people kill bank guards, lots of guards to kill. I understand that there are some quest drops from the guards inside the bank.

Drops Katta fation

All are fine 2 boxing, but once you hit 57ish, forget the second box, you don't need it.

I did all of these at 56 solo w/ epic- bane- jbb- horse- alpha

Baz3
Added info, from my experience dere:

The port from the icon at TS zone in drops you out in the middle of the bank area. You will not aggro any of the static guards there, and can run to the wall or invis up safely. If some one has got the guards moving around, is a good idea to have invis up.

The guards INSIDE the bank are a higher level thatn the guards outside - Centurions, iirc. The one time I got adds (level 58 at the time) two of em came out and wiped me fast. root didn't stick long enough to gate. That is why you should always invis up to go in and bank - go behind the bankers to deal with them.

And another place to hunt is where the walkway around to left from the TS zone in reaches the tunnels. About neg 500 and neg 1800 on the map you link. This has one big advantage to someone learning tohunt these guards, as you can always single pull them if you are careful. you do need to learn to time the roamers. The trick is that these guards in the tunnels aggro on line of sight, so you can edge up so that you can see only one of the two at the first turn in the tunnel. pull and kill, then do the other one.
