Shabura Naarteban
Okay, regardless of whether you can get to PoM or not ... you don't want to do the PoM quests. You're a masochist, sure, but you said you actually wanted gear, and the PoM stuff isn't happening before you die of old age (as an aside - why do people try to con folks into trying for PoM gear ... that's just cruel, people).

I recommend:

- Blessed Coldain Prayer Shawl. Masochism is the prerequisite for tradeskill based quests. 1 reasonably high level group, or 2 groups to be safe, can handle the 8th shawl "war" with no problem. You'll pretty certainly never see an upgrade.
- Circlet of the Falinkan. Proceed only if you are friends with Claws of Veeshan (ally). Easily 1-groupable. While not the powerhouse item that it used to be, you won't see a replacement for this soon.
- Cloak of the Wise. Its a lot of farming (and some luck) in the various Tier 1 PoP zones to get all the pages, but its a nice cloak. Technically, the Heirophant's Cloak is a lot better ... but if you've got more time than money, this one might be for you. Plus - earn great XP while you "quest".
- Regal Band of Bathezid/Spirit Wracked Cords. Faction *really* sucks, and so does trying to snag the Angry Goblin, much less that accursed Urn. That said, you'll likely never replace either. I consider the RBoB to be better than Valor rings. SWC is simply not upgradeable until Vex Thal or equivelent Planar.
- Ring of Dain Frostreaver IV. While there are 2 other rings on this list (Shissar, Bathezid), this is worth looking at in the long term. Ring 9 is not worth wearing if you have the other 2, but its an indoor-usable insta-clicky item, and those ARE handy. Also, if you manage to get Ring 9, on many servers it is possible to host/lead open ringwars, especially if you have connections with some other guild that's into that sort of thing. Many people find ringwars fun, and they often draw help fast. Should you succeed, Ring 10 is definately an amazing item, and one you will never, ever replace.
- Ring of the Shissar. Okay, so its the Ssra Emperor key, and the mobs for the Insignias suck. Its still 1-2 groupable, if you're on your game, and along with the Regal Band, will make a pair of rings that will likely outstrip anything you could get conventionally. Plus, you get to tell people that you have the key to Emperor Ssra. =P
- Sanctum Guardian's Earring. This is the Spirit of Garzicor quest. Proceed only if you are friends with Claws of Veeshan (ally) and into smithing (as in, near 200). The final dragon fight might be too tough for your friends - its a lot like Zlandicar, but its something to consider.
- Skyiron Cudgel of the Ancients. Faction sucks, and parts of this quest (read: Kurn's) sap the will to live. Forget about the clicky effect and check the stats, though - you'll be hard pressed to beat it without raid-mob drops, and you can impress your friends with it - these suckers are still rare.

Non-quested:

- Barbed Dragonscale Boots. Run around 6k on my server, your prices may vary. Best HP boots you are likely to see unless you get a chance for Vindi's boots or something unusual falls in your lap. Should you prefer mana and mana-regen to hp, then consider camping the Boots of Flowing Slime, but expect to have you and your group of friends lose the next couple weeks of their life in there.
- Tae Ew Equipment. The Tae Ew BP, Legs, and Shield will require some hunting in CT and the services of a GM smith. They are likely to be worth the time and trouble.

Don't do:

- Shield of Bane Warding. Tae Ew is better.
- Sigil Earring of Veracity. You don't want to get earring 3 - those reports are the devil. Earring 4 requires some funny tactics that you may not be able to pull off, and earring 5 needs Seru's head, which is totally unaccessible. If you've got the force to do earring 3/4, you've got the force to do Sanctum Guardian anyway (assuming you can deal with the faction and the smithing), and its frankly more worth the trouble than camping reports. I've been there. Trust me.
