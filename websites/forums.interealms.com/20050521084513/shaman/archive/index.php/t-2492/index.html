*INFO* Most Common Item Upgrade/Twink List
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Since a good load of the questions on this board consist of "This is my equipment, what should I get next?" or "I'm starting a monk with xxx amount of money, what are my options?" I have decided to compile a list of the most common upgrade paths for equipment by slot for the use of the public.

This list does not contain anywhere near the full list of what is available for monks, just the most commonly available options. Any additions people think should be there will be most welcome.

* This list contains ONLY droppable items

* The items are sorted by usefulness - Lowest to Highest

* The order of the list is due to my personal opinion of the items, but is generally sorted by Haste/Effect, Hitpoints/Stamina, AC, Stats/Resists, with weight being a factor in all of them

I find this to be a very useful thread over on the Monk boards. I think something like this could be of use here as well, a "common" upgrade list for Shamans. Check out the thread and think Shaman.

I am going to start on a draft of this, I'll pop it up for your revisions as soon as I get a good start.
