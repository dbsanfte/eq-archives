Ruatonim
I'm playing a night elf priest~ currently highest on my server even-alliance side anyhow not sure about horde (level 30.5). I'm not really sure what your strategy is, I hate acronyms... so I'll just spell mine out.

I usually have shield going at all times when something is incoming (I almost always solo btw). I pull with the DOT pain, which almost guarantees it to run full course (note: mine is max extended duration via talents). I then melee as it arrives. At this point I cast starshards since it is channeled and cannot be interrupted thanks to my shield. You, being undead, will have to sub in mind blast here (NOT smite, smite just totally blows in every possible way, unless you have holy damage boost).

I rely HEAVILY on melee damage as its mana-free and saves me any unecessary downtime. In fact, I think this is where a lot of the priests that are having trouble are going wrong--you HAVE to melee as well as cast spells to be the most efficient killing machine possible.

This is usually enough to bring the MOB down as long as it was my level or under. After its dead, my spirit tap kicks in and by the time I find another MOB to kill, my mana is usually almost full or getting close anyway.

I found that if I have to cast more than two additional spells to bring it down, I'm using too much mana and taking too much time to kill. Therefore, I'm being inefficient, and I find something a little weaker to kill. This doesn't necessarily mean that I am killing things under my level exclusively, but the lower levels are especially rough in terms of what you can take down efficiently and reliably.

I must be doing something right as I've managed to stay ahead of all the priests and I'm gaining on some of the slower-leveling hunters/mages/warlocks.

Finally, priests are sort of a slow-building class it seems. What you lack in power in the lower levels, you make up for in the upper levels. My ONLY gripe about the class right now is mind soothe is TOTALLY worthless. I thought it was going to be like pacify, but its pretty much just worthless in all but a limited number of circumstances.

I want to put a disclaimer in here that in order for this to be most effective, you have to STAY AHEAD in terms of equipment. That means a 4dps dagger isn't going to cut it very long, and 3ac cloth tatters aren't going to see you to level 30.

*I forgot to add that my average length of a fight is around 30 seconds from pull to the MOB dying, and this is how its been for the last 20 of my 30 levels. I rarely have to heal after fights nor during them unless I pull multiples. I usually fight my level or up to 2 levels under, although at 30 I usually can take my level or 3 over without much difficulty. Good luck!*

Playing a priest myself. I do mostly grouping, so I haven't bothered w/ shadow spec at all, that would certainly help your solo ability. Going Holy/Disc, soloing isn't terribly fast. I can kill things, but if I get an add, or weren't pretty full to start, it can be rough. Usually pull w/ smite, toss on sw: p, then hit mind blast. Fights normally last 20-30 seconds, less if it's a bit lower level mob, a little more if I pull something 2 levels higher. Hit 33 last night, so things are moving along pretty well so far.

The cannibalize spell looks interesting, but I'm not sure how much I'd use it. On the other hand, desperate prayer absolutely kicks ass, and has saved mine and the groups life at least 5 times so far, generally in instances (deadmines once, blackfathom depths twice, stockades 3 times).
