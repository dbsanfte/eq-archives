Rakyn Vaeltaja
Playing some number game here a bit.

Disclaimer: if anyone finds an error in these calculations and numbers, please correct me; I just tossed these out without giving them much more thought.

Plain vanilla Torpor heals 300hp/tick for 4 ticks, which translates into 50hp/second heal. With AA effects you can extend that to 300hp/tick for 6 ticks, still healing 50hp/second.

Now let's imagine that somewhere out there is a mob that quads for 320 damage, and has about 20 delay between strikes (i.e. 2 seconds). Let's further postulate that on average the mob does 45% of the maximum possible damage on whoever is tanking the thing, which means that in one tick the mob does 1728 damage. Slowed, the mob does about 432 damage per tick, equaling 72 damage per second. With Torpor counteracting some of that damage the net damage taken is 22hp/second. A 5K hitpoint tank will run out of hitpoints in little less than 4 minutes against a mob like that without additional heals. Torpor for that duration takes about 1000 to 1200 mana if you've the proper AA skills and specialize in Alteration, or about 1800 to 2000 mana if you don't have the AA skills.

Let's say that the tank is able to keep the DPS up despite Torpor, and the fight lasts for 5 minutes. You're left with about 6600 hitpoints to heal after keeping Torpor up during the whole fight. That's about 9 castings of Superior Heal with AA and focus effects for total of 2250 mana, or 3000 mana without the AA effects. The grand total mana consumption for healing ranges from 3000 mana to 5000 mana. Mana consumption ranges from 10 to 17 mana/second. Then there's slow, debuff, and so forth, increasing mana consumption even more.

One cast of CanniV will net you 1000 mana with AA enhanced Torpor. It takes a little more than one AA enhanced casting of Torpor to regenerate the lost hitpoints in 36 seconds, so overall with just canniV you'll regen about 15 mana net per tick and might have enough time between canniV blasts to do all your healing and debuffing. Without AA abilities, you'll regen about 10 mana per tick, give or take.

You might be able to be the healer for the tank, but you'd better hope there aren't any resists with slow, and that you won't get aggro at any point :)

Would a new 2K hitpoint heal help? The 10 second casting time is just begging to be interrupted, it seems, and whether the 2K heal for 400 mana will make a significant difference in the above scenario, is to be determined.
