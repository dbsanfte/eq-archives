Have you checked out Scarlet Desert? The Scarlet Cheetahs there are pretty easy to fear/kite if you can find a nice area to pull them to. If you can drag a couple of friends along, you can pull basically anything in the zone. Kinda reminds me of a lvl50-55Dreadlands.
Get to know the zone a bit before you do anything crazy. Some of the tunnels in the walls lead to zone lines, and some lead to caves filled with more mobs. Consider yourself warned.

My favorite spot is along the north wall, near the pillars, one cave west of the cave with the zone to Greigs.

Other places I grouped gettint to 55 were KC, Howling Stones, Chardok, and the Hole entrance.
