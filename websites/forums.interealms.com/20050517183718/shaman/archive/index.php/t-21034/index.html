Only run through if you're nonkos-- or you'll seriously die.

Best to turn off Height Filter with a map to see where you're going.

Entering Arx, you are facing south. Turn left and go forward (east). Go up the stairwell to your left (north). You'll end up in a NE corner (stairwell will continue and go back down to where you came from). Exit the stairwell to the southwest. Take the hallway south all the way to the end, and make a right, entering room through a door to your north. You'll note on your map a secret passageway on your left (west center of room)-- go through and make another left through another door to your south-- enter room and click portal.

This brings you to the maze.

Go:
left (S) at the junction
right (W) at the junction
continue briefly til another junction
right (W) at the junction
left (S) at the junction
right (W) at the junction
right (N) at the junction
left (W) at the junction
continue long way til another junction
left (E) at the junction
continue briefly til another junction
left (E) at the junction
continue long way til the end, click on portal

Basically, if you do it enough, you'll be able to memorize your own path (or just follow the map with height filter off). I just remember:

L,R,L,R,L,R,R,L,L,L,click


This brings you to the central hallway around seru's chamber. Go straight (N) past door (can peek to see if Seru is there if you wanna, he shouldn't agro if you're careful-- even though you're nonkos in the arx, he is likely kos), make a right at the end (E), and the portal is to your left (N, center of hallway) (this is just one big square hallway with chamber in the middle).

This click sends you right to the zonein next to Marus Seru.
