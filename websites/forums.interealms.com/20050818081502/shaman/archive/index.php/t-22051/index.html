Chutoi
Honestly? I'd say pretty hard--though I did it last before they made the changes to content. That first named used to be one of the two hardest encounters in the ikkinz trials. Assuming you have a beefy warrior with good agro proccing weapons and augs, it's doable. Replace one of your dps mobs with a druid. My recommended group would be:

war, clr, dru. ench. shm, dps

Clear around named. One mob is around corner and will add--have ench use an eyeball to target it and (if it's not yellow) you can paci before pull. Alternately, send a pet in after it. The named and the mob will eat the pet in half a second, and charge. Mez/root the non-named and everyone zone. Root/mez should hold until you get back, and you can agro without getting the named.

Shaman are a real hinderance in that encounter. We're poor dps, too low for healing, and high agro if we do start dps. Let the warrior fire repost disc to get solid agro, then go pure dps. I stacked 3 DoTs and almost chained a rain and VS (pause occasionally for crits and the like). Let the druid DS eat the mob up (since it's unslowable). Druid and cleric will have to basically chain heal, and it's a horserace to get it dead before they go OOM.

(Keep in mind, this is pre-change. Not sure how much is different now)

To get the expedition, you south past the first temple (with the smoke pouring out), then follow valley right (west) past the next temple. The path will turn south again, and shortly thereafter, there is another temple on the left hand side of the next intersection. At the NW corner of that T-intersection is a FD leezard monk. Talk to him and follow [ ] script and he'll give you the expedition.

To get in, get lev and SoW up (SoE better). Lev across to the south west wall of the T-intersection, then get high on wall. Go east across road/valley to wall along side of temple. If you have a rogue in group, send him to scout out which of the three entrances it is (left, right, middle). The cleric should BDA the enchanter and DA himself, and everyone run for the entrances with SoE. You can lev over most of the monsters, and, hopefully, either make it in by following the DA folks (as mobs will chase them), or at least die close enough that your rogue can drag you in, or with the new expanded /corpse circle, someone can drag you from the ramp and not agro the mobs. I don't recommend trying to clear, there are a TON of mobs there to get into the temple.

Remember to warn in /ooc about trains to the temple. /ooc TRAIN LEFT IN IKKINZ 1, WARN YOUR GUILDS That way people don't zone out into a train if they have a pull going badly in another trial.
