molgar
I found soloing RM to be too slow to be worth it.

If you have a melee to partner with it may be better.

If your melee partner is 49+ or so and you are very lucky, or start at a non-peak time, you can grab two of the static Ice Giant spawns in Everfrost. They drop more platinum than the RM hill giants, and drop FS/gems/research parts most of the time.

And don't underestimate the income value of SoW potions. Once your Alchemy skill is up to 54 or better you have a 95% chance of success; 10 10-dose potions usually cost me about 600pp to make. The key is to find the right zone to sell in. DL is one of the best, as people are about the right level to (a) still need it because they don't have AA run yet, and (b) have enough pp to shell out. On a good day I can make 500pp in pure profit for zero work while I'm hunting for exp or pp. Most days it's more like 200pp, but still definitely worth running to the nearest friendly alchemy shop every couple days.
