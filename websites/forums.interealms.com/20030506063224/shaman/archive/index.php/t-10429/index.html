I would suggest that you just do the faction if you are that worried about getting your epic asap. I know a few people that have done the faction bit for epic recently and I can confirm that it still works that way. Easiest part of the faction is to kill the dudes in RM and turn the item in to the wandering spirit in WK. Then get to Mistmore and run in, kill adviser, and run back out to kill Dire. I did this a few times at 60, as well as helped a few people out doing this at the same lvl, and every time, we were able to get into and out of Mistmore castle in about 10 min tops. Heck, the ranger himself got to the Adviser and back out in about 5 min while it took me a bit longer since dog dog has severe pathing problems in that area (should have seen the train once after he wandered off into the basement and upon death brought that whole area on top of me).

Basically, for cheap nowadays you can get good equipment for really cheap prices. Check around a bit and keep track of what's being sold and for how much, and then notice if there is a sudden price drop in something. I'm seeing those player made Gallantry Rings now for sale for around 10k a piece sometimes, down from when they were about 30k when they were first introduced.
