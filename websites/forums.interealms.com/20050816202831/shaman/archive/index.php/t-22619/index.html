Chutoi
Actually, while I like the idea . . . I gotta nay say.

The whole problem with slow (and why they had to mitigate it) was that they were having to design encounters with slow in mind. Either they made the mob immune, or had to beef it up something silly so that it was the appropriate difficulty after slow (cripple in this scenario). This spell is more of the same, I'm afraid.

If we have a new cripple line, then mobs just have to be beefed up that much more--and mobs will HAVE to be crippled to succeed, etc. It's a vicious cycle. Fundamentally, without breaking all their encounters (and previous encounters), all they can really do is fix healing, dps, ability to tank, or crowd control/utility. Those are really the only functions they can afford to change without really hosing up all the encounters.

Supposedly, developers are looking at 3 of the 4.
