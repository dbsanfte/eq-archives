veeshanMurtak
Given how fast the fights are over in GoD, I'd think it better to land an early slow for medium % than a late slow for high %.
If you talking single group content (or anything a single cleric can heal on) here neither will be worth it. Even if you completely ignore aggro it will only barely be worth casting the spell.

Very rough numbers:
(assuming a 20 second fight, 600 DPS)

malos+turgur's lands after say 9 seconds, gets mitigated down to 50ish %. Damage prevented: 3300, call it 3K with resists. Damage healable with Daluda's for the same mana: 2800ish. Damage healable by a cleric for 3/4 of the mana: 5800ish.

malos on incoming+turgur's lands after 5 seconds to avoid aggro, gets mitigated down to 50ish %. Damage prevented: 4500, say 4K with resists. Damage healable with Daluda's for the same mana: 2800ish. Damage healable by a cleric for 3/4 of the mana: 5800ish.

malos+turgur's clickie: lands after 7 seconds, gets mitigated down to 50ish %. Damage prevented: 3900, a little less with resists. Damage healable with Daluda's for the same mana: 1700ish. Damage healable by a cleric for 3/4 of the mana: 3500ish.

malos on incoming+turgur's clickie: lands after 3 seconds, gets mitigated down to 50ish %. Damage prevented: 5Kish. Damage healable with Daluda's for the same mana: 1700ish. Damage healable by a cleric for 3/4 of the mana: 3500ish.

50% neg 75 slow: casting it so it lands after 5 seconds to avoid aggro, mitigated down to 33%, damage prevented: 3K, assuming no resists. Damage healable with Daluda's for the same mana: 1300ish. Damage healable by a cleric for 3/4 of the mana: 2700ish.

50% neg 75 slow: casting it so it lands after 2 seconds, mitigated down to 33%, damage prevented: 3.6K, assuming no resists. Damage healable with Daluda's for the same mana: 1300ish. Damage healable by a cleric for 3/4 of the mana: 2700ish.

Conclusions:
Neither spell is better then using TA+malos. A 50% slow that gets mitigated is only useful because it costs less mana - the prevented damage is just as unimpressive as malos+turgur's. A second cleric is still preferable seeing as the prevented damage is pretty similar just assuming the cleric blindly CHs and the shaman finds time to cannibalize.

If slow is to be useful in GoD we either need a neg 60 resist adjustment 75% slow with a 1 second cast and low aggro (say, no counters at all). Or fights need to last longer.

I would prefer the second option myself.
