Chutoi
I'm surprised no one mentioned BLs. Beastlords have some nice mana regen buffs, and most guilds have at most one or two. BLs are usually desired.

Clerics--well, duh. You always need clerics.

Wizards--good on raids, but few people seem to want to group them. Prepare to solo a lot.

Chanters--well, lots of em out there. Anyone can make a chanter, but playing a chanter well is a different question. It's really not that easy a class to play well. I have had chanters I just told to nuke and KEI. I am better at crowd control than a lot of em. :/

Rangers--rangers have a lot of really nice abilities, but most require a lot of AA. One you get a high AA ranger, he's a NICE addition. Bows can do major damage (if ranged attacks are an option for the mob in question as pinning mobs on walls can block arrows). Though it almost always requires a suicide by the ranger, weaponshied is vital to helping pull and position mobs properly (level 60 ability). I LOVE having rangers that can do it.

Bards--there are never enough high level bards. Resists, mana song, AC and damage shields on the MT (for certain really uber mobs). A lot of raids are just not possible without a high level bard, and there aren't that many of em.
