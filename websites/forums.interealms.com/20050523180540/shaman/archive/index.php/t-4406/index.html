Dire is pretty much poison/disease resistant. His mr isnt too impressive though.

Just clear out the immediate area around him. Druid harmony him (to ensure you dont agro his pets).

Druid pulls with snare and gets HT for 500ish. You stick malosi on him asap. Druid roots him down and applies WD/epic/drones and whatnot.

Get a slow on him once you get malosi to stick.

If he resists you, just keep the druid alive while you watch dire rot away.

He doesnt summon so shouldnt be a prob.
