At lvl 35, I hunted Cynthia in Rathe Mtns, Shes pretty decent exp, i think about half 1/3 a blue per whack, she spawns pretty fast though so try to keep at least half mana at the end of the fight,

The strategy i used was ShmvsWiz3: Open with Scrounge, Pet goes in(full buffed) Nuke, Canna, Nuke, repeat nuking until dead, btw she throws some mean punches for a wizie, i think i remember seeing a double or so for 80 somewhere in the fight, unless your careful she will kill your pet in melee.

A 2nd good place is the Tundra Yeti's in Dreadlands, I got bored with cyncthia and the cyclopses of Rathe Mtns at 1 point and wandered over here to check the yeti's out. The are pretty good exp, the pull is a little tricky, can't run to far away from them or they turn and bring their buddies.

Strategy TankKite1: Full buff yourself and pet, med to good mana, 4 or 5 works, open with slow, slowly back up (taking hits along the way, belive me its worth it so they dont get lost) until you reach the cave entrance (where you conviently sat your doggie down.) let pet engage, Poison, Disease, retreat for 4 seconds(back off a bit, until you clear high agro range) sit and rest until your pet is starting to take a hurting (hes down to 1/3 hp, ) then either heal him and take the long route with him finishing off the yeti after you redot'd it, or nuke it to death, personally i take the long way and genneraly end up at 4.5 or 4 mana with my pet well over 4/5 hp. so its continous pulling.
