Well... Im pretty sure my guild would not appreciate my doing a tear MQ at this time. Since we currently have members who are still in need of a childs tear, its a pretty safe bet that i can not help you in that way. However, i can tell you what i did to obtain my Iksar Childs tear.

Go to the Fear plane, go ALOT. Go to Fear some more, with friends, pickup raids, or guildraids whenever you can. Then go some more still, and hope the named are up. When the named are eventualy up and you take down all the golems realize that only 2 of the 3 Golems can spawn an Iksar Broodling. Warn the kill happy melees ahead of time not to kill the Iksar Broodling just because.

(This actualy happened on one raid that i was on, during a bad pull that happened while there were 7+ mobs in camp and in comes one of the named Golems, a melee killed the Iksar Broodling that popped after the golem death with alot of dead players around, and the tear rotted while the Shaman were being rezzed - pickup raids, just say no)

Wait for a few other Shaman in front of you to get their tears as well. After a bit of this, through either blind luck or sheer perseverence and determination you will eventualy receive your tear, probably much sooner than me too, unless you happen to be extremely unlucky.

Eventualy i learned to check the rotation calender after making a handfull of trips to Fear and finding out that none of the named were up once it was broken.

Try these links for the Rathe server:

http://www.jakefitch.com/rta/rotations.asp

That should help give you a better idea who is doing Fear, and when. I wish you the best of luck in your endeavor.
