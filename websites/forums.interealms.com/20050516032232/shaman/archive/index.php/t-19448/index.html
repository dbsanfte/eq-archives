CA is 2%/3%/5% so totally adding up to10% added avoidance, LR adds 1% per rank, so in total of 15% added avoidance.
In terms of avoidance per aa:
CA1: 2%/2aa ~ 1%/aa
ca2: 3%/4aa ~ 0.75%/aa
ca3: 5%/6aa ~ 0.83%/aa

LR: 1%/3aa ~ 0.33%/aa

This is the same gained in terms of mitigation for CS/ID with the same gains per aa spent.
