Baz3
Nathyn's guards are the only npc's in Katta who are not already KOS to you.

I recommend doing the short guards in the tunnels near Twilight Sea zone in. At 55 getting singles will be a good thing, and these guards aggron on a line of sight. The layout there makes it easy to get them one at a time, if you keep an eye on the timer for the two roamers to repop.

At 55 you can still root and rot two (doggie will probably die), but get a third and i suggest a run to the zone, which is not far.

zone in from twilight, invis up, and go right. when you get to the end of the walkway, about to enter the tunnel, make your camp.

running in you will probably see the human roamer, who starts at zone in and roams to your camp.

inside the tummel are two guards to your right - ease up so you can see only one, and pull with slow. The other roamer pops right there, also, so it helps if he is there to be pulled first.

past those two static guards there is a room to your left with Berk Thurstone. same as the guards.

past Berk the tunnel turns left - you can see a pool in the tunnel. Tack and Rowl Shieldstone are on either side of the pool If you have the first few spawns under control, you can pull these guys the same way as the first pair, by getting one in line of sight.

My apologies to whoever first posted this information - I have hunted this spot from 56 through 59 with great exp and nice plat, following the guide posted on this forum.
