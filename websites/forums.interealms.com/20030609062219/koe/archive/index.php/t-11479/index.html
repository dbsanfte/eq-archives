Toddoth
There is no faction of concern in PoJ. For Katta, there are several factions. Including two guard factions. The majority of the guards in Katta are on one faction. However, the guards in Nathyn's mansion and court yard are of their own faction. So, killing them will not interfer with movement in the rest of the city.
