Another option is killing gobs in Warslike woods for the green goblin skin (drops off gGob Soothsayers, or gob Agressors, best located at Gob Fort, but usualy camped due to Grach/shrunken goblin skull earring farmers).

Unless VI nerf'd it, you can:
(1)-kill in Droga for 1 faction increase per kill;
(2)-turn in 1 green goblin skin (to the quest giver Hearld NPC in Chardok) for 3 faction increase;
or (3)-turn in those salts 2 at a time for like 1-2 faction increase (not sure exact amout of faction given on salts).

Hard part about the green goblin skill, is it isn't very common, so you can clear the gob fort many times without any, so it gets slow, unless you are camping Grachnist the Destroyer. So may be quicker to just kill in Droga as you'll have a steady flow of mobs.
