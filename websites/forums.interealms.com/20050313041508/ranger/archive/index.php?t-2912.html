I have 2 b0xed with a shaman from lvl 15-34 on both. I can say that I still take down reds and yellow with my 2 toons without breaking much of a sweat. However, I have broken down this argument several times. If you have a second account and PC, count your blessings and know that any choice is good (I ran into a guy a few weeks ago that 2 boxes rangers on both accounts, he does fine)

Cleric comparison
WHoever said shaman heals are not that great has never played a shaman. at 29 shaman get greater heal (270hp) and a couple other upgrades down the road. Cleric is better on heals, but throw in a shaman regen that gives 470hp in 4 minutes (also improves down the road) and hp is rarely a problem. when 2 boxing with a shaman ven if the heals are not as good as cleric

Shaman DOT are much better than clerics and DD's are comperable. So your real advantage to a cleric is that they have slightly improved heals until mid 30's when they really pull away, and they have rez. funny thing is that when I 2box with my shaman, I never need a rez.

Buffs, no one will argue that any class has better buffs than a shaman. and that is very important. I broke 1khp fully buffed at lvl 29 and never looked back. Another important factor here is that shaman buffs stack very well with ranger buffs. In fact, I have no stacking issues at all until mid 40's. Not true for cleric and chanter.

Chanter comparison
Chanters are great, they change the way you play eq and they can be alot of fun, they are a great partner for a ranger, but your down time spent healing sux. You get haste and slows faster with a chanter than a shaman, but they are the same spells so you will eventually see them with either class. On a side note you get stacking issues with alacrity and ranger buffs real fast.

You do get mez which is a huge help, best equivalent with the shaman is double root capability for crowd control. Mana regen, again chanter wins out big time, but shaman gets limited mana regen later on too. So in summary using a chanter you will trade downtime, instead of waiting for mana recovery you will be waiting for HP recovery, personally I would prefer my downtime to be waiting for mana, safer. Mez and DD spells are the big advantages of the chanter, mana recovery is nice, but you are trading it for the heals, and that is not a good trade in my opinion.

Other issues: Shaman classes can take much more damage that a chanter and probably more than a cleric. This doesnt seem important, but when 2boxing you will run into situations where the caster will get beaten on for a few minutes either before you notice or before you can do anything about it.

Shaman melee output is greater than other options.

Shaman pet offers a great DOT and damage aborbtion when needed, other classes cant offer the pet buffer.

Using mez when 2 boxing is difficult because your biggest enemy is aggro on the caster toon. fleeing with one toon while trying to engage with the other is the hardest thing to do when 2 boxing.

You will be happy with any choice and you may even want to try some of the less common combos, I imagine that a rogue ranger combo would be a terrible thing to encounter, I also think a necro/ranger combo would potent, but difficult. and if I couldn't do a shaman I would try a pally I get rez, I get heal, and I get decent damage output. So do what appeals to you.
