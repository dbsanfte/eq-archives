Khas,

I cant help you with your first question, since I am an offense oriented archer-rangerette, but I can offer advice on the 2nd one.

Based on the zone access you have I would recommend an alternative route to experiencing. Endless quiver would be ideal for this, but once you have that its a great alternative to melee 3boxing.

Aggro kite in HoH. Basements are all good, zonein and hero temples are good too. Aggro kiting has some advantages:

1. You can specialize in being an offensive ranger that will make you more effective on raids. You can focus on archery and SCF to increase your raid dps.

2. Mobs in HoH dont summon so kiting is easy.

3. Most of your cleric/shaman's mana can be used to nuke and kill faster instead of healing/slowing for you. Keep shaman and cleric on a horse for more mana.

4. In HoH if you rootpark human guards til slowed, you will be able to tank them easily without needing CA3-LR. So you can work on getting EQ/AM3 and then start aggro kiting.

==========
Try the following strategy and see if you like it:

Go to HoH, set up in one of the trial basements in a human guard room. Summon a bunch of arrows just to test 1 round with archery kiting. Harmony split 1 guard. Snare, flame lick 1-2 times.

Send shaman pet and cleric pet in. DoT stack with shaman, Cond/SoP with cleric. Flame lick/snare generates insane aggro, so casting it a few times a minute you'll keep aggro on you easily.

Kiting with Frozen Wind, Brushfire will let you generate a decent dps on top of what the shm/clr/pets are doing. If you kite on a horse you will have more mana too Once you get AM3/EQ archery will add a substantial dps on top.

Time your test run and see how fast you killed the mob and how much mana you have left on your toons. Compare it to a melee round on another mob. I think you'll be suprised.

And remember, offensive AAs are FUN. Defensive AA are booooring. :p
