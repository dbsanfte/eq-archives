Zhorn
I'd go for Tae Ew as at 61 you can easily solo CT entrance lizards til they drop a Tae Ew Blood then go solo Windscorn Boat Iksar in Dulak and get an Iksar Blood (they do undercon a bit though...).

Then you just need to find a smith who will do a shield combine for 4kpp (or free if you know a GM smith).

I am charging 4 or 5kpp for successful Tae Ew Shield combines atm. There is 70 to 80% percent chance for success at max mod skill of 252 smithing. Other servers smiths may charge more or less depending on the retail value of the item (15kpp on my server).

Maybe get two of each bloods just to be safe. You also need some easy to get bloods (1 Froglok, 1 Sarnak from bazaar) and 1 Bloodwater from the vendor opposite the Fungus Grove bank.

Edit: BTW, by the time you get cana 5 (which is the first class skill most/all shaman should go for) you are going to want a BUTTLOAD more Hitpoints. Either that or always exp with an Aego/Virtue/etc!
