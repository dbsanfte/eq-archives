I just recently "saw the light" of weapon skills other than piercing.

With my Slime Coated Harpoon I was hitting for 20's max. When I switched to a Dark Ember (1HB) I now average 25-27 damage per swing (I max at 30). My 1HB is in the 90's right now, so I still miss a Fair amount but it is definitely worthwhile. Make sure you are hasted when you are swinging :) Of course, mathematically I'm not sure if the slower 1HB is equal to the faster Piercer but it sure is fun to smack things around!!

Once I get my 1HB up to a respectable level, I plan on working 2HB.

If you are working on Defense, consider fighting a "soft" mob that is unslowed. More hits on you = more chances to raise defense. Personally, I've never had a problem keeping defense maxed as I usually solo after I gain a new level until my melee skills are maxed.

Baz3
Yeah, one hand bash is worth keeping up, but dont expect to out melee even a slowed mob - not as you go up in levels.

2 hb was once the big deal for shaman, as the piercers stunk for both wis and damage. Kunark changed that, and the epic sealed the deal. There are some real good 2hb for damage, but you give up wis and ac in the shield slot (and hp, most likely).

The thing to remember is, that it is much easier to get weapon skills (and defense/offense) up as you level. Trying to catch them up later is tough - you just dont take enough swings to get the increases, and increases are much harder to come by afte 100 anyway.
