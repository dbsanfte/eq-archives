Zyphyr
In theory, you can get armor drops anywhere in the zone. In practice, the farther from the zoneline you get the better your odds.

At the WL zone I have have seen Leather Boots (once) and Leather Bracers (several). That is it, though I have every reason to believe that more than that is possible.

From the static mobs in the Bank area I have seen, in the last month or so (I solo there with my Mage quite often - tolerable XP, fixes CoV hits I take raiding, and Armor drops are a nice bonus):
Chain BP
Chain Legs
Chain Bracer
Silk Robe
Silk Bracer (x3)
Leather Cap
Leather Legs
Plate Legs

I have only seen armor drops in that area from the static Lietenants. They spawn around 1 time in 10 in the place of the 'watchman' type spawns (of which there are 6) and drop armor pieces around 1 in 8. The rest of the time they generally drop Giant Scalemail.

I do not believe that area to be soloable by a 52 shaman (especially one who is poorly equiped), but if you can dig up a couple friends it should be doable. Or there might be a group there willing to let you join.
