shamuofxev
In the long run it doesn't matter - I had sta at 280 with a Wis at 220 for the longest time (and that as a barb with high starting wis and lower sta). A bit of reshuffling of equipment took care of it.
As an ogre you start us with very high sta to begin with. I'd put all points you can into wis, since at low levels that gives a decent return manawise. Sta/wis will come pretty much equally on nice pieces of equipment, so you can catch up on sta later.
But to put it into relation, wearing a glittering ring of judgement over a valorium ring at level 60 makes up for all the wis you might not put in now.

Eilderts
I would say max into wis then either stam or agil. I always do stam but it's your choice.

There is no wrong way. If you want the best possible shaman and work for it hard, you WILL hit the AC soft cap and have 250+ wis. I would go with wis, simply because you will be able to switch out some of your wis gear for hp/mana gear once you hit the cap.

Agil/stam isn't that big of a deal. Sure they're nice, but they have a lot less effect on us than they do most classes. Not only are we NOT melee's, we get MAJOR stam and agil buffs. So I wouldn't worry about it too much. If it's killing you then just level up and buy your next buffs :P.
