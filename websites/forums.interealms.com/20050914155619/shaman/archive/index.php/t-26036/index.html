pulled this from the old Haven Light forums under the app section. figured since everyone was talking about pets it may enlighten those of you that didn't understand how pet hold works =)

Section 3 - You mean I don’t have to spam /pet back off?

Most classes that can use a pet have an Alternate Ability which they can get at level 59 called Pet Discipline. This ability allows you to better control your pet. On raids, pet control or lack of it can lead to a raid not succeeding. I highly recommend getting this Alternate Ability as soon as you can. This Alternate Ability is required for magicians and necromancers. To use this ability you need to type /pet hold using your keyboard. This will cause your pet not attack anything unless specifically told to. It is important to realize that mobs that attacked you while your pet was placed on hold are still on the argo list of your pet unless you clear that list by typing /pet back off. If you did not type in /pet back off, your pet would attack the next target you told it to attack and then go back to the mobs that where on its argo list. Not backing your pet off will likely cause a train and cause unneeded down time. Please remember that spamming /pet back off with out first placing it on hold only tells your pet to stop attacking anything on its argo list, but the very next time you are attacked or are hit by a spell, the pet will go right back to what attacked you. This includes area of effect spells. I would also recommend making a social with /pet hold on one line and /pet back off on the next. This will save time in placing your pet on hold and you will also have the argo list on your pet cleared. To determine what you should be attacking you should be using /assist and HoTT. Ideally, in between every pull you will use your social with /pet hold to keep your pet from argoing the mob that is being pulled before the Main Tank can engage it and build argo. A good rule of thumb on boss type mobs on raids is to wait approximately 10 seconds after the mob is placed and engaged by the Main Tank to send in your pet unless specifically told to wait longer.
