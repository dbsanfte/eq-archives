Juujuu
The process of equiping yourself with really high end gear is much slower than at lower levels. At lower levels, the speed at which you can get new gear is just basicly limited by how much PP you can make. And considering the way mudflation works, you can get some really amazing gear now for incredibly cheap that used to go for a fortune.

Once you can no longer buy any gear upgrades with PP, that's when your equipment upgrade process really begins to slow down. You have to start raiding to get equipment upgrades. Raiding to acquire items isn't a fast process to get outfitted with new gear. If you are in a guild of say 60 people, and you kill whatever targets that drop 3 pieces of gear each kill, you have to kill 20 targets on average before you can expect to win a piece of gear. Factor in things like competition with other guilds and spawn times of targets. If you manage to kill 4 raid targets every week, you are looking at 5 full weeks of raiding things before you can expect to win a single piece of new equipment. If you don't raid that much, it'll be longer. If you raid a LOT as a guild, you might be able to see some new gear more often. But in the end, raiding to get new gear isn't fast at all for upgrading equipment.
