There's nothing wrong with how you distributed your starting points: Charisma would have been wasted. Some people might argue for putting more points into Stamina, and less into Wisdom, but it really doesn't make much difference.

At your level, there really aren't any great tricks or tactics for the Shaman. Until around Level 24 (Cannibalize, Regeneration, and big poison DoT upgrade), or even more so Level 34 (pet), it can often be frustrating to solo, unless you have good equipment, as the class plays sort of like a weak hybrid. Obviously, SoW is the best spell you currently have, and Tainted Breath is a decent DoT at Level 10. One often overlooked spell is Inner Fire: not only does it buff but it can also be used as a heal, with a better mana/health ratio than even our Level 19 healing spell. Outside of fights, cast it between ticks (if you're fast, it can be squeezed in) to heal. Drowsy helps against tough MOBs. Stat buffs aren't too helpful at your level, and probably a waste of mana except for possibly Dexterity (raising weapon skills).
