That was what you thought it would be, not what it actually would be ;). I think for GoD mobs it probably would have been higher than 3-4%.

For those who don't know how it works, the damage modifier works on a weapons base damage, and not on the damage bonus. So for most weapons a max hit would result in a 8-9% increase in total damage, and for a min hit it would result in a 0% increase in total damage. The effectiveness of the mod would depend on your average hit. As we all know, in modern content mobs tend to have low AC, particularly xp mobs, so you would get decent returns on the buff.

The beauty of the old form of Champion is that it had both atk and damage modifier components. The damage modifier should have its biggest effect when mob AC is low (ie your average hit is high), while atk should have its biggest impact when mob AC is high (ie your average hit is low). As a result the buff could be expected to give a fairly uniform performance across all mob types except for those at extreme high/low AC values. It was a major advance over FA, which is limited to high AC mobs in terms of usefullness. Its a real shame that they dropped it for the current piece of garbage.
