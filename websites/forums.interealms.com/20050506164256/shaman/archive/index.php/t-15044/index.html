That's the idea, each spell has a flag for whether or not it is "Deletable" from your spellbook. Lucy (http://lucy.fnord.net) shows it. This feature was added in the patch of Sept 4 2002 -- when the spellbook only had 250 slots and they were about to introduce tons of new spells with PoP.

But I notice quite a few of them are flagged incorrectly -- dropped spells that should not be deletable but are, and vice versa. Makes you wonder how Sony can be so confident about things like spell drop rates when they can't even figure out which of their spells are vendor sold. Some examples: Focus of Spirit, Khura's Focusing, Cannibalize II :eek: are deletable.
