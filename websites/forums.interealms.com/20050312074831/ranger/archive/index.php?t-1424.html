IF you have a tolans bracer and make the thurg gate potion....

Bear pits in Perma. At 58 you can solo pull them every time. Dire wolves and bears are animals (fear kite) and will be almost all dark blue to you up to 60. Spiders are NOT animals and you will most likely have to shoot them full of a couple hundred arrows to effectively kill them without buying so much downtime as to be silly.

The camp isn't TOO hard to get down, and with the XP modifier in that zone, you'll zoom up to 60 (and through the AA pts). I'm still getting good xps there. It's a bit of a pain to get to at times, and it takes a friend cleric to come rez you if things go wrong (and they will eventually), but if you bind in Halas and just gate there during down time, you can pick up plenty of xps on those days when groups just DON'T happen.

Again, great xps. Downsides include awful loot, lonely (if such bothers you -- if you do this with more than 2 people it's really not worth it..the xps just drop off), hard to get rezzed, and a pain to get out (unless voxy is up ... she'll boot you outta zone every time if she is, just run to her lair). Personally, I love it. Been told that I should petition to get one of the barbarian bear hats from all the time I spend down there.

Just a few warnings about Permafrost I'd like to add to save some people a lot of heartache.

Once you drop into the pit, there are only three possible ways to get out, gate/port/TL, Vox boot, or exit through Vox's lair back to elite goblin room.

Do NOT drop into Vox's room if she is not there. You will immediately be jumped on by several Ice Giants and a Priest of Nagafen. In fact, don't even go up the ladder leading out of the pit area because the only thing up there are two Icy Terrors guarding Vox's room which are a pain in the rump.

Also, if you die in the pit, getting back to your corpse can be difficult since many of the IGs will see through camo. Sneak might hold long enough to get around the corner to the pit hallways, but you may end up dying more than once.

If you are unfortunate enough to have died in Vox's room then the real screwing begins. The two Icy Terrors guarding the entrance see right through camo as do the giants waiting just inside where your lifeless corpse will be. To get to it would require dropping once again and being aggroed by the party of giants waiting below who will also see through camo as well as rogue sneak/hide. You'd better have a necro handy.

Perma can be good solo exp, just be warned not to go exploring beyond the pit. The only emergency evacs for a lone Ranger are a Vox boot or Weapon Shield/Thurg Potion. If those fail, expect exp loss and a challenging CR.
