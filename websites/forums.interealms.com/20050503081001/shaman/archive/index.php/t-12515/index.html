Gloriana
With PoP, everyone is able to access the following zones without any sort of key/flag/quest:

PoK - plane of knowledge
PoT - plane of tranquility

PoJ - plane of justice - to access, zone into PoT and run straight out from the building, up and to the left you will see a huge, golden scale on the wall, click the scale to zone in

PoN - plane of nighmare - this one is complicated to describe, zone in from POT and go out of the building and left until you hit the water. Follow the water until you reach a clump of buildings, then you have to wind your way through the buildings until you see a low, wooden sculpture, click it to zone in (get a guide the first time you try to find it)

PoD - plane of disease - zone in from POT, leave the building and go straight and to the right, there is a big, brown, nasty looking, fountain looking thing, jump in to zone

PoI - plane of innovation - as if you were going to PoN, follow the water until you see a huge windmill, go in the windmill and go to the back. From the back, look forward and there are two huge gears, one is moving and one is still, click the still one to zone in.

NOTE: each one of these zones has a "graveyard" located close to the zone point. If your corpse isn't rezzed within X time, your corpse automatically drops at the graveyard, making CR's MUCH easier. In the first tier, that time is either 10 or 15 mins.

Oh, forgot to meantion, that if your corpse pops in the GY, nobody can /corpse it out of there, even you.... So you have to look for a cleric at the GY or one that is willing to run to it.
