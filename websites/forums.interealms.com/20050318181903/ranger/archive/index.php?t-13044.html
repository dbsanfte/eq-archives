Well eventually it might get like that Mirklin but that will be down the road a bit. First the rarity of the spell would need to be built in. I know for just value wise for a raid the shaman, cleric and pure caster spells (the good ones) would be far more valueable than the 2 ranger spells.

If you think about it the pure casters, shaman, cleric etc get completely new spells at 65 so when they turn their shadowed parchment in they have a random chance at 5 or so spells. Verant might have built in a rare spell to come out of that so in fact Group Virtue could be the rare spell for cleric, for example, and so all the other 65 spells would become slightly common since clerics would keep on turning in shadowed parchments in search of Group Virtue. If they keep getting spells they already have then those get distributed into guild and then flow into the public once the guild is full of that spell.

The ranger 65 unique spells are limited to just 2. Once you turn in a few shadowed parchments you most likely will get your 2 spells and have no need to roll on shadowed parchments from that point on. The extras you might have gotten would most likely be distributed within guild. Only if the rangers in the guild keep getting unlucky and get Cry of Thunder or PotW over and and over will extra spells filter down to the public.

62-64 level spells are a bit of a different story since, as I have been told, the parchments can be obtained by pickup groups that most likely would roll on any parchment that dropped.

This is of course limited to just the people who will probably never see the mobs that drop the shadowed parchments. If they do only drop off of the very high end stuff then the casual gamer will be forced to buy his/her spells. It is only these people that this thread really is directed to.
