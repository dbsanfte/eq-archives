It has seemed that way to me as well. To help you find an answer to the ornate situation, here is a bit of info on why there seems to be a lack. Ornate gloves are the common drop from the mini lightning name in my experience. There are two mobs that drop the ornate chain tunic outside of Sol Ro, which is a good thing for shamans. The reason why you may not be seeing the chain arms on your server is that many don't know where the named is at in BoT that drops them. To be honest, I am not quite sure myself, but I believe it is down one of the hallways near backsand. The arms tend to be common as well, so once the server figures out where they drop your bazaar should be full of them. Outside of Sol Ro, there is only one mob that comes to mind that drops chain legs, and I believe it is Oreen in Backwater. From my experience he doesn't like to drop anything let alone an Ornate Chain Legging Pattern.
Not sure where the boots drop, but they are becoming more common on my server. I chose to pass on them due to my secondary having a partial mana preservation focus.

I have had more of a hassle with focus effects. It seems any focus effect that is available to me outside a raid is stuck on a lousy piece of gear that is either ridiculously rare or ridiculously expensive. To top it off the other stats on said piece of gear make it a lousy trade-off. Some items that come to mind are the IH4 backslot item from Chardokb, just about any HoH zone dropped focus items, the rarity of EE4 items outside of ceramics, and quite a few others. I am having a heck of a time upgrading my Ornate arms without losing IH4. The easiest way to do it is to buy a Ceramic Totem of Rathe, but that eats up my range slot where there are plenty of nice level 2 focus item quests that could fill the same slot. LDoN helped us out a bit by giving us a chance at purchasable SH4 and BA4 augments, but they are type 3, so if you want a nice piece of gear with one you will need to fork out about 2600 adv points. GoD looks promising with a hefty amount of new 1-groupable focus items, but progress will be slow for a bit as people gather up drop info.
