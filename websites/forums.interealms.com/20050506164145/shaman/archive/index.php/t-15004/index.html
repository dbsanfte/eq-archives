for things that you cannot climb with levitate...

get 2 shields.
drop one on the ground.
drop the other on the ground in the exact same spot. (they should 'sit' on top of one another. works only with a couple kinds)
climb up onto them.
look straight down and carefully pick up the bottom shield.
drop it.
repeat.

you will 'climb' and as long as you don't fall off, you can get on top of almost anything. do this process as close as possible to whatever object you want to climb. when you get above it, levi yourself and walk over.

shields that I know work are from nathan's guards in katta. they're lore, so take a friend to go get a couple.

Enjoy.
