Sticking this in a new thread plus clarifying it so it doesn't get buried. Stickying this would be appreciated, but I guess it boils down to how much the community cares, personally I think it would be worthwhile, it gives our self buff lines a much needed upgrade and combining them into one whilst fixing a pair of annoyances (Rabid bears usefulness and our melee abilities) that many shaman share.

I think everyone pretty much agrees that Rabid Bear is now obsolete except for farming greens, it was a useful ability when xp spots like Sebilis/FG/Akr were popular, it is completely useless in the PoP environment however, the massively increased defensive skills of the majority of mobs mean its useless for xp grinding, whilst the aoes of boss encounters mean its borderline suicide to use it in the majority of encounters. And of course Hastened Rabidity is pretty much viewed as an insult by many shaman.

Henceforth after some discussion I propose this revamp to Rabid bear/Hastened rabidity.

Basic rabid bear (Lvl 0) - 5 AA

Increase ATK by 200
Increase Attack Speed by 60%
Increase STR by 100
Increase AGI by 100
Increase DEX by 100
+ 50dd proc
+ Double atk skill of 100

Enhanced Rabidity (Lvl 1) - 2 AA

Increase ATK by 230
Increase Attack Speed by 60%
Increase AC by 10
Increase STR by 120
Increase AGI by 120
Increase DEX by 120
Increase All resists by 3
Increase WIS by 10
Increase HP regen by 2
+ 60dd proc
+ Double atk skill of 120

Enhanced Rabidity (Lvl 2) - 4 AA

Increase ATK by 260
Increase Attack Speed by 60%
Increase Overhaste by 5%
Increase AC by 20
Increase STR by 140
Increase AGI by 140
Increase DEX by 140
Increase All resists by 6
Increase WIS by 15
Increase HP regen by 3
Increase Mana regen by 1
+ 70dd proc (With a proc rate similar to 56 summoned cleric hammer)
+ Double atk skill of 140

Enhanced Rabidity (Lvl 3) - 6 AA

Increase ATK by 300
Increase Attack Speed by 60%
Increase Overhaste by 10%
Increase AC by 30
Increase STR by 160
Increase AGI by 160
Increase DEX by 160
Increase All resists by 10
Increase WIS by 20
Increase HP regen by 4
Increase Mana regen by 2
+ 80dd proc (With a proc rate similar to 60 summoned cleric hammer)
+ Double atk skill of 160
+ Slam skill of 50

*Changes = Full time +50 CR is likely unbalancing, so thats gone. The attack is lowered also, it won't make much of a difference but it cuts down on the 'omgs so much attack' whinge likelyhood. Double attack and haste element lowered to limit the potential dps against low ac mobs to a more reasonable level.

The innate proc should be a disease based DD (NO dot element) with NO resist adjustment (IE it lands with the same frequency as BoUltor), this gives more use to Malicious decay/insid malady so we can get the most out of this ability. The reasoning for this is that if we can land poison fine, we are more likely to use BoSaryn/Nife, hence it would be an alternative source of damage when our poison dots and cold nukes simply get resisted too much.

The slam skill level is quite low, but that is to prevent it from being too effective with such a large atk element. It appears to be possible to have a buff give the player a buttoned skill (Ogre/troll/barb illusion grants the player Slam, even if they don't have it in there normal form), although how flexible this is is unknown.

It should stack in much the same way as Rabid bear, but also block voice of the berserker and form of the bear/greater bear/other illusions (To prevent getting around the size issue of being a bear, this would be a fair limitation of using the buff imho).

Its base duration should mirror the mage elemental form line with a 36 min duration and 15 minute recast time on the ability.

Visually 1 of 2 approaches is best, if global model load is an issue, simply replace the existing old bear model with the bear from PoStorms, the PoS bear is MORE complete than the old world bear as it also includes casting animations. Otherwise as suggested, if global model space is no longer an issue, perhaps using the Velious kodiak would be more fitting?

All in all, the buff is basically a combination upgrade of Voice of the berserker, form of the great bear and Rabid bear all rolled into one and brought into line in a way that would actually make it worth casting in the pop environment without it being overpowering since it still has limitations (Size issues and buff slot issues).

Thoughts? Keep it on track and keep it constructive if we want SoE to actually address this, and of course an official insight as to if this is ever likely to be given thought would be massively appreciated (/poke Absor).
