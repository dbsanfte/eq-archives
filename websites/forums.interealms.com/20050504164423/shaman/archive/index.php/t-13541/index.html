I used to run 3 accounts on 3 systems (Monk, Shammy, Cleric) at the same time. I used a belkin box to switch between 2 of the systems. I tried both a PS/2 and a USB switch. The USB box has about a 9 second delay on the switch in order to power up the USB devices. I ended up sticking with the PS/2 as it switched in about 1 or 2 seconds to the devices.

I kept the Monk on my primary PC. The Shammy and cleric were completely hotkeyed on boxes 2 and 3. Having /assist monk or /target monk followed by /cast # would get my heals/slows & debuffs off in time and assure I had the proper target.

IMO it's all about the delay to switch between characters and hot key setup.

I just used this combo to PL my monk and Cleric.
Now that the GF is back I just play my Shammy and let her play on PC#2 and we use the third for my porter or cleric rez.

I 3-box as well - using a 65 Shaman, 63 SK, and 60 Druid (and am starting a new 3-box team on FV soon). I do a similar thing - 2 computers, one of them running 2 sessions of EQWindows at 800x600 resolution and all graphics turned way down or off. This prevents all but the most minimal amount of lag, usually only noticeable when running all three characters through a busy zone on autofollow. My SK (my main) is on my main computer and then the other 2 accounts are on the secondary computer. I also use, as others have mentioned, hotkeys to do most functions. Ie: I have a hotkey on my shaman that does the following when a mob is incoming...

/sit off
/assist *insert SK name here*
/cast 4 (Malo)
/pause 75 (just enough time for the spell bar to refresh)
/cast 2 (Turgur's Insects)

This has worked really well - with the only problems coming when Slow is resisted, in which case I simply have to manually cast slow again. The only difficulty really comes when switching between the Shaman and the Druid windows on the 2nd computer for when I need healing, or when I need to snare, etc. I may start running the shaman account on my main computer alongside the SK, leaving the druid one alone - since the druid is the main healer.
