Sepisis
The mobs aren't all that hard. The problem is that unless you have a chanter or are killing very fast you're gonna get overrun sooner or later.

I tried doing a small bottle with a 60pal, 60 monk and myself a 60 shaman after our chanter left and had to quit after about 15 minutes because we were getting overrun.

To do it you have to buy Gragrot or whatever its called from the Tarmok merchant near the human camp in FG. The larger bottle you buy the faster mobs will be summoned. Then you take it to one of the 3 callers in the 2nd tarmok camp.

Pretty fun little event. I wouldn't try it with out a chanter though.
