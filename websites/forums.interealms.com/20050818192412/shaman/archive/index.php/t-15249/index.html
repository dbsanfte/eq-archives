Should I still spend the 9 aa on QB3? My original reason for getting QB3, was the cast time on torpor. Will the 2 stack? I don't really understand how the stacking works.

An enhancement haste focus item like the Shissar ring will stack with the Quickbuff AA, with a max limit of 50% of spell casting time. As a note, the Shissar Ring does not affect Torpor/Q. You can see by looking at Lucy, that Enhancement Haste only affects spells with a duration equal to or greater than 48 seconds:

http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=2335&source=Live

If you're talking about a Haste/Quickening/Speed of Mithaniel item that affects all beneficial spells, using Q as an example, no matter the combination of focus/AA or cleric spell haste, you can't get the casting time lower than 3.0 seconds (50% of 6.0). This is why I think it's pretty excessive to spend 9 points on QB 3.

As long as we're talking about stacking:

Extended enhancement will stack with SCRM, there doesn't appear to be any max duration. Extended enhancement does not work with Torpor/Q although SCRM does.

SCM will stack with preservation focus items.

Preservation focus items do not stack. If you have an Affliction Efficiency item and a Mana preservation item, depending on the spell cast, the better of the focus items will take precedence.
