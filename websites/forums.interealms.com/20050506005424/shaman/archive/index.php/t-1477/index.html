Bahimiron
Truespirit faction is used for the SK and the wizard epic quests.

How one goes about getting it when one is not a member of those classes is a bit of a toughie. The issue is that the starting point can only be done by members of those classes, and all following items require a certain level of faction, thus seemingly making it impossible. It ain't, though...

...and a chanter would probably have a better time of it than anyone else.

I know a cleric who used charm wands that drop off of golems in Fear to charm the wandering spirit and turn in woe and envy repeatedly until he had allied faction with Truespirit. Unfortunately, it was all for naught, as what he was hoping to do turned out to be undoable. Still, he did demonstrate that the faction is possible to get for the pathetically determined. :p
