Ok, it is not the fastest Xp or greatest loots, but I have had fun hunting in a new zone...

Nedaria's Landing.

I hunt near the entrance and have been making great use of my charm spell. I charm a wolf, bear, or griffon and have it go after some others.

You will get on average 2 kills out of a pet before it turns on you, and if you are lucky, you can take out your former pet as well. Of course, you may also not get a kill before you have an angry mob or two after you, such is the nature of charm.

Most often I run when the charm breaks and med to full for another round.

No real loot except for skins, though I have 3 HQ bear which should sell.

Just an alternative for those who cant wait for their doggie.

I've been duoing here with my 32 pally/shammy combo and it's been great XP. I am camped at the wayfarers camp and just head over and kill what cons blue or better. There are griffawns and kitties further down that still con white/yellow at 32. It's a great zone and I love being able to pop into BB or any other adv camp and start up an adventure at a moments notice. The loot isn't the greatest minus the occasional pelts, but it's a good place to work up fishing too (just bring poles/bait with you as I haven't found a vendor there with them on it - you can sell to the magus however).
