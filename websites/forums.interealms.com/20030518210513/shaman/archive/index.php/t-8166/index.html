you are right, heiro > lodi. What you are lacking is in hp atm. I would not swap your shoulders for igs. Definately finish up your epic. The next thing that you should look at purchasing is a horse, the cheapest one is only 8400 and is by far worth the price. This will help you quite a bit as you aa towards can 5, and as you level to 65 in pop(all outdoors) The horse is something that you will use practically full time and you will grow to love it.

After that, look into a solstice ear to replace the barb one that you have. After doing that, look to get some hp on your no-hp slots. If you can raid hot you can get the nice quest drops there and most have hp or sta. Talisman of benovolence is definately a good upgrade for you also and free is never a bad price :)

Just look to get hp where you can, your wis and mana is ok atm but you are definately lacking on the hp front. If you can get up past 1k ac also, this is an added bonus.
