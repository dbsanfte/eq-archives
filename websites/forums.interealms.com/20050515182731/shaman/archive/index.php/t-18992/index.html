1). Cripple upgrade that targets STR, AGI, DEX, AC along adding a STA debuff
I don't believe NPCs are effected by Stamina. Buffing your pet does not increase it's HP. If a mob's HP *can* be effected by STA buffs, debuffing their STA would also decrease their HP, which would indirectly cause this new spell to do damage to the mob. I personally wouldn't like to see the change, because it would more than likely break mez.

My first thought is to ask for SOE to add an upgraded Cripple with ATK debuffs on it, but that now seems to be the realm of the Druids. In addition to ATK debuffs, they also have resist and AC debuffs (pretty nice ones, too) - E'ci's Frosty Breath (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3442&source=Live), Immolation of Ro (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3437&source=Live), Ro's Illumination (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3440&source=Live), Hand of Ro (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3435&source=Live). The thing about these spells is that most of them have a resist adjustment or a DoT tied to them. Hand of Ro has a -200 Fire resist adjustment AND it decreases Fire resists by 72. And it stacks with Malo(s). E'ci doesn't stack with Ro's Illumination, but everything else stacks with each other. If you add up all the Ro spells, you get a total of -57 AC, -180 ATK, -107 SV Fire, and -1400 HP over a minute. Substitute E'ci for RI and you have -66 AC, -100 ATK, -107 SV Fire, and -55 SV Cold. By comparison, with Cripple, Malos, and Malicious Decay, we have -70 DEX, -80 AGI, -80 STR, -34 AC, and roughly -55 to all saves. And we all know that debuffing stats doesn't have near as much effect as directly debuffing AC and ATK.

If they are going to give Druids low-resist debuffs and debuff/DoT combos, there's no reason why they can't either combine Cripple with a small DoT or give us a low-resist debuff that is a combo of Cripple, Malx, and/or ID. Turgur's and Malos are easily the most useful debuffs in the game, but I feel like Druid debuffs are a little too close for comfort. They already have better DPS and healing than us and buffs that are on par with ours in usefulness. Do they really need to have that many low resist combo debuffs and debuff/dots as well?
