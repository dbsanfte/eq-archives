# 1 - Have fun. Pick up a hobby, read a book, take a break.

Now frankly I'm sorta suprised your guild didn't "suggest" you level your Monk up. But that's more of an aside. I'm also curious why you can't guild your main, but I'll MYOB.

The cudgel quests I saw on eqprices were for a Shaman not Monk.

If you're looking for a decent Monk weapon check out a Waning light Katar. www.monkly-business.com also has a nice spreadsheet. You might have better - I am ghetto.

Now insofar as leveling. (Exp stinks in Seb btw compared to PoP.)

Check out Blargx's eloquent posts on leveling v not leveling. You also might want to check out PoD and see if you can handle single pulls. With your slow and 1100+ AC it won't hurt to give it a SHOT. If so, Pod exp > Seb exp.

If it's money you want, I always thought Guard had better gem drops.

Can a 55 Rogue and 60 Shaman handle Emperor? Yes. Will be tricky getting there, and there are threads that talk about that - but see if you can hook up with a group heading down.

IMO - it doesn't HURT to level to 61. If you still want to hunt Seb Crypt it should still be DB, however possibly not + / - 5 levels.

If getting Torpor and Malo is more important to you, then see about killing Kunark Dragons also.

But have fun. Go whack MOBs in Droga / Nurga for faction and the Ring of Bathezid. (I did, I also MQ the 8th Coldain).

It's a game. Not a way of life and not work. It's supposed to be fun. * IF * you must log on, try a tradeskill. Baking, pottery just for mindless clicking while you talk to your guild.

Have fun.
