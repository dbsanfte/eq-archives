Buy the Jade Reaver. Its droppable, so it is perfectly legal, and honorable to buy one, despite what the EQ junkies on these messageboards say. The Reaver camp in CoM is perpetually camped, and the reaver drops very infrequently there, so the easiest way to get one is to buy it at the bazaar. On the Mith Marr server the Jade reaver currently sells for 10 to 14k. Its worth every penny.

In fact, here are the typical prices for various parts of the ranger epic quest on the Mith Marr server:

1. Glowing Green Stone MQ (from VSR): 10k
2. Jade Reaver: 12k
3. Swirling Sphere of Color MQ: 5k

You do the math. 10k + 12k + 5K = a very cheap way to get an item that provides +30 attack, and +40% haste! Imagine if a 13/21 weapon with 40% haste, +15str, and +30 atk was auctioned in the bazaar; it would sell for 150k to 200k easy.

Trust me, most rangers nowadays solve their epic by spending the cash, not camping rare mobs for ultra-rare drops. Capitalism is your friend.
