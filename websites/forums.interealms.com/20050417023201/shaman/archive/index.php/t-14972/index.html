sharookan

Couple caveats.

A popular camp spot for most seems to be by the rocks behind the big tent. Be careful. Potameids (aquatic dryads basically) sometimes wander the bank of the river (which is directly behind you if you're camped by the rocks). While camping for my half elf mask (which still eludes me, sigh), had a potameid come out of the water and beat Amorae to within inches of her life :p

Your level means absolutely nothing in regards to the poachers. They'll aggro a 65 the same as a 1. But, they seem to have like no magic resistance (Amorae got almost 2 levels grouped with me there and I can't remember ever having a slow or root resisted) though they do hit hard. They're also rogues so don't let them get behind you (or dogdog) or else their dps will spike in a way you won't like.


But yeah, JP is usually where I go when everything else is camped or just not worth the travel time. Solo, I can kill pretty much anything in zone (darkpaw gnolls give me fever though). Partnered with pretty much any melee class, I can make stuff die extremely quickly. Try partnering with a ranger and fear kite griffons and anacondas. Your dots will make those mobs just evaporate :D
