Think why some Group buffs tend to wear off at different times is that at the moment the group buff is cast a packet is sent to the server. Then from the server there is a packet sent to each PC that the buff landed on. Now by this time there is a VERY good chance a TICK is going to have happened. Most likely what has happened is some of the PC's get thier Packet during one tick while others get thier Packet during another. This has now caused those that got thier packet late to get an addittional 6 secs of the buff. If later they get thier packet telling them thier buff should now start to fad a bit late then guess what? They get a few more seconds of a given buff. Well that is how I see it.
