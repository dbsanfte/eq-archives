Nektar
Hrhr, I probably farmed a total of 6 psc's in my days, no kidding :D I started killing them at 56, and back then they were extremly frustrating, but hey I wanted more psc's :D :D

The only problem I find now is pulling them. They phase all around the wazoo, and sometimes when I pull one, poof I get an add on me while I run back. Sucks when you have other people at zone in, especially if they're afk and non-invis :p

Once pulled though, phase spiders are cake and this is why: A phase spider will always phase a pet to where it was originally summoned. Therefore, the trick is letting the pet get aggro the spider from where he was summoned, and voila, no more random annoying phases. And with torpor, it's extremly easy to keep the dog healed.

I do fight them slowly (2 turgurs and 4 epics), but at the end almost always manage to maintain 80%+ mana. For all you ikkies out there, I don't use jbb, nor do I spend mana dotting. The only mana-costly spells I use against them are turgurs, torpor, and celerity (for pet). Not exactly what I call thrilling, but I kill them for the psc, not the challenge.
