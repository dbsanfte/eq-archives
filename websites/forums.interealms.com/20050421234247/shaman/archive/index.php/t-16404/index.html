Tragik
For a two box I'd go with Shadowknight or Paladin.

1. Paladin is great due to stackable buffs, rez, secondary healing (or even primary with good FT/KEI), crowd control ability with stun/root. Pacify line makes pulling mobs that are up to level 55 trivial.

2. Shadowknights bring extra DPS in the form of the pet (starting at level 58+ [previous pets are horrid even as speed bumps) and atk buffs/debuffs, snare, FD pulling, corpse summons, debuff/buff taps. Pick up a pre-nerf circlet of shadow and they're excellent scouts combined with FD.

I'd stay away from pure caster 2 box combos unless you're especially coordinated. Necromancers are good but keeping track of both shaman and necro dots can get tiring. Magician is similiar except that nukes are instant damage and the pets are superior. 61+ they get some great toys to summon to widen the gap between mage pets and nec/bst pets as well.

Beastlord actually wouldn't be bad. They're primarily a melee character with a very powerful pet, some very nice utility spells though the buffs are redundant to yours with the exception of the purity and savage lines. Their pets are nearly on par with mage pets and a decently geared beast can tank just fine with a shaman slowing.

Look at the spell lines and get to know/duo with some of the classes you're thinking of using in a 2 box to get a better idea of what will work for you. I've two boxed several different classes including the ones I've posted about here (except for bst). All have their strengths and weaknesses. I suggested SK/Pal simply because they work well in all situations. Beastlords would fit into that category too, I'd imagine.

Good luck.

Necro and Shaman 2 boxer here.

Currently level 56 and been doing PoP since 51.

My playstyle involves using the shaman as a tank so I geared him up with AC first then HP next in mind. A slowed PoP mob with 1 necro rog pet, 1 alpha'd pet, both fully buffed for damage and hasted, with 5 dots stacked on it from both nec and shaman does not live very long.

With Shaman cannibalize and necro lich line of spells plus with both characters sharing the mana cost for dotting, I hardly experience any downtime.

With necro FD, Fear, and Screaming Terror (necro mez) plus root, I can split camps and handle adds. I am able to handle up to 3 adds but I am sure I can handle up to 4. I have never tried and really don't look forward to unless I have to.

When in a group, it actually gets easier. All people want from the Shaman are buffs and slows and an occassional heal here and there when there is a cleric. An occasional nuke depending on how much mana I have. The bulk of my damage comes from the necros pet plus dot.

When in a group being the only healer, this is where you can see how the necro and shaman fully complement each other. The necro can provide a decent amount of damage with just the pet alone while saving up his own mana to dump/twitch the shaman who will be slowing and healing. This works out exceptionally well and with 4 melees, the group is not really missing any damage from necro dots/nukes. No one hears me say LoM or OOM.

This combination has large range of possibilities. But when LDON came out, I have been forced to play only 1. I still cannot get used to running around constantly with 2 characters. Before LDON, it was so easy to 2 box even in a group since all you did was find a pull spot and work from there.

I would only 2 box in LDON if I am with playing with people that I am familiar with. Maybe when I get confident enough, I will bring both along even on pickup LDON groups.

Well, that is my necro/shaman 2 box experience.
