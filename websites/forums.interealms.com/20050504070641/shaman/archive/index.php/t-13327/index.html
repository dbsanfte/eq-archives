Juujuu
For any raiding shaman, this is probably the route you want to go with and probably never deviate from it, unless you don't like the regen 3 and want something else.
-Run 3
-Regen 3
-Mental Clarity 3
-Cannibalization
-Mass Group buff

After those, here's the path I took I think. I took them because they are all more activatable skills that have really good uses in the right situations:
-Radiant Cure 1 (This skill is amazing. It cures poisons, diseases, curses, snare, root, magic based dots, and all sorts of afflictions that you would have to dispel to remove, and you don't need to mem a spell to do it. Best 2 points you could ever spend)
-Spirit Call 3 (The free damage is nice. Each pet parses about 750-1000 damage depending on the target. Their push factor is both a blessing in some situations, and a curse in others. In the end, 3500-5000 damage every 12 minutes for zero mana is hard to argue with)
-Virulent Paralysis 1 (This skill I wasn't sure was going to be too good, but I found I really like it. It's disease based, so the different resist on being able to root stuff can be useful. The knockback effect is very good for positioning mobs. The root is free mana, so when I'm root rotting stuff, I can save myself about 100+ mana every 2 minutes on a root. The knockback pushes most mobs out of melee range, so you don't need to move much to stop getting hit (good when you are on a horse))

-After these skills, I went for the Weight Watchers 3 skill so I eat less food and water. I'm on my horse a lot, so I hate going through all those hero sammiches and misty thicket pic-a-nic baskets. It's very noticable how little food you go through with this skill. I think a stack of 20 miraculous food/water will last me for about 2 weeks straight.

-After that, because I am often the shaman massing f07, and because I have Q, I got Spell Casting Reinforcement Mastery. Less massing of buffs. More efficient heals. Less often buffing other annoying buffs. Longer lasting Ferine Avatars is nice.

-After that, I got Quick Buff 2. The first level was to get Elder Title. I got the 2nd level so that with my Haste of Mithanial item, I have the max 50% quick buff level for Q. If I replace my Haste of Mithanial item, I'll probably get Quick Buff 3, but for now, I have zero need. I also got QB2 just so I don't have to bug Clerics for BoR quite as much.

That's about as far as I am in my AAs.
Hope it helps you decide.
