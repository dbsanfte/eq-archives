Genocide?

Premise: Take a regular MUD. Every half hour to an hour have everyone start over in a big room. Each player votes what type of war to run - 4 team, 2 team, all against all, etc. Teams are then selected. Once the game starts, everyone has nothing and races around the areas gathering equipment. Upon entering a room with NPC or mobs - the npc or mob explodes leaving behind its equipment for the player to pick up. After a certain amount of time, the war starts and the last one standing is the winner. Upon death a player becomes a ghost and can observe the battle or hang out in the waiting room.

I can see the refuse of EQ becoming something like this as a possible alternative once enough people leave. The major expense of server cost and client base is taken care of, so all that would be needed is to bolt on the war structure.
