For us rangers, the ONLY pieces you can turn in are Thorny Vine.

This has been tested with the RNG non-Thornyvine pieces that drop in Hate, which you can NOT turn in. Only Thornyvine.

Please note: if you loot another class's armor in Hate/ Fear, you CAN turn it in to the trainer OF THAT CLASS in PoK.

Note: for the Old Planes Planar armor that is class/ races specific (like the Iskar SK stuff that drops in Fear) there don't seem to be trainers yet.
