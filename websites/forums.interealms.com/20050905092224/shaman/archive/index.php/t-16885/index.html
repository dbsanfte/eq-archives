echo is TERRIBLE and slow at this level..if you are PLing its just not worth it if you dont have the entire high level area to yourself. If you get the whole area to yourself it might be worth it.

DSP will be slow unless you do hoppers/sambatas, and if you need hides/acrylia for tailoring you might try it.

JPF is my favorite zone for this level...you can get 51 doing the wolves and bears and NEVER EVER run out of mobs to pull. Plus the wisps and griffs drop a tidy amount of cash drops. I leveled my druid here and he could clear 300PP or more an hour with kei (of course he got a level an hour too, but thats another story..). The area along the river is totally safe to pull to and the chances of competition are basically zero.

Dulak or gunthak would be a good choice, and you should get a decent amount of loot here to make it worthwhile. Dulak can carry you to 64 (would be slow of course, but the possibility is there :) ) with dark blues to kill~. Blue diamonds and sapphires and diamonds and star rubies make for great loot; lousy drops off nameds though (except of course soulstripper).
