Quite a big difference in levels, and finding a place where you can BOTH get exp without it being dangerous is very difficult, especially since you are the one that is lower level.

Here are some suggestions (not in order of importance or value):

Maiden's Eye - Toilers or Goranga Gatherers(Blue for both)
--- Don't fight hunters at this level, extremely hard to slow ---

Karnor's Castle - Basement (Some light blue for monk)

Sebilis - Zone or Zone out (Some light blue for monk)

Chardock - Castle/Zone (Some light blue for monk)

Velks - Spiders (Though they are overcamped, all blue)

Katta - Guards (Most light blue for monk, but fast pulls)


Places you should NOT try:

Sol B - Imps/Efreeti (Magic resist on these suckers is waaay too high)

Sebilis - NG (Great loot but the krup wizards and necrosis beetle would own you both at your level)

Akheva - Resists at 52 on the guys inside Akheva (since they are higher level) are not worth it.

-------------------------

NOTE: These results are BEFORE the resist changes, after the resist changes it MIGHT be easier to slow mobs that are even or higher level than you, therefore making some camps in the excluded list a possibility. Try it out at your own risk. Hope this is helpful.
