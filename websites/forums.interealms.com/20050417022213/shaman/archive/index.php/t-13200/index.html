howie
Hmmm. That's kinda tough. Don't know your playstyle, but... First I'd improve your HP and melee skills, otherwise it's gonna be tough to solo stuff. At 55 I was soloing in maidens. I did it like this. Get a pet kit for rover, and outfit him. Might be a good idea to have a spare just incase you have to run for the zone or rover bites the biscuit. Next have all your buffs up to maximize your HP's, or donate and get aego/virtue, and KEI would be a must. Got to maidens zone in just to the right is a big boulders this is where you want to pull to and park your doggie here. Now look for any goranga gatherers, lookout, or followers, these are what you want to pull. Pull with slow run to rock, sick pet, and root/rot his sorry butt! Rinse and repeat until dead. Second alternative is PoJ prisoners. At 55 i didn't get rat adds, or aggro. You need to insure that the iskar Sralm is not in his cell if you are pulling near zone out. He will aggro if you pull another prisoner or sentry. If he is up you can root/rot him by zone out. Once he's dead you can pretty much pull anything by zone out without additional aggro. What I do though is run to the cell tgt a prisoner, then move to max distance away and pull with slow. These both can be done solo, it's not easy but it is doable. As a side note, while I soloed in maidens I maxed all my melee skills. When I got there at 54, pierce was 190, 1hb 101, and 2hb was 161. Left maidens at 55 with all maxed at 200! Good luck and let me know if this works for you.
