You might want to check out this thread (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=2353) and this one (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?threadid=2532) (similar level shaman asking gear questions.)

It appears that on the AC/HP vs WIS scale, you've leaned heavily on the WIS side. I think you'll find the conventional wisdom here is that while a shaman can be effectively played either way, the AC/HP orientation is generally the preferred way to go. Check out this thread (http://forums.interealms.com/shaman/showthread.php?s=&threadid=2533) for details on why.

Recommendations:

Ears: <a href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=4930" target="_blank">Imbued Golden Ivory Earring</a> (cheap- under 100 pp, maybe much less if you provide the ivories- and a great combination of AC and WIS.)

Neck: <a href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6020" target="_blank">Black Alloy Medallion</a> - stats are pretty good (ac 6, +5 wis), it's cheap (couple hundred PP), and it will make every spell you cast under level 44 cost 10% less mana. That's a good thing!

Face: <a href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=5289" target="_blank">Arctic Wyvern Mask</a>. You get a nice balance of AC and WIS with the mask, for just a few hundred pp.

Head: Totemic helm is fine. :)

Fingers: Ideally, you'd like to get some 6 AC, 65 HP rings, but if on a budget 4/45 or 5/55 rings will be cheaper and provide similar benefits.

Wrist: For one wrist, get a totemic or ry'gorr bracer, for the other a <a href="http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=809" target="_blank">Chipped Bone Bracelet (CBB)</a> (with the CBB you get "Affliction Efficiency II" - which will cause all DoTs under level 45 to cost 25% less mana to cast. Again, this should just cost a few hundred PP.)

Arms: JB arms provide great AC for a few hundred pp

Hands: JB gloves (few hundred pp)

Shoulder: Tattered mantle (100 pp or so, maybe less.)

Chest: Imbued Northman, Ry'gorr (few hundred pp) or a Tree Weave (1k or a little less) are decent chest slot items

Back: You're fine here

Waist: Spider fur belt (100-200 pp) is a great belt for the price. A straw belt is a great super-cheap alternative (like 10 pp)

Legs: Your legs are fine- this is a tough spot to upgrade, especially on a budget.

Boots: JB boots are your next upgrade

Your weapons and shield are great- no need to upgrade here.
