Jaraxel,

The Defender 90 (the soft top pick up version) would be the one Lara Croft (Angelina Jolie (sp?)) was driving through the jungle in the Tomb Raider movie after she and it were dropped by parachute from an cargo plane.

I think the vehicle you want is the Land Rover Range Rover 4.0 SE or a variation of it. Land Rover makes the Range Rover, Discovery II, Freelander and Defender 90. For a bit of history see this :

http://www.o-v-r-c.com/Land%20Rover%20History.htm

The U.S. version of the Defender 90 was introduced here in the U.S. in 1993 and stopped in 1997 or first quarter of 1998. They are still manufactured and sold in Great Britain and sold in other parts of the world I think. So you would have to import it into the U.S. and in the process adjust it mechanically to meet US EPA and US DOT standards and whatever other standards the state has that your bringing it into. (California emission standards come to mind). Not a cheap venture (at least for me).

I think in the movie Rainman, the exotic car Tom Cruise was bringing into the U.S. for a customer was being held up by U.S. Custom for EPA issues thus spurring his need to get him declared guardian over Dustin Hoffman? Importing cars not manufactured by the factory to conform to U.S. standards is definately not a cheap endeavor.

The mandatory air bags regulations defeated further U.S. spec'd Defender 90s as at the time they could not be fitted. Maybe they can now with the new types of air bags but nothing has surfaced about Land Rover making new U.S. spec'd Defenders.

Probably more information than you or anyone else wanted to know. ;)
