My advice to you is to not waste your time with any horse except the cheapest or the most expensive. Just buy the cheapest horse there is, put the bridle in your ammo slot, and hotkey it. The only reason to get a horse besides the cheapest is to help you travel across entire zones faster. I finally broke down and bought one when ldon came out, because I was getting sick of running from the butcherblock camp to mistmoore.

Second of all, if you had bothered to visit the necro boards, you would realize that SoW potions are FAR superior to anything besides 100k horses for soloing. There is an acceleration start up period for horses, thus rendering horses largely useless for agro kiting. The only exception to this is for kiting unsnareable mobs, whereupon it does become possible to kite these with a 100k horse, if you have a affliction haste and only use spells with fast casting times.

If you are determined to buy a horse, don't buy anything except the 10k or 100k versions. SoW potions are your friend, and still used religiously by many high level necros when jboots / run3 aren't enough.
