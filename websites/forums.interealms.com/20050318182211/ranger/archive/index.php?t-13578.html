Katalaeia,

PON. Enter zone. From graveyard go north along the wall, being careful to not aggro some of the random spawns along the wall. If any are there it is usually best to leave them to a group or even offer to pull it to a group. Once you get a clear path, follow the wall around but stop well short of the river.

Now turn and face along the river but stay on that plateau. Moving parallel to the river you will eventually find a spot where you can single pull up to 4 ravens.

Pull these back towards the wall, snare, and fear. Be sure to never get too close to the river or you will get hobgoblin aggro and that is just plain ugly. With C3 you can probably kill the 4th raven before the first respawns.
