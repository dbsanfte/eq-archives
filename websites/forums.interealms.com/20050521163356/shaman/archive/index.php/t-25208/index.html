Tyck
There's a cave located partway across the zone on the northern wall just after the bridge.

There's only one mob in the entire cave, a little piggy that you kill to start an event.

Go past him and at the back of the cave there is a pile of rocks.

Right click on the rocks (it will drop your invis) and you will be ported to a cave about the statue and benches in RCoD.
