Borgax
The harder parts of the shaman epic (Hole and CoM final encounters) can be done for multiple shaman depending how long you and yours are willing to stick around.

Once you kill Kirn the first time, you and only a handful of others can easily hold the other room while you wait the 2 hours for another Kirn (hell at 55, I bet you can even do it solo). Others can log to alts or camp out for a couple hours, or if you have a Mage with CotH it's even easier.

We always stick around for a second shaman each time we do the Hole for whoever. The time flies by with a good group of people. Getting to the tower is the hardest part of the Hole...might as well stick around for the easy stuff if you can.

Same goes for CoM pretty much. By the time you finish killing Rak after Neh, you can nearly head right back up and keep doing that rotation over and over again. It's not too hard to convince people to do this since someone always needs to keep at it for a Jade Reaver.

Our epic is quite easy, and although it doesn't repeat quite as nicely as some other epics, with just some minor patience you can go through a whole swath of shamans for the tougher sections in one day on the weekend.


*If* you're still wanting to try the faction method, most people do the Envy/Woe/Shield section over and over whenever they can until well ally. Make sure you at least get yourself the Black Wolf Boots though. One of my favorite pieces of equipment.
