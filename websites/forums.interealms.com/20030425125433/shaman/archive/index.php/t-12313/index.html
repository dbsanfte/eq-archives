Kazim
I think you're correct to put lizard + jbb first.

I think solstice earring is the only FT item you can reasonably expect to acquire before you become a lot higher level and join a raid guild that goes after relatively uber targets. I don't think I've ever seen a FT item for sale in the bazaar.

I'd just plan to canni a lot and go for regen gear. Start with iksar bp, then graduate to a fungi tunic (15pts/tick regen) when you can afford it.

Personally, I think that you have another option which is comparable to regaining mana faster, which is to not use as much of it at once. Get a mana preservation 3 item like an Obulus Death Shroud and an extended enhancement 3 item like an amulet of insight. Personally, I spend a ton of mana on buffs (especially regen and haste), so both of those focus items end up saving me a huge amount of mana per hour.

Look at it this way:

You can either spend 115 mana every 10 minutes for a buff like alacrity. Or you can spend 110 mana every 12 minutes (using the 2 focus items i listed). In the first case, you cast the buff 6 times per hour and use 690 mana. In the second case you cast it 5 times per hour and use only 550 mana. You've spent 140 less mana in an hour. When you add in all the buffs and other spells you cast, the mana savings really starts to add up. Wouldn't surprise me if appropriate focus items didn't end up being comparable to a lot of flowing thought also.

The cloak you are given with your citizenship is upgraded with each step of the quest . The weapon is crap and not worth doing but is a necessary step in reaching the better cloaks.
