Zixor
There are a couple things happening here. First of all, you revieve about 1.5 times more actual ac when you equip an item then what the item stats list. For example, if you equip a 10ac helm you will gain about 15ac. In your case, you are unequiping an 18ac helm so you will lose about 27ac. Then you are equiping a 12 ac helm so you will gain about 18. So assuming your new halm didn't have any agi you would actually lose 9ac by equiping it.

Now we take into account the agi on your new helm. From my experience I get about 1ac for every 5agi (roughly). Since your new helm has 15agi you should gain about 3ac from the agi bonus.

So you lose 9 from raw ac, and gain 3ac from agi. Hence the 6ac loss.
