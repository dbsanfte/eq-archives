Generally a newer expansion will obsolete older spells. Hand of Vitue made it unecessary to get Blessing of Aegolism, for example. Focus of the Seventh made it unecessary to get Khura's Focusing. Doing current content will get you the best spell of the line and you can generally make do with the more easily attainable versions of the spell line (e.g. Aegolism or Focus of Spirit) until you get to the point were you can just concentrate on one hard to obtain spell.

There have been a few spells that are not upgraded in new expansions and are hard to get. Remove Greater Curse in the Luclin era was next to impossible for the average priest to get (at least it was tradeable). With LDoN, they introduced a method for the masses to get the spell. Balance of the Nihil is a similar situation to RGC. It's a spell that has not been obsoleted by newer spells. It's hard to get in it's original form even after allowing for mudflation from increased levels and gear. There needs to be an updated way for Shamans to get this spell in the context of current content. Just like it made no sense to drag a group away from LDoN to get RGC when LDoN was current, it makes no sense to drag a group away from OoW or DoN now that GoD is not current.

If Beastlords are getting a -MR slow, it better not be any easier to get than Nihil unless there is an equally available Shaman equivilant introduced.
