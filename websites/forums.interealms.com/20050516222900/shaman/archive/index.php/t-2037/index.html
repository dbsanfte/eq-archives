Lunasombra
Honestly, they are one in the same. Why you ask?


1) You have X mana. To recover X mana you need to be able to cannabalize. Don't get me wrong a good wis base is nice (currently I'm at 201 but will be going down to 187 to add 14ac 165 hp and 25 mana) but you regen at the same speed from 201 that you do from 187. So, to keep said base up, you need the HP to cannabalize from. If your getting the snot pounded out of you you will have less HP to cannabalize from as well, further lowereing your effective mana base as well as your mana regen.

2) More you get pounded on resists, the more your interrupted. You can be FOM, still wont matter if you can't get a slow off.

3) If you set up a cannabalize key to the minimum pause, hasted, you get off 2 swings. Very nice to add 100 damage while cannabalizing (this just adding to the "combat" thing and a reason to max piercing/1hb/2hb)



Now if all you want to do is root and rot, then yes, a larger mana base is probably in order. Though if all you want to do is be a druid with dots which break their root.... ;)


Good luck to you in whatever you do man and have FUN doing it.
