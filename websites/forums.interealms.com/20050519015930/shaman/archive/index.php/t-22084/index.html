Kenneth
I'm doing my emp key right now.

Com 1 is an easy solo and actually pops quite a bit more frequently than what I have read -- just soloing around the right swimmy, and killing everything in that room a couple times is enough to get him to pop. There are two guards right behind the entrance to the room (in front of the swimmy ramp), but they come single if you pull from just inside the hallway. I've done this guy several times now (after I soloed it, I helped several different guildies get their idols). I've never bothered with the other camps to get this guy.

Com 2 is a little tougher camp (up the right swimmy), and is a much rarer spawn. I don't like to solo this as I cannot kill them fast enough imo. Also, the elites seem to pop more frequently here, and I cannot solo those. I have done this with a mage, a necro, and a wizzy duo though. And, it was super easy each time. However, I've only seen this guy a couple times in about 8 shots at him. I haven't gotten my ring yet, although a couple have dropped.

The insignias definitely require a group (at least for my guild). There are some guards that block the entranceways that need to be split, for one. And, we have even seen two named spawned in the guard spots that we had to break through. The monk pulled em to the right as he ran in first. Then, the rest of us tagged the first one to come back and pulled it to the far left. The named aren't slowable in this camp but are stunable.

The pouch is an easy pick up if you do Cursed at all. If you do, I wouldn't even worry about soloing it.
