1. Trigger is killing any "Earthen Mudwalker", once one is down you have to kill them all to spawn "Sludge Lurker" before respawns.

2. He is pretty easy, only real problem on him is the AE Root can sometimes keep your melee damage out fo the fight. His HP I would say is actually on the lower side.

3. There are only 4 Filth Gorgers, you can kill them anywhere, it doesn't matter.

4. All Sludge Lurkers and Monsterous Mudwalker have minor adds that pop out.

5. If I recall correctly Exile Summoned and Nature's Rebuke work on him (I know they do on Rock Ring). It doesn't really matter where you kill him, I think most push him to a corner (usually at zone in or dust temple), we always just do him in the open.

6. We haven't really encountered this problem with aggro control since the first time or two since we have done him, but I think we first solved this problem by having a Paladin tank him.

We kill him a little bit different. We fight the 5 splitters near the temple and "split" the rushing 4 mobs with 4-6 rangers (we have a lot of them) by snaring and rooting. One chanter helps with AE tash if the come the steps down.

The last mob of this 4 will be hold rootet if non damaged or offtanked by an Paly. Than we clear all respawns around the temple and pull the last rusher inside the temple where the final mud boss with AE root spawns.

The final rusher will be killed in the center of the tempel and an paly grabs aggro and chainstun him until he dies. We keep him centered most times but sometimes he end in an corner of his temple if the pet users mess up the positioning.

Most fights (9 of 10) are an cakewalk and rangers (we have a lot of them *eg*) can pull out some good dps with this tactic.

We tried the "corner at zonein" style as we started with this encounter and have serious problems because we managed it two times to push him "over" the corner and the end was really bad. He is an nasty corpse camper and you dont get an second shot if you wipe at the zone in.
