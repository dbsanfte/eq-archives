If they raise the content in LDoN to work for levels 70's, I expect the point awards will be 100+. The reasons for this will probably be due to your "high-enders" who at 65 could get 105 points for a hard ldon. Your lvl 70 normal LDoN will probably be on the same line of difficulty- hence an equal or better point count. That cuts gear purchase time from 28 wins for Incorporeal to 14 or less- not bad.

Currently 10-12 elemental geared peeps can clear a decent amount of NToV. I expect with 5 more levels your average "bazaar character" will be able to conquer NToV with the same amount of peeps or even less. Same goes for a majority of Ssra content outside of DT'ing mobs. Granted, I am sure the raid quality gear will increase as well, but lvl 70 trivializes a lot of old raid content from a 30 man raid to 12-16 friends. I think this is something that SOE is "trying" to do. 72 man raids were a bad idea on zone stabilty issues, and I think the programmers realize this. We are seeing in content outside of PoP requiring or allowing less people, and lvl 70 will trivialize enough PoP content to where 72 players will not be mandatory. I expect to see a lot of super-guilds either splinter into factions or shrivel up with players leaving the game. I am interested in seeing what is going to happen.
