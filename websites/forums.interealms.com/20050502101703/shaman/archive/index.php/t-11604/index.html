Thorzion
Well...my normal response to this question is use the search feature, but as we have just moved boards Im thinking some of the information didnt move with us. Soo..that in mind...

I usually say that any race is sutible for a shaman. I do somewhat ammend that because although Im not a troll myself (Barbarian here) I do have many troll shaman friends and I think that what the frogloks have done to Grobb is just disgusting. SO I always recommend against the frog now. They also have pretty wimpy stats that will take lots of equipment to make right....which is another strike against them.

Barbs are good all around shaman. 5% exp penalty. can slam to interupt casters. no night vision. good faction with most races, evil races KOS.

Iksar and trolls innate regen which is pretty usefull to about 53 or so. Hated everywhere. Iksar cant use JBB and have special armor restrictions. Better AC bonus though. both carry 20% exp penalty..level pretty slow. Iksar could level from 1 to 60 and never leave Kunark..although exp is much better in some places so you probably want to.

Ogres highest STA of all....which means HP. cant be stunned from front, and are just MONSTERS of soloing. 15% exp hit. hated by good races

Cats no exp hit. go through food and water like crazy. safefall..good newbie areas and easy access to nexus and good exp areas.


If I had it to do over again I would probably roll an Ogre, just for the hiightened STA and unstunnability. Maybe a cat..but I just dont like being furry....

all in all though, as everyone else will tell you, it comes down to what you WANT to play.
