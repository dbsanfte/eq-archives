Grippa
In my humble opinion, before level 60 the most important stats are (in this order):

Regeneration
AC
HP / Wis

HP isn't *that* important until you get AA cannibalize. Regen is totally important, more regen means more canni. AC is always good to stop you getting whacked. And Wis is good (within reason) for a reasonable mana pool and to reduce fizzles.

Opinions vary but most people seem to be comfortable with a wis around 150-170, and then get as much AC as you can without dropping wis too much. But then again some shamans have wis nearer 120 and they still manage ok.

Until you get near the AC cap (speculated as around 1300 but let's not get into that!) then more AC is always good, and should be a priority, except for the BP slot which you should save for a regen item.

All in my opinion of course :) Go with what works for you.
