I saw it twice. The second time was much better, since I didn't have such high expectations of the movie.

My biggest complaint:

1) Boromir got the shaft. In the book, he's much more a 'good guy', which makes his fall to the ring much more powerful, and his death scene more meaningful. In the movie he was like one of those red-shirt guys on StarTrek. Sure, they may have had a few lines but you knew he was gonna die by the end.
When he fought the orcs at the end, it should have been his moment to shine, to show that even though he succumbed to the ring he was still a valiant, noble man. Instead, it was almost as if the director said 'Ok, lets hurry up and kill Boromir so we can show more of Aragorn and Legolas kicking ass'. What a waste.

There were a few more little things that I didn't care for, but once I stopped trying to 'force' the book onto the movie, it was much more enjoyable. And I did enjoy it, quite a bit, in fact.

I'll be getting all 3 on DVD, thats for sure.
