Hedge quest is still a little buggy. You do need EVERYONE to get flagged by the erudite in PoT first, or they won't get the TT lair flag after you kill the boss. You can still enter the hedge to help, however.

Entering the hedge is the real buggy part. If you send up three groups and they all end up in the same hedge, consider yourself lucky. Many times you will end up with 1 group in 1 hedge, and 2 in another hedge (there are three hedges, presumably the quest could be completed simultaneously by 3 sets of groups.) Having a succorer in each group to return to zone & try to reenter into same hedge as other groups can save some time & frustration.

As with pretty much anything, the more higher level (60+) people you have, the better.

You said you lost Mujaki because of not enough DPS. So I assume your clerics ran out of mana? If not, DPS probably isn't the problem. We've done Mujaki with 17, and we aren't uber, so he can certainly be done with less.

From an HP perspective, I wouldn't wear those pants over Kael, SS, Thurg, or Dark Master greaves. Nice upgrade over Dragonleg breeches though.
