Jice Virago
... but not sure what The Corps is doing this weekend. I have some practical advice for you from our own three group experience here:

#1) Two Enchanters is a bare minimum here. There is just too much screwed up pathing here to have anything less. Also having 2 chanters makes doing Lord Rak a walk in the park (I wont post why here, but send me a tell in game if you are curious) giving access to the Atrimonious Shield and Ornate Runed blade. Also as you approach the upper levels the mob density increases dramatically.

#2) Leave your bards at home. Their songs will agro about 5 billion mobs if they sing next to a wall. Every time we have taken bards along past the locked door and they sang at all we have gotten our asses force fed to us. I hate leaving bards behind (our guild uses them extensively) but this is one case where its just not worth it.

#3) If you think a spank is imminent when your on the upper bridge, Dive off so you can get your corpse back in a recoverable spot. Nothing sucks harder than having a dozen naked mele and no lockpicks on your rogue.

#4) Reavers can be pulled without a rogue if your SK is a crafty bastard. Use of Wave of enfeeblement when standing next to the door will yank all the reavers out, as will fearing a single skelly into the door. Your SK then FDs, monk tags, and viola you get a nice easy reaver single pull. As a side note, you can get to the bridge without a rogue, but its tricky and involves abuse of the Gravity Flux spell (and a lot of luck).

#5) Bring a Necro, this zone was made for them. All of the undead mobs have horrid MR in the zone (easy pickings for Thrall of Bones) and selective mana whoring will make life a lot easier on everyone.

#6) Dont pee in the bushes, thats poison oak.
