I solod here for a bit last week (level 56) to get my amulet of the grey wastes.

The snakes hit hard and fast for 130 ish but are easily killed with dogdog and JBB.

The Timeless golums are the best they also hit for about 130 but not as fast as the snakes. but again can be killed with dogdog and JBB. these are my prefered mobs as they don't give out as much damage as the snakes. The golums have a proc that can sometimes fire off and spin you around for a bit.

The boulders are traps that are easy to kill hitting for about 90, but they summon. They are just a pain if you agro them pulling a different mob back to camp.

I stay clear of the rock fiends as they are immune to jbb and have huge HP compared to the other mobs. They can be killed with BoN though.

I set up camp on a wall rather than in the middle of the zone. There are lot's of trains created by the ubers heading to the temple. If you are lucky they send out a shout before they set off. A lot don't though.

the only thing I really dont like about this zone is they way the mobs vanish and re-appear as you run round the zone.

The grey, eh?

I have been XP'ng here since lvl 52. At 54, I was able to solo. I don't have JBB or Fungi. I do have my epic, and that's what makes the difference here.

My recommendations are to first start near the Mons Let zone line. Pull golems first, they are easy. Snakes are next in line, but they tend to have high DR/PR which makes it difficult to DoT them (even after debuffing). Stay away from the Rockfeinds. They are too much work for the trouble they are. I also recommend staying away from the wrom-type mobs. They are called X-something, and they are difficult to root. They usually come as adds which is why they are a pain. If they are solo, then they are easy.

The snakes are the best XP (IMO). Once you're comfortable with the zone mobs. I recommend going to the temple. The snakes are the best XP. There are a lot of snakes behind the temple. It can be difficult to break the camp, but once they start coming as singles .. the XP starts moving :D. There are skellies by the temple too. Only problem with the temple is the trains - trains - trains - trains!. I stay on the back side of the temple and I'm usually out of the way from the trains (as long as the trainie know what side the entrance was on).

I have not done the zone line by scarlet. I did the Nroth East corner a few times, but the traps that trigger the boulders were too annoying. Unless you are working on a certain spawn table for a named mob, I wouldn't bother with the north east corner.

For the longest time, I did these camps w/o meleeing. Just did a lot of root-n-rot w/epic. I melee the mobs now w/pet, but the beating that I get is difficult to recover from.

My next goal is to try the sun reverants in the north west corner. They are lvl 55 mobs .. shoud be great XP. I have grouped up there and most spells stick on them. Should be fun :cool:
