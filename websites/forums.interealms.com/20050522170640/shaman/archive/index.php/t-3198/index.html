I tried all shaman BP's on, and found that regen was always better than everything else. The only time it's not is for when you need resists. But even then, it's usually a borderline call.

The real difference is torpor. I had the CoV dragon quest armor BP(far superior to the thurg BP) in the bank and kept it there before I had torpor, long after I was 60. Fungi was MUCH more useful, most everywhere, most all the time. Occasionally would bust out the CoV BP for dragons, but not always.

At lower levels, before I got my fungi, iksar BP beat all the high-wisdom/good stat/high AC chest items I tried on, and I think I used all of them. I was wearing my iksar BP till 52 or 53, and doing VERY well with it on, soloing, grouping, raiding, etc.

To make it perfectly clear, when soloing my wisdom was down to a mere 120. Does that illustrate how important wisdom is compared to regen -- even a little regen? Same with AC?

In groups I would jack up wisdom to 160-180, depending on what I felt worked best, though I had the equipment to get it up to about 220. Kept the iksar BP on, though.

A shaman will not go wrong by wearing nothing his whole career but iksar bp then fungi then, when he gets torpor, the quest armors or uber drops(Vindi BP and better). Forget froggy BP, emerald dragonscale, thurg, whatever.

Until you get torpor, regen is your god. Post torpor, kick back and look around and decide which stats you'd enjoy the best. Most people either get dragon quest armor and keep it or get it and abandon it when they get Vindi BP, and then only rarely will they have an upgrade. There are very few shaman BP's around, even fewer that are likely to go to a shaman first. Doesn't matter too much, though, there are plenty of more vital spots to upgrade.

For shamans, fungi, jbb, epic, torpor are the things that matter(we can leave out the obvious--slow). After torpor, fungi no longer matters. Malo and focus are what makes him matter to raids(chanters and even rangers can slow too).

Fill in whatever blanks you are missing there. If you're iksar, you won't be able to fill one of them, but won't need a fungi, either.
