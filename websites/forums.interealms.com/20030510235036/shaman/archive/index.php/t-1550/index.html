Donar
Maybe you should group for a while, as there are some stretches in the early days when soloing can be really painful. I remember that right before level 19 was pretty tough, since the heal spell was so weak, and between levels 29 and 34 were also tough (for soloing). Your gear does sound great for your level. Slowing the mobs may be useful to you, but keep the nukes/dots memmed as well. Maybe pull with the slow spells, add a dot, melee a bit and if you're taking too much heat, root the mob, back up a bit for either a heal or nuke the mob and then get back to meleeing. Root probably won't last long, but if you're having some success at getting it to stick, try root/dotting tough mobs. Medding for part of the fight will reduce the med times after the fight, which is a good thing. :) If this is still too slow, your best bet is to find a good group and power out a few levels and get your new spells. In general though, soloing before you get the pet is going to be slow. Regen and canni will help some at level 24, but even then, there will be quite a bit of downtime. The pet, at level 34, will really help alot.
