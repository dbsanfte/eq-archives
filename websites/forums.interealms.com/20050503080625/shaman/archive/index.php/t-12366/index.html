Xev_Grammar
I also highly recommend canni 5.. with canni 5 you can be totally and completely inefficient (i.e. nuke heavy) and still not be oom-- nuking is so fun with fury btw. Without canni 5 you won't often be oom but you also won't be doing very much other than the mandatory slows.

If you want fun stuff, I would go for AAs that give you an ability you never once had...

* Spirit Call is melee damage / distraction / caster interrupter / pusher.
* Virulent Paralysis is a very fast disease based root (as opposed to magic-based.. good to have variety) that doesn't need to wait for refresh (i.e. say I pull with turgur and it resists.. I can VP right away before spell gems ungrey). It's a real life-saver, and it can also be used to push a mob... say a mob of guildies are pushing a mob to and edge and you want them to start pushing the other way, VP it in the other direction, and the mob of guildies has to turn around and attack, thus pushing it in the other direction.
* Radiant Cure is a group-based poison/disease/detrimental cure. Very convenient since most of the time you don't have to go into your book to whip out blood of nadox, but also does something you normally can't do-- it gets rid of detrimental stuff too, like roots, heat blood, malosis, etc... you can do this with annul but you also get rid of beneficial buffs this way.
* Suspend Minion I haven't gotten yet but it is very tempting.. say you want to travel around invis.. just suspend your minion and bring him back later. Or say you want a backup pet.. suspend your minion then summon another one, and when he dies bring back your suspended one.
Spellcasting Fury and Fury of Magic allows you to crit.. people say you really should spend your AAs elsewhere.. I tend to agree, but I'm weird, and really don't regret having SCF 3 and FoM 1.. it's really fun to nuke and crit, especially since each tears of saryrn lets you have a chance to crit three times at least... it's like " resist compensation " as another shaman put it.

If you default recommended good stuff that you can't really go wrong with, definitely get MC 3 and Canni 5...
Then you want stuff like Spellcasting Reinforcement 1-2-3 and Mastery, Quickbuff 1-2 at least, Spellcasting Mastery 1-2-3, Natural Durability and Combat Agility 1-2-3, and MGB.
