see the posts in the hunting section, 50 to 60.

Katta faction is useful for the quests (Rallic pack, Shield of Bane Warding, and the EB item). Some tradeskillers hang out there, as well. Other than that - no value even to the goodie goodies.

killing regular Katta guards is fun. Outdoor zone, so sow and horse work. bank in zone (have to invis to get in there). you can sell to vendors until you go kos, and there are vendors near by, in TS.
