The ceremony had already finished by the time you got there that is why. As soon as the last spirit reaches that room a timer starts and you have a certain amount of time to reach the NPC in that room and turn in the remains to him. So, there is a few ways of delaying killing the final guy, or being really quick about getting back to the gem room, or turning in the items asap. Up to you to find the best way.
