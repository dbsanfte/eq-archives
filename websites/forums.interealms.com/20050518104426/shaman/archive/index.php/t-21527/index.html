The Hyena Shaman
Okay, well, over on the EQ live forums, we have the thread on what shaman AA we want, so I thought maybe i'd hash out this idea...

We have general, archetype, and class AA... So why not racial? As it is, the only difference between the EQ races is the models used. Bring back (or enhance) the things that make a race unique.

For instance, let's say... Barbarians. Wild but honorable animistic people living in the cold reaches of Norrath, right? They don't really have much by way of special abilities, however. So what kind of racial AA could we cook up for these poor bastards?

- Cold affinity. Rather than a simple number added to your resists, this would reduce damage and duration of cold-based spells by a percentage according to how many points ya spend on it.

- Wolf's step. Another runspeed AA, but perhaps with a snare resistance modifier.

- Snowblind Sight. Living in an eternally overcast land blanketed in blinding white snow and cofered by storms, I would think after a while barbarian's eyes adjust to bad conditions, granting ultravision.

Or, how about, oh, those bastard halflings?

- Fireside tales. In AD&D and EQ alike, halflings find themselves having to talk their way out of bad situations more often than not. this AA could either be a lull or a faction increaser, depending.

- Little Sneak. Increases the halfling's innate hide / sneak by a small % per level. OR, it could allow them to hide and sneak at the same time.

- Stupid Lack of Self Preservation. Okay, I'm not good at naming these... But this would make the halfling immune to fear spells.

Just a few ideas. Alternately, we could have a branch that enhances what you already have for your race, or grants the ability of another race. Perhaps Ogres would be immune to melee stun from all sides, while other races could buy an AA to give them a better chance of ignoring it from the front.

A branch off of this idea is Religious AA. Currently religion means jack aside from what imbued items you can wear, and certain factions (Exactly why do Aviaks hate Veeshan?)

For example...

- Retribution of the Tribunal. When activated, increases your melee and spell damage output by a level-based percentage, as you smite those who need to be smoted... Or whatever.

- Shining Glory of Mithaniel. An area effect undead fear and blind. Exactly how big of a train can you make with a button click?

- Innoruuk's Bile. No-fail taunt + taunt-generating DoT effect. Sometimes ya just REALLY need aggro.

Or...

- Apathy of Agnost the Bland. Reduced effects from spells cast by gods and their direct minions

No, no, it's nothing spectacular, just a few random ideas I popped off my head
