You sell your empty staff to a vendor.
He sells his fully charged staff to the same vendor.
Now you both buy them back.

Both staves will have full charges (the vendors only keep charge info for one of each type of item in their inventory.) This is technically an exploit, but it's been public knowledge (and widely used) for a very long time.

Jaradis
You are doing it backwards. You sell the FULL charged staff FIRST. Then the spent staff, the full charge staff will show up in the vendor's inventory first, then when you sell the spent one it stacks with the first full charged one, then you buy both back. With good faction and cha it costs 2500pp per staff.

And its not really an exploit, Verant knows it is done and has chosen not to fix it. In fact instead of raising the prices to insane prices to stop it they chose to raise it to reasonable levels to use it as a money sink. It will cost you 5kpp to get 10 charges or 500pp per rez, hardly cheap. They recently increased the price of the Locket of Escape to 22kpp to buy back. So they definately know its being done and chose not to change it. If they wanted it gone they would make the buy back be 500kpp and be done with it.
