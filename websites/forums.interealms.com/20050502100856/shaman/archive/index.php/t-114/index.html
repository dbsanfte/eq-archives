You'll really miss the shield if you use a 2hb; I know I did. Blackjacks are dirt cheap even if they have a cruddy ratio, and they're good for keeping up 1hb if you melee a lot. Centi Warhammers are also very cheap and are right around a 2 ratio as opposed to the BJ's 3. Piercing is what I'd go for first. Spine Chill Spears are fairly cheap, since they come out of the Ry`Gorr fort too.

Face: An Arctic Wyvern Mask is a nice balanced face item, and pretty cheap since most people that are working on tailoring will make lots of them.
Ears: Black Sapphire Electrum Earrings aren't much more expensive than forest loops and will serve you well for a long time (25 hp/35 mana), and if you know a jeweler you can get a pair for around 500pp.
Neck:Sabretooth Amulets are somewhat uncommon but are usually reasonably priced (15 MR and permanent See Invis). Failing that the good old Chrysoberl Talisman or Black Alloy Medallion will tide you over.
Back: I've got a Shissar cloak (AC 6, 5 wis, and 55 hp/mana) but they can be expensive and hard to find if the Grey isn't very populated on your server. Prior to that I think I had a banded cloak. =\

You didn't mention anything about a shield. Charred Guardian is still the way to go until you get a Shield of Falsehood or better, imo.
