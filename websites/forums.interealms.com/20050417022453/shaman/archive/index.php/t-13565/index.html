2 static drovlargs guarding the door
-sub note if you are to close to the edge inside they attack through the walls like in unrest
2 wandering drovlargs at front pilars
1 wandering cockatrice
2 wandering drachs (recluse is the common one a ranger class with poison dots)
standard 6 minute spawn time

I have seen a level 56 mage earth pet with lava shield get torn up with adds here.
