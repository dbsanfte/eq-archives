It's just quibbling on my part, I suppose, but I rank VT gear as superior to most PoP loot before the elemental planes. My bard is in his second VT capable guild, without ever getting in the elemental planes. I look at the drops from Tier 3 gods like the Zeks (barring rallos maybe, never inspected his loot carefully), Agnarr, Mith Marr, etc... and I don't see a darn thing I want. Usually not anything that's even a solid upgrade from my velious loot (eg cloak of the falling stars off Statue in Kael or Vindi BP), much less my various drops from Vex Thal, or my Ssra Weapons. For my weaker slots, I can still EASILY point at multiple drops in VT that will upgrade them substantially. I can't do it off the Tier 3 gods.

This is one of my pet peeves about PoP. For a VT guild, there is no substantial reward in PoP before you make the elemental planes. Sure, some of the items are good, including some of the ornate armor, but VT gear is substantially better.

HoT < NToV <= Luclin Bosses (depending on the mob) < Ssra Bosses < Lower Tier PoP bosses < Vex Thal, Emperor, Seru < Elemental Planes < Time would be my ranking.
