Strength Your strength is you weight allowance, also, the higher your strength the better the chance you will strike for maximum damage .
Dexterity Increases the ability to hit, and affects you channeling.
Agility Helps dodging and improves AC.
Charisma Helps overcome resistance when "effect" style spells such as debuffs or charms are used on a critter. Also improves faction with NPCs and helps improve prices with vendors.
Stamina Gives a few extra HP per level. Also allows you to fight longer and swim longer without getting tired.
Intelligence Improves the rate of advancing your skills.
Wisdom Determines mana store for priest classes, also affects trade skill failure rate.

Might as well fix these now while the site is being worked on ;)

Strength - Lets you carry more, Slightly increases Max Damage.
Dexterity - Affects proc rate, and helps learn offensive weapon skills more quickly (1hb, offense, etc)
Agility - Adds slight Avoidance AC, and helps learn defensive skills (dodge, defense, etc)
Charisma - Affects Charm spells, and lowers vendor prices. Otherwise useless.
Stamina - Gives more health. At level 60, one stamina is equal to three hit points gained (though there's a formula for it... I think we get 15hp per level base, with an extra 5 at first level, then the HP from stamina... that would give 905hp from levels, and 300 from stamina at 60 assuming you have 100, resulting in 1205 total.

Wisdom - Increases the max manapool for priests, and also affects fizzle rate, although only very slightly.
