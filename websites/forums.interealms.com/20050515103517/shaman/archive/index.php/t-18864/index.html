rookz
well, a benefit of disease based dots is it's limited agro. Unfortunately (though ironically fortunate in your case) our poison base dots really !@#$ off the mobs.

At your level I had some stupidly twink gear and I could WAY out meet tank most the melees I grouped with, so I MTed quite a bit. I would slow on the pull, then drowsy twice, then start swinging. Rarely would I be out agroed after two drowsies, but if I was I would just root and get up on the mob.

I didn't know about the poison dot agro by then, so I never really tried it, but by about 34 I started grouping alot, and I learned quickly that I couldn't use my poison dots in a group unless the tank could pull some serious agro. I'd easily peel mobs of decently equiped SK's at that level range after one slow and my dot, so I am pretty sure your level 24 one will do just fine.

At that point, if all that doesn't work, it's most likely the chanter who's tactics need evaluating.

Thorzion
Well, aside from rooting, your best bet on taking and holding agro is Blind.

A blinded mob will attack the closest player if in range, very simmilar to a rooted mob. Using blind the only time I ever lose agro is if I get out of range and the "run" aspect of blind kicks in. That, coupled with Root, are surefire ways to keep and maintain agro.
