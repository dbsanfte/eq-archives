Harvyst
I hunt in the back near the Twilight zone. There are 2 patches there - a 6 patch and a 5 patch. I can do the 6 patch with MC3 and kei, 6 and 5 if I have Spiritual Radiance or PoTG on top. Once I get canni V, I expect to handle both easily.

Finding a good spot isn't as easy as it sounds. For instance, there is one patch near the right on your way to the back, where if you stand near the wall, the shrooms go back OVER the other way down the hill before coming back. Talk about wierd pathing.

Avoid the lotuses, and I'm not sure about the mutants or the gorangas. I should try conning them sometime to see how bad it would be.
