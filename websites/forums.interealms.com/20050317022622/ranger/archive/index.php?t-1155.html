Personally I'd suggest Mistmoore. As dead as the place commonly is on my server, it's great for solo/duo without the constant risk of other people's trains. Grouping here can be fun too but it's strictly BYOG. It has some sort of xp modifier (being a dungeon), but you can use SOW & Camo & such. The entryway is great as early as 22 or so, and as you get tougher eventually you can move on to the graveyard. Harmony is all you need to keep the spawns there split (great place to learn how to use it right), and the few trains that do go by will rarely path through there. Oh, and the mobs are fairly rich... esp in the GY (plat loot commonly and lots of FS/Ringmail).
