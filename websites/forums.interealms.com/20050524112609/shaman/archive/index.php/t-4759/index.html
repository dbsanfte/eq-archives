Barm - I've done this when grouped with the person who died at the time they died. You can still target them (their corpse) as long as you were in the zone when they died, haven't left it since, they were bound elsewhere, and they haven't zoned back in yet. Used this to find a friend's body at the bottom of the OOT once...luckily it was within sight of recognizable land.
