Widda
Well, it depends. How is your giant faction, how is your dorf faction. What I did, was team up with another shaman friend and duo the whole quest. I made a lev 8 Troll War Twink (for the Kael portion). Here is a quick run through:

1.) I started by killing massive quantities of Coldain Tracking Wolves until each of us had a Blood Wolf spawn and we got our harness (dropable)

2.) Gave War Twinky the harness, ran him to Kael and turned the Harness in to Giant Wolf Tamer. Received Giant version of it.

3.) Ran to Crystal Caverns, had main shammy slaughter orcs until Bottle of Karsin dropped. Twink looted bottle.

4.) Twink ran to Turgadin and handed in bottle. Received Gem.

5.) Ran Twink to Kael (not that hard, remember he is Non KoS to both dorfs and giants) and turned in gem, received giant version of gem. Turned in giant version of gem and giant version of harness to same mob (Giant Wolf tamer), received Controll Harness.

6.) Ran back to GD. Ran main Shaman to Dorf miner cave and logged her there. Had partner invis up my War twinky. War Twinky goes into the Shardwurm Cave and turns in the Controll Harness to Broodmother. Log main Shaman back in.

7.) Korf the Dorf respawns outside the miner cave as a "blue to 51" mob. Wait until the Broodmother arrives, root him dot him nuke him. He hits for up to 117, and is easily done with dog dog.

8.) Loot your new bracer.

Couple comments. The final fight can be done rather easily solo 50+ When we did it the second time, two Korfs spawned and aggroed us. That would have been ugly solo. Only one of them dropped the bracer. I think the quest can be done solo, only challenge is to get someone to invis you twink. If anyone on RN needs help with the quest, my Faction Twink is happy to help.

Widda
From all the posts, I have determined that the final step, the fight with Korf is the one spot that has potential to get things really wrong. Here is my tip of the day to ensure success at that stage.


1.) Set up. Face the Coldain Mining Cave. Turn right 45 degrees. You see a hill, set up (dogdog, etc) on top of that hill.

2.) Korf. Korf will spawn right outside the Cave at the bottom of the hill. Target him (don't attack).

3.) Broodmother. The Shardwurm Broodmother will come from the left of the cave (when facing it). Do not attack her. You should still have Korf targeted when she comes wandering.

4.) Korf, the second coming. A lot of people do not know when to start fighting Korf. This is actually very easy. Target Korf before the BM arrives, you will know when the right Korf is up as soon as you lose your target. Retarget and root the bum. Another advantage of your set up spot is that Korf is actually running towards you, no need to chase.


I hope this helps.
