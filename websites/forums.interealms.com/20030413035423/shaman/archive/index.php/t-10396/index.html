Goldark
Smoke, the epic damage is described above - it increases per tick :)

I parsed alpha 61 dog with celerity and mask doing 30-35dps on the golems in PoI Factory - which are fairly high AC mobs. That's excluding any procs.
BoN - ~30DPS
Epic - ~10 DPS
JBB - ~30 DPS

Total: 105 dps.

Given that you start the fight with debuffs, and the cast times of spells/clickies, I think a total of about 75 DPS is a reasonable conservative estimate. Once you get canni V you may find you stop the JBB casting so much and start to use rains instead, or at least one rain and then back to JBB.

Any healing and such is obviously going to cut into the JBB damage, but the nice thing is that the pet, epic and BoN just keep on dishing the damage while you can do other stuff.

Of course this is all hypothetical for a number of reasons:
1) Aggro - you'll need one hell of a good tank to slow and BoN right off the bat and follow that up with a rain spell.
2) Healing, buffing etc all cuts into time spent dishing damage.
3) Mobs killed before epic reaches efficiency

Don't kid yourself, we're not really a great DPS class (the equivalent mage pet does 60dps or so, beastlord probably more with the procs, necro I dunno), even warriors are making 100dps now, but we can make a decent contribution particularly on the tougher PoP mobs with lots of hp, and when we have a good taunter for MT.
