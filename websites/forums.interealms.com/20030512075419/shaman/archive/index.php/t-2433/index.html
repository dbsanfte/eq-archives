Here is what I (Lvl 36) was doing with some Newbies:

SoW
Quickness
Regen
Talisman of T'Narg

Took (2) Newbies into EF Tundra (1=Lvl 5 Warrior & 1=Lvl 10 Shaman)...Cast the above mentioned on them and let them go at it. I basically sat and med while they fought. Once they got their Mob to <50% I would turn loose my Pet to take them out. Seemed to work very well. I know Using this strategy got the Lvl 5 upto 10 in about 2 hours (He could have been higher but by splitting my mana pool between the 2 of them was a bit of a strain). I would guess he could have been taken up about 8-10 lvls if I had only him to assist.

Does anyone else have any strategy to accelerate training of Newbies. I apologize to those who don't like the morality of PLing but I don't have a problem with it.
