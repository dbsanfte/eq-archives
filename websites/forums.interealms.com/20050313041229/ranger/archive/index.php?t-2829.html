People saying "only bard song haste stack" are very misinformed. Every haste spell a bard gets(except Vah'shir/Ervaj) also adds str or agi or atk, and since the str/agi/atk takes hold, they assume that the haste stacks as well. This is incorrect.

The ONLY bard song hastes which stack are Battlecry of the Vah'shir(level 52), Warcry of the Vah'shir(level 60), Melody of Ervaj(level 50), Composition of Ervaj(level 60).

The way haste works is this. One item haste(whichever item you're wearing that has the highest percent), One spell(whether it be your cloak, verses of victory from the bard, shissar from the enchanter, doesn't matter) haste(whichever spell has the highest percent), one Vah'shir/Ervaj bard song(ONLY these songs).
