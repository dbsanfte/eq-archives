If your goal is to hit Hate second the fastest way is the following:

A) Break in from SW safe spot. Hardest part is if you have a rat in the gate house, you will need to dispatch it and re-inviso for the run. If you have no characters under 59 (58 really) then you are safe to run high on the wall without the south patrol route agroing on you.

B) From the SW safe spot, clear the kira and any vissible banshee.

C) Have your monk FD at the North tag point and have your rouge scout the path to Mastero. Set up your standard Warrior/Ranger tag team. From there on it's just standard monk pulling a pile while the ranger Harmonies the stack and the warrior tags singles. Normally you want to re-harmony the pile on each FD to make sure things are always sans assist radius.

D) Chain Pull everything up to the front of Mastero's place, then clear the revs on the other side of the ramp. Pull a small pocket out of the Mastero chapel.

E) Pull up the ramp and wack any extra ashenbones that are sitting there as well as any minis in the old Inny spot.

F) Move the group up (inviso/non-inviso it doesn't honestly matter) and park up around the corner from the ramp.

You won't get first floor adds from here and you can easily work your way around second without agro from first.

Normally a straight shot to second takes about 20min if people know what they are doing and you have good numbers. With five groups practically don't even need to worry about tagging singles, but it does help to spread things out a little. The reason you want to be quick is so your roamers don't respawn (roamers == wasted time)
