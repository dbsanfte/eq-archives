Most of the dorfs are flagged unslowable. And they are uber-resistant. And if you get thru that resistance, and get their attention, a 44 shammy won't last more than 2 rounds.

When hunting for faction, you typically are pulling the royals, which requires a flopper (monk or SK usually). A good group of 60s in good gear can do the royals, but most people require 2-3 groups of mid-50+ people. Killing the royal dorfs will give Tormax hits, which are hard to find, so you can find people doing faction work here occasionally.

Someone looking for just exp can do ok with a 60/60 shammy/warrior combo. But there are much better exp zones, not to mention the loot in Icewell from yardtrash dorfs is horrid.

If you are talking about the monsters and such below the castle, i can't comment. Never killed them except when down there for a Dain raid.
