Chazic
Just got though killing the Courier yesterday. So thought I would give you the info I figured out though trial and Running hell bent for zone.

The Sables are to your left after crossing the bridge and entering the gate.

To get there for lower level people I discovered a nice little trick to breaking the spawns near the zone in if you have mix between undead and living monsters (Ghost like mobs do not see inv so I think of them as live). Buff your self up like your ready to fight and then set your summon pet button up so you can summon as soon as you need him. Next cast regular invisiblity on your self. Now slowly work you way down the entrance hallway till you can tell what type of mob is standing just to the side of the door way with out agroing it. If your lucky you will have one live one dead. With Invisibley up you will only agro the undead mob till he hits you or your invis drops.

Time out here
Little bit spell knowledge here that is not common knowledge to some people. The spells that provide you with invisiblity to a type of monster will prevent agro of those types monsters even though a monster that it is social to runs by it that is agroed on you.... Hummm not sure if that was clear. Example: Mob A (is undead), Mob B (is Not Undead); You are running with Invisiblity up and you have monters agroed on you. If the monster chasing you runs by Mob B it will not agro due to the fact it can not see you. Now if you ran by a Mob A then it would agro on you as it can see you.

Though in certain spots its not the best idea to be running around with out our trusty pet. Once those first two mobs are dead its quite easy to start pulling the ones at the gate. I found I could pull them 80% time single with just casting a slow on them and had lots room on the bridge to separte them with root. In the stables area most every room had sets of two mobs behind each door. The trick for me was to get them out and to an area away from any possibility of an add.

In the stable areas I found most of the monsters at level 50 would not jump me from a distance right away unless I stopped and staired at them for a sec. So you should be able to step up to the doors at max range and open them with the U Key. Then slowing the one you see or rooting it.

The area to the left as you enter the stables you need to enter carfully. As you enter that stable area there will be two doors behind you as soon as you enter the courtyard so dont stand still at all just hang a right and head for the wall behind the broken wagon. If you start a train at this point I would just trun back around and run for zone. :D If your lucky you will not have gotten anything mad at you. Now the door closest to you will have two mobs in there. I would suggest you try to root one inside and pull the other out to kill it behind the wagon near the wall. Next pull the two monsters behind the other door. Then there are Three on the back wall one on the left and two on the right one up top and one down bottom. I'ld fight them on thier spawns as it seems the safest way to do it.

The area to the right as you enter the stables I pulled to the main hall in the stables as I found I didnt get any adds there and that area seemed to get more adds if I tried to fight in the courtyard area.
