in archery...

- the ability for you to hit a 'magic required' mob, like ghouls and will-o-wisps, is based on the arrow having the MAGIC attribute, the bow does not effect this ability (however, a non-magic arrow will still agro the target, even though it can not do any actual damage)

- the range that you can shoot with a bow/arrow is the range of the bow plus the range of the arrow. So if the bow range is 150 and the arrow range is 100, the maximum shooting range is 250.

- archery is a secondary combat skill, like off-hand and kick are secondary skills, so there is no bonus damage when hitting, and the minimum hit can be for 1 point of damage.

- the damage amount that is calculated for the hit uses the bow dmg plus the arrow dmg added together as the damage output of the weapon. So if the bow is dmg 18, delay 45 and the arrow is dmg 3, then each shot is calculated as if the attack was with a 21/45 weapon.
