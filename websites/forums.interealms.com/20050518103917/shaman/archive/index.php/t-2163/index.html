3 waves of 13 or 14 mobs. ---plus the room mobs (3 or 4 of them) continue to respawn throughout.

first 2 waves come about every 45 seconds
3rd waves every 30 seconds

to trigger put someone in the shackles at the end of the platform overlooking the water, off the high priest room.

try searching this board for Acrylia AND priest - try safehouse too.

We tried with a single grp - 60 bard, 60 shaman, 60 monk, 59 warrior, 60 ranger, 59 cleric. Middle of the road weapons and equipment and we lacked the damage to finish the third wave and had to run for it when the final boss and room mobs respawned with the last 4 or 5 of the wave.
