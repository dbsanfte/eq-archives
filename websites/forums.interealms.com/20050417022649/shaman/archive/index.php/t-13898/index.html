Kozul
just mem both, and what I do is throw malosi at them on incoming, whether it lands or not, slow next. If malosi lands, I never had a slow resist, if it's resisted, I still land slow about 75% of the time. Because of recast time, if slow is resisted as well, then I try malosi again. The second malosi gets resisted once in a while, but I cant remember the second slow ever getting resisted, regardless of whether the 2nd malosi lands or not. I know at 55, I probably don't need to worry about malosi, as I'd guess the chances of either landing independently is about the same, but if you slow on incoming, and you dont have a SK or pally, they're coming right at you (and even with them in group sometimes they still will). If I lead with malosi and wait a few seconds, I rarely get aggro, and if I do no big deal, we can take a few hits :). Also, I usually have cripple memmed too, rarely use it, maybe if we get an add or 2 and our tanks are getting reamed, but I use it mainly to get agro, for the times when the eager wizzy decides to nuke for like 1500 right away (fortunately this don't happen much...but mobs do like to chew on the clerics if they have to CH early in the fight).


I have only been to tier 2 like a handful of times, most of my time in the planes so far has been spent in PoN, last 4-5 levels or so, plus stopping at 55 to get run3 and regen3, so my advice reflects what has worked there for me. Could be different in tier2, but hey, a lot of the fun in EQ is going to new spots, getting adjusted to it, even dieing a bunch of times finding the right way to work it. Since I been back to leveling, I now go to PoD, better xp, but harder mobs, they're generally a few levels higher than PoN, so slowing is a little harder, get some more resists, only thing I changed is I'm less likely to try a slow unless I do land the malosi, but it's a judgment call really, there's going to be a lot of times your group is getting beat on, and it's critical to at least try to get the slow to stick. Same with adds, if you don't have an enchanter or bard for mezz, I try to slow them all ASAP (root too, but you know how root always breaks at the worst time, you'll need them slowed in case - plus, at least in PoN and PoD, I notice root has an average lasting time of probably 5 seconds) so I don't usually bother with anything but slow (If I only get one mob on me, Ill try to channel off a stoicism, if I get more than one on me, there's no bother really...I'd doubt I'd even get the spell off with 2 unslowed PoP mobs on me, and it's not like 180 a tick will help against 2 hitting for 300+.....I'm praying the cleric is on her toes, or that I can channel the damn slow and/or root :) ). I don't go to PoI, xp is crappy for the difficulty of the mobs IMO and the few times I been there wound up in really pitiful groups.
