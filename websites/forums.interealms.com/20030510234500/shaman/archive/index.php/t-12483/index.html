Landragoran Heronwing
Dulak Harbor would be great for you at your current level. The mobs there only have about 2500 hps at most on average, are blue, and don't hit THAT hard anyway . . .plus they drop lewt which you are gonna need. Also you get your quest piece for Stoicism (Mini Torpor, lvl 44, u need this spell badly) from drunken pirates there. You must purchase LoY to get this spell, as the spell is obtained only by quest, and the scroll itself is NO DROP.

That is what I would work on if I were you. And once you feel up to it, take a partner and go to Crypt of Nadox for more fun and gather your ingredients for Blood of Nadox. That's your other LoY quest spell, level 52 spell.

Personally I think that hunting in LoY is much easier than hunting in lower tier planes in PoP, and much more profitable money wise as well. The stuff there randomly drops gems. I have seen blue diamonds drop for instance. Also iksar blood drops from iksars and I guess that sells well in bazaar to tradeskillers. The exp per mob is less, but you can kill many mobs in the time it takes to kill one mob in PoP. I would highly recommend heading to LoY to hunt in your 50's.
