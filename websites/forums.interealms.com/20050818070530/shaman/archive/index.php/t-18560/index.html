Shamans get this wonderful ability called SLOW. Slow actually slows time itself to 25% of its normal rate, so if root breaks you have 4X as long to recast root, and it takes the mob 4X as long to reach you than if it wasn't slowed. This is definately a much better ability than snare.

btw, that was sarcasm.

Shamans do not need snare. If a mob becomes unrooted, we can do one of two things:

1) run away until we have enough distance to recast root. This is easier for us than a necro because we have sow.

2) just let the mob hit you a few times and channel a root, then back up. This is easy for a shaman to do, since we mitigate damage much better than a robed caster, and the mob is SLOWED (see above).

Another thing you can do is just stand far enough away from the mob that you have enough time to cast root before it reaches you.

One final thing of note: All but our highest dots break root due to a DD component, so root isn't a very viable way of soloing. I am assuming that you are primarily interested in soloing? If I am correct, then you should pick necro because they have all the tools you will need to do it well from level 1 to 65. About the sow thing, in another thread you were asking about some of the more expensive horses, so I am guessing you have a bit of plat. If that is the case, you can easily afford mass quantities of sow potions (1k will buy you enough sow potions to keep yourself sowed for 3 days straight).
