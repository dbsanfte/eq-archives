Tarthug
Landed Malosi before at 51 while doing Vox, never landed slow. If you check the shaman sloawable list, you'll see that Vox is immune to changes in attack speed. For the record, it took me like 5 tries to land Malosi before it stuck. The biggest thing IMHO with Vox is a sound strat (keeping respawn down while killing the cow, can be done through great timing) and a decent DPS. Mana sieving is great if you're short on bashers, but as long as you got a truck load of bashers your GTG. Organizing your buffs can be important so that resists are always protected. Getting a decent resist helps, but this is easily matched by going in with a lot of levels. This is a much easier fight when you're 52 than when you're 45. Lots more stuff, but that covers the basics. As a shaman, you'll get resists no matter what you cast, unless you got your epic.
