Amise
A friend and I have been PLing his shaman recently for use in PoP. We went for outdoor areas out of convenience more than anything. 25-30 we did tizmaks in GD and cougars in IC, then moved to Dawnshroud until 35 and killed zelniaks and lightcrawlers. 35-40 were spent in Scarlet Desert killing cheetahs, sunflowers and lightcrawlers.

Avoid casters as much as possible. They are mana-suckers particularly when PLing, since the mobs tend to be much higher in level than the character you're PLing, and spells are rarely resisted.
Kuil
I brought my Cleric up to 36 I think in Sol A. I would run around and grab 4 or 5 Goblins with the ranger bring them back to the cleric and let them beat on me until the DS wore them down. Then the cleric would hit them with a couple AE nukes and clean up the mess. You need to keep track of the goblins you are pulling to make sure they are dark blue to cleric. Also, I would kill any casters before pulling back to the cleric. You can kill Gnomes in Sol A past 36 but they seemed to be camped alot.

Once she hit 36 she gained exp while grouped with my 55 Ranger. We headed to CC and she watched as I tore up Geos and Spiders. We could have headed here earlier than 36 and killed Orcs using same methods for Goblins in Sol A.

Once she got CH we headed to entrance of KC.
