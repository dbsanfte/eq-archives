Beliael
It seems a lot harder than it really is...

I started trying at level 60 and was never quite able to pull it off. Once I got to 62 and the new quick heal it became much easier. First kill was rough, had to root the wurm and let my pet tank him until I could get some health and mana back. Every other fight since then has been fairly straightforward. Had KEI blow during one fight, didn't make much of a difference. On 2 separate occasions hit MYSELF with epic dot and didn't have any problems.

I would start off with trying the GWs that spawn at VP... dealing with the wandering ones near the Ragefire spawn point has its own set of problems. Typically I set my pet at the back part of the finished area a top VP entrance. Just hit him with haste, if I happen to have some extra weapons I give those to him. For personal buffs, virtue is a must for me at this point, preferably have KEI but isn't needed. Hit myself with focus, regrow, sta, VoTB, regrow and Lev. Cann back to fm. Float down to front of VP and wait until the wandering mob is away from the GW. Pull with malo(not needed but makes life easier), pull him along the side(doesn't matter which side) root him, then slow, cripple, insidious decay. Keep him rooted while you regain mana, if needed root again. Once root breaks and you're ready pull him back up on to the top of VP. I usually position myself with my back to the dragon statue. Dogdog should be as close to directly behind the wurm as possible otherwise he tends to push the wurm too much. Keep torpor on yourself at all times, if you're running close to full health hit cann4 a bit. I have cann5 and usually hit myself with Tnargs mending after using it. Just cycle through dots as needed, bane, pox and epic. If running a little low on mana drop bane out for a few. Epic is a pain in the butt to land sometimes due to the casting time but nothing beats free damage. Generally takes me 5-7 minutes. Usually reslow between 45 and 60%.

One thing I have noticed... there seem to be 3 different variants of GWs. All con the same, but some run low 30s dps, others mid 40s and still a tougher crowd that runs in the high 50s. The high 50s variant can be very difficult.

In my experience doing it without torpor would be sheer luck for all but the best equipped, KM simply takes too long to cast. As is I have to use a wicked bone atlat(casting haste) to get torpor in between hits consistently(don't have quick buff yet). Affliction haste and efficiency are the other focus effects I have. oh yeah and have a horsie too!!

Usually run between 3900 and 4700 hps. Using VoTB and virtue my ac is about 1130. Have about 3600 mana. Don't have any avoidance or mitigation AAs.

Good Hunting!! Its a fun fight and quite an accomplishment.
