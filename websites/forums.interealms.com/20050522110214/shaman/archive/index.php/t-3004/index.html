Grippa
The facts about horses:

You meditate while sitting or standing, but NOT while autoattacking, or moving. You do meditate while casting spells. This applies whether horse models are on or off.

You always regenerate hitpoints at your STANDING rate. This applies whether models are on or off.

If you turn off horse models, you don't get the movement benefit of the horse. Nor do you get the safe fall. BUT you can then SoW yourself for normal SoW speed.

Horses are hungry beasts and eat your food. This applies with models on or off.

Horses are buffs and use a buff slot. They can be dispelled or clicked off.

Horses can only be used in outdoor zones. Unlike SoW, if you enter an indoor zone, your horse will be dispelled.

If you camp while on a horse, you go linkdead (obviously a bug). Click off your horse buff before camping.

Some people have reported problems when using invis on a horse. To be safe, click off the horse before invissing.

Some people have reported problems with targetting people on a horse. You may find that while you have a horse summoned, other group members can't cast spells on you, or trade with you.


That's about all there is to know I think. If you just want a horse for the med benefit, buy the cheap one for about 8400pp (with high CHA). I've had one for a few weeks now and it's worth every copper. My personal opinion is that it's better for grouping than soloing, but if you have a JBB a horse is probably a great solo tool also.
