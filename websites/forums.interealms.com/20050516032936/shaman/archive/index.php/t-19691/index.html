TPFKAFaern
Glori (BTW NC STATE had ANOTHER stellar FB recruiting class), you should have no problems. I was doing Halas 10 pound pies with a skill fo 194 (They trivial at 226 iirc).

Some hints on the new UI.

It will open (initially) every time you hit a TS "combiner." (I panicked initially when I couldn't open my Coldain Tanners kit to get at something). From then on it's CNTRL-R-Click (Left) and the container opens up with a blank listing and your skill. Note that Gerloks and their % add ARE shown when you equip them. (As a sum total and very cool).

When you want to make something type it in (The interface is very intuitive) and see if the recipe shows up. If it does NOT show up then you hit the 'experiment' key, make it (You have to be successful) once. You will get a message:

"You have learned the X recipe."

Then ADD that to your favorites.

From then on you can make it at will.

Couple of notes.

1) The container you are using in your inventory does NOT have to be empty. Nor does it have to have the items you are combining.

2) You can blow through things VERY fast. So beware the failures spikes. (TS'ers know what I mean).

All-in-all, VERY nice. Sorry they got Alchemy FUBARED, as far as I can tell - baking is working nicely.
