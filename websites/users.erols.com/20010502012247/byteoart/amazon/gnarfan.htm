Gnarfan

About Me

My parents were powerful necromancers in the dark city of Neriak. I was being trained in their chosen art when Innoruk himself called all Dark Elves to a battle against the forces of light.

My parents, dedicated wholly to their evil god, immediately joined the dark army that gathered in Nektulous and marched upon Freeport. They believed that Innoruk would make them invincible and protect them from harm at the hands of the light. Innoruk did not seem to notice, however, that in his battle, many of his followers died the final death. Many, including my beloved parents, lost their lives in satisfying Innoruk's endless hate and desire to destroy the light.

Angry and disbelieving that the god of my people could be so callous of their lives and dedication, I turned my back forever on Innoruk and on the art that he advocated. The circles of Necromancers that had previously accepted me as an acolyte now scorned me; I had forsaken the proudest arts of Neriak.

Fleeing from their derision, I took refuge in the Tower of the Spurned. This was the bastion of all Neriak's magical crafts except Necromancy. As my heart and soul healed, the Enchanters of the Tower noticed my ability to persuade the merchants to give me the best prices, and my eerie talent of fading into the shadows and surroundings. They led me to the depths of the Tower and taught me the art of Enchanting.

Finally, I had found my vocation! As a young enchantress, I could access any city of Norrath and leave the dark caves of Neriak that held so many painful memories. As soon as I learned how to emulate the forms of the other races, I began to wander Norrath.

One day, when returning from a journey to Oggok, I met a young Necromancer named Serunga. Despite my lowly status in Neriak society, we became friends. Slowly, despite the stigma against it, we became entwined in each other's hearts. I taught him how to feel emotions other than hate, and he helped to soothe the pain of my parents' deaths and my status. I now live mainly in Neriak to be with my love, and I am working to learn more about Enchanting.

My Guild
I have found a band of sisters in my chosen guild, the Amazonian Sisters, and with their companionship and that of Serunga, my heart and soul are healed once more. I fight fiercely for any sister in need.

My Trades

In addition to my studies in Enchanting, I am an aspiring student of the art of jewelcrafting, and the joys of cooking.
