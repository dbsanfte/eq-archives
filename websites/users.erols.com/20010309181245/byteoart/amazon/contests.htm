*Rotating Hosts!

I realized one thing while I ran this contest (other than the low entry numbers). I noticed that I had the position of not being able to enter the contest. It got worse...I'll never be able to enter it, and I'll always be the one shelling out the prize. Hardly seems fair, right?

Well, here's my solution:
Each month, one Officer will be the Contest Host. That officer comes up with a contest, finds the prize (no less than 10 plat value, please), and hosts the contest. Entries will be sent to the month's Host. The officer, on the last day of the month, will decide who's the winner, send me all entries, marking whose entry is the victory piece, and handle giving the prize away. I will post the winner and put up the other entries (if applicable). Then the next officer takes the Host position, and we start anew.

Guild Officers will be required to get me the contest idea and tell me what the prize will be within 3 days of being named "New Host." Hosts cannot enter the contest they participate in. This way, everyone gets to judge, every officer participates, and best of all, every officer gets to submit entry forms for most of the contests...the best of all possible worlds!
