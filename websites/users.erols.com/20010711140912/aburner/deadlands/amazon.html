Cost: 5

Description: Paul Bunyan? Goliath? Hercules? Midgets, the lot of 'em.

Your character has a +2 to his/her size. And it ain't from fat, either. Your character must have a minimum strength of 3d10 to take this edge.

Your character also gets a +2 bonus to all overawe rolls in which the target is unarmed and within reach.
