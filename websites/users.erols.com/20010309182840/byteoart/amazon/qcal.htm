Halfling Appreciation Day
This whole day is to celebrate halflings and all that they bring to our life. During this day, we will "befriend" a halfling (or a few), give gifts, take them hunting, and basically make them feel as special as they really are. Each month we will celebrate a different race.

Initiation Meeting, 8 EST
The meeting shall be an Initiation Ceremony, and a "Ceremonial Dance" contest. Bring your own beer, but please, remember: Guzzling should be reserved for after the business end of the meeting. For this meeting, I need a volunteer to make whips or procure cheap ones. Tailors or wealthy people with nothing else to do with their coin would be perfect for this task. We need about a zillion (heh) of them. Comhi and/or the "Storyteller" will officiate.
