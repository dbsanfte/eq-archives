How I got started in this crazy hobby:

In the days of my youth (pronounced "ute," a la Joe Pesci in "My Cousin Vinny") I stumbled across a book in my local library which discussed and reviewed a number of the then-popular wargames. One of the sections of the book was "Fantasy Wargames," and it included a discussion of the (at the time) recently released game called "Dungeons and Dragons." I was intrigued, so I asked my mom (I was 11 at the time) to buy me the AD&D Monster Manual. Of course, when I got it, I couldn't do a thing with it because I didn't have the actual rules. Eventually, I rectified this situation and started playing AD&D.
