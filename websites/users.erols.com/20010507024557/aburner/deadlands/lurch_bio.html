How I got started in this crazy hobby:

Waaay back in the summer of '78, a buddy of mine said, "Hey, I just got this great new game called Dungeons & Dragons; wanna play?" I was instantly hooked, and went on to become a voracious devourer of All Things Gaming. Even during the lean times when I didn't have a gaming group, I had my nose in one rulebook or another (& I still do...)
