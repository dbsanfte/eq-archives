How I got started in this crazy hobby:

I'm an undercover agent of the 700 Club spying on you godless heathens and blackhearted satanist... err... I mean at a young age I was fascinated with Myths and Monsters and saw some metal miniatures at the local hobby store. I begged my parents to buy them for me. It was a Grenadier pack of Wood Elves with Spears. Many packs of miniatures later (both Heritage and Grenadier) I saw a new book in the hobby store called the Monster Manual. Thinking this would be interesting I begged to get this too. Yes, that's right, I got into the hobby in just about the same way as Afterburner did, but please don't hold it against me...
