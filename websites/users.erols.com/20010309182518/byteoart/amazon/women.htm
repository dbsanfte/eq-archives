It is my belief that whether you are male or female does not matter as long as both genders respect each other. This does not mean one should not be proud of one's differences.

Equality is a delicate topic. I cannot bring myself to hold with the idea that women are superior, that the only way to gain equality is to dominate. Likewise, I do not see men as superior, and would hate to feel dominated by that gender.

The only true way to prevent ignorance is through knowledge. The only way to know what rights you have as a woman is to learn them.

I feel very strongly that a woman's body is her own to choose what to do with, however, I would always choose closer to my heart...and for life. I cannot force another to follow in my belief, nor would I wish to.

I burn with anger every time I hear of a woman being beaten by her spouse, and strive to encourage them to learn that they do not have to put up with it. No woman deserves to be hit.

In my goal to educate, I have gathered these links to give you knowledge, open your eyes, and let you form your own beliefs.

Although "Amazonian Sisters" is a group of female characters played by both the male and the female, and is a fantasy group, I feel the knowledge important enough to give you access to it.
