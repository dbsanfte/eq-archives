Welcome!

In my wanderings through cyberspace, I have occasionally encountered pages dedicated to answering questions of a technical nature. These pages take the form of an on-going discussion in which someone posts a question and then others answer it. (As an example, check out the Windows Weenie page.)

After seeing that, I thought "Why not make something like that for conservatives?" And so I have. This is the Refutation Finder. You may post your questions or comments here, with the ultimate goal being to amass a collection of both facts and rhetorical gambits of interest to the conservative.

Note, however, that I do not yet know CGI scripting. Thus, there will be a delay of a day or so between the time you post something here and the time it actually appears. Note also that I maintain editorial privilege, and certain submissions may be rejected. The purpose of the Refutation Finder is to serve as a clearing house for facts, figures, statistics, and debating strategies. If you want uncontrolled anarchy, visit any of the alt.politics.* newsgroups.

Those two caveats aside, feel free to jump right in! The goal is to make this page the most comprehensive collection of Truth on the Net!
