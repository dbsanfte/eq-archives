Heesun

About Me

I have to admit that I have led a sheltered life, that is, at least until my 16th birthday. I was born into a noble family in the city of Freeport, my father being a well-known paladin in the local Hall of Truth. I was able to grow up without a care in the world, oblivious to the evil that spawned all around me.
But as anyone more than passingly familiar with Freeport knows, it is a city with a dark secret. The Freeport militia, while posing as guardians of the people, are really running the town as a center for their illicit activities. My father, along with the other members of the Hall of Truth, tried for years to shut them down. But alas, his diligent activities against the militia attracted the attention of the militia leader himself, Sir Lucan, who slayed my father before my very eyes during my sweet 16 party. That day changed forever my life as I had known it. Although I still feel that my view of the world is naiive and I have much to learn, I realized then that I needed to know more about what was going on in the world around me, both good and evil. I took to studying the arcane sciences, in the hopes of someday getting my revenge against the Freeport Militia, and especially Sir Lucan, for the brutal murder of my father. However, the militia is very strong, and I must be patient....

My Fiancee
My biggest personal interest before the death of my father was flirting with and gossiping about boys. After that tragedy, however, I became seriously engrossed in my studies of the arcane. I did manage to meet a wonderful paladin, by the name of Jimicapone, who proposed to me for the first time in the heat of a battle. I, of course, could not believe his lack of romantic timing, so I rejected his proposal, despite being attracted to him. But we continued to fight the evils of Freeport together, killing as many of them as we could. I couldn't help but admire his single-minded determination to rid Freeport of the militia - it reminded me so much of my beloved father. Alas, he killed one guard too many, and is now Kill-On-Sight in Freeport. It was because of his inability to enter Freeport freely, that he called for me to meet him outside the city gates one beautiful starlit night. Under the full moon, he got on his knees, pulled out a beautiful magic ring, and proposed to me. Of course, this time, I had to say yes. We have not yet set a date, as we wish to be a bit more experienced before we attempt to establish our new household and lives together. Because of his KOS status with the militia, we are planning to move to Queynos after the wedding, so that we can advance our abilities more conveniently. The purpose, of course, is to return to Freeport later, powerful enough to exact our revenge on the evil Freeport Militia.

My Guild
I am the Light Arts Officer for the Amazonian Sisters, a guild exclusively for women. We are here to help and support all citizens of Norrath, but we feel that we women need to stick together. If you are interested in knowing more about our guild or joining, please check out the page.

My Trades

In addition to being a student of the arcane arts, I am also an aspiring jeweler. I have reached a moderate degree of expertise, and can manufacture enchanted silver jewelry with some ease. Advancing this skill is very expensive, but I hope someday to become a master jeweler. I just love jewelry!
