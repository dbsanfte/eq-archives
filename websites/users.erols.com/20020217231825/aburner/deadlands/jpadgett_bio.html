How I got started in this crazy hobby:

Back in 1980 I saw an article in the Sunday Express Magazine (Don't blame me, I can't help it if my parents are Mr & Mrs Conventional.) about this "new" & "groovy" (Well, thats how we talked at the time. Well, we did in MY neighbourhood when we were pretending we were someone cool like 'Starsky & Hutch'. Oh, the innocence of it all!) game from America called Dungeons & Dragons. I kept going on about this so much that my parents caved in and I got one Basic D&D boxed set for Christmas.
