TN: 5
Speed: 5 minutes
Duration: Special
Range: Touch

Everyone knows the story of how Jesus fed the multitudes with only a few loaves of bread and a few fishes. The Blessed can call upon their Deity to partially reproduce the ol' Loaves 'n Fishes miracle.

The Blessed character must already have enough food to feed a full meal to one person. If the character doesn't have any food to start with, he's outta luck. The Blessed character must also be the person cutting and serving the food.

A successful faith roll means that the power of the Blessed's patron has miraculously multiplied the available food. Each success and raise feeds an additional two folks. (So a simple success would feed three people, a raise would feed five hungry folks, and so forth.)
