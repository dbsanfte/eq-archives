Amazon Alternate Lands Application

I have received many requests from women on other servers regarding starting a branch of the Amazonian Sisters.

If you are interested, please read these brief rules, and fill out the form below.

1) The Queen of each server is the guild-leader. Structure shall remain as it is in this parent server. The Queen of Bertoxxulous is the High Queen, and will be given moderator's rights on all boards for other servers, so she may ensure the behavior fits the mold of the originals.

2) All branches of the Amazonian Sisters will follow the rules posted in the Charter. There will be no men-bashing, no insults, no hate, and no Pkilling. We are a role playing Guild. This excludes us from the Pkilling Servers, but so be it.

3) All branches will create their own web site, but use the same "Amazonian Sisters" logo found on our front page (the animated one.) This logo will link back to this site. Sisters on other servers are welcome to join our boards, as long as they specify in their signature which server they are from.
