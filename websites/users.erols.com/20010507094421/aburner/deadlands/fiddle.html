Cost: 3

Description: Your character must've eaten all her veggies as a kid. Whatever the reason, she never seems to stay down long. Treat all her wounds as if they were one level lower for the purposes of healing only. For example, if her highest wound level is a critical wound, treat it as a serious wound for all healing attempts (be they her own attempts at natural healing, or be they attempts by 3rd parties to use a healing hex, a blessed miracle, or what-have-you).

This edge is not cumulative with the Thick-Skinned edge. And no, you can't use this edge to try and heal maimed noggins or guts.
