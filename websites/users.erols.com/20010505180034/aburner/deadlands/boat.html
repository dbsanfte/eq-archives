Trait used: Nimbleness.

Concentrations: canoe, kayak, pirogue, raft, skiff, etc.

Description: Sometimes a body needs to travel downriver or downstream, and paddle-wheelers just don't fit in every place a body can go. This ability covers small one- or two-man boats which need to be paddled or poled along to get anywhere.
