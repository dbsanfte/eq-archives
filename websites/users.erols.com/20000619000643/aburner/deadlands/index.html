This here's the website o' Afterburner, and it's dedicated to one of the most outstandin' role-playin' games ever t'come down the pike. I'm talkin' 'bout the game o' the Weird West:

If'n ya ain't heard about it, you kin get yerself over to the official Deadlands web page, which was put up by the folks what made the game.

In a nutshell, though, it's a world much like our own Wild West. In fact, right up until 1863, it's the same. But in 1863, things got a mite...peculiar . Imagine a cross between The Outlaw Josey Wales, Brisco County, Jr., and Return of the Living Dead and you got a fair idea of what's going on 'round these parts.

In any event, this particular waterin' hole is still under construction. Poke about if ya want and enjoy the scenery.
