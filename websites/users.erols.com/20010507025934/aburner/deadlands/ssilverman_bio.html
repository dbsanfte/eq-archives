How I got started in this crazy hobby:

Wayyyy back in 4th grade, a friend got the Basic D&D Boxed set for Christmas. He was too dumb to figure it out and tossed it into his closet.

A year or so later, I found an AD&D display in a department store's toy section and convinced (read: nagged) my Mom to buy one of the books for me, because it was all about elves and orcs and everything else that was in the books I was currently reading (The Lord of the Rings!).

The concept "Main Rulebook required to use this Supplement" was alien to my 10 year old mind. I spent a happy trip home in the back seat reading all about zombies and umber hulks and dragons, Oh My! My joy was boundless until I realized a few days later, "What the heck do you do with all this information?" There I sat, dejected and bored, with my very own Monster Manual and nothing to do with it, when who should walk by, but my near-illiterate friend, "Gee, I got something like this at home. It doesn't make any sense either."

The rest, as they say, is peanut butter and chocolate.
