What do the positions stand for?

Guild Leader: All-around head of the guild. In charge of ring-leading the meetings, making the "final" decisions.

Research Leader: Involved in finding out information regarding quests, hunting areas, travel routes, and anything else that will advance the members of the guild. Will be responsible for guides and links regarding guides.

Activities Leader: Organizes events for the guild (not multi-guild. Intra-guild, only) Is in charge of making sure we have games, contests, and hunts, so our Guild remains alive and active. Responsible for seeing plans for events seen through culmination.

Morale Leader: In charge of keeping guild spirits high. Counsels, listens to problems, and is in charge of the entertainment for meetings.

Equipment Leader: In charge of knowing what items the guild has available, and what's been given away. Keeps track of the storage box, and helps people know where to go for their various pieces of equipment.

Interactivity Leader: Responsible for playing "matchmaker" within the guild. Basically, is in charge of knowing the various levels of our members, and their USUAL locations, and hunting times, so they can steer them towards groups within the guild. IE. I'm level 20. So is Juliet. IL will put the two of us together with a couple others, to help reduce the "There's nobody in my level range" complaints.

Spiritual Leader: Is in charge of ensuring the guild's spiritual well-being. Holds "holy quests" as well as presides over all ceremonies alongside the guildleader.

Recruitment Leader: Supervises recruitment within the Guild, keeping the Guild leader up to date and organized. Also in charge of membership drives.

Communications Leader:Assists the Guild Leader in gathering information for the web site, co-moderates the mailing list and boards, supervises the trade skills of the guild, as well as learns of the histories and legends of the lands

Fiscal Leader: Serves as the treasurer for the guild, helping raise funds to equip us and make us stronger.

Diplomatic Leader: Strengthens the guild by serving as ambassador to other guilds, forming bonds and ties between them.

Security Leader: Serves as bodyguard to the Queen, and leads and arranges security for grande functions and diplomatic encounters.
