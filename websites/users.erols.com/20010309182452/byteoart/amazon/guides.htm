Suggested Topics
Instead of the Sword: One handed, two handed, three handed HO! I'm a spellcaster, now what? How to keep alive fighting without weapons.
The Element of Fear: A battle cry, an fear-inspiring expression, or just being big. Warriors rely on many skills to cause their enemy fear. How can you use the element that best suits YOU? (A guide to roleplaying a warrior)
Damage on the Spot: A comprehensive guide to the spells that take out beasts quick and painlessly.
The Noble Path: A guide to Honor and Pride, learning to live by Chivalrous Standards.
Fashion Bug: The styles of the rich, not so rich, and boy I wish I were rich! What goes well for the best-dressed adventurer?
Justice Preserved: How does one bring justice to the lands without constantly feeling the need to slaughter thy neighbors?
Men Need Not Apply: A celebration to the power of women, and why a woman need not rely on a man's aid. Done in a manner that does not insult men.
Your Floating Friends: A comprehensive guide to the pets that tag along behind you, and how to make them pay attention.
Women Wizards: Why do women take the wizard cakes? What makes our lights glow brighter? How can you survive in the lands and keep the spells a-flashing (newbie guide to wizards and roleplaying wizards).
Wizzing Right Along: The benefits to being a Wizard, and a guide to doing it right. Why are there so few wizards? Why is it such an untapped profession?
The Balance: The art of fighting when you need no armor. Why are monks superior to Warriors, and how do you become the best you can be? (newbie guide to monks and roleplaying monks).
The Price of a Song: A bard must truly travel for their art. Where can one find the best stories, and better, where can they find their best (and most magical), songs?
Getting from Here to There: How best to travel from continent to continent, and how to avoid having to beg for teleports. This guide will show the safe route suggestions for our land's most perilous journeys.
The Arms of Nobility: How arms and armor show the strength of a true noble. A technical guide in the "best" sense, but a role playing guide in the heraldic sense.
Hitting the Mark: Rangering with the best of them! Arrows, bows, and best of all...the great outdoors. (A layman's guide to being a ranger from an Amazonian Perspective).
Curtain Calls: The art of being a performer. Storytelling, songwriting, poetry, and our instruments. (A RP guide on bard-dom)
