Juliet

About Me

My childhood was a nightmare. My family, living out in the forest at the time, was captured by the gruesome orcs of Lord Crush when I was five. My Mother and Father were sold to Ambassador D'vinn shortly there after. I saw them leave Crushbone via a secret entrance only known to Lord Crush and to Ambassador D'vinn. I knew some day I would use that secret to escape.

I was forced to work their mines until I was 15 years old. At this time I was moved to work in the castle. This was my opportunity to escape, for the castle was under constant raids.

On the eve of my escape, while I was preparing to sleep, Our Mother Tunare visited me and blessed me with the rudimentary ability to shape shift and to peer into peoples minds. She knew I was destined to greatness and that I was planning to escape that very next morning.

As a final gift, to which I owe my life, She granted me the shape of a smelly orc for one hour whenever I chose to use it. I escaped easily out through the tunnel and never looked back...until it came time for my revenge. I headed far out into the forest not knowing which direction to go. I came to a small valley where I rested and waited for the orc illusion to wear off. When it did and several hours later, I came upon the city called Felwithe.

This was the first time I had seen the place so I wondered in and looked around. I was very hungry so I ..um..borrowed some bread and was promptly placed in prison. With ease I made the guard think I escaped for I turned myself into a rug. After getting caught again, I was carried off to Master Goldsinger, the guild leader of the Enchanters.

I figured that it was either prison or a hard life as an enchanter so I reluctantly chose to learn the ways of the Enchanters. I was under Master Goldsinger's direction for 6 levels at which time I foolishly determined it was time for revenge. I accepted a tenure with the Champions of the Feydarks to defend against the orc onslaught. I was fighting the orcs for many years.

When all of my hatred was extinguished I headed for home where I was welcomed as a hero. I learned that my fame had spread to the guards of both the city that I now call home, Felwithe, and the famed tree city, Kelethin. Whenever I visit these places the guards warmly greet me and invite me to the local bar for war stories.

I now live from place to place changing race as I choose only visiting my friends in Felwithe on occasion.

My Guild
I also have a new family, the only family I have known since that tragic day in my childhood. . I am a member and sister of the Amazons. I find it easy to love them for they stretched out their hand in friendship when no other group cared that I was alive.

My Trades

Because of my slavery and times alone I have become a master potter. I am far from being the best but I am able to make very beautiful pottery. I grew tired of pottery and now I am learning how to craft beautiful and magical jewelry. At present I am studying the crafting of Electrum. I have learned more than thought possible in such a short time and with limited finances.
