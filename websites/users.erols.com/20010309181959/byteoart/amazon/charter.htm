Amazon Charter

The Amazons were founded to provide a Sisterhood where neither good nor evil matters. It is a Sisterhood where a Drow necromancer can have tea with a High Elf paladin. It's a Sisterhood where we gather to laugh, smile, adventure, and comfort each other.

There are no limits on race, religion, or class. At this time we are accepting applications.

Our goals are to provide a solid arm when strength is needed and a soft touch where the gentle stroke of elegence is desired. We know that as women, we are uniquely qualified, as we are superior.

When all is said and done, we want to make the world smile...and provide a few fashion tips along the way.

On a note: This is a serious guild with a serious goal: To be anything BUT serious. Where what we say may have truth, we do this in fun, in a tongue-in-cheek manner. Where our characters strut around knowing the superiority of women, the players may be men or women, and we know that both genders are equal. The guild is made for fun only, and if you have a sense of humor, and can shove your tongue deeply into your cheek...this is the guild for your girl.
