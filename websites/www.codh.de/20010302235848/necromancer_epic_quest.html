Necromancer Epic Quest
1. Head to Nektulos Forest and find the female NPC by the river loc -1000 -700
2. Head to Lake Rathe and find Kazen Facae loc 340 -1600
3. Kill Sir Edwin Motte (level 33 paladin). He is easiest to catch in tavern by the docks in EFp where he is on a rotation with the Tumpy Irontoe.
4. Bring head of Motte to Kazen and recieve a Symbol of the Appretice
5. Bring Symbol of the Appretice to the DE in Nektulos Forest, she'll return to you Twisted Symbol of the Apprentice
6. Go to Najena and camp FBR and when you get it go to Nektulos Forest and give FBR to DE female and get some moss
7. Head to Lake Rathe & give moss and Twisted Symbol of the Apprentice to Kazens apprentice, Emkel. Get Symbol of the Serpant.
8. Go to Swamp of No Hope and find Ssessthrass loc 3800 1600 give him Symbol of the Serpant, receive Scaled Symbol of the Serpant.
9. Head to Chardok. Kill Royal Herbalist and loot Manisi Herb (found on way to overking area, right before the secret door which leads to overking room).
10. Go back to Ssessthrass and give him Scaled Symbol of the Serpant and herb, and he'll return Refined Manisi Herb.
11. Head to Lake Rathe and give Emkel Refined Manisi Herb, he will give you Symbol of Testing.
12. Have about 12-15 people ready (clerics and tanks are a must) and give Symbol of Testing to Kazen. 3 mobs will spawn. Kill these, last drops symbol of Insanity.
13. Go to Timerous Deep, give Symbol of Insanity to Drendico Metalbones (near where evil raft drops you off, on hill) and he gives you his journal.
14. Gather Cloak of Spiroc Feathers (quest in Sky), Eye of Innoruuk, and Blood of Cazic Thule. Bring all 3 along with Drendico's journal to timerous deep. Give to Drendico, he returns to you a PREPARED REAGENT BOX.
15. Take PREPARED REAGENT BOX to Kazen, and give to him...you get back a TOME OF INSTRUCTION.
16. Go to Plane of Sky, give Thunder Spirit Princess 3pp. Then Clear isle to 3rd (Gorgalosk) isle.
17. Give Gkzzallk the TOME OF INSTRUCTION. You get back GKZZALLK IN A BOX. (no battle.)
18. Go to lake Rathe, give Kazel GKZZALLK IN A BOX. (no battle) Get back Scythe of Shadowed Souls. DONE!
