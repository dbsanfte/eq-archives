NEW JUMPGATE BOARDS OPEN AT 3DO

3DO have officially opened up new JumpGate boards at jg-bbs.3do.com for all your posting needs.

All registered Jumpgate Pilots on the 3DO Servers automatically have a BBS account created for them using their current Jumpgate game login info.
