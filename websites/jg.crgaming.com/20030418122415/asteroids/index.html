ASTEROIDS
There are a number of different types of asteroids in the galaxy, each one providing vital resources to the reconstruction initiative.

To mine them simply equip a mining laser to your craft and fly in next to the rock.  Pull your firing trigger once to activate the mining process (if you are in range you will see particles coming off the rock, and your cargo-hold readout increasing).  

Asteroids can be overmined.  If you notice that an asteroid is has obtained a bluish color then the rock has probably been mined too heavily and begun to melt.  When this occurs extraction becomes very slow.  It's normally best to move to a new asteroid if this occurs.  The asteroids will cool down again over time, so just come back later (having a Rotacol is a good way to do this).

Below you will find a list of the different asteroid types, and what they produce:
 

Common Metals Asteroid
This is the most common asteroid which you will find. It contains a range of different metals used in a number of different components.

Because it is the most common, you make the least credits for the mining of these asteroids.

94% Common Metals
2% Ice
2% Precious Metals
1% Semifluxors 
1% Radioactive Metals

 

Radioactive Minerals Asteroid
These are a more rare asteroid, rich in the minerals required for the production of engines and weapons.  Stations pay large amounts for the more rare metals in them.  

Unfortunately these asteroidsare made up of both quality ore and common metals, and fulflilling large orders for Radioactive Metals can take much longer than other mineral types (such as semifluxors).

49% Radioactive Metals
36% Common Metals
9% Semifluxors 
5% Precious Metals
1% Ice

 

Precious Metals Asteroid
This asteroid contains high levels of gold, silver, and platinum.  These are required for the complex wiring of goods produced by the manufacturers of the various factions.

These asteroids are a little uncommon though, so payment for mining them is often a little higher than normal.

52% Common Metals
43% Precious Metals
2% Radioactive Metals
2% Semifluxors 
1% Ice

 

Semi Fluxor Asteroid
This asteroid is full of the silicon used in the construction of semiconductors.  AS such it is highly prized by stations who require the minerals in the construction of all their electrical and computer devices.

These asteroids are found mainly in the Hyperial sectors, and payment for them is high.  They do tend to float out of the sector from time to time though, and careful checking of your radar for lonely asteroids can often reap large rewards.

63% Semifluxors 
32% Common Metals
3% Precious Metals
1% Ice
1% Radioactive Metals

 

Ice Asteroid
This asteroid contains high levels of water, which is a needed resource on all staiton outposts. 

Thankfully they are not terribly uncommon in the outer reaches, but this also means they don't pay so well.

96% Ice
1% Semifluxors
1% Common Metals
1% Precious Metals
1% Radioactive Metals
