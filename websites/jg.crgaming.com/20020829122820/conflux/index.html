CONFLUX SPECIES
 
Conflux Squid C1 - C3
Fast and small, these little squids can be very annoying if enough of them are on you at once.  An experienced pilot can normally handle up to two or three at once though if they have enough skill.

The squids have a fairly slow turn speed after their run, and the best approach is fly past them at speed, slightly angled to avoid their lasers, and then perform a fast turn and attack them before they turn around again.  Quite often only one or two passes are required to dispatch of these lightly armored alien craft.
Class: Probe
Speed: 250 - 300
Guns: 1
Experience: 300 - 400
Bounty: c1,200 - c2,000
 
 
Conflux Snail C4 - C5
Packing more powerful ammunition based cannons, the snails can pack a much bigger punch than the smaller squid probes.  

Their disadvantage lies in their size, and their poor ability to shoot on angles.  The best approach to these is to fly at an angle to the approaching alien, then turn and shoot a strafing line of fire as they pass by.  Being a large target they are fairly easy to hit.  

A few passes is normally enough to dispatch a Snail, but dangerous if they are in numbers.
Class: Bomber
Speed: 326 - 350
Guns: 3
Experience: 500 - 600
Bounty: c2,400 - c2,800
 
 
Conflux Manta C6 - C7
Manta's are not for the faint of heart.  Fast, maneuverable, well armored, and with big guns, these alien fighters know what they are doing.  

Manta's are not a craft you want to deal with for too long, consequently they shouldn't be tried by a pilot without a well equipped ship.  

Manta's also travel at quite high speeds, if you don't think you can handle it start running before it gets in range!
Class: Advanced Fighter
Speed: 450 - 456
Guns: 2
Experience: 2,500 - 2,800
Bounty: c6,600 - c8,000
  
 
Conflux Kraken C89 - C9
Krakens may have less guns than some of the smaller craft, but they make up for this with accuracy and punch.  Their one gun can really pack a punch, and they are very good with it.

Krakens also have much better armor and shielding than the Manta craft, as well as being able to turn on a dime.

These craft are best tackled in numbers unless you are an expert dogfighter. 
Class: Sharpshooter
Speed: 420 - 436
Guns: 1
Experience: 6,000 - 6,500
Bounty: c10,400 - c14,000
 
 
Conflux Eel C10
The biggest currently known conflux species, the Eels have armor, and lots of it.  Fights with Eels are not fast, and they regenerate their shields quite quickly, making them a difficult enemy to overcome.  

Some well timed missile shots are a good way to knock their shields down and lay into their amor.

Once again, an alien best taken on with a group, or with a couple of spare flash-fires for when it gets too hot.
Class: Corvette
Speed: 500
Guns: 2
Experience: 5,000
Bounty: c24,000
