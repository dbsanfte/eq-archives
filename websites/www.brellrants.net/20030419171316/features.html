MMOGBB Features

Fully Customizable Message Boards

MMOGBB provides a fully customizable "look and feel" template for your message board service, allowing you to use your own images and colors. In the coming weeks we plan to add a number of different "base" templates to allow you to make your message board truly unique. You will also be able to assign moderators and administrators of your board, adjust security settings, categorize your forums, and control access to your board.

We're also about to roll out the ability for your message board to be accessible through your own domain name. If you want mypalomidesguild.com or ilovestormhammer.com to be the URL to your message board, we'll be able to do that for you.

Single Userbase

Anyone who registers with an MMOGBB message board is registered for every board in our network. Because we want boards to be accessible under unique domain names, users have to log in to each messge board individually, but registration is only required once.

Private Messaging

Any user of an MMOGBB message board can send a private message to another MMOGBB user. The two users do not have to use the same MMOGBB and the private message can be read from any MMOGBB board.

Image and Homepage Hosting

MMOGBB is gearing up to provide you your own space to host images for your message board as well as a homepage for your guild, your server, or your spoiler site. This will be live within the next few weeks.

MMOG Message Board Directory

In the coming weeks, MMOGBB will be providing to the MMOG community, free of charge, a directory of MMOG message boards across the Internet, whether or not they are hosted on MMOGBB.com.

Professional Support

MMOGBB provides full support for all our message boards. We can assist in the customization and administration of your board as well as provide restores from backup in the case something catastrophic happens to your system.
