Sign up for MMOGBB.com

Okay.

It all sounds good, right? You want to sign up with us?

Well great! But we don't have an automated "sign up" service yet. Because we're cheap, and new. :)

Here's what you do.

First, read and agree to our Terms of Service.

Drop an email to relbeek@brellrants.net and request your board. If you are already a registered user of an MMOGBB board, tell us who you are. Tell us what you want to call your board(ie "Fires of Perdition Guild", or "Star Wars Galaxies Merchants"), and it will be set up.

When the board is ready, register as a user (or simply log in) to your new board, then use Paypal to purchase your board:

We ask that you estimate your board's usage in advance and pay accordingly - but if you get more page hits than you paid for, you will simply be charged the difference at the end of the month.

When payment is received, we will activate your account as an administrator of the board.

It's that simple.

Automated board setup will be coming in future weeks.
