Bulletin Boards for the Online Gaming Community

Welcome to MMOGBB.com.

MMOGBB is an open-source message board system designed for the online gaming community. Powered by a customized version of phpBB, MMOGBB provides space for members of online gaming communities, their servers, player classes, guilds, or special interest groups, to communicate "outside the game," to exchange information, organize events, or just socialize with one another.

MMOGBB is designed to be an economical but full-featured alternative to the more expensive bulletin board services. Though our software is open-source, we provide server space, bandwidth, and technical support for your message board.

MMOGBB is founded and run exclusively by active members of the online gaming community. The line "For gamers, by gamers" may seem a bit cliché, but we believe that people who play MMOGs are going to be the most responsive to the needs of other MMOG players.

Our product has just launched and we're gearing up to add features and functionality to our system. We plan to constantly be improving the style and function of the boards, to continue to bring you the best value for your dollar.
