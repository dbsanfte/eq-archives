MMOGBB Hosting Costs

MMOGBB's pricing structure is simple: $1 per 15,000 page hits per month, with a minimum cost of $5 per month.

Our minimum cost equals that of EZBoard, but as your community grows, MMOGBB can offer over 2/3 savings over such premium services as EZBoard.

We accept payment in 6 month increments at this time. More payment options will become available in the coming weeks.
