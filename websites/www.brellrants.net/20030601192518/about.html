About MMOGBB

Hi. My name's Brian. I've played Everquest on the Brell Serilis server since February of 2000. (You may find me online as Relbeek Einre if I'm on.)

For the last year, I've been the administrator of one of Brell Serilis' community boards. Before that I served as one of its moderators.

As with many of Everquest's message boards, we were originally located on EZBoard. EZBoard is a good service, but we found the price had been climbing too high to continue to host our message boards there. I did some searching and found that we could host our own service at a small fraction of the cost of what EZBoard was charging us.

As a believer in open-source software, I settled on phpBB as our software package. But phpBB has its limitations, and so a few fellow Brellians and friends decided to add features and functionality to the system, to make it what we needed. When the other Brell Serilis community board's administrator saw his upcoming fee for renewing EZBoard, he made the decision to move over to the new server I'd set up.

This presented us with the first major hurdle: allowing two different phpbb-based message boards to share a single user database. This has always been the major advantage of EZBoard over other systems: register with EZBoard and all message boards it hosts will use that registration. So we resolved to modify the phpBB software to properly handle multiple message boards that share a single user table. And for a couple months we reworked the software.

The result is what we want to share to the rest of the online gaming community. We can provide this full-featured message board service at a fraction of the cost of EZBoard and other services, and we get to promote and enhance the overall online gaming community. And we call it MMOGBB - Massively Multiplayer Online Gaming Bulletin Boards.

We invite you to explore MMOGBB.com and to host your message board with us. Please email me, relbeek@brellrants.net, if you have any questions about our service.
