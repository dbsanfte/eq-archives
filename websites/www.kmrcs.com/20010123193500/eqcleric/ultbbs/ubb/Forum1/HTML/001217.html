December 14 patch

Hello Jack,

We're still discussing issues related to the rarity of class-specific armor components for several classes (clerics included). I jumped the gun a little when I said "the next patch", as only a few spell-related items were to be patched on December 14. The rest of the data, which includes updates to class-specific armor rarity, among other things, won't be going in until after the holidays.

My apologies for getting everyone excited prematurely.

-Gordon
********************************
January 20 Patch

>>>Gordon take the cleric challenge! attempt a quest

Ack! No! It does take a painfully long time to acquire this stuff, we've already acknowledged that. The class-specific armor quests are currently in the process of being adjusted to even out the time it takes to acquire these items.
However, since some of the quests share items or NPCs, they all have to be done at once for the most part. Even the Armor of Ro which was updated in this patch isn't reflected in the changes that have to happen to all of the spawn times and percentages.

It'll be done, it's one of the top priorities on the docket. If not the next patch, then the one after.

-Gordon
