B E S T I A R Y

Ghoul  

Ghouls are loathsome creatures with a taste for living creatures and even corpses. Being creatures of the night, ghouls are susceptible to holy or blessed weapons. Take care to avoid being touched by a ghoul -- their grasp can cause paralysis and sickness.
