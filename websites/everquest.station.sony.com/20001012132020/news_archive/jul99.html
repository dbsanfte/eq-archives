Fennin Ro Traveller's Guild 10:45 AM
Explorers and Gypsies alike will want to take note of this prospective Guild on Fennin Ro:

Hail fellow citizens of Norrath!

My name is Deosyne and I play a Wood Elf Ranger on the Fennin Ro server. I would like to tell you of my idea of a guild for the hardcore travelers of Norrath and request that if you are interested in becoming one of the founding members to contact me so that we may get the process underway. :)

Some players regard themselves as powergamers; they play to level as fast as possible and get the best loot. Some players are nesters; they choose a location and attempt to organize and enhance their home area by providing events and build a rapport with the other nesters in that area. Still others are tradesfolk; they adventure only to build up a stash of loot with which to build up their trade skills and become reknowned tradefolk.

Then there are us travelers.

We travelers are always on the move. We level very slowly and often have poor loot because we spend more time going from place to place than hunting, not to mention the corpses that we have left behind due to going to places we really have no business going. :) The thought of exploring an entirely new zone is so much more exciting than participating in an organized raid on Crushbone or attending a trade fair in Qeynos. We are often found in areas being hunted by players 20 levels above us. When given massive buffs and a SoW, we more often than not forget about taking advantage of the buffs to take out those prized yellows and reds and instead start running to somewhere new because we've got the SoW. :) There's a whole lot of Norrath out there and we want to see it all!

Now, I know that we're in the minority of players, but I believe that there are a sizable enough number of us that we should band together into a loose "network" spanning Norrath to provide our fellow travelers with help during our travels, in the way of binds, heals, buffs, food and drink and other helpful basics as we wander. We could provide companionship for one another, especially on those long, trans-Norrath treks that we are apt to take on occasion. We could provide directions to places that we are going and ideas for new places to travel to. We could assist one another in those five zones away corpse retrievals. :)

Now, of course, Everquest isn't just about traveling (or so I'm told :)) so we can also get together on occasion for hunting expeditions, host some events or just gather and regale one another and other Norrathians with tales of our journeys. We can support one another during those grueling periods we sometimes undergo, such as getting rubicite or, more appropriately for us, camping for Journeyman Boots. :)

If you're interested in joining a guild designed around us wandering folk, send me an email to deosyne@bellsouth.net. Include in your email your character(s) name(s), class(es) and race(s), your email address and the server(s) that you play on. No need to include your real name; its not necessary and none of my business. :) Also, include a quick paragraph on your most interesting trip; not necessarily your longest trip or most difficult, but the most interesting journey that you've taken thusfar. OK, its not required, but I love reading them. :) Oh, and although the guild will be based off of the Fennin Ro server, I have received interest in starting similar guilds on other servers, so I will be more than happy to start branches on servers other than Fennin Ro. :)

Let's band together and make our journeys just that much more fun by having like-minded folks who we can share our experiences and assist one another while adventuring in Norrath. Thanks for your interest!

Deosyne
Wood Elf Ranger
Fennin Ro
deosyne@bellsouth.net
