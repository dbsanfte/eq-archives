Xegony Drinking Gathering 5:00 PM
The first Xegony Drinking Gathering will be taking place tomorrow:

Hail citizens of Xegony!

The time has come to sit back and forget about spawns, loot, experience and money for a while. You are all invited to the first Xegony All-Northian Drinking Gathering.

* What is the Idea? *

The idea was conceived by 2 fellow adventurers, Balkin Ironfist and Ellegon Grossfuss. We are both Master Brewers and enjoy having a good time without constant competition. So we wanted to organize a gathering where we can hand out free drinks and have fun with old and new friends.

* Where will the Gathering be held? *

We decided to take West Freeport, as it is relatively easy to reach with boats and TP's and is a newbie zone, therefore there shouldn't be wandering MoB's killing drunk people. We are aware that certain races or classes are KOS in Freeport, but there is no place where EVERYBODY is safe. Just watch out for the guards if your race/class is disliked! :-)

* How do I come to Freeport? *

You can either travel by foot and boat, or you can ask your friendly Druid or Wizard for a Teleport. We are also trying to set up a taxi-service with volunteer Teleporters. More about that in the section "How can I help?"

* What does it cost me? *

Besides some time, absolutely nothing. The alcohol will be given away free of charge. You can tip the brewers, breezers, binders or the teleporters if you want, but it's not at all necessary. This will NOT be a gathering for the rich or to make money. It's only purpose is to have fun.

* When will it take place? *

The First XANDG will start on Saturday, the 12th of August at 6 pm PST (9pm EST, 2am GMT).

* How can I help? *

The easiest way to help is: Spread the word! Tell all your friends, your guild members and the people you meet in the zones. Post it on your guild boards and websites!

In addition, we still need a lot of help from everyone. We decided to search for some services to offer and therefore look out for:

- Brewers: Everyone with the brewing skill can attend and distribute his/her drinks to the attending people. We don't care about your skill, as even Bog Juice is alcohol :-) All we ask, is that you accept the fact that we’re not SELLING beverages. Again, this should be a free event, just for the fun of it.

- Foragers/Gatherers: A lot of the higher skill level drinks need ingredients that can't be bought. Good examples are Vegetables (very much needed), Fruits, Berries, Froglok Meat, Snake Scales or Royal Jelly. So everyone who can forage or collect these ingredients is welcomed to donate or sell them to us. Yes, we will buy them. The simple reason is, that when we make high end brews (like Hulgarsh, Ginesh, or Bleeding Brains), we will most likely gain skill levels (we are both around 140 brewing, but not maxed). Since we don't want to take advantage of your generosity, we are willing to pay 1 or 2 plat per stack of Vegetables and also some pay for the other ingredients. Of course we will gladly accept donations :-)

- Taxis: We want to offer a taxi-service between the various parts of Norrath to Freeport (respectively Commons) and maybe back. Therefore we would need some volunteer Druids and Wizards playing Taxi. We would allow the teleporting personnel to collect tips if offered, but the TP itself should be free of charge.

- Breezer/Clarity: The taxis will need a lot of mana and would very much like a breeze or even a clarity cast on them. If you would be able to help them a bit, please do so.

- Binders: Some of those who attend might like a bind in Freeport. We want to offer this to the ones asking for it. If you can cast Bind and will do it FREE of charge, why not join in the fun?

If you would like to help us in any way, please contact Balkin and Ellegon at xandg@hotmail.com!

* What will I get for helping? *

No money, and no exp. If a player tips you, then so be it. But we ask anyone participating, to offer his services for free. I'm sure you see the good will behind this event and the possibility to make some new friends.

* You still have additional Questions? *

No problem. Contact us at xandg@hotmail.com!

Please help us to make this a great event!

Balkin & Ellegon

Walk Across Antonica Recap 3:30 PM
The Seekers of Knowledge Walk Across Antonica was a success. Here is the storey:

We did it! Approximately 7 hours after taking our first halting steps, a slightly diminshed and very tired band of around 20 intrepid adventurers walked through the gates of Freeport.

We set off from Surefall Glade with 40+ brave marchers, ranging in levels from 1 to 42. Our trek took us through all the Karana regions, Lake Rathe and the Rathe Mountains, the Gorge, Misty Thicket, Rivervale, Kithicor Woods, The Commonlands and on into Freeport. Amazing sights and sharp skirmishes marked our passage. A Griffon fell beneath the weight of a mass attack in North Karana, and a Hill Giant met a similar fate in the Rathe Mountains. A raging band of minotaurs were dealt with in the Gorge, and the Goblin King himself tried to stop our march through Runnyeye, only to find himself laying face down on the floor of his own dark caverns. Two first level adventurers were slain during the walk (one of them twice) but both were recovered and marched proudly into Freeport with us.

We lingered a few minutes in Rivervale, where the loot off the Goblin King was awarded via lotto. Congratulations to Olenka, who won a Blackened Iron Medallion, and Rezzaenia, who won a Blackened Iron Girdle! In addition, every marcher level 10 and below was awarded a prize courtesy of Haemish MacLennan and the Silver Daggers!

Further contratulations are due Olenka, who we believe to be the only level 1 adventurer ever to WALK (not run) completely across the continent unscathed! Well done, Olenka, and well done to everyone who made part or all of the trek with us! I will be forever honored to tell the tale of leading such a fine band on this adventure!

Many thanks are due the Silver Daggers, the Dark Oracles, Cold Comfort and the Karana Council of Guilds for helping to make this the hugely entertaining event that it was. Special thanks to Haemish, Eleysian, Belce, Zoboomafu, Samadani, Milquetoast and Cassia for all your hard work!

Based on the success of this event, we are planning future walks!

If anyone would like to organize a similar event, I'll be glad to share ideas and lessons learned. Email me a doged2@mindspring.com.

Well done, Walkers, and see you all in Norrath!

Dogedme Kingson
Guild Leader
Seekers of Knowledge
