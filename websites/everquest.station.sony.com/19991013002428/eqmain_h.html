EQ Realms Search 11:00 AM

The search is on for a new webmaster at EQ Realms:

Hi there. This is Cralis, the webmaster of EQ Realms.

I am stepping down from EQ Realms, for several reasons. The biggest reason is time, I am going to be running a different site somewhere else, and will be unable to do both, and lately I have been unable to do either.

Last week I announced on EQ Realms that I was looking for someone to take over the news and events, and that I would assist them. However I have had only one response which did not seem to pan out.

So here I am asking all the EQ sites to put out the news. I am stepping down, and EQ Realms will need someone to basically run the news and expand the site. There is a lot of ideas that have been floating around, although of course you will get to run it as you see fit. I will simply remain around to help if needed, write some specific articles, and to help Thadius with Valorblade stuff.

If anyone is interested, you can mail me at cralis@eqrealms.com or to thadius@eqrealms.com.

Thanks!

Cralis
Webmaster @ EQ Realms (http://www.eqrealms.com)
cralis@eqrealms.com

Monster Island Raid Results 10:50 AM

Here are the results from Tarew Marr's Raid on Monster Island:

We started out meeting at the freeport docks as planned, and sailed on to the island of the Sisterhood of Erolsi. From here we gathered outselves together and headed for the Cyclops island!

The clopses proved to be difficult, but against our army of 30-40 people, they fell quickly and easily! We slaughtered at least 10, before sacking the pirate camp for sport! There was one casualty here when Gornit respawned and chased a mage into another clops :(

When we felt Tunare's lust for the death of her enemies was satisfied, we hunted down the great dinosaur, Allizewsaur!

Things got messy here, as many could not find the island, some fell into the water as levitate wore off and had to swim the rest of the way, lost at sea :( Eventually all but one made it to the island, who gave up from being lost and gated home . It was very hard to find for those of us not used to using /loc to find things.

Once all were there that were going to be there, we heard one of us yelling "Incoming!", and the fight began! I shouted a battlecry in Tunare's name and dug my scimitar deep into his heel! (about as high as I could reach)

It was a long battle, and he hit harder than I've ever seen (even harder than Nagfen's claws, I believe), but the army was too much for him, and we were victorious and without casualties!

All in all it was a great event, and everyone that went had a lot of fun! Below are links to the screenshots I took:
