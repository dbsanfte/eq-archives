Answers Volume VI - 4.14.00

This issue of Arch-Mage Answers is a teaser for the new magic in EverQuest: Ruins of Kunark. The following is a brief rundown of post level 50 magic that you can expect to see in the EverQuest exansion. While specific spells are not named, general trends for classes will be detailed.

EverQuest Ruins of Kunark Expansion Spells

It has been long since I have written you, my friend. Time has conspired against me - it seems days, not months, that I have been sequestered in my Tower documenting the spells that our intrepid adventurers bring back from the Ruins of Kunark. While the cataloging of these new magics remains far from complete, it is my belief that you might appreciate some details of what we have so far discovered.

Before I pontificate on the specifics, I would be remiss if I did not mention my amazement as to the potency of our recent acquisitions. These are not spells for apprentices - far from it. Only the most devout in the priesthood and the mightiest of the magi should even attempt the invocation these high magics. Hearken well - these new discoveries will forever change the way that we regard thaumaturgy in Norrath.

 

CLERICAL MAGIC

Look for:

Many new healing spells including:

  • Quick casting, good ratio healing
  • A healing spell better then superior healing
  • 3 new, and different, group healing spells
  • A healing DoT that also cures poisons and diseases
  • New, incredibly potent anti-poison spell
  • A spell that heals your party members when they attack a monster

    A new Resurrection spell

    Anti-undead magic including:

  • A better anti-undead direct damage
  • A superior vs. undead fear spell
  • A group version of invisibility verses undead
  • A single spell that can completely banish a specific undead creature

    New buff spells, including:

  • Upgrades to Resolution and Shield of Words and Symbol of Naltron
  • Group buffs

    A new series of spells that might save you from death - cast before you enter a fight.

    New Stun, Root, Fear, Cancel Magic, Pacify and more.

    I hope that this correspondence has proved useful - I am sure that the meat of it will provoke much discussion amidst your circle of scholars. As a cautionary note, though I know that one of your obvious insight will find it quite apparent, I warn you against leaping to conclusions before you receive copies of these scrolls yourself. While the applications of some forms of Thaumaturgy are readily apparent, others have much more subtle implications and utilization then immediately spring to mind. Investigate, employ and enjoy.
