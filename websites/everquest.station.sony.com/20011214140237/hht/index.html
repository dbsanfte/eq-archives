December 13, 2001 - Thursday

Newbie Holiday-Zebuxoruk

Newbie Holiday on the Zebuxoruk Server! Barbarians Helping Hand and Sacred Journey Guilds are hosting a NEWBIE HOLIDAY in the Halas/Qeynos area on December 16,2001 from 10:00am to 4:00pm est. Levels 1 to 10 are welcome to participate and there will be UBER Newbie prizes including 24 Velious weapons and armor pieces! We will also have Guild Members standing by to buff and heal as well. Hope to see you there!

December 11, 2001 - Tuesday

Guild Event - Morell Thule

The Company of Shadows, a guild on Morell-Thule, will be hosting an Aid Station on Saturday, December 15, from 10 am until Noon CST. We will have free food, drinks, & buffs and many pieces of lower level armor and jewelry to distribute to those who are new to Norrath. The event will take place outside of the west gate of Freeport. We look forward to seeing you there. Please email Maarra (eqmaarra@yahoo.com) if you have any questions!

November 27, 2001 - Tuesday

Guild Event - E'ci

On November 30 at 7 PM Pacific Standard Time, the Golden Phoenix guild will be hosting a Scavenger Hunt. This event will take place in the East Commonlands tunnel for levels 1-20 and will last about an hour. There are no fees to enter the Scavenger Hunt. For more information and rules for this event, visit the Golden Phoenix Guild Hall.

November 26, 2001 - Monday

Guild Event - Erollisi Marr

Hear Ye! Hear Ye! Mithaniel’s Mirth, a Guild on Erollisi Marr will be hosting an Aid & Buffing station for recruiting within Crushbone on Sunday, December 2nd from 1pm - 5pm EST. This zone is ideal for players in the 5 - 25 range with mobs ranging from 1 - 20. Most of the Orcs are melee class with a few casters mixed in. Besides the Pawns, Centurions, Legionnaires, Trainers, Slavers & Oracles, There are both triggered and timed spawns such as The Emissary, Emperor Crush, The Prophet, Royal Guards, The Taskmaster, and Warlords. Most of the Loot in zone is quest related with a few nice items dropping from Ambassador D'Vinn, Lord Darish, Rondo Dunfire, Retion Brenclog, Emperor Crush, and The Prophet. These items include Dwarven Ring mail Tunic, Screaming Mace, Shiny Brass Shield, Bracer of Battle, Dragoon Dirk, Prayer Cloth of Tunare and more!

We will be there to Buff, Heal and join-in the fun. Are you are looking for adventure? Would you like to inquire about our guild? Then please contact peako@soc.net and let her know that you will be there. Include your name, class and level as we may be forming a few groups for the harder areas.

If you are looking for a guild you can call home, one that is dedicated to role-play and exploration, consider joining Mithaniel’s Mirth today.

Safe Travels,
Shmueena Thistledown
Guild Leader and Professional Tree hugger

Guild Event - Tallon Zek

Hail Lords and Ladies!

I, Ssyhk Whirlwind, leader of the Lords of the Dark Horizon, officially announce that in two weeks time, on December 1st NOON US EST TIME / 6PM GMT+1, the Lords of the Dark Horizon will be holding their first Duel Tournament in the PvP arena of West Cabilis, our home! It will be a great opportunity for everyone in our guild to meet and a greater opportunity even to train our PvP skills!

The fighting categories are determined following your level:

* lvl 5 to 9
* lvl 10 to 14
* lvl 15 to 19
* lvl 20 to 24
* lvl 25 to 29
* lvl 30 to 34
* lvl 35 to 40

Now, come to the Cabilis Arena and face your friends, your brothers, your family in what IS the very first Duel Tournament in the history of Cabilis!

This is a members only event, and no one from outside the Lords of the Dark Horizon will be allowed to join the tournament.

Further details can be found on our website and the full rules in our forum "Guild Events and Raids".
