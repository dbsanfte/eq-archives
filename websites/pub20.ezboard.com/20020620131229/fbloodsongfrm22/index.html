Who We Are

 

Bloodsong is in essence a guild dedicated to freedom from constraint attained through abandonment of fear, enhanced self-expression and the extensive use of alcohol. Members need an underlying goodness of character without feeling the need to follow a strict set of rules and regulations. This almost chaotic and anarchistic attitude makes any finite definition of Bloodsong difficult. Each of us expresses the Bloodsong attitude according to their own worldview.

BLOODSONG POLICY FOR RECRUITING NEW MEMBERS

If you wish to join Bloodsong then create a new thread entitled: APPLICATION FOR MEMBERSHIP – YOUR NAME .

As the first post on this thread, tell us something about yourself. As a minimum we would like to know your class, level and race. It would be helpful if you could let us know how long you have been playing EQ, if you have any other characters, is the character that wishes to join your main one, and have you been in any other guilds? It would also be useful if you could state briefly what you want from Bloodsong and what you can offer to us

If you have been included on the "Lanys Shit List" it is also important that you tell us. This will not necessarily mean you will not be invited, depending upon the nature of the posting and the responses.

After starting this thread, it will be moved to membership sponsorship for members to post their support in the same thread. You will be expected to have within the guild a sponsor who will be responsible for introducing you to the guild.

Should any members who see an application have a good reason for objecting, then they are asked to post their concerns the thread. Your sponser will be responsible for talking with you as well as the officers regarding the objections. If a decision is difficult, the matter will be referred to the guild leader for a final decision.

When you and your sponser feel comfortable bringing your application for a vote they will set it up on the Membership Sponsorship forum. Any no votes will be explained either on the forum or to an officer. In the case of "no" votes, membership will be declined. After a period of one week, you will be informed of either acceptance or of being declined

When membership has been authorised, the applicant is asked to contact any officer in game to be invited formally to the guild.

When an applicant has been invited, the officer who had made the invitation is asked to record this fact on the thread in the New Members Forum, so that others are aware the invitation has been made
