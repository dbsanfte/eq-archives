ENC用の道具

　

　

ENC用の道具の事を話してくれます。

　

>hail Sultin.

I am Sultin -keeper of the three [Incandescent]s of power and the [Rod] of Insidious Glamour. If you are an Enchanter, perhaps we can deal.

私はSultin、３つの[Incandescent]s of powerと [Rod] of Insidious Glamourの監視者である。



>what is Incandescent?

There are three Incandescent items of power - [Wand], [Mask] and [Gloves].
Are you [interested] in any of them?


３つの白熱のItemがある − ［Wand ］、［Mask］と［Gloves］。
それらで、どれかあなたは［興味を持っている］か？


>I am interested in Gloves.

My personal favorite - Incandescent Gloves.
If you bring me the components to make another pair, I will give you my gloves.Incandescent Gloves are made by sewing together four other sets of gloves.
Glowing Gloves can be found on our mortal enemies - the Shadowed Men.
Radiant Gloves can be found on Radiant in the mazes of Cazic Thule.
Enchanted and Gleaming gloves can be purchased for 50 golden coins from Tizina of the Lavastrom Gypsies and a bloodstone from Tarn Vislin of HighKeep.
Bring me all four sets of gloves and I will give you a pair of Incadescent ones.


私の個人的なお気に入り − Incandescent Gloves。
もしあなたがもう１対材料を私に持って来てくれるなら、私はIncandescent Glovesを与えるであろう。

・Glowing Gloves　(Shadowed Men)
・Radiant Gloves　(CazicのRadiant)
・Enchanted gloves　(LavastromのTizinaから50GPで購入)
・GleamingEnchanted　Gloves　(HighKeepのTarn Vislinにbloodstoneを渡す)

私に４つの手袋のセットを持って来てください、そうすれば私はあなたに１対のInterested in Glovesを与えるであろう。
　

例えば、Gleaming Glovesの場合、HighKeepのTarnにBloodstoneを渡してみると

　
　

(Tarn Visilin)
The gleam of blood - and now the gleam of gloves.

Your faction standing with BetaNeutral got better
You gain experience!!

　
　

このようにして材料を手に入れて持って行けば良いようです。
