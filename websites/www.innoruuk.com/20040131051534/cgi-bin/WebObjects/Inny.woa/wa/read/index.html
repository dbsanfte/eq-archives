Posting Events

To post an event on the calendar:

  1. Go to the calendar section and display the month you want
  2. Click on the numbers for the day in question -- for instance, the 13 in December 13.
  3. Fill in all information including your guild password.

You do not need to log in. This is an administrative login used by Inny.com staff. Your guild password will be requested when you edit the event.

If you need your guild password, please start with the person who previously booked events. If you are the person booking events, please make sure you do not quit EQ without sharing the password with other people. As a last resort, contact me.

Guild and Event Summary Pages

Clicking on the guild name in the guild list and on the event name in the calendar will now bring you to a detail view page. The guild detail page lists all known information about the guild and any upcoming events they have scheduled. Clicking on the event name in a guild detail view, or from the calendar will show the details for an event, with links back to the guild that "owns" it. Both of these pages are bookmarkable (minus the wosid=foo thing).

In order to edit your guild record, or an event record first go to the detail screen for the guild or event and click "edit record" in the upper right corner.
