Team Epic to Retire
December 30 09:48 AM, by Wolfypaw

December 31, 2003 is Team Epic's last official day of active operations.

We've had a great thing. Officially, 109 people have completed their epics with Team Epic assistance. But the server has matured, and the reasons I formed TE no longer exist. It's time to allow the institution to retire with honor.

This is not a sad thing. Rather, it's simple acknowledgment that we've accomplished what we've set out to do: help a lot of people complete their epics. Mission accomplished.

If you want further details, please visit the Team Epic Website.
