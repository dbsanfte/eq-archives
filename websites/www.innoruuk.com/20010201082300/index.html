Saturday, January 27

More EQ Auctions Shut Down!
9:53AM EST - Leansi Fyre'Fly
Another auction site has been forced to close their EQ sections, this time around it's a place called EGA. Here's the first letter sent out by the admins of eGamesAuctions:

Hello xxxxxx xxxxxx,

This is another update to all members.

We are keeping everyone up do date on the EQ Crisis thus far. Well, our membership keeps growing so we guess the word is not completely out. Yesterday, we were served at our office to stop all EQ related auctions immediatly or go to jail for the eve and seek legal in the morning. The only thing that saved our gear from being seized etc was that we did not make profit in the week we opened.

Again, we put a lot of time, sleepless nights and funds into EGA trying to devote it to EQ. All members thus far have been very supportive and we thank you so much. We have added 2 new categories for the upcoming online games that are "Totally Fine" about us buying and selling! They are Anarchy Online and Ultima Online 2. We also added some console gaming categories to the list.

We hope EGA will still be THE site on the net for Online Game Gear. And if the change arises one day or Sony Online sees there demise this action may cause them in the future. We hope to add that Category again.

We also wish the best to our competitors, playerauctions.com. It seems the same may have happened to them. I hope they hang in there myself, if possible.

Last thing, if you wish to cancel you account. Please just send us an email to support stating please cancel me at this time. We will back-up the status and make it inactive in case you wish to rejoin in the future.


Thanks for the support

Robert
EGA (New Asherons Call) Gamer
A few minutes later they sent this one out:
Hello xxxx xxxx,,

Bad news all,

I just got a knock on the door of my office with court orders from the Alaska Attorney Generals Office to stop all Everquest related auctions immediatly or have all our items seized and we would go to jail (this is no joke).

EGA gave it a hell of a try, but it seems Sony Online Games has a bit more legal presence then we do.

We have to remove the Everquest category from our auction.

We apoligize for this but we had no idea that the courts in all states have already been advised of actions.

Hang in there all and maybe one day it will be our world.

Sincerly

Robert
EGA Head Gamer

Thursday, January 25

EQ-Putting a Hold on Updates
3:26PM EST - Leansi Fyre'Fly
For at least a few weeks we will be doing some internal work that will prevent us from making any updates to the game unless they are critical (and there are no such things that we know of at this time).

We will still be taking in reports about bugs and other suggestions for change (such as the Class Talk stuff), but we won’t be making any changes to the game. And the two-week timeframe is only a guess at the moment, it could go longer.

While this means there will be no changes to the game during this time, we will still be maintaining the servers, the physical hardware, and all other aspects of the game. We are just not changing any aspects of the game software itself.

- Alan

Great wizard Al'Kabor grants quest in Dreadlands!
7:35AM EST - Seani Sweetheals
I was fortunate enough to aid the self proclaimed greatest wizard ever, Al'Kabor, in a spontaneous quest he initiated in the Dreadlands Sunday. Al'Kabor told all that would listen that he needed an item for spell research, that he knew was in the area. After much thought and foraging, I brought him a Frost Crystal from the tundra, completing his quest and earning me the spell Inferno of Al'Kabor and 1000 platinum, plus experience! It was a joyous day indeed, and one I'll not soon forget. Many shall hear of my meeting with Al'Kabor, for it's not everyday a legend is seen!

Landail Nitetripper, Ranger of the Explorers of Norrath

(ooc)Al'Kabor, run by a GM, appeared in Dreadlands and told all of his quest, that he was seeking an item of "frost, cold, snow and ice" for his research. The character was played very well by the GM, and a good time was had by all. No one even attempted to kill Al'Kabor, making the quest run smoothly. Special thanks to Talerili, Andromede, and Salena for helping me figure out the quest! Was an unforgettable experience.
