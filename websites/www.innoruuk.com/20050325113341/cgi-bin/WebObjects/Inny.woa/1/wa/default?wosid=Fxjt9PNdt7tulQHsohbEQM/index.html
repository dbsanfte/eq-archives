For love of Evil
March 08 11:05 AM, by Katydydi

For those of you who knew my husband Lushmusher of Immortalis Kruoris, there was an announcement that was lost about his passing due to the inny.com server going down.

Jason, better known to most of the Internet community over many years as "Evil", passed away due to a blood clot that formed after a surgery that was meant to save his life. He was a good man and a loving husband. He was multi-talented. He loved making furniture with wood by hand. He loved to listen and play music. Jason came to love Everquest after I'd pestered him to try it just once for the first three years we were together. It was instant love after he'd played for just an hour what he'd seen me playing for three years. The time he played for the past year was a joy to him; I thank the entire community and the two guilds he was a part of for enhancing his life.

Lushmusher will remain a part of Everquest and the guild he wished to be in. So if you see him standing about, know he is there with us all in spirit. I am simply the vessel he is sharing himself through.

I love you, Jason.

Katydydi - Ageless Wanderers
