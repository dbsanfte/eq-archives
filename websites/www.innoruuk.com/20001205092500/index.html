*SBLive Soundcards and Hardware Acceleration*

Due to some past problems with SBLive cards, users of those cards who called into tech support were asked to turn "Hardware Acceleration" down one notch in DXDIAG. The last patch corrected those problems and most users should again increase hardware acceleration to maximum.

To do this:

1. Click "Start"
2. Click "Run"
3. Type "DXDIAG.EXE" and press enter.
4. Click on the "Sound" tab.
5. Increase the "Hardware Acceleration" slider to maximum.

Doing this should correct issues such as not hearing certain sounds or sound volume disparities.

The only users who may not want to do this are Windows 2000 users. Creative Labs is currently working on new drivers for Win2K, so you may wish to wait until these drivers are completed prior to increasing hardware acceleration.

Finally, all SBLive users should get LiveWare 3.0 from http://www.soundblaster.com/liveware/. After installing LiveWare, you will then want to REINSTALL the latest drivers for your sound card. The order of installation is important.

- The EverQuest Team

General Defeated! Ragebringer won!
11:55AM EST - Cymric
The combined forces of the Justifiers, Inner Circle, Fortress of Honor, Knights Hospitaller, Silent Justice and friends gathered in Kithicor with one purpose in mind, KILL the General! We got there a bit late so we had to wait till daylight to begin setting up for our battle at night. When it was finally time to begin, a horrible thing went wrong.

The undead guards for the General's House were up! Despite this we lured the Coercer out into the darkness and at the last second I slipped a box into her robe that I knew she would tell the General about when discovered. She discovered the small box all too soon, and immediately the General appeared in the house to inspect it. The brave Ibrahim from Silent Justice then yelled down to him and challenged him to a duel. The General came after him along with his 2 undead guards. Ibrahim intended to lead him into an ambush at High Hold Pass, but alas this was not to happen ... at least not yet. Half way to High Hold the General over took him and killed him where he stood. I with my roguely skills was just a step behind and I appeared instantly to resume leading the General to our ambush. However I was not quick enough either and quickly perished. The worst thing that could happen did.

The General went back to his cabin and summoned up all of his followers to tell them the tale of the ambush intended for him. Now, surrounded by his subordinates, the General was nearly impervious, nearly .... Four or five brave warriors lost their lives attempting to lure him away from all of his minions to our ambush. Finally the General, to sure of his own might, was convinced to follow a monk he thought injured. Fast of the Justifiers managed to corner him alone and lure him directly into our hunting power. However, the battle was still not done, not by a long shot.

Half our people skilled in melee combat had already perished at the General's hands and now he was here for us to fight! Everyone fought bravely, charging him again and again. Many people died, and even as they returned to their bind points, charged in yet again to fall under his blade. After about 5 minutes of constant, high pitched battle, he finally succumbed to our determination.

Later that evening we visited the General's house again to rid the world of his minions as well. After killing the Coercer, I took her robe, a Robe of the Ishiva. Next we killed the Advisor, and took his robe, a Robe of Kedge. What luck! I had remembered Vilinus in West Karana asking for these robes. I quickly had Ristorette use her druidly magic to teleport us to North Karana. After a short run, we managed to catch up with Vilinus in West Karana! I showed him the robes I had gotten and we struck a bargain, he gave me the Cazic Quill. I returned to High Hold Pass and delivered the Quill, the General's pouch, and a dagger I had gotten from Stanos' daughter Renux. He for my efforts handed me a mighty blade forged by Innoruuk himself. Ragebringer was mine!

/ooc Thanks to all who came!

Noctur Docter

EQ Fans to Meet and Greet the Creators and Their Fellow Norrathians in Baltimore
1:24PM EST - Cymric
San Diego, CA - November 29, 2000 - Sony Online Entertainment is pleased to announce that its next official "EverQuest(r)" Fan Faire Event location is to be held in Baltimore, Maryland on February 2-3, 2001. At the event, EverQuest fans will have the chance to meet the people that work behind the scenes to the most successful online role- playing game in history and meet their fellow Norrathians.

"The East Coast EverQuest fans have waited a long time," said Cindy Archuleta, Community Relations Event Manager for Sony Online Entertainment. "We've planned fun activities and panel discussions with different people from Sony Online plus, this is the first event where contest prizes are actually in-game items."

The official launch party for "EverQuest(r): The Scars of Velious(tm)" was a huge success that attracted over 1250 fans in Las Vegas November 4-5, 2000, making it one of the most well-attended events of its kind. Attendees were the first to explore the new continent of Velious and mingle with the creators of EverQuest. "I am looking forward to Baltimore," said Jeff Butler, producer of "EverQuest: The Scars of Velious" and EQ Live. "It has been very valuable to meet the players of EverQuest in past events, since the fans are the ones who have made this game so successful."
