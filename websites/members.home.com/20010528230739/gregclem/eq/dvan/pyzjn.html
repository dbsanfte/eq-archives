Pyzjn With The Glowing Black Stone 
- by Dvan InABottle

I dream of Pyzjn with the Glowing Black Stone
Spawn'd with an undead pet made out of bone
I see her walking where the valley lay
Wand'ring with her skelie then fast blown away.
Many where the dark spells her gnomish voice would pour,
Many were the hunters that showed her deaths door.
I dream of Pyzjn with the Glowing Black Stone
Appearing with an undead pet made of chips of bone.

I sigh for Pyzjn, but she's just never there
Far from where I look not seen hide or hair;
Her pets have vanished and her rare drops flown
Flitting like the dreams that have taunted us and gone.
Now the evil dread skeletons may be camp'd ever more,
Though her soon appearing will always be unsure;
I dream of Pyzjn with the Glowing Black Stone
Appearing with an undead pet made of chips of bone.
