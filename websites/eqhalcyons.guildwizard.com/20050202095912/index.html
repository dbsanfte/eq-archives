Welcome

Who are we? Prosperous, promising and golden. As a noun, halcyon refers to a fabled ocean bird with the power to calm the wind and waves while it nested at sea.  As an adjective, halcyon means everything we strive to be.  Halcyon is a guild emerged from the ruins of many devastated by the hurricanes of change recently plaguing the Everquest World.  Formed by people who are dedicated to the game and their friends, Halcyon began with the vision of creating and maintaining close personal relationships between players while simultaneously building the caliber of those players through grouping and raiding.

Rallos Zek? Down! Application freeze? Over! - 1/15/2005 3:47:31 PM


Well, let me catch my breath! When Halcyon declares they are getting backflagging over with ... they certainly mean it!

In just a week and a half, the god kills (and then some!) needed for planar progression have been checked off one by one from a very long list of encounters needed by thrilled members looking to complete their EP access. Terris Thule, Grummus, Manaetic Behemoth, Carprin and then Bertoxxulous, the HoH trials, Aerrin`Dar, Marr and Marr again, Tallon and Vallon Zek, Agnarr, Saryrn ... and finally Rallos Zek! All have fallen before Halcyon!

Have a screenie of our RZ encounter? Send it to me!
But for now, Iggy and Dany scoffing at RZ's corpse will have to do!

With the defeat of Rallos Zek, which admittedly is still a challenging encounter 2 expansions later, a large core of our members are ready (and as of last night's massacre in Air... already have!) to join us in the Elementals! A big "Congrats!" to those newly flagged! A Rallos Zek kill marks a huge milestone in Halcyon history, solidifying us as a forced to be reckoned with in the EPs. With a Solusek Ro encounter already being discussed, I sense Fennin Ro starting to perspire!

What progression! And in order to continue progressing, we are announcing the following requirements needed to be considered as a Halcyon applicant:
  • Level 65+
  • Up-to-date Magelo profile
  • Elemental Flags (Air, Earth,Water)
These requirements will be in place until further notice. Applications pending at the time the freeze is lifted will not be subject to these new requirements. And that freeze? We will be lifting the application freeze today, Saturday, January 15th. 

As well as flagging requirements, we have taken the time to review our roster and prioritize our needs on a class level. Please note the following chart which outlines Halcyon's current needs. Any individuals meeting the above requirements are welcome to apply regardless;  however, priority will be given to applicants who fall within classes listed as needed by demand. Exceptional players are always encouraged to apply.

Happy New Year! - 1/3/2005 10:35:03 AM

After enjoying another evening in Air before the new year, a fun night which the Avatar of Smoke and a few lesser named were added to our downed target list, we made the decision to do the inevitable: backflag.

<Insert collective groan here!>

It's a new year and we can think of no better time to start!

With the schedule leaning towards targets which will focus our new members on getting up-to-date with flags, the decision was also made to temporarily freeze applications. Applicants who currently are under review for membership are encouraged to join our channel and attend these flagging raids, as once backflagging is complete, our requirements will likely be changing to favor those who meet more specific requirements (such as EP flags!) until further notice.



Last night, Halcyon made a clean sweep of quite a few targets on Karana!  Targets included Grummus, Behemoth, and all three HoH trials (Rydda`Dar, Women, Villagers). Before allowing our tired troops to savor sweet sleep, we crowned our evening with a Mithaniel Marr kill. Congratulations to those who attended these flagging raids and are now that much closer to joining us in the Elementals!

A big thank you to our leader, Ignatiuss, from all those who were flagged! Three cheers for Shydenn, who did an awesome job trying out that new epic on Marr! And a job well done, healers! Halcyon, you are going places ...
