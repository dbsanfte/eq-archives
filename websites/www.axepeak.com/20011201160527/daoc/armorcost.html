Armor Costs for Crafting

OK, for those of you wanting to know how much armor costs, I've got a real basic chart here. All the time folks ask me how much armor costs, an then have heart palpatations when I tell em. It's not like we be tryin ta get rich off clanmates...we should sell things to clanmates at cost. Just enough cash to cover th' raw materials involved.
These prices are for materials cost only...this is what it costs the crafters to make. Prices are rounded up slightly for raw materials wastage and possible failures and are not exact(When yer spendin 75 gold on armor, are ye really goin ta notice that it was rounded up 40 silver?).

Prices are for a full suit. Chain prices assume no helpful tailor is around and leather backing must be purchased from a vendor.
