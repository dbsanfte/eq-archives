The lass kissed me cheek... (back)
By Gumples

Me was 'avin' a gud day,
Me was 'avin' a gud week.
Bu' things got e'en better,
When tha lass kissed me cheek.

Me wuz sweatin' so much
Me was afeared me wou' reek.
Methinks she nae minded
Cuz tha lass kissed me cheek.

Me was skairt ta look,
Me was skairt ta peek.
Me kept me eyes closed,
As tha lass kissed me cheek.

Tha sky 'ad been cloudy,
The weather looked bleak.
Bu' tha sun did come out,
After tha lass kissed me cheek.

Tha most meanest dwarf
Wou' suddenly turn meek
If'n dey'd e'er 'ad
Dis lass kiss 'is cheek.

After it 'appened
Me cou' nae speak.
Me wuz so 'appy
Miss Nalynn kissed me cheek!
