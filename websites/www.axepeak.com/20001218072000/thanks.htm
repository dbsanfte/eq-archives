Special Thanks (back)

No successful guild is the work of a single person. Hence, this page introduces and thanks many of the most influential members of Axepeak.

NYSTRAMO

Nystramo, known as the Olor Kuld among kin, was the founder of the Society of the Stone, which later became Clan Axepeak. No dwarf has ever given more of his time and effort to the guild. He was the chief of Axepeak for a large part of the guild's life, and continually strived to make it a haven for dwarves and roleplay in Neverwinter Nights. The success of Clan Axepeak can be directly attributed to his efforts. I consider myself lucky to have adventured with Nys, and I hope all kin get the opportunity to adventure with him sometime.

 PATROC1US

Pat is one of only two dwarves who can claim to have been there when the Society of the Stone was formed. He has served as both Under Lord and War Lord, and is responsible for many of the current traditions of the clan. His appearances in the realms are now few and far between, but every kin should show Pat (or as we have affectionately dubbed him, "Scowler") the respect he deserves.

MELKARRIS, HIUFANG LI

Although these dwarves are no longer active in the clan, they each contributed greatly to Axepeak during their stay here. All dwarves should remember their names, as we all owe them a debt of gratitude.

DWYVYRN, STARKADH

These stout dwarves have contributed much to Clan Axepeak. They have both willingly taken on any task that was asked of them, helping the guild to stay something resembling organized in difficult times. Each has been more than happy to assist young dwarves gain experience, and all present fantastic role models for new members in terms of roleplay. They are valued advisors, and are among the first consulted when any decision needs to be made.

GIMKUR

The founder of the duergar Clan Crackskull gave Clan Axepeak a shot in the arm when it needed it most. Gimkur's contributions to the roleplay between dwarves and duergar won't be forgotten.
