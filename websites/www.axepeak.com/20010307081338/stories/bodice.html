Bodice Rippin' Time (back)
(as sung by a third rate halfling bard who saw it all)

"Clan Axepeak rolled in late last night,
Drinking and talking, a normal sight,
But very soon they weren't alright,
It's bodice rippin' time!

Seems there was a drow love gem,
Makes the lasses raise the hem,
The lads get hot, chase after them,
It's bodice rippin' time!

The Axepeak lads flashed lusty smiles,
As they surveyed the feminine wiles,
Morning would be full of denials,
It's bodice rippin' time!

The Axepeak lasses were not shy,
A kiss and hug for every guy,
And then the plate mail began to fly,
It's bodice rippin' time!

All too soon their chief comes in,
Dove in to break up the tangle of sin,
And splash some water on his kin,
It's bodice rippin' time!

He flung the gem out the window,
I'm sad to sing, it ended the show,
I'll bet it was some afterglow,
It's bodice rippin' time!"
