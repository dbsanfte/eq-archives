By popular demand, I've put up this basic cost sheet fer weapons. This is a view of what it takes in material costs fer a smith ta make yer weapons.
Prices are rounded up, and are meant to give ye an idea of how much that next weapon is goin ta cost ye, rather than give ye an exact pricing guide. Yer smith will know th' exact cost, and will be able to shave more'n a few silvers off the prices listed here.
