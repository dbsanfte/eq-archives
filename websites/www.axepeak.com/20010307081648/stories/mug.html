Ode Ta Me Mug (back)

by Tigguhr

Now sum o’ tha lads gits tha Lasses,
They be after tha kiss 'n tha hug.
Bu' I nae wants all o tha trouble,
So's I be in luv wit me Mug!

Me mug be a joy ta be holdin',
Round bottom 'n open at top.
When e'r I wants ta, I grabs it,
Nae onct ha' it tol' me ta stop.

'Course me mug be rite fine ta looks a'
I fills it 'n it sure grabs me eye.
When drained it sits wanton 'n empty,
I kinna waits ta fills it up high.

Tho it ha' a big mouth it be silent,
Nae complainin' if I be ou' late.
Lashed snug ta me belt thru tha fitin',
Won't run off if I dies ta tha gate.

In a pinch it does rite nice in pub brawls,
If I kinna gits me axe up too fas'.
Grabs tha handle 'n bonks on a ruffian,
Bet ya nae kin do tha' wit yer lass!

Aye, we be tagether fer ages,
Onct steals it from an Ogre King.
Committed we are to each udder,
Bu' I nae has ta buy it a ring.

On dis St. Vals I drinks ale quite contente'
Bu' me mug then gits bumpt ta tha floor.
Da young barmaid bends o'er ta retrieves it,
Of a sudden I nae be so sure.

"Har Dee Do" I shouts as I eye 'er.
"Moon's ou'dis noche" I growl!
Up quick as a minx she then decks me,
And binds me hands up wi' a towel.

Tossed ou' ta tha alley likes garbage,
I vows ta stay true ta me mug.
Assured o' hot noches tagether,
An' nae question tha we drops ta the rug!
