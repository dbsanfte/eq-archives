Dwarven Ranks and Titles (back)

Dwarves, traditionally speaking, are a very hierarchical society. They love nothing more than worrying about titles and rank. (Well, maybe gems. And ale. O.K., and battle. Hmm.... ;D). To reflect this, Clan Axepeak offers a comprehensive system of distributing titles to members of the clan.

Each dwarf in the clan will have at least one title to include after their name, should they so desire. (And what dwarf wouldn't desire to add a title? The standard title reflects how many experience points a dwarf has earned in the game, and all dwarves are given a title in this system. Other titles are optional, and are earned by systematically killing traditional dwarven enemies found in the game or through promotion to the ranks of guild leadership.

Below, each ranking system is described in detail, allowing any clan member to determine which titles he or she is entitled to.

EXPERIENCE TITLES

1. Any dwarf in the clan that has not yet reached 9th level is known as a Grunt.

2. Any dwarf that has reached 9th level and has passed the Trial of the Soulforger is known as a Clansman/Clanswoman.

Afterwards, higher ranking titles are achieved by achieving higher levels:
 
10-19 Warrior
20-29 Veteran
30-39 Battlerager
40-49 Champion
50-??? Hero/Heroine
???? Legend

It is customary to append 'Dwarven' before all of these titles when signing documents and such.
 
 

CHIEF'S GUARD (Gor Kuld - War Axes)

The Chief’s Guard (Gor Kuld) is made up of full members. A position of the highest honor and rank, the guards are selected by the Rrin Kuld and are known by their number (The Tenth of the guard, etc....). The first of the guard will be the next in line for promotion to Rrin Kuld (Over Axe, 3rd in command), if something should happen to the Chief or other officers (disappearance, resignation etc....). It shall be the new Rrin Kuld's duty to name a new First of the Guard. (No more than 1/6 of the total membership and no more than 10 Guards at any one time.)

WARDENS - (Rorn Barak - War Shields)

The Rrin Kuld may also appoint Wardens as he sees fit, selecting appropriate titles for worthy candidates.
