Dwarven Expressions (back)

 

 

Here is what a typical dwarf will say in certain situations.

"Slag!" - Can be used as an insult or a general curse.

"Sandstone!" - A general curse.

"Gordul!" - Another general curse.

"Well I'll be a bearded halfling!" - An expression of surprise.

"By the beard of Reorx!" - An expression of surprise.

"Death from below!" or "Raugh ar dun!" - The official Clan Axepeak battle cry.
