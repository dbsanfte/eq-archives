Ritual of the Soulforger (back)

Back at the beginning of time, deep within the Seven Heavens
Moradin the Soulforger worked at the fires of the Eternal Forge
Creating the races that would populate Faerun
One day, while Moradin worked at the forge, creating
the souls of the dwarven race a great giant came and challenged the Soulforger
Moradin turned from the souls in the Eternal Forge to face this beldarakin
A titanic battle erupted within the heavens.
Thunder shook all of Toril as Moradin's hammer struck the foul giant.
Lightning flashed across the heavens as the giant's sword glanced off Moradin's shield
For many days the battle raged within the skies,
and all the while the dwarven souls hardened in the fires of the Eternal Forge.
Blood from Moradin's wounds fell onto the dwarven souls,
and so they were imbued with his spirit.
Sweat from the Soulforger's brow also fell on the dwarves,
and so they received his strength and perseverance.
Finally, Moradin delivered a blow that felled the mighty giant, ending the battle.
Returning to his forge, he drew the souls of the dwarves from out of the flames.
Cooling them with his breath, he said...
"This race shall be most dear to me, for it has spent the longest time in my forge,
and has been tempered with my sweat and blood."
With that, he cast the souls down from the heavens, and the dwarven race was born.
And so today we gather, to honor Moradin...
And to prove that his blood runs strong in (name)'s veins.
As Moradin the Soulforger granted the ethereal giant death up above
So shall (name) grant this giant...DEATH FROM BELOW!!!
