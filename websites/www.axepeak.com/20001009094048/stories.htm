The Forger's hand is ever nigh 
To the children born neath earthen sky
Mount as deep 
As sky is high
Belongs to those He forged to keep

The anvil, hammer, mithral vein
The blows of Forger hark to rain
Down upon the virgin steel
Children formed as evil's bane
Knee's which ere to the Forger kneel

Strength and honor laid to geas
Within these souls he forged the key
To how the mountains live and die
The Forgers pledge is given thee
You children born neath earthen sky

Honor keep the kingdom given
Halls of mount must naer be riven
Live and fight and forge and die
As Children of the Earthen sky

A Classic, By Storm MoradinSon
