Thank you, for expressing an interest in joining Clan Axepeak!

Clan Axepeak is a Dwarven guild that focuses heavily on role-playing. Currently, our most significant presence is in Everquest, but we are in other realms as well. if you are interested in a guild that focuses more on role play than in producing the most powerful characters in as little time as possible we are the guild for you. In order to be considered as a member, we ask that you take a few minutes to tell us about yourself and get into the spirit of the Guild by doing the following:

  1. Take a moment to email us a membership inquiry (see below).

  2. We also require players to create a character profile, in the Profiles Etched in Stone section of our Message Board. Note that the first time you go to our board you must register.

  3. Now the fun part! You will need to be recommended by at least two current members. The best way to do this, of course, is to meet up with members and adventure with them. In Everquest, You can find out what members are online by using the "/who clan all" command. Just introduce yourself and let us know you're considering joining the clan. If you are not an Everquest player, introduce yourself on the Boards after you post your profile. Guild members will welcome you.

The kinds of things we look for are role playing ability , ability to work well in a group in Everquest (i.e. sharing loot, looking out for other party members in tough fights, etc.), and overall friendliness, both to Clan and Non-Clan players. Please note that finding clan members and grouping with them is your responsibility. While the Clan Elders will help you find a group if asked, you must take the first step by asking for that help. To get a more complete idea of what we are about, visit the rest of our website. You will also find a complete membership list of the clan here, including a list of Elders should you need to contact us with questions. It will give you a good idea of the kind of experience Clan Axepeak is looking to create for its members.
