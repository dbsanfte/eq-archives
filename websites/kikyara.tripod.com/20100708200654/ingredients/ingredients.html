Ingredients



This is a complete list of ingredients actually known or hinted. If the receipt is still unnamed the "used in" field says unknown. That does just mean that the name is not known to me, the receipt exists anyway. The cp aside a merchant is the price of that ingredient with about 95 charisma and indifferent faction (the prices will be recalculated soon), a % value aside a NCP drop indicates the approximate drop rate (based on the table of Quip Quarrel but adjusted if his values are not corresponding to my own ones). The value in the braces aside a poison name is the tier of the poison. That makes life easier figuring out what ingredients I actually really really need. The notes are added as I felt they are useful. Special Thanks to Rirek Ebonheart for his help with some of the harder ingredients. Enjoy.
