The prices are given for indifferent with charisma 95 in coppers (and will be reviewed soon). If you have missing informations or correctiions please post it on the poison-board. The make and apply are the skill where working with that poison becomes trivial. They are not perfectly accurate and will be updated as soon as any mistakes will show up.
