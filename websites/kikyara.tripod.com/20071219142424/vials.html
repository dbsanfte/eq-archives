Vials



Vials are for storing the produced poison in a flask. You can buy them or produce them with pottery. It is strongly recommended you learn pottery because the produced vials are much cheaper than the bought one and you can make quite a coin from selling them. There are three kinds of vial.

    Poison Vial
    This are the vials for easier poisons. After the first tier they are not used anymore. This is the simplest and cheapest vial of all.
    Lined Poison Vial
    When the poisons get tougher you need vials that are better than the regular ones. Lined vials are the vials for the vials for the first and second tier.
    Sealed Poison Vial
    This is used for the strongest poisons from the second up to the fifth tier. They are quite expensive but the choice of a high seasoned rogue.


Suspension Fluids



The suspension fluids are liquids that are used to dissolve and bind the ingredients. Different suspensions are needed for different poisons. All of this suspensions have to be purchased from a merchant.
    Suspension
    The simple suspension for everyday. Used only in the first tier.
    Constrict Suspension
    A better version of the ordinary suspension used mainly in the second tier. Occassionally you need it also in the first and third tier of poison.
    Larent Suspension
    Used for the tough poison of the 3rd tier the larent suspension has excellent quality and will hold the ingredients closely eternal. Handle this with care anyway.
    Etheral Suspension
    Only the strongest poisons need the etheral suspension. This kind of suspension is the magic kind for ingredients that refuse to bind with any other kind of suspension. Therefore it is expensive and hard to get. Not every merchant is able to sell you this precious fluid.
