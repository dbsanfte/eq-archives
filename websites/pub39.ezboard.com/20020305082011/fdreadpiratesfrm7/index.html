This is the story of how the Dread Pirates came to be....were you one of those spoken about? 
 Tell the Story from YOUR point of view!
~~~~~~~~

Once there was a day when the common rogue turned on even each other.
 The Guilds were Corrupt and self serving, requiring service with little gratitude 
for the hard work. To be in even such a guild required you abandon your honour 
in many forms. Rogues, Gypsies, Bards, and so many others found 
themselves torn...Promises of adventure were certain, but at what cost?
 Roberts was one such rogue. 
Furious when asked to beytray everything the rogue stood for, Roberts 
rebelled. Many followed, and not just Rogues, Bards, Gypies and Warriors...But Clerics, 
Necromancers, Magicians....Even the powerful Elemental 
masters known as Wizards. 


But where would they go? The Lands were already overpowered and abused 
by the corruption and greed...A band of outcasts with Honour had no place 
among them, nor did they wish for one...Desperate Roberts called upon the God Bristlebane
 for help. Bristlebane was amused at this dilemia...
A rogue with honour indeed! How Laughable... 

"I will grant you a Blessing Roberts," he said with a sly smile. "But it comes with a 
catch, are you willing?"


"What kind of catch Bristlebane? Your games can be deadly and I have 
lives to count for" Roberts countered warily. 


Bristlebane frowned slightly, and his eyes glinted with something 
unintelligable. "Don't be a fool Roberts...I am Giving you an entire world of your 
own...although others shall be born into it like any other world, it was made 
for my promise to you. The clause is not deadly...
but nor is it reversable...Answer me and answer now, or forfeit and go back to your 
current world of anarchy and deceit" 

Roberts thought soberly for a moment, lowering a troubled head and silently 
apologizing for bringing whatever "curse" upon the loyal band of followers.

"I accept your offer Bristlebane...Give Me our world"


The God Bristlebane was amused at the apology and informed Roberts as much. 

"Rare is the chance to play such a sport..." He said, smirking, and with that he 
turned and consorted with other gods and godesses...
Centuries to them perhaps, a mere eyeblink to Mortals. "Go to Sleep Roberts...
and tell the others to do the same,
you will awaken in your new world soon." and with that Bristlebane vanished, 
leaving Roberts to the 
thoughts haunting the sleep that would soon come.

Roberts did just that...
Surely enough Bristlebane kept his word....
here was the world of uncompared chance and adventures...
but Roberts awoke Alone..

Half a world away was the loyal followers of Roberts, waking up in a strange new form...
and in a odd city...

GNOMES...They were all gnomes!

...undaunted the crew searched and sought each other out once again...
Donning the Name "the Dread Pirates" after the brethern in Velious, they
 now are back in full force...

And an entire world to explore...and aquire.
