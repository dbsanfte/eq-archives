We will help.  Want to become effictive quickly, thats why we are here. Want to go out and tackel creatures you belive are to powerfull?   We will aid thee.  Want to be someone special. You are and will be treated as such.  Want to be part of an elite fighting force who can battle toe to toe with the best of them?  Well buckel up and be prepaired to practice practice practice!   We encourage mock combat with thy brothers and sisters.  Orginized events will take place often to raise or skills in warfare, magic, and in other skills.  What makes us diffrent from any other guild?  Well we have orginized squads that most other guilds do not.  We will have Mounted archers, and foot archers, we will have calvery, we will have scouts, we will have healers, we will also enlist special persons to be used as spies.  Why not just do things on your own and not take any orders.   We'll going out on your own or in a small group is fine.  But many are not powerfull enough to go out and take on the really powerfull creatures one on one or even 4 on one.  Ever want to take down a dragon?  An Ancient wyrm?  Or a group of murders?  Tired of going to your favorite hunting area only to find killers have now over ran the area and left you to hunt the areas that are over hunted or where no creatures lair?  Well friend if you can commit yourself to practice, and training, and can prove yourself loyal  you may become a member of the militia.  There are a few test, and one must practice the ways of the code.
