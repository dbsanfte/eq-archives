Fearless Black Vs. Unrest

August 28th, 2001

Sunday's Unrest Raid went well. We met at Dagnor Cauldron where Lerkin was very generous to give out loot before the raid. Many thanks to Lerkin! ^_^ We split our members into two groups. Vivii's team was made up of Lerkin, Laelin, Lildee and Vivii. Maulo's team was made up of Ealadn, Sonaki, Karmer and Maulo. We were all given Wolf Form by Lashwana and Tishla, btw many thanks to Lashwana and Tishla, and made our way to the basement. We were in the Ghost room hoping the of getting a Darkbone Skull from a skelly for the Acumen Mask quest but no dice. We held the Ghost Room until we lost Sonaki and Karmer to a small train. While waiting for an exp rez the Ghost, Tentacle Terror and everything in the basement imaginable popped in the ghost room and over powered us. We gunned for the zone but Vivii evaced her team to NK. Maulo's team was either dead or gated. Maulo successfully evaced himself from the pittrp by the bloodpool while being beaten to death by a giant mushroom. We regroup at Faydark and headed back towards Unrest. After Sonaki and Karmer were rezed, we were all given Wolf Form by Lashwana and Tishla, again many thanks to Lashwana and Tishla. We made our way to the hand room in hopes of him popping and dropping a mana bracelet but no luck. At about 7PM PST after killed Hags and PHs we called it a night. We Ported to Faydark and rolled on loot. Some highlights of the loot were a Festering Cloak that was won by Laelin and a Gossimer Sleeves won by Lildee.
