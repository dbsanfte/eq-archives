Fearless Black Vs. Runnyeye II - The Rematch

October 15th, 2001

Great turn out to Runnyeye II raid! We held the "Spike" room for about an hour. During that time we pulled Lord Pickclaw and paid him back for the last raid. While in the "spike" room we looted a lot of Blackened Alloy Armor for Impalanator. We pulled Lord Picklaw and Fearless Black members beat him into submission. He dropped a Grotesque Mask and a couple Blackened Alloy Items.

We moved to the Evil Eye Prisoner, room where we contemplated on how to pull the Evil Eye Prisoners, which we Wizard Class Evil Eyes. We decided to just beat them with blunt objects which worked very well. Tinkya was able to loot the top and middle Rune of the Dwarf for Verde's Crafted Shoulder Quest Pieces. Kajic pulled Lord Pickclaw and Fearless Black members again beat with ugly sticks. He dropped a Blackened Alloy Coif and some armor. Laelin won the roll for the Blackened Alloy coif and Tinkya won the roll for the Polished Bone Bracer. This was a great raid by far! Thanks to all that came!
