Guild Lore and Rules
Guild Lore
Bound together by the shadows of Norrath, good and evil come together to form Fearless Black. Be you Dark Elf or High Elf, Cleric or Necromancer; we all come together as brethren to protect those in need. Fear nothing for you are not alone. Classified you will not be, for all are treated equally in Fearless Black.
Jobs and rules for the Guild Leader

I. Guild Leader will always lead from the front never the rear..
~The Guild Leader will be always lead by example and always have your back.~

II. Guild Leader helps solve guild arguments.
~Everyone needs someone to talk to every now and then. The Guild Leader as well as all the other Guild Officers and Members are here, and willing to listen.~

III. Guild Leader appoints new officers as needed.
~New officers will be created as needed. Officers will be voted on by the current officers in the guild and the guild leader. Final decision is always falls on the Guild Leader~

IV. Guild Leader is to guide our fellow brethren, and keep good guild morale.
~Will keep morale and sprits high. Problems solving, raid disputes, and final decisions will be made by the Guild Leader.~
Jobs and rules for the Guild Officers

I. They are to help the guild leader in planning and carrying out raids and events.
~Officers are to assist and carry out decisions made by the guild leader.~

II. They are to aid in member arguments.
~Officers should be mediators, and mentors.~

III. They are to keep an eye out for potential members.
~When we are ready to invite more members, officers must get together to review and discuss potential members before inviting.~

IV. Officers are in leadership positions and should act as so. Lead from the front and never from the rear.
~Being a bad leader can mean bring your own mirror to raids...so you can watch your own ass.~

V. Officers are encouraged to participate in our website and message board.
~Officers are expected give life to the website and message board. Be chatty!~
Guild Rules and Guidelines for all members

I. Respect your Brethren, your fellow Fearless Black members.
~Treat all members as if they were a sister or brother. In Alliances, please treat fellow allies as brethren as well.~

II. Always help fellow Norrathians in need.
~If you see someone in need or dying, please don’t turn your back! One day you will be in his place, and would want some help.~

III. Respect your Guild Leader and Officers.
~If you need help with anything, don’t be afraid to ask an officer. And if he can’t help, contact the guild leader.~

IV. Don’t be afraid to ask for help.
~Need help with a bind? Quest information, or even directions to a zone? Ask in guildchat, and we can help. Asking for Power leveling is NOT allowed.~

V. Hand-me-downs are cool.
~Hand me downs are encouraged, however asking for free armor/items in guildchat is not permitted. When you get free equipment, don't sell it. If you can't use it pass it down to another member who can use it.~

VI. No ‘Ninja Looting’.
~When grouped, please keep in mind about the others you are grouped with. Ninja looting is not allowed and frowned upon.~

VII. Don’t just drop out of a group.
~If you need to leave a group, give at least a 3-5 minute warning. This will give them time to adjust, or find a replacement for you.~

VIII. No bad comments, arguments or negative energy toward other Fearless Black members in guildchat, /ooc, /shout or the messageboard..
~Arguments should be dealt with in /tells. The problem is between you and another person, keep it between the two of you. If the problem continues, contact an Officer or the Guild Leader for help.~

IX. Wear your guild name with pride.
~When having attached to your name, please keep in mind that you are representing us as a whole. If you feel the need to do something evil/stupid (=P) please go /anon or target yourself and type /guildremove. 99% chance we will understand and invite you back. We also ask that if a friend is to borrow your character for whatever reason that you put him in /anon until your friend is done.~

X. Saying goodbye.
~Upon leaving the guild, one must send either the guild leader or an officer an e-mail prior to the character’s departure. Please say goodbye in guildchat, for we would like to wish a safe journey to the friend who is leaving. May he find his path along Norrath, and know that Fearless Black will always be his shadow, if he needs assistance behind him.~

Other notes:

1. We all know that this is just a game. Real life always comes first; so if you cannot attend raids/events/meetings, please let an officer know. (We do understand that things also happen unexpectedly, RL has a way of surprising everyone.)

2. Explanation of ‘Need Before Greed’: A friend once told me that I was not wrong in disagreeing with the common usage of ‘Need Before Greed’. “Need before greed, is greed itself,” he said. And it is true. Unless your group has planned and made getting a Ranger only item for a Ranger in the group its main goal, need before greed does not seem logical. In a group everyone is putting in his/her part into the group’s success. Healers heal, Tankers tank, and Nukers nuke. So it can be seen that all members should have the option of rolling on the dropped item. Now if twinking was really a bad thing, why were we provided with 8 slots to make characters? Twinking is encouraged, so by all means make your characters happy! =)

3. We are in this guild to help each other out, and to have a good time. Be yourself, and enjoy the company. Know that you are a part of something great, a fun bright group of intelligent people; you are a part of Fearless Black!
