6 . "Yes, you can solo for exp"
 Posted by Sagath on Jul-17-99 at 05:32 PM (EST)
Ever hear of ancient crocs? Way the hell down deep in guktop by the entrance to lower guk. When the ancient croc isn't spawning (which is very, very often that i doesn't), saltwater crocs, lvl 27-31 or 32 it seems, are spawning. while the gen rate is less than optimal (20 minutes for a chance at 1 ancient, 2 salts, or 3 salts as far as i can tell in the 5 hours i've camped there so far), the exp is good and the loot is very sweet if you get the ancient croc to spawn. one word of warning: Steelskin. You *must* use it on the ancient croc. otherwise, youa re toast, cause you can't swim faster than a croc, and the entire platform is surrounded by water.

-Sagath
