8 . "How to feign death....successfully"
 Posted by Neric on Aug-16-99 at 08:39 AM (EST)
Hi there,

first of all, once you attack something, it can see you everywhere in the zone. Invis will not stop that. Personally I think this is BS....but....

Feign Death is complicated ! Here are some things you have to remember:

1. Dont feign death while you have a pet. They will keep hitting you.

2. If you get the message "...has fallen to the ground", your attempt has failed! Get up and cast again...if you can. Make a hotkey for STAND.

3. Feign Death is a spell not a skill (lucky monks)...it can and will be interrupted.

4. Feign Death will not work until you stop attacking the opponent. Switch off melee!

5. It will not work when you have casted DoT on the opponent.

6. When you have feigned successfully...and the opponent has left the area...get up and GATE! They will come back for you ASAP.

7. When you play with a group inside a dungeon, they usually DONT want you to make a pet (to avoid the pet trains). DoT is bugged in dungeons...you will not get EXP if the opponent dies to DoT there. So you will use DD spells a lot. This is the situation where Feign Death comes in handy......

Conclusion: Feign Death will almost NEVER work! Get JBoots and use Harmshield....much more effective (especially in Unrest). On the other side, Feign Death ..as crappy as it is....has saved my life many times. I dont want to miss it.....

Good luck,
Neric Soulstalker (27th lvl Xegony)
