1 . "restoring dark elf faction"

You can kill wood elf slaves in crushbone with ease to restore your indigo brotherhood faction. Note however that you may have to contend with orcs, which will of course ruin your faction. but if you sneak into the slave house after someo ne offs the slavers you can take easy pickings of the slaves. On Veeshan where I am, Crushbone is overrun by players and basically a playground where an orc is mobbed and slain within seconds of spawning, so finding someone else to help with offing the orcs shouldn't be too hard.
