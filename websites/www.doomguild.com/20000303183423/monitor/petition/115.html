2 . "You can stop that from happening"
 Posted by Segal of KoT on Jul-07-99 at 06:57 PM (EST)

You have to turn accept trades off in your options window, then turn it on when people want to give you something. If you turn off trades your pet will not accept anything from another player. They put this in because on the REAL server, Rallos Zek, people would go into combat with necros by first giving their pet a rusty dagger or bixie stinger.

I kill clerics all the time, nobody steals my kills.... I surely don't envy you blue fudge factory players... Necros are one of the best PVP classes in the game.
