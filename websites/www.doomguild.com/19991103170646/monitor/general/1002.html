9 . "Long screen delay between switching to loot"
 Posted by Mortifer on Jul-19-99 at 12:16 PM (EST)
I used to have this problem too. I loved the full screen mode but hated the 2 second delay to loot as it switched over to different resolution Inventory screen. Solution: Go into Options ... general and select the Same Screen Resolution option. This makes the screen switch stay in the same resolution when it switches to the inventory screen. It will be smaller but you remove the 2 second resolutio nswitch! Enjoy that full screen!
