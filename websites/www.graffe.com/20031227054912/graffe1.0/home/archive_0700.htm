Manasink:
Ever since the data files for Kunark were released, everyone has been wondering what it is that the level 58 spell, Manasink, really does. On the spells page, I had originally guessed that it would absorb magical damage.
Recently, I was contacted by Sorasil Lorewind of Solusek Ro. Sorasil was the first wizard to contact me that actually had and has used the spell. Sorasil informed me that Manasink will stack with all other shields and has a very long cast time but couldn't notice what it's real use was without a lot more testing. I finally got ahold of Geoff Zatkin and got a confirmation of what the spell really does. This is his description, "manasink - think of it as another diamondskin, but it ONLY absorbs damage from spells". So, there we have it. The ever-elusive Manasink has been found, used, and now we know what it really does.

A huge thanks to Sorasil for diligently trying to find out what this spell does!

Wizard Shield Skin Trivia:
Wizard skin spells, such as steel skin and diamond skin will not absorb more damage than they were designed to, i.e. attacks will partially land when the skin wears off.

For example, if I choose to provoke a Level 60 Wizard Guild Master who attacks for 351 points of damage, while wearing a steel skin that absorbs 266 points of damage... On the first attack, I will still take 85 points of damage that my shield was unable to absorb.

Just something to keep in mind, when deciding whether or not to recast your skin spells, before they have expired.

Well, I died, due in no small part to my ignorance and distraction. Seems I was still in Lich form and not paying enough attention to that red line in the upper right. Even more sad, I now find myself returned to the Dreadlands, far from friends and corpse. Of course I am thinking at that very moment of my absence, the AC is stepping over my corpse and depression sets in. I shout for assistance and I pray to Innoruuk for a teleport to anywhere near S. Ro. Hope soars when Zanshin call out his willingness to return me directly to the pyramid in S. Ro. We meet, we teleport, we arrive, to the sounds of cries ... "AC HAS SPAWNED." Zanshin takes off for the sands, I follow fast, and over the first ridge there walks the beast. Zanshin engages, I engage, spells fly and the AC falls dead. My premonition found true, the AC corpse 5 yards from my very own. I am pleased at having seen the beast felled, but saddened with the knowing the kill is deserved by my savior. Zanshin asks me if the AC was why I was here and I say yes, for I am still sadly without the dearly sought jboots. Zanshin loots, but then a beautiful sound..."Synthia, loot the ring. I remember how hard life was w/o the boots." I am overwhelmed with joy and brought to tears and cheers. Zanshin will accept no payment but alas I persuade him to accept 10 peridots, mostly to make me feel better.

I want all of Norrath to know of Zanshin's noble deed and of my great appreciation. To the Brothers of Fire (to which I recently made another friend saving) I also express my thanks and my friendship. Heroism and Nobility like this should be sung far and wide. Master Graffe, please see fit to share my story with your followers."
