    Re: Wildstar Beta

    Today was the F2P Re-launch. I didnt like Wildstar when I played last year, didnt get far and deleted it from my computer. Decided to give it a run again today, 12GB download went quick and got in right away. They have had server issues all day, they've been hammered with players, same with new account signups but I can't fault them too much for that. Got to level 10, servers had a lag quite a bit, again due to a lot of players in newer zones which shows the high level of interest but they had to bring the servers down multiple times. I am enjoying it more this time, I plan to stick with it for a bit and see where this goes. Also did some PvP and was actually really fun. Once the early wave of people splits off and the server balances out more I look forward to hitting up more levels and content. I am playing Spellslinger which I am really enjoying. Rough first day for them but I do feel like the game has potential this time.
