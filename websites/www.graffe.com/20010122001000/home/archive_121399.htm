Endymion of Xegony wrote a rant article that is a reply/addition to the Plane of Fear article found on EQ Casters Realm written by Kalyn of Xegony. Click here to read the article from EQC then read below for Endymion's response:
Endymion - "Okay admitted this is biased since I'm a wizard but I wanted to say one other thing about making Fear so hideous. When you have a zone with risks like this people don't want to do them. That means that Paladins (well them less than others since there's 2 billion fetid fiends in the zone), Druids, Necromancers, Wizards (funny, 3 classes verant doesn't seem to like...), and Rangers, are out of luck. If I was an Enchanter or Magician I could have been on a few dozen Hate runs since it came out. On the other hand, unlike the nightly Hate runs, I've only been on 2 fear runs, not to mention that (sorry another wiz gripe) the Frightfingers are so few in number and far and away that they're often not pulled before everyone goes to bed.

Another question to Verant on caster armor: Planes armor has been shown to be the greatest armor in the game, all the thorny vine armor is the best for a ranger to wear, all the valorium is best for a paladin (minus red dragonscale), etc. So my question is, why does more than half the caster armor have ridiculously pathetic stats? The froglok skin mask is better than the cryosilk veil! An easy wizard quest item (runescale cloak) is FAR superior to the pathetic Cryosilk Cloak. I wouldn't even mind if the darn things were +7 to 2 useless stats, but they give negligable mana, worthless resistances, negligable ac, VERY little int (1 or 2), and only 1 or 2 to another useless stat.

Oh and long as I'm ranting, wizards got the shaft in the class specific gloves... enchanters got higher int from their gloves and a huge bonus to their other prime stat, charisma. Magicians got mor int than wizards. Wizards got a very small bonus to stamina.

Well while this turned into a fear, its equipment, and how it relates to wizards rant, I just want to say this:
-There's a reason noone wants to go to fear, but will go to hate which should be equally difficult. Wake up Verant.
-How many level 50 Necros, Enchanters, Magicians, and Clerics do you see playing their characters still? How many Druids and Wizards never touch their 50s anymore and started new characters (myself with a Paladin)?
-Endymion Lunarmyst, the Ranting Level 50 Wizard, Xegony"
