    Re: Alarm Clocks!

    I hate alarm clocks. I tried an radio alarm for awhile, but even ignoring the constant power outages forcing a reset, even music become irritating as an alarm. I hate abrupt noise so much that my body conditions itself to remember when an alarm would wake me up, and wakes me up minutes before so I don't have to listen to it.

    Right now I use the alarm function on my smartphone, which seems to be okay so far. Said alarm is the only noise my phone makes, because alarms aren't the only obnoxious noises I hate.
