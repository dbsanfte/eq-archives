Looks like there is a few out there confused on what Research Practice is. Research practice is something that Verant implemented to help us gain experience in Research without spending hard earned points or wasting VERY hard to find runes. There are two ways to go about this and it depends if you are of a good background or evil background. To practice, you will need the Concordance of Research Tome (which is a combining book much like the Lexicon) and the recipe book which is called 'Runes and Research Book 2'. You learn the recipes from the book Runes and Research Book 2, put the recipe items within the Concordance of Research (you also need a quill and parchment for each combination), and hit combine. Do it correctly and you should have some practice runes. To learn how to get the Concordance of Research and Runes of Research Books 1 and 2, goto the Quests page. There you need to pick if you are Good or Evil and do the quest accordingly.

Although Research Practice is up and running, it seems there is still a few flaws in it. Reports the some of the Beza runes are not being created easily.
