[edit] Dmitry the Wizzy

Actual photo of Dmitry as a child.

Dmitry is the resident target of first resort for gnome abuse on the Graffë forums.

[edit] Childhood to Graffe's Posting

Little is known about Dmitry's origins. Legend has it that he was born to a mixed parentage of lawn gnome and Boston terrier, in a barn in 1772, at which point he was loaded in a time machine and shot into the present time.
