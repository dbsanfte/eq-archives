    Tomorrow night is my first night of a new take on a book club:

    Please consider this your invitation to join The Peanut Park Perusers, a group dedicated to the sharing of fresh and enjoyable reading experiences. PPP seeks to discover contemporary fiction and non-fiction through community effort and exchange. Unlike a typical book club, we will not meet every month to discuss every book.
    PPP’s special characteristics include:
     an opportunity to enjoy new works;
     the input of other people’s choices, potentially broadening our horizons;
     for the price of one new hardcover book, we will each have the opportunity to read 10 other new books.
    Here is how PPP will operate
     We each choose a book that is newly published (not yet available in libraries) or one that you are certain nobody will have read.
     Please email xxx with your choice of book. XXX will ensure that each selection is different.
     We each buy a copy of our chosen book (hard cover is preferred) and bring it to our first gathering at xxx. At this introductory gathering, we engage in the drinking of wine or tea and hopefully, general hilarity. Don’t bring anything except your book. Our mantra...Keep it simple!
     At that first gathering you will be given a grid sheet showing the Schedule for passing your book along that evening and for the next months. On the third Wednesday of the month for the next ten months you will pass the book you currently possess to the next person shown on your grid. You will always be passing to the same person. We discourage early or late passing unless you are out of town on the pass date. At the end of the Schedule you will end up with the book you purchased which you will retain.
     We will meet again at times we schedule at our first meeting. Our gatherings will be casual. We hope to socialize and talk about the books in general and have some fun.
     At the final gathering, we all will have read all of the books. We will have the opportunity to discuss them if we wish. Of course, there will be more socializing and fun. Then we can decide whether the project is worthwhile pursuing again.
    Please reply with your choice of book. If you have duplicated someone else’s choice, xxx will advise and you can choose another book.
    ---------------------------
