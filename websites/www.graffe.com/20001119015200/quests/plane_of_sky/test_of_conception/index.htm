This quest is given by Abec Ianor within the Plane of Sky. This quest will reward you with a ring.

You say, 'I wish to test my powers'

You say, 'Conception'

Abec Ianor says 'Conception is vital. Travel beyond and bring forth an Efreeti Statuette, a Mithril Air Ring, and a Box of Winds. From these I will produce a ring that will protect you wherever you go.
