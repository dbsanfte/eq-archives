    Acoustic fingerstyle guitarist Andy Mckee -- easily one of the best talents I have ever seen. This particular video is the #1 all time music video on youtube, and I think #7 overall, and it should be.

    The link goes to his most popular video there, "Drifting." I urge people to listen to his other stuff on youtube as well, in particular "Rylynn" and "For my Father."     Can't say enough good things about this musician.
