Unique Stein of Moggok:
Curdardh Teyrnon, a level 51 Wizard on Terris-Thule, was recently rewarded with a rather nice variant to the Stein of Moggok by a GM during an event. The difference between his Stein and the normal Stein, is that his can be held in the primary hand slot while the normal Stein can only be held in the secondary slot. This allows him to hold both at the same time!

Some may remember the very original stein, known as the Glowing Stein. This is a direct descendant to that version before it was removed/switched in late August of 99.

Staff of Temperate Flux Trick:
Goeshen sent in a little trick that some of us may know of. If you don't, here it is -

"... I have become aware that this staff will also 'suppress' fire damage shields for the duration of the LowerElement effect on the staff. This damage shield is most typically found on Wizard mobs in OS (Old Sebilis).

This has proven most useful in OS, Chardok and HS (Howling Stones). It will save having to annull these shields, and the cast time is negligible for the group.

This does not work on some dmg shields (the Necromancer at the Jail in OS comes to mind), that use a ice-based shield. At any rate, just another good use for our trusty aggro stick =)

Goeshen Banecaster

Phiningel Poofs:
Pandur of Xegony sent in a little warning on the corpse rotting time of Phinigel Autropos -

"I am just comming from a Kedge Keep raid, and I don't want this to happen to other people :-) After we sucessfully killed Phinigel Autropos, we began to rez people and prepare lotto. Verant stated in one of the last patch-messages that the rot time on high level encounters has been increased. Phinigels corpse decayed in UNDER 5 mins, and with him a robe and 2 other items that i cant remember. A GM that was called to look into this plainly told us that the decay time for under level 55 mobs (Phinigel seems to be level 44) is defined as "5 minutes or LESS(!)". There is nothing guides will do if you cant loot it fast enough.

Maybe you could warn the fellow wizards out there :-)

Pandur Volucris, 48 wiz of Xegony"
