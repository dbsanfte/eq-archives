Abashi on High Level Mob Resistance:

Player: Gordon, could you state your 'view' on the MR of high creatures ( those 49+ ? ) or could we possibly setup some type of poll to see how many enchanters feel they are an effective part of the party when dealing with planes/giants/dragons and what their feelings on MR are?

Abashi: Certain special encounters (dragons, gods) have high resistances (to the point of being immune) to all but one type of magic. Normal monsters typically don't have the incredibly high resists across the board.

We realize that this sometimes limits the damage-dealing potential of some classes, however those classes typically have either other ways of dealing damage (e.g. pets) or have other uses besides dealing damage (e.g. buffs).

-Gordon

More of EQ's History Unfolds:
Madcat Warwiz of Prexus, Nebucadnezzar of Bertoxxulous, and Ebin Ashelin of Bertoxxulous all sent in screenpics and word of a GM ran event that is continuing to teach us players of the history of Norrath. Here is what Madcat said concerning the event:

It started in Everfrost. These four people in Shining Metallic Robes showed up asking to be taken to Permafrost. We were there killing Ice Giants so we met them and took them into Permafrost. At the first ladder into Perma, they met a dwarf who had a chrystalline shard. Supposedly this dwarf stole the shard from the dragon, Lady Vox and was employed by Mayong Mistmoore. The four GM's killed the dwarf after a long conversation with him. Apparently this upset Vox and Mayong. Vox never did appear though she was just heard screaming in the background. From there it moved to Rathe Mountains by the Sphinx. Using the shard, three of the GM's were turned into dragons. Just as quick as the dragons appeared Mayong appeared and I know he killed one of them not sure what happened to the other two. Of course the crowd went ballistic on Mayong but he was dropping people all over the place and he only got to about 3/4 health then disappeared. He dropped my level 48 warrior friend with one spell. OUCH! After all that the remaining GM's gave an ogre warrior the shard which turns out is a nail from Veeshan. Pretty nice 1hs 16dam 24dly effect incapacitate sword. The four GM's were from the Ring of Scale and vowed to get Mayong. Also by killing the ogre warrior who got the sword and rebirthing him somehow the curtains hiding the plane of air were lifted or that is what they said anyways.
