Gem Descriptions | Mana Calculator

Level Twenty Nine

 

Bonds of Force
A direct descendant of the Druids spell, 'Ensnare'.. this will slow your enemy down to walking speed (reduces movement by 55%). Take note that this is an Area Effect spell and can grasp up to 4 mobs in it's radius. Do not cast in melee range for risk of rooting yourself. Also, if there is a couple of mobs in the vicinity of where you cast and it hits them, you will be added to thier aggro list.

Energy Storm
This area effect spell has the added bonus of sapping the effected creature's stamina.

Evacuate North
The Evacuation series of spells works exactly like the Portal series, in that they will transport your entire group to the specified location. However, there is a greater chance of leaving a group member behind in the cast. The upside of these spells is that they cast much faster than the portal spells (once you've cast a portal, you'll realize quickly why you don't want to try it in an emergency). This one transports to North Karana.

Inferno Shock
This direct damage spell based is based on fire, the usual upgrade to your shock spells. 237 damage while using 135 mana.

Imbue Fire Opal
This spell can only be scribed and used by wizards that are followers of the diety, Solusek Ro. You can only purchase this spell from a vendor in the very bottom of Solusek B named Zordak Ragefire (who is part of the cleric's epic quest). This spell is used to imbue fire opals with magical properties so that they may be used to create player made armor.

Magnify
This is the continuation of the Glimpse/Gaze line, offers an extended range of vision and a longer duration. Also gives Ultravision when used.

Portal Fay
This transports an entire group to the same location as Fay Gate. When portaling, all group members should be within 10 feet of the wizard. Some myth holds that having each member click on the wizard as his current target decreases the chance of leaving anyone behind there is no proof behind this but it wouldn't hurt. Portal spells cast very slowly (in upwards of 15 second casting time).

Portal North
This transports an entire group to the same location as North Gate.

Portal Tox
This transports an entire group to the same location of the Tox Gate.

Shock Spiral of Al'Kabor
Like the other Al'Kabor spells, this is an area effect spell that radiates out from the center targeted mob. Also like all of the other area effect spells, it brings alot of attention to yourself from the mobs.

Thunder Strike
Continuing the tradition of bringing you two forms of direct damage spells each level, Thunder Strike offers electrical damage to offset Inferno Shock's heat damage. Note that heat spells function with decreased effectiveness in rain while electrical spells function with increased effectiveness. 247 damage at 150 mana used. Unfortunately, it gets resistes like the level 20 Force Shock spell. Good for disrupting spellcasters.

Yonder
A spell that was taken away from us after beta and only given to Druids, this has once again been added to our spell line. Yonder works much in the way that Fade and Shadow Step do only transporting you a bit further then the others. Been told a great way to get out of a sticky situation is to Fade, Shadow Step, Yonder, Gate. Although this sounds great, I cant imagine having all of those loaded upon my list at once.
