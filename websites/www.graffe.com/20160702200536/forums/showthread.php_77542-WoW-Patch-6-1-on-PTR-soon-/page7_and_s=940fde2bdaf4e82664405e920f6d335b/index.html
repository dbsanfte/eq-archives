    Re: WoW Patch 6.1 on PTR (soon)

    You can make a pile of gold off missions alone, but only in the right circumstances. To get several hundred gold per day, consistently, you'd need the Inn for the treasure missions, and some way of DE'ing items (there's usually a hut available in Premade Groups, but the hut gives crap-all when it DE's). You could make much more than that just by keeping your barn stocked and auctioning off the right salvage items, but a lot of people want to actually use the bloods for personal gear. I see bloods crashing right now, so I suspect the supply is now a problem and that cash cow is about to dry up.

    I don't see initial price being 250 K. Someone like myself, close to gold cap, wouldn't even consider 30 days of time for 250 K. I think it's more likely that the initial price will be in the 50 K range. Googling "WoW gold buying price", I see $0.60 to $0.80 per 1000 gold in the results (I refuse to click through to investigate further). I think the proposed price of the tokens was $20, so 50 K gold would be better than what gold sellers are currently charging. If the devs could figure out a way to kill the level 1's selling TCG mounts/pets in Trade, those tokens would put serious pressure on the gold sellers.

    Re: WoW Patch 6.1 on PTR (soon)

    I decided to see what I was actually making from setting up garrison missions each morning before work as well as each evening before bed. I'll only be vendoring stuff, no disenchanting, no auctioning.

    This is only yesterdays data sample (because I've having to reinstall wow today) but I'll update again in a week with how things have gone.

    Character 1 - Level 100 warrior - 20 followers - level 2 tavern, level 3 salvage, 1 x follower with gold boosting (most followers at 660)
    Mission rewards - 770 gold, 2 blue items (29g), 9 crates (19g grey items, 172g green/blue items) - total for the day 990g

    Character 2 - Level 100 priest - 24 followers, no tavern, level 3 salvage, no followers with gold boosting (most followers at 660)
    Mission rewards - 195 gold, 4 blue items + 1 purple (103g), 14 crates (39g grey items, 227g green/blue items) - total for the day 564g

    Character 3 - Level 100 Monk - 23 followers, level 1 tavern, level 3 salvage, no followers with gold boosting (all followers 645).
    Mission rewards - 1002 gold, 1 blue item + 2 purple items (60g), 15 crates (36g grey items, 241g green/blue items) - total for the day 1339g.

    Looking at that, chars 1 & 3 gold rewards will almost certainly prove to be abnormal. Character 2 probably going to prove to be closest and I'm predicting a norm of about 500g. After a week I'll get chars 1 & 3 to a level 3 tavern and see what effect that has.
