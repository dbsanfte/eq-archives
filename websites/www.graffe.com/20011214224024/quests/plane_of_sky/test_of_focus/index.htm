This quest is given by Neasin Leornic within the Plane of Sky. This quest will reward you with Al`Kabors Cap of Binding.

You say, 'Focus'

Neasin Leornic says 'Focus is a must. Travel among the residents of the sky and bring to me an Iron Disk, an Ethereal Opal, and a Woven Skull Cap. This will prove your ability to focus and I will reward you with Al`Kabors Cap.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Items Needed:

  • Iron Disk: (Droppable) Found upon Azarack's upon Azarack Isle.
  • Ethereal Opal: (Droppable) Found upon Watchful Guards or the Avenging Gazer on Gorgolosk Isle.
  • Woven Skull Cap: (No Drop) Found as a rare drop upon the Keeper of Souls.
