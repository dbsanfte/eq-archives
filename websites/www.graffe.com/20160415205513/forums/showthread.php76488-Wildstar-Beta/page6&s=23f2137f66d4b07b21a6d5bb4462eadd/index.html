    Great to hear that Carbine is responsive to kill quest complaints. A lot of them could just be cut in half, really.

    I try to group these quests. There's one in Whitevale that's needed to progress the regional story - and that's the one with the stealthed agents which clock in at 2% per kill.

    The others I've tried to group as much as possible - also grouping kills. Which brings me to my next point: I've not seen an MMO with as many evading mobs since 2004. It's completely crazy - I've had to abandon quests because the target mobs kept resetting mid-fight, even if you fought them on the spawn, a little bit away from the spawn, far away from the spawn etc. Crazy. I've had quests that were done (Return to X, no other requirements) turn to failed because I did a PvP battleground. Have to re-do the quest to get it completed - and it was a regional story.

    And the engineer bruiser bot can't deal with ANY elevation - if you're fighting a mob that's on a hill, forget him responding to any commands. I've bound stop and dismiss to hotkeys - that alleviates some problems, but not all. In PvP, I get the "press this button to get out of CC"-interface permanently stuck in the middle of my screen, I have to reload the UI to make it go away.

    I'm really really trying to like this game. Truly, I am. But it's pretty hard at times. It's only because I like the core ideas that I've slogged through at this point. Most other games would have been met with an "aaah, fuckit!".
