    Re: Mark Hamill's Trickster Return Spotlighted in New Flash Photos

    If you actually read the quoted parts of the interview, the article writers and then fans are reading way too much into it. Pay careful attention to exactly how Kreisberg uses names, and you'll see it's pretty open to interpretation.

    Spoiler for more spoilery:
    All we know is that the Jay that was visiting them wasn't very trustworthy, and manipulated the team. My theory from earlier in this thread is still plausible. So is the Twins theory and the Earth-X Jay theory.

    We haven't seen any confirmation that the Jay Caitlin fell in love with was Evil.

    Expounding on my earlier theory since we have more details now: In the comics, Bart Allen has the ability to create clones. They can go off, and learn different things, and when they merge back with Bart, he gains their memories. They normally have a limited life span. They decay because they're created by the Speed Force, but they don't generate their own Speed Force like other Speedsters.

    So Jay was high on Velocity 5 or whatever, and created a clone. The Clone was more evil (maybe something happened), and didn't want to die, so he captured the real Jay Garrick, tossed a metal mask on him to prevent creating clones, and locked him up. Then he pretended to be Jay Garrick for some period of time, while he was also being Zoom. This is why Wells says he was a coward. I'm betting that Zoom can consume other clones to sustain his Speed Force, which is why Real Jay (Mask Guy) is still alive. To feed Speed Force clones to Zoom.

    Caitlin's Jay was another Clone. Thats why his cells were deteriorating. If he died (as opposed to being absorbed by Zoom), I think Mask-Jay gained his memories, so now he's aware of what Zoom is doing. I wonder if he wasn't aware the Clone still existed (perhaps if Zoom absorbs them Real Jay doesn't gain the memories, so he assumes that Jay-Clone died)


    The Twins Theory is basically this:
    Jay and Hunter are twins separated at birth on both worlds. On E2, Jay became the Flash, Hunter became Zoom. On E1, Jay was kidnapped by Zoom, had a mask locked on him, and brought back to be locked up on E2. E2 Jay was just killed. (Although we don't see a dead body, so the jury is out on whether he's dead or just maimed).

    Earth-X theory is pretty simple. There is just 1 Hunter/Jay per Earth, and Zoom is just an evil version that is hunting Speedsters. In this situation, we won't know who comes from where until they tell us.


    What I like about my theory is that we won't have to have any goofy depower or overpower mechanisms so that Barry can fight Zoom. It'll be a Team-Up of Real-Jay and Barry.
