If you already have the Staff of the Four, you can skip to here. If you already have your 1.5 epic, you can skip to here. Otherwise start reading below.

The Wizard's second Epic Quest begins at Talwyn Flamecaller in the Lava Storm Mountains. He is a High Elf Wizard located at -2170, -1050. He is high up in a little cubby on top of a wall.

NOTE: Talwyn's Location was changed with the Dragons of Norrath Patch. This is his new Location, in the new Lavastorm.
.

The Four Tests

You say, 'Hail, Talwyn Flamecaller'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'The fire of destruction burns within you, does it not? I can see that your affinity for destructive magic has served you well in your travels. It has served you so well that it has caught the eye of Solusek Ro. He has created and sent me here to discuss the matter of joining his legions. If you do, you will be granted power unheard of on mortal lands. What say you?'

You say, 'I agree'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'I knew you would. . . What wizard could refuse an offer of such power? Since you have proven yourself only through combat and not yet through adventuring, I require you to prove yourself worthy once more. A small set of tests, really.'

You say, 'what tests'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'The tests I require of you are merely to measure your ability as a wizard and follower of Solusek. They will draw upon the focal points of being a wizard. Through intelligence and its application to magic, you can call upon the elements more often without strain. Through subtlety, you are able to be destructive without calling the attention unto yourself. Through research, new, more powerful spells and incantations are discovered. Through channeling, the ability to concentrate on spells is enhanced, even in the most distracting of environments. Once you have completed these tests, return to me with the results, so that I may review them.'
 

The Test of Intelligence

You say, 'I am ready for the test of intelligence'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'Through the intelligent use of magic, we are able to become more efficient at what we do. To prove to me that you have this gift, I will need you to work your way through a set of riddles. These riddles will take you full circle, beginning with me and ending with me. The path between those two points must be discovered by your own mettle. Take this note and do as it instructs. Take care when handling these notes, they are the lifeblood that will guide you on your journey and should you lose them, you will fail the test. I hope to see you back here with the correct answer soon.'

The Test of Channeling

You say, 'I am ready for the test of channeling'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'In the fortress of the Sarnak, amidst some old bones, you will find a small, cursed trinket that I require you to obtain. I say that it is cursed because any that try to take it are surrounded in a swarm of scorpions. The scorpions are small and not dangerous by themselves, but when a hundred of them engulf your body, you can't stand but to run away. With your proficiency in channeling, you must go and retrieve this trinket for me.

Go to Lake of Ill Omen to the Chancellor's Room (Chancellor is the mob who drops the Jade Chokidai Prod for the Howling Stones key). On the table there is a skeleton mob that has no name but is able to be targeted. Killing it will spawn several green-con to 65 scorpions. Load up your Jyll's line of spells and kill them all. One of the scorpions will drop A Cursed Talisman. Bring this item back to Talwyn.

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'Your speed and concentration impress even me. The Test of Channeling is no easy feat to accomplish, especially with so many dangerous distractions nearby.'

Talwyn hands you back the Talisman.

When you have crafted the spell, return it to me so that I may inspect it.'

The Test of Subtlety

Before you may begin the Test of Subtlety, you must have completed the Test of Research.

You say, 'I am ready for the test of subtlety'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'In Steamfont you will find a friend of mine, Yama Twerik, with a bit of a dilemma on his hands. He was able to bring a non-functional clockwork back to Ak`Anon from the Plane of Innovation. He is by no means a wizard, so he needs help recharging the battery of the clockwork to get it working again. Go see, he will discuss with you the details of his predicament.'

Go to Yama Twerik in Steamfont. He is a gnome located on the very outside of the Minotaur Caves. Location 1260, -1760.

You say, "Hail, Yama Twerik"

I would like you to use your best electrical magic to try and resurrect his battery

You say, '"Resurrect his battery"

Yama Twerik says, "Ok good, but here is the thing, we have to be really careful. I don't want to overcharge the battery, and you will have to cast on it while I am holding the correct wires together. If you don't do it at the right time you will either fry me, or it will be a waste and we will have to start completely over. Let me know when you have cleared your mind and regained your magical strength, and are ready to start.

You say, "I am ready"

Yama Twerik says 'Excellent, then I shall begin performing my work.'

Yama Twerik says 'Stay sharp, young wizard, I will give you cue to begin to infuse this creation with your magics. Assembling the Wand

This is where you will begin the quest if you have already completed the Staff of the Four. You may have to hail Talwyn twice to get the appropriate dialogue.

You say, 'Hail, Talwyn Flamecaller'

You must now assemble a wand to help further focus your energies. In the lands of Discord, power surges and fades in an awesome display, the fluxing power flows through all things on the landscape. Go, investigate the lands of Discord; return to me with a fluxing rod, gem, rune, and ore. With them you will be ready to take your first steps as a new soldier.'

The Fluxing Gem

You say, "What fluxing gem?"

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'Fluxing gems have been found growing in the desert of Luclin. They form from the sand that has been blasted by magic. Sun Revenants have been known to collect them. Go to the plateau, and speak the word proclaim your desire to take their gems, this will draw out the elite revenants, one of them should be carrying the fluxing gem.'

A Fluxing Gem is dropped off the Elite Sun Revenant in The Scarlet Desert. To spawn him, go to the cauldron the plateau that is surrounded by aliens. Go inside it (use levitate).**

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'Fluxing runes are carried by the army of Terris Thule. These runes help her minions slip into their victim's dreams more easily. I believe you would be able to find one being carried by the lord of the hobgoblins.'

The Fluxing Ore

You say, "What fluxing ore?"

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'It shouldn't be too hard to find some fluxing ore in an area we have termed the Ruined City of Dranik. Look in areas where one might expect to find ore-veins. Look on the mountain sides, beneath cliffs, even among the loose pieces of rock in the area.'

A Fluxing Chunk of Ore is a ground spawn in the Ruined City of Dranik in the rat caves near the loc 1360, -337 . You can get here with invisibility, and there are several of them lying on the ground. The rats are non-aggro.

Head over to Dranik's Scar and then head to Bloodfields.

Once in Bloodfields, head towards the northern part of the zone and follow the wall to the west, as if you are headed towards the Ruined City of Dranik. Just before you get to the Ruined City, there is a cave on the northern wall.

Go inside this cave and all of the way to the back. There is a pile of rocks there. RIGHT CLICK on the pile of rocks.

This zones you into the Ruined City of Dranik and into the very cave that you need to be in to get your ore. The rats are non-aggro, find a bag on the ground (there are usually several) and pick one up.


Return to Talwyn and hand him the four fluxing items: Rune, Ore, Gem, Staff.

Talwyn Flamecaller says "You are indeed ready to join the ranks as a soldier of Solusek Ro. Every soldier in Solusek's league must wield a staff of power, which will focus your abilities in a way that you have never felt before! Fear will consume you as you feel your essence being ripped from your body. Do not give up, use the staff with faith and hope that you will be rewarded with the most powerful item you could ever imagine."

You receive: Staff of Fluxing Power

Lay Hands on the Staff

The Fires of Solusek

You say, 'what powerful item'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'To lay hands on this staff you will have to construct it of items of this world. Power is not given out freely, you must earn it. You will be pressed to the limits of your knowledge and abilities. To begin you will need to gather the fires of Solusek himself. These fires burn in the deepest part of the dungeons named after him. The pure flame burns near the runes. Bring it to me to examine when you get it."

Head to The Cavern of Exiles (Solusek Dungeon C). Here you must kill the Pure Flame Elemental. He is the end of a ring event.

Zone in from Sol B side, fight south until you get to the room just north of the room where the bridges form a lambda symbol (upside down Y).



The room in question is a circular room with lava in the center. There will be 8 lava elementals in the area. 4 Lava Vindicators and 4 Lava Champions. Clear these.

The room grows warmer as the lava pool begins to steam heavily.

One minute later the next wave will spawn. This time, four Furious Magma Guardians will spawn, one on each of the 4 platforms. Clear these.

The lava starts to bubble and churn. Dark shapes move beneath the molten rock.

One minute later the next wave spawn. This time, two Inflamed Champions will spawn: one on the east ledge, one on the west. Clear these.

The pool of lava begins to boil furiously. Something large is growing within.

One minute later the final wave spawns. This time, the Pure Flame Elemental pops. He will spawn on the middle rock.

Pure Flame Elemental says, 'Why have you awakened me mortals?'

Kill him. He doesn't hit hard, but mitigates slow. Was a very short fight and didn't need any heals, except for the enchanter.

You receive: Wisp of Pure Flame. Head back to Talwyn and hand it to him.

The Ice From the Wastes

Talwyn Flamecaller says "You have obtained the pure flame? That was impressive how quickly you did it, the other wizards I am recruiting have not returned yet. Back to the recruitment, you will need pure ice. The ice is coldest in the wastes of Velious. Retrieve this pure ice and bring it back to me."

Head to Sirens Grotto. It is here that you will obtain a Shard of Pure Ice. This drops of Mistress of the Darkwater. From the succor point, run out and around until you get to an area with water. To your left will be a building with a well in it. Swim down to the bottom of the well. You will now be in a larger pool of water. Swim away from the well and then up to get out of the water. This area has many Sirens in it, including the enchanter epic mob (doesn't see invisibility). At the very top of the ramp is the Mistress.

 

She is surrounded by many sirens that chain aggro each other unless they are pacified. She is not slowable and hits for around 500, but semi-fast. She has an AE spell:

Drowning Panic
1: Decrease Attack Speed by 15%
2: Increase Disease Counter by 10
3: Decrease Hit points by 100 per tick
Resist Type: Disease
Resist Adjust: -100
PBAoE: 150

You receive Shard of Pure Ice. Head back to Talwyn and hand it to him.

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'I can see the pure ice is in your possession. Take care while handling it because it is fragile. To infuse the staff with the powers you have attained you will need to retrieve a tapestry bag from the weavers in the Plane of Tranquility. Go talk to Kalym; he should be able to help you.'
