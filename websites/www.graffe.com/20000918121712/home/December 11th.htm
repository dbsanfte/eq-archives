Someone has put together a Wizard Sit-In on the Mithaniel Marr Server. Read on for more info:
"To make a greater statement and to emphasize the completion of the petition regarding the spell effectiveness cap there will be a sit-in this Saturday (December, 11 1999) from 7-10PM PST on the Mithaniel Marr. All wizards and people who support this petition and wizards in general are invited to create a Human (the face with the eye-patch) in Freeport and attend the sit-in at West Freeport Gate. We will talk about exactly what it is that has upset us so much as well as the petition and other issues. The leaders of this sit-in will be very apparent and I will personally be there as the first of us comes to the gates. I ask that because there is the possibility of there being so many of us that everyone speaks as little as possible and that those attending keep the OOC, auction, and shout channels clutter free as much as possible. There will be times when the leader may ask you to voice your opinions or for specific persons to relate something that happened to them regarding the cap or other problems. Please follow our instructions. I may ask that we all cast our shield spell a time or two so have it memorized. Things may get a bit choppy so hang in there. I hope this will be a good demonstration of our resolve. We like our Wizards. We just don't want to useless to a group, or accepted in out of pity which seems to be more and more the case. Following are some of the complaints voiced by a majority of wizards.

1. Change the level modifier/resistance cap from 1.2 to 1.5 or remove it all together (this would assist ALL spell casting classes).

2. We should not have to depend completely on having an enchanter in the party every time we group in order for our spells to land on things. Possibly wizards should have some way of lowering resistance, whether that be a VIABLE item or a line of spells. Ultimately enchanters are the best at this and therefore because the Wizard studies destructive forces and travel so single-mindedly they should not be quite as effective. Perhaps a slight reduction of the curve by which things higher than us are more resistant regardless of their resistance scores is in order. Or, perhaps better personal protection spells.

3. Lastly remove the lower level nerf. You all know that low monsters resist is meant to prevent kill stealing. Verant has treated this concern with the group damage patch and as such it has out stayed its viability.

PS. As a side note many would also like to see three verities of DD every level analogous to mage elemental pets. This again though is wishful thinking, I must say if we are to push for this or any other auspices it must come after we address our initial concerns.

Remember, these are merely points that will be brought up for discussion at the sit-in. The main point of this rally is to put a little force behind the petition. The other items are important as well. We want Verant to notice.

Thank you
See you there.
Wizard of Norath"

- 10:30am PST
Received a few e-mails concerning a the Mask of the Deceiver. There is a lot of confused people out there, so I'll give you all the correct info on what the lowdown is:
Guise of the Deceiver: No longer dropped from the Ghoul Assassin in Lower Guk. If you already have one, it still works for all classes (just like Journeyman Boots in your upper inventory)
Guise of the Deceptor: Dropped by the Ghoul Assassin, this masked replaced the Deceiver mask but is now only usable by Bards and Rogues (the way the Deceiver mask was supposed to work).

December 8th, 7:00am
--------------------
The following general enhancements have been introduced with this patch.
- /Pet No Taunt and /pet taunt now works
- Bard Levitation is now working as intended.
- A number of spears are now available to the Shaman class.
- Dazzle and Entrance (enchanter) have had their casting times reduced.

- Screaming Terror is now functioning as intended.

*Zone Specific Enhancements*
As previously mentioned, Paw becoming a mid-level dungeon is imminent. So heed our earlier warning!
Both Fear and Hate planes have been enhanced, in terms of how often loot is dropped by NPCs.
