Spell Additions and Changes:
We've got four new spells and one changed spell. Verant has added 'Zone Translocate' spells to the world. These will allow wizards to target a group member and send them to one of four zones. Here is the listing of the Translocate spells -

Translocate: Fay (level 39, 225 mana) - sends targeted, grouped PC to the wizard spires in Greater Faydark.

Translocate: Tox (level 39, 225 mana) - sends targeted, grouped PC to the wizard spires in Toxxulia Forest.

Translocate: North (level 39, 225 mana) - sends targeted, grouped PC to the wizard spires in North Karana.

Translocate: Combine (level 39, 225 mana) - sends targeted, grouped PC to the wizard spires in The Dreadlands of Kunark.

The spell that was changed is the level 51 AE snare, Atol's Spectral Shackles. The resistance check vs. magic was changed from +25 to +15. This should allow us to snare the mobs with less resistances.

Absor On Class Balancing:
Absor posted to the Developers Corner / Class Talks forum concerning the class changes and balancing that many of us are waiting upon -

"We have been receiving a fair amount of mail regarding the status of changes to classes based on the items brought up in the class talk threads that I started a while ago. Right now I have little new information for you, but let me spend a moment talking about this.
The team has read and reviewed the lists that I put together. We've had several informal discussions about them, and a few formal discussions about specific items on those lists.

So far you've seen a lot of changes based on your input. They have been going into the game as they get finished and tested. Things that didn't require a lot of in-depth investigation on our part, such as changing the Bread Pants for Bards, increasing the drop rates for many Kunark spells, fixing the Track Corpse spell and many others.

And even some of the more complicated issues have been looked into. Things like giving Druids and Shaman the ability to act as secondary healers, upgrading Mark of Karn (a very complicated spell to balance), the aggro created by debuff spells (and, yes, the aggro created by healing spells), and others that have required a lot of consideration have been added or changed.

We've also started on some of the much more complicated issues, such as Wizards and their ability to do damage against certain NPCs with the Bane spells.

To finish the lists we'll have to make the time to have some long and complicated meetings over issues that can't be changed without rather a lot of effect on the game world. We have not had those meeting yet. Recently we decided that we would put PvP issues to the forefront, considering that lack of attention they have had over the life of EverQuest.

I just wanted to let you know that we haven't forgotten the issues that you brought to our attention.

Alan"

Fennin Ro Wizard Faire:
Dynamite Shortfuze of Fennin Ro sent in info about a Wizard Faire that is going to be held on Fennin Ro later this month -

Hail good wizards of Norrath!

I come before you today with news of Fennin Ro's first ever Wizard Faire! In months past, I have hosted a Rune Trade on Fennin Ro, and the time has come to expand upon this, with a huge faire... for wizards!

I'm hoping that this can be held on the 28th of April. I know, it's very early to discuss this, and I don't even know myself if I will be available, but I've been getting pressure to start announcing it (especially from Omnip, who is possibly the Faire's biggest supporter). Let me explain how it works...

Before, at the rune faires, wizards of all levels would come together. We would discuss life and have fun (the Ol'Tujim's Fierce Brew helped with that I'm sure), but most prominently, we would trade runes. If you needed a rune, you would ask if anyone had it, and you would likely receive it. Conversely, if you had a rune someone else needed, you would offer it to them. Higher level wizards were on hand to assist lower levels in the research of their spells, and fun was had by all.

No changes are being made to that. We're just expanding on it. This month, we will have contests, games, duels, the rune trade, and above all, fun!

I won't ask that you give it to me until the event (since I can't guaruntee my presence just yet), but I would like to know that these things are available so I can plan ahead with them in mind. If you can't get to me in-game, please contact me at fenninrunes@hotmail.com

Also, I am just one wizard, and in past trades, I have had JUST enough alcohol to please the masses. So if you'd like to bring some of your own brew for all to sample, please, I wholeheartedly encourage it!

We have a few games in mind, and Omnip has some that she won't tell me about (she says it's a surprise and she'll post it on the boards herself), but we can only get better with help. So, if you've got an idea for a game, again, contact me with the procedure and rules at fenninrunes@hotmail.com

I'm really looking forward to this month, I expect it's going to be the best we've had yet! I hope to see you there!
