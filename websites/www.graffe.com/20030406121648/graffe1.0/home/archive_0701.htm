You say, 'Hail, Makil Rargon'

Makil Rargon says 'Greetings, Shard . I am Makil Rargon, an esteemed assistant to the great wizard [Al'Kabor]. What can i help you with?

You say, 'Who is AlKabor?'

Makil Rargon says 'Al'Kabor is the most powerful wizard on all fo Norrath. I'm currently assisting him with his fusibility research'

You say, 'How are you assisting his fusibility research?'

Makil Rargon says 'I'm afraid the master has not authorized me to disclose any information about his fusibility research at this time. Perhaps when the experiments are completed I will be able to explain it to you. For now, I must get back to work.

That is all of the text that can be pulled out of him so far. Possibly when all of the suppression spells are completed and put in the live data, the quest will continue more.

Check Yourself Before You Wreck Yourself:
Just a friendly reminder to go get your virus scanner's database files up to date. There seems to be a random strain of 'TROJ_SIRCAM.A' flowing around (I've recieved 4 different versions of it today alone). It's a worm virus that infects your e-mail and mass e-mails everyone in your contact list. It actually has it's own SMTP engine and it's spreading quickly. If you don't have a virus scanner, you can scan your computer via the web at Trend Micro's House Call. Oh... and no, my systems didn't get infected. =)
