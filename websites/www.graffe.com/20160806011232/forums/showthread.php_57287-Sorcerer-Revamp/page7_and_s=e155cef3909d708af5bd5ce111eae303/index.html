    to me currently the sorc very much feels like the wizard in EQ pre kunark. you dont have much choice in spells, if the mob is strongly resistant/immun to a particular area of magic damage(arcane in our case), you are quite crippled...atleast if it isnt compensated with a specific weakness to another kind of magic.

    in groupsituations melee dps aswell as necro+pets(and i think druid too, just didnt happen to have grouped with one lately) is far superior to me. most mobs dont live long enough for proper CV chain and my other damage spells have way too long cast times/recycle times to competet with the afore mentioned. ae damage is the only thing i can really do somewhat ok, but its not like all fights would be ae ones.

    in raid situations i dont feel the sorc has any outstanding position, not even towards necros or druids.

    im just wondering what the vision for the sorcerer is. we are not a top damagedealer, nor are we very versatile, yet we are the only dps class who really has to fight aggro issues. our surviveability is surely the lowest and so is what we bring in terms of buffs to the group. we may solo better than a rogue(or maybe even a monk, dunno), but surely not better than necros or druids.

    i read a lot of cool ideas(especially ones with flavor), but i bet most of them cant be coded. alone something like "forgetting your last spell" would require an individual history of all spells instead of just an accumulated aggro value.

    i feel our spell arsenal is way too empty. its geared towards soloing or eventually grouping, but it is in no way prepared for raiding.

    i played a wizard in Eq for years and i really love the role of the direct damage specialist and i have fun with my sorc here too(basicly because i dont play with a hardcore guild anymore, who just looks at the best numbers or else i would sit out more often than not). but i feel like noone really ever thought the concept of the sorcerer to the end. the same way like the devs just forgot that they had a class named Psi after abandoning cc from the game (and any sort of haste without much use). a class, which lacks its true role.

    do i read the goal is to put us at the top of the dps list, where we belong to...by what means? solo, group, raid? shall arcane be our maintool and the other 2 more the sort of toys? do we want some upcharging/spellweaving mechanismn(which we prolly couldnt use in group situations) or not. shall we eventually get any sort of escape, as those epic/legendary crits are simply out of our control.
    are we supposed to be masters of all 3 of our domains or just arcane? if so, than we should have similar means in all 3 of them to do our job depending on the situation, while you can split the "tools" to their most fitting domain.

    making heat do more damage after cold and vice versa is a nice idea. our spells allready create weaknesses anyways....
