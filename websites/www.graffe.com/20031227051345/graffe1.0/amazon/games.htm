This game will be a definite purchase. It is bringing the pen and paper game to the computer. You can build and control your own world for others to come play on... with your own rules!
   

Return to Castle Wolfenstein
In a World War II setting, Return to Castle Wolfenstein has to be one of my personal favorite first person shooters. The single player game is incredible and the multiplayer game has to be the best to date. I can't put this game down!
   

Pool of Radiance
Set in the Forgotten Realms of Dungeons & Dragons using new 3rd Edition rules, this game has 3D characters exploring and fighting in an interactive environment.
   
Ground Control
A great 3D real time strategy war game. The graphics are stunning as well as some pretty impressive AI.
   
Baldur's Gate II - Shadows of Amn
The sequel to the original Baldurs Gate, this roleplaying game has stunning graphics as well as a huge wealth of quests. A must have for any RPG fan. One great feature is once you have finished, you can use your characters to play Icewind Dale! See the Strategy Guide as well.
   
Icewind Dale
Much like Baldur's Gate in the way of graphics and layout, this RPG takes place in Icewind Dale, the trilogy by Robert Salvatore. See the Strategy Guide as well.
   
Diablo II
Not really an RPG, more of a hack and slash. Still, it's got a huge fan following and it's loaded with hours of fun.
