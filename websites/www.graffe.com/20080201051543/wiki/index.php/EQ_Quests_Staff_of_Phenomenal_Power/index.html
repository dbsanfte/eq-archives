The Staff of Prismatic Power and the Staff of Phenomenal Power are the Wizard's epics 1.5 and 2.0 respectively. They first appeared during the Omens of War expansion. They contain a focus that reduces hate, and a right click effect that decreases spell resist rate.

The remainder of this article will focus entirely on the quest to acquire these items.

If you already have the Staff of the Four, you can skip to here. If you already have your 1.5 epic, you can skip to here. Otherwise start reading below.

[edit] The Four Tasks

Talwyn Flamecaller
The Wizard's second Epic Quest begins at Talwyn Flamecaller in the Lava Storm Mountains. He is a High Elf Wizard located at -2170, -1050. He is high up in a little cubby on top of a wall. Note: Talwyn's Location was changed with the Dragons of Norrath Patch. This is his new location, in the new Lava Storm.

You say, 'Hail, Talwyn Flamecaller'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'The fire of destruction burns within you, does it not? I can see that your affinity for destructive magic has served you well in your travels. It has served you so well that it has caught the eye of Solusek Ro. He has created and sent me here to discuss the matter of joining his legions. If you do, you will be granted power unheard of on mortal lands. What say you?'

You say, 'I agree'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'I knew you would. . . What wizard could refuse an offer of such power? Since you have proven yourself only through combat and not yet through adventuring, I require you to prove yourself worthy once more. A small set of tests, really.'

You say, 'what tests'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'The tests I require of you are merely to measure your ability as a wizard and follower of Solusek. They will draw upon the focal points of being a wizard. Through intelligence and its application to magic, you can call upon the elements more often without strain. Through subtlety, you are able to be destructive without calling the attention unto yourself. Through research, new, more powerful spells and incantations are discovered. Through channeling, the ability to concentrate on spells is enhanced, even in the most distracting of environments. Once you have completed these tests, return to me with the results, so that I may review them.'

[edit] The Test of Channeling

Bones on the table in the Chancellor's Room of the Lake of Ill Omen

You say, 'I am ready for the test of channeling'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'In the fortress of the Sarnak, amidst some old bones, you will find a small, cursed trinket that I require you to obtain. I say that it is cursed because any that try to take it are surrounded in a swarm of scorpions. The scorpions are small and not dangerous by themselves, but when a hundred of them engulf your body, you can't stand but to run away. With your proficiency in channeling, you must go and retrieve this trinket for me.

Go to Lake of Ill Omen to the Chancellor's Room (Chancellor is the mob who drops the Jade Chokidai Prod for the Howling Stones key). On the table there is a skeleton mob that has no name but is able to be targeted. Killing it will spawn several green-con to 65 scorpions. Load up your Jyll's line of spells and kill them all. One of the scorpions will drop A Cursed Talisman. Bring this item back to Talwyn.

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'Your speed and concentration impress even me. The Test of Channeling is no easy feat to accomplish, especially with so many dangerous distractions nearby.'

Talwyn hands you back the Talisman.

[edit] The Fluxing Ore

You say, "What fluxing ore?" Talwyn Flamecaller says 'It shouldn't be too hard to find some fluxing ore in an area we have termed the Ruined City of Dranik. Look in areas where one might expect to find ore-veins. Look on the mountain sides, beneath cliffs, even among the loose pieces of rock in the area.'

A Fluxing Chunk of Ore is a ground spawn in the Ruined City of Dranik in the rat caves near the loc 1360, -337 . You can get here with invisibility, and there are several of them lying on the ground. The rats are non-aggro.

Head over to Dranik's Scar and then head to Bloodfields.
Once in Bloodfields, head towards the northern part of the zone and follow the wall to the west, as if you are headed towards the Ruined City of Dranik. Just before you get to the Ruined City, there is a cave on the northern wall.
Go inside this cave and all of the way to the back. There is a pile of rocks there. RIGHT CLICK on the pile of rocks.
This zones you into the Ruined City of Dranik and into the very cave that you need to be in to get your ore. The rats are non-aggro, find a bag on the ground (there are usually several) and pick one up.

[edit] The Fires of Solusek

Map of Solusek Dungeon C, Ring Event area is circled in red
Close up of Ring Event area
You say, 'what powerful item'

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'To lay hands on this staff you will have to construct it of items of this world. Power is not given out freely, you must earn it. You will be pressed to the limits of your knowledge and abilities. To begin you will need to gather the fires of Solusek himself. These fires burn in the deepest part of the dungeons named after him. The pure flame burns near the runes. Bring it to me to examine when you get it." Head to The Cavern of Exiles (Solusek Dungeon C). Here you must kill the Pure Flame Elemental. He is the end of a ring event.

Until the GD Library is fixed, click here to see the room.

Zone in from Sol B side, fight south until you get to the room just north of the room where the bridges form a lambda symbol (upside down Y).

The room in question is a circular room with lava in the center. There will be 8 lava elementals in the area. 4 Lava Vindicators and 4 Lava Champions. Clear these.

The room grows warmer as the lava pool begins to steam heavily. One minute later the next wave will spawn. This time, four Furious Magma Guardians will spawn, one on each of the 4 platforms. Clear these.

The lava starts to bubble and churn. Dark shapes move beneath the molten rock. One minute later the next wave spawn. This time, two Inflamed Champions will spawn: one on the east ledge, one on the west. Clear these.

The pool of lava begins to boil furiously. Something large is growing within. One minute later the final wave spawns. This time, the Pure Flame Elemental pops. He will spawn on the middle rock.

Pure Flame Elemental says, 'Why have you awakened me mortals? Kill him. He doesn't hit hard, but mitigates slow. Was a very short fight and didn't need any heals, except for the enchanter.

You receive: Wisp of Pure Flame. Head back to Talwyn and hand it to him.


[edit] The Ice From the Wastes

Map of Siren's Grotto noting spawn point of Mistress of the Darkwater
The Mistress of the Darkwater

She is surrounded by many sirens that chain aggro each other unless they are pacified. She is not slowable and hits for around 500, but semi-fast. She has an AE spell:

[edit] Unleash the Full Potential

You say, 'Hail, Talwyn Flamecaller' Talwyn Flamecaller says 'The power of the wizard courses through your veins thoroughly, ever in search of the opportunity to harness even more power. You have created a staff of immense power, yet even in its glory, is limited. To unleash the full potential of this weapon will be a large undertaking of its own. You must obtain a globe of pure discordant energy and return it to me. Look for assistance from the Plane of Discord from Grathnok Gilforgan. I know not where this being may be, but I do know that once you find him, he will be willing to assist you in finding the globe you seek.

[edit] Pure Discordant Energy

Grathnok Gilforgan is in Dranik Scar along the left zone wall in a building. His location is -460, 164.

You say, 'I need a globe of pure discordant energy.' Grathnok Gilforgan says 'I know about the globes of which you speak and perhaps may even be able to aid you in obtaining one. First, though, I have to ask a favor of you. My people have suffered tireless ages here in Discord, protecting the portal. If we lose our hold on the portal the armies of discord would be able to overrun our home world, much like what has started to happen on your home world of Norrath.' You say, 'what about you lose your hold?' Grathnok Gilforgan says 'It is still too early to tell. . . We are well established here, but the constant attacks and dreary landscape really take their toll on young minds. Adding to that, when an attack is successful it bears heavy in the minds of my people. In a recent attack, a surprise raid upon our encampments, the precious Intricate Horn of Galien was taken. The purpose for this atrocity is as of yet unknown, however this item holds great meaning to us and the favor I ask is simply to return this sacred artifact to me.' You say, 'What intricate horn of galien?' Grathnok Gilforgan says, 'This precious Artifact was placed on our largest tower, where its resonating voice stretched across the planes and through the mountains to rally our troops and warn us of an impending attack. The horn has tremendous sentimental value for us, as it belonged to one of the great leaders of our past. It is a horn made of the finest precious metal of our homeland, laden with runes and etchings and filled with gems and precious stones. It is a signification of our strength and perseverance of the ages and without it the morale is very low amongst our soldiers.

[edit] Sub Quest: Key to Anguish

In order to continue, you need to obtain access to the Citadel of Anguish. Please see Access to the Citadel of Anguish.

[edit] Retrieve the Globe

Head to Anguish!! In a full clearing of Anguish your raid will recieve 3 globes: 1 from Jelvan, 1 from either Ture or Warden Hanvar and the final from Arch Magus.

You will receive Globe of Discordant Energy.

After obtaining the Globe, head back to Talwyn Flamecaller in Lavastorm Mountains. Give him the Globe of Discordant Energy.

Talwyn Flamecaller says 'Your first task is to take the right half of the orb to the highest point achieved by a wizards spire on Norrath. On the western prong, you will find a small tear in space. Place the orb inside this tear; it will absorb the pure essence of teleportation. The second task will take you to the highest point achieved by a wizards spire on Luclin. Find another tear in space there and absorb that essence as well. Return to me with both halves imbued with the proper essence and hand them to me, along with your staff.' He then breaks the Globe Of Discordant Energy into 2 different items. You receive Broken Orb of Discordant Energy and Broken Orb of Discordant Energy.

[edit] Absorb The Essences

The first spire is located in Dreadlands. Proceed to the massive spires just south of the port in spires. Get some form of Levitation, and proceed to scale the North West Spire. When you reach the top (a long way up), you will see 5 glowing containers each named "Spacial Distortion".

Place the Broken Orb of Discordant Energy and hit combine to yield Infused Orb of Discordant Energy.

If you get the orb parts backwards it will not eat them. Simply swap and try again.

The next step is to proceed to the highest spire in Luclin - in Mons Letalis. Once there, go through the tunnel up to the platform, and then levitate and scale the NW Spire again to the top where there is again a container named "Spacial Distortion".

If you get the orb parts backwards it will not eat them. Simply swap and try again.

Place the Broken Orb of Discordant Energy and hit combine to yield Infused Orb of Discordant Energy.
