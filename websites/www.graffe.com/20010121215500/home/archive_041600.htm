Erud Crossing Ice Comet Quest Rod:
I had a short chat with GZ today concerning the six Killer Sharks that now encircle the rod. From what he said, there was always supposed to be six sharks in the area, but he was never aware that they were not spawning correctly. So, it's not a fluke.. it's something we are going to have to learn how to deal with (if you have only a few Killer Sharks on your server, consider yourself lucky). Remember this is a level 49 quest, it's not supposed to be easy. Picture this rod like you were headed into Kedge Keep - get a big, high level group to help you. Here are some suggestions:

  • You to get the rod, Cleric to aggro them and cast Aura, another Wizard to cast evac for the Cleric and himself as you gate out with the rod alone.
  • Wolf Form might still work (no promises)
  • One BIG group to kill off the sharks.

Once again, this is a level 49 Quest. It was never intended to be as easy as it used to be. Please don't send me e-mail concerning the rod.. I've got way to much to sift through right now. If you have questions or comments on it, please take them to the Quest Board. Thank you for understanding.
Thanks!
