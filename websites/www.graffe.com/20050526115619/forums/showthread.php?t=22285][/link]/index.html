OOW Spells

2. Lightningbane
3. Phase Walk

Level 68 - Muramite Rune
1. Spark of Lightning
2. Firebane
3. Spark of Thunder
4. Ether Skin
5. Thundaka

Level 69 - Greater Muramite Rune
1. Meteor Storm
2. Spark of Ice
3. Gelidin Comet
4. Solist's Frozen Sword
5. Ether Ward

Level 70 - Glowing Muramite Rune
1. Circle of Thunder
2. Telekara

5. Bulwark of Calrena

1. Ancient Core Fire
