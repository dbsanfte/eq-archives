    Although the front page should be a secondary concern at the moment, I like the blog idea, and disagree a bit with Lysandor about it being superfluous. I think it could be a great way to broaden the reach of graffe's and attract new participants. Ringo's alpha tester post is a great example; that was my introduction to graffe's. And, I'd guess there are others like me who found there way here that way.

    Some sort of gaming/MMO/RPG news aggregator with special graffe commentary seems like a natural fit. Alpha/Beta announcements and previews would be great. Game reviews or general gaming news would fit as well. That could include topics from the various game-specific boards as well as OT and the 'Other Games' boards. Give some collection of regular posters the ability to publish bloggy posts to the boards as well as the front page (with comments linked to the board thread). This would maintain the boards as they are but add a new way for a casual reader to find interesting content with a link to more discussion. This would probably need an editor to select bloggy-posters and possibly to act as gatekeeper for the front page content. This won't be a small job if the idea really takes off.
