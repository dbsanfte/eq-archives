    Take a barrel and add a bunch of river rocks. Put a screen and/or a bunch of hay on top of those rocks. Burn a bunch of hardwood (such as oak) to ash. Pour ashes into barrel until the barrel is full. Add rain water, let it soak for a few days. Add additional ash as the original ash settles.
