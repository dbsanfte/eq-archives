Welcome to the Tanarus Unofficial League.


Welcome to the Tanarus Unofficial League. I'm WeedMan for those of you who don't know me, and I will be running the League. Also LeMz was kind enough to host this site and is doing a great job with it! I am also always open to suggestions, and would like to try to keep this running as long as possible. Anyone who wishes to help is encouraged to do so, just contact me in-game or through E-Mail

Here is how this is going to work. As of now there will be a round robin to place all the teams. Once all the teams have been placed, a ladder will be set up. All the rules and regulations are in the Rules section, and you can access and view the standings in the corresponding sections of this website.

Also, after each match, Team Captains are to send me the #getlog file, you can use the Contact Me button at the bottom of the page to do so. In the e-mail, please include the date, and name of your opponents, along with the attached txt file.

I wish all teams the best of luck, and hope that everyone has a great time!

I would also like to add that the teams DO NOT have to participate in ALL the events. If you wish not to participate in one of the events, please contact me and let me know.

For example: Team 1 may participate in the 1v1 but may wish not to participate in the 2v2.

Teams that are worried about not having enough active players to participate in the 4v4 or 5v5, let me know and i will consider letting you pick up extra players at this date. NOTE: that once you pick up a player for this he/she must remain on your Roster until atleast the end of the Round Robin.

Team Captains that want to post their e-mail address on the site for CONTACT purposes may e-mail me with the desired e-mail address they would like used to be contacted. This would make it much easier for other team captains and/or players on your roster to contact you about matches and so on.
