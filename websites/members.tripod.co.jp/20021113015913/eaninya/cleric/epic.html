■ Cleric Epic Questについて

各Classに導入され、大いに盛り上がっているFA型巨大Quest。
自分がQuestするときや、知り合いのHelpをするときに、下調べにでも使って貰えれば幸いです。

□ Step 0

Timorous Deepのはるか南、壁に囲まれたINNにいるOmat VastseaがStart NPCです。
この壁は二重構造になっていて、水中にある2カ所のトンネルをくぐって真ん中の島へ行く仕組みになっています。
それぞれトンネルの場所は（n11530,n3796）と（n11325,n1370）。Omatは(n12000,n200)あたりにいます。
例のごとく、別に話を聞かなくてもQuestはできます。
水系の神々と火系の神々・宗教団体について説明が聞けます。

You say "Hail Omat Vastsea"

Omat Vastsea bows deeply.
"I have been expecting you,XXX. The waters have foretold your arrival.I am High Priest Omat Vastsea of the Triumvirate missionaries.Please feel free to enjoy the quiet solttude of this inn."

You say "what is triumvirate missionaries?"

Omat Vastsea says
"The Triumvirate of Water are:E'ci,the Mistress of Ice ; Tarew Marr , the lord of water ; and Povar , the formiess master of vapor and mist. We Triumvirate missionaries are granted great divinatory powers and wisdom through our devotion to the Triumvirate and must defend the waters of Norrath from the ravages of the Tyrant of Fire, Fennin Ro."

You say "what is Fennin Ro?"

Omat Vastsea says
"The Tyrant of Fire, Fennin Ro, is the merciless lord of fire in all it's forms.The rivalry between the tyrant and the Triumvirate is as old as the gods themselves and is an eternal battle. We witness the struggle every moment of our lives as the Oasis of Marr succumbs to the heat of the surrounding desert and is replenished by the swelling of the Lifire River. That fragile balance between the elements must be maintained but the Plasmatic Priesthood threatens that balance."

You say "what is Plasmatic Priesthood?"

Omat Vastsea says
"The Plasmatic Priesthood are worshipers of the Tyrant of Fire,but they have been driven mad by their fanaticism and have forgotten the importance of the balance.In their madness they would set the whole world aflame,leaving nothing but lifeless ash."

□ Step 1

Lake Ratheの(3600,0)付近、小屋のそばに立っている赤いRobeを着た男がまず目標。Hailしてみると…

You say "Hail Shmendrik Lavawalker"

Shmendrik Lavawalker says
"Hail, good XXX!! Be wary near the waters of Lake Rathe! The Riptide goblin king, Lord Bergurgle has been commanding his minions to murder and rob all who come near it! I am here seeking his death but I am afraid I am no match for all of his subjects. I shall reward you greatly for the death of Lord Bergurgle. I simply ask that you bring me his crown as proof.

このZONEにいるLord BergurgleというGoblinを殺して、証明としてCrownを持ってこいと。
で問題のLord Bergurgleは、Aviak Guardが3POPする島の地下にいます。LOC(p2800,p150)
潜っていくとTowerが見えるので、頂上から下を覗いてみますと…

こんな風になってます。DeepというFamale Dark Elf(Lv57 or 58)がいますが、Indifで襲ってきません。
hailにも反応なし。無視していいでしょう。

PHは30min間隔でPOPします。Tower頂上からStunで適当な場所へPullし、殺しましょう。
他のGobまで手を出してると面倒なことになるので、PHだけをkillがいいと思います。
Heal使ってくるのでRoot→DDである程度削ったら、あとはStunで殺しましょう。

晴れてLord BergurgleがPOPして殺すことができたら、Shmendrikが言っていたCrownをLootすることが出来ます。
Lord BergurgleはLv56からGreen。
こいつもCleric Typeで、CHなどを使用します。
絶対にHealさせないようStunを駆使して殺しましょう。
さほど苦もなく殺せるはずです。
LootしたらShmendlikに、証拠としてこれを渡します。
するとそばの小屋にNatasha Whitewaterという水色Robeの女性が現れ、彼を殺してしまいます。

Shmendrik Lavawalker shoves the crown into a scorch marked leather satchel and cackles uncontrollably as madness twists his features and flames dance in his eyes.
"You Ebina have reduced the Riptides into chaos! Without a king to keep them in control they will ravage the settlements surrounding this lake! After the slaughter I shall return and easily burn the remainder of the villages and fishing shanties to the ground! None shall escape the fires of the Tyrant!!"

Natasha Whitewater shouts
"The Triumvirate of Water has decreed your fate, Shmendrik Lavawalker!! I am here to deliver said fate!!"

Shmendrik Lavawalker says
"I'll slay you like I slaughtered your fellow missionaries! The Triumvirate can not decide the fate of a follower of the Tyrant!!"

Natasha Whitewater says
"Enough!! Your existence has come to an end!"

Natasha Whitewater says
"This conflict has been destined by the waters of the Triumvirate!"

Shmendrikが成敗されると、A Spirit of FlameというFire Eleに変身します。襲ってくるのでさくっと退治しましょう。

HPは3000程度でMelee100程度。
Warrior Typeです。
Lv56から/con Green。
こいつを殺すとDamaged Goblin CrownがLootできるので、
Natashaに渡しましょう。

You say "Hail Natasha Whitewater"

Natasha Whitewater says
"The Riptide goblins must have their crown returned to them. If you would be so kind as give me the crown I will make that it reaches them. Hopefully they are capable enough to repair the damage that has been done to it."

Natasha Whitewater says
"I will have this crown returned to the Riptide Goblins immediately! Should you ever come across an Erudite named Omat Vastsea, give him this sea shell. The waters of Norrath shimmer with awareness of your deeds here today!

ここで、Omat Vastseaに渡せとShellを渡されます。
