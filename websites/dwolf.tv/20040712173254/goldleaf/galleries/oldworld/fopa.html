Dark Conqueror

O'nure Rashule was but a simple farmer. One day the force know only as Fopa inhabited his body. Living countless eons off the spirit of others, Fopa grows in strength. Each new life brings him hope of bringing his dreams of crushing Norrath under his heel into reality. One day he knows he will rise again and he shall rake his poisoness grip across Norrath.
