Silverleaf

Telperion, the elder of the two trees of Valinor from the ancient realm known as Middle Earth, had leaves of dark green above and shining silver below. A dew of silver light dripped from his leaves. It is said that the stars are made from the dew of Telperion and the moon from the bloom of his last flower. The line of White Trees are the images of Telperion and many a forrest as sprung up in his name. Legend states that at the end Telperion will reappear.

Strange things have been happening across Norrath. A new continent is discovered, Mayong Mistmoore grows restless, and even dragons, creatures of legends and children's stories, have returned. Out of the mists rises a young druid named Telperion Silverleaf. What does this mean? Is it merely coincidence? Only time will tell.
