Wizardess at Peace

During one of my journeys through Najena, Fazzarya Ayrazzaf sat and meditated on life while in the middle of a roaring fire. How can it be that she can be so at peace in such chaos? So I asked her and these are Fazzarya's words of wisdom:

The Master keeps her mind
always at one with Phenix Arcana, the perfect magic;
that is what gives her her radience.

Phenix Arcana is ungraspable.
How can her mind be at one with it?
Because she doesn't cling to ideas.

Phenix Arcana is dark and unfathomable.
How can it make her radiant?
Because she lets it.

Since before time and space were,
Phenix Arcana is.
It is beyond is and is not.
How do I know this is true?
I look inside myself and see.

If you open yourself to Phenix Arcana,
you are at one with Phenix Arcana
and you can embody it completely.
If you open yourself to insight,
you are at one with insight
and you can use it completely.
If you open yourself to loss,
you are at one with loss
and you can accept it completely.

Open yourself to Phenix Arcana,
then trust your natural responses;
everything will fall into place.
