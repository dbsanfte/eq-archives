A Heart of Gold

Passing through Freeport one day, I passed my good friend Dolamar Goldenheart. He was helping the young folk of Norrath, giving free heals and words of courage to all. Many offered him money for his services, yet he refused them saying that their smiles were reward enough for his hard work.

Never a better Cleric of Brell has there ever been.. Dolamar truely has a heart of gold.
