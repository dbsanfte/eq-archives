Soulmates

As fate had it, Darkhen Blakstaf was passing through East Commons one afternoon when he caught sight of a young enchantress being persued by ghouls. Seeing the damsel in some distress, he rushed to her aid. Together, spells sizzling through the air, the tide of battle begin to turn. The vile undead were vanquished! Victorious, the Erudite turned to thank her rescuer. As she gazed into his eyes her words of thanks died on her lips. It was love at first sight. Finally breaking the silence, Darkhen asked the girl her name.

"Etherealopal," she replied shyly.

"A rare gem," he said, his heart in his eyes, "and most beautiful."

Together they traveled far and wide, meeting new people and having adventures, until finally Etherealopal could stand it no longer. She turned to face Darkhen and gave him a very penetrating look. Finally she asked, "Will you marry me?"

Surprised, Darkhen just stared at her. Finally regaining his ability to talk, he firmly stated, "YES!!"

He then swept her up in his arms where they kissed deeply. Afterwards, then and there, they exchanged vows that forever joined thier souls together so that even though circumstances and miles might separate them, they shall be together always.
