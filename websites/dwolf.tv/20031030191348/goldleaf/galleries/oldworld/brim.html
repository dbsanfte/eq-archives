Brim the Mighty

While passing through the Estate of Unrest, I noticed a young halfling fighting back the hordes of undead. Never fearing for himself, he valiantly faught until all of the creatures were dead. Panting from the exertion, he glanced up and noticed me watching from afar. As he sat to rest, I came forward to speak with the brave druid.

"Greetings!" I said, "Aren't you a little far from home for one so young?"

The halfling replied, "Aye, far from home I am, but we must fight the undead least they break free and spread their evil across the world."

I nodded, understanding his noble cause. Then I gave him a salute and left him to continue his work.

I am honored to have met such a devoted young soul as Brim the Mighty.

Brim Soulrender, Halfling Druid
Tribunal Server
*Retired*
