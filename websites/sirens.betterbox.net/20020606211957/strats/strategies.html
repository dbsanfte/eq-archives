Grouping Strategies


Class CLERICS:
1) Worry about your lead tank. YOU ALL NEED TO MAKE HOT KEYS so that your tank will know when his healing is coming.
2) Your JOB is to HEAL. Remember that healing for 500 is the same agro-wise as doing 500 points to the mob, so if you want to keep agro down, use the Celestial series, though they are not mana-efficient.
3) Use complete heal on tanks and other melee when you have to heal more than 1k damage. Remember the 10 second cast time!
4) If you are out of mana dont be afraid to say so. In fact scream it if you need to.
5) It is also useful to have stun series spells memmed to stun mobs, especially mobs that heal themselves.
Class DRUIDS:
1) Druids can help with group healing, cast regen's, snares, sow's, and ask backups root.
2) DO NOT do a DD unless the mob is over half dead.(DRUIDS ARE NOT TANKS)
3) DO NOT do a group DS, because it is harder to get a mob off of a non-MA when doing thorn damage.
Class ENCHANTERS:
1) Give the main tank time to taunt before you cast.
2) Mez IS GOD!!!
3) Always use your Debuffs such as tash/slow/etc.
Class MAGES:
1) Give the main tank time to taunt before you cast. Try not to cast high power spells until the mob is at least half way dead.
Class MONKS:
1) Your job is to pull.
2) Use FD to pull agro off you.
3) Wait 5 or 10 secs to give the main tank time to get aggro.
4) ALWAYS FIGHT AT MAX MELEE!!!!
5) If you aggro, turn attack off and step back.
6) Remove aggro processing weapons!!!
Class NECROMANCERS:
1) Necromancers have some of the best DoT spells. Try to use one that causes little initial damage to reduce the chance of aggro.
2) 53 get an essence emerald!!!
3) coffin is always handy as well.
Class PALADINS:
1) DON'T TAUNT!!!! DON'T BASH/SLAM!!!! Unless you are MA.
2) Fight in front of the mob.
3) If you aggro, turn atk off and step back, give the main tank time to get aggro back.
4) ALWAYS FIGHT AT MAX MELEE!!!
6) AC IS GOD!!!!
7) Save disciplines until absolutely needed.
Class RANGERS:
1) MAX MELEE ALWAYS!!! The most important thing for rangers.
2) DON'T TAUNT!!! Unles your MA.
3) DON'T WIELD HIGH AGGRO PROCESSING WEAPONS!!!! Unless your MA.
Class ROGUES:
1) You guys out damage warriors 4 to 1.
2) ALWAYS FIGHT BEHIND THE MOB!!!
3) Don't backstab/super kick the moment a mob reaches camp
4) Wait 5 or 10 secs to give the main tank time to get aggro.
5) If you aggro turn atk off and step back.
6) ALWAYS FIGHT AT MAX MELEE!!!!
7) RESIST GEAR is very important in AE fights
8) Remove aggro processing weapons!!!
Class SHAMAN:
1) Always use Debuffs.
2) Give the main tank time to taunt before you cast.
3) If you dont land your spells set a macro to let people know.
Class SHADOWKNIGHTS:
1) DON'T TAUNT!!!! DON'T BASH/SLAM!!!! unless your MA.
2) Fight in front of the mob.
3) If you aggro, turn atk off and step back, give the main tank time to get aggro back.
4) ALWAYS FIGHT AT MAX MELEE!!!
5) AC IS GOD!!!!
7) If a mob is able to be lifetapped SHOUT it, it means the mob has low resist to MR and DR/PR which ever.
8) Save disciplines until absolutely needed, unholy aura is a godly discipline.
Class WARRIORS:
1) The highest level warrior is ALWAYS main tank!!
2) AC is GOD!!! If you are 55+ you should have 1000 AC.
3) Always fight in front of the mob, try to keep him in 1 spot and not let him move.
4) DON'T TAUNT!!! Unless you are MA of course.
5) use BASH/SLAM!!! for aggro
6) Your job is to keep aggro on the main tank then get aggro when/if main tank dies.
7) SET A ORDER IN WHICH TO TANK IN!!(list is based off LEVEL then AC then HITPOINTS)
7) ALWAYS FIGHT AT MAX MELEE (furthest you can stand away from mob while still hitting him, see "The Bubble" graphic at the end of post), with the exception of the main tank who needs to stand on the mobs toes.
7) ALWAYS USE DEFENSIVE DISC UNLESS TOLD OTHERWISE!!!
Class WIZARDS AND ALL OTHERS WITH DD NUKES:
1) ONE RULE ONLY: Wait until mobs hit 50% life before you nuke....
2) Also make sure to wear resist gear to fights where there will be AE!!
--- NOTES
Note 1 If you follow these guidelines and position yourself as shown below, there is no mob that any raid can't handle. If you need to be close to the mob to cast your spells, move in range and after casting, slowly back out so that the tank can regain aggro if necessary. DO NOT RUN!!!!
Note 2 Crushing dmg, slams, bashes and kicks all move the mob much better than anythng else (except a few spells and loss of aggro by MT) if all tanks are on one side of mob and it moves alot during fight.. ya might want to put a few behind as well to keep mob in place. This is the raid leaders job though.
Note 3 The lay out of people should be as follows -
Main tank front rest of melee in back clerics to one side casters to other in a half circle.
Note 4 Remember to make sure mobs dont hit the enchanter or the cleric. ABOVE ALL dont break an enchanters Mez. Thats a quick way to die.
Note 5 Its always good to have a Master Looter(ML). Be fair in your looting, and make sure to state ALL looting rules before you start your ML.
