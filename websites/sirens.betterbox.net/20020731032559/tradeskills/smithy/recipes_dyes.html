Step 1. Make the Extract --
Extracts MUST be combined in either an alchemist's medicine bag, or in
a rogue's mortar and pestle (trivial reports of 35) The "empty vial"
needed is sold by the dye supply merchants - you CANNOT use poison vials
for this recipe.
Extract Ingredients Notes
black empty vial, flask of water, (see notes for the rest) Charcoal OR blue slumber fungus OR green goblin skin OR froglok skin OR red cockatrice feather
(charcoal: various elementals, elemental warriors in the Hole, iron stone guardians in Nektulos, golem-types, including Terror (plane of Fear))
(Blue slumber fungus: ground spawn in LFay Faerie camp, drops off mushroom folk in Sebilis)
(Green goblin skins and red cockatrice feathers are a Kunark drop)
brown rust Russet oxide, empty vial, flask of water Russet oxide - ONLY a 1-resin version
Various elementals, also some lying on ground in Steamfont, elemental warriors in the Hole iron
stone guardians in Nektulos
Blue Permafrost crystals, empty vial, flask of water crystals (Many creatures in Permafrost, on ground in Dire Wolf Pens in Permafrost, ground spawn on the golra island in Timorous Deep)
green Evergreen leaves, empty vial, flask of water leaves are found lying on ground in Greater Faydark, lighter shade of green, uncommon and hard to see
red Sarcoscypha fungus, empty vial, flask of water Fungus types in Upper Guk Innothule Swamp, on ground in LFay Faerie camp
red rust iron oxide, empty vial, flask of water iron oxide - ONLY a 1-resin version Various elementals, also some lying on ground in Steamfont, elemental warriors in the Hole, iron
stone guardians in Nektulos
yellow jack o' lantern fungus, empty vial, flask of water jack o' lantern fungus is found on the ground in LFay Faerie camp, scarecrow types in WK, Unrest, etc
The elementals (earth/air/fire/steam) for the oxide/charcoal drops
can be found in places like Steamfont, Lavastorm and Najena.
Step 2. Make the Dye -
This can be made by anyone, using the above extracts, and combining them
in a medium clay jar(made by a potter, this is a combineable container).
Dye Type Ingredients Notes
dark color 1 resin, 1 extract the LARGE bracer when dyed with 1 resin black is slate (pale gray)in shade
base color 2 resins, 1 extract NONE
light color 3 resins, 1 extract NONE
Step 3. Dye your armor:
Take a completed piece of fine steel plate armor, place it in the forge
along with 1 dye, 1 jar of acid, 1 jar of lacquer and combine.
Once dyed, the color cannot be removed. You CAN fail on this part of the
process (it's a skill check versus your smithing skill - trivial 150),
read your combine message carefully.
