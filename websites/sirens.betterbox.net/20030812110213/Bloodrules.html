The Bloody Rose
House Rules
1. Conduct You must at all times represent the house to the best of your ability. This is an adult house. As so conduct yourselves as adults. Each person is responsible for their own actions.
2. Drama This is a NO DRAMA house. If you have a problem with anyone in the house, try and deal with it outside of the house. If you cannot, you may take it up with one of the three founders of the household (Morterus, Rose, or Cadric).
3. Purchases All major purchases are to be divided in total cost for all of the members of the house. Members are people in the house over the age of 16. Things that we purchase as a whole are such things as food, pavilion, lights, lanterns, cook pots, etc. All items will be agreed upon before purchase by majority (2/3rd) of the house. [Example: Item costs 200$ and there are 5 members in the household, each member will pay 40$.]
4. Food Costs Food costs are to be divided evenly among the house hold members. Your 1st child will not be charged to you for food costs. However, if you have more then 1 youth, you will be responsible for the price of their food. House food will be bought the Wednesday prior to the event. It is up to you to state if you or others (such as guests you have invited) are not going to be attending the event.
5. Guests You may invite guests to attend events with us, as long they are O.K.ed by members of the house (this must be a 100% yes vote). YOU are responsible for your guest conduct. Also, if you invite a guest to an event it is your responsibility to make sure they have paid for their meals after the cut off date, even if they do not show up to the event.
6. Youths Youths are welcome in the house, as long as they are taken care of by the member that has brought them. We are not responsible for your youth, YOU ARE. The house is not a place for free babysitting, or for your youth to run wild. YOU are responsible for any action your youth does that does not show kindly on the house.
7. Pets Pets are also welcome in the house. They must follow the same rules as applied in item #6.
8. House Policy We have a strict three strikes policy. However, everyone is allowed to make mistakes. Any member may be removed from the house at the three strikes point, with no less then 2/3rds vote. You will be notified as to why, but NOT by whom. Certain sever cases will be voted on at the first or second strike (murder, stealing, etc.).
9. Additude The House is a place to have fun. If you are having a bad day, try not to let it rub off on others. Instead let us get you in the spirit and mood of the event. So it’s raining, let’s all pretend that the gods have sent us rain in the desert and rejoice.
10. Chores Everyone in the house must help out with the chores. This includes cooking, washing dishes, packing, unpacking, cleaning, etc. No one likes to do the dirty work, but if we work together it can all be accomplished neatly and in record time.
11. Night Time At no time after dark, may a woman of the house go unescorted (more then 50 feet). She must be companied by at least 1 house male, 1 TRUSTED male from another camp/house, or at least 2 other females of the house.
12. Helping Out Our house is a help house. Each member must put in at least 2 hours at each event to help the SCA members that are holding the event (Constab, Harold, Front Gate, etc). It does not matter if it is a Dragons Lair event, or if they are other holdings of An Tir (Blath An Oir, Aqueterra, etc).
13. Skills We are also a house that likes to better ourselves. Each member must be willing to learn a new skill or have one they can bring to the house. In turn each house member must be willing to teach each other any skill they possess.
14. Prospective Members Prospective members must tourney with us for 1 full season, or 1 year depending on when they petition to join our house. We fully understand that real life, and pocket books don’t allow everyone to enjoy each event. However, please try and attend each event you can, and if you cannot to give us notice so we may plan accordingly (there will be no exceptions). After 1 season or 1 tourney year, you as a prospective house member will be voted upon at your anniversary date. You must have a unanimous vote to join the house. If it is not unanimous you will be informed as to why, but NOT who. In severe cases you may be asked not to attend any more house events before your 1 year anniversary date. (Stealing, death etc.)
15. Meetings House meetings are to be held each month, on the 1st Monday of the month. Attendance is mandatory however we do understand other things do get in the way. If you cannot attend, please give us notice so we may plan accordingly (there will be no exceptions).
16. Adults The house is an adult house. There for we may do things that are adult in nature, connotation, or tone. Smalls will be in bed, or confined to camp at 10 P.M. or earlier (at parents rule). However they should know at any given time there will be adult themes present.
17. Drinking Drinking is ONLY allowed for members of the house over 21. You are to supply/purchase your own drinks. That is in no way part of the house expense.
18. Drugs Drugs are in NO WAY allowed in our house. If you are caught doing drugs in our encampment you will be asked to leave and to never return. NO DRUGS, NO WAY, NO HOW.
