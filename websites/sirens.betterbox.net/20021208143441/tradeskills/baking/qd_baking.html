QUICK AND DIRTY BAKING GUIDE TO 200

Note: This is not a full guide on baking, this is how I mastered the art in 3 hours of work. To get a full guide, see the many sites on the net. I performed this trade skill in Freeport. All items can be found in West/East Freeport and East Commonlands. What I noticed is the created food is only 0.1 pounds for the stack compared to 1.0 pound for rations. Great for monks or weight-conscious people. To learn baking, a couple of items need to be addressed:
  1. Charisma plays a big part in the buying/selling...you will make more profit with high charisma.
  2. The closer you are to the "trivial" level, the better chance of it leveling (and succeeding).
  3. Contrary to some posts, you can increase level even if you fail, though it is less likely.
  4. On all recipes below, only use one of each. Use the CTRL-left click to only pick up one of an item.
  5. You can use a spit (buy one) or an oven (at a town). A spit can not go in a container (it is like a sewing or fletching kit, for example). I used a spit because of portability.
  6. To use the Oven, left click on it. Then place items into the oven and hit Combine.
Preparation
I prepared by going to Freeport and putting everything in my bags in the bank (with the exception of the food/water). Because of weight, I used 7 large bags and left a space free for the spit (3 pounds), though you can use backpacks (they are three times the weight though). To master baking, it took me only about 20pp, though I sold some of the food items (the Batwing Crunchies) I made back to merchant (but I kept the fish fillets and rolls). So I could have broke even or made a little money on the whole skill.

Mastering Baking

Note: I did the quickest route (needed only four ingredients and/or the spit), though it may not be the most profitable.

Location of Ingredients

I am referencing Freeport, because everything was pretty close. Note that I needed about 15 stacks of batwings and fish and about 8 stacks of frosting/sauces, but this can vary.
  • Batwing - loot from bats in Freeport newbie area or I found a person that sold batwings! In the magic guild in West Freeport, go in teleport, through door and upstairs and buy from Lysa Truegreen. Got them for about 2gp 6sp a stack! (Note: After I was done, I had three extra stacks that I sold for 3pp/stack in South Karana!) [Can also buy from Erudite vendor in EC at inn 4 by WC zone]
  • Fish - either fish (cheaper) or buy from vendor in docks by Freeport. Has to be Fresh Fish. Other fish didn't work. About 5gp 2sp per stack.
  • Sauces/Frosting - purchase from Inn 1 in East Commonlands from Parthar. About the same 2gp 5sp a stack.
  • Spit (could use oven) - purchase in Traders Holiday in East Freeport for 2gp 5sp.

Taking Baking past Mastery
The next stage is looking at moving from the 100s to 199 (and possibly beyond). The reason I stopped at 199 was that I did not want to grand master in Baking. Note that if you take a skill to 201, you can't take others past 200.
Note: I was looking for the fastest way to level without doing too many steps and the least number of different components. Most people stop at the fish roll stage because it is great low-weight food (especially for monks).
