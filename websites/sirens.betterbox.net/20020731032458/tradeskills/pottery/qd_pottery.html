QUICK AND DIRTY POTTERY GUIDE TO 200

Note: This is not a full guide on pottery, this is how I mastered the art in 3 hours of work (and under 25pp). To get a full guide, see the many sites on the net. I performed this trade skill in Freeport. All items can be found in Freeport near the Market. To learn pottery, a couple of items need to be addressed.
  1. Charisma plays a big part in the buying/selling...you will make more profit with high charisma.
  2. The closer you are to the "trivial" level, the better chance of it leveling (and succeeding).
  3. Contrary to some posts, you can increase level even if you fail, though it is less likely.
  4. On all recipes below, only use one of each. Use the CTRL-left click to only pick up one of an item.
  5. To use the Pottery Wheel, left click on it. Then place items into the wheel and hit Combine.
  6. To get a usable item, you have to get a firing sheet and place in the kiln. Since firing sheets are expensive and not necessary to advance skill, I never used them. I simply destroyed any 'unfired' items that were made in the wheel.
Preparation
I prepared by going to Freeport and putting everything in my bags in the bank (with the exception of the food/water). Because of weight, I used 8 large bags, though you can use backpacks (they are three times the weight though). To master pottery, it took me only about 25pp.

Mastering Pottery
I did the quickest route (needed only four ingredients and the pottery wheel), though it may not be the most profitable. Did not use the kiln so did not need firing sheets.

Level Information
1-36 Made medium jars.
Needed med jar sketch, flask of water, block of clay.
Needed about 50 of each item to get trivial.
37-101 Created medium bowls.
Needed bowl sketch, flask of water, small block of clay.
Needed about 120 of each item to get trivial.

Location of Ingredients

I am referencing Freeport, because everything was pretty close. Numbers in parentheses refer to eqatlas map (Thanks eqatlas!).
Hint: If you can bind/gate, I placed bind outside the forge in North Freeport so I could make metal bits easily when needed. Or if the forge was used, gate to the other one.
  • Sketches (about 2sp each) - Found in shop to right of bank in Market.
  • Water (1 sp) - Various places where food/water is sold. Place in the market that sells it.
  • Clay (2sp and 4sp) - Forge by bard shop. Not stackable, so uses the most room. If you have extra clay blocks from jars, place one in wheel with one water to make a small block of clay.
  • Pottery Wheel - In Market.

Taking Pottery past Mastery
The next stage is looking at moving from the 100s to 199 (and possibly beyond). The reason I stopped at 199 was that I did not want to grand master in Pottery. Note that if you take a skill to 201, you can't take others past 200.
Note: I was looking for the fastest way to level without doing too many steps and the least number of different components.

Let's continue from the Medium Bowl stage:

High Quality Firing Sheet AND Divine Crystalline Glaze
