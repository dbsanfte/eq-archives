QUICK AND DIRTY TAILORING CHARTS TO 200

Level Information
1-15 Made silk threads (two spiderling silks) and/or silk swatches (two spider silks).
Keep both silk threads and swatches as you will need them for later skills.
16-21 Made silk bandages (two silk threads).
Use for bind wounds or sell to vendors.
22-26 Made silk cords (three silk threads).
Keep silk cords for other skills.
27-36 Made containers depending on the pelt I found:
Tailored Wrist Pouch (silk cord, ruined wolf pelt, 4 slot) - Trivial 26
Tailored Small Bag (silk cord, LQ wolf pelt, 4 slot) - Trivial 31
Tailored Large Belt Pouch (silk cord, LQ cat pelt, 6 slot) - Trivial 36
Or
Made Raw silk armor (pure casters and monks). - Trivial 36
Needs one pattern and one silk swatch (two swatches for leggings and tunic).
37-41 Made Tailored Large Bag (silk cord, LQ bear pelt, 6 slot)
42-56 Made studded leather armor.
(Studs are made by a smith by three metal bits, water, and a file)
Need MQ pelt, pattern, and studs.
Mask - 1 stud, Belt, Bracer, Gorget - 2, Cap, Shoulder, sleeves - 3,
Boots, cloak, gloves, leggings - 4, tunic - 5
57-82 Made cured silk armor (monk only).
Need pattern, silk swatch, and heady kiolas
([brewing] 2 kiola sap, 1 bottle, 1 water in a brew barrel)
Cap - 1 SS, 3 HK Cloak - 2 SS, 2 HK Tunic - 3 SS, 2 HK
Gorget - 1 SS, 2 HK Glove - 2 SS, 2 HK Shldr - 2 SS, 2 HK
Mask - 1 SS, 1 HK Leg - 2 SS, 3 HK Boot - 1 SS, 2 HK
Belt - 1 SS, 2 HK Sleeves - 2 SS, 2 HK Wrist - 1 SS, 2 HK
83-88 Made Wu's fighting gauntlets (monk-only gloves)
1 glove pattern, 1 silk swatch, 1 greater lightstone, 1 vial viscous mana
(Viscous mana is made by a poison vial, a pearl, and an enchanter to cast the 12-level Thicken mana spell)
FYI, one vial viscous mana cost is about 8-9pp!
Or
Made 10-slot backpacks (really popular)
[These sell for around 80-100pp each!]
Backpack pattern, HQ bear skin - Trivial 87
89-108 Made Reinforced Leather armor
1 pattern, HQ wolf/cat/bear pelt, boning(s)
([Smithing] Bonings are 1 small brick of ore, 1 water, 1 File)
Mask - 1 boning, Belt, Bracer, Gorget - 2, Cap, Shoulder, sleeves - 3, Boots, cloak, gloves, leggings - 4, tunic - 5
89-108 Made Reinforced Leather armor
1 pattern, HQ wolf/cat/bear pelt, boning(s)
([Smithing] Bonings are 1 small brick of ore, 1 water, 1 File)
Mask - 1 boning, Belt, Bracer, Gorget - 2, Cap, Shoulder, sleeves - 3, Boots, cloak, gloves, leggings - 4, tunic - 5
109-118 Made Crystalline Silk Mantle
1 mantle pattern, 1 silver thread, 1 crystalline silk swatch (2 crystalline silks combined in sewing kit)
119-124 Made Crystalline Silk Gloves
1 glove pattern, 1 silver thread, 2 crystalline silk swatches
125-131 Made Crystalline Silk Tunic
1 tunic pattern, 1 silver thread, 3 crystalline silk swatches
132-135 Made Wu's Fighting mask
Vial of viscous mana, mask pattern, 4 heady kiolas, 1 silk swatch
Vial of viscous mana - pearl and poison vial and cast of Enchanter spell.
Heady Kiola - 2 Kiola saps and 1 water flask in brew barrel
136-142 Made Wu's Fighting cap
Vial of viscous mana, cap pattern, 4 heady kiolas, 1 silk swatch
143-151 Made Wu's Fighting sleeves
Vial of viscous mana, sleeve pattern, 4 heady kiolas, 2 silk swatches
152-158 Made Wu's Fighting tunic
Vial of viscous mana, tunic pattern, 4 heady kiolas, 3 silk swatches
159-188 Make Acrylia studded armor
superb rockhopper hide, paeala bark tannin (brewed), pattern, acrylia studs
mask - 1 stud
belt, bracer, gorget - 2 studs
cap, shoulderpad, sleeves - 3 studs
boots, cloak, gloves, pants - 4 studs
tunic - 5 studs
188-199 Make Acrylia reinforced armor
flawless rockhopper hide, paeala bark tannin (brewed), pattern, acrylia boning
1 boning: mask, bracer, gorget
2 boning: belt, cap, cloak, gloves, shoulderpads, sleeves
3 boning: boots, pants
4 boning: tunic
