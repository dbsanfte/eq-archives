Autumn War
We have just returned from Autumn War. We had a fun time frolicing with Blatha An Oir. We spent our nights listening to the drummers drum, and to watch the lovely belly dancers. We were also there to watch the highlight of the Bane Camp "The Kissing Auction". No lovely ladys or handsome gentlemen were bidded upon. (we were a broke camp this event.)
We also met two lovely people who accepted to join our camp in our festivities. Many thanks to Rebecca and Brenne our camp companions. We had fine chats, and found these ladys to have a wonderful sense of humor, and ready wit.
We also spent time with Cass and Faylin at the event. They were great to hang out with as well.
Accept for the rain and Lady Roses severe head cold, the entire event was a great success. I hope each event gets better and better.
