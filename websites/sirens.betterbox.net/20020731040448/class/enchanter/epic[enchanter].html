Part 4 Step 1
Name Nadia Starfeast
Place Firiona Vie (Loc: -3870, 2525)
What to do Hail her and ask for a sack. Then say 'What gems?'. Then say What diamond?'. Then say 'What sapphire?'. Then say 'What ruby?'. Then say 'What emerald?'
Item Recieved Enchanter's Bag
Note be careful of your faction here, you may need to raise it if you are kill on sight by killing goblins in Warslik Woods. She will give you another Enchanter's Bag and ask that you obtain the essence of four different stones (Dull Diamond(Enchanted Diamond), Dull Sapphire(Enchanted Sapphire), Dull Ruby(Enchanted Ruby), and Dull Emerald(Enchanted Emerald))
Part 4 Step 2 (Enchanted Diamond)
Place Kaesora (Loc: 85, -415)
What to do Go there
Note Once you enter Kaesora, cast levitate and invis, and go straight forwards and drop down the hole. Don't go all the way down - just keep heading straight ahead onto the ledge in front of you. Enter the room with the three spiders and turn right and go through that door. Directly behind that door there is a secret wall which you go through then just before you hit the corner, cast invis vs. undead. Enter the building you see when you go round the corner
Part 4 Step 3 (Enchanted Diamond)
Name Spectral Librarian or a Tortured Librarian (placeholder)
Place Kaesora (Loc: 85, -415)
What to do Kill the Tortured Librarian until the Spectral Librarian spawns.
Part 4 Step 4 (Enchanted Diamond)
Name Spectral Librarian
Place Kaesora (Loc: 85, -415)
What to do charm him and give him the Dull Diamond.
Item Recieved Enchanted Diamond
Note He will turn into a Enraged Spectral Librarian. If you kill this spawn it will turn into three Freed Souls which cast some nasty area Area Effect spells. They have almost no hitpoints though but take them out quick. They are an optional extra you can gate out once you have the Enchanted Diamond if you like
