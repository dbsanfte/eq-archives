Halfling Cultural Armor
Note that these recipes (other than the acorn oil) are ONLY to be tried 
by halfling smiths and tailors, and they MUST be combined using the halfling cultural forge and Vale sewing kit.

As a point of clarification, the studs and boning used in the actual 
tailoring of these recipes use the normal recipes and ore, in normal 
forges.
  
Smithing 
  
item			high quality metal bits			66
ingredients		2 (stackable) pieces of HQ ore, flask water
			Vale forge
notes			pieces of HQ ore rarely dropping of Runnyeye goblins

ingredients 		high quality metal bits, needle mold, thimble mold, 
			flask water, Vale forge
