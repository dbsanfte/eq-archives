Protection of the Cabbage
Where Any town that has appropriate trade skill equipment
Notes This quest is done totally on trade skills. The following will document the various combines needed to create the nine gifts. Combining these nine gifts will create the spell and earring below. One thing to consider is that I personally would not attempt this quest until I was at least 199 in all of the appropriate skills of: Baking, Brewing, Jewelry, Pottery, Tailoring as all of these items may have high trivials.
Each of these gifts can be done in any order as availability or desire permits.
Cost Note: All of these gifts total can run about 16K depending on successes.
Other Note: Some items need a Mortar and Pestle and/or Glaze Mortar for combining. This is used in Poison Making, though you should be able to combine what you need with no Poison Making skill.
Certain combines require a portable trade skill container (like a Jewelry kit). These have been noted in the check list.


First Gift of the Elves
Item Demi-Sec Champagne
Skills Needed Brewing, Jewelry
Check List of Items
  • 1 Enchanted Gold Bar
  • 1 Opal
  • 1 Bottle
  • 1 Soda
  • 1 Water Flask
  • 1 Wine Yeast
  • 2 Grapes
  • 1 Jewelry kit
Combination
  1. Champagne Magnum = 1 Enchanted Gold Bar + 1 Opal + 1 Bottle (jewellery kit)
  2. Soda Water = 1 Soda + 1 water (brew barrel)
  3. 1 Champagne Magnum + 1 Soda Water + 1 Wine Yeast + 2 Grapes (unstacked) (brew barrel) = Demi Sec Champagne
