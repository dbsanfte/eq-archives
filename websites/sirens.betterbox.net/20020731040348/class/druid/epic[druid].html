Part 2 Step 5 (The Earthstained Note)
Name 1 Dark Elf Corruptor and 2 Dark Elf Reavers
Place East Karana (where you left your party)neg 1500, neg 1000
What to do The Corrupter is your target. If he is NOT attacked within 10 seconds of spawning he will despawn (and you start all over again). Easiest way to handle this, is for you to take care of the Corrupter and have your party take care of the two Dark Reavers.
Item Recieved Fleshbound Tome
Note The Reavers will try to kill all the druids/rangers that you were just dealing with. You do not want this to happen! If the NPCs are killed before you finish killing the Corrupter he will despawn. (suggestion here, an enchanter can make this VERY easy, as they can mez the two Reavers until the Corrupter is dead).

Part 4 Step 3 (Cleansed Spirit of Antonica)
Name Tainted/Corrupted Aqua goblin Shaman
Place The Aqua goblin is found in an underwater tower in Lake Rathe (loc of the tunnel is 2831, -912, -221 and is underwater).
What to do Kill the Corrupted
Item Recieved a Clean Lakewater
Note You should keep enduring breath on yourself and also be in wolf form (less agro problems). It is easiest to snare the mob and run back up to the island (above water) to fight. You can hide behind trees to keep his arrows from interrupting your casts. Only bother to kill the goblins at the top of the tower. If the Corrupted is not up keep pulling/killing the goblins in the tower and eventually the tainted will spawn. (DONT bother Deep! She is a major nasty and will spoil your day. Use harmony to single pull and you will be fine.)

Part 6 Step 2 (Cleansed Spirit of Kunark)
Name Ulump Pujik
Place Swamp of No Hope (He can be located near the froglok village at location Neg 100 Neg 1770.)
What to do Kill him
Item Recieved Froglok Essence
Note He is a level 55 warrior with almost 100% Magic Resistance. Lull will still land but very little else will. Two good groups can do this very easy. This mob takes a very tank heavy group to kill. Make sure you load up with clerics and tanks. Casters won’t be of much use here (including yourself). He double attacks for 250DMG and has 30k Hitpoints so it will take a bit to kill him. He has also been known to run at 30 % health.

Part 6 Step 4 (Cleansed Spirit of Kunark)
Name Corrupted Barracuda
Place The Corrupted Barracuda is found in the main lake of Lake of Ill Omen. You can normally find the barracuda spawns at neg 950 neg 900.
What to do kill Corrupted
Item Recieved Pure Lakewater

The second entrance is on the north side of the inside ring.

Part 6 Step 6 (Cleansed Spirit of Kunark)
Name Dragon Faydedar
Place Timorous Deep - Same area as Step 35
What to do KILL FAYDEDAR
Item Recieved Pod of Seawater!
Note tactics on Faydedar
Load up everyone with buffs (resist poison, resist disease, see invisible etc) Then place on the buffs you want to stay, making sure to save Resist Fire and Magic for last. Remove your SoW and turn run off (Faydedar can also fear). If you sit while the fight is going on, you guarantee agro from Faydedar.
