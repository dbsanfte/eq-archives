QUICK AND DIRTY BREWING GUIDE TO 200

Note: This is not a full guide on brewing, this is how I mastered the art in 6 hours of work. To get a full guide, see the many sites on the net. Brewing is not a flashy skill and you probably won't be selling much (except for Heady Kiolas and Tempers), but you can make a profit very quickly. At skill 102, I made 2-4 pp off of every 20 Ol'Tujim attempts (with 75 CHA). Not great money, but I'm sure I will do better on later levels. Freeport is easier in that you don't have to zone, so if you are a slow zoner, you probably should go to Freeport and do it. All items can be found in East Freeport. To learn brewing, a couple of items need to be addressed.
  1. Charisma plays a big part in the buying/selling...you will make more profit with high charisma.
  2. The closer you are to the "trivial" level, the better chance of it leveling (and succeeding).
  3. Contrary to some posts, you can increase level even if you fail, though it is less likely.
  4. On all recipes below, only use one of each. Use the CTRL-left click to only pick up one of an item.
  5. Both Kaladim and Freeport have two brew barrels. If one is being used, find the other. Kaladim: Pub Kal (south) and Greybloom farms (north). Freeport: Trader's Holiday and Grub and Grog Tavern (East Freeport)
  6. To use the Brew Barrel, left click on it. Then place items into the brew barrel and hit Combine.
  7. Don't test your product while doing this! It will mess up your successes.
Preparation
I prepared by going to Freeport and putting everything in my bags in the bank (with the exception of the food/water). Because of weight, I used 8 large bags, though you can use backpacks (they are three times the weight though). To master pottery, it took me only about 25pp.

Location of Ingredients

I am referencing Kaladim, though Freeport has everything you need.
  • Water Flask - purchase (don't summon) from vendor in building outside of Kaladim or Freeport Inn.
  • Cask - purchase from Pub Kal (or Trader's Holiday-Freeport). If fail in attempt, Casks are returned to you to reuse. Casks are not stackable. Since these are not used up when failing, buy about 70% of what you think you will need or buy as you go.
  • Barley, Hops, Malt, Yeast - purchase from vendor in building outside of Kaladim. Barley, Hops, and Yeast look the same, make sure to right-click on it. Don't use Wine Yeast, that's used for wine.
