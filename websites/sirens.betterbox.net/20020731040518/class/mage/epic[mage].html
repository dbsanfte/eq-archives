Magician Epic - Orb of Mastery Quest


Part 1 Step 1 (Words of Magi'Kot)
Who Rykas
Area Lake Rathetear
Location (+967.70, -665.27)
What to do You say, hail Rykas. You say, 'I am in search of knowledge'. You say, 'I wish to learn of the tale of Magi'kot'. You will then receive a Token of Mastery from Rykas.
Part 1 Step 2 (Words of Magi'Kot)
Who Jahsohn Aksot
Area West Commonlands
Location (+62.78,+3639.68)
What to do You say, 'hail Jahsohn Aksot'. At this point, give the Tone of Mastery to Jahsohn. You say, 'Rykas gave it to me.' You say, 'Yes I have come for the words of Magi'kot.' You say, 'What task.'
Notes He will wander a little so just get close to this and then use the sense summoned spell to be directed to his pet. When you cast it make sure you don't have a pet though or else you will be directed at your own pet. He will ask you to find him some pages.
Part 1 Step 3 (Words of Magi'Kot)
Who dread wolf, Tentacle Terrors, Bloodthirsty Ghoul
Area Dread Wolf(Kithicor Woods), Tentacle Terrors(Estate of Unrest), Bloodthirsty Ghoul(Lower Guk)
Location See notes
What to do Kill each one and loot for the pages.
Notes The first page are dropped off of an enraged dread wolf within Kithicor Woods. He is a common spawn and only appears during night. The second page is dropped off of Tentacle Terrors in The Estate of Unrest (Level 17-33). They only drop from the ones in Unrest, not off of the ones in Najena. The third page is dropped off of a Bloodthirsty Ghoul within Lower Guk. They are level 38-42 and spawn in the same room as the frenzies do.

Part 2 Step 1 (Power of the Elements)
Who Walnan
Area Butcherblock
Location See notes
What to do You say, 'hail Walnan'. You say, 'I am in search of the truth'. You say, 'I have come to learn of Magi'. You say, 'No'.
Notes After killing the Gnome Mage at the Docks in Butcherblock (+1848, +624), you can use the spell. Sense Summoned to lead you to Walnan's pet. Walnan is a rare spawn so be patient if she isn't around at the time. The Gnome Mage does not have to be killed to get Walnan to spawn. Walnan also doesn't always have a pet, so the most reliable way is to get a tracker.

Part 3 Step 3 (Power of the Orb)
Who Rykas
Area Lake Rathetear
Location (+967.70, -665.27)
What to do Once you have all four pages find Rykas. Give him the three items you received from the previous quests, The Words of Magi'kot, the Power of the Elements, and the Words of Mastery. He will then give you the Power of the Orb.
Notes An Elementalist's power comes from the Orb of Mastery. The Elements of Earth, Fire, Wind, and Water are contained within the Orb. The Master of Elements hold the Orb. He can be found in the Plane of Air. No mortal has been able to contain the true power of the Orb. Trilith Magi'kot presented himself to the Master of Elements and the Orb of Mastery. The power of elements brought agony to his mind and body. Magi'kot was not of Balance. Fire burned his eyes unto blindness, Wind put his mind into a spin, Earth entered his body and aged him like the mountains, and finally, Water washed his memories of mortal life away. Failing his test, the Orb exacted its terrible price, and now Magi'kot is part of the Orb. The bearer of this tome should seek out the Master of Fire and present it to him, for there is much left to be revealed.

Part 5 Step 4 (Element of Earth)
Who Vira
Area Temple of Solusek Ro
Location ?
What to do Do the sub quests
Notes Both the Shovel of Ponz and the Broom of Triton are subquests. Both quests are given by Vira in the Temple of Solusek Ro. For the Shovel of Ponz you need a shovel dropped from level 25-32 alligators within Feerrott, the toes of a hill giant from a hill giant, a gargoyles eye from gargoyles in Ocean of Tear, and a ruby from gem selling vendors. For the Broom of Triton you need a broom from alligators in Feerrott, the toes of a cyclops, the feathers of a griffon, and a star ruby from gem selling vendors. For these quests you need to have good faction with the Temple of Solusek Ro which can be obtained by killing Shadow-Men.
