Druid Faction Fix


NAME Holly Windstalker
Place Toxxulia Forest
What to do Track and hunt the Poachers.
Item Needed poachers heads
Notes Note 1 - Return to Surefall Glade. If you are KOS be sure to be invisible. Gillarian Naelev - Hand him the Poacher Heads.
Note 2 - If you are KOS with Gillarian - Bring friend with camo or invis. Cast harmony on Gillarian. Go up to him, and hand him the heads(DONT HIT TRADE) Have your friend cast invisibility on you(THEN hit trade)
NAME Telin Darkforest
Place Any place you can buy muffins
What to do Buy as many muffins as you can carry
Item Needed Muffins
Notes Note 1 - Now go to West Freeport. There is a tavern/inn halfway between the West Freeport gates and the East Freeport Zone. Upstairs is a dwarf named Pandros Flintside. Hand him 4 muffins per slot, for a total of 16 muffins.
Note 2 - If you are KOS with Pandros - Bring friend with camo or invis. Cast Harmony on Pandros. Go up to him, and hand him the muffins(DONT HIT TRADE) Have your friend cast invisibility on you(THEN hit trade)
NAME Alrik Farsight
Place Any area that sells bat wings
What to do Purchase as many stacks of batwings as you can.
Item Needed Bat Wings
Notes Note 1 - Find Niola Impholder(South Felwithe - mage guild) and hand him stacks of 4 batwings per slot (16 total a trade)
Note 2 - If you are KOS with Niola - Bring friend with camo or invis. Cast Harmony on Niola. Go up to him, and hand him the bat wings(DONT HIT TRADE) Have your friend cast invisibility on you(THEN hit trade) Note 3 - You can also kill Kizdean Gix in West Commons
NAME Kinlo Strongarm
Place Crushbone off of Greater Fay
What to do Kill Orcs
Item Needed orc belts and orc legionnaire shoulder pads
Notes Note 1 - turn items into Canloe Nusbeck for faction. You will need 4 belts or 4 shoulder pads at a time to turn in
