The waves thundered against the edges of the cliff, leaving white foam on its ledge, then pulled away as if being sucked back in to the chaos of the sea. A roll of thunder followed in angry response, as hard rain fell from the black cloud filled sky.

A lone figure stood upon the ledge. A dark form dressed in black robes, which clung like a second skin. Aazuma stood her feet slightly apart, her arms raised with palms upwards, fingertips touching. Her eyes closed, she chanted the sacred incantation.

Slowly her hair changed to the purest white, her eyes to a deep onyx black, her skin became a ebony hue. She swayed, drunken in the euphoria of her transformation.


Laughing she cried out

Sirens hear me,
I am one,

Sirens here me,
I am all,

All is mine
I have the power


The thunder and waves roared back as if in agreement.
