Lots and Lots...
There have been a bunch of new updates recently from both Plane of Fear and Plane of Hate. Boy, Verant has really turned on the goodies faucet! Interesting items are pouring out of the planes and places like Kithicor which seem to have undergone an undead invasion of amazing proportions.


October 25, 1999

Upcoming changes...
Well, I'm adding more items as I get a chance. A lot of my time has been spent on redoing the site. We have recently joined GamersX as a affiliate and are trying to bring the site over to their servers. This will entail a fair amount of recoding which is what is currently being worked on. In the meantime, enjoy the updates that are coming in!


October 13, 1999

Too much to count...
There's a bunch of new items added, too many to count (about 15 so far as I write this with more coming). Go ahead and resubmit your searches and browsing because you should all be pleasantly surprised (especially you bards).


October 12, 1999

Sorry about lack of updates...
Due to a family emergency (death in the family) I haven't been on the ball as far as updates go here. I'm in the process of collecting everything I've got so far and putting it up so look for a large update in the next day or two!


October 3, 1999

More from PoF
It being a slow week I'm happy to say we got some more items from the PoF. Specifically, the rest of the Valorium equipment all you Paladins have been waiting for.

For those of you of other classes, we're still waiting to see a lot of your class-specific stuff from PoF so mail it in!
