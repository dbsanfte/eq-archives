History of the Dragons of Xev

Dragons of Xev was founded in November of 2000, at the start of the Xev server in the North Ro tunnel. The members were all low, and had dreams of some day making DoX an all powerful guild. Most of those starting members moved on. Many have stayed and still desire to make DoX what it was intended to be in the start.

Since the beginning days DoX has evolved to a close fun loving family. We've had members come and go...all along they remembered their start and remained our close friends. DoX has been a starting place for many high level players on the Xev server.

DoX's original leader, Snay, left the guild in December of 2001, due to his lack of EQ time.  He turned the guild over to Bonecarver, one of DoX's founding members. Bonecarver remodeled DoX into a guild of variety and fun. DoX was no longer a stepping stone for low and midlevel players.
It became a family that everyone loves, cherishes, and sticks together with, till after many deaths do they part, still even then. they usually hang out together.

In July 2003, Bonecarver passed the reins of the Dragons of Xev to Vahpurr. She is gathering together the members of DoX and expanding the guild through careful  recruitment. If you are interested in joining our family, please see the application page for more information.
