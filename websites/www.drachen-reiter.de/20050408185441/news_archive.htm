Faction Hunt DR style
13/06/03

Now that nobody knows on what faction he is anymore, why not pay a visit to Kael Drakkal? King Tormax and the Statue were up, and off we went.

KT was the first to be rudely awakened, and after lots of armsraising and shouting he dropped like a brick.

Soon to be followed by Vindi, who stood in our way to the Statue. Jumping us when raidbuffed is a bad idea, period.

The Statue tried a similar stunt on us, but after one of the best pushes i've ever seen (juuust passed the gate with the dreaded flag, and into the corner in one smooth move, amazing :-), he also gave up.

No AoW pic yet, unfortunately (and who wants to see a heap of splattered people, eh?), BUT we got him to 8% first try ever. Doing so with 35 people, of which only 3 were Clerics i am confident he too will bite it very soon.

After some sleep and tactics review we entered Nwing again, having 2 options: follow the right path, clearing some respawn and push to the exit mobs, or go the left way, and face Lord Vyemm to start with.

Then, after recovering from this battle, Lord Koi'Doken and Lady Mirenilla met their doom, as did a large collection of Drakes and guardians. Pushing to the exit from this point, it was Jorlleag's turn to die.

Next time we will totally clear this place. We had loads of fun, that's for sure! Great job all and grats on the goodies!
