Wandering the world is good, but wandering the underworld is better.

Tuesdays from June 2002

As I was wandering through the Tenebrous Mountains, I paid a visit to the Coterie of the Eternal Night. While they weren't very talkative, they were otherwise quite courteous in allowing me to tour their impressive keep.
Once a year, I make a pilgrimage to a small village in the East Commonlands. According to legend, Bertoxxulous himself walked here in the flesh and gazed upon Antonica, leaving his gifts in the earth before returning to his plane. It's a good picnic spot.
Since I was in the neighborhood, I decided to stop in for a visit with one of the master necromancers on Luclin. After getting a few tips on minions from him, we spent the better part of the day swapping stories and discussing the finer points of secret lairs.
"A good death is better than a bad life."
