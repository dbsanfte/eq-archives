The only proper way to end to a relaxing day of fishing is by cooking up your catch and having a nice meal, killing anyone who comes near your campsite. One must enjoy the simple pleasures.

A Fish Called Dinner

Your fresh catch is prepared much like any other food, in an oven or (more likely) in your portable spit. The difference is that many of the recipes call only for a certain kind of fish, such as salmon, for example; in that case, any sort of salmon can be used in that recipe.

Several recipes call for items that you won't find on a corpse and can't purchase from a merchant, such as berries. These items can usually be acquired by threatening a nearby druid, and are emphasized accordingly.

Other recipes call for additional utensils, like skewers and smokers. These are emphasized also; have your enslaved potter or smith craft them for you. These items can be used repeatedly, as they are not consumed in the preparation, so having a single one available will suffice.
