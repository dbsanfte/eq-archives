The Well-Read Reanimator

Mysterious Whispers

...of the three Bloodlines which serve Arawn...

...known for Intelligence and Dexterity but...

...accompanied by Horrific Undead creatures...

...their rituals of Unspeakable Darkness...

...with Powers learned on the Battlefields...

...reflected in a Mastery of Ancient Lore...

...are Not to be trifled with.

any are those who have contributed to this Translative work and the community at large after their fashion. Some have performed Laborious studies and tests of necromantic ability; some have presented our common causes with Admirable prudence and persistence; some compose works of a Scholarly nature; some provide a Brilliantly shining example of the power of the Dark Arts; and so it goes.

There is no way to ennumerate or commemorate All of those who have added to the collected Lore of the necromancer, however thankful myself and others may be for their generosity and good will. Some few of these, however, are here duly noted and enshrined (if not entombed) for their works.

Mausoleum of Fame

Behold, and let these examples serve to Drive us all to greater achievement, perfect knowledge, and the light that Walks hand-in-hand with Darkness without reflection.
