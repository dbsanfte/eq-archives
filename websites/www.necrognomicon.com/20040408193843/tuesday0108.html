Tuesdays from August 2001

I once encountered a goblin who was different than his kin – calm, insightful, and rational. After recovering from my initial shock, we had a long discussion concerning the histories of the goblin tribes and how an ancient curse brought them to their current state. We then shared a dinner of roast giant and gnomish spirits, swapping stories until late in the night.
