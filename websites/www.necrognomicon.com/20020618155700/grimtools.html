Appendix

Going into battle empty-handed is a bad idea. Going into battle looking like you just got back from a Felwithe flea market is even worse. Don't give in to those charismatic elven wenches hawking their steins and stones at every lamppost – get yourself some gear that says, "I've come about the reaping."

Implements of Destruction

While there are many necromancers who would disagree with me, it remains my opinion that keeping a good weapon handy is vital. There are several excellent choices for the necromancer. Keep in mind that the right weapon not only serves you in battle, but gives you that menacing aura which sets you apart from the weak and spineless.

Crafted by Bertoxxulous himself, this wicked blade was forged to fulfill his designs in Norrath. With its matching parrying dagger (below), it holds a place of honor for those who serve him.

Instruments of Darkness

While the incredible necromantic power which we wield is without compare, the wise necromancer will enhance that power whenever possible. To that end, an assortment of tools and utensils present themselves for our dark purposes.

This parrying dagger is one of only two items known to have been created by Bertoxxulous himself – the other being Woe, the matching blade which completes the set.
