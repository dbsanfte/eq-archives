Appendix

It came to my attention that the "trivial" levels for researching various spells do not seem to be generally known. To assist in determining these levels for the researched spells of necromancy, this reference guide has been put together.

Research Experiments

Until verified by experiment, I'm not assuming that spells of the same level trivial at the same point. I'm also not assuming that a lower level spell will necessarily have a trivial level below all other higher level spells. That would make sense, and I wouldn't be surprised to see that, but there's always the chance for something odd.

If you have any updates, please send them directly to me and I'll put the new information in the chart. Please send information only on combinations that you have performed yourself. Information should include:

  • The spell combination attempted.
  • Your research skill level.
  • If the combination was trivial or not on a successful attempt. (If you received the "You cannot increase your skill" message, it's trivial. Failed attempts don't generate the message, so they can't be counted.)
