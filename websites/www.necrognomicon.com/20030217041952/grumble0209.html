Grumblings from September 2002

September 15, 2002

It's been a while since there have been any updates of note, and it will probably be two or three weeks before any more appear. Sorry about that – if you want the reasons, drop in the Words of Darkness.

I'm planning on more changes in October though, it being such a fine month for necromancy and all. To that end, I've put up a couple of polls to find out what people would prefer. Those too can be found in the Words of Darkness.
