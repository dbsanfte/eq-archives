Appendix

Any combat encounter will rely on several functions being performed. Who performs these functions and how effective they are at it will determine success or failure. Know who does what, so you know what to do yourself.

Pulling - Who starts the fight?

Damage Dealing - Something has to actually kill the enemy.

Damage Taking - The enemy will beat someone up, given a chance.

Healing - Ideally, the Damage Taker doesn't die in the process.

Crowd Control - How do you keep yourself safe from the angry mobs?

Killing Blow - Who ends the fight?

Plan B - When things go Really Wrong, how do you survive?

If each class is listed here, this page is going to get huge pretty quickly. Each one of these options should probably be on its own page, with an appropriate link.

Buffing and Debuffing should get mentioned, but those functions should probably be rolled into the Damage Dealer/Damage Taker/Healer model, since they're secondary and not primary functions.
