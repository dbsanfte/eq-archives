Appendix

If you're going to call upon the undying forces to do your bidding, don't insult them by sporting some worn-out tunic or a robe only an elf could love. Look menacing! Powerful! Diabolical! It's worth the extra effort, and it will help set you apart from all of those unfortunate souls who turned away from the raw power of death and must therefore be consumed by it.

Looks to Die For

More than anything else, the robe is what makes it clear to the world that you are versed in the arcane arts and are not to be trifled with. It also marks you as a prime target for your enemies, but that's the way it goes. With very few exceptions, the robe will provide you with the best protection and the most distinctive look.
