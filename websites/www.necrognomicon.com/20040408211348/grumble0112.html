Grumblings from December 2001

December 27, 2001

Some fairly observant gnomes noted my disappearance on October 31. A few of them went and searched for me – in vain, as it happens, but they will be rewarded later. While I have no inclination to reveal what I've been doing for the past several weeks, I'm sure someone will make an educated guess when they stumble upon the skeletal hordes.

For now, I'm once again turning my attention to these pages. With transport to Luclin now available, many areas need minor updates, and a lot of areas need major updates. I'll be scribing what I can in the time I have available.

While you're waiting, go and kill something for your Uncle Morty.
