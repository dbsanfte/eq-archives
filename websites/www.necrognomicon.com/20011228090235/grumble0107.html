Grumblings from July 2001

July 31, 2001

I've updated the appearance once again, gotten some graphics in line, and plotted out the remaining items that need to be done. The plan: Rework the recipe page to incorporate the new food recipes; Class information pages for Tactics of Terror; Level information pages for Trail of Blood; Backfill Tactics of Terror with the tactics from Trail pages; set up templates for each of the Cruel World pages; gather remaining screenshots and fill in the Cruel World as I travel about more.

That seems reasonable enough.

July 3, 2001

All right... I'm getting back on track, and the updates should continue again. Joy.

The Research Trivials Chart is currently ugly but functional. When it's finished, the numbers will be plugged in here in the appropriate places. It still has a lot of holes in it, though. If you have any information that would help narrow things down, feel free to just drop a short note over on the message board and I'll update the list.

I've added Goner and Zoner to the menu. Just because I can.
