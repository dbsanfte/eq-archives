The Well-Read Reanimator

Mysterious Whispers

...of the three Bloodlines which serve Arawn...

...known for Intelligence and Dexterity but...

...accompanied by Horrific Undead creatures...

...their rituals of Unspeakable Darkness...

...with Powers learned on the Battlefields...

...reflected in a Mastery of Ancient Lore...

...are Not to be trifled with.

ecromancy is both a Science and an Art, a path of study as well as a way of life (as it were). Many scholars, of necromantic profession or otherwise, have offered comment upon the Darkest of Magics that is insightful, timely, or otherwise worth mention. Some of these works are reproduced here in the hope that such wise words will Illuminate the meaning of this most obscure practice and serve Arawn's Chosen in their continuing endeavours.

The Well-Read Reanimator

While the Authors of these works may speak in phrases foreign and strange, with strings of plain verbiage interspersed with most obtuse jargon, remember always that a Prince may wear the rags of a Beggar yet retain the power to command obedience with a word.

  • The Iron Mage Guide to Necromancer RvR – A valuable overview of what a Deathsight necromancer may rightly contribute to Albion's defense.
  • Norien's Guide to Buffing the Necromancer – Clerics and Friars take heed! Here are the secrets to sharing your Enhancements with the unliving and the undead.
  • What Transfers to the Pet, and When – Although I have compiled this information for reference, much of the content originates with other Insightful souls who have spent considerable effort in determining the veracity of its contents and to whom we owe a Debt of gratitude.

If erudite works of great value to Necromancers come to light which by merit of their Wisdom are deserving of reproduction, the editor would be most interested in learning of them.
