Grumblings from May 2002

May 31, 2002

After a mad tour of the world, I've completed my guide to the Combine Firepots. Travel in shadows.

May 28, 2002

And then, to my surprise as much as anyone else's, there was an update today.

The Words of Disillusionment, which previously appeared as Words of Dissolution, have been properly updated in the research spells and research words pages. Their appearance was unexpected – at least to me – but it does help clear up some confusion.

My list of level 12 spells was incomplete, due to those meddling necromancers on the moon. The list has been updated appropriately. Names have been taken.

I'm collecting the information necessary to finally add my page regarding the Combine Firepots. Hopefully the information presented will be useful, and will justify the risks. I just don't trust magic done by wizards.

Spending some time on a rampage of necromantic slaughter really makes for a fine day. Ahhh...
