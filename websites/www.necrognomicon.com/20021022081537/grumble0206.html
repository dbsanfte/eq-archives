Grumblings from June 2002

June 21, 2002

I've updated by faction listing to include the major cities of Luclin. There have also been a few minor updates of little consequence.

June 12, 2002

After all this time, I finally find myself at the 50th rank of power. It's been an interesting trip, and an enjoyable one.

I'm still working on my Graveyard Tour, and I'm collecting information on Luclin starting factions before I do too much killing.

June 7, 2002

Just a few minor things today, mostly updating information that had changed over the past few months.

The message board is now an EZBoard Gold Community with faster loading and no ads. I'm pretty pleased with it.

I also put together a Magelo profile, if anyone cares about what I'm wearing.

June 5, 2002

Like most other necromancers, I'm trying to figure out how to work the newly-revealed caster focus items to my advantage. A few of them look particularly nice. When I have enough information, I'll set up a separate section for them in my notes.

I'm also collecting information for my upcoming Graveyard Tour – if you know of a good graveyard or tomb (as opposed to a place simply frequented by the undead), pay a visit to the Words of Darkness and let me know.

Speaking of the Words, I put a wrench in my coinpurse and made it a Gold Community. No more pop-up ads as soon as they determine my money is good.

People have been responding favorably to my Firepots Guide, which makes it all worthwhile. Glad to be of service to my necromantic associates.

NecroGnomicon t-shirts and miscellaneous other small items are currently being designed, including at least one general "necromancer" design. When they're completed, I'll be certain to make a large announcement.

My faithful pet Lebarn is the most recent recipient of the Tarnished Bone Medal. Among other things, he helped me acquire another level of power. Ahhh...

"Death – getting medieval on you since the beginning of time."
