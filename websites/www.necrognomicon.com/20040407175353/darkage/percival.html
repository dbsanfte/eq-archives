Mysterious Whispers

...of the three Bloodlines which serve Arawn...

...known for Intelligence and Dexterity but...

...accompanied by Horrific Undead creatures...

...their rituals of Unspeakable Darkness...

...with Powers learned on the Battlefields...

...reflected in a Mastery of Ancient Lore...

...are Not to be trifled with.

ehold! There is one among you who Shines, innocent in the world yet of world reknown. He is the symbol of the Quest, but more importantly of the Quest completed: for of all the Nobility of Arthur's knights, only one achieved the Grail. He was neither the wisest nor the holiest, neither the strongest nor the bravest. Yet did he hold what others sought in vain, beholding with his eyes what others found only in dream.

Percival Lore

Should you think to join those who follow the Grail Knight, consider this: Percival's ranks are loyal and steadfast, and they are many. You will be Welcome indeed, although you seek here in vain if you seek solitude. Know here too that your Actions may speak loudly, but your Words are still heard; take heed then that they are words well-measured.

Those who are friends of Percival may wish to gather with their Peers to discuss, conspire, threaten, exclaim, or otherwise make their thoughts known in one of these places.

  • VN Percival Boards. Rush in where angels fear to tread.
  • Albion, Percival. A forum for the residents of the green and pleasant lands known best to Arthur and company.
  • Percival Dungeons. A forum with post-pubescent moderators. Beware Titi!

Should some place of importance be Excluded here in ignorance, I would be most interested in knowing more of it that I may send others thence.
