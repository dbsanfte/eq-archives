Grumblings from November 2002

November 15, 2002

After a bit of thought and a few rituals I'm not prepared to discuss, I've decided to expand the message boards to include necromancers from other worlds, as well as provide a place for necromantic discussion in general. Hopefully, this will be well-received, and you'll immediately head there to talk about the dread things that interest you. Yes, you! YOU! TALK NOW!

(ahem)

In any case, work on the main area will continue in its slow, inevitable way, much like the creeping hand of death itself.

I've also added a short tale concerning a deep necromantic trance I recently underwent.

Rest well...
