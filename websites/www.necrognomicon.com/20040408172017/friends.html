Appendix

the Dark Age translation

It's always a pleasure when others join me in my ongoing killing spree. Over the course of time, I've made many interesting friends. Some of them I still see now and again, while others have either sought adventure in more challenging lands or faded into memory. I'm sure we'll meet again – but this is a common belief among necromancers.

Friends of Morty

Roserot Swampflower, a powerful shaman among her people, brings her "swamp mojo" far and wide. She's fun to be around, and she has her own theme song.
Slithermoss, Roserot's younger sister, isn't really what I'd call a "fun" troll, but she's definitely powerful for her age and is already garnering some attention from the guild leaders at Night Keep. I look forward to seeing her on a rampage as she continues her necromantic studies.
Amelia Brightblade, wizard extraordinaire and purveyor of fine baked goods. Travel with Amelia is never dull; I'll attempt further explorations with her after I master a few more rituals of darkness.

Morty's Minions

Bogroot is my primary supplier of smithed goods. Why? "Trollstuff no break." He's also a powerful shaman in his own right, spreading the Fear of Cazic-Thule throughout the world.
Leanann Sidhe, an enchantress of high elven origin, can often pick up various supplies in places where I'm less than welcome. Her "Love is stronger than Death" tirades are occasionally amusing. She spends most of her time with Roserot.
Athravan, despite his claims of agnosticism, is really a closet follower of Solusek Ro. Even though he's just starting to come into his own as a magician, his summonings are occasionally quite useful.

[Translator's Note: Legend reports that Roserot Swampflower remains to this day in a shamanic trance, secluded within a Nameless swamp, while Bogroot remains always near to protect her. It is unknown whether any Other of these souls yet remain in the lands of the living. – B]
