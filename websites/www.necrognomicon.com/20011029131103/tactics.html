How I Did It

While I certainly can't claim to be the best or the most successful necromancer, I do believe that I've been around long enough to justify spouting off questionable bits of wisdom in the hopes that others may be strengthened in their dark deeds.

  • Deadly Methods: There are many approaches to combat available to the necromancer. Although some of those approaches are more popular than others, all of them are worthy of contemplation.
  • A Class Apart: Over the course of time, you will undoubtedly find yourself in the company of those who follow different professions. If you familiarize yourself with their capabilities, you'll know what to expect when you fight together (if things go well) or when they turn on you (if things go poorly).
  • Important Safety Tips: When you're fighting, travelling, or just visiting a pesky merchant, it's good to keep a few things in mind.

The Dead Next Door

Raising the dead is more than just a fun hobby – that smiling, friendly skeleton is just waiting for the opportunity to lash out in fearful necromantic vengeance. The wise practitioner of the dark arts will be sure to study the finer points of those undead minions we call up.

  • Autopsy Reports: Detailed examinations of many undead remains have revealed the limits and abilities of our minions.
  • Dead Pet Tricks: The necromancer's bony "pet" is both loyal and fierce, but it is only as powerful as the mind controlling it. It's important to know which commands the walking dead will heed.
  • Dominating the Dead: Taking control of naturally occurring undead is both rewarding and dangerous, and worthy of special consideration.

There are undoubtedly many tactics of which I know very little. If you'd care to share those things with your fellow necromancers, make your mark in the Words of Darkness.
