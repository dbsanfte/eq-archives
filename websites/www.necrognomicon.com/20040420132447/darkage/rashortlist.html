The Recommended List

To make the realistic Development of our abilities all the more clear, let us first discard those which have no proven effect As they are of no use to us, we need pay them no heed. Concentration serves as an apt example.

Let us then turn to those choices which, even though some few Necromancers may find them of benefit, the majority of Arawn's Chosen overlook in favor of more direct gain. Lifter makes this case clear.

When this is done, we are left with something more concise, and perhaps more useful.
