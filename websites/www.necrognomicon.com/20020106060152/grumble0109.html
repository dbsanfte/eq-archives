Grumblings from September 2001

September 28, 2001

Obviously, I haven't been around for a while. Other concerns have taken up my time, and I have a number of projects that will continue to demand my attention. As a result, I'll need to change a few of my immediate plans for the NecroGnomicon.

I'll think about the changes over the weekend, and then start putting things into proper places next week. What I'm currently considering is this:

  • Removing the Cruel World. It's a nice idea, but I can see it becoming quite time-consuming. I'll keep it in mind after everything else is complete.
  • Revising Tactics of Terror. I had originally planned on an area detailing how to work with each class. I may instead focus on the various tactics available to the necromancer, with a few side comments regarding class interaction as applicable.
  • Finishing the Trail of Blood. I like where it's going, even if it does take a while to put those pages together.

So... we'll see. If anyone has other suggestions, feel free to drop on by the message board and share.

Now go out and kill a goblin for your Uncle Morty.

September 11, 2001

There won't be any updates today. My condolences and best wishes go out to those who have been affected by today's tragedies.

September 10, 2001

One of the best places to find all kinds of necromantic information, necro.eqclasses.com, seems to be missing. If you have any information regarding its disappearance, please let us know in the Words of Darkness.

(Nevermind... it's back now.)

I've started the next section in the Trail of Blood, and added a tactic to a previous step. There were two or three other tiny changes.

September 7, 2001

No updates today – the freelance necromancer needed to interview. Such is life.

I also spent my evening on my first Fear raid, and it was quite the fun time. I'd like to thank the fine people in Crimson Legacy for a truly enjoyable experience.

September 6, 2001

A lot of the missing pictures have been filled in, now that I've had a chance to visit Ak'Anon again.

September 5, 2001

Very few updates today, mostly in the form of archiving and a picture or two.

Now would be a fine time for you to visit the Words of Darkness and introduce yourself.
