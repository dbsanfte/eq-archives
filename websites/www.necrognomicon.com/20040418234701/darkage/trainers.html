The Arch-Necromancers of the Temple of Arawn

Those few majestic reanimators who have achieved complete mastery in every aspect of the Dark Arts may be approached by even the youngest of Arawn's Chosen, for they are sworn to Teach that which they know, thus continuing the grand tradition and expanding the Temple's reach into the affairs of the world. Partaking of this boon, though, will likely necessitate a journey to one of these Demonic Tutors, as they are not wont to travel far from their stations.

The Shrouded Isles of Avalon

Since the current hardships of Avalon (and in particular the forbidden necromantic practices of Morgana) were the immediate causation of Arawn's decision to send the Inconnu in assistance, it is to be expected that wise Mentors have made themselves available to those who labor for the isles.

Note that Narah is the sole trainer of Disciples; as such, any who would don the mantle of Necromancer must first demonstrate to her their mastery of the base spells in combat against the lesser foes near the harbor.

The Shores of Atlantis

Our understanding of the planes forming the Atlantean trials grows daily, but those highest in Arawn's hierarchy show little interest in establishing any permanent presence therein, deeming those areas somewhat Unreal. Thus only a single guide, which some would call merely a Token, stands ready for the continued training of those Necromancers daring to face the ancient challenges.

Favorite Color Black

These, then, are the Mighty ones who offer their wisdom to guide the Necromancer on the path of eternal night. Be not afraid to approach them, for despite their Ineffable power they gladly share the lessons taught them by time and toil.
