Appendix

The Words of Power are many, and several of them can be combined into more than one spell. Most of the Words are necromantic in nature, but there are a few that play a part solely in the research of the magician. Those lesser, unimportant Words will be ignored.

"Do You Need Words of Comeuppance?"

For each Word, the spells that can be formed are listed, along with the level of those spells. Words necessary for spells which can only be obtained through research are emphasized, as they are significantly more important.
