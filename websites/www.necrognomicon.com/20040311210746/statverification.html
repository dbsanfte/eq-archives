I accept as given that Piety, Empathy, and Charisma have no effect on any ability of the necromancer, shade, or pet.

Bonuses and Effects from Items

Item bonuses only affect the pet while the item is being worn. This was changed at some point in patch 1.67; previously, certain bonuses were only checked at the time the pet was summoned.

In general, weapons only work normally for the necromancer; the shade can't melee and the pet doesn't use the weapon or inherit any of the weapon's qualities (DPS, Speed, Quality, Condition, Bonus, Proc, Charges). The same applies to armor, which works normally for the necromancer; the shade can't be hit in melee and the pet doesn't use the armor or inherit any of the armor's qualities (AF, Quality, Condition, Bonus, Reverse Proc, Charges). Items used in inventory (charged items, /use, /use2, /interact) function properly for the necromancer; most cannot be used in shade form but exceptions exist, the most notable exception being water-breathing potions. Effects do not transfer to the pet.
