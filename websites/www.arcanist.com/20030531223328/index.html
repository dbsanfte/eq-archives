  -= E3 Madness =-


You have to love E3. Whether you actually get to go, or have to sit at home drooling over tidbits dribbled out by attendees, E3 is inarguably the highpoint of the gaming year.

I play a lot of games. I'm in college, I think there's a law somewhere that says I have to. And from the tidbits I saw, there's going to be a lot to keep us busy over the coming year. Just to taunt and tantalize, I collected some trailers from various upcoming games (EQ2, Horizons, WoW, Dungeon Siege II) and threw them under the Downloads.

Let me just take a moment here. Yes, I said Dungeon Siege II. It boggles the mind, doesn't it? I mean.. I played the first. It was fun for a day or two. Like NWN, it's sorta one big potion fest (not my favorite sort of RPG, but that's another story). From what I saw in the trailer though..whoa. That's NOT the the child of the original DS. It looks to have a much deeper story, and FAR better graphics. Hopefully it's more of a challenge, and not just eye candy. Not to downplay the other companies previewing their games, but I'd say DS2 was perhaps the best looking MMOG that we've seen to date, even giving Horizons a run for its money.

So what games will I be playing? Probably all of them, depending on budget. The true question will be which one I stay with, and I think it's way too early for that kind of decision.
