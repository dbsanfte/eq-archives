April 21, 2000.

April 22, Kedge Keep  Raid Info
KK is awesome and non-crowded, we want to be there this Saturday as early as possible and stay in there for as long as we can. Meet at the entrance to Unrest at noon EST (9am PST).  The plan is to have a low to mid 30's group at the zone line fighting lancer fish (great XP at those levels) and at the same time camping the Fierce Impaler (drops gloomwater harpoon/darksea Harpoon), the 40+ groups camp cauldron bubble for awhile (Sharkbone Warhammer/Hammerhead Helm) once we have a big enough group we go in near the Undertow shark area and camp 3 named mobs there (Undertow, Seahorse Matriarch and Coralyn KelpMaiden.  CoC guild looting rules apply (basically items go to whoever is there participating regardless of group and NEEDS the item, if more than two need it then only they lotto for it, if nobody needs it then a full raid group lotto applies) 

 

Rules for Kedge Keep are :

- Bring at least one Fish Gill Extract potion, and 20 fish scales, they sell at herbalists for some 14pp.

- Time the spawns, all monsters in KK spawn every 18 minutes.

- Time your Enduring Breath, if it goes away during a fight you may drown.

- Never pull ANYTHING with blind, sunbeam, snare, etc, if you have to use spells to pull something it should be a DOT or DD Spell.

- Never kill the tiny shimmering sailfins, they explode for 400 points of damage when killed and do AoE!!!!!! and they are also PH for piranhas. 

- Absolutely NO AoE Spells unless you are 100% sure of what is in the rooms above and below you, else you WILL bring some nasty fish.

- No AFK, you must keep a time on your Enduring breath, you Must buy a potion of water breathing (can't remember the name right now) for emergencies AND/or always have a water stone ready (free from a magician), having fish scales handy (TONS of them) helps too.

- If you charm ANYTHING (all the fish fall under the Charm animal spell line) you DON'T move away from it, you sit where you are, you make sure the pet is RIGHT there with you, if it looses you (it goes on another path trying to get to you, which is a bug in KK) it will go down deep and when the charm breaks, the party is over as it will appear in front of the unfortunate charmer along with A TON of friends... LOADING PLEASE WAIT

Follow this simple steps and you will have a blast underwater =)
