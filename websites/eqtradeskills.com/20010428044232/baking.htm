Well, I have decided to share with you all the way that I became a master baker!  I did it by no means the hard way!  I had a little change to spare so I bought most of the things I needed but I did it in less than 6 hours!!!  So if you want the easy road to be a master baker, here it is!

I started by hitting my guild master and dropping a few points into baking, just to save a little time.  To do this, you need to be in Freeport.  I got a spit there and bought 2 backpacks full of fish from the vendors on the dock.  I then ran to East Commonlands.  Near the first inn, there is a vendor that sells baking supplies. Parthar is his name and he is right next to the inn (west side I believe).  There I bought 2 backpacks full of frosting.  Depending on how many open slots you have,  you may be able to buy more or less than I was.  If you are short on space, don't worry about buying the fish in Freeport.  It just saves you a trip to town in the long run anyway =)  Okay so now we have some frosting!  Now you need to run all the way across the zone heading to West Commonlands.  When you get near the third inn, there will be a small building on the east side of it.  Walk on in and you will see an Erudite named Katha Firespinner.  She will sell you an unlimited number of Bat Wings!  So, here is where we begin.  Buy as many bat wings as you can hold, then start making batwing crunchies in your spit!  Keep doing this until you run out of frosting.  Then stock up on a TON of batwings and run back to the first in to buy more frosting.  Keep doing this until you hit the trivial for Batwing Crunchies (trivial at 45).  Now your ready to start the fish portion of your baking!  If you have the backpacks of fish from Freeport, then you can start right there with Parthar, buy a lot of jug o' sauce and start making fish filets.  You will probably fail a lot but your skill goes up regardless.  When you run out of fish, fill up on sauces and run to the Freeport pier again and buy all the fish you can!  Then do the same until Fish fillets become trivial (trivial at 82).  The last thing you will be making is Fish Rolls, fish and bat wings.  You can get an endless supply of bat wings from  Lysa Truegreen (Academy of Arcane Sciences, W. Freeport) and
Zasrin Blueflame (Academy of Arcane Sciences, W. Freeport), instead of running all the way to East Commonlands again. You get the idea.  Those become trivial at 135.  I stopped there as the recipes that will get you to 142 all require clump of dough and I didn't want to kill a ton of snakes to get eggs =)  Well, thats it!! I feel bad because it was kind of cheesy doing it that way but hey, I wanted to get some info up here and know what I was talking about when I did it.  Since doing this, I have bought a bowl and I am getting ready to start making those wretched clumps of dough and max the skill out!  Hope that helps any of you speed bakers out there.  Any other suggestions or stories would be appreciated so feel free to send em in!
