Monday, May 27, 2002 09:23 PM PST - Chat feature was re-added to the site.  Guild bank is coming along but it is far harder than I had anticipated.  However, as with anything that is worth while, I am sure that the extra hard work and effort will pay off in the end.

We are glad to see new faces amongst those we already know and love.  Make yourself known!  Read the forum and chirp up in guild chat!!

Recently "Ruccus' Rumblers"  gathered together and stomped some Shadowmen butt!  Sounded like all had a good time...shouts of Carpe 50 Shadowmen 0 brought woots and hurrahs from all those online.  It was great to see our younger crowd come together and show off their colors!

The older crowd got together this past week and made mincemeat out of those Frogloks in Lower Guk.  Both Aamon and Melo looted Froglok crowns, while Len and Gaye received Mithril Swords.  It was a blast!  AND!!!! while we were waiting and fighting, Melo taught a few languages to those who had not been able to make the Language Fair.

Edgie, our beloved yet quiet Cleric, dinged 56 as has been his life long dream.  =)

And finally, in a spur of the moment groupage in CoM, Kilorn acquired several parts that he needed for his epic piece.  Luck was on our side that day!!
