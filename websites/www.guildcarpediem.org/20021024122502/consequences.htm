Consequences

First infraction:

1. You will be given a verbal warning via a private tell stating how you have violated the rules.
2. The incident will be recorded in the officer's section of the forum entitled Time Out.
3. An email will be sent to you with a listing of all the rules as they appear on the site. You are to respond to that email letting us know that you have indeed re-read the rules.

Second infraction: (Within a 30 day period from the first infraction)

1. You will be given a verbal warning via a private tell stating how you have violated the rules.
2. The incident will be recorded in the officer's section of the forum entitled Time Out.
3. You will be contacted by either Melo or Supa in game and via email to discuss why you are continuing to break guild policy.
4. You will be placed on probation.

Third infraction: (Within a 30 day period from the first infraction)

1. You will be given a verbal warning via a private tell stating how you have violated the rules.
2. The incident will be recorded in the officer's section of the forum entitled Time Out.
3. You will be contacted by either Melo or Supa in game and via email to discuss why you are continuing to break guild policy.
4. You will be disbanded from the guild.

Notes:

If you have a second infraction that is more than 30 days apart from your first infraction, you will be treated as if it is your first infraction. However, if a pattern develops, or the infractions are severe enough, appropriate actions will be taken.
