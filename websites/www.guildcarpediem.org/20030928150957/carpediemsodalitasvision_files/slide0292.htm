Quick-Death Guilds
 
Group of people say, “Let’s Create our Own Guild!”
These people do not agree on focus of guild, or do not make decisions on what the guild will do.  But the guild is created anyway.
Frequent bickering and arguments within the guild leads to poor use of time, and lack of success.
Guild members who do not want to engage in the bickering (low end of social interaction) or people that are tired of the guild’s lack of success (low on the end-game progress) will abandon the guild in search for another.
Because the guild lacks any kind of foundation, the people that leave start a chain reaction - the guild crumbles and dies quickly.  1-6 month life span.
Difficulty to attain this position:
Easy to create … Easy to lose
