Membership process:

For those visiting the site and may be interested in joining Carpe, let me state the process that is involved:

  • View the site.  Look around.  Visit each page.  Read our charter, vision and guild policy pages.

  • Ask to be granted access to the forum.  Once approved, view the forum.  Read as many posts as you can read.

    • If you do not have a global ID created, please do the following:

      To submit an application to this community, you must be logged in to your Global User Account. If you do not have a Global User Account, click here

      Message from the Administrator of Carpe Diem Sodalitas
      If you are seeking access, please be sure that your user name is recognizable as being EQ related. It is also advisable that you enable the option that allows for you to receive email from the admins of this form (Supa and Melo). It is not enough to have a name that "sounds" EQ-ish. If your name is not recognized by or we cannot trace you directly to EQ, then access will not be given until we have been able to contact you via email. If you have additional questions or want to reach the admins, please email melodious@linkline.com Security is for our members and their right to privacy.

  • Group with members.  When you log on, do a /who all Carpe and see who is available to group with you.  Explain that you are interested in getting to know the guild and it's members better and ask that they relay in Guild Chat your request.

  • Submit an application.  Do not be "cutesy" - humor is appreciated but answers like "Just Because." are not.  The questions on the application are there for a reason.  Answer them all as best and completely as you can.  And while I understand that not everyone is a great typist, please avoid excessive "d00d" speak - it gives me a headache after a while.

  • Wait for an email response from either Melo or Supa.  This means that the email address that you list on the application needs to be one that you actually check.  The email from Melo or Supa will contain information about setting up an in-game interview or conducting it via email.

  • DO NOT HARASS OFFICERS ABOUT YOUR APPLICATION.  They do not have any control over the process.

  • Supa and Melo will give final ok to tag potential member - any officer can then tag him/her.

  • Once tagged, the tagging officer will post in the officer forum that he was tagged.

  • The Good Will Ambassador, will contact new member and be sure that they register for the forum, register on the roster, and go over any additional questions he or she may have.

  • 3 days later, if member has not registered on the roster and forum, he will be disbanded. This may seem harsh, but we do ask and expect a certain amount of dedication - and to not be able to follow these simple rules indicates to us all that you are not truly dedicated.
