Carpe Diem Membership Application

2.    What is your character's class?        

3.    What is your character's current level?

8.    How did you hear about this guild? (If referred by someone, please state their character name.)

9.   Having read the charter, what part of it do you feel appeals most strongly to your individual needs? 

10.  Please understand that we are NOT a "raiding" guild nor are we all about loot and power-leveling.  

11.  What is your real life birthday?
