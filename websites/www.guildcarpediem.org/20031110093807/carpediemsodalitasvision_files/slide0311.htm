•The "theme" of Carpe is one that you will find repeated throughout life.
•
•You will get out of Carpe as much (or as little) as you put into it.
•This means:
•    -if you want events - attend the ones that are planned and help plan the ones you want.
    -if you want people to help you obtain items then you need to help others obtain items too.
    -if you want people to speak to you in guild chat or tells then you have to return the kindness.
    -if you want to be seen and heard and acknowledged be sure that you are seeing and hearing
      and acknowledging those around you.
    -if you want respect for your abilities you need to be sure that you respect others for theirs.
•And if you feel that you ARE doing all of these things and yet you are still unhappy, then you need to seek out an officer or a guild leader or someone that you feel comfortable with and you need to ask them what it is that you can do to create the atmosphere within YOUR family that you want. For that really IS the duty of each member - to help foster the sort of atmosphere that they are happy in. And the reason we are all in the same guild is because our idea of what sort of atmosphere that is is very much the same.
•Carpe is now and always will be what it's members make of it.
