This section is to better list the rules by which all members of Carpe are expected to abide.  Most of these guidelines are common sense and you might read them and say, "Well, duh!"  But what may be obvious to some is sometimes still obscure to others, and the best way we can avoid misunderstandings is to clearly state as many of the guidelines as possible.

One thing that we never wanted to do was create a guild that was bogged down with rules.  Most of our rules have evolved over time out of a need to clearly state what had beforehand been assumed as common knowledge.  We also found that having some guidelines did help with younger players who may not have experienced "guild life" before joining Carpe.

Many of the topics we have listed are not only courtesies for Carpe, but guidelines that should be applied by most of us participating in Norrath - be it in a guild or on our own.  Most of the guidelines boil down to RESPECT... not it's not just a song by Aretha Franklin.  Respect should not be something that is only shown to others when you are dressed in your "Sunday Best."

Having rules or guidelines are in no way another method to control you or your playing atmosphere.  Unfortunately, and especially if you are in a guild, your play style can and does affect other people - even if you do not mean for it to do so.  In Carpe, we try very hard to create an atmosphere where members feel at ease to be who they are without disrespecting the rights of others.  It's not as easy as it sounds... but we have found that those who truly do get what this guild is about can be themselves while still respecting their fellow guildmate.
