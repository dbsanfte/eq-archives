Locations are very important in Norrath - as is sense heading (hint hint).  I stumbled across a listing of most locations and felt it might be helpful to everyone.

To the right are the pages listing the locations.  To make printing easier, I have listed blow links to WORD documents containing the same information.  When you select the hyperlinks below, a box may pop up that asks you to enter your name and password - please click CANCEL on that box.  I have no idea why it does that, but if you select cancel then the file will come on up and you can print it easily.

We do suggest that all members print out a copy of these locations.  It just makes traveling much easier.
