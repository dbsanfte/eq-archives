The guild bank is for the benefit of all Carpe members.  If there are items within the bank which you feel would benefit you, please post to the forum.  Aamon is the guild banker and will do everything within his power to get the item to those who are in need.

Please remember that all items donated to the guild bank are property of Carpe Diem and will be remain guild property even if loaned out.  The bank works off of the honor system.  If you ask to be loaned an item, it is expected that when you no longer need the item, you will return it to the bank so that it may benefit another member in need.
