Carpe Diem is happy and proud to offer Live Chat to it's members.  We encourage you to utilize this feature especially when the servers are down (or you happen to be at work and miss your guild buddies).  

When logging into the chat room, please use your character's name so that we know who we are talking with at the time.  Also, for the profile, list your level, race, and class.

Chat room rules are mostly common sense.

  • We do NOT tolerate harassment of any kind regardless of the way it may appear.

  • We ask that you keep the language as clean as possible.

  • Please remember that "spam" is not a nice thing.

  • While this is a private chat room, it is possible for people to stumble across it by simply going to the website, so please remember that your actions are a reflection of the guild - even outside of the game.

So, if what are you waiting for?  Go chat!!
