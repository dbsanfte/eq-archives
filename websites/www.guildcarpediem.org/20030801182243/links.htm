Carpe Diem Sodalitas is proud to be accepting of all classes - all races.  We encourage diversity in all areas of life.  Each individual has worth - and as a member of Carpe Diem, each of us has a duty to look beyond a person's ties and ancestry and see the person that they truly are.  We are a strong guild made up of unique classes and individuals.  While we may not have many of the more "important classes", we have found that by working together and appreciating the talents we each have, we have been able to form a strong, determined, and powerful team like no other out there.   

Links to helpful sites, locations, and class information are always welcomed.
