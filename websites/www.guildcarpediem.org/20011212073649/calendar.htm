The calendar is for Carpe Diem members only.  Anyone may view it, however, only registered members may post an event to the calendar.  This calendar is not only for raids or organized camps... we encourage you to post any event that you want us to be aware of:  character birthdays, real life birthdays, weddings, planned vacations or time away from the game, etc.

Click on the link provided and a new window will pop up taking you to the calendar.  If you are a member of Carpe Diem and have registered on the forum, then the user password will be mailed to you.  If you do not receive this password, please email Melodious.  Registered users may only edit the events that they post.
