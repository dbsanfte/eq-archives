For those visiting the site and may be interested in joining Carpe, let me state the process that is involved:

  • View the site.  Look around.  Visit each page.  Read our charter and guild policy pages.

  • Ask to be granted access to the forum.  To do this, click on the forum button and follow the directions.  I believe it will ask you to log in or register an account and then ask to view.  Once approved, view the forum.  Read as many posts as you can read.

  • Submit an application.  Do not be "cutesy" - humor is appreciated but answers like "Because." are not.  The questions on the application are there for a reason.  Answer them all as best and completely as you can.  And while I understand that not everyone is a great typist, please avoid excessive "d00d" speak - it gives me a headache after a while.

  • Wait for an email response from either Melo or Supa.  This means that the email address that you list on the application needs to be one that you actually check.  The email from Melo or Supa will contain information about setting up an in-game interview or conducting it via email.

  • DO NOT HARASS OFFICERS ABOUT YOUR APPLICATION.  They do not have any control over the process.

  • Supa and Melo will give final ok to tag potential member - any officer can then tag him/her.

  • Once tagged, the tagging officer will post in the officer forum that he was tagged.

  • Majix, as Good Will Ambassador, will contact new member and be sure that they register for the forum, register on the roster, and are aware of the guild bank policy.

  • 3 days later, if member has not registered on the roster and forum, he will be disbanded. This may seem harsh, but we do ask and expect a certain amount of dedication - and to not be able to follow these simple rules indicates to us all that you are not truly dedicated.
