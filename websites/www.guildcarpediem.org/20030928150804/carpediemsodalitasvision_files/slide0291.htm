Family Guilds
 
Strengths:
Friendly and playful guild chat
Flexibility of things to do
Welcoming of lower levels
Weaknesses:
Poor Raiding Capabilities
Less “Uber” Loot
Do not retain high level players
Frustration with guild progress
Leadership Structure
Frequently, officers and leader as equals
 
Sometimes, distinct leader with officers
who have “less power” than the leader.
Officer Responsibilities
“Welcome to the family!!!”
Keepers of the peace
Make people feel welcome
Recruiting
Spokesmen - advocates for the guild
Member Responsibilities
What responsibilities?
Come have fun!
Treat everyone with respect
