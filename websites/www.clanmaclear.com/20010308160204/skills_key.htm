EQSS Skills Legend
by Tormod
of the Clan MacLear

Note: as the game progresses towards final release, skills will change.  Please report any discrepancies between this list and how the skill works in Norrath to Tormod.

Abjuration
A skill only available to users of magic.  It is the ability to cast spells of a defensive nature (ie. the druids shield of thorns).

Alcohol Tolerance
The ability to metabolize the distorting, disorienting effects of alcohol.  Very useful for a warrior as alcohol has beneficial effects such as temporarily raising stamina and strength.

Alteration
Another magic user skill.  This is the ability to alter reality.  A good example are the teleportation, gate, and other types of ability spells (run faster, see better, etc).

Apply Poison
A skill specific to the thief.  If successful, the targeted weapon will have a chance to poison.

Archery
The ability to use the bow.  This includes the hunting bow, the short bow, and the long bow.  Crossbows are not currently available as weapons.

Baking
As food is a vital part of Norrath, baking can be quite useful.

Backstabbing
Allows the rogue class a chance to do massive amounts of damage in one blow.  The rogue must be positioned behind his mark. 

Bash
Meant to be used with a shield (although it can be used without one), this skill allows one a chance to temporarily stun an opponent as well as a chance to do a small amount of damage.

Beg
Ability to beg coppers from NPCs.  This is not a trainable skill.  It only improves through practice.

Bind Wound
Can be used once a day, on one's self or another.  It allows the user a chance to begin to heal major wounds (health below 20%).  If not done properly this skill can actually cause more damage to the user.

Brass
A bard skill.  Some bard songs are only castable using brass instruments.

Brewing
The fine art of making a brewski.  Your friends will love you if you bring them free beer.  This game just gets more and more realistic!

Block
A special monk skill.  Allows the user a chance to deflect an opponents blow.

Blunt, Slashing, & Piercing Weapons
Ability in these skills in essential to melee combat.  The only remaining melee skill is piercing, which is usually preferred by the rogue class due to its backstab potential.

Channeling
Allows the magic user with ability in this skill to cast a spell faster no matter the spells type.

Common Tongue
Ability to speak and understand the Common Tongue.

Conjuration
A magic-user skill.  Allows the user to call upon the arcane forces to summon something into being.  For example, a magical pet, food, water, etc.

Dark Speech
Ability to speak and understand the special dark tongue of the dark elf, troll, and ogre kind.

Defense
The general ability to save one's own skin in melee combat.

Disarm
A very useful skill that actually causes the target to drop its weapon.  This is often the death knell for your opponent, especially if you pick up the weapon before he or she does.

Disarm Traps
Allows the rogue to make the rest of his party very happy.

Divination
The magic-user skill of making things visible or invisible.

Dodge
Simply put, a chance to get out of the way of your opponents weapon.  A very useful skill for all classes, but extremely useful for magic users who do not like being interrupted mid-spell.

Double Attack
Allows the user a chance to double their weapons attack speed thereby attacking twice in the time it would have taken them to attack once.

Dragon Punch
Special Monk attack.

Dual Wield
Allows the user the ability to wield two weapons and a chance to attack with the second weapon.

Eagle Strike
Special Monk attack.

Elder Elvish
Ability to speak and understand Elder Elvish.

Evocation
A magic-user spell.  This is the skill that allows the user to cast "nukes" or the purely offensive spells like lightening bolt, shock of frost, etc.

Feign Death
A special monk skill.  Allows the user a chance to convince NPCs that you are dead and to attack someone else.  Also a good party trick.

Fishing
Requires a fishing pole, bait, and a body of water.  Yeilds fish, and sometimes sandals or a rusty dagger.  I have found that, up to a certain point, the more times you cast the better the chance of catching a fish.

Fletching
Creates arrows and requires a store bought kit.

Flying Kick
Special Monk attack.

Foraging
A very useful skill of rangers and druids.  Allows them a chance to scrounge up some food or water from the local surroundings.

Hand-to-Hand
Every class starts out with this skill.  You should practice at least a little with it as it is what you are left with after you die and lose your equipment (until you find your corpse).

Hide
Allows the user a chance to blend in with the local flora.  The user does not know if the skill has been successful or not.  The only way to know that you have hidden successfully is by the reaction of others to you jumping out and saying BOO!!

Instill Doubt
A peculiar ability of the rogue, thought to create a sense of impending doom in those that they are in combat with.  Often causes the target to turn its back and flee...giving the rogue a chance to use the backstab skill.  Very nasty combination.

Jewel Craft
A good trade skill for those that want to make money through means other than mass slaughter.

Kick
Available to fighter classes.  Allows the user a chance to due some extra damage.   Also increases your chances of being selected as a Dallas Cowboys Cheerleader.

Make Poison
A skill unique to the rogue.  This is self explanatory.

Meditate
Magic users should get used to using this skill.  Don't waste training points on it at the trainer.  You will use it so much that it will go up on its own.  Allows faster regeneration of mana.

Mend Wounds
A monk ability to heal him or herself.

Offense
The ability to hurt others.

Old Erudian
Ability to speak and understand Old Erudian.

One-hand Blunt
Ability to use a blunt object that only requires one hand to wield.

One-hand slashing
Ability to hit others with one handed blades, such as swords and axes.

Parry
Ability to deflect blows with ones weapon.  Makes a cool sound in-game.

Percussion
The ability of the bard to use the drums.  Some bard songs can only be played on the drums.

Pick Lock
Rogues get this skill and are sometimes the only way to get into certain areas of Norrath.

Pick Pocket
PvP+ may attempt to pick the pocket of NPCs or PvP+ PCs.  PvP- is limited to NPCs.

Piercing
Ability to use piercing weapons, such as spears and daggers. A favorite skill of rogues.

Pottery
The ability to make fine clay bowls.

Research
I don't know exactly what this skill does.  It is only available to certain classes of magic users. 

Riposte
Special attack.

Round Kick
Special Monk attack.

Safe Fall
The ability of monks and thieves to fall from heights and reduce the damage inflicted upon themselves.

Sense Heading
Very useful for one who does not want to get lost.  I recommend practicing this skill after each level until it maxes out.

Sense Traps
An ability of the rogue to detect traps on objects or passageways.

Singing
An ability of bards to cast bard spells that require singing.

Slam
An ability peculiar to the the larger races (no matter the class) resulting in extra damage to the foe.

Smithing
The ability to makes ore into a useful item.  These items are of better weight and utility than those sold in stores.

Sneak
Ability to sneak up on other people unnoticed.

Specialization
When a magic-user attains a high enough level (usually quite high) this skill becomes available.  It allows the user to "specialize" in the magic wielding skill of his or her choice.  The added talent in the selected area allows him/her to cast faster and with less mana usage.

Stringed Instruments
The ability of the bard to use a stringed instrument.  Some songs are only playable with this skill and instrument.

Swimming
Practice this skill.  Often it is under-practiced.  I guarantee it will save your life at some point.  Many areas in Norrath are only reachable by swimming, sometimes, long distances.  This skill increases the amount of time you can swim without tiring and how long you can stay underwater.

Tailoring
Making your own armor can produce better and cheaper armor than can be found in the stores of Norrath. Requires a pelt of some kind (depending on size and type of armor), a pattern, and a sewing kit.

Taunt
Allows the user a chance to enrage the target to such a degree that it considers the user enemy number one and will try to kill you before killing everyone else  This makes everyone else happy.  This is a good way to save your buddies life when he is down to a sliver of health.

Throwing
Ability to use throwing weapons.  Common weapons are the dagger, throwing axe, spear, and shuriken.

Tiger Claw
Special Monk attack.

Thieves Cant
Secret thieves language performed by use of body language.  Also called "the bird."

Track
Ability of the user to sense the pressence of others in the area.

Two-hand Blunt
Ability to use two-handed blunt weapons, such as hammers and great staffs.

Two-hand Slashing
Ability to use two-handed slashing weapons, including two-handed swords and axes.

Wind Instruments
The horn, pipe and other instruments are required for certain bardic song spells.
