12-19-01 - Donation Button Added Torveig

    There is now a PayPal donate button on the left [ed.] side of this page about halfway down the page.  My PayPal identity is "Mac Inc."   The clicking the donate button will link you to the PayPal Secure Server and will allow you to set up a monthly donation of $5.  If you ever want to stop donating, just go to PayPal and cancel your subscription to the monthly donation billing. 

    I want to note that donating is completely optional.  I fully expect that we'll actually have a rush of folks who want to help out.  Don't feel bad if you didn't get to contribute.   If you would like to be put on the waiting list to contribute in case someone stops their monthly donations send me an email at joshua@clanmaclear.com and I will add you to the waiting list.  If we have a large number of folks on the waiting list then we can simply reduce the amount each person is donating so that more people can feel like they are helping to carry the Clan financially.

    The Clan's hosting costs average around $40 per month.  I am going to contribute my share of $5 per month, so we have $35 left to raise.  I'll make a listing that shows how much is being donated each month and those who have contributed to the cause.  ;-)  After you donate send me an email at joshua@clanmaclear.com to let me know what Clan member name you want to have recognized for the contribution.
