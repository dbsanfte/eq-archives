    "Ah! I see ye dinna learn th' Gaidhlig. What I said was, 'Greetings Stranger! Good health tae ye!' 'Tis a shame indeed ye dinna learn th' Gael when ye were young, fer 'tis a wonderous fine language und ye should learn a wee bit when ye hae th' chance!"

    "Aey presume ye hae th' writin' in Anglish, dae ye nae?... Gud, than ye can scribe a wee bit o'this und tha' fer aer scribes ta read before we let ye in. 'Tis a fearsome thing this writin'.
