Welcome to the Clan MacLear Gladitorial Games

Greetings traveler, if you have found this place then you have been invited to participate in the Clan MacLear Gladitorial Games. The games will be held in the arena in the bazaar of Shadow Haven on the 11th of October beginning at 2:00 PM EST. Adventurers of all seasons and ranks will be welcome to participate. Should you wish not to participate you may come and cheer for the gladiators as they battle to the death with each other. So, if you wish to sign up then please continue on to the registration desk. Otherwise we hope to see all of you amongst the crowd!
