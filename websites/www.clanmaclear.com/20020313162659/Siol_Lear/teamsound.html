Ronod's Team Sound Tutorial

    Ronod's Team Sound has swept the Siol Lear like a tital wave.  

Attached are several images which form a short tutorial for setting up TeamSound and configuring Network  settings if you are using a router (Linksys in this example).  If, after following these  recommendations, you are still experiencing difficulties, please email me the following information and I will try to help you.
 
1) Processor type and speed (example P3 850MHz)
2) Operating system (example Win 98SE or Mac OS 9)
3) Are you on a Local Area Network (yes - no)
4) Internet connect type (Cable modem, 56K dialup, DSL or ADSL)
5) Sound card or sound chip type - is it full duplex or half duplex?
