The Quest of Stone: (updated 14 April 99)
This is the only commonly known Quest. All others are known only to The High Council, Keeper of Quests and to the participant. To leave the lowlands of Lads and Lasses and enter the Highlands of the Kinsman or Kinswoman, you must complete the Quest of Stone. To complete the Quest, you must travel far from your homeland of ice and snow to find four circles of stone. There you must capture power of the place in your soul. To symbolize this internalization, you must take with you from that place an item that can only be got from that region. - You must enter the Ring of Toxxulia on Odus and bring back a Kobold Hide from the forest. - Then you must enter the Ring of Misty Thicket and obtain a Giant Wasp Wing that can only be found there. - Next you seek out the Ring of Butcherblock where you will collect a Scarab Carapace. - Finally you visit the Ring of Greater Faydark and bring back a Jar of Royal Jelly as proof. Good luck and safe travels, Future Kinsman.
