This section is dedicated to the wacky and dynamic world of Everquest, which is a game that is just so full of constant excitement and wonder that many people appear to be quitting fulltime jobs to camp MOBs. A lot of the people playing the game seem take it way too seriously and forget that it still is just a game, a game that's supposed to be "fun", not "work". A Newbie's Guide to Everquest - 5.22.00
Getting started playing Everquest is quite a difficult task.
