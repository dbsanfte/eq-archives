What is TweakEQ™?

TweakEQ™ is a commerical quality 3rd party utility, that places an intuitive, easy-to-use graphical interface between you, and several configuration files, allowing straight forward customization of basic to advanced level features, with unprecedented control.

Features

Extremely user friendly, with extended features for advanced level editing.

Options expand automatically or interactively when new game options are added or even removed.

Real-time visual feedback, see your settings exactly as they would appear in-game.

Convenient Drag & Drop support.

Highly optimized and completely 32bit API and DirectX™ driven, no need for several unnecessary OCX's and DLL files.

Safe and reliable, tested thoroughly on a large variety of systems by casual computer users and programmers alike, dozens of integrity checks are performed before saving data, and the author has a professional background in producing high-quality games for Microsoft® Corporation.

Carefully examines your existing data, and corrects errors possibly caused by other programs.

Dozens of ready-to-use custom made styles for Chat Text Colors, Interface Colors, Keyboard Programming, Game Configuration Settings and Game Interface Layout.

Dozens of carefully selected background textures, optionally use your in-game screenshots, or images anywhere on your computer.

4 Palettes to choose from: Standard, Large Color Ramp, Triangular Ramp, and Current Background.

A customize palette tool, where you can store your favorite colors, or view a history of colors you've selected.

Manage your notes.txt, and friends/ignore list for all your characters on any server with ease.

View hardware settings, memory and hard drive usage, statistics and more.

Eliminate Legacy files no longer used by the game, view game resource usage statistics, or clear your texture cache.

Network/LAN support, TweakEQ™ can interactively change settings remotely, so it only needs to be installed on 1 computer.

Unlimited Undo/Redo, with Use Defaults, Revert to Original, and Restore to Saved.

Save and Share your settings with friends using TweakEQ™, or generate a webpage to show your settings.

The Layout Editor allows you to interact with the interface settings, as they would appear in-game.

Updates on-demand, to keep TweakEQ™ compatible.

Technical Support directly from the Author, and several Technical Support Representatives.

Configuration options to allow you to tell TweakEQ™ exactly how to operate (eg. whether or not to prompt before saving).

Automatically prevents you from choosing options which could have a negative impact on the game, such as choosing a text color which is assigned as transparent index by the game.

Carefully examines your system, making sure you cannot use or change things not supported by your computer hardware, such as resolutions your videocard and or monitor are not capable of.

The ability to change TweakEQ™ to support other languages.

Comes Virus and Trojan Free Guaranteed, with thorough Scan Result Reports included.

Note: This is an incomplete listing of features, I will attempt to update this listing as time permits.

System Requirements

-Windows™ 98(se)/NT/Me/2000/XP(Home/Pro)
-Microsoft® DirectX™ 8.1. (3D acceleration not required)
-AMD™/Pentium™ 233mhz or Faster.
-8mb Ram.
-4mb Hard Drive Space.
-EverQuest™, or any of it's expansions are not required, but highly recommended.

What's not supported?

-Windows™ in Large Fonts Mode.
-Windows™ 95 and earlier versions (though it should still run).
