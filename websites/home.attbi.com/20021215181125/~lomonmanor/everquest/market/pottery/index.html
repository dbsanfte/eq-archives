Pottery

The building feels scorching compared to the relatively cool outdoors. The heat from a kiln fills the entraceway, but quickly diminishes as you proceed further into the room. A pottery wheel fills the corner, near shelves of clay, and barrels of water. A familiar looking half-elf stops bustling about a shelf of scrolls and turns to you, with a sketch in each hand.

"Welcome to the pottery shop of Turlo Lomon. How can I help you?"
