This is a 1/24 scale kit of a GTA. It comes molded in high gloss black (according to the box) and includes plated parts, vinyl Goodyear tires, and simulated alloy wheels. Copyright date on the kit is 1987. No further information is available at this time...
  Factory sealed  $30     Model Store MPC Kit# 6209



This is a 1/25 scale kit of a GTA. It can be built one of two ways, stock or custom. I have bought and built one of these kits and it was a really good kit. It featured some good detail for a 1/25 scale kit.
Revell Kit# 7470

1989

AMT/ERTL Kit# 6269




This is a 1/25 scale kit of the 20th Anniversary Trans Am. On the boxtop its called a Firebird GTA but its obviously modeled after the anniversary TA. A site visitor pointed out to me that this particular kit is shown built with the Knotchback rear decklid versus the standard one. It can also be seen on the lower side shot. This was an option offered on the real thing by Pontiac but wasn't a very popular one. If you want to build an '89 GTA with a knothcbaclk decklid this is the kit for you. I have not been able to determine if the kit also comes with a standard decklid. Anyone know??? No further information is available at this time...

  Factory sealed  $35     Model Store AMT/ERTL Kit# 6932


This is another 1/25 scale kit of the 20th Anniversary Trans Am. Like the kit above it is misidentified as a Turbo Firebird GTA. The 20th was a turbo but it was not a GTA. It was a Trans Am. This is probably the nicest kit modeled after the 20th, you get extra Indianapolis Pace Cars decals not included in any other kit.
Monogram Kit# 2905




This is the only kit on the site so far that you'll see me recommend you not buy. I purchased one of these kits several years ago only to find out halfway through the build that it was impossible to completely build the kit. I can't remember what the problem was specifically but I believe it was a defect in the mold. Either way it was impossible to complete the kit and I threw it away. It was frustrating because you are about 50% finished with it when you realize the defect.
Update: I have had at least one report that this kit was re-released and that it built-up just fine.
Monogram Kit# 2905





This is the same kit as above with a slightly different boxtop. Its possible this was a later release to fix the problem I experienced with the earlier kit. If anyone can verify this please send me an e-mail.
