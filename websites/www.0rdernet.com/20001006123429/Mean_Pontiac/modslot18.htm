1998

  Factory Sealed  $18     Model Store Revell Kit# 85-2539




This is a brand new kit recently released by Revell/Monogram. Its a 1/25 scale kit of the 1998 Ram Air Trans Am. The first interesting thing about this kit is that even though the Ram Air option was re-introduced in 1995 only one other kit besides this one was produced with a Ram Air hood. The only kit to get the first RA hood was the Lindberg Firehawk kit shown above. This is the second kit to offer a RA hood and is of course the new 1998 hood. Although the finished kit depicted on the box is purple the parts come molded in white. The box is dated 1998.
