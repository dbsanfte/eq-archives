Revell Kit# 7215
click ->




click -> 		This is a 1/25 scale Revell kit of a 1982 Trans Am (box identifies it as a Firebird). Its molded in black and is dated 1982. The body in the kit is designed a little differently than most. There is no T-Roof where there should be one but an open space between the windshield and the body. The window glass is all one piece and encompasses the front and rear windshields and the glass T-Tops. The model also features a nice compliment of chrome pieces for the engine and rims. The decal set is only a few bird decals of different sizes. This is consistent with this model Firebird where decals were few.
