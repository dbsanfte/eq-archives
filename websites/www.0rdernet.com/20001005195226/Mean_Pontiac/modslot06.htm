Monogram Kit# 1606




This is a very interesting kit. Monogram did a series of these kits entitled Luminators. The entire kit was mad of a translucent material and through the use of some lighting was supposed to glow when finished. The kit calls this TA a "Neon Racer". The kit is a snap-tite and was also supposed to glow in the dark with a black light. No further information is available at this time...

Monogram Kit# 2716



The first kit in the WAR series of TA kits is a Monogram brand 1/24 scale 3'n1 kit.  The kit can be built in a Street Machine, a Drag Race or Stock version.  The kit is dated 1985 and is molded in black. Kit includes two sets of tires, stock and big and littles (bigs being slicks). There are three sets of wheels, 2 being Center Line type (wide and skinny) and a set of WS6 type snowflake wheels (all wheels are chrome). The T-tops  have already been cut from the roof at the factory and includes tinted glass for the openings as well as for the front and rear windows.  Also included are tinted head lamp covers. The rear spoiler and wheel caps are pre-molded to the body. Decals include pretty much what is shown on the box cover.
