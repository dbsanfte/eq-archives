MPC Kit# 1-0808




This is an MPC kit of an 82' Firebird. It comes molded in black and includes such optional parts as side pipes, roll cage, optional custom body panesl and racing wheels. The tires are BF Goodrich. In the engine compartment you can op to install a TBI system or a more aggressive looking dual turbo setup. This kit is dated 1982. No further information is available at this time...
MPC Kit# 1-0675






This is an MPC kit of the customized 82' Trans Am used in the TV series "Knightrider." Other than the 2000 Knightrider model released a few years ago by AMT/ERTL that was a kit of the newly designed Knightrider car this is the only kit modeled after this veyr popular car. Its molded in black. Chrome includes the stock rims (as seen on boxtop) and a set of custom ones that look like the interlaced wheels that would be released in the late 80s.   You also get chrome valve covers, side pipes (not seen on the knightrider car), carburetor, and air cleaner. The chrome pieces are pacakged seperate from the rest of the kit in a small cello bag. The front and rear widnsheild and the T-Tops  are dark to simulate tinted windows. Tires included are BFG Radial TAs. A tubocharger and a flat hood are included along with the side pipes and custom wheels to customize the kit if you'd like. None of these features appeared on the real car. The body is molded and designed to resemble K.I.T.T. and the interior is also designed like the car from the TV show. The boxtop contains several pictures of the real car in action from the show. This kit is dated 1982.

Revell Kit# 7215


This is a 1/25 scale Revell kit of a 1982 Trans Am (box identifies it as a Firebird). Its molded in black and is dated 1982. The body in the kit is designed a little differently than most. There is no T-Roof where there should be one but an open space between the windshield and the body. The window glass is all one piece and encompasses the front and rear windshields and the glass T-Tops. The model also features a nice compliment of chrome pieces for the engine and rims. The decal set is only a few bird decals of different sizes. This is consistent with this model Firebird where decals were few.
