click -> 		Jeff Patton owns this awesome black Firebird 428 Coupe. Thats right it has a 428 in it built with a crane cam and roller rockers. Jeff also decided to add headers, a Holly 750 Double Pumper, an MSD 6AL, a Blaster coil, and an HEI distributor. The headers vent the engine exhaust through a 3" exhaust system complete with dual Flowmaster mufflers and a cross-over tube. The shifting duties are handled by a B&M Shfiter connected to a Turbo 400 transmission. Centerlines are used to put rubber to the pavement. With a set of 4.56 gears in the rear the car is definitely not lacking for power.
