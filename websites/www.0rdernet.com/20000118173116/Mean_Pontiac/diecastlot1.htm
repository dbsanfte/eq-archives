click -> 		These are 1:18 scale diecast 73' SD455 Trans Ams. They are produced by ERTL and have been released in several colors. The doors open and allow a view of an intricately detailed interior featuring a chrome instrument panel that actually has the correct machine turned pattern. A 4-speed shifter, detailed bucket and rear seats which are also the correct patterns, and door panels are other highlights of the interior. The hood can be raised and lowered to reveal the 455 which is molded in the correct silver blue color. The engine compartment is fairly well detailed with an alternator, pulley-belt system, the correct shaker/air cleaner setup complete with the air cleaner duct for extracting air through the front end, a master cylinder and even the correct heater box that was installed when the A/C option was deleted.
          The wheels feature the correct Honeycomb rims and move back and forth when the steering wheel is turned. The tires are real rubber and feature a detailed tread pattern and the wording "Firestone" "Wide 60 Oval". The decals are all the correct style and color and in the correct locations. The rear end is beautiful and well detailed. The correct style tailpipes protrude out the back. The underside of the car was not neglected either. The front and rear suspension is detailed and correct with sway bars and other suspension components. The exhaust is also correct and complete. Pipes exit out of headers and travel the length of the chassis to meet at the rear of the car on either sides of a single muffler. The tranny is however incorrect. Its well detailed and painted a silver color to simulate a real tranny but the transmission pan is the correct "Texas" shape for a Turbo 400 automatic transmission which doesn't match the 4-speed shifter located in the interior. A driveshaft is connected between the transmission and the rear end. These are excellent diecast models of this legendary Trans Am.
          Pictured are the white version, the red version, and the black & gold version. The red and white cars are available anywhere. The black and gold one is a special offering that can only be bought through Peach State Collectibles.

I can acquire these cars for sale on occasion. E-mail me for details.
