Funny Cars and Other Misc Kits

AMT/ERTL Kit# 1042


This is a 1/25 scale kit snap-tite kit. It features a '92 Pro Street Firebird. This is a new kit having been released this year. Its called a snapfast plus because it comes bundled with paint, glue and a paint brush. No further information is available at this time...
AMT/ERTL Kit# 8084


A few years ago Knightrider was revived for a short time and with a new car. This is a kit of that car. Its 1/25 scale and called the Knight Rider 2000 KITT 4000. Features directional chrome wheels, Goodyear Gatorback tires, and the complete KITT interior. Copyright date is 1991.
