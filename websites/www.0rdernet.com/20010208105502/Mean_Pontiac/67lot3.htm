click -> 		This beautiful red Firebird belongs to Virgil Bowles of Arlington, Texas. Virgil is in the process of "fixing-up" this one. The bird was originally a Sprint 6 car but had a chevy engine residing in the engine compartment when he bought it  so the first order of business will be to install a 428 he's currently building for it. In his own words Virgil says, "I bought it and saved its life from shame." The block has been bored 0.30 over and a new 068 cam was installed. The heads are #62s and are topped with 1.65 roller rockers. New ARP bolts have also been installed throughout the engine.
     On the inside a 4-speed tranny provides for exciting shifts. Virgil also decided to install a 160mph speedo and a tach where the fuel gauge used to be. He got the idea for the change from an article in Hot Rod magazine. The rest of the interior is to remain stock.
     Other cosmetic changes planned include the installation of a 400 hood decorated with 428 emblems and a paint job resembling the Mertel Motors cars built in 1967.
     Virgil is looking for any pics of the Mertel Motors cars to help him with his paint job and I wouldn't mind having some for display on this site. If anyone can help with these pics please take the time to send me an e-mail.

This beautiful Plum Mist Ram Air I Firebird belongs to Jim Mattison of Pontiac Historic Services. Only 65 "Ram Air" Firebirds were built in 1967, 45 manuals and 20 automatics. This car is one of the ultra rare 20 automatics. Factory equipment installed besides the Ram Air engine include:

"Plum Mist" color
 Deluxe Parchment Interior
 Fold Down Rear Seat
 Wood Wheel
 Hood Mounted Tachometer
 Full Gauges
 Power Disc Brakes
 Rally II Wheels
 Redline Tires
 3.90:1 Safe-T-Track Rear Axle

According to Jim there are only three of these cars known to exist and this is the only one that is complete which further increases the rarity of this car. Jim is the owner of Pontiac Historic Services. For $35 you can send PHS the Vin# from your Pontiac and you'll get a package of information on your car including a copy of the original build sheet.
