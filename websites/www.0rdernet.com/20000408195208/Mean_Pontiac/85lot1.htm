click -> 		This cool Trans Am belongs to Dave Barkley. A 305 Tuned Port Injection engine resides under the hood, which is mated to a 3+1 automatic transmission. Body modifications Dave has performed include the addition of an 89' GTA Aerowing, tinted windows, and charcoal GTA wheels equipped with Goodyear GSC tires. Suspension mods include Chassis Engineering sub-frame connectors, Bilstein rear shokcs, and Energy Suspension Urethane bushings installed throughout the rear end. The drivetrain was also upgraded with the following additions: an air filter canister and tubing from an 88' TA, a K&N air filter, Summit air-foil, Hypertech thermo-chip, a 160 degree thermostat, SLP cat-back stainless exhaust system, B&M shift improver kit (stage 2 configured) and a posi rear end with disc brakes from an 89' Camaro RS. Finally the car received a complete repaint in solid blue. Dave took the liberty to remove all markings during the process.
