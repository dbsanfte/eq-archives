MPC Kit# 6332

click -> This model is a MPC brand 1/25 scale kit. The kit is not dated.  The kit is molded in black. The kit is designed to be built stock and even though the box cover states it has many optional parts, there are very few included (if any). This kit only includes the stock snowflake rims in chrome. The T-tops from the body have been pre-cut, however there are not T-top inserts for it.  The rear spoiler and wheel caps are separate parts from the body and need to be glued on. Decals include all that are on box cover plus the sail panel birds, front fender and spoiler Trans Am decals as well as a small Pontiac decal for the right of the rear spoiler.

Monogram Kit# 2716

click -> The first kit in the WAR series of TA kits is a Monogram brand 1/24 scale 3'n1 kit.  The kit can be built in a Street Machine, a Drag Race or Stock version.  The kit is dated 1985 and is molded in black. Kit includes two sets of tires, stock and big and littles (bigs being slicks). There are three sets of wheels, 2 being Center Line type (wide and skinny) and a set of WS6 type snowflake wheels (all wheels are chrome). The T-tops  have already been cut from the roof at the factory and includes tinted glass for the openings as well as for the front and rear windows.  Also included are tinted head lamp covers. The rear spoiler and wheel caps are pre-molded to the body. Decals include pretty much what is shown on the box cover.
