How to place your ad:

  • Send me an e-mail containing the following personal information and the text ad you would like to display with the images (I am currently designing a form to handle this information)

          1) Your full name
          2) Your address (Only the state and city will be printed)
          3) The method of communication with which you wish to be contacted: either phone, e-mail,
               fax or all.

         *The text ad should be a reasonable length.

  • Send me up to six images of your car to fill the categories below:

    • A front view

    • A rear view

    • A driver side view

    • A passenger side view

    • An interior view

    • An engine view

        * Note: You may submit all or some of these views but the price remains the
           same.

        * I reserve the right to refuse to display low quality images. New ones will be
           requested. I also reserve the right to alter the size of an image for optimal
           display on the site.

Notes:

  • You ARE required to send at least one image of your car. NO "text only" ads are available.
