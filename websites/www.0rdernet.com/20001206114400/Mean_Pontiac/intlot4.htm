This 1989 Firebird Sports Coupe is owned by Englishman Shane Clark. Shane writes:

    "Thought you might like to add my Firebird to your fine gallery. It is a 1989 Firebird Sports Coupe (base model with the 2.8 V6) and lives in Norfolk, England. It was originally built in Van Nuys and shipped to Belgium, where it lived until imported into England two years ago. It is in virtually stock condition, apart from being resprayed all black (it was originally black over silver) with tinted rear lamps and having had a set of 15" finned wheels fitted from a 1983 S/E. The options on the car include T-Tops, Power Locks, Power Aerial (no longer fitted), Radio Cassette with Graphic Equalizer, Cruise Control, Remote Hatch Release, 5sp Manual Gearbox, Heated Rear Screen, Cargo Cover and 4-Way Manual Driver's Seat (medium grey interior). The Firebird is in excellent condition, and has covered 120,000 miles. It has been featured in the European magazine "Classic American" and goes to a number of car shows here in the UK. It is reasonably rare over here as Pontiacs aren't imported by GM."

Cheers,
Shane
