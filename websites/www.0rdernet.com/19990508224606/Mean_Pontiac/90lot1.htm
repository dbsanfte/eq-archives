The 1990 Pontiac Firebird, Formula & Trans Am GTA
Lot#1

click -> Great close-up front shot of a maroon GTA. This was one of the best looking colors available during the late 80s and early 90s. The introduction of the 91' models in mid 1990 made for a short run of this particular model year. It was basically the same car as the 89' and saw little change or improvement. Engine option in 90' were a 5.0L TPI rated at 200hp, a 5.0L TPI with a 5-speed manual tranny rated at 225 hp, and the 5.7L V-8 rated at 235hp. The 5.7L now came with dual exhaust.

click -> This pic was most likely used in an ad. The standard Trans Am wheels were the HiTech Turbo wheels colored a dark grey. Leather was no longer available on the TA and the WS6 package was still an available option.
