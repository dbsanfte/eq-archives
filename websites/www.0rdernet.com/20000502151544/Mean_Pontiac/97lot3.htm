click -> 		Sulphron is the given nickname of the person that owns this stunning black Trans Am. If you want to know about the nickname you'll have to e-mail and ask him. As for the car it was bought from Jim Harris Pontiac in Warrenton, VA on August 20, 1997 to replace the aging 86' Trans Am he had been driving. A breakdown of its factory equipment follows:
     The WS6 Performance Package which added the Ram Air induction system, 17" aluminum wheels, and P275/40ZR17 Goodyear tires. The prized LT1 5.7L SFI V-8 engine. A 4-speed automatic transmission and Performance rear axle with a 3:23 gear ration. For creature comforts T-Tops, power windows, power door locks, power mirrors and power seats are also included as well as leather seats and a leather wrapped steering wheel. Finally the eardrum annihilating Monsoon 500 watt 10 speaker stereo cassette system complete with a 12 disc CD changer provides some tunes.  
     Planned modifications? Doesn't everybody!? A throttle body air foil, performance computer chip and a K&N Filter are just some of the goodies in store for this bad bird in the near future...

Sulphron has his own website aptly named "Sulphron's Trans Am Page". Stop by and visit if you'd like to see more of his Trans Am.

click -> 		Matthew Porter owns this excellent example of Pontiac performance engineering. Matt's Trans Am exhibits a number of the 97' offerings for options. On the exterior the Firebird comes with T-Tops, Fog Lamps, Sport mirros with blue-tinted glass, and an uplevel spoiler.
     On the inside you'll find an interior that rivals the more expensive European imports with such features as Leather seats, a leather wrapped steering wheel, parking brake and shifter, power equipment across the board including windows and locks, cruise control, and air conditioning. Further comfort enhancements include one of the best factory stereo systems available today. A CD player comes packaged with a Graphic Equalizer, AM/FM stereo and a 4-speaker coaxial speaker system. All packaged on a car sporting nearly 300 horsepower.

Matt hosts his own personal webpage called MattPage. The site provides more information about his car and all the specs that apply to all 97' Trans Ams. You'll also find some information about him and some of his other hobbies. Feel free to stop by and check it out.

click -> Bob Morgan owns this radical convertible Trans Am. Ordered in October of 1996 it was finally  delivered in April of 1997 and in his own words, "was worth the wait". Bob wasn't fooling around either when the order was placed. Almost every option to be had was checked off including the Ram Air WS6 package, power seats, 12-disc CD changer, 16" chrome wheels and a factory alarm to name a few. With the WS6 package this convertible comes with 305hp 5.7L and with the addition of a 6-speed tranny I think Bob's gonna get about all the wind in his face he can stand. Bob writes:

"6 speed stick, 1800rpm at 76mph!"

Planned upgrades/mods for the future are 17" wheels (as if the 16s aren't big enough already!!), a better suspension, and a supercharger!!

Bob also owns a cherry 1974 Formula 350. He's the original owner and has completely restored it. It can be seen a 74' Firebirds Lot#1.
