Multi Packs

AMT/ERTL Kit# 8747


This is a 3-pack from AMT/ERTL. It features an '82 Firebird. All 3 kits are snap-tites. Supposedly the Firebird is only available in this pack. No further information is available at this time...
AMT/ERTL Kit# 6625

Another 3-pack from AMT/ERTL this one features a '97 Trans Am. Unlike the kit above this Firebird can be had in its own kit. Its AMT/ERTL #6154
Monogram Kit




This is the only all-Pontiac multi-pack I have ever seen. This one features two Firebirds, the "White Lightning" Pro Street Firebird (Monogram# 2748) and Special Edition black and gold Trans Am. It also features an exclusive '64 GTO kit that was only available in this multi-pack. I bought this kit and built all of the kits except for the pro street Firebird. All the kits were the larger 1/24 scale and had very good quality and detail.

MPC Kit# 6214




This kit is dubbed the "Indy Pace Car Set" because it features three different pace car kits: the 1980 Indy Pace Car, a Camaro convertible, and a Corvette convertible. No further information is available at this time...
Monogram Kit# 2712




This is a 2-pack from Revell featuring a ZR-1 Corvette and a 90s something Formula. Both kits are 1/24 scale and as an added bonus you get some kind of collector card for each car. No further information is available at this time.
