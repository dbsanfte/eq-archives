click -> A maroon Formula. There were 16,670 formulas produced in 89' Thats more than the total number of TAs and GTAs produced together. The Formula continued to be a big seller due to its lower price, lighter weight and the same hi-performance engine options that could be had in the Trans Am. The HiTech Turbo wheel continued to be the standard rim installed on Formulas.
