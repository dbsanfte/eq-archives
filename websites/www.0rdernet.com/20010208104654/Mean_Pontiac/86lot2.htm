Wayne Peters owns this award winning Trans Am. Wayne writes:

     "My car is 100% stock and original including paint. This past year (1999) I took 2nd place at the Trans Am Nationals in "D" stock (82-92) class and 1st place in the Trans Am class (TA-3) at the AMES Pontiac Nationals in Norwalk, OH.
The car has 20K miles and I bought the car new at Axelrod Pontiac in Parma, OH (Cleveland Suburb) in January of 1986. It has the LB-9 305 (TPI), the 700R-4 trans, WS6 suspension package, T-Tops, air conditioning, power windows/locks, tilt wheel, AM/FM cassette, power hatch release, deluxe cloth interior, factory body side molding and GM floor mats. The factory black finish is flawless, as is the Dark Carmine interior. The car is only driven to local shows and cruise-ins and the Nationals in Dayton."
