1992 cont...

  Factory sealed  $28 Monogram Kit# 2952


This is a 1/24 scale kit of a Formula. The kit is molded in metallic green. Surprise! How many of you, like me, thought this kit was molded in black. If you look REAL close at the box there seems to be a bare hint of green but it may actually be black. I haven't had one of these kits open yet but I'll bet this is the dark green that Pontiac was using in the early '90s, one of my favorite colors actually. Clear and plated parts are also included as well as black vinyl tires. Copyright date is 1991.
