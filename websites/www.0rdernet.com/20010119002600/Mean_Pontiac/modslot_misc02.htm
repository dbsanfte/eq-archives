Funny Cars and Other Misc Kits

MPC Kit# 1-0801



This is a 1/25 MPC kit of a funny car based on a '79 to '81 Trans Am called Black Belt.It comes molded in black. Features include chrome parts, an optional open parachute, wide rear drag slicks, an authentically detailed body, a detailed cockpit and funny car chassis, and a blown hemi racing engine. When built you are supposed to be able to flip-up the chassis just as you would on a real Funny Car.

MPC Kit# 1-0802




This is a cool MPC kit featuring a Top Fuel Firebird Funny Car called the "Burnout Bird". I believe the scale is 1/25. When built you are supposed to be able to flip-up the chassis just as you would on a real Funny Car. According to the box it features real drag shocks, extra wide rear slicks, racing decals as seen on the box, and a fully detailed long nose drag racing chassis. The box also claims the engine you build for this kit is a blown hemi racing engine. 
MPC Kit# 1-0867


This is a 1/25 scale MPC kit of a funny car based on an '82 or '83 Firebird called the Nitrobird. It comes molded in red and features: chrome parts, detailed funny car body and chasis, racing decals, wide racing slicks, blown and injected hemi racing engine, and a wheelie rack and drag shoot. Like the others above, once built you are supposed to be able to flip-up the chassis just like a real funny car.

Revell Kit# 6078
This is an interesting combo model kit/Toy offering from Revell that feature an early 80s Firebird. It must be assembled like a model kit but the end product could be "raced" via an installed pullback motor. Bodys were molded in either red or yellow and were very compact, not conforming to any standard modeling scale.

Revell Kit# 6121
This a Revell kit of the concept car made by Pontiac called "The Banshee". The kit is entirely molded in red and is 1/25 scale. Chrome includes the set of special custom rims and a few parts for the top of the engine. Decals included are only an arrowhead symbolf for the front nose and two "Banshee" stickers. This is a really good kit but the interesting window/roof design comes as one clear plastic piece in the kit and you are required to paint sections of it to simulate the body extensions. This can be prove hard to do painting straight lines. Otherwise its a great kit.
