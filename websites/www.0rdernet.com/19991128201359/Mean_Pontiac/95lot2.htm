Mike Alford own this special Trans Am. Its a Comp T/A and #57 of 164. The Comp T/A was a special package offered by PMD in 1995 to commemorate the anniversary of the BF Goodrich Comp T/A tire. The package was installed by Street Legal Performance (SLP); the same folks who installed the Ram Air setups on later models. Besides a large array of performance enhancements the package also included a special silver and grey paint job that is reminiscent of the 25th Anniversary T/As in 94' and the familiar paint scheme given to the 70' to 72' Trans Ams. This particular T/A is one out of only eleven that came with T-Tops and an automatic. Heres how Mike's Comp T/A is outfitted:
Grey Exterior w/ Black Stripe
CompTA Badging
T-Tops
Graphite Leather
6 Way Power Seats
CD Player

1 of 72 (SLP) Vanity Plate

Zaino Wax Treatment
Valentine1(V1)
STB
Mobil1
SFCs

click -> 		Jeff Goodell is the proud owner of this beautiful black Trans Am. Don't let the hood fool you, this is a 95' Trans Am. Jeff added the Ram Air option himself after the purchase using all factory WS6 parts. Jeff has also performed the following modifications to it:

K&N Filter
Edelbrock Headers
Flowmaster Catback Exhaust
Auto Specialties Crank Pulley
Hypertech Air Foil
Hypertech Power Programmer
GM 4.10 Gears with LPW Rear End Cover
Crane Gold 1.6 Rockers with Crane Double Valve Springs
Hurst Shifter
BFG Drag Radials on an extra set of Factory Wheels

These mods have provided impressive results. Jeff writes:

     "So far, it has run 8.43 @ 83 mph in the eighth mile, which should be around a 12.97 @ 105 mph in the quarter. My goal was to prove that an LT1 f-body can hit 12's without internal engine work (heads, cam etc) or power adders (supercharger or NO2). I think i have reached the goal, but will verify it when I visit a quarter mile track. We have two local eighth mile tracks in the area I live."

Jeff also owns a simply awesome Twin Turbo 88' GTA. If you like third or fourth gens or turbo powered cars you must take the time to check this one out. You can see it at the 88' Firebirds Lot#1.

This sleak black Trans Am is owned by Brendan Vetusky. Brendan writes:

     "I have had the '95 for 2 months now. I got it in an effort to have something more reliable to use on long distance evening drives, and have something nice while giving the '84 a little break. (It gets annoying after a while to break down on the highway and have to walk to the nearest phone to be towed to the parts store) But I love this car too! I don't think I will ever build another car w/ out fuel injection. I love the crisp response! I also love the 6 speed. I will definately put one in my '84! The '95 is bone stock for now. Future plans (when the wallet permits) is to put on a ram air hood (98 style), a chip and exhaust. It's a clean car and was well (still is) taken care of. It should have a long happy life."
