  Factory sealed  $25    Model Store MPC Kit# 1-0819



This is a 1/25 scale MPC kit of a highly customized 81' Turbo Trans Am. It bears a hefty resemblance to the Warlord kit also produced by MPC and based on the 79' body style. This one is called the "Turbo Bird". No other information is available at this time...
Revell Kit# 7230




This is a 1/25 scale Revell kit of a highly customized 81' Trans Am. The kit is molded in black and is dated 1981. The body is loosely based on the George Barris customized Trans Ams of the late 70s, featuring IMSA style flared fenders and a whale tail spoiler. The T-Tops are pre-cut and tops are provided. No chrome pieces are provided and the decal set is what you see on the box.
