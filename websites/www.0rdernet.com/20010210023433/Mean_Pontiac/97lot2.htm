click -> A Hurst Firebird lightin' em' up!!! This car was born out of a special deal between Pontiac and the venerable Hurst Co. Hurst has been a part of the Muscle Car and Sports Car market for a long time. They provided shifters to several of Pontiacs cars throughout the 60s and 70s and also provided the T-Tops for the 76' and 77' Trans Ams. These cars were limited editions and an order only car.
