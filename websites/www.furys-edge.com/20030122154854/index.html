Today's update by Kalran:

Saturday morning...what to do... I know; It's time to finally send that god damned worthless epic back to its sender. Luckily our warriors decided to take those glorified glowsticks and use 'em to illuminate an otherwise dark orifice of Rallos Zek the Warlord. Gnomes all across the planes rejoiced and even rewarded us with the ability to entered the coveted 4th tier planes. Always seeking something new though, FE couldn't stop with putting the overzealous spazoid face down in the dirt, we went on to bitch slap Solusek Ro and his 5 sisters (okay, they're not all female, but they all hit like girls). Now that all 4 of the elemental planes are available for pilfering we'll be striving to bring justice back to Pain and Suffering by killing everyone's favorite gods and godesses.
