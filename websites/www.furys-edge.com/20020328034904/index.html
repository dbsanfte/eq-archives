Avatar of War: Dead for the first time. - update by Draekk 03/11/2002


Avatar of War

Dead to 48 FE

Congratulations to everyone on a job well done. The fight left little to be desired, and lasted just over 10 minutes. After more than half a dozen previous attempts, it was a huge adrenaline rush to see it all finally come together. Hopefully he'll become part of the weekly schedule. Expect to see his feet sticking out of a wall near you.

Loot from this beast was a Ring of Destruction for Kardenzer, a War Bow of Rallos Zek for Fenny, and a Blade of Carnage for Boogalooga. Congrats guys.
