Spell Stacking problems??
Ever tried to stack the Rangers Call of Earth with the Enchanter Epic spell? they dont stack. Verant says:
Actually, they both add armor class. I believe that the ranger spell adds greater armor class than the Speed of Shissar, and that would explain why it is the spell to take precedence.

This is not a bug. There will be cases when you must choose one spell over the other. Not all spells will stack, nor are they intended to. Making decisions about which spells to have cast on you is the same sort of decision that you have to make about which weapon to use or which armor to wear.

Where Can I Find Ro's Fiery Sundering ?
Alan answers with some tips:
There are several places where you can obtain this spell. I hesitate to list them, but I'll tell you this much. It drops in Wakening Lands, Iceclad, and the Tower of Frozen Shadow. And we'll be increasing its drop rate in the Tower a bit.

And having such a good spell be hard to get isn't contrary to our intent. Good stuff should be hard to get. We're adjusting the frequency of its appearance a little. But it's never going to be easy to find.

Scale of the Wolf Clarification
A player asked: I posting to learn if the way Scale of Wolf works currently - drops the way in invis does if you cast or attack or are attacked - is a bug or as intended?

Verant replied:
This effect was changed several patches ago. Scale of Wolf will only drop if the spell expires, the enchantment is dispelled, the person with the buff turns on auto attack, or the person with the buff casts a spell. The spell will not drop if a spell is cast on the person with the buff, or the person with the buff is attacked.

Arena Fight on Rodcet Nife

Time: 8:00pm EST
Date: Tuesday May First
Server: Rodcet Nife
Where: The Arena, adjacent to Lake Rathe
