Purpose:

The characters who are part of this guild are working together as friends and loyal companions.

This is a guild to come to for honest characters who are in the game for fun and to meet interesting people.
 
We are not a hack-and-slash guild. We're looking for new friends, some fun of role-playing, and a chance to have a good time. We don't just role-play, we do a lot of hunting, but we do role-playing with the hunting.
 
We welcome characters of all races, classes, genders and religions. The more interesting, the better, but anyone may join the Spiritwalkers.

Rules:

  • Deal honestly with people, even ones who aren't part of this guild. Honesty and integrity are part of being a Spiritwalker. Treat your fellow Norrathians as you would have them treat you. Render aid when you can, and support people of good will.
    It shall be considered grounds for immediate removal from the guild to cheat or lie or otherwise behave in a dishonest fashion.  This means with other guild members or with those outside the guild.
  • Treat your character as a "real person", not as a bunch of numbers and powers, bundled with some cool magic items or tough armor. In other words, give the character a bit of personality, a little history, maybe a few (or a lot of) quirks. Role-playing is a requirement of being a Spiritwalker. While we're not "Role-play Cops," we do like for people to play in character.

  • Looting: The leader of the hunt should determine looting procedures. We recommend that this be spelled out before the first battle if at all possible.
    Remember that the key to happiness with looting is to make sure that everyone gets an equal share. If the group is looking for a special item, make sure that everyone has a chance to get one, or that you have agreed before hand on who should get it.     The guild does not dictate looting procedures.

  • This is not a Player Vs Player guild. We do not condone Player Vs Player activity. Members are free to engage in dueling if they wish, but it is not encouraged.

  • The game is all about FUN. Remember to have fun playing the game, and to let others have fun playing.

  • Members of Spiritwalkers are free to have characters in any other guild they wish. The only caveat to this is that persons belonging to multiple guilds are not allowed to hold officer positions in Spiritwalkers.

 

Who is in charge:

The Spiritwalkers have a very simple power structure. The guild is headed by the Guild Leader, Mortekai Spiritwalker.
The Guild Leader is assisted by Officers. Officers are noted in the People section of the guild roster page with bold type.

Officer Duties:

  1. Assist the Guild Leader with member inductions.
  2. Answer Member and Nonmember questions about the guild.
  3. Serve to mediate disputes between guild members and/or non-guild members.
  4. Any specific tasks assigned by the Guild Leader.
