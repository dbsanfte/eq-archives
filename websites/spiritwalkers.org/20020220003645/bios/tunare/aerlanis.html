Spiritwalkers Guild Member Bio

Name: Aerlanis
Race: Erudite
Profession: Necromancer
Likes:  
Dislikes:  
Quirks:  

Aerlanis was born into a wealthy Erudite family. Her father one of the High Council, her mother a warm-hearted Enchantress.

Aerlanis took to learning very early, intent on becoming an Enchantress like her mother.

Things turned dark for Aerlanis in a way few can conceive. Foul Bandits struck into the heart of a Peace Negotiation Party Aerlanis' Father was leading across the Plains of Karanas.

After a long battle the Erudites lay dying. Being so young, Aerlanis could not fight, yet was spared for an even worse fate. She was taken .. kidnapped .. and forced into slavery. She was sold to the Heretics and forced into submission. Aerlanis, was broken of spirit and honor -- wishing to see her family again, she did as she was told and spoke to no one.

After years of labour and abuse from the Heretics, she became trusted by them. She was allowed to aid them at their rituals. Aerlanis became wary of the practices which the Necromancers used to raise the dead. She kept quiet and watched everything.

Aerlanis began to learn the basic secrets of these Heretics and took to pilfering scrolls and artefacts used by them. She practiced in the darkness of her little room. Until finally, she did it .... A Skeleton began to rise from the ground, assembling itself into a form and communicating with her. It was a voice she had not heard in a very very long time. A tender voice of compassion and love. It was her FATHER. She had wanted do much to see her family that she had brought her father back from the dead. He gave her strength and the will to go on.

Aerlanis kept practicing any spell she could. She continued summoning other family members to sit and talk to. Finally, she found a spell she could use to escape. During a wild Heretic ritual she was able to decipher a spell to gather shadows about her. After the ritual was done the Heretics all lay in a heap of drunken blood-ncrusted maniacs. She used this new spell and walked into the shadows and away from the vile Heretics

She now uses her new-found powers to summon her long dead relatives to aid her in survival. In one of her journeys she befriended a young Druid. Rainbird was her name. Rainbird was not frightened of the leering skeleton which accompanied Aerlanis. They became very close and Rainbird introduced Aerlanis to the Spiritwalkers. Aerlanis was welcomed to the guild and now has a another family to accompany her, but will always keep her long-dead family by her side.

If you happen to see Aerlanis mumbling and staring at her Skeletal pet, remember this is one of her family.
