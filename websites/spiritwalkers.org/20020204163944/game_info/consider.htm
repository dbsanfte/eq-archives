Faction Information

  Faction levels are very important in the game, as they determine who will love you and who will try to kill you. Higher faction standings result in better prices from merchants, and the ability to do quests given by certain NPCs. The numbers assigned below are assigned aribtrailly by me, and do not represent any numbers assigned by the EQ software. Faction is composed of several factors, including Race, Religion, and Actions. Factions can often be changed by doing quests or killing monsters.

Consider Messages

  Consider Messages are one of the most important tools for determining the risk of an encounter. The message conveys the level of the creature realtive to yours. This changes at various levels, the most notable change coming at level 25, when the threat-level of the creature is still reported by realtive level, but is measured in terms of threat to a balanced group rather than an individual. I have omitted the first part of the consider message, which reports your faction standing withe the /consider target.

Some note about this table: I've never gotten a character higher than level 52, so information on levels above that are largely speculation on my part. Also, these level ranges are approximations. I don't know for sure that ANYONE has bothered to try to verify these at all levels.
