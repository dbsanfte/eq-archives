Spiritwalkers Guild Member Bio

Name: Ixla
Race: Teir'Dal (Dark Elf)
Profession: Enchanter
Likes: Troll women
Dislikes: Being abandoned by his party with his body still inside the dungeon
Quirks: Ages slowly, terrified of growing old and ugly

Ixla first began adventuring in Tunare’s Norrath when the land was very young and barely formed. Friends he made in his first hunts in the Nektulos forest have reached 50th or beyond and retired before he was 20, and another couple of generations of 1-to-50 have occurred since then, while Ixla has barely aged past 35.

Apparently, when very young, he nearly drowned in a fountain of youth. In fact, his mother, a priestess of Innoruuk, expressed disappointment that he survived. Young Ixla thought that all priestesses of Innoruuk beat and tortured their male children. Eventually, frustrated at his penchant for turning up alive yet once more, she said, "Ixla, your worhtless mouth isn't worth the food I feed it. Go and never return to this house again."

That is how Ixla ended up at the Tower of the Spurned in Neriak, gravitating toward the profession with the greatest opportunity for concealment and escape - enchantment. Choosing wizardry or magery would have been foolish. They are forthright magical fighters, and Ixla had no taste for head-on fights.

This sad if not uncommon youth also shaped Ixla's attitude toward religion. He has dedicated his life to frustrating Innoruuk and his message whenever and wherever possible. Ixla takes great and perverse pleasure in being kind, considerate, helpful, and mild-mannered. Most of all, he judges others by their actions, not by the god they profess to worship. Nevertheless, Ixla remains proud of his Teir'Dal heritage and still enjoys visiting his native city. However, he is very careful to avoid visiting the Red Temple beyond the Third Gate.

Ixla joined the Spiritwalkers because his close friends Electrae and Shandahr had become members. Alas, since then, Electrae and her cousins (Wsyteria, Lysteria, etc.) have left for another world, while Shandahr's elder kin (Drexxen, Armis, etc.) are now voidrunners. But this gradual transformation of circles of frinds simply serves to expand his own.

Recently, Ixla was delighted to receive the princely gift of a Shardwurm Coat from some senior voidrunners. Having never found robes that attractive, he is overjoyed to finally have a elegant and attractive appearance that makes him unique among the enchanters of Tunare.
