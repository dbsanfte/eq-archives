War of Flowers - Vallon Zek
[2 Comments] - Aurum @ 8:23 PM PDT
I received the following from Mlaria:

I wish to stage Norrath's first "War of Flowers." This is a name taken
from a ritualized war that the Aztecs would have with the tribes that
lived in the surrounding area. They were wars that were conducted in
times of peace so that warriors could "prove" themselves on the field of
battle, as well as to capture human sacrifices to offer up to the sun.
(This is a simplistic explaination of what the "War of Flowers" or
"Flowery Wars" where. It's some damn intersting reading and I suggest if
you have any interst in this sort of stuff to do a search on the web
about it.)

This war will take place in 3 zones:
West Commons.
East Commons.
Desert of North Ro.

Object and Rules:
1. Each team will pick 4 players that are their Standard Bearers
(Standard as in a flag)
2. The Standard Bearers will be of levels 9th-11th, 16th-18th, 23rd-25th
and 37th-39th.
3. Each Standard Bearer will be given a jewel (the standard) that must
stay in a lootable slot.
4. Each Standard Bearer MUST stay in the 3 war zones with his anonymous
and roleplay turned off so his/her location can be verified at all
times.
5. The war is won when 1 side has looted all the opposing teams
standards. (jewels)
6. Teams will be Good vs Evil.
7. Out of Range healing is acceptable.
8. CR will not be respected (except as stated in rule 11)
9. If the Standard Bearer leaves Norrath for any reason, he is in
essance dead. Wether LD, RL or whatever.
10. If a Standard Bearer leaves the 3 zones, wether getting killed and
returned to his bind point by a MOB, accidentally zoning, gets knocked
out of a war zone by something like a wiz spell or any other reason,
they are dead and must surrender their standard.
11. If a Standard Bearer is killed and his Standard (jewel) is not
looted, then the Standard Bearer will be afforded standard CR
privilages. If the Standard Bearer does not return to his corpse within
15 minutes then he is dead and must surrender his Standard (jewel).

Discussion on rules/team leaders/etc will continue on the War of Flowers
msg boards at:
http://pub8.ezboard.com/bministryofhate.html

In the Service of Innoruuk,
High Priestess Mlaria Thex
Ministry of Hate
mlaria_thex@hotmail.com

Ordering Kunark Q&A
[Post a Comment] - Aurum @ 7:41 PM PDT
Verant Internet Relations Manager Gordon Wrinn posted answers to many of the questions regarding the ordering, shipping and delievery of Kunark that have been asked there. Here's the Q&A:

    Who is Verant's fulfillment house?

    We're using a company named Zomax. Their web site is http://www.zomax.com .

    Do you think the majority of US customers who ordered the expansion yesterday, and chose UPS ground, will receive their update prior to the 24th of April?

    All of them should. We've been assured by Zomax that shipping anywhere in the world will take no longer than two weeks.

    Why do some people (like myself) not have the "Store" button on the server select screen?

    We'll have an email address up soon for those without "Store" buttons. Assuming the email address in your profile is current, you'll most-likely be contacted prior to that.

    1- Why dont you put a quantity box that would let you ship more then one expansion at a time (I cant believe zomax couldnt handle that simple thing)?

    Unfortunatly that wasn't an option for this product, and we are exploring options for the future, though that doesn't help people right now.

    2- Will there be a cheaper, painless way of shipping in the future, like USPS? (even if it would mean people getting the expansion after april 24th)

    No other shipping methods are planned for the Expansion product.

    Another question: Will the retail box be released to vendors prior to the go-live date?

    Retailers will not be able to sell the retail box prior to the launch date.

    The only difference is instead of a city/state, APO is used for the city and AP is used for the state. Currently, I cannot change my billing information to reflect this properly, since only the 50 state abbreviations are listed as a drop down choice. Could you please add AA, AE, and AP to that list?

    We can't get anything added to the list at this point, however you can specify an APO/FPO address in the shipping options, and Zomax will handle those by hand.

    Will we be able to enter the new zones as soon as we get the CD? Or will the new zones go live on the 24th when the CDs are on the shelf.

    You will not be able to access Kunark-related content until they go live on the 24th.

    ok I currently have an active everquest account, but due to certain reasons I can't play the game till may 17th. This means I can't even log in to order the expansion. Will the expansion ordering be available at that date also? Or will I have to buy the full version after a certain date? Also which company is doing the shipping (ups, fed ex...) thanks.

    Currently we have no plans of shutting down the ability to order the expansion, however it makes sense that at some time we will. We'll most likely post a patch message at that time to warn people, though I think you'll be fine through mid-May.

    What is the cost of the "RETAIL" package? In the US will I be expected to pay $40 USD or is the "RETAIL" package going to be $16.95 as well?

    The cost of the retail package will vary depending on the distributor. I do believe that a couple of web-based distributors are taking pre-orders at present.

    I have purchased the expansion, but do not meet the minimum requirement for the computer...yet(come on tax check ). Should I wait to install the expasion? Could I install it but not go to the new continent, or would this crash my game?

    You can feel free to execute the registration procedure and do the installation prior to your upgrade, but I'm not able to comment on what will happen should you try to visit the new lands. Bad framerates and graphics problems would probably be likely.

    What I don't understand is *When* we will actually be able to go into kunark?

    Will it be when we get the CD's?

    You'll be able to visit Kunark when the zones are opened on the servers. This is scheduled for 4/24.

    Just wondering about how many days it will take it to reach Sweden

    Zomax assures us that shipping will take no longer than two weeks to reach anywhere.

    Would it be at all possible to allow somoene going to the store, buying the full $40 game, no S&H costs or anything, using that code to upgrade the account they already have and keep (or activate if they want) a non-kunark newbie account good for transfering items or whatever?

    If you buy the retail version, you can either use the account key to upgrade your current account OR use the account key to create a new Kunark enabled account. Not both.

    Also this new account would have the 1 month free that comes with the game. The guide told me this was possible, you seem to be saying its not...if this is the case would it be at all possible to allow it?

    Should you choose to use the retail box account key to upgrade your existing account, you would not be eligible for a free month. You will only get a free month of play if you use the retail box account key to create a new account, of course this would leave your current account unable to access Kunark.

    ---

    Keep the questions coming, I'll check back for more later.

    -Gordon

You can visit the thread on the Gameplay Board by clicking here.Thanks Gordon!


Purchasing Ruins of Kunark
[Post a Comment] - Aurum @ 5:41 PM PDT
Gordon Wrinn posted the following message on the Gameplay Board concerning the ways one can purchase the expansion:

    Hello all,

    To clarify the different purchase options:

    If you buy the UPGRADE, you can use it to flag your CURRENT account for Kunark. This will allow you to access all Kunark-related content (including levels above 50) with all of your characters, on your current account only.

    If you buy the RETAIL package, you can do one of two things:

    1. You can use the key to create a NEW account, where all characters on that account will have access to Kunark. If you do this, you will receive a free month of play with the NEW account, but it does NOT authorize your CURRENT account to access Kunark content.

    OR

    2. You can use the key to upgrade your CURRENT to access Kunark content in the same fashion as the upgrade. If you choose to do this with the RETAIL package, you will NOT receive a free month of play, and you will not be able to use the account key to create a NEW account.

    Please post further questions about your purchase options underneath this thread so that I can be sure to address them all.

    -Gordon

A few minutes later he added this bit about shipping & handling and international orders:
    I'll address the S/H issue soon, but right now I'm looking for questions. The question, "What's the deal with the international shipping prices?" has been noted, and I'll address that when I grab the rest.

    Moving straight on to the Gordon Infobits, here's all of Gordon Wrinn's current posts on the Gameplay board:

    • The Hole
      [Quote from original post: "We want people do 'do' dungeons instead of camp"
      -Verant

      The following is an proposal for the zone called "The Hole", which I feel would add a much needed level of excitement and skill to the mid-level Everquest game.

      We've all heard of the Planes, the high-level, ultra-hard zones that require teamwork, efficiency, and have some lovely loot. However, being level 46-50 is not something that we can all experience, sadly.

      Now, most of us have heard of Hell Levels. They are a result of a mathematical inaccuracy in the experience tables that require you to gain a lot more experience than intended to level up. 30, 35, 40, and 45 are by definition, "Hell Levels".

      What I propose is a place where mid-level characters could get pre-plane experience in a similar environment, a high-risk, high-reward dungeon that would be open for mid-level people only. There would be more experience per mob killed due to difficulty and to make leveling through Hell a bit easier. Of course, people die in The Hole, so, as I said, it's Risk vs. Reward.

      The zone could be open for players level 34-41, which encompasses 2 hell levels, 35 and 40, respectively. The limits are in place so that the people intended to be there are there, no high-level campers, no powerlevelers.

      The loot would be nice. Something for every class, I hope. Specifically, I would like to see a Shadowknight weapon of Ghoulbane strength if not better. Components for nice quests could be found here, perhaps some rare poison for the rogues, a new spell for the casters. Wouldn’t you like to see a spell earned instead of bought?

      In a nutshell, the entire point of this proposed Hole is a proving ground for sub-planar characters. It’s to get them off their camping arses and into a big, scary Hole. I’m done now, but below were several other ideas I considered, that may or may not be “over the top” for something like this.

      OTHER IDEAS-

      The Screams of Pain- Based on a random timer that spans at least 6 hours, every so often a high-pitched screaming noise can be heard coming from the depths of the Hole. This quickly acts as a warning to any people currently adventuring in there, as they have 60-80 seconds after the screams begin to act. When the 60-80 seconds are gone, full zone repop. This does two things,

      a. Makes the dungeon even more challenging, scary, and unexpected.

      b. Prevents camping and farming of the Hole. The intention is to earn what you get down here; respawn camping is lame.

      The Boss- The Hole is a physical hole ripped from Norrath in a great war of magics. The “Boss” mob should be tough enough to reflect this. Since the proposed level range for the players is somewhat large (7), balancing a Boss is difficult. Making it too high would make casters useless unless they were about to leave the zone (upon reaching the higher limit). Level is really just a number, so a level 38 Boss buffed to ungodly amounts for that level. Cazic Thule, Inoruuk, and the Dragons reportedly have 32,000 HP, so I would think that around 14,000 would not be unreasonable.

      The Boss Room, an alternative to The Boss- The “Boss Room” would be very unique, and if possible would greatly enhance the experience. For every person in The Hole (about 30 for an average raid, let’s say), then there are 1/6 mobs created. If there are 30 people raiding, there are 5 monsters, very tough. Buffed to extremes, but not quite as much as The Boss. They would vary in class and loot. Of course, I don’t know if the technology exists to allow this kind of spawning.

      The Boss, part II!- In an effort to torment players, Bruce Springsteen music is repeatedly played while in the zone.     • Gameplay Engine Question
      [Quote from original post: Why is it that when you are inside a cottage, house or building creatures can attack you through the wall even though you cant see them. This basically means in places like East Karana you cant take refuge almost anywhere. Why shouldnt you be able to get in a house and hide w/o a lion invisibly attacking you through the wall? this applies to griffins and other creatures as well. Other buildings in other zones too!]

      Not really a bug. In order to reduce the amount of "lag" that can happen due to internet latency, then engine handles object collisions client-side. Most houses and such are objects (rather than world geometry) and are subject to this.

      It also has the benefit of eliminating a good number of issues that would have to be dealt with in order to limit possible exploitation. For instance, if this wasn't the case, someone could sit inside a house and cast upon a creature that couldn't fit through the open door.

    • Corpse Decay

      Corpses above level 5 remain for 24 online hours or one week offline, whichever occurs first. In other words, your corpse should be fine.
