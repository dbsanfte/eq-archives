EQ Fest 2000 Update
[2 Comments] - Aurum @ 1:33 PM PST
From Guntar:

Hail and well Met!

The time has come and many brave EQ adventurers will be donning their armor and heading out for EQ FEST 2000. I want to thank all of you who have shown great support for this event, and to those who were not able to make it this year, I send my best wishes and sincere desire to see you at the next EQ FEST!

WEATHER

The weather for the weekend will be a chance of isolated t-storms with lows in the 50's and highs in the low 70's. Be sure to bring adequate protection from the elements. The Friday night social gathering is all inside, but Saturday and Sunday will be out in the open. (We will be under a very large Pavilion style tent on Saturday.)

EQ FEST CONTESTS

I am seeing several people sign up for the contests who are not on my list as attendees. As such, there will be a secondary sign-in sheet located at the front table which you will need to fill out and return to me after the Friday Night Social Gathering. If you will not be attending Friday night, you may fill it out first thing Saturday morning and give to me prior to the contests.

ADMISSIONS

Based on the above statement. Let me make this perfectly clear. If you have not paid your registration, or made special arrangements with me, you will not be able to enter the private area where EQ FEST 2000 will be held. This does not mean you can not come to the Texas Renaissance festival, it just means you will not be able to take part in the EQ related festivities. Although I hate to see anyone miss out, it would not be fair to those who paid their admissions to allow this. The web site was very clear on the admission policy and cut off dates.

PUNCTUALITY IS A VIRTUE

For those of you who are attending the fest, it is very important that you be on time for the Friday night dinner and the start of the event on Saturday and Sunday. This will insure that you receive all the necessary information you will need to have a well planned weekend. I want to make sure everyone has the opportunity to take part in all events.

Thanks again for all your support! I look forward to seeing you all this weekend, and for those not coming, next year!

Guntar Pontdecardon
EQ FESTIVAL 2000
Thanks and good luck with the event!

Redemption of Eragru - E'ci
[1 Comments] - Aurum @ 5:58 AM PST
Remmah sent the following report:

Last night outside of the borders of castle Mistmoore,
I was approched by Eragru Pryysh, who asked for my
aid.

He told me his tale, and asked me to gather companions
to right a terrible wrong. To summarize his story, 100
years ago, his party in Lesser Faydark was wiped out
by a horrible beast, where he, and his companions,
Ibumar, Emar, Borshan, and Xyan were defeated. They
were taken to a strange stone room, and their captor
informed them of what they had become. They were
vampires, and served their lord for 50 years, before
he dissapeared and they gained their freedom. However
the curse they suffered under affected them, and
Eragru's companions could not turn back to the life of
light they once knew. Xyan Darksoul and Borshan
Unslain yearned to unlock the secrets of the dead, and
gain ultimate power. Xyan studied under the dark elves
his power of necromancy, growing strong as time
passed. Borshan Unslain journied to the place in
Norrath where he could be closest to his god Innoruuk
and serve as his Knight. Emar Vyshesan journeyed to a
far away land, full of badgers and lizards, to seek
informaion of a temple and hone his skills of
enchantry. Finally, Ibumar Rafsha, who initially
resisted, as did Eragru, could no longer bear her
terrible burden, and retreated far to the north to
practice her skills of martial arts in frozen
desolation.

Eragru prayed to Tunare, and was finally forgiven. He
was blessed with a vision of how to be redeemed, our
task was to slay these beasts, and return their heads
to Eragru, so his spirit can be free.

I gathered companions, and we set off to find Xyan
Darksoul, in the home of dark elves, Neriak. We
journeyed to the forest of Nektulos and found Xyan
awaiting us on a great stone bridge. He scoffed at our
task, then set upon us, aided by nearby darkelven
guards and dragoons. Our party fought valliantly,
chasing the foul one deep into the forest, defeating
his skeletal pet and finally putting his tortured soul
to rest.

We took his head as Eragru asked, and journeyed on to
the forest of Kithicor, near the veil to the plane of
hate, hoping to find word of Borshan Unslain. We did,
and found his decaying form at the place of his death.
Another had defeated him before us, and left. Some
magics prevented us from taking his head, so I went
back to speak with Eragru. I found that the other
vampires had been defeated, and their victors
returning for what reward they might claim. I was
rewarded for the death of Xyan, and we waited for the
other brave adventurers to return the heads so Eragru
could be free.

I would thank Prunezilla, for the slaying of Borshan
Unslain, the ShadowKnight of Kithicor, Eldoth, for the
death of Emar Vyshesan, the Enchanter of Droga, and
Rasputin for slaing the foul Ibumar Rafsha.

Also, among my party I would extend deepest gratitude
and congratulations to Willaena, Alkrun, Akabar,
Walsfeo, Keudiven, Iila, Cuinn and Filiene, your help
my friends was invaluable, and I hope you enjoy your
rewards.

For the death of Xyan we were rewarded with a Ghastly
Robe(Walsfeo), and Sacrificial Dagger(Akabar), as well
as the booty we claimed from the corpse of Xyan,
Polished bone bracers (Keudiven, Filiene), a hooded
black cloak(Willaena), a Sheer bone mask(Iila), a
combine dagger(Cuinn), and a braided cord
belt(Alkrun).

We were offered a monetary sum as well, but declined
it, as the pleasure of aiding a soul in need was our
true goal.

Rest well, friend, you have earned it.

Gordon Infobits
[Post a Comment] - Aurum @ 6:30 PM PST
Here's this evening's edition of the Gordon Infobits:

  • Player Morale
    I definitely understand what you are saying and appreciate the way you've stated your thoughts. It's nice to see a good opportunity for straight-forward discussion here on the boards.

    [Quote from original post: Doesn't exactly paint a trustworthy picture, does it? ]

    Maybe it does paint a trustworthy picture, but perhaps not in the same fashion as what you would have hoped. I do think that players should and can trust that we'll do what we can in order to ensure that EverQuest remains a game that people want to play. Afterall, it's been shown that people do no like to play games that are too easy and where there is no challenge in playing. It's been shown that there is no feeling of accomplishment that comes, for instance, in obtaining an item that "everyone" else has, or has relatively unrestricted access to.

    It's one of those things that can have a positive and negative effect. As has been said before, to make an omelette, you have to break a few eggs.

  • Scheduled Downtime Page
    [Quote from original post: At the moment it does not mention the fact that there was supposed to be a patch today, and not suprisingly it also does not mention that the patch has been cancelled.]

    So it's basically a wash :) Seriously though, you're correct that there should be something up there, if not concerning the postponed patch then when we're having the next one. I'm talking with ops and test and hope to have another patch scheduled soon. We've got quite a few things going on now with Velious preparations, the launch party in Vegas, and some server maintenance, that things are a bit complicated.

  • Stun Arrows?
    Not arrows. We can't assign an effect to arrows that will proc when the arrows are fired. However, we do now have the ability to create bows that proc an effect. So yes, it is possible that this will be something that you may see in the future.

  • Level 50+ With Velious But Without Kunark
    Yes, you will be able to exceed 50th level with the purchase of Velious, even if you do not already have Kunark.

  • Testing Board
    [Quote from original post: Hobart and Ester seem to be AWOL most of the time the past couple months. Posts are starting to get bumped like on General and that isn't good for anyone...]

    They are busy testing and fixing issues. Communicating on that board isn't part of their official decription, but they asked that the forum be set up to get a better idea of what the players are experiencing.

    They aren't there to address issues they've already looked into, but to keep moving forward whenever possible.

    [Quote from original post: It is unacceptable to have only one person (Hobart) hired to look into bugs... ]

    Please don't confuse one or two people responding to posts with one or two people looking into issues. Not everyone here is authorized to post on the boards, they are behind the scenes looking into your issues.

  • ETA For Next Patch
    We're planning for late next week. We wanted to give everyone as much time as possible to upgrade their drivers while running some more diverse tests in-house.

  • DX7A
    [Quote from original post: I've tried everything I can think of doing, and still EQ crashes for me between the Server select screen and the
    character select screen, after the loading screen. I have run complete file checks and tried 3 drivers from nvidia
    and 1 from gateway for my Nvidia Vanta chip. I don't have a soudcard, and this has never caused me problems
    before. None of the drivers I have tried (including Detonate 3, the newest driver from nvidia) are certified for
    directx 7a. Microsoft lists a compatable version (ver 4.6 bld 3.84) and says to obtain it from the manufacturer,
    but neither Gateway nor Nvidia have a driver under this designation. Is my chip compatable? What driver should I
    use and where can I get it? Is there something else I'm missing? ]

    Try launching TESTEVERQUEST.EXE. There's some instructions in that patch message considering the order of driver installation and what to do if you have problems. I noticed that you mentioned that you don't have a sound card.... Are you sure? Most computers these days come with them by default.

    If you think that your crash may be sound-related, try sticking a file in your EverQuest directory by the name of NOSOUND.TXT. It doesn't matter what's in the file; It's basically a debug tool to let people try with the sound engine disabled.

  • Nerfing the Bracer
    Actually we haven't any current plans of "nerfing" the bracer, but to be honest I really don't think that it would be a horrible idea. I wouldn't say that it would be to "hide" anything about the effect on your epic weapon, but it has been said that people didn't like the effect because they'd rather click on the bracer.

    Still, like I said, no plans along these lines, but I wouldn't be surprised if it becomes far less available in game.

[6 Comments] - Aurum @ 4:37 PM PST
Early, early this morning on the Arena board Lady Dae published the following message:

    Lady Daegarmo entered The Arena, to be greeted by hundreds of smiling faces, all of them anxious, yet nervous to see what glory and honor the day would bring them.

    Leaders of great armies approached and introduced themselves with an incredible energy that words simply could not explain.

    These leaders talked amongst each other, making an incredible social event where discussions were filled of great feats, of dragons slain, of immortals killed, of dungeons plundered, and
    even of the best brews that were made in the lands. It was a festive moment that you could not help but to feel apart of.

    Morale was its highest, as everyone strutted around, happy simply making it to the finals, platemail boots barely leaving footprints in the soft sand.

    Lady Daegarmo's smile could not be mistaken as she knew that all of her teams effort to make this tournament a story of legends was unfolding. As she turned a corner, she suddenly
    stopped smiling, as she saw a team in distress.
    She ran up to the group, and smiles were not seen on the faces of these champions. Lady Daegarmo asked "What is wrong? Do you not have your gear? Is your team missing members? What has caused you to not enjoy the festivities that are in front of you?"

    With heads hung low, and spirits crushed, the party answered. "We beg your forgiveness, for we have failed you. You see, our team cheated to win our regional event, to earn our right to be here with the true champions, and we are so heartbroken by our actions that we are unsure if we wish to compete with those who earned their right to compete."

    "Our homeland has found out of our dishonorable deeds, and does not bless our presence here. We do not have the hopes and dreams of our people behind us. We do not have their prayers and their wishes to give us strength to defeat our foes. We ask for forgiveness and wonder if we should still enter the event."

    This was not a request to enter the event, without question it was a plea to dismiss the group so that they would not need to make the hardest decision they would ever have to make, the decision they knew in their hearts was right.

    Lady Daegarmo paused and thought for a moment. The team was defeated, a lost cause before they entered the match. With as high of spirits and hopes that the other teams had, no amount of skill or courage could make up for the loss of morale by this team. As a Knight herself, the Lady pondered what punishment she would give those involved.

    Disqualifying the team was an option, but by those who truly understood justice, knew that such a decision would be considered merciful, for it ended the pain and suffering of having to
    bring themselves to resignation.

    Lady Daegarmo spoke "You have admitted to me freely the crimes you have done against your homeland. Send this message, that I give you the right to enter this event. Spread the word
    so that all of your countrymen know that you can freely make this decision to enter this event, or resign if your hearts so tell you that is the path you wish to follow!"

    There was no relief on the eyes of this team, for the burden was not lifted from them. The weight so strong that they could not breathe without effort.

    Daegarmo left and attended her duties as the primary judge of the event, knowing the decision was already made. Many great teams came forward, showing outstanding skill and spirit, refusing to let themselves be broken. This team quietly watched the matches, without the enthusiasm of the others. The bouts held not the fascination to them as it did the others, for they knew their turn would never arrive.

    Lady Daegarmo instructed Lord Akkirus to take the next match, knowing what lied ahead. A team full of energy and spirit ran to its side, and immediately formed the requested starting
    positions and formations. Their faces glowed, their armor shined, and yet Daegarmo did not move. The shattered team then approached, and faced Akkirus. Words were said that Daegarmo could not hear, but she could quote them if asked.

    Akkirus was unsure of what to do, and turned around to face the elder of the Knights.

    "My Lady, the second team wishes to resign, they state you know the reasons behind the decision, what should we do? All lands must be championed!"

    Lady Daegarmo sighed, knowing the ultimate justice had just been administered. "They may retire in honor, and I accept their resignations. The battle they just engaged in was far worse then any team here could assault upon them. They have been redeemed in my eyes, for they have done what is right. State that they have resigned, do not give a reason why to the other teams, simply let them leave in peace."

    Akkirus nodded and delivered the message as intended.

    Later that night, several hours past the famed event, the Ruler of the Lands of the only team that resigned that day approached an exhausted Lady Daegarmo. He knocked and entered to
    seek advice on how to further handle the situation.

    "Should I take their titles? Their prizes? Their status in our lands? What shall you have us do in the name of justice?"

    Lady Daegarmo remembered to a time long ago when she did wrong, and had to learn from a mistake she made. She remembered the rule she broke, as vividly as the day it happened.

    She could never forget the distrust that was placed against her, and the path to redemption of her honor. The armor she wore during that time didn't shine, her sword felt unjustified for the
    longest time regardless of the great deeds she accomplished with it. Her shield was not worn with the same pride until her redemption was completed.

    "The greatest of titles, the most glorious of prizes, the highest symbols of status cannot hide that which we know is truth, and can not erase the pain that we do to others."

    "The titles and rewards hold not the value to those involved that they do to those who truely earned them, and I assure you that many will hide them, or destroy them because these things will remind them of the victories they could of accomplished."

    "My request is to leave them in place, for each member of that team will redeem themselves someday, and hold those items not of the deeds that they did to accomplish them, but as reminders of the day those things lost their value in the hearts of those that wield them"

    Lady Daegarmo fell asleep before her guest departed, too tired from the days events to stay awake. The elder of most elves knew in her heart, she already forgave them, and that the
    punishment the group would face in their own lands was worse then any should could ever of administered.

    Lady Daegarmo

Gordon Infobits: Sunday Edition
[Post a Comment] - Aurum @ 7:41 PM PST
Here's a few more Gordon Infobits hot off the Gameplay Board for your reading pleasure:

  • Official Tradeskills Board
    Yak reads it quite regularly. Remember however that time he spends reading and replying to issues there is time that he can't spend actually implementing the suggestions. He's a game designer and has a lot of work that he needs to do in addition to replying there, which he does in his own spare time.

  • Why?
    [Quote from original post: Why do MOBS "pop" in and out of view in game. ]

    They actually don't from the NPCs point of view. Warping occurs as a consequence of prediction on the client. The client operates in a fashion such that limited bandwidth is used to display NPC position. The client recieves a package from the server that tells the client the position, vector, and speed of the NPC, and any actions that that NPC takes between packets is predicted. When NPCs change their behavior between packets, that behavior is not relayed to the client until the next packet.

    Currently, updates are sent more often for NPCs and PCs that are in a certain radius from the PC that is observing those NPCs. However, when there is a large number of mobile objects in a close radius, updates cannot be sent instantaneously.

    We're putting some code in Tuesday that will hopefully drastically reduce warping. Updates for group members, along with those for anyone/anything that you have targeted, will be sent far more often. As such, the chance of what you are observing appearing to "warp" should be reduced.

  • Asleep at the Keyboard
    People falling asleep at the keyboard or otherwise forgetting to log off as in your case is a fairly common occurance. The GM staff tries to help out when they can. I'm happy to hear that everything worked out for you.
