#4   Name: Arcticflaeme, Tarew Mar Shaman (www.tidalweb.com/lupine)   Date: 8/9 @ 11:32 AM PST
I looked up several colors for the new modifications.
Each color is expressed as R=red G=green B=blue. You will need all three numbers for each color, even if the numbers are zero.

Black: R=0, G=0, B=0
White: R=255, G=255, B=255

Yellow: R=255, G=255, B=0
Orange: R=255, G=128, B=64

Red: R=255, G=0, B=0
Crimson: R=128, G=0, B=0

Green: R=0, G=255, B=0

Light Sky Blue: R=128, G=255, B=255
Royal Blue: R=0, G=0, B=255
Navy Blue: R=0, G=0, B=160

Medium bluish-purple: R=128, G=128, B=255
Medium lavender: R=128, G=129, B=192
Purple: R=128, G=0, B=128

Medium Pink: R=255, G=128, B=192
Hot Pink: R=255, G=0, B=128

Have fun with it! Hope it helps!

***********************************************************
Here is the file info you need to modify if you choose to:
Modifying this file is optional.

*EQCLIENT.INI documentation*

The [TextColors] section contains entries for various text colors in the client. All colors are to be set with Red, Green, and Blue values, in
decimal. Valid settings for each color range between 0 and 255. "Black" (0 red, 0 green, and 0 blue) will be white in overlay mode, and
black in normal mode.

Here is a list of user colors to use as a guide when editing the file:

1 - Say
2 - Tell
3 - Group
4 - Guild
5 - OOC
6 - Auction
7 - Shout
8 - Emote
9 - Spells (meming, scribing, casting, etc.)
10 - You hit other
11 - Other hits you
12 - You miss other
13 - Other misses you
14 - Some broadcasts (duels)
15 - Skills (ups, non-combat use, etc.)
18 - Default text and stuff you type
20 - Merchant Offer Price
21 - Merchant Buy/Sell
22 - Your death message
23 - Others death message
24 - Other damage other
25 - Other miss other
26 - /who command

The [Defaults] section contains some default setting for the client. Here is a list of valid settings:

RunMode - TRUE for run, FALSE for walk.
ScreenMode - NORMAL for the default window view, FULL for the full view with overlays

- The EverQuest Team

EQLoot
[Post a Comment] - ShadowLight @ 10:31 AM PDT
EQLoot a search engine for EQ items, has opened up.


MINI-PRESS RELEASE

Date: August 9, 2000
Subj: GAMEZ.com Announces EQLoot.com Web Site

We at GAMEZ.com are pleased to announce the release of our newest game
site, EQLoot.com (http://eqloot.com), a search engine for EverQuest
items. EQLoot.com provides EverQuest gamers with a quick and simple way to
locate statistics and information about all known game items such as armor,
weapons, etc. EQLoot.com indexes the best EverQuest game sites that
contain item databases and presents the information in a central database
allowing users to quickly find several links to the items they are after.

If you are searching for information about an item and you want to quickly
find all the links for it in one location, EQLoot.com is worth checking out.

Three's Company Tournament
[3 Comments] - ShadowLight @ 10:12 AM PDT
Bazzer Fox has announced the first Three's Company Tournament.


Hello, I am pleased to announce the plans for Soul
Cadre's first ever Three's Company Tournament on the
Torvonnilous server! The tournament will feature
public team combat, 3 on 3, open to all races and
classes.

Vital Statistics:
Date: Friday, August 18th, 2000
Time: 8p.m. Eastern, 5 pacific
Place: The Arena, off of Lake Rathetear

The tournament's competitors will form their own teams
within various weight classes:
Feather Weights - Level 11-15
Light Weights - Level 16-20
Medium Weights - Level 21-25
Heavy Weights - Level 26-30
Super Heavy Weights - Level 31-35

The level restrictions will be strictly enforced
downwards, but competitors wishing to enter into a
higher weight class than befits their level are free
to do so. For example, a level 25 player is free to
enter into the Heavy Weights, but a level 26 player
may not enter the Medium weights.

Each team will have three players of any race or
class, no more, and no less.

Prizes:
The combatants of each winning team will be
individually rewarded very well, with item or monetary
prizes. Alcohol will be provided to all who desire it!


Basic Tournament Rules will be followed:
No buffs will be allowed prior to combat. Cancel
magic spells will enforce this policy. Noticeably
heavy twinks will also not be allowed to participate.
Magical pets will be allowed for all pet summoning
classes. Interference by a third party in any combat
will result in that person's expulsion from the
tournament, a restart of the combat, and will likely
incur the wrath of the gods. All combatants will be
required to stay on the main platform in the Arena
during their combats, while all spectators will be
asked to remain at the periphery of the platform.

To Sign Up:
Have your team at the Arena by the specified time,
and follow our directions for which Soul Cadre
official you should contact to enter into your chosen
weight class. Each team should appoint a group leader,
who will sign you up, and pay the entrance fee of 5
gold pieces per level of each entrant in the group (
IE 3 level 30's pay 45pp to enter the group). In the
interest of cooperation, and expediting play, please
follow instructions given by the Tournament Officials
on the scene.

We look forward to hosting this event for the

EQLoot
[Post a Comment] - ShadowLight @ 10:31 AM PDT
EQLoot a search engine for EQ items, has opened up.


MINI-PRESS RELEASE

Date: August 9, 2000
Subj: GAMEZ.com Announces EQLoot.com Web Site

We at GAMEZ.com are pleased to announce the release of our newest game
site, EQLoot.com (http://eqloot.com), a search engine for EverQuest
items. EQLoot.com provides EverQuest gamers with a quick and simple way to
locate statistics and information about all known game items such as armor,
weapons, etc. EQLoot.com indexes the best EverQuest game sites that
contain item databases and presents the information in a central database
allowing users to quickly find several links to the items they are after.

If you are searching for information about an item and you want to quickly
find all the links for it in one location, EQLoot.com is worth checking out.

Three's Company Tournament
[3 Comments] - ShadowLight @ 10:12 AM PDT
Bazzer Fox has announced the first Three's Company Tournament.


Hello, I am pleased to announce the plans for Soul
Cadre's first ever Three's Company Tournament on the
Torvonnilous server! The tournament will feature
public team combat, 3 on 3, open to all races and
classes.

Vital Statistics:
Date: Friday, August 18th, 2000
Time: 8p.m. Eastern, 5 pacific
Place: The Arena, off of Lake Rathetear

The tournament's competitors will form their own teams
within various weight classes:
Feather Weights - Level 11-15
Light Weights - Level 16-20
Medium Weights - Level 21-25
Heavy Weights - Level 26-30
Super Heavy Weights - Level 31-35

The level restrictions will be strictly enforced
downwards, but competitors wishing to enter into a
higher weight class than befits their level are free
to do so. For example, a level 25 player is free to
enter into the Heavy Weights, but a level 26 player
may not enter the Medium weights.

Each team will have three players of any race or
class, no more, and no less.

Prizes:
The combatants of each winning team will be
individually rewarded very well, with item or monetary
prizes. Alcohol will be provided to all who desire it!


Basic Tournament Rules will be followed:
No buffs will be allowed prior to combat. Cancel
magic spells will enforce this policy. Noticeably
heavy twinks will also not be allowed to participate.
Magical pets will be allowed for all pet summoning
classes. Interference by a third party in any combat
will result in that person's expulsion from the
tournament, a restart of the combat, and will likely
incur the wrath of the gods. All combatants will be
required to stay on the main platform in the Arena
during their combats, while all spectators will be
asked to remain at the periphery of the platform.

To Sign Up:
Have your team at the Arena by the specified time,
and follow our directions for which Soul Cadre
official you should contact to enter into your chosen
weight class. Each team should appoint a group leader,
who will sign you up, and pay the entrance fee of 5
gold pieces per level of each entrant in the group (
IE 3 level 30's pay 45pp to enter the group). In the
interest of cooperation, and expediting play, please
follow instructions given by the Tournament Officials
on the scene.
