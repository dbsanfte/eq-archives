Standards

 

Gaming”

The FWH Members participate in a variety of different types of on-line games.  Our Home Page will direct you to the main games currently being played by the FWH members.  Please visit our various message boards located under the Public Forum button.  Most of our boards are open to the general public.  Feel Free to post as often as you like.
 

“Membership Procedures”

Membership is limited to a maximum #(to be determined due to our growth in AC) of ‘active’ players plus the leaders.  Upon reaching our maximum membership levels, new candidates will be placed on a waiting list.  New members are not always the ‘next’ person in line on the waiting list as the leaders of the clan (referenced on the ‘leaders’ page) may select anyone from the list to be voted upon.  No new member is admitted into the clan unless they receive at least two ‘yes’ votes from the three leaders.

If you are interested in joining our clan, please contact FWH_Richard and tell him of your interest in the clan.  Or, leave a message for them on our Public Message Board (click on the Forums button).  Richard and the other voting members will want to get to know you and play some games with you.  The voting members are FWH_Deathon, FWH_Ares (AKA Silent), and FWH_Richard.  After we get to know you, individual votes are cast by the three leaders.  Upon voting, the appropriate candidate will be notified of the decision.  New members will also be posted on our message board welcoming them into the clan along with a request for all FWH members to ‘bookmark’ your new FWH name.

It is also important to play with as many FWH members as possible as our members let the leaders know whom they think should be accepted or rejected.  We listen closely to our members and their opinions weigh heavily in the decision making process.
 

“Honor List”

Players who are no longer active within the clan, but wish to remain in the clan are placed on our FWH “Honor” list on the Members page.  They can continue to play under the FWH name so long as they continue to uphold the FWH standards.  Any “Honor” member may be immediately re-instated as a “Warrior” on the Warrior list (upon their request) even if this creates a situation where we exceed our ‘maximum’ clan membership limits.  We do not turn away our friends.  Any inactive members who wish to leave the clan altogether will be permanently removed from the Warrior and/or Honor lists.
 

“Standards”

Our members strive to fight with honor and integrity.  We don’t use cheats unless all players agree to them.  We beat our enemies to a pulp fairly and squarely, with no quarter given.

While we avoid yelling back at angry opponents, we will and do retaliate against an abusive player.  We are after all human.  At times, we have also taken abusive players aside after games and had long talks with them regarding conduct and sportsmanship.  This has generally led to a positive resolution to an otherwise unpleasant situation.  We have and will continue to eliminate any clan members who are overly abusive to other players or who create unacceptable playing situations.

We are interested in strong players as well as ‘up and coming’ players who are learning the ropes.  They don’t have to be the best of players, but we do expect them to be the most honorable of Warriors.  Having said that, we don’t lose too often as a team of 2 or 3 players.  When we do lose, it’s usually a fun game for all while the victors seem to appreciate the conquest.

We have made many friends on the zone and within our clan.  Indeed, some friendships have become long-lasting friendships, which of course makes the gaming experience that much more enjoyable.  After all, in the end, it’s not about war, but about peace and friends.
