Stat Overview:

Iksars come out as having the most smarts which will mean you will have more mana and be able to raise in other non-melee skills quicker. Trolls have the worst starting wis and wont have as much mana. The other races are all average in there wis.

Ogres have the best Sta and Str which will make them better meleers. They won't run out of stamina as quicky when using heavy weapons. They also have the most HP. Iksars are the weakest and least ammount of hp of the beastlord classes. Trolls and Barbs also have higher hp then the other races but not as much as ogres. Cats are average.

Iksar and Vah Shir have highest Agi which will give them slightly better ac, but Iksars also have there ac bonus which is even better. Ogres have the worst agi.

Vah Shir have the best Cha which will cut down the cost of items compared to the ogres who have the worst. This isnt very important in the later levels but it can make an untwinked newbie char's life easier to have higher cha.

Dex - Not the most important stat, helps procing weapons go off more often, but not drasticly much more. It also helps raise melee skills quicker the higer it is. Iksars have the highest in it.

So it basicly comes down to this:
Iksar: Best Mana and AC but weak
Ogres: Best Str and HPs. but lower mana and worst ac
Trolls: Better Str HP then ave, but worst mana
Barbarian: Average but little more str and hp
Vah Shir: Average but little more ac

Race Abilities:

Barbarian:
Gods: Mithaniel Marr, The Tribunal

EXP Mod: +5% extra to level

Good:
Slam: This is good for stunning mobs since BL kick can't stun.

Good faction in all good cities: Makes life easier. Easy to find banks and vendors.

Bad:
Blind in dark: Means your forced to carry light stones with you until the high levels when BL get spells to fix it. Still hard to see in outdoor zones even with light stones.

Iksar:
Gods: Cazic-Thule

EXP mod: +20% extra to level

Good:
Regen: Double the hp gain is always good at any level. Its less mana you and others have to spend on healing. Mostly usefull while medding then healing yourself after your done medding.

Swimming starts at 100: Means less hassle trying to learn it later. Most of us will need swimming eventually. Very usefull on PVP servers where it can be an early edge over slower swimming foe. But its still nothing too usefull when your fighting out of water.

Forage: Capped at 50 and its only usefull before we get summon food and water. Not really important at all.

Scale skin AC bonus: Iksars have an AC bonus which isnt anything to ignore since BLs armor selection isnt all that great. Its about +1 ac every other level, at level 60 they would have an extra 40 ac.

Infravision: Lets you see in the dark well enough to get around. Not as good as Ultravision.

Bad:
+20% Exp Penalty: Tied with Trolls for the worst exp penalty in the game. Just means you will level a little slower then ther other BL races which most have bigger then average exp penalties too.

Hated: Iksars are KOS in all cities in the old world and all cities in Kunark except for Cabilis. Its hard to get good faction in most cities. But they like Iksars in Temple of Cazic Thule. They are not KOS in Ice Dorf town in Velious and Shadowhaven and Cat town on the moon.

Cant wear plate: Well since beastlords cant wear plate anyway this doesnt really much matter. But there are a few plate wrist items other BL races can use. Not a big loss for Iksars though.

Ogres:
Gods: Rallos Zek

EXP Mod: +15% extra to level

Good:
Slam: This is good for stunning mobs since BL kick can't stun.

Cant be stunned from front: Less stuns is good.

Lots o' hit points: This drives most clerics insane too.

Infravision: Lets you see in the dark well enough to get around. Not as good as Ultravision.

Bad:
Evil: Only not KOS in Dark Elf, Troll, and Ogre Cities. Also not KOS in Evil fort in Kunark, Ice dorf town in Velious, and some cities on Luclin

Big: Only an annoyance most of the time. You will get stuck more often in dungeons then the little people will. And you get in other peoples way.

Other Considerations:

Iksars have the best level 1-20 hunting grounds then any other race. They will have it easiest in thoes levels finding mobs to kill. Field of bone is the best newbie zone in the game. Also the Vah Shir have good hunting grounds. The moon has well designed zones. Barbarians have the third best hunting grounds with lots of weak animals that drop items for tailoring.

Most Beastlords are also Vah Shir since there new, so if you dont like being the new Fad race then don't pick the cats. At least 80% of beastlords are Vah Shir on my server.

Vah Shir currently have the best Quests. The other races quest seemed like Verant spent 10 min making.

Most Beastlord weapons and armor drop on the moon, So plan on taking a Lunar module to the place eventually. Vah Shir start there so they have better access to the items. But the moon is a very laggy place on most peoples computers. Might be very annoying to go there. I get mixed reports on how good the zones there are for camping and exp. Most say its ok exp. others say it sucks.
