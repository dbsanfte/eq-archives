Haste Conversion Chart

The following table lists a rough conversion between damage haste and delay haste and swings per minute.

The chart is broken down by weapon delay in increments of 5 and beginning at 10 delay.  There are two basic ways to use the char;

    1.  Round the weapon's delay to the nearest increment of 5, and cross reference to find the rough effect at various levels of haste.  For example, assume you have a Gnoll Hide Lariat which has a delay of 20.  You would find the chart that lists Orig Delay of 20.  Cross referencing on the chart, you can determine the rough effect of say an FBSS, which gives 21% damage haste or 17% delay haste.

    2.  Round an item's permanent hate to the nearest increment of 5, and cross reference to find the rough delay effect at various weapon delays.  For example, assume you have a Cloak of Flames, which gives 36% Damage Haste. Cross referencing the chart, you find that it has a delay haste of 26%.  You can then go a bit further and assume that you have a Gnoll Hide Lariat again.  The new delay for this weapon becomes 15, and you go from 30 swings per minute to 41 swings per minute.
