very new applicant must read the following before applying for a soul.

The following information should be contained in your post:
  • Full name(s) of primary and all other characters in the Prexus community
  • Other guilds in which you have been a member in the past
  • The name of your sponsor
  • Why you are interested in joining our guild
  • Applicants must have reviewed our Charter and Codes of Conduct and agree to them fully. Please indicate this in your post

Application Requirements:

  • We do not accept applications from those who are currently guilded. Guild shopping is frowned upon; while you are seeking membership in HoS, refrain from applying to other guilds
  • Your primary character must be at least level 56. YOU MUST BE 56 BEFORE YOU POST FOR APPLICATION.
  • Have one sponsor that is a current full member in Harmony of Souls. This person is responsible for mentoring you through the application process.

Now that you’ve posted your application, what’s next?


On the 21st of the month, we clean the application forum. Every application is MOVED to our private discussion area. If your application is NOT here, that means it is in the discussion area being considered. To be accepted into the guild, you need to have one sponsor and ten supporters. We vote privately and confidentially on applications and this information isconfidential; please do not ask a guildmember to disclose this information.

On the first of the month, there will be one post listing all approved applicants. If you are not on that list, then your application, for some reason, was not approved. The most common cause of this is that not enough HoS had positive input about you.

 

You Are an Apprentice Now!

The sponsor will serve as the applicant's mentor during the apprenticeship phase. Apprenticeship will last for a minimum period of 2 weeks and may be extended for up to 2 more weeks at the discretion of the guild officers. During that time the apprentice has the following restrictions:

  • No 2nd character may be added to the guild.
  • An apprentice may not sponsor another member to the guild.
  • An apprentice has no voting rights within the guild regarding guild decisions.

If at the end of the apprenticeship period, that apprentice has proven honorable and worthy and no objection has been made, the apprentice will be granted a Soul and all the benefits associated with full membership. If full membership is denied, the apprentice will be notified by the Recruitment Officer and removed from the guild.
