Guild By-Laws

ll members of Harmony of Souls are required to abide by these laws. Any prospective member is asked to review them prior to induction.

Begging
Begging from other players is not tolerated, either within the guild or outside. Begging is not to be confused with a request for assistance. All members of the guild are free to ask for help at any time, but no member is obligated to assist another in any way. Members are expected to use their own judgment when making requests, but such requests are subject to interpretation by the online membership.

Retaliation
No members of Harmony should take it upon themselves to seek punitive retaliation against another player. In the cases of a legitimate grievance, seek the assistance of a GM. If you are not sure how to manage a situation, consult one of the Harmony officers. It is the officer's role to seek a peaceful and fair resolution to the conflict.

Kill Stealing
Kill stealing from other players will not be tolerated. This rule applies to intentional killing of another players pull or hunt. It also applies to usurping of a spawn without permission either in a group or solo. This rule is specifically exempt in free-for-all zones. However, even in those zones you should try to find a fair and equitable solution to disputes.

In the case of an accidental kill steal, which happens, you must make a point to apologize to the wronged player. In the interest of fairness, you may wish to offer that player the option to loot the corpse.

If you are the victim of kill stealing, please make the appropriate petition to a GM, or consult with one of the Harmony officers.

Language
Norrath is made up of a completely mixed population men, women and children. Therefore it is improper behavior to use profanity in any form of public communications including; shout, out-of-character, guild say and say. It is also frowned upon in tell.

The guild is diverse. People will say things that you perhaps wouldn't, but that is their right as an individual citizen. However, there are certain lines that should not be crossed. Derogatory comments about race, religion, and sexual orientation will not be tolerated. It is contrary to our guild's objective to create an environment that makes any individual unconfortable. As members, you should seek to make a pleasant experience for yourself, and your guildmates.




Conduct Violations
If any officer is a witness to, or has a report made to him/her regarding a violation by any guild member, a warning will be issued. If the behavior persists, actions will be taken against that member that may result in:

    • Penance to the wronged individual
    • A demotion or revokation of that members rank
    • Removal of that member from the guild
