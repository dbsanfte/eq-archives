armony of Souls is an open enrollment guild. All races and classes may find a niche within our guild. The guild exists to help members of similar values and beliefs to form strong, long lasting friendships.

We place one demand upon our members... you must actively seek to create deep and meaningful friendships with others. Through friendship and mutual respect, our guild will grow and flourish.
