Raid Ranks
ur guild follows a strict chain of command during multi-group camps and raids. Each group member answers to the group leader (typically a ranked member), the group leader then answers to the raid leader. This chain of command is not to be circumvented no matter what the circumstances.

Each ranked guild member has gone through a series of cooperative training and understands how to coordinate with each other. During these camps, each group leader will designate a second in command.

 

aid Captains are guild members who have successfully arranged and managed raids between the levels 30-39. This position is given by the acting senior raid members, and is not automatic.
