Beware the Jabberwock! 
Monday, March 13, 2006, 09:05 AM
In a stunning display of "this will never work" HoS has tallied another 1st in Ahn'Qiraj by defeating Moam on our first try Saturday evening. I couldn't have planned our first Moam kill any better. Well done ma raidas!

Following our victory we headed over to Ossirian for a look-see and were summarily executed. I, myself, was only stomped for 6669 damage. Glorious!

I'll beakdown loot in the recap thread tonight but it was a reasonably good outing. Two warrior and druid books and a new backstabby rogue! Grats Uzed on swanky new paladin legs, and grats to our first(?) epic-exalted-faction-weapon-turn-in-thing.
