/random 1 100 isn't so random after all. 
Friday, July 21, 2006, 05:39 PM - Fun Stuff



It was a dark and dank night in the bowels of The Molten Core. A proud band of adventurers were fighting their way to challenge Lord Ragnaros. In the aftermath of a battle with one of Ragnaros' minions, our staunch warriors found a piece of suitable armor on a corpse.

Proud of their discovery and eager to to see which warrior would have the right of wearing such a finely crafted piece of metal, our protectors rolled their dice.

Morbus, with a glint in his eye, watched as his die turned up a 96. Silently he cheered, confident that he would finally get to wear this piece he so desired. We cheered the result of his roll as we stood beside him.

Then silence fell across the room.......the next warrior threw his dice down and.....BAM!....another 96 turns up. Serrat giggled to himself as he watched a bead of sweat, not the result of the recent battle, roll down Morbus' cheek....a cheek that, just moments before, flanked one side of a steadfast smile.

Awash with surprise, dread and a sinking feeling, Morbus watched as his smaller comrade, Bellisarius tossed down his dice. DOUBLE BAM.....another 96.

Screams of laughter, shock and awe filled the chamber as Morbus collapsed to knees. Sobbing silently, our most steadfast warrior could feel the muscles in his throat constrict as his nerves began to quiver.

Morbus could not believe his luck...........
