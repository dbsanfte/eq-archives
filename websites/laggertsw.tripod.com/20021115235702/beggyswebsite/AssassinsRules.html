Assassins Guild Charter

Rules for all Assassins

1. No unwanted attacking (direct or indirect) of other assassins. Except inhumes.

2. Any problems between assassins should be taken to a GM/HM. If the problem involves a GM/HM, then it should be taken to a council made up of older assassins.

3. No assassin shall attack any other player killer at random (no reason) unless the victim has stated beforehand that they do not object.

4. If another player steals non replaceable items from an assassin, fails to steal from an assassin, or if someone attacks an assassin and then runs away, the assassin has full guild permission to hunt down and kill that player and take their stuff back. Any other PKing is discouraged, but the guild accepts that there are special situations. The guild would prefer that you negociated with the person in question before killing them though.

5. Corpse looting/dragging is also discouraged, but the guild accepts that there are times when this is the only way to teach someone a lesson. We do ask you return their quest items though.

6. No assassin shall discuss the contents of The Book with a non-assassin, on penalty of death.

7. No assassin shall reveal the details of The Run to anyone.

8. Any personal feuds that an assassin has gotten into before the charter has been accepted by the guild are now void. This charter gives your enemies a clean slate. Do not attack them for anything they did before the charter was law. HM/GMs are available on request to help mediate feuds.

Housemasters

1. They serve as mediators between assassins in their house and other assassins/non guildmembers.

2. Advise, Train, Help out and provide information for any assassins not just those in their house.

3. Deal with non guildmembers who steal quest items, or attack an assassin after an assassin has come to the housemaster for help

4. Discipline members of house if necessary.

Guildmaster

1. Settle anything the housemasters cannot do.

2. Any problems with assassins from non guildmembers that cannot be dealt with between the assassin and person should be taken to GM.

3. Discipline housemasters (with aproval of other housemasters).

Powers of Guild

1. The Guildmaster can "sanction" assassins/non assassins by their own decision or by request of a housemaster.

Voting GM/HM to office

1. At the request of 2 other housemasters or the guildmaster, a vote for a new housemaster can be called.

2. At the request of 3 housemasters, a vote for a new guildmaster can be called.

Non Assassins

1. Any revenge against assassins for a successful inhume, robbing an assassin of items they can not get again, or randomly killing an assassin will be dealt with extreme prejudice by the guild.

Note: Extreme prejudice is 1 warning, and then death.
