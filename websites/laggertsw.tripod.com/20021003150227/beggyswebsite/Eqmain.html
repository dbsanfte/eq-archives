Everquest Pages



 
 
 
 

Everquest is a online computer roleplaying game set in a fantasy world of Norrath, a world where swords and  magic prevail against beasts that would slit your throat as soon as look at you.  The world is populated by many races of creatures who are all played by actual users from anywhere in the world who fight against the computer controlled characters (NPC's) (against each other also in the Player Vs Player Servers) to gain experience points which allow them to increase their lvl and become more and more powerful.  Verant have made the game so in order to achieve certain goals you will have to interact with other players, for example to take down a dragon you would need around 30+ people to even stand a chance, solo and you would be dead in around 2 seconds.

Once you are set up and ready to play you must first create a character.  Dont just rush into this as you may scar your character in the long run if your not sure what you are doing.

Starting Out

Once you have your character set up its time to log into Norrath and start to earn a living.  Your first task will be to find your guild in your starting city and hand the leader a note in order to join that said guild, for example if you picked a Ogre Shaman you would start in Oggok and would have to locate the shaman NPC who is hiding in the cave complex.  Once this is done you will be given a small amount of exp and usually hailing the Guild Leader will produce small quests he/she may ask you to do.  Once that is done arm yourself with your starting equipment and head on out into the newbie area, this is a small area usually around the starting citys with low level monsters that you can practice on and gain a few starting levels.  NOTE: the consider button is your friend, consider ALL monsters that you are thinking about attacking before you do so, the colour and message will tell you if you stand a chance against that said creature.

At this point dont worry if you die, u wont lose any exp (till lvl 5) and you will start again with your starting equipment.  Loot everything the monsters drop and sell them to the NPC vendors for cash, it wont be much but at this point you need everything you can get.  DONT buy from vendors (armour, weapons), as if you /auction for stuff there will no doubt be higher lvl characters about that can make or sell you items better than what the vendors sell you and for alot cheaper.  Dont play the game single player, its a multiplayer game and thus should be treated as thus, if you see other people around your level wandering around then ask if they would like to group up, grouping up allows you to kill creatures you could not normally kill for better loot and better exp.

Here is a list of the most common zones I hunted in with my shaman, they got me to lvl 60 in no time for a none twink and are the best exp zones in the game.
 
Feerrott (ogre newbie zone) Levels 1 to around 8 or 9

Oasis Levels 12 to 20
Lake of Ill Omen Levels 20 to 30
Frontier Mountains Levels 30 to 35
Overthere Levels 35 to 47
Dreadlands Levels 47 to current level of 51
Sebellis / Velketors Laberinth / City of Mist Levels 51 to 60

By the time you reach around 10th level you should have the game mechanics imprinted in your head, go exploring, meet new friends, join a guild and most of all HAVE FUN ! :)
