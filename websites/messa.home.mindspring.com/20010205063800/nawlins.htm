Nawlins Mojogun

 

Once upon a time, in what seems like a dream ago, I was known as Paul.

I created computer graphics and composed music for computer games. I had begun a new project in my studio in New Orleans. It was to be called The RP-RPG. (The Real Person role-playing game). It was during the final beta CD ROM burn that the men in white coats grabbed me. I managed to stuff the CD in my shorts just as they threw the net over me.


When I came to my senses, I was in the "New Orleans Sanitarium for the RPG Challenged and Addicted" and embarrassingly dressed in a paper-based frock (with a slit up the back).


Nurse Ally would come in once a day and take all the programmers out for exercise, which consisted of gurney races to the roof of the sparkling white sanitarium. On my first race to the roof with the huge weight of nurse Ally on the gurney I noticed that she really enjoyed the ride because she kept screaming things like "Oh Yes", and "That's how I like it, danger...DANGER!" I thought this rather kinky, even for the French Quarter. (But then, we really do have a streetcar named Desire.) I also noticed from the roof top vista that there were no roads in or out of the sanatarium grounds. There was an oil stained helipad on the roof.

I was completely surrounded by a great dismal swamp!


Perhaps I was considered dangerous, but to whom?


Then one fateful night during one of our famous little hurricanes, while in the midst of the gurney race with nurse Ally screaming "Oh Yea Baby" and such...I took a quick right into the service elevator...determined to set a new record for the trip to the roof. As luck would have it, the electricity flashed on and off many times due to the raging storm and the elevator began to plummet down the shaft.

Nurse Ally became insistent. Screaming louder now and tearing at my paper frock (with the slit up the back).

The free falling elevator must have fallen through the security level. Just as the electricity and the elevator lights flashed wildly on and off again; my fall was broken by the rotund nurse Ally who had maneuvered into a position just below me. The jerk of the rapid deceleration was a welcome surprise for both of us. As the now suddenly calm nurse lit a cigarette, she said "Go and tell not a soul". The elevator door opened and to my delight I found that I was on the lobby level. I made for the wind buffeted entrance doors as fast as a Cajun peels shrimp and made it out before the security locks and cameras flashed back on with renewed generator backup power.


Unfortunately, I found myself stripped half-naked in the torn paper frock (with the slit up the back) that used to say 'New Orleans Sanitarium' on it and in the middle of a great dismal swamp that was in the throws of a violent hurricane. I ran with unusual swiftness with a 100 mile per hour wind at my back.


When the sun rose over the dismal swamp I was as lost as Dixieland band with sheet music. I managed to save the CD of RP-RPG and piece together a swimsuit looking affair from the tattered paper frock (with the slit up the back) using tree sap and bog mud. It now said "NawlinS" in bits and scraps across the front, but at least I managed to keep the nifty little pocket to protect my CD.


Survival was rugged. I was reduced to drinking bog water and eating frogs. The combination had a strange effect on my speech as my tongue swelled and I itched and scratched away the bog mud constantly.


And so it went... I was getting dimmer and loosing control of my hygiene, my speech and my mind.


I began to say tuf like, Gee, me wish fo clean drinkz and bigrz frogz.... when one day, me runz into a bigz ugley womenz lady troll. Her name bez Kaglari.
She saz " Hey U! U bez in bad wayz..Cum wif mez."
I saz,"Uh Uh..u bez tu ugley fo me. U beez worzt den nurz Ally!"
She say "Mez?" and she grab Naw by his diaper n drag him to water and den she say "lookz in dat water felcksion." Me duz..and me see annuder troll!

But him looks jus like Naw!


Yub, it all tru !


Kaglari take me to her guildz hall n teach Naw sum wizdumz. She gib Naw a new tunik and teach me to summon clean drinkz and blak bredz. She show Naw howz to bash froggz and makz pickled frogluks.

.... Me lub dat part....


Den she telz Naw, "Goz getz me sum bonz and frogluk skinz and when ya duz dat putz all dem roamin bad skelz bak in me clozet agin one motime agin 4 timez." So Naw duz dis alot n getz bonkt on da head by dem lightz briginz noblez in da swampz ...alot.

An den one day Kaglari say," Yu bez 20 Naw..me call yo MoJo man now and gib ya da nu God Ogun to plez alot." She splain dat Ogun be da Voodoo God o' war and dat me bettr beez a gud shaman troll cause him gotz da gud mojo and juju. And, if'n me beez reel gud n sqush out da lightz briginz noblez, him might hepme makz da bigz potion what makz everbodez nut kill ya so muchz til ya cantz get no older.

Den massa Tnayr find me squshn dem lightz briginz elfz in da swampz in frontz of da entrnanz ta GUK. Him saz me bash gud and did me wanna be da Keeper o' da Darkness (sic: WInds of Change). Me sez "Yub" and now me servz da massa Tnayr, Ogun, Innoruk, and Kaglari. Me gotz lotzo frenz wut dunt bonkt Naw. Naw hep newbiez trollz nut getz bonkt so bad by dem nastez lightz elfz no more. Me hatz elfz..speshel dem high noblez wut be makinz newbiez nut get no older.

Ogun and Kaglari gib Naw da power o' majik. So if ya beez wun o' dem druish elfz wolfiez wut kill Troll Basherz.....

ya better bez lookin for da Naw!

Cuz him turn ya back inta a wimpy lil druish princess right wen da Basherz want yaz da worzt!


Well, dat me storiez and me stickin wif it. Naw be goin on 50 now and him hearz in da wind dat der bez a new landz called da painz o' air and on dat painz der iz da bigz flyin ting dat dropz da Laptop o' Intel. So Naw bez getin olderz and waitz fo expanshunz n hope dat hez bez got dat, cuz himz wantz ta play dat RP-RPG.


....and get a real life
