The Quest

(or spoiler below)

Okay, first talk to Aradune in the Plane of Fear's temple. He'll tell you to go to Lil Honeybugger and abort her child for a rogue in Neriak.

Once you get state license for this, you must hail her and say "Aradune deserves more of our money!" 50 times. She then replies by saying "Indeed! I need you to go and collect six Griffawn Wings, which are no drop, no trade, no rent, lore items that last for five minutes after picking them up."

Once you do that, you get a "Stevie Wonder Idol" in return, which contains 6 charges of "Illusion: Stevie". The idol goes in your second hand slot, and is DEX+34, CHA -105, and STA +45. In Illusion: Stevie modes, bards cannot sing their songs and you must pay merchants to look at their shops.

Give the Stevie Wonder Idol to Aradune, and he will tell you "Watermelon [circus] cannot stop Smedley phlegm."

Reply "Brad McQuaid is God on Earth" and he will say "Yes, indeed, Meldrath is [very old]."

Reply to this "The player-run economy is the kewlest" and he will tell you that you need to bring him the Undies of Ungar off of a level 89 Ogre named Ungar, who powergames all day long. Ungar can be found killing Lord Innoruuk over and over again in the Hate Plane.

Hail him and ask for the Undies, and he tells youto bring him a Troll Diaper in exchange. Kill any newbie Troll and bring him the diaper. You get the Undies of Ungar, a no drop lore item that causes you to consume food three times as fast and adds armor class 145. It looks like halfling platemail.

Give Aradune the Undies, and he will reply that you now need to get him the favor of all the players and make them worship his player run economy. Here is where you can make the quest easier. Say "Richard Garriot can be given your PIN number" and he will reply that you are an evil sumbitch, and that if you bring him the head of Richard Garriot and the Ego of Richard Garriot, you will get the Holy Mace.

First, kill Richard Garriot. His head is a No Lore, No Drop, No Happy, No Eat, No Numkins item. After killing Richard Garriot, you must kill the Ego of Richard Garriot that spawns. It looks like a giant willowisp and has several pets, all named things like "BoobiesRcool" and"myDikcankillU". If you kill The Ego of Richard Garriot, you get "Richie's Ego". Richie's Ego is a no everything item, that contains unlimited charges of Charm Self.

Combine both in the Bottle of Toilet Duck you got from Aradune, give it to him, and you get 3 copper and a first rank shaman spell. This spell is currently broken.

P.S. Be sure to get screenshots of all transactions so they can do you no good in case the quest is broken.
