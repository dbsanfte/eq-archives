Welcome to the Dragon Knights guild website. We are a small band of friends that have joined together for a common goal, to enjoy ourselves while playing games.

We currently play Dark Age of Camelot on the Merlin server in the realm of Midgard.

The old EQ site is still stored at eq.dragonknights.org if you wish to see some of our roots.
