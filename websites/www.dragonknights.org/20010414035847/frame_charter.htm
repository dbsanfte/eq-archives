In times of turmoil such as these, it is our call to carve order from chaos.  There are many forces in Norrath vying for dominance, but we will have our will known.  We are a guild not bound by race, but unified in mind and manifest in strength that flashes in greatness on the battlefield.  Our bond to our guild mates is supreme and the power we possess exists in a realm beyond the senses of mortals.  Our deeds speak of a heritage from which grand stories are told.  Through blood spilt and souls transmuted our fates are bound one to the other.  Let Norrath learn to fear the Dragon Knights.
Raids
Many in Norrath are content to spend vast amounts of time with safer endeavors, such as camping and farming. We the Dragon Knights prefer the invasion, the Raid! The challenge of a raid gets the blood boiling and burns away the excess of the body and the mind. Through this we elevate the group as a whole to a single fighting entity capable of seeking newer and more immense challenges.

This is not to discount the need for updated gear and equipment. Indeed we pull together to gain those items. And we cannot discount the experience needed to attain the next level of progression. Indeed we pull together to this end also. But, be you mindful, these are but secondary to the Raid.
Time Zones
So that all may attend we make effort to schedule raids appropriate to our members. Though days of the week are important in this regard, it is our experience that the time of the event is critical to a successful campaign. Most of us are on the Pacific Coast (PST) while others may be found oversees in Europe. And yet some others are closer to the East Coast. On this I must be blunt and get to the point, most raids are scheduled on Wednesday or Friday at 8pm PST. Weekend raids will generally start around 11am PST. There will be exceptions as we cherish our existing members for whom this is a problem. But, I ask that you seriously consider this when seeking membership.
Squads
As one guild we venture forth, but as independent squads – working together – we conquer. Just as 6 Knights form a squad, so also do squads constitute the guild. Just as a single Knight of a squad plays a role, each squad is capable of acting independently as a self-sufficient entity. Once accepted among our family you will be assigned to a squad.
Level
We strongly encourage all potential Dragon Knights to attain level 50 before seeking acceptance. In the members section of our site you may notice several people not of this level. Be at ease however with the knowledge that they are being trained by sponsors within the guild.

We also recognize that many people stay together, friends in arms. If you have such a group please feel free to apply as a group. Friends who fight together make a truly deadly squad and we will make every effort to ensure you stay together while maintaining class balance.
Loot!
There have been many duels fought, friends parted, and tears shed over the right to jump in and claim the prizes of war. Alas reward is sorely missed for a battle well fought. 'Tis a tough and difficult decision to award a glorious item to one and and not another. The High Council has debated this manner for many moons seeking to be fair and just, being true to our fellows in arms. One cannot discount long standing and unflagging devotion. One cannot discount the merit that is bestowed to one just entering our ranks. We strive together as a family and we cannot deny reward to any of our number. In this vein, the Loot Officer of the High Council will enforce our policy and bestow items according to the policy set forth by the High Council.

For most items, those discovered during normal course, a simple lottery is exercised. Members included will generally be limited to those who are participating in the raid and of a class best suited to the items use. Of the members meeting this criteria, ones who have previously won (any item) will be excluded until all members of the class have been awarded an item. Once all members of that class have received an item (any item) all are allowed back into the lottery for the next item. Members who will not put the item to use are asked to voluntarily exclude themselves from the lottery. In the event an item drops which may be passed from one to another (droppable) and all participating member most suited to its use own the item, then that item may be awarded to either another member in participation, held for a member not participating, or deposited in the Guild Treasury at the discretion of the Loot Officer.

On occasion some in attendance will not be a Dragon Knight, but this does not exclude them from the treasures of war all together. At the discretion of the Loot Officer these people may be included in an item lottery. However, this will likely be done only after attending members have received such an item. Indeed, many fine people have been awarded items on our journeys.

Precious and rare loot (e.g., Rare Dragon Loot, BP, Robe, Rare weapon) is seldom discovered and is cherished above all else. All our members fight hard and die for the cause, but some more than others. Some uphold our honor daily while others are seen less often. Extraordinary effort and contribution to the guild cannot be discounted. For this reason rare items are awarded based purely on Guild Seniority. Seniority is based upon many factors and all must be counted: long standing, participation, and contribution. The High Council decides seniority based on all of these things and rare items will be awarded accordingly.

Finally, if an item is awarded but no longer needed by that individual (and the item is droppable) then we ask that the item be returned to the Guild for re-distribution to another member. If you find such an item in your inventory please notify the Loot Officer for guidance.
Joining Dragon Knight
Times change... What once was, is no more - and in its place we strive to achieve something greater. We strive for something more complete and whole, more perfect...

The Dragon Knights now stand as a proud symbol in our land. What once began as a small unity has risen to a mighty roar. The name Dragon Knights inspires many to rally at our side and push the evils of our land deeper into the shadows. Brave souls from across Norrath have proven their bravery and valor in battle. These warriors have proven devotion to our cause and have been welcomed into our fold where they proudly stand with us. In number we are numerous and with our unity we are strong.

Now reaching to greater achievements we strive to mold and temper the steel of our friendship. This, so that we may pull even closer together in spirit - creating a bond no war, nor death, nor evil god may pull apart... With increased size is achieved strength - to a point. And at that point additional size becomes a weakness. A weakness that is, until the core of the warrior is again placed on the forge to be tempered even stronger still.

In recognition of this cycle we are now choosing to severely limit additions to our guild so that this necessary part of the cycle may be fulfilled. All is not bleak however - for many weary travelers well deserve a place in our midst. A new cycle for acceptance has been enacted and, though the journey may be longer, still provides light in the lonely darkness.

For those wishing to gain acceptance a Dragon Knight sponsor must be found. Our members do not take this responsibility lightly to be sure. I will leave it in your care to determine the best way to achieve this friendship and trust. Dragon Knights hold certain values in very high esteem, such as honesty, and it is these values which provide a basis for acceptance.

Once a sponsor is found you should feel free to post your application on our message board. Be sure to include the sponsor and any information about yourself. As was custom before, it is still required that potential recruits join us on the field of battle. In battle the true nature of the warrior is revealed and it is the core of the individual we wish to know.

As a sponsored applicant you are encouraged to attend raids and make every effort to team with our members. Actively seek Dragon Knights for company so that we come to know each other. In this rely upon your sponsor to make your introductions. If your sponsor is not available seek a member of the High Council (primarily Mystica or Garro). During this time our members will be invited to comment on your worthiness to join us. In this arena your past deeds may come to boost your effort or deny it. There is no set duration for this process except to say when we know you well enough your application will be decided.

Please take heed, we take these steps to limit those who would join us. Few will succeed where others may lose interest. We do this for those reasons I have mentioned and no other. If you are denied acceptance it may well be that we open our doors to you in times yet to come. For those who choose to endure this process the rewards are great and in our midst may you find peace.
Sponsors
As a Dragon Knight you know the fabric that binds us and the quality of people who now fight at your side. Through the pain of battle you have learned to trust us, even though death may encroach steadily nearer. Even in the most dire of times we stand as one and endure. We seek the greater good in all things.

Perhaps you find these qualities in another, not of our guild. If you come to trust them as you trust us, if you see the true heart of a Dragon in them - then you may stand proudly at their side as a sponsor. It is the wish of the High Council that you carry the title of Dragon Knight for at least one month before becoming a sponsor and that you only sponsor one person at a time. As a sponsor you are asking us to depend on them as we do you.

In becoming a sponsor you are also accepting additional responsibility. A sponsored applicant likely does not know many of us - so we ask that you introduce them to others. If the applicant is to attend raids or general activities they must know of such things. Please keep them informed and make every effort to include them as appropriate. Ensure that they understand our charter and that any questions they may have are answered. These things we place in your care until the application is decided.
