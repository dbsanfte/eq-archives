Gorenaire had spawned and was chasing a couple of us around in the Dreadlands trying to get some revenge, so we dispatched Dragon Force to teach her a lesson.  The fight with Gorenaire is always interesting.  She can be a real pain in the ass or she can be a major wuss.  The raid went perfectly this time and she was a major wuss.  We only suffered one casualty.  She was so embarrassed she died with her head buried in the ice.

March 27th, 2001 - Patch Day!
The patch seems like a pretty good one.  The server side filtering seems to be working and now we can finally see particle effects on epic weapons but still have spell effects turned off.  Also a couple new powerful spells for wizards and we finally get the self only port spell to Dreadlands -- 8 months after Kunark goes live.  Anyway, we spent the day testing out the changes in the patch.  Brik tested out his Fearsome shield and sure enough, it had been nerfed.  Felcor tested out his Incarnadine BP and that had been nerfed too.  Dragon Force tested out the server side filtering in a Trakanon raid which seemed to be working well.  Finally, Eldain tested out some mod rods to see if they too had been nerfed...they hadn't.

March 25th, 2001 - Big Update!

Dragon Force has been pretty busy lately but I've been slacking on the updates cuz I've been really sick for the past few days.  I took lots of medicine and I'm feeling a lot better today so I thought I'd just make one big update.  Ok let's start with Trakanon who we had a little bad luck with.  We killed him but because of a little miscommunication, he wasn't alone so we all pretty much ended up dying afterwards.  We were able to get the Mrylokar's BP that went to Maglan but a CoP and some teeth rotted.  No big deal, there will be many more.

And today Dragon Force defeated Woushi and Severilous.  Congrats Dragon Force!
