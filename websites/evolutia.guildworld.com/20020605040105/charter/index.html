Evolutia Prime

The Evolution of Norrath has come upon us.

Good must join forces to Overcome the Evil of the land. Evolutia Prime will unite all those in Norrath that have not seen their destiny. The path that we follow has been chosen for us from the time we were born on to this land we call home. Our path leads us down a road of uniting the "Elite" of Norrath.

Evolutia Prime's Destiny is as follows:

  • Learn to use your skills to their fullest.
  • Use your skills for the good of Norrath.
  • Enrich the lives of other Adventurers of Norrath.
  • Never turn away from someone who needs help.
  • Help others see their destiny in Norrath.
  • Teach others how to use their skills. Make them the "Elite" of Norrath.
  • See in others what they cannot see in themselves.
  • In battle, fight with Honor. Never run from a fight where others may die.
  • Be proud of who you are...NEVER hide your tag of Honor
