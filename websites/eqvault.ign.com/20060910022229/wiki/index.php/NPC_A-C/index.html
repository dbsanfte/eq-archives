NPC A-C

From EQVault Wiki

Agents of Mistmoore
 Those creatures working for the vampire Mayong Mistmoore. Drachnids in both Fironia Vie and the Dreadlands as well as creatures in Castle Mistmoore are known Agents.

Akel Ha Ra
 First high Priestess of the Akehavans on Luclin

Akhevans
 Tall, four-armed beings that are the greatest creation of Luclin, goddess of Shadow, residing on the southern end of the moon of Luclin. They see only shadows and are masters of necromancy. When they die, they become pure shades of shadow. They are governed by a High Priestess. They created the Va’Dynn, or Stonegrabbers to serve them. They build the Temple of Akheva, also known as the Temple of Luclin and the city of Vex Thal.

Al'Kabor
 A great Erudite, Al'Kabor is the most powerful wizard living on Norrath. A companion to Firiona Vie and her cohorts, he quests for knowledge of the sorcerous arts and ancient secrets.

The Search for Immortality:
So, Al'Kabor returns to his roots, to Erudin, to begin his research on the watermen, The Kedge. He now needs the help of adventurers to continue this research.

Al'Kabor requires a few components. He cannot be bothered with the menial task of retrieving his own spell components, thus he tasks a group ofadventurers with the responsibility of bringing him the components he needs. First, he sends a group of adventurers to retrieve four gnoll teeth. With these in hand, he dispatches a group to retrieve an aviak charm from South Karana.

Finally, Al'Kabor needs something with a little more staying power, so to speak. Mammoth tusks from the beasts that roam the frozen plains of Norrath are said to hold this special property. With these items collected, Al'Kabor continues his research. To continue his search for immortality, the wizard Al'Kabor needs certain items to replicate a most heinous ceremony. In his studies in the library of Erudin, he has read about a scroll of great power, and the fateful journey leading to its loss at sea.

The Fateful Voyage of Shark's Bane The ship Shark's Bane was chartered to carry the scroll from Butcherblock to Freeport during some of the worst storms that ocean had yet seen. Despite being crewed by rough and seedy veteran seaman, none of whom were strangers to travel in treacherous conditions, the voyage turned disastrous.

Upon reaching the Ocean of Tears, the ship's captain determined it would be prudent to seek refuge at the Sisters of Erollisi Isle. His order to the tillerman to steer hard to port was met with no response. Turning, he watched as the poor soul was swept over the rail on the crest of a wave. The captain scrambled toward the rigging. A second wave breached the starboard side. The captain shut his eyes tightly and prepared for the impact. When none came, he opened his eyes. A muffled screamed escaped him as he watched the wave, now standing impossibly on the deck, sprout great arms and reach for him. The cacophony of snapping timbers and the thundering of water were all that bespoke the final voyage of the "Shark's Bane".

Al'Kabor's knowledge of the ship and its fate are the results of his studies in the great Library of Erudin. However, as is the case with tales of this sort, much of the text was based upon hearsay. But there was mention of one Tillerman Jairik as the only survivor of the tragedy.

Allize Taeew
The lizardmen of Cazic Thule

Allize Volew
Lizards who are the enemies of the lizardmen of Cazic Thule

Antonius Bayle
Mayor of Qeynos.

Arcane Scientists
Enchanter, magician, and wizards guild in West Freeport. The enchanters are guided by Romiak Jusathorn, the magicians guided by Lorme Tredore and the wizards guided by Opal Darkbriar. The Arcane Scientists have allied with the Knights of Truth against the Freeport Milita, and are opposed to the necromantic guild, also lead by the inscrutible Opal Darkbriar.


The Arisen
The ancient cult of Emperor Sathir known as the The Arisen are rumored to have acquired the lost scroll of the royal guard that tells the secrets of Charasis. They are said to care for the crypt of the late Emperpr Sathir.

Ashen Order
Located in Freeport, this peaceful Monk's guild is under the direction of Guildmaster Puab Closk. Guild trainers awarded Colored Sashes to mark a monk’s progress in training. The Ashen Order is Allied with the Knights of Truth in North Freeport and the Silent Fist Clan, a second order of Monks in North Qeynos.

Atrebe
Grandson of Venril Sathir, and son of King Rile. He assumed the Sebilis throne after his father was drowned at sea by Trakenon and the other young dragons. King Atrebe used the dark arts to create the Sarnaks, by combining dragons with Iskar. He also joined a dragon with a froglok, creating a race of flying mounts for the use of his armies the Sokokar. Atrebe waged war successfully against the dragons before being succeeded by his son Ganak.

Aviak
A sentient race of bird people living in South Karana and Lake Rathetear.

Biddyn
First Gnome king of Akanon, and writer of the "History of the Gnomes."

Bixie
Small creatures resembling a bee. They live to bother travellers.

Blood Sabres
Evil Cult of Bertoxxulous the Plaguebringer headquartered in the sewers of Qeynos.

Brood of Di'Zok
The Brood of Di'Zok is one of two groups of sarnaks found in Kunark. The Brood of Di'Zok is aligned closely with the Sarnak Collective, and can be found within the walls of Chardok, where the prince, queen and overking reside.

Brood of Kotiz
The Iksar Necromancers in West Cabilis are known as the Brood of Kotiz. They have vowed to destroy the group of outcast Iksar known as The Forsaken.

Brownie
A race of tiny man-like creatures, the brownies are known for their aggressive tendencies and their hatred of all the other races. You can find their formidable outpost in the Lesser Faydark on Faydwer. Nillipuss the brownie loves to taunt the citizens of Rivervale.


Burynai Legion
The Burynai, a race of badger like creatures, are found throughout the land of Kunark, most frequently in and around the Mines of Nurga in the Frontier Mountains. Miners by trade, they have shamans among them.


Butcherblock Bandits
Dwarves that prefer to live outside the normal dwarven society, as thieves and rogues.

Calliav
Visionary and scholar, who made mental contact with Zebuxoruk and learned many secrets about the cosmos.

Carandril
Past Lord of Kelethin. He gave Akanon to the gnomes.

Carson McCabe
Leader of HighHold, in the area of HighPass.

Chetari
A colony of sentient rat people in Dragon Necropolis.

Chosooth
Vampire Warlord. Ancient ruins in Kaesora are possibly the remnants of the structures that were built by this ruler.

Circle of Unseen Hands
Rogue's Guild

Clan Runnyeye
Goblins that lived in Runnyeye and Beholders maze.

Claws of Veeshan
The followers of the dragon goddess Veeshan

Cleaving Tooth Clan
This tribe of Kunark Goblins is aligned with the Goblins of Mountain Death. (Allakhazam’s Magical Realm)

Clerics of Tunare
The Clerics of Tunare can be found in the High Elf City of North Felwithe. The Paladins under the guidance of Guildmaster Tynkale can be found out front in a large hall, and around back a small hall is reserved for the Clerics under Guildmaster Yeolarn Bronzeleaf. The members of this guild dedicated to the Mother of All, Tunare, support the Elven King Tearis Thex and a mysterious group called "Anti-Mage." The Paladin Lord Jyleel is in charge of a secret elven order called the Koada'Vie and not all of thier tasks would normally be associated with Paladins. (Allakhazam’s Magical Realm)

Clerics of Underfoot
In South Kaladim past the mines and bank is a tunnel leading to the cleric and paladin guilds. The Cleric guildhall is a small building in front of the long Paladin guildhall, and here you can meet Priestess Ghalea. The Clerics of Underfoot are peaceful folk that support the Dwarven King Kazon Stormhammer and the Paladin guild called Miners Guild 249. (Allakhazam’s Magical Realm)

Clockwork
The gnomes have fashioned many marvelous mechanical creatures that inhabit both the city of Akanon and the outlying area of Steamfont Mountains. Many have numerical names. The clockworks clean, give directions, make, sell, and buy items, and guard the city. Sometimes the clockworks go rogue, and will attack travelers.

Clurg the Bartender
An ogre with many connections to certain factions in Ogguk.

Coalition of Legal and Illegal Tradefolk
In East Freeport hidden beneath the docks under the Seafarer's Roost, is an entrance to the passages holding the Rogues Guild called Coalition of Tradefolk, Illegal. These Rogues under Guildmaster Elisi Nasin walk the dangerous edge of neutrality. Care should be taken visiting them, as they are not far from the evil guild called the Dismal Rage. Their activities support the Coalition of Tradefolk, who are the merchants of Freeport, and Carson McCabe the leader of Highpass Keep. In addition they support the Corrupt Qeynos Guards and the Freeport Milita who are also quite corrupt. (Allakhazam’s Magical Realm)

Coldain
The race of Dwarves who make their home in Thurgadin, in the lost continent of Velious. This race of dwarves were isolated on Velious because of Prexus's wrath. The Coldain’s initial settlement, which was known as Froststone, now lies in ruins, a grim reminder of ancient slaughter at the hands of ice giants. Their hero of the Coldain is Collin Dain.

Collin Dain
The warrior hero of the Coldain. Saving his band of dwarves from Prexus's wrath, later Dain was sacrificed himself by leading a party of deadly ice giants through the Crystal Caverns into a trap where he was killed along with his foes. Snowchipper, his axe, and his crown were recovered, but not his corpse.

Combine Empire
While the elder races regrouped and reestablished themselves, a small group of Barbarians were suddenly transformed both physically and intellectually. Most believe this to be the last major and direct act of divine intervention, and perhaps the reason so little is known about this period is that the gods wish it to be so, deciding afterwards that they would have less to do with their creations. In any case, this small and enlightened group were the fathers of the human race, and they rapidly gained a foothold throughout the lands, studying the lost art of geomancy.

The Combine Empire, as this lost race of humans is called, spread throughout the known world, but then died even more quickly than it grew, and for reasons still unknown. And while they are the ancestors of every human on Norrath and their relics and ruins still litter the lands from Odus to Faydwer, little history of this period remains. (The History of Norrath, Verant International)

Corrupt Qeynos Guards
Not everything in Qeynos is as it should be.

Craft Keepers
In the Erudin Palace can be found the Hall of the Craftkeepers. Inside Enchanters practice their art under the guidance of Guildmaster Lanken Rjarn. The Craftkeepers Support the High Council of Erudin and the High Guard of Erudin. They oppose the Heretics found in Paineel. (Allakhazam’s Magical Realm)

Craknek Warriors
Guntrik leads the Craknek Warriors of Oggok. Craknek Warriors like Clurg and hate the Troll Shadow Knights who are called the Greenblood Knights. (Allakhazam’s Magical Realm)

Crimson Hands
The Wizard Guild in Erudin is called the Crimson Hands and can be found on the upper floors of the Erudin Palace. On the top floor of the Hall of Crimson Hands you can find the Wizard Guildmaster Ghanlin Skyphire. The Crimson Hands Guild lend their magical support to the High Council and High Guard of Erudin, and oppose the Heritics of Paineel. (Allakhazam’s Magical Realm)

Crusaders of Greenmist
The Iksar Shadowknight guild.

The Story of The Crusaders of Greenmist:

The Crusades of 1312 A.G.
This is the time of the scattered and defeated Iksar empire. The great dragon, Trakanon and his allies of scale have left the empire in rubble and our people are divided yet again. The once grand city of Sebilis is claimed by the one who decimated it.

The remaining Iksar are scattered among the outlands of Kunark. Their places of refuge are now the dwellings of dark denizens. In the wilds, a few castes come to unite. One of these is the remnants of the Crusaders of Greenmist. They are lead by a powerful shadowknight named Rok Nilok. The army of shadowknights number in the hundreds. Within their ranks are a few mystics and legionnaires. The tribe names itself after their leader, The Crusaders of Rok Nilok, and they shall purge the lands of Kunark.

The Crusaders burn across the Dreadlands and death comes quickly to those who dare stand in their way. They slay many giants and many goblins. There shall be no prisoners or slaves. The empire shall be rebuilt by the hands of the Iksar. All others shall perish.

Karnors Castle is the first major objective of the Crusaders. It has now been taken over by goblin clans. The Goblins soon find themselves under siege. For twenty days they hold ground until the Crusaders choose to assault as a band of goblin deserters flee. A swift battle ensues and the castle is reclaimed.

The Crusaders now set their sights on the city of Cabilis. While the march is designed, Rok Nilok discovers something very evil. Nothing is known of his discoveries. No record exists of it's evil. All that is known is that Rok Nilok made a pact and soon shall suffer for it.

During the battle for Cabilis, a strange rain falls which shrinks them to the size of dolls and their heads are set aflame. Nilok's head burns red. Thus the goblins squash the would-be emperor and his minions. (History of Norrath, Verant International)

Crushbone Orcs
The orcs of the Faydark, allied under Emperor Crush, have their stronghold of Crushbone constantly in a state of war with the armies of Felweithe and Kethelin and Kaladim. The Crushbone Orcs are allied with the Indigo Brotherhood of Neriak, the Guild of Warriors from the Drow city. The Ambassador of the Dark Elves can often be found in consultation with Emperor Crush himself. Perhaps it is their habit of enslaving their enemies which upsets the Emerald Warriors and Felweith's Champions so, but the warrior guilds of Kethelin and Felweithe have alligned with Storm Guard from Kaladim in a valiant effort to wipe the orcs from their pristine forests. (Allakhazam’s Magical Realm)
