Clearing up Pre-Order Confusion  [ 3 Comments ]
Print News | Category: Official News
Some players are reporting that some Best Buy and Gamestop stores (as well as other retail outlets) are not offering pre-orders of the Shadow Odyssey expansion, or even planning on stocking it all! It seems to depend on where you live, if you can get a retail box of TSO in the store.

But it's okay, because Associate Producer Kirstie explains:

There is no retail pre-order item. However, some retail boxes will come with the dire bear pewter figurine, and as I understand it those are more likely to come in the first wave of boxes that get to stores.

All retail boxes, whether you tell the stores you want it before hand or go in and buy it from them/order it online on or after launch day will allow you to get a dire bear in-game. There are no special codes, it is tied to you consuming the key in the box on your account.

The only pre-order item out there is the shadow beast in a cage house item and that is for people who pre-order the digital download (ie, buy a digital download before Nov 18th).

I hope that clears it up.

- Kirstie

You can still order online from a number of outlets, and get same day shipping of the box if you want to spend the extra bucks.

Miss Doomcookie, who is the lore maven of the game and a great storyteller, has posted a new tale By the Wings of Dragons, Part II.

"The adventurers stood in the center of a massive cavern of jagged stone. They were surrounded on all sides by members of the Ring of Scale, dragons of many colors and shapes that lounged around the room, sprawled over rock formations that seemed well-worn to their shape, suggesting they had assumed these traditional posts for some time. There was a dark dragon with gray and silver scales and a pair of curled horns, and a blue one with horns running down the length of its head and snout, and in the center was what Eylee took to be Phara-Dar herself, the leader of the Ring of Scale. Her scales shimmered a beautiful azure color, with purple on her wings and chest. Her head was long and elegant and her eyes were frighteningly intelligent.

Xygoz gestured to the group to stand in the center and then reverted to his natural form. The gold dragon joined the others in the circle, watching the party with inquisitive eyes.

"You've come," said Phara-Dar.

Bayle glanced at the rest of the group and then stepped forward uneasily. "Yes," he said, "Master Xygoz said you wished to speak to us."

"Yes," said the dragon, stretching her wings and letting them settle against her back once more. "We know what you're doing here. You have the Staff of Theer.""

Friday, 24 October, 2008


  Najena's Hollow  
Print News | Category: Official News
Check out one of the new zones from the Shadow Odyssey: Najena's Hollow.

"For many years Lady Najena has been residing within her Hollow Tower, an inverted citadel found deep beneath the Lavastorm Mountains. There she dwells, attending her research and becoming stronger in her command of the elements. She now boasts an impressive number of elemental minions that do her bidding with blind devotion, as well.

It must be noted, however, that years of research and lack of contact with the outside world have made Lady Najena a bit paranoid. To ensure that no one interrupted her research, the Mistress of the Elements placed an indomitable safeguard to protect her sanctuary. By harnessing geothermal energy through steam vents she found a way to flood the tower with molten lava at a moment’s notice. This brutally effective security device has kept both Najena and her research safe from outside influence... Until now.

Recently, an excavation effort in the Lavastorm Mountains uncovered an entrance into the Hollow Tower. The first explorers to make their way inside were met with a lava flooded hall...

The lava prevented further exploration of the tower, for only those especially invited by Najena as guests have ever been able to enter her domain. Further exploration will be necessary to find a way to drain the lava from the tower’s passages.



Adventurers attempting to enter her domain have caused Lady Najena to recruit help to keep her Tower free of meddlesome explorers who seek to purloin the precious items she has collected over the years. One of these mercenaries is an accomplished tinkerer by the name of Fitzfangle, who is known for his work on the steam vents which control the lava flow as well as his other more... explosive inventions.

With the aide of her new supporters and her summoned minions, the powerful conjuress has now regained her hold on the keep, allowing her to once again continue her research as she sees fit.

Those wishing to venture within the Hollow Tower should be warned: This is no place for unseasoned adventurers to be vacationing. The dangers of Najena's lair are found not only from the elemental beasts that lurk within its walls, but also the threat of lava which can be unleashed in a moment, should an intruder be discovered. "

Is the Haunted House Soloable?  [ 5 Comments ]
Print News | Category: General News
Some players are complaining that the haunted house is too difficult to solo this year, considering the end mobs are heroic. Why the change?

Gninja commented:

"We are trying to do things which appeal to multiple audiences. We have the solo players, the group players, the raiders, and the mixture of all type players. Providing time works in our favor we try to bring content that can please as many people as possible. We might occasionally have a heroic zone or a raid even but we also try to make sure there is something people can solo. "

He also mentions that if the instance gets bugged, just zone back in and it will reset.

Most people are able to get through it by themselves, but if your gear is a little bit older and you don't have better than apprentice spells, you may have some difficulties. Player advice seems to be, take it slow. Good luck!

Limited North Qeynos Haunted House Access  [ Comment ]

The Haunted House in Loping Plains is available for those who can't get access to the NQ one. It does scale, and there is a horse for ease of transporation to Sonborne. Just watch out for the vampires!

Wednesday, 22 October, 2008


  Nights of the Dead Begins Today  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: Official News
Note: It's not up yet this AM, but hopefully should be later today. Perhaps there was an act of Innorruk?!

The Nights of the Dead celebration (barring acts of Innorruk) will run from Wednesday, October 22, 2008 until Monday, November 3, 2008!



Ghosts and ghouls and things that go bump in the night!! It's that time of year again and the Nights of the Dead celebration will soon be upon us.
As in previous years, there will be trick or treating and the haunted mansion in Qeynos and Freeport. There will also be decorations all around Norrath and Ponchy will be offering up his quest in Antonica.

However, THIS year there are some new additions! Not only will there be decorations in Gorowyn for those of the scaly persuasion, some smugglers have managed to sneak a few decorations into Neriak. Now, you can either revel in the fun, or you can curry favour with Queen Cristanos and smash them, either way be sure to talk to the Nights of the Dead celebrators in the various zones for the history behind the festivities!

There are some fun new Tradeskill recipes going in and those tricksy Gigglegibber Goblins are "handing out" costumes!



There's also a brand spanking new Haunted Mansion out in the Loping Plains and some new transportation to see you safely through the zone on your way to check it out.

A Little Lore
Nights of the Dead is a holiday originating from the people of Ro, but now celebrated throughout Norrath. During this week long celebration, the people of Ro temporarily undergo illusions that make them into skeletons and zombies and they join together in banquets and parades in the streets where they knock on doors asking for treats.

This celebration stems back to the early days of the Age of Destiny. The undead assaulted the city of Maj'Dul at the command of the Godking of the Silent City. It seems they were looking for something...

Perhaps everyone celebrates the event because the undead found what they were looking for? Or maybe they just don't have anything to hide?
