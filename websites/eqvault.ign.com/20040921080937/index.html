Dev Corner Infobit: Warrior AA Refund
Print News | Mail News
EQLive's Developer Corner was updated to announce impending changes to the Warrior's abilities:
Unfortunately, the Warrior AA Press the Attack has its range set much higher than we intended it to be. Our thought was that AA was meant to be a positioning tool, and not a new pulling tool.

As such, we are reducing the range in our next update. Any AAs spent on this ability will be refunded at that time. We apologize to anyone who purchased the ability in hopes of using it as a new pulling tool. A new version of the AA, with the correct range, will be available to anyone who wishes to purchase it.
Easy come, easy go.

-- Aurum @ Fri 17 Sep 2004


  

Thu 16 Sep 2004

  Recent EQLive updates
Print News | Mail News
EQLive has gotten really busy lately - and will likely get even moreso just like it does every time a new expansion comes around. Here's a quick rundown of posts in the past 24 hours.

Tureptan Spirit has been renamed

The beastlords spell Tureptan Spirit has had a healing component added to it, as well as a name change to Thrushers Mending. This change went live with the update on the 14th.

Thanks all.

Brenlo

And another...
More Omens Coming

Greetings all,

We are aware that some outlets may not yet have copies or are currently out of copies of EverQuest: Omens of War. We are working with our retail partners to get more copies out and they should arrive over the next few days.

Thanks folks,

The EverQuest Team

And finally...

Patching Difficulties?

We have found that some users have been having difficulty with patches recently, due to some modifications we made to the patching server. We have since addressed the issue and there should be no problems going forward. For those of you who are having problems with patching, we recommend flushing the Internet Explorer cache, which should fix any problems. Be sure to save any login data you may need as it will be lost after you clear your cache.


I'll keep them coming as they are posted.
