Thursday, 9 Nov, 2006


  Google Ad Help  
Print News | Category: Features
The Vault Network is asking the community to help us preserve our "Don't Destroy the Game" philosophy and mantra. Our parent company IGN has partnered with the good guys over at Google to run its "Google Ads" across all of IGN's sites, which includes the Vaults. With that comes the chance of Google running a gold seller-type ad that might slip by the existing filter we help set up to ensure these ads are blocked. Because we told Google that these gold farmer type ads suck and don't belong on the Vault Network. They were more than happy to work with us to keep such ads off our sites. So, we will be watching to make sure we remove any that happen to slip through and muck up our stuff!

There is a gold lining to this situation (pun intended!). We have a system in place where any community member can submit an ad they might see via a simple and easy web form. You only need to provide one of two pieces of information: Name of Ad and/or the Link for the Ad -- that's it. Click here to learn more and where to submit.

  The Serpent's Spine Team Q&A  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: Features - Columns
RPG Vault has published a really amazing The Serpent's Spine development Team Q&A where members of the Sony team that created the latest expansion talk about some of their personal favorite elements. This is a must read...
Jonric: What are some of your favorite enemies in The Serpent's Spine, and why do you regard each of them as special?

Carly "Xenaida" Towle
Game Designer, EverQuest: The Serpent's Spine
Queen Pelzia rules Stone Hive from atop an ornate throne within the hive itself. She has been alluded to in lore before, but actually seeing her in game amidst the evolved Bixies of Stone Hive is something special. Her encounter is particularly interesting, because Stone Hive is a low- to mid-level zone, and she is one of the first bosses that players will encounter as they play their way through The Serpent's Spine that requires some strategy and possibly more than a group to defeat.
