Staff Info

Site Manager

Ken "Ulairi" Johnson

Ken Johnson joined Vault Network in 2000 part of the Middle-earth Online and Horizons Vault teams. In May of 2003 he was hired by Artifact Entertainment as a World Master to work on the live event team for the MMORPG Horizons. Since leaving Artifact Entertainment, Ken Johnson has rejoined the Vault Network team and returned to the University of Wisconsin.

Ken Johnson fell in love with online games with the original release of Ultima Online in 1997. Since then he has played or beta tested almost every online game and looks forward to each new release. Outside of online games he enjoys adventure, action, and pen and paper role-playing games.

Currently Ken is the Site Manager for EverQuest Vault.

Site Staff

Ryan "Draknorr" Bohmann

Ryan is a 22 year old who really stinks at writing his own bio. Ryan started playing games when he received a Nintendo, and has continued to play ever since. His first MMORPG was a little-known game called The 4th Coming, which he beta tested in the mid-90s. After testing The 4th Coming he moved to EverQuest beta and has been hooked on MMO titles ever since.

Ryan has played every major MMORPG on the market, and loves console RPGs as well. While most of his time has gone to EverQuest, he has been hooked on every MMO title at one point or another.

Doug "Thullson" Hall

Doug is a Correctional Officer in the Middle of Nowhere, TN. He has been writing as a hobby since he was a teenager and tends to take his cues from authors such as Anne Rice and Stepehen King.

Although he refuses to divulge his actual age, it's fairly easy to guess. He began his MMORPG career with Everquest beta and continued it well on through the years. He has played such games as Asherons Call, Dark Age of Camelot, and Shadowbane. Doug is currently fighting crime in Paragon City in City Of Heroes and hopes to continue his MMORPG addiction for as long as he can still see the screen.

Ben "Alizar" Hicks

Ben is currently a college student getting ready for the last year of his undergraduate degree. An unreformed online game addict since Subspace was the new thing, he has played virtually every Blizzard game released post-Starcraft and has at one time or another played Asheron's Call and Everquest, and dabbled in most other major MMORPGs.

Ben joined EQVault in order to help out with various tasks and to help proofread some of the articles it provides the Everquest community.
