  Changes coming to Tacvi
Print News | Mail News
The most talked about GoD zone is about to get some changes:
In the next update, tentatively scheduled for December 15th, some changes will be made to the Gates of Discord zone, Tacvi. The zone will have increased loot drops and over a dozen new items added to its list.

We will also be changing the zone reset timer from 3 days to 5 days as some of the new items will be a bit more potent than those currently in the zone.

Go get your flags and be the first to find the new items.

Should be fun to see what they do!

OK, in an effort to at least calm down the EQ vs. EQ2 discussions on our board I promised someone i'd post my 2 cents after Fan Faire. Well here it is. Here's a clip:
For the past couple months Ive been playing a lot of EQ2. While my characters have not reached high levels, I have had the opportunity to learn the basics. As of the end of beta I had a level 22 Ranger and 14 Warrior. Im writing this article mostly due to all the threads Ive seen on various boards (including our own) comparing EQ to EQ2. They are very different games aimed at very different types of players. While there is a chance youre like me, and can enjoy both, many people will greatly enjoy one and not the other.

"Station Access" now live
Print News | Mail News
If you're an EQ player who wants to mess around with EQ2 but does not want to leave EQ - check out the new Station Access subscription SOE just started offering for $21.99 / month.

Not only do you get access to EQ and EQ2, but EQOA, Planetside, and Station Pass access which includes some of the smaller SOE games. Considering just EQ and EQ2 alone would cost almost 30 bucks a month separately - this is a great deal!
