  International FAQ  
Print News | Category: General News
Have you ever been curious how the international servers and features of EQII work?

Fact: EverQuest II supports the French, German and Japanese languages and has servers for each, which means text and voiceover are translated into French, German and Japanese. Each version is playable in its respective language when connected to French, German and Japanese-language servers.

The French server is called Storms. The German servers are called Innovation and Valor. The Japanese server is called Sebilis.

Check out SOE's International FAQ for more information on these servers.

Fighter Balancing Issues  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: General News
EQII clearly has a problem with fighter class balance. Two classes hold sway as the "must have" at raid time: Guardians and Bezerkers, mostly because they have the best taunts and/or aggro in the game. What about the rest? Let's consider Shadowknights. What happens when one class becomes less desirable because they come in low on the parse with their DPS and can't keep aggro? No raid slots, for one, and a lot of unhappy players, for another. Bruisers and crusaders are also getting the short end of the stick.

The development team has acknowledged there is a fighter class balance issue, but they aren't willing to make a quick fix just yet. Aerilik, one of the mechanics designers, had this to say about the issue on the SOE boards:

"We are taking a longer term approach with fighters because the issues are rather broad. For example, most tanks hold aggro through dps and specialized buffs instead of using their taunts. At the same time taunts have gone up linearly but damage has gone up in a more exponential matter. To make taunts a more primary role of holding aggro is going to take some time. Then there are other issues like survivability and defined tanking roles.

We are kicking around a lot of ideas and concepts but like I have said tank balancing is more of a long term project to do it right. We could band aid things and improve certain classes but that wouldn't really get to the deeper issues of fighters which is what we are really trying to fix here."

The EQII player community hopes that "long term" doesn't mean a year from now. Class balance should also include itemization and design balance, as well.

For some great ideas and discussion on this issue from the player community, click the link above.

Official GU 47 Press Release  [ Comment ]
Print News

For any of you who'd like to know what's in GU 47, but haven't been in game yet, here's the press release from Sony:

LIVING LEGACY EVENTS CONTINUE WITH EVERQUEST(r) II'S GAME UPDATE "STORM BREAK"

WHAT:
"Storm Break", EverQuest II's most recent game update, brings players more Living Legacy in-game events, including continuation of the unrest caused by the Void Storms in-game event. Players will notice that not only has construction of Guild Halls begun, but they can discuss that and other in-game features with their friends through the new, in-game voice chat now available at no additional cost with their subscription.

"Storm Break" features:
Void Storms Part 2: The Battle
The Void Storms live in-game event continues with "The Battle" which includes a massive call to arms for all citizens of Norrath to defend their home world. The wild energy storms seem to be porting Shadow Beast creatures from the Void. This live event is open to players of all levels and everyone is able to obtain a reward for participating.

Guild Halls Construction: The workers outside of Qeynos and Freeport have broken ground on major construction projects just off of the coast of Antonica and Commonlands respectively. Visit the docks to see the new activity!

In-Game Voice Chat: The servers are abuzz as voice chat is going live allowing players to talk person-to- person in game as part of their subscription. In addition, players will be able to create their own password-protected custom channels and set up automatic channels for Guilds, Raids and Groups.

Item Revamp: Old world items from levels 1-59 have been updated to bring them inline with the rewards from the newer adventuring areas. No need to get new items, as players' equipment may be updated as they wear it!

For more details log onto www.eq2players.com.
