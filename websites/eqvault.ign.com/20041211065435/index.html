Fri 10 Dec 2004

  Paladin and Monk changes/fixes
Print News | Mail News
Ashlanne posted two seperate updates today concerning Paladins and Monks. The first, dealing with the Paladin's Holyforge disc and Slay Undead AA Ability. Here's the clip:
Hello Paladins,
I have news about Holyforge!! Holyforge was not working properly due to a change that was made to the Slay Undead AA.

Holyforge was not corrected to work with Slay Undead when this change was made. This issue has been corrected and will go live in the 12/15 update. Holyforge will now increase your chance to slay undead, and if you the Slay Undead AA you will have an even greater chance of a slay with Holyforge.

Next Monks are going to have some AA changes in the Dec. 15th patch:
Greetings Monks,

I have some information about some AA changes that will be coming to you in the 12/15 update. These AA's will be refunded and you will be able to purchase the new version of them. Below are the details on what is changing.

Crippling Strike has been changed to an activated ability that does an eagle strike attack as well as a snare. This attack should never fail, although it can be resisted. Reuse time is 60 seconds and is linked to both Stunning Kick and Eye Gouge. Added a 250 endurance cost.

Stunning Kick has been changed to an activated ability that does a round kick attack as well as a stun. This attack should never fail, although it can be resisted. Reuse time is 60 seconds and is linked to both Crippling Strike and Eye Gouge. Added a 250 endurance cost.

Eye Gouge has been changed to an activated ability that does a tiger claw attack as well as reduces the targets offensive ability. Reuse time is 60 seconds and is linked to both Crippling Strike and Stunning Kick. Added a 250 endurance cost.


-- Draknorr @ Fri 10 Dec 2004
