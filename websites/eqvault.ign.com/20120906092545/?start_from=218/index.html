MoorBlurbs  
Print News - Official News
There were a few this afternoon, so I am going to compile them all right here. First up, Moorgard talked a bit more about mounts:
Moorgard:
Racial mount choices are certainly something want to do, but they're unlikely to be done by ship. They require specialized animations, which of course requires time. We will have horses and flying carpets at ship, as I said before.


Next he talked about languages:
Moorgard:
Further, languages of the playable races will need to be learned to receive certain quests in the starting cities. Artisans play a key role in helping players learn other languages


Finally we have something that will make the questers start to sweat with anticipation. It looks like there won't be a limit on the number of quests you can have.
Moorgard:
We do not currently set a limit on the number of active quests you can have at one time.


So there you go, read up.

Caster University SOE Trip - Part 2  
Print News

The Caster University has thrown up part 2 of their SOE trip impressions. Obviously this article focuses on ... you guessed it, magic, hence the name Caster's University and all. It's a good read and since i'm such a nice guy, here's a sample:

At the core of the spell system is a design that focuses on an upgrade/tier based architecture opposed to the old style replace and forget fiasco. No longer will you have spells sitting in your spell book that are useless (or ones that cost 5 mana so that you can get skills up that you’ve left behind). Keeping skills up in EQ2 seemed much easier and the fact that downtime is somewhere around a couple minutes tops eliminates the need to have 5 mana cost spells around just in case you do slack on those skills. As for the tier based spell upgrade system, at your most basic level you have “Apprentice I” which is your basic model for that particular spell. Over time you are allowed to either purchase upgrades to these base level spells or have crafters create them for you in which case you then scribe into your spell book. Your Apprentice I Lightning Bolt that does 10 damage at level 1 could possibly be upgraded to Master IV allowing it to do X amount more damage and cost X amount more power later on. Spells are not replaced, only upgraded… which for me is something I’m quite relieved about.


Make sure you hit that link up to read the whole thing.

  Mount Information  
Print News
Moorgard made his rounds on the official forums tonight and spoke briefly about mounts. Saddle up!

Our mounts at launch will be horses and flying carpets. We're hoping to offer optional mounts for certain races, but it's uncertain whether that will be at launch or sometime later. There currently are no plans for class-specific mount types.

-- @ 23:20 PST


Sound The Bell  
Print News
On the official forums Moorgard discussed the use of bells. Yes, I said bells. Here's a sample:

The Isle of Refuge is an instanced zone. Since two friends entering it may end up on different instances, we put in a mariner's bell that brings up a search window. Enter your friend's name and you can be sent to the same instance he or she currently inhabits as long as you're of the same alignment.

There are lots of mariner's bells throughout the cities, but there they serve a different purpose. In Freeport and Qeynos, they're used along the waterways as a quick means of travel from one district or village to another.


Hm, now that's interesting. Check the entire thread out here.
