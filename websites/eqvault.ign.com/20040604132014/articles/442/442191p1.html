Champions of Norrath Preview
Latest preview of the Action RPG game for the PS2 from GameSpot
November 25, 2003 - GameSpot presents un updated preview of the game. Champions of Norrath: Realms of EverQuest is an Action RPG game slated for release in February 2004. It is being developed by Snowblind Studios and has some similarities with Baldur's Gate: Dark Alliance but promises more, including multiplayer and online modes.
