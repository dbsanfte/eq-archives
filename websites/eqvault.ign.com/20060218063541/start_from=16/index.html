Monday, 18 October, 2004 - EverQuest Vault -- EQ News


  Couple news items for Monday  
Print News | Mail News
Rather then posting multiple news items, easier to condense these into the same.

Loral over at Mobhunter has a new update up today. The article focuses on the weaknesses of Omens of War. Here's a clip:

The Omens task system could be the future of quests in Everquest. All of the tools are there to help players accomplish quests and travel across Norrath. While the current quest options are limited, scripted encounters and continuing story lines could take players through exciting quests far different from what we're used to. Add scripted and locked encounters, add an overarching story line, add more rewards than just cash and experience, and you will create a strong new quest system in Everquest for all levels. Include quests for level 68 and above. All players should have the option to perform tasks.

Good article all around - check it out

Next up, Stratics posted the HoC chat log from last weeks developer chat. If you did not participate in the chat and would like to read the log, it's right here.

Monday, 4 October, 2004


  Ask SOE Q&A #1  
Print News | Mail News
Today we have the return of one of EQ Vault's oldest features, Ask SOE!

A little over a month ago I asked people to reply to a thread on our boards with questions you would like to be sent off for SOE to answer. These questions are directly from them!

Here's a clip:
blapfrog: Are there any easter eggs players may not be aware of in the game; along the lines of the smedly grafitti in qeynos, and perhaps griffons doing the macarena when charmed?

There are bunches of easter eggs in EverQuest. One example is in the tutorial, each NPC there is named after a member of the design team. You can also find most of the design teams corpses in a high level Gates zone.

You can read the full article and all six questions here

Remember these questions come straight from you! If you want to get in on the action for the second set of questions - reply to this thread with your questions.
