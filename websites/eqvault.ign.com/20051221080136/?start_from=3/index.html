Upcoming Update  
Print News | Mail News
The next scheduled update for EverQuest is planned for October 26, 2005. Here are some of the things that are currently planned:

  • New Default UI Skin
  • Modifications to DoD Missions & Loot
  • EverQuest will now use the Operating System's mouse so players can easily go between applications while in windowed mode
  • Take a look at Kithicor for some holiday fun
  • Players will have the ability to make their helms invisible
  • Improved controls in the guild bank
  • New command for opening a web browser from the game command prompt

  • Fix for the Pet HP for items
  • Fix for the loss of mana/HP while zoning

Monday, 3 October, 2005

  EverQuest Nominated for 'Walk of Games'  [ Comment ]
Print News | Mail News
EverQuest is one of the nominees for the "Walk of Games" silver star.

Walk of Game Walk of Game is the first-of-its-kind landmark honoring the icons and pioneers of the videogame industry.

Walk of Game is located inside Metreon, a 350,000 square-foot entertainment complex at Fourth and Mission Streets in the heart of downtown San Francisco.

Every year, gamers worldwide cast their vote on www.WalkOfGame.com to induct the next set of honorees to receive a permanent 24"x 24", customized steel star on the Walk of Game. From the list of nominees, the top four in the category of Games/Characters and the top two in Lifetime Achievement will be inducted. Inductees to date are: Nolan Bushnell, Shigeru Miyamoto, Halo, Link (The Legend of Zelda), Mario, and Sonic The Hedgehog.

The list of nominees is compiled from a survey of industry members and technology, entertainment, and gaming media. SCA Interactive, a leader in online promotions, powers the Walk of Game voting interface.
You can find the details here on the SOE live boards. Vote now!

Thursday, 22 September, 2005

  Community Summit at SOE Headquarters  [ Comment ]
Print News | Mail News
Next weekend starting the 30th of September SOE will be hosting community leaders and press at their headquarters for the second community summit conference. The theme the of the summit is "Progression of EverQuest from the past, to the Present and on to the Future" according to Kytherea, community relations manager.

Attendees are bringing issues from their guilds, classes, and from the SOE EQLive boards.

The conference will consist of round table discussions about issues as well as informal conversations with the producers, developers, and other SOE staff members in San Diego.

If you have issues you would like addressed on the progression of EverQuest, please contact me at kwill@vaultnetwork.com and I will pass them along!

-- Kwill @ 11:18AM Thu 22 Sep 2005


Beta Rewards  [ Comment ]
Print News | Mail News
From Kytherea at EQLive:

If you participated in the Depths of Darkhollow beta, please make sure the email address we have on record in your SOE account registration information is current. In the next week or so we will be contacting everyone who is eligible for a beta reward by email. If your email address is not correct and you do not receive an email you will not be able to claim your reward and we will not send out emails a second time.

Please be aware that playing in the beta does not automatically make you eligible for a reward.. To be eligible you will need to have earned a minimum number of tokens; I do not currently have that number.

As I have more information to share about the Beta Rewards I will let you know so watch for further announcements here.

Also, please do not petition in-game about the beta rewards, GMs do not have any information to share with you about the Depths of Darkhollow beta rewards.
