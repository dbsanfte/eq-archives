  Zone Revamps: Splitpaw and Mistmoore  
Print News | Mail News
EQ Live has an article up describing some of the upcoming changes to Splitpaw and Mistmoore that will hit Legends the 15th, and all other servers Janurary. Here's a clip of the Mistmoore article:
In Mistmoore's Catacombs, the servants of the Trueborn, a sect of vampires with Mayong Mistmoore's blood flowing through their veins, grew tired of their masters throwing them into the fray against the Norrathian intruders. They came to desire more power for themselves.

It was not long before the first words of dissension crept through the catacombs on the will of whispers. The Trueborn servants congregated in dark, musty halls away from the eyes of their masters and over time their numbers grew along with their ambitions. Their time had come and soon they would rise up to claim their rightful place in Mistmoore Castle, gaining accolades from Lord Mayong Mistmoore himself for their brilliance and bravery -- or so they supposed.

Tuesday, 30 November, 2004


  Changes coming to Tacvi  
Print News | Mail News
The most talked about GoD zone is about to get some changes:
In the next update, tentatively scheduled for December 15th, some changes will be made to the Gates of Discord zone, Tacvi. The zone will have increased loot drops and over a dozen new items added to its list.

We will also be changing the zone reset timer from 3 days to 5 days as some of the new items will be a bit more potent than those currently in the zone.

Go get your flags and be the first to find the new items.

Should be fun to see what they do!

-- @ 1:38PM Tue 30 Nov 2004

  New Announcement on EQ Live boards  
Print News | Mail News
Looks like a new event has come to Norrath. Here's the full post:
Five Elvish trumpeters stand playing a loud melodeous fanfare. When their tune ends, a herlad steps forward. His tabard bears the symbol of the Koada`Dal in the upper right corner and the Feir`Dal in the lower left corner.

Hear ye! Hear ye! On behalf of King Tearix Thex, you are all coordially invited to join the Elves of the Faydark in celebrating the Festival of Leaves! We thank the forest for our prosperity over the past year and ask the spirits of the wood to protect us over the next. Join us in grand feasts and other special events held in celebration. Certain members of the Kelethin and Felwithe Guard will provide you with more information as the Festival makes it's way to your realm.

The herald steps back behind the trumpeters. They play a short tune then turn and leave.

Monday, 8 November, 2004


  EverQuest vs. EverQuest 2  [ Comment ]
Print News | Mail News
OK, in an effort to at least calm down the EQ vs. EQ2 discussions on our board I promised someone i'd post my 2 cents after Fan Faire. Well here it is. Here's a clip:
For the past couple months Ive been playing a lot of EQ2. While my characters have not reached high levels, I have had the opportunity to learn the basics. As of the end of beta I had a level 22 Ranger and 14 Warrior. Im writing this article mostly due to all the threads Ive seen on various boards (including our own) comparing EQ to EQ2. They are very different games aimed at very different types of players. While there is a chance youre like me, and can enjoy both, many people will greatly enjoy one and not the other.

If you want to call me an idoit, flame me, etc, just click "Post a comment" up top!

"Station Access" now live  
Print News | Mail News
If you're an EQ player who wants to mess around with EQ2 but does not want to leave EQ - check out the new Station Access subscription SOE just started offering for $21.99 / month.

Not only do you get access to EQ and EQ2, but EQOA, Planetside, and Station Pass access which includes some of the smaller SOE games. Considering just EQ and EQ2 alone would cost almost 30 bucks a month separately - this is a great deal!
