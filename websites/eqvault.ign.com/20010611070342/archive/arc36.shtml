    -Gordon

Continuing to deliver break-through interactive gaming, Sony Online Entertainment (SOE), a subsidiary of Sony Corporation and Sony Pictures Digital Entertainment, is a worldwide leader in providing online entertainment. The Struggle Within
[6 Comments] - Aurum @ 4:37 PM PST
Early, early this morning on the Arena board Lady Dae published the following message:

    Lady Daegarmo entered The Arena, to be greeted by hundreds of smiling faces, all of them anxious, yet nervous to see what glory and honor the day would bring them.

    Leaders of great armies approached and introduced themselves with an incredible energy that words simply could not explain.

    These leaders talked amongst each other, making an incredible social event where discussions were filled of great feats, of dragons slain, of immortals killed, of dungeons plundered, and
    even of the best brews that were made in the lands. It was a festive moment that you could not help but to feel apart of.

    Morale was its highest, as everyone strutted around, happy simply making it to the finals, platemail boots barely leaving footprints in the soft sand.

    Lady Daegarmo's smile could not be mistaken as she knew that all of her teams effort to make this tournament a story of legends was unfolding. As she turned a corner, she suddenly
    stopped smiling, as she saw a team in distress.
    She ran up to the group, and smiles were not seen on the faces of these champions. Lady Daegarmo asked "What is wrong? Do you not have your gear? Is your team missing members? What has caused you to not enjoy the festivities that are in front of you?"

    With heads hung low, and spirits crushed, the party answered. "We beg your forgiveness, for we have failed you. You see, our team cheated to win our regional event, to earn our right to be here with the true champions, and we are so heartbroken by our actions that we are unsure if we wish to compete with those who earned their right to compete."

    "Our homeland has found out of our dishonorable deeds, and does not bless our presence here. We do not have the hopes and dreams of our people behind us. We do not have their prayers and their wishes to give us strength to defeat our foes. We ask for forgiveness and wonder if we should still enter the event."

    This was not a request to enter the event, without question it was a plea to dismiss the group so that they would not need to make the hardest decision they would ever have to make, the decision they knew in their hearts was right.

    Lady Daegarmo paused and thought for a moment. The team was defeated, a lost cause before they entered the match. With as high of spirits and hopes that the other teams had, no amount of skill or courage could make up for the loss of morale by this team. As a Knight herself, the Lady pondered what punishment she would give those involved.

    Disqualifying the team was an option, but by those who truly understood justice, knew that such a decision would be considered merciful, for it ended the pain and suffering of having to
    bring themselves to resignation.

    Lady Daegarmo spoke "You have admitted to me freely the crimes you have done against your homeland. Send this message, that I give you the right to enter this event. Spread the word
    so that all of your countrymen know that you can freely make this decision to enter this event, or resign if your hearts so tell you that is the path you wish to follow!"

    There was no relief on the eyes of this team, for the burden was not lifted from them. The weight so strong that they could not breathe without effort.

    Daegarmo left and attended her duties as the primary judge of the event, knowing the decision was already made. Many great teams came forward, showing outstanding skill and spirit, refusing to let themselves be broken. This team quietly watched the matches, without the enthusiasm of the others. The bouts held not the fascination to them as it did the others, for they knew their turn would never arrive.

    Lady Daegarmo instructed Lord Akkirus to take the next match, knowing what lied ahead. A team full of energy and spirit ran to its side, and immediately formed the requested starting
    positions and formations. Their faces glowed, their armor shined, and yet Daegarmo did not move. The shattered team then approached, and faced Akkirus. Words were said that Daegarmo could not hear, but she could quote them if asked.

    Akkirus was unsure of what to do, and turned around to face the elder of the Knights.

    "My Lady, the second team wishes to resign, they state you know the reasons behind the decision, what should we do? All lands must be championed!"

    Lady Daegarmo sighed, knowing the ultimate justice had just been administered. "They may retire in honor, and I accept their resignations. The battle they just engaged in was far worse then any team here could assault upon them. They have been redeemed in my eyes, for they have done what is right. State that they have resigned, do not give a reason why to the other teams, simply let them leave in peace."

    Akkirus nodded and delivered the message as intended.

    Later that night, several hours past the famed event, the Ruler of the Lands of the only team that resigned that day approached an exhausted Lady Daegarmo. He knocked and entered to
    seek advice on how to further handle the situation.

    "Should I take their titles? Their prizes? Their status in our lands? What shall you have us do in the name of justice?"

    Lady Daegarmo remembered to a time long ago when she did wrong, and had to learn from a mistake she made. She remembered the rule she broke, as vividly as the day it happened.

    She could never forget the distrust that was placed against her, and the path to redemption of her honor. The armor she wore during that time didn't shine, her sword felt unjustified for the
    longest time regardless of the great deeds she accomplished with it. Her shield was not worn with the same pride until her redemption was completed.

    "The greatest of titles, the most glorious of prizes, the highest symbols of status cannot hide that which we know is truth, and can not erase the pain that we do to others."

    "The titles and rewards hold not the value to those involved that they do to those who truely earned them, and I assure you that many will hide them, or destroy them because these things will remind them of the victories they could of accomplished."

    "My request is to leave them in place, for each member of that team will redeem themselves someday, and hold those items not of the deeds that they did to accomplish them, but as reminders of the day those things lost their value in the hearts of those that wield them"

    Lady Daegarmo fell asleep before her guest departed, too tired from the days events to stay awake. The elder of most elves knew in her heart, she already forgave them, and that the
    punishment the group would face in their own lands was worse then any should could ever of administered.

    Lady Daegarmo
    Online Events Coordinator
    Verant Interactive

  • Why?
    [Quote from original post: Why do MOBS "pop" in and out of view in game. ]

    They actually don't from the NPCs point of view. Warping occurs as a consequence of prediction on the client. The client operates in a fashion such that limited bandwidth is used to display NPC position. The client recieves a package from the server that tells the client the position, vector, and speed of the NPC, and any actions that that NPC takes between packets is predicted. When NPCs change their behavior between packets, that behavior is not relayed to the client until the next packet.

    Currently, updates are sent more often for NPCs and PCs that are in a certain radius from the PC that is observing those NPCs. However, when there is a large number of mobile objects in a close radius, updates cannot be sent instantaneously.

    We're putting some code in Tuesday that will hopefully drastically reduce warping. Updates for group members, along with those for anyone/anything that you have targeted, will be sent far more often. As such, the chance of what you are observing appearing to "warp" should be reduced.

  • Asleep at the Keyboard
    People falling asleep at the keyboard or otherwise forgetting to log off as in your case is a fairly common occurance. The GM staff tries to help out when they can. I'm happy to hear that everything worked out for you.

New Patch Information
[5 Comments] - Aurum @ 3:50 PM PDT
Back on the Gameplay Board, Hobart published the following announcement regarding the upcoming patch:

    The patch introduces all the DirectX7.0 or greater enhancements. It also introduces a number of User Interface (UI) changes.
    All EverQuest players will need to upgrade to version 7.0 or greater of DirectX™. It is also highly recommended that players check for updated sound and video drivers from their manufacturer. We also highly recommend that players install Directx7.0 or greater and then their latest video or sound card drivers after Directx7.0 or greater as been successfully installed.

    Players can download DirectX7.0a from http://www.microsoft.com/directx

    If players do not know what brand of sound card you have, you can right-click on "My Computer", go to properties, click on "Device Manager", and open "Sound, Video, and Game Controllers". If you use a SBLive sound card, you can run "LiveUpdate" from the Windows Control Panel off of your start menu. View our Sound FAQ (http://everquest.station.sony.com/s_sound.shtml) for driver locations and more details.

    Those players with the SoundBlaster Live series of sound cards should also have LiveWare3.0 installed.
    They can be found at http://www.creativelabs.com

    Next, go to the website for your 3D accelerator. There are a number of different manufacturers, and it is preferred that you visit the website for the manufacturer of your card. You can at your option go to the chipset manufacturer site. Most will be able to use either www.3dfx.com (for Voodoo cards) or www.nvidia.com (for TNT/GeForce cards). We recommend those that have NVIDIA chipsets goto the NVIDIA website listed above. Download and install the latest drivers for your card and operating system. See our Video FAQ (http://everquest.station.sony.com/s_video.shtml) for more details.

    Many players have the latest drivers and directx7.0 or greater installed on their systems. However, they still experience some graphical anomalies. At this time it is recommended that they reinstall DirectX7.0 or greater and then install their latest video drivers next. This has been proven to fix the majority of graphical problems.

    We also suggest that players take some time before the patch on Tuesday and login to the Test Server to make sure their systems are up-to-date. You can log on to the Test Server by running the testeverquest.exe. We recommend that before doing this that player's back up their eqclient.ini file to a different directory. The changes on Test Server will alter this file, and when returning to the Live Servers will result in their settings being set back to default.

    If after you have completed all the above updates and are still experiencing graphical or sound problems, please do the following.

    1. Run DXDIAG.EXE from your Start menu (click start, run, then type dxdiag.exe)

    2. Click the "Save all Information" button at the bottom of the program window.

    3. Save the file as dxdiag.txt

    4. Email that file to eqtesting@verant.com along with a complete description of your problem, any text from any error messages that are presented, and a phone number that you can be reached at if you are willing to assist our test department over the phone.

    5. If you experience a crash to the desktop while running EverQuest, please email your dbg.txt file from your EverQuest directory to the email address above.
