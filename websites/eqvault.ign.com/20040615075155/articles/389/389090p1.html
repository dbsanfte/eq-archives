Top Ten: Reasons You Are Addicted To EverQuest
August 12, 2002 - 10. You are being picked on by the class bully, so you threated to log on your 48 wizard to fry his bacon.

09. You stop at your front door to load your house.

08. You refer to your father as "Baron".

07. You try to convince your wife that the Ocean of Tears is a great spot for a vacation. She declines.

06. You tell your teacher that an orc at your homework.

05. You label your favorite hunting rifle, "LORE ITEM".

04. You begin to chant "Tae'Shan" when your boss gets upset with you.

03. You are on a long trip with your mother and you suggest to "Gate" back to save time.

02. You give two fire beetle eyes to your girlfriends parents in hopes to raise "faction" with them.

01. You are being chased by the neighborhood dog, and after a failed Mez, you run around the street corner and feign death.
-- Izbane and Stozic
