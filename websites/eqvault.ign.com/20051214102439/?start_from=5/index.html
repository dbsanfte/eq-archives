Dark Elf Scouts: Beware!  
Print News | Mail News
A war is brewing in Norrath and it appears that some of the less experienced players in the game are getting a taste of it ...

Invisible dark elf scouts have been wandering around East Commonlands, hitting for about 800 a pop! The situation was especially bewildering to the newbies who were working on the low level quests in this zone, now being invaded by dark elves, especially because they probably didn't have see invisibility abilties. But this should be changed about now, according to Absor, who did feel bad for the poor folks who don't have see invis and can't actually tell when they are about to become a casualty of this latest Norrathian conflict:


We're working on changing them a bit to eliminate this problem. The scouts will be scouting and it will be your duty as light-loving citizens of Norrath to find them and keep them from their nefarious task. Hopefully we'll have this ready to live update (meaning the zones will come down, not the servers) tonight (July 8).


Kytherea adds:
...with the changes that are being made to these npcs in the live update later this evening, you will have the choice to engage the Teir'Dal scouts or not. They will be "scouting" instead of sneaking up on and wiping out every being they meet.


Hope that means things have been changed by today. So be on the lookout for strange NPC Dark Elves, they don't want to give you a cookie!

Wednesday, 29 June, 2005

  Things not in the patch message ...  
Print News | Mail News
There is a new potion vendor in Plane of Knowledge selling the Elixir of Clarity: the effect is +14 mana regen for 30 minutes, 14/tick, and does not stack with KEI. Cost: 39pp.

New spells are in Lavastorm, not Dranik's Scar.

There are also some problems with the patch, specifically with the pet commands and with instanced zones such as Lost Dungeon of Norrath, Dragons of Norrath, and others.

/assist raid is not working. /assist name appears to be working normally.

Grumbuk posted on EQLive:A few things did go wrong with the update, we're working on everything reported so far. Too early to say when exactly everything will be fixed. Some of the problems are related to data files that are read only at world startup so it's likely a server bounce will be required. It's unclear if a client update will be required yet. I'll post more as I have time & relevant information to add.

-- Kwill @ 7:14PM Wed 29 Jun 2005


Patch Message June 29  
Print News | Mail News
The big patch is done...

Some highlights:

The Veteran Reward buffs are now immune to being dispelled. The Veteran Reward buffs will no longer freeze their timers in the guild hall.

The exodus bug where a player could possibly die during the small amount of time after the spell is cast but before they actually zone, should now be fixed.

Teleport bind(AA) will no longer leave the caster behind.

The raid window is now available to all players.

The LFGroup windows are now available to all players.

The Potion Belt will now remain visible after zoning (if it was visible before zoning).

You will no longer get experience back from a corpse whose rez timer has expired when using the Veteran AA Expedient Recovery.

The new tutorial was launched for 10 and under.
