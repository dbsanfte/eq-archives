EQII Vault Topic Of The Week #3  
Print News | Category: Vault News
Come to our forums and participate in "Topic of the Week #3: Supporting your alternate characters".
Quote Berek:
"EverQuest II aims to be a well balanced game in almost every aspect. This includes curbing extreme power leveling and especially ways to exploit the game illegally. Two major ways they intend to do this is through the closed-combat system and item level restrictions.

The closed-combat system essentially allows you to fight monsters without interruption from outside players. It also aims to reduce kill stealers and ninja looters. This system can be a good thing and a bad thing. I'll let you point out the positive and negative aspects.

Item level restrictions put a cap on how high or low you can equip a particular piece of equipment. You will be able to equip anything below your level, although the effectiveness will decrease the farther the base level of the item gets from your experience level. For the upper range you will be able to equip anything up to five levels higher than you.

The question I bring about in this weeks TotW is this:

How do you intend to support your alternate characters as well as guild member characters with these restrictions? Will guilds play a bigger role because of these elements?"

Weekly Recap: 8/29 - 9/4  
Print News | Category: Features
This week was a bit faster then last week, and we had a few nice features and news. The biggest was for EverQuest: Online Adventures players. On Thursday we posted an all new EQOA Dev Diary. Earlier in the week, we had some new fan fiction from Avalon, and two exclusive OoW AA abilities. Wednesday we had a never-before-seen screenshot from the upcoming PS2 game, Champions of Norrath: Return to Arms. Finally on Friday we had two new epic riddles in one day and some pencil art of the upcoming Ranger's Epic 2.0.

News this week was somewhat slow, but a few things happened. Monday SOE cleared up some confusion over focus effects post-65. On Wednesday SOE opened up the registeration to the upcoming Fan Faire in New Orleans.

That's it for this week. Remember if you have any news send it along to me and i'll get it up.
