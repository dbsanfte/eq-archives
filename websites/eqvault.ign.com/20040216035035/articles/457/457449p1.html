Champions of Norrath: Gearing Up For Beta?
Reports of beta invites circulate the internet, as the PS2 RPG gets ready to begin testing!
October 31, 2003 - It's coming! Champions of Norrath, the single player RPG being developed for the PlayStation 2 by SOE, is getting ready to enter the testing phase of its development. Reports have been coming in that beta invites have gone out to a select few players. This could mean that those of you anxiously awaiting a chance to play are that much closer!

You can learn more about this exciting title through our Weekly Q&S Series as well as on the Official Website.

Below we have a copy of an official beta invite email that was sent out and then passed along to us. You can judge for yourself.



This is great news for fans of this title. It really looks like it could be a blast! Thank you to those who passed the word along.
-- Lepidus
