New Zone Added to EQ Underfoot  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: Official News
Sony Online Entertainment (SOE) has just announced the addition of a new zone called Convorteum that is exclusive to players who have access to the Underfoot expansion. Beginning today, players can login and experience the new content as well as receive a new bonus item.

The Convorteum add-on delivers a new fortress constructed in effort to resist the wrath of Brell. Fueled by the resentment of his creator, The First Creation is plotting his vengeance, and it is up to the players to overcome this challenge. Convorteum includes six new raids, multiple new quests, and one new mission for players to enjoy in addition to the previously released Underfoot content.

Current and Inactive EverQuest subscribers who have yet to purchase Underfoot can enjoy a free three-day trial of the expansion from March 10, 2010 through March 12, 2010. Additionally, EQ subscribers who have already purchased Underfoot can login to experience Convorteum on March 10, 2010 and will receive an exclusive Gnomework Mercenary Skin if redeemed prior to March 31, 2010.

For a limited time, the Underfoot expansion is available from the Station Store for a discounted price of $34.99 plus applicable taxes. Offer ends March 31, 2010. Included with the purchase of Underfoot, players will receive 500 Station Cash, Legends of NorrathTM Loot Scenario and the Mark of Brell In-Game item.


More information can be found at SOE's official website at http://www.everquest.com/everquest.vm.

  Experience a Virtual Wonderland  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: Official News
SAN DIEGO, CALIF. – Dec. 16, 2009 – Sony Online Entertainment LLC (SOE) fills players’ virtual stockings with holiday cheer this holiday season with in-game activities and festive events in its online video games. This December, EverQuest®, EverQuest® II and Free Realms players will enjoy celebrations filled with themed events, special quests and seasonal treats. Pirates of the Burning Sea™ will be celebrating Junkanoo, Wookiee Life Day returns to Star Wars Galaxies, and in Vanguard Saga of Heroes the annual Festival of Gloriann has arrived.

In-game events are as follows:

EverQuest®: The annual Frostfell event runs from Dec. 10, to Jan. 7 in EverQuest. In the Plane of Knowledge, the Frostfell celebrations have been spoiled by a state of thievery, and it is up to the players to save the celebration. Many families have been awakened by ruffians and monsters breaking into their homes and carrying off presents intended for the children of the Realm. Players are needed to help track down the monsters that committed these crimes and return the missing presents. Players should also visit the Marketplace to check out the all the new Frostfell items.

For detailed information, please visit www.eqplayers.com.

EverQuest® II: Frostfell, the Norrathian festival of kinship and giving, has returned to EverQuest II. Players can join in the celebration scheduled from Dec. 10, to Jan. 7. Gardy, the ex-giftgiving Frostfell elf, is offering a new quest. Santa Glug has several brand new gifts to give out to one and all. The merchants in the Frostfell Wonderland Village have new holiday goodies for purchase and tradeskillers can make several brand new items. Players should also be aware that this year, fruitcake really is the gift that keeps on giving! Hitting a friend with a throwable fruitcake will spawn one in their inventory for them to throw back.

For detailed information, please visit www.eq2players.com.

Print News | Category: Official News

10 years running and the 16th expansion is released. Wow! I still think back to when I first discovered the glory of MMO's through EQ and the intensity and focus it instilled in gamers such as myself. Every so often I find myself wandering back to EQ to rediscover that glory, and with the release of the Underfoot expansion I just may have to drop back in and celebrate the holidays in Norrath.

Rather than my rambling on about the expansion, I'll let the press release do the talking:

SAN DIEGO, Calif. – DECEMBER 15, 2009 – Online gamers can now immerse themselves in EverQuest® Underfoot™, the latest expansion from the genre-defining massively multiplayer online game by Sony Online Entertainment LLC (SOE). Underfoot is the unparalleled 16th expansion for the EverQuest franchise, and is available today via digital download at www.everquest.com for the suggested retail price of $39.99.
“Celebrating a decade of EverQuest throughout this year has reinvigorated our community and development team alike, and that passion is clearly on display with the latest expansion,” said Thom Terrazas, Producer, Sony Online Entertainment. “Underfoot presents a world unlike anything you've experienced in Norrath, providing a deep and fun experience for all our players.”

  EverQuest Book Now Available!  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: Official News
Remember that EverQuest piece we did earlier this year for the game's 10th Anniversary? Well if you recall correctly, Sony had mentioned they were working on a hardcover book detailing the ten year quest the game has undergone.

That book is finally available! It retails for $34.99.

The hardcover volume includes:
  • The story behind the creation of EverQuest and EverQuest II

  • Interviews with key contributors to the EverQuest legacy, including John Smedley, Russell Shanks, Brad McQuaid and others

  • Fantastic artwork illustrating every page, including reproductions of EverQuest and EverQuest II box covers and highly detailed art pieces from Legends of Norrath

  • Concept art and promotional images of famous EverQuest and EverQuest II characters and locations

  • Descriptions and commentary from the development teams behind the EverQuest franchise
    • So go check it out, looks like a great collector's item.
