Spirit of the Wolf Change Clarification  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: Official News
So how exactly will the new GU 47 Spirit of the Wolf spell work when it goes live? It's not being nerfed, it's an upgrade, according to Dev Chris Kozak:

"Yes this is an upgrade because its now a targetable group buff instead of a single target buff. It's definitely not a nerf just something new that came along with some epics and makes sense on this spell."


-- @ 14:07 PST


No Update This Week  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: Official News
Looks like "Storm Break," GU 47, is not going to happen this week .... so you have a little more time to complete those Void Storm quests.

The SOE team made it a point to announce there would be no update, which was thoughtful!

"Hey folks,

While we have not announced a planned date for Storm Break (the next EQII Game Update), I know that some people sense a rythm to our cycle and will anticipate when the next one is coming. To the point that they take days off to be ready for that game update. In the past these folks have been a little perturbed when we don't happen to update as they have expected so I wanted to make this post to be clear on it.

We aren't updating this week.

We never published an intent to update, and this is not a "push back" from that unstated public date -- but I'd like to ensure that folks who wake up on their perceived update day and don't have an update have no reason to think we don't like them. The work on Storm Break is moving along just fine, and we hope to have more information and an indication of when it is going live to you sometime later this week, but please don't get so excited that you take days off -- especially since we haven't announced the date yet."


-- @ 14:03 PST


  

Saturday, 19 July, 2008


  GU 47 Test Update Notes  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: General News
Curious what the GU 47 changes will be? Most of the changes on test will go live, so you can have a sneak preview now.

Of interest:

During "Storm Break" (GU47): The Void Tempests are continuing to threaten Norrath!

Librarian Feldarn of Freeport's Academy of Arcane Science and Librarian Dungil of Qeynos' Concordium are looking for volunteers to help battle the onslaught of darkness from beyond.

Qeynos and Freeport have broken ground on major construction projects just off of the coast of Antonica and Commonlands respectively. Visit the docks to see the new activity!

All Named and Boss NPCs in Kunark now drop Adept I and Master I scrolls equal to or less then their level. (No more farming Karnor’s Castle to track down those remaining 71-74 scrolls).

Many of the named creatures in Sebilis, Chardok and Karnor’s Castle no longer have place holders, have a longer respawn time, and will now always drop legendary rewards.

The random Adept 1s and Master spells you get while killing creatures in the level 1-20 range will now only drop spells you or your group members need.

Adventure writs now award 500 faction points per completion.

The cities of Neriak and Gorowyn now offer titles and other rewards for those who have earned faction with their city by completing writ quests.

A new shade of formal wear is now also available to those who are ranked as ally with any city writ faction.

Worldwide equipment, ranging from level 1-59 (including heritage quest rewards), have been increased to create a better progression from Timorous Deep to level 80 Rise of Kunark zones. This increase includes accessories, armor, and weapons, as well as many tradeskill items. Shields and items with special effects have not yet been changed, but will be included in a future update.

Shaman/Druid
Spirit of the Wolf will now apply to the entire group within a 50m radius instead of being single target.

Stormbreak: GU 47 Previews!  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: Official News
It's almost upon us .... Game Update 47.

From EQII players:

Voice Chat:
Create your own password-protected custom channels
Automatic channels for Guilds, Raids and Groups
Group and Raid windows have speaking indicators
Leaders can invite any other players into Guild, Group and Raid channels
Moderator controls (kick, ban, mute, etc.)
Logitech G15 keyboard integration
Ability to individually mute and set volume per user that you can hear
Troubleshooting tools to get you chatting instantly!

Our development team has done a great job ironing out all the usual kinks associated with hopping into voice chat for the first time, you can read all about that within the Official EQII Forums here. Chat with your guild, your group, or friends across servers! Read here to see what folks on test have to say about this great new addition.

Item updates:
"Level 1-59 Item Revamp
Items all across Norrath have gotten a little love from the development team! Old world items from levels 1-59 have been updated to bring them more in line with the rewards from the newer adventuring areas. Even better, you won't have to worry about getting anything new. Everything will be updated automagically as you wear it! Gnome mechanics across the world have snuck into your rooms while you were sleeping to update all kinds of items, from armour to heritage quest rewards, so be sure to check your inventory."

Spell Drops:
"From now on, when the random Adept I and Master I spells drop from critters in the level 1-20 range, they will only be spells that you or members of your group can use!"

Dungeon Revamps:
Sebilis and Chardok have new mid-zone respawn points! Also, many of the named critters in Sebilis, Chardok, and Karnor's Castle are less shy and more generous with the quality of their "phat lewtz."

Crafting Trivia  [ 1 Comment ]
Print News | Category: General News
Yesterday, a question came up in my guild chat: "what is raw bone?"

After some searching, it turns out that "bone" -- in this case, "severed bone" -- is a rare wood harvesting drop.

It does seem odd that bones would be classified as wood, but nevertheless in EQ2 they are wood crafting items, and used accordingly. So if you come across one in your travels, you got lucky!

Our harvesting guide, has both common and legendary/rare drops listed in table form, and all the bone types can be found in the rares listing.

GU 47 Changes to RoK Chain/Leather Armor  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: General News
In case you missed this post a couple of weeks ago:

Just a heads up in regards to a change planned to help alleviate the issue with leather/chain legendary set pieces dropping when a druid/shaman isn't in attendance. We're removing both the Wayward Moon and Enigmatic Sacrifice sets from the drop tables, and replacing them with a Sylvan Moon and Cabalistic Sacrifice set. These sets will only be usable by Fury/Wardens and Defiler/Mystics. None of the existing items will be changed, they will just cease to drop after GU47.

For those interested in swapping out their old Wayward Moon/Enigmatic Sacrifice sets for the new Sylvan Moon/Cabalistic Sacrifice sets please seek out Joonius Khristus at Terens Grasp in Kylong Plains.
