Wednesday, 29 September, 2004


  All new EQOA Developer Diary!  
Print News | Mail News
Today we have an all new EQOA Developer Diary for you! This round hunting experiences for the high end crowd is the main topic. Here's a clip:
EQOA has some issues with the availability of hunting locations for characters in the upper level ranges, say 55 to 60. Much of this stems from the original zone layout of the game and the Frontiers expansion. When laying out zones it can be easy to fall into a balanced approach where you have equal number of zones for all level ranges. Or, even have more zones available for lower level players over high level ones. This makes perfect sense at the inception and early running of a massively multiplayer game like EQOA, but as the game matures, more and more players will have higher level characters.

Check out the full article right here.
