Tue 31 Aug 2004

  Exclusive Omens of War Alternate Advancement Abilities
[Post A Comment] - Print News | Mail News
Today we have two new OoW Alternate Advancement Abilities from SOE. This round they are for the Paladin and Rogue classes:

Paladin
Steadfast Will - This ability grants a chance to endure what would otherwise be a stunning blow, from any angle, without being stunned.

Rogue
Nerves of Steel - Each rank in this ability gives rogues an increasing chance to remain hidden when a spell hits them. At the highest rank, a resisted spell will never break Hide and there is a good chance that the rogue will remain hidden even when a spell successfully hits them.

Neither are as class-defining as a few of the earlier ones we posted, but both look very useful in some situations.

Exclusive Omens of War Alternate Advancement Abilities
[Post A Comment] - Print News | Mail News
Today we have two new Omens of War Alternate Advancement Abilities. This round we have a new Mage and Druid ability.

Druid
Call of the Wild - This ability allows you to call a fallen
comrade's spirit back to their corpse. This resurrection does not
restore any lost experience, but the player can still get an experience
resurrection after being affected by this ability.

Magician
Heart of Stone - This ability will allow you to turn into an
embodiment of elemental earth, greatly reducing the damage you take from
melee attacks.

The Magician ability sounds a lot like the last druid ability we posted. Seems many classes are getting a "get out of jail free" spell this time around.
