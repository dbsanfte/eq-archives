Monday, 6 December, 2004

  New Poll up!  
Print News | Mail News
Check out the right side of our front page for a new poll. With the holidays coming around this poll asks "What do you want for Christmas". Of course we all want to win the lotto, but this is an EQ site, so you have EQ-related choices!

If you want something specific in-game post it on our forums and let everyone know! Get a discussion going about it, who knows what could happen.

Our last poll asked "Do you have your Epic 1.5 yet?" Here are the results:

Yep! [20 votes]
No [82 votes]
Don't even have my Epic! [117 votes]
Not even level 50 [52 votes]

So looks like the most people that voted don't even have their epic yet. I guess if you're a fairly new player to EQ, before Epic 2.0s and 1.5s, most Epic Weapons are fairly useless due to how good some of the droppable weapons coming out of recent expansions were. Interesting results!

Tuesday, 30 November, 2004

  Changes coming to Tacvi  
Print News | Mail News
The most talked about GoD zone is about to get some changes:
In the next update, tentatively scheduled for December 15th, some changes will be made to the Gates of Discord zone, Tacvi. The zone will have increased loot drops and over a dozen new items added to its list.

We will also be changing the zone reset timer from 3 days to 5 days as some of the new items will be a bit more potent than those currently in the zone.

Go get your flags and be the first to find the new items.

Should be fun to see what they do!

-- @ 1:38PM Tue 30 Nov 2004
