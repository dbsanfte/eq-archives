Realms of Norrath Book In Stores
EQrpg's Realm of Norrath: Freeport Is Now Available.
April 17, 2003 - EverQuestLive.com has been updated with the news that a new EQrpg book is now available. "Realms of Norrath: Freeport" is the latest written companion to EQ's table top RPG.
EQrpg's Realms of Norrath: Freeport in Stores Now
Realms of Norrath: Freeport is White Wolf's latest book supporting the popular table top EverQuest Roleplaying Game.

Realms of Norrath: Freeport contains an unbelievable number of quests, NPCs, and monsters, not to mention descriptions of major regions such as Freeport, the Commonlands, and the Desert of Ro.

For EQ enthusiast everywhere, regardless which EQ title they play, Realms of Norrath: Freeport makes for the perfect supplement for your adventures.

For more information about the EverQuest Roleplaying Game, check out http://www.EQrpg.com and http://www.white-wolf.com.
This is interesting news for players of EverQuest.
-- Lepidus
