Wednesday, 20 Oct, 2004


  Auto-fire for Archery now in-testing  
Print News | Category: EQ Live
Ashlanne posted over on EQ Live that one of the most requested features ever for EQ is finally in testing - archery auto-fire.

Here's the full post:
Greetings all,

I am pleased to announce that the designers and programmers have decided to implement auto-fire for archery. This will be making its way to the Test Server in the next couple of days. Once it is on the Test server please feel free to post here with your feedback, and to report any problems you find with the new ability.

Thanks all!

This is great news. Hopefully it's a feature everyone can take advantage of and not a high level AA ability.

EQ Vault Wants You!  
Print News | Category: Community News
It's that time again. We're looking for volunteers here at EQ Vault. All positions are purely volunteer, but have a few perks such as personalized board titles if you post on our boards.

To apply for any position e-mail draknorr@vaultnetwork.com

Here are a few of the positions we’re looking to fill:

Community News Updater

Find community stories from various EQ Servers and report on them. It can be anything as small as a big public raid being planned, to something off topic on a server board that's being linked all over the EQ-community. On average, 1 update a week is required for this position.

Guide Writer

Write guides based around a class, zone, level range, anything! The more in-depth the guide is the better.

Fan Fiction Writer

Write EQ-themed Fan Fiction! We accept both newly created and previous works, so if you have some stuff you did a year or two ago send it on in!

EQOA News Reporter

Keep EQ Vault readers up to date on happenings in EverQuest Online Adventures. Basically the main responsibility would be catch all official developer updates and posts daily.

Of course if you have any skills that you feel would be an asset to EQ Vault contact me! I'll happily accept help from anyone, my only request is that you're self-sufficient enough to not need hand-holding.

Monday, 18 Oct, 2004


  Couple news items for Monday  
Print News | Category: Community News
Rather then posting multiple news items, easier to condense these into the same.

Loral over at Mobhunter has a new update up today. The article focuses on the weaknesses of Omens of War. Here's a clip:

The Omens task system could be the future of quests in Everquest. All of the tools are there to help players accomplish quests and travel across Norrath. While the current quest options are limited, scripted encounters and continuing story lines could take players through exciting quests far different from what we're used to. Add scripted and locked encounters, add an overarching story line, add more rewards than just cash and experience, and you will create a strong new quest system in Everquest for all levels. Include quests for level 68 and above. All players should have the option to perform tasks.

Good article all around - check it out
