UGO: Top Online Games Of 2005  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: General News
Under Ground Online has listed the top online games of 2005, and even though EQII released late 2004, they still gave it an A-. Read about it here.
"For the past two weeks, young Pavlen, my half-elf rogue, reached level 15. What I've seen impresses me a lot. So today I write a review of EverQuest II from the eyes of a long-time EverQuest player.

Character generation alone shows the depth of this game. What impressed me most was how different two characters of the same race can look. The variations between two half-elves were as extreme as the difference between high elf, half elf, and wood elf. The wide range of hairstyles, hair color, skin color, and even physical size helps create a truly unique character. The details of cheek height, chin size, eye slant, and mouth width probably went too far; few people will see your big goofy Bruce Campbell chin as you race around the city, but more detail is always better...."
Go read the whole thing! :)

More Server Transfer Status Updates  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: Official News
You can read what Moorgard posted here:
"I wanted to let everyone know the status of the server transfer option we made available over the weekend.

We first made the option to move to a new server available on Runnyeye and took a small number of players so that we could ensure the process was successful. When that test was complete, we opened up the /movelog option on additional servers and moved a larger batch of players the next day.

Yesterday we again opened up the /movelog option but have not actually transferred the characters yet. We intended for the command to be available today as well, but it was accidentally shut off when the server was restarted. We have enabled the command again, so players on the servers listed below can use the /movelog command if they choose to do so.

Please note that we have not yet announced when the actual moves will take place. We are waiting until we build up a large enough batch of requests to do them all at once. We should have our desired numbers in the next day or two, with character moves taking place after that.

In the meantime, please feel free to play your characters as normal. You won't lose progress you make, as character data is copied over at the time we initiate the move, not when you type the /movelog command.

The table below lists the servers that have been given the transfer option, as well as the destination server that is available:

Mistmoore ==> Neriak
Antonia Bayle ==> Neriak
Valor ==> Innovation
Runnyeye ==> Faydark
Permafrost ==> Faydark

We'll post another update to let you know when the next batch of transfers will take place.
===========================
Moorgard
EverQuest II Community Guy"
