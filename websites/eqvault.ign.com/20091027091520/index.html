  EverQuest Book Now Available!  [ Comment ]
Print News | Category: Official News
Remember that EverQuest piece we did earlier this year for the game's 10th Anniversary? Well if you recall correctly, Sony had mentioned they were working on a hardcover book detailing the ten year quest the game has undergone.

That book is finally available! It retails for $34.99.

The hardcover volume includes:
  • The story behind the creation of EverQuest and EverQuest II

  • Interviews with key contributors to the EverQuest legacy, including John Smedley, Russell Shanks, Brad McQuaid and others

  • Fantastic artwork illustrating every page, including reproductions of EverQuest and EverQuest II box covers and highly detailed art pieces from Legends of Norrath

  • Concept art and promotional images of famous EverQuest and EverQuest II characters and locations

  • Descriptions and commentary from the development teams behind the EverQuest franchise
    • So go check it out, looks like a great collector's item.
