Top 10 Bummers About Being an Erudite
LadyBug strikes again with another hilarious piece!
June 30, 2003 - Heidi "LadyBug" McDonald is officially the Humor Contributor for the Camelot Vault, but she takes time out of her schedule to contribute to the other games as well. Today we provide you with the Top Ten Bummers About Being an Erudite! Enjoy!
Top 10 Bummers About Being an Erudite
People who think you're Grace Jones keep asking for your autograph.

You still have to devise a clever way to dig your way out of the underground city.

You get annoyed that someone as smart as you is still reduced to wearing a thong... those really chafe!

Kids keep bribing you to do their algebra homework.

The producers won't let you on "Who Wants to Be A Mithrillionaire" because of the unfair advantage.

Snotty comments from mailmen when delivering your issues of Playboy: "Here's your pornography, Mr. Nobel Laureate!"

Much like the Professor from Gilligan's Island (who could build everything but a boat), you are cursed with a genius that prevents you from inventing anything actually useful, like a homing device for your dead body or a money replicator...

Nothing on TV but PBS and the Discovery Channel.

When you lose your keys, there is such stigma involved!

When girls say "Aw, you're so SMART!" you know you're going to lose a date to the tank idiot over there with the souped-up horse.
Thanks again Heidi! Funny stuff can be sent to us by emailing it to eqnews@vaultnetwork.com.
-- Lepidus
