Allakhazam's Realm

This is one of the best info sites about equipment, weapons and quests and its easy to get around the site as the layout and design are made simple to use. The site also contains alot of excellent maps.

Its a must to visit this site if you want to know about all the equipment, weapons and quest in EverQuest.

 This is one of the best news sites you can visit.

EQ Castersrealm
 This is absolutely the best info sites about spells and research for every class with the abillity to cast spells. The site also has messageboards and news concerning the spell casting classes.

This is a great site dedicated to the zones of Norrath. They have alot of great maps and player information about every zone in the game.
