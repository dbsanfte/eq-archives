and Aegis Lux is illustrated by 3 different colors:

 

 Red             - Which illustrates EVIL, factions we will try to work AGAINST.

 Green          - Which illustrates GOOD, factions we will try to work FOR.

 Blue             - Which illustrates our NEUTRALITY, members may choose themselves.

 

For an in-depth description, simply click on the Faction and you will be expedited to the wondrous realm of AllaKhazam
