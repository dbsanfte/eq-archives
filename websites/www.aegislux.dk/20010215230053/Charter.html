THE 5 BASIC TRUTHS:

 

FOR THE COMMON GOOD:

A member of Aegis Lux will acknowledge the fact that they always work for the common good. Hereby defined as the majority of good races and the civilised kingdoms. No personal pursuits may ever interfere with the upholding of the basic truths, neither within nor outside the guild.

WITH TRUTH AND HONOUR:

These basic virtues are fundamental to any members of the Aegis Lux. Our actions should reflect this. We will always act honourable to our fellow man, for that is what separates us from the Dark Races. Truth is what binds us together. A member of the Aegis will always act and speak truthfully both within and outside the guild.

TO DEFEND AND PROTECT:

We are The Shield of Light. It is our duty to defend and protect those that are in need or cannot defend themselves. We are NOT judges by divine charter, nor should we act as marshals of law. But, a member will act swiftly to aid or assist those in danger in any way they can.

BY WISDOM AND COMPASSION:

Although the ends sometimes do justify the means, that which defines Good, is the ability to act with wisdom and compassion. These traits should be common for all true members. These are the pillars, on which we build and grow. We, of The Aegis Lux, will bear that in mind, always.

WE ARE ONE AND ONE IS ALL:

No one members actions are ever more important than the whole of Aegis Lux. We live and die by the actions of our fellows. The chain on which we base our trust is only as strong as the weakest link. Always help a member and expect no less from them. Evil is great and has many faces. Only together can we prevail. Such is the 5th. and final truth, and on this we act.

 

RULES OF CONDUCT:

 

General:

All conduct should reflect the 5 Basic Truths. All members will act according to these fundamental virtues.

In-game specifics:

We will not define any specific rules of conduct for members of the Aegis Lux. We expect that a belief rooted in the 5 basic truths should defer any dishonourable behaviour. Treat fellow players, as you would want them to treat you.
