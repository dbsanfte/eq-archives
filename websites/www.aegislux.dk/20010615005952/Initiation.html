Initiation Guidelines:

Prerequisites

·          Agree upon the 5 basic truths:

In order to obtain membership in Aegis Lux, you must first of all agree to the 5 Basic Truths stated in the Charter. Without accepting these as the guidelines of your conduct, you will not even come into consideration.

·          Agree upon the policies of conduct in the Faction Table:

All members of Aegis Lux adhere to the policies of conduct as outlined in the Faction Table. This shows what factions we work for, against and those whose relations we are neutral/players choice. Applicants are urged to review the Faction Table and make sure they can uphold the policies contained within.

·          All new applicants must be at least level 30:

All applicants applying for membership in the Aegis Lux must be of at least level 30

·          Only good-aligned characters:

Being a guild based on good-aligned characters, Aegis Lux will not accept applicants that are Shadowknights or Necromancers.

·          Only good races:

Also, races that we deem, as inherently evil, such as Dark elves, Trolls, Ogres and Iksars cannot be accepted either.

 

How to begin Initiation:

By recommendation:

Applicants can come into consideration by being recommended by an officer or a 1st. or 2nd.Circle member. These applicants will have a preferential position over those who have applied personally. The person recommending the new member will then act as a mentor/sponsor for the new applicant, and in the end of the evaluation help to determine whether or not the new applicant should be accepted as 2nd circle member.

By personal application:

All Applicants must apply to the guild, personally, sponsored or not. If the charter and the reputation of Aegis Lux appeal to you, feel free to mail us at newapplicant@aegislux.dk. Either for an elaboration of the Charter, an in-depth dialogue or simply to ask if you may join. You can find the officers names on the member's rooster.

 

Secondary Characters:

Secondary characters:

Members may have as many “secondary” characters in the guild as they like. The secondary characters must follow the same Initiation rules as any other applicant (see above). Already being a member does NOT guarantee initiation for any secondaries.

The Council will also be very strict in their evaluation of peoples online time and the members contribution to the Guild. We do this to eliminate “sleepers” in the members list. The Guild is not to be a simple chat forum for twinks. If you have more than one character in the Guild, we expect you to play all characters or they will be removed.

After initiation:

The reckoning:

During the first 30 days of your new membership you are considered as being on a probational basis. During this period it is not uncommon that new applicants are approached by either the Guildmaster or an officer and called upon to prove his or her faith in the virtues of the Aegis Lux.

Such a reckoning can be done either through a quest set forth by your superiors, a line of questioning or merely reflected by the behaviour you have exhibited during your 30 days of probation.

If the reckoning goes well, you are accepted as a fully-fledged member of Aegis Lux. If the officer or Guildmaster finds that the applicant have exhibited behaviour deemed unworthy of guild-membership, the applicant must again enter a 7 day probation, after which the member will enter a new reckoning or be duly expelled.

All applicants will advance to 2nd. Circle members, after a successful reckoning.
