Initiation Guidelines:

Prerequisites

Agree upon the 5 basic truths:

In order to obtain membership in Aegis Lux, you must first of all agree to the 5 Basic Truths stated in the Charter. Without accepting these as the guidelines of your conduct, you will not even come into consideration.

All new applicants must be at least level 20:

All applicants applying for membership in the Aegis Lux must be of at least level 20, with two notable exceptions:

1.      You are applying with a current members recommendation (see below) and have that person as a sponsor. Then you can apply at level 15. The sponsors name must be clearly stated in your application.

2.      You already are a member of the guild and your applicant is a secondary character. Then you may also apply at level 15 and your primary character will then act as your “sponsor”. The Council must be informed that this is a secondary character (or twink) when you apply, so the members-list can reflect this.

Only good-aligned characters:

Being a guild based on good-aligned characters, Aegis Lux will not accept applicants that are Shadowknights or Necromancers.

Only good races:

Also, races that we deem, as inherently evil, such as Dark elves, Trolls, Ogres and Iksars cannot be accepted either.

By recommendation:

Applicants can come into consideration by being recommended by an officer or a 1st. or 2nd.Circle member. These applicants will have a preferential position over those who have applied personally. The person recommending the new member will then act as a mentor/sponsor for the new applicant, and in the end of the evaluation help to determine whether or not the new applicant should be accepted as 2nd circle member.

By personal application:

Applicants are urged to personally apply to the guild. If the charter and the reputation of Aegis Lux appeal to you, feel free to mail us at newapplicant@aegislux.dk. Either for an elaboration of the Charter, an in-depth dialogue or simply to ask if you may join. You can find the officers names on the member's rooster.

Secondary characters:

Members may only have one (1) secondary character as a member of the guild. That means that no member may have more than two (2) characters as members of the guild at any time. This is to eliminate “sleepers” and undue administration on the part of the Council, Webmaster and the Guildmaster. The secondary character must follow the same Initiation rules as any other applicant (see above).

After initiation:

The reckoning:

During the first fortnight of your new membership you are considered as being on a probational basis. During this period it is not uncommon that new applicants are approached by either the Guildmaster or an officer and called upon to prove his or her faith in the virtues of the Aegis Lux.

Such a reckoning can be done either through a quest set forth by your superiors, a line of questioning or merely reflected by the behaviour you have exhibited during your 14 days of probation.

If the reckoning goes well, you are accepted as a fully-fledged member of Aegis Lux. If the officer or Guildmaster finds that the applicant have exhibited behaviour deemed unworthy of guild-membership, the applicant must again enter a 7 day probation, after which the member will enter a new reckoning or be duly expelled.

All applicants will advance to 2nd. Circle members, after a successful reckoning.
