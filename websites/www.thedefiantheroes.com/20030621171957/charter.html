    Defiant Heroes Guild Charter
         

     

     

    DH is an offshoot of a guild called Shanara's Legacy. We were formed in late 2001 and have been steadily gaining strength since then. The guild was formed by Rintro Deadlus, who transferred leadership to me in early 2002. Since then we have gained substantial strength, to the point where some of us in leadership positions are actually expected to make decisions and stuff.

    We are not an Uber l33t raid guild.

    We DO however do our fair share of raiding, and lately are tackling some very substantial opponents.

    If someone who is a member of another guild, or is unguilded wishes to request a raid, try catching me or one of my officers in game and we can probably accommodate you - time permitting.

    Right now we're after epic parts mainly, but are taking detours for any interesting loot. If you want a raid done, just e-mail our esteemed webmisteress, and she'll get it put on the calendar.

    Our membership is composed of mainly level 55 + members, although there is no official level cap within this guild. Basically if you're a reasonably intelligent individual, preferably with a sense of humor, you're probably perfect for us.

    We are, without question, the most insane guild on Povar and possibly the whole of EQ.

    The number one thing I request of members is loyalty. Not only to DH but to the ideals on which this guild was founded. Honor, selfless and often suicidal bravery, intelligence, and most importantly unity.

    DH is not just a guild, it's more like a family. A very, very dysfunctional family.

     
    Official Guild Rules

     

    Rule 1. Do not talk about Fight Club.

    Rule 2. Do not be greedy. What makes the individual stronger makes the guild stronger. The first question we all need to ask ourselves is what benefits the guild most.

    Rule 3. Do not piss off other people. Turn the other cheek. This one is important. A lot of these individuals are our friends and allies. Do not KS, train, monopolize a camp, or talk dirty about other guilds. Well... to their face anyway.

    Rule 4. There is no rule 4.

    Rule 5. Remember. We are a family. Work together. And like any family, if you offend members of it, cousin Vito will fit you with a pair of cement shoes and you'll get a much closer look at the bottom of Dragnors Cauldron than anyone is likely to want. Basically don't fight with other guild members.

    Rule 6. Always do everything you can to help your fellow guildies. It will be returned ten fold when you find yourself in need.

    Rule 7. Puff, Puff, Pass. Remember. Just say NO... to Bogarting.

    Rule 8. Try to avoid cussing profusely within the guild channel, or saying anything TOO offensive. We do have kids playing that aren't already jaded beyond all repair. This rule is void if you have good reason. Such as being humorous or failing a tailoring combine.... bleeding tailoring....

    Rule 9. Officers are expected to exemplify all the qualities that make this guild great. Honor, compassion.... Oh who am I kidding. If ya think ya can manage to stand around and look important, and maybe recruit a couple people along the way, then you're probably fully qualified.

    Rule 10. Never take ANYTHING too seriously. Particularly rules.

    Rule 11. Above all else.... Beware of the Fnords.
