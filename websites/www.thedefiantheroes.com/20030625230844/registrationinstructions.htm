    Guild Member Registration Instructions

    To have your character listed on the members page here is what you need to do.....

    1. Go to www.magelo.com and look up near the top for the button that says Register. Click it.

    2. Enter Username, password, Valid E-mail address, and stuff. Click “Create my account.”

    3. Go to your e-mail account that you submitted and click on the activation link they sent you.

    4. It will open a browser window and they will congratulate you on opening your account. Click on “Back to Magelo”

    5. Click on the button next to Register that says Login. A new window will pop up and you will have to input your username and password.

    16. Click the link on the menu to the left side near the bottom that’s labeled, “Characters.”

    17. On the new blank page you will see a button way at the top that says “Add a character.” Click on it.

    18. Input name, Surname, any photo u want to use, and Background photo

    if you want to use the official DH background photo then use this URL: http://www.thedefiantheroes.com/magelobackground

    It takes a sec to load but it matches the website :-)

    19. When your done, click the link “Add this character.” You are now taken to the overview screen and you see your photo (if you had one), your stats and several links.

    20. Click on “Manage his/her profile(s).” Once you clicked on it you are brought to the next screen. Now go down the entire list of items and clicking on edit and then selecting the item you want to place there and then moving on. Please try and be accurate in your equipment listings.

    22. After entering all of your character's information you will have your new Magelo link. Copy that link and email it to Ryverthorn.

    Your character's profile will be posted within 1-2 days of being recieved. When you update a character you DO NOT need to email the link again.

    -=REMEMBER=--=REMEMBER=--=REMEMBER=--=REMEMBER=-

    It is your responsability to keep your character(s) profiles updated ;-)
