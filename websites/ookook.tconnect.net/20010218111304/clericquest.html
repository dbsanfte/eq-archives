Summary so far. Let's keep moving on a new thread here.

1) Go to Lake Rathe from South Karana and turn left. First clearing on the wall, where the cabin for our armor quest is, is an NPC is a red robe named Schmendrick Lavawalker. Hail him and he tells you to bring him Lord Bergubble's crown.

2) Swim across the lake to the island and down into the goblin cave (entrance is p2831 n912 n221). Swim along the ceiling of the tunnel and stay toward the top of the water as you head for the tower at p2800 p150. Sit on top of the tower and look down -- there are 3 goblin spawn spots. Two side spots are 6 min spawns, center is 29 min spawn with PH for Lord Bergubble. He is a L45 cleric -- kill him (pull to surface of island is easiest) and loot his crown.

3) Stand very very close to Schmendrick Lavawalker and give him the crown. Natasha Whitewater will spawn and walk from the cabin and kill Schmendrick Lavawalker. You must be closer to him when he dies than she is! A spirit of flame will spawn and attack you -- kill it and loot damaged crown. Hail Natasha Whitewater and she will ask for the damaged crown -- give it to her and she will give you a shell to give to Omat Vastsea. (NOTE: If you kill Schmendrick Lavawalker & the spirit of flame first, you will get a damaged crown. More than one cleric can turn in damaged crowns to Natasha Whitewater after she is spawned, but only the person who turns in Lord Bergubble's crown will get a potion Effect Flame Arc - use unknown yet.)

4) Go to Timourous Deep and swim to loc n11530 n3728. There is an underwater tunnel leading thru the stone wall that will get you to a beach area with a ramp and hut. Omat Vastsea stands here at approx n12000 n2000. Hail him and give him the shell - he will give you a statue (looks like a totem) to give to a hidden plasmatic priest in Temple of Sol Ro.

5) Go to Temple of Sol Ro, upstairs on left at loc p336 p52 p28. There should be a keeper, a seeker and a seeker. The keeper on the left despawns at 7am game time and then a new one spawns (same name but L55 male). Have some friends with you! Give him the statue and he will immediately attack you (his name changed to "a plasmatic priest"). He nukes and can CH, so have some bashing-types with you. Kill him and loot his bloody robe (and a Darkwood Staff).

6) Go to Sol A next to the King room approx loc n791 n375 p59. There should be a PH named "a large fire goblin", although it may be a fire goblin shaman. Just slaughter the room (muahaha) repeatedly until Lord Gimbrox spawns. Kill him and loot his ring.

7) Go back to Omat Vastsea in Timerous Deep. Give him the bloody robe and Natasha Whitewater will spawn again. Hail her and give her the ring.

When you give the ring to Natasha, she will give you a shell to give to Naxton Deepwater in Burning Woods (iksar cleric in blue robe) who wanders the north wall from Chardok westward.

8) Have a group ready! Give the shell to Naxton Deepwater and a L55-56 NPC will spawn.....

So far no news after this - some people are working on this step now.
