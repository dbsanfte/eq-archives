Rules & Regulations

Enforcement of Rules & Regulations

  • These rules will be enforced both in game, IRC, and on the message boards.

    Rules of Conduct & Violations

  • Unprofessional conduct towards other Ebonlore Nation members will NOT be tolerated.
  • Respect the chain of command.  Have an issue talk to an Officer first!
  • Theft from other Ebonlore Nation members is an AUTOMATIC suspension from the guild.
  • Treason against the Nation (Betrayal, Leaking of Private Information) will be swiftly dealt with by the council.

    Professional Appearance

  • Every Ebonlore member is expected to treat other Ebonlore Nation members with respect and maturity.
  • SB is a highly political MMORPG. Please think about how your actions will affect the guild before you act.
  • There is no excuse for ignorance and stupidity. If you’re having a problem talk to an officer we will do our best to educate you.

    Rules of Engagement

  • If you’re attacked you may choose to fight or flee and call for reinforcements.
  • NO "Random" PKing of Guilded Characters!
  • Non-Guilded Characters are open season but you should consider them as possible recruits first!
  • All KoS Guilds will be attacked on sight. Call for backup!
  • They area surrounding the Nation city will be considered an off limits zone to any Non-Ebonlore Nation Member. Give ONE warning to leave then kill them!
  • You may choose to defend your exp area. Give ONE warning to the encroaching party then you may attack.
  • Sorry NO killing of other Ebonlore Nation members.

    Grouping

    Ebonlore Nation PvE Exp Groups
  • All members are STRONGLY encouraged to ONLY participate in Ebonlore Nation led groups.
  • Group leaders will be the ONLY ones looting kills. NO EXCEPTIONS!
  • Gold Split will be turned on in all Ebonlore Nation led PvE Exp groups.

    Ebonlore Nation PvE Guild Raid Groups
  • Guild Raid leaders will be the ONLY ones looting kills. NO EXCEPTIONS!
  • Gold Split will be turned off.

    Ebonlore Nation PvP Hunting Groups (Officer Led Only)
  • The Officer leading the hunting party will be the ONLY one looting kills. NO EXCEPTIONS!
  • Gold Split will be turned on.

    Siege Groups
  • Your Squad Leader will be the ONLY one looting kills. NO EXCEPTIONS!
  • Gold Split will be turned off.
  • All gold/items will be turned in to the guild banker at the end of the siege.

    Division of Items and Gold

    Ebonlore Nation PvE Exp Groups
  • Only Ebonlore Nation members are eligible for item drops.
  • Gold will be split automatically.
  • Items will be distributed on a participation/merit basis to guild members by the group leader.

    Ebonlore Nation PvE Guild Raid Groups
  • Only Ebonlore Nation members are eligible for item drops.
  • All gold collected will go to city upkeep/building.
  • Items will be distributed on a participation/merit basis to guild members by the guild banker.

    Ebonlore Nation PvP Hunting Groups (Officer Led)
  • Only Ebonlore Nation members are eligible for item drops.
  • Gold will be split automatically.
  • Items will be distributed on a participation/merit basis to guild members by the Officer leading the raid.

    Siege Groups
  • Only Ebonlore Nation members are eligible for item drops.
  • All gold collected will go to city upkeep/building.
  • Items will be distributed on a participation/merit basis to guild members by the guild banker.

    Attendance for Guild Functions
  • Guild IRC Meetings - We'd like to see all that can make the weekly Sunday Noon PST IRC meetings to show up.
  • Guild Message Boards - It is the responsibility of every member to check boards on a regular basis.
  • Guild Raids (PvE) - Every member online is asked to attend (No Attendance = No Loot).
  • Guild Raids (PvP) - Every member online is asked to attend (No Attendance = No Loot).
  • City Defense - Every member online is REQUIRED to defend the city when we are under siege.
  • Offensive PvP Sieges - Every member online is REQUIRED to attend (These will be scheduled in advance so you can make plans).
