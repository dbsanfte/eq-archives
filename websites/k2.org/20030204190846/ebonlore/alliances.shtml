1. Rules of Conduct
  • All Ebonlore Nation members actions reflect upon their guild and Nation they are sworn to.  Keeping that in mind, all members are required to act in a professional and mature manner.
  • Unbecoming conduct is cause for both suspension and dismissal from the Nation.
  • Unprofessional Conduct, Theft, Treason, and Violation of the Sworn Guild Bylaws will be swiftly dealt with by the Nation Council.

    2. Professional Guild Appearance
  • All Ebonlore Nation guilds will maintain a professional looking website either hosted or linked from http://k2.org/guildname.  Each guild is free to publish their own material.
  • All Ebonlore Nation guilds will use their Ebonlore Nation Guild Signatures when posting on public message boards.  All signature files should contain at least the following: The Ebonlore Nation Logo, Guild Name, Username, and the http://k2.org/guildname URL. Signatures must look crisp, clean, and professional.

    3. Nation In-Game Rule Set
  • No random PKing of guilded characters
  • Non-Guilded characters are Open Season but you should consider them as possible recruits first.
  • Nation KoS list members are to be attacked on sight (Call for backup if you need it)
  • No killing of other Ebonlore Nation members
  • No non-premissive looting of other Ebonlore Nation member corpses

    4. Recruiting Policies
  • Each Guild will maintains it's own recruiting policies.
  • Nation member guilds will NOT recruit members from within the Ebonlore Nation.
  • Recruitment of alt's into other Nation guilds is not allowed.
  • All Nation guild members will start off as Ebonlore guild members until they can afford a guild charter.

    5. Message Boards
  • All Nation guilds will direct their membership to signup and use the Nation Message boards at http://k2.org/cgi-bin/ib311/ikonboard.cgi
  • Nation guild members will register as membername<guild acronym>
  • Private Guild forums will be provided for all Nation Guilds once they reach 10 members registered on the boards.
  • Nation Forums will be accessible to all Nation State Guilds
  • Links will be provided in the Nations Recruiting forum to all Sworn Guilds.

    6. City Building / Trainers / Shops  

  • Sworn Guilds that actively participate in building the Nations City will be able to use trainers for free.
  • All Trainers and Shops in the Nations Capital City will be owned by Ebonlore
  • Sworn Guilds may choose to build additional cities (At their own expense)

    7. Nation Events (Sieges, Raids, Defense of the Cities)

  • Planned Sieges of opposing forces will be a carefully scripted Nation activity.  Wolfman (Nation Tactics Officer) will be leading the charge for sieges and Faltin (Guild Ambassador) will negotiate any surrender terms.  
  • Siege information will be posted in the Nation Events forum for all to view.  Nation Guild Leaders are expected to rally their members for these events.
  • Nation Events for PvE raids will also be announced in Nation Events Forum.  These will not be mandatory but you are encouraged to participate.
  • City Defense is a mandatory call to arms for all Nation Members.  Nation Guilds that fail to answer the call to arms will lose training privileges and face expulsion from the nation if they have been previously warned.
  • Guild Leaders may contact Darken Rahl (Nation Event Coordinator) to get their events on the calendar.

    8. Gold and Items Distribution from Nation events
  • All gold and items will be pooled for distribution by the Nation Banker (Ewle).
  • First priority in loot distribution is the reimbursement of the parties that funded the event.
  • Left over treasure will be then divided up upon a membership participation basis by guild to the Guild Leaders.
  • Each Guild Leader will distribute the treasure to their guild as they see fit.

    9. Nation Council
  • Each Nation Guild will get one seat on the Nation Council.
  • The main purpose of the Nation Council is dispute resolution.  For example Treason by a Guild, Failure to uphold Mutual Defense packs, etc.
