The whole premise behind a magic based system is that the more benefit conferred by a spell, the more mana it should cost to cast that spell.

As such, the mana cost for casting a CH should be proportional to the amount of HP's ultimately being restored. In other words, it should take more mana to completely heal a lvl 60 tank versus a lvl 50 tank (or equivalent hybrid).

My proposal is to make CH so that it is a variable mana cost spell. The program checks the total hp of the intended target and this then determines how much mana would be consumed by casting CH on that target.

The spell would accomplish what it was meant for: providing a "complete" heal.
