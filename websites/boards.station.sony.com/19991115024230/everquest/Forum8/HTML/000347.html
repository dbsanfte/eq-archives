Fadelman, which pelts are you referring to, specifically? The HQ bear pelts and MQ cat pelts seem pretty rare, but if you have the plat to sink into you, you may consider just doing reinforced attempts on the easier to come by HQ cats. They are still somewhat rare, but nearly so much as the others. Check the inns in East Commons, East Karana, and West Karana regularly. You'll have nights where you find nothing, and nights where you'll hit the jackpot. (I found over 200 HQ cat pelts in one night on three vendors in a little cluster village in West Karana one night.)

As for the pelts... I had no noticable difference using my magic (bard songs) versus using my dragoon dirk when it came to pelts. As for the loot of a creature being spawned at death or spawn: consider monsters such as Dvinn who has the dagger when he dies only if he had it (and was using it) while he was alive (ie, when spawned).

(I will note that I can not 100% say that all loot is generated at spawn, just that it is most likely the case... never know when I'll have to eat my words later, so I'd better add some seasoning now, huh?)

Raiel Kendan, Travelling Minstrel of Karana
