Since I mainly solo, and don't have much raid experience, I'll target my comments at Lich.

The Lich line has had its current effect for over a year (ever since the 33% damage reduction patch to PvP spells).

In that time, I'm positive that you guys have balanced mobs and encounters to take its increased effeciveness into account.

I would strongly urge you to not make negative changes to this spell line. Necros rely on it. It allows those of us who like to solo to do so effectively, and the price we pay is to be less effective than other classes on large scale raids.

It should be possible for you to positively balance necros for raid situations without nerfing Lich. Remember, that many of us don't do raids very often, and pre-50 most necros probably solo.

I agree with the people who don't like the fact that we are just considered to be mana batteries and corpse summoners. It would be nice if we had more offensive abilities on raids. I think that's all. To re-iterate though, don't nerf Lich - it's relied on by us, and has been for a long time now. It is balanced in its current form, it just is more effective than originally intended - but I believe that originally the penalties for using it were too harsh.

Look at it in conjunction with Clarity 2 - if a necro has C2 on, he can advance at the same rate as if he were using Lich because he doesn't have to cast high-mana cost lifetaps to heal himself as often. Don't ruin solo play to fix a perceived problem at the raid level.
