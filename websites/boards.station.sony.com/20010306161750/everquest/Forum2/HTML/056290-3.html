Necro Spells that I think should be looked at - by circle...

Level 1

Sense the Dead - Take Necro pets off the list of things this spell points at so it can really be used as an undead monster seeking spell.

Level 12

Sight Graft - Many ways to make it useful

1. Allow the sight perspective change back and forth between yourself and pet when you change targets from yourself to pet and back again.

2. Allow this spell castable anywhere in the zone, so at least if you have to cancel it to get your own sight back, you could leave your pet at an area you are trying to watch, and cast the spell to check on the spot from time to time.

3. Take over the skeleton completely, being able to move, etc, like Eye of Zomm. Have sight graft break when PC is hit

Any of the above would make the spell at least useful.

Spook the Dead - Make undead fears more efficent in terms of duration-resistability-mana. In most cases as we gain these various undead fears, our regular fear is just as efficient, lasts just as long etc. This makes the undead fears not worth memorizing.

Wave of Enfeeblement - Making it targetable and function like most other area spells would be nice.

Level 20

Track Corpse - Having the direction your facing every tick is dangerous in many zones if your travelling a long way to a corpse. Making this spell similiar to ranger tracking i.e. "You sense the corpse to your left, right etc" would be prefered.

Word of Shadow - Usefullness ? If it was targetable area spell, yes. If its casting time was less, maybe. Otherwise only semi useful to collect bone chips or something like that if you have really really good concentration.

Level 24

Screaming Terror - Who knows on this one, last I check there is still a level cap on it? Is it still indoors only as well? Tried for several months to make this one useful. I appreciate trying to give us a memory wipe spell. But if your going to do it then just give us a weak enchanter one, don't try and be anymore creative, this poor spell has seen more changes than any spell in the game =D.

Level 29

Word of Spirit - See word of Shadow above
Surge of Enfeeblement - See similiar spell above

Level 39

Wrod of Souls - I think u get this by now =D

Level 44

Dead Man Floating - Wonderfull spell, probably my favorite. But in zones that don't allow levitate, is there anyway of just stripping the levitate component when zoning in or buffing with this in these zones? Most of the zones that won't allow the lev component it would still be extremely useful to have the underwawter breathing, and added poison resistance.

Level 55

Chill Bones - Changing a monster into a skeleton is one of the most entertaining effects we get. However, compensating for the fact that Ignite Bones is highly resistable 10 levels later seems a bit off. 500 DD is not useful at 55 regardless of the neat effect. Moving this one down to replace Ignite Bones would be a godo move, and replace this with something else.

Some Overall Stuff--

50 plus DOT's - I understand that you can only make dot so powerful before it become unbalancing.
That said, I find it odd that the most efficient dot we get is our level 51 splurt. Some of the others we get post 50 have their place, but nothign touches Splurt for damage for the mana. I would think that we would get something else even more efficent than splurt sometime between 52 and 60, even if it didn't do much more damage.

-All 50+ spells that require essence emerald other than resurect...

Why ? I never see anyone casting any of these spells that require the essence emerald except for the res. First of all the component is absurd to get, which is understandable for the res, I can live with it. But, no one is going to use one of these components for a mega life tap or mega mana regen, or anything like that and take the chance that it might save someone, when they can use their component to res that person if they dont make it instead.

The only usefullness of these other essence emerald spells have is for ubber guilds with huge numbers of members where it is required of their guild people to sacrfice themselves every now and then to make these for their necros.

No one else will ever have enough of this component to ever use these spells, except for the res. Which means the average player has several 50 plus spells that he/she will never be able to cast.

I would reccomend taking all esscence emerald required spells out of the game except for the res and replace them with other spells, or change the component of the exisiting ones.

Other than all the things mentioned above, necros are doing just fine, and I am pleased with both their playability, usefullness in groups, and overall balance.

The only thing I can't comment on is raids on ubber monsters. I typically stay in smaller groups and don't participate in these kidns of events.
