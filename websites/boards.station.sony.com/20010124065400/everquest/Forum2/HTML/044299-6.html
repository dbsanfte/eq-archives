I'm assuming the code is something like this: Mob dies, and sees where the damage came from. If they can't find where it came from, they assume it was environmental and poofs the corpse. If they can find it, they see if the damage came from an NPC. If so, the corpse will poof. If none of the above conditions are true, a corpse is generated.

Link-dead players are considered NPCs. Thus, if they get the killing blow, the game thinks that an NPC killed the mob, and doesn't generate a corpse.
