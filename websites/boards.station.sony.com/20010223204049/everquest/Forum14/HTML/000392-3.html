I play several classes including a Cleric and a level 53 Shaman. I have some ideas to both balance and ease implementation.

ITEM: Standing Meditation.
DESCRIPTION: My idea for Wraith form at the bottom of my post covers this area and several others, please examine it.

ITEM: Defense skills.
DESCRIPTION: I think anyone wielding a shield or shield-like item should get shield Bash regardless of class. Give it to all of us and it's balanced.

ITEM: Stuns.
DESCRIPTION: I think all of us would like to see stuns work on high level MOBs. Maybe make them specific for other casters so that Enchanters are overall better at stuns (i.e. improve Enchanter stuns to work on any MOB, improve Cleric stuns to work on any Undead MOB, give Magicians stuns against all Summoned Elementals, give Druids stuns vs. all animals, give Shaman stuns vs. all spirits, etc.).

ITEM: Death Pact / Divine Intervention.
DESCRIPTION: Take away components for all spells of all classes that do not create a permanent item.

ITEM: Healing Over Time.
DESCRIPTION: Fix the bugs.

ITEM: Aegolism.
DESCRIPTION: Like I said above, remove components for all temporary spell effects from all classes.

ITEM: Symbol of Naltron.
DESCRIPTION: Again, remove components from all temporary spells.

ITEM: Shield of Words.
DESCRIPTION: More AC is always good.

ITEM: Divine Light.
DESCRIPTION: Once any caster maxes spell casting ability at level 50, I think there should be no chance for fizzle (obviously they can still be interrupted though).

ITEM: Heroism/Heroic Bond.
DESCRIPTION: I think a spell caster of any level should be able to overwrite beneficial spells cast on him/herself with his/her own spells. Either that or give a message window like with a Ressurection that allows a caster to choose whehter or not he/she wants to allow a beneficial effect to override his/her current beneficial spell effect.

ITEM: Abolish Disease.
DESCRIPTION: I don't think anybody really cares about this as anything but a negotiating point. If Clerics want the same Cure line as Shaman, give it to them. I don't see why all healers shouldn't have the same Cure line at least. They should have had it to begin with.

ITEM: Invisibility (personal only).
DESCRIPTION: Please read my idea at the bottom of this post for Wraith Form. I think it's an awesome idea for Clerics.

ITEM: Self-only Gate to Temple.
DESCRIPTION: There are supposed to be special benefits to worshipping each specific Deity. Give all casters that actually worship a Deity a spell that will self-only Gate them to their race's Temple of that Deity.

Questing is always a good approach, as long as you don't make it a static spawn that must be killed or forced to spawn which causes people to camp it for days on end (contact me if you want my ideas on possible solutions to this problem). NO FUN IN CAMPING REAL TIME DAYS FOR QUEST NPCs! You should be able to move from one step to the next without having to schedule an appointment with the rest of the server.

ITEM: Holy Armor / Spirit Armor / Guard / Armor of Faith / Shield of Words / Bulwark of Faith line of armor spells
DESCRIPTION: As above, allow ANY caster to overwrite beneficial spells on him/herself with his/her own spells or give a choice window.

ITEM: Banishment of Shadows.
DESCRIPTION: As above, remove component costs for spells that do not create a permanent item.

ITEM: Group Resist Poison.
DESCRIPTION: Again, this is a non-issue for the rest of us and this obviously benefits everyone, not just the Cleric. Give it to all healer classes if necessary.

ITEM: Root or Mez undead.
DESCRIPTION: Clerics in every genre I've played in the last 20 years (don't laugh, yes I'm old, hehe) have had tremendous power over Undead. They could turn, destroy and even animate dead. Necromancers were the undisputed masters of the dead, but Clerics had direct influence from their Deity making them powerful indeed.

Solution - Clerics already have a root line of spells. Instead give them a Mezz line vs. undead only and an Aggro-wipe line also vs. undead only. Give Paladins the same spells at later levels.

ITEM: Gate to Temple (self only).
DESCRIPTION: Adding the Wizard spell that sends anyone in a group to their bind point was a great start for our meleers, but all non-hybrid casters could gate already. I think all casters would find a Gate to their Temple very useful.

**ITEM: 51+ buffs
**DESCRIPTION: This is something I want for all of us. Make the duration on ALL buffs of EVERY 51+ caster the same as Shaman Shrink (i.e. Permanent until dispelled or the character zones, camps, goes LD, or dies).

As these buffs are already limited to high level characters, it will not create a whole host of new 1st level characters powerleveling to 50. This will also negate some of the post 50 caster boredom and free up more mana so casters don't have to perma-med and can get into the action more.

**ITEM: Form of the Wraith (self-only)
**DESCRIPTION: This is an idea I always thought would be cool for a Cleric. All of the casters but Clerics now have some kind of transformation spell. My idea is when a Cleric casts FoW, he/she turns into a ghostly outline of their starting racial appearance (i.e. no armor or item shows so it's not graphic intensive).

It should be retroactive so it's similar to the other classes with stronger variations at higher levels (I'd like to see Shaman Bear form like all other transformation casters too - all of them have some new variation or increased power as they attain higher levels - shameless Shaman plug).

The Wraith form would have improved AC and mana regeneration. It would also have the Invisibility component like Air Elementals where after a certain amount of time the Wraith would auto cast a type of invis on itself. The Wraith wouldn't actually become invisible, but would have the same benefits and limitations as normal Invisibility.

This would take care of so many problems including but not limited to: Cleric boredom at higher levels with all that sitting to med, a need for an Invisibility spell, a cool new form to look forward too as levels progress while at the same time balancing Class issues. All of this would make the Cleric much more fun to play.

OK guys as I read through all these posts I see one type of argument that is purely non-productive:

"Class XXXX should get skill XXXX because or RP_ReasonXXXX"

For instance:

"Clerics should get standing meditation because a cleric's god would be watching them and helping them get mana"

"Druids get heals because nature heals and grows"

Every single one of these arguments has absolutely no basis in GAME BALANCE. Whatever impact these views had ended once verant made the classes and defined them so long ago. As it is now I see every other post claiming that clerics (or whatever class) should get / should not have some random ability because of some random roleplaying justification.

In all of your arguments about why clerics (or any class) should or should not have a given ability, please use objective BALANCE related issues. Not some hooey about how random_god00 should give you your random_ability03. I can garauntee you absor is skipping any post that starts out that way. Well absor actually seems to read EVERYTHING so I guess I wouldn't go that far, but he SHOULD be skipping posts like that. I would.

---------------------

Clerics should get some form of mana regen (be it standing or otherwise) because:

Every caster class in the game already has it. Why do they have it? Because verant obviously saw a need for faster mana regen at higher levels. If there is such a need for faster mana regen at higher levels clerics should get some type of mana regen ability. If these are NOT the reasons verant gave shamand and druids their mana regen then clerics should NOT get it AND verant should look at exactly WHY druids and shamans DO get it. (i.e. perhaps take it away in the balancing)


Clerics need better melee:

Balance: We do not last appreciably longer than any 'pure caster' (nec/mage/wiz/ench) when higher level mobs rip into us, and the reason we last that extra hit or two is because we have symbol.

Cleric should be:

int caster < cleric < hybrid/melee

We were given (a) defensive skill and the ability to wear plate based items to this end. Note: clerics can not be any of the larger races, which gives any random shaman better tanking ability than us.

Solution: Up the dodge skill. Up the defense skill. Give us another defensive skill if necessary.

What many at verant don't seem to realize is that as you approach and pass 50 is when a lot of balance issues occur. Gaps widen between some (hybrids/tanks) gaps close much too far between others (clerics/druids/shamans -- note I am FOR giving druids/shamans more heals).

Take a level 50 or so cleric and do some tests with how often/how hard they get hit by a variety of monsters. Then adjust skills/AC so that is more in-line with how the balance should be.

Thanks for listening =)

--Cadsuane the Cleric
