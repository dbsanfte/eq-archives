As Alan asked, we should avoid too many ideas that, like the (IMO) ill-conceived previous summons, have nothing to do with the Magician class, summoning or the four elements.

Although a *Charm Summoned* line would certainly make a Mage popular in the Hole...)

Class interdependancy is not completely broken yet, despite all the shuffling of spells and abilities lately. No reason to let a Mage summon items that replaces the need for another class entirely.

Well this is going to be buried in page 4 or 5, but I'm going to try to put some honest thought into this, so I hope it stil lets read.

I have a 55 Magician, and IMHO the biggest drawback of Magicians is lack of group-desirability. VI has a push for grouping over soloing, so maybe they'll be on board with my general theme. I shall try NOT to infringe on other classes abilities (reluctantly) or suggest items that are better than in-game drops.

To begin - what do groups want? They want safety and faster kills. The best night/group is one that doesn't experience deaths and gains a lot of exp in the process. Now we can't totally prevent them, but any added summons which make deaths of group members moderately less likely, will be welcomed by groups.

1. Arms of Safety: Worn item, R-click effect, 5 sec cast = Root-self with immobilize (can't attack or cast spells), minor illusion and Hidden with duration until cancelled or camped (like any illusion).
This would help a character who needed to go AFK, but didn't want to log out.
I can also imagine this summon being used at the tail end of a wipe out. Imagine being in Hate and the Majority of people have just been wiped out by a nasty train. Your Magician quickly (sic heh) CoH's you to a somewhat distant location and you activate you're ARMS of the CONJURER. You morph into a stone, are rooted in place, can't cast or attack, but you are somewhat safe from the remaining mobs that are running about frenzying on the remaining poor saps who didn't choose the Magician as their group member.
Now this isn't a completely safe thing... something could always wander by who sees through Rogue-Hide.
Additionally I don't see this as infringing on any classes skills - as you obviously couldn't do any CR or scouting with this summon, that a Rogue could do.

2. Gloves of the Taunter: Gloves that add 25% to a classes natural taunt and come with some intermediate level AC (standard chain). I invision this being helpful when you have a Monk/Rogue who are higher level then the Warrior/tank you have, and you are struggling to keep the focus on the Warrior who is designed to take the beating better. This will help the group function more optimally, allowing each member to better do their designed roles. This will also reduce the amount the cleric needs to be healing the bleeder-tanks (Pal, Rogues, Monks, Rangers) when you have a full Warrior in your group. Improved group-efficiency = more desired into group.

3. Crown of Focus: I'm trying to think of something that would make other pure casters want a Magician. Clarity is out obviously. But how about a damage booster. Wear this crown, and the DD spells do 10% more damage. The draw-back - Head slot occupied.

4. Wings of Resistance: Resistance items seem really in vogue right now - everyone wants to pick up their resists (for good reason). This item provides a wing (worn in the little used range slot) that adds 5 to all resists. This may seem minor when compared to some of the resist buffs out there (aka the Enchanter Group RM which adds 55 Resist Magic), but I still think that this would be welcomed by groups.

5. Mind of the Twins: Group communication is always an issue. People have long requested that more than one group be able to be in the same group chat. This has been turned down by VI.
This item provides two twin "Brains." The wearer of one brain can see the group chat of the other brain wear's group. Say for example that both leaders of 2 groups wore this... then each leader could see all the chat going on in both groups... but their comments would still only be seen by their own group. No one else in the group sees the other group's chat, and it only works if both owners of the "Brains" are wearing them (again draw back of loosing the Head slot item). This is only good for 2 groups, but again might improve communcation and increase desirablity into a group. Helping to know when to charge.
