As with any group buffing spells, you have to weigh the advantages against the disadvantages, and EVERY group buff has both. For example:

Chloro: 200 mana per cast
Pack Chloro: 400 mana per cast, reduced duration

In instances of 3 or LESS members in a group, I go with the Chloro version. The mana cost is 600 vs 400, but I have to cast less frequently. For groups of 4+, always use Pack Chloro, as it saves mana even if having to rebuff more frequently.

Thorns: 120 mana, 24 damage
Pack Thorns: 300 mana, 20 damage

With thorns, you have to evaluate which is better on the basis of mana and damage (duration is about the same). If you have 3 or more tanks, go with the Pack to save mana. Also go with the Pack if your groupmembers besides tanks are taking a lot of hits.
