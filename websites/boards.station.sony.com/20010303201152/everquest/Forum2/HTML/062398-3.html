Has anyone considered why the mobs are so tough?

Pre-patch (when the aggro for heals was less) tanks could be healed with relative impunity - old news. Verant made the mobs tougher to compensate for the toughness of the tanks - if a tank can be healed for a bar of clerics mana, the tank has effectively 100K HP (give or take) so the mobs were made tougher to compensate. This was 'balanced' (dirty word!) because the mobs were taking and giving out as much damage as the group of tanks were.

Post patch, the equation is changed - because as soon as the cleric dies (nearly instantaneous) the tanks no longer have the same HP pool as the mobs, and are thus quickly reduced to confetti.

This is my reason behind wanting the cleric aggro reduced to the old levels. Not because it wasn't broken - it WAS broken - but because the game was previously set up to work with it, and now with the change the game is unbalanced toward the mobs. It would be much simpler to put cleric aggro back where it was than to reduce the HP of the mobs, then reduce the damage of the weapons that tanks have to match, then reduce the AC of the mobs so the tanks could hit it, then reduce the AC of the tanks so the mobs could hit them, then..... NOBODY, least of all I, would want this.

Please, folks. Let's just put cleric aggro back to the old state.
