Lyynn,

A few problems here.

The biggest problem is your video card. The SiS 530 chipset is not a supported video card. Your drivers for it do not support past DirectX 5, EverQuest uses DirectX 7. You may be able to get better performance from your video card by updating the drivers. You'll need to get them from SiS (http://www.sis.com.tw/support/download/530.htm)

You will also want to increase your page file (virtual memory.) You can set the values by right clicking on the My Computer icon > properties> Performance> Virtual Memory. We recommend that you allow Windows to manage your virtual Memory, or set it to a minimum of 500MB. If you can't get 500MB you'll need to free up some hard drive space by removing unused software.
