These are my opinions - 50+ rogue, 60+ days played, started over a year ago - so I hope I make sense.

[Current Skills]

Evade: I have a sneaking suspicion (no pun intended!) that Evade doesn't work exactly the way you think it does, which is why you're considering 'nerfing' it. I'm sure you THINK you know how it works, based on reading your code and a few tests. But I think you MAY be mistaken. I'm gonna pull out the old 'Alchemy' reference... just because you THINK it works that way doesn't mean it DOES work that way.

Evade works fine as is. If you change it, please make sure you test it thoroughly. And, more importantly, make sure you're able to back it out if something goes wrong. Remember, whether it works out or not, there will be a lot of people claiming it's broken. Just do this right the first time and you won't have TOO many complaints.

Damage Shields and missiles: Good, about time.

Locked Chests: Nice idea. Very old idea, but thanks for bringing it up to the team again. Note, at this time there are a few locked inventory (quest) items, but we cannot pick these locks... perhaps if this idea goes through you can allow us to pick these locks as well. Also, if it does get implemented, there should also be an option to Trap them, giving us 3 skills to take advantage of - Sense and Disarm Traps as well as Pick Locks.

Report Backstab messages: As long as Special Attack messages can be filtered out, I like this idea. No, it will NOT cause people to cry Nerf when they find out how much damage we are capable of. Well, it will, but they won't be justified. What, you want ANY class's damage output to be nerfed? MOBs are tough enough as it is, don't ever try to nerf anyone's damage output - ask for increases for others, but never ever ask for anyone's abilities to be nerfed, it only hurts everyone.

Sneak and Hide: Please, whatever you do, DON'T break Sneak when dealing with Merchants, turning in Quest items, or Swimming. Breaking Hide makes sense (EXCEPT while Swimming!), but Sneak is required in some cases. Realize that these are 2 seperate skills, no need to break one just because the other does. Some factions are extremely difficult if not impossible for some to improve, and Sneak has worked this way since day 1 - you'll see more complaints than you know what to do with if you break Sneak. And remember, it affects a lot more classes and races than Rogues.

Haste for missile weapons: Good idea, and not unbalancing at all, especially since missile/throwing weapons are pretty much useless except for pulling (there are rare exceptions). This would be a bonus for everyone.

Illusion Masks: Not a big deal, but it would be convenient to be able to kill illusions at will.

Hide/sneak overwriting invisibility: Hmm, not sure how to react to this one. Recently I've noticed that Invisibillity spell effects were changed so that a Hidden Rogue could not have any type of Invisibility spell cast on them. And if they Hide while invis, it breaks Invis - Hide is the most powerfull Invis 'spell' in the game, affecting both live and undead NPCs. Maybe just double-check the code to make sure it works the way it should - I'm not 100% positive that Hiding breaks Invis succuessfully and keeps the Rogue hidden/invis.

Increased Safefall: Kick it up to 100 until level 51, then maybe let it increase to 125. I never understood where the hard cap of 94 came from, heheh.

Sneaking in water: Let us start Sneak while underwater - see my previous comments. Please do not break the Sneak skill.

Stackable Poisons: Using poison at all is pretty uncommon, but you know that. Making them stackable like Alchemy potions would be nice though.

Poisoning in Full Screen mode: Never noticed, but then I've hardly used them. Good to fix though.

Sneak skill increases: Not needed, but would be nice.

Speed increase while hide and sneaking: Starting the speed increase sooner would be very nice, and level 40 or so sounds good to me. It doesn't have to ever go higher than full normal speed, and that should be somewhere between 55 and 60 in my opinion.

Tracking: Um, not really needed, but a nice option if you allow it. Would have to wait until all the Tracking enhancements have been implemented and tested for Rangers (long overdue), and limit it's use of course. Maybe just Players, or just unnamed NPCs, or something like that. Again, I don't feel we need this, but a limited form of Tracking could make sense.

Sense Traps: I guess the addition of Range was added with Velious. Before that, there was no Range limit. We used to be able to sense the traps in SolB, Paw, and Timorous Deep from anywhere in the zone. But then, of course, those traps were harmless and pointless. If there is a Range now, increasing it would be nice, and shorten the refresh time to maybe the same as Sense Heading.

[Future Possibilities]

Stun with Backstab: Interesting idea for a future skill. Blunt weapons only, and probably only from behind. We'll see.

Poison on thrown weapons: Makes Sense, but poisoning weapons would have to be entirely revamped - we don't actually poison the weapon apparently, just the weapon slot.

Soulbound Lockpicks: Nice idea, maybe from the same guy in Sol Temple that gives out the Burning Rapier quest. Just don't make it as insanely drawn out and boring (BB Cask, anyone?). A few more mid/high level quests (outside of Velious) for Rogues would also be cool.

Apply Poison Animation: Sounds like they're already planning this for the upcoming patch - good idea. A Backstab emote would be even better, heheh.

Throwing Weapons with longer range: Good idea, would help multiple classes.

Set Traps: Neato!

Hide/Sneak across zone borders: Long overdue! Maybe someday.

[Conclusions]

Rogues are one of the classes that are in least need of adjustments. I'm glad they're at least trying to make improvements. Let's cross our fingers!
