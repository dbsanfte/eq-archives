Escape, Warriors are the ONLY class in the game that have absolutely no method of making an emergency escape.
INT casters can root and gate or gate while their pet is left behind to die.
WIS casters can either root and gate, or just use sow to run away
Bards.. well they just outrun whatever may be chasing them
Shadowknights can leave their pet behind, giving them enough of a headstart to make it to a zone (usually), or they can use a darkness to slow their opponent
Rangers can snare or root and sow
Paladins can root for a head start, or they can at least run, and use LoH if they feel like they arent going to make it
Monks can FD
Rogues have instill doubt, although this is the least reliable method available
this should be truly considered a balance issue, all classes have a method of escape that should provide them with some sense of security should they have to tread dangerous ground alone.
