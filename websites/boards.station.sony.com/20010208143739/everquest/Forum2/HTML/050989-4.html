Here are my thoughts on the balance status of Wizards:

Keep in mind I play a level 58 Wizard and have played almost every other class up to higher levels as well.

Things that right:

Soloing: I have been able to solo by kiting up until 58 even though it is very inefficient to do so.

Major raids: Due to the existence of the lure line of spells wizards are wanted on major raids.

Pre level 40: Wizards don't have much of a problem getting into a group in level less than 40.

Things that are wrong:

Getting into a group post Level 50:

It is still very hard for a wizard to get a group for just normal ep hunting. The only way I have been able to get a group is if I am attached to an Enchanter or Cleric.

Why is this? There are several reasons:

1. Wizards are not the masters of damage post 50:

Warriors, Monks, Rangers and Rogues with good weapons can do as much damage as wizards per sec as wizards can. But a wizard can only do it as long as they have manna. The argument has been in the past that these classes expend HP as they do their damage so that is balanced. This is only partially true. Only the main tank takes damage as they dish it out. Tanks have not always been able to dish out damage as fast as wizards.. This really started after the really powerful weapons were added with the release of Kunark and the release of the epic weapons. As over time powerful weapons more available the problem will get worse.

2. Wizards are not able to do a large enough % of the mobs HP very quickly post 50:

The amount of HP of monsters has increased drastically at post 50. This has had the effect that wizards nukes do a smaller % of the total HP of the monster. Even the 49th level yard trash has more hit points than a 60th level wizard can do with all her manna. This means that wizard's traditional roll of emergency take down person in a group has been eliminated at these levels.

3. At post level 50 wizards are no longer able to effectively do auxiliary crowd control:

Not only do monsters break root a lot when a wizard is trying to crowd control them but monsters higher than level 51 summon so rooting the mob away from combat does not work as a method of crowd control.

Ok, here are my proposed solutions to the problem. One or all of these could be implemented to a greater or lesser degree to alleviate the problem:

1. Increase the speed in which wizards get back manna. This can be done in several ways:

Increase the effectiveness of Harvest at post 50. It only gives 1/2 of a bubble back to a wizard with 200 int and no manna jewelry. With manna jewelry it can give back much less. Make it give back a % of total Manna or double or triple the amount it gives back.

Ad another higher level Harvest that takes a component like a peridot and gives back a lot more manna. Make it renew every 10 min and give back 1000 manna or renew every 5 min and give back 500 manna. Remember that each one of these spells permanently takes up one of the 8 spell jewels and that creates a major trade off to memorizing these spells. Level 55 is a good level for the higher level spell.

Increase the effectiveness of manna skin so that it gives back 4 manna a tick instead of 1.

Increase the cap on meditate. It hurts wizards more than most other classes that after level 53 meditation does not give manna back any faster.

All or just one of the above methods could be used to a greater or lesser degree to give wizards back manna faster.

2. Make wizard able to better help Enchanters with crowd control. Post 50 only bards can help enchanters with crowd control very effectively. Here is a way to do that.

Give wizards the ability to do single target mesmerize. At level 39 give wizards the level 4 spell Enthrall. At level 55 give wizards the level 34 spell Entrance.

Enchanters would still be the only ones to have the more effective single target and AOE crowd control spells. The effect of this would be that not only would enchanters die less often wizards would be much more desirable in a group for auxiliary crowd control.

3. The last way to increase the effectiveness of wizards in a group would be to increase the amount of damage they can do per second. This can be done several ways:

Allow wizard spells to have critical hits. Give wizard nukes a % change to do double or triple the max spell damage to mobs. This has been brought up before but shot down due to the increased agro factor. As a 58th level wizard I do such a small fraction of the damage to a monster in a battle this is not really a problem. I just wait until the mob is at half health and nuke until it is dead.. If the spell did a critical and did more damage I would just nuke less and use less manna.

Decrease the time to cast, recovery time, and manna cost of wizards nukes so that they can again out damage tanks in a combat.

Like tanks have better weapons, and mages and necros have focuses for summoning, add items to the game that reduce the manna cost for wizards casting nukes. Have one for fire, ice, and magic.

Well that is all of my suggestions.. A lot of things to consider..

Personally I like method 2 the most since it does not increase the power of wizards but makes them more wanted in groups.

I also think there should be more teleports in Kunark and the "kunark dungeon only" should be removed from the existing ones or teleport to caster's bind point should be added.. but as Absor has stated teleports are not really a wizard issue.

Thanx,
-Myst
