I’ve come to see Velious as kind of an uber-guild playground. If I was a high-time-commitment player who could easily assemble multi-group fighting forces, Velious would be great: lots of uber-mobs to take down and two new planes. And I’m genuinely happy for people who meet that description.

If I had the time to do Kael raids (killing the mobs dropping corroded armor), I could just do plane raids and get the stuff more directly.

I wasn’t expecting a fast or easy way to get planes-type armor; these quests should take substantial time to complete. I was just hoping for a way to get it that is feasible for a casual player (i.e., step by step over a period of time).

Velkator's
----------
There is a lot of merrit in the idea of making a dungeon difficult by having slipery ground, and bizarre falling-levitation effects moving you about in odd ways. However, the first attempt at this probably should not have been combined with abnormally difficult residents. If the creatures here had been of say lower guk or karnor's toughness level throughout, this probably would have been an excellent dungeon. Also, this is the first area (on my list anyway) that we encounter creatures that both have a ton of hit points for something their level, and far too much MR. Some encounters through Kunark had to have abnormal MR to avoid being annihilated too quickly, sure. But when you have something with well over 32k hp, I can see just about no reason it needs to have extreme MR. Unmezzable flag...sure, enchanters can still contribute directly. In fact, extreme resistance wasn't even a good solution in Kunark. The desired effect was to require more people to succeed in a raid, but the implementation did so by making X% of the avaliable classes contribute minimally,which was not very nice. With the potential for extreme HP totals on the new creatures, there is no reason to retain that class-unbalancing crutch of unreasonable resistances.
