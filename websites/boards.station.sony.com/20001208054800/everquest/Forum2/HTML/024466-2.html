My entire character is role-played on the name Mr Mojo Risin. He is a boisterous Ogre that's part poet/bard and part shaman. He sings Doors songs in zone IN OGRESPEEK often with Norrathian references. He duz not eat lil peeples, he is a vegemetarian. He is well-known by his peers as a gud shaman and da life ov da party. (Please forgive me if I slip into Ogre, he just has a way of coming out...) You can ask any of the people I have grouped with what kind of player I am, and those that have played with my paladin will tell you a bery different story than those that have played with my Ogre. Why? Because I role-play my Ogre no less than what you would expect from a 300lb casting singer named MrMojo Risin!
