OK I am going to take a direction most will flame me for, but oh well.

I am part of what is likely a very small group (and maybe I am the only one who knows), but I am a druid in RL. I follow the principles of druidry, and that is why my character is a human druid - seemed logical to make a char as close to myself as possible if I was gonna be living him so long (I was warned before I got the game how addictive it is ). So playing a druid as a druid I get some certain RPing satisfaction out of it. But there are some things that bother me...

One is that the EQ druid is far too close to the D&D druid IMHO. Now surely I am not expecting it to be uber close to RL, that would be no fun, but there are some principles that bother me. The first is that druids do not use bladed weapons. In fact, back in the time when druids were a major influence on what is now known as the "Celtic" cultures, it was a blasphemy to even draw a blade in the presence of a druid priest. I thought it a sort of slap in the face that our quest weapon is a scimitar but hey. I guess it's probly too late to change that in game so it's livable.

Another part of that is that we can't wear robes See there are 3 levels of druids in RL: Bards, Ovates, and Druid Priests. Now since Bards are in EQ a class of thier own then I assume we are the priests. In Rl the garb if a druid priest is a white robe with gold adornments.

And lastly: we can't practice alchemy. This is the single most major thorn in my paw about EQ druids. Druids have a deep, very, very deep connection with nature. As such we are able to glean a lot of knowledge about the plants and animals around us. This is followed in the charm animals line (which admittedly may be a bit underpowered), but the charm plants thing is way off base. Druids have a very good knowledge and use of herbal remedies etc. This should most definitely be in game. I understand that shamans get it from the whole shamanic lore etc, but this is a skill that fits much better with druids than any other skill.

Other than that I think we are fairly balanced. The druids stock in trade is being a jack-of-all-trades. We're good at many different things, you'll not likely find a true druid today who doesn't have lot of knowledge in a lot of areas. As such this holds really well from an RP perspective: we are good at a lot of things, but the best in few.

We are not the best healers, but we shouldn't be druids are not about healing.
We are not the best nukers, but druids are not about wonton destruction.
We are not the best fighters, but druids do not fight unless forced.
What we are ther best at is protection, protection of the world and people around us.

This reflects well in game IMHO. As was said before we are some of the best PLers second only maybe to Monks (due to FD). This reflects the RL belief of a druid that the young need the most training, the most help. So there may be a few too many of us, but hey it makes up for the sore lack of us in RL

So that's my 2 copper (well OK maybe 10 or 12 ), So summary: leave us alone except disable swords or any bladed weapons, add alchemy, and add robes. Then I'd be happy, and I could pretty much just upload myself into EQ and live in the server Lawnmower Man style hehe....
