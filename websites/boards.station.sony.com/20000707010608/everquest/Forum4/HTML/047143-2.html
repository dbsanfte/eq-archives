"02l 04l 05w 07t 08t 10t 12r 13r 15* 18f 19*"

What it means to me is:
l = Left Room
w = Wandering
t = Trio Room
r = right Room
* = Room we are in
f = Frenzy Room

The #'s stand for the minute hand of the /time that they will spawn next.

Thus a spawn will be at:
02 after the hour in Left Room
04 after the hour in Left Room
05 after the hour Wandering
07 after the hour Trio Room
08 after the hour Trio Room
10 after the hour Trio Room
12 after the hour Right Room
13 after the hour Right Room
15 after the hour Room we are in
18 after the hour Frenzy Room
19 after the hour Room we are in

Then let's say we wait and at 02 after the hour the one in Right Room spawn, is pulled and killed.
/time reads Earth time 16:03:42.
I then Shift + Up a few times to scroll back to my line.
I add the next spawn of this to the end of it, which is 03 + 30min or 43.
I press Shift + Home to move cursor to begining of line.
I press Shift + Right four times to move cursor 4 spaces forward form begining of the line.
I press Backspace 4 times to erase the old spawn time of 02.
Thus it now looks like:
"04l 05w 07t 08t 10t 12r 13r 15* 18f 19* 43l"

Which I then press Enter to say it.
Why do I say it?
So I can later after the next kill, press Shift + Up to scroll back and edit it for next time.
If I do not press Enter to Say it, it will not keep the recorded changes.

That is how I pinpoint the spawn times. All within what EQ gives me, no outside forces. I am just creative, nothing more. I would appreciate it if I could explain myself to them at some point. However, I feel they own me at least an appologie when I get back or something.
