The SPACE REQUIREMENTS for Wurmslayer is SPECIAL. The TRADING of Wurmslayer is exactly like any other item therefore it is GENERAL. The software only has a bug if the general case is broken which it is not when one is considering TRADING not SPACE REQUIREMENTS.

This is not a programming flaw. It is as intented as indicated by the creation of a messages that tells you that there is no room and the item has fallen to the ground. Should the design be changed? Well that is a different matter entirely. I think the prevailing opinion is that it would be desirable that the "if there is no room for an item in a trade, its falls to the ground" design should be changed.

Hello all.
Just a quick post to let you know that the Wurmslayer not fitting into a regular backpack or regular-sized container is not a bug. It is as intended. It is the responsibility of the player to know of this requirement of a large backpack that can accomodate the Wurmslayer (or an open main inventory slot). The player who is trading it to another should make sure that the recipient has enough room in their inventory before trading it to anyone. This is especially true in an area where a lot of other Everquest players are in. A good suggestion is to do these type of trades off the beaten path so to speak. So if an item such as the Wurmslayer is dropped, than it won't be as difficult to avoid someone else picking it up.
