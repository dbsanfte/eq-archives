That staff makes a difference for sure (+1 Level), but that Earth pet (51) does very well without it. The level range for the 51 Earth is Level 41 to level 45. The 41 pet hits for 50 and the 45 hits for 58. They have 2500 to almost 3000 hit points. And they root like mad.

All the 51 to 56 pets have the level range of 41 to 45.

At 57 the Earth pet upgrades and the level range goes to 45 minimum and 49 Maximum. That pet hits for Min 52 to Max 60.


I am using the 54 water pet right now. Just like the Earth his range is 41 to 45 and he hits for 48 to 56. But this guy also Back stabs for 166 and casts a 100-150 DD spell. He only has around 2000 hit points, but since he has better AGI and DEX then the Earth he can last longer with fewer HP’s.

Depending on how long the fights last a Mage pet usually does several thousand damage, the mage can do a lot of DD damage as well so that really adds up.
