As an adjunct to the refutement of this argument, how realistic is it that you have to use a mouse or cursor keys to move your head and body around in the game? If someone gets you killed while playing gems, they would have gotten you killed elsewhere. If one or two out of every six people in a zone decide to solo because it's more fun now that they have gems (not gonna happen), then you will simply be left creating groups from the remainder; perhaps pulling a little less if you are one or two members short of the maximum, but not dividing the experience among those people either.

Like it or don't. It is, like the new binding points which so many are already complaining about, a pure addition to the game. Nobody's class got nerfed with the addition of the game or the binding points. Nobody lost any items with the additions. In short, nobody gets hurt. That would be MY opinion.
