EQ got the restriction of type down (although I think seeing green PLATE is kinda neat, it's just not in the spirit of a ranger IMO), and yes, the AC should be lower than other classes, BUT a ranger should be better with Dual wielding weapons (but not better than a warrior), and should be ableto doge, parry, and riposte more effectively than any other.
