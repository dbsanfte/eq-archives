Clear and simple fix for wizards:

1) Familiar that acts like a mini-bard with 4 types of effects (mana/regen/haste/resists) selected at summoning time. Familiar has no attack abilities.

2) All damage spells sliding gradually upward in efficiency to 6/1 ratio from 1-60th level. Casting times should increase smoothly from 1 to 6 secs for newly acquired spells, but drop to 50% of original cast time as you level past the spell ring. Example, a spell takes 4 sec to cast at 29th, but takes 2 sec to cast at 44th.

3) Add 2 intermediate mana-free right-click wands between Solists Icy Wand and VP-level drops. Suggest simply making Wand of Ice (KK drop) right-click 6 sec cast. Add Wand of Fire/Shock/Lightning as quests and/or drops. Note, charged wands are useless without recharge point. Possible compromise is to require a reagent for wand cast.

4) Increase skill caps on Offense/Defense and weapons usage to something higher than current, but below hybrids.

5) Significantly reduce casting time on Okiels Flickering Flame or increase its duration.

6) Add a spell that allows a single melee attack from the wizard to do backstab like damage. Damage and hit-avoidance similar to equal level rogue, as opposed to a magic-type resistance check. Rogue can do it frequently, while a wizard uses mana and has a longer recast time.

7) Snares and Roots should have both single and AE forms.

Just my opinion, but a good start.
