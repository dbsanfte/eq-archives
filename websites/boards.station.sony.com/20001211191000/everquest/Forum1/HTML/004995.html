Again the programmers have come through with a possible fix. If you are having a problem where the client doesn't save the information for some of your in-game settings, like sound levels and key mappings, delete the eqOptions1.opt and eqOptions2.opt files from your EverQuest directory. EverQuest should then rebuild those files when you next log in and set those settings, and save them properly.

Again, this is a possible fix, I can't promise that it will work for everyone.

Also, you may have to clear out your eqclient.ini file (or delete it).

I hope this helps some of you out.

Alan
