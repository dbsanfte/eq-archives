Joining a Player Guild can be a rewarding experience or it can be something somewhat less than rewarding.

I would not do a /ooc asking to join a guild. Every guild is different and each has it’s own set of rules and goals. (Both usually referred to in the guild’s charter)

The best way to choose a guild is to ask players that you are grouping with about their guilds. Ask about their charter, entry requirements (level, alignment etc.) and whether they have a website you can visit.

Take the time to find a guild that fits the desires and goals you have for your char. You will be glad you did.

There are 2 types of guilds.

NPC guild: These are the ones that are in game, and with which you can gain/lose faction with. These are important as they are the ones that have the trainers and spells you need, as well as certain quests you can complete.

PC guild: These are the player created ones that you can join. These guilds usually have certain goals in game, from just hanging out and having fun to the uber guilds that raid constantly and concentrate on getting epics/uber loot.

If you want to join a PC guild, I suggest that you want til level 20, 25 to join one. When you group with people, take note of the guild tag of your group mates. If you like these people, ask to group with them again. Get to know them, and let them know you are interested in joining there guild.
