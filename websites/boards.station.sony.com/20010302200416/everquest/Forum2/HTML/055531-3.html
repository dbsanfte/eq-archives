That's the thing.

We're not necromancers. We are the masters of plague,hatred,and fear,surely. It's the three SK deities.

Plague-which means diseases. The only spell Bertoxx followers get that is "theirs" is Disease Cloud. Infectious Cloud and possibly even a few Disease-based DD's.

Hatred-We all ubertaunt. Innoruuk is also a "dark" god,so our Darkness line(and being highly necromantic,our lifetap lines) are the right choice. Our taunt skill should be capping at the top of the list. Our spells should be focused towards keeping us at the top of the hatelist.

Fear-Our method of soloing. Every SK wants nothing more than the pleasure of chasing down their victims and killing them. Slowly. It is the aura of fear that allows us to torment our foes as we do so well. Fear spells should be a part of the SK spell complement from the first day we get spells.

The SK's purpose is to cause these things and to inflict the greatest misery possible on the opponent,as it pleases our dark gods,whichever one we follow. Hence,we ARE a defensive class-insofar as an SK's primary soloing tactic is fear kiting and wearing down targets with DOT's and our pets...as opposed to nuking the heck out of them or sitting there and attempting to overwhelm them via sheer damage like a warrior.

What should we be in groups? The hatemonger. Able to draw mobs like a magnet,because we can push ourselves so far up the hatelist it's like everyone else had Evade. We make the mob come to us,we make the mob flee from us,we bury the mob in DoT's and we keep alive with superior defense skills and lifetaps.

Give us a discipline that lets us cast in battle properly. Optimally,it hastes our spell casting while it lasts and renders us uninterruptable. To a lesser extent,give us superior channeling skills and high Bash caps to allow us to properly deliver our dark powers unto the foe,point-blank where we belong. This would allow us to compensate somewhat for our inferior HP/AC to warriors in the highest levels by allowing full and speedy use of our spells against opponents. If this discipline also allowed us proper chances of landing spells,it'd be perfect (after all,this is meant to be used in-your-face and we're going to taunt the heck out of whatever we hit) ala the resistance-reduction tricks we get with our HT-boosting disc.

I have to say that we SHOULD NOT have heightened offensive caps. We prey on the weak. We win by "dirty tactics" and our best one-on-one tactic is invoking utter terror in our foe and striking at his unprotected backside. This is not a style that encourages superior weapons skills.

We are the men(and trolls,and dark elves)what wear the black hats around here. Let's act like it.
