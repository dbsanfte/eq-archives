Well, I couldn't think of anything better, however some of them might be worth noting.

In addition to the many automatic skills and abilities that characters will receive past 50th level, we've added player-controlled intellectual "disciplines" to the repertoires of the pure casters (NEC ENC MAG WIZ) and priest (CLR DRU SHM) classes. These disciplines are activated using the /discipline command on the command line, and have a duration and base reuse time associated with them. You cannot use any other discipline until the reuse time of the last-used discipline has expired. You can type /discipline on the command line to view the disciplines available to you, as well as the amount of time before you can use another one.

Necromancer Disciplines

RESISTANT (Level 30): +3 to all resistances increasing to +10 at 50th level. This discipline lasts for 1 minute, and has a base reuse time of 1 hour. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as you level.

FEARLESS (Level 40): Immunity to "Fear" and all spells that cause "fear". This includes your summoned pet. This discipline lasts for 30 seconds, and has a base reuse time of 1 hour. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as you level.

UNHOLYAURA (Level 45): For the duration of the discipline drain type spells are unresistable. This discipline lasts for 3 minutes. This discipline has a base reuse time of 72 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the necromancer gains additional levels.

HERETIC (Level 51): The necromancer will not believe any promises or enhancements made by the tuning team. He is a heretic after all. This discipline lasts for 3 minutes, and has a base reuse time of 15 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the necromancer gains additional levels.

SPECIALIZATION (Level 52): The necromancer will cast all spells as if they were all under his primary specialization skill. This discipline lasts for 3 minutes, and has a base reuse time of 15 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the necromancer gains additional levels.

RESEARCH MASTERY (Level 53): The necromancer will be able to research spells as if he had a skill of 255. This discipline lasts for 3 minutes, and has a base reuse time of 15 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the necromancer gains additional levels.

CONCENTRATION (Level 54): The necromancer will be able to cast spells without requiring the spell component(s). This discipline lasts for 1 minute, and has a base reuse time of 45 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the necromancer gains additional levels.

DEFILER (Level 55): The necromancer is defiled. He immediately suffers the effects of being ressurected. This discipline lasts for 3 minutes, and has a base reuse time of 15 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the warrior gains additional levels.

HOPELESSLY FURIOUS (Level 56): When using this discipline, the necromancer will automatically have every attack landed on him by the opponent he or his pet is facing. This discipline lasts for 9 seconds, and has a base reuse time of 60 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the necromancer gains additional levels.

DUCK QUACK (Level 57): The necromancer will have the added benefits or statistics of wielding his epic weapon without actually wielding it. The necromancer will be Tormented by Shadows. There will be a broadcast announcement zonewide that the necromancer begins to quack. This discipline lasts for 3 minutes, and has a base reuse time of 30 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the necromancer gains additional levels.

KILLED ON SIGHT (Level 58): When using this discipline, all hits landed on the necromancer will be for at least four times the base weapons damage. This discipline lasts for 12 seconds, and has a base reuse time of 30 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the necromancer gains additional levels.

NECROPOTENCE (Level 59): When using this discipline, the necromancer cannot be hit in melee combat. This discipline lasts for 8 seconds, and has a base reuse time of 60 minutes. The reuse time for this discipline will decrease as the necromancer gains additional levels.

WARLOCK (Level 60): The necromancer will look like a spectre, and he cannot be inspected. This discipline lasts until he zones.

=== Summary #1 ===

I have read through the entire thread and have compiled a summary that you might find useful. I will try and add to it if something new is brought up. Please note, the vast majority of the ideas below are NOT mine but rather those of the community at large. I am merely the editor and compiler with one exception.

Changes fall within several categories

Lifetap
1. If a tap is cast on a mob that is less than six levels above the player, then the tap will check resistance as normal, but always return the full amount of health of the spell to the necromancer even if resisted.

2. Really a corollary to the above in that it allows partial damage while still returning full health of the spell to the necromancer.

Fear
3. Provide a fear protection line that allows the necromancer to make the target immune from fear for a time.
