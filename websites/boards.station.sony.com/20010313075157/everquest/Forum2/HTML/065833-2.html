My own feeling is the mis-con on the mobs is where most of the problems come in. The level 45 to 50 mobs(based on con), are often as difficult as level 55 mobs on Kunark because of damage output.

Look at the Kobolds in Velketor's. They are level 47 to 52 according to con. Yet they hit for 130 to 165 damage per hit(double attack). This compared with the mobs in Karnor's Castle, which hit for only around 135 per hit, and have half the hit points.

This sort of mis-con also makes the reward not worth the risk in terms of XP gained. The spiders just getting up to the Kobold area con green, yet hit harder and are more difficult than most of the blue con mobs from Kunark(to a level 52).

This is the whole problem with Velious in general, it was DESIGNED with the warrior, rogue, cleric group in mind. The need for casters in these encounters is lower because of it(the high damage output of the mobs makes casters die too quickly in most of these situations). A balanced group of 6 level 50s should have a good chance of killing solo level 47 mobs, and a more difficult, but still survivable chance at killing 2 or 3 of these same level 47 mobs. Instead, these have been tailored to be farmed by level 55+ groups.

Low blues shouldn't be the target of the 55+ crowd, but because of the poor game balance, low blues are the only things that most groups will risk for XP hunting. That's pretty sad, and is why many people are leaving as soon as a good alternative comes out.

I've always felt that the uber-guilds weren't any better than the rest of EQ's guilds except for one thing -- a near military degree of organization.
