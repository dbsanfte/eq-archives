First of all, Let me start out by saying thank you for having a thread about PvP. I can imagine that of all the potential problems that arise in the game, the most divisive and time consuming must come from the PvP servers.

Here are my thoughts and suggestions:
Thoughts
1. EQ is a VERY different game on PvP than it is on the Blue servers. Our strategies, goals, alliances and tactics are ruled by our relationships with other players.

2. On PvP servers, we expect that adversity can or will be the outcome of any situation. On Blue servers, other players are there to interact with, and help you…on our servers they are there to we wary of (at least initially).

3. For us, the greatest goal is being able to beat someone else. That is why we levels, gain equipment, learn tactics, and make alliances. Everything else is secondary.

4. The best analogy between the types of servers is that EQ on blue servers is more of a game like Myst…the goal is solving riddles, and personal achievement through accomplishment. While those things are somewhat important on PvP servers, they are only valued at all if they give you an advantage over someone else. In other words, on Blue servers they are an end in themselves…on PvP servers, they are merely a means to an end.

5. If you apply the same lines of reason to these servers when making changes as you do to others, it will not work. For all intents and purposes, it MUST be viewed as a completely different game.

Suggestions
1. Regarding a true 4 team server; I love the idea, but understand the concerns that people have about relationships they have grown. The suggestion then is that where possible, allow players an option of transitioning a characters to a race that allows that class. I realize that make take some doing, but if we take as a given that relationships are most important (both positively and negatively) but if I am in a guild that will become all Elves, and I have a lvl 50 Dwarf cleric, allow me to turn him into a lvl 50 High Elf cleric. As long as my equipment will transfer (and in those cases where it won’t, create some equipment substitutes), the relationships I have are much more important than my race.

2. This is a big one…STOP TESTING **** ON BLUE SERVERS AND IMPOSING IT ON US. Nothing will make people angrier than imposing rules on them that the people creating them don’t understand the consequences of.

3. Create some real enforceable guidelines when it comes to loot and scoot. I realize that it is a player made guideline, but that was because there was a void from you guys. It isn’t a good system (hell, it isn’t even a good system) but at least it is understandable and consistent.

4. Get some GM’s that really do something. I would imagine that on PvP servers, only about 10% of the requests/complaints come from game problems (corpses stuck in walls, LD and can’t find corpse, etc.), and that 90% come from interpersonal problems. Give them some teeth…I would rather see some of them use a little judgment and make some wrong decisions, than stand there ineffectively doing nothing.

5. For God’s sake, get rid of faction hits on duels. Duels are supposed to be honorable, trust me I think the townsfolk would understand if one of their own fought honorably and was defeated, verses being gangbanged after slaughtering a tough mob. This should be an easy fix.

6. An 8 level range for killing others is insane in some cases…the lower played doesn’t stand a chance. Bring it down to 4.

I'v got plent of other items, but they tend to be race-class specific, and I don't think it servers the purpose well to introduce them into this forum.
