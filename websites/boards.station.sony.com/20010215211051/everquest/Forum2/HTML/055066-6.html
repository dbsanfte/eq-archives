For those who are still looking at the arguments comparing wizards to other classes, I'd like to point out that what people are saying is NOT an issue we see with the other class, but a problem with the wizard class itself.

If the wizard class is supposed to be the masters of magical damage, then we should NOT be doing just as much, or close to the amount of DD damage as the other casters are able to do. Rather than nerf the other classes, wizards would like to see a proper balance. Our DD spells vs your DD spells plus your pets is NOT balanced, and wizard DD spells end up being "not enough" to compensate.

Unresistable roots, AoE roots, increased mana recovery(to let us get back into action sooner), and so on are NOT calls to make us BETTER than the other classes, but are a request/demand that we at least are considered balanced against all other classes, not just other caster classes which DO have their own balance issues vs. melee classes.

Rather than arguing with other classes, we should be asking why melees with the right equipment can outdamage us. There's no spell upgrades which increase our damage output. A level 46 warrior could potentially get a full set of PoG armor, and some uber weapons, then outdamage a level 55 wizard in a battle. This is simply because there is no "power upgrades" to wizards. We get new spells, not through quests, but through rare-drops which are very rare, and which we need to wait on anyway.

In the pre-50 game, we had to wait for the next set of spells to do damage of a different type. While this was annoying, when we DID get new spells, they were MUCH more powerful. Conflagration doing 606 damage at 44 was a VERY nice jump from the previous shock spell. Going to 49, we went from 606 to 1110(more damage had a MUCH longer casting time). At 51, we got draught, 3 second cast doing 643 damage. Not a problem, the mana cost made it worth it, and the short casting time reduced interuptions. BUT, as I have already posted, Voltaic draught should do at least 800 damage for the same cost, or a slightly higher cost. But it does the same damage. How do we go up in level, need to get these new spells, but don't do additional damage? I've posted a list of most of the post 50 spell lines in a previous posting, so won't go into it here.

In short, we want our class balanced, not against the other classes, but against ALL classes.

While this might not necessarily improve wizard ‘balance,’ it would sure go a long way to help with the ‘bad press’ that admittedly we as a class have fostered up to this point, however, it was with good cause.

But let me add a little more and elaborate on this suggestion…

Showing the mana status bar in the group window falls under the category of ‘group feedback,’ of which the wizard class has very little. The lack of wizard feedback to group members compounds the problem of ‘bad press.’ This is because many classes are unable to perceive whatever usefulness the wizard is able to currently bring to the group.

And as we all know, perceptions affect the ‘real’ world – and Verant’s world cannot escape this reality because we, as players, all exist in the real world and bring all the complexities of real life into Verant’s world. If you doubt what I’m saying, just think ‘stock market.’

So how about it? Why not, for once and for all, reconsider the notion that mana DOES NOT have to be so darned mysterious.

Other things that sorta fall under this discussion and related to wizards, their mana, and how a group could better perceive the wizards contributions:

1) Show the mana bar. Ok we discussed this, however allow client-side to disable this feature for those that don’t want it cluttering up their screen.
2) For god’s sake, display a text message that let’s group members see the damage a nuke lands on a mob for, in the form of a number, just like we can see how much damage a meleer does to a mob, in the form of a number. Allow Chat options client side to disable this text.
3) Display a text message for group members showing when a mob has been affected by a caster ‘crowd control’ type spell. A_mob_01 has been stunned by wizze_02. A_mob_02 has been snared by druid_03. This would be beneficial for all casters. Everybody is typing these messages anyway, why not make it more fluid for the players so they don’t have to type these message during a fight. Likewise, show a text message when the effects of these casts on the mob have ceased. This would allow for instant changes in the dynamics of group strategy during a fight. Again, put a client-side chat option to disable this text for those that don’t want it.
4) Display a text message to the group that shows full and/or partial resists, so that group members know when the caster is having problems landing a nuke on a mob. This could help a lot with changing group strategy during a fight without the need for the caster to type frantically type: ‘hving prbs lading nkes…’ Again, put a client-side chat option to disable this text for those that don’t want it.

Basically, put some feedback about what the wizard (and other casters for that matter) is doing to help the group kill mobs. As it is now, many players out there who’s life does not delve too deeply with the ins and outs of EQ (ie the casual player) mostly perceive the wizard as a player that mostly sits over in the corner and tries to joke a lot to keep people liking him/her.
