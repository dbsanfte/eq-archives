If you don't want to make our pets progress like they did in the pre 50 levels to keep up with the mobs and other classes then at least you could give us some summons that would spice up the diversity of the pets in these levels.

Basically they would be pet only wieldable weapons with different utility procs on them. Here are my four suggestions.

1) A sword that procs a self damage shield. Probably lava shield but if you wanted to make it a level 57 summon then go ahead and make it cadeau of flame. This is in no way overpowering since the mage can already cast this on his pet. Just saves some mana and some hassel. And since it's a proc it's not like it's even very reliable.

2) I was first thinking of a snare sword but that would be a bit too powerful so i think it would be better to use the weakest version of snare out there. I'm no expert on the different snare spells but the lowest level necro darkness spell would be a good contender. Very weak movement slow down rate and a small amount of damage but still better than nothing.

3) A slow proc. Pure and simple. A weak slow, not to be anywhere near equal to shaman or enchanter slow but maybe 15-20%. Just so that the mage can throw the group a bone in the event that it doesn't have a real slower.

4) A weak level tashan spell. Maybe one that does -20 to all resists. This would also be nice as a taunt spell.

At first glace this might seem like a bit much but let me add this. First of all, these are procs which if you haven't noticed don't go off too often in the hands of a pet anymore since that fix. Second, they in no way step on the toes of any other classes since they are extremely obsolete to the spells that are currently used by those classes at those levels.(simply adding a pinch of utility to mage pets incase your group is lacking one of these functions but in no way shape or form a substitute for them) And third, something has really got to be done about our 51+ pets. No, really, something has got to be done. Our pets become powerful at level 49 following a fairly consistant rate of advancement but then in the final 10 levels of the game, the 10 hardest levels of the game, they are simply given a few more levels. Meanwhile melee classes are getting weapons that let them hit for 2 and 3 times what they could before kunark came out and we're fighting mobs that 200% and 300% harder than the ones we were fighting at level 49 while our pets get about a 10-15% boost in power. If the mobs i'm fighting have about 4 or 5 times the amount of hitpoints as the ones i was fighting at level 49 then why should my pets only have about 5% more hitpoints than the ones i summoned at level 49? If you check it out you'll find my numbers are pretty acurate.

Like i said, i don't care if you don't want to buff our 51+ pets to a respectable level but at least give them a whole new angle while keeping with the theme of the class.
