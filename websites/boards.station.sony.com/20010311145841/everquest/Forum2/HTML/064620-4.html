I play a 56 ranger, and let me address a few of the concerns you are presenting about Melees from a rangers perspective.

1. Rangers defense = Druid/Shaman defense over 50. We recieved no defensive upgrades with Kunark.

2. We do a lot of damage, but just like casters, when we go all out we die.

3. Our HP is barely higher than casters. Rangers have the lowest HP/AC of all melees. Our armor has lower ac than all other melees, and Cleric and Shaman have similar AC to us.

3. There are balance issues between melee classes. Not just between melee and caster. Rogues and Monks do insane damage and have maxed out defensive skills in all categories. Higher than warriors even. They are damage dealers. Rangers are damage dealers too, but we have caster defense. The tank classes (warrior, paladin, SK, and to an extent Bards) do not do the damage that the damage dealing classes do, with warriors being the only exception as that is all they do; melee, but they still rank behind the damage dealing classes (Now that verant has moved rangers fully into the damage dealer category) but only barely. SKs, Pallys, and Bards do very little damage, but have spells to help them tank/taunt.

4. I have my epics, so in raid situations where I am the only one to have epic weapons (Im the only one in my guild to have finished his epic as of now), I usually make my group KS group. That is until the nuker group decides they want to get the exp, and then there is no way in hell the melee groups will outdamage them. In god type raids its the nukers that make or break the raid. Sure you need enough melees to build up hate, but it is the wizzies, mages and droods that do all the damage. Velious is a different animal and there are serious balance issues for casters there, but not in Kunark or the old world. Try doing Trak or VS without wizzies. Try doing Inny or CT without nukers. Not gonna happen. You cant do these mobs without the quick damage that you guys do.

Now with this said, do I think that casters should have some of the things you ask for in this thread? Yes I do. I do think they need to have much less resistable higher damage spells, and a disc of sorts to decrease cast time is a great idea. I think all casters should have some sort of ability to regen mana at a faster rate that increases as your mana pool does.


So I do support you guys... you guys getting improvements makes us all better. But remember not all melees have 1100 ac and 4k hp, cause unbuffed I have 853ac and 1851hp at level 56 with full planar and Tolans armor. Monks and Rogues have similar hp, but have much higher AC. There is only one class that does a lot of melee damage, and has high HP and AC - Warriors, and they should be the only ones as they are the best all around melee class.

Sorry for babbling... This post intrigued me. Though I saw a lot of closed mindedness on both sides, there are some good ideas being presented here. Verant should take a look but remember... Improve, dont nerf!!
