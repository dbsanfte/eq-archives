Peace of Formosa was a Taiwan-based guild, a collection of hard-core cheaters and exploiters. A great deal of them were in the end permanently banned. Them using an emulator to stage a Sleeper fight is extremely likely.

If I recall correctly, when the Sleeper wakes up, first thing he does is mass death touch everyone in the zone. Then, he sweeps through a number of other places, Skyshrine in particular. Warders will no longer spawn once sleeper is up, they are replaced by Ancients. And respawn is 3 days. I believe the encounter as depicted is impossible on a real EQ server.

As to key to Sleeper's...
Well, first you need a talisman from a First Brood dragon. You can get one from one of living First Broods (like Harla'Dar) at CoV faction expense, or you can get one from renegade Zlandicar in DN, or get a shard of Hsagra's Talisman off any of the Kael bosses.

Then, you need someone with key to Jaled'Dar chamber. Key is obtained by killing Zlandicar, or by gaining faction with Zlandicar, killing Neb (leader of Paebala) and handing Neb's head to Zlandicar.
Hand the talisman to Shade of Jaled'Dar, and you'll get your Sleeper's tomb key.
