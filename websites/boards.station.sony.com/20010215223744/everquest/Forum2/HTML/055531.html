From reading these threads and seeing responses by the people in charge, it seems to me the vision of hybrids is this:
Rangers - offensive
Bards - all around utility (and songs)
Paladin/SK - defensive

Now, with this is mind, why is it that, although Paladins and Shadow Knights are supposed to be THE defensive hybrids, BOTH those classes are the ONLY hybrids that do not have a defensive discipline!

Rangers, at level 60, get Weaponshield. This allows them to parry ALL attacks directed at them for 15 seconds, making them invulnerable to melee attacks in that time.

Bards, at level 55, get Deftdance. This gives them the ability to automatically dodge EVERY attack AND automatically duel wield (off hand chance to swing will be 100%) for 10 seconds, making them invulnerable to melee attacks in that time and doing increased damage.

Now Paladins DO have a defensive skill at 60. Sanctification, making them immune to MOST (and I need a pally to tell me what most includes) types of harmful spells. If this makes them immune to "uber" mob AoE spells, this is an excellent ability. This, however, SILL precludes them 'taunting' a mob off a warrior to share damage, or off a spell caster to keep them alive because, even WITH a higher defensive skill, Paladins just aren't going to last against mobs that may very well be over 60.

Shadow Knights, on the other hand, do NOT have a defensive ability per se. Although it can be argued that Leechcurse, the level 60 discipline that heals self for every point of damage done, CAN be argued to be defensive, it is by no means an effective defensive discipline. Shadow Knights, according to Verant, are NOT an offensive class, we do NOT have the same skill caps in offense or weapons as warriors, rogues, monks, or rangers. We do NOT hit as often, and do not do as much damage, even with the same weapon, due to our lower attack rating.

If we can't hit, and do less damage when we DO hit, this skill is useless. I GUARANTEE you, if I'm supposed to be "defending" the others in my group and raid, and by this I presume to mean taunting and taking hits, the amount of damage I do to the mob and heal myself IN NO WAY will offset the amount of damage I take, being single, double, triple, and even quad hit for 300 to 600 (or more) points of damage per shot.

I propose giving Shadow Knights (and Paladins) Weaponshield. I would suggest level 58, getting a defensive ability 2 levels higher than warriors (5 levels higher than monks and rogues) but sooner than Rangers, we being the 'defensive' hybrid and they being the 'offensive' hybrid. This would in no way "step on the toes" of fighters (warrior/monk/rogue), given their ability to auto parry AND riposte every attack, but still give us the ability to actually 'defend' those in our raid and live to tell about it. The amount of damage we prevent with this will be MUCH greater then the amount of damage we can 'heal' ourselves.

This ability alone would, at the very least, make us a viable option to the fighter classes if no or not enough are available. We wouldn't be AS good, but at least we'd be effective. And if WE are more effective, the whole RAID will be more effective. Fighters disciplines last a few seconds, when their discipline ends an SK could taunt off the fighter, keeping THEM alive, while still allowing them to do the massive amounts of damage they can do. If you want us (including Paladins, they could use this ability as well) to be "Defenders" then you have to give us the ability to defend. Otherwise, it's like telling us you want us to be pilots, and not supplying us with a plane . . .

Whaddaya think?
