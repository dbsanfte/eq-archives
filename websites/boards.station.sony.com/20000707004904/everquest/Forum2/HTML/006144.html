I have been using the ATI Rage Fury for about a year with only minor problems. However, I the begining I was having those sorts of problems. You may need to "Ghost" your drivers.

Bring up System Devices and write down the driver or drivers listed. Reboot and bring up "System Devices" in "Safe Mode". If your "Monitor" (for example) has more drivers listed in Safe Mode" than in "Normal Mode", you have Ghost drivers.

The MicroSoft Tech. told me all the drivers for the Monitor must be removed and then Reboot. Win98 and 2000 are designed to find the correct driver and install it. CAUTION: Only do one hardware per reboot. For example, do display adp. in save mode then reboot. Then do sound etc.

Again CAUTION: Only "Ghost" Modem, Video, Monitor, and Sound. E-mail me if this is to cryptic. Good luck.
