I don't believe that making paladin spells available at ealier levels is a good answer. This will only overpower paladins in the earlier levels. Our buffs are balanced at the level we get them, assuming that there isn't a cleric present in the group.

The problem is a stacking issue, not the strength of our buffs. If we had buffs of comparable strength to the ones we have now that stacked with cleric buffs, it wouldn't be nearly as much of an issue. Then of course, you start to run into the 15 buff limit. <shrug>

Offense
paladins have no dual wield
paladins have lower skill caps in offense and weapon skills
paladins have a lower damage table than pure melee classes

These 3 things don't have a cumulative effect, they have an exponential effect. Paladins currently have the lowest damage output of any class against MOBs with average MR. Maybe this is by design. I honestly don't see this changing, although I do think 2 of the defincies listed above should be enough, there isn't a need for 3. Alternatively, high end weapons that aren't designed for dual wielding can be finally put in the game for paladins. SKs have the MoSS and the Ebon Mace, but paladins really do not have an equivalent weapon.

Please consider raising the bash skill for paladins until it is closer to the cap for warriors. It's kinda your job to figure out how high

Defense
I'm not sure paladins should have a defense skill as high as Warriors, maybe very close, but not the same. I will reserve judgement until I see the results. I realise that warriors have higher AC items available, have more hp, and have defensive disciplines. On the whole, in ordinary xp grouping situations, we tank just fine. It is only in the big game hunts that we lack tanking ability. Some sort of discipline that allowed us to tank better for a short period of time would probably do more to even out this balance without making us tank TOO well in ordinary encounters.

Special abilites and spells
Stuns are the bread and butter of paladins. Stuns are one of the primary benefits of adding a paladin to a group. In the planes, they are the main benefit of adding a paladin to the raid. Our damage output is neglible, but our ability to interrupt casters is unrivaled by any other taunting/melee class.

Stuns are essentially our defining role in the high end game. At least it used to be. Now there are many MOBs that can't be stunned, can't be interrupted, or both. Other melee classes are asking for the ability to stun now. Warriors got stunning kicks with ROK. I believe monks got a kick that will stun with ROK. Rogues were asking for an ability that allows them to stun. Stun NEEDS to be made viable for the paladin again. It was our primary niche. Please allow stuns to interrupt casting on MOBs over 55 even if it does not actually stun them. Please do not hand this ability to more classes.

LOH should be a complete heal post 50. I can't think of a single balance reason why it should not. There are items in game that allow for complete heals with no mana usage. Having one complete heal every 72 minutes for 50+ paladin would not be unbalancing. On the other hand, LOH is simply not the defining skill it was in earlier levels. Now that a tank will regularly go through 3 or 4 times his normal hp in a single, normal encounter, LOH becomes less and less of the "OMG! you saved my butt!" type of thing it was in earlier levels. Now it is "oh, you used LoH on me? err, thanks. Was that after the 3rd complete heal?"

Heals for paladins can't really be made more efficient for paladins for balance reasons, I think most paladins deep down know this. The healing role of most paladins in raids and groups at higher levels is drop a heal or 2 on someone to buy time for the cleric (read as: real healer) to heal them. We don't need better or bigger heals, we need faster heals. Even if that means they are much less efficient.

Please consider giving paladins the yaulp 4 spell. We desperately need some kind of atk buff. Any kind of atk buff. I don't know if you realise just how pitiful paladins are in the damage department.

recap
give us a defensive discipline INSTEAD of making our defense cap the same as warriors.

Get rid of the quadruple whammy on paladin damage: offense, weapon skill, no dual wield, lower damage tables.

Raise our bash skill slightly.

Let us interrupt 56+ caster MOBs with our stuns and bash.

Faster heals.

Stackable buffs? Maybe yaulp 4 instead? or Yaulp 5

My wisdom is pretty high also, for other not so wise races this could pose a serious problem.
