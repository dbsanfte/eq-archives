What some of you guys don't seem to get is that the cleric nerf (i.e. bp nerfs, changes to healing aggro, etc.) affects not just clerics, but ALL tanks and especially warriors as well. Ever since Absor started the class balance/discussion threads, warriors have been saying that taunt is broken at the high end level. It just plain doesn't work, and especially on "uber" mobs, you just can't taunt them off of the casters, off of monks, off or rangers, off of shadowknights. Now, you add aggro to the cleric's best heal and all of a sudden taunt REALLY doesn't work, because in order to keep from getting that much aggro built up the cleric has to use a lesser heal or there's no way the warrior will taunt the mob off of the cleric. So the warrior gets healed less, and keeps the mob on him, but now he needs more heals, which sucks up mana faster and at some point the warrior is just begging for SOMEONE to get it off of him because he can't be healed fast enough. Against "uber" mobs, from ice giants to Naggie and Vox, to Icepaw Kobolds, plane mobs, and all the way up to crazy things like King Tormax or Velketor, tanks can't tank for 30 seconds. It can't be done without chain casting rune, or complete heals.

But the thing that these cleric changes really screws up isn't even healing. By making the cleric class more dangerous to play, they've made sure that "uber" raid-type encounters, like dragons and plane raids, Trakanon raids, Velk's Lab, etc. take more time, people, resources and deaths than before. No one goes on a planes raid, or a raid on Velk's lab, or a dragon raid without expecting to die at least once. For melee classes, this means dying and popping up at whatever god forsaken faraway bind point they are bound at. Now, since there are less clerics, how does the tank get back? Ressurection. The one ability that no other class gets in the game (unless you count the staff of forbidden rites or the paladin revive helm). Now since you expect to die at least once, and if a melee, you are bound far away, the only way back is by res or the mage call of heroes and the only way to regain the experience just lost is res. And to get back to the body quick enough to be able to roll on items you get, you have to have res.

Less resses means more time ressing, more items that decay because they can't get rolled in time, etc. And just boosting the healing powers of druids and shamans just doesn't do it. It doesn't make up for the lack of a cleric.

And then we get GEMS? And this is supposed to make playing a cleric or caster more fun?
