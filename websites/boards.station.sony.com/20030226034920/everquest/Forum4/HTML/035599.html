The level titles are added automatically. The AA titles come after spending the required number of AA points. 6 spent in general gives the first AA title, 12 spent in archtype gives the second AA title and 24 spent in class specific gives the third AA title. After spending the required AA point to give the title you have to zone or re-log before the title appears in front of your name.
