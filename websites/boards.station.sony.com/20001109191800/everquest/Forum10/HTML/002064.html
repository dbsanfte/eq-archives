I guess this isn't really the right board for this discussion, but as long as we can keep it civilized it should be okay..

(This is all my opinion, based on what i've seen in the game)

There seem to be several different types of twinking:

1. Newbie player twinks
A newbie player is given lots of good stuff by friends, eager to get them up to the same level as they are.

This results in characters running around with over the top equipment and making the same newbie mistakes we all did. I have seen quite a few complaints along the lines of 'My fourth level character lost all their bronze armour to corpse decay etc...' They don't learn the game the hard way,, and end up ruining the fun of earning their own stuff.
This twinking really bugs me, and I refused to be twinked by a friend when I started for those reasons.

2. Newbie gift twinks
A newbie player is given a minor item to help them start off

I have accepted ‘twinks’ like this, but only unsolicited and just one or maybe two items. Giving a first or second level character a minor armour piece or lightstone will not make a huge difference, and can be given for small task such as collecting bonechips etc. If I give anything away, I always tell the newbie to pass it on when it is no longer useful..

3. Character twinks
An existing character gives items to their starting character to get them through low levels quickly

I only have one character (at 12th lvl), so I am probably biased against it. There are doubtless many reasons to twink a character like this, and maybe when I have ran through a few characters, I will come to accept it as a way to avoid endlessly running through the lower levels.

To answer your question Sir_denion why wouldn’t I want to group with your character? Because as far as I can see, you haven’t worked to get the equipment you have. If I sold all my gear, I would probably be able to buy an exe axe, or two pieces of crafted, and here you are with it all.

I don’t mind people who give me advice, and human interaction and dissemination of knowledge is what makes EQ more than just 3d baldurs gate, but if a twinked character starts to give me advice, I’m already predisposed against that character. If you had dispensed the same advice in bronze with a mino axe, wopuld you have got the same response? Yes? Well then either they were jerks or your advice was too much like criticism.

I would say that if you want find people to group with, group with other twinks.

About the skills:

Actually, trolls and ogres don't learn weapon skills any slower than other races. The reason is that weapon skills increase on dexterity, not int or wis. I'm pretty sure that both ogres and trolls have a fair dex rating.

Also, at low level (pre 20), that exe axe is not making a heck of a lot of difference in how quickly the mob is being killed. All chars below level 20 have a max amount of damage they can do. Even if they had a 50 damage weapon, they wouldn't be able to do any more than the max, which is around 25 or 26 pre-level 20.

Last, their armor does not make a huge difference, either. A level 30 warrior has a higher AC naked than a level 5 in full crafted. AC goes up with level, as do hit points. Don't get me wrong, having great armor definitely helps, but not as much as you might think. It doesn't make them invulnerable.
