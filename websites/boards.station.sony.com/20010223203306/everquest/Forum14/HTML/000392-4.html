To touch on the mana regen issue...

Pre 50 clerics didn't need mana regen. In fact, no classes did. Clarity was a luxury that enchanters brought to the group and thus made them desirable.

Post 50 things changed. Agro radiuses went up and many areas became undoable without some form of mesmerize. Most importantly, however, we went from 51 to 60 without any increase in mana regeneration. We all cap at 252 at level 51. There is no way to change this without recoding EQ.

Where clerics are left stuck is we have no way to compensate for this. Going from 0 mana to full (an average of 2000 mana) takes a good 10+ min. As such, clerics are literally being reduced in effectiveness post 50 because we cannot function normally without some form of Clarity. As much as people complain about clerics being required, it is as true to say that post 50 clerics require an enchanter.

Yes, we can still heal. But it becomes very, very hairy. In the end, where skill could once compensate for the lack of mana regen this is no longer true. It often pushes clerics beyond their limits.

I believe Verant knows this. All our casting armor and items are geared around manaless casting. The Donal's BP, IMO, was nothing more than a cop out: an option for the highest level clerics to heal without Clarity. A level 51+ cleric can't regen 400 mana in 20 or even 30 seconds. Even worse, they designed mobs around this concept. Even now, there are mobs undoable and, as if it was some final kick to clerics, every mob will forever be in doubt thanks to clerics. People will wonder "Was this designed for the BP?" and they will look at us. And we will simply stand there, unanswering, wondering why we can't heal against the biggest mobs in the game.

But I digress... the point is we need compensation for this lack of regen. More so in Velious where mobs hit harder and faster. Do not tie us to the enchanter to do our jobs. Grouping was supposed to make things faster but instead all it has done is assure us we will go out of mana much more quickly.

A previous poster said it isn't about roleplay, it's about game balance.They are correct. It IS about balance. No, cleric mana regen doesn't make much roleplay sense but it is badly needed to counterbalance the lack of Meditation upgrades post 50. An out of mana cleric is useless and to function effectively we MUST have an enchanter.

Please remove this restriction from us. Please give us SOME form of increased mana regen.

We spoke a bit about these issues at the Fan Faire in Baltimore and I just have a couple of points to make.
