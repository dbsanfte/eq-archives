The problem is NOT that bard damage doesn't scale post-50; the damage is a specific example of the more general problem.

The problem is that other classes abilities improve non-linearly as they go up in level; with this non-linearity becoming more pronounced post-50.

Damage is not a particular strength of bards; and one we get fairly late. It is a reasonable expectation that roles will change over time; the role a bard might play at 20 is not the necessarily same role as at 40, nor is it necessarily the same role as at 60.

(c) Non-Linear Effectiveness
