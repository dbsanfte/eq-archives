Well I've run the gamut of maybe half a dozen message boards, including this one, reading threads on adamantite armor. I think the overwhelming consensus of players (and definately include me in this) is that Dark Elves are getting royally screwed in armoring. I'll even say that High Elves are in the same boat we are seeing as how they need basically the same components. Someone on another board stated that it seems only races that CAN be enchanters are being forced to enchant AS WELL AS imbue their armor. Unfortunately, this doesn't pan out. Gnomes are covered under Brellium armor which does not have to be enchanted in order to be imbued. DItto for Humans (hell they can make several different imbued-only versions of plate armor). So why are Dark and High Elves being singled out in this? Why can every other race in the game but us make imbued only armors, (including plate armor), using gems that are either far easier to get or much cheaper to purchase than Blue Diamonds? Ivory, for example, drops off any mob over level 15 or so and sells to an npc merchant for 10plat or so whereas blue diamonds are only to be found in the Planes and level 50+ dungeons and sell to npcs for 200+plat each. As a result DE and HE smiths are being forced to pay 500-600 plat or more PER BLUE DIAMOND. How is this considered fair, or balanced? As of this writing I can't even make enchanted plate armor because I can't enchant blocks of ore. I'd have to enchant 3 large bricks and then use them to make 1 block in order to make 1 folded sheet of adamantite. This means I'd need 10 blue diamonds just to make a breastplate (if the enchanted chain jointing recipe wasn't non-functional). At 500plat each that's 5000plat just for the enchanting components. This doesn't take into account essence of shadow, or elven blood, or even the ore or molds. Then of course there's the failure rate, which I'd be ok with if this other nonsense was dealt with.
I really feel that the Dark Elf and High Elf smiths deserve a fairer shake when it comes to cultural armor then we are currently getting. Give us an imbued only version of our cultural armors just like everyone else has. This isn't challenging, or fun, or exciting. It's abusive and masochistic.
