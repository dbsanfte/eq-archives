Forged Throwing Weapons

When I saw there was going to be new forgable throwing weapons, I had a brief flash of hope. Finally maybe there would be a semi-decent throwing weapon available. Unfortunately, those hopes were crushed after I saw the new stats. I'm sorry to tell you, but these new weapons aren't worth it. Lets compare a few examples:

Dmg Dly Wt Rng Cost(cp) Stack of 20(cp)
Throwing Knife 5 36 .5 45 11 220
Forged Throwing Knife 5 35 .5 60 ~107 ~2100
Shuriken 4 26 .5 60 11 220
Forged Shuriken 4 25 .5 65 ~107 ~2100
Throwing Axe 6 30 1 120 34 272 (nonstackable, 1 Backpack full)
Forged Throwing Axe 6 29 1 55 ~1070 ~8500 (nonstackable, 1 Backpack full)
Forged Javelin 7 33 2 205 ~1070 ~8500 (nonstackable, 1 Backpack full)

Compared to Fletching

Dmg Dly Wt Rng Cost(cp) Stack of 20(cp)
Hunting Bow 5 43 3.5 50 (MOB drop)
Rough Hickory, Hemp 10 50 4 50 268 (purchase for 1pp or less)
Carved Elm, Silk String 10 39 4 75 5157 (purchase for 8-12 pp)
Trueshot 20 45 100 200000 (Quest or purchase, Ranger only)

Class 1 Wood Point 1 50 5 100 (Field pt, wood shaft, round fletch, large nock)
Class 2 Wood Point 1 150 20 400 (Buy from players 1pp/stack)

As you can see, the only reason to use throwing instead of archery is for roleplay reasons (Unless you are a Bard or caster with no choice) I have also excluded throwing boulders as they drop off of giants and are only usable by large races. At present, my critical hits with throwing knives do 17 damage, less than the base for a Trueshot or other higher level bow. Now I don't expect throwing weapons damage to equal archery (though it would be nice), but I don't think it is too much to ask to have a couple of decent throwing weapons available. The changes I would request are as follows:

First, your costs on existing smithable thrown weapons are too high by a factor of 10.
Second, non-stackable throwing weapons are a complete waste of time and computer space, make throwing axes and javelins stackable.
Third, there needs to be at some changes made in the smithable throwing weapons. I would suggest the following:

Dmg Dly Wt Rng Cost(cp) Stack of 20(cp)
Forged Shuriken 4 25 .5 65 ~10 ~210
Forged Throwing Knife 6 32 .5 100 ~20 ~400 (Buy from players 1pp/stack)
Forged Throwing Axe 12 60 1 75 ~60 ~1200 (Buy from players 3pp/stack)
Forged Javelin 8 40 2 205 ~60 ~1200 (Buy from players 3pp/stack)

This would provide basic throwing weapons, as well as 2 upgrades, and an additional higher damage, short-range option at a price players would actually pay. These changes would not have a big impact on gameplay since throwing and archery are only useful for pulling, but it would provide another option, particularly for rogues like myself that prefer throwing weapons. It would also create a few forged weapons that are marketable, unlike the existing forged weapons whose fine steel counterparts are cheap and readily available. If these would become trivial in the 75-100 range, they would make a nice stepping stone to banded as well. Anyway, these are my thoughts on the subject. Comments, anyone?
