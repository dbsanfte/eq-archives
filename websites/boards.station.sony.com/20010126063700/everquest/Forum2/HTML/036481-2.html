It is a fact that EQ has become less and less of a role-playing environment and due to that fact more and more people are naming their characters "Debbie Doesdallas" or "Poppin Fresh" due to the lack of fantasy "feel" remaining in the game these days.

I've been playing this game since Beta4 and back then (obviously) we were a small community of players. We role-played and all tried to come up with some creative names to match the setting. Later after having reached 20th level we had to petition our last name and so made efforts to do it right. Today with +300 000 players and dozens of servers there is overcrowding and with that comes the bad apples.

Sadly, more and more, Norrath is becoming a place for Dewds and power levelers and that blame falls on a lot of shoulders (Verant's and ours) but nowhere and at no time has anyone managed to convince me that the rights of those who choose to disrespect the game rules, who consider EQ to be on par with Quake or Diablo to be above those who still want to role-play and preserve some essence of what EQ was originally all about.

If you want to act the fool and name your character Flush Medown then go right ahead but consider it fair play when I petition your name in an ongoing effort to clean up the trash.

Don't get me wrong I laugh at those funny names just like the next guy but I enjoy EQ in a different way and Verant has given me the ability to make a difference by ratting some of you out. So I use it like a plumber uses a wrench on a leaky pipe- I fix it.
