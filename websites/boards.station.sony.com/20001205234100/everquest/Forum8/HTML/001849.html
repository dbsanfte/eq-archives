Spiderling silk. Kill spiderlings, rock spiderlings, widow hatchlings, whatever they happened to be called in your area. Two spiderling silks = silk thread. Two unstacked silk threads = silk bandage. Do this and do this often and patchwork will be a breeze. And you can use the bandages for that Bind skill you are going to be needing

Skill up on Spiderling Silks first.
[Spiderling Silk + Spiderling Silk] = silk thread

now, either stockpile these, or /auc them to other players. High-level smithing needs silk threads in some ingredients in some recipies.

This will get your skill up to 20ish, and _never fail_.

After that, either make bandages (2 silk threads), or start on the ruined armor. Your success rate will be much higher. For cash, you can make Leather Padding (silk thread + LQ pelt (almost any type)). Leather Paddings sell for 3-4 pp or so each to players.

Swing over to www.eqtraders.com for more info, including Trivial lists (ie, difficulty of making each item).

All times are PT (US)
