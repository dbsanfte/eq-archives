Chloroblast:
Test - 428 HP / 175 mana (2.45 HP/mana)

Sup Heal, by way of comparison, is listed as 583 HP for 250 mana (2.33 HP/mana). So the HP/mana efficiency of the new spells is worse than the old (reversing the normal trend of greater efficiency with higher levels).
