When I was a young cleric, I had a rusty mace until 9th level (when I could summon a hammer). I did have PW armor, but it was all donated to me. What I did was head to Nro and hunt there. I ended up getting a full set a PW piece by piece, as well as several plat ( a little at a time) over a few levels just from donations.
