Items Summonings

And by reasonable I mean one or two spells an evening as oppose to 1 or 2 a week everywhere else. And since magician spells in general are set as rare drops off the spell table, good luck finding them. All spells of a given level should have equal commonality on the table. As it stands you have dozens of some spells for single instances of others.

Caster vs. Melee damage
---------------------------
Ok on further examination. I have this to say.

After the recent expansion of the hate list. Each HP of damage done in melee counts for more hate that each point done in spell casting. And who gets experience is base on hate list calculations.

So we go back to the day-to-day grind portion of the game with one group doing it all, this is where you make your experience. The nukers fall apart here. It takes 60-120 sec to kill a mob and if the nuker tries to deal the same damage as the tanks in this scenario they quickly run out of mana trying to do the sustained damage of a melee.

To the point where any dual wielding melee over an evening does 30% more damage than a magician plus pet or wizard.
