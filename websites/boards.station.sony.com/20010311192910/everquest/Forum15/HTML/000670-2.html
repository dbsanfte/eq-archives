The list is lacking what I think is the most important thing needed for High level players and that's something that allows us to contribute to the Uber Mobs.

You've increased the difficulty of creatures in Velious, particularly boss mobs. Because of the recent nerfs to manaless heal items it can be assumed that using fewer people and augmenting them with these items is not the way you want to go. So the other route is to throw more people at the creature until it dies. There is a problem with this though, Your system can't handle it. You've shown us that we need numbers to overcome these creatures yet your system won't permit the numbers to gather in the same place. So...in order to make the raid work, the organizers have to pick and choose what will be most effective in this case (what takes damage, what puts out damage, and what mitigates damage). Those three roles are filled by the Warrior, Rogue, and Cleric respectively. These creatures are pretty much what we have left to kill at this stage of the game (55+). Shadow Knights aren't the only ones that don't fit in that equation. You've made these creatures pretty much totally magic immune too, so what are the casters supposed to do? You've basically increased the difficulty by making about half of the classes in the game largely ineffective. I wouldn't be suprised if you were seeing some form of this complaint in nearly all of these class talk threads.

As to how we should contribute to these battles in a maner such that we can justify our lag inducing presence, we don't fit in to the damage mitigation category which leaves us with damage taking and damage dealing. Ideally we should be a combination of both, but as you saw from logs posted earlier, our damage output is pretty low compared to the other classes. As for damage taking, at this point and against a flurrying quad hitting creature our defensive capabilities are laughable. Those two areas are the areas that need improvements though since those are the ones that fit into our class description. But increasing our defense stat by a few points won't cut it. Warriors who don't use their /defensive discipline aren't so much better off then we are when facing the likes of Velketor. There's no reason to believe that this increase will be the solution to our problem when it already doesn't work on someone else who has higher parry, riposte, and dodge skills then we do, unless they use their discipline.

Even though you've divided class abilities up into pieces that fall into categories like solo-ability and group-friendliness, I think when it all comes down to it (and it's all coming down to it now), each class would like to have something significant they can contribute in most all situations. At this stage of the game, most all situations = Multi-group encounters against named HIGH level creatures that drop some amazing items. Shadow Knights (and many other classes) just aren't viable when it comes to these encounters. We hinder more than we help.
