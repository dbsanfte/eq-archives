Well I have some experiences I'd like to pass on while fighting in KC pre and post patch.

PRE: Able to hold my own using celestial and complete as needed. Had mobs agro me but after 50 levels I was used to this and handled it effectively.

POST: I did not notice much of a change in the way mobs aggro using celestial heal. HOWEVER, when using any direct heal spell the reaction was quite a bit different. One superior heal got me aggro'd by a mob that was a bit stubborn about being taunted off. Also whenever a mob was mezzed and I used a direct heal, when mez broke the enchanter was completely safe because I was the main focus of its ire.
It was posted somewhere that you should move away from mezzed mobs when healing. I decided to try this tactic out. It doesn't work. Mobs still loved to come play tag-the-cleric with me.

I've also read that this fix only effects the TARGET if they are 51+. Well I totally disagree with this. While in KC we pulled the Knight of Sathir. This is a level 51+ mob who loves to summon. Our groups main puller was a level 50 warrior. When he returned with this mob (and 2 of his buddies) I had no choice but to cast complete heal as he was almost dead. This immediately sealed my doom.
I was summoned as soon as the heal landed and all three mobs wanted my hide for their collection. I attempted (key word) to cast my divine series but to no avail. EVERYONE else was doing their jobs as well; enchanter tried to mez, warrior taunted, mage nuked but I still was reading "loading please wait..." in under 10 seconds.

This change has made a huge impact on the way I play my main character now. AND YES! my main character is my cleric and will continue to be so. I'll just have to keep my eyes open to any new developments or try to group with another cleric from here on out.
