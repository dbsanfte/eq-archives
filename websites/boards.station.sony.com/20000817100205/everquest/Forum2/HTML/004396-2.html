The 5th Team
--------------------------------------------------------
What if people could be send in exile by their own team?
Here is how it -could- be done:
If.... lets say 100people type the command /exile playername onto another player of their own team (in a specified amount of days), the player will be moved to a 5th team - the ones in exile.
This 5th team can be attacked by all other teams and can also attack themselves.
The player can also send himself into exile.

An other Team -can- now assimilate him into their own team. A dwarven bandit in exile could be - for example - then belong to the darks if they agree to take care of the outlaw.
This could be done, if... lets say 150 players on a team type /unexile playername (in a specified amount of days).
The outlaw then is asked if he wants to be integrated into this team.

----------------------------------------------------------

This really shouldnt so hard to be coded by verant.
With this, the following things would be possible:
- You can play an real outlaw bandit PK, also covered by the rules of the game
- Heavily disliked people can be banned from their own race
- You CAN now play an evil human, elf, gnome, etc - if the evil team agrees to let you in.
- an elf who does not like his own brethen could find a home among the humans and is also recognized by his color
- we now have REAL teams independent of the race - the teams now are like FACTIONS not like races.
- unbelievable possibilities to roleplay!
- Your actions have an effect who you can trust (want to play a invulnerable-samerace-healer to assist an enemy team? you will soon be banned from your own team.

This solution sure needs some adjusting, but all in all - i think its great and its easy to code!
Less work to do it - but heavily improved pvp-gameplay!
