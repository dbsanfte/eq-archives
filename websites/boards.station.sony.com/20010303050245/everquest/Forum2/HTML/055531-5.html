It's pretty late in the game for new ideas.. (If these are in fact new.. This thread is beyond long and I've not read all of it.)

Anyway, I'm trying to think in terms of changes to the SK class that a) would help the classes survivability, and b) would stay in tune with the vision (and reality) of the dark knight the SK is.

Ok Necromancers are born to be liches. They control army's of the dead (well one badass collection of bone chips, anyway), and their role is to sit back, rot, and drain the life force out of every creature they find.

With SK's being necromantic/warriors I envision them as the knights of darkness. Who else but the vampire fits this description?

Whereas the necromancer experiments and plays with all aspects of death and disease, the vampire exists for one sole purpose... To feed.

Ok, with this in mind here's the reality of the situation..

Hybrids in general are lesser version of warriors (in hp and attack ability). This is made up for in different ways for each class.. Rangers have weak hitpoints but are the quisinarts of the forest. Paladins have lower damage but can pretty much heal themselves, allowing them to stay in the game almost twice as long without assistance.

SK's are also defensive tanks in that we don't hit as hard, but we taunt very well and are meant to hold the mob's attention while other classes chew it up.

The problem in my eyes is that our spell selection is not focused at all towards what it is we do or what we're about.

Although it's too late to change I don't really think we need pets. Our pets are basically a DOT with legs.. Like all our spells they're about half our level so they're generally better against low blues than whites, yellows, or god forbid reds..

We have a smattering of DoT's that taunt well, but aren't very focused.. They're hard to get off, and most seem to feel that in the time they're cast we can do more damage with our weapons. Other than that we have a mix of useful/useless (or at least oddball) utility spells.

Now with pets and DD spells I understand the concept of keeping the spells gained at half the level of what an equivalent caster gets. A pet at equal level to an SK would probably make the SK feel secondary to the pet, and overpower the character. DD spells are designed to do damage in line with what any other class might be doing (theoretically).. So if a warrior could pop off the damage of a wizard while beating up the MOB with his twig of mighty thumping, that would be off balance.

DoT's on the other hand are not designed to be devastating by themselves. A necro's DoT's are deadly because they are stackable. He keeps the creature off him and piles up 5000+ damage in maybe 5 spells.. Then he just has to stay alive while they take effect.

If a necro only had one DoT.. Even a really good one.. And had to chain cast it, end to end, to kill a creature.. He'd be dead long before the creature.

Well SK's have a situation similar to this.. They have One or two lines DoT's instead of half a dozen, that don't really hurt the mob as badly as the damage they'd do just fighting anyway. What's worse.. These DoT's are about 10 levels behind the power of the MOB they're expected to use them on.

What I propose is a change in the spell line that would in line idea of SK's as taunters, defenders, and life stealers...

One (this is old): Make HT a lifedrain at exactly half the level of an equal level paladin's heal. (In other words if a paladin heals 500 pts at level X a level X SK does 250 damage and heals 250 points). How this could be out of line or unbalancing I do not know.

Two: change the SK's DoT lines to things that add utility to the character and fit the vision:
Darkness is perfect. Vampires often beguile their opponents before going in for the kill. But the rest? Disease (Disease Cloud and Asytole), that's not us. Heat Blood? Heart Flutter's nice in theory.. But we only get the one spell.

We're vampires.. We drain the life power from our foes and use it to beat them up! All of our offensive/defensive spells should be in that vein..

The spell line that's missing, and would fit more than any other is the LEACH line.. Being able to sap life from an opponent is one unique ability that is not out of line with who we are or the power we should have. It's also the DoT that makes us most group friendly.. Darkness is often overridden by snares, and thus useful for solo but not as useful in groups.. The other lines are too small and go unnoticed (and often are less useful than just plain attacking).. Lifetap extends the life of the person who's purpose is to taunt the mobs.. Hurts the mob.. and lessens the amount of healing the clerics need to do to keep the SK going.

Now I get the feeling that Verant was afraid they were going to overpower SK's creating a tank that has no downtime, but this simply isn't the case. Give a SK leach spells, even at the same level as the necros.. And they're still going to get beat down severely. The leach spells are designed to mitigate the damage a necro does to HIMSELF in the course of a combat. No spell in the game will mitigate the damage a like leveled mob does.

Ok, after all that ranting that's my suggestion..

SK's are the vampire knights of EQ. They don't need DD, increased damage, or in my opinion even pets. But give them say darkness and leech DoT's of comparable level to the necros who get them (feel free to remove the other DoT's that don't fit the class), and I think you'll have a class that people can sink their teeth into.. Cast Times: These are not appropriate for the higher lvl SK's, with
