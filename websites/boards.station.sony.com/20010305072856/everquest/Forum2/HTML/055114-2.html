I actually asked the rogues of the Safehouse for their opinions on this, and here's the long list of possible things we've come up with (note that these are not all viable options, and are not listed in any particular order, but from a large group a rogues gathering ideas, this is what at least some of us would like to see done with the rogue class):

POISON
A) Make more affordable (vials, etc). With most success rates, mastering poison can costs thousands of plat, even assuming you kill hordes of green creatures for components and make your own vials with pottery. Possibly improve the drop rate on components. Possibly even make the suspensions brewable to reduce the cost on that end.
B) Stackable poisons. Something similar to the shaman alchemical potion bag would be nice, possibly even quest for it, but carrying a backpack that will only hold 8 poisons is kinda hard. Or even, instead of charged poisons, something similar to alcohol where the number of vials in the stack is shown as the lil' number on the icon (each vial in the stack still being just one charge). However it can be changed, that is one of the primary requests of almost every rogue spoken to (even though we've been told before that it is impossible with the currect code, it's still something we're asking for).
C) Change to a short-term random proc of the poison instead of a 1-shot use on first attack, similar to the poisons received when fighting Snakes or Spiders. It's not likely that all the poison comes off the blade, on the very first hit of combat. If it proc'd then it would also not go off on the very first hit. But, say, proc up to X amount of times randomly before you zone. I can see how some would get the impression that on the higher DD poisons this could be very over damaging, but think this is all by chance. You have just as much chance as it going off on the mob you really want to have it go off on, or it could proc all X amount of times on the very mob you don't want it to proc on
D) Allow poisoning range slot weapons. 'Nuff said there.
E) Make applications always succeed, and base the apply time on the Apply Poison skill.
F) Create a line of poisons that are drinkable, rather than applied on the weapon -- some nasty effect on the wielder rather than on the opponent. Classic ideas of this sort can even be found back in Shakespeare -- the poison Juliet drank to feign death for a day, anyone?
G) Work a bit more on the resists against poison -- it can be rather annoying to spend a minimum of 2pp on to upwards of 15pp, assuming we succeed on the first try at making the poison, only to have it resisted when we try to use it.

EVADE (big request)
Allow Evade to work while moving. The bug where if you are moving or getting hit while trying to evade, and getting the message "You must be perfectly still to hide!" should be fixed. This is very important. Perhaps when a mob is beating on you, you couldn't very well just dissapear while he is making contact to you, but, it does not make sense for you to have to be still to have to duck away from the combat. Evade should work better on higher level encounters too. Countless times have I evaded successfully with many high level taunters there, and the mob just won't get off me.

SEE INVIS
A) Should not work on hidden rogues. We are hiding in shadows, not invisible, which is why hide works on both live and undead creatures at the same time. Give casters a See Shadows spell to see rogues, but stop letting See Invis work.
B) We are not hiding on accord of magic, it is all skill. See invis is to detect people invisible to the naked eye by magic. No see shadows spell, because no magic should be able to see a camoflauged rogue.

GENERAL OFFENSIVE/DEFENSIVE
A) Offense is just fine. Rogues are the masters of melee damage, and should stay that way.
B) Defense has a few things that can work out with it, mostly dealing with the evade skill (see below).
C) I think rogue riposte and parry skills should be slightly increased. I know rogues combat-wise are already excellent, but we should be able to avoid damage a little better. Sure, if hit, it should (and does) hurt a TON, but we should be able to avoid it more.

CURRENT SKILLS
A) Instill Doubt: works very rarely, even at 200+ skill levels. Also, fix problem of backstab not working immediately after an ID, but the button not being greyed out -- either grey out the backstab button when ID is used, or allow backstabs immediately after a successful ID.
B) Disarm: works very rarely, even at 200+ skill levels.
C) Swimming: I'd like to see the amount of time we can stay underwater doubled. I think this is definately in-character for a rogue; this applies for monks also.
D) Safe Fall: Let us master Safefall. Heck, raise the cap to 200. Who do you see jumping off a three story building and not taking any damage? A cat-burgler or a Tibetian monk ? It hardcaps at what, 94? Thats level 19? You think I'd learn something new in 40 levels.

I. Poison

a) Poison vials need to be able to be combined with charges like a potion. There is no reason either mechanically or gameplay that they can't. If they can't be combined, just like potions, they take up too much room in our inventory to be carried.

b) Poison Interruptions- When attacking a mob, if you are moving only a little bit poison casting is interrupted. If you are attacked in the middle of the proc, it will be interrupted. This should not happen. Give me a reason gameplay wise that an applied poison can be interrupted? Don't know how hard it would be to code, but look at it.

c) Poison Application- How hard can it be to put poison on a knife blade? But we still fail at this quite often, and skill raises are few and far between.

II. Evade

a) Moving- If you are moving, or hit with small knockback, etc, while you evade, you fail. Seeing as how evading is 'Ducking out of combat' I don't see why moving prevents it from working. This occurs because evade is bound on the same key as hide. Either find a way to fix this, or move Evade to it's own button that doesn't check for movement.

b) Hate List- Sometimes this ability doesn't work at all. And you can't evade onto a pet (Mob will not switch aggro to npc's). Please consider some of these things when you make any changes to the evade skill. And realize that this one skill is EXTREMELY important to the Rogue's viability. It is the only way we survive.

III. Grouping- Rogues still have problems finding groups pre-40. Yes, after 40 (and after 50 especially) we come into our own, but before this we really don't offer much to a group that varies from other people.

IV. Soloing- While I know you don't want to look at soloing when doing these threads, just remember that the Rogue is the only class in the game that has to farm green mobs for hours to be able to solo post 20. Unless a rogue has a lot of money to spend on poison components (Twink or good trader) it takes hours on end to collect enough components to *maybe* be able to solo a blue mob.

V. Illusions

a) It would be nice if they would zone with us. Not needed, but added functionality (Enchanters have been asking for this too)

b) Allow us to click them off like you can with SoW and other buffs. Give that to enchanters too. That should make everyone happy.

VI. Skills

a) Disarm- Worthless skill unless in PVP. Weapons have no effect on mobs besides procs, and we can't disarm magic weapons so...

b) Instill Doubt- Nice skill outdoors. Is extremely hard to raise. Absolute death if used in a dungeon. The backstab doesn't work afterward because of the 'kick' damage component associated with ID. Just grey out secondary attacks after instill doubt is used successfully.

c) Safefall- It's level is fine with me as far as Verants interpreation of the rogue.

d) Sense/Disarm Traps- Some traps just can't seem to be sensed, even with a maxed out sense skill. I can see the big rock crashing down from the ceiling in Sol B. I can stand in front of it. I can disarm it, but I can't detect it...

e) Backstab- Would be nice to see a message along the line of critical hits, but we get enough spam already.

f) Pick Pockets- One of the banes of our existence. Necessary to get our epic, pisses off some people in groups (It hits them in their wallets). Something needs to be done with this, but I don't know what.

g) Assassinate- Is a neat ability. Don't touch it. Some people are complaining that it doesn't kill Velious mobs in one hit. My response is "So?".

VII. Armor

a) Ravenscale is annoying to get. You have to random with others in your group, where as other classes get their quest armor pieces automatically. Change Ravenscale so that it's a quest.

b) Assassins Armor- Is this stuff Tunare's joke? It looks horrible. No assassin would be caught dead or alive in flashy gold armor. You say we can't have a better particle effect on Ragebringer because it wouldn't be "roguely"? Have you SEEN this armor?

c) Mrylokar's Armor- Boots and Gauntlets are still the 2 rarest piece's of Kunark armor. Do a server count and you will see what I mean. Maybe people are hunting in these areas, but I am more inclined to believe that the drop rates and spawn times are lousy.


That's my initial response. Let's see where this goes.
