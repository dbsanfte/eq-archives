    August 17, 2000:  I am happy to anounce a huge, cool new addition to the site.  We have now added the Everquest Strategy Discussions.  It allows for strategy discussions based upon race, class, skills, and eventually zones (the zone discussion is not active yet). It is set up in a standard threaded format, and can be realigned to your own specifications.  The idea is to post information you believe will be of use to other players.  The information that is posted is then rated by everyone who reads it as to how useful it is, so that people looking for information can find the most useful first and just eliminate those posts that are not worthwhile.  I look at it as a place we can both teach and learn from each other.  After 15 posts, you will be assigned a karma rating, which determines the level at which all of your future posts will be rated.  Also, those that maintain a high rating will get assigned titles and get their posts highlighted.  A more detailed explanation of how the section works, and how your ratings will be determined is in the strategy frequently asked questions section.  As we develop sites for other games, such as Diablo2 and Shadowbane, this karma rating will carry over to those other boards as well.

    Since it is new, there is not yet a lot of information there.  I would encourage the experienced players to go over there and add in your hard earned knowledge of the game for others to read and share. As with everything else on the site, it is your input and participation that makes this whole thing work.
