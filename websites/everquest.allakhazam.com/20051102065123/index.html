October 31, 2005

SOE has announced the death of gaming artist Keith Parkinson, whose credits include many of the original EverQuest designs and posters, including the "life-breath" of Firiona Vie. He moved on from the world of EverQuest in 2002, co-founding Sigil Games Online, and there took on the role of art director. SOE has posted some more accolades of his career, as well as some of his artistic creations in an announcement on EQLive.

Keith Parkinson died of leukemia on October 26. He was 47.

Some random issues are addressed in the latest Halfling Hodgepodge "extreme" developer question & answer article. Paladin stun aggro, shroud power, and Crushed Diamond Dust are among the items covered.
