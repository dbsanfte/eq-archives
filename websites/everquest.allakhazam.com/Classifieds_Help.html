    How to Buy and Sell Items using the AllaKhazam Classifieds

    These ads are strictly for in game transactions using game currency.  The classified ads are a great way to get rid of items or to find items you want without going through the time and effort of /auctioning them.  They are also a great way for craftsmen to advertise their wares and for players to find craftsmen on their servers.  If you are a jeweler, tailor, smith, etc., I would suggest you add all the items and prices for your craft under your character so that you can arrange to make your sales at your convienence and that of your customers.  Or better yet, get a premium membership and list yourself as a merchant right on top of the lists.  There is also an added feature that you can see everyone who is wearing each item by clicking on the pricing data in the item screen.

    Placing an Item for Sale:  Anyone can use this feature.  You must list your character as public to sell items.  In the character screen, you need to go to tools to get to the screen where you can add items.  You can then add items for sale by typing in the name if the item and hitting "attach more items".  Pick the item you want to sell from the list and change it to "Put up for sale" and add a price to the slot next to it.  Leave any other items at "discard".  Then click OK and that item will be added to your for sale list.  Keep doing this for each item you want to sell.  Premium members can also place items for sale by going to the item listing and using the premium form at the bottom to add the item to their character's for sale list.  Be sure to delete an item after you make the sale or you will keep getting e-mails on it.  Also, make sure that the e-mail address you have listed is the one you want people responding to.  When you get an e-mail from a potential buyer, you simply have to arrange the time and place in the game where you want to make the sale.

    Placing an Item in the Want to Buy List:  This feature is only available to premium members, although anyone can browse the list and sell items to those who have listed them  The method is identical to how you place an item for sale, only chose the want to buy option instead.

    Buying or selling from the classified lists:  Click on the appropriate list under equipment, and you will get to a list by server of all the items people have listed under their characters.  This list gets updated every hour.  If you click on the person's name, it will bring up the character profile with every item he or she is offering and every itme on his or her want to buy list.  Above the item list is that person's e-mail.  You can arrange the time and place and final price of the sale through e-mails.  You can also find out who is wearing, wants to buy and has an item for sale from that item's individual page.  This is particularly useful if you have done a character upgrade search to find the items that are the best upgrades for the equipment you are wearing.  That is listed under the link that says "more".
