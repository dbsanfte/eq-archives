    January 9, 2003

    Today's patch is a big one.You can now play Everquest in a window.  This is a huge change for users of this site.  You can now open up the site in your browser, launch Everquest, and use alt-tab while in game to browse our content and get advice while you are actually playing the game.  If you have been reluctant to pay the extra three bucks for our premium service up until now, you might want to rethink it.  If you want to spend as little time as possible looking up information and as much time as possible playing the game, you really have to get our premium membership.  Both to help out our current premium members and to show those who haven't yet joined what they could do, I've listed below just a couple of ways to use the premium features when you alt-tab out of the game
     
    • Shopping in the bazaar?  When you see something interesting, alt-tab out.  Then use the advanced equipment search to look up items of similar class/race/slot/stats.  Hmm.  That item isn't as good as you first thought.  There are five other items with better stats that sell for about the same price.  Good thing you didn't waste the plat on it.
    • Found a trade skill item and are wondering what recipes it works in?  The premium trade skill search brings them right up.  Moreover, when you go to make the item, you can just alt-tab back and forth between the full recipe page and the game to make sure you get each step right.  The regular recipe page doesn't lay out every step on one page like that.
    • Use that equipment by zone list.  It is going to save you a ton of time and effort.  If you are hunting in a zone, alt-tab out to the list and on one page you will see everything that drops in the zone with the stats of each item, what Mob drops it and what level you need to be to get it.
    • Use the advanced equipment search to the fullest.  Found something new and are wondering what it is for?  Look it up.  Want to know where to go to find the highest ac breast piece for your barbarian warrior?  Alt-tab out and do a quick search.  Looking for a focus item?  One that boosts backstab?  One with Haste?  One with the best combination of ac, wisdom and stamina?  Alt-tab out and do a quick search and then head for the zone to find your camp.  The guy you just saved is a 250 Smith and offers to make you whatever you want?  Alt-tab out and search for the best crafted items you can use so that you don't waste it on something wimpy.
    • Now that you can alt-tab back and forth to see what you are wearing and enter it, it is easy to maintain a character on the site.  Keep your character updated and then use the equipment upgrade search to find items available to you that are an upgrade to what you are wearing.  Put them in your wish list and then when you are in the game, alt-tab back and forth to check your list and see where you need to go to get the upgrades.
    • Use the quest search to look for quests for your class in the zone you are in.
    • Just dinged to a spell level?  Alt-tab over and click on the spell search and bring up your new spells and then click on each spell to read all about them.  Premium members get a list of vendors who sell the spell.  Now you know where to go.
    • There are a ton of other ways to use the site with the new interface, but this will get you started.
    This may sound like a hard sell, and I guess in a way it is, but I really believe that with the new alt-tab ability our premium search features give you a serious leg up on anyone who doesn't have the same access.  With it, you are going into each situation prepared, and without it you are basically flying blind.  Before, you could get away with spending a ton of time offline doing research and not using the additional searches, but now you are going to need to get that research done fast, while you are in the game, and the old way just won't work.  For around three bucks a month, it seems kind of silly not to take advantage of all that. Here is the signup link.

    December 31, 2002

    Happy New Year everyone.  It's been an amazing year for us here.  I get so many hundreds of e-mails every day that I don't always get a chance to thank everyone who submits information individually, so let me take a minute here to thank everyone who submitted information over the past year.  We really appreciate everything you have done for us.  Without you, this site would not be what it is.  I'd also like to thank all of you who visit us regularly, post comments, submit feedback and make this a vibrant community in its own right.  Heck, for that matter, I just want to thank everyone who comes here and uses our site in any way.  Finally, I want to give a heart felt thanks to our premium members.  /Cheer.  Without you, this site would not exist at all in any form, so we all owe you a debt of gratitude.  We've made some great additions over 2002, like the recipe database, the spell database, the wish list, the want to buy list, vendor listings on items, and extra quest and equipment search abilities, not to mention expanding our database to over 16,300 items, 2,100 quests, 10,500 NPC's and 3,000 recipes.  We now have almost 200,000 registered users and have also gone over a half million comments posted on the site, which is simply amazing.  Expect this to continue over 2003.   In fact, 2003 should be an interesting year for us.  We're not a big corporation, like some other sites.  We're just a couple of players who love games and decided to catalogue what we know and what others would submit to us to share with the rest of the players.  Of course, in the beginning we never imagined it would get this big, but, don't worry, after four years of this we are up to the challenge.  We already have a couple of new things in the works for both EQ and DAoC that I think will be great additions to the site.  We will also be expanding into the next generation of MMO games, so those of you who plan on switching over to such games as SWG, EQ2 and EQOA will still be able to come here and get the same quality of information you have learned to expect from us.  And of course we will continue to cover all of the changes and expansions to EQ and DAoC and report new equipment, quests etc. that are found.  Here's hoping that all of you will continue to come along for the ride.  Again, thanks to everyone and have a happy and prosperous new year.
