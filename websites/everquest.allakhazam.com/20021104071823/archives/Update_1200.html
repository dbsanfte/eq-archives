    December 25, 2000:  Merry Christmas Everyone.  For those of you who found Everquest under your christmas tree this morning, welcome to Norrath.  I'm betting that you will soon be telling the santa in your life that this was the best gift ever.  Of course, it is likely that they won't be seeing as much of you for the next few months as you immerse yourself in the game.  If you are new to the game and are looking for information on it, you have come to the right place.  I have tried to assemble a complete information resource here, and if I don't have the information, I try to send you to a site that does.  There is a lot here, so take your time and check out each section.  You should especially check out the quest and equipment sections, and read through the forums and the strategy sections.  Feel free to register in the forums and post questions.  With a few notable exceptions, most of the regulars here are happy to answer your questions, and new posters are always welcomed.  Have fun.

    December 20, 2000:  I just wanted to remind people that part of what makes some of the features work on the site is your continued input.  This of course means submitting new items, quests, skills, etc. that you discover as well as sending in corrections on those that are already listed.  It also means rating up and down the comments added to the items, quests, mobs and strategies by the other players.  This lets everyone get past the drek and quickly find the more interesting information.  And, please keep adding prices to the items whenever you have a good idea of their price.  Because the database started out with very few prices in it, we have defaulted the price part of the character update search to include items that do not have prices (if not, you would have found very few items when you did a search).  Thus, the more prices that are added in, the more accurate this search will become.  Also, the more prices added to a single item, the more likely that it will be priced correctly, so feel free to add a price to an item even if someone else has already done so.  Thanks.

    December 2, 2000:  I am happy to announce that the new equipment database is up and running.  As usual, thank Illia for doing a great job on the programming.  This can be used in several ways.  In its simplest form, it can be used in the same way as the old lists.  Just click on a slot and you will get a list of all the items for that slot, just like the old list.  The order of this list can then be changed,and it can be filtered down to a specific class (and stat, assoon as we add that in) if you want.  A more complex search can bedone using the search feature.  This lets you search for a specific term, or narrow a search by class, slot, item level, item price, and stat. And, for an even more in depth search, you can enter your character's information, including the items he or she currently owns and the amount of platinum pieces you have to spend, and do a search for updates that will tell you which items are better than the ones you currently have that you can either afford to buy or can reasonably hunt for at your level.  I think this is the coolest feature of the database.  Be sure to check it out. (Though the equipment you can afford feature won't work too well until more pricing information gets entered into the database).  The equipment database is (or will soon be) integrated with the quest database and with Illia's Beastiary, so that you can immediately go to the quest description if the item is part of a quest, or go to the Mob description it the item is dropped by a Mob, and you will be able to click to the item description from the quests and Mobs.  And, as with the rest of the database,you can add your own comments to each item, and rate the comments that other people have added.  As with the quests, there is a submit a correction button that opens an e-mail for you to submit corrections or new information on the item.  If you have information that you believe should be added to the body of the description (such as what Mob drops the item), please use this rather than adding it as a comment.  Itis much more likely to get added that way.

    Just a note.  Although we are launching the database today, there are still a few things we plan on adding to it over the next few days.  I just wanted to get it up and running prior to the release of Velious on Tuesday, mainly so I didn't have to keep adding each new item to both the old equipment lists and the new database. Also, being brand new, most of the items do not yet have a price on them.  If you know the going price of an item, please add it in.  Some of the search features rely upon the items having prices, so it would be a great help if everyone can add in prices when they know them.  Thanks.
