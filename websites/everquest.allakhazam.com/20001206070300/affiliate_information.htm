Affiliate Information

Requests about affiliating with my site are welcome.  I am looking for sites that fit the following criteria:

1)  You cover information which compliments and expands upon that covered by this site, and does not just repeat it.

2) Your site contains good information and has been updated frequently.  Graphics aren't a real concern to me, but content is.

3)  Your site has been around long enough to demonstrate that you are willing to put the time and effort into it to make it a great resource for its visitors.
