May 10, 2000:  NOTICE:  Man do I hate to do this, but I feel I have no choice.  The message forum has just gotten out of hand lately. My view of the forums is multifold.  One, they give people new to the game a chance to ask questions about things they can't otherwise find on the site, and get them answered by the many veterans who come here.  Two, they give the new and experienced players a chance to argue and debate topics on the game.   Three, they allow all players to share information about what they have discovered in the game or speculate about what might be found in the future.  Four, they allow people to post messages to be read by the EQ community.  And finally, they allow people around the world with similar likes and interests a chance to talk together and meet when they can't get on the game itself.  I have tried my best to not censor the boards more than absolutely necessary, and have tried to let people post whatever they want, figuring it is really your forum, not mine, and you should decide what you want to talk about.  I have always believed in the right of everyone to have an opinion, even if I don't actually agree with that opinion, and have let any legitimate or even not so legitimate topic be posted and debated.  But many of the threads on the boards are going beyond opinion and are becoming simply trash and flame.  Curse words are starting to be used far too liberally.  (A few choice curse words are sometimes an effective method of emphasis, so I have tried not to use a filter, but too many people rely upon them to make their whole point).  Generally taboo and adult subjects are being discussed with far too much directness.  I'm a pretty easygoing person, have seen just about everything in my lifetime, and am not generally bothered by too many things, so when I see posts that disturb me, I know it has to be bothering a lot of people.  As a result, I am changing a few things about the forum.  First, I have added a few word filters.  I may add more later if necessary.  Second, you will have to register now to post, and registrations will be confirmed by mailing your password to your e-mail address.  (I'm going to leave the webbbs message forum open to unregistered for now).  Third, there will only be one registration allowed per e-mail address.  Hopefully, these changes will address the problems we have been having.  if it doesn't, the next step is to appoint a person or persons to moderate every thread.  I really don't want to do that.  What's frustrating it is that I know the trouble is from a very small number of people, and I hate that it has to effect everyone.  None of this is irreversable, and I personally hope that maybe those who are causing the troubles will go away and we can go back to allowing completely open posting.  I can tell you that it really bothers me that I have to do this.

I feel like I should change the subtitle of the site to Your Kunark Community, since not a lot has been added for the rest of Norrath lately.  I'm not ignoring the other zones, but nothing has really changed or been added there recently.  Or at least nothing that has been sent in to me.  So on with the Kunark updates (and a few non-Kunark ones):  Fashion Table ; 1 Handed Slashing Weapons ; 2 Handed Blunt Weapons ;  Chest Slot ;  Hands Slot ; Legs Slot ; Piercing Weapons ; Shields ; Wrist Slot ; Firiona Vie Quests ; Cabilis Quests ;  Lake of Ill Omen Quests ;  Crushbone Quests ;  Dreadlands Zone ;  Paineel Zone ; Guild Links
