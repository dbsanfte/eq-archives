    February 14, 2002

    Happy Valentine's Day.  We have a sweet treat for V-day: a new spell database. Every spell and item effect now has a complete description.  You can search through the database by class and level or just search for a particular spell (other brings up the list of item/NPC spells, which is pretty cool to browse).  It is also linked to the equipment database.  You can click on the effect listed on the item to go directly to the description.  In addition, the spells list every item that has that effect.  Plus, the spells are linked to their scroll which lists where it is dropped or bought.  We've got much of the drop info for the higher level spells, but are still getting and entering the information on where many of the lower level scrolls are sold.  It's a big job.  Our ultimate goal is to link every scroll to the vendor who sells it, but that's going to take a while.  The list is linked from the menu, or you can go to this link to check it out.  Oh yeah, and right now the abilities are showing up as levl 1 spells.  We're working on moving them to the right area.

    The spells are done similar to the items and quests, with the ability to add comments to them.  Please help out by going through and entering your comments on how to best use the spell, or what to watch out for, or any trick or strategy particular to that spell.  Take the opportunity to share your knowledge with the community.  I hope that this can develop into a place where you can really learn and debate the merits of the various spells and find out all you need to know about how they work.
