    November 13, 2001:  We've changed the strategy forums so that you can no longer post anonymously.   I don't really like doing that, but some pretty obnoxious and vile posts had been added there lately, all by anonymous posters (the erudite and ranger forums being the worst).  Those who have posted here a long time know that I'm pretty liberal in allowing all types of posts, including flames.  The rating and filter system generally does a good job of raising up the good posts and dropping the bad ones into oblivion, and for the most part I feel everyone should get their say on things no matter how stupid they might sound.  But there are limits beyond which a decent person doesn't go.  By making you have to post under your name, it means that all of your posts can be rated and contribute to your karma.  Your karma not only dictates if you ever get a posting rate title, but also is the level at which all of your posts start.  Thus, if your karma ever drops below 1.5, your posts will effectively disappear from the normal filter setting as soon as you post them.  I will also ban posters who consistantly break the rules.  In general, I'd rather let the rating system work against (and in favor of) posters, since if someone is so bad that his posts drop below that 1.5 average, it is the obvious consensus of everyone who posts here that he is not worth hearing from, but in the worst cases I will ban someone from posting.

    It is both free and easy to register to post. Just click on login in the index and the create an account and register a username and e-mail address and you will be sent a password. (or click here).
