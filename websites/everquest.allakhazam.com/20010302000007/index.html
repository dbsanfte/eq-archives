    February 20, 2001

    I'm really excited about today's addition.  When I was at the Fan Faire in Baltimore, there were a couple of guys selling official Everquest items, including some really cool t-shirts, mousepads and keychains.  The company is called RPGwear, and right now they are the only company licenced by Sony to sell official Everquest T-Shirts.  They have some great renditions of the Everquest box covers and also some unique designs that only an EQ fan would understand (which I think is cool).  I thought to myself that I just had to hook up with them to get their items listed on my site so that everyone here could check them out.  Of course, as usual this turned into a bigger project than I imagined.  I took their items and combined them with the existing amazon.com book section and added in a few other sections and basically created an Allakhazam store.  Of course the best part of it is the RPGwear stuff, which is perfect stuff for all of us Everquest fanatics (although I am also pretty fond of the science fiction and fantasy book reviews).  Plus, as a bonus, RPGwear put together several special package deals for you, including a special price for all three cover art t-shirts and a  "Got Sow?" package deal.  Click on the Phat Lewt Button (or just click here) in the index to get to the store and see what is available.

    February 19, 2001

    I have a nice sized update today.  Keep an eye out here.  We have a couple of nice additions planned for the next few weeks.  Look for a whole new section, perhaps as early as tomorrow if I can get it done in time, where you can get officially licenced Everquest items.  Also, you may have noticed that you can now attach items to your character's inventory and in a wish list of items you want to get.  I normally wait until something like this is done to post anything, but people were wondering about the new areas in the character section.  This is still really in the testing stage, but we hope to have it set up so that you can also buy and sell items (for platinum pieces of course) quickly and easily here without having to mess with the auction, which can be ponderous.  In effect, you will be able to set up your own store or find the item you want without spending hours in Greater Fay /auctioning.  Plus, you will be able to view all of the characters that have been marked as public to see what everyone else is wearing, along with a few other cool things.
