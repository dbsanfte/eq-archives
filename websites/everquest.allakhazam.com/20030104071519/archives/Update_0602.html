    June 24, 2002

    It's been a while since I mentioned this, so it bears repeating.  There is a virus out there called the Klez virus that fakes the address it is coming from, so that it may appear to be coming from this site.  It isn't.  We have nothing to do with it and no control over it.  Do not open any attachment to an e-mail that appears to come from here.  Heck, don't open any attachment in an e-mail, period, unless you are absolutely certain about the source.  And do yourself a favor and install a virus protection program onto your computer.  While I'm on the subject, if you have installed a program like Kazaa or Morpheus, you may not realize that they install scumware in your computer which will take over your browser and pop up ads on you while you surf the web.  If you are a premium member and are getting popups, or if your virus program is going off when you are on the site, that is probably the explanation.  I suggest you download and install a program called ad-aware that looks for these programs and deletes them from your computer.
