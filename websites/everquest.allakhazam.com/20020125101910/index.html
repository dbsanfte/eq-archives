    January 22, 2002

    If you know the result of a quest, or additional information on an item, such as what drops it, or corrections on something entered wrong, go ahead and enter it as a comment like many of you have been doing, but also please copy your entry and paste it into an e-mail to allakhazam@allakhazam.com.  Thousands of comments get entered every day.  We would need to have something like 5 people working 8 hours a day just to go through the comments and try to move what is valuable up to the main description.  There are some people doing that, but not to that extent, so if you really want to make sure that your information gets to the right person, e-mail it to me.  And if you see a particularly good comment someone else entered that, say, lays out the solution for a quest that is still listed as unsolved, copy it and send it to me.  I guarantee anything e-mailed to me will eventually make its way into the database.
