    September 9, 2002

    As I mentioned on Friday, we know that the trade skill section is still not complete.  Fletching and Human Smithing are two that we haven't entered yet, for an example.  We hope to have everything entered by the end of the week when the premium sections close off.

    We also plan on gathering all the vendor information and adding it in much like we now have for the spells.  For that we will need some help, expecially from the enchanters and bards out there.  If you have a character with a charisma over 110 and can spare us a little time, we would like a little help gathering information.  Please log into the game and turn on your eq log and then systematically go to every vendor in the zone you are in (and any others you are willing to travel to) and click on every item they have for sale.  Just click on them to get the "That'll be x platinum x gold for the sosandso." message. You can go right down the line of items for sale quite quickly and just move from vendor to vendor recording the info.   Don't worry about the things that were sold to the vendor and wouldn't normally be sold by him.  If we get enough submissions, we will be able to look for inconsistancies and weed out those items.  Once you are finished, camp out and e-mail that portion of your log file to illia@allakhazam.com (include the zone name in the correspondence).  We will parse all that information directly into our database and hopefully will build up a list of every item that is for sale in the game, with where you buy it and approximately how much it costs.  And please, your charisma must be over 110 in order to do this so that the prices will stay consistant (also ideally, we would like you to be neutral to the vendors, but will just have to deal with those variations).  Thanks.  We appreciate the help.

    OK I promised something cool for the near future and even though we are not quite done entering all of the information, we decided to roll it out today so that you have it for this weekend.  We now have a complete Trade Skill Recipe Database.  As with everything on the site, the recipes are available to everyone for free, but premium members get a much more complex version of the recipes as well as advanced search and linking features.  For now, the entire section is available to everyone while we test it and work out the kinks and enter the rest of the information, so feel free to put it through its paces and see how it works.  Next Friday, we'll close off the premium sections.  There are links to and from all of the items and ingredients, so when used in conjunction with the equipment search you can find the recipe for any player made item or any trade ingredient you can find or buy.  I want to thank Steve over at EQTradeskills for sending us excel files with all of his recipes on them and also Niami at EQTraders for giving us permission to use the recipes from her fantastic site.
