    August 14, 2001:  Just thought I would run down a few of what I feel are some of the nicer features of the site, in case you've been just stopping by and reading the updates and haven't checked out everything else:

    1.  The Character section.  This one is my favorite.  Enter your character's information and how much money you want to spend and then look to see which armor is available at that level/price range.  You can also use this section to add items to the classifieds.  Expect some nice upgrades to this soon.
    2.  The Forums.  We have two sets of forums, both of which are very busy and entertaining.  The general Ubb forums are great for general conversations and questions, both game-related and multi-topical.  The strategy forums cover each class, race, skill and zone, and are a great place to learn from other players or show off your knowledge.  Use the filter setting to find the best information.
    3.  Quests and Equipment.  Don't forget the search features for both quests and equipment.  These are nice time-saving areas.  Also check out the index for other ways you can search through the database.
    4.  EQ Atlas.  If you need maps and zone information, don't forget to visit our friend Muse over at EQ Atlas.
    5.  The Bestiary.  Illia has catalogued extensive information on all of the Mobs in the game.  Read about it before you go to kill it.
    6.  The News.  Keep up to date on what's happening in the EQ world, and while you're there check out things from other MMORPG's as well.
    7.  Links.  Check out the 2,862 other Everquest sites listed in the links section. This is regularly updated and revised.

    Of course, there is more, but that is sort of the highlights.
