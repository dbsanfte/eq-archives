    November, 2000 Updates

    November, 2000

    November 30, 2000:  I've been busy trying to get the equipment database up to snuff while Illia gets together the new server, so I only have a small, but I think interesting, update today.  I have been sent in a little more information on the new Velious Zones, including the names and small descriptions of the three I was missing.  Thus, I have updated my descriptions of many of the zones.  From what I hear, The Temple of Veeshan should make Veeshan's Peak seem like a walk in the park.  Be afraid.  Be very afraid.

    November 29, 2000:  Just 5 days now until Velious is released.  I was sent a report from the folks at gamespy that the game is gold and ready to ship, so the release should be on time.  I'm stocking up my coffee supply for next week, and will try to get things up as fast as I can, so be sure to send in what you find.  I updated the Velious Zone List with the little information I have been able to glean on them (don't expect much).  We will also be adding the new zones to the quest, strategy and bestiary lists this week, but won't add any actual information until the game is release and people start sending submissions, so don't be surprised to see new zones there.

    November 7, 2000:  I'm back from the Velious launch in Las Vegas.  It was nice meeting so many of the people who use and enjoy the site.  I brought 70 t-shirts with me, and they were gone in about 10 minutes.  There was not a lot of new information on Velious there, but we did get a chance to see the game.  They had the game up on a bunch of computers, and I have to say that the graphics look very impressive.  If you are over level 30, this seems like a must have expansion, if only for the additional variety of zones and the huge number of new items that will be available there.  Of the new Mobs they talked about, the ones that drew the most applause were the flying monkeys and what was labelled the most terrifying creature in all of Norrath -- the little white bunny rabbit.  There was no word on whether there would also be a holy hand grenade to go with the killer rabbit.

    I'm sorting through my e-mail now and will put up an update later today.  I wanted to put this up now to remind everyone in the U.S. to take the time to go out and vote -- well, unless you are voting for Bush, in which case this would be a good time to start a long camp somewhere :).   Seriously, every vote is important, and your vote does matter.  Please take the time to let your voice be heard.


    Congratulations to Wakefield Kiyugami of the test server for being the first person to finish the Monk Particle Weapon quest (though it is not really a weapon).  The result is the Celestial Fists.
