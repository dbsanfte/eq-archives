    September 18, 2000:  Unless it is postponed again, tomorrow is the big trade patch.  Hundreds of new items are getting added to the game through the blacksmithing skill.  Some highlights:  On top of intelligence/wisdom, strength will play a role in developing a skill.  Higher strength will mean fewer failures.  You can pick one of the general trades and specialize in it, going above 200 skill points.  Almost all of the trade skills will be needed to make the new items, so either start working on your skills or find some friends who are into the trades.  Leather padding, needed for the new plate armors, requires the tailoring skill.  Templars, which replace water in many recipes, must be brewed.  Dyes are made by alchemists and through the rogue poison skill.  And the vial used to make the dyes must be made through pottery.  Also, I am told that the races not included in this patch will get their own set of racial skills in an upcoming patch (all except for half elves).
