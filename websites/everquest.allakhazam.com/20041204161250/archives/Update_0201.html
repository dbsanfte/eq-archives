    February 15, 2001:  With the recent drop in revenue from banner ads throughout the web and to this site in particular, many people have written to me to suggest a way to volunteer a payment for their use of the site.  I have to admit I resisted the idea at first.  Call it pride, but I don't want any charity.  But then I realized it was really only asking people to pay what they thought was a fair amount for the enjoyment they get out of the site.  Plus, by being voluntary, it would not shut out those who either could not pay or for whatever reason did not want to pay.  In response to this, I've instituted a voluntary payment system through paypal.  I think it is pretty self explanatory, but I want to emphasize that it is strictly up to you whether you want to use it or not. Click on the donate image in the index, or go to this link to see the details.  Thanks.
