    In honor of the new Luclin expansion, we are doing our own mini expansion of services right here.  We've added a several new features, mostly to the premium services, but also to the site in general, and have a few more we hope to get out soon.  So that everyone can get a look at them, they will be available for free to non-premium users for the next week.  Check out what we have for you today:

    1.  A whole new character equipment upgrade search.  We've expanded the options for this feature and revised the way you get the results to make it even more flexible and useful than before.  You can now search for weapon and other hand held item upgrades on top of the existing armor upgrades.  Instead of two search perameters, you now get three.  You now have more flags to filter the results, such as excluding or only searching the expansions, and filtering in or out crafted and no drop items.  And the search results now give you a score to show how much better the item is than your existing one, the complete stats for the item, and its zone, level and price.  This is a great tool for finding the best items available for your character.  This is of course a premium feature, but for the next week anyone can try it out.  If you want to try it out, click on login in the menu (assuming you are registered -- if not register first), create a character, edit his or her equipment and hit tools to get to the search.

    2.  A new forum and bookmark system.  Several changes have been made to the forums (non-Ubb)

         a.  New look to the posts.  You now have the option to choose side view in the forum settings.  This separates the comments a little more cleanly and makes things a little easier to read.  The old look option is also available and still the default.  To see this setting, click on anything that has comments and in the forum preferences, change the far left pull down from threaded to side view and click change.  Check off save and change to save this as your preference.

         b.  Bookmarks.  With around 300,000 comments, it is getting harder to remember where you saw that particularly useful post you wanted to save.  Now, as a premium member, you can bookmark a post and save it in your user settings.  You can then easily find it again by checking your user admin page (login in the menu).  Now that great smithing guide you saw someone write up a couple of months ago is easy to find because you bookmarked it and saved it to your user page.  If you have an account with us, even if it isn't a premium account, you can try this feature out for free for the next week.

         c.  Best Comments.  This is part of the bookmark feature, but deserves it's own listing.  After a comment is bookmarked by 3 people (we will play with the number depending upon what ends up on the list), it will get listed on the best Comments list.  This is accessable by everyone, not just premium users.  Hopefully, over time, this will be the way to go to find the best user-generated information on the site in one easy place.

        d.  Other changes.  By popular user request, you can now choose if you wish to reply to a post or edit one of your posts by clicking on an easy to find icon right on the post.  You can also use basic color tags (blue, green, teal, yellow, etc.) to color and highlight the text in your posts.

    3.  More journal features.  You can now password protect your journal, so that only those who you give the password can access it.  Guilds may especially find this feature useful.  You can also make the journal private so that you are the only one who can access it.

    I told you we had some cool additions coming.  Even though they are premium features, the new character equipment upgrade search and the bookmarks will be available to everyone for the next week.  They will become premium only again next Monday.  So be sure to try them out this week.
