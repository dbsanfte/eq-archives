    July 18, 2002

    IMPORTANT:  I got several e-mails this morning entitled "Cool T-Shirt", with the from address listed as allakhazam@allakhazam.com, which asks you to fax an order for a U.S. flag T-Shirt.  This is not from here.  If you do a view source on an e-mail you can see where it actually comes from.  Do not send these people your credit card information!  It is very likely a fraud meant to get your credit card number.  I am contacting the FBI about this.  We will never send out any unsolicited e-mails from this address.  There are multiple viruses and other rip-offs that are faking e-mail "from" addresses out there, not just from us but from other sources you would normally trust.  Never download anything you get in an e-mail that is proportedly from here or provide credit card information or any other sensitive information (except of course through our secure server to sign up for the premium service).
