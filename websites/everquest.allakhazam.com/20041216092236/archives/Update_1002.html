    October 18, 2002

    In case you've missed it, Monday is the release date for Planes of Power.  As always, we welcome your submissions of the items, quests and recipes that you discover in the new zones.  Please feel free to submit everything you find and we will get it up as fast as we can.  The first couple of weeks after an expansion are always crazy here.  To make it go smoother, there are a couple of guidelines for submissions that we prefer you follow:

    Please crop any screenshots and convert them to either jpeg or gif format.  Then attach them to an e-mail addressed to allakhazam@allakhazam.com.  Include information on what zone the item dropped in and what mob dropped it, and, if you know, the level you need to be to get it and the rarity of the drop.  Don't zip the screens.  When I'm swamped, I tend to skip any screenshots that are zipped, bitmaps, need any extra graphics work, or are missing information and go right to the ones that are ready to post.  For a quest, try to write it out in a format that can I can just copy and paste to post.  If I see a quest that needs a ton of editing, I tend to put it on the back burner until I get more time to work with it.  Thanks.  We appreciate all of your submissions and will do our best to post information on the new zones as fast as we can get it up.
