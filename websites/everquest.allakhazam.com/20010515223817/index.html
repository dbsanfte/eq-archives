    May 15, 2001

    We now have our news section open.  It is still in beta and will undergo some changes over the next few weeks, including moving to the main allakhazam.com url with its own graphics and layout.  We are going to have a number of different people posting there, with the hope that you can get varying perspectives on things from the different posters.  As we spend more time with it, hopefully it will grow and evolve into something both unique and fun.  Since gaming news sites are a dime a dozen, we hope to make it something a little different.  Much of where it will go depends upon the feedback we get for it.  Right now, it is a mix of original articles and links to interesting articles on other sites.  Remember this is still basically a beta test and we are mainly posting it now because of E3, so the content will grow with time.  You are welcome to submit your own editorials/articles to be posted.  We reserve the right to edit anything you submit and to pick and choose what we will post.  The more gramatically correct and well written a submission is, the better chance it has of being published.  Thursday, Friday and Saturday we will be posting up our coverage of E3, and, while I will try to sneak in a content update, it may be that our only posts will be in that section the rest of the week.

    Enter Our Contest

    We're having a contest.  The winner will be drawn from all valid entries and will win a $50.00 amazon.com gift certificate.  In order to enter, you just have to spend a couple of minutes filling out a simple questionnaire.  The purpose of the questionnaire is to gather information about the people who use the site to provide to potential advertisers.  As has always been our policy, we will not give out or sell any individual, personal information.  Instead, the information will be combined into a set of charts which will be made available for everyone to see.  So taking a few minutes to fill out the form will a) help us out a lot, b) give you a chance to win a cool prize, c) not affect you personally in any way, and d) give everyone a chance to see some interesting information about the people you play the game with.  Please be truthful and fill out every question as best as you can.  Sorry, but we can only allow one entry per person and children under age 13 are not allowed to enter. Click here to enter the contest.

    Also, just an update on the site speed.  I know it has been slow lately.  Just when you think everyone who plays Everquest must know about this site, a whole new group seems to find it.  In January, an average of 52,000 people visited the site each day.  At the end of January, we decided to add a new server to handle the load.  This worked for a while, but by March we were getting about 66,000 visitors a day and things started to slow down.  Illia did a mass rebalancing of the servers and things sped up again.  However, this month, we are up to around 80,000 visitors a day (about 1/4 of the people who play the game!).  Basically we are maxed out.  We're pricing out another new server now to handle this increased load, especially since we are close to launching a couple of new sites.  But that will take some time to get in and get set up.  So please be patient.
