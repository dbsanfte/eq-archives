    August 15, 2005

    Welcome to the Monday update. Grobb, Innothule Swamp, and the Rathe Mountains have been the subject of bestiary additions & subtractions.

    -The Trolls are back in Grobb. It's much like it was pre-Froglok invasion.
    -The Frogloks have established a tent city in the southern portion of the Rathe Mountains.
    -Off to the west of the tent city are the Innoruuk-aligned Frogloks - Shadowknights, Clerics, Rogues, Necromancers.
    -Apparently the wildlife in Innothule Swamp didn't take too well to the Trolls' reclaiming of Grobb. Many creatures retreated to the Rathe Mountains along with the Frogloks..
    -The local hill giants and cyclopses of Rathe were dispersed by the influx of Frogloks. Don't look too far.
