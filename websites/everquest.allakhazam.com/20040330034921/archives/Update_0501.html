    May 25, 2001:  It's Memorial Day weekend.  Which means it is the end of the month.  Which means it is time to make my monthly plea for voluntary donations.  Believe me, I hate it as much as you do.  Nevertheless, your monthly donations are crucial to keeping this site afloat.  I seriously doubt we would have made it this far without them.  It costs almost $6,000 a month just for the bandwidth to run the site, and given today's ad market, the ads we are running don't bring in nearly that much.  Plus, it is a full time job keeping things accurate and up to date and bringing you complete daily coverage of the game.  During the past month, over 250,000 of you visited the site, with more than 80,000 visiting every day.  Simple math says that only a small fraction of you need to make a contribution in order to keep the site afloat.  But please don't leave it to the other guy, because the fact is that very few actually take the time to donate.  Think of how often you spend $5 or $10 on something frivolous and then ask you if your enjoyment of this site is worth that.  It only takes a minute, so please make a contribution.

    Also, just an update on the site speed.  I know it has been slow lately.  Just when you think everyone who plays Everquest must know about this site, a whole new group seems to find it.  In January, an average of 52,000 people visited the site each day.  At the end of January, we decided to add a new server to handle the load.  This worked for a while, but by March we were getting about 66,000 visitors a day and things started to slow down.  Illia did a mass rebalancing of the servers and things sped up again.  However, this month, we are up to around 80,000 visitors a day (about 1/4 of the people who play the game!).  Basically we are maxed out.  We're pricing out another new server now to handle this increased load, especially since we are close to launching a couple of new sites.  But that will take some time to get in and get set up.  So please be patient.
