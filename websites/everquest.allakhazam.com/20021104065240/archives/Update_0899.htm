    August 2, 1999:  Just a warning that many of the quest items are now no drop and no trade.  This means you can't give them to someone with a higher faction to complete the quest, and you will have to carry them around with you until you complete the quest.  I personally don't think it's a bad move to force you to complete the quest yourself.  This is why faction was added to the game.  I do think they should make no drop items bankable so that you can at least store them until you are able to use them.  Monks and magic users, being somewhat limited in their carrying weight will have a much tougher time completing quests now.  The irony is that Verant, which has professed a disdain for spoiler sites like this one, has made this site and others even more essential, since when you find a no drop quest item, you will either have to quickly figure out what to do with it or else destroy it.
