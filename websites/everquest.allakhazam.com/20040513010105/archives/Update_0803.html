    I haven't mentioned this in a while, so I figured I would mention it again.  We never have and never will send out any spam e-mail, or do any mass mailing of any sort.  Nor do we or will we sell our e-mail list to anyone.  If you received an e-mail from someone with an official looking @allakhazam.com e-mail address that was not a reply to something you sent us, it simply did not come from here.  Moreover, it most likely is a virus like Klez, which grabs a return address from the infected computer, so whatever you do don't open the attachment on it.  Also, there have been several occasions when some con artist used our return address to try to defraud people, because it is an address people tend to trust.  Whenever it's been brought to our attention, we have contacted the FBI about it -- not that it's ever resulted in any arrest, or as far as I can tell any real investigation, but we have and will contact the authorities.  So I am telling you now, don't trust an e-mail from us that was clearly mass mailed.  That's because it isn't from us, and thus isn't trustworthy.  Just ignore it and delete it.
