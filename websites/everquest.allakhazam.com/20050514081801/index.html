    A fair chunk of Friday updates are lined up for you tonight, but before we get to those:

    I thought I'd take this opportunity to tell you how much we appreciate those of you who are contributors to this website.

    A thank you to our premium members, who are the backbone keeping this site online.
    A thank you to those of you who post comments under our item, bestiary, recipe and quest pages..
    ..who contribute to the forums..
    ..who discover bugs and get on our developers' cases about them..
    ..who take the time to crop screenshots and submit information (and sometimes even host them on private webpages - a shoutout to Saraban of Darkwind who has submitted a ton of information on the high-end game)..

    For those of you who come here only to scrape off the tip of the iceberg: We here at the Allakhazam website are not just mindless drones pumping out an item database for you. No no, there is much more beneath the cover you see here.

    Some points of interest would be the EverQuest Forum, which is among a host of gaming message boards here. We have a Character Profile system, a spell database, and some EverQuest history, to name a few things. Our registration page can be found here.

    We also have a Premium System set up for a minuscule fee to keep us functioning. Among its features are advanced item, bestiary, quest, and recipe searches, as well as expanded item display, a character search, and an Auto Profile Updater. There are no ads for premium users, and the service covers the entire Allakhazam website (should you play more than one MMORPG).

    The bottom line? If you are a user who comes here strolling through pages anonymously and prefer to keep it that way, by all means keep at it. There is nothing wrong with a little bit of anonymity. This message was for those of you who may not have been aware of all what we have to offer here. If we've shed some light for you, great. If we've bored you, we turn our attention to today's updates.

    Have a very good weekend.
