    February 29, 2000:  Happy leap day.  Don't get to say that too often (every four years to be exact).  I look at it as one extra day before having to pay your taxes.  Interesting things are happening around Norrath.  GM's are appearing singing songs.  Mayong Mistmoore made an appearance and tortured a poor unsuspecting Woodelf.  Threats are hanging out there that the dragons are returning.  It seems like Verant is planning on ushering the expansion in with some sort of big event, especially when you consider that all this is happening with the expansion still almost two months away.  Sounds like fun.  I keep e-mailing Verant asking them to let me into the beta test of Kunark so that I can give everyone some preliminary information about what it looks like and what they can expect.  (hey I can be discreet if I need to be).  Maybe one day I'll get lucky and they'll actually e-mail me back.  If not, I'll have to rely upon those who are beta testing Kunark to send in some information as we get closer to release (hint, hint).

    February 23, 2000:  For those of you who see me in the game and wonder why I'm still a level 22 wizard, it's simple.   I rarely play the character AllaKhazam anymore.  There's a huge thread on the official everquest message board on why wizards are generally worthless compared to other classes.  While I would not quite say the class is worthless, I am definitely having a lot more fun playing my other characters (a bard, necromancer and druid  -- all in the teens now -- can't decide which I like more.  I also have a low level rogue) than I ever had playing the wizard.  I just find the other classes I play are far more useful in a group, are more interesting to play, and are easier to solo, which I have to do a lot since I often only have an hour or two left to play the game.

    February 22, 2000:  Whew.  You start to think things are getting slow and then they go and make all these major changes.  Many of the Paineel quests have been solved now, but there are still a few floating out there.  There are also a number of new NPC's which were added in the last patch.  Although I am not quite sure which ones are for which quests yet, they are most likely for either the ranger level 50 spell quest, the paladin level 50 spell quest, the fiery avenger quest, the Tome of ages quest, which seems to be for a shadowknight weapon, or one of the Paineel quests.  I'm just going to have to wait for someone to solve each quest and send in the information before I can update them.  I know there are a number of guilds working hard at doing just that, so I figure it's only a matter of time.  If you solve one, please send in the details and I will try to make sure you get the credit.  You may also have noticed that the Iksar are being added as a race to many items.  From the items I have seen we can deduce that they will at least be able to play a monk, shaman and shadow knight.
