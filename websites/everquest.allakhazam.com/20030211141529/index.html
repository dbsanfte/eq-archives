    February 7, 2003

    In preparation for Ykesha, there was a GM event yesterday and the Frogloks invaded and took over Grobb.  Trolls will no longer start in Grobb, but instead will start in the Neriak Foreign Quarter.  The frogloks renamed the city Gukta, and it will be the starting city for the Frogloks.  I'm not sure whether all of the quests that were formerly in Grobb moved to Neriak, or whether they were just removed from the game.  Likely it's a little of both.  Obviously, there will be a new set of quests introduced with the new city.  For now, I'm leaving the zone and quest descriptions alone until we are sure what to do with them.  Also, the character creation screen now has Frogloks.  You can play with the look of them, but not actually create them at the moment.

    Just a reminder to our premium members that your 10% discount coupon applies to the Ykesha cd at EBgames.com.  Also, Everquest Online Adventures, the PS2 version of Everquest, is scheduled to ship on Tuesday.  Your coupon is also good for that purchase.  You can see your coupon on your account page.  Use the links above to buy the games.

    February 5, 2003

    If you've been having problems lately with the speed of the site, you aren't the only one.  Unfortunately, it has nothing to do with our servers or our isp.  We've upgraded our servers and connection quite a bit over the past year and are now running well below capacity.  The problems some of you are experiencing are due to connection issues between some of the upstream providers which are slowing down the packets between your computer and our servers.  Specifically, in my case at least, the tracert numbers jump when I hit psi.net and cogentco.com.  I can only hope they are working on it.  I know we are way too small a site to be able to complain to them and get anything done about it.

    Also, I again want to remind you that you must be starting at everquest.allakhazam.com in order to access the database.  Just using allakhazam.com will not work.
