    April 4, 2001

    RPGWear has two new shirts out, both of which are really cool designs:  The Fan Faire shirt for this weekend's fanfaire, which is a great-looking general Everquest shirt, and the first of their new line of Everquest Class shirts.  This one features the Druid.  For visitors of this site, they are offering a discount if you buy both shirts.  Check it out in the Phat Lewt section.  These are definitely worth a look.  Also, keep an eye out for a new, really cool AllaKhazam T-Shirt, coming any day now.  I just know you are going to want to have one of these.  Finally, many people have written to me saying they would like to make a voluntary payment, but either could not or did not want to use paypal.  Thus, for those people who prefer snail mail, I now have a post office box.  It is listed in the voluntary payment section.

    Also, when Verant eliminated their forums, they also unfortunately eliminated the Newbie Zone, which was a very useful area for new players to go to ask questions and find out about the game from a regular group of informed and helpful players.  Fortunately, I have been told that the regulars at the Newbie Zone took it upon themsleves to establish their own forum. Click here to check it out.

    Finally, we are ready to open a new, much anticipated, feature for the site:  [tada]  Classifieds (platinum piece based of course).  Illia has been working hard on this feature for a while and parts of it have been up for a few weeks, but now it is hopefully ready for prime time.  If you were playing around with it and added items for sale that you don't have, please go in and delete them or you will start to get e-mails about them you don't want.  (In fact, I would suggest everyone wait another day until you e-mail the sellers on the items).  Here's how it works.

    Sellers:  You must list your character as public to sell items.  In the character screen, you can add items for sale by typing in the name if the item and hitting "attach more items".  Pick the item you want to sell from the list and change it to "Put up for sale" and add a price to the slot next to it.  Leave any other items at "discard".  Then click OK and that item will be added to your for sale list.  Keep doing this for each item you want to sell.  Be sure to delete an item after you make the sale or you will keep getting e-mails on it.  Also, make sure that the e-mail address you have listed is the one you want people responding to.  When you get an e-mail from a potential buyer, you simply have to arrange the time and place in the game where you want to make the sale.

    Buyers:  If you click on For Sale in the index under equipment, you will get to a list by server of all the items people have listed for sale under their characters.  This list gets updated every hour.  If you see an item you like, click on the person's name and it will bring up the character profile with every item he or she is offering.  Above the item list is that person's e-mail.  You can arrange the time and place and final price of the sale through e-mails.  You can also find out who is wearing and selling an item from that item's individual page.  This is particularly useful if you have done a character upgrade search to find the items that are the best upgrades for the equipment you are wearing.  Right now you need to click on the pricing data link.  We will add a more intuitive one soon.

    This is a great way to get rid of items or find items you want without going through the time and effort of /auctioning them.  It is also a great way for craftsmen to advertise their wares and for players to find craftsmen on their servers.  If you are a jeweler, tailor, smith, etc., I would suggest you add all the items and prices for your craft under your character so that you can arrange to make your sales at your convienence and that of your customers.  There is also an added feature that you can see everyone who is wearing each item by clicking on the pricing data in the item screen.  So, for example, if you wanted to know everyone who has gotten their fiery defender (at least those who have made their characters public), you would click here.

    You are bound to discover a few bugs as this gets used, so feel free to send in bug reports or suggestions on how to improve the feature.  I am guessing that most of the people who posted items in the auction were looking for something more like the current classifieds.  Thus, we are going to take down the auction section.  If you posted something up there, don't worry.  We will leave it up for another week or so, but please don't post any more auctions.  Use the classifieds instead.
