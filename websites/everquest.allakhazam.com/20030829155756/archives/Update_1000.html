    October 30, 2000 - Just a reminder that the game servers are scheduled to be down most of the day tomorrow for a huge patch.  So if you were thinking that tomorrow would be a good day to call in sick and play some Everquest (admit it, you've done it before), you'd better get ready to go to work instead.  Also, after tomorrow, you will need to have the newest version of directx to play the game.  Save yourself a headache and go download it today (www.microsoft.com/directx/).  The database on this site is always slow when the game servers go down.  Just be patient, or go into the other sections.  I am ordering a new server that should solve that and also let us put up the new equipment database, but it won't be in for a couple of weeks.

    I have a number of new updates to keep you busy with while the servers go down.  Of course, while the servers are down, feel free to visit the always busy and entertaining Everquest Forums and add your opinion to the many discussions going on there.
