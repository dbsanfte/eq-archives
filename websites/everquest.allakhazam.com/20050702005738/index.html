    June 28, 2005

    Have you ever tried to explain EverQuest to your family, friends, and coworkers? I thought I would point out an interesting thread on our EverQuest forum: Sonka posted a question not easily answered by most - How do you explain EverQuest to non-players? A simple question with some complicated reactions.

    On to the updates. We have some new zone additions to our database (actually, they have been there for a few days now). We have also updated some current zone entries to be in line with what you see in game. For example: "Freeport East" was renamed "East Freeport," "Freeport West" was renamed "West Freeport," etc. These changes were made to Kaladim, Cabilis, and Qeynos (rest assured, these cities have not vanished).

    We are looking for the in-game names of the following instanced zones so that we can add them to the database: Befallen, Hate's Fury, and any missing Epic 1.5/2.0 instances (we know they are out there). Send an email to railus@allakhazam.com if you have that information.
