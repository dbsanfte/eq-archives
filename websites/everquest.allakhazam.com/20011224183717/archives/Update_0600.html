    June 30, 2000:  After much time and effort (and a lot of cursing), the first part of the site database is now up and running.  Over 700 quests have been entered into our new Quest Database, searchable by zone, class, faction and quest item, and including such information as time to complete, minimum level, best class, factions involved, and more.  It also features the ability to rate the quest and add comments.  In the process, some of the information has been updated as well.  The database also integrates with Illia's Beastiary, so that you can just click through and get the information you need on the Mobs involved.  In addition, since the database and the bestiary now cover the faction information, that section has been updated to point you to these resources.

    Currently, the equipment pages still point to the old quest pages.  I'll update those over the weekend.  If you have the old quest section bookmarked, you may want to change your bookmark, as that section will eventually be coming down.  I personally think this is a great resource.  I hope you like it.

    Some credits are due.  I'd like to thank Illia for doing a terrific programming job.  I also want to thank the following people for helping with the data entry (sure hope I don't miss anyone): Sweetj, Hammerlikk Woundweaver, Karin Starbright, Forbin Dragonfyre, Lemorte Ressiat, Conadin, Colbey Callinstin, Chrysania Kiga'rasku, Shagwood, Buliwyk and Sovinnai.  If you see them, give them a buff or a sow.

    In addition, the following sections have new or updated information: Face Slot ; Feet Slot ; Hands Slot ; Head Slot ; Shields ; General Links ; Guild Links

    June 21, 2000:  As you all know, I don't normally do game news here, preferring to concentrate on information, and leaving the news in the competent hands of such sites as Caster's Realm, Everlore and EQ Stratics.  But I just wanted to post my sympathies to those of you who play on the Test Server and had your characters wiped yesterday.  I know I'd probably be banging my head against a wall if they wiped out all of my characters.  While they seem to be offering a couple of options to at least let you keep the level of your characters, it's still a pretty raw deal, and a poor payback to those who have helped Verant by testing out their updates before they went live.  It also makes no sense.  If their problem was that the test server people were not in synch with the live servers because they were too well equipped, how will it improve that by leaving them with no equipment?  Here's hoping that, at the least, they restore the players' levels and re-equip them with items appropriate to their class and level.

    For those saying "it's just the test server", I encourage you to read Illia's editorial about the player wipe here.

    OK on to the Updates.  Several people from the Test Server sent in items they had found prior to their characters getting wiped (as they were cleaning out their directory prior to uninstalling the game).  Many of these are from Veeshan's Peak, and a few are rewards to Plane of Sky quests.  I added them to the equipment lists with a caveat next to them that they are from the test server and thus subject to change.  It's still fun to get a glinpse at some of the Veeshan's items.  I also added some other itmems.  Thus, I have about 20 new items for you today.  Also, for some reason my Webbbs board just stopped working.  I'm trying to figure out what's wrong with it and will get it back up as soon as possible.  The Ubb forums are still OK.  Here are the updated sections: 1 Handed Blunt Weapons 2 Handed Blunt Weapons ;  2 Handed Slashing Weapons ; Bows and Arrows ; Chest Slot ; Face Slot ; Feet Slot ; Legs Slot ; Packs and Bags ; Piercing Weapons ; Shoulder Slot ; Waist Slot ; Plane of Sky Quests ; Guild Links
