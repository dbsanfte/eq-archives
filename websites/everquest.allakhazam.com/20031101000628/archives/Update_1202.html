    December 14, 2002

    If any of you are beta testing Everquest Online Adventures for the PS2, we are looking for your help.  We're putting together a site for that and are looking for information submissions, especially for quests and items.  Basically, take a look at what we have listed for quests and items here and figure we are looking for similar information for EQOA.  We hope to put up a site for that much like this one.  Make sure you make clear the info is for EQOA, so I don't get it confused with the PC version.  We are also looking for a couple volunteers who have some free time to spare and want to help us build up the site and enter all of the information on it.  It's a major commitment to do that, so you have to really enjoy it.  If you think that is for you, e-mail me and tell me a little about yourself.  Thanks.

    --Allakhazam

    Also, Illia would like to add:

    We've done a complete rebuild of how we handle spells.  Thanks to Lucy (http://lucy.fnord.net/),  our spell database now automatically updates daily.  This includes a new spell search,  new spell display pages, and for premium users - more spell information on the item display.  Focus effect searches now work properly on the advanced search, and on the spell search, you can search by spell description (eg:  search for "decrease spell mana cost" to get all Mana preservation effects regardless of effect name.

    Due to extensive re-building of the table structure on the backend, some features may not be working as expected.  Please send me an email if you find features which seem to have broken because of this.

    -- Illia
