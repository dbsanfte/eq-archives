October 19, 2005

Today's new quest additions are currently (for the most part) templates. We just wanted them in the database even if we did not have information ready to dole out on them. If you have sent in a submission on a spell progression mission (or anything else for that matter), it will be added soon. We are still sifting through a ton of Darkhollow information funnelling in.

There are a few key pieces of information needed for missions:
-The name of the mission
-The name of the mission-giver
-The zone(s) in which the mission takes place
-The level required (approximate) to participate

And, if you can swing it while still focusing on actually completing your mission, this additional information is a big help in keeping track of things:
-In-game task text, update messages, and steps
-Names and levels of NPCs encountered
-Quest pieces (any minor items required to progress through the mission?)
-Stat Loot (normal or hard? chest, reward, or mob drop?)
-Mission strategies you wish to divulge (how hard do mobs hit? special abilities/spells?, etc.)

Thank you as always for the submissions; keep them coming (can be sent to Railus and/or Fleven).
