    How to Buy and Sell Items using the AllaKhazam Classifieds



    The classified ads are a great way to get rid of items or finding items you want without going through the time and effort of /auctioning them.  They are also a great way for craftsmen to advertise their wares and for players to find craftsmen on their servers.  If you are a jeweler, tailor, smith, etc., I would suggest you add all the items and prices for your craft under your character so that you can arrange to make your sales at your convienence and that of your customers.  There is also an added feature that you can see everyone who is wearing each item by clicking on the pricing data in the item screen.

    Sellers:  You must list your character as public to sell items.  In the character screen, you can add items for sale by typing in the name if the item and hitting "attach more items".  Pick the item you want to sell from the list and change it to "Put up for sale" and add a price to the slot next to it.  Leave any other items at "discard".  Then click OK and that item will be added to your for sale list.  Keep doing this for each item you want to sell.  Be sure to delete an item after you make the sale or you will keep getting e-mails on it.  Also, make sure that the e-mail address you have listed is the one you want people responding to.  When you get an e-mail from a potential buyer, you simply have to arrange the time and place in the game where you want to make the sale.

    Buyers:  If you click on For Sale in the index under equipment, you will get to a list by server of all the items people have listed for sale under their characters.  This list gets updated every hour.  If you see an item you like, click on the person's name and it will bring up the character profile with every item he or she is offering.  Above the item list is that person's e-mail.  You can arrange the time and place and final price of the sale through e-mails.  You can also find out who is wearing and selling an item from that item's individual page.
