    I know I've mentioned this before, but please read this.  If you get a popup ad that seems inappropriate for this site, i.e. it trips your virus program, is pornographic, changes your homepage, or I don't even know what else, it is not actually coming from here.  It is coming from your own computer.  There is a type of program officially called "adware" but which is more often called "scumware" that gets installed without you even knowing it when you install many legitimate programs (not just Kazaa, though that is a big one that does it).  This sits in your computer, learns your browsing patterns and pops up ads on you as you surf the web.  We have no control over the ads this feeds you.  Go here to read a story on these programs.  I personally think these programs should be outlawed and the people who made them taken out and shot.  You can protect yourself from these programs by downloading a free program called ad-aware that looks for them and deletes them.
