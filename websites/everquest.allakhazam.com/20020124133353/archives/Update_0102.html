    January 10, 2002:  Since a number of new people seem to be discovering the site since Christmas, I thought I would put up another explanation of the premium service.  With almost 8,000 items, over 1,500 quests and more than 7,300 Mobs, plus news, forums and tons of other game information, I believe we have the most complete Everquest information database available anywhere.  We are very proud that so many feel this is the number one place to go to find out about everything Everquest. (You may be surprised at the number of accounts we have with an @sony.com e-mail address).  And we have continued to make sure that everyone can access all of the information on the site for free.  Want to view equipment?  Click on equipment by slot.  Quests?  Click on quests by zone.  Those are available free to anyone who wants to use them.  However, while the information is accessable for free, certain services and search features are not.

    Therefore, if you really want the information to come alive for you, you will need to pay a very small fee and get a premium membership.  With a premium membership, you can search the equipment database in just about any way imaginable, including entering your character's equiment and doing a global search for all equipment available at your level and price range that would be an upgrade to your current set.  You can bring up detailed lists of all of the items found in a particular zone.  You can search through quests by class and level or just by search term to find the perfect quests for your character.  You can list yourself as a merchant to let people know which trades you can do for them and negotiate sales by e-mail instead of trolling around in the game, and post up any number of items in the classifieds.  You can create and moderate your own forum to post game information, rants or stories.  You can bookmark the best comments you come across so that you can quickly find them later for easy reference.  You can search the character database to find specific players on your server.  You can conduct game business using your very own @allakhazam.com e-mail address.  You can add a title to your posts to personalize your posting name. And you do all this free of those annoying banner and popup ads, which instantly disappear the minute you sign up for premium membership.  All of this for a price as low as $2.50 per month ($9.99 for 3 months, $17.99 for 6 months or $29.99 for one year).  Many people pay more than that for their morning cup of coffee.

    Why are we charging for these services?  Believe me, we would love to give them away for free.  However, our site runs on 4 (soon to be 5) dedicated servers and every day we get over 125,000 visitors who read almost 2,000,000 pages of information.  This uses a ton of bandwidth and costs a lot of money, and anyone who has followed the state of the internet the past year knows that advertising basically does not pay any significant revenue anymore.  We felt the premium membership was a good compromise that let us keep access to everything free for those who could not afford to pay, yet gave those who could an excellent package of features well worth paying for.  It is what keeps the site running and lets us continue to add to the database like we have been doing to bring you accurate and up to date game information.  You are welcome to continue to access the information using the free service.  That's why it is there.  Try it out, and if you find that you truly want to be able to make the equipment and quest database work wonders for you, consider signing up for the premium service (you can always cancel within the first five days for a full refund).  A complete explanation of the benefits can be found here.  Or you can click here to instantly sign up and start to enjoy the service.
