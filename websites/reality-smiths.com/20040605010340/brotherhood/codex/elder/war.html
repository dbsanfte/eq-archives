Compendium Publicus

Rallos Zek, the Warlord, rules the Plane of War
with an iron fist. His skin is jet black with
visible pulsating lava-like veins. His world is
a scene of constant strife and battle, corpses
littering the ground, and the air dark and
filled with smoke.
