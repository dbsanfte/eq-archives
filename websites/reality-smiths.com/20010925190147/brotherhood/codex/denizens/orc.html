Compendium Publicus

Bred by the gods for battle, Orcs are single-minded
in their desire for conquest. They abuse the weak and
quickly cower beneath the strong. Ideas and objects
unrelated to war have no value to an Orc. They are
the mortal enemies of Dwarves and Elves, and seek
to slay them whenever possible.
