Medals System Announced -
Posted Tuesday, March 13, 2001 - 4:22 PM EST by Kaelon Xerxentis
Today, Arch Chancellor Kaelon Xerxentis announced the implementation of a new Medals System, a program that recognizes initiative, leadership, and merit in the Council of Arcana through the creation of chivalric orders and the designation of publicly visible accolaides. Recipients of Medals will be listed in the Hall of Heroes. Please feel free to read the full announcement.
