The Quaestorial Commission of 3182 was called by Arch Chancellor Kaelon Xerxentis from the Council's Headquarters in High City of Erudin in order to ascertain common misperceptions and questions regarding the nature, policy and procedure revolving around the Council of Arcana. Herein are questions asked and answered in the hand of the Arch Chancellor himself. Further questions for consideration to be added to the Quaestorial Commission may be e-mailed.

Q: What is the Council of Arcana?
A: The Council of Arcana is a guild in Norrath, the world of EverQuest, a massively-multiplayer, online role playing game developed by Verant Interactive, distributed by 989 Studios, and funded by Sony Corporation. It is located on the "Quellious" server.

Q: How many members does the Council of Arcana have?
A: The Council of Arcana's updated membership is available from its online Council Ledger. The Council of Arcana has members throughout all classes and skill levels, but is ultimately a single, exclusive and highly honorable association of friends and family.

Q: Who governs the Council of Arcana?
A: The Council of Arcana is led by the Arch Chancellor, currently Kaelon Xerxentis, a Wizard of Solusek Ro, from the High City of Erudin. Underneath the Arch Chancellor, who renders all final decisions regarding procedure and policy, is the High Council, an assembly of elders that oversee discipline, membership and activities of the guild.

Q: What is a Junior Councilor, Councilor and Senior Councilor?
A: Members in good standing of the Council of Arcana are grouped into three categories. Junior Councilors are members that have just joined the Council and are growing accustomed to the environment and the family about them. Councilors are veteran members of the Council, and recognized for their continued commitment. Senior Councilors have officer-like powers and have some minor, guild-keeping responsibilities, but do not serve on the High Council, nor do they make policy.

Q: How does one join the Council of Arcana?
A: The Council of Arcana's membership system is exclusive, and adding new members follows a member-initiated invitation system. You cannot 'apply' to join the Council, you must be invited to join. When a Councilor, Senior Councilor, High Councilor or the Arch Chancellor sponsors a member, it is broadcast over guild chat so that any objections may be collected at that time. No objections withstanding, the candidate becomes a Junior Councilor and is inducted into our guild.

Q: Are there requirements for membership?
A: Yes. To remain a member in good standing, you must observe the Code of Honour and recognize the chain of command as specified in the Prime Charter. Ultimately, members must strive to perfect, embody and exemplify the Principles of the Council. While the Council is a family of friends and comrades in adventure, it does not tolerate blatent behavior that misrepresents and has poor implications on the rest of the Council.
