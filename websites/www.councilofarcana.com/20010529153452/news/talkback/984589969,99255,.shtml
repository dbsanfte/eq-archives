Patch Day! -
Posted Wednesday, March 14, 2001 - 12:12 PM EST by Kaelon Xerxentis
Today is Patch Day. The servers went down at 3 am PST for 9 hours, which means that they will be back at 11 am PST / 2 pm EST. This patch includes numerous changes - including Epic Quest changes, Fear/Hate upgrades, a new zone, and much more - check out the patch details (and keep an eye out for Trux's latest upgrade!).

During patch: Members should check into the Council Ledger and update their character profiles and details. New members (or members that have yet to add their profiles) should add their characters to the ledger, following on the on-screen instructions. Creation passwords can be found on the message boards, or, alternatively, you can use the standard creation password found on the add a character page. ALSO... be sure to check out the Medals Program, just announced yesterday. (Also, read the medals announcement for feedback and more info.)

After patch: We're considering having an event after the patch. Please check it out. Let's have fun this patch day! =)
