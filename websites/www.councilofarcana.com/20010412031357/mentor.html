The Council of Arcana's Mentorship Program is an ongoing, multiple-front effort to educate new, trial-period members (titled 'Junior Councilors') about the principles, codes and ideals inherent in our guild's community. At the end of the Mentorship Program's period (which lasts usually between 10 and 20 hours of actual grouping with one's mentor), the High Council then determines whether or not to (a) promote the Junior Councilor to full-fledged Councilor, (b) eject and dismiss the trial member from the guild, or (c) re-engage a new mentorship period. The following documents are crucial for the Mentorship Program's understanding and completion.

Once a Junior Councilor has completed his or her mentorship period and grouped with his or her sponsor for 10-20 hours, both mentor and junior councilor should fill out the two corresponding reports below.

Mentor Report
Report for Mentors to fill out at the end of the Mentorship Period.

Junior Councilor Report
Report for Junior Councilors to fill out at the end of the Mentorship Period.

Membership Reform Act of 3184
The MRA serves as the central text and legislative document governing membership admissions, the mentorship program and uniform membership policies for the Council of Arcana. It is available as a reference.
