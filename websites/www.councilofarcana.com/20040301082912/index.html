Guild Web Site Relaunched
Posted by Kaelon on December 05, 2003 at 07:55 AM ET
(0) Comments
Greetings, and Welcome to the Council of Arcana Web Site! If you are reading this, then, congratulations! You are witnessing first-hand the rebirth of one of the oldest guilds on the Quellious Server and the resurgence of its web site. I just recently finished setting up this web site and deciding on a central design scheme, both for the site and the forums. Speaking of which, please feel free to head on over to our message boards or check out our gallery of screenshots. Be sure to register an account so you can gain access to lots of special features.

As a caveat, the Council of Arcana web site is still undergoing final preparations and construction, so there is some content that is still being implemented (such as another three years' worth of screenshots left to be uploaded by yours truly). Please remain patient and in the meantime, have fun!
