Each Medal and Award in the Council of Arcana is awarded on the recognition of merit and accomplishment. When one receives a Medallion, he or she enters into an elite field of Councilor - wherein one gains instantaneous membership in one of the elite orders of adventurers. The Medals below reflect a wide array of possible areas wherein Councilors may be recognized for accomplishment. The medallions are divided into various categories: Highest Honors, Civic Honors and Military Honors.

THE HIGHEST HONORS


Council Medal of Honor

 The Council Medal of Honour
The highest honor that any member of the Council of Arcana can attain, the Council Medal of Honor recognizes the greatest accomplishment in setting often unbeatable standards of excellence, glory and honor. Recipients of the Council Medal of Honor are addressed as "Your Most Honorable Excellency", and have the right to address and advise the Arch Chancellor on any and all affairs for the welfare of the guild. The words encircling the medallion are "For God and the Empire", with the image of the current reigning Arch Chancellor.
The Star of Kaelon
Named after the first Arch Chancellor and founder of the Council of Arcana, the Star of Kaelon is awarded to Councilors that have repeatedly defended the Council of Arcana with their lives, or have taken the extraordinary step of saving the life of the Arch Chancellor or Vice Chancellor. The Star of Kaelon is also awarded for uncovering treason and disloyalty within the Council ranks. It is worn over the left breast, higher than all other civil and military honors - except for the Council Medal of Honor. Encircling the initial "K" are the words, "Honour and Integrity."


Star of Kaelon


Star of Allanna

 The Star of Allanna
Named after the first Arch Chancelloress and one of the chief reformers of the Council of Arcana, the Star of Allanna is awarded to Council members that have committed their lives to making the Council of Arcana a better, safer place, improving the meaning of community and exemplifying its many virtues. The Star of Allanna is also awarded for accomplishments in culture, art, literature, and expression within the Council and, as such, the most creative and energetic members of the guild have often received this honor, often considered their most glorious accomplishment.
The Cross of Conquest
For the bravest and boldest adventurers, the Cross of Conquest is considered the most covetted recognition of their labors of combat, and these recipients gain the honor of advising the High Council on military matters. The Cross of Combat is awarded to only those who have organized and commanded a completely successful raid against the following: a god, a dragon, the old world (Antonica, Odus, or Faydwer), the continent of Velious, and the continent of Kunark. Because of the difficulty in accomplishing all of these, adventurers often spend a lifetime striving for the Cross of Conquest.


The Cross of Conquest

THE CIVIC HONORS


The Golden Heart

 The Golden Heart
One of the mods visible civic honors, the Golden Heart is awarded to members of the Council of Arcana who have displayed extraordinary selflessness above and beyond the call to the virtues of the Prime Charter. Recipients of the Golden Heart have exemplified the Virtue of Selflessness and Charity and are standards by which others are eventually measured.
The Coin of Malrissa
Named after one of the ancient founders of the Council of Arcana also creditted with being one of Norrath's first exchange investors and merchants, the Coin of Malrissa is awarded for leadership in economic and financial circles - such as superb knowledge of commerce in Norrath - and it is also awarded for notable donations to the Council of Arcana treasury.


The Coin of Malrissa


Sigil of the Sacred Six

 The Sigil of the Sacred Six
For members of the Council of Arcana that have regularly fought for the values of the Sacred Six Virtues - Law, Order, Justice, Truth, Honor and Comradery - the Sigil of the Sacred Six is dutifully awarded. The Sigil is also awarded for extraordinary acts of honor and justice which demonstrate both the piety of the individual and the excellent representation of the guild by the recipient.
The Medallion of Glory
For excellence in leadership and civil administration within the guild's governing bodies - whether through direct service or volunteering, the Medallion of Glory is awarded in comemoration of this excellence service for the governance of the Council of Arcana. Advancements in the institutions of the Council, the procedures and policies are often rewarded with this medallion.


The Medallion of Glory


Shield of Charisma

 The Shield of Charisma
Advances in speech, oratory, philosophy and rhetoric usually merit decoration with the Shield of Charisma. Council of Arcana members that set the proper example over guildchat - in courtesy, honor and respect - are also eligible to receive the Shield of Charisma. Finally, the Shield of Charisma is also awarded to ambassadors and negotiators who represent the Council of Arcana with particular skill.

THE MILITARY HONORS
The Cross of Courage
For those adventurers that have, in the midst of combat, engaged in a selfless act that placed him or herself in extreme jeopardy or death and danger to allow and assist another guildmember to find escape or freedom, the Cross of Courage is awarded for such bold and courageous an act.


Cross of Courage


Star of Valour

 The Star of Valour
When commanders of a raid are successful in leading an assault that results in the death of one of the Gods, the Council of Arcana recognizes them with the Star of Valour. Because a charge on a God requires both tactical knowledge and superior bravery, those commanders directly responsible for overseeing the forces of the victory - the pull leader and the raid commander - are eligible to receive this prestigious medallion.
The Crescent of Kunark
Commanders of raids and expeditions into the heart of the dark continent of Kunark are eligible for the Crescent of Kunark when their raiding expedition is completely successful in acquiring the defeat and conquest of the entire dungeon in Kunark. All raids upon all Kunark dungeons are eligible for this medallion.


Crescent of Kunark


Crescent of Norrath

 The Crescent of Norrath
When commanders of a raid are successful in leading a complete conquest and obliteration of the highest level denizen of a dark dungeon of the Old World, he or she will receive the Crescent of Norrath. Dungeons located in Odus, Antonica and Faydwer are all eligible for this explorative adventure, and they must be of dungeons suitable for the level of the commander and his or her participants.
The Star of Ferocity
The Star of Ferocity is one of the most sought after medallions for military commanders, for it demonstrates one's ability to exert the strength of the guild. In order for a commander to receive the Star of Ferocity, he or she must prepare a raid or adventure on a moment's notice, without any prior preparation or planning. If successful in attracting the participation of numerous guildmembers, then said commander shall be eligible for the Star of Ferocity.


Star of Ferocity


Star of Supremacy

 The Star of Supremacy
Decisive in battle and successful in combat, commanders that display innovative tactics and uncanny propensity towards advanced strategies of military pursuit receive the Star of Supremacy. The Star of Supremacy is a medallion recognizing excellence in leadership on the battlefield.
The Crescent of Velious
When commanders of a raid are successful in leading an incursion against the arctic hinterlands of Velious, whether it be the frosty peaks of an outdoor wilderness or the stone-clad fortresses of opposing races, they do receive the Crescent of Velious.


Crescent of Velious
