The Council of Arcana is a guild which cherishes itself upon upholding the foundational virtues of Law, Order, Justice, Honor, Truth and Comradery. Above all, the culture of Honor stands out as an element by which members conduct themselves both in public and within the guild, and forms the overall basis for judging a person's actions and the content of their character. As such, the following Code of Honour operates as the rules of conduct for members. Persons found to be "in breach of Honour" can be expelled from the guild by the High Council.

GENEROSITY AND CHARITY
All Council Members are to offer aid, advice, charity and generosity to the deserving, the proving, and the needy. Sharing of loot and booty is to be on an equitable and fair basis, without misleading or misguiding any team or party member, enabling the equal exchange of goods for their value. Trade and commerce are to be conducted with honesty, truth and no intention of deception or manipulation of the markets.

COMBAT AGAINST A CITIZEN
Should a duel of mutual interest be engaged, Council members agree to fight the duel in honor and without manipulation. There shall be no other type of combat against another citizen, including player-killing, generating a train of monsters for intentional or malicious purpose, or engaging in acts which may unfairly cost someone else his or her life.

KILL-STEALING
The Council of Arcana abhors the enormous, low and hateful dishonor of Kill-Stealing, and members are to actively lobby against such actions, never even considering to take these actions, whether out of own volition or in retalliation against another action taken against him or her. The resolution of kill-stealing should be wrought before a Game Master, and all incidents against members should be brought to the attention of officers.

RESPECT AND VENERATION
All members of the Council of Arcana shall respect one another, fellow citizens of Norrath, and the Game Masters, Guides, and other officials through which the World of EverQuest is made a fair place for opportunity and success. Any intentional disrespect, in this form, or any other form, represents the guild poorly and harms the collective as a whole. Discussions over guildchat should be of a friendly, open nature, encouraging mutual respect and recognition as well as encouragement and support for one another and each person's goals. While spirited debate is always encouraged, meaningful and intentional personal assault, bashing or verbal abuse against a person is strictly dishonorable.
