IV and CoA to raid Plane of Fear -
Posted Saturday, March 17, 2001 - 2:04 AM EST by Kaelon Xerxentis
Immortal Vision and the Council of Arcana are to launch a joint raid on the Plane of Fear. Following Councilor Shmeep Shmee's stroke of bad luck in the City of Mists, we are committed to going back and assisting her in reacquiring her final components - including the Tear off of the Iksar Broodling from the Plane of Fear. We will also take this opportunity to tour the newly revamped, repopulated, and strengthened PoF and, in appropriate tradition, obliterate it entirely. You can find more information at the IV/CoA Pof Raid. The raid shall last from March 19 to March 21 (dates subject to change), beginning on Monday night at 8:00 pm Eastern Time. Please direct any questions or comments to Laer, Kaelon, or this thread. Also, please check the news item below this one for upcoming events this St. Patrick's Day Weekend.
