Members of the Committee,
Fellow Councilors,

I would like to bring to your attention that Senior Councilor Raevenn has petitioned the Arch Chancellory for intervention and protection of her person and post during times at which she is unable to presently fulfill the full responsibilities of the post of chair of the Committee for Newbie Events due to certain circumstances. After consideration, I have accepted Senior Councilor Raevenn's resignation as head of the Committee, and have decided to personally intervene and issue an Edict of Protection on her behalf.

Raevenn is to be assigned as an Adjutant of the Chancellory -- an officer that directly advises the Arch Chancellor, and is solely accountable to my person. The position of Adjutancy removes persons from the political process and alleviates certain degrees of regular accountability when it becomes evident that this is a productive step that we must take in order to safeguard their well-being, protection, safety or in other cases, simply alleviate their work-load and provide an opportunity for meditative relaxation and refocusing of energies. Yet at the same time, the post of Adjutant is a fully empowered one and has the duty of counsel to the Arch Chancellor and is assigned to my personal retinue.

Consequently, the post of Senior Councilor of Review for the Committee on Newbie Events has become vacant, and will require a successor to match Raevenn's dedicated and committed spirit to both the Council and this committee's mission. After consideration, I have decided to name Councilor Gabriale, upon his acceptance, the new Senior Councilor of Review for the Committee on Newbie Events, upon his notification that he accepts this responsibility and duty.

While we are saddened to see Raevenn leave the leadership of this committee, as Adjutant, she will have the full flexibility she needs to participate anywhere in the Council that she so chooses, while at the same time removing her from any accountability or responsibility, and protecting her with the full force of the Arch Chancellory. At her side is, of course, a fellow Adjutant, the Councilor Adjutant Clarisse.

If you have any questions, comments, or thoughts, please post them here. Thank you for your support. So has it been written, so shall it be done.

Kaelon Xerxentis
Arch Chancellor
