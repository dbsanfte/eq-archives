Continuing Adventures -
Posted Saturday, March 24, 2001 - 5:32 AM EST by Kaelon Xerxentis
Last night, the Council of Arcana embarked on another massive set of adventuring marathons. Councilors began at 8 pm Eastern Time with a completely successful raid into the City of Mists, wherein Shmeep Shmee acquired her Epic Weapon (see the news item below). Councilors proceeded afterwards and ran a successful raid into Nagafen's Lair (Sol B), camping the Efreeti spawn and obliterating virtually the entire dungeon (nox, bugs, bats, imps, etc.) along its path, while also welcoming its newest members - Cyphlor and Natiliana. THIS WEEKEND: The Plane of Hate Raid is scheduled to proceed as intended, although it may be modified by the raid leadership as we approach encounters. It commences at 2:00 PM Eastern Time - meeting place at the South Ro Wizard Pyramid. Please read the latest on the PoH raid. As I will not be in attendance, I wish you all the best of luck and the finest adventures! NEXT WEEK: Numerous events are shaping up nicely for next week. At first glance, I see this on Tuesday and Thursday, this once he pops, this in the works, and so much more! Please contribute thoughts in the private forums, and -- always -- have fun!
