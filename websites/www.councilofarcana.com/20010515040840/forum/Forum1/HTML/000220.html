As you could tell from the News page, today is a major Patch Day for EverQuest. Why not spend your online time surfing the web and posting messages on the discussion forums with some real-time excitement, and join our chat party! People will be in and out throughout the day, chatting and doing other stuff while they're chatting, so it's a great way to pass the time with your guildmates

The #CouncilofArcana chat room is IRC-based, so you can use IRC-type commands, and access it from any IRC client (like mIRC, http://www.mirc.com ). Just point the client (if you're not using our web site's Java chat) to chat.planetz.net (port 7000, even though 6667 works for me) and /join #CouncilofArcana from there. Java-based chatters can use the /float command to make the java window pop out so they can surf and chat at the same time .

All chatters (both via the Java-based chat and the client chat) will want to Register their desired nicknames (e.g., "Kaelon"). To register your nickname, do a /NickServ help and follow the prompts from there. This way, your nickname remains unique and unused (and I can start assigning ops to chatters so that can run the chat room ).

I'll be dropping by the chat room after my morning classes (so around 4 pm Eastern, hopefully sooner) and chat with everyone while I'm doing my lab writeup, posting messages, and just fooling around. Hope to see you all there!

Kaelon
