The Council of Arcana is a registered guild in the world of Norrath, the land in which the game EverQuest occurs. This web site serves to provide a community area for members of the Council of Arcana and also presents information to those not familiar with our guild. Council of Arcana is located on "Quellious" Server. To navigate our site, please select from one of the topics on the left. The listing to the right displays a few of our many upcoming events from which members benefit. News and announcements can be found below.

Public Meetings to Begin Tonight -
Posted Thursday, February 22, 2001 - 2:47 PM EST by Kaelon Xerxentis
In starting a new tradition, the Office of the Chancellory will begin holding weekly meetings, every Thursday night at 9:00 PM Eastern Time, over our Chat Channel, wherein all members are welcome to attend with thoughts, questions, concerns, ideas and suggestions. The sessions will last 30 minutes, and will enable members to brainstorm with the leadership, officers and fellow members. For more details, please read the announcement. Thanks!

Swords of Truth Fear Raid Concluded -
Posted Thursday, February 22, 2001 - 4:23 AM EST by Kaelon Xerxentis
The Swords of Truth Raid on the Plane of Fear concluded successfully with an earlier-than-scheduled withdrawal from the Plane of Fear when Quellious was brought back online very late last night (2 AM EST from my count). Excellent drops, wonderful comradery with our allies Swords of Truth, and the high spirit of adventure united us in the common pursuit to defeat the Minions of Fear. PLEASE NOTE: Council of Arcana members with characters still camped in the Plane of Fear who did not withdraw last night, please read this announcement for guidance. Thanks, and once again, congratulations to all!

Introduction to the Council Published -
Posted Tuesday, February 20, 2001 - 3:38 PM EST by Kaelon Xerxentis
At the request of several members, I have published an Introduction to the Council of Arcana, complete with updated questions and answers, and a detailed power-flow chart describing the hierarchy, positions and responsibilities. I hope that this is useful!

Tuesday Infobits II -
Posted Tuesday, February 20, 2001 - 3:37 PM EST by Kaelon Xerxentis
In continuing with the tradition of doing lengthier news updates on our web sites every Tuesday, I'm proud to present Tuesday Infobits II. This past week, the Council of Arcana cleared The Hole and slew the infamous Master Yael thanks to the skilled leadership of Shmeep, Allanna, Dindayen, and, of course, Laer and our companions from Immortal Vision. Additionally, a few more members obtained their Phenocryst's Focus - including Jahve, Fanielar, and Sinuuyanea. This week, several members are joining the raid of our allies, the Swords of Truth, on the Plane of Fear and sharing in the comradery and good spirits of the adventure. Please feel free to check our forums for other upcoming events.
