TITLE V: ADVANCEMENT PREREQUISITES

Art. 41: Councilors of the Council of Arcana shall be able to advance through the hierarchy of leadership when, through merit and initiative, they are recognized and offered the opportunity to undertake serious responsibilities and charges for the welfare of the collective, by the appropriate individual or organ of the guild.

Art. 42: Councilors must have served certain periods of time as full members of the Council of Arcana prior to being legally considered for such advancement opportunities.

Art. 43: In order for a Councilor to be elected, selected or appointed to the Senior Council, or as a Senior Councilor, he or she must have been a full Councilor for a minimum of approximately ninety (90) days, and have no demerits or inconclusive holds upon his or her record.

Art. 44: In order for a Councilor to volunteer to serve on the War Council, or any other advisory or ad hoc committee, commission or council, he or she must have been a full Councilor for a minimum of approximately thirty (30) days, and there must be no objections from any officers of the guild or any other participants or members of the body into which the Councilor is entering.

Art. 45: The High Council, with the oversight of the Arch Chancellor, shall oversee the fashion in which any and all advancements are performed in the Council of Arcana, and may designate any method as deemed appropriate or necessary for such an activity.

Art. 46: Should the High Council designate an election for a post, then a public election shall be held accessible to all guild members for witness and only full members ranked Councilor or higher for final deliberation status.

Art. 47: All elections of the Council of Arcana shall have any and all nominees seconded by the general membership and approved by the body or consultative institution wherein the position resides.

Art. 48: All elections of the Council of Arcana shall have, at minimum, five (5) days during which to conduct preliminary deliberations and presentations, and three (3) days during which to conduct final deliberations and/or voting.

Art. 49: Advancement through the Council of Arcana shall be based exclusively upon merit, and retention of said advanced positions shall be based solely upon the capacity and performance of the individual to undertake and uphold the responsibilities and duties of the office into which he or she has entered, as deemed appropriate by the leading organs and institutions of the Council of Arcana, or through other guiding principles as specified in the Prime Charter.
