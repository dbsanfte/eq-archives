The Council of Arcana is a highly exclusive guild in the World of Norrath. Its unique selectivity provides that membership should only be granted to those persons who are invited by a currently existing, veteran member. In order to be considered for membership in the Council of Arcana, you must also fill out this application. The Council's General Assembly and Board of Admissions will examine your application and after a short period return with a decision. Currently, only players on the Quellious server can be considered for membership in the Council of Arcana. Membership in the Council of Arcana can be infinitely rewarding through our system of family and community, and it is strictly a long-term, serious and dedicated commitment. Please read the Introduction to the Council of Arcana if you have not done so already. If you have been invited and interviewed, please fill out this petititon to join and we can process your application.
