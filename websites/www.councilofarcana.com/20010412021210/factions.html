Through interaction and discourse, the Council of Arcana establishes certain stances towards individuals. Although the Council regards itself above petty, mundane issues such as guild wars and inter-guild relations, the High Council has made certain to vocalize its view of others - whether they be individuals or groups. They are identified below, cited for the reasons meriting their association with the Council.

Warmly
Those viewed 'warmly' by the Council enjoy the highest level of association with the Council while still retaining an individual identity separate from the Council of Arcana.

Immortal Vision
The Council of Arcana shares an Alliance with Immortal Vision, and through the spirit of comradery and partnership welcomes its members into the Council of Arcana extended family. Immortal Vision is a guild of extremely capable and honorable adventurers, whose commitment to adventure is rivaled only by their dedication to the principles of family and comradery.

Swords of Truth
A brave and courageous gathering of adventurers, the Swords of Truth and the Council of Arcana have been allies in the pursuit of friendship and fun and have regularly taken on challenges together, side-by-side. The Swords of Truth adhere to a strict set of principles of Love, Honour, Selflessness and Respect. We approach Norrath together in fraternity, comradery and trust.

Kindly
The Council views others 'kindly' through the degree to which they cherish our way of life, respect our customs and traditions and honor our sacred principles.

Ebane
Ebane helped found the Council of Arcana, establish its selectivity and exclusivity requirements and has remained an honorable and loyal friend, even after her departure from the guild. She aids those in need with no questions, and offers her friendship unconditionally as we do to her.

Miing
Miing is a warm, caring and loving individual, who has forged friendships with numerous Council of Arcana members.

Amicable
Persons that show benevolence, grace and live by the virtues of benevolence demonstrate their association as being Amicable, and are regarded as friends of the Council.

Dragon's Bane
Dragon's Bane Online Gaming Group (OGG) is an honorable guild of adventurers and companions, who take an active interest and share the Council of Arcana's focus on family. CoA and DB have a common interest in the protection and promotion of the Quellious Server Community, and we exemplify our friendship through an active partnership in adventure, social and community events.

Malrissa
A Founder of the Council of Arcana, Malrissa abdicated her position in the Council and left the Plane of Quellious in search of new opportunities elsewhere. While she will be missed by many, her decision is also respected, and her individuality asserted, she is viewed as a friend of the Council.

Sana
Offering advice and insight into the world of commerce of Norrath, Sana has on many occasions fought by the side of members of the Council and has also prevented members from making purchases which would otherwise be ludicrous.

Indifference
All citizens of Norrath outside of the Council of Arcana automatically are viewed indifferently. This is the standard faction to which members of the Council of Arcana posture themselves, but sometimes it may be to heighten a state of neutrality between the Council and another group.

All persons and associations unspecified hold a standard faction of indifference with the Council of Arcana.

Dubiously
For taking steps which hinder the Council of Arcana's progress or offending our way of life, these persons are viewed 'dubiously' by the Council.

Bermann
A kill-stealer that frequents South Karana.

Opacus
Ninja-looted the Cloak of Feather drop from Quillmane.

Sildidar
A kill-stealer that frequents South Karana.

Xpoc
A kill-stealer and former member who harassed Council officers and repeatedly showed lack of insight and immaturity in dealing with the civilizations of Norrath. It was later discovered that his involvement in Norrath has bordered on repeated nuisance, self-service and sacrificing others for his own personal gain. He was quickly ejected from the Council.

Threateningly
Individuals who have done more than simply irritate the Council of Arcana, but seek conflict and vehemently oppose the Council of Arcana at every step are viewed upon as being Threatening. For the elimination of these persons, the Council usually offers a sincere reward.

Azair
Misled the Council on his intentions when he joined and tried to sabotage the guild. Intentionally engaged in disreputable conduct and commerce with other members of Norrath, he is not to be trusted and is a danger both to himself and anyone around him.

Kilios
An associate of Korianis (see below), his faction standing is through association and in assisting Korianis in carrying out his deeds.

Korianis
A former member of the Council, Korianis was ejected from the Council of Arcana after repeated acts of disrespect (such as kill-stealing). He then intentionally trained three founders and refused to return previously-looted body loot to its original owner. Since then, he has made repeated attempts to assault our message boards with profanity and has violated the privacy of members by sending unsolicited mass e-mails. He remains a danger to himself and those around him and should not be trusted.

Slapz
A loot-stealer, Slapz - most likely a secondary twink character - ninja-looted Sir Lucan, during a Council-sponsored raid. His direct assault against the guild caused him immediate faction readjustment, yet this alignment serves to warn others about the dangers of ninja-looters and how real a threat this truly is.

Scowlingly, Ready to Attack
The Council of Arcana does not engage in inter-guild conflicts. With this said, it is very improbable that individuals would be found on our 'scowlingly, ready to attack' list, but those that are can expect armed confrontation whenever before the Council and those that defend our way of life.

Currently none have attained this disgrace.
