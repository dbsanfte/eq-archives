In 3179, on a cold winter's eve while most of Norrath suffered under the cruelty of Nature's Torment, the High Council of the Council of Arcana convened in secrecy in the Temple of Solusek Ro, near the Lavastorm Mountains. Therein, a pact between those present was sealed which established for all eternity the Principle of the Council -- a collection of principles and guiding notions about the Council's existence, its purpose and its ideology.

PRINCIPLE OF ALIGNMENT
The Council of Arcana shall be dedicated to the promotion of Six Sacred Virtues -- Law, Order, Justice, Honor, Truth and Comradery. The Council also recognizes the eternal struggle between forces of benevolent and malevolent resolve. In this polarising affair, the Council elects to remain neutral and uninvolved, seeking instead to advance its own agenda through its membership - holding no prejudice or preference to those who subscribe to the forces of Light or Darkness.

PRINCIPLE OF COMMUNITY
The Council of Arcana cherishes the notion of Comradery, and will strive to enable all of its members to be equal contributors and participants within its family, deriving no more nor less than what they contribute. The Community of the Council of Arcana must be one based upon Trust and Truth, and as such, all members shall have a vested interest in the operation and welfare of the guild, but ultimately, must work together to keep individual concerns alive amidst the chorus that is that of the collective.

PRINCIPLE OF SOVEREIGNTY
The Council of Arcana shall be an order respectful of authority and venerable of sovereign power. As such, its membership shall acknowledge the seniority of its leadership, not question the decisions made in the interests of the guild welfare when appropriate, and work to defend, protect and honor the leadership of the Council. The internal mechanizations, administrative operation, and consequently, the generation of policy and procedure are strictly monopolized by the leadership.

PRINCIPLE OF HONOUR
The Council of Arcana shall be a guild of honour, whose members conduct themselves with mutual respect and appreciation for themselves and for fellow creatures of Norrath. The Council shall not tolerate kill stealing or intentional, unprovoked player killing, and will take decisive actions as a community to discipline such infractions as well as fight vehemently against those who perform such acts against the Council's membership. The Council shall not engage in aggression unless first provoked or an issue of safety, sovereignty, or these principles is brought into question.

PRINCIPLE OF STANDARDS
The Council of Arcana shall be an institution of capable, dedicated, competent persons who appreciate and contribute to the world of Magic, Arcana and Spellcasting. Its membership shall include persons without prejudice of gender, class, race or religion, with the one exception that all members take an Oath to uphold these Sacred Principles and fight to defend the Council of Arcana, its membership, leadership and the community as a whole.
