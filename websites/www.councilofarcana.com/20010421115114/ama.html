PREAMBLE
We, the Councilors of the Council of Arcana, do hereby set forth these titles and articles to govern and guide our actions, procedures and policies in the act which is most fundamental to any guild or association of adventurers: its capacity to expand those within its organization. We remain committed to the proposition that in objectivity, clarity and detail shall we find the comprehension for effective laws, and as such, follow to write these by the Principles proclaimed by our Ancient Founders, and in the eternal spirit of Law, Order, Justice, Truth, Honor and Comradery.
