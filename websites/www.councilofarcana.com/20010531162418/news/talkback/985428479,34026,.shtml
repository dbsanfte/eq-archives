Shmeep obtains Epic Weapon! -
Posted Saturday, March 24, 2001 - 5:07 AM EST by Kaelon Xerxentis
Last night, in a thunderous raid upon the City of Mists, Councilor Shmeep Shmee - protected and escorted by her family members and trusted Council companions - stormed the very nexus of that dark and tormented city, and enstilled within it a Final Justice. Shmeep has obtained her Epic Weapon, and the Queen of Spear and Spell stands triumphant as one of the many growing sentinels of how the virtues of family have triumphed in harmony. (Herein is an image of Shmeep wielding her Epic Weapon.) I invite you all to please join me in congratulating Shmeep in acquiring her Epic Weapon! Congratulations, Shmeep!
