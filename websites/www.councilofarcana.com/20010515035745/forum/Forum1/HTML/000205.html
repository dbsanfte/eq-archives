Earlier today, in an informal ceremony in the Dreadlands, Callaile Amanda was recognized as 'Senior Councilor General'. I just wanted to recap some thoughts about Callaile, and invite you all to share your thoughts about this wonderful person.

The Council of Arcana is an association of friends and forms a family. We strive to live up to the expectations set forth by the Code of Honor and the Principles of the Council. I can think of no one that exemplifies them more clearly than Callaile. Her relentless selflessness, generosity and sincere support of her fellow members is unparalleled, and is usually at the expense of her own time and energies. She remains a role-model and inspiration for us all.

After speaking with other Senior Councilors and High Councilors, I decided that there was no better way to recognize Callaile than through this prestige that she rightfully deserves. As Senior Councilor General, Callaile has no responsibilities or requirements to fulfill - other than being her wonderful self - and she is recognized as an officer of the guild. She can /guildremove and /guildinvite persons as needed to carry out the directives of the High Council, but is not required to. She seemed genuinely worried that she wouldn't have enough time to fulfill this role, but there is no role to be filled, really -- it is a post that deserves its sole recognition and a merit that stands on her own accomplishments.

Congratulations, Callaile!

I invite all of you to share your thoughts about Callaile and join me in congratulating her on this accomplishment and thanking her deeply for being the supportive and nurturing person that she is.

Kaelon Xerxentis
Arch Chancellor
