When members of the Council of Arcana are recognized through merit or excellence with a Medal, their names appear herein in the Hall of Heroes forever memorialized. If you wish to nominate someone for a medal, please first review the list of medals available and then submit a nomination by way of our contact section. Awards are sorted alphabetically, as are the recipients. Members of the High Council have recused themselves from receiving medals, as they are part of the panel which decides recipients. Without further ado, we do hereby present before all the citizens and adventurers of Norrath, the Hall of Heroes of the Council of Arcana.

THE HALL OF HEROES

The Highest Honors
The Star of Allanna
• LATYAN KIEM: For the creation and maintenance of the Council of Arcana's Lyceum, a place for the public expression of the creative arts and imaginative works. (March 2001)

The Civic Honors
The Coin of Malrissa
• SINUUYANEA NOCTURNE: For her mastery of numerous trade skills and comprehensive coverage of the Norrathian Markets, providing members with a clear insight into the commercial and economic world of trade in EverQuest. (March 2001)

The Golden Heart
• ALTA PRIMAVERA: For her repeated friendship, kindnesses and selfless acts of compassion and friendship to all that seek aid, she is recognized with this decoration. (March 2001)
• EXPEDITER HIERO: For deferring earlier this month an item which he had legitimately won in a raid against Lord Nagafen, deferred to another player who had arrived late and rolled late. He was not compelled to do this, but did so out of his own sense of selflessness and honor. (March 2001)
• RIAH LAVEAUX: For her selfless love and compassion for fellow members in providing them with guidance, direction and a warm shoulder. (March 2001)
• SHIVSII ENCHANTASAURUS: For his repeated acts of selfless kindness, genuine generosity, kindness and sincere dedication to the community as a whole, he is recognized with this decoration. (March 2001)
• TALSIA: For aiding a fellow guildmember and companion, Alanina/Traitea, with access to EverQuest and entering her into a rehabilitation program following her unfortunate loss of character. The guild recognizes Talsia's selflessness and true dedication to a noble friend. (March 2001)

The Medallion of Glory
• HEILIG HANGRANATE: Recognized ex officio for his exemplary works in serving as a member of the Senior Council with distinction, and for having acted always in honor and glory, exemplifying the virtues of excellence in leadership, and setting the example for future generations of officers. He stepped down from the Senior Council, furthermore, for what he believed in. (March 2001)
• RIAH LAVEAUX: For her work as an outstanding and exemplary officer of the Council of Arcana, the guild does hereby recognize her for her efforts and resounding dedication to our guild's governing principles. (March 2001)

The Sigil of the Sacred Six
• LANENYA SILVERFYRE: For her tireless efforts over the past year in offering selfless love and guidance to all members, fighting repeatedly for the Justice of our Virtues. She remains a shining example of a dedicated guildmember committed to defending our principles. (March 2001)

The Military Honors
The Crescent of Kunark
• ALAYTHIA: For organizing and leading completely successful raids previously into the heart of Nurga and Kaesora. (March 2001)
• MORUDOTH: For organizing and leading a completely successful raid into the heart of Nurga. (March 2001)
• SHMEEP SHMEE: For organizing and leading two completely successful raids into the City of Mists. (March 2001)
• VANDALL: For organizing and leading completely successful raids into Nurga and Droga. (March 2001)

The Crescent of Norrath
• DERXUS SILVERSTAR: For organizing and leading a completely successful raid against Lord Nagafen. (March 2001)
• SPHYRE ARIN`TAYN: For organizing and leading a completely successful raid against Lady Vox in January of 2001. (March 2001)
• TERA BRAVESILVER: For leading a completely successful raid against the Lord Efreeti Djinn. (March 2001)
• WINOR SACRIFICE: For organizing and leading two consecutive and completely successful raids against Lord Nagafen. (March 2001)

The Medal of Supremacy
• IULDIIN: For, while leading a successful raid into the City of Mists, developing an ingenious and never previously seen tactic of using fire elemental pets against an insidious foe and focusing military efforts on providing coverage for these main artilleries. (March 2001)

The Star of Ferocity
• VANDALL: For organizing and leading an impromptu, three-day raid into the Plane of Hate. (March 2001)
