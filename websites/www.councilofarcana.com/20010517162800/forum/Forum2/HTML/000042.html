Edited info from Kaelon:
(last updated 16 May 2000)

What -- Raid on Kedge Keep
Time -- 6 pm, May 19th (Friday)
Meeting/Bind Place -- Outside Tunnel to Unrest in Dragnor's Cauldron
RSVP -- Please sign up if you can make it!

I was asked by Kaelon if I would like to organize this Kedge Raid and I decided I would, so here it is.

Date:
I am not exactly sure of a date, but would think a good date would be May 19 or 20 so that is about 2 weeks from this post.

Requirements:
I think this raid would require at least 2-3 groups with 1-2 40+ groups. A minimum level for this event is around 35 which makes it targeted at the higher level guild population.

Hopefully there will be a fair number of 45+ participants.

Objectives:
I have a somewhat vested interest in this raid because I would like to obtain the lock of hair from the golden haired mermaid for the Rain Caller bow quest.

Other objectives will be to try for other named spawns such as cauldronbubble and cauldronboil.

Preliminary Plans:
I think it would be a good idea to set-up camp in the south room on the second level which gives us access to the golden haired mermaid and all other mobs on the 2nd level (plz read about this room and the golden haired mermaid in the Ultimate Guide to Kedge). From there we can move on to other named spawns and places to camp.

Dangers and Cautions:
There are many dangers in Kedge Keep, one being with enduring breathe. Everyone should keep enduring breathe on and have a water stone with them at all times.

-It can be dangerous to use AoE spells in Kedge so don't use them

-Charm is very effective in Kedge (especially druid's charm) but the charmed mobs have the habit of running away so it is important you keep track of charmed mobs and probably uncharm them right after the fight.

These dangers and cautions can all be read about in the Ultimate Guide to Kedge.

HIGHLY Recommended Resources:
Phreekittys Ultimate Guide to Kedge Keep is the primary resource I used to plan this raid and everyone should have a look at it. It took me forever to read it . You can get to this guide through this link: http://everquest.allakhazam.com/Phreekittys_Ultimate_Guide_to_Kedge_Keep.htm

The Merry Bands Map of Kedge Keep was the map I used and should be printed out by everyone. It is a good map and looks nice . You can find it at this link: http://everquest.allakhazam.com/maps/Kedge_Merry_Band.html

Please post any suggestions, qwuestions and when you think a good date and time would be below. Also post whether you think you could attend or are interested in attending.
