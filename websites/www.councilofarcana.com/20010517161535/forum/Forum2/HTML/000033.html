First I would like to say Greetings to you Council of Arcana Guild members!! I am Queen Allanna DaLioness, Leader of the Silent Echos, allies to you CoA.

I am to inform CoA of a Guild Event my guild is tossing on May 13th in the land of Norrath. All CoA members are invited to attend and help us out if they are free to. Details on the Event are posted on our website at http://www.geocities.com/Silentechos1/
Before the Event, the guild will have a little mini Raid on Crushbone to help get items for members who need to raise faction with Greater Faydark. The Actual carnival itself starts at NOON EST with 5 total events. If anyone is able to help us out, we need it! We really need people who are willing to do teleports to the various events, but will take anyone who will help us out. After the Carnival, my Guild will be headed to The Arena off of Lake Rathetear zone to have a little fun, and you are also welcomed to attend that as well.

To kinda explain why we do this, We, the Silent Echos, believe in helping the citizens of Norrath as much as we can. To do this, we toss Guild Events that help out the lower levels citizens in the land of Norrath usually once a month. We offer free Binds, buffs, prizes, ect…to those who need it. And to have a little fun as well.

Please let us know if you are able to help us out with the event! We would love to get to know more of you CoA members as well and you are all always welcomed to attend any event in which we are holding. Qenaman Lifeweaver is the person who is running the show for our Spring Carnival, so if you have questions please ask Me or Qenaman.

Thank you for you time, and good hunting to all of you,

Queen Allanna DaLioness
Leader of Silent Echos
Wizard of the 30th Circle

WHEN: Saturday, May 13th
