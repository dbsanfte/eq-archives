Hi all, just wanted to invite you all to a little event that Crush and Swill is going to sponsor. We are inviting all interested parties to join us for a raid on Upper Guk Saturday (Apr. 22) at 8am EST. The raid will be led by Stondogg and Grimblades from Crush and Swill. Our objectives are to smoosh the ancient crocodile for some gatorscale pieces, grab a Ghoulbane or two =), and lay the beat down on the Squires. Most of all, our main objective is to drink a lot of swill and have an awesome time! Swill will of course be provided by CaSC. So if you're up for some fun, stop by the entrance to Guk (east side of Innothule swamp) and join the party!

Seeya,
