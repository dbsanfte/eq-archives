KAELON XERXENTIS: A BIOGRAPHICAL PROFILE
Full Name: Kaelon Andropos Xerxentis
Date of Birth: 2 June 3150 N.D.
Place of Birth: Erudin Palace, The High City of Erudin, Odus
Race: Erudite
Occupations: Sorceror, Historian, Politician
Specialization: Evocation
First Mastery: Research
Council Ledger Profile: Herein

Education:
- Preliminary: Grammaticus, Erudin
- Advanced: Scholae Wizardrae, Erudin Palace
- Professional: The Crimson Hands, Erudin Palace
(Focus in Tactical Spellcasting, Metamagical Sciences and Offensive Thaumaturgy)

Employment History:
- SCRIBE, Great Library of Erudin (3155-3158): Responsible for cataloguing, transcripting and decyphering documents for regular use by the citizenry of the High City of Erudin.
- CURATOR, Special Artifacts of Erudin palace (3158-3161): Responsible for maintaining, preserving and protecting artifacts dating from the Early Kerran Period of Odus; led an expedition to the excavation of Kerra Isle in 3160.
- APPRENTICE, Scholae Wizardry (3161-3162): Responsible for translating documents from Old Erudian to Contemporary Erudian and Common, as well as performing minor research. Learned the fundamentals of evocation, alteration, abjuration and divination.
- ADVENTURER, The Crimson Hands (3166-Present): Following graduation from the Crimson Hands in Erudin Palace, Kaelon Xerxentis received the Sr. Tunic of his guild and released to perform studies as a Journeyman Wizard.
- LOREMASTER, Antonican League of Bards (3170-Present): Conducted research and correspondences as a traveler from Western to Eastern Antonica. Obtained a Writ of Historical Mastery from the Soerbaird in 3171.
- HIGH COUNCILOR, High Council of Erudin (3172-Present): Following the formation of the Council of Arcana, as Erudite Law decrees, Kaelon Xerxentis obtained a seat obtain the High Council of Erudin - the governing body of the High City of Erudin. Helped maintain Erudin's Neutrality in the Teir`Dal Incursion into Freeport and Highpass Keep. Supported the declaration of War against Qeynos in 3186.
- ARCH CHANCELLOR, Council of Arcana (3172-Present): One of the original nine founders of the Council of Arcana, founded on 5 August 3172 in Erudin.

Personal Family History: A reclusive and solitary Erudite, Kaelon courted late in his life and wed the High Elf Queen Allanna Dalionness in 3189. Allanna Xerxentis gave birth to a daughter, Fanila Xerxentis, a Cleric, in 3191.

Hobbies: Military Science, Philosophy.
