What is this site for?

The EQ Women message boards came into existence around July 2000. One or two other attempts had been made to create such a community, but for some reason or other didn't quite take off. The boards, and now this site, are by women and for women (though we are not exclusionary, any insightful comment is welcome, whoever you are). Women gamers are more prevalent than one might think, but one thing we do tend to have in common is a lack of female friends with similar interests in real life! So at last, here is an opportunity to share our interests with other women...
