Some Poems from Shauna (Kandaorma)

Untitled 
March 20, 1998 


You call me paranoid... 
It's how I survive 
Others build walls 
It seems you've perfected it 
I, on the other hand 
Am not a mason 
Paranoia is my wall 
When you stand there 
Pretending to let me through your wall 
I know behind your back 
You are holding a blade I know, 
For there are many blades sticking out of me heart 
(And even more in my back) 
It's not paranoia, 
My dear... 
It is experience 

"A Glimpse of Time: March 9, 1998 

Heaven... 
The tender look in your eyes 
The sweet taste of your lips pressed ever so gently to mine 
The tender touch of your hands 
As they gently caress my body 
As we lay side by side 
Completely taken by the moment- 
But it was all a dream 
A glimpse of time 
To be locked in the past 
And I wonder- 
Do you remember it too?
