What is this site for?

The EQ Women message boards came into existence around July 2000. One or two other attempts had been made to create such a community, but for some reason or other didn't quite take off. The boards, and now this site, are by women and for women (though we are not exclusionary, any insightful comment is welcome, whoever you are). Women gamers are more prevalent than one might think, but one thing we do tend to have in common is a lack of female friends with similar interests in real life! So at last, here is an opportunity to share our interests with other women...

EQ Women is not just a site for women who play Everquest, though that is obviously it's primary function! The community itself is central to everything, and arose from the message board that was actually set up before the rest of the site. The women here have more in common than Everquest : we are social types who like to talk, we like gaming, we tend to like Fantasy and Science Fiction (especially the former), and above all, we are astonished to find other women like ourselves!

The site is more of a club than an informational site or magazine, though it serves that function too, and we welcome more members every week....

Above all this site is what you make it, as submissions on any subject relevant to women with the above interests are very welcome indeed.
