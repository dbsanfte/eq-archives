Ezboard login problems

 

It has come to my attention that a sizable percentage of people have real problems accessing the messageboards, even when they are a member, now that the board has gone to Membership By Approval. It seems likely that this is a cookie problem, so I thought I would list various things you can try to fix it!

 

  1.  Delete your ezboard cookie (you can locate this using the view files option in your internet explorer options dialog).
  2. Ensure ALL cookie options are turned on. In IE 6 this is found on the privacy tab of the internet explorer options. Click Advanced and then ensure that accept first and third party options have been selected, then also make sure there is a tick in the box marked always accept session cookies.
  3. If you still have problems reaching eqwomen, login at the ezboard main page and go to your control center. Once there click on my preferences and turn OFF high security.
