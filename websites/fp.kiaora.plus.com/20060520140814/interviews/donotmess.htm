Do not mess with a 21 month old's mind
ok, here's the problem.

My daughter is 21 months old. She knows where her toes are, her nose, cheeks (on her face), her chin, her ears, her hair, and her eyes are. She also knows where her bellybutton is. If you ask her where these things are she points to them. However she is VERY concerned that her belly button will disappear. This is because her bellybutton is covered so when you ask her where it is, she immediately has to check to make sure it's still there. And she is constantly amazed that it is.

Now, Sara has some outfits that are body suits or overalls. She also has PJ's that are the footie ones that zip up. All of these outfits cover her bellybutton so she can't get to them. If you ask her where her belly button is she freaks out till she is down to her diaper with nothing else on and she can see her bellybutton. Furthermore, she will refuse to put on ANY clothes so that tricky belly button doesn't thwart her again.

So, why is it that when ever I put her in one of these outfits, some member of the family thinks it's funny to ask her where her belly button is? Then they point and laugh as she goes into a demonic rage at the disappearing belly button and the clothes which were designed to thwart her at every turn. Mean time, I have a 21 month year old demo possessed child freaking out over her missing bellybutton. And you can't just unsnap the clothing to show it to her. Oh no. The missing belly button must not only be found, but cannot be allowed to go missing again. So now we have nudie time. And if I change her diaper and cover the belly button we have a whole new issue. Literally she won't leave the diaper changing table till I adjust the offending diaper so that the proper respect to the bellybutton is shown.

It is also apparently fun to ask Sara where Mommy's belly button is. And she comes running up and will not leave me alone till she lifts up my shirt to find it. And if I am wearing jeans, they must be moved to find the missing bellybutton. And of course after my bellybutton is found we must go looking for hers cause it might have escaped. Those belly buttons are tricky things.

All I ask is that when she is wearing clothing that does not allow for easy access to the belly button that people do not ask her where her belly button is. This makes my job much easier. And furthermore, since I have had my bra exposed many times now in her quest for my belly button, I would request that we stop asking her to locate mine. It's been there for a long time and I am not concerned about it leaving anytime in the near future.

Please. Stop the belly button questing madness!


Thank you for your support.
