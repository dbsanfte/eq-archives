School Rant
Some background on my rant.

I am enrolled in school full time and I am majoring in Programming. One of my core classes is C++. Ok, now to the rant

GGGGGGAAAAAHHHH!!!!!!!!! Apparently expecting DeVry to use some common sense is asking way to much. First, the state of Illinois requires all schools (including colleges) to have updated shot records. This means that I have to provide my shot records. Cool, I get them from my highschool. This would be the highschool I graduated from 10 years ago. I have that sent in. They say that's not enough. They need a tetanus shot and a measles shot. I point out that 1) I have sent in the tetanus shot record from OSU. Apparently they lost it. Have to get another one cause I sent my only copy. (I am a fool) 2) I had the measles when I was 3 and I have a natural antibody. No good. They need the shot. I go to my doctor and she says "Your blood work from your pregnancy shows that you have an immunity to the measles. You don't need the shot." She faxes over a form that the STATE PROVIDES saying that I don't need the shot because my blood work shows I already have the immunity. The school refuses to accept it. So I have to get a measles shot. Why? Cause the state requires it. However, the state accepts the form from my doctor. I called and asked. I had a letter sent to them showing that the form was ok. What does my school do? Oh...go ahead and guess.

That's right, makes me get the shot. Bad words being shouted right now. Editing them out for the benefit of the young or easily offended.

So I get the shot thing fixed. After 2 months of hassle.

On top of this my core class of c++ is kicking my butt. Why you ask? Oh that would be because the book they make us use is an advanced c++ book. And, we are in intro. So that make sense, in the same way that child proof bottle caps on aspirin keep kids out of the bottle. It doesn't. So, I am hoping that my teacher is able to teach so I can understand it. That would be a waste of time since my teacher took a c++ course 2 years ago and hasn't used it since. So he is teaching from another teachers syllabus. So he has NO CLUE! My book is useless. So I borrow my sister in law's book to learn from which is great. Mean time the prof is trying to teach and he doesn't know what he is doing, I am confused the book is useless and I am getting a C for a grade.

Apparently expecting my school to put someone who is knowledgeable in the course as a teacher is asking too much. Especially considering my tuition is roughly $10,000 a year. I foolishly expect that my teachers will be knowledgeable and my books will be written for the level of the class. How silly. Why should I expect that? I am about to track down one of the deans and bludgeon them with my C++ book. Then I track down the moron who recommended this book and rip off his/her arm and beat them with the bloody end. Then I go find the moron who thought it would be cool to put my prof in a c++ class. And I will bring my prof with me and we will take turns holding down that moron and kicking him/her around the butt area since that is where their brain is stored.

What kills me is my c++ teacher is qualified to teach my systems class. My Systems teacher is qualified to teach my C++ class and they would both rather teach the class they aren't teaching. And my systems teacher would rock at teaching C++. So it's not like there weren't other options.

On top of this my dean is so sick of me she won't return my calls. The guy who signed me up messed up ALL my paper work. Now I have to give him credit. That takes a lot of work to categorically mess up every single bit of paperwork I had to do. That is a level of incompetence that quite frankly is impressive. I mean, when he screws up he goes full out. Anyone could do a half assed job, but he is so dedicated he did a full assed job. Much props to him. My dean has been helping me handle all the ignoramus who was my councillor during the sign up process so dedicatedly screwed up. She has helped me with the shots, the financial aid issues, the academics screw ups, and everything else. She has been great, but she has put me on the take a message list. Every time I call her aid transfers me directly to voicemail. Very annoying.

If you see on the news that a student in the Illinois area went postal with a c++ book in DeVry, you will know it's me. I need to get this degree done so I don't have to deal with this stuff anymore. I wanna trade in my current frustrations for ones that at least pay me.
