I am Asherea Mystwalker. Also known as Naharet Darkhealer, Naharet Nitecatcher, Kindaria, Stella and many others. Samantha Aurora Dawn being my chosen name, my real name is Samantha Dawn Blair, Samie for short. I currently reside in Anchorage, Alaska, USA. Raised in Seward, Alaska until the ripe old age of 17 when I moved out, I have lived in Alaska my whole life. I am but 20 years of age. I married my first love, Aaron, on New Years 2000. I dabble into the occult from time to time, depending on my mood. I also write short stories as well as poetry and I also draw when the feelings inside me urge me to. I read a lot, as I am a sponge for knowledge. Favorite books include: The Vampire Chronicles (Anne Rice) and the Mayfair Witch Chronicles (also by Anne Rice), Carrie (Stephen King), Dracula (Bram Stoker), and many more random ramblings of other authors. I listen to music a lot. My favorite artists include: Tori Amos, Type O Negative, The Cure, NIN, Korn, Fiona Apple, and Smashing Pumpkins. Hobbies and other interests include: Elemental Magic, Vampire Legends, Anime, Dancing in the Rain, EVERCRACK!!!, The Ocean, Boating, Swimming, and all things dark and gothic in nature.
