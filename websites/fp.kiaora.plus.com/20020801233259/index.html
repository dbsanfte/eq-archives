Oddest game ever? Black & White certainly has a lot to offer any RPG and god game fans...

Black and White from Lionhead is simply the strangest and most absorbing game I have played since errr... Deus Ex I guess :) read on

 His Dark Materials by Philip Pullman. The Trilogy including Northern Lights, The Subtle Knife and The Amber Spyglass has been an international bestseller. You will find it next to Harry Potter in the bookshops: personally I think it's rather better..

The Fresco by Sheri S Tepper is a feminist look at alien first contact. As usual, Tepper displays no fear of using the most fantastic ideas to drive home her point.
