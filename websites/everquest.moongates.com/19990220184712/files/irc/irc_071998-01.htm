July 1998 EFNet #EverQuest Chat
Page 1/ 6

Log Opened: Wed Jul 01 20:57:47 1998

<RaV> Good evening Brad!

<Aradune> hello everyone :)

<RaV> One moment and we'll get started. We'd like everyone to please refrain from nick changing during the chat.

<WarDragon> I'd like to welcome everybody to this months chat.
<WarDragon> We are going to start with questions fromt eh EQV chat board, then take questions through the relay sytem, which will be explained later
<WarDragon> Any misbeahvoir will result in a ban, so don't bother :)
<WarDragon> and don't mind my typos :)

<EQV-Qs> Is the game on schedule?

<Aradune> Hello all, and thanks for coming.
<Aradune> Yes, the game is on schedule, and everything looks great for a 4th qrt release. done

<EQV-Qs> Is there an age restriction for beta?

<Aradune> No, we have a section on the NDA for a parent or guardian to sign. done

<EQV-Qs> Why all the secrecy about beta?

<Aradune> A couple of reasons -- mainly what Shon touched on in the last interview, and also the fact that there is competition out there.done

<EQV-Qs> Games.net winners, where to they stand in the beta?

<Aradune> They were and are considered for phase one. done

<EQV-Qs> Many people want to know more about necromancers and how they manipulate the dead or living to create undead.
<EQV-Qs> Is there a tracking skill, and how does it work?

<Aradune> Necromancers animate corpses of NPCs, turning them into 'pets' that will do their bidding.
<Aradune> And yes there is a tracking skill, but I can't comment on exactly how it works, other than to say it's becoming very handy. done

<EQV-Qs> Can you tell us about missile weapons, and if they are retrivable? How about weapons that could be both hand and missle weapons, like a dagger or axe?

<Aradune> We have throwing axes and other weapons that can be used both as melee and missile wpns. done

<EQV-Qs> Can you tell us more about the interaction between classes and religion? Will all clerics abilities/limitations be dependent on the individual god?

<Aradune> At this point, the deity a cleric chooses to follow does not affect which abilities/prayers he gets. done

<EQV-Qs> How does hiding work in a multiple POV environment? Can you tell us a little more about thieves abilities?

<Aradune> Can't comment on those specific skills at this time, in that they are still being tuned. done

<EQV-Qs> Is it at all possible that someday in an add-on to EQ you could introduce horses or dragon riding?

<Aradune> Yes, it's a possibility. We definitely plan on having expansions, which could include more features, more lands, and more playable PC races/classses. done

<EQV-Qs> Tell us about lycanthropy and vampirism in EQ? Are they sun/moon sensitive?

<Aradune> Lycanthropy will most likely be in the game... vampirism is unknown at this point, as to whether it will be in-game at ship. Both are linked with time of day. done

<EQV-Qs> Please tell us about clothing, how much customization is possible?

<Aradune> There are several texture sets that change depending upon what you are wearing, some of which are tintable to different colors. done

<EQV-Qs> Please tell us about sound, what kind is supported, how detailed are the sound effects?

<Aradune> I can't say too much about sound, in that it's still being worked on. We'll get it as sophisticated as we can by ship. It will be midi, and will use higher end sound cards. There will be many sound effects, and we are researching 3d sound, but no committment at this point. done

<EQV-Qs> Please tell us about what factors you are considering in balancing the classes.

<Aradune> A few factors are very important to us -- first, that the classes are balanced such that you can play what you feel like playing -- what you want to role-play -- as opposed to being compelled to play a certain race/class because its simply better.
<Aradune> also, we want compelling reasons to play all race/class combos. we don't want any 'dud' classes, but that doesn't necessarily mean that they all need to be able to stand toe-to-toe with each other in combat
<Aradune> rather, that they are all useful in adventuring and questing. done
