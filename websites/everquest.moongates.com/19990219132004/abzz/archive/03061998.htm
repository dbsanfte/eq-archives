Dear Abzz,

I'm about to chew my fingernails down to the quick worrying about whether I'll get into the EverQuest beta or not. If I don't do something about it now, I won't have any fingers left to fill out the beta application. Help!

Signed
-Nubbin
Dear Nubbin,

Well, first off I have to say that you shouldn't worry so much about the beta. I know it's hard waiting for such a cool game, but that's what we have to do. If you fill out an app you're guaranteed to be in at least the last two phases of beta (the last phase of closed, and then the open beta after that). Other than that, you just need to suck it up and go on with your life. If you don't, you'll probably start writing advice columns or something. And I don't want the competition.

About your fingers -- Quit yer whinin'! Typing with your toes is a lot easier than everyone makes it out to be.
Dear Abzz,

He says I should get out more, and start talking with real people instead of these "freaks" online. I really like my online friends and don't want to lose them. Do you know of any way I can show him that ax-murdering stalkers aren't the only people you meet online?

Signed

Dear Living,

Depending on the age of your mom's sperm donor, you need to remind him of the CB craze of the late '70s. All the “freaks” he used to speak with on the CB have kids now too, and those are the friends you're talking with online. That line shut our pop up, anyway.

It's based on all those 12-step programs you find like AA, NA, OA etc. I've boiled it down to just the two essential things a random pkiller needs to do to end his unfortunate addiction.

1) Take 12 steps away from your computer
2) Stay there

See? All fixed.
