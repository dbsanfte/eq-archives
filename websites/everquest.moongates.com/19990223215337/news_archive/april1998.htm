April 27 1998
2:05pm EST
Seems like a lot of stuff happened during my absence. #EverQuest on EFNet has had a hostile takeover by people belonging to a group calling themselves RPG Net. The old ops on the channel has taken back control of the channel and are discussing issues of tighter security before re-opening the channel to the public. The takeover attempt was made possible due to the fact that one of the #EverQuest ops(nick withheld) made a silly mistake of giving out his password to another person posing as one of the #EverQuest bots, and then using that password to gain access. Please check back here and on EQ Vault for the latest news on the channel. Both I and WarDragon are the ops on the channel. Rest assured that the monthly chats will go on, maybe just at a later date than the intended date due to this incident.

EQX has released a new issue.SeVeN

April 19 1998
9:19am EST
Just some bit of info about new RPGs in development that has come to my notice. Septerra Core by Valkyrie Studios is a Final Fantasy type RPG. The artwork for the game looks very nice and the premise is set in a cyberpunk type world. If Valkyrie Studios can produce what it promises, this RPG should be a good contender against Fallout.

Another RPG is LMK by Larian Studios, a German development house. LMK stands for The Lady, The Mage and The Knight or Legenden Der MagierKriege in German. For those of you who knows German will realize that the German name for the game doesn't translate at all into the English name. It means Legend of The Mage(something), my German is very rusty so correct me if this translation is wrong. The graphics looks like Ultima 7 on steriods. I don't know much about the story of LMK as the English version of their site hasn't gone online yet. Judging from the graphics and what I could make out from the German description, this game is high fantasy. It should be an interesting game to watch for.

Lastly Might and Magic 6 should be out in stores shortly. I will write up a short blurp on it once I get my hands on a copy. It should give an enjoyable RPG experience while waiting for the EverQuest Beta to start.SeVeN

Aradune a.k.a Brad McQuaid dropped by #everquest on EFNet for a brief break. He has requested that no unofficial visit logs be posted without his prior consent, so for all of you out there running other EverQuest sites, please respect his request. This is exactly what Brad stated.

<Aradune> I'd appreciate if people ask me before posting unedited logs of me appearing here.
<Aradune> I don't mind typically, but I'd prefer to see edited logs

Brad revealed in this short visit that he is working late tonight and that the EQ team has completed the database separation for the first few phases of the beta. What this means is that they have finish going through the beta applications and put the applicants into separate databases which will probably be categorized by beta phases. Thus all we can hope now is that our own application is sitting in the phase 1 closed beta. Keep your fingers crossed.SeVeN
