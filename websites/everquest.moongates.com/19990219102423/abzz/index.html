Hello everyone, and welcome to the brand spankin' new Dear Abzz advice column. My name is Abzzcesse, and I've been invited to help out the good readers of this site by lending an ear to the problems they experience, and try to espouse some well-thought-out wisdom concerning said difficulties. If I can't manage that, I'm supposed to be a smart aleck.

Assisting me in this new endeavor will be my dear, dear twin brother Abzzstain. I will be consulting him often for his views on varying topics. Between the two of us, we should be able to spout nonsense on just about anything.

Why are we doing this, you may ask? Well, somewhere along the line folks started listening to twins giving advice. Guess we're the natural evolution of that trend. So here we go.Abzzcesse
