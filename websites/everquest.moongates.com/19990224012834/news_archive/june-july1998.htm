July 14 1998

3:50am EST
The new screenshots that were released yesterday can be found on the Official Site. SeVeN
July 2 1998

2:45am EST
The log of yesterday's chat is now edited and posted. The piece of information that Brad revealed in the chat that might most interest those who couldn't make it there is that the beta has already began. Phase 0.5 started when E3 ended. 40 people are on it right now. Phase 1 will start in 2 weeks. Phase 2 will began when they are satisfied with the results that phase 1 produces. Phase 3 will be the first non-NDA phase which means that you can talk all you want about the game in phase 3. SeVeN

June 22 1998
