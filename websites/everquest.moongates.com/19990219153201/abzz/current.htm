Dear Abzz,

All of my friends and I are in love with you , what should we do?

Signed
-Never Knew Love like This Before
Dear Never,

Cesse’s take: Aaah, unrequited love. It’s one of the hardest trials of the human emotional experience. But it doesn’t have to be all pain and sorrow. Make this a learning experience. Write down all the things you like about your unrequited love, and make a similar list of any others you may have had. Combine all these wonderful aspects and promise yourself that THE perfect mate will have ALL those things you value most. It might not help you get that one particular person, but it’ll help you recognize when your dream girl comes along.
