Aegeis of Life

Many Thanks to Snowxan Darkbane for getting me the Quest information.

Chapter 1 : Searching the lands, acquiring fame, fortune and might, you wander the streets of Qeynos.  You are aware of a Lord Grimrot, wandering the Plains of Karana, killing people and using great magics to transform himself, you know he must be stopped and have gone into Qeynos to seek more information.  You find yourself at the "UFO" and decide to visit (Paladin).  He seems troubled when you find him.   You ask him of Lord Grimrot and he begins...

Chapter 2 : He tells you of Grimrot's evil deeds and how he must be destroyed.  Lord Grimrot has 2 forms.  Even though you may kill his human self, which has been done before, he comes back and wanders the land as a skeleton!  You must destroy Grimrot in both of his forms and return his decayed heart, and a scythe(NO DROP).  Once you return these items, the people of Qeynos will gift you with a symbol of your Goodness!  The Aegeis of Life shall be yours.

Chapter 3 : You set out from Qeynos, the citizens granting you gifts of flowers and food.  You wander a day and night through the Western Plains and through the Northern Plains.  You finally reach the river and find the great stone bridge that will bring you to the Southern Plains of Karana.  Guards at the stone bridge tell you of Lord Grimrot and know he appears near them every once in a while, casting magics on the Guards and scaring them to death.  You cross the bridge and climb a hill to the west, watching the lands, searching for Lord Grimrot.

Chapter 4 : After what seems AGES, you notice Lord Grimrot lurking along the shores, kneeling along the waters and fishing.  You approach Lord Grimrot and draw your Blade.  Lord Grimrot stands, drops his pole and unsheaths a great Scythe!  "A pity one such as thee shall fall to thy doom!" And with that battle ensues!  A long and hardened fight, Lord Grimrot finally falls to a blow that shatters his Scythe and kills him outright.  You pull the Scythe from his deadened hands and sit down to nurse your wounds.  A short time later you look to the shore and find that Lord Grimrot's corpse is gone! 

Chapter 5 : You make camp for the night to rest up before continuing your search.  Late that night, you hear a clicking in the darkness and stand up, pulling your blade from its sheath.  You begin to smell a vile, putrid stench.  As you peer into the trees, a skeleton, dressed in Lord Grimrot's jacket slinks towards you.  You can see his heart, black and shrunken, dripping a blackened juice that oozes his spirit and soul.  You call upon the might of Marr and Ward his attacks, swinging Ghoulbane and bashing with your Shield.  The skeleton puts up a good fight, but in the end, no Undead can truly harm you!  You pull the skeletons heart out from its broken ribcage and watch it disintegrate before your eyes.   You stumble into the Guard Towers and sleep the rest of the night.

Chapter 6 : The Guards make you a hearty breakfast in the morning and set out for Qeynos the moment you are ready.  You arrive back in Qeynos and news travelled fast ahead of you.  (Paladin) was waiting for your arrival.  You hand him the Heart and Scythe of Lord Grimrot, triumphant and pleased.  (Paladin) hands you a Golden Encrusted Shield, naming it the Aegeis of Life.  You strap the Shield to your back and thank him profusely.  Wassail!

 

Notes : The faction requirements for this Quest are right off the maps.  Even at amiable faction, if you are not maxed out with faction, you will have to find and kill Grimrot all over again.  There are a number of ways to restore faction.  Here is Snowxan's comments and what he did to gain the proper faction:

The only way I found you can get a reasonable amount of faction (and a nice percentage of loot, i made about 150pp in one run) is the bandit sash quest. I fill up 5 full backpacks about 12 times before my faction was amiable with the Temple of Life... and I THOUGHT that I could turn in the scythe and heart to the head paladin in Qeynos... I was WRONG! Then I had to go out and kill Grimrot again with a few friends of mine. This takes a LONG time to complete, but well worth it.
