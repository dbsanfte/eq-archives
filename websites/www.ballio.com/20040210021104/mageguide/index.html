Compatibility

This site uses CSS Level 2 to control the layout and colors.  Why?  I can't draw graphics.  I've tried.  Honest I have.  I suck at it.  And, even if I could draw, this site is not commercial.  The bandwidth is paid for by me and my wife (who doesn't even play the game)!

CSS2 is text -- it's something I can handle both visually and in terms of bandwidth.  If your browser doesn't support CSS2, this site will look like crap.  Upgrade your browser!  The latest versions of IE, Navigator and Opera all support CSS2.
