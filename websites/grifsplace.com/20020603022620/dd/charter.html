Charter of the Dragon

The Dragon Dynasty is engaged in the colonization of Norrath in the EverQuest game. True to form, all we survey is our domain. The main purpose of the Dynasty's members should be to have fun, take pride in their guild, and work together to make it flourish. The Dragon Dynasty is a community of people who have joined together for this reason. We are friends and family who will respect each other and treat one another fairly while following the command structure of the Dynasty.
