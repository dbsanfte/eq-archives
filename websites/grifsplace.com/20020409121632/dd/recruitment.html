Question: "What do I get out of it?"

Answer: The benefits you receive come from the other members of the Dynasty.

Protection
Companionship
Friendship
Trust
Honesty

These are just a few of the benefits you will receive. Many others are just as important, but there are too many to name. Suffice it to say, we are a family.  We help those who help others.  You will find that the more help you offer, the more likely you will find others in the Dynasty helping you.

Do not expect to be given a fixed amount of money or items when requested, you will be given what can be spared by your friends. This generosity is a two way street and you will be called upon to provide when you are able as well.

Do not expect to be power leveled.

Do not expect to get level 50+ items at level 15.

Do not expect free handouts with out putting any effort into making yourself part of this family.

 

Question: "What will I have to do as an applicant?"

Answer: The following criteria must be met in order to be considered for membership in into The Dragon Dynasty.

* Obtain Sponsorship from two current and active guild members
* Submit an application for membership
* Login to The Dynasty Forum and make your self known to the Membership
* Learn the Code of Honor and adhere to it.
* introduce yourself to all available members of the Dynasty. One way to do this is to take advantage of
  our message board system. You must register with us in order to post.
* Get to know us. The guild is about friendship and adventure. You will find that we are actually a nice
   bunch of folks.
* Have fun! The game should be about having fun.

In order to become a full member you have to work at it everyday and when finally accepted, then the true work will begin. Every member of the Dynasty contributes to the overall health of the guild. Only through our combined work can the Dynasty truly prosper for all.  The guild is not only about loot and adventure. It is about loyalty, friendship and trust.  These are the true rewards.

 

Sponsorship and What It Means

Any "active" Dynasty member in good standing can sponsor someone for membership into the guild.
When considering sponsorship, The Council will evaluate potential guild members based on the following:

* How do you conduct yourself when playing your character?
* Can you make a contribution to our guild as a member?
* Are you going to give our guild a good or bad name?
* How well do you do in grouping situations?
* How well do you do in solo situations?

 

Sponsoring a Member

Please remember that all members represent The Dynasty through deeds and actions. The person sponsored today will reflect back on the guild as a whole.

Please take care when offering to sponsor someone.

Please also keep in mind the following when evaluating someone for membership into The Dragon Dynasty:

* Maturity
* Helpfulness
* Dependability
* Loyalty

Also.. .please post a message on the message board stating your sponsorship. It is also your duty to see that your recruit gets the web address and makes themselves known on the message boards as well.

 

The Dragon Dynasty has a very strong moral code. We only allow entrance to those who share similar beliefs and respect the meaning behind the words Honor, Honesty& Friendship.

The Dragon Dynasty believes that membership is a privilege and honor. By submitting your application you agree to acknowledge and follow the Code of Honor. You also agree to take an active and productive role in the guild. You will join member hunts, and come on to channel as you enter the game and let the members know you are in the game. Only one "toon" is considered your "main", however, your alternate "toons" are welcome on guild chat as long as you identify yourself ahead of time. Your Dynasty member "toon" may not belong to any other guild/clan/order/empire etc...in the EverQuest realm (The Rathe server). You also agree to keep the guild updated as to your Email address or other means of communication. This will not be made public unless you authorize the posting on the board.
