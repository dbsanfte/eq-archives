We are acively recruiting...

We are currently revising and updating our recruiting methods.  As a rule, we will never recruit a guilded player.  If a person guilded in a guild other than Noctus Erus, we will explain that the participation is in no way a recruiting effort.  If raid invites would like to join Noctus Erus, they must raid with us unguilded for a while before consideration for membership. 

 
Recruiting changes (May 1, 2002)

In an attempt to better manage our recruiting and membership additions, we will be utilizing special web tools that will allow us to better view current membership status and goals.  We have needed classes that we are currently lacking.  We are also in the midst of setting deadlines and goals for progress in the guild.  We are working on a five-month plan that will see us in certain zones taking certain MoBs.  We are also looking to the next six to ten months and evaluating where we would like to be and what recruits we need in order to reach those goals.

This is an exciting time in the life of Noctus Erus.  We have a solid plan and realistic goals.  We are confident in the outlook for the guild.  If you wish to contribute to the excitement, please talk to one of our officers on-line.  We would love to chat with you.  No /cyber please, except to Healier!
