rules of engagement

 

 

 

General:


- Every action you make as a guild member is a reflection of the guild. While you wear the guild tag, you represent the entire guild. You should show respect to all players by spoken word or action. If a problem arises that cannot be resolved in a civilized manner, you are expected to consult an officer.
- Our guild does not train, kill steal, harass, belittle or taunt fellow members or other players.
- We do not invoke the PnP (Play Nice Policy) under any circumstances.
- Respect you guild officers and fellow members. We are your support, strength and family. They should come first in all things, whenever possible.

Communication:


- Guild chat abuse will not be tolerated in any form. If a confrontation between two guildies occurs, you are expected to act responsibly and work your differences out in direct tells. If this cannot be accomplished for whatever reason, consult an officer. Under no circumstances should you bring an argument into guild chat or resort to abusive language.

Guild Tag:


- You are expected to keep your guild tag on at all times. If you find it necessary to hide your guild tag from the rest of Norrath and your guild, please rethink your commitment to Noctus Erus. (Note the difference between /anon and /role. The first renders you totally anonymous, stripping everything including your guild tag, while /role allows you to hide your level and class while retaining your guild tag.)

Raids:


- Only officers are to view or loot mobs, unless you are specifically instructed to do so by an officer. They will call the loot and begin the bidding process.
- You are expected to follow the raid leader's directions on a raid. If you choose not to follow directions or become disruptive, then you will be asked to leave.

Any violation of the above rules can result in immediate dismissal from the guild or a probation period. The punishment will be determined by the severity of the action and will be discussed by the officers.

Leaving and Re-joining the Guild:


- In the event you choose to leave the guild for any reason, the following must occur for you to be allowed to rejoin.
- First, the officers will determine whether a vote will be called on the ex-member. If the officers decide that a vote will not be allowed, then the ex-member will not be allowed to re-join.
- Second, if the officers approve to vote on the ex-member, then a vote will be held and all guild members on at that time will have an opportunity to vote. A majority decision must be obtained for re-joining.
- Lastly, once a ex-member is voted back into the guild, they will lose 1/2 their prior Dkp balance.

DKP:


- Dkp is updated by Healier (rottloverr@hotmail.com) from files sent to him by several members. For this reason your Dkp balance on the website will never be current.
- Dkp will be updated two to three times weekly and the date it was updated will appear at the top of the page.
- During any bidding for loot, the Dkp balances on the website are the bible. You are not allowed to include any raids you have attended from the time the Dkp was last updated to the present bidding. You are also responsible for subtracting any Dkp for items you have won since the last update from your total. Under NO circumstances should you ever have a negative Dkp balance.
- No bids will be accepted for alts for any reason. Even if the item is no drop and no one wants.
- Any items won by bidding Dkp are not to be sold or traded without specific permission. If a member no longer needs an item, then it should be handed back into an officer and another bidding will take place. No Dkp will be given for handing in an item. You should just take pride in knowing you are helping make your fellow guild members and our guild as a whole stronger.
- You are required to keep your own list of raids you have attended with date and mob. You will be asked to e-mail this information in the event you Dkp balance becomes incorrect.
