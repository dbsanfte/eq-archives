noctus spotlight

 

 

 

Hmm. A spotlight on me?

 

Game Play:  I treat EQ as a hobby.  It is more than just a game.  Although EverQuest appears on the shelves of a computer store mixed in with computer "games", I look at my time on-line as a hobby.  Because of that, 90% of the time I am on I treat my play style seriously.  I always try to remember there are real people behind those images and I try to act accordingly.  I take pride in my skills as a cleric primarily, but also in any other class I decide to play.  I learn what needs to be known to play that class with all the skill that I can.

 

Real Life:  In real life I work at General Electric and am the Marketing Director for one of their electronics divisions.  I have worked at GE for about 7 months.  Prior to that I was at Sony and was an Assistant Director in their on-line marketing.  I have a Bachelor's Degree from the University of Illinois and a Master's Degree from Northwestern University.  I am married and about to have my first children (twins, 1 boy and 1 girl).

 

Guild:  I love my guild, Noctus Erus.  I love logging in and seeing the chat fill my screen.  I am proud of our accomplishments and do all that I can as an officer to take care of our guild members.  I would say that my role as an officer is to promote the goals of each member and to be the sounding board when things go wrong.  I try to encourage everyone to do well and to think positive.  I treat our members as family.  Because of that, I take any attacks on them as an attack on me personally.  They are my friends.

 

Goals:  My in-game goals revolve around the well being of others.  I could care less about loot if it means sacrificing relationships.  I will never let loot get in the way of having fun and being challenged.  I want my guild to succeed as a unit.  I want each member to get as much out of the guild as they put in.  Personally, I would like to simply increase my in-game skills and increase my alternate experience points to GOD-like level.

 

I would like to add a not of thanks to several people...

 

Gillianna, Carnivore, Maximo, Lenorae, Draksus, Drakbien, Healier, Kalot, Dloc, Raene, Finnien, Gankak, Essej, Fitz, Mystykal... I know there are more.  You guys have been an inspiration to me and many of you have helped me along.  I want to thank the guilds I have been involved in, including AoA, SoV, and NE.  I also want to thank KEG for all their work and time put into Esmeralda's Epic and other old gear I still have.
