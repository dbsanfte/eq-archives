Membership Joining
1. MUST BE 58+ to join but can raid with us if you are 55+.
2. Jboots/innate run 3.
3. A insta cast right click item (example shrunken goblin skull earing), for buffing reasons.  This is used to compensate for dispelling MoBs like Western Wastes dragons.
4. Resists in CR FR MR 100+ at same time with resist gear equiped.
5. Zone knowledge , or if not go to eqatlas.com and print out some maps.
6. Currently unguilded and applying for no other guilds.
7. As we are currently starting to lessen recruits we will be asking you to hang out and come to events that we are doing, so you can get a feel for us and get to know us before we can vote on your application and/or approval to Noctus Erus. If you dont know what we're doing simply send Esmeralda, Healier, Lenorae, or Drakbien a tell and ask.

We also would like to know:

1. How many hours a day do you play? weekday? weekend?
2. Do you have an alt at least level 25+?
3. What guilds have you raided with before or have been a member in?
4. What velious faction are you on? (We are ally to Giants as a guild.)
5. Are you willing to learn through trial and error?
6. How can Noctus Erus benefit from having you in our guild?
7. What do you hope to accomplish personally if you are invited to join Noctus Erus?

8. What is your current epic status?
Membership Commitments

As a member in Noctus Erus, you commit to the following.

 

1. Putting the guild as a whole above your individual needs.

2. Obedience to the guild leadership and rules of the guild.

3. Setting personal goals and allowing the guild as a whole to help you accomplish them.

4. Consistantly showing up for raids and being on time and serious about the mission of the event.

5. Actively participating in guild discussions on our message board.

6. Honesty.

7. Maturity.

8. Talking to officers about any complaints or issues that you might have before quitting, spreading rumors, or slandering another member.

9. NEVER respond to attacks on our guild or membership openly.  Allowing the guild officers to represent the guild is extremely important.

10. NEVER openly talking bad about current or ex-members in any circumstance.
