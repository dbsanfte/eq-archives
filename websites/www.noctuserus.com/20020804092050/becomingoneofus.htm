becoming noctus erus

 

 

 
How we vote on invites:
When all requirements are met and an invite has attended 5+ raids we will take a vote. One negative vote from NE member against the invite will reject your application. We don’t expect everyone to get in. The rules for recruiting are strict to keep certain people out of the guild. Please do not bother applying if:
- you have bad name for kills stealing, ninja-looting, flaming, etc
- you are unable to provide guild with the information we require
- you are joining in hopes of uber loots for yourself and your alts
We will freeze inactive applications. if you chose to apply you have to attend raids as all NE members do. It’s understandable to miss one or two, but being online regularly and not raiding while having an active application with us is unacceptable. If you are not a team player you probably just don’t belong with us.

To raid with us you need to be:
- Level 55+
- Resist gear: 100+ Cold/ Fire/ Magic UNBUFFED resist in the same time.
- Instant buff item (jboots, telescope, Shrunken gob earring etc) Don't underestimate this requirement, for the named dragons we kill with AE dispel its very important you could save your "real" buffs by instant-procing some buff spell.

To join:
- Level 58+
- Resist gear: 120+ Cold/ Fire/ Magic UNBUFFED resist in the same time. Must be able to obltain 120+ PR and DR if needed.
- Instant buff item (jboots, telescope, shrunken gob earring etc)
- Have been unguided for at least 2 weeks
- Currently not applying to other guilds.
- AA Run3, jboots or ability to cast sow, soe, selo, etc
- Ability to cast invisibility or having invisibility potion always ready.
- 150+ Swimming and EB item
- Participation in NE raids. You need to attend 5+ raids until we vote.
- Have fun!

In your application please include following information:
- Name, Class and level of your main character
- Profile at www.magelo.com detailing your current equipment, if you dont have one yet, plese set it up now
- Any alt's you have ( names, classes, levels )
- Why did you chose Noctus Erus?
- List any people you know in the guild.
- What are you trying to accomplish by joining?
- How can guild benefit from you joining?
- List the guilds you were a member of and reasons for leaving
- What guilds have you raided with?
- List large achievements such as epic, rare spells, high trade skills, etc
- What keys do you have?
- What Velious faction are you on ?
- What time zone do you live in?
- When do you usually play? How many hours a week?
- Are you willing to die over and over again, and move towards our goal without frustration and/or complaining in guild chat?
- Will you make fun of gnomes ? :-)

More Information about the guild:


Faction:
We are on Kael faction and most of us KoS to Dragons and Dorfs. We do not require you to adjust your current Velious faction, however we do expect to see you on every raid even if its against your current faction, mages are the only once that will not take faction hits on such raids unless they chose to. However you must have ally faction to Kael in oder to bid on any ToV armor.

Loot:
We use DKP (Dragon Kill Points) loot system as it is the most fair of all. You earn you DKP on raids (most named mobs have DKP value assigned to them). And later when loot has been announced and you are present on the raid and could benefit from dropped item you can bid your DKP on it.

Raids:
We raid almost every day, and members are requared to atend them if they are online. It is posible that you will find it very hard to find time for exp, but thats what it takes. If you are online during the raid (/anon or playing an alt) and doing something on your own without making arangements with officers, you will be charged a fine of DKP or simply removed from the guild. Its simple - when we raid, we need everyone.
