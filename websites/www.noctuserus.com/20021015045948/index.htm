So we were a bit bored on Friday night and were deciding what do do.  Someone came up with the idea of Fear and Cazic Thule.  We headed there, got Carnivore his epic and he gated back just in time for the pull.  It was our first time doing CT and he dropped handily.  The only ones left standing when the big guy fell was a cleric, a rogue and a bunch of Paladins.  Who says Paladins aren't worth their weight in gold?

 

 

Tunare was on our wish list.  The members kept begging and begging to do Tunare.  So, the officers gave in and the call rang out.  We came, we saw, we conquered.  A little tidbit of info...  There were NO deaths on this kill.  Another first for Noctus Erus, the guild everyone loves to hate!

 

Grats to Draksus on Golden Leaf Earring, Silverleafe on Essence of Nature, Anling and Mhayla on Sap Encrusted Branch, Velthak on Berserker's BP, and finally, Banesong on Melodic BP.

Eashen of the Sky < Noctus Erus.  This was our first attempt at a real named Mob in N ToV and it went without a hitch.  Just beyond this location lies the rest of our goals...  This is just the beginning...

 

Sontalak has been a pain in the butt for us.  We have to keep sneaking by him to get to ToV so we decided to get him out of the way once and for all.  Some how I feel that we will meet again, and again, the same fate will befall him.

We want to quickly throw out a giant THANK YOU to all of our friends and extended family who continue to support us and help us as we keep on growing.  We would be remiss without acknowledging all the people who have helped us along the way. Without your help and care, we would not be where we are today. To all of our friends, THANK YOU!
