Noctus Erus

Membership requirements:

 

Raid requirements:

 

Level 59 to raid for all classes except for Clerics and Mages who can be 58.

All resists must be hardware enhanced to 100+ for at least 4 statistics.

Reading and signing off on guild initiate rules of engagement.

 

Membership requirements:

 

Level 60 to become a member for all classes except Clerics and Mages who can be 58.

All resists must be hardware enhanced to 100+ for at least 4 statistics.

One full month of raiding with 30 FULL raids minimum to be considered an active applicant.

Reading and signing off on the guild rules of engagement and guild conduct policy.

Reading and signing off on the guild disciplinary policy.

 

Members will be subject to an interview by an officer where you will be asked about your history, play style, previous guilds, and references.  We may contact references as needed to verify information as well as to ask witness of credibility.  These references cannot be an initiate of Noctus Erus either currently or in the past.  The references must include at least one member in good standing of Noctus Erus.

 

Once invited into the guild, you will be placed on a 30 day probation period in which you will fully comply with all of the rules and regulations of the guild.  The only difference between a probationary initiate and a full member in good standing is that you will receive no warnings or hearings regarding any disciplinary issues that arise contrary to the policies you have signed.  You are eligible for immediate expulsion while on initiate probation.

 

After 20 FULL raids, the membership will be asked to vote.  No initiate will be told who has voted for or against you.  An officer will bestow the results to you as the time comes.  There might be instances where you must raid longer than 20 FULL raids.  This in no way inhibits your chances of membership.  As we grow, some members require more time to get to know a potential member.  Think of this as your opportunity to continue to meet guild members and get to know us a little bit more.

 

If you have any questions, please email Esmeralda and I will confidentially respond as soon as I can.  We are a DKP (Dragon Kill Points) guild and thus will be eligible to earn DKP after 5 FULL raids with us.  You will be able to bid on loot in accordance with our DKP guidelines which you will receive in your initiate packet.

 

Noctus Erus guild leader is Lenorae.
