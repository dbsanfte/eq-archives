In Loving Memory

This page was created to honor a Soldier we lost in our journeys.
May her spirit carry on in our memories.

Knight-Errant Kilandra MacLeod, Scribe
SoF member January 2000 - December 2000

Kilandra was a kind, dedicated, and loyal member of SoF from the day she joined our ranks. Her contributions to the guild as Scribe were very well known. She was a good friend to everyone and will be dearly missed.

In December of 2000, shortly after she chose to leave EverQuest to pursue her Real Life goals, we found out that she had died tragically in a car accident.

The following is the transcript of the memorial service held in-game. The sheer number of people who attended, both SoF and others, is a testament to her kind and generous spirit.

The following is a captured log of the event and has been formatted to the best of this author's ability to preserve the event. It is quite long.
