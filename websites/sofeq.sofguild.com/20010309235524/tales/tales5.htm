10-11-1999

Of Giants, Dragons, and Bravery of Men

 written by Kellen Caldorien

The scorching sun and the lava heat permeating from the ground of the Lavastorm mountains did nothing to spoil the mood of the stalwart adventurers calling themselves the Elite Legione. Fresh from a victorius slaying of the foul dragon beast Lord Nagafen, everyone was in high spirits as they prepare for a journey to the Northlands to invade the lair of the infamous ice dragon Lady Vox.

I was one of the few people invited to join the forthcoming adventure.  As usual, Ceinwen the druidess was on my side and we were both excited and nervous about the events about to unfold.   In the history of our world (ooc: the Xegony server), Lady Vox has never been defeated and the multitude of attempts to slay her have all ended in death and failure.  That night, everyone hope the result will be different.

I flipped through my songbook back and forth, thinking what songs will be most useful.  I try to predict what the battle would be like.  Gathering knowledge from stories brought back by survivors of previous expeditions, I have a few ideas of what monsters we are going to face and what to expect when fighting the dragon herself.  I remembered something about pit traps in the dungeon where you can be blown into by icy terrors.  I also heard something about Dragon Fear, a roar so dreadful, it takes away the courage from an  adventurers heart causing you to run away and possibly into more danger and ultimately death.   But the most fearsome thing I have heard about Lady Vox is her Ice Breath that can kill an adventurer instantly.   I was sweating as I strive to memorize the songs I've chosen to aid me and my group in the upcoming battles.

I close my song book and try to contain my anxiety.  I look at the band of adventurers around me and I see them drinking and joking around. "By the gods, aren't they nervous at all?"   I think to myself. "Well I suppose they aren't, being experienced dragonslayers and all, we should be fine, shouldn't we?".   A gruff warrior handed me a bottle of Ol'Tujim Fierce Brew,  "Here lad, this will calm ye down!", he bellowed.   "Don't ye worry! We shall be feasting by Vox's body by the night is over!!" he assured me and everyone else as he went around handing everyone some of that brew.

I took the bottle of Fierce Brew to my mouth and drank heavily.   I felt my throat on fire and felt my muscles tense and bulge.  No wonder this is such a popular pre-battle drink!  My uneasiness was gone and I am eager to get going.   I stood up and look up in the heavens and left my fate to the gods. "By your will oh Holy Mother Tunare!  I shall live or die this night, but please give me the courage so that my songs will not fail my companions in this mightiest battle of my life!!"  Then the call to start the journey was made.   The wizards and druids called forth the magics that instantly transported the group halfway across the continent.    

We appeared at the Northern Plains of Karanas.  I took out my battle drums and sang a steady beat to accelerate our journey by foot.  I can feel the wind in my face as we ran across the plains, swifter than any creature in Norrath!   Running west we arrived in Qeynos Hills in no time, and a quick transit through Blackburrow we reached the edge of the Everfrost plains, the frigid lands of the North.   We waited for a few others to catch up (too bad they don't have a bard and have to rely on Spirit of Wolf to increase their speed hehe), before we made our way east into the Caverns of Permafrost.   When we got there, we saw that the others are already amusing themselves beating up on the Ice Giants that guards the entrance.  We joined in on the fun while we waited some more for the rest of the groups to arrive...

Finally, the leader of the Elite Legione shouted "IT IS TIME!   EVERYONE GO IN!! GO IN!!"  Saying another quick prayer, I drew out my weapon and entered the dungeon of ice.  The ice goblins guarding the hallways were no match for the group as they were quickly dispatched.  I nary saw a goblin alive and just followed a trail of goblin blood leading to the room where the Ice Giant Diplomat waits.

His resistance was met with a rain of swords and burst of fire magic that ended his life with a quick thud on the icy ground.   The next room, there were 2 ice giants,  but the group was too powerful and again the ice giants were quickly slain.   Someone shouted "Okay folks, that's the easy part, rest and meditate and in a few minutes we will be moving on".   Time elapsed and everyone was ready to go.  Another call was made to start walking deep into the dungeon.  We were warned not to stray from the path being taken for danger lurks in every corner.

As we walk along the dark hallways, chilled air coming out of our nostrils,  I was wondering why the place was so quiet.  Then I heard some commotion up by the front of the line!  An Icy Terror has attacked the group!  I heard a yell  "Backs to the wall!!   Backs to the wall!"   I faced the Terror and saw a cone-shaped like creature with deadly tentacles all over its body.   I felt a massive force hit my chest as I slammed against the wall knocking the air out of my lungs.   That force would've knocked me hundred yards back to the hallways we just passed had I not turned aside in time!!   Breathing deep and getting back on my feet, I joined the fray singing a battle song that make muscles bulge with strength and feet agile as a cat.   Every now and then I would see someone being blown away from the battle as the Icy Terror was putting up a good fight.  In the end however, the Icy Terror has met a similar fate like the Ice Giant Diplomat before him.

We stopped at a corner not far from where Vox's lair is.  The order was made to rest and meditate.  Everyone was also told to use any buffing spells available for the next fight will be against 3 giants, one of which is the priest Nafegen, and the fight after that will be Vox herself.   The plan was to lure the 3 giants that guard the entrance to Vox's lair, and kill them in the corner we were in, for to fight Vox with her guards is not only foolish, but altogether fatal.  If Vox takes notice, then the order was to immediately evacuate the area.  Sounds like a good competent plan.  I knew that this group can definitely handle 3 Ice Giants.

However, no matter what kind of plan you devise, some things can go wrong and tonight was no exception...

The monk Shaolin was given the task to lure the 3 giants to the group waiting to ambush.  When every was all buffed and ready, he was given the order to pull.  The ground shook as I saw 3 giants running towards us.  The priest of Nafegen, an ice giant magi, and an ice giant, but wait!!!!   NO!  There's another Icy Terror hot on the monk's trail!!  Hmmm…no matter this is not a disaster.   Everyone focused on killing the priest first for he was deemed to be the most poweful of the lot.  Jixl and Ganthin, their battle commanders, were amongst the first to draw blood and others turned to assist them.   I jumped in the battle singing Verses of Victory once more and attacked the priest.   The air smelled of smoke and fire as magics of flame and lighting filled the hall striking the giant where he stood.  The warriors, paladins and rangers hacking and slashing their way to through.  The rogues were swift and deadly with their backstabs and the healers kept vigil to see that everyone stays healthy enough to fight another.    

I felt another massive force hit me as I was thrown back a good distance but fortunately not into anything dangerous.  I tried to keep away from the Icy Terror everyone was ignoring and joined the attack on the priest again.   The priest was finally slain and a shout told everyone that the ice giant magi was next.  Gaelad, the enchanter was holding the other Ice Giant in check and the fight should've been easy.  But things started to go wrong…. When the magi was nearing its life's end it ran away and no one was able to stop it.   No one dared follow the magi into the depths of the dungeon and we just waited for it to come back, so we started to kill the remaining ice giant and icy terror.  But the ice giant magi had a trick up its sleeve.  He has summoning magic!!!  He has the power to teleport you from where you stand back to him!!  All of a sudden, we hear the magi's deep voice shouting "You will not evade me" calling us by names and summoned people to him one at a time!!  It was horrible seeing someone just disappear in front of your eyes and then suddenly hearing them scream from a distance!!   There was nothing you can do except pray that you don't get "chosen".

Nevertheless, the fate of the summoned ones was of less concern for the time being until the ice giants and icy terror were dispatched.  The group continued their attacks until the icy terror and ice giants were slain.  Then some of the people that were summoned were able to find their way back to the group but were not by themselves, but with more ice giants after them!!  The group geared for another onslaught of giants and I saw a few corpses laying around.  The previous fight did have a few casualties, and still a few missing ones.  A loud voice echoed from the halls  "You will not evade me, Kellen!" as my vision grew dark and I felt a tug as I was summoned by the magi.     My eyesight came back and I felt the fist of an ice giant pound on me.  A shock of ice slammed against me as I tried to evade another blow from another ice giant.  I was summoned to a place I know not!!  I didn't know where to go and I'm getting attacked by two ice giants and the magi!!   I sang a song of healing and intersperse it with a psalm of warmth to keep me alive long enough to escape.  Then another adventurer appeared near me…and then another!!  More and more people are being summoned and the ice giants started to attack them instead of me!   They started running towards a hallway and the ice giants ran after them.   I followed them knowing they are more familiar with the caverns and maybe they could lead me back to the group.  We round a corner and I saw flares of magic and heard the sounds of fighting!!  There they are!!  I went back to my group and sang my battle song once more.   More corpses are on the floor as the continued barrage of ice giants coming from everywhere is taking its toll.   There were only a few left fighting but they will not be daunted into submission.  Everyone came to fight a dragon and fight a dragon it will be, not lose from a bunch of ice giants.  The battle has to be ended but the magi has to be stopped first.   The magi was lured back to the group and this time it was not allowed to ran away which made things turn for the worse the last time around.   Finally, the magi was killed and all the remaining ice giants were slain as well.   Cheers for both victory and relief rang throughout the air.

I looked around the area, several corpses strewn around.  No one came out of the battle unscathed and everyone alive are now recuperating and healing their wounds.  The casters are meditating and the clerics are preparing to call forth their gods favor into resurrecting our slain companions.  I sat down wearily and sang a song of clarity, a peaceful hymn that clears the mind of the listener enabling them to gather energy for the next fight ahead... against an Ice Dragon.

This is it.  The fight against Lady Vox.  The band of weary adventurers summoned their courage and whatever is left of their strength.  There was no time to recast all the buffing magic, only the most important ones,  for to wait longer would mean that the ice giants will come back and the group can ill afford another fight of that magnitude.  

The plan was announced.   Shammy, the guildmaster of the Elite Legione, will go inside the lair with Invulnerability and absorb the initial onslaught of the dragon's ice breath.   The next ones to go in would be casters' pet followed by the warrior-types and then everyone else.   Under no circumstances will Vox be fought outside her lair.  If she gets out and everyone is not yet inside, the order was to evacuate.  

I started humming a psalm of mystic shielding and had a levitate spell cast on me.  The song is supposed to help prevent being affected by Dragon Fear and if I do get feared, I do not accidentally fall down a pit trap if I lose control of myself and started running.   Then I heard a shout that Shammy is going in!   Everyone held onto their weapons tightly and I heard the whispering of arcane words as the magic users prepare to send in their pets.

All of a sudden chill of fear filled the air!!  I felt my hair rise and bile well up in my throat.  It was the Dragon Fear!!!   The roar was faint and it grew louder and louder!!  Something is wrong!! Deadly wrong!!   People were running around and screaming madly!!  DRAGON FEAR!  I resisted it with the help of my song. There were those not fortunate enough and they fell into the bear pit traps. Those that can gate, gated out when they got control of themselves again.  Those that can't, had to fight their way up.  I looked up and saw her.  OH MY GOD!  IT'S LADY VOX!!  AND SHE'S OUT OF HER LAIR!! I didn't have time to start singing Psalm of Warmth when I felt the freezing winds of her breath.   Two people directly under her died instantly.  Others died when her claws rend them to bits and pieces.  A white dragon of ancient beauty, mercilessly slaughtering anyone who dares invade her lair.  It was a horrible (and yet awesome) sight to see!  

Remember the original order was to evacuate if she gets out, but only a few heeded it, for the deaths of the fallen will not go unavenged.   Those who recovered from the dragon fear shouted to everyone "GO INSIDE!!  GET HER INSIDE!!"  More bodies lay dead as the ranks were quickly decimated.  It was a sign of things to come.  Everyone ran inside her lair trying to lure the dragon back and quickly reformed to start the attack.

I saw the dragon go back to her lair and after receiving a few healing spells I quickly rushed inside her huge cavern room.   I ran towards the fray and attacked the dragon and tried to damage her as much as I could with my weapons.   Inside her lair, getting feared is not as dangerous as outside so I sang my psalm of warmth and cycled it with my battle song.    More and more people fell from her terrible claws (ooc: 205 damage each) and her ice breath was wreaking havoc.   NO!!! I screamed as I saw my friend, Calek collapse and die as her barraged of attacks concentrated on him.   I felt her sharp claws slashed through my armor and I writhe in pain as I saw her eyes gazed threateningly at me.   Thankfully a warrior caught her attention as he delivered a very powerful blow!   A bright flash of lighting and a huge ball of fire came streaking through the air and hit the dragon square in the chest.  She screamed in agony!!   Warriors, Paladin and Rangers were hitting her with their mighty weapons trying to slice through her frozen scale skin.   She was bleeding from more than a dozen wounds all over her body.  She was taking a lot of damage and we knew we had a chance of winning (ooc: She was down to about 40% health).  BUT THEN SHE HEALED HERSELF!!!

I briefly saw the faces of those people near me surprised and then turn into resignation as with our current state with just a handful remaining standing, defeat was certain.    A renewed Lady Vox roared once more driving fear into the hearts of the bravest of men.  

I was close to death.  I stepped back from the dragon and ran towards my wife who was ready to cast her healing magic when another wave of icy winds slammed into our backs.  Thankfully I was far enough not to bear the brunt of the damage as I saw more people die, if not from her breath, from her sharp talons.   We crawled out of her lair back to hallways where some people are meditating to gather enough energy to evacuate.   Then  I heard Lady Vox once more, roar with all her mighty splendor. "Begone! puny mortals!"   I could almost hear her mock us.   Shouts of "GATE! GATE!" were heard when the beast came out of her lair once again.  I told Ceinwen, "Succor now!!" but she says "I have not enough mana!"  I softly sang to her a song of clarity to ease her mind and ignore the chaos surrounding us.  I felt my life ebbing away as another frozen wind slammed us against the wall.  This is it…our life is at its end… I saw Vox rear her head around the corner fixing her icy glare upon me.  I close my eyes and submitted myself to my fate.  Then my vision grew dark and I lost consciousnes...

The warm sun and the chirping of birds woke me.  I slowly opened my eyes to see visages of gigantic treants stumbling around the druid stone structures in the Karanas.  I am alive!  I can't believe it!  Mumbling a quick prayer of thanks to the Mother of All, I stood up and saw Ceinwen severely injured, drained of magic but alive as well!!.  I took out my lute and sang a hymn of restoration and the bardic magic of song slowly closed our wounds and restored our health.

As I sang and strum my lute, I remembered our ill-fated journey, the mighty battles we fought, the joys of our victory and the agony of our final defeat.  I vowed that the bravery and audacity of the people who dared to challenge Lady Vox will not be left untold.

Some day, Lady Vox will fall, and on that day I hope to be in the midst of the final battle once more...

OOC:  This is a recount of a failed Lady Vox slaying attempt at the Xegony Server the night of October 8th as seen through the eyes of yours truly, Kellen.  Obviously I embellished it a bit and also did not include the fact that people died multiple times (especially those that bound themselves inside the dungeon to get back into action immediately) just because it doesn't make good for story telling hehehe… Hope you enjoyed it :)
