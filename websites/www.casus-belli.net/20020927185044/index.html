    An Update On The Past Weeks Events
      Posted By: Gandien on 08-23-02 @ 17:08:58  
      Late last week our daytime crew headed to PoG to clear for Tunare. It was a picture perfect kill that once again yielded nice rewards.

    Earlier this week we headed to the Deep to take on the Burrower Parasite. Everyone performed flawlessly and the fight went off without a single transition.

Excellant work by everyone and congratulations on another AoW kill!!
