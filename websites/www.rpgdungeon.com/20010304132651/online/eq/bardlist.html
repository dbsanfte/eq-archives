- May be played on wind instruments
- May be played on brass instruments

BARDIC SKILLS
LEVEL TITLE EFFECT
5 Percussion Your skill with percussion instruments such as the drum.
8  String Your skill with string instruments such as the lute or mandolin.
10 Dodge Your ability to dodge blows in combat.
11 Brass Your skill with brass instruments such as the horn.
12 Forage This allows you to forage for food while you're in the wilds. (Wood elves start with this ability.)
14 Wind Your skill with wind instuments such as the flute.
17 Dual Wield This skill allows you to equip two single handed weapons.
24 Safe Fall Allows you to fall from heights without taking full damage.
25 Hide This lets you hide from others mimicking invisibility. You will not be able to tell if this working.
26 Instill Doubt This can cause a target to run away.
30 Disarm Traps This skill lets you attempt to disarm traps.
35 Track This skill allows you to track others in your zone.
40 Pick Locks With the help of some lockpicks this allows you to pick locks.
