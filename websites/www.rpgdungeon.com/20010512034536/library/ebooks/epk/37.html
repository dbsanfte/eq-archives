Everfrost Peak

 

You've already tried force and the only result was a bruised shoulder. You'll never succeed in melting an enchanted ice door.

You decide to leave. Taking your axe out of your backpack, you start to cut footholds in the ice.

"Goodbye," chuckles the door. "Do drop in again."

It takes a long time but eventually you climb back up the snowy trail. You rest for a while to regain your strength and then start out toward your village.

You will have to tell you story to the Elders. Perhaps you will be able to try once more to reach beyond the Everfrost Peaks. Maybe there will still be time. . .
