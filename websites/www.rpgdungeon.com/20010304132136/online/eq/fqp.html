The ForeverQuest Project was born from the belief that Role-playing should be a consistent activity on all the EverQuest servers. The ForeverQuest Project teams are dedicated to bringing to life epic stories on the stage Verant has created. So we ask that for a while you leave behind all things mundane and prepare yourself to enter a world of love, intrigue, and magic...

Within him there was the power of creation... The power to take emptiness and form wholeness. From this power the nameless one took and created the foundation that all worlds are made of, stone. He then took this stone and created small pieces of stone with which he instilled the power to create as well.. He then cast these stones out among the void to create what we now know as the universe.. The nameless one also named these stones... Being
and the force that keeps the world alive, he named these stones Heartstones... Simpolyzing their role in the universe.. The stones then went out and created the worlds that are here today, and even some that have long been forgotten.. It was this moment that the actual term time came to be, for this was the beginning of the universe..
