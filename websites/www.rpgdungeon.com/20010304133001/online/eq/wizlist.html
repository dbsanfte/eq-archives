EverQuest
Wizard Spell
Guide



Spells are listed alphabetically by level. Since many later descriptions refer back to lower level spells, we have added bookmarks to help you trace spell lines.

Level 1
Frost Bolt The first in line of the bolt line of spells (direct damage spells). Although this will be your most damaging line of spells, you need to aim them, making it difficult to hit a running monster or monsters behind obstacles.
Minor Shielding The first wizard shielding spell. You can only cast it on yourself. It adds armor class (ac) and hit points (hps)
Numbing Cold An area effect spell that is centered around the person casting it. If you are fighting two or more monsters, using this is a better idea then trying to take them on one at a time with spells.
Sense Magic Tells you about magic items.
Shock Of Frost The shock line starts with this direct damage (DD) spell. Shock spells are guided, unlike bolts, which you need to aim.
Sphere Of Light A light source that you cannot drop, sell or trade.
Level 4
Fade A spell that teleports you to a spot somewhere near you. It is most commonly used as an escape tactic.
Gate Teleports you home to your bound point.
Glimpse This spell zooms your vision out into the distance for a relatively short time.
Ice Strike An area effect spell which damages all the monsters around you.
O'Kiels Radiation This spell can be cast on your character or anyone else. It makes a fire shield that damages all monsters that hit you. It can be very effective when used properly.
Root This spell stops your enemies dead in their tracks. It prevents them from moving for a period of time and allows you to hack away at them.
See Invisible This spell allows you to see invisible objects.
Shock Of Fire This is the second in the line of shock spells.
Level 8
Column Of Frost An area affect spell which centers around your target, making it possible for you to hit a group of monsters from a good distance away.
Eye Of Zomm Creates an eye that you can use to look around corners and to scout areas.
 Fingers Of Fire A fire area effect spell which centers around you and hits the monsters within a certain radius.
 Firebolt This spell is the second in the bolt line of spells. While aiming takes awhile to get used to, the damage is devastating.
 Lesser Shielding This is the second in the line of shielding spells.
 Shadow Step Basically an upgrade to fade, it does the same thing but takes you a farther distance and can be cast quicker.
 Shock Of Ice Shock of ice is next in line of the shock spells. Like the others, it is guided and does direct damage.
 Sense Summoned Points you to the nearest elemental.
 Level 12
 Bind Affinity This spell allows you to move your "home point." If you die or cast Gate, it will take you to this spot. You can bind yourself or another party member.
Cancel Magic This is a dispel magic spell of sorts. It takes all buffing/debuffing spells off you or a party member.
 Fire Storm Fire storm is a fire-based rain spell which can damage several monsters at once.
 Frost Spiral Of Al'Kabor This is an area effect spell that centers around the caster and hits several monsters.
 Gaze This spell is an enhanced version of the Glimpse spell. It allows you to see into the distance.
 Halo Of Light Summons a headband that is used as a strong light source.
 Resistant Skin Adds to your resistances towards several things.
 Shock Of Lightning Shock of lightning is a direct damage spell that follows the line of shock spells.
Level 16
Bind Sight This rather useless spell allows you to see through the eyes of the targeted being.
 Flame Shock This is a fire direct damage spell in the shock line of spells.
 Heat Sight Lets you see in the dark if you are a class without infravision.
 Invisibility This one makes the target invisible for a pretty short amount of time.
 Lightning Bolt This is an upgrade to the bolt line of spells. It can do a lot of damage, but once again, it needs to be aimed
Pillar Of Fire Pillar of fire is an upgraded rain spell and is fire based.
Project Lightning A good spell if you are jumped by multiple enemies. It is an area effect spell.
Shielding This is the third in the line of shielding spells. It adds to hit points, ac, and resistances.
Shield Skin A damage absorbing spell that can only be cast on yourself. It takes some of the damage that a monster does before wearing off.
Level 20
Elemental Shield This spell adds to your resistance to both cold and fire.
Enstill Enstill is a better root spell. It has a rather long duration.
Fire Spiral Of Al'Kabor This is a fire-based area effect spell.
Force Shock Force shock is an upgrade to the shock line of spells. It does a lot of damage and takes lots of mana.
Gate to Tox, North and Fay These spells allow you to gate to the location specified by the particular spell. For example, the gate to Tox will teleport you to the Tox Forest.
Sight An infravision spell that lasts for a long time.
Tishan's Clash A stun spell useful for stopping a caster from smashing you with spells.
Level 24
Cast Force An offensive spell that takes quite a bit of mana. I often don't find myself using due to the more damaging shock line of spells.
Gates To Cazic, Nek, Ro, Commonlands, and West Karana These are personal travel spells which will teleport you to one of the respective locations. See Gate for more information.
Frost Shock Cold based offensive spell that won't eat up too much mana.
Leather Skin Leather Skin will absorb a certain amount of damage that would otherwise hurt the caster.
Levitate This spell allows you to levitate. It is useful for traveling over water or lava. Just make sure you aren't in a dangerous spot when it wears off.
Lightning Storm This is a lightning based rain spell that does good damage and is pretty mana efficient.
Inferno Shock Direct damage offensive spell.
Major Shielding Major shielding is an upgrade to the wizard's shielding line of spells
Level 29

Energy Storm		 This is an area effect rain spell which features a good damage:mana ratio and also drains stamina.
Evacuate: North This spell sends you to a far away land in the North. The evacuate line of spells cast faster than the portal spells, but there is a chance that a party member will be left behind, which isn't usually a good thing.
Magnify This is an upgrade to the glimpse line of spells. It has a longer duration and zooms in farther.
Portals To Fay, North and Tox This is a spell that isn't used much when fleeing as evacuation spells due to a long casting time, but this version doesn't leave people behind as much.
Shock Spiral Of Al'Kabor This is an area effect spell.
Thunder Strike Thunder strike is a direct damage spell.
Level 34
Alter Plane: Hate This spell will teleport your party into the plane of Hate.
Circle Of Force This is an area effect spell.
Evacuate: Faydark Evacuates you to greater Faydark.
Greater Shielding This is the level an upgrade to the wizard's shielding line of spells.
Ice Shock Freeze your enemy in pain with this direct damage spell.
Lava Storm Lava storm is a fire based rain spell.
Nullify Magic This is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.
Portals To Cazic, Nektulos and Commons These are the group travel spells to the respective locations.
Steelskin Steelskin is a damage-absorbing spell like leather skin.
Thunderclap Thunderclap is a direct damage spell.
Level 39
Evacuate: Ro This evacuation spell will send your party on a trip into the desert of Ro.
Force Spiral Of Al'Kabor This spell is an area effect spell which centers around the caster.
Immobolize This is an upgrade to Root.
Lightning Strike Lightning strike is a direct damage offensive spell.
Portals To Commons, Ro, and West Group travel spells to their respective locations.
Shifting Sight The top of the line of the Sight spell for a wizard.
Level 44
Alter Plane: Sky This spell will open up a portal to the elemental plane of air.
Arch Shielding The last in the line of wizard shielding spells.
Conflaguration A nasty, powerful direct damage spell.
Diamondskin Diamond Skin is the last in the line of the absorb damage spells.
Elemental Armor Increases your resistances to fire and cold attacks.
Evacuate: Nek This is the nektulos forest version of the wizard's evacuation spells.
Force Strike Force strike is a powerful direct damage spell.
Frost Storm Frost storm is a powerful frost based rain spell.
Level 49
Evacuate: West This is the last in the line of wizard evacuation spells.
Ice Comet The most powerful spell in the game, delivering a whopping 1000+ damage.
Markar's Clash A stun spell which will stun your opponent long enough for several ice comets to hit them.
Supernova Supernova is one of the most powerful area effect spells.
Wrath Of Al'Kabor A very powerful area effect spell.
