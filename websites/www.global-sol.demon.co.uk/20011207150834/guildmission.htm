The Emissaries of Dread guild and all of its followers believe in uniting to fight for common goal. Times in Narrath can be tough and questing as one hero is a dangerous pastime. As a member of Emissaries of Dread you instantly receive the protection and help from other Emissaries of Dread members.

From time to time a hero may want to quest for a weapon, a spell or another item. Questing for such an item can be achieved quickly and more easily with members of the Emissaries of Dread with you. Emissaries of Dread and its members vow to help each other in various quests that may need to achieve.

The Emissaries of Dread guild are an active people. Raids on Planes, ToV and Dungeons within the Norrath realm are common. All Emissaries of Dread members are expected to join these raids at least once every two weeks - for this service they are rewarded.

Unlike other guilds Emissaries of Dread requires its members to group actively with each other when camping. This promotes fellowship and unity.
