The Emissaries of Dread guild are an active people. Raids on Planes, ToV and Dungeons within the Norrath realm are common. All Emissaries of Dread members are expected to join Friday, Saturday & Sunday raids each week. For these raid days members are awarded points which later they can use to bid for items. The guild has strict rules regarding official raids - if you are online then you must attend the raid. You do not have to attend the official raids if you are not able to logon - however you will not gain points for those missed official raids.

EoD believes in quality and not quantity. We strive to achieve more with less. We prefer our members to be the best that they can be. We therefore trial all new members for at least four or five raids to ensure they are dedicated and know their class.

Unlike other guilds Emissaries of Dread requires its members to group actively with each other when camping. This promotes fellowship and unity.

To Join EoD you should:
be level 60 or over.
have resistance gear which brings ure unbuffed figures to over 90
be able to make the official Friday & Saturday. 7pm GMT-2am GMT
be factionless. EoD Kills Dwarfs, Giants & Dragons.
