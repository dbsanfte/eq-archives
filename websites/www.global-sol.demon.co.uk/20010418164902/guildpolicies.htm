Member Policies
Each guild member must oblige all policies and rules as set out below. Should members fail to act in accordance with Emissaries of Dread policies the guild leader may issue a warning to that effect. If warnings are not observed then the guild leader reserves the right to remove that member from the guild without any further notice. Removal of the guild also includes removal of that members privileges to the guild website.

1.Guild members must participate in a guild raid at least once per month.
2.Guild members must participate in all guild wars. You will be notified in advance when a Guild War will begin, whom it is with and its duration.
3.Guild members must participate in at least one 'Guild Member Quest' per month. The Guild Website details such quests.
4.Guild members must abide by the 'Pro Guild Grouping' policy:
Whenever there are more than three members of Emissaries of Dread within the zone (which you are currently in at the time), if those members form a group and ask you to participate (by giving invite) you will be obliged to join (as long as that group will give sufficient experience and loot as would be gained outside of that group).
Please read the Emissaries of Dread FAQ on Pro Guild Grouping for more information.
5.New applications for membership of Emissaries of Dread will have the following procedure:
iApply formally: New Applicants must complete an application form from the Emissaries of Dread website.
iiLevel Check: Only players 45+ will be allowed to join (this excludes the founding 10 members)
iiiPolicy Agreement: Each new member must agree to the guild polcies as listed above.
ivMember Nominations: Each new member must have five nominations from existing guild members before the new applicant can proceed.
vNew Member Quest: Each new member must complete successfully a Quest given by the Guild Leader. The proceeds of such a quest (be it an item, platinum or spell) will be placed into the Guild Bank which can be later used for raid funds.
6.Guild members must agree not to be abusive (unless in jest) to one another or to a guild officer. If such an event occurs warnings will be given and if it persists those members breaking this rule will be removed from the guild.
7.Members must agree that all guild information (raids, quests, guild wars) remain confidential and not leaked/published to other persons outside of the guild.
8.Members login and password details to the Emissaries of Dread website are to remain confidential and must not be given to any other person.
9.Members are required to check the Guild website at least once per week to gain latest information on quests, raids and wars.
10.Emissaries of Dread does not promote mixed guild raids. Please see Guild Raids.

Officer Policies
All of the above polcies apply for officers and in addition:

1.An Officer position is given on the basis that he/she will accept a certain responsibility within the guild. This responsibility is handed out negotiated with the guild leader prior to becoming an officer. Should that officer fail to carry out their responsibility then they may have the officer status removed and become a regular member.
2.Officers are expected to help guild members more so than any other Emissaries of Dread member. They may be required to assist a player out of difficulty or to sort out a dispute.
3.Officers must not invite members into the guild on their own initiative. New applicants must follow the procedures stated in the policies guide.
4.Officers are required to set an example to other guild members and be pro-active in guild wars, grouping, raids and other guild aims.
