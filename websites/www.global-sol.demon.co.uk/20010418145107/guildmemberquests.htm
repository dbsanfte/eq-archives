Each week there will be an organised quest for a member of the guild. You are obliged under guild policy to assist in at least one member quest per month. Player quests may be FA Quests, Spell or general item quests. Questing time is limited to around 2 hours or so although members may assist longer. Quests selected for 'Member Quests' will generally be quick to run with advantages (xp, loot) for all where possible. FA quests of course may take more than 2 hours - these will be split among various days/weeks where required.

Members may request that a quest they would like to complete by filing in the appropriate form on the website. Quests will be scheduled in accordance to length in guild and urgency/requirement.
