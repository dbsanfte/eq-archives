Each week a raid will be planned. Raids will be decided by the guild officers and leader. The raid location and desired aim can however can be discussed and requests made via the Emissaries of Dread website. Should there be many requests for raids the Website can queue them.

It is a policy that to remain a guild member he/she must participate in at least one raid per month. Failure to participate will result in a warning and then later removal from the guild. Registration for a raid will occur via the Emissaries of Dread website. The list of levels, classes and details of the raid location and aim will all be detailed on the website. Raid loot is handed in the following manor:

1.Each guild member joins the Emissaries of Dread with 20 raid credits (RC).
2.Each time a guild member participates on an official raid they will receive an additional 5rc.
3.Whenever a valued item is found on a raid the following procedure will be adhered to: If there is no race/class witch matches the item criteria then the above rules apply to all members. Please note that a member may refuse to use their RC/participate in lottery for a particular item - and therefore the item will bypass them - they however retain the RC for another loot on this or another raid.

iThe Item is checked (not looted) and broadcasted out to other members (Class, Race & Stats).
iiAll members which match that Race & Class will be included in the RC check.
iiiEach member included will have their RC checked, should there be an overall highest RC rating amongst those members they will be automatically awarded that item.
ivIf two or more members have the same RC then that item will be subject to lotto. The lotto is made by the raid leader using /rand 1 n (where n is the number of people in the lottery). The item is assigned alphabetically in relation to the random number returned.
vOnce an item has been won that member's RC is reduced to 20 again. That member may still participate further items on that raid.
4.The Guild does not promote mixed guild raids. Where a raid a ran by Emissaries of Dread all of its rules and policies apply. Non guild members will not be included in loot. Should a mix guild raid occur and it is not organised by Emissaries of Dread officially the RC system does not apply - neither will it count towards a guild members RC score.
5.Raid disputes will be settled by either the Guild Leader, Officer or Raid Organiser in that order. Their decision on loot is final as long as it adheres to these policies.
