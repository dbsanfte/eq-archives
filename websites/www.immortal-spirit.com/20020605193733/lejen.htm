Lejen

Guild Leader

Level 38 Warrior

 

                                                 

 

Lejen was born the 4th in a litter of 9.  He enjoyed his young life amongst his brothers and sisters within the safe walls of Var Shal.  He grew fast and sadly he watched as his family diminished little by little.  His parents would die in battle with the Grimlings and so would many of his siblings.  The ones that did not battle died of rare diseases that inflicted them.  Yet somehow Lejen would survive.

As the long battles against the Grimlings began to slow, Lejen decided that he would journey afar to let his mind stray from all that he has lost.  He journeyed to the Lands of Norrtah where he met Cerryn, a human.  Cerryn would guide him and with this guidance he would learn that his luck in survival was not luck at all, but rather a divine calling.  He was immortal, as was Cerryn.  He could not die... of course his life could be stifled momentarily, but eventually his soul would return and he would rise again, to fight on.

So he decided he would find other like himself.  Those that could not die.  Together they would forge an empire of friends... they would become a family of immortals.  They would become Immortal Spirit.
