Welcome to the City of Heroes Chapter of Immortal Spirit!

Immortal Spirit is a gathering of gamers that have come to form a bond of friendship through the medium that we all enjoy. We are a casual group, whose principals come down to two major points: Fun and respect.

The fun part’s easy, in-game we group with each other, lend a helping hand when needed, and generally hang out. Out of game, we have a forum with which we can communicate, as well as a Ventrilo voice chat server that allows us to not only plan out our strategies more quickly, but it also allows us to get to know the identity behind the hero, as it were.

The respect part’s just as large. The Golden Rule still rings true, especially since we’re heroes and all.

Interested? That’s excellent! Check out or forum’s CoH section and post an application, we can’t wait to see ya!

Dirge, Leader of Immortal Spirit Supergroup
