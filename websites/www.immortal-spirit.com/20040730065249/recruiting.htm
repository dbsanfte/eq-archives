WANTS YOU!

 

Summary:

Immortal Spirit is a casual Super Group on the Infinity Server.   By casual we mean that members are not expected to play everyday or numerous hours per week.  The general idea is to enjoy the game and to enjoy it with friends whenever possible.  Immortal Spirit does stress the importance of team play however, after all what is the point of an MMOG if one is alone all the time.  We as a Super Group tend to try to help each other whenever possible even if it means sacrificing a good xp team or somethng similar.  Currently the majority of Immortal Spirit plays in the CDT and EST timezones.  Most play during the early evenings and late evenings.   As we are still building our number base of active players, we only average about 5-10 players online at peak hours.  Hopefully this will change with the addition of new members.

Immortal Spirit is RP friendly, although RP is not required it is welcome and enjoyed by many.  I t can be sometimes difficult with the existence of voice chat.  But plans are i n effect to create a RP voice chat server... for it is believed that true RPers can truly shine in this fashion.

Requirements:

Currently there are only a few requirements for membership.   As far as the game is concerned you must be at least Security Level 10 to join Immortal Spirit.  Real Life friends and family members of current members do not have to meet this requirement however.

Also a mature gaming attitude is required.  Granted humor and jest are always welcome, however immature attitudes and rude behavior and take away from the gaming experience and is not welcome.

Members are required to register on the Immortal Spirit message board, although posting is not required.  This is mainly to assist in maintaining the roster and to maintain a valid email address in case I need to contact you.  Keep in mind that the email address can be hidden so members may email you but it is done through the message board so the email address remains unknown.  The forum is for communication.  Members should communicate absences from game via the message board in the case of extended absences or shortened play time.  Members whose "Last online" date exceeds a 2 week period without notification will be removed from the Super Group.

Application:

Interested heroes must go to our forum at www.immortal-spirit.com/forum, register and fill out an application in our recruiting section.

Bonus:

Ventrilo- Immortal Spirit does use Ventrilo voice chat for online communication.  It is not required to have but is highly recommended.  Keep in mind that you can use Ventrilo to listen but you do not have to chat if you do not wish to.  Ventrilo can be downloaded at www.ventrilo.com It is a free download.  Setup instructions can be found at their website and additional instructions can be found on our Forum.

Bio Page- The webpage is slowly being built.  Eventually members will be allowed to have a bio page of their own hosted on our website.  This page will have an image of your hero, statistics, and history.  This page will be by request only, but it will become available eventually.
