Background:

Simon Cross was a renowned scientist specializing in energy and electrical emmissions.  Working for the Pargon City Police after the Rikti Invasion, Simon was developing a prototype power armor to assist the Police in combating the villains of Paragon City.  After a year of hard work and millions of dollars he had finished the protoype armor and was preparing to field test if for the police.  Unfortunately a competing agency who was also developing a prototype power armor decided to eliminate the competition...

On one fateful day, Simon was working late making last minute preparations on the armor.  Suddenly a loud boom echoed off the walls of his lab and the main doors collapsed.  Several 5th Column Soldiers stormed into the room.  One ran up to Simon and kicked him in the head knocking him to the ground.  Barely conscious he watched as the soldiers set several bombs around his lab, set the timers, and swiftly departed.  Simon crawled as best he could to try to get to the bombs, but the blow to his head left him extremely groggy.  Seconds passed like days... and as the final beep of the timer sounded, Simon prayed...

Simon awoke in a hospital bed.  He did not know the time or day... or how he survived for that matter.  A young lady stood beside him.  Her blonde hair swayed gently in the breeze from the open window.  It was Alex, his assistant.   They spoke quietly for some time.  He learned that he had been unconcious for almost a week.  His lab was destroyed.  All his documents, computers, and equipment we destroyed... save one.  The prototype!

With all documentation destroyed, nobody could figure out how to operate the power armor Simon had developed.  The Paragon City Police backed out on the purchase as all equipment and documents of the project had been destroyed.  Simon recovered after a few more weeks and was able to retrieve the prototype and return it to his private lab.  He discovered that it had barely any damage at all and was still operational once the power pack had been recharged.  He donned the armor and tested all its functions, it wtill worked properly.  And so it was then that he decided to use the armor to hunt down his attackers.  Those that had almost ruined his life and career.  He assumed the identity of Cold Volt and entered the streets of Paragon.

It had been a few months.  He had stopped numerous crimes yet still had learned so little of the true source of his attackers.  The 5th Column were stubborn with their information and resistant in relinquishing it to him.  So he decided to attempt to modify  his armor to make it more powerful so he could attempt to delve deeper into the headquarter of the 5th Column.  Here his life would change once again...

Simon had just installed a new flight system.  It had a tendency to overheat though, so he had to modify the cooling system.  A misplaced wire would cause the power pack to explode sending thousands of volts of electricity through his body.  He was unconcious again... He would awaken a few days later.  He felt odd.  A strange sensation rippled through his muscles... muscles?!  His clothes lie ripped on the floor.  Bulging muscles covered his body.  He felt energetic and powerful.  He knew something was odd so he called Alex and they began some tests the following day.  Simon discovered that when the experiemtnal power pack exploded the electrical field generated by the explosion fused with his body's cells and tissue.   Small electrical impulses surged through every cell of his body, working each muscle as though he was constantly excersizing.  This explained his new body build and also his new power.  Through time Simon discovered that he could channel the energy stored in his body and convert it into a kinetic force expelled through his hands.   It made for a powerful punch.  The energy within his body worked in wondrous ways, healing wounds rapidly and toughening his skin.  He was powerful even without his power armor.

Simon decided that further experimentation was needed.  But in the meantime he would test out his new powers in the fight against crime.  WIth his new appearance and powers he decided to assume a new identity once again.  And so he became Capt Paragon.
