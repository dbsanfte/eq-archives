Welcome to Immortal Spirit!

Immortal Spirit is an organization of video gamers from all over the world. Most members were found through Massively Multiplayer Online Games such as Everquest and City of Heroes, however with the vast number of video games available our members generally do not limit themselves to one type of game.

The goal of the organization is to provide online gamers a means to easily communicate and interact with each other so that the video game experience can be shared and also to help form lasting friendships among fellow gamers. To help accomplish this our site has a Bulletin Board for written communication and also a Voice Chat server so that our members can actually speak to each other as they play.

All persons are welcome to apply for membership to Immortal Spirit. Please check out our forum or links for specific games that we are currently involved in. Thank you for visiting us, and happy gaming!

Bishop, Immortal-Spirit Founder
