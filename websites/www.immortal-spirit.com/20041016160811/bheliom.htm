Background:

Obsessed with power and technology from an early age, Bheliom has studied electricity all his life. After developing a pair of gloves capable of emitting controlled bolts and an anti-gravity belt, he decided to fight crime as a way to test his inventions. If he happens to help someone as well, that's just a pleasant bonus. Constantly developing new gadgets and refining his old ones, Bheliom strives to prove himself the best in his field.
