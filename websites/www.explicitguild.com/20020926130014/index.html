Posted by: horok on Wednesday, September 04, 2002 - 09:18 PM EST
Ingredients

Paladin super slayers
Clerics
Rampage tanks
MT going LD
LD folk opening doors and unmezzable adds say hello
Mages who make drawings out of mod rods
Misc melee folk
Misc nuking folk
Cure folk
Arch Lich Rhag`Zadune
Determination

Preparation:
Add perfect flowing fight with LD folk opening doors and unmezzable adds saying hello. Empty bowl.
Then, mix MT going LD with Rampage Tanks becoming mince meat as list changes. Empty bowl.
Find your mages who make drawings out of mod rods in between rebuffs, add with Clerics.
Combine this with MT and Rampage Tanks to form base.
Add in some cure folk, misc nuking folk, misc melee folk and some paladin super slayers(dont forget!)
Mix vigourously till a smooth flawless raid is formed.
Sprinkle some determination on the top and the meal is ready.

Method:
Cook smooth flawless raid for 15 minutes.

Arch Lich, we slay he.
