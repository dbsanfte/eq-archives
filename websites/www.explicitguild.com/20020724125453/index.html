Toto, I don't think we're in Kansas anymore...
Posted by: Montague on Thursday, July 11, 2002 - 12:35 PM EST
Holy shit... We're just starting out so basically our only members are the old school VM guys and a few more awesome people we've picked up along the way; But I've gotta tell ya... we have owned every single mob we're tried so far.

On what we'll call "Opening Day", the day we formed, we started off by opening up a can on Klandicar over in WW. With help from a few of our friends in the Denizens we won with only 24 people, flawlessly. And since no point system or anything is in effect yet, we /random'ed for the ST key, congrats Faie!

Now, around this time I lost track of the days so, in no particular order, this is what died:

- Ring of Slime in CT. 14 people there total, 10 guildies laying down the smack (this was tough, as we had almost got ran over by another guild while we were forming up - but we prevailed).

- Vindi. I wanna say we had 12 or 13 people, but I could be off by 1 or 2.... either way we killed him with [as far as I know] - the smallest numbers ever... and we did a great job at that.

That about covers all the relavent info, we also did Vaniki and the Gimlik quest a few times... but I doubt anybody really cares about that ;p.


Also note: I'm working hard to get the site fully functional so you might incur some problems in a few areas, just hold tight while I work out all the bugs.

For now, peace.
