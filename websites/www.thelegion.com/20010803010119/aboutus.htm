What is the American Legion?

The American Legion is the largest veterans organization in the world, with a membership of more than 3 million in the United States, District of Colombia, and seven foreign countries.

All members of the American Legion are honorably discharged men and women who served their country on active duty in the Armed Forces of the United States during World War I, World War II, the Korean War, Vietnam, Lebanon, Grenada, Panama, and the Persian Gulf Wars and Conflicts.

The American Legion was founded and organized in 1919. Since that time, The American Legion has touched virtually every facet of American Life, fulfilling its main objective of service to the community, state, and nation.

Last year alone, needy children and families received more than $6.9 million in cash and gifts. Legion posts provided over $2 million in emergency aid to veterans last year. Deserving high school students were awarded $2.5 million in college scholarships. Over $47 million was spent by The American Legion and its affiliated organizations for the benefit of children and youth last year. Local charity campaigns received over $12.5 million in donations from local Posts. Legionnaires across the country donate more than a million hours each year in the Veteran's Administration "Voluntary Service Program" helping patients in VA Medical Facilities. Legion members donated over 2.5 million hours of service to their communities.

The American Legion has also, for many years, conducted programs which have been designed to benefit the youth of our nation. Some of these programs are:

• Legion Baseball
• Boys State and Boys Nation
• Scholarships [including Eagle Scout Scholarships]
• Flag Education
• Family Support Network
• American Education Week
• High School Oratorical Competition
• Junior Shooting Sports Program
• Legion Child Welfare Assistance
• National Emergency Fund
• Substance Abuse
• Missing Children
• Special Olympics
• Teen Suicide Prevention
• Temporary Financial Assistance

The American Legion is an organization that cares about America and its Children!
