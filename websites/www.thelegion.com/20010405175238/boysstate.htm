1999 Hoosier Boys State
Post 44 Delegates:

Ryan Egerski
Mike Schrader
Sean Selby

A message from the delegates reads...
"Boys State is an awesome experience! It is not easy to put in words the dynamic feeling that results from it. We felt really happy that we decided to go by the end of the week, and actually wished it lasted a little longer. The experience of meeting people that had similar interests was something that touched us. The combination of state party conventions, special schools, and political positions to attain was truly powerful, as well. The fun learning experience we shared made us feel extremely proud to be Americans and statesmen alike and we are sure that the next group to represent Post 44 will feel the same way. Thank you Post 44 and Mr. Stan Harris for allowing us to go!"
