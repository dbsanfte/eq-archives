Crock Pot Macaroni


1-16 oz   Box         Macaroni
2         Cups        Milk
1         Large Can   Evaporated Milk
1         Stick       Margarine
1         Large       Egg
3         Cups        Shredded Cheddar Cheese
To Taste	      Fresh Ground Black Pepper



This recipe was given to me by a very sweet 'Georgia Peach'. She has helped me a lot with both the cookbook and the home page. Donna, I can't say 'Thank You' enough for all your help.



Directions:

Cook the Macaroni according to the package directions and drain. In a large bowl, combined the cooked macaroni and the Butter.

Combined the Egg and the Milk together in a small bowl.

Put all the ingredients in a Crock Pot, stirring to combined it, then cook it on low for 4 hours.
