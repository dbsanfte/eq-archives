Once a band of Mercenaries called "Dark underground" , they wandered all of Norrath fighting evil and spreading their ideals of loyalty, friendship, and truth. The mercenaries were accused of insanity for holding onto such ideals in such dark and evil times. This ever vigilant group grasped the accusations of insanity and became "Arcane Asylum"

Arcane Asylum now continues its battle against evil. Their numbers grow larger daily as others seemingly develop the same "insane" ideals

Beware Norrath , The Asylum has been unleashed upon the lands and shall not be ignored.


Loot rules

There are alot of beliefs about looting policies and the guild will not dictate how loot is divided amongst groups but will stand on a general looting policy. A group is free to adopt an alternative looting method.

Lotto's in camps are to be handled at the discretion of those grouped. The group leader is responsible to identify the loot method used. The group leader is also responsible to inform any person joining the group of the used loot method.

The most logical loot policy for the guild is "need before greed". Present usable classes should be first to lotto for any items . The guild also hopes that any non-useable items will be considered for fellow guild members for purchase or as a gift.

It is also the responsibility of any group member to ask about the loot policy that is being followed in any group they are part of. Find out the looting before you get to something that is of importance. If you have a problem with looting, the character with that problem should pursue the resolution of that problem through the proper channels. Depending on the problem, they should contact an officer of the guild, an officer of the loot-whoring member's guild, and possibly a GM.


Conduct

The guild is for the purpose of providing a enjoyable enhancement to Everquest. Each member will act in a respectful manner to all other members. Members are not required to be the best of friends but are required to respect , aid and look out for each other. Grudges will not be tolerated and any disputes will be solved , nothing will be left unfinished. Hopefully any disputes can be settled between the affected parties quietly and between themselves as adults. Any dispute that cannot be resolved in this manner will be taken to the Arcane Council. It is the involved parties responsibility to involve the council .

No member will abuse another member in any way. General overuse of profanity in guildchat and groupchat or direct tells , unwanted suggestive comments , begging , etc. is considered abuse . As soon as a member tells another member to "please stop" , any continuation is considered abuse.

You are responsible for everything that your character does regardless of whether or not you are the actual person playing that character at the time. Your character is a member of our guild and that is all that people see.

Any abuse will be reported immediately to a member of the Council and said Council member is to deal with the matter in a timely manner. Any action taken by the Council is to also be reported to the other council members and to the Arcane Lord.
