ANGELIC FEW

We are the defenders of the weak, guides to the lost, and keepers of peace and order throughout the lands of Norrath. Each of our members is devout to one another, defending the other with their life. Nobility and Honor are not words spoken but are the embodiment of our purpose. We are a group of friends, brothers and sisters, striving to bring as much fun as possible to the  world of Norrath.

What does Angelic mean? Angelic means good, humble, kind, holy, devout, virtuous and self-sacrificing. These are the qualities that can be found in each of our members. All of us are of good and kind hearts. We are devout to our members in every way. To die so that the others may live and fight another day is the highest honor. But not only is sacrificing one’s life an honor, to help a friend with money or to get that item they need is also the meaning of self-sacrificing. Those within the folds of ANGELIC FEW have the highest respect for each other and are good friends. Nothing is better than a stout Ale shared with your friends after a long day of destroying evil in Norrath.

If you ever find yourself in serious trouble, cry the name ANGELIC FEW and we shall assist. When you are lost, we will be there to guide you to your destination. The most important thing to remember is have fun and enjoy the world ofNorrath.

ANGELIC FEW HISTORY

It was a dark and stormy night in the hills outside of the port city of Qeynos. A young Ranger, Zarstall, was foraging in the woods for fresh fruits and water. He heard the sharp crackling of lighting in the distance, then the sounds of a gnoll shirking in pain. He immediately began to run full speed towards these sounds and soon came upon 3 gnoll watchers pounding on a little Halfling druid. Zarstall let loose with a well placed bowshot and brought one of the gnolls down. He then let loose a few more shots into the 2nd gnoll, which died as well. By now the druid had regained control and finished the remaining gnoll with a burst of fire.

Ceidas, the young druid, turned to Zarstall and thanked him for saving his life. Zarstall built a small shelter and started up a fire to keep them warm. Meanwhile Ceidas applied some bandages to his wounds and called upon the power of Karana to heal his battle scars. Zarstall threw up a spit and started to roast up some rabbit meat he had foraged. The two adventures told stories throughout the night and exchanged foraging tips. From that fateful night a long lasting friendship had begun that shall never be broken.

  Ceidas and Zarstall traveled to the depths of Blackburrow; home of the Gnoll scum that curses the city of Qeynos. Within its stinking confines they came across a lone magician having a little trouble with a group of gnolls. Zarstall jumped in with sword swinging trying to taunt the gnolls off the mage and Ceidas let loose a lightning strike in the center of the group. The battle was over quickly and Shadrew expressed her gratitude for the help. Ceidas began healing Shadrew while Zarstall kept guard and protected the area. Another friendship had started, now the group of 2 had become 3 and will last forever.

Ceidas, Zarstall, and Shadrew left the evil lair of Blackburrow and traveled to the Island of Erud's Crossing to explore. As the Seaking moored up to the dock on the small island, Zarstall spotted trouble over the first sand dune along the beach. All 3 began running towards the disturbance, Ceidas stumbling through the sand, his little legs not meant for beach running. As Zarstall crested the dune he saw a Skeleton fighting 3 Zombie looking Sailors and a Dark skinned Elf engaged in battle. The stories of the dark race of elves had been they were an evil and vile people and not to be trusted. However, this dark-elf was in trouble and the 3 friends didn't hesitate to aid him. This was a hard fought battle that once again ended in victory. Xinuilt, master of the dead, thanked the group for rescuing him from certain death.

Zarstall gathered up some driftwood and tinder then started up a nice fire. Ceidas healed all the battle wounds for the group while Shadrew summoned some extra water to sooth the parties thirst. The four sat around the fire and told stories to each other about home and friends. It soon became clear that Xinuilt, while a dark-elf necromancer, was much different than most of his race. He was of a kind heart, willing to help those in need and trouble. Xinuilt was quickly accepted as a friend and joined the circle of ever expanding friends.

  As the 4 friends traveled throughout the lands of Norrath they meet new people and the group grew more and more. Anjin the master Wizard, Sanjaro Barbarian Warrior, Yemen the Rouge, Coneeb the Holy Warrior, Lugbash the warrior and Crysanthia master of enchantment and illusions joined the ranks of friendship. Then came along Tireasis master tracker, Izcurly the friendly druid, Snaildar the warrior of his god, Wiznywnde Nuking master and Darkmistery the enchanter. What started out as two friends has grown into a much larger group of brotherhood (and sisterhood). Everyone in this group had searched the lands for this kind of friendship and respect for each other. Most have tried to find this in the many guilds that populate the world. However, all had come to realize that none of these guilds could offer what had been established here.
