Corned Beef Hash


2	   Tablespoons   Canola or Vegetable Oil
1	   Large         Chopped Yellow Onion
4	   Cups	         Ore-Ida® Frozen Hash 
                         Brown Potatoes
1	   Cup	         Libby's® Corned 
                         Beef, Chopped
1/2	   Cup	         Reduced-Sodium, 
                         Chicken Broth
1/4	   Cup	         Chopped Fresh Parsley
To Taste	         Salt
To Taste	         Freshly Ground Black Pepper



I got this one out of a magazine while waiting in the Eye Doctors office, and his staff was even willing to make a copy of the page for me. I love these new glasses, now I can find more good recipes to share with everyone.


Directions:


In a large cast-iron skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the Chopped Yellow Onions and sauté until they start to brown. *5 to 8 minutes*. Add the Ore-Ida® potatoes and cook, stirring until the potatoes are brown and spots become crusty, approximately 8 minutes. Stir in the Corned Beef and Chicken Broth and cook, scrapping up any browned bits, until all the liquid is absorbed. *5 to 8 minutes*. Add the Chopped Parsley and season with the Salt and Black Pepper
