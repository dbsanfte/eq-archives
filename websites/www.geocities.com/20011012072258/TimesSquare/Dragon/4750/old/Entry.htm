Welcome to the EQRPA!

We hope you enjoy your visit. EQRPA is dedicated to providing a fun stable roleplaying environment for all players of Everquest. We are currently in the development stages as an organization, and thus are looking for many people to...

  • Become members - Any organization needs members. If you: want to roleplay, will follow the Rules and Regulation, commit to at least a couple of hours a week playtime, and will participate in feedback/surveys and sponsored events - then we want you! Should you be uncertain but interested, give us a few hours a week and see how you like it... if its not for you then you may leave the association.
  • Become Chroniclers - Chroniclers is the EQRPA version of GMs and Guides. As a Chronicler, you will need to make a much larger time commitment than the average member. Not only will you maintain in-game personalities to sponsor events and hand out dynamic quests, but you will participate in the co-ordination of events outside of the game. Details for becoming a Chronicler will be posted soon.
  • Beautify our web page - Do you have a talent which could help our web page out? Perhaps you have a knack for web page design, or maybe you're a graphics design artist - any and all submissions are welcome. We can always use skilled people.

It's official

We are proud to announce that Lanys T'Vyl is now up and running, and the EQRPA will be establishing themselves on this server first. Now that it is up an running, we look forward to members and non-members alike submitting their roleplaying backgrounds for posting. Happy gaming to all!
