UNREST


Unrest is a wicked, sinful place! Camped out, so what, theres so many things to kill... After getting through Dagnor's Cauldron, and into Unrest, you can expect to see many things there. Death Beetles, Skeletal Monks, Lurking Mummies, Festering Hags, Tormented Dead, Carrion Ghouls, Dark Boned Skeletons, and much more! A spooky haunted house, and it's always 6am there. Not a trip for the weak I must say, but level 15's can solo the Death Beetles.



Take the boat from Freeport to Butcherblock Mtns, it will be the third stop. Do not get off the boat until it zones into Butcherblock! Once there jump off the front of the boat and swim past the customs area to the shore. From there you can follow the shore south and then follow the right side wall. It has some crooks in the wall, so I recommend printing out the Butcherblock map I have availible on my maps page. Also get a copy of the Dagnor's Cauldron map so you know where the goblin camps are. Once in Dagnor's you go along the right wall, but it's hard to hug the wall, as it is very jagged. Instead, try to stay halfway inbetween the lake and the wall. After you pass the second camp you will notice a cave in the wall, welcome to Unrest.

If I am at Unrest at the time you come across on the boat, I would be happy to come help you find the way. Again, I stress making copies of needed maps.
