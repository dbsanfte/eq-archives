Dave's Breakfast Home Fried Potatoes


5     Large   Potatoes, peeled, cubed and boiled
1/2   Large   Onion, chopped
1     Lb.     Bulk Sausage



I played with this recipe in my head for several days before putting it down on paper and then actually trying to prepare it. Except for having to cook the potatoes the night before and refrigerating them, this recipe is very quick and easy.


Directions:


It's best to cook the potatoes the night before and then refrigerate them. Cut the potato cubes into 1/8" slices.

In a large skillet, cook the sausage. When the sausage is completely cooked, remove it from the skillet and set aside. Do NOT drain the grease from the skillet. Place the sliced Potatoes and the Onions in the skillet and fry, stirring occasionally until the potatoes start to brown. Return the cooked sausage to the skillet, cook for an additional 5 minutes, to re-heat the sausage and then serve.
