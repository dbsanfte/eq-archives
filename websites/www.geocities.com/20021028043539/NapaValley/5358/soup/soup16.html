Broccoli & Crab Bisque


1        Cup        Sliced Leeks
1        Cup        Sliced Fresh Mushrooms
1        Cup        Fresh Broccoli, Chopped
1        Clove      Garlic, minced
1/4      Cup        Butter
1/4      Cup        Flour
1/4      Teaspoon   Dried Thyme, crushed
1/8      Teaspoon   Black Pepper
1        Small      Bay Leaf
3        Cups       Chicken Broth
1        Cup        Light Cream
3/4      Cup        Swiss Cheese, shredded
1-6 oz   Package    Frozen Crabmeat, thawed and drained



My sweetie loves crab meat and when I saw this recipe, while surfing the Internet, well it just seemed like the natural thing to do, copy it, try it and then if it turned out good, posted it to my home page. Guess what? It turned out excellent.



Directions:

In a 3-quart saucepan, melt the Butter and then cook the Leeks, Mushrooms, Broccoli and Garlic until they are tender. Stir in the Flour, Thyme, Black Pepper Bay Leaf and 1/8 teaspoon of Salt. Add the Chicken Broth and Cream all at once. Cook, stirring until it thickens and bubbles. Continue to cook and stirring for an additional minute. Put in the Cheese and stir until it is melted. Add the crabmeat and heat thoroughly. Prior to serving, remove the Bay Leaf and discard it.
