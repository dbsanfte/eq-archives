Philadelphia 3-Step Carmel Pecan Cheesecake


2-8 oz   Package       Philadelphia® Cream 
                       Cheese, softened
1/2      Cup           Sugar
1/2      Teaspoon      Vanilla Extract
2        Large         Eggs
20       Each          Caramels
2        Tablespoons   Milk
1/2      Cup           Chopped Pecans
1 - 9"   Each          Graham Cracker Crust



While waiting to see the Doctor I located this recipe in one of the magazines in his office.


Directions:

Using an electric hand mixer, mix the Cream Cheese, Sugar, and Vanilla Extract until they are well blended. Add the Eggs and continue to mix until they are well incorporated.

Over low heat, melt the Caramels with the Milk stirring frequently until the mixture is smooth. Stir in the Pecans and then pour the caramel mixture into the Graham Cracker Crust. Top the caramel mixture with the Cream Cheese mixture.

Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 40 minutes or until the center is almost set. Cool and then refrigerate for at least 3 hours, preferably over night.
