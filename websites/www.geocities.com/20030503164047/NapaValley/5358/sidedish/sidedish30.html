Creamy Parmesan Corn


1-l Lb.   Package       Green Giant® Select® Frozen 
                        Shoepeg White Corn
1/2       Cup           Whipping Cream
2         Tablespoons   Grated Parmesan Cheese
1         Dash          Nutmeg *optional*



I found this recipe while searching for different Side Dish recipes.


Directions:


Cook the White Corn according to the package instructions and then drain.

In a medium saucepan, bring the Whipping Cream to a boil and continue to boil for approximately 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Add the Parmesan Cheese and stir until it is melted. Pour this mixture over the corn.
