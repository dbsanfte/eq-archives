Scrambled Egg Bake


1    Cup           Cooked Ham, cubed
1    Bunch         Green Onions, Diced
12   Large         Eggs, beaten
8    Medium        Fresh Mushrooms, sliced *optional*
2    Tablespoons   Butter
2    Slices        White Bread, cubed
To Taste           Salt
To Taste           Black Pepper
                   Paprika, for color

     Cream Sauce

2    Tablespoons   Butter
2    Tablespoons   All Purpose Flour
2    Cups          Milk
1    Cup           Cheddar Cheese, grated
1/4  Teaspoon      Salt
1/4  Teaspoon      Black Pepper


If you guessed that I got this recipe by surfing the Internet, jump up and give yourself a 'high five' because you are correct. This is a great Sunday morning breakfast or brunch.



Directions:

The Cheese Sauce should be made first. Start by melting the Butter in a large pan and then adding the All-Purpose Flour to form a roux. Add the Milk, Salt, and Black Pepper and stir until this mixture thickens. Stir in the Cheddar Cheese and continue to stir until the cheese is melted. Remove this mixture from the heat and set aside.

In a very large skillet, melt the butter and then add the Ham, Green Onions and the Mushrooms and cook until the vegetables are tender. Add the Eggs and scramble on low heat until just set, not hard scrambled. Fold the egg mixture into the cheese mixture and then pour this mixture into a greased baking casserole dish. Cover this mixture with the cubed Bread and sprinkle with Paprika, for coloring. Bake for 30 minutes in a pre-heated 350-degree oven.
