Pottery Guide

General:

          Pottery is the skill of molding clay into various objects and firing them in a kiln to produce the finished product.  Unlike other skills, it is a two stage skill, that requires first combining the ingredients on a pottery wheel, and then taking the unfinished product and the appropriate firing sheet, and combining those items in a kiln for the finished product.  If you have any information on this skill, please contact me.

 

Things to Note:

·        Pottery is a relatively inexpensive skill; about 30 pp to become a master

·        Pottery is a very useful skill for bakers, as most of the products can be used by the bakers

·        Pottery is also very useful to poison makers and tinkerers

·        Some people find it less expensive to just destroy unfinished products, as firing sheets are expensive and vendors won’t buy them

·        When making poison vial, many different animal skins can be used, they are listed in the second table

·        Firing Sheets are abbreviated as follows: HQ (High Quality Sheet), Q (Quality Sheet), and N (Firing Sheet)

·        When adding more than one of the same items in a recipe, do not stack them or you will not only fail but be told that it is the wrong combination

·        Most Importantly, every piece of pottery requires a flask of water in the pottery wheel combination, in order to be successful
