Pineapple Cheesecake Pie


2         Packages   Instant Vanilla Pudding
1-20 oz   Can        Crushed Pineapple
1         Pint       Sour Cream
1-6 oz    Each       Graham Cracker Pie Crust



I found this recipe while surfing the Internet. The original recipe calls for all low fat or fat free ingredients.


Directions:

Prepare the Instant Pudding according to the package directions. Combined the Crushed Pineapple, including the juice, with the pudding. Stir in the Sour Cream and mix thoroughly. Pour the mixture into the Graham Cracker Pie Crust and chill for at least 2 hours prior to serving.
