FIRST STEP WINNERS

FIRST PLACE

"Artificial Sun"  

by Donovan

Alone and in a state of mind,
Not unlike a circus.
The world around me fast asleep;
The world inside me wide awake.
Evening; morning; line is blurred
Long since has been the same to me.
Association with the norm
Is not within my reach.
The world outside is flying by
Like fireflies in summer twilight.
While safe inside I'm still and calm,
I do not move, I make no sound.
Society is not my friend,
More a passing acquaintence.
Another evening/morning gone;
Insomnia is like a shield. So as I sit still as a stone,
My mind inside is racing
I sit alone, my light is on
My artificial sun.



SECOND PLACE


"Your Lover"

by MysticBlade

Arrived directly from hell,
I fall in love when I first saw you.
I couldn't think by myself anymore,
And my heart was already with you.

With your only presence,
You fill every bit of my life.
With your beauty and your mind,
I couldn't have a better life.

Only one they pass without you
Turn out to be a real nightmare.
But with you at my side,
I have no fear of those nightmares.

Love is a word with small meaning
When speaking of my feelings for you.
I would like to know a better way,
But I can say only one thing, I am fond of you.
