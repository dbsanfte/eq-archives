Easy Hot Dog Cheese Roll-Ups



16   Each      Cocktail Franks
6    Each      American Cheese Slices
1    Package   Pillsbury® Refrigerated Crescent Rolls



A lot of times, the simpler the recipe, the better. This is a very simple recipe and one that the kids can get involved with preparing.


Directions:

Remove the Crescent Rolls from the tube and carefully separate them into triangles. Retaining the triangle shape, cut each one in half. Wrap 1/3 of a slice of Cheese around a Cocktail Frank and starting at the large end of the crescent roll, roll them up. Bake in a pre-heated 400-degree oven for approximately 10 minutes or until the crescent rolls are a golden brown.
