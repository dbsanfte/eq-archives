A Guide for Druids
by Breanse Silverwynde

Creation :
When creating a druid you will start with 30 ability points . Wisdom is the most important .i recommend pouring 10 of those ability points into this category.
The rest distribute as you see fit ;)

Getting started :
When you enter the game you will be close to your guild , Soldiers Of Tunare . Enter the guild and give your note to Heartwood Master -He will , in return , give you a guild tunic and some experience points. When you 'Hail' him , he will give you a small task to complete . Finding your way to the elevator/Lift is the most difficult part of beginning ,if you are not already familiar with Kelethin. I have found , though , that searching for it is the best way to learn your way around the tree city.

Spells :
After you have hunted for a while and you have sold the loot you collected it is time to buy a couple new spells. I recommend as your first spells ...burst of flame or Burst of fire . Second being Snare , which at lvl 26 is still my favorite spell and stays in a spell slot.As a young druid the spell grasping roots may come in handy , but after leaving the newbie grounds it becomes useless.

Burst of Flame / Burst of Fire :
DD spell for 5 damage (if i remember correctly)

Snare :
Slows the creature down and is used for kiting.
