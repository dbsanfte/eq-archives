Elite Guard

The Elite Guard defends the guild, and protects all Dragonslayers. Elite Guards take whatever action necessary to assist in corpse retrievals from difficult areas. Elite Guards are called upon in Roleplay Events to defend the guild and our Queen.
Any actions taken by Elite Guard members, in absence of the Princess of Battle or Captain, must be reported right away to the Captain of the Guard. EG members report to the Captain who in turn reports to the Princess of Battle. One member of the Elite Guard will be named as the Queen's Champion, chosen by the Queen herself. To apply for the Elite Guard you must be over level 15 and complete the quest.

Please see the Barracks pages for further information.
