Marinara Sauce



3	  Tablespoons	Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1	  Large	        Yellow Onion, chopped
6	  Cloves	Garlic, minced
1	  Lb	        Fresh Mushrooms, sliced
1/2	  Teaspoon	Dried Crushed Red Pepper
1-28 oz   Can	        Italian Plum Tomatoes, chopped,and
                        the juices reserved
1- 5 oz   Can	Tomato  Sauce
1	  Teaspoon	Sugar
1 1/2	  Teaspoons	Dried Basil, crumbled
3/4	  Teaspoon	Dried Oregano, crumbled



Yet another recipe I found while surf'in the Internet, looking for something else.



Directions:

Heat the oil in a large heavy skillet over medium heat. Add the Chopped Onions and sauté until tender, about 5 minutes. Add the chopped Garlic and sauté` and additional minute. Add the Mushrooms and the crushed Red Pepper and sauté until the mushrooms just begin to soften, about 5 minutes. Add the tomatoes and the reserved juices. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes. Add the Tomato Sauce, Sugar, Basil and Oregano. Cover and cook 15 minutes. Uncover and simmer until slightly thickened, approximately 5 minutes. Season with Salt and Pepper.
