Interesting Texts from Kael


Below are some NPC texts I've been able to discover while talking to various Kael Drakkal inhabitants.  Asterisks (*) mark ones I believe may be the first discovered so far, so you could be the first to solve a unique new quest! An excellent map of Kael Drakkal and its npcs can be found at this link.

The most promising thing about this particular
zone, is that it appears most of the mobs have
the same quests as dropped items available.
So if a mob is known to drop an item, you can
almost be certain of some phrase for those
with Kael faction to trigger a quest resulting
in that same item. This means that there are
many quests as yet untried/undiscovered,
because players are only now beginning to
notice this connection.

                                           Iceshard Manor: Wonderful possibilities in this
                                           npc-filled building within Kael, run by the Ice-
                                           shard brothers. Most interesting developments
                                           to-date are an unknown trigger for the second*
                                           of three quests given by Vorken Iceshard, and
                                           a book detailing what I believe to be the rise of
                                           the hatred between the giants and dragons* as
                                           supplied by Bjoskhua Blackfist. For another
                                           interesting find on Bjoskhua and his brother
                                           Clrakk, including an unsolved quest or two, to
                                           salivate over, check here.  Another Blackfist brother within the manor, Nojas Blackfist, appears to have something in store for us as well. The details of how I came to stumble across the possible start of the quest for Iceshaper* are in this Nojas text. Additionally there are the puzzles of Reivaj the Battlerager. Many, many of the other npcs in this castle alone have yet to be fully understood. Let the quest fanatic salivate!

Korakaz: Located to your right as you approach the Wakening Lands zone within Kael, this guy is definitely a quest npc; another player noticed that, until 'amiable' faction, the npc would respond to hails with 'I cannot trust you with this information yet'. Unfortunately, even now at warmly, I cannot get much further with him. If you hail him, firstly he says: "I will cut them all down! Every last one of them!" After repeating the first sentence of his, I got "The dragons and the Coldain! All of the infidels! They must be crushed! They must be smashed!"  The only further response I can get from him is by repeating "crushed" or "smashed" which only gets me "Leave me be or I will crush you too!". Someone out there will have the insight or luck into further triggering him; anyone up for the challenge? ;)
