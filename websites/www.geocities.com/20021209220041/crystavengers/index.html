***Latest News from Caster's Realm***

 

March 22, 2002 - Friday

  • PLANES OF POWER - OFFICIAL WEBSITE

The official website has now been put live for the Planes of Power expansion in EverQuest.

Currently listed features include:

  • 18 new planar zones that push your teamwork abilities to the limit
  • Hundreds of new items and monsters
  • Exciting new quests and challenges await even the most experienced players
  • Two planar cities that provide hubs for travel and player interaction
  • A central storyline links the content from every zone into one compelling quest.
  • Further character progression making players even more powerful

Other interesting facts include that there will be a beta test (details not yet determined). There are also no plans to redo any of the Luclin player animations as part of this expansion.

March 21, 2002 - Thursday

  • FOCUS ITEM NERFAGE

Seems that as part of this morning's patch changes were also made to focus items. Unfortunately since this was not mentioned in the patch message the changes are still filtering in from the servers.

Currently it appears that the casting time reduction items only work on spells which are between 4 and 9.5 seconds. Spells which have a casting time longer than 9.5 or shorter than 4.0 will no longer be affected by the focus items.

It also appears that the damage increasing focus item no longer works on some high end spells.

Please keep info coming in as you find out any other stealth changes which may have happened to these items.
