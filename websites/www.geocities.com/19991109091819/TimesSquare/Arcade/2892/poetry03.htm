TENDER FOOT WINNERS

FIRST PLACE

"Final Rest"

by Damned

That time has passed and so this time has come.
And as I lay here in this dark,
Feel her presence so I shut my eyes.
For we both know why she has come...
Not as a creature of the night and dark,
But as the light of pure gentle warmest love.
To do the duty that was given her by God;
To do it one more countless time.
But still shed pure tear of compassionate demise
And let me gently die and pass.




SECOND PLACE

"The Foul"  

by Arbotros

What horrible beasts I find in this place!
Despicable creatures of disgrace!
Why are they here?   Why do they smell?
Wretched spawns of a stench-ridden hell!

Trolls!!   I find, in this once peaceful wood,
Killing all life, these things are no good!
What haven could they possibly have in these woods filled with life?
When everywhere they go they bring stench and utter strife?

And what about the ogre kind?
Bigger, dumber, and uglier, too!
Yet relative to the monstrous troll,
Strong in muscle, but their brains are null.

And rabid wolves follw them where they lead,
Like the hunting dogs they are,
Yet the trolls they follow are not their prey,
But the person they find will feed them that day.

Woe are we!   To now such foulness!
And to find such creatures on the prowess
Hunting us, ha!   Fools they are!
But they kill our weak, the foulest by far!

And why are they here?   I repeat once more.
Here to make our lives quite sore!
The scent they bring, is fatal to some,
But to see their face, can turn one blind and dumb!
