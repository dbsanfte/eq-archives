Breakfast Burritos


10   Each          Large Flour Tortillas
1    Large	   Diced Yellow Onion
1    Large	   Diced Green Pepper
8    Slices	   Cooked and Crumbled Strips 
                   of Bacon
1    Small	   Package of Frozen Hash 
                   Brown Potatoes
2    Cups	   Shredded Cheddar Cheese
6    Large	   Eggs
1/4  Cup	   Milk
4    Tablespoons   Margarine
3    Tablespoons   Bacon Grease 
1    Package	   Taco Seasoning Mix
1    Small Jar	   Salsa



This is yet another recipe that I discovered as I "cruised" the Internet. I really did mean to save the Internet address, but forgot to.



Directions:

Fry the Bacon in a skillet and reserve the bacon grease. Fry the Hash Brown Potatoes in the reserved bacon grease, until hot but not browned. Keep them warm. Warm the tortillas according to the package directions. Whip the eggs and milk until frothy and then add the Diced Onions and Dices Green Pepper, Bacon Pieces, Taco Seasoning and fry as you would scrambled eggs. Take a warm tortilla and assemble as follows: Hash Browns, Shredded Cheddar Cheese, Egg Mixture, a little more cheese and then the Salsa. A dollop of Sour Cream can be added if desired and roll as you would a burrito.
