Explorers wanting to join must be of the 10th season or higher .

New members must send master info to the webmaster to update web page.

Any master that acts in a questionable manner, the issue will be addressed by the guild mistress and a warning will be issued. Second offenses will not be tolerated and the master will loose guild membership.

All masters may visit the guild web site to follow the activities and events of the guild. Open communication is a must, please post all ideas and issues good or bad.

By joining the guild explorers agree to play in a fun and family oriented environment.

Guild members will help and assist other members whenever possible and within reason.

All decisions will be made by the Guild Mistress and will be final.
