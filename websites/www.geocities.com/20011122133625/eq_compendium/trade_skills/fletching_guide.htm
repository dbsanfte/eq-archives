Fletching Guide

General:

Fletching is a skill that results in bows and arrows. All bows are composed of the basic components of a staff, string, and possibly a tool. Just place them the fletching kit, and hit the combine button.

Arrows are made up of 4 different components, the point, shaft, fletch, and nock. The components must be put into a fletching kit, one at a time, and then combine. If anyone has the trivial levels for any of the bows below, please contact me.

 

Things to Note:

        Fletching is a great skill for anyone that can use archery

        Fletching, while not the most expensive skill, can become very expensive when making the more advanced bows

        Darkwood bows have a success rate of 40%, even at skill level 200

        All the player made bows and arrows are far superior to the store bought ones

        The properties of the bows and arrows are cumulative

        The trivial levels of components are not cumulative however

        A success when creating arrows results in 5 arrows, except with magical arrows, which results in 10

        Silver Tips result in magical arrows, which can hit ghouls and willow wisps
