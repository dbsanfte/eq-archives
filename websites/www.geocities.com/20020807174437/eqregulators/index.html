Welcome to the Regulators of Norrath Home Page. Founded in August 1999 by High Lord Scotticus Rex the Regulators of Norrath holds the distinction of being one of the oldest guilds on the Tribunal Server. Founded on the principles of Strength and Honor and strong on-line family commitment the Regulators of Norrath have weathered many storms. They still weather storms today but with over 2 years on-line existence its clear that the Regulators of Norrath are here to stay. The code of Conduct is followed by all and insures that all members are treated equally and fairly. A place you can make your home and always count on to be there when you need a friend.
