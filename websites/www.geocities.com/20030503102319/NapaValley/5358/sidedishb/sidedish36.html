Broccoli Casserole with Corn Flake Crust


1         Tablespoons     Margarine
2         Cups            Crushed Kellogg's 
                          Corn Flakes
2         Tablespoons     Margarine
2         Tablespoons     Flour
3/4       Cup             Milk
1         Cup             Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1-11 oz   Can             Whole Kernel Corn, drained
1-10 oz   Package         Frozen Broccoli Cuts, thawed & drained



I got this recipe from the Kellogg's® home page. If you want new and exciting recipes, and you DON'T surf the net, you are really missing out on some excellent recipes and really what the net was intended for. Supplying quality information for everyone.


Directions:


In a medium saucepan, melt the 1 tablespoon of margarine. Add the crushed Kellogg's® Corn flakes and stir until the corn flakes are thoroughly coated with the butter. Set this mixture aside as it will be the topping for the casserole.

Place the thawed, drain Broccoli Cuts in the bottom of a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish.

In another medium saucepan, melt the remaining margarine and then stir in the Flour. Add the Milk and stir until the mixture is smooth. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly. Once it begins to boil, cook for one more minute then incorporate the Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese, stirring until the cheese is melted. Remove this mixture from the heat and stir in the corn.

Pour this mixture over the Broccoli Cuts and then top with the Crushed Corn Flakes.

Bake in a pre-heated 350 degree oven for approximately 25 minutes or until the dish is thoroughly heated and the corn flakes have browned.
