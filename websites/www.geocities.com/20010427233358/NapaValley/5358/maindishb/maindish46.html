Texas Brisket


1         6 Lb.       Beef Brisket
1/2       Cup         Vegetable Oil
1-12 oz   Can         Beer
1         Clove       Garlic, minced
2         Teaspoons   Lemon Juice
2         Teaspoons   Salt
3         Whole       Cloves
1         Envelope    Lipton® Dry Onion Soup Mix



This is a great Sunday evening meal, which can slow cook as you watch your favorite Professional Football Team play.


Directions:

In a large bowl, combined the Vegetable Oil, Beer, Garlic, Lemon Juice, Salt and Cloves and mix thoroughly. Marinate the Beef Brisket in this mixture overnight.

Remove the brisket from the marinate, sprinkle the top of the meat with the Onion Soup Mix, wrap tightly in aluminum foil and bake in a pre-heated 275-degree oven for 4 to 5 hours. Discard the marinade.
