These pages will set you up for life in EverQuest. They have more information and links than I would ever care to try to collect. They are also some of the most visited sites on the web! Impressive!

AllaKhazam's Magical Realm - Your Source for EverQuest Information. I visit this page every day.

EQ'Lizer - As good as AllaKhazam's Page.

The Rangers Glade - Hey, I play a ranger, so this is the place to go. I'm sure you can learn stuff here that will be useful.

EQ Atlas - Tired of Getting Lost? This is the best atlas/map site that I've found!

EQ Traders - A site that lists ALL the trade skills and gives hints on how to become a master.

Fletching Guide by Aoenla - The best fletching guide I've found! Very comprehensive.

Caster's Realm - A Very cool site for you spell caster types. Lists all the spells, what they do, and where to get them! Very Useful! Not only that, has excellent creation guides for every class too (no, the end all stat for warriors is Charisma, dump all your points there! hehe).

EverQuest Stratics - News and information. A standard for most games out there.

EverQuest Experience - A neat calculator for figuring out how much experience you need for that next level. Only works for solo players.

Group Experience - A download that will help you calculate what sort of experience you'll get in a group. Use it with the other table and you should be able to figure out most anything.

EverQuest Mobs - This site lists all the mobs in the game by their location, what level they are, how many hit points they have, and they type they are (warrior, druid, necromancer, etc.). Very useful.

EQ Druids - One of the Ranger's parent classes. Also a link here so my best friend can find the site.!
