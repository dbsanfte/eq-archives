Level Requirements



To best deal with the requirements of the officer positions, the following minimum level requirements will be observed for each of the officer tiers.

•Champions will be level 35 or higher
•Guardians will be level 30 or higher
•Heralds will be level 25 or higher
•Torchbearers will be level 20 or higher


Of course we may come across those individuals that don't meet the minimum required level for certain officer positions that the guild may feel they are qualified for. Those individuals will be judged on a case by case basis, and the level requirements may be waived for them.
