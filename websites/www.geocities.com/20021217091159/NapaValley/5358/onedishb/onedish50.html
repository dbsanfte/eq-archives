Philly Cheese Steak Sandwich


1     Medium        Onion, sliced
1     Medium        Green Bell Pepper cut
                    into thin strips
2     Tablespoons   Butter or Margarine
3/4   Lb.           Deli Roast Beef, cut
                    into thin slices
4     Each          Hoagie Rolls
1/2   Lb.           Provolone Cheese, sliced


My wife had never had a Philly Cheese Steak Sandwich and when I found this recipe on the net I knew it was perfect for the cookbook. She fell in love with the sandwich and we have had them several times, for both Saturday or Sunday lunch and also for dinner.



Directions:

In a large skillet, over medium heat, melt the Butter and then add the Onion and Green Pepper. When the onions become transparent, mix in the Roast Beef and heat until the beef is warm. Evenly divide this mixture into 4 portions. Place a piece of Provolone Cheese on top of each portion, cover the skillet with a lid and reduce the heat to low. Slice the Hoagie Rolls in half, lengthwise, and when the cheese has started to melt, place a portion of the beef/cheese mixture on each roll.
