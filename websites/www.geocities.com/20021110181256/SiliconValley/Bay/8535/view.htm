The Shadow
The shadow is often the dump of unwanted and feared aspects of life. Evil, chaos, and cruelty are mistakenly
associated with what is only the inherent darkness that each of us carries. Great opportunity and energy lay in the
shadow. As it is developed we gain powers that most people do not have. In increasing our awareness of the
shadow, we help balance our lives. The mystic upholds dark qualities put down by those who have not accepted themselves totally. They fear and condemn the spiritual aspirant for properties they have denied themselves to let surface. These are misoneistic and underdeveloped accusations and are the reasons for the slaughter of the native Americans, the medieval
European witches, and the feudal Japanese ninja. When a man condemns someone he often does so subconsciously in order to hide his own faults. Be careful who you make your enemy, for you will often see yourself looking back at you.
