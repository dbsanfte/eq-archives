Potato Soup
 

3       Medium          Minced Leeks (White 
                        Parts ONLY)
1       Medium          Finely Chopped 
                        Yellow Onion
3       Ribs            Finely Chopped 
                        Celery
2       Tablespoons     Butter
4       Medium          Diced Potato
4       Cups            Chicken Stock
1/4     Lb              Dices Cook Ham
To Taste                Salt
To Taste                Black Pepper



This is one of my Dad’s recipes. He was kind enough to allow me to included in my Cook Book. Thanks Pa-Paw.!!!!!


Directions:

In a soup pot, sauté the Minced Leeks, Chopped Onion and the Chopped Celery in the Butter for approximately 3 minutes.

Add the Diced Potato, the 4 cups of Chick Stock and the Salt and Pepper to the soup pot.. Simmer the vegetables, covered, for 15 minutes or until they are tender

Put 1/2 of the soup mixture in a food processor and blend until it is creamy smooth. Return this mixture to the soup pot. Add the Diced Cook Ham and simmer for approximately 10 more minutes

Note: If the soup is to thick, 1 to 2 cups of cream can be used to thin it. If the soup is not thick enough, Instant Mashed Potato’s can be added to thicken it
