Blue Cheese Salad Dressing



8     Ounces     Sour Cream
8     Ounces     Mayonnaise
To Taste         Crumbled Blue Cheese



As a teenage, I remember my Mom and Dad using this salad dressing on their salads and wondering what was wrong with them. I mean, French Dressing was the only type of dressing to be put on a salad. But through their patience’s and understanding, well now it is the only type of dressing I will use.



Directions:

Combine the Sour Cream and Mayonnaise(Salad Dressing) in a bowl and mix thoroughly. Add the amount of Crumbled Blue Cheese necessary to achieve the desired taste.


Refrigerate for at least 2 hours prior to serving.
