Hot Hoagies



6     Each   	Torpedo Buns
1/2   Lb       	Sliced Honey Baked Ham
1/2   Lb       	Sliced Bologna
1/2   Lb       	Sliced Salami
8     Ounces   	Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
               	Lettuce
	        Tomato Slices
                Italian Dressing



All of my brothers-in-law worked for the same Pizza Shop at one time or another. This sandwich was made by the shop for those who did not like pizza.


Directions:

Cut each of the Torpedo Buns in half lengthwise but not completely through. Place a layer of each type of meat on the bottom half of the bun. (I recommend that the Salami be put on last).

Cover the top half of the bun with a generous portion of the Shredded Mozzarella Cheese. Place the sandwich in the oven under the broiler or in and electric toaster oven under the broiler. Pay close attention to the sandwich so that it does not burn. When the cheese is melted and the bun has toasted a little, remove it from the oven and put on some Lettuce, Tomato and Italian Dressing.

Serve this sandwich with either Potato Chips or French Fries.
