ULTIMA ONLINE GUILD TIPS



Many players are overwhelmed by the numerous Ultima Online guilds currently active. It is natural to wonder which guilds to join. Should you join one, or two or many? Certainly the nature of your UO character will influence the type of guild that will be attractive to you. Browsing through UO guild pages will give you an idea of their alignment (good, evil, neutral), their UO goals, e.g. world domination, mercantile, magical, underworld, fun. However, one good way to get to know an active guild is through its members. Visiting the Origin chat server can be very helpful. It will give you an opportunity to get to know guild members and make a more informed decision with regard to which guilds to join. The chat server and most guild web pages can be reached through the Origin site, click here to access them.The chat rooms may be reached by clicking on the "Town Square " button once there.


Another excellent source is the "Ultima Online Guild Central" web site. This site conducts thorough reviews of Ultima Online guilds as well as narratives of interviews with guild leaders. It is a must for anyone considering joining a guild. Although relatively new, UO Guild Central holds great promise as a resource for UO. Click here to access it.
