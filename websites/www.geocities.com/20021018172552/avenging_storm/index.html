Avenging Storm was formed:May 28th, 2000

 

       



   We are the Avenging Storm. Lead by our courageous and loyal leader, Kindoz Truart, we are Noble and Just in our dealings with others, whether it is to step in between a dying adventurer and the beast that would kill him, or to avenge their death should we arrive too late. We travel across the vast depths of Norrath, seeking to push back the blanket of Evil that attempts to shroud the land. Should Evil try to stand in our way, it will be met with a fury not known since Karana himself walked the plains...."
