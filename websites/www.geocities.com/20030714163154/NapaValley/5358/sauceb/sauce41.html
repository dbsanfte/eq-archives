Jalapeno Hollandaise Sauce



1        Package   Knorr's® Hollandaise Sauce
1-2 0z   Jar       Pimentos, drained
1        Medium    Jalapeno Pepper, seeded and minced
                   Your choice of cooked vegetables 
                   such as asparagus or broccoli or 
                   cauliflower, etc.



I gave a coworker my recipe for New Potatoes and after making them, and loving them I might add, she gave me this recipe. It is extremely easy to prepare and very, very good.



Directions:

Prepare the Hollandaise Sauce according the package instruction and then add the Pimentos and the Jalapeno Pepper. Heat the sauce and then pour it over the cooked vegetable.
