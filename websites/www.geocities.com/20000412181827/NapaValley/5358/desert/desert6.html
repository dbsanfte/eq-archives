Cheese Cake


1       Cup        Kellogg's® Corn Flakes, 
                   Crumbled
1/3     Cup        Butter
1-8oz   Package    Soft Philadelphia® Cream 
                   Cheese
1       Can        Eagle Brand® Sweetened  
                   Condensed Milk
1/2     Cup        Lemon Juice
1       Teaspoon   Vanilla Extract
1       Can        Cherry Pie Topping



We have "racked" our brains trying to remember where we got this recipe and just simply do not remember. That also leads to this question: "Why do they call it a "cake" when it is made in a pie pan???


Directions:

Melt the Butter in a sauce pan. Combined the melted butter and the Kellogg's® Corn Flake Crumbs in a 9 inch pie pan pressing firmly and evenly over the bottom and sides of the pie pan to form a crest. Chill this crust for at least one hour.

Using an electric mixer, beat the Philadelphia® Cream Cheese until it is light and fluffy. Add the Sweetened Condensed Milk and blend thoroughly. Stir in the Lemon Juice and the Vanilla Extract.

Turn this mixture into the chilled pie crest and smooth out the mixture evenly. Refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours. Remove from the refrigerator and top with the Cheery Pie Topping just before serving.
