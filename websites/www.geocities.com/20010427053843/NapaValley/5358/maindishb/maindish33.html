Hash-In-A-Cup


1 - 15 oz   Can           Mary Kitchen® 
                          Roast Beef Hash
1/4         Cup           Yellow Onion, diced
3           Tablespoons   Heinz® Ketchup
2           Medium        Green Bell Peppers
2           Large         Eggs, hard boiled



Taking a different approach to finding a new recipe, I started with what I had in the pantry, took a brand name product, in this case Mary Kitchen roast beef hash, and started surfing the Internet to find a new recipe. I located this one on the Hormel home page. (www.hormel.com).


Directions:

In a medium size bowel, combined the Roast Beef Hash, Onion and Ketchup and mix lightly with a fork.

Halve the Green Peppers, removing the seeds and the white membrane. Parboil the peppers for approximately 5 minutes, remove them from the boiling water and allow them to cool. Stuff the hash mixture into the drained peppers and arrange them in a greased casserole dish. Top with one or more of the slices of egg and bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 20 minutes.
