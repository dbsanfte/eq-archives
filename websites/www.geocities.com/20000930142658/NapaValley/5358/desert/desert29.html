Dottie's Pan Cake


2 1/2   Cups          All Purpose Flour
1 1/2   Cups          Sugar
1/2     Cup           Cocoa
2       Teaspoons     Baking Powder
1       Teaspoon      Vanilla Extract
1/2     Teaspoon      Salt
2/3     Cup           Vegetable Oil
2       Tablespoons   White Vinegar
2       Cups          Coffee (Water can be substituted)



A very, very close ICQ friend emailed this recipe to me. For those of you that are not familiar with ICQ, it's a 'real-time' chat system that is available on the Internet. Thanks Dottie. *KOTC*.


Directions:

Mix all the dry ingredients in a 13"x9" baking dish. Make 3 wells in the dry ingredients and then add the Vegetable Oil to one, the White Vinegar to the second and the Coffee and Vanilla Extract to the third. Using a fork, slowly mix all the ingredients together, mixing thoroughly.

Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 35 to 40 minutes. Allow the cake to cool prior to serving.
