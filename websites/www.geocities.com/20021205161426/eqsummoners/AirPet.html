The Air Pet
    The Air Elemental is probably the most specialized elemental in a magician's arsenal.  Although it has low hitponts and high agility, making it harder to hit, it's physical attributes take a back seat to it's other abilities.  The Air Elementals stun is extremely effective against hard hitting monsters and interrupts spell casting.  Also, it's ability to invis itself makes it a lovely non-aggroing companion.
Note: Level 50+ Air pets now have the ability to cause damage with their stun spell.
