      Guild Prevail as True Heroes ! !

I'm here live on the scene in Permafrost where a hunting mission which has been organised by
the Antonican Brotherhood, is well under way. It's cold, scary and very dangerous. Many have
met their death here, yet these brave souls have ventured deep inside the heart of this dungeon to
battle bad and evil in a relentless bid to remove Norrath of this vile terror that is sweeping the
lands. Many attended, ranging from mighty warrios to all powerful casters and blasters. Although EVAC to South Ro was at the ready, it need not be used due to the organisation and control all members had and kept. While there I witnessed only one train.
It was cold and very lonely.
Curiousity overcame one member which led to the surprise attack of some unfriendly Ice Giants who were quickly backed up by a spawn of angry goblins. All members made a safe getaway, returning only minutes later with armoured guild backup to regain their position and with hold it strongly.
A cleric by the name of Kabrom acted as high level buffer and healer. His job was important, as to replenish his teams was one of the most major roles. Thorbad the druid, who was also guild
leader, joined in by blasting the enemies with his superior spells. To even make it so deep into this dungeon was thanks to him and his camoflauge and group SOW. Valdran the Shaman also gave out buffs and some awesome blasts. If not for his mighty strength and dexterity buffs for the tanks, then he was most surely there for his superb display of staff crushing. Paulos the warrior, a man of great power and skill, bravely fought like a true fighter, mastering his weapons in a battle til the end. Tikibuku the ranger, led the group with his excellent tracking and mapping knowledge. He acted as guide and a great tank and served his role most well. Dellara the druid and Zersh the rogue joined to take on a nearby room. With a druids buffs and the skills of a rogue, they prevailed as a powerful team, destroying all in their path. Later as the night progressed and the icy winds blew, they were all joined by Quinath the ranger, Derithon the wizard, Hammon the paladin and Sabalon the warrior who all displayed great skill and courage in expulsing the evil creatures of Permafrost.
An excellent raid all in all, but who planned this? Who was behind the highly skilled organisation and strategic decisions of this mission? It was none other than Aracc Sownrun of the Antonican Brotherhood. He not only fought with the strength of a warrior, the wisdom of a druid and the mind of an enchanter, but also ported members, kept track of raid details and last but not least led the raid with dignity and responsibility. To my knowledge, this is one of the best raids I have attended in a long while. I hope you'll join me when I take a trip to Befallen on the
Just some of the mighty guild !
4th of February to yet again report on another raid on behalf of the Antonican Brotherhood !

Paulosio Shadowhawkio

GUILD STILL ON TOP FORM !!
The musty stench that relenquished in the dimly lit caverns of splitpaw, and the all powerful gnolls who resided and roamed freely, posed no threat to the almighty Antonican Brotherhood. All was quiet. It was late Tuesday afternoon upon the world of Norrath, and in the adjoining zone of south Karana, due north from the lair of Splitpaw, the raid posse gathered upon the finely constructed stone bridge of Antonica.
Mighty warriors and fine casters travelled from all over the continent ready to battle the unknown power that lay behind the zone line of Splipaw.
The Pre-Zone meet up
When all members were present, the preparations began. Everyone was split into two groups. Group one consisted of Derithon, Sabalon, Budweis, Venau, Caning and Skyware, with Derithon as Leader and Evacer, and Budweis as main group leader. Group two was headed by Paulos, along with Quinath, Dhakkon, Elventrackr, Temijin and Aracely, Temijin was main healer and evacer and also kept health checks on group one by assisting Budweis. Everyone was briefed and all the raid details were finalised. Upon the word go, the all powerful army marched across the wide plains of Karana until they reached the tunnel that would take them into Splitpaw. Paulos and Quinath headed operations and used guild talk to communicate the two groups. After a final group status check......they moved in. Everyone made their way to the doors which led to the bridge room. All were prepared for their first battle, but wait.....? group one had lost their leader. Of all the tricks and traps in Splipaw, Derithon had managed to find the worst one. He was trapped between an outcropping rock and the ceiling. No amount of movement could free him, and no-one possessed the power to help him. After a quick discussion, Derithon decided to port out to the nearest spire and then make the run back to Splitpaw, this time avoiding the trap!!!!
The Briefing !
Both groups proceeded into the first room where they encountered their first batch of gnolls. The speed and skill of the fighters proved too much for the enemy, and they were dead before they knew what had happened. They cautiously made there way through each room and across every causeway, wiping out all who opposed them. While resting at regular intervals to heal and regain mana, caning from group one became trapped in a small room when the door shut down behind him. Everyone frantically searched for a way in, but it wasn't until Quinath located a switch on the
nearby wall, that caning was eventually freed.
They were now deep in the heart of the condensated dungeon. The sound of dripping water was joined in by the howls of close by gnolls, soon drowned out by the clashing of weapons and the projectile casting of spells. Upon reaching a clearing, they took a breather and rested by the waters edge. It wasn't long before they were disturbed by two high level snakes which hit with a lethal poison, of which took five casts to cure. After the short battle, everyone cooled off and took a little swim. Paulos was much excited at the thought of seeing the female high elfs and wood elfs in wet clothes...bah ! what a loser haha. From here they proceeded to the deepest part of the lair and shortly arrived in the executioners room. After destroying all enemies, they sat and discussed their next move. They decided to make their way back and hunt out the gnoll Ischva (a named gnoll which dropped an expensive robe.) On route there, they encountered a dangerous and fierce battle, unfortunately losing one member. Here they rested, mourning the loss of Budweis. Paulos was given the task of dragging her corpse to the zone. When they arrived at the Ischva gnoll room, they found it to be camped. It was getting late, and after a successful raid they decided to fight their way back to the zone, unknowingly aware of the grave danger that lay ahead.
Arriving at a room, which was close to zonepoint, they thought that two gnolls would be an easy kill to a group of twelve. Little did they know that each room surrounding them contained high level casters that soon joined in when they heard the battle cries of their relatives. Each group had no chance. Whilst the gnoll casters were healing their allies, the skies also rained down blistering the skin of the groups...they realised they were slowly dying.
Luckily some managed to gate out, but among those who died were; Paulos, Quinath, Sabalon, Elventrackr and Caning
Group ass kicking !
