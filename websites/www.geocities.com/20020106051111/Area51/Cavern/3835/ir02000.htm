The Band of the Iron Fist
We are not a player's guild, or any other type of guild for that matter. We are an organization. Our purpose? To preserve the role playing aspect and pure machanincs of this marvelous game. We are players, guides, and GM's from every server...and most of all we are friends. Kill stealing, taking advantage of game bugs and/or exploits, camping magic items without need just for their monetary value, greed, IRL harassment of a personal nature, playing dark races and hiding behind the role play flag for the soul purpose to satisfy personal greed by stealing...the list goes on. We feel these things degrade one of the greatest on-line RPG computer games ever created, and we work against this degradation. We do not seek notoriety, therefore we work anonymously. We know who we are, and we know what we stand for, Player's Rights. Ten dollars may not mean a lot to some people, but we feel that a little bit from everyone can go a long way. So next time, before you insult someone else, steal a kill, or neglect other players because of personal greed - stop and think about what you are doing, not only are you degrading this game, but also the thousands of other people who play EverQuest just for the sheer joy of playing. Never forget, the person behind that troll, barbarian or human IS a real person, and you never know what you may reap from the seeds you sow.
