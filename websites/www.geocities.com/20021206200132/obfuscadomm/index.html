About Consortium Obfuscado


PLEASE NOTE THIS GUILD NO LONGER EXISTS. THIS PAGE IS MERELY FOR A WEBPAGE WORK SAMPLE.

Consortium Obfuscado was started by a core of people, who after being in a few guilds where mergers, server splits, absorptions, etc, didn't work, decided to take matters into their own hands. We decided to create a small, friendly, yet solid and staying Guild, and make something of it.

We hope to grow to about 50 people (accounts, not characters), that are like a family to each other. We have a range of characters from beginning to 50+.

We have formed friendships with many people from various guilds, and have formed an alliance between two other wonderful guilds: Blade of Tunare and Twilight Raiders. This is helping with groups, raids, etc., and getting CO members out there, exploring in new places.

We are not an overly large guild, and are not really into power-playing, but we do enjoy grouping together, helping other guilds with raids or groups and in general, play to have a good time.

Hopefully CO will make a good impact on you, and if you have a character that you think could contribute to our guild, and would enjoy playing with those who want to explore, kill anything in the path, do raids or just get together for a couple hours for some maiming and pillaging, please contact us. We'd be glad to meet you!
