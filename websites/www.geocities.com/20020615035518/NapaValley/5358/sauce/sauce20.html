Honey Mustard Sauce



1/2   Cup          Chicken Broth
1     Teaspoon     Coleman's® Dry Mustard
1     Tablespoon   Honey
1     Teaspoon     Cornstarch
1     Tablespoon   Water
To Taste	   Salt
To Taste	   Black Pepper



I appropriated this recipe from another recipe page on the Internet but I'll be dipped if I can remember the address of the home page. Well to the owner of that home page my deepest apologizes.



Directions:

In a small saucepan, bring the Chicken Broth to a simmer. Add the Coleman's® Dry Mustard and Honey to the broth and mix thoroughly. Combine the Cornstarch and Water and mix well, making a slurry. Add the slurry to the broth mixture and stir. Add the salt and black pepper, to taste, and continue to stir until the sauce thickens.
