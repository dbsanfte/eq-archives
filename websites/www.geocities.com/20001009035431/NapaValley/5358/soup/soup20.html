Donna's Cottage Cheese Salad


1-3.4oz    Package     Strawberry Jello®
1-8 oz     Container   Cool Whip® Topping
1-16 oz    Container   Cottage Cheese
1-4 oz     Can         Crushed Del Monte® 
                       Pineapple *optional*



When Donna first told me about this recipe and the ingredients, I just knew 'she had lost it'. Boy was I every wrong. This is a simple yet very tasty salad.



Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, fold the Jello® into the Cool Whip® Topping. When these two items are well incorporated, fold in the Cottage Cheese and the Pineapple. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour prior to serving.
