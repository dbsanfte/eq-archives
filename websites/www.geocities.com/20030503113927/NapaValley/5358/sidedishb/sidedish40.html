Fiesta Rice


1     Can    Campbell's® Chicken Broth
1/2   Cup    Water
1/2   Cup    Pace® Chunky Salsa
2     Cups   Uncooked Minute Rice®



The Campbell's Soup Company had a tear-out insert in a magazine that featured several recipes for chicken. Since my one stepson loves chicken, I 'appropriated' the recipes from a magazine in a Doctor's office. This was actually a side dish to the Quesadilas that they were featuring.


Directions:


In a saucepan, combine the Chicken Broth, Water and Chunky Salsa and bring this mixture to a boil. Stir in the uncooked Rice, cover and remove from the heat. Let the rice stand for 5 minutes and then fluff it with a fork.

*Note: This recipe doubles and triples with easy by merely doubling or tripling the ingredients.*
