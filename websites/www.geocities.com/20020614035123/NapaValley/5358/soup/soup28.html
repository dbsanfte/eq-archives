Watergate Salad


1         Package     Instant Pistachio Pudding
1         Can         Crushed Pineapple
1         Container   Cool Whip® Whipped Topping
1         Cup         Chopped Nuts
1 1/2     Cups        Miniature Marshmallows
1-12 oz   Container   Cottage Cheese



This is yet another recipe from Donna's personal, "I'm keeping it hidden from you" cookbook. I have seen glimpses of the cookbook and it contains a lot more recipes then she has shown me so far.



Directions:

In a large bowl, combined the Pistachio Pudding, Crushed Pineapple, Chopped Nuts and the Cottage Cheese and mix thoroughly. Fold in the Marshmallows and then the Whipped Topping. Chill for approximately 30 minutes and then serve.
