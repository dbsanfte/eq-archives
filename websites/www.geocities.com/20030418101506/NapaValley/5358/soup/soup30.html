Fruit Salad OR Dessert


1-20 oz   Cup         Pineapple Chunks, drained
1-15 oz   Can         Mandarin Oranges, drained
1-7 oz    Can         Flaked Coconut
1-15 oz   Cup         Cherries, drained and halved
2         Cups        Miniature Marshmallows
1/2       Cup         Chopped Nuts, *optional*
1-8 oz    Container   Sour Cream



This is another recipe that my wife pulled out of her 'Secret' cookbook. She is just giving them too me one at a time in an attempt to keep the suspense building. I love you Sweetie.



Directions:

Combine the Pineapple, Oranges, Coconut Flakes, Cherries, Marshmallows and the Nuts. Fold in the Sour Cream and refrigerate for at least one hour prior to serving. This may be served on either lettuce leaves, as a salad, or in a sherbert glass, as a desert.
