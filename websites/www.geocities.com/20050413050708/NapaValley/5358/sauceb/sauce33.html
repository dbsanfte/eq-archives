Red Lobster® Biscuits



2     Cups       Biscuit Mix
1/4   Cup        Margarine, melted
2/3   Cup        Milk
1/2   Cup        Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1/2   Teaspoon   Garlic Powder



Kristin, a Sweet Young Georgia Peach, emailed this recipe to me. (Well actually her mother did) I have not had the opportunity to thank her yet so this is just one means of saying that. "Thank you Kristin, please keep those recipes coming."



Directions:

Mix the Biscuit Mix, Milk, and Cheddar Cheese together until soft dough is formed. Beat vigorously for 30 seconds. Drop, by spoonfuls, onto a cookie sheet. Bake 8 to 10 minutes, or until golden brown, in a pre-heated 450-degree oven. Brush with the melted Margarine and Garlic when they are removed from the oven.
