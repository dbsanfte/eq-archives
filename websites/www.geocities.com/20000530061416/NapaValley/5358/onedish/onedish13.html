Tuna-Noodle Casserole


1/2 of a Lb.   Package	Creamette® Medium Egg 
                        Noodles, uncooked
1 - 10-3/4 oz. Can	Campbell's® Cream of 
                        Mushroom Soup
1 - 5 oz       Can	Evaporated Milk
1/2            Cup	Sour Cream
1 - 6-1/2 oz.  Can	Tuna, drained and flaked
1/2            Cup	Frozen Peas, thawed and drained
1 - 4 oz.      Jar	Mushroom Stems and Pieces, drained
1/4            Cup	Finely Chopped Onions
1              Cup	Crushed Potato Chips



This recipe came from a book published by Borden®, Inc. in 1990 that we still have amongst all the cookbooks and recipes boxes. This is just one of the many recipes that I prepare from the recipes in the magazine.



Directions:

Prepare the Creamette® Egg Noodles according to the package directions and drain them.

In a medium mixing bowl, blend the Cream of Mushroom Soup, Evaporated Milk, Sour Cream, Tuna, Peas, Mushrooms and the onions. Add the cooked noodles and mix well. Pour this mixture into a greased 2-quart casserole dish. Top with the Crushed Potato Chips and bake in a 350-degree oven until it is hot and bubbly, approximately 35 to 40 minutes. Any leftovers can be refrigerated and re-heated in a microwave.
