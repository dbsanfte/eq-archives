Peachy Cream Cheese Pie


1-6 oz    Each       Graham Cracker Pie Crust
1-14 oz   Can        Sweetened Condensed Milk
1-8 oz    Package    Philadelphia® Cream Cheese, softened
1/3       Cup        Lemon Juice
1/2       Teaspoon   Almond Extract
1-29 oz   Can        Sliced Peaches, drained and patted dry



I have joined several recipe sites on the Internet and part of their attraction was because they daily e-mail recipes to those that request them. This is one of the recipes that I received.


Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, beat the Cream Cheese until it is light and fluffy. Add the Sweetened Condensed Milk and blend thoroughly. Stir in the Lemon Juice and Almond Extract.

Spread about 1/3 of the filling on the bottom of the Pie Crust. Reserving several slices for garnishment, arrange the Peaches on top of the filling and around the edge of the crust. Top with the remaining filling and garnish with the reserved peaches. Chill for 2 hours prior to serving.
