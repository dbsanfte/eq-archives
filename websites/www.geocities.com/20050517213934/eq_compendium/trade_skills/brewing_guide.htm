Brewing Guide

General:

Brewing requires a brewer’s barrel and the ingredients below.  Put all of these items in the barrel and hit the combine button.  If you have new recipes or have the trivial levels of the brews below, please contact me. 

 

Things to Note:

·        Brewing is a very good skill for tailors to have

·        Fishing is a helpful skill for brewers

·        Supposedly, some brews have unique properties

·        Tempers are used in Racial Blacksmithing

·        Oak Bark Tannin and Acorn Oil are used for Wood Elf and Halfling Tailoring, respectively
