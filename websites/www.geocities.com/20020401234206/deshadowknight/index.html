SHADOW KNIGHT BEGINNER GUIDE

   We are the evil warriors of Norrath. Specializing in the dark art of Necromancy as well as weaponry, the Shadow Knight is a powerful mixture of both worlds. The Shadow Knight is 3/4 warrior and 1/4 Necromancer, thus having skills of both classes. The downside to this is that because we are such a powerful hybrid, the Shadow Knight road is a long and difficult one. The Shadow Knight is universally hated and is KOS (killed on sight) in almost all "good" cities - the human Shadow Knight being the only exception, and even they are generally treated with distain by most merchants, who will not sell to or deal with them. We also pay an experience penalty for being a hybrid class...thus making us the slowest-levelling class in the game. Why then put all that effort into a class as difficult as this? Because at later levels, the well-trained Shadow Knight is unmatched for his ability to kill, to create mayhem and terror. The high-level Shadow Knight is a force to be reckoned with and respected for his abilty to deal severe damage in many forms.
     Initially, the Shadow Knight is nothing more than a weakened warrior, lacking the ability to either kick or to dual-wield weapons. At level 6, however, we gain the Bash skill, which is used with a shield. Then at level 9, we gain Spell-Casting abilities, which allow us to weaken and harm our enemy. The Dodge skill is ours at level 10 and at level 12 we gain the ability to regenerate mana faster through the Meditation skill. As we grow in power and skill, so grows our ability to taunt and kill our opponents. We gain many other skills and abilities at later levels.
     Do you now wish still to be a Shadow Knight? Know then that the path you seek is long, difficult and painful. Many who seek this way fall, giving in to their weakness. They choose an easier way than ours. Should you persevere, however, you will become in time one of the most powerful, awesome forces of evil known - The Shadow Knight! Choose well, friend....
