Creamy Chicken Casserole


6     Each       Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breasts
1     Can        Campbell's® Cream of Chicken Soup
1     Package    Stove Top Stuffing® for Chicken
1/2   Soup Can   Milk
8     Slices     Mozzarella Cheese



I found this recipe or a version of it, on the Internet while surfing through the Napa Valley, Cellar community of Geocities. The original recipe called for Swiss Cheese but I substituted Mozzarella.


Directions:

Place each piece of Chicken between two sheets of plastic wrap and using a rolling pin, beat the chicken down to a consistent thickness. This will accomplish two things: 1) It will help to tenderize the meat and 2) it will allow an even cooking. Place the chicken in the bottom of a 9" x 13" casserole dish. Cover the chicken with a layer of the Mozzarella Cheese.

In a medium bowl, combine the Cream of Chicken Soup and using the empty soup can, 1/2 can of Milk. Mix thorough and pour over the Mozzarella Cheese using a spatula to spread it evenly.

In a medium bowl, combine the Stove Stop Stuffing® and the Seasoning Mix package. Sprinkle this mixture over the soup.

Cover the casserole dish with aluminum foil and bake in a pre-heated 375-degree oven for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and continue to bake for an additional 15 minutes.

A second box of stuffing mix, prepared according to package directions, or cooked rice makes and excellent side dish with this meal.
