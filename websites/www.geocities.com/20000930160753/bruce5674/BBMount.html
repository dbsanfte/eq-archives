Thr Butcherblock Mountains and Port Faydwer

The Butcherblock Mountains are a good place for a young Dwarf to hunt. With everything from Decaying Skeletons and Goblin Whelps up to the feared Undead King, you can find plenty of treasure and other items. Walking out from the entrance to Kaladim, you will see two paths, one heading to the left, and one heading to the right. The left hand path takes you towards the crossroads, where the roads to the Greater Faydark (left at crossroads) and Dagnor's Cauldron (right at crossroads) meet. The other path in front of Kaladim leads to Port Faydwer, where a traveler can catch a boat to Antonica. Beware though, as aqua goblins often try to crash the gates. There's rumors of an aqua goblin warrior by the name of Glubbsink in this area. He's reputed to carry a rare weapon. In the plains surrounding this area, you can find various goblins, large insects, and sometimes weaker skeletons. Just the perfect place for a new warrior to sharpen his or her skills! There's also reputed to be a hidden, undead-infested pass in this area that will lead a person to the crossroads area. The area near the cossroads is very interesting. There's many more powerful monsters here, ranging from large snakes up to basilisks and the occasional ogre. The large spiders are my particular favorite. A popular person can sell one spider silk for a gold or more, and the spiders will often have 2 silks on them. A tidy profit from one kill. As you head towards the Greater Faydark from the crossroads, you will pass several excellent areas to hunt, all off to the left side of the road. You will pass a camp of the Butcherblock Bandits first. Be careful of these miscreants. They have no probelm with waylaying travelers for their gold. There are several goblin camps in this area too, with everything from whelps to goblin wizards (VERY DANGEROUS) appearing. There's also the famed Chessboard. This is one of the most popular hunting areas on Faydwer. It's excellent expirience for mid-level warriors, but the only loot of consequence is the Blackened Iron Crown reputed to be carried by a rare Undead King. Besides the king, you will find Undead Rooks, Knight, Bishops, and Pawns. Most carry bone chips, but some are armed with low-quality weapons. Beware the necromantic abilities of the Undead Knights and Bishops! Occasionally while wandering in this area, you may come upon two ogres: Corflunk and Zarchoomi. They have been rampaging through this area for a while now, and Byzar Bloodforge is offering a bounty on both their heads. Corflunk is reputed to be a fairly powerful warrior, while Zarchoomi has been observed casting shamanic spells. Beware!
