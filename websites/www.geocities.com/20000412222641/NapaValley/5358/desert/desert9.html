Apple Crunch


4	Cups	   Sliced Tart Apples
1/2	Cup	   Water
1	Teaspoon   Cinnamon
3/4	Cup	   All Purpose Flour
1	Cup	   Brown Sugar
1/2	Cup	   Butter



A co-worker book marked her favorite recipes in a Cook Book from the Kelley Avenue Christian Church in Oklahoma City and let me borrow it so that I could obtain some additional recipes. This is one of those recipes.


Directions:

Arrange the Sliced Apples in a deep, buttered pie tin or baking dish. Pour the water over the apple slices and sprinkle them with the Cinnamon and half of the Brown Sugar. Blend the remaining Brown Sugar, All Purpose Flour and the Butter with a fork. Pack this mixture firmly over the apple slices and bake in a 350 degree oven until the apples are tender and the crust is brown, approximately 30 minutes. Server warm with Whipping Cream and Vanilla Ice Cream.
