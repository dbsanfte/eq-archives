Cheesy Crescent Tostado Pie


1         Lb.           Lean Ground Beef
1-16 oz   Jar           Old El Paso® Salsa, divided
1-16 oz   Can           Old El Paso® Refried Beans
1-8 oz    Can           Pillsbury® Refrigerated 
                        Crescent Dinner Rolls
3         Tablespoons   Cornmeal
2         Cups          Shredded Cheddar Cheese, divided
1         Cup           Shredded Lettuce
1         Each          Tomato, chopped


I must confuse, I found this recipe on the Pillsbury® Web Site and you can rest assured I'll be returning to that site for more recipes.



Directions:

In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat. Drain off all the excess grease. Stir in 1 cup of the Salsa and the Refried Beans. Keep this mixture warm.

Separate the Crescent dough into 8 triangles. Place the triangles in a 9 or 10-inch pie pan, pressing over the bottom and up the sides to form a crust. Sprinkle with the cornmeal.

Spoon the hot meat mixture into the crust. Sprinkle with 1 cup of the Shredded Cheddar Cheese. Bake in a pre-heated 375-degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until the crust is golden brown. Top with the Lettuce, Tomato and the remaining Salsa and Cheese.
