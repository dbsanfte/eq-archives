Tinkering Guide

 

General:

          Tinkering, basically, is the skill of invention and innovation.  It involves taking various parts, found in either shops or from hunting, and combining them in a toolbox to create useful items that often mimic magic.  Please contact me if you have any other information about Tinkering.

 

Things to Note:

·        Tinkering is a very expensive skill

·        Tinkering is a Gnome racial ability

·        While a Gnome racial ability, other races can do tinkering, but their skill will never increase

·        Gnomes start with a skill of 50, and once they get to level 16, the skill will increase 5 points for every level past 15

·        Don’t stack items when the recipe calls for more than one of the same item
