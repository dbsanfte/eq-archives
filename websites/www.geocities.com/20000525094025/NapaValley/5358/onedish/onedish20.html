Tyler's Frito Chili


1     Lb	Lean Ground Meat
1     Can	Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes
1     Can	Ranch Style Beans
1     Can	Campbell's® Condensed Tomato Soup
1     Medium	Yellow Onion, Diced
1/4   Teaspoon	Cayenne Pepper
1/2   Teaspoon	Chili Powder
1     Bag	Frito Lay® Corn Chips
1     Cup	Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1     Cup	Shredded Lettuce
To Taste	Salt
To Taste	Black Pepper



This recipe came from one of my home page building students, Tyler. He is a very picky eater so when he told me about this recipe, I couldn't wait to make it. Talk about simple, easy and quick to prepare, Thanks Tyler.



Directions:

In a large skillet, brown the Ground Meat and 1/2 cup of Chopped Onions.

Add the Diced Tomatoes, Ranch Style beans (including the juices from both cans) and the Condensed Tomato Soup. Add the Chili Powder and Cayenne Pepper and salt and black pepper to taste.

Simmer this mixture for one hour, stirring occasionally.

Crumble the Frito Corn Chips® in a bowl and cover them with the mixture. Top with the Shredded Cheddar Cheese, Dice Onions and Lettuce.

Serves 4-6 or one Cajun……
