What is Evil?
Evil is defined by the 4 evil races and 4 evil deities of Norrath
--Teir'Dal, Ogre, Troll, Iksar
--Innoruuk, Cazic-Thule, Rallos Zek, Bertoxxulous.

Evil is also defined, for those that fit neither race nor deity requirements, by the factions one chooses. These people may enter the ranks of Midnight Council once they have proven themselves by achieving the following:
--Max faction with Dread Guard Outer (Nektulos Guards)
--Max faction with Dread Guard Inner (Neriak Guards)
--Max faction with Dark Bargainers (Neriak Merchants)
--Max faction with Indigo Brotherhood (Neriak Warrior Guild)
--Bottomed Guardians of the Vale
--Bottomed Faydark's Champions
--Bottomed Clerics of Tunare
--Bottomed Knights of Truth


Inquiries about membership may be directed at Belmonte, or any of the officers listed above.
