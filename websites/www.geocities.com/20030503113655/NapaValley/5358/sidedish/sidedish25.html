Jenny's Red Skin Potatoes


2	Lbs.	Red Skin/New Potatoes
2	Tablespoons	Vegetable Oil
1	Tablespoon	Rosemary
	To Taste	Salt
	To Taste	Pepper



Two young ladies from Michigan were visiting my friends here in Texas and Jenny prepared these potatoes for dinner one night. Turns out she had attended a Culinary Arts School and wow, what a cook.


Directions:


Boil the Red Skin Potatoes until they are almost done. Remove the potatoes from the water and allow them to cool. Once they have cooled, cut them into wedges and Salt and Pepper them to taste.

In a large pan, heat the Vegetable Oil and then add the potato wedges. Sprinkle them with the Rosemary and sauté them until they are heated thoroughly.
