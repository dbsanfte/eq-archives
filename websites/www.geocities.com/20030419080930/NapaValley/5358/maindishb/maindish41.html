Deviled Crab


2	Lbs.       Crabmeat, drained and flaked
2	Cups       Cracker Crumbs
1/2	Cup        Finely Chopped Celery
1	Cup        Minced Onion
3/4	Cup        Melted Butter
3/4	Cup        Heavy Cream
1	Teaspoon   Dry Mustard
1/2	Teaspoon   Salt
1/4	Teaspoon   Black Pepper
1	Teaspoon   Chopped Parsley
1	Pinch      Cayenne Pepper
4 to 5  Drops      Louisiana Hot Sauce



Once again, my surf'in the Internet has yielded another excellent recipe. Well actually, this is the best of several recipes combined into one.


Directions:

Melt the Butter in saucepan and then add the Cracker Crumbs and the Heavy Cream. Cook over medium heat until this mixture thicken and then remove it from the heat source. Combined the remaining ingredients with the Butter/Cracker Crumbs/Cream and stir thoroughly. Place this mixture in a greased casserole dish and bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 30 minutes. This dish can also be cooked in miniature or regular muffin tins.
