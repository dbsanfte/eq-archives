Cocktail Smokey Links Sausages



1-12 oz   Bottle    Heinz® Chili Sauce
1-10 oz   Jar       Grape Jelly
1-16 oz   Package   Cocktail Size Smoked 
                    Link Sausages



This recipe was given to me by a very sweet 'Georgia Peach'. She has helped me a lot with both the cookbook and the home page. Donna, I can't say 'Thank You' enough for all your help.


Directions:

Combined the Chili Sauce and the Grape Jelly in a large saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, for 3 minutes or until the mixture begins to boil.

Add the Cocktail Size Smoked Link Sausage and cook for an additional 3 minutes or until the sausages are thoroughly heated, stirring frequently.

Serve with toothpicks.
