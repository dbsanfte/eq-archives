THE VAULT
This site will be run and maintained by some of the officers of the guild. Everything in and on this page belongs to the GUILD and not individual members. Here you can view and make a request or bid for an item. If you can't find or see what you are looking for, then post a message on the 'message board' clearly stating what specific piece or item you are searching for. From this we can look into ways of aquiring and organising raids to retreive such an item. Guild money is loanable and priority will go to those with good reasons and to those who donate well to the vault. All officers have created 'dummy' chars in different cities around Norrath. These chars will be used to bank all items and keep track of all details associated with their bank. All information will then be relayed back here were members can keep track to. We ask all members to please donate their unwanted items and coinage to the vault. Upon the request for an item, the guild treasurers will discuss the matter and let you know in due time. You also have a better chance bidding for an item rather than requesting it. For more details contact any of us online.
