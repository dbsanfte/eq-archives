Dave's Pork Chop Casserole


4           Lean      Pork Chops
1           Cup       Yellow Onion, 
                      coarsely chopped
1           Cup       Green Bell Pepper, 
                      coarsely chopped
1           Package   Rice-A-Roni® Spanish Rice
To Taste	      Salt
To Tate	              Black Pepper
1 - 14 oz   Can       Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes
1           Cup       Water



This recipe is a direct 'off-shot' of Donna's Pork Chop Casserole. It all got started as I was shopping at my local grocery store, looking for something else when I came upon this style of Rice-A-Roni®. I have a recipe for Spanish Rice and thought, why not give this one a try. Hey, it worked. It's good and it's easy and quick to make.



Directions:

Brown both sides of the Pork Chops in a hot skillet with a little butter. Remove them from the skillet and place them in a 9" x 9" casserole dish. Cover them with the chopped Onions and the chopped Green Peppers.

Brown the Rice-A-Roni® according to the package instructions and then spread it over the pork chops.

In a bowl, combined the Diced Tomatoes with the water and the Rice-A-Roni® Seasoning and mix thoroughly. Add this mixture to the casserole dish. Cover the casserole dish tightly with aluminum foil and bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 1 hour. Remove the aluminum foil and continue to bake for an additional 20 minutes.
