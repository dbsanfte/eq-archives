Here are the classes from everquest. The most powerful groups will be a mixture of many different classes. Some races may not be able to become some classes however.

Warrior - masters of armed combat, trained on the battlefield. Have the mighest hit points of any class.

Cleric - healers, but they can also use their spells offensively.

Paladin - holy knights, they share some strengths and weaknesses of both the cleric and warrior.

Ranger - cross between warrior and druid, they are at home in the outdoors.

Shadow Knight - evil twin of the paladin class, they share some of the skills of both the warrior and necromancer.

Druid - completely at home in the outdoors, they are friends of the animals. They can call uopn nature for help.

Monk - they attempt to shape their body's into weapons. Because of this, they dont use many weapons and armour, but are often blessed by the gods with spells that help their attacks.

Bard - his ability it to play songs, which have a magical effect as long as the bard continues to play. He has knowledge of the warrios, but only for self defence.

Rogue - some say they are assasins, other say common thieves, but they are both. Skilled in many weapons, they can pick locks and climb. But they cannot match a warrior in a full on battle, but prefer to attack from behind.

Shaman - tribal witch doctors, with a primary focus of healing. But also have some offensive spells.

Necromancer - evil, spiritual based spells. They are able to animate skelotons and the dead to serve them.

Wizard - capable of seeing and transport spells. But in offensive spells, no class is a match for them.

Magician - able to call forth creatures to serve for them, they are also capable of some offensive spells.

Enchanter - spells able to chant people, places and things. Abilitys include inhancing a weapon, or charm beings to make them fight for a new master.
