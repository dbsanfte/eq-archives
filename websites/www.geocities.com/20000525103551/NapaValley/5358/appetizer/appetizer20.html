Partytime Drunken Li'lSausages



1     Cup	Heinz® Ketchup
1/2   Cup	Heinz® Chili Sauce
3/4   Cup	Beer
1/4   Cup	Finely Chopped Yellow Onion
1/2   Cup	White Sugar
1     Teaspoon	Louisiana Hot Sauce®
1     Pound	Li'l Sausages or Cocktail Franks



This is yet another recipe I found while surf'in the Internet. This is excellent for Sunday afternoons while watching your favor football team.


Directions:

Combine all the ingredients, except for the sausages or franks, in a saucepan and heat over medium heat until almost to a boil. Lower heat and simmer, uncovered for 5 minutes. Add the sausages or franks and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes until they are heated through. Serve hot in a chafing dish.
