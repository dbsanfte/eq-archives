Welcome to the Aiire Royal Academy.

As the primary insitute of higher learning in the Dal Kingdom, the Aiire Academy offers education and training in a broad range of areas. The mission of the Aiire Academy is to prepare young elves for futures in positions of service to the Royal houses and the people of the Dal Kingdom. Commanded by General Megamillion Aiire, the Academy strives to instile the values of service before self and excellence in its students.


Aiire's Royal Academy is a part of the Guild "Dal Kingdom" on the Everquest server Vallon Zek
