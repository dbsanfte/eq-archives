Cheddar Baked Potato Slices


1     Can        Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/2   Teaspoon   Paprika
1/2   Teaspoon   Black Pepper
4     Medium     Baking potatoes, cut into 1/4" slices
1     Cup        Shredded Cheddar Cheese



I normally 'surf' the Internet on either Saturday or Sunday morning looking for new and interesting recipes. This particular Saturday morning I found a wonderful site and I was able to obtain several recipes.


Directions:


In a small bowl, combine the Cream of Mushroom Soup, Paprika and Black Pepper.

In a greased 2-quart baking dish, arrange the potato slices in overlapping rows. Sprinkle the Cheddar Cheese over the potato slices and then spoon the soup mixture over the cheese. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in a pre-heat 400-degree oven for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes or until the potatoes are fork tender.
