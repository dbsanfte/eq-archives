BIOGRAPHY CONTEST RULES

I. Topics

The idea here is to tell alittle story about your character. Just about anything goes -- however, NO obscenties nor smutty innuendoes will be permitted!


II. Participants

Any Realm player of any level and of any alignment. You may even wish to create a special character for this contest. Only the Bio will be judged -- not the appearance of your character.

III. How to Enter

There are two methods to enter:
    • Send an email to our Judges using the link below
      1. Let them know you wish to participate.
      2. Indicate the name of the character you will be using.
      3. Attach a screen caption of your Biography ("About") Screen from that character {PLEASE use ONLY .GIF, .JPG, or .PCX extensions}.
    • Send a Magic Mail to ALL of our Judges.
      1. Let them know you wish to participate.
      2. Indicate the name of the character you will be using.
      3. Go to Channel 9; announce that you would like to enter the Bio Contest; then arrange to meet with an M&M member so they can obtain a screen caption of your character's "About" Screen. In turn, that member will forward the graphic to our Judges.

Let your creative juices flow. Your biographies will be judged on originality, content, and whatever else might strike the fancy of our Judges. Some of the categories that may be considered are:
    • Most Humorist
    • Most Medieval-like (Sword & Sorcery; Fantasy; etc)
    • Most Poetical or Imaginative
    • Most Realm-related
The above categories are not cast in stone nor might this be the extent of the categories considered. The Judges plan to be very flexible and a great deal depends on the type and number of entries.
