CALLOUS TOES WINNERS

FIRST PLACE

"Of Love's Pain"

by Borimir228

Of all the hurt,
of all the pain,
I never thought I would feel this shame.
The shame of love,
the shame of blame,
the thought of losing so much I gained.


SECOND PLACE

"Baba cool"
                   

                      by Aile

Dawdling on the road like a snail
wearing your misery on your back,
head lost in the smoke of your schilom,
your eyes look where men are not.
What do you see, Baba? Baba cool?

Lost butterfly amidst your own flowers
gathering dreams of happiness
droves of elephants gallop in your head,
ravaging savannas during miles and miles.
But where are you going, Baba? Baba cool?

Immeasurable space is stretching around you,
light years away, you're the only king of kings.
Cosmis energy; all is calm and quiet
are you not too lonely up there?
What are you looking at, Baba? Baba cool?



THIRD PLACE

"Icarus"    

              by Macro

Don't you know, I worry 'bout you, cause you're my special child,
You've been tied close to my heartstrings since the first time that you smiled,
And I'm proud to see you're growing, but I'm scared you're growing wild.

We talk together lately, you're the mimic, I'm the mime,
In emotions and confusions, all the reasons lose their rhyme,
Until the words all flow together, and we're just spending time.

We all make our lonely choices, it's a task you can't refuse,
Everyone's a gambler, and it's an easy thing to lose.
But take a moment to look through the doors you're closing as you choose.

Well don't tell me you're not listening, I do tend to ramble on ...
I'm only trying to help; I turn, and you're already gone,
And you think I'm playing games again, and cast you as a pawn.

Don't you know, I'll always love you, I see the silver and the fire
That burns in you, I know you can fly to the sun and higher.
If I worry, it's just 'cause I've learned -- how easily we tire.
