Charter
We the Greater Druids,

find it upon ourselves to venture forth across this great land to protect and aid all that ask it of us and to promote prosperity for each in thier own way.  Let it be known throughout the land that the Greater Druids hope to make a difference and keep the breath in the lungs of Norrath.  We vow to set an example to those who have lost thier way, and those who have strayed from the path of light.  We have learned through experience, understanding and most important friendships that all races and classes can follow the paths we adhere too.  Your outward appearance is not the determining factor, it is the beliefs inside each which show the kind of person they are and actions which prove it.  It's a common sight to see a group of Greater Druids wandering the countryside or exploring the deep, dark, dungeons of Norrath.  Never be surprised if with your back to the wall, at your most desperate moment, or in your greatest hour of need, there will be a Greater Druid nearby who will lend a hand without question or thought of reward.


With these ideal's set forth we are Honoured to proclaim that the Greater Druids are evermore and always Present, Proud and at the service of the forces of our mother Tunare and the ruling Deities of the Realms.
