Seafood Pasta Primavera



2    Packages   Louis Kemp™ Crab Delights® Chunks
2    Cups	Broccoli Florets
1/2  Medium	Red Bell Pepper,Sliced into
                Thin Strips
1    Medium	Carrot, Cut thin and diagonally
8    Ounces	Skinners® Thin Spaghetti
1    Package	Knoor's® Classic Hollandaise Sauce Mix



Well it's the weekend, my turn to cook and experiment. This recipe came from the label of a Louis Kemp™ Crab Delights® package, and like the U.S. Marine Corp Recon Teams, I "improvised". The Hollandaise Sauce Mix is my idea and it worked out just "right".


Directions:

Cook the Skinner's® Thin Spaghetti according to the package directions.

Fill a medium saucepan three quarters full of water, add all the Vegetables and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and add the Louis Kemp™ Crab Delights® Chunks, cover and simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 7 minutes. Drain.

As the Vegetables and Crab Chunks are cooking, prepare the Knorr's Hollandaise Sauce Mix according the package instructions.

To serve, put an individual serving of the spaghetti on a plate, a generous helping of the crab and vegetable mixture and cover with the hollandaise sauce. Some Cheese Bread, see the recipe under the category of "Sauces, Dressings, Etc.", will accompany this meal just right.
