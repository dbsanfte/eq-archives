Simple Salisbury Steak


2       Cans    Campbell's® Cream of Mushroom 
                Soup, Divided
1       Lbs.    Lean Ground Meat
1/2     Cup 	Bread Crumbs
1/4     Cup 	Finely Chopped Onions
1 1/2   Cups  	Sliced Mushrooms



This recipe comes from an advertisement by the Campbell's® Company in Woman's Day magazine. The date of the magazine is November 26, 1991. Yes I still have the magazine...


Directions:

In a bowl, mix thoroughly 1/4 cup of the Cream of Mushroom Soup, Ground Meat, BreadCrumbs and Onions. Shape firmly into 6 patties.

In a skillet, over medium heat, cook the patties, a few at a time until browned on both sides. Spoon off the excess fat.

Stir in the remaining soup, plus the entire second can along with the Mushrooms. Return the patties to the skillet. Reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer 20 minutes or until the patties are done, turning occasionally.
