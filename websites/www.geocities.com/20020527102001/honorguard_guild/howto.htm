How-To:

Take a Screen Shot

Members are asked to take a screen shot of their characters for their profiles. They may
also wish to know how to take pictures of events for the photo album. The following
explains how.

1) Start Dark Age of Camelot.
2) Find an event or place to take your screen shot.
3) Remove offending text, boxes, etc. Press the Tab key to do this.
4) Hold down the Shift key and press the N button to remove names.
5) Adjust the Camera.
    a) Hold down the ~ key and then use the arrow keys to adjust the angle.
    b) Use the S and X keys to adjust the over-head view.
    c) Hold down the ~ key and use the up-down arrow keys to zoom in and out.
6) Pose. Dance, clap, down, salute, or cheer. What ever floats your boat.
7) Press the - key on your key pad to take the actual screen shot.

Retrieving the Screen Shot

Here's the part that gets a little confusing. After taking the screen shot, you must find it to
send to me.

1) Find the Mythic folder. This is usually right under the C drive. Open it.
2) Open the Camelot folder.
3) Inside the Camelot folder there are more folders, LEAVE THEM ALONE.Scroll down
instead. You will find a file called sshot001.tga or some version of that.
4) Because it isn't a normal picture, you will have to use a differant program other than paint
or other normal picture viewers. I use View Print. You can download this free for 30 days
off the Internet. The address is www.hamrick.com.
5) If you have a program that will open it, check and make sure it is the picture
 you want. If you'd be so kind as to trim it down a little, just so the file size isn't
so big, that would be great.
6) Attach the screen shot to an e-mail addressed to Akane1221@ameritech.net.
7) If it is a picture for the album, feel free to add a short sentence to describe it.
