POETRY CONTEST RULES

(Sorry, every contest has to have a few of these)

I. Categories

There were three* different categories for walking your entry into the contest:

*NOTE: The Judges decided to add more categories.

Click on the links below to see the winners in each category.

FIRST STEP -- first time writers
TENDER FOOT -- hobby and occasional writers
BLISTERED SOLES* -- between hobby and long time
CALLOUS TOES -- long time poets

HONORABLE MENTION* -- to all other entrants as
our "thank you" for participating


II. Content

The content of your work can be about any subject -- your pet cat, the Realm, love, the seasons, fantasy, etc. However, NO obscenties or smutty innuendoes will be permitted!


III. Submit It

A. Please submit your work to our Judges by clicking on this link:

Contest Judges

B. Be sure to include your Realm Character Name, what Category you are entering, the Title of your Poem, and your Poem itself.
