Texas Trash



2           Cups       Cheerios®
2           Cups       Rice Chex®
2           Cups       Corn Chex®
1/2         Box        Mr. Salty® Pretzel Sticks
1 - 12 oz   Can        Mixed Nuts
1/8         Teaspoon   Worcestershire Sauce
1 1/2       Sticks     Margarine
1/4         Teaspoon   Chili Powder



This recipe was published in the "Kelley's Cookin'" cookbook written by the Kelley Avenue Christian Church in Oklahoma City.


Directions:

Combine the Cheerios, Rice Chex, Corn Chex, Pretzel Sticks and Mixed Nuts in a large mixing bowl, mixing them together thoroughly.

Melt the Margarine in a saucepan and then add all the seasonings. Pour this mixture over the cereal mixture and stir. Spread the mixture on a cookie sheet and place it in a preheated 300-degree oven for 30 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes. Cool on paper towels.
