Chicken Enchiladas


1-10oz   Can           Campbell's® Chicken 
                       Broth
2        Cubes         Chicken Bouillon Cubes    
                       for extra flavoring
1        Can           Drained & Chopped 
                       Tomatoes, reserve 
                       the juice
1        Can           Drained Rot-Tel® Mild 
                       tomatoes & Green Chiles, 
                       reserve the juice
1        Bag           Frozen Corn (Optional)
2        Tablespoons   Butter
2        Tablespoons   Vegetable Oil
1        Medium        Minced Yellow Onion
1/2      Teaspoons     Cayenne Pepper
1        Teaspoon      Chili Powder
1/4      Cup           Flour
1        Cup           Whipping Cream
1/2      Cup           Sour Cream
4        Cups          Cooked, SHREDDED Chicken
3        Cups          Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1        Teaspoon      Cornstarch
To Taste               Salt
To Taste               Black Pepper

5        Each          Tortilla Shells



I was telling a Young Lady that I work with about my cook book and she asked to see it. She copied several of the recipes and in return gave me this one. (Note: When ever you are preparing Mexican food, always shred the meat by hand, trust me, it makes all the difference in the world.)



Directions:

In a large pot, bring the Chicken Broth, Reserved Tomato Juice, Reserved Rot-Tel Juice, Chicken Bouillon Cubes and the Tomatoes to a boil. Turn the heat to low and let it simmer, covered. If your are going to use the corn, added it to the pot before bring it to a boil.

In a large skillet, heat the Butter and Vegetable Oil on medium hight heat. Add the Minced Yellow Onions and saute for approximately 4 minutes, stirring constantly. Add the Rot-Tel Tomatoes & Green Chiles and cook an additional 2 minutes. Add the Cayenne Pepper and Chili Powder and stir well. Slowly add the Flour, blending until it is mixed thoroughly. Add the Whipping Cream and cook on low heat until the mixture has thickened. *Note: this should be a very thick mixture.*

Ladle 1 cup of the stock mixture into the skillet and stir. Pour the contents of the "skillet" into the stock. Slowly stir in "3" cups of the Shredded Cheddar Cheese, the Sour Cream and the Cornstarch. Cook for 5 minutes and then add the Shredded Chicken. Cook for an additional 5 minutes to heat thoroughly. Add the Salt and Black Pepper to taste.

Spoon some of the mixture into either a corn or flour Tortilla shell, roll it up and place it in a baking dish. Once all the tortilla shells are filled and placed in the baking dish, pour the remaining mixture over the top and bake at 350 degress for 20-30 minutes. Sprinkle the remaining cup of Shredded Cheddar Cheese over the top of the enchiladas and bake for an additional 5 minutes.

(HINT: To make the tortillas shells easier to work with, place a small bowl of water in the microwave oven and heat the water for 15 seconds. Then place a single tortillas shell in the microwave and heat it for 15 seconds. Immediately put the stuffing in the shell and roll it up.)
