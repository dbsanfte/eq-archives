Potatoes and Broccoli Au Gratin


2     Lb.        Potatoes, peeled and halved
1     Cup        Chopped Fresh Broccoli
1/2   Cup        Chopped Onion
1/2   Cup        Ricotta Cheese
1/2   Teaspoon   Dried Dill
1/2   Teaspoon   Salt
1/8   Teaspoon   Ground Red Pepper
1     Cup        Sour Cream
3/4   Cup        Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese



I carry an entire copy of my cookbook, on diskette, in my brief case, just in case I get to type up a new recipe at work. I also carry copies of several new recipes that I have tried, again, in case I get a break at work and get a chance to type up the recipe. This is one of those recipes and for the life of me, I can't remember where I got it. My best guess is the Internet.


Directions:


Cook the Potatoes in boiling water for 15 to 20 minutes or until they are tender. Drain them, reserving 1 cup of the cooking liquid, and return them to the pot along with the 1 cup of cooking liquid. Mash with a potato masher until they are slightly chunky. Add the Broccoli, Onion, Ricotta Cheese, Dill, Salt, Red Pepper, and Sour Cream to the potatoes and stir thoroughly.

Coat a 7" x 11" baking dish with cooking spray and spoon the potato mixture into it. Bake in a pre-heated 375-degree oven for 35 minutes. Sprinkle with the Shredded Cheddar Cheese and bake and additional 5 minutes or until the cheese melts.
