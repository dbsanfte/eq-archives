Darkforge Armor Quest Items
More will be added to this section as I compile the complete information for the quests. The quests are given in the Temple of Solusek Ro off Lavastorm and are given by either of two undead knights in the temple (depending on which armor piece you are questing. When handing in quest items, be VERY CAREFUL to hand the items in to the correct undead knight as a mistake here will cost you all your hard work! I will have a list of which items come from which knight later. All I have for sure at this point is that the Darkforge Helm comes from the knight on the LEFT (facing the knights).
