Dear Bill Hicks Fan,

It's 1997 and I have good news for all of you... Bill's final words are going to be heard.

RYKODISC will release all four Bill Hicks CD's February 25th. It is my opinion that all you hard core Hicks fans should run {not walk} to buy RANT in E Minor. After editing Rant, I went through every Hick's production ever made, and they all pale in comparison. The HBO specials are like easy listening after hearing Rant all summer. We taped ten shows which in my head are Bill's most fearless, uncompromising work ever. For those of you who are not emotionally ready to hear about Barbara Bush's flaccid labia lips, get Arizona Bay. Arizona is Bill's comedic Dark Side of the Moon. We scored and performed music under a brilliant 50 minutes of stand up. Some of my favorites include Charlie Hodge - the man that brought Elvis his scarves and water; Bill's plea of logic to fundamental Christians "If the world is only 12000 years old and the Bible covers it all, how come they don't mention dinosaurs? Did God put fossils everywhere to fool us?" RYKODISC will also be reissuing Relentless & Dangerous. You can buy them separately, or the first 100 people that buy all four, will receive a free video.

The other news is that I'm setting out to make a feature film about Bill. The film will be much more than just a documentary, but a story of how Bill affected an entire generation. There will be some reenactment scenes, but the actor occasionally portraying Bill will always be obscured while we hear his real voice. We will mostly be using archive footage, because, let's face it, there just isn't any actor who could play him.

Anyone out there that has any rare Bill footage should contact me. It is my hope that the bands TOOL, Radio head & Rage Against the Machine, along with many close friends and partners of Bill will participate. I'm grateful to have the renowned Austin film maker Rick Linklater on board as production consultant along with many other talented individuals such as {director} Tommy Pollatta, [writer] Ken Lieck,{ex comedy partner of Bill's} Dwight Slade & {musician /Attorney} Courtney Anderson. I will keep you all posted, as this project will take years.

As in the past you can still order some tapes through the mail. "Sane Man" is Bill's first stand up comedy concert, filmed in 89. This show helped him land his first HBO special. Most Hick's fans agree that this is Bill in his most fierce form. One of Bill's favorite projects, "Ninja Bachelor Party" is the first ever ''Karate Opera''. It is the story of a young boy addicted to Robitussin, who dreams of one day becoming a ninja warrior. The film stars Bill, yours truly, and took almost ten years to complete. Before Bill's death, we were filming a sequel in San Francisco. "Marble Head Johnson" another figment of Bill's imagination, is a ten song cassette that includes the hit "Chicks dig Jerks & Wizards have Landed".

Other projects that are now available are "On the 7th Day" Bill at the Branch Davidian Compound on the seventh day of the siege, "The Sacred Cow Halloween Special" a two hour compilation of films & videos featuring Bill & friends taking phone calls in Hell. "SEA MAN", Sacred Cow's underwater rockumentary, See humans share hallucinogenic drugs with Dolphins, and a Shark feeding that turns into a frenzy. The film is narrated by Clarence Mumford {Ninja Bachelor Party} Bill does not appear in this one, but I do.

The Austin Chronicle did a great story about Bill {Over due Bill} it is on line at {October-4, 1996 volume 16, no. 5 } also look for the Arena story }The King and Queen of cable access}Arena{July 96 issue, Vol. 7, no. 3} & the RYKODISC announcement of the albums release.

Well that's it for now, hope you all enjoy Rant in E Minor & Arizona Bay and watch for a record release party in a town near you. I also recommend buying the new TOOL album AENIMA; not just because there is a picture of Bill and a 13 minute song "Third Eye" {which is about the war on drugs} and features sound bites of Bill; but because it's the freshest heaviest sound since "Never Mind". I had the pleasure of getting to hang with them after their show in Houston, and let me tell you, they are the most focused unpretentious guys around.
