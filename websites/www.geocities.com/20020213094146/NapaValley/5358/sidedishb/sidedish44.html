John's Turkey Dressing


24          Each          Biscuits
1           Each          7" round, 2" deep
                          Loaf of Cornbread
6           Large         Eggs, slightly beaten
2           Lbs.          Onions, chopped
6           Ribs          Celery, chopped
1           Cup           Roasted Pecans, chopped
1 1/2       Tablespoons   Poultry Seasoning
1           Tablespoon    Sage
2-14.5 oz   Cans          Chicken Broth
To Taste                  Salt
To Taste                  Black Pepper
                          Pam® Vegetable Oil Spray



My wife's father gave this recipe to me. When the kids moved from Georgia, and didn't have this with Thanksgiving Dinner they were pretty upset. You can bet I learned how to make it before Christmas Dinner though.


Directions:


In a large bowl, finely crumble the Biscuits and the Cornbread. Add the Eggs, Onions, Celery, Pecans, Salt and Black Pepper to the bowl and mix. Next add the Poultry Seasoning and the Sage. Stir in the Chicken Broth and mix thoroughly.

Spray two 11"x17"x1" sheet cake pans with the Pam® Vegetable Oil. Spread the dressing mixture evenly in the two pans. Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 45 minutes. Allow the dressing to cool for approximately 5 minutes and then cut into 3" squares.
