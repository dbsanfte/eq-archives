Shrimp Wrapped in Bacon



1        Lb            26-30 Count Shrimp
15       Slices        Lean Back (about 1 lb)
1-14oz   Can           Pineapple Chucks
1/4      Cup           Sweet'n Sour Sauce
1/3      Cup           Picante Sauce
3        Tablespoons   White Wine
Toothpicks



Just think, until I got on the internet, I thought the only place to find recipes was on the back of soup cans in the grocery store. The Internet has allowed me to shop for recipes without every leaving my favorite chair.


Directions:

Cut bacon slices in half. Partially cook bacon slices on a shallow pan at 400° for 3 minutes (or microwave on high in two batches for 2 to 3 minutes each); drain on paper towels.

Select 15 pineapple chunks and cut each piece in half. Place pineapple piece and 1 shrimp on each half slice bacon. Wrap bacon around the two and secure with a toothpick. Repeat.

Combine sweet'n sour sauce, picante sauce, and wine. Stir well and brush on each appetizer and then place them on a baking sheet. Bake in a 425° oven for approximately 7-8 minutes. Replace the toothpicks with fresh toothpicks and place on serving platter. Garnish with pineapple chunks and serve remaining sauce on the side, if desired.
