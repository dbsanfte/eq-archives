7 Cup Salad


1   Cup   Nuts (Your choice)
1   Cup   Cottage Cheese
1   Cup   Sour Cream
1   Cup   Pineapple
1   Cup   Fruit Cocktail
1   Cup   Coconut Flakes
1   Cup   Cherries



It doesn't take an MIT graduate to figure out where the name of this recipe came from. I haven't counted up the number of recipes my wife sprung on me the other night, but this is one of them. I have a feeling she is keeping several more in reserve for when I get upset because I can't find any new recipes that really perks my interest.



Directions:

Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl and mix thoroughly. Refrigerate the mixture for 2 to 3 hours before serving.
