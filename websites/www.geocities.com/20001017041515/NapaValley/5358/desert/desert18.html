Ginger Snaps


3/4	Cup        Crisco® Shortening
1	Cup        Brown Sugar
1	Large      Egg
1/4	Cup        Molasses
2	Cups       All Purposes Flour
1/2	Teaspoon   Salt
1	Teaspoon   Cinnamon
1	Teaspoon   Crushed Cloves
1	Teaspoon   Ginger
1 1/2	Teaspoons  Baking Soda

1	Cup        White Sugar



A co-worker made these cookies and brought them to work. He offered me one and once I tasted it I had to have the recipe. By the end of the day, I must have eaten 4 or 5 of them. Just wish I could have had a tall, cold glass of milk to go with them.


Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, combine the Shortening, Brown Sugar and the Egg. Cream these until they are fluffy.

Add the Molasses, Flour, Salt, Cinnamon, Cloves, Ginger and Baking Soda to this mixture and mix thoroughly. Roll the dough into small balls and then roll the balls in the White Sugar. Place the balls on a greased cookie sheet and bake for 7 to 10 minutes (7 to 8 minutes is recommended) in a preheated 350-degree oven.

Remove the cooked cookies from the cookie sheet and allow them to cool on a wire rack.
