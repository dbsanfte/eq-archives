Baked Ham



1       3 Pound       Hormel™ Black Label™ 
                      Fully Cooked Ham
1       Can           Beer
1       Tablespoon    Brown Sugar
12      Whole         Cloves



Normally a recipe is handed down from Grandmother to Mother to Daughter. This recipe worked in reverse. Now, my wife's Grandmother, Dana, who I might add is a Tea-Toddler, actually buys a six pack of beer just to make this Baked Ham and she saves the remaining 5 cans just for me. Thanks Dana!


Directions:

Place the Fully Cooked Ham in a shallow baking dish and score the top of the ham both across the width and the length. Put a clove in the intersections of the scores. Pour half of a can of beer over the ham and then bake it in a 350-degree oven for 30 minutes, adding beer as needed.

At the end of 45 minutes, cover the ham with the Brown Sugar and baste the ham with the hot beer in the bottom of the baking dish. Cook for an additional 30 to 45 minutes, adding beer as needed.

Remove the ham from the oven and allow it to sit at room temperature for 5 to 10 minutes prior to slicing it.
