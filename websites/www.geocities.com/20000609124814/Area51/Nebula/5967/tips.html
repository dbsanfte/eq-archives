Game Tips

Fight by a zone line or guards in case you get in trouble.

Buy backpacks for extra inventory space, including the bank.

Always stock food and water.

Ruined hides + pattern in sewing kit=patchwork clothes.

Practice your skills often, especially sense heading.

Harm Touch only comes once a day, use it wisely.

Keep a fire beetle eye in your main inventory for light until you can afford a lightstone.

Decaying skeletons have good cash and weapons for newbies.

Use taunt skill to distract monsters from weaker party members.

A cleric can make a group into a killing machine if they use their mana for healing and not attacking.

Keep track of where you are, use the paths or landmarks as guides.

Swimming works better in first person view.

Runes and certain sites spawn creatures, be careful when sight seeing.

Kill, Loot, Sell, Buy, Repeat.

Store a backup weapon and such in bank in case of death where you can't loot your body.
