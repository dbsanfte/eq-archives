Sweet & Sour Sauce



1        Can           Campbell's® Chicken Broth
3        Tablespoons   Cornstarch
1        Medium        Yellow Onion, Quartered
1        Medium        Green Pepper cut into 
                       2-inch long strips
1/4      Cup           Sugar
1/4      Cup           White Vinegar
1-8 oz   Can           Chunked Pineapples, Undrained



This recipe was given to me by a co-worker in exchange for my Cheese Dip in Rye bread recipe.



Directions:

In a mixing bowl, combine the Cornstarch and the Chicken Broth and mix until smooth. Set aside.


IIn a skillet, over medium heat, add the Chucked Pineapples, Sugar, Vinegar, Onions, and Green Pepper Strips. Heat to a boil. Stir in the cornstarch mixture and cook until the mixture boils and thickens, stirring constantly.
