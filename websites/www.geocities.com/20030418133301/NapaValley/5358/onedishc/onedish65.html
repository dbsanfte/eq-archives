Crab & Hollandaise Sauce


2        Pieces    Sourdough Bread
1-5 oz   Can       Shredded Crab Meat, drained
                   Butter
1        Package   Knorr® Hollandaise Sauce
1        Cup       Milk
1/2      Stick     Butter


Listening to my boss tell of a crab dish that he had in St. Louis, Missouri and also in Hartford, Connecticut, I concocted this recipe. I am waiting for him to try it and tell me how close I came.



Directions:

Prepare the Hollandaise Sauce according to the package instructions.

Spread butter on the 2 pieces of Sourdough Bread. Spread 1/2 of the can of crab meat on each piece of the bread and then place several small pats of butter on top of the crab meat. Put the bread on a cookie sheet and place under a broiler for 2 to 3 minutes or until the edges of the bread start to brown and the butter has melted.

Put each slice of bread on a plate and cover with a small ladle of the Hollandaise sauce.
