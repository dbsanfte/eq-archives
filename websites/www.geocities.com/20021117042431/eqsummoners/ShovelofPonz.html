Sovel of Ponz
    The Shovel of Ponz is the focus item for the Magicians Earth Elemental.  This item raises the elementals physical stats where they are lacking (i.e. Agility) as well as rasing it's Armor Class.
Home
Earth Pet
This item can be obtained through a quest by talking to Vira in the Temple of Solusek Ro, Located in Lavastorm.
