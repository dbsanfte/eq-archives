Manicotti


12          Each      Manicotti Shells
1           Lb        Ricotta Cheese
1/4         Lb        Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
1/4         Lb        Shredded Romano Cheese
1/2         Cup       Parmesan Cheese
1/2         Cup       Seasoned Bread Crumbs
1/4         Cup       Parsley
1/4         Teaspoon  Salt
2           Lb        Ground Meat
2-30 1/2oz  Jars      Spaghetti Sauce
1/4         Cup       Parmesan Cheese (Topping)



I was positive this recipe came from one of my wife's Italian friends but while "proof" reading this recipe, she informed me that it came from the "Food" section of a Canton, Ohio newspaper.



Directions:

Cook the Manicotti shells according to the package instructions. (NOTE: if you add a few drops of vegetable oil to the water the shells will not stick together)

Combine the Ricotta Cheese, Romano Cheese, Mozzarella Cheese, Bread Crumbs, 1/2 cup of the Parmesan Cheese, Parsley and Salt in a large bowl and mix thoroughly. Stuff the cooked shells with the cheese mixture.

Brown the Ground Meat and combined it with the 2 jars of Spaghetti Sauce in a large pot. Simmer the sauce for approximately 15 minutes.

Put 1/3 of the Spaghetti Sauce in a 3 quart baking dish and place the stuffed shells on top of the sauce. Cover the shells with 1/3 of the Spaghetti Sauce. The remainder of the Spaghetti Sauce should be served on the side. Bake the Manicotti in a 350 degree oven for 25 minutes. Sprinkle with Mozzarella and Parmesan Cheese and bake for an additional 5 minutes.
