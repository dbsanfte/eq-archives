Highkeep - Hidden Chambers


Highkeep Hold, the city located within Highhold Pass, is ruled over by the shady Carson McCabe. Without going into a lot of historical detail, I recommend you plan an outing here one day and explore the interesting 'hidden chambers' on the third floor of the castle. Also on the third floor is where the royal family resides -- you can find Carson McCabe, his Erudite wife Lady McCabe, and also Carson's not-so-secret paramour, Mistress Anna. Occasionally Princess Lenya also appears upstairs, though she isn't in the main section of the floor with the others; she, too, is in the hidden chambers. Do not confuse her with the illustrious 'Princess Lenia' in the jails of the Highkeep basement. That princess is a phony.

Whatever mystery surrounds the royal family, it has not yet been fully discovered. I assume, since some of the text when I spoke with the NPCs appeared to be broken, that this is because the quest is not in working order. However, maybe it just takes the right person with a new idea, to open wide the secrets contained here.

To my knowledge, most races should be able to roam the Keep freely, but please check before entering there and make sure you will not regret it.

As I mentioned, the secret chambers are on the third floor of the castle. Once you enter the main castle hall (marked by two large white pillars), take your second left to get to the upper floors of the castle.
