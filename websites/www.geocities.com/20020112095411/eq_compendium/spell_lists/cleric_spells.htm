Cleric Spells

General:

          Clerics are the best healers in the game, as a result, their spells are based on healing, buffing, curing, along with a few other utility spells.  Clerics have a few offensive spells, but in higher levels these spells will be practically useless, except against Undead.  If you have any other information about Cleric Spells, please contact me.
