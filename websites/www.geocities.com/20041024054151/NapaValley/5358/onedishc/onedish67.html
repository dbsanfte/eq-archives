Muffuletta


1/4   Cup          Green Salad Olives
3/4   Cup          Black Olives
1/4   Cup          Chopped Onions
1     Tablespoon   Chopped Peppercini Salad Peppers
1/4   Teaspoon     Mashed Garlic
1/4   Teaspoon     Black Pepper
1/2   Teaspoon     Louisiana Hot Sauce
1/4   Cup          Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/4   Lb.          Sliced Ham
1/4   Lb.          Sliced Salami
1/4   Lb.          Sliced Provolone Cheese
4     Each         Torpedo/Hoagie Rolls


This sandwich originated in New Orleans and it shows the influence that the Italians had on the area. My wife and I first experienced this sandwich while celebrating our first wedding anniversary in Shreveport, La. We plan to return to Shreveport every year, on our anniversary, but not to enjoy this sandwich, but to visit our money that we left in the gambling casinos there....LOL!!!!



Directions:

In a medium bowl, combined the Green Olives, Black Olives, Onions, Peppercini Salad Peppers, Garlic, Black Pepper, Hot Sauce and the Olive Oil and mix thoroughly.

Cut the hoagie buns in half, lengthwise, and spread 2 to 3 tablespoons of the olive mixture on the bottom half. Top the mixture with the Sliced Salami, Ham and Provolone Cheese. Cover with the top of the bun and cut in half. For an added touch, use a toothpick to secure a Pickled Round Pepper to the top of each half.
