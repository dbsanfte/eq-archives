French Fried Onion Rings



1   Medium        Yellow Onion
3   Large         Eggs
3   Tablespoons   Corn Starch




On my days off while working as a cook at my Mom & Dad’s restaurant, my wife and I would visit her mother. I’m not saying that she put her other daughters up to asking me to cook for them while I was there, but I’m not saying she didn't either. This was, and I guess still is, one of their favorites.


Directions:


Peel the Onion, cutting the top and bottom off of it, and then cut the onion into rings. The size of the rings is up to the individual.

Put the eggs and a handful of onion rings into a small sauce pan. Cover this mixture with a light dusting of Corn Starch and mix well. Place each onion ring into a hot Deep Fat Fryer and cook them until they are Golden Brown.

Note: This recipe may leave your cooking oil a little mess.
