Tyler's Chicken Quesadilas


1         Lb.         Skinless/Boneless Chicken
                      Breasts, cubed
1         Can         Cambell's® Cheddar Cheese Soup
1/2       Cup         Pace® Chunky Salsa
10        Each        8" Flour Tortillas
1-16 oz   Container   Sour Cream
1-16 oz   Jar         Pace® Chunky Salsa


I found this recipe while waiting in a Doctors office. It was on a recipe insert in a magazine, which I 'appropriated' while no one was looking. Tyler requested that it be served with Sour Cream and Salsa on the side.



Directions:

Cook the cubed Chicken in a nonstick skillet until it is done and all the juices have evaporated, stirring often. Add the Cheddar Cheese Soup and the Salsa to the skillet and heat thoroughly.

Spread approximately 1/3 cup of the chicken mixture on half of each tortilla, to within 1/2" of the edge. Moisten the edge with water, fold the tortilla in half and seal. Place the tortillas on two baking sheets and bake in a pre-heated 425-degree oven for approximately 5 minutes or until they are hot.

Serve the tortillas with Sour Cream and Chunky Salsa on the side.
