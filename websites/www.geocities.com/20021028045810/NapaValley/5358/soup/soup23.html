Apple Snow Salad


1-8 3/4 oz   Can           Crushed Pineapples
2            Large         Eggs, beaten
1/2          Cup           Sugar
1/4          Cup           Water
3            Tablespoons   Lemon Juice
1            Dash          Salt
2            Cups          Diced, Unpealed Apples
1/2          Cups          Chopped Walnuts
1            Cup           Whipping Cream



My wife brought this recipe home from work. Now I even have her getting recipes for us to try and include in the cookbook and on the home page, provided they are good and we like them of course.



Directions:

In a large saucepot, combined the Pineapples, Eggs, Sugar, Lemon Juice and Water. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens. Transfer the mixture to a large bowl and chill thoroughly. Once chilled, stir in the Apples and the Walnuts.

Whisk the Whipping Cream until soft peaks are formed. Fold the cream into the apple mixture and freeze until firm.
