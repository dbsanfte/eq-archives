Saucy Penne 'n Three Cheese Bake


1/2       1 Lb. Package   Penne Pasta
1/2       Lb.             Lean Ground Meat
1-26 oz   Jar             Spicy Tomato & Pesto
                          Pasta Sauce
1-15 oz   Container       Ricotta Cheese
8         Ounces          Shredded Mozzarella
                          Cheese, divided
1/4       Cup             Grated Parmesan Cheese
1         Large           Egg


My Stepdaughter found this recipe in the coupon section of the local newspaper. Obviously they were advertising their products, whose names I have not included in the recipe.



Directions:

Cook the Penne Pasta according to the package instructions and then drain thoroughly.

In a saucepan over medium heat, cook the Ground Meat until brown and then drain off the excess grease. Add the Pasta Sauce to the pan and stir.

In a medium bowl, combine the Ricotta Cheese, 1 cup of the Shredded Mozzarella Cheese, Parmesan Cheese and the Egg.

In a lightly greased 2 1/2 quart baking dish, layer the Meat/Sauce mixture, Penne Pasta and Cheese mixture, forming two layers of each ingredient. Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 20 to 25 minutes, sprinkle the remaining Mozzarella Cheese on the top and continue to bake for an additional 5 to 10 minutes or until the cheese has melted.
