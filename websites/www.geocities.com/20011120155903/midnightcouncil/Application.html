APPLICATION PROCEDURE
(please read before filling out application)

APPLYING
Applications which are received will be viewed by The Council on the following Monday in which they are received. The Council will evaluate each and every applicant. Reputation, integrity, ability, and dedication will be focused on by The Council.

TRIAL-PERIOD ACCEPTANCE
Applicants which are accepted into the guild, will fall into a 30-day trial period. In these 30 days, The Council will watch the progress of new members, and at the end of those 30 days, re-evaluate these members and their behavior and contribution(s) to the guild.

The Council will coach and counsel members for the duration of their trial period, showing and explaining to them, what calibre of players it is searching for.

Also within this trial period, The Council will send applicants on quests they (The Council) design. These quests quite often require the assassination of an NPC of a faction that Midnight Council has sworn to wipe out of the face of Norrath.

RE-EVALUATION : REMOVAL
If, at the end of the 30-day trial period, The Council is not pleased with the performance of a new member, that member will be /guildremove-d and given the reasons for why they were found to not be of the desired calibre.

Any events with which The Council finds completely irrecantable/irrepentable, will result in immediate removal.

RE-EVALUATION: ACCEPTANCE
If, at the end of the 30-day trial period, The Council is pleased with the performance of a new member, the member will be informed of their permanent acceptance into Midnight Council and a revelry shall be held to honor them.
