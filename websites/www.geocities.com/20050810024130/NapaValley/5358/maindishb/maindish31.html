Sausage Stuffed Bell Peppers


1/2	Lb.           Ground Pork Sausage
1	Cup           Chopped Yellow Onion
1/2	Cup           Chopped Green Bell Peppers
1/2	Cup           Chopped Celery
1/4	Cup           Chopped Green Onions, 
                      green part only
1/4	Cup           Chopped Parsley
4	Tablespoons   Bread Crumbs
4	Tablespoons   Grated Parmesan Reggiano Cheese
4	Medium        Yellow Bell Peppers (Green can be                       substituted)
1 1/2   Cups          Cooked long grain rice
To Taste	      Salt
To Taste	      Black Pepper



Again I must thank the Food TV Network, in general for this recipe and Emeril Lagasse in specific. This recipe came from one of Emeril's cooking shows.


Directions:

Cut the Yellow Bell Peppers in half, lengthwise, remove the seeds and the white membrane. Season the peppers with Salt and Black Pepper.

In a large skillet, over medium heat, brown the Pork Sausage, about 3 minutes. Add the Chopped Onions, Green Pepper, and Celery to the skillet. Season with Salt and Black Pepper and sauté for approximately 4 to 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are soft. Add the Cooked Rice to the skillet and mix thoroughly. Season again with Salt and Black Pepper. Cook for an additional 3 minutes, then remove the skillet from the heat. Stir in the Green Onions and the Parsley. Spoon this mixture into the Yellow Bell Pepper halves.

In a mixing bowl, combined the Bread Crumbs and the grated Parmesan Reggiano cheese and mix. Sprinkle this mixture over the top of the stuffed peppers. Place the peppers in a shallow baking pan, adding just enough water to cover the bottom, and bake in a pre-heated 400 degree oven for 30 minutes, or until the tops are crusty and brown.
