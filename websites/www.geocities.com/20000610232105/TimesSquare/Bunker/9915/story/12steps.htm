The Twelve Steps of Everquest Anonymous

1. We admitted we were powerless over EverQuest - that our lives had become unmanageable.

2. Came to believe that a Power (or game manufacturer) greater than ourselves could restore us to sanity.

3. Made a decision to turn our will and our lives and our money over to the care of The Power as we understood Him.

4. Made a searching and fearless moral inventory of our characters.

5. Admitted to our guild leader, to ourselves and to another being the exact nature of our combats.

6. Were entirely ready to have The Power remove all these defects of character

. 7. Humbly asked Him to remove our missed attacks.

8. Made a list of all persons we had let escape, and became willing to hunt them down and kill them all.

9. Made direct threats to such people wherever possible, except when to do so would injure us or others in our party.

10. Continued to take character inventory and when we wanted something we camped for it.

11. Sought through chat and meditation to improve our conscious contact with The Power as we understood Him, praying only for knowledge of His will for us and the power to carry that out.

12. Having had a spiritual awakening as the result of these steps, we tried to carry this message to gamers and to practice these principles in all our quests.

Note that no disrespect was intended to The Alcoholics Anonymous groups or individuals. archmagi@mediaone.net
