Baked Refried Bean Dip



1        Can           Old El Paso® Refried Beans
2        Tablespoons   Chili Seasoning Mix
1/4      Cup           Green Onions, chopped
1        Cup           Sour Cream
1-8 oz   Package       Philadelphia® Cream Cheese
2        Cups          Shredded Cheddar Cheese (reserve 
                       some of the cheese to sprinkle on 
                       the top prior to serving)
6        Drops         Louisiana Hot Sauce



While surf'in the Internet I came across this recipe. My stepson loves Refried Beans and that's the part that caught my eye.


Directions:

Mix all the ingredients together in a baking dish, reserving a portion of the Shredded Cheddar Cheese to sprinkle on the top when the dip has completely cooked. Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for approximately 30 minutes. Sprinkle with the reserved cheddar cheese and serve corn chips.
