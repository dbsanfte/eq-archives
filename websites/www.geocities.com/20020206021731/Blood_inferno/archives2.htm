The Ice Dragon, Lady Vox
Steam rose from her limp body.  The blood from a thousand slashes seeped from her skin into the melting ice.  The smell of burnt skin, scorched from the fireballs and lightening bolts, singed the nostrils of the wounded who rested nearby.  Her glazed eyes stared helplessly into the air above her.

The ice cave was silent.  The air was chillingly cold, yet each hero sweated profusely.

Without a murmur clerics began to bandage and heal the wounded.  Warriors sheathed their blades.  Wizards and Mages rested their minds.

The battle was over and the Ice Dragon, Lady Vox, had been felled.  Members of Blood Inferno along with the help of friends had put her to rest. 

This would not be the last time.  The guild of friends had already made plans to venture into an alter plane to cull the hordes of Ire Ghasts and Ashenbone Drakes.  Their call goes out to those who are brave enough to venture with them to apply within.  The reward is greater than loot, its the bond of friendship and the victory of a great battle.
