Freeport Quest's

Submitted By Shayne Brown:

One of the NPC's in the Necromancer/shadowknights Guild in the Freeport slums will send you to Sir Lucan Deleer for a quest. Deleer will in turn ask you to go out to the inn on the Western edge of the Commonlands and slay a messenger.

This is difficult since you don't know the messenger's name and the lands of the Eastern Commonlands are dangerous. Here's what you do:

1. Got out to the Eastern Commonlands.

2. Using direction sense head West.

3. You will see a large lake and probably a few giants walking around (don't worry, giants shouldn't give you the time of day.

4. Go around the lake to the inn.

5. Behind the inn are two men, one sitting and one fishing.

6. Take out the one sitting with LifeSpike from afar - use your pet too.

The messenger isn't very tough. Actually, the hard part is getting out there and back.

Hail! My name is Thaddeus and I am a lvl 11 DE Necro on Xegony.
Well after seeing all the broken quests for us necros, I have finally completed one.
You receive the quest from the necro on the top floor of lodge of the dead.
Anyway he gives you a letter naming several agents the are employed by the guild.
The one for this quest I used is Kizdean Gix in west commons.
I would urge anyone to be careful of traveling here until higher level unless you know the area well. (lvl 8 at least, just my opinion)
You can find the NPC over by the 2 militia men posted by the toll booth.
Ask him if he has any messages for Neriak and he will give you a letter addressed to one of the members in the lodge of the Dead.
Return the letter for a paltry reward (2 copper) but a nice faction adjustment.
I tried to go to highpass hold but there are about 4 orcs all coned red tombstone to me at the entrance so be careful here.
Hope this helps any necros out.
