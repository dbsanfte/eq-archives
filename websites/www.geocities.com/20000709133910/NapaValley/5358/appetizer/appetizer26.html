Fried Pickles




12            Each    Vlasic® Dill Pickle Spears
2             Cups     All-Purpose Flour
1             Cup      All-Purpose Flour
2             Large    Egg Yolks
3/4 to 1   Cup       Beer
1             Pinch     Baking Powder
To Taste	       Salt
To Taste	       Black Pepper


This is a favorite appetizer in Texas and Louisiana but in order to get the ingredients I had to watch a TV problem from New York City…New York City.????? Actually it came from Emeril Lagasse's cooking show, Emeril Live, which is one of the featured shows on the Food Network.



Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, whisk the Egg Yolks, Baking Powder and 3/4 of a cup of beer. Whisk in 2 cups of the flour slowly to form a batter. If the batter is too thick, add enough of the remaining beer to thin it.


Dredge the Dill Pickle Spears in the remaining cup of flour and then dip them into the batter coating them completely and allowing the excess batter to drip off. Gently place the coated spears into the hot oil and fry them until they are golden brown, approximately 3 to 4 minutes. Remove the spears from the oil and drain them on a paper-lined plate.
