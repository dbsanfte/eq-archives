Carnation® Banana Wafer Pudding


1/2     Cup        Sugar
1/4     Cup        Sugar
1/4     Teaspoon   Salt
1 2/3   Cups       Undiluted Carnation® Milk
3       Large      Eggs, separated (Yolks & Whites)
1       Teaspoon   Vanilla Extract
1       Box        Nabisco® Vanilla Wafers
4       Each       Bananas



This is yet another 'secret' recipe that my wife finally let me see. This is a very simple, yet wonderful dessert.


Directions:

In the top of a double boiler, combine the 1/2-cup of Sugar, Salt, and the Carnation® Milk and heat thoroughly. Beat the three Egg Yolks and then add two Tablespoons of the milk mixture to them, tempering the Egg Yolks. Gradually stir the tempered yolks into the milk mixture; return to the double boiler and cook, stirring often, until the custard thickens. Remove the custard from the heat and stir in the Vanilla Extract.

Line the bottom of and 8" x 8" baking dish with a layer of the Nabisco® Vanilla Wafers. Top the wafers with two of the Bananas that have been cut into 1/4" slices and then half of the custard mixture. Repeat with another layer of wafers, bananas and custard. Line all four sides of the baking dish with wafers.

Beat the 3 Egg Whites until they form stiff peaks and then gradually add the 1/4-cup of Sugar. Beat until stiff peaks are formed again. Spread the mixture on top of the custard and bake in a pre-heated 425-degree oven for 5 minutes, or until the topping is lightly brown.
