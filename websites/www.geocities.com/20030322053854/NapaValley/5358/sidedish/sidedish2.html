Brocoli with Cheese Sauce


1     Head       Broccoli
1     8-oz Jar   Cheez Whiz®
1     Teaspoon   Butter
1     Teaspoon   Lemon Juice



This is just an old family recipe. No one is exactly sure where it came from or just who exactly created it.


Directions:


Cut the Broccoli into single floweretts. Bring 2 quarts of water (or enough to completely cover the floweretts) to a boil. Add the teaspoon of Lemon Juice to the boiling water and the floweretts. Boil the flowerettes until they are tender. Drain the fowerettes and put them into a mixing bowl.

Put 4 to 6 ounces of Cheez Whiz® and the Butter into a bowl and microwave it until it is completely melted. Combined the melted Cheez Whiz with the cooked flowerettes and toss thoroughly.
