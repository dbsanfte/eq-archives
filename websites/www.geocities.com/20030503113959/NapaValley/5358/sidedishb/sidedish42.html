Eggplant Italian Style


1         Large   Eggplant
1         Large   Onion, chopped
1         Lb.     Lean Ground Beef
1/2       Cup     Progresso® Italian Bread Crumbs
2         Large   Eggs, beaten
1         Cup     Shredded Cheddar Cheese
To Taste          Garlic Salt
To Taste          Black Pepper
1-16 oz   Jar     Spaghetti Sauce



This is yet another one of my wife's personal recipes. I say personal because it was in her cookbook. I have got to find that cookbook and look through it when she isn't around. *EG*


Directions:


Cut the Eggplant in half lengthwise and scoop out the pulp, leaving the shell intact. Cook the shell in boiling water for approximately 5 minutes or until they are tender and then drain them.

Chop the pulp and combine it with the Chopped Onion, Ground Beef, and Garlic Salt in a large skillet and sauté the mixture until the beef is browned.

In a large bowl, combine the Italian Bread Crumbs, Eggs, Cheese and Black Pepper and mix thoroughly. Add the beef mixture to the bowl and stir. Fill the eggplant shells with this mixture and place them in a 2-quart baking dish. Pour the Spaghetti Sauce over the stuffed eggplant and bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 1 hour.
