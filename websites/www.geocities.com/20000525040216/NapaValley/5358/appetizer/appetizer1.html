Deviled Eggs with Olives



6     Hard-Boiled    Eggs
1/4   Teaspoon       Salt
1/2   Teaspoon       Dry Mustard
1/4   Teaspoon       Black Pepper
1/4   Cup                Finely Chopped Green Olives
3     Tablespoons    Mayonnaise 
2-3   Sliced             Green Olives



I was just hungry for Devil eggs one day and started searching the many cook books that I own. I found this recipe in one of Betty Crocker's® Cookbooks.



Directions:

Cut peeled eggs lengthwise into halves. Remove the yolks and place them in a mixing bowl. Mash the yolks, with a fork, until they are very fine. Add in the Salt, Pepper, Chopped Green Olives and Mayonnaise and mix well. Spoon the mixture back into the whites of the egg heaping it slightly. Place a Sliced Green Olive on top of each egg. Arrange eggs on a large serving platter. Cover and refrigerate no longer than 24 hours.
