Pudding In A Cloud


2   Cups      Thawed Cool Whip® Whipped Topping
2   Cups      Cold Milk
1   Package   JELL-O® Instant Pudding & Pie 
              Filling, any flavor (4 Serving 
              Size)



I just purchased a cookbook published by the JELL-O Company and this recipe was in it. I had originally found the recipe on the side of one of their JELL-O products a long time ago and had completely forgotten about it until I started reading through the cookbook.


Directions:

Spoon the Whipped Topping evenly into 6 dessert dishes, using the back of the spoon to spread the topping onto the bottom and up the sides of each dish.

Pour the Milk into a medium bowl. Add the Pudding Mix and stir with a wire whisk for 2 minutes. Let this mixture stand for approximately 5 minutes and then spoon it into the center of the whipped topping.

Refrigerate for at least 2 hours prior to serving.
