Considering joining the Hawk Lords?  The process is fairly easy.

FIRST: Read the Creed of the Hawk Lords and see if you can live within those rules.

SECOND: Go to our Forum area and post under the "Recruitment" Area.

THIRD: Contact one of the Officers in game and discuss your application.

FOURTH: Group with some other Hawk Lords so you may be evaluated as a potential       candidate.

FIFTH: Have other members comment on your application and be a bit patient.

   We are currently accepting applications for all classes, especially Clerics.  We prefer that new members be level 30 or above, however this is not a strict rule.
