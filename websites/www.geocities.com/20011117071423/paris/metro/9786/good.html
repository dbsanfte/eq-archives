Circle of Honor
 

Welcome to the Hall of Antitheses; a guild for the world of EverQuest.

The Hall is made up of both Honor and Chaos.  We feel that both are necessary to succeed because where one lacks the other exceeds.

It runs along the same line as Yin & Yang.  Both sides of life are demanded on word.  Here in the Hall, Yin is respected for what it is and Yang is respected for what it is.

 
Rarely will you see a touch of gray!

You are Honor for these Antitheses.  Check out "What is Honor" on the menu below for a more detailed look at Honor.
