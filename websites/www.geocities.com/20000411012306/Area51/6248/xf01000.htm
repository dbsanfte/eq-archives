I have been around EverQuest a long, long time. I have seen what goes on behind the scenes as a Guide, I know many a GM, and a few programmers...I have seen through their eyes as well. I am not saying I am an "EverQuest Expert" by any means, but I DO know the game of EverQuest. I know the difference between rumors and truths, among other things. But before any of this I was a player...just like you, and I still am a player - just like you. Over time I have watched how "The Powers That Be" viewed the importance of players slowly fade away...but I have not, hence the birth of EQ Crossfire. This place is for you, a place to vent your frustrations, voice your concerns and praises, or debate and discuss certain aspects of this marvelous game. I will post voting sections, guest editorials, my personal views on some of the aspects of EverQuest, and answer questions and dispell rumors, honestly, with the information I have available to me and what I have learned over time. If I do not know, then I will work to find the answer. Over all, this is your podium, your soap box. I do this to help preserve the role play aspects and pure mechanics of this game - to help this game evolve into what we all know it is capable of. I hope you would do the same, not just for yourself, but for other players, like yourself, everywhere.
