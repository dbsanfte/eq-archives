Vegetable Casserole


1     Large Can   Veg-All® Mixed Vegetables
1     Small       Finely chopped Onion 
                  (red if possible)
1     Can         Drained and sliced Water Chestnuts
1     Cup         Mayonnaise
1     Cup         Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1/2   Teaspoon    Salt
To Taste          Black Pepper

      Topping

1     Package     Ritz® Crackers, crushed
1/2   Cup         Melted Butter or Margarine



This recipe cam from a very, very good ICQ friend who goes by the screen name of "BLUE EYES". Thanks Myra, this is a wonderful recipe. Kisses!!


Directions:


Combined the Mixed Vegetables, Onion, Water Chestnuts, Mayonnaise, Cheddar Cheese, Salt and Pepper in a casserole dish and mix thoroughly.

Combined the melted butter and crushed Ritz® crackers, mix thoroughly and place this mixture on top of the casserole.

Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 30 minutes. Allow the casserole to cool for approximately 10 minutes prior to serving.
