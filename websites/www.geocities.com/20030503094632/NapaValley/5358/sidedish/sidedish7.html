Brussels Sprouts with Cheese Sauce


1     Package    Green Giant® Brussels Sprouts
1     8-oz Jar   Krafts Cheez Whiz®
1     Teaspoon   Butter
1     Teaspoon   Lemon Juice



This is just an old family recipe. No one is exactly sure where it came from or just who exactly created it.


Directions:


Cook the Green Giant® Brussels Sprouts according to the package instructions. Add the teaspoon of Lemon Juice to the boiling water and the brussesls sprouts. Drain the brussels sprouts and put them into a mixing bowl.

Put 4 to 6 ounces of Cheez Whiz® and the Butter into a bowl and microwave it until it is completely melted. Combined the melted Cheez Whiz with the cooked brussesls sprouts and toss thoroughly.
