Apple Cheesecake


1-9 oz    Each          Keebler® Ready Crust® Graham
                        Cracker Pie Crust
1-21 oz   Can           Apple Pie Filling, chilled
2         Tablespoons   Chopped Pecans
1         Tablespoon    Lemon Juice
2         Large         Eggs
1/2       Cup           Sugar
1/2       Teaspoon      Vanilla Extract
1-8 oz    Package       Philadelphia® Cream Cheese, softened
Dash                    Salt



Leave it to Ernie and the other Keebler® Elves to come up with a simple yet elegant desert.


Directions:

In a large bowl, combined the Cream Cheese, Sugar, Lemon Juice, Vanilla Extract and Salt and mix until well blended. Add the Eggs, one at a time and mixing after each addition.

Place the crust on a baking sheet, pour the filling in and bake in a pre-heated 325-degree oven for 25 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the center of the pie comes out clean. Allow the pie to completely cool and then spread the Apple Pie Filling over the top of the it. Garnish with the chopped Pecans.
