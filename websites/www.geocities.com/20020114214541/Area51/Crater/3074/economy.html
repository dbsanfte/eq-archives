"My Take on the money system in EQ"

 

1 Platinum = 10 Gold = 100 Silver = 1000 Copper

 

I Personally like the way that EQ approached the money system. Its much better then the tired idea of a single currency like just gold pieces. I really only have a few issues on the money system.

 

1. Some Classes have a really hard time *cough* *cough* Cleric *cough* *cough*, were have a extremely hard time finding for money for there spells, there armor, there weapons and there food and water. I played this Character up until Level 7 during my first days of Beta and never kept more then a Platinum piece in my bank. Hybrid classes like Paladins and ShadowKnights go through this but not until later levels Clerics get this gift at lvl 1:)

 

There is only one key to making money in EQ, Salvage everything off your kills. Get a few backpacks ASAP to hold everything in. 1 snakeskin may not seem like anything but a dozen of them make 1 gold piece and on average you find 2 per snake.
