Jewelry Guide

 

General:

          Jewelling is the skill that involves taking precious metals and gems, and then combined them, in a jeweler’s bag, to form various pieces of jewelry.  These pieces of jewelry can then be worn and sometimes has special properties.  If you have any information about jewelling, please contact me.

 

Things to Note:

·        Jewelling is possibly the most expensive skill

·        Jewelers can make enormous amounts of money, as even higher level character will look for certain pieces of jewelry

·        Jewelry takes on magical properties, when you enchant the metal bar, before combination

·        Enchanters make the best jewelers, as they have the spell necessary to enchant metal bars

·        Enchanting does not increase the difficulty or skill level of items

·        Platinum Jewelry doesn’t become trivial
