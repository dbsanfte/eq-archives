Fried Tuna Balls



1-5 oz    Can      Star Kist® Tuna, drained
1         Medium   Potato, boiled and peeled
1         Large    Egg
1/2       Medium   Yellow Onion, minced
1         Cup      All Purpose Flour
To Taste           Garlic Salt
To Taste           Black Pepper
To Taste           Cayenne Pepper



This is yet another recipe from Steve's Louisiana Cajun Cookbook. When I first made this recipe, there were 'no' measurements. Trial and error got to this point. For more of a tuna flavor, increase the amount of tuna to two 5-ounce cans.


Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, mash the potato. Add the Tuna Fish, Egg, Minced Onion, Garlic Salt, Black Pepper and Cayenne Pepper to the bowl and mix thoroughly. Form into balls and roll the balls in the flour.

Fry in hot oil until golden brown.
