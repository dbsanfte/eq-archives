Sawdust Pie


7       Large    Egg Whites
1 1/2   Cups     White Sugar
1 1/2   Cups     Graham Cracker Crumbs
1 1/2   Cups     Pecans
1 1/2   Cups     Flaked Coconut
1 1/2   Cups     Chocolate Chips
1       9-inch   Pie Shell, unbaked



This was sent to me via company email. It had the URL address for PieRecipe.com included in the recipe.


Directions:

In a large bowl, mix the Egg Whites (unbeaten) and the White Sugar. Stir in the Graham Cracker Crumbs, Pecans, Flaked Coconut and Chocolate Chips. Mix by hand until well blended.

Pour this mixture into the unbaked Pie Shell and bake in a pre-heated 325-degree oven until glossy and set, approximately 25 to 30 minutes *DO NOT OVERBAKE*. The center should be gooey. Serve with sliced bananas and whipped cream.

Note: The first time I made this recipe, I used Keiblers® Ready-To-Bake Tart shells that were made with Graham Cracker Crumbs. They were plain and simple ..... AWESOME.!!!
