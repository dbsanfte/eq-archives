Sauerkraut Salad


1 Lb.	Jar	Vlasic® Sauerkraut
1/2	Cup	White Sugar
1/2	Cup	Chopped Carrots
1/2	Cup	Chopped Celery
1/2	Cup	Chopped Green Pepper
1/4	Cup	Chopped Yellow Onion



I can't tell you how happy I am to finally be in touch with my Cousin Sally. She is providing me with all her wonderful recipes. Keep them coming Cuz.!



Directions:

Wash and drain the Vlasic® Sauerkraut. In a large bowl, combine the drained sauerkraut and the Sugar. Cover the container and refrigerate over night.

Prior to serving, add the Chopped Carrots, Celery, Green Pepper and Yellow Onions to the bowl and mix thoroughly.

This is nice to make ahead of time when you are entertaining.
