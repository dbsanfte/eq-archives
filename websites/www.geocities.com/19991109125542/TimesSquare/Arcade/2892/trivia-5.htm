The contest will be held on a gossip channel to be announced about ten minutes prior to the starting time. There will be no entry fee.

Due to the nature of this event, we must limit the contest to a certain number of participants. However, we are planning to hold these contests often. So, if you don't get in the first time, please try again.

THE RULES ARE AS FOLLOWS:

When the channel number is announced, all participants must join that channel. Once our limit has been reached, the channel will become "private" and that contest will be closed to any new players.

Once the contest begins, there will be NO TALKING on the channel except by the Question Giver, Score Keeper, or with permission of either as indicated below.

The Question Giver will state the question only one time. These will be Trivia-type questions on any topic -- not necessarily Realm-related.

Each participant then types out their answer -- ONE ANSWER ONLY per player. The player who gives the first correct answer with the correct spelling wins that round and gets one point. The winner of each round will be announced by the Question Giver.

Please DO NOT discuss the question, the answer, nor the standings of the players during the contest.

At the end of the series of questions, the player with the most points will be declared the winner. In case of any ties, there will be a play off round which will consist of three questions with the same rules being applied.

There will be prizes for more than first place. The exact number of prizes to be awarded will be announced later in the week.
