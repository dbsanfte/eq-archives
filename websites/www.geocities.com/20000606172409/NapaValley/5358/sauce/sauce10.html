Joe's Bar-B-Que Sauce



1-16 oz  Bottle  Kraft Mesquite Smoke Bar-B-Que Sauce
1-12 oz  Can     Beer



I was fortunate enough to be invited to Joe's for dinner one night when he was grilling Country Spare Ribs and he used this sauce on the ribs. They were awesome! According to Joe, the cheaper the beer, the better the sauce. He also told me that this sauce, as long as it's keep refrigerated, would last until it is completely gone. Just store it in a container that has a lid.



Directions:

Combined the Bar-B-Que Sauce and the Beer in a microwavable bowl and "Nuke" it for two minutes. Stir it and it's ready to be used.

Of course, I am going to experiment with it a little Brown Sugar and some sauté Diced Onions. I'll let you know how that works out.
