Home Fried Potatoes



Leftover            Parsley Potatoes
5	Tabs        Butter
1	Tablespoon  Diced Yellow Onions



This recipe is from my childhood days, and please don't ask how long ago that was. The Grey/White hair or the lack of hair, should tell you that. Laughing Out Loud…..or LOL!!!, if you prefer. My Grandma Brown use to make these for me every Saturday and Sunday morning when I stayed with my Grandparents, whom I love and miss very, very much.!!!!


Directions:


The recipe for Parsley Potatoes is available in the SideDish section, so please take a minute and view that recipe.

Slice the leftover potatoes into 1/8" slices.

Melt the Butter in a large pan over medium heat.

Add one half of the potatoes and the Diced Yellow Onions to the pan. Add the remaining potatoes and let them brown. Once one side is brown, turn them and let the other side brown. Continue to turn them until they are golden brown on both sides and heated thoroughly.

I normally serve these potatoes with a Shredded Cheddar Cheese Omelet and it just completes the breakfast. But they are also wonderful for dinner as well.
