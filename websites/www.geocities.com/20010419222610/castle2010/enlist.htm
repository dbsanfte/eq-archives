Just a couple of notes to keep in mind as an person interested in joining the CDF.

  • If you have not looked over the web page in full I highly suggest that you do so before attempting to enlist. This is so nothing is a surprise to you once you are in the guild.

  • Understand that you well be a soldier and although you may move up in the ranks to ask for such a thing is not acceptable. You will answer to those that have been placed above you. You will be a Private in the army. We use ranks and titles because in our experiences with other guilds, the more organized we are the more fun people will have. If you know your place, you will find it more fun to role play. Rank that is earned is more interesting and means more to an individual then rank to is just given.

  • We expect our members to look over the web page as much as possible. If you can get online to play EQ you can look at the web page. Members should at least review the boards to find out anything new that is going on.
