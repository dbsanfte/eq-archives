ULTIMA ONLINE

CIRCLES OF MAGIC

Back to Main Page




The First Circle:


  • Wis Uus (Bird's Eye)
    Casts a bird's eye view of the world. Duration contingent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss, Nightshade


  • Uus Jux (Clumsy)
    Temporarily reduces the dexterity of a single creature. Damage power contingent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss, Nightshade


  • In Mani Yem (Create Food)
    Casting creates one food item at the targeted location. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Ginseng, Mandrake Root


  • Rel Wis (Feeblemind)
    Temporarily reduces the intelligence of a single creature. Damage power contingent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Ginseng, Nightshade


  • In Mani (Heal)
    Healing effect on a single creature. Magnitude contingent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Ginseng, Nightshade


  • In Por Ylem (Magic Arrow)
    Casts a fireball at a single target. Damage power dependent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Nightshade


  • In Lor (Night Sight)
    Creates a magic light that improves the vision of the caster. Those around the mage will not benefit from the effects of the spell. Duration dependent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Spider's Silk, Sulfurous Ash


  • Des Mani (Weaken)
    Temporarily reduces the strength of a single creature. Resistance ability contingent on creature's skill or stamina. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Nightshade



The Second Circle:


  • Ex Uus (Agility)
    Temporarily increases the skill of a single creature. Magnitude dependent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss, Mandrake Root


  • Uus Wis (Cunning)
    Temporarily increases the intelligence of a single creature. Magnitude dependent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Mandrake Root, Nightshade


  • An Nox (Cure)
    Cures poison. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Ginseng


  • An Mani (Harm)
    Opposite of In Mani (First Circle), this spell damages the health of a single creature. Magnitude dependent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Nightshade, Spider's Silk


  • In Jux (Magic Trap)
    Creates a one time explosive trap on any usable object. Trap goes off the next time object is used. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Ginseng, Nightshade


  • An Jux (Untrap)
    Removes trap spell present on a single object. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss, Sulfurous Ash


  • Uus Sanct (Protection)
    Protects a single creature from physical and magical attacks. Magnitude dependent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Ginseng, Sulfurous Ash


  • Uus Mani (Strength)
    Temporarily increases the strength of a single creature. Magnitude dependent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Mandrake Root, Nightshade



The Third Circle:


  • Rel Sanct (Bless)
    Temporarily increases the health, agility, strength and intelligence of a single creature. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Mandrake Root


  • Vas Flam (Fireball)
    Casts a fireball at a single creature, more powerful than that conjured by In Por Ylem (First Circle). Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Sulfurous Ash


  • An Por (Magic Lock)
    Magic locks a chest. Spell Reagents: Sulfurous Ash, Blood Moss, Garlic


  • In Nox (Poison)
    Poisons a single creature. Antidote=An Nox (Second Circle) Spell Reagents: Nightshade


  • Ort Por Ylem (Telekinesis)
    Manipulate and/or use a targeted object without touching it. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss, Mandrake Root


  • Rel Por (Teleport)
    Moves caster to the targeted location which must be visible on the screen. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss, Mandrake Root


  • Ex Por (Unlock)
    Unlocks magical locks including those created through An Por (Third Circle). Spell Reagents: Blood Moss, Sulfurous Ash


  • In Sanct Ylem (Wall of Stone)
    Creates a temporary wall. Must be targeted on open ground only. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss, Garlic



The Fourth Circle:


  • Vas An Nox (Archcure)
    Cures poison on multiple creatures around targeted area. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Ginseng, Mandrake Root


  • Vas Uus Sanct (Archprotection)
    Temporarily protects multiple creatures in targeted area from physical and magical attacks. (Like Uus Sanct, Second Circle.) Spell Reagents: Garlic, Ginseng, Mandrake Root, Sulfurous Ash


  • Des Sanct (Curse)
    Temporarily reduces health, skill, agility and intelligence of a single creature. Magnitude dependent on skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Nightshade, Sulfurous Ash


  • In Flam Grav (Fire Field)
    Creates a Fire Field. All who touch it suffer damage. Must be targeted on open ground. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Spider's Silk, Sulfurous Ash


  • In Vas Mani (Greater Heal)
    Heals a single creature. Magnitude greater than In Mani (First Circle.) Spell Reagents: Garlic, Ginseng, Mandrake Root, Spider's Silk


  • Por Ort Grav (Lightning)
    Casts a lightning bolt on a single targeted creature. Magnitude dependent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Mandrake Root, Sulfurous Ash


  • Ort Rel (Mana Drain)
    Drains all mana from a single targeted creature. Creature's mana will regenerate normally. Most have a strong resistance to it. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Mandrake Root, Spider's Silk


  • Kal Ort Por (Recall)
    Cast on "marked" object to teleport back to location where object was marked. (Silent on whether spell affects the caster only or includes the party traveling with him/her.) Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Blood Moss, Mandrake Root



The Fifth Circle:


  • In Jux Hur Ylem (Blade Spirits)
    Creates a deadly, spinning column of sharp blades. Blade spirits move quickly, tracking the closest moving creature within their sensory field. All that they touch are grievously wounded. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl Mandrake Root Nightshade


  • An Grav (Dispel Field)
    This spell creates a void at the place on which the mage focuses. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl Garlic Spider's Silk Sulfurous Ash


  • Kal In Ex (Incognito)
    This spell must be directed at a single creature. The affected creature will no longer seem overtly good or evil to those who encounter it. The duration of the spell depends on the skill of the mage who casts it. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss Garlic Nightshade


  • In Jux Sanct (Magic Reflection)
    Creates an invisible shield of etheric energy around a creature. This shield reflects the next spell cast on the protected creature back to the mage who cast it. Each In Jux Sanct spell reflects just one spell. It should be used with caution, for not even the mage who casts this spell is immune to its effects. Spell Reagents: Garlic Mandrake Root Spider's Silk


  • Por Corp Wis (Mind Blast)
    Mages attempting to cast this spell should take care to avoid the deception of pride, for Por Corp Wis pits the mind of the mage against the mind of the intended victim, and the less intelligent of the two is harmed. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl Mandrake Root Nightshade Sulfurous Ash


  • An Ex Por (Paralyze) This spell paralyzes a creature as long as the caster's skill can maintain the spell. Some creatures are able to resist the paralyzing effects. Spell Reagents: Garlic Mandrake Root Spider's Silk


  • In Nox Grav (Poison Field) Creates a temporary wall that poisons all who touch it. This spell must be cast on open ground. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl Nightshade Spider's Silk


  • Kal Xen (Summon Creature) Summons a random creature to fight for the caster. The more skillful the mage, the longer the summoning can be maintained. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss Mandrake Root Spider's Silk



The Sixth Circle:


  • An Ort (Dispel)
    Destroys a single undead creature. Skillful or lucky Mages may be able to dispel an elemental or demon on occasion. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Mandrake Root, Sulfurous Ash


  • Corp Por (Energy Bolt)
    Direct an energy bolt against a single creature. Magnitude dependent on the skill of the caster. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Nightshade


  • Vas Ort Flam (Explosion)
    Set off an explosion on a targeted location. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Mandrake Root, Sulfurous Ash


  • An Lor Xen (Invisibility)
    Makes targeted creature (object?) invisible. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss, Nightshade


  • Kal Por Ylem (Mark)
    Marks current location on an object so later the Recall spell (Kal Ort Por, Fourth Circle) can be cast on it to teleport back to that location. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Blood Moss, Mandrake Root


  • Vas Des Sanct (Mass Curse)
    A more powerful version of Des Sanct (Fourth Circle), curses multiple creatures in the targeted area. Spell Reagents: Garlic, Mandrake Root, Nightshade, Sulfurous Ash


  • In Ex Grav (Paralyze Field)
    Creates a field that paralyses all who touch it. Some creatures may resist spell effects. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Ginseng, Spider's Silk


  • Wis Quas (Reveal)
    Reveals any invisible objects or creatures in the vicinity. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss, Sulfurous Ash



The Seventh Circle:


  • Vas Ort Grav (Chain Lightning)
    Casts lightning on everything within the targeted area. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Blood Moss, Mandrake Root, Sulfurous Ash


  • In Sanct Grav (Energy Field)
    Creates a temporary electrically charged field that damages all who attempt to pass through. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl, Mandrake Root, Spider's Silk, Sulfurous Ash


  • Kal Vas Flam (Flamestrike)
    Casts a pillar of fire on the chosen target. Spell Reagents: Spider's Silk, Sulfurous Ash


  • Vas Rel Por (Gate Travel)
    Casting this spell on an object that has been marked with the Kal Por Ylem spell calls up a red, pulsing gate. Anyone who enters this gate will teleport back to the location marked on the object. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl Mandrake Root Sulfurous Ash


  • Ort Sanct (Mana Vampire)
    This spell allows the mage to drink in all of one victim's mana. The mage's mana is replenished, and the victim is left with none. (The victim's mana is then restored normally, however.) This spell is incredibly difficult to achieve, as the level of resistance to it is high. The best defense against it is the In Jux Sanct, or Magic Reflection, spell. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl Blood Moss Garlic Mandrake Root Spider's Silk Sulfurous Ash


  • Vas An Ort (Mass Dispel)
    Vas An Ort destroys all undead in the area on which the mage is focusing. A mage has a better chance of destroying a single elemental or demon with this spell than when using An Ort (Sixth Circle). Spell Reagents: Black Pearl Garlic Mandrake Root Sulfurous Ash


  • Kal Des Flam Ylem (Meteor Swarm)
    Casts a storm of flaming meteors upon the area on which the mage focuses. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss Garlic Mandrake Root Sulfurous Ash


  • Vas Ylem Rel (Polymorph)
    Transforms a creature into another type of creature. Be forewarned - there is a random element in this spell, for the mage who uses it cannot control into what form the victim is transformed. Some creatures may be able to resist transformation. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss Mandrake Root Spider's Silk



The Eighth Circle:


  • In Vas Por (Earthquake)
    Creates a tremor in the earth that causes all mobile creatures in a large area around the caster to stumble and fall. The caster will, however remain serenely upright. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss Ginseng Mandrake Root Sulfurous Ash


  • Vas Corp Por (Energy Vortex)
    Creates a quick-moving vortex of energy that will attack any creature coming near it. The vortex harms (An Mani; Second Circle), poisons (In Nox; Third Circle) and curses (Des Sanct; Fourth Circle) any creature that touches it. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl Blood Moss Mandrake Root Nightshade


  • An Corp (Resurrection)
    Resurrects a human ghost. Spell Reagents: Black Pearl Garlic Ginseng


  • Kal Vas Xen Hur (Summon Air Elemental)
    Summons an air elemental to fight for the caster. The more skillful the mage, the longer this spell can be maintained. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss Mandrake Root Spider's Silk


  • Kal Vas Xen Corp (Summon Daemon)
    Summons a demon to fight for the caster. The more skillful the mage, the longer this spell can be maintained. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss Mandrake Root Spider's Silk Sulfurous Ash


  • Kal Vas Xen Ylem (Summon Earth Elemental)
    Summons an earth elemental to fight for the caster. The more skillful the mage, the longer this spell can be maintained. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss Mandrake Root Spider's Silk


  • Kal Vas Xen Flam (Summon Fire Elemental)
    Summons a fire elemental to fight for the caster. The more skillful the mage, the longer this spell can be maintained. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss Mandrake Root Spider's Silk Sulfurous Ash


  • Kal Vas Xen An Flam (Summon Water Elemental)
    Summons a water elemental to fight for the caster. The more skillful the mage, the longer this spell can be maintained. Spell Reagents: Blood Moss Mandrake Root Spider's Silk
