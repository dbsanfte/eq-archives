Glowing Hand
Glowing Hand is one of the oldest guilds surviving on the Seventh Hammer server.

Our goals are as follows:

Form a guild with people who we like to play with.

Promote fair play.

Help our fellow guildmembers with their quests and in the procurement of items they want.
