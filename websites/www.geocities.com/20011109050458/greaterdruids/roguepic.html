Rogue - Ragebringer
Most of this information is complete, though if you find any details I have left out, please post it or email me with it and I will fix it.
1)     Find Malka Rale in the Qeynos Aqueducts at location +380, -210, +80.  Say "I will help you" to get "Stanos' Pouch."
                   *   This will be done at least 3 times throughout the quest unless a) Stanos is already up or b) you get friends to get it for you.
                   *    Malka's placeholder is "A courier."
                  *    "A courier" will despawn at 8 p.m. game time, then Malka will spawn at 10 p.m. game time in the place of the courier.
                    *    The courier does NOT need to be killed to get Malka to spawn, just wait till 10 p.m.

2)     Travel to North Kaladim and locate Founy Jestands at loc +520., +307, -35.  Pick pocket the "stained parchment top" from him.  He is a rouge GM near the bank.
                    *    He will despawn at 8 a.m. game time and respawn at 10 a.m.
                    *    He will NOT always spawn with the parchment piece.
                    *     If you get caught while PPing then zone ASAP - the zone is close.

3)     Travel to Neriak 3rd Gate and locate Tani N'Mar at loc +650, -1300.  Pick pocket the "stained parchement bottom" from him.  He is a rogue GM in rogue guild hall.
                    *     He will despawn at 8 p.m. and respawn a total of 5 times between then and 10 p.m. (This may be a bug)
                    *     He will NOT always spawn with the parchment place.

4)     Find Stanos Herkanor in Highpass Hold (in secret smugglers camp - must go through underwater tunnel) at loc +10, +325, +45.  Give him both the "stained parchment top" and the "stained parchment bottom" at the same time to receive the "Combined Parchment."
                    *     If Stanos is not in the camp then give Anson McBale "Stanos Pouch" you received from Malka Rale to spawn him.  (Be sure to stand on the table behind Anson when giving him "Stanos Pouch" or Stanos may not spawn - this happens because you may be standing on Stanos' spawn point.)
                    *     If Anson is not in the camp you will have to kill his smuggler placeholder.
                    *     The smugglers are fairly high level and will require a full well rounded 50+ group to kill.  (Although they can be killed with less, it is unsafe to do so.)

5)     Travel to Lake Rathetear and locate Eldreth in the island tower at loc +2600, -550.  Hand Eldreth the "combined note," 100 platinum, and 2 bottles (unstacked) of milk all at once to receive the "scribbled note."  If you don't have at least indifferent faction with him, SNEAK when turning in the items.  Eldreth will then respawn.
                   *     Eldreth is approx. a 4 day spawn (Real Life Days).  He can spawn a bit before or a bit after, maybe six hours, the 4 day spawn time.

6)     Travel to the Plane of Hate to pick up the "book of Souls."  This is a static spawn of 6-8 hrs (unconfirmed) at loc -60, +320.  The book spawns on the bedside night stand in the bedroom located in the top floor of Maestro of Rancor's building.
                   *     Wandering NPC's and MOBs will pick up the book if left lying, so be quick in picking it up! 
                   *     The "Book of Souls' is an 8 hour spawn.

7)     Travel to the Steamfont Mountains and locate Yendar Starpyre (a gnome in an SMR.)  After he has been located, hail him to keep him from running (saying "book" also stops him) and give him the "Scribbled Note" you receieved from Eldreth.  He in turn will give you another note called a "Tattered Note" then despawn.  After you have received the "Tattered Note" from him you must wait for him to respawn.  After you locate him a 2nd time give him the "Tattered Note" and the "Book of Souls" at the same time.  He will then despawn and a level 50 female human rougue named Renux Herkanor will spawn nearby (she quads for 200+ and wields a weapon which procs clockwork poison - a spinning stun spell.)  Quickly engage combat or she will despawn in a short period of time.  After Renux has been killed, loot the "jagged Diamond Dagger" and the "translated Note" from her.  She also drops an off-hand only 1HS sword called the Trochilc's Skean which procs clockwork poison and 2 new pieces of Ravenscale armor.
                    *     Yendar wanders the entire zone so have a tracker.
                    *     Yendar is a totally random spawn spanning anywhere from many hours to a few days (Real Life Time.)
                    *     The "Jagged Diamond Dagger" can also be obtained through a side-quest involving Vilinus the Small (See Side Quests.)
                    *     WARNING: It is said that after Renux is killed, Yendar will respawn immediately after.  THIS IS FALSE INFO - YENDAR IS RANDOM SPAWN.

8)     Return to Highpass Hold and re-locate Stanos (if he is not up see #4 above) in the smuggler's camp.  Give him the "translated note" you looted from Renux and in turn you will receive "a sealed box."

9)     Travel to Kithicor Forest and locate the Dark Elf House at loc +800, +2400.  Give the box to any one of the night spawns there to spawn General V'Ghera.  Pull the General to a safe spot and kill him.  Once he has fallen, loot the "General's Pouch" from him.  Also, if he dropped the rare "Cazic Quill" loot this item as well.  he drops many other things that are not needed for the quest and may be given to those that helped you through the quest.
                    *     If he did not drop the "Cazic Quill" you must obtain it through a side quest involving Vilinus the Small (See Side Quest.)

10)    After you have obtained the "Jagged Diamond Dagger," the "Cazic Quill," and the "General's Pouch," travel to Highpass Hold and give them all at once to Stanos to receive the Ragebringer. (If Stanos is not in the camp, see #4 above.)
