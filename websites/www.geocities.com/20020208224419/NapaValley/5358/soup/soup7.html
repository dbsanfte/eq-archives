Macaroni Salad


6-8 	Ounces         	Uncooked  Macaroni Pasta
2      	Ribs           	Sliced Celery
1/2    	Cup            	Diced Onions
4      	Large          	Hard Boiled Eggs
1      	Can            	Drained Tuna
10     	Each           	Sliced Salad Olives
6      	Tablespoons   	Mayonnaise
4      	Tablespoon      Sweet Pickle Juice
                       	Paprika



This is my wife's recipe which she created by combining my Mother's and her Mother's recipes. Both of the original recipes call for macaroni as the pasta to use but of late we have been using spiral pasta in the recipe.



Directions:

Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, drain and rinse. (Note: If you add a few drops of vegetable oil to the water the pasta will not stick together.)

Dice 3 of the Hard Boiled Eggs to be used in the salad and slice one of the Hard Boiled Eggs for decoration on the top of the salad.

In a large bowl, combined the cooked pasta, Celery, Onions, Diced Hard Boiled Eggs, the Salad Olives and the Drained Tuna.

In a small bowl, combine the Sweet Pickle Juice and the Mayonnaise and mix thoroughly. More Mayonnaise or Sweet Pickle Juice can be added until the desired consistency is achieved. Incorporate this mixture into the salad.

More of the Sweet Pickle Juice and the Mayonnaise mixture can be prepared and put into the salad until the moisture content of the salad is to your satisfaction.
