3 Cheese - Cheese Ball



1        Jar        Old English® Cheese Spread
1-8 oz   Package    Philadelphia® Cream Cheese
1-4 oz   Package    Blue Cheese
1        Teaspoon   Worcestershire Sauce
To Taste            Chili Seasoning (Optional)
To Taste            Chopped Nuts (Optional)
                    Ritz® or Premium Saltine® Crackers

Directions:

Allow the three Cheeses to come to room temperature. Mix them together along with the Worcestershire sauce and form them into a ball, as best as you can. Place the ball in a dish, cover with aluminum foil and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. The mixture will then be firm enough to form into a better ball.

Roll the ball in either the Chili Season or the Nuts, your choice, although neither is really required. Serve with your choice of crackers.
