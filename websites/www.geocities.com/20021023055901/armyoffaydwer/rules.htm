Following are the rules all members of Army of Faydwer must abide by, to ignore these rules with blatant disregard is unacceptable and will result in disciplinary measures.

As with all "Army's" there is a set code of conduct which is expected to be followed.  

In the Army of Faydwer they are:

1.  All members shall obey their superior Officers, and respect their words and wishes at all times.  

2.  If any Guild member has a dispute with another member of the Guild or of an allied Guild, they should contact an Officer as soon as possible, who will endeavor to resolve the situation.  If there is still no satisfaction then they can request a private talk with the Guild Leader.

3.  We are not primarily a Player Killer (PKing) guild.  There should be no killing of another player unless it is in defense of yourself, a Guild member/allied Guild member,  friend or innocent.  PKing is also acceptable during a raid (eg: Dark Races raiding Greater Faydark), or a guild/player which is an enemy to us.  

4.  Absolutely NO corpse camping, nor cross-teaming with Dark races (grouping with intent to PK) is allowed.

5.  All Guild members must display the values that are held sacred to Army of Faydwer at all times, bringing honour and pride upon the Guild - by bearing our tag you represent us to potential members.

6.  As we are an Army, and make some attempt at role-playing, it would be appreciated if Officers and Guild Leader were to be saluted when meet (face to face) in game.  Also please try to remember this with other known Officers/Leaders of Allied Guilds.

7.  Regular checking in of Guild events, news and our message board is requested, to keep all up to date with current goings-on in the Guild.

8.  All rules set out by Verant and common courtesy are to be obeyed without hesitation, allowing everyone to enjoy their time spent in Norrath.

 

 

Officer Only Rules

1.  At present I would prefer all new recruits to be of level 20+, but as this is not always possible and we are trying to build our strength recruits of level 10 up can be permitted entry to our Army.

2.  When recruiting new members, please make the effort to do so in person - this way we can confirm their race and they get to meet at least one of their Officers.

3.  I would also greatly appreciate a role-playing type invite, I know most of my Officers have witnessed me recruiting and have a basic idea of what I would like.

4.  Please tell them our guild website URL and post their name, race, class and level on our message board.  This way they become introduced to our family and rules. 

 

Any further questions please email me or ask an officer in guildchat.
