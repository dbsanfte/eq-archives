Spell Strategies

Only through chaotic strategies will victory be ours.

 

With the vast amounts of spells at our disposal it comes apparent that some spells work better in combination with others. This page will unlock some of these.

 

**********Note all these spells should be attempted behind your Skeleton pet for best effect**********



 

Character levels 1-3 (Access to circle I only):

 

Siphon Strength + Disease Cloud: First attack steals the strength and second diseases target.

Target is now weak and cannot regain HP.

 

Character Levels 4-7 (Access to Circle I and II only):

Clinging Darkness + Poison Bolt: First attack slows the target down and does damage over time. Compounded with Poison for even more damage over time. Target will steadily lose HP over the course of the battle.

Clinging Darkness + Poison Bolt + Disease Cloud: Same effect as the top but with the target unable to regain Hp's from the disease. **Note: by adding Fear in this one you can kill the target without him even getting close enough to hit you**

Clinging Darkness + Fear + Life Spike: Target slowed and has damage over time from the clinging darkness. Fear makes the target attempt to run away. Leaving you time to repeatedly cast Life Spike to drain its Hp's and them to your.

Fear + Gate: Just in case you need to get away in a hurry this sometimes works :-)

 

Character Levels 8-12 (Access to Circle I, II and III only):

 

Fear + Gather Shadows: If you just lost your pet and a pissed off NPC you can't handle is coming after you. This will make the target run away for a moment then you cased Invisible on yourself.

Fear + Mend Bones: Gives your Pet a few good shots at the target's back while you heal it up.
