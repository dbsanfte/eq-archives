If you're interested in serving the good of Norrath, enjoy working and interacting with others, and are willing to abide by the guild charter , Avenging Storm is interested in having you as a member. Please use the following application to submit your character for consideration.

Please note that you must have both a working e-mail address and two in-guild sponsors in order to be considered for admittance. Also be aware that the biography you complete here will be used on the guild page as your official profile. As such, proper grammar and punctuation are strongly encouraged! No guild applications will be accepted without two in-guild sponsors, a reason for wanting to join, and a thoroughly developed character history.
