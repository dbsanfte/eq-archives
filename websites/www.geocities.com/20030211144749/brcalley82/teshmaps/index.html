The Hole Tower Map

 There are no good maps of the ghost tower within The Hole so I went in with a level 1 hafling rogue and snuck around making this map up for a raid I had to go on. It ain't pretty but it gets the point across I think.

The Chardok Map

 This map wasn't actually done by me. It's done largely by Elsbin of The Seventh Hammer. This map takes his four part Chardok map and sticks all the pieces together with a little photoediting. I wasn't completely happy with the final product, but I just wanted a more realistic Chardok map.

The Sol B (Nagafen's Lair) Map

 This map wasn't actually done by me. It's done largely by Questor of The Norrath, one of EQ's finest map designers. He's a Japanese map designer and damn good, but his english is terrible and some of his maps are largely in Japanese, which us unfortunate. This map takes his three part Sol B map, sticks all the pieces together with a little photoediting, removes his notations and puts in the drops and MOB notations, etc. This is easily the best Sol B map out there and it IS to scale.

The Butcherblok Mountains Map

 When I first started EQ, Fellowship of The Griffon held one of Xegony's first post beta celebrations. They were holding a drunken footrace as part of it and I intended to win. You had to run across Butcherblok mountains from the docks to Greater Fay so I mapped out the zone to make myself familiar with the area. Turned out to be a damn fine looking map, even if not very printer friendly, hehe.
