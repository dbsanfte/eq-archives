Death by Chocolate Cookie


2-16 oz   Packages   Baker's® Semi-Sweet Baking 
                     Chocolate, divided
3/4       Cup        Brown Sugar, packed
1/4       Cup        Butter or Margarine
2         Large      Eggs
1         Teaspoon   Vanilla Extract
1/2       Cup        All-Purpose Flour
1/4       Teaspoon   Baking Powder
2         Cups       Chopped Nuts (optional)



While I was waiting in a Doctors office, I was scanning through the "Ladies Home Journal" when I found this recipe. Believe me when I tell you, these cookies are 'rich'.!!!!


Directions:

Coarsely chop 8 squares (1 package) of the Chocolate and set aside. Microwave the remaining 8 squares of chocolate in a large microwavable bowl on HIGH for 1 to 2 minutes. Stir until the chocolate is melted and smooth. Stir in the Brown Sugar, Butter, Eggs and Vanilla Extract. When these ingredients are well incorporated, stir in the Flour and the Baking Powder. Next stir in the coarsely chopped Chocolate and, if desired, the nuts. Dry by 1/4 cupfuls onto an ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 12 to 13 minutes or until the cookies are puffed and feel set to the touch. Cool on the cookie sheet for 1 minute and then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
