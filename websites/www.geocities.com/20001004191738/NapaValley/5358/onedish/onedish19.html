One Dish Chicken Bake


4      Each	Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breasts
1-6oz  Package  Stove Top Stuffing Mix® for Chicken
1      Can	Campbell's® Condensed Cream of  
		Mushroom Soup
1/3    Cup	Sour Cream or Milk
1/4    Cup	Margarine, cut into small pieces



Yes, you probably guessed it by now, this is yet another recipe that I 'appropriated' on the internet.



Directions:

In a large bowl, combined the Stove Top Stuffing Mix®, the seasoning packet, 1 1/2 cups of HOT water and the Margarine, stirring until it is moistened and then set it aside.

Mix the Cream of Mushroom soup and the Sour Cream, or Milk, in a medium size bowl.

Place the Chicken Breasts in a 12 x 9 inch baking dish and pour the Soup and Sour Cream mixture over them. Then cover them with the Stove Top Stuffing mixture.

Bake the casserole in a 375 degree oven for 35 minutes or until the chicken breasts are thoroughly cooked.

Makes 4 servings.
