Skills

Skills are grouped together by there ability (combat, spell caster ect.)

Magic User's:

Abjuration: Defense magic falls into this one. (all Shielding spells, Endure disease, Cancel Magic ect.)

Alteration: Ability to alter reality, (now why can't this skill work in RL). Most Necromancers spells fall under here. (Life spike, Gate, Hungry Earth Ect..)

Divination: Spell casting ability to make things turn invisible or vice versa (Dead Eye, Sense Magic, Gather Shadows ect.)

Evocation: Direct damage spells fall under here, Necromancers have very few spells using this skill (Expulse-Undead, Word Of Shadow ect.)

Conjuration: Ability to Summon object/pets, used most second only to alteration (all your skeleton pets, Poison Bolt, disease cloud ect.)

Channeling: Spell Caster ability to get off spells faster and not be interrupted. Goes up Automatically in Combat.

Meditation: Allows you to recover MP faster, The longer you meditate the faster you gain. Only use one bonus point on this at level 1. Meditating goes up very fast.

Specialization: Most magic users get this at lvl 30 it's the ability to concentrate on a type of magic (IE evocation, alteration). Necromancers get this skill at Level 20. Allows for less mana and less casting time for those types of spells.

Research: Suppose to be able to research new spells, not known how it works yet.

Combat:

Defense: All races/classes has this it's your basic defensive ability. Auto gains in combat no need to waste skill points

Offense: On the other side of the coin, you're basic offensive ability. Auto gains in combat.

One Handed Blunt: Your ability to bash things with a blunt object (clubs, maces, baseball bats ect..)

Two Handed Blunt: Recommended this be your Weapon skill. Staffs use this Skill.

Piercing: Ability to Poke someone in the ribs, head, neck whichever. Your starting weapon uses this.

Dodge: (level 22 required) In the Words of the immortal Mr. Myagison "The best defense is no be there."

Hand to Hand: All classes races get this but only monks can excel in it.

 

Other Skills: Necromancers get all the Trade skills like every other class. (Tailoring, fishing swimming) they're all very self-explanatory so I'm not wasting my time :-)
