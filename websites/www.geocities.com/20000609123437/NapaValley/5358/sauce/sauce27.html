Sausage Gravy



1       Lb.           Ground Sausage
1/2     Cup           Chopped Onions
3       Tablespoons   All Purpose Flour
1 1/2   Cups          Milk
1/8     Teaspoon      Black Pepper



I just learned how to make biscuits, believe it or not, and learning how to make a good sausage gravy just seemed like the next logical step. I found this one while 'surfing' the Internet.



Directions:

In a large skillet, brown the Sausage and Onions over medium heat, stirring with a fork to break up the chunks of meat. Drain off all the excess grease. Sprinkle the Flour over the cooked sausage mixture stirring to coat the meat. Reduce the heat to low and cook for 1 to 2 minutes, stirring constantly. DO NOT ALLOW THE FLOUR TO BROWN. Slowly add the milk, stirring constantly. When the mixture thickens and boils, stir in the Black Pepper and then simmer for an additional 5 minutes.

Note: For a "thinner" gravy, add more milk.
