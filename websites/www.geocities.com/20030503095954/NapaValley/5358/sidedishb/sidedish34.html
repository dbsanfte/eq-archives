Baked Creole Eggplant


2     Each     Eggplants, diced
1     Medium   Onion, chopped
1     Cup      Peeled and Deveined Shrimp, chopped
1     Rib      Celery, chopped
1/2   Cup      Butter or Vegetable Oil
1     Cup      Plain Bread Crumbs
To Taste       Emeril's® Essence Creole Seasoning *see the
               Sauce, Misc., Etc. section for this recipe*
To Taste       Salt
To Taste       Black Pepper



While looking for a good Gumbo recipe I found this one. It's a very good side dish and once you start using the Essence Creole Seasoning, you'll find many different ways to use it.


Directions:


Boil the diced Eggplant until it is soft.

Sauté the chopped Onion, Shrimp and Celery in the Butter or Vegetable Oil. Combine the Eggplant with this mixture and stir thoroughly. Put this mixture into a baking pan and sprinkle with bread crumbs. Bake for approximately 30 minutes in a pre-heated 350-degree oven.
