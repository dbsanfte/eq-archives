Heroes and Villans

This section of my guide has been put up as a courtesy to other players who have been aided or wronged by other players. Norrath is a world populated by warriors and mages, paladins and rogues, and many others. Naturally, some people are going to fight to make Norrath a better world, while others are only out for themselves. This is a place to give voice to the names and deeds of those who have helped or harmed you. Fame can be a dual-edged sword though, as I well know (OOC: Holger, do you have anything for a newbie??? PLEAAAAAASE?), so think well before you submit someone. To have a person added to this page, send an E-mail through this link to me with the person's character's name, your character's name, their deed(s), the server where the deed took place, and anything else about the submitee. And if they are to be a hero or villan! Note that I will put your name and E-mail up here, and any false submissions will be removed. Oh, and before I forget, if you send a picture, I will include it with the post!

By Brell! I can't believe how many submissions I've gotten since I advertised this site on Everlore and EQVault! Trust me folks, advertising works! Thanks to you all for your submissions, and keep 'em coming!

Heroes

Zael, Erollisi Marr Server (Submitted by Holger Soulforge, Jan 30, 2000)

This level 35 Dark Elven magician helped me retrieve my corpse from the King room in Permafrost Keep twice. He invised me several times and aided me when our hot-headed group decided they could take the king room and IGD all by themselves, while I was retrieving my corpse. He helped me through the resultant train, and then even bought the Zaharn's Coronet I had gotten at the time. Thanks for your help friend, and good luck to you!

PS, If you have a chance to group with him, don't pass it up! Undoubtedly one of the best group magicians I have ever seen.

Gililavyn, Xegony Server (Submitted by Nanyan, Feb. 7, 2000)

This Dark Elf necro helped Nanyan by selling him a Flowing Black Robe for only 17pp! Wish I had been in his place <g>. Nanyan says that he believes that Gililavyn did this because he cares about the people of Norrath, even the newbies :) To quote him "We need more big brothers and sisters to look out for the younger troops!"

Feelx, Prexus Server (Submitted by Wolfpaw, Feb. 8, 2000)

This 50th level Druid helped Wolfpaw complete the Short Sword of Morin quest for rangers, by slaying the feared Ghoul Messenger and letting him loot the letter from the corpse. On top of that, he also gave Wolfpaw a Turtleshell Helm and Bone Legplates! It's good to see someone who is willing to help those less powerful than they are out of the kindness of their hearts!

Infynite, Brell Serilis Server (Submitted by Gildright, Feb. 7, 2000)

The leader of the Order of Eternity Guild on Brell, this hero has helped his guildmates numerous times, including giving up a camp to aid Gildright in retrieving his corpse from a place where he was KOS due to bad faction, as he has done for several others. A shining example of what guildmates should be for eachother!

Follun Over, Tribunal Server (Subitted by Coyotes, Feb. 7, 2000)

This mid-thirty's Halfling Druid has been a great help to lower level players on the Tribunal. Weather merely giving advice about a zone or monsters, giving a quick loan, or providing free teleports, this little guy has made Norrath a better place for all of us. If you're on the Tribunal and looking for a good person for a high level party, look for Follun Over!

Crushem, Tribunal Server (Subitted by Coyotes, Feb. 7, 2000)

Another submission by Coyotes, this powerful ogre warrior saw a player in need and helped him. Coyotes was trying to learn the fine art of smithing, which is very expensive. While thus in training, he was unable to buy armor or weapons for himself. While hunting Dervishes in the Desert of Ro, Crushem joined the group which Coyotes was hunting with. He took one look at Coyotes and simply said "you need better armor," then walked away. He came back bearing a near-full suit of banded mail, which he gave to the amazed barbarian Warrior, free of charge! It's philanthropy like this that serves as a shining example to us all.

Kulu, Bertoxxulous Server (Submitted by Leedsen, Feb. 8, 2000)

This level 46 druid attempted to help a young paladin get his Deepwater Harpoon, by solo-pulling Spectres! He spent over 30 minutes helping young Leedsen, all the time in danger of dying. Unfortunatly, they soon found out that the weapon had been 'nerfed,' and had to give up. It's good to see someone who cares enough about other people to place themselves in danger to aid them. Even though they were unable to get the Harpoon, it's the thought that really counts!

Guvon, Innoruuk Server (Submitted by Mortex, Feb. 17, 2000)

This 20th level warrior helped Mortex retrieve his corpse from the feet of one of the more feared creatures native to Norrath: the vicious Sand Giant. Mortex, who was only level 8, was killed 3 times by the beast as he attempted to retrieve his own corpse. Fortunatly, Guvon happened along, whacked the Giant, and ran for the zone, leading it away from the relieved Ranger.

Mahlerion, Erollisi Marr Server (Submitted by Ninir, Feb. 7, 2000)

Malherion was submitted by Ninir because he is one of the best things in Norrath: a true friend. He has provided numerous teleports for Ninir and other players, but Ninir was happy for him "simply putting up with me."

Maleran, Fennin Ro Server (Submitted by Aaeluian, Feb. 8, 2000)

This 15th level hero pulled Lord Darish of Crushbone and an Orc Emmisary off of Aaeluian and told him to run, sacrificing himself to save the young bard of 5 songs. He then gave Aaeluian the loot off them, and apologized for taking the experience! Later on the same night, Maleran gave Aaeluian 10 platinum and a second Dwarven Axe, plus he power-leveled him to level 6! A true hero if I ever saw one!

Kazan, The Rathe Server (Submitted by Tilion, Feb. 8, 2000)

This Troll hero purchased 160 arrows in a back pack from Tilion, an aspiring wood elven fletcher. Not only that, but he intentionally overpaid, giving the young elf a much needed boost for training. It may be unusual to see a good Troll, but it certainly seems that there are some out there :)

Korur, Mithaniel Marr Server (Submitted by Nate, April 16, 2000)

Korur is a cleric of Brell Serilis on the Mithaniel Marr server.  He has made Nate's day and saved his life, helping him to escape from danger on more than one occasion. He is always around when someone needs help and is never afraid to step up to a challenge no matter how big or small it is! A true knight in shining armor!

Janika, Erollisi Marr Server (Submitted by Holger, May 29, 2000)

This level 36 monk, helped me a great deal when our group was eliminated in the Crypt of Dalnir. We were jumped by 4 Kly casters, and due to Dalnir's unusual construction, I wouldn't have been able to get my body. Janika, at great risk to herself, snuck back down into the dungeon and looted my corpse, bringing me my armor and waepons. Then, because she had accidently dropped my Mithril Staff, she went back down to get it, but unfortunatly the staff had already vanished :( Thanks alot Jan, losing my corpse has always been my personal nightmare!
