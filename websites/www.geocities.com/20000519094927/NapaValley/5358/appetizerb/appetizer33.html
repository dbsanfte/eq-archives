Velveeta & RO-TEL Cheese Dip



1	Lb.   Velveeta® Pasteurized Process Cheese
1-10 oz	Can   RO-TEL® Diced Tomatoes and Green Chilies
1	Bag   Tortilla Chips



Once again, the 'coupon' section of the newspaper has supplied me with another good recipe. Like I keep trying to tell everyone, watch the coupon ads and the labels on the cans of various products, such as Campbell's® Soups.


Directions:

Cube the Process Cheese and in a medium saucepan, combine it with the Diced Tomatoes and Green Chilies. Stir over low heat until the cheese is melted. Serve with the Tortilla Chips. This dip is also good with crackers and raw vegetables.
