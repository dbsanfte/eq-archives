Banana Fudge Cookies


1     Package       Chocolate Cake Mix 
                    *8 & 1/2 ounces*
1     Large         Egg
2     Tablespoons   Water
1/3   Cup           Mashed Ripe Bananas
6     Ounces        Semisweet Chocolate Bits



Geocities has voluntaries that review each and every homesteader's page to make sure they are in compliance with the rules of Geocities, which means that they are kid 'safe'. It's a thankless job, but one that is extremely necessary and 'Important'. This recipe is posted on the home page of one of those Community Leaders. Thanks Mary for all your hard work and the recipe.


Directions:

Combined the Cake Mix, Bananas, Egg and Water in a mixing bowl. Beat with an electric hand mixer at medium speed until the mixture is smooth. Stir in the Chocolate bits. Drop a rounded teaspoonful of the cookie dough on a greased cookie sheet, approximately 2 inches apart.

Bake the cookies in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for approximately 8 minutes. Remove the cookies from the baking sheet and cool on a rack.
