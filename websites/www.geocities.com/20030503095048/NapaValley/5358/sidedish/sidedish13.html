Scalloped Potatoes & Ham


2     Cans          Cream of Chicken Soup or 
                    Cream of Mushroom Soup
1     Cup           Velveeta® Cheese, cut into cubes
1/2   Medium        Yellow Onion, finely chopped
2     Tablespoons   Butter
1     Cup           Heavy Cream
8     Medium        Potatoes
2     Cups          Cooked Ham, cut into small cube



This is still another recipe I found while "surf'in" the Internet.


Directions:


Peel and slice the Potatoes into a large casserole dish. Add the Cubed Ham and the Cubed Velveeta® Cheese.

In a large bowl, mix the Soup, Onion, Butter and Heavy Cream. Pour the liquid mixture over the potatoes, ham and cheese.

Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 1 1/2 hours.

Serves 4 - 6.
