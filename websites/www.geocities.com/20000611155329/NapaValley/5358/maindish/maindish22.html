Garlic Chicken Strips



3    Each        Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breasts
1/2  Stick       Butter
1    Tablespoon  Finely Diced Fresh Garlic
To Taste 	 Salt & Pepper



This recipe is Windy's favorite and I am so glad that she allowed me to post it to my home page and include it in my Cookbook. Thanks Windy and I hope you enjoyed all the cartoons. They are just for you Little One.!!!


Directions:

Cut the Chicken Breasts into strips.

Melt Butter in a large pan. Sauté the Finely Diced Garlic for a couple minutes in the pan on low heat. Add chicken strips and cook on Medium heat for about 10 to 15 minutes until they are done. This is a quick and easy recipe.
