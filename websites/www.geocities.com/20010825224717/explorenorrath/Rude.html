Disappearing Rude Individuals



Unless you were living in a cave at the time (which is quite possible, in Norrath ;), you'll recall the recent unexpected appearance of the Rude Individuals in several areas across the land. They were apparently led by a Qeynos individual, Mordavin Telase, who has since disappeared, as have his underlings who so frequently slaughtered newbies with wild abandon. An NPC in that city now makes reference to him and his uncivilized acts, and urges the populace to find his current whereabouts. Intriguing storyline indeed! You can find some reference to this vanishing act here, for your convenience in further exploring this mystery.
