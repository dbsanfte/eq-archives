Grilled Shrimp on a Stick


1   Lb.      26-30 Count Shrimp
2   Sticks   Butter
2   Medium   Lemons
5   Each     Wooden Skewers



I had completely forgotten about this recipe until I started looking through one of my Seafood Cookbooks. This is quick, easy and a 'shrimp lovers' delight.


Directions:

Peel and devien the shrimp.

In a saucepan, melt the butter and then add the juice of one of the lemons to it.

Place 6 to 8 shrimp on each skewer, keeping them close together. Place the skewers on a griddle and based them with the melted butter/lemon sauce. Turn them every minute or so and continue to baste them until they turn pink and are completely cooked.

Serve the remaining butter/lemon sauce and wedges of the lemon.
