Coconut Cookie Bars


        Pastry
3/4     Cup           Butter
3/4     Cup           Brown Sugar
1 1/2   Cups          All Purpose Flour

        Cookie Filling
2       Large         Eggs
1       Cup           Brown Sugar
1       Teaspoon      Vanilla Extract
2       Tablespoons   All Purpose Flour
1/2     Teaspoon      Baking Powder
1/4     Teaspoon      Salt
1 1/2   Cups          Coconut Flakes
1       Cup           Chopped Nuts, your choice



I now have my wife getting recipes for me at her place of employment. When someone brings in something good to eat, she will get them to write down the recipe and bring it home for us to make and include in the cookbook. Thanks Sweetie…..Smooches.!!!


Directions:

For the pastry, mix the Butter, Brown Sugar and Flour together and blend thoroughly. Place this mixture in a lightly greased 13"x9" baking dish. Bake in a pre-heated 325-degree oven for 10 minutes or until lightly browned. Remove from the oven and set aside.

For the cookie filling, beat the eggs using an electric hand mixer for approximately 1 minute then add the Brown Sugar and the Vanilla Extract and continue to beat until the mixture is light and fluffy.

Mix the Flour, Baking Powder and the Salt together. Add the Coconut Flakes and Nuts to this mixture. Stir this mixture into the egg mixture and mix thoroughly. Pour this mixture over the pastry, spreading evenly. Bake in a pre-heated 375-degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until the filling is firm. Cool and then cut into bars.
