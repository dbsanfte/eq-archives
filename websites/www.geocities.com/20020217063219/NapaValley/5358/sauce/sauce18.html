Diana's Zucchini Bread 



2       Cups        All Purpose Flour
2       Teaspoons   Baking Soda
1       Teaspoon    Salt
1/4     Teaspoon    Baking Powder
3       Teaspoons   Cinnamon
1 1/2   Cups        White Sugar
3       Medium      Eggs
1       Cup         Vegetable Oil
2       Teaspoons   Vanilla Extract
2       Cups        Grated Zucchini



After giving a co-worker many of my recipes, she finally shared her recipe box with me. This is one of the recipes that caught my eye and she encouraged me to make it. Although the recipe says to bake in 2 loaf pans, I also discovered that muffins could also be made using this recipe.



Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, combined the Flour, Baking Soda, Salt, Baking Powder, Cinnamon, Sugar, Eggs, Vegetable Oil and Vanilla Extract and mix thoroughly. Blend in the Grated Zucchini at the last.

Bake in 2 loaf pans in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for approximately 40 minutes.
