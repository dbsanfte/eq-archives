Crab Dip



8     Ounces        Frozen Alaska King Crab
3     Ounces        Cream Cheese
1/2   Cup           Mayonnaise
1     Tablespoon    Lemon Juice
1/3   Cup           Chili Sauce
1     Teaspoon      Horseradish
2     Tablespoons   Chopped Green Onions
	            Parsley for Garnish



I found this recipe on a Home Page on the Internet that featured recipes. Since my daughter loves crabmeat, I just could not resist making a copy of it for my cookbook.


Directions:

Drain and cut up the Crabmeat.

Blend the Cream Cheese and Mayonnaise until smooth.

Add the Lemon Juice, Chili Sauce and Horseradish. Stir in the Crabmeat and Green Onions. Chill for 1 hour. Garnish with the Parsley.
