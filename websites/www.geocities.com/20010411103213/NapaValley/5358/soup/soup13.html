Karen's Potato Soup


4         Large	        Potatoes, peeled and diced
1         Medium	Yellow Onion, diced
2         Cups	        Diced Full Cooked Ham
1         Cup	        Diced Carrots (optional)
1-12 oz   Can           Evaporated Milk
2         Cups          Milk
2         Tablespoons   Butter
To Taste		Salt
To Taste		Black Pepper



This recipe came from an extremely close friend who is just about ready to move to Texas. Congratulations to Joe & Karen.



Directions:

In a large pot, combined the Potatoes, Yellow Onion, Carrots, and Ham and cover with water. Add the Salt and Black pepper to the pot and cook until the potatoes are tender. Add the Evaporated Milk, Regular Milk and the Butter and simmer for 5 minutes.
