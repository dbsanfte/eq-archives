The Firepot Room
of Timorous Deep

I took a loooong swim in the sea of the Timorous Deep and discovered an abandoned set of Wizards' Spires almost completely submerged. At the base of it is a small portal with an airlock of sorts. A sizable fall brought me to the exit-less room.  At first I was a little worried that I might end my days there ... until I began to investigate the markings and symbology of the Firepots.  
I remembered my grandfather telling me legends of a long lost method of transportation known as "Miragul's Highway".  It was a series of firepots that would teleport the user from one to the next.  I assumed there would be a connection to that legend.
While I've learned that I was not the first to have discovered this Firepot Room, it sure felt like I was.  It would appear that this room was certainly Miragul's handiwork.
