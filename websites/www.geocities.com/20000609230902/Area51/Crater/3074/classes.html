Necromancer: I probably bias here, but o'well.

Good:

1.The best class for soloing, You can make your own group:)

2. No other class can take a target and turn it into a diseased, poisoned, blinded, slowed and weakened shell of itself, Faster then Necro's.

3. The only class that can weaken, siphon life and drain spirit of a target and use it to buff ourselves.

Bad:

1. No one Likes us, Must be the human ears we keep around our neck.

 

Shadow Knight: Basically a Hybrid between a Necromancer and a warrior.

Good:

1. Its a good class for those die hard warriors that want some magic's later in there life.

2. Harm Touch Only useable once an EQ day but destroys your target (almost)

Bad:

1. Your not really liked, like necromancers. You can't make pet's like necromancer's. Kinda sucks.

 

Monk: Smacking giants in the kneecaps takes Guts or Stupidity.

Good:

1. The only class that can honestly say that it's no big deal to die. All your offense and defense power comes from skills (Dodge, Block, Round House, Flying Kick etc...)

2. You save ALOT of money not buying weapons, spells or high-end armor.

Bad:

1. The Lack of Magic means you can't travel far from home and return fast (Gate, Bind Affinity)

 

Wizard: Damnit that hurt.

Good:

1. The Best class for Dishing out Hordes of spells on a Target. No other class can do as much damage, faster then a wizard.

2. Great Character to have in a group.

Bad:

1. Lack of good buff or healing spells. High damage spells means the targets will go after you first for causing that damage.

2. This class is the worst at Soloing, without any healing or buff spells if a target gets to you, you will be hurting fast.

 

Magicians: Being evil scares me so I will be a magician instead.

Good:

1. They have many pets to choose from.

2. Gets create, water, and weapon at lvl 1.

Bad:

1. All Magician Fighting is done before the fighting starts, if a magician is jumped he is hurting.

2. Spell Books seem to fill up very fast (one slot for every type of elemental everytime they learn a new circle)

 

Enchanter: No Comment as Most of their spells do not work yet

 

Ranger: "I have a fear of social events"

Good:

1. Good fighter class, and spells at later levels.

2. Foraging skill allows you never to go hungry or thirsty again.

3. Tracking skill allows you to pick you targets, Its like your own radar.

Bad:

1. Your not aloud to attack animals without taking a Guild hit. You level slower because you can't level up on wolves or bears.

 

Druids: Those Darn Tree Huggers.

Good:

1. Well-balanced amount of buffs, healing and offensive spells.

2. Animals will not attack you.

Bad:

1. You cannot attack animals.

2. Most of your spells will not work unless you're outside, so they're useless in dungeons.

 

Rogue: I just love stabbing my dagger into the back of my enemy's. Don't you?

Good:

1. You get most of the best melee skills in the game backstab, Duel Weld, Double attack.

Bad:

2. Lack of magic abilities.

 

Shaman: Conan with a Medical Kit.

Good:

1. You're basically a Warrior with buffs and healing.

2. You're the only class that gets Alchemy.

Bad:

1. You're not a strongest Warrior or the strongest Cleric.

2. You gain spells every 5 levels instead of 4.

 

Warrior: Your basic hack and slash.

Good:

1. You can use any weapon or armor in the game

2. You can be any Race you wish.

Bad:

1. Can you say BORED TO TEARS, smash, rest, smash, rest, smash, and rest some more.

2. No magic's.

 

Cleric: Heal me Please.

Good:

1. You get the best Buff's and healing spells.

2. Only Necromancer's kick's undead Butt better then you.

3. You can use blunt weapons and can use armor and your magic user at level 1.

Bad:

1. Everyone wanting you to heal them or to bind there affinity:)

2. Get spells every 5 levels instead of 4.

3. Buying Weapons, Spells and Armor makes you a very Poor Cleric.

 

Paladin: Honor and Truth are my Calling. Sounds dull to me.

Good.

1. Your a Warrior with Healing and Defensive Buffs.

2. Lay on Hands heals you to full health ones per day, great in emergencies.

Bad:

1. Your guild is very demanding; you have to always have to watch your faction.
