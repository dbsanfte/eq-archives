History Of The Greater Druids
The Lost Age:
The Greater Druids came into being in the darkest of times during the lost age.  It was during these days when all hope had been abandoned and dreams shattered that a small group of elven druids hardly old enough to defend themselves against the smallest of desert creatures, schemed to save their village and families.

These young elves had already been through initiation into the standard guild of druidic study - as are all children who have The Gift, but they sought something else, a different type of affiliation.  The youn ones had heard tales of great druids that roamed the east regions of Tunaria.  Druids of great power and wisdom.  It was these mythical heroes that the young ones sought alliance with.

The young ones as these elves called themselves, believed that if they could somehow convince these fabled druids to join them they could save thier families and villages from the suffering that had followed the wrath of Solusek Ro.  They believed that together the druids could make a better world for themselves and all those ravaged by the plight of the Fire God.

And so they ventured across the vast territory of what is now called the continent of Antonica.  And it was across this land that their many adventures led them to greater and greater a foe.  And with each passing battle and each passing year the young druids were transformed into adults, some having already reached thier 19th season.  It was one of these older druids who had accidentally discovered that he could teleport.
Fate:
After a particularly hardy battle, the young ones had decided to camp early for the evening.  So one of the older druids set his cloak on the greound to lay upon, he noticed a scroll.  He picked up the weather beaten parchment and read teh words upon it.  Strange words, familiar, yet somehow new to him.  It was after chanting the lyrical phrases several times over that he suddenly felt cold.  As he looked up he noticed that the world around him had suddenly stopped, frozen in mid action!  And just as suddenly as they had stopped, they started to move again.  But these were not the same people!

You can imagine the fear of a youn druid after such an occurrence. It was by fate that there was another person nearby.  A short and scruffy looking creature, who calmly walked up to the young one and clasped his shoulders.  In short order, he had fully explained to the young one what had happened, and he understood this was just a taste of the great many powers that the druid would gain as he grows.  And so it was from this halfling druid that the youn elven druid learned of the greater wonders of nature.  And it was this halfling that had reunited the young ones again.  And it was he that the young ones wished to lead their struggle.  The struggle for survival.

They formed their alliance, a guild known as the Greater Druids.  And with each passing year, the guild became a more powerful force for any to reckon with.  But the years wore on and the times were good.  Laziness had come over many of the members of the guild.  What need did they have for a guild such as this?  It has been many ages since the druids suffered so.  It was with these slothful thoughts that the guild slowly sank into nilhilty. All except one child.
Rebirth:
Many years later a halfling who called himself Hopper appeared in the city of Kelethin.  He spoke of a guild called the Greater Druids  A name that had been heard in many a bards tale and children's stories.  A name that was joked about in the taverns.  A guild that was more fantasy than truth.  He spoke of the greatness that was known of these druids.  He spoke of honor, charity, and camaraderie.  With such fervor and passion did he speak that, even amongest many scoffs and jeers he was able to recruit a fine many young druids.  It was this group of druids that breathed life back into the guild once more.
