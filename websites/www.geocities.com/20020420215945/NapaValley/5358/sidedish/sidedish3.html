Cabbage Casserole


1     Head          Cabbage
1     Cup           Shredded Cheddar 
                    Cheese
1     Cup           Hot Milk
1     Teaspoon      Salt
2     Tablespoons   Flour
2     Tablespoons   Sugar
2     Tablespoons   Butter
1/8   Teaspoon      Black Pepper
1     Teaspoon      Salt
1     Cup           Water



Some very good friends in Gadsden, Alabama, Bill & Ruthie Chastain, gave me this recipe. It has become my daughters favorite Main Course, Side Dish and After School Snack.


Directions:


Clean and core the Head of Cabbage and slice into 1/2 inch sections. Put the cup of Water and 1 teaspoon of Salt into a skillet. (I use and electric skillet). Bring the water to a boil and then place the cabbage slices in the skillet, cover and cook for approximately 10 minutes.

In a small bowl, combine 1 teaspoon of Salt, Flour, Sugar, Black Pepper and mix well.

In a small saucepan, heat the cup of Milk until it is hot but DO NOT bring it to a boil.

Butter the bottom of a 3 quart baking dish. Remove the cooked cabbage slices from the skillet and place them in the baking dish. Cover them with the Flour, Sugar, Salt and Black Pepper mixture and then dot them with the butter. Pour the hot milk over the cabbage and then cover them with the Shredded Cheddar Cheese. Bake them in a 350 degree oven for 35 minutes. (These re-heat well in a microwave oven.)
