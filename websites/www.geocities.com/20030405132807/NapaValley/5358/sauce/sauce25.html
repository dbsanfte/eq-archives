Cream Puffs



3/4   Cup        Water
1/3   Cup        Butter or Margarine
1/2   Teaspoon   Salt
3/4   Cup        All Purpose Flour
3     Large      Eggs



I discovered this recipe in one of my favorite magazines, Southern Living. They also have stuffing that goes in them, which I will try later, but first I plan to fill them with the cheese spread that is used in my Cheese and Rye Bread recipe.



Directions:

Bring the Water, Butter and Salt to a boil in a medium size pot. Reduce the heat to low and add the flour, slowly, beating with a wooden spoon until the mixture leaves the sides of the pot.

Remove the pot from the heat. Add the eggs, one at a time, and beat until the mixture is smooth and creamy. Repeat this procedure until all the eggs have been added to the mixture.

Drop this mixture by teaspoonfuls onto a buttered baking sheet and bake at 400 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes or until lightly browned and puffed. Transfer them to a wire rack to allow them to cool.
