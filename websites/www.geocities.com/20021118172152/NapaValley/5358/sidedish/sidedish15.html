Broccoli Casserole


2-10oz	Packages   Frozen Chopped Broccoli
1	Can	   Campbell's® Cream of 
                   Mushroom Soup
2	Large	   Eggs, beaten
1/2	Cup	   Chopped Yellow Onion
1	Cup	   Mayonnaise
1/2	Cup	   Milk
1	Cup	   Shredded Cheddar Cheese.

Topping

1	Stick	   Butter, melted
1	Package	   Ritz® crackers, crushed.



A very dear ICQ friend gave me this recipe. If you love Broccoli, as I do, this one is a must. "BLUE EYES", thank you so very, very much.


Directions:


Cook the Broccoli according to the package directions and then drain it. Place the cooked broccoli in a large mixing bowel and stir in the remaining ingredients. Put this mixture in a casserole dish.

Prepare the topping by mixing the Melted Butter and crushed Ritz® crackers. Spread this mixture over the Broccoli mixture and bake in a 350 degree oven for 35 minutes. Allow the casserole to cool for approximately 10 minutes prior to serving, this will allow it to thicken.
