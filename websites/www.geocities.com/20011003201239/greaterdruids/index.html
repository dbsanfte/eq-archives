Called through the stargate into the grove
By Gods, or gifts, or desire of love
By song, or poetry, or other gift
Or through the wisdom of the ages to sift
Truth and Justice are the reward
For all those swearing by the sword
We all come to the same call
where al are one, and one is all.
Calling to the Dragon in the land
We find the best of what we can
To see the power raised where we stand
by calling the Awen into man.
Ceridwen's inspiration comes
From below into above it runs
A wellspring of ideas flows
From the spirit of this land it goes.
Learning the patterns of the poem
Patterns of the plucked string tones
Models of the rhyming ways
Patterns of the nights and days,
Songs, lilts, games or trials
Patterns of the turning spirals
Band our hearts together as one
Protection from all work is done.
By holy stone we swear our oaths
Or blood of family or tree ring growth
With grain, drink, and living milk
Our glories told by robes of silk.
Honours given by the chairs
Of the Druids council there
Comes the tokens high and low
Of earth, sky, sea and stone.

Leon Read, Seattle WA
