Double Layer Chocolate Cheesecake


2-8 0z   Packages   Philadelphia® Cream Cheese, 
                    softened
1/2      Cup        Sugar
1/2      Teaspoon   Vanilla Extract
3        Squares    Semi-Sweet Chocolate, melted and 
	            slightly cooled
2        Large      Eggs
1        Each       Oreo® Cookie Crust, (ready to use)



A friend on the Internet, who received this recipe from a site that emails recipe everyday, emailed this one to me. There are plenty of sites like this that just automatically email you things, from recipes to jokes.


Directions:

In a mixing bowl, blend the Cream Cheese, Sugar, Vanilla Extract and Eggs until smooth. Remove once cup of this batter.

Incorporated the melted chocolate into the remaining batter mixture and when it is mixed completely, pour the batter mixture into the Oreo® Cookie Crust. Top this with the reserved one-cup of plain batter mixture.

Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 40 minutes or until the center is almost set. Cool on a wire rack and then refrigerate for at least 3 hours prior to serving. Over night is best, provided you can wait that long.
