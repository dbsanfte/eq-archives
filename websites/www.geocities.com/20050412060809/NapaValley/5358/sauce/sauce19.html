Emeril's® Essence Creole Seasoning 



1 1/2	Tablespoons	Paprika
2	Tablespoons	Salt
2	Tablespoons	Garlic Powder
1	Tablespoon	Black Pepper
1	Tablespoon	Onion Powder
1	Tablespoon	Cayenne Pepper
1	Tablespoon	Dried Oregano
1	Tablespoon	Dried Thyme



This is straight from Emeril Lagasies home page. It is a wonderful seasoning for just about any meat or vegetable dish.



Directions:

In a large mixing bowel, combined all the ingredients and mix thoroughly. Store in an airtight container.
