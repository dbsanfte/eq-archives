Fried Zucchini


3 or 4   Medium   Zucchinis
1/2      Cup      Vegetable Oil
2        Cups     Beer Batter Mix

	 Beer Batter Mix

1        Cup      All Purpose Flour
1        Cup      Beer



I guess you get two of my recipes with this one. Just be sure to keep everyone out of the kitchen as you are making these or else you will think you are done making them only to find out they have all already been eaten.


Directions:


In a large bowl, combine the Flour and Beer. Mix thoroughly until there are no lumps. Put the batter in the refrigerator for approximately 20 minutes.

Fried Zucchini

Put the Vegetable Oil into an electric skillet and heat to 350 degrees.

Cut the Zucchini into rounds, about 1/4" thick.

Dip the sliced zucchini, one at a time, in the batter mix, coating them completely and then 'place' them in the hot oil. Allow them to cook until they are golden brown on one side, and then turn them over. Once they are completely golden brown, remove them from the oil and drain them on a paper towel.

Place cooked vegetables in a warm oven until ready to serve.
