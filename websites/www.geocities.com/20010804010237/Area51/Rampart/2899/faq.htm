How do U record your audio?
    At first we usually just ran a beat trough the comuter, instrumized it, ran it back to the tape deck, played it at a low volume while we use our mike, than miz em together. But lately we usually get an instrumental from the store, or instrumentalise some Puerto Rican rap, than stick it in out $300 machine with the double MICs and were done.
     
    Update 2/22/98: Rico got a new Kareoke component that muffles the voices in the track, but it's all good.

Capaal Rules:
  1. No gang ups. No 2 on 1's or 3 on 1's (2/17/98). Update: There can be groups against one if it is accepted by ALL the MCs (2/22/98)
  2. Don't bite other MCs lyrics (2/17/98)
  3. Don't get other MCs to write your lyrics (2/17/98). Update: If you use a line er so than give shot outs. Like: "Like P, I be BOUT IT!". Kno? (2/22/98)
  4. Don't run from any battles (2/17/98) Update: If you don't accept the battle, you loose it (2/22/98)
  5. The defender from challenges, choose the beat (2/22/98)
  6. When the MCs are randomly chosen for a battle, the judges choose the beat (2/22/98)
  7. You can't bust a verse longer than 30 seconds in a session battle (2/22/98). Update: You pass the mike when you terribly mess up, the judges say so, or whenever you want (2/22/98)
  8. There has to be more than 1 judge. (2/22/98)
