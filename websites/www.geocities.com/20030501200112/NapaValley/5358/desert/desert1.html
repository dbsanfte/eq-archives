Marbled Cheese Cake Bars


2     Cups              Finely Crushed Oreo® 
                        Cookies (Approx. 24 
                        cookies)
3     Tablespoons       Butter, Melted
3     8 Ounce Packages  Softened Philadelphia 
                        Cream Cheese®
1     14 Ounce Can      Eagle Brand® Sweetened 
                        Condensed Milk
3     Large             Eggs
2     Teaspoons         Vanilla Extract
2     1 Ounce Squares   Unsweetened Chocolate, 
                        Melted

Directions:

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Combined the Oreo® Cookie Crumbs and the Melted Butter and press firmly on the bottom of a 13"x9" baking dish.

In a large bowl, beat the Philadelphia Cream Cheese® until it is fluffy. Gradually beat in Eagle Brand® Condensed Milk until smooth. Add the Eggs and the Vanilla Extract and mix thoroughly.

Pour 1/2 of the batter mixture evenly over the cookie crust. Stir the melted Unsweetened Chocolate into the remaining batter mixture Spoon the remaining batter mixture over the first layer of batter mixture. Using a knife or metal spatula, gently swirl through both layers of the batter mixture to marbleize it.

Bake for 45 minutes or until set. Cool, chill and then cut into bars. Store covered in the refrigerator. This makes 24 to 26 bars.
