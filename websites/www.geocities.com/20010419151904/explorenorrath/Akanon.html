Ak'Anon - Clockwork Dragon


Ah, that mysterious tinkerer Tinmizer and his legendary clockwork dragon. Some people speculate the dragon does not exist. Some say there is a hidden entrance to its lair deep within the bowels of Ak'Anon. Some say it is already somewhere in-game, but is deactivated and needs a gnome battery to come back to life. For a gnomish history, go here.

Hailing NPCs in Ak'Anon one day, I came across a rare spawn who had the following to say on it.

[Tue Jan 02 21:48:13 2001] You say, 'hail Kozyn Gigglephizz'
[Tue Jan 02 21:48:13 2001] Kozyn Gigglephizz bows politely.  'Greetings, my friend.  Have you come to turn in one of those malfunctioning containers called [Tinmizer]'s [Fabulous Compactor]?  I shall gladly exchange it for a few nice gems.'
[Tue Jan 02 21:48:20 2001] You say, 'Who is Tinmizer?'
[Tue Jan 02 21:48:20 2001] Kozyn Gigglephizz chortles with glee.  'Ho, ho, ho!!  Oh, my!  Tinmizer was a bit of a goof when it came to tinkering.  I am from the [T.B.I.] and I was sent to clean up the mess he made.  He was tinkering defective containers and selling them.  We exiled him and destroyed all his containers.  Now we hear the scrapman found some and was using them as a reward for deeds.  We have warned him.'
[Tue Jan 02 21:48:46 2001] You say, 'What TBI?'
[Tue Jan 02 21:48:46 2001] Kozyn Gigglephizz lowers his voice and leans closer to your ear.  'Ssshhh.  This is against regulations, but you look like you could keep a secret.  T.B.I. stands for the Tinkerers Bureau of Investigation.  We are a clandestine organization reporting directly to King Ak'Anon.  We handle all tinkering glitches, if you know what I mean.'
[Tue Jan 02 21:49:56 2001] You say, 'What Fabulous Compactor?'
[Tue Jan 02 21:49:56 2001] Kozyn Gigglephizz looks sternly at you.  'The compactor is a defective piece of junk.  Crafted by the clumsy hands of [Tinmizer].  My work shall be done as soon as I exchange all the compactors for precious gems.  We take a loss here, you know!!?'
[Tue Jan 02 21:50:32 2001] You say, 'Where is Tinmizer?'
[Tue Jan 02 21:50:32 2001] Kozyn Gigglephizz shakes his head in sorrow.  'Alas, for tinkering defective merchandise, he was exiled from Ak'Anon.  First, the [clockwork dragon] and now the [Fabulous Compactor].  What could be next?  No one has seen nor heard of him since.  Probably ended up as a feast for the ogres.'
[Tue Jan 02 21:50:32 2001] Kozyn Gigglephizz begins to cast a spell.
[Tue Jan 02 21:50:45 2001] You say, 'What clockwork dragon?'
[Tue Jan 02 21:50:46 2001] Kozyn Gigglephizz shakes in fear.  'Oh!!  I have seen it.  A thousand feet tall and smells of burning oil.  It was tinkered by [Tinmizer]. Yep, that's it all righty.  Tinkered by the loon.  He started out just making a clock, a GIANT ONE!!  It was in the form of a wurm.  He called it [Big Bynn], you know, after his familiar named Bynn.  And then... then it escaped.  Yeah.. That's it.'
[Tue Jan 02 21:50:58 2001] You say, 'Who is Bynn?'
[Tue Jan 02 21:51:03 2001] You say, 'Who is Big Bynn?'
[Tue Jan 02 21:51:11 2001] You say, 'What Big Bynn?'

What are those strange devices in Ak'Anon and Steamfont that control things? Also the theory about a gnome-built submarine that sailed to Odus and later discovered Velious. A four-continent tie-in. Read about it all here. Don't forget to see the quest that mentions a giant bridge the gnomes were building to Erudin here.

A dozen in-game references exsit that refer to the boat in Ocean of Tears that sailed from Ak'Anon (but how?! there is no ocean landing.. or is there?). Boat beacon mounts, giant sails, a fabulous compactor... do a search on Allakhazam's site on some of these items for additional information.

Gnomes in the Western Wastes too?! They really get around. Read about them here, or about the gnome in Timorous Deep who is located on a gadget-laden island here. And what about this contraption? So many gnomes to check out, so little time.
