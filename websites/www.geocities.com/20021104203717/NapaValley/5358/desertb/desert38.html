Chocolate Éclair Cake


          Cake
2       Boxes         Vanilla Instant Pudding,
                      regular size
2       Cups          Milk
1       Teaspoon      Vanilla Extract
1       Box           Nabisco® Graham Crackers

          Icing
2       Envelopes     Liquid Baking Chocolate
3       Tablespoons   Light Karo Syrup
3       Tablespoons   Soft Margarine/Butter
2       Tablespoons   Milk
1 1/2   Cups          Powder Confectionery Sugar
1       Teaspoon      Vanilla Extract



This recipe came from a wonderful couple that I periodically chat with. They were gracious enough to allow me to include this recipe on my home page and in my cookbook, and they didn't even mention 'Royalties'. ROTFLMAO!!


Directions:

Cake

In a large bowl, mix the Vanilla Instant Pudding, Milk and Vanilla Extract for approximately 2 minutes, making sure it is well incorporated.

Place a layer of Whole Graham Crackers on the bottom of a 13" x 9" pan.

Spread 1/2 of the pudding mixture over the Graham Crackers and then place another layer of Whole Graham Crackers on top of it. Spread the remaining pudding mixture over this layer of Graham Crackers and then top that with one final layer of Whole Graham Crackers.

Icing

In a large bowl, combined the Liquid Baking Chocolate, Karo Syrup, Margarine/Butter, Milk, Sugar and Vanilla Extract and mix thoroughly. Spread this mixture over the top of the last layer of Graham Crackers. Refrigerate over night.
