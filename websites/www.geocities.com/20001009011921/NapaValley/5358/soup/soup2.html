Broccoli Bisque



1 1/2      Pounds         Broccoli
             or
2-10oz     Packages       Frozen Broccoli
2          Cans           Chicken Broth
1          Medium         Onion
2          Tabblespoons   Butter
1          Teaspoon       Salt
1/2        Taste          Black Pepper
2          Teaspoons      Lime Juice



This is another recipe that I have no idea where it came from. It is recorded on a 3"x5" note card with instructions that are not very good. Hence the reason for the cookbook that I am trying to put together.


Directions:


Wash the Broccoli and trim off the flowerettes and steam for 8 to 10 minutes, or cook the frozen Broccoli according to the package instructions. Drain the boroccoli once it is cooked and add the Chicken Broth to the pot. Bring the pot to a boil and then remove it from the heat.

Quarter the Onion and saute them in the Butter. Once the onion is sauted, add it to the broccoli. Add the remaining indgredients to the pot and stir thoroughly. Put the mixture in a food processor and process until the mixture is smooth. Return the processed mixture to the pot and heat.
