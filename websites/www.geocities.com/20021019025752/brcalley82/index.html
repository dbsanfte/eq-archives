    • The Befallen Catbox

      • (Cat boxes are areas in zones where players can't go. These are boxes at loc 0,0 with default textures on the walls. You can't do anything in these rooms, but people sometimes get teleported to these rooms when the game can't figure out where to put a player. The solution to getting out is either gating or petitioning to get a guide to move you out. There are catboxes in pretty much every indoor dungeon in Norrath. The cat is rumoured to belong to one of the game developers.)
