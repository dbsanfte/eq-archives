The Fire Pet
    The Fire Elemental is one of the weakest and strongest elementals depending on the situation.  Fire Elementals have good strength and agility, but have the fewest hit points of any elemental.  To make up for this they have a self casted damage shield that inflicts damage each time it's opponent strikes it.  This can wither down a fast attacking enemy quickly.
Note: Level 50+ pets now have the ability to cast a wide variety of wizard based spells (i.e. Force Shock, Flame Shock, Shield Skin, etc.)  These elementals also cast O'keils Embers on any nearby pets.
