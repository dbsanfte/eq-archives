Vague memories of a happy childhood pushed Atos on through the forrest in search of her stolen heritage. She remembers little of that long ago time living near Neriak with her clan, before the Erudites took her away. All those years of serving in silence while secretly listening and learning from her masters. The older she grew, the more she realized how cruel others were to her race. "I wish all humans were dead":she sighs:. Now, having escaped her captors, on her own and nearly starved, she heads in the direction of Neriak more on instinct then knowledge. No hope, no direction, only hatred. Until she met Kiote' and Xu'raz of Darkmoon........to be continued.
