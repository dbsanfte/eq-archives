Donna Kay's Birthday Cake


1       Box           Pillsbury® White Cake Mix

           Vanilla Frosting

2 1/2   Cups          Sugar
3/4     Cup           Carnation® Evaporated Milk
3       Tablespoons   Karo® Light Syrup
3       Tablespoons   Margarine or Butter
1       Tablespoon    Vanilla Extract
1       Pint          Fresh Strawberries, sliced



My Mother-In-Law prepares this cake for my wife on her birthday.


Directions:

Prepare two cakes according to the package instructions and let them cool on a wire rack.

Vanilla Frosting

In a medium saucepan, bring the Sugar, Milk and Syrup to a boil and cook until the mixture will form a soft ball in cold water. Add the Margarine and Vanilla Extract to the mixture, remove it from the heat and beat until it is cool and thick enough to spread.

Put a layer of strawberries on top of the first cake and then drizzle with the frosting before it gets to thick. Put the remaining layer on, cover the top with strawberries and then frost the entire cake.
