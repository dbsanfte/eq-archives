Below is a list of Everquest related sites that have great information and are some of the best out there.

EverQuest Links

AllaKhazam's Magic Realm - A great Everquest site that always has the most current information about the game. Updated almost daily and has awesome information on equipment and quests, probably the best around. Also features good zone maps and trade skills section.

EQHaven - A great site for forums. Forums are server based and class based. Updated regularly.

EverLore - A great site for whats going on in the lands of Norrath. Updated daily with lots of player submitted news and events. Has guides that are unique in the fact that they are part guide and forum.

EverQuest HeadQuarters - Another great site featuring news, player guides and forums. Updated almost daily.

EverQuest Home Page - The official Everquest Home Page by Sony. This is the main Everquest website that describes the game as well as other misc. stuff like news, policies, etc.

EverQuest News - EverQuest news from the Not Crazy Network. Features EQ news, server events and the Ask Milo Page.

EverQuest Stratics - Another great site for news in EverQuest. This site features a little bit of everything.

Ruins of Kunark - The official Everquest - Ruins of Kunark Home Page by Sony. This website gives you a preview of the Everquest expansion that is expected by March of next year.

EQCasters - Possibly the best Everquest website dedicated to the Casters of Everquest. Information is updated almost daily and isn't catered to any one spell casting class, but rather to all four pure casters.

EQ Druids - A great site that is dedicated to our brothers and sisters, the Druid. Has almost everything and anything you would want to know about druids from quests to equipment to actually creating a druid. Definately a must visit site if you play a Druid.

EQItems - An Everquest website that is dedicated to compiling a complete list of the items in Norrath. Very easy to track down items with its built in item search, which can be customized to type, race, class, armor class/damage, stats., weight, magic and no drop.

EQLizer - A great Everquest site that always has current information about the game. Updated almost daily, this site features a great overall view of the game from class's to beasiarity to equipment to zone guides. Also features one of the most complete lists of spells for all classes.

EQVault - A great Everquest site that always talks about the current events in the Everquest world. Updated daily, this site has a good forum section that is broken down by servers and other areas of interest. Also features some good player sumbitted player and trade guides.

Rangers Links

Adventurers Journal - A very nicely done personal page written in a roleplaying fashion of the adventures of Temyez, a Half Elf Ranger of Tunare on the Bristlebane server.

The Ranger's Glade - Another wesbite that is dedicated to Rangers. It features player submitted guides and forums for Rangers to learn and help each other. Also features a nice section where Rangers can submit profiles of their Ranger for all to see.
