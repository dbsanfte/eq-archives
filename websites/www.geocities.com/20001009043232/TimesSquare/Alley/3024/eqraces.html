EverQuest

Character Races


I will be updating this page as more information becomes available. For now, I will provide information on the current character races of Norrath.



BARBARIAN

Barbarians hail from the city of Halas, in the continent of Antonica. Their size is large and have the special ability of "slaming". However, this advantage is balanced by their inability to see in the dark. Classes open to the Barbarian are Rogue, Shaman, and Warrior. They are best suited for the Warrior class.

DARK ELF

Dark Elves call the city of Neriak, in the continent of Antonica their home. Their size is small and are blessed with the ability to hide and UltraVision. Their evil nature, however, disadvantages them to a degree. Classes open to Dark Elves are Cleric, Enchanter, Magician, Necromancer, Rogue, ShadowKnight, Warrior and Wizard. Because of their evil ways, they do not make very good clerics and the Paladin profession is totally closed to them. However, they do make good Enchanters, Necromancers, Wizards and Magicians.

DWARF

Under the rule of King Underfoot, the dwarves hail from the city of Kaladim in the continent of Faydwer. Their size is small, have Infravision and a very well developed Sense Heading skill. They have no natural disadvantages. Classes open to them are Cleric, Paladin, Rogue and Warrior and they excell equally well in any of these classes.

ERUDITE

Erudites start in the city of Erudin in the continent of Odus. Their size is medium and they are blessed with very high intelligence. However, like their Barbarian cousins, they have no night vision. Classes available to Erudites are Cleric, Enchanter, Magician, Necromancer, Paladin, and Wizard. Erudites make the ULTIMATE Necromancers in all of Norrath.

GNOME

The home of the Gnomes is the city of Ak'Anon in the continent of Faydwer. Their size is small, they have UltraVision and have an uncanny ability to tinker. Gnomes have no innate disadvantages. Classes open to them are Cleric, Enchanter, Magician, Necromancer, Rogue, Warrior and Wizard. Their forte is as clerics and mages.

HALF ELF

Half Elves have the distinct advantage of being able to start in the elf cities of Felwithe and Kelethin, in the continent of Faydwer, as well as the human cities of Queynos and Freeport, in Antonica. Their size is medium and are blessed with Infravision. Half Elves have no known disadvantages. Classes open to them are Bard,Druid, Paladin, Ranger, Rogue and Warrior. They make wonderful Paladins and Bards.

HALFLING

Halflings hail from the city of Rivervale in the continent of Antonica. Their size is small and they have InfraVision and a well developed ability to hideh. They have no known disadvantages. Classes open to them are Cleric, Druid, Rogue and Warrior. Of these, the excell as Rogues and Warriors.

HIGH ELF

High Elves call Felwithe their home. It is a city in the continent of Faydwer. They have InfraVision and are able to wear both small and medium sizes. They have no innate disadvantages. Professions open to them are Cleric, Enchanter, Magician, Paladin and Wizard. They are master magick users especially as Wizards and Enchanters.

HUMAN

Humans hail from either Queynos or Freeport. They are both port cities in the continent of Antonica. Their size is medium and they have the distinct advantage of gaining levels faster than other races. However, they are proficient in only one language when they start, and as their Barbarian and Erudite cousins, they have no night vision. They do have a wider selection of classes open to them. These are: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Enchanter, Magician, Monk, Necromancer, Paladin, Rogue, ShadowKnight, Warrior and Wizard. The Human race is the only one in Norrath that can be Monks.

OGRE

Ogres are ugly. Their size is large and they hail from the city of Oggok in the continent of Antonica. They have UltraVision and the ability to bash but they are EVIIIL, and their nature disadvantages them. They can only be ShadowKnights, Shamans and Warriors. Since they are mentally challenged, their best profession is Warrior.

TROLL

Trolls are much Uglier than Ogres. They hail from the city of Grobb in the continent of Antonica. Their size is small and they also have the advantage of InfraVision and starting with regeneration and slaming ability. However, their evil nature works against them. Classes open to them are ShadowKnight, Shaman and Warrior. Like their Ogre cousins their mental abilities are nothing to boast about. Consequently, their best profession is also Warrior.

WOOD ELF

Last but not least, this type of elf hails from the fair city of Kelethin in the continent of Faydwer. Like their cousins the High Elves, they can wear either small or medium sizes and are blessed with Infravision. They have no known disadvantages. Classes open to them are Bard, Druid, Ranger, Rogue and Warrior. They are the Ultimate Bards and also make excellent Rangers and Druids.
