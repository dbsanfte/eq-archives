As I laid nearly unconscious in the desert, bleeding to death from a recent attack on the dervish cutthroats, my mind wandered and my vision blurred. The strange sensation of blood soaked sand on my face distracted me from the pain. In the back of my mind I could feel the end coming. The sun warmed the exposed , clean side of my face. Contemplating the contrast of both sides, I felt as two persons. A revelation of life swept through me and for a moment I truly saw how life is woven into the fabric of being.
I awoke in Neriak, astounded to be alive. What source of trickery was this! Had I been raised from the dead by some necromancer? I had died, I know I must have died out there in the desert. Still in a daze I noticed I was in the cleric's guild house. The guild master Perrir Zexus explained that he had seen others like me that Innoruuk had also chosen to rebirth so that we may show his children the true way. He invited me to stay on with the guild and learn the practices of our father.
After training with Ithvol K'Jasn and living with the clerics, my new powers make me feel as one with the forrest. I thank Innoruuk endlessly for this chance to help spread his ways throughout the world. I have taken up a clerics warhammer now, and travel seeking out my dark brothers and sisters so that I may bless them with our father's protection. As a priestess of Innoruuk I enjoy speaking his teachings. Come to the rune in Nektulo's Forrest sometime. You will find me there offering our father a gift, performing a sacrifice, or leading a prayer. Come, dark children of the night, and join in my rejoicing of Innoruuk. We shall spread his hate across the lands!
