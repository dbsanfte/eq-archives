MIRAGUL'S LAIR

Not an absolute stranger to these caves, I came here months ago to pluck a small rod from the floor of a cabin (#3).
This night, however, had a different objective.  As I entered, a hardened druid passed me by and assured me that the Lich was roaming.  I'd heard rumours of 53 Seasons and Seeing Invisibles.

Well, to cut the story down, the Lich was indeed present but of even less seasons that I (so, ~45) and definitely did *not* see through either my Hiding skills nor the Invisibility to Undead of my friend.

That's not to say, of course, that Miragul himself does not possess the original attributes (53, SeeInvis) but the Lich is still considered to be a more than formidable opponent.

Let me also add that my recently-sought faction with the Heretics proved to be for naught with the Lich as he was still seeming to wish to spill my blood (KoS).

The chamber itself, shown at top of page, is in the area marked #2.
