Everquest Files

Here's a duo of useful utilities for EverQuest: EQ-Numbers, which calculates mana, weapon damage, and even takes into account double attack and dual wield; and EverQuest Zone Explorer, a nice little utility that lets you explore the zones offline. It's prone to crashes, however, and you can only walk. You're also limited to using the tutorial character, Soandso. I'll be adding more files as they become available. You'll need WinZIP or a similar utility to unzip these files.

EQ Numbers Ver. 2.2B Contains everything you need for EQ: weapon damage calculator w/simulation, Mana Calculator over 5 levels, etc.

EQ Zone Explorer A very nice utility that allows you to explore zones offline.

EQ Spells Ver. 1.10 A MUST FOR EVERY CASTER! This program contains information on all spells for all classes. It requires some updated system files to run. Get them here
