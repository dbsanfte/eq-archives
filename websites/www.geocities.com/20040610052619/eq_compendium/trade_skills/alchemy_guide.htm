Alchemy Guide

General:

          Alchemy is the skill of taking various components found from a vendor, placing them in a medicine bag, and, after hitting the combined button, creating potions.  Alchemy is only available to shamans that are level 25 or above.  If you have any information on alchemy, please contact me.

 

Things to Note:

·        Alchemy is an extremely expensive skill

·        Unlike other skills (Tailoring, Blacksmithing, etc.) Alchemy is bound by levels, at a rate of 5 skill points per level above 25

·        Potions can only be used while standing still

·        Effects caused by potions are limited to the spells limitations

·        If the spell that the potion emulates requires a component, so to will the potion

·        Taking two of the same potion will cancel out the effects of that potion

·        Potion buffs stack on normal buffs, but usually don’t stack on other potion buffs
