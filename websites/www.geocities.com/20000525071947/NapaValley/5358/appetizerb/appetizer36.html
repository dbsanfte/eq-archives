Crab Dip II



3/4       Cup           Mayonnaise
3         Tablespoons   Prepared Horseradish
3         Tablespoons   Onion, finely chopped
1         Tablespoon    Worcestershire Sauce
1-12 oz   Can           Crab Meat
2         Dashes        Louisiana Hot Sauce



On a fluke, I changed 'Search Engines' on the internet and the new search engine had a listing just for recipes. This is the first one that I copied, tried and decided to keep as part of my cookbook. Believe me, I have tried many, many bad recipes.


Directions:

Thoroughly mix the Mayonnaise, Horseradish, Chopped Onion and Worcestershire Sauce in a bowl. Mix in the Crabmeat and then season to taste with the Salt, Pepper and Louisiana Hot Sauce. Cover and chill for at least one hour prior to serving.
