Broccoli Cheese Chicken and Stuffing


4 1/2   Cups          Pepperidge Farm® Herb 
                      Seasoned Stuffing
2       Tablespoons   Margarine or Butter, melted
3/4     Cup           Water
1       Package       (10 ounces) Frozen Chopped 
                      Broccoli, thawed
6       Each          Skinless, Boneless Chicken 
                      Breast Halves.
1       Can           Campbell's® Condensed Broccoli Cheese Soup
1/3     Cup           Milk 
                      Paprika



Yet one more recipe I 'appropriated' off the Internet.


Directions:

Crush 1/2 cup stuffing and mix with 1 tablespoon margarine. Set aside.

Mix water, remaining margarine and broccoli. Add remaining stuffing. Mix lightly. Spoon into a 3-quart shallow baking dish. Arrange chicken over stuffing. Sprinkle paprika over chicken.

Mix soup and milk. Pour over chicken. Sprinkle reserved stuffing mixture over soup mixture.

Bake at 400ºF. for 40 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink. Serves 6.

TIP: To melt margarine, remove wrapper and place in microwave-safe cup. Cover and microwave on HIGH 30 seconds.
