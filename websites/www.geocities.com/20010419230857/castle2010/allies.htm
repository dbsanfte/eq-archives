Allies

Rule for guild alliances: (Soldiers that are contact about an alliance will contact a member of the officers board)

The Coalition Defense Force (CDF) will not fall into alliances lightly. Allied guilds must be like minded in general and negotiations with the guild will be conducted by the Commander a senior officer before the commander gives approval. Once an alliance has been formed the CDF will take it seriously. All soldiers will be given standing orders to defend their allies as if they were a part of the army itself. Soldiers with the capability will assist through magic whenever possible by buffing and healing. Allies will be respected as brothers and allied officers will be given respect in accordance to their ranks. In turn we expect the same. To many reports of allies ignoring soldiers of the army during battle or refusing to assist can and will led to the alliance being abolished.
