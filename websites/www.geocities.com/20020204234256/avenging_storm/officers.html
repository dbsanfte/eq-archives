Avenging Storm Officers help with the running and administration of the guild. Duties of an officer include but are not limited to:

  • Interviews and invitation of new members
  • Mediation of disputes between members
  • The planning of guild events
  • Collection of monthly dues
  • Attendance of officer meetings whenever possible
  • Voting on possible guild policy changes

Guild offficers are voted upon by the guild, and all should do their best to exemplify the ideals of Avenging Storm as they, like other members, are our representatives to Norrath at large. The following is the current officer roster for Avenging Storm as of October 19th:
