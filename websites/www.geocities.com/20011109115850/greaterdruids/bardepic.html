Bard-Singing Short Sword
Most of this information is complete, though if you find any details I have left out, please post it or email me with it and I will fix it.
A)   Baldric Slezaf in Dreadlands start it, needs 4 items (if you turn in these items he has requested, you will get the sword as a reward)
1)   page 24 (actually in two halves)
2)   page 25
3)   Lute made by Forper Fizfla

B)  To get top part page 24 go to West Karana and see Konia Swiftfoot (located in one of the guard towers)
1)   Konia Swiftfoot starts the race gives you a torch.
2)   Take to Fajio Knejo in Misty Thicket (close to the wall guards)
3)   Take that torch to Andad Filla in South Ro (close to Innothule zone)
4)   Take that torch to Misty Tekchita in Lake Rathe (by arena zone)
5)   Take the ring you get from there back to Konia Swiftfoot.

C)   Botom half of page 24-(rewarded by Konia Swiftfoot), wants Mahlin's Bongos.
1)    Braenar Swiftsong (located in South Karana, near the fountain Nostrolo is at) will give you an invoice after you ask "what doll".
2)   Solusek A give the gnome vendor (Marfen Bsomething, sells Tinker bags) the invoice and recieve a "doll".
3)   Take the doll to the Estate of Unrest and give the "doll" to Serra, who is next to the gnomish spelunker.  Serra will give you a note.
4)   Take the note from Serra back to Braenar, he will give a new note for Maligar.
5)   Take the note from Braenar and recieve "Mahlin's Bongo's).
7)   Take the Bongo's to Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana to get the bottom of Page 24.

D)   To get page 25 go to South Karana where you will find another woodelf bard Kelkin Mekia she wants 3 items.
1)   Guts of a named chromatic drake in skyfire (Edrik the Old).
2)   Guts from the new named wurm in Burning Woods (Nezekezena).
3)   Guts from the Huge Oynx Drake in Rathe Mountains (Blackwing).

E)   Now you need Forpar's Lute.  Forpar sends you on 3 quests for the 3 parts before constructing the lute.  Hand in the following 3 parts to get the lute.
1)   Neck of the Lute (see F)
2)   Body of the Lute (see G)
3)   Trakanon Gut Strings (see H)

F)   (this reward's the Head & Neck only of the Lute, then gives quest for body).
1)   First you need to get horn from Ocean of Tears (Alluring horn from Quag Maelstrom).
2)   Take the alluring horn from Ocean of Tears to a half elf bard in Butcherblock, by the docks (Vedico).
3)   Take the note she gives you to Forpar, he will speak, gives you a note and a list.
4)   Backbone of an Ancient Fishman-Phnagel's Spine from Kedge Keep.
5)   Amalgyn Tentacle-Amygdalan Tendril from Plane of Fear.
6)   Petrified skull of a Lycanthrope-petrfied werewolf skull from Karnor on Drolvarg Warlord.
7)   Take all 3 items and the note back give to Forpar and he gives you back the Head and Neck of the Lute and the quest for the body.

G)   Body of the lute, quest by Foplar: (will award the body).
1)   Large red Dragon Scale (Lord Nagafen of Solusek B (Nagafen's Lair) or (Talendor of Skyfire).
2)   Large white Dragon Scale (Lady Vox of permafrost or Gorenaire of Dreadlands).
3)   Metal bits (smithed metal bits, only one is needed).

H)   Strings for the Lute-Trakanon Gut Strings.
1)   Kill Trakanon, loot gut strings.
