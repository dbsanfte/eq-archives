The Guild Charter for the Knights of Eternity

    The Knights of Eternity is a guild of friendship, sharing, fun, and family. These principles are our guides. Let us follow them to honor and glory.

    We are friends. We work together, play together, laugh together, fight together, and occasionally, we die together. Each one of us cares how the other feels, considers those feelings at all times, and supports the well being of each other, as well as of ourselves.

    We share. We give to each other of our food, our equipment, our knowledge, our skills, our resources, and our time. We expect nothing in return, but we receive the support of our friends when our own time of need arises. Not one of us feels they are owed anything. We all give freely of ourselves.

    We have fun. None of us prevents any other from having fun. None of us have fun at another's expense. We enjoy each other's company, but do not restrict the fun of anyone. If one of us must leave to another place, and that causes another to find another source of fun, it is not held against anyone. There is no place here for grudges, they are not fun.

    We are a family. Each is the brother and the sister of the other. We do not hurt our family. We heal when we can. We defend when there is need, even at the loss of our own lives. There is no greater glory then to share honor with our family. There is no greater service then giving our life for our family. There is no greater joy then knowing our family is there for us.

    We are the Knights of Eternity. The infinite horizon is our realm. The infinite stars are our destiny. With these principles of friendship, sharing, fun, and family, we shall
certainly prevail.

--Camila Razgraest, Knight of Eternity

 

A Member's Perspective:

"By serving each other, we become free for all eternity."

Norrath: a vast world, containing many lands, people and conflict. A world in trouble and enslaved to itself. Selfishness is destroying our world. Selfishness of the individual, selfishness of the races, has put the world of Norrath in peril.

The Knights of Eternity represent folks from all lands, races and classes. It is our desire to insure the survival of Norrath. By rising above ourselves and overlooking each others differences, there is hope for our lands. By serving each other, we become free for all eternity.

Here you will find Dark Elves helping Wood Elves, Paladins fighting with Necromancers and Clerics healing Shadow Knights. Join us in our quest, our Ever Quest, to free you and the world of Norrath for all eternity.
