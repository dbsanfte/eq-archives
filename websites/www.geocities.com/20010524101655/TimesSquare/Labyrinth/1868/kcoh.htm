The Knight's Code of Honour

These are the basic codes governing the virtues of the Knighthood

A Knight is Sworn to Uphold Valour

You shall be courageous in all your actions

A Knight's Heart Contains Only Virtue

You shall always be the champion of that which is Right and Good and the foe of Evil and Injustice

A Knight's Steel Defends the Weak

You shall come to the aid of those weaker than yourself

 

A Knight's Actions Defend the Innocent

You shall come to the aide of innocent victims

A Knight's Power Knows Only Righteousness

You shall behave lawfully, and live your life for the good of all

A Knight's Words Speaks Only Truth

You will always speak the truth with wisdom and honor

A Knight's Wrath is Justice

You will use your authority to act against evil, in a fair and lawful manner
