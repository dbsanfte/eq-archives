---Site Update---

    Update List:
  • January 14th, 2000 Complete site redesign.

The EQRPA site has been undergoing a major transformation. You may still visit our old site or go straight to our new site (Please note, this is our new site location at www.geocities.com/eqrpa instead of www.geocities.com/chronicler_kithra - we're doing this so we're more convenient to get to). We left both pages up so you could compare and send us comments your comments.

Improvements to our site include:

  • New layout, allowing those unfamiliar with us to browse start to finish while letting repeat visitors jump to virtually any point in our site.
  • New Background Submission form - allowing players to submit their race/class/diety, any titles or nicknames they have, specify gender and whether is they wish the character to be a member of the EQRPA.
  • Use of Style Sheets. Assuming you are using a fairly current browser, the style sheets vastly improve the appearance of the site. More style modifications will be added on as time permits

Future improvements include:

  • The constant updating of character backgrounds, adding new servers as submissions come in.
  • Adding message boards to the site
  • Details on our "GM" program - for the bold who wish to help coordinate all our members and their stories
  • A general roleplaying page, what it is and how to get started (for those that have never tried it before)
  • And, of course, graphics to spice up the pages appearance
