Yummy Cake


1-18.5 oz   Package   Yellow Cake Mix
1/2         Cup       Butter, softened
4           Large     Eggs, divided
1-8 oz      Package   Cream Cheese, softened
1-16 oz     Package   Powdered Sugar
1           Cup       Chopped Nuts



My stepdaughter made this cake and upon sampling my first bite I knew it had to become a part of my cookbook and therefore my home page. Thanks Kristin.


Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, combined the Cake Mix, the Butter and '2' of the Eggs and beat until the mixture is smooth. Spread this mixture into a 'greased' 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan.

Combined the Cream Cheese and the remaining '2' eggs in a mixing bowl and beat well. Gradually add the Powdered Sugar beating until the mixture is smooth. Stir in the nuts and then spoon the cream cheese mixture evenly on the cake batter.

Bake for 45 minutes in a pre-heated 350-degree oven. Cool before serving.
