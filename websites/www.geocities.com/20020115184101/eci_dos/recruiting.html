About Us
We are a friendly guild and our mission is to help each other while  having a good time playing EverQuest.
New members are always welcomed.

The goal for the Dominion Of Souls is to grow in the number of members along with providing the best fun time as our members explore the world of EverQuest while leveling their character's.

Together we can slay the evils of the land!
How To Join
Welcome future Dominion Of Souls members!
Thank you for your interest in our guild.  We feel the DOS
is one of the best guild's on E'ci.

Our members are people who enjoy playing the game, meeting new people, grouping to share their skills and helping not only each other but everyone in the game.

If you feel you can be such a member then please complete our Application by clicking on the link at the top of this page.  You will be contacted shortly after.  You can also contact one of our members within the game and they will be glad to put you in contact with an Elder or an Officer of the guild.
Come join us and have some fun!
Note:Officer's, Elder's and Member's if your interested in recruiting someone please read the guidelines noted below.
Recruiting Guideline's
  • Recruits MUST be interviewed and group several times with an Elder  prior to inviting him/her into the guild.
  • Recruits should be informed that a "Application" must be completed to ensure that character's name and character info gets listed on the guild's Roster.   Listing this info will allow members to view stats of other members for grouping purposes.  Without a completed Application that character's info will not be posted. 
  • Recruit people of any class, race or level.
  • Recruit friendly, polite and well mannered persons.
  • Officer's can invite recruits into the guild only after the guidelines for joining have been completed.
  • Only the Guild Leader and Officer's have the power to remove members from the guild.  Officer's must notify the Guild Leader ASAP after they have done so.
  • Guideline's must be followed so that  removal of any members is avoided.
  • Officer's welcome new members into the guild CHAT so he/she can meet other members.
  • Officer's can invite alternate characters of current members.  Alt's may be any level.  Alt characters should be informed to complete another Application if they want that Alt's info and stats posted on the Guild Roster.  Without a completed Application  his/her info and stats will not be posted.
  • When recruiting a new member the Elder's and guild Officer's should direct them to the "Dominion Of Souls" web page.
  • Friends from the game or real life friends can be invited into the guild only after the application and requirements are completed.
  • Do NOT advertise in zone chat or elsewhere that you are recruiting.
  • Do NOT invite people who you know nothing about.
  • Do NOT actively recruit members from other guilds unless they ask you about your guild.
IMPORTANT: All current and new member's please periodically complete another application so we can keep the guild's Roster up to date.   Thank You!
