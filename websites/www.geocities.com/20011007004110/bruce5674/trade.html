Trade Supplies

Kaladim has much to offer the artisan and the tradesman. All guilds offer limited training in the various disciplines, and we have facilities for most of the trades. Here's a listing for your conveniance:

Blacksmithing:
Molds: Outside Kaladim in the Butcherblocks. Look in the three houses off to the left.
Forge: Outside Redfist Metal in South Kaladim, Beside Everhott's in North Kaladim.
Ore: No known supplier.

Pottery:
Sketches: On road to the Greater Faydark, last set of houses before the pass.
Clay: Same place.
Pottery Wheel: By Gurtha's Wear in South Kaladim.
Kiln: By Gurtha's.

Jewelry:
Jeweler's Kit: Upstairs in Everhott's Forge.
Gems: Check one of the Dwarves in the Mine (Info provided by Hammerforge, a cleric on the Innoruuk Server).
Metal Bars: Same place as Kit.

Brewing:
Still: In Pub Kal.
Recipies: No known supplier.
Ingrdients: No known supplier.

Baking:
Oven: Next to Gurtha's.
Recipies/Supplies: Outside Kaladim, in the three huts.

Sewing:
Kits: Tanned Assets, Gurtha's.
Pelts: Try the Greater Faydark for ruined wolf pelts.
Patterns:Tanned Assets.

Alchemy:
No supplies what so ever.

Fletching:
Kit: No known supplier.
Vanes, Shafts, Heads, Fletchings, and Nocks: The female Dwarf in the Shield and Spear.
Bowyer's Supplies: No known supplier.
