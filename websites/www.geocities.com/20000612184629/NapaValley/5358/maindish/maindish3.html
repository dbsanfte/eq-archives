Stuffed Green Peppers 



3        Whole        Green Peppers
1/2      Pound        Ground Meat
1        Cup          Plain Dry Bread Crumbs
1        Tablespoon   Chopped Onions
1        Teaspoon     Salt
1/4      Teaspoon     Black Pepper
1-8 oz   Can          Tomato Sauce



This recipe came from one of Betty Crockers® cookbooks.



Directions:

Cut the Green Peppers in half, lengthwise and core them removing all the seeds. Rinse them and let them drain on a paper towel.

In a large mixing bowl, combined all the ingredients and mix thoroughly. Place a portion of this mixture in the green peppers and place the stuffed green peppers in a baking dish. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and cook in a 350 degree oven for 45 minutes. Remove the aluminum foil and bake an additional 15 minutes.
