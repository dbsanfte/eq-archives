Eggplant Casserole


1     Large         Eggplant
2     Large         Eggs
1/2   Cup           Canned Milk
1/2   Cup           Plain Bread Crumbs
1/2   Medium        Onion, chopped
3/4   Cup           Shredded Cheese
3/4   Cup           Cracker Crumbs
4     Tablespoons   Butter or Margarine
To Taste            Salt
To Taste            Black Pepper



Yet another recipe from my wife's 'Secret' Cookbook…….LOL!!!!


Directions:


Pare the Eggplant and cut it into 1/2-inch slices. Boil the eggplant in salted water for approximately 15 minutes or until it is tender. Drain well and then, in a large mixing bowl, mash the eggplant. Add the Eggs, Milk, Bread Crumbs, Onion, Cheese, Salt and Black Pepper to the mashed eggplant and mix thoroughly.

Grease a 1 1/2-quart baking dish and then pour the eggplant mixture into it, spreading it out evenly. Sprinkle the Cracker Crumbs over the top and dot with Butter or Margarine.

Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 30 minutes.
