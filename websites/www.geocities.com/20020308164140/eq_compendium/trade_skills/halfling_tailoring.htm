Halfling Tailoring

General:

          Very little is know about this skill, other than that racial studded and reinforced armor can be made through this skill.  All these items can only be made by Halflings using a Vale Tailoring Kit. The Brute Hides can be found in various zones on Kunark, including Frontier Mountains and Warsliks Woods.  If you have any other information about this trade skill, please contact me.
