Frozen Lemon Mousse


1 -12 oz   Can    Evaporated Milk
1          Cup    Sugar
1/4        Cup    Lemon Juice
1/2        Pint   Raspberries or Strawberries



This one came from a home page that set a romantic mood involving dinner. It also had a Beef Roast Flambe, similar to my Steak Diane. Gentleman, using food to set a romantic mood really does work, and the ladies appreciate it and show their appreciation, if you get my drift. *EG*


Directions:

Pour the Evaporated Milk into a stainless steel mixing bowl and place it in the freezer until ice crystals start to form around the edges of the milk. Once the crystals have started to form, whip the milk until it is very stiff. Beat in the Sugar and the Lemon Juice gradually.

Pour the lemon mixture into two parfait glasses or champagne flutes or wine goblets and freeze for several hours. Just prior to serving, garnish with fresh Raspberries or Strawberries.
