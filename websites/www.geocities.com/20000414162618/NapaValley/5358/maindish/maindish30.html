Pepper Steak


1 1/2	Lbs.         Round Streak, cut into 
                     1/8" strips
1	Large        Green Bell Pepper, cut 
                     into 1" pieces
1	Large        Yellow Onion, quartered
1	Stalk(Rib)   Celery, chopped coarsely
1/2	Cup          Soy Sauce
1/2	Cup          Vegetable Oil
2	Cups         Water



A co-worker brought in on of her cook books so that she could make a copy of one of the recipes she makes and brings to work. As I glanced through the rest of the cook book, I noticed this one. This is one of my favorite meals at a Chinese Restaurant.


Directions:

Put the Soy Sauce and Vegetable Oil in a large bowl and marinate the Steak Strips in this mixture for 1/2 hour.

Pour the marinate and Steak Strips into a pan, add the Water, cover and simmer until the meat is tender. Add the Green Pepper, Onions and Celery and continue to simmer until the vegetables are tender.
