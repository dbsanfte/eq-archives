Jalapeno Cornbread



2            Cups        Self-rising Cornmeal Mix
1-16 oz      Container   Sour Cream
3            Large       Eggs, lightly beaten
1/3          Cup         Vegetable Oil
1            Medium      Onion, chopped
1-8 1/2 oz   Can         Cream-style Corn
1-4 oz       Can         Chopped Jalapeno Peppers
2            Cups        Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese



This cornbread was brought to work by one of my coworkers. The minute I tasted it I knew I had to have the recipe. Linda was kind enough to bring it to me the very next day.



Directions:

Mix all the ingredients together in a large mixing bowl. Lightly grease a 9" x 13" pan and then pour this mixture into it.


Bake in a pre-heated 350degree oven for 1 hour or until the top is Golden.
