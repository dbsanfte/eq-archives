Ken's Chicken


4	Each          Chicken Breasts, 
                      skin on and ribs in
4	Tablespoons   Lemon Juice
4	Teaspoons     Lawry's® Season Salt
1 1/2	Cups          All-Purpose Flour
2	Teaspoons     Salt
2	Teaspoons     Black Pepper
1	Teaspoon      Poultry Seasoning
1	Each          Paper Bag



I received this recipe from a very, very good friend in a chat room. It only took her a month to get the recipe from Ken. You see he doesn't give up his special recipes easily. I started to ask her how she finally persuaded him to give it to me, then decided I should NOT Go There.!!


Directions:

Preheat the oven to 450 degrees.

Put the All-Purpose Flour, Salt, Black Pepper and Poultry Seasoning in the Paper Bag.

Pour 1 tablespoon of the Lemon Juice over each chicken breast and then sprinkle 1 Teaspoon of the Season Salt on each breast.

Put the chicken breasts, one at a time, in the bag and shake well, this will cover the breast with the ingredients. Place each chicken breast, skin side down on a rack.

Place the chicken breasts in the oven and immediately turn the oven down to 425 degrees. Bake at this temperature for 35 minutes. Turn the breasts over, and turn the oven down to 375 degrees and continue to cook for an additional 20 minutes.
