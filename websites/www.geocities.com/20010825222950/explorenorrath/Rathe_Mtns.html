Rathe Mountains Jeweler


There is a curiousity in the Rathe Mountains jewelry shop: a gnomish jeweler by the name of Darfumpel Zirubbel who actually offers his services, not just merchandise. He is located in a hut at pos4100, pos900, near the Lake Rathe zone.

The drawback, of course, is that nobody seems to know (beyond one quest for Shadow Knights) how to get him to talk about what he can do for you.  Possibly a broken or unimplemented series of quests, but interesting nonetheless.

One word of note: He offers two services, gemcutting and jewelry repair. For the gemcutting aspect, all that triggers him as far as rare stones thusfar is the shadow knight's hyacinth stone (I tried all normal and planar gems, and none worked). There is, however, a tinkered gemcutter that members of the gnome race are able to make, and its use is yet unknown. Go here for some interesting player comments and theories regarding this unique device.

Here is the text of my conversation with him, including key phrases that wouldn't work. My next course of action will most likely be scouring the boards for mentions of 'broken' jewelry, and seeing if either asking him about the items, or giving them to him, results in any progress on this. Good luck in your endeavours to puzzle this NPC's existance out!
