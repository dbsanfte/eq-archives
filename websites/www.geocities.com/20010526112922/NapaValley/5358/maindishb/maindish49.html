Chicken Marinade


4         Each       Boneless/Skinless
                     Chicken Breasts
1-15 oz   Bottle     French's® Worcester Sauce
1         Cup        Honey
1         Teaspoon   Cinnamon



I was talking to a coworker during a smoke break, yes I am ONE of those, and he gave me this recipe. He specified that it HAD to be French's® Worcester Sauce and no other brand would do. I didn't have a problem with that because I use mostly French's® brand products and their Worcester Sauce is one of them.


Directions:

Place the Chicken Breasts, one at a time, between two sheets of wax paper and using a rolling pin, beat them until they are approximately 1/4" thick.

In a large bowl, thoroughly mix the Worcester Sauce, Honey and Cinnamon. Place the chicken breasts in the sauce, cover the bowl with aluminum foil and put them into the refrigerator. The chicken should marinate for at least 8 hours and should periodically be turned.

This chicken can be either cooked on a Bar-B-Que grill, for approximately 3 to 4 minutes per side, or in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes. You need to be extremely careful as this chicken cooks up very quickly.

Note: Towards the end of the cooking cycle, you can baste the chicken with either Honey or your favorite Bar-B-Que Sauce.
