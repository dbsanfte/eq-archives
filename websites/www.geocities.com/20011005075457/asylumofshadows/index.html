Want to welcome eveyone to the guild web page. All the older members in the guild who havn't registered your characters go to members page and register your characters asap. All you new members and 1 timers here goto the new members guide that will help you and let you know what you need to do.
Below I will give you a brief description about the diffrent pages in our guild web page to help guide you to the area you want to go to:
New Members Guide-helps the new members in the guild what they need to do there 1 time to the web page.
Message Board-where the guild communicates with each other.
Officers Round Table-officers only area.
Guild Members-area for all guild members go to register thier characters in the guild(guild magic).
Recent News- Recent news in the guild.
Upcoming Raids-Places where the guild will be raiding...gives time /dates(sundays usually)/where/lvl range/printable maps/objectives/ect ect.
Weekly Hunting Guide-Area designated by the guild where a certain lvl of characters can hunt and get good exper...will change every 1-2 weeks.
Charter-What the guild beliefs are
Guild Vault-Items that are avaliable to guild members.
List of Members-List of all the mains in the guild so we can determine what class's we need to concentrate on recruiting into the guild.
Guild Law-This is the law by which we are bound to in this guild.
Guild Council-List of the officers in the guild and thier e-mail adress's.
Guild Info-Misc info on the guild and the requirements to become an officer.
War Room-Guild chat room for guild meetings and misc use.
Links-Links to other sites related to EQ ...broken in to 3 catagories general/class specific/and maps..weapon...armor.
Fun Page-Area that has nothing to do with EQ...got games/recipies and other misc things in there.
Antonican Brotherhood-Link to AB a guild were allied with.
