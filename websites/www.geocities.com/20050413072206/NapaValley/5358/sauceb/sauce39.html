Hot Cranberry Punch



2-38 oz   Bottles       Cranberry Cocktail
2-36 oz   Cans          Pineapple Juice
8         Each          Cinnamon Sticks, broken in half
2         Tablespoons   Whole Cloves
2         Tablespoons   Whole Allspice
2         Cups          Brown Sugar, lightly packed



This recipe came from the Office Christmas Party where my wife works. You will need a 36-cup electric coffee pot.



Directions:

Pour the Cranberry Cocktail and Pineapple Juice in the coffee pot and then finish filling the pot with water.


Put a filter in the basket of the coffee pot and then place the Cinnamon Sticks, Cloves, Allspice and Brown Sugar in the filter. Turn the pot on and let it run through the normal brewing cycle.
