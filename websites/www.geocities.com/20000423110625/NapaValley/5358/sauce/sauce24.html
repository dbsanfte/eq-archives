Ranch Salad Dressing



2     Teaspoons    Dried Minced Onions
1/2   Teaspoon     Salt
1/8   Teaspoon     Garlic Powder
1/2   Teaspoon     MSG (if desired)
1     Tablespoon   Dried Parsley Flakes
1     Cup          Mayonnaise
1     Cup          Butter Milk



I also found this recipe on the Internet. It's very simple, easy to prepare and tastes great.



Directions:

Combine all the ingredients in a glass jar and shake well. Refrigerator for at least 2 hours prior to serving and make sure to shake it well just before using.
