Candy Corn & Peanuts



1-14 oz   Package   Candy Corn
1-16 oz   Jar       Planters® Dry Roasted Peanuts



Now this may sound a little crazy to you but if you have ever had a "Pay Day®" candy bar, these snack tastes just like it. A special thanks goes out to the Nurse that my wife works with that gave her this recipe. *S*


Directions:

In a large bowl, combined the Candy Corn and the Peanuts. Mix them well and then store them in airtight jars.
