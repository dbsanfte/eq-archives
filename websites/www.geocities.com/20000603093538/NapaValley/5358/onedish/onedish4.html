Weekend Brunch Casserole


1     LB        Owens® Extra Mild Country 
                Sausage
1     Can       Pillsbury® Crescent Rolls
2     Cup       Shredded Cheddar Cheese
3     Large     Eggs
3/4   Cup       Milk
1/4   Teaspoon  Salt
1/8   Teaspoon  Black Pepper



While grocery shopping at a local Tom Thumb Page supermarket, I sampled the Owens® Extra Mild Country Sausage in the meat department and discovered this recipe on the side of the package. I actually find a lot of recipes this way. The best place to find new recipes is in the soup section of the supermarket. The Campbell's® Soup Company is always publishing new recipes on their products.



Directions:

Brown the Owens® Extra Mild Country Sausage in a medium skillet, stirring occasionally. Drain all excess grease from the skillet.

Remove the Crescent Rolls from the cans. Line both the bottom and sides of a 5 x 9 baking dish with the crescent rolls. Pour the browned sausage into the baking dish and cover it with the Shredded Cheddar Cheese.

Combined the Eggs, Milk, Salt and Pepper in a large bowl and mix well. Pour this mixture over the sausage and cheese and bake in a preheated 425 degree oven for approximately 15 minutes or until the eggs are set. When the eggs are set and the crescent rolls are a golden brown, remove it from the oven and let it stand for 5 minutes and then cut it into squares.
