Quesadillas



1	Lb          Lean Bacon
1/2	Medium      Yellow Onion, Chopped
2	Cups        Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1-8 oz	Container   Sour Cream
6	Each        Tortillas



A friend encouraged me to try these at a Mexican restaurant one day at lunchtime. Needless to say, I fell in love with them and I quickly figured out how to make them. It has become a family tradition for me to prepare these on Christmas morning for breakfast. Although my kids will eat them any time I make them.


Directions:

Fry the Bacon and drain it on paper towels. Pour off the bacon grease, reserving 2 tablespoons in the pan. Sauté the chopped Onions, cooking them until they are tender and translucent.

Place 1/2 cup of water in the back corner of the microwave oven and heat, on high power, for 20 seconds. Place a Tortilla in the center of the microwave and heat for 15 seconds, with the cup of water still in the back corner. (Note: This will make the tortilla very pliable and easier to work with)

Remove the tortilla from the microwave and place 3 to 4 strips of fried bacon on one half of the tortilla. Place a portion of the sauté onions over the bacon and then sprinkle some of the shredded Cheddar Cheese on top of that.

Spread some Sour Cream on the top half of the tortilla and then fold the top half over the bottom half. Fry, in an ungreased skillet, until each side is a little brown and the cheese is melted. Cut into triangles and serve with Sour Cream.
