Hidden Valley Ranch Red Pepper Dip



2    Cups      Sour Cream
1    Envelope  Hidden Valley Ranch® Original 
               Ranch® Salad Dressing Mix
1/2  Cup       Diced Bottled Pimentos, 
               (Drained) or Diced Red 
               Peppers
Dippers        Assorted Raw Vegetable



I haven’t a clue where this recipe came from. My best guess is that it was part of a "flyer" in our local newspaper.


Directions:

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix thoroughly. This dip is best if it has been chilled overnight.
