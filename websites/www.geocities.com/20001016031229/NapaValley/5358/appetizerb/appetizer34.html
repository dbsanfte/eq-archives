Parmesan-Spinach Spread



1-10 oz   Package    Frozen, Chopped Spinach, thawed
1-3 oz    Package    Philadelphia® Cream Cheese, softened
3/4       Cup        Mayonnaise
1 1/4     Cups       Freshly grated Parmesan Cheese, divided
1/4       Cup        Minced Onion
1         Teaspoon   Dried Italian Seasoning
1         Teaspoon   Louisiana Hot Sauce
1/2       Teaspoon   Garlic Powder
1/4       Teaspoon   Freshly Ground Black Pepper
1/2       Teaspoon   Paprika
                     Assorted Crackers or Party Rye Bread



This appetizer was brought to one of the office Birthday Parties. Immediately after tasting it, I requested the recipe from the fellow employee that had prepared it. Thanks Linda. *S*


Directions:

Drain the Spinach and press between paper towels to remove any excess moisture.

In a medium bowl, combined the Cream Cheese and Mayonnaise, stirring with a wire whisk until smooth. Add the Spinach, 1 Cup of Parmesan Cheese, Onion, Italian Seasoning, Hot Sauce, Garlic Powder and Black Pepper and stir thoroughly. Spoon this mixture into a 1-quart casserole dish.

Bake, uncovered, in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 10 minutes. Sprinkle with the remaining Parmesan Cheese and also the Paprika and bake an additional 10 minutes.

Serve with assorted crackers or party rye bread.
