Shrimp Ball




1/2        Lb.           Small Shrimp, cooked and 
                         coarsely chopped
2          Tablespoons   Minced Onion
1/4        Teaspoon      Salt
1 - 8 oz   Package       Philadelphia® Cream Cheese, softened
1          Tablespoon    Fresh Lemon Juice
1          Tablespoon    Horseradish
1/2        Cup           Parsley, finely chopped


In the process of helping the Napa Valley Community Leaders with a summer fun game, I came across this recipe. This is a great appetizer for just about any type of party that you attend and are requested to bring something.



Directions:

Mix the Shrimp, Onion, Salt, Cream Cheese, Lemon Juice and Horseradish together and combined thoroughly. Divide the mixture in half and shape each half into a ball. Roll the balls in the Parsley and then cover with plastic wrap. Keep the balls refrigerated until you are ready to serve them. Serve with a variety of crackers.
