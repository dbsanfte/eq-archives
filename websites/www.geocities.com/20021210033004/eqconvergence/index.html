Convergence is a guild on the Seventh Hammer server in the world of Norrath. We are a guild built around friendship, honour and trust. To us, it isn't about the great loot or constant experience (yes it is nice) but about each other. We fight together and die together in honour. We honour the play nice rules and always exhibit good conduct to bring honour to us and the Convergence name.

Invite Policy: Our trial member guidelines are quite simple. Each trial member must be of a mature nature, have 5 sponsors in good standing with the guild and have reached level 15. The prospective member will be asked to post about themseleves in the Trail Member Forum and should be aware of our charter to see if they agree with our philosophies. After the trail member has posted each of his sponsors should reply. Once the 5 sponsors have posted you will become a full member of the guild.

Loot & Guild Raid Policy: Each raid leader will post the loot rules of their raid. We honor a need before greed policy and the raid leader will post any specifics regarding their raid. If you disagree with their loot order, you don't have to go. It is the responsibility of each raid leader to post their raid, the start time, loot rules, number of people required, expected commitment and any other information about strategy and logistics.

Disputes: If a member is causing dishonor to the guild with their actions it will be reviewed by the Officers and myself. If the act is such a disruption to others, they will be guild-removed and on probation for 2 weeks. During those 2 weeks, the Officers will talk to all people involved including the member and vote on if the member will be permanently removed or reinvited.
