Arch Mage Armor
To start this quest find Kyla Frostbeard Located in Frostbeard's Furs & Leather in Thurgadin.

You say, "Hail Kyla Frosbeard"

Kyla Frostbeard says, "Greetings to you.  I seek those who call themselves magicians.  Are you a magician, little one?"

You say, "I am a magician"

Kyla Frostbeard says, "I thought so.  I have several tasks for you to accomplish.  Once you have completed them I will have a cap, a robe, sleeves, wristbands, gloves, leggings and boots to reward you with."

To obtain the armor you need to turn in the appropriate piece of Torn Enchanted Silk clothing and the required Gems.

     Note:  Torn Enchanted Silk drops from level 55+ Adjuncts in Kael Drakkal.
