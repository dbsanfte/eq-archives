Grandma Flynn's Hot Dogs


1   Package   Oscar Meyer's® Hot Dogs
1   Package   Hot Dog Buns
8   Slices    Boar's Head® American Cheese
To Taste      Mustard, Ketchup, Diced Onions, Pickle
              Relishes
              Aluminum Foil


If you are a "true" Hot Dog Lover, as I am, you will love this "Down & Dirty" Hot Dog recipe. This one comes from my ex-wife's mother, who had to feed 8 kids on a Cops Salary.





Directions:

Place an Oscar Meyer Hot Dog that has been wrapped in a piece of Boar's Head American cheese in a Hot Dog Bun. Garnish it with the desired condiments. Wrap it in Aluminum Foil and place it in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes.

Just that simple, but "Oh so Good"……hmmm, seems there is a TV Chef that uses the saying………LOL!!!!!
