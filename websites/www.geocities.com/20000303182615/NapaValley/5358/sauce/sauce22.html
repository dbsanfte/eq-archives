Seafood Cocktail Sauce



1/2   Cup          Heinz® Ketchup
1/2   Medium       Lemon, peeled, seeded and 
                   cut into wedges
1/2   Tablespoon   Horseradish
1/4   Cup          Heinz® Chili Sauce
1/2   Teaspoon     Worcestershire Sauce



I was able to find this recipe on the Internet by using one of the many browsers; I just can't remember which one I used. Oh well, it ain't easy getting old.



Directions:

Put all the ingredients into a blender or food processor and mix until the sauce is smooth. Refrigerate for at least one hour prior to serving.
