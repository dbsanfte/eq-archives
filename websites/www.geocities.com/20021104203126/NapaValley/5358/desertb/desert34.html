Holiday Rum Balls


2       Cups          Vanilla Wafer Crumbs, 
                      finely crushed
1 1/2   Cups          Confectioner's Sugar, divided
1/2     Cup           Walnuts or Pecans, chopped fine
1/4     Cup           Dark Rum
1       Tablespoon    Dark Rum
1/4     Cup           Dark Corn Syrup
2       Tablespoons   Butter, melted



There are web sites on the Internet that will automatically email you everyday, some send Jokes, some send automated cartoons. I have a web site sending me a new recipe everyday. This was one of the recipes that I received during the 1999 Christmas Holiday season.


Directions:

In a large bowl, combine the Vanilla Wafer Crumbs, 1 cup of the Confectioner's Sugar, Walnuts/Pecans, all the Dark Rum, Corn Syrup and melted Butter. Knead these ingredients together and then separate the dough and roll into 1-inch balls. Roll the balls in the remaining Confectioner's Sugar. Arrange the rum balls on a baking sheet, cover well with plastic wrap, and let them sit for 48 hours.

Yields approximately 3 dozen.
