Dave's Mashed Potatoes


1/2   Cup        Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1/4   Cup        Bacon Bits
6     Servings   Hungry Jack® Instant Mashed Potatoes



I concocted this recipe after having something similar to it at a local restaurant. I also use these potatoes in my Open-Faced Roast Beef Sandwich.


Directions:


Prepare the 6 servings of the Instant Mashed Potatoes according to the package instructions but prior to adding the Potato Flakes, stir in the Cheddar Cheese and Bacon Bits. Once the cheese starts to melt, add the Potato Flakes and finish cooking the Mashed Potatoes according to the remaining instructions.
