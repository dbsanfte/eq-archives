Farmer's Chili


1   Lb.         Lean Ground Beef
1   Can         Kidney Beans
1   Can         Pinto Beans
1   Teaspoon    Salt
3   Teaspoons   Chili Powder
1   Large       Onion, chopped
1   Can         Diced Tomatoes
1   Cup         Water


This is another recipe that my wife had stashed away in her personal cookbook.



Directions:

Brown the Ground Beef and Onion in a large skillet, draining off any excess grease. Add the Kidney Beans, Pinto Beans, Salt, Chili Powder, Diced Tomatoes and Water and simmer for two hours, stirring occasionally.
