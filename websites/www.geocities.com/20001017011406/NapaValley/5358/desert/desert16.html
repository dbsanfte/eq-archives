Creole Loaf Cake


2     Cups          Sugar
3/4   Cup           Wesson® Oil
2     Medium        Eggs
2     Tablespoons   Nestles® Cocoa
2     Cups          Self Rising Flour
1     Teaspoon      Baking Soda
1/2   Cup           Buttermilk
1     Teaspoon      Vanilla Extract
1/4   Teaspoon      Salt
1     Cup           Boiling Water



When Donna told me how moist this cake turns out I just had to have the recipe. She muttered something about Royalties but I didn't catch it all. LOL!!!


Directions:

In a large bowl, cream the Sugar and the Wesson® Oil. Beat the eggs into this mixture and mix thoroughly.

Sift the Self-Rise Flour, Salt and Nestles® Cocoa into another bowl. Alternately add some of this mixture and the Buttermilk into the Sugar/Oil/Egg mixture until it is all incorporated. Add the Vanilla Extract to the mixture and stir.

Mix the Baking Soda in the Boiling Water and then add it to the mixture and mix thoroughly. Pour the mixture into a greased baking pan and bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour in a pre-heated 300-degree oven. DO NOT overcook.
