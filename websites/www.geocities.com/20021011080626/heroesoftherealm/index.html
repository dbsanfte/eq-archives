Heroes of the Realm is a guild of friends who play Dark Age of Camelot together in the Hibernia realm on the Morgan Le Fey server.

Charter:

Heroes of the Realm is a small, close-knit guild of friends who enjoy the company of our mature, fun, and friendly guildees.  We all help our members, and non members, whenever possible.

 

Goals:

The goal of each and every member of Heroes of the Realm is to have fun. We believe you should enjoy playing the game at whatever level you are and there are fun things to do at every level. All members are treated and respected equally, regardless of their class, level, or how they like to spend their time in the game.

 

Raiding and RvR:

Heroes of the Realm and our alliance group together and raid together often. We encourage members who are interested in participating or planning a raid or event to post your comments or ideas under Events in our forum. Anyone can plan a raid for their level range. Participation in raids is strictly voluntary, no one should ever feel pressure to attend a raid or feel bad about not attending.

 

Recruiting:

Heroes of the Realm is currently recruiting mature, good natured players who make the game more fun for all of us to play. To join us, you must be meet some of the current members and have them sponsor you into the guild. Once invited, your rank will be Knight for an evaluation period of 2 weeks. During those 2 weeks, we ask you to really get to know the Heroes of the Realm and consider if this guild is right for you.  At any time during your 2 week evaluation period, you may leave or be asked to leave, no questions asked and absolutely no hard feelings. At the end of your 2 week evaluation period, should you decide to stay with Heroes of the Realm, you may be promoted to Hero rank or your evaluation period may be extended.  Once you are promoted to Hero, you will become an equal and valued member of the guild with full privileges of wearing the guild emblem and posting in our members only forums.

 

When making your decision to join Heroes of the Realm, please keep in mind that we do not aim to be big and uber.  We do not promise to be able to put together a full, balanced group for every level range at every hour of the day or night, we do not promise to power level our members, and we do not promise to provide you with the best gear in the game.  However, our members are extremely generous in passing on equipment we can't use, will usually drop everything to help a guildee in need, and group together whenever possible.  Every member of this guild must be able to function as a mature individual as well as a fellow guildee.
