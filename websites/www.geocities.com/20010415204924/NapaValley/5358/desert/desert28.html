Coca Cola® Cake



1       Cup           Soften Butter
2       Cups          Sifted All Purpose Flour
1 3/4   Cups          Sugar
3       Tablespoons   Cocoa
1       Teaspoon      Baking Soda
1       Teaspoon      Vanilla Extract
2       Large         Eggs
1/2     Cup           Butter Milk
1       Cup           Coca Cola®
1 1/2   Cups          Miniature Marshmallows

        Frosting

1/2     Cup           Soften Butter
3       Tablespoons   Cocoa
1/2     Cup           Coca Cola®
4       Cups          Sifted Confectioner's Sugar
1       Cup           Coarsely Chopped Nuts (Optional)



This is one of the recipes that my stepdaughter had in her personal cookbook. Seems like everyone in the family has their own. Well, except for the boys, they rely on everyone else to cook for them.


Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, combined the Butter, Flour, Sugar, Cocoa, Baking Soda and mix. Add the Vanilla Extract and the Eggs to the bowl and again mix the dough. Next add the Butter Milk and Coca Cola® to the bowl and mix. Fold in the Marshmallows and mix thoroughly.

Pour the mixture into a greased 13"x9" baking dish and bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 40 to 45 minutes. Do not over bake this cake as it will become very dry.

Place all the ingredients for the frosting in a large mixing bowl and mix thoroughly. Pour the frosting over the cake while it is still warm.
