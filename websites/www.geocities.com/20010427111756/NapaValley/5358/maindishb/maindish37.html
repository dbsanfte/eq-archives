Tuna Triangles


1-12 oz   Can           Chicken of the Sea® Tuna, 
                        drained and flaked
1         Cup           Shredded Cheddar Cheese
2         Tablespoons   Ranch-Style Dressing
1-8 oz    Can           Crescent Roll Dough



I do love the Sunday newspaper and all the coupons, especially those that have recipes to go along with the coupons.


Directions:

In a mixing bowl, combined the Tuna, Cheddar Cheese, and Ranch-Style Dressing.

Separate the Crescent Rolls into four 'rectangles'. On an ungreased baking sheet, press each rectangle to about 4" x 8". Cut each rectangle in half, forming eight squares. Mound the tuna mixture on the diagonal half of each square, leaving about 1/2" border around the filling. Fold the dough squares in half to form triangles. Press the edges with a fork to seal, and cut a slit in the top of each one.

Bake in a pre-heated 375-degree oven for 14 to 16 minutes until the crescent rolls are golden brown.

Note: Up to 1/4 cup of chopped peppers, onions, celery or olives may also be incorporated in the Tuna mixture.
