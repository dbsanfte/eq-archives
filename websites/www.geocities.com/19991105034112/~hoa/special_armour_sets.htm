Special Armour Sets

Barbed armour is a line of magical armour, which can be found at Befallen. Barbed armour is stronger than chain mail and it also gives magical bonuses. For example barbed arms are AC7 and give the wearer a +2 bonus to his dexterity

Chittin shell armour comes in two flavours.
The chittin shell shield is useable by all shield wielders. It is armour class 15 and grants +10 vs. poison. It is non magical however (can't use it to bash magic beasts).
Only large races can wear the chittin shell tunic. It is armour class 17 and grants +10 vs. poison.

Blood stained armour is a form of magical chain mail found at Unrest. This is great armour for level 20-35 rangers, shamans and rogues.

Bone armour can be found on the ogre shamans in. This armour is can be worn by all classes and all races. Each piece has some sort of magical bonus. For example the turtle shell helmet gives +3 strength. Bone armour has the same armour class as chain mail and can be worn even by mage types. The weakness of this armour is that it is quite heavy

Damask armour is armour designed for pure spell casters. It is very light and features a better armour class than cloth. It can be found in several zones including Befallen and Najena

Green plate mail is extremely powerful high level plate mail armour. It is unfortunately only wearable by human sized characters. Several classes that cannot normally wear plate mail however including rogues, rangers, shamans and even monks can wear it. It also features magical bonuses. To give an example of what statistics to expect green plate mail gloves have an armour class of 14 and a weight of only 5. These gloves give a +7 bonus to strength and +5 to dexterity.

Lupine armour is special armour derived from lycanthropes. It comes in several versions. Some lupine armour can be directly found on lycanthropes such as the lupine armbands. Others must be gained by questing for Kart Ice bear. Lupine armour is excellent armour for all classes but especially for monks and pure casters. All races and all classes can wear it and it is extremely light. It is the same armour class rating as leather but its magical bonuses usually more than make up for that. Lupine armour all gives magical bonuses. For example the armbands grant +3 strength and +3 dexterity.

Shiny silk armour is great armour for mage types. Not only is this armour lightweight and stronger than leather but it also has magical properties. For example the gloves are +2 intelligence.
