Creole Noodles


3/4         Lb.          Fully Cooked Ham
                         in one piece
2           Medium       Celery Ribs
1           Small        Onion
12          Ounces       Wide Egg Noodles
1           Tablespoon   Vegetable Oil
2-14.5 oz   Cans         Stewed Tomatoes
1-1 oz      Package      Frozen Whole Kernel Corn
1           Tablespoon   Creole Seasoning
To Taste                 Salt


I am constantly getting 'spammed' via my hotmail address and particularly from xoom.com. I finally gave up and visited their site. They had a search engine for recipes which I used and located this recipe.



Directions:

Prepare the Noodles according to the package instructions and drain.

Cut the Cooked Ham into 1/2-inch chunks

Dice the Onion and the Celery and sauté them in the Vegetable Oil over medium high heat. When the onions are translucent, stir in the ham and continue cooking until the ham is light browned.

Add the Stewed Tomatoes, Frozen Corn and the Creole Seasoning and bring this mixture to a boil over high heat. Once the mixture starts to boil, reduce the heat to low, cover and simmer for 10 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

Place the cooked noodles in a large bowl and top them with the ham mixture. Toss before serving.
