Fancy Hamburgers


1        Lb.          Lean Ground Meat
2        Tablespoons  Vegetable Oil
1        Ounce        Butter
3/4      Teaspoon     Lemon Pepper
3/4      Teaspoon     Salt
1        Tablespoon   Heinz® 57 Steak Sauce
2        Ounces       Butter
1        Large        Onion, chopped
1-8 oz   Jar          Sliced Mushrooms
4        Medium       Tomatoes, quartered
1/2      Teaspoon     Salt
1/2      Teaspoon     Lemon Pepper
1/2      Teaspoon     Oregano
2        Cups         Shredded Cheddar Cheese



This is yet another recipe from my wife's 'Super Secret' recipe book. One of these days I'll find the whole book.


Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, combine the Ground Meat, 3/4 Teaspoon of Lemon Pepper, 3/4 Teaspoon of Salt and the Steak Sauce. Mix thoroughly with your hands and then form this mixture into 6 patties. Heat the Vegetable Oil and 1 ounce of Butter over medium heat and pan-fry the patties.

While the patties are frying, heat the 2 ounces of Butter in another pan and add the Onion, Mushrooms, Tomatoes, and 1/2 Teaspoon of Salt, Lemon Pepper and Oregano. Cook until the mixture is thoroughly heated, stirring frequently.

Remove the patties from the skillet and put them in a 13" x 9" casserole dish, cover them with the Onion/Mushrooms/Tomatoes mixture and then sprinkle the Shredded Cheddar Cheese over the top. Cook in a pre-heated 425-degree oven for 15 minutes.
