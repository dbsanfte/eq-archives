Greetings & Welcome, travelers of Norrath!!!

I am Dorfen Trueshine, humble leader of this truly great guild.  We welcome you to our home, and hope that you find all that you are looking for here. If you have any questions regarding our guild or any of the members or policies in particular, please do not hesitate to send me or any of the other officers in game a tell and we would be more than happy to give you all the information that you would like to know.  We are a very old guild that believes in giving back that which has been given to us.
Mother Tunare has given us all life, and blessed us with all that we have, as have all our other dieties.... and we, the Greater Druids, believe in giving back to the people what has been given to us.  Buffing those of the lower levels, and even those of higher levels, rezzing, and escorting people through difficult zones.
All things that we do to give back what we have been given, and we do it with an open heart and expect nothing from it.

To all those who wish to become a part of this family, we do have certain requirements that must be met.  All pledging members are required to be of at least the 14th level, to have grouped with another member of GD that will vouch for you, and to be willing to uphold the charter, regardless of the situation.  If you believe that you can do this then we welcome you to join us.

If you have any questions regarding becoming a member, then please send me a tell or any of the other officers a tell, and we will answer your questions as soon as we can.  May the All Mother bless us all and keep us safe.

Dorfen Trueshine
Humble leader of the Greater Druids
