Groups recommended:

Depending on strength and experience, I recommend 24 people, although less is possible. More makes it difficult since the number of key to access the different island is restrained.

Group 1:2 tanks, 1 monk, 1 shaman, 1 enchanter, 1 cleric
Group 2:2 tanks, 1 melee, 1 wizard, 1 enchanter, 1 cleric
Group 3:2 tanks, 1 melee/nuker, 1 shaman, 1 necromancer, 1 cleric
Group 4:2 tanks, 1 melee/nuker, 1 nuker, 1 necromancer, 1 cleric

Notes: necromancers can be substituted with any casters if you got 3 groups and less. They are useful for summon corpse if you need to move people from island to island, but else a magician, shaman, druid, wizard can do the job, but magicians and necromancers will prove the best since they'll provide you with extra tanking power.

Equipment needed:

Cloudy Stones of Veeshan: cost from 200-250pp. Can be bought in Erudin at the library from Sornita Eltern. You will need one for each port the wizards will do.
Coffins: cost around 180pp. You can buy them on first island in Plane of Sky, from Key Master. The ones he sells are the non stackable ones.
Reagents for spells: varies. The point here is that you won't be going back down until your raid is finished, unless you die. And since we all don't wanna die, then bring everything with you.

Recruiting:

Plan in advance. Schedule on your appropriate server calendar. Sky raids are usually spread on 3 days, or 2 good days. It's not a zone visited very often, since xp gain is very slim and loot can sometimes be meager, but there's nothing worse than showing up and seeing that the zone is cleared.

Prepare your groups in advance, and be sure to have back ups. You know that some people won't show up. And if you plan to clean the plane, be sure they are ready to stay for all days scheduled.

The best place to form up is Greater Faydark. Bind all people there. In case of a wipe out, it will be easier to reorganize if everyone is at the same spot.

WORDS OF CAUTION:

The Sky inhabitants have a peculiar notion of aggro. Sirran will assist mobs that are fought next to him (you don't want that, Sirran hits for 2000). Also, all the efreeti will attack you if you either duel close to them or unintentionally cast a non player friendly spell on someone (ie, bards AoE damage on group, etc...). Same is said for quest hall on island, do not duel and be careful of AoE on island one.

 This island will have different strategies depending on what is the goal. If you are there only for the monk epic, it is best to set the camp in the windmill as Gwan has a knockback proc and will throw people off the island. Simply hand the presence the scale from Eejag, tell Gwan you are ready and carefully walk him to the windmill. Neither the Blade Storms or Noble Dojorn will assist.

If you want to kill Noble Dojorn. There are 2 main strategy. The first one is to use pathing to your advantage. If you aggro the Blade Storms, Dojorn will assist, and the other way around too (no matter where you stand on island). If a monk feign death on the Blade Storms and Dojorn, there is a chance that they will path to island 3 (Gorgalosk) before eventually coming back to island 2 (takes about 5 min). It is thus possible to split the mobs. The second and simplest way is to have 2 warriors tank the Blade Storms and have the rest of the raid kill Dojorn. The Blade Storms are mainly speedbumps and annoyance in killing Dojorn.

The single teleport pad on the island will take you with key of the misplaced to Azarack Island. Respawn on the Presence is 2 hours, Dojorn is 7 days.

  Ekim Nottap

Split pattern is 1 Harvester becomes 2 Carriers and each Carrier becomes 1 Tamer. The best and easiest way to deal with this island is to stay on Gorgalosk island. Send one necro with harm shield or a cleric with DA to Pegasus island or a monk with good safe fall. It is possible to jump back down to island on a very precise spot. So the player aggros a pegasus/grifon, then jump down to Gorgalosk island. The initial pull is pretty easy. When it splits, have a high level chanter either Dictate or Rapture one of the Carriers. Kill the first split completely then deal with second split. Once the first split is killed, Ekim Nottap will spawn. You have 80 min to kill all spawns before Keeper of Souls spawn. This island will yield 28 keys. If the necro aggros more than one and jumps down with the mobs on Gorgalosk Island, the mobs will not path back to Pegasus Island.
