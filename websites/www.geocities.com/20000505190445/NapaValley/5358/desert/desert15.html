Cream Puff Dessert


1          Cup         Water
1/2        Cup         Butter or Margarine
1          Cup         All Purpose Flour
2          Medium      Eggs
3 - 8 oz   Packages    Vanilla Instant Pudding
3 1/2      Cups        Milk
1 - 8 oz   Package     Philadelphia® Cream Cheese
1 - 8 oz   Container   Cool Whip
1          Jar         Hot Fudge



Sunny-Mi strikes again with another wonderful recipe. Boy, if I ever publish this cookbook, I will owe a lot of people part of the Royalty Money. NOT!!!!!


Directions:

In a medium saucepan, bring the Water and the Butter to a boil. Once the butter is melted, add the flour and mix completely.

One at a time beat the eggs into this mixture, mixing thoroughly before the next egg is added. Spread this dough over a greased cookie sheet and bake in a pre-heated, 375-degree oven for 20 to 25 minutes.

Mix the Vanilla Instant Pudding, Milk and Philadelphia® Cream Cheese in a medium bowl. When the mixture is creamy, spread it evenly on the cooled crust. Spread the Cool Whip on top of the pudding mixture and refrigerate.

When ready to serve this dessert, cut it into 3" squares and then top it with the Hot Fudge.
