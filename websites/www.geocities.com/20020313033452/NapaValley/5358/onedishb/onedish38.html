Tuna Dinner for Four


1        Package   Skinners® Egg Noodles
1        Can       Campbell's® Cream of 
                   Mushroom Soup
1-7 oz   Can       Star Kist® Tuna, drained
2        Cups      Potato Chips, crumbled
To Taste           Salt
To Taste           Black Pepper


For some reason, I got on a 'tuna' kick while looking at Steve's Louisiana Cajun Cookbook. The Potato Chips in this one is the thing that really caught my eye.



Directions:

Cook the Egg Noodles according to the package instructions and drain.

Put the cooked noodles into a buttered casserole dish. Put the Cream of Mushroom Soup and the Tuna on top of them. Season with the Salt and Black Pepper and mix thoroughly.

Top this mixture with the crumbled Potato Chips and bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 30 minutes.
