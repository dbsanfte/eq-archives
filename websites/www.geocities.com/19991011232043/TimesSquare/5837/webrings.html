Pools of Darkness Walkthru

Upon entering the cave, you smell the musky odor of dragon. Peering foward into the gloom, you lift your torch hoping to light the way, but the darkness is impenetrable. Sword drawn you proceed into the cave. The only sounds are those of your feet, your breathing and the beat of your heart. You pause for a moment, lean up against the wall and wipe the sweat off your brow. It is damnably hot. After a moment, you continue your journey into the depths.

Suddenly, you sense a change in the air; a subtle change, detectable only by the most experienced DragonSlayers. Raising your guard you proceed. The cave makes a sweeping turn to the left, the ground rising at a steep grade. Using both hands you proceed up the rocky incline. You reach the top—aha! The treasure you seek!

But wait... that's strange. Where is the dragon? You look frantically upward; to the sides of the cave; to the dragon's horde. Panic grips you. Your heart leaps into your throat. Dragonfear washes over you-the beast is here! Some instinct tells your stunned brain to move your legs, but they do not move. You glance wildly around you, awaiting your doom, but it never comes. Rivulets of sweat drench your brow running into your eyes, stinging them.

A noise. Coming from the treasure horde. The mountain of gold is moving! And suddenly... dragon.
