Easy Crab Puffs



1        Jar                Old English Cheese Spread
1        Stick              Margarine
1        Can               Crabmeat
1/2      Teaspoon      Seasoning Salt
1 1/2   Tablespoon   Mayonnaise 
6 - 8    Each             English Muffines
Dash                       Garlic Powder



While surfin' the Internet on Thanksgiving Day, I came across this recipe and just had to copy it for my page. But this time I remembered to get the address of the Home Page where I found it.
….WWW.GEOCITIES.COM/NAPAVALLEY/2218….



Directions:

Mix together all of the above ingredients except the English Muffins. Chill overnight for flavors to blend. Spread cheese/crabmeat mixture over the English Muffins and broil until the cheese is melted.
