The Water Pet
    The Water Elemental is the composite of all elementals.  Their strength, hitpoints and agility rank in the top two of all elementals.  The fact that they are well balanced on stats and their water attack spell alone make them a great choice for most situations, but add to that their fast regeneration rate and you have one powerful lil ball of water.
Note: Level 50+ pets now have the Rogue ability to Backstab when positioned behind their target.
