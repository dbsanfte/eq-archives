Cheese Beer Bread



3 	Cups	Self Rising Flour
1/3	Cup	Sugar
1	Large	Egg, beaten
12	Ounces	Room Temperature Beer.
2 1/2	Cups	Shredded Cheddar Cheese



I hate to admit this, but I can NOT bake.! I saw this recipe on HGTV and fell in love with it. Even I can make this one.



Directions:

Mix the Self Rising Flour and Sugar thoroughly in a large mixing bowl. Add beaten egg to the bowl and stir. Add the Room Temperature Beer and stir (Note: a Non-alcoholic beer can be used with the same results). Stir in the Shredded Cheddar Cheese and mix thoroughly.

Pour the mixture into a dark, greased bread pan and bake at 350 degrees for approximately 1 hour until it is golden brown and sounds hollow when thumped.



A little note, 2/3 cup of dried herbs can be added to the mixture INSTEAD of the Shredded Cheddar Cheese.
