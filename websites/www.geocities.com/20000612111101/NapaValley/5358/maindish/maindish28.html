Sausage and Green Peppers


1           Lb.        Eckrich® Smoked Sausage
1           Teaspoon   Vegetable Oil
1           Medium     Yellow Onion cut 
                       into wedges
1           Medium     Garlic Clove, minced
1-14.5 oz   Can        Stewed Tomatoes
2           Medium     Green Peppers cut into thin strips


Going through the freezer, I found a package of Eckrich® Smoked Sausage. Now I am a lover of sausage, but I wanted a new recipe to cook. So, surfing the Internet I found this recipe.


Directions:

Using a fork, prick the sausage in several places. In a Dutch Oven, or large pot, simmer the sausage, uncovered, in water for 5 to 6 minutes. Drain the sausage and cut it into 2-inch pieces.

Using the same pan, heat the Vegetable Oil and cook the sausage for 5 minutes or until it is brown. Remove the sausage and add the Yellow Onion and Garlic to the pot and cook until the onion is tender. Drain and excess grease and return the sausage to the pot. Add the Stewed Tomatoes, cover and simmer for 30 minutes.

Add the Green Peppers to the pot and continue to simmer for an additional 30 minutes, uncovered, until most of the liquid has evaporated
