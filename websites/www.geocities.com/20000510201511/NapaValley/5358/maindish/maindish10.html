Smoked Kielbasa Wrapped in Bacon



2-1lb  Packages     Hilshire® Kielbasa
1      Jar          Vlasic® SauerKraut
1      Tablespoon   Brown Sugar
8      Slices       Hormel® Black Label Bacon



When possible, I make this for my supervisor on "Bosses Day", along with the Twice Baked Potatoe's *See Side Dishes* and a Tossed Salad.


Directions:

MicroWave the Hormel® Black Label Bacon stripes for approximately 2 minutes, just enough to start the cooking process, but yet keeping them soft enough to wrap around the Hilshire® Kielbasa.

Cut each ring of the Hilshires® Kielbasa in half. Cut a "pocket" into each piece of the kielbasa *Making sure not to cut all the way through*.

Drain the Vlasic® SauerKraut and and mix in the Brown Sugar. Stuff the pockets of the kielbasa with the sauerkraut. Wrap two stipes of the partial cooked Hormel® Black Label Bacon around the stuffed kielbasa and secure them with a toothpick.

Cook the kielbasa in a smoker for approximately 3 hours and don't......I repeat.....DON'T lift the lide.
