Come One! Come All!
To
The Valentine's Day Massacre

to be held in The Realm on
Saturday, February 14, 1998
at 9:00 PM EST
at Tourney Grove

sponsored by the
Muse & Merriment Guild

General Information:

  1. There will be only one fight at a time.
  2. In order to participate, each player will give 600 gp to
    our "Fight Director".
  3. The "Fight Director" will assign the order in which each
    pair will fight.
  4. Each fight will be limited to 10 minutes each.
  5. A Fight can be to the "death" or until one participant
    says "I yield!"


Prizes:

  • The prize money for each fight will be 1,000 gp to the
    winner of that fight.
  • There will also be drawing(s) for some special prize(s).


How This Will Work:

  1. Each pair of fighters will "group" with a "PVP-on" Umpire.
  2. Umpires do not fight nor will it be permissible to inflict any harm to our Umpires.
  3. One Umpire will provide a blow by blow description for the designated gossip channel and another Umpire will do the same for those in the screen crowd.
  4. If neither fighter has died or yielded when the bell rings,
    the fight is a draw and each of those fighters will get 500 gp refunded.
  5. Any level or character type can fight, but we would suggest that the most interesting fights will be between pairs closer in level.
  6. Players will select their own opponent! Please be paired up at the time you pay your entrance fee.
  7. You can only fight one time until all the pairs are done fighting. Then, if time permits, there can be a rematch or even a new match.

More details will be provided at the time of the tournament.

Check back to this page often before the Fights for any additional information or procedural changes.
