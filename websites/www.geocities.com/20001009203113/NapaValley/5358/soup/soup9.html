Super Seafood Salad


1     Package       Louis Kemp® Crab 
                    Delights® Flakes
1/2   Cup           Miracle Whip® Salad Dressing
2     Medium        Chopped Celery Stalks
3     Tablespoons   Finely Chopped Yellow Onion



This recipe came from the label on a Louis Kemp® Crab Delights® Flakes package. There were two other recipes on the label as well.



Directions:

Cut the Crab Flakes into small bite size pieces and combined them with the remaining ingredients. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
