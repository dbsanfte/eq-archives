Baked Beans Casserole


4	   Slices   Thick Bacon
1	   LB	    Lean Ground Meat
2-15.5oz   Cans	    Pork and Beans
1	   Cup	    Ketchup
1/2	   Cup	    Brown Sugar
1/2	   Cup	    Barbecue Sauce
1	   Medium   Onion, chopped
1	   Medium   Green Pepper, chopped
To Taste	    Garlic Powder
To Taste	    Salt
To Taste	    Black Pepper



I found this recipe on the Internet and it listed an ingredient that wasn't precise as to what it exact called for. So I left it out and I also added the Bacon. Once again the Ole Wizard has worked his magic and come up with a "keeper".


Directions:


Cook the Bacon until it is crisp. Then drain it on a paper towel. Once it is thoroughly drained, crumble it.

Brown the Ground Meat in a skillet, draining off the excess grease. Add all the other ingredients, including the crumbled bacon and mix well. Pout the mixture into a baking dish and cover it with aluminum foil. Cook in a 350-degree oven for 20 minutes. Remove the aluminum foil and continue to cook for an additional 20 minutes or until it is bubbly and lightly brown on top.
