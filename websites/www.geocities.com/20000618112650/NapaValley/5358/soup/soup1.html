French Onion Soup



2     Large     Yellow Onions
1/4   Cup       Butter
1/2   Teaspoon  Sugar
3     Cans      Condensed Beef Broth
3     Cans      Hot Water
1     Teaspoon  Worcestershire Sauce
1/2   Teaspoon  Paprika
To    Taste     Salt
To    Taste     Black Pepper

4     Ounces    Shredded Swiss Cheese



This is, beyond-a-shadow-of-a-doubt, one of my all-time favorites. The recipe came from a series of cookbooks that were purchased for me for Christmas several years ago.



Directions:

Peel, quarter and thinly slice the Yellow Onions.

In a LARGE soup pot, melt the Butter over medium heat. Add the Yellow Onions and the Sugar and sauté the onions until they are Golden Brown. Add the Worcestershire Sauce, Paprika, Condensed Beef Broth and the Water to the pot and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes uncovered. Salt and Pepper the soup to taste.

Ladle the soup into a bowl and sprinkle the top with the Shredded Swiss Cheese. (Croutons also go well with this soup).
