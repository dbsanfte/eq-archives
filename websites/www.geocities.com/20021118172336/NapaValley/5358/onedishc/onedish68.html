Kristn's Cheesy Salsa Chicken & Rice


1 3/4     Lbs.      Chicken Tenders
1-16 oz   Jar       Medium Thick 'n Chunky Salsa
1         Can       Chicken Broth
1-8oz     Package   Velveeta® Prepared Cheese,
                    cut into pieces
2 1/2     Cups      Uncooked Minute® Rice


Kristin found the original recipe on the Internet and emailed it to me. I don't normally modify a recipe until I prepare it at least once, but this one is the exception to the rule.



Directions:

Remove the tendons from the Tenders and then cut them into bite size pieces. Spray a large skillet with no stick cook spray, add the chicken and cook for 4 to 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through. Stir in the Salsa, Chicken Broth and Velveeta® and bring to a boil.

Stir in the Uncooked Rice, remove from the heat and let stand for 7 minutes or until all the liquid is absorbed and the rice is tender. Stir before serving.
