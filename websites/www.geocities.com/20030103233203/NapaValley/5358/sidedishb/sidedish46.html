Veg-All® Vegetable Casserole


1           Medium   Onion, chopped
2-14.5 oz   Cans     Veg-All® Original Vegetables,
                     drained
2/3         Cup      Hellman's Mayonnaise
3/4         Cup      Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1           Stick    Margarine or Butter, divided
1/4         Lb.      Saltine Crackers, crushed
To Taste             Salt
To Taste             Black Pepper



I allowed a coworker to look through my cookbook and make copies of any and all the recipes that she wanted. In return, she brought to work her favorite cookbook and allowed me the same privilege. This is one of the recipes from that cookbook.


Directions:


Melt 1/4 of the stick of Margarine/Butter in a large saucepan and sauté the Onions until they are translucent. Add the Veg-All®, Mayonnaise and Cheddar Cheese to the pan, stirring until the ingredients are well incorporated. Pour this mixture into a casserole dish.

Melt the remaining Margarine/Butter in a saucepan and then mix in the crushed Saltine Crackers. Spread this mixture over the top of the vegetable mixture.

Loosely cover with aluminum foil and bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 30 minutes.
