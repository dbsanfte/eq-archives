Sausage Balls



1  	Lb.    	Sausage (hot or mild)
1/2  	Lb.     Sharp Cheddar Cheese, Grated
3      	Cups  	Bisquick
1       Cup     Milk



Just think, until I go on the internet, I thought the only place to find recipes was on the back of soup cans in the grocery store. The Internet has allowed me to shop for recipes without every leaving my favorite chair.


Directions:

Cook sausage in a skillet until it loses its pink color, stir to break up sausage and drain off fat. Mix Bisquick, Milk, Sausage and Cheese. Form into 1 inch balls and bake on cookie sheet in 350 degree oven until brown. If you like, you can use 1 lb. of cheese
