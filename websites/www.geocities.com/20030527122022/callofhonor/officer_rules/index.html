The Guild Leader reserves the right to Guild Remove should it become necessary.
The Guild Leader reserves the right to make final decisions.
You as officers are Sirra's right hand, you have the right to question her decisions, however, DO NOT question her in front of non-officers.  Please reserve all questions and disputes for direct tells or email.  Show the Guild Leader the respect due her position.
It is your responsibility to make sure all the Guild Rules are being followed.  If you see a rule being broken, you are to act upon it with efficiency but also courtesy.  Do not eprimand over Guild Speach unless absolutely necessary.  Officers are to direct all new members to the web page rules if a rule is broken for the first time on your watch.  All officers are still required to follow the same rules as regular members.
All new members must show a strong sense of honor, loyalty, and the ability to listen and follow instructions.  They should be looking for a family guild not a strict raiding guild.
All officers are to make sure the new members they invite understand and obey the Guild Rules.  If a member is being reprimanded  you are to post on the Forum Boards and let us know what happened so that we can keep track. 
1 email address is required for each person invited.  If it is an alt, there is no need for another email.
All officers are to notify the guild leader each time they guild a new member or invite an alt so that the web page and records may be updated.  The Leader is to receive this notice within 5 days of the invitation by email along with the new member's email address.  Any unknown characters after a 5 day period will be removed.
Any officer at any time, but during scheduled or emergency meetings, may call a spontaneous Guild Raid or Event.
Officers are to handle any situations or problems first.  If the officer is unable to make a comfortable decision, or needs help, they are to contact Hanamil.  If Hanamil is unavailable they may go directly to the Leader.  If neither Hanamil or Sirra is available, either wait till one of us is online or, if in an emergency, consult with another officer in the guild and then together make the decision.  You were made officers for a reason, it is obvious that Sirra trusts you to make smart and fair choices or she would not have requested  you to take the position you now hold.
