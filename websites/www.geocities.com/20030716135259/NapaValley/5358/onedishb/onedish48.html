Chicken Casserole II


2-3       Lbs.        Boneless/Skinless Chicken 
                      Breasts,cooked and cubed
8         Ounces      Seasoned Stuffing Cubes
1         Cup         Chicken Broth
1         Can         Campbell's® Cream of Mushroom Soup
1-4 oz    Jar         Sliced Mushrooms (optional)
1-16 oz   Container   Sour Cream
1/2       Stick       Butter


If you guessed that this is another recipe that I obtained off of the Internet, jump up and give yourself a 'high five' because you are 100% correct



Directions:

Mix the cubed Chicken Breasts, Mushrooms, Cream of Mushroom Soup and the Chicken Broth together in a large baking dish. Place the Stuffing cubes over the chicken mixture. Melt the butter and then pour it over the stuffing

Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 35 to 40 minutes.
