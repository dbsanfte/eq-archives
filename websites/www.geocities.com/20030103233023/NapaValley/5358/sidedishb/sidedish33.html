French Fried Eggplant


1   Large,Long   Eggplant
2   Large        Egg, slightly beaten
2   Cups         Italian Bread Crumbs
2   Cups         Vegetable Oil



I use this basic principal for several different recipes. It's best to use an electric skillet when frying the eggplant as it much easier to control the temperature of the oil, 350-degrees is the best.


Directions:


Peel the Eggplant and cut into 1/4" round slices. Soak in salted water for 30 minutes, then rinse and pat dry.

Heat the Vegetable Oil in an electric skillet to 350-degrees.

One at a time put the eggplant into the egg, coating it completely and then into the breadcrumbs, turning so that both sides are coated. Put the breaded eggplant into the hot oil, turning once, until they are golden brown. Keep warm until you are ready to serve.
