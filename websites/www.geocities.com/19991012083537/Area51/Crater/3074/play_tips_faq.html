FAQ's:

Q:

I am a Gnome Necromancer and I was wondering where I could get a toolbox?

Question asked by Windsor.

A:

Unless this was changed since beta, you can buy these from a mechanic in town (one is behind the library another is in the first room you hit when entering Steamfront Mountains.)

Q:

Can Damage over time spells be stacked? Will three casts of the same spell do triple damage over time? Does poison bolt effect Dex. and Agil?

Questions asked by Temujin.

A:

Some spells can be stacked such as poison and clinging darkness. Only one spell of the same kind can be used for damage over time. (IE. Two poison bolts will only do the damage over time of one.) At this time Dex and Agil, are not effected by poison.
