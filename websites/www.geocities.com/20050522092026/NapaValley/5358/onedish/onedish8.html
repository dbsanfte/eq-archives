Kielbasa & Sauerkraut


1     Package         Kielbasa
1     Quart           Sauerkraut
3     Tablespoons     Brown Sugar



Over the years, I have purchased many kitchen appliances for my wife. Some of which she was very happy to receive and others, well, they are hidden in a cupboard somewhere. The Crock Pot, and the recipe book that accompanied it were a welcome addition to the kitchen. This recipe came from the cook book that was enclosed with the Crock Pot



Directions:

Cut the Kielbasa into "bun length pieces". In a bowl, mix the Sauerkraut and the Brown Sugar. Put half of this mixture on the bottom of the Crock Pot. Place the Kielbasa on top of it and then cover the Kielbasa with the remainder of the Sauerkraut/Brown Sugar mixture.

Cook the Kielbasa & Sauerkraut on High 3 to 4 hours. (Note: If you have someone who does not like Kielbasa, such as my son, put a couple of Hot Dogs in the Crock Pot one half hour prior to serving the meal.)

Serve the Kielbasa on Hot Dog Buns with a little Dijon Mustard and covered with sauerkraut.
