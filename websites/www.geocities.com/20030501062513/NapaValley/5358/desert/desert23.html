Summer Fruit Dessert


2       Pints      Strawberries, cleaned
1       Package    Vanilla Instant Pudding 
                   Mix (3 1/2 oz.)
1       Cup        Half-&-Half
1       Cup        Sour Cream
1       Cup        Plain Yogurt
1/2     Teaspoon   Vanilla Extract



I am really not sure where this recipe came from. It just showed up in my stack of recipes to try and as everyone knows, I don't post a recipe to my home page or include it in my cookbook until it has been prepared.


Directions:

Thorough blend the Vanilla Instant Pudding, Half-&-Half, Sour Cream, Plain Yogurt and Vanilla Extract in a large mixing bowl. Chill for at least 2 hours prior to serving. Spoon the mixture over the Fresh Strawberries, or other fresh summer fruit.
