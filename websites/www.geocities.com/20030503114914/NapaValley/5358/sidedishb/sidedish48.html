Cheddar Potato Strips


4     Large        Potatoes, cut into
                   1/2-inch strips
1/2   Cup          Milk
1     Tablespoon   Butter or Margarine
1     Cup          Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1     Tablespoon   Minced Fresh Parsley
To Taste           Salt
To Taste           Black Pepper



The new cooking magazines are starting to arrive and the recipes are wonderful. This one is from the magazine "Taste of Home".


Directions:


In a greased 13" x 9" baking dish, arrange the Potato Strips in a single layer. Pour the Milk over the potatoes and the dot them with the Butter. Salt and Pepper them to taste.

Cover with aluminum foil and bake in a pre-heated 425-degree oven for 30 minutes or until the potatoes are tender. Sprinkle the Cheddar Cheese and Parsley over the potatoes and bake, uncovered, for an additional 5 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
