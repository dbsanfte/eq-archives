Faustian - Necromancer
Outcast. Unloved. Unwanted. The whole world scowling at him ready to attack, Faustian has paid a cruel price for his study of necromancy.

His birthplace of Paineel was not a bad place to start. But it was a small, parochial backwater with exceedingly limited facilities. By his 12th year, Faustian had outgrown his roots and set off to see the big wide world with a spring in his step and a song in his heart - and not the slightest clue of what awaited him.

To escape the poor, remote continent of his birth he had to set sail from the docks of Erudin. Sadly, many of the residents of that unpleasant city had other ideas, attacking him at every opportunity. Thanks to his ability to gather shadows Faustian eventually did sneak aboard a boat to Qeynos - and *another* populace out for his blood. Luckily he found refuge in the sewers below that city.

Slowly and painfully Faustian grew in power, first at the expense of the gnolls of Blackburrow. One fortunate side effect was that he gained enough favor in the eyes of the Qeynos guards that he could openly walk the streets. What he did not realize was that the favor with the guards was counterbalanced by disfavor with the necromancers guild. Soon a visit to the guild was met by swift death at the hands of those he had thought were his friends. That was a bleak and bitter day.

In time Faustian outgrew Blackburrow and went on the South Karana with its aviaks and later the bard. Here he met Derok, a fellow necromancer and a true friend. Derok recruited Faust into Defenders of Life. In time it became clear that guild was more about feeding the ego of its leader than the welfare of its members, so they both soon quit.

From South Karana he moved to Rathe Mountains and eventually to Everfrost and the Ice Giants who so kindly contributed to his bank balance. During this period he met Aanandar, another true friend who brought Faustian into Blood Inferno, for which he will be forever grateful.

These days he spends most of his time in Kunark, usually in Burning Wood or Skyfire. Faustian is always eager to team with guild mates and hopes to see you soon.
