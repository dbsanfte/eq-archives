Cucumber & Green Onion Salad


3     Large        Cucumbers *approximately 
                   6 inches in length*
1     Bunch        Green Onions
1     Head         Lettuce
1     Large        Tomato

      Marinade

1/2   Cup          White Wine Vinegar
1/2   Cup          Sugar
1     Tablespoon   Dill Weed
1     Tablespoon   Salt
1/2   Teaspoon     White Pepper



Yet another recipe that I found on the internet.



Directions:

Slice the Cucumbers thin and place them in a mixing bowl. Slice the Green Onions on a diagonal as thinly as possible and add them to the mixing bowl.

In another mixing bowl, combine the White Wine Vinegar, Sugar, Dill Weed, Salt and White Pepper. Whisk with a fork until thoroughly blended. Pour this mixture over the cucumber/green onions and turn with a spatula until all the pieces are well coated. Marinate for 1 hour or more in the refrigerator.

Place a leaf of lettuce on a serving plate and spoon the cucumber/green onion mixture on top of it. Garnish with Tomato wedges.
