What Is The M&M Guild?

    Philosophy:

    We created a guild that was more like a club (a group of people organized for a common purpose) or a fellowship (the companionship of individuals in a congenial atmosphere and on equal terms) rather than a hierarchy. You could say we were more like a family only led by a team rather than by a leader.

    Purpose:

    We wanted this guild to be based on FUN -- not on service or accomplishments. We wanted creative people who could put their heads together and come up with inventive ways to have fun in the Realm (and other games) besides combat, leveling, bartering, and such which at times can just become stale! Perhaps story telling, comedy, games of some sort that can be hosted by our guild for all in the Realm to enjoy. And for five good years we did just that!

    History:

    The Muse & Merriment Guild officially began on March 26, 1996 through the on-line game called "The Realm". However, a number of situations arose and it is my sad duty to report that due to irreconcilable differences, the Muse & Merriment Guild officially broke up effective July 5, 2001.

    Disclaimer:

    This page remains in place to be certain that any and all visitors are aware that the one and only Muse & Merriment Guild no longer exists. Let it be known that any organization using this name is not now nor was it ever related to the original Muse & Merriment Guild. All prior M&Ms in good standing have joined other guilds through the game EverQuest or are presently guild-less.
