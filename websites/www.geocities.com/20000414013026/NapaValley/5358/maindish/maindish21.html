Baked Prawns with Garlic and Herbs



1/2	Cup         Virgin Olive Oil
1/2	Cup         Dry Vermouth
1/4	Cup         Fresh Lemon Juice
1/4	Cup         Minced Fresh Parsley
4	Cloves      Garlic, finely minced
1 1/2   Teaspoons   Chopped Chives
2	Each        Green Onions, Diced
24	Each        Uncooked, Extra Large Prawns,
	            Shelled and deveined
To Taste            Salt
To Taste	    Black Pepper



Before anyone asks, yes I have many, many cookbooks. I try a lot of recipes and this is one of them, but I changed it to my tastes. The Chives and Green Onions are not part of the original recipe, those are my additions. BTW, and that's internet talk for "By The Way", I use either 21-25 or 26-30 count shrimp, and not the prawns.


Directions:

Combine the first 7 ingredients in a medium saucepan and bring to boil over medium-high heat, stirring frequently. Remove from the heat. Taste and season with the salt and pepper, according to your tastes. Let this mixture cool.

Arrange the prawns/shrimp in a single layer in a baking dish. Pour the mixture over the prawns. Let the prawns marinate at room temperature for 1 hour.

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Bake the prawns/shrimp, basting once or twice, until pink and translucent, about 15 to 20 minutes.
