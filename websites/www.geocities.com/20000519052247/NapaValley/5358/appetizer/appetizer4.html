British Brunch Fondue



1/4     Cup        Butter or Margarine
1/4     Cup        Gold Medal Flour
1/2     Teaspoon   Salt
1/4     Teaspoon   Pepper
1/4     Teaspoon   Dry Mustard
1/4     Teaspoon   Worcestershire Sauce
1 1/2   Cups       Milk
2       Cups       Shredded Cheddar Cheese


Dippers:


Hot brown and serve sausages, cut into thirds, hot canned tiny whole potatoes, cherry tomatoes, toasted English Muffins, cut into pieces, or whatever party snacks you enjoy.


FOOTBALL...FOOTBALL...FOOTBALL!!!

Kathie's grandmother, Dana, gave us a Betty Crocker Recipe Card Library and this is one of our favorites from that library. And just in case you haven't already guessed, this fondue and a can of "soda-pop-with-the-foam-on-top" is just fantastic on a Sunday afternoon while watching your favorite Pro-Football Team.


Directions:

In a medium sauce pan melt the butter over low heat. Blend in the flour, Salt, Black Pepper, Dry Mustard and the Worcestershire Sauce and continue to cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture is smooth and bubbly.

Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the milk. Return the pan to the stove and bring to a boil stirring constantly. Boil and stir for 1 minute. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the cheddar cheese. Stir the mixture constantly until the cheddar cheese melts.

Pour the mixture into a fondue pot and transfer the pot to a source of heat at the table. Adjust the heat when necessary to keep the fondue warm.

Spear the Dippers and swirl in the fondue with a figure-eight motion.
