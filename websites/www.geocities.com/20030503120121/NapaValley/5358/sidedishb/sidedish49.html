Dave's Macaroni and Cheese


1        Cup         Uncooked Macaroni
16       Ounces      Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
1/2      Cup         Chopped Pepperoni Slices
To Taste             Salt

         White Sauce
3        Teaspoons   Butter
2 to 3   Teaspoons   All Purpose Flour
2        Cups        Milk



My In-Laws visited us for a long weekend and they finally got a look at my cookbook. They were excited about it and my Mother-In-Law gave me her recipe for Macaroni and Cheese. Using her recipe and my Mothers recipe and just a little imagination on my part, this recipe was developed. Be careful on the amount of salt that you add, as the Pepperoni is very salty. *Note: you will probably not use all the white sauce.*


Directions:


Cook the Macaroni according to the package instructions and then drain.

To prepare the White Sauce, melt the Butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Once it has melted, add the Flour and stir until it is smooth. Slowly add the Milk, stirring constantly until it is well incorporated. Allow this mixture to come to a boil, stirring occasionally, and then remove it from the heat and allow it to cool.

Put half of the cooked macaroni into a greased 2-quart baking dish. Spread half of the cheese over the macaroni. Spread all the chopped Pepperoni over the cheese and then layer the remaining macaroni and cheese on top of the first layer. Pour enough of the White Sauce over the macaroni/cheese mixture until the sauce just starts to cover the layers. Bake in a pre-heated 425-degree oven for 30 minutes.
