In-Game Message Boards


Funny, the things you miss if all you ever do is hunt. Around Norrath, there are several message boards (server-specific) within the game where players can post and view messages left by other players. What a neat idea! Below is a screenshot of what they look like. To use them, left-click on them and they will load onto your screen.

Known locations of various boards:
- Highkeep Hold (2 on the second floor of the castle, dealer's room)
- Qeynos (1 in South Qeynos in the Pub/Inn)
- Freeport
