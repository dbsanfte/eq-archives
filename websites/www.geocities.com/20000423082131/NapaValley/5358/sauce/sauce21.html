Tyler's Spicy Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce



1/3   Cup           Grey Poupon® Country 
                    Dijon Mustard
3     Tablespoons   Honey
2     Tablespoons   Heinz® Ketchup
1     Clove         Garlic, minced
1/2   Teaspoon      Louisiana® Hot Sauce



Yep, ya'll caught me surf'in the Internet again. I am telling you, if you don't use the Internet, and not just for recipes, you are missing out on one of the greatest things to come out of the 20th century.



Directions:

In a medium bowl, combined all the ingredients and mix thoroughly. Refrigerate until you are ready to serve with Chicken Fingers or what ever type of meat that you enjoy honey mustard with. But be forewarned this does have a spicy 'kick' to it.
