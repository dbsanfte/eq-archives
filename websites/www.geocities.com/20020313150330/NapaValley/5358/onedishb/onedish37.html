Steve's Shrimp Etouffee


1 1/2   Lbs.	Medium Shrimp, peeled and deviend
		(26 - 30 count)
1	Large   Yellow Onion, chopped
1	Stick	Butter
1/2	Cup	Wine (red or white according to your
		preferences)
1	Can	Golden® Cream of Mushroom Soup
1	Can	Water
To Taste	Cayenne Pepper
To Taste	Louisiana Hot Sauce
To Taste	Salt
To Taste	Black Pepper


A co-worker, who is in his own right a gourmet cook, told me of this recipe and how it had won many, many Etouffee Cooking Contests. The secret is, well, I promised never to reveal it, but you are reading the recipe just as it was given to me. This will feed 4 'normal' people, or ONE CAJUN.!!!!



Directions:

In a large pot, sauté the Onions in the Butter and Wine until they are translucent. Add the Cream of Mushroom Soup and Water and mix thoroughly. Add the shrimp, cover and cook for 15 to 20 minutes. Serve over cooked rice.
