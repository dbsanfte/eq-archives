The Newbie Monk Guide Starting from Qeynos
by Jeremy Moyer

Levels 1-4= Find your guild house, Hail everyone there and repeat in question form the [bracketed words] that they say. You will learn that you need 4 gnoll pup skins or scalps to get your first headband, the White one, which gives you +1cha +1sta. Put 2 Practice Points in Kick and the rest in Mend, all the other skills will level by themselves with no problem. Put Kick in your hotkeys area and then head for North Qeynos. You are now ready to fight the beasties in Qeynos.

Avoid gnoll pups, green snakes, bats, and decaying skeletons until level 2. Go after black red snakes and large rats. Collect snake fangs and rat skins and ears; they are the best way to earn money at the lower levels. Only sell your stuff to the vendor inside to Silent Fist Clan house, he gives the best prices in Qeynos. At level 2-3 start attacking most enemies, except for gnolls with the last name of Darkpaw (Wait until level 5 to go after these guys). At level 4 you are awarded with your first special attack, Round Kick! Same delay as Kick but hits much harder, does about 10dmg (14dmg on a good day). You are now ready to head out into the Qeynos Hills.

Level 5-8= If you follow the road you will find a crossroad with a large boulder that is commonly referred to as the "marker", if you continue to head towards Surefall Glade you will come across a cabin that has two merchants who will sell you tailoring supplies. Farther on is just road and forest until you get to the entrance to Surefall Glade. It may look intimidating and it is, inside are bears and poachers, that's it. If you take the right at the marker you will head for Karana, (important note: Do not go in Karana until level 12 or unless you have a group, you will not survive.) along the road is a Guard House with a couple of Guards.

BlackBurrow, or BB, is the home of the gnolls in the Qeynos Hills, most of the time you can expect to see 30-40 people in this one zone, makes it very hard to hunt there. Start with scrawny gnolls and the occasional gnoll until level 7; this is when you can start going after gnolls and patrolling gnolls. BB is notorious for the monster, as in huge, trains that come up from the basement of BB, so if some one shouts "TRAIN!!" get out of BB fast! To the North of BB are the Ruins, where one can see (and fight) all manner of skeletons: Decaying, plain, Dread, Putrid, and Varsoon. You see Varsoon, run for Surefall, further northwest, and don't stop until you are safe with the Guards. Varsoon is a high level NPC that will tear a newbie apart.

Level 9-10= Group with other people your level and go into to lower levels of BB, you'll want to group with a warrior or someone with Taunt because as a Monk you hit harder, faster, and more times then most classes. Gnoll Guardsmen, elite guardsmen, gnoll brewers, and the elusive gnoll shaman all wait for you in the lower floors of BlackBurrow.
