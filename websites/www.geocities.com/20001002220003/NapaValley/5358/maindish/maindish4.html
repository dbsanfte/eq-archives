Lemon Broccoli Chicken



4    Each        Boneless, Skinless Chicken 
                 Breasts
1    Can         Campbells® Cream of Broccoli 
                 Soup
1    Head        Broccoli
1/4  Cup         Milk
2    Teaspoons   Lemon Juice
1/8  Teaspoon    Black Pepper
4    Slices      Lemon
1    Tablespoon  Vegetable Oil



When ever I am in the mood to cook something new and different, and just can't find anything that looks or sounds good in one of my many cook books, I go to the grocery store and head straight to the soup section. The Campbell's® Soup Company almost always has a recipe on the back of their soup cans. This recipe came from a can of Cream of Broccoli Soup. The original recipe does not call for the broccoli flowerettes, that part is my inovation.


Directions:

Cut the flowerettes off the Head of Broccoli and steam them until they are tender.

Put the Vegetable Oil in a skillet and brown the Chicken Breasts on both sides.

In a mixing bowl, combined the Cream of Broccoli soup and the Milk. Stir in the Lemon Juice, the Black Pepper and the steamed Broccoli. Pour this mixture over the browned chicken breasts. Place a Lemon Slice on each breast, cover, reduce the heat and simmer for approximately 10 minutes, or until the chicken breasts fork-tender, stirring occasionally.
