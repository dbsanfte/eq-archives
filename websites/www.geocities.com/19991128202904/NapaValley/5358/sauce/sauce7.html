Cream Cheese Cake Frosting



1	Cup          Unsalted Butter at 
                     Room Temperature
2-8 oz.	Packages     Philadelphia® Cream Cheese
                     at Room Temperature
1	Tablespoon   Vanilla Extract
1 1/2	Cups	     Powdered Sugar
1	Pinch	     Salt



A co-worker used this frosting on a cake that he brought to work. I finally persuaded him to give me the recipe.



Directions:

Beat the Philadelphia Cream Cheese and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in the Vanilla Extract and a pinch of salt. Gradually beat in the powdered sugar. Cover and refrigerate the frosting until firm enough to spread, approximately 15 minutes.
