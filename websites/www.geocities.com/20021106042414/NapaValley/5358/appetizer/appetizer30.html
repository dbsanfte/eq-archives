Deviled Eggs Mexicana




12   Large              Eggs, hard boiled
3     Each               Mezetta® tamed Jalapeno Peppers, diced
2     Tablespoons   Liquid from the Jalapeno Peppers
1     Teaspoon	     Green Jalapeno Tabasco® Sauce
4     Tablespoons   Hellmann's® Mayonnaise
1     Teaspoon       Dried Dill
1/2  Teaspoon       Dry Mustard
1     Teaspoon       Salt
2     Teaspoon       Black Pepper
                             Paprika


Yep, got too much time on my hands and once again I found this recipe while surfing the Internet.



Directions:

Hard-boil the Eggs, let them cool and then peel them. Once they are peeled, cut them in half, lengthwise and put the 'yolks' in a large mixing bowl. Mash the yolks, with a fork, until they are very fine. Add the remaining ingredients, EXCEPT for the Paprika, and mix thoroughly. Spoon the mixture back into the whites of the egg, heaping it slightly. Arrange the eggs on a large serving platter and sprinkle with the Paprika. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve, no longer then 24 hours.
