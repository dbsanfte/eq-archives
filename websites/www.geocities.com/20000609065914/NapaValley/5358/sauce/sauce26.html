Gloria's Pico De Gallo



3        Medium   Yellow Onions, chopped
5 - 10   Small    Jalapeno Peppers, sliced
4        Large    Tomato's, seeded and chopped
1/3      Cup      White Vinegar
To Taste          Salt
To Taste          Garlic



A very good friend brought this to work for one of our get-togethers. She was kind enough to give me the recipe. Thanks Gloria.!



Directions:

Combine the Yellow Onions, Jalapeno Peppers and Tomatoes in a large mixing bowl and mix thoroughly. Add the salt and Garlic to the mixture and combine thoroughly again. Add the vinegar and once again toss the mixture so that all the vegetables are coated with the vinegar.

Refrigerate for several hours before serving, stirring occasionally. (Note: For the best results, prepare this one-day in advance.)
