Salsa-Connie



1                Lb             Bacon, cut into small pieces
1/2             Cup           Chopped Onions
1/2             Cup           Chopped Green Peppers
2 - 10oz     Cans          Ro-Tel Diced Tomatoes and Green Chiles
1                Teaspoon   Salt
2 or 3         Each          Chopped Jalapeno Peppers (optional



Connie emailed this recipe to me. She informed me that her husband Jerry invented this appetizer and named it after her because she enjoys it so much.



Directions:

Fry the Bacon until it is crisp. Pour off all the drippings, except for about 2 tablespoons. Then sauté the chopped onions and green peppers in the drippings add the tomatoes, salt, and Jalapeno peppers. Simmer together on low heat for approximately 20 minutes. Serves six.
