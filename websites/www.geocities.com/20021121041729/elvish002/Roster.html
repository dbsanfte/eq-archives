A Note on the Ranking System -

Due to the difficulty of keeping up with everyone's exact level, we have created a system of ranks, whereby one rank covers a range of ten levels as follows:

Level 01 - 10  Initiate
Level 11 - 20  Hunter
Level 21 - 30  Explorer
Level 31 - 40  Champion
Level 41 - 50  Master
Level 51 - 60  Grand Master
