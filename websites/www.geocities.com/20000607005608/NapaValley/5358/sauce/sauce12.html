Beer Batter



1     Cup   All Purpose Flour
1     Cup   Beer
1/2   Cup   Vegetable Oil
	    Sliced Zucchini, or Green Tomatoes, or
	    Egg Plant or Squash.



This is an excellent, simple and easy batter to make and it tastes great. Not to worry, the alcohol will evaporate once you deep fry the vegetables. And a little hint of an upcoming recipe, 'Chicken Strips'.!!!



Directions:

In a large bowl, combine the Flour and Beer. Mix thoroughly until there are no lumps. Put the batter in the refrigerator for approximately 20 minutes.

Put the Vegetable Oil into an electric skillet and heat to 350 degrees.

Dip the sliced vegetables, one at a time, in the batter mix, coating completely and then 'place' them in the hot oil. Allow them to cook until they are golden brown on one side, then turn them over. Once they are completely golden brown, remove them from the oil and drain on a paper towel.

Place cooked vegetables in a warm oven until ready to serve.
