Dave's Crab Cakes


1     Lb.           Lump Crab Meat
1/2   Cup           Minced Celery
1/2   Cup           Minced Onion
1     Tablespoon    Chopped Green Onions, white
                    part only
2     Tablespoons   Dijon Mustard
2     Tablespoons   Hellman's® Mayonnaise
2     Tablespoons   Bread Crumbs
To Taste            Cayenne Pepper
To Taste            Salt

1/2   Cup           All-Purpose Flour
1     Cup           Vegetable Oil



I took my wife out for dinner and they had crab cakes on their menu as an appetizer. After tasting them, I bribed the Chef for his recipe with one of my 'secret' recipes. I modified his just a little and left out the Tarragon


Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, combined the Crab Meat, Celery, Onions, Green Onions, Mustard, Mayonaisse, Bread Crumbs, Cayenne Pepper and the Salt and mix thoroughly. Divide the mixture into equal portions and form into a patty. Lightly dust each patty with flour, place the patties on a plate covering them with aluminum foil and refrigerate for at least one hour, although over night is much better.

Heat a large frying pan and then add the Vegetable Oil. When the oil is hot, place the patties in the oil and fry for approximately 2 minutes per side or until the patties are golden brown.
