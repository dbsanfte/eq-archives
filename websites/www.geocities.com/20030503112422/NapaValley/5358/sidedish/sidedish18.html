Pineapple Casserole


1   Large Can    Pineapple chunks, drained
1   Cup          Sugar
3   Tablespoon   Flour
1   Cup          Shredded cheddar Cheese

            Topping

1   Package      Ritz® Crackers, crushed
1   Stick        Melted Butter or Margarin



"BLUE EYES' came through again with yet another wonderful recipe. 'Myra, I sure hope you aren't expecting any royalties from the cookbook once it is published'……….LOL!!!!!!


Directions:


Mix the Flour, Sugar and Shredded Cheddar Cheese together in a large mixing bowl. Put the Pineapple Chunks in a casserole dish and cover them with the Flour/Sugar/Cheese mixture, mixing thoroughly.

Prepare the topping by combining the crushed Ritz® crackers and melted butter. Put this mixture on top of the pineapple and spread evenly. Bake in a 350-degree oven for approximately 25 minutes. This dish should be served warm.
