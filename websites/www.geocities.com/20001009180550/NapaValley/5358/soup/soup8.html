Cucumber Salad


4     Medium     Cucumbers, thinly slices 
1     Medium     Yellow Onion, thinly slices 
3/4   Cup        White Sugar 
1/2   Cup        Water 
1     Cup        White Vinegar 
1/2   Teaspoon   Dried Dill



I found this recipe on the internet. I have been looking for a recipe of this nature and although I will be "doctoring" it up to get the final results that I want, it is good enough as is it to post immediately.



Directions:

Bring sugar, water, vinegar and dill to a boil and pour over cucumbers and onions. Chill for at least three hours prior to serving. (Note: the liquid amount will double or triple as this chills.)
