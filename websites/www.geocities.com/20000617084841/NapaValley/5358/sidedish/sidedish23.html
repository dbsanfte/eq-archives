Sweet Potato Casserole


3	Cups	Cooked, mashed Sweet Potatoes
1	Cup	Sugar
2	Medium	Eggs, beaten
1	Teaspoon	Vanilla Extract
1/2	Cup	Butter, melted

	Topping

1	Cup	Brown Sugar
1/2	Cup	All Purpose Flour
1/2	Cup	Butter, melted
1	Cup	Crushed Walnuts



I meet a very sweet young lady in a chat room and we chatted about a lot of things, recipes being one of them. She was kind enough to give me this recipe and boy if you like Sweet Potatoes, you will love this one. Thanks Sunny-Mi, *Big Hugs & Kisses*


Directions:


In a large mixing bowl, combined the Mashed Sweet Potatoes, Sugar, Beaten Eggs, Vanilla Extract and Melted Butter. Mix these ingredients thoroughly and then put it into a casserole dish.

Mix all the topping ingredients in a large bowl. A little water can be added to this mixture in order to make it spread easier. Spread the topping mixture over the top of the sweet potato mixture and bake in a 350-degree oven for 30 minutes.

This dish can be served either hot or cold.
