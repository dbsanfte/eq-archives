                   Our Conception
The discipline of terror was a foresight of 2 young darkelfs making thier way in the land of neriak.
  The mage(allak) and necromancer (grimreapr) sat and invisioned a world of chaos and destruction. A land that the elder darkelfs talked about and past down to thier kindred.It was a way the old norrath once was...a land bountiful of evil and plentiful of chaos.
So the 2 youngsters not knowing what the world had instore for them set a foot to spread the word of darkness across the land. It didn't take long for the young travelers to find a few with the same wants and needs of the old world...that few would rapidly turn into small dark army spreading like the black plague of old.
Banding together they formed a guild so that the purist would know whom had abolished thier ways of life. Soon the name would be feared and respected by those of good and evil nature alike. This band of evil summoned thier names to be the Deciples Of Terror!
