Heavenly Hash Squares


             Cake Mix

2            Sticks        Margarine or Butter
2            Cups          Sugar
4            Large         Eggs
2            Teaspoons     Vanilla Extract
1 1/2        Cups          Self Rising Flour
4            Tablespoons   Cocoa
1 1/2        Cups          Chopped Pecans

             Topping

4            Tablespoons   Cocoa
1            Stick         Margarine or Butter
1            Box           Powdered Sugar
1/4 to 1/2   Cup           Milk
1            Package       Miniature Marshmallows



My wife requested that her mother send this recipe to me. Needless to say, once I got the recipe via 'snail mail', her and the kids insisted that I make it immediately.


Directions:

Cake Mix

Preheat the oven to 350-degrees. Place the Butter in a 13" x 9" baking dish and place the baking dish in the oven to melt the butter.

In a large mixing bowl, combined the Sugar and Eggs *DO NOT USE AN ELECTRIC MIXER*. Add the melted Butter and Vanilla Extract to the bowl and mix well.

In a separate bowl, mix the Flour, Cocoa and Pecans. Add this dry mixture, a little at a time, to the wet mixture. Once all the dry mixture has been incorporated, pout it into the 13" x 9" baking dish and bake for 35 to 40 minutes.

Topping

While the cake is baking, combine the Cocoa, Butter and Powdered Sugar. Add the Milk until the desired consistency is achieved. The frosting should not be to stiff.

When the cake is done, remove it from the oven and cover the top with the Miniature Marshmallows. Finally, cover the top of the cake with the frosting. Allow the cake to cool and then cut the cake into squares.
