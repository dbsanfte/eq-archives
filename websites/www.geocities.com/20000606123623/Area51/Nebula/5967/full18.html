Hail Darkmoon! The war of keeping our forrest pure of race continues. After visiting the Priest of Discord my eyes were truly opened to the ways of evil. Though I stand alone now at the pass, waiting for guard killers, many Dark Elves have joined in when need be. Erudites, humans, high elves, all races come to Nektulo for only one purpose, to attack our great city's guards. *sighs* If only their foul blood would not spill on our sacred ground when I slay them. As the war continues many lives on both sides have been lost. Yet some Dark Elves still do not assist, and slink off into the night like cowards! Have they not the feelings I do for my home in Nektulo's Forrest? How can they stand by while our lands are attacked? I have resorted to many drastic deeds to accomplish our security. The multi-racial bands of killers have been pushed back into the commonlands for now. They await there for me to leave, but I shall not. Instead, I will attack first, bringing down a pack of orcs on them while they rest just outside of our forrest waiting to attack again. "Beware trespassers! ATOS is on duty tonight in the forrest. Leave or die."
