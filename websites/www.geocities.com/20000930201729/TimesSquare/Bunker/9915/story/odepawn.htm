Ode to an Orc Pawn

Oh Mr. Orc Pawn,
Why do you hate me?
I am merely a human in your world!

Oh Mr. Orc Pawn,
Why can't you see?
I am barely a level four!

Oh Mr. Orc Pawn,
Please oh please!
Don't cast your "Darkness" spell!

Oh Mr. Orc Pawn,
I guess it is to be.
I have killed you with my sword!

Oh Mr. Orc Pawn,
I am so sorry...
But your scalp does come in handy!

by Lyndra
