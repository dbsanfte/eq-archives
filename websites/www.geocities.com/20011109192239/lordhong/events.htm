Raid of Dalnir's Crypt 10/7/00

Another successful raid in Dalnir on 10/7/00 was as much fun killing MOBS as it was meeting new friends/guild members that we may have never grouped with before.  Most of the characters we brought were alts, which was even more interesting.  The groups were assigned by level which seemed to work out very well, although our group killed much faster (had to put that in).  The combination of guild members was very diverse (not just the usual 12 druids, and a tank), we had clerics/tanks/enchanters/druid/necro/monk and our favorite Wizard Ereana (who was always there in case things went bad).

I think we all enjoyed ourselves during this raid, it was well suited for our levels, and most have never been there before.  Everyone was extremely helpful, Sparkly gave so many clarities that his mem'ed spell must have been smoking.  And as Kudar said, it was a true event because Dariele's Character died (sorry).  We did have a couple deaths which seems inevitable on these events, but this is EQ, and I hope all got rez'ed.

Loot didn't seem all that great, but we were there for the fun and it was loads of fun.  As usual we got started about 2 hours late and maybe we can work on that a little more.  Weekends are tough trying to make the event on time when real life stuff comes up, and I was late too.  Thanks to all who attended the event, it was all of us that made it so much fun.  I can't wait to hear when the next event will be scheduled (I heard a rumor about Mistmoore).

What a great bunch o people we have here.

--Brilnayr Lifeless
