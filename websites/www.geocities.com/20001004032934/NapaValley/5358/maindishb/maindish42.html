Stuffed Pork Chops


4   Each          Pork Chops, approximately 1" thick
                  with a pocket cut in them
1   Box           Stove Top Stuffing®
2   Tablespoons   Vegetable Oil



This is one of my oldest recipes and to be perfectly honest with you, I really don't know where I got it. The trick to getting the pocket in the pork chops is to get the butcher to do it for you. I have yet to find a butcher shop that would not willing cut a pocket in the pork chops upon request.


Directions:

Prepare the Stove Top Stuffing® according to package instructions and allow it to cool to room temperature. Once it's cool enough to handle, fill each Pork Chop pocket with a generous portion of the stuffing.

In a large skillet, heat the Vegetable Oil. Place the stuffed pork chops in the oil and sear both sides until they are slightly browned.

Put the pork chops on a cookie rack and the cookie rack on a cookie tray and bake in a pre-heated 325-degree oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until the pork chops are cooked.

I normally prepare a second package of Stove Top Stuffing® to serve with the Stuffed Pork Chops.
