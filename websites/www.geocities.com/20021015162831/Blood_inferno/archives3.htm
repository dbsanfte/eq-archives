07 August 2000     Planar drop tables
Hey again!

I have spent some time trying to develop a good system for planar loot.  There are many people who have varying ways of determining what is fair ranging from guild only gets to loot on one extreme, to everyone on the raid can loot, on the other extreme with varying degrees in between.  Please refer to the link in the sidebar under Guides, or follow this link for the article.

What I have compiled is some probability tables for Planar loot only (Armor).  This generally applies to the Plane of Hate and the Plane of Fear.  It does NOT apply to loot that is randomized from normal mobs in the normal zones.

In these tables I describe what I believe to be a major flaw in the current loot system and proposes an implementation of a new loot system.  I would like our guild to take a look at the guide and make responses to the idea on our message board.

I am also going to open it up to the public and ask for feedback.  I hope it stimulates some intriguing discussion.

30 June 2000     Raids:  Plane of Fear, Lady Vox, Plane of Hate -- New poll
Greetings guys and gals.  Today I have some pictures and a few comments on some great raids that our guild has been recently sponsoring.

Dinbas Soulreviver led our guild on a Plane of Fear raid last weekend.  The raid was typical of Planar raids.  Many people lost their lives on the initial break-in of the zone, but after things got rolling, the dividends started to pay off.  Good job Dinbas and those other guild members who assisted with the raid.

Here are a few photos from the raid.

               

Revenge was the word as the guild took down Lady Vox, the ice dragon of Permafrost, on Thursday.  Many may remember the failed attempt on Lady Vox some months ago.  Thursdays raid was not to be the same.  Only a few casualties happened during the pulls of ice giants, and two casualties went down during the actual assault.  Some very nice items were dropped including two Ruined Bolster Belts, two 100% weight reducing bags, two bard horns, and some words.  Congratulations to those who were lucky enough to get a good roll on dragon loot!

12 June 2000     Guild Meeting - Fishing Tournament
Here is some interesting news for the guild.  

The officers of the guild recently held a meeting to discuss some important guild issues.  You can read the detailed minutes of the meeting which have been posted on our message board at this link.  Here is a short summary of some of the changes:

     * Boreal Ace was voted in as a guild Officer.  Congratulations!!

     *Assistant officers have been designated for both higher and lower level players to assist with guild planning events.

     *Guild members playing their alternate characters should remember to place their main characters name in carats at the beginning of any text they say in guild chat.  This should help people identify with the person rather than the character and not feel alienated by new names.  i.e.  "<Necklare>  Hi everyone!"

     *There were some changes to the recruitment policy.  They have been posted on the recruitment policy page as well.

The first ever guild fishing tournament was held in Fironia Vie on Monday night at 7:30pm EST as scheduled.  It turned out to be a short fun-filled event.  Players were given an allotted amount of time to catch the heaviest total catch, and to catch the heaviest single fish (without getting too drunk).   Each winner was awarded a nice prize.  The winners were as follows:

  Heaviest Total Catch Weight Heaviest Fish Weight
1st place Khelvan 43.6 pounds Booja/Khelvan (tie) 2.5 pounds
2nd place Melea 21.6 pounds Annamal 2.1 pounds
3rd place Drathmar 16.7 pounds Melea 2.0 pounds
 

Congratulations to all the winners and thanks to everyone who participated.   As a reminder the event was held as a precursor to a server wide event that our guild will be holding in the very near future.  This will be the first of its kind on any server.  Please make any comments you have about this event on our message board.
