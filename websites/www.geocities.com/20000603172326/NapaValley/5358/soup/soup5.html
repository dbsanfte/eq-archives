Chicken & Cheese Soup with Green Chiles



1 1/2   Cups          Chicken Stock/Broth
2       Cubes         Chicken Bouillon cubes 
                      for extra flavoring
1       Can           Drained and Chopped
                      Tomatoes 
1       Can           Ro-Tel® Mild Tomatoes 
                      and Green Chilies
1       Bag           Frozen Corn (Optional)
2       Tablespoons   Butter
2       Tablespoons   Vegetable Oil
1       Medium        Minced Yellow Onion
1/2     Teaspoons     Cayenne Pepper
1 1/2   Teaspoons     Chili Powder
1/4     Cup           Flour
1       Cup           Milk
1/2     Cup           Sour Cream
2       Cups          Cooked and Shredded Chicken
2       Cups          Shredded Cheddar Cheese
To Taste              Salt
To Taste              Black Pepper



I was telling a young lady that I work with about my cook book and she asked to see it. She copied several recipes and in return gave me this one.


Directions:

In a large pot, bring the Chicken Stock and the Tomatoes to a boil. Turn heat to low and let simmer, covered. If you are going to use the corn, add it to the pot at before bring it to a boil.

In a large skillet, heat the Butter and Vegetable Oil on medium high heat. Add the Chopped Onions and sauté for approximately 4 minutes, stirring constantly. Add the Green Chiles and cook an additional 2 minutes. Add the Cumin and Chili Powder and stir well. Slowly add the Flour blending until it is all mixed together. Add the Milk and cook on low heat until the mixture has thickened. (Note: this should be a very thick mixture.)

Ladle 1 cup of the stock mixture into the skillet and stir. Pour the contents of the skillet into the stock. Slowly stir in the Shredded Cheese and Sour Cream. Cook for 5 minutes and then add the Shredded Chicken. Cook for an additional 5 minutes to heat through. Add the Salt and Black Pepper to taste.
