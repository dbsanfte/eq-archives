The Earth Pet
    The Earth pet is the brute of all pets.  With it's high strength it is the hardest hitting elemental,  but also takes a great deal of hits due to the fact that it's agility is the lowest.  The earth elementals high hitpoints help make up for this somewhat.  Due to the fact that it's one tough lil critter and it's root spell being able to hold a monster off the frail magician, the earth elemental is the elemental of choice for the soloing magician,
Note: Level 50+ pets now have the ability to cause damage with their root spell.
