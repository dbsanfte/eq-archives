Chicken Rice


4    Cups          Uncooked Kraft® Minute Rice
1    Can           Campbell's® Cream of 
                   Chicken Soup
4    Tablespoons   Butter
22   Ounces        Water
1    Can           Chunked Chicken, flaked



This one just came to mind while I was preparing the Creamy Chicken Casserole the other night. That dish turned out so well I just had to give this one a shot. Hey, it worked and everyone loved it.


Directions:


Put the Chicken Soup, Butter and the Water in a large saucepan and bring it to a boil. Remove the saucepan from the heat, add the Chicken and the White Rice and cook the rice according to the package instructions.
