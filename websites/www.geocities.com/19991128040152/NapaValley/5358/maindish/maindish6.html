Swiss Steak



2        Lbs          Round Steak, cut into 
                      serving pieces
1        Teaspoon     Salt
1/8      Teaspoon     Black Pepper
1        Large        Yellow Onion, Sliced
1-16 oz  Can          Diced Tomatoes



Many years ago, right after we purchased a Crock Pot, we bought the book "The Unwatched Pot" by Paula Franklin. This is one of the recipes from that book and one of my daughters favorite recipes also.


Directions:

Combined all the ingredients in the crockpot.

There are several cooking times available. Just pick the one that fits your schedule:

1)  Cook on Low 9 Hours
2)  Cook on High 3-4 Hours
3)  And this is this one I use, 1 Hour on High, then 5 Hours 
    on Low and finally, 1 more Hour on High.
