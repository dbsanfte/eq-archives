Crystal Carrots


1     Tablespoon    Vegetable Oil
1     Teaspoon      Butter or Margarine
12    Medium        Carrots, cut diagonally into 
                    1/2" thick slices
1/2   Tablespoon    Salt
1/3   Cup           Boiling Water
2     Tablespoons   Brown Sugar
1     Teaspoon      Grated Lemon Rind
1     Tablespoon    Lemon Juice
2     Tablespoon    Butter or Margarine
2     Tablespoons   Chopped Parsley



Yep, if you guessed that this one came from the Internet, you guessed correctly. I guess I just have too much time on my hands. But I do cook all these recipes before they go into my cookbook or are posted to my home page, so the time isn't wasted.


Directions:


In a large skillet, combine the Oil and 2 Tablespoons of Butter and heat until the butter melts. Add the Carrots and sauté until the carrots are shiny. Add the Water and the Salt, cover, and simmer until the carrots are crisp-tender. Add the Brown Sugar, Lemon Rind, Lemon Juice and the remaining Butter. Simmer, uncovered, until the sugar is melted, the liquid evaporates, and the carrots are glazed. Sprinkle with the Parsley.
