Marie's Party Mix



1-6 oz    Package   Nabisco® Cheddar Sport-Shaped
                    Snack Crackers *SPORTZ*
1-12 oz   Can       Planters® Cocktail Peanuts



Joe and Marie invited us to their home for a Saturday night cook out. Marie had this party mix on the table when we arrived and it tasted so good I just had to make it as soon as possible.


Directions:

In a large bowl, combined the Cheddar Sport-Shaped Crackers and 1/2 of the can of Cocktail Peanuts. Mix them thoroughly and this mix is ready to serve.
