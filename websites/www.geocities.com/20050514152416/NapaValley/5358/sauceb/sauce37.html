Thousand Island Salad Dressing



2       Cups          Mayonnaise
1/2     Cup           Heinz® Ketchup
2       Tablespoons   Celery, chopped
3       Tablespoons   Onion, chopped
4       Tablespoons   Sweet Pickles, cubed
1 1/2   Teaspoons     Black Pepper
1       Teaspoon      Salt



This is yet another contribution from Donna to the cookbook and the home page.



Directions:

Combined all the ingredients in a large bowl and mix thoroughly. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours prior to serving.
