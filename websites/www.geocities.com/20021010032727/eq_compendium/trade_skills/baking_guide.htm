Baking Guide

General:

Basically, baking involves taking the necessary components, putting them in an oven, smoker, or spit, and hitting the combined button.  By baking, you can create food items that are both longer lasting than store bought food and slightly amusing.  Most bakers recommend putting between 5 to 15 practice points in this skill before beginning.  If anyone has any other information about baking, please contact me.

          Also, a lot of the trivial numbers in the tables are estimates or guesses.  I have been told that anything involving a mixing bowl is above 100, and I have heard varying trivial numbers for anything involving fish.

 

Things to Note:   

·        A spit is essentially an oven that you can carry with you everywhere, thought be careful as some recipes call for ovens or spits specifically

·        Pots & skewers are reusable items, unless you combined the wrong items

·        Fishing is a very good complimentary skill

·        In the oven section of the table, O = oven, Sp = spit, Sm = smoker, and Mx = mixing bowl

·        Baking is a very cheap skill to master, with some people attaining a master level after 20 pp.

·        Foraged Eggs that are know to work are Cockatrice Eggs (Overthere)

·        Supposedly, if certain food items are eaten in the right order, then something special is supposed to happen (of course no one I have heard of knows has ever encountered this)
