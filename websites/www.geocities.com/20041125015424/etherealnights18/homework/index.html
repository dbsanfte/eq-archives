Officially approved by thehatelistings.org. This is a HL who primarily hates homework. Of course unless someone likes homework, then I guess.. you like homeworks then. Exactlyjust what are they? But imagine you do a homework after going to school for 5 days just to reach Saturday and you find yourself you just have to sleep late just to make up with it. Doesn't homework make you feel sick or what! hehe No need to grab a slogan and arm yourselves for hating homework, this hatelisting will just "help" you express it. ^^

What is a hate fanlisting?
 A "hate"listing is the exact opposite of a fanlisting- it's a site/listing for all of those who hate a particular person, place or thing. The purpose of this site is to be a hub of all of the hatelistings out there.
