Donna's Ground Meat Casserole


2     Cup        Elbow Macaroni, uncooked
1     Lb.        Lean Ground Beef
2     Cups       Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1     Cup        Shredded  Sharp Cheese

      White Sauce
1/4   Cup        Margarine
1/2   Cup        Yellow Onion, finely chopped
1/3   Cup        Flour
1     Teaspoon   Salt
1/4   Teaspoon   Black Pepper
2     Cups       Milk


Marc's Ham Casserole was derived from another recipe and this one was derived from Marc's recipe. And the experimentation goes on!



Directions:

Cook the Elbow Macaroni according to the package directions, drain, rinse and set aside.

In a large skillet, brown the Ground Beef.

To make the White Sauce, melt the margarine in a saucepan. Add the Yellow Onion and cook over medium heat for about 5 minutes. Stir in the Flour, Salt and Black Pepper. Add the Milk gradually and continue to cook until it thickens, stirring constantly.

In a lightly buttered 2-quart casserole dish, arrange half of the macaroni in the bottom. Put half of the cooked Ground Beef over the macaroni. Put half of the Shredded Cheddar and Sharp Cheeses over the beef. Repeat with a layer of macaroni, a layer of beef and a layer of cheeses. Pour the White Sauce over the layers.

Bake for 25 minutes in a pre-heated 350-degree oven, stirring every 10 minutes.
