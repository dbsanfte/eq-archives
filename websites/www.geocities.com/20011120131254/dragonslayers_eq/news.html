confirmed druid ports

Confirmed the self only Ring spells to Velious are at the druid guild in Kelethin, didn't see the newest Surefall Glade spells there though.
no more cross wk runs

Well, I know I need the exercise, but I am so, so glad that ports to far west Antonica are in the game now.  I have personally verified that a vendor in Surefall Glade is selling the spells.  Vendor's name is Raken.  Rumors have it that a vendor in the druid's guild in Kelethin also is selling the spells.  The ports take you to Surefall Glade to the druid trainers.  Haven't heard anything about any new wizard ports.  Also, rumored to be in existence now are self only spells to GD, WL, and CS.
monster mash

The last patch allows the playing of monster NPC (low level, though).  But has been put on hold for who knows how long?  Quoted from Alan “Absor” VanCouvering from the official EQ forums.

I thought I'd pop in and explain to you all why you can't play as monsters at the moment.

When the patch went long today (I'm very sorry about that) the servers came back up pretty much at prime time. We decided that it might not be the best thing in the world to turn on project M right after a patch and to coincide with prime time. We thought that all of those factors combined might be a little more than the PvP servers or their players could handle.

We'll turn it on some time soon. And I'm probably not going to announce it…

Alan
