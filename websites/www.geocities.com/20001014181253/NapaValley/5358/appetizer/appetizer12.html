Linda's Cheese Dip



1           Lb.   Owens® Mild Country Sausage
1/2	    Lb.	  Ground Chuck
1 - 5 Lb    Box   Velveeta® Cheese
1 - 10 oz   Can   Rotel® Tomatoes
1 - 10 oz   Can   Rotel® Tomatoes with Green Chiles



This recipe was given to me by a co-worker just prior to celebrating another co-workers 40th Birthday. This was Linda's contribution to the festivities.


Directions:

Fry the Owens Mild Country sausage and the Ground Chuck until brown. Drain off the excess grease and set the cooked meat aside. Cube the Velveeta Cheese and place it in a Crock-Pot which has been turned on High. Add the two cans of Rotel Tomatoes and stir frequently. Once the cheese is melted, add the cooked meat and stir completely. Turn the Crock-Pot to low and serve with Corn Chips.
