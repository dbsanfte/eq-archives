Italian Meatballs and Spaghetti


	Meatballs


1     Pound         Lean Ground Meat
2     Tablespoons   Italian Bread Crumbs
1     Large         Egg
1     Tablespoon    Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/2   Teaspoon      Salt
1/2   Teaspoon      Black Pepper



	Sauce


1             Pound        Lean Ground Meat
1             Medium       Diced Yellow Onion
1             Tablespoon   Wesson® Vegetable Oil
2 - 15 oz     Can          Hunt's® Tomato Sauce
2 - 14 ½ oz   Can          Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes, undrained
4 - 6 oz      Can          Hunt's® Tomato Paste
3             Teaspoons    Crushed Basil
3             Teaspoons    Sugar
1             Teaspoon     Garlic Powder
1             Teaspoon     Oregano
4             Tablespoons  Grated Parmesan Cheese

                           Hot cooked spaghetti



This recipe was given to me by a Lady that is deserving of my "Special White Rose". She is very, very special to me and I thank you very, very much, from the very bottom of my heart. KOTC.!!!!!



Directions:

In a bowl, combine all the Meatball ingredients and shape this mixture into 20 - 1 ½ inch balls. Place them on a baking sheet and bake at 375 degrees for 25 minutes and then drain them.

In a large saucepan, sauté onions in the Wesson® Vegetable Oil, on low heat, until they are tender. In a skillet, brown the ground meat, draining off any excess grease. Put the browned meat into the saucepan with the onions. Put the undrained Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes into a food processor and Puree in bursts of ten seconds until the tomatoes are reduced. Stir in the pureed tomatoes and the remaining ingredients except the cheese and spaghetti. Simmer while the meatballs bake. Add the drained meatballs to the pan, cover and simmer an additional 20 minutes. Stir in the Grated Parmesan Cheese and serve over the spaghetti. Make approximately 5 servings.

Note: If you like Italian Sausage, substitute ½ pound in both of the Meatballs and the Spaghetti sauce.
