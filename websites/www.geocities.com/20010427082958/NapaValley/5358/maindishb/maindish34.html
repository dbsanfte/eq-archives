Crab Stuffed Chicken Breasts


3	Ounces        Philadelphia® Cream 
                      Cheese, softened
2	Tablespoons   Onions, minced
1	Tablespoon    Fresh Parsley, minced
1/4	Teaspoon      Dried Dillweed
1	Teaspoon      Garlic, minced
1/8	Teaspoon      Lemon-Pepper
1-4 oz	Can           Crab Meat
4	Each          Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breasts
3	Cups          All Purpose Flour
2	Large         Eggs, beaten
3	Cups          Progresso® Italian Bread Crumbs
2	Tablespoons   Butter
2	Tablespoons   Vegetable Oil



While visiting one of the many home pages on the Internet, I found this recipe. The combination of chicken and crabmeat, sort of a 'Surf n Turf', really caught my attention.


Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, combine the Cream Cheese, Onion, Parsley, Dillweed, Garlic and Lemon-Pepper. When these ingredients are mixed thoroughly, fold in the crabmeat.

Using a small knife, cut horizontally through the center of each chicken breast, creating a pocket. Fill each pocket with 1/4 of the stuffing. One at a time, dip each chicken breast into the Flour, then the beaten Eggs and then the Italian breadcrumbs. Place the chicken breasts on a plate, cover them with aluminum foil and refrigerate for 1 hours.

Melt the butter with the oil in a heavy, large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook them until they are cooked thoroughly and Golden Brown on both sides, approximately 10 minutes on each side.
