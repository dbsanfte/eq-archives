    Friday, April 06, 2001

What's wrong with this picture?

 

I am sitting in Rivervale waiting on a resurrection is what's wrong!  No actually there is nothing wrong with this picture, I am damn proud of it, just wish I didn't have all the time waiting on a resurrection on my hands to take it.  Low and behold a great raid goes sour as Verant drops the servers on us!

Last count was about 36 Blood Inferno members on this raid led by Semid.  Wish all of you were there.

Bah... so here I am updating the web site cause the servers are flooded with people trying to get back online, looked like Verant reset 15 or so servers to fix the connectivity problem we were all facing tonight.

Oh well, lets get back in there and kick some ass!  Oops, did I say that?

 

Big Calendar Changes!
Woot!  I was finally able to get a good client to serve as our calendar host.  This is great news for the guild.  It will finally allow YOU to post your raids directly on the calendar.  There has to be a few limitations to this to prevent people from messing with it so I currently have it set up to allow people to only view and add events.  Key leaders in the guild will have the ability to edit posts in the calendar as needed.  Should you need a post edited or deleted please post a message on the guild message board.  The site is also set up to send an email to our guild email account every time someone adds a new event! (please be courteous and don't Spam the guild email)  It is really awesome.

Please click on the Event Calendar link in the side bar to view our guilds new events calendar.

To use the calendar simply click on the actual number of the date and then create an event.  There is currently no differentiation between time zones, so I ask that everyone post the times in EST or specify if the raid time is anything other than EST.  You can even link your post to our message board sign up sheets by typing the link in the second field at the bottom... its really great!

I was also able to load up a few key images on a free image hosting site called Honesty.com to prevent problems with some of our images not being visible on pages other than our guild web site.   For now all is working fine.  The main issue is the guild signatures.  I have seen a few people upload their own pictures to free sites which is what I have chosen to do and edit the link in my Ezboard preferences.  I suggest if you want to continue to use the signatures, you should upload the picture you chose to a free image hosting site such as "Honesty.com" as well. (Thanks Zythen)

Please feel free to provide any feedback you have on the calendar by posting comments on our message board, or hey, try using the comments page for once!

 

Wedding Pictures - Phinigel Autropos Slain!
Well guys... ever wonder how to get one of those beautiful Wood-Elf ladies to marry you?  Crelone shows us the secret... look closely

The secret is to make sure no one is looking (Neohex in background), and then get on your knees, proper like, and propose.  If you've been treating her right you will be teleported in front of Brell's own GM, Oribi, and be wed in matrimony (see below).

 

Congrats to the happy couple, and to Brell Serilis' finest guild leader, Melea.

In keeping with the highest tradition of Blood Inferno raids, Phinigel Autropos was slain today.  Unfortunately, he didn't drop the rare drop spine that was needed by our guild mage Aanandar.  We did manage to get a few quest bones from his lair for two of our guild Paladins, Andlar and Marathdamane.  Both Gathis and Nadia won great loot items from the raid.
