Garlic Butter Spread



2      Cloves       Minced Garlic
1/2    Small        Minced Onion
1/8    Teaspoon     White Pepper
1/2    Lb           Butter
To Thin             Peanut Oil



My family enjoys Garlic Toast and I prepare it with more than just pasta meals. But by the time you prepare the bread, you have created a real mess. Garlic powder and pepper is everywhere, not to mention the bread crumbs. So, in order to cut down on the clean-up time, we put together this spread which can be stored in the refrigerator for a long time. Now the only mess we have is the bread crumbs.



Directions:

In a medium size sauce pan, melt the butter being careful not to let it come to a boil. Add the Minced Garlic, Minced Onions and the White Pepper and stir well allowing the onions to sweet. Use the Peanut Oil, if necessary, to thin out the spread.


Once all the ingredients are combined, pour the mixture into a soft spread butter container and allow it to cool to room temperature. Put the Garlic Butter in the refrigerator and then when ever you want garlic toast, just put the spread on bread and place it under the broiler.
