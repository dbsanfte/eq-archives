Triscuit® Nachos



20    Each   Kraft® Triscuits
1/3   Cup    Salsa
1/2   Cup    Cheddar & Monterey Jack Mexican Style 
             Shredded Cheese



This recipe came off the back of a box of Triscuits that were in the break room at work. This is a very quick, simple and tasty appetizer.


Directions:

Arrange the 20 Kraft® Triscuits on a cookie sheet. Top the triscuits with the Salsa and the Shredded Cheese. Bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted. These can also be prepared in a Microwave oven by putting the temperature on HIGH and heating for approximately 1 minute, or until the cheese is melted.
