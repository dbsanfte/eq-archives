RUNNYEYE RAID

ERA held a lvls 15+ RunnyEye Raid on Saturday, December 12, 2002 and it went GREAT!!! Attendance was slightly lower than expected but we had enough for at least one full group throughout most of the raid.
The raid leader of this one was Slayerxx, our new Assistant Deputy, and he did a wonderful job! In attendance at the raid at one time or another were the following members: Burmaben, Cynless, Loliela, Namin, Romantica, Slayerxx, Stasu, Vortania, Voruh, Wisesoul, and Ziniz.

  • Two Blackened Alloy Waistbands
  • One Blackened Alloy Longsword
  • Two Black Alloy Girdles

The total PP value of everything we brought home was over 1200pp!!! (The medallion alone is valued at over 600pp!!) Let's hope all our raids go as well as this one and we hope to see you all there at the next raid!! :-)
