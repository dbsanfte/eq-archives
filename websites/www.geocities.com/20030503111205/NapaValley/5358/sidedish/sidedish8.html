German Potato Salad


6     Cups 	   Sliced and Cooked 
                   Potatoes (about 3 pounds),
1/2   Lb 	   Cooked Bacon, diced
1/3   Cup 	   Bacon drippings
1     Tablespoon   Flour
1     Tablespoon   Sugar
1     Teaspoon 	   Salt
1     Teaspoon 	   Celery Seed
1/4   Teaspoon 	   Ground Black Pepper
1/2   Cup 	   Chicken Broth
1/2   Cup 	   White vinegar.



This is yet another recipe that I downloaded from the Internet. As a result of this one though, I have decided to start to include the home page address of the location from which I "appropriated" the recipe.


Directions:


Cook the bacon until it is crisp. Remove the bacon from the pan reserving the drippings. Heat the drippings and blend in the flour, sugar, salt, celery seed and pepper. Gradually blend in the broth and the vinegar. Cook, stirring constantly, until slightly thickened. Combine the potatoes and 1/4 of the bacon bits. Pour the sauce over this mixture and toss them together gently. Put the mixture in a serving dish and place it in a 325 degrees oven to reheat for 1/2 hour.
