News 12-16

Hail Rangers of Norrath! I recently visited The Rangers Glade and read the great news about their webmaster. Below is the following news from his site :

The time has come....

For Eala Tuathanas and Sunmoon Solusterra to be wed! *cheers*

We have chosen the beautiful Surefall Glade as our venue.

It is a short hike from Qeynos and should present no problems as far as enemies along the way. The wedding will be held at 7PM EST. We realize that may be a little late for our european friends, but with the impending holidays, it is tough to schedule convenient for everyone.

Tunare has blessed me in my travels through Norrath and I in turn will shower those blessings upon my wife. That meaning, we do not really need, nor expect gifts for us. *grins* Of course, any booze would be welcomed and dealt with!

So, I hope you all can share in our happiness and join us this Saturday. As with the last Ranger event on Veeshan (which gathered more that 100 of us) you can simply make a new character on Veeshan if you are not already there. I have 5 excellent Druid brethren available to whisk us around the world so that you all may join us.

Much as last time we will also try to organize some fun events for us all as well! I encourage one and all to attend this grand event.

Sunmoon Solusterra (Lost Order of Akalabeth)
Glademaster of The Ranger's Glade

Congratulations Sunmoon!
