Cajun Shrimp Balls



2     Tablespoons   Butter
2     Tablespoons   Chopped Green Onions
2     Tablespoons   Chopped Fresh Parsley
2     Tablespoons   All Purpose Flour
1/2   Cup           Milk
1/4   Teaspoon      Louisiana Hot Sauce
1/2   Teaspoon      Salt
1/2   Lb.           Cooked, Chopped Shrimp
2     Large         Eggs
2     Cups          Bread Crumbs
                    Vegetable Oil for frying



This recipe came from one of the Featured Home Pages in the Napa Valley community of GeoCities. There is an unbelievable amount of recipes that I have obtained by surfing these pages.


Directions:

In a large skillet, melt the butter and then add the Green Onions and Parsley and cook for 1 minutes. Blend in the Flour, Salt and Hot Sauce and then stir in the Milk. Cook, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil.

Remove the mixture from the heat; stir in the Shrimp and the chill the mixture for approximately 30 minutes.

In a bowl, beat the Eggs until they mixed. Shape the Shrimp mixture into balls, using 1 Tablespoon for each ball. Dip the balls into the Bread Crumbs, then the egg and then the Bread Crumbs again. Chill the shrimp balls for 30 minutes and then fry them in 350-degree oil for 2 to 3 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
