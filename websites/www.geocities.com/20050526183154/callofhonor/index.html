Call of Honor

Call of Honor is a Guild in Everquest located on the Nameless Server.  Together as a group of friends and family we seek to prove that Honor still exists within the land of Norrath.  Together we call to those who seek to defend Honor to stand with us and unite against those destined to destroy Honor and Justice.

 

Call of Honor to begin rebuilding process

On April 17, the senior officers of Call of Honor held a meeting to discuss the future vitality of the guild. The outcome was the agreement to a rebuild and active recruiting. Lately the guild has been waning in active memberships, and our new goal is to restore the guild to what it once was. Raiding and other guild activities are in the works for weekly deployment. The appointment of new officers was also addressed at the meeting, with the addition of Wanudce, Ronoele, and Glulaien to their ranks.

Webpage Mayhem The webpage is currently under heavy construction, so please be patient while we get everything nailed down. We are planning on having an online application for the guild as well as a calendar for showing monthly events. If you have any ideas for what you'd like to see on the webpage, or have a screenshot you'd like to send us, email them to Wanudce.

Everquest Anniversary Skelly's Gone With the removal of the nightly skelly army's from newby zones, we see peace again in our land. Many reports from players show mixed emotions about this. It seems the nusiance of lower level characters was the optimal gear source for the mid ranger's. One can only sit back and wonder at the amount of xp lost durning these troubled times. I think a thank you is in order for all those clerics that came to many a player's aid.

Weekly Raids and Events We are planning on having weekly guild events. Raids are of couse going to be on the lineup, as well as "work-on-so-in-so's-epic" nights. If you have other ideas, we would love to hear them. Drop an email or a tell to any officer with what you think would be fun for the guild to do. Your input is what we are looking for here, because we do this for the members. If you think its fun, no doubt we will too.

Help your fellow Guildy's As a loose guidline, we would like to see more active grouping among guild members. Basically, if you are looking for members in a group, look to the guild first. By grouping with guild members your promote the guild bond as well as get to know one another better. These bonds are what make raids more successfull. So if you are in a group and looking for more, or are just solo and wanting someone to tag along, send a shout in guild tell.

Brewing Tradeskill recently found to promote Alcohol Tollerance Scientists in the Plane of Knowledge have recently found a link between brewing and drinking. It would seem that many players are tasting the fruits of their labors, some even while still brewing! Evidence can be witnessed by the incomprehensible speech and rude walking patterns. PoK guardians are forming a special task force to deal with "drinking while riding", as it seems some folks are actually taking drogs and horses our for a spin while under the infuence. Make sure to look both ways before crossing that path!

Super-Glue found in Sony's back pocket!As many of you have found out, extra care must be taken while traveling in Norrath. Many tree's and ladders are coated in superglue. The glue is colorless and odorless, with no warning given to the unsuspecting adventurer. Should you get stuck, here are some things to try : back up the same way you went in to get suck. Camp and log back on, sometimes this does the trick. If all else fails, contact a wizzy or druid and try to get a port.
