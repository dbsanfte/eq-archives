Joe 'n Karen's Texas Orgasmic Chicken


4   Each     Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breasts
6   Large    Shrimp, peeled and deveined
8   Strips   Lean Hormel Black Label® Bacon
4   Dash's   Essence Seasoning *See Sauces, 
             Dressings, etc for the recipe 
             for Essence*
To Taste     Salt
To Taste     Black Pepper

Directions:

Butterfly the Chicken Breasts being careful NOT to slice them completely through. Open the chicken breasts and lay them out on a work area.

Cut the shrimp in half lengthwise and place three halves in the center of each chicken breast. Fold the chicken breasts over the shrimp and then wrap each one with 2 strips of bacon, securing the bacon ends with toothpicks. Salt and Pepper both sides of the chicken breasts and sprinkle one dash of Essence on them.

Place the prepared chicken breasts over medium-heat charcoal. Joe prefers to use Mesquite chips to smoke his chicken, but I am sure and any wood will work. Grill for 15 to 20 minutes per side or until they are golden brown. Note that the bacon will probably scorch a little.

Joe 'n Karen recommend side dishes of a Garden Salad and Spanish Rice to accompany the chicken breasts.
