Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookies


3/4	Cups	      Butter Flavor Crisco®
1 1/4	Cups	      Firmly Packed Brown Sugar
2	Tablespoons   Milk
1	Tablespoon    Vanilla Extract
1	Large	      Egg
1 3/4	Cups	      All-Purpose Flour
1	Teaspoon      Salt
3/4	Teaspoon      Baking Soda
1	Cup           Nestle's® Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
1	Cup           Large Pecan Pieces
		      {Note: If the nuts are omitted, 
                      use 1 1/2 cups of Nestle's® 
                      Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips}



This recipe was cut out of a magazine and it has so much Butter Flavor Crisco® on it that is difficult to read.


Directions:

Pre-Heat the oven to 375 degrees.

Cream the Butter Flavor Crisco®, Brown Sugar, Milk and Vanilla Extract in a large bowl. Blend until the mixture is creamy. Blend in the Egg.

Combine the All-Purpose Flour, Salt and Baking Soda in a bowl. Add to the creamed mixture gradually. Stir in the Nestle's® Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips and Nuts.

Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough 3 inches apart on an ungreased baking sheet.

Bake at 375 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes for chewy cookies. (They will look light and moist - DO NOT OVERBAKE). Bake for 11 to 13 minutes for crisp cookies.

Cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes and then remove them to a cooling rack. Makes 3 dozen - 3 inch cookies.
