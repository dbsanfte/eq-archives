Honey/Mustard Salad Dressing



3/4   Cup           Light Mayonnaise
3     Tablespoons   Honey
2     Tablespoons   Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard
1/2   Tablespoon    White Vinegar



My wife loves Honey/Mustard Salad Dressing and we have never been able to purchase any in the grocery store that she really enjoyed. It became my responsibility to locate a recipe on the net that she would enjoy. This recipe is the results of that search.



Directions:

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix thoroughly. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours prior to serving.
