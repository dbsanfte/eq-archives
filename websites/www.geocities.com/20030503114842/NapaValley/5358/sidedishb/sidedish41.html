Spicy Black-Eyed Peas


1         Lb.         Dried Black-Eyed Peas
1/2       Lb.         Bacon, chopped
1/4       Cup         Bacon Drippings
4         Cups        Water
1         Large       Onion, chopped
1-16 oz   Can         Crushed Tomatoes
2         Teaspoons   Garlic Powder
2         Teaspoons   Chili Powder
1         Teaspoon    Salt



This past New Years Day I discovered just how much my wife enjoys Black-Eyed Peas. She is from Georgia and I assumed it was a tradition. I soon learned that she really enjoys them, regardless of what time of the year it is. This one is for you Sweetie.


Directions:


Wash and pick over the Black-Eyed Peas. In a large pot, or Dutch oven, combine the peas and the Water and bring to a boil. Reduce the head and cook on low heat, covered, for 3 minutes.

Add the Bacon, Bacon Drippings, Onion, Tomatoes, Garlic Powder, Chili Powder and Salt, cover, and simmer for approximately 1 hour or until the peas are tender.
