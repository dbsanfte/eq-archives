Tales of Lady Vox 

 
 

 

It is said that in the farthest reaches of the frozen plains of Everfrost, there is an icy keep inhabited by Ice Goblins. And within the depths of the frost-bitten caverns dwells the Queen of these Goblins, the dragon, Lady Vox. Having spent more than a few of our formative years helping the residents of Halas by eliminating the goblin threat, we could not resist finding out if the tales of Lady Vox were true. Watching many of the elders of Norrath gather in Halas in mighty forces, we stopped our pursuit of the elusive Goblin Archealogist and followed them through the twisty cavern passages, deeper than we had ever gone before. Our wondering eyes beheld the throne room of the King of the Ice Goblins, and to our utter amazement, Lady Vox answered the challenge posed by the brave adventurers. Below is what we saw before we were charmed by the awesome forces of Lady Vox and ran in utter fear before our very fast and painless demise...
