Double Chocolate Brownies


1 1/2     Cups        All Purpose Flour
1/2       Teaspoon    Baking Soda
1/2       Teaspoon    Salt
2/3       Cup         Margarine or Butter
1 1/2     Cups        Sugar
1/4       Cup         Water
2-12 oz   Packages    Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips, divided
2         Teaspoons   Vanilla Extract
4         Large       Eggs
1         Cup         Chopped Walnuts or Pecans



My stepdaughter surprised me with these brownies one weekend. I didn't realize just how good a cook she can be. Now I am wondering how many other recipes she has 'tucked' away…..LOL!!!


Directions:

Combine the Flour, Baking Soda and Salt and set aside. Combine the Margarine, Sugar and Water in a saucepan and just bring it to a boil. Stir in 1 package of the Chocolate Chips and the Vanilla Extract. Stir until the chips are melted and the mixture is smooth.

Pour the chocolate mixture into a large mixing bowl. Add the Eggs, one at a time, and beat well after each one is added. Gradually add the flour/baking soda/salt mixture and mix well. Stir in the remaining chocolate chips and the nuts.

Note: TSpread the batter in a greased 9" x 13" baking pan and bake in a pre-heated 325-degree oven for 30 to 35 minutes.
