Crab Casserole


1          Lb.           Shredded Crabmeat
1 - 8 oz   Package       Philadelphia® Cream 
                         Cheese, softened
2          Cups          Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1	   Tablespoon    Lemon Juice
2          Tablespoons   Mayonnaise
           Dash          Worcestershire Sauce
           Dash          Onion Salt


A visitor to my home page signed my guest book, and as is customary, I visited her page and found this recipe. It is an excellent combination of CrabMeat and Cheddar Cheese.



Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, combined the Cream Cheese, Lemon Juice, Onion Salt, Worcestershire Sauce and the Mayonnaise and mix thoroughly. Flake the crabmeat and then add it to this mixture along with 1 1/2 cups of the Shredded Cheddar Cheese and mix thoroughly. Place this mixture in a casserole dish and top it with the remaining Cheddar Cheese.

Bake in a 350-degree oven until bubbly. Serve hot.
