Scythe of the Shadowed Soul
Most of this info is complete, though if you find any details I have left out, please post it or email me with it and I will fix it.
1)     Head to Nektulos Forest and find NPC by the river loc -1000, -700.

2)     Head to Lake Rathetear and find Kazen Facae loc +340, -1600.

3)     Kill Sir Edwin Motte (level 33 paladin.)  He is easiest to catch in tavern by the docks in East Freeport where he is on a rotation with Trumpy Irontoe.

4)     Bring head of Motte to Kazen and recieve a Symbol of the Apprentice.

5)     Bring Symbol of the Apprentice to the DE in Nektulos Forest, she'll return to you Twisted Symbol of the Apprentice.

6)     Go to Najena and camp FBR (flowing black robe-dropped from Najena.)  Give FBR to DE female in Nektulos Forest and get some moss.

7)     Head to Lake Rathe & give moss and Twisted Symbol of the Apprentice to Kazens apprentice, Emkel.  Get Symbol of the Serpent.

8)     Go to Swamp of No Hope and find Ssessthrass loc +3800, +1600 give him Symbol of the Serpent, receive Scaled Symbol of the Serpent.

9)     Next go to Chardok.  Kill Royal Herbalist and loot Manisi Herb (found on way to overking area, right before the secret door which leads to overking room.)

10)   Go back to Ssessthrass and give him Scaled Symbol of the Serpent and herb, and he'll return Refined Manisi Herb.

11)   Go to Lake Rathetear and give Emkel Refined Manisi Herb, he will give you Symbol of Testing.

12)   You'll need about 12-15 people ready (clerics and tanks are a must) and give Symbol of Testing to Kazen.  3 mobs will spawn.  Kill these, last one drops Symbol of Insanity.

13)   Go to Timorous Deep, give Symbol of Insanity to Drendico Metalbones (near where evil raft drops you off, on hill) and he gives you his journal.

14)   Gather Cloak of Spiroc Feathers (quest in Sky), Eye of Innoruuk, and Blood of Cazic Thule.  Bring all 3 along with Drendico's journal to Timerous Deep.  Give to Drendico, he returns to you a Prepared Reagent Box..

15)   Take Prepared Reagent Box to kazen, and give it to him.  He'll give you back a Tome of Instruction.

16)   Go to Plane of Sky, give Thunder Spirit Princess 3pp.  Then clear isle to 3rd (Gorgalosk) isle.

17)   Give Gkzzallk the Tome of Instruction.  You get back Gkzzallk In A Box (no battle.)

18)   Go to Lake Rathear, give Kazel Gkzzallk In A Box (no battle.)  Get back Scythe Of The Shadowed Soul.
