Chicken Parmesan



4    Each          Boneless, Skinless Chicken 
                   Breasts
2    Cans          Italian Style Stewed  
                   Tomatoes
2    Tablespoons   Cornstarch
1/2  Teaspoon      Crushed Basil
1/4  Teaspoon      Pepper Sauce
1/4  Cup           Grated Parmesan Cheese

>

Normally, I will write down a recipe that I find in a magazine while waiting in a Doctor’s office, rather than cut it out. (Lord knows there is always time). This recipe however, was cut out from a magazine and I sure hope I did not do that to one of the magazines in the Doctor’s office. On second thought, I just saw my latest statement from the Doctor and I hope I did get it from one in his office


Directions:

Put the Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts in a baking dish, cover and cook for 15 minutes in a 425 degree oven.

In a medium sauce pan, combine the Italian Style Stewed Tomatoes, Basil, Cornstarch and Pepper Sauce and cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce is thickened.

Pour the sauce over the chicken breasts, top with the Parmesan Cheese and bake and additional 5 minutes uncovered.
