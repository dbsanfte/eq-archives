Potato Casserole


1 - 2 Lb.    Package     Ore Ida® Frozen Hash Brown 
                         Potatoes, thawed
1 - 12 oz.   Container   Sour Cream
2            Cups        Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1            Can         Campbell's® Cream of
                         Chicken Soup
1            Stick       Butter, melted
1/2          Cup         Yellow Onions, chopped
To Taste                 Salt
To Taste                 Black Pepper



I found this recipe while viewing one of the Napa Valley Communities featured pages.


Directions:


Place the thawed Hash Brown Potatoes in a greased 9" x 13" casserole dish. Season with Salt and Black Pepper.

In a medium mixing bowl, combine the Sour Cream, Melted Butter, Onions and the Soup. Mix thoroughly and then pour this mixture over the potatoes. Sprinkle the Shredded Cheddar Cheese over the top of the potatoes and bake in a pre-heated 350-degree oven for 35 minutes.
