White on White Cake


1           Box         Betty Crocker® White 
                        Cake Mix
1 - 15 oz   Can         Cream of Coconut 
1 - 8 oz    Container   Frozen Whipped Topping, 
                        thawed
1 14 oz     Package	Flaked Coconut



My downstairs neighbor made this cake for her husband. He insisted that she bring me a piece of it and upon tasting it I immediately request a copy of the recipe. This is so easy and so wonderful. Just make sure you have a gallon of cold milk available. *S*


Directions:

Bake the White Cake according to the package directions. While the cake is still warm, using a fork, prick holes in the top of the cake and spoon the Cream of Coconut over the top of it. Cool the cake completely and then spread the Whipped Topping on the top of it. Sprinkle the flaked Coconut over the top of it.

Refrigerate any leftover cake.
