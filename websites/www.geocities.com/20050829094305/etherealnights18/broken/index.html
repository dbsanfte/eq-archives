About. . .
A broken link is one of the most annoying things that can happen anytime while surfing and at the same time one of the things unnoticed when one is not careful checking if all the links are in their proper.. er.. links. It's annoying surfing someone else's site, click an interesting link and suddenly you'll find it's just a broken link... *cough* Either the page itself is nowhere ot be found, wrong target name, wrong url, or simply because.. it's entirely merely broken! If you are still not sure what a broken link is and a little confused on what I am talking about then click here.

What is a hate listing?
A "hate"listing is the exact opposite of a fanlisting- it's a site/listing for all of those who hate a particular person, place or thing. The purpose of this site is to be a hub of all of the hatelistings out there.
