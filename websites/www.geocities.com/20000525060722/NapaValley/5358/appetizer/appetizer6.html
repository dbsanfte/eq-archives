Ham Roll-Ups



6      Slices        Cooked Ham
8     Ounces      Spreadable Philadelphia Cream Cheese®
6     Each         Vlasic® Dill Pickle Spears



Where I currently work, we look for reasons to bring food to work....Birthdays, Holidays, you name it, we will bring food. This recipe is one that I got from one of those "food feasts".



Directions:

Spread a layer of Philadelphia Cream Cheese® on a single piece of ham. *Note: the cream cheese should be at room temperature and it might even be necessary to microwave a small bowl prior to spreading it.* Cut one of the Kosher Dill Pickle Spears in half, lengthwise and then roll it up in the Ham/Cream Cheese. Cut the Roll-Up into bite size pieces and the refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
