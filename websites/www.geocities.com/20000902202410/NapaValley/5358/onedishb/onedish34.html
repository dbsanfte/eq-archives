Cheeseburger Pie


2   Lbs.   Lean Ground Beef
3   Cups   Hungry Jack® Instant Mashed Potatoes
2   Cans   Peas, drained
2   Cans   Campbell's® Condensed Cheddar Cheese Soup


While surf'in the Internet, I came across this recipe. I guess I should start writing down the address of the various pages where I find recipes and linking them to my home page, but then if I did that, people might leave my page before they completely review it……okay, so I am being selfish.



Directions:

Press the Ground Beef into the bottom of a rectangular baking dish and bake it in a preheated 350-degree oven for 45 minutes. When the meat is cooked, remove it from the baking pan and drain off any excess grease. Return the meat to the baking dish.

Make the Mashed Potatoes according to the package directions.

In a saucepan, combined the drained Peas and the Cheddar Cheese Soup and heat until the soup thins out, adding a little milk if necessary, and stir occasionally.

Top the meat with the Mashed Potatoes and then the Peas/Cheese sauce. Cut into squares just prior to serving.
