Taco Soup


2   Lbs	         Lean Ground Beef
1   Medium	 Yellow Onion, chopped
2   Cans	 Diced Tomatoes 
1   Can	         Pinto Beans
1   Can	         Black Beans
2   Cans	 Corn
1   Can	Ro-Tel   Tomatoes (Mild or Regular)
1   Package	 McCormick's® Taco Seasoning
1   Package	 Hidden Valley® Ranch Dressing Mix
1   Cup	         Grated Cheddar Cheese
1   Medium	 Avocado, peeled and sliced
1   Bag	         Tortilla Chips



I don't post a recipe to my home page, or include it in my cookbook until I have tried it. I haven't tried this one, but my best friend did and he sent it to me. Seems he got it from a co-worker by the name of Donna Wilson. Thanks Donna, from both Joe and myself.



Directions:

Brown the Ground Beef and chopped Onion, draining off any excess fat. Add all the remaining ingredients, including the juices.

Simmer for 30 minutes. Serve with grated cheese and sliced avocados on top and tortilla chips.
