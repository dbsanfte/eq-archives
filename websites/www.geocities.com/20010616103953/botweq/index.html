An Everquest Guild on The Nameless Server.

The Brotherhood of the Wolf is dedicated to making the game Everquest: The Ruins of Kunark™ a fun experience for everyone involved with our guild and friends of the guild and guildmembers. We are knight errants, free knights sworn to protect Norrath from evil in all its forms. The idea for the guild came from the books The Runelords and The Brotherhood of the Wolf by David Farland, as well as my character Borenson.
