THE PURISTS


CLERIC

The cleric focuses primarily in healing and defensive spells.
A variation is the SHAMAN, who tends to give more emphasis
to tribal lore in his healing and defensive spellcasting.

DRUID

The druid hails from the woodlands. Because of this
he feels more comfortable using nature based spells.

NECROMANCER

This spellcaster devotes him/herself to the dark arts
and prefers to concentrate on death related spells.

MAGICIAN

The mage concentrates his efforts on summoning magick.

ENCHANTER

The Enchanter focuses energy in embedding and empowering
objects with magical forces.

WIZARD

The most powerful and learned of the purists. The wizard has
great facility with both offensive and defensive spells.


THE HYBRIDS


PALADIN

Paladins are a combination of cleric and warrior.

RANGER

Rangers are warrior druids.

SHADOWKNIGHT

These are warrior necromancers, followers of the dark arts.
