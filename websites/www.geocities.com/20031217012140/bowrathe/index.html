All the leaders decided that it was too much like work to try to keep this guild going, and who wants to play a game if it becomes a job? Thank you for playing with us and we wish you the best in your future endeavors. Hopefully we will cross paths in future endeavors.

6/02/02  Major changes in the guild. Too many problems were happening so we've had to take drastic action to get the guild back to where we wanted to go when it was founded. There is no more CLM positions. The only officers in the guild now are the Council of Five. Everyone in the guild is on a 1 month probationary period. Meaning if you aren't following the rules laid down in the guild policies, then you will be removed from the guild. Due to either inactivity or people leaving, our mains roster has been trimmed by over 50 people in the last 3 days. We still have over 100 mains in the guild, but now we are looking at making the guild a better place for everyone. We want quality not quantity and if someone doesn't fit into that or doesn't like how things are currently run, we ask that you send us an e-mail letting us know that you are leaving and /guildremove. We've had WAY to many people that we knew were good quality players leave us over stupid things that were happening and it stop HERE AND NOW. Even one leaving is too many. WE WILL NOT HAVE IT HAPPEN ANYMORE.
 On to lighter news. We had 8 people break into the planes roster this week. Bladehammer, Relith, Drokow, Ardal, Dondo, Vardane, Yweni, and Bgie are the newest additons. GREAT JOB!!!
 Other than that, nothing else to report. If you have any questions or concerns over how things are going, e-mail us at BoWRathe@yahoo.com.
