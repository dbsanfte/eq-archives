The Irontoe Manifesto
"We are the sons and daughters of Kaladim, the Forge of Norrath.  The glory of battle is a fire raging deep within the chasms of our Dwarven hearts.  United under the banner of the House of Irontoe, we are a Hammer, pounding out justice on Faydwer and beyond.  To Oggok and the foul creatures that dwell there, we are an Axe of molten hate and we bring them death, both swift and sure."
- Dumpy Irontoe
