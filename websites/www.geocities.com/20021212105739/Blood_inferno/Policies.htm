Blood Inferno Mission Statement

Blood Inferno is a family guild. Our interests lie in promoting unity and friendship amongst members in all facets of EverQuest life. Whether it be raiding or social events we shall strive to make the Blood Inferno name synonymous with honor though our words and actions as we journey through Brell together.

 

Policies and Procedures  -  A general guideline

 

     This is not a complete list of rules but should help folks to know the direction of Blood Inferno.

1. The first and most important rule is do not dishonor the guild or guild mates in any shape form or fashion. Blood Inferno was built on honor and has worked hard to uphold our good name. This also applies to interaction with other guilds in Norrath. Be always conscious of how you represent Blood Inferno to others.

2. Loot Rule pertaining to total Blood Inferno raids: If an item drops it will go to the person or be randomed among the people that can use it in the groups. If there is no one that can use the item it will be randomed to all. Once an item is won, you are out of the roll unless a class specific item drops for your class and you wish to roll on it, at which time the item previously won will be rerolled. The purpose of this is to ensure that more members walk away from raids with something. If you win a good item on a total guild raid but in a raid later on win an even better item, the first item is to be "handed" down to someone within the guild that can use it. The purpose of this is to keep good items within our family.

3. If you form a group, do not turn a Blood Inferno member away if you have too many, even if it means making two smaller groups or getting more people to make two full groups, etc. We are family...you would not turn family away.

4. If you have a conflict with another Blood Inferno member, ask an officer or, if none are available, another member to be arbitrator and log to a chat to settle the dispute. Do not argue in guild say or simply say nothing and let the problem escalate...take the initiative and settle it the right way. Sometimes I think you will find problems are a result of miscommunication and can easily be settled.

5. Please watch your language in guild say. This is a family guild with young people and ladies in attendance...do not insult them with bad language.

6. We do not charge each other for services rendered or items given, unless we need to recoup the purchase price in which case it is cost with no mark up.

7. We do not beg for items, money or teleports from our members.
