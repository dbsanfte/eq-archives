Three Cheese Baked Ziti


1-15 oz   Container   Ricotta Cheese (part-skim
                      is optional)
2         Large       Eggs, beaten
1/4       Cup         Parmesan Cheese
1         Lb.         Lean Ground Meat
1-26 oz   Jar         Prego® Meat Flavored Pasta Sauce
1-16 oz   Package     Ziti Paste
1         Cup         Shredded Mozzarella Cheese


Yes sir, the Old Wizard is getting old because I can't remember where I got this recipe and I just got it recently. I am pretty sure it came off the Internet.



Directions:

In a large skillet, brown the Ground Meat and then drain all the excess fat. Stir in the Pasta Sauce and set aside.

In a large bowl, combined the Ricotta Cheese, Beaten Eggs and the Parmesan cheese.

Cook the Ziti Paste according to the package directions, drain and the combined it with the Meat/Pasta Sauce mixture. Spoon 1/2 of the Pasta/Meat/Sauce mixture into a 13" x 9" baking dish, evenly top with the Cheese mixture and then the remaining Pasta/Meat/Sauce mixture. Sprinkle with the Mozzarella Cheese.

Bake for 30 minutes in a pre-heated 350-degree oven or until heated thoroughly.
