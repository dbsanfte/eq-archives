We are a small guild of friends.
The intent of this guild is to strengthen our bonds with each other while helping our fellow guildmates in this most difficult of lands.......

Norrath posseses many challanges and together with time and patience we hope to overcome them together......

At times like this a friendly word of advise or a tip learned from many deaths can bring forth the light, or the night to cover your escape.....

What we lack in numbers we make up in the quality of the people that make us up......

Also what we lack in numbers we make up in the guilds that would call us friend........

Grave Dancers Union has always been here...Grave Dancers Union will continue on.......

It is with this thought that we stive forth into battle....
