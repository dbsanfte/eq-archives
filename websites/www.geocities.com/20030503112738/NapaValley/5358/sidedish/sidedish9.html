Swiss Vegetable Medley


1-16oz   Bag         Frozen Vegetables
1        Can         Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/2      Cup         Shredded Swiss Cheese
1/3      Cup         Sour Cream
1/4      Teaspoon    Black Pepper
1/2      Can         Durkees® French Fried Onions 



         Topping:

1/2      Cup         Shredded Swiss Cheese
1/2      Can         Durkees® French Fried Onions



This is one of the recipes that is partly responsible for putting together this cook book. Not only does my wife have it written down on a piece of paper, but she also has the Durkees® French Fried Onions can from which she obtained the recipe.


Directions:


Thaw and drain the 16 oz bag of Frozen Vegetables. (The best vegetable combination for this meal is Broccoli, Carrots and Cauliflower.)

In a large bowl, combine the vegetables, Cream of Mushroom Soup, Sour Cream, Black Pepper, 1/2 cup of Shredded Swiss Cheese and 1/2 can of the French Fried Onions. Transfer this mixture to a 1 quart baking dish and cover with aluminum foil. Place the casserole in a 350 degree oven and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and sprinkle the top of the casserole with 1/2 cup Shredded Swiss Cheese and 1/2 can of French Fried Onions and bake uncovered for an additional 5 minutes or until the onions are golden.
