    "Strength & Honor"      
       
                       Guild Goals
(1) Strive for honor and loyalty among group members,
(2) Treat all guild members. as well as non-guildmembers with respect
(3) Promote roleplaying not only within the guild, but with anyone you come across within all of Norrath.
                     Guild rules
(1) No begging, but asking for assistance in attaining items is not only acceptable but promoted
(2) When dealing with guildmates, in buying and selling of items or services, dont overcharge them, but that doesnt mean give it away either
(3) No cursing, swearing or outright rude behavior towards a guildmate or any other player. This is not to be tolerated, nor does it fit into what we are trying to do.
(4) Kindness is something seldom forgotten, and most generally, paid back at some time.
(5) Always, if available, help a fellow guildmate, be it with buffs, encouragement or advice.
(6)be nice and try to avoid getting caught up in the "me" aspect in the game. Socialize and forge friendships within the guild. A known ally is a huge advantage when things go bad in a fight.
(7) Have fun, after all this is just a game, Enjoy yourself in the Eq world, and make the most of it. Your actions can haev a long lasting effect not only on the guild, but on all of Norrath.
 ***Breaking or ignoring of these rules is possible means for removal of the guild. .***
