Bing Cherry Salad


1        Can       Bing Cherries
1        Can       Pineapple
1        Cup       Chopped Nuts
1        Package   Cherry Jell-O®
1        Package   Strawberry Jell-O®
1-8 oz   Package   Philadelphia® Cream Cheese
2        Cups      Coca Cola®



I have searched the Internet, bugged the devil out of friends, and looked through I don't know how many magazines trying to find new recipes. Low and behold, my wife has a personal cookbook that she started while she was in Nursing School. This is one of the recipes from that cookbook. By the way, Bing Cherries are a very dark red cherry.



Directions:

Pour the juice from the Cherries and the Pineapple into a large mixing bowl. Dissolve the Jell-O® and the Cream Cheese in these juices. Allow this mixture to cool and then add the Cherries, Pineapple, Nuts and Coca Cola®. Put this mixture in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours to allow it to solidify.
