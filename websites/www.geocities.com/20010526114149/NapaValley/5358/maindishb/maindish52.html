Zesty Sausage and Beans


1-16 oz     Package   Eckrich® Smoked Sausage cut
                      into bite size pieces
1-15.5 oz   Can       Dark Red Kidney Beans, drained
1-16 oz     Jar       Thick and Chunky Salsa
                      Cooked Rice		
                      Sour Cream
                      Chopped Fresh Parsley or
                      Cilantro, *optional*



This one came off the Internet from the Eckrich home page. Most Food Manufacturers have their own home pages and they feature their products in various recipes.


Directions:

Combined the Sausage, Beans and Salsa in a large skillet. Bring this mixture to a boil over medium heat. Cover, reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes or until hot, stirring occasionally.

Serve the Sausage mixture over the Rice and top it with the Sour Cream and Parsley.
