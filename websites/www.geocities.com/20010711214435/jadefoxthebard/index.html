THE PHILOSOPHY BEHIND THE GUILD

 

 

The Flamboyant Swashbucklers strive to be one of the most open and free-minded guilds ever created.

Located on the Prexus server we are a group of like-minded people that emphasize roleplaying above all else.

The Flamboyant Swashbucklers represent the gentlewomen and gentlemen adventurers of the vast seas of this world. We are not an evil guild- at the contrary: though we serve no one, though we are free to do whatever we wish, we support everyone that is in need of help. Be that a damsel in distress, a newbie looking for a backup or anything else that fits our daredevilish background. A strong companionship between its members is an important part of our charter and everyone who has joined our ranks can count on the support of her or his fellow buccaneers.

As disclaimed above high romance and intrigue, exploration and adventure are another integral part of the idea of this guild. There is of course no charge for anyone - neither for joining, nor is there a monthly fee. The game as it is demands a guild leader but this position is only a formal one. Every guild member is a leader, every guild member has the same rights. All can become officers. If there are problems or questions that require voting of some sort we will contact each other via e-mail and meet in Norrath to discuss the matter.

The Flamboyant Swashbucklers are open to all classes, to all races. Though a paladin also being a pirate would ... well ... be a little bit unordinary. But anyone who really wants to join can. Before joining some roleplaying is necessary to "prove" that the new recruit is a true pirate and gentlewoman or -man. That's all.

So if you are looking for a guild that doesn't confine you, if you love to be free and yet still belong - join the Flamboyant Swashbucklers. You can leave anytime. And you will be welcomed back.
Anytime.
