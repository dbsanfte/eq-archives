Saturday March 16, 2001
more news

I've been really busy and haven't had much time as of late to keep the news bytes up to date, but here's a taste of what may be/should be going live on next week's patch.  From eq.castersrealm.com.

In a nutshell:

  • No more looking at the spell book to med

  • Lots of changes for level 10 and under players

  • Resurrection spells change for clerics and paladins

  • Egress/succor/evac changes for druids and wizards

  • No more hell levels pre-level 50 (no retro xp will be given for those of us past level 50)

  • Implementation of new global chat channel commands

  • New armor/skin layer tints

  • More spell stuff

  • And shorties go Hybrid
