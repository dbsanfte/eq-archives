Rules

 

The rules are fairly simple, following them is all that is required of you to remain in the fellowship.

 

1. HAVE FUN!!! (Isn't this the point of a game???)

 

2. Make an effort to help those in need, whether it be by fighting or healing.

 

3. Please, PLEASE do not kill-steal. It's extremely annoying.

 

4. Do not join any other guilds while a member of The Fellowship of the Elementals. (This prevents conflicting interests.) But do not hesitate to adventure with others outside of the guild.

 

5. Also, drop in once in a while just to let us know you're still around. Leave a message on the board or something.

 

That just about covers it.
