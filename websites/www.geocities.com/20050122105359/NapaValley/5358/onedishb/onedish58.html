Chuckwagon BBQ Rice Round-Up


1          Lb.           Lean Ground Beef
1-6.8 oz   Package       Beef Flavored Rice-A-Roni®
2          Tablespoons   Margarine or Butter
2 1/2      Cups          Water
2          Cups          Frozen Corn
1/2        Cup           Kraft® Original Barbecue Sauce
1          Cup           Shredded Cheddar Cheese


I found this recipe on the Rice-A-Roni® home page. Most food processors/manufactures now have their own home pages and feature, amongst other things, recipes. The only change I made to the original recipe was to double the amount of Cheddar Cheese, but that's a family choice.



Directions:

In a large skillet, over medium high heat, brown the Ground Beef until it is well cooked. Drain all the excess grease and remove the cooked ground beef from the skillet and set it aside.

In the same skillet, over medium heat, melt the Butter and then sauté the rice-vermicelli mix until the vermicelli is golden brown. Slowly stir in water and the Corn. Bring this mixture to a boil and then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes or until the rice is tender.

Remove the skillet from the heat, stir in the Barbecue Sauce and the cooked ground beef. Sprinkle the Cheddar Cheese on top of the mixture, cover and let it stand for 3 to 5 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
