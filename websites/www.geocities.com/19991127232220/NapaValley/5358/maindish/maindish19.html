Drunk Chicken



4   Each	  Boneless, Skinless 
                  Chicken Breasts
2   Tablespoons   Butter
3   Ounces        Cream Cheese & Chives
1   Small Can     Mushrooms (optional)
1   Package       Italian Dressing Mix
3/4 Cup           Sherry
To Taste          Salt
To Taste          Black Pepper



This recipe was given to me by two good friends from a chat room at Chatropolis, Kaye and Charlie. This is an excellent recipe, and for those of you worried about the Sherry and the alcohol content, all the alcohol will evaporate as the Chicken Breasts are being cooked, so this is safe for children and 'Tea Totters' as well.


Directions:

Brown the Chicken Breasts in the butter in a large skillet. Add the Italian Dressing Mix and cook until the chicken breasts are tender, turning several times.

Put the cooked Chicken Breasts in a baking dish and add the Cream Cheese & Chives, Sherry and the Mushrooms. Bake at 350 degrees for one hour.
