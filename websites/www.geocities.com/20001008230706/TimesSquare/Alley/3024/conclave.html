ABOUT THIS SITE

Since Norrath, the world of the game of EverQuest, is an evolving world, this site will be updated as more information is released and changes implemented by Verant Interactive, the developer of EverQuest. Although some general guidelines are provided, my intention is to focus this site on the pure spellcasting classes in general, and wizard characters in particular. Please bear in mind that many of the views expressed herein represent my opinions based on experiences in Norrath, and my evaluation of information gathered from other sources. The purpose is to orient the visitor who is considering establishing residence as a character in Norrath. Your own opinions may differ from mine and I welcome discussion. Please feel free to express yourself by visiting the Message Board.

May Solusek Ro and Tunare bless you throughout your travels in Norrath:)
