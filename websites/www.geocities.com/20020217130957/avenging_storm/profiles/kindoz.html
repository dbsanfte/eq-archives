BIOGRAPHY

Kindoz. Many wonder about his childhood; few know. He knows nothing more of his father than that he was an elven bard of the name Albanaral Nightlark. He fears what he might do if he were to learn more. His first actual memory was of being lost in the Western Plains of Karana. He was only three years old at the time and was attacked and badly beaten. Before his life slipped away, he felt the healing touch of a woman paladin who Laid Hands upon him. The woman decimated his attacker and brought him with her to the Temple of Thunder in Qeynos.

The paladin took Kindoz under her wing, teaching him privately. This special attention garnered the resentment and cajolerie of his peers who mocked him for being trained by a woman. When the time for final trials came, however, Kindoz bested all his opponents. By this time, young Kindoz had become quite self reliant, thinking himself impervious to the slings of jealousy. Then one day the female paladin didn't come home.

Distraught at the absence of his mentor and friend, Kindoz left Qeynos to adventure through Norrath. Over those first few years, he made several friends and came to form the Avenging Storm, a guild dedicated to preserving and promoting the good of Norrath. He met and fell in love with Shara, a half elf paladin. Their love was not in the stars, however, and Karana turned his face away from Shara, resulting in her death and two episodes of rebirth. Lost to the world, Kindoz looked for a shoulder to cry on but found much more in Taliana. Kindoz found room in his heart to love the half elf ranger, and they were married shortly after. The two adopted Carthiny Hopebringer and had two children of their own, Meggan and Catrina.
