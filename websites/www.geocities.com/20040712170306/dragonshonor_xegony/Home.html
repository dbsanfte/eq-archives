Dragons Honor Charter
Dragons Honor members always seek to lead by example. Demonstrate generosity and kindness to his fellow guild members and as well as others. Dishonorable conduct that will reflect back on the guild is not tolerated. Intentional training, Kill Stealing, and harassment of other players will lead to disciplinary action against the offending guild member. Sexual harassment will lead to immediate removal from the guild, period.

Dragons Honor strives to help the guild and others to conquer the evils that plague all of Norrath. With dedication, loyalty, valor and most of all Honor and Pride.

When Dragons Honor guild members encounter rude and intimidating behavior. They should try to correct the situation if possible. Always feel free to contact an officer for help if needed.

While many guilds aspire to the conquest of evil, the Dragons Honor guild is devoted above ALL else to developing and encouraging good play and honorable
conduct in its members. Just because others fail to live up to the call of being a hero, is no reason for us not to.
