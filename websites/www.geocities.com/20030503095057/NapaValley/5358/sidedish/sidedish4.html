Hickory Style Baked Beans


2     Cans    Pork and Beans(Medium size cans)
1/2   Lbs     Cooked and Cubed Ham
1     Medium  Chopped Yellow Onion
1/2   Cup     Brown Sugar
9     Ounces  New Orleans Style Barbecue Sauce
To Taste      Salt
To Taste      Black Pepper
To Taste      Chili Powder



This recipe can be cooked either in the oven or on a barbecue grill. Since I can only cook hamburgers and hot dogs on the grill, with any sucess, I cook mine in the oven.


Directions:


In a large mixing bowl, combine the Pork and Beans, Cubed Cooked Ham, Chopped Onions, New Orleans Style Barbecue Sauce, Salt, Black Pepper and the Chili Powder. Pour this mixture into a baking dish and sprinkle the top with the Brown Sugar. The sugar will melt into the dish so it is not necessary to sir the beans at this time.

You can also place stips of bacon on the top of the beans if you deisre. Place the baking dish in a 350 degree oven, uncovered, and bake for 1 hour, or until the beans are at the desired thickness.

This is one of those Southern dishes where your tongue will "slap" your eyes for looking at the beans to long and not letting it have a taste.
