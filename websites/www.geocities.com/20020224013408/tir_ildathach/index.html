As a child I looked across the many-colored land. Finding a color I liked, I plucked it and kept it for my own, but soon that color faded. As a growing druid I found some colors much to my distaste, and so removed them from the 'scape. But as Hierophant, I now see all colors as one....

Go forth, child, for I now pass the mantle to you. Invoke a gathering in the name of Draiocht, as in the days of old:  one Circle to guide the heart, one Circle to guide the hand, and one Circle to tell the tale.

--  the Hierophant Rowan, spoken from her deathbed
