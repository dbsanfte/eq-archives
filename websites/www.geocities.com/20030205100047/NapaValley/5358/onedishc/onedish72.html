Easy Sloppy Joe Bake


1           Lb.      Lean Ground Beef
1           Medium   Onion, chopped (1/2 cup)
1-15.5 oz   Can      Sloppy Joe Sauce
2           Cups     Shredded Cheddar Cheese, divided
1           Cup      Bisquick® Original Baking Mix
1/2         Cup      Milk
1           Large    Egg


My stepdaughter got this recipe from the inside of a Bisquick® box and I'll bet it was interesting to watch her do it as the box was still full.



Directions:

In a large skillet, cook the Ground Beef and Onion until the meat is browned. Drain off the excess grease. Stir in the Sloppy Joe Sauce and mix thoroughly. Stir in one cup of the Cheddar Cheese and when it is well incorporated, transfer the mixture to a greased 2-quart baking dish. Sprinkle the remaining cup of Cheddar Cheese on top of the meat mixture.

In a bowl, combine the Bisquick®, Milk and Egg and stir until well blended. Pour this mixture over the top of the meat mixture and bake in a pre-heated 400-degree oven for 30 minutes.
