Beth's Macaroni & Cheese


2     Boxes	Kraft® Macaroni & Cheese Dinner
1     Pound	Lean Ground Meat
1/2   Cup	Diced Yellow Onion
1/2   Cup	Diced Green Pepper
1     Can	Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes



"Out of the Mouth of Babies"…..This is a recipe that my daughter just dreamed up. Maybe some of the "Ole Wizard's Magic" is rubbing off on my Daughter…..LOL!!!!!!



Directions:

Cook the Kraft's® Macaroni & Cheese according to the package instructions.

Brown the Ground Meat. Add the Onions and Green Pepper. When the vegetables are cooked, drain the Hunt's® Diced Tomatoes and add them to the meat mixture. Remove this mixture from the heat.

Once the Kraft® Macaroni & Cheese Dinner is cooked, add the meat mixture stirring to completely incorporate it.
