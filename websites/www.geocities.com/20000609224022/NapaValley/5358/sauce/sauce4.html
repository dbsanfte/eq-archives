Cheesy Bread



1	Loaf	Italian Bread
2	Cups	Kraft® shredded Cheddar Cheese	
		Parkay® Soft Spread Butter
		Hellmann's® Real Mayonnaise



This is my Aunt Janet's recipe, which I learned from her without her knowing it. Sure glad she can't use a computer.



Directions:

Cut the Italian Bread into 1/2 inch slices and place them on a cookie sheet. Spread the Parkay® Butter on the bread slices and then the Hellmann's® Real Mayonnaise. Cover the bread slices with the Kraft® shredded Cheddar Cheese.


Place the bread into a pre-heated 350 degree oven until the cheese has melted and the edges of the bread have turned a light brown.
