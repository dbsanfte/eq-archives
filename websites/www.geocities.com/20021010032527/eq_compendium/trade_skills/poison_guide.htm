Poison Making

General:

          Poison making is the trade skill used by thieves to make various poisons from vendor bought and found items.  To make poisons, the poison ingredients, suspension, and a vial must be placed into a mortar/pestle, and then combine.  If you have any other information on Poison making, please contact me.

 

Things to Note:

·        Poison making is a Thief only skill

·        The Apply Poison skill is accessible at level 18

·        Pottery is an excellent complimentary skill, as you need pottery to make the vials

·        Failure to make a poison will return the vial, if the recipe is correct

·        Poisons are treated like spell: they can only be used once, they will fail if you are moving, and they can be resisted

·        When you apply a poison, your character will immediately sit

·        Only piercing weapons can be poisoned

·        Poisons, when applied, will be added to the weapon in your 1st melee weapon spot, no matter what you try to apply it to

·        If you zone, weapons loose their applied poison

·        Poisons are stronger when made by higher level thieves

·        Each poison vial has one chance for application and for failure

·        A poison goes off, whether successful or not, the first time you stab a mob with the newly poisoned weapon

·        When applied, the weapons take on a charge (again like a spell), and many charges (applications) can be added to the weapon

·        Poisoned weapons are not transferable
