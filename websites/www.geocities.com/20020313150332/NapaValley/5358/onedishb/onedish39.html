Tuna Casserole with Celery Soup


1-7 oz   Can       Star Kist® Tuna, drained
1        Can       Campbell's® Cream of 
                   Celery Soup
1/4      Can       Milk
1 1/2    Cups      Cooked, Frozen Peas
1 1/2    Cups      Grated Cheddar Cheese
1        Package   Pillsbury® Crescent Rolls


Steve's Louisiana Cajun Cookbook strikes again. The original recipe calls for one package of refrigerator biscuits but I substituted the crescent rolls for them. This is a quick and easy recipe to prepare when time is of the essences.



Directions:

Put the Tuna in a casserole dish and then pour the Cream of Celery Soup on top of it. Using the empty soup can, measure 1/4 can of Milk; stirring the milk around to get all the soup out of the can. Pour then milk in the casserole dish. Next add the Peas and half a cup of the grated Cheddar Cheese. Stir this mixture thoroughly.

Spread the Crescent Rolls out and then place them on top of the mixture. Sprinkle the top of the crescent rolls with the remaining Cheddar Cheese and bake in a pre-heated 450-degree oven for approximately 15 minutes or until the crescent rolls are done.
