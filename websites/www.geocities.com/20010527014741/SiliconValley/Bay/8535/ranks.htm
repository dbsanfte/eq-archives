The Ranking System
 
 
Guildmaster
 The Guildmaster is in charge of everything that has to do with the guild
Regent
 The Regent is 2nd in command of the guild. He/she is in charge of everything in the leader's absence
Chancellor
 High rank just below Regent, 3rd in command of the guild.
Captain
The Captain is in charge of a squad. A squad isn't formed until 40 members join The Shadow Knights
High Knight
The High Knight of The Shadow Knight is 2nd in command of a squad
Knight
The Knights are members who are in the guild for about 3 months.
Low Knight
The Low Knights is a full member of The Shadow Knights
Initiate
The Initiate is not a full member of The Shadow Knights, in order to become a full member, the Initiate must prove him/her self that is able to become a full member (Note: Before the beta testing begins, an initiate will be upgraded to Knight within 3 weeks of joining)
