-=oO Cryptic Descension Oo=-

The fall of the Great Iksar Empire was a devastating blow for the mighty
lizardmen of Kunark. Defeated by those they enslaved, the Iksar was a race like no other. Brutal and deadly, the Iksar rose from the ashes of war to conquer all, not resting, nor showing mercy in there tyranny. They were once the greatest of all beings. Men feared them, gods respected them, all would fall to there knees and beg for there forgiveness. But, that one faithful battle, the Ring of Scale, along with there petty allies rampaged through Cabilis like a flood of flesh and steel. Destroying the great walled city and freeing thousands of slaves from the bloody grasp of the Iksar Empire. These events triggered a resistance...a new beginning. A cryptic rebirth of the Iksar.

First, a mere reflex of revenge by a lone soul, only known as Rikuun Kor'baak. But soon, it would become so much more. He dreamed of bringing back to the Iksar Empire back to its former glory. Believing that if he, along with his minions, enslaved the world, not just Kunark...but Norrath. Through enslaving Norrath the lesser gods would pay heed to him, knowing he now controls there creations. Through this control the dragonkind would fall to there knees before the Iksar, knowing not only that every race is against them, but every god is too. He begun to get far to deep into his thoughts, beginning to transform into something unworldly. Some believe he was posessesed, some believe he is insane. But only he knows the truth.

He now travels Kunark, searching for new souls to fall within the ranks of his world, a descension of apocalyptic portions has begun...and all you can do is watch...and bleed. Scream, and pray as the beautiful irony begins.
