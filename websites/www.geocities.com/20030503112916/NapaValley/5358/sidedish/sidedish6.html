Green Bean Casserole



2-16oz       Cans       Cut Green Beans
3/4          Cup        Milk
1-10 3/4oz   Can        Campbell's® Condensed 
                        Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/8          Teaspoon   Black Pepper
1-2.8oz      Can        French's® French Fried 
                        Onions




This recipe came from a Manufacturers Coupon worth 15 cents off the purchase of Durkee® French Fried Onions. I never did use the coupon because I want the recipe. The experation date of the coupon was "8/31/91"....guess its too late now!!!


Directions:


In a medium bowl, combined the Green Beans, Milk, Cream of Mushroom Soup, Black Pepper and "1/2" of the can of Durkees® Frech Fried Onions. Pour this mixture into a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish.

Bake, uncovered, in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes or until completely heated through. Top with the remaing French Fried Onions and bake an additional 5 minutes or until the onions are golden brown.
