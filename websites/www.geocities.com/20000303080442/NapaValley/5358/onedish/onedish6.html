Macaroni & Cheese


8     Ounces     Long Horn Cheese, Grated
8     Ounces     Swiss Cheese, Grated
8     Ounces     Sharp Cheese, Grated
8     Ounces     American Cheese, Grated
8     Ounces     Brick Cheese, Grated
2     Cups       Macaroni

      White Sauce

2     Teaspoons  Butter
2-3   Teaspoons  Flour
2     Cups       Milk
To Taste         Salt



My Dad worked in an Isaly's store in Pittsburgh, Pa. while going to Avalon High School. This was one of the items on their luncheon bar. He could never get the cooks to tell him the exact recipe but between him and my Mom, they got pretty close. This is the results of their trying and trying to get it right. By the way, serve this with some toasted bread, and you have a meal. (Note: Grate your soft cheese first and this will help keep your grater clean.)



Directions:

The White Sauce must be made first and it is done as follows: Melt the Butter in a small pan. Slowly add the flour stirring constantly. Slowly add the Milk, again stirring constantly. Bring to a boil and then remove it from the heat source.

Cook the Macaroni according to the package instructions. Drain the macaroni and put it in a large baking dish. Pour the white sauce over the macroni and stir completely.

In a large bowl, combine the Grated Cheeses and mix thoroughly. Pour this mixture over the macaroni and bake for 45 minutes in a 350 degree oven, stirring occasionally.
