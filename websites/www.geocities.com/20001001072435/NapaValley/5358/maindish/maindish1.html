Meatloaf Topped with Cheddar Cheese


2      Pounds    Ground Beef
2      Cups      Plain Bread Crumbs
1 1/2  Cups      Shredded Cheddar Cheese
3/4    Cup       Barbecue Sauce (Kraft® 
                 Regular)
1/2    Cup       Diced Yellow Onions
1/2    Cup       Chopped Celery
1      Teaspoon  Salt
1/4    Teaspoon  Pepper
1      Large     Egg



During the Christmas Holidays, several years ago, the Kraft company advertised, both on Television and in the TV Guide®, various recipes that they had developed (obviously using their products). This is one of those recipes that I prepare for the family. It is one of the few that I have not changed in one form or fashion.



Directions:

In a large mixing bowl, combine the Ground Meat, Bread Crumbs, Yellow Onions, Celery, Egg, Salt, Pepper, ONE cup of Shredded Cheddar Cheese, and 1/3 Cup of the Barbecue Sauce. Mix these ingredients well and form into a loaf.

Place the loaf on a cookie cooling rack and put the rack in a shallow baking dish.

Bake the Meatloaf at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Remove the meatloaf from the oven and brush it with the remaining Barbecue Sauce and top it with the remaining Shredded Cheddar Cheese. Return the meatloaf to the oven and cook it another 15 minutes. Remove the meatloaf from the oven and let it sit for 5-10 minutes before cutting it. (Makes great left over meatloaf sandwiches)
