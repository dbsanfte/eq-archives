General Instructions:

  1. This will be a single elimination tourney.
  2. Level divisions are as follows:
      • Levels 50-100
      • Levels 101-200
      • Levels 201-300
      • Levels 300-up
  3. The entry fee is 2500 gold.
  4. The competition will be progressive. In other words, the more you win, the more you fight. Winner of each battle will move up to the next battle until there is an overall winner in that Level and class division.
  5. The loser of each battle will receive 500 of his gold back and be entered into the drawing which will take place at the end of the tournament. (You need not be present to win the drawing. Winner will be notified via Magic Mail).
  6. There will be a Referee in the cloud with the combatants, therefore the following spells will be illegal:
      • ALL Mass Spells will be illegal
      • The Beserk Spell will be illegal
      • "Beserking" the Referee will not be allowed
      • All "Item Destruction" spells are illegal
      • Killing or Hitting any Referee will be an automatic loss
      • Only a maximum of 3 heals per combatant
  7. The Referee will have PVP "on" and will join with one of the combatants. Then the Referee will "jump" the opponent. This eliminates the PVP being left on for all participants.
  8. The overall winner in a Division will get half of the gold collected in that division. The other half of the gold will go to the M&M Guild to replenish their treasury.
  9. All Fights will be to the "death" or until one participant yields.
