Kids Spaghetti


1	Lb.	Lean Ground Meat
1/2	Cup	Diced Yellow Onion
2	Cans	Franco-American® Spaghetti



This is a recipe that my mother concocted when I was a young teenager. This was her answer to "home made" spaghetti. I loved it then and still love it now and so do my kids. My brother on the other hand won't even talk about. He hates it with a passion. Note: Only Franco-American® spaghetti will make this recipe right for my taste buds



Directions:

Brown the Ground Meat and Diced Yellow Onion in a very large skillet. Drain off all the excess grease. Add the 2 cans of Franco-American® spaghetti to the skillet, reduce the heat to simmer and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes, stirring frequently.
