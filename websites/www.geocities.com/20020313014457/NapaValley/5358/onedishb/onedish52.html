Italian Chicken and Rice


1           Lb.    Boneless/Skinless Chicken Breasts 
                   cut into strips
1/2         Cup    Chopped Onion
1           Cup    Chicken Broth
1-14.5 oz   Can    Del Monte® Diced Tomatoes with Basil,
                   Garlic and Oregano
1 1/2       Cups   Uncooked Rice
1-14.5 oz   Can    Del Monte® French Style Green Beans, drained
To Taste           Grated Parmesan Cheese


Let's hear it for the coupon section of the Sunday newspaper. This recipe was part of that section and of course it was for Del Monte® products. This recipe is a great last minute type of meal, very simple and quick.



Directions:

Coat a large skillet with nonstick cooking spray. Over medium-high heat, cook the Chicken and Onion for approximately 3 minutes or until the chicken is no longer pink. This mixture will need to be stirred frequently.

Carefully stir in the Chicken Broth and the undrained Diced Tomatoes. Bring to a boil and then stir in the uncooked Rice and the Green Beans. Reduce the heat and simmer, covered, for 5 minutes.

Remove the skillet from the heat keep it covered and let it stand for 3 minutes. Serve with grated Parmesan Cheese if desired.
