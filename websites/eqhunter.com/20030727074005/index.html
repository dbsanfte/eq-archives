Welcome

Hello fellow Rangers, Warders and Forest Stalkers.   I see you have found a hobby of mine, this website.    

With your help, this site will become the ranger training manual for the high end ranger levels 60 through 65.   One would hope that by this level a player would know everything.  No one knows everything.  If you know 80% of everything, and know where to start looking to find the last 20% then your in good shape.  This will be the place for the last 20%

If you want rangerly chit chat and friendly (and sometimes not so friendly) discussions, or want to tell the world you just dinged, then go to The Ranger's Glade.  You won't find that here.

Access to this site is free, but not available to everyone.  Your application for access will be to author and submit material to compliment our online content.  We are about exchanging information, not leaching it.  Once your submission is received the current members will evaluate it based upon accuracy and usefulness to the level 60+ crowd.
