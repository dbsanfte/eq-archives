Finally, the gnome just flat out refused our aid and became resigned to his fate.

Jelvan was pretty much the same deal. We knew he was being tormented, but we just couldn't seem to quite succeed in setting him free. However, what he failed to understand was how stubborn we can be when we set our minds to something. .....






Once again, we passed the challenge and stood victorious! Great work, folks!!
