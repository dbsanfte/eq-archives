So! We decided that instead of getting owned by Rikkunduin the Hoser Dragon of Doom, we would take a good gander at Txevu and see what kind of horrors it had in store for us. We had not really heard anything on the positive side about the zone, and were a little apprehensive at first.

But, as past experiences show, when the going gets tough, the tough get going!

THE RUNT!!

Phew! He may be a Runt, but he is certainly no pushover.

GO GO GOOD TEAM!

/sigh

People were just not paying attention though, after our victory. We also had the TB mafia busy handing out dye and pressuring people to color themselves orange and black for halloween "or else" they "might have to log permanently" if you "get my drift"

So... we ended up getting split up and lost and wandering around in some tunnels until we ran into a High Priest or something

/wink




Was a very good night for us=) Keep up the good work, folks!
