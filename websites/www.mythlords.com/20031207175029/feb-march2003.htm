03/26/03 - Membership Applications are closed

Myth Lords rank’s are filled with intelligent and friendly folks who are anxious to play together and always willing to help one another.  I am proud to wear a Myth Lords tag.

Over the past month, we have bolstered out ranks with a few more friendly faces.  We have a great thing going here, and we don’t want to spoil it by outgrowing ourselves.  We have decided to close the door on taking any new applications for the time being.  We have solid core of people in Myth Lords, and we wish continue having a strong core. 

We will maintain our current wave of recruits, inviting them to group with us and completing their application process.  

Myth Lords will start accepting new members again soon; however, we are determined to remain a small guild of friends.  As such, our ‘recruit’ process will change.  Basically, if someone is spending a lot of time with us, and they seem to be a good fit for us, we will ask them to apply for membership.

We realize that this change may cause us to miss out on some good people.  It’s a price we are willing to pay to preserve integrity of Myth Lords.  If you have a friend that wants to become a Myth Lord, have them group with us regularly -- experience groups and raid groups.  If somebody sticks around long enough, they will be asked to apply for membership.

- Baat

03/03/2003 Meet Grieg Veneficus

First, some history about our good buddy, Grieg:
So one day a few weeks ago we are all bored and need something to do.  Most of us had spent little or no time in Grieg's End, so we decided to poke around a bit.  We notice that GV himself is is up, so we decide to introduce ourselves.  We sent a warrior in to say, 'Hi'.  Well, the warrior dies, camp is trained, and we buy coffins.  So now we are little upset with this Grieg fellow.

A couple weeks pass and we decide to go see our new friend Grieg again.  We head down, half the people go in the portal, and  half stay out (don't ask!) and die, then camp gets trained and we buy more coffins.  I'm sure Grieg missed our companionship that that day, but I swear I heard him snickering!

As days pass, we hear Grieg him mocking us from his twisted little home several zones away!  So we gather 3 or 4 groups together and head out to punish him for his insolence!  Grieg got a good laugh out of that one.  We fought for about 10 minutes only got him to 73%, then bought yet more coffins!

Now we are just fed up with this jerk!  A bunch of us put our heads together and decided that this foul tempered mage pet had to DIE!  6 days later, about 4 groups of peaceful, fun-loving Myth Lords went to visit El Señor Grieg.  Later, while running through Nexus, we saw this:

[Sat Mar 01 11:12:25 2003] Grieg tells OOC, 'paying well for a necro or SK with summon corpse spell! PLEASE!!!!!'

Ahhh Justice!  I am SOOOOOO Proud of everybody in Myth Lords!  We all worked together to develop a plan of attack, and more importantly, EVERY single person there did a fantastic job preparing and executing the attack!  This is a huge accomplishment for us!  Excellent work gang!

Grats to Quinin on Etched Bone Ulak x2 (too bad they are lore!) Grats to Snuzle on Moonfire and Mask of the Stalker.  Grats to Ahaboo on a VT shard.

Next, we went to see the Clockwork Dragon.  That's a fun encounter!  He oiled us down (our rogues found this strangely exciting /boggle), steam cooked us, then shocked the holy #$%^ out of us!  He will die next run!
