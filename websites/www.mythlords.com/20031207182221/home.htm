Welcome to the Myth Lords website.  We are an Everquest guild on the Zebuxoruk server.

About us

We are a small guild of tightly knit adventurers on the Zebuxoruk server. We are a casual guild that raids two to three times a week.  We believe in skills and tactics over numbers, in other words, we like to do things with a small force. Our typical raid size is 3-5 groups.  We enjoy challenges and we have a blast playing together!
