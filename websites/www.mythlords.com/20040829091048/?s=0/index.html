Inferno!
8/24 - 2004 . by Baat Faastard
These last 2 weeks have been phenomenal for us! We've been enjoying ourselves as we learned a bunch of new encounters and we have been laughing at ourselves as we wipe out to trivial mobs! Heck, we even managed to wipe to the Emp in Ssra! hahaha! DOH!!

Highlights of the past 2 weeks include clearing VT in record time; visiting our old friend, Zun'Muram Votal - the 2 headed freak; spending quality time in the elementals; and killing two false gods, Solusek Ro and Mithanial Marr (they are Goa'uld I tell you!)

We had some great successes (and failures) in Plane of Fire recently! Our last two visits there started with wipes to General Druav Flamesinger....you know, the easiest boss in pof!? Anyway, we ended both nights with kills of General Reparm and Blazzax the Omnifiend!!!! WOOOT!! It has become apparent that we are guaranteed to wipe at least once a day, so we might as well wipe first to easy stuff and then get on with raiding hard stuff! :P

We had seriously expected to wipe on both Reparm and Blazzax, I can't tell you how excited we were to beat both events on the first try, and in the same night! AWESOME WORK MYTH LORDS!!!!!!

FOUR guild firsts this week!!!!
General Reparm
Blazzax the Omnifiend
Quavonis Firetail
Magmaton
