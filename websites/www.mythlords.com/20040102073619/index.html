12/30/03 - Ups and downs and back up again!

After being sooo excited about our first Emperor kill two weeks ago, we couldn't wait to go back and prove that we could do it alone.  So we went back and wiped horribly.  A few days later on patch day, the servers were being funny, we assembled to take on the emperor, and Ssra went down, not once, but twice.  So we logged back in a waited a few more hours :(  Once Blood popped, we engaged, and promptly wiped to the emperor again.  At least the second wipe was to a bugged emperor, the first wipe we just sucked.

A week later, we gathered just about our entire raid force, and defeated the emperor, with nothing but Myth Lords this time.  We had a great turnout, and the emperor died in about 13 minutes.  Yay!  We felt better about ourselves again =)

Last night we went to ssra for the cursed cycle, we killed the 10 triggers, and boom, no glyphed!  While we checked logs making sure we actually killed all 10 (we did kill all 10 btw) we noticed that Blood had spawned.  We decided to just kill Blood and call it a night.  On the way to blood, the dang emperor spawned.  But we only had 30 people....  we figured what the heck, we can summon our bodies out afterwards!

49 minutes after Blood died, the emperor was dead at our feet!!!  It was a huge exciting fight!  His health went down soooo slowly! hehe.   1 year ago today, we had decided that if we wanted to get anywhere, we would do a lot with a  little.  We then killed Vindi with 3 1/2 groups (thats good for guild that needed his loot still).  Last night we killed emperor with 30 people that have never set foot in VT (or anything higher than tier 3 pop).  We have stuck to basics, we did a lot with a little :)  


What a rush!!!  Awesome work Myth Lords!!!!!  =)  =)  =)  =)

The night that we wiped for no good reason, we also managed to agro the Emp at the zone line.  It's really fun to zone in to ssra naked and take a 4k HT followed by angry emperor melee.  We kited him away and figured out how far we could go to not die (which wasn't far at all).  While we summoned corpses, a GM showed up to provide entertainment for us.  He got himself killed over and over by the emperor =p  Good times =)

12/10/03 - A long, fun and very unexpected road! (edit 12/11/03 - loot list added)

At the end of December last year, Myth Lords began exploring the raiding aspect of Everquest.  We never expected to get very far, but we did have one clear goal - Have Fun!  Our first major kill was on December 30, 2002 - that was the day we killed Vindi.  I think it took us 4 hours or so to sort out the pull and get a clear run at it.  We had 3-4 groups of us there, all Myth Lords and a few friends that eventually became Myth Lords.

We went on to raid small targets, looking for stuff that we could handle with just 3 or so groups.  We killed Gozzrem and Telkorenar a couple of times and we worked on getting everybody epiced.  Our next major kill was Grieg Veneficus in Grieg's end, that was the first day of March 2003. 

At this point we had gotten a bunch of us flagged for bot and had begun learning bot towers.  This was back when bot was a flagged entry only zone and named mobs usually dropped nothing (even the tower bosses).  All of our hard work learning the towers paid off when they became itemized to drop 2 drops everytime.

By April  we were up to 4-5 raiding groups in size (up from 3-4), and good things began to snowball on us.  We discovered that we could kill Xanamech, Yelinak and various other ST key droppers, so we began to acquire sleepers keys.  The big move was when we took 22 people to see what Aary was like, and we killed him!!!! We cleared NTOV shortly after that.

By June, we had grown a little more, we could field nearly 6 groups on a good day!  We began our farming campaigns to get everyone up to speed: 41% haste, FT items, ST keys, Ntov, BoT towers, Dain belts, etc.  At this time we considered forming an alliance with another guild so that we may progress further into the game.  We teamed up with DBN for an Aerin'Dar kill, and spoke with 2 other guilds about possible alliances.  We decided to stay  as we were see where we would end up.

At the end of September, we, as a guild, decided that the Ssra/VT route was the best choice for a guild our size.  So we began the grueling task of farming emperor keys and emperor bane weapons.  Instead of destroying morale and making us all hate each other, the key camps for the most part really brought people together.  I was very impressed with the amount of help and dedication many members put in to help others get keyed!

In Late November we decided it was time and we set a date to camp the emperor.  Server patches kept popping up and delaying our camp.  Patch days are usually bad for us on highly contested targets:  our entire raid force is 50 people, we never have all 50 on, so unless it is scheduled, racing to a spawn like the Emperor just isn't going to go well for us. 

Again a surprise patch foiled our plans to camp the emperor this week.  However, a bunch of Myth Lords got on early enough to see the spawn and the cry went out as we scrambled to call each other and get enough online before another guild could come to roll over us.  A guild did show up, but instead of rolling us, they helped us! (thanks AOH) they gave us a few more people (8) and we gave them some rifts.

So without further ado, here is the headline:

 Myth Lords Killed Emperor Ssraeshza!!!!


The excitement mounts as we stare into the Emperor's chambers!


The money shot!  WE KILLED EMP!!  Grats Myth Lords!!!

I had to log right when he died, so I will post loot as soon as I find out who got what.

We also killed AoW for the first time this week, this was our second shot at him, the first shot was the day Ldon came out, and we blew it that day!  We were pretty dang thrilled about getting this kill, but it seems unimportant today! lol
