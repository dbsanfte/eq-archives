You *MUST* Sign Up To Get Points
Wednesday, February 11, 2004 @ 5:42pm - Posted by Aliseisu
If you attend a raid and are not in the database, it will be impossible for the officers to assign you points after the raid. You absolutely MUST have an account and your character(s) in the Hall of Heroes to obtain the points. If you use a character during a raid that is not in the Hall of Heroes, there is NO WAY that we can assign points to that character. So do yourself and the guild a favor and get your guild account so you can be authorized to add your characters. Once we activate your account, you will be able to add all your characters to the Hall of Heroes. Thistlenaught has been policing this in-game tonight and I'm sure he and the rest will be watching for those that are not in the list of characters. It takes a few minutes on your part to sign up, then a few minutes for us to activate your ability to add your characters. After that, you can add your characters. See you at the raids!

Aliseisu Dryaden
CotGD Webmaster/Guild Leader
