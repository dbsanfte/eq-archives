The Hand of Maestro dead!
Sunday, March 28, 2004 @ 11:47am - Posted by Aliseisu


The raid on plane of hate went as scheduled and a warrior walked away one step closer to his completed epic. Tankarektus won the roll for the drop. The Hand of Maestro was taken down with ZERO casualties. Great Job!! The healers did an awesome job keeping the main tank alive as well. You can see more and more how far we have come in a short amount of time. Lets keep working at improving our raid skills, and most of all, listening to the directions and keeping ourselves focused an on task.
Many epic mobs have been dropped in the past month, some have completed their epics, and we are still moving forward. There's lots more to come. We have clerics that need to get their click sticks and Ragefire in Skyfire is just another steping stone that needs to be kicked from our path to get this done. Lets keep the raids rolling and the drops will follow. We are gearing up for bigger and better things.
A note to all members about our "tough-love" policy during raiding... We may not seem as friendly during the raiding process, but that's not cause we don't care. Understand that we NEED everyone to follow directions and KNOW the zones we are raiding in if things are to go smoothly. Only attack when you are given the command to do so, listen to your group leaders and raid leader, and things will go smoothly. Then we won't have to use the "tough-love" on you. CotGD is a family guild that has turned raid guild. And as such, during raids those in command will expect nothing but competance in your class, ability, and obediance. Enough said. We Rock!

Aliseisu Dryaden
CotGD Webmaster/Guild Leader
