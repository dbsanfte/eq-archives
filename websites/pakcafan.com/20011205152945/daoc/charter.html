Ranking Structure


Rank 0 - Den Alpha
This is me, the Guildleader. Nothing about this can be changed short of the title. This rank has too much in game functionality for the purposes here. My role outside of game functionality is simple - to promote the growth and success of the guild as a whole. By means of direct in game leadership, or by taking a more reserved, advisory role if the situation warrants it. Ultimately I am responsible for every action done by a guild member who wears the Pak tag. In light of that, know that I put honor and truth over most things. Do not dishonor the Pak name.

Rank 1 - Inner Council
These are my trusted, devoted, and proven Officers from our EQ branch. They are my council for decisions that span any games we might be involved in, as well as themselves having specific duties. The members of this rank are: Derringer/Arlen, Redleaf/Waran, Ainin, and Khirgoth/Perigoth. Their duties encompass: Arlen is the Master of the Hunt, and will work very closely with myself on Battlefield tactics and will lead the forces into RvR conflict on most occasions he is present (in tandem or without the Den Alpha). Waran is our message board admin, and Kennel Master. The Kennel Master is the person responsible for keeping track of cubship times (1month) as well as promoting/suggesting promotion out of Cub status. This will remain in DaoC. Ainin is the second in command, and currently organizes DaoC recruitment. Finally, Perigoth is the Keeper of the Scrolls - when anyone is in need of statistical or listing information the burden falls on his shoulders.
These members all have the ablity to invite/remove, promote/demote, access to ospeak/ohear, aspeak/ahear (alliance)

Rank 2 - Officers
These are members that go above and beyond the call of duty. They have shown themselves to hold the ideals of Pak'Cafan in high regard and practice these ideals without making a concious effort. This is the highest rank a member can aspire to.
These members can invite/remove, and promote. They have access to ospeak/ohear and aspeak/ahear.

Rank 3 - Cwn Annwn
This is a DaoC specific rank that I have reserved specifically for the use of the Master of the Hunt (Arlen). Rules of Conduct

Pak'Cafan holds Honor, Family, and Fun as it's founding virtues. These virtues are a marker for the way we conduct ourselves in our dealings with our fellow guildmembers, non-guildmembers and even realm-enemies (in DAoC). In most situations applying these virtues and asking yourself if your conduct fits within them is enough. Sometimes a person can forget themselves in the heat of the moment and be blind to what is right or wrong.

The following is a list of do's and don't based on the above virtues. Those who trespass against this list can be disciplined in the following. (this is not a complete listing, but a short guideline)

Cubs: If the trespass is grevious enough an officer may disband a cub with no warning and without consulting the other officers. A cub is basically on probation till they prove themselves to be Pak material*

Pak Hunter or above: Can be put on probation, demoted or both. If the issue is grevious, the officers will decide together what the repurcussions will be up to and including removal from the guild. An officer may not remove a Pak Hunter of above without said conference of officers.

Message Board Do's and Dont's

Do:
1. Offer your opinion and your support for things you feel positive about.
2. Offer *constructive criticism* for things you do not feel positive about. This should make the person you are disagreeing with:
a. See that you understand their point of view.
b. State your point of view
c. Post a solution that compromises both point of views or just say 'I am not sure where the middle ground is.'

Do NOT:
1. Call guildmembers or anyone else derogatory names on the boards. (Now this may be done in jest but you had best be sure it will be taken as a jest or followed by an apology)
2. Post messages that are derogatory or that inflame an issue. This does not mean we do not want you opinion. If you want to post a dissenting view on something see the Do list.
3. Post messages requesting Power Leveling. If someone offers to PL you great. Do Not Ask.
4. Post messages asking for items. Posting for something to be crafted and offering to pay for it is ok. If the crafter chooses not to be paid even better. But expect to pay for at least the materials. (A little more does not hurt)

Game Do's and Dont's

Do:
1. Try to help or advise those who ask for assistance whether guildmembers or not. Go out of your way for Guildmembers.
2. If you have room in your group look for Pak members to fill it. If a non pak person is asking to group and you have room please accomodate them unless they prove themselves not to be Pak Material*.

Do NOT: 1. Spam requests for assistance in guild channel. Make a request and wait patiently. We all have things we want to do too.
2. Train mobs on someone with the intent to kill them. Trains happen, do your best to announce it then run like hell. Doing this intentionally is a serious offense. If someone is doing it to you talk to an officer and appeal.

Observing the above and conducting ourselves according to Pak virtues will ensure our untarnished reputation is maintained accross all the games we engage in.

The officers of Pak'Cafan

*Pak Material: Does not have anything to with being L33T or Uber. It has to do with honor, courage and family and upholding those virtues. We may be the biggest guild in DAoC but we will not act as if we are.
