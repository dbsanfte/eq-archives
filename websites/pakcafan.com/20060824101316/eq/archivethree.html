May 31st, 2005

WE DID IT!!!

For those of you who didn't think we were progressing fast enough, may I present you with some simple addition...


That's right, we're an ANGUISH guild now! We're sorry we took so long to finish these trials off, we'll try to go faster next time. We'll try, we really will, but we seem to have some pesky lockout timers keeping us from doing some of our later wins again...

All kidding aside, it feels great to have all our hard work finally paying off. Things were looking pretty grim towards the end of last year. With the release of EQ2 and WoW our numbers dwindled, and we were barely able to scrape up the numbers to kill a sand giant. We pushed through all of this though, recruiting like mad, gearing up like mad, and backflagging like mad. Things were really starting to look up. We were progressing again, we'd beaten Inktu, gotten our first King kill, and had our sights set on the Queen, Tacvi, and Anguish.

And then we had the server merge...

And we also had finals, graduations, vacations, etc. It was spring, with summer just around the corner. Our numbers always drop this time of year, we were not to concerned. We did lose a few to the real world, a few due to personal issues, and a few more to the promise of fame and fortune. But again, we were not overly concerned. We buckled down, changed our focus a bit, and went full steam into MPG to start beating these trials down. I don't think any of us truely believed we would get through them so quickly, but after our first wins in our first couple trials, we knew it would not be long at all.

I want to thank each and every member of Pak for the continued support of this guild. Thank you for logging on, thank you for your patience, thank you for playing your chars to the best of your abilities, and mostly, thank you for sticking with the guild we all love when the going got rough.
