  <tr>
    <td width="1010" colspan="2">The ancient race of the Xulous practiced ancestor worship to an extreme, their priests wearing the hollowed out corpses of beloved dead rulers as ceremonial costumes. From the necrotic rituals of these people the Plaguebringer was born. Over time he subverted and dominated their religion, replacing the remembrance of the dead with the worship of death itself.
    <p>Feeding from the Xulous, Bertoxxulous grew stronger. Once his power was sufficient he exterminated the entire Xulousian people and ascended to the Planes of Power with their kings as his undead slaves, locked deep within the burial vaults of Lxanvom. For countless milennia he has held them in thrall and spread foul rot and decay throughout the mortal realms.</p>
    <p>Soon we shall bring this vile reign to an end.</p>
