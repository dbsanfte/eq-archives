    Umbral Plainscomments:5
    Last Sunday Healers United and many friendly guilds sent in 36 of their greatest adventurers into the hostile lands of Umbral Plains. It was a great exploration starting at the zone's enterance and heading north to the north western corner. Many wandering creatures were slain and even a few of the zone's unique mobs.

    Here is a list of drops: Dream Weaver Pantaloons to Ellenroh, Flesh Eater's Eye to Tundariel, Skull Marked Shield to Badilea, Dark Assassin Boots to Sanerin, Gantru Charm to Acquillian, Gantru Charm to Catlin, Unknown Spell to Stonehewer, Unknown Bracer to Zolina, Dream Weaver Web Shield to Kabia.

    It was a great raid and one I will be doing again. Expect us to go a little deeper next time.
