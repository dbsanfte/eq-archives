Another successful Kael bashcomments:3
I just wanted to thank everyone who was able to make yesterday's Kael Arena raid.

We had a LOT of folks show up, six groups in total including a lot of pickups. Even with so many people we were able to move pretty quickly to the Arena area with only a couple of LDs on the way. Once we dug into the Arena area we fought for about four hours, taking out three Protectors of Zek and Kallis Stormweaver, a big level 58 named giant.

Among the drops were two sets of corroded plate leggings (Archivis and Gadal), a set of chain sleeves (Keyo), and two pairs of silk gloves (Kabia and Ililden). I think we saw one more drop of chain but I don't remember what it was. Two rangers got heads for their helmet quests in Plane of Growth. Unfortunatly we saw no eroded leather.

Overall it was a successful raid with no wipeouts and only a couple of deaths. Thanks to everyone who helped out including Coelin with transport and Dahlea with loot.
