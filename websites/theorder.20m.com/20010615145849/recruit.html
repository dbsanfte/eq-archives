Recruitment Policies


Section 5 Recruitment and Indoctrination
The floodgates are open wide on recruitment by all officers. These are the stipulations and requirements to be inducted within the Order.

1. Be of at least 15th season
2. Applicant shall create a background and submit it and email address to the website.
3. The Dark Lt. of his potential caste shall then forward applicant a 10-question examination on Order History and policies by email.
4. Applicant shall then have an Oral board held by his respective Shadowcommander and 4 other members chosen by him on Guild history and policy in game.
5. The applicant shall then be watched and grouped with to determine suitability within the Order.
6. At the end of the 2-week period, the First and Second Tier Officers shall convene to vote on the applicant's entry. The vote must be majority.
7. After the vote is taken the member shall be inducted if found worthy and will then have 48 hours in which to post their information on guildmagic.
8. The Dark Lt. of new members class will then take down all information and thoughts on new applicant as well as the inducting officer and email them to Dark Consul Rirek immediately after induction so that all members are held accountable.
9. After above steps are completed, the dark Lt. will then choose a member from their caste to teach or aid the new recruit in whatever way they can. A sort of big brother/sister program. This way the new members shall receive adequate attention.

If any of these steps are not witnessed or recorded, the member shall be removed immediately from the order without question. No application from here on out will be approved without the above steps.

Upon receiving confirmation for induction following speech shall be given as the applicant is inducted:
"Do you ____ seek entrance into The Order of the White Hand?
Reply
"Kneel ____" "I, _____, Do grant you acceptance by The Order of the White Hand. I grant you entrance into the dawning of a new era. We accept you with open arms and treacherous hearts. Stand and be recognized dark brother/sister. Stand and be welcomed"
Then accept them into the guild and broadcast their entrances in guild speak.


Alternate Characters within the Order.

As of today's post all members are allowed ONE alternate within the Order. The alternate will only be inducted when the primary has reached the season of 30. All members who have alternates within the Order at this time will be allowed to keep those alternates even if they are above said requirement. If an alternate is to be created, during the application of the alternate, which requires only a background and First Tier Authorization, the background must state that this is an alternate for (primary chars name).

Follow the hand if you wish to continue your application
