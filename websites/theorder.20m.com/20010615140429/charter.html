 The Order of The White Hand

Motto: Through His Divine Darkness all shall be conquered and returned to its rightful rulers.

Charter:

The "Order of The White Hand " was founded to preserve the very essence of Darkness and Evil. The very ideals of the Teir'dal and other evil aligned races. This preservation has come because of the spiraling downfall of the Teir'dal and all things evil into obscurity and commonality. We seek to preserve the very traits of treachery, deceit, hatred, murder, and power through conquest. We feel as if betrayed by our fellow brethren in that role-playing has been abandoned in favor of a system of friendship and game playing like a common chat room mixed with Mario brothers.... kill for the sake of exp. and mix with all others regardless of race or religion. We believe that in order to repopulate Norrath with the traditional role-players of ole' who are at this time disgruntled and disappointed with the style of playing, a group comes together to promote role-playing, intersystem politics, and a sense of uneasiness throughout Norrath.

Behold, a guild of Villains comes into being. This charter is created as a summary for those with black hearts and cruel, cold, malicious intelligence.

We, The Order of The White Hand, do solemnly declare that Treachery and deceit are not dead. We shall sow the world with lies and underhanded dealings. We shall reap of their misfortune as we rob them of their pride and riches when our true nature is realized. We shall promise friendship and allegiance when there is something to gain and as soon as that use is fulfilled and when there is no more use to be made advantage of, we shall drive our daggers into their backs to fulfill our agreement. All in Innoruuks name and will.

We, The Order of The White Hand, do solemnly declare the Hatred has not faded from the world. We shall curse the light with our dying breaths. We shall strive to remove the taint of light from the world and the hearts and souls of all races and peoples... such is the will of Innoruuk. We shall strive to enslave and convert all practitioners of the false faiths of the bringers of light. All shall know the Hatred of Innoruuk and of The Order.

We, The Order of The White Hand, do solemnly declare that greed and conquest are not forgotten. We shall strive to expand our borders and sway others and their kind under our banner as our power grows. In the end all shall be under our shadow and Darkness shall reign supreme. For in the beginning there was darkness... so it shall be in the end and eternal.

Lastly but not least and surely not the end, I Thelaskir Overlord and Master of the Order of the White Hand, I swear by my very undying soul that Evil has not fled. Evil exists surely in the hearts and souls of I and mine and makes the core of what the Order of The White Hand stands for. Evil is the explanation for all that we are and all that we will be. Instead of turning from the Darkness and primal evil as our brethren have, we shall embrace it and wallow in the Darkness and decay of destruction. Let none be safe from the order..... Let
none oppose the Order and remain living. For those who do not serve us in life shall surely serve us in death.

So says The Order of The White Hand. So it is
written... so it shall be done!

Section 1. Rank and Responsibilities

The following is a constructed list of ranks and their responsibilities for each rank and office. I understand that these responsibilities may seem tedious. That is why all offices (tier officers only) are allowed to enlist the aid of another member, who is not of officer rank, to aid in the performing of his duties. First Tier officers are allowed to enlist from officer ranks as an aid in performing tasks befitting the responsibilities of held office.

The collective officers of First, Second, and Third Tier are known as the Citadel of Darkness. Within the guild they are more commonly known as the Conclave of Overlords. All officers without office or title are to be Overlords in training. The reason behind manpower, rank, and training, shall be made clear in my closing statements at the end of this publication. As for the numbers under the chain of command, per rank, these numbers are for a later date. We are still too small and meager in power and numbers. As the guild grows, these numbers shall have a more significant meaning, that is why there is no minimum number and there is a set maximum. All enlisted personnel shall start at the rank of Iblith. You must earn your rank as enlisted. This is to promote competition within the guild. I want only the strongest, most cunning, and intelligent individuals holding leadership within my army. Section 3 Rank Advancement. Enlisted & Officer

Requirements for enlisted rank advancement are as follows:

Trials of Position
Trials of Position shall be held on the first and second Saturday of every other month. Location of trial of position shall be within the Arena. Members shall duel 1 on 1 in rounds of elimination. These are single elimination. Bottom line is, you lose, and you're out of the contest. A list of applicants shall be taken at the first of the previous month. Scheduling battles shall be done by Second tier officers. Guild attendance at said trials is strongly encouraged.

Prerequisites for participation: Applicant must apply for sponsorship from member holding rank of officer. Member must then have earned selection points equal to rank desired. (For selection point system, see figure below) Member must have held previous rank for two months.
Selection point system:
Selection, or rather, Reputation points, is cumulative.
Jabbuk- 6400pts
Sargtlin- 3200pts
Oloth- 800pts.
Kyorlin- 400pts.
Kyorl- 200pts.
Malla- 100pts.

Gaining Reputation points:
2pts. Per level of individual successfully recruited into the Order.
100pts. Per duel fought and won against person of equal level.
10 pts. Per duel fought and won between Order members during role-play.
Points will be awarded during guild quests and events.
Points will be awarded for extremely good role-playing.
Points awarded for significant items donated to guild through role-play or battles fought. Not by buying or twinking.
…and more ways to acquire points at a later date.

Losing Reputation points:
Demerits. Demerits shall be given in failure of assigned duties (discussed under leadership) given for non role-play. There are other ways…. Generally if you fail. Just think of it that way. You fail… you lose.

All Tier officers are able to bestow reputation points. This job is usually left to the Dark. Lt's and their aides however. Acts mandated as being worthy of reputation, not witnessed by members of the Order, must have a log or screen shot to verify performance with.

Section 5 Recruitment and Indoctrination
The floodgates are open wide on recruitment by all officers. These are the stipulations and requirements to be inducted within the Order.
1. Be of at least 15th season
2. Applicant shall create a background and submit it and email address to the website.
3. The Dark Lt. of his potential caste shall then forward applicant a 10-question examination on Order History and policies by email.
4. Applicant shall then have an Oral board held by his respective Shadowcommander and 4 other members chosen by him on Guild history and policy in game.
5. The applicant shall then be watched and grouped with to determine suitability within the Order.
6. At the end of the 2-week period, the First and Second Tier Officers shall convene to vote on the applicant's entry. The vote must be majority.
7. After the vote is taken the member shall be inducted if found worthy and will then have 48 hours in which to post their information on guildmagic.
8. The Dark Lt. of new members class will then take down all information and thoughts on new applicant as well as the inducting officer and email them to Dark Consul Rirek immediately after induction so that all members are held accountable.
9. After above steps are completed, the dark Lt. will then choose a member from their caste to teach or aid the new recruit in whatever way they can. A sort of big brother/sister program. This way the new members shall receive adequate attention.

If any of these steps are not witnessed or recorded, the member shall be removed immediately from the order without question. No application from here on out will be approved without the above steps.
Upon receiving confirmation for induction following speech shall be given as the applicant is inducted:
"Do you ____ seek entrance into The Order of the White Hand?
Reply
"Kneel ____" "I, _____, Do grant you acceptance by The Order of the White Hand. I grant you entrance into the dawning of a new era. We accept you with open arms and treacherous hearts. Stand and be recognized dark brother/sister. Stand and be welcomed"
Then accept them into the guild and broadcast their entrances in guild speak.

Section 7 Things to come and guild events….

The Planning committee:
The planning committee shall have one permanent member as the Officer in Charge.
Six members shall be chosen each week, rotated through the roster weekly, to plan and schedule guild events and raids. Two shall be chosen weekly. One for the lower level members and one for the higher-level members. These events will be held on the following week after they have been assigned. So.. If you are chosen as planning committee on week one, your events and so on will be held on week two. This gives everyone ample opportunity to plan for the event and no one will be left out. As well as choosing events and raids, the planning committee shall choose a "Zone of the Week" in which the weekly mandatory groups shall meet and where the members of the Order will be strongly encouraged to hunt to promote inner guild grouping and so on. The zone of the week will be chosen to held on the following week and posted on the guild Hunting Boards. Everyone, at one time or another will serve on the planning committee… even my self.

Looting Policies:

We follow the need before greed looting order.

Groups made up of Order members:
1. If a class specific items drops it goes to that person in the group.

2. If there are multiple people in the group that can use the item, it is rolled for between those that can use it.

3. If no one in the group can use it - then everyone can roll for it.

4. If the group doesn't have anyone that can use the item but knows of a person in the Order that can, we encourage you to give it to the other person.

Closed Raids (members of the Order only)

Loot Holders and Loot Commander are assigned at the beginning of the raid by the Raid Commander.

1. Loot Holders are responsible for looting all MoB's their group kills there is no splitting of the coin.

2. No drop items are reported to the Loot Commander immediately. The loot commander will have those eligible for the no drop item role. Person winning role will loot item.

3. Loot Commander will divide coin/loot at the end of the raid. Any costs the Guild had in hosting the event will be deducted from the loot prior to it being divided out.

4. Items will go to those that need them. If more than one person needs an item there will be a roll for it.

5. Questable items will be rolled for by those that can use them and those that need them for a quest.

Targetted Raids (open to those outside the Order)

1. Loot will follow the above policy except when targeted loot is dropped. Targetted loot will go to the person who needed it and setup the raid.
