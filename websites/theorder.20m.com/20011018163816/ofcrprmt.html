Officer Advancement

In order to challenge an officer for their position they must be seen as unfit for duty.

First Tier:
A first tier officer must be seen as unfit for duty by no less than 5 first tier, 2 second tier and 1 third tier officer.
Second and Third tier:
Second and Third tier officers must be seen as unfit for duty by all other second and third tier officers.


Registering complaints:
Enlisted ranks may register a complaint against their first tier officer to their appropriate Shadow Commander.
First Rank officers may register a complaint against their Shadow commander with their appropriate Lord.


Complaints:
First Tier: Once a first tier officer receives 5 registered complaints their position is open for Trial.
Second Tier: Once a second tier officer receives 5 registered complaints their position is open for Trial.
Third Tier: Once a third tier officer receives 5 registered complaints their position is open for Trial.


Trials for Position:
First Tier:
Once a first tier officer receives 5 complaints there will be trial held. Trials will be scheduled by the appropriate Shadow Commander. A list of charges will be read. All officers are required to attend. The defendant may make one statement in their behalf. A vote of unfitness is taken. If the officer is found to be unfit for duty by 5 First tier, 2 Second tier and 1 Third tier officer(s) the position is open for applications.

Second and Third Tier:
Once a second/third tier officer receives 5 complaints there will be trial held. Trials will be scheduled by the Overlord. A list of charges will be read. All second/third tier officers are required to attend. The defendant may make one statement in their behalf. A vote of unfitness is taken. If the officer is found to be unfit for duty by all second and third tier officers the position is open for applications.


Application for Position
First Tier:
For enlisted ranks to apply they need to register for trials with their Shadow commander. The Shadow commander will be responsible for scheduling the trials.
Applicants must complete the following with a score of 75 or more to be eligible for the final test.
Dynamic quest given by Shadow Commander score 1 - 10 multiplied by 2.5 = final score Max. pts 25
Current rank 5pts per rank ie Iblith = 5pts, Malla = 10pts. Max. pts. 35
Current skill sets 5pts for each skill that benefits the guild ie handing out tailored armor = 5pts. Max. pts 25
Guild event (possibly the most important part) - applicant must schedule, plan and run a guild event. This will be scored by all officers. 0=Failure, 1=Successful. The officer that is being challenged will not score the event. Max. pts 20

Total points possible 105.


Second/Third Tier:
First/Second Tier officers need to register for trials with the appropriate Lord (third tier applications go to the Overlord). The Lord will be responsible for scheduling the trials. Applicants must complete the following with a score of 85 or more to be eligible for the final test.
Dynamic quest given by a Lord score 1 - 10 multiplied by 2.5 = final score Max. pts 25
History as First Tier officer score of 1 - 10 given by Third tier officers. Max. pts 30
Current skill sets 5pts for each skill that benefits the guild ie handing out tailored armor = 5pts. Max. pts 25
Guild event (possibly the most important part) - applicant must schedule, plan and run a guild event. This will be scored by all officers. 0=Failure, 1=Successful. The officer that is being challenged will not score the event. Max. pts 20.

Total points possible 100


Final Test.
All applicants that are successful through the Application process will duel in a single elimination event. The final one standing will duel the defending officer. The winner will take the rank of the officer.
