Croso's Location Finder

This is the homepage for Croso's Location Finder, a Everquest UI mod that allows you to see the locations of any Zone in the game from any other Zone. Basically it's a connections map with /loc's. I have built upon the work of Athee from eqgui.com. Due to neglect, and the need for PoP data to be added, I'm maintaining my own copy of her wizard, and sharing it with everyone who finds it as useful as I do.
