Don't ya Love Patch days? / Utadin
09/26/02

Well, things have been kind of slow the last while. I guess school and work finally caught up to a bunch of members. Others spent this time getting experience or aquiring the love known as 'Ssrafarmage'. I have taken over the web updates as Kirei needed a break. Anyways....

So we have a patch today, and things are looking pretty slow at first. Mob spawns are later then normal, and the raiding guilds all make their choices of zones to hit. Tamorfin really wanted a new BP, so the call goes out to head to Kael for a quick Vind kill while more people log in. Of course, chat channels are busted, kinda funny how useful these have become and when they are broke we almost panic. So, we kill Vind and Candarye wins the Breastplate, a new blouse as she says. At this time, Statue of Rallos Zek spawned, so...we put him to rest beside Vindi. Sorz gave up a nice cloak to Doljon, someone won the scroll but I was too busy to notice who, and Bettybettybetty ends up with the ST Talisman.

So, we look around and notice Idol is up, and the other big guilds are busy elsewhere. The call was made to finally give this guy a shot and see what he is all about. Just when we start thinking what we need to do, the Idol wanders over and starts killing people. So, we camp or die and during this, Kael fooking crashes. Do we have luck or what? We finally have a legit force for Idol/AoW and the zone crashes grrrrr.

So, zone comes back up a few minutes later and guess what......Vindi and Sorz are up again heh. This time Vinds BP goes to Agai, and Sorz drops a Swiftblade to Tamorfin. The winner of the ST Talisman is Marjolene woot. So, here we have the Idol again, and we are ready this time.

Buffs are done, mages have been summoning rods for hours(or so it seems) and we got our rotations set. Things are pretty ghetto cause chats are still broke so the spam in zone is unreal, just like old times. So, the monks pull Idol...but ooops, one of our monks forgot to make sure the lead off MT had his defensive disc ready. 10 guesses which monk it was /glare leezard. So, after we rez and rebuff, we are ready again.

Things can't go any better at the start. Trak is all over Idol like he is on sheep, and the next few tank switches go good. Then a few of our lady clerics flashed some leg, and the Idol went and whacked them. Things went to hell from there, with agro being fickle and more tanks/healers dying. We are now out of tanks, and we realize we are toast, Trev(complete in full planar gear rofl) tanks while people camp out.

No miracle 'First try, First Kill' this time, but its only a matter of time. Anyways, we thought we would go kill some dwarfs before the upcoming patch to fix the patch, but Yelinak decided to see what was going on in Skyshrine, so our bravest mage sucks it up and makes that damn run up top while people laze about. Anyways, CoH, Buff, wait for Guurn to turn in Tormax's head, kill.

Yumin finally spent some points and takes home Yelinaks head to mount on the wall until he gets some factioning done. Grats Namin on the Mask and a new Robe. And the lucky dwarf cleric Karnos gets the Talisman for the ST key, no he isn't keeping it for uber cleric haste.

Another patch day coming up, what will we do tonight......
