Back to PoP /Utadin April 7, 2003
Its no secret we haven't done much in PoP for the last bit, we have been hitting VT as much as we can...mainly cause after putting in so much work into getting there, hell no are we going to just leave it. That being said, PoP progression is important.

We failed to Ryda Dar a few days ago, and RD is our current 'bane'. So, we went to the 'other' side of the flag path, and finally visited Saryn. One word of note for pet users, when you come to a mob that casts some sorta 'translocate' spell on you, either /pet hold or kill your pet....see your silly pet views this 'helpful' spell cast as 'harmful' and thus gets agro, and can potentially kill the very very green NPC, thus pissing off the rest of the raid who didn't get a chance to hail said NPC. Besides that, was actually a fun trek to Saryn, and the Saryn fight wasn't too bad, if a little hectic at the end. Here she is alive, and here she is dead heh.

Grats to Tamorfin on Twin Blades of Sorrow, and Krang on the Orb of Infinite Terror.

From the last patch day, we snagged Blood and Emperor and Cursed again.
