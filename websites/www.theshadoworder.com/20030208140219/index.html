The Weekend Past /Utadin February 3, 2003
Lets start with Friday. XTC was visited by us for the first time in a while. Grats Guardd on slippers, Jervis on the Dirk, and Xorphitus on the Wraps. We then moved to The Deep, and did a quick Burrower kill. Mishakaal and Sijur got some black caps, Rahkyr got Ring of Depths and Thievin got a new poker. Off to HoH we go, lets see how we do. We set up and did the 'Rasco' trial, pretty easy, then moved to RD again. We thought we had this, but nope, didn't work out. We will keep plugging away. For revenge, we went and hit up the Zek brothers, Vallon gave up another Maul to Candarye and Veneal won the Pendant. Tallon gave Orme the Battle Charm, and Mesos the Visor Of Endless sight.

Saturday seen us hit Grummus, then followed that by hitting CoD for some flags. We then hit up Agnarr again, and this time Lladar and Trevian upgraded their bows(again) to Longbow of the Stormcaller. Time to relax, and go try out HP. We tried it a little different then normal, and seemed to work and at the least...it kept us awake. Grats to Esto on the Mantle, Ailya on Wraths Edge, and finally....Poooooooooooooochin got his shield!

Sunday we didn't do much, most of us exp'ed somewhere or another until our scheduled raid tonight for ... Bert! Man, this thing is a rush. We were doing pretty good, knocking down the mobs left and right. Then, for some reason we haven't figured out yet, we had agro from mobs at the CoD zone in. People were getting summoned left and right and smoked. Things looked bleak. Everyone was either dead or zoned out, and this only an hour since the Ring started. Mobs were still popping downstairs. So...we said hell with it, we rezzed as quick as we could, got minimal buffs(/hugs mgb) and ported back down. We got things under control, kudos enchanters, and we were back in action again. Time was getting short though, we had just gone 20 minutes without doing anything in the ring. We knock down one named, then another, and while the clock is ticking...we can see the goal. The last 3 named were killed and we can hear the clock now..tick tock tick tock...we get pumped to kill the last named and then prep for Bert. We then kill it, and wham, in a doozy of a 'ooops', we got Bert in camp! While we tried, we just weren't ready, and at 80% or so we cleared out. Oh well, disappointing end, but was great fun and intensity. Next time /nod.
