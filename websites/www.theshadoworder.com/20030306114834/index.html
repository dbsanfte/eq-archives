Grats Orme on the Bracer, and Wikhed on the Mace.

So, the big guy again. This time, we just let the ranger die, so its really flawless heh. Grats to Seckzie and Gorou on new haste booties, and myself on a new Robe, woot! This time Kaelwyn scribed GSS. And oh yeah, more rifts!

Anyways, thats enough for me tonight. 3 Emperor kills in 8 days...grats to the guild on sticking together and finally hitting our goals, we aren't done yet though.
