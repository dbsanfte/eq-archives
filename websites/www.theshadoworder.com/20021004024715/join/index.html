Welcome to The Shadow Order online application form.

TSO is a raiding guild that is family oriented. Our primary motivation is sharing the fun and excitement of the higher end game with our guildmates. TSO does not consider loot as our number one concern or motive. Naturally, the loot is part of the fun in EQ, but we obtain loot primarily to better our guild and in turn enhance our abilities and raiding performance.

TSO is based entirely on skill and technique of execution, instead of numbers and brute force that a lot of the other guilds utilize. We strive to push the envelope in what is possible to do with numbers that are usually under the norm.

We are always looking for dedicated players to join our ranks. If you are interested, please fill out the following form. First, you need to meet the minimum requirements, and once that is in place, your application will be evaluated. That by itself, however, doesn't guarantee you will become a member.

Minimum Requirements

  • Must meet level requirement, which is class specific.
  • Must Love Raiding
  • Play daily for at least 3-4 hours on average, during 6pm EST - 2am EST timeframe
  • Must tolerate being told what to do without getting uptight
  • Must tolerate failure and death. If you will freak out after a wipeout, TSO is not the place for you. Sometimes the only way to succeed is to learn from failure.
  • Must have a good attitude, and look on a positive side of any situation.
  • Healthy sense of humor required
