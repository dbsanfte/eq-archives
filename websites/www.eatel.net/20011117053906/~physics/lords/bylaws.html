Guild Rules 

  1. The Guild and Guildmembers Come First--> This means you help guild mates if you can...also we strongly request that you Group with guildmates when ever possible...if you are close in level and are in the same zone.  

  2. Please Make Every Effort to Attend Guild Meetings and Guild Events--> We will announce guild events and meetings on the website message board, But our prime request is you make every effort to attend the meetings!!!  

  3. Always make Lords of War look good...especially in groups. 



Good Grouping Rules
  1. There is only one puller in a group. Please do not aggro mobs if you are not the designated puller.
  2. Focus on one target. Use /assist |puller name| when fighting in a group...the Mezzers will thank you and your party will live longer.
  3. Always give the group time to replace you...You don't have to wait for hours for them to find a replacement, but let the group know you are leaving soon.
  4. Follow the looting rules. If FFA, try not to over loot...group members tend to remember ppl who over loot. Some Loot policies are:
FFA -- Free For All. No specific order for looting...one important rule...NEVER loot while there is fighting going on...always wait until battle is over.

Alpha -- Loot done in alphabetical order. Usually *Rolling* for Special items.

Alpha Item -- Group member loots everything until an item is found...Group will decide on the switch item(s).

Master Loot -- One person loots everything...sells all items then loot is split afterwards. Do this with Groups you trust!

Split On/Off -- Make sure to ask if Autosplit is on or off...and remember Autosplit just splits coins, so if the corpse has 1PP and 5CP the person who loots gets the plat and the rest of the group gets 1 CP each.

*Rolling* -- Using the command /rand 100 to get a number from 1 to 100 ... Usually highest number wins. All group members must be close together for rolls.

All For One And One For All!
