About the Inner Council

The Inner Council is a Everquest Guild
from the Sullon Zek server.
We are a Neutral Aligned guild.
The Inner Council was formed Jan 3rd 2002
for mutual benifit of Newts.
Either through Grouping, Tradeskills, PK parties, and Raids.

We have very few requirements for membership.
You must be at least in your 15th season on Sullon Zek.
You must have the desire to bring honor to the Inner Council
and to the Newt team.

This site is a portal for Inner Council members, and newts alike.
