November 2, 1999
this was a some news off AllaKhazam's Magical Realm .


Verant has just opened the Official Ruins of Kunark Web site with information about the
upcoming expansion. The most interesting news there is that the target date for the
expansion is March, 2000 and there will be beta testing for the expansion starting
around January. Lets see, that gives me 4 months to get to level 50 so that I can take
advantage of the new levels and spells. Guess I better stop dying all the time.
Sanderson ZakD'marr went digging through his files from beta and came up with an old
map of Kunark. I have added a section for Kunark that includes the map. There are
almost certainly going to be numerous differences between this map and the final
product, but I am guessing that much of the structure layed out there is correct. And it is
kind of fun to get a sneak preview.
