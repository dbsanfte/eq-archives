Everlore Druid Epic Quest Discussions
 
 
1.
Go to: Telin Darkforest, Burning Woods, (p3238 x p2855), wood elf male in chain armor.
  • 'hail Telin Darkforest'
  • 'what are you doing in the forest'
  • 'what answers'
  • 'what evil'
  • 'what do you suspect'
  • 'what about innoruuk'
  • 'what action' : you only need to ask this part to get the Worn Note
Receive: Worn Note
  • Telin Darkfores's faction belongs to Faydwer Champions.
  • How to raise faction: Give bat wings to the Magician's guildmaster, Niola Impholder, will raise faction with Faydwer Championns, Keepers of the Art, King Tearis Thex, lowers faction with The Dead. Remember, you can purchase bat wings from the erudite vendor in East Commons. If for any reason Niola would not give you the bat wing quest, you will need someone who can cast Invisibility to help you. First, give Niola 4 stacks of 4 bat wings, while the trade window is opened, have someone cast Invisibility on you. Click OK to trade. Wa-lah. Faction is raised.
  • Faction can also be built up fast by doing the muffin quest in freeport
Note: If you don't mind the walk to this guy you may want to bind your self to he /loc, You will need to come back to him in step 4
  
 
2.
Go to: Faelin Bloodbrair, Greater Faydark, (p1500 x p500), wood elf female in leather armor.
  • Faelin Bloodbrair roams the northern part of the zone near Crushbone. Her placeholders are the orc pawns. She is a rare spawn and she does not stop roaming when you hail her. She will despawn after you hand her the Worn Note from Telin Darkforest.
Give: Worn Note

Receive: Felin's Ring 		 
 
3.
Go to: Giz X'Tin, Kithikor Forest, roams along the roads, You can find him in the day time 8am to 8pm, dark elf male in leather armor.
  • After you hand him Felin's Ring, a lvl 50 Teir'Dal Reaver will spawn and attack you, but you can run away with SoW. You do not have to kill the Teir'Dal Reaver.
  • Small note: Don't help another druid or ranger with the Corruptor in East Karanas if you still have to do this part. Killing the corrupter gives you bad Faction with Giz so that he won't give you back the coin for Telin.
Give: Felin's Ring

Receive: Dark Metal Coin

5.
*you will need a full group of 50's and up to help you kill this DE corruptor before! you start step 5 or you have to start all over to step 1*

Go to: Athele, East Karana, (n3666 x n1588), wood elf female in leather armor.

Give: Worn Dark Metal Coin

Receive: Braided Grass Amulet

Go to: Sionae, East Karana, north of Athele, (n2607 x n1591), human female in leather armor.

Give: Braided Grass Amulet

Receive: Frayed Braided Grass Amulet

Go to: Nuien, East Karana, west of Athele, (n3656 x p301), half elf male in leather armor.

Give: Frayed Braided Grass Amulet

Receive: Frayed Braided Grass Amulet

Go to: Teloa, East Karana, east of Athele, past barb village, on the zone wall, (n3836 x n1846), half elf female in leather armor.
  • After giving Teloa the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet, a Dark Elf Corruptor and (2) Dark Elf Reavers will spawn (all are 50 lvl SK). The 2 DE Reavers will head to the druids and kill them. Find the DE Corruptor and kill him fast. He depops after 10 seconds if not attacked, also, depops after the DE Reavers kill the 4 druids. If the 4 druids were killed by the DE Reavers after you killed the DE Corruptor, you will have to wait for 1 hour for Athele to spawn.
  • Athele will despawn after you give her the Fleshbound Tome.
Give: Frayed Braided Grass Amulet

Spawns: Dark Elf Corruptor (kill), (n1400 x n1000), 2 Dark Elf Reaver (you can wait at a bit north of Athele and lure them away from the druids) Loot: Fleshbound Tome.

Go to: Athele, East Karana, (n3666 x n1588), wood elf female in leather armor.

Give: Fleshbound Tome

Receive: Earth Stained Note

7.
Hardened Mixture
  • Forage the following 4 items and combine them in the Shiney Tin Bowl.

    1. Chilled Tundra Root - Everfrost
    2. Sweetened Mudroots - Misty Thicket
    3. Speckled Molded Mushrooms - Innothule Swamp
    4. Ripened Heartfruit - Greater Faydark
Note: This can take several hours. Combine those 4 items in the bowl and you get a Hardened mixture. You may need high forage skill over 100 to get the items.

Receive: Hardened Mixture (Hold till you do step 12)

9.
Platinum Speckled Powder
  • Forage a Rose of Firiona in Firiona Vie

Go to: Merdan Fleetwood, Surefall Glades, (p245 x n60), human male in chain armor.

Give: Rose of Firiona

Receive: Wood Painting

Go to: Human Skeleton, Frontier Mountains, roams, skeleton. As the Human Skeleton is the only undead in the entire Frontier Mountains zone, it is very easy to locate if you have someone cast Sense Undead and track it down. Classes that are able to cast Sense Undead are, cleric, sk and necro. I always found this guy behind the giant fort near the 2nd big hill.

Give: Wood Painting

Receive: Silver Chained Locket

Go to: Niera Farbreeze, Surefall Glades, (p111 x n188), human female in chain armor.

Give: Silver Chained Locket

Receive: Platinum Speckled Powder (Hold till you do step 11)
