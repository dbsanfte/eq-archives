**why is Wolf form KOS in Fironia**
The city governments have been enacting leash laws lately to try and curb the number of strays running wild through the cities. Wolves that aren't on leashes (e.g. a pet) will be attacked and killed on site by many of the guards.
That’s an RP reason anyway and I thought some might get a kick out of it :)
(OOC: Most NPCs were viewing a druid in wolf-form as a BetaNeutral NPC, instead of as a wolf. This was fixed a few weeks ago in many of the cities, and should have been in the patch message.)
-Gordon
**POLICIES ON VEESHANS PEAK**
In order to avoid any appearance of impropriety in the zone, as sometimes follows when we've assisted in corpse recoveries in other zones due to claimed bug-deaths, we're not going to risk that for Veeshan's Peak. Veeshan's Peak is incredibly dangerous, and in order to guarantee that everyone is operating under the same set of conditions, we've elected to make the zone "enter at your own risk".
Source: Everquest Gameplay Forum.

**Field of Bone lock-up bug**
Gordon Wrinn elaborated on a newly discovered bug on the Kunark zone "Field of Bone"
Here is the scenario: When I zone into the Field of Bone from the Iksar town, I cant see myself on the screen, I cant turn, cant change my view. If I taunt a mob or check my inventory, the game crashes to my desktop. Other players say I am crouching... I hit D to uncrouch and still the same problem. If I attack, it says I'm sitting. So I
stand and the problem still persists. I reinstalled my drivers.. went and downloaded the most current drivers according to the manufacturer and Verant, and reinstalled Directx7. I tried both the dx7 and glide versions for my Voodoo2 card and still no fix. I cant use my primary video driver at all (Rage Fury) so I didn't even bother with that. I have always used the v2 for EQ.
Finally I gave up, deleted the character, started anew and searched for other newbie zones... I found one. So I start hunting there, and finally die... and sure as #&$@ the bind point is in Field of Bones So now I'm stuck again

We've received the reports of this issue. It's being caused by a problem with one of the Field of bone files in your EverQuest directory. For some reason the patch server isn't detecting the problem and repatching it. We're not sure which file it is yet, as all of our systems here are working fine, but when we do find out we'll let you know right away.
If anyone who's having this problem would care to assist, you can try deleting one of the field of bone files, repatching, then seeing if it works. If it doesn't, delete another and repeat. We don't want to recommend that everyone delete all of the files as they total over 9MB.
As soon as we get it narrowed down, we'll let you know and also fix the patch server.
