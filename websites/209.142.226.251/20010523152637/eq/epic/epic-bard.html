Everlore Bard Epic Quest Discussions
 
 
1.

The quest begins in the Dreadlands. Baldric Slezaf is a bard who wishes to complete a great symphony. He requires 4 items to do this, and your reward for them is the Singing Short Sword. The 4 items are.

1. Top half of Page 24 of a composition by "The Maestro"
2. Bottom half of page 24
3. Page 25
4. Mystical Lute made by Forper Fizfla

 
 
2.

To get the Top half of page 24,

Go to: West Karana and talk to Konia Swiftfoot in the guard tower (n516 x n2433).

you say: 'hail Konia Swiftfoot'

She gives you a torch and starts you off on a relay race of some kind.

First you must take the torch to Fajio Knejo in Misty Thicket (Near the wall guards /loc n127 x n55).

give: Torch
Receive: Torch

Fajio says: 'Run Onwart, my friend, to the far off Desert of Ro and give this torch to Andad Filla.

Next, you have to go to Andad Filla, who hangs near the zone to Innothule Swamp in the Southern Desert of Ro (n3043 x p1243).

Give: Torch
Receive: Torch

Andad says: You still have a way to go! Seek out Misty Tekchita near the arena at Lake Rathe. Run like the wind!

After that you must see Misty Tekchita in Lake Rathetear (near Arena zone).

Give: Torch

Receive: a Ring Proof of Speed

Go back to Konia Swiftfoot .

Give: Ring

Receive: Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Top

 
 
3.

To get the Bottom half of page 24,

you must also deal with Konia Swiftfoot. She will trade you the bottom half of page 24 for Mahlin's Bongos.

First you must visit Braenar Swiftsong in South Karana (n19 x p96).

You say: what a Doll

Receive: Solusek Mining Company Invoice

Take the invoice to the merchant (Marfen Binkdirple) in Sol A who sells the tinkerer's bag

Give: Solusek Mining Company Invoice

Receive: a Mechanical Doll

Take the Doll and give it to Braenar's daughter Serra in the Estate of Unrest, who can be found next to the Gnomish Spelunker.

Serra will give you a note.

Take that note back to Braenar Swiftsong in South Karana (n19 x p96).

Receive: another note for Maligar.

Maligar can be found in West Karana, at apx. loc (p1300 x n11500). When you give the note to Maligar, it will spawn his Estranged Doppleganger who you must kill. You will need a group of high 40's , low 50's to kill him.

Loot: his head and bring it back to Braenar Swiftsong , who will reward you with "Mahlin;s Bongos".

Take the bongos back to Konia Swiftfoot, and she will give you the Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Bottom.

 
 
4.

To get page 25 you must talk to Kelkin Mekia a female npc in South Karana, she talks about her broken lute strings and she wants the guts from three dragons.

a. Guts from Edrik the Old, a named drake in Skyfire (Chromodrac guts)

b. Guts from Nezekezena, a new Wurm in Burning Woods (Red wurm guts)

c. Guts from Blackwing, a new onyx drake in Rathe Mountains (Huge onyx drake guts)

Get her the 3 guts and she give you page 25

  
 
5.

To get Forpar's lute, you will have to ask the lute maker himself... Forpar Fizfla in Steamfont Mountains. He will send you on three quests, giving you more of the lute as you complete each one.

The first quest is for the Head & Neck of the lute. Before Forpar will even discuss the lute with you, you need to visit his friend, Vedico Windwisper, in Butcherblock Mountains on the docks. She requires you to retrieve her horn. You must loot the Alluring Horn from Quag Maelstrom in the Ocean of Tears and return it to her, when she will then give you a note to take back to Forpar. Forpar will then offer to make the head & neck of the lute if you get the following items.

1. Backbone of an Ancient Fisherman. Looted of Phinagel in Kedge Keep.

2. A strong tentacle from one of the long lost amalgyms, from one of the Amygdalen Tentacle Whips in the Plane of Fear

3. Petrified skull of a Lycanthrope, which drops off Drolvarg Warlord in Karnor's Castle

After he has given you the Head & Neck of the lute, he will offer to make you the Body for the following items.

4. Large Red Dragon Scale. The red scale from Talendar of Skyfire has been confirmed to work

5. Large White Dragon Scale. The white scale from Gorenaire of the Dreadlands has been confirmed to work

6. Metal Bits. 2 were used and confirmed to work.

Finally, Folpar will complete the lute if you bring him the Undead Guts of Trakanon as material for the strings. Give Folpar the guts PLUS the Head & Neck PLUS the Body, and he will reward you with the Mystical Lute.

Go to: Dreadlands. Baldric Slezaf is a bard who wishes to complete a great symphony

Give him: Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Top, Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Bottom, Page 25, Mystical Lute

Receive: Singing Short Sword
