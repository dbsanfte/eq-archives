-John Smedley CEO of EQ sent some announcements out to the news sites.

Hello All,

I'd like to let everyone know about several of important things:

1) We are going to be putting up 4 new servers over the course of the next
5-6 weeks. We aren't blind to the overcrowding in any way and this is a
major step towards helping this situation. It may seem as though we aren't
attentive to this issue, but it's been a major challenge to update all of
the machines for Ruins of Kunark as well as adding more servers. Although
we haven't decided this for certain, it is likely that on at least 2 of the
servers we will probably be moving people from the more overcrowded
servers.We will be SURE to give much more advance notice this time as we realize
our mistake the last time we did this.

2) We are currently adding the hardware neccessary to run the new Kunark
Zones. This will mean some downtime each day for 1 -2 servers per day for
about the next 20 days or so. Each server needs to be taken down one by
one for about a 6 hour upgrade. This is a significant hardware upgrade and will
have a lot of good effects on the game overall.

3) We are adding more customer service people to help run EverQuest.

4) The price point of EverQuest: Ruins of Kunark has been determined as
$16.95 plus Shipping and Handling. We will offer different shipping options
that should satisfy everyone, including special international shipping
options to make sure our International customers are satisfied. In early
April you will be prompted when logging into EverQuest. This will give you
the opportunity to purchase EverQuest: Ruins of Kunark online and bill it
right then and there. We will announce the date this will be going up well
in advance. Please note this price is ONLY for current customers upgrading
only.

5) Very shortly we will be adding another ISP in addition to UUnet and
CERFnet.

John Smedley
President and CEO
Verant Interactive, Inc.
