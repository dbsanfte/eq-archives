    The Everlore Team managed to get some info on the new Velious expansion.

  • One new Continent - Velious which contains 16 new adventure zones.
  • including: ice caves, crystal caverns, crypts, dungeons and frozen towers.
  • Brand new character graphics.
  • Additional spells and weapons and an enhanced 3D game engine.
  • Velious will be geared towards levels 35 - 60 players, although the level limit will stay at 60.
  • All-new ships that will sail to Velious
  • Velious will focus primarily on new zones, quests, items and more variety in how players appear in-game.
  • A new cast of visually stunning non-player characters including White Dragons, and Frost giants, Ice dragons, Snow orcs, Cave bears, Snowfang gnolls, Storm giants and Vicious ottermen
  • There will be no new player races or classes.)
  • There will be teleports into various locations in Velious (rumor has it they may work differently, however.
  • There is no starting city in Velious.
  • Official Velious site is up now
