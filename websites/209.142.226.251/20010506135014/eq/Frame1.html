May 3, 2001

**MORE Details on Shadows of Luclin Expansion** - 10:14 am

  • The new graphics models will allow for facial expression when you are angry, surprised, scared, etc.
  • You will be able to access Luclin from the wizard spires and platforms. Not sure yet if it will be Wizard only or not though.
  • The Beastlord will be a Shaman / Warrior hybrid. It is described as 'the first class that can tank and conjure a pet' (this is CGW's words not ours or SOE's).
  • The pet you conjure can zone with you as you go from one zone to the next. A zone will be set up specific as a Bazaar. This will be a formal area to buy and sell without all the shouting.
  • The new race will be 'good' in alignment.
  • Half the world will always be in dark and half in the light. You will also be able to travel through the core of the moon where there will be cities and dungeons.
