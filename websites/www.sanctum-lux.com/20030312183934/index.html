Welcome to Guildpage of Sanctum Lux

 

Sanctum Lux seeks to be a truly Good Aligned Order, protecting the innocent, aiding all good folk and being vigilant and striking out against the evils that roam the lands.

It is the aim of the guild to heavily support role-playing, stick to it's original goals and be a warm, helpful family for all members.
