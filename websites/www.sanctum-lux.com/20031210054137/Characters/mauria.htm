My story is sad but true, my name is Mauria Erosbringer a humble Paladin of the Goddess Erollisi. I have be told that to heal you must share and forgive. I can't believe I will ever forgiven, but the welcome I recieved has open me to share. Erollisi has bless be with a family.

<Sitting by the fire looking over the forest top watching the sunset. Its a lonely night staring down at the mace imbedded in pass. She sits and ponders the events of her life. Each twist and turn for the Goddess Erollisi.>

<No one but the wind can hear her talking to her memories Tears fall unnoticed. >

Father and mother it been many years, please forgive me, the goddess has brought me home to a family of adventurer. Maybe you have heard of them father, Sanctum Lux? Many warriors of Mithaniel and Erollisi have call them home. ((shrugs and smiles) Knowing you two, the twins probably were badgered into leading them to me and I to them (chuckles and wipes the tears away)). I just hope I will never fail the goddess or them. I wish to become accepted into their ranks someday when I become worthy to be a full sister. <Sit in the solitude of her mind for sometime>

Remembering the pass twist and turns of her short life.

(A sad smile creases her face) Mauria remember the first time she every laid eye on this forest. Her family was summons back to the temple of Erollisi and was half way home when her father sensed great evil. He ordered his two squires to remain behind to watch the pack animals and to protect his daughter. While his wife and him investigate. She remembers her mother running towards her jumping on her horse and lifting Mauria into the saddle. Mauria looks over her mother shoulder and sees her father and his two squires plant there feet and remove their mace for battle. Her father armor is glowing with the glory of Mithaniel Marr as the tide of dark creatures closing in. Her father looks over his shoulders and yells that he loves her right before the first wave of dark creature attack...

For three days, they ride the dark creature never too far behind, no more than two days behind. On the Fourth Day, the raiders capture them and make them slaves. Mauria was a slave for six year before escaping. She cannot forget the nights the Dark Elf leader would come and drag her mother away bringing her back beaten and bleeding. Mauria dedicated her life to the goddess praying for an opportunity for escape and the wisdom to see it. She rotted forgotten for five years before the leader began notice Mauria was more than a way to keep the priestess in control. Mauria, eleven season when her mother could not deflect the leader attention. The dark elves dragged Mauria upstairs for there nightly entertainment, where she passed out.

She awakes to her mother singing and her body tingling, screaming and kicking. She doesnt understand what happen, but vows to the goddess Erollisi, never again will she be touch by anyone like that again. (Hangs her head and sobs). Another few days went by before the drunken leader visit her again. However, he never realizes his mistake coming for her alone. Mauria strike him across the head, using a femur, from the former tenet, crushing his temple.

Her mother and she reach the pass before pursuit catches up. Her mother is too tired and weak to go on. She orders Mauria to run for help and she will delay the remaining dark elves as long as she can. Mauria know she will never see her mother again in this life and looks back to see her mother singing to the goddess in rapture hold her fathers Mace.

It was two days before a Cleric and Paladin of Marr discovered her, by that time in was already to late to return with help. The pass had be come a memorial to her father and mother. She still comes to this place when she needs time alone. Her family always seems closer here.

She finishes the rabbit strew and kicks the fire out before walking back to her new family Sanctum Lux. Vowing that if the goddess lets her, she will destroy the dark elven nation.
