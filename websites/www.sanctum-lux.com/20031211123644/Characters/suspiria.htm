**sighs heavily** Greetings, my dear family. I am sure most of you have wondered who Suspiria is, and what his background is for a long time now. Truth be told, so have I. Most things I cannot recall, but what I do know, I will tell you now...

My name is Suspiria Astyrhim, which is a name that I have given to myself, as I have no recollection of family. Suspiria has a meaning of darkness, as that is all I can recall from my beginnings. I felt dark and alone, so decided that I shall call myself Suspiria. As for where I have come from, the only thing I can say about my beginnings, is that I remember walking aimlessly thru the land of Greater Faydark as a wee lad, and coming upon a city which I have come to know as Felwithe.

As I wandered in, quite warily, I might add, as I seemed to recall great pain and hurt, I spotted a door up ahead and thought I might be able to find some food or shelter there. As I entered, I saw no one, save for a priest, 2 others next to him, and up on the platform stood an elf named Tynkdale.

It was to this elf Tynkdale that I approached and hailed, but got no response. Disappointed, I turned to go and put my hands in my pockets. Upon doing so, I found a small scroll, which I pulled out to look at. Thinking maybe I could tun this over to this elf standing on the dais, and maybe find some food and shelter for the eve, I handed it to him.

What a change in response did I receive! Immediately I was given food and drink, and welcomed as a young Paladin under the watchful eye of Tunare, and my master Tynkdale. After talking to him a bit about this, I had decided I would be honored to be a young Paladin under his watchful eye, and see where that would take me. I must admit, I was a bit wary tho, but as I had nothing, and this stranger was willing to take me in, I would do my best and nothing less.

I set out the next day to begin my training outside of the castle walls. Slaying the evil beasts that roamed the lands there, and running back to the protection of the guards when I became overwhelmed. This I did for many days and nights, swiftly growing and learning my weapons skills. It was there that I also met a Magician by the name of Gribb.

Gribb saw that I was young, and a bit reckless and disorganised, and set to help me relax and fight calm and with precision. He helped me with weapons and armor that he obtained in hunts in other lands. Soon, I saw less and less of him, as he ached to travel afar, but he continued to help me when time allowed.

But through all of this, I felt there was something missing. In my limited travels, I met many with familys, and was constantly asked about mine. I told them I knew not of my family, only that the slaying of evil in the lands of Norrath, and trying to lend aid to others for this fight, was what I felt in my heart needed to be done. I still have no rememberance of any immediate family, and felt a great emptiness inside. It was then that I began a quest to find fellowship within a guild that may serve to fill the emptiness I felt.

In my travels, I began talking to others who belonged to familys, and there were many. There were certains things that I knew of without doubt, and until I found the family that put those ideals up front, I would continue my journey alone. These were: Rid Norrath Of All Evil. No matter how long, hard or far it took me; Put Others Before Yourself. Whether it be in battle, in the splitting of the loot, in heals, or in death, I knew I must serve to get the other out, or just keep them alive; No Evil Inside The Family. They are good to use for the betterment of Norrath, but there should be no evil inside, as they will quickly turn; And just plain fellowship of having a caring family to help look after.

After great and exhaustive research, I believed I found the family I was seeking, and that was Sanctum-Lux. So immediately I sent a message to a Paladin by the name of Quelitin, asking if I may be of service to the family. He resonded quickly, saying that I must contact the guildfather Khazidar. Of course I did immediately, and in time he did respond. Soon I had an interview set up to speak with him.

I met this great little Dwarf in Shadowhaven, at the guild headquarter, and we spoke at length. Of course, the first thing he did upon Salutations was hand me ale. As I get drunk quite easily, I figured I would save it, and speak first. After some time, he asked me if I would like to join the family. I excitedly said I would, and he immediately welcomed me, and made introductions. The warm response I received from the family was overwhelming, and brought tears of joy to my eyes. I immediately made a vow to do whatever I could to help, and keep the family in greatness and in light.

From there, I set out with the charter in heart to do my part to help change Norrath into a place of light and honor. But I also started to have doubts about my name. No one asked the meaning, tho some knew, I am sure. But I felt the need to bring pride upon our family, and knew that I needed to obtain a surname that would mean the opposite of darkness, and set out to find that name while in my journeys.

Eventually, I came to hear about a band of Elves of Light, from an area called Astyrhim in legend. As there is a great story behind that, and that is too much to tell here, and with much contemplation, I decided to choose that as my surname. And so it was with pride to my family that I had decided to call myself Suspiria Astyrhim, proud member of Sanctum Lux.

As for the rest, I must say that without the help and leadership of our dear departed Father Khazidar, I do not believe I could have become the Paladin that I am. Tho I still have much to learn, he became a friend and mentor to me, and I will miss him dearly. But I made a vow to him, and that I shall keep, to keep this family in light and in greatness. For I have found the family that I sought, and without anyone of the members here, I could not obtain the life that I have sought.

And for that, I thank all of you.
Your Brother In Arms
Suspiria Astyrhim
