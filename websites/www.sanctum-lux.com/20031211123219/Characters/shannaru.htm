My family has always been considered poor to a high elf's standards, due to the fact we have always lived outside the beautiful city, Felewithe, so we can better protect the lands of Norrath. My father is a paladin who I share the same name as, Shannaru and my mother is Foragil (Star from the North) I wanted to become an enchanter so I could hunt with my father and support him with my magic, but that changed when father hurt his back and was not able to continue his profession as a Paladin of Tunare, thou he say's once a paladin always a paladin, its in the heart, not just a job. What ever that means. Let me say this, he was not happy to learn i follow the nameless one, I am agnostic. I do have a heart that serves love and good justice but i also seek power and true power is held for an enchanter who serves the nameless one. That is why my father introduced me to his other family, Sanctum Lux, who I have the honor to serve and bear a witness too, that we are the care givers for the lands and peoples of Norrath.
