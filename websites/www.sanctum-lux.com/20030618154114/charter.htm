Sanctum Lux Charter

 

All members are to follow these rules:


The Rule of Decorum

All members must at all times portray the guild in a good light

The Rule of Fellowship

Guild members must always, if they are able, offer their services to a fellow guild member in distress or great need.

The Rule of Honesty

All members must be honest and truthful at all times

The Rule of Innocence

No member may kill an innocent

The Rule of Law

All guild members must at all times obey all laws of the lands of Good

The Rule of Light

No evil character or race may join the guild

The Rule Of One

Guild members may not group with more then one evil person/race

The Rule of Protection

All members are to aid all good in Norrath and be vigilant against evil

The Rule of Rank

Guild members are to address guild members accordingly

 

The Rule of Respect

Guild members are to show respect at all times to each other


The Rule of Silver

No member of the guild may beg

The Rule of Unity

No guild member may raise a hand against another member of the guild, unless taking part in a 'guild event'
