Jarrl is a Vah Shir from the city of Shar Vahl located on the dark side of Luclin. Most of his early life was spent training in many types of musical instruments. Most knowledge and history of the Vah Shir is past along in song or story. Jarrl had a great desire to learn all he could about any thing he could. And he, and with his music he could pass it along to others of his race. But when Vah Shirs came to the right age, they get an urge to hunt. Jarrl like many others of his mates went off to hunt outside the walls of Shar Vahl. He learned much from experience and mistake while in the wilds of Luclin. Soon Jarrl was done with his train and felt it was time to explore his own world and the world of Norrath. Upon his arrival in the land of the Elves. He was invited to join a certain group. This group of beings seeming to be what he was looking for. But they where not, they where corrupt, greed, and fools. Still he stuck with them in hopes they would change, and he knew no others in this strange land. While in Felwithe he heard the weeps of an Elf maiden. He inquired what was wrong. She explained how she had fallen in battle to foul Orcs. Jarrl went off to recover her lost items. While getting the items, but he him self was slain by some of the Orcs. Darkness over came him. But then there was light, he was returned to the world. Suffering from weakness of the return. A small furry creature stood below him. As his eyes focused they gazed upon an armor clad Dwarf. Jarrl thanked the small fellow, and explained the happenings. Khazidar was his name stated the Dwarf. With the Elf even maidens stuff returned. Jarrl headed off, but he meet up with Khazidar a few days later. After talking for hours, Jarrl join The Sanctum Lux.

Some time he did spend with his new family. Before becoming a full member. Then after leading a raid into the deeps of Nagafen's Lair he was promoted to an Officer. The Hearld as he was called During this time he helped out those in the family. Getting corpses from some very bad places. Like under dragons and those lost in foul places. He grow more powerful with time, eather in a raid, battle group or alone. While alone he would travel the lands and planes dealing with those who would do others wrong. As like all Vah Shir he does not follow a god, and he did not even belive they existed till he layed eyes on Tunare her self. Then he know they did. But still he followed his heart to know what was right. But even those of a good heart can fall into the dark...

One day well, on the Plane of Knowledge. Jarrl befell a curse or some sorts. He wondered around thinking he was a Paladin fellow and marched him self into the depts of Neriak. There he was transfored or take over they say by the Innoruuk him self. Where then he went to sit and speak of the coming destruction of the world as we know. Till Khazidar and a small task force came and expelled the demons from Jarrl setting him free from what had befell him.

As Sanctum Lux grow, Jarrl was promoted to Guardian. While he did have many informants in the world and connects. He did like to check things out. So he went off to Neriak with Guaridan Bovi, his good friend. To check out some rumors.  The mission was a disaster, Bovi was caught outside the city and jarrl was caught inside by Tier'dal after his illusion failed.  Both were tortured but no information was gained from them.  After many days of pain both were executed and their heads returned to Sanctum Lux's headquarters.  The mourning lasted for many many days. His remains where collected and places in the deep tombs of Shar Vahl...

They his corpse lay, till the Spirits returned him to live. He was reborn as a Shaman. But something was pulling him, something he did not expect. The music he once know was calling him. He returned to his tomb. Where he searched though his old items. And he found his drum, that Selo him self once owned. But it glows of more magic then before. He reached to touch it. Magic notes fluttered about him playing a song that he never heard or would hear again. When it stopped. He was once again a Bard. Every thing he had lost was returned. And this time he would not fall prey to evil. Be would prey on evil for the Music, for the, Spirits, and for Sanctum Lux.
