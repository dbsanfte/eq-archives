Sunday's Raid on Split Paw! - 05/09 Sunday at 3:00pm Central

We are meeting at the White Stone Bridge in North Karanas to raid the Dungeon of Split Paw in South Karanas.  To get to the bridge from Qeynos head out the gates to the Qeynos hills.  Follow the right edge of the Hills into the valley with the road going down the middle, follow this road till you enter West Karanas.  From West Karanas follow the right edge to the river and follow the river all the way to North Karanas.  Keep following the river and not far into North Karanas will be a large White Stone Bridge.    We are encouraging that the loot you acquire in this raid go to the guild fund.   Anyone above level 6 should have fun in here with the size group we will have.

05/04/99 - Catch up on the news!

Quest Development Committee Meeting this Friday!

I would like to see if we can get together on Friday night at 9:00 CST at the Lion’s Mane in Qeynos to discuss who will be involved and to share ideas on what can do for the guild. The purpose of this group is to design and implement quests for the guild for special events. These quests are to appear spontaneous to everyone involved outside this group. The committee will work together practicing their acting, writing story lines, and coming up with new quests on a regular basis. If you are interested please stop by this meeting, if this time does not work out for too many people we will try a different time.

The Guild Finds one of the Heartstone Priests!

On Sunday night Maxx and Caleel and some others found one of the Heartstone Priests in Qeynos.  We hope to have the entire night documented and posted on the site soon.   Come hear the entire story at the guild meeting this Thursday in Qeynos.
