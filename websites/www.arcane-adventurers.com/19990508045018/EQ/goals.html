Arcane Adventurers is a Good, Lawful, Non-Pk, Role-playing Encouraged guild.

Goals:

To have fun, share our ideas and experiences, and in general, enjoy one another’s company!

  • Our main goal is to organize an ever-growing group of people together as a whole. To build a foundation of relationships with others, so that we may influence them towards the Path of Righteousness; thus increasing our standing in the world.
  • Leading by example will be a mainstay of our cause. We will show the world a strong, generous, and helping face. Never will it be said that we did not have time to help others in need.
  • Defending the weak and helpless should become second nature to us. We must never use our powers to cause harm to others, save only in defense. Beings of an evil alignment are just that, evil, and thusly do not fall under this.
  • We will always strive to improve and expand our own personal abilities and knowledge by sharing our resources and experiences with one another.
  • By uniting as a whole, we will pool our resources to possibly purchase a guildhall, have the developers make us a guild skin for our characters, and further expand our influence upon the world.

 

Rules:

Those that chose to ignore these rules can leave the guild, or will be taken off the roster.

  • Respect your fellow guild brothers and sisters.
  • No in game cheating allowed. Anyone found using a bug or anything of that nature will be removed from the guild.
  • Actions committed by a guild member that reflect negatively on the honor and name of the guild will be punished and/or banished from the guild.
    •
  • Abide by the Chain of Command at all times. If you have a question, please address it to a guild brother or sister one step up from you on the Chain of Command.
  • When addressing a fellow guild member that is of higher rank then you, it is encouraged to please use their title and name while addressing them in public. At all other times you can use Brother/Sister or their name as the form of address.  This is not something we require it but it shows respect and dedication to your peers.
  • We all desire peace. With this in mind we will never directly attack other good aligned people, save in our defense. No exceptions allowed! If you are found trying to pick fights, participating in thievery, or generally belittling the cause of this guild, YOU WILL BE BANISHED FROM THE GUILD.
  • Being a generous and forthright player is a requirement. You will take the time to help those in need. You will help those of lesser stature train and become better at what they do. You will be generous to others with you while adventuring and you will take the time to listen to others needs. No matter if they be of this guild or not. A non-member in need is a potential future member.
  • Only good aligned characters are allowed, no Dark Elves, Shadow Knights, etc... The game takes into account your actions and this will influence how NPCs react to you. If a Cleric of Nife and a Cleric of Innoruuk were to group together, they’d both lose HUGE amounts of standing within their respective guilds and smaller amounts to other related parties.
  • All members are required to read the message board daily and to keep up with changes on the web site. Many members work hard on this tool for the guild, it is not put up for fun. Members not keeping up with changes will be warned once and then removed from their band, the second time removed from the guild. All changes are sent to the members by ICQ in the form of a URL link.

 

Guild Law:

The Guild Laws were created in an effort to formalize the guild’s response to negative actions that reflect poorly upon the guild. The Thane is the Keeper and Enforcer of both the rules and the laws. All judgements, if not clearly defined, shall be decide upon by the full counsel.

The Thane will not participate in an active manner in the judging counsel. Instead he will act as an impartial advisor to both sides and provide his knowledge of the laws in an open format. Once judgement has been passed, the Thane will see that the judgement is carried out in its full extent.

The Laws are broken down into three parts: General Crimes, Crimes against the Guild, and Crimes against the People and State.

General Crimes

These are Crimes that are broad in nature and are not directly related to the guild. An example of this type of crime is the use of a bug to further advance your character.

Law: No member(s) of the Arcane Adventures is allowed to use a bug or exploit in any way, shape, or form.

Judgement: Banishment from the guild and that person’s name publicly declared anathema.

Crimes against the Guild

These are Crimes that by their nature, break down the overall purpose of this guild or in some manner besmirches the guild’s honor and name. An example of this type of crime is any act that breaks the guild’s rules or revealing guild secrets.

Law: No member(s) may reveal any information gained by this guild, for guild use, to anyone save another guild member.

Judgement: The member(s) will be denied guild support of a period of 24 hours real time. A second offense will be taken up before the full counsel.

 

Crimes against the People and State

These are Crimes that by their nature negatively influence the world in which our characters will live. An example of this type of crime would be the killing of a neutral or good aligned character, whether it is a player or NPC.

Law: No member(s) will ever commit the murder of any other player or NPC that is of a neutral or good alignment

Judgement: The full guild counsel will publicly try the member(s). A second offense will result in banishment from the guild and that person’s name publicly declared anathema.

 

ICQ Usage:

Many of our members use ICQ for communicating to each other. We encourage you add everyone to your ICQ list and rename each name with the suffix AA (Example: "Cyxe - AA") this way all the members will be easy to find. Questions relating to the guild or game should be posted on the message board so everyone can learn from your questions. Leaders are there to assist you but please don't overwhelm them with messages that do not pertain to them.

Trust-Tax:

To achieve our goals and maintain a stable treasury we believe we all need to donate money to the treasury, managed by the Reeve. It is called a "Trust-Tax" because it is not based on a set amount or percentage. Everyone is encouraged to donate what he or she can. If we notice you never donate to this cause it will eventually creep up on you...

Members that donate large amounts will receive awards, trophies, etc..
