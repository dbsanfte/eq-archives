Hello all,
I would like to send a note to Verant re: my concerns. I will accept any constructive criticism on what I have written below, and I would like to know if you have a email address at Verant you would recommend other than the generic one one the verant Everquest web page. Thanks.

Jula


Dear Verant,

After thinking about the recent Alchemy revelation, i.e., that Verant truly believed the skill was working, I have come to a conclusion which makes me uncomfortable. It's not that Verant could have produced such a flawed skill design in the first place, or that countless numbers of petitions and feedbacks could have been ignored since. These things can be understandable given the complexity and scope of the Everquest product. What concerns me is that the only way this could have happened is if no one at Verant is playing a high level Shaman. This means that no one at Verant is 'looking out for the Shaman'. I know, I know Verant has no favoritism, but regardless of the best intentions, it seems obvious that the classes which the decision makers at Verant play are going to be the classes that get the most attention, just because those classes are going to be foremost in their minds. In the last month, patch after patch has gone by with lots of class tweaking. Some have had people up in arms, some have been nice changes, and with each patch the Shaman board breathes a sigh of relief that the Shaman wasn't nerfed again, but it seems to me that actually nothing has really changed at all in the Shaman class, for quite a while. I think this is a serious problem that Verant needs to address. Each class needs to be heard and understood. On the whole, the Shamans are a good class, but there are many serious problems which at least should be investigated by Verant. Disease line, high debuff resists, lack of usefulness of stat buffs are examples of the recent concerns on the Soerbaird shaman board. I think the Shaman community would be less troubled if at least we knew we were being listened to, and that someone at Verant was in the Shaman's corner. With the Alchemy events, we see that exactly the opposite is true. We are not of importance to the developers at Verant. This is not acceptable and needs to be changed. Thanks for your time.

Jula 24 Shaman Prexus
