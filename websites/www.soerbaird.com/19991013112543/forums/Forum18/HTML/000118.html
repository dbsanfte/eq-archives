The Squad Ring is magic ring with 1 charge of Heal (80hp). It is obtained through a Rivervale quest which involves getting your faction with the warriors guild up to Warmly + (you need additional faction even after ‘Warmly’). I earned my Squad Ring somewhere around 12th level and now at 18th I’m completely addicted to it. Based on last few levels, I’ll be addicted to it even when we get Heal for 80hp at 19th.

Several things about the Squad ring make it addictive. You don’t need to wear it on a finger for it to work. Any top level inventory slot will do. You can invoke the Heal spell when moving and without fizzle. You may even be able to cast on another PC, I’ve never tried, but it only works when you target yourself and when I’ve forgotten and was still targeting the MOB, it did not heal me. (and I died )

I’m mostly solo due to limited play time and significant AFK, and the Squad ring is my magic rabbit. Typically I’ve engaged a mob, Dot’ed, DD, and am down to melee when I realize that I’m going to fall below 2 bubs of health and start to slow down. With the Squad ring charged, I can wait until I’m at 1 bub of health before I must turn to flee for the zone if the MOB is winning. As I run, I target myself and use the ring by right clicking on it. The heal usually brings me up above 2 bubs and full speed with SOW. This allows me to pull away from the mob and reach the zone.

Although the little warriors love me, the clerics are apprehensive, so I can’t get the ring recharged. However, I can repeat the magic words ‘am I one with the wall?’ to the Marshal and receive ANOTHER, charged squad ring. This is very handy for deaths, as I’m mostly bound in Rivervale due to this addiction and can pick up another LORE, NO DROP ring before I run back to my body. When I loot my corpse, the old, used ring disappears when I try to pick it up. If I’m just getting a charged ring, I’ll destroy the used ring before I ask for a new one.

***Spoiler***
Completing this quest can be time consuming, but the Goblins in Misty give you the correct faction and there are many quests to raise warrior guild faction in Rivervale. One quest (Leatherfoot Raiders) involves bringing back PB skins from Everfrost. This is easy if you have SOW and are bound in Rivervale. Every3rd PB skins pays off with 3 gp +, so you could even buy them and break even.

Morgen spent 3 levels in Misty Thicket, becoming one with the Wall.

You can target another person, I've done it on a party member and on a player not in our party.

You can do it on the run, on the jump, tumbling down hill, stunned, sinking into the depths of the deep blue sea. 0 casting time, target, click and heal!

This is one sweet sweet item. Orcs in the commonlands help a lot, gather up those slashed belts.

Once your shaman gets 9th, leave Halas for Misty Thicket. Good place to level, another fun new land to explore, many small quests to do (Pirana hunt!) Black bears and young kodiaks drop the prized HQ bear hides, giant wasps drop the occasional venom sack, and you can stick 6 honeycombs in the bank for the wandering high level type to buy so they can do the glowing stein quest. Just don't get too greedy or they will go get their own, easy enough to do.

Warriors probably would be better off staying in Black Burrow, better loot once you can take down elites and sit in on a HS room group. Much better fights, gnolls are prime targets, great way to max out your skills.

Either way, starting faction is easy, just kill many gobbos, the shattered beads are worthless, destroy them, keep the RunnyEye warbeads.
