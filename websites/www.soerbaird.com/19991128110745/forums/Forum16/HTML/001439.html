Wizards are a rarer, higher level spawn, that appear further in than the Flag Room. THey pop in the Preacher, Archeo, and outside the Alchemist regions. Can spawn in the Throne room. You need to simply slaughter the spawn until they pop.

Oh, they will appear in lieu of the Preacher sometimes, and at about a 1/3 rate. So get a gang together, smash the Preacher room, and get some good loot, too.
