So ye have decided to be a ranger and ye are a wood elf. Listen to my words and ye shall be a strong ranger.

Things you should know:

-Wolves are your friend, kill them if you wish but when you go to Qeynos do not touch wolves or bears because certain npc's will attack you if you do

-Buy a bow when you have money to spare and get into fletching at level 3

-Don't put training points into 1h slash at level 3

-You can not take Dvinn on till level 21 -Sell/buy at ranger's guild only, all the merchants are mean

-Go straight for leather, patchwork is a waste - go straight for leather and instead of leather wristbands go for legionnaire bracers they sell around 2 for 1pp.

First I would recommend going to your guild it should be around your starting area. Just turn around and find Faydark champions. Give the note to Maeslyn Trueshot. After that is done put on your tunic. Train with your guild master. Put everything into 1h slash and find your way around till you get to a lift.

Start tracking and hunting Giant wasp drones, decaying skeletons, and bats. As soon as you hit level 2 hunt orc pawns and pixie tricksters. Do not, I repeat do not hunt orc pawns with weapons until level 3. Keep doing this till level 5 then track some orc centurions until level 8 or so.

That is it for now when I reach level 9 I will continue on this guide.
