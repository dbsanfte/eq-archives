Enchanter Epic Quest

Part One
 Jeb Lumsed is in the form of a Sarnak Imitator in the Burning Woods.
In Erudin hail Stofo Olan..
The Ink of the Dark -
In Qeynos, talk to Reania Jukle
You recieve an Empty Ink Vial.
If you charm the Ghoul Scribe in Lower Guk, and hand him the vial, you will recieve the Ink of the Dark..

A Mechanical Pen -
In Akanon talk to Rilgor Plegnog 
You give Rilgor Plegnog a Shiny Metallic Robe.
You recieve A Mechanical Pen.

A White Paper -
In West Karana, talk to Chrisln Baker
You turn in the Paper and Quill.
You receive a Bandit Sash.
Receiving the sash will spawn Thracken Griften, a level 55 very agressive Bandit at around loc 800,-11570. You need to kill him to get the white paper.

Turn in all three pieces to recieve the Copy of Notes.

You give the “Copy of Notes” to Jeb Lumsed.
You receive a coin called Jeb's Seal.

Say 'I need a sack' to the Sarnak Intimidator which is Jeb.
You receive an enchanter's bag.

Part Two
Test of Illusions -
Modani Qu'Loni is the master of Illusion. He can be found in The Overthere, in the form of a Scorpis..
You give Modani Qu'Loni Jeb's Seal.
You receive an Enchanter's Bag.

Joren Nobleheart in Felwithe.
Turn in the head of the Prince from Chardok, you will be rewarded with the Chalice of Kings.

Vessel Drozlin in East Cabilis.  Kill him.  Loot Xolion Rod.

Bozlum in Oggok a pair of Large Muddy Sandals.She will give you a Scribbled Parchment which you need to take to Brokk Boxtripper, a merchant located outside Bozlum's caves..  Brokk will then give you a Gift to Bozlum. Return that gift to her and she will give you Snow Blossoms.

Verina Tomb in Neriak 3rd Gate..  Kill her and loot Innoruuk's Word.

You combine the four items in the bag.
You hand Modani Qu'Loni the combined item..
You receive a piece of the Staff of the Shissar.

Part Three
Test of Enlightenment -
Mizzle Gepple (aka, Clockwork VIIX) is the master of enlightened magic, located in Ak'Anon.
Find Cazel in the Oasis Of Marr.  Kill him and loot The Spoon.
The Key can be found on a Mesa in the canyons of The Overthere..
The Scroll can be found in the Crypt of Dalnir behind a bed..
The book is on a table in the plane of Sky..

Combine these items to make You receive A Sack for Mizzle.
You give A Sack for Mizzle to Clockwork VIIX.
You receive the 2nd piece of staff.

Part Four
Test of Charm -
Nadia Starfeast in Fironia Vie.
You receive an Enchanter's Bag..
The diamond is given to the Spectral Librarian in Kaesora..
The Sapphire is given to Faelia Goldenwing, a named Chromodrac in Skyfire..
The Ruby is given to the Wraith of Jaxion, who is found in the City of Mist, near the Lord room..
The emerald is given to Impaler Tzilug, a named scorpion in the Overthere..
Once again, remember to combine the items in the bag provided, and then hand in the bag.
Recieve 3rd Piece of Staff.

Part Five
 Test of Phantasm -
The Wraith of the Shissir spawns in the plane of Fear. It seems to live near the temple, and will appear randomly, every three days or so..
Kinde's Ghost can be found deep, deep in the Hole..
Forsaken Revenants, both male and female can drop the Essense, though it is very rare..
The Tangrin is found in the Field of Bone..

All four pieces need to be combined in the sack which Polzin provides..
Recieve 4th Piece of Staff.

Combine the 4 items in the sack provided to recieve the Staff of the Serpent..
