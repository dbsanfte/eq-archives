Those interested in becoming a member of EDoN should do the following.  Thank you for your cooperation in this matter.


1.  Read through this web site, paying special attention to the Charter, Rules, and Raiding sections.
2.  Contact a member of EDoN in game and ask any important questions you may have about the guild.
3.  Go to our Message Board and post in the New Members Section of your interest to join.
4.  Send an e-mail to the Venerable Fillie Truespirit which includes your character's name, level, class, and race.


If you follow these four simple steps I am sure you will be invited soon.  Remember once you are invited, you have a probational period of 30 days.  In this time, you can see whether EDoN is for you, and we can see if you are for us.  Thank you!
