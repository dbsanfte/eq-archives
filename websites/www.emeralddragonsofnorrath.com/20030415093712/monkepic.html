Monk Epic Quest

Part One
Tomekeeper Danl in Erudin.
The book he requires is called 'Immortals', which can be found on various named mobs in Skyfire (Guardian of Felia, A Lava Walker, A Wandering Wurm, Black Scar).
Give Tomekeeper Danl the book of Immortals.

He gives you a piece of paper called Danl's Reference.
Before continuing on, you will need to complete the following quests in this order:

Red Sash of Order (required to get the Robe of the Lost circle)
Monks of the Whistling Fists (to obtain the Robe of the Lost Circle, which is required to get the Robe of the Whistling Fists)
The Lost Circle (to obtain the Robe of the Whistling Fists, which is required in the next part of the monk epic)

Part Two
Red Sash of Order -
To get the sash, you need to retrieve the blackened Wand (dropped by Priest Amiaz who is a random (rare) spawn in the lower level of Befallen behind the secret door) and the Sapphire of Souls (aka Darkened Saphire) from a thief in Najena named Ekeros. Hand those items plus the orange sash into Reyia Beslin and get a Red Sash.
taff.

Monks of the Whistling Fists -
Brother Qwinn in Southern Karana seeks the return of the code of Zan Fi, which was stolen by him. It is dropped by Targin the Rock, a rare spawn in the Sol B throne room. Bring him the Code and a purple silent fist headband and you will get a special needle. Brother Zephyl in Rathe Mountains wants the Idol of Zan Fi, which is in the possession of Brother Raster, who spawns in the Mino Elders room in Lower Guk. Returning this Idol, plus a red sash, results in a special tailoring pattern. These two items need to be combined in a sewing kit with a swatch of shadow silk (tailored) and a Scroll of Jonathans Whistling Warsong, which is bought from the Bards Guild, to make a monk only item called the Robe of the Lost Circle..

The Lost Circle -
Brother Balatin is a skeletal monk located on a pyramid in the wizard spires of the Dreadlands.
You can get the Robe by completing the Rathe Mountain Whistling Fist Quest. The flute is split into two pieces. One is a Metal Pipe off of an Iksar Betrayer in Chardok (Identifies as Fi). The other is a Metal Pipe off of a Drolvarg Pawduster in Karnors (Identifies as Zan). Hand these three items in to Brother Balatin and he will give you the Robe of the Whistling Fists.



Part Four
Next, head to the hidden oasis in Timorous Deep. Once there, speak to Lheao..
Hand in the Robe of the Whistling Fists and Danl's Reference.

Now head to Trakanon's Teeth and find an NPC named Kaiaren. He is located at Loc -1609, -2679, in the undead ruins..

Now go out and find Kaiaren's true form, which spawned when you gave the book to the angry form. This version is also called Kaiaren, and spawns at approximately +300, +2400 in a building next to the lake. He is indifferent to all.  Hand him the book..

At this point, head on over to the Lavastorm Mountains with a group and find an imp named "A Fire Sprite" and say "I challenge Eejag." There will be a shout to the zone (if no shout, it means Eejag is already up).

Head over to the fire pit by the entrances to Sol A and Sol B. Under the middle smoke plume, about half way down, you'll see Eejag (an Iksar) swimming. He cannot be pulled so you'll have to fight him where he is -- in the lava.

Once killed, he drops a Charred Scale..
Head back to Trakanon's Teeth and hand the Charred Scale to the true form of Kaiaren.

Now head up to the Plane of Air and find an NPC named "A Presence" on Noble Dojorn's island. It is in the form of a shadowman so only the name is visible.
Hand the Charred Scale over to the Presence.

Once dead, loot the "Breath of Gwan" and go directly to the Mines of Nurga to find a sleeping ogre.
He drops Trunt's Head.

Take the head to Lake Rathe and find a 58th level dark elf named Deep who lives in the lake.
Head to the Overthere, and find Astral Projection in the bottom of the scorpion chasm. He does not need to be fought.
Hand Trunt's Head over.
He will give you an Eye of Kaiaren and then despawn.

Part Five
 Now head to the Lake of Ill Omen for the final fight of the monk epic. You can find Astral Projection (same form as the Overthere version) on the platform in the lake itself.
When you give the Eye of Kaiaren to the Astral Projection, he despawns. Deep and Vorash will then both spawn.
Xenevorash will then spawn on the platform.
He drops a Demon's Fang.

Finally, head back to Trakanon's Teeth and hand Kaiaren (either form) the Demon's Fang and the "Book of Celestial Fists."
You receive the Celestial Fists.
