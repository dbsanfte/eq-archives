Beastlord Epic Quest

Part One
Historian Sharar is in the Library of Sharl Vahl.  Hail and talk to her.
You say, 'Hail, Historian Sharar'
You say, 'Yes, I'll take the book to Dumul'

Take book to Elder Animalist Dumul.  Talk to him.
Hand Dumul the Historian's Book
You say, 'some spirits are disturbed'

From Shar Vahl you need to head North, zone to Hollowshade Moor, then Grimling Forest.
Hand a polished Acrylia Sphere to Scout Husman
The scout will march straight out and assault a camp of grimlings.
Keep the scout alive during the initial camp fight and the follow up waves of grimlings. There are about 5 Grimlings in the camp, and the waves consist of 3 to 5 grimlings a piece. Eventually the Grimling Officer will appear, kill him & loot his scalp

Hand Scout Husman the Scalp, He will give you the Copper Medal of War

Return to Dumul in Sharl Vahl. HAND Dumul the Copper Medal of War
You say, 'I am ready'
You say, 'What new magic ?'
