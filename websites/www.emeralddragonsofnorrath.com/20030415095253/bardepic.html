Bard Epic Quest

Part One
Find Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana inside Guard Tower 1 at loc (-516, -2434)
Hail Her.  You will receive the torch.
Arrive in Misty Thicket near the entrance to Runneyeye Citadel. You will find Fajio Knejo at loc (-120, -54)
Hail Him.  Receive another Torch.
Arrive in the Southern Desert of Ro near the Innothule Swamp. Find Andad Filla at loc (-3032, 1216)
Hail Him.
Arrive in Lake Rathe outside the entrance to the Arena. Find Misty Tekcihta at loc (2419, 2583)
Receive Proof of Speed ring
Arrive in West Karana at Tower 1 and talk to Konia again.
Receive Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Top..

Part Two
Braenar Swiftsong in South Karana.  Talk to him..  (-21, 88)
Say 'What Doll'
Go to Solusek A and give the gnome vendor, Marfen the invoice and you will receive a doll. Marfen is the same vendor who sells the Tinkerer's bags, he is found at loc (-304, -1083)
Take the doll to the Estate of Unrest and give the doll to Serra, who is next to the gnomish speulunker. She can be found by going through a underwater channel at the right side of the zone. Serra will give you a note.
Then take the note from Serra back to Braenar, who will give a new note for Maligar.
Take the note from Braenar to Maligar. Maligar is found in West Karana at loc (1179, -10878). His spawn is every 30 minutes or so, not bad, if you mess up (turn the head into the wrong guy), you can try again in a bit. After you give Maligar the note, he will spawn his Maligars Enraged Doppleganger.
Once you kill the doppleganger, loot his head. Unless you want to kill Maligar, you can run at this point to the zone.
Take the head back to Braenar and receive "Mahlin's Bongo's".
Take the Bongo's to Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana to get the bottom of Page 24.

Part Three
Go to South Karana and hail Kelkim Menkia, she is between the centaurs and treants along the zone line that is a "drop-off"..
The items she needs to make the strings for her lute are as follows:
Guts from a named chromatic drake in Skyfire Mountains - Eldrig the Old: about level 51 and he summons
Guts from the named red wurm in Burning Woods - drops off named wurm Nezekezena
Guts from the Huge Oynx Drake in Rathe Mountains - Blackwing: level 40 something

Kill Nezekezena in Burning Woods (neg 470, neg 2550)
Kill Blackwing in Rathe Mountains (neg 1700, neg 15)
Kill Eldrig the Old in Skyfire Mountains (pos 500, neg 4000)
Once you have all the 3 guts from these mobs, catch a ride back to Antonica. Give the three guts back to Kelkim in South Karana and you will get page 25..

Part Four
Vedico Windwisper may be found on the docks in Butcherblock inside one of the shops at loc (1010, 3010)
Find Quag Maelstrom in the Ocean of Tears on the Seafury Cyclops island (1914, -6600).
Take the horn back to Vedico in Butcherblock and she will then give you a note to give to Forpar.
Head to Steamfont Mountains and find Forpar Fizfla hanging out in one of the windmills, the second one from the druid ring. His loc is (pos 128, neg 600).
Receive Forpar's note.
Backbone of an Ancient Fishman - Phinigel Autropos's spine (Kedge Backbone) from Kedge Keep
Amalgym Tentacle - Amygdalan Tendril from Plane of Fear
Petrified skull of a Lycanthrope - Petrified werewolf skull from Karnor's Castle on Drolvarg Warlord.
Take all 3 items and the note back give to Forpar and he gives you back the Mystical Lute Head.
Say 'What next pieces?'
Large red Dragon Scale - Talendor of Skyfire, Lord Nagafen or Ragefire in Naggy's Lair, or Nortlav Scalekeeper in The Hole
Large white Dragon Scale - Gorenaire of Dreadlands or Lady Vox in Permafrost
Metal bit - 1 smithed metal bit, from smithing Tradeskill, trivial at 21
Once you have these 3 items, port over to Forpar in Steamfont.
Hand Forpar the red dragonscale, white dragonscale, and 1 metal bit.
Receive Mystical Lute Body
Finally, get the strings for the Lute - Trakanon Gut Strings. These drop from Trakanon himself (Level 65 dragon in Sebilis). There have been some recent changes to the quest. The Trakanon guts no longer drop from the regular Trakanon.
With your newly acquired Trakanon guts, head to Steamfont to meet your friendly little gnome again. Give the following lute parts to Foplar to receive the complete Mystical Lute:
Mystical Lute Head
Mystical Lute Body
Gut strings
Receive Forpar's Mystical Lute

Part Five
To get your singing short sword give following items to Baldric Slezaf back in the Dreadlands:
Top of page 24
Bottom of page 24
Page 25
Mystical lute from Foplar
He will reward you with the Singing Short Sword!
