Alchemy

Alchemy is the making of potions.  Only Shaman's can dabble in this tradeskill.  They can make anything from Illusion potions to Stat potions, to Porting potions, etc.  Below are some good links to sights that have Alchemy information on them.  Please feel free to explore them if you are interested in Alchemy, and raising your skill.


EQtraders has good information on Alchemy.  From Trivials, ingredients, and where you can get the ingredients.

Everquest Compendium has a guide on Alchemy.

Simak's Everquest Website also has a guide on Alchemy.

Trader's Workshop also has information on Alchemy
