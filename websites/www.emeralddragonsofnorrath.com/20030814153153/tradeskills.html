Tradeskills are skills that are used in trading to other players.  They are usually profitable to the person selling things made from these skills, and benefit the buyer, without the buyer having to master the skill themselves.  Mastering any skill takes Time, Patience, and Plat. Most players who have mastered one or more tradeskills are glad that they did.  Though Foraging is not exactly a tradeskill, it is a skill, and it is used in trading.  That is why it is listed.  Not all classes can master all of the above tradeskills.  For instance, only Shaman's may use Alchemy, Rangers, Druids, and Bards may use Foraging, Rogue's may use Poison, INT Casters may use Research, and Gnomes may use Tinkering.  Other than those few exceptions, the rest of the tradeskills may be mastered by all races/classes.
