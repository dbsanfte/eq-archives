Well, it's sort of a long-standing issue. For years now, there has been some heat between the two class boards. Now the shoe's on the other foot ... so the debate has picked back up again. Warriors are still stinging from their ineffectiveness in PoP. Paladins are still stinging from their Kunark days. And everyone's got a bone to pick with everyone else.

That's pretty much the long and short of it.
