Level 29, hmmm, well there are a number of places you can try. Lake of Ill Omen still gives decent experience for a few more levels. Frontier Mountains just next door to LOIO is someplace to go. You could try Iceclad in Velious, if I recall right the cougars are about the right level or maybe just a little too high. I also liked the lizard men outside Temple of Cazic Thule although getting singles isn't easy. And there is always South Karana at the aviak village, still some exp there. Also Splitpaw near the entrance. Oh, and I really liked Gunthak in LoY, in fact I'd probably recommend you try there first, probably the best place.

Thing is though, you're going to be in bad shape unless you can find at least a healer to team with. Soloing is going to be a serious struggle. And frankly I just can't suggest anywhere in the level 29 area where getting a group is going to be easy, there just aren't that many new players in the game and the players who've been playing a while tend to PL a lot.
