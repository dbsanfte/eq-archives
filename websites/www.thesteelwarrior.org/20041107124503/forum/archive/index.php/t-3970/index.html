I hate to be the one who has to play the "prick" role but seriously...

You are in a scenario where you have outlevelled your gear. You're "afraid" to level, and you don't want to raid, or at least join a raiding guild. You dont' appear to be able to press /disc evasive or /disc defensive for mobs that are apparently hitting you so hard that you can be taken out in less time than it takes for CH to land.

This game requires some things, one of which is to step off of the exp treadmill and collect gear. If that means via tradeskills, farming hill giants, or small time nameds for small chunks of plat at a time, then do it. Or get involved with a raid or something, just get some better gear, you are a damned warrior after all, you live and die by your gear, like it or hate it, it's a fact of life.

I'd suggest getting off of the treadmill and doing something worthwhile.

Go to Sol B and camp the Efreeti, you'll either get boots that can still be sold for some nice plat (focus effect and nice stats for casters with your playstyle), or a ring that will be a nice upgrade.

Save up some Adventure points if you're doing LDoN and buy some armor, it's not that expensive for some nice upgrades.

Go to Kael and get some armor, there's still people killing giants you know.

Go to Western Wastes with 5 friends and kill some dragons, some of them have some nice sellable gear and/or neck pieces.

Go to Siren's Grotto with a group, there's some nice gear dropping from there.

Hell, go to Hate and exp, you can at least get some indo armor that can be worn and then turned in for experience later.
