I just wanted to say i picked up two these two last night and i have been very impressed. Prior to that i was using a GSoT.

IMO, the procs on both of these two were getting me more aggro than a GSoT proc. I based this on the fact that sometimes, if im not MA, i can use a GSoT and when it procs, i dont automatically get aggro, unless it has a streak of 2 or 3 procs in a row.

With the copper hammer and bloodsword, both were getting me instant aggro when they procced. I tested them out in BoT and although the mobs were immune to the stun portion of the copper hammer proc, the aggro was still noticeable.

My other benchmark for aggro is whether an early proc from a weapon will keep aggro on you when slow is casted, without taunting. Frmo what i saw, both were able to do this.

taking into account the decent dps and nice stats (120 raw hps woot) plus resists and others, id def say these two would be hard to beat for the price - i picked both up for total of 30k.

I will probably use the GSoT still for exp grinds for the added dps, but im happy that i now have a decent set of DW aggro proccers until i get my epic and another EB wep.
