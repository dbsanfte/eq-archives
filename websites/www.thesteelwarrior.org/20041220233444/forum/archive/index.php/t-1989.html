Brutul
07-16-2003, 02:21 PM
Turn in a copper node, a bundle of super conductive wires, and an intact power cell to Nitram Anizok to start the event. Follow him around the zone and protect him from the trash mobs that will aggro him. When you get to the dragon area, he walks up to the dragon and it becomes targetable. Dragon is slowable, hits for 1300, so he's pretty mean unslowed, but not bad after slow lands. Might lose a few tanks if you can't get slow to stick early. If you're just after the loot I think it's ok if the gnome dies after the dragon is targetable, but you won't get factory flags unless you keep him alive. Been a real long time since I did this, so somebody probably has better infoz.
