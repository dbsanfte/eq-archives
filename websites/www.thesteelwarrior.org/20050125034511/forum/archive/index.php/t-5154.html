Angel,

The quest shoulders you could get easily with 1 or maybe 2 other people are the Draconic Pauldrons. See my magelo for stats. Dependent on Skyshrine faction of course. Very easy quest to do. Get to WW, kill 4 mobs to left just outside of the tunnel, loot 4 heads, turn them in and done.

Since you are level 65, do LDoN. There are some nice drops in all the zones and the eventual points you make you can buy some upgrades. The legs in my magelo are 760 points in Everfrost for example. The North Ro camp armor is probably the best for pure melee in LDoN.

To replace your haste, try and get a soulscream belt. Save your plat for it if necessary. Depending on your servers economy, it'll cost you between 25 and 40k. That's 31% haste on your belt slot with nice stats.

Last bit of advice I would give to you is replace your rings with Valorium rings of Gallantry and your earrings with something better. Since you are on a limited budget, bloodmetal earrings of engagement are probably out since they are 20 to 30k on my server each.

Hope this gets you going in the right direction on some decent upgrades.

Mosquito Leg Earrings (ac8 sta7 hp65 dr7) have come way down on some servers. Cheaper than the Bloodmetal. There are alot of earrings these days that are nice. Earring of the Solstice (ac5 str6 sta5 wis8 int8 hp55 mana55 fr5 cr5 mr5 FT1) is still nice and easy quest.

Check out some of the bows to replace your range item. Bone Carved Shortbow (ac5 sta7 agi7 hp50 dr10) is one example.

Since you are obviously doing LDoN with all the augments you have. Go to PoK and get your Intricate Wooden Figurine and put your Adventurer's Stone on it and then do the simple quests to make your Adventurer Stone give you stats. Not much, but it is free.

Crinose Cape of the Werewolf (ac12 str7 dex7 hp80 cr12) is usually a cheap back item with great hps.

Your rings seem fine to me since you have augmented both already.

I would agree with everyone above though about your haste. The hands / waist combo (hp0 sta5) you have is terrible. Even if you don't improve your haste much, you should be able to increase hps / stats with better gloves and haste belt. Soulscream Belt is sweet but can be expensive. Do some research and check out other haste options if you have too.

Take care and have fun!

I suggest that you just get a nice group (at the least, someone to snare, slow, tank and heal), take them to veksar, and just LIVE there for a few weeks.

Soulscream belt - a very nice 31% haste belt with good stats, you won't be replacing it until you can get a 40+% haste item from a raid.

Kylong Darkmail Gauntlets - SEXY SEXY gloves, plenty of STA, AC and HPs.

Mithril Spiked Collar - Good AC/STA/HP neck item

Those are just a few examples. And thats just stuff you can use yourself, Veksar is filled TO THE BRIM with named mobs that drop loot that EVERY class can use. Even if the most popular named spawns are camped, nobles, highborns, and highborn commanders (which there are entire rooms of spread across veksar) have chances to drop nice loot too, so unless theres 90 people in veksar camping every square inch of the zone, you should be able to find some nice spot to sit down in and kill.

I also suggest you work on skyshrine faction - theres two quests there that will yield EASY upgrades. Look on allakhazam for "Draconic Pauldrons" and "Bracer of Benevolence".

The rest of the upgrades I can suggest I wear myself, so if you want to, check my magelo alternate profile "Upgrades (soonish)" If you see stuff you like, look it up on allakhazam to see how to craft it or where to camp it. Paying for items isn't your only option, you know. :P
