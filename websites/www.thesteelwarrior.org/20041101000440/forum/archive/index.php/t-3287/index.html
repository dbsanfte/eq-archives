Kiya
My suggestions, this will likely require faction work or plat farming. ;)

Boots: Brellium would be a sturdy set to last you a while, or thurg/skyshrine, if you want to hunt or do MQ's.
Bracers: Again, Brelliums will last you a long while. Or you could quest bracers of benevolence, if ya tend to have plat problems, like me, hehe.
Back: I would start saving for a better cloak, be it hierophants, brellium or the greater pernicity quested one.
Ears: Electrum black sappires (2ac/35hp) or solstice earring quest shouldn't be too hard to come by.
As for weapons, until you are of level for the GSoT, a frostbringer will do a decent job.
