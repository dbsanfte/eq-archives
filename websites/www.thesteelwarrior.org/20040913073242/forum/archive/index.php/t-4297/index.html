Wait, I figured it out, this is about the STA post. At some levels, the amount of hitpoints you get per STA goes up. If you get STA AAs before you level, your hps will indeed go up more than if you didn't. This isn't a trick though, if you get your STA AAs after you level, you will have the same hps. You will get more hps when you train STA than you would have if you trained them before you leveled.

I can see what made you believe you were getting extra hitpoints, but it doesn't really matter when you get the STA AAs, you will wind up with the same total hitpoints. Hope this helps.
