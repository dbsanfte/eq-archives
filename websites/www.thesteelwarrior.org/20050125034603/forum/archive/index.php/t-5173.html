Just wanted to start a thread on this topic.
Currently in Beta the Tribute system equates to buff bots.
I know this idea has popped up here before, with mixed emotions.
Based on what I have been able to play with so far, you give items/pp via a UI with the Tribute master in your home town(read out of the way places) and are able to select a number of different effects, like hp regen, atk buffs, and other effects.
Their are various lvls of these buffs, like 5 ranks, and they scale not on the amount given, but based on char level.
The effects don't take up buff slots, but each one has a timer accociated with it. Currently they are aruond ten minutes in duration. You can spend that just run back and forth between your home town and your hunting grounds.
The upkeep is about 200pp for a full set of these buffs for 10 minutes.
I'll play around with it some more, to determine their full effects.
