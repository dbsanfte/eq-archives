BUT, the good news is, with your new mitigation ... and your healing will disc ... and your unlinked discs ... you actually have a shot at doing some interesting farming.

Tradeskill items are good. Spider Silks are quite popular. Or LQ hides for making padding. Or make the padding/silk swatches and sell them.

You can farm coin. Killing dark elf guards inside neriak was always popular back in the day. Or dorf guards in butcherblock.

Or just go and rape a lower level zone (Sol A, Najena, Runnyeye, whatever).

Or you can hit some LoY zones (Dulak's Harbor with the boats are quite the popular spot) and farm raw coin/gems.

Sebilis is still a spot to snag some coin.

Just tossing out ideas.

I hate farming money, so don't do it often.

Good luck. Have fun.

My little secret is Permafrost. Usually get about 50ish pp and a pile of gp per clearing of the gobbies. Tares Lichen and Snowfall Algae sell to vendor for 6gp each and are stackable, wt 0. Plus a few 20-200pp bazaar items and some bronze/FS/ringmail, minor gems, research crap etc.
But the money maker is permafrost crystals, which sell in bazaar for 40-50pp each and I get about a stack per hour. There used to be a decent market for Ice Goblin Blood too, but I think MLers in Miragul's are holding it out, because the market is flooded now.
