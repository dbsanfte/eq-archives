Nonosassy
Hello, my name is Nonosassy and I am a Warrior on the Rallos Zek server. I know most of you are not on PvP servers, and I'm hoping to start a conversation for us PvP warriors.

Up till present, Warriors have not been a PvP class at all, due to our lack of any PvP oriented tools. Most of us have learned to cope and can do quite well, but for me atleast - my edge used to be my invulnerability. I could take quite the beating, resist many spells, take small melee damage and dish out a melee thrashing.

The latest change to our PvP AC (which is now 80% effective, so a 2000ac warrior only would have an effective 1600ac) has hit us HARD. 400 AC loss is significant for this fellow, and I'm sure you've all noticed a big difference in getting hit as well.

With the resist changes, our inherant class MR advantage is basically useless. Well, lets face it, 33 extra MR wasn't a boon to us since Vellious came along - and it's effectiveness has creeped down ever since. This was one of our special class "tools" we were given; and while all other classes tools have grown and been improved upon - this particular one of ours has been rendered insignificant.

While the melee range has been increased (supposidely a significant change, and I do notice a little difference), I -atleast- have still been plagued with "Out of range" messages when the person appears to be right infront of you... extremely noticeable when fighting a bard for instance. I am sure this is purely a lag issue, but it does have a significant measure of importance in PvP for all melee classes.



Now what would you other PvP warriors like to see?


I for instance, have decided that our Incite line should have some sort of PvP usefulness. I figured that a 1 second unresistable stun would do a world of help to our PvP state. I do not believe it would be an over-powered ability, given that 30 second reuse for 1 second of stun wouldn't be enough to significantly alter any fight... given our non-existant PvP tools, it would be an incredible increase of power for us.
