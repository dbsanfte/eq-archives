Below is a full (but a little incomplete) guide to all the named traps in earth.

Every named (Save trees and apparently Glimmerstone) has 5 traps associated with it. Every "type" of trap will spawn either the named or PH every 5th time a trap of that type is tripped. Ie. if you trip all five traps of one type you will get a named or a ph, if you trip only one trap, but trip it over five respawns, you will get a named or ph. Any combination of traps and trips that adds up to five will net one named or ph.

Here's a map showing all the trap locations, and what you can expect so spawn from it.

http://home.cogeco.ca/~eqhost/Bigrr/earthtraps.jpg
