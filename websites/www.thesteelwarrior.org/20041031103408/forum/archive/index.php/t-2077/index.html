I think I know what he is referring to.

Used to be a program called Copeland's Weapon Analyzer. Not a bad little piece actually. It allowed for class (melee types), level, base STR, Haste item, and what weapon(s) you wanted to see. Gave you Weapon info, dmg, dly, max, mode, mean, and swings per minute. Showed you average damage per minute as well. Also gave you the ability to add weapons to the database.
Alas, the last version I saw was 1.85. I think some of the damage factors for 2-handed weapons have changed, so it is no longer accurate. Still, was a neat little program in all.
