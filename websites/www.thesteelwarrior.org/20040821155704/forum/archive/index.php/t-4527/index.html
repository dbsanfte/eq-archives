Skills - Disciplines - Warrior Advancement

[b] Explanation of Each Category

Skills - Add New Damage/Hate, Defensive or Utility type skills
- Upgrade Old Skills (Kick, Taunt, Disarm etc.. )
- Make a New Skill system (skills that take account of stamina, stats etc...)
- Advance the warrior's damage/defensive capability through new passive skills.

Disciplines - Add New Disciplines at certain levels, or in the form of "quests".
- Upgrade the Old Disciplines, changing duration and reuse times.
- Upgrade the Discipline Mechanics, changing way timers are linked etc...
- Add new forms of Disciplines like Auras or Stances etc...

Advancement - Heighten the reward Warriors get from Items. (hp/ac bonuses, ways to use mana etc...)
- Upgrade the equipment with Melee Focuses, Effects or New sorts of Effects etc....
- Make new Items Warriors get through quests and drops.
- Advance the Damage Tables.
- Upgrade the Warrior's Status(Mitigation, HP, AC, STA etc...)
- Bring in New caps to skills (Taunt, Kick, Parry etc...) as well as Specialisations.
