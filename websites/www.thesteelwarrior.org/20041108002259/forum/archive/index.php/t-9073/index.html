There's a little known feature at The Steel Warrior that you can make use of. You can press shift+T to call up a list of optional titles. They're based on your post count, your verbal level, and the amount of damage you've inflicted in The Outhouse.

At under 100 posts, your options are limited. I think you'll find "Board Troll" and "Frickin Noob" as the only alternatives to "Registered User."

Once you blow through 400 posts, you'll find titles there like "Commentator", "Expert Parser", and "I <3 EQ".

If you read all of this thread (http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?t=8375&page18&pp=15), then you have access to the epic level titles.
