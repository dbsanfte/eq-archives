Continue your epic journey!

Follow the Wayfarers Brotherhood to the war-ravaged Realm of Discord to help end the tyrannical ways of the Muramites once and for all. Mata Muram, the leader of the Muramites, is rumored to be building the ultimate invading army. It's up to you to thwart his plans.

Experience EverQuest: Omens of War and the twisted lands of a realm where war is persistent and death is constant. Face the fear of the Battlegrounds of Discord. Explore the shattered landscapes of the Discordant Plateau. Then take your fury to a landscape that teems with Muramites preparing to invade not just Norrath, but countless innocent worlds. Adventure solo, or in groups and raids to complete "epic quests" and earn prestigious weapons.


Features:

Improved Graphics - New zones with detailed environments featuring enhanced graphical elements, including spell effects and creatures to fight.

Level Cap Increase - Develop your character beyond level 65!

Epic Weapons 2.0 - Specialized, hand-tailored Epic Weapons if you Complete one of any 16 high-level epic quests.

Guild Tribute System - Enhanced tribute system allows you to turn your loot into favor points for your whole guild to enjoy.

Low Level Task System - Request quick and easy tasks to gain experience and collect cool loot.

Custom PC Titles - Exciting new Custom PC Titles for your characters. Do a variety of adventures and get bestowed a title added to your character's name.

All-New Content - New disciplines, AA's, items, spells, quests and tradeskills continue to make EverQuest the most-compelling and persistent online world ever created.

I think the problem is that their desperation is leading to decisions that will negatively impact the game of Everquest. This, in turn, will lead to more account attrition.

They should realize that it is inevitable that new games will take away some of their playerbase and should plan accordingly. The best way for them to deal with that, in my opinion, is to fix the current issues people have with Everquest to make what already exists more stable and more enjoyable to play.

I also think that eventually any game becomes boring, and that people are going to leave it for something new. More and more content is not going to get the upper tier players that have completed all the content to stay around forever. Everquest's best chance for longevity is to attract new players, people who haven't experienced the game yet, and the best way to do that is to make the game more user friendly, which they have done. Making it larger and larger and the max level higher and higher is not going to attract new players, it is going to deter them.
