Arathena
recently my frined told me that for the CoF and CCoW, you have to be lvl 35+ to be the benefit of its haste effect. Id this true?

This is false, as far as I know. Because of the way the EQ spell effect engine works, all effects must have an associated 'caster level'. In the case of worn items, this is a static number, attached to the item. The spell 'Haste', then, is calculated on you as if cast upon you by a person of the level attached to the item. The CoF has a caster level of 35, and, because of the way the haste spell is written, it will give you caster level + 1% haste, or 36%, no matter what level you are. The FBSS has a caster level of 20, thus giving 21% haste. A Belt of the Destroyer has a caster level of 40, thus giving 41% haste, at leve 1, or level 65.

If the effect is not in the red, as on a recommended item, you are getting the full benefit of the item.
