07-13-2004, 01:45 PM
If you are restricting yourself to purchased armor, then Farwater is the end of the line. Tak LDON armor is slightly better than Farwater overall, but you'll be earning points slowly until you get near 65, so that's not a short term solution.

There is a full suit of plate armor spread amongst the front 10 GoD zones on single-group encounters. This armor is about as good as the LDON suit overall, some pieces are better and some are slightly worse. This is the best non-raid loot armor that you can obtain at this time.
