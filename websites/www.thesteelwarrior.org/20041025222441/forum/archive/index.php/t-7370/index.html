Tiejinnie
There was a similar question posed about 1 week + ago on GoD tanking.

This is a version of it:

1. Natimbi - Qinimi - Riwwi are relatively easy, the mobs hit for 400+ max (Riwwi).
2. Barindu - Ferubi are a bit harder, mobs hit from 500+ to 1k+
3. Vxed - Tipt - Kod`Taz mobs hit 1k+ often

The recent retuning patch did lower the difficulty level of the mobs, as they hit for max-dmg a lot less now. However, they still do hit for max dmg, so if nothing else, this has made for a more spikey pattern in the damage from the mobs, making it hard for healers.

Considering a full 6-person Group:
For (1), I'd guess 6 - 7k buffed hp, 1.3 - 1.4k AC is enough
For (2), I'd guess 8k buffed hp, 1.5 - 1.6k AC is enough
For (3), I'd recommend 9k buffed hp, 1.65 - 1.7k AC minimum.

The above are assuming full defensive AAs btw. Although they do attack for max dmg a lot less now, you'd still want to avoid or mitigate as much dmg as possible, since you have to factor in the Chaos Claws proc, as well as the spikey dmg pattern.

Based on your Magelo, I'd put you between (1) and (2), so that means tanking in Riwwi, Barindu should be no problem. Ferubi could be a good testing ground to see how you stack against the mobs there. Sewers, Vxed, Tipt are a definite no-no at your stage.
