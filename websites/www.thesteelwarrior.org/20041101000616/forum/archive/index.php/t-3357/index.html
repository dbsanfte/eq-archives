At 65 the dmg bonus is 13, so lets say you have a 16 dmg weapon mainhand. Max hit is 95(108), Min hit is 1(14). The numbers in the brackets are the real numbers you get with a dmg bonus, non bracket numbers are what you'd get on a normal hit without the bonus (so like you would with your offhand)

So basically its Normal hit + dmg bonus = total. If you are fighting a mob with really high ac or one with an ATK debuff(Creator in ssra comes to mind), you'll notice a lot of 14's and 1's spamming in your hits window. This is because you are getting the minimum hit in your mainhand as well as your offhand(offhand has no bonus). You can really notice the dmg bonus in fights like those.
