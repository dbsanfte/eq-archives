Dulak's Harbor, start with the entry mobs, then try the roaming guards around the first warehouses that you see (the warehouses have more mobs like them), then try the boats in order of difficulty. Eventually you'll find your comfort spot. This zone can keep you going into the early 50's, at which point Torigiran and Nadox are right next door.

Dulak certainly, and for more than just XP.

While you're there, help your shaman partner get a drop he needs for his Stocism quest, the empty flask. Drops rarely off "a drunken pirate" mobs, who spawn along the row of houses you see on the right once you get out of the entry tunnel.

Both of you will be VERY happy you did so. Stoicism is a damn powerful healing spell at 44.
