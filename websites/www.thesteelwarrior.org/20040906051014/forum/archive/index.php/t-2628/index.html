Do this ring event to open the floor to his area

Life and Death Event (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2559)

The Life and Death event occurs at N300 P350
The floor opens at N280 P550

Map (http://maps.eq-toolbox.com/images/Acrylia_1.png)

Have fun.
