I haven't seen any of those guys around, Wirmy... sorry to say, a lot of the old blood has up and gone, either for good or to Shadowbane (poor bastards).

Anyway, revamps in mechanics.... Number 1, spells cant kill you in a single shot. No spell is allowed to do more than 75 percent of your max HP, so mana burn cant insta gib from full any more.

Second, bows do 66 percent to players, which sucks for rangers at lower levels, but at higher lvls they can still kick a whole lot of ass.

MOST IMPORTANTLY, MR has been nerfed A LOT, so while 120 mr might have made you invincible before now it's gonna take about double that.

A lot of other things have changed in mechanics, but off the top of my head those are what come to mind....


In terms of the community, a LOT has changed. The server is no longer PK, it's primarily an anti server, as all the major power has solidified in top-end anti guilds which have sort of set the precedent. The higher lvl you go the more anti the server gets, as most PKs are unguilded and from lvl 14-30ish. What you end up seeing is isolated areas of PvP and then overall blue-server type existance, since there really aren't even guildwars between the anti guilds because they are too "anti" to fight each other.

Which, of course, makes the PKs and PK guilds even more important these days. It's still dangerous as hell to go anywhere, but there's very little power to do anything to the top guilds at all... which is pretty damn lame, when you think about it. Every once in a while the Curse or someone shows up and causes some problems during a Ringwar or whatnot, but there just aren't enough GROUPS of PK faction to really do much to shake the server up. It's kinda sad, really, and we're working to change things a bit, because that's really something the server lacks right now (a difinitive evil faction).

Anyway, GNOMES WILL RISE ABOVE!
