Gorlab
I'll know more after talent wipes go in so I can try out all the talent trees but so far here are my impressions.

Protection tree is not "needed" but then again the end game isn't in yet. Prot warriors do tank noticably better than non prot spec'd warriors. I full expect to want 25-30 points in here on release.

Arms has many people excited but really its not that great IMO. I am in the minority here mind you! Mortal Strike is currently overpowered and the concensus even among warriors is that it needs and will get the nerf bat. If you ignore that ability then tactical mastery is about the main thing in arms and that requires 20pts + 5 in it to max it. However if you look at the 20 points you spend to get its prereqs they suck.
You can spend 5 in a weapon spec which isn't bad.
Improved strike & rend are 6 points together, but only in battle stance. You spend most of your group time in defensive stance (unless you're crazy which many warriors are). So you could swap to battle in order to rend the mob and swap back but is it worth 6 talent?
Charge gives more rage but you really dont use this terribly much in instances and its only the 1st mob even when you do use it.
Hamstring is plenty slow enough so no need to up it...maybe for PvP but Im a PvE'er so I don't care about that.
Thunderclap is a junk skill atm, and spending talent to raise the damage still wouldn't make it useable.
Adding daze (basically snare) to pummel is useless as you have hamstring (real snare) in that stance and you use pummel to interrupt spells.
So with all that said and done you end up spending talent points in areas that aren't terribly good at all in order to get TM...the main use of which is ability to snare in defensive stance. I'd rather save the points and have someone else snare!

Fury now is quite good, unbridled wrath is my favorite talent of all ... it doesn't seem like much but it really does up your rage generation a lot...in all stances. Enrage, flurry, cruelty all up your dps nicely in all stances. And demoralizing is a nice shout again for all stances. So I'm currently leaning towards 25 points in fury and the rest in protection on release.
