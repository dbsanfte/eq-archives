the ice mobs sometimes cast <Frost Touch>, and the fire mobs cast <Pyrilien Charm> (only seen Riftseeker cast this...), even the trash sometimes cast it (specially ice mobs). There are 3 ways to avoid it from easiest to hardest


#1 have a bard for resist songs :P
#2 have clicky buffs as your first couple slots, when you pull him click those buffs off and have classes that can dispell cast on you when you're mezzed to debuff the <Frost Touch>. Good idea for everyone in group to do this. May not work first try, thus have multiple clickies and have your needed buffs you want way down the last slots. As soon as the group sees "A xxxxx guard begins to cast <Frost Touch>, have a dispeller cast Cancel Magic (1buff dispell) if you dont got good resists.
#3 have an offtank (good idea to have one) tank it while you're getting dispelled
#4 have enough resists from your gear (350CR+ to resist)
...
have a paladin tank them mezzers while you are mezzed via /disc Sanctification (resist *most* harmful spells for short time)
... or have him watch the fight to rez the group if it wipes, not like he will speed up the kills anyway (necros are GODS here too for summon/rez). Good thing is, the mez has a Feign Death effect so once mez wears off on your pull, you can try again (hopefully no one agros the mob to your cleric whos mez is wearing off!)
Ive stood right next to a mezzer and his mez wore off and just ran away with no agro.
