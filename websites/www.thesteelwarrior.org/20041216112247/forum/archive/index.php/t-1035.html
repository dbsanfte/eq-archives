As for copying the paladin boards, here's the deal with that.

The Overlords Sanctuary always existed. However, access was given to those who obviously played the higher end game, and knowledge of the forum travelled around. I would get PMs asking for access from guildmates of people with access, etc.

However, the posts in it were lacking. There wasn't enough traffic. In trying to increase the traffic there, It was suggested to make it public. I figured if we made it public, it would ruin the entire point of it..but Ainmer showed me the paladins board, and said that if we made it open - but heavily moderated it like they did, we could increase the posting and conversation in there without the downsides. It also gives lower players an area to read to get the information they will need eventually.

So - that's what happened. We didn't "copy" the paladins board per se, we simply made a forum public that was private before.
