Want to fix warrior exp group aggro and not trivialize raid encounters?

Give us an /aggro disc that works like voice of thule (but stacks, so we don't take away SK utility) and has a toggle (like they wanted to give paladins with slay undead). And while we have that active, we can't use any other disc.

Easy enough... can't use it while tanking anything that requires defensive, so it shouldn't cause any balance issues on high end content. And knight's always say that we shouldn't get better aggro control because we have /disc defensive and that makes us much better tanks. Well, this forces us to choose between better aggro control or our famous disc.

Unlike many other suggestions I've read, this on also has the benefit of not causing ping-pong between two warriors, because this is something we can control, rather than "always on."

If you don't want it on a toggle, then make it a 60 second disc with an 80 second reuse timer.

We still won't have "snap aggro" but it WILL improve our initial DPS aggro while we are waiting for a proc.
