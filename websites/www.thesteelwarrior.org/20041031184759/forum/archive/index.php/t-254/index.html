Undafut
Still can get several, definitely should have Lull; high level mezzes are lovely to have. The Thul Tae Ew Adept often drops zilch.

Best place to set up camp if you want to keep that first corner monopolized is clear to it, and go halfway between it and the next corner. You can keep both corners open easily with one solid group. 2 good groups can do laps.

Each corner is a static spawn of 3 or 4 mobs, between each corner is a wanderer. There are mobs on a ramp that begins on the north face of the pyramid and goes counter clockwise; the NW corner spawns are close to 3 spawns on the pyramid, and you often will end up with friends. Fully spawned there are an additional 1 or 2 wanderers that make laps around the pyramid. Those 2 are a fairly long respawn, tho. Once you clear them, you won't have to worry about them for a long time.
