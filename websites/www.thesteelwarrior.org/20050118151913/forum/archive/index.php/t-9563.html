Just a quick and dirty once over.

Get rid of the obsidian bead hoop! Camp/buy another mosquito leg earring or make a solstice earring!
You could do some north ro LDoNs to pick up a new necklace, amulet of mummy wrappings, also, it should not be too expensive in the bazaar if you don't enjoy doing LDoNs. This will net you a couple more ac and more hp.

There ya be, each suggestion easily under 20k, if your server's prices are anything like Torv's (i.e. running stupidly high).

My first suggestion would be to stop dissing people who suggest upgrades for you.
A 51 AC BP may "stink" but it is better than than the 36 AC BP magelo says you're wearing. Cultural BPs are not the strongest cultural slot, but when you can find them they are fast, easy and cheap to get. the 15 AC bump you get will be worth the marginal loss of total hitpoints the first time you're off-tanking something and it takes a swing at you.
You mention selling a soulscream belt, but there's none in your Magelo and in fact I gather that if you had one you sold it and you're using tribute haste, since I saw no other haste on you before I got too irritated to keep looking. Depending on your raid schedule/loot that might be affordable for you, but it is an unusual choice for someone with a 2AC/3AGI earring and a sub-cultural bracer. And no, you are not better off with the 19 AC 15 hp 2-resists 15 END bladesman's bracer than you would be with the underfoot bracer at:
AC: 25
WIS: +5 **HP: +30 **MANA: +30 **Endurance: +30 **
SV FIRE: +5 **SV DISEASE: +5 **SV COLD: +5 **SV MAGIC: +5 **
SV POISON: +5 **
Recommended level of 45
WT: 3.0 **Size: LARGE
Class: WAR CLR PAL
Race: HEF DWF HFL GNM
Deity: Brell Serilis

If you get an ornate bracer, that's great, but the Underfoot is better than what you have now on the magelo.
Kiya's right to pick up on the earring, too, as it's about your weakest item. I'll take your word on the idea that a raid drop upgrade is going to happen, but why wait even an hour? Buy something droppable and re-sell it if you get better. Maybe the tribute haste is doable for you, but i'd be surprised if you weren't better off looting a soulscream or a spiked seahorse yourself and using the items you're tributing to buy visible armor upgrades.
Given that your primary role for some time in raids is dps/offtank, get a dd aug for your dps weapon. LDoN icicle strike or some such. then get a Yeksha or Anger III aug for your GSoT incase you have to MT outside of raids or hold aggro while steamroll tanking on raids.
