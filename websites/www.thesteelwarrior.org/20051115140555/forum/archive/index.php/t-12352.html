Get somebody with the DM north key to open the door for you. Keep in mind I havn't been there in a little while so this won't be 100% accurate.

Anyways, I think there will be a mob directly infront of the hallway you enter in, go ahead and pull him. Then I generally wait for the patrol that is coming from the left. After the first mob and the patrol are gone go right and kill the first two mobs by the teepee looking thing. Then you've got 2 more ogers as you go left on the rampart thingy and kill some bugs. Keep going and you'll end up like exactly across the courtyard from where you entered at. The guard that drops the hedgecutter is directly down there and is easily a single pull.

Kinda rambled I know but I hope it was of some help, Aka
