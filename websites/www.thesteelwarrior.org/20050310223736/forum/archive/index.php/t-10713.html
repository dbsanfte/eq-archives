On my server Morris-Thule, EQ2 and WoW did make an impact on the server population.

Right after a couple of raiding guilds folded.

However now, every raiding guild that stuck it out, has moved on progressionwise., things have been getting alot better. To me EQ is still alive, thriving and fun, worth the time invested still.

For flags, there was a recent addition of a way to get flags for PoP. When you kill an elemental god (like FR), when you hail PP, it will give you the flag to the zone. Same thing with time now (which can be 85/15'd into). So if you join a guild that is capable with current flags to kill the big mobs, it can backflag its members this way.

You are lucky, did some of the grind from 65-70 already.

As for your sidenote. In Oow you have the option of doing WoS, MPG then RSS (after you ding 69) for exp and equipment. My general rule for GoD + expansions, is if you want to get upgrades, go to the hardest lvl that you can handle.

With DoN, there is a guildlobby where guilds that are recruiting can post what classes / lvls / AAs they require, good way to find what guilds are open for you .. chances are most raiding guilds are reqruiting since population is abit down.

GL, welcome back from iraq and hope you have fun in EQ
