Daemonwynd
For what reason would you not use DI in groups?

/boggle... the real question is why WOULD you use DI in groups.

DI is a FIVE HUNDRED MANA spell. It only lasts ten minutes. Not only that, it has a rather significant component cost of two emeralds, which cost what, 15 plat each?

SO you're wasting the equivalent of 2 cheals or so of mana (after specialization checks and the like) plus what, 30 plat? every 10 minutes... just on the off chance a heal won't land in time. The only time you always want to have DI up in experience groups is if your cleric is completely incompetent and you don't trust them to keep you alive, and you're willing to have a cash split that's close to nothing after costs of all those emeralds, plus the extra downtime from all that wasted mana... or if you're tanking in a zone which is too tough for your gear and AA's to handle.

Due to its high cost and limited duration, DI is only really used when you think you've got a very high chance of not surviving the next 10 minutes. If you're dying enough in an experience group that you need DI up at all times, you've got some serious problems.
