While it is not the "best" in the game, there are some really good upgrades you can get in the EP prior to Time.

If/when your guild kills Coirnav, he has a great earring. It is the highest AC/HP combo you will find EP wise. Yeah, you will piss off casters right and left, but who cares.

Avatar of Earth from PoEB, has a decent BP, little better than VT. It could easily fill in until you get to Time, because once in Time it will take you some time to get to the BP's.

Arbitor of Earth from PoE had a decent shield. AC70ish with Furious bash IV (adds to hate).

There is a ring or 2 that drop in the Plane of Water on misc. named mobs.

Bracer of Earthen energy from PoE. Another quality item..it will allow you to put haste on your wrist slot and free your back for much better AC items.

Honestly, there is a ton of things that will upgrade you in the EP's. You just need to do some research to see what fits you best. Aslo, do LDoN. 2600 adventure points adds 50AC(raw) to slots.
