once you can solo a quest for gear it's 99.9% worth....fuck all.

save your coppers and hunt your bazaar.

Once you hit level 65 and pour on the AA, you'll find your HP will go up on it's own...your stats will go up as the quality of your groupmates buffs increase also.

As most people have said, a lot of the AC upgrades aren't worthwhile if it's 100% soloable by a warrior...loyalist shield of honor is mostly soloable, but not entirely...and I'm still waiting for final mob to spawn myself, so can be large time sink. The only exception to that rule, is camping in the bazaar is soloable...if you can manage to accumulate the money. The other strategy would be to try grouping in areas were upgrades drop (e.g., GoD, Hard LDoN's) or can be earned (e.g., LDoN).

Other than that, my advice would be to scan every post where people ask about armor upgrades and look at people's magelo's to get ideas for upgrades...that's what I do. :) I myself have alternate profiles on magelo to see what I'd look like if i were a platinum king (bazaar) or ldon lifer (gear from adventure points).
