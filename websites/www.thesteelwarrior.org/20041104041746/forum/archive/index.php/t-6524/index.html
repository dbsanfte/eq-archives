Not sure if RZ would be on track, I would assume he would even though he's not targetable, but anyone can check him. Just go to the tunnel towards the pit as soon as you get to the pit look straight up and you will see a ledge, that's where fake RZ will be. You will need to move to the side in order to see him on the ledge but he's clearly visable twirling his fiery axe around.
Decaillin
The two named Decorins that start the script are always up (respawn time is the same as every other mob in the zone), the main thing you need to look for is Rallos Zek.

He can be tracked, or you can look for yourself to see if he is up with invis, just run to the edge of the pit and look up, you will see a little balcony, and if RZ is up, he will be standing there.
