My perspective of a Guadralek.... I totally love it for the following reasons.

DPS is pretty decent
AC = 50 totally owns making it a very good tanking weapon
Effect is unresistable 179 DD + knockback 1 = enough to aggro
HP = 100

Even with no stats other than HPs and AC my stats are maxxed anyhow with buffs. The 50 AC actually is around 75 AC when equipped.

I used mine unaugmented and loved it, and then augmented and I love it even more. I also have a BoC unaugmented, and for aggro the augmented Gaudralek is the winner(I'm needing a couple EF adventures to augment my BoC).

You won't go wrong with this sword - also the graphic is very nice.

Edit: Using this sword till elementals? I would say yes. Augment that thing with Anger 3 and you should have no problems.
