Don't waste any AA on STA. You might not be capped now, but sooner or later you will be, and every AA you spend on STA will be wasted. Generally most people recommend getting defensive AAs first, then working on the offensive ones.

run 3
regen 3 or metabolism 3 (if you use a lot of stats foods this is handy)
natural durability 3
area taunt
combat stability 1
combat agility 1
physical enhancement 1
planar duarability 3
CS 3
CA 3
Lightning reflexes 5
innate defense 5

Pick any order you want, but that should get you started.
