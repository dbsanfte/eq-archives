Allot also depends on you.

Will you have to buy everything or do you have friends/guild to help with quests.

Personally I am a former guild officer with Raid Leader exp. I am pretty good at finding quests where multiple people can benefit.

Your best option would be to

- Buy nice haste item CoF or CoCW or such. This will be your last purchased haste item til you get 41% dropped item. (35k-60k)
- Buy nice agro weapon, If you plan on doing Epic then spend small amount of plat here. You can get two nice agro weapons for 10k. Other threads have info on real cheap agro weapons.
- Get armor good enough to hold you over but nothing that will break the bank - Acrylia BP, Engraved Field Plate, stuiff like that with good ac stats and under 2-3k each. Look for bargins and mass purchases. Can prob equip all armor slots for 16-30k
- Buy Gallantry rings. On my server they are now 4 and going down fast. 6-8k
- Save about 10kpp for gems and get some Thurgy armor farmin going on.
- you should have spent 73k to 141k by now depending on your choices. If you have plat left get some dps weapons to use when not tanking.

If you are patient, have friends who can help with quests, and smart buyer you can be ready for NToV type raiding and have 50kish left to play with.

The key to that plan is getting you Thurgy armor. Plate House drops are really nice, you get average of one drop per cycle, which is like 6 mobs in cycle plus 4 locals. The armor is slightly better than cultural as well.
