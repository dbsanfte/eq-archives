As far as the items go,
wingblade:
with 3 groups of 55+ you can get to the spiroc isle usually in about 6hrs of raiding (give or take an hour or so). Just be prepared to have to kill the spiroc lord a few times to get the wingblade. It took me 6 trips to PoA before the spiroc lord actually dropped the blade, then he dropped it 4 times in 6 kills. If you have a couple groups post 60 you could probably do it faster. Just besure to leave up the mob that keeps the lord respawning if he doesn't drop it the first time. while your there might as well get all your warriors the blade. He also drops the blade I think SK's need for their epic.
hand:
really should do this like someone else said before they revamp hate. Maestro or the hand he spawns always drops this and can be done with a single post 60 group, or 2-3 groups 55+ if you are careful.
red scale:
by the scale or kill Nortlav in the Hole. Scale generally sales for 7-9k.
Ball of everliving Golem:
The Golems are kinda a pain in the but since fear sucks breaking in. The golems are a 3day spawn so they are generally taken out pretty quick after a patch. The ball will normally drop from one of the three Golems. Any of them can drop it. I don't remember a time when one of them didn't at least drop the ball.

So imho I would go for hate first, then either it is a toss up for PoA and PoF since there are a few epic drops needed from each place for a multitude of classes, it might be easier to see which ppl in your guild need what pieces from what plane and pick that way. The scale would be the easiest to get of the three since it can be bought. Btw, if you got a green scale for 15k you are one lucky warrior. When i was trying for my scale, i couldn't find one for under 120k. Anyways that was my 2cp worth. Good luck.
