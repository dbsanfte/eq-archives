Answering your third question. When corner tanking (if that is ever an option again) you need to be able to fit into the corner well. Certain races such as Ogres,Trolls, and Barb's can't shrink down enough to fit in the corner. Vah Shir have trouble fitting in the corner with their tails. Wood elf and Dark elfs seem to be a good choice for this because of their slenderness. Human's are also a good choice when double shrunk. I think gnomes are the best bet for corner tanking because you don't need shrinks, but then you will have to deal with a whole life of harassment. Hope that helped.
