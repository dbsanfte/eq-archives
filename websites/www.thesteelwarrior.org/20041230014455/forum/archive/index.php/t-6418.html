Most of the nice things about playing a woodelf warrior are intangibles.

It mostly makes a difference early. At low levels, the Ogre has the advantage on stats and stuff. By the time you're 65 with 180 AAs, there ain't much difference.

The major lasting advantage of being an Ogre is that you can't be stunned from the front, which doesn't suck.

The major lasting advantage of being a wood elf (up to now, don't know what new engine will be like) is that you corner well, and ogres have sucked at cornering now for quite some time - basically you need an illusion to be a gnome or a woodelf or something (ironic, eh).

My advice is play what makes you happy, because otherwise you'll burn out and leave.
