wandor
Chance are that you won't be doing much raid tanking anyway, so I wouldn't be in a huge hurry to get new weapons. Take anything you can get your hands on, everything is going to be a good upgrade. Right off the bat you should get a ring, earring, neck from tier 1 since those are likely rotting. If you really want to duel weild, you might consider a combination of Cudgel of Wrecking (Tier 2), Ethereal Destroyer (Tier 3), or Edge of Eternity (Saryrn). If you prefer 2hers then go for either a Timeless Coral Greatsword (Tier 3) or Vanazir, Dreamer's Despair (Terris Thule). VDD is a fine weapon with good agro that can be used for DPS or tanking, you might hold out for one.

Other than that, Tier 1-4 should be a gold mine for you. Most of the drops are fairly common so they start rotting after only a few months in Time.
