Get those shoulders right away :)

Muramite proving grounds has a bunch of prospective upgrades for you. You'll need to bring a solid group to keep you up in there though. Theres a mask, ear, neck, legs, and ac aug upgrade for you there at the minimum. I have the mask and neck on my magelo.

Vxed has an ear, and legs, and face. Tipt has a 30 hp aug, ferubi has some AC augs from the pixxt elites.

Buy a horz with your money. Try to round up some people to kill tormax and get boots of the storm and velium encrusted gauntlets. Or you could buy them, too.

Hey spike, I found myself in just that same boat as you. I am in a non raiding guild and have not received any sort of raid loots since back in the day when i got my BoC. today however i am 9100 unbuffed hp and like 1700 AC. heres how i did it.

First and most important, EXP EXP EXP. you want hps and better tanking skills, get lvl 70 and bang out those AAs. im up to 660+ aas atm and im guessing that about 1500 of those HPs came right outta AAs, as well as dmg mitigation skills which make me a more favorable tank. anyway, down to the nitty gritty for gear.

Be a loot whore, roll on anything and everything that is sellable. Take it and make some $$$ for those items that you want that are tradable raid drops ie BoC, boots of the storm, velium gaunts, horz (notice a kael trend? P). Also, i was lacking a haste item, hopped on allas and found 2 tradable 41% items. i am using a Scalecracker atm which i bought for 40k. 33 ac 15 sta 100 hp 41% tradable RANGE SLOT. Gotta keep after those rare old school loots that are more lesser known. crystalized acid bracers, scalecracker, iron cog ear etc. CT and Kael are really warrior goldmines.

For no drop loots and attunable loots:
GoD and OOW has a nifty feature: most zones in these 2 expansions have 2 or 3 rare random loots that are for the most part, very nice, as im sure you're aware. try to group in those zones as much as possible, not so much in PoPower. Work your way up too, from WoS and RCoD up to MPG and RS. Do tipt/vxed trials for loots, get KTed for loots etc etc. The oow armors from what ive heard are also wuite good. about ready to do turn ins for mine, just need faction and i can go ahead and get a full set. Very easy to get the pieces.

Most important advice i can give though is this: EVERYONE HAS A PRICE. if something you want drops in a group and you dont win the roll, try your darndest to buy the loot rights off that person. people dont expect you to do this and usually will say yes for a stupidlow price like 25k or something, just cause they are caught off guard. they will regret it later, but thats not your problem.

anyway, i'll stop ranting, but there are MANY paths to greatness, just gotta be ruthless.
