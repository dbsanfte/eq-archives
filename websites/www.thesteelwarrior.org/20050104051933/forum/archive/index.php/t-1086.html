If you do things the standard way ( how someone solos this thing is beyond me and I find that amazing to say the least ) you will need at least 2 groups of higher levels and I think 3 groups would be better if the average is around 60ish.
You get her to spawn by killing the Luggald Ambassador.
The Luggald Broodmother walks around and causes other luggalds to spawn and attack you, usually about 4 at a time. I recommend having a monk follow her around and pull the spawns to you as soon as they Pop. This goes on for quite a few waves until she becomes targetable and you can engage her in battle.
She isnt targetable before that happens. You need the numbers to take the mobs she spawns down before there are to many to handle. Treat it like the Plane of Justice trial of execution event and you will be fine.
Once solo she isnt that hard to take down, she hits for around 300ish, max 490. I believe she is slowable ( altho I am not certain ).

Good Luck on this.
