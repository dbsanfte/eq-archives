Note: This is NOT for discussion of "what needs to be done".

This is simply a small poll on if an AA should be required to let warriors do their job.

We all know PoP is a problem, the major reason is that warriors have no way to reliably gain initial agro at the beginning of the fight, unlike hybrid tanks. However, I don't see this as a PoP-only problem. This problem never started in PoP, it simply became more prevalent because:
1. You NEED that initial agro for the slower/healer to do their job fast, or else they get hurt/killed.
2. Mobs that hit as hard as PoP mobs were mainly in the high end fights in previous expansions. Now, much more casual and lower level warriors are fighting mobs that hit this hard.

Lower level warriors suffer from the same problem, and many if not all don't have the equipment (or the same type of equipment) as higher end warriors, making the problem compounded.

I don't want this to be a discussion on what needs to change. Many suggestions made by all parties involved seem to give the "solution" as a new AA. Not discussing whether these ideas are any good or not, lets hear about if warriors as a whole overall should be required to buy an AA in order to do their job.
