As far as starting in Kael goes I'd take a look at Thurg quest armour and see if it really is an upgrade to anyone cos my guess is that it isnt. There is plenty of bazaar buying stuff these days that is better.

With those numbers I'd start in either Halls of Testing if you see that the gear is worth it for you, or alternatively WToV cos you will also get a shot at Gozzrem and Telkendor who drop some quite nice loot.

From there I'd look to hitting Bastion of Thunder towers for the 2-3 groupable named up there for ornate BPs and other goodies.

Then some of the smaller Luclin named - Rhag 1, Rhag 2, Va'Dyn, Rumblecrush, Doomshade, Evolved burrower etc..

I the traditional progression of Kael arena - Halls of Testing - Velious named - NToV - Luclin Named etc is pretty redundant now with the amount of bazaar buyable gear and one groupable gear that is much better than what is available in Kael / HoT. Splitting your guild into 3 XP groups in BoT would probably get you better loot, more XP and be much better value to your guild than a night in Kael arena. Ofcourse your guild probably wants to raid so look at the normal guild progression path, discount anything that wont give your guild upgrades and start there, I'd recommend WToV if you arent concerned with maintaining dragon faction as you'll get both velious armours and a shot at the WToV named.

We can talk you through it too :) Thats what we are here for :) :)

But yah, I would work on getting those 12-18 people killing Vindicator first and make sure your clerics can do a Cheal Chain for 5 minutes without screwing up.

If you can kill him once, you can kill Statue just about any time...

When you can walk all over his butt...hint: DC chanters with hasted pets = win!

Then you can work on taking out Eashen of the Sky and his Buddy Ikatiar the Venom(ous)...

Make sure two of your tanks are getting to the 8k(ish) hit point range and then you should try and Find King Tormax. This fight will require some more DPS than you are used to so make sure you can Kill Vindicator in under 3 minutes by the time you try him.

Eashen and Ikatiar are only slightly harder than Vindicator.

You can try and Kill Dain Frostreaver around this time as well. He is a very tough pull, so allow lots of times for your monks to practice up.

If your clerics have lots of exp time in between raids, try and get them to dig up some LDON Flowing Thought augs. Also make sure people get their Coldain Shawls and Cabbage Earrings.

A Bard for Resists (especially with Psalm of Veeshan) will help your guild a TON.

Try and kill the Va'dyn in Akheva Ruins and have your warriors wear the 100 hp neck slot items (shaderock).

Velketor will have some good items for your casters as well, and if you can kill Vindi, you can kill him, just make sure you know about when to pop disc, etc...

After that, your next big fight will be Aaryonar. He has a ton of hps and you need to be able to shield your casters effectively from his AE, as well as be able to handle the respawns (which you can)
