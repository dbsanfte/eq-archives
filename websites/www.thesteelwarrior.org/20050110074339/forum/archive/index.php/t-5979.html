Pacification does indeed work in Sky, and beautifully. Mem blur is the one thats disabled. I had the same blur idea you did, but no go.

I visited Sky again a few days ago, (2/27) and using just VoQ and a mount, I was able to recast Pacification on the Guardian every 20 seconds and kept him unaggroed for the duration of the clearing. We tried using a bard, but he got resisted fairly soon, and I had to take over.

Also, for Sirran, just pull Lord a good ways back the first time. Once Sirran pops, appoint a "lucky" monk to pull him away. This also worked beautifully. Once Sirran is over near the clearing camp, he's not a problem. Then kill Lord a bit closer to his camp (since his actual aggro range is tiny, this is safe. It's the assist range thats bad)

We forgot to do it, but I've heard that if you have someone hail Sirran, it resets his 20 min despawn timer, so have someone run over and hail him after each fight, and he's safely out of your way.
