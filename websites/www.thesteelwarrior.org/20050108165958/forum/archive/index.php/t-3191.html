Maelissa
09-24-2003, 05:05 AM
Any AC to HP conversion is based on the assumption that the two items are built in a balanced fashion and that your gear without the upgrade is also balanced. To get a better estimate, you need to compare the AC of everything you have to the raw HP of everything you have and then weight that based on the ATK and damage output of the mobs you're planning on fighting. Other stats aren't as critical (except as they convert to AC and HPs) but can definately weight a decision that would otherwise be close.

If you have really high AC on most pieces, HPs gain in the ratio. If you have really high HPs on most pieces, AC gains. Once you've hit the AC soft cap of what you're going to fight, HPs are pretty much all you need to consider (the soft cap is based on the monster's ATK rating ... that's at least one reason why it's different in PoP than in older expansions). In short, it's a balancing act. There's no one simple equation that will answer the question for you.
