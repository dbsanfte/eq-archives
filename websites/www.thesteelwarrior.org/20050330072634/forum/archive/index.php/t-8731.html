Full spoiler ahead :)

Clear the temple of all mobs along the first room and last room where pile of bones is found.

Walk up to the pile of bones, type Open. If the loot dropping version is up, a Pixxt Priest Summoner will spawn + 4 Pixxt Temple Guardians, otherwise, a Pixxt DIRE Summoner + 4 Pixxt Temple Guardians will spawn. The Priest/Dire summoner is inactive while the 4 Temple Guardians are alive, split them and pull them to camp, they are all mezzable and fully slowable.

After all 4 are dead, pull the Priest to camp - he is unslowable, and puts out rather high DPS but has almost no HP, stonewall, then defensive, and enjoy the loot.

For us, the shoulders have been by far and away the most common loot.
