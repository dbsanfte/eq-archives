Depending if you like Venril Sathir Faction, you can try Karnor's castle. Can usually start going there round 46/47ish.

After 51ish, head to Velks Lab, or Chardok(bring your own group for Chardok), or sebilis (dont know how popular Seb still is, havent been there in a year)

When your around 55, then start looking for Teir 1 planes groups.

Thats prolly your fastest route. Though not the only places, these ones seem the best EXP and more popular places to go. Other places arnt as popular usually due to difficulty, or the EXP just plain sucks.

If you can con some unsuspecting healer to go with you, Kedge Keep is a great EXP zone, has the highest EXP mod in game (dungeon mod + Underwater mod). round 25% if i remember correctly.

The hole wouldnt be a bad place to try to get to either, at 46 you can handle the zone in mobs, though, this is another zone you have to bring your own group to. And you could get lucky and land an IGS.
