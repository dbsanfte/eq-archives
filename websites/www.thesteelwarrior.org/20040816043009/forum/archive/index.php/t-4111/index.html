Hulanta
Based on all the data, anecdotal evidence and ranting I have read, I only see one way to truly fix the difference between pure classes and hybrids.

Give that spells will always be given to hybrids and that will continue to improve them and that pure classes don't get anything and that itemization looks like it will continue to be broken, I offer the following solution:

Next uber expansion:

Pure classes now can proceed from level 65 to 75.
Hybrids now can proceed from level 65 to 70

Create good bonuses in hps and some AAs that only exist for level 71 to 75.

This doesn't fix warriors and other pure classes in the early game. However, clerics, monks, rogues et. al. should be pleased by being able to level beyond the classes that supposedly had to give something up to be a hybrid (which we now know is over 90% a fallacy if not 100%).

Innate bonuses to warrior AC, roguish ATK, cleric heling efficiency, etc. should also be granted.

Hybrids can still be uber with getting very nice gear, new spells, and their current utility. However, pure classes will be allowed to be the best at what they do. Warriors will once again be the first choice for tanking something (in 95% of the cases I hope) because that is all warriors really do. Other pure classes will excel at what they do as well.

This doesn't nerf hybrids, it only gives an advantage in the pure area that the pure classes chose to be good at.

Opinions? I know this is all too little too late if it were to happen, but I think it is reasonable.
