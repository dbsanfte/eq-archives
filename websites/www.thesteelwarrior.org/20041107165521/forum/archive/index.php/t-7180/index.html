Glatius2
I have yet to see a SO enhancement, but the DO enhancements come in two flavors, from what I've seen. If you are, as an example, a mutant, you'll see DO enhancements that are technology/mutant and magic/mutant that you can use. One type won't combine with the other, but you can combine a tech/mutant with a tech/mutant and so forth, within the particular type, like a generic (damage with damage, endurance reduction with endurance reduction).

Also, you can use DO enhancements at level 12, as I recall.

I can't speak as to how much of an improvement they are since it's very hard to judge with the naked eye.
