Since your username is One Wise Gnome, I'm assuming you're a gnome. In the hopes that you worship Rallos Zek, get a full set of Fierce Heraldic (80kish on my server) or get some Fine Heraldic or the Gnome clockwork armor otherwise (especially hasted legs), couple of 6 AC 65 HP rings (until you're old enough to buy Gallantry rings), some 30 HP earrings (until you're old enough to buy Mosquito Legs).

For weapons, get the best efficiency for the cost for each slot: Sword of the Bloodsworn 1HS, Copper Hammer of Strinking 1HB, Witness of Hate Greatstaff 2HB, War Marshall's 2HS, and the 13/21 Goblin dagger (forgot the name, sells for about 500pp) to work up piercing (don't bother with 2HP since 1HP works it up anyway).

With leftover cash you should be able to get a Cloak of Flames; if not, pick up a Cloak of Crystalline Waters or a Siblisian Berserker's Cloak instead.

Oh yeah, and a Goranga Idol or a Idol of the Throned/Wavecrasher if you have leftover cash.

Hope that helps.
