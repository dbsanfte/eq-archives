For 2 handers. Highest Damage, Lowest delay above 28 (unless you have a lot of atk mods), look for big damage procs. DD over Dots, as dots piss off enchanters/bard to no end. >=)

For 1 handers. Again, best ratio. Main hands should be fastest (that's the old line of thinking). Honestly, its been a long time since I've looked for a DPS 1h. Procs are good. I imagine FGx2 would be pretty fair DPS becuase they're quick and have a nice DD proc. My 1h set up is normally good for around 100-110 DPS at best with CoR as my only DS.

On the other hand, I can average a really high 140 DPS with my 2h, and FA. I'm sure it'll get better when I put a good DD proc on it.
