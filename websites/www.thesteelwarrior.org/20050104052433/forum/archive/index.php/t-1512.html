http://lucy.fnord.net/dmgbonus.html

Damage bonus is added onto the hit after everything else is calculated as a stratight add.

The long and short of it is that the lowest you can ever connect with (in main hand) is 1+ Bonus, due to it being on every hit.

This is the main attraction to fast weapons, a fast weapon "collects" the bonus more often.

If you look at the chart, wrt 2Hand weapons, you can see the big cliff at 28 delay.

If you have what looks like a good 27 or lower delay 2hander, it actually in real life, stinks.
