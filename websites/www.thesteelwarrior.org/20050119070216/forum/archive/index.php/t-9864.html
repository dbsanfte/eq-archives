You can "block" the AE.

There are actually 8 AEs -- four are unresistable debuffs, and four are the DDs that hurt you.

The unresistable debuffs are cureable (I think 45 counters, so not easy really) and last for something like 30 seconds. They lower one resist by a large amount (200?)

The DDs are tied to the unresistable debuffs, and are -300 resist check. Cure the dot, get your resists back up, then resist the AE. The DD AE goes off about 18 secs after the AE.

So, THEORETICALLY you could cure the Debuff, then resist the -300 (rather than the theoretical -500 resist check with the debuff on you). It's not viable to block everybody's, but if you have a lot of shm/pal with cures, you could cut down the DPS a group takes (and then have healers who only need to do single-target heals to get those who werent cured, rather than mana-intensive group heals)

On Simon Says, the only time you should ever move is when you get a move emote. That means if you're standing in a platform with nothing on it as the result of a move, you stay there until they tell you to move again (it'd be better if you moved to somewhere that you can hit stuff, though). Moving without seeing an emote is simply asking to get nailed by a stop emote while moving. You will not be able to stop on time to react to a stop emote if you're already moving.

11-30-2004, 01:50 AM
If you look at each of the 6 platforms the Dragorns are, moving from one platform at the start of a move to another platform is always enough. Moving from any one platform to the center platform is also always enough. If you only move when being told to move and move exactly one platform away (to side or to center), you'll always end up either at the side platforms or the center platforms so you don't have to worry about roomwide AEs either.

If you get two consecutive moves and you're not sure if the first one is off, I usually go to the center platform during this time since it's easy to trace a path that's always safe from the first move, and then run back to starting point if I get another move emote.
