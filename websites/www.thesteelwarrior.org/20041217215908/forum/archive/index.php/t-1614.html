There are three ways to do the Royals. Keep in mind each royal has 8 or so guards in a small area guarding them.

1) Method one is the easiest. All you need is one lvl 62 chanter or 65 cleric to pacify all the guards. Pacify every mob except the royal. Then just single pull the Royal out of the room and kill.

2) Method two is the traditional way to kill them and the pre-pop way. Get two or three 55 plus chanters and zerg the royal with the chanters AE mezzing the room.

3) Method three needs several good AE nukers. Rush the royal like in method two and just AE the hell out of the adds.

With the force you described I doubt you could have taken the Queen but you may have been ok with the King and Prince. But doing chardok without a single person who knows the place is tough. There is a pit trap above the King room and you can get adds with mobs warping easily.

The way to do dok is AE like Fae said, let me explain. You need two or more chanters and ae nukers. Dont AE MEZ, have the chanters AE STUN then the nukers kill. On the royals you MUST have the mt get control of the Royal or he,she will kill your chanters fast.

The Queen is not that hard, kill the king and wait one spawn in the kings room to get in sync with the spawn, buff up and a wiz with EYE target the Queen and then have the MT assist the Wiz to get the Queen on target. Then have the MT rush the Queen's room and engage the queen on the platform with his back to the wall then have the raid enter and AE stun and nuke. The MT will be the only one in front of the Queen and keeps her busy while her guards are killed then everyone jumps the queen from the back, the MT has argo lock by now.

We have only failed one time on the Queen because I did not have her Targeted and she took out the chanters in 10 sec. I could not get a target on her with >20 peeps and 8 mobs in one small room.

There are many ways to do this but what I have found is the more steps the WORSE the plan, remember KISS.
