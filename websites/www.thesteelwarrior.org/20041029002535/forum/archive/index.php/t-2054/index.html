They don't proc more, they proc the same amount over a period of time. ie: BoC mainhand + Non-EB offhand you'd get the same amount of procs over 1 hr constant fighting as you would with a single BoW.

If things were "equal" a BoW would proc less than a boc - considering it swings less times in the same period of time. Since it procs the same, that shows the compensation
