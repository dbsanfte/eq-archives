Ahz
Griglon, you have to remember that most of the warriors here are hard-core elemental-and-better types. So what they say sucks, only sucks because they are far beyond it.

When you don't want aggro, there isn't a thing wrong with the weapon. For MTage, however, it is "almost good."

We here on SteelWarrior are a bunch o' optimizers. I know I don't get lewt often, so when I do, I wait until it is the best thing I can reasonably get. The concensus here is that the CHoS and SotB are a very effective aggro-producing weapon pair. So, I'd prolly begin with those weapons as a baseline and upgrade from there.

The perceived problem with your hammer is that it generates no additional aggro. You can add an anger augment of course, but you could also up your effectiveness by adding the same augmentation to the CHoS or SotB. We are so dependent on procs for aggro that there isn't much room for individuality where weapons are concerned. Are you prepared to put the fruit of 23 LDoN wins into a weapon that isn't the best you can reasonably obtain?

So, I'd sell it, especially if you can get the nutty amounts mentioned above. Stick it out there for a silly amount and see if it sells. Reduce it a little evrey day. If it doesn't sell, I'd trade it even for a CHoS.
