Beornegar
Nightvision isnt nearly as big a deal as it used to be. Another upgrade to your helm that would be relatively inexpensive would be a champions helm. Not great, but serviceable until you can get better. Your helm, bp, boots, and waist are your weakest slots. The boots have way too low AC, I'd say replace them with champs, raldukan, ornate, or LDoN. Champions gear is an upgrade to several of those slots until you can afford or camp better. Waist, soulscream belt from Veksar is nice and easy to get if you can actually get the camp. A good, easily attainable BP is the darkguard chestplate from the theatre camp in Veksar, dropped by trooper muruk I think.

Edit: For shoulders, draconic pauldrons or strength of the elements. Both quested, draconic is a much easier quest, but the str of the elements had more ac and has sta and raw hps, where the draconic just has 12 sta.
