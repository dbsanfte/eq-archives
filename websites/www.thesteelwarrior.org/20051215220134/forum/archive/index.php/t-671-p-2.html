Some corrections:
Bracer of the Hidden drops very rarely from Sarnak Lego's in the sarnak fort in LoIO. Easy to do solo, just rare.
Eyepatch of Plunder is 3.5second cast time.
Incarnadine bp drops off of chardok king.
Bangle of Disease Warding is targetable, best to have at least a healer partner.
Rage bracer doesn't really become useless. It's good for setting rampage, and aerampage mobs when you don't have enough arrows. It has good range, so can be used for pulling also, though the cast time is annoying.
Velious bp healing doesn't become useless if you do anything solo, or for healing so the priests can save mana.
Shrunken Goblin Skull Earring doesn't stack with Strength of Tunare line, but does stack with Brells, which most warriors prefer, so it's not a real problem.
Pegasus Cloak doesn't require batwings iirc.
Seru boots are self only.
