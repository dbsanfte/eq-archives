Your skill on dual wield determines how frequently you will swing your offhand.

Your skill on double attack determines how often you will swing again for an additional attack anytime either hand swings.

Your weapon skill for the attack form you are using determines how frequently you will hit a target and how hard you will hit for.

At level 60 and beyond all warriors innately have the ability to possibly swing 3 times instead of twice on their primary hand. This "triple attack" skill is not really a skill but is simply an augmentation of our existing double attack skill.

I'm gonna separate the hands.

Mainhand (or 2H weapon)
A- Main hand attack (roll to see if missed)
B- Dual attack check (roll to see if you get a 2nd attack)
C- Dual attack roll (roll to see if missed with 2nd attack)
D- Triple attack chek (roll to see if you get a 3rd attack)
E- Triple attack roll (roll to see if missed with 3rd attack)

Offhand
F- Dual wield check (roll to see you swing offhand)
G- Dual attack check (roll to see if you get a 2nd attack)
H- Dual attack roll (roll to see if missed with 2nd attack)
I- Triple attack chek (roll tp see if you get a 3rd attack)
K- Triple attack roll (roll to see if missed with 3rd attack)


(of course if you fail the dual attack check, hense dont get a
second attack, there will be no Triple attack check)

Ferocity = Increase B and G percentage (chance for a successfull roll)

Punishing Blade : Increase B and D percentage for 2H only
(chance for a successfull roll)
