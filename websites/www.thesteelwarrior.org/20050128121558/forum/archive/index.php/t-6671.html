5500 hp unbuffed with all your hp aas shouldn't be tough. Getting 1500ac unbuffed without raid loot or a LOT of LDoN points is another story. I have over 5800hp, but only about 1350ac.

For AA: General- run3, other3 (regen, sta, metabalism, cha all have their proponents) cost and total 6. Grats on Baron. Archtype- ND3 cost12, total 18. Grats on Veteran. Class- AETaunt cost 5 total 23. Back to archtype- CA3 cost 12, total 35, then CS3 cost 12 total 47. Back to class- PE cost 5, total 52 or PoP - PD1 cost 3, total 50. PD will give more hp than PE, but PE also adds to your avoidance and mitigation. Note that's not what I did, but I didn't have any intention of being a raid tank until I suddenly was one.
