What the original poster probably meant, and what Amad tried to answer was this: Lets say you were level 50 with 100 stamina, and before dinging 51, you grind out a few levels of stamina AA, and then ding 51. You gain like 40 hp, and keep grinding normal exp until you hit level 65, and you'd end up with... 4200 hp or so.

Now let's say you have 100 stamina at level 50, and ding 51, you might gain like 35 hp. If you stay at 100 stam all the way to 65, lets say your total would be 4000. You then grind out some Stamina AAs, increasing your hp to say 4200.

Either way, whether you grind stam/hp AA and then level to 65, or grind 65 first and then work on stam/hp AA, you'll end up with the same number of hp in the end. So rather than do AA first, it's considered better to get 65 so you look more appealing to groups in need of a tank, rather than being level 60, with 12 AAs.
