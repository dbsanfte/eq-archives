I play an L62 Dwarf Warrior. I'm in better stat shape than a human warrior friend of mine, but equipment will be making up any differences. I am just under 1200 AC and good HP for the level (can't remember it off-hand). I run around 1350 AC and 6-7k HP with full raid buffs. I am in mostly Thurg plate, Radulkan Helm, Hate BP, Lodi shield for back.

Most of my friends are "good" race players and so it made sense for me. Haven't seen any problems other than the stun and that is more annoying that anything. I've been able to go places pretty easy and I think being too big (hence needing shrink potions) would have been more annoying than getting stunned on occassion. What does amaze me is a wimp mob can stun you in some cases, but it doesn't last long and you are just more determined to pound the mob's face into a cherry ooze of bloody pulp.

It also depends on whether or not you are going to end game, and how much twinkage you want to do. The other small races have advantages too, like exp bonus or hide/sneak (dwarves don't get those). At L62 with ok equipment, I max out on STR/STA with raid buffs. Close to max on DEX. AGI is around 200, so it's all good. The catch is that at the levels getting there, I needed the STR/STA, so I really don't think how you put those points is going to matter a whole lot.

I chose Agnostic, and it hasn't hampered me that I've seen. Choosing Brell might help in The Hole, but I really haven't played there much and once you do, you mess it up anyway, so it's not a factor. As far as cultural there isn't a noticeable difference.

I have a Hate BP, and the HP from it was close to the HP from a Brellium BP. The Hate BP gives better offensive stats and the Brellium has 16 more AC.

If you want to solo at all, then I'd go for a Troll, but warriors are NOT solo classes. If you really want to solo, go with a hybrid and twink it out. The best combo is warrior/druid or warrior/cleric. Find a good healing partner and you can level fast and have a great time exploring the zones.
