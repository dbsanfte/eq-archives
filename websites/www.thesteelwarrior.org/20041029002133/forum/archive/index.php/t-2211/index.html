Heyas-

Couple tips for the fight;
1). his stun proc sucks, bigtime. check is vs. MR, and even with 500mr it'll stick a lot.
2). fairly risky with only 1 tank- strongly suggest a DI chain and high cha if you try that. Have a backup tank clone aggro periodically perhaps, just in case.
3). use some instant aggro stuff to build up some hate during the pull; snare glands or sticks, root webs, etc. all work very well. Plan on being stunned a LOT, if it happens early your clerics are gonna get eaten.
4), massive DSes are good, may end up being your primary source of DPS during the fight if you don't have a decent crew of high FT and/or clicky nukers.
5). GRC or equiv needed for mt for sure, also for others that screw up and accidentally get dotted. Low health aggro WILL pull seru off the MT. Adds (if any) will also SUMMON the MT if he goes low health, pulling seru into Bad Places, so beware.

Grobble
Farming banes is a pain, so if you do it at all just get bows for your rangers. It's a relatively long fight no matter what way you look at it, so bring all the nukers you can. Wizards are great if they know what they're doing.

Also great for this fight (as with many luclin fights) are necromancers. A necro with seduction of saryrn (their highest level lich spell) can keep several dots on seru for the entire fight without worry of going oom. Their best dots are fire based, disease based and poison based and usually land 100% of the time. Also, with FD, they should have no problem with aggro.

Pretty sure our necros outdamage our wiz on seru pretty easily.

As was stated it isnt very hard to keep a tank up against seru, his melee is < 800 and the dot is only about 300/tick iirc. Adds really aren't much of a problem and if you get them, you'll have plenty of bored melee around to take them out.
