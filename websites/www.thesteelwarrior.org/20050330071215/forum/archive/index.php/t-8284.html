Pull the golem on her left (your right when facing them). The golem on her right (your left) does not aggro on the DD pull. Respawns can be a problem if you didn't time/space them out correctly. We always let all the pats respawn once all of the static spawns were cleared. This way we could plow through the respawns in about 10-14 minutes leaving plenty of time for the DD fight to ensure no respawns get in the way.

I haven't done the trial in months so I have no idea of the current fight. Originally she had 150k hp, then it was dropped down to 100k. I brought a bunch of pumice stones for the melee and casters since right clickies allow them to instantly cast a memmed spell. With 5 of us dispelling we made sure to debuff her as much as possible. The warrior would use riposte disc at the start then stonewall for the rest of the fight. Depending on your dps, the fight should last approx 2 min, still leaving some time on your disc when the cruel illusion spawns... a much easier fight than the DD. I think I parsed the CI for like 300 dps with stonewall.
