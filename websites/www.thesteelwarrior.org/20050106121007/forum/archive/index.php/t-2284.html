Even at level 46 it's time to start differentating yourself from damage dealer (Warrior DPS is not the best by far) to Main Tank.

If they proc at your level I suggest getting a couple of Troll remanufactured Yaks for aggro. They're 12 Damage 22 Delay or so and not a big step down in DPS. Copper Hammer etc. would be fine too. Just not weapons that don't proc an aggro producing effect.

Finally, when you can afford it, better haste.
