I don't think you are understanding how summoning mobs work, in conjunction with Root.

Summoning Mobs will summon whoever is at the top of their hate list once they have been damaged to a certain point. Most creatures start summoning at 98 or 97%, but some can summon at 1 point of damage taken. They can summon every server side tick, or 6 seconds. A Summoning mob will not summon the person on top of the hate list if they are already within immediate melee range.

Root does not make any adjustments to the hate list. All root does it make the mob attack the closest person via proximity. If a chain nuking Wizard is at the top of the hate list of a non summoning mob by a good amount, and the mob is rooted, the mob will begin to beat on whoever is closest. After root breaks, if the wizard is still #1 on the list, the mob will immediately go back to chasing the wizard around.

On a summoning mob, if the same chain nuking wizard is on top of the hate list, and the mob gets rooted, it will begin to attack whoever is closest via proximity, but will summon the wizard every 6 seconds, melee him until he runs his fingle wiggling ass out of proximity range (just farther than the closest PC), and then begin to hit whoever is closest again.
