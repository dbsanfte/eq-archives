I'm sure someone else will come up with good suggestions, but my biggest observation of your two profiles is that you have hardly any AC augments. I would put Blood Ruby of Prowess in every slot that it can fit for starters - it is the single best return on investment that LDON points can offer. You've got maybe a dozen Faceted Gems of Might listed in your "doable" profile, but those things don't grow on trees. From my experience, AC augments in Ferubi are far more common than the Natimbi augment. More realistically you should shoot for the Ferubi AC augments, they are excellent, and not that rare, and a couple of them will have a huge impact on your AC.

Finally, and I know this will suck as a suggestion, but if you care about AC then you really have to retire the Spirit Wracked Cord. That thing is a nice quest reward, but it does not befit a warrior who cares about AC. To replace them there's the arms that drop in sewers, or you can accept a 40hp loss and go for ornate, or that is one of the best slots to buy Royal Attendant or Farwater.

Also remember you can buy a lot of AC through tribute (70 or more, I forget the exact amount).

Edit - something I forgot about is that you can now get weapons in OOW that are equivalent to the Great Blade of Storms, but which have a fair chunk of AC on them. Most of the weapon links that I've seen in the past week have had 10-25 AC on them.
