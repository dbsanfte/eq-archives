Actually, Ornate BP is not only better, but easier to get than a Vindi BP. Vindi is on an 8hr repop and can only drop 1 BP at a time. You also have to deal with his guards and clearing the arena out.

Eindride Icestorm on the other hand has a 6hr repop. You can lull your way all the way to him, you only have to kill 5-6 giants to clear enough room to fight him. He also can drop as many as 2 BP molds at a time.

If you are looking to upgrade a Vindi BP, I would go for ornate, Cursed, Seru, Speed, AHR, Rathe, Time - in that order considering difficulty (not rarirty) to get. I don't include Farwater, which I would put as slightly better than Ornate, because you will spend days farming the ingredients to make one.
