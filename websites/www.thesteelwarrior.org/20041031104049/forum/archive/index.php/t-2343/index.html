Gimmel
Actually, though the entry for the spell in Lucy shows a cast time of 6.0, the gaunts themselves show a cast time of 3.0.

But yes, I think people are falling into the old "Bard haste stacks with spell haste" fallacy. The only bard haste songs that stack with regular spell haste are the Ervaj line and the various overhaste songs like the VahShir line and Rizlona's. Standard bard haste does NOT "stack" with other hastes in the that you get additive effect of both hastes. Both icons are there, and you get the stats of both effects, but only the higher haste takes effect.
