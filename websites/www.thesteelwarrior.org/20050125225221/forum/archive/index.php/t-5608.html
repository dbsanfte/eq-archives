For your gear, maybe invest in a some droga weapons for a little better agro, but otherwise your gear is looking fine. Do some Miragul's (EF) LDoN's and pick up an anger aug (I'd grab anger2 - better than anger 1 but not as many points / as much as a grind as anger 3) for your GSoT and you're sorted for agro until you pick up something from a raid.

As for augments, do adventures, pickup stat and resist augs and put them in your gear. The buyable augs to look out for (other than procs) are 40hp augs for BP/Legs/Helm/Arms/Feet for 1150 points at EC, and 10AC augs for face/shoulders/neck/waist/back for 510 points at BB, but you probably wont want to shell out on those until your gear is at a point where you know you wont be getting an upgrade for a while.

None of your gear really stands out as needing attention, so whatever upgrades you do get you will probably be paying a high price for very little gain.

Maybe an elemental bow eg. Stonewood compound bow would be a decent investment, but it might be worth sitting on your cash for now and waiting for a good deal on a BoC or SoD or something else kinda special to come your way.
