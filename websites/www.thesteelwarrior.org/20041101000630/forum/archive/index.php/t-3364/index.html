Cuclain MacLlir
Look for items with higher ac and raw hp instead of stats.

1- Ditch that veil. Anything with ac and/or hp is better.
2- Ulthork Hide collar is 8ac 30hp -30m and like 10pp in bazaar. Awesome newbie neck piece and you can make a few plat switching out.
3- Tattered Mantle is 12ac 20hp, a great shoulder item for newbies.
4- I like your weapon choices, buy both are 1hs. Don't neglect your other skills while leveling up.

At level 25, you're not going to make a ton of plat. Silk swatches and leather padding are good. LDoN may be good if the cash is scaled down from what I've gotten at 63-65.
