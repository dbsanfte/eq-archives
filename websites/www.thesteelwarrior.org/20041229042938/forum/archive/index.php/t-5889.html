I've done a lot of parses and math on this topic in the past. I'm at 40/46 regen atm (Troll + 19 worn). Plus I can toss on a Fungus Vest for 35 worn regen and 10th ring for 10 more regen. Most of my testing has been done soloing. This was done in order to formulate how much DPS I could 'mitigate' from mobs through regen while soloing to achive the least amount of possible downtime.

After all of my math and parsing, the best I could really do with high uptime is still PoN mobs, at around 30-50 DPS slowed. Moving to PoV, Tactics, Elem etc the DPS just goes up too fast to be able to compensate for while solo. Regen just doesn't happen fast enough (6 seconds).

I should also add in this was with CoW and SoPP both aug'd with Lifetaps.

It seemed like a real nice solo setup in theory, but when put to the test, it's managable, but certainly no where close to the exp you can get in a normal Elem exp group. And no where near overpowered. Too bad.

For raids/groups:
Like has been stated you can get buffs that give more regen and stack:

Nature's Recovery 30 per tick
Natureskin 4 per tick
Spiritual Dominion 9 per tick
Replenishment 25 per tick
Chorus of Marr 20 per tick

So max standing regen unbuffed is 61. You can get buffs that add up to 88 regen. Add in these buffs and you're at 149 max HP Regen.

This amounts to 24.8 DPS 'mitigation'. If you are not at full health for every single tick, which is unlikely. So you might cut this number in half, or at least 3/4 of it. For a realistic tanking DPS 'mitigation' of ~12 DPS.

I suppose that's pretty useful. But not everyone is a Troll/Iksar, and finding 35 worn regen w/o giving up a ton of HP/AC is difficult for 99% of all warriors.
