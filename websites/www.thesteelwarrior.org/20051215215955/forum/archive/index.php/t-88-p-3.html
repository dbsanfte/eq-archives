05-14-2003, 04:36 PM
CS doesn't work like defensive. The average hit against you does go down but your min and max hits remain the same. With defensive not only does your average go down but so does your max hit. So with defensive a healer can reliably increase the time between CHs for the duration of the /disc because there is no chance that you will get a string of hits for the max damage or even close to max damage.

The advantage that CA has over CS as others have mentioned is that you can't mitigate a proc. So with CA you'll take 10% less damage due to misses and be affected by 10% less procs. With CS you'll take 10% less damage due to mitigation but you'll still get hit with the full effect of all procs because you were still hit the same number of times. Against a mob that doesn't proc anything the affect should be identical.
