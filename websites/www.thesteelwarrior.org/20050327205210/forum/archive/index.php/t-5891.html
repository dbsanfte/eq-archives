Fennin's rampage isn't AE, it hits 6 people (maybe more, but I've seen at least 6 die to it each time). Given the gear you can expect while fighting him it's almost guaranteed death for any nontanks that got hit by rampage and even tanks could get dropped by a rampage.

You can avoid the AE or not, it's to you. There's really not much you'll lose from meleeing him. At worst all your melee gets splat but if they got even some melee time at all it's got to be more DPS than they'd have done from range. At the gear level you're expected to fight him, melees simply die too fast so that the chance of accidentally moving FR is quite slim (you got to be alive to move a mob!).
