On my /testbuffed warrior there, standing took 2-3 ticks to go up 1%, but then only 2 ticks for another, and then repeat. Sitting took 2 ticks to go up 1%, then 1 tick, then 2 ticks again, then 1 tick and 1 tick...it's a bit faster than caster mana, apparently, but they get meditate, I don't know if melee will get.

Tried using Invigor (http://lucy.fnord.net/spell.html?id=222&source=Live) on the paladin, but that did nothing.

Honestly, it all depends on just how much Endurance it takes to use the skills. Jumping still takes half a bubble out of it. Death refills the bar completely just like a normal caster mana bar.

All characters have the new stamina, but it goes up much much faster on non-melee/hybrids, like 2% per tick sitting. Looks like they fixed stamina from going up 10%/tick to going up depending on your stats, and pure melee getting a much larger amount due to their abilities.
