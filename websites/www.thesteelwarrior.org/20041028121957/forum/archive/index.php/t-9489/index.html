Spikela
If you don't care terribly for an earring... Silver Earring of Haste works wonders. Stats are shit, but the 41% haste in an earring slot that's groupable is just amazing. Can be grouped pre-elem with ease. Get you (warrior) and a druid, druid, cleric, bard, necro/chanter/druid. You will just agro the mobs. That's all. Low down on the strat below.

Umbral Plains mini-boss: Doomshade

Enter Umbral Plains. Have puller pull trash leading to the Dark Master campsite. Kill the wimps. Kill the first Dark Master when everyone is ready. Time begins: 20 minutes. Kill the other 3 dark masters before the first one spawns again (20 minute timer) and WALA! Doomshade spawns if he hasn't been killed in three days. Have bard FM pull Doomshade to near the tunnel, then stop him. enchanter/necro (if you have one) should charm something. Bard should pull Doomshade into camp, you agro him. Use defensive (stonewall isn't necessary unless you think you can dent his massive HP or need DPS agro). Bard should either use both high-end DS songs or charm and DS. Mage DS on you, druid DS AA on you, alternate when the first wears off. He quads, flurries, hits FAST and isn't slowable. Max hit in the 300s without defensive sooo... with 103 bard DS + charmed pet + 1/2 nukers + your minimal DPS + all your DS added together times his attack speed... he'll go down pretty darned fast. 45 minute run tops. Rarely, rarely drops the earring, has other nice toys for alts though. Took 8 tries for me to get earring. Good luck!
Drozan_RN
Can do sewer trials in GoD and get The Talosian (sp?) girdle that is 36 % haste. Or patiently wait til your guild starts doin Earth rings and get the 41 % haste bracer that drops.

I think too much emphasis is put on having to have 41 % haste. Haste cap not counting bard over haste is 100 %. Vallons is 67 % haste from a Chanter. Swift like the Wind is 60 % from a shaman. So with 36 % haste you will be over the cap with a Chanter and 4 % under with a Shammy. The only time you will be hurting if there is no haste person in your group which is generally pretty rare.

I personally dont see you losing that much Dps by having 5 % less haste.

This is just my 2 cp.

P.S. I wear woven shim choker and the Tal belt for haste. I plan to get the Blue flame neck or another nice neck and use Girdle for my haste. In time as I get upgrades I may get higher haste but not focusing only on that.
