My point is, that if you are using a Blade of War, and he is using a Cranial Bludgener, what will 'skew' the damage you do to each other will be that the riposte damage you two do to each other will be uneven.

- Blade of war swings X1 times / minute
- Cranial Bludgener swings X2 times/minute
- Blade of war against a warrior with maxed double riposte resulsts in Z1 ripostes/minute (function of delay only)
- Cranial Bludgener results in Z2 ripostes/minute
- Blade of War average hit is equal to Y1
- Cranial bludgener average hit is equal to Y2

Therefore - while melee DPS would be just (X1 * Y1) vs (X2 * Y2) (excluding proc DPS) the 'damage' you would deal each other in a duel would be ((x1+y1) + (z2*y1) ) vs ((x2*y2) + (z1 *y2)) and z2*y1 >> z1 * y2
