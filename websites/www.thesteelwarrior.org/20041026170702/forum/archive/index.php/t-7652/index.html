I tried to keep these as balanced and versatile as possible, while adding a few unique things. Anger of Vadd will work on aggro procs but not the Incite-line, which is intended, because adding any more to Incite/Mastruq is a balance issue against knights' snap aggro capability. The Decorin proc is a Vie effect which uses the short buff box, mitigating 3% of DB+DI, giving protection against spike damage and a bit of overcap shielding, I guess. It only lasts 2 ticks because if it were on all the time, it would be another balance issue on xp content against knights (though with very high combat effects it could be sustained).

The versatility I was striving for when I designed this was simple - I wanted to create a setup for warriors like Battleblade and Celestias, that want high aggro, tanking weapons, while having a lower aggro combo for warriors like Frodlin. Then there needed to be an easy, fast way to switch between the low and high aggro setups so this doesn't happen -

So MT down ...

/furious
/put the epic 2H in a bag
/combine
/put back the 1H for tanking mode

Pure aggro - Brutish/Darkblade + Nihilite
Tanking - Nihilite + Decorin
Low aggro - Decorin + Nihilite
Lowest aggro - Decorin + whatever

So MT down ...

Decorin and Nihilite swords switch places
Done

For the 2-hander enthusiasts; Blade of Tactical Fusion can be used as an aggro or DPS weapon, since it should be less aggro than the two epic swords paired. Its high PPM and damage means you might have to wait a second or two or cycle after the MA engages if you don't want to pull aggro, but it doesn't have as much aggro over time as the paired swords.

PPM (mainhanded at 305 dex, including WA5 and unknown070 but not combat effects)
Nihilite - 4.65
Decorin - 3
Tactical Fusion - 4.65

None of the swords have augment slots, and they're balanced for that, because they are combinable. When LDoN released and the original epic was altered to make it augmentable, it also allowed warriors to own 2 red blades, 2 blue blades, and a 2-hander all at the same time. Being able to use two Decorin swords would be 10% shielding + near-constant 3% vie. Being able to use two Nihilite swords would be far more aggro over time than balanced.

The lore and the names of the swords would indicate a long three-part epic, split between Gates/Omens content dealing with the Nihilites' struggle against Mata Muram for the Nihilite sword, PoP/Velious (Kael) content dealing with Rallos Zek for the Decorin sword, and something different (New Veeshan's Peak maybe?) for the scabbard to combine them. Lots of room for new content in old zones, and quest lore. I'd write a brief outline on it, but I'm not on Sony's payroll :D

That's my Vision(tm). I'm not a warrior, so if I'm wrong on something, fine.
