Abynormal777
I'd go for warlord gauntlets instead of ornate. The reason is, you can always sell the warlord gaunts later if an upgrade becomes available, whereas with the ornate you just lose the plat forever.

Similarly, there are good tradeable boots with 75 to 90 hp and good ac (forget the name...I'm pretty sure they are listed on a recent ldon post on this board).

Both the gauntlets and boots normally go for around 90k on Emarr.

As to the greaves, if you like ldon, I'd go for those. If you want something quick you can buy now (again, losing plat forever), ornate is good. It also depends on your sta - if you're not capped already or don't always have a shaman around, the extra 30 sta would be nice. by the way, I don't know about your server, but the cheapest I've ever seen ornate pants for on Emarr is 140k.
