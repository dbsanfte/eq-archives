The first room with mobs has 3 slimes. The outer 2 slimes are PHs for Red Mass (slime boots). Hang a right past that room and there is a mosquito in the corridor. He is PH for Diseased which drops mosquito ear. Hang a left from mosquito. This last room has a slime close on the right, and a slime in the far back left. That far back left spawn is green mass PH for the girdle.
