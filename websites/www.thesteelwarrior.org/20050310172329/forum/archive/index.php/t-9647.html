I started on Metal with Metallica / Maiden / Megadeth. Back then we used to play them on our band, you know, mostly a covers band.
After a few years of listening to Heavy Metal mostly, I was at a buddy's house and heard the melody that would change my life... Stratovarius's Destiny album was playing.

From that moment on, my road went to Power / Melodic / Classic Metal, and nowadays, I can't listen to any metal band that has no "melody" on their songs (forget about Thrash :p ), no more growling-rusty voices, after hearing Tobias Sammet or Timo Kotipelto, Kiske, Tony Kakko, and God knows how many more great Power Metal voices (let's not forget about Tarja Turunen ;) ).

Some great Power / Melodic / Progressive / Gothic Metal bands? Blind Guardian, Nightwish, Stratovarius, Angra, Judas Priest, Edguy, Rhapsody, Sonata Arctica, Dream Theater...
