First of all, you'll gain 500 hp from Sturdiness 5, and almost 150 hp from getting your unbuffed stamina up to 305.

You'll get probably about 500 more hp if you level from 65-70 and keep your stamina close to cap.

Your gear is typical Luclin-era gear, but is behind the curve for POP/GOD era. The following suggestions are all purchaseable or single-group obtainable, and none of them are from insane places like Tipt.

A purchased elemental bow will raise you by about 88 hp.
Purchased steel rings of earthen resistance will raise you by about 40 hp.
Purchased bloodied boots of dark rites will raise you by 70 hp.
Ornate greaves (bought or looted) would raise you by 58 hp.
Ornate bracers would raise you by 46 hp.
Purchased/looted crystallized acid bracers would raise you by a further 128 hp.
Looting Pitted Vambraces from sewer trial would raise you by 70 hp.
Looting several pieces of hard LDON mission loot (ring, cape, mask, neck, earring) would raise you by 163 hp.
Purchased Halas 10 lb Meat Pies would raise you by 46 hp. Tormenter Stir Fry another 12 hp.

Augments: I'll assume you're not going to grind your way through thousands of LDON points. If you got two +25 hp augment from Natimbi/Ferubi, three +12 hp augments from normal LDON, and three +40 hp augments with LDON points, you'd gain 241 hp.

Charm: If you do four of the five POP alternate access quests, and did enough LDONs to gain the above gear, you'll gain about 70 hp.

Tribute: Increases you by up to 150 hp I believe.

Together these non-raid upgrades would put you at 8001 hp, without having even bought a piece of LDON armor, and without having upgraded your velious BP.
