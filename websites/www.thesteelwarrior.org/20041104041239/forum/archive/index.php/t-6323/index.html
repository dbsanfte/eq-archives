Kiya
Wrist: Replace the Geode bracelet with either Fine heraldic bracer (unimbued human cultural), Full mithral bracer (unimbued high elf cultural) or a Bracer of benevolence if you don't know a smith of either race or don't have the plat.

Hands: If you are Claws of Veeshan faction, get skyshrine gauntlets any way you can. They'll last you a very, very, very long time. If you aren't, a decent alternative would be kylong darkmail gauntlets from Veksar.

Face: Your current mask looks pretty decent. However, if you really, really feel like looking for an upgrade to it, I'd look for Mask of Flurishing Fungus, a Grimling Contraption, or a Mask of Frozen Mana.

Ear: I'd replace Wretched earring of pain with a Solstice earring, a mosquito leg earring or a bloodmetal earring of engagment, whichever is most do-able for your budget.

You may want to consider upgrading your sleeves in the future, either skyshrine or one of the LDoN armors (EF or NRO camps).
