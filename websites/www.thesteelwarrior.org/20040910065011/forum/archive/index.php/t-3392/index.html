Beornegar
The newbie quests are a colossal drag, and at 24 the armor will be near worthless to you in most slots. You should be able to get some half decent weapons and armor dirt cheap in the bazaar. Siphon about 300pp off your main and buy a mith 2hander and the cheapest armor you can find in the bazaar that's actually decent. (you'll sometimes see crafted, crusty, rygorr and other decent armors dirt cheap) Get a group in Netherbian lair and get one of the ac 20 breastplates that drop off the Netherbian swarm leader. Once you have decent enough kit to solo all the bandit camps in west karana without getting too low health to heal back up before they respawn, collect bandit sashes and turn them in to Chesgard the Paladin at the Temple of Thunder in South Qeynos. He'll give you a bronze weapon for each of them, which you can sell to the merchant that stands next to him. You can make about 400pp in a few hours there. Take that to the bazaar and you can probably buy a GSoT at the rate the price has been dropping. Keep doing that until you're satisified with your gear. If you can get yourself in full bronze and a mith 2h you'll be better off than I was at lvl 24, and the bandits are how I made money back then.

I personally found the newbie quests to be a lot of fun and still had the newbie gear in a few slots at 20-24. So it's not a bad start... and if you enjoy questing then I would do them. I did them I think at level 10-12 and I think it took me 3-4 hours to get every single piece. So that's 3-4 hours for a complete set of armor. Of course I had a guide who knew where to get everything....

Not everyone likes to plat farm and buy all their gear in the bazaar. :p Although that is surely the best way to gear up and get the best gear as fast as possible....

For weapons the weighty polearm is a good cheap weapon. I think the actual rec level is 50ish, but stats are decent starting mid-20's.

As for other pieces... Gothic armor is pretty good and cheap. Or resold crafted, or like was said crusty or ry'gorr. Netherbian chitin is also a good bp. For plat I actually killed greens in warrens for bronze weapons and bamboo armor. Sounds like the other suggestions below will yield money quite a bit faster though....
