As many of you know, I've been a HUGE proponent of warrior fixes on this board and our previous ezboard site. As most were when they heard about these changes, i was stoked to learn of the melee fixes coming. While the openings system hasn't been added, incite and the new disc system has been in place for a bit more than a week now. So, what is the final verdict?

My vote: AMAZING. Simply put, these are by far the best days warriors have had since late SoV. But, just saying Yea or Nay isn't enough. Let's analyze deeper:

Pro:
- Incite. I swear this skill is like getting that one birthday you've always wanted. Yea, it's not massive hate. Yes, it has an endurance cost. Yes, it has short range. Whatever. But fucking DAMN, this is what we've wanted for 3+ years. This is very noticeable on-demand agro. 400 hate, hate dot, 30 second reuse. And really, by the time the skill refreshes, you're practically at 100% endurance again. No question this is the best thing that's happend to warriors since RoK.
- Incite, part II - Ranged agro. Now, I know, it's only 100 range, and that is quite short. 150 would have definetely been nice. But holy crap, assist a puller, bust it and that mob is yours. On multiple pulls, incite is an absolute gem. If the mobs are close and you bust incite on one, the other one is all over you like Johnny Cochrin on a celebrity criminal case. This gives the mezzers and slowers ample time to engage without lubing their ass up
- Mitigation changes. Perfect. Now, 4-5% isn't massive, and when using a disc, it still comes out to the same amount as we had prior. HOwever, the bottom line is, warriors finally have non-disc mitigation better than anyone other class. Can't argue that
- Failed taunt message. Useful little tool, especially in determining when to breaK mez/use incite

Now, naturally, these changes aren't perfect. There are some cons:
- Taunt. The failed message really opened my eyes. The skill has an even lower success rate than I imagined. I thought taunt was at least successful 50% of the time. HOwever, from recent parses that I've ran, I'm thinking taunt has more of a 30-40% success rate, with annoying streaks of up to 6 straight failed taunts. NOw, I don't really know if this has always been true and I've not noticed, or if this maybe is a recent change to prevent "agro-lock." Either case, I think this is a bit low.
- EB weapons. I know, I've said this before on other threads. But I swear, since the upgrades it seems I can't for the love of me buy a proc on my BoW. Now, oddly enough, this only seems to be true on my BoW, as my Call of the Sky proc is going off at pretty much the same rate.
- Endurance. Incite is decent cost. HOwever, some of the discs are really a drain on endurance. Stonewall, which I was so excited about, is turning into little more than a raid tool due to the snare, and especially the endurance drain. Healing will; WAYY too much endurance. DEfensive is fine, haven't ran evasive yet. The offensives I haven't goten a chance to run, but I will try to do so in the next few.


Now, I know that i have a few cons listed there. Make no mistake though, the Pros absolutely demolish the cons in my mind. I wouldn't mind seeing a few tweaks on the system, especially with the taunt and endurance (I'm thinking endurance is gonna play a bigger role as openings develop). But, even if they don;t, these changes are simply awesome.

BUT, in no way should anyone try to fool themselves into thinking that the disparity between knights and warriors is gone.
