Couple other things:

- Everyone needs to be flagged by killing all the minis (and clicking the portal out of their rooms) in order to be able to zone up to Sol Ro chamber without doing gay raid pushes.

- Those that are Sol Ro Tower flagged, but not Sol Ro chamber flagged can be "pushed" into his chamber (85% rule), and even if they leave the raid won't be ported out.

- When you go up into Sol Ro Chamber, port up ready to fight, you will be aggroed by 2 relatively nasty guards on a 45 minute respawn.

- Organizing Sol Ro kills with some people flagged for chamber and some not flagged for chamber, and some flagged for zone and some not flagged for zone is a *very* major pain in the butt and time consuming process, so be ready to spend some time dealing with "raiding people in".
