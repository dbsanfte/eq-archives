Brabble
Yes, that's right--Tribunal shoulders, even, would be better.
At that point, you're pretty much in cultural/slightly better stuff and that's good at 53. You can think about LDoN augments for the weapons at that point (or, for that matter, right now).
I would say, though, that Halas 10-pound meat pies (dex/sta/agi 5, hps 30) would be slightly better for you than MTPs (str/sta/wis/int 5, hp 20) -- and cheaper.
Grobb liquidised meat (str/sta 6; int/wis -1) would get you +6 Sta at the loss of 5 Dex over the Qeynos afternoon tea, and most warriors with unmaxed sta would rather have it than dex. (the +6 str on the Grobb and the +5 AGI on the Qeynos being more or less trivial). However, Tribunal armor is heavy on the STa and light on dex, so you might not want to bother if you already have a bunch of tea.
