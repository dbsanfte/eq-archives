You get there by swimming to the bottom of the lake in LOIO where the bloodgills (who got a new model) spawn and go into a hallway thing inside the underwater building. Once you zone in, the first small area is underwater, but about 5 seconds of swimming gets you on dry land.

Mob's dont hit very hard, have good cash loot (6pp+), look like King Koopa, and drop FS-type weapons. Aggro range is pretty small.

The zone itself is humongous, the first thing you will do is get lost. The zone looks amazing, even the water looks good. There are tall, skinny waterfalls from farther than you can see... leaks from the lake (nice bit of design imo). Someplaces you go through water, most you walk.

Highest /con I found was even (58) and the hardest I got hit was 325. There are named mobs supposedly pretty tough somewhere in there but my group didn't get that far back.

however, mobs have a tiny aggro radius, you really have to be right on top of them to aggro you, so if your careful you should be able to get single pulls all the way through.
