Gnomos
OK, we fight AOW on the east side of the arena, in the SE corner where the ramp comes down from the upper level. When you get aggro, be standing against the south wall. Have the raid push him against the wall, and then towards you. You stay against the south wall, and back up as he moves towards you.

A lot of the "corners" in the arena have small posts in them, and are really 2 small corners. If you are on the east side of the arena in the SE corner, looking at the corner, you can corner him in the right small corner, but it doesn't work well in the left corner. So make sure to keep him against the south wall, and push him into the right of the two corners.
