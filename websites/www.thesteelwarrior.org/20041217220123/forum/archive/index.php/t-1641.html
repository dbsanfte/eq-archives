CS and associates (PE, ...) is not pure mitigation, but increased chances to mitigate

if u have CS2 (5% i think ?) and u where usually able to mitigate 40% of the incoming blows (number taken out of nowhere), then u now will be able to mitigate 40%*1.05 = 42% of the blows.

as the chances to mitigate are depending on AC (more specificaly mitigation AC ) and on class, more AC increase CS efficiency (ex : instead of going from 40% to 50%, mitigation chance goes from 42% to 52.5%), and at same AC, CS efficiency is dependant on class.
It prolly doesn't mean RealMitigationAC=MitigationAC*CS_mod, but this formula at least explain the way CS is working.

as a bonus, CA is working exactly the same than CS , but for avoidance AC instead of mitigation AC.

I know it is prolly innacurate, but I like to compare mitigation AC and avoidance AC for a given char to know if he will get greater benefits from CS AAs or from CA AAs ...

some more Datas for you :
AC formula (thanks to Sylphan):
Avoidance = (T from table) + trunc(Defense * 16/9), but never less than 0
Caster Mitigation = trunc(Buffs/3) + trunc(Defense/2) + (Equipment + 1)
Anyone else's Mitigation = trunc(Buffs/4) + trunc(Defense/3) + trunc(Equipment * 4/3)
Displayed AC = trunc( (Avoidance + Mitigation) * 1000 / 847 )

(table can be found at http://www.magelo.com/forum_flat.html?mess_num=16087&page_num=3)

if u want numbers for your char, magelo do the maths for you : add &debug=true at the end of your magelo profile : http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=XXXXX&debug=true
