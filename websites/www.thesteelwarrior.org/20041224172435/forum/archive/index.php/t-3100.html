I thought this was pretty neat...but then, I like numbers, and I know many people don't.

If you load up a magelo profile, (like mine)the url looks something like:
http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=280354

Try sticking "&debug=true" on the end of the url:
http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=280354&debug=true

It'll make an extra box appear with detailed info on how stats were calculated, including a breakdown of avoidance and mitigation AC.

According to magelo, it looks like AC from items is strictly mitigation AC, and AC from agility is strictly avoidance AC. At least on my magelo.
