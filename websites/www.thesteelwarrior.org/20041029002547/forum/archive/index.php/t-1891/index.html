Its probably easiest to pull him up to the hot zone in the 1st few times you kill him - that way you have an ez cr if you wipe.

His ae is like unresistable 400 pt dd + strips 1 buff, put the clerics around a corner so they dont get hit by ae. Everyone else should have some type of insta clicker (goblin earring, etc). Load up the instaclicker + 3-4 junk buffs, but hp buffs + haste on last.

He is slowable, so have everyone healing your mt till he is debuffed + slowed, after that 1-2 clerics can keep your tank up (can add 1-2 more if mana is an issue).

Just keep him slowed & beat him down.
