Kavak
Depends what you want to be. I could never see myself as a paladin really, regardless of class balance. Too snooty and stuff. I prefer to be seen as a mellee monster that relies on his honed skills to advance.

Everyone has something they would like to be, I guess at the end thats really what you have to ask yourself, because if you choose a class on pure numbers you will probably get burnt out soon anyway if its not what you want to be. I will probably quit EQ before I ever rerolled to anything else (well beserkers could change that, we will see).

For raid roles, if you have your heart set on being a leader by example and always being the centre of the fighting, pick the warrior. They have much better means of getting aggro these days, but you will still have to work for it. If you want very good utility for a hybrid, near aggro lock from a few store bought spells, and love being a good support tank class, pick paladin.
