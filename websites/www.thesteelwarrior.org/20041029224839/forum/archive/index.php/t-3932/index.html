Haass
Well, lets be serious here for a minute, not every warrior will tank boss mobs. Hell, the majority of them will probably never even tank Vindi. The raid warriors are simply the vocal minority, which makes them sound a little more prevalant than I think they actually are.

For your average joe six pack, who maybe raids 1 nite a week and doesn't do anything any more hardcore than an adventure or some shit, the BoC is NOT worth the price tag, period. It's already above and beyond the aggro that average non raid warriors will need, and you can get that fine with a SoD, or even a copper hammer these days.

If you're in a raiding guild, and high on the tanking list, then yes, a BoC is a wonderful weapon and it's probably in your best interest to try to get a hold of one because it will enable you to progress faster and easier than without one. If you're not, then don't waste the plat. A sceptre of destruction pumps out more spell based DPS (which isn't an issue since non raid warriors don't typically fight MR mobs), controls aggro in a very similar way, and is half the price to boot
