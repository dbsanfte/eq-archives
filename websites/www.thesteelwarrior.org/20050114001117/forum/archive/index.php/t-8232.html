Fitemore
07-27-2004, 07:42 AM
Venomous axe has a simple DD proc, and therefore doesn't generate additional aggro. VGLS is the same way... Pure DPS aggro. Our DPS aggro will NEVER compare to that of rogues, monks, berserkers, rangers, wizards, mages, beastlords and so on. Therefore, warriors need to get weapons that proc some sort of additional aggro to stay ahead. The Droga combo procs stun spells, which are very decent aggro and much much MUCH better than pure DPS. THAT is why the droga weapons are worth the price, even with poor stats.
