The rumor that agi helped run speed came from the fact that when you were encumbered your agi dropped and you began to run slower. It was proven false like Binn said.

Hussar
09-16-2003, 09:07 PM
DI is 500 mana, costs two emeralds, and lasts 10 minutes. It's not an exp group thing. It's not just a high end raiding thing. It's useful for whatever mobs are considered powerful enough relative to your guild. So elemental people would be using it on Reparm, HoT level people would be using it on Vindi, etc. Basically, if you get down to below 15% hps it has a chance to heal you, so no matter what you raid, if something goes wrong and you get that low, it can save you. If your cleric starting the CH rotation gets a lag spike at the beginning or slow wears off before it can be reslowed and your clerics are doing the rotation for the mobs slowed dps, etc, it just might save the raid. Though, lower difficulty stuff isn't as hard as it once was, so it's not used quite as often by the guilds that kill them. Whereas there are some elemental mobs where people use a DI rotation in addition to the CH rotation to keep the tank up.
