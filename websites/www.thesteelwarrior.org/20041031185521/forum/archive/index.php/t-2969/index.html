Zebedi
I added Ryjorkian Mist (75dd poison based) to my Hategiver (EB)

Some facts:
They both proc, independantly of each other.
Proc rate on EB wasn't noticeably changed.
The 75dd proc rate seemed to be the same as the EB proc rate.

Some speculation:
We know that certain weapons have a higher proc% than others. A Hategiver will be more likely to proc EB than a BoC. The fact that the 75dd proc I added to my hategiver was going off aproximately the same number of times per fight as EB leads me to believe that the proc rate on the WEAPON determines the number of procs for the Augment.

This would suggest that if you add a proc augment to a high frequency proccing weapon, that you'll get more bang for your buck than a lower frequency weapon.

We'll know for sure when some cleric adds a proc to a Zek hammer.
