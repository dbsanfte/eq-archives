61 war, 52 cleric (at the time)

I do not have snare weapons.
My cleric is a worshipper of Inny, and a dark elf, so I do have the "snare necklace" (which is not worth relying on, even if it is better than nothing - clinging darkness just doesn't have a lot of duration or snare-effect).

Pacify worked fine, dispelling the casters and shamen helped a LOT (gotta get rid of SoW - it'll be on every shaman and every mob near a shaman).
