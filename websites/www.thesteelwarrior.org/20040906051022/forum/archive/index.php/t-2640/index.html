I'm looking for a warrior on Xev (level 65 preferred) to assist with some tests on heal aggro hypotheses. Details of the hypotheses and proposed tests are posted below.

Please post here or email shadowfrost@eqcleric.com if you're interested in helping out, thanks.

___________________________________________________

Hypothesis: Heal spells are always n amount of hate, regardless of the actual amount healed. In other words, a Complete Healing spell that heals 1hp is the same aggro as a Complete Healing spell that heals 17,500hp.

Test:

Tank selects a non-proccing weapon combination.

(1) Choose a test mob like a Fungus Grove shroom, tank pulls it with a taunt but does not attack. Cleric casts Complete Healing on the tank immediately the mob is pulled. Number of hp healed are logged. Tank attacks, but does not taunt. It is checked how much damage inflicted is required to switch the critter back to the tank. Repeat test five times.

(2) As test one, but the cleric uses Word of Replenishment (group heal generally considered to have high aggro).

(3) As test one, but cleric allows tank to drop to low health totals before healing.

(4) As test two, but cleric allows tank to drop to low health totals before healing.

I'll consider the hypothesis disproven if the tank needs to inflict significantly more damage on the critter in order to regain aggro; otherwise the hypothesis is supported.
