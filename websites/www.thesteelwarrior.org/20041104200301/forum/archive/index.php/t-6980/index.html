I'm using an Anger III auged EoE in mainhand and unauged SoPP in offhand, and I'm pretty content with the aggro. I realize most of the aggro comes from the auged EoE, but a SoPP is nice DPS. And it not only procs a rune, but a 80hps heal as well. The heal is nice for farming stuff, solo'ing etc.

For MTing it isn't the ideal combination, mainly because of the lack of aggro on the SoPP and the extra buff, but you can tank with it and hold aggro pretty well.

What most people seem to forget is the fact that with dual-wielding you're gonna need two 1-handers :eek: The chances of a person getting an EoE or JOS and DBoTW within a few weeks are slim, so picking up a SoPP to fill up one of the spots until you get an EoE/JOS and a DBoTW is actually a pretty good option ;)

I couldn't find what weapons you're wielding right now, but if you're wielding a Bloodfrenzy or something else that would go well with an EoE or even a DBoTW, you might consider passing on the SoPP.
