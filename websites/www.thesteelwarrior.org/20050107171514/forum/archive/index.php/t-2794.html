I use the GSo4W.

The dps of this weapon is phenominal. I parsed 113dps against Master of the Guard and usually average 110dps (100dps tanking) against BoT mobs. That's not including the procs which my parser doesn't pick up, and this weapon procs its 75dd constantly. Mind you, I have no offensive AA. :)

Originally posted by Trondir
ST is farmed on Stromm, plenty of SoD around already... they do proc like mad. I am not disputing that there are plenty of SoDs around or that they can be farmed on SH. I could have an SoD if I wanted one, but until I see a parse showing that it has a proc rate significantly higher - like 2-3x more than a GBoS, then it's just an overrated weapon with a ridiculous price due to its rarity on most servers.

People complain/praise the proc rates of lots of weapons, but when you actually parse proc rates you'll find that even weapons with noticable differences like BoC vs. BF, the proc rate is not nearly as different as you think. If the proc rate of an SoD was really 3x that of other weapons with a somewhat standard 2 procs/min that most weapons seem to hover around then you would see considerably higher DPS numbers than most SoD parses suggest. If an SoD parses out at around 2 procs/min, the procs would account for about 9-10 DPS, which most parses I have seen support this. If it really proc'd 3x as much and was doing 6 procs/min then the proc by itself would account for 27-30 DPS, which would make an SoD an incredible DPS machine, which the parses do not show.
