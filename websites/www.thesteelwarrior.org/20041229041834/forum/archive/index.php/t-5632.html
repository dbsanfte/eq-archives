The tribute haste is item haste. With the release of the expansion, you now have 5 more inventory slots than you used to, they are just invisible. A tribute benefit is an item (hidden from you) that is added to one of those 5 slots. The haste items (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/itemlist.html?searchtext=percent+benefit) are 20, 25, 30, 35, and 40% item haste.

well here is some quick math. note the assumptions. your assumptions will vary.

assume i play my warrior 13 hours a week. assume i have haste on all the time. assume its 40points per 10 minutes since i honestly cant remember what it costs. assume 5 points cost 1 plat worth of items.

total cost per year is 13 hours x 6 (10 minute segments) x 40 times 52 weeks /(5 points per plat) = 32448plat.

nowthen assume i can replace my runed bolster belt (5ac) with a heavy rubicite girdle (25ac) and sell my rbb for 30k and buy the rubicite for 8k. i save 22k immediately gain 20 raw ac and have to pay 32k over the course of one year. assume also that the rbb and rubicite lose one third of their value in one year so that it would have cost 22k/3 or 7333 to hold onto the rbb for the year. subtract the 7k from the 32448k to get 25117k as my yearly cost of trading the haste tribute stat and 20 extra raw ac for the 34 or so haste on the rbb.

i gain 6 haste and 20 raw ac for 25k per year.

some people would trade their rbb for a cloak of flames worth 70k to get that upgrade on haste which i think is less than 6 percent and that 70 would also depreciate by 26k in one year's time.

what it all comes down to is ... if you have a lot of money it may be worth it. if you dont then it wont. but i know people pay 200k for certain choice items so for the likes of them it certainly appears it would be well worth it.
