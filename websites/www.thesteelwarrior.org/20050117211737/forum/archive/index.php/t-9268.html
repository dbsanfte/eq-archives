It won't matter if it's a crush, slash, pierce since it'll have the same amount of push. Special attacks like kick / bash have more push than a normal attack but they happen less frequently.

As far as pet push, it is somewhat dependent upon the type of pet. If it's a rogue type (ie some necro pets) or some mage pets that do not get a kick or bash, then the push is going to be less than a warrior type pet. Anyone that has played a BST and solos often knows that pet push can be offset by fighting on the opposite side of your pet. 2 benefits of this 1) limited mob movement 2) pet hit percentage is higher. Pets get blamed for the push when they will do relatively the same amount of push as a normal player.
