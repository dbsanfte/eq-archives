Battleblade
The three Plate tanks each have the ability to add a proc buff to their weapon. Excluding items which may be right-clicked to accomplish this, we now have;

Paladins
Ward of Nife: ~300DD vs. undead/1 hour duration
Pious Might: 90DD + stun/1 hour duration

Shadow Knight
Shroud of Chaos: 65 lifetap (much more with appropriate AA?)/1 hour duration
Shroud of Discord: 115 lifetap (much more with appropriate AA?)/1 hour duration

Warrior
Aura of Runes Discipline: When it procs, it puts a 400 HP damage absorb buff in the Bard song window for up to 5 ticks.
Duration 1 minute/Reuse 15 Minutes

Both Paladins and Shadow Knights have a weapon proc self-buff that can be kept on the weapon for all practical purposes at all times and is arguably as good as procs generally found built into weapons or obtainable with augments.

This Warrior weapon self-buff is available only ~7% of the time and although the 400HP absorb is more powerful in the amount of absorption than is found on most weapon procs (BBB, SEWS - Rune III/230 HP),mobs do hit a little harder since we moved on from Ssra and PoEarth. And the persistance of the Rune Effect buff is patheticly short (Rune III ~1 hour) vs. 5 ticks.

Compared to the weapon proc self-buffs the other two Plate tanks recieve, it's pathetic.

Compared to weapon proc buffs Warriors could do with 2 or 3 year old right-clickable items like Gloves of the Crimson Sigil and Symbol of the Planesmasters, it's pathetic.

Compared to the 100% of the time availablity and persistance of buffs like Rune III available on weapons from previous content or procs from augments, it's pathetic.

Concidering that Warriors have only 2 disciplines that can be obtained from single-groupable mobs the drop Muramite runes, I'm shocked something as weak as this that's only available ~7% of the time would even be put in the game.
