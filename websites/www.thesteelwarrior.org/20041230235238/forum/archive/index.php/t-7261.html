i wouldnt go out of my way to get agility gear, and i always kill wrulon buff as SoV puts me at 276 agility. that said, i will never turn down the agility.


i do know of tests conducted by enchanters whereby you did not get the full extent of the total domination aa line unless you had near to max charisma. ie if you had TD1 and 250 cha you got the full effect .. but needed 290 for TD2 and 305 for TD3. this doesnt have to do whith the cha's innate aide in keeping a charmed mob charmed.

it has been speculated that you dont get the full effect of the innate defense line unless you have "x" ac ... the same with agility and avoidance aa's. i'm not sure of the truth to these cases, but sony builds lots of things in this game similarly, so i wouldnt be at all surprised if its set up in a like manner.

ps- on my ranger (max defensive aa) i notice a huge difference btw 220agi and 305 agility when tanking. i havent tested it on my warrior, and i imagine the inc dmg wouldnt be as noticably different due to increased mitigatoin potential, but i can tell you that it really did make a difference on my ranger

in HoH my average dmg taken per fight from Valorium guardians were

duel wield + avatar = 8k dmg per fight average
2hander w/ 220 agility = 6.5k dmg per fight
2hander w/ avatar proc = 5.5k dmg per fight

did somewhere around 20 fights on each to make sure it wasnt just lucky/unlucky rounds.
