Just curious on peoples' opinions on this... The biggest current issue with balance seems to be stackability. A group of 6 people, requires a tank, slower, healer at bare minimum. Ideal groups add CC and a puller to that. Now, that's not always optimal and whatnot, and there is overlap between some of those roles of course.

What do ya'll think of increasing group size as a solution? The same classes fill the same required spots, however there is room for extras. For example, having 2 tanks and 2 healers might be a very viable, because you could still have room DPS after a slower and a puller. Even in a traditional group, less optimal classes still have a spot in a larger group, because the required spots can be filled with spots to spare.

Why doesn't the raid function fulfill this, you might ask. Well, not only is the experience severely decreased in a raid, you have to have the groups set up to be somewhat independent. Without easy health watching, it's more difficult to heal the members in other groups. Also, it's simple perception. If groups had room for 8 people, for example, people would get 8 people to fill it. Once a group has the basic group requirements met, they're normally much less choosy about who fits in.

Problems: LDoN would need to be rebalanced, or at least take in to consideration group size better. The max speed does make it less important though. Raids may or may not need changes. UIs would require a small update. Lots of other stuff that just isn't occuring to me right now.
