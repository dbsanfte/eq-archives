Kromagnon
It's not Min Hit + (Y x Z). It's not Min Hit + anything.

You will never get hit for the Damage Base. You get hit for DB + ( n * DI ) where n=1 to 20. Minimum hit is DB + ( 1 * DI ), bashes/kicks are DB + n where n=1 to SomeNumberLessThanMobDI.

It's also (the way I understand it) a 'post-hit mitigation', like a rune. The hit is rolled and calculated and then passed through the post-hit phase for actual damage recieved.

Mob DB/DI stays unchanged. But when that mob hits you for a min hit of 1000, you only take 1000-x% damage (where x is your shielding mod).

The question is: Does the shielding mod only kick in on minimum hit? And if that's the case, is it 'broken' or the way it is intended to work?
Iustus
Shielding reduces the DB. If a mob has a DB of 100 and you have 4% shielding, then the mob hits you with a DB of 96. Every hit will be reduced.
