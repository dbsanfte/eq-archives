Neksnappa
First off, your gear should be fine for your level; but once you start grouping in any of the planes besides justice, you'll need either really good buffs, or better gear. Unfortunately, you're to the point where a few hundred (or even thousand) plat will only get you one or two upgrades, and they'll be minor ones too.

Start doing kael raids/groups, and turn in lots of toes/heads in thurg, try to get enough faction for thurg armor. Look for thurg helm, bp, legs, and boots first and foremost. Any more pieces of fierce heraldic armor you can get will last you for another 10+ levels, so get any if you see them for cheap.

Other slots to upgrade:
Haste- Try to find a good deal on a Sash of the Dragonborn, Silvery Belt of Contention, or a Shadowy Assassin's Sash. There's also a neck-slot haste item called A Worn Golden Tooth that shouldn't be too expensive

Range- Either get a nice bow that has ac, stamina, and raw HP on it, like a Darkwood Bow of Suffering, or pick up a Goranga Idol and keep that equipped until you need to use your bow.

Neck- Either get the Worn golden tooth, or get a clattering bone necklace if you can.

Offhand- I never was a big fan of that axe, the proc never seemed to add much aggro for me. I'd say the Sword of the Bloodsworn should be enough aggro alone for now, so try getting a good ratio weapon in secondary. (Defender's Lightblade is good, or a frostbringer, they're dropping in price really fast)

Overall, the biggest upgrades for you will be buying fierce heraldic legs/boots/bp, and getting thurg armor to fill in the other slots. Your gear looks pretty good though.
