The heraldic stuff is nice, wouldn't upgrade much of that unless you had a lot better item to replace it and you will need guild for that.

Basically the earring of solstice and the neclace you have kind of suck a lot and those are what I would lose first if it was me. Earring will be expensive, neclace will be less than 1k and a few adventures to get augment for stamina to drop.
