Single target rampage that has a decent range so it's a good idea to let RT pull with DA or you risk having a caster that isn't paying attention and is sitting on inc get on rampage.

There are a few ways to fight this depending on the number of healers you have. You can either range fight it or you can MGB heal fight it. Tactics depend on your guild.

Approx 650-700k hp, max hit 1913, kick for 580, bash for 575, swings around 3.7 delay once slowed. His melee is weak so it's just taking care of rampage and keeping everyone alive with his AE if you melee him. It's good practice for elemental-type fights (ie Blazz, Babs, Wise, etc)
