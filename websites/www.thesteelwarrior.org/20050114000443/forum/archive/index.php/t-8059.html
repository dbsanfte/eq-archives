Hmm. If you do sell the Blood earring, I'm not sure I'd use the money to replace the flesheaters with a valorium. If you need cash and decide to sell the earring, I'd carefully look at all your slots for replacement ideas.
Switching blood earring for another mos. earring nets you 2 AC, 7STA, 15 Hps, and 7sv disease, but costs you 15 DEX, 15 STR, 15 AGI and 5 fire. Of the costs, only the DEX is potentially a bother; losing 15 DEX by itself isn't a big deal, but obviously as you work on maxxing your proc aggro you kinda want your upgrades to increase, rather than decrease, your DEX.
Switching out the Flesheater's for another valorium at your level nets you 5 each on fire, magic and cold saves, and 60 hitpoints and uh, 37 END. It costs you 7 AC. You might find you can get a cheaper 60 hitpoints in another armor upgrade that would not cost you AC.
