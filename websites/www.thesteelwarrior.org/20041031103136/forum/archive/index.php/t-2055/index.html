Beornegar
You'll definitely want a better haste item than 16% if you're level 52. If I were you I'd go to Gulf of Gunthak talk to the human female there that gives the melee only haste neck item quest, then go to Dulak's harbor and get the quest drops(torn luggald robes, and glass shard off the tavern wench). Saves you some cash, and it's a 2 part quest. The first item is 20% haste, the second in the series is 25% haste with some decent stats(drops are a little tougher to get). And most importantly, it's relatively easy to get the drops, and it's free. Final piece is called "Amulet of Dark Waters" or something like that. If you just have to spend your money on it, get a really good haste belt, rbg or better. There's a new haste belt in Veksar, 31%, not sure what it's selling for though.
