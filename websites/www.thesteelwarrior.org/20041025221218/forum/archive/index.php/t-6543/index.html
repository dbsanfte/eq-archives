Eriatha Egan
Air rings - Take a tracker at the zoneline and look for the following yellow con mobs on track:

apparition of wind
apparition of smoke
apparition of mist
apparition of dust

If those are up, then the Avatars are up. To spawn them, you go to their associated island and complete the ring event. For Wind, you kill the bee island. For Mist, you kill the phoenix island. For Smoke, you kill the air elemental island. For Dust, you kill the spider island. I could have Mist and Smoke backwards btw. Don't bother with Dust until you've gotten some heavy gear under your belt. It is by FAR the hardest and is the bottleneck of acquiring your Xegony Key.

As for earth rings, they always piss me off. I don't think you can ever know when exactly they're up unless you're on the clock to when other guilds or yourself have last killed them. They're pretty easy though.
