Well, the damage reduction is almost identical. They both decrease the incoming melee damage by the same amount. The only real difference is that avoidance also reduces contact procs as well. With PoP and GoD introducing tons of mob procs, avoidance probably reduces mob DPS more than mitigation. Also, AC doesn't really represent avoidance (mobs missing in any way) almost at all. Probably whatever ac gained from agi goes towards this, the rest goes towards mitigation.

As for the original question, I have to admit that I'm uncertain. You'd have to have someone without ID (perhaps without even CS) parse at various ac levels, then parse again with the added AAs. To the question of waiting or not, don't. Defensive AAs are the most important AAs you can get as a warrior.
