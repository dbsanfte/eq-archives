Ok, a little bit of history here... BACK IN THE DAY... yeah back in the day... there weren't any Copper Hammers of Striking, Swords of the Bloodsworn, Cord Hilted Spiked Driver, or Great Blades of Storm that could be attained with any bit of ease whatsoever. And by ease I mean 1-3 groups.

The supposed weapon that defined warriors in Velious was Jaelen's Katana, a 14/19 1HS with an 80 hp lifetap. Of course there was the Sceptre of Destruction, but that required ST key access and for the sleeper to be sleeping. There were VERY few agro weapons period (you also couldn't offhand the red epic sword at the time).

Move on and your list looks something like the epic, Blade of Carnage v2.0 (it wasn't always 15/20 with an enraging blow proc), and the VERY hard to get, rare, and new weapons from Luclin, inside Ssra - Blasphemous Blade (which didn't always have a proc), Hategiver, and Bloodfrenzy. When the list we were talking about was made, it was made with everybody in mind. If you couldn't get any of the weapons I just mentioned you'd of course have to settle for something else. Ichorflow was a weapon you might have to settle for.

Ichorflow at least had SOMETHING going for it besides pure DPS. Every little bit helps. It's not on par by today's standards for agro generation, but when it was added to the list, it was much better than what anybody could buy or readily find available.
