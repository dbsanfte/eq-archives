Yep, I'm just a lurker and ill-qualified to make a post like this. But I've had this idea rolling around in my head for a few days and I just HAD to share it...

Activate Weapon
Warrior Class Skill

Three ranks, 6 AAs each
Prerequisite: Combat Fury 3

This refinement of a warrior's natural weapons expertise allows the warrior to harness the magical abilities of her weapon at will. After a period of concentration, the warrior may command her weapon to use its combat effect on her next strike. The warrior may continue to attack during this time, and does not face a channeling check, although she may be interrupted by a stun.

Concentration (casting) time: First rank, 15 seconds. Second rank, 12.5 seconds. Third rank, 10 seconds.

Details: Only applies to the "Combat" effect of the weapon in the primary slot. Augment-applied effects, and casted combat innates, are not effected.
