Once you hit 65, I think the best thing you can do is focus on grinding AA's in camps that can help you with the new Omens armor quests. First off, you need the AA's. Second, might as well start collecting the parts you need for the quest. Third, some of the camps will also be giving you the required Loyalist faction. Finally, some of these camps will aslo drop decent sellables and attunable upgrades for ya. Also, once you are doing the WoS and Ruined City (and Eventually MPG) camps some decent drops from trash mobs for you.

Another area you could improve is your weaps. Some of these would make nice weap upgrades for ya: Either Droga weap, Mace of Memories, GBoS, CHSD, SoMWV, Bygone Butcher's Cleaver. Also, some LDoN's for agro augs would be good idea.

Edit1: There is not a single piece of LDoN armor that i would buy at this point. Agro augs first. AC augs next. HP augs third. All of the Armsmasters stuff is better than Royal Attendant. (If you don't anticipate getting Armsmasters BP or Legs I could see you buying RA if you just have too many LDoN pts layin around, hehe)
