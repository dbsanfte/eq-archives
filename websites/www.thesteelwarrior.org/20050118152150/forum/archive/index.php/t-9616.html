It is also a 1 hour ish crap buff for dispelling mobs. It has too long of recast to be used in battle, but can be used before a fight to space buffs, etc.

I also have found it invaluable for soloing, faction work, farming pp, tradeskill drops, etc.

If your stamina isn't maxed they are very comparable to the warlords steel guants as well to actually wear.

I did get mine as a drop so not sure I would spend plat on them, but I have used mine more than I ever thought I would. At the time I got them they were a big upgrade and I wore them until I got the warlords steel guants. I still religiously use the haste as a filler until I can be rebuffed and to front load some buffs I don't care if they are dispelled.

As someone said earlier, the haste does not stack with other forms of spell haste from an enchanter or shaman, but stacks with haste items like your belt for when you don't have better than 30% spell based haste available.
