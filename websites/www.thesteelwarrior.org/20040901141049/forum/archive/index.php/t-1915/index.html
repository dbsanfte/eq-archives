Mahhj
Somewhat new to raiding, just starting to get into the thick of things with my guild, I can tell some of them are new as well.

I try to do my job pretty well, engaging when called and not drawing aggro as best I can while doin the most damage I can. Raiding for a rogue isn't too different then exping.

My question is, when a mob gets loose on the casters, or an add aggros on a healer, should I be pole vaulting to peel it off?

Or is that the job of the paladins and shadowknights who aren't the main ones tanking (usually) anyhow?

I realize the warrior who is tanking the current monster isn't in a position to just jaunt on over and try and grab the mob off someone, so whose job is it?

Should anyone with more hp/ac be trying to get it off someone with less? Or is my job to help the warrior finish off the current mob as fast as possible so the new one can be confronted?

Thanks folks, any suggestions would be appreciated.

Redhenna
Some things to think about in that situation:

1: Communication. If you see an add on a caster, call it. Tanks tend to have their face burried in a mob, and may not see it at first. If they know as add is in camp, and where it is at, they should get it under control fast(hopefully).

2: Is the mob mezzable? If yes, then as a rogue you are almost the last choice to peal agro off. You have no agro tools outside melee damage, which will break the hopefully incoming mez. This is why pallies excell at this role, being able to peal a mob off a caster with no damage stuns, ensuring that mez will land.

3: If you have communicated that it is there, and mob is not mezzable, and no tank is jumping on it, then most likely it is best to jump on it, at least to buy time.

If it is just the mob the raid force is killing that some caster overagroes, then yes, grab agro fast if you can, then let tank taunt back of you.

Your best bet is to talk to your raid leader, and find out what he expects from you in those situations. Most raid leaders welcome questions of that type, especially if asked at a convienient time(that is, not im middle of a raid when he is buried in details).
