Predicted DPS quality using (dmg*2+bonus)/delay
Predicted hate quality using (dmg*1.25+bonus)/delay

GSot4W has 3.81 DPS quality, lance has 3.90 DPS quality. Far enough apart that the GSot4W proc probably doesn't make up the difference, even if you have WA5.

GSot4W has 2.81 hate quality, lance has 2.70 hate quality. So the GSot4W is a lot more likely to give you unwanted amounts of hate than the lance is (assuming your MT isn't using an Anger III-aug'ed BoW or something).

For DPS? I'd go with the lance. It's less hate, and more damage.
