Kinda hard to answer, but will try:

Its not an agro "miracle" cure, but yes it is noticeable and a nice additional help. I dont think its quite as much hate as 1 EB proc, 30-50% or so would be my guess.

1 proc wont make a mob snap to you or be enough to counter a slow, but 2 in a row might.

Its very handy to use if you are second in a MT rotation and you want to stay #2 on the agro list.

If getting the AHR gloves is out of the question, then I dont think you will be sorry about buying Symbol. It can give that little extra oomph when you need it. And you can always retire it to cast from inventory at a later point, so its not like it will ever be a total waste.

The proc rate is decent, it procs a tad more than the nightmares proc on my vanazir.
