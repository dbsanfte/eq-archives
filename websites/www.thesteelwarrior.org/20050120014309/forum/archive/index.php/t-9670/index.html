Actually, You can't use the lvl67 rune - if you inspect it, you'll see it excludes melee classes.

We get the following abilities for the rune turn-ins:


Level 66 (Minor Muramite Rune) - Aura of Runes Discipline
Level 67 (Lesser Muramite Rune) - No Ability Gained
Level 68 (Muramite Rune) - Savage Onslaught Discipline
Level 69 (Greater Muramite Rune) - Bazu Bellow
Level 70 (Glowing Muramite Rune) - Shocking Defense Discipline
