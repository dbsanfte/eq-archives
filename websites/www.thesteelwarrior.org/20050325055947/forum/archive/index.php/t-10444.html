They are in different places.

Vicar is in the middle of the 'Gazebo' surrounded by quite a few other mobs. ph a chaplain of Fire.

Sorcerer is on the lower level platform after you come in from the top part, at the far end with a soldier/defender directly behind it. ph a magus of Fire

Captain is in one of the central huts, as you look towards charm birds its the top right one. He is I think the 2nd one from the left in the house. ph a soldier of fire.

Coercer is on the top level on the far side of castle 1, ph a diviner of fire.
