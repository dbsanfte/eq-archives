MB is a 100% kill, if you do it right.

The first thing you need to realize is his AE silence is radius 100.

The second thing you need to realize is he has 4 stackable dots, 900/tick, all radius 100.

The third thing you need to realize is, if the raid gets dotted, blood aggro will be a major problem. You can't taunt off of blood aggro.

The fourth thing to realize is supernal remedy is range 200. Clerics can stay outside all the AEs and heal using remedy.

The fifth thing to realize is MB only has like 150k hp. If you have a stable fight, you can take your time killing him, with a small number of people.

Raid force: 1 group on each corner to keep all the spiders dead.
1 MT
4-5 clerics
a group of rangers.

When MB goes live, tank runs in, beats on him a bit to get aggro. Pull him to the MIDDLE of the south wall. Don't pull him to a corner, or he will be able to AE people out in the hallway through the wall, as it is an outdoor zone. Once aggro is established, the rangers run in to the north wall, where they are also out of the AEs. Then they plink him dead.

MB is not a zerg fight, it's a ranged fight. All you have to do is set it up right. Use your knights on the corners to keep the spiders dead.

The alst thing to realize about this fight is that when MB is dead, you haven't won yet. often you will have spiders still aggroed on the corners. You need to extract your raid force from aggro fights so they can hail the gnome, without having tons of spiders aggro the raid and wipe you. So, once MB is dead, the rangers then run to a corner, and kill spiders til the group is clear of aggro. Then run to another corner and do the same thing. Work your way around to each corner, until there are no more ongoing fights, and all the spiders are peaceful. Finally, have a monk tag the gnome and pull him out into the hallway, and FD. This way people won't take 4K DD from going in teh room to hail him.

This is what we finally came up with way back when we were learning this, and it's how we still do it.


CC is key. We put enchanters in all four corners outside MB room and a bard and druid on each side or each corner if enough. Everyone kills spiders til MB becomes targetable, then enchanters start to mezz new spiders and we clean up any existing ones by the doors. Once both sides are clear of agro, we engage.

Melee engage him with MT at north wall and keep him there, all ranged dps hits him from south wall - rangers, mages, wizards, necros, etc....

Healers are south using long range heals.

As soon as he dies, everyone exits room on same side (we use east.) Monk tags gnome out and loses agro.

CC groups either gate or die.

We use bestow DA or bard DA on whoever wins drops to run back in and loot their item.

MB dies now in less than a minute, but for new people who need the flag they will have lower dps so two minutes probably for the fight. You need good CC to keep a half dozen or maybe more spiders mezzed for a couple minutes. We use bards as runners outside. They can help mezz, or they can pickup spiders that get missed and bring them back to the enchanter, etc... Druids with enchanters can heal if needed and can also snare or root spiders.

This thread is pissing me off, it bugs me everytime a guild tries to zerg this encounter just running everyone they can into the room to get hossed out by his AE Silence and AE DoTs. First thing you have to realize is that the Manaetic Behemoth has very little HP. In fact you could 2-3 group the retard if it werent for his script.

There in lies the key. You CAN 2-3 group the behemoth, and you do.

First I start off with the MA group. The WAR who is gonna tank behemoth, a cleric or two, and then any RNG WIZ MAG we have.

From there I just fill up as many groups as we can as we have RNG WIZ MAG. I usually toss a CLR/DRU or two in the groups to kinda "reserve" them for being on spot heals for the WAR.

At this point you will have everyone who is going to actually be bothering with the behemoth grouped up.

1x WAR, all RNG MAG WIZ, whatever CLR/DRU you want to be spot healing you (4-5 is more than enough).

It's at this point you throw everyone else into spider groups. First I toss 4 invites to people i know are competant enough and tell them to form the most balanced group they can.

This sets up the 4 core spider groups. NE SE NW SW corners. From there I just have people form up as many balanced groups as there are people left over. I usually set them to "wander" groups I have them if i only have 1-2 extra.

So now we have 3 types of groups

DPS Groups (all WIZ RNG MAG, 1x WAR, whatever spot healers you want)
Spider Groups (4x): NE SE NW SW corners to kill spiders.
Wander Groups: I usually set them West or East walls and have them split up evenly at first to their corresponding North/South spider groups. They are the people who are "on call" for their side.

By on call I mean I ask all the spider groups to let me know asap if they're backlogging, I will then call the resident "Wander" group to go help them temporarily.

So now we have the raid setup.

Go place them in all 4 corners, two corners per side of MB room. They have that little lamp in them.

The DPS Groups setup on the West wall directly in front of the behemoth's door.

After everyone gets setup, you make the call to start killing spiders. It's at this point everyone except your DPS groups start hossing spiders. This is otherwise known as "Yay, look at the raid leader xp move" mode. You commence this for 10 minutes or whatever the timer is to get him to become "live".

There is a secret method I use here which negates any possibility of a wipe, accidental agro, overagro, etc. It enables my raid to disc/burn from the second the MA engages and its virtually impossible to take agro from him. However that is the part of my strat I will keep secret (at least for now).

But that little method is not needed.

Next you have your MA run in, pop defensive, blow Incite, and start hossing the hell out of him for about 10-15 seconds to work up agro. At this point you call rangers disc and light nukes. At about 80% Call full burn. I also tell all the shadowknights in the raid to break from their spider group, run to the room, blow Harm Touch, then resume to their spider group. Thanks to the DX9 patch no one has to keep the door open for arrows to hit, but somsone should anyways just for visual purposes.

Basically the only person in the room is the MA. Your 3 groups of DPS (2-3 work fine) blow up the behemoth like butter, and grats a flawless kill. The important thing is to KEEP KILLING SPIDERS even after he dies. Leader runs in, checks loot, awards it quickly, people loot it quick and return to spider groups. Once you're done with the corpse you call ALL MOVE TO EAST WALL.

It's at this point EVERYONE in the raid regardless if they have spiders or not. It's critical EVERYONE moves no matter what is going on. They just run through the behemtoh room if they're on the west side, and condense down to the behemoth door if on the east side. This will place your entire raid right at the flag gnome. You then tell everyone to kill any spiders that are agro, but do not attack new ones. Once you're clear of spider agro hail up your flags.
