Sassinak
Hate becomes an analytical issue once you have a damage bonus. The more "damage bonus per second" a weapon dishes out, the more natural hate it produces. That's the gist of the theory that you read in the library, anyway.

At levels 27 and below you do not have a damage bonus, and at levels 50 and below you do not get a special 2-hander bonus. So your only concern should be DPS right now, and procs once you have some available to you.

Revisit the aggro issue when you reach your 40's and most weapons start proccing for you, and then revisit it again at level 51 when your 2-hander bonus kicks in.

I always read Haass posts with interest.
It looks to me like you have plenty of weapon for the job at your level. Use your bow, taunt, provoke and /shield properly and your lack of aggro should never be the primary cause of a group member's death.
Any first-character warrior who reads this should note what Haass said about the weapon he had. Centi Short Swords even today will do very nicely until you are 40ish; if you don't have Uber weapon #64, don't worry.
There are two main adavantages in holding aggro before level 50: first, your group will expect you to; second, you should be the most efficient person in your group at damage mitigation. The second factor here is important in experience groups, because the faster you kill stuff the better off the group is. All groups will have some sort of bottleneck; sometimes it's the respawn rate at certain camps, but most often the bottleneck is healer mana.
Well-equipt monks, rangers and rogues can gank your aggro, but if they do, they will ultimately slow down your group's experience, because they take more healer mana than you do. You have to do what you can to keep the mob's attention, but other classes have to figure out how to do max damage while riding under your hate, too.
Argin
Like Doggabone said, the haste an item gives doesn't have anything to do with your character's level. Items have a "level" that is static and is used for determining this haste, lucy includes this in the item's info (for example, go to lucy.allakhazam.com and look up cloak of flames, if you click on the raw data there's a field called "level", which is 35 for CoF, haste is item level +1.)
Brutul
