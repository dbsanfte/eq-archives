It is actually very easy to max out your weapon skills in the early levels (50 and less) if you switch your weapons after you have maxxed your skill for each level. Make sure you have several different low-delay weapon types.

Some things to keep in mind:

Your dex stat will affect your skill-up rate. Get a dex buff when trying to raise your skill level.

Piercing is one category. There is no 1-handed and 2-handed seperation.

You cannot get skill-ups on green con mobs.

Just keep an array of weapons on you at all times, this way you can swap weapons in and out as you gain skills.
