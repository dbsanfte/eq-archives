First off, If you intend to only have the warrior use the cudgel I would sell it. It has nothing for agro. You are better off with the CHoS and the SoTBS. I really cant see too many upgrades that would be a huge improvement. I would try to pick up a planar bow of some sort, Featherwood, Obsidian Wood, etc...

Other than that, you could either try to save for a Helm of Zek, or you could just do LDoN's when you get to 60 and then Hard missions when you get to 65 to get upgrades. As it stands, you pretty much have the best buyable gear, aside from the Helmet of Zek and a new BP.

While you *could* buy Earrings of Engagement, Cloudy Ring of Pnuematic force, etc...the gains would be pretty minisucle for the amount of cash you spend.
