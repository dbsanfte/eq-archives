Disciplines explained
Note - as you level, the refresh time on disciplines decreases. For instance, by the time you reach level 60, the refresh time for Evasive will be lower than the refresh time for Defensive.

All disciplines share the same refresh timer. If you use Mighty Strike at level 54, you can't use another discipline at all for 60 minutes.

A word on Evasive vs. Defensive - Evasive is best when the lower refresh time is advantageous and when a mob has a low DI and a high DB. Also Evasive is more effective when your AC is high in relation to a mob's ATK and is handy in avoiding mob procs. Defensive cuts a mob's DI by half. For more on DB and DI, check this thread.

Evasive
Obtained: level 52
Effect: You hit less often but are also hit less often. Lasts for 3 minutes and has a base reuse of 15 minutes (7 minutes @ level 60).

Charge
Obtained: level 53
Effect: You hit more often but in turn are hit more often. Lasts for 14 seconds, has a base reuse of 30 minutes (23 minutes @ level 60).

Mighty Strike
Obtained: level 54
Effect: All of your successful hits are critical hits. If you're beserked, your hits become crippling blows. Lasts for 10 seconds, has a base reuse of 60 minutes (54 minutes @ level 60).

Defensive
Obtained: level 55
Effect: Melee damage received and given is cut by 40%. Lasts for 3 minutes, base reuse of 15 minutes (10 minutes @ level 60).

Furious
Obtained: level 56
Effect: All melee attacks received in your frontal arc will be riposted. Lasts for 9 seconds, base reuse of 60 minutes (56 minutes @ level 60).

Precise
Obtained: level 57
Effect: You have a greater chance to hit but also a greater chance to be hit. Lasts for 3 minutes, base reuse of 30 minutes (27 minutes @ level 60).

Fell Strike
Obtained: level 58
Effect: Your base weapons damage is multiplied by 4. Lasts for 12 seconds, base reuse of 30 minutes (28 minutes @ level 60).

Fortitude
Obtained: level 59
Effect: You cannot be hit by melee combat while this discipline is in effect. Lasts for 8 seconds, base reuse of 60 minutes (59 minutes @ level 60).

Aggressive
Obtained: level 60
Effect: Melee damage given and received is increased. Lasts for 3 minutes, base reuse of 27 minutes.
