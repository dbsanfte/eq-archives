I'll just use this opportunity to make a shameless reference to my gear guide post "Indeathinite's High-End Gear" or whatever I decided to name it. Check it out. You have good gear in mind, and I explain why I chose the items I did and what bonuses you can receive from them--most of the items you have listed are on my list as well.

As for +Def...I am having trouble with these items, myself. Not acquiring them, for I have already done that for the most part. My biggest problem lies in the question of whether or not you can have "too much defense." Sure, it makes you take less damage, but does it make you a better tank? I am currently investigating this. The drawback with so much armor/+Def items is that with so much dodge/block/parry/damage mitigation, are you generating enough rage to have a sufficient supply to use your threat-generating abilities. So far I haven't had enough time to test the balance between these things, but after 425 defense it seems as if +Sta gear becomes much more important. You've successfully reduced the critical hit rate on yourself to almost slim-to-none, and the additional defense doesn't seem to make as much of an impact as previously thought.

I have my own questions about this, and I intend to do investigations on such whenever I have enough time. As for now, I do not know.
