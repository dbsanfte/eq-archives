I know that you were asking about quested armor, instead of bought armor, but I reccomend picking up some of the new armor made with the LDoN tempers. Gothic is the best warrior armor, with plusses to strength and stamina, as well as magic resist on all the pieces. Calcified armor has bonuses to agility; deep cavern has dexterity; frosted has intelligence; and sandblasted has wisdom. All of these pieces have better armor class than crafted. Lots of people are using these recipes to skill up, and most of them are using the forge in PoK (they have to buy the superb leather paddings from the dwarf there, anyway). Check the vendors in the forge building to see if tradeskillers have sold their stuff and the vendors are reselling cheap. Or talk to one of the tradeskillers and see if you can work something out. I started giving away for free all my calcified breastplates and greaves, because I couldn't get rid of them all in the Bazaar. I think these pieces are a great mid-level item.

You can use a mixture of Gothic, Calcified and Deep Cavern to get good ac and fairly good stats. I recommend trying to get sta the highest then dex then agi then str. Str is probably the least important except for the fact of the DE so the Gothic sounds like the best way to go and then try to augment the armor. Then, when she saves enough she can buy some armor from the Adventure merchants. MM has good stuff for 150 points each, Takish has ornate quality gear, and I cant remember about the other ones. She can also get some good weapons this way and get some plat to spend, while also leveling up. Fast weapons are the best at lower levels when working on skills so find a fast weapon and damage doesnt matter too much. GL with the twinking.
