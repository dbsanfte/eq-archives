All the priest classes have their advantages for duoing with a warrior. Cleric has rez and heals the best, but has very little utility, Shaman has slow and more utility, but doesn't heal that well. Druid is a good balance of healing and utility. Since you already have the cleric I would just go with that. No other class is enough better to warrant leveling a new character, and with a ToD a cleric is hard to beat. Having rez is very nice. Main drawback to the war/cleric (that's what I do when I 2-box) is lack of invis.

Also, with the cleric/war combo, you'll be able to pull singles using pacify in a large number of areas. The shaman/warrior combo would have to either find easier to pull hunting areas, or you'll be tanking multiple 70% slowed mobs at once.

If you can get your hands on a Truncheon of Doom and some Cobalt Greaves, you'll be sitting beautifully. You can invis others with the Greaves. Failing that, an Incarnadine BP for the warrior and potions/vanishing devices for the cleric, or potions/devices for both would be great utilities to keep stocked at all times.
