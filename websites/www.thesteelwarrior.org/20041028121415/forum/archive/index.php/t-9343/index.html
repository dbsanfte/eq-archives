STA is easily maxed in any normal exp group. You can either:

A) Have a shaman cast STA on you and it should more than max it if it already isn't
B) Have a Beastlord cast STA
C) Get some Kizrak's Greaves and click them for some STA

Next, if you use the commonly accepted formula for the tradeoff between AC/HP you get 1AC = 6HP. So get a lot more bang for your buck if you get the AC augments, simply because 10AC is about equal to 60HP, or slightly less plus you're paying less than half of what the HP one costs.

If you really want some HP augs just get some Kyanite from OoW zones and have a JCM3 crafter shine them up for you. They turn into 45HP augs and they can be put in three different slots, leaving the rest for AC.
