Hi Lathen,

AA Mitigation is a chance to cut down the damage of a hit that was incoming to you to a lower DI Standing. EI, Max hit 19DI, down to 9DI, for example. Defensive is a "Hard on" ability which definatly takes it down halfway, and furthermore you have the chance of mitigating it down even more, to my experience.
Arathena
To the best of my knowledge, the two abilities stack: However, CS/ID does not change the DI + DB of a mob: A mob that hits 950 will always hit 950, regardless of AA. Instead, the damage distribution of the mob is altered, such that the mob is less likely to actually hit you for 950, in such a way that the over time value is the appropriate % less.
