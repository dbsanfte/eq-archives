I've done Dozekar 23 times now, probably half of those on my own 6boxing 65 war/clr/dru/enc/nec/mag. Don't underestimate Dozekar as he's a decent fighter when he isn't slowed. Even tashed and mala'd he can be hard to slow and I've had 3 (pretty common) to 9 resists in a row. There are some keys to him and understanding them can make him pretty trivial:

1) MT must have a clicky item OR have a group member cast a buff on him to act as a clicky. The same is true for anyone else staying in his AE range, for me doing group MR on the enchanter makes it very easy, I however keep a clicky on my warrior. Take the time to order your buffs and put a good 6 or so junk buffs up in there, keeping the vital ones for each class very low on the list, just in case. He doesn't cast that often, but pre-planning doesn't hurt any either.

2) His AE debuff with 400dmg is unresistable, hence follow rule 1.

3) Positioning him and keeping healers out of AE is helpful, but not 100% necessary. However, if you're cutting it close, why add the extra risk? I fight doze in the fire pot to the left as you face into HoT. I place all my casters to the left wall, before the fire pot (basically before the wall indents for the fire pot)

4) Yes, he can be done in all Skyshrine armor as that was how I did him about 6 times, since then I've added some ornate armor and more recently EP level armor (very little). That has only made him easier, it was never necessary in the first place. Can you do him with the group you have? I cannot say, but I can say it will be a decent challenge. When I first did him it was hard for my druid and cleric to keep up on the heals when he's unslowed, so you must slow him asap and keep him slowed, do not let that slip. Once slowed, you will have a long fight ahead of you, very very long. I do him in 23minutes by having one toon at a time go up and nuke (must be in melee distance) until I need to reslow. My necro keeps a lot of dots on him (about 1400/tick) fairly consistent through the fight, so with just a war/sk on him your DPS will be much lower.

Basic info I've gathered on Dozekar:
Dozekar the Cursed
This mob [Summons] [Enrages] [Casts Spells]
Max Hit 900
Ave Hit 405
DPS 138
Max Spell 400
Average 19 min 56 sec to kill
Average Total HP 371,604 (he has high regen, fastest kill was 6 or so minutes and we clocked him at 320K then)
20 wins out of 23 encounters

If you can do Dozekar then the other minis will not be a problem. The problem, sort of, will be getting to them. You will have to clear your way to them unless your SK is top notch and ballsy enough to run to the minis and pull them to the first entrance way. I do this with my warrior and have the mage CotH me back up to the doorway and it can be tough making the runs. If you can get an enchanter with Dire Charm AAs then grab a wyvern from NToV and you can plow through to the minis quite easily. You will also be able to do 2 at once if necessary, once slowed (pet can offtank a slowed mini, with some backup healing).
