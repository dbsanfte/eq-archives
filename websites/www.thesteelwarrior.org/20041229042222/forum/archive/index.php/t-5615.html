Stun Resist is pretty much for spells only. The ~70% physical stun resist that SE3 gives is more than enough for just about anything. Sure it'd be nice if it stacks like how you think it does (i.e. +5% stun resist = 75% vs physical) but it's really not necessary.

While you can't get enough stun resist to use it as part of your strategy, every stun you resist helps, whether it's aggro if you're tanking or just more DPS if you're not.
