Here's somepropositions about what devs can do about displines, while keeping in mind that discipline should not overpower us or trivialise (too much) the old content.

1st proposition : Unhook discipline times.
It could be a great idea, but very unbalancing against some encouters. for exemple any bossmob01, you engage him with Furious, you chain with Untouchable, then defensive... that is almost 20 seconds without any melee damage, solid initial aggro and defensive mitigation for 3 mins... unpraticable.
So the idea is to unhook discipline by type :
Long duration / short duration , or
offensive / defensive

2nd proposition : tune current disciplines :
Some discipline are useless as they are, some others are just candy eye, and there is others we cannot use because the reuse time is waaay too long.
- Resistant : every melee and hybrid got this one... However it is useless as it is, especially because resist were revamped. to justify the reuse time, this disc must give the equivalent of the level of the player in all resist (+65 to all at 65). so we may consider use it in certains situations.
- Fearless : well no idea on this one.. I will never use it unless we can see "so and so begins to cast fear" :p.
- Evasive : it's good as it is.
- Defensive : good too.
- Precise : well, this one is a sort of mini-aggressive.. I never seen the utility once I dinged 60. maybe reduce a lot the reuse time, and transform it in a 100% tactical mastery (well 100% with TM3)
- agressive : this one need a reuse time very short too to be usefull... also retune the downside : getting hit for 1000 by a mob who normally hit for 800 is awful.
- Charge, mightystrike : candyeyes... but a candy that can't let us use defensive for 30 mins is baaad, reduce reuse time
- Furious, Untouchable : I think they are good as they are... but the reuse time is, again, too long.

Also I think all the 3 minutes disc should be retuned to not have the downside anymore. it was needed for Kunark, it is no more.

3rd proposition : Add new disciplines for 61-65 :
Here's some idea :
- disc level 61 : for 12 seconds you cannot be hit by any magic effect (heal included :p).
- disc level 62 : for 3 minutes, all you ripostes will infict 4x damage, while all your parry and dodges have a chance to become ripostes.
- disc level 63 : for 8 seconds, your weapon proc every swing.
- disc level 64 : for 3 minutes, all magic damage will be reduced by 2/5.
- disc level 65 : for 3 minutes, you will have the combined effect of both aggressive and defensive (long reuse time here :p)



Comment, flame, add, at will :).
