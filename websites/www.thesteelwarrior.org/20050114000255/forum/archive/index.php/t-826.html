As you approach the level that the Windblade was intended for it will crank out some better DPS for you.

A few things to keep in mind though, warriors aren't damage dealers. Other melee classes WILL outdamage you. I am not sure what your guild is trying to kill but the easiest DPS upgrade you could make is a better haste item IMO. You will have a hard time though getting one for under 10k though.

Eodin has some good advice. The Frostbringer will last you a long time. I used one until 63 when I finished my epic. The GCM is another very good weapon for the money.

Your weapons are certainly fine for your level. I still do not have a Windblade at 63 and probably never will. I can't justify spending 30k to upgrade from a 45/44 to a 50/44 2hs.
