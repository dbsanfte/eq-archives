I would strongly suggest the alternate access quests to the Planes of Power zones. There is a quest for every zone pre-Sol-Ro. They're a lot of fun for a group and result in the whole group getting access to the zone, a great charm (and then nice upgrades to the charm) and a free AA point.

I suggest starting with the Crypt of Decay quest and leaving Halls of Honor, Plane of Torment, and Bastion of Thunder for last.

Once you have done those, you can start working on your ornate armor quests.
