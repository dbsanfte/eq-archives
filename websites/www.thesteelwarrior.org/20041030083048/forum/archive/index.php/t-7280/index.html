If I were you, I would strive to get "real" aggro weapon(s)... either a 2hander or 2 1handers with stun, debuff, or pure +hate procs. The cleaver you have is a great dps weapon, but relatively poor when it comes to aggro. The general consensus among warriors is that you're better off keeping a dps weapon a dps weapon, and give hate/stun augs to your aggro weapons.

Basically, aug'ing this weapon with an anger or ykesha aug would make it a decent aggro weap... but buying a GSoT for 400pp and putting an anger or ykesha aug in it would be better in terms of overall aggro.

Aug the cleaver with a lifetap or +dd proc, and try to get some weapons with stun, debuff, or +hate procs to aug and use as aggro weapons.
