Brabble
You're set for weapons, it looks like, for a good long time.
BP is good.
In a few levels you'll want more haste. There are a lot more haste options now than there used to be, and you might be able to use your shaman to get some of them. There's a thread on warrior haste items in the library, but look around at your options.
I really don't know MM cultural armor options for frogs. Might not be any. the gothic stuff is what you should look at changing out first, I think. If you just want to upgrade enough for a few levels (as opposed to stuf that will take the warior to 60-65) you could look at

Barbed ringmail (from Velks; e.g. legs:AC: +25 Str: +3 Sta: +7 ),
steel wrapped (drops in Nadox; e.g. legs: AC: +27 Fire Resist: +4 Disease Resist: +4 Cold Resist: +4 Magic Resist: +4 Poison Resist: +4 HP: +45 Mana: +30),

These would in the 2k plat range each, I think. they are wearable by frogs, and woudl certainly be upgrades to gothic. Odd bits like cobalt are around too, though depending on the piece would cost more.
Kylong darkmail stuff is around too, but it's (no drop?) out of Veksar, so would be tough for your frog to get at the moment, I think.
Other kylong stuff is dropable. The kylong shinguards (AC: +35 Sta: +4 Wis: +6 Int: +6 Agi: +8 HP: +45) are pretty good. Haven't priced 'em, but would guess in the 5K range.

Something in there might be a place to start.
