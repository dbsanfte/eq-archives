Drops a REAL nice neck, that literally a level 35 can get. Sonic Wolf Choker, i had it till i got my shaderock amulet. Drops off Scarflank in south sonic wolf camp.

Havn't done it in a while, but basically you kill the named spawns to get one camp to attack the other. Once one race has control over the entire place, the place where the Vah Shir normally are will get overrun by that race and a "leader" will spawn. you can camp the minor 3 camps forever as long as you don't kill the leader. To respawn the war, of course, you kill the leader.

Oh, the owlbear mama leader, she drops a +3% meditate FT1 range O.O. well worth the RvR. GL~
