Llador
As noone has mentioned it...

As the Spiroc Lord is the boss when he dies, Sirran the Lunatic will spawn over the corpse. You will probably know he is the NPC you need to get the keys from. Once up he remains for 20mins unless you hail him to keep him up (for players awaiting rezzes etc). If you fight near him he will aggro, As he doubles for 2k that is not advisable :p

My advice would be, assuming the Isle is clear except for the Guardian (a static mob to the right of where you port up) and Lord, as follows:

From the port up go clockwise around the edge until you are on the straight to the Lord. Pull the Lord to that corner so Sirran will be some distance away when he pops. Once the Lord is dead then all move through the middle of the Isle to near the Lords spawn point and pull him away to fight out of aggro range from his repop and Sirran. If he is wielding a sword then he drops the epic piece.

If you have a bard with the Fading Memories skill then there is no problem keep pulling the Lord to the same spot and the bard can pull Sirran away out of range and use Fading Memories. Just watch the 20min timer.

Lathen
My guild will be going to the Plane of Sky shortly. I'll post our experiences here once we've had a go.

I did a great deal of prowling around the web to get info on these encounters. Some of the best was right here on The Steel Warrior. Here is the plan I've developed based on those posts. If anybody sees any glaring mistakes, please do speak up!

The Mobs:
1) There are three groups of "trash" spirocs on the island that need to be cleared. In each group, there will be a mob called "the Spiroc Vanquisher". This mob must be killed first. If he is still up while you're killing the other mobs in his group, they will respawn immediately.

2) There is a mob called "the Spiroc Guardian" that you have to deal with in some way. You can perma-spawn the boss of the island ("the Spiroc Lord") by keeping the Guardian alive. You would do this if you wanted to farm the warrior epic drop. If we decide to do this, one method is to pull the Guardian aside and keep him more or less perma-mezzed.

3) The boss of the island is "the Spiroc Lord". He is the mob who drops the Spiroc Wingblade for the warrior epic. If he has the drop, you will see the weapon equipped on him. He death-touches every 45 seconds, so he will probably kill at least 2 people every fight. In addition to the death touch, he has an AoE lightning strike for 300 or so. When he dies two things can/will happen:
a) "Sirran the Lunatic" will spawn over the Lord's corpse. This NPC will instantly join into any fight that is underway, and since he hits for 2k, this is a bad thing. No other fights should be going on when the Lord dies.
b) if the Guardian is alive, a new instance of the Spiroc Lord will pop at the original spawn point.

The Plan:
So, if we intend to spawn the Spiroc Lord more than once, here is what the various gurus recommend:
1) Clear the "trash" groups making sure you kill each group's vanquisher first.
2) Split the guardian away from any mobs around him and park him with a mez. Assign an enchanter to be his little friend, re-mezzing him as necessary.
3) Pull the Spiroc Lord off his spawn point and kill him as fast as you can. Rez people back into the fight as quickly as possible.
4) When he dies, loot quickly and silently and move as far away from Sirran the Lunatic as you can.
5) Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you have all the drops you need/want.
