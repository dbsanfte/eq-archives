The whole route from zone in to any wing only requires about 10 successful pacifications. ER+pacify works about with 75-80% success rate on the sentries & guardians. High level root can take care of those catastrophic resists, or you may prefer to keep gate up on the cleric, and just gate out and run back in the event of aggro. With the 1 hour spawn rate, you should have no trouble running the cleric back in from zone in after gating, and you should be able to reach the wing of your choice in about an hour.
