is the ring of slime event worth doing? How long does it take? how many people do you need?

Yeah it's worth doing. There are two noteable *awesome* pieces of loot that come out of the event. One of them is the 15/20 15sta 100hp rogue dagger and the other is the 25ac 8sta 100hp warrior bracers. Both are incredibly rare. :P

Difficulty wise, the RoS is not hard. The Acid Mass casts a wicked AE though, so you absolutely must resist it every time. To do that, you must have your MR higher than 250. For warriors, this isn't hard to do with buffs, a bard, and our MR advantage, but other classes may find it slightly harder to do.

The Acid Mass is fully slowable, thankfully. He quads for 700's, if I remember correctly, and dishes out ~100-120dps over the course of the fight to a fully defensive AA's warrior. The fight itself is -LONG-. The bastard has a ton of hp. Let me take that back. The bastard has a metric fuckton of hp with Luclin style ac. Expect the fight to be ~20 minutes or more, as he has anywhere from 750 to 850k hp.

He drops 1 to 3 loots every time. Usually when he drops 3 loots, 2 of them are duplicates. His loot is either insanely awesome or absolutely pathetic, so expect to be let down a few times. :(
