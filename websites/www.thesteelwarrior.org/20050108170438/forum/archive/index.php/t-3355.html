They respawn instantly.

We use a single group to do CC on emperor. This group consists of two Shammies, two SK's, and two Enchanters.

We pretarget all mobs, with temporary offtanks assigned to the 4 "secondary" mobs. Each of the 4 crowd ocntrol people also have both of their assigned targets pre-targetted (using the "toggle target" hotkey)

We pull blood, enchanters mez their primary targets, toggle and mez secondary targets. SK's engage primary targets, build some agro, then toggle and engage secondary targets. Shammies slow offtanked mobs, mages and shammies debuff mezzed mobs (malo/'sini)

From that point, the enchies can easily keep two mobs each mezzed, and the SK's can easily offtank two mobs each. The shammies keep everyone healed and the offtanked mobs slowed.

Once the 8 are locked down, I call assist on blood, we kill him and move into position for Emp.

Crowd control is key, and I strongly recommend you take your team to Ssra sometime when the emperor is not up and practice locking down his room a few times. Make your CC team hold their mobs for 30 minutes.

Once you have that part of the encounter down, the Emperor himself is fairly easy, so taking the time to practice when he's not up makes it much easier to ensure a success when you have the competition breathing down your neck and it's do-or-die on the Emperor himself.
