Loosscru
The key to doze is insta clicks. He AE dispells the first buffed slot and he does it often. So just keep your buffs up and tanking him is easy. We always pull him to the HoT entrance just like any other AE HoT mob. He is so close that it isn't a hard pull. Depending on the mana pools of your clerics and their AAs even one HoT level equiped Cleric can heal a HoT level tank.

I did my first doze at 60 with about 6k buffed and we had 3 clerics. The third cleric got so bored that he was meleeing and nukeing. The hard part is that it can be a long fight, doze has allot of hp and some nice regen. Good luck.

Oh, and AoW cornering is when you back the MT into a corner so no one else can get riposed. Not realy needed on doze because your rangers will likely be blocked on arrow shots because doze is small enough to get wedged behind the fire pot. For this reason we always fight him in the open.
