Gonna throw another thought out that occured to me recently.

A few of the threads here come out and say that warriors need solidarity to get 'fixed' and that we all need to come to some agreement about what the problem is and how we want it fixed to present to the Devs or whatever.

Throughout these threads where we have countless warriors giving examples of how they are less effective in XP groups than knights and that the advantages of knights agro control make a warriors mitigation advantage not enough to earn us the tank spot in XP groups.

To counter these though there are quite a few warriors from Elemental+ guilds who are saying that the opposite is the case. Warriors are the #1 group tanks and things are infact in our favor, something I can believe looking from the perspective of a warrior with a BoW and decked out in Raex's (which isn't me btw). Not gonna do any quotes here, but Karthunk has been a fairly strong advocet of this in earlier threads if you want to look it up. (go go search feature)

Thinking about this has lead me to a few new conclusions which hopefully arent justing retreading the same old BS~

When I used to LFG in PoS/V as (at the time) and unguilded 65 warrior, 4.3k hps unbuffed, flesh grinder + red epic for weapons - so I considered myself very middle of the road - the people I was competing for group spots were not warriors from the top guilds on my server, but warriors usually from guilds raiding NToV / starting to hit Ssra, and below. The knights I was competiting with however ran all the way from elemental equiped knights from the server's top guilds down to unguilded knights in cultural / velious gear.

So the thought this gives me is that when the top tiered guilds aren't raiding, they use warriors to tank in their XP groups. Sure when you're XPing with your guildmates you have the advantage that people will probably control agro better and know each others play styles better which is a situation more favorable to warriors tanking, but it suggests that warriors are once again the preferred tanks at the high end for XP, which only goes to compound the issue further for warriors levelling through the 60s in pickup groups / teir 2 planes because not only are they competing against knights in their own league who are now given the nod as being the prefered tank because of agro control, but they are also competing against paladins / SKs from raiding guilds often equiped in Ssra / VT / Elemental gear that give them a huge AC / HP advantage over an lower tiered guild / unguilded warrior in cultural / velious gear. It certainly wasnt uncommon for me to be grouping with knights who had upto 1k HPs unbuffed more than me in PoV. Who are you going to let tank?

So the 'problem' we have with class balance isn't just how to fix warriors in the XP game, but how to make warriors more effective in the tanking role at the PoP Tier 2/3 level without making them even more powerful at the PoP Tier 4/5 level or simplifying the game at the raiding level.

Im not suprised the Dev's dont have the answer and haven't put in a 'fix' yet, because I've read almost every post on the topic on this board and several on other class boards over the pass 6-9 months, levelled my warrior and a chanter from 60-65 in PoS/V which has given me a good view of the 'problem' from both sides of the fence, and I wouldn't know where to start.
