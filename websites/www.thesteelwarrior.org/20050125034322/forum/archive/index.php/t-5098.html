For your back slot, I'd look at doing the Loyalist Shield of Honor quest. Spend a smidgeon of your 100K and you can get some Illusion: Human potions if you're Ogre or other dark race. It'll save you MASSIVE factioning. (I went from Dubious to Warmly I think)

If you wanna spend a little more, you might be able to buy Valdanov's ashes in the Bazaar... Do some research on this first, as there are two sets of ashes (Valdanov's and Heradius's, you want Valdanov's) with the exact same name. Anyways, it makes a great back-slot item, especially aug'd with 10AC from MMC.

From there, 2nd Bloodmetal earring and the bow are still doable within you budget likely.
