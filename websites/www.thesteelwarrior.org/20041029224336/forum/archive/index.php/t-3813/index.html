Mofu-Troll
Have your MT tank the mini that is up upon zone in to his lair, rest of the raid force (except your portal squad) kills the adds. Once the portals and adds are dead, everyone assists the MT on the mini (agnarr is being snare kited all this while btw.) When the mini is dead agnarr should be tanked near the stairway up to karana, have your casters in the stairwell safe from aoe. Each time a mini spawns (99/74/49/24 i think) the MT stays on agnarr along with Rampage tank. The rest kill the mini , once its dead all go back to helping the raid MT on agnarr. When you get to the last mini, we like to kite the last mini while we finish off agnarr.

Hope that helps ;)

Grobble
It's pretty trivial once you've done it a few times and have a few wizards, but can be very ugly until you have the encounter down.

Basically what we do it port up debuffers and a ranger or 2 to snare agnarr. Nobody can damage Agnarr. This is very important. So make sure damage shields are down and you should be able to get the portals disintigrated, Agnarr snared/kited and the first mini occupied fairly easily. Also, as long as nobody damages Agnarr, it seems as though no adds whatsoever will come from the disintigratable portals. At least we haven't gotten any the last few tries as long as we don't damage him.

Once Agnarr is debuffed and snared, we have someone kite him upstairs until portals and first mini are down, at which point everyone moves up. MT corners Agnarr to the right of the entrance to karana room, clerics hide outside the room against the wall on the stairs. When the 2nd mini is about to pop, everyone but cheal chain and MT and a few others head down and kill, rinse, repeat.

We've killed Aggy with as few as 35 people and 3 or 4 clerics using this method. He's ridiculously trivial now as long we have at least 3 wiz.
