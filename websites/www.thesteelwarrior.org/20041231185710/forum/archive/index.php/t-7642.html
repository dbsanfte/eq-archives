Your magelo says you have 242 stamina and a whole lot of empty augment slots if it is current. You are pretty close to the point of diminishing returns on stamina. After 255 stamina it isn't as many hit points per stamina point so I would also say go for raw hit points as the main criteria for upgrading gear. On raids, with pp1, you should max out to 285 stamina (305 stamina with pp5 AA) when you get buffs. Most gear you loot that has a lot of hit points will have some stamina on it anyway. As far as getting pp5, I would suggest you get the rest of your defensive AA skills before you max pp since those will give far more benefit than the extra 20 stamina from that will for now.

If you are doing LDoN a lot, as a short term fix, try to get a few of the 6 point stamina augs if no one wants them if you want to increase both while you wait for better gear. That is mainly just for when you have no shaman but if you can eventually replace a stamina aug with a pure hp one that is the better choice in the long run.
