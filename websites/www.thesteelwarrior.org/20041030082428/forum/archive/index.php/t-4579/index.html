Flair
I think you have multiple questions in your post:

Q1: Was your BoC decision good?
Heck yes....2x proc main hand of anger 3 very smart. Odds are you wont be replacing this weapon set for awhile.

Q2: What to do with your offhand?

I'd sell the split blade of destruction (trade value still quite high). Unless you want to create a dual wield dps combo. My reasoning: there are other 1 handed agro weapons out there and if you do mod this with Anger you will probably replace it with something else down the road. Then you would have a dilemna: keep an antiquated weapon (losing out on considerable pp) or strip the weapon to sell losing your points.

Q3: What else offhand?

I guess it depends on your goal. If its to max agro I'd suggest the Great blade of storms (from BoT tower) or Cord Hilted Spiked Driver (PoI dragon) and mod with an anger 2 or the yak mod from D guk (both being 510 pts and mitigates risk of "wasting" ldon points).

If its to add dps...perhaps you should save for club of mad researcher or the d guk blade (1150 & 1492 ldon pts respectively) and mod them with the +2 dam mod from the N Ro camp. You could also keep the Split Blade and add a + dam proc onto it.

I agree with the posters above though: for DPS mode you probably better off with a 2 hander with a dam proc mod.
