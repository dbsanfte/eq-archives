There's no reason to believe the mods don't work. It's certainly not something you can notice and maybe not even easily noticeable by parsing. Over the long run, the effect of such mods probably amounts to having low tens if not single digit effective HP versus melee attacks. It's nothing to get excited about but if instead of say dodge 5% it has something like "+10 HP but only good versus melee attacks", would you say it's useless? Of course not. It's an extra 10 HP that you didn't have before, even if it's a situational use.

Or, to put it this way, if +8% dodge or whatever doesn't matter, would you be fine if next patch warriors dodge suddenly goes down by 8%? After all if getting an extra 8% doesn't matter to you, losing an 8% shouldn't either.

The effect of mod items is pretty minimal. That said, nothing else would've been there anyway if the mod wasn't there, so there's nothing to complain about.
