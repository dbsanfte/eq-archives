Join as many raids in the Arena in Kael as possible. Just show up and ask to tag, most groups dont have a problem with that. big hits are Vindi and AoW raids. That or join whatever group thats in there. Bank and Plate Cycle folks are good hits also.

If you're rich, run to the bazaar and buy Giant Warrior Helms and Frost Giant Toes. Hand them into Lt Melvin in SS near the WL side for faction. You'll need a ton of them now though.

Anyways this time I walked out to check faction and I was at dubious. I think that's pretty good, even if I was one hit from threatening and one hit above it when I finished (which is pretty unlikely).

The only downside to Tormax area is you definetly need a good FD puller. Arena is very easy to pull, OTOH. Ring wars and boss hits are most likely extremely good faction, but you have to wait for those. Helms and toes still seem to be nice faction as well, but not quite as much as they used to be.
