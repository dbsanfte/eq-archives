Khiloor
Get rid of that substandard resist gear. You're giving up way too much AC/HP with those Velium Blue Diamond Bracers and neck.

Good alternatives are the Bracelet of Fortitude and/or Fierce Heraldic Bracer(s).

I see this all the time and what most ppl fail to realize, EVERY cultural armor bracer has +5 sv all as well as AC and HP, sometimes STA and WIS thrown in. Even the non-cultural stuff (Fine Heraldic) have AC/HP and +5 resist all. There is no reason why at level 60+ you should have Velium Blue Diamond resist gear of any sort.

Cheap good resist neck is Dragon Tooth Choker, or PoK Talisman if you're into questing for one.

Large races have a Stone of Morid as range slot for extra resists if needed. Keep the IGS until you get Strength of Elements or a NToV / Ssra drop.

Keep the froggy crown just in case you need the extra resists(which you probably won't).

Your guild can do Statue?, scoop up a Vindi BP for good resists as well.

And oh yah. Fierce Heraldic boots and legs, pronto.
