Brutul
The bloodfrenzy will always proc more than your BoW. It is one of the very few weapons in the game with an enhanced proc rate. Yes both have exactly the same effect. How much did you parse the BoW vs. BF/AHBS to determine your dps? The BoW should be considerably better than that combo for dps, even with ambidex and no punishing blade.

Procrate is fixed. As your weapon delay goes down, so does your chance to proc on each swing so for the most part procrate is independent of delay. The bloodfrenzy should proc about 1.5X as much as a BoW but it is due to the weapons innate procrate modifier and not its delay.
