I would start with the less expensive items:

under 10k upgrades:
helm - Fine Heraldic
rings - Valorium ring of galantry x2
shoulders - 65hp dragonwing mantle
hands - Fine Heraldic

10k-50k:
ears - Mosquito leg earring x2
shoulders - forged shoulderpads
back - heiro cloak
2hb - Greatstaff of Thunder
belt (haste) - RBG/RBB
belt - Heavy rubicite girdle
1hs (DPS) - Shortblade of Lightning
1hp (DPS) - Horn of Hsagra
2hs (DPS) - Windblade

50k-100k
back - Cloak of Flames
1hp (DPS) - Gladiator's Jereed
1hs (DPS) - Fleshgrinder

100K+:
bracer - Crystallize acid bracer
helm - Helm of Rallos Zek
range - Stonewood compound bow
ring - Steel Ring of Earthen Resilience
1hs (agro) - Blade of Carnage
1hs (DPS) - Twin blade of destruction
helm (haste) - Cowl of Mortality

Keep in mind that when you upgrade your haste, you will end up having to replace 2 items. Ideally, aim for getting a CoF+Heavy rubicite girdle, its the best combo you are going to get.

I would also save for a Greatstaff of Thunder. You just can't beat it for agro and DPS, it is seriously that good.
