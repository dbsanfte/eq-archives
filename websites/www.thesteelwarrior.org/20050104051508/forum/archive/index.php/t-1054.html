The Truncheon of Doom still drops.

I acquired one this last weekend.

I found that my 2-box is sufficient to camp anywhere in the upper part of OS - a 60 warrior and a 52 cleric. Pacify = single pulls, or doubles, but with the wondrous snare necklace of hate priests, I'm golden.

Quite literally, I camped out my toons in the armsman room, logging in the warrior first (to ensure proper aggro), with the cleric following closely behind. Chain stun to avoid fleeing (and helps with any enchanter ph's), and presto, you have a camp.

You can't see the Truncheon of Doom when he's got it, and his hits don't change; that is, the Armsman doesn't change to "bash" or "pound" or anything else, it's still hits.
And it was right there in his inventory.

I'm probably going to continue to camp them, and sell them / give them to friends, to help out other slow-deprived peeps on my server.

Great XP - I de-leveled from 61 to 60, and haven't regretted it for one sec.
