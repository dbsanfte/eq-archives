Depends on how often the mob hits in my opinion, if a mob hits for 3k (take blazzax or reparm in fire) Evasive is better. If the mobs max hit is about 1500, use defensive - Furious on pulls has a chance of messing up a ch chain, and if your healers mana pools aren't up to par they waste mana at the start of the fight, which can blow at the end (if defensive fades before the kill is over).



I say mobs that hit for 2k+ and have high damage procs (krizik the mighty,blaz, reparm, etc..) use evasive on, if a mob is slowable/partially but hits 1500 max or so, use defensive (and switch to sheild around 20% if you need an additional boost in hp/ac)

Ramp tanking; First person who gets aggro is main tank, second person who gets aggro ends up as rampage tank, ramp tanks only have to hit the mob one time to be put on list (buffing the main tank after he shoots an arrow/bolt/incite/bellow can also put you on ramp list.)

Furious > Defensive imo at all times, cant proc on a riposte, can proc on a miss - aka the mob wont proc anything on ya if your using furious (i'm not totally sure because I dont/haven't used it enough, but I believe fortitude just makes you immune from melee damage and not spells.)

Using defensive or evasive has nothing to do with the mob's max damage and everything to do with its DI relative to its DB and whether it has any really high DD procs that could tip the scale to evasive.

Dueling someone would not be a good way to compare the two as mob damage (DI/DB) can be very different than player damage.
