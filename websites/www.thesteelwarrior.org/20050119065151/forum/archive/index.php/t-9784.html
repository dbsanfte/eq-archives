For the most part everything looks pretty good. The only "weak" spot that I see is your shoulders. 20AC is nice, but some HPs would be nice, too. Get either some cultural shoulders (forget what Mith Marr cultural is called) or something similar. Horn-Spiked Shouldplates are good, too if you have the money. Cultural bracers would be a lateral upgrade in terms of HPs, but you'd trade in some stats for a good jump in AC. Look into some ornate gauntlets, too. Big increase in AC/HPs and similar stats. Plus the haste clicky is awesome if you don't have a haster in the group. Weapon wise you are perfectly set. If I were you I'd just focus on levels right now and eventually AAs' Everything else will come in time, as it looks like it's doing now.

Now that I think about it, get some Halas 10lb. Meat Pies and some Grobb Liquidised Meat. For about 100pp you'll gain 30HPs, 11str/sta and 5dex/agi.

EDIT: The cloak that I have in my magelo will be a good upgrade, too. If it's anything like Torv, it won't run too much more than a Pernicity cloak, has more raw AC plus a clicky that's 26AC or so and stacks with anything you'll ever see if you aren't in a raid guild. On top of that you'll gain raw HPs, dex, agi and some nice saves.
