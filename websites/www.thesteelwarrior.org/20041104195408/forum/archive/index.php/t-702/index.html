Mathas
All the Praesertums con yellow at 65. They all hit in the 500s, and proc a banish every 5-10 seconds, like Gerald said. Matpa(heart) is an enchanter, and Bikun(shoulder) is unslowable. That's really about the only differences between them that I could tell.

As far as how many hp they have... I think my HT dropped them about 4-5%, so very roughly 140-150khp, not counting regen. Spawn time is one week on the dot, as far as I could tell.

Easiest way is with a slower, 2-3 pet classes, and a cleric or two. They won't banish pets, and a mage epic pet or a post-60 mage/necro/bl pet can easily tank them slowed. Two clerics kept a 62 mage air pet alive against the unslowable one, and were NOT strained on mana doing so. The others are even easier.
