I've been soloing during downtimes, and I'm not really set up for it so if you are in any way (druid bot, lifetap aug, bind wounds, etc.) you'll have better success than I.

Just sniping pulls from the wanderers in the large "field" I can kill ~25-30 per hour, which is better faction than just about any XP camp you can find. That's with only self buffs. Throw in a regen or a haste and it goes up to ~40-50 pretty quick. I'm tempted to buy a lifetap aug just so I could kill those guys without having to take a regen break every so often.

If you're going to solo/duo them, I recommend staying away from the castles at the NC and BF zonelines. The mobs in the field have ~6K hp, the ones by the castles can get as high as 10K.

Yes, I realize that if you do the math that's still something like 40 hours of solid solo factioning, but it is something you can do while LFG, waiting for a raid call, etc. and still make decent progress.
