toMAYto toMAHto

Your melee dps and melee dps aggro will be greater (though not by much at all) if you mainhand the 12/19 and offhand the 13/20 thanks to the damage bonus the primary weapon receives.

*12/19 puts out more dps and dps aggro mainhand than 13/20 does mainhand
*13/20 is a better raw ratio therefore making it the better offhanded weapon

On the flip side, however, the proc on the 13/20 is 179dd + stun while the proc on the 12/19 is only 75dd + stun (higher level). So the tradeoff about equals out dps-wise.

For raw aggro purposes I'd think the SotBS primary and CHoS secondary would be overall better, as the 100 more damage per proc does not equate to much aggro whereas with the afformentioned setup you'd have both a more efficient offhand and primary ratio setup.

In the end though the difference is teenie-weenie. Do what you want, they are roughly equivalent weapons.
