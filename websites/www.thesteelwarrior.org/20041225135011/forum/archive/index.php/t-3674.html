I've used VDD, VDD + 125 Ykesha , VDD + Anger 3, BoW, and BoW +125 Yk. They are all excellent aggro. The neked VDD was only a problem when tank gets thumped with a deaggro curse or slowed, or versus a hot BoW (ie yk augmented). (If you are relying on largely dps aggro, a slowed VDD is in a world of hurt ...)

What is best depends largely on what you need it for.... if you need absolute maximum possible aggro (either to compensate of stun happy pallies or SoS happy wizzies ) the Anger 3 VDD simply rocks. If you are looking for maximum DPS, go ahead pop a cheap 150 poison proc or whatever on it. If you factor in the average 2 proc / min proc rate, you're looking at best a 5 dps increase (assuming never a resist). Given the VDD will dish a minimum of 125ish dps ( and often much higher ), your need will dictate whether an at most 4% increase in dps is worth more than 2 chunks of 600 hate / min...

Frankly, I think the VDD deals more than adequate damage in its base form. Give me that extra 1200 hate cushion anyday .....
