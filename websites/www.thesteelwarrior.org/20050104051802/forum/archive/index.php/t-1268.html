I have a BBB, and most of the warriors in my guild are packing at least a BoC. When we're running through VT and I split a mob, if another warrior is hitting it at the same time, they will almost invariably take aggro away from me. However, in PoP, I find I hold aggro better than a BoC wielding warrior. The ac difference has enough of an impact in PoP that I pump out enough DPS to stay ahead with a few procs.

For exp type situations, BBB locks down aggro just fine. Good DPS, reasonable aggro proc... It's good enough for exp no problem.

I have a BBB and Hategiver and Bloodfrenzy combo if I need insanely fast aggro.

But... I use BBB almost exclusively for tanking, using Emp 2h for exp groups or when I'm not MT.

BBB aggro is good for the long term, I've found EB to be like Anarchy, you can snag snap aggro with it fairly well, but for long term (10+ min fights) BBB will be your best bet.

Also, and the main reason I switched to 2h, is that tanking mobs with 15/19 and 16/20 with max haste is going to completely and utterly fuck your shit up with Ripostes. Especially if the mob hits for 2k+. In that regards, BBB >> duel weild.
