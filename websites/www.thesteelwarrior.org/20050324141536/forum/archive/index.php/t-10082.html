Depends on level entirely! At 70 I am lucky to get 1% AA per kill in the south field. This results in an AA every 2.5 hours or so. And I get about 2% AA per kill in the north field, which is noticeably harder then south field due to the Elites (which means CC proc) etc. Which results in about an AA every 1.5 hours.

In BoT I get 1% AA a kill. No matter where I go. Still about 2.5 Hours per AA.

At lower levels around 65 or so you might see as much as 5% AA per kill in north field. In which case with a good group you can see an AA every 45min. Or in the South field at 65 you can get 3% AA a kill and get an AA every hour or so.

XP scales down quickly as you level, to a point where the south field is not worth your time at level 68 and your forced to go to the north field for good xp in WoS.

I am guessing its not an option, but you can still get 4% AA per kill at tables/c2 in PoFire at 70. If you can't go there, MPG is the next best thing at 4% AA per kill also, just quite a bit harder then PoFire imo.
