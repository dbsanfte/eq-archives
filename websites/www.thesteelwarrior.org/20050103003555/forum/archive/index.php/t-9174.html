I solo with my tanks exclusively. Some are better at it than others. Fire/Fire is good for that purpose. Pull with taunt, make sure they don't fire at you until your pull is away from his buddies, otherwise they'll chain agro. Tele foe sucks for pulling singles as it is considered an attack and makes his buddies more aware when pulled making harder to split. In any case, you should ba able to handle at least 5 or 6 mobs while soloing. It takes forever to kill (defeat) thugs with most of the tanker primaries. if you aren't able to handlea more than one thug at once, then you either have a very bad build or are too low a level to be making judgements. Tankers are just really low damage scrappers until 8 or so, and that shouldn't take more than an hour or 2.

As always, know your opponent and plan accordingly. Hellions are weak to cold and strong to fire. Skulls are weak to energy/strong to neg energy. Vaz are weak to lethal... The lists goes on, so when leveling, you need to try an focus your time hunting foes that are weak againsts your attack first then the ones that use attack that you have a strong defense to.

Use your active (toggle) armors, auto powers are weak in comparison, they serve as back up when yo face opponents that can drop your toggles. make a beeline for 14 to get you travel powers then its smooth sailing for then on out.

That's about all the advice I can give. The game is cake at present, there's really not much variety yet, but thats the case with most new MMOs. i odn't expect to stick with CoH after WoW releases, but its a very fun hold over until then. I would still be playing EQ, but for some reason I couldn't bring myself to log back in for a few weeks, so I just decided to drop my account. I can't really say what went wrong, I just didn't have the heart to log back in anymore. I suspect there are a few others here that can say the same...
