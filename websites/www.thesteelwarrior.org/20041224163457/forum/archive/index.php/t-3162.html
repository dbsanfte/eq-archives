Mana to HP 1/2/3 (PoP Advance): (Warrior, monk and rogue)

This is a passive ability; it does not need to be activated.
Requirements: Level: 65, Planar Power at Level 5.
This ability raises your maximum hitpoints by 2, 5 and 10 percent of the total amount of mana gained from equipment and spell effects. (The percentages are based on mana from equipment and spells and do not include additional bonuses from intelligence or wisdom.)
Only pure melees get this AA skill.

Greater Taunt (Class): (Warrior only)

This is a passive ability; it does not need to be activated.
Requirements: Level 61, Must spend 12 ability points in the Archetype Abilities.
This ability gives the warrior a chance to gain aggro from an NPC regardless of any taunt bonuses that the current aggro holder may have.

Unlinked Discipline (Class): (Warrior, monk and rogue)

Type: Activated, Refresh Time: 0:30:00
Using this ability makes the next discipline used not effect the current discipline timer. There is a 3 minute duration after the discipline is used before another discipline may be activated.
