At your level, your HPs are actually looking pretty good. Once you get your AAs and level 65, you'll be seeing mid-8k or 9k hp. I'd suggest working on levels and whatnot in LDoN, and aug some of the stuff you wont be upgrading in a while (legs, helm, possibly BP).


As for the SoDs, its really up to you. SoD's are fun, but the aggro is just "eh". Get anger3 on them, you'll be holding aggro pretty good, but you could get roughly the same aggro from the droga weapons (although the proc damage wont be as good).

To compare the two:
You'll have more dps with the dual SoDs
You'll have roughly the same aggro with dual SoDs
You'll have better stats with droga weapons
You'll have a lot more cash to spend with droga weapons.

As for AAs, I'd go to 65 first, then get defensive/hp, THEN worry about your aggo/dps. If you find yourself losing aggro a lot, it may be time to either get Anger3 or some WA/DPS AAs a little early.

But...since you're posting here for advice, I have to give my 2 copper :p

Although warriors do some damage, that's not out job. Our job is to hold agro and survive the beating while everyone else kills the mob. Anything you can do to increase your agro will help the group. Yes, dual SoD's will give you more dps and is fine in PoV, but when you get into higher content you want the wizards/mages/etc. to be able to nuke as fast and hard as possible - which means you need the best agro you can get. Dropping one SoD and getting a BoC will give you more agro, and I suspect the same is true for the CHoS but I'm not certain on that one.

But, like I said...it's a game so do whateve makes you happy ;)
