I saw a parse of 8 % dodge producing around 1 or 2 percent more dodges.

The 7 AC (more like 8 or 9 when AGI included unless you are max AGI) is much more important than the 15 HP. Every tradeoff you make for hp adds up. If you have several items and can trade them in and out as you upgrade, your goal is to have the max AC you can get in blocks of 50 AC. So if you are sitting at 1699 with max AC gear, trade out several items to remain 1650 or higher. But I personally don't want to always be checking my AC every time I upgrade, so I go for the best AC and HP I can get on my gear. Standard rule most people follow: 1 raw AC = 5 raw HP.
