Frostpaw
I would aug in this order:

1. BP
2. Legs
3. Helm

Only reason i would aug helm last out of these three is that its droppable. You can wait to invest any points in that one until you see if an upgrade is coming your way in a timely fashion. If an upgrade seems imminent you might be able to hold off and aug the upgrade, then sell the HoRZ without losing the 1150 points.

Also, just my opinion, but i would not spend the 1150 pts. on 40hp for the arms or boots. Seems very likely you can get better before spending the points on those 2 slots.

A lot of this depends on how much LDoN you do also. If you do a ton of LDoN, then maybe it doesn't matter so much what order you aug in.

p.s. Contrary to my advice to you, I happened to 40hp aug my HoRZ first because it was the only piece of armor i had that i felt was worth augging with 40hps.
