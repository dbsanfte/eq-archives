I do not know any sites but I know you need three to start an instance, you can just ask someone in the zone to group with yah for a second to get the instance. You can stay in the instance with just 2. Make sure the third is about your level though because the level of the mobs are set from the lowest members level.
The Dranik's hollow instance giver is in front of the 3 caves, static spawn dragorn with a noc static spawn beside him. Ask him about the caves to start an instance. The sewers and catacombs instance giver is in Ruined city he is to the left by a tree once you leave the zoneline tunnel. He is also a dragorn. you can invis to him. I have never had a wanderer aggro me while talking to him but I cannot say for sure if it is safe to stand by him.
