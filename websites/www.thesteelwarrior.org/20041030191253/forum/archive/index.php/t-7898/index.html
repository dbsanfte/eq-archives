Not sure of the status on your bank account but there's a few things you can buy in the bazaar that will be good upgrades for you. If you came into some money, I'd look at getting

1) Elbow Spiked Armplates (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16485). You'd lose STA but you'd gain 40 raw hp.
2. Replace at least one earring with a Bloodmetal Earring of Engagement. Should run ya 15-18k ideally (?) maybe less. Alternatively, you could camp the Lizard earring in Cazic Thule zone (not sure where it drops).
3. Amygdalan Chain Mask has 85 raw hp on it.
4. Valorium Ring of Gallantry would also be a small boost.

You might find that levelling to 60 is worthwhile.

About weapons: I used Frostbringer and the snare whip when POP was still kinda new, then used a GSoT (was rare back then, had a lot of inspects), then I used CHoS / SoTB for quite a while. They held better aggro than a GSoT. I fooled around with a Stormrider Carapace (FB3) but I can vouch that not only did I lose DPS with a shield, I also found a noticeable loss in aggro. I'd reallly vouch for the CHoS / SoTB combo... or at least your CHoS / sandstone sword... either of those would work tons better than your shield. Good luck.
