One 58 in a group of 65s will not drop you to a 58 misssion. The easy way to think about it is how much are you +/- over 65.

A 65 gives you +2 levels. 5 65s give you +10 levels. A 58 is -5 levels. With one 58 and all 65s you are still +5 levels. 2 58s will drop you (-5x 2= -10, 4 65s = +2x 4= +8. Add the two and you are -2 levels froma 63). Either you had 2 58s in the group, or 3 people who were not 65.

I wish there was some kind of midlvl between Normal and Hard. With 8k+ hp buffed, Im not too good for Hard but Normals are too easy...

That isn't possible without reworking the entire LDoN architecture; adventures are based on average level which is indexed by 5s, to add a middle ground they would have to add a second type of 65-base adventure which doesn't exist right now (and may not be codeable without revamping LDoN entirely).

If I were a dev, when Omens pushes the level cap to 70 I'd change the level 70-base adventure (which is 65-hard, or a normal at 70) to the middle ground many people have been asking for (~600 hitters, 80ish points) and move the current 70-base template to 75-base, meaning what is now 65-hard will be the hard adventure at 70 (998 hitters, 105 points).

That's one upside to raising the level cap, for all those that say there's nothing good about raising levels again; it finally gives LDoN a medium level without re-coding its adventure system.
