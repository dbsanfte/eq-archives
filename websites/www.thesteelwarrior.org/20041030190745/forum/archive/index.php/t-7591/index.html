Daabu
Get a better offhand imo for holding aggro. A greatblade of storms from BOT towers would be great if you're able to get one.

Feet: you'll get +5hp +6ac with boots of storm, or +15hp +5ac with the HardBB boots

Legs: Look into getting ornate...camp if you have to, or try to get your guild to raid Tagrin in tactics (he's kinda rough, harder than bot towers imo). Alternatively, you can get the tak LDoN legs, which are arguably a little better, but you'd be spending points on them instead of on +40hp or +10ac augs.

Back, there's a haste back from hard EF ldon that has already been mentioned. Definately worth getting

Ring: EF hard has a decent ring (100hp if i recall?)

Wrist: This is the slot I'd definately suggest going to get a tak bracer. Reason: you can +40hp aug the ldon bracers, but you can't for the ornate. However, considering you already have ornate, you may or may not want to do it (you'd lose 6ac and the attack, but have the ability to get it up to 110hp)

Hands: get the Ruj hard gloves, definately.

Neck: I'd suggest the hard neck from Ruj, 14ac 90hp 6svall. Once you can move augments around, that's definately worth doing
