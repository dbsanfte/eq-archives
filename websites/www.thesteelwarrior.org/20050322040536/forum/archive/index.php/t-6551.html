Fire Trucking = This is one of the more difficult methods of tanking. The reason for doing this method is if you are killing a caster mob. By keeping the mob moving, it will have a difficult time trying to cast spells upon you and your raid. In this method, the mob will NOT be touching a wall or corner. This technique only gets easier with practice of your raid force. When the mob is pulled into your camp, tag the mob as usual. Build up agro until you feel comfortable. Tell your raid to assist you on the mob. The mob will be pushed wildly in random directions the first few times you attempt this. Don't worry, its normal. Your responsibility is to hold agro and direct the mob's direction during this. Your entire raid will want to attack from the mobs backside and you will be walking backwards throughout the entire fight. Some tanks are random in the directions that they go. Some tanks prefer to just walk around backwards in a large circle. Some may even try to do a wall-walk without a wall! This method takes a little bit of time to get comfortable with, but in due time, it is very effective.
