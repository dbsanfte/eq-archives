Sylas
another thing that no one really considers, is when the mob is down to less than 10%, say has 1k hps left, and the wizard lands that 6k+ crit, his DPS counts that 6k damage, but the melee who lands the quad + kick/backstab/FK for 1200 dmg still would have slain the mob. The last hit for a melee is the only hit that skews his DPS, yet the wizard's last nuke get skewed completely.

so if a warrior has done say 200k dmg over 45 min LDON, and has landed 30 killing blows, his DPS is skewed by say 30X avg hit (say 100 damage), for 3k.

but if a wizard has done 300k dmg over 45 min LDON, and has landed 30 killing blows, his DPS is skewed by 30 X avg nuke, say 3k, or 90k.

assuming that half of the dmg dealt was necessary to finish off the mob, the warrior gets 1.5k "fake" damage added to his DPS total, while the wizard gains 45k "fake" damage added.

another way to say it is, if you killed 90 mobs and each mob has 10k hps, then your total group damage total should be 900k, but with nukers the total group damage is closer to 1.1-1.5M. against a raid mob that last nuke going over the total HPs left of the mob is no big deal, so the overnuke doesn't mean anything. But in exp groups where you are killing a mob a minute for 5 hours straight, then it adds up big.

Well, assuming no bard in the group:

Standing regen = 3 (at 65), FT = 15, MC = 3, KEI = 14, Pot9 = 8, that totals 43 per tick, familiars add some, not sure about WIZ self-buffs, also they can get some from using Harvest, so let's say 50-55 mana per tick if there's no bard in the group and they never med.
