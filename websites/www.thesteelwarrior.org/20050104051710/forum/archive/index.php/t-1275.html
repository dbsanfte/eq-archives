Hmm... I suppose it depends mainly on the situation, and what kind of DPS your guild needs to be kicking out.

In general (ie, all exp groups and most raids), I think I would use GBoS in main hand. It gives you a higher steady stream of DPS, the aggro of its proc is nice to begin with, and you only need 1 or 2 Enraging Blows per fight to make a mob stick to you like glue anyway. Before any procs go off, it's all about the dps.

BoC in mainhand should kick out more raw hate over time, if you need a lot of aggro so your guild can sustain max dps against a mob with huge HP (upper end luclin targets come to mind. Against Emp, I'd be using BoC main)
