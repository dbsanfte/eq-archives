I run WoW on a low-end compy. The graphics don't look near as good on my wife's box, but I don't experience alot of in-game lag once the game gets loaded and I'm out fighting.

1.0ghz Athlon
512mb SDRAM
Geforce2 MX400 64mb
20gb ATA66 drive

Loading times are longish when you log into high population cities like Ironforge, but it's not unbearable. Up to 90sec max.

If you are thinking of upgrading a few things to make it run better on your comp, I'd suggest; 512mb DDR (at least), video card that FULLY supports Dx9c, ATA133 or SATA drive with at least 40gb of space.

Also, when installing WoW, do everything you can to install it on its own drive partition of at least 8gb in size. Most likely you'll need to defrag this partition right after installing the game and before patching. The game installs itself almost completely fragmented (/growl). WinXP SP2 is almost a necessity for it to run smoothly. Oh, last but not least give your CDROM some loving before and after installation. It will most likely be constantly spinning for 1 to 3hours while you install.
