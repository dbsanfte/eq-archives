Personally, I'm digging it! I didnt get to try my 52 warrior but my 25 war on the PvP server did awesome.

Was soloing OJ con's with ease last night in 1k needles. Looks like we get full rage on a block/dodge/parry and it "seemed" like we're getting more rage per hit.

Thunderclap is much much nicer now imo. Last rank will be a 30 second 10% slow with better damage.

The HS boost is nice. Not overpowering though imo. But its an option to consider for dps, especially for the non-MS builds =) For the Fury builds, Imp. HS might just be worth it now. I couldnt really tell if the off-hand not swinging while HS was being used bug was any different though.

The biggest change I saw was the client bug that was reporting mob defensive moves as misses. A lot more parrying and blocking, and seemingly fewer misses. It takes a little getting used to for sure.

Our opponent dodging abilities lighting up Overpower is a BIG boost imo. It "seemed" like it was lighting up as often as revenge did. I've got the Imp. Overpower talent and it was reeeeal nice to crit an overpower when the buggers dodge my sunder *big grin*

I didnt get to try out the Execute fix personally but I loved that ability before hand, it can only be better.

Looks like the Imp. Sunder talent has lost its worth. The Imp. Thunderclap doesnt look like its gonna be useful either now that getting rage isnt a challenge.

To me at least, this puts the warrior class back where it belongs. What'd you guys think?
