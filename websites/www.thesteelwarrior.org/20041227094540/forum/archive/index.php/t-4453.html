"You have to be level 51 and have your bind wound skill to 210. At that point, you can bind wound up to 70%. With AA's you can go all the way to 100%."

The above statement is TRUE for monks.

It is FALSE for warriors.

As Varaho states, it's a few levels after monks.

I will know the exact level if I ever get my 52 warrior up a few more levels.

:-)

To review, the way to get 100 percent Bind wounds capability:

1- Get your bind wounds skill up to 201. Anything after 201, allows you to bind wounds to 70 percent of your health.

2- Get all three levels of the general alternate adavancement ability, First Aid. It costs 3 points. It allows you to bind wounds for another 10 percent of your health for each point spent. So level 1 would give you 10 percent more. Level 2 another 10 percent. And level three you would have added a total of 30 percent more health you can bind up to.

70 percent + 30 percent = 100 percent.

Monks get the first aid AA ability because it's a pre-requisite for one of their class abilities, Critical Mend.

Warriors tend to ignore it, since in their 50s, they tend to never use bandages much.

Plate tanks tend to have healers in their entourages. :P
