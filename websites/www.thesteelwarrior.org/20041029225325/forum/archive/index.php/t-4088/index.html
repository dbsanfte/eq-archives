Kendolekk
Use the BoC in situations when you NEED agro (Raids).

For XP, unless agro is way out of control due to others, just use your GSot4W. Especially when dps matters like LDoN.

Ambidex helps a little, but won't help put your DPS with BoC anywhere near GSot4W.

Put Anger on the BoC. If you put it on the GSot4W you will no longer be able to use it for dps when you are NOT tanking.
