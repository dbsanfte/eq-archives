Battleblade
IMO, the best possible path for you is:

Pimary/Secondary
Blood Frenzy(Force of YKesha)/Wrath's Edge(Anger III)

Get Aten's gloves in VT
BoW(Anger III)

Get Ambidexterity, Tactical Mastery and WA5
DBoW(Anger III)/Blood Frenzy(Force of YKesha)

Other plaers use different setups. But the real point of my suggestion is that BF and Aten's gloves are very nearly PoTime quality and there is no need to replace them. Only because of heavily reposting mobs will you want to get a BoW.

Wrath's Edge is a candidate for replacement before you get a BoW. But the most likely choices - CHSD, SotBS, BoC etc. aren't going to be that huge of an upgrade.

I'd save my LDon points for weapon and 40HP augs. You're going to need them.
