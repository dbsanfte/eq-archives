I don't think you're gonna notice a big difference between GBoS/Windspeeder and the Ornate Greatblade, especially if you don't aug the windspeeder. If you really want to go dual wield I'd get a copper hammer of striking, to go with the GBoS. The ornate greatblade is a far superior weapon in terms of stats/hps/dps of course, so if you can keep aggro with it it is much better than your dual wield combo. If it was me I'd probably upgrade the OGB to anger III and try that for a while. You're not gonna be an aggro juggernaut, but I think you should do ok. GBoS and Copper hammer with A3 would be an aggro upgrade for sure, but at the loss of a lot of hps and dps.
