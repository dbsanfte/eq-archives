I have done the Cursed script with 13 people total, all of which were elemental (or less) equipped. You have to realize that, unlike most PoP and even a lot of [early] GoD encounters, Luclin encounters are glorified endurance fights, and when you're fighting these Ssra named with only two groups, they take a long, long, LLOOONNGG time to kill. The biggest difficulty for us when fighting in the Cursed script was not so much Cursed himself, but actually Exiled. As most of you know, Exiled death touches, and it requires you to recycle your members, putting extra strain on the already limited resources that you have (rezzing/buffing = resources!). We beat him, but just about every single one there tanked him. Tank down? Paladin tanks. Paladin down, rogue tanks. Rogue down, second rogue tanks. Second rogue dead, fuck... the bard is tanking! Meanwhile, the second cleric is rezzing up and buffing the fallen, all of which are jumping right back into the battle for a second round of death touches.

Cursed himself reminds me a lot of fighting Quarm for the first time, where the emphisis isn't really about keeping your tank alive, it's just about keeping your raid alive in general! In this case with so few people, you can't afford to lose a single one.

The fight is doable. I've done it. It's pretty hard despite being pretty old content now, but goddamn, it was also some of the most fun I've had in a long time. It's a refreshing challenge from mobs that assrape you before you can even count to... 1. :D
