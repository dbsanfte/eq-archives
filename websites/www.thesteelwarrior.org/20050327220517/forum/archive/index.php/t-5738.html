Yes, seems that the 2nd floor is going to be a permanent feature now on this encounter.

Tip on 2nd floor:
1. Adds are there to distract you.
2. Adds cannot be rooted, snared
3. Adds can be mezzed
4. Adds can be charmed
5. Emerrik's AE is a target AE. Emerrik can cast the spell upto a range of 200, but the range of the AE on the target is only 30. (This is the 90% Slow, 3k AE).

Put all these together, and you should know how to overcome the 2nd floor.

3rd floor is a breeze after that.
