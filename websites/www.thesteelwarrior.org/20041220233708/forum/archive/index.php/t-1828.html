There are probably a gazillion and a half similar posts where people argue the pros and cons of each race, but I still say racial slam that ogres, trolls, and barbarians get is a huge plus. Also, while in the endgame starting stats dont mean much, in the beginning, the high str/sta of the large races is tough to beat. If I had to start over again, I'd probably be a barbarian again, because they get many advantages of being a large race, but still fit better into most places than ogres and trolls, and can wear medium sized armor. Also, while faction is nowhere near the issue it was pre PoP books, barbarians can move freely through most starting cities, ogres and trolls can't. Helanic is some damn fine armor also. Nightblindness isnt an issue unless you are in a cave and don't have a lightsource of any kind. Even then, carry a glowing stone band(infravision ring) for emergency use and you're set. And if I decided to be something other than a barbarian just for variety, I'd play an iksar. Ugly or not they have some damned nice racial advantages, particularly regen and innate ac bonus.
