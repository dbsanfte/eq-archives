At 48, you will definitely be of interest to groups hunting zone out. Traditionally 2 camps, left and right of the zone out corridor.

Mobs are easy there, and xp rocks to about 51/52. If you end up as puller, you'll notice there are some odd adds, until you level up a bit. You'll be pulling rats/prisoners and the guards (coolest mob model, IMO) - the prisoners are all different classes, and you'll learn which is the biggest PITA (Zanoux, mid left cell)

There are no faction hits, everything at the zone out is indifferent and runs at low health, rats being the worst. (They cast an innate SoW)

XP Starts slowing down fast at 51/52, we moved to the PoJ Basement (aka Mavuin's Hall) which is ok xp there til 54ish. You might also be ok tanking the PoI zone in mobs, but without a chanter/bard for CC, that can get ugly.

At 54ish, grab a chanter/bard, healer and a slower and head over to PoD and kill the flies there (From Zone head across water to first shore, they spawn on the right), they dont hit hard, but the xp is very good. You WILL get a rat add from time to time that will be a harder fight, do NOT pull Malarians.

Otherwise, PoN yard trash is your next bet. (Bugs, Bushes and Birds (Spiders, Trees and Ravens))

55-58 , its a slow grind, as PoV groups wont want you tanking (nor should you really til 58 min) I've heard people say Crypt of Decay Zone in mobs are good, but havent played around with them.

XP in LDoNs will still be a good option, it won't come in as steady, but gear wise, it will be better.

Hope that helps a bit.
