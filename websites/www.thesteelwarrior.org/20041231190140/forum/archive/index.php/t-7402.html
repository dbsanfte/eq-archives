Taunt success depends on the mob's level relative to yours. The higher they are, the more you'll fail.

What weapons do you use? Are they augmented? Do you mash taunt? Do you incite often? Do you have WA5? Warrior aggro isn't perfect, but if you've done all you can to maximize your aggro, it's the high aggro classes fault.
Lathen
06-02-2004, 12:30 PM
Slight and pedantic clarification --

One of the developers stopped by in the past few weeks and confirmed that the only factors which affect the success of taunt are 1) your skill level in taunt, 2) your level, and 3) the target's level.

So, yes -- you'll fail more if the mob is higher level than you.
