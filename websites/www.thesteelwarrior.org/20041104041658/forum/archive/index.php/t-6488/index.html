Sassinak
I recommend trying Karnor's castle undead.

As your cleric levels up through the 50's, it will get tons of handy utility spells, which will help your duo considerably. You would do well to gradually shift your main focus in the duo to the cleric as you obtain more of these tools.

Wake of Tranquility lets your cleric single pull almost anything below level 60. Very low resist rate. This spell makes your cleric the puller.

Yaulp IV allows you to regen mana while standing and while casting those long CH spells. You should never have problems with sitting aggro ever again after you have this spell.

MoR makes quite a difference when you first get it. It noticeably shortens fights. MoK is less dramatic, but you may still notice its benefit if you have fast weapons.

Hammer pet and summoned proc hammer combined with Yaulp 4/5 will add at least 25 DPS to your duo. Quite useful when plowing through blues of low-medium difficulty.

Hammer pet is your most reliable way to break root. Cast it whenever you want to reel in a mob that you've root-parked.

Blessed Armor of the Risen will help solve your downtime for the cleric. This spell + Yaulp 5 = KEI mana regen while standing.

Celestial Elixir will change the way you approach healing. Much more useful than the level 44 version, it can be actually more efficient than CH if your hit points and efficiency are not top notch.

Never forget the Atone spell. It does get resisted a lot at these levels, but it can save your cleric's butt if you have aggro management issues within your duo. If a pull can be rooted, then it can be aborted using atone.
