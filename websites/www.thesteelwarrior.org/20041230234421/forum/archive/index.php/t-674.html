Personally I like to upgrade 1 slot and make it last. You know about Kael and that's the place to be at your level. Your rings will hold you for a long time if you want, as the next upgrade is expensive.
You could sell off your cloak and with the extra money get a Cloak of the Maelstrom ( 12ac, 55hp, 6 sta I think) for around 2-4k. That is a quality upgrade that can last until Hiero cloak (20-25k), or a haste back item (too expensive for me atm).

Now for the goodies, Free Upgrades:
-Goranga Spiked Club ( 12/23 1hb ) from Maidens Eye rots all the time

-Axegeroth's Belt of Defense (spelling?) with is around 20ac plus 5 str, I think. will be a nice upgrade from ME.

-Grodan's Idol is a nice upgrade from the Goranga Idol and it is free in ME as well.

-Quest Praetor's Blade of Debility for a free aggro weapon, or buy a pair of Black Blade of Tormenting (9/22 + DoT proc) for around 400-800pp each.

I had similar equipment at your level, as well as a short bank account which hasn't changed. Just level, save money and upgrade with quality items so you won't have to worry about that slot for a while.

Also, hunt in PoJ! If you can get a lvl or 2 and hunt in the Enforcer Room ( full of rockgolem looking creatures and Ptarg fiend looking creatures on the way to Mauvin the prisoner ) you will get alot of Raw Diamonds, Diamonds and Blue Diamonds. When you feel comfortable enough to click one of the doors and take out a dangerous prisoner in this hall, you can get several different loots both selable and upgrades for caster friends. Also a nice 2hs drops there, 29/36 I think, the name eludes me atm.

I will take the opposite side and recommend a lot of small upgrades.

There's a lot of stuff that's pretty cheap now if you do a little shopping around. Crusty armor, tattered mantle, coterie general cape, spider fur belt. Crusty can be had for around 50 apiece, and the other ones 100-150p each. An acrylia bp should be under 1k also.

On a budget, you take what you can get for weapon upgrades. snare whip is nice, bed leg clubs are pretty cheap, barnacle axe may also work.

This should bring your equipment to a reasonable level, fill the holes and have a base to build on.
