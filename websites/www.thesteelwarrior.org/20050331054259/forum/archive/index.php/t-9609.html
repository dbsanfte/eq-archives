depending how often you raid time, could be a long wait for dbow, even with gazillions of dkp.

if your just looking to upgrade your bow asap, theres some options.

if they going to flag you for qvic, you can hope for a fleshbiter from uqua. from qvic when u get there there is chaotic black scimitar.

another option would be the fang of feratha, 2hander from feratha in riftseekers. stun proc, 53/30, 75ac & 275 hps.. not difficult to kill, just a fair amount of hps.

one last option, if you have lots of friends from your server, do 1.5 and slap a couple of anger/ykesha augs on it.

not sure what DW weapons you have atm, but if you going to go the DW route, theres also a fairly easy to get EoE clone from wall of slaughter, velitorkin's femur. 19/19 stun proc, although the stats are not so hot.

theres some discussion on whether the new 2handers like matchless claymore are equivalent of better to a DW setup with DBoW, but personally i dont see me giving up DBoW for a while yet. it was like night and day when i finally got it.
