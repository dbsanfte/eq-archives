Have no fear, you can be a Mithaniel Marr worshiper and a warrior and make it in the game just fine. I made my warrior when Kunark came out, well before diety armour even existed. GSoT is pretty decent for agro and goes cheap these days, at least on SZ, got mine for 1200. If you're looking for gear I'd suggest questing for velious skyshrine armor. Tons of HP on that stuff. +90 hp on the gloves for example.

oh and quick comment regarding one of the gear statements made earlier in this post.

"HP is king and sta=HP"

mostly true but imho hp > sta because you can always max sta w/ other gear and buffs so it hits a cap. There's no cap for hp. If it comes down to a choice take hp over sta. usually that's not a problem tho. hp & sta seem to come hand in hand on many items.
