I'd say the general consensus is get level 65 first, then AA's.

Oh, and your Haste item is pretty low. But you could do some LDoN adventures and get a nice item you can stuff a Haste augment into, like the 36% haste augment from Mistmoore which needs a type 3 slot and fits into Head, Back, Range and Waist slots. Not to mention you can aim a bit higher on those since they do not cost pp, but rather adventure ponts.

Now, since you already have fair items in back, waist and range, that really leaves the Helmet ready for an upgrade.
So lets make an example :

Helmet (duh) -- Royal Attendant Helmet of Sand (1492 Takish points)
Haste (36%) -- Gravestone Fragment of Flailing Dead (1150 Mistmoore points)
+40HP -------- Aug2 Glowing Saphire of Courage (1150 Takish points)
+10 STA ----- Aug All Peerless Sphere of Fortitude (357 Rujarkian points)

And you'll end up with a helmet like this : Augmented Helmet (http://www.magelo.com/eq_item_info.html?num=44301&slot1=42214&slot2=42167&slot3=42165)

Offcourse, it's the helmet and the haste augment that will make the bulk of it, so that what one would go for first ... ie. first Helmet, then Haste.

Alternatively, you could put in a Glowing Prism of Brawling (+5DEX +5STA +20HP) from Guk instead of the Glowing Saphire of Courage (+40HP) one. They cost the same amount of points though and you'll hit the STA cap eventually.

And even if you got a better haste item, it would still be a very nice helmet. :)
Not to mention you get some exp while doing adventures.
