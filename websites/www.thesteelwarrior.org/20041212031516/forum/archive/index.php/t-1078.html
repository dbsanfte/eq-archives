Typically defense is the first skill that maxes for me because I get beat on almost constantly. :) If you are heavily twinked, the mobs may be missing you so often based on your gear that you arent getting adequate defensive skill checks to catch you up. Also, if you're really low level, like under 10 or so, leveling is so easy now it's almost broken, (old school eq'er here, took forever to get to 20 back in the day) so it may take a while to get your skills to catch up to your level. If you still have a problem after about level 10, strip off the high end gear, put on some banded, and go to the lowbie zone of your choice and get pounded on by a bunch of dark blue mobs.
On a side note, for quick weapons, gnoll hide lariats are the most fun weapons in the game for the level they can be attained and the level they proc. 3/20, proc stun, dual wielding them you wont do crap for damage, but with these and haste you are a casting mob's worst nightmare and you WILL have aggro. Used them in Dalnir against the healers there at lvl 30ish, between them and slam the healers were screwed. :)

Stats that influence skill gains for various abilities:

Str = Offense gains (damage shield hits also raise this)
Dex = Weapon skill gains
Agi = Defense gains

Some say int has an influence over this as well, but i'm of mind it only applies to non combat skill sets.
