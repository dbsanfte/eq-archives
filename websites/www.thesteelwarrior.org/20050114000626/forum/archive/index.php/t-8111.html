I was thinking about possible new AAs and came up with one that I think would be pretty cool. It's basically a very toned down version of warrior MOB enrage ability. Call it Adrenaline Rush or something:

Adrenaline Rush:
3 levels
Increases the chance of defensive skill checks like dodge, parry, riposte to fire off when we go berserk. Level 1 10% more than usual, level 2, 25% more than usual, level 3 50% more than usual. Tweak percentages as needed for balance.

Note, this is a 10/25/50% bonus to our CURRENT levels. This is not a straight 10/25/50% chance for all of those skills to fire so in reality it would just be a moderate increase in avoidance while we're berserk.
