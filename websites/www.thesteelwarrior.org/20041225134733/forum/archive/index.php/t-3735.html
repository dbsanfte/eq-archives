Originally posted by gulug
not sure how much of a tougher fight it will be with 3 groups. anyone else care to comment? This guy can easily be done by 3 groups of competent people even with Velious/Luclin type gear. I have a group of friends that farms smaller raid targets and we've done him with 12 before, most of my friends are Ssra/VT level gear including the tank (6300hp unbuffed).

Also, saying that the haste neck and hate hammer drop often is pretty misleading. The neck is more common than the hammer, and that drops 1/3 or 1/4 kills in my experience. Of course with such a fast repop, it's only a matter of time before you get enough for everyone and their twinks.

As for outfitting the warrior, try to get in on some BoT tower kills, get a few nice items. I would also recommend setting up residence in either Veksar or BoT. If you can get a group to hunt Veksar regularly, you can pick up some nice gear pretty easily. If you can tank BoT effectively, then park there until you are level 65+50aa, by that time you should have gotten luck on some ornate armor drops. You might also consider doing LdoN for exp and APs. The cash drops are not bad so you can spend your time saving to buy upgrades with your split of the plats, and you can buy some LDoN gear with your APs. In any case, don't do any AAs other than run3 until you get to 65.
