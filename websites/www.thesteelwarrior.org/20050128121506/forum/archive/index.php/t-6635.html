I think you have another option other than you listed. Do you have to join a Time enabled guild right now? Why not find a guild that is just entering EP flagging and make friends there and help them progress? I think you are doing yourself a disservice by skipping all the PoP content and rushing right to Time. Unless you're only goal in this game is to have the best gear, I think you might find more enjoyment progressing with characters at your level.

When I joined my current guild about a year ago, they were farming Tawro/Radiir in Umbral. We were barely able to field 14 players at our raids. We progressed on to Doomshade, Zelnithak, Rumblecrush, Vindicator. We were fielding 20 players at raids. We decided to add the Akehva Ruins nameds to our hit list. Va'Dyn and IV soon fell our growing guild. The first fight of IV with 24 players avg. lvl. 61 was a complete rush. We moved on to ToV and ST key dragons with our standard 24 man force. After a few months we were in Sleeper's Tomb! The nameds fell to our small force of 15 keyed members. Avatar of War was always a dream of ours. About the time we entered ST, we found Statue up and decided to give it a shot. Much to our surprise, we downed that punk with a couple extra groups of friends. Soon after, the recruits started rolling in and more baddies fell to our might; Aaryonar, Tormax, Dain, Shei Vinitaris, Grieg, ST Ancients, THO, Burrower Beast, BoT Towers, Terris Thule. I'm very impressed with my guild accomplishing all of this with strategy instead of numbers. Highlights: ST Ancients w/ 16 players, Aaryonar w/ 19, Terris Thule w/ 29, Burrower Beast w/ 21.

During this past year, some of my friends guild-hopped and skipped content to reach PoTime as fast as possible. Now they are stuck doing 6-8hour raids in EPs. They raid with 100 other players they hardly know. They fight for PoTime content with 6 other guilds on our server. I send them tells about what we are doing with what little raid force we have. They usually reply with "Wow, I haven't killed him/ only killed him once =( ". They may have way better gear, but I have a since of accomplishment and friendship. Just something to think about.
