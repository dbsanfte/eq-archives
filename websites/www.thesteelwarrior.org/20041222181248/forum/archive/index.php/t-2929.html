There is truth mixed with sarcasm and satire below:

Changes:

1. Remove complete heal for clerics. Give them fast low mana spells that heal for more to make them uber healers with greater mana flexibility for healing than they get with CH rotations. This also makes warriors more viable tanks because clerics, like all classes are lazy and would prefer healing a warrior than a knight if this tool was taken away.

2. Remove slow from all but bards. This would also give warriors a better role because warriors would be best to heal with slow gone. Don't retune encounters for removal of slow... just make the game hard and interesting again.

3. Remove buffs of haste on players. Allow pets to still be hasted. Make the only haste a player can get be from bards or items. Allow different kinds of item haste to stack. Tune melee damage accordingly by having a haste item that works for everyone and then a class specific haste item that only works on that class. This will allow better differentiation between the melee classes in DPS.

4. Remove all disciplines. They were silly and continue to be silly. Defensive/Evasive epitomizes the problem.

5. Currently, all raw AC = 1.6 AC on a warrior. Increase this to 1.8.

6. To make up for loss of slow on mobs and haste on players for shaman and enchanters and beastlords, beef up shaman DoTs and debuffs, enchanters debuffs and ability more easily mez, and beastlords pet DPS.

7. Make shadowknights and paladins fight better on a mount than on the ground. They are knights, make them be knights! The extra DPS would be enjoyed by them, but the clumsiness of fighting on a horse would make it more work.

8. Allow rogues to walk on walls.

9. Increase the reuse time on nukes to force a greater variety of nukes to be used.

10. Retune raid exp so that it is roughly equal to the experience you would receive in an experience group per hour (definitely not per kill).

11. Add a plane that requires that you have a Vex Thal key to enter. Make the plane contain about 100 trash mobs, 2 mini mobs, and Aten Ha Ra so it is possible to actually get loot off of her without doing a long boring raid.

12. Increase pure melee's resistances to all magics. By not using magics, they should be innately better able to withstand the magics.

13. Give monks and warriors a natural spellshield equivalent to twice their level.

14. Make pets provide greater DPS. (This is done through the fact that only pets can be hasted now as mentioned earlier.)

More to come later.... (Back to work)

Groups that don't have a Cleric and a Warrior should perform worse as a result or need to exp in much easier/low experiance rate zones.

You want to see a little more balance and more fun? I see three actual tiers in EQ, zones and mobs for: top raiding guilds, up and coming guilds, and experiance zones that should also contain upgrades for the casual player. LDoN is going to be a huge boon for players that are not raiding (or at least for some classes).
