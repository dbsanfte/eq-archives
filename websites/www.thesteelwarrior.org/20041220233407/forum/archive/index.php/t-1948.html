I group with rangers almost every day. One of them I've been grouping with since we were both in our thirties....while I was on my hiatus from the game he passed me up.....by 300AAs. Anyway, I've seen him dish 270DPS before (using TS) and not once did that mob turn away from me dishing out my little 68DPS. The only time a ranger get's bow agro in a XP group situation is when he starts poping the mob on incomming. Assuming you have a monk puller, when the monk FDs then the ranger is the next target (because he has done more damage to the mob since your two arrow shots = 1/100th of the two he dropped in on incoming.) This is easily fixed, and doesn't require special AA's or items, tell the monk not to FD till you've engaged.

Now if the ranger pulls (as in most outdoor zones these days) of course he's gonna have agro when the mob comes into camp. If the rangers any good at his job you will grab agro shortly and he will take several steps back and you'll not see him get hit again. While rangers know they can take or give up agro whenever they want it, they also know they don't want it most of the time. Ranger agro is great where it is right now.
