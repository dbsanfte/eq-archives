I was thinking about this very subject a bit over the past day or two. I haven't tanked "high end" boss mobs yet (Vindi, Doz, BoT/Tactics minis are about as far as my guild had gotten) - so take this all with a grain of salt.

IMHO, defensive and evasive are detracting more from being a warrior than say a 10% bump in baseline mitigation and avoidance would give us.

Why you ask? Here's my thoughts:

A "good" tank basically will always keep defensive/evasive (D/E henceforth) open just in case it is needed. This means that we nearly never use our other disciplines. This means that we never realize our potential DPS upside that many people factor into our class.

If we got rid of D/E we would start using agressive, enrage, etc. like they were meant to be used - to increase our DPS and to provide a strategic offensive ability.

The down side? All those boss mobs that were desiged to be tough with a D/E warrior just got ~20% harder to tank. When I hear stories about Pallys and SK's being able to MT in PoTime, I find it hard to believe that this is a valid arguement to prevent getting rid of D/E.

So, I would say that SoE should dump D/E, and give us a 10-15% innate bump in mitigation and avoidance. Of course, this does next to nothing for the aggro issue - that's another pandora's box.

Findra
It's pretty simple how stun effects mob melee swings - and I think it is the same way for PC's too:

1. Everytime we swing a timer is started (proportional to weapon delay, modified by haste).
2. When the timer is up, we swing again.
3. If we are stunned when the timer is up, we do nothing until the stun wears off.
3a. When stun wears off, we swing again.

Therefore, stuns *can* reduce mob melee damage (in addition to all the spell interrupt and maybe dodge/parry/riposte supression?), as long as the stun overlapps the end of the mob's melee timer.

On a slowed mob, this is what separates a good Pally from a keyboard masher. A good pally will time the stuns to land just before the mob swings again - gaining the most mob idle time for the stun. Don't think it can happen? It is little different from Warriors that watch for tash, malo, slows and cripples to land on the mob and hit Taunt immediately afterward.
