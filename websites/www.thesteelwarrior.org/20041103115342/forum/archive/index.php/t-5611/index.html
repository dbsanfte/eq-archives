wandor
Ok, lets get something straight first - you can put Anger 3 on ANYTHING and it is going to be at least above average for holding agro. That's just common sense.

Now, here is what you need to keep in mind when augmenting weapons -

1. What do you use the weapon for? If you have a DPS weapon - DON'T PUT ANGERX on it!!! Now, you are taking a DPS weapon and turning it in to a half ass agro weapon. GSoT+angerX is going to be better agro than any dps weapon with angerX. For the couple hundred PP this is going to cost you, suck it up and use it until you can get a SEWS or BoW or other real agro weapon. Don't waste the points trying to make a DPS weapon in to an agro weapon. If you have ever wiped to a mob at 1%, then you know how important it is for everyone to do the max amount of damage you can do. Put life tap or DD augs on DPS weapons.

2. For agro weapons, there is something else you need to consider. The vast majority of warriors have at least a SotB and a CHoS available to them through the bazaar. While these are fine agro weapons, chances are very good that you will have a viable upgrade some time in the near future. Unless it is a weapon that you think you will be weilding for several months, put anger2 on it rather than anger3. There is no point in wasting the extra points because a) you will probably be upgrading soon and b) 2x anger2 is better than 1x anger3 for less points. I would not recommend getting anger3 until you get to a point that there are no viable weapon upgrades for at least a couple of months.
