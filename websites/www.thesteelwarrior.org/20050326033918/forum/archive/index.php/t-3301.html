Maestro is a real badass. Our MT fell once and I immedietly picked up the slack, as I was supposed to, but the clerics couldn't transfer to me fast enough. I was dead within seconds. His melee can be downright ridiculous especially with his proc.

Kill those hands at 75%, otherwise you'll never get passed Maestro's "new" regen. At 30-40%, the banshees come alive and that's when the raid tends to fall apart. One of them AE manadrains, one of them charms, and one of them sings a fire resist song for Maestro. Root them to keep them in place and offtank them, focus on finishing Maestro. He actually doesn't have that many hp at all.

Lastlyl, our MT mentioned his proc isn't a lifetap. In our developing stage for this guy, we had our raid go out and try to finish off the banshees. Despite being proc'd on many multiple times, Maestro's health never went up.
