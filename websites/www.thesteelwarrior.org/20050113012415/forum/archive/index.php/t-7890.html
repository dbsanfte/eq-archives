I strongly suggest doing the Strength of the Elements quest, easy and the places to obtain the pieces aren't hard to get to.
1. Go to Lavastorm Mountains, grab a fusible igneous ore from the ground.
2. Kill Phinny in Kedge Keep, he is a pansy, take fusible coral ore.
3. Head down to the Hole where Guardian of the Seal is (jump down from Paineel with SoE) pull the animated constructs next the GoTS and loot fusible earthen ore from one.
4. Take a port with a druid to Cobalt scar, zone into Siren's Grotto and evac thru and zone into Western Wastes. Run around the zone a bit and get druid to track Guardian Kozzalym, easy mob, kill and loot fusible velium ore.
5. Track for Makil Rargon, human, speak to him about fusibility research etc etc. Get books, get box, put all the ores into the box, hit combine and hand it back to Makil Rargon.
6. Now you have some awesome Warrior shoulders!
