Hola-

For raids, an anger 3 aug'ed bow + WA AAs + bellow (and for the paranoid perhaps a hate amp from ench/sk and a buff proc) was quite sufficient to hold aggro in all but unusual cases (aggro wipe/reduction/FD effects, etc). Basically, the dps put out by the tank is rotten anyway, and if a 2h is good enough to hold aggro and minimizes the dmg taken (riposte + damage shield) then it's doing it's job. That was PoP and for a large part GoD.

Things change now tho, since the stats on 2 newer 1h weapons are significantly better than on the "best" 2hs, and there are AAs that eliminate the extra ripo/ds damage. Or if max turtle mode is needed, there are some very viable shields that work well with top end 1h weapons and still allow aggro locks... and that "block" AA could be huge.

For grouping, the additional DPS from dual wielding even while tanking makes things even clearer, again given the offhand AAs. And for non-tanking situations, 2 low aggro high dps 1h weapons have always been better (except vs. DSes, obviously).

That all make sense? The BoW is still a good weapon, but it's 2 expansions old now, and from a pre-elemental encounter. Works until something better is available, it's not like there are so many choices unfortunately (i.e. dark blade of luckydrops).

RdT.
