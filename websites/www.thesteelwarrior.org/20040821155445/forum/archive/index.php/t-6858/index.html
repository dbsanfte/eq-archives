Go into your start menu and click run. From there type msconfig. This will bring up a new window. Click selective startup and uncheck load startup items then reboot. The only thing you need to have running in Windows98 is explorer. The rest are just eating resources.

Thalzaar
Win98 really sucks for freeing up RAM. It's the main reason win2k/xp is much better. win98 pretty much caps at 512 ram and anything passed that, win 98 won't recognize or use effeciently.

anyhow, i did a quick google on your loadqm and i'm assuming you use MSN to connect to the internet. Apparently, according to Microsoft, this will take alot of resources for it's autoupdate feature. You can read about it from the knowledge base HERE (http://support.microsoft.com/?kbid=309418)

also, get rid of the qtask. it's just quicktime manager and it's not needed and just takes up ram that could be used elsewhere

and as usual, download spybot search and destroy, update it and scan for spyware. they have a nasty habit of slowing down your system. Run a defrag if you haven't in a while either. It will definately help.
