For the issue about failure of taunt, I don't think you realize how powerful Taunt is when it succeeds. If it succeeds 40% more often than it currently is, you have AE Taunt that refreshes 100 times faster than the current one even with maxed out Hastened Instigation. If you have a nuke that say takes 1/10th of the mob's HP away and it only works 50% of the time, it is still way more powerful than anything else any class can do for damage.

For running out of endurance, it only happens with certain types of groups, assuming the warrior himself is doing a reasonable job at budgeting Endurance.

There's no way you can use up your Endurance bar on a great group. It just doesn't happen. Part of the definition of 'great' is that the group knows what they're doing. A great group works fine with warrior before Incite and it still works fine now.

If you've a group that sucks, such a group will quickly either replace you (so you couldn't possibly run out of endurance because you're not there anymore), die (and you don't use endurance if you're dead or waiting for someone to med), or most likely just have some horribly low efficency that you'd never run out of endurance. I certainly can't imagine a group that thinks a warrior sucks at aggro actually go out and fetch 3 mobs at once unless they have a death wish.

You run out of Endurance on groups that think they can handle 3 or so mobs at once but they really should not be. This happens a lot on LDoN more so than PoP. It's actually pretty hard to run out of endurance for PoP groups because you just won't have that many mobs for you to use your endurance on. Of course, you can also budget your endurance poorly in any group.

I'm of the opinion that any group I run out of endurance on from just using Incite is worse than even a group that cannot control aggro because the latter I can save them with Incite (ignoring the ones that have death wishes), but the former I eventually won't be able to.
