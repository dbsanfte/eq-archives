To determine this, look at the unaugged bracers. You have an unauged kizrak at 28ac 45hp, and you have an unaugged MTBB at 28ac 75hp.

You have a +15ac aug on the kizrak, I'd say put the +15ac aug on the MTBB instead.

If, however, you were wearing both bracers, and you upgraded one, and you just want to know which you should wear until you get a second bracer, I'd suggest wearing the Kizrak's for now.

If you have a while before you upgrade bracer again, I'd hold out for another +15ac augment (or if you already have both augs, remove one before going to ferubi next time you go) and slapping it on the MTBB.

If you are going to get a new bracer within the next 3 weeks or whatever, just stick with the Kizraks, wash off the sta aug and sell/twink the MTBB.
