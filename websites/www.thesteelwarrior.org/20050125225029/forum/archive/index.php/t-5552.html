As a general rule in a DKP system, you should always upgrade the item that gives you the biggest upgrade. What I normally do is to rank the items I have against the items I have the potential of getting, then sort them by the amount of the upgrade. If you are really motivated you can do this with an Excel spreadsheet. Once I have everything ranked, I use that as a guide to what I bid on. I tend to only go for items that are a top 4 or 5 upgrade for me so I am always upgrading the item that needs it the most. You can get a lot more out of DKP this way and you are a lot less likely to upgrade the same slot in quick succession.
