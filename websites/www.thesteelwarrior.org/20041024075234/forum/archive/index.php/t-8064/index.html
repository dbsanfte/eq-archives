Okay my guild jumped onto this today, here is what you need to do.

1) To the best of my knowledge, and according to everyone I've talked to, you have to do the OLD VP key quest. Before you can get your new VP key, you must have Key of Veeshan. Once you've completed that quest, you are really almost done. Apparently you can also collect 3 essences from 3 Kunark Dragons. I'm just posting the method that about 25 or so people used today to make their new keys.

2) Go to Skyfire with a raid force. There are 4 orbs in Skyfire that when you click on them it gives some text about you feeling a power, blah blah blah. Once all have been drained, you go to the VP zone in, there is a new big dragon there, say "I am ready" and Suled Dar's Shade will spawn right next to the lavalake in the middle of the zone.

3) Kill the Shade Dragon. We did it with 40 people, and he has very few hps, though his AEs are pretty mean. Was mainly just a zerg, but at a *guess* if you have good tactics and competent people I'd say you need at least 20 or so to do this. The info we have on him is only that's he KoS, redcon, perma rooted, mitigates slow, low hps. No loot on him.

4) Loot one of the Tomes from the 6 ancient chests that spawn around the dragon. I think there were 6 chests with 8 tomes each, so that's 48 tomes for your trouble.

5) Go back to the huge dragon at the VP zone in and give him the Tome. He'll hand you back Brood Dragon Blood.

6) Go NW some of the VP hall in Skyfire until you find another named drake, who is indifferent to all surrounded by some guardian drakes who are KoS and hit hard, for Skyfire mobs(300, 400ish). Kill those guards, hail the named Drake, and say "like to know" and he will give you a 4-slot container, Drained Cipher of Veeshan.

7) There are now 2 ways to make the Ciper of Veeshan, which is an any type 30/30/30 augment which will go in any gear. It is the new key to VP, and once you zone in, it's on your keyring.

a) This is the way I know, that was used today in my guild. Take your old Key of Veeshan, put it in the Drained Cipher of Veeshan with the Brood Dragon Blood, combine, and you get it.

b) This is the new quest, which is as of yet unconfirmed. It involves gettign 3 different dragon essences, from god knows who, but there are definitely 3, since it is a 4slot container. Combine those 3 essences in the Drained Cipher of Veeshan along with the Brood Dragon Blood to make the Cipher.


Okay, that's all I know. Sorry for not including any locs or anything, but this is my memory from doing all this stuff this afternoon, and I'm too lazy to get the specific details for spawn locs and stuff, but this is all accurate as far as it goes. As I type this a bunch in my guild are working their way through the new VP atm, all of them using the old key, and getting their Brood Dragon Blood from the kill this morning.

But I don't have my key done, so I get to be bored and post stuff :P

GL on the new and old quest.
