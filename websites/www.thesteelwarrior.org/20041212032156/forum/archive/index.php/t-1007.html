If you're the MT for your guild you want tactical mastery 3 next. I see a nice difference in increased number of hits with TM2, so TM3 must be even better. It'll definately help with your agro.

For aggro based it'd make sense to get the attack increasing skills first, like Ferocity or Punishing Blade of Ambi. It is not clear whether piercing a mob's special defense makes them any more angry than not piercing it (and certainly very hard to verify) while it can be safely assumed that if you're attacking more often, that should net you more aggro (well, it might turn out to be false, but seems like a good guess as any).

From a damage point of view, Tactical Mastery and Punishing Blade are probably the best, and both have their limitations (only from front and only 2H weapons). Ferocity is probably the 3rd best, if only because it always works. Crit hits, Flurry, and Ambi are all decent skills but the first two are a bit pricy and the latter only works with DW (and isn't exactly cheap either).
