While Paladins can do other things than just tank, they rarely get a group slot to do them. I am almost always invited to a group to be the tank, and if not it is because no DPS class was LFG.

The Pacify issue is being a bit overblown here. Up until level 60, a Paladin can use Pacify to pull singles in most experience camps they fight in. After level 60, Pacify won't work on the mobs that give the best experience (you get the best experience from mobs within 5 levels of you). By the time you are 65, Pacify is not a factor in our group-ability.

Up until 60th level, Warriors do ok compared to Paladins, so the extra utility of Pacify is not as much an issue as it would be for higher level Paladins. From 61 – 65 Pacify can be used by a Paladin who is 'bottom-feeding' by grouping to fight lower content. A Paladin would have more than enough HP/AC to outright tank in those camps anyway, so there would be little reason to require a Warrior tank for that group.

Regarding the Paladins of Norrath thread about Pacify, some Paladins (including some influential ones) argue for being able to Pacify mobs up to 60th level, and a couple people advocate getting Pacification.
