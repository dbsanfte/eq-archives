Azaza
If you've been collecting pieces gradually, have the means to get the harder pieces (PoSky blade, Chardok Queen blade, Ball in Fear, arguably the scales), and they're actually an upgrade.... go for it.

Generally, EP level and higher players have better weapons to use over the epic. Below that it's just a matter of preference whether people actually want it.

I use the 2h and red blade regularly and I'm not complaining. It took me a long time to get the various pieces, and I'm happy to have gotten it.

But there are several (relatively) easier to obtain, headache free, better weapons out there.

As long as you can do your job, that's what matters.
