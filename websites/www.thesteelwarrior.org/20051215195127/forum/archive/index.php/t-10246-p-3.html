I think rage is the key. A mage or a warlock knows for a fact exactly how much mana they have to work with. The same holds true for a priest, shaman, or druid. A rogue gets a set amount of energy and his energy refreshes at a pretty steady rate. A hunter has more mana than he knows what to do with and non-stop firing won't deplete it. Rage, how it's generated and used, needs a serious adjustment. There may still be room for a modification or two after that, but rage mods would be a big step in the right direction.
