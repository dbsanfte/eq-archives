This week (as of Noon EST Friday):

Total posts: 426
Total threads: 39

Topic: GoD Riddles
Issue: Random people can ask for a riddle in zones such as Yxtta and then when others come along they wont be able to get a new one, effectively locking them out.
Resolution: Corrected riddles to allow them to reset - Will be live in next patch.

Topic: AAs being linked to levels in OoW and lvl 65 exp nerf.
Issue: People were concerned that you would need a certain number of AAs in order to level up 65-70. They were also concerned that level 65 exp would be nerfed to 50% as it was in Kunark.
Resolution (AA): "There are no plans to require AAs to level from 66 to 70." -Rytan
Resolution (Lvl 65 exp nerf): "Also false." -Rytan

Topic: Raid content for non high-end guilds
Issue: Provide raid content that casual/family guilds can do.
Suggestions: Ideas for scalable content, decreasing respawn time on more casual raid mobs. (Discussion still ongoing)

Topic: Update on Class Issues
Response: "As to answers to the compiled class lists. Right now the team is focusing on things that are broken or bugs. Many of those changes will be coming in the next few weeks. Anything involving changes to DPS, is being evaluated and will be a longer term fix. Wish list items will be evaluated after those changes." - Brenlo
Note: We have 2 interviews planned in the next couple weeks. One with Brenlo regarding the current state of the wizard class. The other with Rytan focusing specifically on the class issues we submitted and changes he's thinking of making based on those issues.

Topic: GoD Baking
Issue: There are no recipes for Taelosian Wheat
Resolution: No response yet (fairly new thread)

Topic: Exodus Bug
Issue: If you spam click Exodus but still die when you get back to your bind point exodus goes off anyway.
Resolution: Waiting on response

Topic: The Level 30-50 Number
Issue: Lots wondered how they calculated it (did it include bazaar mules, etc)
Resolution: "It is true that most accounts are in that range. However I do not think we will be releasing the exact numbers. We do take mules into account as well and have an estimate as to how many of the accounts are secondary or tertiary accounts.

One thing to remember is that EQ has over 500K subscribers worldwide. That's a lot of folks spread across 48 servers, plus the foreign servers. The casual gamers will not have the same play habits as the "Uber" gamers and thus it is rare that their paths will cross. It is also a matter of perspective. The hard core player tends to hang out in hard core places while the casual game is putting along in many other zones. Thus the perspective among those groups will be much different. It is easy to say, "well all I see are high level people," when you only frequent high level places. There are nearly 300 zones in this game. People are everywhere." - Brenlo

Topic: Breakdown in Communication Quest
Issue: Primals dropping pages, Night Watchman Fxi spawn
Resolution: Primals will now drop pages 100% of the time. In addition mobs all across the zone will drop the pages (though much more rarely) allowing a single-group progression through this step. Night Watchman spawn has been tweaked to make it a more common.

Topic: Guild Wars
Discussion (ongoing): Ideas for having an instanced Guild war zone with certain rules and such. Could have instance specific win conditions, durations, etc.

Topic: LDoN
Issue:
(1) Request to be able to return items for a refund.
(2) Request to be able to remove augments without destroying them.
Resolution:
(1) Rytan is looking into a system for refunding items. Almost definitely this will be implemented in some form although the exact percentages and such are still up to debate (many people suggested 50% return).
(2) Looking into trade-skill based solvents that could be used to remove augments without destroying them. Most likely low level augments would use simple easy to get components and type 8 augments will require a stronger solvent that will require a little more uncommon components (my suggestion was using some of the GoD tradeskill stuff - or making these components drop in hard ldons) They're also considering making the solvents straight up vendor purchased.
Note: Discussion still ongoing on both points, will keep you all updated.

Topic: FV Roleplaying
Issue: Many people go to FV for the no drop rules rather than to roleplay
Discussion: Two senior SoE staff (not sure if they'd want their names here) have been very active in this thread. They're looking into ways to reward those who do RP along with other suggestions. (Discussion ongoing)

Topic: New PC Models
Issue: People were curious as to new PC models that Smedley told us would be released hopefully sometime this year. There was concern over them being outsourced.
Response: All artwork goes through SoE art people before hitting the game to make sure its how they want it. There's a rumor out there we get pointy hats, but the only official word I got on that when I asked was: "I am going to make you sit on a pointy hat. Silly Wizard. Trix are for kids." -Brenlo

Topic: Spell Recovery Bug
Issue: With fast casting spells sometimes you can't misplaced error messages casting.
Resolution: (What spell recovery bug? =D) Being looked at.

Topic: Experience in Natimbi/Qinimi/Riwwi
Issue: Exp is very low for these zones which are aimed at casual non high-end players
Resolution: Just posted today, no response yet

Topic: Norrath-themed Expansions
Discussion: What expansions could be done using Norrath lore (no aliens!)
Lots of good ideas here including an 'underground norrath', miners stumbling into the Underfoot through the hole, adding a bunch of new zones off Antonica and other old continents (Dragonscale Hills anyone?) Still going on here.
