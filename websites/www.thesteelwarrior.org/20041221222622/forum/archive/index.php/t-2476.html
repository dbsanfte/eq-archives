Depends on your STA.

It would be difficult to work out the raw hp you gain.

You would have to determine exactly how much hp/sta you get, then multiply that number by your unbuffed STA, then subtract the answer from your total unbuffed HP.

Then when you dinged, you would have to determine your exact hp/sta again, multiply that by your unbuffed STA, subtract it from your total HP, then subtract that number by the number you got from the equation above.

at lvl 60+ , warriors get lvl/10 hp per sta ( under 255, half that over 255)
so when u ding at 60+, u get STA*0.1 hps, sta capped at 255, plus STA over 255 * 0.05
meaning a 200 sta warrior will win 20 hps, a 255 sta warrior will win 25.5 hps, and a 300 sta warrior will win 25.5 + 4.5/2 = 27.75hps
