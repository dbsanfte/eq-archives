Nimic
Follow up stupid question --

I thought you had to be standing right at the tribute master to turn on your benefits. Has that changed?

Benefits can now be enabled and disabled anywhere. Changing benefits still requires being in front of tribute master. Only Guild officers/leaders can turn Guild tribute on and off. To enable guild tribute you must first "opt in" and this can only be done at tribute master.
