10-20-2004, 04:20 PM
Before you start, figure out where you want to fight him. Then have your healers do a range check on MT, and have everyone but MT and rampage tank at the healers. Have your RT pull to ensure they get rampage. Make sure at the very least, MT and RT have levi/dmf, and beat the tar out of it. Do a ranged fight and it may last awhile, but you have a better chance of success as your healers arent wasting mana healing and curing people.

Also of note. he doesn't summon until he is at 98% health, so if he does the knockback at 99-100% you need to reposition him and yourself or it can get ugly.
