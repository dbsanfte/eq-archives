First off, to enter BoT (Bastion of Thunder, zin is behind PoS zin) you must be level 62+, or have completed Askr the Lost's quest (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2269) . Note that in order for this quest to work, you must have passed the trials in PoJ (aka be FLAGGED to enter PoV/S).

Agnarr the Storm Lord*(70) and Karana(70) are the highest level mobs, requiring keys from killing Tower Named.
*Agnarr is split up in to 3 mini-events. Evynd Firestorm(68) is the first one, once he is defeated you port up and kill Emmerick Skyfury(68). After he dies you port up and kill Agnarr.
There are 4 spawns during the Agnarr fight, one spawns with him(slowable), the others at 75% HP(slowable), 50% HP(slowable), and 25% HP(not slowable). Their names are (in order of spawning) Jolur Sandstorm(67), Ekil Thundercall(67), Oljin Stormtide(67), then Hibdin Cyclone(67).

There are 4 main tower bosses, these are our main targets:
NE Tower - Brynju Thunderclap(67) - Lightning Giant
Amnquetil Brynjulffr(64) spawns in the room with the portal up to the Arena level.
Atle Cloudburst(64) spawns in the first room on the left when you zone in.
Bordir Bjomolf(64) spawns in the first room on the right off of the first long hall.

NW Tower - Kuanbyr Hailstorm(67) - Sandstorm Giant
Kuanbyr is protected by 3 wasps, Hrani, Leistr, and Iri, who spawn in the room with him, all 65, each drop an EP.

SE Tower - Auliffe Chaoswind(67) - Tornado Giant
Erech Eyford(64) spawns at the last portal before Auliffe

SW Tower - Eindride Icestorm(67) - Blizzard Giant
Fogl Iceshard(64) spawns after the first portal.
Galm Snowdrift(64) spawns in the first room on the left when you zone in.

All mobs are referred to as "Minis" because they are EXP Camp Named. "'Boss' Minis" are level 65 and drop better loot.
West EXP Camp (Lightning Giants)
"Boss" Mini: Hreidar Lynhilling(65)
Minis: Wybjorn(64), Torstien Stoneskin(64)

South EXP Camp (Sandstorm Giants)
Middle South Minis: Ymir Stormseer(64), Thangbrand(64) (spawn across hall from eachother)
Deeper Middle South Minis: Thorolf Sutherland(64), Valbrand(64) (spawn across hall from eachother)
Deep South "Boss" Mini: Guakr Sandstorm(65)
Deeper South Mini: Rorek Steelthorn(64)

North EXP Camp (Tornado Giants)
1st Level Mini: Elif Whitewind(64)
3rd Level "Boss" Mini: Laef Windfall(65)

East EXP Camp (Blizzard Giants)
Minis: Eindride Icestorm(64), Riodhr Torrentwind(64)
"Boss" Mini: Oreen Wavecrasher(65)
