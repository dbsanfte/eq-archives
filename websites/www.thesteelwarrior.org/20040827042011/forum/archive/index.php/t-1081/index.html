Ylander
If you are doing the guk faction as a solo warrior and have dead side to yourself, to maximize the number of kills i recommend you to start clearing from the safehall towards the zoneline of upper guk and kill every frog you can find.

The frogs on this side have significantly lower amount of hps compared to the frogs on the other side of Safehall but net the same faction hit. When everything on that side is cleared, go back to safehall. If nothing have repopped yet, start clearing the other side until things start to repop on the 'lower' side again.

Also afaik Sebilis frogs does not give any more faction hits then lower guk, both dungeons net the same faction hit i.e. one faction hit per dead frog.
