Well just a few small suggestions.

Helm replace with Raldukan helm maybe or Velious quest helm.

Earrings I would replace 1 with Earring of the Solstice, very nice hp and other stats for the money (buy everything to make it for about 3-6k) or Black Saphire Electrum earring (not sure on cost, I would think cheap but 35hp on it)

Mask replace with Death Mask of the Elysians (4-6k) decent stats
Necklace replace with Clattering Bone Necklace (not sure price, should be cheap)

Replace Cloak with anything HP, Ancient Sea Horse Hide cloak is pretty cheap 1k or so. Heiro Cloak 75hp about 15k, Malestrom cloak about 5kish

Weapons maybe a frostbringer but not needed that much there.

Overall at 54 you are not doing bad, much better than I was for sure. Like I said shoot for every slot to have some +hp stat on it, no matter how small it may be it adds up.

From what ive heard the barnacle axe is nothign special aggro wise. I would sell it and pick up a frosty. Although i see you have a club of the ice ocean that should work well with the snowchipper aggro wise.

I would sell the rest of your weapons and aim to pick up a decent 2 hander with a ratio of 1 or thereabouts.

theres quite a few good ones out there these days. pity your piercing is so bad, otherwise i would say get a narandi's lance as they are good value for money and hold aggro pretty well.

from here on in, i wouldnt spend bits and pieces on upgrades. save up and get a good upgrade for each slot till everything is well kitted out. for starters you need to ditch the crusty. the ancient seahorse hide cloak is also a good bet. id aim to get your ac up to around 1100 if possible.

if your CoV faction is good, do the Bracers of benevolence quest, they willl get you 2 good bracers that you can use for a long time.

Eventually youll have to ditch the SCHWs. Theres a lot of decent gloves that you can be using in their place. Id look to pick up a haste belt at some stage.

Also, earrings once, save up for a solstice earring, they are about 5k and good value for money.

BP wise id get in on some PoG raids or Kael raids and try and score a drop. Othewise, save up for one of the cultural ones. I know ur agnostic but i think you can use the rigid steelweave stuff. You can also pick up some of that for other parts of your body.

Radulkan helm as someone said is a good upgrade. I see you hafe some moktor so stick out hunting in UP for a bit and hopefully you can score one.

I know making money is hard for a warrior but its not impossible by any means. If you guild can manage, camp out OS for a few weeks. Try and get in the crypt if you can and you will start to see the money come in.

Re aaxp, its been said before and ill say it again, levels beats aaxp, at least through the 50s. Dont forget, if you face mobs as a MA and they con higher to you, which you will do if you go to PoP, then taunt just wont work. So id get to 60 or 61 before you start doing aaxp.
