Symbol of the Planesmasters

Aten gloves don't outlive that.Wrong.

The resist on Saryrn symbol is irrelevant for hate generation. The proc creates just as much hate on a resist as it does when it lands, with most of the hate coming from the stun portion.

The only advantages Saryrn symbol has are duration of the buff, slightly better dps, and the stun cap. The duration really isn't an advantage given the fact you can rebuff Aten gloves at any time. The cast time on symbol is a liability. The dps isn't really a factor. Call of fire proc is -100 resist adjust, making it land on almost as much stuff as Pestilence Shock. It's a marginal dps increase at best. Even then, you're a tank, not a dps class. The stun is still capped at 65 which makes it almost useless on raids. About the only thing it can do that CoF can't is interrupt casters in xp groups.

The advantages Aten gloves have are super fast (0.5 sec) cast (can rebuff mid-fight while tanking, can use in a low slot on pellers, etc.), and inventory clickable. You don't have to fumble to swap em in to click if you've upgraded the slot. Most people have to sacrifice range slot choice to get Saryrn symbol - no Quarm stone, no VZ orb, no RZ shurikens, no VZ bow, etc. Even if they get both symbol and another range, they gotta swap to buff. Anybody with a Saryrn symbol who gets a spiffy new GoD range with Quarm-like stats in a few months is gonna have to swap, etc., etc. A lot of people with Saryrn symbol would gladly trade it for Aten gloves, just like Emilia posted above. They're 10x more convenient to use and one of the best clickies a War will ever see.

So I repeat. Aten gloves will outlive all that stuff, Saryrn symbol included. Get them if you can.
