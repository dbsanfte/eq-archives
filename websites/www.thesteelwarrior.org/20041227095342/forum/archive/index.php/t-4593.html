You don't have to have the person targeted while you shield them. You just have to be within arm's-reach of the person.

I have a couple ways of shielding:

1) for the person who's most likely to get aggro and die (usually enchanter), I make a hotkey just for them, which says "/shield Soandso". I don't have to target them when I use this macro. If it's a pickup group, then I also add a line saying "I'm taking half of Soandso's damage for 24 seconds, stay near me!"

2) for everyone else, I have a generic /shield hotkey. If I'm not getting hit and my taunt fails, then I assist the mob and hit the shield key to protect the person who's taking the beating. Then I hit my "return-to-previous-target" key and continue fighting the mob as usual.

When you use shield all the time, it'll be available every 2nd fight, pretty much.
