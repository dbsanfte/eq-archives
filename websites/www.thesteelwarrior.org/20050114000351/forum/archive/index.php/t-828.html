I'm assuming from your current gear that your cash flow probably is not significant. If that is the case, you're at the level now where you can begin to build a four to five digit bank account. Get your guild together and raid Lower Guk. Go to Velks. Go to Karnor's Castle and camp Cappy. Get your Sebilis key and tag along with a few high level friends. Hell, borrow some money from a rich pal and get a blacksmithing store started. I don't know the rules for agnostics who blacksmith, but I'm sure you can get a lucritive business off the ground (and you can make some good gear for yourself, while you're at it).

E-bay 10k from Yantis. Keep a keen eye out in the Bazaar, buy low, sell high. Send tells to high level players relentlessly to "spare some cash plz or weapons u dont need thx."

TAG ALONG ON AS MANY NAGAFEN/VOX RAIDS AS YOU CAN. DO IT FOR MONTHS. IF YOU DON'T WIN ANYTHING, DON'T GIVE UP.

However you do it, here is what to do with your new-found fortune:

1. Earrings - Get rid of both of the ones you have and get two of the player-made +30 (or is it +35?) HP earrings. I don't know the name of these earrings, but check the bazaar search engine under "ear" and find them. They should be only a few hundred plat.

2. Ring - Bank the ring with the saves, and purchase another 6 AC 65 HP ring. THIS WILL HELP BIG TIME.

3. Neck - Clattering Bone Necklace. If you have to go cheap, get a Glimmering Mithril Torque.

4. Face - Death Mask of the Elysians. Cheap: Spider Eyes.

5. Wrists - Wrists are pretty much ass for low-budget warriors since Cobalt Bracers sell for 10k+. Camp some silver chitin wristbands, or do the Bracer of Benevolence quest (twice) when you're high enough.

6. Vambraces - Player-made, like your shoulders. Cheap: Cobalt.

7. Gloves - Most people complain about SCHW on warriors, but I disagree in your case. You lose 15 AC having these as compared to Cobalt Gloves (25 AC), but if you get a haste belt at your budget, you'll lose even more AC replacing your good belt. Keep SCHW until you get a Seblisian Berserker's Cloak, Runebraided Girdle, or better.

8. Shoulders - fine for your budget.

9. Breastplate - get a Barbed Ringmail Tunic, or save up about 8k and buy a smithed BP, which will boost both your AC and HP immensely.

10. Cape - fine for your budget. Maybe a Dire Wolf cloak if you have some spare pp.

11. Belt - Fine. Some may argue for a spider fur belt, but I personally feel that the extra AC would benefit you more.

12. Legs - You ought to be able to pick up some Blood Runed Greaves fairly cheap, which would up your AC TREMENDOUSLY (39 AC) and give you a 25 HP boost. After replacing your jewelry, I recommend that this should be the next slot you work on.

13. Shoes - slip on those Cobalt Boots that you have bagged. The extra AC will benefit you much more than the +4 STA and other stats.

14. Range - fine.

15 Weapons - Use the Weighty Polearm all the time; even though it will result in a slight decrease in DPS, the AC, AGI, and HP are important. Sell your Wind Lance and (if your server is like mine) pick up a Sebilite Croaking Dirk (10/18 with stats) for under 2k. Buy the 30/40 warrior only 2hb and a Jade Mace 1hb to work your skill - these should be cheap, especially the 30/40 which goes for under 50pp sometimes. Please, do not make the same mistake I did. UP ALL OF YOUR WEAPON SKILLS. When money permits, think about buying a Frostbringer for taunt. You may take the above suggestions and get a Black Blade of Tormenting now. If worse comes to worse, get two Short Swords of the Ykesha.

Finally, check back on these boards often and read other's adice posts. Many people don't answer these posts because the same questions have already been asked ... you need only look for them. Listen to people here; most (if not all) of them know what they are talking about, and can more than likely give you more qualified advice than I have offered. Though I may have more experience than you in this game, I've only leveled to 53 with my warrior, and I also have much to learn.
