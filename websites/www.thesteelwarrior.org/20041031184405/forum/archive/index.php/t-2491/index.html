A L50 Warrior in full crusty flat out owns a L30 twink.

Even if you don't worry too much about equip, your should be fine till L50 with the armor you got.

Also, try to pick up an Argent Protector (2hs), Blood Point (piercing), Stormwood Battlestaff (2hb).

Try to get into some groups with a Pally or SK. At L30-ish, there's not much of a difference in hitpoints, and generally the knights end up tanking. Use this to your advantage and build all your weapon skills. I used to equip whatever weapon i had the lowest skill in each time i started a session.

Post L60 is when you'll really be appreciated, so don't let those knights get ya down, I'm running into more and more situations where they make me tank instead of the knights.

Your on the right track, just put that damn lammy in your PRI! ><

Also, a note on duel wielding. Delay has no effect in the offhand. A "duel wield check" is rolled each time your primary weapon is used. So you want that primary weapon swinging as fast as you can, so you'll get more chances at a successful duel wield.

Work those 2handed stats! They are extremely important, because when you end up tanking PoP mobs, slow (but HIGH damage) 2 handed weapons means the mob won't riposte on you (for 500+ damage) as much. Duel wielding means you theoretically could catch 4 of those per round! Currently one of the best purchasable 2h weapons is the Greatstaff of Thunder. Who know's what LDoN will bring. So keep all them stats maxed. Don't be afraid to use the trainers. Most L60+ ppl have 100 training points just sitting there unused.
