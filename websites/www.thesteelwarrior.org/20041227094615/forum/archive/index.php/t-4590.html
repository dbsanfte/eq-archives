Resistant and Fearless are on the same timer, 30 min refresh

Evasive and Defensive are on the same timer, 10 min 30 sec refresh

Charge and Mightystrike and Agressive are on the same timer, 30 min refresh

Fellstrike and Fortitude are on the same timer 45 min refresh for fortitude, 30 for Fellstrike.

I couldn't get furious or precise to work, npc ate the tomes but I didn't get the skill. /bugged it

Any of the disciplines that are not on the same timer can be used independently, so theoretically you could hit resistant on inc (this is actually worth clicking now, +20 to all saves for 5 minutes), use fortitude to nullify all melee damage for 12 seconds when the mob gets to camp, go defensive when fortitude wears, and then use mightystrike or charge once defensive is off.

30 minutes later, you can do it all again. Very nice, major improvement over the old system.
