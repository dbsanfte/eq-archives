Eoden
Rings - Get 2x Valorium rings of Gallantry to replace the velium fire wedding rings, the extra stamina should give you more HPs even with reduced stats at lvl 50.

Waist - Go for either a Soulscream belt (might be able to camp this one in Veksar) or a Rune Branded Girdle to replace your FBSS

Ranged - Get either a Nightmare Wood Compound bow, Darkwood bow of suffering or a bone carved short bow for the ranged slot. If you can handle not having a bow there an Idol of the Thorned is another cheap option with good AC and stats.

Neck - A clattering bone necklace (rare but campable from the grey), a dragon tooth choker or a mithril studded something from Veksar

Helmet - Helm of the Raldukan is a decent helm for hps/sta/ac. Buy an armour dye with it so you dont have to have a pink hat (unless you want to have a pink hat ofcourse)

Earrings - Cheap: the 2ac 35hp black sapphire JC'd earrings, Vah Shir Claw earring. Medium Price: Spiderfang earring, electrified earring of the tempest, blood earring of rage. Expensive: Mosquito leg earrings or Blood metal earring of Engagement.
