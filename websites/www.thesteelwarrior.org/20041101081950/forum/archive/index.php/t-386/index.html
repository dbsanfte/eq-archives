He spawns straight back & left in akheva.

The best place to fight him (imo anyways) is in one of the corners of the room straight back from zone in (the big room you make the left in to get to him).

Hussar
We usually pull him to zone in. Easy as hell mob, just takes some time to kill. Make sure you don't let him touch walls near zone in, as he's been known to warp / despawn. He drops some decent stuff, but not too often. He really likes to drop the PoS bracer / belt (both have ac, and no other stats). If you pull him to the hall from zone in, it's a good place to practice for your guild on keeping a mob centered.

Rhagulim
My guild took him down with about 12-15 lvl 55+ and a lvl 63 tank. We had a puller tag the mobs as we went along and cleared the zone until we reached the large corner area Gallerus mentioned. Our puller continued down the hallway to where he's located pulling the mobs to us until everything was cleared, then Va'Dyn himself. Other mobs will repop before he's killed, so control the push and keep him close to the corner and away from the walls and fallen pillars. He took about 10-15 minutes to kill. He wiped us the first try when the enchanter hit him with an early slow that resisted and got aggro and was killed. Make sure he's pulled with something low aggro so the MT can regain aggro quickly and you should be ok. Once the slows land and a CH chain starts in rythmn you'll be fine, just watch the enrages and control his location.
