Some rangers on test have reported what the reward is, albeit unverified. Anyway according to the patch message there is such a NPC who you are now meant to hail, so at least that part is true. I cant bring myself to believe the rest till i log in later today, but if there is any truth to it, well, hot damn!

Theres a new mob in a building near the Soulbinder named Gram Dunnar.

Hail him. He will give 2 []'ed words.
The First one gets you a Charm that has stats specific to your class and the number of POP zones you have access to (not fixed on what flags you had BEFORE you got the charm, it will ramp up when you get more POP zone flags)

The second key word you have to say 'I will tell stories'.

****If you have XP going 100% into AA points.... ****

Each time you say this, you will get >> *1* AA point <<
Say it multiple times. You will get 1 aa point for each flag you have.

Before the changes I was flagged for:
Storms/Valor
BoThunder
PoTorment

I had 3 flags, and i had 100% of my XP going into AA points. I said it 3 times and got 1 full aa point each time for a total of 3 aa points. When I said it again, he did not give me an AA point, he just said 'I've heard that story before' or something like that.

This is an XP GAIN, not a flat out AA point gain. If you have 100% XP going into AA, then you will gain exactly *1* aa point. If you have all/some XP going into regular leveling, it will split appropriately. Your mileage will vary.

Also, apparently, each time you get flagged for a new zone, you can Hail this guy and say the keyword and get another AA point worth of XP. This is *UNCONFIRMED*! (I may be wrong)

Now, the Charm...

This charm ramps up depending on how many PoP zones you are flagged for.

If you have no flags, the charm will have no stats.
