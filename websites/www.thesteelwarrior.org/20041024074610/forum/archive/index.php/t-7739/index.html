You sir, have the right idea, but the wrong way to go about it. Most quests in EQ are utter shit, and not worth your time. You are however correct in assuming that if you're low on cash, you've gotta go get some stuff yourself. So lets see here, Face slot, a couple of very good options are Crystal Spider eyes (8ac, 35hp) that drop in Velketors, and the Enshrouded Veil (7ac 30hp 10 fire) that drops in Howling Stones. If you have a regular group, turn your attention there.

Boots can be replaced with Cobalt (long camp in KC I think), or Champions (Velious quested out of Kael). Either way, you'll be sitting around in one spot for awhile, but like I said, as long as you have a regular group, you'll do fine, and gain XP while you're at it. Alternatively, Kingly Boots of Splendor drop at the table room in droga and are passable.

Legs are gonna be a pain in the ass, not much you can do but deal with it. Champions is an option, but they're rare as hell. Kylong Darkmail is a possibility, but you should probably steer clear of Veksar for a couple more levels.

As for wrists, there is a 15ac 40hp bracer in Velketors..silver chitin. Go get some of those. The options for gauntlets are the aforementioned Champions, or Cobalt out of KC again.

Now, bear in mind that all of this hinges on the fact that you KEEP LEVELING. I'm serious, you need to keep working towards 60, because all of this stuff becomes drastically easier the higher level you get. Also, the one thing that seperates the successful from the unsuccessful in EQ is the quality of their friends. I had a regular group I played EQ with for almost 4 years, and my gear was often times better than people who were in much larger guilds than I was. If you have RL friends that play, get them together, get coordinated and start working on goals. If you don't, then it's time to start making some friends in game. Find people who know what they're doing and develop relationships with them. The difference between being a 3500/1100ac warrior and being a 5500/1300ac warrior is oftentimes all about the people you associate with. You CAN accomplish things in EQ, but you need to realize that you can't do it alone, and you can't do it at 52.
