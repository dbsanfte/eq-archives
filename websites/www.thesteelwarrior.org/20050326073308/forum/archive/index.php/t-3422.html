I solo'd the prophet, hardest part is getting him alone, but once you got him alone he's easy. You'll need some luck too, keeping a camp cleared all by your lonesome self is very hard. I normally check all camps to see if a named is up, whenever I am in the zone, and sometimes you get lucky, a named is up, and no-one is camping him :)
