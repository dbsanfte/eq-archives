and to elaborate on Apolyon's reply (which I agree with)

Eindride Icestorm in water tower drops Great Blade of Storms (easily done with 2 groups if that, and drops ornate breastplate mold)
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=17368

King Dronan in Droga drops Copper Hammer of Striking *tradeable*
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=22010

You could buy or kill for Blade of Carnage *tradeable*
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6447

Earth Seer Gamolk in Droga drops Sword of the Bloodsworn *tradeable*
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=22009

Ungi the Swift in RoCD drops Bygone Butcher's Cleaver *tradeable*
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=30674

Those are all one handers, but I'm sure there are some tradeable two handers you could find that would serve well with an A3 (edge of envelopment comes to mind)

For rings, you might consider dropping the valorium rings for steel rings of earthen resilience
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=18255

for feet there's these two
bloodied boots of dark rites
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=23588
and boots of the storm
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=6582

I'd drop one or both of your wrists in favor of
kizrak's
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=17303
or crystallized acid
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=12394

Granted not many of these will really come cheap, but if you have money to spend they're good picks. Also, get the aggro weapons first! What good is a lot of hp and ac if you cant hold aggro.
