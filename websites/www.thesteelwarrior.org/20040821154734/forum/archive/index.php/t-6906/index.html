It really depends on the direction your guild is going in terms of a main tank.

On one hand a paladin in decent gear/AA can work just fine as the focal in quite a few raid scenerios. On the other hand, sometimes that extra beef along with the versitality of the unlocked discs makes the undoable doable.

From my experience most anything Luclin or lower can be tanked quite well by a paladin of moderate stats. It's once you try to progress in PoP that the gap begins to show, and getting a warrior to that power level will be the easier route.
