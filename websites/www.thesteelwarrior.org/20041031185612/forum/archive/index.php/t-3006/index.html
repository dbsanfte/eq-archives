Your armor looks very nice for your level, I'd focus on better weapons first. The frostbringer is good, however, replace the CSS in your offhand. If you are looking for aggro weapons, I'd reccommend camping or saving for a copper hammer of striking or sword of the bloodsworn. Both drop in the new Droga.

Once you have that, then I'd look for some of these...

Back: helanic tundra cloak, hierophant's cloak, iksar hide cape, cloak of greater pernicity, OR move your haste into the back slot! (cloak of flames, cloak of crystaline waters)
Ear: Bloodmetal earring of engagement, mosquito leg earring
Finger: another valorium ring of gallentry
IF you move what slot your haste is in, you can try to get thurg or skyshrine gaunts, both very decent.
