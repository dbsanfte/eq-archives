Gellio,

The barny will make a decent off hand aggro weapon with it's pique proc. However, the heat blade proc and shock of ice procs are not equal (in this instance), due to how weapon procs work and your weapons:

The weapon's primary proc has a chance to go off. If it doesn't, then the augment proc has a chance to go off, if not then it continues down the list (spell procs and the like work in here). The thing to keep in mind is that aug'ing a weapon which already has a native proc means that the aug proc will not go off as much as the innate or a weapon that only has an aug proc on it. Search the boards here and you'll find folks logging data that shows it (wandor's piece on weapon affinity is a prime example)

Once you have the copper hammer, if you want damage (i.e. not main tank) then use the bladecatcher offhand. If you are main tanking and need to keep aggro, use the barnacle axe offhand.

Either the copper hammer of striking or sword of the bloodsworn is better aggro and should be placed in your primary. If you manage both weapons, go for the copper hammer mainhand and the bloodsworn offhand.

Typically lower dly weapons are better for agro.

Actually, there is a better way to work it out. Quite a bit of testing/parsing was done in this thread:
http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?t=3789&page=3&pp=15

The general concensus of the thread is that:
(DB + 11 * MINIMUM)/delay
Where MINIMUM = Damage/Delay rounded to the next whole integer
Is a good calculation for hate per second generated by a weapon. This of course is pure melee hate and does not cover procs/augs/etc.
Also, you get hate regardless of whether you hit or not. The actual hit amount does not matter as your hate is based on the maximum possible damage you could've done, so even a critical does not generate more hate, as per the thread.

So, working this for each of the three weapons (mainhand):
barnacle axe - 9/21 - (13 + 11 * 1)/21 = 1.14
bladecatcher - 12/24 - (13 + 11 * 1)/24 = 1.00
defenders - 23/34 - (13 + 11 * 2)/34 = 1.03

(offhand)
barnacle axe - 9/21 - (11 * 1)/21 = 0.52
bladecatcher - 12/24 - (11 * 1)/24 = 0.46
defenders - 23/34 - (11 * 2)/34 = 0.65

Keep in mind that damage bonus only applies for the mainhand, so for offhand you have no damage bonus (0). Which means the forumla works out to (11 * min)/delay, so basically the better the ratio, the more hate/second it pans out to.

So, yes, in this instance, the faster weapons wins out, but if you had a wurmslayer (25/40) it works out to 1.15 hate/second.... So the key is to really run the numbers on the formula and see what you get. Of course, never forget innate procs (like I always do) and augments as they'll up that number a bit. Mainhand weapons proc 2 per minute and offhand 1 per minute (roughly, without advanced AAs) so that can help decide the overall winner.

Seeing as the barnacle axe has a 100 hate, that will add 200 hate per minute or another 3.33 hate/second to that weapon. There is also hate associated with the 50dd portion.

Hope that helps
