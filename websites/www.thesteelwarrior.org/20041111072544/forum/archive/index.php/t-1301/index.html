Fitzwillow
Pick up "A Game of Thrones" if you haven't already, George R. R. Martin. It's head and shoulders above any other fantasy series currently going. (EDIT: maybe inappropriate if you're looking for lighthearted... this is serious as a heart attack.)

For a great high fantasy story with sci-fi undertones, try "Lord Valentine's Castle" by Robert Silverberg.

For an absolutely beautifully written series (which these days would be one long novel), try "The Book of the New Sun", published separately as "Shadow of the Torturer", "Claw of the Conciliator", "Sword of the Lictor", and "Citadel of the Autarch" by Gene Wolfe.

And for sheer fun, which sounds like what you're looking for, pick up "Fool on the Hill" by Matt Ruff -- it's an epic fantasy (of sorts) set on the campus of Cornell University.
