well unless someone is snared/rooted/blind I wont count on getting anything else but 1 round every few seconds on him

there are pretty much 3 sceens posible ... Mass PvP, Group PvP, 1vs1 PvP

1vs1 PvP is either Melee or Casters ... if your fighting a melee its usually just plain jousting ... the better gear wins usually (add lag and minor skills) high damage such as plankton is god given there since you it hardly matter if you have a 30 delay or a 15delay (if both have same speed, aka run speed, SoW, SoE, Selos) once a person is faster and has the better ratio he can usually dominate the figth

If you figth a Caster 1vs1 it depend on his strat ... some casters stand there and tank you while they heal and nuke ... good ratio wins there ...
... other casters run around and sometimes stop to nuke/dot/wahteever ... high damage wins there because they will usually start running once your kick interupts them, which means you only get 1 swing at them if they dont run once stuned you should prolly use a good ratio weapon


as for mass and group PvP, it usually comes down if someone is rooted/snared/blind ... if those stick the better ratio weapon is prefered ... (unless its a REAL large figth ... then they migth not be alive for another melee swing ;) ) if they dont stick its pretty much same as for 1vs1 ... if they run go high damage, if they "tank" go ratio

like Aspit said its very situational aand common sense will get you though most figths


as for other "toys" such as ToD and tash sticks ... dont waste your time on those, by the time they proc your a corpse usually

gear advice ? well you played a rogue ... same rules apply to warriors ... get HP ... AC/Resists/Atk after HP (until a certain point, but its gotta be a while before you can say you have "enogth" HP), well make sure your MR is high enogth


PS: @Aspit I dont think every caster can sit there and "tank" you with plaknton (esp. you) ... since there are quite a few casters (lower levels, less geared) that would die in 1 maybe 2 swings :p
