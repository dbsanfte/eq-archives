1. is there a "good" bridge between greatstaff of 4winds and BoW? been looking for something to go between a 41/31 and 48/32 but having problems to fill the gap. anyone got any ideas as to what i may want to go after? i'm currently in a "pre elem" guild and want to see if i want to just burn my ang3 on the 4wind stick? or hold out for a BoW quality weapon before burning my 1150 points.

There really isn't much that you're going to find to be better ratio than the staff unless you're in EPs. Really, you're looking at it the wrong way. You really want an aggro setup, which would be a BoW, SEWS or the like, or a DW setup like a BoC and GBoS or something. The staff is really more of a DPS weapon; the proc isn't much aggro at all.

2. the age old question of 2h vs 1h... would a GSo4W be "better" then tooling around with BoC + club of the mad researcher? I know 1h will give me more aggro but is it really a good trade off considering the riposte i'll be eating?

Ripostes are over rated on most mobs. Sometimes they can cause spikes and kill you. Most of the time they're not significant enough to worry about. EP gods/minis can hit hard enough in a round that an extra hit via riposte could be an issue, otherwise don't really worry about ripostes.

3. would a "fully loaded" club of the mad researcher (17/20 ang3) be main handed or BoC + ang3 be main handed?

BoC mainhand. 2 aggro procs primary > 1 aggro proc primary. DPS isn't overwhelming for aggro.

4. would BoC out aggro a double edged stormblade? or would it be about the same aggro?

BoC wins. Again, DPS just isn't that good of aggro for us. You will never win a DPS war with a rogue or wizard, you HAVE to use something else to get ahead.
