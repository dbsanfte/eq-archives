If you can kill giants with little or no downtime, I'd do that.
But at 52 I couldn't.
LoY mobs are good.
If you have a bazaar mule, you also might try going to East K and killing every lion and spider you see, then transfering LQ, MQ and HQ lion pelts (not the skins) and the spider silks to the mule for sale at market price. Very low risk; about the worst thing that can happen is that some L65 druids will be beating you to the spiders.
Another thing you can try is to go to the bottom of Runnyeye and kill every single goblin you see. They seem to drop on average 1-2 pp and either blackened iron or blackened alloy armor/weapons, good for a couple-three more pp each. Obviously, volume is the key here, but there are a lot of goblins around, and it's quite likely you'll be the only player in the zone. Luckily--and this is the real reason a warrior might consider farming here -- there's also a goblin banker on the lowest floor (which is where you'll be hunting) who will let you bank even after your faction couldn't possibly get worse--just don't sit down near him. So there's no need to run long distances to a bank, etc: just drop the armor you loot in your shared bank slots when you are full, switch to alt to vendor it, then repeat cycle. Bring bandages and food and you're good to go. Lord Picklaw and the named batttlewizards (who you can easily solo) also drop the blackened alloy medalian, which is kind of a decent item for young (<lvl 44) caster alts for you or guildmates. They'd probably sell in the bazaar as well.
