i know how much you dont want to hear the advice of giving up a hoh with its 35 raw ac.

so here is a post from someone who used one from about level 30 until actually i think level 65 but then gave it up for a copper hammer

it was a painful decision giving up all that ac because i used to pride myself on it.but i gave it up for an innate procing weapon which i then anger augged. and i have no regrets even if i cringe to look at my ac and know it could have been so much higher.

but what it comes down to is you cannot fight the battle alone in your group as if your ac all by itself is going to make or break the effectiveness of the group. the better the agro you have the faster your group can kill the mob you are on and the less they will need to heal you. the better your agro the less often mobs will hit others in your group and the less they will need to be healed as well. and injustice as it may seem people are rarely impressed with your mitigation ability near so much as they are impressed with your ability to keep a mob glued to yourself.

Warriors aren't elegant or pretty, we bash stuff and it bashes back. Your job is to hold agro as a primary, secondary to take having agro well, and thirdly to do a little DPS. Get an inate agro proc weapon, it'll help you tons.

Just because your attack raises when when BoC is in primary doesn't mean it raises for both hands, primary hand atk is the only one shown, but both are calculated separately. Just as is range, kick, bash, and slam, you just don't see the numbers.
