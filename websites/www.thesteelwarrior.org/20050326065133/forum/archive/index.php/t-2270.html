very simple, if your healers can't supply the mana for keeping you alive while disc wears off, warn the rotation 30 seconds in advance (do a /disc to check timer), and 10 seconds before your disc drops, make the next tank call ROTATION ON ME or something, then in those 10 secs the secondary tank pounds on the mob before ae taunting, and raid leader calls for everyone to stop attacking. when ST knows he has aggro he calls assist.

if your healers can do a fast rot infinitely, just do it from the start.
