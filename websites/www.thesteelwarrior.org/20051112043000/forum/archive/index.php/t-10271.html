Battleshout isn't really a luxury imo, its pretty much a must have up at all times, its the first thing I spend rage on when charging into battle before switching to defensive(assuming its not up) and if I'm mid battle I never let it drop below 15seconds(GotMoo Buff Mod in Cosmos is a great way to keep track of this without having to hover over your icons). Battleshout means you're hitting more and doing more damage both of which are rage builders, not to mention the bonus in dps increase to any other melee in your group. I've found that if you're in a good group that doesn't have to spend lots and lots of time between fights building mana(meaning your healers/nukers don't blow full manabars on trash) then its very easy to keep Battleshout up by chain charging between groupings of mobs.

As for shield block, there are only two times I use it 1) when I'm between shield bashes and want to force a Revenge to try and stun a caster 2) when I'm up against a boss thats beating the crap out of me, every little bit helps and if I'm getting the crap beat out of me I usually have rage to spare, especially once all my sunders are in and I just have to toss one out every once in awhile to keep it from fading. I really noticed a difference chaining shield block on Eranikus winning with a druid/paladin heal team vs the same group's first attempt which was an utter failure wiping at 50%(of course losing one rogue about 20seconds in, the first time I was slept, probably had alot to do with it too).
