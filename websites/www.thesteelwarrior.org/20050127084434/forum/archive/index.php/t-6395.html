Well first off you might want to get some stamina augs (the dropped ones--don't waste points to buy any) as a temporary fix since your stamina is kind of low. That will give you 6hp per stamina up to 255 stamina and in your case thats a lot of hit points.

I would also suggest you focus on gear with just the most hit points you can get until you get over 6k unbuffed. By then you will probably have some SSRA loot and (possibly VT stuff since your guild is killing the emperor). At that point your AC should be close to 1400 somewhere. When you are in elementals focus on getting AC up as well but for now just work on hp primarily because that will help the most.

You also should work on some of the defensive AA like ND1, 2, and 3 and PE etc. since they will increase your hp and help you tank a lot better.
