wandor
Ornate = biggest waste of plat ever. Period.

If you can farm it yourself, then ornate is not a bad choice since it only cost you 500pp to make. If you are actually paying for the armor, then ornate is the worst choice you can make. Not only is LDoN armor better, but if you pick and choose the items per slot, you can get equivalent/better than ornate with straight bazaar tradeable items. If you REALLY don't want to put the time in to get LDoN Armor, then I would start shopping the bazaar for these items:

Ears: Mosquito Leg Earring, Lizard Bone Earring, Iron Cog Earring, Tear of the Rainmaker, or Bloodmetal Earring of Engagement
Neck: You have a decent neck, get a 10ac aug for it
Face: Amygdalan's Chain Mask, Thulian Visage of Horror
Helm: Helm of Rallos Zek
Rings: Steel Ring of Earthen Resilience
Bracer: Mana-Threaded Bone Bracelet or Crystallized Acid Bracer
Arms: Elbow Spiked Arm Plates or Dried Clay Armguards
Shoulders: Horn Spiked Shoulder Plates or Forged Shoulderpads
BP: even though you have ornate already, get an NRo BP, 69ac 200hp with the right augs
Back: Crinose Cape of the Werewolf, Sol Ro Emblazoned Cloak, Hierophant's Cloak, Iksar Hide Cape
Waist: Heavy Rubicite Girdle
Boots: Boots of the Storm or Bloodied Boots of Dark Rites
Range: Featherwood Compound Bow or Stonewood Compound Bow

Your other slots should do fine with what you have already. You'll notice that I have recommended that you replace your haste item without recommending a new haste item. Just use tribute, you are giving up a lot of AC/HP with less than raid acquired 41% haste.
