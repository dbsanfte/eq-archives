Superchum
I MQ'd gnollslayer between accounts once.

Spiroc Blade, while riskier shouldn't be any different.

Just be careful. Get both your toons there near the NPC's before doing anything. Make sure the coast is clear (Freeport's not exactly the newbie hub it used to be, you should be fine, especially if you wait till either really late at night or get up really early in the morn, when populations are low).

Have a friend come along and stand near the NPC to stand guard. That way if someone does walk up in between logging out and logging back in, your friend can at least TRY to dissuade the person from doing anything until you get back.

Other than that, good luck.
