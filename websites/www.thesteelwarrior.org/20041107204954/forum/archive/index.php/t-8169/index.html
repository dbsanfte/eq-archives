WARRIOR ALTERNATIVE ADVANCEMENT

**********

General AAs

**********

Name: First Aid
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the amount healable for a player using the bind wound skill.
This skill grants a 10% increase in the amount healable for the first point, 20% for the second, and 30% for the third.

----------

Name: Improved Lung Capacity
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the duration a player can hold their breath.
It improves capacity by 10% for the first point, 25% for the second, and 50% for the third.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Agility
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base agility by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the stat cap.
Agility is important for increasing your chance to avoid being hit in melee combat. It does not increase your damage mitigation.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Charisma
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base charisma by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the stat cap.
Charisma is important for merchant prices (although it is far easier to get items for this than points).

----------

Name: Improved Natural Cold Resistance
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base Cold Resistance by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Cold Resistance is important for increaseing your chances of avoiding taking maximum damage off cold based attacks.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Dexterity
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base dexterity by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the stat cap.
Dexterity is important for increasing your chance to proc with a melee item, or to use passive abilities such as Critical hits.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Disease Resistance
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base Disease Resistance by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Disease Resistance is important for increasing your chances of not being affected by disease based spells.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Fire Resistance
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base Fire Resistance by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Fire Resistance is important for increaseing your chances of avoiding taking maximum damage off fire based attacks.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Intelligence
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base intelligence by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the stat cap.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Magic Resistance
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base Magic Resistance by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Magic Resistance is important for increasing your chances of not being affected by magic based spells.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Metabolism
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability reduces the rate at which food and water is consumed by a player.
1 point reduces hunger and thirst by 10%, 2 points by 25% and 3 points by 50%.

---------

Name: Improved Natural Poison Resistance
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base Poison Resistance by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Poison Resistance is important for increasing your chances of not being affected by poison based spells.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Regeneration
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This is a very useful ability for any player. It increases the speed at which they recover hitpoints.
Each point increases the base heal rate by 1 per tick. I.e. 1 point is 1 hitpoint increase per tick, 3 points is a 3 hitpoint increase per tick.
This regeneration stacks with all other forms of hitpoint regeneration in the game, including base regeneration bonuses which Trolls and Iksar get.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Run Speed
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases base movement rate for a player.
It does not stack with any other speed enhancement such as Spirit of Wolf, Journeyman boots or a horse.
This is primarily useful indoors in areas where these buffs cannot be used.
Each point gives around a 10% boost. A player with 3 points runs just slightly slower than a person using Journeyman boots.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Stamina
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base stamina by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the stat cap.
Stamina is important for increasing the maximum number of hitpoints a character can obtain. At high levels this can amount to a significant amount.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Strength
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base strength by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the stat cap.
Strength impacts the carrying capacity of a character, and is one of the primary stats which affects maximum damage.

----------

Name: Improved Natural Wisdom
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 5 Levels
Point Cost: 1
Description: This ability increases the base wisdom by 2 for each point invested (maximum of 10).
Putting points into this raises the characters base, but does not allow them to exceed the stat cap.

----------

Name: Tanaan Mastery
Level Requirement: 51
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 6 Levels
Point Cost: 3 per Level
Description: Allows players to train an additional tradeskill past the 200 cap per level.

Archtype AAs

**********

Name: Combat Agility
Level Requirement: 55
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 2 points
Level 2: 4 points
Level 3: 6 points
Description: This ability increases the players ability to avoid being hit altogether in combat.

----------

Name: Combat Fury
Level Requirement: 55
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 2 points
Level 2: 4 points
Level 3: 6 points
Description: It increases the chance to land a critical strike.
The first point adds a 2% chance, the second increases that to a 4% chance and the third raises it to a final 5-6% chance.

----------

Name: Combat Stability
Level Requirement: 55
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 2 points
Level 2: 4 points
Level 3: 6 points
Description: This reduces damage taken from every blow which lands on the player.

----------

Name: Fear Resistance
Level Requirement: 55
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 2 points
Level 2: 4 points
Level 3: 6 points
Description: This ability increases the players chance to resist being feared, including Dragon Fear.
This ability also increases the chance to break out of a fear sooner.
1 point in this ability increases resistance by 5%, 2 points by 10% and 3 points by 20%.

----------

Name: Finishing Blow
Level Requirement: 55
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 2 points
Level 2: 4 points
Level 3: 6 points
Description: This ability adds a chance to kill a fleeing creature with a single blow. The creature must be under 10% of their total hitpoints and fleeing (non-fleeing monsters are not affected by this ability.)
The first point adds a 1% chance, the second ups this to a 2% chance, and the final point caps it at a 5% chance.

---------

Name: Improved Natural Durability
Level Requirement: 55
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 2 points
Level 2: 4 points
Level 3: 6 points
Description: This spell increases the hitpoints of a player by a base percentage.
The first point increases their hitpoints by 2%, the second by 5%, and the third by 10%.
This impacts the players base hitpoints (determined by class, level and stamina).

----------

Name: Natural Healing
Level Requirement: 55
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 2 points
Level 2: 4 points
Level 3: 6 points
Description: This is a very useful ability for any player. It increases the speed at which they recover hitpoints.
Each point increases the base heal rate by 1 per tick. I.e. 1 point is 1 hitpoint increase per tick, 3 points is a 3 hitpoint increase per tick.
This regeneration stacks with all other forms of hitpoint regeneration in the game, including base regeneration bonuses which Trolls and Iksar get.
This ability stacks with the Improved Natural Regneration from the General Abilities.

Class AAs

**********

Name: Ambidexterity
Level Requirement: 59
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 1 Levels
Point Cost: 9
Description: This ability increases the chance for the Warrior to successfully attack with both equipped weapons during a round of combat.

----------

Name: Area Taunt
Level Requirement: 59
Ability Type: Activated
Re-Use Time: 15 minutes
Max Training: 1 Levels
Point Cost: 5
Description: This ability moves the warrior to the top of the hate list of all monsters in the area.
This is useful even on single enemies when a defensive tank needs to immediately acquire aggro.
It can be used every 15 minutes.

----------

Name: Bandage Wound
Pre-Requisite(s) First Aid
Level Requirement: 59
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 3 points
Level 2: 6 points
Level 3: 9 points
Description: This ability allows the warrior to use their Bind Wounds skill to greater effect.
The first lets the amount healed be increased by 10%, the second by 25% and the third by 50%.
There is a pre-requisite of 3 points in the First Aid skill.

----------

Name: Double Riposte
Level Requirement: 59
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 3 points
Level 2: 6 points
Level 3: 9 points
Description: This ability gives the Warrior a chance for a double riposte any time he succesfully ripostes. It does not increase the base chance to riposte however.
The chance to double riposte is 15%, 30% and 50% respsectively for the number of points invested.

----------

Name: Flurry
Pre-Requisite(s) Combat Fury
Level Requirement: 59
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 3 points
Level 2: 6 points
Level 3: 9 points
Description: This ability allows the Warrior a chance to Flurry with their primary attack weapon.
This allows them to hit for an extra two attacks per round if it activates.
There is a pre-requisite of 3 points in the melee skill Combat Fury.
The chance to Flurry is 5%, 10% and 20% respectively

----------

Name: Physical Enhancement
Level Requirement: 59
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 1 Levels
Point Cost: 5
Description: This ability adds an additional 2% to the Natural Durability, Combat Agility and Combat Stability archetypes.
There is no pre-requisite that these archetypes be trained in, however the greatest effectiveness is achieved from doing so. It is believed that you need at least 1 point in an archetype to get the 2% increase in it (i.e. you would get no benefit in Natural Durability if you only had points in Combat Agility and Stability).

-----------

Name: Rampage
Level Requirement: 59
Ability Type: Activated
Re-Use Time: 36 minutes
Max Training: 1 Levels
Point Cost: 5
Description: This ability allows the Warrior to attack all targets in range once every 36 minutes.
While not a very useful ability, it does at least look cool.

---------

Name: Warcry
Pre-Requisite(s) Fear Resistance
Level Requirement: 59
Ability Type: Activated
Re-Use Time: 1 hour 12 minutes
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 3 points
Level 2: 6 points
Level 3: 9 points
Description: This ability allows the Warrior to make their entire group immune to Fear.
Each skill level increases the duration of the immunity.
