My suggestions:

legs: Bloodruned greaves (39ac, -5agi, 25hp), or some flavor of Dragonhide breeches
neck: Glimmering Mithril Torque (15ac, 2str) or Clattering Bone Necklace (6ac, 6str/sta, 30hp)
fingers: upgrade to velium fire opal, or valorium rings of gallentry, if you feel really spendy
face: Crystal Spider Eyes (8ac, 35hp/mana, 3int/wis) or Gantru War Mask (11ac, 5sta and other stats)
head: Raldukan Helm (drops in UP)

If the alt has the faction, also consider MQ'ing some Thurg armor.
