Friedrick
You should keep both weapon skills up to snuff. I personally have found that many warriors overlook 2hb because it is slow to train usually (lack of fast training weapons that are worth a crap) and it can lead to some great deals for us warriors who are on a budget. For instance, there are 2 handers that drop from CT, one is 2hs, one 2hb. Both are 33/36 but the 2hb is consistently cheaper on Tunare than the 2hs. Both are nice weapons for warriors and net the same DPS.

And I can think of 3 that I would use atm, one of which I do and that is the ToD. When a slower dies on a raid or I am not the MT in an exp group, I use it to slow with. The GSoT and GSoFW are two more awesome 2hb's.

For tradeable weapons there are 4 that I know of that I would consider for a warrior looking for DPS and utility.

2hb:
ToD Utility weapon 33/44 proc's 50% slow DPS is so-so
GSoT 31/30 good DPS and good aggro from the stun proc

2hs:
WMBS 31/28 better DPS than GSoT but less aggro due to no proc
WB 50/44 same as above

Although there are more 2hs weapons available with ratios around 1:1 I think the 2hb atm is the way to go. You can lose a little DPS but have better aggro and utility.

Oh, and I think you are confusing Lore items and No Drop items, you can give Lore items to a twink but not No Drop items.
