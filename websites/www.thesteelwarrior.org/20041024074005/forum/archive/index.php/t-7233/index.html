The AC camp is tedious, but jboots are very nice, especially if you can't afford a horse or are too low to get Run3.
Some people like the OT gate hammer, though I'm a Tribunal Barb and the faction hassle has never seemed worth it to me.
Oddly, the quests that have paid off most handsomely for me have been other people's quests. The cleric epic is pretty nice for a lot of clerics, and some parts of it, such as the skyfire shards, have mindnumbingly low drop rates. By mindnumbing, I mean you can send hours hunting and not get any of the drops (yep, more than one of them) you need. I've helped a number of clerics with this and every single one of them has come close to having a nervous breakdown. You help a cleric through that, and it's like having free rez and and buffs for life.

Add in the Enduring Breath neck item from Katta that you get for 3 Shissar Organs. In Echo Caverns there's a lonely shissar (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=7129) who is warrior-soloable. Enjoy!
