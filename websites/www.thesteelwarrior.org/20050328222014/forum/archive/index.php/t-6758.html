There are 6 rooms, with 2 clicky per room.
To every question, there is an answer from 1 to 12 (numerical value).

And it moves in a clockwise fashion. The NE building is 1/2. You can click on any pot to start the event and you can have 1 wrong answer. The 2nd incorrect one will bring the golems. There's also a wait period in between clicks, roughly 30-45 seconds.
