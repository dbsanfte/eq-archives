I'm posting this from EQLive for those that may be unaware that SoE has determined how they will address issues in GoD. Please forgive me for posting it again, but it is relevent information about a topic that has generated a lot of posting activity on TSW.

Brenlo stated:
Conor is correct, much of what is asked for by Paladins and SKs is specific to warriors and that will not be changing. Like it or not, warriors are supposed to be and will always be better able to tank than Pallys and SKs. The fact that warriors can tank the harder content in Gates, while the knight classes cannot, does not mean Pallys and SKs are broken, rather that warriors are better tanks and that the content is too challenging. There is supposed to be a disparity between the tanking abilities of warriors and knights. Paladins and SKs want to be able to tank in the single group encounters, and they are right, they should be able to, but not at the expense of other classes.

So now a plan is in place to adjust the content so it will still be tough for warriors, while also being achievable for the knight classes. Currently this is our solution. We will not be adding defensive discs or abilities to the knight classes. We will be tuning the content so that it is a challenge for everyone and excludes no one. We want a group to be able to succeed with a warrior, shadow knight or paladin as a tank.

We do realize that you have other concerns as well, and the team is looking into them. I hope to have some news for you soon.

later, he further clarifies by posting:
We do view you as a viable tank class, just not as good as warriors. That is why we are adjusting the content, so you CAN tank. If we did not want you to tank, why would be adjusting the content to allow you to do so?

I believe that even warriors think the current content is too difficult. It was not intended that only the best tanks of the three tanking classes could do it, and even then, only with the assistance of multiple healers. You should not have to have 2 clerics and a warrior to complete any of the trials. But the problem extends beyond the trials. Warriors should not be the only viable tank in Gates and that is what we are trying to fix through the content because it is a content problem.

Now as to all of the other concerns I have seen tossed about, as I have said, the team is considering them. Things that are broken or don't make sense will still be fixed. All I am attempting to clarify for Knight classes, is that we will not make changes at the expense of warriors and their unique abilities. So keep the suggestions and comments coming and I will continue to pass them along and let's see if we can get through this together.
