I have this in my mind for a little while so I don't remember if it's actually my idea or if it has been brought up allready but anyway:

Why not give warriors a skill that makes their weapon proc? You click the hotkey -> the weapon procs.

Of course there should be a reuse timer, it should be decided if it makes both weapons proc if DW'ing, etc. But before fine tuning, let's just take a step back and look at the possibilities:

- A better control of agro (not dependent of the randomness of procs or an unreliable taunt skill)

- Utility (there are many procs with debuffs)

- DPS (there is a numbrer of good damaging procs so depending on the reuse that could be substancial dps)

- Tactic (with the number of odd procs around, it gives a little more room for creativity when it comes to fighting tactics)

I like the versatility and the simplicity of this solution and I guess it is pretty easy to implement in the game. Obviously, though, it is very equipement dependent but warriors have, after all, been equipement dependent since day one.

I would even find it appropriate to give a similar skill to all PURE melee, the ones suposed to be the weapon experts you know. Make the reuse for warrior significantly lower maybe...

Certainly not the end-all do-all of the agro and melee problems, but a step in the right direction I guess.

What do you think?

PS: I am not familiar with the board and have been oriented here so I am not familar with the many ideas that have been tossed around on these very board. My appologies if that idea allready got debated and burried for some reason :P
