Parsing has been done about hate generation.

Phantron
05-08-2004, 04:08 AM
There's a certain amount of aggro you must have over everyone else before you can move a mob to you and it varies from mob to mob. Incite by itself is never enough to move a mob to you as long as anyone else has aggro.

So let's say you incite a mob for 400 hate, someone does 1 hate (say, boggle), but he is closer to you than the mob. The mob checks its aggro list, you have 400 hate, but the person with 1 hate is closer and 400 isn't enough to move it, so it stays instead to beat the person with 1 aggro.

I'd estimate the amount of aggro you need to move a mob is around ~700 (an EB can usually move a mob).
