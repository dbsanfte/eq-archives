Greetings Warriors

I'm a Warrior that will often swap out weapons during fights, especially when I am soloing, even more so when I play an alt, and swap out swarmcaller for a slow when bow kiting gets boring(on my Ranger alt).

As of late I've spoken to several people, of different classes, that state that they do not like swapping weapons, because they say it is a pain to open up their inventory, a bag, and then proceed to swap things around.

I thought perhaps some do not realize the much easier way to swap out weapons and such, so I thought perhaps I would share it.

Turn to an empty page of hotbuttons. I have many of my normal hotbuttons set up on Page one, and then a pulling/tanking set up on Page 2.

So I turned to page 10, which was one click backwards, and I set up my swapping system.

when you have those 10 hotbuttons that are all empty on page 10(or whatever #.) open up your inventory, and then left click and hold on your primary, secondary, and range slots. Click until they pop up on your cursor, then place them into the hot buttons, generaly Primary/Secondary on top, and the range/ammo below it.

Then, generally I only use 4 bags and a quiver, leaving me 3 empty inventory spaces. I then put those 3 empty inventory slots into the hotbuttons, and when soloing, or needing to swap weapons, I keep my Swarmcaller or extra bow, or whichever I may need, in those inventory slots.

Then it is only a matter of clicking one direction to a new set of hotkeys, clicking on the weapon I want, and then sliding it into the spots. I don't take up any space or loose site of my target with the bulky inventory screen, I don't have to open up several bags, etc.

To me this has worked very nicely for quite some time now.

I hope it helps some one.
