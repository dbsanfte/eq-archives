There IS a mitigation cap: http://www.worldofwar.net/guides/damagereduction.php

Mitigation caps are harder to reach than they seem. Not only because of the insanely diminishing returns (making armor altogether less valuable at some point like Grey stated) but because most of the time you're going to be tanking a level 62 mob (most bosses are 62, even if they don't state it (bosses after you hit 60 that is)). Your mitigation drops down two tiers against mobs 2 levels higher, so the scale changes, and the cap becomes even harder to reach.

It'll probably be a long while before anyone hits the cap, even if it's possible now its just not worth it. after you have ~60% mitigation, aiming for avoidance becomes absolutely hands down more efficient, as you'll avoid the diminishing returns.
