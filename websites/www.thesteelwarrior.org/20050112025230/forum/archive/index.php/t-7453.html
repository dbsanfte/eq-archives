Wurmy's are great from 20 to 40ish, at which point you can go about looking for aggro proc weapons. Up to 50+ just keep a good ratio weapon in offhand, ie Jagged Blade of Mourning, Centi LS, etc, or something with a aggro/debuff/stun proc (barny axe is decent)
I would steer clear of a lammy period; I've never really had great results from one. True, they're fast, but from 20-40+ a wurmy kills them in damage, and a better ratio weapon that's slightly slower, like a 12/23 or 13/25 is better than a lammy as well.

Also, if all you care about is keeping aggro, you might want to carry around a gnoll hide lariat or two. 3/20 is sucks damage, but they are aggro gods pre-50. Cheap too.

And PS: If you do use a lammy, DO NOT put it in offhand. 9/19 is terrible for offhand, anything with a better ratio is preferable. Either go for slower, higher damage in primary, like a wurmy, or a faster 1hander in primary, like a lammy. Trying for the best of both worlds doesn't work, since the wurmy is primary only
