Ikaav's mitigation comes from a spell (Chaos Guard). They don't have any mitigation to the basic 3 weapon types.

Discordling take 110% damage from blunt. That's about the only weapon weakness I've noticed. Pretty sure Ukuns are not weak against blunt in OoW but if so, it'd be 110% like they're in GoD. It's worth noting that mobs of the same model do not necessarily have the same weaknesses, at least not across zones.

In trial of weaponry you'd see a lot of things like:

You score a crit (500)
You slash XXX for 2000.

The slash XXX for XYZ is the actual damage you done applying resistance/weakness. The damage from crit is what you should have done.
