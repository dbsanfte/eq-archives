I have about 500 megs of parses with it... it's the best weapon you will ever get your hands on, hands down.

Primary hand DPS at 1630 ATK with haste is about 90-95 on PoP mobs. Add about 10-15 dps from procs. It's on par with t he DBoTW in the main hand (in terms of DPS) and much easier to get.

Pair this weapon up with the DBoTW and your DPS will range from 170-200+ dependant on the mob and your ATK levels. It's not unusual for me to parse 200/220+ DPS in certain trials or just grinding in certain zones.

Yes, it beats the ass out of the BoW/SEWS for DPS if you pair it properly.
