Since no one actually answered you...

AoW style pinning is when the MT backs into a corner(maximum shrunk preferably) and has the raid push the mob onto them. The walls will stop it from being pushed overt he warrior and keep it firmly into a single position, and right on the warrior so range is easy to maintain. Mob doesn't move at all. A 90 degree corner is best if possible.
