Fitemore
Lol, sell that horse back ASAP. There's a turn in quest for it so you can get back some of your money. Get a different primary weapon, that one sucks really bad until you get up higher, then it'll only just suck. Get a Sword of the Bloodsworn and a Frostbringer for levels 40 until 50, when you should get a Copper Hammer of Striking to replace the Frostbringer. For now, just look in the bazaar for a weapon with a better ratio than that Defenders Lightblade. 13/34 is just bad; the Centi shortsword would be alot better primary.

For your armor: 6hp = 1ac = 2 sta for now, 6hp = 1ac = 1sta for later. Don't worry too much about str, dex, agi, wis, int or resists. Just search the bazaar for upgrades, keeping in mind recommended levels. 6ac 65hp rings are really good for cheap with no recommended level, for example. Just budget yourself and upgrade everything you can.

You don't have haste, get a haste item. There's nothing that'll improve your damage and aggro more than haste.
