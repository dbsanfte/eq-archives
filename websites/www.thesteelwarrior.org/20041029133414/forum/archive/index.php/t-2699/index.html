dethvegi1
The way we do the Terris Thule. I usually pull her to just a tad right of where she is. We have our clerics / priest classes setup to the left where butterflies spawn. Usually the butterflies will head straight towards the clerics anyway. We have 2 enc's setup , along with any AE'er we have at the time. Enc's stun them. Wizzies / Druids burn them down. During this time we offtank Terris, and put all melee on butterflies until they are dead. Once they are dead, MT calls engage again. Dealing with the dispell is pretty easy. Now with the garg's. We select 4 designated kiters before hand and make them stand at their assigned post until the gargs become targetable. Once they are targettable, they kite the gargs outside around her throne. They wll not aggro from low hp of anyone out there. From this point just kill TT herself. Then have an sk or ranger tag a garg off a kiter. Kill it, Rinse and repeat until the other 3 gargs are down and distribute loots.

The first few times we did her, there wasnt' a sound strat that we had yet. We've killed a few times using this strat and works pretty damn well. We had less then 40 our last kill of her. I was the only to die. Its not a fight you want to rush through. Take a bit of time with her. HP is pretty low and her melee isn't all that bad. Once you learn the encounter it will be a cake walk for you to knock her off.
