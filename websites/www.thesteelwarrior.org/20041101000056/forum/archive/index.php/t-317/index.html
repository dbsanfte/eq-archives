Dandolo1
THO is a typical SoL fight... Long.

We have dropped him twice now, first with 37ppl and 2nd with 46ppl, and both times our MT's defensive disc has recycled.

The slow AoE is a pain, but is easily cured with RC (most of our casters are RC1 or RC2). I'm pretty sure it's magic based, so bring your bards and that will help some. With 417svM I was resisting about 2/3rds of the slows.

The fizzle AoE can be avoided by casters quite easily. The range is pretty large but THO is perma rooted beside a huge obstruction, so any/all shrunk casters can hide from the effects.

Clearing there is a joke for 20 min, then semi tricky on the mobs just outside his room. We had four warriors engaged at one time, so make sure you've got enough tanks (or better pullers) to go around.

But as I said before, this is a 15-20min fight. The mob is slowable and debuffable, so cleric mana isn't really an issue.

Hope that helps.
