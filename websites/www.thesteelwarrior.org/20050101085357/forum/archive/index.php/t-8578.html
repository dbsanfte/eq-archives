First of all, I can explain your "Endurance penalty" and it wasn't introduced by the Devs. I'm willing to bet that your base stats are far lower when naked than with gear on, and since your endurance pool is based upon your total of your combat stats, naturally you'll have less once you put on your gear.

Second of all, some people are WAAAAAYY too goddamned incite happy. I understand that some of you have only been playing warriors with the new endurance system, or have been brainwashed into thinking that incite is the only way you can hold aggro, but you DO NOT NEED it to hold aggro. You don't need to incite every damned pull, warriors didn't lose aggro EVERY pull before. You only need to incite when Taunt fails and you're not proccing enough at the moment to hold aggro. Go look thru a log sometime, and I'm willing to bet that a good chunk of your incites are basically wasted because an aggro proc comes between the time you incite and the time when anyone could have possibly taken aggro from you.

You're right, endurance is not mana, but it's your endurance, and if you want to use it, you need to learn how to use it efficiently, or you're going to end up with an empty bar and be so crippled by the thought of not being able to do anything that you think you're supposed to be able to do that you're going to suck as a result.
