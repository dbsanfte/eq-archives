Ok, so everyone knows that idle time is the devil's playground ;)

I found myself facing 2 hours of time in which I had nothing to do that would have any actual impact on my life/future, so I had to find SOMETHING to do! So, seeing the thread of "discontent" (not general, but it was there) over the warrior epic 1.5's right click + focus, I sat down with my sketchbook, and started to draw out another, alternate if you will, epic to the first one. I'm in the process right now of writing a new story behind it (since frankly, the one for the 1.5 as it stands is kind of...weak as in: oh I had another vision, go and beat this guy up plz kk tks, oh wait time for some variety! go pick this random piece up off the ground!) As of right now, I just have the start of the quest, and the clickable effects + focii (for both 1.5 and 2.0)

If there's enough interest, I'll try to finish some of the pencil sketches I have been working on. Also, input on any of this would be much appreciated, as well as constructive criticism. The 2.0 effects and stats are not set in stone yet (neither are the 1.5's for the matter, but they have a comparison to go against) Also, some of the stats have been omitted (weight, rec/req levels) since they're not really the point

Ruined City of Dranik - Tiorpat Tornwing

You say "Hail, Tiorpat Tornwing"

Tiorpat Tornwing looks at you out of haunted eyes. Gesturing at the ground around him, he replies in a quiet, rumbling voice "Have you come to witness my folly human? Leave me be. You cannot (understand my pain)."

You say "How come I cannot understand your pain"

Tiorpat Tornwing glares at you. "Insolent -race-! You did not watch (Discord) twist your world into a black, foul world that killed its people at the whim of one!"

Tiorpat laughs derisively. "You must be young to not know what Discord is. Discord is the antithesis to everything any thinking, logical being strives for. Discord is chaos, anarchy, (destruction)"

You say "What destruction?"

Tiorpat's sight cloud over, and he screams in terror. "By the gods, I cannot bear to speak of it again, it (pains me) too much, even just thinking of it."

You say "How does it pain you"

Tiorpat lifts one of his tattered wings and shows it to you. "Look at my wings, they are not exactly functional as you can see. The wounds the incarnation of Discord itself, (Mata Muram), have left their mark on me. Speaking of them makes them flare to life anew. Pain that you cannot imagine."

You say "Who is Mata Muram?"

Tiorpat stares at you in disbelief. "Tell me you have not heard of Mata Muram, the great despoiler? If you do not know his name, then you are naive and unknowledgable indeed. Mata Muram is the reason my world is in the state it is in now. He is the reason Taelosia lies in shambles. His armies are feared as being relentless, ruthless and willing to go to any length...aaaaahhhh!!" Tiorpat's words are cut off as a spasm of pain racks his body.

Tiorpat fixes you with a plaintive look, barely in control of the pain flashing through his body. "Help me...(potion)..."

You say "Potion?"

Tiorpat nods slightly. "Find...Aauuughhh! Muramite...grand...healer...labyrinth..."

You say "What type of wounds?"

Tiorpat sighs. "Have you forgotten what I had said before? No matter. The wounds inflicted by a weapon of Discord never fully heal, and you carry them forever, scarring your body and your soul. Mata Muram (did this) to me."
