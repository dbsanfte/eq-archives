These are my proposals on how Bliz should start to fix many of the Warrior issues. Mainly, lack of exceptional weapon damage, Tactical Mastery, and stances in general. These are modest changes in my opinion. Your input is appreciated.

>>General<<

Enable weapon set cycling via hotkey and tabs in inventory. Up to 3 sets. (An excedingly useful ability available in Diablo 2)

>>Stances<<

Defensive: You absorb 20% of any damage taken, but deal 15% less damage while in stance.

Berserker: Your attack speed is increased by 20%, but you take 15% more damage while in stance.

>>Skills<<

Tactical Mastery: Requires level 25
You retain up to 35 rage points when you change stances.

Tactical Supremacy: Requires level 35
You gain the ability to use all skills in Battle Stance.

Battle Shout: Increase duration to at least 5min.

>>Talents<<

Tactical Mastery (Arms): REMOVED

Precision (Arms): Requires 5 points in Deflection.
Increases your chance to strike your target by 1, 2, 3, 4, 6%.

Anger Management (Arms): Requires 10 points in Arms Talents.
Decreases rage decay by 50% while out of combat.

Impale (Arms): Requires 3 points in Deep Wounds.
Critical Strike damage done by abilities increased by 20, 40%.

Two-Hand Spec (Arms): Requires 15 points in Arms Talents.
Increases damage done with Two-Hand weapons by 2, 4, 6, 8, 10%.

Cruelty (Fury):
Chance to score a critical hit with melee weapons increased by 2, 4, 6, 8, 10%.

Shield Spec (Prot):
Chance to block an attack with a shield increased by 2, 4, 6, 8, 10%.

Anticipation (Prot):
Increases your defense skill by 3, 6, 9, 12, 15.

Iron Will (Prot): Requires 5 points in Protection Talents.
Increases your chance to resist stun, charm, FEAR effects by 4, 8, 12, 16, 20%.

One-Hand Spec (Prot): Requires 10 points in Protection Talents.
Increases your damage with one-hand weapons by 2, 4, 6, 8, 10%.

Defiance (Prot): Requires 25 points in Protection Talents.
Increases threat generated by attacks in defensive stance by 4, 8, 12, 16, 20%.
