Tough event and kinda frustrating, but for a VT level guild, it shouldn't be too bad. Everything is undead, so Necromancers can assist in mezzing. Having a solid Enchanter/Necro/Bard lockdown crew is essential for this unless you plan on offtanking 10+ mobs every time. It's usually a good idea to have a monk or "Kamikaze" to run in and break up the spawns in the rooms, as they all spawn on top of each other. None of the kings are that hard, but the later ones will lifetap for 5k, and that can get very nasty.

Bertox is another matter all by himself. He doesn't have that many hp. In fact, he doesn't have that many more than the kings, but his melee dps is rather insane. He quads for 1900 (1100's in Defensive). The entire key of this fight is keeping your Rampage tank alive. Bertox ramps a lot and it's like keeping two MT's alive the entire fight. If you don't pay attention to rampage, he'll burn through your entire raid on that premise alone.

Other than that, it's dps dps dps. It's roughly a two hour event with a lot of trash killing, so good luck.
