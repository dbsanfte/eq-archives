Get the BL to 62 to get a good pet, then hit up Maidens Eye, Griegs End, PoNightmare, PoInnovation, PoJustice. My wife is a BL and Im a warrior we can duo in PoStorms/Valor easy. Just remember to be hasted and regen and Spiritual Dominion. Have Infusion of Spirit, dex, str and Talisman and your good to go. Pull with slow and taunt the mob off the BL. Its very easy really. In the Planes, I switch to 2HS because riposte from the <20 delay weapons makes me take a lot more damage.

One other thing, cast the pet proc that doesnt cause a knockback. it helps when 2 boxing.

12-06-2004, 12:47 PM
I agree on Grieg's End as well, I'm a 69 beastlord and I can mow through mobs there like a chainsaw with only regen and paragon to heal, an equally geared warrior should make killing these guys trivial even if you use the beast only to slow and buff and hotkey an /assist, /pet attack on the beast while he is on autofollow. At 69, the entrance mobs at both DSP and SD zones are light blue, but the inner mobs are DB all the way to 70. Solo I can do an AA here in about an hour and 15 to an hour and 30 minutes. Even duo AA exp moves decently here (sometimes I'll pick up an LFG lowbie and have them follow me around). There are also lots and lots of random named mobs throughout the zone and some extremely nice tribute loots. Most of the items don't sell for much in the bazaar, but the tribute value is quite nice. No need to worry about snare here because nothing runs and if you wander around, you'll never ever have to worry about running out of mobs. I often go here if I only have a short time on, because in the 30 minutes I'd spend LFG, 10 min to run to a zone where the group is, 10 more minutes to buff, and 15 more minutes to start really pulling I could have made nearly an AA from non-stop pulls in GE.
