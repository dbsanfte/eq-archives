There's only one part to this quest. Turn in the jade for the hammer. It does require amiable faction though. How you get the amiable faction doesn't matter.

Puppet strings do still drop. They are not rechargable, but that is the rule, not the exception, for charged clicks (unless tinkered). As I said, do all you can to make sure it is fully mr debuffed.

As far as getting faction, it's not worth it. Takes a ton of braids, they give very very little faction. With bottomed out faction, the only aggro I ever get is the skelly you pop next to. If you swim far out in the water you can avoid all other aggro. Or you can just run out the near gate, and train anything that is on you.
