Basically it's 17 raw ac and 3fr/cr/mr versus 55 raw hp and 10dr/pr, and venge2 (for what it's worth). Most people use a 6:1 hp to ac ratio when comparing gear; meaning the Myrm bracer is better in that respect. The gore bracer has 11 more saves and 10 more atk though, as well as some stam.

The deciding factor I think is... are the turn-in pieces for the gore bracer nodrop? If they are, turn them in, and keep them both on you... the myrm for when you tank, the gore for when you need saves and more hp (AEing mobs basically). If you can MQ the bracer of gore, for a decent amount of cash, I would do that, and spend the money on other slots where you'll get a bigger return for your cash.
