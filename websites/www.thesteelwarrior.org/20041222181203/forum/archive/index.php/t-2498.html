Small question : Whats the actual effect of shield ?
Max hit reduction ?
Min hit reduction ?
Min/Max hit reduction ?
Actual hit reduction ?




Shielding is a straight % decrease in damage bonus of a mob.

For instance, Fennin Ro's Damage bonus is 1000.

With Shielding 2, then 2% of 1000 or 20 would be taken off every hit. So every hit by Fennin would be 20 less than it normally would.
