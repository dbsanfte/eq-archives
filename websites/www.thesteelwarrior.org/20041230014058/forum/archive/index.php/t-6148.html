A critical hit is about 70% more damage. A Crippling blow does about 150% more damage. Fellstrike doubles damage and caps the minimum damage to weapon X 4 + damage bonus. For example the lowest you'll hit with Vanazir is around 280 regardless of how low your hit was.

Fellstrike beats Mightystrike unless you're at berserk. It also beats Precision/Aggrssive since you don't hit 50% more often or do 50% more damage per hit with either disc and the duration is only twice as long. Fellstrike is especially good against high AC targets since it raises your minimum damage as well.
