Charm of the Brotherhood (http://lucy.fnord.net/item.html?id=61002) is fairly decent. Go to Dulak's Harbor and collect a Silver, Bronze, Gold, and Platinum Medallion of Service. Take all 4 to Firon, an NPC on the roof of the Neriak Warrior's Guild. If you are a good race(or just have really bad faction with dark elves) bring a few invis potions or have someone come with you to re/cast Invis on you. There is a guard who stands on the far side of the roof, but he won't bother you if you keep Firon between you and the guard.
The medallions drop from pretty much any mob in the zone. My main got his doing the treasure sorters and scouts just past the zone line. My lil Froggy dinged 40 last night out in DL and I'm already sick to death of it. I'm headed down to Dulak tonight with him to see if I can get this charm for him. Its nothing great, but 5 extra hp for having a second warrior in the group never hurt anybody:D
