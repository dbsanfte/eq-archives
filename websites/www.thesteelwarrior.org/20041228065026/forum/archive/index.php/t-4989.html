Warriors Disc
Fortitude = no combat related damage for 10 secs -> use to cost you no more disc for 45 minutes. because of the long refresh timer involved in that disc few if any ever used it.

But that has all changed .....
A scenario you as an SA tank have just watched the MA tank die, you can AE Taunt , than hit Incite (new agro disc) now NPC has your attention. hit disc "Fortitude" for the next 12 secs you can attack NPC and not take any damage /with no loss in agro whats so ever...... This also allows those Clerics the much needed time to successfully target you and begin the CCH. Once your disc has expired you can immediately go to normal evasive or stone disc.

Another scenario is as MA you can go Fortitude than defensive.



Another disc that i have recently discovered value in is Resistant disc ...... if you are not MA or SA , use this disc when attacking nasty AE mobs. IT WILL stack with bard song AND has an incredibly long duration with no loss of endurance, which means when its time to go offensive you can click it off and than hit killing machine disc. BTW healing disc will not sufficiently offset AE damage so you are better off with resistant disc in effect.

hmm .. i always considered furious to be a defensive discipline first.

(tantors tusk is a favorite of many). now on the pull you swap the weapons with the hotkeys (no need to open inventory). engage the mob with the high damage looong delay weapon and the mob hits you with his characteristic damage spike. in the 9 seconds or whatever it is he hits you say 15 times but they are all riposted. now make the quick switch back to your favorite agro weapons again using your hotkeys and go defensive.

same is true for debuffs in general.

the ripostes did about 2000+ damage. groupmates ooh and ah!

the first heal wont have to come in for a long time because the mob is slowed just as furious is wearing off denying the mob any spike damage at all.

you have succeeded in generating huge agro before the first heal or slow was cast. on the toughest mobs this is likely to save the lives of the poor folks who are tasked to be first on the lineup for the debuffs.

you have gotten past the hardest part of surviving .. the initial engagement.
