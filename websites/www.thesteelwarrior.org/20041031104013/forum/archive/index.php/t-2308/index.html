Beornegar
Honed Blades of Ykesha are not campable. They're player made, and they seem pretty rare, mainly because in the early days of cultural smithing, some races got shafted pretty badly. Trolls, who make the yaks, are one of those races. There don't seem to be a whole lot of troll GM smiths, but that seems to be slowly changing now that they get better rewards for all the time and cash dumped into reaching GM skill level. Also, regular ssoy's, which these are made from, don't proc until level 37, so if you're below that, they're not going to be nearly as good as they will after, but the ratio on them is damn good either way. They'll last you well into your 50's, maybe longer.
