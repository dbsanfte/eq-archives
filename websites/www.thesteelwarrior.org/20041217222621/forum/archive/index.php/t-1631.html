From all my parsing and empiracle (sp) evidence:

5/6 AA = 1 level (for 61-65)

So... for the 6 AA's you would need to get ND3, you could be 1 level higher. From our parsing, we saw a nice 4-5% increase in dps per level, a 7-8 % decrease in damage taken per level, 60 or so hps per level, and of course, better resists, etc...

So, if you look at the data, for ND3 (6 aa's), yer gonna get something like 200 hps or so. Whereas, for 1 level, you get 60 hps plus 7% less damage per battle. If you are taking more than 2000 hps (7% of 2000 is 140) in a battle, then the 7% decrease is better.

The numbers speak volumes...

Level > AA.
