Things we've tried:

- grouping: we knew that the penalty would be bad, but we were unable to find any docs that tell us how close in level she needs to be to get exp (thats worth our time).

- tag mob, me out of group: works okay, find a place with a very high density of mobs, and use something like a low refresh wand or instacast / low mana spell.

- quests: mongo can push newbs through quests like mad, works pretty decent, only downside is either of the above killing methods gimp about half of the exp you would of picked up solo.

- instanced zones: mobs yield alot of exp that will trickle down, even if you are grouped with the twink (it's not great, but it's not 0).
