Drunder50 is correct. For the SK 1.5 epic who does not have 1.0.

Did him twice with 1 group with 2 SKs, cleric, shaman, enchanter, and wizard; I played the SK, Cleric, and Wizard. Max hit is for 1600 quad and rampages. I was lvl 65 (if i recall about 8500 hp/1800 AC buffed and max PoP defensive AAs) at the time and he hit me for max often enough compared to other mobs that DI fired. I don't recall the minimum hit anymore but I want to say it was about 400s. He's slowable but it's mitigated quite a bit.

We used a charmed pet for DPS along with the Wizard. The SK who MT'd (me) was chain CHealed, and the other SK was the RT got healed by the shaman.

There is no point to raiding him unless you are planning to get a SK their pre-epic piece. And he doesn't always drop the quest piece either.

Hope that helps out.
