Water Elemental Form (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2800&source=Live) is another kind of free mana regen. 4 mana per tick.

Mystical Transvergence (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=2716) is 1080 mana (360 mana x3), over 15 minutes. Subtracting the 350 mana it costs to summon a rod (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3188&source=Live) (400 mana base, minus 2.5% for 50 specialize conjuration, minus another flat 10% for having SCM3) leaves 730 mana over 150 ticks: 4.8 (technically I'll assume 4, since EQ truncates just about every number) mana per tick, that stacks with every single form of mana regen, and doesn't require a buff slot.

That puts Mage self buffed, 0 FT/MC mana regen at 43 mana per tick: 27 medding, 8 from Xegony, 4 from Water Elemental, and 4 from rods. Rods are active mana regen, so yes you have to remember to click them as often as possible... but if any caster forgets about the rod in their inventory, they didn't need the mana that badly anyway.

Elemental legs summon rods for free, increasing the mana regen from rods to 7.2, and the mage's mana regen from 43 per tick to 46.

Wizards' active mana regen, from Harvest (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=1744&source=Live) and Harvest of Druzzil (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3338&source=Live) totals 800 mana over 10 minutes (100 ticks): 8 mana per tick.

Druid: 27 + 8 (Nine) + 4 (Mask of the Forest (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3453&source=Live)) = 39 per tick

Shadow Knight: 22 (medding) + 15 (Pact of Hate (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=3488&source=Live)) = 37 per tick
