A few ideas

A slight upgrade for an earring would be a jagged bone hoop. 6AC 40HP. These are really cheap now. Under 500plat.

Barbed dragonscale boots are nice. 19AC 9stam 30HP. they are a little under 3k on my server.

For about 1400plat you can get a Sarnak bracer of victory. Crappy AC, but nice HPs. (especially if your stam isnt maxed) 6AC 10stam 20chrisma 30HP.

If you are looking to spend some dough upgrading your haste item, look into a soulscream belt. Its 9AC 9str 10stam and 31%haste. Ive seen these recently for 15k - 18k on my server. (Im on Cazic Thule btw)

And I totally agree with the above poster about the BB LDoN vendor armor. It is only 150 points a piece and there are some very nice stats and aug slots on them. BP is +40 AC +6 Str +6 Sta +6 Wis +6 Int +5 vsFire +5 vsDis +25 HP +25 Mana for example. Even at your level 150 points would go pretty fast.

Good luck
