Vergil
If a warrior and a paladin were of the same level, had the same HP and the same AC, the warrior WOULD, theoretically, have an advantage in damage taking - because they have higher defensive skills (as mentioned, dodge, pioste and parry)...

BUT the paladin will, in reality, take less damage as the MT: they can use a slow 2H weapon (avoiding POP mob ripostes) and their stuns will allow the slower to slow the mob earlier with no fear of agro.

Less ripostes + faster slows = less damage overall.

With regards to the other question (does level really make a difference?) - I don't believe that I have ever seen parses that prove that there is a difference in mitigation based on levels...but I believe that level does effect this, even when removed from skill gains.
