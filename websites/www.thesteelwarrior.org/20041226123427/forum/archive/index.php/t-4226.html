11-12-2003, 12:28 AM
You actually gain about 50hp per level 61-65.

All stat caps increase at a rate of 5 per level 61-65.

Your damage bonuses go up - it goes from 11 to 13 for 1h, 2h bonuses go up as well, but I didn't really track those (since they're dependant on weapon delay).

You don't get an innate quad attack... though you can potentially quad with a 2h and Punishing Blade AA (or potentially hit 6x with PB and Flurry)

Leveling opens up some AAs which you can't get at level 60 or even level 64.

Levels really are > all. That's not a myth. Take an identical geared/AAed/etc level 60 and level 65 warrior, the level 65 warrior will own the level 60 at both taking and dishing out damage.

*However*, it may be beneficial for some people to stay at level 60 and AAexp/plat/gear farm for awhile (people who have leveled beyond their gear - ie level 60 warriors with 3k even or less hps). One added benefit of level to 65 is tier2+ AAexp is well... damn uber compared to how AA exp was in the Luclin era, but you really have to be up to par gear wise to take the beating from mobs that often hit for 550+.
