Hi all-

Something occured to me that might be interesting to test... follow the train of thought here-

Situation: multiple weapons "share" the same proc, say enraging blow, like the BoC and the BF.
Observation: BF procs quantitatively more often than a BoC (or other non-mod'ed weapon, which are all about 2.1 procs/min).
Conclusion: this strongly suggests that the proc modifier is on the weapon, not the proc used. Nothing new here.

BUT... what about added procs? For instance, what happens if you put an AUGUMENT PROC on a "high proc" weapon? Does it share the increased proc rate, or not? Has anyone parsed an upgraded BF yet and compared it with a BoC perhaps?

I'm planning on doing this test w/ my BF eventually, but it won't be for a while, as I'm still working on 10ac and 40hp augs for my old gear... I suppose I could nab a mirror 1 proc for 30pts and give it a test tho, maybe I'll try that 2nite. I can also check with call of sky (buff proc) pretty easily (orb of sky) too while I'm at it...
