Agnostic vs Rallos Zek - Agnostics get minorly better faction with most old world stuff, however, who cares about old world anymore as it doesn't affect you in day to day things. Rallos Zek allows you to use Fierce Heraldic armor at 45+ I believe. It is cheap, and very high quality (better than Velious quested by a good bit, many slots even non visable).

Halfling bonuses - Sneak is an amazing skill. It allows you to get indifferent faction with anything you are behind. This means better prices from Apprehensive merchants, being able to use KoS / dubious merchants, able to do quests that require indif without needing the faction. And being able to walk past KoS mobs if they are looking away from you.

- Hide is also useful. Since you won't be a rogue, you can't hide / sneak and be able to walk around invis. But you can have an unlimited duration invis as long as you don't move. It can fail though, so you should have a mob targeted that you know is a faction other than Indifferent and doesn't see invis. That way when you hide you can test if it does work (by the mob becoming indif).

- Small race. You don't need a shrink device to travel in dungeons.

- Exp bonus. Warriors have a natural exp bonus (5% I believe). But also, Halflings recieve a bonus (again, 5% I believe). Leaving you with a 10% exp bonus which makes you the fastest leveling class / race combo.

Disadvantages - You'll be an ugly assling =( .... oh wait, redundant there, sorry.

Starting Points - Depends on what you want to do. As was mentioned above, if you are gonna be a serious raider, go for Cha. If you are going to be casual, go for Sta. Reason for cha if you're a raider - in higher end raids you will be getting Divine Intervention cast on you a lot. It is an instant heal if you drop below 15% health. But it can be either full / partial / or fail. The result is based on the cha of the person who has the spell on them. 305 cha and it will rarely, if ever, fail, and almost always full. Also, at the higher end game, sta gear comes easily, so the points are kinda wasted if you can get the gear.
