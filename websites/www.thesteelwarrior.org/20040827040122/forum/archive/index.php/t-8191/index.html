First go back to EF and do /corpse. If the corpse is still showing as being in EF somewhere, it may have fallen under the world or be stuck in the rock you died on. Your equipment will be on the first corpse. Additional corpses (if you get killed trying to get back or whatever) won't have anything on them unless you pick something up on the way ;) . Starting at level 11 you lose some xp every time you die. Empty corpses will have a 3 hour timer on them so you can be resurrected and gain back some of the lost xp. Corpses that have equipment on them should have week long timers (though only a 3 hour rez timer).

For future reference, rez timers count down any time you have your account loaded (even at the character select screen).

- Pirsquared, level 59 cleric on E. Marr

Brabble
A couple of things.

Make sure you keep the chipped bone rod in the bank, lol. Obviously, if you carry it with you, it will be on your corpse when you die.

If you have trouble finding your corpse (or getting to it) in Everfrost again, try asking politely in /ooc for help reaching your corpse. There's an LDoN camp in the zone and at least a third of the characters there not only can solo everything in the zone but know darn well they aren't doing anything at all for the next 20 minutes. They might help you out, especially if you say you have a loc but are having problems running naked to the corspe without getting killed.

Start fletching. You can make arrows right away and a bow shortly after that (or you can buy a bow in the bazaar, but make your own arrows). Those orc shaman in Everfrost are nasty but stupid. Even if they root you and run off, they'll turn around to cast on you before they get out of bow range. If you've picked a good spot to fight them, you'll still be able to see them, and you can shoot them to death, heh.

Also, if you haven't noticed this already, you'll get a message when you are fighting the shaman that they have begun to cast a spell. As soon as you see that, use your bash hotkey. It will interrupt their spells. Your bash won't refresh as fast as the shaman can cast, but you can interrupt 1/2 to 1/3rd of the spells they try to cast.

Brutul
If you think you are at the spot where you died but don't see your corpse, it could be just a graphics bug. Type /corpse and if you are near it you will get the "summoning Mikemac's corpse" message. If you still can't see it, type /tar mikemac's (with the 's at the end so you don't just target yourself) and see if it comes up on target. If it does, you can type /loot and get your gear. Corpses are funny in that they can sometimes sink into geometry, like if you die next to a wall, your corpse may be (on your screen at least) hidden almost completely inside the wall where you can't see it, or just a toe will be sticking out, but /corpse will get it to your feet if you're in range.

And yea, don't buy chipped bone rods in the bazaar, anyone selling them there is just a rip-off artist. You can buy them from NPC vendor in any city for 5pp or so (for a 3-charge rod).
