I have done the Dranik's Hallows: Murkgliders Hive expedition with a full group 3 times now (once a night), as well as a few times just duoing with my cleric, and I am quite impressed with the experience. I started doing them at level 68, however, so as mentioned, every time the mobs have almost all been level 66.

That said, for level 66 mobs, they're a complete joke. I found it difficult to convince people to do these, as they have a severe fear of being unable to mez things without a high end enchanter with their new mez spells, but in actuality, in my experience, mez was not only unnecessary, it would've slowed things down.

My first group there, which was more of an exploration since we had no idea what to expect, was: 68 War (myself), 65 War, 65 Clr, 65 Dru, 66 Rng, and 67 Shm. Obviously, we were pretty low on DPS, but the mobs are so weak in terms of damage output, I found it very easy to tank two or three at once as long as slows were done, without any risk whatsoever. (Note: I buffed to about 9000 HP with Virtue, Focus, and max stamina at the time of our adventure, so not high end by any means.) The second Warrior was essentially the offtank, and on multiple pulls we'd just split things up and go to town - As mentioned, pulling is very easy and you can usually tell before pulling whether you'll get one or two mobs (rarely, you can get three, but not often). On a side note, as also mentioned, since the indifferent mobs assist, I would suggest killing them along the way, as they may roam back onto you during another fight - had a few times this happened when we were trying to speed through to get to the bugged Dragorn spawn, and ended up with 4 or 5 in camp. Again, with slow, very managable.

Edit: Wanted to add a minor note. All of the group members, with the exception of the shaman and myself, were in pre-Ssra level gear, with a few random Ornate pieces and the like. Our experience rate with the aforementioned group was about 8 - 9% an hour for level 68, whereas my 65 cleric got his remaining 8% to 66 and finished with 42% into level 66, and we spent just over 3.5 hours there.

I've seen 5 named spawns in the Murkgliders, and have yet to see a rune drop (not a big deal to me personally, just a tidbit of information.) Unfortunately, the regular drops from the two named mobs (they are the ones mentioned by the expedition giver; I don't know if there are any other nameds spawns, but I somewhat doubt it) are complete crap for the levels. You might find someone in the group or your guild who could use the upgrade, but most likely it's just going to be Bazaar fodder, which was disappointing =/ (We saw a number of 65 hp/mana leather bracers and ditto in the form of caster boots, as I recall).

As for the bugged Dragorn, since I've only done one of the three instances, I wasn't able to confirm it, but after reading others posts claiming there is a bugged Dragorn in the other instances, perhaps there is a reward for collecting the drop from each of the three Dragorns and handing it into the expedition giver or another NPC? I'll try to sneak down there (invis seems to work quite well with a few exceptions, at least in the Murkgliders) before the fast spawns start and kill the Dragorns. I did turn in one ring from the Murkglider version to the expedition guy, but he didn't respond or give anything back. However, it may be worth noting, he did eat the ring - he did not give the normal "I have no use for this Bashiae, you may have it back." Perhaps that indicates something, I'm not sure.

Anyone else tested handing in any of the Dragorn drops?
