"cloak of greater pernicity 25k"

Do the quest.

It's MUCH cheaper.

I recently had this exact quandry. I lucked into a Hiero cloak that I had been after for ages.

At the exact same time I found the elusive boar horn for sale, and finally had the items I needed to finish the pernicity quest.

At level 52, Hiero had 3 less hit points, and 2 less AC.

But the dex on Hiero put me over 200 dex.

The str on pernicity put me over 1k atk easily.

I was using pernicity until a friend offered me 3 valorium rings for the pernicity cloak.

Much better deal for me.

In any event, if you go PERNICITY ... just do the quest.

The two items to get from innovation are EASY to snag.

The other two items from the higher planes are tradable.

Cloak sells for around 12k on Povar. I got the horn and the shard for 4k each. So I saved 4 thousand plat just doing the quest. I shudder to think how much plat YOU could save if they sell for 25k on your server.

Good luck. Have fun.
