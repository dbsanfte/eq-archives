Facts:
The upper 4 horses are Charmable and Mezable, they are just very resistant. Think Druid charm.
Some of the horses spawn see invis and some spawn otherwise. There can be 1-4 of eitehr type to a max of 4 total.
Killing the 4 horses up top will spawn 2 guardians deeper in the lair. Sometimes they will spawn on the 1st kill, sometimes on the last. Every time I have it figured out, the next one changes.
Phini drops 4 Epic parts as part of his loot table. A robe for rogue, backbone for bards, Staff for mage, and a staff for wizards. there can be 2 of one or any two of 4, or more often then not, a research page and a enchanter/shaman wand.
The druid epic drops off a corrupted horse which spawns ELSEWHERE in Kedge Keep.
If there are 6 horses in phini's lair, someone attempted phini and failed. The lower 2 horses are already spawned and 2 more will not spawn.
Lastly, phini can spawn at a level that can leave a 30 minute corpse, but it's rare.

So, here is some advice. Phini used to rush you when you pulled and he would swim past the horses not agroing most of teh time because he wouldnt' be in range long enough. Unfortunately when they changed the NPC casting rules a while back it drasticly changed Wizard mobs, and Phini is a WIZARD. he will cast like crazy, and if the initial Ice Comet he usually opens with doesn't land, expect Lures. For a lower level raid (30s-50s) spellshield the puller and pretent it's any tough mob. If you're a higher level party (high 50s can do phini if they are skilled, and a 65 of appropriate class can solo him.) just treat it like any Named encounter. If you pull horses, just remember that Complete heal takes a while to cast. Phini has 18k HPs and a high DPS group can kill him before it lands.

As far as a 60 druid and warrior? I bet you can IF you can get a clean pull. Don't screw around, just pull 4 horses and then phini with the 2 guardians. I bet the druid can charm the top horses and grind down this way. (Charm animal.) If not, evac early cause getting a rez is a pain.
