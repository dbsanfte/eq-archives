Feet -- LDoN drops are your best bet here. The Everfrost hard boots are 34ac 85hp and somewhat common.

Legs -- Best you can get, really, is the ornate legs. Till then, you're good.

Wrists -- you have a few options. If you can get a few friends, you could look at the Ring of Slime event in Cazic Thule. Theres a 100hp war-only bracer that drops from there, and is by todays standards single groupable. Ornate wrists are easily campable in tactics or BoT. You could also hit up Velk's for a silver chitin wrist, 15ac 40hp. Theres also the rock-molded wristguard from Chardokb, with 30ac 25hp (hp is low, but the AC is pretty nice)

Arms -- try to get yourself some Ornate. Alternatively, theres a set of arms that drop in Riwwi that are quite nice, but the name escapes me. I'm not sure if you and your friends are capable of Riwwi, I've never exp'ed there myself.

In general, if you can find someone else to tank for you, or if you are confident of your healer (like it sounds you are), you could try some hard LDoN for some loot upgrades. There's a 75hp 28ac plate bracer, a 100hp ring, an 85hp necklace, and a 90hp earring (not to mention the boots and the gloves you're wearing).
