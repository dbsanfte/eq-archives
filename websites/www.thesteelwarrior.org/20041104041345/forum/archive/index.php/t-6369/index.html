Bunker summary: 2004-04-02 1:50 pm to 2004-04-02 11:29 pm

Kaesorn, wearing his healer hat, preaches the virtues of high-aggro procs; says that a warrior's DPS just doesn't matter at all compared to the ability to hold aggro.

BabaHanz says he prefers CHSD/SotB over CHoS/GBoS on raids, because of the decreased knockback. Editor: if you're MTing, everyone's behind the mob, on the opposite side from you, and your push seems like it wouldn't matter. Payn says to compare weapons by max hit: (6*damage+bonus)/ratio. With max haste (you have a 36%+ haste item, and a top haste spell on you), Skarlon > Exe Axe > Mith2h. With only item haste, Exe Axe > Mith2h > Skarlon. With no haste, Mith2h > Skarlon > Exe Axe.
Editor: I prefer to compare weapons by modal (most common) damage: (2*damage + bonus)/ratio. However, these weapons are all close enough in delay that the order that results is the same.
http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?s=&postid=94104#post94104

Ahz prefers Mir legs (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=23659) to Tak legs (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=23473). Mir legs have 4 less AC, 10 less STR and AGI, and 15 less HP; they have 8 more STA and CHA; they have 10 each vs. magic and cold instead of 13 each vs. fire and disease. They cost 760 vs. 1492.
http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?s=&postid=93734#post93734

dethvegi1 says to mainhand the BF even if you have an aug'ed GBoS, because it has nearly the same proc rate even without an aug. Editorial opinion: with increased proc rate, BF does 2170 hate per minute. GBoS aug'ed with Anger III does 1200+958 or 2158 (assuming stun is 300 hate). Roughly the same hate. However, the GBoS does it in four procs (average) per minute, while the BF does it in three. And the GBoS has a slightly better ratio than the BF. Thus the GBoS will have slightly more reliable early aggro, and probably roughly equal long-term aggro, making an aug'ed GBoS probably slightly better to mainhand than a BF.
http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?s=&postid=93716#post93716
