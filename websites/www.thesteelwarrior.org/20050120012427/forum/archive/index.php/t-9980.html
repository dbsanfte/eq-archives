Ah, "The Haste Question".

You're always going to have to sacrifice when it comes to haste. It's mandatory, and the items that have it are always statistically less significant then standard items that would normally fit that slot.

Judging by your magelo, you seem like you're a person with a high playtime and a high amount of motivation, meaning you're willing to work hard for what you want. I like those kinds of people =P. The best choice for you would simply be getting into Ikkinz. You're gonna have to do some work and defeat both Ikkinz1 (Trial of Singular Might) and Ikkinz2 (Trial of Twin-Fates) to gain access to Ikkinz3 (Trial of Tri-Struggles), but Ikkinz3 is where the money is, not to mention you'll be that many steps closer to accessing Ikkinz raids. What you can get there is, mentioned above, the Crafted Wristband of Strife:

Crafted Wristband of Strife
MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM NO DROP
Slot: WRIST
AC: 25
STR: +5 STA: +20 WIS: +10 INT: +10 AGI: +15 HP: +160 MANA: +140 ENDUR: +140
SV FIRE: +15 SV DISEASE: +15 SV COLD: +15
Haste: +46%
Class: WAR PAL RNG SHD MNK BRD ROG BST BER
Race: ALL
Slot 1, Type 8

It drops off a Kyv named (Hexxt something) that will be up the moment you enter the zone. If you add a Flameshield Stone to it, it'll become an extremely respectable 50ac 160hp 46% haste item that can, honestly, last you a very, very long while. Other benefits you'll gain by conquering Ikkinz is its other loots: Glyphed Prayer Ring (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=68796), Resonating Earring of Battle (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=68764), and Carved Vrex Totem (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=68768). Also of worthy mention, and I honestly think this is an aug a lot of Warriors overlook a lot of times, is the Glyphed Sandstone (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=68800). It drops off Diabolic Destroyer (Ikkinz1) as well as the Pile of Bones at the end of Ikkinz3.

In the meanwhile, snag Raex gear every time you can. Fill in the offslots with shit you can single group, like the stuff in Ikkinz. It's tough, it's really freakin' tough, but I can tell you right now that the work in Ikkinz pays off tremendously.

The Ikkinz4 and 5 (raids) are easily doable by an elemental guild as well, btw. ;) It's not until Ikkinz6 that I'd recommend Time gear, but a guild on my server did it with pretty minimal elemental farming actually. Hell, they even beat Ikkinz7 (Keeper of the Altar) before they were Time flagged, haha.
