12-21-2004, 12:52 PM
One thing I found is that you need to make sure you go through all the prompting accurately. If you miss one thing, its a pain to figure out what that was.

I'm assuming that when you say quest you got the giant head, sash, beard and bone from the POS giants. Then handed them into Askr the lost in the tunnel in POS. Then you get the bag for putting the medallions into. Combine the two meds in the bag and give them to Askr the lost.

When you do this you should get the character flag.

Then you have to enter BoT through the tornado area in POS.

Then go talk with the dwarf in POK Gram Dunnar that gave you the charm and say, "Tell stories" that should get you the AA. If you don't get it.. then something didn't go right in your POJ Trials and you can check that by going to Seer and get it straightened out. She should tell you what is needed.

Hope that helps.
