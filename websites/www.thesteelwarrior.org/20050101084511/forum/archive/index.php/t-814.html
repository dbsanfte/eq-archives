- You try to taunt people before initiating a conversation, making sure you have their full attention.

- You try to bow kite the paperboy

- You hit up the local pharmacy and ask where they keep the sow potions.

- You are late for work and yell "Dammit wheres my black Greaves!?"

- You are injured in a car accident and continuly shout at the paramedics - "COmplete HEal dammit!!!"

- You fashion a "Wurmslayer" using a broken rake and some duct tape.

- Your wife is holding your credit card so you try and disarm her.

- After severing a limb in an accident you shrug it off and attempt to bind wound.

- You get into a bar brawl, after taking a few punches to the head you yell to your friends, "Did you slow this guy!?!??!"

- Instead of laughing you say LOL or ROFL and use other emoticons instead of expressing emotions.

- You bring in your dead goldfish to the vet and ask for a rez.

- You attempt to "Interupt" a conversation by throwing your shoulder into the speaker.

- You are at the strippers and attempt to Area Taunt the dancers.
