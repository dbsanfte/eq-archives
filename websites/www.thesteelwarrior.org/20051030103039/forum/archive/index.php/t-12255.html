3k faction from Neutral to Friendly
6k from Friendly to Honored
10k from Honored to Revered
21k from Revered to Exalted
1k to max Exalted (for Frostwolf insignia)


I just finished AV, first time I have done it since patch. Wow. What a huge difference.

Usually it would take all day to finish one instance. Now it takes 3 or 4 hours max, that I've seen. We won in 2 hours.

As far as faction, biggest bonus i've seen is killing the opposing sides leader. For Horde, it's Vanndar. If you are in raid that tags him, you get 700 faction. That's huge.

I believe everyone in zone gets 1 faction point when someone does turnin. I saw some that were 2 points, not sure what was handed in. I bet opposing side supplies (Irondeep for Horde).

So faction is just ticking away, it's great. You can stay at the front lines, and not loot, and still get plenty of faction. If you do loot, each turnin is 1point. For the spines/hearts/etc. Didn't run any supply runs, those are probably more points.

Killing NPC's nets faction I believe, especially enemy commanders. I'm notsure on capturing GY's or towers and whatnot, but I would imagine so.

Bonus honor is given when you leave.

I think the patch vastly improved alterac valley. It's more about the players instead of the gaurds and summons now.

I'm having a blast and can't wait to get exalted.
