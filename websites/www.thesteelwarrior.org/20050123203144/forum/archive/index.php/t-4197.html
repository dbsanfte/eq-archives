arf arf

I think you know this, but if you remove the aug, you have to buy a new one. If you're gonna add an aug to any blade ... hrm ... I'd add an aug to your SotB first, and make it an aggro aug (Yakesha or Anger, and I prefer the Anger augs myself). You will eventually want to upgrade that offhander for something more aggro-ish, and I'm betting that, like me, you're budgeting your points, and so there is little value in augmenting it. You could, however, put a cheap Yakesha augment on each of those weapons for now and have a decent DW aggro combo. But augment the SotB first - it's your mainhand weapon AND it's the one you'll keep the longest.

You may as well keep the DD aug on your Ornate in the meantime, you're only going to lose it forever by removing it. Later, when you have the points to burn, you can put another DD aug on it (OR you can get a better DPS weapon, and augment THAT with a DD or Lifetap widget). Keep it as a DPS weapon, there are times you can use it safely and see some nice big numbers. For what I can get my hands on, 2H > DW for DPS, but I like to have a DW DPS (non-aggro) set up available for fun, and the Ornate fits in there well.

One more note about the GSoT ... not only is it good aggro, but riposte damage on the Planes will hurt you much, especially when it comes in spikes. Your gear is a little better than mine I think, but not so much that you're not feeling the pain when you DW as much as I. I read it here first, and have the bruises to prove it - 2H weapons reduce the damage you take from ripostes, and do better DPS. Your survivability and aggro generation improve. Personally, I prefer to DW (it's a style thing), and I do in LDoN most of the time.

Whether or not SOE decides to make the stamina bar relevant to the game in the future is anyone's guess, but until it is I wouldn't give it a second thought.
