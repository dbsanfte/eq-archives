What I'd like to see happen is to have them assign AC/HP/mana like the already do but that's just the base for the item. When a specific class puts it on that gets modified based on their class. So a 35AC 125hp/mana necklace on me is a 50AC 175hp 0mana necklace, but on a knight it's a 42AC 140HP 100mana necklace, and on a wizard it's a 20AC 100HP 175mana necklace.

They could do the same thing with focus effects. Assign focus effects to some sort of category. Instead of assigning a specific effect to the item, assign it something like category 3 level 4. Instead of Flowing Thought 2 you make it category 1 level 2 and on a melee it becomes aura of battle.
