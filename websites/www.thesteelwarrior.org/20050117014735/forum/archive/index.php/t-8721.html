A Anathema is fine for the level your guild is at. When you take into consideration how many hours/days you will waste camping BoW's (unless your really really lucky) versus spending that time in Ele's getting molds/loot, its not worth it. You can pick up a SEWS while rampaging through the planes that is almost as good.

Also take into consideration the fact that OOW aa's seem to revolve around reducing offhand damage taken. It seems OOW will reduce 2handers effectiveness which is depressing to 2hander addicts. Two handers simply can not hold the same agro as equal level dw weapons. They also do not have comparative stats. You will lose out on a lot of stats, mostly hp, when using a 2hander. The only thing a lot of 2handers have going for it are decent ac. The higher end you go up, the more 1hander's ac levels catch up.

I suggest let your other guild warriors mt the mosses you deem require a 2hander, and simply worry about picking up decent 1handers and a shield.
Then again what do I know?

While I have every intent to get a handful of BoWs for my guilds warriors, our Anathemas are serving us incredibly well even in the Elemental Planes. Will the BoWs be -neccesary-? No. Unless your healers are all botted by morons, you'll be able to keep it off of them, and you won't die. Will they be very nice to have? Yes. They'll let your DPS widen the throttle just around enough to be noticable.

Once you bring down RZ once or twice, once you drop Solusek Ro a few times, there's a whole lot more out there than 'just earrings and crap'. Consider that in the time that it takes to drop RZTW for 2-3 drops, just one of which -might- be a BoW, you can shake down the planes for 4-6 pieces of elemental armor/weapon, and you can probably do it with half the forces. Except for the BoW, Rallos loot all has one issue or another with it, Elemental loot doesn't nearly so much. The BoW is an excellent upgrade from the Anathema, it just won't rock your world the way upgrading from a Xanamech Hammer and SotBS would.
