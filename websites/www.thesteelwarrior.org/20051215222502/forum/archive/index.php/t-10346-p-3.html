It means, among other things, that a mage with the same STA as a warrior will have more HP than the tank, as the mage's base HP will be lower. But a warrior is gonna have more STA gear than the mage, and so this doesn't come up.

A few posts pointed out that, with this model, you could die from having your STA debuffed, by losing more HP than you had from STA. I couldn't see any means to debuff that much STA, tho. One post said that at some point in the game, there weren't any statistic increases on level ups, but there were HP increases. When stat gains were folded in, they tweaked the math so that the final gains (HP and Mana) remained consistent. I dunno, wasn't there. It's all hearsay, and so use it wisely and remember I guarantee nuffin.
