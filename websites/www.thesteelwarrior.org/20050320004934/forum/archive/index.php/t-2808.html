Lavastorm is a good idea cuz there's actually gypsy vendors that sell bandages there.

It took me a LONG time to go from 185-192... and stayed at 192 until i dinged 57. Then i payed my trainer for the last skillups from 192-201. Cost me a few more plat (probably like 100pp), but it took less time. With BW3 AA skill i can BW to 100%.

I have Champs bp as well as 210 BW and I use them both, it's situational. Here's some things i have learnt using them both.

1. I can actually BW myself up quicker than with Champ BP because of the slow cast time on the BP.

2. I duo and trio with a shammy and monk combo most of the time. My BW to 100% on the shammy he can Canni forever if he wants. Downtime consists of shammy Canni and me BP healing myself, and me using my bandages on the monk (he can BW to 70%) while the shammy gets his mana back.

3. You can BW yourself while invis. BP is a cast spell so it drops invis. This has helped me in tight situations.

4. I BP heal all the time after a rez because it's just easier.

5. Large Soiled Bag is easily obtained and can hold 200 bandages with almost no weight. 200 bandages x 85 pts a bandage = 17000 hps of healing. I rarely have gone under 80 bandages at any time.

Some things to think about before abandoning BW.
