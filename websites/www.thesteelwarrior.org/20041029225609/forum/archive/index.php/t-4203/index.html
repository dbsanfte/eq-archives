you are nearing the top of the level range where soloing will be a viable option so start thinking group-minded. your job is to be in the monster's face and protect the casters who as you will level up you will notice dont absorb damage well. i think most will agree with me that blue con mobs (especially at the lower end of the range) are going to be more profitable for experience than higher conning mobs which mostly just lead to long down times while your group recouperates. crushbone is a very good zone to fight in at your level because high levels come by from time to time and give you free buffs :) ... but do not dishonor the warrior name by begging for them.
