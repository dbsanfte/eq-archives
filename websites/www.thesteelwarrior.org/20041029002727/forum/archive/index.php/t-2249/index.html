Wake of Tranquility requires line of sight. Pacify, calm, lull, etc. do not.

Getting to the basement of Karnor's as a pair of 2boxed 48s would be, ummm, difficult.

Howling Stones is a one way dungeon. You'd have to be insane to go there with a warrior/cleric 2box setup. Plus, golems run, and once they run, you'll die from the resulting train. Oh, and you'll immediately aggro 2 harmtouching skeletons on zone in. Hope your warrior zones faster then the cleric!

Sebilis frogs run. Going to be a problem for you.

My suggestion? Echo Caverns, the Taskmaster camp. Killing the Taskmaster's kind of tough, but very doable. Mobs run here, but it's easy to keep enough room cleared (clearing exp-giving dark blue mobs) to give the cleric enough time to land a root, and root lasts on these mobs. With KEI, you may have enough mana for the cleric to nuke fleeing mobs effectively. The social range on these mobs is low, so mob fleeing isn't as much of a problem here as in Howling Stones or Sebilis. Best of all, it takes only a couple minutes from time to log in, to when you can start killing. Very, very easy to get to.
