If you're concerned with aggro from what i see you have 2 options:

#1 If you are in a raiding guild go after dragon in PoI for CHSD, or other aggro weapon from ssra etc.

#2 Buy a GSoT (2hb, around 200 - 300pp) and save your money till you can buy a BoC or some better combo.

In short no a SBoL will totally suck for aggro, but it makes a decent DPS weapon combo. If you are into LDoN you could work towards a 16/19 dagger with an aggro proc that would be good however around 3500 points it's not going to be quick or easy. In the short term GSoT should tide you over till you can get a good 1hs option. I would also go the sword in primary slot since it swings faster if you elect to keep what you have. I use shadowheart (11/17 no proc) and SoBS for 1hs, and GSoT for aggro. More often than not i just use the GSoT because it simply works better. Maybe if I augment the shadowheart it would be a nice combo but for my buck i am sticking with the 2h option. When your primary is swinging with a 17 delay before haste . . . enrages and DS can eat you up fast.

i've also thought about getting a WMBS and augmenting it (prob not as good as a augmented GSoT but worth a thought).
