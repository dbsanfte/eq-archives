Eoden
Good advise there so far.

Belt - You should definitely try and go for a haste upgrade. Soul Scream belt is probably the best option because it seems to drop fairly often, has nice stats, decent haste and there should be a few people interested in grouping in Veksar. RBG is another nice option or if you can get haste in another slot (SBC's have nice stats and are coming down in price) a putrid belt of rotten hide is nice HPs and cheap for the waist slot.

Shoulders - If you are on CoV faction then the Draconic Pauldrons are a very fun easy quest to do for some nice shoulders. Have a look in the keep for a walk through as there are some good posts on this here.

Wrist - I'm not sure what options are available for an agnostic Ogre in cultural, but even the non-imbued bracers (eg fine heraldic) are very nice with 25AC, 30hps and 6svall. Otherwise something like a bracer of defense would be a small upgrade and relatively cheap. If you are on CoV faction the Bracer of Benevolence can be done with a small group - check the keep for a walkthrough on this one too.

Earring - I'd definitely look into questing an Earring of the Solstice. Can get the bits for this for around 3k on tribunal now which is very cheap for a 5AC 5Sta 55HPs earring with some saves thrown in.

Helm - Helmet of the Radukan is cheap these days too, just add in another 50pp for a dye so you dont have a pink hat.
