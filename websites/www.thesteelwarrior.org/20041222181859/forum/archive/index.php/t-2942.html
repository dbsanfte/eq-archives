ATK is 2 components, weapon skill and raw ATK.

Weapon skill = 1hb, 2hs, piercing, etc., has an effect on average hit and accuracy.

Raw ATK = Affects average hit. More raw ATK pushes your average hit from low/medium to medium/high.

Hit percentage is fixed, pretty much. Pure melee (Warrior, Rogue, Monk) and I guess anything else with a 250 weapon skill (Rangers, Beastlords using H2H) tend to connect ~65% of the time from the rear, around ~60% from the front. Hybrids (Knights) hit about ~60% of the time from the rear and about ~55% from the front. Pets, which are level 60, also connect at a ~60% rate from the rear and ~55% from the front.

Mind you, these are all taken at level 65 and can vary depending on mob level. Hitting from the front can also vary depending on a mob's active defense skills. These numbers seem to remain consistant with several diferent high level mobs: Vindi (70), King Tormax (70), Acidic Mass (65), THO (63), and the Tower Lords (67?), so I assume it true for most all mobs as well.
