The "Tribute Master" should show up in Ctrl-F, use that to find him. Here's what I posted in another thread:


You can start buying tribute effects at level 20, but I think you can look the effects at any level, whether you can actually use them yet or not. The effects all have descriptions, so you can browse them and see their prices.

Go to your home town, hit Ctrl-F, choose the "Tribute Master", follow the path to them, and right-click. If the tribute master won't talk to you, then that means there's another Tribute Master in the same city that you should use instead. (e.g. Kelethin has two Tribute Masters, but only one of them will work for each wood elf.)

You need to donate money or tradeable items to the Tribute Master to get tribute points. Turning on tribute effects uses up tribute points every 10 minutes. You can enable/disable the tribute effects whenever you want, but you can only change your selections while you are at the Tribute Master.
