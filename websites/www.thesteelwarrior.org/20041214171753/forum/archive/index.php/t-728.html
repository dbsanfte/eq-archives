In regards to LR vs ID, Sscaley did a huge parse that strongly suggested that they reduced melee damage received by about the same amount.

In regards to CS3 vs ID5, CS3 will generally reduce melee damage received by a greater amount then LR5 and the *cost* of CS3 (12) is less then LR5 (15).

Once I had both CS3 and CA3, similar to Mathias, I also went the LR route over the ID route, but that really boiiled down to spell casting, as they are believed to reduce melee damage received by approx the same amount, but I now have the (not huge, but advantageous) benefit of avoiding hits and still getting spells off.

At the end of the day, you truly want to get both. I believe you would be splitting hairs to try and argue that one is inheritly better then another.

By that same school of thought, however, it never ceases to /boggle me how many tank classes go *all out* to get LR5 and then ignore ID and sometimes even CS!! That is just plain silly. These skills are damn near equivalents in terms of reducing melee damage received, with the CS/CA group being superior to the ID/LR group.

Dont ignore them--you want BOTH
