This is an exaggeration imo, or at the very least a bad analogy. GBoS/CHSD combo is going to work much better with an enc slowing, or even a over zealous shaman, because with a single proc you are pretty much in shape to tank through tash/slow. GSoT4W has great agro over time, because it is a very fast hard hitter.

With that said however, in general exp groups and when not MTing on the raid, it is very rare to see this weapon out of my hands. It is one of the best warrior dps weapons that I've ever seen pre elemental, and can hold its own with emp sword and seru sword.
