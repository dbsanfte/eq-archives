- Items grouped by stacking issues, all the items under one heading do not stack with each other.
- Items not in the database are named and linked to their lucy entry.
- I am excluding Augs, Potions, Shields(with worn DS), weapon procs, and items with nonreplenishable charges for various reasons such as very limited availability, requiring plat to maintain supply, unreliability, and the reason behind Shields with wornDS should be obvious.


Self only Clickable DS Line - no stacking issue except with its own line.
Cloak of Thorns4DS
Crystallized Serpent Eye4DS
Cloak of Icy Shadows6DS
Bone Chill Shield6DS (Note: Due to stats rare to see a warrior snag this, especially with the Cloak of Icy Shadows being from the same zone and having the same click)

Mantle of the Accused (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=13680) 6DS

Puresteel Mantle10DS
Girdle of Intense Durability15DS
Highest Possible DS from this set: 15DS



Self only Clickable DS Line - Does not stack with Shield of the Arcane, the KizrakBP click.
Spined Chain Mail3DS
Girdle of Living Thorns4DS
Highest Possible DS from this set: 4DS

Rluas's Chromatic Ring (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=30388) 5DS

Crown of the Tempest Guard (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=8954) 5DS

Gauntlets of Dark Embers5DS

Ring of Dain Frostreaver IV10DS
Veil of Flames17DS (Note: Does not stack with any other illusion spell)
Cloak of Retribution20DS
Highest Possible DS from this set: 20DS



Clickable DS Line (Mage) - Does not stack with Mage/Druid DS line.

Blazing Vambraces (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=4299) 9DS (Note: Targeted click, able to buff others)

Highest Possible combined worn DS: 25DS



Excluding Augs, Potions, Shields, and any item I'm unaware of, the maximum selfbuffed DS is 64. Add to that a maximum worn DS of 20. Total maximum selfbuffed/worn DS is 89.

03-23-2004, 03:37 PM
Excluding Augs, Potions, Shields, and any item I'm unaware of

I didn't include any of the above in my list and heres why . . .

Augs - The point buyable type require that you have gear with type3 slots(LDoN point buyable gear) and at my current gear level none of the LDoN gear is worth the effort, so I didn't put any effort into researching them. I know there are type7 or 8 Shield of Pain augs that drop from LDoN raids but lets face it the number of guilds doing those is pretty small, most guilds don't have the patience or drive to deal with them, including my own.

Potions - These require you to use up lots of bag space and have plat to keep your supply replenished as well as time to run to the baz, thats too many upkeep factors imo.

Shields - This was refering to shields with worn DS, like the LDoN hard drop shield I believe, limited availability and not very useful.

Also add to that list nonreplenishable charged items, the ring from BoT is a good example. I'm concerned with unlimited charges or rechargeable via mana batteries or quest turnins.
