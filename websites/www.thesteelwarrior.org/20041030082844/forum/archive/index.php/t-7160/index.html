Tiejinnie
Well, it would really depend on where in GoD are we talking about?
1. Natimbi - Qinimi - Riwwi are relatively easy, the mobs hit for 400+ max (Riwwi).
2. Barindu - Ferubi are a bit harder, mobs hit from 500+ to 1k+
3. Vxed - Tipt - Kod`Taz mobs hit 1k+ max

The recent retuning patch did lower the difficulty level of the mobs, since their ATK rating seems to have been brought down significantly.

For (1), I'd guess 6 - 7k buffed hp, 1.3 - 1.4k AC is enough
For (2), I'd guess 8k buffed hp, 1.5 - 1.6k AC is enough
For (3), I'd recommend 9k buffed hp, 1.65 - 1.7k AC minimum.

Btw, I'd advise maxing out your LR5 / ID5 asap, because although they do attack for max dmg a lot less now, you'd still want to avoid or mitigate as much dmg as possible, since Chaos Claws is pretty much almost un-resistable, unless you have very high resists to all.
