paladiin
I've tanked PoV golems at 62 with GSo4W with little to no agro problems, and seemed to do better than my GSoT in terms of agro. This was with either of them augmented.

However, I found my GSoT worked better in BoT, especially when grouped with super-duper DPS classes, but that was because I really needed the proc to stay ahead of them in terms of DPS.

Since I love my GSo4W for DPS duties (which I do a lot, and trade it between my war and monk on FV) I've been hesitant to Anger aug it. But doing so would certainly make for an impressive, consistant agro weapon.

My only concern is that in the upcoming GoD expansion, there is rumored to be an AA which will increase weapon procs. While the 75DD natural proc of the GSo4W is nice and increasing it would be a small DPS increase, it will be interesting to see if a double-hate GSoT would actually be better than a higher DPS/single hate proc GSo4W for agro. However, at this point it might be a mute argument, as I suspect either would still be great agro.

I've never heard of anyone augmenting the GSo4W with Anger III and then regret it. Will it be better than a Hate III GSoT? Probably a little better since it'll hold agro better in a drought of proc'ing, but I don't think it would be a night-and-day difference.
