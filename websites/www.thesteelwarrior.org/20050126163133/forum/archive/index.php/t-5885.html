First of all, anything > empty slots. So get them filled with anything cheap to start. Crustacean, Crafted, Validus Custodus, anything cheap and that's plate. Don't go expecting to tank anything serious with this though - it's just a band-aid until you can get geared up.

Soulscream Belt or Enchanted Sash of Wonder. One of these two should be on your waist.

http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=21436
http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=22156

However, skip the buying it part. I camped the Soulscream at 53 with a mid 50's group. It drops in Veksar. I believe the drop rate is a bit better than the Enchanted Sash of Wonder from Droga (personal experience). Should be able to get one in ~2 hours (depending on your luck, but that's what I've both seen and heard).

For your back, a very cheap option is a Crinose Cape of the Werewolf. It should be under 1k for most servers now. However, it drops fairly commonly from named in Mistmoore themed LDoN's (average group level 63). You might be able to get lucky and just win one if you do some hunting with some 65's there.
http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=23258

For your feet, a free option would be Kingly Boots of Splendor which drop in Droga. A group at your level could handle it no problem.
http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=21771

A cheap ear slot would be a Jagged Bone Hoop, likely under 1k in the local bazaar. Or if you're doing average level 63 LDoN's at Mistmoore for the above mentioned cape, you might come across a Lupine Frenzy Earring which would do nicely.
http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=23220
http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=21940

For your neck, you could try to find an inexpensive Amulet of Mummy Wrappings in the bazaar, or again hunt for it in an average 63 LDoN, this time in Takish-Hiz. A Ribbon Choker should be another inexpensive option in the bazaar.
http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=23829
http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16451

For your face, a Grobb Hide Mask is el cheapo option, should only be a few hundred pp in the bazaar, or can be camped with a group very easily at your level in Torgiran Mines (probably just easier to buy it though).
http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=19256

For range, I'd stick a cheap-o Goranga Idol there. Better than nothin.

Keep in mind there are fairly decent, and inexpensive or campable items. There are of course better out there for you, but should give you some survivability in areas where you can get bigger upgrades (like Ornate drops in higher tier PoP).

Ignore all the druids etc that say the PoM revamp ruined the zone, crawling and taking out various challenging named around the zone that drop loot every time is much better than camping Stomples for a year.

Only named see invis, only named summon. Everything except halfling-type mobs are charmable. Everything, including named, are lullable. Repop time is 30 minutes, named aren't very rare (1 in 4ish). Expect a few wipes and there isn't a graveyard, but it's not hard to invis back in.

May take a day or so to learn the zone; ports and false walls get confusing. Most of the pit traps were removed in the revamp, but the trap just south of gorilla professor is definitely still there. I'm not sure there's a way out if you fall in the pit, but there's quite a few mobs down there.
