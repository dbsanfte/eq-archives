General rule is 5HP = 1AC. There is 5 more AC on the Kizraks, but 30 more HP's on the Charged Magnetic. Pretty much a wash there. Other stats are pretty similar, but the Charged Magnetic has better resists, better stats, and a bit more endurance which is imo better than +10 atk.

In my opinion the Charged Magnetic is better, but if you are really an AC freak then you might want to get the Kizrak's. I'd pay a bit more for the Charged Magnetic, but I'm willing to bet the Charged Magnetic is much more costly due to the cost of lightning cores. However, if you can get either one without actually paying the cost via drop(s), they are pretty comparable.
