The group you describe doesn't have enough DPS to win the execution trial.

As other posters noted, your hitpoints won't be the issue; you'll fail because the timer will elapse before you whack a mob. Mages and Druids are awesome for this trial because of their ability to banish summoned (the rock mobs). If you have one or more of those classes with you, make sure they do NOT banish the mob you're beating on. If they banish an unengaged mob, you've gained an extra 30 seconds to kill the target you've been already working on.
