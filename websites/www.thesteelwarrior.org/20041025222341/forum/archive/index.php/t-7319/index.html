wandor
Most guilds use the pet agro rules to keep VZ on the MT. Your MT is getting stunned, they all do. The trick is that if a mob has more than 4 people on the agro list, it will automatically attack a player, it will not attack a pet. Keep pets on VZ to trigger this and make sure everyone else is at max melee range. As Kalorn said, VZ loves proximity agro.
