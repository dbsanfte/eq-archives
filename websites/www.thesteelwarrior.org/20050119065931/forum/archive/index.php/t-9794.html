1. & 2.
main hand : whip > long sword
off hand : long sword > whip

explaination :
please note that breal's formula : Hate_per_Swing = DM + DB * mod1 / mod2
mod1/mod2 = 1.4 for warrior without hate mods (works for 1Her, don't remember if it's the same for 2Her). let's call DBM = DB * mod1 / mod2 for the one hander. (note that at lvl 37 all one-hander have a DB of 4).
DM is the total domage (thus normal domage plus elemental domage plus bane domage).
assuming they will both swing x times each round.
round per minute being Rpm = (1+Haste%) * 600 / DL
we get Swing_Hate_per_minute = x * (DM + DBM) * (1+Haste%) * 600 / DL
x and haste% being equal for each weapon (they are in the same slot and of the same type), we get the ratio between weapon 1 and weapon 2 being : ( (DM1+DBM) * DL2 ) / ( (DM2+DBM) * DL1 )

Numerical result :
Mainhand : Whip/Sword ratio is 1.07 thus whip is better
Offhand : Whip/Sword ratio is 0.92 thus sword is better

I wouldn't worry about upgrading that whip until the sworn of the bloodsworn/copper hammer of striking are usable. The whip is a very good weapon at your level; in fact the droga weapons do not generate better melee aggro. What they'll do is generate aggro for you through their procs. And, from your late 40s on, procs will be a progressively more important part of the total aggro you generate.
Oh and there's no shame in pounding that provoke key, particularly before you have other endurance-eating discs. I'd try to time it so that the provoke key was always up when new mobs hit your camp (or when you get a successful taunt on a mezzed mob and you're ready to break mez--taunt then provoke is nice). Otherwise, use it as endurance allows.
Not sure what armor you're wearing, but if i had that weapon set up and 10K plat, I'd be thinking of how to save another 10K plat over the next several levels so I could get better haste sometime in your 40s. You're very apt to spend more on haste pre-level 60 than you are on aggro weapons.
