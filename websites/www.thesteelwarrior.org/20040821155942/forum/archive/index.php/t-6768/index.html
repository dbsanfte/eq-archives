Brenlo and SoE,

Warriors have some legitimate concerns about how SoE might choose to resolve Knight issues in the Gates of Discord expansion.

Many Warriors are concerned that their major class defining ability, Defensive (and related disciplines like Evasive, Stonewall, etc.), may be "shared" with other classes, either as a discipline or a spell equivilant. Some of us believe that while a "burst mitigation" ability like Weapon Shield might be appropriate, Defensive in sufficient amount and duration to reliably tank the most brutal battles, particularly boss mobs, is not.

Some of us are concerned that in repairing any difficulties SoE will forget that they are dealing with three different classes:

Warriors who are unmatched at surviving the most brutal battles and focusing their opponents on them rather than more fragile party members.

Paladins, Holy Warriors who are primarily tanks, but who's other defining characteristic is casting priestly spells to assist in their and their group's survival.

Shadow Knights, Necromantic Knights who are primarily tanks, but who's other defining characteristic is weakening their opponent and assaulting it's abiltiy to survive with sword and spell.

Warriors are also somewhat concerned that their survivability advantages will end up applying only to raids and we will find ourselves to be Knights without Spellbooks in most content.

While we believe the game must be fair and fun to all classes that play it, we are understandably concerned that the unique identity of classes and content where their unique capabilities can be applied is preserved.

Warriors too would like to be aware of changes that SoE is contemplating.
