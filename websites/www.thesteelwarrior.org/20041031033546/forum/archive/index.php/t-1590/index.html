Take your 1k to the bazaar, and search for items with decent ac and lots of + sta and hp for each slot. I'll list some cheap upgrades that I can think of-

Head: Skull-shaped Barbute
Fingers: Platinum or Velium Fire Wedding Rings
Wrist: Get bracers of scavenging instead of the kodiak
Shoulders: Tattered Mantle or Crested Spaulders
Back: Spider fur cloak is nice, or any of the + 40 or 50 hp cloaks out there
Legs: Get crustacean legs. +16 stamina is sweet
Boots: Crusty again, +12 stamina is cool.
Range: Gorange Idol should be < 100pp, and is a great item.
