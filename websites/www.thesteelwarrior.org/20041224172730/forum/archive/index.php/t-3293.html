It depends. If you're raiding, levels outweigh aa's, and you'll be getting the gear you need. If you don't raid, you're better off getting aa's by 60 to offset not having the high end gear. That gives you a chance to get your ac and hitpoints up, and time to acquire the gear before you find yourself a level 65 paper tank.

Then too, you want to take into account who you group with -- if you're always grouped with the same people, and you want to do LDoN with them, you'll need to stay within a few levels of each other. So, if you play more, get AA's to keep near them in level. If you're doing pickups, that's less of an issue.

Honestly, I'd at least get Run 3 now, simply because it's such a help when pulling in dungeons, and regen 3 isn't a bad choice either. Past that, I would go for Natural Durability by 60 at least.

As always, your mileage may vary.
