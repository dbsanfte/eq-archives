There's lot of reasons your friend might not have gotten into whichever guild, two of the most likely reasons are: 1) They weren't looking for warriors 2) They weren't looking for warriors without their defensive AAs finished.

Getting Combat Stability, finishing Lightning Reflexes, and getting Innate Defense should be pretty high on your priority list.

Gear-wise, your weapons arent bad, but niether are really agro weapons. AHB really isnt a bad weapon though, pair it with a Copper Hammer of Striking, Sword of the Bloodsworn, Blade of Carnage etc. and he should do fine.

There's plenty of upgrades you can get, bazar or otherwise, looks like the most needed upgrades are a replacement for Myrmidon Bracer, your Boots, and you Bow.
Calinthalus
If you have friends willing to camp with you, there are quite a few good upgrades to be had. If you're already over 1492AP in NRo, I suggest doing some hards in BB. There's a very nice pair of boots there, "Bloodied Boots of Dark Rites". Other than that, LDoN armor is pretty nice if you aug it correctly......little low on the saves and AC, but much nicer than bazaar bought stuff.

Another suggestion is, if you have friends that can help you camp then, the Secrets of the Planes quest is pretty easy other than the final piece, which is MQ'able. It's not a bow, but it's a good range item with 100hp and a dodge mod.

You might think about putting Anger3 augs on your blades (if you don't have the cash for a heavy agro wep). Or, the GSoT is decent agro with the aug....and you can buy it for pocket change.
