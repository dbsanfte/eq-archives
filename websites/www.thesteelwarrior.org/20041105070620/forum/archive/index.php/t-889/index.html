You're right, it shouldn't happen - someone made an error, or didnt set rampage correctly.

Mobs have two seperate lists, Hate list, and rampage list.

Often times someone can get on the rampage list from healing prior to engagement of the mob (ie. during the pull) and not know it, and won't say anything about it, then the rampage tank will hit the mob or shoot some arrows and wonder why he isnt on rampage.
