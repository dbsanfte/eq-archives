rathsar
Suggest you ditch some of the 'AC gear' and go for hp's.

Chet Bone Shield is not he best, suggest Dire Wolf Hide Cloak for 500pp or so.

Ditch Grif Earring in Favour of Black Sapphire Electrum for more hp's - outlay might be zero or you might even be able to trade.

Face - Grobb Hide Mask is much better and only 200pp on a good day.

Enchanted Dwarven Plate Bracer sells for few hundred plat if you are lucky - temp upgrade for the Scavenging one.

Cobalt Gaunts - sell them and Buy Worked Zathril (around 1K on my server if you are patient) - effect on cobalt gaunts is poor.

Generally as you are no doubt finding out, mobs at high end smack you about whether you have a few more points ac or not and peeps will ask what your HP are far more often.

Good luck with it my fellow Dwarf.

Frostpaw
In regards to the GSoT..max your 2hb ASAP and you will feel like you're cheating when you use it, hehe. Holds agro very very well. At 65 still using in BoT and anywhere else that riposte damage can be a problem. Use on the Bosses in LDoN assasinate missions too with great results.

Aside from gear you can buy, you are a nice level to go to UP and try for some very nice armor upgrades off the Gantru Raldukans. (This will take a little time bro, dont head out there thinking you gonna get half a set of armor in a night. Used to be much easier. Not as easy as camping Elysian, hehe). Note: if you win a Helm, bank it in shared bank slot before returning to UP cause odds are you will see several Helms before a Bp or Legs. You can add to your cash stockpile with any extras. If you get out there with strong group, you can even try tackling Radiir/Spirit of Radiir for Shadowheart-> 11/17 piercer with nice stats that would take an anger aug nicely.

Also great level to go to Vexsar with the right group. That Kylong Darkmail sells nicely, and would provide upgrades for you in several slots.

Finally, keep plugging away at LDoN. Provides some solid drops, cash flow is ok, and never too early to start saving points.
