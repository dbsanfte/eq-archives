Would just like to discuss this idea a bit :p,
because to tell you the truth, I don't really like it.

Yes, multi-tanking is something that hasn't been touched much in this game. And it could of been an interesting path to take for Warriors.
-Warriors being able to control a massive amount of mobs, and survive (would fit class description too!)
-Hybrids being able to uncomparably control one or two mobs at a time.

There are slight problems though.

First of all... when controling a massive amount of mobs, it would be silly to spread out the damage. (except in very few circumstances).
Damage would be focused on one mob at a time... so aggro on the other mobs isn't that critical.

Another problem is the warrior's tankage ability itself...
in ldon groups ive been tanking 2 sometimes 3 mobs as often as possible. Luckily they hit like wimps.
But if you want a multi-tanking tool, it would be for tanking 5, 6 + mobs..
and a warrior's tank effectiveness would need to be tweaked some to support that.
Which would bring us back to square one.. about upping Warrior hp/mitigation blablabla

And dont forget that you'de have to retarget each mob, to introduce into their body the aggro dot.
Leave the mob you were tanking, target another, dot it... what happens to the mob you were tanking? with people hitting on it... they'll certainly get the aggro.
And if you make it so the dot alone, is as effective as a decent Warrior aggro... then it would be overpowered (or seen as overpowered by SOE anyhow).. "Double Warrior aggro just like that?.. never."


There's a nicer way, in my opinion, for multi-tanking. And that already exists partly..
Rampage and AE taunt. Yes they suck bad now, hardly serve of any use.. (except tank switches and the first seconds when theres more then 3 mobs around.. yay).

There was that idea about linking discs and a aggro skill, to a stamina-like bar. (that I've spammed around already way too much :p ).
So imagine linking Rampage and AE Taunt to that...

Want multitanking?? save up your stamina for multiple rampages (use the right weapon, and you've got loads of mobs dotted or nicely aggroed)..
of course, after all those rampages, you'de have little room for a disc.
So if you wanna make a gigantic train and "exploit" multiple rampages with Earthshaker... you can't.. because you wont have room for /disc Fortitude, and .. well.. duh..

Well that's how I see it,
feel free to discuss, criticise, hug etc...
