Dintorr
is warrior a class that needs money to keep him in the line of battle with aggro and damage

The answer is a definite "maybe". If you plan to twink your warrior with top of the line bazaar gear, then yes ... it would be real easy to spend 1M plat or more for top of the line bazaar gear. If you plan to farm all the gear yourself then it won't really be that expensive, just time consuming.

Do you have a regular group/guild that you play with? If so, then as long as your equipment/AA's are suitable for the level of content that your group is doing then you'll be ok. If you plan to start a warrior tomorrow and be tanking GoD content in a month then you will be in for one helluva surprise, no matter how much money you spend.
