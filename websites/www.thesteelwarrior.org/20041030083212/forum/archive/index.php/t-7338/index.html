Mourneblade
I'm pretty new to the warrior class, so don't take my advice as gospel.

Here is a link to what I currently have: Rize's Magelo (http://www.magelo.com/eq_view_profile.html?num=979831)

With this gear at level 57 I have roughly 6500hp and 1300ac raidbuffed (Brells, PoT9, Kazaad and Fo7)

Minus the Fabled BP and the Sting of Betrayal you have a good idea of what 200kpp will buy... Now I don't like to advertise that this is a twink, as I farmed most of these items with my Enchanter and/or guild events... but I did dump quite a bit of plat into him.

For the mainhand/offhand combo I don't feel it's necessary to have 2 proc weapons until you begin taking on larger mobs post 55ish. I would recommend Copper Hammer of Striking in mainhand, and Sword of the Bloodsworn in the offhand. I have ZERO problems maintaining aggro with that combo, and the encite line of discs.

Fierce heraldic items are great AC/HP boosters until you can move into no-drop or LDoN upgrades. Just take a look at the magelo listed above and hopefully you'll have a good idea of where you could stand. I think at 46 I had roughly 5700hp buffed... which isn't bad.

Good luck on your warrior. I really enjoy mine.

I agree with the weapons/armor suggestions.
The other critical item is haste. Since you have the funds, I'd look for at least 31% haste, though 36% would be better. You can't max your haste with 31% items, but it'll be very close and for a while you won't be maxxed anyway because you won't have top haste buffs. So, 31% would be cheaper and do you for many levels, and once you wanted to upgrade, you can always sell 31% haste.
