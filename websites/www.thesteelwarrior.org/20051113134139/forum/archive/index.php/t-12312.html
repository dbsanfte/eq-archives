The imp revenge requirement for concussion blow was lifted in the 1.6 patch. You no longer need to put 3 points (or any) into improved to revenge to be able to buy concussion blow.

Deflection, tactical mastery, and imp charge are arms talents, not fury.

Your build seems fine, yet i do disagree with some of your choices. The 2nd and 3rd points of imp shield block are not worth the points imo. imp sunder is ok i guess if you like to spam sunder armor, but i would have put points in other things. if you took the extra 2 points out of imp shield block and got rid of imp sunder, you could then get 5/5 cruelty, which would net you 5% crit. This extra crit is useful in both pvp and pve (more rage and more aggro).

I tried 13/5/33 for a week. It was ok for pvp, great for pve. I like ms and big reaper crits more though, so i respec'd. I am now playing with 31/5/15 and have no problems at all tanking MC so far, yet i can still hold my own in pvp.
