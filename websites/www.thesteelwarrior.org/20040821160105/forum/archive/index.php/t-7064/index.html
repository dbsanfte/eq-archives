When you speak about things like balance in a game like everquest, you are not comparing apples to apples. You are comparing apples to oranges and attempting to balance them so that they, in the end, are balanced and people want to play both.

You cannot say "in this situation, comparing this apple to this orange, the apple can do this and the orange can only do this, therefore the apple should be jucier" because in a different situation, the orange may have a large advantage on the apple. A good example would be clerics and druids. Druids cannot heal nearly as efficient as clerics, so therefore druids should have healing up'd to the point that they can? No, because clerics cannot solo nearly as efficiently as druids can.

When you are talking balance in a game with so many different characteristics and classes, you have to consider the class overall and its strengths, not just compare its weaknesses to the strengths of a class that is stronger in that area. So you see, soloing ability DOES have something to do with tanking if you want to look at balance overall
