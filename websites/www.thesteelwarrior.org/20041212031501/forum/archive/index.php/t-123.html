(General aa)
First 3 aa should be for
Runspeed 3.

Next
Regen is often the generally accepted next 3 aa points
(personally when i did these i two boxed all the time so i went with 3 points in stamina)

Welcome to Baron.

Natural Durability 1 will cost 2 aa
ND 2 costs 4 aa
ND 3 costs 6 aa.
Warriors are often ranked by their HP
boosting your HP is a very common way to go.

Welcome to Veteran.

Next (Class AA)
5 aa points of AoE taunt.

You may just want to level up a bit.
You gain alot of HP and dps by leveling.

AA's are easier to acquire in PoS, PoV, BoT etc.
and at level 60 you will have a hard time getting a group on most servers.
