Personally...I find merit loot system to be one of the worst loot systems period. For large guilds, it doesn't work fairly, there will always be some sort of favoritism, some sort of human error, you name it, it is one of the most flawed (random probablly being the worst). Having been in a 100+ member Elemental guild, that was strictly on merit system, you could see it pretty clearly. In fact, the guild fell apart due to burn out and constant complaining from guild members, most of the arguments had to do with loot decisions...large discrepencies of people getting choice loots, certain classes even getting very little loot compared to others, the list goes on. I am 99% sure if we were a DKP guild, we would have progressed to Time...instead they fell apart. I realize this doesn't help you, but all that you have said, I would think to leave the guild as it seems they treat members unfairly under their current loot system. Find a DKP guild, things will go smoother imo, sure you still get loot whores, but at least they gathered the points to get said item, you can't really argue with that, just with their ethics.
