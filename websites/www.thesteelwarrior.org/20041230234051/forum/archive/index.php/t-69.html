I have done this 2 times personally.

First time we did this was last year, a couple months before PoP came out. The biggest pain clearing the island except for the guardian and Lord was Guardian has low aggro range (like all of them) but HUGE assist range. We had to off tank him while everyone camped out. Had 17 people.

Most recently we did it and it was alot easier, nm the levels of course.
Before your first pull on the island, get someone to pacify the Guardian. First pull, we got 2 vanquishers and their associated lessers, 2nd pull, last vanquisher and associated. Easy.

Only issue we had is after death of Lord, Sirran pops on his place. So you either need to move Sirrran, or move to a different pull spot. We moved sirran, just get someone, monk works, to hit him from max range, and run like hell. Contrary to first time, I guess we were killing fast enough, Sirran only repopped on death spot 1 out of 2 times.

And yes, be prepared for DT's. One sacrifice on pull, then the first MT will go down about 30s into fight (depending on length of pull from his spawn, 45s between DT's). Should easily be able to kill him in under 90s.
22 DT's in one day is enough for me :(
