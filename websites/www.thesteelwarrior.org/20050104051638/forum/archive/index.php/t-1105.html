Folks, sta is just hitpoints. It is not important that you can cap your STA with buffs. Just look at it as hitpoints. If I can get an item that is -10 sta + 100 hps over what I am wearing now, I'm all over it. Try to max your hps...if that means taking off STA gear for raw hps, do it, even if it means you won't be max STA buffed. Do whatever gives you the most hitpoints. Raw hitpoints are better than STA hitpoints in all cases, but don't spend a lot of time thinking about whether your STA is capped or not, get the items that give you the best max hp number buffed.

Having 305 unbuffed STA is nice to save a buff slot, but make sure you're not losing more hitpoints than you're gaining from STA

This is kind of confusing, but what I mean is, look at your HP number, not your STA number.
