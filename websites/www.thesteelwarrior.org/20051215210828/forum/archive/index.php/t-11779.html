I can confirm that the proc on this weapon is more of a "oh, what a nice surprise" then something I can count on a regular basis. That said, when I was deuling outside of IF last night I had 2 stuns in about 8-10 matches, 3 of those matches lasted over 2 minutes. I also had 2 stuns over an hour-long BG match the same night... it's nothing I'd count on in a 1v1 matchup. The damage on this weapon however is awesome, when Seal of Command procs the damage can be upwards over 800, that combined with regular weapon damage my total 1-hit damage becomes 1k+ - regular hits are around 250 with 350 crits. By far the best weapon for a L50 melee weapon user that I've found. So far I outdamaged a Kang-using warrior and another warrior using the Princess Theradras Scepter.
