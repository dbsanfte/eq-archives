Scarlet Monastery is a wonderful place to pharm in the 40s. learn how to pull the chapel (which is not hard if you're patient) and you will be a blessing to groups. Mograine drops a really awesome shield that complements a Sword of Serenity (from In the Name of the Light quest) nicely. also, Herod drops a rather good 2h axe, perfect for multiple targets at once.

Razorfen Kraul has some good stuff, too. the last boss drops an Icemetal Barbute. last boss in Uldaman - you might need to be escorted by higher level guildies or friends for this - drops a 40ish DPS 2h sword.

it's good that you got some Thorium for the resists and Mithril for the AC, but raw armor is not everything in groups. if you're gonna camp the AH, get Bear items. still though, I recommend you find some spots where you can pharm money and get a mount asap.
