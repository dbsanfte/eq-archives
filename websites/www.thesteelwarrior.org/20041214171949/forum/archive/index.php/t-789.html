I don't know about your server, but on my server (one of the older ones) the low level fighting areas are a barren wasteland of empty camp spots or powerlevelling 2 boxers.

At 36 your best bets to find a group are probably going to be in Gunthak, Dreadlands, Overthere specifically. Most other areas your level (if anything like my server) Will be one desolate place, especially since warriors are so easily 'twinked' out with gear much too powerful for their level. If you're wearing actual gear intended for someone your level you might find yourself critisized by potential groups for no good reason.

I know 36 is probabaly a big accomplishment for you, but if I personally were forced to start over again from level 1 it would be on the breand new 'newbie' server Stromm, or Firiona Vie where you will probably be more likely to find people your level range properly equipped and eager to group.

I did 30-50 on Tholuxe Paells in about two months of casual playing (an hour here and there weeknights, a few hours on the weekends), so here's what I did:
30-38: The Overthere is usually the best place to go, but you can also try the Great Divide or Dawnshroud Peaks, all three places had plenty of groups during primetime (on TP).
38-45: The Dreadlands is your best bet IMO, there's usually at least 5 groups that will take you in. Something else you might want to try is getting a healer to partner with in Dawnshroud Peaks, I got 40-43 in no time killing Large Zelniaks with a shaman
45-47: You can go to Karnor's Castle now, or even just stay in the Dreadlands.
47-50: I just did the Plane of Justice straight through, great xp, never any trouble finding a group, but you can always stay in Karnor's until about 50.
Hope this helps!
