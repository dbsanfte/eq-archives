Not all flags show on your /key feature.

Lower CoD does for instance but many sucjh as PoJ trials do not.

To find out what flags you have, go to see the seer she is in PoK in one of the buildings near the soulbinder.

Hail her (she isthe one sat on the floor) and you can then either ask for guided meditation or have your memories unlocked. Unlock memories is for if you have done flags out of story/plot sequence.

Meditation will give you a list of statements highlighting what parts of the plot hence what flags you have. (some flags wont apear here, so then check /key)

Note; the information seems to appear at least for me as a /tell. So if you have tells filtered, your flag info from meditation appears there.
