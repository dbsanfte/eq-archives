With ER4 and pacify, you should be able to single pull to the zone in as long as you're careful. You will have to be at absolute max casting range with ER4 to avoid prox aggro.

Thanks for all of the advice guys. I found it to be pretty much as described.

The big thing was getting an ER4 item for my cleric so I could split mobs.

I've had a ToD since kunark, I'd be lost without it.

While I *could* duo there to my hearts content, I found I attracted guildies to the zone pretty regularly, so I ended up with a group most times after a short wait. It's amazing what adding a bard to a war/cleric combo will do for your kill rate :)

While I could do the zone in mobs with just self buffs and virtue, I found that my cleric NEEDED crack of some kind. The mobs (even 50% slowed) just do too much DPS to keep up with otherwise.


Buffs I was using were..
Grim Aura
Shield of Flame (blazing vambracers from ragefire) 9pt ds
Call of Sky (orb of sky from VT) 35pt proc buff
Lesser Shielding (cobalt arms) 30hp some AC and MR
Strength of the Kunzar (cobalt boots) to keep my str maxxed.
Miraculous Visions (ornate gaunts) 30% spell haste.
34 pt DS potions
Virtue

I'd pull with the cleric (with Mark of Retaliation-33 point DS), and use ToD till it procced..
I'd switch to prismatic 2h until avatar procced.
I'd switch to Greatstaff of the four winds to finish off the mob.

Unless ToD procced on the first couple of hits, slow wouldn't wear off till very near the end of the fight. I wasn't bothering to re-slow because the loss of DPS didn't make it worthwhile.

76 points of DS outdamaged my melee before the mob was slowed .. by alot.

I was a little worried about cashflow (spending 3k for 100 doses of shammy ds) but the first piece of silk ornate I got took care of that problem for the forseeable future :)
