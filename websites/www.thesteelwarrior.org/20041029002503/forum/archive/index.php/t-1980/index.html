Kromagnon
As a SEWS owner, I will try and add some perspective.

Background: Our guild is utterly cursed with EB weapon drops. 38 AoW kills, 5 BoC (2 on the first, 2 on the last). 25+ Cursed kills, 0 Bloodfrenzy. 30+ XTC kills, 1 Hategiver. First 4 RZTW kills netted no BoW. So when the first SEWS dropped, I jumped at it. Up until last week it was still the only SEWS on the server (one poofed on a rival guild during a zonecrash), while there were 5+ BoWs.

Stats: 35AC 185HPS 48/32 vs 50AC 175HPS 45/30 - its a bit of a wash to me.

Damage: with 1576ATK (base + SoV) and only PB3 for offense I can generate 100-135DPS from the front, and 130-170DPS from the rear. I have just recently purchased Fero3/TM3 so I have no conclusive numbers yet.

Agro: It seems I have to work a little more to establish agro at the beginning of a fight, unless I use AT. But as the fight wears on, I find the agro curve feels smoother. With BoC procs, the agro from EB 'seemed' snappier, but spikier. With SEWS, I feel more confident as the fight goes on. It's definitely got an agro-over-time edge. Once I have solid agro, I almost never lose it. The rune proc will not pull agro away from another tank unless you get the odd '3 procs in 2 rounds' thing.

As the MA/MT on raids, I rarely have a runaway mob problem, with or without VoT. In XP groups, I only struggle occasionally with agro, usually at the very beginning of the fight.

Proc Rate: I parsed one of my more recent logs and came up with the following:

46070 hits
43477 misses +
----------------------
89547 swings
2611 procs

34.2 swings per proc, so lets say 1 in 35 to make it round.

My survivability is generally slightly higher than other tanks in my guild, which has been noticed by the healers, given that in between any two heals, I mitigate 230-690 damage, depending.

Synopsis: I am very happy with my SEWS, although I am a bit of an iconoclast. It is not a weapon for the lazy warrior, it is not a magic bullet. As an MT weapon it has its quirks, but does the job nicely to my tastes. I can farm greens all day too :) .

Do not save up your DKP for this weapon. Don't put it on your wishlist. If you luck into one, awesome, but keep your eye on the BoW. A BoW is not only more suitable for a warrior, it is far far far more common (13-14 RZ kills on server, 5-6 BoW. 40+ Mud ring kills on server, 3 SEWS). Let your knights get this weapon. It keeps them from bidding on BoWs :p .
