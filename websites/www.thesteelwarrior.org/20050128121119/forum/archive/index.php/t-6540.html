Miniature nosering would be an easy upgrade for your face slot. Take a group in to Siren's Grotto from Cobalt Scar, and either run over if ya know the way, or succor over, but get over to the Western Wastes side. From there, go back in the hallway leading from the zone out, take your first left, and follow that to the open area with the pool in the middle. Kill everything in sight. The miniature nosering is the common drop from a fairly common named mob, and enthralled something I think? Instead of looking like the usual Siren's Grotto walrus/elephant mutant mob, these look like tundra kodiaks. If you bring a group in there, everyone in the group should have their own within a few hours.

If you have a single group or 2, go to HoT, and get yourself some gauntlets of the myrmidon.

If you know a smith, or depending on what your server's bazaar is like, a smithed cultural collar would be an upgrade for you, at possibly a very cheap price..fine heraldic if you're human/barb/half elf, or the elven full mithril version for any of the elven flavours.

That, and do as much LDoN as you can. Even if you don't buy the armour from there, the augments you can buy are all well worth it.
