Arathena
I'd go for the flame gilded immediately - It will give you a significant bonus to your damage (and aggro) output, it's not a particularly bad cloak, and, as the real bonus, you don't have to spend raid points / worry about Merit/counciling for it.

Grongl
Flame Gilded Cloak hands down - pretty easy to get if you have a group of folks willing to jump the wall with you when there's a named up. Back in the days of field 2 shakerpage we pulled like 7-8 of em outta there. Put em on a couple new tags, couple twinks, even a few on clerics with shitty cloaks. It's got reasonable stats, it's separate from whatever loot system your guild has, and you can pop a MM 10ac aug on it for 510 pts to make it last longer. There really aren't really any "wow" cloaks from Elementals. It's a good slot to stick haste in til you hit Time.

Haste ring really sucks longterm - locks you into a low AC haste item and ring when there are much better rings to be had.

MM Belt is a myth and guilds rarely do MM again once everybody's flagged.

FMM is a solid item but loses you the basically freebie Puresteel AC. Some guilds have bad luck with them, but we got I think 7 Puresteels out of our first 8 kills. If your guild is still in or has yet to do PoAir farming, hold off on FMM.

Earth bracer eats AC away from Raex and you'll get competition from clerics for the focus.



I'm assuming your guild isn't doing VT - otherwise there are a couple really solid alternatives in there.
