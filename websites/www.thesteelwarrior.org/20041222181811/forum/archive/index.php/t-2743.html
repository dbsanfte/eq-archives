Hi guys,

Just doing some random calculations wrt sustained offensive power and I was wondering if any of you with maximum offensive AA and EP Gods level weapons (or lower) could give a good guesstimate on the possible sustained DPS on the trials in PoTime mobs. It doesn't have to be DPS while tanking, just a general guess as to which band we're talking.

For wizards (which I play now) I came up with a conversion factor of 7:1 (damage:mana including ~10% resists) which I think is not totally unrealistic, accounting for all the damage and preservation foci, specialization and all that. A fully offensive AA'd and geared wizard's sustained DPS would thus approach 140 (120 mana/tick regen). However, the mana-pool acts a buffer that allows the DPS to be stored and released when a mob is in camp (take this into account before you start saying it's too low), but the 140 is what the DPS would be if you had a fight lasting infinitely.

Have some, but not many offensive AAs, and SoL weapons, no PoP... and I'm at around 100 DPS on average in Trials while tanking. When I'm not tanking I average around 110-120 DPS.

Other better equipped wars in guild can get up to 160-190 DPS on some mobs, but I'd put their sustained DPS somewhere more around 150 DPS when not tanking.

When tanking or hitting from the front you loose around 20-30 DPS, maybe only 10-15 with TM3. When Defensive you loose around 40 DPS. I'm not sure how much DPS drops with Evasive.

So even though warriors may be capable of doing decent DPS (Rogues can get around 220-240 DPS, Bards around 70-80 DPS), they rarely if ever maximize DPS because of their duty as a tank.
