Ok here is some parsing from my logs last night in BoT. I was fighting the wussy bees up in the top CY and using dual wield combo of BoC and GBoS. All of the mobs fought were "Stormriders" some of which are clerics, some of which are warriors.

Gartuff Level 65 Warrior all defensive AAs maxed
Number of Fights: 37
Total damage Taken: 165,714
Average hit (not kick or bash) damage: 293.65
Number of slash attacks made by me: 6047
Number of kicking attacks made by me: 245
Number of times my attacks were riposted: 424
Mob hit rate on me: 50.19%
Average mob dps against me: 47.48 dps
Max mob hit: 555
Min mob hit: 162

So Using this we can derive the following

Times I was hit by a riposte: 213 (424 * 50.19%)
Total damage I took from ripostes: 62,547 (213 * 293.65)
Percent of damage I took that was from ripostes: 37.7% (62,547 / 165,741)
DPS I took from ripostes: 18.01 dps (47.78 dps * 37.7%)
Mob riposte rate: 6.7% (424 ripostes / (6047 slashes + 245 kicks))

So in conclusion against a mob with a max hit of 555 I took 37.7% of my damage from the mob riposting my attacks. This seems impressive, but when you convert this number to dps, my ripostes were only helping the mob do an additional 18 dps to me. This is ~1,000 damage per minute just from ripostes.

One other thing to note is that the riposte damage doesn't scale up or down depending on the mob being slowed, the riposte damage only scales up and down depending on the number of attacks that I make against the mob. If I was tanking unslowed bees their DPS against me might be as high as 150+ dps, but I would still be only taking the same 18 dps in riposte damage. In this case fighting unslowed mobs the riposte damage would only be ~10-12% of the total damage instead of the 37.7% I parsed.

I don't have any really good parses of my using just 2h on PoP mobs, but I will try to capture a log of me using my GSo4W and do the same parse. Obviously you are going to take riposte damage while tanking the real question here is how much damage do you take using 2h vs 1h.
