You can get an invis/lev, jump down the pit, and run to him. However, there are undead mobs in the zone that will spawn between you and him. There may or may not be any in the way when you go check, thats a pure crapshoot.

As for how hard he hits, my general rule of thumb is to disregard anything Alla's says and go find out for myself. Usually the people who post that stuff are dress wearers, not defensive warriors, and they almost always overstate it. The last time I tanked him with defensive up max hit was in the low 700s or thereabouts. I would say the majority of hits were in the 400 range. You should be fine tanking him IMO.

The easiest way to get to him is have your map up, have sow, levi and invis. run into the pit till you hit the opposite wall and go into the furthest left doorway. If you do that, chances are pretty good that you will avoid any of the see inviso mobs. Then go in that doorway, take your first left then first right, he is in that room on the south side of the room.

he does hit fast and can take down a 12K warrior in no time. when I did him we had 2 warriors, 2 clerics, a druid and a chanter. We grabbed a charm pet, and went to town, having the druid heal the pet, the other warrior standing back using arrows just in case the first warrior went down. He does AE ramp so have your healers keep their distance too.

Look carefully at the picture on Allakhazam. There are enough clues in that pic to show you exactly where he spawns. Always look at the details of the area around a mob when you want to find its spawnpoint. The border behind him on the wall is actually part of a large map which is on the wall of the Overseer's room. His room also has a single bed, worktable and armor rack. There is a chest at the end of his bed with a sword leaning on it. He spawns between the bed and the table.


Hope that helps.
