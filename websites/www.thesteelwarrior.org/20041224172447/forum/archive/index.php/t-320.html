I recommend high AC/hp/melee stat buffs if you are crazy enough to try and solo. ( I have tried it, I can pick off ltblue rats, phear my leet solo warrior skills~ :p )

Use two-handed weapons to maximize DPS and lower mob damage output. Mob damage output is what will kill you so you need to get rid of as much as possible in any way shape or form. Also DPS is quite frankly what kills mobs so the more of that the better.

Go lightly on the procs: the less you try and use, the less of a grip RNG has over your life. I normally go with two proc weapons ( ToD+Snare whip )

Potions should help you solo allot ( DS some healing/regen and I think a haste one are out there ) but they add up in PP costs over time, something that is scarce in tier one of PoP. So get an 8th ring or better and an eye patch of plunder if you can.

Bandages: You will become one with the mummies if you do not have a click heal BP or you will hate soloing even more.

Soloing is hard.
