I recommend Capn Bry's AddOns (http://capnbry.net/wow/)

Specifically, Weapon Quick Swap and Stance Sets.

WQS basically lets you create macros by using the item name, so you don't have to worry about bag slots. Very flexible but simple.

Stance Sets is click and drag, where you can associate weapons to specific stances. If you want to streamline things, this is the way to go. With my current macros, If something dodges me while i'm sword and board in defensive stance, I can hit one button to switch to battle stance (stance sets automatically switches me to my big 2hander), and tapping the same button again uses the overpower skill.

Personally, I don't use stance sets anymore, I like the control of manually switching between shield-1h and 2hander, cause sometimes I don't want to change weapons when I change stances. But for others it can be very useful.
