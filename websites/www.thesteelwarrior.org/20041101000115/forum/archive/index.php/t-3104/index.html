wandor
The problems with the red epic are numerous. First of all, there is the obvious - the ratio sucks balls and its too slow. You can get better than 14/24 for under 1000pp in the bazaar. Don't forget that the basis for a solid agro weapon is the underlying DPS. If your weapon isn't doing damage to keep the mobs attention between procs, then it's not really doing the job.

Next is the proc itself. Unlike DD+stun procs, the DD+hate only has 1 resist check. When the proc is resisted, it results in little to no agro generation. This is where DD+stun procs have a huge advantage over any resistable proc. Not only does it get the advantage of having 2 resist checks - one for the DD and one for the stun portion, but a resisted stun still generates near if not equal to the amount of hate that a landed stun generates.

Next we have the stats. While the epic does have some pretty decent stats - 15sta 50hp and 5 save all - they are not really all that spectacular when you consider there is no AC on it, the STA goes to waste rather easily once you are capped, and 50hp weapons are a dime a dozen.

Lastly, there is the percieved proc rate. This has been discussed countless times now and other than a few well know exceptions - BF and cleric hammer come to mind - no one has shown any proof other than "gut feeling" that any weapon procs more than the standard 2 procs per minute. As a matter of fact, the only people who have produced parses so far support the theory of 2 procs per minute.

My opinion is still the same as it was before LDoN, only stronger now. The epics are a fashion statement and a status symbol. There are way too many better weapons with better ratios, better stats, and better procs that are easily obtainable by any warrior at epic level.

Why would you want to augment a mediocre weapon when you can augment an outstanding weapon and make it that much better??
