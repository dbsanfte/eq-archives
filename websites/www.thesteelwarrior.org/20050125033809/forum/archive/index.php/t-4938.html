It's unfortunate that some of us can't just answer a damn question. In answer to your question:

Guard 3 procs a buff that lasts 2 or 3 minutes and will act like a rune on 5% of all hits until it takes 50 points of damage per guard point... so 150 for Guard 3.

Spell Guard 3 procs a buff that lasts 2 or 3 minutes and will act like a rune on 5% of all spell damage until it absorbs 50 points of damage per spell guard point... so 150 for Spell Guard 3.

The spell reflecting you are thinking about is Mirror.
