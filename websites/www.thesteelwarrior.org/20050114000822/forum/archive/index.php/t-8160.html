- Grind out Sturd 5
- Fill up all available slots w/ 40hp augs
- Fill up all available slots w/ 10ac augs
- Do lots of Hard LDoNs for: Earing of Sand tides, Mana-Threaded Bone Bracer, Bloody Boots of Dark Rites, Necklace of the Troglodyte, Lost Ancient Elven Ring, Warlord Steel Gauntlets, Cape of Broken enchantments.
- Do GoD trials and try to get: Polished Garnet Loop (vxed), Burnished Antique Greeves (vxed), Bracer of the Heartstriker (tipt) ... a few other nice things here.

You can grind serious aa's while doing both the trials and the LDoN's, and you can also grind out the points for the augments and work on earning gear at the same time while doing ldon. If you were to do all of these things you would have 0 problems tanking in EP's (aside from mobs like reparm where everyone has problems).

I'd imagine you'd put on ~1000 hp or more and a few hundred ac.
