Brudal the Fat
Not sure how your guild will end up handling it but you quickly see phase1 and 2 loot rot. I know you hear people say that about elemental mobs to but in time it becomes true quickly.

So, with that in mind I can think of several upgrades for you in phase 1 that will most likely not be competed for, even for new people to time if they farmed the elementals for a while.

Ring of Force
Pulsing Emerald Hoop
Smooth Onyx Torque

Phase 2 loot you can look at Girdle of Restoration, Cudgle of Wrecking(assuming you dont have a decent 1h dps weapon). Gauntlets of Distraction.

All the upgrades I listed are not real upgrades to elemental armor so competition should be low for you. Normally I wouldn't recommend settling for these drops but in your situation of playing catch up I think its the best route for ya. Otherwise you can bank and save DKP till you can get items you would truely want (read: phase 4 thru Quarm). Depending on your guilds agressivness that could take a month or 5 months, so you have to make the call of what helps the guild the most in the longrun not what makes your magelo look phat. Having HP and AC that can be of use to your guild means more than staying in the top 20 rankings imo.
