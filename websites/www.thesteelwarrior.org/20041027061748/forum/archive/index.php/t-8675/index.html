Culture = player made armor diety armor. Example - Helanic Tundra, Fierce Heraldic, Dark Prince etc. There are also forms of these but with weaker stats available for people who chose to be agnostic or any other.

Tribute is a new thing that I have not taken the time to learn about. All I know is there is a certain npc in your hometown, race specific, that you can give droppable items to for points and those points can be used for effects such as enduring breath or haste.

If you are going to start a barbian warrior I'd suggest buying Helanic Tundra, it is The Tribunal class specific and has great stats. Find someone who crafts it and try to make a deal with them. I bought most pieces very cheap and probably spent a total of 20kish on the whole set.
