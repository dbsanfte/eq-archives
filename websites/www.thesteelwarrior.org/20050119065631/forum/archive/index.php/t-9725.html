First and foremost, I would get rid of the DD augs on my weapons and get anger augs. After that, I'd look for an earring with some raw HP such as another mosquito leg earring or a bloodmetal earring. If you don't have easy access to any money or a way to get it, I might suggest selling off the Blade of Carnage and buying a pair of Droga weapons. Aggro will probably be better than your current setup, and at the loss of a few stats you'll have (depending on server) over 200k to throw around. Use that to get:
-a Bloodmetal Earringx2 or a Fiercely Hooked Claw/Bloodmetal.
-Depending on prices you might be able to have enough to get a Helmet of Rallos Zek. If not, Ornate, Magnetic or a Dented Draconian Helm are all good choices and very good upgrades.
-A new cloak would be nice, too. Whether it's a Conqueror's Cloak or a Shroud of the Fallen Defender (which is great for the clicky alone) you could make up a lot of stats/HPs there.
-Stonewood Compound Bow
-Articulated Battleleader's Gorget
-Mask of the Kuua or a Blazing Eye Skull Mask

Keep your DD augs until you can replace them with hate or stun augs from LDoN or the one that drops in the Bloodfields (there are several threads about camping it here). Other than that your gear looks pretty good. Just work on those AAs/levels some and the gear will continue to get better. LDoN is great if you can get groups for it, and don't forget about OoW expeditions, very good risk vs. reward for drops there.
