Always a good strategy.

My only question though, is ... what is it you plan to spend the loads of cash you have left-over on exactly?

A cloak of flames will remove the need to upgrade your haste for quite some time, thus easing the stress you feel to get that 41 percent haste item if you ever do start "raiding" or killing mobs that drop 41 percent haste items.

Also, it will give you an edge over your standard, pickup group bretheren who on the average run around with 31 percent haste (the soulscream belt is quite the rage these days). Which will, here and there, benefit your DPS and aggro capabilities.

I'm not saying it's worth the 110 k.

I'm just saying, it's worth considering what it is you want out of your 110k.

Haste is a good investment. The cloak of flames is still one of the top notch haste items in the game, and will provide you with some palpable benefits.

If you've got a huge shopping list of items you want to upgrade or spend plat on, then yes, the save your platz route is probably the way to go.

But if you're pretty happy with your gear, and that 78k or so would just sit there in your bank, not being spent on anything ... then why not splurge and get the better haste?
