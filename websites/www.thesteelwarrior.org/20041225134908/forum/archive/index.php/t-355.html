Get some Black Sapphire Earrings for the HP - roughly 400pp each
Get a better shoulder slot , quest the draconic pauldrons, easy.
Buy a Clattering Bone Neck - 4k or so, cheap.
Replace Cobalt with a Silver Chitin Bracer - 5k.
Quest the Eyepatch of Plunder, easy for 1 group to do, Free.
Buy ANY boots/arms/gauntlets that have >30 HP and you can afford.
Get the Rubicite Girdle, major upgrade.

Do NOT put on AA XP , it's a waste of time in the low 50's.
HP > AC for XP grinds. If you're not raiding and getting smacked on by level 65 mobs you'll do fine with any AC over 1000.
Your LEVELS will improve your AC drastically, don't sweat it.
