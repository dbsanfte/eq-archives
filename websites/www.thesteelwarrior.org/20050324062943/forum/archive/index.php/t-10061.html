My estimates are done with the following assumptions:

You get hit 50% of the time
having 10 avoidance makes you get hit 1% less (i.e. 49% instead of 50%)
You have 10K HP buffed (easier to calculate).
A mob's DB is 1000 (which is actually common on ubers)
You can take 4 hits before dying (because there would not be any point to tank anything if you can't).

Now although DB = 1000 obviously doesn't apply to trash, but most ubers don't have any different min/max distribution compare to the average trash so the same estimation still applies. For example a trash might have 1/5th the DB as an uber but you can probably take 5 times as many hits before dying as well.

With these numbers you get 1% shielding = 40 HP, 1 avoidance = 20 HP.

And both effect become more significant as you get more HPs and as you stack them.
