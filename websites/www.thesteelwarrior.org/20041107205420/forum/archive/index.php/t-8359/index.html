Just wanted to throw this in.. All the above are good suggestions and by all means adhere to them. As far as maintaining aggro goes, it is the entire groups responsibility to help the Tank Maintain Aggro. You can only do so much. If a caster is over nuking or a Knight is stunning during the fight (For example) then they are NOT doing their part in helping you maintain aggro. It can be frustrating at times believe me but there will be times when others are only making it more difficult for you. If I am the MT and I see another group member with an aggro proc weapon I ask them to replace it with a non procing wep if possible. If you get things straight from the start then usually the others will do what you ask. Some on the other hand take it personally and wont help out. Don't ask me why. I weild the Sword of the Bloodsworn (Pri) and Copper Hammer of Striking (Offhand) The stun on the Bloodsworn works on mobs up to level 65, Hammer up to level 55. For a two hander I use the Greatstaff of Thunder. GSoT Stun proc also works on mobs up to lvl 65. For a non-raiding (PoP/GoD) warrior, these have done real well for me.
