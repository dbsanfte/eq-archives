This is my advice, given what I've read and info I got for a similar question:
Avoidance > Mitigation > HP (not absolute, but generally this is true, if you're not getting one-rounded or two-rounded).

Avoidance will allow you to not get hit possibly saving 300 - 1k+ points of damage and saving cleric mana and keeping you alive longer.

Mitigation means when you are hit, it won't be as hard, again, you will probably save more hitpoints of damage this way than you can add HP through AA alone (presuming it's not a trivial mob).

WARNING: These examples do not use actual improvements and results may be somewhat exaggerated versus reality.

Say if you're a 8k tank and you are fighting GoD Sewer Plant mobs with no hp aa, avoidance aa or mitigation aa. Chances are (without a real high AC), well before the GoD revamp for knights, you would be getting hit for near 1k damage a lot. Let's say that is roughly 4 rounds (including a few misses here) and your dead (without a heal).

If we add let's say, 1K HP from most of the HP AA you can get, that's what? 1 more hit or less than 1 round.

If at max avoidance and you can miss a 1K hit every other round, that's like adding 2k HP in the original scenario of 4 rounds til death or a good chance you'll now survive at least 5 rounds.

At max avoidance and mitigation you are now getting hit on average for 75% damage, you might now last 6 - 7 rounds.

Add the HP aa and maybe it's quite likely you'll last the full 7 rounds.

WARNING: All numbers here are make-believe and no one should expect these numbers to be realistic as compared to EQ. It is just an attempt to explain that although everyone in game looks solely at how much HP a warrior has, that argument is only valid if they all have maxed their defensive AA.

There are also numerous other threads will you find more specifics, like if you are tanking mobs that proc something nasty (3k+ lifetap, etc.) that avoidance is best.

If the proc nothing and have a really high atk value, mitigation might be better.

If you are dying in one round and 500 hp will let you live through first round or you are not ma and dying due to dots a lot, then it might be best to max hp.
