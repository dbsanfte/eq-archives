Here is Bernlo's Q&E ( Question and Evasion)


Some of these have a wizard focus, but there are enough general questions that I thought I'd post it here. Except for two or three questions, these were all submitted by players on Graffe's.

1) Any eta on adding new elemental level LDoN armor? I am trying to
decide whether to save points or continue purchasing upgrades.

Hehe this is a nasty rumor going around. We do want to look at adding
more items to the adventure merchants, but if/when we do it will not be
elemental armor.

2) Any more information on when we will see GoD legs/chests looked at
for possible retuning?

I would imagine this could happen after Omens

3) Any plans to implement a guild alliance tool in order to improve
comunication and coordination between allied guilds?

Something I will pass along to the team.

4) Will Research tradeskill ever see a trophy?

Probably will not happen as it is a skill you can use training points to
max the skill out. The team would like to have some reward though and
will evaluate what that may be.

5) Will Resarch ever be updated to research current spells?

Completely depends on the spells, and if we see a need for them to be
more common. This probably won't apply to no-drop and ancient spells.
(Per Maddoc)

6) Will we receive new pillar spells in Omens? (They have
been on our wish list since Kunark - you can find our old
wish list from then on caster's Realm asking for some)

Spell lists are not final for Omens so I really cannot say just yet.

7) Why does AA work on right clicks for shaman, necros ( ie thier dot
pants can crit each tick), and every buff class in the game (ie their
right click buffs, maelins methodical mind, maelins meditation, form of
endurance and the like from time) lasting nearly twice as long. Yet when
wizards right click a robe, we can not crit.

I am not sure why this would be. I will pass it along to QA as it may
be a bug.

8) Would like to know the possibility of having the buff
timer on pet buffs. Would like to know the possibility of
the ability to click off buffs at will (for pets).

Buff timer is a possibility and I will forward it over to our coders.
It might be something tied to leadership or an AA though (that is just
me supposing)
Removing pet buffs, I do not see happening soon.

9) Any chance of having voice graft added to our familiar?

As it would require some code changes (familiars are treated differently
in code than standard pets) it will not be happening anytime soon.

10) What other zones are in the pipe for revamping (not in appearance,
but for content), and will the majority be done similar to VP (raising
the level of the zone) or simply changing the content around a bit?

For now we are turning our focus to Omens of War. I would look for
revamp announcemens after it ships. I think you will see a varied
approach, some levels will be beefed up while others are changed to be
more appealing.

11) Any plans to instance any old content? The emp in Sraa
is quite annoying for smaller guilds trying to get into VT.
His loots are decent enough larger quilds kill him alot for loot and
smaller guilds have a difficult time getting people the rifts they need
for VT.

No plans at this time. It would be a massive undertaking though.

12) Any plans to reduce spawn time on older content?

Not currently.

13) Any plans to change wizard resists in GoD? (Our only big nuke is
fire but all the major bosses have high FR with low
CR/MR)

Not currently but it is something the team can re-evaluate after Omens.

14) Can they add a ceiling to zones they felt were exploited
by Grav Flux and un-nerf it? (Grav Flux no longer fluxes you
- it was nerfed to stop people jumping over walls in PoTime)

That would require re-building the zones. We may do this when the zones
are eventually updated to the new graphics engine, but it's not going to
happen anytime soon.

15) Is it possible to add a raid option to the LFG tool, so that if you
are waiting for a raid invite you can put /lfg on, and click the raid
button in the LFG tool, This would allow you to keep the /lfg tag above
your head but would not include you in the LFG list when other players
use the LFG tool?

Interesting idea. I will see what the guys here think.

17) What is the status of single player LDoN or similar type adventures

None coming anytime soon. I think the task system in Omens may fill
this role though.

18) Where does the Plane of Sky stand on your "list" of
zones in need of a revamp?

We have not plotted out revamps just yet so please stay tuned.

19) can you reveal any information about the Omens Task
system?

Not yet, but I think you will be pleasantly surprised.

20) Would it be possible to add a tradeskill path to the solvents?

This is something under discussion.

21) Does Firiona Vie have any regrets over her breast
reduction surgery? =D

She had surgery?

23) How does the team (i.e. Rytan) feel about some form of Group
Harvest?

No idea, but I will ask him.

24) What is the possibility of seeing Portals added to some
GoD zones - particularly Barindu and Qvic (Reminder that
Nexus was the only port at beginning of luclin and then more were added)

It is always a possibility =)

25) Lately there has been a lot of threads about
solo-content. What sort of plans are there in the next 4-5 months for
some sort of alternative for progression than grouping?

The Task system! The system will be a fun alternative to grouping for
players with short play times. The task system initially will be for players
of 1st to 50th level, but wil be expanded to all levels after launch.

Thanks again Brenlo!
