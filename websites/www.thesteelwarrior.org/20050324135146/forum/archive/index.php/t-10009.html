For a brief time they removed the PoGs, but then necromancers started soloing there for great experience and cash, so they were then put back into the game. Having said that, If you were able to initially take them out (which I think you would) you would also need to vigilantly time the respawns (45 minutes I think?) to ensure you aren't fighting a mob when they respawn. That might get icky. Furthremore I believe you commented about wanting something that was relatively stress free? This may not be the way to go.

You could also pharm Kael Arena, take lots of bags and vender the trash weapons, bazaar the heads and toes. If you get lucky with a BP, they MQ fairly well for the Grand Robe of The Oracle quest (the Phylactery will get you nowhere quest).

You can also duo in Harbinger's Spire, stay away from the Monk epic fellow, but there are lots of trash nameds there that my wife likes to solo when groups are lacking, thus I'm sure you could duo them easy. Loot is probably the cash equivalent to what you might get for SCHW, not sure how trash loot sells though, but you'll also make experience > velks.

Really though, the money is in the tradeskills. I used to think that 5k/hour was great, but when you start pumping out high quality tradeskill items (alla elemental tradeskill stuff - yes I know you're shammy isn't EP, just using this for a reference point) that still sell for 75k+ a shot, it's tough to think in lower terms. There are some neat tailored tradeskill armors for silk / leather wearers with stuff that drops in Nobles, that sells fairly well - and speaking of Nobles, you could duo most any trash named there for runes and good droppable loot. A druid ft2 waist slot drops off the named Bazu in the SE corner of the map that sells for around 100k, would be easy-peasy for you to camp also. While there you could pharm bloods and hides, either use those to make armor or sell them for decent plat. Something for some rogue poison drops there also that I believe sells fairly well. Just steer clear of Rock Thrower (another named Bazu but he'll rip you up).

My ranger friend used to solo Rivervale guards and make ~300 plat every 15 minuteish, you could do that solo without even your shammy. For that matter you could do the same with a trip through soldungeona.

I'll try to keep thinking up more stuff. OH - you could solo the zone in area of Plane of Hate very easily, and turn in all nodrop old school armor for plat gems in poK.
