Here's the thing...

From 1-20 you have if I'm not mistaken... a damage cap of 30 points per successful hit. You have no damage bonus in the mainhand. So, what you would look for before level 20 for REAL Min/Maxing is the fastest weapon that can hit that damage cap.

From 20 to about 40ish, oh.. 45 ish is where stupid twink warriors make their biggest errors. They think that they can grab a lamentation and that it's actually a good weapon, or that they can grab some low delay weapon and it's meaningful.

It's not.

Until you reach a damage bonus of around 10, your BEST bet is to get the highest damage weapon you can get hold of with the lowest delay you can find... in THAT order of importance and buff up your Atk via Strength and +atk (i.e. Gobbie Earring and Woven Giant Beards).

It's not until you actually get a consistent damage bonus with mainhand that low delay weapons become meaningful. Keep right clicking that 1h weapon that you have, when the damage bonus is 10... then you should start using the low delay stuff. Until then, high damage > low delay

An easy way to keep your skills maxed is to make sure you have your primary and sec slots and a couple of inventory slots hotkeyed in your hotkey bank. then you can swop in and out of weapons mid fight without having to open ur inventory. Some of the new UIs out there have customised inventory slots which allow this easily, so you may want to consider getting a UI if you havent already got one.

Also, no point having 2 1hs weps. Keep one in primary, then rotate a 1hp or a 1hb in your secondary. That will ensure that you get skill ups across the board. If you find any skill lagging behind, put that wep into primary for a bit.
