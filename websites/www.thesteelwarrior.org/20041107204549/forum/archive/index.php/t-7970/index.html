No key required for inner Acrylia anymore, and yes, it is fairly decent XP at a low difficulty. Getting to the clicky to inner Acrylia is a bigger hassle than actually pulling and fighting once inside.

For another alternative of similar difficulty to inner Acrylia & Chardok B, try Nadox. For a tougher challenge (but still easier than PON), try Torigiran Mines or Sol C.

You will need a snare whip or root in Sol C and CT - that is the main reason why those two zones are less utilized than the others mentioned here.

Sassinak
Sol C (the Caverns of Exile) is attached to Sol A and B at the bug/bat zone line. You end up at opposite ends of the zone depending on whether you entered from Sol A or B. Note that in Sol C, the lava hurts about 4x as much, and you have to survive a short swim once you zone in.

The mobs at the entrances are like "normal L65 LDON" mobs, and they get more like POI mobs as you go deeper. There are several ring events that you can trigger, and there are quests that you can do in the zone, such as a quest for a souped-up cloak of flames.

I have attached 3 maps to this message, showing you how to get to Sol C, plus a map of Sol C itself.
