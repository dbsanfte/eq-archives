I finally got a chance to go up and take a look around. I must say I am VERY impressed with what I saw.

The zone is absolutely beautiful. They spread out the 1st floor a lot, now there is room to crawl. They also fixed the pathing and wall agro issues as far as I could tell. Not one single suprise train all night. The trash mobs where still pretty easy, but I suspect they are well tuned fora level appropriate raid. It seems like they are targeting the 58-63 crowd in a raid situation.

Maestro of Rancor is stout. Don't go up thinking you are going to do this guy with 2 groups, not gonna happen. I won't completely spoil it, but itis now a ring style event. He does quad for 1100 and he is immune to slow. Spells do land if you pick the right element and controlling the spawns as the event progresses is super important. Loot was very good for the effort. You can see the INT caster pants and killer 1hs that dropped in the thread I posted in the Bunker.

Unfortunately we started our mini raid at 1 a.m. so we weren't able to get to Inny, but we will certainly be going back to see how hard he is now.

Overall SOE did a great job with the changes I have seen. Hate is now a viable raid zone and not nearly the PITA it used to be.
