Ok I added a guard 2 to a weapon finally just to goof around with it, some obeservations:

1. Subjective observation: there appears to be no agro component with this proc. I saw no difference in MOB behaviour when this proc'd with myself not already having agro.

2. Subjective observation: the mitigation from the proc buff appeared measureable (i.e it appears that it was a straight pct mitigation off each hit taken). I did not parse it so I can't give you "hard" numbers....I eyeballed a stream of hits both with and without the proc up and saw a difference in damage taken. Perhaps if I'm not too lazy I'll pull some parses down with and without the proc up.

3. Subjective observation: by all previous wording of this affect I would have expected it to mitigate a certain %damage until a cap is hit and then have the proc buff vanish. During the fights in which I used the weapon with the proc I never lost the icon...makes me curious if there is a straight time duration on the effect rather than a damage cap that it can take before dissipation (aka it doesn't appear to work like a rune).

Ok these are straight up subjective observations...not empirical aka "hard" but probably better than nothing (which exists to date on this subject here). Take them with a grain of salt and if dissatisfied spend 500 pts and buy your own guard 2 to parse ;-)
