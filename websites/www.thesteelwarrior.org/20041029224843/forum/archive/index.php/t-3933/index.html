I'm a long way from being able to afford the equipment I'm listing here, but I'm patient and would like opinions on approaches to soloing at this level:

I'm thinking that equipment-wise, you would be looking at Fungi Tunic, Truncheon of Doom, 35% haste item (CoCW) and Kizrak's Gaunts (or possibly the Eyepatch of Plunder). I'm thinking that bind wound, in general, is not going to be that helpful, (i.e. - it's not worth putting AA points into increasing the base percentage you can heal) because it's too slow, relatively speaking.

I'm thinking that because of that, total HPs are not that important - if you have enough to safely survive the mob you're fighting, then the only thing that matters is trying to minimize the number of hp's you lose during the fight, and minimize the time it takes to get them back.

I'm thinking that high Dex would be important - to get the Truncheon to proc early if nothing else.

I don't really know what level of AC to aim for. If I'm fighting the lowest old-world dark blue mobs I can find, how much AC is going to be useful? I just don't know.

Bonequest
When you have 201 + in BW, you can heal to 70 percent. When you get the lev 51 first aid AA skills, you get 10 percent more per level so 3 AA spent will allow you to go to 100 percent. Also, this allows you to bind wound others to 100 percent. It is very nice to help the ocasional newbie you see and kind of satisfying to watch one bandaide take them from 10 percent health to 100 percent.

The level 59 BW AA I don't really know how much it helps since I dont have it and there is a PoP AA as well that adds to it. I think those just increase amount per BW attempt so they are more of an efficiency thing but still probably useful if you solo a lot.

You also will probably want to squeek in as much regen as you can. Look to get all the regen AA, as many aura of battle and regen items as you can. I don't have one but I bet a fungi tunic helps a lot. It may not seem like a lot but just the 6 regen/tick from AA points gives you an extra 60 hp in a 1 minute fight and 300 hp in a 5 minute fight and could mean the difference between you soloing light blues and barely dark blues. I have 19 standing regen now and I don't have the level 55 regen AA or a fungi so its not hard to get up around 25 to 30 standing regen a tick at level 65. Ornate arms are 3, vindi BP is 2, spirit wracked cords is 2, stonewood bow is 2, belt of dwarf slaying is 2, fungi tunic is 15 I think, and 6 from AA is also possible.

To skill up BW, get a bunch of bandaides and go to lavastorm and stand in lava to get hit points down to around 100 then get out of the lava and bind back up until skill is over 201. This takes just a few hours to max the skill so its not too bad. Also, get an enbalmer's skinning knife which has a proc that summons a stack of bandaides. Very useful if you solo.

I partially soloed for many levels (when I was around 51 to 54) when I was bored but it is so very slow. You may not want to do it very long (especially if you don't have a lot of hit points unbuffed) but if you can't get a group it is at least something to do to pass the LFG time.

Also, I used the PoG BP back in my soloing days and bandaides plus regen are way better than any 15 second clicky bp so don't even bother with one unless you already have it. If you have a cobalt bp, probably it would be better to sell it and get a stonewood bow then you get good hit points and some regen.

Was solo'ing parts for my VP key. (yah i know. don't go there). and the KC basement camp was better than I imagined. Area marked with 1 on EQ Atlas KC basement map.
