try west ToV, the named there are doable with about 18 and drop some pretty nice stuff, also might want to try Eashan and Ikiatar, they drop really good stuff for the encounters you will be able to face. Vindi is a must for your tanks, and you could even start workin on sleeper's tomb by taking down Zlandicar and Klandicar, both doable with 3-4 groups.

Once you get two groups flagged for sleepers you can continue working on keys for rest of guild, as well as taking down the nameds in there for your folks that have already gotten keyed. Avatar proc weapons are great dps increasers for a melee heavy guild, or even one that isn't melee heavy... especially for your high dps melee classes.

From there you could probably start doing Rumblecrush/Doomshade in umbral plains, as well as Va'dyn/Insanity crawler and even Itrar Vius eventually in akheva. Servitor of luclin in grieg's is a fun encounter, and can yield some pretty nice stuff as well.

From there you will start to attract more people, and will build a bit, head to NToV, and then work from there.

I hate to give away my secrets, but here it is.

Spend some time at the enforcers/punishers camp in PoJ.

Exp - good. Cash - around 1K an hour, maybe more. A few nights there and many people can have decent resist gear.

There's also some cheap ways to up your resists for HoT. Runed bone fork is one example. Crown of the froglok kings also adds some big numbers to key HoT resists.

Really, though, the best way to get resists in HoT up is to have a bard singing the right songs. Really good gear can't match the improvement in resists you get from the bard songs.

And remember, many of the mobs in HoT aren't AoE. There are enough that can AoE so that it's plenty annoying, but there are many that don't.
