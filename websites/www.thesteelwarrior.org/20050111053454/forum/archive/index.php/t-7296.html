Since a shaman with the Cleave1 bracer from Tipt can crit on melee, when they couldn't before, it indicates it's a fixed number increase on chance to proc rate, rather than a % increase.

Mighty Strike Discipline
Slot Description
1: Chance to Critical Hit(10000)

Indicates they use a 1-10000 scale for chance to crit, 10000 being 100%

Cleave I
Slot Description
1: Chance to Critical Hit(20)

3: Chance to Critical Hit(20)
4: Chance to Critical Hit(20)
5: Chance to Critical Hit(20)

8: Chance to Critical Hit(20)
9: Chance to Critical Hit(20)
10: Chance to Critical Hit(20)
11: Chance to Critical Hit(20)

As I understand it, those repeated values are meant as blockers rather than additions, hence per 10000 hits, 20 of them will crit as a result of the bracer, or you will crit on 0.2% of melee hits as a direct result of this bracer, but this DOESN'T stack with crit enhancing discs such as the rogue disc: Twisted Chance.

The direct % ones listed above however are direct reported %s, so I'm guessing your above assumption is correct.
