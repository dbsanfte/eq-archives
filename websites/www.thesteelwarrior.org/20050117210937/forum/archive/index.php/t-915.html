Depending on your situation ($$) there are plenty of choices for you.

If you have the money, hands down the best newbie (twink) item is Wavecrasher. Procs at level 1 and is a 20% slow and 20hp DoT for 10 ticks. But it also costs ~12-15k on Xegony. Also adds +9 to AGI and some AC.

If you are like most newb warriors (much better than a twink like me) and can't afford toys yet, go with either Centi (SS or LS) - you can get the SS for ~200-400 and LS for ~1500-2200 on Xegony. I lucked out with my Bard when he was only in his 30's and got a Centi Longsword for 1k! :-) These weapons are NOT lore either, meaning you can wield both at the same time post DW.

You may also want aggro management - lots of choices there. Black Blade of Tormenting, Barnacle Covered Axe, etc.

Whatever your choice - good luck in your decisions, and update the boards with your findings. These boards are resources to the newer EQ players as well as the experienced.
