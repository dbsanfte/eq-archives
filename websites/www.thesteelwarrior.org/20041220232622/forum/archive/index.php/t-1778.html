Here is what you need to make a ring:

Combine a brick of valorium and a vial of distill mana in a Tanaan forge (any forge in PoK) to make an enchanted bar of valorium.

Give an enchanter a metallic liquid and a raw diamond, have him cast imbue valor. He should handd you back a yellow diamond of valor. (no fail on this)

Now take your imbued valorium, the yellow diamond of valor, and a razorfiend talon and combine them in a Planar jewelry kit to make the ring.

The trivial on the ring is over 250, so I would highly recommend that you have an enchanter with Jewel Craft Mastery do the final combine for you.
