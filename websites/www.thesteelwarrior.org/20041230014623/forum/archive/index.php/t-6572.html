the amount of ac gained per amount of agi scales on the current amount of agi. for instance, if you have 50 agi, you will gain much more ac per agi then if you have 290. by looking at these figures and forming a graph, there is a plateau at around 150 agi. after that point the amount of ac gained by boosting agi is very minimal.

as far as agi going to an obscure "avoidance" form of ac, i doubt it. sony has already stated that each class type (silk, leather, chain, plate) has a set basic mitigation and avoidance. each class then has a modifier that sets its place among those armor types. this is why a silk class with 1000ac will not mitigate as much as a plate class with 1000ac. this is also why many classes avoid better then warriors. in addition to this, different mobs have different hit percentages. GoD mobs hit a lot more then PoP mobs.
