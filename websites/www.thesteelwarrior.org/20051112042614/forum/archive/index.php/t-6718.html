1st thing. I've seen 3 posts from Karubi here and all three have been full of utter garbage. Do not believe a word out of his mouth and you'll be doing yourself a favor. Closer to 40-60% of your exp will come directly from quest turn-ins. Less if you do harder content/mobs.

2nd thing

In case anyone missed it. The big problems with the rest state were twofold. It took 8 hours to go from ANY state to well rested. This was intentional and a poor design decision. It's being changed to incremental. Whether that means 2 hours flat per state, or 1 minute of rest = 1 minute of rest no matter how long there I don't know. The other problem was the amount of exp to drop a state is static through all levels. 20,000 I believe. Sure it might take you 4 hours to drop to Rested at level 10, but I routinely get down to Normal in 4-5 hours now easily at 30+. Faster if I do an instance or fight lots of + mobs. This is also being changed, the amount of exp to drop a state is going to scale.

Really that's the only 2 issues I ever had with the system, and they should both be getting changed. So whoopie.
