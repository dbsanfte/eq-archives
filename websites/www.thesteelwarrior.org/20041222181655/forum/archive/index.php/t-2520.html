Like Tugurok said, it is going to take you roughly the equivalent of 10aas worth of experience to get to 65. For that amount of exp you are getting higher stats caps, a better taunt button, better chance at resisting spells/AEs, lower agro radius for pulling and safer travel, and more base HP. You could buy AA skills to achieve the same thing (for more than 10aa I might add), but the better chance to taunt and lower agro radius are only available by levelling.

Level > AA
