Work on the Plane of Hate Marsinger Gem quest if you don't feel like dishing out the cash for a Stonewood Compound Bow (http://www.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=19096). I'm nearing the completion of the second melee gem and already have the book for the 3rd.

Mind you, though I'm not 100% sure, the PoH quests can no longer be MQ'd. Some say they can't, some say they can, but if I were you I would not take the chances.

Pieces for the first gem all drop of mobs near the zonein. Bring a couple of guildies and AE the stuff. It's pathetically easy.

The main piece for the second gem, called the Etched Soldier's Crest, drops off the dark elves located near Innoruuk and west of Maestro's cathedral. More specifically, you must fight the Rogue/Warrior dark elves (Agents, Assassins, Warlords, etc). These guys are levels 62 to 64, have ~20/25k hp, and triple anywhere from 540's to 620's depending on their level. You will be able to tell their level by how much they hit (62 - 540's, 63 - 580's, 64 - 620's). The good thing is - They do not run, they do not gate, they do not summon, and give damn good exp.

You will need the Dissertation of Dark War in combination with the 2nd gem to complete the 3rd gem quest. Some of the minis drop the Dissertation of Dark War, but I'm not sure exactly which ones. By look, I would imagine Overlord R`Gahbsa (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=12194) and Grandmaster R`Tal (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=6627) do. I'm positiveMaestro of Rancor (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=1596) drops the Dissertation of Dark War because I just looted it off him last night.

The rest of the pieces drop off various minis located throughout PoH in order to finish the 4th gem though.
