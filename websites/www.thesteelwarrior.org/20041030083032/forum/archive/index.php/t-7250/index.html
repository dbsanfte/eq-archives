Keane Abrams
It just applies to the original weapon's innate proc, a weapon's increased proc rate will not affect the augment proc rate. Clerics have augmented their hammers and they do not get an increased proc rate.

PPM is an average. Go parse 8+ hours on the Katta banker, count up the procs and divide by the number of minutes. At 305 dex you will get ~2.0 ppm unless the weapon has a proc mod.
