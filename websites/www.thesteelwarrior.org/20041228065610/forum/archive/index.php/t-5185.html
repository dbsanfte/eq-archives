Someone has probably posted this before, but I have seen others that haven't thought about it so....

If you are having problems of occasional splats on tank switching or heavy hitters like General Durav in fire or RZ try this with the new disc system.

Hit incite, and furious and Ae Taunt when you get the mob. This should give you enough agro to hold it while the mob is being slowed and if you are lucky one of your riposte will proc EB or what ever you may be using.

Watch Furious carefully and get your mob positioned quickly. Once furious is down get onto your next disc (my pref is stonewall if mob is positioned right, def if not).
