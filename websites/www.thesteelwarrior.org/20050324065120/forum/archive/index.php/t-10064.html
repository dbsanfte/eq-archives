Each mob you kill in the quarter of the zone they spawn in has a chance of triggering the Hammermaster and the Brawler. So for the Hammermaster make sure you pull the hallway to Rondo and the other mobs outside the room. Brawler is the same. Spearmaster and Blademaster seem so easy to get because you have to kill so many mobs to get to their rooms, thus they are often up and waiting for you by the time you get there. Hammermaster and Brawler take a bit more effort, but once you pull enough around them they will eventually spawn.
