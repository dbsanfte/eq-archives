First off, definitely shoot for a DBoTW. An ED is pretty much worthless, EoE is something worth picking up, and even a Vanazir is a nice weapon if you really dont think you have a shot at a DBoTW ( but really that is what you should shoot for ).

Imp Parry 3: Forlorn Mantle of Shadows
