This is an attempt to answer the frequently asked questions involving gearing and leveling a Warrior (levels 1-65). This is aimed for those wishing to powergame, skipping content to get to the famed raiding content that EQ has to offer.

44-51 The Hole. (low atk, good for porcupine tactic, good xp bonus)

55-62 PoValor Inner Cavern. (very tough, but very rewarding)
AAs PoValor Inner Cavern (pause at level 60 to work on those AAs)
62-65 BoT Earth Wing (familiar area, good xp for the effort)

First post here so be kind

My experience soloing my Vah Shir Warrior to 41. (created before the tutorial and left at level 9 for a long while). He was created to do the Warrior part of the Sonic Wolfbane quest for my shaman main but I'm having more fun with him than my main right now.

1-3 in the pit in Shar Vahl
4-7 in Shadeweaver's Thicket
7-24 Various sections of Paludal Caverns (Just say no to bandit camps though)7-12 SWT side bugs
12-16 HM side bugs
17-24 HM side Shrooms and Shik Nars
24-32 Marus Seru (easy singles and don't hit hard, decent money from the LQ hides too)
32-41 Dawnshroud Peaks (again easy singles and don't hit hard, Loot blows)

As a Vah Shir I cannot use any of the cultural armors so I really start with a handicap. Some Bazaar bargain hunting really helped recently though.

I found the best twink item I had was the Ceremonial Iksar Chestpiece (can't use a fungi as Vah Shir). Up to 41 the regen easily surpasses the higher AC of anything else you can get at that level. Also have a Grimiling Contraption, a Darkwater Pearl Ring and a Brazen Earring of Health(seems a rare item). My Bind Wounds skill sucks since I rarely get damaged to less than 70% by any dark blue MoB and regen brings it much of it back before I can get the next kill back to my pull spot. Filled in with a lot of Deep Cavern stuff my main made for the Dex boosts (always thinking of skill-up rates).

The regen is running out now though but I think it will last me until the Archtect's Bejewelled Chestplate is worth the 4kpp they are going for on my server.

As for weapons I really struggled early on with some nodrops from the Hollowshade Moor War and Grimling Forest. The Greatsword of Grimling Slaying is pretty decent up to the 20s but the 46 delay starts to make it hard to keep the skill up after that. Used a Skull Shaped Sacrificial Hammer and a Runed Mace of the Moor for Blunt skill ups. Once the Hammer started to proc at 25 it noticibly upped my life expectancy (a 50 point damage absorption proc came in real handy at 25).

Otherwise I went with fast weapons early on to get skills up - A Lamentation, Shissar Warsword, Obsidian Shard, Shaded Dagger and Blood Point were good until the mid 30s when thing started to look up.

Probably better stuff out there but this is what got me through the low end nicely.
