Worried about riposte and not get ambidexterity is like not using a haste item because mobs will riposte you more often when hasted.

Ripostes are something to worry about but it doesn't make sense to do less damage just so you don't get riposted. The best overall DPS skill is probably either Tactical Mastery or Punishing Blade, but if you're not using a 2H the latter is of no use to you. The former is only useful in XP groups unless you happen to MT a lot too (sure you're not there to do more damage, but more damage is still more damage). Also Tactical Mastery is actually less effective against easier enemies (because they don't use special defenses very much to begin with) so you want to fight fairly high level (low 60s) mobs to get the most out of TM.
