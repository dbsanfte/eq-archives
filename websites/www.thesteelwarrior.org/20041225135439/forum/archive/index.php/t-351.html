General consensus, with all the expansions enabled, is to use Paludal Caverns to about 23-ish, then hit up any of several zones: Echo Caverns, Sol A, OT, etc.
Once you hit 35 or so, try DL Yeti's. At 40+ hit up City of Mist.

After that, the flavor becomes too much to really dictate - you'll have worked out your style by then, and you'll have a good idea of where to go.

That list is based off of using fast pulling, quantity vs quality, and ZEM's.

Since it'll be new and conjested with new people, this is what i'd suggest- make whichever race you want, do the langseax quest 1-2 times depending on if you'll be expecting to find a better weapon by the time you hit 13, then go to FoB. It's the best newbie zone IMO, and has a wide area of low level mobs so crowding shouldn't be too much of an issue. After about lvl 10, enter kurns (if it isn't too crowded) lvl there till about 14-15, then hit Paludal (again, if it isn't jam packed with peeps) till 22-4ish. Alternate places for lvl 10-24ish=LoIO (has newb mobs up to stuff you can lvl off of till 30s) Nektulos killing guards (lvl 15ish unless they changed them, and not bad for the ole bank fund) Lavastorm, Steamfront (for 10-14ish) etc etc. After 24 or so, I'd move to netherbian lair. Lots of low-20s lvl mobs, and not too hard. After this, hit up dawnshroud till 40ish, then move to DL. After 45-50 (depending on how patient you are and how you are equipped by now) you can move to KC/velks/seb/chardok/hole/the plethora of low-mid 50s exp zones. After 54-55, start hitting up tier 1 planes (PoN and PoD are the best for exp, PoI is the best for money IMO) After that, it's really all about personal preference.
