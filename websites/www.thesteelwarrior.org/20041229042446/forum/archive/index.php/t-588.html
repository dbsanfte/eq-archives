To add to what Shik said ... think about perhaps a Windblade to switch to once your Truncheon of Doom procs (BTW, ToD is an absolute must have for soloing purposes at those levels) ... think about perhaps an invigorate casting BP to use in downtime ... and try and get as much utility as you can get.

Things like a really fast horse or JBoots or SoW potions or Run 3. They will help you escape when things go REALLY bad.

Things like snare weapons, and the slow procs and stuff like DD procs to increase the damage you do and give you an edge.

Self buff clickies. Self heal clickies. Self haste clicky like the eyepatch of plunder.

All of these things will help the warrior solo.

Good luck.

And remember, soloing as a warrior is slow and boring. It's definitely possible, it's just slow and boring.
