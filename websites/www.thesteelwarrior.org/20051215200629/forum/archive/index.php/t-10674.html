We've never cleared the courtyard and we never close the doors. I think that's a myth.

Regardless of whether courtyard mobs are linked to Morgaine ( I don't believe they are ), clearing the wanderers in the courtyard or closing the door is sound advice, particularly if you are low level.

If the wanderer closest to the doors aggros you because the door is open and you are too far back because you are trying to avoid aggroing more mobs from the cathedral, then it will sit there and nuke you, and everything that goes near it will aggro as well.

If everyone is over level 36 this is less of a problem, but low level aggro in there is pretty harsh.
