The warrior epic is only useful if:


You want it for role-playing purposes
You want to embark on a quest of epic proportions for a sense of accomplishment
You want to look awesome when your bazaaring/running through zones. The glow really impresses the newer folks :D


If you want it because its a "good" weapon, don't bother. There are MUCH better weapons out there, and the best (affordable) dual wield combo goes for less than 25k total (if you don't know what they are, go look up "Copper Hammer of Striking" and "Sword of the Bloodsworn"). Last I checked, the scales costed at least that 25k EACH, and if you've got a solid group of friends you can go camp the weapons in droga for fun and profit.

If for some reason your too poor to buy them and dont have any friends to camp the weapons, you can level yourself to 55 and buy a Greatstaff of Thunder for less than 100 PP. Its a solid agro weapon, I use it and its more than enough for me, and it'll only get better once I stuff an anger proc in it.
