You need 6 in General and 12 in Archetype before you can get AE Taunt in Class. Thus, you must spend 23 points to get it.

Bor will give you the medallion and the forge for the Ornate armor at level 55, all you need is the rest of the stuff. Also, the forge and medallion are one-shot deals. You have to hand him 500pp for each piece you get. But, since the forge is Lore, hand in, get the items, combine (the forge poofs), hand in again for another medallion and forge...repeat as needed.
