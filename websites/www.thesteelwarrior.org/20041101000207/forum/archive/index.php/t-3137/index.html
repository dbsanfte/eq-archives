Maadaasaakaa
Or go the trade supplies route, go to your bazaar find out what sells for what, then work out how to acquire said items and sell em !

A couple of free examples that payed for a lot of gear for me;

Acyrlia ore, take some bandiads and kill grimlings in tenebrous mountains, go left from zone in till you find the first mine, keep clearing the mobs over and over, you will get ore, keep any windstones that drop they also sell well to trade skill folk. Sell all the other drops, runes and gems to vendors in Katta, (where you will also buy you bandaids). A nice bonus to this spot is killing the Grimling runner who drops a satchel you need for the relic pack quest. So.. you can also make and sell relic packs, 10 slot giant 90% wr bags, sell for 800-1000 plat each on MM.


Ore also drops in Acyrlia caverns more commonly, but you wont be able to solo in there, so take a few friends, youll get XP as well.

Another method to make plat but requires some patience and a tailoring skill of 60 or so is to farm and make leather padding.

Head to marus seru, and kill rockhoppers like you are hell bent on their extinction, bow pull 6 at a time and get slaying them. They drop two kinds of hides, keep both, one type can be combined with a pattern to skill up tailoring. The other combined with shadling silk threads makes leather padding, there is a great spot in shadeweavers thicket over in the south east corner for farming the silk just keep whacking the skeles (look up the details at eqtrader), on mith marr leather padding can sell for up too 25 plat each, and they stack soo one stack =400-500 plat.

I burned myself out on leather padding as I used it to skill up smithing and sold the leftovers.

Both of these methods will net you good plat vs the time invested.

And as has been mentioned many, many times play the buy low sell high game in the bazaar, you just need a small amount of start up capital and get going for it!
