Follow Wandor's suggestion if you are having little problem with aggro; buying the Anger III first is the most efficient path to get where you eventually want to be. In fact, Wandor's suggestions are typically the most efficient way to get to where you will eventually want to be as a strong 65 war.

However, Yoda's suggestion will get you more hate sooner. Following his suggestion would simply mean that eventually you'd probably end up selling back the Ang. II augs, though, and having to do more adventures to get Anger IIIs.

Being efficient is generally better, but it really sucks if you're MT and can't hold aggro right now. It's one thing to be attempting the most efficient path, but quite another to gimp your groups for dozens of LDoNs and whatever else you do in the next 8 levels while you efficiently save up for Anger III augs. So, if your standard fighting partners are okay with your aggro generation, save for Wandor's path. If aggro is consistantly unstable and you frequently serve as MT, try Anger II or Yak on your mainhand (making sure that your mainhand is a weapon with a good innate proc-- ie, droga). If the quicker-fix Anger II or Yak stabalizes your aggro, save for the Anger IIIs. That way you only have to sell back one Anger II.

It depends a bit on your grouping situation, too. If you group with the same friends all the time, you can actually explain what you plan to do in the augment department. They might be okay with you waiting for the better stuff later.

Basically, you'll have to balance your current aggro needs against the obvious advantages of long-term efficiency.
