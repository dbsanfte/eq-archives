Sword of the Bloodsworn is easily the best weapon you can get for the price range; and in all honesty, it should really sell for more, but with the camp in Droga being duoable and on a quick respawn, the market is flooded with the Sword and the Copper Hammer of Striking.

On my server, it's not uncommon to see the Copper Hammer going for 9k or so, and it's fairly easy to haggle down to 7500 or so. If this is true for your server, I'd recommend the Copper Hammer for it's superior ratio/dps and the extra 104dmg on its proc. If a Copper Hammer is too expensive, then by all means get a Sword of the Bloodsworn. You won't regret it.
