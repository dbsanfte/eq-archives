In this thread about DPS AAs

http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?t=6770

Some suggestions for the poster about what to get next for AA's came up. He doesn't have AE Taunt yet and a lot of people listed that as the #1 AA for him to get next.

I wanted to chime in my 32 coppers worth, but rather than derail a good discussion about DPS AA's, I thought I'd kick off a new thread.

As for myself, I got AE Taunt as soon as I could. I basically followed the "cookie cutter" AA path and now at 124 AA (defensive all done, grunting out the GoD aa's /cry), I have a hard time remembering life without AE Taunt.

It is definately an awesome ability to have in my bag of tricks. Allow me to list some of my better uses for AETaunt:

Single Group
1. The shit hits the Fan (for example a 4 pull on Hard LDoN). You have Crowd Control (Enchanter, Bard) but your group WILL probably die before they can get locked down.

AETaunt, followed by FORTITUDE (or Furious if they are all facing you) + hotkey that says "I HAVE AGRO FROM AE TAUNT ~ REGROUP AND LET'S DO THIS!!!" allows you to hold all the critters at bay for 15 seconds.

You know what, a good CC guy can probably lock down 2 of them in that time, and once Fortitude is over, you can probably tank 2 by turning on defensive long enough for the 3rd to get locked down, rooted, slowed or otherwise mitigated.

Guess what, you just saved a wipe. T3h Win!

2. Overagro (Usual pull happens, maybe an add, but long and the short of it, someone besides you has agro and even with /shield they will probably bite it.. np, just AE Taunt for a guaranteed agro)

Guess what, you just kept your group mate from dying. T3h Win!


Raid Setting
1. All time best use of new discipline timer splits - the tank switch.
Your MT dies, your ST dies, a few other peeps are dying along the way, wipe could happen but someone has to get on that bitch ass mob NOW or its nekked party at the GY time!

AE Taunt
Furious (Fortitude if there are other mobs that may hit you from behind)
Incite (get an agro lead on anyone else who may be hitting mob)
Hotkey /rs AE TAUNT!!! I have agro on >> %t << HEAL MY ASS NOW!!!

Pray that enough healers are alive and awake to switch the ch chain over to you and then proceed to tank mr. bitch ass mob down for t3h win!


2. In a zone without a Grave Yard (Vex Thall for example). Raid about to wipe? Bunches of mobs in camp?

When the raid leader starts calling for clerics to camp their asses out, you can help buy them time by doing the same as #1 above for the group setting.

AE Taunt
Fortitude

Hope you did it at the right time to buy some time to get a cleric out. Hey you are going to die anyway, if you can contribute to a successful recovery, especially when faced with the possibility of a wipeout that leads to a coffin using, corpse summoning, go home no phatty loots for you kinda wipe, then that is a good thing, perhaps even t3h win!


Ok, sure, maybe you could pull some of that off with just normal taunt, but normal taunt doesn't work worth jack on red con boss mobs (in my experience). So, don't neglect AE Taunt when considering your AA line up. It is, t3h win!!!
