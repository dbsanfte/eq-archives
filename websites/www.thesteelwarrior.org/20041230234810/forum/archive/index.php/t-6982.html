Coroq
05-06-2004, 04:45 PM
Lathen, you just repeated my conclusion :) I consider a weapon with a high aggro proc aug'd with ang3 to be "damn fine", and getting 2 of them isn't always real easy.

But in general, if the 2-hander you have is of similar or greater quality than your 1-handers, it's better aggro (in addition to the obvious better dps and fewer ripost). I'm talking for instance of a typical warrior that weilds a BoC + minor PoP weapon (say, cord hilted) then goes elemental and gets a BoW. Yes with dual weilding you get a bonus of about 50% more procs thanks to your offhand swings, but you lose out a lot on steady dps aggro. The offhand gets fewer swings and smaller damage bonus, and as others have shown on this board the "potential damage" of your offhand is weighted artificially small. So the BoW is the best aggro option until you start getting weapons from tier4 of time and beyond where there a few "damn fine" 1 handers.
