Mostly you need AA's. But you can make a lot of improvements right away by putting some attention to your gear.

Your absolute top priority should be to replace those pieces of Blazing Fennin Ro armor. They are crap by any standard. Keep them in your bags as clicky toys. I would kick off your 200k spending spree by buying replacement items for those slots - practically anything would be better, but cultural or ornate would be decent investments.

Most pieces of ornate would be an upgrade for you; I'd suggest spending time grinding your AAXP in SolRo, where you will get ornate drops perhaps 1/hour, thus killing two birds with one stone. The other place you may like to spend time is in LDON to amass points. You probably don't want to buy LDON armor, since you will be getting elemental and VT gear in time. Aim to buy 40 hp and 10 AC augments with your LDON points.
