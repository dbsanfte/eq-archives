I'd agree they are talking about healing the ramp tank, unless it is a mob that does an aerampage (hits all people within a certain range). In that case, people need to watch their health and back off when they get hurt too much.

Is there something tanks can do to avoid the Rampage?

Rampage is set based on order of aggro. So assuming no one messes up and you set rampage right, you should know who is going to get hit or not. But, it is best that tanks take the rampage and not avoid it, since if they aren't getting hit, someone who is a lot more squishy will be. Though you usually have a set ramp tank, so other tanks won't have to worry about it unless he dies (which is why you have multiple people get aggro in a certain order).
