Zun’Muraam in Ferubi
Pull him + 2 pets, kill pets then kill Zunmuraam. Pull him to the corner of the stage and put casters around the wall. Once adds dead kill him. He has EP quality loot

Weaponsmith in Ferubi
Hand in the 4 pieces from the Pxxt nameds, spawns ghost and named Weaponsmith and 4 or 6 adds, mezz half, Tank mezz and kill other ones then DPS smith.


Both events doable by lower numbers of VT geared or early elemental. Good for a few hours of raids and seem to be up on my server a lot.
