The poll's missing something. Improving taunt doesn't fix the warrior issue. if you remember, they already HAVE improved taunt, and it didn't fix the problem. The problem is bein gable to not lose aggro to begin with, just like the hybrids can, albeit to a lesser extent. The only way taunt's going to have any benefit at all is if you lose aggro. Then you mash taunt and hope for i tto work so you can a:get the aggro back, and b:indirectly generate aggro. The warrior issue is that they cannot generate their own aggro, they have to rely on aggro generating weapons, and the use of taunt to ride OTHER people's aggro. While hybrids have innate abilities to generate aggro through their spells, warriors are hamstrung with relying on other people and raid level weapons, as well as luck.

There needs to be added to the poll, something like this:

Give warriors an innate aggro generation ability.

Something like a skill that refreshes every oh, 30 seconds or so, that generates aggro with a skill level of 1-250, as, say, 3aggro per skill point in it. At 50 skill, it does 150 aggro. 150 skill, 450 aggro. At maximum skill, it does 750 aggro. Call it "Taunting Blow". So this ability scales up in levels. Explained as "Since the warrior is the master of melee combat, they, after time and experience, learn how to hit the mob so as to not necessarily seriously injure the mob, to to hit it in its most painful spots, enraging the monster and causing it to attack the warrior nearly to the exclusion of all others." Just as the monk is the master of pure melee damage production, the warrior is the master of pure melee pain production.

This way all warriors have the ability to generate their own aggro, enough to covers slows and debuffs, and they're not hamstrung by having to find weapons that pretty much always require a raid to get.

I think this would solve the warrior aggro issue, and wouldn't be too tough to implement. I believe it's a better choice than any 3 of the options that are presently in the poll.

Edit: This skill, if properly implemented, would fix the issue that EB was implemented for back in Velious as a "patch fix" for warrior aggro issues. This skill could well replace EB, as such EB could be changed to another effect, such as a lifetap or big DD or other effect that would be a fair replacement.
