Charm Stats per level (2 thru 6 are interchangable depending on which you get first )
AC 2, Str 1, Dex 1, Sta 1, Agi 1, HP 10, Resists +1 to all - PoS/PoV (PoJ trials)
AC 5, Str 3, Dex 2, Sta 3, Agi 2, HP 20, Resists +2 to all - (see below)
AC 7, Str 4, Dex 3, Sta 4, Agi 3, HP 30, Resists +3 to all - (see below)
AC 10, Str 6, Dex 4, Sta 6, Agi 4, HP 40, Resists +4 to all - (see below)
AC 12, Str 7, Dex 5, Sta 7, Agi 5, HP 50, Resists +5 to all - (see below)
AC 15, Str 9, Dex 6, Sta 9, Agi 6, HP 60, Resists +6 to all - (see below)
AC 17, Str 10, Dex 7, Sta 10, Agi 7, HP 70, Resists +7 to all - Tower of Sol Ro (Agnarr, Saryrn, Bertox, MM)
AC 20, Str 12, Dex 8, Sta 12, Agi 8, HP 80, Resists +8 to all - Plane of Earth/Air/Water (RZ)
AC 22, Str 13, Dex 9, Sta 13, Agi 9, HP 90, Resists +9 to all - Plane of Fire (Sol Ro)
AC 25, STR 15, Dex 10, Sta 15, Agi 10, HP 100, Resists +10 to all - PoTime

Any of these will add one level.
