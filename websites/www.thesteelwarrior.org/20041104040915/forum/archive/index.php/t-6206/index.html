Spliskin
I held agro pretty well with a Yak augged Hategiver and BoC unaugged till I got my Blade of War. Your dual wield combination is better than what I had so I wouldnt think you would have much problem.

When I got my BoW I didnt have enough EF adventures unlocked for an Anger 3 proc. So I stuck a slimestone fragment of dread on it as a "temporary measure" since I'm my guild's MT. To be honest, I kind of doubt I'll bother burning off the yak aug, I like the extra dps, the proc works on many mobs even if the stun doesnt, and agro is so good that on long fights I can shut off attack for a minute at a time if I want (i.e. the Clerics are fumbling their rotation and I want to avoid ALL riposte damage) and just throw an occasional incite. I really feel the yak proc is = anger 2 and better dps.

If you really feel you need more agro, throw a yak aug on the BoC...but I didnt regret not auging my boc and just waiting for a BoW.
