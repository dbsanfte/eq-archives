First off, to enter the Plane of Tactics (Drunder, the Fortress of Zek) zone-in at the circle of stones (near PoV and HoH ent) it is the one with the telescope and compass on it. You must have participated in killing the Manaetic Behemoth in the Plane of Innovation or completed Wembly the Forlorn's quest (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2292) in the Plane of Storms.

Spawns in the Pit
A War Boar (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10828 newwindow) not a traditional named, but drops loot.

an Enraged War Boar (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10829 newwindow) a frenzied initiate is PH.

Zerlin Morlok (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10994 newwindow) War wraiths are PHs.

a Stampeding Piglet (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10838 newwindow) spawns during the stampede that passes through the Pit.

Spawns in the Basement (enter through pit or tunnel north of Pit)
Diaku Armsman (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10528 newwindow) not a traditional named, but drops loot. What is PH?

The Diaku Armorer (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?
id=12191 newwindow) Can spawn in place of any spawn in it's room.

The Diaku Supplier (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10969 newwindow) Can spawn in place of any spawn in it's room.

The Diaku Overseer (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10968 newwindow) Spawns once a day.

Glykus Helmir (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10970 newwindow) Spawns once a day.

Tagrin Maldric (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=12423 newwindow) Spawns once a day.

Tallon Zek Wing
Shadow Master Vinta (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10392 newwindow) spawns randomly in the hall of Tallon Zek's wing, red/yellow con ogres (65+) are PHs.

Tallon Zek (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10357) 3 day respawn.

Vallon Zek Wing
Anival the Blade (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10391 newwindow) spawns randomly in the hall of Vallon Zek's wing, red/yellow con ogres (65+) are PHs

Vallon Zek (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10358) 3 day respawn.

Rallos Zek Wing
Hinvat Deathbringer (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10993 newwindow) spawns in the hall of Rallos Zek's wing, red/yellow con ogres (65+) are PHs.

Rallos Zek the Warlord (http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=10359) RZ script is 2 days "respawn" and is started by killing Decorin Berik -> Decorin Lieutenant -> TZ -> VZ -> Mini RZ on the upper floor

09-23-2003, 05:32 PM
Your link sends to a restricted area.

Hinvat can spawn pretty much anywhere in the path to RZ trigger. If you go to the top of the pit (and don't jump down), then go west is the RZ corridor.
Vinta can spawn in pretty much any spot on the south corridor near the zone in.
Same for Anival, but north corridor.
Since I can't see your map, I'll refer to the EQAtlas map.
Armorer, Overseer, Glykus, Supplier all spawn in the Basement / Pit map.
Glykus is at the very end of the south path, which is shown as grey instead of the same color as the rest.
Out from the pit you have North Hall and South Hall. Inbetween those two, on the west side and not the pit side, there is a tiny room, that connects to a larger room to the north. The small room is where Overseer spawns.
The room north of the pit, where you have to go out through north hall, go north, then turn right is the dining room. In there both Supplier and Armorer can spawn in one spot just north of the doorway at a table.
Looking at the map, there is a place with lava. South of that there is a four way intersection. The room west of that has the other Supplier spawn. If you go east, then turn north immediately when you can, is where the other Armorer spawn is.
Tagrin spawns in the room north of the lava.
