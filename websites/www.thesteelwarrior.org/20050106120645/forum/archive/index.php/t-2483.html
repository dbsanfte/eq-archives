If you're worried about cash, I don't see why you'd go after bloodmetal earrings when you can get one solstice + something cheap. Blood earring of rage isn't that good, solstice owns it in both stats and price. On my server solstice earring ingredients go for like 4k total which is damn nice for a 5sta 55hp 5fr/cr/mr earring. Soulscream would definantly be worth it just cause it's better haste and damn nice stats which leaves your glove slot open for like a SS gloves MQ if you could(90raw hp) The upgrades I'd suggest is keep the jagged bone hoop, get earring of the solstice, soulscream belt, clattering bone necklace, and look for a SS gloves MQ to buy. Some minor upgrades would be tossing the rock molded wrist for another crimson darkscale or fh bracer, dented kunzar helm(8sta 35hp, goes for cheap on my server like 2-3k) and grimling contraption(5sta 50hp aura of health mask) If you want an earring to replace the jagged bone hoop I'd look at a mosquito leg earring cause, well at least on my server, mosquito leg earrings are down to 18k if you look around and bloodmetal are at 35k with not that much better of stats. Another cool upgrade to look at if you ever have some extra money is chokidai hide pauldrons(15sta 55hp), bought those for 15k on my server, pretty nice hp gain, low AC but eh.
