In the Library forum there are a lot of good threads with basic information.

ND3 is an abbreviation for Natural Durability 3, and PE is for Physical Enhancement. Both of them are AA abilities. THIS LINK (http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?s=&threadid=88) has information explaining all the AA abilities.

As for your current choices - most people think that runspeed 3 is the best three points you can spend out of the 6 required in the general category. You'll never, ever outgrow being able to move faster. So that point is well spent. I would follow it up with another 2 points in run speed. The point in first aid really isn't a bad thing if you solo and use bandages to heal up to minimize downtime. I think the only real mistake you can make with the 6 general points is to not get run3.

AC and HP are what warriors are all about - so you have that right. Stamina is important because stamina = HP. Strength really doesn't do much for you.

Hope that helps.

Hail and well met.

I'm a newly minted Warlord myself. When I hit 55 I diverted all my exp to AA only long enough to get run 3 because I did a lot of my exping in dungeons at that time, and it still is very handy in Kael, Seb, Droga and the like. Once I got run 3 I put all my exp back into leveling experience

Ac and HP definately, followed by stamina for sure. After stamina opinions start to vary. I get less +agi stats from my gear than any other melee stat personally, but adding dex has a small effect on how often your weapons will proc for sure, and I think also how often you will critical hit or crippling blow

ND3 is the third level of Natural Durability:

Name: Improved Natural Durability
Level Requirement: 55
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 3 Levels
Point Cost: Level 1: 2 points
Level 2: 4 points
Level 3: 6 points
Description: This spell increases the hitpoints of a player by a base percentage.
The first point increases their hitpoints by 2%, the second by 5%, and the third by 10%.
This impacts the players base hitpoints (determined by class, level and stamina).

PE is Physical Enhancement:

Name: Physical Enhancement
Level Requirement: 59
Ability Type: Passive
Max Training: 1 Levels
Point Cost: 5
Description: This ability adds an additional 2% to the Natural Durability, Combat Agility and Combat Stability archetypes.
There is no pre-requisite that these archetypes be trained in, however the greatest effectiveness is achieved from doing so. It is believed that you need at least 1 point in an archetype to get the 2% increase in it (i.e. you would get no benefit in Natural Durability if you only had points in Combat Agility and Stability).
