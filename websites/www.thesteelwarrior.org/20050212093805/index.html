Epic effects being updated!
- by Brutul On Jan 16, 2005 - 1:01 PM
http://eqforums.station.sony.com/eq...6&no_redir=true

Hey guys,

We've looked at the issues that players have brought up with the epic 1.5/2.0 effects and took those into consideration when making these modifications. We’re looking specifically at effects currently. Here is the list of changes that will be occurring to the epic weapon effects:



Warrior -
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...232&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...248&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...264&source=Live http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...280&source=Live

1) The click effect will be a 6/3 minute recast for the 1.5/2.0. The effect will be changed to a smaller version of a straight rune, instead of a melee rune over time.

2) Cast time changed to .5 seconds

3) The focus effect will be changed to a permanent hate mod, something better than echo of anger



Cleric -
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...233&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...249&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...265&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...281&source=Live


1) No changes will be done at this time



Paladin -
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...234&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...250&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...266&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...282&source=Live

1) The click effect will be changed for the 1.5 and 2.0. The 1.5 will have a 50% heal mod, and the 2.0 will have a 75% heal mod. The duration on this buff will be 30 seconds. The stun component of the proc will be capped at level 73. Resist adjustment on proc will be set to -300 chromatic

2) The focus effect will be changed to a focus that reduces the recast time on group heals



Shadowknight -
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...236&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...252&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...268&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...284&source=Live

1) The click effect will be changed for the 1.5 and 2.0. The 1.5 will have a 55% lifeleech mod, and the 2.0 will have a 80% lifeleech mod. Resist adjustment on proc will be set to -300 chromatic

2) The focus effect will reduce the recast time on all 30+ second nukes



Ranger –
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...235&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...251&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...267&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...283&source=Live

1) The click effect will be a 6/3 minute recast for the 1.5/2.0. The 10 hit limit will be removed

2) The focus effect will reduce the recast time on 30+ second nukes



Druid -
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...237&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...253&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...269&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...285&source=Live


1) The click effect will be a 6/3 minute recast for the 1.5/2.0. The duration will be changed from 1 minute to 30 seconds, and the spell hit limit will be increased to 20. 30% mod for the 1.5 and 50% mod for the 2.0. Resist adjustment on proc will be set to -300 chromatic

2) No changes to the focus effect.



Monk –
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...238&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...254&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...270&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...286&source=Live

1) The click effect will be a 6/3 minute recast for the 1.5/2.0. The effect will be changed to increase max HP of the monk by 25% with a 30 second duration, and the proc will be changed to a 300dd + spin target for the 2.0, and the 1.5 effect will be scaled up as well. Resist adjustment on proc will be set to -300 chromatic

2) Cast time changed to .5 seconds

3) The focus effect will be changed to reduce the reuse time on Innerflame by 4/8 minutes



Bard –
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...239&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...271&source=Live

1) The click effect will be a 6/3 minute recast for the 1.5/2.0. The effect will be made more powerful: The increase to crit mods will be 12% and the accuracy mod will be doubled for the 2.0, and the 1.5 will be scaled up as well. Resist adjustment on proc will be set to -300 chromatic

2) The mod on the 2.0 is will be increased to a 2.9 mod to all instruments. The 1.5 mod remains a 2.2 mod to all instruments



Rogue –
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...240&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...256&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...272&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...288&source=Live

1) The click effect will be a 6/3 minute recast for the 1.5/2.0. The click will be changed to increase the damage taken mod on NPCs by 10% and the max hits limit will be increased to 125 hits for the 2.0. The 1.5 effect will be scaled up as well. Resist adjustment on proc will be set to -300 chromatic

2) Cast time changed to .5 seconds

3) The focus effect will reduce the reuse time on their discipline by 4/8 minutes

Shaman -
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...241&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...257&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...273&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...289&source=Live

1) The click effect will be a 6/3 minute recast for the 1.5/2.0

2) No changes to the focus at this time



Necromancer -
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...242&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...258&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...274&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...290&source=Live

1) The click effect will be a 6/3 minute recast for the 1.5/2.0. We’ve also lowered the duration on the pet to 30 seconds and have doubled the pet’s damage, as well as the recourse amount. This should alleviate the issues with the pet lasting one minute, but only being effective for a fraction of that time due to killing NPCs too fast. Resist adjustment on proc will be set to -300 chromatic

2) The focus has been removed, and a worn effect will be placed on it which will increase all DOT crits by a fix percentage



Wizard -
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...243&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...259&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...275&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...291&source=Live

1) The click effect will be a 6/3 minute recast for the 1.5/2.0. The effect will be changed to a group buff and the amount of resistance percentage will be increased to 50 for the 2.0. The 1.5 will be scaled up as well

2) No changes to the focus at this time



Magician -
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...244&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...260&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...276&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...292&source=Live

1) The click effect will be changed to a 5 minute duration, with a 4.5 minute recast, making it a permanent effect. The strength of the buff will be reduced slightly: The proc on the 2.0 will be 200 damage and the defensive proc will not fire as often. The HP, Regen, and Mana regen remain the same. The 1.5 will be scaled as well. Resist adjustment on proc will be set to -50 chromatic

2) No changes to the focus at this time



Enchanter –
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...245&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...261&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...277&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...293&source=Live

1) The click effect will be a 6/3 minute recast for the 1.5/2.0. We’ll be reviewing the code on which runes wear off in which order, to make sure the epic effect wears off last. The lower hate on the 2.0 will be changed to 1200 hate and the 1.5 will be scaled up as well

2) The focus effect will now lower hate on all detrimental spells



Beastlord –
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...246&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...262&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...278&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...294&source=Live

1) The click effect will be changed to a 5 minute duration, with a 4.5 minute recast, making it a permanent effect. The strength of the buff will be reduced slightly, similar to the changes made to the Magician buff. The 1.5 will be scaled up as well. Resist adjustment on proc will be set to -50 chromatic

2) The focus effect will lower recast times on all nukes over 30 seconds



Berserker – http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...247&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...263&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...279&source=Live
http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.ht...295&source=Live

1) The click effect’s duration will be lowered substantially, to 2 ticks. It will be changed to a proc with a recourse that puts the buff back on you. So buff will stay up as long as you stay in combat. The damage will be tuned around this change. Also, each proc of the buff will have a small jolt added to it to reduce agro.

2) Cast time changed to .5 seconds

3) The focus effect will now reduce the reuse time on Blind Rage by 4/8 minutes



We’re open to feedback on any of these changes. We’ll also be requesting an increase in the number of buffs that can go into the short duration buff box; we’re thinking 9-12 buffs, and then moving all epic effects to that box. Please post any feedback on any of these changes here.

Thanks!

-Zajeer
