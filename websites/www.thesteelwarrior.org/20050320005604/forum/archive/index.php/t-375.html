yeah man, take a shot at Vindi....there's your stepping stone on up to the next level critters.
We were about halfway through getting Dozekar The Dirtnap items for us when we took a run at Vindi. From there on through you'll see where you need to work on "working as a team".

It's good you got your Tank all nicely equipped; but how do the rest of ya's fare? You're gonna first and foremost need your clerics with a good size manapool; same for your shammys and druids...having your casters set up with the proper spells/etc. there's so much more to a boss fight at that level than having a beefcake MT.

Talk to your officers along with the guildleader and start crunching numbers on your guildmates and see how you all are equipped..go from there and no matter win or lose; keep trying the encounter even if you fail a few times. You'll get it and feel great about it.
