Frosty procs at level 40, FYI.

The previous post is very good for getting back on your feet. However, if you can swing it, I'd reccomend a suit of cultural armor. (However, that won't help you if you are an agnostic ogre or troll. :( ) I would not touch the cobalt for everyday wearing unless you are getting yourself the breastplate or legs (both of which are still pretty pricey).

I'd grab some silver chitin handwraps for haste, they seem the most reasonable in price and will tide you over til you can camp higher haste or purchase it. For aggro weapons, a frostbringer or a pair of troll smithed ykeshas should do the trick.
