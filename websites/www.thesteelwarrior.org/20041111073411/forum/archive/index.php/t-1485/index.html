Draganetti
My /assist /shield hotkey works great. If you are having problems, it's probably due to 3 factors:

1) Your shielding target is out of range. (You have to be right next to them.)

2) The mob has already turned back on you, and you're trying to shield yourself.

3) you don't have the pauses worked out, so the game thinks you are trying to shield the mob (there is some lag involved with /assisting).

/assist
/pause 1
/shield

This has always worked for me. Remember there is lag involved, so make sure you don't try to target the mob again immediately. You have to wait for the shield message. (This is another common error, especially if the /pause is too long.)
