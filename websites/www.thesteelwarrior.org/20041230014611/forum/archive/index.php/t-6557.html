Guys, this may seem like its splitting hairs here a bit, but ughres do NOT have higher innate hp!!! They have a higher starting STA which results in some extra hp until that gap is closed with equipment or even AAs as they get farther along. A barbarian, an ughre, a dorf, a gnome and any other race you care to think about, in identical gear/AAs in which all have maxxed sta, will all have the same hp. Stamina is actually one of the easiest stats for us to raise.

Slam is an ability shared by ughres, trools and barbs. Frontal Melee Stun Immunity is an ughre ability. Innate Regen is a trool and lizzerd ability. Get the trend?

Some of the racial abilities are more powerful and some less, and to counter them you can consider things like base starting factions (which used to matter before the release of Luclin and still is some concern to lower level chars and role players) and racial experience penalties.

3-4 "what race is best" threads a week and people still struggle with that...
