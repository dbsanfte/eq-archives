I think the original question was based on a faulty asumption. I hear this one more often then you would believe so if this isn't what you were thinking thor then I am sorry.

I think Thor was told some where down the line that you get some X number of HPs when you level. This number is based off sta and AAs etc.. but it is a static level up bonus number or something. This type of system is used in D&D based games as well as a few others.

For example if you were lvl 64 with 100 sta, dinged 65 then got up to 200 sta you would have less hp then if you got the gear to get you to 200 sta before you dinged. In D&D it works like this. You get a hp bonus every level based on a ran 1-10 + a sta bonus (yeah its not sta in D&D). The bonus only comes into effect when you ding. (IE a bonus of 4 hp and a roll of 10 gets you 14 more hp, if you get yoru sta bonus to +5 you still have the same base hp. If you got the sta bonus to +5 before ding and rolled the 10 then you would have gained 15 hp) EQ does not work this way.

So thor in answer to yoru question, if you ding 65 then get max sta and nd3 pe etc... or got all you could at 60 then dinged 65 you would have the same hp. It won't matter. Getting the sta/AAs before or after the ding has no effect on your possible max hp.

Getting a level only changes the way the hp calculations work (basically giving you more hp per sta) but your hps are never set in stone.

I hope I was clear.

In answer to yoru second question, with all AAs and the newest and greatest gear from time... I think 1.1k buffed is possible. I haven't really checked though but the number is likely around there.

The percentage never changes.. I believe somewhere it was said that PD3, ND3, PE give an 11% bonus to your HP

So, if you're naked with 2800hp, the above AA will give you 308hp or so.


If you're in full elemental gear with 7600hp you'll get 836hp..

The percentage never changes. As you get new gear the bonus is applied as soon as you put it on. If you get ND3, PE, PD3 (which you can't get) at level 60, then level to 65, you'll end up with the exact same HP as you would if you went 65 then did those AA.

The HP you receive when you ding isn't calculated on your current stats, it's based on a set number, then your bonuses for Sta and whatnot are added after the fact. In other words, if I have a base sta of 100 and I ding level 61, I'll get the same ~50hp that a troll with a base sta of 140 gets.
