Cazic Thule -- Amygdalan Chain Mask (1 groupable), 85 raw hp. Also, Lizard Tooth Earring, 70x raw hp.

Plane of Tactics -- Bloodmetal Earring of Engagement (must be crafted, but the parts drop here). If you aren't flagged, you can either try to get in on a Behemoth raid, or you can farm diaku medallions in the desert in Plane of Storms (one groupable).

You might want to think about grinding out some LDoNs with an eye toward gearing up to do hard LDoNs. Going for some flags (PoV/PoS at least) will give you some free AA which will boost your tankability lots as well as giving you a fairly decent charm. Once you get some more hp and AA under your belt, start eyeing hard LDoN drops: 90 hp neck and gaunts in Ruj, 85 hp boots, 100 hp ring in MM, etc.

Grinding out normal LDoNs in Tak for the plate armor there (or go the bargain route in EF, which has a side benefit of unlocking the anger augs) might be a good midterm goal.

Good luck.

edit: If you get the cash, keep a lookout for ornate helm mold, it would be a fair upgrade for your current helm. I wouldn't bother with boots/bracer though -- you can get better in LDoN. If you take your guild to Kael and find King Tormax up, he might drop Boots of the Storm (75 hp) for ya.

Daabu
I'd suggest camping in BoT for a few pieces of ornate, or hitting up the bazaar.

Ornate wrists are a substantial upgrade to what you have, AC wise.

Boots of the Storm are a bit pricey, but 75hp 35ac. Alternatively, if hard LDoN is an option for you, there are the bloodied boots of forgotten rites, or something, that are 34ac 85hp ... these are usually cheaper than the boots of the storm, too.

Ears and Wrists have already been mentioned. I may also suggest buying the 40hp aug for your ring from NRo (1050 or 1150pts, I think). Its expensive, but you will want some raw hp on your ring (either that, or get another Valorium Ring of Gallantry)

If you have lots of plat to spend, you could look into getting a Helmet of Rallos Zek from AoW, 55ac 100hp. Of course, that's gonna cost you a lot of plat :-\

Really, LDoN and/or Ornate will be your best bet for gear upgrades, as has already been stated.
