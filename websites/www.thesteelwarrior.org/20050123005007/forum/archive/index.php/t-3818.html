Keane Abrams
10-22-2003, 09:31 PM
You can only have one proc go off per swing. The innate weapon proc will always go off before the augmented proc. Also, adding a proc has been proven not to lower or raise the proc rate of the innate proc.

Basically when you swing with your normal weapon, the innate proc will either fire or it won't.

When you add an augmented proc, the same thing occurs BUT if the innate proc does not fire, the RNG will see if your augmented proc fires or not.

Bottom line, 2 procs means 2 chances on every swing but never 2 procs on the same swing.
