Leader Abilities for Groups and Raids in GoD


When enabled, 20% of the leader's xp will go to a separate xp pool. When that pool is filled the leader will receive a point (like with AA). These points can be spent to purchase the following abilities.


Group Abilities:


Mark NPC (3 lvls, cost 2)
NPC Health (1 lvl, cost 2)
Delegate Main Assist (1 lvl, cost 4)
Delegate Mark NPC (1 lvl, cost 4)
Inspect Buffs (2 lvls, cost 4)
NPC Awareness (5 lvls, cost 2)
Spell Awareness (1 lvl, cost 4)
Offense Enhancement (5 lvls, cost 3)
Mana Enhancement (3 lvls, cost 3)
Health Enhancement (3 lvls, cost 3)
Health Regeneration (3 lvls, cost 3)
Find Path to PC (1 lvl, cost 4)


Raid Abilities:
Mark NPC (3 lvls, cost 4)
NPC Health (1 lvl, cost 4)
Delegate Main Assist (3 lvls, cost 4)
Delegate Mark NPC (3 lvls, cost 4)
NPC Awareness (5 lvls, cost 2)
Spell Awareness (1 lvl, cost 4)
Offense Enhancement (5 lvls, cost 3)
Mana Enhancement (3 lvls, cost 3)
Health Enhancement (3 lvls, cost 3)
Health Regeneration (3 lvls, cost 3)
Find Path to PC (1 lvl, cost 4)
