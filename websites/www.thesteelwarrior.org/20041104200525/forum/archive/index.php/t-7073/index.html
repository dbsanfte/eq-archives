Eoden
in the scale of things these days Skyshrine gear is pretty poor and at best a stepping stone.

Usually I would recommend cultural ahead of Skyshrine with the exception of the Gauntlets and BP, even the non-deity specific stuff is superior to Sky Shrine in most cases, however as an agnostic ogre I think you are somewhat screwed in this department.

Really unless you are making XP and getting the things you need to quest your armour for free I think you are pretty much wasting your time focusing on Skyshrine.

The best bits:
Myrmidons Gauntlets - 27AC, 90HPs. The best bit of the whole set. Alternative's to these for comparable gauntlets are Ornate (less hps, more AC, clicky haste), Warlords Steel Gauntlets (more AC, same HPs, more stats) or those other tradeable ones from Velious - Velium Gemmed Gauntlets? (75HPs, more AC but overpriced usually)

Myrmidon's BP - 54AC, 90HPs. A decent BP with a clicky heal. Unfortunately theres a quest that requires a Velious BP that makes a FT4 caster/priest BP/robe so MQ's for this are getting even more expensive than they already were. Considering how easy Vindi is these days getting a Vindi BP should be much less painful, or an Architect's Bejeweled BP costs next to nothing and is perfectly servicable.

The rest -
Helmet of the Raldukan is pretty much the same stats and costs bugger all in the bazaar.

Bracers of Benevolence will give more HPs from a quest in Cobalt Scar (does require CoV faction though). Manathreaded Bone Bracelet from Hard LDoN or Ornate Bracers are far superior.

Boots of kingly splendor have considerable more HP, Barbed Dragonscale Boots are another option, the Hard LDoN boots are excellent (bloodied boots of dark rights)

Legs - Greaves of the orcish master are on par with Skyshrine. Kylong Shinguards are a respectable alternative.

Some other options which I would consider much better use of your time would be either LDoN - either the EF plate (Malevolent xxx of Suffering) at 700pts a piece of the Tak plate at 1400 points per piece would be far superior to Skyshrine, or you could try XPing in Veksar, alot of the Kylong gear is also comparable to Skyshrine and you will get better XP while you farm it and you can sell it on for profet when you upgrade it.
