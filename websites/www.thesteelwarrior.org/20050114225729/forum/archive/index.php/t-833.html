I don't know what prices are like on your server, so I'll just use my server (Torv) as an example.

You have 15k. Sell your SCHW (3k) and Putrid Belt (8k). Now you're at 26k. Buy Skyshrine Gloves (4k) and FBSS (2k). You still have 20k, plus you'll already have more ac/hp than what you had.

Next buy two Fierce Heraldic Bracers at 5k each. This leaves you with 10k. Idol of the Thorned (2k), Skyshrine Boots (4k), and Steel Wrapped Leggings (27ac 45hp 4svall) at 4k. Now you're broke. But wait, there's more.

Quest some Draconic Pauldrons and an Eyepatch of Plunder. This leaves you in pretty decent shape, except for agro weapons.

You might want to consider selling the GCM (3k) and buy a Black Blade of Tormenting and Barnacle Covered Axe, or possibly questing that 8/22 with stat debuff proc in Seru.
