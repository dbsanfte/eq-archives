In my opinion, bracer or gloves is the best slot to get.

If you get ornate bracer or ornate gaunts, you cannot put a 40hp aug in it. If you get the Takish bracer or Takish gauntlets, you can (giving you 110hp in wrist slot).

I'd get a bracer if I were you...but others will have other opinions.
