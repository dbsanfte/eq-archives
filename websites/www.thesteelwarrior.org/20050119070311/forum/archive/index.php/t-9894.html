get a class that can sense summoned.

To explain that a bit further, he's the ONLY thing in the zone with a summoned pet. So if you get a wizard (your wizard buddy will be tickled pink. He or she got this spell at level 8 and I GUAR-AN-TEE has never cast it.) to port in, cast their detect summoned spell. If they detect anything, Sev is up.

You can also track him from the top of the stairs with a fairly low level ranger. Of course, the top of the stairs isn't a very safe place for a low level ranger. Check out the root muncher boards. They keep a list of locations from where a level one ranger can track things.
