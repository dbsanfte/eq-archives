I think for places like BoT and if you want to take down giants in PoS, especially the slow mitigating ones, then you should be aiming for as close to 4k unbuffed as possible. That will put you at or around 6k with normal buffs.

If your struggling to find HPs, then I would definitely go the route of getting as much defensive AAs as possible before you start exping in places like that.

But the poster above is right - its not just a question of whether you can single tank and be kept up by a cleric. In most places ive exped in - CoD, HoH, BoT , PoS, there will always always come a time when you have to contend with 2 or maybe more mobs. Assuming you have a ench on the ball, thats still maybe a few seconds of taking punishment. Of course you have the option of maybe hitting your riposte disc or fortitude, but if you don't want to burn it, then you have to be able to soak it up. And no one can soak it up better than a warrior with decent hps and his defensive aas.
