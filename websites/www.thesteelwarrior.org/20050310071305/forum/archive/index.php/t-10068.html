If he has friends in the game, it doesn't matter how many people are on who are not his friends.
On the other hand, if half of his friends are already tanks, he might try something else if he intends to play with them eventually.
It's not that expensive to provide a warrior with useable gear for the first 50ish levels. Before that point, your friend will really like playing a warrior or he won't. If he does, heh, he'll put up with the problems of finding better gear. If he doesn't, he'll switch to something else.
I wouldn't worry too much about not being able to twink/pl to the max. In some ways, it's detrimental to a new player to level too fast/be too twinked.
I think warriors are the most fun class to play (though I certainly haven't played them all). I think that because I like to tank. If I didn't like to tank, warriors would be about the worst class to play. Your friend will know after being in one half-way decent, non-pl'd group in M Seru or DSP or OT what he thinks (I don't think he'll know in PC, because that's a bar fight, not a tanking situation). If he gets to level 33 and he doesn't like tanking, he'll do something else. If he does like it, you won't be able to talk him out of the warrior.
