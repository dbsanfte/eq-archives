"What am I to expect?"

Long, boring, silly, STUPID Luclin-era camps.

If you thought camping shards in Maiden's Eye was bad ... you're not prepared for this quest.

"Do I have to have ally faction with Katta Kastellum?"

Don't think you need ally. Just non-KoS. But the people I've helped with this quest have mostly been paladins. In any event, kill enough vampires, and believe me, you will, and you'll have no faction issues.

"Think we would be able to finish this quest?"

Get bane weapons on everyone, and you should be able to finish the quest. But from everything I've seen, the vampire is much easier to just kite, so you avoide the annoyingly LARGE lifetap.

Then again, level 65s should be able to deal with the tap rather well.

"Or would we have to bring in reinforcements?"

Either bring a bunch of druid friends and have them kill the guy ... or make sure everyone has vamp bane weapons and go to town. Either way works.

"Another question is, is it worth it?"

Depends solely on one thing: How much do you VALUE your own time? It's a timesink. It's a LUCLIN timesink. If you value your time even just the tinsiest bit, it's not worth it at all.

Good luck. Have fun.

Er, pets DO need bane weapons for Zevfeer. I went by "no need" and the pet owners told me I was wrong. Pet procs land, but their melee will not.

Also, Heratius is not kos as far as I know. I watched him stand at his spawnpoint (he doesn't move), close to one of the normal vamps without being molested. He does however despawn, sucker did so in front of me, as I was going to pull him.

I don't believe in any of the linked spawn theories..he was a no show for 6 hours the day before, then after the despawn he was back within about 2 hours, both times we destroyed the castle.
Only odd thing I found is that if Heratius is up, the named vamp on the top floor (Trix-something) is not up. Take it for what you will.

To the original question..apart from Zevfeer, its a pretty simple quest. You can do the whole thing except maybe Zevfeer with your group probably. Is it worth it? To me, it was. Although I did get lucky. No 12 hour nonstop horror camp or the likes. I think all in all I spent roughly 10 hours, spread over several days.

There's no locked door, thats for different katta quest stuff and the ashes do drop every time as far as I know. Be careful if you think to buy them. Heratius drops ashes of the same name, but they ID differently, as Ashes of Heratius and Zevfeer respectively. You must have Zevfeer ash. Verify before blowing cash!
The summon thingie is pottery made, 100ish skill should be plenty. I did with less, but ate several failures (quest items are returned, only minimal money loss).

Good luck.

I have one of these and absoutely love it. That much AC in a back item opens up a lot of other slots for higher HP items.

My experience was that Heratius spawns, when you kill him Valdanov spawns. The lifetap is incredibly annoying. As has been said, either get a bunch of folks with bane weapons, or a few casters to kite him around.

Any non-KOS faction is good, but after camping the castle for a while you'll be more than high enough. GO ahead and kill the other vamps there, they can drop some decent cash items on occasion and you won't feel quite so frustrated after 4 hours of monotony. :)

Is it worth it? I think so. I swapped my Cloak of Flames for a Seahorse belt for the massive AC boost from this thing and have since upped my hitpoints considerably without losing AC from its previous level.

Plus its nice to be sitting at better than 1300 AC while many others struggle to break 1250.
