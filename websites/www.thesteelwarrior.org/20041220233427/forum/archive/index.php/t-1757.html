Alla's has some good info on all the 10 ring quests, starting with Garadain's Blanket (#1) (http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=934) . Links to all the other ring quests are contained there. Also, this (http://gameznet.com/eq/quest/garadainsrings.html) site has some good info on the first 8 ring quests. Hope this helps you a bit as far as the ring quest goes. As far as other quests to consider, look at the Keep area, and see what quests people are asking about. Usually, these are the ones that are most worth doing. Also, look at different gear suggestions that are made in the Bunker section of the forum. Many of those gear items are quested. I used to keep that section open in one window, with all's open in another, looking up different pieces of gear.

the mathematical philosophy of taunting and damage mitigation REALLY hurts my head...

Do not get to worried about the hardcore, indepth aspect of those things. The basic concepts of only taunting when you loose agro(when skill is maxed anyway), and that more mitigation is good will take you a long way in game.
