One of the groups I hunt with often has a 2nd warrior, and we've had quite a bit of success, as you have some nice options. I thought I'd share a good example and a senario for Kyv Heartstriker Jhiru that we've had good luck with. We've now finished all 1-6 GoD trials with 2 warriors in the group, and no enchanter.

Warrior A - say this is your stronger warrior, although this works if both are about the same
Warrior B


Warrior A pulls Kyv Heartstriker Jhiru with an arrow.
Warrior B uses a pumace stone while Kyv Heartstriker Jhiru is running in to both debuff him and set rampage.
Warrior A starts on him, and after takes some damage, kicks on Reposte. By the time it runs out, Warrior A has been healed back to full.
Warrior B sees A starting to take damage again, then AOE taunts, and after taking a hit or two, Repostes as well, and get's healed back up as well.
Warrior A now sees B taking some damage and AOE taunts it back, and tanks it on to the end.
At this point Warrior B has the choice of going Stonewall which will basicly 'push' agro to your stronger warrior, in addition to cutting in half the rampage damage, or, if it's going well, kick in another offensive disp.

This also gives your slower almost 30 seconds to get him slowed (which by now Jhiru is down to between 25 and 50 health left), while noone is at too much risk of taking damage, and with all the reposting, should not have a chance at taking agro either.

Also, since we both have our epic 1.5's we get the bonus 600 hps up for the whole fight.

Anyhow, just thought I'd pass this on.... no charmed pet requred at all :D
