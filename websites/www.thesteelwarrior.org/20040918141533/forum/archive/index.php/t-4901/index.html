Battleblade
While I certainly never agreed with the specifics of the changes released thus far, there is no question in my mind that they address needed improvements and make playing a Warrior more interesting and require more skill.

Yes, unfortunately as with all non-trivial changes there have been some glitches and even server instabilities. But I was quite surprised with how well it all hangs together and solves the problems without being unbalancing.

In short -

Thank you for taking time out of your new development efforts and delivering these well-thought out changes very quickly after you committed to doing them.

I started this thread in hopes that those who agree with it's sentiments will add their messages of appreciation.
