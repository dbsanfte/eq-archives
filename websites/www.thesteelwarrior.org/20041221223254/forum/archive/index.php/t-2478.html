It doesn't actually increase how often you proc at all. All it does is gives you the chance to crit a straight dd proc. At level 1 a crit adds 33%, level 2 is 66% and level 3 is double normal. But if the spell can be partially resisted, then it still can be partially resisted. An example would be, when I first bought Ingenuity my very first crit proc was for 2, on a 75pt dd proc =p.

As Emilia said, the crit rate is very low. It's basically a toy aa, but can be kinda fun, especially if you have a high damage dd proc (like the 750pt one from Time =p).
