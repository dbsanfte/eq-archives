Carwenst
Ok, here are my thoughts.

I personally have used a low end combination of debuff, hate, and DD. Most of that is due to my access to other weapons.

What I have noticed, and someone can tell me if this is just my perception or not, is that debuff weapons are fairly useless after their first proc on a mob. That is why I switch mine out after it procs.

DD seems to add ok aggro, but all in all I would not say that it will make or break you. I use it a lot mainly because I do mainly FFA exping with a stable group of friends and the more damage the faster the kill. We have two healers in our group and each is assigned one of the "tanks" to cover, so I don't worry too much about aggro occassionally bouncing to the ranger who is the other "tank".

Direct hate procs seem to work ok, but I would need something better than the barnacle covered axe to really have an opinion on that.

Not having used the other types of weapons I cannot really give you a ranking. However, make sure that you consider that the weapon you are using also makes a big difference. You can have two weapons that proc the same effect that are very different in their aggro generating capabilities based on proc rate and dmg/dly.
