Don't be fooled by price tags ... the Fierce Heraldic will serve you much better overall than the Cobalt. (When you see, later, that the GSoT only costs 150p ... buy one and be happy.)

Higher AC, for one thing. And raw HP are better to look for than STA, (within reason of course) as you can always add HP but you can cap STA. And at that, 1 STA point being worth 6HP to me at the moment ...

12 STA + 50 HP = 110 HP gained.
5 STA + 80 HP = 110 HP gained.

As soon as you hit STA caps (or broach them), you lose HP gains on the Cobalt, and the FH looks even better.

On top of which ... 40K v. 10K. You can get a hella lotta upgrades across your gear with the 30K you'll have left over.
