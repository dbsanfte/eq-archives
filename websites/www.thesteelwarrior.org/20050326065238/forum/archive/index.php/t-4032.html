Our first Terris kill we had about 5 groups. Terris herself has the DPS of a BoT krieger. The hordes of butterflies are stunnable- 2 enchanters and the right strategy and they're a non-threat. For the dispell, you have to MGB Virt/Focus/Haste... Or withdraw past dispell range at 52%, ranger her down to 49, and switch tanks/rebuff one tank. Again, relatively trivial to deal with. The only real threat in the fight is the gargoyles.

First Brood dragons are also good: Yelinak and Sontalak are much weaker with PoP standard DPS, and Zlandi and Klandi are even gimpier.

IV and THO should also be easy for you to drop, and while the melee loot from them is relatively sorry, it's a solid boost to the casters.

The other option, as I see it, is to consider an alliance with one or two other guilds on your server in a similar situation to your own. If eventual PoP progression is your goal, at some point, you will simply need more people to make it work.
