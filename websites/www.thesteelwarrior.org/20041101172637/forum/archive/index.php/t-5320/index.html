Grongl
I've gotten pretty much every weapon from VP except Maelstrom and Blood Orchid Katana. Verdix/Veldrak's was the SoD before the SoD existed, but its age is long past. Feverblade and Sword of the Shissar used to be great for stacking up the dot procs, but have long outlived their usefulness. I used Sword of Rile and Scimmy of Lifestealing for soloing for the longest time, but I haven't really dusted them off since my solo aa days in FG. The two VP weaps that I still use on a regular basis are Spear of Constriction from Xygoz and Howling Cutlass from Nexona.

Spear of Constriction procs Ensnare, the only War-useable weapon that does. Ensnare has roughly the same movement decrease % as regular Snare (from silken whip, etc.) but lasts more than 3x as long. Makes a big difference.

You're probably thinking, "wtf do you use Howling Cutlass for?" Well I'll tell you. Look at the proc: Shrieking Howl (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=1803). It has a 5-foot pushback component. It's an insanely great weapon for positioning mobs. You might think that a BFG or Stronghorn would be superior, but the 15-foot push from regular knockback is, in game terms, a huge distance, which can push your mob completely out of position and make things worse. I've found the 5-foot from the cutlass to be damn near perfect for slapping mobs back into place - just enough, not too far.
