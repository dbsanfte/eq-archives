There is a dwarf in EFP right next to the other epic quest guys, once you have epic, hail him and he will tell you that you give him the 2h epic + scabbard and get back the 3 augmentable epic swords.

Here is the quest info:
http://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2645

Note: If you want to get 2 red and 2 blue blades you can get a mage summoned sword (sword of runes? not sure the name) and put it in your scabard and combine to make 2 new 1h epics and then you turn in the 2h + scabbard and get the other ones. I like having the 2 blue blades for doing fletching skill ups since they give me a huge dex boost.
