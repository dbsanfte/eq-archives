Diarmid
This isn't really a warrior-centric question, but I thought I'd take a shot with it here and see if you all have any experience with extended range items.

Will the extended range caster focus items provide enough range to keep casters (clerics especially) out of the AoE range of mobs like Xanamech or Grummus or Aerin'Dar? Or do different AoEs have different ranges?

I am part of a relatively small guild who is trying to work their way up to these larger mobs and was wondering if I should start suggesting these focus items to our casters? I have very little firsthand knowledge of these things and am hoping you can help me out.

Many thanks and fight the good fight!
wandor
ER items work from a % on the item. I believe most are either 15 or 20%. The bad news is that Cheal has a range of exactly 100, so the best ER items can only get you to 120 range from the mob. To put that into perspective, most AEs are in the 200-300 range. Except in the case of some PBAEs and mobs with a small AE rampage, clerics are pretty much screwed.
Bulbog
Different AE's have different ranges, look up some AE's on lucy and you'll see they have different listed ranges. For some AE's ER4 items will help casters stay out of range but I guess I can't give you an exact answer cause it depends on the range of the spell.
Grimmlokk
One thing to note on ER items: They only make the range/focus check at the beginning of the spell cast. If target is in range when you start and moved just out of range, it will have no effect.
Diarmid
Thanks guys..sounds like they aren't worth the time or trouble. Appreciate the feedback...fight on!
Kaesorn
They're nice when you can set it up right, but get them if you're already going for the item in the first place for the casters. I wouldn't specifically go after them in place of a better item.
wandor
sounds like they aren't worth the time or trouble ER is well worth the trouble. Most mobs get pinned against a wall or cornered anyway so it is unusual to move very much once the mob gets placed. This focus is particularly inportant for clerics since Cheal only has a range of 100. Lucky for clerics, that's a level 39 spells so ER2 or better will work. You can easily get everyone a Supple Scale Armband from KC if they don't have some form of ER.
