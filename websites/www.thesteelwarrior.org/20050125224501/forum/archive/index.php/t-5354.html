Here's a few more suggestions:

First, how's your dual wield setup working for agro? I'd personally be using your GSoT in your bag, but if you wanted to dual wield I'd of course recommend the Droga weapons to start. Your current dual wield combo seems a little iffy for agro at your level.

Next, haste. Your Dragonborn sash is only 2ac, and no other stats. Enchanted Sash of Wonder or Soulscream Belt are excellent candidates for an upgrade to your sash. Not only are they 31% haste, but have nice stats too.

Next, there are several "areas of opportunity" in your armor. Your wrists probably could use immediate attention. Velious armor would be a nice AC boost, a spot where you're a little behind at. I'd also look at your legs, as the AC is a little low here too. Velious is an option, but Kylong Darkmail would work here too. Lastly, the AC on your shoulders is a little weak, considering it's essentially a cloth item you've got there.

And of course, Ornate and LDoN armor would be upgrades too. You should probably be shooting for either/or as soon as possible.
