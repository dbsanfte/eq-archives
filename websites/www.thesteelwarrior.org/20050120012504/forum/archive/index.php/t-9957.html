I really wouldnt spend too much time trying to get a BBoTE. Considering the weapons you have, the augments you have in said weapons, and the availability of WA5, i'm not sure a BBoTE is worth the timesink required to obtain one (unless you're just plain lucky). It's an aggro upgrade for the mainhand (arguably), but in my opinion not what you need to be concentrating on atm. If you're having aggro problems get another augment and train Weapon Affinity up to level 5 (a must). That in and of itself will do much more for your aggro than anything else.

If it's a guild raid where you use points, i'd not spend em if i were you ... hold out for a BF or be content with the fine setup ya have :D .

I'd concentrate on armor at this point.

Just a side note - If you're running the encounter that drops the BBoTE, chances are you will see BF's drop with comparable frequency, and the BF runs circles around the BBoTE. I've seen warriors go months/years with zero luck on either of them ... rare as fuck.
