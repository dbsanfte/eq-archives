Hmmm. Your post suggests to me that I need to get out more. You appear to be extremely, even surrealistically, well equipt for your level. Every piece of resist gear I'd normally recommend would involve big cuts in your AC/hitpoints.
Runed bone fork fits in the range slot & has some resists, but can't remember if any of them are magic resist. Bloodmetal earring of engagement has 15MR on it at lvl 65, but not sure what it works out to at level 43. I only bring it up because it appears you have access to a good source of cash.
You could look around for Holy cake (stat bonus food w/ magic resist, I believe). There are other, more rare resist foods out there too. Look around EQ Traders for exact stat bonuses. You could switch these in before taking mobs that have given you problems before.
Also, you do have a resist disc. No reason not to use it against mobs that give you trouble, especially named in LDoNs. It's on a 30-minute timer, I think, which might give you pause if the mobs you're talking about come before the named, but otherwise no real reason not to use it if ya got it.
I assume you are slamming mobs to interrupt if possible. I assume that your groupmates with the capability to interrupt are doing so as well.
I assume you are asking for and getting resist buffs from your group members.
Other than that, pfffft.
