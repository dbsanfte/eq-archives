Assuming you can meet the lvl req velium gemmed is better.

Yes, windblade does have ac15 and sta12 on it (although if you can afford either of these weapons you should atleast be able to max sta with a shammy buff) but the velium gemmed has 50more raw hp and an additional aug slot, for a proc and additional stat boost. I dont know if i would worry about the 15ac too much, atleast on my server, the difference in price would allow you to buy a velium gemmed great sword and still have about 10-20k left over for other upgrades.
Apokolipse
I'ved tried both, I like the dps from the velium Gemmed, but it depends on who's tanking. If I'm using my 2H Slash at all, it's cause I'm not MT and therefore I don't want aggro. The windblade is nice and slow and doesn't grab as much aggro. If you've got a MT with strong aggro skills, go with the Velium. Especially when you're building skills, the slow delay on the windblade takes forever to get to 250.
