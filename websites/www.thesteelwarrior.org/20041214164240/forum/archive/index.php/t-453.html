Must be out of ignorance.

Gnomes only have 2 minor problems as warriors, that are related to being short. Some steps require individual hops, such as a couple flights in Kael. Other people get up them faster. And because we are short, sometimes people have a hard time seeing us, and being able to follow the fight.

Both of these are correctable by carrying a maximizing device. Also, dying your armor a bright color helps the visibility problem.

While the drawbacks to being a gnome are minor, the long term benefits are major. To start with, most of the time our small size is a benefit. It is generally easier to maneuver in fights. Also, we are the perfect size to tank a standard corner pin. Most of the time we end up shrinking all the fatties down to gnome size anyway. With gnomes you don't have to go through that hassle all the time.

As for stats, top warrior gear has loads of str and stamina, once you're maxxed, you're even with the big races. Actually, you are ahead, because your dex and agility start out higher. While top end gear is loaded with str and sta, dex and agi are a bit more uncommon.

But, the single greatest benefit to being a gnome is, gnome only gear. In particular, clockwork vambraces. These babies make all the difference. 40% self haste buff. No need to drag around an enchanter or shaman for haste buffs. This buff stacks with all the haste gear. I currently use Cloak of the Fire Storm, so when I solo I'm running around at 80% haste all the time. This means I XP faster than any other race. In the time you gain 7 aa as an ogre, I gain 10. If you group in a chanter or shaman to haste you, you will kill a bit faster, but you have to split XP with them. I don't.

Play what you want to play, period. In the end, the race isn't going to matter much. While you level from 1 to 50 (and depending on whether you're power-leveled or not) some races have minor advantages that may make leveling a bit easier:
Halflings get an exp bonus. Combined with the warrior class exp bonus, they're the fastest levelers in the game (about 15% faster than humans). If I decided to do it all over again...I never would have deleted my halfing warriorette and replaced her with a barbarian. There was much to be said RP-wise for a black-skirt-outfit-wearing, 2-handed-wielding little death machine. ;)
Gnomes, dwarves, frogloks all have some small advantage, not the least of which is size. Size is important when you realize your barbie, troll, ogre, and sometimes cat or lizard, can't go into someplace without a shrink being cast on them. All the small races also have infravision or better. Frogs can only worship Mith Marr, though, so that does limit them on some religious armor choices.
Trolls and Iksars level slower, a full 25% slower than halfling warriors. For that slowness you get regen and are hated mostly everywhere. Iksars do get natural armor, though, so the sting of not having access to plate in the early levels isn't that bad.
Dark Elves, Wood Elves and Half Elves are fairly rare as warrior choices, affording more possibility for RP (because a lot of people are going to ask you "why the heck did you make a ____" and you can then go into your spiel). They all have infravision or better night vision, can wear most if not all warrior useable plate armor, and none have an exp penalty.
Cats (Vah Shir) offer plenty of RP choices, along with a reasonably solid set of stats. They get safe fall (important when considering that trolls or ogres can hurt themselves walking down regular stairs). They have infravision but consume more food than other races and do not have the slam ability of the other large races.
Barbarians are big humans with the slam ability. Still, my warrior is a barbie and as much as I've wanted to replace him over the years, I still play him (he just made 58, as a matter of fact).
Actually, my question to you is, if you're so concerned about RP, why not start a warrior in the Firiona Vie server, instead of hanging out in a regular server like BBane?

Really, the choice of race means less than the choice of server in your situation.
