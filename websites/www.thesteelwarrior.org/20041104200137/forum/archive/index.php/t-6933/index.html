Fitemore
The Render isn't anything special for aggro due to the low aggro proc, but it should be some very decent DPS. Reasons: 21/24 (at best) is a very good ratio, but a 75 point lifetap is basically no aggro at all. You will never turn a mob with that weapon. To naysay previous posters, you would be served well replacing it with a GBoS. You'll lose some ac/hp, but you'll increase your aggro (~400 hate proc with GBoS, ~75 hate proc with the Render).

HG is an upgraded BoC. Slightly better ratio and better stats, same proc and proc rate. That being said, it's better than any tradable weapon. You could only replace it with a BF or DBotW or the like for DW weapons. Augment it.

Whatever offhand you think you'll be keeping for a long time, augment. An extra 600 hate proc per minute (1.5 procs per minute with WA5) is nothing to complain about. Don't blow the points on a weapon you'll replace quickly though.
