Beornegar
Since they fixed the recommended level bug on weapons, you'll want to avoid weapons with a rec level like the chos and orb. You may want to get them and bank them for later use. At low levels, you don't really need proccing weapons anyway, and if you just have to have a proc, gnoll hide lariats are big fun to tank with, especially in caster heavy zones. (I used to always have a pair with me when tanking in Crypt of Dalnir). When you get to the level where procs become more necessary for holding aggro, something with a ykesha proc, frostbringer, venomous axe of the velium brood, or black blade of tormenting are all decent lower end aggro weapons that will work until you're high enough level to use the droga combo.

Edit: For a really cheap 2hand slasher, a Mithril 2hander is hard to beat for a twink. Decent ratio, haste, and sells for about 50pp last I checked.
