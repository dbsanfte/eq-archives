Brabble
Ohbb's comments suggest two really important things for young warriors to keep in mind when they read gear suggestions here: first, different servers have different economies (and one server's economy will shift a bit over time). Thus, there can be some variability in relative prices of gear in the same general level of functionality. Much of the standard gear recommendations (crusty/crafted, LDoN armor, player-made cultural armor such as Fierce Heraldic and Tundra armors) should really serve as baselines for your bazaar searches. Search by slot, your race, class, religion and budget to get the best bang for your buck.
Also, here's a tip: LDoN armor such as gothic, sandblasted etc. often ends up being sold to the vendors in PoK that are right next to the forge. For example, vendors there will sell the 27 AC breastplate for around 95-110 plat depending on your CHA. You will be hard pressed to find a 27 AC breastplate for less in the bazaar, but it can happen. The main thing is to check both places and get the best bang for your buck.
Also you should check your server's price on opal encrusted steins. They give you 25 CHA. Even if you buy no gear at all from vendors, the steins will eventually pay for themselves if you equip them when you sell your vendor loot.
