07-13-2003, 02:17 PM
Back when I did Vox runs I took ~36.

2 heavy tank groups of 3 wars/pals/SKs, 2 clerics, 1 bard each
2 general groups of 3 melees, 1 shm, 1 ench, 1 cleric each
2 nuke groups of 1 druid, 2 wizards, and 3 nukers/DPS melees each

The 2 heavy tank groups charged first, got her in the center of the lair, the melees beat her down while the clerics healed with DL/SH and the bards played resist songs. The 2 general groups charged when Vox had 90% health, same general strat as the first two groups only the clerics and shaman assist healed while the chanters sieved. The last 2 groups charged at 70% and unloaded with fire nukes under her ass (really) at 50%. Nothin to it!

Nowadays 20 can do it if you're all 50-52 and have good gear. 1 enchanter is needed, 2 are better (for mana sieve).

Getting to Vox is easy, just don't fall into the bear pits. =P The hard part is pulling/killing the giants in her lair, most people pull them to the double doors by King and kill them there.

Once the giants are dead ya gotta buff up, a good place is the room adjacent to Vox's inner lair, it's got a bunch of ramps leading from it. Then it's just a matter of a charge strategy unless you wanna just pound her with all your groups at once which is viable with the right groups.
