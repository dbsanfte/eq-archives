/me wishes gear like that existed when I was 45.

/me notices you have Required Level 46 gear equipped.

/shrug. At any rate, you're nowhere near maxing STA, so pick up a Vah Shir Claw Earring. The extra AC alone makes it worth it, 4 STA and 6 DEX just makes it (seemingly) obvious. Perhaps a Wolf Fang Earring for the other ear, as well (4AC 3STR 2STA). Or, even better, pick up the parts for an Earring of Solstice (All the parts needed for the unfailable combine are droppable, then just turn the result in to a guy in Fungus Grove).

Clattering Bone Necklace will give ya another decent boost to STA and AC, short of that, gather a group up and try to get a Shaded Medallion from Maiden's Eye. 15 AC with nice resists, hard to beat as far as pure AC goes.

Crystal Spider Eyes or Elysian Death Mask for face...though I would rate these at low priority.

Bracelet of Fortitude, decent AC, great STA, and 6 to all resists...hard to beat.

If you have the cash, RBG, upgraded haste, and you free up your hand slot for Vellious quest armor.

Grab a few of these upgrades, then focus on leveling and getting faction and gems to MQ / Hunt in Kael for some Champs / Myrmidon's armor. The gems are easy enough to get or buy, and the MQ's are going for dirt cheap now days.

EDIT: Err, just re-read your post and noticed the budget...with that said, the list above may still be helpful for a general path to take. As for what I believe would be the most efficient way to spend the money you have at the moment, buy the parts for the Earring of Solstice. Why? It's an earring that will last you a good long time. Long after most of the other things will have been replaced, you'll still be wearing that "caster's earring". I'd save any extra cash for future upgrades.
