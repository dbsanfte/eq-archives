When you enter the palace, the doors at right and left used to be pickable by rogue, but not anymore.
You need someone with the key (north wing key) to open it, key wich drop behind them, on mobs called Va Xakra, guarding the nameds, Thall Va Xakra.
North and South, 1 Thall Va Xakra, 2 Va Xakra, each Va Xakra drop 2 keys, so 4 Keys at north, and 4 keys at south.

For your first run, bring someone who already have a key, ask him to open the door, then get your keys.

Other Key (north tower key) is usually not a pb to find / use, but for the first one, it is quite annoying since door isn't rogue pickable anymore.

If you can't find someone with the north wing key that want to open the door for you, there is some tricks to open it, but not as easy :)
