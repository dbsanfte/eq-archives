Go for cultural or try to farm a Champ's BP from the Kael Arena. Personally, if I had it to do over again (wearing Champ's now) I would stick with tradeable BPs until you can get Vindi or better. A cultural may not be quite as good as a Champ's, but when you do get an upgrade, at least you can sell the cultural.

Since you are low on funds, I would gather enough ingredients to make 2-3 BPs. Do all of the sub-combines yourself or get a friend to help if you can't do them yourself. Then find a smith to do the final combine. Chances are really good that you will be able to get the final done free if you have enough to give the smith a couple other combines for himself.
