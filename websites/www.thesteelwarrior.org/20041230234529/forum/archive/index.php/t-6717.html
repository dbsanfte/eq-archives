Discs are broken up into four different timers. You can use only one disc at a time. If you use a disc, discs on the same timer can not be used until the timer of the one you did use is up. However, you can use another disc immediately after as long as it is on a different timer, and you have enough endurance.

Couple questions:

a) what does the "make fragile" variable do in the healing will abilities?
b) what is the practical difference between defensive and stonewall? ie, same mitigation, just less melee dmg on the former and no crits + snare on the latter -- when would you use one over the other?
c) i hear people talking about whether they use fellstrike or mighty strike -- is this to imply that the other offensive disciplines just typically aren't used in favor of these two?
Hussar
04-20-2004, 08:39 PM
Make Fragile means the discipline will end if you attack or cast a spell in any way. You can be hit, just can't hit back.

Defensive and Stonewall do have the exact same mitigation. Both make it so you can't crit. The only difference is the decreased dps vs snare. Defensive is best used when you will be moving a lot (knockback mobs, cornering, your guild pushes a lot, etc). Also when you are raiding, and your dps doesn't matter, defensive is usually best. Stonewall is best used when you won't be moving much, or if you are doing stuff with low numbers when your dps will matter (especially GoD single group trials).

Regarding offensive disciplines. I believe fellstrike simply is more damage than aggressive, even if it's not, most people like the speed of the dps with fellstrike (30 seconds) vs aggressive (1min). Also, fellstrike increases your hit by a good bit, so fellstrike basically is the disc to use to get the huge damage crit / crip hits for your screen shots =p. Precision is less damage than fellstrike. Charge is also less dps. Fellstrike is overall better dps than mighty strike too. But, if you are berserk, you can use mighty strike to stun the mob a lot. Which can help save your life if you are solo, or if the heal is going to be late.
