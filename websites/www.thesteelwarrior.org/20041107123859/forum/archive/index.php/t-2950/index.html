Gyrfalcon
I am curious to see if this XP group is viable:
5 Warriors
1 Cleric

Have the warriors trade off MT position. The MT would pull the mob, establish agro, then go defensive (aka 40% damage reduction). The other warriors would then whittle the mob down, while the cleric keeps the current MT healed. The MT position would then switch to the next warrior in rotation, and then rinse and repeat. The only caster who could pull agro would be the cleric, and as long as the other warriors are using high DPS low agro weapons, while the MT in rotation would switch out to high agro weapons, I don't see the mob ping-ponging. By the time it comes around for the first warrior to tank in rotation, he should be able to use a new discipline. If not, the cleric gets some med time.

In order for a group like this to work, the warriors would have to be a well drilled team, and the cleric would have to be aware which warrior is up in rotation.

Think about this the next time you see four of your fellow class members sitting around LFG in PoTranq. I'd like to hear from anyone who tries this experiment before I do.
