20 AC per level is a good metric building up to level 40, but after that it depends on what content you do what what you do in that content.

I know then when I was running 900-950 AC unbuffed I could handle the arena, but I'd get my head kicked in with 1050 AC (~1100 buffed) in PoN.

If you are DPS, STR is the most important thing. All the HPs in the world won't save you from a deficient AC - know a paladin then went high HP/low AC and I had a much better survivability with high AC/low HP.

You have to get a sense for how much AC you need for the content you are doing and maximize HP while keeping HP over that level. I'd say over 1000 is fine for non-bosses in Velious, 1150 for early boss mob tanking in Velious. 1250 is the Velious softcap. AC doesn't mean jack in Luclin, and the more the better in PoP - not that I spend a lot of time there, so I don't have a sense of it, but 1175 seems good enough for PoN/PoD/PoI.
