Derrict
SEM rune proc could cause some buff stacking problems during raids, especially ones where resist buffs are needed or the mob will do several AEs. If you have 14 buffs and leave one open for a mgb or DoT heal and the SEM procs, mob hasn't hit you yet and an mgb goes off, it won't land on you. SEM might be fine for exp groups where you'll have more open buffs, but think about getting another offhand (ie EoE) if you want to upgrade you BF.
