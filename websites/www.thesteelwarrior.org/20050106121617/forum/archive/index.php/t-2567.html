At that level, you would only really need a couple of weapon set ups, any more would just be a waste of money.

For dual weild aggro, grab a frosty, you can pair that up with your tash stick if you want, of grab a defenders lightblade for you offhand for extra dps.

for dps, just grab one of those big 2hs weapons, if you have the money a windblade is quite nice, and could be used in aggro situations. For cheaper versions, get lvl 51 and get an axe of dismay or savage stone axe, both super cheap in the bazaar.

Hope that helps, don't worry too much about accumulating weapons, you'll just end up upgrading them in a few levels.
