Hmm, I guess that depends alot on what sort of combat gear you are wearing(and what kind of weather conditions you are in, cold or warm climate). The combat pants we had back when I was in the army was of a pretty "rough" material, I am not sure it would have been nice to "go commando" in those at all(although I admit I never tried :p).

After a few months I found out that by far the best underwear for wearing on training missions/marches/excerises at least for me was simply a pair of bicycle thights. Very comfortable and keeps you nice and dry, especially when it rains which it did alot during my recruit days hehe. Thights make it alot more bearable to wear clothes on top that are soaking wet, by rain or sweat. Not to mention they also keep you warm, and are alot lighter than the other alternatives we were issued for when it was cold.

But then again, this was in a nordic climate(cold and wet). For warmer conditions "going commando" might be better, I dunno :p
