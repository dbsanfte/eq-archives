I've heard the proc is decent aggro. The problem is, the weapons ratio is only 37 / 40, subpar compared to the standard buyable 2handers (GSoT, WMBS, WB). The quest is long, and really is a bitch. If you have your smithing up, and don't mind taking a lot of time working on it, then it might be worth it. Though GSoT is really cheap these days, and I believe would outperform it in dps, aggro, and hps. It would be a very unique weapon that few would know about though, and would be a fairly proud item to wield, knowing what you had to go through to get it.
