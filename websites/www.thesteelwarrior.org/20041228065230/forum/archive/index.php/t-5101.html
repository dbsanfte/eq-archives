Regarding mez'd mobs and incite:
Just a note, for this I'm considering all taunts successful (I know thats far from true, but just for examples)

Lets say your chanter has 1000 points of hate on the mez'd mob, while you have done nothing to it yet.

If you Incite, you will have 400 hate. Then you taunt puts you at 1001. Next tick, you will be at 1071 (assuming nothing else done to mob in that time).

But if you Taunt first, that would put you at the same 1001 points of hate. Then using incite makes you jump up to 1401, and next tick would be 1471.

So it is actually much better to use taunt first, followed by incite.
