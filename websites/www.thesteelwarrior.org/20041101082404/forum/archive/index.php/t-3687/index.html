In this case, your warrior tanks, IF he dies (only if your cleric went OOM or you forgot to heal him), you only have to rez him, he doesnt require to med (no mana) and only have to rebuff him.
So, Ping Pong = 40sec/mob + med time of your cleric (at some point)+ time used to rez/rebuff casters (and you have 5 casters)
Hold Agro=60sec/mob - Xamount of secs that are gained if the cleric decides to use his spare mana to nuke + time used to rez/rebuff the warrior (which is less than 1 caster and IF he dies)
