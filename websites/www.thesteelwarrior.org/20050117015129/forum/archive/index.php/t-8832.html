I would leave the flagging to guild raids. Don't pass up a freebie, but I wouldn't sweat it till guild is raiding PoP flagging mobs. (not sure if you guys getting Emp keys and doing VT but if you are then add that to your to-do list)

I would combine your AA work with your LDoN whenever possible like Payn said above, and not sure what you guild policies are but would not skip raids if I were logged on.

Even though some of the haste items seem tempting I would hold off on burning your DKP on one (I use tribute myself, you could sell belt and do that).

As far as weap-vs-armor I would say take whatever comes first. Some weaps you don't see that often, and to pass on one you have a shot at might mean you never get one (I'm thinking BF/Hategiver...BoC is pretty common once you start doing AoW). Add BoT tower bosses to your guilds targets, and add GBoS to your agro weap list. Try and spend DKP on things that will last you a long time, and/or things you can get at a bargain.

Buyable upgrades obviously depend on your cash situation. Skip ornate unless you win it yourself. You can't re-sell it if you upgrade via raiding or LDoN.

Anyway, thats my 2cp. Good hunting to you.
