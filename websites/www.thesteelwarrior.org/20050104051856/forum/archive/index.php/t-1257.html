You are in a tough situation because 25k would buy you a lot of nice upgrades. You could get Earring of the Solstice, 2x Valorium rings, Heirophant's cloak all for 25k and you would pick up a ton of stats and HP.

On the other hand, once you get a GSoT, you are set until your guild can get you RB weapons or you can afford to drop 250k+ each for an SoD and a BoC. I have had a GSoT on my now 56 warrior for a bit over a month now. I basically use my warrior to tank when we do small group kills of mobs like Gore, Trak, Vindi and so on. I also use him to tank tier 1 and 2 when there is no one else available. I have had zero problems holding agro with that weapon. My main is a 65 wiz and I can dish out some dps with the agro my warrior is getting. Keep in mind that the vast majority of mobs my war is tanking are all deep red so he depends on agro from procs and DPS, taunt key might as well not even exsist. I am pretty pleased with my GSoT and I heartily recommend it to anyone who is not capable of getting EB.

In your position, I would get the GSoT then start working on getting 1 upgrade at a time as you can afford it.

Just correcting some info about it's drop:

Drops off of Rorek in South wing of BoT. He is a random / rare spawn, does have a set spawn point though. He usually drops nothing at all. BoT drop rate increase was on Tower bosses only, not minor wing named.

It can be resisted a lot, but a resisted proc still is a lot of aggro, and I really doubt you are wanting it for the actual stun instead of the aggro.

Personally, I used it for a month or so. It held aggro great for me.

As for other upgrades. Your stamina is pretty good, at your current level Boar would max out stamina for you. So really, valorium rings would not be much of an upgrade (you would get 8 dex, 8 agi, and 8 str... but thats not worth it). Hiero would be a great buy, or Iksar hide cape. Clattering bone necklace, solstice earring are other good upgrades. But all the hps in the world means nothing if you can't keep aggro, so aggro should be your number one worry. While imo a GSoT would be the best aggro for you, you also could get a frosty and while not have as well of aggro, you could then afford some other upgrades. Frosty should be decent enough, though not near a GSoT.
