Renix
Don't worry too much about equipments, you're doing great for your level. I think I had a bit less when I was 39...

Once you hit 50ish, you may want to consider taking a break from XPing to catch up, gear wise. Theres lots of stuff out there you'd be able to duo with a shaman, druid, or cleric and make money from, or even net some upgrades. Velkators Labrynth would be a good spot once you hit 46ish, and by your 50s you should be able to easily handle the frenzied (or other named) camps with a shaman. The frenzied velium broodling drops Silver Chitin Wristbands, and even at 63 I still use em, mostly because they're the only affordable or easily obtainable wrist items. Even after you get two of em, they go for at least 6k on most servers, and on ARo its common to see them for 8k. All the mobs in velks have a chance to drop velium bricks and blocks, which are usually an easy sale in the bazaar at 100pp per brick/block.

You could also try some Legacy of Ykesha zones, you could probably handle the Gulf of Gunthak with a partner/group at 39, and then move into dulak at 46-50. Most of the mobs there have a chance to drop a gem, and theres also the boats and various other nice camps that give you an equal shot at gems as any other camp, but also drop loot that sells for several thousand plat.

If you really want to get some cash now, you could try going to the feerott and killing any spider in sight for spider silks/spiderling silks. They sell pretty fast in the bazaar for roughly 10 plat each, and if your willing to skill up a bit in tailoring you could also make leather padding (http://www.eqtraders.com for recipes) which sells for about 20-25 plat each in the bazaar. I suggest you just tough it out until 46 or so, then move into one of the zones i suggested with a group or partner, preferably a partner so you could get a bit more of the loot and be able to afford some upgrades in reasonable time. As for what gear you should be looking for, look for stuff that has some AC on it and HP on it. All of your slots should have decent AC and at LEAST 20 HP. STA is nice, but its easily buffed to its maximum and doesn't give you a very noticable HP boost until 55+. I imagine most of the gear that you'd find with AC and HP on it also has some form of STA on it anyways.

I would suggest reaching a minimum of 3k HP and 1k AC by the time your 50ish, that would be more than enough to handle the common level 50 experience zones.
