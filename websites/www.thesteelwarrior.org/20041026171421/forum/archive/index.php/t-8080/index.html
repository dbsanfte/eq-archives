Throag
We got 25ish people keyed for the new VP last night, so we decided to do a massacre trip in there to sea what the new dragons were like.

First of all, you have a statue you can click to get out of VP right at the zone in. Zone in is safe.
There is a lot more trash than there used to be there, the trash dies pretty fast and hits for 500ish but keep in mind that even though we were only 25, we are a txevu guild, so the difficulty wasn't meant for us obviously :). First nice surprise: all the wurms ae single dispel like in ToV.

After clearing some trash we decided to go pay a visit to Hoshkar, he had an AE I always resisted and didnt hit for too much, pretty easy fight even for an elemental guild I think. After that we went to Silverwing, he has some nice tricks that can make the fight pretty interesting for an elemental guild, but no too hard either.
Then we tried to go to phara dar but unfortunately the door is sealed, we figured we had to kill all the dragons in the zone to get the door to open, so we went to Nexona. When going deeper into the zone the trash begins to get harder (lots of yellow or red con mob instead of the blue cons at entrance). Unfortunately it was getting late so some people had left, we were down to 19 people and 2 clerics and died to Nexona. This fight can be pretty fun too but overall given the right gear and/or raid force Nexona shouldn't be a problem either.
After that we decided to leave so I haven't much more to say apart from the fact that on the way to the nameds we killed some random quest givers who dropped some items.

About loot, I think that given the difficulty and the aim of the zone they are pretty well done. Obviously, most of the clikies suck but they are clickies, either they suck or they are overpowered....
