I made the magelo before I made the list, so it's not quite the most hp/ac possible. I had already done the buff calculations when I saw the cloak from Vxed, that I decided a few more hp wasn't worth doing the math over again. With OoW buffs and 305 stamina though, that optimum setup would be close to 11k hitpoints.

what level does this single group need to be?

Since the zones vary so greatly in difficulty, you can progress in those zones. Qinimi is easier than Riwwi is easier than Barindu is easier than Ferubi is easier than Vxed is easier than Tipt. Sewers difficulty varies from about Riwwi to about Vxed. The real value in this lies in the fact that if Tipt or Vxed is too hard for you (or your usual group, if you have one), you can gear up in Ferubi or Barindu. If Ferubi is too hard for you, you can gear up in Riwwi or Qinimi. There's 6 other expansions worth of other upgrade paths as well, but Gates provides options everywhere from bazaar gear to post-time.

Depends what you are talking about ... if you have all your defensive aa's then i'd say you put up a fighting chance in the sewer trials but will have difficuly in the mountains. Ferubi is rather easy as long as you bring good crowd control and dps ... none of the monsters put out that much dps until you hit the yellow/white cons. Named, on the other hand, are quite a different story.

If you can do hard LDoN without too many problems then you should do fine in the sewer 1-4 trials. If you can do Hard LDoN very easily then you should put up at least a fighting chance in the mountains.

As always, though, a talented healer and a great group can make up for any short-comings on the gear side.

*edit- just looked at your magelo. At under 5k hps and 0 aa's you will most likely have some serious problems in GoD trials, though you still will probably do just fine in qinimi and on mini named and xp crawls through ferubi (as long as your group is good). I would shoot for another 1k hps, another 300ish ac before you delve into the trials.
