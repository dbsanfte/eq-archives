If you have your Epic already; combine it to make the Jagged Blade of War ( the 2Her )
Next, put the Jagged Blade of War in a bag or whatever.
Now, find a level 18 or higher Magician.
Have said Magician summon a Sword of Rune ( it's a level 18 spell for a weapon to give the pet )
Have Magician give you the sword and take it and put it in your Red Scabbard. Click the 'Combine' Button on the Red Scabbard and "poof!" Out comes your Blade of Tactics and Blade of Strategy ( the 1Hers )..

Put Red Scabbard in Bank. Don't Destroy it :D

and they fixed the quest too a while back; you used to be able to just complete the Red Scabbard and make the Epics without getting the rest of the parts but no longer will it work.
