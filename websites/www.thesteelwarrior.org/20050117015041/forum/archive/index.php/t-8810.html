There are 4 wings. north, south, east, and west. There are 2 names in north, but the only one I've ever seen is Laef windfall, he is the boss and spawns near the CY teleporter, dropped a flowing water staff last night, sells for 60k, lost the roll, LOL. Mini bosses have no PH, they just pop in front of their respective teleporters every 6 hours.

South is the best wing for names as there are six of them. Go in the first room, then left up some stairs, and then right up some stairs. You are now at the "Tables" named for the tables which are right in front of the name PHs (krigers). Safe spot is in the corner where no mobs spawn. Names here are Thangbrand and Ymir Stormseer, kriger PHs. Up to the left from here is the "Forges" where 2 more names spawn. There is no safe camp here, so either clear the room below them and pull to there, or pull all the way down to the tables camp. Names are Thorolf Sutherland and Valbrand and their PHs are krigers or the occasional jord far. Keep going past the forges and you enter into a great room at the end of which is the earth boss Gaukr Sandstorm. Instead of taking a left and going to Gaukr Sandstorm at the end of the room, go straight across to the opposite side. Just down the first stairs is the PH for Rorek Steelthorn which is usually a kriger but can also be a militis. Last of course is Gaukr Sandstorm at the very end of the main room. There is a very safe spot to pull to in the left corner from the teleport pad. This is a great camp for warriors because at 65 you can single pull the whole room without any adds.

East has the easiest name to get to Eindride Icestorm who is just up the left hallway from the first room, you can pull him to the courtyard after clearing only 2 mobs, kriger PH. Keep going to the very end of that hallway and in the last room is Riodhr Torrentwind. Kriger PH as well. If you take a right when you enter and go all the way up you will get to Oreen Wavecrasher, the water boss. He can be pulled after clearing only 2 corner mobs. Pacify other mobs and pull him to the corner. The teleport is right behind him.

West is the easiest camp to get to, just go up to the top of the stairs and start pulling. All the way in the far back right side are 2 names with kriger PHs. Wybjorn can be pulled to the camp at the stairs and killed there but you better rush the room for Hreidar Lynhillig, the west wing boss, because he gates. If you clear to the left side from the main camp you will wind around right to the teleport room. No names spawn in here but there is a little room off to the left where Torstien Stoneskin spawns, kriger PH. You really should rush his room because he debuffs and gates. Keep casters at longest range possible and they won't get debuffed. Forget about melees because it is a fast 2 slot debuffer, just rebuff after the fight.

I have a 65 druid which I park in the safe room to track. Once you know where all these names are it is pretty easy to get there. Just /ooc a campcheck and then throw together a group and /ooc moving to "name" camp.

Travelling is fairly easy because you can invis to almost anywhere in the mini areas (not the towers). There are a few caveats though. Do not get too close to krigers or names as they will see you, running by is pretty safe though. Do not stop and stand in one place too long while invis because you can be de-invised by little storms that pass by. Finally be careful if running by elementals as they will see thru invis at times. Thats because they are used as pets and when they revert back they can see invis. If you see one of these carrying swords RUN LIKE HELL!!




hope this helps :-)

It's better to have elixir land before the mob makes its first hit. You can start casting elixir as soon as your warrior gets into range (this is not too hard to do while 2-boxing). But when healing is really tight like you described, it is an even better idea to use the cleric's AA hotkeys. Divine Arbitration is your oh-shit-warrior's-gonna-die-now button, it's instantaneous and zero aggro, and you should interrupt your CH to use it if the first heal isn't going to land. When you just want an elixir and don't have time to cast it, use the Celestial Regen AA instead, as it is instantaneous cast and also no aggro. Also, if you know that a named mob is the next pull, throw Vie on the warrior, and even a DI if you can afford the reagent cost. When a mob is tough enough that you barely survive with disciplines, it is definitely tough enough to justify DI - and get a charisma buff from the chanter/shaman to go with the DI.
