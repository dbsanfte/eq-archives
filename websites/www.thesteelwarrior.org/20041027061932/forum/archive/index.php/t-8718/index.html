Since you're playing with friends you could always give it a shot and see what happens. I tanked my first one with about your stats, and we won, but we had a bit more stacked group than it sounds like you do. Get some FT on your cleric friend, and make friends with a good monk to pull and some good dps and you should be able to do it. Stick to EC and EF at first too, they're generally easiest to single. Plus, the bracer you want drops from EF.

Max hit on a warrior in hard ldon is 1018 or thereabouts, and they'll hit you 3-4x a round pretty commonly without defensive AA's or good AC, so you need wicked healing if they don't get slowed. Run DI, and have a HoT on for the pulls. Dispell pulls and EARLY slows are your friends too. Get some augs for your weapons ASAP too (yaks from lguk are a good fit imo) so you can get and hold aggro off those early slows more reliably.

If you don't like a challenge, don't try until you're done with luclin hp and defensive AA, and your cleric has ft7 or so.

Edit: I notice you said you don't have a lot of time to spare usually on normal LDoN's. If thats with your group of 4, you'll probably have the DPS for a hard with two others. If thats including 2 extra dps, you'll never kill enough in a hard unless you pick up some better DPS or start running a CoD pet. Once you get past the basics (staying alive) hard ldons are all about the dps, just because of the timer.
