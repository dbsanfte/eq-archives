Tested out the new melee downtime change this morning, here are the results:

Testing with 8682HP.

Starting at initial regen of 28Hp/tick:

Regenned HP at

28/tick for 10 ticks
32/tick for 10 ticks
44/tick
56/tick
68/tick
80/tick
92/tick
104/tick
116/tick
128/tick


at that point I'd regenned to full. 8kHP regenned in 10 minutes. This is without bandaids.

So, as we can see, after an initial bonus of only 4HP/tick in regen, every minute there is a +12HP/tick bonus to sitting regen, which is cumulative and seemingly without limit.

Testing with bandaing while sitting showed 84HP/tick with 210 bandage, stacked as stated in patch message. Doesn't interrupt HP increase.

Using bandages while sitting and regenning, allowed me to regen 8KHP in 8 minutes instead of 10, for an approximately 20% decrease in downtime over sitting and regenning alone.
