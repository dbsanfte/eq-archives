Well, here's what I can tell you so far.

Parry > Block. For 2 reasons.

1. Parry requires melee weapon. Block requires shield. So if you go 2h, you still benefit from parry.

2. Parry absorbs the entire hit, Block only absorbs as much as the block rating from your shield + some modifier for strength.

Personally, I'm going straight defensive until I see a reason to do otherwise. Soloability appears to increase with weaponry. Going from a 7.6 dps sword to an 8.6 dps axe is just fuckin huge on the soloability scale, at least from what I've seen.
