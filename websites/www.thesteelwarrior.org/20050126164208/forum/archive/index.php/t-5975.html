Yep, the Malevolent gear is going to be the best bet if you are doing lots of LDoN. I'd start with the Greaves and the Boots in your case. For the price of one Takish piece you can have 2 Miragul's pieces. And there really aren't too big of differences between them:

Malevolent Boots:
+31 AC +5 Str +5 Agi +5 Dex +3 vsMg +3 vsFire +3 vsCold +3 vsPos +3 vsDis +55 HP +65 Mana

Royal Attendant Boots:
+34 AC +5 Sta +6 Agi +4 Wis +4 Int +4 Cha +4 vsMg +4 vsFire +4 vsCold +4 vsPos +4 vsDis +70 HP +80 Mana

Difference (raw): 3AC, 5Sta, -5Str, 1Agi, -5Dex, 1Saves, 15hp.

Malevolent Greaves:
+44 AC +8 Sta +5 Wis +5 Int +8 Cha +10 vsMg +10 vsCold +70 HP +65 Mana

Royal Attendant Greaves:
+48 AC +10 Str +10 Agi +6 Wis +6 Int +13 vsFire +13 vsDis +85 HP +80 Mana

Difference (raw):4ac, -8Sta, +10Str, +10agi, 15hp, -10svm, -10svc, +13 svf, +13svd.

So not counting saves (which are almost identical in amount, and differ in types) here's what you miss out on going with Malevolent at half the cost on these pieces:

-7ac, -3sta, -5str, -11agi, +5dex, -30hp.

So sure, that's a small amount of important stats, but for the cost for where you're at, I'd certainly go with the Malevolent pieces.


As for the GSoT, I'd agree that I wouldn't stress too much about the Droga weapons. You'll eventually come across the camp open and pick em up (nice to have a choice of dual wielding and 2H) but until then the GSoT works just fine. Once you're swimming in LDoN points, you can pick up a Ykesha augment from Guk, or an Anger aug from EF and make it even a stronger agro weapon, but I've tanked BoT with a GSoT+Provoke so it's certainly a nice weapon as you've also discovered.

Aside from those, next time you have several k of plat pick up that second Vallorium Ring. You'll be plenty geared up for 65 LDoN's and XP situations.

Yes, the 760 armor sets are much easier to attain, and about two-thirds as good as the 1492. The 1492 chests have 100 hps/100 mana typically, and the 760 chests have 75/75. I got the 15 stamina ring the first day Lost Dungeons went live, and have never seen it drop since. Both my 1h blunt weapons are dungeon items too (in my bag).

Have you considered High risk LDoN? Even if you can't complete it in time, drops there are really sweet if you can manage to kill a mini-named.

I agree with ignoring the Tak armor.

How does your group tank? Do you always MT or the paladin? Have you considered shareing agro?

With the paladin stunning a lot, it'll help reduce enemy DPS. It'll be tough to get agro back off him, but when Taunt works, it works. You can also /shield him if he gets low and you don't get a successful Taunt.

If the paladin is MT, I'd want more DPS. I equaled the DPS of a 63 rogue I grouped with recently, and I surpass 65 monk damage if they don't have much vengeance. Consider Vengeance augs and the 150* cold DD aug for your WMBS. Ykesha may also be a consideration if you want agro with your DD.

*The lesser cold DD augs are not nearly as good because the 150 one has -25 resist modifier. If you want a lesser DD, go with non-cold.
