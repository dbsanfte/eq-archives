Shaman can make potions that send you to the succor point of various newbie zones (Field of Bone, Everfrost, Feerott, Innothule and Nektulos, I believe). The component parts to making this potion are considerably lower than making the "gate" potions, and the savings is generally passed on to the consumer.

Thurg gate potions are a good option for those who are on OK terms with the coldain.

If you are not KoS in the Outpost in the Overthere, do the "hand in berserker tails" quest ad nauseum. Once your faction is high enough, you can get the Overthere Hammer, which procs a gate to the Overthere (near the PoK book, that's handy).

There is a quest in PoK for a necklace with gate. All of the pieces for the first stage of the necklace can be gathered in Tier 1 PoP.

I am not sure if they still drop, but gloves with a "gate to nexus" clicky effect are one of the possible rewards for the "escort the gnome" event in Cazic Thule.
