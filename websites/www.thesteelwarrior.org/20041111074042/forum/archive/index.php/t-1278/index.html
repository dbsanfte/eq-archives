Greetings!
You've probably got a few training points lying around at your local Warrior's guildhouse. Those could be used to prime the pump - so to speak - but are not really necessary. Throw at least one training point into each.
After that, getting skill is a matter of swinging at XP-giving mobs. Logic follows that the more swings you can get in, the faster your skill will raise. DEX helps too, so I'm told. What worked for me to get lagging skills up was to get weapons that are fast for their type, and low hitters. Then get the best Haste I could afford. Then find a place where I could fight mobs that flee when low on HPs. If you can team up with a snarer this is much safer; or , just snare 'em yourself with a whip or Kunzar K'unch. After beating the mob into fleeing, I'd switch to my training weapon and lay into it. Brought peircing, 2HB, 1HB, and H2H all to max (for 30th) with this method.
A safer method that garners no XP is to ask groups that are in a XP area for your level if they mind you practicing on their kills. Always be polite, and mindful not to break their pattern of killing (off-tanking, mezz-breaking, etc.).

Hope this helps! May Brell fill your cup with his own hand!
