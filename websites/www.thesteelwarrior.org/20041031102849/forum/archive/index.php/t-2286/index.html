The following is a small list of the areas most in need of upgrade and a few suggestions:

drovlarg mantle: forged shoulderpads, fierce heraldic pauldrons

orc fang earring: bloodmetal earring of engagement, mosquito leg earring, earring of the solstice (no drop, but i believe the pieces are MQable)

hero bracers: crystal chitin wristband, bracelet of fortitude, fierce heraldic bracer

shissar fangs: idol of the thorned, nighmarewood compound bow

grotesque alloy mask: crystal spider eyes, death mask of the elysians, fierce heraldic visor

Not sure what these run on your server, but if nothing else just check out whats available in the bazaar in your price range: ac, hp, and sta are your #1 concerns.

Not sure if you're looking for weapons, but you may want to invest in something with a good aggro proc like a greatstaff of thunder - these are under 5k in many servers now.
