If someone had said "I'm starting a dark elf warrior" three years ago, I'd have said "You have clearly lost your mind". Back then the lower levels were alot tougher (and a lot slower). The game has been streamlined to make getting to the mid levels easier and faster, and had most faction issues eliminated, since then. Before, large races had the best starting stats, and barbarians were the only large race that didnt have pretty severe faction problems. Also gear to compensate for the frailty most smaller races start with was much harder to get. With the game the way it is now, play what you want, when you get to 60+, you can stand an ogre, an elf, and a gnome side by side, and there will be little to no difference in their stats. Only real difference will be who looks better. And clearly opinions vary on that one. Personally, I like being a burly, tattooed, kilt wearing oaf that runs around smashing stuff and getting drunk, but that's just me. :)
