The event itself is broken into I think it was 3 levels. You get ported into the first area, Open a trap door, and jump down a tube or throat into a stomach with mobs. Once you clear that area, ya jump down to the next area. There is no way to get back up, and I dont know if the mobs inside will eventually respawn, or if they like LDoN and only spawn when you first start the event.

When you leave the final area you will find the real mob spawned but I am pretty sure you come out outside of his aggro range, but we did land on a couple of tormentors.
Aurthur
Did the event last week for fun with a group 10 folks. Very-very-very interesting encounter. =) There is one level in particular that the next time we'll probably go through and see if it respawns (the one with 2 down tubes). The hope is that if we simply clear the 10 or so skinless creatures on that level then wait 25 minutes, they'll respawn like normal zone mobs...if not, then supposedly it should be possible to egress out of that area, fight back to Baraguj and get reported back in...so long as you don't kill the 'Unimaginable Horror' at the bottom. If you do, after he dies you will be ported out to 2-4 mobs...at the top of the ramp/entrance area to Baraquj's tower. He'll be respawned at the previously empty base of the tower, and he's not rooted, hits extremely fast, seriously mitigates slow, and was pretty tight with 10 people (1 cleric, 1 druid).

Note: if you do go to the tower and the Baraguj you see is the one at the bottom of the tower (not the top) then someone has killed the stomach event, but left the 1300-hitting named up...he won't port you to his stomach again (have heard of folks getting that surprise on their first trip).

On one stomach clearing, we got 2 bloods. (oi).
