v0 is item haste, v1 is icon haste (spells, clickys, some songs), v2 is ervaj song line haste and v3 is vah shir/rizlona's line of bard songs. v0, v1, and v2 stack up to 100% and v3 is 'overhaste' and raises the haste cap by that percent and may also add that amount of haste. Been argued both ways and cant remember any definitive logs proving one way or the other.

Since your bracer is 46% v0 haste, and say you have VQ which is 68% v1 haste, youd be at 114% haste which would be capped at 100%. If you had v3 haste, say Rizlonas, your haste cap would be raised to 130% and you would then get that extra 14%. Thats assuming Rizlonas doesnt also add 30% in which case the 14% from v0 and v1 haste is lost.

So you may/may not need v3 haste to get the extra haste from your bracer depending on how you think v3 haste works.
