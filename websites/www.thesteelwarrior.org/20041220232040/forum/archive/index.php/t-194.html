HP per point of stamina is:

HP = (ND* INT[Lvl x LM / 300 ])

From my crib notes, taken from elsewhere (I believe this is the formula Magelo uses):

Total HP = INT [ND* (5 + INT[ Sta x Lvl x LM / 300 ] + INT[ Lvl x LM ])]

ND is the multiplier bonus they use (Natural Durability, Physical Enhancement, etc; percentage based, so ND = 1 for no mods, 1.1 for ND3)

LM is a class and level dependent multiplier as indicated below:


Level multipliers known:

Monk, Rogue, Beastlord and Bard:
Level 1-50: Levelmultiplier = 18
Level 51-57: Levelmultiplier = 19
Level 58-65: Levelmultiplier = 20

Level 1-65: Levelmultiplier = 15

Ranger:

Level 51-55: Levelmultiplier = 24
Level 56-59: Levelmultiplier = 25

more accurate formula ( shamelessly stolen from magelo forums ). this formula is exact, with 1 small problem : they are still trying to figure out when are the TRUNC. until now, this is the best they have :

HP = TRUNC[ 5 x ND ] + TRUNC [ Lvl x LM x ND ] + TRUNC [ TRUNC [ ( STA - TRUNC [(STA-255)/2] ) x Lvl x LM / 300 ] x ND ]

Where the term TRUNC [(STA-255)/2] is always 0 or greater (i.e. when STA is below 255, it is considered equal to 0). LM is a class and level dependent multiplier as indicated in previous post

my 'TRUNC' and Undafut's 'INT' means truncated and integer , and mean you remove the decimal part of the number : 1.2 becomes 1, 10.999 becomes 10 ( it is truncated and not rounded), ...


back to your 33 warrior with 144 stamina : ND = 1, LM = 25, Lvl=33, so
HP = [ 5 x 1 ] + [ 33 x 25 x 1 ] + [ [ ( 144 - 0) x 33 x 25 / 300 ] x 1 ]
HP = 5 + 825 + 396
HP = 1226hp , more hps from your stuff = 179 = 1405 hps. luckily i get same result than magelo , using same formula they use :)

and on your magelo profile :
Base Direct Indirect Total
1105 179 121 1405

Base is your naked HPs , Direct is hp from stuff , Indirect is hps u gain from going from 100 to 144 stamina ( with stuff ).
