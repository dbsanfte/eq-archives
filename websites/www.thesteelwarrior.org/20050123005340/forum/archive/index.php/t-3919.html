It is easy to avoid adds on Mujaki. Kill all that you can when they are forming up. Once Mujaki becomes targetable, have a couple of SKs go pull. SK 1 snares Mujaki with darkness, SK 1 runs around the area to separate Mujaki from the unsnared adds. Then SK 1 FD and SK 2 pulls Mujaki. If any adds still, SK 2 can FD and another person can tag off him. SKs having fear immunity helps but not necessarily required. Can substitute a monk for SK2 and a sacrificial ranger/druid for SK1 if they don't mind eating a death to pull, since ranger/druid snare lasts longer.
