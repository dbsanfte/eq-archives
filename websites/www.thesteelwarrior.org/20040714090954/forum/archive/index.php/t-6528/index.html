The "over the top" thread got me thinking a bit and I sat down with a spreadsheet to calculate proc based hate.

Solid Assumptions with emperical evidence
- Primary hand innate weapon procs always proc 2x per minute unadjusted (dual wield or no)
- Secondary hand innate weapon procs always proc 1x per minute unadjusted
- Weapon Affinity increases proc rate 10% on the innate and augmented weapon procs per AA level ending with a 50% bonus at WA5
- Rarely, weapons have a proc modifier that increases proc rate on the innate weapon proc

Assumptions without emperical evidence
- A 0.0 second stun is 400 hate
- Proc damage adds linearly to the hate with a 2/3 modifier (150 dd+0.0s stun = 500 hate)

Then:

DBoTW+Anger3 & EoE+Anger3 8345
DBoTW+Anger3 & EoE+125Yak 8170
BoW+Anger3 3900
EoE+100Yak & BoC 4820
