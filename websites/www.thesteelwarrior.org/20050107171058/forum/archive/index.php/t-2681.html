Assuming that both you and the pally are 60+ by the time you do this, I would really not worry about the AE Fear. Get a Guard of Druzzil before the fight and you should resist all of them.

As for the DoT, 100hp/tick is not nearly as bad as it sounds. Make sure you get regen and spiritual dominion. With those 2 buffs, not including natural and worn regen, the DoT is only doing 76hp/tick. Make sure the SK casts Zevfeer's bite and that number is reduced further to 56 hp/tick. Even the wussy casters should have over 3500hp with buffs (make sure to get good buffs in PoK before you go). So if the fight lasts 3 minutes you are only talking 1680 damage if you don't resist it.

The rest of the fight is cake. He doesn't even hit as hard as a lot of PoP exp mobs, I seem to remember him hitting in the 600-700 range. I think you will be surprised at how easy this fight really is.
