I would like to see pure melee classes haveing an inate haste of 60% (due to focused training). The haste cap would remain the same but with a 40% haste item the pure melee classes would never be less than 100% haste. I cant see how this would have any overpowering effect in a raid or group , but it would help us solo if we cant find a group , and it could help us hold aggro against mobs that dispell your buffs.

The second idea is a bit out there but i like it ;) . Pure melee would be able to enter any populated area and "attract a follower" it would be similar in power to the top SK pet. Since pure melee can do very little in the way of buffing i think they should get the same "follower" each time they use the ability. The advantage to this is that all that no drop gear cluttering up your bank could be given to the "follower". Weapons, armor , ,haste item whateva, makeing them helpful to a pure melee soloing or adding some dps if grouped.

While this might not address many of the problems that plague todays pure melee classes , I think it would help reduce the frustration of playing the LFG game, which can and does suck. I dont pay to play EQ so i can sit on my ass waiting for someone to take pity on me. This way i could at least solo somewhat effectivly while waiting for a group.
