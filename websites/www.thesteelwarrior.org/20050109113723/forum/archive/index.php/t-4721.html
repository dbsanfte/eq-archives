Move on a few years to just before the advent of PoP and they have these skills but agro was not such a big issue. They got these skills from a balancing effort of the developers in reaction to knights being much less attractive in groups and raids than the classes fulfilling the same jobs. A situation that has mirrored the last 10 months for warriors to an extent.

As for less mana used with a warrior tank unfortunately thats not the case alot of the time as has been so often mentioned. The wait to slow and ping-pong damage on most of the other players will drain the mana of a cleric far faster.

With a group watching its agro and a slower waiting till agro is established will again often mean more mana used as the costly fast heals hit your mana pool for more than an extra CH ever will when the RNG is being stingy on those all important procs. Not always the case but certainly not uncommon.

Itemisation is certainly an issue and I am glad to see the developers make an effort to address warriors both low and high lvl. I am hoping to see the players and developers reactions over the next month to see a resolution to the problems we have in the game at the moment. Hopefully the lines of communication will stay open and not the closed door with a suggestion box pinned to it thats been the case for so long now.
