06-14-2004, 07:12 AM
You might be able to find some people hunting in Neth lair; I recently leveled an alt and found some folks there--not many, but they happened to know how to play. There are usually groups in M. Seru.
Loot's not that great in these places, though.
You can try the spires outside Splitpaw in South K.; often folks are just soloing there, however. Gnolls drop fine steel weapons, though, and you can probably take the gnolls solo. It's a long run out there.
You can also try High Hold Keep.
And don't forget LDoNs.
In a couple of levels you can try the Overthere and then the Dreadlands.
For loot, you'll probably start to notice that farming in certain areas will result in better loot than you'll get in most experience zones. Any kind of silk will sell well in the bazaar. You can hunt spiders in East K (and the lions, which drop hides that sell well in bazaar); You can also travel to Shadeweavers and kill worms and rockhoppers (silks and hides again).

EQdiva has an excellent compilation of the ZEM listings.

However, after having levelling this Warrior from level 1 to level 56 basing my experiencing solely on the list found there, I've begun to wonder if in fact the base experience of specific zones is in fact higher than that of other zones. I understand this goes against years of what people have believed, however I have found that dark blue mobs that are one level in some zones with high ZEMS happen to apparently give the same amount of experience (or less) than lower level cons in zones with equal ZEMs.

This is the link: http://www.eqdiva.com/eq/zoneLevels.aspx

An example of what I'm speaking of goes something like this: Estate of Unrest and Solusek A both have a 73% ZEM. However, the level of mobs in Solusek A are higher, yet I would get more exprience killing in Unrest than I would killing in Solusek A at any given level between 25-29. Two likely, although not the only 2 possible, explainations would be either the ZEM info given is inaccurate, or base exp/mob compared to level of character exping is higher in certain zones than in others.

edit: when I state I would get more experience killing in Unrest, I mean on a kill by kill basis. Certainly the mobs in Unrest would have less HP, thus granting faster kills and faster overall experience, but that is not what I was referring to. Whereas I might get 2%exp/kill in Unrest, I'd get less in Sola.
