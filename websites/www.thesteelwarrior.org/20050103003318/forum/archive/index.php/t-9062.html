Quick easy version to bypassing faction in thurg.

Go to pok, the brewing vendors by the large bank, go to the brew barrel and skill up on ale untill it trivials at 68.

Go to thurg, make sure you have some sort of invis and hand the dorf 35pp. You`ll get jumped for sure, but if you can`t handle 2-3 green con dorfs then you don`t deserve the epic anyway =P

Regular coffee beans, I had an alt buy them, combined in a brew barrel in the bar where the quest npc is, both generic brew and coldain coffee trivial below 68.

Stand next to the quest npc, drop invis and kill 3-4 more green con dorfs, hail and then hand in the coldain coffee. The dorf will walk, invis up and follow him, when he stops hand in the items. There is no real danger here, at most you will have to kill 8 green/light blue con to 65 npcs .
