It has been confirmed by the chat with SOE devs on Gamespy that the armor "Augmentation" does infact change the item itself. These changes are not like the armor dye system, infact he admitted that they had to rewrite coding for the entire item base to be able to handle this.

To go on further with this, the changes made to an item are not permanent. You CAN take off an augmentation from an item however you must bear in mind that the upgrade reagent will be lost forver. Therefore you should think twice before putting that +10str/-10sta mod on your sword, for example.

Also, the devs went on to say that the reward system is going to be on a purchasable system. From what I gathered from what was said, you can sort of "buy" the rewards based on your success through the dugeons. They didn't elaborate on it, however it does seem like you will have some choice in what loot you actually will be receiving from participating in LDoN events.

Further more, the devs explained that there will be 48 new zones all of which you have access to participate in whether you are 32 or 65 for example. They went on to explain the hardware upgrades for this, and said something along the lines of 55,000 players can be participating at once. I could be wrong, will have to double check it when they post the logs.
