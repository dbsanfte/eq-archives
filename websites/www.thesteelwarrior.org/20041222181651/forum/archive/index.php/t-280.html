You said you are HoT capable, so I am not sure if you are doing it now, have some of the pieces etc...

Ear - I know a lot of people like the Solstice, but I am guessing you will be doing HoT gear next. In light of that - pick up 2 Electrified Earring of the Tempest - 7ac 35hp 35mana 12svm 12svc 12vf. By doing so, you will have to swap out less gear for resists and keep your ac/hp gear.

Back - Again, from an HoT MT standpoint, kill Lodi and get a Lodi Shield. By getting the shield piece, you're saving yourself about 13k instead of outright buying it, and getting 20svc and 20svf, as well as EB.

Shoulder - get an Imbued Granite Spaulder. I see them as low as 3k on DRo. 20ac, 10 save to all.

Finger - get another Valorium Ring of Gallantry.

Those are the things I would get in your situation since the upgrades will flow well once you hit HoT. You're looking to spend about 23k on all of them, easily farmed in a week if you know the right spots and have the time.

For me, the difference tanking in HoT with those resist items, versus using BD gear was night and day.

As far as AA's, you may consider doing CA2 or 3, and CS2 or 3 before you do PE. The extra 2.5% hp you'll get will probably be outweighed by the increased mitigation from those 2 AAs. Just my line of thought...
