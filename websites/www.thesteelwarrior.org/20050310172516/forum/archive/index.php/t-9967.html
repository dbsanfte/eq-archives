I've taken the time to breakdown some statistics for TSW up to this point in the year.. I figure someone else may find this interesting as well:

Up to this point in the year, TSW has had:
1,304,635 Sessions
11,796,003 Pageviews
151,873,535 Hits

Averages:
3,564.58 Sessions/day
32,229.52 Pageviews/day
414,955.01 Hits/day
9:40 Average time spent per session with 9.04 pages viewed per session

The busiest time of the year was September when the epic was posted, at which point we experienced 174,613 sessions in a month - almost double the monthly average.

During the year, we've had approximately 260,000 unique visitors to the webpage - over 3/4 of them returned at least once.

37.6% of users started their browsing at the forum index page, 27.02% went straight to a thread, whereas 1.45% (!!!!!) used the homepage.

91,702 sessions were referred to us from Google.com (#1 search term - steel warrior) while 15,754 came from Yahoo - Google canada was the #3 search engine with 5,174 referalls.

Out of the resolved visitors, Roadrunner internet is our most popular ISP followed by Comcast and ATT.

1.66% of our visitors are from the US Military - slightly above the 1.35% from Japan.

That's all for now =)
