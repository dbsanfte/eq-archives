Similar tactic to those posted above.

Clear the room before HP

Have a DA person drag the entire HP room (12 mobs) into the jail/prep area.

Priests = tank mez (clerics, will summon, heal, *dispels other mobs*)
Zels = mezzable
Disciples = cleric/necro banish, or charm

DA pull the entire HP room back to the jail. If your puller is a bard, they can hit Fading Memories as mobs just start coming into the jail, this will split the room into 2 pulls.

Get the priests tank mezed, kill everything else. 2 knights, a druid, and a shaman should be able to hold down the 4 mobs once things settle down.

Charge the HP room, have chanters ready to mez the zels as they respawn. Make sure they are ready for the HP to dispel mezzes...

Have necros ready to banish the disciples as they respawn. The raid should break off from the HP and kill the disciples if you don't have enough necros. Charm is bad here, the HP will dispel it and the disciple eats the necro.

An additional option is to have a couple chanters mez the Zels in the jail. This needs to be done carefully or the priests will just keep dispelling the mezzes. We prefer to have the chanters come into the HP room so they can contribute to the HP fight as well.

You don't need to clean the golems in the room before HP. You just need to kill the 2 chapel guards. The golems won't aggro if you stay in the middle.

After that, pretarget and pull the whole room and split / mez/banish-kill as explained above.

Then send MT alone in HP room, ask him to go on the other side of the room and aggro HP with some arrows or some aggro spells so he turns around and use his AE stun on him.
Then move the raid in HP room and kill HP.
