You must have the 3 ikkinz 1 man trials completed as he is part of the kod taz task quest. He is a fun mob to kill, splits 2 ways then 2 ways again, but is really easy to gimp. Anyway the splits drop no loot, but they do drop the item you need to finish the quest.
