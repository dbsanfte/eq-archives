Varmill
You combine the Blades and Hilts in the Red Scabbard. You will then receive 2x 1 handed Weapons, Blade of Strategy and Blade of Tactics. Now this is where it gets interesting. With the Introduction of LDoN and Augments, you can now have four 1 handed weapons and one 2 hander.

Combine the two 1 Handers in you scabbard to make the 2 Hander. Have a mage summon you a Sword of Runes. Put the Sword of Runes into the Scabbard and hit Combine. You will now have two 1 Handers and a 2 Hander now.

Take the 2 Hander, Jagged Blade of War and the Red Scabbard to the Dorf next to Wenden and Kragek in East Freeport and hand him the 2 Hander and the Scabbard. And Receive new Augmentable Versions of the Jagged Blade of War, Blade of Strategy and Blade of Tactics.

You Should Now have, Two Red Blades, Two Blue Blades and the Jagged blade of War.
