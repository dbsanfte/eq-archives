There is no "minimum weapon delay". The effective delay of your weapon is delay/(1 + haste_percentage_as_a_decimal). So if you are 41% hasted, with a 22-delay weapon, the effective delay is 22/(1+.41), or 22/1.41, or 15.6, which I believe (but am not certain) rounds up to 16.

Basically, you can ignore haste when comparing weapons. It scales linearly without regard to absolute damage, delay, or ratio. The same weapon combination that is best at 0% haste is still best at 100% haste.

Since haste is calaculated as a function of the number of attacks per time, haste scales equally with all weapons.

The myth about a minimum weapon delay comes from parses inaccurately calculating weapon speed due to the log file time stamps being in 1 second intervals.
