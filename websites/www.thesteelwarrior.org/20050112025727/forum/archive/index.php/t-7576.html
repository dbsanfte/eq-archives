Or do I miss another interesting option here?

Nope. Go anger 3.

Option 1: WA 1-3 for 12 AAs

I'd get sturdiness 1 and maybe 2 after that... it's probably worth the points for the return at that low of cost. DPS AAs are important, but frankly the added aggro is better, and the low levels of sturdiness are too, in my opinion. If you want to raid in the EPs, get sturdiness done and WA done for the ass kicking factor, then worry about your DPS. Tanking doesn't require DPS, and EP guilds won't care if you have any of those AAs. They'll care about your hp/ac primary, aggro secondary for your application.

EDIT: Switch your weapons. Mainhand the GBoS until you anger 3 your BoC at very least, maybe keep it mainhand afterwards. You're only getting 4 procs per minute when you should be getting 5.
