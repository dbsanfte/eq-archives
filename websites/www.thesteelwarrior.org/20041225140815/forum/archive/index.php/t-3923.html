Gimmel
10-29-2003, 07:22 PM
You get a certain amount of hate per swing, not using haste reduces your overall hate production significantly. Procs are a significant source of aggro for warriors, but they are not the whole picture. Read more here: http://www.thesteelwarrior.org/forum/showthread.php?s=&threadid=3789

As far as riposte goes, it's not a matter of how big a percentage of total damage it is, it's a matter of putting yourself at a much higher risk of taking an extremely bad round that your healing isn't going to be able to deal with.
