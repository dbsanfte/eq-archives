Bulbog
Kill the yellow cons at the top spawns the blue cons. 5 waves of the blue cons, very easy. Mini named then spawns, it may charge your camp I forget, really it's harder than peregrin, has decent hp. Then 5 more waves of blue cons this time they charge your camp. 4 red cons spawn, bring them down to 10% and they auto regen and run away back onto the pyramid. Peregrin then charges your camp after the 4 are killed and back up on the pryamid. There's a bug where you can accidentally kill the red cons if you're not careful which bugs the event.

Haladan
After you beat down the last Mound of rubble(making it return to temple), a Rock Monstrosity will spawn. And he will come running to you asap. Hits for the same as Perigrin R. So be ready for him. After he die, you get the stone heaps auto pulling in packs of 4 or 5. When you have killed enough spawns of these, Perigrin spawn and auto pulls(charges raid), if you get PH he will stay at temple. If you need a break between Rock Monstrosity and Perigrin, just mez one of the stone heaps til you are ready again.
