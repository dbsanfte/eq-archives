I wrote like a dumbass. I will attempt to clarify.

Stamina is in the fitness power pool.

Stamina is the last of the 4 powers in the pool.

All the power pools bottom most power "open up" when you have met the following criteria:

1. You are 20th level
2. You have taken 2 of the 3 previous powers.

So in Fitness power pool the powers are:

Swift
Leaping (forget the exact name)
Health
Stamina

In order to get "stamina" you must be level 20 AND have taken 2 powers out of the first 3 listed (swift, leaping, and health).

I hope I made it clear now. So to get the killer kick ass stamina regen you need to "waste" (IMO) 2 powers instead of getting more useful powers.
