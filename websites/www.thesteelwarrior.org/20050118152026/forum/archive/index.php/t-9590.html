The boots and bracer idea above are good ones in the armor category; there are better items out there for both slots, of course, but cultural tends to be pretty strong in those slots, and very very good for the cost.
But I'd also point out that your gear is extremely good for the level of that warrior. If a mob thumps you, it's not your gear that will be the cause. You could level to 65 with what you've got on and be okay. And even if you have a lot of plat right now, that might not be a bad idea, depending on your server's economy. On mine, some items are dropping in price, and I would think that this is an effect of OoW and some pretty nice gear turning up.
I'd sell the splitblade. If my shaman were in a pick up group with a warrior I didn't know, and the warrior equipt a splitblade for tanking, I'd be really really nice to him. That's because a warrior using a splitblade is obviously the alt of a caster or is being twinked by a caster, and the caster might be a cleric, and you never know when my warrior main might need a rez. I would also, however, assume that my shaman is about to die.
Your magelo is showing you also have a GSoT and a WMBS, so I'm not sure you do really need help in the 2HS and 1HB categories. If you are in a dps role, the WMBS is pretty decent. Of course, if you sell the splitblade you can get a better 2HS, but the price climbs fast beyond the WMBS.
