Basically, it depends on wheither you need the GoD AAs yet. I did Run, regen, ND3, CS/A1, PE, CS/A2, then WA1. If you have procing weapons (all wars should) and having any sort of aggro probs, WA will clear that up fast. Just with the first lvl of it, I proc almost 2x more. I just incite on inc and turn on auto attack and I never have aggro probs again (yes I use taunt if the chanter is slower, mezzer, and tasher). I don't see any other GoD AAs really being all the much more useful then the normal defensive AAs. Would say do sturdiness when done with defensive AAs if your HP sucks (like mine). Other then that, I wouldn't throw in any GoD AAs into the normal defensive line.
