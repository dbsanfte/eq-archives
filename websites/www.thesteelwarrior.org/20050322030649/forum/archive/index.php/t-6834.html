arf arf

I'm assuming that this is it (http://www.microsoft.com/downloads/details.aspx?familyid=7fc46855-b8a4-46cd-a236-3159970fde94&displaylang=en).

Yes, thats the one. When you get it installed, on the left you should see a bit list of programs. Look for Everquest, then when you click on it an entry in the right with eqgame.exe should show up. Right click on this entry, and then click "disable entry". This will enable alt tabbing out, but be warned that the windows key and ANY window that would normally pop up over programs will now pop over EQ instead of EQ staying on top.

To make it easier, use alt tab and cycle through until the last two (or three + windows, dependant on how many your boxing) windows tabbed were both EQ. Then you can just press Alt tab to switch to the other window. Its not as convenient as EQW, but it doesn't violate your EULA and its just as easy to use, with a little effort.
