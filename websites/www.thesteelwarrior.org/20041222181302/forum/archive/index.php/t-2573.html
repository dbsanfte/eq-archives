My idea:

When a warrior (level 1-65+) is targetted by a mob, the mob is innately slowed for as long as the mob is targetted on the warrior.

This slow has the same agro effect as any slow and will stack with any other slows on the mob. The slow is not "mob" based, it is mechanics based. When a warrior is targetted, attacks land on the warrior 10% slower then they would on any other class, just because the warrior is targetted. Mobs immune to slow still are slowed by 10% because they have targetted a warrior.

This is all because a warrior is a fierce fighter and it is harder to land a blow on them. This makes the mob need to slow down to try land a blow.

Pros:

Warrior receives 10% less damage if mob is slowed 10% (duh) and is therefore more viable as a tank without putting the warrior on a different defensive table.

More agro received on warrior because the warrior frustrates the poor mob who can't quite deal with the warrior as efficiently as they would deal with other classes.

Trondir, that's not how haste works.

22 percent haste increases damage output (not delay) by 22 percent, so damage is 1.22 times what it was. Delay is 82 percent of what is was in case you were curious (1/1.22 = new delay, since damage and delay are inversely related), and at capped (100 percent) haste, delay is 50 percent of what is was (if you swing twice as often, you do twice the damage, what a revelation).

For a 75 percent decrease in damage, 1 - .75 = .25 (assuming slow is really a 75 percent decrease in damage). This is a 4 fold decrease in delay.

Stacking a 10 percent slow on that means 1 - .85 = .15 - which is 60 percent of the just slowed damage (.15/.25).

A 10 percent stacking slow, then, is insanely unbalanced. Well, how about one that doesn't stack?

Assuming slow doesn't stack, a 10 percent slow is a total waste of time since it's not enough to do away with a slower and too little to not be overwritten by any slow that's actually cast (65 BST has 65% slow, SHM has 75% slow magic based and 40% disease based and ENC has 65% slow).

Although in this idea's defense it's not nearly as stupid as making AE taunt a level 1 warrior ability.
