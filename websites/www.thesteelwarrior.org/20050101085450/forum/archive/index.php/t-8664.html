That really isnt a valid discussion. Why? AA's are why.

Bazaar monk and warrior, lets say each have 50AA.

Warrior = 50 in defense
Monk = 25 defense, 25 offense, or worse, 50 offense.

Now, Time, KT or better gear, each have...say 350AA

Warrior = All defense and offense maxed
Monk = All offense maxed too

Bazaar level monks and warriors have a large disparity in possible DPS due to how they choose to spend thier first 50AA. Once each have 300+ AA, its a different comarison, as both will likely have nearly, if not every, DPS increasing AA.
