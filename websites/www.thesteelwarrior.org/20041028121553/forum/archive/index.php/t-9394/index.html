Kiya
Gave the profile a once over. Here are a few suggestions that popped into my head that are not ldon merchant armors:

Neck- Amulet of Mummy wrappings, Shell Inlaid Collar, Serpentine Necklace. All are droppable and shouldn't cost too much in the bazaar.
Ears- Mosquito Leg Earrings or Bloodmetal Earrings of Engagement. Both are good ears, if you feel like camping one (Mos. Leg) or paying good plat for the other.
Face- I'd reccomend picking up a Mask of Frozen Mana (dropped from EF ldons) or a Mask of Flourishing Fungus (dropped in Guk ldons) and moving your 10ac augment on to that. You won't lose any AC and you'll gain some HPs.
Finger- Replace your Flesh Eater's Ring with another Valorium one.
Shoulders- You could go to Plane of Mischeif and camp a Grand Pauldron of Study with 2 or 3 partners. I have one in my profile.
