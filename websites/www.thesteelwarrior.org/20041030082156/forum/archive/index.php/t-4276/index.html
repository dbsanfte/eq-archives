Here are some typical weapon acronyms you'll see:


WMBS- War Marshall's Bladed Staff
SoD - Sceptre of Destruction
CHoS - Copper Hammer of Striking
BoC- Blade of Carnage
SoTBS- Sword of the Bloodsworn
GSoT- Greatstaff of Thunder
GSot4W- Greatstaff of the Four Winds
SBoD- Splitblade of Destruction
BoW - Blade of War
SEWS - Stone Etched War Sword
EE- Edge of Eternity (or essence emerald if in a different context)
DBoW - Dark Blade of the Warlord
VDD - Venazir, Dreamer's Despair (Though most people just call it Venazir)
