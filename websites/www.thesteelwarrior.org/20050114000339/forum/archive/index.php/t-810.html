Let me dispell a myth for you, it might help you make a decision. You secondary hand swings completely independent of your primary hand. The only thing that determines when your off hand swings is your dual wield skill. You can equip a lightstone in primary and your secondary will still swing at its normal rate.

That being said, the Shortblade will do more damage consitantly. If you fight mobs that are suseptible to fire attacks, then the FG should do more damage due to procs. Ironically, FG proc is almost worthless in the zone it drops in.
