If you have someone who will port you every once in awhile for free (if they charge it cuts into your profits), just go kill Ulthorks in EW, at 40, you will still get a little bit of xp, but they will be super easy kills (recommend an Embalmer's Skinning Knife or some other form of easy, cheap bandages). Gather 4 Ulthork Tusks at a time, get your porter to get you to Cobalt Scar, turn the tusks in and cross your fingers hoping for a blue diamond. You can get many different gems and other items. Sometimes you will get some research components worth only 2P or so, but most times you get high level gems or some kind of jewelry worth 120P a pop.

Hope that helps.

Forget trying to make straight PP from mobs. You will make a ton more money selling trade skill components in the baz. Go farm spiderling silks, spider silks, and pelts. Use the spiderling silks and pelts to make leather padding and use the spider silks to make silk swatches. On my server people pay around 25-40pp per silk swatch and 30-40pp per leather padding.

Another good source of income is farming velium. If you can easily kill the orcs in Crystal Caverns, farm the velium they drop. I would recommend keeping the stackable pieces since you can get more in your bags, but the blocks sell pretty well too. Pieces go for 25-50pp each.
