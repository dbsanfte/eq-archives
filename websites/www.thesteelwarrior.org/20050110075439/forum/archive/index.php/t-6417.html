The next time you tank a boss mob for a considerable amount of time, parse it, as that'll give you a good idea of a Warrior's Riposte/Parry/Dodge rates. The bulk of a Warrior's active defenses comes through Ripostes, followed by Parry, then Dodge, so something like a +3% Riposte modifier will probably do you much more good than a +3% Dodge modifier. In reality though, they all suck, but when stacked, like having all three modifiers present in your gear outfit, I hear they can make a small, but still sizeable difference.
