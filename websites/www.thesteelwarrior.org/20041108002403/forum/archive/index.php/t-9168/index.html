for a ranger cleave would be better than ferocity since fero increase melee swings and cleave add a chance to crit.

Lauralei
It would depend where in the game you are. If you are Time+ raiding player Ferocity would be probably be better than Cleave, since Ferocity most likely improves your melee dps more so than Cleave does. The primary form of combat in terms of dps at the Time+ level of the game for rangers is melee.

If you are a non raider the chances of you outdamaging your bow dps with a melee set up are slim, so I would say Cleave, since it should impact both your bow and melee, and give you greater returns than Ferocity.
