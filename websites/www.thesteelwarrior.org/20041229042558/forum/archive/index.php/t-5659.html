Small clarification on HOT heals and aggro....

Assuming both instances that the HOT was casted Before you aggroed the mob.

1. You are at 100% hp. HOT does nothing until you engage mob.

2. You are NOT at 100% hp. HOT is healing you when you engage mob.

In case 1, the mob simply does not go for the healer, cos they are not healing anything. Set the healer in between the mob and you, so if the healer was on the aggro-list, the mob should go for the healer first. In this case, it doesn't.

In case 2, because the HOT actually did heal you when the mob was aggroed, the healer is now on the aggro-list. Similarly, place the healer in between the mob and yourself. Unless you pulled using a high aggro method, the healer will get whacked by the mob before it reaches you.

I've been duo'ing with my wife's cleric for like forever, and have found this to be always true for us.
