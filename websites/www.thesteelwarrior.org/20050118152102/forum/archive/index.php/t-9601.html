Well, although the droga pair would be a good bet eventually, I'd suggest another course for now: get the Great Staff of Thunder for a couple of hundred plat and use it to tank.
Start saving your plat now. You mention 5K, but you really need 20-30K (on my server) for 31-36% haste. Or, start whining to your guildmates about how it's time to go to Veksar or Sirens Grotto to get your own. Looting your own haste 31 or 34 or 36 percent haste saves you a LOT of farming at 55.
if you get haste in your belt slot, you can upgrade the AC in your hands. you've got a weak bracer, and could use better ear pieces, boots...uh, well, your BP is good. Do the bazaar search thing, and search by AC.
You might be worried about your low 2hb skill and using the GSoT, but it will only hurt your dps, not your aggro. You have mighty strike, so if you want to practice your skills, head to the ice giants in Everfrost, get the back camp, kill giant, bind wound, rinse and repeat. the giants will be light blue and darkblue; you'll weapons skill-ups, fair loot for 55, and there's a zoneline right nearby (you shouldn't need it, but it's there).
Try to get a feel for what your weakest pieces are and upgrade with an eye toward best bang for the buck.
