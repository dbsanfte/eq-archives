The alternative to North LDoN armor set (all 1492 points) has been the Everfrost LDoN armor set (all 760 points)...you will give up some hp/ac and minor stats for reducing the # of wins required to get armor. It really depends on how fast you earn points, how likely you'll be to upgrade beyond either of these sets and what interests you at the time.

Currently, however, there is another 1492 option that the development team leaked out too soon, it does not require points in any particular theme that I am aware of...and is very comparable (possibly, slightly better melee stats) than the North Ro armor set. The warning with this, is, the devs may nerf the stats (or improve them, even).

In the (near?) future, there has been talk about a better armor set than the current 1492...and questions as to whether LDoN bought armor will be redeemable for partial point refund. This is all speculation of course, until any of it makes it to the live server. Given that a level 65 gets 51 points per normal ldon win that takes at least 30 min for a really good group (not counting time to get adventure, time to form group, time to buff before going into dungeon) to a more normal average of 60+ minutes, it will take you some time to save up enough points for either armor. A hard LDoN is 105? points for a win, but is really more like nightmare versus normal than 'hard', from what I hear, lol...um, and is much more likely to take close to the full 90 minutes for a win or end up with a partial.

If you want armor semi-quick and have most of the faction necessary, consider farming armor drops in Kael / HoT / NToV. There is no racial requirement, but it is no drop and requires faction with Thurg / CoV / Kael respectively...and scales from easy to kill, but rare drops to hard to kill, but with a decent drop rate (of course, not all the drops will be plate, etc.).

That being said, either of the 760 or 1492 armor, will blow most of the armor away (excluding doze quests). Decisions, decisions, decisions!
