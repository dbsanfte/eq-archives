Stick with VDD and spend your dkp points on elemental BP (effect Echo of Anger = 15% aggro modifier). VDD is a good tanking weapon in PoP and you can augment it with Yekisha or Rujak Venom so it will still be a useful dps weapon.

Pick up DBoW, EoE, or JoS when you can. Seems like every time we killed RZ DBoW dropped, so every guild warrior, twink, and recruit got one, but rot still got more. As a tank I've always placed more importance on survivability (gear) than on weapons.

I do most of the main tanking for my guild and have never had a problem holding aggro using VDD (augmented rujak venom). Recently I changed my setup to DBoW and JOS (both augmented anger 3). The new setup generates more aggro, but as I said, I never had problems establishing or losing aggro using VDD. I still switch back to VDD when we're doing Time because of the ripostes on dual wield.

Good luck to you in your progression through Time and GoD.
