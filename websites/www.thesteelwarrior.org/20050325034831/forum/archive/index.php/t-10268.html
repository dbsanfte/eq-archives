I think a lot of people confuse the question of questing vs grinding. Unless you spend your entire time traveling from continent to continent there is no such thing as leveling by just questing (at least not on the horde side). At some point the XP lost from the time it takes to travel to zone X to get quest Y done becomes greater than if you just grind in one place instead.

It's really just common sense. It doesn't make sense for you to run all the way to The Hinterlands at level 38 to do the one quest you can do there (Grim Message), and then run all the way back to STV.

Generally if you've at least say 3 quests in one area that doesn't require you to jump through too many hoops (and doable, of course), I'd get them out of the way first.
