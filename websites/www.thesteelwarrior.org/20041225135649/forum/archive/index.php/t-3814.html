When PoP came out my warrior was 60 with 20AA and my chanter was 52. Everyday I would log on my warrior and stick LFG on and sit in PoV for about 45mins to an hour. Occasionally I got a group, but usually I'd get bored of waiting and log my chanter on. I never had to wait more than 10mins for a group and I often had invites before I even had my LFG tag up.

The enchanter hit 65 2 days after the warrior.

The difference...
Warrior's compete with 2 other classes for the tanking spot. They don't stack particularly well in PoP groups, they are often 3rd choice for that tanking spot, and theres too damn many of them.

Enchanters compete with 3 other classes for the slower/CC spot. They get chosen for groups for 4 reasons: haste, slow, mana regen and CC. They are the best at haste and CC and 2nd best for slow. There are also plenty of who would rather get a chanter in their group than buy C3. They stack well with the other classes that fill the slower spot. Enchanter + Beastlord, Enchanter + Shaman, Enchanter + bard are all good combos to have in a group.
