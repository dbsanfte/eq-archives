I can give you one possibility about that re-targeting problem.

At some point, one of the patches remapped my hotkeys and added F8 to my attack. So, when I press attack, I automatically re-target whatever is nearest to me, which is not necessarily what I had targeted before. Check your keyboard mapping, this might solve your problem.

As an aside, 95% of the time, it's very useful to have attack and F8 mapped together. The other 5%, it's a royal PITA.
