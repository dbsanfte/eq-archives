What shall we do with those wizards who decide to stand 200 feet away from a non-summoning mob in PoFire/HoH/Tactics/Whereever, and press the nuke button like a rogue presses backstab, causing the mob to run over to him/her while all the melees stand around thinking "where did the mob go?"?

Well my solution is simple, yet beautiful.

You'll need some basic reflexes/reaction skillz for this, but trust me, it's worth it.

As soon as the mob disappears from view and those red "You cannot see your target" (wizards tend to stand way behind the front of the raid) or "Your target is too far away, get closer" messages begins to spam your screen, quickly /assist the target you were on and check if a Wizard appears in your target box.

Now usually, your immediate reaction should be to run over to the mob and start taunting and /shield-ing the aggro-er... if the aggro-er was any other class but a Wizard.

So, memorize the names of the Wizards on your raid, and from then on when they appear in your target box after /assisting the trash mob, simply move onto the next mezzed/offtanked mob and call assist after the other one gives you raid exp. Let the Wizard tank.

You might get badmouthed in /gu /rs /tells /shout from these Wizards after a few of these "short-cuts", but hey... it's not like they heal or buff you now is it. Who cares! ;)
