Don't diss the Lodi shield. It rocks. She (?) is almost capped out on stamina, so it'd be a 11ac for 50hp tradeoff for the Cloak of Greater Pernicity, which is a pretty even trade and imo slightly in favor of the Lodi. Even a Hiero cloak or an iksar hide cape is a 13 ac for 75hp trade, which again is almost even. If you want to keep a high ac cloak, look at a Seahorse hide cloak. 3ac for 25hp for that trade. Another option is a Crinose Cape of the Werewolf, which would be 11ac for 80hp, definitely in favor of trading out the Lodi. It should also be fairly cheap due to it dropping very frequently in LDoN.

Note: I use a 1ac = 6hp formula.

Other than that, look for a new mask. It might be hard to find a better one, I know, but at least keep your eye out. Steel Rings of Earthen Resilience would upgrade your finger slot alot, but they also tend to be very expensive. Yer gear is probably fine, just keep one eye on upcoming raid drops. http://www.darkwind-guild.com/SoL/ has a very good listing of raid drops from Velious on.
