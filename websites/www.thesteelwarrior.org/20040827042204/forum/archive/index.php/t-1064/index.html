gerili
Solo classes:

Necromancer: Very capable of soloing, and to the wise a very good addition to any group needing dps.

Enchanters: Enchanters have been able to solo with the best of them at high levels, tend to struggle a bit at lower levels, especially with no snare. A very welcomed class to any group.

Shaman: Very capable soloers and groupers. Able to solo a lot of mobs others have not. Very welcomed in groups.

Beastlord: Able to solo from 1-65. Some very good abilities at high levels, although dont see a lot grouping very often.

Wizard: Good soloers till round 60 or so, and can go to 65 with the right gear. Welcomed in raids, and some groups for their dps.

Mage: Good soloers till round 60ish. Life gets harder for them, can still be done just not nearly as easily. Welcomed in a lot of groups for dps.

Druids: Able to solo from 1-65, and very well. Probably the most versitile soloer IMO, nearly every option available, but then again im probably biased :) I play a druid. Have some desireablilty in groups but every idiot has a druid and has given them a bad name.

Of course couple other classes can solo but not as effeciently, especially at higher levels.
