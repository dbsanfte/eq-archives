Sturdiness is 100 hp per level, the cost scales by 4 each rank, so 4, 8, 12, 16, 20 == 60 aa for 500 hp.

ND is 2, 4, 6 AA and appears to be greatest bang for your buck. Plus it gets your archtype title out of the way.

What I did was play with magelo, giving myself each AA, and calculating out how many hp/aa I got. Provided magelo's calcs are reasonable (which they seem to be) I came to the conclusion that the best HP order was:

ND3 Sturdiness1 PE PD3 Sturdiness2-4. YMMV.

Mitigation AA's help a bunch, but you're right people only ask about hp/ac so they're not much good for e-penii comparisons / getting groups.
