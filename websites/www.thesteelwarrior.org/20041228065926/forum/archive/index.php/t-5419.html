Lets say LR5 = 1% more hits avoided
Lets say ID5 = 1% shaved off each hit
Overall they will have the same benefit.

Now take 5 hits that will get you to low hp agro.

Reduce them by 1% ... Probably low remain hp agro

Have a 1% chance to avoid each hit ....
With luck you may avoid one of the hits

That one will be the one that will keep you alive.

Both are necessary, for a non MT LR is better (augments streaks)
For a MT ID is better (less streaky)
