Hey man.

I was the raid leader for a guild that was moving around at this level for a while.

If you can really get yourself to 65 and the AA's you are talking about...

Then I would suggest trying to do BoT Towers a bunch if you have all 4 clerics.

You are light on hps atm, but you can get ornate BPs for every class from the towers.

Vindi will give you his BP, but if you are farming Eindride, you probaly dont need Vindi.

There are FT2 hats on Brynju for your priest classes, and he drops the wicked shank for the rogue. He also drops the Chain BPs.

Auliffe will drop GSo4W for BST for better DPS and Leather BPs. He can drop the ice wand for your casters.

Eindride as mentioned before has great hybrid shoulders, Plate BPs for you and your clerics, and a Great Blade of Storms for your main hand.

Start with Eindride. He is the easiest. Your DPS will be lacking at first, but with 4 clerics you will live. Get your BP and GBOS.

When you get him down, move to Auliffe. A bard will help a lot in these encounters, and you can always invite one and allow him to roll. You need him to avoid the stun.

Finally, take a stab at Brynju. He is great reward, but you need higher DPS to keep the area clear. A COD pet will help a lot for that fight.

Leave Whats his Stupid Earth Tower guy alone for a while. Your casters wont like it, but its more pain than it is worth at first.

Clearing these guys out every 8 hours or so will yield a great amount of loot for your crew, plus you can get ornate pieces from Minis along the way.

I dont think you have the DPS for Xanamech. I dont think Kael holds anything that great for you that you cant get more easily from BoT towers. NTOV can gain you all some upgrades, but it is longish respawn and without a decent amount of DPS, it can take a long time to clear through it.
