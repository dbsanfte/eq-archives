08-18-2004, 04:13 PM
Most guilds have given up on CH chains. Without chains, the limit of healing that can be poured out on an individual tank has skyrocketed, making it much simpler and easier for a single cleric to recast DI without slack forming. With an increase in healing power being funneled to the tank, the damage from ripostes is less of an issue, especially with increasing hp making DI more and more reliable. Max hits in Defensive are increasing in proportion to maximum hit points of warriors, but the window grows narrower without anything to alter the statistical probabilities as AC continues to reduce the frequency of max hits and spikes become more a result of a string of successful hits rather than a string of max hits. All of those combine essentially to create a situation of reliable healing power. Mana becomes an issue, after a while, but with healing so strong, tanks can sacrifice the loss of ripostes from a 2hander for the dramatically increased aggro output of dual-wielding, also increasing the DPS of the raid as a whole, resulting in shorter fights, which was the aim of GoD in the first place.

And I expect a warrior dual-wielding a solid DPS combo will still do more damage than the Halberd, or at least comparable. Need parses to be certain, though.
