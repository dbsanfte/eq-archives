Haste is always going to be tradeoff.

No matter what slot you dedicate to haste, there will always be some item that's got more hit points or higher AC that could go in that slot.

Cord of Midnight? There's quite a few belts out there with more hit points than it.

Shadow Footpads, you've already mentioned boots of storm.

Envenomed Moccasins, Grey Suede Boots ... same deal.

Earring of Endless Speed? Have you seen the other stats on that? Haste is all it's got going for it.

Shiny Trinket of Speed? That's a swapping nightmare when using a bow. And there's still some better range items than it for raw stats.

Gauntlets of Dragon Slaying?

Breastplate of Speed?

See, it's always going to be a tradeoff.

You could just quest the Silver Bracelet of Speed from Doze. That's a pretty damn good bracer no matter what.

The tradeoff there, though, is the time it takes to quest it.

Good luck in whatever item you choose.
