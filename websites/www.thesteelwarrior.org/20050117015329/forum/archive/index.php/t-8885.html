Modified UI bag is the best way to switch weapons. Keep the bag open at all times and just drag the weapon up to the open inv slot in the bag.

I switch out moderately frequently. My typical aggro setup is SoD/CHoS. Sometimes I'll just stick with GSoT, just depending on how much aggro I really need depending on the group make up. I'll swap in an anklesmasher if I need to snare. I'll swap in a ToD if I need to slow. I'll swap in a GSot4W if I want DPS. I'll swap out the SoD with a SotB if I want to stun a higher level mob since SoD only works to 55 (or GSoT since it works to 65 also). So, yeah, there are definitely occasions to swap out weapons, but they aren't that common. I could use a GSoT pretty much all the time as long as I had a slower and snarer in group or the mobs didn't run as many don't in a lot of the zones I fight.

As for piercing. I never spent any time on it. Only time I found I needed it was when I went to do loyalist shield quest, and if you don't buy the ashes you're going to need some piercing skill. But heh, I can't see any real compelling reason. Just make sure to keep *all* of your other weapon skills maxxed, or close enough so if you get a great weapon of any type you will be able to effectively use it right off the bat. I use 1HS, 1HB, and 2HB all the time. I don't really use 2HS much, but someday I might get my hands on a BoW, and then I'll need the skill.

In my hotkey box of the default UI, I put my primary and secondary slots on the bottom two keys (Do this by pressing and holding the cursor over your inventory slots till it sticks to the cursor. I hope that made sense)

In a key above those two, I have a inventory slot also pasted there. (Ususaly for Earthshaker)

When doing LDoN and its a huge bad pull, Ill swap the secondary with Earthshaker, then swap earthshaker for primary, Rampage, Furious, then switch back in reverse order.

If Im messing around with and aggro happy XP group I will substitute the Earthshaker for my GSot4W with life tap.
Build aggro till I see about 4 rage procs, then swap into the GSot4W and contribute to group DPS.

If Im main slower (Dont ask) Ill put Truncheon of doom in the spot.

If Im tanking a raid level mob where I spend most of the time soloing it (Saryn comes to mind), Ill put my Stormriders Carapice there.

Im sure there are other situations that warrent quick convenient weapon switching, but to be honest, its normally just my Earthshaker sitting there waiting for an "Oh shit!" situation so I can get AE Aggro and save AE taunt for later. (With a bad pull, AE taunt is a temporary solution. If not mezzed or offtanked, someone will peel and add from you and things start to get really hairy. AE nuking first and saving AE Taunt for later in the fight can ususaly save the group)
