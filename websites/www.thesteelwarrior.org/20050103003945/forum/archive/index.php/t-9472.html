The dodge mod fits in type3 slots, so an AC or HP augment isnt really an option (well AC is, but that's better put into type1...I guess I could see 2 10hp augs there though...)

To answer the original question, 8% dodge mod will stack with your shoulders. One increases your skill, the other increases your CHANCE to dodge, which are two different things.

As for the dodge mod, at a time 5% dodge wasn't worthwhile but 8% was because your skill crossed a threshold (I think 8% took you over 200 dodge but 5% didn't but I could be wrong...) However, having a dodge mod increases your dodge rate a small but parsable amount. I think I'm going to agree with the above poster and say that I'd rather get a 10ac aug there if the slot allows it.
