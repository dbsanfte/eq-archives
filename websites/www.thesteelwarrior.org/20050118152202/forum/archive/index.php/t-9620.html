Dang, Sakryn mentioned Kensington trackballs before me !

Anyways, I used to use a Kensington Trackball for some beginner CAD work, and it was great. The ball is rather on the large side, so it carries some weight when you spin it, making it rather non-twitch game friendly ... but lotta precision.

It was the type of trackball where it's in the center of 4 main buttons, like this one :

So if you are going to do anything that takes precision, then I can recommend that type of trackballs.



Note : Kensington Trackballs are a bit on the expensive side, since they are usually aimed at professional work and such. But they are good quality and well worth the price, in my opinion.
