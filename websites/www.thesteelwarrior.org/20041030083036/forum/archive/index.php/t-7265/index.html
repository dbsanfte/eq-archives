Lilwee Beasty
If your at the MM/Bert point progression wise as a guild, you should be Sol Ro flagged relatively soonish. I'd concentrate on AA exp until you have Sol Ro access, then do Sol Ro for not only great exp (till you get EP access) but ornate, which will be great gear upgrades for you.

Once you have a solid core of AA's (70-80, and most of your defensive-HP ones) and have all the ornate you need (minus a BP) start doing hard LDoN's. The exp is not bad, and the random drops are usually pretty nice upgrades at that point in your progression.
