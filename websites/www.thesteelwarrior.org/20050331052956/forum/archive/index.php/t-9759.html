Went back to WC last night with a 17 war, 16 hunter, and me (17 shaman) and found the zone in to the instance. Its one of the few spots I've found that you actually have to "zone" into like we're used to in EQ.

First thing we see when we zone in is an NPC that tells us that we need to help kill the reptiles in the cavern so some guy can be free to do his thang. I didnt notice at first, but when you talk to the NPC you get a nice lil druid buff that up's your stats =) Very cool.

First few mobs we're pretty much the same as the outside mobs, lvl 17ish elites that were no problem for us. Then we ran into a lvl 21 druid 2 rooms into the instance that whooped our ass. It looked namedish, but the hunter said he saw a few more deeper in so its apparently tough trash mob. We never got past this first meanie. We tried about 8 times experimenting with different approaches and ended up just not having the healer mana to keep the tank up through the whole fight. So after about an hour or so of banging our heads on it we decided we'd need more peeps to hang there and opted to leave (it was getting late).

While there we learned that after about five or so Shaman rezzes they start making you wait 30 sec, then 2 min to accept the rezz. I didnt rezz the tank anymore after it told her wait 2 min so I dont know if the wait gets any longer. I was surprised at the message at first, but it kinda makes sence. By the time that message started showing up the tanks durability was pretty much shot anyways. Interesting penalty for repeatedly rezzing.

When you get boared with questing and killing normal mobs, go check out one of these instanced dungons. Apparently they are tuned to the level of the group so at higher levels they will be equally challenging. Im just jazzed that my lowbie hooves gets a chance to fight against mobs this hard. These truely are some kick ass dungons!!
