How did you get multiple attempts at Inny? He's been short-term blowable for us the times we failed.

If you are are max melee range, you can do damage to an AE rampager while being outside his rampage range, by about 2 feet. There isn't much margin for error, and your melee need to backup and get "do not see target" messages to ensure they're at max range. That said, Inny's AE rampage is the weakest I've ever heard of. He quads in the 700's and his attack is pretty low (so not all max hits).

I'd recommend you range attack inny as you are able (with rangers/casters) and use your other melee for other things, at least for certain parts of the fight. (Casters have to be inside melee range to land spells by the way, can you hear the devs laughing?) Once the CC is locked down and if you have some extra healers, certainly monks and rogues can probably engage in melee. The trouble is inny has some knocking around as I recall, and you don't want to wall/corner him so your rangers can take him down. So, it's hard to keep inny positioned well and still, which means melee will be taking some damage. But again, it's not that bad as long as you don't zerg every melee at him at the beginning. Certainly, not before he's slowed and crippled. . .

One time inny's AE rampage kindof bugged out on us. It seemed to be affecting some people, but not others who were able to just stand right beside him. I still have no idea how or why this happened.

Just treat AE rampage like regular rampage but hitting multiple targets at once.

Mobs with AE rampage must be avoided by meleeing at max range, because if the mob does any reasonable amount of melee damage there's just no way you can keep people up against AE rampage for any extended amount of time. The only exception is Saryrn and it is obvious that you're supposed to take the AE rampage from her. Mobs do not necessarily have to have a melee range > rampage range, but on all mobs with nontrivial AE rampage, you can usually melee outside the AE rampage range.

Actually there are various degrees of AE rampage. Fennin Ro for example only hits about 6-8 targets when he does an AE rampage. Of course most AE rampagers just flat out hit everyone. You can usually get an idea of how many target it hits by the number of swarm pets that died.
