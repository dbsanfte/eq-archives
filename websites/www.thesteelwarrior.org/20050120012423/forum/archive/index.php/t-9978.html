Short answer = yes it affects both.

Pretend for a moment that you get 100 mainhand swings in one minute (really fast weapon). With 20% haste you now get 120 (multiply by 1.2). With 50% haste you get 150. With max haste (no bardsong) you get 200 swings a minute. With 100% haste + bard overhaste you'll get 215-220 swings in one minute.

Granted you will have more mainhand swings than offhand, but yeah haste affects both of them.

You could probably afford an FBSS (21% haste) pretty easily at your level. There are others but they get more and more expensive. Best buyable haste is 36%. Most people will settle for 31-32% buyable belts. Oldschool nodrop haste caps at 41% (gotta kill bosses for em). Some of the newer haste items in cutting edge content has 45% or more worn haste.
