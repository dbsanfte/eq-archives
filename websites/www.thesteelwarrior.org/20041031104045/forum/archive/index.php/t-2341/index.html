Sangiovese
After reading all the threads about this previously, it seems to me that the consensus is:

Just putting the shield in your offhand is not enough aggro to offset losing the offhand weapon.

BUT....

Swapping between the shield and your offhand weapon (i.e. "the Ruatha Swap" or "Ghetto Bashing") is a decent improvement in aggro.


For any not familiar with it:
The basic idea of the Ruatha Swap is to equip your shield when bash becomes available, do the bash, and then quickly put your offhand weapon back in and swing it until bash is available again... and repeat over and over. This isn't as hard as it sounds, especially with some of the UI mods available.

Hope that helps some.
