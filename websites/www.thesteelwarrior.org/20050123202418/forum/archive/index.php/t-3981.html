Before you farm LDoN to get anger procs, consider this:

At level 50 you will not get very many points per adventure. It would be more efficient to level closer to 60 and then do adventures if you need those proc augs. Just to put it in perspective, I get 51 points for a regular adventure at 65 and on my 41 ranger I get like 3 or 4 points. Anger 2 is a 400 hate aug and is like 510 LDoN points at Everfrost. Anger 3 is 1150 points. If you are around level 50, I think you probably would get like 15 to 20 points per adventure if that and it would take two to three times as many sucessful adventures to buy the same item that a level 65 would need. The difference in time spent would be much better spent on leveling and would bring far more benefit to your character. It is much more time efficient to level first and then get those augs.

Same for the equipment, at 65 you will spend far less time and get much better equipment per time spent on adventures than you would at 50. A level 50 would probably level up to close to 65 before he got enough points to buy some of the nice Tak armor anyway, just from the experience that the adventures would give him but he could level 2 or 3 times faster in PoP and still get the armor faster in the long run by waiting until level 65 to do the adventures.
