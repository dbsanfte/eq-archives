SUMMARY
Darkblade absolutely wipes the floor with it in terms of agro. On blue con, low resistance mobs the Fleshbiter will result in 10-15% less incoming damage due to the stuns.

I'd go with the Darkblade every single time if you have the choice. If you don't have an option to get a DB, the FB is a reasonable substitute, but there's a clear difference in terms of agro, and the stats are too close to diffferentiate between.
