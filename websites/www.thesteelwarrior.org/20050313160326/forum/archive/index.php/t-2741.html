We enter phase 4, set up 3 groups (assuming numbers available)

War, war, war, war, dru, brd
Clr, clr, clr, clr, clr, clr, brd
Clr, clr, clr, clr, clr, clr, brd

War = top 4 MTing warriors
Clr = rotation clerics (solo or duoed)

Rest of the raid groups up as best they can with what's left

This is optimal for TZ/VT/RZ/Bert/Inny, and the rest don't need such specific groups. Non-tanking wars in the first group just need to be careful not to take damage faster than the druid can keep up with. if they do and are not next in line to tank, they back out of AE.
