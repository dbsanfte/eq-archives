I found upgrading my BoW was one of the best things I'd done. I'd suggest running through Miraguls (Marble Sphere of Ire, 1150 points) or Guk missions (Slimestone Fragment of Dread,
760 points), choose one and augment that BoW. More aggro > less aggro, always.

While you're working on that, I'd suggest Mistmoore Catacombs until you have enough for the 10ac augment (Brilliant Blood Ruby of Prowess, 510 points) for your shoulders and cloak. Next in line is definitely Ruj missions for 40hp augments (Solid Coal of Prowess, 1150 points) for, well, everything else.

Here's a good searchable database for LDoN augments: http://www.squidbox.com/ldon
