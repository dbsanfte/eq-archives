If you hit the "View New Posts" link (which is on the left side, under "Welcome Back, User_name!") it will display all threads with new posts since you last clicked "Mark All Forums Read".

Now that the main page is different, you can't click the TSW logo to get back to the master board with all of the forum links, instead you have to click on the first "The Steel Warrior" (in this thread it looks like "The Steel Warrior > The Steel Warrior > The Outhouse > Message board oddity"). Once you're back at the master board you can click "Mark All Forums Read" on the right side (across from "View New Posts") and all the new post indicators will turn off. When you come back everything with new posts will be lit up and "View New Posts" will take you straight to them.
