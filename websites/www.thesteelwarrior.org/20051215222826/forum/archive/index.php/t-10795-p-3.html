Just something I was thinking about when considering a respec to arms

Crit increase by 1% from weapon spec and cruelty is 1% average increase in dps without other talents to boost crit damage.

Lets say a warrior has these dps talents that stack so nicely
Cruelty 5/5
Deep Wounds 3/3
Impale 2/2
Two-Handed Weapon Specialization 5/5
Axe Specialization 5/5
for a total of 20 talent points.

first add the average dps increase from 2h spec
dps == 1.05 of normal dps

multiply that by average dps increase from crit % boosters taking into account impale's boost of %20 to crit damage
1.05*(1+0.1*0.2) == 1.071

then assume that the warrior has a fairly nice crit rate of 25% and add the dps boost from implale that comes from the 15% crits that dont come from crit % boosting talents remembering to take into account the 5% dps boost of 2h spec
1.05*(1+0.15*0.2) == 1.0815

now assume that deep wounds 3/3 will do between 20% and 60% of its weapon damage each time it lands (25% of all hits)
1.05*(1+0.25*0.2) == 1.1025 (on the lower end)
1.05*(1+0.25*0.6) == 1.2075 (on the upper end)

so from 20 tallent points we get
.05+.071+.0815+.1025 == .305 (on the lower end)
.05+.071+.0815+.2075 == .41 (on the upper end)

thats from 1.525% to 2.05% dps increase per point (not to bad at all)

and thats just the increase from 20 points

on the other hand a prot speced warrior can get 1h spec for 2% dps boost per point. this seems compareable to the 1.525% to 2.05% dps boost per point seeing as 1 handers offer a little less dps to be boosted than 2 handers do.

any corrections or thoughts?
