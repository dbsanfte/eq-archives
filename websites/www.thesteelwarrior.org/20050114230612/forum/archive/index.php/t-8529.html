Here are some alternatives/upgrades to what you currently have on... nothing should be too pricey either.

Wrist: Replace the junk bracelet of quickness with something like Silver Chitin bracer, just anything with ac and hp.
Cloak: Pick up a crinoose cape of the werewolf for cheap. 12ac 80hp, but the rec level won't give you full stats until 50+
Rings: velium fire wedding rings are better for tanks than the bands you have, but look into a valorium ring of gallantry or two once you level up towards 50.
Shoulders: Tattered mantle, 12ac 20hp, is an upgrade for under 500pp, and should last you until you can get some decent nodrop ones, or raid some. Or if you can find the Chokidai Hide ones, they are like 5ac 15sta 55hp or something. Strength of the Elements is a quest you should do eventually.
Face: Grimling Contraption... AC, hp, and regen1, with no rec level!
Neck: Amulet of mummy wrappings or whatever it is... another cheap LDoN drop, with great stats but a rec level
Ears: Worker's Earring of Efficiency is a great great twink ear, even if you have better haste. 5ac 55hp is hard to beat, and it should be less than 4k

Total cost of these items is like 12-15k, which you could make most of by selling off the items you are replacing
