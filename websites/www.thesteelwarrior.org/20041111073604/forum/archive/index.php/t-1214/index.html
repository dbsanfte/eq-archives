HoH is a great zone imho. Mobs dont hit hard, bunches of mobs, lots of camps, fast re spawns. I havent found any mobs that see thru invis and most can be single pulled. It gets boring quickly though cause its so easy, but hey, they almost always drop Hero Parts :P

I perfer to walk the zone just clearing anything in my path.
