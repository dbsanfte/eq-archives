I am unfamiliar with any type of objective method of measuring hate. Subjectively to me stuns seem to be marginally better aggro than the debuff on the BBC. I mainhanded a SoD for awhile and switched to a BBC and my aggro seemed slightly worse, but the difference was minor. I sold my SoD to buy a BoC and then offhanded the BBC and my aggro is great now.

If I had to do it again I'd still buy the BBC. The reason not being that it is better aggro than a MoM, I'd expect the stun on the MoM to be slightly better aggro. But the difference is so minor that to me I'd rather just go with which weapon I like the looks of best. If you like the mace look, go for the MoM. If you like the big ass sword look get the SoMV. If you like the hatchet look of the BBC get it. I really really like the combo look of the BoC with the BBC offhand, just looks sweet.

If you're all about performance I'd go with the MoM. Keep in mind that the stun still won't work on mobs past level 55.
