Omnus
First get the sod/boc question out of the way. Almost everyone I have talked to who used a sod feels it has about the same agro as a boc proc. So there really isn;t a reason not to mainhand the sod for more damage and offhand boc if both have anger 3.

Most people prefer chos vs sotbs. I personally ahve found the stun proc from sotbs very useful and is still carry one around to help with casters everyonce inawhile.

Considering your sotbs has anger 2. Mainhanding it for ahwile is still a good idea. You will get more procs with the auged sotbs, than the boc. On average, mainhanded, at 305 dex you would get 2 boc procs for 700 agro and with the sotbs you will get 4 total procs. 2 for 400 agro each and 2 for about 400-500 agro each (the stun). Not to mention the sotbs will be less streaky since you have mutiple procs. Any proc from your weapons should take agro from a slow.

It should go without saying and I am sur you already plan on it, but save and put anger3 on the boc. For an all round improvement on your dps and agro, you could grab your chos and put a 510 ykesha proc from guk on it. But the change to your offhand might not be worth it, depending on how much ldons you want to do. It also seems that you could benefit alot from ldon gear so my suggestion would be save and anger3 the boc, then mainhand it. Keep your sotbs the way it is and then save for a nro BP and 40 hp augs etc.
