Demorgoth
You can currently state 3 procs on a single weapon:

1: Weapon's natural proc - eg: Enraging Blow
Proc rate hardcoded to the weapon

2: Additional buff proc - eg: Call of Fire
Proc rate hardcoded to the spell

3: LDoN Augment proc - eg: Force of Ykesha
Proc rate hardcoded to the augment

Testing has shown these procs work simultaneously and independantly. Although noone has provided evidence of multiple procs off the same single swing, there have been parses showing that all additional procs have the same proc-per-minute rate irrespective of weapon type or delay. If it procs ~100 times in an hour on a wieghted axe, it'll proc ~100 times per hour on a bloodfrenzy or a FS short sword too.

Actually, that's not quite true. According to Sony if your weapon has a natural proc and an augment, the augment can only go off if the natural proc doesn't. It checks for the natural proc first, then the augment.
