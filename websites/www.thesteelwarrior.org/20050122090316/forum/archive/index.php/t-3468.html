First, you've got 2 haste items. Ditch the SCHW, as its not doing you much good (the belt is 31% haste, so your SCHW haste isn't being used).

Next, your mask needs immediate help. Crystal Spider Eyes should be dirt cheap, and be a big upgrade over what you have.

I'd also look at upgrading your neck item. Clattering Bone Necklace is a good item. A Ribbon Choker would also be an upgrade, and would probably be pretty inexpensive.

Since you've ditched your SCHW, you'll need new gloves. Cobalt is a cheap AC upgrade, though going with something with AC and HP is preferred. Might look into picking up a Champion's MQ, or attending some kind of Kael Arena raid to get some corroded gauntlets for the quest.

For your helm, take a look at a Helm of Raldukan or Dented Kunzar War Helm. Either one would be a nice boost in AC, and add hp's and other stats.
paladiin
10-08-2003, 02:49 PM
One more thing -

As mentioned, Fierce Heraldric is going to be the biggest upgrades for you for many of your slots, for quite some time. Your main focus should be collecting FH stuff. The suggestions I made are only fillers until you can do so. So budget accordingly - don't take a Champion's Gauntlets MQ for 3k if you can get a FH Gauntlets for 4k or 5k. In the long run, you'll want FH anyway, so smart shopping may save you plat down the road.
