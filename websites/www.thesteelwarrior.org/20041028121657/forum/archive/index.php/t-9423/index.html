We've had 6 DBotW in about 9 months of time, 1 fleshbiter in.. I think 5 barxts. If you're desperate, go for the fb, but DB will eventually drop, you're doing time every week, but you're going to do barxt a few times for flags, then a few times for backflags, and that's it. It's entirely possible you'll never see a FB drop, because Uqua is not something you'll ever be farming for loot. If you think you'll stop doing time before you get a DBotW, grab an FB if you see one, otherwise, I'd hold out, you don't want to get into a position where you spend points on a FB, then have to turn around and spend points on a DB a few weeks later.
