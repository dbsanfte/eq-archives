wandor
I have owned just about every weapon de'jour for the last 2 years and I can say with all honesty that the Wavecrasher disappointed me the most. While a "did not take hold" proc DOES generate some agro, it's nothing near the agro of a resisted proc or landing a slow. If you have a slower in the group the proc is basically negated - it's just short of worthless. Couple that with the 9/19 ratio and you are not even making any DPS to generate agro.

The only reason I would ever recommend this weapon to anyone is if you don't have access to a ToD and you hunt without a slower frequently. This weapon amounts to a Lammy with a situationally useful proc at about 10x the price.

If you need agro weapons and you are on a budget, sell this while it still fetches 20k+ and get one of any number of stun procing weapons, you will not be disappointed.
