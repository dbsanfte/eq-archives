Some cheap upgrade ideas...

Face - something with HPs or Stamina. Deathmask of the Elysians is ok, Crystal spider eyes if you are keeping your budget down.

Helm - Champions MQ if you can get it, otherwise a Raldukan Helm is good but include 1 armour dye into your budget unless you want to have a pink hat.

Gloves - Skyshrine MQ would last you along time if you can get it, otherwise Thurg MQ or even Indicolate gauntlets are nice. Kylong darkmail gauntlets or nightmare terror plate gauntlets are good too.

Boots - Barbed dragonscale boots or either a thurg or SS MQ depending on what your budget is.

Wrist - swap the bracer of might for either a silver chitin wristband or kylong darkmail bracelet. If you have a few friends check out the Bracer of Benevolence quest aswell (walkthrough is somewhere in the keep)

Earrings - swap the cougar claw earring for the 2ac 35hp/35mana black sapphire earring.

Neck - Clattering bone necklace, Dragon tooth choker, Mithril Spiker Collar, Tracking Wolf Collar

Back - Cloak of the Maelstrom, Iksar hide cape or Heirophants Cloak
