Found the link -
Korby's guide had a link:

To camp the Spiroc Lord: The Guardian must be alive to farm the Spiroc Lord. In order for the Guardian to respawn, at least one follow Spiroc must remain alive. If the Guardian is not up but there is at least one follower left alive, all must camp - someone has agro. Once agro is cleared, the Guardian will respawn. If possible, the follower Spiroc(s) can then be killed as long as the Guardian is NOT killed. Any mobs that come to assist the Lord must be trained away and dealt with - as soon as the Lord dies, Sirran spawns and he WILL assist the remaining Spirocs. The Lord should NOT be fought in his spawn spot as he will immediately respawn and you will have to wait for Sirran to despawn from the previous kill (only ~20 mins). There is the option of trying to train Sirran away, but it soon may become difficult to place the 3 (or so) mobs remaining so they do not assist one another. Just remember - Guardian and Lord will assist one another; Guardian must be up for Lord to respawn; Lord's death spawns Sirran, who will assist both Guardian and Lord. Sounds fun doesn't it? ^.^
