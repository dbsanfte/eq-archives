Regarding your original questions about the healers switching to the SA, as a 65 cleric main I'll give you my take on this. The biggest problem I've found as a healer is switching targets from the MA to the SA, etc...we all know how to make /target MA and /target SA keys, but the stinkin "/target" command has a range about as long as a gnome's pecker. End result is, MA dies, hit your /target SA key, and you get this lovely message that you cannot see the player. So some will try to /assist the mob to get the SA, but typically the SA doesn't have the aggro right away so you get a wizzie or ranger instead. Others will try to mouse target the SA, through the video lag and giant tigers...too late...SA dead!

The best tank-switch advice I can give is for the dedicated tank healers to pre-target the MA an SA in succession before the fight, and then use the "toggle two targets" hotkeyed to a key. Thus, they can have the MA targeted...if he goes down, immediately hit your toggle key to target the SA. The range on the toggle is the same as the range of mouse targeting someone in your group window.

Thirdly, healers can use the raid window to target the next tank, as this range is also the same as the group window. Takes up some screen real estate however, but better than a wipe.

Just my take on this...good luck!
