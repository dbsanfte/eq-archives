First off... get your defensive aa's.. ASAP. (Speaking as a cleric, I'd rather have a lower level with their defensive aa's well underway than a 65 with only run 3. I can tell the difference in the two.)

If you have good CoV (ally) faction, do the bracer of benevolence quest twice, and do the draconic pauldrons quest. Decent rewards and no plat spent. And if you have the inclination, head in to Temple of Veeshan do do Halls of Testing. Easily done with 1 or 2 groups. More quests, little to no plat spent (depending if you hunt or buy your gems).

Next stop, veksar. Camp the soulscream belt, thus freeing up your hands for decent hp gaunts (SS or ornate). Or if you don't feel like taking an exp group to veksar, keep and eye out and whack kunark dragons for an RBB or CoF, or the CS dragon for CoCW.

If I were in your position, I would get a group and go live in Veksar for a while. You can get enough upgrades to make BoT named a ton easier to tank, which is the goal you shoudl set for yourself. Luckily, you can also get pretty good exp in Veksar, the faster you get ND and some defensive AAs the better. Once you have bled Veksar dry, you should be able to move your residence to BoT and start working on getting some ornate molds.

Veksar drops:
Kylong Darkmail Gauntlets (A Highborn or A Noble)
Boots of the Kunzar Wanderer (A Plagued Slave)
Kylong Shinguards (Feral Lord Gulok)
Darkguard Chestplate (Trooper Muruk)
Soulscream Belt (A Rotting Shopkeeper)
Mithril Spiked Collar (A Kylong Crusader)
Kylong Darkmail Girdle (An Undead Chef)
Kylong Darkmail Visor (A Kylong Crusader)
Kylong Darkmail Pauldrons (A Decaying Slavemaster)

All of these are regular named with PHs, you just need to find the camps and set up shop. None are particularly hard and they drop other tradeable items that should make getting a group to go down there with you pretty easy. With a few of these upgrades and ND3 you should be able to get up around 4khp unbuffed, which should make tanking BoT named a lot easier.

Best part is ... no faction work!
