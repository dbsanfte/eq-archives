When POP first released, and this was one of the very first raid type events a guild did (after killing Grummus, which was basically the first along with Terris), this was decent RvR, IMO. The real reward was progression, your Bertox key, something to do when you weren't in Ssra or VT. Not two loots + a rune every single time like you see now. It's towers RvR that became too sweet of a deal for players at that level, not Carprin cycle that's too poor.

If you think of Carprin cycle as flagging for progression as it actually is, and BoT towers as a free handout as it actually is, maybe things won't seem so bad.
