Your gear is pretty good, and if your guild is working on getting into VT, you will reach 9k for sure, especially if you pick up some other drops along the progression (say from Cursed and Emp):

Cursed and Emperor Examples:

Bloodfrenzy would give you +40 HP over your GBoS
Torque of the Wyrmlord +55 HP over your Mummy Wrappings

VT Examples:

Umbracite Bracer +30 HP
Belt of Mastery +40 HP
Silver Steel Cloak +60 HP
Earring of Battle +30 HP
Leggings of Fiery Might +35 HP
Crimson Runed Mask +40 HP

That right there is +330 raw HP, add in ND3 and that should push you to 9k. Granted, getting all of these to drop and being awarded or spending the DKP on them can be dubious at best :D (Never saw a Silver Steel Cloak or Leggings of Fiery Might drop for us ever), but it is possible. From now on, most of your upgrades willhave to be from raid mobs, or possible Hard LDoN missions unfortunately, you have pretty much hit that cap imo.
Sulvar
Do LDoN hard missions. They drop 90-100 hp items for most slots for the random spawn named. You should also try and get those +40 hp augs from the EC camp, very nice for increasing hp totals.

From raid mobs pre-emp here are some things that you can get

Boots: Boots of the storm from tmax or the studded yttrium boots from seru

Cloak: Try to move your haste to another spot and try to get 41% haste. Doz drops parts for a 100 hp 41% haste cloak or for a 100 hp 41% haste bracer.

Arms: Arch Lich drops some nice 85 hp arms. You can also use seru's torque in the arm slot for 100 hps.

Hat: Tormax and Avatar of war both drop 100 hp hats. Lots of other mobs do also

Neck: Arch Lich drops 100 hp neck that is not too bad, seru also drops 100 hp neck that has very nice resists

Belt: Dain belt is pretty nice 100 hps and AoB
