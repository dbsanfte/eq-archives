I guess for some basic tanking stuff--

Treat everything like it reposts for 10k. NEver give the group the front of the mob.

Always give pets the back of the mob. If no pet casters, let the rogue get back. Generally I find that the rogues are smarter than the pets. The will learn to follow the pets (:-p).

If you are backing a mob into a wall, turn 90 so that you are going parallel to the wall. If you are heading for a corner, jump into the corner before the mob gets there.

Levels seem to go so fast (particularly in PC) that you don't have time to cap your skills. Pick one or 2 that you will try and keep maxed. The skill stops at 40-50 and 60-65 will let you get your laggin skills up.

Unless you are uber I can almost garentee you that you will spend quality high level time with the droga pair--Copper Hammer of Smiting (CHoS) and Sword of the Bloodsworn (SotB) so having 1hs and 1hb is a good thing.

2h will likely be a Warmasters Bladed Staff (if you have the plat) for a while.

And don't forget those early level trians for new skills!
