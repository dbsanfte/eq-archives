I just wanted to post some info on this mob. When I was reading the VP threads I only saw partial info. Sorry if this isn't needed.

Hits main target for around 1100 max. I parsed it at around 500dps. AE rampage hits for 2kish max. Casts the 2 spells listed below but the real kicker of the fight is that it has a 2 damage unresistable knockback proc on the main target. I got knocked across the zone and summoned back a few times. There was no spell cast message and with 500 to all resists i never resisted it. It would hit me 3 or 4 times in a row sometimes so it procs very commonly.

We basically tried him with 8 people and the only reason we lost is because of the unresistable knockback. His main target dps is horrid so just make sure all the melee's are max melee range. Make sure everyone has 500 to all resists. Then just make sure when the main tank gets knocked across the zone and doesnt get summoned back that you have someone to fill his spot.

His ae rampage is really unforgiving. He one rounded my fully time geared rogue friend with rampage.




Shock Breath (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=5159&source=Test)

Infinite Mass (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/spell.html?id=4125&source=Test)

Logistic items from an attempt last night with an open raid:

1. He wont ae rampage once slowed; slowing asap is a key to victory
2. His KB isnt bad if you get knocked into lava as opposed to into the land (esp with lev)
3. His AE can be resisted if you have max MR (I think its a -300 check)
4. Use pallie to tank for agro lock...warrs get knocked back too often and then take too long to reaquire agro.
5. Casters must be in melee range to hit...time the AE to cast
6. His ae has a godly range (300+?)
7. He is rooted (for what that is worth)
8. He sees through SoS (CR is a fun time heh)
9. His KB effect is in a forward arc.
