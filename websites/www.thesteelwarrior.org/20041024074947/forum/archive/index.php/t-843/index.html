I prepared this guide to help a guildy do it. Hope it helps:-

This is my guide to getting the Bracer of Benevolence
MAGIC ITEM NO DROP
AC: +17 Str: +8 Sta: +8 Magic Resist: +12 Fire Resist: +5 Cold Resist: +12 HP: +30
Weight: 0.1
Classes: Ranger Paladin Shadowknight Bard Monk Rogue Warrior Beastlord

First off you need to get a set of scout tools (looks like a small bag) from Commander Leuz in Skyshrine (need ally faction with SS). To find commander Leuz, enter into SS from the WL side - or go from CS side and head to WL entrance.

From the WL entrance, Go through the gate.
Turn right at the "T" just beyond the gate.
Follow passage to the first room. This is where the Lieutenant is for the Giant Warrior Helm turnins.
Go through the arch to your right.

Follow passageway and take first left into another room. In this room will be the Commander and two other drakes around a long table.

Hail him and you can either go through all the chat but if all you want is the tools, just do the following.

1. Hail
2. Say "what tasks"
3. Say "I accept"

You will then get a set of scout tools.

Next, you need to go to WW and look for scout charisa. She spawns on the hill above the tunnel entrance to WW and is on a 12 hour timer. Do not give her the tools if she is holding a bag in her hands - means someone else is doing it. Only if her hands are empty. If empty, give her the tools.

This will spawn 8 giants near the shoreline just south of the Scout. Seven Kromzek Warriors and one Kromzek Captain.
All you want is the Kromzek Captain. He can be single pulled if 60. Kill him and loot the Broken Disk that he drops.

Give the disk to Scout Charisa. She'll give you a "Scout's Note" that you can return to Commander Leuz in Skyshrine. She'll also give you the Robe of Benevolence.

To get the Bracer, give the robe back to her and she will give you the Bracer. For casters, there is a talisman of benevolence they can get, and if you want this all you have to do is hand in the bracer.

Talisman stats are as follows

AC: +7 Wis: +15 Magic Resist: +12 Cold Resist: +12 HP: +38 Mana: +55
