ok first off if u dont have notepad you suck...

this is a how to on how to make your Target on a HOTKEY come up as a link.

1. open your EQ folder. EX. C:/program files/sony/everquest

2. open your "charname_servername.ini" file... EX. rustedone_innoruuk.ini, this is where if you dont have notepad you are the suck.

3. now you click on EDIT, and REPLACE, in the "find what" section you put "%t"... in the "replace with" you put.... "0026915-00001-00001-00001-00001-00001C7442069 %T"

4. now you click replace all... this will change ALL of your current hotkeys to be this way...

5. you have to zone, camp, or log in to make this work now.
Hope that helps
