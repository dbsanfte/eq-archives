Except in very specific circumstances, tanks only spam taunt if (a) they don't know what they're doing or (b) something's going wrong.

Correctly timing taunt is an important event for a group, just like a complete heal or mez.

Having tried it yesterday, it's nothing to be scared of and didn't lead to spam, but was much clearer than /hailing to identify the next mezzed mob, and and hopefully made the group more comfortable that I was doing my job when our chanter got aggroed. It certainly made ME more comfortable.
