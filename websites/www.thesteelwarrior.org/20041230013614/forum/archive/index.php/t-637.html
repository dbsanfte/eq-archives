Windblade != aggro!!! Please don't waste hard earned cash on a windblade for aggro. It is a pure DPS weapon. If you're going to spend that kind of cash, buy a Sebilisan Berzerker Cloak for haste.

If you want another aggro weapon, buy a frostbringer and equip it offhand.

If you do get a better haste item, you can replace the neck with a clattering bone necklace. You also might want to think about ditching the Lodizal shell boots and the IGS. You can sell the IGS for ~6k and buy something from the dragonwing mantle line (the ones that drop from the WW dragons).

Also think about getting STA boosting items.
