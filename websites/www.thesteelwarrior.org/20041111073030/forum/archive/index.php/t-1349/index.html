How do we fight VS?

Easy, Rangers tank him. Rangers get a 20 second melee immunity /disc as opposed to the Warriors 8 second one. We have killed him in less than 20 seconds, but it isn't necessary. All you have to do is have 2 rangers. On pull, the first ranger chain snares for aggro and engages Weaponshield. Eveyone goes max damage on VS. Usually this is enough to kill him. If it isn't, have another Ranger chain snare in the last 5 seconds and the first Ranger start jolting. This is usually sufficient to swith aggro and the second ranger can tank for another 20 seconds. It's pretty easy to kill him in less than 40 seconds if you are experienced. Just don't mess up because it takes a while for Weaponshield to reset.

Sometimes we lose a ranger, but it's rare now. VS may get off 1 or 2 taps in the fight. We didn't do this last time we fought him, but even with me tanking him he dropped really fast and his DPS is pretty negligable to me at this point.
