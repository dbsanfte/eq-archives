Divine aura / harmshield will block the DT. Stun no longer resets the DT timer and I never had any luck permastunning. Avoid the first DT, eat the 2nd, and should be dead before third.

My preferred strat:

- Set up raid in hallway south of 4-way
- Pull ALL the adds out of shei's room without aggroing merchant shei
- Send in bard to pop 4 adds and park them somewhere (north room works)
- Pull and kill merchant shei
- Have an SK charge shei, warrior 10 sec behind him. SK pops harmshield before charging, first DT bounces.
- SK clicks off harmshield and HTs shei. Warrior AE taunts and uses furious with a big 2-hander. By this point the rest of the raid should be set up on the dias behind shei blasting away.
- Chain nuke till OOM. Aggro wont be an issue after HT and furious.
- Gasp in amazement at the crap hammer she drops.
