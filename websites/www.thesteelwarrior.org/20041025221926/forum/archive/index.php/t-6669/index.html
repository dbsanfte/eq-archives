Dubb
Question, are these the same type of Rune's that drop in Baston of Thunder or is it some thing different found only in Gate of Discord zones?


Glyphed Rune Word - Planes of Power - turned in for random L65 spell. Whichever NPC you hand it in to determines what class spell it will be.

Taelosian Geomancy Stone - Gates of Discord - turned in for specific L65 spell or discipline. The class looting the stone determines what class spell/disc will be returned. The turn-in NPC is the same for everyone.

By the way, the Taelosian Geomancy Stone seems to be a somewhat common drop off any named in KT or Ikkinz.

Every class gets a nice little upgrade at something, so most people seem to think however they get passed out is fine. For instance wiz vs. war: wiz gets 10% more damage from his best damage spell, and that's about all a wiz will do in most fights, so he does his job about 10% better. Meanwhile warrior gets 10% more aggro from Incite by using BoM, but Incite/bom is only generates a small fraction of our aggro. And BoM doesn't help us tank any, so our total improvement is very small.
