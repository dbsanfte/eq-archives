Also on the the gear note...

If you don't have a good guild that raids often or pretty good gear already I wouldn't advise PoP. The loot is generally non-existant especially for tanks since the casters that use components will get first dibs on the drops.

Velk's, KC,OS, and Veksar are all pretty good for cash and item drops assuming you are still in need of such things.

Another thing, keep in mind that after 61 you'll be spending most all of your time in PoP so get out and see the other places while the exp is still pretty good.
