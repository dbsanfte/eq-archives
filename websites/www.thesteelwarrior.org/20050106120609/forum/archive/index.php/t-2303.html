The quest armor drops can be found as rare drops, allong with all other types of armor (silk, chain, leather) in Kael arena area or plate drops are uncommon in "plate house" area.

It is a cycle started that goes along these lines

Take about 12 or 18 people 50- 60 and move to plate house. May need about 24 level 50s.

Had an excellent guild for this but lost it sorry.

Goes something like say a phrase.

Dwarf spawns , someone smacks him.

two low 50 giants spawns.

Once each one of these dies a 54(?) spawns

Once a certain one of the 54 dies a 55 spawns.

Then a 58ish

Then a 60, tanking him is easy even without slow for you. With chanter and sham should be able to land slow on a couple of tries.

Then the dwarf spawns again and makes a run for exit. Need to kill him before he runs or no cycle again for like an hour or something.

Also there are 4 local spawns plus two or three longer timed named spawns in area.

There is also a spawn up stairs you will prob deal with on level 60 cause his resists are huge and snaring him can be a problem.

Each cycle should drop 0-2 (usually one) item needed for Thurgy plate armor.

Get the needed gems (non bp gems are real cheap now a days on my server (20-100pp each) and faction (kindly or warmly to coldain) and you have armor slighty better then cultural.


Sory I do not have exact information about cyle like phrase or mob names and who spawns what but my info regarding that was lost. Was on old guild website and they gone now

A good trick I was shown was charm the mob whose death line eventually triggers the 60 spawn. Kill him when other two mob line is gone.
