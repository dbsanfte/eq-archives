EmiliaEQ
Ripostes are a MYTH

90% of warriors will not be affected by the "riposte nightmare".

The reason is simple, 90% of warriors will rarely encounter
situations in which you will get killed because of that riposte.

Ripostes become really DANGEROUS when you reach a ratio
that is clearly above 15 % of your current hp.

Max hp / Mob max hit = Ratio

If you have 7khp and fight a mob that hits for 700.
You are not in real danger.
You will very rarely see CH for over 6K.
That extra one hit from the riposte, will very rarely kill you

If you have 10khp and fight a mob that hits for 3000.
You are in really big danger.
You will see MANY CH for over 7Khp.
And if you get a riposte on one of those 7K+ CH ..
You're probably dead.


Now to go back to the question :
Keep your current weapons.
Dont even bother with new augments.

Wait for BF/HG (if possible)
And try to get a sews/bow (even more important).

If you cant, well dont worry, just get a shield out when disc drops
on some really really hard hitters.
