I thought there was some kind of anti-kiting code in HoH that prevents you from picking up too many mobs.

At any rate, the damage Earthshaker does is exactly O(n^2) where n is the number of mobs, not anywhere close to exponential. The proc damage depends on the # of mobs, and the proc hits all the mobs hence you get n^2 at the end.
