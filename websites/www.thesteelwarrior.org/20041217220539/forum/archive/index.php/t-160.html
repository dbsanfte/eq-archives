Don't rely on what level other classes can do their epic turn-ins but, as Hussar pointed out, go talk to the NPC's yourself. For example, you can't get a rogue epic till level level 50, but you can get a druid epic at level 46/47 (the right-click effect won't work till 50, but you can still get the leaf-blower at 46/47).

Basically, it's different for a lot of the classes, and to add insult to injury, your own quest NPC may tell you to go hunt rats for a while longer, but they may talk to a like-level PC from another class; he may not give them the item or allow a turn-in, but he'll talk. (BTW, I'm referring to that Redblade guy in Freeport...he told my level 5 warrior mule to take a hike, but spoke at length about broken instruments with my level 5 bard...go figure.)
