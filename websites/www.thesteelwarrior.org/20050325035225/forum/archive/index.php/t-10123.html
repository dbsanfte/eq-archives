Armor has a soft cap in WoW. I believe that once you get to around 7k armor at level 60, the mitigation benefits aren't really worth it anymore, although I haven't gotten near that point yet. The mouseover tells you exactly how much extra mitigation is provided by AC. There is no need to guess at all, even in light of whatever cap that exists unless the information you get on mitigation is wrong.

For example if I have 1000 HP and adding a shield gets me from mitigate 50% to 60% then that's exactly the same as adding 250 HPs ((1000/0.4 - 1000/0.5) X 0.5). Given melee damage in WoW has a pretty small interval AC and HP might as well be the same when it comes to melee damage.

Now of course there's the stuff that doesn't get affected by AC but does get affected by HP to worry about, but that's another issue.
