This idea has come out of spell crits and Ranger Bow Crits. Judging them against our normal crits and cripples when fighting I seem to think our crits are lacking. I think they should be upgraded. Not only in damage output, but there should be a stunn associated with it and should have the agro generation of paladin spells. Barboki,

That's not a totally wrong headed way of looking at things. But increasing the success rate of the Taunt button is much better than decreasing the refresh rate. Rolling the dice faster is not a reasonable substitute for decent house odds especially if you don't know if you've gotten any winning rolls.

With all the crit related disciplines, crit rate is somewhat scalable to effort put in. I agree that our crits and our offensive disciplines lack the flash and probably the effectiveness of things like Endless Quiver and SU3.
