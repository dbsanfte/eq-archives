milloeq
shiny brass shield from the orc trainer at the hilltop just to the right of the zone in to Crushbone, is an easy drop at that level. Also bazaar hunting for a chitin shell shield or cracked darkwood shield (highpass gnoll named drop) should be easy, maybe 10-15p for either. As for 1hs, without much plat you might be able to find a crystallized longsword (the 11/29 one not the 10/38 or whatver) or anything in the bazaar that has better than 10/30 at that level.

Also dont neglect 1hb and 1hp, both are equally viable anymore since they fixed the skill cap on piercing, and both have some super choices for low and higher levels.

some cheap 1hb include a mudman enforcer (8/20 +3str), velium etched stone mace (8/24 +3wis i think) centi warhammer (15/32 +stats not sure which) etc etc. 1hp include slime coated harpoon, jade chokidai prod, and a bunch more i cant think of.

Good luck on MR

imo, the best 1hs for your level that you can buy, and it's cheap if you can find it (it came from old droga, no longer drops)...Whip of Strangulation....its only 6/24, but the proc is nice...20dd + 12hp/tick, agi/str debuff, starts procing at level 10.
