Gnomb
We usually exp in the 2 tunnels leading off the arena so we have more mobs to kill.
Second good camp is the armorer/supplier area.
They drop some nice loot like my clerics gloves, a int caster headpiece with extended range 4, some nice hybrid boots although they are not that bad for a cleric either and of course runes.
Northwest and southwest tunnel as the camps are called are pretty safe although they seem to have changed northwest somewhat.
Last weekend when we were in NW (to get shikarii level 65 and i was on my cleric) i saw a ton of adds coming from further inside, not to mention a lot of tortured soul spawns (they are roamers that spawn upon a npc death randomly) i never seen before in that number.
Btw. the first crossroads mentioned with the diaku elite and other nasty mobs leads to the Vallon Zek (right side) and Tallon Zek (left side) spawns.
I hear people exp there as well but i wouldnt bother.
Gindan Flayers can split around 40 health in 2 similar mobs with full health(mezzable but hit for 800+), Hendin Assassins have a nasty 800 point AE and a knockback that removes top buff.
Although some nice stuff can drop off the named around there, like a 15/22 damage piercer (droppable) and ornate drops (my ornate pants dropped on a raid when we killed the named clearing to VZ) i havent exp'd in these parts of tactics.

And if you see 'You hear a pounding of hooves' it is usually followed by an /ooc Piggy, at least on Terris
