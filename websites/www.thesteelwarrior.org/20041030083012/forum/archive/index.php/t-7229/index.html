Brutul
Best stuff to farm at low levels is tradeskill stuff. Lesser shades in shadeweaver's thicket drop shadeling silks much quicker than newbie zone spiderlings and are used in leather padding for smiths. You could also go to mons letalis and kill greyhoppers for low quality rockhopper hides and make the padding yourself. The silk and padding should sell pretty well in the bazaar depending on your server. At 41 you may also be able to get a group in Dulak Harbor, and gem drops are pretty good there, with stuff like blue diamonds dropping of of exp mobs.

Brabble
As Omnus points out, Hollowshade grimlings are an option.
If you get a couple levels and can find a partner also in need of cash, you can (carefully) make your way to the Grimling caves in Ten. Mountains. In addition to other nice loot, they drop windstones (check the prices on your server's bazaar and I doubt you'll vendor a windstone ever again). Also, the grimling runner, who drops the rallic pack pattern, spawns or runs by at least one of those caves.
I didn't start farming there until level 50, when I could do it solo, which is why I suggest you get a partner. But you'll both make good plat doing this, and, at your level, some experience.

Edit: I happened to do something last night while on a 52 War at the grimling caves that may be of interest here. With Regen 3, sitting, focused will (until it ran out) and max bind wound skill for that toon's level, I was healing myself for over 300 hitpoints a minute. Doesn't really help in a fight, but at that level it certainly cuts down on your downtime if you plan on farming. In your 40s, of course, regen AA is not an option yet, but now that you can sit and at the same tim BW up to 70%, you might find it worth it to max your BW skill if you plan on farming a good bit.
