OK, being nitpicky, the Plankton Laced Greatsword edges out the Windblade in practice. The is mainly because the Windblade apllies DB more often in comparison to overcome the DB and better "strait-up" ratio that the planton is given. On first look, the plankton looks ungodly good, but due to the way DB scales with delay, the windblade comes out ahead.

Here's your base figures in DPS based on (DMG+DB)/DLY. (Your current weapon included for comparison.) The higher the better. DB figured for level 65.

Name - BaseDPS
Plankton Laced Greatsword (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=25986) - 2.123
Windblade (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=25979) - 2.159
Dathor Great Hammer (http://lucy.allakhazam.com/item.html?id=8137) - 1.705

The windblade edges out ahead ever so slightly, but I wouldn't lose any sleep over it. If you can't get a windblade and wanna camp something, go for the Plankton. If you have the cash, go for the windblade. A windblade will be a huge upgrade for you.

You don't need a parse to run these numbers.
