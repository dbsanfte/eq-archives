To corner a mob:

- MA gets shrunk to gnome sized
- MA engages mob and runs with his back into the corner.

This leaves the mobs back facing the raid, and only the MA on the front.

when assist is called, I normally look down and run backwards, not sure if this actually helps keep the mob in mellee range, but it works so keep doing it )).

Effective way to deal with mobs that have nasty frontal AOE's, or if in small space. Also nice since mob pushing isn't a real issue.

I still prefer to fight mobs in the open, helps with ranger DPS.
