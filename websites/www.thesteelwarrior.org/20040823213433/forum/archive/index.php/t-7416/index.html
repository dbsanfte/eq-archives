Retnaburn
[1] Place your Jagged Blade of War (2hs) into the scabbard.

[2] Place a Summoned: Sword of Runes into the scabbard.

[3] Hit combine.

At this point you will have the unaugmentable 2hs *and* both 1hs on your cursor. Place them all in your bags before zoning or you risk losing 2/3 of them.

[4] Go find your buddy Gartrog Smitehammer in East Freeport.

[5] Hand him the unaugmentable 2hs *and* the scabbard, but *not* the 1hs. Make sure you are not invis.

At this point you get back the augmentable versions of all three epics. This means you have a single 2hs and four 1hs.

If you play EQ on a mac, skip steps 4 and 5 but save that scabbard for future use.
