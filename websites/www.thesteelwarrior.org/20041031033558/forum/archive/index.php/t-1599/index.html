Neksnappa
Lol I don't get why everyone says mainhand BCA over frosty.

DON'T!

The proc's on frosty and bca generate almost exactly the same ammount of aggro, and frosty's 12/22 is so much more dps than bca's 9/21 that there shouldn't be an argument at all.

Aggro: Frosty/BCA
DPS: Either jade mace/ GCM or GCM / Centi, those two combo's are neck and neck for dps, and gcm/centi gives you a ton more hp/str.

Best bet for you is to get 51 and buy a 1:1 ratio two-hander. I have frosty/bca as my dual wield aggro weapons, and a 44/44 for dps, and I almost always use the 44/44, because the pure damage it puts out generates as much aggro as frosty/bca, and mobs drop a lot faster in groups with it. The only time's I would use frosty/bca are when you have no haster in group, or your dex is so high you get crits like mad with frosty/bca.
